Title:
Mexican Tostada Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 4 oz can diced chile peppers
1 tablespoon corn meal
1 can refrigerated pizza dough
1 can refried beans
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup chopped tomatoes

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook meat until brown.
Drain off fat.
Add 3/4 cup water, chile peppers, taco seasoning mix. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 15-20 minutes until most of the liquid is gone.
Meanwhile, grease a 12X8 baking sheet and sprinkle with corn meal.
Form pizza dough onto cookie sheet and bake in 400 degree oven for 5 minutes.
Spread refried beans over partially baked dough.
Spoon meat mixture over beans.
Bake for 10 minutes or until crust is golden.
Sprinkle with cheese and return to oven 1 to 2 minutes until cheese melts.
Top with lettuce and tomatoes.

Description: 
This is an easy mexican pizza made with refrigerated pizza dough.  Serve with taco sauce and sour cream for a great mexican treat.
