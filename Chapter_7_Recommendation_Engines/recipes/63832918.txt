Title:
Lu's Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065281.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising flour
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup shortening
3/4 cup + 2 Tbsp buttermilk

Directions:
HEAT oven to 450Â°F. Spray shiny cookie sheet lightly with no-stick cooking spray.
Place flour and baking soda in large bowl. Cut in shortening with pastry blender or fork until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Add butter milk; stir with fork until soft dough forms and mixture begins to pull away from sides of bowl.
KNEAD dough on lightly floured surface just until smooth (10-12 times).
Roll out dough to 1/2-inch thickness. Cut with floured 2-inch round cutter. Place biscuits with sides touching on prepared cookie sheet.
BAKE 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Brush with melted butter. Serve warm.
