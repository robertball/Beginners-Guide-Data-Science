Title:
The Gibson Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4563030.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
2 fluid ounces gin
1/2 fluid ounce dry vermouth
1 cocktail onion

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; pour in gin and vermouth. Stir well; strain into a chilled glass and garnish with a cocktail onion.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
It's a martini, basically, where a cocktail onion replaces the olive.
