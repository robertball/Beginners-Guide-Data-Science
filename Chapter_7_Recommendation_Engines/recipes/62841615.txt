Title:
Bolognese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs olive oil
6oz pancetta finely chopped
2oz finely chopped onion
2oz finely chopped carrot
2oz finely chopped celery
10oz finely chopped beef
1 Tbs tomato paste
4 oz red wine
6oz milk plus extra if needed

Directions:
1.Heat the oil in a heavy saucepan and brown the pancetta.Add the chopped veggies and cook until onions are translucent.Add beef and cook until brown and crumbly.
2. Stir in tomato paste and wine, then slowly add milk stirring until absorbed.
3.Cover and let simmer gently for 3-4 hrs. Stir occasionally to keep sauce from sticking to pan and add milk if sauce becomes too dry.
4. When finished sauce should be thick and smooth with the meat being almost disintegrated, season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Chef Lucky
Description: 
Classic sauce, sometimes i will add a bit of chix stock or beef stock just to up the flavor a bit and a touch of cheyenne pepper.
