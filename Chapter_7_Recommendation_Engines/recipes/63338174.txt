Title:
STATE FAIR CREAM PUFF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/2 cup butter
1 cup flour
1/4 tsp salt
4 eggs
2 TBSP Milk
1 Egg (lightly beaten)

Directions:
In a large saucepan bring the water, butter and salt to a boil over medium heat. Add flour all at once and stir until smooth and a ball forms.  Remove from heat.
let stand about 5 minutes. Add eggs, 1 at a time beating well after each one.
Continue beating until smooth & shiny.
Drop by 1/4 cupfuls 3 inches apart onto greased baking sheets. Combine milk & egg and brush over puffs. Bake at 400 for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown.
Immediately cut a slit in each for steam to escape; cool
