Title:
Ginger dressing/ osaka sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup olive oil
2 1/2 T gingerroot
1 stalk celery
1/2 t. sugar
1/4 c. toasted sesame seeds
1/8 t. celery seed
1/2 t. ketchup
1/4 c. chopped onion
1/3 c. vinegar

Directions:
Put all in mixer and blend for 1 minute. Store in refrigerator. Makes about a pint.
