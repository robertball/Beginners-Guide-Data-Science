Title:
Mocha Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1 cup sifted flour
1/8 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 square chocolate (can substitute 1 oz. cocoa powder)
2 TBS butter
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
Topping:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
4 TBS cocoa powder
1 cup cold strong coffee

Directions:
Melt the chocolate and butter over hot water or in the microwave.
Blend in milk and vanilla.  Mix well.
Stir into dry ingredients.
Pour into greased 8 x 8 inch pan.
Pour topping over before putting into 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes then turn upside down onto serving plate.  Top will be runny.

Submitter: 
Lisa Proctor
Description: 
Handed down from my grandmother, this family favorite disappears as soon as it's cool enough to eat.
