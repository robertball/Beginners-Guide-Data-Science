Title:
Classic Pudding Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3733898.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1/4 cup white sugar

Directions:
Beat milk, evaporated milk, instant pudding, and sugar in a bowl until thoroughly combined. Pour into ice pop molds and freeze until firm, about 3 hours.
