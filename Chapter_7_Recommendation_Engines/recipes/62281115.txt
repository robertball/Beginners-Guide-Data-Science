Title:
Jim's Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
21 oz Pink or red salmon drained
2 -tablespoon mayonnaise
2 -Eggs beaten
1/4 lb - (1 sleeve)  saltine crackers crushed
1 1/2 - teaspoons Worchestershire sauce
1- teaspoon crushed red pepper
1- teaspoon dry mustard
1 1/2 - teaspoons fresh lemon juice
Butter or vegetable oil for cooking as needed

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together in a mixing bowl. Refrigerate 30 minutes then form into 6 patties. Fry in vegetable oil or butter approx 4 minutes on each side until golden brown. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
My mom made these salmon patties all year! they were a favorite of all us kids and the neighbors too.
