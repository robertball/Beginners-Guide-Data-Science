Title:
Corned Beef Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) tin corned beef
1 egg (beaten)
1 small onion (grated)
1/8 teaspoon thyme leaves
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 medium potatoes
parsley

Directions:
1. Boil potatoes and mash. Set aside.
2. Beat egg and mix with corned beef.
3. Add grated onion, black pepper,
thyme, chopped parsley, and mashed
potato.
4. Shape into icecream scoop sized
balls and lightly flour.
5. Fry in hot oil and flatten before
golden brown in color on both sides.
*Good if served with rice, baked beans and letucce and tomato.

Submitter: 
Chris T
Description: 
Corned beef, potatoes and spices combined to make a crispy patty.
