Title:
Braised Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. bone-in beef short ribs
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 T vegetable oil
1/2 package bacon, diced
1 large onion, slicked 1/4 in. thick
1 T. minced garlic
1 T. tomato paste
2 T. flour
2 T. spicy brown mustard
1 12 oz. bottle Irish stout
1 bay leaf
1 cup beef broth
2 T. chopped parsley

Directions:
Season beef with salt and pepper. Heat oil in large skillet till almost smoking, cook short ribs in skillet turning to brown on all sides. Work in small batches so it is easy to move and brown well. As each batch is done, transfer to a crock pot.
Cook bacon until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Reserve 1 T. bacon drippings.
Saut√© onion in bacon reserved bacon drippings until soft and translucent, stir as needed. Add garlic, tomato paste, flour, mustard 1 tsp. salt and 1.2 tsp. pepper. Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Remove pan from heat, add stout, stirring to scrape bits from the pan. Pour over short ribs in crock pot. Add bacon, beef broth and bay leaf.
Cover and cook 8 hours on low until meat is tender and falls off the bone.
Remove beef and skim fat from the cooking liquid. Remove bay leaf and add parsley. Serve over mashed potatoes or egg noodles.
