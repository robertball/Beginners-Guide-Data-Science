Title:
Quick and Easy Papayascotch Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped papayas (about 2 very large ones), seeds and peel removed
6 cups sugar
Â½ cup lemon juice

Directions:
Put papaya pieces, sugar, and lemon juice into large pot.  Bring to a boil and let boil for two minutes, then turn low and let cook for an hour until liquid turns color.  Stir often with a potato masher, mashing as you go.  Pour into hot sterilized jars and seal.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This tasty jam recipe is cheaper to make than most, since it doesn't call for pectin.
