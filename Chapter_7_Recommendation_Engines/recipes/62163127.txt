Title:
Pepper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 8oz  bag Shredded Monterey jack cheese with jalapenos
2- packs  8oz Cream cheese ( softened)
1- Clove garlic (minced)
1- 14oz Can Whole Tomatoes (drained and chopped)
1-2 jalepenos diced

Directions:
In casserole dish add shredded cheese, cream cheese, garlic, tomatoes and diced jalepenos. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes or until brown and bubbly.

A great dip for crackers or tortilla chips
