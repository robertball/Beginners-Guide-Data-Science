Title:
Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1942074.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBS Crushed Garlic
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
1/4 Cup Dry Bread Crumbs
1/4 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
4 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breasts

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
2. Warm garlic and olive oil to blend flavors. In a separate dish, combine bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese. Dip chicken in olive oil/garlic mixture, then into bread crumb mixture. place in shallow baking dish.
3. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until no longer pink and juice runs clear.
