Title:
Hawaiian Cake (No alcohol Version) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 oz.) Box White cake mix
1 (3.5oz or 4 serving) box Instant coconut cream pudding mix
1 Tbsp. Rum extract
1 (8oz.) Can Crushed Pineapple (Undrained)
Frosting:
1 (8oz.) container Extra Creamy cool whip, thawed
1 (3.5oz) pkg Instant Coconut Cream pudding mix
1/4 cup flaked coconut
1 tbsp. coconut extract
1 tsp. Rum extract
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Prepare cake mix as directed on package and add 1 box of instant pudding mix, 1 tbsp. rum extract and the pineapple.  Mix until well blended.
Bake 30-35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean.
Frosting:
When cake is fully cooled, Mix 1 pckg of instant pudding mix with milk and blend slowly until smooth and creamy
Stir in entire container of cool whip, coconut and Rum extract, and 1/4 cup of flaked coconut until well blended and spread over cake
Top with toasted coconut.
Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
I know there are alot of variations for this kind of cake but I experimented a little and found a recipe that my family really likes. I hope yours does too. It's flavorful without being overpowering like some other recipes I've tried like this. It doesn't last long in Our house!
