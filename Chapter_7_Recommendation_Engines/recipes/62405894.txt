Title:
Saffy-tini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 shots premium vodka (like Gray Goose)
2 shots Chocolate Liqueur (Godiva)
1 shot Amaretto (Disaronno is my favorite)
Ice, for shaking
Chocolate shavings (optional) for garnish

Directions:
Shake vodka, chocolate liqueur and amaretto together with ice in a cocktail shaker until well mixed and cold. Strain into a martini glass, and garnish with chocolate shavings, if desired.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
I had this martini in a lodge in Vail - and the owners had named it after their dog, who loved to lay in front of their fire place. I recreated it at home, and decided to re-name it after my dog, Saffy, who likes to lay by my fireplace!
