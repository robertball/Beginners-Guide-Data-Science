Title:
Josh's sourdough bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. flour
2 c. warm water
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 c. oil
2 T. starter
caraway seeds, opt.

Directions:
Mix, pour into oiled pan. Place another oiled pan on top. Let rise for about 8 hours.
Bake for 25 minutes covered and 25 uncovered at 375 degrees.
