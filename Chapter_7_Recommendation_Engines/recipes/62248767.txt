Title:
Iced mint tea w/ vanilla vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bunches of fresh mint/1 1/2 c diced
1 1/2 c sugar
3 qt water
1 1/2 c vanilla vodka

Directions:
Combine first 3 and boil, stir to dissole sugar. Cool to room temp. and chill 2 hours
Strain mint leaves out.
Mix in vodka in a pitcher and serve with mint leaves
