Title:
Pickled Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 1 1/2 cups carrots, thinly sliced on the bias
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
1 T sherry or sherry vinegar
1/8 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp Kosher salt
1 tsp caraway seeds *optional*

Directions:
Combine all in non reactive bowl.
Stir until sugar and salt have dissolved.
Refrigerate a minimum of 2 days before eating.
If using a bowl, place a small plate on top to keep carrots submerged.
