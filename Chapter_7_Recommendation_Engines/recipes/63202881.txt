Title:
Shaun's Potato Bake Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/828448.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pound red potatoes with skins on cut in 1" cubes
1 lb turkey kielbasa or sausage (low fat) sliced 1/4" pieces
1 - 1 1/4 lb chicken breast cut into bite size pieces
1 medium white onion coarsely chopped
2 tbsp. red wine vinegar
2 tsp. olive oil
1 1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning (or to taste)
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. olive oil
1 tsp. Kosher salt
1 tsp. coarse black pepper
1 1/2 cups Mexican Cheese blend or Mozzarella/cheddar shredded cheese
1 tbsp. salt free seasoning such as Mrs. DashÂ® Original
1-2 tbsp. butter or margarine
Sour cream

Directions:
Place chicken in a plastic zipper bag.  Add red wine vinegar, 2 tsp. olive oil, Italian seasoning and garlic.  Marinate in the fridge 30 min to 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Boil potatoes in tsp. salt water until almost done (still slightly firm) approx 10 - 15 min
While potatoes are boiling, brown turkey sausage in pan sprayed with olive oil.  Remove from pan and add onions.  Saute until they start to be come translucent.
Spray 2 qt square casserole dish with non-stick spray.  Add potatoes, slice approx. 1-2 Tbsp. butter evenly over potatoes.  Sprinkle with non-salt seasoning and pepper.  Top with turky sausage and onions.  Bake for 18 minutes.
While potatoes and sausage are baking, cook the chicken in the saute pan with 1 tsp. olive oil until almost done.
Spread chicken evenly over potato/sausage mixture, cover with cheese and bake until cheese is melted (about 10 minutes)
Serve with sour cream

Submitter: 
Kilyena
Description: 
This is a delicious and hearty meal my husband Shaun came up with one day when all we had was potatoes, turkey sausage,chicken and cheese. It was a huge hit with us as it is with everyone we've made it for.  It's one of our all time favorite recipes.
