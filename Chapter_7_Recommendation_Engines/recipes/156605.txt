Title:
Corny Jalapeno Hush Puppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986185.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup self-rising flour
1 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 cup milk
1/2 cup diced onion
1/2 cup Birds Eye® Sweet Kernel Corn
1 tablespoon jalapeno pepper, diced
Oil for deep frying

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. In a separate bowl combine the egg, milk, onion, corn, and jalapeno. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.  In an electric skillet or deep fryer, heat oil to 375 degrees F. Drop batter tablespoon by tablespoon into oil. Fry 2 to 3 minutes, or until golden brown, turning occasionally. Drain on paper towels, serve warm.

Submitter: 
Birds Eye®

These southern delights make a great appetizer for guests or go great with BBQ or any of your favorite comfort foods.
