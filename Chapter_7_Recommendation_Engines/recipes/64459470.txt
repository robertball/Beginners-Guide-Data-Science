Title:
Prosciutto Spinach Spirals Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refrigerated pizza crust
2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/3 cup garlic and herb spreadable cheese, softened
6 oz prosciutto
5 cups loosely packed spinach leaves
1 large shallot, finely chopped

Directions:
Heat oven to 375. Spray a large cooking sheet with cooking spray. Unroll pizza dough onto sprayed wax paper. Press dough into a 16x12 inch rectangle. Sprinkle half of Italian seasoning over dough. Carefully turn dough upside down onto cookie sheet. Sprinkle remaining Italian seasoning. Spread cheese evenly over dough, top with prosciutto, spinach and shallot. Starting on 1 long side, roll dough tightly, press seams to seal. Bake 20-30 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 10 minutes. Cut diagonally and serve with marinara sauce.
