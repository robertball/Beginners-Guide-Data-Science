Title:
Apple Cinnamon Metabolism Booster Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
water
1 apple thinly sliced
1 cinnamon stick

Directions:
Drop apple slices in the bottom of a 2 qt. pitcher and then the cinnamon stick. Cover with ice about 1/2 up then add water. Let sit for a couple of hours. Makes one big pitcher (2qts?). Refill water 3-4 times before replacing apples and cinnamon.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
Flavored water to help boost metabolism and increase your desire to drink more water.
