Title:
Sugar Diamond Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (no sub.)
1 cup sugar
1 egg, separated
1/2 tsp. almond flavoring
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
pinch salt
1/2 cup cut mix glace (candied fruit) or chopped pecans (or a combo)

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.
Add egg yolk and flavoring. Mix well.
Add flour, cinnamon and salt.
Spoon into a greased 15" x 10" x 1" pan.
Using plastic wrap over the dough, press evenly into pan. Remove wrap.
Beat egg white till foamy and brush over dough.
Sprinkle evenly with fruit or nuts.
Alternate: use cinnamon with an additional 1 tbsp. sugar to sprinkle over top before baking.
Bake @ 300 x 30 min.
Cut while warm.
These freeze well and keep a long time.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These are usually on my Christmas baking list - they add some nice variety to a tray of bars, add color from the candied fruit, are light tasting so you don't fill up before you can sample other sweets and have a nice balance of sweetness.
