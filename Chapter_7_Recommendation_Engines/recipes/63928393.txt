Title:
Ricotta Gnocchi & Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089237.jpg
Ingredients:
Gnocchi:
8oz Fresh Ricotta
3/4 Grated Parmesan
2 eggs
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1/2tsp pepper
1 1/2 - 2 cups flour
Sauce:
1 - 15 oz can of Diced Tomatoes
1/2 pint Grape Tomatoes, halved
3 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 Tbsp Garlic, chopped
6 Basil leaves, chopped
1/4 white wine
8oz Mozzarella, cubed
Salt/pepper or red pepper, to taste

Directions:
Gnocchi:
Mix ricotta, Parmesan, eggs, garlic powder, salt & pepper until blended. Slowly mix in flour.
Cut dough into 4 pieces & roll into 1/2 inch thick ropes. Cut into 1/2 pieces. Boil in salted water for 1-2 mins., or until gnocchi float to the top.
Sauce:
Heat olive oil and saute halved tomatoes, until tender. Add garlic and saute for 1 minute longer. Add wine & bring to a simmer. Smash tomatoes with a fork. Add canned tomatoes, basil, salt & pepper. Reduce until thick, about 10 minutes. Add cooked gnocchi. Remove from heat and add cubed mozzarella. Serve with shaved Parmesan.

Submitter: 
Samantha Jones
Description: 
I love Gnocchi but I always found them to often be too heavy. This recipe offers you a light fluffy and tasty version. Enjoy!
