Title:
Pumpkin and baby bella mushroom pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onions
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 cloves garlic
2 containers baby portabella mushrooms
1-15 ounce can of pumpkin
1 can vegetable or chicken soup broth
1 teaspoon sage
1/2 teaspoon salt or pepper
1 box whole wheat pasta

Directions:
1. Cook pasta, set aside
2. Heat oil in a loarge nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until soft.
3. Add portabella mushrooms.
4. Add pumkin, soup broth, sage, and salt.  Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Pour on top of pasta and enjoy.
