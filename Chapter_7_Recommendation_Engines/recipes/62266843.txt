Title:
Linda's Hearty Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T Olive Oil
1 Cup chopped onion
1 T chopped garlic
1 lb of cubed lean beef (1/2" cubes)
2 T flour
2 T Paprika
3 T Tomato paste
14 oz can diced tomatoes
2 cans beef broth
4 medium red potatoes, diced
4 carrots, diced
1 green pepper, diced
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp marjoram
salt and pepper to taste.
1/2 cup sour cream (added before serving)

Directions:
In a dutch oven add olive oil and onion, cook until translucent, 5 minutes.  Stir in garlic.
Add beef and cook until browned.  Sprinkle with the flour and paprika.  Stir and cook 3 minutes.
Stir in tomato paste, diced tomotoes, beef broth, potatoes, carrots and green pepper.  Add water to cover.
Add 1 bay leaf, marjoram, salt and pepper, to taste.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer 30 - 45 minutes.  Remove bay leat.
Stir in 1/2 cup sour cream.  Stir until smooth.
