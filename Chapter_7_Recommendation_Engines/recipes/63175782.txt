Title:
JJ's Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can pinto beans
1 can black beans
1 can Rotel tomatos
1 packet ranch dressing mix
1 packet taco seasoning
1 cup frozen corn
5 cups water
Sour Cream
Fritos
Cheese

Directions:
Cook and drain the beef. Drain the pinto and black beans. Mix the beef, beans, tomatos, corn, ranch mix, taco seasoning and water in a crock pot. Cook on low for 8 hours. Serve with Fritos, Sour Cream and Cheese.
