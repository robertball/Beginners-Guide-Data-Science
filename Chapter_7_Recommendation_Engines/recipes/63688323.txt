Title:
Eye of Round Roast Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. roast
2 T. large flake pepper
2 T. salt
3 T. minced (or granulated) garlic

Directions:
Mix together pepper, salt, and garlic.
Generously rub onto the roast, coating evenly and massaging it in.
Cook as usual.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Tried at home, and it certainly gives the meat a wonderful flavor and tenderness.
