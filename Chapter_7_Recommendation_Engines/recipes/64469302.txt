Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2458706.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
Peanut Butter (Jiff's Whipped Creamy works best)
Nutella
Marshmallow creme
Mini Marshmallows (colored and fruit-flavored works best)

Directions:
1. Slice the banana
2. Spread nutella OR peanut butter on each banana slice
3. Pile marshmallow creme on top of each banana slice
4. Arrange mini marshmallows on top of marshmallow creme

Submitter: 
Rachael Acheson
Description: 
Need a sugar rush for GIsHWheS? Pop these mites onto a plate and watch your dino friends gobble (or perhaps chomp? ravage?) them up!
