Title:
Corn Bread Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted, plus more for pan
1 1/2 cups yellow or white cornmeal
1 cup all-purpose flour (spooned and leveled)
6 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon fine salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup buttermilk
3 large eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Butter an 8-inch square baking pan.
In a large bowl, stir together cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and baking soda.
In a medium bowl, whisk together melted butter, buttermilk, and eggs.
Add buttermilk mixture to flour mixture and stir just until combined (do not overmix).
Transfer batter to pan and smooth top.
Bake until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
Let cool in pan on a wire rack, 15 minutes, before serving.
