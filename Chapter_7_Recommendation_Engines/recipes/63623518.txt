Title:
Easy Zucchini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of zucchini
1 stick of butter
1 tsp of salt
1 tsp of curry
1 can of chicken broth

Directions:
Melt butter in a pot.
Cut up zucchini. (leave the skin on)
Put all of the ingredients into the pot with the butter and cook until zucchini is soft.
Puree or blend the soup.
Enjoy and serve it warm!

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
This creamy (but cream-free!) soup is a great addition to any meal. It's quick and easy, you will have your guests thinking that you have been cooking all day!
