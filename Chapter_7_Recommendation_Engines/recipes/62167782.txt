Title:
Cherry Almond pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2- 1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sliced almonds
ice water
filling:
2-3 cups fresh pie cherries (tart when fresh)
1/2-3/4 cup sugar
1 Tbls cornstarch
1-2 Tbls almond extract
1-2 Tbls amaretto liquer (optional)
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
crust:
cut small chunks of butter into flour and mix and mush with hands until butter is combined with flour and resembles tiny pebbles or cornmeal.  Add almond slivers.  sprinkle with ice water till dough holds together in a ball.  cover in plastic wrap and refrigerate at least a half hour.
filling:
take out cherry pits and half cherries.  add sugar (less if using sweet cherries) and salt and toss. combine almond extract, amaretto, and cornstarch till smooth. combine with cherries and mix well.
roll out crust and prebake for 15 min.  fill pie shell with cherry mixture.  bake on 400 for 35-45 minutes or until edges of dough are golden and filling looks bubbly.
(topping optional:  any leftover dough makes lovely lattice topping, brush with egg and sprinkle with sugar)

Submitter: 
lunalady
Description: 
Fresh cherry and almonds, a simple combination that works remarkably well!  I like it a little less sweet, so adjust accordingly...
