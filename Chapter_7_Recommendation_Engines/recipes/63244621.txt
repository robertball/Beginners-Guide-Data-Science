Title:
Healthy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 LB ground Turkey
1 LB ground lean Pork
1 1/2 vidalia onion (chopped)
1 medium green pepper (chopped)
1 jalapeño (minced)
1 15.5 oz can Bush's Chili Magic chili starter mild
1 28 oz can Rotel
1 14.5 oz can fire roasted diced tomatoes w/ garlic
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes w/ green pepper & onion
16 oz chicken broth
1 1/2 Tbsp cocoa powder
1 1/2 Tbsp chili powder
2 Tbsp oregano
2 Tsp garlic salt
2 Tsp Paprika
2 Tsp cumin
1 Tsp cayenne

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan coated with cooking spray add 1 onion (chopped) cooking until tender - about 10 min. Add Chili Magic chili starter, oregano & chicken broth. Simmer for 20 mins.
Meanwhile, in a large stock pot coated with cooking spray add the remaining 1/2 onion, bell pepper, jalapeño cooking until crisp tender about 5-8 min. Add remaining spices stir & cook another 2 min. Add turkey & pork cook until no pink remains.
*Optional* (I added this step primarily because my husband hates the texture of & won't eat beans but i wanted to include the nutritional benefits & flavor - so in essense i "hid" them) Remove bean mixture from heat & cool slightly. Blend bean mixture in blender until smooth.
Add bean mixture & canned tomatoes, including Rotel. Simmer for three or more hours. Top with your favorite garnishes or enjoy as is!
