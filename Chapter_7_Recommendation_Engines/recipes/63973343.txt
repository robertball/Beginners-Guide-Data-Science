Title:
Blueberry Microwave Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup water
2 cups blueberries, fresh or frozen
1 tablespoon cornstarch
2 teaspoons lemon juice

Directions:
Mix sugar and corn starch together.  Add water and lemon juice, mix.  Add blueberries.  Microwave on HIGH for 4 1/2 - 5 minutes (8-9 if frozen).  For thicker sauce use up to 1 Tbsp. more cornstarch.
