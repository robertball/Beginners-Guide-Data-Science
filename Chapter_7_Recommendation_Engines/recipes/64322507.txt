Title:
Canadian Rocks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb butter
2 cups sifted flour
1 tbsp water
3/4 to 1 cup chopped pecans
1 tsp vanilla
5 tsp powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream butter, add flour slowly.
Add vanilla, water, sugar and nuts.
Refrigerate. When mixture is slightly cool, work into balls, he size of large marbles.
Bake in 350 degree oven until golden brown. (check in 25-30 min).
Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
