Title:
Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 C sugar
1 T. cornstarch
1/4 t. salt
3 C. whole milk
2 egg yolks
1/2 can evaporated milk
1 C. whipping cream
1 T. vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine sugar, cornstarch, and salt in medium saucepan.
Stir in milk over medium heat and keep stirring until mixture begins to simmer
Reduce heat to low and simmer 1 min. Set aside.
In small bowl, lightly beat egg yolks. Stir in 1 C. milk
Stir egg mixture into other mixture.
Cook and stir over low heat 2 min. or until slightly thickened.
Stir in evaporated milk, whipping cream, and vanilla.
Cool to room temp.
Pour into ice cream canister. Freeze according to directions.

Submitter:

Description: 
Smooth and creamy. You can add anything to it.
