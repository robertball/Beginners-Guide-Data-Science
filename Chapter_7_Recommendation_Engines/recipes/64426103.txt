Title:
Easy Drop Beignets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c water
1 c milk
1 large egg
3 c all-purpose flour
2 Tbsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 tsp sugar
3 c vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except oil in large mixing bowl until smooth.
Pour oil into deep skillet or pot and heat over medium to medium high heat.
Once oil is hot, drop batter by spoonful into the hot oil and fry, turning once, until golden brown and puffy (they will be floating).
Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
Serve.

Submitter: 
BMG
Description: 
This is a quick recipe for beignets which does not require yeast or rolling and cutting.
