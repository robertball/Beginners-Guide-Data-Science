Title:
Quick Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Zatarain's Jambalaya Rice Mix
1 can of chicken broth
1/2 cup of water
Turkey Sausage, sliced
Hot Italian Sausage (optional)
Grilled Chicken (optional)
1 can of diced tomato (drained)
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 red pepper, diced
1/2 green pepper chopped
1/4 stick of butter
1 T of vegetable oil
Creole seasoning (see allrecipes.com recipe)

Directions:
Heat butter and oil in large skillet
Boil chicken broth and water for rice
Drop all chopped vegetables, can of tomatoes, and sliced meat into butter/oil and simmer while rice cooks
Once the water/broth mixture is boiling, drop in the rice and spices and cook, covered for 20-25 minutes
Serve vegetables and meat on top of rice
Serve with biscuits
