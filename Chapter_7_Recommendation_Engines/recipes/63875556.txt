Title:
Holiday Treat Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075147.jpg
Ingredients:
3 TB butter
10 oz. mini marshmallows
6 c. Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
24 oz. white chocolate chips or bark
holiday sprinkles

Directions:
Melt the butter in a deep pan.
Add marshmallows and stir until melted.
Stir in cereal until well blended.
Spread in a greased 13 x 9 pan.***
Once cereal mixture has cooled, cut into 64 small squares.
Melt white chocolate in the microwave in 30 second intervals; stirring after each time until completely melted.
Dip the cereal squares in to the chocolate and lay on a piece of waxed paper.  Add sprinkles while the chocolate is still wet.
Allow chocolate to set before serving.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
Marshmallow treats have always been one of my guilty food pleasures.  The white chocolate just takes them to the next level, in my book.
