Title:
Two Tone Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups (packed)brown sugar
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup evaporated milk
1/2 cup butter
1 Jar Marshmallow Cream
1 Tsp Vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 Package Butterscotch Chips
1 Package Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Combine sugars, milk and butter in a medium saucepan.
Bring to a full boil over medium heat stirring occasionally
remove from heat
Add Jar Marshmallow cream and Vanilla
Stir till smooth
to 1/2 the mixture add the package of butterscotch chips and 1/2 cup nuts
To the other half of the mixture add the package of chocolate chips and the rest of the nuts
Stir each til smooth and all chips are melted
Pour Butterschotch mixture into greased 9 inch square pan
Pour Chocolate mixture over the top
Chill til firm
