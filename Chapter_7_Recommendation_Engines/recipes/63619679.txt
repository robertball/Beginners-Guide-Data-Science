Title:
Just Beachy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 shot orange Stoli vodka
1 shot Peach Schnapps
1 part Whipped liqueur
1 part Pineapple juice
2 parts cranberry juice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in shaker filled with ice.
Shake vigorously.
Pour into glass and garnish with a pineapple chunk.

Submitter: 
Dutchgirl
Description: 
This drink makes you feel like you're on a beach under an umbrella.
