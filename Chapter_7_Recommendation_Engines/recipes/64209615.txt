Title:
Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups granulated sugar
2 cups heavy cream or evaporated milk
1 1/2 cups corn syrup
1 cup butter
1 cup chopped nuts
Few grains of salt

Directions:
Boil all together except for the nuts and 1 cup of cream.
Boil for 30 minutes.
Add second cup of cream and boil to a firm ball stage (248Â° F.).
Add nuts and pour without stirring into a well buttered 9X13 pan.
When cool cut into squares.
