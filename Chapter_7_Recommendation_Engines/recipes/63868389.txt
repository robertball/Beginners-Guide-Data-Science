Title:
Crispy Little Tortilla Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 flour tortilla (Mission whole wheat soft taco size) (130c)
1 oz.(1/8 cup) shredded part skim mozzarella cheese (75c)
1-2 TBSP pizza sauce (20c)
6 Hormel turkey pepperoni (30c)

Directions:
Place sauce on pizza.  Top with cheese, pepperoni, and other vegetables if you like.  Bake in preheated 425 degree oven for about 8 to 10 minutes.

Description: 
This makes a thin crispy pizza.  It's really good!
