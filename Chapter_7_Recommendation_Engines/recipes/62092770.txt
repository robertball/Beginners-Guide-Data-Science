Title:
Grilled Venison Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 venison steaks
1/2 cup Heinz 57 sauce
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Season steaks with garlic salt and black pepper.
Mix 57 sauce and honey.
Spread over both sides of the steak.
Broil at 425 for about 20 minutes.
Turn once and brush with sauce again.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Simply seasoned steaks to die for. So deliciouse they will melt in your mouth. A combination of 57 sauce,honey,salt and pepper. Sounds to simple to be good, But believe me it is great.
