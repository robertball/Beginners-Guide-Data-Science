Title:
White Bean 'n' Ham Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15.5 ounce) cans great northern beans, rinsed and drained
1.5 medium carrots, diced
1/2 small onion, chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil (approx)
2 1/4 cups ham (or chicken) stock
8-12 oz cubed fully cooked ham
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mash the beans; set aside.
In a large saucepan, saute the carrots and onion in olive oil.
Stir in the stock, beans, ham, salt and pepper.
Cook over medium heat until heated through.
