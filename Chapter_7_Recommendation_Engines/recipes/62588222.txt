Title:
Ajlimojili sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 garlic cloves, peeled
10 sweet chili peppers, seeded
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
juice of 3 lemons
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Grind all the dry ingredients in a mortar. Add the lemon juice and oil and mix well.
