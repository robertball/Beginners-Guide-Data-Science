Title:
Pakistan's Firni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/876978.jpg
Ingredients:
Milk 3kg
Granulated sugar 200 g
Cardamom powder 1/2 teaspoon
Rice(powdered) 1/2 cup
Condensed Milk 1/2 can
Screw pine ,few drops
Sterling Silver leaves,to garnish
Almonds,to garnish

Directions:
Boil milk in a pot and cook till it changes colour to light pink.
Add rice and cook;keep stirring continuously otherwise it will form lumps.
Add cardamom powder and sugar to the milk.
When it starts getting thick,add condensed milk and screw pine.
Take Firni out in bowls and cool.
Garnish with almonds and silver leaves before serving.

Description: 
Beautiful Rice-pudding like dessert.Sweet and gives a smooth texture to you mouth after lunch or dinner.Its a family favorite now.
