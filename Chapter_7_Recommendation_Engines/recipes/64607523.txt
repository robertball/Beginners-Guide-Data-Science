Title:
Boston Cream Icebox Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
3 cups milk
1 (16 oz) package Graham Crackers
1 (8 oz) Cool Whip
2 packets vanilla instant pudding
Topping
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Line a 9 X 13 inch pan with aluminum foil or parchment paper
Completely cover the bottom with a layer of graham crackers, breaking the graham crackers into smaller pieces to completely fill in all gaps
In a large bowl, combine pudding packets, cool whip and milk, mix well
Pour half of mixture onto the graham crackers
Add another layer of graham crackers and top with the remaining half of the mixture
Add another layer of graham crackers for the top.
For the topping, combine the sugar, butter, chocolate chips, milk and vanilla in a sauce pan and place on medium heat.
Once all ingredients have melted and are fully incorporated, pour over the graham crackers
Cover tightly and freeze at least 4 hours or overnight
Slice, serve and enjoy
