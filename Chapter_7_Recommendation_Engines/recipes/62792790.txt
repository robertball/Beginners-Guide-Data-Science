Title:
Triple Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1/2 cup chopped green peppers
1/2 cup chopped red peppers
1 (15.5 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, drained
3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
-combine first 6 ingrediants in a large bowl
-whisk together vinegar, oil, pepper, juice, and salt
-pour over bean mixture, stirring to coat
-cover and chill

Submitter: 
Rachael_Marie
Description: 
This is a great bean salad to go with any meal.  It has great flavor, and will make you want to come back for more.
