Title:
Air Fryer Avocado Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado
1/4 cup ap flour
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg, beaten with 1 tsp water
1/2 cup panko
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat fryer to 200
In a shallow bowl mix the flour, pepper and salt
In another shallow beat the egg and water
Place the panko in another shallow bowl.
Peel and cut the avocado into 8 sections
Dredge the slice through the flour and shake off excess
Dip the slice into the egg and allow excess to drip off
Press the slice into the panko
Place on a plate and repeat with the other slices
Generously spray the slices and arrange them in the bowl of the fryer, sprayed side down.
Spray the tops of the slices.
Cook for 4 minutes, flip and cook for another 3 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Wonderfully crunchy on the outside and creamy on the inside.  Great with  garlicky aioli .
