Title:
Red Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lg dried new mexico chilies
2 garlic cloves
1/2 lg onion, minced
1-20 oz can tomatoe sauce
1 T oregano, dried
1 t cumin, dried
salt and pepper to taste
for Roux:
1/4 cup oil
1/8 cup flour
Pureed sauce

Directions:
Boil chilies and cloves in 2 cups of water and be sure to get both sides of chilis cooked.  Drain.  Pull chili stems out and if you get it just right the seeds will pull out with the stem.  Puree all ingredients together in a blender.
When making enchilidas / huevos rancheros you will need to make a roux.  In a medium saucepan heat oil then wisk the flour in to make a paste and add sauce stirring briskly till it is thickened.

Submitter: 
Vivian Chavez
Description: 
Use this sauce for a 8x8 pan of rolled enchiladas or saturday morning Huevos Rancheros.
