Title:
MA's So Satisfying Oven  Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Untrimmed beef brisket
2 teaspoons Liquid smoke
1/4 cup Your favorite brisket rub or combination of spices and seasonings
3 Tablespoons cooking oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Wash and dry brisket.
Combine remaining ingredients and massage into all sides of the brisket, concentrating on the fat cap especially.
Add additional seasoning if necessary cover well.
Place brisket, fat cap up, in a large roaster, uncovered, and into the hot oven for 20 minutes. Lower rack if necessary.
Cover roaster, lower heat to 300 degrees and roast on low for approximately 3 hours or until very tender.
Remove from oven/roaster, place onto cutting board and cover with foil. Let rest for at least 20 -30 minutes before cutting.
