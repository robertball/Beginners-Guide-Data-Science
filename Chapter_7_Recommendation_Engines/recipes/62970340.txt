Title:
Melted SnowMan Cookie. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/734452.jpg
Ingredients:
Sugar Cookie.
3/4 c Butter Softened
3/4 c Sugar
1 tsp Baking Powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1 tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla
2 c Flour
decorations
3 c icing sugar
1/4 c water
1/2 c choc chips
12 lg marshmallows
red , Yellow Food Coloring

Directions:
For the Cookie Base You can use any sugar cookie.
In a Your Mixer , Mix the butter for about 2 minutes until fluffy add sugar , baking powder, salt beat until combined . beat in egg, milk, and vanilla. beat in flour.  cover and chill for about 10 minutes. use tbsp scoop out a slightly bigger than a golf ball size hunk, then for into a cookie shape with your hands bake on 350 until edges are golden brown cool on wire rack.
Butter a plate and then put the marshmallows on it.microwave on high for about 10-15 seconds . squish them down a bit until the bottoms kin of gooo out. let sit.
mix icing sugar & Milk until a little bit thicker than a drizzle consistency. You can just play with the texture as you like it. Pour over top of the cookie over the edges as well.
Arms.
Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave for 30 seconds add to a Piping Bag with the smalles tip and decorate.
the nose and mouth are colored icing sugar and milk.
stick marshmallow head on the cookie and you are ready to decorate . Freestyle effect is way better/
TIP.
I also tried melted Marshmallows for the base for my son Who doesnt care for icing sugar. tasted great as well .
