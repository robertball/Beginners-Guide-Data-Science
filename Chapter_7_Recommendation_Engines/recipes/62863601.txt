Title:
Fancy Gourmet Hamburger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup red wine vinegar
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp salt divided
4 sprigs thyme
8 black peppercorns
1 bay leaf
1/8 tsp chili flakes
1 medium red onion sliced into rings
1 3/4 lb ground chuck
2 cups shredded lettuce
1/4 cup aioli or mayo
1 tbs chopped parsley
ketchup and mustard
4 toasted ciabatta rolls

Directions:
Pickle onions first by combining vinegar, water, sugar, 1/2 tsp salt, thyme, bay leaf, peppercorns and chili flakes in a saucepan and bring to a boil
Add onions, turn off heat and let rest for 20 minutes, drain
Preheat grill. Form 4 patties. Add 1/2 tsp salt. Place 1 cheese slice on each burger during last 2 minutes of cooking
Combine lettuce, aioli and parsley in a bowl to form a slaw
Spread mustard on top bun and ketchup on bottom bun
Grill the pickled onions.
Place burger on bun, cover with onions and slaw
