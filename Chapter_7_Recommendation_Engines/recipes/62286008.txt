Title:
Pan Seared Tilapia with tomato caper sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354543.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tilapia fillets
salt & pepper
2 Tbls. olive oil
2 medium tomatoes
2 Tbls. capers
2 1/2 Tbls. butter
1 1/2 Tbls. Worcestershire sauce
1 Tbls. lemon juice

Directions:
Seed and dice tomatoes. In a hot frying pan, heat 1/2 Tbls. of the butter. Add the diced tomatoes, capers, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Cook on low heat quickly for about 2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the remaining 2 Tbls. of butter and set aside. DO NOT REHEAT.
Salt and pepper the fillets. Heat a frying pan over medium heat; then add the oil. When oil is hot, add the fillets and brown on both sides, turning only once. This will take about 3 minutes per side. Remove to a plate and spoon the tomato sauce over the fillets.
