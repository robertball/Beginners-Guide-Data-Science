Title:
Verlys' Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pie crust shells
1 heaping teaspoon flour
1 heaping teaspoon sugar
Apples - peeled and sliced, enough to fill a pan
2/3-3/4 cup sugar (depends on the sweetness of the apples you're using)
2 tablespoon flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Stir together 1 teaspoon flour, 1 teaspoon sugar, and sprinkle on bottom of crust.
Fill pan with apples - a mix of apples gives the best flavor.
Mix remaining sugar, flour, and cinnamon, and pour over apples.
Dot with butter and lay top crust on.
Wet bottom crust edges, and seal together with top crust.  Cut small slits on top crust.
Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 325°F and bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pam Haugo
Description: 
This is my mom's recipe that she's been making for years. She uses a mix of apples that she grows in her apple orchard. It's always a hit!
