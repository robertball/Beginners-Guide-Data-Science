Title:
Butter Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. bag dried large lima beans
1 stick butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tbsp garlic powder
1/4 cup Bacos or bacon bits
5 cups water

Directions:
Simmer beans in the water for 1 hour
Place butter, salt, pepper, garlic powder, brown sugar and bacon bits in a medium baking dish.
Pour beans and water into baking dish and stir.
Bake for 2.5 hours at 325 degrees, stirring occasionally.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe has been in my family for years.  Sweet, buttery beans baked with bacon bits.
