Title:
Five color salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small red onion, chopped
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can corn, drained
1 can white hominy, drained
1 large red bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup or less salad oil
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp dried marjoram
1 tsp garlic powder
1 TBS sugar

Directions:
Chop onion and red bell pepper. Place in refrigeratable bowl. Drain and rinse black beans. Add to onion and bell pepper. Drain corn and hominy and add to mixture. In a small bowl mix together oil, vinegar, and dry ingredients. Whisk together well. Pour dressing over veggies. Put tight lid on bowl and shake until dressing is evenly distributed. Place in refrigerator 8 hrs or overnight to marinate. Keep chilled. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Stefani Ferguson
Description: 
Red onion, hominy, corn, black beans, and red bell pepper marinated in a sweet tangy dressing. Very addictive! Especially good for potluck meals and family gatherings.
