Title:
Peppered Red Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large green chilis
2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
1/2 cup corn oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
3 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
6 cups water
6 teaspoons beef bouillon

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, bringing to a boil. Simmer for 30 minutes. Blend with a hand blender.
