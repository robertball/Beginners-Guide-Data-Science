Title:
Chicken & Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For Chicken Stew:
2 Tbsp clarified butter
1 lb chicken boneless skinless thighs
4 slices bacon
1/2 half red onion (finely chopped)
2 stalks celery (chopped)
2 carrots (chopped)
5 new potatoes (chopped)
2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
1 can or 1/2 bag frozen sweet corn
1 cup chicken broth
1 can mushroom soup
1/4 tsp smoked paprika
1 cup 2% milk
salt & pepper to taste
For Dumplings:
1 cup Bisquick
1/3 cup milk

Directions:
1.  In large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and cook the chicken until just cooked through.  Remove the chicken and allow to cool. Shred or cut into small pieces and set aside.
2.  In a medium skillet, cook the bacon until browned & crispy; drain on paper towel, allow to cool, then crumble and set aside (reserve 1 Tbsp. bacon grease).
3.  Scrape the chicken drippings from skillet into stock pot or Dutch oven.  Cook onion, celery, carrots, potatoes & garlic over medium heat until onions are soft & transparent.
4.  Add broth, paprika, chicken, corn, soup, milk, reserved bacon grease and simmer for 20 mins.
5.  Prepare dumplings by mixing Bisquick & milk to sticky dough.  Drop by spoonful into stew.  Cook for 10 mins uncovered, then 10 mins covered.
6.  Serve with a garnish of crumbled bacon.
