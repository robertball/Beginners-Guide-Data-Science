Title:
Iced Strawberry Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982320.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups hot water
5 tablespoons NESTEA® Iced Tea Mix with Sugar and Lemon
1/4 cup strawberry preserves
1 cup ice cubes
2 slices lemon (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE water, Nestea and preserves in 1-quart pitcher. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours; strain before serving, if desired.
SERVE over ice; garnish with lemon slices.

Nestea®
Description: 
Good after school or on warm days.
