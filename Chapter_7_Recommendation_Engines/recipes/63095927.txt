Title:
Spicy Hot Chicken Legs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken drumsticks or thighs
1 (5 ounce) bottle hot red pepper sauce or your choice
1/4 cup butter, cubed
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon paprika
salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups blue cheese salad dressing

Directions:
mix spices rub on chicken pieces, marinade in bag overnight, Place the drumsticks in a slow cooker,  top with butter. Pour the hot sauce over the chicken, Cover, and cook on High for 3 hours, take out place on baking sheet cook on 400 for 30 min. put back in crock pot till ready to serve.  Serve chicken legs with blue cheese dressing on the side.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
This is just like spicy chicken wings, only less work.  Cooks up nicely in the slow cooker! A quick meal that is super easy and oh so yummy! For crispier chicken, bake in a 400 degrees F oven for the final 30 minutes.Modified from: Spicy Hot Chicken Legs
