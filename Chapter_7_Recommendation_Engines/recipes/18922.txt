Title:
Big Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656393.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup crushed ice
6 fluid ounces vodka
1 fluid ounce apple jack
1 dash Calvados

Directions:
In a mixing glass, combine crushed ice, vodka and applejack brandy. Stir and strain into 2 chilled cocktail glasses. Top with a splash of Calvados, garnish with a lady apple and serve.

Submitter: 
Leslie
Description: 
I discovered this recipe on the Upper West Side at a swanky bar. This will be the new 'in' drink!  A Lady apple makes a great garnish if you can find one.
