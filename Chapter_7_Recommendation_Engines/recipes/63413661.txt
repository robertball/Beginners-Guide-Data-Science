Title:
Zucchini Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded zucchini (do not peel zucchini)
1 cup Bisquick
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cup grated Parmesan
2 tbsp. chopped parsley
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
1/2 tsp. oregano
Dash of pepper
1 clove garlic - minced
1/4 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs, slightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Place zucchini in a strainer and allow to drain, then squeeze excess liquid out in a paper towel.
Combine all ingredients then spread in a greased 9x13 pan.
Bake about 20 minutes or until set.
