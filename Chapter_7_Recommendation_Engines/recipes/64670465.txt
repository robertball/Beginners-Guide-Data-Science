Title:
Slow Cooker Leg of Lamb with Garlic and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5 lb leg of lamb, bone in
10 garlic cloves
5 rosemary sprigs
10 thyme sprigs
3 bay leaves
salt
3 T olive oil
3/4 c low sodium beef stock
3/4 c sauvignon blanc

Directions:
rub lamb with 1-2 T olive oil
cut few holes in top of lamb
make paste of some of garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt
rub over lamb and into holes (full garlic cloves in holes)
put lamb in slow cooker and pour white wine, stock in bottom
add remaining garlic, thyme, rosemary
slow cooker on low for 10 or more hours
preheat oven to 400
remove from slow cooker, put in baking dish
rub with 1-2 T olive oil
bake 20 min until fat is browned, crispy
