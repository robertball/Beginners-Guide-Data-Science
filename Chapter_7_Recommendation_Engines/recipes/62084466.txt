Title:
Trishie's Black Forest Pavlova Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422498.jpg
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
8 oz caster sugar
pinch of salt
2 Tbs cocoa powder
pinch if cream of tartar
1 tsp white wine vinegar
2 tsp cornflour
4 drops vanilla essence
1 can cherry pie filling
1/2 pint whipped cream
1 chocolate bar grated

Directions:
Pre heat the oven to 180'C
In a clean DRY bowl, whisk egg whites until frothy, add salt and whisk until firm. Add sugar gradually, whisking all the time until stiff and glossy. Add the remaining ingredients and turn out onto a deep based pie dish (which has been lined with greased grease proof paper) making sure to mound up the sides.  Put into the oven and immediatly reduce heat to 150'C and bake for 1 hour. Turn off oven leave oven door ajar and leave pavlova to cool in the oven for approx 1 hour. When pavlova is cool remove greaseproof paper pour cherry pie finning into pavlova cover with cream and add grated chocolate to finish!
