Title:
Best Ever Meatloaf I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
13 pounds lean ground beef
8 2/3 cup tomato juice
6 1/2 cup rolled oats
8 1/2 eggs
2 1/3 cup chopped onion (dried onion)
2 tbl teaspoon mrs dash
2 tsp ground black pepper
3 cups diced green pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine meat, tomato juice, oats, egg, chopped onion, salt and pepper. Mix lightly but thoroughly.  Shape into 6 loaves.
Bake for 45 min, then put thin coating of ketchup on top and bake an additional 15 min or until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear.  Drain.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Jody Comerford
Description: 
Rolled oats, tomato juice, and onions combine with lean ground beef for an easily prepared meatloaf. It bakes in one hour.Modified from: Best Ever Meatloaf I
