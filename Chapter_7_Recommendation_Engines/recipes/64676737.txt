Title:
Asiago Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chopped onions
1 cup chopped celery
3 cups chopped carrots
6 tablespoons butter
4 cups chopped and peeled potatoes
1 cup white wine
3 cups chicken stock
2 cups half and half
3 cups Asiago cheese, grated
Salt/pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (more if you like)

Directions:
In large sauce pot, melt butter; saute onion, celery , carrots until tender
Add potatoes, wine and stock.  Add salt and pepper
Cover and cook for 20 minutes
Remove from heat and stir in cheese
With an immersion blender, blend soup until creamy or slightly chunky
Garnish with more cheese, if desired
