Title:
Yukon Gold Potatoes, mashed Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Yukon Gold Potatoes, 5 pound bag
salt
pepper
sour cream
milk
butter

Directions:
1. peel and cut up potatoes into cubes and put in pot to boil till tender
2. with potatoe masher, mash the potatoes up as much as you want.
3. add butter, salt and pepper and sour cream to personal taste,
4. add a little bit of milk to get desired creaminess and texture

Submitter: 
Erin Tucker
Description: 
Easy simple mashed potatoes that are creamy and mouth watering.
