Title:
Almond Cherry Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sheet puff pastry dough
1/2 cup dark sweet cherries in syrup, drained
1/3 cup cherry preserves
1/2 tbsp. almond paste, shredded
1/3 cup sliced almonds
Powdered sugar - optional

Directions:
Heat oven to 400.
Unfold puff pastry and cut into 9 squares.
Arrange squares on a baking sheet.
Lightly brush edges of squares with warm water.
In a bowl, combine cherries and preserves.
Divide almond paste and cherry mixture evenly among squares, placing in the center of the square.
Sprinkle with almonds.
Fold and roll up the edges of dough to create a ring.
Bake for 12 minutes or until light golden brown.
Dust with powdered sugar if desired.
