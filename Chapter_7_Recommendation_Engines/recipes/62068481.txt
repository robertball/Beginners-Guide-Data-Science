Title:
Mom's Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T margarine
1 med. onion chopped
1 small can sliced mushrooms
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk
2 8 oz. jars of kraft Old english cheese
or  (16 oz. vevetta)
2 10 oz. pkg. frozen broccoli- thawed
1 can of French's French Fried Onions

Directions:
1.  Saute' onion in margarine till onions are transparent.
2.  Add mushrooms, soup, milk and cheese.
Heat untill cheese melts in.
3.  Remove from heat and add broccoli.  Mix together.
4.  Put into deep casserole dish and bake for 35 min.
5.  Spread french fried onions on top and bake about 5 more min. untill onions are nicely browned.
6.  Serve!

Submitter: 
Laura Moody
Description: 
This is a holidy tradition at our house, it's great with ham or turkey.  Enjoy!
