Title:
Rotelle with Smoked Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups broccoli florets
10 oz. rotelle pasta
4 Tablespoons butter
4 Tablespoons olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 lb. smoked turkey, diced
2/3 cup roasted red bell pepper, diced
1 cup chicken broth
Grated parmesan or romano cheese to taste

Directions:
Cook broccoli in large pot of boiling water for 2-3 minutes.  Remove to ice water, drain, pat dry and set aside.  Cook rotelle in same pot of boiling water until al dente.  Drain and set aside.
In large skillet or wok, heat butter and olive oil over medium-low heat.  Add garlic and red pepper flakes and sauté 3 minutes.  Add turkey and sauté 4 minutes.  Add broccoli, pasta, roasted red peppers, and chicken broth.  Increase heat to med-high and stir until broth is absorbed.  Serve topped with grated cheese.
