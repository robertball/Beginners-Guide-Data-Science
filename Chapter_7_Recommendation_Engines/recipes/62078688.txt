Title:
Tilapia All-In-One Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 lb. tilapia fillets (any white fish can be substituted)
- 3 cups cooked rice
- 16 oz. frozen stir fry vegetables (broccoli, snow peas, carrots, water chestnuts, etc.), thawed
- 6 oz. shredded sharp cheddar cheese
- 6 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. chicken bouillon granules
- ½ tsp. pepper
- ½ tsp. paprika
- ¼ cup lemon juice

Directions:
Melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat.  Stir in chicken bouillon granules, lemon juice, pepper and paprika.
Lightly grease a 9”x13” baking dish.  Combine rice with vegetables, cheese and half of the butter sauce.  Spread in bottom of baking dish.  Place tilapia fillets in a single layer on top of rice mixture.  Pour remaining butter sauce over fillets.
Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
