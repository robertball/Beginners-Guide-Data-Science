Title:
Kahlua Peppermint Cocoa with Homemade Hot Cocoa Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089855.jpg
Ingredients:
Hot Cocoa Mix:
2 1/2 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup sugar
8 ounces semisweet or bittersweet chocolate, chopped
Kahlúa Peppermint Cocoa:
1/4 cup hot cocoa mix
3/4 cup milk
1 1/2 fluid ounces Kahlua Peppermint Mocha

Directions:
For the cocoa mix, stir cocoa powder, sugar, and chopped chocolate. Store in an airtight container.
To make cocoa, heat the milk and cocoa mix in a saucepan over low heat. Stir until chopped chocolate is melted, cocoa mix is dissolved, and milk is hot but not boiling. Pour into a mug and stir in Kahlua. Garnish with marshmallows, if desired.

Submitter: 
Kahlua®
Description: 
Make a big batch of hot cocoa mix then whenever you need a warming cup, just heat some milk and bring out the Kahlua Peppermint Mocha.
