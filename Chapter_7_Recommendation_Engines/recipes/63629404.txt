Title:
Ashley's Very Easy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of Ground Beef or Turkey
2 Packets of Chili Seasoning (Mild, Original or Hot)
1 Sweet Yellow Onion
2 Cans of Italian Style dice tomatoes
2 Large cans of Van Camp's Pork & Beans

Directions:
Brown ground beef, and drain.
Dice the onion into small pieces.
Combine Van Camp's Pork & Beans, cans of tomatoes, onion, meat and chili seasoning into a crock pot.
Cook on High for 2 hours or Low for 4 hours
Once hot and onions are transparent the chili is done.

Submitter: 
Ashley Rawls
Description: 
Quick and easy chili. Little preparation and is sure to be a crowd pleaser.
