Title:
Orzo Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole-wheat orzo
1 tsp sea salt
1 cup frozen baby peas or edamame
1 small red pepper diced
1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
1-2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
Fresh ground pepper

Directions:
1. Large pot to rolling boil, stir in orzo and salt - 5 to 6 minutes
2. Drain & transfer - add all remaining ingredients
