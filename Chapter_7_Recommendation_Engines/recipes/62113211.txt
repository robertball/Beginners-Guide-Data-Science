Title:
Simple Italian Wedding Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Cans Chicken Broth
3 Cans Stewed Tomatoes
1 Whole White Onion (diced)
2 lbs Italian Sausage (cut into 1" medallions)
2 lbs frozen Cheese Tortellini
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
Garlic Powder and Italian Seasoning to taste
Salt and Pepper to taste
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Combine Chicken Broth, Stewed Tomatoes, Onions, Garlic Powder, Italian Seasoning, Salt and Pepper in a large stock pot and bring to a boil
Brown the Italian Sausage with Olive Oil and add to the stock pot grease and all
Stir in Tortellini and cook for 10 minutes on medium heat
Serve with Parmesan Cheese
