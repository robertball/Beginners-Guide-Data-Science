Title:
Shrimp and Tortellini Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
36 fresh cheese tortellini
24 large cooked shrimp
12 cherry tomatoes
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 16 ounce container sour cream
1 packet Hidden Valley Ranch dressing and dip mix (packet)

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package directions.
Heat tbsp olive oil in skillet and add garlic, saute until tender.  Add cooked shrimp and heat through.  Add cooked tortellini to the skillet.  Mix and remove from heat.  When cooled, skewer alternating shrimp and tortellini on wooden skewers, ending with a cherry tomato (tortellini, shrimp, tortellini, shrimp, tortellini, tomato).
To make dip: Mix sour cream and ranch mix...
Serve skewers with dip.
