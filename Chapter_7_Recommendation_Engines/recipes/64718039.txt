Title:
Ceviche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ package (8 oz small shrimp), cooked, tall-off, peeled, deveined and thawed
½ cup diced cucumber
1/3 cup diced red onion
1 tomato, seeded and diced
1 small jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced (approx. 1 Tbsp)
3 Tbsp. fresh lime juice
1 Tbsp chopped cilantro
¼ tsp cumin
salt and pepper to taste
1 avocado, chopped
1 package tostados

Directions:
Prepare and combine all ingredients in a bowl; mix well to combine flavors
Refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
Serve cold with tortilla chips or tostados. Or serve with toppings as shrimp tacos.
