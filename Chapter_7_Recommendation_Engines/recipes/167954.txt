Title:
Island Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983195.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/4 cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 cup KRAFT Thousand Island Dressing
6 hamburger buns, split, toasted

Directions:
Preheat greased grill to medium heat. Mix meat, 2 tablespoons of the steak sauce, onion and garlic. Shape into 6 patties. Mix dressing and remaining 2 tablespoon. steak sauce; set aside.
Grill patties 7 to 9 minutes on each side or until cooked through (160 degrees F).
Spread bottom half of each bun with 1 tablespoon of the dressing mixture; cover with burger and top of bun.
