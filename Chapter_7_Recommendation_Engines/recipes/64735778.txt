Title:
fried chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart buttermilk
3-5 lbs Chicken pieces of some kind
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
mix buttermilk
place the chicken in buttermilk and refrigerate for the night
Preheat oven at 375
put flour in bowl and mix with meat rub
remove chicken from buttermilk and coat with flour mixture
spray pan with pam and place chicken on
Bake in oven for 45 minutes depending if on a bone
