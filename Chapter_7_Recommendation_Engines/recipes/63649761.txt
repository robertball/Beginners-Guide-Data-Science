Title:
Buffalo Style Chicken Sausage Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz pkg al fresco Buffalo Style chicken sausage, sliced into 1/2" pieces
4 8-10 inch Tortillas
4 tbsp Blue Cheese Dressing
20 Arugula leaves
1/2 cup Celery, Chopped into bite-sized pieces
18 Hot red pepper strips (optional)

Directions:
Grill sausages until internal temperature reaches 165&deg; and slice into &frac12;" pieces.
Lay out tortillas and cover with arugula leaves.
In a small bowl, mix sausage pieces, blue cheese and celery pieces together.
Spread mixture evenly over arugula leaves.
Place hot red pepper strip over mixture.
Roll tortilla tightly and slice diagonally across the middle.
