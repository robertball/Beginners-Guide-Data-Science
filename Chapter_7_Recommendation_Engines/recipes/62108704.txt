Title:
Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of frozen hash browns (regular size bag)
1/2 cup butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 medium onion, finely chopped
8 ounces Velveeta cheese - cut into chunks
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter in a large pan.
Stir in Velveeta cheese until melted.
Stir in soup, onion, salt, and pepper.
Add bag of frozen hash browns and mix well.
Transfer to a 9 X 13 inch baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees until golden brown - approximately 40 minutes.
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Linda B. II
Description: 
These cheesy potatoes go great with everything, every season!  I also bake them in the crockpot on low for 3 hours to take to potlucks and family gatherings. They disappear!
