Title:
Big Ray's Chicken Florentine Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5806726.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
3 cups shredded cooked chicken (or turkey)
1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 cup cottage cheese
1 cup spinach, chopped
1/4 cup Neufchatel cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup loosely packed minced fresh basil
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
2 Tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. Cook macaroni according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine chicken, soup, Swiss cheese, spinach, mayonnaise, basil, garlic powder, thyme, Neufchatel cheese, cottage cheese and pepper.
Drain macaroni; gently stir into chicken mixture. Transfer to an ungreased 13" x 9" baking dish. Toss bread crumbs and butter; sprinkle over casserole.
Bake, uncovered, 40-45 minutes or until bubbly

Description: 
Creamy and comforting, this chicken and spinach bake is sure to be a hit at dinnertime and a great way to use up left over chicken. The toasty bread crumb topping delivers a bit of a crunch
