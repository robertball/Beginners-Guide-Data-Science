Title:
Wow Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 lb ground pork
1/2 lb cooked and crumbled bacon
2 eggs
1 small can of mushrooms, drained
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 lb swiss cheese, shredded
Options:
chopped onions

Directions:
Oven at 350 degrees. Mix pork and beef together. Add eggs, salt and pepper and mushrooms, mix well. Flatten the meatloaf and in the center place 3/4 of the bacon and 1/2 of the swiss cheese. Roll to form into a loaf and bake for 1/2 hour or until done. Add the remaining cheese and bacon on top and bake for 5 more minutes.
Options:
Add chopped onions with the salt, peppers and mushrooms, if desired.

Submitter: 
Seahorsie
Description: 
It's a very tasty meatloaf with alot of flavors. This was actually an experiment that turned out great and my family demands it. This is a large recipe, I have 5 kids.
