Title:
Crepe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1 cup almond milk
2/3 cup ground oats
1 pinch salt
1 1/2 tsp coconut oil

Directions:
In a blender, combine egg whites, almond milk, oats, salt, and coconut oil. Process until smooth, cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
Heat skillet over medium heat and spray with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup of batter into skillet, tilting to completely coat the surface. Cook for 2-5 minutes, turning once, until golden. Repeat with remaining batter.

Top with Nutella and sliced banana, then roll.
