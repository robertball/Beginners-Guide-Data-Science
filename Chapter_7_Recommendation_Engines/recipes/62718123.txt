Title:
Buffalo Ranch Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/618691.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1/4 cup ranch salad dressing
1-2 tsp. buffalo hot sauce
ground cayenne pepper for garnish
1 stalk of celery for garnish

Directions:
Hard-boil the eggs; cut in half lengthwise.  Remove cooked yolks and put them in a bowl.  Add salad dressing and hot sauce to taste and mix until smooth.  Put egg yolk mixture in pastry bag or a baggie with a corner cut out; pipe yolk into egg whites.  Sprinkle each egg half with cayenne pepper.  Cut celery into tiny stalks and add one to the top of each egg.

Submitter: 
flutewoman3
Description: 
My family loves deviled eggs, so I decided to make a little twist on our regular recipe.  It's inspired by the flavors of buffalo chicken.  It's spicy, but not too spicy for my young kids, and it's easy to change the level of spiciness to suit your taste.
