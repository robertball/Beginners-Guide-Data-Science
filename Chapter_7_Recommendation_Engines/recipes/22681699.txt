Title:
Vegetarian Summer Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pkg. chicken flavored ramen noodle soup mix
1 medium onion, diced
1 c. green pepper
1 c. broccoli, small pieces
1 c. black olives, sliced
8 oz. monterey jack cheese, cubed
8 oz. FF Zesty Italian Salad dressing'

Directions:
Cook noodles according to directions. Drain and rinse with cold water. Mix in the chicken flavor packets and all other ingredients.

Submitter: 
JulezinWI
Description: 
This is a great salad for summer parties since it can sit out a little longer without refrigeration. There is no mayo and has a lighter flavor.
