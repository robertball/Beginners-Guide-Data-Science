Title:
Cincinnati Sitwell's Big Banana Blowout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole banana
1 scoop Edy's espresso chip ice cream (or anything similiar)
espresso shots,cold (2 I think)
brewed coffee, cold
chocolate syrup
Redi Whip

Directions:
combine all ingredients in a blender, and pulse until desired consistency (for me it is smooth, thick, and chips at the bottom. LOL

Description: 
I have to plan on this because it is most certainly not calorie friendly. but if you are ever in Cincinnati, Ohio and can get to Sitwell's in Clifton, this is WELL worth the wait.
