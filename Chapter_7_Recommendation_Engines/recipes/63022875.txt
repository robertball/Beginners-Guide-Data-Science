Title:
Simple Mashed Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups mashed sweet potatoes mashed with milk
1/2 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
placed mashed sweet potatoes in well-greasd baking dish. Pour thick maple syrup that has been boiled with butter over mashed potatoes. Bake at 350F until top begins to carmelize.
