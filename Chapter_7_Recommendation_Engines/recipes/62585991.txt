Title:
Easy Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/551197.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. 80/20 ground beef*
1 lb. 93% lean ground turkey*
2 cans Diced Tomatoes (28 oz.)
2 (12 oz.) bags frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 chopped onion (med.)
3 tsp. (heaping) "Better Than Bullion" Beef Base
1 1/2 c. water
1 1/2 tsp. Sea Salt
1 1/2 tsp. Lawreys Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp. Fine Ground Black Pepper
1/2 tsp. Basil

Directions:
Chop onion. Brown both turkey & ground beef on medium heat and add chopped onion.  Drain fat (if desired).  Once brown, add diced tomatoes, water and all spices. Stir well. Add "Better Than Bullion" Beef base. Stir again. Add two bags of frozen, mixed vegetables. Stir well and bring to boil.
Once soup is boiling, reduce heat to low for 20-30 minutes stirring occasionally. Adjust spices to taste!
Serve with cornbread or Ritz Crackers!

Vegetable beef soup with tomatoes that's quick, easy and tasty!
