Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2474760.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12-oz package chocolate chips
1 10-oz. bag miniature marshmallows
1/2 cup butter
1 cup smooth peanut butter (whipped)
Non-stick cooking spray

Directions:
Spray 13x9 pan generously with non-stick cooking spray.
Melt butter in a saucepan.
Place marshmallows in large bowl.
Add chocolate chips into saucepan then heat and stir over medium-low heat until chips are completely melted and mixture is smooth.  Fold in peanut butter into mixture.
Pour mixture over marshmallows and gently stir until evenly mixed and marshmallows are coated.
Spread mixture into prepared pan then refrigerate overnight.
Cut into desired sizes/shapes and serve with cold glass of milk!
