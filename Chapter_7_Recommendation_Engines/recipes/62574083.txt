Title:
East Sweet and Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1/2 pound lean ground beef
1/2 pound lean ground turkey
1/2 c milk
1 large egg
2 T minced onion
1/2 c quick oats
2 T minced parsley
1/2 t salt
1/3 t pepper
SAUCE
1/3 c ketchup
1/4 c cider vinegar
1/4 Splenda
2 t Worcestershire sauce
2/3 cup water
1 T plus 1 t cornstarch

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Make meatballs and place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes.
Place sauce ingredients in saucepan and simmer over medium.
