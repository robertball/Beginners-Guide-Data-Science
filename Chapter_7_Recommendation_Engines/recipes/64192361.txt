Title:
Black Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1540913.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup black rice
3 cups of water
1 1/2 cup of coconut cream
1/2  cup of sugar
1 tsp vanilla (optional)

Directions:
Cook the rice for 45 min.
Add 1 cup of coconut cream and the sugar.
Cook another half an hour.
Serve with more coconut cream or add fruit.
