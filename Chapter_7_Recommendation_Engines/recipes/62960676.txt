Title:
Italian Herb Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/730354.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Slices Of Italian Herb Bread
3 Slices Of Genoa Salami
3 Slices of Hard Salami
3 slices of capicola
1 Tablespoon Of Olive Oil
1 Tablespoon Of white vinegar
2 slices of Provolone cheese
3 slices of tomatoes
3 slices of onions Optional (sliced very thin)
2 oz of shredded lettuce
1/4 teaspoon of kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon of salt pepper
1/4 teaspoon of Oregano

Directions:
Take the bread and drizzle some olive oil on the insides of the bread.
Layer the meats, Provolone cheese, tomatoes, and lettuce on the bread.
grind fresh black pepper, salt, and Oregano over the lettuce, and tomatoes. Drizzle some olive oil over the lettuce. then drizzle white vinegar over the lettuce.
Serve

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
When I was 17 and 18 years old I worked in a store like a 7/11, It was called The Parkway Market At Exit 25 On The Garden State Parkway, and we made hoagies all day long. the most popular was the Italian Hoagie, this sandwich is a take off of the Italian Hoagie. I made the bread from my version of  Italian herb bread.
