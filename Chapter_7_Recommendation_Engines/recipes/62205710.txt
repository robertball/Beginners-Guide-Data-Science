Title:
Cream of Asparagus Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/306428.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. fresh asparagus spears
49 1/2 oz.can of chicken broth
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon season all
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon creole seasoning
1 tablespoon parsley flakes
1 stick butter
1 (12oz) evaporated milk
1 package (1.25oz) hollandaise sauce mix
1 (16oz) jar creamy alfredo pasta sauce

Directions:
wash asparagus
boil in water until tender (drain)
combine asparagus and next 8 ingredients in large pot
in small sauce pan melt butter and stir in packet of hollandaise sauce mix until smooth, slowly add evaporated milk, continue to stir until thickened, add this to pot of soup, add alfredo sauce, cook on low for 30 mins. Remove from heat and puree, drain through mesh discard pulp

Submitter: 
Lori Simolke
Description: 
This soup is delicious hot or chilled served with a salad
