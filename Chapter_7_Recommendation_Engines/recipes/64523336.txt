Title:
Crista's Hamburger Soup with Fresh Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
2 garlic cloves, chopped finely
1/8-1/4 cup caramelized onion, chopped
6 cups beef broth
2 large carrots, diced
2 celery stalks, diced
2 tomatoes, diced
4 medium red potatoes, peeled and diced
1/4 large head cabbage, sliced thinly off the head
1 ear corn, grilled, and cut off the cob
1/2-1 Tbs fresh thyme or to taste
1 tsp fresh oregano or to taste
1 Tbs fresh parsley or to taste
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown hamburger meat in soup pot or dutch oven, add in garlic and caramelized onions when 50-75% browned. Add remaining ingredients. Cover and cook over medium low until carrots and potatoes are tender. You can let it simmer for an  hour to blend the flavors.

Submitter: 
Crikkitt
Description: 
A delicious hamburger soup using natural ingredients and lots of fresh vegetables. Perfect for those days when you are craving comfort food. Serve with a fresh green salad and cheddar garlic biscuits and you have a wonderful meal.
