Title:
Cheeseburger Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground beef
1 can of cheddar cheese soup
1 can of tomato soup
1 1/2 c water
2 cups of uncoooked medium shell pasta

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown beef; drain. Add soups, water and pasta. Bring to a boil. Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes or until the pasta is tender. Stir often.
