Title:
Super Rich Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
4 tablespoons butter
1 8oz package Shredded Extra Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 8oz  package Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
4 oz Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
4 oz Shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/2 Cup Cream, Half and Half, or evaporated milk
1/4 cup mild or medium shredded cheddar cheese
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the oil and the pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain well and return to cooking pot.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Toss cooked macaroni with 2 tablespoons butter.
Meanwhile, mix in a saucepan, the remaining 2 tablespoons butter, extra sharp cheddar, Monterey jack, mild cheddar, and mozzarella. Heat on medium heat, stirring constantly, until combined. Mixture will be very thick. Stir in cream.
Remove from heat, and let cheese mixture cool for two minutes. Beat in eggs.
Spray 2.5 qt cooking dish with non-stick spray. Transfer macaroni to dish. Stir in cheese mixture, salt, and pepper. Top casserole with Â½ cup shredded cheddar cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes or until hot and bubbling around the edges; serve.
