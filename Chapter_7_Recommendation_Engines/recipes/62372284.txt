Title:
Orange Juice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
4 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup orange juice
1 T. orange zest
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup orange juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a large bundt pan.
Combine the cake mix, pudding mix, water, oil, zest, and eggs together.  Mix with an electric mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes.  Pour batter into bundt pan.
Bake for 60 minutes, or until knife inserted in cake comes out clean.
Combine the butter or margarine, sugar, and orange juice in a saucepan.  Boil this mixture for about 2 minutes.  While still warm, poke holes in the top of the cake with a fork.  Pour orange juice mixture over cake.  When the cake is saturated place it on a plate, and dust top with confectioners' sugar.

This cake is similar to a rum cake, but you substitute orange juice for rum.Modified from: Orange Juice Cake
