Title:
Apple Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chopped cabbage
1 unpeeled red apple, cored and chopped
1 unpeeled Granny Smith apple, cored and chopped and sprinked w/1 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 carrot, grated
1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
2 green onions, finely chopped
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup brown sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cabbage, red apple, green apple, carrot, red bell pepper, and green onions. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise and brown sugar. Pour dressing over salad.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
This is our favorite cole slaw recipe, a yummy combo of fruit and veggies in a sweet dressing.Modified from: Easy Apple Coleslaw
