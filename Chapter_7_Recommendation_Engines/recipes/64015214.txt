Title:
Powdered Sugar Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
55 grams powdered sugar
1 tablespoon1 milk
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon or cocoa (optional)

Directions:
Sift powdered sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl.
Add milk and stir.
Adjust for consistency.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Versatile icing/glaze for sugar cookies, donut muffins or pound cake.
