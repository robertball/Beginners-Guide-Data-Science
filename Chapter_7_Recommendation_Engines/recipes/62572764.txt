Title:
Deep Dish Ontario Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. brown sugar (or 1/2 c. brown sugar Splenda)
1 1/2 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. vanilla
3 c. chopped apples
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg slightly beaten
1 c. sour cream
Crumble topping:
1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. brown sugar (or 1/3 c. brown sugar Splenda)
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
3 Tbsp. chilled butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450F.
Combine sugar, flour and salt.
Mix with egg, sour cream and vanilla.
Add apples and combine thoroughly.
Turn into unbaked pie shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.
While baking make crumble topping by combining flour, sugar, cinnamon and nutmet.  Cut in butter until clumped together.
Remove pie from oven and reduce temperature to 350F.
Sprinkle crumble topping on pie and return to oven.
Bake for 30 more minutes until apples at center are tender.

Submitter:

Description: 
A family favorite, this pie is a staple at all our gatherings!  It's creamy apple center and crunchy sugar topping will thrill your tasebuds. We've adapted it to be diabetic friendly.
