Title:
Healthier Homemade Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281813.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) package Smart Taste macaroni, or whole wheat macaroni
2 cups shredded low-fat Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3 cups low-fat (1%) milk
4 tablespoons butter, divided
2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 cup whole wheat bread crumbs
1 pinch paprika

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to the package directions.  Drain.
In a saucepan, melt 2 tablespoons butter over medium heat.  Stir in enough flour to make a roux.  Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly.  Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick.  Put macaroni in large casserole dish, and pour sauce over macaroni.  Stir well.
Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat.  Add breadcrumbs and brown.  Spread over the macaroni and cheese to cover.  Sprinkle with a little paprika.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Serve.

Submitter: 
MakeItHealthy
Description: 
To make this rich mac and cheese recipe healthier we use whole wheat bread crumbs, lowfat milk and cheese, Ronzoni Smart Taste noodles, and reduced the amount of butter.  Serve with a salad for a healthy meatless dinner.Modified from: Homemade Mac and Cheese
