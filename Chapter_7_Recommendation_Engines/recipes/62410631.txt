Title:
Jambo Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/437412.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. olive oil, divided
1/2 onion, diced
1 cup matchstick carrots, diced fine
1 small green pepper, diced
16 oz. can chick peas, drained
1 tbsp. turmeric
1 tbsp. curry powder
1 1/2 tsp. paprika
2 cloves of garlic, crushed
2 cups cooked pasta
1/2 cup frozen peas
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat 1 tbsp. olive oil in a saute pan over medium heat.
Add garlic and saute until cooked, about 3 minutes.
Add carrots, onion, and green pepper.  Saute additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add turmeric, paprika, and curry powder.  Stir.
Add chickpeas and cook for 2 minutes.
Add pasta and 2 tbsp olive oil.  Stir to coat everything well.
Add frozen peas, stir.  Cook additional 2 minutes. Serve.

Submitter: 
Carolyn Chieffe Perrine
Description: 
Influenced by a complex layering of African spices, this pasta dish is an easy to follow and super fast recipe.  Quick enough for weekdays, but exotic enough for company.
