Title:
Mom's Turkey (without stuffing), Turkey Stock and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 bay leaves
1/2 salt and pepper
herbs - savory, thyme, or sage
carrots and celery cut in large chunks
Turkey Stock:
1 each turkey neck, gizzard and heart
1 stalk celery with leaves chopped
2 whole cloves
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp salt and peppercorns
Gravy:
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp peper
6 cups turkey stock

Directions:
Turkey:
Don't forget to remove wrapping, giblets and neck.   You must remove stuff from both cavities of the turkey.
Pat dry with a paper towel inside and out.
Inset 2 bay leaves in body cavity and 1 in neck cavity.
Sprinkle cavities with salt, pepper and herbs.
Skewer cavities closed.  Tie legs together with kitchen string and tuck wings under  back.  Place on rack of roasting pan OR lay chunks of carrots and celery in the bottom of the pan, breast side up and cook at 325F (160C) until thermometer reads 185F (85C).
Turkey Stock
Chop turkey neck in 5-6 pieces.
Cut gizzard and heart in half.
Add to 8 cups of cold water and bring to a boil.  Sim off foam.
Add the remainder of turkey stock ingredients.  Cover and simmer over low heat for 2 hours.  Strain.  Let cool.
Gravy:
Skim fat from pan juices in turkey roasting pan.  Whisk in flour, salt and peper.  Cook aver medium heat while scraping pan for about 1 minute.
Gradually whick in stock and bring to boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, stirring, unitl thickened, about 5 minutes.  Strain through fine sieve.

Description: 
When possible, I buy fresh turkey.  I find it much juicier than frozen turkey. 
Turkey stock can be prepared in advance (up to 2 days before.
