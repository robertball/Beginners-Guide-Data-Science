Title:
Holiday Morning French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter (1 stick)
3 tsp cinnamon
3 tart apples, thinly sliced
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 loaf italian bread cut into 1" (enough for 9x13" pan)
6 eggs
1 1/2 cup whole milk
1 tbsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix the brown sugar, butter, and 1 tsp cinnamon in 9x13" dish.
Add the sliced apples and dried cranberries to the pan. Toss to coat well, spreading the apple mixture evenly.
Arrange the slices of bread on top of the apples.
Beat the eggs, milk, vanilla, and remaining 2 tsp of cinnamon in medium bowl until well blended.
Pour egg mixture over bread and soak completely.
Cover and refridgerate for 4-24 hours.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees and bake covered in aluminum foil for 40 minutes, uncover and bake for 5 minutes more.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is always a big hit with our family. The combination of apples along with the cinnamon and brown sugar gives it a slight apple pie taste.. and the cranberries give it the right amount of sweetness. (Kid-friendly, too!!) Enjoy!! :)
