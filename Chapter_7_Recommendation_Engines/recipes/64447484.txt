Title:
Rick Gallop GI Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/3 c fresh whole wheat bread crumbs
1/4 c each wheat bran and chopped fresh Italian parsley
1 clove minced garlic
1/4 tsp each salt and black pepper
12 oz lean ground turkey or chicken
2 c low fat chunky vegetable pasta sauce
1 c cooked chickpeas
1/2 diced green bell pepper
6 oz whole wheat spaghetti

Directions:
-in large bowl, stir together egg, breadcrumbs, bran, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper. mix in ground meat using hands until well combined. roll mixture into 1" round meatballs and place on aluminum foil lined baking sheet. bake 350 for about 12 minutes or until no longer pink in the middle.
-in large saucepan over medium heat bring pasta sauce, chickpeas and bell peppers to simmer. add meatballs and simmer 15 minutes.
-meanwhile, in large pot of boiling salted water cook pasta for about 10 minutes or until al dente. remove meatballs to small serving bowl. drain pasta and add to pasta sauce. toss to coat well. serve with meatballs.
