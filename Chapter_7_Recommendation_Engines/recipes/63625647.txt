Title:
Spinach & Feta Chicken Sausage Fettuccini Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz al fresco® Spinach & Feta Chicken Sausage, fully cooked
1 15 oz Classico® Light Creamy Alfredo Sauce
8 oz Pasta, fettuccine
2 Tbsp Olive oil, extra virgin
1 Garlic clove, fresh, finely minced
1/4 Cup Onion, fresh, chopped
1 Tbsp Rosemary,fresh, finely chopped
1/2 Cup Sundried tomatoes, sliced julienne
1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese, shredded
1/2 Cup Feta cheese, crumbled
9 oz Spinach, baby, fresh
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Cut the al fresco Spinach & Feta Sausages on the bias &frac14;" thick.
Cook the fettuccini according to package directions. Drain and reserve &frac14; cup of pasta water. Keep warm.
Coat a large skillet with cooking spray and heat over high heat. Add the olive oil & saut&eacute; the garlic and onion for 2 minutes.
Add the sliced sausage pieces and saut&eacute; for 3 - 4 minutes. Fold in the fettuccini, rosemary, sundried tomatoes and cheeses. Add the light creamy Alfredo sauce and reserved pasta water. Simmer over medium heat for 8 - 10 minutes or until well heated.
Toss in the baby spinach at the end, and stir until all the ingredients are incorporated together (about 1 minute). Remove from heat and serve immediately.
