Title:
The Perfect Peppermint Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz Milk chocolate chips
12oz White chocolate chips
¼ tsp peppermint extract
About 8 candy canes

Directions:
Line cookie sheet with tinfoil
Melt Milk Chocolate: Melt in Double boiler on MEDIUM LOW/LOW heat until smooth OR Microwave Milk Chocolate: 3 mins 15 sec on 50% power and stir until smooth
Pour into cookie sheet and smooth out to even layer
Put in fridge and leave in for about 30 mins until hard.
Meanwhile, unwrap and crush candy canes in double layered baggy, sealed tightly.  Do not crush completely and leave some larger chunks for topping.
Use a colander to sift larger pieces out and set those separately from the finer crushed candy.
Melt White Chocolate: Melt in Double boiler LOW heat OR Microwave: 20 second increments on 50% power, stirring between (about 4 increments) until smooth
Add ¼ tsp peppermint extract, stir in
Add finer crushed candy cane, stir in
Pour into cookie sheet and spread evenly over hardened milk chocolate.
Top with larger candy cane pieces, press in softly.
Refrigerate overnight
Break into pieces and serve or put into goodie bags =D
