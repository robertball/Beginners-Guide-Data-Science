Title:
Turkey Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium turkey thighs, boned, skinned and cut into 1" pieces
2 small white or red potatoes, cubed
1-3/4 cups fat free reduced sodium chicken broth
1-1/2 cups frozencorn
1 cup water
1 can (8oz) no salt added sauce
1/4 cup tomato paste
2 TBS. dijon mustard
1 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1/2 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except parsley in slow cooker. Cover and cook on low 9-10 hours. Stir in parsley; serve.
