Title:
Luxurious London Broil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
London Broil (scored with knife on both sides)
4 Tbl spoon Balsamic Vinegar
4 Tbl spoon Olive Oil
2 Tbl spoon Teriyaki
1 Tsp spoon Italian Seasoning
1 Tsp spoon Lime Juice
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Score London Broil with a sharp knife. Mix remaining ingredients in a food storage container with lid large enough to hold London Broil. Marinate overnight (more is better) turning over once or twice. Grill on Medium (I prefer cast iron griddle) for 4-6 minutes per side for Medium Rare.

Submitter: 
Holleta
Description: 
This will make some of the most juicy and delicious London Broil to be found!
