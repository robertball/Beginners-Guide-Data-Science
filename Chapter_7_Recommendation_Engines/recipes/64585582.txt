Title:
Shaved Radish and Pine Nut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 Tbsp fresh orange juice
1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
3 cups very thinly sliced radishes
1 cup fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves
5 tsp pine nuts, toasted

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in large bowl, stirring with a whisk. Add radishes, parsley, and pine nuts to bowl; toss to combine.
Cals: 107; Fat 9.4g (sat 1.2g); Pro 2g; Fiber 2g; Sugars 3g; Iron 2mg; Sodium 16.3mg

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Crisp, peppery radishes take center stage in this salad, where they're brightened with orange juice and vinegar.
