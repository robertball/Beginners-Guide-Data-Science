Title:
rice-mushroom fritters with tzatziki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg rice pilaf mix
16-20 oz fresh sliced white mushrooms
2 large onions, chopped
4 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 cup grated swiss cheese
salt and pepper to taste
oil or butter for frying

Directions:
1. Prepare rice pilaf according to package directions
2. Meanwhile, saute onions over med. heat in some oil until tender, about 7 minutes. 3. Add mushrooms, saute for another 7 minutes until fully cooked through. Remove from heat.
3. In a large bowl, combine eggs, milk, rice pilaf, onion, mushrooms, salt and pepper, and swiss cheese. Mix well.
4. Heat butter or oil in a saucepan, and  form small fritters by spooning mixture onto pan.
5. Brown each fritter on both sides, remove from pan, place on absorbent paper.
6. Serve with tzatziki.

Submitter: 
shaindy
Description: 
I made this recipe myself when i found myself faced with too many leftover mushrooms and rice. Crispy, chewy, they are the perfect accompaniment to any mediteranean meal. Serve with tzatziki sauce.
