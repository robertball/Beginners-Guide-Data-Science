Title:
Bill's Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can lima beans
1 can butter beans
1 can dark red kidney beans
1 "larger" can of Campbells beans
1 can of black beans
1 cup catsup
1/2 lb of bacon cut into pieces
1 cup of brown sugar packed
1 tablespoon of dijon/country mustard
2 tablespoons of white vinegar
1 large onion chopped
2 in pieces of additional bacon for top if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix ALL beans With juice in large casserole. Add in  1/2 lb of bacon cut into little pieces and the chopped onion.
Mix together catsup, brown sugar, mustard & vinegar. Add to beans and bacon. Place little bacon strips on top. Bake at 350 degrees until not runny - about one hour. Better next day if you can wait.
