Title:
Beef Chili - No Bean Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 lbs stew meat or chopped chuck roast
canola oil
salt
pepper
2 medium chopped onions
jalapeno pepper
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
heaping tablespoon garlic
32 ounce can chopped tomatoes. Juice strained and set aside.

Directions:
Season meat with salt/pepper and brown in oil. Meanwhile, chop onions and jalapeno and place into crockpot. After meat is browned, transfer to crock pot. Use reserved tomato juice to deglaze skillet used for browning meat. Scrap all meat particles from pan and pour tomato/meat drippings into crockpot. Cook on high for 6 hours.
