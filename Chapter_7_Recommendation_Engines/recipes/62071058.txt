Title:
Best Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Flour Tortilla Shells
6 Slices American Cheese
6 Eggs
1/4 cup Chopped Onion
1/4 cup Diced Tomatoes
1 teaspoon Green Chili's
Salsa (optional)
Sour Cream (optional)

Directions:
Heat skillet on medium heat.  Add onion, tomatoes & green chili's.  Cook until onions are transparent.  In separate skillet, beat eggs & cook.  Add vegetable mix to eggs & stir together.  Place 1 piece of cheese in center of each tortilla & fill each tortilla with egg mixture.  Put in microwave & heat for 15 seconds to melt cheese slightly.  Serve with salsa or sour cream.
