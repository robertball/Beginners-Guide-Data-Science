Title:
MONKEY BREAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4(12-oz) cans Pillsbury® Grands!® Jr. Golden Layers® refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup whipping (heavy) cream
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°F. Spray 12-cup star-shaped fluted tube cake pan with cooking spray. Separate biscuit dough into 10 biscuits; cut each biscuit into fourths. Layer biscuits in pan.
Mix remaining ingredients; pour over biscuits.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until golden brown. Immediately turn pan upside down onto heatproof plate; let pan remain over biscuits 5 minutes. Serve warm.
