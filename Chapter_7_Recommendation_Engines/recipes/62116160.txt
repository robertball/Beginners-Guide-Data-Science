Title:
Monster Munch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz almond bark, divided
2 cups pretzel sticks
2 cups graham cereal
1/2cup salted peanuts
1 cup  m&ms;
any color food coloring
colored sprinkles

Directions:
Place 2 squares of almond bark in double broiler to melt.  Place pretzel sticks in large bowl.  Add melted almond bark and stir until coated.  Spread out on to waxed paper, seperating pieces; let cool.  Melt remaining almond bark;  add food coloring.  Place cereal in large bowl.  Add half of the bark and stir until coated.  Add m&ms,peanuts and remaining bark; stir until coated.  Stir in pretzel sticks.  Break into small clusters and spread out on waxed paper. Immediately sprinkle the sprinkles; let set.  AND ENJOY!

Submitter: 
ChristineD
Description: 
A wonderful sweet snack.  I make this snack for all holidays.  Change the color of the candies and food coloring for any occassion.
