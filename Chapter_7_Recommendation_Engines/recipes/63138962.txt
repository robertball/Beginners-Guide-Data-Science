Title:
faux carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package spaghetti or linguine pasta
2 teaspoons olive oil
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 package turkey bacon...may sub regular bacon
Grated or Shredded Parmesan to taste
Red Pepper Flakes to taste....more is better
Minced Garlic to taste
Salt and Pepper to taste....heavy on the pepper
2 teaspoons thyme
2 teaspoons oregano

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente
While water is heating for pasta, cook bacon up in a cast iron skillet for best taste.  Drain bacon on paper towels when finished....then tear or cut into small pieces before setting aside
Drain pasta once desired tenderness is achieved.  Put pasta back in pot and add the olive oil and margarine.  Mix well.  Add everything else including the bacon and season according to your own taste.
Mix thoroughly.  Add the Parmesan last.
Serve immediately:)

Submitter: 
Pamela Cressey Gileno
Description: 
It's got the flavor of a carbonara, but with a lot more zip thanks to the addition of red pepper flakes.  This dish is super easy and pretty quick.  It's something I sort of made up off the cuff
