Title:
Spagheti Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS:
1LBS ground beef/turkey
1/2 TSP garlic
2 TBSP bread crumbs
1/4 TSP Oregano
1/4 TSP Thyme
1/4 TSP Parsley
1/4 TSP Cayenne pepper
1 onion chopped
1 egg
2 TBSP Romano cheese
salt/pepper to taste
SAUCE:
1 LG can diced tomatoes
1 LG can tomato sauce
2 TBSP Romano Cheese
1 LG can tomato paste
1/2-3/4 can water (red wine is an excellent substitute)
salt/pepper/garlic/parsley/thyme/cayenne pepper/ and oregano to taste.

Directions:
FOR MEATBALLS:Mix ingredients, with hands, in a bowl. Form meat mixture into balls with hands. Brown in skillet and place in crock pot.
FOR SAUCE: Pour all cans and sauce ingredients into crockpot on top of meatballs. Let cook all day on low (at least 5-6 hours). Serve over cooked spaghetti noodles, and enjoy.
