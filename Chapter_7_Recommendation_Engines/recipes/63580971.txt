Title:
Hot Pepper Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
42 Hot Peppers (1/2 without seeds)
1 Tbl Salt
1 Pint Yellow Mustard
1 1/2 Cups Water
1 Cup Flour
1 Quart Vinegar
6 Cups Sugar

Directions:
Grind peppers, add mustard, vinegar, sugar, salt. Bring to a boil. Make a paste with the flour and water. Add to boiling mixture. Cook for 5 minutes. Pour into pint jars and seal. Water bath for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Brad Miller
Description: 
This is great on burgers and other places you would use a mustard sauce.
