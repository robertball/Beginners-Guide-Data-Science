Title:
Cranberry-Lime Margarita Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983842.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups water
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen cranberry juice cocktail
1/2 cup fresh lime juice
1/4 cup Equal® Spoonful*
2 cups ice cubes
1 cup diet ginger ale or tequila
1 lime, sliced

Directions:
Combine water, cranberry juice, lime juice and Equal(r) in punch bowl; stir until Equal(r) dissolves. Stir in ice cubes, diet ginger ale and sliced lime; garnish with fresh cranberries if desired.

Submitter: 
EQUAL®
Description: 
Make this tangy punch with or without alcohol. Cranberry juice concentrate provides the ruby red color and lime juice the tanginess. Garnish each serving with a lime slice.
