Title:
Team Up: Key West Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 tablespoons soy sauce
3 tablespoon honey
3 tablespoon vegetable oil
3 teaspoon lime juice
3 teaspoon chopped garlic
16 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
3 resealable freezer bags - gallon size

Directions:
Prop open resealable freezer bag and add soy sauce, honey, vegetable oil, lime juice, and garlic. Add chicken and seal bag. Combine ingredients by messaging with hands until all ingredients form one even sauce. Freeze flat.
Thaw completely in refrigerator overnight (or another way if you prefer). Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Lightly oil the grill grate. Discard marinade, and grill chicken 6 to 8 minutes on each side, until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
dinnercoach
Description: 
Make in bulk. Store 2 bags in the freezer and grill one up tonight. Double the recipe, prepare with friends and go home with three bags each. Save money by buying ingredients in bulk.
