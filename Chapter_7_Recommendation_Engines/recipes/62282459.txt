Title:
Crockpot Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean beef stew meat, sliced thinly
3 T. flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
4 c water
3 tsp beef bouillon crystals
1 T. Worcestershire
1 tsp ground mustard
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1 can diced tomatoes
3 med potatoes, diced
1 onion, diced
3 scraped carrots, sliced
3 stalks celery, sliced
1 - 1 1/2 cups frozen green peas

Directions:
In the bottom of crockpot, stir together beef, flour, salt, and pepper.  Add remaining ingredients and stir well.  Cook on low 10 hours, or high 5 hours.

Submitter: 
B L
Description: 
Simple, easy, delicious family favorite.  My family loves this stew served with Jiffy cornbread muffins, which are slightly sweet.
