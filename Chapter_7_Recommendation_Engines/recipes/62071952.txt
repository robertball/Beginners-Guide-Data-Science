Title:
Grandma's Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup diced bacon
4 medium potatoes, peeled and chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cups water
3 cups of cream-style corn (2-12oz cans)
2 tsp salt
pepper to taste
2 cups of half and half or light cream, scalded

Directions:
In a large pot, saute bacon until browned and crisp.  Pour off all but 2 tbsp of bacon fat and crumble bacon into small pieces.  Add chopped onions and potatoes to reserved bacon fat/bacon.  Saute for 5 minutes.  Add water, corn and salt, cover and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring frequently.  Add scalded half and half and heat through but do not boil.

Submitter:

Description: 
My husband's grandmother used to make this chowder for him when he was a child.  When we got married, she passed the recipe onto me.  Enjoy!
