Title:
Fried Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices cornbread
1 tbs. oil
1 tbs. butter

Directions:
Heat skillet, add oil and butter. Cut cornbread in half.
Fry cornbread, cut side up, until crispy. Flip and fry other side. Drizzle each piece with honey and serve.
