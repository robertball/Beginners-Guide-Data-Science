Title:
Gramma's Apple Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PUDDING
long stick of rench bread
1/4 cup raisins, soaked 10 minutes in boiled water
2  peeled and sliced apples
1 cup brown sugar
2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1 stick butter
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg and allspice, each
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 eggs, beaten
VANILLA SAUCE
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup milk
1 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
bake 1 hour in water bath

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
Bread, raisins and apples are baked with eggs and cinnamon, then topped with a sweet, warm vanilla sauce in this comforting dish.Modified from: Gramma's Apple Bread Pudding
