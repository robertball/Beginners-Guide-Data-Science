Title:
Southwestern Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. salad pasta (or any small tubular pasta)
DRESSING:
1/3 cup vegetable oil (or a little less)
1/4 cup fresh lime juice (or a little more)
2 Tbsp+ chili powder (to taste)
2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp salt
1 or 2 cloves garlic, crushed
***
1-1/2 cup fresh or canned corn kernels
1 15 oz. can black beans, drained & rinsed
1/2 cup each diced red & green bell pepper
1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
1 cup bite-size pieces plum tomatoes
Curly-leaf lettuce

Directions:
Cook pasta as label directs, until al dente or firm to the bite;drain.
While cooking, whisk all dressing ingredients (oil through garlic) in a large bowl until smooth.
Add pasta;gently t oss t o combine.  Let cool to room temperatur, stirring occasionally.
Add corn, beans, peppers and half the cilantro; toss. Spoon onto platter lined with lettuce leaves.  Garnish with tomatoes and remaining cilantro.  Serve at room temperature or chilled.
** This recipe originally had a seeded and finely chopped jalapeno rather than the chili powder; I like the flavor better in this version, and the color is gorgeous!

Submitter: 
Lisa Provost
Description: 
This recipe is very popular for potlucks -- make a double batch!
