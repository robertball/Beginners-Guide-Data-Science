Title:
Mary Alice's Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 beaten eggs
2 squares melted unsweetened chocolate
1/2 cup flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
a dash of salt
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients except nuts with electric mixer until well blended. Add in the nuts by hand.
2. Pour into greased and floured 8"x8" pan. Bake at 325Â° for 30-40 minutes.
3. Let cool (if possible) before eating.
