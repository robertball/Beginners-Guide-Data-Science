Title:
CREAMY SPINACH AND BACON DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 {16Oz. container} Sour Cream
2 {8Oz. Pkgs. each} Cream Cheese, softened
1 {10Oz. Pkg.} frozen chopped Spinach, thawed and well drained
6 Thick Silced, Hickory Smoked Bacon, cooked to a golden brown, well drained and crumbled
1/4C. Red Bell Pepper, well chopped
1/4C. Scallions, greens and white parts, well chopped
2 Cloves Garlic, crushed
1/4Tsp. Tabasco Pepper Sauce

Directions:
In a large-size mixing-bowl, mix sour cream, cream cheese, spinach, bacon, bell pepper, scallions, garlic and Tabasco until well blended.
Pour into air-tight bowl and cover, place in frig. and chill for at least 12 to 24 hours before serving!!
Serve with flavored crackers and/or raw vegetables!!

Submitter: 
Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
This is a very easy recipe to make, it is also very savory, tasty and just a bit peppery!!

This is a great dip for family or friend get togethers or even sports parties!!
