Title:
Breakfast Veggie Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/651796.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten or (3/4 C liquid egg substitute)
1/2 C low fat cottage cheese
2 oz sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 C fresh spinach, chopped
1/2 C tomato, chopped
1/8 C bell pepper, diced
1/8 C onion, diced
salt and pepper to taste
preferred seasonings

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a small mixing bowl and pour into a greased casserole (I use 6x6).
Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes

Submitter: 
linda2d
Description: 
Easy , healthy breakfast. Very forgiving, I use all kinds of vegetables for this. Reheats well.
