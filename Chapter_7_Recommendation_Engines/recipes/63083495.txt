Title:
Best Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados
1/2 lemon, juiced
2 tablespoons chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, chopped
handful cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Cut the avocados into halves. Remove the seeds, and scoop out the pulp into a small bowl.  Use a fork to mash the avocado.  Stir in lemon juice, onion, salt, olive oil garlic, and cilantro.  Cover the bowl, and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
