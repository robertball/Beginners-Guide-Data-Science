Title:
Fluffy White Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3523963.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup white sugar
3/4 cup evaporated milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream butter for 5 minutes. Add shortening and cream for another 5 minutes. Beat in sugar. Add evaporated milk and vanilla. Beat until very light and fluffy.

Submitter: 
janiebear925
Description: 
This fluffy white filling is made with butter, shortening and evaporated milk.
