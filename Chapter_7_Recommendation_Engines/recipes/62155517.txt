Title:
Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (16 oz.) kidney beans,drained
1 can (16oz) yellow strin beans,drained
1 can (16oz) green string beans,drained
1 can (16oz) chickpeas,drained
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup salad oil

Directions:
Combine kidney beans,string beans and chickpeas in a bowl. In a saucepan,boil together sugar and vinegar for 10 minutes. Add salad oil and pour over vegetables. Let sit overnight in refrigerator.(Other vegetables ,such as onions,peppers and olives can be added)

Submitter: 
Patti
Description: 
A three-bean salad that can become four with chickpeas
