Title:
Tastes of the Orient Gingered Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups diced unpeeled apples
1 (8 oz) can water chestnuts, drained and diced
3/4 cup finely diced celery
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup lowfat vanilla yogurt
1/4 cup light mayonnaise
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
2 Tbs. sugar
1/8 tsp. salt
1/4 cup chopped pecans, toastedw

Directions:
In a bowl, combine apples, water chestnuts, celery, and cranberries. Stir together yogurt, mayonnaise, ginger, sugar, and salt. Pour over apple mixture and toss to coat. Sprinkle with pecans.

Submitter: 
larkspur
Description: 
This luscious salad combines crisp apples, crunchy water chestnuts, chewy dried cranberries and other ingredients with a creamy dressing. It is hard to stop eating this healthy salad!
