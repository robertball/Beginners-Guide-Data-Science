Title:
Apple Sage Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 teaspoon sage
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon thyme
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon paprika
2 tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon salt
4 boneless pork chops
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
2 red apples, thinly sliced
1/2 cup apple juice
1 tablespoon brown sugar

Directions:
Mix flour and all the spices in a small bowl. Sprinkle both sides of pork chops with 1 tablespoon of seasoned flour.
Cook the pork chops in hot olive oil in large skillet on medium-high heat until browned on both sides. Once browned remove pork chops from skillet. Add onions to the skillet and cook for 3 minutes or until tender. Add the apples to the onions and cook for an additional 2 minutes. Add the apple juice, brown sugar and the remaining seasoned flour, stirring until well mixed.
Return pork chops to the skillet. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes.
