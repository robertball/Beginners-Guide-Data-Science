Title:
Cucumber Soup with Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium- size cucumbers
1 can (14.5 oz) chicken broth
1 cup chopped tomato
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Peel the cucumbers, and quarter them lengthwise.  Remove the seeds.
Puree 2 of the cucumbers with the chicken stock in a blender.  Pour the puree into a large bowl.
Chop the remaining 2 cucumbers.  Combine the cucumber slices, tomatoes, lime juice and cayenne pepper to the cucumber puree.  Chill.

Description: 
Healthy five-ingredient cucumber soup that is delicious and refreshing.
