Title:
Waffle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks butter or margarine;
1/2 to 2/3 cup cocoa;
3 eggs;
2 1/4 cups white sugar;
3 tsp. vanilla;
3 cups flour;

Directions:
Melt butter and cocoa in microwave.  Let cool.  Mix in eggs, vanilla, and white sugar.  Stir in flour.  Drop by tsp (I use Pampered Chef med. cookie scoop) onto hot waffle iron.  Bake for 2 min.  Remove from iron and let cool.
