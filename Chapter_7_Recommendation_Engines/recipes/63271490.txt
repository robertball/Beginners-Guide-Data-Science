Title:
PACIFIC RRIM CUCUMBER SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/858875.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Japanese cucumbers
2 cups Daikon radisehes
1 cup Maui onion
2 cups grape tomatoes
1 tablespoon fine sea salt
1 teaspoon white pepper
1/4 cup seasoned rice vinegar
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 bay leaf
2 tablespoons cilantro (Chopped)
1/4 cup chives (Chopped)

Directions:
Peel cucumbers and radishes. Slice cucumbers in half lengthwise; use a spoon and remove seeds.
Slice cucumbers and radishes very thin.  Place on a paper towel and sprinkle fine sea salt over cucumbers and radishes. Let the stand for 10 minutes.
Cut grape tomatoes in half.
Peel and cut Maui onion in half. Slice onions very thin.
In a small saucepan bring vinegar, sugar and bay leaf to a boil. Wisk until sugar is disolved. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and discard bay leaf.
Rinse cucumbers and radishes under cold water. Pat dry with a paper towel and place in a glass bowl. Add remaining ingredients to the salad. Pour the dressing over salad and toss well.
Chill salad for 30 minutes. Reseason with salt and pepper to taste. Serve on chilled plates.
Garnish with a sprig of cilantro.

Description: 
A cool sumertime salad with ingredients from the Pacific Rim. The salad has fresh herbs and a Rice Vinegar dressing. This salad can be served as a appetizer or a side dish.
