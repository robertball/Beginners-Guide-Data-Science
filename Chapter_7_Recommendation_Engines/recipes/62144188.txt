Title:
Smoky Cheddar Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
8 oz. smoked sharp cheddar cheese, finely shredded and at room temperature
1/4 c. butter, softened
1/4 c. real bacon bits
2 Tbsp. green onions, very finely chopped (use white & green parts)
1 c. smoked almonds, chopped

Directions:
In a medium bowl or food processor, combine the cream cheese, cheddar and butter; process or beat until creamed. Stir in bacon bits and green onions. Roughly form into a ball and wrap tightly in plastic wrap. Chill in the refrigerator for 4 hours or overnight. When chilled and easy to handle, unwrap and roll in the chopped smoked almonds. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Jacki_In_Simi
Description: 
Using smoked cheddar, real bacon bits and smoked almonds gives this cheese ball a tasty twist! The bacon bits are normally found on the salad dressing eisle along with other salad toppers.
