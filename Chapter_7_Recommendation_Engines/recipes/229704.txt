Title:
Simple and Sweetie Blueberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991492.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (21 ounce) can LUCKY LEAFÂ® Premium Blueberry Fruit Filling
6 tablespoons butter
3/4 cup sugar
1 cup self-rising flour
1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Melt butter and pour into a 2- or 2.5-quart casserole or baking pan. Mix sugar, flour and milk.
Pour mixture over butter, but do not mix. Pour the LUCKY LEAF Premium Blueberry Pie Filling on top, but do not mix.
Bake for 1 hour. Serve warm with ice cream, if desired.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Nothing could be easier and more satisfying than this blueberry cobbler served warm with ice cream.
