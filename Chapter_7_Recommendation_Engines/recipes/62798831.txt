Title:
Lime-Cilantro Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup lime juice
2 Tbsp. white vinegar
1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1 clove fresh garlic, minced
3/4 tsp. spicy brown or Dijon mustard
3/4 cup light olive oil

Directions:
Puree lime juice, vinegar, and cilatro in a blender; add sugar, garlic and salt.  Puree until smooth; add mustard.  Slowly pour oil in a thin stream while continuing to blend.  Serve immediately or keep in the fridge for 4 to 5 days.

Submitter: 
drjwall
Description: 
This is a delicious tangy vinaigrette.  It's so versatile, it can be used for mixed greens or taco salads.  Enjoy!
