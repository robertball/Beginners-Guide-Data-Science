Title:
Strawberry Banana Nut Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe banana
1 cup chopped walnuts
3 cups buttermilk pancake mix
1 1/2 cups water
16 ounces of low fat yoghurt
1 pound fresh strawberries, sliced
cinnamon sugar

Directions:
Mash the banana in a large mixing bowl.
Add the chopped walnuts, pancake mix and water. Blend.
Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto a hot griddle. Flip the pancake when the bottom is golden brown and the surface has bubbled.
Spread approximately 1 tablespoon of yoghurt onto each pancake and top with the strawberries. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
After looking at many pancake recipes, I put this recipe together using my favorite ingredients -- strawberries, bananas and nuts. It is a satisfying and rich breakfast dish, that can be served for company, yet it is quick and easy to make.
