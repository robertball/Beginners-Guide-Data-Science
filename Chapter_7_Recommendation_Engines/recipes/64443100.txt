Title:
Fruit Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2365846.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Watermelon slice
2 kiwi sliced
10 red grapes halved
5 strawberries sliced
1/2 cup feta cheese crumbled

Directions:
Cut a thin slice from watermelon as pictured.  Top with all remaining ingredients

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
A fun and unique way to serve fruit salad.  I usually make this at parties and it always gets comments on how cool it is.
