Title:
Asian Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 salmon filets
1-2 tbsp. canola oil
2 medium or 1 large shallot, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 inch of fresh ginger, minced
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup seasoned rice wine vinegar
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp. sugar
Green onion

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet.
Add shallots and cook until opaque.
Add garlic and ginger and cook until tender.
Remove from pan and reserve.
Place salmon serving side down in skillet and cook until lightly brown.
Flip and brown other side.
Mix soy sauce, vinegar, water and sugar.
Add to skillet with shallot mixture.
Bring to a simmer and cook just until salmon is done.
Garnish with sliced green onion.
