Title:
Grandma's Maple Cinnamon Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon Grand Marnier
2 tablespoons melted butter
2 tablespoon maple syrup

Directions:
In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt. In another bowl, combine egg, milk, butter, Grand Marnier and syrup
After whisking the Wet Ingredients together, Slowly add the dry and whisk together till they are blended and not lumpy
Pour batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto a lightly greased hot griddle; turn when bubbles form on top of pancakes. Cook until second side is golden brown (pancakes are not Fluffy).
