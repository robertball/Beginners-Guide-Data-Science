Title:
Harvest Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb dry lima beans
1 teas salt-
appx 4 cups chicken broth and 2 cups water.
12 oz breakfast sausage
2 large granny smith apples, diced
1 large onion, diced
3 stalks celery, diced
1-2 cloves garlic, smashed and chopped fine
1 28 oz can diced tomates
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons yellow mustard
1/2 teas pepper, or to taste

Directions:
Rinse lima beans to remove any dirt.  Place cleaned beans in large Dutch oven with salt and 8 cups water.  cover pan and soak overnight- drain and rinse the next morning. Place cleaned beans back in pot with chicken stock and water. Place pot on burner and heat until about to boil-reduce heat to simmer and cover pot.
While the beans are simmering crumble and cook sausage, chopped apples (do not peel), onion and celery in a large skillet until sausage is done.  Add garlic and cook another 30 seconds.  Remove lid from beans and add sausage mixture to pot.
Add tomatoes with liquid, brown sugar, mustard and pepper.  Simmer for an additional hour or until beans or cooked.
