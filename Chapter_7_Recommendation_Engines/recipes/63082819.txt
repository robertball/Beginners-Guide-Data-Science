Title:
Cattleboys Beef on a Bun Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lb. beef roast
Sauce:
1/2 chopped onion
2 tbsp. butter
2 cups ketchup
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 tbsp. worchestershire sauce
1/2 cup Cattleboys BBQ sauce

Directions:
The day before, preheat oven to 350. Put (frozen) roast in roaster with 1 1/2 inches of water. Sprinkle with steak seasoning and cook for four hours.
Make sauce while roast is cooking (saute onion in butter, then add remaining ingredients).
Once cooked, thinly slice roast, place in crockport, pour sauce over it, and keep in fridge overnight.
The next day, put crockpot on low and cook for four hours.
