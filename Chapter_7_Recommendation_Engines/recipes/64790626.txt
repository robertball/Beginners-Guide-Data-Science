Title:
Easy Savory Rice Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C orzo
1 1/3 C rice
2 Tbs butter
2 Tbs vegetable oil
1 TBS ranch dressing powder
1 tsp dried parsley
3 1/2C hot water
2 chicken bouillon cubes

Directions:
In a bowl mix hot water and bouillon cubes until cubes dissolve.
Heat orzo, rice, oil, butter, ranch drsssing powder and parsley over medium heat until orzo is brown.
Add bouillon water to pan.  Mix and bring to a boil.  Cover, Reduce heat to low. Cook for 15-20 minutes until rice is tender/ done.
