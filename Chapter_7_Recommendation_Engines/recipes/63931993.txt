Title:
Marinated Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs fresh whole button mushrooms
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1 large egg yolk
1/2 tsp minced garlic
2 tsp dijon mustard
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp Italian seasoning blend (italian dressing mix)
1/2 cup EV olive oil w/1/2 cup water
2 Tbl shredded mozzarella (opt for non-dairy dish)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Spray 13x9 glass dish with nonstick spray.
Rinse & dry mushrooms (remove extra woody stems). Place in prepared dish
In small bowl, mix vinegar, egg yolk, mustard, garlic, onion powder & italian seasoning. While whisking the vinegar mixture, slowly add oil in thin stream until fully incorporated. Drizzle over mushrooms & stir fully to coat with marinade. Roast at 400 for 15 min. Remove from oven & baste with marinade in pan
Turn oven to low broil.  If using cheese, sprinkle cheese over mushrooms & return to broil for 2-3 min until cheese is golden
