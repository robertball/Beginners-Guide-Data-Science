Title:
Streusel Topped Rhubarb Stir Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup sour cream
4 cups chopped rhubarb(fresh or frozen)(drain if frozen)
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/3 cup cold butter
1 cup flour for streusel topping

Directions:
In large bowl, cream together butter or margarine and brown sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla.
Sift or stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually stir in to butter mixture.  Fold in sour cream and rhubarb.  Spoon batter into greased 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish.
Cut in white sugar and cinnamon, butter, and flour; sprinkle over batter.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes, or until tester comes out clean.
