Title:
Chocolate Dream Cake - Low Fat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16oz) Angel Food Cake Mix
3/4 Cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1/4 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup Boiling Water
1 1/4 Cups Water
Frosting and Filling:
1 Tbsp. Instant Coffee
1 1/2 Cups Skim Milk
1 (1 1/3 oz) Pckg. Whipped Topping Mix (Dream Whip)
1 (1.4oz) Pckg. Sugar-Free Instant Chocolate Pudding Mix

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350'.
Line a 10x15 (With deep sides) Inch Jelly Roll pan with waxed paper
Whisk together cocoa, sugar and boiling water
Cool above mixture to luke warm temp.
Prepare cake mix per package directions, using 1 1/4 cups water and adding cocoa mixture to batter
Spread batter evenly in pan
Bake 20 minutes or until top looks dry.
Invert onto a large wire rack, remove from pan and waxed paper and cool.
Frosting and filling:
In a medium bowl, dissolve coffee in milk
Add topping and pudding mix
Beat on low speed until moistened
Beat at high speed until soft peaks form, then chill for 5 minutes
Cut cake crosswise into thirds and cover each layer with topping and stack layers, Cover top of cake last with layer of frosting
Cover and chill for 2 hours before serving.
