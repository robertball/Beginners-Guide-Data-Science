Title:
Casarecce with Broccoli and Italian Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/974211.jpg
Ingredients:
One package of Casarecce or any bite-size pasta
Five cups of broccoli tops
One cup of chopped yellow or white onion
Two tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
Two cloves of garlic, chopped fine
One package of Johnsonville Sweet Italian Sausage
One cup of Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut broccoli tops into bite-size pieces. Blanche. Set aside.
Saute onion in olive oil until golden brown. Add garlic, saute until fragrant. Remove from heat and set aside.
Cook pasta until al dente. Drain.
Remove sausage from casings, crumble and brown. Drain liquid.
Add sausage to pasta. Mix. Add broccoli and half of the cheese. Mix.
Garnish with remaining Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Ross
Description: 
Pasta, pork and cheese are a classic combination in provincial Italy. This is the food of the people!
