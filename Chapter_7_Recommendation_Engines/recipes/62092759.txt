Title:
Tanner's Best Bacon Walleye Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 strips of bacon - cooked but not crispy
4 walleye fillets (or orhter white fish)
1 onion halved and sliced thinly
1/4 cup butter
2 tablespoons honey
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Line 9x13 pan with foil.
Place fillet over four pieces of bacon.
Cover with 1/4 of the onion.
Drizzle with Butter and Honey.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Cover with foil and grill or bake at 400 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
Fish should flake easily when done.
Serve fillet with bacon and onions.

Submitter: 
BRENTWASHINGTON
Description: 
Incredible flavour combinations enhance the walleye.  Bacon and onion with honey will make this a family favourite for you too! Quick and easy.
