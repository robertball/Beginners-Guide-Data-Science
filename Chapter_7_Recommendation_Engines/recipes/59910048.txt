Title:
Quick and Easy Broiled Tilapia Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/270917.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup high temperature olive oil
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon pepper and spice mix (such as lemon pepper)
2 pounds tilapia fillets

Directions:
Preheat your oven's broiler. Coat a pan with non-stick spray.
In a flat  bowl, mix together the Parmesan cheese, oil, Italian seasoning (or other spice mix) and lemon juice.
Dip fillets in the parmesan cheese mixture so they are coated on both sides and lay in the pan.  Broil a few inches from the heat for 2 to 3 minutes. Flip the fillets over and broil for a couple more minutes. Remove and serve immediately.
