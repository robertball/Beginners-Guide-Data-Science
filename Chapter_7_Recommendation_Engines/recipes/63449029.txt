Title:
Cheesy Broccoli Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c chopped potato
1/2 c chopped carrots
1/2 c chopped celery
2 cups broccoli florets cut into very small pieces
3 T olive oil
1 T butter
3 c chicken broth
2 c whole milk
1/2 c flout
2 1/2 c finely shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 c shredded swiss cheese
1 T chopped italian parsley

Directions:
Melt butter into oil over medium heat.
Add vegetables and sautee until tender.
Add chicken broth, bring to a boil and then reduce heat to simmer for 30 minutes.
Blend milk and flour and then whisk mixture into broth.
Add cheeses and parsley,and cook until thickened and bubbling, stirring frequently.
