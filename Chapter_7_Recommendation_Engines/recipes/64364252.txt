Title:
Liat's Sweet 'n' Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2156841.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 egg
1 onion, chopped
1 cup bread crumbs
Salt and pepper
Sauce:
1 cup water
1/2 cup cider vinegar (less if you like it sweeter)
1/2 cup ketchup
2 Tbsp. corn starch
1 cup brown sugar (more if you like it sweeter)
2 Tbsp. soy sauce

Directions:
Form meatballs and brown in oven at 350.
Stir sauce ingredients into saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring over medium heat.
Pour sauce over meatballs and enjoy
