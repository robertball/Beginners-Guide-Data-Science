Title:
Quick Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can apple pie filling
Cinnamon as desired
1 large egg
1 tsp. vanilla
½ cup white sugar
½ cup brown sugar
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
½ cup butter, melted
Whipped cream or ice cream

Directions:
Empty filling into 8-9" square pan and sprinkle with cinnamon to taste.
Beat egg slightly and add vanilla.
In another bowl, mix sugars, flour and baking powder.  Add egg mixture and mix until crumbly.  Distribute evenly over pie filling.  Pour butter over top and bake in 375 degree oven for 30 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream.
Note:  If canned pie sliced apples are used, sprinkle with 1 T. sugar.

Submitter: 
CHER
Description: 
This is a quick and delicious apple crisp that you can throw together for a last minute dessert.  I have been making it for years and it always turns out good.
