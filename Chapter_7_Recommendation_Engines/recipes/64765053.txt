Title:
Jennie's Italian Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7475716.jpg
Ingredients:
-  1 lb grass feed beef
-  1 lb veal
-  1 envelop Lipton Recipe Secrets Beefy Onion Soup mix
-  3/4 cup bread crumbs of your choice
-  2 eggs
-  3/4 cup marinara sauce
-  1/3 cup ketchup
-  chopped onions
-  chopped garlic
-  Pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large bowl.  Shape into loaf in 13X9 inch baking or roasting pan.  Bake at 350 degrees uncovered until done, about 1 hour.
