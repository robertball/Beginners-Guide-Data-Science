Title:
Pulled BBQ chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion chopped
1 small clove of garlic minced
4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons light brown sugar
1 tablespoon olive oil
One and a half teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon chipotle chili powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons cider vinegar

Directions:
Sprinkle onion and garlic over the bottom of the cooker and arrange the chicken on top.
In a bowl, stir ketchup, cider vinegar, brown sugar, oil, W-sauce, chili powders, and salt. Pour over the chicken. Cook on LOW for 7 to 8 hours.
Remove chicken. With 2 forks pull apart the meat into shredded chunks, return to cooker and stir. Cover and leave on warm setting until ready to serve. (Serve on buns.)
