Title:
Pineapple coffee cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
3 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1 large egg
1 cup plain or vanilla yogurt (can substitute sour cream)
1/4 cup canola oil
1 t vanilla
1 1/2 cups pineapple tidbits, drained, blotted dry and chopped. Set aside 1/3 cup pineapple juice for recipe.
1/4 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup brown sugar for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Lightly oil or coat with non-stick spray an 8-inch square pan.
In a bowl, whisk flour, both 1/4 cup white and 1/4 cup brown sugars, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
In another bowl, whisk egg, yogurt, oil, and vanilla until smooth.  Add dry ingrediants and stir until just blended. Add 1/3 cup juice from can of pineapple.
Fold in pineapple tidbits.
Pour batter into pan.
In a small bowl combine pecans and 1/4 cup brown sugar for topping.   Sprinkle over batter in pan.
Bake for 25 - 35 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. You may need to cover top with foil half way through baking.
Let cool in pan on wire rack for about 20 minutes.
Searve warm or cold.
