Title:
Sweet Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of green beans or 1/2 pkg of frozen green beans
2 T butter
2-3 T brown sugar
garlic salt
pepper

Directions:
Cook beans to your desired doneness in microwave or on stovetop. Drain. Add remaining ingredients and heat though to melt butter and sugar. Season to taste with garlic salt and black pepper.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
These became a staple item at our house after my kids dicovered green bean bundles at Easter one year. This is a lighter and quicker interpretation of that concept. A spoonful of sugar makes the vegetables go down!
