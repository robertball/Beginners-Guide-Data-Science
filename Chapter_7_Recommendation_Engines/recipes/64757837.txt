Title:
Yummy Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium sized bunches of broccoli, flowers only
1 cup of pecans, toasted (optional) and chopped.  **NOTE:  Walnuts are too overwhelming for this recipe
1 cup raisins
1/4 cup red onion, peeled, sliced thin
1 pound bacon, cooked crisp, crumbled
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup vinegar

Directions:
Break the broccoli into bite-size pieces, discard stalk.  Add nuts, raisins, onion and bacon.
Mix mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar.  Pour over broccoli and toss gently.
