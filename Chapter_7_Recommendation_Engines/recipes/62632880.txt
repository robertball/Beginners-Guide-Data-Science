Title:
Succulent Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds chuck roast
Montreal Steak Seasoning
olive oil
1 large garlic clove, minced
1 cup beef broth

Directions:
Take the chuck roast and season Montreal Steak Seasoning and garlic. Brown in olive oil on all sides in a large skillet over high heat.
Place roast in the slow cooker and pour in beef broth.
Cover and cook on low setting for 8 to 10 hours.
Optional: Roast potatoes in the oven and steam carrots and peas to serve alongside roast. Whisk a little flour into the leftover liquid once roast is removed to create a delicious gravy.

Submitter: 
CARI
Description: 
Browned chuck roast is slow cooked for 8 to 10 hours to create a succulent, deliciously tender pot roast.Modified from: Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast
