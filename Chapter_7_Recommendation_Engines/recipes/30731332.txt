Title:
Wheaties Steak & Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb stew meat
2 Tbsp shortening
1 small onion (sliced into rings)
1 cup water
2 large potatoes
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1 1/4 cup milk
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup cheddar cheese
1 1/4 cup Wheaties crushed

Directions:
Melt shortening in skillet. Cook meat & onion until meat is brown. Add water, heat till boiling, cover and simmer for 50 minutes.
Heat oven to 350. Peel and thinly slice potatoes. In a bowl mix, soup, sour cream, milk, salt & pepper.
Pour meat (with juices-if any) into a 9x13 pan. Arrange potatoes on top of meat. Pour soup mix over potatoes, sprinkle with cheese then cover with crushed cereal. Bake for 1 1/2 hours uncovered.
