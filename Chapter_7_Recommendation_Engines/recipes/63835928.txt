Title:
Kicked up Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes
2 1/2 pounds red potatoes, skin on, quartered
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 (8 ounce) tub PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese Spread
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 teaspoon salt
Meat mixture
2 pound ground chuck(drain grease after browning)
3 Tsp of butter
1 packet McCormick Aus Jus mix
1 Packet Ranch Dressing mix
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
Rest of the toppings
1 (6 ounce) Package of french fried onions
1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (10 ounce) can diced carrots, drained
1 (10 ounce) can peas, drained
2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Cook potatoes and garlic in boiling water in large covered saucepan 20 min. or until potatoes are tender; drain.
Mash potatoes until smooth.
Stir in remaining ingredients until well blended.
While potatoes are boiling brown the meat in a           large skillet and drain the fat.
Add the 3 Tsp of butter, Packet of Au Jus mix and Ranch dressing mix and 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper.
Spread ground chuck mixture into the bottom of a 3- quart casserole dish and top with e french fried onions.
Top with corn, carrots, and peas.
Spoon mashed potato mixture over vegetable layer.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over mashed potato layer and continue baking until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 10 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Thor
Description: 
Ground chuck is seasoned with Au Jus and Ranch Dressing mixes, with Cayenne Pepper for that extra Kick. Layered with canned vegetables, Garlic mashed potatoes, and Cheddar cheese creating a twist on the traditional shepherd's pie.Modified from: Marie's Shepherd's Pie
