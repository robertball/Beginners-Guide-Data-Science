Title:
White Chocolate Peppermint Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white chocolate chips
6 oz. cream cheese block, softened
3 cups icing sugar (or less)
Peppermint candy cane pieces

Directions:
Melt chocolate in microwave for 1 min at a time until melted.
In a small bowl beat cream cheese and slowly add icing sugar. Add melted chocolate chips; mix well.
Frost cooled squares.
Garnish with peppermint candy pieces.
