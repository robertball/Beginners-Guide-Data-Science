Title:
Italian Beef Skillet with Tomatoes Mozzarella and Burrata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7111631.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb grass fed ground beef
bacon grease or tallow for browning meat if desired
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 to 1 tsp dried oregano
sprinkle of fennel seeds (maybe 1/2 tsp)
salt to taste (maybe 1/2 to 1 tsp)
10 or so cherry tomatoes
10 or so small mozzarella balls
handful of basil, chopped
1 large burrata ball, sliced

Directions:
brown meat in bacon grease or tallow over medium high heat
add garlic, oregano, fennel, half of basil, and salt
cook on low until beef almost done
add tomatoes
once tomatoes soften, add mozzarella balls until they start to melt
add other half of basil
spoon into bowls and add slice of burrata on top
drizzle with olive oil or herb vinaigrette
