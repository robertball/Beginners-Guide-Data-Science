Title:
Sarah's Salsa by Paula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (28 ounce) cans whole tomatoes
1  (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
Juice of 1 lime
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, chopped
2/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 large yellow onion, chopped (vidalia if possible)
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp cumin

Directions:
In a food processor, place whole tomatoes, diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, lime juice, jalapeno pepper, cilantro, yellow onion, garlic salt and cumin. Blend until smooth. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Paula S.
Description: 
Canned tomatoes make this delicious and versatile salsa fast and easy. Adjust the ingredients to taste.Modified from: Sarah's Salsa
