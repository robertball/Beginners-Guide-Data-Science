Title:
Peanut butter and honey muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c. flour (white and wheat mix)
1 T. baking powder
3/4 tsp. salt
3/4 c. honey
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 1/2 c. milk
2 eggs, lightly beaten

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients.  Make a well in the center.
Combine honey and peanut butter in a bowl.  Add milk and eggs and stir well.
Add honey mixture to flour mixture; stir just until moist.
Spoon batter into muffin cups (about 18).  Bake at 400 for 18 minutes or until done.  Remove from pan and place on wire rack to cool.
