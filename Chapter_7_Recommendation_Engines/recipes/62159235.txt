Title:
Balsamic Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 medium onion, chopped fine
3 large cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons italian herb mix
1/2 teaspoon ground fennel
1/2 teaspoon chili flakes

Directions:
heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. add onion and salt and saute until translucent. stir in garlic, herb mix, fennel and chili flakes. add balsamic vinegar and continue to heat until slightly syrupy. add tomatoes and tomato paste. bring back to a simmer and serve over pasta.
