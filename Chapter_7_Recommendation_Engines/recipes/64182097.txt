Title:
Hot Cowboy Cookie Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1504290.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Old Fashioned Oatmeal
1/4 cup Raisins
1/4 cup Pecans
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon
1 3/4 cup Milk
1 egg (beat)
2 teaspoon butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 tablespoon Semi Chocolate Chips
1-3 tablespoon of Milk (optional)

Directions:
combined dry ingredients: Oats, Raisins, Pecans, Sugar, and Cinnamon. Set aside
In large sauce pan, on med high heat bring to boil  wet ingredients: Milk, Egg, Butter, and Vanilla.
Add Dry Ingredients to wet cook about 5-7 minutes
To desired thickness.
Let Cool Slightly.
Add Milk if desired
Sprinkled a few Chocolate Chips on top.
