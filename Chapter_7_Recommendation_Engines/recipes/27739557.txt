Title:
Jen's Ginger Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
All approximate
1/4 cup grapeseed oil
3 TBS. Terriyaki
1-1/2 TBS. Red wine vinegar
2 tsp. soy sauce
1/2 cup ginger dressing
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/8 tsp. ground mustard
1/4 tsp. balsamic vinegar
2 breasts of chicken
sesame seeds
1/2 box of harvest wheat linguine
1 bag of Kroger frozen veggies

Directions:
Cook chicken in all liquids except ginger dressing.  At the same time boil water for noodles and cook.  Add veggies after chicken is cooked.
Add all dry spices.  Then add ginger dressing.  Toss to coat.  Add noodles to veggies and chicken.
