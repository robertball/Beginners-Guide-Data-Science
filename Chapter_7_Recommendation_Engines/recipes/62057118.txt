Title:
Fiesta Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup picante sauce
2 Tbsp taco seasoning mix
Dash garlic powder
1 (4-1/2 oz) can chopped black olives, drained
1 (4-oz) can chopped green chilies
1 cup finely shredded cheddar cheese
8 flour tortillas
Salsa

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat cream cheese, sour cream, picante sauce, taco seasoning, and garlic powder until smooth.  Stir in olives, chilies, and cheddar cheese.  Spread 1/2 cup on each tortilla.  Roll up each tortilla jelly roll style.  Wrap in plastic wrap.  Refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight.  Slice into 1-inch pieces.  Serve with salsa.

Submitter: 
ACPHIFER
Description: 
A festive appetizer with cream cheese, sour cream, and picante sauce wrapped in flour tortillas.
