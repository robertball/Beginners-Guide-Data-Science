Title:
Sweet Potato Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Turkey
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 cups cubed sweet potato, canned or fresh
28 oz can diced tomatoes, undrained
28 oz can black beans, drained
1 tbsp pumpkin pie spice
1 tbsp chili powder
2 tsp cumin
salt and pepper to taste
cheyenne pepper to taste, optional
Optional: When serving top with shredded cheddar cheese, cilantro and/or sour cream

Directions:
In a large soup pot on medium heat, brown turkey until crumbly and no longer pink, about 15 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients to the browned turkey. Simmer on low heat for 3 hours, until sweet potatoes are soft and fully cooked. Ladle into bowls and garnish with cheddar cheese, fresh cilantro and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Nicole Berger
Description: 
I love the blend of sweetness from the sweet potatoes and the little bit of kick from the cheyenne pepper. The pumpkin pie spice seems a little strange but it adds a little something that makes this recipe a bit different then your usual chili...but in a good way!
