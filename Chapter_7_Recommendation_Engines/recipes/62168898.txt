Title:
Mom's Mini Meatloaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 c. lemon juice
1/2 c. water
2 tsp. seasoned salt
1 egg
16 saltine crackers, crushed
1/4 c. chopped onion
1/2 c. ketchup
1/3 c. brown sugar
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. allspice
1 tsp. dried mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, lemon juice, water, seasoned salt, egg, crackers and onion until just combined.
Form the meat into five small oval meatloaves and place into a baking dish.
In a small bowl, mix the ketchup, brown sugar, cloves, allspice and dried mustard until well combined.
Spoon the sauce over each meatloaf and spread slightly to coat the tops.
Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
Uncover the dish and continue baking for 10 more minutes to brown the glaze slightly.
