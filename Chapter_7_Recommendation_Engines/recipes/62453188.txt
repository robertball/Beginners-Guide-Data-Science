Title:
Audrey's basic Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup cocoa
2 eggs
1 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cups flour
1/2 cups chopped nuts (optional)
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
Melt butter and cocoa, stirring occasionally
In medium bowl beat eggs until frothy.  Add sugar, flour, nuts and salt.  don't stir yet.  then pour cocoa mixture over topy and stir all together.  Scrape batter into greased 8 x 8 inch pan.
Bake in 350 oven for about 30 minutes until the edges begin to show signs of pulling away from the sides of the pan.
