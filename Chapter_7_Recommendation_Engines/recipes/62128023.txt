Title:
Caramelized Broccoli & Onion Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 box of jumbo pasta shells, cooked al dente and drained
3 small heads of broccoli, washed and chopped
1 red onion, thinly sliced
3/4 cup ricotta
5 cups or 1 jar of prepared marinara sauce
crushed red pepper
olive oil
crushed garlic
salt & pepper
parmesan or mozzarella cheese for toppping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 f
Heat a non-stick skillet on med/high, cook onions in small amount of olive oil until translucent, add broccoli, red pepper & salt.  Do not stir very often to allow broccoli and onion to caramelize.
When broccoli and onions are carmalized, reduce heat to low, add ricotta, garlic & pepper to taste , stir well.  Remove broccoli mixture from heat.
Line the bottom of 1 & 1/2 qt. or 9x13 baking dish with 1 cup of marinara sauce. Stuff broccoli and onion mixture into shells and place into dish. Top with remaining marinara and any cheese you wish for garnish.
Bake uncovered for 25 - 30 minutes or until dish is heated through and sauce is bubbling.

Submitter: 
Scarlett
Description: 
This vegetarian dish is a nice change from the traditional cheese laden spinach stuffed shells.  It is quick to prepare and the serving amounts are easily adjusted.
