Title:
Emet's Roll Recipe We do not use this one Recipe

Ingredients:
1 teaspoon sugar
1 (1/4 ounce) package dry active yeast
1 cup tepid water (105-110 degrees)
3.5 cups flour
4 Tablespoons sugar
1.5 Tablespoons Shortening
2 Tablespoons Milk
1 Egg
1 Teaspoon Salt
1.5 Tablespoons Melted Margarine

Directions:
Directions:
1 Combine the teaspoon of sugar and yeast in tepid water(107) and wait 10 minutes until foamy.
2 Using electric mixer throughly mix milk, butter, sugar, egg and salt in with yeast mixture.
4 Mix in separate bowl, the flour, sugar and shortening.  Shortening should be broken into small pieces before adding into the flour/sugar.
3. Add flour mixture to yeast mixture and use electric mixer for first few cups of flour.  Once into a ball remove from bowl and continue to knead for 4 minutes.  Let rise for 1 hour.
4.  Punch down dough and add melted margarine.  Roll out and break into pieces.  Roll pieces into balls and put 2 in each cup after spraying pan with Pam.
5.  Let rise for 1 to 2 hours.
12 Cover dough loosely with plastic wrap for 45 minutes.
13 Bake rolls in preheated 350 degree oven for 20-25 minutes, or until light brown.
14 Serve as soon as they are cool enough to throw.
