Title:
Fried Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Frozen Broccoli
Olive oil (enough to coat the bottom of your pan)
Crushed red pepper seeds (to suit your taste)
Dash of salt (to suit your taste)

Directions:
Heat oil and red pepper seeds in skillet.
Rinse broccoli off and pat dry with paper towels.
Add broccoli to oil and fry until it starts to get crispy.
Add salt and serve immediately.
