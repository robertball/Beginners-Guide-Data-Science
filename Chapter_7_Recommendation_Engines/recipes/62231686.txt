Title:
Vermicelli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups vermicelli noodles, broken into 2" pieces
3 teaspoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup salsa
1 can condensed tomato soup
4 cups beef broth
cheddar cheese, to garnish

Directions:
Heat oil in a large soup pot over medium low heat. Stir in noodle pieces and cook until browned and puffed. Remove from pan; set aside.
Add onion to pan, adding more oil if necessary. Cook, stirring occasionally, until soft. Stir in salsa and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Stir in soup and broth. Add back noodles. Simmer, uncovered for 15-20 minutes until noodles are soft. Serve topped with grated cheddar cheese.

Description: 
An easy, slightly spicy soup, perfect for a fall evening.
