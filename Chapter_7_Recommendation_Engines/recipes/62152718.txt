Title:
Simple Chick Pea Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup water
15 oz. can chick peas (garbanzo beans), undrained
15 oz. can tomato sauce
14 oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
3 oz. pkg. beef flavor ramen noodle soup
1 cup chopped fresh spinach
1 cup chopped, cooked ham or chicken (optional)

Directions:
Combine water, chick peas, tomato sauce,  and diced tomatoes in a saucepan. Bring to a boil.
Break ramen noodles into quarters and stir into tomato mixture. Simmer 2 minutes and remove from heat.
Stir in contents of beef flavor packet, spinach, and ham or chicken (if desired). Cover and let stand a few minutes or until thoroughly heated.

Submitter: 
Fran Weidman
Description: 
This is a ridiculously easy and inexpensive recipe that's full of flavor, and perfect for a rainy day. Serve with some fresh bread or rolls, and you've got a hearty meal!
