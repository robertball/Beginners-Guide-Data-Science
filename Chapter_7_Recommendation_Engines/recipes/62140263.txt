Title:
Grampa Dave's "Stay Out of Salt Lake" Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 to 1-1/2 lbs. Ground Chuck (or Round)
(2) 14-1/2 oz. cans diced tomatoes
(5) 15 oz. cans Pinto Beans - drain 2 cans
(2) medium onions - diced
(5) heaping Tbsp. Chili Powder
(1) heaping Tbsp. Cumin
(5) cloves Garlic - minced or crushed
(1) small can chopped green chilis
1/2 tsp. Garlic powder
1/2 tsp. Onion powder
(1) 12 oz. can or bottle of beer
1/2 cup brewed coffee

Directions:
Crumble and cook the beef until almost medium and drain off the liquid
Add the chopped onion and cook, covered, until the onion is translucent
Add the minced garlic, Green Chilis, Garlic powder, Onion powder, Chili powder and Cumin and cook for a couple of minutes
Add the remaining ingredients and cook over low heat for an hour (or more)

Submitter: 
David Dominianni
Description: 
Grampa Dave is at it again with a tried and true recipe for chili that uses beer and coffee as flavorizers. This version of the American favorite is not too hot, but real long on flavor. If you like my Barbeque Sauce, I promise you'll like this, too!
