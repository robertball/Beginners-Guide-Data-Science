Title:
California Turkey Ranch Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground turkey (I use 97% fat free)
Hidden Valley Ranch dried dressing mix (4 oz pack)
2 T Worcestershire
Monterrey Jack cheese slices
avocado, sliced
red onion, sliced
Mayonnaise
Focaccia rolls

Directions:
Mix Worcestershire and ranch dressing into turkey.
Form into 4 patties.
Grill, adding cheese for last 30 seconds.
Spread mayo on both sides of roll.
Add burger, onion. avocado.
Enjoy!

SALLY G
Description: 
Flavorful, moist, turkey burgers that won't have you asking where's the beef?!
