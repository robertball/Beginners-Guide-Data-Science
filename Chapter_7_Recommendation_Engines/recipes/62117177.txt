Title:
Taco Ramen Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 envelope taco seasoning
3/4 C. Salsa
2 1/4 C. Water
2 pkg. Ramen Noodles
Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Brown hamburger until no longer pink. Drain, and return to pan.  Add salsa, taco seasoning, and water.  Bring to a boil. Stir in Ramen. Stir occasionally, separating noodles as they cook. After noodles are soft and sauce has thickened, remove from heat. Top with shredded cheese. Cover and let rest for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ryan Stroud
Description: 
A new idea for Ramen Noodles with a mexican flair.
