Title:
Low-Fat Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices turkey bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 onion, chopped
1/2 yellow pepper, seeded and chopped
2 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 cups whole corn kernels
1/4 cup light cream

Directions:
in a medium saucepan, cook bacon until crisp. Add the onion, bell pepper and 2 tsp water to saucepan, saute until soft. Add broth and potatoes, bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, until potatoes are almost tender, about 15 minutes. Stir in corn, simmer 5 minutes longer. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Transfer 2 cups of the soup to a food processor and puree. Pour back into saucepan, stir in cream and heat to serving temperature. Serve topped with bacon.
