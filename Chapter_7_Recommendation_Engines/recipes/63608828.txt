Title:
Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/8 teaspoon garlic salt
1 small onion
1 (15 oz) can tomatoes
1 lb can tomato sauce
1 teaspoon sugar
1 Tablespoon Italian seasoning
10 ozs lasagna noodles
1 lb. cottage cheese
1/2 C Parmesan cheese
2 beaten eggs
2 t. salt
1/2 t pepper
Mozzarella Cheese (3-4 cups)

Directions:
Brown ground beef; add garlic salt and onion.
Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, sugar and Italian seasoning.
Simmer uncovered at least 1 hour.
Cook lasagna noodles.
Combine cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, salt and pepper.  Mix Well.
Place layer of noodles in 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan.   Spread with layer of cottage cheese mix, then a layer of sliced mozzarella cheese and layer of meat sauce.  Repeat layers.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
