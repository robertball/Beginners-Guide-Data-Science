Title:
Nesto's Beer and Chile Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 garlic clove (mashed)
2.5 cups of saison beer
.5 cup roasted pablano chile - diced finely
1 tbs roasted serrano chile - diced finely
1 tsp lemon juice
1.5 tbsps all-purpose flour
.5 lbs monterey jack - shredded
.5 lbs sharp cheddar - shredded
.5 lbs emmentaler cheese - shredded
.25 lbs aged monterey jack - shredded
3-4 qts cubed french baguette, vegetables and fruit (broccoli, cauliflower, carrot sticks, green apples)

Directions:
Rub sides of fondue pot with smashed garlic clove.
Place garlic and beer in saucepan, and heat over low until simmering.
Increase heat and cook beer and chile mix at a simmer for 10 minutes.
Toss cheeses with the flour.
Add lemon juice first, then whisk cheese into beer and chile mix by the handful, making sure each addition melts before you add the next. Keep at a bare simmer - do NOT boil.
Transfer to a fondue pot and set over a low flame.

Submitter: 
Nesto
Description: 
Saison and pablano chile fondue with just a little serrano chile for some heat.
