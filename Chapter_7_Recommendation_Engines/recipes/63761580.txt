Title:
Aunt Connie's Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1043038.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
4 cups brown sugar
2 cups evaporated milk
2 cups icing sugar
1/2 cup chopped nuts (walnuts)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large sauce pan (dutch oven size)bring to a boil  butter, brown sugar and evaporated milk.
Boil for 10 minutes stirring constantly with a wooden spoon.
Remove from heat.
Stir in icing sugar with electric mixer.
Beat until icing sugar melts and fudge is light in colour.
Stir in nuts and vanilla.
Quickly pour into prepared 9" square pan.
Cool.

Submitter: 
Loretta's Recipe Journal
Description: 
Handed down from Aunt Connie; this is a big favourite of ours, it is so tasty.
You'll want to lick the beaters, the spoons, the pots...It is crumbly to cut into but the crumbs are tasty too.
