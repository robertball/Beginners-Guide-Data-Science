Title:
Cheesy Vegetarian Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves minced garlic
1 can corn, drained
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 can dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
2 Tbsp Taco Seasoning Mix
2 cups salsa
Half bag  of tortilla chips
2 1/2 cups shredded cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Spray 9 x 13 pan. Cook onion and garlic in oil. Add corn, beans, seasoning, and salsa. Stir and cook til warm. Spread half the chips in bottom of pan. Top with half  the bean mixture, then half the cheese. Repeat. Top with more chips. Bake 20 minutes.
