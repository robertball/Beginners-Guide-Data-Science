Title:
Super Cina-Toast Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F3497872.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice whole wheat bread
1 tablespoon buttery spread
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon white sugar
1  rose petal (optional)

Directions:
Toast slice of bread to desired crispness, 2 to 3 minutes. Spread buttery spread across the slice. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar evenly over toast. Decorate with a rose petal.
