Title:
Banana Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1050376.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe bananas
Aprox 1 cup buttermilk
4 1/2 cups (675g) all purpose flour
2 tbsp (25g) baking powder
3/4 tsp (5g) salt
1/4 cup (50g) sugar
1/3 cup (75g) butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400C/200F.
Place bananas in blender/magic bullet.
Fill to 2 cups (500ml) with buttermilk. Blend until smooth.
Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together.
Cut in butter with fingertips until crumbly.
Add banana mixture to flour being careful not to over mix dough.
Turn onto floured countertop/cuttingboard.
Pat dough flat with hands into 1" thick disc. Do not use rolling pin.
Cut dough with 2" biscuit cutter dipped in flour. Place on floured cookie sheet and into oven on top rack.
Bake for 8 - 10 minutes or until golden/hollow sounding when tapped.
Remove from cookie sheet and cool on wire rack.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Shantelle
Description: 
Nothing beats the smell of baked goods with bananas... until you get to eat what's in the oven! These scones are delicious hot and perfect with a pat of butter and some tea!
