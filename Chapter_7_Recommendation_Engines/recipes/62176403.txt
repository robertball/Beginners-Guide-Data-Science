Ingredients:
1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained, liquid reserved or 4 oz. fresh mushrooms
1 green bell pepper, choppe
3 Tbsp. butter
3 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups hot chicken stock or broth
2 cups cooked chicken, chopped
4 ounces chopped pimento

Directions:
Cook and stir mushrooms and green pepper in cooking oil over medium heat for 5 minutes.
Add butter to pan and continue cooking for about 1-2 minutes to reduce water in the butter. Blend in flour, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is bubbly.  Remove from heat.
Slowly stir in milk and stock, stirring continually.  Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.  Boil and stir for 1 minute.
Stir in chicken and pimiento.  Heat through.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
A great way to use leftover chicken or turkey.  Green pepper and red pimientos make this a pretty dish to serve at Christmas or anytime.  Serve over cooked rice or noodles. Can be made ahead and reheated.Modified from: Chicken a la King I
