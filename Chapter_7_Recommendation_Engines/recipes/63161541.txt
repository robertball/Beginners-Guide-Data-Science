Title:
Suzy's Homemade Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 good size apples
1/2 cup water(a little less then 1/2 cup)
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Peel and slice apples in wedges, add water, apples, both sugars and cinnamon to crock-pot give a good stir and cook on low for about 4 to 5 hours. Then mash with a potato masher.

Submitter: 
smonty48
Description: 
Slow cook in the crock-pot. I make this with my grandsons...they love it!
