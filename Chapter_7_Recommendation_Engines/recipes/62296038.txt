Title:
Slow Cooker Pot Roast with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
1 (8 OUNCE) package mushrooms
3/4 cups water
3 pounds chuck roast

Directions:
Salt and Pepper the meat and brown in a skillet sprayed with olive oil or cooking spray. Place meat in the slow cooker. Mix cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix and water. Place on top of roast in slow cooker. Place mushrooms in slow cooker.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This simple pot roast makes its own gravy in the slow cooker.Modified from: Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast
