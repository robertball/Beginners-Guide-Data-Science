Title:
coco-choco-nana muffins! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/633361.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
3 mashed overripe bananas+ 1-2 banana for on top
1/2 cup shredded coconut
1/3 mini chocolate chips

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). place paper liners in muffin pan
in a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. in a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. stir banana mixture into flour mixture, adding coconut and chocolate chips; stir just to moisten. fill muffin liners nearly to the top. slice remaining bananas into thin circles and place 2 or three atop each muffin.
bake in preheated oven for 18 to 22 minutes, until center of muffin springs back when pressed lightly. allow to cool for 5-10 minutes in pan and then cool on wire rack.
