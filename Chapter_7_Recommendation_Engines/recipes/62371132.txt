Title:
Quick Easy Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz can tomato sauce
4 oz diced tomatoes
1 green pepper, diced
2 slices of an onion, diced
1/2 teaspoon garlic
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon paprika
4 slices american cheese

Directions:
Mix everything together and simmer for about an hour.

Submitter: 
Karyn Watson Woodward
Description: 
I usually buy pre-made spaghetti sauce, but was out one night and the boys really wanted spaghetti.  I searched on here but was disappointed that I couldn't find a sauce recipe that wasn't more complicated than I wanted to do.  Here's what I made, and it was a hit.
