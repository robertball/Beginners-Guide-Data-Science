Title:
MiMi's White Chile Delicioso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. skinless, boneless chicken breast 9cut in 1/2" cubes, raw)
1 med. onion chopped
1 1/2 tsp. minced garlic
2 cans great northern beans (rinsed and drained)
1 can chicken broth
2 (4 oz.) cans chopped green chilis
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 c. sour cream
1/2 c. 1/2 and 1/2

Directions:
In large skillet, saute chicken, onion and garlic until chicken is no longer pink.
Place chicken mixture and place in large pan add bean, broth, green chili's, cumin, oregano, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and simmer, stirring occasionally. Simmer at least 30 minutes. Remove from heat, add sour cream and 1/2 and1/2, return to heat and simmer until desired thickness is reached!
