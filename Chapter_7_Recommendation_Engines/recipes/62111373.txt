Title:
Porketta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound boneless sirloin pork roast
6 cloves garlic or more depending on your liking
2 bunches fresh dill
olive oil
salt
pepper

Directions:
cut slits in pork roast
stuff each slit with salt, pepper, garlic clove and a few sprigs of fresh dill
put stuffed roast into a ziploc storage bag
drizzle olive oil over roast
salt and pepper over roast
marinate overnight
before roasting allow roast to come to room temperature
preheat oven to 525 degrees F
place roast on rack in shallow roasting pan
roast for 25 minutes at 525
reduce heat to 325
continue roasting for one hour, 5 minutes
remove from oven
let stand 15-20 minutes
slice roast paper thin, or as thin as is possible
