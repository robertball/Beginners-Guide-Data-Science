Title:
Amazing Dipping Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. crushed red pepper
2 tsp. ground black pepper
2 tsp. dried oregano
2 tsp. dried rosemary
2 tsp. dried basil
2 tsp. parsley flakes
2 tsp. minced garlic
1 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
-Combine all ingredients in a jar or bottle and shake well to combine.
Let sit for at least 1 hour before using.
Serve with fresh-baked bread for dipping.

Submitter: 
Melanie Greer
Description: 
An amazing blend of dried herbs combined with olive oil to dip your favorite bread in.
