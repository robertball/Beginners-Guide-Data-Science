Title:
A - Z   BREAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  Cups Sugar
1  Cup Oil
3  Eggs
1  tsp. Salt
1  tsp. Soda
3  tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
3  tsp Vanilla
3  Cups Flour
2  Cups A - Z

Directions:
Grease and Flour 2 Loaf Pans. Preheat oven to 350. Blend Eggs, Sugar and Oil well. Add Salt and Vanilla. Add choice of A-Z. Mix in Dry Ingredients. Bake 45 Min until done. ( Check doneness with toothpick ) I habe baked some loafs almost 1 hr.

Submitter: 
Rick Falsetta
Description: 
Make 2 loafs of your favorite bread by adding your favorite fruit or vegitable from apples to zucchini.
