Title:
Bleu Cheese-Buffalo Chicken Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 cooked rotisserie chicken breasts from the deli (about 2 cups when shredded)
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
.
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce
8 ounces bleu cheese crumble, divided
.
1 bunch celery, cleaned and cut into 4 inch pieces
1 (8 ounce) box chicken-flavored crackers

Directions:
Shred chicken into bite-sized pieces, discarding skin and bones.
Place chicken and cream cheese in a medium saucepan; stirring constantly, cook over medium heat until the cream cheese is smooth and melted.
Remove from heat and stir in sour cream, Red Hot Sauce and 6 ounces of the bleu cheese.
Transfer the mixture to a mini slow cooker.
Heat on low setting until hot and bubbly.
When ready to serve, top with remaining bleu cheese.
Serve with celery sticks and crackers.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
Rotisserie chicken from the deli and crumbled bleu cheese give a fresh taste to a traditional appetizer.Modified from: Buffalo Chicken Dip
