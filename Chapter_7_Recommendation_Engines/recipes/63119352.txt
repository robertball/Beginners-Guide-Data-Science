Title:
Easy Szechuan Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into strips
3 Tbsp Hoisin sauce
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1/2 cup low sodium chicken broth
2 Tbsp rice vinegar
2 Tbsp sugar
2 teaspoons chile-garlic sauce
1Tbsp canola oil
1 Tbsp minced, peeled fresh ginger
2 or more garlic cloves, minced
1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
2 peeled carrots, thinly sliced on diagonal
1/4 cup unsalted, dry roasted peanuts

Directions:
Combine chicken, 1 tablespoon of Hoisin sauce and one tablespoon of cornstarch in medium bowl.  Stir to coat chicken and set aside.
Combine remaining 2 Tbsps Hoisin sauce, 1 Tbsp cornstarch, the broth, sugar, vinegar,chile-garlic sauce in small bowl and set aside
Heat a wok or non stick skillet over medium high heat until drop of water sizzles in pan.
Swirl in the oil and add the chicken mixture.
Stir fry until almost cooked through, about 3 minutes
Add the garlic and ginger and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds
Add the peppers, carrots and peanuts
Stir fry until crisp tender, about 2 minutes
Add the Hoisin sauce mixture and cook, stirring constantly until the mixture boils and thickens and the chicken is cooked through, about 1 minute
Makes 4 servings

Submitter: 
hhsandy
Description: 
This yummy stir fry is easy to make and party perfect.  Everything can be chopped in advance and cooked quickly.  Serve with white or brown rice.  Great with beef, pork, or tofu for a vegetarian version.
