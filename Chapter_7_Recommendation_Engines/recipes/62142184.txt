Title:
Dipped Fortune Cookie Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/581163.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounce Bakers White Chocolate Baking Squares or Vanilla Almond Bark
12 Fortune cookies

Directions:
Line a baking sheet with wax paper. Melt the chocolate squares in the microwave at 30 second increments until melted. Dip the back half of the fortune cookie into the melted white chocolate and let dry on lined baking sheet. You can easily adjust the amount if you need more or less.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Fortune cookies dipped into white chocolate makes the perfect appetizer for a party or a great dessert after a Chinese dinner.
