Title:
Brussels Sprout Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 oz honey butter
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1 lb shredded or thinly sliced Brussels sprouts
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons white cooking wine

Directions:
Saute' onions in skillet with honey butter until golden and glazed.
Add Brussels sprouts, season with salt and pepper, and cook until crisp-tender.
Finish with wine.

Submitter: 
Pat
Description: 
I was treated to this dish by a St. Louis caterer, Patty Long Catering, and found a similar recipe by the Food Network.  This recipe is modified to my liking.  I hope you will modify to yours. I used a food processor to make this a quick and easy side dish.
