Title:
Impossibly Easy Ham 'n Swiss Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510692.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cubed ham
1 cup shredded swiss cheese
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup Bisquick
1 cup milk
1/4 tsp salt (optional)
1/8 tsp pepper
2 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9-inch pie plate. Sprinkle ham, cheese and onions in pie plate.
Stir Bisquick, milk, salt, pepper and eggs until well blended. Pour into pie plate.
Bake 35-40 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes.

A great way to use up leftover ham!
