Title:
Wedding Kuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough:
3 eggs
1 cup sugar
1 cup cream
6 cups flour
1/2 cup melted shortening
1/2 tsp. salt
3 tsp. baking powder
Topping:
3 eggs
2 Tablespoons melted butter
1 1/2 cups cream
Sprinkle:
2 cups white sugar
3/4 cups brown sugar
4 or more crushed graham crackers
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all dough ingredients together to make a soft dough for easy handling. Pat in pans.
Spread with 4-5 Tablespoons of Topping mixture in each pan.
Sprinkle evenly sugar and cracker mixture over topping.
Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
