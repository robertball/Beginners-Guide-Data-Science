Title:
California Turkey Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3995626.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package JENNIE-O® Ground Turkey
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
4 slices Havarti cheese
1 cup arugula leaves
1 large tomato, sliced
1 avocado, sliced
4 small brioche or onion roll hamburger buns, toasted

Directions:
Heat grill to 400 degrees F.
Gently combine ground turkey, salt and pepper. Shape into 4 patties.
Cook turkey patties as specified on the package. Always cook to well done, 165 degrees F as measured by a meat thermometer.
Serve burgers topped with cheese, arugula, tomato and avocado.

Submitter: 
JennieO
Description: 
A big, juicy turkey burger topped with fresh avocado, thick-sliced tomato and melted cheese? Yes, please! It's no wonder thousands in L.A. agree that this burger tastes great made with turkey.
