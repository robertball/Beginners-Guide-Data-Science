Title:
Fried Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979511.jpg
Ingredients:
12 TysonÂ® Fresh Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast Tenders
1 cup buttermilk
1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
1 1/2 cups self-rising flour
3/4 teaspoon salt
vegetable oil for frying
1 (10 ounce) package romaine, endive, carrot, and radicchio salad mix
2 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
1/2 cup Ranch dressing

Directions:
Combine buttermilk and pepper sauce in pie plate. Mix flour and salt on waxed paper. Dip chicken into buttermilk, then coat with flour; repeat once.
Heat 1/4 inch oil in large nonstick or cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken, in batches, 5 minutes, or until deep golden and juices run clear when thickest part of chicken is pierced. Transfer to paper towels to drain.
Toss salad mix and tomatoes with dressing in large shallow bowl until evenly coated. Top with chicken.

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods
Description: 
Chicken tenders are dipped into buttermilk and coated with flour before being skillet fried until crispy. Toss a bag of salad greens and tomatoes with ranch dressing and top with the chicken.
