Title:
Chocolate Frosting/Ganache Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup cocoa
1 cup white sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Mix cocoa and sugar in a sauce pan. Add vanilla, butter and milk. Boil for 1 minute, then set aside to cool. Spread/ice while warm.
