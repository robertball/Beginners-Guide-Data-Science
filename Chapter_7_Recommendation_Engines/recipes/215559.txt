Title:
Gourmet Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988502.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices toasted pumpernickel, rye or black bread
6 chopped hard-boiled, free-run eggs
1/3 cup chopped celery, with leaves
1/3 cup chopped chives
1/3 cup finely chopped dill pickle
1/3 cup finely chopped arugula
2 tablespoons Hellmann's® 1/2 the Fat mayonnaise-type dressing
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix everything in a bowl and serve between 8 toasted slices of pumpernickel, rye or black bread.

Submitter: 
Hellmann's®
Description: 
Chopped chives, dill pickle and arugula with a dab of Dijon mustard give the gourmet treatment to this egg salad sandwich on hearty pumpernickel bread.
