Title:
Quick Triple Berry Frozen  Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup water
2 cups heaping,frozen triple berries from Costco
1 cup plain Greek yogurt - non-sweetened
3/4 cup non-fat milk

Directions:
Combine the sugar and water is small sauce pan and bring to a boil. Put berries in a blender, pour hot simple syrup over berries,stir.  Add yogurt and milk.  Blenderize the mix.  Pour mix into prepared ice cream maker.  Process until desired consistency.
Spoon into air tight container and freeze for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Debra Cadiente
Description: 
My first attempt at ice cream with my new Kitchen Aid ice cream bowl.  Worked out awesome.  Light on the fats and calories.
