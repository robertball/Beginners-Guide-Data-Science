Title:
Dog Food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3# gd beef
1# beef liver
2 cups white or brown rice
3 hard boiled eggs (with shell)
1/2# carrots
1 large yam
16 oz frozen peas
2 Tb apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 tsp powdered garlic (optional)

Directions:
Grind liver in food processor. Shred carrots, yam in food processor. Grind hard boiled eggs in food processor, shell and all.
Combine all ingredients in pan and brown until cooked.
Pack into pint jars with 1" head room and fill with beef stock. Heat in pressure canner for 70 minutes at 10#.
