Title:
Hawaiin Pineapple Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crushed pineapple
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup light soy sauce
6 each chicken breast

Directions:
In a small saucepan over medium heat, mix crushed pineapple, brown sugar, and soy sauce. Remove from heat just before the mixture comes to a boil.
Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill chicken breast. Place chicken breast in pan and pour pineapple mixture over cooked chicken breast. Garnish with mandarin orange slices.

Submitter: 
Scott Stivers
Description: 
Grilled Chicken Breast topped with a tropical mixture of crushed pineapple, brown sugar and soy sauce. 
Garnished with Mandarin Oranges on top.Modified from: Pineapple Chicken Tenders
