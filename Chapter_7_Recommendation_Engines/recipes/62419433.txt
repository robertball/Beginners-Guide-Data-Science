Title:
Salisbury Steak a la Sarah Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 egg
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder or to taste
1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
2 pkg Liptons Onion-Mushroom Dry soup mix
4 tbsp all-purpose flour
1 jar sliced mushroom

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together 1/3 cup condensed French onion soup with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg,black pepper,dry mustard and garlic powder. Shape into 6 oval patties.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown both sides of patties. Pour off excess fat, if any.
In a saucepan,blend flour and dry soup mix and stir in three 1/2 cups water, rest of can soup and jar of mushrooms (with liquid) and mix til smooth.Bring to a boil and simmer 5 min. til thickened.  Pour over meat in skillet. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to a simmer. cover and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Description: 
Ground beef is mixed with condensed onion soup, bread crumbs, and egg, then formed into patties which are browned and simmered in a sauce made with more soup and seasonings.Modified from: Salisbury Steak
