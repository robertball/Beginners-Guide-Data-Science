Title:
Baileys Mudslide Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976916.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1/4 ounce Godiva® Chocolate Liqueur

Directions:
Pour Baileys(R) Irish Cream, Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, and Godiva(R) Chocolate Liqueur into a shaker.
Shake the cocktail and serve over ice or blend with ice.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Adding a touch of richness to any drink, Baileys® Irish cream liqueur mixes fine Irish whiskey, fresh dairy cream and a hint of chocolate flavors.
