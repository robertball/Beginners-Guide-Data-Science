Title:
Spicy Asian Marinating Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c Tamari (reduced-sodium soy) sauce
1/4 c water
1/4 c sesame oil
1/4 c rice vinegar
1/4 c brown mustard
1/2 t chili oil
1/8 t smoked salt
ground garlic, to taste
black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Whisk until well-blended.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
Great as a tempeh marinade, but can also be used otherwise.  When the item is done marinating, be sure to rinse it with water to remove excess sodium.
