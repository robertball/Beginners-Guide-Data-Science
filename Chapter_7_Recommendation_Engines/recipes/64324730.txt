Title:
Beef Bourguignon II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 pound sirloin strip steaks, cut into 2 inch lengths
4 Tbs Earth Balance
1 onion, chopped
1 bunch fresh carrots, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups red wine
1 cup beef broth
1 bay leaf
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
8 oz fresh cremini mushrooms, sliced
1 (16 ounce) can canned onions, optional

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the flour, salt and ground black pepper. Coat the beef cubes with this mixture.
Melt the butter or margarine in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the meat and brown well on all sides. Pour this into a 2 quart casserole dish.
Return the skillet to the heat and add the onion, carrots. Saute for 5 to 10 minutes, or until onion is tender add the micro planed garlic for the last minute. add the wine, bay leaf, parsley and thyme. Pour over meat.
Bake, covered, at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 2 1/2 hours. Remove cover, add mushrooms, bake for 30 more minutes.

Description: 
Beef is browned and braised in red wine with carrots, garlic,  onions, and herbs. Mushrooms are added for the last half hour of cooking.Modified from: Beef Bourguignon II
