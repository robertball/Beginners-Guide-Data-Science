Title:
Creamy pumpkin curry soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T butter, 1 onion-chopped, 1 clove garlic-minced, 1 tsp fresh ginger-minced, 2 tsp yellow curry, 1 ½ T flour, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper, 1 (14oz)can chicken broth, 1 cup water, 1 (15oz)can pumpkin puree, 1 (13.5oz)can coconut milk, ¼ C cream, 1-2 T sugar (to taste)

Directions:
1) Melt butter in large pot and sauté onion until soft, about 5 minutes.  Add garlic, ginger, curry powder, flour, salt & pepper and cook 1 minute more.
2) Stir in broth, water and pumpkin, mixing well.
3) Puree soup in blender until smooth.  Pour back into pot and cook on low for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4) Stir in coconut milk, cream and sugar.  Heat through and serve.

Submitter: 
Trina
Description: 
This easy, satisfying soup combines curry, ginger and coconut milk with canned pumpkin to give both a spicy warmth and a subtle sweetness. For thicker soup, add more flour. To cut calories: Use light coconut milk and omit or substitute the cream with half & half.
