Title:
Super Easy Slow Cooker Ham and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777195.jpg
Ingredients:
1 48 oz. Jar great northern beans (NOT dry beans)
1 lb. Chopped cooked ham
Onions to taste

Directions:
Add beans and ham into slow cooker.  Stir.  Cook on low for 3 Hours.  Top with onions as a garnish.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
Ready in a slow cooker in 3 hours.  Super duper easy.  Add as much or little ham as you want.  Top with onions to taste.  Server with Grandmother's Buttermilk Cornbread from this site.
