Title:
Chunky Milk Chocolate Pecan Pie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup finely chopped pecans
FILLING:
3 large eggs
3/4 cup corn syrup
3/4 cup granulated sugar
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 (11.5 ounce) package NESTLE® TOLL HOUSE® Milk Choocolate Morsels
1 1/2 cups coarsely chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
FOR CRUST:  BEAT flour, butter and brown sugar in small mixer bowl until crumbly. Add chopped pecans and stir.  Press into prepared baking pan. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
FOR FILLING:  BEAT eggs, corn syrup, granulated sugar, butter and vanilla extract in medium bowl with wire whisk. Stir in morsels and nuts. Pour evenly over baked crust. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until set. Cool in pan on wire rack.
