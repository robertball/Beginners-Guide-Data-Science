Title:
Mom's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  Sirlion Steak
4 stalks of celery
2 med size potatoes
1 can stewed tomatoes
1 tablespoon Ketchup
3 tablespoon of Worcestershire
1/2 onion
Pinch of sugar
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon salt
Water to cover veggies
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Start the sirloin in butter with the onions until the meat is brown then add the water, stewed tomatoes, ketchup, salt, pepper, Worcestershire, pinch of sugar and veggies. Add enough water to cover the veggies. Cook in the pressure cooker. When you hear the "diddle" turn in down slightly and cook for an addition 15 mins. Take off the lid and cool the lid with water in the sink before opening it up.
