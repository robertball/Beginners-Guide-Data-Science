Title:
Sweet Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 sweet potatoes
2 cups brown sugar
2 # of butter
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 2 quart casserole dish.
Cut potatoes in half lengthwise
Melt butter and place in casserole dish
sprinkle sugar and salt and pepper in bottom coat generously with sugar
place potatoes flesh side down in dish
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
