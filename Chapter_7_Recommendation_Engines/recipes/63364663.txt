Title:
Party Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup salsa
2 1/2 cups cheese, shredded (Jack, Muenster or cheddar)
8 (10-inch) flour tortillas
Olive Oil

Directions:
1. Combine mayonnaise, salsa and cheese.  Spread mixture over 4 tortillas and cover with remaining 4 tortillas.
2. Brush tops with olive oil and brown under the broiler for about one minute. Turn, brush with more olive oil and brown the other side.
3. Cut into wedges and serve. (Quesadillas may be prepared a day ahead and refrigerated, then broiled just before serving.
