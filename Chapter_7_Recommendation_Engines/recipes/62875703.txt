Title:
Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. extra lean ground beef
1 packet taco seasoning
1 cup water
2 cups salsa
1 (19 oz/796 ml) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup corn niblets (frozen or canned)
6 small tortillas, halved
2 cups shredded cheese
Â¼ cup chopped green onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Brown beef in skillet. Drain. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in salsa, black beans and corn. Remove from heat.
Place half of meat mixture in a 8 or 9 inch square baking dish. Top with half of the tortillas, overlapping where necessary. Sprinkle with half of the cheese. Repeat layer and sprinkle with green onion.
Bake uncovered until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 15-20 minutes.
