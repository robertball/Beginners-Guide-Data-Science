Title:
Scallops in Honey and Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. Scallops cut in half (if small) or in quarters (if large)
1 cup dry bread or cracker crumbs
1/4 lb. melted butter
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 Tbsp. honey

Directions:
Rinse and dry scallops.
Roll scallops in crumbs and divide equally among six individually greased baking dishes or ramekins.
Combine honey, butter and oil.
Drizzle mixture evenly over each portion of scallops.
Bake at 475 degrees for 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Baumannowski
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by a local seafood merchant and I have used it many times over the years.  It is a very simple and delicious way to serve scallops.
