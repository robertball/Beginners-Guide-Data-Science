Title:
Slow Cooker Mesquite Chipotle Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 lbs 99% extra lean ground turkey breast
1 TBL olive oil
1 TBL dry mesquite grill seasoning [Such as McCormick's Grill Mates Mesquite Seasoning]
1/2 large sweet onion, chopped
2 (15 oz) cans black beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 oz) can red beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 oz) can corn, drained
2 (10 oz) cans diced tomatoes and chilies
2 cups chicken broth
1 bag  microwavable mixed frozen vegetables (without sauce) (optional)
2 tsp chipotle chili powder (adjust to taste)
2 TBL chili powder
1 TBL onion powder
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp oregano

Directions:
Sprinkle turkey with the mesquite seasoning.  In a skillet add olive oil and then add the turkey and brown until no longer pink.
Add browned turkey into a slow cooker.  Saute chopped onion in the same skillet as the turkey.  Add onions and remaining ingredients to slow cooker.  Cook on low for 6 - 8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.   Top individual servings with cheese and chips (optional) or serve with cornbread.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
The combination of the smoky mesquite and chipotle chili seasonings give a great flavor to this healthy turkey chili.  The vegetables add color and boost the nutrition.  It is great on cold days or while cheering on your favorite team.  It is delicious topped with cheese and onions and served with cornbread or corn chips.
