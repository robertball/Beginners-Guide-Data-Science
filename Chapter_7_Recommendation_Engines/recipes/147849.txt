Ingredients:
1/4 cup honey, warm
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 cups All-Bran速 Original
2 cups raisins

Directions:
Combine honey and oil and drizzle over Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal. Toss to moisten. Spread evenly on baking sheet.
Bake at 325 degrees F about 15 minutes. Stir in raisins and bake 5 minutes longer. Cool. Store in airtight container.
