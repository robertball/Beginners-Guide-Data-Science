Title:
Meat balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lbs Fresh market ground beef
2 extra large onions, finely chopped, or 3 large
6 tbl salsa mix
2 cans tomato sauce
2 tbl garlic powder
2 tbl parsley
2 tbl parmesan cheese

Directions:
mix all together and bake at 350 for 30 min.  Make meatballs with 1/4 cup measuring cup.
