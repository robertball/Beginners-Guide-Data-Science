Title:
Five Spice Asian Sticky Chicken (or five spice sauce) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c avocado oil
1/4 c coconut aminos
1/2-1 tsp fish sauce
1-2 tsp rice vinegar
1/2-1 lime, juiced
1 T five spice
1 tsp garlic powder
1 -2 garlic cloves
1 tsp fresh ginger
salt
red chili flakes
used 1/2 rotisserie chicken, cut into pieces

Directions:
combine all with hand blender
brush onto chicken
bake for 30 min
