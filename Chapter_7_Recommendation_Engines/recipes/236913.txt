Title:
French Fry Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520208.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons garlic salt
2 teaspoons onion salt
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons ground paprika

Directions:
Whisk garlic salt, onion salt, salt, and paprika together in a bowl. Transfer to a sealable container for storage.

Submitter: 
S.RalstoninGA
Description: 
This simple mixture is great to keep on hand to sprinkle over hot French fries! Store in an airtight container between uses.
