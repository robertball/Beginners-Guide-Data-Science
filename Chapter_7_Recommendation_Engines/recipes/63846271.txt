Title:
Vegetable Quiche Cups to Go Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10oz Package Frozen Spinach
3/4 Cup Liquid Egg Substitute
3/4 Cup shredded reduced-fat cheese
1/4 Cup diced green bell peppers
1/4 cup diced onions
3 drops hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Microwave spinach on high 2 1/2 minutes.  Drain excess liquid.
Line a 12 cup muffin pan with foil backing cups.
spray the cups with cooking spray.
Combine egg-substitute, cheese peppers, onions and spinach in a bowl.  Mix well.  Divide evenly among the muffin cups.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes until knife in center comes out clean.
Can be frozen and reheated in microwave.  Any Combination of appropriate veg and cheeses can be used.
