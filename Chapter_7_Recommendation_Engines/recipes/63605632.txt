Title:
Mexican Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 1/2 Cups Quinoa (cooked)
1 cup frozen corn
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup chopped cilantro (more if you are using it for garnish)
Dressing:
1/2 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons honey
Juice from 1 lime (or about 1/4 cup lime juice)
6 slices pickled jalapeno peppers, chopped (from a jar)
1/4 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Toss all salad ingredients in a bowl together, then add dressing and toss again. Garnish with extra cilantro on top if desired.
