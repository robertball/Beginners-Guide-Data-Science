Title:
Beef & Macaroni Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
3 Cups Water
1 14.5oz Can Petite diced Tomatoes (un-drained)
4 oz. Tomato Sauce
1 Cup Small Elbow Macaroni or Shells
1 1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Onion Powder
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1 tsp. Italian Seasoning
1/8 tsp. Cayenne Pepper
1 tsp. Worcesetershire Sauce
2 tsp. Sugar
2T flour
3T Water
Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Directions:
Brown the ground beef in a large skillet, drain fat.  Add water and the next 10 ingredients.  Cover with a lid and simmer for 8 minutes or until macaroni is done.
Mix flour with water until there are no lumps; stir into beef & pasta until mixed well.  Let sit a few minutes until thickened.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
Not exactly sophisticated but when you need a quick and easy meal like the one in the box, dinner can be ready in about 20 minutes.  Good served with a green salad and garlic bread.
