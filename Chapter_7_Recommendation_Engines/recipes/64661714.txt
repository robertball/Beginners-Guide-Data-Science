Title:
Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups egg noodles, prepare per package directions
1/4 chopped onion
Oil or butter for sauté
1-1/2 cups beef broth
1 teaspoon dried parsley flakes
3/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon salt or season salt
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 cup Milk
1/2 cup sour cream
Packaged meatballs or beef tips, cooked

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions
Sauté onion in oil
Add broth and seasonings, bring to boil, cook 5-7 minutes until liquid reduces to 1/2 c
Add milk, simmer covered 3-5 minutes.
Add sour cream, mix well
Add cooked meat or meatballs and cooked noodles, bring to temperature.
