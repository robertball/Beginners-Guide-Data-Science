Title:
Easy Whipped Pudding Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/928987.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. (1 tub) whipped topping
3.4 oz (1 package) vanilla pudding mix
1 cup cold milk
2 tsp corn starch

Directions:
Empty pudding mix into medium bowl. Pour in cold milk and whisk until the mixture thickens. Fold in whipped topping and mix well.
Easy as pie! Feel free to use any flavor of pudding! Enjoy!
