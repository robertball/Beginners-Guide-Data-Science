Title:
Baked Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups red potatoes, cut bite size
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup bread crumbs
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Prepare baking sheet with cooking spray (well coated).
Toss potatoes & melted butter to evenly coat.  Mix together the dry ingredients;; add to the potatoes and stir. Spread the potato wedges in single layers on the baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until browned, occasionally moving the sheets around to assure even baking, about 30-45 minutes.
