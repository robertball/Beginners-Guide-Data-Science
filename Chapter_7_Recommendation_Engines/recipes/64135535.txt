Title:
Stuffed Pepper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1349281.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb ground sirloin 90% lean
2 onions chopped
2 cups chopped green pepper
2 14.5. oz. cans diced tomatoes with garlic and herbs
2 15 oz cans tomato sauce
1 Tblsp Better than  Bouillon  Beef Base
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 qts water
15 oz V-8 juice
1 cup uncooked long grain white rice

Directions:
Brown ground sirloin with onion. Add chopped green peppers. Saut√© till peppers are softened. Add tomatoes, sauce,seasonings, juice and water. Add rice and bring to a boil. Turn down heat and continue to cook until rice is done. Reduce heat to simmer. Add a little water if too thick. Cooks in approximately 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Paula S.
Description: 
Reminds me of my grandma's wonderful stuffed green peppers, but with a lot less effort!
