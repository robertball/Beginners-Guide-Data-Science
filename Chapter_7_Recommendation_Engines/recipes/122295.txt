Title:
Golden Burger Spirals Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/841199.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green pepper, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed golden mushroom soup, undiluted
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1/2 teaspoon salt
8 ounces spiral pasta, cooked and drained

Directions:
In a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat, cook beef, onion and green pepper until the meat is no longer pink; drain. Add the soup, tomato sauce, 1 cup cheese and salt. Stir in pasta. Transfer to a greased 2-1/2-qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Lisa  Sinyard
Description: 
'This recipe was given to me by a fellow dental hygienist nearly 20 years ago,' relates Lisa Sinyard if Lexington, Alabama. 'After a busy day, it's a convenient meal the whole family enjoys. It's also an excellent dish to take to potlucks and church socials,' she adds.
