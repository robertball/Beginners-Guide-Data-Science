Title:
Pammy's Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz orange juice
8 oz pomegranate juice
8 oz sparkling lemonade or limeade
1.5 - 2 bottles of red wine
2 oz lemon juice
1 Tbs sugar
Pinch of cinnamon
3 cups diced fresh fruit (apples, oranges, lemons,and/or papaya)

Directions:
If using papaya, peel before dicing.  Pour all ingredients into a large container and stir.  You may enjoy immediately or keep in the refrigerator for up to a week.  Serve over ice.
