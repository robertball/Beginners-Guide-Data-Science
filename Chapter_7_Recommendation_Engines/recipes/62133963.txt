Title:
Sour Cream Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 cup butter
2 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
¼ tsp. salt
1 ¼ tsp. Baking Powder
For Topping:	1 cup chopped nuts
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup sugar

Directions:
Cream sugar and butter, add eggs, sour cream and vanilla, blend well.  Sift together flour, salt and Baking Powder, add to above mixture.  Continue mixing until well blended.  Pour ½ the mixture into a 13 X 9” pan, sprinkle ½ of the topping over.  Pour rest of batter on top, sprinkle remaining topping over batter.  Bake at 325° for 45-50 minutes.
