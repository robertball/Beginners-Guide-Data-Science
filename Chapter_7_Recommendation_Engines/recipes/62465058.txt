Title:
Aromatic Cream Cheese Apple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/474612.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon Cinnamon
1 cup peeled, diced tart apples
1/2 cup bran flakes cereal
TOPPING:
1/4 cup Brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 tablespoon Butter (Softened)
2 tablespoons A P Flour

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream together the cream cheese, sugar and butter.  Then add the eggs, followed by the rest of the wet ingredients, milk, lemon juice and vanilla; beat until smooth. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; stir into cream cheese mixture just until moistened. Fold in apples and bran flakes.
Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full. Combine topping ingredients; sprinkle over batter. Bake at 375 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.
