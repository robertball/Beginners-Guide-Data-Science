Title:
South American Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs beef chuck roast
2 carrots, peeled and chopped
2 jalapenos, chopped
2 medium peppers, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
4 medium radishes, tops removed and cut in half
1-2 sweet potatoes, chunked
1 can pineapple chunks
1 can (14.5oz) stewed tomatoes
2 cups frozen corn
1 can coconut milk
1/4 cup canola oil
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp ground cumin
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in frying pan over med-high heat.
Season roast with garlic, cumin, salt and pepper and brown on both sides
Remove roast from pan and put in slow cooker with the rest of the vegetables and ingredients for 8hrs on low

Submitter: 
Casey McMahon
Description: 
Easy to start in the morning and let in sit in the slow cooker for 8 hours and forget about it!
