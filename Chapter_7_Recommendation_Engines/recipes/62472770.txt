Title:
Tortellini Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium zucchini, sliced
2 brown onions, sliced and quartered
1 (15 1/2 oz.) can garbanzo beans, undrained
1 (16 oz.) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 1/2 cups chicken stock
3/4 cup dry vermouth or water
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. dried basil
1 bay leaf
Salt and pepper to taste
12 oz. evaporated milk
1 package mini tortellinis (7-10 oz.); cooked according to directions and drained
2 oz. grated Romano cheese
4-6 oz. grated Jack cheese

Directions:
Bring first 11 ingredients to a boil in a large, heavy soup pot.
Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Add the evaporated milk and stir.
Add the tortellini.
Place some jack cheese in the bottom of each soup bowl.
Ladle soup into bowls and serve topped with a sprinkle of Romano cheese.
