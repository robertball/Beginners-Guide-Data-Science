Title:
Creamy Chicken Enchilladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 4 oz. can diced green chiles
1/2 tsp. salt
1 large cube chicken bullion
1 c. tomato puree
10 flour tortillas
2 c. cooked cubed chicken
2 c. heavy cream
4 oz. grated Monterey Jack
Vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
1.  Simmer the onion until it is translucent and soft.
2.  Add garlic, tomato puree, chiles, chicken, and salt.  Stir occassionally for 8 minutes and set aside.
3.  Heat cream with bullion cube in shallow pan and set aside.
4. Fry tortillas in hot vegetable oil to a slight crisp without browning.
5.  Dip the tortillas in the cream sauce, add a small amount of filling, and roll.
6.  Place rolls in greased 9x13 pan.
7.  Pour the remaining cream mixture over the enchilladas and top with cheese.
8.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
LISA FRIEND
Description: 
These are creamy enchilladas that still have a crispy texture to them.  The combination of cream sauce and tomatoes is perfect!
