Title:
Tasty tuna salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074018.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boiled eggs, 2 cans tuna in oil, 1/2 cup sweet relish, 1/2 roma tomato diced, 2 tbsp sugar, 1/2 cup miracle whip

Directions:
1. Boil eggs for about 15 minutes
2. Drain Tuna
3. Mix tuna, eggs, relish, tomato, sugar and miracle whip in bowl and chop tuna and eggs with spoon and stir until well mixed together.
4. serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Eva Maria
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe that is light and filling and extremely versatile.
