Title:
Caribbean Curried Rice and Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729647.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tsp.butter
1 cup uncooked basmati rice
1/2 cup uncooked orange or red lentils
3 1/4 cups water
3 tsp.chicken bouillon
4 Tbsp.sliced green onions
2 tsp. curry powder
1/4 tsp.salt
1/4 tsp.garlic powder
3-5 drops hot pepper sauce
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet over med.high heat.
Add rice and lentils.Cook stirring for 2 min.
Combine water and bouillon;add to rice mixture along with all remaining ingredients,except frozen peas.
Bring to a boil;reduce heat to low and cook covered for 15 min. or untill moisture is absorbed.
Toss in frozen peas;turn off heat and cover for 3 min.Gently fold peas and rice together.
