Title:
Pineapple Grilled Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
1/4 c brown sugar
1/4 c soy sauce
1/4 tsp garlic powder
one small can of pineapple slice in juice
pinch of pepper

Directions:
place  sugar, soy sauce, garlic powder, and juice from pineapples in a bag and mix together,add chops. Marinate over night. grill and brush chops with marinate often. grill to brown pineapple and serve with pineapple on top of chops. yummy! chicken breast can be subsituted for chops just avoid basting with marinate

Submitter: 
jhopkins
Description: 
are a delightful and quick meal for the grill. i usually serve with a baked potato but my daughter loves them with wild rice.
