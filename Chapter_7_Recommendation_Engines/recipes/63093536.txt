Title:
Kimberly's Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
4 eggs
1 ¼ c. oil
2 c. sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
2 c. flour
2 tsp. soda
2 tsp. powder
½ tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
3 c. grated carrots
Frosting:
½ c. butter, softened
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
4 c. powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. chopped pecans (sometimes I use these and sometimes I’m not in the mood!)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9”X13” pan.
Beat together eggs, oil, white sugar and 2 tsp vanilla.
Mix in flour, soda, powder, salt and cinnamon.
Stir in carrots.
Pour into pan.
Bake for 40-50 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes then turn onto wire rack and cool completely.
Frosting:
Combine butter, cream cheese, powdered sugar and 1 tsp. vanilla.
Beat until smooth and creamy.
Frost cooled cake.
Sprinkle pecans on top.
