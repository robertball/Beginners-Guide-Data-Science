Title:
Hot Buttered Rum Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pints vanilla ice cream
3 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 Tbsp vanilla extract
1lb dark brown sugar
1lb powdered sugar
1lb butter
2 beaten eggs
1/4 tsp cloves
1/4 tsp allspice

Directions:
Blend with mixer and store in Tupperware in the freezer.  Add 2 heaping tablespoons to boiling water.  Don't forget to add the rum (to your taste)!
