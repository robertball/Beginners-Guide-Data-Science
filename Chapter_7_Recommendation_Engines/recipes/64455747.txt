Title:
Faux Taco Via' Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Medium White Onion, quarted
1/2 Cup Sliced Pickled Jalapenos, drained
2 Garlic Cloves
1/2 Cup Cilantro
1 Tbsp Chili Powder
1/2 Tbsp Ground Cumin
1 Tbsp Ground Black Pepper
1 Tbsp Ancho Chili Powder or other Mild Mexican Chili such as Guajillo
1 Pinch Chipolte Pepper Flakes
1.5 Tbsp Salt
2 Tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
2 Tbsp Ketchup
1 28oz. Can Crushed Tomatoes

Directions:
Put the Onion,Jalapenos, Garlic, Cilantro, Chili Powder, Cumin, Black Pepper, Ancho Chili Powder, Chipolte Pepper Flakes, Salt, Lime Juice and Ketchup in a food processor and blend until all ingredients form a paste.  Add the can of Crushed Tomatoes and blend until smooth.  Store in the refrigerator.  Serve with your favorite tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Lisa Ermgodts
Description: 
My kids love Taco Via' especially their sauce and Nacho!  Add this sauce to a squeeze bottle then top a tostada with shredded Colby cheese and this sauce and some sliced pickled Jalapenos, broil or microwave until the cheese is melted and Yum!
