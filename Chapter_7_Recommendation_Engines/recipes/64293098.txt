Title:
Amber's Cilantro Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1937288.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup lime juice
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1/2 bunch of cilantro
1 tablespoon honey
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
3/4 teaspoon spicy brown mustard
1/2 avocado
3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Blend the lime juice, vinegar, and cilantro in a blender on high for 30 seconds or until smooth.
Add the honey, garlic, and salt and blend on high for another 30 seconds, or until smooth.
Add the brown mustard and avocado and blend on low while drizzling the oil for 30 seconds or until well mixed.

Submitter: 
Amber LeAnn Lee
Description: 
This is a delicious variation of another dressing recipe I tried. This one substitutes white wine vinegar for white vinegar, honey for brown sugar, and sea salt for regular table salt.
