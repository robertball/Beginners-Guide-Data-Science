Title:
Herb Crusted Haddock Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound haddock or cod, fresh
1 sea salt, plus more
3/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper, plus more
2 large eggs
1/2 cup breadcrumbs (paleo, gluten free or italian style)
3 teaspoons Italian seasonings
1-2 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
1 14 ounce tomato puree
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions:
Take the haddock or cod and cut into 3 inch slices.  Season both sides with salt and pepper. Whisk the eggs in one large bowl and add the breadcrumbs with Italian seasonings in another bowl or large plate.
Take a large saut√© pan braising pan over medium-high heat.  Add in oil and allow to get very hot.  Dip each piece of haddock into the egg then dredge into herb breadcrumbs.  Add to the pan and cook each side for 3 minutes (until golden brown).
Add tomatoes and garlic to the pan and let cook for about 2 minutes then pour in tomato puree then basil and garlic.  Cover and cook on medium for 7-10 minutes.  Taste the sauce for any additional salt or pepper.  Go ahead and add in a few red pepper flakes!
Serve right out of the pan!
