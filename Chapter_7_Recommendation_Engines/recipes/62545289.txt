Title:
Pumpkin Crunch Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie
1 Graham Cracker Crust
15 oz. can Pumpkin Puree
3 eggs
1/2 C Heavy Cream
1/2 C Honey
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
pinch of cloves
pinch of nutmeg
pinch of salt
Topping
1/2 C Sliced Almonds
1 C Coconut Flakes
1/4 Honey
2 Tablespoons Butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. In medium bowl, mix, pumpkin, eggs, cream, honey and spices. Pour into pie crust and bake 50 to 60 minutes - until almost completely set in the middle. Near the end of the pie cooking time, melt butter in a sauce pan, stir in honey until mixed and then add coconut and almonds and mix. When pie is almost set in the center, add the topping, spreading evenly and bake for another 10 to 15 minutes, until top is golden brown. Remove and cool completely before serving.

Classic pumpkin pie with a deliciously sweet topping!
