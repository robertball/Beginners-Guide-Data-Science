Title:
Green Chili Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or cubed
1 cup salsa verde
3/4 cup sour cream
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1 pkg corn tortillas
2 cups of Mexican blend cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Combine chicken, 1 cup salsa verde and sour cream in a large bowl and season with salt and pepper, cumin and chili powder.
Spread 1 layer of chicken mixture in a baking dish and cover with corn tortillas, then cover with a layer of cheese.
Repeat with second layer of chicken mixture and cheese.
Cover dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for another 10-15 minutes, or until cheese id melted and bubbly.
Remove from oven and serve hot.
