Title:
Potato Salad - Italian Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large potatoes
1 small red onion
2 large red tomotoes
1/4 to 1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1 tbsp. chopped garlic
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel and cube potatoes. Boil potatoes unil tender. Drain and set aside to slightly cool.
Dice onion. Cube tomatoes. Mix all ingredients together. Refrigerate to chill.

Submitter: 
Anna B
Description: 
My Grandmother and Mom used to make this wonderful salad all the time. It's a great tasting alternative to the old mayo based salad. One of my families summer favorites.
