Title:
Vegetarian Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (14 shells) manicotti
3 cups cottage cheese
1 cup parmesan cheese
2 tbsp. fresh parsley
1 egg
2 tbsp. finely diced onion
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. fresh ground black pepper
Sauce:
3 cups tomato sauce
1/2 cup diced onion
2 cloves minced garlic
2 tsp. olive oil
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. dried basil
salt & pepper

Directions:
Prepare sauce by sauteing the onion and garlic in the olive oil until transparent.
Stir in tomato sauce, spices, salt & pepper.
Simmer for 20 minutes.
Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place manicotti shells in salted, boiling water and cook until 3/4 done.
Rinse with cold water and set aside.
Mix together cottage cheese, parmesean cheese, parsley, raw egg, onion, and spices.
With a teaspoon, fill each manicotti shell with the cheese mixture.
Place 3/4 cup of the prepared tomato sauce in the bottom of the baking dish.
Line up the manicotti in the baking dish.
Pour over remaining sauce.
Cover baking dish with tin foil and bake for 20 minutes.
Remove foil and cook for an additional 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Hunter's Mom 2008
Description: 
A simple dish that satisfies the vegetarian family, but is so tasty a family that normally eats meat will enjoy it!
