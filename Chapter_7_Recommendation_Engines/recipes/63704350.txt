Title:
Saffron Vegetable Jumbalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029510.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
- 5 links of sausage (mild or spicy), broiled for 5 minutes and then cut into chunks
- 1 lb peeled and devained shrimp
- 2 red bell peppers, chopped
- 2 green bell peppers, chopped
- 1 large yellow onion, chopped
- 4 celery stalks, chopped
- 6 garlic cloves, chopped fine
- 1 32oz can of diced tomatoes, drained
- 1 6oz can tomato paste
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning
- 3 tablespoons butter or olive oil
- sea salt and pepper to taste
- 1 pinch saffron (optional)
- 1 package of saffron yellow rice or brown rice

Directions:
In large sauce pot, heat butter or olive oil on medium-high heat
Add peppers, celery, garlic and onion - saut√© for 10 minutes
Add drained can of diced tomatoes and tomato paste and stir
Add seasoning and stir
Add chicken chunks, stir and continue cooking for 5 minutes on medium heat
Add chunks of sausage, stir and continue cooking for 3 minutes on medium heat
Add shrimp and reduce heat to low and cover and let stand for 15 minutes
In separate pot, make rice according to package directions.
Once rice is ready, spoon meat and vegetable mixture over the rice in individual bowls! Enjoy!

Description: 
A different take on jumbalaya! Enjoy meats and shrimp with tons of vegetables over saffron rice. YUM!
