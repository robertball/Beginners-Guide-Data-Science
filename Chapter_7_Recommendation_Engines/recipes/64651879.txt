Title:
Southwest Chicken Salad Bento Box Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540972.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken Salad:
2 cups shredded, cooked chicken breast
1/3 cup diced red onion
1/3 cup red pepper
1/2 cup corn
1/2 cup black beans
1/2 cup shredded, cheddar cheese
1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
1 chipotle pepper
2 tablespoons liquid from canned chipotle peppers
1 cup ranch dressing
Sides-
bite size tortilla chips or crackers
mini sweet peppers
mixed berries
Allrecipe's Urban Legend Chocolate Chip Cookies-
http://allrecipes.com/recipe/9804/urban-legend-chocolate-chip-cookies/
cookies- Urban Legend Chocolate Chip Cookies

Directions:
Chicken Salad-
In a large bowl, toss together the chicken, red pepper, red onion, corn, black beans, cheddar cheese, jalapeno. Add ranch dressing, chipotle peppers, and liquid from chipotle peppers and chipotle pepper liquid. Chill until ready to serve.
Prepare and Bake  Allrecipes Urban Legend Chocolate Chip Cookies as directed
To prepare Bento Box-
Serve desired amount of chicken salad, using a silicone muffin cup if needed.
Add chips or crackers
Fill other compartments with mixed berries, peppers, and cookies

Submitter: 
My Hot Southern Mess
Description: 
Bring the flavor of the southwest to chicken salad! It is the perfect way to spice up your bento box.
