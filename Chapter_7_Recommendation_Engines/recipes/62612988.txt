Title:
Upside Down Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
15.5oz pizza sauce
Other pizza ingredients if desired
8oz mozzarella
2 eggs
1c milk
1T oil
1c flour
1/2t salt
1/2c Parmesan

Directions:
Cook hamburger and onion; drain.
Stir in sauce (add any other pizza ingredients, chopped pepperoni, bacon, mushrooms, etc).
Place in 9x13 pan and cover with mozzarella.
Combine eggs, milk, oil, add flour and salt and beat until smooth.
Pour over cheese and sprinkle with parmesan.
Bake 30 minutes at 400.
