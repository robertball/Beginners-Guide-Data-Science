Title:
Sangria - Andrea's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ Cup	Sugar
1 Shot	Cognac
2 Shot	Rose’ Vermouth
1 Shot	Gin
The Juice of ½ lemon
The Juice of ½ Lime
200ml	Lemon Fanta
200ml	Orange Fanta
2 Cinnamon Sticks
3 Cups	Red Wine (does not have to be good quality, I use a cabernet that comes in a gallon jug…you’ll never now it)

Directions:
Stir all ingredients, add fruit for garnish (Orange rings and Strawberries are good), and serve over ice
(Got this recipe from a guy in Spain, have never been able to find lemon Fanta, but use Sprite instead, and Orange Crush or Shasta…
