Title:
Oriental Chicken and Vegetables in a Sweet Black Bean Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken tenders cut in cubes
1 teaspoon minced ginger
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 tablespoons oil, for frying
1/2 pound green beans
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1/2 pound mushrooms, sliced
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 tablespoons black bean sauce
1 tablespoon oil, for frying
1 8 ounce can of sliced water chestnuts, drained
2 tablespoons honey

Directions:
Mix chicken with ginger, black pepper, garlic and sesame oil in a bowl; cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
Heat oil in a large skillet or wok. Saute beans, red pepper, and mushrooms over medium-high heat for about 3 to 5 minutes. Set vegetables aside.
Blend cornstarch, 1/2 cup broth and black bean sauce together in a bowl.
Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken and stir fry until chicken is lightly browned and no longer pink on the inside, about 5 minutes.
Stir vegetables, water chestnuts and honey into chicken. Add the cornstarch mixture and continue cooking until ingredients are hot and sauce has thickened.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Seasoned chunks of chicken, stir-fried with green beans, red peppers and mushrooms are served in a sweet and savory black bean sauce. Water chestnuts add an extra crunch.
