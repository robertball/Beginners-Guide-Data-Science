Title:
Light Asian Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c Napa cabbage, shredded
2 c cubed mango (very small cubes)
2 tsp grated fresh ginger
zest and juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Combine the cabbage and mango in a salad bowl. In a small bowl, combine the ginger, lime zest and juice and toss. Serve immediately or chill until ready to eat.
