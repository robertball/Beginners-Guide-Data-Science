Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup shortening
3/4 cup sugar
1tsp. vanilla
1 Egg
4tsp. milk
2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Thoroughly cream shortening, sugar and vanilla. add egg, beat till light and fluffy. Stir in milk. Sift together dry ingredients;blend into creamed misture. divide dough half chill 1 hour.
On lightly floured surface, roll dough to 1/8 inch. thickness, cut into shape with cutters.
Bake on greased cookie sheet for 6-8 mins.
Cool and enjoy!
