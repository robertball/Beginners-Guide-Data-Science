Title:
Vegan  Artichoke Spinach Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16-oz. pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 15-oz. can water-packed artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 16-oz. vegan cashew-tofu ricotta "cheese" sauce
12 oz. shredded vegan mozzarella cheese, divided (3 cups)
½ cup grated Vegan Parmesan
1 tablespoon of ground flax seed (measure after grinding) with 3 tablespoons of water.
3 Tbs. Amore Herb Paste, divided
¼ cup red wine
1 25-oz. jar Eden Spaghetti Sauce
Pasta dough

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Stir together spinach, artichoke hearts, ricotta "cheese", 2 cups mozzarella, Parmesan, flax "egg", and 2 Tbs. herb paste in large bowl. Set aside.
Bring wine to a boil in small saucepan over medium-high heat. Boil until reduced by half. Stir in remaining 1 Tbs. herb paste, then spaghetti sauce, and bring to just a simmer. Remove from heat.
Roll out a recipe of ravioli pasta dough and cut into 48 squares. Spoon 2 Tbs. cheese mixture into pasta square. Top with a pasta square, and turn down edges pressing with fork. Boil gently about 5 min. Strain out water, and top with remaining sauce.
Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup "mozzarella".
Bake 10 to 12 minutes, or until cheese on top is melted and bubbling. Cool 5 minutes before serving.
