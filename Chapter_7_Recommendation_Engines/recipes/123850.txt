Title:
Cucumbers with Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984096.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 cups sliced cucumbers

Directions:
In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar and salt. Add cucumbers; stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Michelle  Beran
Description: 
It wouldn't be summer if Mom didn't make lots of these creamy cucumbers. Just a few simple ingredients--mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar and salt--dress up slices of this crisp garden vegetable.                                           --Michelle Beran, Claflin, Kansas
