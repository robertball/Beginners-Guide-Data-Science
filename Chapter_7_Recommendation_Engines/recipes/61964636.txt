Title:
Artichoke and Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups steamed rice (use broth)
2 jars marinated artichoke heart, save liquid
1 can artichoke hearts
2 stalks celery
1 bell pepper
1 small can black olives, chopped
4 green onions
1/4 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Stir mayo to taste with reserved artichoke liquid.  Mix all the ingredients with rice and coat with mayo mixture.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Serve immediately or stick in fridge for a cold summer salad.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
Light and tangy summer salad.
