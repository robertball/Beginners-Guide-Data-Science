Title:
Simple Baked Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Salmon fillets/steaks - (Can also use Trout fillets)- deboned
Butter
Optional: Medley of Herbs - dill - oregano - marjoram - thyme;
Optional: Fresh Lemon Juice
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 190c/380f/Gas 5
Butter a large piece of baking foil, place fish on it, spread more butter round the fish, season with salt and pepper and, if using, sprinkle over some fresh lemon juice and/or fresh herbs.
Wrap in a parcel and place on baking tray in oven and bake for 10 to 18 minutes or so or until butter still running clear and fish just cooked through.
