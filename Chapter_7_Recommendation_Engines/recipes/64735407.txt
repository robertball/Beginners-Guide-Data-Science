Title:
Honey Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 1/2 Tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 dashes cayenne pepper
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tablespoons salted butter
1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Mix honey, lemon juice, salt and cayenne pepper in small bowl.  Stir to combine.
Heat cast iron skillet; add olive oil
Saute garlic, then add shrimp
Use spatula to toss the shrimp; add honey mixture
Continue to cook until shrimp are camelized
Add butter , stir.
Add parsley
