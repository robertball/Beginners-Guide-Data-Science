Title:
Easy Raspberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb salad greens
1 pt raspberries
1 jicama
4 oz slivered almonds
raspberry vinaigrette salad dressing

Directions:
Chop up jicama and mix ingredients together. Serve immediately before salad greens wilt.
