Title:
BBQ Pork Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless pork loin or chops, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons Wesson® Pure Vegetable Oil
1 medium onion, cut into strips
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes in Sauce
1/2 cup Hunt's® Barbecue Sauce
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
2 large carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
6 cups cooked egg noodles, drained, kept warm
chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
Heat a large nonstick skillet over high heat. Add pork and cook 3 minutes or until no longer pink. Remove; keep warm. Add oil and onion. Cook over medium-high heat about 3 minutes or until tender.
Blend in tomatoes, BBQ sauce and brown sugar. Add carrots and pork; mix well. Cover, reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes or until carrots are tender.
Remove lid and cook an additional 5 minutes or until sauce thickens. Serve over noodles and garnish with parsley, if desired.

Submitter: 
Hunt's
Description: 
Sweet and tangy barbeque-style pork is served over hot egg noodles.
