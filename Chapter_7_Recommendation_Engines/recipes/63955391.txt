Title:
Baked Firecracker Chicken Wraps (taquitos) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts
3 Tbsp taco seasoning
1 cup salsa
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (pepper jack for spicy)
16 whole wheat flour tortillas, fajita size
1/2 recipe avocado lime dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 and lightly grease an 9x13 baking dish. Rinse and pat chicken dry and place in baking dish.
Sprinkle taco seasoning on both sides of the chicken then drizzle salsa over chicken.
Bake for 25 minutes. Remove chicken from oven and preheat oven to 425.
Once chicken is done cooking, shred it in a stand mixer with paddle attachment. Clean the baking dish while chicken is shredding and then grease it again with cooking spray.
Evenly divide the shredded chicken and grated cheese over each tortilla. Roll them tightly and place in a the 9x13 baking dish with the seam side down. lightly oil the tortillas and bake in oven for 12-15 minutes until edges are browned. If edges do not brown after 15 minutes, turn heat up to 475 and bake an aditional 5 minutes.
