Title:
Cheese Herb Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
3 cups flour
2 tablespoons dry milk powder
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
2 tablespoons butter, softened
5 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
5 tsp Italian seasoning
2 1/2 tsp  active dry yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Select the Basic or White Bread cycle, and press Start. Rub outside with Butter after taking out of oven.

Submitter: 
LINDASGARDENSIDE
Description: 
Fantastic with any Italian dish! I use fresh herbs from my garden when they are available. When using fresh herbs, double the given amount. The smell will make it impossible to wait. ENJOY!Modified from: Cheese Herb Bread
