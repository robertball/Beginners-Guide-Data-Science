Title:
Poke and Pour Gingerbread Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
2 1/4 cups flour
3/4 cups brown sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. mace or nutmeg
1/4 tsp. cloves
3/4 cups water
3/4 cups oil
3/4 cups molasses
2 eggs
Butter Sauce
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cups water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and lour bunt pan. Blend all cake ingredients on low until moistened, the 3 minutes on medium speed.
Pour into prepared pan. Bake 45-55 minutes.
While cake is baking, combine all sauce ingredients. heat until butter melts. Keep warm.
Once cake is done prick top of the hot cake with a long tine fork. Spoon sauce over cake. Let stand 30 minutes, invert cake onto a serving plate. Serve hot or cold. Garnish with whipped cream.
