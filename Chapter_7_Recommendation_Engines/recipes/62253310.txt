Title:
Quinoa saute with black beans and corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
1/2 onion, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 jalapeno, minced
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon ground corriander
1 teaspoon mexican oregano
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup quinoa, rinsed well
1 can black beans
1 can corn, (or 1 cup frozen corn)
2 cups vegetable or chicken stock
1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil in saute pan over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, two to three minutes, until onion starts to soften. Add red and green bell peppers and cook two more minutes. Add garlic, jalapeno, spices and quinoa. Stir constantly until the quinoa starts to toast. Add stock, beans and corn. Bring stock to a simmer, cover and reduce heat to low. Cook 12-15 minutes, until quinoa is cooked through. Stir in cilantro and serve.
Serve on its own or with avocado, cheese, tortillas, whatever strikes your fancy.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Lynn Gunderson
Description: 
A healthy and satisfying main or side dish of quinoa, vegetables and beans.
