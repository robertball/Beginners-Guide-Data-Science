Title:
Pumpkin Pie Smoothie for 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup yogurt
1/4 cup pumpkin
1 ripe banana
Pinch of ginger
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
Ice to taste, or 8 normal size cubes

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in the blender. Add ice to desired thickness and enjoy!

Submitter: 
AddedSugarFreeME
Description: 
I love making this smoothie. Sometimes I add chia seeds for a bit of texture.
