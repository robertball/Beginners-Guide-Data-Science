Title:
Crisco Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 stick or cup Crisco
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
3 tablespoons milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 egg
1 3/4 cups flour
3/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 F.  Combine peanut butter, shortenin brown sugar, milk and vanilla in bowl, beat until well blended.  Combine flour, baking soda,and salt; add to creamed mixture at low speed, mix until just blended.  Add 1 bag mini-Hershey kisses.  Drop by rounded tablespoon 2 inches apart on greased cookies seets, flatten by criscrossing with fork.  Bake 7-8 minutes until just beginning to brown.  Cool 2 mines on baking sheet and remove.
