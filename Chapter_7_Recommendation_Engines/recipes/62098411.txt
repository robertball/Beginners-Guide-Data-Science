Title:
Sarah's Spicy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ - ½ lbs. ground turkey
½ sweet onion minced
3 cloves garlic minced
1 green bell pepper minced
1 jalapeno pepper minced
4 tsp chili powder
2 tsp cumin
½ tsp salt
½ tsp pepper
½ tsp oregano
2 16 oz. cans dark red kidney beans drained
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 6 oz. can tomato paste
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
¼ cup packed brown sugar

Directions:
Brown ground turkey in large pot.
In a food processor mince finely onion and garlic and add to ground turkey. Cook for 3 min.
Add green pepper and jalapeno to the food processor and mince finely. Add to ground turkey.
Add seasonings to pot and cook for additional 3 min.
Add beans, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and brown sugar to pot.
Bring to a simmer, cover and cook for at least one hour, stirring every 10-15 minutes.
