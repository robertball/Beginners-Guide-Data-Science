Title:
Meatless Balls (Vegan, Low Fat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029400.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages of tempeh
3 Tablespoons Chia Seed
1 1/2 cups warm water
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 onion chopped
2 stalks celery chopped
2 cloves of garlic
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup Italian bread crumbs
1/4 cup fresh parsley
1/4 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Slice tempeh and steam for 15 minutes.
Let tempeh cool.
Add Chia seeds to water and let sit for 5 minutes.
Blend Chia seeds in a blender.
Add salt
Blend again.
Saute chopped onion, pressed garlic and celery in olive oil.
When tempeh is cool mix in a food processor bowl.
Add Chia seed mixture.
Add bread crumbs.
Add onion mixture.
Add basil and garlic powder.
Mix together.
Form into small balls and saute in olive oil.
Flip, cover on low heat and brown for about 15-20 minutes.

Description: 
Serve these guiltfree balls with pasta and sauce for a tasty adventure into meatless entrees.
