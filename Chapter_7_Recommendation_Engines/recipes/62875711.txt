Title:
Stuffed Jumbo Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Half box jumbo shells (20 or so)
Red sauce
30 oz. ricotta cheese
2 cups baby spinach, chopped
Â½ cup parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper
1 cup grated mozzarella

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
Cook the shells. Mix ricotta, spinach, parmesan, salt and pepper. Stuff the shells with mix. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, ladle the sauce on the bottom. Place shells on sauce, sprinkle with mozzarella.
Bake for 12 minutes. After 12 minutes, set to broil. Heat until the cheese begins to brown (about 2 minutes).
