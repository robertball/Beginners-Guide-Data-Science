Title:
The Best Chicken Enchiladas EVER! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T butter
2 large onions, thinly sliced
2 c chopped cooked chicken
1/2 c roasted red pepper
6 oz. cream cheese, cubed
1/4t salt
1/4t pepper
2T toasted pecans, chop fine
Sauce:
4 (4oz) ortega green chilis
1/2 small onion, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
2t dried oregano, crushed
1t ground cumin
1/2t sugar
1 (14.5oz) chicken broth
1/2 c salsa
10- 8" flour tortillas
2 Cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring often; add sliced onions, and cook 20 minutes or until caramelized. In a large bowl combine onions, chopped chicken and next 4 ingredients, stirring until combined. Set aside.
Pulse chilies and the next 5 ingredients in blender several times until combined. Bring chile mixture and chicken broth to boil in a saucepan over high heat. Cook 5 minutes or until slightly thickened. Mixture will be the consistency of a thin gravy. Remove from heat and stir in salsa.
Spread 1/3 chile mixture evenly on bottom of 13x9x2 baking dish. Spoon chicken mixture evenly down center of each tortilla; roll up, and place seam side down in prepared baking dish. Top with remaining chile mixture; sprinkle with cheese.
Bake at 375 at 20-25 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
~Gina~
Description: 
The filling in this enchilada recipe is simple, but the combination of chicken, carmelized onions, roasted peppers and cream cheese combine together to make the best enchiladas ever.
