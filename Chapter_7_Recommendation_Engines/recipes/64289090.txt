Title:
Shrimp Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
3 tbs butter
3 tbs olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp ground white pepper
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1 lemon, juiced
1/2 c parmesan cheese
5 tbs italian bread crumbs
1/4 c parsley, minced
1 lb cooked pasta

Directions:
1. Melt butter with the olive oil in saute pan over medium - high heat.
2. Once melted through add garlic, white pepper, red pepper, oregano, and cayenne pepper.
3. Before garlic browns (about 3 minutes) add the shrimp, and coat in sauce.
4. Add lemon juice, parsley, bread crumbs, and parmesan.  Saute until shrimp cooked through.
5. Place pan under broiler on high to melt cheese (1 to 2 minutes).
6. Serve over cooked pasta.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
This was my Dad's go-to recipe for special occasions when we were growing up.  The spice and heat make you feel all warm and toasty inside.
