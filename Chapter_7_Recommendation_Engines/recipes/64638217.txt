Title:
Ed's Barbecue Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Pounds Pork Baby Back Ribs
3 Garlic Cloves, minced
1 Tbsp sugar
2 Tsp salt
1 Tbsp paprika
2 Tsp cumin
2 Tsp chili powder
2 Tsp pepper
Barbecue Sauce:
2 Tbsp Butter
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
3 Tbsp Lemon Juice
3 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp Cider vinegar
1 and 1/2 Tsp ground mustard
1 Tsp celery seed
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 F . Rub ribs with garlic. Place in a roasting pan. Bake uncovered until tender about 2 hours.
Mix sugar, salt and spices, sprinkle over ribs. Remove from pan, cool slightly. Refrigerate covered 8 hours or overnight.
In a small saucepan, heat butter over medium heat and saute onion til tender.  Stir in remaining ingredients bring to boil.  Reduce heat cook uncovered til thickened about 10 minutes stir frequently.
Brush ribs with sauce, grill covered over med heat til heated through 12-15 min turning and brushing occasionally with sauce.  Serve with remaining sauce.
