Title:
Olive Garden Toscana Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 pound spicy Italian sausage
3/4 cup diced onions
1 slice bacon; diced (uncooked)
1 1/4 teaspoon minced garlic
2 cans (4 cups) chicken broth
5 cups water
2 medium Russet potatoes (cut in half lengthwise, then sliced into 1/4 inch slices)
2 cups Kale leaves; cut into thin strips
2/3 cup cream or Half & Half

Directions:
Cook sausage, breaking it up finely.
Place onions & bacon in large saucepan and cook until onions are almost clear
Add garlic and cook another minute
Add broth, water and potatoes, simmer 15 minutes.
Add sausage, kale & cream.  Simmer 5 more minutes
Serve immediately.
