Title:
Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 heaping 1/4 cup of FROZEN mango (8 cubes)
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup VANANA (banana and vanilla) yogurt.(available at trader joes)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender. Blend until smooth and enjoy!

Submitter: 
emzily23
Description: 
This refreshing and filling smoothie is great for breakfast, afternoon snacks or a light dessert! Very easy to make and tasty too! Fresh mango with a slight banana taste ...
