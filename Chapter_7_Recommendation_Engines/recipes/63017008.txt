Title:
Cheesy Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754861.jpg
Ingredients:
Flour Tortillas
1 small can Rotel and Chilis
1 lb. Ground Beef
1 Packet Taco Seasoning
16 ounces Velveeta Cheese
4 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1- 15 ounce can Pinto Beans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In skillet, brown ground beef. Once brown, follow instruction on taco seasoning packet. Drain.
Melt Velveeta cheese and rotel together in microwave until cheese is completely melted.
In a greased casserole dish, layer three tortillas on bottom. Add ground beef on top of the tortillas. Next, drain can of pinto beans and add to casserole and sprinkle with 2 cups shredded cheese. Layer 3 more tortillas and pour velveeta mixture over the tortillas. Put remainder of shredded cheese on top of the casserole.
Bake for about 15 minutes, or until velveeta is thickened an tortilla edges are browned.
