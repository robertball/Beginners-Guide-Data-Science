Title:
Smoked Salmon Pita Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole wheat pitas
8oz light cream cheese
¼ c toasted walnuts  finely chopped
¼ lb smoked salmon cut into thin strips
2 c fresh spinach  torn
¼ c thinly slices red onion
½ c feta

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400º and toast pita for 10 minutes
While the pita is toasting mix the cream cheese and walnuts together until well blended
Remove pita from oven and allow them to cool for 5 minutes
Smear 3 TB or desired amount of cream cheese onto each pita (there should be some cream cheese left over for later use)
Layer each pita with spinach and then the smoked salmon
Sprinkle each pita with red onion and feta
Serve

Submitter: 
tristin
Description: 
These filling pita pizzas make for a great lunch or dinner.  Each pits is packed full of protein and vitamins.  Who knew that something so tasty could be good for you?
