Title:
Pumpkin Bar Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/4 t. pumpkin spice
1/4 t. cinnamon
1/4 t. nutmeg
dash of milk to thin

Directions:
Cream together butter or margarine with the cream cheese. Then stir in vanilla,pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Next, add the powdered sugar, slowly, keep beating until mixture is smooth.
Add milk to thin.
