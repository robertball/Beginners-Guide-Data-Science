Title:
Savory Chicken and Oregano Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 15 oz cans Great Northern or cannelloni beans, rinsed and drained
3 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 cups cooked chopped chicken
2 bell peppers, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 4 oz can chopped green chiles, drained
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp minced fresh oregano

Directions:
Place all ingredients except oregano in a slow cooker.  Cook on low 8 to 10 hours or high 4 to 5 hours.  Stir in oregano and heat through before serving.
