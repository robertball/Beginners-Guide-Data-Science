Title:
Herb and Vegetable Hamburger Noodle Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound 90% lean ground beef
6 large baby carrots, cut in fourths, about 1/4 cup
1 large slice of a yellow onion, about 3 tablespoons
1 cup dry whole grain elbow noodles
2 cups fat-free milk, warm
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp mustard powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 Tbsp Worsheshire sauce
3 Tbsp fresh herbs (I used parsley and oregano), minced

Directions:
Grind carrots in food processor. Add onion and dice a few times. Over medium heat, brown ground beef with carrots and onion. Drain grease.
In a small bowl, mix together garlic powder, mustard powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle over ground beef mixture and stir unil combined.
Stir in warm milk, tomato sauce, and Worsheshire sauce and bring to a slight boil.
Stir in elbow noodles and fresh herbs. Reduce heat and simmer for 12 minutes.
Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes before serving.
