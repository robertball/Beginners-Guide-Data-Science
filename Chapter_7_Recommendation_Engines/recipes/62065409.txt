Title:
Bacon & Watercress Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200 grams fresh watercress, cut into short strips
300 grams bacon bones, cut into chunks
1 kilograms potato, cut into chunks
4 cups chicken stock
1 chicken breast, cooked and diced
extra watercress leaves for garnish

Directions:
1. In a soup pot, cook bacon bones in chicken stock for about 30 minutes. Remove bacon bones from pot and set aside.
2. Add in potato chunks and cook until soft. Add in watercress strips and cook for 5 minutes.
3. Puree the potato and watercress mixture.
4. Stir in the bacon bones, diced chicken and garnish with watercress leaves.

Submitter: 
justluv2cook
Description: 
This comforting winter food is thick and rich, best slurped up while it's hot.
