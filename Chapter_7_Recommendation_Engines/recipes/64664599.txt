Title:
Natalie's Husker Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T EVOO
1/2 onion, diced
1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 lb Italian sausage
2 t. Worcestershire sauce
8 oz Velveeta cheese
8 oz salsa

Directions:
In a skillet, saute the onion in the EVOO until they are translucent. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage to the onion and brown. Add the Worcestershire, Velveeta and salsa. Stir occasionally until the cheese has melted. Transfer to mini crock pot. Serve with Fritos or corn chips
