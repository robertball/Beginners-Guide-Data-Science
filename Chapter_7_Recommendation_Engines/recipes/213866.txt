Title:
Cheesy Bacon Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989119.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups hot mashed potatoes
8 ounces KNUDSEN Sour Cream
6 slices OSCAR MAYER Center Cut Bacon, crisply cooked, crumbled
1 cup shredded mild Cheddar cheese
2 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until well blended.
Serve while hot

Submitter: 
KNUDSEN
Description: 
Make your mashed potatoes stand-out with the addition of sour cream, crumbled bacon, shredded Cheddar cheese, and green onions.
