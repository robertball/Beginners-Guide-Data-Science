Title:
Strawberry Shortcake drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz. Pureed Strawberries
1 1/4 oz Amaretto
2 scoops ice cream
1 dash of vanilla extract
1/2 cup crushed ice
1/4 oz of Vanilla Vodka added is also fabulous

Directions:
Put all ingredients in blender and mix until blended.
Pour into glass and top with whipped cream and a strawberry.

Submitter: 
sanzoe
Description: 
This is a favorite girls night drink for me.  We love to mix these up anytime of the year but even better on a hot summer day.
