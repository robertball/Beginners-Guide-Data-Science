Title:
quick and easy grilled cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of bread
1 tbsp soft butter
2 thin slices of sharp chedder cheese
1 tbsp parsley
1 tsp basil
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp dill weed

Directions:
butter one side of each slice of bread
lay cheese evenly to one unbuttered side of one slice of bread and sprinkle with herbs add other slice of bread so both buttered sides are facing out
place sandwich in a medium saucepan on medium heat for 3 min.
carefully flip sandwich and wait another 3 min.
remove sandwich from heat and  enjoy

a grilled cheese sandwich with a garden twist
