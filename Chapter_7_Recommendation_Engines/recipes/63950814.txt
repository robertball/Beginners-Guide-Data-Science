Title:
Insanely Easy and Delicious Cheesy Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1095962.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, softened
1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder, or more to taste
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, such as Kraft® brand
1/4-1/3 cup prepared Italian salad dressing, such as Good Seasons® (prepare according to package directions)
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1 French baguette, split in half, lengthwise

Directions:
Preheat oven to broil and line cookie sheet w/ foil.
Place bread onto cookie sheet; set aside.
In a bowl, mix together the butter, garlic powder, parmesan cheese and Italian dressing, until thoroughly combined (this may take a few minutes).
Slather mixture evenly over both halves of bread.
Top each half with grated mozzarella cheese.
Place under broiler and bake until browned and bubbly, about 5 minutes.
Cut into serving size pieces and serve.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
I'm always on the lookout for new garlic bread recipes, and when I wanted something different than the usual, this recipe was born. This version is rich, cheesy and sinful...Enjoy!
