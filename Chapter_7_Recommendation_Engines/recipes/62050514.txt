Title:
Shish Kebab Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Paul Newman's Balsamic Vinagrette Dressing
6 tbsp. soya sauce
6 tbsp. honey
6 tbsp. olive oil
2 tbsps. freshly grated ginger root
2 tsps. montreal steak spice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and whisk.  Pour marinade over cut up chicken breast cubes, pork, vegetables and marinade for 2 hours in the fridge.
Alternate vegetables and meat and grill shish kebabs on BBQ for 20 minutes, turning often.  Use remaining marinade to brush kebabs.

Submitter: 
GibsonsGirl
Description: 
A delicious tangy marinade for chicken, pork or vegetarian shish kebabs.
