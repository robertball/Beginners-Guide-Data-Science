Title:
Creamy Chocolate Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup cocoa
1 Tbsp baking powder'1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup water
1 tsp vanilla
3 egg whites'1/2 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Lightly grease a 10x15 pan and line with wax paper
In a large bowl beat egg whites still stiff
In another bowl mix together flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt
In another bowl beat egg at high speed gradually adding sugar, continue to beat until creamy and pale. Beat in water and vanilla. Fold into flour mixture then fold in egg white mixture. {our into prepared pan, spread evenly. Bake in 375 degree oven about 10 mins. Immediately inver cake onto a dishtowel sprinkled with 3 Tbs powdered sugar. Peel off waxed paper and immediately rolls cake and towel into a cylinder, starting with the short side. Let cool completely on rack.
Unroll cooled cake and spread with whipping cream and re-roll. Do no be concerned with cracks). Wrap filled cake with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
