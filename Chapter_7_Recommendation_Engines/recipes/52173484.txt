Title:
Broccoli Salad IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup bacon bits
1 head fresh broccoli florets
1/2 cup golden raisins
2 tbsp white vinegar
1/2 cup hulled sunflower seeds
1 red onion, chopped
1 cup creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip â„¢
1/3 cup white sugar

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside.
In a large bowl, mix together the bacon, broccoli, pepper, raisins, onion and sunflower seeds.
In a small bowl, whisk together the creamy salad dressing, vinegar, and sugar. Pour over broccoli mixture and toss well to coat. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.
