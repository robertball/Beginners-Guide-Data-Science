Title:
Barley Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677750.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Barley
1 Cup frozen sweet corn  thawed
1 Pkg frozen chopped broccoli  thawed
1/2 Cup chopped red onion
1 Medium carrot  shredded
3 Tablespoons lemon juice
2 Tablespoons Olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook barley according to package instructions.  Cool.
Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.
Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
A healthy salad that goes with any main dish. You can easily adjust this recipe to suite your taste.
