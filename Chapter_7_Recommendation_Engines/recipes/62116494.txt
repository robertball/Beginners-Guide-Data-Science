Title:
The Best Million Dollar Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. flour
4 tbsp. sugar
1 c. rolled oats
1 c. chopped pecans
1 c. melted butter
1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 c. sugar
1 16 oz. can crushed pineapple
1 c. chopped pecans
1 12 oz. whipped topping
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, oats, pecans and butter until dough forms a ball.
Divide dough in half and press each portion into pie plates to form a crust.
Bake crusts at 350 degrees fro 10 to 12 minutes. Allow to cool.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add sugar and mix well.
Fold in remaining ingredients and pour filling into cooled pie crusts.
Cover pies and refrigerate at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Shannon :)
Description: 
Cream cheese, whipped topping, pineapple and pecans are combined to create light, refreshing filling for this pie.
