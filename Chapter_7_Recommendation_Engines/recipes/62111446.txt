Title:
Amazing Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¾ cups gluten-free baking flour blend
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
½ tsp guar or xanthan gum
½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
1 T. Ener-G egg replacer
2 T. apple or pineapple juice
2 T. canola or extra-light olive oil
1 cup rice milk or milk of choice
1 T. lemon juice or vinegar
½ cup apple or pineapple juice
1 tsp gluten-free vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat waffle iron.
Whisk together flour, soda, baking powder, salt, guar gum, and cinnamon.  Set aside.
Combine egg replacer and 2 T. fruit juice; whisk until frothy.  Whisk in olive oil, milk of choice, lemon juice, ½ cup fruit juice, and vanilla extract.
Whisk wet ingredients into dry ingredients; if it seems too thick, add additional fruit juice until desired consistency is reached.
Pour ½ cup batter (depending on your waffle maker) onto heated waffle iron.  Follow the directions for your waffle iron, and repeat with remaining batter.

Submitter: 
CALLALILLIE
Description: 
These waffles are divine!  Light and tasty, crisp yet tender, easy to make; AND they are free of gluten, dairy, eggs, soy, and refined sugar.  Pamper yourself, your family and friends!
