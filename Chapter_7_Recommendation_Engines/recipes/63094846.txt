Title:
Rossi's Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/784013.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Bell Peppers colour of your choice
For the meat mixture:
1 lb of ground meat
1 egg
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 tsp garlic minced or powder
1 tbs parsley
1/4 tsp each salt & pepper
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup uncooked white rice
1/4 cup of canned tomato soup, reserve the rest for the sauce.
Non stick cooking spray
---------------------
For the sauce:
1 can - 1/4 cup of tomato soup (remaining)
1 cup of V-8 juice for little bit of spice or plain tomato juice

Directions:
Cut tops off peppers, wash inside and out remove seeds put on paper towel to absorb water.
Mix all meat mixture ingredients in a bowl
Mix tomato soup and V-8 juice in separate bowl
Stuff peppers with meat mixture
spray non stick cooking spray in corning ware dish preferably with a lid. Pour 1/2 cup of soup mixture on bottom of dish then arrange stuffed peppers meat side up in dish. Pour remaining soup mixture over peppers and sprinkle with more parsley if you like.
Cover and bake at 375, let set a few minutes before serving and enjoy~

Submitter: 
Giamarie Saponaro
Description: 
This is one of many great Italian recipes started by my Grandpa John who's passion to cook started in the Army in WWII and after for the VFW and American Legion. Trust me if he cooked, they ate the only the BEST!
