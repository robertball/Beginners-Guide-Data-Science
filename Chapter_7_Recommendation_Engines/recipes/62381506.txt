Title:
Slumgullion w/Revisions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
elbow macaroni (for 3 servings)
12 oz ground beef
1/2 large onion, chopped
1 can tomato sauce
mushrooms can or fresh
minced garlic
1 can stewed tomatoes
worchestershire sauce (about 1 Tbl)
red pepper flakes

Directions:
Brown beef with onion & mushrooms (if using fresh ones). Add other ingredients.
Add cooked macaroni. Stir & simmer until sauce is a little thicker.
