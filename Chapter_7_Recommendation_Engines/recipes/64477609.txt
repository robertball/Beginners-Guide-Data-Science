Title:
Basil Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 t salt
1/4 t ground pepper
1 T olive oil
2 T balsamic vinegar
1 T honey
2 T chopped fresh basil

Directions:
sprinkle both sides of chicken with salt and pepper
In a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil.  Add chicken, cook 5 minutes or until lightly brown - turn chicken, and cook 6 minutes or until done.  Stir in vinegar, honey and basil, cook 1 minute, turning chicken to coat with glaze

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
Love the tangy-sweet flavours of this easy, but oh so elegant glazed chicken, ready in 15 minutes, great weeknight meal
