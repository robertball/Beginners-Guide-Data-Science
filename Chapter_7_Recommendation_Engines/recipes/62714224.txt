Title:
Spicy Spinach Salad with Pasta & Chicken in Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup chicken broth
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 to 1 teaspoon Tabasco sauce (red original)
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
2 teaspoons Oriental Sesame Oil
2 cups loosely packed fresh spinach, rinsed and patted dry
8 ounces linguine, cooked and drained
2 cups shredded cooked chicken
1 cup shredded carrots
Â¼ cup sliced green onions (white & green parts)
Garnish:
slice green onions
peanuts

Directions:
Combine chicken broth, soy sauce, garlic and Tabasco in medium saucepan; bring to a boil over medium heat. Remove from heat.
Whisk in peanut butter and sesame oil until smooth.
Arrange spinach on 4 plates.
Toss linguine with 3 tablespoons peanut sauce; spoon over spinach.
Add chicken, carrots and green onions to remaining peanut sauce.
Spoon chicken mixture over linguine.
Garnish with extra green onions and peanuts sprinkled over each salad.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
You can make this oriental spinach salad for 4 as spicy as you wish by the amount of Tabasco sauce you use.
