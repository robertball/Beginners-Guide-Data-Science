Title:
Island Greens Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5418757.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup baby spinach leaves
1 banana, cut into chunks
1/2 cup pineapple chunks
3/4 cup Almond Breeze Original Unsweetened Coconut blend almondmilk

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
This quick green smoothie provides what you need for a fruity, veggie snack--with spinach, banana, pineapple, and almondmilk.
