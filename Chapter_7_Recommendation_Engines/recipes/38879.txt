Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package NEWTONS Fruit Chewy Cookies, any flavor, divided
1/2 cup boiling water
1 (3 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Lemon Flavor Gelatin
1 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Lowfat Cottage Cheese
1 cup fresh fruit pieces

Directions:
Stand 14 cookies end-to-end around side of 8-inch springform pan.  Chop remaining cookies; place evenly on bottom of pan.  Set aside.
Stir boiling water into gelatin in small bowl 2 to 3 minutes or until completely dissolved; cool slightly.  Pour into blender container.  Add cottage cheese; cover.  Blend until smooth; pour over chopped cookies in pan.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until set.  Arrange fruit on top of cheesecake.  Garnish with thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping.
