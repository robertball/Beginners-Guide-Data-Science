Title:
Ritz / Rollo cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ritz crackers
rollo chocolate candy oHershey Liss Caramel filled

Directions:
Oven 350* Ritz salty side down on cookie sheet...place one candy ( rollo or kiss) cook 2-5 min ...take out of oven and place ritz cracker on top salty side up...squish down to flatten melted candy...cool

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
2 ingredients...crackers and candy...use peanut butter kisses or peppermint ones for variety
