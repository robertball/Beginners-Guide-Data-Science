Title:
Crab Dip Mountain Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029480.jpg
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. packages of cream cheese, softened
6 Tablespoons of horseradish OR
6 splashes of Tabasco sauce
6 Tablespoons ketchup
2 teaspoons sugar
1-2 Tablespoons lemon juice
2 Tablespoons dill weed
2 Tablespoons parsley flakes
2 splashes Worchestershire sauce
2 cans crab meat
Your favorite cocktail sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together well, adding crab meat last.
Mound mixture and make a depression at the top.
Fill depression with cocktail sauce and let drip down the sides.
Serve with Ritz crackers.  [See illustration - and no, it's not a hat!]
