Title:
ENGLISH LEMON CURD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3989258.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/4 c. freshly grated lemon zest ( 6-8 lemons)
1 c. granulated sugar
6 beaten eggs
1 c. softened unsalted butter

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together in a 2 qt. saucepan.
Place saucepan over low/medium heat and cook, stirring constantly until  curd is thick and just coming to a boil.
Remove from heat and cool in saucepan, stirring occasionally to keep the surface soft.
When cooled to room temperature, pour curd into jars, seal and store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Marti
Description: 
Lemony tart and just a little sweet.  Wonderful on scones or croissants.
