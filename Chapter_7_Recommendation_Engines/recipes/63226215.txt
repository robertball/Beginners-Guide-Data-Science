Title:
Quick Tomato Basil Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pasta (any kind)
1/3 C. Parmesan Cheese (fresh grated)
2/3 C. Fresh Basil
1 C. Whipping Cream
14.5 oz Can of Diced Tomatoes with Garlic & Onion

Directions:
Start pasta. While pasta is cooking:
Cook tomatoes over med-high heat until liquid reduces (5-7 min). Reduce heat and and in cream and basil. DON'T let it boil. When heated through, toss in your pasta (drained) and cheese. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
April K.
Description: 
Rich sauce to pour over any pasta.
