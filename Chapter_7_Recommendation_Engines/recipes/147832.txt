Ingredients:
1/2 cup All-Bran速 Original
2 cups prepared pancake mix
2 1/2 cups milk
2 tablespoons shortening, melted

Directions:
In medium mixing bowl, combine Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal and pancake mix. Add milk and shortening to mixture and stir only until combined.
Bake on hot griddle at 375 degrees F until brown on both sides, turning only once. Serve hot with warm syrup or fruit topping.
