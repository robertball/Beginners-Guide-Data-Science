Title:
Spanish/Austrian Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/627030.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 uncooked sausages (any kind will work)
2-3 red and/or green pepper, sliced
1 onion, sliced
6 mushrooms, sliced
1 TB olive oil
2 cups white cooking wine

Directions:
Brown sausages in oil.
Cook all ingredients with white wine. Let it reduce.
Slice the sausage and serve.
Serve with Spanish Tortilla and fruit.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Blake and Andrea made this for us when we were in Barcelona. Since Andrea is Austrian, we're guessing this dish is more Austrian than Spanish.
