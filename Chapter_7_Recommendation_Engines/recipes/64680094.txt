Title:
Portabella Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Portabella mushroom caps
2 Tbsp olive oil
12 Turkey pepperoni slices
Shredded fat free mozzarella cheese
Marinara sauce
Pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 F
2. Remove inside ribbing of the mushroom caps with spoon. Brush olive oil on both sides of the mushrooms and season with pepper.
3. Cook mushroom caps until they've shrunk and are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
4. Remove from oven and with a tablespoon, spoon marinara sauce on inside of the mushroom then top with cheese and turkey pepperoni.
5. Return to oven until cheese has melted.
