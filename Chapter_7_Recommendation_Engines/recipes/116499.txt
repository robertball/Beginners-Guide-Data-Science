Title:
Lemon Vinaigrette Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/582707.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head bibb or Boston lettuce, separated into leaves
1 large tomato, sliced
1 small cucumber, sliced
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons snipped fresh dill
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Arrange the lettuce on four salad plates. Top with tomato and cucumber slices. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the oil, lemon juice, dill, salt and pepper; shake well. Drizzle over salads.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home Test Kitchen
Description: 
For a lovely side dish, try this pretty arranged salad. With delicately flavored Boston lettuce and tomato and cucumber slices, its drizzled with a homemade lemon and dill vinaigrette thats ready in minutes.
