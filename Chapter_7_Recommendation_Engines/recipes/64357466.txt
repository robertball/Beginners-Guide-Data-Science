Title:
2 Minute Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb confectioners sugar
1/2 c cocoa
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 c milk
1 Tbsp vanilla
1/2 c butter

Directions:
Stir together sugar, cocoa, salt, milk, and vanilla in a microwave safe bowl until blended.
Top with butter.
Microwave on high for 2 minutes.
Stir until smooth.
Pour into an 8-inch dish that has either been greased or lined with wax paper.
Chill in refrigerator until solid - about 2 hours.

Description:
