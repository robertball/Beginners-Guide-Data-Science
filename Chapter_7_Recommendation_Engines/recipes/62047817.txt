Title:
Wilted Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	Large Bunch	Fresh Spinach
½	lb	Fresh Mushrooms  (canned mushrooms OK)
4 	Slices 	Bacon
¼	Cup	Vinegar
½	Tsp	Salt
2	Cloves	Garlic
¼	Tsp	Fresh ground black pepper
¼	Tsp	Ground oregano
¼	Tsp	Dry mustard
¼	Tsp	Sweet basil
½	Tsp	Sugar
2	Each	Hard Boiled eggs sliced (Optional)

Directions:
Wash room temperature fresh spinach thoroughly; remove all stems from leaves; dry on paper towels.  Turn dried leaves into a large salad bowl.
Wipe mushrooms with a damp cloth and slice lengthwise.  Add to spinach.
Fry bacon until crisp.  Remove from skillet and crumble coarsely.  Set aside.
Combine vinegar, salt, garlic, pepper, oregano, mustard, basil and sugar.  Heat bacon fat remaining in the skillet.   Add vinegar mixture, pouring carefully so dressing does not spatter.  Stir to mix ingredients.  Heat thoroughly.
Pour hot dressing over spinach leaves, toss well and sprinkle with crumbled bacon, top with hard boiled egg.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
LeChef
Description: 
This is the original Wilted Spinach Salad as first served by The Cove restaurant in Los Angeles.  The only addition is the optional hard boiled egg garnish.
