Title:
Creamy Wine Curry Sauce and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breast
1/2 Sweet onion, chopped
3 Cloves fresh garlic, minced
2 Tbs butter
1/2 Bottle dry white wine
1/2 Cup heavy whipping cream
1-1/2 Tbs yellow curry
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp thyme
1/8 tsp onion powder
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
On medium heat, add 1 Tbs of butter to a medium size pan and melt.
Sprinkle chicken with salt, pepper and onion powder, add to pan and cover.
Cook for 5-7 minutes on each side or until juices run clear.
Remove chicken from pan and let rest.
Add 1 Tbs butter to pan with onions and garlic.
Saut√© until onions are soft and translucent.
Add white wine to pan and let reduce for 5-7 minutes while scraping all the brown bits to add to sauce.
Add curry, nutmeg, cinnamon, thyme and salt & pepper to taste.
Turn heat down to low and add heavy whipping cream. Mix sauce thoroughly.
Add chicken and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
Plate chicken and pour sauce over.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This is such a tasty, flavorful dish. If you love curry and wine you are going to really enjoy this!
