Title:
Allie & Nick's Deep Fried Chili Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Serves 4
1 1/2 lbs. steak strips
corn flour for dredging
oil for deep frying
1 each: yellow, red & green bell peppers cut into strips
1 jalapeno pepper finely diced (optional)
1 serrano pepper finely diced (optional)
2 carrots, peeled & cut into thin strips
1 large onion cut into strips
Sauce: 1 small bottle of sweet chili sauce
2 T oyster sauce
6 oz bottle of dark soy
4 cloves of crushed garlic
2 T fresh ginger, crushed

Directions:
Coat beef in flour, shaking off excess & deep fry in small batches, set aside.  Mix peppers, carrots & onions together. Shallow fry in until al dente, in a separate pan in 2 T oil. Remove & set aside with beef. In same pan add 1 T oil & fry garlic & ginger until soft. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce & mix well. Add chili sauce last & mix until thickened. Add beef & vegetables to sauce, mix well. Serve with white rice or egg noodles.

Submitter: 
NYRED
Description: 
Deep Fried beef with a sweet & sassy chili sauce.  It's a real experience for your tastebuds!
