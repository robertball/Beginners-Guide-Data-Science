Title:
Halushki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Kielbasa or Smoked Sausage
garlic powder to taste
salt and pepper to taste
1 onion, chopped
1 large head cabbage, cut into squares
1 pound large egg noodles
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Cut kielbasa and cook in a greased sauce pan or deep skillet with chopped onion and fry until very brown and well cooked.  Remove and set asided
Add a little Butter to the saucepan or skillet and mix up the drippings a bit. Place cabbage into the pot, and allow to cook down completely. Add bits of water if needed.
In a separate large saucepan, boil the egg noodles in water until cooked.  Drain noodles and mix in butter.
When the cabbage has cooked down, add kielbasa and cooked noodles and mix completely.

Submitter: 
Michele Mathis Lavell
Description: 
This is a traditional Polish recipe handed down from my grandma.  Its quick, simple, inexpensive, tasty and makes enough to feed an army.Modified from: Halushki
