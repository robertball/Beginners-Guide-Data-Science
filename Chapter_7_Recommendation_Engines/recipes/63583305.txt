Title:
Red Potato Salad Restaurant Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs Red Skin Potatoes
5 Large Hardboiled Eggs Cut to Bitsize
1/4 Cup Green Onion
1 1/4 Cup Mayo (Hellman's)
1 1/2 Teaspoon Celery Seeds
1 Teaspoon Garlic Salt
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes till tender or steam I pre cut my potatoes to serving size before boiling
Mix mayo, celery seeds, garlic salt and green onion
Gently fold in potatoes and eggs

Submitter: 
ChrisB
Description: 
This is a clone of Red Hot and Blues recipe, my favorite potato salad. Simple yet good.
