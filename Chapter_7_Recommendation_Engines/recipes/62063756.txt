Title:
Pretzel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Butter, softened (no substitutions)
8 oz. Cream Cheese, softened
1 pkg. Ranch dip (dry)
1-2 T. Parmesan Cheese, shredded
Coarse Ground Black Pepper (optional)
Pretzels

Directions:
Blend the softened butter & cream cheese together well.  Add the Ranch dip & 1 1/2 T. of the cheese & mix.  Spoon into a serving bowl & sprinkle the coarse ground black pepper on top.  Serve with pretzels.

Submitter: 
Dee
Description: 
An amazingly easy dip to make!  Not for the dieter, but oh-so worth it. After taking it to work, I never have any leftovers for home.
