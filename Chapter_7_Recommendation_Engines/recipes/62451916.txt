Title:
Healthy Banana Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe bananas
2 cups rolled oats
1 cup raisins-optional
1/4 cup honey
1/3 cup applesauce
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mash the bananas.  Stir in oats, dates, applesauce, and vanilla.  Mix well, and allow to sit for 15 minutes. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly brown.
