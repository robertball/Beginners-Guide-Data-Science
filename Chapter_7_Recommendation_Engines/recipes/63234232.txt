Title:
Broccoli Cornbread with Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8.5 ounce) packages cornbread/muffin mix
4 eggs
1 cup cottage cheese
1 & 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (divided)
3/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
1 large onion, chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat eggs, cottage cheese and butter.  Add cornbread mixes and stir just until moistened. Fold in broccoli, onion and 1 cup cheddar cheese. Transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish and sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup cheese over the top.  Bake at 375 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting. Serve warm. Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Susie P
Description: 
This moist bread with a sweet corn flavor can be served as a side dish with any meaty entree.  Adding cheddar cheese helps seal the deal to convert the picky broccoli hater.Modified from: Broccoli Corn Bread
