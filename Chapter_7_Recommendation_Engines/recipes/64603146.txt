Title:
8 pita breads made with bread machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/8 cup water
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 1/2 tsp sugar
3 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp yeast

Directions:
Put all ingredients into bread maker to make the dough
When the dough is ready, roll out to 8 individual rounds  and let rise 30 mins
Bake in 450 degree oven 5 mins on rack
When puffed up, remove from oven and place on cooling rack and place a damp kitchen towel over top until cool.
