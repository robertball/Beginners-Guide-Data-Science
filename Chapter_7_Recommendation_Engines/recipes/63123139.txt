Title:
Marvelous Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb 80/20 beef
1lb ground Italian Sausage
3 cans (15ish oz) crushed tomatoes
1 can (15ish oz) Tomato puree
2 cans (15ish oz) diced tomato drained
2 medium onion shredded
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 carrot peeled and shredded
1tbs dried parsley/dried basil
Â½ tsp red pepper flake
Tsp sugar
1-2 tbs olive oil
Garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper to preferred level.

Directions:
Brown beef and sausage in a skillet with 1/2 of all spices.  Remove from skillet but leave some of the liquid behind, add olive oil.  Cook onion/carrot/garlic until translucent.  Move to large pot.  Combine all ingredients and let simmer on low heat for about an hour.  Yum!

Meaty and hearty sauce is perfect for lasagna or any pasta.  Thick and chunky.
