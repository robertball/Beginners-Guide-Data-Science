Title:
Chicken Carnitas in Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs skinless bonless chicken breasts (approx 6)
1-2 onions, sliced
8-10 cloves garlic, minced
1 orange, juiced (1/2 c)
3 limes, juiced (1/2 c)
1 bottle beer (Omission Lager)
2 T Cumin
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp chipotle powder
1 1/2 T oregano
1 1/2 tsp salt
grinds black pepper
dash cayenne

Directions:
put all in crock pot and cook on low 8-10 hours or high 4-5 hours
shred with fork
brown portion of chicken chunks on sheet pan in preheated broiler for 5-10 min, adding liquid as needed
serve with guacamole, cheese, cilantro, green onion, lettuce, greek yogurt/sour cream, pico de gallo, tortillas as desired
NEXT TIME:  Less lime, less spice, more orange, maybe tomato sauce
