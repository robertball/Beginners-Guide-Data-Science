Title:
Knockoff Outback Steak Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Old Bay Seasoning
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon cumin

Directions:
Mix together well and store in a container with a tight lid.  Rub on steaks, pork chops and chicken.

Submitter:

Description: 
I love the seasoning at Outback and found this on another site.  Love it on all kinds of meat.
