Title:
Metropole Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/966003.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups ice
1 1/2 fluid ounces cognac
1 1/2 fluid ounces dry vermouth
2 dashes bitters (such as Peychaud's BittersÂ®)
1 maraschino cherry, for garnish

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour in cognac and vermouth, then shake in bitters. Stir well, strain into a chilled glass, and garnish with a cherry.
