Title:
Spinach and Cheese Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 packages (10oz.) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 1/2 cups half and half
3 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2 cups shredded Gruyere cheese - about 6 oz.
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Butter a shallow 1 qt. baking dish.  In a large bowl, combine all ingredients, but reserve 1/2 cup of the cheese.  Spread into prepared baking dish and top with remaining cheese.  Bake until set and top is golden brown, about 30 to 35 minutes.
