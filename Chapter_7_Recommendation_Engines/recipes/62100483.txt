Title:
Cilantro Green Onion Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 bunches fresh cilantro
1 bunch green onions
1 cup roasted almonds
3 oz. grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup olive oil
about 3/4 cup chicken or vegetable stock

Directions:
In a food processor, combine all ingredients except stock and puree. Add enough stock to give your sauce the consistency of a thin gravy.

Submitter: 
MMENIGMA
Description: 
This is a great sauce to serve over grilled or baked chicken or fish, as well as a wonderful sauce to make tamales with (mix with cooked chicken, pork or tofu).
