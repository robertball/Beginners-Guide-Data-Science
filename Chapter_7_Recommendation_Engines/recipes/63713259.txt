Title:
Simply Delicious Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031756.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb - ground turkey or ground beef
1-2 pkgs. - taco seasoning mix*
1 - can black beans, ( or ranch, red, or pinto) undrained
1 - can Rotel tomatoes**
1 - can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 - 10-12 oz bag of tortilla chips, crushed***
1 - small onion, chopped
2 cups - cheddar or mexican blend shredded cheese
1/2 cup - water
Non-stick cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees during prep.
Brown and drain ground turkey. Return to skillet.
To meat, add beans, Rotel, onions, soup, one package of taco seasoning, and water.
Let mixture simmer to combine flavors.  If intensity of taco seasoning is weak, add some of 2nd packet, to suit taste.
Spray 9x13 inch baking dish/pan with non-stick cooking spray.
Add layer of tortilla chips, follow half of meat mixture, and half of cheese.
Repeat layers as above, ending with cheese
Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 20-30 minutes.
Note: Spray foil with non-stick spray to keep it from sticking to casserole.
Serve with extra tortilla chips, shredded lettuce, tomatoes, sour cream, black olives, and/or guacamole.

Submitter: 
Ms. G.
Description: 
A quick, easy, and simply delicious taco casserole. When you eat it on a bed of shredded lettuce w/ chopped tomatoes and chips, it's just like having a taco salad!! Yummy!!
