Title:
Gluten Free Brownies (with beans!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans 540 ml (rinsed thoroughly)
2 egg whites
1 full egg
1/3 cup plain pureed pumpkin
3 tbps cashew butter (or vegan butter/vegetable oil)
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/8 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla extract
6 packets stevia
4 tsp agave or honey
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
*optional - 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Spray mini muffin tin with olive oil spray
Place all ingredients excluding chocolate chips into your food processor and blend until smooth, scraping down the sides occasionally.
Remove blade and with a spoon, stir in chocolate chips
Spoon batter evenly into muffin tin
Bake for 20 minutes
Let brownies set for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.

Submitter: 
Winnie Chan
Description: 
This brownie is so delicious, you won't be able to tell that it is gluten-free! (and don't tell your kids about the beans!)
