Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot Moroccan-Spiced Chicken Thighs with Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2991689.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
6 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1 can diced tomatoes, drained (14.5 oz.)
1 can chic peas (29 oz.), drained and rinsed
1 lb carrots, cut into 1" pieces
1 large onion chopped
1/2 cup pitted black olives
1 cup couscous
Fresh cilantro

Directions:
Mix  seasonings in crock pot.  Add chicken and toss to coat with spice mixture.  Add vegetables and chic peas.
Cook on low for 6 to 7 hours (or high for 3 to 4 hours) until chicken is tender.  Remove thighs and shred (or chop ) meat and return it to the liquid with vegetables.
10 minutes before serving, prepare couscous according to package directions.
Serve with couscous topped with cilantro.
