Title:
Apple Vanilla Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3812460.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small chilled tart apples, sliced
1/3 cup skim milk
1/4 cup orange juice
1/4 banana, frozen
2 tablespoons vanilla whey protein powder
1 tablespoon flax seeds
1 ice cube

Directions:
Blend apples, milk, orange juice, banana, protein powder, flax seeds, and ice cube in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
v6nittany
Description: 
A friend gave me a ton of tart apples from her backyard tree. I modified another smoothie recipe to create this delicious breakfast.
