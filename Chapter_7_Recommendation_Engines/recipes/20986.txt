Title:
Very Easy Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/236521.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) jar cocktail sauce
3/4 pound imitation crabmeat

Directions:
In a medium bowl, blend the cream cheese and cocktail sauce. Cut the imitation crabmeat into small pieces and combine it with the mixture. Refrigerate 3 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Shannon
Description: 
Everyone will enjoy this quick dip. Rich, flavorful imitation crab is delicious when combined with cocktail sauce and cream cheese. Dip your favorite crackers or bread, and enjoy!
