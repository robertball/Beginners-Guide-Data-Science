Title:
Warm and Creamy Bacon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983631.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 cups Daisy Brand Sour Cream
3 ounces bacon bits
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup chopped green onion

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Place the mixture in a 1-quart baking dish. Cover. Heat the dip for 25-30 minutes or until hot. Serve with assorted fresh vegetables, crackers and/or chips.

Submitter: 
Daisy Brand
Description: 
Dig into this creamy bacon and Cheddar dip with crisp veggies, crackers, and chips.
