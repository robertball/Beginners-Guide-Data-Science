Title:
Buckwheat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/611133.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup buckwheat flour (Bob's Red Mill)
1 1/4 Tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/4 cups milk
2 large eggs
1/3 cup peanut oil
3 Tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F. Grease or paper line cupcake tin.
Combine flour(s), sugar, baking powder and salt in bowl, mix well with fork or whisk. In a separate container combine milk, eggs, oil and whisk until blended. Add melted butter. Add to flour mixture and pour into prepared muffin tin, filling cups to 2/3 full.
Bake for 20-23 minutes or until muffins spring back when touched. Cool, butter and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dhyana
Description: 
These tasty muffins have that grainy buckwheat bite you're looking for without being heavy.
