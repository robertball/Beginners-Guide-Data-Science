Title:
Potato Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion chopped and cooked till soft
1 cup sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 cups shredded cheese
6 cups frozen hash browns
1/4 cup melted butter
1/3 cup bread crumbs
1/3 cup parmesan
Optional
Cubed ham
corn

Directions:
Mix sour cream, soup, and 2 cups cheddar cheese and cooked onions together, add hash brown and put in large greased casserole dish. Drizzle with melted butter and top with bread crumbs and parmesan.
bake 350 degrees for about 60 minutes

Description: 
very creamy and delicious. Good hot or cold as a side dish for breakfast, lunch or dinner
