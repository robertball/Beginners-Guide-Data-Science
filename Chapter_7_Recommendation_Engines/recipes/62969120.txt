Title:
Flax Seed Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/3 c. margarine or butter, softened
2 Tbsp. ground flax seed combined with 1/4 + 2 Tbsp. applesauce (sweetened)
1 1/2 c. mashed bananas
1 2/3 c. all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 c. nuts (are optional)
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease the bottom only of desired loaf pan.
In a small bowl mix together the ground flax seed and applesauce. Set aside.
In medium bowl, mix sugar and butter til smooth.
Add to this the flax seed and applesauce. Stir to combine.
Add mashed bananas to the mix. Combine well.
In a separate bowl combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
Now add your wet ingredients to your dry ingredients. Stir til moisten...don't over stir.
Add nuts in if desired. Stir.
Pour into prepared loaf pan.
Bake until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean...about 1 hour.
Cool 5 minutes prior to removing bread from pan.

Description: 
Ground flax seed is an excellent replacement for eggs in baked goods. You won't even miss the eggs in this banana bread.
