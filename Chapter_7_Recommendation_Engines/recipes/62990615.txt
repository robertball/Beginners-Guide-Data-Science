Title:
Polenta and Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Polenta (prepackaged)
Mushrooms
Red or yellow peppers
Onions
Asparagus
Zuccini
Yellow Squash

Directions:
Toss vegetables in olive oil. Salt and pepper generously.  Roast at 450 until tender. Cut polenta into slices and pan fry until hot throughout.  Serve roasted vegetables over hot polenta
