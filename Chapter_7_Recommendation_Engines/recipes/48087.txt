Title:
Santa Fe Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 TysonÂ® Fresh Chicken Leg Quarters
2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon cumin
2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
. Wash hands. Place all ingredients except chicken in a small bowl; stir until smooth. Place chicken on a large platter. Rub spice mixture evenly over chicken. Wash hands. Let chicken stand 5 to 10 minutes OR cover and refrigerate up to 8 hours.
Preheat grill to medium. Place chicken on grill; cover. Cook 25 to 30 minutes or until done (internal temp 180 degrees F), turning occasionally.
