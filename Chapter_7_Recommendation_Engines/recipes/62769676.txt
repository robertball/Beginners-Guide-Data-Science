Title:
Slow Cooker Cranberry Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
1 (2-3 pound) pork roast
1 (16 ounce) can whole cranberry sauce
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
2 tablespoons corn starch
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Place onion soup mix in the bottom of a slow cooker. Place roast in the slow cooker. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Top with cranberry sauce. Add potatoes and carrots on top.
Cover, and cook 8 hours on Low.
Remove roast, and set aside. Set slow cooker to High. Whisk together corn starch and water, and slowly mix into the liquid remaining in the slow cooker to create a thick gravy. Serve with the roast.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
This is a family favorite! Simple but absolutely delicious! It makes a fantastic gravy that complements the roast and goes great with mashed potatoes!Modified from: Slow Cooker Cranberry Roast
