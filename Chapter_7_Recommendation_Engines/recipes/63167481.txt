Title:
baked stuffed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large Russet Potatoes
2.5 lbs ground beef
1 cup cheddar cheese grated
small onion
olive oil
2 cloves garlic
salt and pepper
worcester sauce
lite sour cream

Directions:
saute chopped onion ang garlic-add beef and brown.
bake potatoes-when done cut in half and scoop out insides into large bowl
mash potatoes with sour cream add meat mixture-combine and stir in cheese
stuff potato shell return to the oven and bake for 20 mins.
