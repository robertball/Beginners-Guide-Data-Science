Title:
Sauteed Cabbage with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon-cut up
1/2 head each green and red cabbage, chopped
2 stalks celery-diced
1 shallot-minced
3/4 C water
3 T red wine vinegar
3 T butter
1 C bread crumbs-Italian

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Place bacon pieces in lg. skillet over med. heat. Saute until crisped brown. Remove with slotted spoon to paper towel to drain.
Pour off all but 1 T of grease. Add cabbage and celery to remaining grease. Add bacon in. Saute until cabbage begins to wilt (about 10 min.).
Add shallots, stir to combine. Saute 2-3 more min.
Add red wine vinegar and water. Bring to boil, continue stirring.
Reduce heat back to med. and allow some liquid to evaporate. Cook for 10 more minutes to desired tenderness you want cabbage.
Season with salt and pepper.
Pour into baking dish. Sprinkle bread crumbs over top and place pats of butter on top. Bake until heated through, about 10 min.
*Could also broil, on low, just until top is browned.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description:
