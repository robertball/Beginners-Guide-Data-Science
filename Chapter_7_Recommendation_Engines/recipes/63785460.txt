Title:
Crock pot Chicken Taco meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tbl taco seasoning
2 cups chicken broth
2 lbs of chicken breast

Directions:
Disolve taco seasoning into chicken broth
Place chicken in crock pot and pour broth over
cover and cook 6-8 hours
shred chicken when done cooking
