Title:
Wonderful Yogurt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
2 cup sugar
2 teaspoon vanilla extract or almond
3 eggsy
4 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
4 containers (5.4oz) greek flavored yogurt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 4 bread pans.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg until smooth. Combine the flour, baking soda, and baking powder; stir into the batter alternately with the yogurt. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
Bake for 50 minutes in the preheated oven, until a knife inserted into the crown comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Patsy Damm Maciej
Description: 
This cake uses any kind of yogurt you have in the fridge. It is an easy way to make strawberry, raspberry, blueberry and peach cakes. Very tender and moist in the center. Excellent!Modified from: Wonderful Yogurt Cake
