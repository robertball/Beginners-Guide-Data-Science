Title:
Cashew Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups or 750 g Stock & Barrel jumbo Unsalted Cashews
1/2 - 1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 tsp sea salt (if using unsalted nuts)

Directions:
Add cashews to a food processor bowl 1 cup at a time and puree with oil drizzled in, alternating between nuts and oil, and process until desired crunchiness is attained about 10-15 minutes.
Put into a container big enough and refrigerate to keep oil from separating.
