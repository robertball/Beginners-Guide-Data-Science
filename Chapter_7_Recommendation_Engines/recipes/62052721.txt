Title:
RaRa's Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds chicken,deboned and cubed
2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups cold water
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup melted butter
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
Boil chicken in water to cover and add salt and pepper. In a mixing bowl, mix flour, water, and melted butter.  If too thin add more flour, if too thick add a little more water. Roll out on a floured surface to 1/4 inch thick, and cut into 1 inch strips.  Add dumplings to boiling chicken, until done about 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
