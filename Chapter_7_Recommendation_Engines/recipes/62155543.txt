Title:
Cherry Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crushed cornflakes
1/3 cup butter, melted
1 package(8oz.) cream cheese, softened
1 can (15 oz.) sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 tsp. vanilla
1 can (21 oz.) chilled cherry pie filling

Directions:
Mix cornflakes crumbs and butter thoroughly in 9" pan. Press firmly and evenly over the bottom and sides of pan to form crust. Chill. Beat cream cheese until light and fluffy. Add condensed milk and blend thoroughly. Stir in lemon juice and vanilla. Pour into crust. Refrigerate 3-4 hours, or until firm(do not freeze).Top with filling just before serving
