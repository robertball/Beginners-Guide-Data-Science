Title:
Green chiles stuffed with beans and quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. black beans
3/4 c. feta
3/4 c. cooked quinoa
1/4 c. chopped cilantro
2 T. chopped red onion
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
8-10 green chiles, large
1 egg
flour
cornmeal

Directions:
Combine ingredients through salt.  Stuff chiles.  (Don't worry if some split a little.)
Beat egg with a little water on a plate.  Pour some flour on another plate.  On the third plate, mix some cornmeal with a little flour.
Dip the chiles rellenos into the flour, then egg, then cornmeal.
Fry in a layer of oil till golden, turning to brown all sides.
