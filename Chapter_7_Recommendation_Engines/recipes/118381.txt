Title:
Green Beans with Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987881.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds fresh green beans, trimmed
2 cups finely chopped sweet red peppers
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons olive or canola oil
3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, or 1/3 as much dried basil
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place beans in a steamer basket over 1 in. of water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil; cover and steam for 6-8 minutes or until crisp-tender. In a nonstick skillet, saute red peppers and onion in oil. Add seasonings and beans; toss.
