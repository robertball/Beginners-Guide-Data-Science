Title:
Old-Fashioned Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound meatloaf mix
1/2 t salt
1/4 t ground pepper
1/2 cup onion, diced
1/2 cup green or red pepper, diced
1/4 cup carrot, shredded
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 cup tomato, diced
3 T ketchup
1/2 cup quick cooking oats
TOPPING:
1/4 cup ketchup
1 T mustard
1 T brown sugar

Directions:
Saute onion and pepper just until slightly translucent.
Mix all ingredients, except topping, together until well incorporated.
Form into a loaf shape in 11x7 pyrex dish.
Mix topping ingredients and spread onto meatloaf.
Bake at 375 degrees for one hour or until done.
