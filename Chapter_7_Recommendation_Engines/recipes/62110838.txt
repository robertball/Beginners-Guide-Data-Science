Title:
Smoky Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 bottle of liquid smoke
1 bottle of smokey BBQ sauce
4 slices of pastrami
8 ounces Montery Jack cheese - shredded
1 tomatoes chopped
1/2 cup green onions chopped
1 can black olives

Directions:
Marinate the chicken in the liquid smoke overnight.  Can use a large zip lock bag.
Bake the chicken in a 9X13 inch baking dish with some BBQ sauce at 350 degrees for 20-30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked.
Remove the sauce and juice from the pan.
Add additional BBQ if desired.
Place the pastrami, cheese, tomatoes, green onions and black olives (other toppings?) on top of each piece of chicken.
Broil until cheese is melted.
