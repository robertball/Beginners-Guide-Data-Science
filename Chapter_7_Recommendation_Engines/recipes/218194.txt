Title:
Creamy Ham and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979373.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup KRAFT Mayo with Olive Oil & Cracked Pepper Reduced Fat Mayonnaise
1/3 cup milk
2 cups chopped cooked ham
2 cups croutons
1 1/2 cups KRAFT Shredded Three Cheese with a Touch of PHILADELPHIA
1 tomato, chopped
1/4 cup chopped red onions

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine ingredients.
Spoon into 2-qt. casserole.
Bake 35 min. or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Kraft
Description: 
Ham and cheese--a classic pairing--make a delicious, hot and creamy casserole that's on the table in under an hour.
