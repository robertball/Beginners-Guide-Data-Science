Title:
Pork Medallions Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979761.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) pork tenderloin, cut into 1/2 inch slices
1/2 (250 g) tub PHILADELPHIA Herb & Garlic Cream Cheese Spread
1/3 cup 25%-less-sodium chicken broth
1/4 cup KRAFT Signature Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
1/4 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped

Directions:
Heat large nonstick skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium-high heat. Add meat; cook 2 min. on each side or until lightly browned on both sides.
Add all remaining ingredients except basil; cook 6 min. or until cream cheese spread is melted, sauce is thickened and meat is done, stirring frequently.
Sprinkle with basil.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia Cream Cheese
Description: 
Our quick and creamy Alfredo sauce is the crowning glory of this delicious pork dish.
