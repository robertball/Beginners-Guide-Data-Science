Title:
Tasty Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15 oz) cans black beans
1 (11 oz) can yellow corn
1 t salt
1/4 t black pepper
2-3 cloves garlic, diced
4 fresh tomatoes, diced
1/4-1/2 red onion, diced (to taste)
1/4 cup scallions, diced
chopped cilantro, to taste
juice of 1/2 lime

Directions:
1. In a saucepan over medium heat, cook beans, corn, salt, pepper, and garlic until heated through.
2. Combine warm ingredients with chopped fresh ingredients. Squeeze lime juice over salsa, mix together.
3. Chill in fridge for several hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
Always a hit at parties--people are often amazed to know you made it yourself! Quite a bit of chopping involved, but otherwise a breeze to throw together.
