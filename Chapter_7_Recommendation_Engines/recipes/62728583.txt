Title:
White Wine Sauce - Vegetarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4T coconut oil
4T flour (any wheat type) or corn starch
1C white wine
4C veggie broth (homemade or pre-packaged)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt the coconut oil, then remove from heat and let cool slightly.
Fold in the flower or corn starch so that there are no lumps.
Slowly add the wine and broth, evenly mixing the ingredients.
Return to heat, stirring until the sauce is thickened.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Serve immediately.

Description:
