Title:
Molasses Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup of shorting
1/4 cup of molasses
1 cup of sugar
2 cups of flour
1 egg
2 teaspoon of cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon of salt
2 teaspoon of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon of cloves
1/2 teaspoon of ginger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees farenheight.
Melt shorting in saucepan.
Remove from heat-let cool.
Then add sugar, molasses, and egg(beaten well).
Sift flour with baking soda, cloves, ginger, cinnamon, and salt.
Add this to the first mixture and mix well.
Chill in refridgerator.
Form 1/2 inch balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Dip a glass bottom in sugar and flatten balls with sugar coated glass.
Finally bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes.  let them cool and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Melanie
Description: 
These cookies are origially Sweedish, and are called Pepparkakor They are served on December 13th, or Feast of Saint Lucy.
