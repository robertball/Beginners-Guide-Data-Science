Title:
Honey Mustard & Bacon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/572213.jpg
Ingredients:
5 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 C. Honey Mustard (I used Honey Dijon)
1 box pre-cooked sliced bacon
1 1/2 C. shredded cheddar cheese
2 T. olive oil
salt and pepper to season chicken

Directions:
Cook bacon in microwave as pkg directs-then chop
salt and pepper chicken.
Saute` chicken in heated oil on med high
brush tops of chicken w/ honey mustard and sprinkle w/ bacon, then cheese.
cover to melt cheese

Submitter: 
Amy Russell
Description: 
Great chicken recipe when your tired of the same old chicken dishes.
