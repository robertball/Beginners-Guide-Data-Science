Title:
Pina Colada Dip for Coconut shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup pina colada drink mix ( liquid kind)
1/4 cup crushed pineapple well drained
1 tablespoon brown sugar

Directions:
Mix everything together and chill for about an hour to let the flavours meld together.

Submitter: 
BEC
Description: 
A creamy dip that goes great with coconut shrimp or chicken.
