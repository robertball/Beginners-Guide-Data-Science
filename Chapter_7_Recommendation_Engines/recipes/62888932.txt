Title:
Mexican Sour Cream Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/697578.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked long grain white rice
1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
1 1/2  cup reduced fat sour cream
2 (4 ounce) cans diced green chile peppers
1 14.5 ounce diced petite tomatoes
1 package of taco seasoning mix
1 1/2  cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese, divided
1 1/2  cup frozen whole kernel corn
dash of cumin
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot, bring the rice and chicken broth to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 20 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
In the pot with the cooked rice, mix the sour cream, green chile peppers, 1/2 of the Monterey Jack cheese, corn, tomatoes, taco seasoning mix, cumin and cilantro. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to the prepared casserole dish,
Bake uncovered 25 minutes in the preheated oven. Add remaing cheese and cook until cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
