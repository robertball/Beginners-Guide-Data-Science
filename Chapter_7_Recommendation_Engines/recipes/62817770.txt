Title:
T's Easy Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664443.jpg
Ingredients:
2 8oz packages cream cheese
3/4 - 1 cup sugar
2 large eggs
1/4 cup margarine (1/2 stick)
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 9" graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Allow cream cheese, margarine, and eggs to come to room temperature. Cream margarine and cream cheese together until smooth. Add Sugar and beat in eggs. Add vanilla. Pour mixture into pie crust. Bake for 35 minutes or until slightly firm. Refrigerate until thoroughly cooled.

Submitter: 
BROWNSKYNLADY
Description: 
The tastiest and easiest cheesecake you will ever bake! This quick and easy cheesecake base can be eaten plain or topped with your favorite fruit topping.
