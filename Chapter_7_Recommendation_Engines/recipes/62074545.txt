Title:
Mars Bar Rice Krispie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Mars Bars
1/2 c butter or margarine
3 cups Rice Krispies
1 cup milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt butter and Mars bars on low heat in large saucepan until smooth. Remove from heat. Stir in Rice Krispies. Press into a greased 8 inch square pan. Melt chocolate chips in a pot with a tsp of butter stir until smooth. spread chocolate evenly over squares. Put in refigerator until cool. Cut into squares and serve.

Submitter: 
JulsCampbell
Description: 
A delicous square passed down in my family that combines two favorite snacks into one amazing treat. Always a hit at parties.
