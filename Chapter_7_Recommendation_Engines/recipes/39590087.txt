Title:
Crispy Chicken Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
vegetable oil
2 C bisquick
1 can or bottle (12oz) beer (1 1/2 C)
1/4 C grated Parmesan cheese
2 # boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch pieces
salt
1 bottle sweet and sour sauce

Directions:
Heat oil (1 inch) in Dutch oven or deep fryer to 375 degrees. Mix baking mix, beer and cheese, let stand until mixture thickens, about 5 min. Dip chicken into batter, letting excess drip into bowl. (Make sure pieces are separated). Fry 10-12 pieces at a time, turning if necessary, until golden brown, 3-4 min. Sprinkle with salt. Serve immed with sweet and sour sauce.
