Title:
Country Ham and Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. cubed cooked ham
1/2 c. chopped green onions
1/2 c. margarine or butter, melted
3 eggs, well beaten
1 (14.75-oz.) can cream-style corn
1 (11-oz.) can vacuum-packed whole kernel corn with red and green peppers, drained
1 (7- to 8 1/2-oz.) pkg. corn muffin mix (Jiffy is just the right size)
1 c. shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°F. Grease 12x8-inch (2-quart) baking dish.
In large bowl, combine all ingredients except cheese. Pour into greased baking dish. Bake at 350°F. for 45 to 55 minutes or until golden brown and set.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Bake an additional 1 to 2 minutes or until cheese is melted. Let stand 5 minutes before serving. Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
This retro dish is absolutely delicious! A great way to deal with left-over Easter ham.
