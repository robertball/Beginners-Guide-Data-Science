Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
3 cups of frozen broccoli
1.5 cups of quinoa (uncooked)
3 cups water
2 lbs of boneless chicken breasts (frozen ok)
.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Place quinoa on bottom of pan
Place frozen broccoli on top
Add water
Place chicken breasts on top of broccoli
Add cream of mushroom soup on top (do not mix in)
Add cheddar cheese on top
Cover with aluminum foil
Bake 30 minutes covered
15 minutes uncovered

Submitter: 
Paula DiBacco
Description: 
Easy recipe; can freeze and eat later; great for packed lunch or an all in one dinner.
