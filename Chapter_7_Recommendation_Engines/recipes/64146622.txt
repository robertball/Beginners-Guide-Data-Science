Title:
Salmon Tuna Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can 14.7oz salmon
2 can tuna
1 egg
1/4 c flour
1 sleeve saltine crackers
oil for frying

Directions:
Drain salmon and tuna. In large bowl mix with hands tuna, salmon, egg, crackers. Form in to hamburger sized patties. Heat oil in skillet medium heat. Dust patties in flour then add to hot oil. Fry with lid on 10-15 minutes each side. Remove from pan and serve.

Submitter: 
Wendy Meyer
Description: 
Tuna patties with a twist. Salmon flavor with tuna is milder. Kids and adults love the crispy outside and meaty patty.
