Title:
Chicken Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless,skinless chicken breasts
1C Flour
2Tbsp Olive oil
1C Dry white wine
1C Chicken stock
Juice from 1/2 lemon
2Tbsp Small capers
1Tbsp Unsalted butter

Directions:
Pound chicken breasts to 1/2 inch thick and dredge in flour. In a large skillet heat olive oil until hot over medium high heat. Add chicken and brown on both sides. Carefully add wine and chicken stock. Bring to a boil and cook to reduce sauce by 1/2, about 15 minutes. Add lemon juice and capers, cook for 2 more minutes. Remove chicken to serving platter. Add butter to sauce and stir until melted. Pour sauce over chicken.

Description: 
A delightful Italian chicken dish made with white wine,lemon and capers. Simple yet elegant.
