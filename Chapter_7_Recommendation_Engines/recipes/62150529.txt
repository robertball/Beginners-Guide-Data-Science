Title:
Rolled Stuffed Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork tenderloin
1 red pepper, cut in strips
1 onion, sliced in rings
4-5 mushrooms, sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
Handful of fresh basil, coarsely chopped
1-2T olive oil
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat saute pan.  Add olive oil. Saute peppers and onions until semi soft.  Add mushrooms. Saute 1 minute. Add garlic and basil and saute 30 seconds more. Turn off heat and set aside.
Lay tenderloin out and slice lengthwise about half the way thru. Pound out until it lays relatively flat. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Lay sauted vegetables on meat and roll up jellyroll style and secure with string.
Coat baking dish with non stick spray and place rolls in baking dish. Sprinkle tops with seasoning salt and pepper.   Bake at 375 for about an hour or until meat thermometer reaches 165. Let rest for 15 min. then slice and serve!

Submitter: 
Audrey Ansell
Description: 
This is a simple roast to prepare but has an elegant presentation that looks like you spent hours on it.  It is moist and flavorful and I get rave reviews each time I serve it.  Goes well with roasted new potatoes.
