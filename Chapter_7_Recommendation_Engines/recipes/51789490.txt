Title:
Old Fashioned Soft Sugar Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 C shortening
1 C sugar
1 egg yolk
1/2 C buttermilk
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 egg white beaten until stiff

Directions:
Preheat oven 375
Sift flour,salt,soda together
Cream sugar,shortening,egg yolk
until light and fluffy
Alternately mix in flour and buttermilk
Add vanilla
Fold in egg white
Drop by rounded tblsp 3" apart on greased cookie sheet
With spatula flatten to 1/2" thick
Bake 20 minutes or until golden
makes 1 1/2 dozen
