Title:
Quick Garlic Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups self-rising flour*
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon Onion Powder
1 teaspoon Parsley Flakes
1 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup melted butter(salted)
1 egg

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour, cheese, sugar and garlic powder. In another bowl, whisk the milk, oil and egg. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Pour into a greased 9-in. x 5-in. x 3-in. loaf pan Or put in muffin pan filled 1/2 way in each muffin. Bake at 350 degrees F for 55-66 minutes for bread pan or 20 minutes for muffins or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.
*To make 1cup self rising flour mix 1 1/2tsp baking powder with 1/2tsp salt and fill remainder of cup with flour.
