Title:
Gazpacho Classico Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, peeled
1 carrot, diced
Several stalks of coriander
1 pint tomato juice
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons vinegar
Fresh garlic to taste
Several ice cubes

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in blender or smoothie maker until completely pureed.
Pour into pint glasses, and serve immediately. Can also be refrigerated and served later.

Submitter: 
E
Description: 
A pureed version of the amazing Spanish cold summer soup. For best taste, make with vegan/organic products.
