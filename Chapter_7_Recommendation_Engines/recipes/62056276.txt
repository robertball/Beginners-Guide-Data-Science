Title:
Amazing Sour Cream Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. butter (2 sticks)
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 pint sour cream
3 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
pinch salt
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup walnuts
Topping:
1/3 cup sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a large tube pan. In a large bowl, beat butter until smooth. Add sugar and beat until combined. Beat in one egg at a time. Stir in the vanilla and sour cream. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add flour mixture to the wet mixture, one cup at a time. Then add chocolate chips and walnuts. Pour into prepared pan. In a small bowl, combine 1/3 cup sugar and 2 tsp. cinnamon. Sprinkle on top of batter and swirl through using a butter knife to create a marbled effect. Bake at 350 for 1 hour, or until wooden pick inserted comes out clean. Cool in pan, then turn out onto serving plate.

GINAGINA
Description: 
Deliciously moist coffee cake, with chocolate chips, walnuts, and cinnamon.
