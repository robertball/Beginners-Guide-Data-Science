Title:
Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 cups old fashioned oats
1 cup each of:
almonds
pecans
sunflower seeds
pumpkin seeds
pistachio nuts
coconut
wheat germ
3/4 cup canola oil
3/4 cup honey
Dash of vanilla

Directions:
Mix  all in a large bowl
Heat oil, honey and vanilla
Add to mixture and stir
Bake at 300 on cookie sheets
Stir every 15 minutes for an hour
