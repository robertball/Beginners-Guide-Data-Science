Title:
Pumpkin Pie Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup All-Purpose Flour
1/4 Tsp Baking Powder
1/4 TsP Baking Soda
1/4 Tsp Cinnamon
1/8 Tsp Nutmeg
1/8 Tsp Salt
1/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
2 Tbsp Butter, Softened
1 Egg White
1/4 Cup Canned Pumpkin Puree
2 Tbsp Skim Milk
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract (divided)
8 Muffin Liner Cups
2 Tbsp Lowfact Cream Cheese (room temperature)
2 Tsp Margarine
2 tbsp Powdered Sugar
2 Tbsp Plain Yogurt

Directions:
Heat over to 350. Mix flour, baking poweder, baking sodea, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt until combined. Set aside.
Mash granulated sugar and butter in another bowl until combined. Stir in egg white, pumpkin, mile and 1/2 tsp vanilla extract. Add dry ingerdients, stir until combined. Pour batter into muffin cups- about 3/4 full. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes.
Beat cream cheese, margarine and powdered sugar until smooth. Add yogurt and remaining vanilla; beat until combined.
Frost cupcakes when cupcakes cool.
