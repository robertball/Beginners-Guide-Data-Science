Ingredients:
2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp smoked Paprika
1 TBL fresh chopped garlic
1/4 cup chopped DILL relish
1 TBL SWEET pickle relish
2 TBL Zatarans spicy mustard
2 TBL extra-virgin olive oil
2 TBL Sriracha Sauce (Rooster Sauce)
1 TBL Sriracha chile garlic sauce
1 TBL fresh lemon juice
1 TBL Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and serve.

Submitter: 
Jeffrey Morris
Description: 
I have tasted and prepared many versions of Remoulade Sauce.  This is tasty and rich and easy to make.  Most of these ingredients are quite common in a Foodie's pantry. To make a milder version, remove the Sriracha Sauce (Rooster Sauce).
