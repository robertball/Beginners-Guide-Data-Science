Title:
Oven Fried Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986839.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup MIRACLE WHIP or MIRACLE WHIP LIGHT Dressing
2 teaspoons Creole seasoning
2 pounds catfish fillets
1 cup crushed POST TOASTIES Corn Flakes
1/2 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Mix dressing and seasoning; coat fish. Coat fish with combined crushed corn flakes and cheese.
Place fish in 15x10x1-inch baking pan.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 25 to 30 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork.
