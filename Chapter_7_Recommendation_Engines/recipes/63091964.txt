Title:
Sour Cream Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969253.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cucumbers-sliced thin
1 small Onion-sliced thin
1/2 C Sour Cream
1/3 C Vinegar
3/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
3 Tbsp Sugar
1/4 tsp Dry Mustard
1 Tbsp Parsley

Directions:
Slice cucumbers and onion thin, mix dressing ingredients and pour over vegetables, mix and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
sassyoldlady
Description: 
Thin sliced cukes and onion in a sour cream sauce.
