Title:
Famous Molasses Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2 cups shortening
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup molasses (light or dark)
2 eggs
4 t baking soda
4 cups sifted flour
1 1/2 t cloves
2 t ginger
4 t cinnamon
1t salt

Directions:
Mix shortening, sugar, molasses and eggs together.  Mix baking soda, flour, cloves, ginger, cinnamon and salt together.  Add to shortening mixture, mixing well.  Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes. Sprinkle with raw sugar while hot
