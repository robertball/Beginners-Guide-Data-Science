Title:
FAT GRANNYS  OLD FASHIONED SWISS STEAK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8(4oz)cube steaks
1 lg sliced onion
1 lb sliced mushrooms
1 tsp minced garlic
1 (15oz) can diced tomatoes in tomato sauce
4 cups flour
1 cup vegetable oil
1 stick butter
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp coarse ground pepper
1 tsp granulated garlic
1 tsp granulated onion
1 tsp celery seed
1 tbl maggi seasoning
4 cups beef stock

Directions:
pre heat oven to 350 degrees.
In a 6x8 baking pan add the flour, salt and pepper.stir to combine.
dip the steak into the flour and coat well.shake off excess flour and put on a wire rack.continue until all 8 steaks are coated.we will get back to the steaks.
--- now for the sauce ---
In a medium sauce pan,on medium high heat,melt the butter, add the onions and mushrooms.saute for 3-4 min.add the mince garlic, seasonings and tomatoes.stir for about 1-2 min.add the beef stock and stir. reduce heat and simmer for 5 min.after 5 min.stir and turn heat off.
--- back to the steaks---
heat a large skillet to medium high.add 1/2 of the oil.when the oil is hot add 4 of the steaks into the skillet.after 1 to 2 min.turn the steaks and brown the other side for another 1 to 2 min.( very important: you are not cooking the steaks.you are just browning them on each side.they will cook in the oven.)remove and set on paper towels.add the remaining oil to the skillet and when oil is hot add the 4 remaining steaks.cook the same as the first 4.set aside with the other steaks
In a 9x13 baking dish add 1 cup of the sauce to the bottom of the dish (spreading evenly).arrange steaks in 2 rows of 4.pour sauce over all of the steaks.cover with foil  and bake for 45min.remove from oven and serve.
