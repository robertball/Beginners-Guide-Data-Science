Title:
Dilly of a Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cooked pizza crust
1/2 cup each sour cream, mayo
3 oz cream cheese softened
1 tsp each dill, onion, garlic, seasoning salt
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Assorted vegetables ( mushrooms, peppers, tomatoes, onions, etc)
salami slices or other meat sliced]

Directions:
Blend sour cream, mayo and cream cheese, add seasonings and spread on cooked pizza crust. Arrange vegetables and meat on top and sprinkle on cheese. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
