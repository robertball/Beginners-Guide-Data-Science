Title:
vinegar braised chicken with green Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken thighs and legs or thighs
2TBS olive oil
1 medium onion sliced
6 cloves garlic smashed
2 bay leaves
2 sprigs thyme
11/2 c wine vinegar
1 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup sour cream
1 bunch collard greens

Directions:
season chicken liberally with salt and pepper.Add oil to dutch oven. add chicken skin side down. brown and flip to other side.cook 5 more minutes. Transfer to platter.
reduce hear to med. Add onion and garlic to pot and stir, scraping up remaining chicken bits. Add bay leaf and thyme. Cook onions 3-5 minutes or until soft and golden. Deglaze pot with vinegar, stirring and scraping up crusty bits. Simmer 5 mins. until liquid is reduced by half.
add chicken stock and bring to boil.Add greens and stir until wilted. Cook and cover greens until tender about 45 mins. Add chicken skin side up and cook for about 10 minutes.
Transfer chicken to serving platter and bring sauce to a boil. reduce heat and add sour cream. cook 3 mins.
