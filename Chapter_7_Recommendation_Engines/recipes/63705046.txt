Title:
Teriyaki Beef with Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2369741.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup Kikkoman Teriyaki (Less Sodium
Honey (see note below)
½ tsp. ginger
¼ tsp. ground black pepper
1 tbsp. olive oil
½ tsp. butter or margarine
1 medium onion
½ lb. mushrooms
3 lbs. stewing beef

Directions:
In a one cup measuring cup measure ½ cup of teriyaki.  Add honey until it measure 2/3 of a cup.  Add ginger and black pepper.  Mix and set to one side.
Chop onion and mushrooms.  Cut stewing beef into bit size pieces.
In a non-stick fry pan set on medium high heat oil and margarine.  Add onion and fry for about 2 minutes.  Add mushrooms and fry until slightly brown.  Add beef.  Fry turning frequently until all sides are brown but not cooked all the way through.
Add the teriyaki mixture and mix.   Turn the heat down to simmer and cook for at least 2 hours.  (The longer the more tender the meat)  Serve with rice, egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
