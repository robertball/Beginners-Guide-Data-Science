Title:
Texas Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
2 cubes butter
2 C Flour
2 eggs
1 C water
2 C Sugar
1/2 C Sour Cream
4 T Cocoa
1/2 t. Salt
1 t. Soda
Frosting
1 cube butter
6 T milk
1 T Vanilla
4 T Cocoa
1 lb powder sugar
1 C Walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Cake: Boil butter, water and cocoa.
Remove from heat and add flour, sugar, and salt
Beat eggs, sour cream, and soda
Pour on 12x18 baking sheet.
Bake at 350Â° for 22 min.
Frosting: Boil butter, cocoa and milk.
Remove from hear and add powder sugar, vanilla, and nuts.
Mix well and frost cake while still warm.
