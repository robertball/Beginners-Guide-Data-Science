Title:
Hot Mocha Mix in a Jar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987249.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 1/4 cups non-fat milk powder
3/4 cup powdered non-dairy creamer
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/4 cup instant coffee granules

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, milk powder, creamer, cocoa, and instant coffee. Mix together until well blended.  Store in a sealed container.
To serve, heat one cup of water, and stir in 2 to 3 heaping teaspoons of cocoa mixture.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This hot mocha mix is a great gift idea.  Simply place the mix in decorated jars or nice glass containers, write mixing instructions, and give to your favorite teacher or coach for the holidays.
