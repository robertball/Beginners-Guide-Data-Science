Title:
Big Ray's Scotch Date Roasted Potatotes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1151613.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped dated
2 cloves garlic
1 Tbl minced fresh rosemary
1/4 cup scotch whiskey
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
2 Tbl butter, melted
2 lbs red potatoes, quartered
3 scallions, thinly sliced
1/4 cup blue cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil and coat with cooking spray.
In a blender or food processor, combine dates, garlic, rosemary whiskey, salt pepper, vinegar and melted butter.  Puree until smooth.
In a large bowl, combine sauce and potatoes and mix until completely coated.  Spread onto prepared baking sheet and bake until browned and tender, about 25-35 minutes.  Serve with scallions and blue cheese.
