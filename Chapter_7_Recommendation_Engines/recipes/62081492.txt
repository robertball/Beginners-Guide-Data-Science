Title:
Darling's Maple Walnut Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. butter
12 oz evaporated milk
24 oz white chocolate chips
1 jar marshmallow fluff
1 tbsp. maple extract
2 c. chopped walnuts

Directions:
Melt butter with milk in large saucepan.  Add sugars.  Cook over medium heat until softball stage 234 degrees.  Remove from heat stir in marshmallow fluff, white chocolate, and extract until all is melted.  Stir in nuts.  Pour into foil lined 9 by 13 pan.  Cool to room temperature.  Makes a big batch.

Submitter: 
DarlingDash
Description: 
I came up with this recipe when I realized I had put too much butter in the beginning of making a peanut butter fudge.  It is delicious.  Glad I messed up....this time.  Feel free to half the recipe and use an 8 by 8 pan.
