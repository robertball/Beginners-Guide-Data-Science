Title:
Mexican Martinis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 limes
2 shots good tequila
1 shot orange liqueur (Grand Marnier or Gran Gala)
1/2-1 shot Rose's sweetened lime juice (optional)
1/2 - 1 t. superfine sugar
Jalepeno stuffed olives
Coarse/kosher salt for glasses

Directions:
Squeeze the limes into a cocktail shaker using a lime squeezer. Salt the rims of two chilled martini glasses using a bit of the lime juice.  Place a jalepeno stuffed olive (or 2)into each glass. Add the tequila, orange liqueur, Rose's sweetened lime juice, and the sugar to the lime juice in the shaker glass. Fill with ice, shake vigorously, and strain into martini glasses.

Submitter: 
JACKSMOM
Description: 
These are a bit labor intensive but worth it. Fresh limes and a good lime squeezer are a must.
