Title:
Southern Sausage Cheese Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups water
2 cups quick grits
1 teaspoon salt
1 pound smoked sausage-cut in pieces
1 pound processed cheese-cut in cubes
1 1/2 stick butter

Directions:
Place sausage on a baking sheet with sides.  Cook in preheated oven at 350 degrees. Cook for about 15 mintues until heated thoroughly.  Put the water in a large pan and bring to a boil.  Add grits slowly while stirring.  Bring back to a boil and reduce heat, cover with a lid.  Cook grits for 7 minutes, stirring frequently.  Add 1 1/2 sticks butter and stir until melted. Add cheese and stir until melted.    Pour grits in to heated slow cooker, add sausage and stir well.  Leave heat on low until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
SHARONAIR
Description: 
A great breakfast or brunch dish all in one that is especially great for the holidays!  You can mix it and put it in the crock pot so that everyone can eat at their leisure.
