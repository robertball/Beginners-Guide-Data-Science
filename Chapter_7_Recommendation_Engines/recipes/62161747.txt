Title:
Baltimore-Style Peach Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup sugar
2 1/2 cups flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 cups milk
3/4 cup sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon
5 large peaches, peeled and sliced
2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
With electric mixer, blend first five ingredients. Spread in greased and floured 15x10" jelly roll pan.
Mix together the sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle half the mixture lightly over dough base.
Arrange peach slices in rows on top of cinnamon sugar. Sprinkle with remainder of cinnamon sugar mixture and drizzle melted butter over all.
Bake for 25-30 minutes.
After cooling, slice into squares.

Submitter: 
FoodJunkie
Description: 
I grew up eating this cake...all of the local Baltimore bakeries sell them. I never realized that this type of cake is unique to Baltimore until I grew up and moved away for a bit. True Baltimore peach cake is a flat, square cake topped with peaches.
