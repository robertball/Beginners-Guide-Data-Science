Title:
Crazy Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.0 c sugar
1.5 c flour
0.5 t  salt
1.0 t  baking soda
3.0 T cocoa
--
6 T vegatable oil
1 T vinegar
1 t  vanilla
1 c water

Directions:
1. Put first five ingredients in 8 or 9-inch pan and stir with fork until well mixed.
2. Make 3 wells in the dry ingredients. In one well put oil, in another put vinegar, and in another put vanilla.
3. Pour water over all and thoroughly mix.
Bake at 325 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
