Title:
Super Simple Berry Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034851.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 Tablespoons honey
1 cup vanilla yogurt
2 cups sliced strawberries
1 cup blueberries
1/2 Angel food cake

Directions:
Beat the cream cheese, honey & vanilla yogurt together until well mixed.  Cut the angel food cake into small one inch cubes and layer half of the cubes in the bottom of the serving bowl.  Layer one cup of sliced strawberries and Â½ cup of blueberries on the layer.  Pour half of the yogurt mixture over the fruit and spread evenly.  Place remainder of angel food cubes on fruit, top with the remainder of the strawberries and blueberries.  Press down lightly.  Pour the remainder of the yogurt mixture over the top.  Cover and refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving.  Garnish with whipped cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
A quick and easy dessert.  Any summer fruit can be substituted for the strawberries and/or blueberries.
