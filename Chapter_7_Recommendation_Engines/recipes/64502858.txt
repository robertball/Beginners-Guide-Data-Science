Title:
Stuffed Delicata Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2621129.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Delicata Squash
2 Medium Sized Apples (of any variety)
2 Slices of Bacon
1/4 cup onion
1/8 cup goat cheese
Olive Oil
Rosemary
Garlic
Cinnamon
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven at 400 degrees.
2. Cut the Delicata squash in half, longways, and clean out the seeds.
3. Brush the inside of the squash with olive oil and bake for about 30-45 minutes, until it is soft and browned on the edges.
4. While the squash is baking, dice the apples and onion and saute them with a little bit of olive oil.
5. Dice the garlic and add it to the saute.
6. Add rosemary, cinnamon, salt and pepper to taste.
7. Let the apple and onion mix simmer as you fry two slices of bacon. Make sure to fry the bacon until it is crispy, but not burned.
8. Once the squash are done baking, pull them out of the oven and fill the middle with the apple and onion mixture. Crumble the bacon on top.
9. Finish the dish off with some goat cheese.

Submitter: 
Olivia
Description: 
This delicious fall recipe combines the savory and sweet for a dish that warms the soul. I first made it after receiving some Delicata squash from a friend's farm. I never knew squash could be so good!
