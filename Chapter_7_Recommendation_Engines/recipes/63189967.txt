Title:
Shooter Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/822248.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lg Onion
1 Lb bacon
1 Lb mushrooms
1/2 Lb Swiss Cheese, Sliced
2 ribeye steaks
1 round loaf italian bread
Dijon
Horseradish
Worcestershire

Directions:
Cook steaks until rare
Cook bacon
Cook saute onions and mushrooms
Hollow out bread and save top
place 1 steak in bottom of bread
top steak with dijon/horseradish on one half, worcestershire on other
Layer bacon over steak
Layer cheese over bacon
Layer in mushrrom/onion  mixture
Layer remaining steak on top, with pan juices
top steak with dijon/horseradish on one half, worcestershire on other
Layer more cheese on top
Pu top back on
wrap in butcher paper
wrap in foil
compress with weight until flattened
slice and serve.
