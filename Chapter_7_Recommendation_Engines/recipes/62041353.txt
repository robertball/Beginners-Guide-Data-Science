Title:
Lemon Drop Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ice
half a lemon
lemon or citron vodka
triple sec
simple syrup
sugar

Directions:
1  Squeeze juice from lemon half over ice in a shaking container.  Addemon half to ice and juice.
2  Add equal parts triple sec and vodka to container.
3  Add one teaspoon simple syrup.
4  Shake vigorously and strain into chilled cocktail glass with a sugared rim.

Submitter: 
DOCTOR KITTEN
Description: 
A delicious and refreshing sweet lemon drink.
