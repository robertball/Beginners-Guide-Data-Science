Title:
Magically Moist Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Chicken pieces or boneless, skinless chicken breast
1/2 c. Mayonnaise
1 1/4 c. Italian Seasoned Breadcrumbs, or Plain Bread Crumbs with your own seasoning mix

Directions:
Brush chicken on all sides with mayonnaise.
Shake chicken, one piece at a time, with breadcrumbs in large plastic food bag.
Arrange chicken on rack in broiler pan.
Bake at 425F degrees for about 40 minutes or until golden brown and juice runs clear when pierced with fork.
