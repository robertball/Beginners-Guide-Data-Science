Title:
Garlic Rosemary Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts (28 oz)
1 T fresh rosemary
16 garlic cloves, crushed
3 small red onions, diced
2 t. olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Combine onions, rosemary, garlic and olive oil.
Place chicken in a baking pan, top with onion mixture and bake for 30 minutes, or until no longer pink in the middle.
