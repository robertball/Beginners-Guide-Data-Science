Title:
crockpot beef stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds beef cut into 1 inch pieces(use whatever, very good with sirloin(expensive)  or chuck roast(cheap))
season salt
pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 large onion, sliced
8 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 can (10.75 oz) cream of mushroom soup
2 large cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon A-1 sauce
1 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon cornstarch dissolved in small amount of water(if needed depending on how thick)

Directions:
Place the beef in the bottom of a slow cooker, and season with salt and pepper, and garlic powder to taste. Place onion on top of beef, and then add mushroom soup, mushrooms, garlic, and A-1, and stir.
Cover, and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours. Stir in the sour cream and cornstarch, (if needed), and continue cooking for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
greenbabby
Description: 
Easy delicious weeknight meal. Just throw everything in the slowcooker before you go to work. Serve over egg noodles, rice, or even mashed potatoes!
