Title:
Summer Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Sandwich Size English Muffins (Jumbo)
1 Tall Jar roasted red peppers
4 Jalapeno Jack Cheese Slices
1-2 Avocados
1/2 Red Onion thinly sliced
Parmesan Cheese
kosher salt

Directions:
Split muffins, place onion down first, cover with avocado, jalapeno cheese, red peppers and sprinkle with parmesan and a pinch of kosher salt.  Put in broiler on low and watch carefully until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Cari
Description: 
These are so easy and delicious! Even great the next day cold!!!
