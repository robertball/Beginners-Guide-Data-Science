Title:
Dinomite's  Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2452260.jpg
Ingredients:
CUPCAKES
1 box white cake mix
3 egg whites
1 1/4 Cups water
1/3 Cup vegetable oil
2 Cups melted mini marshmallows
2 TBS melted butter
FROSTING
2 Large sweet potatoes
1 tsp cinnamon
2 Cups confectioner's sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 TBS water
6 TBS Butter
TOPPING
24 regular marshmallows
24 sparklers
cinnamon to adorn

Directions:
In a medium pot boil water for potatoes. Boil the potatoes until fork tender. Let cool. Preheat oven to 325. Prepare cake mix according to the box. In a medium pot melt together the marshmallows and butter. Once the marshmallow is melted, fold into the cake mixture. Grease or line the cupcake pan. Fill each whole 2/3 of the way full and bake for 22-29 min. FOR FROSTING: in a large mixing bowl, mix together all frosting ingredients until light and fluffy. Set aside. Once the cupcakes are done and have had a chance to cool, pipe the sweet potato frosting onto each one in a delicate way. Top each cupcake with a large marshmallow, sprinkle cinnamon on top, and ad a sparkler. Light and enjoy!
