Title:
Skyline® Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4466198.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, slightly softened
2 (16 ounce) cans chili without beans (such as Skyline Chili®)
2 cups shredded mild Cheddar cheese, or more to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spread cream cheese evenly in the bottom of an 8x8-inch baking dish; top with chili. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over chili layer.
Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
MIDWESTCINCY
Description: 
A quick and easy Cincinnati favorite!
 Excellent dip. Garnish with jalapeno peppers and serve with tortilla chips.
