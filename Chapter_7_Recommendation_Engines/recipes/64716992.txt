Title:
Roasted Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5787915.jpg
Ingredients:
3 heads broccoli
2 tbsp olive oil
1 pinch salt
1 pinch pepper

Directions:
Cut the broccoli into florets, leaving a little bit of the stem on each piece.
Lay the broccoli florets flat onto a baking sheet covered with tin foil. (Be sure not to overcrowd the pan.)
Drizzle with olive oil and toss until well coated.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Roast in a 400Â°F oven for about 15 minutes.
Remove from oven and enjoy!
