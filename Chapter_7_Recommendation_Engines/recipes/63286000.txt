Title:
Easy Cheater Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 cup half and half
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1 tbsp garlic powder
Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, melt the butter. Once nearly melted, add the cream cheese. For smoother melting, cut cream cheese block into 6 or 8 pieces. Once butter and cream cheese are melted and blended, add the cup of half and half, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder and pepper. Stir until well blended. Remove from heat and set aside to allow to thicken up. Stir occasionally while thickening. Serve with your favorite pasta.
