Title:
Crisp Pickled Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs fresh picked green beans
6 1/2 pint canning jars and caps
2 1/2 cups white vinegar
2 cups water
1/4 cup salt
1 clove fresh garlic
6 fresh dill sprigs
3/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
Cut beans 1/4 inch shorter than height of canning jar.
Prepare jars and caps for processing
In a 2 quart saucepan over high heat, heat vinegar, water, salt and garlic to boiing, in each hot, sterilized jar, place 1 dill sprig, and 1/8 tsp of red pepper flakes. Pack beans tightly into jars. Discard garlic and immediately ladle simmiring liquid over beans to 1/4 inch from the top. Close jars according to manufacturer's instructions. Process jars in boiling-water bath for 10 minutes. Cool jars and check that they have sealed.

Submitter: 
Jani  Whitsett
Description: 
This recipe is from my Grandmother's cookbook she made to pass to all her grandchildren. They come out very, very crisp with a wonderful dill flavor. The red pepper flakes give them a nice punch. They are much better than the recipes that call for cooking the beans first.
