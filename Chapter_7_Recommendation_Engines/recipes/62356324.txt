Title:
Chocolate Cookie Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (8 squares) Bakers Semi sweet baking chocolate (I usually just use semisweet chocolate chips)
1 pkg (6 squares) white chocolate (white chocolate chips)
2 tbsp. peanut butter
10 Oreo Cookies

Directions:
Put each chocolate type in two bowls and microwave on half power until melted.  Add in peanut butter into the white chocolate and stir until blended.  Crumble half the cookies in each bowl.
Drop spoonfuls of chocolate mixtures onto wax paper covered sheet, alternating the different chocolates.   Cut through with knife for marble effect.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm.
