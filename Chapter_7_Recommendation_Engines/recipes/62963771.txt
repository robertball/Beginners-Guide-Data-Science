Title:
Tamale Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup chopped green pepper
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 15 oz can whole kernel corn, drained
1 4 oz can sliced black olives
1 T. sugar
2 tsp. pasilla chili powder
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 box Jiffy cornbread mix
1 egg
1/3 cup milk

Directions:
Saute ground beef and spoon off fat.  Add onion and garlic, saute until meat is browned.
Add next four ingredients and spices.  Simmer a few more minutes.
Turn into a greased 2 quart casserole.
Cover evenly with shredded cheese.
Mix together ingredients for cornbread, spread over casserole.
Bake at 375 degrees for about 40 minutes.  Test the cornbread with toothpick, bake until cornbread is done.
