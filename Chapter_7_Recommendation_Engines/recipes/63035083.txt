Title:
Sausage, Potato, and Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/788087.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bunches kale, picked over, cleaned and torn into bite sized pieces
12 whole red potatoes, sliced thin
1 whole chopped onion
1 1/2 lb. Italian sausage
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes (more to taste)
2 cups low sodium chicken broth
2 cups whole milk
4 cups half-and-half
splash of heavy cream
fresh or dried oregano
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare the kale and set aside.
In medium pot, boil the sliced potatoes until tender. Drain and set aside.
In a large pot, crumble and brown the sausage. Drain as much fas as you can. Stir in the red pepper flakes, oregano, chicken broth, milk and half-and-half. Simmer for 30 minutes.
Add the potatoes, a splash of heavy cream for richness, then stir in the kale. Simmer an additional 10-15 minutes, then serve
