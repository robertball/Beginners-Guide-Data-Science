Title:
shrimp salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/338658.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound salad shrimp
2 roma tomatoes, diced
1/2 of a  medium-size red onion, diced
1/4 cup minced cilantro
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 finely minced cloves of  garlic

Directions:
combine all ingredients in a large glass bowl. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour for flavors to combine. Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
witchywoman
Description: 
This is an awesomely simple, light & refreshing salsa that goes great with tortilla chips.
