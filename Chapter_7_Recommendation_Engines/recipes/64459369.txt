Title:
Easy Baked Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 25 oz. bag frozen ravioli
1 jar marinara sauce
2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400. Spray bottom and sides of a 13x9 inch baking dish. Spread 3/4 cup o pasta sauce in baking dish. Arrange 1/2 the frozen ravioli in a single layer over sauce, top with 1/2 of the remaining sauce and 1/2 the Mozzarella. Repeat layers once. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 10 minutes or until bubbly. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving,
