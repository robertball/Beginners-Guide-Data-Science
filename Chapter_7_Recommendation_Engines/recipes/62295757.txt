Title:
Double Layer Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 Tbs. milk or half-and-half
1 Tbs. sugar
1 c. pumpkin
1-1/2 c. thawed Cool Whip topping
1 graham cracker pie crust
2 packages (4 serving size) vanilla instant pudding
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground cloves

Directions:
Mix softened cream cheese, milk and sugar in large bowl until smooth. Gently stir in whipped topping. Spread on bottom of crust. Pour 1 c. milk into bowl and add pudding mix. Beat with wire whisk until weel blended,2 minutes (mixture will be thick). Stir in pumpkin and spices, mix well. Spread over cream cheese layer. Refrigerate at least 3 hours. Garnish with additional whipped topping if desired.
