Title:
Country Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups thinly sliced peeled apples - mixture of Granny Smith and Macintosh
3/4 cup sugar
4 T flour
1/4 t salt
1/2 t cinnamon
1 cup heavy cream
2 uncooked refrigerated pie shells
For topping:
1 T sugar
1/4 t cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Put one pie shell into a pie pan.
Line with apples.
Mix together remaining ingredients and pour into pie shell.
Cut 2nd pie shell into strips and form a lattice over apple pie.
Mix together the 1 T sugar and 1/4 t cinnamon and sprinkle over lattice work.
400 degrees for 50 - 60 minutes
