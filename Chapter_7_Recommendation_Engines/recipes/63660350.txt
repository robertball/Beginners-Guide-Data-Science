Title:
Jennie's Poppyseed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c flour
2 1/2 c sugar
1 1/8 c oil
3 eggs
1 1/2 c milk
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 tsp poppyseed
1 1/2 tsp salt
Glaze:
3/4 c sugar
1/4 c orange juice
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 tsp butter flavoring
1 1/2 tsp almond extract

Directions:
Mix all loaf ingredients together and beat for 2 minutes.  Grease and flour 2 bread pans.  Bake 350 for 60 minutes.  Let cool 5 minutes and remove from pan.
Mix glaze.  Put loaves on foil & poke holes in top with fork.  Pour glaze over while warm.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
An awesome bread that's great for breakfast or brunch and makes a wonderful gift!
