Title:
Cornbread Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes cornbread mix (baked hard)
Longhorn Cheddar Cheese bite size
Bell Pepper (red, yellow, green--diced)
Green Onions diced
1 carton cherry tomatoes cut in half
1 can whole kernel corn drained
Bacon fried crisp and crumbled
1 pint sized miracle whip

Directions:
Cut corn bread into squares, fold all ingredients into cornbread squares.
Serve cold

Description:
