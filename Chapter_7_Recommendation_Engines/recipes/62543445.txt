Title:
Lemon Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c white sugar
grated rind of 2 lemons
juice of 3 lemons
5 beaten eggs
1/3 lb of butter

Directions:
Use low heat or double boiler.  Melt butter with sugar in pan.  Slowly add beaten eggs and lemon juice. Add lemon rind and stir constantly until thickened. Remove from heat and cool.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
Old family recipe traditionaly used at Christmas to spread on toast or crackers or put in cooked tart shells. Can be put in sterilized jars and frozen.
