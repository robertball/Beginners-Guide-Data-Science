Title:
Cheddar Apple Pie Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 tsp. gr. cinnamon
1 each green & red apple finely chopped
1/2 c. pecan pieces, chopped
1 (8oz) pkg cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 c. shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 c. sour cream

Directions:
Combine brown sugar and cinnamon in medium bowl.
Add apples & pecans.
Toss until evenly coated.
Mix cream cheese and cheddar cheese in medium bowl.
Add sour cream & stir until well blended.
Spread into 9" pie plate and top with apple mixture.
Bake 20 minutes @375 degrees or until heated through.
Serve with Wheat Thins.
