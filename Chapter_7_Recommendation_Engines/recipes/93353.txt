Title:
Jimmy Dean Breakfast Boat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985807.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces Jimmy Dean Roll Sausage
6 eggs
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup sliced sauteed mushrooms
3 cups cooked tater tots
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute sausage in a large skillet, add salt and pepper to taste. Beat eggs, adding cheese and mushrooms. Combine cooked sausage with egg mixture.
Line baking dish with tater tots and pour egg and sausage mixture over the tater tots.
Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until egg mixture is firm. Sprinkle with extra cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Jimmy Dean
Description: 
A quick breakfast treat the family will love. Tater tots are baked with eggs, sausage, mushroom, and Cheddar cheese.
