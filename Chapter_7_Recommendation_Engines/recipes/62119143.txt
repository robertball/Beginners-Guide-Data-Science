Title:
Roasted Red Pepper Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Garbanzo Beans, drained & rinsed
1/3 cup Tahini
1 10 oz jar Roasted Red Peppers, in water. Reserve some liquid.
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
1/2 tsp cumin
Cayenne Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in food processor until almost smoothe.  Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
kuisinebykaren
Description: 
This hummus whips up quickly and is great on fresh pita bread.
