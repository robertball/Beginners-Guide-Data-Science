Title:
Apple Scotch Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortning
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp soda
1 cup Quick Oats
1 1/2 cup chopped apple
1 cup butterscotch chips
1/3 cup milk with 1 tsp vinager

Directions:
Combine
1/2 cup shortning
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp soda
And Mix well
Add the 1 cup Oats
Alternately add 1 1/2 cup flour and 1/3-2/3 cups milk mixed with vinager
Stir in 1 1/2 cup chopped apple and 1 cup butterscotch chips
Bake 400 10-15 mins
Either remove from greased muffin pan right away or use muffin cups
