Title:
Instant Russian Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/159112.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup instant tea powder
2 cups orange-flavored drink mix (e.g. Tang)
3 ounces lemonade-flavor drink powder
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together powdered drink mixes, sugar, and spices.  Place in jars.
Instructions to include with gift:<BR>  1. Place 2 to 3 rounded teaspoonfuls in a cup.<BR>  2. Add boiling water and serve.

Submitter: 
Laura
Description: 
This is an instant tea mix that can be given as a gift in a large jar. It tastes like mulled cider.
