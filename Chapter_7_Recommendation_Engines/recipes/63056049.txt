Title:
Ground Meat in Cashew Nut Sauce with Chickpeas (Keema Matar) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/779830.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbsp light vegetable oil
2 cups finely chopped onions
2 tsps finely chopped garlic
1 tsp finely chopped fresh ginger root
1 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp turmeric
½ tsp red pepper
2 bay leaves
2 pounds lean ground beef, or ground lamb
1 ½ tsp kosher salt
2 ½ cups fresh chopped tomatoes
3 tbsp cashew nut butter
1 20 oz. can chickpeas with liquid
2 tsp garam masala or ground roasted cumin seeds

Directions:
1.    Heat the oil in a large pan and add onions. Over medium heat, cook onions until they turn a caramel brown (about 25 miunutes), stirring constantly.
2.    Add garlic and ginger, and cook for 2 minutes.  Add cumin, coriander, turmeric, red pepper, and bay leaves. Stir for a moment or two.  Add meat.  Cook it, breaking it up with a fork or wooden spoon, until it loses its pink color.  Add salt, chopped tomatoes,  nut butter, and chick peas.  Add ½ cup hot water, and bring contents to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 45 minutes.  Turn off heat, and stir in garam masala.
