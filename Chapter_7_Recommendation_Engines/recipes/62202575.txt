Title:
English Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 TBS light corn syrup
3 TBS water
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup chopped almonds
6 small chocolate candy bars, approximately

Directions:
Melt butter in a lsrge sauce pan. Then add sugr, corn syrup and water. Cook to hard crack stage (300*),
stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and quickly stir in vanilla extract and 3/4 cup chopped almondds
Pour mixture onto a flat piece of buttered heavy duty foil placed on a cookie sheet. Tip cookie sheet to evenly distribute toffee
Let cool 5 minutes then place the chocolate candy bars on top to melt. Spread evenly and sprinkle with chopped almonds. You can slso use chocolate bars with almonds.
When toffee is completely cooled, break into pieces and store in a tightly covered container.
