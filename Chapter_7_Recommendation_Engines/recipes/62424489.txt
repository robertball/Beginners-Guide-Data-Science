Title:
Mandarin Orange Jello Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3oz. pkg. of sugar-free orange jello
8oz. container of low fat whipped topping
2 can of mandarin oranges, well drained
1 large or 2 sm. pkgs. of sugar-free instant vanilla pudding

Directions:
Make jello according to package directions
Mix in vanilla pudding and topping with whisk or electric mixer
Pour into 9x13 pan and sprinkle oranges evenly over mixture
Refrigerate until firm

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Taste like a dreamcicyle. Got this from Donna. This has only 13 carbs per serving and is a good diabetic recipe.
