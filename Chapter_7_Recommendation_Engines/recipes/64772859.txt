Title:
New Years Mussels Salad 2020  / Mies Muschel Salat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 med. potatoes
7 oz. Cooked Mussels
7 oz Chopped "Sauer-kraut"
4 oz Mayonnaise
2 large Pickles
white pepper.
fresh Dill for garnish

Directions:
Boil, peel and cut potatoes into 1/2 inch slices. cut each mussel into halves. chop the Kraut into bite manageable lengths.  add a pinch of pepper. mix in mayonnaise before serving, Garnish with Pickle slices and top with chopped Dill.
