Title:
Baba's Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2883498.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. mashed sweet potatoes
1 c. sugar
1/4 c. milk
1/4 c. oleo
2 eggs
1 c. nuts
1/2 c. flour
1 c. brown sugar
Marshmallows

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except marshmallows.
Place in casserole dish and top with marshmallows.
Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
US92
Description: 
Austen's grandmother (Baba) always made these sweet potatoes, leaving a section free of marshmallows just for Austen.
