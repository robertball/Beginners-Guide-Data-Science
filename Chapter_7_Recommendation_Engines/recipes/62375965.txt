Title:
Whats His Faces Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. LEAN Ground Beef
One small Onion
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 Cream of Mushroom can skim milk OR 2 % (1 extra optional RESERVED)
1 Cream of Mushroom can fat free 1/2 and 1/2
1 Cream of Mushroom can plain Yogurt

Directions:
Finely mince a small onion, or use food processor and then add it to and mix it into ground beef.
Roll out into 1 inch meatballs.
In a non-stick skillet brown all sides.
in a bowl place all the rest of the ingredients and mix well.
If cooking in the skillet add to skillet, cover and simmer one hour.
If cooking in oven add everything into a oven proof 13 x 9 dish, cover with foil and cook at 350 Deg. for 45 Min. remove foil and cook 15 more min.
If in a crockpot add all and cook on high for 5 hours of low for 9 hours.
If using a skillet or oven you may need to add the extra can of milk for more gravy.

Description: 
A delecious blend of flavors so good no one will even suspect it is low fat, low carbs and low sodium.
Three ways to cook it too, Crockpot, Oven or Skillet.
