Title:
Mainland Poke Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound raw fish (ahi or salmon) cubed
1 cup shoyu
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1/4 cup finely chopped sweet onion (Vadalia is a good substitute for Maui)
2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 tablespoon sesame seeds
2 tablespoons finely crushed macadamia nuts
1/2 tablespoon grated ginger
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon crushed red pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mixing thoroughly.  Refrigerate  and marinate for one hour.

Submitter: 
Halley Jarrett
Description: 
It's called mainland poke because it's made with thing that are easy to find on the mainland. Traditional poke sometimes has ingredients that are difficult to find in some places. I grew up in Hawai'i and I miss the food, so I made this!
