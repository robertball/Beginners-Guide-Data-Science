Title:
Shrimp and Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. cooked; Crab Meat (canned or Frozen)
6 oz. of 200ct. cooked; Shrimp;    "    "
4 oz of Corn.  (canned or frozen)
5  boiled Eggs.
12oz. fresh green Cabbage
8oz. Mayonnaise
Salt to taste
Mint leaves for garnishing

Directions:
Mince the Crab and Shrimp meat;  then Dice the Boiled Eggs.
Macerate the Shredded Cabbage with salt until it Tears;  then Drain.
Drain the Corn and combine with all the other Ingredients.
shortly before serving Combine with the Mayonnaise and Garnish with fresh sprigs of mint.
The Crab may be substituted with fresh or frozen "Surimi" ( Imitation Crab product ).
