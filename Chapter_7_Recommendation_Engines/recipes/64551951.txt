Title:
Big Ray's Easy Chicken Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3314458.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups of shredded chicken  (precook this in a slow cooker so you just have to shred it or can used store roasted chicken).
16 oz bag of frozen broccoli (cooked)
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
Garlic powder and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
Pour into a greased 11' x 7" baking dish
Bake for 25 minutes.
