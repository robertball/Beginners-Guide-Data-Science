Title:
Broccoli Bacon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads raw broccoli
1 medium red onion
1 lb. cooked bacon
2 c. miracle whip
2/3 c. sugar
1 T. vinegar

Directions:
Rinse broccoli. Cut into pieces.
Dice/mince onion.
Crumble bacon.
Mix all ingredients together.
Chill before serving.
