Title:
Extremely Simple Strawberry Coated Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 [ 14 ounces ] can sweetened condensed milk
1 [ 11 ounces ] package chocolate chips
3 teaspoons strawberry extract

Directions:
1. Line a 9x9-inch pan with aluminum foil.
2. Combine sweetened condensed milk and strawberry extract and chocolate chips together in a microwave-safe bowl; melt in microwave until smooth, 1 to 3 minutes. Stir until smooth. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
3. Refrigerate until set, at least 1 hour. Lift Strawberry coated fudge onto a work surface and cut into 12 to 16 pieces.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe is an extremely simple recipe that has many different variations!
