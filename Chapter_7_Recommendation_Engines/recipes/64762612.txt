Title:
Keto Maple Sour Cream Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 T melted butter
1/2 tsp maple flavoring
1/4 c sour cream (or sub pumpkin puree for half)
1/2 c almond meal
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/8 tsp salt

Directions:
combine wet ingredients, add dry ingredients
blend with hand mixer
cook in pancake skillet coated in butter

Description: 
easy sour cream/buttermilk pancakes; add flavorings of choice
