Title:
Apple Pie Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 thick chops, bone-in
1 cup white wine or apple juice
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp grated ginger or powdered ginger
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp powdered cloves
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl or freezer bag with zipper, and add the chops.  Marinate 4 hours in the fridge, then grill or bake to your preference.  Serve with applesauce and a slice of cheddar!

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
Nothing goes better with pork than apples...this takes it up a notch!
