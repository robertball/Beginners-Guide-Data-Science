Title:
Best Bar Cookies from Cake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup + 1 TBLSP water
1 egg or 2 egg yolks if allergic
1 (18.25 ounce) package cake mix
1 cup chopped pecans
1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
Following can be added in place of any of above extras:
1 cup coconut
1 1/2 cup peanut butter chips
1/ 1/2 cups white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Mix in water and egg, then stir in cake mix. Finally, mix in nuts, coconut and chocolate chips. Spread mixture into pan.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven. Cool completely before cutting into bars.
