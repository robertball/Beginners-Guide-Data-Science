Title:
Instant Pot Barbacoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4lbs Chuck Roast
Salt and Pepper to taste
SoakedGuajillo Pepper, 3
Passilla Pepper, 3
Onion quartered
Garlic, 5 cloves
Cumin, 1 Tbsp
Oregano, 1 Tbsp
Apple Cider Vinegar, 1/4 cup
Lime Juice, 2 Tbsp
Agave Syrup, 1 Tbsp
Cinnamon, 1 Tsp

Directions:
Soak the guajillo and passilla peppers in hot water for roughly 20 minutes until soft.
Chop chuck roast into roughly 2"x2" pieces, and salt and pepper to taste.  Broil the meet until all sides are dark brown and crusty.
While meat is browning, add the softened guajillo and passilla peppers, onion, garlic, cumin, oregano, apple cider vinegar, lime juice, agave syrup, cinnamon into a blender and blend till smooth.
Transfer the browned meat and juices to the instant pot, and add the sauce from the blender.  Mix till sauce is fully incorporated.  Pressure cook for 60 minutes.  Manually release pressure and shred meat.
Serve meat on freshly made corn tortillas with pickled onions and cilantro.
