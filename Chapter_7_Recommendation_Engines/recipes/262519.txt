Title:
Garlic Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F5071738.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
½ cup Frank's <sup>&reg;</sup>edHot(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Original Cayenne Pepper Sauce
½ cup sour cream
2 teaspoons McCormick(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Garlic Powder
2 cups shredded cooked chicken
1 ½ cups shredded Colby Jack cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix cream cheese, hot sauce, sour cream, and garlic in medium bowl until well blended. Stir in chicken and 1/2 cup cheese.
Spoon into 2-quart baking dish. Top with remaining cheese. Cover with foil.
Bake 30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
McCormick Spice
Description: 
The game-winning combo of McCormick(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Garlic Powder and Frank's <sup>&reg;</sup>edHot(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Sauce comes together in this zesty dip loaded with chicken and cheese. Top with additional <sup>&reg;</sup>edHot Sauce and ranch or blue cheese dressing, if desired. Serve with dippers like carrots and celery or pita chips and watch it disappear before halftime.
