Title:
Lemon and Thyme Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tilapia fillets
4 sprigs of thyme
1 large lemon sliced thin
2 tbsp olive oil
salt and papper to taste

Directions:
Preheat the over at 400.
Place the fillets on a foil lined cooking sheet (See note).
Season with salt and pepper to taste.  You will not need much salt.
Sprinkle the thyme over the fish.  Make sure each fish gets some thyme!
Place 2-3 limon slices on each fillet.Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
Place the baking sheet in the over and cook until the fish feels firm.  This will take 10-15 minutes depending on your oven.
Enjoy

Submitter:

Description: 
A light main dish that will leave you looking for more.  The lemony component of the thyme works well with the lemon slices and flavors this fish beautifully.
