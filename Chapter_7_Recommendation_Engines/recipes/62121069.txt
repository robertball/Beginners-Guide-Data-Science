Title:
Baked Stuffed Shrimp Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of uncooked shrimp peeled and deveined
2 sticks of salted butter
1 large onion finely chopped
1/4 cup white wine
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 lb box of buttery round crackers finely crushed and divided
1/4 mayonaise
1 tbs of dried parsley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Arrange shrimp in a 9x13 dish.
In a large saute pan, melt butter.
Add white wine, onions and garlic and cook until soft.
Add the mayonaise and crushed crackers to the saute pan, reserving 1 cup of crackers, and stir until everything is incorporated.
Top shrimp with the cracker/butter mixture, making sure to cover all of the shrimp.
Sprinkle remaining crushed crackers and parsley over the casserole.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes or until top is golden brown.
