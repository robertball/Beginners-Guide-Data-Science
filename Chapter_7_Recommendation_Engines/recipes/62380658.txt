Title:
Italian Cream of Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. butter
1 lg. Onion, chopped
2 1/2 C. veg. Stock
2 lb. Italian plum tomatoes, skinned anf roughly chopped
pinch of baking soda
2 C. dried fusilli
1 T. superfine sugar
5/8 C. heavy cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in lg. SP, add onion and sauté for 3 min.
Add 1 3/4 C. of veg. Stock to SP, with tomatoes and baking soda.
Bring to boil and simmer for 20 min.
Remove SP from heat and set aside to cool slightly.
Purée the soup in a blender or food processor and pour through
fine strainer back into SP.
Add remaining veg. Stock and the fusilli to SP. Season to taste.
Add sugar to SP and bring to boil. Then lower heat and simmer 15 min.
Pour soup into bowls, swirl the heavy cream around surface of soup.
Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve with croutons.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A creamy tomato soup. Way better than Campbell's.
