Title:
Roasted Brussels sprouts with balsamic reduction and cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Brussels sprouts
1/2 c olive oil
1 c balsamic vinegar
3/4 c sugar
1 c dried cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Trim/clean sprouts and cut in half.
Arrange on 2 cookie sheets and coat with oil.
Roast until brown, 25-30 minutes.
Combine balsamic vinegar and sugar in saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cook until very thick.
Drizzle the balsamic reduction over the roasted sprouts and add cranberries.
