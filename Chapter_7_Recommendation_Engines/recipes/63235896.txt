Title:
Buttermilk Biscuits a la KFC Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/842911.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp baking powder
1/3 cup vegetable shortening
2/3 cup buttermilk

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 425
2. Sift together the dry ingredients into a large bowl
3. Cut in the shortening
4. Make a nest in the flour mixture and add the buttermilk. Gently knead the mixture with your hands until thoroughly combined and a soft dough forms
5. Ona floured cutting board, pat the dough to a 1/2 inch thickness and cut with a biscuit cutter or the rim of a glass.
6. Please the biscuits on a cookie sheet and bake for 12 minutes, or until golden brown
