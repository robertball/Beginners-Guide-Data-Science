Title:
Cinnamon Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookies:
1/3 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup slivered almonds, toasted
1 1/2 cups sifted caked flour
2/3 cup powdered sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 cup ice water
Cooking spray
Topping:
2 tsp. powdered sugar
1/8 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Place butter and sugar in a bowl and mix until well blended.
Grind almonds in blender or processor until finely ground.
Combine flour, ground almonds, powdered sugar, salt, cinnamon and cloves.
Add flour mixture to butter mixture and beat until just combined.
Add ice water and beat in low until moist.
Gently form dough into a ball, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate 1 hour or until thoroughly chilled.
Preheat oven to 300.
Shape dough into 30 1" balls and place 2" apart on cookie sheet prepared with cooking spray.
Bake at for 25 minutes.
Allow to cool completely.
For topping, combine powdered sugar and cinnamon.
Sprinkle mixture evenly over cooled cookies.
