Title:
Emma Smith's Oatmeal Raisin Cookie Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1787500.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
3/4 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
1/2 cup milk
4 cups rolled oats
2 cups raisins
2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon cloves
2 cups nuts

Directions:
Mix shortening and sugar together; add eggs one at a time, beat well.
Add milk and mix well.
Mix flour, soda, salt, and spices together in a separate bowl, then add them all at once.
Mix well.
Add raisins and mix.
Add oatmeal and mix.
Add nuts and mix.
Spoon onto baking sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees until golden brown.
About 7-8 minutes.
