Title:
Rich Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985308.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package yellow cake mix
3 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
1 egg
TOPPING:
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 egg

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the dry cake mix, butter and egg; mix well. Spread into a greased 9-in. square baking pan.
In a small mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, confectioners' sugar and egg; spread evenly over batter. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. Store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Tammy  Helle
Description: 
I take turns with some of the ladies at church to provide coffee time snacks for adult Bible class and Sunday school. These gooey bars, a traditional St. Louis dessert, are a favorite. -Tammy Helle, St. Louis, Missouri
