Title:
Luscious Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023832.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 (3 ounce) package instant lemon pudding mix
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix
3/4 cup water
1/2 cup lemon juice
3 cups confectioners' sugar, sifted
lemon zest

Directions:
Grease and flour a bundt or tube pan.
Beat eggs until thick.
Add pudding mix, cake mix, water, and oil; beat with an electric mixer on medium speed for 5 minutes.
Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake at 350Â° for 50 minutes.
Cool for 10 minutes and invert on a wire rack.
Cool 10 minutes longer.  Place rack on waxed paper.
Mix lemon juice and 3 cups confectioner's sugar in a saucepan.  Heat to boiling.  Pour hot glaze over cake, sprinkle lemon zest over glaze.
Let cool for approximately 1 hour.
