Title:
Simple Chocolate Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 oz. swiss chocolate, broken into small pieces
1/2 Cup whipping cream

Directions:
Combine ingredients in fondue pot or chafing dish. Stir until melted. Serve with fruit, pound cake, marshmallows, etc., to dip into the chocolate.

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
This dessert was a favorite in our house when I was growing up. I was surprised when mom shared the recipe with me and I learned how simple it is!
