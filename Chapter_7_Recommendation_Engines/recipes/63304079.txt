Title:
Picnic Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups potatoes, diced and cooked
4 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup minced onion
3/4 cup chopped sweet pickles
1 TBSP salt
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
1 1/2 cup mayonnaise (NOT Miracle Whip)

Directions:
Mix potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, pickle, salt, sugar, vinegar, and mayonnaise well.  Put in a large bowl and store in refrigerator for several hours before serving.
