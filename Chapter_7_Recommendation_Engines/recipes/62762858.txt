Title:
Jim's Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639337.jpg
Ingredients:
4 whole skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 tsp salt and pepper ( or to taste)
1 cup Miracle Whip lite
1 Tbs Achiote ground pepper
1 cup chopped fresh basil
2 cloves crushed garlic
3 stalks celery, chopped
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper.  Roast at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 35  minutes, or until juices run clear. Let cool, and chop into chunks.
Combine the chunked chicken,and all the ingedients together and refrigerate. It tastes better the next day too!

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
The heady aroma and taste of sweet basil defines this cold chicken salad tossed with Parmesan cheese and dressed with a puree of miracle whip lite, basil, garlic and celery.Modified from: Parmesan and  Basil Chicken Salad
