Title:
Michelle's Tamale Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 frozen beef tamales, microwaved, husked and mashed
1 (15 ounce) can chili without beans
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes and green chiles
1 (2-pound) loaf Velveeta
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low until melted.  Stir occasionally until the cheese is melted.  Keep on low or warm setting to keep the dip warm.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
michelle o'c
Description: 
Stick everything in a slow cooker for a few hours and you'll have a warm, melty yummy dip for chips.
