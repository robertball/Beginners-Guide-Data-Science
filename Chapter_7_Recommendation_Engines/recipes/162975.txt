Title:
Leftover Dog Pile Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1059773.jpg
Ingredients:
2 hot dogs
3/4 cup chili with beans
1 slice bread
1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 tablespoon chopped onion
2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard

Directions:
Place the hot dogs onto a microwave safe plate and cook in the microwave 1 to 2 minutes until hot. Place the chili into a microwave safe bowl and microwave 1 to 2 minutes until hot. Slice the hot dogs in half lengthwise, and place onto the bread slice cut-side down; sprinkle with half of the cheese, the chili, the remaining cheese, onion, and mustard.

Description: 
This is an easy way to make a meal in with leftover chili.
