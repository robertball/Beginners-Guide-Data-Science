Title:
Captain Crunch Nobake  Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/309832.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups Captain Crunch w/ berries
4 cups salted peanuts
2 packs almond bark, white

Directions:
Melt almond bark on low heat or microwave.
Stir in the rest of ingredients.
Mix well.
Drop spoonful on wax paper.
Let cool.
If candy cools to fast,put in microwave and melt again.
