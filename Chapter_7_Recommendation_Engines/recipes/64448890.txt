Title:
Tomato Watermelon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large seedless watermelon, cut into 1-inch cubes
8 tomatoes, cut into 1-inch dice
24 mint leaves, sliced thinly
2 large sweet onion, thinly sliced
2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup olive oil
4 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
2 pinch coarse salt, or to taste
2 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste

Directions:
Gently mix watermelon, tomatoes, mint, onion, and feta cheese in a large bowl.
Whisk olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper together in small bowl; drizzle over salad and gently toss to coat.
