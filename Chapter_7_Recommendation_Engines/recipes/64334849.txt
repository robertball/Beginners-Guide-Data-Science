Title:
Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken
1/2 cup butter
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup celery
2 cups dry bead crumbs
1-1 1/2 tsp thyme leaves
1/2 tsp sage
sal and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
add butter to top of bird
Stuff bird with stuffing
cook for 1.5 to 2 hours
Stuffing
Fry onions and celery
add sage and thyme leaves
Mix with bread crumbs and stuff the bird before cooking
