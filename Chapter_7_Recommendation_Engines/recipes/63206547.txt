Title:
Honey Mustard Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/830087.jpg
Ingredients:
1 -1 1/2 lb pkg of center cut bnls pork chops
Can use other cuts, Sliced at 1/2".
1 Tbsp of butter or olive oil
garlic powder
1/4 cup Honey
2 Tbsp of Golden Mustard

Directions:
Prepare the honey and mustard in a small bowl.
Heat a skillet on med-high and add the butter or olive oil. Place your pork in pan and sprinkle garlic powder on one side. Let cook for 3 minutes and turn over and repeat garlic powder and let cook for 3 minutes. Brush honey/mustard mix onto one side of pork with a basting brush. Turn and let cook for 5 minutes. Meanwhile brush rest of honey/mustard mix on other side of pork. After one side has cooked turn over and cook other side for 5 more minutes.
Always test for doneness at 165* on meat thermometer.

Submitter: 
AuntE
Description: 
Quick, easy and tasty way to prepare pork chops inside or outside. Just skip skillet if using grill.
