Title:
Cream of Any Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chopped frozen or fresh vegetables
1 1/4 cups chicken broth
3 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup flour
2 cups milk
4 ounces cream cheese
8 ounces sour cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put vegetables into a 3 quart pan with chicken broth. Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 10 to 15 minutes or until tender.
Reserve 1/2 cup of vegetables and puree remainder of vegetables and liquid in blender.
Melt butter in same pan. Add flour and stir until smooth. Gradually whisk in milk. Add pureed and reserved vegetables.
Add cream cheese and simmer over low heat until cream cheese is melted. Do Not Boil.
Mix in sour cream and season with salt and pepper just before serving.

Description: 
Quick, easy, and delicious. Use your favorite vegetable fresh from the garden or simply use a frozen bag of mixed vegetables.
