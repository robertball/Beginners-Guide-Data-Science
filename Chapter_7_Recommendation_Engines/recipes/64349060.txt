Title:
Asian Tofu with Snow Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons rice wine
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 pkg tofu or tempeh, cut into thin strips
8 ounces snow peas

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, rice wine, brown sugar and cornstarch. Set aside.
In skillet over medium high heat, stir-fry ginger and garlic for 30 seconds. Add the tofu and stir-fry for 2 minutes or until evenly browned. Add the snow peas and stir-fry for an additional 3 minutes. Add the soy sauce mixture, bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Lower heat and simmer until the sauce is thick and smooth. Serve immediately.
