Title:
Simple Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb bag red potatoes
4 Tbs white vinegar
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Scrub the potatoes and cut into eighths (more for large potatoes).
Boil the potatoes until tender.
Spread potatoes on large tray; shake 2 capfuls vinegar over the potatoes.
After 30 minutes, toss potatoes and shake another 2 capfuls vinegar over them.
When potatoes are completely cool, toss in a large bowl with mayonnaise, celery seed, salt, and pepper.

Submitter: 
Lauren Fitch
Description: 
This is a simple potato salad, good for picky eaters!
