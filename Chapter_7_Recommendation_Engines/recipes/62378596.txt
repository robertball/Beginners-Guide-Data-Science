Title:
Jim's Porcini Filet Slather Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 ounce dried porcini mushrooms,Ground fine in a blender
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1/2 tablespoon red pepper flakes
1/2 tablespoon coarse cracked black pepper
6 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a bowl after you grind the porcini to a powder and top youir steaks with the mixture
