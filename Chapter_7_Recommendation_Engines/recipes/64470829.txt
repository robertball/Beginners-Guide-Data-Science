Title:
Amazing Crock Pot Tri-Tip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs beef tri-tip
1 cup beef broth  ( from beef bullion cube)
1 packet of dry Italian Dressing mix
2-4 dashes of hot sauce
2 Tbls Worcestershire Sauce
Garlic salt
Ground mustard

Directions:
In a small bowl mix together beef broth,  dry Italian dressing, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce
Rub the tri-tip with the garlic salt and ground mustard, to taste.  Place meat in crock pot.
Poor liquid on top of meat in crock pot
Cook on low for 8 hours.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
cpmustang
Description: 
This is an amazingly easy for a busy mom to make during the week!  I got this recipe from a friend and I made it yesterday.  Dinner was fabulous and the leftovers for lunch today were even better.

I prepared the ingredients the night before so the meat could marinate even longer.
