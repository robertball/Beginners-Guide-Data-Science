Title:
Pizza Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf Italian bread
Grape or cherry tomatoes
Olive oil
Fresh mozzarella

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400ยบ F.
Slice bread 1 inch thick.  Spread the bread slices out on a cookie tray.
Paint on olive oil.
Slice mozzarella so that there is one slice of mozzarella for every slice of bread.  Place each slice on a slice of bread.
Halve the tomatoes, and place two halves on each slice of bread.
Bake for about 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the tomatoes are cooked.
Let sit until the cheese is not runny.
