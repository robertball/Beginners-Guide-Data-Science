Title:
Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pie crust
3 eggs
1 cup milk
12 oz shredded swiss cheese
1 small onion
salt and pepper
optional ingredients: 1 cup each small broccoli florets, diced ham, crumbled bacon, browned hamburger, etc.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Whisk together eggs, milk and some salt and pepper.
Mince onion.
Place pie crust/shell in pie dish. Sprinkle layers of onion, cheese, and optional ingredients. Pour in half of egg mixture.
Finish adding all cheese, onions, and optional ingredients.
Pour remaining egg mixture on top.
Bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Emma J
Description: 
Delicious egg quiche with ham, broccoli, and really anything you want to put in it. Great for small company.
