Title:
Chicken BLT Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Breaded chicken breast fillets (I recommend the Bagged Tyson Chicken breast fillets)
15 Sliced of cooked bacon
3 Roma tomatoes, sliced
5 Lettuce Leaves
5 Kaiser rolls
5 Slices of American Cheese
Mayonnaise

Directions:
Cook chicken fillets according to package directions. Lighty toast kaiser rolls in oven or toaster oven. To assemble sandwich spread a layer of mayonnaise, add chicken and top with cheese, bacon, lettuce and tomato.

Submitter: 
chicklet
Description: 
Simple ingredients I had around the house made this tasty sandwich that my family and I loved. Easy weeknight dinner I like to serve with french fries.
