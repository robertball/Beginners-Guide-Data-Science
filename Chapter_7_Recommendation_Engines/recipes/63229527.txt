Title:
egg cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840034.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large eggs
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp parsely
2 tbsp milk
1tsp melted butter
1 package bacon
1/2 cup diced ham
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella
6 thick slices gouda cheese

Directions:
cook bacon to about half way done (not crispy)
wrap a slice of bacon around the inside of non stick muffin tin. chop up leftover bacon and put on the bottom of each tin (if any additional bacon simply add to egg mixture)
wisk eggs, butter, milk, salt, pepper, parsley, then add ham, mozzarella cheese and any additional bacon.
pour mixture into bacon lined cups. top with gouda slices and bake at 350 for about 15 minutes
