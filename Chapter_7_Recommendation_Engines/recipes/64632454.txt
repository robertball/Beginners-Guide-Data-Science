Title:
HAWAIIAN ICE BOX CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 pkg yellow cake mix
3 egg whites
1 1/3 C water
Pudding Pie Mix:
2 C milk
1 pkg (3.4 oz) instant banana or vanilla pudding/pie mix.
1 can (15 oz) unsweetened crushed pineapple, drained.
2 C Cool whip, thawed
1/4 C shredded coconut

Directions:
Cake:
Combine cake mix, 3 eggs and water in large bowl, beat at low speed 30 seconds.
Increase speed to medium & beat 2 minutes.
Spray a 13 x 9 x 2 bake dish with Pam.
Pour batter into it and bake for 35 minutes (or til cake springs back when lightly touched in the center) at 350.
Let cake cool in pan on wire rack.
Pudding Pie Mix.
Combine Mild and pkg of mix in a medium bowl; beat at low speed 2 minutes or til thickened. Cover & chill for 5 minutes.
Poke 48 holes in the top of the cake using the handle of a wooden spoon.
Assemble:
Spread pudding mixture over cake.
Drain the Pineapple and spread over cake.
Sprinkle Shredded coconut over cake
Store in refrigerator.
