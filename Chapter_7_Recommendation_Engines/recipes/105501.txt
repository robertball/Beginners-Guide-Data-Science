Title:
Soft and Sweet Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992899.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
2 1/4 cups 1% low-fat milk
1 1/2 cups SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
5 cups 1/2 to 1-inch cubes day old French bread
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees  F.
Whisk together eggs, milk, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, cinnamon and vanilla in a large bowl. Gently stir in bread cubes and raisins. Pour mixture into a 1 1/2-quart ungreased baking dish.
Bake 40 minutes or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
A sweet use for leftover French bread.
