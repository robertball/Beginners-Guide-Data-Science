Title:
Pan con Tomate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 roma tomatos
1 french baguette
olive oil
salt

Directions:
Grate tomato into a bowl.
Slice and toast baguette.
Drizzle oil on bread.
Lightly salt bread.
Spoon tomato sauce onto bread.
Eat.

Submitter: 
Justin Will
Description: 
A Spanish favorite, usually consumed for breakfast, but is really applicable any time of day. Think of it as a simplified bruschetta-based tapa.
