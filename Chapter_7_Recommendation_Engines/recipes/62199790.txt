Title:
Texas Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg (24 oz) hash browns
2 cans cream of potato soup
2 pkg Hidden Valley Ranch dressing
2 8-oz pkg cream cheese

Directions:
Partially thaw hash browns and put in slow cooker. Mix rest of ingredients and add to potatoes (stir in). Slow cook 7-8 hours. Stir before serving.
You will want to add milk as needed.

Submitter: 
Buck Sommerkamp

Excellent with Thanksgiving dinner.
