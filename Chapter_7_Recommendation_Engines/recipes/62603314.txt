Title:
White Chocolatey Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 squares melted cooled unsweetened chocolate
2 3/4 c sugar (next time reduce to 2 1/2)
2 eggs
1 1/2 t vanilla
1/2 tsp soda
1/4 c cocoa
2 1/2 c flour
1/2 t salt
2 T milk or cream if batter is too thick
1/2 pkg white chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt chocolate, cream with butter and sugar until fluffy. Add vanilla and eggs and mix well. Sprinkle soda over top. Stir in cocoa and flour 1/2 cup at a time. Mix well and stir in chips.  Bake in 1 inch balls at 350 for about 8 min.
