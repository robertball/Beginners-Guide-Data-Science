Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 large onion, minced
1 large bell pepper, minced
2 ribs of celery, diced
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 cup long grain white rice
1/28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
1 cup chicken broth
1-1/2 tsp seasoning salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp cayenne
1/2 tsp cumin (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375* F.
Heat butter in a large iron casserole or dutch oven over medium heat.
Add rice.  Heat stirring until rice turns opaque.
Add onion, garlic, bell pepper, and celery.  Saute until soft about 3 minutes.
Add the tomatoes, chicken broth and seasonings.
Bring to a boil, cover and place casserole in oven.
Bake for 40 - 45 minutes or until moisture is absorbed.
