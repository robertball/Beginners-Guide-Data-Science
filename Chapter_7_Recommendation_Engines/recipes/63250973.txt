Title:
Nancy's Outstanding Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs russet potatos whole(about 8)
1 tsp salt
3 tbs cider vinegar
3 eggs
1 c mayo
1 tsp dry mustard
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tbs (plus a little more if needed) hot water
2 stalks celery cut in 1/4" dice
1/2 red bell pepper stemmed, seeded, cut in 1/4 in dice
1/2 oinion diced
10 cornichons diced (optional)
3 scallions thin sliced

Directions:
Place whole potatos in a large pot with enough water to cover. Add 1 tsp salt. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and cook until tender, about 25-30 minutes. Peel, cut in 1" dice, drizzle with vinegar and set aside
Put eggs in a pan, cover with water, bring to a boil, cut off heat and cover. Let sit for 13 minutes. Drain, cover with cold water. When cool, peel and dice 2 eggs, slice the third and set aside.
in a bowl, whisk together mayo, dry mustard, 2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper. Thin with hot water. Stir in diced egg.
Put all chopped vegetables and potatos in a large bowl. Pour egg mayo mix on and combine. Garnish with sliced egg.
