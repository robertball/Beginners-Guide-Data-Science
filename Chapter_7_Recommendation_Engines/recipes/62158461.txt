Title:
Simple Summer Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle Merlot or Merlot Blend
1/2 carton Pineapple Orange Juice (they sell this pre-blended)
1/2 liter Ginger Ale or Club Soda

Directions:
Mix ingredients, and allow to sit overnight in fridge.  Before serving, slice orange, lemon and lime, and serve.
You might want to double this recipe because it is sure to be a hit!

Submitter: 
Brooke129
Description: 
Red Wine, Pineapple/Orange Juice, and Ginger Ale make this Sangria the easiest Summer drink out there!
