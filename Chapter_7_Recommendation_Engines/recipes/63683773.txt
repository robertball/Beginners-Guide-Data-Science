Title:
Spicy Balsamic Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2lb slicing tomatoes, cut into thin wedges
balsamic vinegar
rice vinegar
basil, dry
ginger (powdered)
1.25lb chayote, thinly sliced (may substitute cucumber)

Directions:
Place the tomatoes in a large bowl.  Add equal amounts of balsamic and rice vinegars until they are covered.
Add basil and ginger to taste.  Stir.
Let sit for an hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Then drain off and reserve the vinegar mixture.  Do not rinse the tomatoes.
Combine the tomatoes and chayote.  Serve with the vinegar mixture for dressing.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
This salad goes well with both Mexican/Latin American food and Italian/Mediterranean food.  It bursts with balsamic-ginger spiciness, tomato sweetness, and chayote cooling.
