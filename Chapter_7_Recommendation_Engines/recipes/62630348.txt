Title:
Hershey's Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup sugar
1 cup flour
7 T Cocoa
2 t baking powder
1/4 t salt
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup butter (or margarine), melted
1 1/2 t vanilla
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/4 cup hot water

Directions:
Oven to 350 - 8" or 9" square pan
Whisk together 3/4 cup sugar, flour, 3 T cocoa, baking powder, and salt
Stir in milk, utter and vanilla and beat until smooth
Spread batter into ungreased pan
Stir together remaining 1/2 cup sugar, brown sugar, and remaining 4 T cocoa
Sprinke evenly over batter
Pour hot water on top (do not stir)
Bake for 35-40"
Let sit 15" before serving
