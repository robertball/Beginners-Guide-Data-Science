Title:
Big Ray's Big Shouldered Chicago Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3538013.jpg
Ingredients:
1 jalapeno (remove the seeds and membranes if you don't want the heat)
Juice of 1 lemon (3 Tbsp)
1 Tbsp dried oregano
3 Tbsp olive oil
3 cloves of garlic, minced
8 boneless chicken thighs

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in the food processor.  Pulse until combined and jalapeno is finely diced.  Pour into a resealable bag, add chicken.  Toss to coat.  Let marinate in refrigerator overnight and then grill 5 to 10 minutes per side, until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A version of  Tavern Chicken -but this is better than the original.  I use boneless chicken thighs,because they are juicer than breasts but any piece of chicken can be used.
