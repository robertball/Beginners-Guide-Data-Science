Title:
Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (8 squares) Baker's Unsweetened Baking Chocolate
1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter or margarine
2 cups sugar
1 cup half and half
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Microwave chocolate and butter in large microwavable bowl on HIGH for 2 min. or until butter is melted.  Stir until chocolate is completely melted.
Add sugar and half and half.  Stir until well blended.  Microwave 5 min. or until mixture is thick and sugar is completely disolved, stirring after 3  minutes.
Stir in vanilla.
Fun idea:  Serve warm as a fondue with dippers, such as strawberries, apple or pear slices, banana chunks, angel food or pound cake cubes, or assorted cookies.
