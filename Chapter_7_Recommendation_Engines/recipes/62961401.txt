Title:
Chorizo Tapas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Shallot - sliced
1 clove Garlic - finely chopped
300g/10oz Chorizo - cut diagonally into 2.5cm/1in chunks
2 fresh Bay Leaves
200ml/7fl oz Dry Red Wine

Directions:
Add the oil to a frying pan on medium hot heat;
Add the shallot and garlic and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until softened;
Add the chorizo and fry for 1-2 minutes, or until crisping and the oil is released;
Add the bay leaves and wine, and cook until the wine has reduced to just coating the chorizo;
Remove the bay leaves and serve with bread and cheese.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A favourite Spanish tapas given a red wine twist. 
A fabulous lunch time snack served with a cold beer, crusty bread and cheese.
