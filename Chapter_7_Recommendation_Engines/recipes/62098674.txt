Title:
Stef's Classic Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2238052.jpg
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1/2 cup mayo
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
3 tablespoons dill relish
4 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
3/4 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Paprika, for garnish

Directions:
Place eggs into medium sauce pan and cover them with cold water.  Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.  Pour out hot water and run cold water over eggs until water stays cold.
Peel cooled eggs and cut them in half lengthwise.  Place all yolks in a medium bowl and mash.
Add dill relish, mayo, prepared yellow mustard, Parmesan cheese, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, garlic powder, and last salt and pepper to taste.  Mix well and spoon mixture into halved eggs.
Garnish with paprika and chill until ready to serve.

Classic deviled eggs from the south!  Hope you enjoy them as much as my family does!
