Title:
Rockin's Crunchy Cole Slaw Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package ramen noodles crushed(season packet discarded or use to flavor a future recipe)
1/4cup toasted slivered almonds(can be done in a hot dry skillet, stir frequently till turning brown and toasty)
1/2cup chopped green pepper
1/2cup chopped red pepper
3 green onions thinly sliced
1-lb bag of slaw mix
Dressing:
3Tbls. suger
2Tbls. vinegar(white or cider)
1/2cup oil
1tsp. each salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine all your vegetables broken soup noodles and almonds in a large bowl.
In an airtight container combine the dressing ingredients.
Place a lid on it and shake till well incorporated.
Pour over veggy/soup mixture and stir till salad is well covered with the dressing.
Serve immediately. (to make ahead of time you can simply layer the vegetables and noodles with the noodles on top. Place the almonds on last, cover and refrigerate. Add the dressing just before you are going to serve the salad.)

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
My Mother-in-law Ruth gave me this recipe over 30 years ago. You will see many variations of this salad and you can tinker with this recipe, with good results, but this is how my family enjoys it the most. It is also very colorful!
