Title:
Mixed Fruits Sandwich Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bread
20 g butter
1 banana
1/2 apple
1 small orange
1 kiwi fruit
4-6 pcs grapes
1 Tbsp honey
whipped cream

Directions:
Prepare and slice fruits.
Cut the bread crusts if you prefer then spread butter on one side of each bread.
On the nonbuttered side of bread, spread the honey then arrange the sliced fruits on top.
Top with another slice of bread with the buttered side facing up.
Preheat the sandwich maker.
Place the sandwich in the sandwich maker then cook for around 5 minutes or until the sandwich is crispy.
Remove the sandwich from the sandwich maker the place on a serving plate. Top with whipped cream before serving.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
Here's a recipe for mixed fruits sandwich. Cooking it in a sandwich maker made the fruits sweeter. For this sandwich, I've used banana, apple, grapes, kiwi fruit and tangkan (Okinawa orange). If you want to make this hot sandwich at home, feel free to tweak using the fruits that's near you.
