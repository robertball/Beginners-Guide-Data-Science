Title:
One Bowl Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. semi-sweet chocolate morsels
2/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/8 tsp salt
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Microwave chocolate and milk in 1 1/2 qt. microwavable bowl for 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute.  Stir until chocolate is completely melted and smooth.  Stir in vanilla, salt, and nuts.  Spread in greased or buttered 8x4 or 9x5 inch loaf pan.  Refrigerate until firm, about 2 hours.
