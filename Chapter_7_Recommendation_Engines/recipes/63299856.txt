Title:
Pace Picante Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 ounces petite cut tomatoes
14 ounces tomato sauce
14 ounces water
3 fresh jalapeno peppers
1 large yellow onion
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon chili powder
salt, if needed

Directions:
Bring all ingredients to a boil in a pot.
Lower heat and simmer for about 30 minutes or until it is the desired thickness (Salsa thickens when it cools.).
Remove from heat and cool in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
This easy sauce tastes exactly like Pace Picante, but with much less salt, and nothing you can't pronounce. Since salt content varies so much in canned tomatoes and sauce, add salt after it's cooked and cooled, if needed.
