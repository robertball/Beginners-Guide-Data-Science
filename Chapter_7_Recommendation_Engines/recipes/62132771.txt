Title:
My Favorite Margarita Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
2 egg yolks, slightly beatened
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 oz. tequila
1 oz. triple sec
2 egg whites
1 9 inch graham cracker crust
1/2 cup whipping cream, whipped
1/4 cup sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine the condensed milk, egg yolks, 1/2 cup sugar, lime juice, tequila and triple sec in a bowl.
Mix well till all combined smoothly.
In another bowl, beat egg whites with electric mixer till slightly stiff.
Fold the egg whites gently into the milk and egg mixture.
Pour slowly into the graham cracker crust.
Bake for 20 - 25 minutes.
Remove from oven and let cool completely on a wire rack.
Fold the 1/4 cup sugar into whipped cream and mix gently.
Spread over the cooled pie.
Chill pie overnight.
Garnish with thin lime slices if desired.

Submitter: 
IrvineHousewife
Description: 
My friend Judy gave me this recipe many years ago. The hint of tequila and triple sec combined with the tartness of the lime juice in a sweet filling leaves a lingering sensation in your mouth!
