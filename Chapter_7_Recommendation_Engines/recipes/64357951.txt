Title:
Couscous with Sage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup couscous
3/4 tsp sage
1-1/2 cups chicken broth
2 Tbsp butter

Directions:
In bowl, mix couscous and sage.
Boil chicken broth.  Add chicken broth and butter to couscous.
Cover and let set for 5 minutes.  Fluff with a fork and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Nice side dish for fish or chicken.
