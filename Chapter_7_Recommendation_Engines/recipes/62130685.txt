Title:
Amazing Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Diced Chicken (I used left overs from a whole baked chicken)
1/8 Cup Green Onions - Thinly Sliced
1/8 Cup Celery - Diced
1/8 Cup Carrots - Thinly sliced and cut into small pieces
1/4 Cup Mayo (Can be adjusted to your liking by using more or less mayo)
1/8 Cup Ranch Dressing
Salt and Pepper, if Desired

Directions:
Combine Chicken, Green Onion, Celery, Carrots and Mayo in Bowl.  Stir Together.  Add Ranch Dressing and Stir.  Salt and Pepper to Taste.  Refrigerate and Serve Alone, on Toast or on Crackers..

Submitter: 
MICHMEIG
Description: 
The ingredients in this chicken salad combine together very well.  I was not sure about adding ranch dressing, but it gave the chicken salad a little something extra.
