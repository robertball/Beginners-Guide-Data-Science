Title:
Swine & Garlic Taters with a hint of Lime Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666412.jpg
Ingredients:
Approx. 2 lb pork tenderloin sliced into 1 inch medallions
3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
The juice and zest of 1 lime
2 cloves minced garlic
1 small diced shallot
1 sprig chopped Rosemary
1 1/2 tsp sugar
1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 1/2 tsp olive oil
1 tsp butter
5 yukon gold potatoes, small diced
3 tbsp heavy cream
1 tbsp butter
1/4 tsp lime juice
1 tbsp butter
1 clove minced garlic

Directions:
Whisk together olive oil, lime juice, zest, garlic, shallot, rosemary, sugar, salt, and pepper. Pour over pork medallions in a sealed plastic bag and refrigerate for at least two hours.
In small sauce pan, sweat the garlic in the butter over low heat. Set aside.
Boil potatoes until fork tender. Strain them and put them in a large mixing bowl. Add heavy cream, butter, sauteed garlic, lime, salt and pepper to taste. Whip the potatoes with an electric mixer until they are smooth and no lumps are present.
In a saute pan over high heat, saute 5 medallions at one time. Cook meat until appropriate temperature is reached.
Serve with potatoes and enjoy!
