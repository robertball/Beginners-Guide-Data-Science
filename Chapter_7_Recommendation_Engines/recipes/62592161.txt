Title:
Nana's Tilapia with Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
Dash of ground black pepper
Dash of Old Bay seasoning
1/2 cup celery
1/2 cup green pepper
1/2 cup onions (green onion)
1 can tomato sauce or whole tomatoes (chopped)
3 to 4 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Season fillets with onion powder, , garlic powder, black pepper and Old bay seasoning coating both sides. Pour oil into skillet over medium-high heat. Place celery, onions  and peppers into oil and saute. Remove from skillet, place aside. Place fillets into skillet and sear both sides about 4 minutes each. Remove fillets from oil. Drain on a paper towel to discard excess oil.  Place onions, peppers and celery back into skillet adding tomato sauce. Adjust heat allow veggies to absorb the sauce. Place fillets into the skillet and cover with sauce and allow to simmer about 5 minutes. Enjoy!
