Ingredients:
3 cans (7oz) chunk light tuna, well drained
6 eggs (lightly beaten)
2 Roma tomatoes chopped
1/2 mid size onion chopped
1/2 can (14.5oz)whole corn kernels (drained)
1 serano chili pepper finely chopped(optional)

Directions:
Place the tuna in a large bowl, add the chopped tomatoes, onion, corn kernels and chili and mix well
Add beaten eggs to the bowl and stir all ingredients until well incorporated
Heat 2 Tbs of oil on a large pan, and carefully add the tuna cakes - 1 heaping spoon of mixture at the time. The cakes will expand a little, so do so do not overcrowd in the pan.
Cook on medium heat heat until lightly brown on each side, around 2-3 minutes per side.
Serve topped with any or your favorite salsa, or with any sauce you would use with crab cakes.

Submitter: 
PattyQ
Description: 
This is a delicious replacement for crab cakes and goes well as appetizers or main dish accompanied with some rice and a crispy salad.
This recipe was given to me by Mrs. Vargas, mother of two of my husbands third grade students, and it has become a must make on busy days!
