Title:
Jan's Wild Rice, Cranberry and Pecan Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2246830.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet instant yeast
2 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 large egg
2 tablespoons butter room temperature
1 cup warm water
1 8.5 oz package Uncle Ben's Ready Whole Grain Medley Brown & Wild Rice
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup dried cranberries, chopped
3 - 4 cups bread flour
1 egg beaten with 1 tablespoon of water

Directions:
Cook package of rice as directed.  Allow to cool to room temp.
In a large bowl combine yeast, sugar, salt, one egg, butter, water, rice, pecans and 3 cups bread flour. Using a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment mix dough until dough is smooth and elastic.
Add additional flour 1/4 cup at a time or until dough no longer sticks to sides of the bowl and forms a ball.  You may not need all of the flour.
Add in dried cranberries and knead for 1 minute to incorporate.
Shape dough into a ball. Place dough in a greased bowl, and turn once. Cover, and let rise in a warm place until doubled.
Punch dough down. lightly oil work surface and turn dough out of the bowl. Divide into 20 equal portions. Roll each portion into balls while cupping your hand rolling dough in small circles.
Grease a large casserole and place rolls in 5 rows of 4 about 1 1/2 inches apart.  Brush rolls with egg wash. Cover with a damp cloth. Let rise until nearly doubled, 35 to 40 minutes.
Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) oven for 20 to 25  minutes.  Remove from baking pan and cool on a wire rack.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Deliciously hearty rolls that are perfect for your holiday meal.  Rolls pair well with leftover turkey or ham and are exceptionally good for chicken salad sandwiches. We also enjoy them toasted for breakfast.
