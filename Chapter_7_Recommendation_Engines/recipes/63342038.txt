Title:
SWEET POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 SWEET POTATOES
1 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1/4 CUP WATER
1/4 CUP BUTTER
DASH OF SALT
1 BAG OF MINI MARSHMALLOWS

Directions:
BOIL SWEET POTATOES 3/4 WAY DONE (CUT INTO EITHER 2 OR 4 PIECES)
BRING TO A BOIL BROWN SUGAR, WATER, BUTTER & SALT. PUT MIXTURE OVER POTATOES AND ADD A BAG OF MINI MARSHMALLOWS AND BAKE AT 350 UNTIL POTATOES ARE DONE
