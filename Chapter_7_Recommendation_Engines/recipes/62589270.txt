Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/553889.jpg
Ingredients:
3 boneless, skinless chicken breast
10-12 count flour tortillas
1 large can of red enchilada sauce
1 small can of hot red enchilada sauce
1 small can of green chilis chopped
8 0z package creamcheese
2 bags of mex blend cheese
2-3 jalapanos
2 cloves garlic
1 quart heavy cream
2 cups yellow rice

Directions:
Boil chicken in stock or water remove when cooked about 20 minuets.
Start rice
Season chicken with salt or seasoning salt. Shredd
Finely chop garlic and jalapanos sautee in e.v.o.o until tender
Add green chilies and cream cheese to garlic and peppers. Use a spatula to break up cream cheese and stir together
Add 1/2 cup mex cheese blend to the cream cheese mixture stir
When cheese mixture is mixed well add shredded chicken and turn heat off
Fill tortillas with mixture and one spoonfull of rice each. Wrapp and place into a 9x13 pan
Pour heavy cream and enchilada sauce over the the enchiladas then top with the rest of the shredded cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minuets.
