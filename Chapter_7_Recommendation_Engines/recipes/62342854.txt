Title:
Healthier (and cheaper) Marinated Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, broken into small florets
8 ounces homemade Ital. dressing w/ Canola oil
1/2 cup homemade mayo w/ Canola oil
1/4 cup bacon bits (use fresh bacon)
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place cauliflower in a large bowl and cover with Italian dressing. Cover & refrigerate at least 1-3 hours, overnight is best.
Drain cauliflower and stir in mayonnaise, bacon bits and cheese.

Submitter: 
Brent Rice
Description: 
A head of cauliflower is marinated overnight in Italian dressing, then combined with mayonnaise, bacon bits and Parmesan cheese.Modified from: Marinated Cauliflower
