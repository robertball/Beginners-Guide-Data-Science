Title:
No-Yeast Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c flour
1 tsp salt
2 3/4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp garlic powder
2T olive oil
3/4 to 1 c water

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, salt and garlic powder together in a large bowl.  Add 3/4 c water and the oil.  Stir with a fork or wooden spoon until a soft dough forms.  If dough is too stiff, add water, one tablespoon at a time.  It should be soft but not sticky.  Knead about 2 minutes on a lightly floured surface until smooth.  Roll out on a pizza pan and pre-bake crust for t minutes at 450 degrees before adding toppings.
