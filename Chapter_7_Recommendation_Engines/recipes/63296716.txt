Title:
blueberry orange cranberry sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh cranberries
1 cup blueberries
1/2 cup orange juice
1 cup water
1/2 cup fine white sugar
pinch of salt
pinch of white pepper
1 tsp lemon zest
1/2 tsp corn starch

Directions:
in a medium sauce pan, heat the sugar and water until it is dissolved.
add the orange juice and lemon zest.
put the fresh cranberries into the mix.
cook for 10 mins on low heat.
add the blueberries, pepper, salt and cornstarch.
cook for 10 more mins, or until the cranberries explode and sauce thickens.
let cool completely before serving.
Enjoy!
