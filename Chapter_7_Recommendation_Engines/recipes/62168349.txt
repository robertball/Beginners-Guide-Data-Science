Title:
Utah Funeral Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
6 cups Potatoes thinly sliced
1 can  cream mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk
1 1/4 cup sour cream
2 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, grated
1/2 cup thinly sliced onion (optional) **
salt and pepper to taste
5 tablespoons melted butter
2 cups corn flake crushed

Directions:
In a pot of boiling water boil potatoes slices until cooked but still firm fresh potatoes or thaw frozen potatoes if your using frozen. Place potatoes in a  greased 9x13 casserole.
Combine soup, milk, sour cream, cheese, and onion  and salt and pepper to taste and mix well. Pour over potatoes.
Mixed cornflakes and butter and sprinkle over the top of potatoes and sauce.
Bake uncovered at 375 F for 30-45 minutes or until hot in the middle and browned.
Serves 8.

Submitter:
