Title:
Crescent Samosa Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs oil
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 lb ground beef
1 cup diced potato
1/2 cup diced cooked carrot
1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
5 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp curry powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp coriander
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp turmeric
2 8 oz crescent rolls
Dipping Sauce
1/3 cup honey
1 tbs lime juice
1 tbs soy sauce
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
2 garlic cloves, minced

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet. Add onion and 2 minced garlic cloves, cook about 5 minutes until onion is tender. Remove from pan. Add ground beef, cook and stir 5 minutes until browned. Drain. Stir in onion mixture and remaining filling ingredients. Heat oven to 375. Separate 1 can of dough into 4 rectangles,  firmly press perforations to seal. Cut rectangles to form 8 squares. Spoon heaping tbsp of filling in center of each square. Fold over dough forming triangles. Press edges firmly to seal. Repeat with remaining can of dough. Place on 2 cookie sheets. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until golden brown. In small bowl combine all sauce ingredients, mix well. serve warm with dipping sauce.
