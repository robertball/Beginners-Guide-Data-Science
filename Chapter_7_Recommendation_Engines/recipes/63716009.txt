Title:
Boat Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of hamburger
1/2 cup of ketchup
2 tablespoon mustard (any type you prefer)
1 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon of black pepper
1 Cup of instant oatmeal
1 Egg
1 Pack of hotdog buns (or 6-8 bread slices)
6-8 slices of cheese (American is best but others can be substituted for taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Mix together Hamburger and egg.Then add the ketchup, mustard, oatmeal, salt and pepper. Mix well until well coated.
Use a cookie sheet and place out 6-8 hotdogs buns (or bread slices). Spoon on a large spoonful of meat mix into the buns and spread. Place in oven for about 10 minutes
Add the cheese slices on top and put in another 10-12 minutes until meat is cooked thoroughly and buns are crispy.
