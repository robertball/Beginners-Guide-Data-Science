Title:
Lentil Soup Recipe

Ingredients:
1 medium onion
1 large sweet pepper yellow/orange/red
1 Tbs minced garlic
2 celery stalks with leaves
1/2 lb. diced smoked sausage
1 package lentils
14 oz diced tomatoes
64 oz chicken broth
2 bay leaves
1/2 can tomato paste
splash of red wine
splash of Worcestershire
salt and pepper
