Title:
Vegan Chocolate Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soy milk
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup canola oil
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup cocoa powder
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees
Combine the soy milk and apple cider vinegar, let this mixture sit out for two minutes to curdle
Whisk together the soy/vinegar mixture, sugar, oil, and vanilla. Beat until foamy
In a seperate bowl, mix together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cinnamon.
Add dry ingredients to the wet, in two batches.
Put liners into cupcake pan, and fill about 2/3.
Bake 16-18 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center comes out cleam.
Cool completely before icing.
