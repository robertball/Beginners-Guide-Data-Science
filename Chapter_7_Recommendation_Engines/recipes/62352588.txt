Title:
Broccoli Raisin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of broccoli
1 cup raisins
1 small can sliced water chestnuts
1 small red onion, diced
Dressing: 1 cup miracle whip, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 tsp milk, 2 tsp red wine vinegar

Directions:
Chop broccoli and place in large bowl. Add raisins, chestnuts, and onion.  Combine ingredients for dressing in small bowl. Pour over broccoli mixture and stir to combine. Cover with saran wrap. For best flavor refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
oscar'smom
Description: 
A refreshing crunchy sweet salad. Perfect for summer afternoons around the grill.
