Title:
Ranch Baked Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/707815.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded cooked chicken
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing
1/4 cup diced green chilies, rinsed and drained
4 9-inch flour tortillas, warmed
Salsa, Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® dressing and guacamole, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine chicken, cheese, Ranch and chilies in medium bowl. Divide chicken mixture evenly between each tortilla; fold in half to seal.  Place quesadillas on baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Garnish with salsa, Ranch and guacamole, if desired.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
In this version of the quesadilla tortillas filled with shredded chicken, green chiles, cheese, and creamy Ranch dressing are baked in the oven.
