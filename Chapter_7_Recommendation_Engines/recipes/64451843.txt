Title:
Momma's Fig Preserves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2390103.jpg
Ingredients:
6 - 7 cups quartered figs
3 cups sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger (tube or fresh grated)
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt
1 TBL lemon juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and bring to a simmer.  Continue to cook 3 - 4 hours at a low simmer, stirring every 20 minutes or until desired consistency.
Can using your favorite method.

Submitter: 
MA McBridges
Description: 
Ummm UMMM fig preserves!  Spread on buttered biscuits or toast, even great with cream cheese on a bagel  :)   ENJOY!  

With love, Momma
