Title:
Chicken Boobies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large or 6 average boneless chicken breasts
1 can creamy chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 stick butter
1 box Uncle Ben's Wild Rice Mix
Splash of Cooking Sherry
Parmesan Cheese
Almond Slivers

Directions:
Combine soups and butter in saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally until butter is melted.
Add sherry.
Combine 1/2 soup mixture with Rice and seasoning package. Spread in bottom of 13x9x2 baking dish.
Place chicken breasts on soup mixture. Cover with remaining soup.
Sprinkle top with cheese and almonds.
Bake at 275 for 2 1/2 hours.
