Title:
Easy Garlic Swai Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 swai (also known as basa) fish fillet
1 tsp. minced garlic
cooking spray
Cavender's all purpose Greek seasoning

Directions:
Spray a nonstick skillet with cooking spray.  Heat skillet to medium hot and saute garlic til lightly brown.
Scoop garlic to one side and add fish.  Season with Greek seasoning.
Scoop garlic and place atop the fish.
Cook until edges of fish turn white and underside is nicely browned, then turn fish.
Cook until it flakes easily.

Submitter: 
LaDonna Lonberger Sanders
Description: 
Low fat, low calorie, easy, and delish! 
Swai is also known as basa and is less expensive.  It has a very mild flavor and is as close to chicken as fish can get--not fishy at all!
