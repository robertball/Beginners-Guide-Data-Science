Title:
Mom's Hot Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Gallon Apple Juice
2 Tbsp. Red Hots
3-4 Cinnamon sticks
1/4 C. brown sugar
1 Tbsp. Whole cloves

Directions:
Pour juice in container and add remaining ingredients in percolator tray.
