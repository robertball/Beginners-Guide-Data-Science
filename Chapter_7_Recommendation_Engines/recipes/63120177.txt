Title:
Ricotta Pasta with Fresh Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz whole wheat Penne pasta
2 TBSP Brumel & Brown butter spread
1 cup reduced-fat Ricotta Cheese
1/2 cup reduced-fat parmesean cheese
10 oz fresh spinach
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook Pasta.
Transfer pasta to serving bowl; reserving 1/2 cup of the water the pasta cooked in.
Toss pasta with 1 1/2 TBPs Brummel & Brown
Cover to keep pasta warm
In a bowl mix ricotta chesse and pasta water; blend well.
Stir ricotta, parmesean, sal and pepper into pasta
Cover and steam spinach
Drain spinach well and chop coursely.
Toss spinach with remaining brummel & brown.
Toss spinach with pasta.
