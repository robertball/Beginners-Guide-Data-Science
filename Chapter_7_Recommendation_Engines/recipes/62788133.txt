Title:
BLT Dip... hold the lettuce! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/650708.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 cup mayo
1 (8oz) package cream cheese, softened
2 cups sharp shredded cheddar cheese
2 (2.5oz) packages of pre-cooked bacon
1 medium onion, chopped
2 TBS dried chives
15 Roma grape tomatoes, cut in half

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees F.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine the sour cream, mayo and cream cheese. Mix with and electric mixer until completely incorporated together. Remove bacon from the packaging and chop it up into 1/2 inch pieces, add to the cream cheese mixture and stir together.
Cut the Roma tomatoes in half and add to the cream cheese mixture.
Add the chopped onion, chives, and the shredded cheese.  Stir until all of the ingredients are completely mixed together.
Spread the mixture into the bottom of a glass 9" pie plate and bake for 30 minutes or until it is hot and bubbling.
Serve with corn chips or crackers.

Submitter: 
Miss B
Description: 
This gooey and delicious dip is the perfect combination of bacon, onions and tomatoes!  Better make a double batch because one will disappear quickly!
