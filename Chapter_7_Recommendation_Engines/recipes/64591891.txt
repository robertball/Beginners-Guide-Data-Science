Title:
Creamy Buttermilk Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes
4 garlic cloves
2 green onions, thinly sliced
1 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg

Directions:
Bring potatoes & whole, peeled garlic cloves to boil.
Cook until potatoes are tender (about 25 - 30 min.)
In a measuring cup, stir buttermilk with mustard, salt & nutmeg.
Drain potatoes & garlic. Return to saucepan & place back on hot burner. Gently stir until any water evaporates. Turn burner off.
Mash potatoes & garlic; stir in buttermilk mixture with green onions. Add more seasoning if desired.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A delicious variation of mashed potatoes without adding lots of calories.
