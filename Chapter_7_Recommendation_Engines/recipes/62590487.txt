Title:
Chicken Curry In A Hurry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c plain yogurt
1c mayo
3T chopped onion
1t ground ginger
2-3t curry (more or less as needed)
1lb chicken in strips
1t paprika
1/2t pepper
cooked rice

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with paprika and pepper.
Cook chicken in sprayed skillet.
Mix yogurt, mayo, ginger, onion, and curry.
Pour over chicken and heat through.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
