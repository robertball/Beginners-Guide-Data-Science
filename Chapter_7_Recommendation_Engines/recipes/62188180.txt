Title:
Scallion Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
2 tbsp water
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ginger
1/2 cup scallions
1 tsp baking powder
2 tsp sesame oil

Directions:
In bowl, add all ingredients except the sesame oil and mix til batter is smooth.
Heat oil in skillet on medium heat.
Pour batter into skillet to make 4 pancakes.
Cook until bubbly, about 2 minutes.
Flip and cook 1 minute, or until desired browness is reached.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
A nice change from sweet pancakes. They accompany savoury dishes nicely.
