Title:
Scalloped Herb and Garlic Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1128606.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled, thinly sliced
1 onion, sliced
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups KRAFT Creamy Herb & Garlic Shredded Cheese with a TOUCH OF PHILADELPHIA
2/3 cup milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Spread 1/3 of the potatoes onto bottom of 2-L casserole dish sprayed with cooking spray; top with half each of the onions and flour, then 1/2 cup cheese. Repeat layers. Cover with remaining potatoes.
Pour milk over all layers; cover.
Bake 1 hour or until potatoes are tender, uncovering and sprinkling with remaining cheese for the last 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese with a TOUCH OF PHILADELPHIA
Description: 
A short cut method to the great taste of cheesy scalloped potatoes.
