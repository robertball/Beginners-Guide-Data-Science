Title:
Jalapeno Popper Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sliced mild jalapenos (about 15 per sandwich)(set on paper towel to dry off as much liquid as possible)
Butter (desired amount for spreading on bread for grilling)
Your choice of bread for grilling
Thinly Sliced Pepper Jack Cheese
Softened Cream Cheese

Directions:
Butter one side of each piece of bread. Placing the buttered side of one piece on the other piece of bread spread cream cheese evenly on inner side of bread. Lay the jalapeno slices in a layer on the cream cheese, then place on the griddle/pan. Layer pepper jack cheese on top of jalapenos, and then place the other piece of buttered bread, butter side up, on top of cheeseyness. Grill on each side until golden brown and melty.
