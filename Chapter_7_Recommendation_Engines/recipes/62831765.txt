Title:
Tortilla Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ozs. Cream Cheese, soften
1/3 cup of chopped NM green chile
1 clove of garlic minced
package of flour tortilla

Directions:
Mix together cream cheese, green chile, and mince garlic. Spread a couple of tablespoons of mixture all over tortilla and roll them up. Cut in to 1 1/2" pieces and place on serving platter. Takes about 10 to 15 minutes from start to finish.

Submitter: 
rlt11_NMC
Description: 
This is a very simply appetizers but well liked. When we go to parties and I ask what can I bring this is requested a lot.
