Title:
AUG - Santa Fe Cooler revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
Makes: 12 servings
1 2-inch piece fresh ginger, sliced
2 cups water
1 can (12-ounce) frozen lemonade concentrate
1 bottle (12-ounce) beer, Red Stripe®
31/2 cups ginger ale, Canada Dry®
11/2 cups vodka, Cîroc®
Ice cubes, lime slices (optional)

Directions:
Directions
Bring sliced ginger and water to a boil in a small saucepan. Remove from heat and let stand for 20 minutes. Strain, reserving ginger water. In a large pitcher, stir to combine thawed lemonade concentrate, ginger water, beer, ginger ale, and vodka. Pour in a glass over ice. Garnish with lime slices(optional).
