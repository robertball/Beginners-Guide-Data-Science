Title:
Creamy Brandy Alexander Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/746122.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon brandy
1 tablespoon creme de cacao
1 1/2 cups milk
1 pint French vanilla ice cream
1/4 cup sweetened whipped cream
1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions:
In the container of a blender, combine the brandy, creme de cacao, milk and ice cream. Process until smooth. Serve in glasses topped with whipped cream and a dash of cinnamon.

Submitter: 
Chrissy DeCosmo Fessler
Description: 
This delicious drink is a great de-stressor for those hectic holidays!
