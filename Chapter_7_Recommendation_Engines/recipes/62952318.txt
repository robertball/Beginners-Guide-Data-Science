Title:
Apple Puff Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cooking apples, cored, thinly sliced
1/3 stick butter
1/3 cup sugar
Cinnamon
Freshly grated nutmeg
6 eggs
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup flour
1 cup milk

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
In an 9x13 pyrex dish, melt butter.
Add sliced apples, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg (to taste) and cook slowly until sugar is melted and apples begin to soften.
In the meantime, mix eggs, vanilla, flour and milk; pour liquid over apples.
Bake for 20-30 minutes until puffed and done in center.
