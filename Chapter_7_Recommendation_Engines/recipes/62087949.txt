Title:
Traditional Cured Fish (Umai) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red snapper fillets, deboned with no skin
1 onion, chopped
1 red onion, chopped
1 tbsp crushed fresh ginger
2 limes (the juice and 1 tbsp rind)
1 fresh red chilli, crushed
Salt to taste

Directions:
Slice the red snapper into thin strips (cut against the muscle).
In a large bowl, mix the chopped onions, ginger, chilli and lime rind using a wooden spoon.
Add in the fish, squeeze in the juice of the 2 limes. Add salt to taste. Mix gently.
Leave to stand for at least 15 minutes to allow for the fish to be cured.
Served chilled as appitizer or as a side dish to grilled seafood.

Submitter: 
Mad Izatie
Description: 
This is a traditional Melanau recipe which my father makes all the time, and is a favorite amongst relatives and friends. It does not involve cooking.
