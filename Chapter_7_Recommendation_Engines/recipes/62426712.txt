Title:
Spaghetti & Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448262.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c Bread Crumbs (Italian Seasoned)
½ c Parmesan Cheese
2 pkg French Onion Soup Mix
2 cloves Garlic (minced)
4 lg Eggs
1 T Worcestershire Sauce
2 T Italian Seasoning
½ t Salt
1½ c Milk
4 lb Ground Beef
1 can Tomato Juice
1 can Tomato Paste
2 T Beef Bouillon
1 lb Spaghetti

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350° on broil.
MIX first 9 ingredients in a large bowl.
ADD ground beef, but don't over mix.
ROLL mixture into 1"-1½" balls. (PC scoop works well)
BROIL for 10 minutes.
TURN and broil for another 10 minutes.
BOIL tomato juice, paste and beef bouillon in a stockpot.
ADD meatballs, reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes.
SERVE over spaghetti.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description: 
This is my recipe.  These are the easiest, most tasty meatballs that you broil in the oven.
