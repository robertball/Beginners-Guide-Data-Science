Title:
Kool-Aid Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
46 oz. jar whole dill pickles
2 Cups water
1 Cup sugar
2 packets cherry Kool-Aid

Directions:
Drain and discard pickle juice.
Cut pickles in half and replace in jar.
Mix sugar and Kook-Aid into water until dissolved.
Add mixture into pickles until covered.
Refrigerate 24 hours.

Submitter: 
Valorie
Description: 
Cherry Kool-Aid flavors these dill pickles to produce a day-glow result.
