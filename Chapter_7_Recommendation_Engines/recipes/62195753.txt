Title:
Pork Chops and Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops (thick cut)
2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 clove garlic minced
1 and 1/2 cup water
1 cup brown rice
1/4 cup onion minced
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 cup white cheddar cheese shredded
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Season chops with salt and pepper and brown. Add onions and garlic to pan, saute until soft. Mix together ingredients in a 9x13 baking dish. Top with chops and bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 hr or until rice is done and sauce has thickened.

Submitter: 
LAMBR
Description: 
An easy tasteful weeknight dinner. Whole grain brown rice in a light creamy tomato sauce with tender moist chops.
