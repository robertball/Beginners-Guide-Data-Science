Title:
Nacho Platter Ole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Nacho Platter Ole
1 can refried beans
1 pkg. tortilla chips
1 can chili or 2 c. homemade chili, heated
1 jar (16 oz.) Cheez whiz cheese sauce
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Spread beans onto center of large serving platter. Arrange chips around beans. Pour hot chili over beans. Microwave cheez whiz as directed on label. Serve as a dip with chips.
