Title:
Asian Steamed Pork Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2948870.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 inch piece ginger grated
5 shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and finely chopped
1 scallion, finely chopped
1/4 pound ground pork
1 tablespoon light soy sauce
1 tablespoon Shaoxing rice wine or dry sherry
1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
2 teaspoons cornstarch
Sea salt to taste
freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix well with your hands until incorporated
Brush the steamer with vegetable oil; arrange the meatballs in the steamer and cover.  Steam 8 to 10 minutes, or until the pork is cooked through.
