Title:
Lily's Chicken Surprise Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz. pkg. penne(or your favorite noodle)
1/2 cup onion, minced
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 1/2 lbs. chicken breast cooked and cubed
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1`soup can of milk
1/2 cup mayonaise
3 tablespoons spicy brown mustard
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 cup shredded cheddar
1 10 oz. bag of frozen peas
1 small head of broccoli, cup in bite size pieces
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
ground pepper to taste.
Broken potato chips to cover top of casserole

Directions:
Cook penne according to package directions until al dente.  Drain and set aside.  While penne is cooking, saute onion in olive oil until soft, about 3 min.  Transfer onions to a large bowl and add next eleven ingredients.  Mix well.  Stir in cooked penne and mix again.  Transfer to a greased 9x13 casserole.  Top with broken potato chips and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Mary P.
Description: 
A wonderful cream chicken and penne casserole that everyone in our family loves. It seems like a lot of ingredience but it's everything you generally have on hand so it mixes up quick
