Title:
Doodhi (oppo sqaush) omlet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3427008.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium grated doodhi (oppo squash)
1 medium grated onion
2 garlic + ½ inch ginger + 1 or 2 green chilies - crushed or made into a paste
½ tbsp garam masala
¼ tsp turmeric powder
1 cup oats
2 ½ cup rice flour
Salt as per taste
1/2 tbsp cilantro (chopped)
3 or 4 cup water
1 cup Oil

Directions:
In a bowl take grated doodhi (oppo squash) and grated onion. Add garlic-ginger-chili paste, gram masala, turmeric powder and cilantro and salt.
Add oats and rice flour and 3-4 cup water and mix it well to create batter and keep aside for 10 minutes.
Heat 7 to 10-inch nonstick omelet pan or skillet over medium-high heat until hot and apply 1-2 tsp oil on the pan or skillet.
Take a big spoonful of the batter. Pour it on the pan. As soon as you pour it, quickly spread the batter in a circular manner to get a medium and round omlet.
Pour some oil on the top and edges.
As one side is done completely, flip and cook other side.
