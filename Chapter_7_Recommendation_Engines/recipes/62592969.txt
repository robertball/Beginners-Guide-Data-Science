Title:
Make it anytime salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 ounce can diced tomatoes in liquid
1 15 ounce can diced tomatoes with greed chiles - drained
4 green onions - chopped
2 cloves garlic - minced
2 Tbsp fresh cilantro - finely chopped
1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 tsp kosher salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in food processor and blend to desired consistency. Chill for at least one hour. Serve.

Submitter: 
J Scholfield
Description: 
This is a great recipe for salsa in the winter when fresh tomatoes aren't available.
