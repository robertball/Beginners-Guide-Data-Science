Title:
Crockpot Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 Chicken breasts
1 medium onion chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) Can chicken broth
1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatos with green chili
1 Jar Salsa Verde
1/2 Teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1 can corn
2 (15 ounces) can white beans
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain liquid from corn and white beans.
Mix everything together in crockpot and place the chicken breast on top.
Let cook for 6-8 hours on low
Shred chicken
Serve with sour cream and cheese

Submitter: 
Starr
Description: 
Made a chicken chili recipe and it was bland no flavor so took the ingrediants and added my own flair. My family loves it when it gets cold because they know this will be on the menu. It's very flavorful and so healthy. Enjoy!!!
