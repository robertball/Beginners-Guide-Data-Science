Title:
Sweet Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123022.jpg
Ingredients:
1 quart dill pickles (drained and sliced thin)
1 small onion (cut fine)
2 cups white sugar
3 Tablespoons vinegar
1/2 teaspoon celery seed

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients in a medium bowl.
2. Let set for 24 hours then refrigerate.
