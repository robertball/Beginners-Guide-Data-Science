Title:
Saucy Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ large green bell pepper roughly chopped
6 medium button mushrooms sliced
½ small onion roughly chopped
2 T butter
2 cloves of garlic
Splash of lemon juice
Splash of lime juice
¼ C of white wine
¼ C of diced tomates
14 large cooked shrimp
2 C of cooked pasta
¼ C crumbled feta cheese
Freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Saute peppers, mushroom and onions in butter to desired tenderness.
Add healthy splashes of lemon and lime juice and simmer for 2 minutes.
Add white wine and allow sauce to reduce by ½, around 5 minutes.
Add diced tomatoes and top with cooked shrimp.
Cover pan with lid and put on low, and cook pasta in the meantime.
Once pasta is done and drained, added to sauce/shrimp mixture and toss in feta.
Serve with garlic bread!
