Title:
Black Bean and Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup ditalini pasta
1 1/2 cups grape tomatoes - halved
3/4 cup avocado - diced
1/2 cup poblano chile - chopped
1/2 cup cucumber - chopped
1/3 cup red onion - chopped
2 tbsp. cilantro - chopped
1 - 15oz. can black beans - rinsed and drained
2 tsp. lime zest
2 tbsp. lime juice
1 tbsp. cider vinegar
2 tsp. olive oil
3/4 tsp. garlic - minced
3/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 lime - cut into 4 wedges

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente, drain and cool completely.
Combine tomatoes, avocado, poblano, cucumber, onion, cilantro and beans, stirring well.
Whisk zest, juices, vinegar, oil, garlic, salt and pepper.
Add pasta and dressing to bean mixture and toss to coat.
Serve with lime wedges.
