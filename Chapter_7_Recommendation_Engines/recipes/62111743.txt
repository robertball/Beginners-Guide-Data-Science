Title:
Veggie Dill Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free mayo
1 cup fat-free sour cream
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tbsp beau monde seasoning
1 tbsp dried dill weed
1 tbsp dried onion flakes
1 tbsp dried parsley

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in medium bowl. Refrigerate for at least two hours, preferably overnight.  Serve with baby carrots, colorful bell pepper strips, radishes, cauliflower, snap peas, celery, etc.  Garnish with fresh parsley sprigs for entertaining.

Submitter: 
Kris Swiggum
Description: 
EASY, inexpensive, crisp, flavorful, great party appetizer!  Never have I had to take leftovers home!  I keep some in the fridge most of the time for a mostly healthy after-work snack.
