Title:
Ham Steak over Mixed Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. package boneless ham steak
2 bags mixed greens
1/3 cup reduced fat feta cheese
black pepper
dressing:
3 T olive oil
1 T dijon mustard
1 T lemon juice
1 t champagne vinegar
1/4 t black pepper

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium high heat.
Coat each side of ham steak with cooking spray and season with black pepper.
Grill ham steak until grill marks form, approx. 4-5 minutes per side.
Cut ham steak into bite size pieces.
Whisk dressing ingredients in a large bowl.
Add greens until well coated.
To serve, plate greens. Add ham and feta.

Submitter: 
Jandree
Description: 
Ham steak paired with a mustard dressing makes a quick low-carb dinner.
