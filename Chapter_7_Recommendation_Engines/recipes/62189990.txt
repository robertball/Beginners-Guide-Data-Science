Title:
Chicken braid Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c chicken chopped
1 c chopped broccoli
1/2 c red bell pepper
1 garlic clove pressed
4oz shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c mayonnaise
2 tsp dill
2 containers crescent rolls

Directions:
mix chopped chicken with broccoli, red bell pepper, garlic and cheese.  Add mayo and dill.  Unroll crescent dough, cut 1 inch strips on longest sides.  Spread filling in middle then lay strips accross the top.  Bake at 375 for 25-28 minutes.
