Title:
Bok Choy Thai Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut water
1 frozen chopped banana
1 apple, cored
1 leaf bok choy
2 tablespoons broccoli sprouts, or to taste
1 tablespoon shredded coconut
1 tablespoon chia seeds
1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root
1 teaspoon spirulina powder (optional)

Directions:
Combine coconut water, banana, apple, bok choy, sprouts, coconut, chia seeds, ginger, and spirulina powder in a blender; blend until smooth.
