Title:
Southern-Style Frying Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB Lard Or Vegetable Shortening
1/2 CUP Butter
1/2 CUP Country-Style Ham, Diced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a saucepan over low heat.
Simmer for 30 to 40 minutes, skimming as needed.
Strain and store in an air tight container in an area away from light or use immediately for pan/deep frying.

Submitter: 
Chef Chris
Description: 
If you're looking to make some true Southern-fried food, then you'll definitely want to use this oil.
