Title:
Tomato Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/683721.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large tomatoes, diced
2 large cucumbers, diced
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup diced green pepper
1 8-ounce bottle Italian salad dressing
2 tsp. sugar

Directions:
Mix tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, and green pepper in bowl. Mix sugar and salad dressing and pour over vegetables. Chill before serving.
