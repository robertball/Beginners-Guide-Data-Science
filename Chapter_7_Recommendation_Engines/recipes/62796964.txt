Title:
Parmesan Crusted Swai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Swai Fillets(Approximately 7-8 inches)
2 Eggs
1 teaspoon granulated Garlic or Garlic Powder
1 teaspoon Black Pepper
2 cups Grated Parmesan
4 Tablespoons Canola Oil
4 Tablespoons Olive Oil
2 Tablespoons Parsley
1 Lemon cut into wedges

Directions:
Beat eggs slightly with Garlic and Pepper. Coat the fish fillets with the beaten egg, draining off excess. Roll the fillets in the Parmesan to coat. Let sit 5 minutes before cooking.
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until hot, but not smoking. Add the fillets and cook on one side,about 2 to 2 1/2 minutes until golden brown. Turn over and brown other side. Sprinkle with Parsley and serve with Lemon wedge.

Submitter: 
John W.
Description: 
A light a crispy piece of Vietnam delight. This is a great tasting catfish for those who don't really like the American variety. My kids loved it and they ages 1 to 5. Its simple and goes great with various Pilaf and Risotto sides. Enjoy.
