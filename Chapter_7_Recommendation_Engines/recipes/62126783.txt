Title:
Uncle Ronnie's Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 pound chuck roast
2 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons Montreal Steak grill seasoning
2 tablespoons oregano
salt & pepper
1 14 ounce can beef broth
water
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Mix garlic powder, grill seasoning, oregano and salt & pepper to taste together in a small bowl. Rub generously on both sides of chuck roast.
Place in slow cooker and pour beef broth around it. Add bay leaf and just enough water to cover roast.
Cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Janine
Description: 
After trying many different recipes for chuck roast, this one is the keeper! My family goes crazy when they smell this working in the slow cooker. It is packed with flavor!
