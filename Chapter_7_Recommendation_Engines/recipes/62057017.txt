Title:
best bean soup ever!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion chopped
1 green pepper chopped
1 yellow pepper chopped
2 Tbs olive oil
2 16oz cans kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 16 oz can pinto beans undrained
1 can black beans undrained
2 cans (14 1/2 oz) stewed tomatoes
1 can chicken broth
1-2 cups medium picante sauce
1 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Saute onion and peppers in olive oil until soft. Add the rest of the ingredients and heat until bubbly. Garnish with cheese, chopped green onion, avacado, etc. Freezes well.

This is a very easy and delicious soup. It also looks very pretty.
