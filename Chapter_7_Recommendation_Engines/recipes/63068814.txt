Title:
Left-Over Steak Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Left-over pre-cooked steak
4 Tortillas
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 green onion, diced
2 cups of chihuahua cheese
Handful of canned jalapenos
Serve with salsa, sour cream, and my jalapeno lime dressing

Directions:
Preheat the broiler
Chop garlic and green onions and slice steak
Place tortillas on baking sheet and layer all ingredients
Place topped tortilla in broiler for 3 minutes then remove
Spray secondary tortilla with Pam and place on top of hot quesadilla and flip
Broil/bake for 2 - 3 more minutes
