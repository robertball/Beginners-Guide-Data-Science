Title:
Veggie Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 can sliced water chestnts drained and minced fine.
1/2 cup minced sweet onion
1 16 oz. sour cream
1/2 cup Hellmans mayo
1 T. soy sauce
1/2 t. garlic powder
1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped or (1 T. dry)

Directions:
Mix all well and chill.
