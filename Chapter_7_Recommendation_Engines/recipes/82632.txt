Title:
eDiets Spinach-Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 cups fresh spinach
8 cherry tomatoes
1/2 small red onion
3 tablespoons fresh shredded carrots
1 hard boiled egg
2 1/2 tablespoons salad dressing, low fat or nonfat; low sodium
1/2 cup croutons

Directions:
Rinse, dry and tear spinach into bite-sized pieces; rinse and cut tomatoes in half; chop onion and slice hard boiled egg. Place spinach and all vegetables in salad bowl and toss with dressing. Top with egg slices and croutons and serve.

Submitter: 
eDiets
Description: 
This is a classic spinach salad with hard boiled egg, carrot and tomato. A few croutons top this one off beautifully.
