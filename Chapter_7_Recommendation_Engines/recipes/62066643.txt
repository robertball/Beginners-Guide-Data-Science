Title:
Baked Chicken with Salsa and Sour Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 packet of taco seasoning
1 cup sour cream
1 cup salsa
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese or Mexican blend cheese

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2) Place chicken breasts in a gallon ziplock bag and pour in taco seasoning.  Shake to coat chicken.
3) Mix salsa and sour cream together.
4) Place chicken breasts in baking dish and spoon salsa-sour cream mixture over chicken.
5) Bake for 30 minutes.
6) Sprinkle cheese over top of chicken and bake for another 10 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted.
