Title:
Instant Oatmeal Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup powdered milk (or non-dairy powdered coffee creamer)
1 cup confectioner's sugar
2/3 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly; store in an airtight container.
For each batch of oatmeal, combine 2/3 cup quick-cooking oats with 1-2 Tablespoons of mix.  Add boiling water until desired consistency is reached.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
This tastes remarkably similar to the maple and brown sugar instant oatmeal varieties you buy in little packets.  Save money by making your own!
