Title:
Baked Creamy Lemon Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3588429.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb farfalle (or pasta of choice)
2-3 chicken breasts
2 Tbs butter
2 Tbs olive oil
3-4 med cloves of garlic, minced
1 lemon, zested and juiced
1/2 of an 8-oz block of cream cheese (can use Neufchatel)
1 c sour cream (can use light or fat-free)
1/2 c grated parmesan cheese
1/2 c white wine (optional)
2 c chicken stock (2-1/4 if not using wine)
2 Tbs flour
1 Tbs parsley
1 tsp thyme
1/2-1 tsp salt (to taste)
1/8-1/4 tsp black or white pepper (to taste)
1/2 - 1 tsp crused red pepper (to taste)
* Can substitute 1 lb peeled & deveined shrimp for chicken, or keep meat-free
* Asparagus or broccoli is a great addition to this dish

Directions:
Slice or cube chicken and season lightly with a little salt and pepper
Cook pasta for HALF THE TIME indicated on box to reach "al dente" (should be only slightly cooked). Drain, cover and set aside.
Preheat oven to 375
Bring a large pan to temperature over medium-high heat and add butter and olive oil
When butter has melted, add chicken and cook til lightly browned
Add wine, if using, and cook for a minute or two
*If adding veggies, put them into the pan now
Add garlic and lemon juice and cook for a minute or two (don't allow garlic to brown)
Reduce heat to medium-low and add cream cheese, stirring til melted and blended
Add flour to sour cream, mix well and then stir into pan
Add chicken broth, the lowest amount of salt, black pepper, red pepper, parsley and thyme
Bring contents of pan to simmer til slightly thickened (add more broth if too thick)
Remove pan from heat
Add grated parm and lemon zest, stir well and adjust salt & seasonings to taste if needed
Add cooked pasta to contents of pan and blend well
Pour into a greased casserole dish OR 9"x13"pan, sprinkle with extra grated cheese
Bake 15 minutes, covered
Remove cover and bake 7 to 10 minutes more, til lightly browned
Serve topped with a little extra lemon juice if desired

Description: 
A delicious creamy blend of sauteed chicken, light but creamy lemon sauce and pasta baked til the top is crispy and lightly browned
