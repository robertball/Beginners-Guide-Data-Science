Title:
Chicken with Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast halves, boneless and skinless
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cornstarch
1 Tbsp & 1 tsp oil
1/2 slice ginger, chopped fine
1/2 scallion, chopped fine
1/2 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 tsp sherry
2 tsp soy sauce
Sugar, pinch
2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Slice shicken in cubes.  Mix chicken with salt, cornstarch and 1 tsp oil, set aside.
Heat 1 Tbsp oil in wok to 275 degrees F.  Stir fry ginger and scallion 1 minute.  Add chicken and stir fry 1 minute at 400 degrees F.  Add mushrooms, sherry, soy sauce and sugar.  Stir fry 1 minute.
Add cooked rice and cook for 20 minutes on low heat.
