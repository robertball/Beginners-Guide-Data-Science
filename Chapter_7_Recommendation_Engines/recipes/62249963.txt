Title:
Janet's Caramelized French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe Caramelized Onions
1 10 oz can beef consomme
2 c. water
1/2 t. thyme
1/4 c. dry white wine
6 c. large croutons
1 c. shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in slow cooker.  Cook, covered, at HIGH 2 1/2 hours or until thouroughly heated.  Stir in wine.
Ladle soup into 6 ovenproof bowls, and top evenly with croutons and cheese.  Place bowls on a sheet pan.
Broil 3 inches away from heat 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Serve immediately.
