Title:
Mango Cherry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1273039.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pitted cherries
1 cup chopped mango
1 cup water
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Blend cherries, mango, water, and ice cubes together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Savvy1324
Description: 
This exotic summer fruit smoothie is a tangy, cherry-chunked, fun snack that will cool you down in the summertime.
