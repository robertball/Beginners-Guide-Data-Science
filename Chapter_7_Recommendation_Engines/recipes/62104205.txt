Title:
Chicken with Rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast, deboned and halved
1 cup flour
1 tsp salt
1 tbs thyme
2 tbs olive oil
2 cups chopped fresh mushrooms
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup white wine
2 large sprigs fresh rosemary (or 3 tbs dried)
4 cloves garlic, smashed

Directions:
GET READY
Put chicken in a zip top bag and tenderize.
Mix flour, salt, and thyme in another bag.
Add oil to non-stick pan over medium heat.
Add chicken broth to a pot over high heat.
SET
Add rosemary,garlic & mushrooms to broth.
Add chicken to dry ingredients and coat.
Pan fry chicken about 3 min until crispy on outside, then set aside.
GO
Add broth mixture, wine, and chicken back to skillet.  Cover and simmer for 45 min - 1 hr.  You can add more flour or a mix of cornstarch and cold water to thicken the sauce.

Submitter: 
KitchenWitch
Description: 
This is a super easy recipe with easy to find ingredients.  It can be made ahead, and reheated stovetop or in a slow cooker the next day and still tastes great.
