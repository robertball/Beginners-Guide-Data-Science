Title:
Pan Seared Parmesan Crusted Filet Mignon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5064509.jpg
Ingredients:
4 filet mignons
Kosher salt
Garlic salt
Minced garlic
Paprika
Fresh ground pepper
FOR THE CRUST
1/4 cup unsalted butter melted
1/4 cup Italian Panko
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
salt - to taste
pepper - to taste

Directions:
Prepare the crust at least three hours in advance.  combine all of the ingredients and mash together to form a ball of dough.
Wrap the dough ball in a sheet of plastic wrap and form it into a log. refrigerate it.
Combine the kosher salt,  garlic, salt, minced garlic, paprika, and pepper into a rub and rub into both sides of the filets.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Heat a cast iron skillet on high heat.
Add 1 teaspoon of butter and 1 teaspoon of olive oil to the skillet
Add the filets and grill for 2 minutes per side.
Place the skillet with the filets in the preheated oven for 6 minutes
Remove from oven; turn the oven to broil, Top the filets with 1/2 inch slices of the crust.  Place the filets under the broiler for about 2 minutes or until browned.
Remove from oven and let rest for at least 5 minutes before serving
