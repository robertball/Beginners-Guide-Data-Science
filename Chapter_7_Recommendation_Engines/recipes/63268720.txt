Title:
Southern Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2/3 c. sugar
1 c. dark corn syrup
1/3 c. melted butter
1 c. pecan halves
1 9" pie shell
dash of salt

Directions:
Beat eggs with sugar, salt, corn syrup and butter. Add pecans.
Pour into unbaked pie shell.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 40 minutes.
