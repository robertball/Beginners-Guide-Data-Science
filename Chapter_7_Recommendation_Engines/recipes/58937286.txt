Title:
Zucchini Cornbread Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups shredded zucchini
1 onion, chopped
2 eggs, beaten
1 (8.5 ounce) package dry corn muffin mix
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
12 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 can of drained corn.

Directions:
Shred zucchini and let sit for about an hour in a collander with a little salt sprinkled on the zucchini. Squeeze remaining water out before use.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
In a large bowl mix together the zucchini, onion, eggs, muffin mix, corn, salt and pepper.  Stir in 8 ounces of the cheese.  Spread this mixture into a greased 2 quart casserole dish;  top with remaining 4 ounces of cheese.
Bake in a preheated oven for 60 minutes.
