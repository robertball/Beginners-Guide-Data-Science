Title:
Easy FRESH Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 Roma Tomatoes chopped
1 small onion chopped
1 clove minced garlic
2 T. fresh squeezed lime juice
1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Put in a container with a tight lid and store in the refrigerator until ready to use.  Best after a few hours.

Submitter: 
ttkc
Description: 
I keep this in the frig. for tortilla chips and also to add to my cooking, ie. brown beans, casseroles etc.
