Title:
Cuervo Golden Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983730.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Cuervo Especial Gold
1/2 ounce Grand Marnier
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
1 cup ice
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Rub rim of a chilled margarita glass with half a lime.
Dip into salt to coat.
In a shaker with ice, add Cuervo Especial Gold,  Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix, and Grand Marnier.
Shake vigorously.
Strain into glass filled with ice.

Submitter: 
Jose Cuervo
Description: 
Jose Cuervo Especial and Grand Marnier create a classic, premium margarita.
