Title:
Cinnamon Apple Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1 1/2 lbs pork tenderloin
2 TBS cornstarch
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 TBS brown sugar
2 apples, peeled, cored and sliced

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2) Place the tenderloin in a roasting pan or casserole dish.
3) Combine the remaining ingredients in a bowl and stir. Spoon apple mix around the pork.
4) Cover and bake 30 minutes. Remove lid and spoon mix over pork. Return to oven and bake 15-20 minutes longer or until pork is browned and cooked through
