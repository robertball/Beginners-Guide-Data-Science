Title:
Debbie's Pecans Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Flour
1/2 Cup Sugar
1/4 t. Salt
3/4 Cup Butter
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Cup Light Corn Syrup
1/2 cup Butter
4 Large Eggs, beaten
2 1/2 Cups chopped Pecans
1 teasponn Vanilla

Directions:
Combine first three ingredients.
Cut in 3/4 c butter with pastry blender until mixture resembles fine crumbs.
Press into greased 13 x 9 pan using a piece of plastic wrap.
Bake at 350 degrees for 17-20 minutes or until lightly browned.
Combine brown sugar, corn syrup, Â½ cup butter in saucepan.  Bring to boil over medium heat, stirring gently.  Remove from heat, stir 1/4 of hot mixture into beaten eggs, add to remaining hot mixture.  Stir in nuts and vanilla.  Pour over crust.
Bake at 350 degrees until set, about 35 min.
