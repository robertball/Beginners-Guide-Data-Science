Title:
Seafood Stuffed Trout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 13" - 16" inch trout, cleaned & Butterflied
2 eggs separated (whites saved)
1 c. bread crumbs
2 eggs, separated
2 tbsp. melted butter
6 oz. crab meat or chopped cooked shrimp
1/2 tbsp Old Bay Seasoning
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/2 tsp. onion powder
Lemon pepper to taste
2 scallions, chopped fine
Juice from 1/2 of a fresh lemon

Directions:
Wash and dry fish.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Mix remaining ingredients except egg whites.
Beat egg white stiff and fold into stuffing.
Fill each fish generously.
Brush with melted butter.
Place on greased baking sheet.
Bake 5-10 minutes at 400 degrees. Then broil 5 minutes until fish is tender and brown.
No need to turn.
Skin and heads may be removed before serving if desired.

Submitter: 
Brad
Description: 
I wanted a recipe similar to one I had at a restaurant with flounder, but for use with trout I had caught.  After doing some experimentation this is the closest I could get.  I've also made it on the grill and it turns out great.  My family requests it all the time!
