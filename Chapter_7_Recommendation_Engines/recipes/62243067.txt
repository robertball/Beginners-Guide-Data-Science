Title:
Healthy Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups oats
1/2 cup sunflower seed
1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
1/4 cup wheat bran
1/2 dried cranberries
1/2 cup light maple syrup
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300.
Mix everything together in big bowl.
If it seems to dry, add more maple syrup.
Spread onto two sheet pans and bake for 25-30 minutes, stirring every so often.
Let cool and enjoy

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
I wanted to make a granola without oil. So I went by a basic recipe and changed it to fit my tastes.
