Title:
Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 strawberries, hulled
1/2 c skim milk
1/2 c plain milk
3 T sugar
2 t vanilla
6 ice cubes, crushed

Directions:
Combine all in a blender.  Blend until smooth. Pour and serve.
