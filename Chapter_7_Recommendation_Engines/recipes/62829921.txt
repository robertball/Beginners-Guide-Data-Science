Title:
Szechuan Chicken with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast strips (about 2 ounces each), cut into cubes
1/4 cup egg substitute
1 TBL cornstarch
1 tsp black pepper
1 TBL canola oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper (adjust to taste)
1 TBL chopped green onions
2 TBL hoisin sauce
4 TBL rice vinegar
4 TBL soy sauce
1 TBL sesame oil
1 tbl brown sugar
1 package of frozen asian style mixed vegetables with NO sauce

Directions:
In a small bowl, add hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and brown sugar.  Set aside.
Place frozen vegetables in microwave and cook according to the package instructions.
Pour egg substitute in a another bowl and add chicken, cornstarch and black pepper.  Stir and set aside.
In a wok or skillet, add canola oil, garlic, ginger, and crushed red pepper and stir.
Next add chicken and cook for about 2 minutes.
Pour sauce over chicken and stir.
Add in vegetables and stir so everything is heated through and coated with sauce.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
I modified the Szechuan Chicken with Mangos recipe from the AllRecipes video to come up with a more pantry friendly and quick recipe as well as get some vegetables in it.  Serve with your choice of white or brown rice for a great meal.
