Title:
Honey Mustard Dressing- Transitions Lifestyle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup plain yogurt (no sugar)
1 tablespoon spicey brown mustard
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the yogurt, mustard, honey, and lemon juice. Store covered in the refrigerator.
