Title:
Roasted Garlic Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cannellini or garbanzo beans
3/4 cup virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
1 head roasted garlic, pealed into cloves
1 teaspoon salt
Crushed black pepper to taste

Directions:
Dump it all into a food processor, blend until smooth.  If prefered, heat in the microwave until warm.  Serve with crusty bread, breadsticks, pita chips, or what have you.  It's almost a meal in itself.

Submitter: 
Sharon
Description: 
Perfect with rustic bread, pita chips, or even the healthy types with veggies.  I prefer it served warm, but you can serve it cold as well.  You can use either cannellini beans or garbanzo.  The cannellini beans make for a looser dip.
