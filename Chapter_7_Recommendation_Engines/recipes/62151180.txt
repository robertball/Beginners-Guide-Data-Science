Title:
Beaver Tails Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 egg
3/4 tsp salt
3 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp butter
3 1/4 cups all purpose flour
3/4 tsp yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in order into bread maker.
Select dough cycle.
Remove dough to a lightly floured surface;
cover with large bowl and rest x 10-15 mins.
Divide dough into 10 pieces.
Gently stretch into 8x6 inch ovals(i.e. beaver tail
shape)
Deep fry for 30-45 seconds turning once until Lightly
browned(*make sure oil is NOT too hot or dough will be
tough.)
Immediately roll into cinnamon/sugar topping.

Submitter: 
ERIN
Description: 
This recipe is passed down from my Nana who was famous for her doughnuts. This is one she would make us on a cold winters day after skating all day on the outdoor rink.It is a popular treat when skating on the Rideau Canal in Ottawa,Canada.
