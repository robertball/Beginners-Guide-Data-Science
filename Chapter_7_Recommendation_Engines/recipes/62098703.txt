Title:
Sesame Ginger Chicken with Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle Newman's Own sesame and ginger salad dressing
4 pieces boneless skinless chicken breast
1 bag white rice

Directions:
Place chicken in plastic bag and cover with dressing
Meanwhile cook the rice in a rice cooker or per bag's instructions
Place in refrigerator for aprox. 30 min.
Cook the chicken and marinade in a skillet over medium heat using the marinade to keep the chicken from sticking to the pan
Serve over rice

Submitter:

Description: 
Sumptious chicken soaked in a sesame ginger marinade, would you believe its a salad dressing?
