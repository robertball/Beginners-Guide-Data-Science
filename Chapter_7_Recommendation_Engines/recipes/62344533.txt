Title:
Greek Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup 	Feta Cheese
1/3 cup 	Parmesan Cheese
8 oz	        Cream Cheese
1 Tablespoon	Sun Dried Tomato Pesto

Directions:
In food processor ~ blend until all ingredients are well incorporated.
For best flavor, let sit over night.

Submitter: 
CCANDYLLAND
Description: 
This delicious dip was sold at a local meat market where I used to live.  Since I no longer live in that area, I decided to try and duplicate the taste. (I have substituted Italian 4 cheese blend for the Parmesan with success.)
