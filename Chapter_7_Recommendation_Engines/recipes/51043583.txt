Title:
Low-Carb Zuppa Toscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Ground Sausage
1 large onion (approx. 3/4 cup)
8 cups chicken broth
4 cups Kale - washed, dried, & shredded
6 slices of cooked bacon crumbled
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 head or frozen bag of cauliflower
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Cook sausage and onions together until sausage is well done, drain well.  Add chicken broth, bacon, kale, garlic, and cauliflower.  Simmer for about 15 minutes until cauliflower is tender.  Add heavy cream and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.

Low-carb version of a delicious creamy sausage soup.
