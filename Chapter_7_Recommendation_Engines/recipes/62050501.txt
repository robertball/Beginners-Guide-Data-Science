Title:
Hard Pink Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can  frozen pink lemonade concentrate
1/2  large bottle  chilled soda water
1 1/2 cups gin
1 1/2 cups cranberry cocktail

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together.  Serve with lots of ice and garnish with lemons slices or strawberries.

Submitter: 
GFRIDAY
Description: 
This is a wonderful summertime drink.  Perfect for cookouts or poolside.
