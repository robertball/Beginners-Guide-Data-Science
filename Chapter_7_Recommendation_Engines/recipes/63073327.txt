Title:
Cranberry Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz. Package cherry jello
1 1/2 Cups boiling water
2 14 oz. Cans Whole Cranberry Sauce
2 Oranges cut up in pieces
1 Cup diced apple
1 Cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Dissolve jello in boiling water.
Add cranberry sauce and oranges.
Chill until jello is the consistency of egg whites (about 45 minutes).
Then mix in apple and walnuts.
Pour jello into a mold or serving dish.
Chill another 2 hours, or until jello is firm.
