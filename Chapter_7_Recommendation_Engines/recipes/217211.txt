Title:
Harvey Wallbanger Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups ice
2 fluid ounces vodka
5 fluid ounces freshly squeezed orange juice
1 tablespoon Galliano® liqueur

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour in vodka and orange juice. Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass. Pour a floater of Galliano(R) over the top. Serve with a slice of orange.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
If you can make a screwdriver, then you're one small step away from a Wallbanger. Just float a little Galliano® (an herbal Italian liqueur) over the top and you're there.
