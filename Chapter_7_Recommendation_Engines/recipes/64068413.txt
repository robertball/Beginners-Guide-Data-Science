Title:
Big Ray's Slow and Saucy Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131718.jpg
Ingredients:
6 bone-in country style pork ribs, cut in half
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 Tbl canola oil
1 large onion, cut into rings
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup beer
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp of rice wine vinegar
1 tsp liquid smoke

Directions:
Sprinkle pork chops with garlic powder, salt and pepper. In a large skillet, brown chops in oil on both sides; drain.
In a small bowl, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, beer, vinegar and liquid smoke. Pour half of the sauce into slow cooker. Top with pork chops lay onion rings on top then remaining sauce. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours or until meat is tender.
