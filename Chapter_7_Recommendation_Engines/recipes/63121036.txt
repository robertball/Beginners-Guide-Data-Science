Title:
Mac and Cheese with Crab and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1 lb box macaroni elbow noodles
2 cups whole milk
2/3 of a large block Velveeta cheese
8 ounces sharp cheddar cheese-shredded
8 ounces monterey jack cheese-shredded
8 ounces swiss cheese-shredded
6 ounces fontina cheese-shredded
1 pound crab meat
1 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
1.  Cook noodles to al dente according to package directions.  Drain, and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process.
2.  In a large saucepan combine the milk, cheeses, and pepper.  Cook over low heat stirring frequently until the cheese mixture is smooth and creamy.
3.  Fold the crab meat and bacon into the cheese mixture.
4.  In a large mixing bowl, combine the noodles and cheese sauce, gently folding to mix thoroughly while not breaking up the crab meat.
5.  You can either serve as is, or you can do one of the following:  transfer to a baking dish and bake at 350 degrees for 20-30 minutes or until the top is browned and bubbly; or refrigerate overnight and then cook in a crockpot the next day on low for 4 hours or until heated through.

Submitter: 
JRS_cook
Description: 
This is an ultra rich and delicious 'upscale' mac and cheese recipe.  By combining 5 different cheese, you get an ooey gooey comfort food meal with a twist.
