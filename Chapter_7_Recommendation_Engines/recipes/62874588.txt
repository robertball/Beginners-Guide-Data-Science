Title:
Sweet Potato Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups drained and mashed Sweet Potatoes
1 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup Milk
2 Eggs
1 Stick Margarine
1 T. Vanilla
Topping:
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Flour
1/2 Stick Margarine

Directions:
Combine all main ingredients, put in dish. Mix topping ingredients and put on top. Bake for 30-40 minutes at 400 degrees.
