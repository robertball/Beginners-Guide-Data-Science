Title:
Sausage Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Jimmy Dean regular sausage
2 - 1/2 cps Bisquick
2/3 lb. sharp cedar cheese, grated

Directions:
Mix all together.  Roll into balls and place on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.

Karen Fraley
