Title:
Jess's Lone Star Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/423692.jpg
Ingredients:
*Can of corn drained
*Green/Red pepper i use half of each cut to small bean size slices
*2 cans of Rotel to your personal favor strength I go with mild for a group
*Can of black bean drained
*Can of Black Eyed Peas drained
*Can of red kidney beans (optional)
*Bottle of Italian dressing... I like to use the light
*Red Onion adds great flavor (optional)

Directions:
Mix ingredients drain beans and allow to marinate over night for the best flavor. Served chilled with or with out tortilla chips, on top of a salad or even on top of a hamburger, or brat. ENJOY!
