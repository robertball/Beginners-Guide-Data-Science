Title:
Southern Style Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  14.5 oz cans of cut green beans (do not drain liquid)
3 slices bacon-diced
1/2 onion-diced
1 clove garlic
1 tsp oil
1 tsp kosher salt
Pepper to taste
1 Tbsp sugar

Directions:
In a saucepan, Saute diced up bacon and in 1 tsp oil just until it starts giving out its fat.  Then add diced onion and saute until soft, add garlic and saute 30 seconds more.
Add salt, pepper and sugar and saute another 30 seconds.  Then add green beans including the water in the can.  Bring to a boil and turn down to a simmer and simmer for 30 minutes or until there is only 1/3 of the liquid left.
Serve.....
