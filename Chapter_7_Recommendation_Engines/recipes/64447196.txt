Title:
Tinfoil Dinners Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 medium potato shredied
1 or 2 large carrots shredded
1 onion finely diced
salt
pepper
season salt
garlic powder

Directions:
Season the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic, and season salt by kneading it into the meat.  Separate into 4 - 1/4 lb. patties.
Make the patties thick with a depression in the center to hold the veggies.
Place the pattie on a section of tinfoil.  Put your desired amount of veggies on the pattie in the depression.
Fold the tinfoil over the meat and veggies so it is well sealed.
Cook in campfire for 25 - 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
My great-grandfather passed this recipe down through the family and it's been included as a camping must since I was a young child.
