Title:
Sauteed Brussels Sprouts with Walnuts and Lemon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c chopped walnuts
1 1/2 lb Brussels sprouts
2 T EVOO
zest and juice of a lemon
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Toast walnuts for 8-10 min.
Shred the sprouts into thin slices with food processor.
Heat oil in large skillet, add sprouts and lemon zest. Cook about 5 min. Season and add lemon juice and walnuts.
