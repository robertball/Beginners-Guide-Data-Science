Title:
Asparagus and Ham Fritatta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	1 bunch asparagus, cut into 2 inch pieces and steamed, but still crunchy
•	5 eggs
•	8 oz. heavy cream
•	Juice of half a lemon
•	12-16 oz diced ham
•	S & P
•	2 T. butter
•	8 oz. Manchego cheese grated

Directions:
1.	Preheat oven to 350.
2.	Combine eggs, cream, lemon and ham in a mixing bowl and whisk together.  Add salt and pepper.
3.	In a large oven proof sauté pan, melt butter and pour in the egg mixture.
4.	Add asparagus to the pan, letting them sink in a bit.
5.	Cook for about 2 minutes over medium heat.
6.	Sprinkle with Manchego.
7.	Transfer pan to the oven for 20-25 minutes.  Check center with knife, should come out clean.
8.      Cut into 6 pieces.

Submitter: 
Laura Moody
Description: 
I love frittatas!  This recipe is great because you can swap out any meat, veggie or cheese and it's still yummy.
