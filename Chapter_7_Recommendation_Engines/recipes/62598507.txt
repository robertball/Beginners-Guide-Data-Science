Title:
slow cook chuck roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
any size chuck roast (depends on how many you are cooking for)
a-1 steak sauce
lipton beefy onion soup (box)
cream of celery or any cream soup
salt
pepper

Directions:
Put in chuck roast then we layer by adding one packet of the lipton soup one can of the cream of celery about 2 tablespoons a-1. Salt and pepper. You can't overcook this. I usually put it on for 5 to 6 hours. When you put a fork in the roast it should shred apart.

Submitter: 
Nikki
