Title:
Hamburger and Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1361683.jpg
Ingredients:
1	pound ground beef
1	large onion chopped (1 cup)
½	cup uncooked minute rice
½	teaspoon salt
½	teaspoon pepper
1	can condensed tomato soup, or 1 ¼ cups home made smooth tomato soup.
¼	cup water
4	cups shredded  green cabbage
Sour cream, if desired
Chopped chives, if desired

Directions:
1.	Heat oven to 400 F.
2.	  Cook beef, and onion in 10 inch skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until beef is brown; drain fat.  Stir in rice, salt, pepper, soup and water.
3.	Place cabbage in un-greased 2 quart casserole.  Spoon beef mixture over cabbage.
4.	Cover and bake about 45 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Top with sour cream and chives.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
great Casserole for using up leftover cabbage, burger or just to keep the budget on track.  It's so Quick and easy it makes this dish a go to.
