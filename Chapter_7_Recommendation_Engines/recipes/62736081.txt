Title:
Spanish Potato Tortilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/627034.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable (or chicken) broth
1/2 cup olive oil
5 potatoes, either sliced thin or diced
1 onion, diced
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 zucchini, chopped
4 large eggs
1 tsp parsley
salt and pepper

Directions:
Fry potatoes 25 minutes in vegetable broth in 10 inch fry pan. Transfer to skimmer.
Add oil, zucchini, onions and garlic to pan. Fry for 10 minutes. Transfer to skimmer.
Wipe out pan. Beat eggs and add spices.
Heat skillet to medium high. Add 1 TB oil. Add everything. Cook 5 minute and lower temperature to medium low. Cook 8-10 minutes. Flip. Cook 5-6 minutes
Let cool for 10 minutes.
Serve with salad, lettuce, and tomato.
