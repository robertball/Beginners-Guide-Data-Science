Title:
Geman Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large potatoes or equivalent
1 medium onion finely chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley or 1 Tbsp dried parsley - can sub for coriander/cilantro
1/4 cup of virgin olive oil
2-3 Tbsp of vinegar to taste
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes till soft but still firm. Let cool and peel.  Slice potatoes into a bowl.  Add other ingredients and mix all together.  Season to taste.
You may add your favorite herbs to this recipe to add your own special touch.

Submitter: 
Lisa Hayes
Description: 
Delicious light potato salad.  Low Cholesterol levels.
