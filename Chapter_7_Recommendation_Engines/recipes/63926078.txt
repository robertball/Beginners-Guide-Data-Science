Title:
Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lbs. of plum tomatoes
1 can of water (1:1 water to tomato ratio)
1 stalk of celery left whole
1 onion peeled and studded with whole cloves and  3 bay leaves
1/2 cup sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cup flour
1 stick of butter - softened
1 cup of milk or cream

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large pot.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for about 30 minutes.
Remove celery stalk and onion.
Add sugar, salt and pepper.
Blend mixture with an immersion blender until smooth.
Mix butter and flour until smooth then whisk into soup a third at a time.
Increase heat and bring back to a boil and cook for 1 minute or until thickened.
Remove from heat and add milk or cream.
If necessary, return to low heat and warm through.
