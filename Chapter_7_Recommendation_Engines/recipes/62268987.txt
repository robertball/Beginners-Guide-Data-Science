Title:
Standard Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
2 1/2 tablespoons milk
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon extract flavor (use vanilla, almond, pepermint etc...)
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon corn syrup

Directions:
Melt the butter and add to rest of ingredients.  Mix until creamy.
Icing will set in about 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Crystal Halm
Description: 
Great on bunt cakes or cookies. Change the extract flavor to match the cake or cookies you are making. Glaze will set in about 20 minutes and will have a nice shine to it.Modified from: Vanilla Glaze
