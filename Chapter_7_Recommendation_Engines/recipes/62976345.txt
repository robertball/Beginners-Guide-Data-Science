Title:
Wheatgerm Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cidar vinegar
1 Tbsp.Dijon mustard
1 Tbsp.tarragon
5 Tbsp.wheat germ oil
2 Tbsp.wheat germ
salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp.honey

Directions:
Mix ingredients well-store in the fridge.
Drizzle on your favorite salad.
