Title:
Peanut Butter-Toffee Cheesecake Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (19.5 oz)Chocolate Fudge Brownie Mix
1/2 c. Crisco Vegetable Oil
1/4 c. water
2 eggs
1 - 8 oz. package cream cheese
1 - 14 oz. can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c. creamy peanut butter
1 - 8 oz. bag Heath toffee bits
1 c. milk chocolate baking chips
3 tbsp. whipping cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Spray 9x13 pan with nonstick spray.
Mix brownie mix, oil, water and eggs, spread in pan, set aside.
In bowl beat cream cheese until fluffy, add milk and peanut butter.  Beat until smooth.  Stir in 1 cup of the toffee bits.  Spoon over batter in pan evenly.
Bake 30-40 until cheesecake is set and sides are golden brown.  Cool for 30 minutes, then refrigerate for 40 mins.
In microwave melt choc chips and cream until chips are melted.  Spread over cheesecake layer.  Sprinkle with remaining toffee bits.  Cool complete for about 1 hour.
