Title:
Sour cream Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs of potatoes (Yukon gold works great)
1 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled
One red Onion diced
2 or 3 carrots diced
2 celery stalks diced including leaf tops
1 small red or green pepper (optional)
Salt & peper to taste
16 oz Sour cream

Directions:
boil potatoes until fork tender, cool, peel, and dice
add remainder of ingredients and toss
Chill and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Merrianne
Description: 
For those who don't care for mayonaise and those who like sour cream.
