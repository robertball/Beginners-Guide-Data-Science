Title:
Baked Ham and Cheese Party Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 dozen party (mini) buns or mini king hawaiian rolls
1 lb. ham slices
1 lb. swiss cheese slices
3/4 cup melted butter
1 1/2 tablespoon of poppy seeds
1 1/2 teaspoon of worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon of dried onions
1 1/2 tablespoon of dijon mustard

Directions:
Cut rolls in half if not already cut and/or remove tops, put bottoms in greased 9 x 13 pan. Mix butter, poppy seeds, worcestershire sauce, dried onion and mustard. Layer ham and cheese on rolls starting and ending with ham. Replace tops. Pour mixture over rolls and bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
LisaT
Description: 
These small, delicous sandwiches are perfect for any party. They are so good that even the pickiest of eaters will eat these.
