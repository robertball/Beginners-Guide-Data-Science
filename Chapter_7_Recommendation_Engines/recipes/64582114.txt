Title:
Salmon Croquette Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3661202.jpg
Ingredients:
-2 cans of salmon
-1 sleeve of saltine or ritz crackers
-1T black pepper
-2T yellow mustard
-2 eggs

Directions:
-Pour about 1/4" worth of vegetable oil in skillet and heat up on medium high heat.
-Put salmon in bowl and remove bones.
-Add eggs, pepper, crackers, and mustard and then mix well.
-Shape into patties and then place in oil.
-Fry each side about 2-2 1/2 minutes.
