Title:
Healthy Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups oats,quick or old-fashioned
3  cups unsweetened coconut
1  cup  each of slivered almonds, sunflower nuts, pecans, peanuts, walnuts, sesame seeds,.......
1/2 cup sucanat or date sugar, white or brown sugar
1/2 Tbsp.  almond or vanilla extract
1/2 Tbsp.  lemon or orange extract
2-3 cups dried fruit

Directions:
Dump all ingredients in a large bowl. Stir to mix well.  Bake on cookie sheets in a 170 degree oven overnight or for 8-10 hours.

Submitter: 
ARKBUCKS
Description: 
This tasty granola is great because it doesn't have any oil, making it lower in fat than most granolas.    You can change it to fit your tastes.
