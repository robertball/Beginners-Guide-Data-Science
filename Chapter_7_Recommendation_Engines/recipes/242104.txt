Title:
Dry Rub for Ribs or Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2377072.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons paprika
1 1/2 tablespoons ground black pepper
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
Whisk brown sugar, paprika, black pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and red pepper flakes together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Love to cook
Description: 
Use this delicious rub on ribs or chicken. Rub into ribs or chicken and refrigerate overnight.
