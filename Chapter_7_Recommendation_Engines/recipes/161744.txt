Title:
'Secret' Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/752861.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ketchup
1 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup diced onion
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Combine the ketchup, vegetable oil, mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, onion, salt, and pepper in a blender; blend until smooth. Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
My mom has made this dressing forever. It's been a family favorite right next to homemade ranch dressing.
