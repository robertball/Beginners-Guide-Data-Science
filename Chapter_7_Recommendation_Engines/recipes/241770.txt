Title:
Baked Zucchini Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2176927.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded zucchini
3 cups RAGÚ® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
4 basil leaves spritzed with olive oil (optional)
Salt and black pepper to taste
4 small canning jars or ramekins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Mix shredded zucchini with the Ragu(R) Old World Style(R) Traditional Sauce until coated. Add salt and pepper as desired. Portion into individual 1 1/2-cup Mason jars or small ramekins. Add 1/4 cup of the shredded cheese on top of each. Top with a basil leaf spritzed with olive oil, if desired.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until cheese is lightly browned. Let cool until individual containers have cooled enough to handle. Serve!

Submitter: 
RAGÚ®
Description: 
Shredded zucchini and Ragu® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce are topped with shredded mozzarella cheese and baked in individual serving dishes for an easy weeknight side dish.
