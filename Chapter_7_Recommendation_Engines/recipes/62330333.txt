Title:
Flubber Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Solution A
1 1/2 c warm water
16 oz white glue [like Elmers]
food coloring
Solution B
4 tsp Borax
1 1/3 c warm water

Directions:
Stir solution A in a bowl.
In a separate bowl stir solution B throughly.
Pour solution A into solution B.
Lift out the flubber and kneed air bubbles.
Discard solution B.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
It works like silly putty and if it gets on the carpet you can pick it right off.  Does not rub in like play dough.
