Title:
Chocolate chip cheesecake bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 40 ounce tub of prepared cookie dough
2- 8 ounce packages of softened cream cheese
1- cup of sugar
2- eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cut cookie dough in half. Press half of the dough in the bottom of a greased 9x13 inch pan. In a mixing bowl, mix the cream cheese, sugar and eggs. Spread over dough. Crumble remaining dough over cream cheese mixure. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.

Description: 
A super easy, but rich bar for any occassion.
