Title:
Salmon fillets in a honey mustard glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Dijon Country Mustard
1/4 Cup Honey
1 tablespoons Mayonnaise
1/2 tablespoon lemon Juice
Old Bay Seasoning
1 lb of Salmon (Skin & Bones removed)
Foil

Directions:
1st prep sauce- Mix together Dijon Country Mustard, honey, mayo & lemon juice. Let stand for 20 min to allow for a good marrying of flavors. Then separate about a 1/4 cup of sauce to marinate Salmon &  to prevent cross contamination. Refridgerate remaining sauce for use with side dishes or for later use. Preheat Oven to 325 degrees. Clean Salmon, Cut into two fillets, season salmon with old bay to your liking. Place each fillet on its own sheet of foil. Brush Salmon with Honey Mustard sauce reserved for marination. Make Boats of foil for steaming Salmon and close foil around fish. Place in baking dish and put in oven for 30- 45 min or until fish flakes nicely. Remove salmon from foil and place on dinner plate. Enjoy!
