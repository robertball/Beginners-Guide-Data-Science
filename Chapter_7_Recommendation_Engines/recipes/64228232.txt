Title:
Pecan Cream Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lg. pkgs. Cream Cheese
8 1/2 oz. can crushed pineapple (drained)
1/4 c. finely chopped green pepper
2 Tbsp. finely chopped green onions
1 1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
2 c. finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix ingredients and chill.
Then form into ball or roll.
After forming into desired shape, roll in 2 c. finely chopped pecans.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description: 
Delicious cheese ball to serve with crackers.
