Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-cooked eggs
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon sugar
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
Paprika

Directions:
Slice eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks and set whites aside. In a small bowl, mash yolks with a fork. Add the mayonnaise, sugar, pickles, mustard and salt; mix well. Stuff or pipe into egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.

Description: 
This recipe comes from the Durbin Inn, a well-known restaurant in Rushville, Indiana from the 1920s until it closed in the late '70s. The eggs are delicious, and it's easy to make more for larger gatherings. -Margaret Sanders, Indianapolis, IndianaModified from: Deviled Eggs
