Title:
Strawberry Ameretto Pastries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pkg. frozen puff pastry sheets, thawed
1/2 cup sliced almonds, divided
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 8oz. sour cream
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/4 tsp. almond extract
1 1/2 cup frozen whip cream, thawed
12 large strawberries, sliced thin
1 orange

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Unfold pastry dough onto large stone or cookie sheet. Finely chop half the almonds. Combine almonds & sugar in prep bowl; sprinkle evenly over surface of dough; press lightly.
Using a pizza cutter, cut dough lengthwise into 3 strips; cut strips crosswise into 4 squares; total of 12 squares. Separate squares evenly over surface. Bake 16-18 mins. or until puffed & golden brown. Remove from pan to cooling rack; cool completely
Whisk sour cream, powdered sugar, & almond extract in bowl. Fold in whip cream. Split open each pastry square; arrange half of the strawberries into each bottom shell. Top each with about 2 heaping Tbsp. of whip cream filling & sprinkle with almonds. Top with remaining strawberries; zest orange over strawberries using a microplane grater.
Place tops of pastry shells over each one. Serve immediately.
