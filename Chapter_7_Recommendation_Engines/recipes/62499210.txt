Title:
holiday chinese doughnut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/498106.jpg
Ingredients:
oil for frying
1 tube refrigerated biscuits
1 cup sugar
2-3 Tbs ground cinnamon, to taste
1-2 Tbs ground nutmeg, to taste

Directions:
1.) Heat oil in a deep fryer.
2.) Mix sugar with cinnamon and nutmeg in a small bowl, adding more or less spices per your tastes.
3.) Drop 1-2 biscuits at a time into the deep fryer. Cook about 2 minutes or until golden brown. Carefully turn the biscuit over to cook the other side.
4.) Remove biscuit from oil. Let dry on paper toweling or on a wire rack for just a minute.
5.) Toss biscuit in the sugar-spice mixture until biscuit is coated. Repeat with the rest of the biscuits.

Submitter: 
tiffany.renee
Description: 
Adding nutmeg to the traditional buffet-style Chinese doughnut gives this easy desert a sweet and spicy aroma that tastes like Christmas!
