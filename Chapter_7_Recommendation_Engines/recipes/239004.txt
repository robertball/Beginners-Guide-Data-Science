Title:
Evan's Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1487476.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup paprika
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup dark brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon sea salt
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon iodized salt
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Whisk paprika, light brown sugar, dark brown sugar, white sugar, sea salt, ground black pepper, garlic powder, iodized salt, onion powder, cumin, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl until smooth.

Submitter: 
Evan
Description: 
Just a simple, smoky sweet rub I created one day. If you're looking for more bite then add a bit more cayenne pepper. Works great for oven-to-grill ribs! Apply to your ribs of choice and enjoy!
