Title:
Peanut Butter Ice Cream Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed milk
1/2 cup butter or margarine (1 stick)
8 Tbsp of Creamy Peanut Butter
1/2 tsp of Vanilla

Directions:
Heat butter and condensed milk until butter is fully melted.  Turn off heat, add peanut butter and vanilla.
