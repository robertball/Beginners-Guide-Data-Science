Title:
Perfectly Potato Cheddar Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Medium Potatoes
2 1/2 - 3 c. water
Perfectly POtato Cheddar Soup
Salt n pepper to taste

Directions:
Layer potatoes in greased casserole dish.  Mix water and soup and pour over potatoes.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour until tender.
