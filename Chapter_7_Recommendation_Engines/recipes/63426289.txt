Title:
Indian Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927244.jpg
Ingredients:
2lbs Whole Chicken cut into small pieces on the bone
3 tbs Oil
1 tsp Cumin seeds
2 Serrano  chilies diced
1 1/2 inch Cinnamon stick
2 Onions, finely chopped
3 Tomatoes blanched and  purred
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1 1/2  tsp red  chilly powder
4 Black cardamoms
3 Bay leaves
2 Cloves
1 tbs Ginger-Garlic paste
1 tbs Coriander powder
Fresh cilantro leaves for garnishing
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil in a pan. Add bay leaves, cinnamon, black cardamom cloves, cumin seeds and saute for a minute.
Add onions and saute lightly until a little brown. Turn off the heat.
Transfer this mixture into a blender and grind into a fine paste with a little water. Transfer this paste in the pan and saute on a low heat. Add green chilies and tomato puree and saute
Add ginger-garlic paste and saute, stirring continuously. Add red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder and saute. Add chicken and saute for 3-4 minutes.
Add salt and mix well. Add 700 ml water, cover and cook for 10-15 minutes.
Garnish with coriander leaves and serve
