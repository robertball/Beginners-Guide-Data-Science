Title:
Snicker Doodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/528792.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons white sugar
1 tablespoon cinnamon
2 3/4 - 3 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons cream of tartar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup softened butter, or butter flavored shortening.
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar (light or dark)
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs

Directions:
Pre-heat oven 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
In a small bowl, mix the white sugar and cinnamon together. (This is what you will be rolling the dough in.)
In a medium sized bowl mix flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, cinnamon and salt.
In another bowl, cream the butter and sugars. Add vanilla and eggs, mix till combined.
Slowly add the flour mixture to the butter and sugar mixture, just till combined.
With cookie scooper, scoop out dough balls and roll in the sugar and cinnamon mixture, place on un-greased cookie sheet.
