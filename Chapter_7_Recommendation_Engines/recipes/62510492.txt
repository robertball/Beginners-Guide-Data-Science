Title:
Homemade Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507215.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/4 cup chili powder
2 cups vegetable broth
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
salt

Directions:
In a medium saucepan heat oil, add flour and cook for 1 minute while stirring. Add chili powder and cook for 30 seconds while stirring.
Stir in broth, tomato paste, oregano, cumin, and salt to taste. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and cook for 15 minutes. Use in your enchilada favorite recipe.

Submitter: 
Cupcake Princess
Description: 
I love to make this easy enchilada sauce, it is so worth making this instead of buying a can of enchilada sauce. Perfect sauce to use for making enchiladas or smootherd burritos.
