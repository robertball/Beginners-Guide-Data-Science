Title:
Baked Vidalia Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Vidalia Onion Chopped ( does not have to be finely chopped)
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese or 4 0z. shredded Swiss. (I prefer Swiss)
1 cup real mayonnaise
1 clove garlic chopped
hot pepper sauce to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and bake.
375 degrees for 25 minutes or until golden brown
Serve with favorite crackers or dippers.
