Title:
Roasted Potatoes with Onions And Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Yukon Gold potatos, peeled and quartered.
1 onion coarsley sliced
1 red pepper sliced
1 green pepper sliced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F
In large glass bowl toss, potatoes, onion, peppers, garlic powder, oregano ,paprika, salt and pepper with olive oil to coat all mixture.
in 9x 13 inch baking pan sprayed with cooking spray spoon in mixture in an even layer.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 -50 minutes till potatoes are tender and nicely browned. Stiring mixture every 10-15 min. If it sticks to pan add more olive oil.
