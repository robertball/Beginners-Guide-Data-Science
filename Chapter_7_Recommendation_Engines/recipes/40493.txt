Title:
Stouffer's Baked French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979652.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package STOUFFER'S® frozen Harvest Apples, defrosted
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1/3 cup packed dark brown sugar
3 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
6 slices bread

Directions:
MELT butter in 9 x 13-inch baking pan. Add brown sugar and Harvest Apples; stir well.
WHISK eggs, milk and vanilla extract in medium bowl until well combined.  Dip bread into egg mixture; place on top of apples.
BAKE in preheated 350 degree F. oven for 35 to 40 minutes or until bread centers are firm.

Submitter: 
Stouffer's®
Description: 
Thick and delicious French toast is excellent for a Sunday brunch.
