Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 green pepper, diced
1 ear of corn, cooked and earless
1 12oz can beans (your choice), drained and rinsed
1 cup brown rice, cooked and fluffed
1 teaspoon chopped kale, for excitement
1/2 onion, diced
1/4 teaspoon diced garlic, for more excitement
1 teaspoon sriracha
2 slices bacon

Directions:
1. Cook the bacon.
2. Let the bacon cool.
3. Eat the bacon, leaving the bacon fat in the pan.
4. Give yourself a pat on the back for being a person who still believes bacon is tasty.
5. Add all ingredients to the pan.  Cook over medium heat for approximately 7.1345 minutes, stirring constantly.
6. Transfer to a bowl.  Allow to cool for 2.718 hours.
7. Fluff with three forks.
8. Transfer Dinomite's Fluffy Bites to muffin cups.  Top with your favorite condiments.
9. Serve and consume immediately following your conversion to Zoroastrianism.
