Title:
Granny Skeet's Applesauce Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 cup shortening
2 eggs
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 cups applesauce
3 cups flour
2 teaspoons ground cloves
2 teaspoons nutmeg
2 teaspoons cinnamon
½ raisins
½ cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cream together shorting and sugar.  Add eggs.  Dissolve baking soda in applesauce and add.
In another bowl mix flour, cloves, nutmeg and cinnamon. Add to applesauce mixture.
Add raisins and walnuts.
Bake at 350 degrees until cake tests done when a toothpick is inserted.
Frosting when cooled.

Submitter: 
SCOOBYVC
Description: 
When this cake bakes it will fill your house with the most amazing smell.  It always takes me back to the apartment my Granny lived in and how wonderful this cake tasted.
