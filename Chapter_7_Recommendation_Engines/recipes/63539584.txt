Title:
Chicken, Rice, and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
fresh chicken breasts
3/4 cup of uncooked rice
1 cup skim milk
1 bag frozen broccoli
1 can of fat-free cream of chicken soup
mixed spices (i.e., oregano, basil, thyme, onion salt)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Mix ingredients into casserole dish and cover.
Bake in over for 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
The easy and healthy dinner is a great choice for a cold winter evening.
