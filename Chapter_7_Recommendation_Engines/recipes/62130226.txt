Title:
Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
1 1/4 pounds skinned, boned chicken breast
4 garlic cloves. minced
1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
1 (28-ounce) can whole tomatoes drained and chopped
5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
2 cups milk
2 eggs, lightly beaten, or 6 tablespoons egg substitute
1 1/2 cup (6 ounces) cheddar cheese
6 (6-inch) tortillas, cut in half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
Coat a large non-stick skillet with cooking spray
place over medium heat until hot.
Add chicken
cook 6 minutes on each side or until done.
Remove chicken from pan and cool.
Shred chicken with forks.
Recoat pan with cooking sprayand place over medium heat. Add garlic and saute' 5 minutes or until tender.
Add shredded chicken, red pepper, and tomatoes and cook 10 minutes.
Remove from heat.
Combine flour, salt, cumin, and coriander in a medium saucepan.
Gradually add milk, stirring with whisk until blended. Place over medium heat; cook 7 minutes or until thick, stirring constantly.
Gradually add hot milk mixture to egg whites, stiring constantly with a whisk.
Spread 1/2 cup white sauce in bottom of a 2 1/2-quart round casserole or souffle' dish coated with cooking spray. Arrange 4 tortilla halves over sauce; top with 2 cups chicken mixture, 1/2 cup white sauce, and 1/2 cup cheese. Repeat layers twice, ending with sauce.
Set remaining 1/2 cheese mixture aside.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees farenheit for 40 minutes or until hot.
Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
Bake an additional 5 minutes.
Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
