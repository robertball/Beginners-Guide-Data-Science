Title:
Big Ray's Vegatable Primavera Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1127948.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. spaghetti, uncooked
3 cups small broccoli florets (or 12 oz. frozen)
3/4 lb. baby carrots, cut into thin slices
1 can (4 oz) mushrooms, bits & pieces
4 oz (1/2 of 8-oz. pkg.) cream cheese, room temperature cubed
1 cup  milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1/3 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp  garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp Italian seasoning

Directions:
Cook spaghetti in large saucepan as directed on package, adding vegetables to the boiling water the last 3 min.
Drain spaghetti mixture; return to pan.  Add additional ingredients and mix until cream cheese is completely melted and mixture is well blended.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
With this creamy Alfredo sauce made with cream cheese and sour cream, you can add whatever vegetables your family likes.
