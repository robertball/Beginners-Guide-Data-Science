Title:
Simple Avocado Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1160018.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 ripe avocado, peeled and pitted
1 banana, cut into chunks (optional)
3 tablespoons honey

Directions:
Blend milk, avocado, banana, and honey together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sugar High
Description: 
For people who love avocado. This yummy milkshake is very creamy and smooth.
