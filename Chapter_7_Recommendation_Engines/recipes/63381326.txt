Title:
Sam's easy beef stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz egg noodles
3 Tblsp olive oil
1 lb sirloin steak thinly sliced (for easy cutting, put the steak in the freezer for 20 minutes before cutting)
Salt and pepper to taste
1 medium onion thinly sliced
1 green bell pepper thinly sliced
1 lb button mushrooms sliced
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup beef broth (I use low sodium organic by Imagine, lowest sodium content!!)
1/2 cup Sour cream
1 Tblsp dijon mustard
1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce
Garnish with fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Cook the egg noodles.  Heat skillet on medium/high heat, add 1 tblsp oil (I prefer olive).  season steak with salt and pepper, cook until browned, 1-2 minutes per side, then, set the meat to the side.  Heat the onion, white wine, green pepper, mushrooms add remaining oil cook on medium/high heat for 5-6 minutes, or until onion and pepper are tender.  Add broth, worcestershire sauce, mustard, heat for an additional 4-5 minutes.  Add the beef, sour cream and any remaining ingredients and simmer for 10-15 minutes.  Pour over noodles, garnish and serve!!!
