Title:
Apple Walnut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups mixed greens
1 fuji apple, sliced or diced
1/2 cup walnuts, roughly chopped if desired
4 ounces Cabot's reduced fat cheddar, diced small
1/3 cup Ken's Steakhouse Balsamic Viniagrette

Directions:
Toss together all of the ingredients and serve immediately.

Description: 
Sweet fuji apples, nutty walnuts, creamy cheese and savory viniagrette
