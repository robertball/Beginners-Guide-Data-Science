Title:
Krista's Awesome Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052044.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large cans crushed tomatoes
1/4c olive oil
2-3 cloves garlic
2 tsp dried basil
2 T sugar

Directions:
brown the garlic with the olive oil
Add the crushed tomato (keeping temp low)
add the dried basil and sugar
simmer on low, stirring occasionally, 1-1.5 hours

Submitter: 
Donna Carlson
Description: 
make a lot and freeze it
