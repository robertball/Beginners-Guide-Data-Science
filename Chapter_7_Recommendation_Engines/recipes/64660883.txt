Title:
Machi's Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks of butter
2 TBSP garlic
A bread

Directions:
Pre Heat Oven to 350
Cut your bread
Put 2 sticks of butter into a pan along with 2 TBSP of garlic
Let the butter melt and boil and bubble
Remove the butter from the stove and pore it into a shallow dish
But the bread into the shallow dish
Cover the bread in tin foil
But the bread into the oven for 45 minutes
Enjoy!
