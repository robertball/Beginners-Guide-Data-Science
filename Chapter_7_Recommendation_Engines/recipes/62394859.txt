Title:
Red Curry Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/654331.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup mayonnaise (I use Hellmann'sÂ® Light)
2 teaspoons Thai red curry paste
Zest of 1 lime
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
In small bowl, combine all ingredients with a whisk until smooth and well mixed. Cover and refrigerate until serving.
