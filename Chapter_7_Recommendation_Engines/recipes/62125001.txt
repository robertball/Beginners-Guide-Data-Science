Title:
Extra Spicy Slow Cooker Cincinnati Style Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ancho chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 pounds extra lean ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1 15 oz can  tomato sauce
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes, drained and rinsed
4 fresh jalapenos, diced
1 15 oz can light red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 1/2 cups water
4 bay leaves
8 oz extra thick spaghetti (DeCecco Perciatelli), cooked
shredded Cheddar cheese, to taste
Hot pepper sauce, to taste

Directions:
Mix all spices together in a bowl and set aside.
In large pan, cook meat and onions.
Drain fat from mixture and place into slow cooker.
Pour spice mix, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, water, kidney beans, jalapenos and bay leaves on top of meat mixture.
Cook on low for 5-6 hours.
Serve on top of cooked spaghetti and top with cheddar cheese and hot pepper sauce.
