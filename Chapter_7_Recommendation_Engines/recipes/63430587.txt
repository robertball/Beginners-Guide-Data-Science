Title:
Easy Cake Batter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Cake mix (Any flavor! this is the flavoring right here!)
1 Cup Confectioners Sugar
1/2 Cup Butter (1 stick)
1/4 Cup Milk

Directions:
Pour 1 Cup Cake Mix in flavor of your choosing and 1 Cup Sugar into a large microwave safe bowl, stir until blended. Cut Butter into quarters and then add into bowl, Add milk and do not stir!
Microwave two minutes, stir immediately.
Pour into a greased pan and set in fridge for one hour. Cut squares and serve!

Submitter: 
Grace Locke
Description: 
The easiest and most fun recipe for fudge! Use yellow cake mix for a very batter-y taste or experiment with any other flavor! My favorite is Strawberry.
