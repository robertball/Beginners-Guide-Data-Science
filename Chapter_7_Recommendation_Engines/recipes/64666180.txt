Title:
Vietnamese Grilled Pork Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients for grilled pork:
1 lbs of pork belly or pork butt.
3 table spoons of fish sauce.
1 tea spoon of glutamate monosodique.
1 tea spoon of five-spice powder.
1 fresh red chili.
3 lemon grasses.
3 to 5 cloves garlic.
1 tea spoon of sugar.
5 to 7 bamboo skewers.
Ingredients for dipping sauce:
4 table spoons of fish sauce.
4 table spoons of sugar.
1 to 2 tea spoons of glutamate monosodique.
5 to 7 cloves garlic.
1 to 2 fresh red chilies.
half a big lime.
A full mug of water.

Directions:
Direction for grilled pork:
Cut the meat into cubes.
Mince the garlic and lemon grasses.
Mix the meat, garlic, lemon grasses, and the remaining ingredients together. Marinate for 1 to 3 hours.
Skew the marinated meat through the bamboo skewers.
Grill the meat (best on charcoal).
Direction for dipping sauce:
Mince the garlic and squeeze out the lime juice.
Mix all the ingredients (except water) together.
Wait for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
Pour in the water.

Submitter: 
Nguyen Duc Cuong
Description: 
For this dish, we need to prepare two things (1) the grilled pork, and (2) the dipping sauce.
