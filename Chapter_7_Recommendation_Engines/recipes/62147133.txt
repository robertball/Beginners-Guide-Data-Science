Title:
Quick and Easy Potato and Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of potatoes
2 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves.
48 ounces (3 pints)chicken broth, stock, or base
2 teaspoons chopped basil
1/2 package frozen chopped spinach (optional)
1 teaspoon roasted minced garlic
salt
pepper

Directions:
Peel and cube potatoes, place in soup pot.
Cube chicken, place in soup pot.
Add spinach, basil, and garlic.
Salt and pepper to taste (pepper gets strong the longer it cooks)
Add broth, stock, or base.
heat on high until boiling, turn down to med-hi and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occationally.

Submitter: 
Chris Wilkie
Description: 
Easy to make, hot and filling soup simple for any bachelor, student, or busy parent to make.
