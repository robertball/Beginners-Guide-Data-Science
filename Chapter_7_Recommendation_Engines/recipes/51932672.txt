Title:
Grilled Mediterranean Vegetable Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small eggplant, sliced into strips
1 red bell peppers
1 tablespoons olive oil, divided
1 portobello mushrooms, sliced
3 cloves garlic, crushed
3 tablespoons mayonnaise
Herb bread from bread machine cookbook

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Brush eggplant and red bell peppers with 1 tablespoon olive oil; use more if necessary, depending on sizes of vegetables.  Place on a baking sheet and roast in preheated oven.   Roast eggplant until tender, about 25 minutes; roast peppers until blackened.   Remove from oven and set aside to cool.
Meanwhile, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil and saute mushrooms until tender.  Stir crushed garlic into mayonnaise.  Slice enough bread for 2-3 sandwiches.  Spread mayonnaise mixture on one or both slices.
Peel cooled peppers, core and slice.  Arrange eggplant, peppers and mushrooms on bread.  Grill in panini machine.
