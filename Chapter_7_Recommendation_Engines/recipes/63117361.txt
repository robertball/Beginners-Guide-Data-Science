Title:
peanut butter cookies(betty crocker 1972)- note- soft cookies cook 8 minutes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening( half margarine-softened)
1/2 cup pnb
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar(packed)
1 egg
1 1/4 cups sr flour

Directions:
mix thoroughly, shortening,margarine,pnb,sugar,brown sugar and egg-
blend in flour-
cover and chill
heat oven to 375
shape dough into 1 inch balls
place 3 inches apart on lightly greased baking sheet
with fork dipped in flour,flatten in a criss-cross pattern to 2 inches
bake 10-12 minutes- til set but not hard
