Title:
Salpicon ~ Cold Beef Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Lbs Beef Chuck
2 Packages of Mint Herb
1 1/2 Whole Onions, chopped
2 Bunches of Radishes, chopped
4 Lemons
2 Tablespoons Salt

Directions:
Prepare beef chuck: Use a pressure cooker or a small stockpot to cook the beef, cover with water and add salt.
Bring mixture to a boil over high heat, skimming off any foam.
Reduce heat and simmer for about 1 hour.
Once the beef is tender turn off heat and let cool in pot for 30 minutes.
Chop beef coarsely, trimming any fat.
Chop the mint.
Dice the onions.
Chop the radishes.
Combine all ingredients including lemon and salt to taste.
Chill 2-3 hours to let the flavors marinate.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Karen Flores-Perez
Description: 
Each country has their own recipe of Salpicon. Ours is a very simple recipe that my Salvadorean family has been making since I was a child. The Salpicon recipe we use is a tangy cold beef salad with a great mix of fresh flavors. I hope you enjoy it as much as my family does.
