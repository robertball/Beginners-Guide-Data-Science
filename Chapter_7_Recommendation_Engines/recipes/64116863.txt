Title:
Rhubarb Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of rhubarb
1 can of cherry pie filling
1 1/2 cups of brown sugar
1 cup of flour
1 cup of oats
2/3 cup of soft butter
1 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp of nutmet

Directions:
Heat oven to 375
Spray 9x13 pan
Arrange cut rhubarb in the pan
Mix remaining ingredients and sprinkle over rhubarb
Bake until golden brown, serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
This is quick and tasty treat for your bumper crop of rhubarb!
