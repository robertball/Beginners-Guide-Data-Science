Title:
Cayenne Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
1 (1 ounce) package ranch salad dressing mix
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 (16 ounce) package pretzels

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the oil, dressing mix, garlic salt and cayenne.  Divide pretzels between two ungreased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pans. Pour oil mixture over pretzels; stir to coat.
Bake at 200 degrees F for 1-1/4 to 1-1/2 hours or until golden brown, stirring occasionally. Cool completely. Store in an airtight container.
