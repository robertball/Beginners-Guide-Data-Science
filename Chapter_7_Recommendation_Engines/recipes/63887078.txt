Title:
Slowcooker Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1080559.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast cut in strips (or steak strips)
2 bell peppers (seeded and cut in strips)
1/2 red onion (sliced)
3 cloves of garlice (minced)
1 can El Pato
14.5oz can diced tomatoes (drained)
1 Tbs each: cumin, oregano
1 tsp each: chili powder, cinnamon, sea salt, pepper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a crockpot and cook on low for 6-7 hours. If sauce is too thin, add a little kuzu root or corn starch to thicken.
