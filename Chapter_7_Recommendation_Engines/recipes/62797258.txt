Title:
Mexican Chili-Lime Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Four Chicken breasts pre-cooked and shredded
One can of diced tomatoes
3 teaspoons cumin
1/2 large onion diced
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons Goya Recaito "Cilantro cooking base"
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
Rice
1 cup uncooked rice
1/2 cup fresh chopped cilantro
1 tablespoon lime juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Start rice cooking in rice cooker or boil in pot on the stove (bring 1 cup of rice and 2 1/2 cups of water to a boil, cover and reduce heat to simmer for 20 minutes.)
In 9x13 baking dish put chicken, tomatoes, onions and spices.  Cover with foil and bake for 20-30 minutes or until heated through.
When rice is cooked, mix in 1 tablespoon of lime juice and cilantro.  Serve cacciatore over rice.

Submitter: 
Heather Campbell Fife
Description: 
This Chili-lime inspired twist on chicken cacciatore is a hit with the whole family.  Rarely does my husband say this is a keeper!  Was fantastic served over ENSeeber's Lime Cilantro Rice.
