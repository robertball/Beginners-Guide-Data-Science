Title:
Molded jello salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small lemon jello pkg
1 small lime jello pkg
2 cups boiling water
1 3/4 cups crushed pineapple in thick syrup (1 can)
1 cup evap milk
3 tsp horseradish
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup mayonaise (not miracle whip)
1/2 lb small curd cottage cheese

Directions:
Add both jello's to the boiling water. Dissolve and stir for 2-3 minutes. add pineapple and evap milk. Mix well. Refridgerate for 45min-1hr (this keeps the pineapple from separating). Remove from fridge and stir slightly. Add horseradish, walnuts, mayonaise, and  cottage cheese. Mix well. Pour into an oiled jello mold. Refridgerate for at least 4 hrs. If needed, quickly dip into warm water to help release from mold.

Submitter: 
DIALIEN
Description: 
I got this from my Mother 33 years ago. The ingredients seem like an awful combo, but it is so yummy!!!! There is just enough horseradish to give it a tang that keeps everyone wandering what that ingredient is.
