Title:
Not-Your-Kids' Root Beer Float Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup half-and-half
1/2 cup vanilla ice cream
2 fluid ounces root beer-flavored schnapps

Directions:
Blend half-and-half, ice cream, and root beer-flavored schnapps together in a blender until smooth.
