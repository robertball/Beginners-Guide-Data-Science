Title:
Baked Potato Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs russet potatoes, cut into 1/4" thick slices
4 Tbs olive oil
1 Tbs Coarse salt
1 tsp crumbled, dried rosemary
Fresh ground pepper
Coarse salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400F
Pat the potatoes dry with paper towels.
Place the potatoes, oil, 1 Tbs salt, pepper & rosemary in a large bowl,
toss to combine.
Arrange the potato slices on 2 prepared baking sheets
spacing them 1/4" apart.
Bake, rotating the sheets halfway through, until the potatoes are crisp & golden brown (about 30 minutes).
Spread the potatoes out on parchment paper, let dry for 5 minutes, then if desired sprinkle with salt.
