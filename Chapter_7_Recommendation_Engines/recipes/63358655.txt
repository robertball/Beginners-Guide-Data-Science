Title:
Aunt D's all day spaghetti sauce and meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894376.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce
1 large can of tomato paste and same amount of water
1 large can of tomato sauce and same amount of water
1 medium onion minced and separated in half
1 cup of chicken broth
1 cup of beef broth
1 teas parsley
1/2 teas oregano
1/2 teas basil
2 bay leaves
1 1/2 teas salt
1/4 teas black pepper
1/2 cup parmesan or romano cheese
2 small mushrooms chopped small(optional)
1/4 small red or green pepper chopped small(optional)
Meatballs
2 lbs of ground beef
2 eggs
1 teas parsley
1/2 teas oregano
1/4 teas basil
1/4 teas pepper
1 teas garlic
2 teas salt
1/2 cup grated cheese(parm or romano)
3/4 cup bread crumbs
water if needed
olive oil as needed

Directions:
In a large pot put about 1 or 2 tb of olive oil and saute the onions and other vegetabkes if you have them.  When they are cooked add the paste, sauce, water, broth and spices and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally.
To start the meatballs put all ingredients in a large bowl and mix...if the breadcrumbs are too dry add water sparingly until desired consistancy is achieved.
Shape into small bitesize balls and fry in a pan of olive oil.  Turn 1/4 of a turn until browned on all sides.  Remove from pan and place in bowl.  They will be added to the spaghetti sauce for the last hour of cooking.
The sauce should be cooked down and have more water added to it at least 3 times..ut should be cooked no less than 4 hours.  Taste with a crust of bread to see if additional spicing is needed.

Description: 
This is an old family recipe that always brings back childhood.  It must cook for a minimum of 4 hrs for the flavors to get to know each other.  We always make a big batch and freeze the rest.
