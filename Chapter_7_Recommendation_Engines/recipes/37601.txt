Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 3/4 cups water
1/3 cup catsup
1 teaspoon onion powder
2 cups elbow macaroni, uncooked
3/4 pound VELVEETA Pasteurized Prepared Cheese Product, cut up

Directions:
Brown meat in large skillet; drain.
Stir in water, catsup and onion powder. Bring to boil. Stir in macaroni. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover. Simmer 8 to 10 minutes or until macaroni is tender.
Add VELVEETA; stir until melted.
