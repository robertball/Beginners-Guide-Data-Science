Title:
Corn Dog Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8.5 ounce) boxes of Jiffy cornbread mix
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup grated Cheddar cheese
9 hot dogs, cut into small pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease muffin tins.
Stir together the cornbread mix and the brown sugar in a large bowl. Whisk the eggs and milk in a small bowl until smooth. Fold the eggs and cheese into the dry mixture until moistened.
Spoon mixture into muffin tins until 2/3 full.  Add 1 hot dog half  to each muffin.
Bake in a preheated oven 14 to 18 minutes, or until golden brown.
