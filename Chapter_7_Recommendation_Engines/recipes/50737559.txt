Title:
Crunchy Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh green beans, blanched
2 cups frozen peas, blanched
4 cups frozen whole kernel corn, blanched
1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 green or red bell pepper, chopped
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Blanch vegetables and combine in a large bowl.
In a small bowl, mix together oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, and water.  Pour over vegetables, and stir to coat.  Refrigerate, covered, for 4 hours or overnight.
