Title:
A Catering Company's Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
4 tablespoons dried parsley
1/3 cup soy sauce
3 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine beer, garlic, parsley, and soy sauce.
Marinate meat in the mixture for a few hours to overnight; cover and refrigerate meat while marinating. Cook as desired.

Submitter: 
Siegela
Description: 
This is a basic recipe and stands alone for chicken - go from there to add your own seasonings for steak, pork, etc.  I put beer in just about every marinade.
