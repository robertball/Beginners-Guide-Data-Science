Title:
Awesome cranberry sausage stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4588254.jpg
Ingredients:
2T oil
1 small onion diced
3 stalks celery diced (about 1 cup)
1 lb Italian sausage
1 package artisan stuffing
3/4 C dried cranberry (50% less sugar)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and lightly grease a 13x8 baking pan.
Sauté onions and celery in oil for 10 minutes and remove from pan. Remove sausage from casing and sauté in onion/celery pan until cooked. Return celery and onions to pan with sausage and cook for a few minutes.
In a medium bowl mix the remaining ingredients along with the onion, celery and sauce mix. Stir gently until liquid is gone and items are coated. Place in baking dish and bake covered for 30 minutes.mremove cover and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes. Enjoy!
