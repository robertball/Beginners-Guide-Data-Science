Title:
Chris's Quick and Easy Lemon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4.3 ounce) package non-instant lemon pudding mix
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
4 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tbs lemon zest
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Cook pudding as directed on package. After pudding thickens, turn down heat to low. Stir in cream cheese, condensed milk,lemon juice and lemon zest. Stir frequently as mixture may stick to saucepan.
Pour mixture into graham cracker crust. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight. When pie is ready, serve with whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Chris Maloney
Description: 
Cool and easy, even your kids can help you make it!!! They'll love the pudding and cream cheese filling and the graham cracker crust!Modified from: Quick and Easy Lemon Pie
