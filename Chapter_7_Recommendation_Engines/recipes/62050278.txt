Title:
Kitty's Cranberry Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 can pineapple tidbits
- 2c. sugar
- 1 bag fresh cranberries( 12 oz.)
- 1c. white raisins(golden raisins)
- 1/2 tsp. ginger
- 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp. allspice
- 1c. walnuts
- 1 pkg. cream cheese ( low fat if desired)
- 1 pkg. triscuit crackers

Directions:
Seperate pineapple tidbits from juice.  Put juice in plastic, microwave safe bowl.  Add to juice: sugar, cranberries, raisins, ginger, cinnamon, and allspice.  Microwave on high for 15-18 minutes, stirring often.  Then add walnuts and tidbits.  Stir well.  Let cool.  Serve on top of cream cheese and eat with crackers.  Keeps in fridge up to 4 months.
