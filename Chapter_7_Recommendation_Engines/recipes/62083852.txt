Title:
easy green cury chicken with rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion (minced)
1 clove garlick (minced)
2 tbls extra vergin olive oil
1 tbls green curry paste
1 1/2 cups choped green beens
1 16 oz can coconut milk
2 tbls brown sugar
1 tsp salt (or 1 tbls fish sauce)
1 cup long grain white rice
2 cups water
1 1/2 to 2 lbs chicken brests or boneless chicken thyes (cut in 1 inch cubes
1 tsp lime juice

Directions:
heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok.
add onions and garlick and cook at a medium temp until you can smell the onions or till clear.
add green cury past and cook for 2 minutes  wile mixing.
add chicken, cook the chicken untill its slightly cooked.
(in a seperate pot, boil water for the rice. add the rice and cook for 10 minutes)
add the coconut milk salt and brown sugar to the chiken and simmer for 10 minutes or until the chicken is done.
add the pepper and the green beens and simmer for another two minutes.
add lime juice*
salt pepper to taste
