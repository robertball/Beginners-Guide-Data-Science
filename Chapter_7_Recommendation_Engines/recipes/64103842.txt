Title:
Baked Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 Tbsp cornstarch
Dash salt
1 egg, slightly beaten
2-1/2 cups milk
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1-1/2 cups cooked rice
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Blend sugar, cornstarch and salt.  Add egg and milk and beat well.  Stir in lemon juice, rice, and raisins.
Pour into ungreased 1-1/2 qt casserole.  Place casserole in pan of hot water, 1 inch deep.  Bake about 1-1/2 hours, stirring occasionally, until pudding is creamy and most of liquid is absorbed.
