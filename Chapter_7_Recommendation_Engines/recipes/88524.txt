Title:
Icy Banana Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5516294.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bananas, peeled and sliced
6 ice cubes
5 cups milk
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
In a blender, combine the bananas, ice cubes, milk and sugar. Cover, and blend for 2 minutes, or until ice is finely crushed.
