Title:
Stuffing Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts or equivalent cut
2 cups grated cheese (swiss, cheddar...)
1 box stuffing of choice
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 can corn with juice

Directions:
spray 13 x 9 pan with pam
stir together all ingredients minus the chicken
layer in pan
place chicken on top
salt and pepper to taste
cover with foil
cook 350 degrees for 1 hours
