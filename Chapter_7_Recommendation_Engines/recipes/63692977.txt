Title:
Watermelon-Raspberry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027149.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups seedless watermelon, cubed
1/2 cup red raspberries
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup water, divided
1/3 cup white sugar

Directions:
Make a simple syrup in a small sauce pan by combining sugar with 1/3 cup of water. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat and cool.
Puree the watermelon and raspberries with the remaining water in a blender until smooth.
Pour into a frosty pitcher and stir in simple syrup and lemon juice. Chill for at least one hour.
Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
This simple drink is awesome on a hot day. You can also add in a shot of rum, gin, or vodka for a fun adult cocktail.
