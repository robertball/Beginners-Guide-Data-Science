Title:
Wild Rice with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C wild rice
2 Cans Campbell's Beef Consomme
I pk mushrooms, quartered

Directions:
Soak rice over night.  Drain and pour in consomme and mushrooms. Put in baking casserol and bake, covered at 350 for 1 1/2 hours.
