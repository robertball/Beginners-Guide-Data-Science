Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium sized eggplant
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 pound mushrooms, sliced
3 garlic cloves, minced or pressed
1 15 oz. can tomato sauce
3/4 tsp. dry basil
3/4 tsp. oregano leaves
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
olive oil for brushing

Directions:
Rinse eggplant.  Cut into 1/2 inch thick slices and brush all sides with olive oil.  Arrange in a single layer in a shallow baking pan and bake, uncovered at 425 for 20-25 minutes.  Should be well browned and tender when pierced.
Meanwhile, heat about 1tbsp olive oil in a wide frying pan over medium heat.  Cook onion, mushrooms and garlic, stirring constantly until onion is soft.  Add tomato sauce, basil and oregano; simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Evenly arrange about half the eggplant in a 2 qt. casserole dish.  Top with half the sauce, then half the mozzarella cheese and half the parmesan.  Repeat layers.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for about 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Allow to stand for several minutes before serving.
