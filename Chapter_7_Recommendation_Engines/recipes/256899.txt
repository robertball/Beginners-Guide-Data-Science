Title:
Creamy Pineapple Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4461304.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fat free milk
8 fresh pineapple chunks
1/3 cup ice cubes
1 packet Classic French Vanilla Flavor CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® LIGHT START™ Complete Nutritional Drink
1/2 banana
1 tablespoon pineapple juice

Directions:
Place milk, pineapple, ice, Carnation Breakfast Essentials Drink, banana and pineapple juice in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation Breakfast Essentials
Description: 
This frosty Creamy Pineapple Drink is a great way to get your daily servings of fruit. Check out this Carnation Breakfast Essentials recipe today!
