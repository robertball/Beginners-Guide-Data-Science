Title:
Misti's Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups vegetable oil
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 cup molasses
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 cloves fresh minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon bajeska

Directions:
Whisk together in a bowl.
To use, marinate chicken for at least 8 hours before cooking as desired.

Submitter: 
carter_crazy
Description: 
Savory and sweet flavors from molasses, vinegar, and soy sauce mingle with garlic and onion powders in this tasty marinade that's ideal for chicken. Allow enough time for the marinade to work its tasty magic overnight.Modified from: Misti's Chicken Marinade
