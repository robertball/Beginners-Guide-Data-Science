Title:
Dave's Power Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 C. flour (any kind)
.75 C. ground flax seed
.75 C. oat bran
.5 C. honey
2 t. baking soda
1 t. baking powder
1 t. salt
2 t. ground cinnamon
.75 C. milk
2 eggs
2 T. apple sauce
2 apples - shredded in food processor
2 C. carrots - shredded in food processor
.5 C. raisins
.5 C. other dried fruit or mini choc chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease muffin pan
Mix all ingredients thoroughly
Bake 20 - 22 mins
