Title:
Steak Sauce Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs fresh lean ground beef or turkey
6 Tbls steak sauce
1 8 ounce can tomatoe sauce
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 cup Italian style bread crumbs
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
1 Tbls Italian seasoning
1 small chopped onion
1 small chopped bell pepper

Directions:
Mix steak sauce in a small bowl with tomatoe sauce.  In larger bowl combine remaining ingredients add steak sauce mixture, reserving a small amount in bowl.  Blend well.. pat into loaf pan, spread remaining sauce over top and bake at 400 degrees for apprx. 1 1/2hrs.  Serve with warm mashed potatoes and green beans.

Submitter: 
RHONDA35
Description: 
A zesty not your average meatloaf combined with the steak sauce of your choice, bell peppers, fresh onions and other yummy ingredients blended and baked to perfection!  My family requests this one.
