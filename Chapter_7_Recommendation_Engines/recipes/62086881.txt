Title:
Slow Cooker German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds potatoes, peeled and sliced
1 cup onion, chopped
1 cup celery, sliced
1/2 cup green pepper, diced
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup oil
salt, to taste
pepper, to taste
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
6 slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled

Directions:
Combine the potatoes, onions, celery, green peppers, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper in a 5-6 quart slow cooker. Cover and cook on low 5-6 hours.
Garnish with parsley and bacon.

Submitter: 
dramamath
Description: 
I tried this recipe for some friends who had requested my German potato salad.  I didn't have time to make in on the spot so I gathered my ingredients and put it in a slow cooker.  They loved it!
