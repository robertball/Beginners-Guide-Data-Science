Title:
Chicken or Crab Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped green onion
4 Tbsp butter
3/4 cups dry sherry
3 cups diced cooked chicken or crab
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
4 Tbsp butter
6 Tbsp flour
2 cups HOT milk
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 egg yolks
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 cup swiss cheese, grated
Crepes: 1 cup COLD water
1 cup COLD milk
4 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups flour
2 Tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Crepes: Put all ingredients for crepes in a blender, cover and blend for 2 minutes. Refrigerate for two hours.
Using a 6 inch crepe pan, make crepes as thin as possible.
Filling: Saute onion in butter. Add sherry. Mix with crab or chicken and set aside.
Sauce: Melt butter and add flour and blend. Add milk, seasoning and boil for one minute. Remove from heat.
In a small bowl, beat yolks and cream. Add egg mixture to sauce mixture stirring constantly. Fold in grated cheese. Sauce should be thick.
Reserve one cup of sauce to pour over crepes. Add remaining sauce to filling and mix well.
Place two heaping Tbsp of mixture across one side of each crepe and roll up.
Top each crepe with reserved sauce.

Delicious as a light lunch or an appetizer for a more formal meal.  Fabulous with a glass of chilled Chardonnay!
