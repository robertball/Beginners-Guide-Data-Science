Title:
Baked Potato Soup (Crockpot) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 BAKING POTATOES
6 CANS CREAM OF POTATO SOUP (I USE CAMPBELLS)
2 QTS WHIPPING CREAM
1 BAG OSCAR MEYER BACON BITS
1 MED. ONION (OPTIONAL)
SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE (OPTIONAL)

Directions:
BAKE POTATOES UNTIL DONE. LET COOL. CUT INTO CUBES (YOU CAN LEAVE THE PEELS ON IF YOU WANT TO). POUR WHIPPING CREAM INTO CROCKPOT, ADD THE 6 CANS CREAM OF POTATO SOUP, ADD POTATOES, ONION (IF DESIRED), AND BACON BITS. COOK ON HIGH FOR ONE HOUR, THEN TURN TO LOW AND COOK FOR 3 TO 4 HOURS MORE. IF DESIRED, TOP WITH CHEDDAR CHEESE WHEN SERVED.

Submitter: 
Lesa Kindle Renner
Description: 
Delicious and creamy ! ! Really good on a cold day.
