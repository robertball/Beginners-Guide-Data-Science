Title:
Cream Cheese Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg cream cheese
1 cup shortening
7 tbsp sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups sifted flour
pinch of salt

Directions:
Blend cream cheese and shortening until fluffy.
Add sugar and mix well.
Then add vanilla, flour and salt.
Drop from tsp onto cookie sheet and press with floured fork.
Bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes.

Cream cheese cookies....put colored sugar on before baking.
