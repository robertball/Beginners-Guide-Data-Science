Title:
Homemade Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123275.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups (1/2 pints) whipping cream
6 eggs
3 cups sugar
Milk
Vanilla

Directions:
Mix together and beat whipping cream and eggs.
Add sugar and mix well.
Add some milk and pour into ice cream freezer.
Add more milk until freezer 2/3 full.
Add vanilla to taste (3 Tbsp. ?).
Freeze until can't freeze no more!
