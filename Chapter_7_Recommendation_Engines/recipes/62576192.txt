Title:
Eggnog Tapioca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup large or small-pearl tapioca
2 1/2 cups milk (2% or whole is best)
2 cups eggnog
1/2 tsp salt
3 eggs

Directions:
Soak tapioca in 3 cups water for 6 hours or overnight.
Combine milk and eggnog in a 2 qt saucepan and heat on medium low until room temperature. Add salt and tapioca. heat on  until small bubbles appear at sides of pan. Cover, turn heat to low and cook 1 hr, stirring occasionally.
Whisk eggs until blended, then add a little of the hot mixture to the eggs and blend until light yellow.  Then add eggs slowly, whisking into the hot mixture.  Turn heat to medium low and cook for 15 min or until mixture is thick.  Remove from heat. Can be served warm or cold.

Submitter: 
Jedimom
Description: 
Classic Holiday flavor in a delicious tapioca pudding.  Try it with Pumpkin Spice Eggnog, too!
