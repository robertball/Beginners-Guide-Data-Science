Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Chicken Breasts
1 14.5oz can Crushed Tomatoes
1 14.5oz can Petite Diced Tomatoes
1 10oz can Red Enchilada Sauce
1 12oz bag Frozen Chopped Onions
1 4oz can Diced Green Chile Peppers
2 heaping teaspoons Minced Garlic
1 14 oz can Chicken Broth
1 12 oz bag Frozen Corn
2 cups Water
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Chili Powder
1 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Black Pepper
1 Bay Leaf
1 Tbsp Cilantro

Directions:
Place onion, chicken, tomatoes, enchilada sauce, green chiles, and garlic in slow cooker.
Pour in water and chicken broth, seasonings and bay leaf.
Stir in corn and cilantro.
Cover, and cook on Low setting for 6 to 8 hours or on High setting for 3 to 4 hours.
Remove chicken, shred and put it back in.  I did this after 3 hours on High and let it cook more after.
Garnish with tortilla chips, cheese, sour cream, jalapenos, avocado, lime slices....whatever you like.
