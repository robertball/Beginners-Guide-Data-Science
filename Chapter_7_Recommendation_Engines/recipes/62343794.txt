Title:
Easy Chicken Rice Casserole for 40 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
35 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (cut in half after cooked)
7 pkgs dry onion soup mix
10 1/2 cups uncooked white rice
7 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken/celery/mushroom soup- use some of each
15 cups of water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine 1/2 of all ingredients, except chicken, in each pan, stir to mix together well. Top with frozen chicken breasts- turn to coat. Start baking at 10am. Should be done by 11:30. Cover with foil and turn off oven. Cut chicken fillets in half before serving.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
Make in the 2 large church pans. Assemble at church as it's messy to transport.Modified from: Easy Chicken Rice Casserole
