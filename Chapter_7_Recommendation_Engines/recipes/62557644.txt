Title:
Frozen Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566993.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
2 tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups milk, divided
3 cups ice
whipped cream, for serving

Directions:
In a small microwavable bowl microwave chocolate, stirring every 30 seconds until completely melted. Stir in sugar, cocoa powder, and vanilla. Slowley wisk in a 1/2 cup of milk, set aside to cool.
In a blender blend together the chocolate mixture, remaining 1 cup of milk, and ice on high speed until creamy, add more milk or ice if needed. Pour in glasses and top with a dollop of whipped cream, serve immediatly.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is so good! Make sure to top it off with freshly whipped cream to make it even better.
