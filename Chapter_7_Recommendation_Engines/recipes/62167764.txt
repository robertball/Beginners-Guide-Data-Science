Title:
Romantic Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package refrigerated spinach and cheese ravioli
2 lemons total, 1 zested
2 small or 1 large cloves garlic, chopped
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1 stick unsalted butter
shaved parmesan cheese
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring salted water in a medium sized pot to a boil. In a smaller pot, add butter, garlic, parsley, the zest of 1 lemon and the juice from both lemons, salt & pepper to taste. Heat the mixture on low and simmer for 10 minutes. Meanwhile add ravioli to boiling water and cook according to package directions (about 5 to 10 minutes). Drain ravioli and toss with sauce in a separate bowl. Top with parmesan cheese.
