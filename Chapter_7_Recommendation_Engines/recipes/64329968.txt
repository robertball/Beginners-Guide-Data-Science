Title:
Pasta with Sausage, Tomatoes and Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces bow tie or penne pasta
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 pound sweet turkey Italian sausage, casings removed and crumbled
1 diced onion
1 green bell pepper, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 (15 ounce) cans Italian-style diced tomatoes, drained
1 1/2 cups fat free half & half
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons dried parsley flakes

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
Heat oil in a large, deep skillet over medium heat. Cook sausage evenly browned. Stir in onion, green bell pepper and garlic, and cook until tender. Stir in tomatoes, cream, and salt. Simmer until mixture thickens, 8 to 10 minutes. Add a little corn starch if needed.
Stir cooked pasta into sauce, and heat through. Sprinkle with parsley.
