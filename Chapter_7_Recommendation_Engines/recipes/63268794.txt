Title:
Banana Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. flour
1 c. sugar
1/2 t. baking soda
1 t. salt
1/4 t. nutmeg
3/4 t. cinnamon
3/4 c. margarine
1 egg, well beaten
1 c. mashed ripe banana
1 3/4 c. rolled oats
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix flour and next 5 ingredients. Cut in butter with knives or pastry cutter until the mixture resembles coarse meal.
Add egg, banana, oats, and nuts; blend well.
Drop dough by teaspoonful onto baking sheet about 2" apart. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
