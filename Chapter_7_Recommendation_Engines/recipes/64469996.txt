Title:
Terrona's potatoes Romanoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5.  baking potatoes, peeled, cut into pieces
1/2 pint sour cream
3   green onions, chopped
1/2 pound processed cheese, cubed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and mash.
In a 9 x 13 inch casserole dish combine potatoes, sour cream, green onions and processed cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Terrona Postom
Description: 
Mashed potatoes are baked with sour cream, green onions and processed cheese for a stick to your ribs side dish your whole family will love.Modified from: Potatoes Romanoff
