Title:
Nan Cassidy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 lbs red potatoes cleaned, but with skin
1/2 cup salted butter
1 cup heavy cream
1-2 cloves garlic minced
1/2 cup chopped parsley
zest of 1 fresh lemon
salt and pepper

Directions:
Boil potatoes with skin until fully cooked.  Smash with potato masher. Whip with cream and butter.  Blend in garlic, salt, pepper, parsley, and lemon zest. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
JSCHMAND
Description: 
Creamy, whipped red potatoes with parsley and lemon zest.  A little unorthodox, but so delicious!
