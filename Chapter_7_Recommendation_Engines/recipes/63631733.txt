Title:
Jennie's Black Book Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009835.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2c Diced Chicken or Ham
1 1/2c Shredded Swiss or Cheddar Cheese
3 Eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2c Milk
2 Tbsp Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place meat and cheese in well-greased 9-inch pie plate.
Beat together eggs and milk, and pour over meat and cheese.
Sprinkle Parmesan over top.
Bake 30-35 minutes, or until knife inserted halfway from edge comes out clean

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
A quick, easy crust-less quiche!
