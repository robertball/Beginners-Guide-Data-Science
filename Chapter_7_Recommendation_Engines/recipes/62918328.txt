Title:
Cheesy Turkey Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/711597.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/4 = (2oz) cup cream cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon dry bread crumbs
1 tablespoon Italian dry salad seasoning
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil, and spray with cooking spray.
Combine the chicken, eggs, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Form mixture into 20 meatballs; place on prepared pan.
Bake in center of preheated oven until juices run clear, 17 to 18 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
