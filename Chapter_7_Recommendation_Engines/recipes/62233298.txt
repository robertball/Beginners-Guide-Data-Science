Title:
Cinnamon Raisin Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Self Rising Flour
5 Tablespoons Granulated White Sugar
1 Bottle(12oz.)Beer at room temperature
1/2 Cup Dark Raisins
1/2 Cup Gold Raisins
1/2 Cup Walnuts, chopped
1 Tablespoon Ground Cinnamon
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
1/4 Cup Butter, melted

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375*F. Grease a 9"x5" bread pan and set aside.
2. In a large bowl mix together flour, sugar, raisins, walnuts, and spices.  Mix dry ingredients well.
3. Add room temp beer to dry ingredients and mix until well incorporated.
4. Scoop mixture into greased bread pan and level with the back of a spoon. Pour melted butter over the mixture covering well.
5. Place pan on the middle rack of the preheated oven and bake 45 to 55 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alex H. aka "Mr. Spicy"
Description: 
This lightly sweetened bread does well as a breakfast item served buttered alongside a nice hot cup of coffee.
