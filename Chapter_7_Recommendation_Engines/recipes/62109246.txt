Title:
BARBACOA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3-4lb tri-tip or lean chuck steak, cubed to 1 1 1/2 inch pieces
1 1/2 tbs of california chile powder
2 tsp of pasilla chile powder
2 tsp of salt, or more to taste
2 tsp of black pepper
3 cloves of garlic cut in half
1 bay leaf
4 small cloves
1 jalepeno cut into largely chopped pieces, with or without seeds, depending on your heat preference
1 1/4 cups of water, may need a little more throughout cooking to prevent it from drying out.

Directions:
in a medium stock pot, add cubed beef and all of the seasonings. Distributed evenly, drop the garlic, cloves, bay leaf, and jalepeno into the pot, also distributed evenly.  pour in the water, and cook on a low simmer for about 3-4 hours, depending on the meat.  stir occasionally. When tender enough, shred with two forks, and serve hot with rice and beans. Enjoy
