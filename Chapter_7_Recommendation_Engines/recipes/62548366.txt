Title:
Chicken Bean Salsa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Jars of Northern Beans
1 Large Can of Diced Chicken
8 oz Pepper Jack Cheese
1 Jar Medium Salsa

Directions:
Drain and rinse northern beans.  Place everything in the crock pot and cook on high for 4 hours.  Serve with tortilla strips.
