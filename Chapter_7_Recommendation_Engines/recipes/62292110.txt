Title:
Tangy Beef N' Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb Lean Hamburger
3/4 Lb Bacon
1 Cup Onion -Chopped
2 - 1lb 15oz Cans Pork & Beans
1 - 1lb kidney beans (drained)
1 Teaspoon salt
1 can Buttered lima beans (drained)
1 Cup Ketchup
1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Tablespoon liquid smoke
3 Tbsp White Vinegar
Dash of pepper

Directions:
1.Brown ground beef in skillet. Drain off excess fat and place into crock pot.
2.Brown bacon and onions. Drain of excess fat and place into crockpot.
3.Add all remaining ingredients into crock pot, mix well.
4.Cover and let cook on low for 4-6 hours.
