Title:
Instant pot Creamy mushroom orzotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5966176.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup orzo
2 cups chicken stock, divided
2 Tbsp butter
2 garlic cloves minced
2 ounces sliced mushrooms
1 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp crushed rosemary

Directions:
Set instant pot to sauté.  Melt the butter. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant.
Add the orzo and 1 1/2 cups chicken stock. Stir to thoroughly incorporate.
Cancel the sauté setting. Place lid on pot. Change pressure cooker to high pressure.
Cook for 6 minutes. Quick release pressure
Remove the lid and add in the mushrooms. Switch to sauté mode.
Add in the Parmesan cheese, rosemary, garlic salt and remaining chicken broth 1 tablespoon at a time until the orzo takes on a creamy texture.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
Typically risotto is made with arborio rice, this is a take on risotto using orzo.
