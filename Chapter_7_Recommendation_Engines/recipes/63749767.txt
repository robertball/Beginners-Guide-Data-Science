Title:
Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1040520.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
1 onion, finely diced
1 teaspoon pressed garlic
1/2 lime, juiced
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 jalapeno finely diced
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
3-4 tomatoes diced

Directions:
Place the stewed tomatoes, onion, garlic, lime juice, salt, jalapeno and cilantro in a blender or food processor. Blend on low to desired consistency. Mix in fresh tomatoes.
