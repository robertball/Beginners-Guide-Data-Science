Title:
Ranch Medley Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag (1 lb) frozen broccoli, carrots and cauliflower
2 cups diced cooked ham
2 cups refrigerated diced potatoes with onions
1 container (10 oz) refrigerated alfredo sauce
1/2 cup ranch dressing
24 gluten free butter crackers, crushed (like ritz)
2 tbsp butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
1) Heat oven to 350. In large bowl, mix frozen vegetables, ham, potatoes, Alfredo sauce and ranch dressing. Spread evenly in ungreased 13x9 (3 quart) glass baking dish.
2) In small bowl, mix crackers and melted butter. sprinkle over top of casserole.
3) Bake 40-45 minutes or until bubbly and topping is golden brown.
