Title:
Texas Style Sweet and (mildly) Smoky Bar-B-Que Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1026194.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C ketchup
2 T brown sugar
2 T Worcestershire sauce
1 T apple cider vinegar
2 T rum
1/4 C maple syrup (+/- 1/2 C)
8 dashes smoked chipotle Tabasco
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 T prepared mustard
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a small saucepan over medium heat, stir together all ingredients.. Bring to a simmer, stirring almost constantly, tasting to add more maple syrup or salt as needed, then remove from heat and allow to cool slightly before brushing on your favorite meat.
