Title:
Better Than Beef Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. whole wheat couscous.
3 C water
1 packet taco seasoning (or your favorite AR recipe)
Taco toppings:
hard or soft shells
lettuce
tomato
cheese
salsa
sour cream

Directions:
Bring water to a boil and add seasoning.  Whisk well until it is dissolved.  Remove from heat.
Add couscous and stir briefly, then cover and stand until water is absorbed, about 5-8 minutes.
Fluff with a fork and serve - just like your favorite tacos!

Submitter: 
Carrie Causey
Description: 
Your hungriest beef-eater won't miss a thing when he bites into this moist, delicious taco!  Use whole wheat couscous, because it's firmer - more like meat.
