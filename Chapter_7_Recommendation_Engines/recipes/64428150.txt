Title:
Shauna's Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5280734.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2/3 cup Brown Sugar
1 1/3 cup white sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
3 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
2 cups Chocolate Chips
2 Cups Pecans - chopped

Directions:
Cream together butter, sugars, vanilla and eggs.
Mix in flour, soda, salt.
Add Chocolate chips and pecans
Bake cookies at 350 for 12 minutes.
