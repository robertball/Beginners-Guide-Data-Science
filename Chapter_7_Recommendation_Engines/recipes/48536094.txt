Title:
Quick and Easy Sauteed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 (10 ounce) bag spinach leaves
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt, or more to taste
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the spinach to the skillet and cover; allow to cook 5 minutes. Stir in the garlic powder and salt and cover again for another 5 minutes; remove from heat. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve.
