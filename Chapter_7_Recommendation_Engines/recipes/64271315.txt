Title:
Crostini Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup black olives
1/2 cup pimento stuffed green olives
1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup grated Monterey Jack cheese
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
1 baquette

Directions:
Chop the olives by hand or in a food processor. Stir olives, parmesan cheese, butter, olive oil and garlic until well blended. Stir in Monterey jack cheese and parsley.
Cut baquette into 1/4 thin slices. Arrange bread slices on baking sheets and spread some of olive mixture on each. Broil 3 minutes or until bread is toasted and topping is bubbly.
