Title:
Italian Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
1 tsp Italian herb seasoning
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp salt
4 medium potatoes, skin on- cut in wedges
1 Tbsp Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In large bowl mix oil, Italian seasoning, paprika and salt.  Add potato wedges and mix until well coated.
On a dinner plate, arrange potatoes cut side down in spoke-like fashion.  Cover with wax paper and cook in microwave on High for 10-15 minutes (until tender).
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Let stand 3 minutes.  Serve with catsup.
