Title:
Broccoli Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 10 oz packages of frozen chopped broccoli
1 can cream of mushroom
1 can cream of chicken
1 cup of instant rice
1 onion
3 sticks of celery
cheese (block velveta) can use cheese slices also

Directions:
saute onion and celery in butter, then all ingredients except cheese and put in 2 quart cassarole dish and top with cheese and bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes or put everything in crockpot and cook on low for a few hours.

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
A family favorite, this cassarole is baked in the oven but can be done in the crockpot as well.
