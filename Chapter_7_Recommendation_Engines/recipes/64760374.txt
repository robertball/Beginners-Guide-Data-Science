Title:
Beef Stroganoff Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c shredded beef
1 whole onion, sliced
1 c mushrooms
1 8oz block cream cheese
1 c beef bone broth, stock or juice from pot roast
1/2-1 c sour cream
2-3 garlic cloves
1/2 tsp onion powder
5 slices smoked gouda cheese
salt if needed

Directions:
place meat on bottom of glass baking dish
saute mushrooms in tallow or bacon grease until softened
saute onion in tallow or bacon grease until charred
put mushrooms and onions in layers over meat
blend cream cheese, sour cream, bone broth, garlic, onion powder, salt with immersion blender
season to taste
pour mixture over meat, mushrooms, onions
put slices of cheese on top
bake 350-400 until cheese melted and golden, bubbling, maybe 30 min
