Title:
Pork Chops With Mushroom Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T butter
1 onion, chopped
1 rib celery, chopped
3/4 # mushrooms, sliced thinly
1 t dried thyme
1/2 # baguette (or other crusty bread), cut into 1/2-in cubes
1 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1 1/3 C chicken broth
3 eggs, beaten
2/3 C half and half
1 T cooking oil
4 center-cut pork chops, 1-in thick

Directions:
Heat oven to 325 degrees. Butter 9 x 13-in baking dish. In al large nonstick frying pan, melt butter over moderately low heat. Add onion and celery; cook, stirring occ., until starting to soften, about 3 min. Increase heat to mod. high. Add mushroons and thyme and cook until mushrooms start to brown, about 5 min. Remove pan from heat. Stir in bread cubes, 3/4 t salt, and 1/4 t pepper. Transfer mixture to baking dish.
Stir together broth, eggs, and half-and half. Pour mixture evenly over mushrooms and bread. Put dish in lower third of oven and bake for 25 min.
Meanwhile, heat oil in frying pan over mod. heat. Sprinkle remaining 1/4 t each salt and perrer over pork chops. Add chops to pan and brown, about 3 min. per side. Remove chops from pan. Remove bread pudding from oven after baking 25 min. and set pork chops directly on top. Return to oven and cook until meat is done and pudding is just set, 10-15 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
One-inch pork chops used. If thicker, put in oven sooner so eggs don't curdle
