Title:
Glazed Lemon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c soft butter
3/4 c sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 tbsp lemon zest
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp baking pwd
1/4 tsp salt
1 2/3 c flour, packed down
1/2 c milk
Topping:
1/4 c lemon juice
3/4 c sugar

Directions:
Beat butter and sugar till light and fluffy.  Add egg, zest and juice.  Mix.  Add pwd, soda and salt.  Mix well.  Mix 1/2 flour and 1/2 milk.  Stir all then add remaining flour and milk.  Mix well. Drop by tsp onto ungreased cookie sheet (or parchment paper).  Make them small (cherry-sized).  Bake at 350 for 12-14 min.
Topping:
Heat lemon juice in microwave, stir in sugar till dissolved.  Brush hot cookies with glaze using a pastry brush.
