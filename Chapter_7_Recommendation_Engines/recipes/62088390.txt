Title:
Brenda's Black Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 regular size can goya black beans
2 table spoons Goya frozen Sofrito
2 table spoons Goya frozen Recaito
1 small can tomato sauce
1 packet goya zazon
apporox 2 teaspoons adobo

Directions:
Empty black beans into non stick sauce pan
add two cans of water (using black bean can to measure)
add tomato sauce, sofrito, recaito, zazon, and adobo.
Bring to boil, let simmer about 5 mins
add rice, and cover
Reduce heat to med/low.
Cook until rice absorbs moisture
Let stand 3 mins, and serve

Submitter: 
Brenda Vandervort
Description: 
Easy side dish that everyone will love! Is great with pork chops, fried chicken, Steak, or by it's self.
