Title:
Cheesy Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 loaves of Pan Frances bakery bread
1 c. real mayonnaise (not Miracle Whip)
4 c. shredded Colby jack cheese
pickled jalepeno peppers
sliced olives
diced red onion
diced pepperoni
italian sausage crumbles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F.
Slice bread lengthwise into to two flat halves per loaf.
Combine cheese and mayo until spreadable and spread on to the bread. Top with various veggies and meats as desired.
Bake at 400 F until cheese is melted and slightly browned.
Remove and slice, serve warm.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Mmmm! One of the yummy creations by husband brought to my family. Not low-cal - but so good. Top it however you like it, sort of like pizza.
