Title:
Buffalo Style Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/287622.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tilapia fillet per person
1-1 1/2 tsp of dried dill seasoning
1-1 1/2 tsp of garlic powder
course salt & ground pepper
1 1/2 tbsp of Frank's Red Hot sauce
1 tsp olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
Line a pan with foil and spray or brush with olive oil
Place fillet on the pan and sprinkle with the garlic powder, dill, salt and pepper.
Brush the fillet with Frank's Red Hot sauce
Bake on the center rack for 12 to 13 minutes with a final minute on broil.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
This is my quick dinner when I work out at night and need a protein charge after. It's healthy, fast, super easy and flavorful.
