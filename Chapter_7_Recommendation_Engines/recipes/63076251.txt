Title:
Restaurant Style Toasted Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1095606.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz package of meat filled OR cheese filled ravioli,  thawed
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup water
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 cup flour
1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
Vegetable oil for deep frying
Marinara sauce for dipping

Directions:
Mix water with eggs and beat well, set aside.
Mix Italian seasoning with flour.
Mix garlic salt with bread crumbs.
Heat vegetable oil in a large, heavy skillet to 350 F.
Dredge ravioli in flour, then egg, then bread crumbs.
Carefully place the breaded ravioli in the hot oil and deep fry for 1-2 mins, until the ravioli are browned, floating and slightly puffed.
Remove from oil and drain. Serve with warm marinara for dipping.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
Ever since I was a kid, toasted raviolis were my favorite thing from the menu at Olive Garden. Now I make these at home for way less than the cost of eating out. My kids love to dip these and my husband always asks for them when his friends come over to watch football.
