Title:
Bean and Bacon Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of bacon, diced
1 small onion, minced
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tsp. mustard
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup molasses
1 can kidney beans, drained
1 can white beans, drained
1 can piquintos, drained
1 can baked beans

Directions:
Cook bacon in a large skillet until translucent but not browned.  Drain grease; reserve for later use.
Combine ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, and molasses in a large saucepan; add bacon and onions.  Heat on stove over medium-high heat until bubbling.
Add kidney beans, white beans, piquintos, and baked beans to saucepan.  Heat until bubbling again.
Simmer until ready to serve.
