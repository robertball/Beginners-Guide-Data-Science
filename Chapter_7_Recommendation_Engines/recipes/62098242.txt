Title:
Orange Pancakes w/ Orange Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pancakes:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 tblsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 tblsp white sugar
3/4 cup orange juice, room temperature
1/2 cup milk, room temperature
1 egg, room temperature
1-1/2 tsp orange extract
3 tbls butter, melted
Orange sauce:
1 cup orange juice
1 tsp orange zest
1 tblsp sugar
1/4 cup water
1 tblsp corn starch

Directions:
In large bowl combine flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in center; set aside.
In a small bowl, lightly beat egg and orange extract. Mix in orange juice, milk, and melted butter. Pour into well of dry ingredients and stir until smooth.
Pre-heat lightly greased griddle over med-high heat. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto prepared griddle, brown on both sides; remove from heat and serve warm with orange pancake syrup: Recipe follows.
Orange Sauce: Combine 3/4 of the orange juice with the orange zest, sugar and water in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to a simmer.
In a small bowl, combine reserved 1/4 cup of orange juice with cornstarch, mix thoroughly. Add to sauce pan, mixing well, and continue to simmer for 5-minutes or until mixture has thickened. Remove from heat. Serve w/ pancakes, garnished with orange wedges and fresh mint.
