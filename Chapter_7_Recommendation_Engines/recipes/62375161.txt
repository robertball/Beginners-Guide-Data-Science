Title:
Country-Fried Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cubed beef steaks
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp Montreal seasoning
1 egg
2 TBS milk
2 cups crushed Ritz crackers
vegetable oil
3 TBS flour
1 1/4 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup milk
dash of Worcestershire sauce
dash of hot sauce
additional pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients and sprinkle onto steaks until completely
covered with a light layer.
Whip together egg and milk. Dip steaks in egg mixture until fully wet.
Dredge in cracker crumbs.
Pour vegetable oil to depth in 1/2 inch into large skillet.
Brown steaks on both sides over medium heat.
Cover, reduce heat and cook 15 minutes or until tender, turning occasionally.
Drain on paper towels.
Reserve 3 TBS drippings in skillet. Stir in 3 TBS flour.
Cook over medium heat for 1 minute.
Add broth and next three ingredients. Stir constantly until thickened.
Serve steaks with gravy and additional pepper.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A classic comfort food. Breaded cube steak with a country gravy.
