Title:
Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
bone In ham
Apple cider
Brown sugar
2 onions
1 c chicken broth

Directions:
Baste bone in ham with apple cider.  Coat ham with a thick layer of brown sugar. Put a small layer of chicken broth in bottom of roaster, cut onions in half and place on all 4 sides. Roast slowly at 350, basting with juices until hot.
