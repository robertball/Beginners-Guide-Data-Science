Title:
long cooking ratatouille Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/1 lb eggplant
salt
3 tbsp olive oil
3/4 lb (2 med) onions, thinly sliced
3/4 lb mixed red and yellow bell peppers, cored, seeded, and cut into slices about 3/4 in wide by 1 1/1 in long
4-6 large garlic cloves, minced or pressed
1 1/1 lb mixed yellow and green zuchini, sliced about 1/2 in think (if very thick cut in half lengthwise)
fresh ground black pepper
3/4 lb tomatoes , peeled, seeded, and coarsely chopped
1 bay leaf
1-2 tsp fresh thyme leaves or 1/2- 1 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp oregano
2-4 tbsp slived or chopped fresh basil, to taste

Directions:
cut the eggplant into 1/2 in cubes. salt generously and place in a colander in the sink. let sit for 30 mins to an hr. meanwhile, heat the oven to 500. rinse the eggplant and pat dry, place in a large casserole and toss with a tablespoon of olive oil. place in the hot oven and roast for 15-20 mins tossing from time to time, until the eggplant is lightly browned and fragrant. remove the casserole from the oven down to 350.
heat 1 tbsp of olive oil over med heat in a large skillet. add onions and cook, stirring often, until just about tender, about 5 mins. stir in peppers and a gen pinch of salt. cook, stirring often, until the peppers have softened and smell fragrant,5-10 mins. stir in 1/2 the garlic and cook for another min, then season with salt and pepper and transfer to the casserole with thte eggplant. heat the remaining 1 tbsp oil in skillet and ad the squash, more salt to taste, and some freshly ground pepper and cook stirring until squash begins to look translucent, 5- 10 mins, stir in remaining garlic, stir together for a min, or until fragrant, and transfer to casserole. add 1/2 tomatoes to the casserole along with the bay leaf thyme and oregano and more salt to taste. toss everything together, cover and place in the oven. or you can not place in the oven and instead leave out for for a few hrs or refrigerate it for a day.
bake ratatouille for 1-1 1/2 hrs, stirring every 30 mins, until veggies are very soft and fragrant. stir in the remaining tomatoes during the last half hr. the ratatouille should be very soft and juicy. remove bay leaf and stir in basil shortly before serving
