Title:
Greek Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3710537.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 c mayo
2 tbsp Greek vinaigrette
2-4 tbsp chopped red onion
1 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and chopped
1 small tomato, seeded and chopped
1/2 c kalamata olives, sliced
1 banana pepper, seeded and chopped
2 oz crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients and spread on plate.
Top with remaining ingredients.
Chil at least three hours, serve with pita chips.
