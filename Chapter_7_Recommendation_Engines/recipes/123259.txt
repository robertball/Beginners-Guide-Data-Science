Title:
Chicken Noodle Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976130.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package chicken flavored ramen noodles
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup broccoli florets
1 cup cauliflowerets
1 cup sliced celery
1 cup coarsely chopped cabbage
2 medium carrots, thinly sliced
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup fresh or canned bean sprouts
1/2 cup teriyaki or soy sauce

Directions:
Set aside seasoning packet from noodles. Cook noodles according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet or wok, stir-fry chicken in oil for 5-6 minutes or until no longer pink. Add vegetables; stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until crisp-tender.
Drain noodles; add to the pan with contents of seasoning packet and the teriyaki sauce. Stir well. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Darlene Markel
Description: 
Darlene Markel of Sublimity, Oregon relies on Ramen noodles to stretch this appealing stir-fry. 'You can use whatever vegetables you happen to have on hand,' she suggests. 'The dish is different every time I make it.'
