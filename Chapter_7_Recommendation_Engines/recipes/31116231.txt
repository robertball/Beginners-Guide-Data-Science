Title:
Best Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/4 cups milk

Directions:
Blend together dry ingredients in a medium sized mixing bowl.
Stir in milk until most of the lumps are gone.
When griddle is hot enough, pour batter on.  Should make about 6 good sized pancakes, enough for 2 people that are really hungry.
