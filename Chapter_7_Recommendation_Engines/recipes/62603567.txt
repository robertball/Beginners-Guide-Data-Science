Title:
Quinoa with peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/561262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
2 cups chicken broth
1 tbsp butter
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic
1 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp black pepper
3/4 cup frozen peas
1/2 cup grated locatelli cheese
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Melt butter in medium size sauce pan. Add quinoa, stirring constantly for 2 minutes to toast. Add chicken broth, onion, garlic, thyme & black pepper. Cover & let come to a boil. Once boiling add frozen peas. Cover & simmer for 15-20 minutes or until quinoa is done. Stir in 1/4 cup locatelli cheese & parsley. Transfer to serving bowl & top with remaining locatelli cheese.
