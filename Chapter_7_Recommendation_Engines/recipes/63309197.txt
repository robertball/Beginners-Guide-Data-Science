Title:
Luscious Easy Summer Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lemon cake mix (check box for extra ingredients needed)
1 small box lemon instant pudding
2 cups cold milk
1 large container Cool Whip, thawed

Directions:
Grease and flour a 10-inch tube pan.  Set aside.
Prepare cake mix according to package directions.  Cool completely.
Meanwhile, combine lemon pudding with milk and whisk until thickened.  Cover and refrigerate.
After cake has cooled, carefully slice into three equal layers.
Place bottom on serving place and cover with 2/3 cup of lemon pudding.
Place middle layer on top and cover with 2/3 cup of lemon pudding.
Complete with top layer.  Cover entire cake with Cool Whip, including the middle hole of the cake.  Spread remaining pudding in a circle on top of the Cool Whip.
Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Judy in Delaware
Description: 
This is a light, refreshing, very easy-to-make cake.  It's been my son's favorite for more than 20 years.  Perfect for those summer-time gatherings.
