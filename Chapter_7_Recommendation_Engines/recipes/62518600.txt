Title:
Sweet Italian Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511283.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon
6 green onions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
4 (15 ounce) cans green beans, drained
2 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style diced tomatoes
2 pinches dried basil
2 pinches dried oregano
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Line cookie sheet with foil and place bacon on it in a single layer. Cook bacon in oven at recommended temperature for 8-10 minutes until crisp.
Remove bacon from the foil and drain on paper towels. *Do not discard grease.
Pour the grease into a large skillet, add the onions. Cook and stir over medium heat until softened.
Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds more. Remove from the heat.
In a saucepan, combine the green beans, tomatoes, basil, oregano and brown sugar. Crumble in the bacon and add the onion and garlic from the skillet. Warm over medium heat until heated through, 5 to 10 minutes.
