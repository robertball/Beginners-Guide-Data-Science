Title:
Ricotta Almond Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-15oz container ricotta cheese
4 large eggs
1 tsp almond extract
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup veg. oil
1 box white cake mix
3/4 cup heavy cream
Glaze -
2 cups confectioner sugar
1 tsp almond extract
3-4 Tbsp milk
sprinkles or toasted sliced almonds for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Spray 10 inch springform pan with non stick spray
1. Cream together ricotta, eggs, extracts and oil.
2. Add cake mix and heavy cream and mix until well blended.
3. Bake about 60 minutes until center is set. Cool in pan 5 mins and remove pan edges and cool completely.
4. when cool, mix glaze ingredients and pour over top and sprinkle with garnishes.
