Title:
Pickled Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil (used to read salad oil)
1/8 cup vinegar (white or yellow)
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
2 cloves of garlic pressed
1/4 t thyme
1/4 t marjaran
Large pinch of rosemary
1-4.5 oz can or jar of whole mushrooms (can use buttons too)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  I use a glass mayo or pickle jar.  Drain mushroom liquid and then add mushrooms.  Chill overnight.

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
You can tell this is an old recipe when one of the ingredients says Salad Oil instead of Olive Oil!  Got this from my Mother who was the Queen of cocktail parties!
