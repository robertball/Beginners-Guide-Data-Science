Title:
Salmon and Potato Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 peeled and cubed med. to lg. yukon gold (or russet!) type potatoes.
2 or 3 chopped green onions
1 14 oz. can drained pink salmon
1 can evaporated milk
1 can Cream of Celery soup
1 cup shreaded Monterey Jack cheese
2 tsp. liquid smoke seasoning
1 cup canned french fried onion bits
1/2 stick margerine
Salt and peper to taste
salt and peppper to taste

Directions:
Parboil the cubed potatoes until not quite done, drain & place into a heavy casserole dish. Dot with margerine pieces.
Sprinkle the green onion onto the potatoes.
Use a fork to break the salmon into pieces and layer onto the potatoes.
Beat the Celery soup with the evaporated milk and add the smoke seasoning. ( Tip: Pre heat it in the microwave! )
Pour the soup mixture over the potatoes & salmon.
Distribute the cheese over the top of the potatoes & salmon.
Sprinkle french fried onions over the casserole and bake in a 350 degree oven until bubbly.
Put this in front of cold, hungry people and get out of the way
