Title:
Mamma's Yummy Meatballs. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef or veal
3/4 cup of Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 grated reggianno parmeasan cheese
1/2 tsp garlic powder,
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg slightly beaten
1 tsp died oregano
1/4 cup tomato ketchup
4 cups tomatoe sauce for cooking meatballs

Directions:
In large bowl mix, groung veal or beef with breadcrumbs, parmeasan cheese, pepper, salt, garlic powder,egg, oregano and ketchup.
Form meatballs into balls size of golf balls.Place on wax paper lined platter.Refrigerate 30 min.
Heat tomatoe sauce in large pot with a lid  till boiling
Lower heat to low and drop meatballs into sauce.
Simmer on low for 1 hour,stiring every 5min so sauce does not stick.

Submitter: 
BramptonMommyof2
Description: 
I grew up eating these meatballs. Secret is to cook them in tomato sauce and NOT fry them first.I still make these the same way for my family.
