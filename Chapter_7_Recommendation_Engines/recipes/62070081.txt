Title:
Dulce de Leche  Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Crème Anglaise II (recipe from this site)
½ cup dulce de leche, plus more for serving
4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
2 large eggs, at room temperature
1 lb. crustles challah bread, torn into pieces

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 425° and butter a 2-quart shallow baking dish. In a large bowl, disolve the dulce the leche with the crème anglaise and the butter. Whisk in the eggs. Add the challah bread , stirring and gently mashing until soaked. Transfer the bread mixture to the prepared baking dish and bake for about 15 minutes, until firm and bubbling around the edges.
Broil the pudding for 1 to 2 minutes, shifting the dish a few times, until browned all over. Cut the bread pudding into squares, drizzle with dulce de leche and serve.

Submitter: 
Casandra Yaz
Description: 
Extra Dulce de Leche over the bread pudding when serving it, makes this recipe extra special.
