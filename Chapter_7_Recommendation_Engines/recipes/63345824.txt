Title:
Strawberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup canola oil
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1.5 cup chopped strawberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) oil an 8 cup muffin tin, or use paper liners.
In a small bowl, combine oil, milk, vanilla extract, and egg. Beat lightly. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, baking powder, cinnamon, and sugar. Toss in chopped strawberries and stir to coat with flour. Pour in milk mixture and stir together.
Fill muffin cups. Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 20 minutes, or until the tops bounce back from the touch. Cool 10 minutes and remove from pans.

Submitter:

Description: 
Strawberry muffins that can be made with fresh or frozen strawberries. If using frozen berries,  thaw slightly, then chop with a knife.Modified from: Strawberry Muffins
