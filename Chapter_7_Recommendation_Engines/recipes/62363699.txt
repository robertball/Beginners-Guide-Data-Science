Title:
Marinade for Steak I - modified Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp garlic - minced
1 Tbsp dijon mustard
4 Tbsp vegetable oil
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp black pepper
1.5 oz onions - minced
1 serving beef bouillon (better than bouillon brand)

Directions:
Microwave boil 1/4 cup water in large measuring cup, add bouillon and mix until dissolved.
Add remaining ingredients, stir well and pour over meat.
Marinade for an hour or more in sealed container.
Give it a shake every now and then to remix.

Submitter: 
Andre
Description: 
Great meat marinade. This makes enough to marinate about 2 lbs of meat.Modified from: Marinade for Steak I
