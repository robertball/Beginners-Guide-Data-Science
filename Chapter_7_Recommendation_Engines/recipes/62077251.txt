Title:
MELT-IN-YOUR-MOUTH Beef Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 lb chuck roast
28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
15 once can diced tomatoes
1 green pepper cut in large pieces
2 onions cut in wedges and sliced
1 cup mushrooms cut in quarters
1 cup dry red wine (Cabernet or Merlot)
3 tablespoons chopped garlic
1 teaspoon fennel seed
1 teapoon thyme
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 tablesppon sugar
1 1/2 cups water
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Heat olive oil in frying pan on top of stove on medium high heat. Add chopped garlic and chuck roast. Season roast with salt and pepper to taste. Brown on all sides.  Add roast and garlic to slow cooker along with the remaining ingredients. Cook on low heat for 6 hours. Serve with garlic mashed potatoes.

Description: 
Tender Roast Beef cooked cacciatore style in your slow cooker with lots of bell peppers, onions and mushrooms in a hearty red wine tomato sauce. Serve with garlic mashed potatoes and garlic bread.
