Title:
Creamy Corn Crab Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium onion, chopped
2 tbsp butter
3 cups chicken broth
3 cups frozen corn
3 potatoes, peeled and diced
1 can (6oz) crabmeat, drained, flaked and cartilage removed or 1 cup flaked imitation crabmeat
1 Cup milk
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender. Add the broth, corn and potatoes; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 min. Remove from the heat; cool slightly.
In a blender or food processor, puree half of the corn mixture. Return to pan. Stir in the crab, milk, salt, and pepper; cook over low heat until heated through (do not boil).

Submitter: 
Tracy Shaw Jenkins
Description: 
Creamy soup thats yummy in the winter with some toast.
