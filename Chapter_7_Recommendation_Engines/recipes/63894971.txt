Title:
breaded blue-cream cheese jalapenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. Cream Cheese
2.5 oz. Blue Cheese
3 diced Fresh Basil Leaves
12 Jalapenos (Cored & Washed)
3 Eggs
4 oz. Panko Flakes

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and blue cheese together thoroughly,add in diced basil.
Stuff jalapenos with cheese mixture.
Crack eggs into a bowl and beat.
Dip jalapenos in egg then panko, repeat until jalapenos are thoroughly coated.
Place on a baking sheet, cook for 15 minutes at 350 or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Haro Foodie 01
Description: 
Light, Nutritious appetizer but still a very good compliment as a side-dish for a traditional Mexican dinner.
