Title:
Strawberry Shortcake Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4463807.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups nonfat plain yogurt
2 cups milk
2 cups fresh or frozen unsweetened strawberries
6 crushed shortbread cookies
4 teaspoons granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (e.g., Splenda Â®)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
6 tablespoons whey protein powder (optional)

Directions:
Place the yogurt, milk, strawberries, cookies, sweetener, vanilla, and optional protein powder into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.

Submitter: 
trinityman
Description: 
A yummy breakfast treat or a low-guilt dessert. You can substitute vanilla wafers for shortbread cookies if you wish.
