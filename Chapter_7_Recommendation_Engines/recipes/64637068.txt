Title:
Creamy Rice Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked brown rice
4 cups water
pinch salt
(optional coconut sugar, ground cinnamon, frozen blueberries)

Directions:
1) Grind brown rice to a fine meal.
2) Put rice, water and salt into pot and bring to boil whisking continuously then reduce heat to low; cover; simmer for 10-15 mins.  Whisking occasionally.
3)Add optionals if desired.
