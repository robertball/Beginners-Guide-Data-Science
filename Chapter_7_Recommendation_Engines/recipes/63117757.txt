Title:
Mini Cupcake Cupcake Toppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793639.jpg
Ingredients:
Miniature Reeses Cups
Frosting of choice
Decorations of choice

Directions:
Remove the outer foil wrapping from the Reeses Cups, but leave them in the paper cup.
Using the frosting and decorating tip of your choice, pipe a small swirl of frosting on top of the Reeses Cups.
Decorate as desired.
Place on top of your cupcakes for an edible and totally adorable decoration.
