Title:
Alexander's Juicy Roast Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 to 4 pound) Roast Beef
1/2 cup Water
1/2 cup firmly packed Brown Sugar
1 envelope Onion Soup Mix
1 Onion, chopped
1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder, or to taste
Salt and Pepper to taste
8 Onion Hamburger Buns (or regular Hamburger Buns if you prefer)

Directions:
Place roast in crock pot.
Stir remaining ingredients (except the buns, of course) together and pour over roast.
Cook for 8-10 hours stirring occasionally.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe will make about 8 Roast Beef Sandwiches!
