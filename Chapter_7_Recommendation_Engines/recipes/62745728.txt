Title:
Pancake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/631716.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Cups All-purpose Flour  (3 lbs.)
2 1/2 Cups Instant Dry Milk
1/2 Cup Sugar
1/4 Cup Baking Power
2 Tbsp. Salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Stir together to blend. Put in a large airtight container such as tupperware. Store in a cook, dry place. Use within 6-8 months. Makes about 13 cups of mix.

Submitter: 
deltasky
Description: 
This is just a simple mix that I use. This mix works for both waffles and pancakes.
