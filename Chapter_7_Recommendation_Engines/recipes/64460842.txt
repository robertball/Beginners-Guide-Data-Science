Title:
Roasted Sweet Potato Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2419597.jpg
Ingredients:
Salad
1 cup quinoa, rinsed
2 small sweet potatoes (yellow or purple)
1/2 cucumber, seeded and diced
1 yellow pepper, diced
1 red pepper, diced
1 cup broccoli, chopped
Dressing
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
juice of 1/2 lemon
juice of 1/2 lime
2 tbsp maple syrup
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 deg F
Peel and cube sweet potato, then toss in 2 tbsp olive oil and roast for 20-25 min until tender, set aside to cool
Cook quinoa, set aside to cool
While quinoa and sweet potatoes are cooling, chop red pepper, yellow pepper, cucumber, broccoli
Toss all salad ingredients, store in refrigerator until ready to serve
Mix all dressing ingredients, store separately in refrigerator until ready to serve
Add dressing, top with chopped cilantro and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Melanie McClare
Description: 
Purple sweet potatoes add a beautiful colour to the mix in this healthy, fresh tasting, zesty salad!
