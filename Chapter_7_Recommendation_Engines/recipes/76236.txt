Title:
Homemade Chai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984257.jpg
Ingredients:
3 slices peeled fresh ginger
3 (1 gram) packets SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener
12 black peppercorns
3 whole cloves
2 cardamom pods
1 (3 inch) cinnamon stick
2 cups water
2 Darjeeling tea bags
1/2 cup 2% reduced-fat milk

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil.  Remove from heat. Add teabags, cover and steep 5 minutes.  Remove spices and tea bags with a slotted spoon and discard.
Add milk and cook over medium heat until thoroughly heated.  Do not boil.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Enjoy the exotic flavor of Chai tea without leaving the comfort of your own home with this easy recipe.
