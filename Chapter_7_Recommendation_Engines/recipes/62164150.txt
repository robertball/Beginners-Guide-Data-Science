Title:
Good Blueberry Coffeecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. all purpose flour
1 1/4 C. sugar
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking powder
2/3 C. butter or margarine (room temperature)
1 C. milk
2 eggs
Topping
1 C. blueberries
1/4 C. sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Grease a 9 x 13 baking pan and preheat the oven to 350.
In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar salt, baking powder. Using a pastry blender or two knives, cut in the butter or margarine until the mixture resembles small peas. (like pie crust)
Blend the milk and eggs together and add to the dry ingredients. Stir with a fork just until blended. Do not beat.
Spread into the prepared pan.
Sprinkle 1 cup of blueberries over top
Blend the 1/4 cup sugar and cinnamon together and sprinkle over all.
bake 350 for 40 minutes. Test for doneness with toothpick.

Submitter:

Description: 
Using basic ingredients found in the kitchen, this recipe is easy to assemble and bake, especially when blueberries are in season at their peak.
I am always asked for a copy of my blueberry coffeecake recipe  and I'm happy to share it with you.
