Title:
Fajita Salt-Free Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cornstarch
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon sugar
3/4 teaspoon crushed salt-free chicken bouillon cube (optional)
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon oregano (preferably mexican oregano)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.  Store in an airtight container.  About 2 tablespoons = 1 packet of seasoning mix.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
An easy, inexpensive, delicious and salt-free version of the favorite seasoning mix.  No need to buy those seasoning packets anymore.
