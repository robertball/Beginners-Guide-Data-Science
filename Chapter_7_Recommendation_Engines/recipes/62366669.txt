Title:
Chicken Enchilada Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups chicken broth
8 (6 inch) corn tortillas, cut into 1/2 inch strips
1 (10 oz) green enchilada sauce
1 can of Rotel tomato & chilies
1 (10 ounce) can red enchilada sauce
1 teaspoon ground cumin
4 cooked,boneless chicken-shredded
1 cup 1% milk
Garnish with:
1 tomato, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced, if you like.
Chopped green onions and cilantro.
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
tortilla chips, crumbled
1 tsp. granulated garlic
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine chicken stock and tortilla strips in a large pot. Cook over medium heat until strips soften and broth thickens somewhat.
Stir in enchilada sauces and cumin,can of Rotel. Stir in chicken and milk; heat through.
Divide among four bowls and garnish with diced tomato, jalapeno pepper, and cheese, cilantro, green onions. Serve with tortilla chips.
