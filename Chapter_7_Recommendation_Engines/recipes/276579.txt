Title:
Marinated Fruit Salad  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
3 kiwis, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices
1 cup seedless grapes
1 green apple, cored and chopped
2 Fuyu persimmons, peeled and thinly sliced
3/4 cup pineapple-orange-banana juice
1/2 cup orange marmalade
2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
2 bananas

Directions:
Combine pineapple, kiwis, grapes, apple, and persimmons in a large serving bowl.
Stir together pineapple-orange-banana juice, marmalade, and ginger in a small bowl. Pour over fruit and toss to combine. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 6 hours.
Slice bananas. Remove fruit salad from refrigerator and add bananas; toss gently to combine.

Description: 
This tropical fruit salad with pineapple, persimmons, kiwi, apple, and grapes is marinated in orange marmalade and juice.
