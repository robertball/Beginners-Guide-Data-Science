Title:
Orange Banana Smoothie with Chia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3875882.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup vanilla-flavored or plain Greek yogurt
3 mandarin or clementine oranges - peeled, pith removed, sectioned
1/2 sliced, frozen banana
1 tablespoon truRoots® Organic Chia Seeds
3 ice cubes

Directions:
Place yogurt, oranges, banana, chia seeds and ice cubes in blender container. Cover and process until smooth.

Submitter: 
truRoots®
Description: 
For an on-the-go breakfast or after-school snack this orange and banana smoothie with chia seeds can't be beat.
