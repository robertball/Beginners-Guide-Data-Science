Title:
No Bake Raspberry Peanut Butter Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3084283.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup NuttZo or any nut butter
1/4 cup light honey
1 cup GF rolled oats
1/4 cup golden flax meal
1 tbsp chia seeds
1/2 cup raspberries

Directions:
In a bowl, melt honey & nut butter in the microwave 30 seconds
Add remaining ingredients to the bowl & combine
Line a square baking dish with parchment paper
Add mixture and press & flatten using fingers into corners
Set in the fridge 3 hours
Remove & cute into bars
Store in a container in the freezer for up to 1 week

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Deliciously gooey Raspberry Peanut Butter Granola Bars made with fresh raspberries, peanut butter, oats and chia seeds. A healthy homemade no bake snack that takes just 5 minutes to make!
