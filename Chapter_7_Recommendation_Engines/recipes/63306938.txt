Title:
One Bowl Chocolate Cake III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups white sugar
7 cups all-purpose flour
3 tsp. yellow food coloring
2 tbls. baking powder
2 tbls. baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
8 eggs
4 cups milk
2 cups vegetable oil
3 tbls. vanilla extract
4 cups boiling water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two long cafeteria pans & one half pan.
In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, food color, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
