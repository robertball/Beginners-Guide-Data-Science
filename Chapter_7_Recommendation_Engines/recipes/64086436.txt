Title:
Mango Raspberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz fresh or thawed and drained frozen mango chunks
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tbsp fresh lime juice
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
2/3 cups skim milk
1/2 cup fresh or thawed and drained frozen raspberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Spray a 2 quart baking dish with cooking spray.
In a medium bowl, stir together the mango, nutmeg, and lime juice.  Set aside.
In another medium bowl, mix flour, brown sugar, baking powder, salt, and milk until blended.  Pour batter into baking dish.  Spoon mango mixture and raspberries over batter.
Bake 40 to 45 minutes until top is golden brown.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
This fun cobbler is easier to get every last drop of juice thanks to having the crust on the bottom rather than the top.
