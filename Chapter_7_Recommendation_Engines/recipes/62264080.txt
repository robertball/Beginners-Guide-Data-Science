Title:
Imperial Butter Tart Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/340230.jpg
Ingredients:
Bottom:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup butter
Filling:
1/2 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 tablespoon flour
1 cup raisins

Directions:
For bottom: Combine flour and sugar. Cut in butter until it resembles crums.  Press mixture into a 9 x 13 inch pan.  Bake at 325ºF for 15 minutes.
For Filling: Cream butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy.  Add eggs one at a time, mixing each one in well.  Add vanilla, baking powder and flour.
Sprinkle raisins over cooled bottom.  Spread filling over top and bake at 325ºF for 25-30 minutes.  Cool until set.
Can be put into the fridge an hour before cutting into squares for easier slicing.
1-1/4 hours time is allocated for this recipe which includes the prep time.
