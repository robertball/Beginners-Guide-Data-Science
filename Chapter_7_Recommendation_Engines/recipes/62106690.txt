Title:
Linda"s Zestys Mustard Potatoe Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lbs peeled boiled potatoes
6 hard boiled eggs
1 large green pepper
1 large onion (sweet onion works best)
2 Cucumbers Peeled
1/2 Jar of Mircle Whip salad dressing
1 1 oz jar of mustard Hot dog relish (sweet kind)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cooked Potatoes till tender but not mussy,cool, cut up into large chunks,put into large bowl
add hard boiled eggs,onions,green peppers,and cucumbers,and mix
in a medium bowl, mix mircle whip and relish,
add to potatoe mixture and mix well
cover and chill than enjoy.

Submitter: 
Linda Kirchen
Description: 
My family loves salads not just in the summer but anytime. I took my grandmother potatoe salad recipe and add a little zip to it and my family just eats it up the twist is the mustard relish that I put in it
