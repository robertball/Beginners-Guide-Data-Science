Title:
Pie Crust III - Whole Wheat version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat "white" flour
2/3 cup canola oil
6 to 8 tablespoons ice water

Directions:
Mix together flour, oil, and ice water.
Divide dough in half.  Roll each piece between two pieces of plastic wrap.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
I needed a whole wheat crust and was out of shortening so I subbed whole wheat white flour.  Needed a bit more water for it to come together, though.Modified from: Pie Crust III
