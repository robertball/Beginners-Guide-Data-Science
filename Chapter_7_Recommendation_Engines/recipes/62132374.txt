Title:
Hot Dog Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg elbow macaroni (8 oz)
6 hot dogs, boilded and cooled
1 small green pepper, chopped fine
2 pimentoes, chopped
1 can peas (8 oz) drained or use frozen
3 scallions, sliced
1/2t salt
pepper to taste
1/2 c mayo
1T vinegar
dash Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions, drain and cool slightly.
Cook and slice hot dogs into 1/4 inch rounds and place in bowl with macaroni.
Add the remaining ingredients and blend well.

Submitter: 
Carolina
Description: 
Even kids who like hotdogs like this salad!
