Title:
Colorful Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Slaw
1/4 head each, red and white cabbage, cored and thinly sliced
1/2 red bell pepper, cored, seeded and thinly sliced
1/2 yellow bell pepper, cored seeded and thinly sliced
3 tablespoons shelled snap peas, cut in thin strips
1/4 cup Persian cucumber, thinly sliced
1/4 cup grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
1 small onion, chopped
Dressing
3 Tb Red wine vinegar
1 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp Greek yogurt
1 tsp Celery seed
Salt & Pepper to taste
Optional: 1 tsp sugar or stevia
Optional: garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Combine slaw ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Combine dressing ingredients in a small mixing bowl and stir until smooth. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
