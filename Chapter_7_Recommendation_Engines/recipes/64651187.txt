Title:
Diabetic BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539540.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Large Cans (29 ounces)Tomato Puree
4 TBS Mustard
1/4 Cup Honey
2 TBS White Vinegar
1/2 Tsp All Spice
4 TBS Worcestershire
4 TBS Lemon Juice
4 TSP  Black Pepper
2 TBS Minced Garlic
2 TBS Minced Onions  (Note:  We have some picky eaters so we opted for Minced Onions.  If you like regular onions you can saute some down instead of using the minced)

Directions:
Directions:
In a large pot add two large cans of tomato puree, minced garlic, and onions and simmer for 8 minutes
Slowly stir in remaining ingredients and mix well.  Allow to simmer 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Michelle Hughes
Description: 
This is a tangy BBQ sauce that goes great with chicken, pork chops, or anything you want with BBQ sauce
