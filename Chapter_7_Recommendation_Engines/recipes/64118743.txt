Title:
Chicken Denise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. thin sliced chicken cutlets
1/8 tsp. salt
3/4 of a medium Miracle Whip
1 C. Parmesan cheese
1/8 tsp. oregano
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Mix Miracle Whip with Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and oregano. Spread evenly over chicken in a baking dish. Broil for 10 minutes, then bake for at 400 for 20 minutes.
