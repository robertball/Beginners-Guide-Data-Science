Title:
Giant Double Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp vanilla
3 cup chocolate chips
2 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Cream butter with brown sugar. Mix in eggs, sour cream and vanilla.
Melt 1 1/2 cup chocolate chips and blend into creamed mixture.
Stir flour with salt, baking powder and baking soda. Stir until smooth.
Fold in remaining chocolate chips. Bake at  350° for 8 minutes.
