Title:
Genevieve's Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. Old Fashioned Oatmeal
1 C. sliced almonds
1 C. shredded coconut, loosely packed
1/2 C. flax seed meal
3 T.  unsalted butter
1/4 C. honey
3 T. agave nectar
1/4 C. light brown sugar, lightly packed
1 1/2 t. pure vanilla extract
1/4 t kosher salt
1/2 C. pitted dates
1/2 C. dried apricots
1/2 C. dried cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Butter 8x12 pan. Toss oatmeal, almonds and coconut together on a sheet pan and bake 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally until browned.
Transfer to bowl and add flax seed meal.
Reduce oven to 300.
Place butter, honey, brown sugar, vanilla, and salt in a small saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat. Cook and stir for a minute.
Add dates, apricots and cranberries and stir well
Pour the mixture into the buttered pan.
Wet your fingers and lightly press the mixture evenly into the pan.
Bake for 25-30 minutes until lightly golden brown.
Cool at least 2-3 hours.
Cut into bars or break up into crumbles.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This is the perfect of sweet and savory. Not too sweet and perfect for breakfast or a snack.
