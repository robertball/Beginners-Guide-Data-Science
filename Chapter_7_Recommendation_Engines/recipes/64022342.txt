Title:
Cabbage, Macaraoni and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups elbow pasta
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 medium onion, halved and thinly sliced
1/2 small head of Savoy cabbage - thinly sliced
2 garlic cloves - minced
1/4 tsp. pepper
3 - 14.5 oz. cans of chicken broth
2 cans of cannellini beans - rinsed and drained
Grated Parmesan

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions.
In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat.
Add cabbage, onion, garlic and pepper and cook until cabbage begins to wilt - about 6 minutes.
Stir in broth, beans and 1 1/2 cups of water.
Heat to boiling.
Stir in drained pasta to cabbage mixture and heat through.
Sprinkle with Parmesan before serving if you lik.
