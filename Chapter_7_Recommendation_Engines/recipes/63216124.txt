Title:
No Bake Sweet Marie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Peanut Butter
1/2 cup Corn Syrup
1/2 cup Brown Sugar
1 Tbsp Butter
1 cup Peanuts
2 cups Krispie Rice Cereal
1 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
In a sauce pan, melt together first four ingredients until boiling.
Add the peanuts and cereal.  Stir.
Pack into an 8x8 pan (sprayed with cooking spray)
Sprinkle entire top with chocolate chips, let melt (a few minutes) then spread evenly.
Refrigerate to harden chocolate, but it's not necessary to keep in the fridge (easier to cut when room temperature)

Submitter: 
FourMs
Description: 
This is a Canadian chocolate bar favorite.  Peanut butter, peanuts and chocolate...what can go wrong?  Soooo delicious and super easy!
