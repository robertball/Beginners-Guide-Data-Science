Title:
R & R Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Cooked rice (no minute rice)
2 C. Cooked and mashed rutabaga
1 Tbsp. butter
1/2 C. Milk
1/4 C. Sugar
1 Tsp. Salt
1/8 Tsp. Pepper
Dash of nutmeg
2 eggs, separated

Directions:
Preheat the oven and butter a 2 quart casserole dish.
In a large mixing bowl mix the rutabaga with the milk, butter sugar salt and pepper.
Stir in the cooked rice.
Beat the egg yolks and  blend in the nutmeg then fold into the rutabaga mixture.
Beat the egg whites until peaks form and fold into the mixture
Bake in buttered casserole dish for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
Serve.

Rutabaga and Rice baked in a casserole dish for a tasty side.
