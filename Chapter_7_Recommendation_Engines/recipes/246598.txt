Title:
White Chocolate Pretzel Crisps® Sparklers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3682360.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package white chocolate chips
1 (7.2 ounce) package Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
Gold sugar sprinkles
White sugar pearls

Directions:
Microwave white chocolate chips in a microwave safe bowl until beginning to melt. Stir. Microwave another few seconds until completely melted.
Dip different parts of Original Pretzel Crisps® into the chocolate. Coat with gold sprinkles or white sugar pearls and set aside to cool and harden. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
Description: 
White chocolate and sparkle-y sprinkles on Pretzel Crisps® make festive party snacks.
