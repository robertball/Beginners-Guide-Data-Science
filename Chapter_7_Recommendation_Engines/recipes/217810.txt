Title:
Rosemary Lemon Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980197.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Lemons (Juice Only)
24 ounces Club Soda
4 Rosemary Sprigs
4 tablespoons Sugar
4 ounces SauzaÂ® Blanco Tequila

Directions:
Combine lemons, club soda, sugar and tequila in pitcher. Mix well. Refrigerate until cold. Place 1 rosemary sprig in each of the 4 serving cups. Pour margarita and drink cold or iced.

Submitter: 
Sauza
Description: 
The rosemary infuses the flavor of the tequila while the lemon provides a hint of sweetness in this refreshing margarita that goes perfectly with all kinds of appetizers and grilled foods.
