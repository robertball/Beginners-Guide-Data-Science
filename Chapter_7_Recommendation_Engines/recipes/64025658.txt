Title:
Creamy Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion - chopped
2 T. butter
2 cans (14.5 oz.)diced tomatoes
2 cans (103/4 oz.) tomato soup - undiluted
1 1/2 c. milk
1 t. sugar
1/2-1 t. dried basil
1/2-1 t. paprika
1/8-1/4 t. garlic powder
1 pkg. cream cheese

Directions:
In a saucepan saute onion in butter til tender.
Stir in remainder of ingredients (except cream cheese) and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat. Add cubed cream cheese, cover and heat until melted.
Serve immediately.
