Title:
Buffalo Chicken Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts cut into strips (about 1 pound)
1 1/2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
3 Tbsp Medium Buffalo Wing Sauce
3 Tbsp Move Over Butter
2 Tbsp Whole Wheat Flour
1/4 tsp Salt
1 tsp Ground Black Pepper
1 tsp Ground Cayenne Pepper

Directions:
Mix flour and peppers, set aside.  Salt the chicken.  Heat oil and fry chicken until cooked though.  Sprinkle flour mix on the chicken and toss to coat.  Add spread and wing sauce to the pan.  When the spread has melted toss to coat the chicken evenly.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Clover
Description: 
I wanted a buffalo chicken recipe that would cook faster and with less calories and fat than wings.  Looking over a few different recipes, this is what I came up with.
