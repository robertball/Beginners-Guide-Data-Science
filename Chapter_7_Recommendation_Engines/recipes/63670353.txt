Title:
Cornbread Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1019521.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Martha White Sweet Cornbread Mix
1 can (drained) whole kernel corn
1 package sausage (or any meat works)
1/4 cup oil
1 cup cheese
5 additional eggs (not the eggs for the mix)
1 tomato

Directions:
Preheat oven per package instructions on mix.
Put oil in skillet (cast iron best)
Dice up sausage and cook to golden brown outside(this preheats your skillet for the cornbread)
Make mix according to instructions.
Stir in corn and 3/4 cup of cheese into cornbread mix.
Pour over sausage in skillet and spread evenly.
Crack 5 eggs on top.
Place sliced tomato on top.
Sprinkle 1/4 cup cheese on top of all
Bake A LITTLE LONGER than time on mix box due to all toppings.
Take out of oven and let rest for 5 mins then serve.
