Title:
CHEFMATHEMATIKS Super Lemon Mayo Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/597836.jpg
Ingredients:
8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (or however many you want; Minimum 4), 1/3 cup mayonnaise, 3/4 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs, 1 Teaspoon of Garlic Powder, 1 Teaspoon of Onion Powder, 1 Teaspoon of Cajun Powder, & 1 Teaspoon of Cumin, 1 Lemon

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease a shallow baking dish,
2. Mix Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Cajun Powder &amp; Cumin Powder into the Mayo,
3. Coat chicken breasts with a small brush on all sides with mayonnaise, and roll in bread crumbs until coated. Place coated breasts into the prepared pan,
4. Bake uncovered for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chicken is no longer pink in the middle, and the juices run clear,
5.Before serving Once sprinkle the fresh lemon juice on the tops and ENJOY!
