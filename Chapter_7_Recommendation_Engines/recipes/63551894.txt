Title:
Healthy Tabouli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/970343.jpg
Ingredients:
- 2 cups cracked wheat (bulgur)
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 cucumber, chopped into small pieces
- 1/2 cup red onion, chopped into small pieces
- 2 ripe tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- 2 cups fresh chopped parsley
- 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
- 1 15oz can chickpeas (optional; for a heartier version)
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup sunflower oil
- 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- black pepper, to desired taste

Directions:
Soak the cracked wheat (bulgar) in the hot water until the water is absorbed, about 30 minutes. Drain any excess water, and squeeze dry.
In a large bowl, prepare the vegetables as noted above. Add in oils, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Mix gently.
Slowly add in the cracked wheat and mix gently.
Serve chilled.

Submitter:

Description: 
This tabouli is unique and absolutely delicious!

Consider adding chickpeas for a heartier meal and extra protein!
