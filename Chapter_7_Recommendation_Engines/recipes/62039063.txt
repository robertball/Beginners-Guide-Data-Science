Title:
Coffee Liqueur Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tsp. Instant Coffee Granules
1/4 cup Hot Water

Directions:
Combine coffee granules with hot water, stir until dissolved.

Submitter: 
Stephany Brown
Description: 
Use instant coffee and water to make an Extract or strong infusion to use to flavor your recipe.  I have used instant coffee to soak the layers of cakes and in Tiramisu.
