Title:
American Cheese and Tomato Omelet with Fresh Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4506755.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 tablespoon milk
salt and ground pepper to taste
2 slices american cheese
3 slices tomato
8 basil leaves

Directions:
Heat a 6-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat; pour egg mixture into the hot skillet, tilting so egg mixture covers the entire bottom of skillet. Cover cook egg mixture, 2 to 5 minutes, while checking for firmest.
When omelet is firm enough; lay out cheese, tomato, and basil.
Cover cook egg mixture, up to 5 minutes, while checking for firmest.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
Nothing like summertime when you can use fresh ingredients from the garden. Tomato and Basil just go together in a omelet.
