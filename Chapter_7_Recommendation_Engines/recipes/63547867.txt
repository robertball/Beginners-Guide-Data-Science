Title:
Hot Ham and Swiss Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups (8 oz) shredded swiss cheese
1 1/2 cups finely chopped ham (6 oz)
1/2 cup sliced green onions
2 TBL all-purpose flour
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 TBL spicy brown mustard
1/2 tsp caraway seeds, slightly crushed
Townhouse flipsides crackers

Directions:
1. In medium bowl, stir together cheese, ham, green onions and flour. Add mayonnaise, mustard and caraway seeds. Mix until combined. Spread in 9" pie plate.
2. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees about 25 minutes or until heated through and bubbly around edges.
3. Serve warm with crackers.
