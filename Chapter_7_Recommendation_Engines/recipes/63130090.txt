Title:
Italian Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hot and mild Italian sausage mix
3 clove garlic, minced
1 potato diced
1 onion diced
4 cups broth
1 28 oz can Italian-style stewed tomatoes
1 cup sliced carrots
2 cups cannelloni beans ( soaked over night and precooked.
2 small zucchini, cubed
1/2 cup noodles
2 cups spinach - packed, rinsed cut fine
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Soak beans overnight and precook the next day.
Saut√© onion and during the last few minutes add garlic. In a stockpot or Dutch oven, brown sausage.  Stir in  broth, tomatoes and carrots, and season with pepper. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
Stir in drain beans and zucchini. Cover, and simmer another 15 minutes, or until zucchini is tender.
Remove from heat, and add spinach.  Replace lid allowing the heat from the soup to cook the spinach leaves.  Soup is ready to serve after 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lynn
Description: 
This soup is easy to put together, and the flavor of the spicy sausage is balanced nicely by Great Northern beans, zucchini, fresh spinach, and carrots.  Makes a delicious winter supper.Modified from: Italian Sausage Soup
