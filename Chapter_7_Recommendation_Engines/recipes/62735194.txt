Title:
Sauce of Eden Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz Bertolli Vidalia Onion Sauce
2 Tbs EVOO (Extra Virgin Olive Oil)
1/4 cup merlot (red wine)
1 habanero pepper (fresh)
1/2 Tbs fresh oregano, chopped
1 Tbs fresh basil, chopped

Directions:
Halve habanero pepper, set aside.
Heat EVOO in large saucepan, saute garlic, add merlot, reduce.
Add sauce, oregano, basil, all habanero seeds into sauce.
Simmer for approx. 3 hours over low heat.

Submitter: 
Chef Eden
Description: 
A deep and robust red sauce with a natural, flavorful heat, perfect for spaghetti, lasagna, and penne.
