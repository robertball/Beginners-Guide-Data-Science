Title:
Tuna Mushroom Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991995.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package wide noodles, cooked and drained
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained
1 (4 ounce) can mushroom stems and pieces, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 1/3 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup crushed saltines
3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
paprika, tomato slices and fresh thyme, optional

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine noodles, tuna and mushrooms. Combine soup, milk, salt and pepper; pour over noodle mixture and mix well. Pour into a greased 2-1/2-qt. baking dish. Combine saltines and butter; sprinkle over noodles.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes or until heated through. If desired, sprinkle with paprika and garnish with tomato and thyme.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
Serve this casserole when you're short on time and need something hearty and comforting.
