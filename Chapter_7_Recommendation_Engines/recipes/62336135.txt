Title:
Whole Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 TBSP yeast
1 cup milk
1/4 cup shortening
1/3 cup honey
1 TBSP salt
2 eggs
6 to 8 cups whole wheat flour

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water with 1/2 tsp sugar
Add milk, shortening, honey salt and eggs.
Beat in 3 cups of flour.
Place sponge in a warm place for 1 hour.
Stir in flour to make a good dough.
Knead.
Let rise until doubled.
Punch down and shape into loaves.
Allow to rise.
Bake at 350 for 40 minutes to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
SIXMITTS
Description: 
This bread uses a sponge and is 100% whole wheat.
