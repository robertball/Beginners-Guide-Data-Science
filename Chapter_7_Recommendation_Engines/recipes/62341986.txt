Title:
Stuffed Chicken Breasts with artichoke hearts, feta cheese, capers and black olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken breasts
1/2 can artichoke hearts (in water)
1/2 can ( sliced black olives)
1/4 jar capers
1 small container of feta cheese.
Italian salad dressing (or the apprpriate ingredients)

Directions:
Marinate the chicken for one to two days in italian dressing (or ingredients).  Once marinated throughly pound out into 1/2 inch thickness.
Mix the feta cheese, artichoke hearts, olives and capers together into a "tapenade" and stuff the chicken (meaning roll the ingredients into the chicken breast)
Use tooth picks to hold the rolled chicken together while baking.
Bake in 350F for 30 minutes in a pan greased with olive oil!
