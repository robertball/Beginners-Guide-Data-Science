Title:
English Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c flour
2 T sugar
1 T baking powder
1/2 t salt
1/3 c raisins
6 T margarine softened
1 egg beaten
1/2 c milk

Directions:
Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.  Cut up raising and put softened butter in the bowl.  In a pastry blender mix in dry ingredients and butter mixture until very crumbly. l Make a well in the center of the batter.  Mix the milk and egg together in another bowl, then put into well adn stir the two together.  Handle batter as little as possible.  Grease cookie pan and use a tablespoon to drop batter onto it.  Makes spoonfuls an inch apart.  Preheat oven to 400'F and bake scones for 15 minutes.  Have with tea and enjoy.
