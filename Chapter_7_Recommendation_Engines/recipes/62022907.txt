Title:
Shoo-Fly Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
1 cup shortening
2 cups sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup baking molasses
1 tsp soda dissolved in 2 cups warm water

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients to form a crumb mixture.  Take out 1 cup of crumbs for topping.  Mix baking molasses, soda, and water, add to dry ingredients.  Pour in a 9 x 13 inch pan and sprinkle cup of crumbs over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
