Title:
Oven Baked Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4lb pork ribs
1 Tbsp sea salt
1 Tbsp paprika
1 Tbsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp onion powder
1 Tbsp chili powder
2 tsp ground black pepper
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Place the oven rack to middle of oven and preheat to 250 F.
Combine all spice ingredients together in small bowl and stir to combine. rub both sides of ribs with the spice rub and place the ribs on baking sheet, rib side down. Bake ribs for about 2 hours 45 mins , turning occosinally. Broil ribs for 5-10 mins to brown meat side . Enjoy
