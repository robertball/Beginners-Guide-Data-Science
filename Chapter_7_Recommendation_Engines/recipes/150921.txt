Title:
Smirnoff Lemon Drop Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979869.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1/4 ounce triple sec
3/4 ounce lemon juice
Sugar

Directions:
In a shaker with ice, add Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, triple sec, and lemon juice.
Shake well.
Strain into chilled martini glass rimmed with sugar (optional).

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Clean and ultrasmooth Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka gives this cocktail a clear, pure taste unmuddied by impurities or harshness.
