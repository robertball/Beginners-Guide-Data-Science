Title:
Bleu Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz. Sour Cream
1 pkg. dry onion soup mix
2 oz. crumbled bleu cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients well. Serve with your favorite chips or spread onto burgers before serving.

Submitter: 
Jessica53214
Description: 
This is a great dip. It adds an extra dimension to the standard onion soup dip. It tastes wonderful as a dip for chips but it also gives burgers a nice touch too.
