Title:
Low-fat Baked Teriyaki Boneless Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon cold water
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 Boneless skinless chicken breast

Directions:
In a small saucepan over med-low heat, combine the cornstarch, cold water, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ginger and ground black pepper. Let simmer, stirring frequently, until sauce thickens and bubbles.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place chicken pieces in a heavy aluminum foil lined 8X8 inch baking dish. Pour sauce over chicken. Cover with dish with aluminum.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Turn pieces over, and bake for another 15 minutes, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
