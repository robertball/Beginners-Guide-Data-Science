Title:
Yankee Avocado Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 Hass avocado, halved, pitted and peeled
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
4 (4 ounce) hamburger patties, 1/2inch thick
4 hamburger buns
4 lettuce leaves
8 slices tomato

Directions:
In bowl, combine avocado and lemon juice, mash until creamy. Stir in horseradish, red onion and salt. Place plastic wrap on surface of Yankee Avocado Spread; set aside.
Over medium-hot coals, grill hamburger patties for 4 to 5minutes on each side or to desired degree of doneness. Open up hamburger buns and grill.
On each grilled bun bottom, layer lettuce and tomato slices. Top with grilled hamburger patty and spoon on Yankee Avocado Spread. Place on bun top. Serve.

Submitter: 
Fresh Chilean Hass Avocados
Description: 
Add a little zip to your hamburgers with this spicy horseradish and avocado spread.
