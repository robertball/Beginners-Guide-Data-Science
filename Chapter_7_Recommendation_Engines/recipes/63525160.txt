Title:
Vegan Flourless Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962487.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup choc. chips
3/4 cup Cacao powder
4 Chia Seed Eggs
1/4 cup Agave
1/2 cup raw sugar
1 cup almond flour
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Pre heat oven 375
Melt Chocolate chips in double boiler
Make Chia Seed Eggs
Blend Agave, vanilla, Chia Seeds, raw sugar in blender.
Mix together Almond flour and Cacao Powder
Combine wet and dry ingredients mix thoroughly
Bake in an oiled pan.
Bake for 35-40 minutes

Vegan Flourless Chocolate Cake, a rich and delicious treat without the eggs and butter!
