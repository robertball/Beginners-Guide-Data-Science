Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup shredded white Cheddar cheese
3/4 cup Hidden Valley速 Original Ranch速 Salad Dressing
1 cup corn flakes, crushed
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine corn flakes, cheese, salt and pepper in pie plate.
Evenly coat the chicken with the dressing then dredge in the corn flake mixture.
Place the chicken on a parchment lines baking sheet and bake for 30 to 35 minutes until golden and crispy.
