Title:
Strawberry Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1 1/2 C mini pretzels, crushed in blender
1 stick of butter, melted
1/2 C sugar
MIDDLE LAYER:
8oz container of Cool Whip
8oz pkg. cream cheese, room temperature
3/4 - 1 C sugar
TOP LAYER:
2 C Water
6oz pkg. strawberry jello
12oz pkg. frozen strawberries, sliced

Directions:
CRUST: Stir together CRUST ingredients and press into 9x13 glass dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes. Let cool completely.
MIDDLE LAYER: Mix sugar and cream cheese together. Fold in cool whip. Spread over cooled crust.
TOP LAYER: Bring 2 C water to a boil. Pour over 6oz pkg of jello in separate bowl. While jello mixture is still warm, stir in strawberries until completely defrosted. Place in fridge and let mixture partially set. Pour over middle layer.

Submitter: 
April Heath
Description:
