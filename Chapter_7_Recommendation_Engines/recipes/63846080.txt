Title:
Meme's Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068226.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups sugar
3 eggs
3 tsp vanilla
2 cups grated zucchini
3 cups all purpose flour
3 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ
In a large bowl, whisk together the oil, sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Stir in zucchini.
In a small bowl, combine flour, cinnamon, salt, baking soda, and baking powder.
Slowly add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
Fold in walnuts
Grease (or use paper liners) muffin tins. Fill tins 3/4 full.
Bake for 20-25 minutes, our until toothpick inserted in center comes up dry.
