Title:
Whole Wheat Carrot Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
3/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup applesauce
1 cup carrot puree (baby food)
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp ground cloves
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/3 to 1/2 cup chopped apples (1-2 apples) or raisins

Directions:
Combine oil, eggs, sugar, applesauce and carrot puree and mix well
In a separate bowl, combine baking soda, baking powder, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves and mix well. Slowly add to the first bowl containing the wet ingredients. Mix well.
Add walnuts
Peel and chop up the apple and add last.
Put parchment paper on the bottom of a 9 x 5 inch loaf pan. Pour the batter.
Cook at 325 F for 55 to 60 min or until a toothpick comes out clean.
