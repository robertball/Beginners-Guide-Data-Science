Title:
Homestyle Red Skin Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5LB BAG OF REDSKIN POTATOES
6 HARD BOILED EGGS CHOPPED
3/4 CUP DICED CELERY
1/2 CUP DICED SWEET ONION
3/4 CUP DICED CARROT
1 1/2 CUPS MAYONAISE (i.e. HELLMAN'S)
1 CUP SALAD DRESSING(i.e. MIRACLE WHIP)
1/2 TBL SP SUGAR
1/2 TBL SP CELERY SEED
1/2 TBL SP WHITE VINEGAR
1/2 TBL SP YELLOW MUSTARD
SALT AND BLACK PEPPER TO TASTE

Directions:
BOIL POTATOES IN SALTED WATER WHOLE UNTIL JUST FORK TENDER, REFRIGERATE TO COOL.
MIX ALL REMAINING INGREDIENTS EXCEPT EGGS IN LARGE MIXING BOWL ADDING SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE.
CUBE POTATOES WHEN COOL AND ADD TO BOWL LEAVING SKIN ON.
PEEL AND COARSLEY CHOP EGGS AND ADD TO BOWL.
FOLD INGREDIENTS GENTLY UNTIL COMBINED.
COVER AND REFRIGERATE FOR AT LEAST 2 HOURS OR OVERNIGHT

Submitter: 
MURDOCH
Description: 
Potato salad is one of those back yard BBQ must haves, and this recipe will not dissapoint. You can use the same recipe for macaroni salad as well just substitute the potatoes with macaroni noodles.
