Title:
Italian Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. box Rotelle
1 16 oz. bottle of Wishbone Italian Dressing
McCormick Salad Supreme
1/4 lb of Genoa Salami cut up into small strips or cubes
1/4 lb of pepperoni cut up into small strips or cubes
1/4 lb of cooked ham cut up into small strips or cubes
1/4 lb of sharp cheddar cheese cut up into small strips or cubes
1 green pepper cut up into small strips or cubes

Directions:
Cook pasta until al dente.  Transfer pasta to a large bowl.
Add 3/4 of the bottle of salad dressing to pasta.
Place in refrigerator to cool for at least a half hour.
Once pasta is cooled add salami, pepperoni, ham, cheese, & green pepper into the pasta.  Stir well.
Add remaining salad dressing, stir well.
Sprinkle Salad Supreme into pasta salad and stir.

Submitter: 
Janette Zellers

This is our family party staple, everyone from the kids to the grandparents enjoy this!
