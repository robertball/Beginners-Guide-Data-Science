Title:
Good Old Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 pounds potatoes, peeled and chopped
2 pkg italian dressing mix
2/3 cup white wine vinegar
2 cup olive oil
12 green onion, chopped
2 cup chopped celery
4 cup chopped crisp bacon
2 (24 ounce) jar dill pickles, drained and chopped
8 cups mayonnaise

Directions:
Place the potatoes in a large pan of water and boil over medium-low heat until tender, about 12 minutes. Drain the potatoes, and cut up into 3 inch cubes.
Prepare the dressing mix with the vinegar and the olive oil.
Stir the onion,pickles,celery and bacon. Add to potatoes.  Add  mayonnaise just prior to serving.
