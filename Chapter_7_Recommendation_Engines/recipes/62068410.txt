Title:
Doug's Chinese Ground Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
3-4 stalks celery, thinly sliced diagonally
3 carrots
1/2 C. dry roasted peanuts
1 T. soy sauce

Directions:
With a vegetable peeler, pare top layer of skin off carrots, then continue paring to get 2 inch-piece strips of carrot.  Discard carrot cores.
Brown ground beef over medium heat.
Add carrots, celery, and peanuts.
Add soy sauce.  You may need to adjust the amount of soy sauce to your taste.
Serve with hot cooked rice or inside lettuce leaves, burrito-style.

Submitter: 
KathCook
Description: 
Ground beef, carrots, celery, and peanuts cooked simply.  You can serve this with rice or as lettuce wraps for a low-carb meal: peel large lettuce leaves off iceberg lettuce, fill with ground beef mixture, and fold up burrito-style.
