Title:
Marvel's Easiest Dippers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/547875.jpg
Ingredients:
2 multigrain tortillas
1/8 tsp olive oil
Seasoning of choice: salt, sesame, pepper, paprika, fresh chopped rosemary, or thyme, or basil....

Directions:
Brush tortillas on one side lightly with olive oil
Sprinkle on selected seasoning(s)
Cut into four pie-shaped quarters
Then cut each quarter into thirds (one tortilla makes 12 wedges)
Curl two wedges in each muffin pan hole (2 x 12 = 24)
Bake for 15 minutes at 375 degrees in middle of oven
Turn off oven and leave in for another 5 minutes
Serve warm or cool and store in an airtight container

Submitter: 
Marvel's Kitchen
Description: 
Got a great dip? But tired of fatty, oversalted chips?  Here's a fast way to make some healthy and tasty dippers!
