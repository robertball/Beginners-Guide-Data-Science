Title:
Grandma's Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922094.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Loaf White Bread
1 Loaf Wheat Bread
Turkey Giblets
2-3 ribs of Celery diced
1/2 small onion diced
3-4 eggs
Chicken Stock (if needed)
1 stick Butter, melted
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp Poultry Seasoning
2 tbsp Parsley flakes
2 Tbsp fresh sage diced
1 Tsp Rosemary
1 tsp Thyme

Directions:
Boil turkey neck and giblets in a saucepan with a pinch of salt
Shread bread into a large stockpot or bowl
(Optional) Dice giblets and add
Add Diced Onion and Celery
Add eggs and melted butter
Add all seasonings
Mix well moistening with the liquid from the giblets. If you run out of liquid from the boiled parts use chicken stock.
Stuff the bird, any excess can be baked separately in a bowl.
