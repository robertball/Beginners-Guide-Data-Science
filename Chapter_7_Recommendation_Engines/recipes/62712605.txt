Title:
My Work In Progress Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4# Ground Beef
1/4 Cup Chopped Onion
1/4 Cup Finely Chopped Carrots
1/4 Cup Chopped Celery
2 Tbls. Chopped Red Pepper
1 Clove Minced Garlic
2 Tbls Butter
2 Slices Bread
Milk
1 Teas. Seasoning Salt
1/2 Teas. Oregano
1 Tbls. Worchestershire Sauce
2 Eggs
1/4 Teas. Pepper
1/4 Cup Catsup
1/4 Brown Sugar
1 Tbls. Mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Sweat onion,carrot,celery and red pepper in butter until almost soft then add garlic for about 1 min. or so.
Grease or spray a loaf pan.
In a large bowl tear bread into small pieces and add enough milk to just moisten. Let sit for 2 or 3 min. then add egg,seasoning salt,oregano,worchestershire sauce,salt and pepper;mix together. Add groung beef and combine all ingredients.
Put ground beef mixture into loaf pan and put into oven for 30 min.
Combine catsup,brown sugar,and mustard. Spread on top of meatloaf after it has cooked for 30min. and cook for 30 min. longer.
