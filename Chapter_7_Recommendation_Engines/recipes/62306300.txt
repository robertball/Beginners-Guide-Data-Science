Title:
Braised Red Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- Red Cabbage, 1/2, sliced thinly
- Olive Oil for sautéeing
- Redcurrant jelly, 1 Tablespoon
- Red Wine Vinegar, 2 Tablespoons

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
In a casserole, over medium heat, warm the oil, then sautée the cabbage for about 4 minutes, or until soft.
Add the Redcurrant jelly, raise the heat and stir well, cooking for another minute.
Add the vinegar, swirl it around, put the lid on and slide the casserole into the oven to cook for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Swiss Phil
Description: 
An healthy, easy side; very good with Pork Chops for example.
