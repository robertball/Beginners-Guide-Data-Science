Title:
Rosemary Asparagus Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Chopped Yellow Onion
1-2 Cloves Garlic, chopped
2 tsp Dried Rosemary
1 Tbsn flour
2 Tbsn butter
2 cups Chicken Broth
1 cup cream or half and half
1 15oz can asparagus spears
1 15.5 oz can white beans (navy, cannellini, butter)
olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour enough olive oil to coat bottom of pan.
Cook onions, garlic and rosemary in oil over medium heat, just until soft.
Add flour and butter. Stir until butter is melted and flour is mixed in.
Add broth, cream, asparagus and beans. Salt* and pepper to taste.
Turn heat to high. Bring just to a boil, then decrease to medium heat.
Cover and let simmer for 10 minutes.
Remove from heat and let cool for at least 5 minutes.
Pour soup into food processor or blender. Blend on high for 3 minutes.
Pour soup through cheesecloth or strainer.

Submitter: 
Eva Cristina
Description: 
Quick, easy, and tasty! Creamy asparagus soup with white beans, flavored with rosemary and pepper.
