Title:
Hotdog Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb pkg hotdogs - chopped
1lb ground beef
1/2 finely chopped onion
2 cloves chopped garlic
1 egg
1 TB fresh chopped basil
1/2 cup bread crumbs or crushed cracker of choice
1tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
6 slices American cheese - optional

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and form into hamburger patties.
Grill 10 mins per side over medium heat.
Top with cheese if desired until melted.

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
For the person who wants one of each. The hamburgers are stuffed with hotdogs and grilled to perfection.
