Title:
Judy's Famous Corned Beef & Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 lbs corned beef
4 tablespoons mustard
1 cup brown sugar
1 large head of cabbage
potatos,carrots & sweet potatos if desired.

Directions:
Fill a large stock pot with water & bring to a boil. Add the corned beef (and seasoning packet if desired) to the water. Boil at least 1 hour per each lb. of meat. When tender, carefully remove the corned beef & put in a large baking pan that is lined with foil & sprayed lightly with vegetable oil. Reserve the water in the stockpot.  Make the glaze with the brown sugar & mustard. Stir together and spread on the corned beef. Place in preheated over at 300 degree for 30-45 minutes.  Add your cabbage wedges & potatos & carrots to the reserved cooking water & boil until tender. Serve the veggies with your corned beef. Cut against the grain & enjoy!
