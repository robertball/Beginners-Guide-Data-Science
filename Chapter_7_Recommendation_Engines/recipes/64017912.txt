Title:
Cardamom Maple Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1116049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Whole Salmon Fillet cut in 3" sections
1/4 Cup Grapeseed Oil
1/8 Cup Maple Syrup
1 1/2 tsp Salt
1 tsp Ground Cardamom
1 tsp Ground Coriander
1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper
1 tsp Paprika

Directions:
Combine Spices and Salt in a flat bottom bowl
Add in oil and maple syrup
Mix thoroughly with a spoon
Preheat Non-Stick Frying Pan to Med-High (350)
Dredge Salmon cuts in mixture (evenly coat)
Place in frying pan and cook until desired doneness
*Be careful not to use too high a temperature or the paprika/maple will burn.
Use the alternative method in the notes section that will result in an amazing way to assure moist fillets every time.

Submitter:

Description: 
I am a salmon lover.  This is a great recipe for a slightly exotic flavor of Indian inspiration with a maple twist.  The flavor is exceptional, delicious and unique.

Orange zest may be added for an extra flavor twist.
