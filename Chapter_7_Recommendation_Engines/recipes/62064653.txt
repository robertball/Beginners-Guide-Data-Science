Title:
crustinee w/italian spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 baguette sliced into 12 pieces
1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes
1/4 stick butter
drizzle olive oil
4 oz cream cheese

Directions:
drizzle olive oil over bread and toast until light brown
in a food processor mix butter sun dried tomatoes and cream cheese - mix until creamy
spread on custinee and serve (awesome garnished with black olives, avacado slices or cavari)

Submitter: 
Pam Rosterman
Description: 
this yummy appetizer is easy and will make people think you're a gormet cook
