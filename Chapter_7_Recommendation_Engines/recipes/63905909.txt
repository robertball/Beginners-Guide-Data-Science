Title:
Cheddar Bacon Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 cup Progresso plain panko bread crumbs
1/2 cup finely shredded cheddar cheese
1 package or jar cooked real bacon bits or pieces
1 package uncooked chicken tenders, not breaded

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 400Â°F. Spray large cookie sheet with cooking spray.
2. In shallow bowl, beat egg. In large resealable food storage plastic bag, place bread crumbs, cheese, and bacon. Dip chicken in egg. Place in bread crumb bag; seal and shake to coat. Place chicken on cookie sheet.
3. Bake 15 to 20 minutes, turning once, until chicken is no longer pink in center and bread crumbs are golden brown.
