Title:
Fresh, Easy Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/866192.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tomatoes, Chopped
1/2 Medium-Sized Onion, Chopped
1/2 - 1 Jalapeno, Minced
1/4 Cup or so Cilantro, Chopped
Juice from 1/2 Lime
Salt to Taste (about 2 pinches)

Directions:
Mix ingredients.  Let sit for a little while to bring out the flavors.  Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Cooper Barnhart
Description: 
This is my family's favorite salsa recipe.  It's easy to make, yet tastes like your favorite Mexican restaurant made it.  Take it to a party with tortilla chips or add it to your menu on South of the Border Night.
