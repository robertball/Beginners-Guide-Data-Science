Title:
Carrots a l'Orange Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 carrots
1 1/2 T marmalade
1 T butter
dash of nutmeg

Directions:
Cook carrots in boiling water in covered pan umtil almost tender. Drain well. Add marmalade, butter and nutmeg and mix thoroughly.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
Fun way to rev up the same old carrots.
