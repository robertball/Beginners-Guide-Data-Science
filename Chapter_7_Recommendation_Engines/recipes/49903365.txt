Title:
Spinach Con Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
J. ALEXANDER'S SPINACH CON QUESO
1/4 cup margarine
1/2 cup diced yellow onion
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons chicken base
1 cup half-and-half
1 cup 2 percent milk
1 cup chicken stock
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons diced FRESH jalapeno
2/3 cup diced tomato
4 pounds Velveeta -- diced
4 ounces frozen chopped spinach -- thawed and drained

Directions:
In a large skillet, melt margarine. Add onion and garlic. Saut√© on medium heat until onion becomes translucent. Cook 5 minutes. Add chicken base, half-and-half, milk, stock, lemon juice. Stir often until thick. Turn off heat.
Add jalapeno, diced tomato and cheese. Stir until cheese melts.
Drain spinach. Add to cheese mixture. Stir to mix thoroughly. There should be no clumps of spinach. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
