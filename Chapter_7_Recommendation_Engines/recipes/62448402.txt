Title:
M's Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024506.jpg
Ingredients:
1	small can of mixed peas and carrots, or half a full size can of each, drained
1/3	cup butter or margarine
1/3	cup all purpose flour
1/3	cup chopped onion
½	teaspoon salt
¼	teaspoon pepper
2	cups chicken broth
2/3	cup milk
2 ½ 	to 3 cups cut up cooked chicken or turkey
1	package (15 oz.) refrigerated pie crusts, puff pastry shells, or puff pastry for crusts.

Directions:
1.	Heat oven to 425 F.  Drain peas and carrots; set aside.
2.	Melt butter in 2 quart saucepan over medium heat.  Stir in flour, onion, salt and pepper.  Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture is bubbly; remove from heat.  Stir in broth and milk.  Heat to boiling; stirring constantly.  Boil and stir 1 minute.  Stir in chicken, peas and carrots; remove from heat.
3.	Roll one pastry to 12-inch circle.  Ease into ungreased square pan, 9 X 9 X 2 inches.  Pour chicken mixture into pastry lined pan.
4.	Place remaining circle over chicken mixture.  Roll other pastry into 12-inch circle; place over chicken mixture.  Turn edges of pastry under and flute.  Bake about 35 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
comforting casserole dish.  Great use for leftover rotisserie or roasted chicken.  And freezes well too.
