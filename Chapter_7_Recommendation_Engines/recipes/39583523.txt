Title:
Mexican Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-15oz can whole corn, drained
1-15oz can black beans, rinsed, drained
1-10oz can diced tomatoes w/ green chilies
1-8oz sour cream
1-8oz jar chunky salsa
2 C shredded cheddar cheese
2 C cooked brown rice
1/4 t pepper
1 bunch green onions, chopped
8oz Monterey Jack and jalepeno cheese
21 oz can sliced olives

Directions:
Preheat oven 350. Combine 1st 8 ingred. Put in lightly greased 9 x 13 baking pan. Sprinkle w/ remaining ingred. Bake 45 min
