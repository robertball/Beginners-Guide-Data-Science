Title:
Cream Cheese Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060479.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound cream cheese, room temperature
1 cup Jamaican hot pepper sauce
assorted snack crackers

Directions:
Place the cream cheese onto the center of a plate, and pour the hot pepper sauce over so that it drips down the sides. Serve with assorted crackers.

Submitter: 
Cindy Smith
Description: 
So easy, but everyone will love this cream cheese spread. Garnish with a bit of greenery for festive occasions.
