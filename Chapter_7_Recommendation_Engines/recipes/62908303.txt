Title:
Robin's Potato Leek soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large leeks, chopped
2 tsp garlic
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
3 cups thinly sliced potatoes
4 cups chicken stock
1 tsp each parsley, thyme, marjoram
ground black pepper to taste
salt to taste
1 cup milk
3 Tbsp. light cream cheese

Directions:
Gently sweat the chopped leeks, onion and garlic in butter or margarine until soft, about 8 minutes. Do NOT let them brown.
Add potatoes and stock to the saucepan. Herbs, salt and pepper to taste; do not overdo them!
Bring to the boil, and simmer very gently for 30 minutes.
Puree in a blender or with plunge mixer until very smooth.
Gently stir in the milk and cream cheese.
