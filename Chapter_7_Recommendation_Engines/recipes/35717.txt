Title:
Banana Orange Swirly Goodness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/380627.jpg
Ingredients:
2 frozen bananas, peeled and cut into chunks
1 orange - peeled, segmented, and seeded
1 (8 ounce) container raspberry yogurt
1 1/2 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
In a blender, blend the bananas, orange, raspberry yogurt, honey, and nutmeg until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jasperbell
Description: 
Throw together a bit of banana, orange, and raspberry yogurt with a pinch of nutmeg and honey and you have a yum-a-licious breakfast treat or snack!
