Title:
Kahlua White Russian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993254.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces Kahlua
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce cream or milk

Directions:
Pour over ice in a rocks glass. Try with Kahlua Hazelnut, French Vanilla or Mocha. For a Skinny White Russian, add Skim or Soy Milk

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
The White Russian cocktail is a classic blend of Kahlua, vodka, and cream, served on the rocks.
