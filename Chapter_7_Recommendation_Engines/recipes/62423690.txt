Title:
Connie's Zucchini 'Crab' Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups grated zucchini
1 egg, beaten
1 1/2 tablespoons butter, melted
3/4 cup bread crumbs
2 teaspoons fat-free mayonnaise
1/4 cup minced onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pinch paprika
1 pinch nutmeg
1 pinch crushed bay leaves
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
Salt & pepper to taste
Flour to coat
1/2 cup vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine zucchini, egg, butter, bread crumbs, mayo, onion, garlic, and seasonings. Mix well.
Shape mixture into 6 patties. Dredge in flour.
In a medium skillet, heat oil over medium high heat until hot.  Fry patties in oil until golden brown on both sides.
