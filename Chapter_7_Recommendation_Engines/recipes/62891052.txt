Title:
Rhonda's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 plum tomatoes washed and quatered.
1 small red onion peeled and quatered
2 cloves fresh garlic peeled
1 10oz can rotel (tomatoes and chiles undrained)
1 4oz can chiles (undrained)
1 teaspoon of dried cilantro
salt to taste

Directions:
Place the fresh tomatoes, onion and the garlic in the bowl of your food processor and pulse until well chopped.
Place the rest of the ingredients in the bowl and pulse until well blended.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
If you like fresh tasting salsa this is it! You can vary the heat simply by using mild verses hot peppers and rotel.
