Title:
Mississippi Mud Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 - cup butter
2 - cups sugar
1/2 - cup unsweetened cocoa powder
Pinch salt
4 eggs, beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 - cups flour
* Can add 2 cups chopped pecans
Frosting:
1/2 - cup butter melted
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 pound box of powdered sugar (4 cups)
1/2 - cup milk
4 - cups miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Heat oven to 350, grease and flour 9x13 inch baking pan.
Mix sugar, cocoa, and salt.  Add melted butter and mix. Stir in eggs and vanilla. Add flour and optional nuts and stir.
Bake 35 min.
Frosting: mix melted butter and cocoa. Add 2 cups of powdered sugar. Stir in milk. Add remaining sugar.
Spread marshmallows over the hot cake. Let stand for 15 min. spread frosting over top.
