Title:
Microwave Cooked Kimchi Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200 grams thinly slices pork
1 cup kimchi
1/2 yellow onion
1/4 piece scallions
1 tsp sesame oil
salt
pepper
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp sake
green onions (topping)

Directions:
Add sesame oil, salt and pepper to pork then mix and marinate for 10 minutes.
Add soy sauce, sake, kimchi and onions to the pork then mix.
Cover the microwaveable bowl with pork then cook in the microwave for 4 minutes.
Take the bowl from the microwave then mix. Put back the cover then microwave for 3 more minutes.
Take the bowl then mix. Top with green onions before serving.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
Simple, easy and delicious. This recipe doesn't need a lot of ingredients because the kimchi is already tasty. Rice would be hard to resist if this dish is on the table.
