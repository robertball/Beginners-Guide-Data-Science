Title:
Best Banana Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1355363.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe medium sized bananas
1/2 c. full fat coconut milk
1 t. vanilla (optional)

Directions:
peel bananas.
cut bananas in chunks.
blend banana, coconut milk and vanilla in blender by pulsing till smooth but with some small pieces of banana left. a stick blender works very well.
pour into popsicle mold and freeze overnight.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Ripe bananas, ones that have begun to speckle, are naturally sweet and ideal to use for making this creamy frozen treat. With no added sugar and only 3 ingredients this popsicle is a popsicle you won't feel guilty indulging in.
