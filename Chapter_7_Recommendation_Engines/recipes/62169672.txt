Title:
PUDINA (MINT) CHUTNEY Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 cups pudina (mint) leaves
2 â€“ 3 green chillies
2 tablespoons lime juice
Salt to taste
1 tablespoon sugar

Directions:
Add all the ingredients into a food processor and grind into a fine paste. You might need to add
some water to help grind all the leaves. Check salt and sugar and add more to taste.

Submitter: 
SUSMITA
Description: 
This is a very versatile chutney and can be used in many ways. I love mixing this chutney with butter and spreading it on bread for sandwiches. I also add some to my omelets.
