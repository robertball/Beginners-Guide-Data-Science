Title:
Coconut Curry Roasted Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium butternut squash, about 3-3.5 lbs
1 medium onion
1 leek (white part only)
1 to 2 inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled
1 medium garlic bulb
4 cups good-quality chicken broth or stock (preferably homemade)
1 cup coconut milk
1 heaping tsp of curry powder
dash of cayenne pepper (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil. Cut squash in half, scoop out seeds,and put on baking sheet with cut sides down.
Cut the garlic bulb in half cross-ways and wrap each half in aluminum foil. Peel and quarter the onion, cut the leek and ginger into 1 inch pieces. Add garlic, onion, leek, and ginger to the baking sheet with the squash. Roast for 45 min to an hour until vegetables are golden and skin of squash is easily pierced with a fork.
Squeeze out roasted garlic cloves and scoop out flesh of squash into a food processor or blender. Puree squash, garlic, ginger, onion, and leek in batches with 2 cups of broth.
Pour puree into soup pot over medium heat, and add remaining 2 cups of broth/stock. Whisk in coconut milk and curry powder, and season with salt and pepper (and cayenne if using). Heat through gently and serve.

Description: 
A creamy butternut squash soup with the warm flavors of curry and ginger, and creaminess from coconut milk. A great way to warm up in the fall and winter months!
