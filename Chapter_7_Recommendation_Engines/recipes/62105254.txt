Title:
Pimento Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8-oz. pkg. shredded shart cheddar cheese
2 8-oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 small jar chopped piments, drained
handful green onion to taste
Chopped pecans

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and cheddar cheese with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended.  Add lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, pimentos and onions.  Refridgerate several hours or overnight.  Form cheese mixture into a ball.  Roll in chopped pecans and serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Barb Andrews
Description: 
This cheese ball disappears as soon as I set it out.  I've given this recipe out countless times.  It is asked of me to bring to every occasion!
