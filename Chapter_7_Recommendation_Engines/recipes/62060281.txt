Title:
Middle Eastern Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups milk
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup short grain rice
1/4 cup unsweetened shaved coconut
1 tsp. vanilla extract
Nuts for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Heat milk with sugar on medium heat for approximately 10 minutes.
Add the rinsed rice to the heated milk and continue stirring on medium heat for approximately 20 minutes. The rice needs to be fully cooked and the liquid must reduce by 1/2.
Once the mixture has thickened, remove from the stove, add the Â¼ cup of coconut and vanilla and mix thoroughly.
Serve in individual bowls or in one large serving dish and let it cool then refrigerate.
Garnish with any nuts you have available at hand.

Submitter: 
Mama & her Girls
Description: 
A very simple desert that's a staple in all Middle Eastern homes. Make sure you never leave the pot unattended as the milk might burn.
