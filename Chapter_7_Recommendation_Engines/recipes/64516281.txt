Title:
Luckman Coffee-Mocha Frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TB mocha powder
1 cup strong brewed coffee and espresso
1/2 cup ice
1/2 cup nonfat milk
whipped topping
chocolate syrup

Directions:
put all ingredients except last 2 in a blender that can handle ice. blend all of it until relatively smooth.
pour into appropriate sized cup and add whipped topping and syrup to your liking.
