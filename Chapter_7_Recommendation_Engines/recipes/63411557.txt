Title:
Soft Spread Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup olive oil, you can use any mild tasting oil you like

Directions:
Beat softened butter in Kitchen Aid or cuisinart until light and fluffy. Slowly add oil while continuing to beat. Add sea salt to taste (tiny bit) Beat for about five minutes more. Divide into smaller covered containers and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
mauigirl
Description: 
By blending butter and olive oil, you'll create soft spread butter that's lower in saturated fat.  Create a flavored butter by adding garlic or cinnamon, or anything you choose!Modified from: Soft Spread Butter
