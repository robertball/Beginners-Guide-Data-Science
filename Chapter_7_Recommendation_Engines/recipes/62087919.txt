Title:
Gouda and preserved ginger hors d'oeuvre Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Gouda Cheese, preferably imported from Holland
1 jar ginger in syrup
toothpicks

Directions:
Remove rind from cheese and cut into 3/4 to 1 inch cubes. Cut preserved ginger pieces in half and spear onto a cube of cheese with a toothpick. Repeat until all cubes are used. Serve at room temperature.  Can be made ahead.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
This is a very tasty and unusual hors d'oeuvre that you can put together in no time flat.  It is of Dutch origin and uses two favorite flavors: cheese and ginger, or tangy and sweet.
