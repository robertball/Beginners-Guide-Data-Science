Title:
Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ box elbow macaroni, cooked drained and cooled
1/3 red onion, diced
3 hard boiled eggs, diced
½ pint grape tomatoes, halved
½ jar of olives, halved
1/3 c mayo
1 ½ T milk
1 ½ T vinegar (cider or white)
¾ T sugar
1 tsp paprika
1 T seasoned salt (Lawry’s)
1 tsp black pepper
½ tsp cilantro
2 tsp yellow mustard

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix.
Adjust seasoning to taste.

Submitter: 
Kristin C
Description: 
A nice alternative to the heavy mayo based salads. Still uses mayo, but lots of veggies make it lighter and fresher.
