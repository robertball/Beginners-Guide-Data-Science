Title:
Cherry Berry Coconut Limeade Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3774230.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups cherries, pitted and halved
1/2 cup chilled coconut milk
1/4 cup strawberries, stemmed and hulled
1/2 banana
1 lime, juiced

Directions:
Place cherries, coconut milk, strawberries, banana, and lime juice in a blender. Cover and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Viscouse
Description: 
Craving a refreshing, sweet, but tart drink to cool you down on a hot summer's day? This cherry berry coconut limeade smoothie will do the trick!
