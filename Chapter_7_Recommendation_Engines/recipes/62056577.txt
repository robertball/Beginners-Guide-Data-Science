Title:
Lazy Man's Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1 can apple or cherry pie filling
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, soda, salt, vanilla, eggs, oil and pie filling together.  Pour into a greased 9x13" pan.  Combine brown sugar, cinnamon and nuts and sprinkle over batter.  Bake at 350F for 40 to 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lynda McLean Falloon
Description: 
As the name implies, a quick and easy, yet moist and tasty cake.  You can make the cake from scratch, as in the recipe, or prepare your favorite cake mix and just add in the pie filling and topping.
