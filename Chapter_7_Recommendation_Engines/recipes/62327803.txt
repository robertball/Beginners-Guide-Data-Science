Title:
Cocktail 707 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798880.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces lemon vodka
2 ounces plain vodka
2 ounces pear vodka
4 ounces Cran-Rasberry Juice
1/2 of lime juiced
Lime wedges, for rims

Directions:
Chill glasses until ready to serve.
Pour all liquid ingredients over ice into a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into prepared glasses. Garnish with a lime wedge
Or serve over ice.
