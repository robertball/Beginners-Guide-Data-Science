Title:
Kebab Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Catsup
1 Tsp. Sugar
1 Tsp. Salt
2 Tbsp. White Vinegar
2 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
2 Tbsp. Oil
1/2 Cup Water
1 Tbsp. Minced Garlic

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in sauce pan.
Bring to a boil then reduce heat to simmer for 10 minutes.
Let cool prior to pouring onto meat.
Marinate overnight.
