Title:
Fuzzy Moguai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
6 1/2 fluid ounces sweet and sour mix
4 fluid ounces mandarin flavored vodka
4 fluid ounces orange liqueur
ice cubes

Directions:
Combine the sweet and sour mix, vodka and orange liqueur in a shaker or large glass. Stir, and pour into two glasses with ice.

Description: 
Boredom makes some pretty interesting drinks that taste surprisingly good. This citrus flavored sipper is mild yet potent. It will sneak in and whoop your butt like a little ninja.
