Title:
Savory Quinoa & Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs olive oil
1/2 C Israeli couscous (or barley or orzo)
1/2 C Quinoa
1 1/2 C chicken broth
1/2 C Orange juice
1 tsp salt
2 Tbs chopped dried parsley
1 Tbs chopped dried or fresh rosemary
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1 medium green apple, diced
1 cup dried cranberries or craisins
1/2 cup slivered almonds or chopped pecans, toasted
Dressing:
1.5 Tbs olive oil
1 Tbs Maple Syrup
1.5 Tbs Apple Cider vinegar

Directions:
Add olive oil, quinoa, and couscous to pot and toast over medium high heat for 5 min. Stirring frequently.
Add chicken broth, orange juice and salt; bring to a boil.  Cover and reduce heat to simmer for 10 minutes.
Remove from heat and add spices, apples, craisins, and toasted nuts; cover.  Allow to cool for 10 minutes.
Mix together dressing.  Taste test quinoa and couscous.  If it needs more salt to suit, add to the dressing before mixing into the quinoa and couscous.

A moist quinoa recipe with a great deal of versatility.
