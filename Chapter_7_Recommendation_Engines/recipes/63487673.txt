Title:
Maurice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cooked shredded chicken breast meat
3/4 cup julienned ham
1 cup julienned swiss cheese
1/2-3/4 cup mayo
2 tbs sweet pickle relish
1 tbs lemon juice
1 small clove garlic minced (optional)
s/p
1 tbs dijon mustard optional
romaine lettuce chopped

Directions:
stir together the mayo, sweet pickle relish, lemon juice, and garlic and mustard.  season with salt and pepper to taste. you may thin the dressing with 1-2 tbs milk if desired.  arrange chicken, ham and cheese over a bed of lettuce (in a serving bowl or individual salad paltes).  toss with some of the dressing and reserve the remainder to serve on the side.  garnish with chives, tomatoes or hard-boiled egs
