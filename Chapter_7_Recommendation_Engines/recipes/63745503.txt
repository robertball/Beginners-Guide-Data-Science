Title:
Bean Salad/Corn Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can shoe peg corn, drained and rinsed
1 red bell pepper
1 bunch green onions/scallions
1-2 tomatoes
1 clove garlic
fresh cilantro
fresh parsley
-----------------
2.5 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 lime, juiced

Directions:
Chop all ingredients into small diced pieces.  Mix oil, cumin, garlic and juices together.  Pour over bean mixture

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
Also known as Texas Caviar, this is great served with Fritos or tortilla chips.
