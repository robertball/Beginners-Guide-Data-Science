Title:
Raita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cucumber, peeled
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dry roasted cumin ( you may need to roast and crush yourself if you can't buy.)
4 fluid oz full fat yogurt
1/2 tsp sugar
1 garlic clove crushed
1 Tbs chopped mint
1 Tbs chopped fresh coriander

Directions:
1. Finely grate cucumber toss with the salt and let sit 1 hr.
2. squeeze out as much of the liquid as you can from the cucumber.
3.add remainder of ingredients, mix and chill.

Submitter: 
Chef Lucky
Description: 
My Indian co-workers cook about once a week here, they just call this Salad. Either way it's awesome
they add small diced carrot,tomato,onion and chiles. This is the base though.
