Title:
The Best Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups mashed banana
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9x5 inch pan.
Cream butter and sugars until smooth and creamy. Beat in eggs,vanilla and banana's. Mix the flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon togetherAdd flour mixture to butter and sugar mixture, stirring just until combined. Do not overmix.
Pour into prepared pan and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 1 hour (or till toothpick comes out clean).  Remove from pan and let cool, store in refrigerator or freeze.
