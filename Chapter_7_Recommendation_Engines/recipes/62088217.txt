Title:
Chocolate-peanut-buttercream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5 cups confectioners sugar
1/3 cup cocoa power
1/2 cup margarine at room temp. (or butter)
2 T peanut butter
1 teas. vanilla
4-6 T milk

Directions:
beat margarine and peanut butter until fluffy. Slowly mix in powdered sugar and cocoa powder. Add milk as necessary. Beat until thoroughly blended. Beat in vanilla

Description: 
It was my brother's birthday and there wasn't frosting around. I whipped this up and it came out awesome. Its easy and very tasty. I used it to frost a homemade yellow cake
