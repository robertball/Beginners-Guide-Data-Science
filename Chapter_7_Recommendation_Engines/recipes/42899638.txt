Title:
Corn Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 Cup Sifted Flour
1 1/2 Tsp Baking Posder
3/4 Tsp Salt
2/3 Cup Milk
1 egg well beaten
1 can Corn drained

Directions:
Mix Flour Baking powder, and Salt together
Add Milk
Add Egg
Add Corn
Drop by spoonfulls into hot (375 degree) oil
