Title:
Favorite Thai Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can coconut milk
1 pound raw diced boneless chicken
1-2 diced bell pepper
1/2 mushrooms sliced
2 peeled and diced potatoes
3/4 cup diced or sliced carrots
1/2 pint grape or cherry tomatoes halved
1 medioum onion finely shopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon chili garlic paste
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
2 teaspoons curry powder- more to taste
1 teaspoon honey
1 teaspoon peanut butter
1-2 teaspoons soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon hot sesame oil
1 teaspoon fish sauce optional

Directions:
Saute onions in a tablespoon or two of oil until they sweat and are translucent
Add garlic and saute over gentle heat for a minute
add chili paste, salt, pepper, chili powder and saute for a minute
add diced chicken and cook until just cooked
add can of coconut milk and two cans of water
add curry powder, turmeric, lemon juice, sesame oil, potatoes and carrots
simmer on medium heat until the flavors meld and everything is thoroughly heated through
add honey and peanut butter and mix in thoroughly
continually check for seasoning
add mushrooms and bell pepper once the curry starts to get thicker and has the right taste
cook until all vegetables are cooked through
serve with any variety of white rice

Submitter: 
Sumaia Cooks!
Description: 
This Thai curry is easy and made from scratch. Can feed a whole crowd and keep them happy. Heat can vary based on taste but the spice adds a lot of flavor.
