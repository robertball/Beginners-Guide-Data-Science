Title:
No Bean Veggie Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium potato, peeled and diced
1 small onion, diced
1/2 cup walnuts or pecans (optional)
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cup oatmeal
1 cup breadcrumbs
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon sage
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon oil, for frying

Directions:
Pulse potato and onion (and walnuts if you are using them) in food processor until finely chopped.
In large microwave safe bowl, cook oatmeal according to box instructions.
Add potato mixture, oil, breadcrumbs, salt, sage, and soy sauce. Mix thoroughly. More soy sauce can be added if mixture is too dry.
Form into patties and fry until both sides are browned and patty is cooked through. Patties can also be placed on an oiled baking sheet and cooked at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes, flipping patties half way through cook time.

Submitter: 
Crystal R.
Description: 
This versatile and delicious veggie burger mix can be made into patties or crumbled up and used in place of meat in other recipes.
