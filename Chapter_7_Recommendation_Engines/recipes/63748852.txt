Title:
Hawaiian Ginger-Chicken Stew for 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tsp Sesame or Canola oil
1/2 LB chicken tenders or boneless breast cut into 1 inch pieces
1 piece fresh ginger (1 inch) sliced into matchsticks and minced
2 or 3 cloves of garlic finely minced
3/8 cup dry sherry
1 1/2 cup reduced sodium chicken broth
1 cup water
1 TBS reduced sodium soy sauce
1/2 tsp (or more to taste) Asian red chile sauce
1/2 bunch mustard greens or chard (stemmed and chopped) about 3 1/2 cups or 1 cup frozen chopped mustard greens

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium high heat add chicken and cook until done, about 6 minutes. Transfer to a bowl with tongs.
Add ginger and garlic to pot and cook until fragrant (10-20 seconds) add sherry and cook until mostly evaporated, scraping up any browned bits (1.5-3 minuted) Add broth and water, raise heat to high, bring to a boil for 5 minutes. Add soy sauce, chile sauce and mustard greens (or chard) and cook until greens are tender about 3 minutes. Return chicken and any accumulated juices to the pot and cook until heated through. (1-2 minutes)
