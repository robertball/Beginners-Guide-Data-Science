Title:
Grill Master Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Wings:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup Italian-style salad dressing
3 pounds chicken wings, cut apart at joints, wing tips discarded
Sauce:
1/4 cup butter
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1/4 cup hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHotÂ®), or to taste

Directions:
Combine 1/2 cup soy sauce, Italian dressing, and chicken wings in a large, zip-top bag.
Close bag and refrigerate 4 hours to overnight.
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat or turn oven to 400 degrees.
In a small saucepan, melt the butter. Stir in the 1 teaspoon soy sauce and the hot pepper sauce. Turn off heat and reserve.
Remove the chicken wings from the marinade and pat dry.
Cook the wings on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until the chicken is well browned and no longer pink, 25 to 30 minutes or
Bake in oven 40 min. Turning after 20 minutes.
Place grilled wings in a large bowl. Pour butter sauce over wings; toss to mix well.

Submitter: 
Mary Alice
Description: 
Tangy Italian dressing and savory soy create an easy marinade that is just terrific on grilled chicken wings.Modified from: Grill Master Chicken Wings
