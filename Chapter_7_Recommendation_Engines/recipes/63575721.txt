Title:
Fresh Tomato and Artichoke pasta sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 large slicing tomatoes, peeled (Hot water method)
1 jar of artichoke hearts (slice or break apart )(your choice, I use the ones packed in oil with some flavor)
1 - large onion sliced (cut slices in half and separate)
1 - 5oz container feta cheese
1 pkg fresh mozzarella cheese thinly sliced or use shredded.
Thinly shredded Parmesan cheese (optional)
olive oil
red wine vinegar or red wine (your choice)
Garlic (optional)
1 box your choice of spaghetti
1-2 Tbls dried basil or fresh basil(more or less to taste) optional
1 Tbls dried oregano (more or less to taste) optional

Directions:
Slice the peeled tomatoes into 3/8 - 1/2 inch slices
In a large 13x9 pan layer Tomatoes, sliced onion, artichoke hearts, some of the sliced mozzarella cheese.  Sprinkle layer with feta cheese and parmesan if desired.  Sprinkle layer with spices if desired.  Sprinkle layer with red wine and olive oil. Add some garlic to first layer if desired.
Repeat layering duplicating above until all ingredients used.
Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
Cook in preheated 400-425 degree oven for 50-60 minutes till the whole thing is bubbling
Remove from oven and using a knife and fork cut everything up right in the pan.
Serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
Raccoon
Description: 
My sister and I invented this in Vermont.  She can't have aged cheese but feta and fresh Mozzarella are OK.  The artichokes just add a nice touch.
