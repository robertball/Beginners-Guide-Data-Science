Title:
Creamy Dilled Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions, thinly sliced and separated into rings
1 cup dill pickle juice
1/2 cup fat free sour cream (i use regular)
1/8 tsp celery seed
sprig of fresh dill (optional)

Directions:
Combine onion rings and dill pickle juice in a bowl.
Let marinate, covered in the fridge for 2 days.
Drain well.
Add sour cream.
Mix well and sprinkle with celery seed and fresh dill if using

Submitter: 
smitty
Description: 
I'd like to share a recipe that I got from a friend a few years ago.  It is great for a summer 'make ahead' side dish.  It's a good go-with at barbecues and when grilling. Enjoy!!!
