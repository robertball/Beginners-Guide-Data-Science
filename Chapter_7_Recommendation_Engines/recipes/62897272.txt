Title:
Shrimp Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/701277.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut, sesame or other oil
8 oz shrimp
1 or 2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon fresh ginger
1 green onions, thinly sliced
1 diced carrot
2 oz frozen peas, thawed
2 cups cooked rice, cooled completely
1 eggs, lightly beaten
Finishing sauce
1/4 cup of Chicken stock
1 tablespoon of fish sauce
1 tablespoon of soy sauce
1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon of dry sherry (optional)
1 tablespoon of sesame seed oil

Directions:
fry the shrimp 2 Min's, and set it to the side.
Stir Fry, Ginger
Add Onions
Add Garlic
Add carrot
Add peas
Add Rice
When the rice has been warmed, pour in sauce and stir fry Rice Mixture.
Plate Rice Mixture
Add 1 Table Spoon Of Oil Into Pan
Add Blended Egg And And Scramble
Top Rice With Egg
