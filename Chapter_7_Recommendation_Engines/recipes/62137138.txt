Title:
Pasta with Lemon Olive Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp fresh lemon zest
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup Olive oil
2 large garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
3 Tbsp basil, julienned
Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
1 lb fresh linguine
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Combine the lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, olives and tomatoes.
Cook pasta and reserve 1/4 cup pasta water. Drain and toss pasta with olive oil mixture and reserved pasta water. Toss with basil and parmesan cheese, season with salt and pepper.
Garnish with extra parmesan on top.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
I usually serve this as an appetizer course as it is so light and refreshing;  however, it also makes a lovely light lunch or dinner, especially in the summer. The key is to always use fresh linguine, and buy a block of good quality parmesan, grating it into large, thin slivers.
