Title:
Cajun Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. olive oil
2 T. Cajun seasoning
2 T. fresh lemon juice
2 T. fresh chopped parsley
1 T. honey
1 T. light soy sauce
2 tsp. dried cilantro
1 lb. uncooked, shelled, deveined shrimp

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except shrimp in a 9"x13" baking dish.  Add shrimp, tossing to coat.  Refrigerate 1 hour.  Bake at 450 degress for 10 minutes, stirring a couple of times.  Serve over linguine or rice with lemon wedges.
