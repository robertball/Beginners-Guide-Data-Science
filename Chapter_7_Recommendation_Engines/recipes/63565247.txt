Title:
Crockpot Hawaiian Chicken (Gluten and Dairy Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/973733.jpg
Ingredients:
* 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (thicker the better)
* 5-6 cloves of garlic, chopped
* 2 zucchini, cut into half moons
* 1 red onion, chopped
* 1 red pepper, cut into chunks
* 1 yellow pepper, cut into chucnks
* 1 20-oz can pineapple chunks with juice
* 1 14oz can coconut milk
* 2 cups chicken broth
* 1 teaspoon cumin
* 1 teaspoons paprika
* 2 teaspoons red curry
* 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
* Dash of sea salt and pepper, to taste
* 3 teaspoons honey
* 1 Tablespoon olive oil
* 1 cup dry Jasmati Rice

Directions:
Drizzle olive oil in the bottom of the slow cooker;
Place chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker;
Season chicken with sea salt and ground pepper.
Add the garlic, red onion, red pepper, yellow pepper and pineapple chunks with juice;
Add all the spices and honey;
Add the broth;
Cover and cook on LOW for 5-6 hours;
30 minutes before serving, add the coconut milk, stir, and cover let cook another 20 to 30 minutes;
Cook Jasmati rice according to directions on package;
Serve crockpot meal over rice. Enjoy!
