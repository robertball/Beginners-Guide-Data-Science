Title:
Fresh Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1  10 oz. Cool Whip
1/4-1/3 c. Amaretta liquor
Any combination of fresh fruit in season but especially good with Apples, Pears, Bananas and Pineapple

Directions:
Mix the sweetened condensed milk with the Cool Whip. Add 1/4 C. Amaretta Liquor first and taste. Add more if you want a stronger flavor and chill.
Wash cut and plate your choices of fruit and serve.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A light and refreshing dip for fresh fruit year round. Makes a nice light and easy to make dessert.
