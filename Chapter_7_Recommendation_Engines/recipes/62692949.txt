Title:
Jan's Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/606847.jpg
Ingredients:
8 large apples of a mixed variety- peeled, cored and chopped
3/4 to 1 cup cider or apple juice depending on how thick you like your sauce.
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine all the ingredients. Start with 3/4 of the juice and add more if needed.
Cover, and cook over medium heat for about 20 minutes, or until apples are soft. Stir often.
Allow to cool, then puree with an immersion blender.  For chunky sauce use a potato masher.
Freezes well.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
This fresh applesauce is a favorite at our house. We use a wide variety of apples fresh off the trees!   It is easily adaptable to your own taste levels for spice and sweetness.  Freezes exceptionally well.
