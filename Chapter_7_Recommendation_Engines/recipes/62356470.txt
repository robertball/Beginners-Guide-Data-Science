Title:
Perfect Vanilla Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/402496.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, softened
1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 large eggs
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 Tbs. cornstarch
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
2 1/2 Cups all-purpose flour
1/2 Cup milk
3/4 Cup heavy cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In a mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar until smooth, then blend in vanilla
Beat in eggs, one at a time, until fluffy
In a measuring cup or small bowl, combine salt, cornstarch, baking powder, and flour
Sift flour mixture into ixing bowl in 3 to 4 batches, alternating with milk and cream. Beat for about 30 seconds until batter is smooth. (Batter will be pretty thick)
Line cupcake pan(s) with paper or foil liners, and fill approx. 2/3 full
Bake 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean
