Title:
Rice Krispie Banana Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup butter flavored shortening
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
pinch salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3/4 cup chopped pecans
2 bananas, mashed
1 1/2 cups coconut
3 cups Rice Krispie cereal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Beat together shortening, brown sugar and white sugar until creamy. Mix in vanilla and egg until smooth.
In separate bowl whisk together flour, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg. Using beaters on low, mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients until just mixed.
Using a wooden spoon, add each of the remaining ingredients (pecans, bananas, coconut and cereal)one at a time, stirring between additions.
Drop by large tablespoons onto cookie sheet. Bake at 375 for 11 - 15 minutes. (Until lightly brown and appear firm in centre) Let cool on cookie sheet for 5 minutes then remove to wire rack.

This was originally an oatmeal cookie but I made it with Rice Krispies one day when I was out of oats and everyone enjoyed this version more.
