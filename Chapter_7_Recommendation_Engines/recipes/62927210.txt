Title:
Zucchini Bread Gluten Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup applesauce
1/2 cup maple syrup
1 tablespoon coconut oil, melted
2 cups grated zucchini, skins left on
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup buckwheat flour
1 cup teff flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 cup raisins
1 cup slivered almonds
zest of one lemon

Directions:
1.  In mixing bowl, combine eggs, applesauce, maple syrup, coconut oil, zucchini, and vanilla.  Add remaining ingreadients one at a time stirring or mixing on low speed in between.  Transfer mixture into greased bread pan.
2.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour, or until a tester inserted in the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
flamingo6584
Description: 
I had so much zucchini from my garden this year! I recently decided to go gluten free, so I experimented with several different flours, and came up with what I think is the best zucchini bread I have ever made.
