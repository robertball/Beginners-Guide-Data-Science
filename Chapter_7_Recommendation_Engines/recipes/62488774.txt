Title:
No Bake Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened.
16 oz. cool whip
1 cup powdered sugar
1 graham cracker crust
Favorite pie filling

Directions:
Beat together cream cheese, cool whip  and powdered sugar. Pour into pie crust and top with favorite pie filling.
Let stand in refrigerator for 1 hour. Cut and serve.
