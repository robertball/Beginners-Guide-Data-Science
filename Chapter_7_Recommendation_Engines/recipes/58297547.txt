Title:
Fresh Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Qt Fresh Tomatoes
2 Stalks (1 C.) Chopped Celery
1 Sm-Med Onion - Chopped
1 Carrot, Grated
1/2 Stick (1/8 lbs) Butter
4 1/2 C. Chix Stock
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp. Black Pepper
1/2 Tsp Curry Powder (Optional)
2 tsp Sugar
1/4 C. Flour

Directions:
Bring 4-6 qts of water to a rolling boil.  Blanche tomatoes until the skin will pull-off(3 MIN). Plunge into an ice bath to stop cooking. Peel & dice tomatoes removing at least 1/2 the seeds. Set tomatoes aside.
Saute' Celery, Onion, Carrot in Butter.  Add 4 C. Chix Stock, Salt, Pepper, Sugar, Tomatoes and heat to a gentle boil. Simmer 20 mins.
Blend Flour with 1/2 C Chix Broth and Stir-in to soup and heat until thickened.
