Title:
Savory Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3780146.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 tablespoons olive oil or coconut oil, divided
2 pounds bottom round beef, cut into cubes
1 yellow onion, chopped (about 1 cup)
1 large shallot, chopped (about 1/4 cup)
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried sage
1 butternut squash, cubed (about 1 1/2 cups)
1 1/2 cups low sodium beef broth
1 tablespoon arrowroot

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat add in 2 tablespoons of oil. Once hot add in beef and brown on all sides for about 7-8 minutes.
Add in remaining oil then add in onion, shallot and garlic.  Cook for about 3 minutes then add in salt, thyme, sage and pepper.  add in butternut squash and continue cooking for 3-5 minutes carefully stirring using a wooden spoon.
Pour in beef broth and add in arrowroot then stir well.  Bring to a boil.  Cover and place in the oven for 1 1/2 hours checking after 1 hour.
