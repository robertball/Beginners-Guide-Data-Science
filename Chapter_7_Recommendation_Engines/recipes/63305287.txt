Title:
Turkey Drumsticks Perfecto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 small turkey drumsticks
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1/4 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
1 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Cover turkey with cold water.  Add 1/4 cup salt to water and brine for at least 2 hours in refrigerator.
Drain, rinse, and pat drumsticks dry with paper towels.
Brush butter over meat and sprinkle with pepper.
Place buttered, peppered legs on rack in roasting pan in which the chicken broth has been added.
Cover with lid.
Place on bottom oven rack in cold oven.
Bake 350 degrees for 1 hour, 45 minutes to 2 hours or until thermometer reads 180 degrees.
Remove from oven and let rest 10 minutes.
