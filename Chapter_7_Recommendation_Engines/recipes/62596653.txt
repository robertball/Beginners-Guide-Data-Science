Title:
Tasty Heallthy & Quick Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb boneless chicken breasts, sliced, or chicken breast tenderloins
1 cup lowfat buttermilk
1 tablespoon Hungarian paprika
.
1 1/2 cups wheat bran, divided
1/2 cup breadcrumbs (seasoned, plain or panko)
1/2 teaspoon coarse sea salt (or regular salt)
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
In a measuring cup, stir together the buttermilk and paprika until well blended.
Place the chicken pieces in a plastic bag.
Pour the milk mixture over the chicken and massage the bag until all pieces are covered.
Refrigerate for 2 hours up to 24 hours.
When ready to prepare, preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Drain the chicken and discard the buttermilk.
In a shallow, long dish stir together 1/2 cup of the bran and all of the breadcrumbs, salt, garlic powder and Parmesan cheese (if using).
Place the chicken pieces in the crumb mixture and turn to coat well.
Place the remaining bran in a 3" to 4" wide strip down the center of a baking sheet.
Place the coated chicken pieces on the bran.
Reduce oven heat to 375 and bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink in the center.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
Using bran as part of the breading adds no extra calories to the chicken and is very good for you to boot!
