Title:
Sundsvallkakor Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749827.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 c. vegetable shortening
1 c. sugar
1 egg yolk
1 c. shredded coconut
2 c. all purpose flour
1 t. ammonium carbonate

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees
Cream butter and shortening til smooth
Add sugar and beat until fluffy
Beat in egg yolk.
Stir in coconut and set aside
In a second bowl, blend flour and ammonium carbonate.
Add flour mixture to butter mixture and blend.
Roll into small balls the size of walnuts.
Place on ungreased baking sheet and flatten with the tines of a fork in cross cross pattern
Bake 15 minutes, until the edges are slightly browned.
Let cool on sheets slightly and then remove to cool completely on wire racks.

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Delicious coconut Swedish cookies. Ammonium carbonate is available at a good German or Scandanavian deli or even online.
