Title:
Mickey Mouse Cookie Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
large oreo cookies
mini oreo cookies
chocolate Bark chocolate
candy pop sticks ( might try popsicle sticks)

Directions:
melt Bark stovetop
place large oreo in a stick ( center it)
make mouse ears by placing two small oreos- inside large one- one on each side like ears
dip whole pop in melted choc and place en
on wax or parchment paper- cool in fridge to hard
wrap each one when hard
