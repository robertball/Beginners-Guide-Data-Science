Title:
Awsome Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cubed beef stew meat or hamburger
2 cans condensed golden mushroom soup
1 can sliced mushrooms
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon onion powder
2 teaspoons crushed garlic
1/2 cup cooking sherry
1tablespoon parsley flakes
salt/pepper to taste
1 8 ounce package cream cheese

Directions:
Add all of your ingrediants to the slow cooker EXCEPT the cream cheese. Mix well. Cook on low for 4 hours, add 1/2 of your cream cheese. Cook on low for an additional 2 hours, add the rest of your cream cheese.
Spoon stroganoff over your favorite pasta.
