Title:
Grecian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large onion, halved and sliced
1 tablespoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Greek Seasoning
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 medium zucchini, sliced
1/4 cup pitted, chopped kalamata olives
2 ounces crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Coat chicken with flour.  Heat oil in large skillet; add chicken.  Brown on all sides.  Remove from skillet.
Add onion and Greek seasoning; cook and stir 2-3 minutes or until lightly browned.  Stir in tomatoes, zucchini and olives; bring to boil.
Add chicken.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes or until chicken is tender.  Top with cheese during last 3 minutes of cooking.  Serve with orzo or other pasta.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
Capture the robust and sunny flavors of the Mediterranean in this colorful medley of chicken, zucchini and tomatoes seasoned with McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Greek Seasoning - a traditional blend of onion, garlic, oregano, lemon, and even a hint of mint.
