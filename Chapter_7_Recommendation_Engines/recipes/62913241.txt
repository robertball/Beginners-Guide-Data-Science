Title:
Zesty Peach Salsa topping for any grilled fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped fresh or frozen peaches
1/4 cup chopped sweet red pepper
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped*
2 tablespoons orange juice
1 tablespoon minced fresh cilantro
2 teaspoons lime juice
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients; cover and refrigerate until serving.
After grilling fish of your choice, top with peach salsa.

Submitter: 
Kinza
Description: 
Our Test Kitchen came up with a fun way to serve halibut by marinating it in citrus juices and brown sugar, then topping it with a refreshing peach salsa. The colorful entree is pretty enough to serve to company.Modified from: Halibut with Zesty Peach Salsa
