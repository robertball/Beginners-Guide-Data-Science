Title:
Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12- 15 brussel sprouts (cut in half)
2 tsp brown sugar
1 tbsp olive oil
2 garlic cloves
pinch of salt

Directions:
1. Saute the garlic cloves in the olive oil for 2 mintues.
2.  Add the brussel sprouts and saute for an additional 4-5 minutes.
3.  Add the brown sugar and a pinch of salt.
