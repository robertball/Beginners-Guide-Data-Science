Title:
Autumn Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2612005.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb bacon, diced
6 chopped green onions
1 cup chopped onion
2 Carrots, diced
4 stalks Celery Diced
3 Fresh bay leaves
3/4 cup flour
2 pounds potatoes, peeled, medium dice
5 cups Clam Juice
2 cups heavy Cream
1 cup frozen corn
4 cans baby clams
Parsley
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
In large, heavy stock pot, over medium high heat, render bacon until crispy, about 8 minutes.
Stir in the green onion, onion, celery and carrots, cook about 3 minutes or until vegetables start to cook through.
Season veggies with salt and pepper, stir in the flour and cook for 3 minutes until a light rue is formed.
Stir in clam juice, potatoes and bay leaves, season with salt and pepper, bring to a boil, stirring occasionally, simmer until potatoes are fork tender, around 15 minutes.
Add the cream and corn, bring to simmer.
Add clams, simmer for 5 or more minutes, remove bay leaves and ladle into sour dough bread bowls, sprinkle with a little fresh parsley.
