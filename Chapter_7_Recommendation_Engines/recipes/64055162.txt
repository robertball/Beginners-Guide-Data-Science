Title:
Smokehouse Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
8 small tostada shells
1 medium tomato, diced
2 cups shredded lettuce
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 can bush's black beans, drained
1 can bush's smokehouse tradition grillin' beans

Directions:
1 In medium skillet, brown beef over medium heat until cooked through, approximately 8-10 minutes or until beef is no longer pink.
2. Add beans. Cook on medium heat for 5 minutes, until mixture is heated through.
3. Top each tostada with 1/3 coup beef and bean mixture, lettuce, tomato and cheese. Serve.
