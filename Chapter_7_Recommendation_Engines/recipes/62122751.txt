Title:
Country Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Potatoes, thinly sliced
1 bunch of broccoli, trimmed and sliced
4 Carrots, peeled and sliced
1/2 C Celery, sliced
1 Medium Onion, chopped
1/4 C Vegetable oil
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 LB. Smoked Sausage or Kielbasa

Directions:
Heat sausage or kielbasa in skillet for 10 min, set aside and keep warm.  Saute sliced potatoes, broccoli, carrots, celery, and onion in oil over medium heat, cover for quicker cooking time.  Salt and pepper to taste.  When vegetables are tender place sausage on top and heat through.

This is a favorite in our house, and the best way to eat smoked sausage
