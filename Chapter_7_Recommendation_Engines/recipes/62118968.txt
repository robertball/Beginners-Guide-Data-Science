Title:
quick and easy mexican casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 Pkg taco seasoning(reserve 1 tbsp for topping)
1 10 3/4 oz can condensed tomato soup
1-2 cans Spicy chili beans or kidney beans (depending on how many beans you like!)
Topping:
1 cup Hungry Jack Buttermilk pancake mix, or 1 pkg complete pancake mix
1 tbsp reserved taco seasoning
1 cup Mexican 3 blend shredded cheese
1/2 cup milk
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Brown ground beef; drain.
Stir in taco seasoning, soup, and beans.
Simmer while preparing topping.
Combine all topping ingredients and blend well.
Pour meat mixture into ungreased 13x9-inch pan.
Spoon topping over meat mixture and lightly spread.
Sprinkle extra cheese on top.
Bake uncovered for 20 min or until golden brown.
Searve HOT!
