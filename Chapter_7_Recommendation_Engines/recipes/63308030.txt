Title:
Sour Cream Wonder Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (2 sticks) butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup sour cream
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
Filling
1/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup finely ground walnuts
1 tsp nutmeg and/or cinnamon
1/2 cup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Mix batter and filling separately.
Pour half of batter into a bundt pan.
Then pour the filling and top with the other half of the batter.
Bake about 30-35 minutes or until done.
