Title:
Sirloin Tips and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c. chopped onion
2 1/2 pounds beef sirloin
fresh mushrooms, chopped
1 cup beef broth
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 tsp. thyme
salt to taste
freshly ground pepper, to taste
3/4 cup red wine
1/4 c. water
1-1/2 tsp. cornstarch

Directions:
Cut beef into bite size pieces. In a large skillet over medium/high heat, heat the olive oil and brown beef cubes with the onion and garlic.
Add broth, tomato sauce, salt, pepper, thyme and red wine. Cook covered for 45 minutes.  Add fresh mushrooms and cook covered an additional 15 minutes.  Mix cornstarch into water.  Remove cover and add cornstarch/water mixture to thicken.

Submitter: 
Valerie Brunmeier
Description: 
Beef sirloin cubes browned with garlic and onion then simmered in tomato sauce, beef broth and red wine until tender. Great over mashed potatoes.Modified from: Sirloin Tips and Mushrooms
