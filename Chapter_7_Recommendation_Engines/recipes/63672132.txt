Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 8 oz Pkg of softened Cream Cheese
1/2 Stick Butter
1 lb Box Powdered Sugar
2 tsp Vanilla
1/2 Cup Chopped Walnuts

Directions:
In a bowl, beat together the cream cheese, butter and vanilla. Add in the powdered sugar slowly. Beat until smooth. Fold in the nuts and stir until the nuts are well incorporated.
Frost the top of the carrot cakes only. Place one cake on top of the other.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
This is the best frosting for carrot cakes! Yum!!
