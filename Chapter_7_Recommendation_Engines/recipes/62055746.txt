Title:
Easy Potato & Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. Butter
3 T. Flour
1 1/2 C. Chopped Onion (frozen is fine)
2 tsp. Minced Garlic
2 cans Chicken Broth (14 oz. each)
32 oz. package Frozen Southern Style Hash Browned Potatoes
Pinch of Salt
1/2 tsp. Ground White Pepper
1/2 pint Heavy (whipping) Cream

Directions:
Melt butter in large saucepan or dutch oven over low heat.  Add flour and stir until smooth.  Stir and cook for 1-2 minutes.  Add onion & garlic then cook until onion is tender & turns translucent.  Slowly add broth, stirring constantly, until well mixed.  Add potato, salt & pepper then bring to a boil over medium heat.  Cover & reduce heat.  Simmer, stirring occasionally, for 15-20 minutes until potatoes are tender.  Gently shake cream then stir into soup and heat slowly until it just begins to simmer.
*If desired, garnish with a bit of sour cream & chives.

Submitter: 
AMYGANYO
Description: 
A quick & easy potato soup that is creamy!  This is a recipe for which I keep all the ingredients around... just for a cold. blustery day!
