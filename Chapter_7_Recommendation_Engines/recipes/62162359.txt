Title:
Creamy fruit popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 container (6 or 8 oz) of fruit yogurt
1 Tablespoon sugar
1 cup of fruit juice
1/2 cup of another fruit juice (or the same as the first juice)

Directions:
Empty yogurt into a 2 cup measuring cup.
Stir in sugar, until dissolved.
Stir in 1st fruit juice.
Stir in the 2nd fruit juice.
Pour into molds and freeze.
Enjoy in 4 hours.

Submitter: 
ying
Description: 
Fruit pops made with ingredients available in your kitchen.
Try this recipe with cranberry, pomegranate or blueberry juice for their color.
I like Tovolo molds! They come in interesting shapes and are easy to use.
