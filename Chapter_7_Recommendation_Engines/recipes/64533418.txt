Title:
Pumpkin Blueberry Cashew Chocolate Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3073411.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cashew butter
1 ripe, mashed banana
1/2 cup pumpkin puree
1/2 cup almond flour
2 tbsp walnut oil
2 egg whites
1 tbsp honey
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup blueberries*
1/8 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees
Prepare a muffin tin with liners or by spraying with nonstick cooking spray
Add all ingredients to a food processor except blueberries & chocolate chips & blend well
Gently fold blueberries & chocolate chips into batter by hand
Add batter to muffin cavities
Bake 25-30 minutes until a toothpick can be removed clean
Allow muffins to cool 15 minutes in tin before removing & enjoying
Keep muffins in the refrigerator up to a week or freeze up to a month

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Bursting with sweet blueberries & chocolate, these creamy flourless Pumpkin Blueberry Cashew Chocolate Chip Muffins are a decadent, healthy fall treat!
