Title:
Hot Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. chopped, cooked chicken
1 c. miracle whip
2 c. chopped celery
1 onion, chopped
1 small can sweet peas, drained
1/2 c. slivered almonds
1 tsp. accent
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 c. crushed potato chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except the cheese and potato chips.  Mix well.
Pile mixture lightly into a 9 x 13-inch baking dish.  Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top of the mixture.  Sprinkle the crushed potato chips over the cheese.  Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
This dish is tantalizing to the tastebuds.  It has a wonderful blend of chicken, cheese, celery, onion, miracle whip and almonds that puts a pizzazz into the ordinary casserole.  This is one of my family's favorites!
