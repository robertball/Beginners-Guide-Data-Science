Title:
Favourite Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 540ml/18 ounce can garbanzo beans, drained
1 lime juiced
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
1-2  cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon coriander
1 tablespoon Tajin
1 tablespoon tahini
1/2 teaspoon salt
water necessary for desired consistency

Directions:
Blend garbanzo beans, lime juice, olive oil, cider vinegar, garlic, cumin, coriander, Tajin, tahini and salt in a blender; stream water into the mixture and blend until desired consistency is achieved.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
Basic hummus with garbanzo beans, garlic, cumin, salt and olive oil. Extra flavour added with Tajin, tahini, coriander and apple cider vinegar.Modified from: Super Easy Hummus
