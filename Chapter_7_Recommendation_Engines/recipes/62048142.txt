Title:
Creamy seafood soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. bay scallops
1 lb. flounder
6 oz. large shrimp peeled, tails removed
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 T garlic powder
1 T onion powder
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup white wine
salt and pepper to taste
seafood seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Cut raw flounder filet into bite-sized pieces.  Place in small casserole dish along with raw scallops and shrimp.  In a saucepan, heat next 7 ingredients and salt & pepper until hot and bubbly.  Pour over seafood and bake for about 30 minutes or until bubbling.  Serve in bowls topped with a sprinkling of seafood seasoning and parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Joni Zalot
Description: 
Scallops, flounder and shrimp come together in a cheesy, creamy wine broth that's delicious and low carb!
