Title:
Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tilapia filets
lemon pepper
8 corn tortillas
tartar sauce
angel hair cole slaw
cilantro
chili sauce
fresh lime

Directions:
Sprinkle lemon pepper on fish and bake according to package directions.
Cover tortillas with wet paper towels and soften tortillas in the microwave, 30 seconds (depending on how many you have).
Place 1/2 half fish filet on two tortillas (one will fall apart). top with tartar sauce, then cole slaw, add cilantro, chili sauce and finish with lime juice.
