Title:
Chicken stuffed with Bacon and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, thin, boneless chicken breast halves
1 cup grated Mozzerella cheese
6 slices bacon (cooked and cropped)
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon paprika
6 tablespoons butter or Oilve Oil
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/4 cup chopped spinich
1 cup heavy whipping cream
Salt/pepper to taste
2 teaspoons minced fresh garlic
2 teaspoons fresh Thyme leaves
2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Pound chicken breasts if they are too thick. Place cheese and bacon  on each breast within 1/2 inch of the edges. Fold the edges of the chicken over the filling, and secure with toothpicks. Mix the flour and paprika in a small bowl, and coat the chicken pieces.
Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat, and cook the chicken until browned on all sides. Then remove chicken to casserole dish and cook in the oven until done on 350 degrees.  Add the wine and left over bacon, garlic, lemon juice and spinich. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until wine is reduced.   Then add chicken stock and heavy cream and let thicken.
Pour over chicken and top with fresh thyme leaves.
