Title:
Roasted Eggplant and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant, peeled, halved and sliced
2 red bell peppers cut in thick strips
1 green pepper cut in thick strips
1 onion, sliced
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350.  Place the eggplant, peppers and onion in a non-stick baking dish.  Drizzle with oil.  Back 20 min basting regularly.
