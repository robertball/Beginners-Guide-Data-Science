Title:
Orzo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. orzo
3 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/2 cup olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted (optional)
1 1/4 cup feta, crumbled
1 cup thinly sliced scallion greens

Directions:
Cook orzo in salted water until tender; drain.
Whisk together oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper in a large bowl; add hot orzo and toss.
Cool orzo, then toss with pine nuts, feta, and scallions.
Season with more salt and pepper.

This is an easy and delicious side dish- great for a potluck.
