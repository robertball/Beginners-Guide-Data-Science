Title:
Hello Dollies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
¼ cup sugar
½ cup butter, melted
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
3/4 cup butterscotch chips
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325°.  Combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar and melted butter. Press into the bottom of an 9x9 inch baking pan. Layer the chocolate chips, coconut and pecans over the crumbs. Pour the sweetened condensed milk over the top.  Bake at 325° for 25 to 30 minutes or until filling is set. Let cool and cut into squares.  Note - you can use ¾ cup chocolate chips and ¾ cup butterscotch chips is you want.
