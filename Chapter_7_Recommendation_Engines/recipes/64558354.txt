Title:
Ozark Fruit Salad with Orange Poppy Salad Dressing-Daniel Fast approved Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3417128.jpg
Ingredients:
Salad:
8 cups Spring mix (May use 4 cups of Spinach and 4 cups of Romaine instead)
4 kiwi fruit, peeled, cut in to half moon shapes.
2 cups of oranges, peeled, separated into wedges and then cut into half.
1 cup of blueberries
1 1/2 cups of Strawberries, cored and sliced
1/2 cup of slivered(or sliced) almonds, toasted
Dressing:
1/4 cup of olive oil
1/4 cup of orange juice
2 Tablespoons of Lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon of poppy seeds
1/4 teaspoon orange zest
1/8 teaspoon ground mustard
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Salad :Mix all ingredients together in a large salad  bowl. (Hint: If your salad mix is compacted,  rinse first and let drain for 10 minutes[average]).
Dressing: Combine all ingredients in a jar.(small pickle jar will usually work). Shake well,
Note: if dressing sits for too long it will separate, give it a good long shake to re-mix ingredients

Submitter: 
stephanie whitmire
Description: 
This recipe was introduced to me while participating in the Daniel Fast and its become a normal around this house. Wonderful green salad with plenty of fruit and dressing you make yourself. Nothing but pure food here. (and a little bit of oil)
