Title:
Rice Krispie Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine
1 pkg. (10 oz.-about 40) regular
marshmallows, or 4 cups miniature
marshmallows
6 cups Rice Krispies Cereal

Directions:
1)Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat.  Add marshmallows & stir until completely melted.  Remove from heat.
2) Add cereal.  Stir until well coated.
3) Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press evenly into a 13x9" pan.  Cut into squares when cool.
VARIATIONS
EXTRA THICK:  Use 6 tbsp. margarine,
1 pkg. (16 oz) regular marsh-
mallows & 10 cups cereal.  Follow
above directions & carefully stir
cereal into marshmallow mixture.
A 4-quart or larger mixing bowl is
needed to stir the mixture
together.
PEANUT BUTTER:  Stir 1/4 cup peanut
butter into marshmallow mixture
before adding cereal.
PEANUT OR RAISIN:  Add 1 cup peanuts
or raisins with cereal.

Submitter: 
SARA R
Description:
