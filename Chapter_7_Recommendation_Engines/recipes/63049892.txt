Title:
Spicy Thai Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/786103.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb thinly sliced beef, cut into bite sized pieces
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces
1 clove garlic
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp crushed red pepper
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup sesame oil
1 pound spaghetti pasta
6 tbsp honey
6 tbsp soy sauce
1/2 cup green onions, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
3/4 cup peanuts, chopped
1 can bamboo shoots

Directions:
Cook the meat and garlic in the olive oil, until done. Drain.
Cook the noodles according to package directions. Drain
Transfer meat mixture to a plate.
Combine crushed red pepper with vegetable and sesame oils. Saute in pan for two minutes. Strain out pepper.
Combine oil with honey and soy sauce.
Pour over noodles. Add meat. stir until combined.
Put into individual serving dishes and top with chopped ingredients.
Serve!
