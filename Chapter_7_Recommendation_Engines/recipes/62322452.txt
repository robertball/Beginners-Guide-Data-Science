Title:
Blackened Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/379258.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/8 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Soak butterflied chicken in sea salt water.  1 hour.
Marinade for 4 to 6 hours.  Grill until temperture is reached.  Also can be smoked then grilled. Smoke for 6 hours applewood or cherry with cowboy charcoal.
