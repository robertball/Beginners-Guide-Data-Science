Title:
Zucchini soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
3 shallots finely chopped
2 pounds zucchini thickly sliced
4 cups chicken broth
one and a half teaspoon curry powder
one and 1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a skillet, melt butter, cook the shallots and zucchini for 3 minutes.
In a slow cooker, add broth, curry powder, salt, and cayenne. Cover and cook on LOW for 5 to 6 hours.
Puree and serve.
