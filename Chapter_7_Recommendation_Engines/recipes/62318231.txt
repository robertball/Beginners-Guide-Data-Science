Title:
Spicy Baked Bean Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package hot Italian sausages, casings removed
1 28oz. can of baked beans
1 15oz. can of garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 15oz. can of black beans, rinsed and drained
1 15oz. can of kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 15oz. can of black-eyed peas, rinsed and drained
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1 1/2 cup ketchup
1/3 cup brown sugar
2t dry mustard

Directions:
Fry up the hot Italians sausages in a large pot; add chopped onion and bell pepper and cook until vegetables are soft.  Drain fat.
Add everything else into the sausage mixture and mix well; pour into a oven-proof casserole dish; cover and bake for 2 hours at 325 degrees.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
These beans are so good!  They are sweet and spicy with a hint of mustard flavor.  I make these often during the summer to take to picnics and pot-lucks; people always ask for the recipe.
