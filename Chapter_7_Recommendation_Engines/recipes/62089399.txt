Title:
Elderberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry for double crust 9 inch pie
2 Cups Elderberries (cleaned and washed)
2 Cups Sugar
2 Cups Sour Cream
4 Tablespoons Flour
1/4 teaspoon Salt

Directions:
Roll and place bottom crust in a 9 inch pie plate.
Mix Elderberries, sugar, sour cream, flour and salt together.  Place in pie crust.
Roll and cut steam vent in pie crust. Place top crust on pie thoroughly sealing.  Flute edges.  Place on a pie saver and put into a 350 degree oven.  Bake for about 45 minutes until crust is golden brown.  Cool.  Any leftovers should be refrigerated.

Submitter: 
Randy
Description: 
This is an old fashion pie that is sure to please the eye as well as the taste buds.
