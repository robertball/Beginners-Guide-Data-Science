Title:
Simple Strawberry Sherbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts strawberries
1/4 cup lemon juice
3/4 cup of sugar
1/4 cup milk( your choice, the higher the fat content, obviously the creamier your sherbet will be)
1 egg white

Directions:
1. Puree or mash the strawberries.
2. Combine the sugar and lime juice, stir well. Then, mix in the strawberries.
3. Whip your egg white until stiff, then fold into the strawberry-lime mixture.
4. Pour into freezable containers, and cover with saran wrap.
5. After two hours, stir your sherbet, from then on, stir it every hour or so for three hours, then let freeze over night.

Submitter: 
cookielover
Description: 
One night, we just decided to try to make sherbet, it came out very delicious! If you don't have the milk, don't add it, we didn't add any milk and it came out fine, although the milk makes it way creamier.
