Title:
White Trash Hot Dog Boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/504264.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package of 8 hot dogs
6 medium russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/2 cup warm milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine
3/4 teaspoon salt
Dash pepper
4 slices Kraft singles cheese (or any type of cheese you like)

Directions:
1. Heat hot dogs up in the microwave or bring to a boil in water.
2. Place potatoes in a saucepan and cover with water. Cover and bring to a boil; cook for 20-25 minutes or until very tender. Drain well. Add milk, butter, salt and pepper; mash until light and fluffy.
3. Cut hot dogs lengthwise and stuff with mashed potatoes. Place 1/2 a slice of cheese on top of each hot dog.
4. Place under broiler until cheese is bubbly and melted.

Submitter: 
momto3boys
Description: 
This is an old family recipe....My kids love it. They usually eat 3-4 hot dogs each. You could even make this a vegetarian meal by substituting your favorite vegetarian ingredients.
