Title:
Chicken Enchilada Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588806.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
4 cups water
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn
1 (15 ounce) can ranch style beans
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1 1/2 Tablespoons chili powder
1 1/t Tablespoons cumin
1 1/2 teaspons garlic powder
1/2 onion, chopped
1 Tablespoon butter
salt, pepper to taste
Assorted toppings:
Grated cheese
Sour cream
Black Olives
Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Place water in dutch over, cook the chicken in the water over a medium high heat.
Remove chicken from water and shred.  Do NOT drain the water!
Add the chicken soup, corn, beans and tomatoes to the water, mix well. Add the shredded chicken, mix in the garlic powder, cumin, and chili powder.
In a small skillet, saute the onion in the butter until lightly carmelized, add the onion to the soup, stirring to combine.
To serve, place tortilla chips in the bottom of a soup bowl, ladel the soup on top, garnish with your preferred toppings, cheese, sour cream, black olives, additional tortilla chips etc.,

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
This soup takes the flavour of enchiladas and turns it into a soup that is both savoury and hearty.  It's good on those blustery winter days, or nippy fall days!  It can easily be prepped, frozen and then popped into a slow cooker.
