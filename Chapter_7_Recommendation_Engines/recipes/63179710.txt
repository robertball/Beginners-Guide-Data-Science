Title:
BANANAS FOSTER-FILLED FRENCH TOAST Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FILLING:
2 TBSP BUTTER
1/4 CUP DARK BROWN SUGAR
1/4 TSP CINNAMON
FRENCH TOAST
1 EGG
1/4 CUP MILK
1/4 TSP VANILLA
PINCH OF SALT
1 BANANA PEELED & FINELY CHIPPED
1/8 TSP RUM EXTRACT
1 LOAF FRESH SOFT BREAD
BUTTER FOR FRYING
POWDERED SUGAR

Directions:
FILLING-MELT BUTTER IN SMALL PAN, THEN ADD BROWN SUGAR & CINNAMON.  COOK OVER MED HEAT UNTIL BUBBLY. STIR IN THE BANANAS AND RUM, THEN REMOVE FROM HEAT
FRENCH TOAST-BEAT EGG, LIQUID, VANILLA & SALT. SET ASIDE. PLACE A TSP OF FILLING IN THE CENTER OF A SLICE OF BREAD. COVER WITH ANOTHER SLICE. CUT AND SEAL THE LAYERS WITH THE CUT-N-SEAL. DIP FILLED BREAD INTO EGG MIXTURE. LIGHTLY SAUTE ON BOTH SIDES IN MELTED BUTTER. SERVICE WARM, DUST W/POWDERED SUGAR

Submitter: 
Colleen Boni
Description: 
*USE CUT-N-SEAL FROM PAMPERED CHEF
