Title:
Air Fryer Fried Okra Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5661428.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large egg, beaten
1/2 pound okra pods, cut into 1/2 inch slices
1 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup flour
cooking spray
salt to taste

Directions:
1. Place egg in a shallow dish and gently stir in sliced okra.
2. Combine cornmeal and flour in a gallon size plastic zip bag.
3. Drop 5 slices of okra in the cornmeal mixture, zip the bag, and shake. Remove breaded okra to a plate. Repeat with remaining okra slices.
4. Set air fryer to 400 degrees. Place half of the breaded okra slices in the basket of the air fryer and spray with cooking spray. Cook 4 minutes.
5. Shake the basket and mist with cooking spray again. Cook an additional 4 minutes.
6. Shake the basket one last time and cook for 2 minutes. Remove the okra from the basket and salt to taste. Repeat with remaining half of the okra slices.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Fried okra made easy in an air fryer without the guilt or mess of a deep fry.
