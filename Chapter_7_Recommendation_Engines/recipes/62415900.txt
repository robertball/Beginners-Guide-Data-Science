Title:
Cold Oven Lemon Pound Cake With Less Sugar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine
1/2 cup shortening
2 cups white sugar
3 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup milk
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/8 teaspoon Lemon Extract
2 tablespoons Poppyseed

Directions:
Do not preheat oven. Grease and flour a 10 inch tube pan.
In a large bowl, cream together the margarine, shortening and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla & lemon extract.  Beat in the flour alternately with the milk. Pour batter into prepared pan. Sprinkle with Poppyseed.
Place cake in cold oven, then set the temperature to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Bake for 90 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
