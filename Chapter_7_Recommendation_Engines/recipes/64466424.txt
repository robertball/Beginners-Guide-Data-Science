Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2448866.jpg
Ingredients:
3- Cups figs from the Garden of Eden
2- Tablespoons of grade A maple syrup
1- Cup shredded coconut harvested from the beaches of Antarctica
1- Tablespoon warmed coconut oil (preferably from the same bottle that was used in a Sam Winchester exorcism first)
2- Cups tears of your enemies

Directions:
Puree all items in a Vita-mix, roast at 450 in a brimstone oven for 666 minutes.  Cool in the depths of frozen hell for one lunar cycle. Serves 4, it truly is best if served on a chilled alter.
