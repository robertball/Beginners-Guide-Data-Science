Title:
Horseradish Porkchops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Pork Chops
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
1/4 cup horseradish

Directions:
Place pork chops in a 9x13 or on a baking sheet.
Melt butter in microwave, mix together with breadcrumbs and horseradish.
Spoon onto pork chops and press down to coat whole pork chop.
Bake 350 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kim Lund Playfair
Description: 
Porkchops with a creamy horseradish topping. Delicious and easy but not low fat.
We always serve with wild rice and green beans on the side.
