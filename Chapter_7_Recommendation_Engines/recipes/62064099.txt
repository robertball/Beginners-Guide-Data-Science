Title:
Easy Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 (15 oz.) jar spaghetti sauce
1 (10.5 oz.) can tomato soup
1 T minced onion
1 t oregano
3 T sugar
1 8 oz. package spaghetti
2 cups shredded cheese

Directions:
1. Brown ground beef and drain. Add spaghetti sauce, tomato soup, minced onion, oregano, and sugar. Simmer over low heat for 1 hour.
2. Cook spaghetti to desired tenderness, drain, and fold into sauce. Place into large casserole dish.
3. Bake, covered, at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.
4. Remove from oven, top with shredded cheese, and heat until cheese melts.
