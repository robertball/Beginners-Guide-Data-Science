Title:
Sesame Pasta Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sesame seeds
1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta
1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/3 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
1/3 cup rice vinegar
1 teaspoon sesame oil
3 tablespoons white sugar or Splenda White Sugar Blend
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 cups diced, cooked chicken breast meat
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1/3 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup baby carrots, halved
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup snow peas, cut in half
1/2 cup water chestnuts, halved
1 cup red bell pepper, chopped

Directions:
Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
Add sesame seeds, and cook stirring frequently until lightly toasted.
Remove from heat, and set aside.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente.
Drain pasta, and rinse under cold water until cool.
Transfer to a large bowl.
In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine extra-virgin olive oil, soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, sesame seeds, ginger, and ground black pepper; shake well.
Pour sesame dressing over pasta, and toss to coat evenly.
Gently mix in chicken, carrots, celery, snow peas, water chestnuts, cilantro, green onions, and red bell pepper.
