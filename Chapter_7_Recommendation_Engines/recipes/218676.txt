Title:
Ham with Maille® Orange Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982461.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup orange marmalade
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup Maille® Old Style or Dijon Originale Mustard

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in small saucepan. Bring to a gentle boil, stirring until thoroughly combined. Use as a sauce for sliced ham (also good with pork chops or pork tenderloin).
This is also excellent as a glaze. Just spoon over entire ham about 30 minutes before ham is done, basting two or three times.

Submitter: 
Maille
Description: 
Here's a delicious orange sauce with mustard that's perfect served with sliced ham, pork chops, or pork tenderloin, or as a glaze spooned over your favorite baked ham.
