Title:
Delightful Cherry Berry Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup blueberries or strawberries
1 cup blackberries of raspberries
1 cup cherries
6 scoops (about 2 cups) vanilla ice cream
1 1/2 cup milk

Directions:
put all ingredients into a blender,
whirl on high for about one minute,
pour into cups and serve.

Submitter: 
geranium
Description: 
This recipe is a mix between a smoothie and a milkshake. It combines the tart flavor of berries with the creamy texture of ice cream.
