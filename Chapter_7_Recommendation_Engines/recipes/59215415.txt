Title:
Rich Beef and Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds stew beef
1 T Cooking oil
1 cup thinly sliced carrot
1 cup sliced celery
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup coarsely chopped green pepper
4 cups beef broth
1 - 14.5 oz can tomatoes, undrained, cut up
1 cup purchased spaghetti sauce
2/3 cup pearly barley
1 1/2 t. dried basil, crushed
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. ground black pepper
1/4 fresh parsley

Directions:
Cut meat into 1 inch piece. In a large skillet brown meat, half at a time in hot oil.  Drain well.
Meanwhile- in a 3.5 to 6 qt slow cooker combine carrot, celery, onion and sweet pepper.  Add broth, undrained tomatoes, spaghetti sauce, barely, basil, salt and pepper.  Stir in browned meat.  Cover and cook on low for 9 to 10 hours on low or on high for 4.5 to 5 hours.
