Title:
Gang up Salmon Quiche with Hollandaise Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507079.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C Flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/6 C butter
5 tsp ice water
1/2 small onion diced
1 small red potato diced
3 oz leftover poached salmon
2 eggs
1/2 C milk
1 recipe Southern Hollandaise Sauce

Directions:
Make the dough: mix dry ingredients together (flour & Salt) cut in butter, til mixed and crumbly. Toss with 1 tsp of water at a time until the dough holds together. Roll dough out thin enough so that it will fit your personal sized tart or pie pan. Lay in the pan, and prebake the crust for 10 minutes in a preheated 350 degree oven.
While the crust is cooking, sautee the diced onion and potato. Cook until potatoes are mostly done. Add S&P  to taste. Put in bottom of pie pan. top with flaked salmon. scramble eggs and milk together, and fill the rest of the pie pan. Bake the quiche for 30 minutes at 350 or until the middle is set.
To serve - top the quiche with a drizzle of hollandaise sauce.
