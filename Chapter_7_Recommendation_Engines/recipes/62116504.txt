Title:
Lemon Chive Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/372744.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chicken broth
1/4 tsp. salt
2 tbsp. olive oil
3/4 cup couscous
1 tbsp. lemon zest
2 tbsp. fresh chives, finely snipped
1 tbsp. lemon juice
Salt and pepper
Chopped scallions (optional)

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, bring chicken broth, salt and olive oil to a boil.
Stir in couscous, lemon zest and chives.
Remove from heat and let sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
Add lemon juice, salt and pepper.
Garnish with chopped scallions, if desired.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Subtly seasoned with lemon and chives, this simple, quick-cooking side dish pairs wonderfully with baked fish or chicken.
