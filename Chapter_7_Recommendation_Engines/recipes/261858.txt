Title:
Abbey's Easy Chocolate Chip Cake Mix Bar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F7744762.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 (12 ounce) bag chocolate chips
½ cup vegetable oil
2  eggs
⅓ cup brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix yellow cake mix, chocolate chips, oil, eggs, and brown sugar together in a bowl. Pour into an ungreased 9x13-inch baking pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes.
