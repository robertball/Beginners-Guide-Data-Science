Title:
Italian Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 lbs lean hamburger
2 tbsp Worcestershire
1 cup chopped celery
2 can diced tomatoes
4 large can tomato sauce
2 large can water
2 cans consomme or beef broth
2 small cans tomato paste
2 tbsp salt
1 tsp celery salt
1 tbsp oregano
1 1/2 tsp basil
2 tsp marjoram
2 tsp rosemary
1 tbsp ground pepper
1 tbsp cayenne red pepper
few drop tabasco sauce
4 tbsp brown sugar
4 tbsp sugar

Directions:
In frying pan, brown hamburger meat and transfer to large pot.
Brown the celery and add to hamburger meat.
Add spices, tabasco sauce and sugar to meat and celery and mix well.
Add tomato sauce, tomato paste and water.
Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for a severa hours.  An hour before serving, remove lid and continue simmering.
