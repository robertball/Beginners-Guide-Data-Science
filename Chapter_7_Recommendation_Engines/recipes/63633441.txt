Title:
Outback Special Steak Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TB Old Bay Seasoning
1 TB light brown sugar
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Mix all together and store in an airtight container.
Rub on both sides of steak (or pork chops) before grilling.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This clone of Outback Steakhouse's steak rub is great! Works great for steaks, pork chops and even seafood!
