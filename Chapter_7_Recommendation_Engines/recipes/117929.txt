Title:
Spicy Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989960.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoons reduced-fat plain yogurt
1 tablespoon snipped fresh dill
1 1/2 teaspoons hot pepper sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 garlic clove, minced
1 pound lean ground turkey
4 Kaiser rolls, split
4 lettuce leaves
4 tomato slices

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the onion, yogurt, dill, hot pepper sauce, salt and garlic. Crumble turkey over mixture; mix well.
Shape into four patties, each about 3/4 in. thick. Grill, uncovered, over medium-hot heat for 6-8 minutes on each side or until no longer pink. Serve on rolls with lettuce and tomato.

Submitter: 
Mavis  Diment
Description: 
The hot pepper sauce comes through nicely to spark the flavor of these moist turkey burgers shared by Mavis Diment from Marcus, Iowa. 'This is a good low-fat burger without the boring taste of typical low-fat foods,' she notes.
