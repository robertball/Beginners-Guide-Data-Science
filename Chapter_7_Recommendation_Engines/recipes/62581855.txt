Title:
Snappy Pumpkin Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c finely crushed ginger snaps
1/2 c melted butter
8oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1/2 c powdered sugar
2 tbsp milk
Topping
3 c cold milk
2 small pkgs vanilla pudding mix
1 can solid pack pumpkin
2 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
2 c whipped topping

Directions:
Combine ginger snaps & melted butter & press into a 9 by 13 pan. Bake @ 325 for 10 min. Cool. In a mixing bowl beat the cream cheese, powdered sugar & milk until fluffy. Spread over the crust. In another bowl beat the milk & pudding for 1 min. Add pumpkin & spice & beat till well blended. Fold in whipped topping. Spread over the cream cheese layer. Chill for at least 3 hrs. Cut into squares & garnish w/ more whipped topping.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a bit of a different pumpkin dessert from what you usually see. Perfect for people who aren't crazy about pumpkin pie but still want to have a traditional holiday dessert.
