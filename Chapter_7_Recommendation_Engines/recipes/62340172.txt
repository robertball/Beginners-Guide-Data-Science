Title:
Oreo Mint Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Oreo's - any kind, crushed
1 8 oz. pkg cream cheese
1 pkg. almond bark blocks (either white or choc)
Sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
Cream together Oreo's and cream cheese and form balls.  Melt bark and dip balls into bark.  Once done, you can put sprinkles on them or drizzle with any other type of almond bark.
