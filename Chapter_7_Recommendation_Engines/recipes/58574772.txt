Title:
Tuna Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/613374.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups raw elbow macaroni
1 large can Albacore Tuna
1 cup celery (diced)
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1 cup of mayonaise
2 Tblsp chopped sweet pickle
4 oz. cheddar chese (cubed)
1 box of frozen sweet peas

Directions:
Cook macaroni, drain and cool
Add all ingredients
Mix and chill

Submitter: 
Katrina Freed
Description: 
Kitty's birthday salad.  Traditionally we have made this every July for years and years.  Until Grandma Wells died the kids never knew it was originally her recipe.
