Title:
Grandma Linda's Tomato Aspic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups tomato juice
1/4 cup chopped celery leaves
1 tsp salt
4 whole cloves
3 tbsp lemon juice
1/3 c chopped onion
2 tbsp brown sugar
2 bay leaves
2 envelopes unflavored gelatin
1 c finely chopped celery

Directions:
Mix two cups tomato juice with next six ingredients, simmer uncovered for 5 minutes, strain. Meanwhile soften gelatin in 1 cup cold tomato juice. Dissolve in hot mixutre. Add remaining tomato juice and lemon juice Chill until partially set. Add celery. Pour into 5-cup ring mold.
