Title:
Pickled Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can sliced ir diced beets, drained, 1/4 cup liquid reserved
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup finely diced red onion
1 tsp yellow mustard

Directions:
Combine everything but the beats in a small saucepan.  Boil until onions are translucent and sauce is syrupy.  Allow to cool about 10 minutes.  Pour syrup over beets and toss to coat.  Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
