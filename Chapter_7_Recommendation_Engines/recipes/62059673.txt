Title:
Kat's Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 onion, finely chopped
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp oregano
1 lb. ground turkey
1 28 oz. can whole tomatoes
1/2 small can of tomato paste
1 cup water
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 bottle of your favorite beer
1 tsp red wine vinegar
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
3 tsp sugar
1 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tbsp chili powder
2 tbsp cumin
2 tsp paprika
1 tbsp salt
2 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
1 green pepper, de-seeded & chopped
1 10 oz. bag frozen corn
1 15 oz. can red kidney beans
1 15 oz. can white kidney beans

Directions:
1. Saute the onion and garlic with the olive oil over medium heat in a large cooking pot until the onions are translucent. Add the oregano and cook a couple minutes more.
2. Add the turkey and cook thoroughly.
3. Now, add the canned whole tomatoes, and use a spatula to break into small pieces.
4. Mix the tomato paste with the cup of water.
5. Add the tomato paste/water mixture along with the rest of the ingredients to the pot.
6. Bring to a boil, then allow to simmer, uncovered, for about 30 minutes, or cook longer if desired.
If mixture too thick, add more chicken broth or water.
Delicious served over rice!
