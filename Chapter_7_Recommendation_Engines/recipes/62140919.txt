Title:
Flavor Craver Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chili sauce
1 (8oz) cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 tsp. horseradish
1 can (4 1/2 oz) tiny shrimp, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Blend chili sauce gradually into cream cheese.
Mix in mayonnaise, onion, horseradish.
Fold in shrimp.
Cover and chill at least one hour.

Submitter: 
LEXI70
Description: 
A chilled dip that is full of flavor and sure to please.  The longer it's chilled the better the taste!
