Title:
Cindy's Roasted Red Pepper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/803644.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese
1 14oz jar roasted red peppers
1 1oz package Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing mix (or 3 Tablespoons of bulk Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing mix)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Rinse the roasted red peppers, drain and place in food processor.  Add the cheeses and the dry Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing mix.  Process until mostly smooth.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
Delicious served on toasted french bread or as a dip for flavored crackers.  Use in place of pimento cheese.. yummy!
