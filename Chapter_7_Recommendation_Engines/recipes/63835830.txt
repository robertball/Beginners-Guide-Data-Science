Title:
20-year Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3# Granny Smith or other tart apples
Juice of 1 lemon
1/2 c white sugar
1 T quality cinnamon
1/2 t sea salt
1-2 shakes of quality nutmeg
2 T AP flour
2 T tapioca pudding base
1/2 stick, 4 T butter
1/4 c Heavy cream
Pie crusts of your choice, (homemade or purchased)

Directions:
Peel and dice apples, toss in lemon juice.
Combine flour, tapioca, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Sprinkle dry mix over apples, combine and coat.
Place bottom in pie dish.
Place apple mixture in bottom, add butter in pats evenly.
Wet pie edges with heavy cream, (to create seal).
Place top on pie, (crimp and seal).
Use fork to make vents in top.
Use pastry brush or paper towel to wet top with heavy cream, (for even browning).
top with granulated sugar for texture.
