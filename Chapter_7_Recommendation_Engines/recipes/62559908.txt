Title:
Golden Grahams S'mores bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/542600.jpg
Ingredients:
1 10 ounce box of Golden Grahams cereal
5 cups mini marshmallows
1 11.5 oounce bag chocolate chunk chips
6 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Melt butter in a large sauce pan over low heat.
Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
Remove from heat and stir in cereal.
Add chocolate chips and mix until evenly distributed.
Press mixture lightly into a well greased or parchment lined 9 by 13 pan.  (You might want to grease your hands with a bit of butter or Pam spray for this.)
Cool and cut into slices.
Store in a covered container to keep them fresh.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Gooey and sweet with a bit of crunch just like traditional s'mores.   I believe this recipe was once on the cereal box back in the 70's.  Still yummy!
