Title:
Deep Red Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups apple cider
3 cups dry red wine
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp. whole cloves
1/4 tsp. allspice
1 stick cinnamon

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on low 3-4 hours.
Remove cloves, allspice and cinnamon before serving.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Can use all apple cider and no wine if desired.
