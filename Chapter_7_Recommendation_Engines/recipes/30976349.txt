Title:
Velveeta Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cubed Velveeta Cheese
2 tbsp milk

Directions:
Combine Velveeta and milk in 1 1/2 quart microwavable bowl.
Microwave on high 4 to 6 minutes or until Velveeta is completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring every 2 minutes.
Serve immediately.
