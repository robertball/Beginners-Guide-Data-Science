Title:
Mom's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293102.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1 1/2 cups sugar
2  egg
3  ripe banana; 1 cup mashed
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 pinch salt
4 tablespoons milk; or buttermilk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar; add eggs. Combine flour, soda and salt. Add dry ingredients. Mix in bananas, nuts, milk and vanilla.
Bake at 350Â°F (reduce heat for dark or non-stick pans) for 1 hour (or until toothpick inserted is removed dry). At high altitude, it takes at least 75 minutes.
