Title:
Pita Pocket Breakfast Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
2 large egg whites
1 tbs milk
1 cup baby spinach torn into small pieces
4 grape tomatoes sliced in half lengthwise
2 green onions, diced
Sea salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup Feta cheese crumbles
1 whole wheat pita pocket, cut in half
2 tsp olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a medium bowl whisk together eggs, egg whites, milk, spinach, tomatoes, green onions, and salt and pepper to taste.
Pour egg mixture into  an 8" non-stick skillet, place in oven on middle rack.
Brush both sides of each pita half with olive oil, place on foil and warm in the oven during the last 2 minutes  of cooking time.
Cook eggs approx 15 - 18 minutes or until puffy and just set in the center, but not hard.
Sprinkle on the feta cheese and return to oven for one more minute.
Remove skillet from oven, cut omelt in halfand place each half in a pita pocket.  Press edges into warm pia and serve immediately.
