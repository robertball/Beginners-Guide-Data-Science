Title:
Party Style Tater Tots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen tater tots
1 bag preshredded pizza blend cheese
2 cans hotdog chili
20-30 jalepeno pepper slices (optional)
6 tablespoons buttermilk ranch dressing

Directions:
Bake tator tots on a non-stick pan as directed on package until golden brown, about 18-20 minutes.
In a casserole dish, layer cooked tots, chili, then sprinkle cheese on the top. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
Remove from oven and add the jalepeno pepper slices to the top of the casserole and bake an additional 5 minutes.
Serve warm, drizzled with buttermilk ranch.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
Tater tots combined with chili, cheese, buttermilk ranch and jalepeno peppers make this the hit of any party!
