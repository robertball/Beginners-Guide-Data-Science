Title:
Knorr Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1080821.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pkgs. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained very well
1/2 - 1 cup chopped onions
1 cup sour cream
1 cup mayonnaise
1 (8 oz) can water chestnuts, diced
1 (1.4 oz) pkg. Knorr Vegetable Soup Mix

Directions:
Mix onions, sour cream, mayonnaise, water chestnuts, and Knorr dry Vegetable Soup Mix together.
Add the spinach (make sure all water is squeezed out) and stir well.
Refrigerate and let set overnight for flavors to meld.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
My family has made this spinach dip for over 30 years. It's been passed down from grandmother to daughter to granddaughter. We've served it at holidays, wedding receptions, and cookouts. It's just a great fresh dip to serve with your favorite bread or crackers.
