Title:
Fiesta Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooked rice, 1 can drained ro-tel, 1 can drained beans of choice (black best ),
4 ears of grilled corn on the cob

Directions:
Cook 2 cups dry rice. grill 4 ears corn on cob.. drain 1 can Ro-tel. drain 1 can black beans or choice..
when corn cool , slice kernals off and add to cooked rice... add drained Ro-tel, and can of drained beans... you can add fresh chopped parsely before serving.

Submitter: 
rick roper
Description: 
I just made this up because corn was so cheap at store, looking for uses... 
This is colorful tasty and healthy side dish or eaten alone...
