Title:
Easy Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 French baguettes
Small jar prepared pesto
Roma tomatoes
Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Thinly slice baguettes; toast in 350 oven for 3 minutes. Spread prepared pesto over toast, add thinly sliced tomato, top with mozzarella cheese. Bake until cheese is melted.

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy, pretty appetizer with only 4 ingredients (if you buy the pesto already prepared). Served and it was GONE, with rave reviews as it disappeared!
