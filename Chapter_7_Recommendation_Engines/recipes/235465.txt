Title:
Pumpkin Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F2611939.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups confectioners' sugar
¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
¼ cup pumpkin puree
1 tablespoon shortening
½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Beat confectioners' sugar, butter, pumpkin, shortening, and vanilla extract together in a bowl using an electric mixer until frosting is fluffy and smooth.

Submitter: 
MrsFisher0729
Description: 
Fantastic flavored frosting perfect for fall! Fantastic on caramel, spice, or chocolate cupcakes! Add more confectioners' sugar for a thicker consistency and more pumpkin for a thinner frosting.
