Title:
Mint Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120595.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup tightly packed mint leaves
1 cup water
1/2 cup cider vinegar
3 1/2 cups sugar
5 drops green food coloring
3 oz liquid pectin

Directions:
Wash mint, remove stems, and coarsely chop the leaves.
Place mint leaves, water, vinegar and sugar into heavy kettle or large sauce pan, and bring quickly to a full boil, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat, add food coloring and pectin then return to a full boil, boiling 30 seconds.
Remove immediately from heat, skim and then strain through two layers of damp cheesecloth.
Pour the strained hot liquid into sterile jelly jars.
Process jars in hot water bath 15 minutes.
Refrigerate any unsealed jars

Submitter: 
Brian E. Nahodil
Description: 
This easy to make mint jelly is a perfect accompaniment to Lamb, or as an added surprise to an exotic sandwich and even great on toast.  By coarsely chopping the mint leaves and using a good abundance of green food coloring, you get a full flavored result with a tempting and appetizing color.  Bon Appetite!
