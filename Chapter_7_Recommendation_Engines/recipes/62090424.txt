Title:
simple butter cream sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
melt butter in saucepan over medium low heat.  Slowly add heavy whipping cream.       Salt, pepper, or garlic can be added if desired.  Great on pasta or vegetables.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
heat butter and heavy whipping cream together to make this sauce.
