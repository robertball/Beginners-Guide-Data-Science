Title:
Aunt Esther's Chicken-Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6534861.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cooked chicken breasts, diced (about 2 cups of cooked chicken)
1 box jumbo shells (approx. 24 needed)
1 6-serving box Stove Top Chicken-flavored Stuffing
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup mayonnaise
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Prepare shells and stuffing mix seperately, each to package directions.
Add chicken and mayonnaise to the prepared stuffing.
Stuff shells with chicken mixture.
Mix soup and broth; pour and spread a little in a 9 x 13 baking dish.
Place shells, open side up, in dish.
Pour remaining soup/broth mixture over all and sprinkle cheese on top.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 40-60 minutes, until hot and bubbly.
