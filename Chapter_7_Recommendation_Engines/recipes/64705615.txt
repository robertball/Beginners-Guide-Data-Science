Title:
Overnight Stuffed French Toast with Fresh Fruit Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb cinnamon swirl bread, without crusts, cubed
1  8 oz pkg cream cheese, cubed
12 eggs
1/2 real maple syrup
2 c milk
1 lb fresh fruit (blueberries or strawberries  or ?)
1/4 c sugar
1/4 c water
1/2 tbsp cornstarch (mix with small amount of water before adding to fruit mixture)

Directions:
Grease 9x13” baking dish and place half of bread cubes on the bottom.
Evenly place cream cheese cubes on top of the first bread layer and then place remaking bread cubes on top of cream cheese cubes.
In a large bowl, beat together eggs, syrup and milk. Pour egg mixture over bread, cover with aluminum foil and chill overnight.
Preheat oven to 350°. Bake for 30 minutes with foil on. Remove foil and bake an additional 30 minutes.
For sauce:
Reserve some fresh fruit for garnish.
Combine approximately 2 c of remaining fruit, sugar, water, and cornstarch in a small saucepan and bring to boil. Stirring constantly, boil for 5 minutes. Reduce heat to simmer about 5-10 more minutes, until mixture is thick.
Serve warm fruit syrup over slices of fresh toast. Top with whipped cream, optional.
