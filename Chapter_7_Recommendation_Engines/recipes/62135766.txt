Title:
Lime Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Ears Corn
2 TBSP Butter
1 Lime

Directions:
Place ears of corn in individual foil wrappers.  Add one teaspoon of butter to each ear.  Squeeze lime equally over the four ears of corn.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
John Cox
Description: 
Grilled corn with a hint of lime.
