Title:
Mary Frances' Antipasto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceburg lettuce
2 whole tomatoes, diced
2 whole cucumbers, diced
1 whole onion, chopped fine
1/4 lb hard salami
1/2 lb ham
1/4 lb provolone cheese slices
1/4 lb munster cheese slices
1 jar green olives
olive oil, to taste
2 baguettes, sliced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except bread.  Toss well and chill.
Serve with sliced baguettes.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
My former roommate and friend made this for New Year's one time, and I loved it!   It's a new holiday tradition for me!
