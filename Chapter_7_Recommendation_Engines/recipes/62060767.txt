Title:
VELVEETA  CHOCOLATE  FUDGE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Velveeta cheese
1/2 lb. oleo
2 lbs. sifted powder sugar
1/2 c. cocoa
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. nuts, optional

Directions:
In Microwave:  Melt cheese and oleo together.  Cook 30 seconds on high.  Stir well.  Cook another 30 seconds.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well.    Pour into a 9 x 13 inch greased pan.  (Best when greased with butter or butter flavored Crisco).
