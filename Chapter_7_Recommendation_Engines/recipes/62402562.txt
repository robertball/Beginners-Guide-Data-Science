Title:
Peanut Butter Balls 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C butter
1 lb powdered sugar
8oz bar milk chocolate
1/2 cake paraffin wax
2C peanut butter
1C Rice krispies
6oz semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Blend butter, peanut butter and sugar together. Add rice kripies. Roll into balls and chill overnight.  Melt chocolate bar, chocolate chips and wax in a double boiler. Dip balls in mix and allow to dry on wax paper.
