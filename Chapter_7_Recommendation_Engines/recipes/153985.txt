Title:
Easy Triple-Cheese Lasagna (with Meat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980521.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can CONTADINAÂ® Seasoned Crushed Tomatoes with Italian Herbs
1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage (casings removed)
6 uncooked dried lasagna noodles
2 cups shredded Italian cheese blend or mozzarella cheese
1 1/2 cups cottage cheese or ricotta cheese
2 tablespoons grated or shredded Parmesan cheese
Fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
Spoon 1 cup crushed tomatoes in 2-quart rectangular baking dish.
Cook meat in large skillet; drain. Stir in remaining undrained tomatoes; bring to boil.
Place 2 uncooked noodles in dish; top with 1/3 each of the meat mixture and Italian cheese. Repeat layers. Dollop with cottage cheese. Top with remaining noodles, meat mixture and Italian cheese.
Cover and bake in preheated 350 degrees F oven 1 hour. Let stand 15 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Garnish with parsley, if desired.
