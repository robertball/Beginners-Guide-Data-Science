Title:
Creamy Tropical Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990113.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package ladyfingers, split
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1 ripe banana, mashed
1 teaspoon CRYSTAL LIGHT Strawberry Orange Banana Flavor Drink Mix
1/4 cup fat-free milk
1 (8 ounce) container COOL WHIP FREE Whipped Topping, thawed
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin orange segments, drained

Directions:
Line bottom and side of 1-1/2-qt. glass serving bowl with ladyfingers; set aside.
Beat cream cheese, bananas and drink mix in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended and smooth. Gradually add milk, mixing until well blended. Gently stir in whipped topping and oranges. Spoon into prepared bowl.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm. Store leftover dessert in refrigerator.

Whip up this impressive fruity dessert for any special occasion.
