Title:
Instant Pot Beef and Veg Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound extra lean ground beef
2 celery ribs, finely chopped
2 med. carrots, finely chopped
1 lg. onion, finely chopped
1 med. red pepper, finely chopped
3 lg. cloves garlic, finely minced
1 19 ounce can white kidney beans, drained and rinced
1 19 ounce can red kidney beans, drained and rinced
1 28 ounce can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup beef broth
1 tsp cumin
1 tbs chili powder or more to taste
1 pinch red pepper flakes or more to taste
1 tbs fresh lemon juice
1-2 tbs tomato paste
1 tbs + 1 tsp olive oil, divided.
Optional toppings, shredded cheddar cheese, chopped green onions and sour cream.

Directions:
Add 1 tbs of oil in the pot, saute function med.
When the pot is hot add the beef and brown, season with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.
Drain and set aside.
Add 1 tsp of oil to the pot, saute function high, and add the onion, carrot, pepper and garlic mixture. Saute until the veggies are soft, about 5 minutes.
Add the cumin and chili and cook until fragrant, 1-2 minutes.
Add the beef broth and deglaze the pot.
Add the red and white kidney beans and stir well.
Add the diced tomatoes, do NOT stir.
Place the lid on the instant pot and press the Bean/Chili button. Press the adjust button and press the - button to decrease the time to 10 minutes.
Use natural release.
Stir in the tomato paste, 1-2 tbs depending on hot thick you want your chili.
Let this sit in the pot for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Extra lean ground beef and lots of veggies are cooked with diced tomatoes for a hearty meal for a cold day.
