Title:
Chicken Moe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1-14 oz. can condensed cream of chicken soup
1/3 cup water
2 tablespoons spreadable cream cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Ground black pepper, to taste
375 g. box medium bowtie pasta

Directions:
Slice chicken breasts into 1/2 x 2-inch strips.
Heat vegetable oil in a large frying pan.
Add garlic and chicken and saute until chicken is cooked and starting to brown.
Add cream of chicken soup and water to frying pan and stir to blend. Heat to boiling.
Reduce heat and add cream cheese. Stir until melted.
Add parmesan cheese and black pepper and mix well.
Reduce heat on sauce and keep warm while pasta is cooking.
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain well, and toss with sauce.
