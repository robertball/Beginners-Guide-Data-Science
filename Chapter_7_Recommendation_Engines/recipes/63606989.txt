Title:
Garlic Lime Shrimp in Tomatillo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1003828.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons of light virgin olive oil
3 garlic cloves minced
1/2 cup of yellow bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup of green bell pepper, chopped
1/8 tsp. pepper
24 precooked Jumbo Shrimp
Juice of one lime or to taste
Herdez Tomatillo Verde Sauce 12 oz. jar

Directions:
Heat up olive oil over medium heat in a large fry pan.
Saute yellow and green pepper for 10 minutes.
Add minced garlic. Saute for 1 minute.
Add shrimp, pepper, lime juice and saute for 2 minutes.
Add one jar of Herdez Tomatillo Verde Sauce
Cover and simmer over low heat until heated through.
Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
Jumbo shrimp sauteed with olive oil, garlic and lime juice and then simmered in Herdez Tomatillo Verde Cooking Sauce.
