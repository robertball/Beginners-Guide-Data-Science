Title:
Homestyle Kalua Pork w/ Cabbage in a slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lb pork butt or pork shoulder with bone-in
2 Tbs Kosher salt
2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp ginger
1 Tbs soy sauce
1 tsp liquid smoke
2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 large cabbage, shredded

Directions:
Place roast into slow cooker. Mix all dry ingredients and rub onto pork. Mix sauces and pour onto meat. Cook on low for 10 hours.
Shred cabbage and put in slow cooker. Cook on high for 30 min. Shred pork and serve with rice, buns, or poi.

Submitter: 
Mei
Description: 
Inspired by the luaus in Hawaii, I set out to make an easy, tasty slow-cooker version of kahlua pork. The meat is fall-off-the-bone yummy. This recipe is not overly smokey, but you may add more smoke if you like.
