Title:
Low Fat Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened (1/3 less fat OR Fat Free)
1/4 cup Light Miracle Whip
1/4 cup low fat OR fat free sour cream
1/4 cup grated Low Fat Parmesan cheese
1 tbsp grated Romano cheese
3 cloves of garlic, peeled and minced
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
3/4 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained thoroughly
1/4 cup shredded low fat mozzarella cheese
dash of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Lightly grease a small baking dish.
2. In a medium bowl, mix together softened cream cheese, mayo, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, garlic, garlic salt, red pepper OR cayenne, salt and pepper for taste.  Once that is mixed well then stir in the artichoke hearts and spinach.  Mix well.
3. Put the mixture into the prepared baking dish. Top it with 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese. Bake in the preheated 325 degree oven for 35-40 minutes, until bubbly and lightly browned. (Cooking times may vary depending on your oven, so adjust cooking time as needed.) Let it stand and cool for 5-10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
This recipe is for a Low Fat version of the famous Spinach and Artichoke dip. It is delicious served warm. I have made this dip so many ways now to try to get the best tasting dip, yet low in fat and healthier.  This stuff is really divine served with Reduced Fat Triscuits, bread, raw veggies, or even by itself!  You will be glad you tried it!
