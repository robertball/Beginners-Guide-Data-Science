Title:
Chili Dog Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hot dog or hamburger buns
8 hot dogs
2 (10 ounce) cans of Hormel no bean chili
1 small onion chopped
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
Fritos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Separate the buns and place in the oven to toast lightly while the oven heats.  Tear up the buns and arrange the pieces in the bottom of the dish evenly. Slice the hot dogs into bite size pieces and layer the pieces over the buns.  Sprinkle 1 cup of cheese over the hot dogs.  Spread the chili , sprinkle with the chopped onion, then spread some mustard over the chili and the onion. Top off with the remaining cheese.  If desired, crush fritos over top of cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes.
