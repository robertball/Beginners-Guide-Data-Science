Title:
Homemade Kahlua Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz. Instant coffee (not Yuban)
4 C.  Sugar
2 C   Boiling water
1 Pt. Brandy
1    Vanilla bean cut into 1" pieces
1 Tbsp. Glycerin

Directions:
Mix coffee and sugar into boiling water till it dissolves.
Remove from heat and let cool.
Add vanilla bean and brandy.
Put into a well sealed glass container and store in cool place for 30-60 days.
After storage strain and add 1 tbsp. glycerin to thicken.

Submitter: 
Celeste
