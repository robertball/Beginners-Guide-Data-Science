Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups macaroni
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 T dill relish
3 hard cooked eggs
1 cup Miracle whip
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon Beau Monde â„¢ seasoning or 3/4 tsp celery seeds

Directions:
In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
In a large bowl, combine pasta and vinegar. Mix well and allow to sit 15-20 minutes.
Add cheese, miracle whip, dill relish, eggs and seasonings. Mix well and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Jacque
Description: 
This is pure picnic food, but can work as a winter 's evening supper as well. Macaroni noodles are cooked up and splashed with vinegar and then folded into the cheese, dill relish, eggs, and celery. Then comes the herbed mayonnaise. Serves six.Modified from: Macaroni and Cheese Salad
