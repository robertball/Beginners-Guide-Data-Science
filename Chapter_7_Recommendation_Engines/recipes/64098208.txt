Title:
Bread Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch celery, small diced
1 large yellow onion, small diced
1 lb. unsalted butter
4 TBSP poultry seasoning
1 4" hotel pan dried, cubed bread
1/4 chicken base
2 TBSP seasoning mix

Directions:
In a sauce pan, add onion, celery, and butter.  Saute until onions are translucent.  Add seasoning mix, poultry seasoning, chicken base, and water and bring to a boil.
In a large bowl, slowly mix together.  Do not overmix!
Place in a 4" hotel pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 20-30 minutes uncovered.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird

Moist and flavorful; perfect for a fall meal.
