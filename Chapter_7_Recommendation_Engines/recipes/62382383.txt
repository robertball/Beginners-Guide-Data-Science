Title:
Our Favorite Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 cup fresh or frozen fruit (whole berries, chopped mango, pineapple, kiwi, etc)
1 cup any flavor sweetened yogurt
1/2-1 cup any flavor 100% fruit juice

Directions:
Put banana, fruit, yogurt, and 1/2 cup of juice in a blender.  Blend until smooth.  Add additional juice if a thinner consistency desired.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
The Bunny Chef
Description: 
This is a basic smoothie recipe that I made up a few years ago.  I've made it for breakfast hundreds of times!  It's a great start to any day and very fast to make.  You can use all different combinations of fruit, yogurt and juice.  I've never had it turn out less than delicious.
