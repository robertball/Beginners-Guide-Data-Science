Title:
No=bake fruit cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound box graham crackers--crumbled
1 quart pecan halves
2 cups rasins or chopped dates
2 sticks butter or margarine
1 pound pkg. marshmallows
3 sm jars of bottled maraschino cherries.

Directions:
Mix crumbs, pecans, rasins or dates and cherries.  Melt 2 sticks of margarine or butter with marshmallows and mix with other ingredients.  Pack into 2 buttered loaf pan or four sm loaf pans.
