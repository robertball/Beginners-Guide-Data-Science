Title:
Picnic Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981955.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts cranberry juice
3 cups pineapple juice
3 cups orange juice
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 liter ginger ale, chilled
1 navel orange, sliced

Directions:
Combine the juices in a large container.  Refrigerate.  Just before serving, stir in ginger ale and orange slices.

Submitter: 
Marion  Lowery
Description: 
This pink cooler is deliciously thirst-quenching on a warm day. Seeing its color, folks guess it might be pink lemonade. They're pleasantly surprised to discover the bubbly blend includes cranberry, pineapple, orange and lemon juices. -Marion Lowery, Medford, Oregon
