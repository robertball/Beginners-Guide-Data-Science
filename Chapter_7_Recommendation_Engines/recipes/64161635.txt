Title:
Black Bean and Smoked Sausage Rice Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Smoked Sausage (Turkey Kielbasa preferred)
1 cup of dry white rice (or 1 bag of boil-in-bag rice)
1 can of black beans
1 small onion
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup of chicken stock
Creole seasoning
Red pepper flakes
1/2 cup salsa
Butter

Directions:
Cook rice according to direction. Once cooked, fluff, and mix with small amount of butter, salt and pepper, set aside in the microwave.
Chop sausage, onions and garlic.
Melt butter in medium skillet, brown sausage and set aside in the microwave.
Drain and rinse beans.
Saute onions and garlic in melted butter, then pour chicken stock in. Add creole seasoning and red pepper flakes and then salsa. Simmer for 5-6 minutes.
Serve sausage over rice and beans.
