Title:
Best Ever Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4lb green beans
Pinch salt
2T EVOO
3 cloves garlic sliced
1-1/2c parmesan cheese
1/2 lemon

Directions:
Trim green beans.
Boil green beans in salted water for 6 minutes or longer if desired.
Drain beans and reserve some of the cooking water.
Put pan back on heat, add oil and cook garlic until golden.
Return beans to pan and coat in oil.
Add a ladleful of cooking water, Parmesan, and juice from lemon.
Stir and simmer until water and cheese form a sticky sauce.
Serve immediately.
