Title:
Italian Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Salt and white pepper
Flour, for dredging
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
1/4 cup Marsala wine
1/4 cup chicken stock
1/3 cup sliced fresh mushrooms

Directions:
1.) Cut each chicken breast in half, and pound between layers of plastic wrap until each is about 1/4 inch thick.
2.) Season lightly with salt and white pepper and dredge in flour.
3.) Heat olive oil and butter in a large saute pan over medium-high heat.
4.) Shake off excess flourfrom chicken breasts and saute about 2 minutes per side until lightly browned.
5.) Add Marsala, chicken stock and mushrooms.
6.) Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook until mushrooms soften and sauce thickens slightly, about 5 minutes.
