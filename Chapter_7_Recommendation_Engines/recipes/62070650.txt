Title:
AAA Kicked up Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. smokey bacon, diced and cooked
reserved bacon drippings
3 eggs
1 1/2 c. buttermilk
3 jalapeno peppers, minced WITH SEEDS!!!
2 c. monterey jack cheese, grated
1/2 c. beer
1 1/2 c. corn meal
1 c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
3 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
1 Using a 12 inch cast iron frying pan, render bacon until crisp, reserve drippings.
2 Preheat oven to 350 degrees
3 In a large bowl, beat eggs and add the beer, buttermilk, bacon drippings, cheese, peppers and bacon.
4 In another bowl, incorporate dry ingredients with a fork.
5 Add dry ingredients to the wet mixture one cup at a time, to avoid lumps.
6 Allow batter to rest for 20 minutes.
7 Pour mixture into cast iron pan and bake on middle rack for 30 minutes
8 Rotate once after 20 minutes so Cornbread browns evenly.
