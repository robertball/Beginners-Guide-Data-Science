Title:
Chili Con Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1 tablespoon butter
2 medium tomatoes
1 - 4 ounce can green chilies, chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Saute onions until soft in a frying pan at medium heat - do not brown.
Add tomatoes, green chilies and salt to onion and simmer, uncovered, 10 minutes.
Add cheese, stirring until cheese has melted.
Serve immediately with tortilla or corn chips.  Keep warm in a fondue pot over low heat.

Submitter: 
Jan Mowbray
Description: 
A delicious dip with just the right amount of bite
