Title:
Polenta alla spianatora Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T EVOO
2 lb sausage
2 onions
2 lg fennel bulbs
6 garlic
1/4c red wine
1 lb 12oz canned chopped tomato
1t sugar
basil roughly torn
some water
1lb 2 oz cornmeal
1 3/4 c parmesan

Directions:
1T oil and sausage, pour off extra fat. add rest of oil, onion, fennel-cook 15-20 min. Add garlic, wine, bubble one min-add tomato, sugar and cooked sausage.
Bring water to boil, add 1 T salt and corn meal. Simmer 30 min-add most of cheese
Clean table, spread cornmeal on table in 2 bowl shaped portions, spoon ragu in the middle garnish with basil and remaining cheese.
