Title:
Buffalo Wild Wings Asian Zing Sauce Clone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp corn starch
4 tsp rice wine vinegar
1/2 c corn syrup
1/3 c sugar
1/4 c chili garlic paste
1 T low sodium soy sauce
squirt of lemon
1/4 tsp ginger, grated (or ground if you must)
1/8 tsp garlic, optioal

Directions:
Whisk rice wine vinegar into cornstarch and set aside.
Add all other ingredients to saucepan and bring to simmer over low heat.
Whisk in cornstarch mixture, and allow to come to boil over low/med heat. Allow to reduce slightly.
Remove from heat and cool
