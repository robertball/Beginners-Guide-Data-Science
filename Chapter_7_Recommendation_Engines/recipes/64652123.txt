Title:
Diamond Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head romaine lettuce, washed, dried and chopped
10-12 grape tomatoes, halved
1/4 of a red onion, sliced thin
1/2 of a 15oz. can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1- 6oz. jar marinated artichokes, chopped
1- 2oz. can sliced black olives, drained
1/2 cup chopped pecans (can toast, if desired)
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
Italian dressing, to taste

Directions:
Place romaine lettuce into a large salad bowl or individual salad plates.
Top with tomatoes, onions, garbanzo beans, artichokes, olives, pecans and blue cheese.
Top with Italian dressing and toss to coat.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Many years ago, I enjoyed this salad in a restaurant and was determined to re-create it at home...I did, and this version is even better! Great served with steaks.
