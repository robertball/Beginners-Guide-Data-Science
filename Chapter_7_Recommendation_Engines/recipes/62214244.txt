Title:
Kahlua Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups finely crushed oreo cookies
1 cup finely chopped walnuts
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1/3 cup Kahlua liquor or any coffee flavored liquor would work
2 tablespoons dark corn syrup
Assorted items for rolling the balls into (ie: cocoa powder, fine colored sugar, sprinkles, finely crushed walnuts or oreo crumbs)

Directions:
Combine cookie crumbs, walnuts and confectioners' sugar in a large bowl.
Add Kahlua and corn syrup; mix well.
Shape into balls and roll into desired toppings.
Chill overnight. Store in air tight container in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
These are so tasty! They are just a little something different from the usual rum ball. My family actually prefers these to rum balls. Enjoy!
