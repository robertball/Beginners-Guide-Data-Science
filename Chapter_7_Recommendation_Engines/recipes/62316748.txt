Title:
Lentils with Chorizo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/375753.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb un-cooked lentils
1/2 lb mexican chorizo
1 chicken boullion cube
1/8 teaspoon cumin
6 slices bacon
1 roma tomato-diced
1 tomatillo-diced
1 small white onion-diced
2 cloves garlic-diced
1/2 bunch cilantro chopped
1/2 cup water
Toppings/side:
cojita cheese
sour cream
tortillas

Directions:
Follow directions on package for lentils.  Add the chicken boullion cube to water with lentils.
Remove casing and cook chorizo until brown, about 10-15 minutes.  Set aside in bowl.
In same pan lightly fry bacon for about 10 minutes, do not over cook it you want soft chewy bacon.  Save juices in pan. Set bacon aside on cutting board and chop into small peices.
Add all chopped ingredients into same pan except cilantro.  Mash all ingredients until smooth, about 5 minutes.  Add cilantro last minute, and mash again.  Place mixture into blender with 1/2 cup water and blend until smooth.
Pour mixture into the pot of lentils along with cumin.  Stir to combine.  Add chorizo and bacon to lentils also, stir again and remove from heat.
Dish into bowls and top with cojita cheese, sour cream.  Serve tortillas with it.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is quick easy mexican dinner.  Was looking for a new way to use lentils that the family would enjoy and the addition of chorizo really adds great flavor to the lentils.
