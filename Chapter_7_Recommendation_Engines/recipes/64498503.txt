Title:
Spinach and Stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2612217.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped onions
1 package frozen chopped spinach, cooked and squeezed dry
3/4 cup melted butter
1 cup parmesan cheese
2 cups Pepperidge Farm cubed herb stuffing
2 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.  Put in baking dish spread evenly.  Sprinkle with extra parmesan cheese. Bake approx 25 mins at 350 degrees.  Serve very hot.

Submitter: 
Chrissy Gaynor
Description: 
Spinach, stuffing mix, parmesan, and eggs combine for a savory, filling casserole.
