Title:
Blueberry Breeze Coconut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4459975.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup blueberries
1 cup coconut milk
1/2 green apple, cut into cubes
1 tablespoon flax seeds
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 drops liquid stevia

Directions:
Combine blueberries, coconut milk, green apple, flax seeds, vanilla extract, and stevia in a blender; blend until smooth, about 1 minute.

Description: 
Healthy and delicious blueberry breeze coconut smoothie packed with healthy ingredients like flaxseed and green apple.
