Title:
Poblano pepper poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 fresh poblano peppers (washed)
8oz cream cheese
1/2 c Mexican blend shredded cheese
1 Jalapeno pepper, washed, seeded finely diced
One box of Pre cooked, ready to serve bacon

Directions:
Roast poblano peppers under broiler (or over open flame on grill) till skin is black on both sides.
Place hot peppers in a brown paper bag and close. Let rest for 10 minutes.
Mix cream cheese, shredded mexican cheese, diced jalapeno pepper in a bowl and set aside.
Preheat grill or oven to 350.
Remove peppers from bag. Peel black skin as best you can and slice lengthwise. Remove seeds.
Fill with cheeses and top with 1/2 slice of pre cooked bacon.
Bake in oven or grill for 10 minutes till cheese is warmed through.
Enjoy
