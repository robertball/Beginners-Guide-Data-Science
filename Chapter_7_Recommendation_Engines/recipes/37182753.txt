Title:
Yummy Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Stuffing:
6 apples (chopped)
3 T. flour
1 T. cinnamom
1 c. sugar
Topping:
1 c. flour
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 stick of butter

Directions:
Stuffing:In large mixing bowl mix all ingredients together. Then pour into deep dish pie shell.
Topping: In medium bowl mix all ingredients together. With hands,
sprinkle evenly on top of pie.
Bake: 450* for 20 mins. turn heat down to 350* and bake for 30-45 mins.
