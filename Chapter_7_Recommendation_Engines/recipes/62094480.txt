Title:
Pickled Watermelon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
water melon rinds
1 qt. water
4 T. salt
1 qt water
1 T. alum
2 c. cider vinegar
4 c. sugar
Spices tied in cheesecloth to form a sack:    4 t. whole cloves
8 sticks of cinnamon
sm. amt of whole mustard seed.
food coloring

Directions:
Peel off most pink and outer rind, cut into 1 inch square pieces.
Cover with 1 qt. water and 4 T. salt. Cover tightly.
Let set overnight.
In the morning, drain and rinse rinds.
Add 1 T. alum to 1 qt. water and pour over rinds. Cover tightly.
Let set 2 hours.
Cook, covered, until alum is clear.
Drain.
Add cider vinegar, sugar, and spice sack.
Heat to boiling, then simmer 15 min.
Add green food coloring.
Cook until clear.

Submitter:
