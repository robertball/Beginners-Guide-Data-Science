Title:
Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices (3oz.) bacon, chopped
1/2 cup chopped onion
3-1/2 cups (2, 14-1/2oz cans) of beef broth
2 cups (15oz. can) great northern white beans, undrained
2 cups (1 lb. 1oz.) Contadina Dalla Casa Buitoni Country Cooking Sauce with garden Vegetables
2 cups sliced zuchini
2 cups (10oz pack) frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 cup dried small macaroni
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Combine bacon and onion in large saucepan.  Cook over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes or until bacon is crispy; drain.  Stir in broth, beans and liquid, and cooking sauce; bring to boil.  reduce heat to low; cook for 5 minutes.
Stir in zucchini, mixed vegetables and pasta, bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; cook for 8-10 minutes or until vegetables and pasta are tender.  Sprinkle with cheese.
