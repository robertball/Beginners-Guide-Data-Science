Title:
Really Good Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 bottle of Italian dressing (wishbone Italian dressing)
* 6 cups flour
* 10 pieces of chicken skinned

Directions:
1) Soak chicken in Italian dressing over night.
2) Dip the chicken in the flour and fry it in vegetable oil in a frying pan. Chicken is done when it is white all the way trough.

Submitter: 
franny
Description: 
A delicious moist and flavorful alternative to the typical fried chicken.
