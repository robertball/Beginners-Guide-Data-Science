Title:
"Roasted" Corn and Jalapeno Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4525686.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can sweet corn drained
1 jalapeno chopped
1/2 large red onion chopped
1/8 cup cilantro chopped
1/2 lime; juiced
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Put the corn and jalapeno in a saut√© pan and cook on high until you see the char marks. Do not add any oil or butter to the pan (very important.) Set the pan aside to cool. Add the red onion, cilantro, lime juice and salt and pepper to a mixing bowl. When the corn and jalapeno mixture is cooled down, add to the mixing bowl and combine.

Submitter: 
Sarah Moore
Description: 
This quick and easy salsa is delicious and will be a crowd pleaser!
