Title:
Apple Spice bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/892288.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups All Purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups apples - peeled, cored, and chopped
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla flavoring
2 eggs
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon clove
1 cup of either walnuts or raisins if you want.

Directions:
Directions
1.Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Prepare 1 loaf pan with cooking spray.
2.Mix flour, baking soda, salt, walnuts/raisins, and apples in a large bowl. Whisk oil, sugar, eggs, vanilla and cinnamon/clove/nutmeg together in a small bowl; add to flour mixture and mix until just moistened. Put mixture in loaf pan.
3.Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 75 minutes. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
