Title:
Seafood Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990074.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Gorton's® Garlic Butter Grilled Fillets
1 chopped celery stalk
1 chopped carrot
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon chopped garlic
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon fresh chopped mint (or dried)
1 submarine sandwich roll, split

Directions:
Cook Gorton's Garlic Butter Grilled Fillets per instructions on package. Cut cooked Grilled Fillets into sixths. Mix all remaining ingredients in a bowl and add the Grilled Fillets. Stir until thoroughly mixed. Place salad in a sub roll and serve immediately. Salad may be kept in refrigerator for 3 days.

Submitter: 
Gorton's®

Craving seafood?  This mouth-watering sandwich will hit the spot.
