Title:
Ham N Asparagus Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989852.jpg
Ingredients:
16 spears fresh asparagus, trimmed
1 tablespoon water
16 thin slices fully cooked ham
DILL SAUCE:
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cucumber - peeled, seeded, and diced
1 teaspoon dill weed
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Place asparagus and water in a microwave-safe 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. dish. Cover and cook on high for 2-3 minutes or until crisp-tender. Immediately place asparagus in ice water; drain and pat dry. Wrap each asparagus spear with a slice of ham. Just before serving, combine sauce ingredients. Serve with roll-ups.

Submitter: 
Mary  Steiner
Description: 
These light refreshers from field editor Mary Steiner of West Bend, Wisconsin go together in a jiffy, so they're perfect when unexpected guests drop by.
