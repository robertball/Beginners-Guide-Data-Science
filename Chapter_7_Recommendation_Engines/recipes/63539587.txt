Title:
Jerimy's Okie Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Chorizo Sausage
1 26 ounce can of Hunt's Zesty and Spicy Pasta Sauce
1 1/4 cup Elbow Macaroni
1/2 cup diced Bell Pepper
1/2 cup diced Onion
1 cup Corn
1/4 teaspoon Garlic Salt
1 3/4 cup Water

Directions:
Cook chorizo until done. Drain off grease and set aside chorizo.
In a medium sauce pan saute onions, bell peppers, and corn in a small amount of oil or butter.
When vegetables are starting to get brown edges add pasta sauce, water, macaroni, chorizo, and garlic salt.
Bring to a boil and then reduce heat and simmer until macaroni is done (about 10 minutes).
Serve in bowls and sprinkle cheddar cheese over the top and enjoy.
