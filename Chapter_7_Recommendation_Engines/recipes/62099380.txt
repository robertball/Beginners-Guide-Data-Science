Title:
Savory three bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz chicken or veggie stock (low sodium)
20 oz diced tomatoes
2 16 oz cans red kidney beans
1 16 oz can cannelini beans
1 16 oz can butter beans
1/2 medium white onion- diced
4 cloves garlic- minced
1/2 tsp fennel seeds
1/2 tsp celery seed
1/4 tsp marjoram
1 c. peas
1 c. corn
salt
pepper

Directions:
Rinse and drain all beans
Place all ingredients except peas and corn into 4-5 qt slow cooker. Stir.
Cook on low for 8 hours
Add peas and corn (if using canned; drain and rinse). Add to soup. Stir.
Cook for one more hour.
Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
This is one of my favortie fall/winter time slow cooker recipes.
Full of flavor and protein!
