Title:
Adult Shirley Temple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz cherry flavored vodka, such as Three Olives brand
6 oz. lemon lime flavored soda
1 oz grenadine
Ice
Maraschino cherry for garnish

Directions:
In a cup with ice, pour the cherry vodka, soda, then grenadine. Give it a little stir and enjoy. Garnish with cherry.
