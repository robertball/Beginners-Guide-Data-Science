Title:
Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. powder sugar
1T milk
2 T butter
1/4 t. vanilla

Directions:
Soften butter. Add vanilla.
Stir in milk.
Add powder sugar until desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A basic butter-based frosting.
