Title:
KFC Coleslaw - Knock Off Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664879.jpg
Ingredients:
8 C. Finely chopped cabbage (about 1 cabbage)
1/2 C. shredded carrots
3 T. onion, finely minced
1/2 C. sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 cup milk
1/4 buttermilk
1/2 c. mayonnaise
2 1/2 T. lemon juice
1 1/2 T. white Vinegar

Directions:
Combine the sugar, salt, pepper, milk, mayonnaise, buttermilk, vinegar and lemon juice in a large bowl and whisk until smooth.
Add cabbage, carrots and onion and mix well.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
