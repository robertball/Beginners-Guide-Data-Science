Title:
Vegan/Gluten Free Chewy Peanut Butter Cookies (from Heaven) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6731661.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups creamy peanut butter
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup mashed ripe banana
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place all ingredients in bowl of stand mixer.  Mix well and drop by tablespoonsful on parchment lined cookie sheet.  Dip a fork or old fashioned potato masher in Morena sugar and press cookies slightly.
Bake for about 10-12 minutes until just slightly golden brown.  Remove to wire rack and allow cookies to rest on cookie sheet for about 5 minutes, then use spatula to remove to wire rack to cool completely.  Makes about 30 cookies
