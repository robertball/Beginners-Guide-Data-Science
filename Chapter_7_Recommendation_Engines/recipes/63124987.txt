Title:
Grilled Shrimp Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812678.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinated Tomatoes
1 1/2 lbs Roma tomatoes, cored and cut into 1 inch pieces
20 medium fresh basil leaves, stems removed and cut into 1 inch pieces
2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 tbs garlic, minced
1 tsp italian seasoning
salt to taste
2 tbs butter
1/2 cup white wine
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1 lb angel hair pasta, cooked
1 cups mozarella cheese, shredded
1 pound shrimp peeled and deveined

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
combine tomatoes, basil, olive oil, garlic, italian seasoning and salt in a large bowl and blend thoroughly.  Cover and set aside and marinate for at least 1 hour.
Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.  Add butter and let melt.  Stir in white wine and bring to a boil.  Add heavy cream and parmesan cheese and bring to a simmer.  Let sauce reduce to desired consistency.  Add cooked drained pasta and marinate tomatoes to skillet.  Stir to coat pasta with sauce.  Transfer to serving platter and top with mozzarella cheese.  Saute shrimp until cooked through.  Place serving platter in broiler for 2 minutes until cheese melts.  Top with shrimp and serve.
