Title:
Apple Pie in a Jar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 quarts peeled, cored, and sliced apples
4 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup corn starch or Everclear
1 tablespoon salt
10 cups water
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 tablespoons nutmeg
2 teaspoons cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large heavy pot and cook until thick and bubbly.
Ladle into hot quart jars, top with lid and band.
Process in a pressure cooker for 10 minutes at 10 pounds or for 25 minutes in a water bath cooker.

Submitter: 
Patricia Canerdy
Description: 
An easy apple pie filling, just like the costly ones found in the grocery store.  Can be used as a topping, for pies, or cooked in oatmeal.
