Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Enchiladas II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/268495.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 tablespoon taco seasoning
1 cup sour cream, divided
1 1/2 cups cubed cooked chicken breast meat
1 1/4 cups pre shredded Mexican cheese blend, divided
1/4 cup finely chopped green onions (optional)
6 (12 inch) flour tortillas
1 cup of enchilada sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 370 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, mix the green chiles, 1/2 cup sour cream, taco seasoning, chicken, optional green onions and 3/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese. Stir together.  Lay six tortillas out flat and divide the mixture between them.  Roll and place in the baking dish.
Mix 1 cup enchilada sauce and 1/2 cup of sour cream.  Pour over the tortillas and top with 1/2 cups of cheese.  Cook for 25 minutes or until cheese is melted and center are hot.
Pass with hot sauce, chopped cilantro and scallions, if desired.
