Title:
S'Mores Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
2 cups miniature marshmallows
4 (1.55-ounce) milk chocolate candy bars

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees. Line an 8 x 8 baking pan with buttered foil.  In medium saucepan, combine butter and brown sugar.  Cook over medium heat until the brown sugar dissolves and mixture is bubbly.  Stir in vanilla, graham cracker crumbs, and marshmallows.  Continue to stir just until marshmallow begin to melt.  Spread evenly in prepared pan.  With back of spoon, press mixture to make an even layer.  Set candy bars side by side, in single layer to cover top.  Place in oven 5 minutes or until chocolate is shiny all over.  With spatula, spread chocolate to evenly cover top.  Remove and chill in refrigerator.  Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Gretchen Gowens Smith
Description: 
I got this recipe years ago and every time I make these they disappear quickly.  They are delicious and easy to make.
