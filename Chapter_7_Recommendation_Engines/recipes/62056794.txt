Title:
Easy Chocolate Fix Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. sugar
1 3/4 C Flour
1/2 C baking cocoa
1 tsp. salt
5 eggs
1 C vegetable oil
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 to 1 C. chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredient except chocolate chips until smooth. Pour into 9x13 (greased) pan and sprinkle chips all over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 min.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
This is a great way to get your chocolate fix!  These are easily thrown together in just a few minutes, and the result is a moist homemade brownie with about the same prep time as boxed!  I sometimes substitute 1/2 C. applesauce for 1/2 of the oil, and egg substitute for the eggs.
