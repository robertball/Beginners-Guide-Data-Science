Title:
Bratwurst Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bratwurst cut into 2 inch pieces
1 can of green beans
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1-2 medium sized red peppers cut into strips
1 medium sized onion chopped
8 small red skinned potatoes cut into bit sized chunks
8 oz of shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup of water or low sodium chicken stock
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients into slow cooker and mix together well. Cook on high for 3 hours or on low for 6 hours. Times may vari depending on slow cooker.

A wonderful, but simple hearty stew great on a winters afternoon!
