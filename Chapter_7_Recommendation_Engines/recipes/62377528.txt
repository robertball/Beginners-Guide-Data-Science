Title:
Chocolate Almond Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T. butter, room temperature
3 1/2 c. confectioners sugar, sifted
1/2 c. cocoa powder
1/3 c. evaporated milk
1/2 t. almond extract

Directions:
Place ingredients in a medium size bowl.  Mix well with electric mixer.  Spread onto your favorite cake recipe, enjoy.

Submitter: 
PacNWCook
Description: 
A rich chocolately frosting with a hint of almond to give it that extra oomph.  By far the best recipe I have tasted.
