Title:
BBQ Chicken Tortilla Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* Honey BBQ sauce
* Tortilla's (soft tortillas, you can even use wheat tortillas)
* 4 ounces (i use more) shredded cooked chicken
* 1/4 cup diced red onion (or more), divided.
* 1 Tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro or parsley divided.
* 1/4 cup low fat finely shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste.
* 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, or more to taste.

Directions:
1)Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F (200 Degrees C).
2)Spread 1 Tablespoon (more or less) of the BBQ sauce on the tortilla.
3)Mix 1 Tablespoon BBQ sauce (more or less) and chicken in a bowl until coated. Spread chicken mixture, half the red onion, and half the cilantro onto the tortilla. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and cheddar cheese over the chicken mixture. Top with the remaining red onion and cilantro. Place pizza on a baking sheet.
4)Bake in the preheated oven until the tortilla is crisp and cheese is melted, 5 to 7 minutes.
