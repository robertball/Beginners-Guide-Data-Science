Title:
Elaine's Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 peppers (red are the best)
1 lb ground beef
2 T shopped onion
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1 C cooked rice
1 can (15 oz)tomato sauce

Directions:
Cut off the tops of the peppers and clean out the seeds. Boil peppers in salted water for 5 minutes.
In saute pan, brown beef and onion. Stir in remaining ingredients & 1 cup of sauce.
Stuff peppers with the beef mixture and place in a greased baking dish.
Pour remaining sauce over peppers. Cover and bake for 45 minutes. Uncover for an additional 15 minutes.
If you desire, you can use more tomato sauce (8 oz)poured over the peppers.
