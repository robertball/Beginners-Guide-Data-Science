Title:
Banana Curd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/746984.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large over-ripe banana
1 tsp lemon juice (half of lemon squeezed)
1 cup of sugar
1/4 cup melted unsalted butter
1/4 cup Looza Banana Juice
4 egg yolks(whisked lightly)
1/4 tsp banana extract (optional)
1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Directions:
In a blender, puree bananas and lemon juice till smooth.
Whisk egg yolks lightly in a small bowl.
In a double boiler, mix sugar, butter and banana juice on high heat till mixture is completely blended with whisk and sugar is somewhat dissolved.
Add pureed bananas to mixture, completely blended in.
Slowly add egg yolks in ribbon pour, stirring quickly with whisk, so eggs don't cook, fully blended.
Add extracts and blend.
Stir occassionally over high heat boil 15-25 minutes or till mixture is thickened.
Turn off heat, let curd sit in double boiler for 5 minutes without stirring.
Stir once more.
Remove top boiler with curd to completely cool.
Once cool, stir it again and spoon into container/jars and refrigerate.

Submitter:

Description: 
When life gives you over-ripe bananas, don't go conventional, make Banana Curd instead!  It's creamy, dreamy and ohhhh so rich!  Great as spread alone but also use in fillings for desserts, rolls or just use your imagination!  This is by far the best tropical curd!
