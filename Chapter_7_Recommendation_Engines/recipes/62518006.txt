Title:
Gammy's Best Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3C Sugar
1C Oil
4 Eggs, beaten
1-15 oz can Pumpkin
2/3C Water
1/2 tsp Salt
2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
3-1/2C Flour
2 tsp Nutmeg
3 tsp Cinnamon
2 tsp Baking Soda
3/4C Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix all wet ingredients in mixer, add dry ingredients and mix well (do not over mix).  Fold in nuts.
Grease and flour 3 loaf pans.  Divide batter evenly.
Bake at 350 for one hour (or until toothpick comes out clean)

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
This is the most flavorful pumpkin bread I've ever had.  It makes 3 loaves at once.  I'm glad to finally save this recipe because every year I have to call my mom to get it ;)
