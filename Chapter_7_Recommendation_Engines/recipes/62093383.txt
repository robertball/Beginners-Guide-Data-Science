Title:
Strawberry Limeade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz can frozen limeade (i.e. minute maid)
3 Cups cold water
1 1/2 Cups Strawberries frozen plus 4 fresh strawberries
zest of 1 lime
red and green colored sugar
3 Cups crushed ice

Directions:
In pitcher blend together water and limeade set aside
In Blender, combine 1 cup of limeade mixture, strawberries, and lime zest blend until smooth. Pour mixture into pitcher of limeade stir well.
To serve, rim glasses with mixture of colored sugar, and fill with ice, pour limeade over and enjoy.

Submitter: 
lisascooking
Description: 
This is even better than the one they serve at the drive-in restraunt.  The blending of sweet strawberries and tart lime is very refreshing.
