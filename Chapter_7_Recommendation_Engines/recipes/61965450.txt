Title:
Wild Rice Salad with Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup wild rice
1 cup long-grain white rice
2 cups chicken broth
8 green onions, sliced
4 celery stalks, sliced
1/2 cup cashews
1 cup dried cranberries
4 tablespoons red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
4 teaspoons sesame oil

Directions:
Cook rice in chicken broth.  Let rice cool. Combine rice, onions, celery, cashews, and cranberries in bowl.  In a second bowl, mix vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, vegetable oil and sesame oil.  Pour dressing on salad.  Mix well and refrigerate for several hours. Serve.
