Title:
Spinach, Pasta and Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
2 green onions, thinly sliced
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups water
3/4 cup uncooked orzo
1 tbsp. lemon zest
1 15 oz. can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 tbsp. fresh oregano, chopped
1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/8 tsp. salt
6 oz. pkg. baby spinach
1/3 cup grated Parmesan

Directions:
Heat sauce pan and add olive oil to pan.
Add garlic and onions, saute about 30 seconds.
Add chicken broth and water, and bring to a boil.
Add orzo, lemon zest and chickpeas.
Reduce heat, cover, and simmer about 10 minutes or until orzo is done.
Stir in oregano, lemon juice, salt, pepper and spinach.
Top each serving with Parmesan.
