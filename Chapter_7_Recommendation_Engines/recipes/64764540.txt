Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb breakfast sausage (hot if you like it spicy)
1 Tbsp minced garlic
8 oz cream cheese
2 - 16 oz white mushroom caps (2 large size containers...this can make a lot based on how high you stuff them)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Brown sausage with garlic. Be sure to chop the sausage well while browning and don't leave any large chunks.  Drain visible grease.  Add cream cheese to browned sausage and mix till melted and mixed well.  Spoon mixture into mushroom caps and bake or air fry until mushrooms are soft. Bake for about 20-25 minutes.  Air fry for maybe 10-12.
