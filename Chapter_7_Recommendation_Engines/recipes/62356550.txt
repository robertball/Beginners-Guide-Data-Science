Title:
Garden Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen pizza dough - thawed
1-1/2 cups broccoli - cooked
1-1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup tomatoes - diced
1/2 cup green pepper - diced
1 8-oz package shredded mozzarella
olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cut pizza dough in half
On a floured surface, roll dough into 2 8-in pieces.
Place dough on greased baking sheet
Brush each piece with olive oil
Add enough broccoli, mushrooms, tomatoes and green pepper to cover half of each piece of dough.
Sprinkle with desired amount of mozzarella cheese.
Fold dough over, being sure to cover veggies.
Pinch edges of dough together to seal.
Brush top with olive oil
Bake for 20 minutes or until tops are lightly browned.
