Title:
Garlic Butter Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1508239.jpg
Ingredients:
6 pork chops (bone in or boneless)
6 tablespoons margarine
1 teaspoon garlic powder (or 2 cloves garlic)
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
1. In a large skillet over med-high heat, melt margarine and garlic.  (If using garlic cloves, mince garlic first).  Cook garlic until golden.
2. Season pork chops with salt and pepper.
3. Add pork chops to skillet.  Cook for 5-6 minutes per side or until juices run clear.  Baste frequently with garlic butter mixture.
4. Serve with remaining garlic butter sauce.
