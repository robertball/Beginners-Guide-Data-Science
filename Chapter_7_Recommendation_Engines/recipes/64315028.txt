Title:
Sweet Restaurant Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
3/4 creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whipâ„¢)
1/3 skim milk
1/4 cup splenda
1/2 teaspoon celery seeds

Directions:
Combine the coleslaw mix .
Whisk together the salad dressing, milk, splenda,   and celery seeds in a medium bowl; blend thoroughly. Pour dressing mixture over coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Jody Comerford
Description: 
Starting with prepared coleslaw mix this recipe for a creamy coleslaw delivers a bit of tang through the use of vinegar, making it a perfect accompaniment for fried chicken, burgers, or fried fish.Modified from: Sweet Restaurant Slaw
