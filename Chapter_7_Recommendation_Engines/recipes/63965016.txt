Title:
Fresh Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
1 large banana
3/4 cup pineapple, cranberry or grape juice
3 ice cubes
1 teaspoon honey, optional

Directions:
Peel banana and break into chunks.  Put banana in blender.  Add strawberries, fruit juice and ice.  Add honey for a sweeter shake.  Blend on high speed until thick and smooth.
Serve immediately.
