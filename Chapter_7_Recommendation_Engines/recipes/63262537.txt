Title:
Slow Cooker Spareribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 oz) can of condensed tomato soup
1 onion, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp soy sauce
4 pounds pork spareribs, separated

Directions:
Place separated spareribs in the slow cooker.
In a mixing bowl combine the tomato soup, onion, garlic, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce and soy sauce.  When well mixed pour over the spareribs.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours or until ribs are tender.
Remove ribs from the slow cooker and discard the cooking sauce.  Brush ribs with BBQ sauce glaze and grill them for a few minutes to finish.

Submitter: 
Michael St. Laurent
Description: 
Falling-off-the-bone tender spareribs.
