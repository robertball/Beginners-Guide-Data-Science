Title:
Pumpkin Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves of Garlic
3/4 tsp. Salt
2 Tbs. Tahini
2 Tbs. Lemon Juice
1 15 oz. can Garbanzo beans (drained)
1 Tbs. EVOO (Extra Virgin Olive Oil)
1 Cup canned Pumpkin
1 tsp. ground Cumin
1/2 tsp. ground Cayenne
Paprika
Roasted shelled pumpkin seeds

Directions:
Pulse garlic, salt, tahini & Lemon juice in in a food processor or blender until smooth.
Pour in the drained beans and EVOO and pulse again until smooth.
Add the Pumpkin, Cumin and Cayenne and process until all is blended.
Refrigerate for several hours before serving (it's best the 2nd day).
Just before serving sprinkle some Pumpkin seeds (Pepitos) and paprika on top to make it look nice.
