Title:
Dirty Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658258.jpg
Ingredients:
Dirty Rice mix (enough for 10 servings)
2-3 pounds ground buffalo; cooked
2 bell peppers, diced
1 onion, diced
2 cans diced tomatoes
2 Tbls. oil
1 pound cheese, grated

Directions:
Make Dirty Rice according to recipe.
Put oil in pan and cook ground buffalo with onions and bell peppers.
Add buffalo mixture and drained, diced tomatoes  to rice when it is done.  Top with grated cheese.
