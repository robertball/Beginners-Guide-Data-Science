Title:
Game Day Jalapeno Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large block (2 lbs.) american cheese, cut into 1" cubes
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. spicy sausage
1 15 oz. can tomato sauce
1 8 oz. tomato paste
1 4 oz. can diced green chilis, drained
1 4 oz. can diced jalepenos, drained

Directions:
Brown and drain ground beef and sausage, set aside
Melt cheese in a pot over medium heat, stirring continuously and being careful not to burn
Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, chilis and jalapenos, stir until blended
Add meat
Put dip in a crock pot on low heat and serve with tortilla chips

Submitter: 
Kel Kel
Description: 
This dip is sure to please football fans!  Leftovers freeze very well.
