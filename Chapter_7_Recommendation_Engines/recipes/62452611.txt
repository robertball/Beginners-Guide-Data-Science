Title:
Italian Chicken and bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32oz of chicken stock
1 15 oz jar/container  parmesan or 4 cheese spaghetti sauce
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 tsp italian spices
1/2 tsp coarse ground pepper
3/4 cup diced carrots
3/4 cup diced celery
1 15 oz can small white beans
2 cup cooked shredded chicken
2 cups coarse chopped fresh spinach

Directions:
In a large pot stir together chicken stock,spaghetti sauce,garlic,carrots ,celery, italian seasoning and pepper bring to a boil and then reduce heat and let simmer 15-20 mins. add the rest of the ingredients return to a simmer and continue to cook 10 mins. Serve with grated parm cheese.

Submitter: 
Val-Flowers
Description: 
A wonderful combo of tomato,spice and cheese. As a starter or a meal. Serve with a loaf of hot fresh garlic bread.
