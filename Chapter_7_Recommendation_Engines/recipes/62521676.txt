Title:
Crockpot chicken stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts cubed
2 T butter
1 pkg italian seasoning mix
1 can diced green chiles
1 can cream of chix
1 pkg cream cheese
1 cup broth or water
onion and garlic optional

Directions:
place all in crock pot
cook on low 6-8 hours or high 4-6 hours until chix cooked through.
serve over egg noodles, rice or potatoes.
