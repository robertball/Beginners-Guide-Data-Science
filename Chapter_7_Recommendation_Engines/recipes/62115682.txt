Title:
chipotle corn chowder chizam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups corn
2 larg potatos diced to 1/4 inch cubes
1 red bell pepper finley choped
2 medium shalots minced
2 cloves garlick minced
3 carots choped fine
1 or 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sause {caned} to tast
i tblsp olive oil
1 tblsp butter
2 liters chicken stock
1 liter heavey cream
4 tblsp fresh choped celontro

Directions:
in a large pot med to high heat add olive oil and butter shalots garlick and red pepper.
cook for 2 minutes or untill chalots are clear with a nice aroma.  add chipotle carots ant potatos. ad the chicken stock to about 1/2 inch over solids.
bring to a boil.  continue to boil until the potatos are soft.. about 20 minutes.
add the heavy cream.  turn heat off just befor it boils than add the celontro. let it mary for ten minutes than serve.
goes well with jalipenjo corn bred
