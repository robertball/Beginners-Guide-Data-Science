Title:
Easy Chicken Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/532164.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded chicken (2-3 breasts)
14 oz can Italian style diced tomatoes (I like the basil, garlic, oregano one)
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 T olive oil

Directions:
Bake or boil chicken until done. save in the refrigerator until ready to use.
Preheat oven to 400* F.
Coat bottom of 8x8 pan with olive oil.
Shred chicken and put in pan. Salt and pepper as desired.
Cover with can of tomatoes and then top with cheese.
Bake uncovered for 15-20 minutes.
