Title:
Chicken and artichokes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
3 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 packet Grill Mates Zesty Herb marinate
1 jar capers
1 can quartered artichoke hearts

Directions:
Combine zesty herb mix, olive oil and vinegar . Marinate the chicken in mixture for 2 hours .Place chicken in a glass baking dish .Drain  capers and artichokes . Add them to dish. Bake uncovered for 30 min at 375
