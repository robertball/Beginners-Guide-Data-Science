Title:
Pretzel Rollo Candy / Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. small bag unsalted pretzel
2. bag of rollo candies ( must unwrap foli off each one)
3. pecan nut halves

Directions:
oven at 350* ...Place on lightly greased baking sheet pretzels...place one rollo on each pretzel and bake 2 min
Take out of oven place a pecan half on top and press down...allow to cool
May subsitute the rollos with Dove Caramel filled chocolate or with Hershey kisses caramel or peanutbutter filled
