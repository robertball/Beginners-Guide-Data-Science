Title:
Kid's Pizza Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup elbow macaroni
1 TBS butter
1/2 cup pepperoni slices
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
1/2 cup shredded cheddar
1/2 sliced mushrooms (or favorite pizza veggie topping)
1 cup marinara
1 TBS paremesean cheese for topping

Directions:
Im a pot boil lightly salted water.
Cook pasta 7-10 minutes or until tender, drain.
In a casserole dish combine butter and pasta.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
Cook in a preheated 350 degree oven for 10 minutes.
Sprinkle with parmesean before serving.
ENJOY!
