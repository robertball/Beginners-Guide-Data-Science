Title:
Amish Friendship Bread Starter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c. Flour
2c. Milk
2c. Sugar

Directions:
You start with
1c. Flour
1c. Milk
1c. Sugar
Make sure to never use metal to either store the starter or to stir it! Always use plastic or glass.
Stir it every day for the first 4 days.
Day 5 add
1c. Flour
1c. Milk
1c. Sugar
stir well; stir it every day for the next 4 days; add one cup each of flour, milk and sugar on day 10, stir well - and you should be ready to use the starter.
How to keep the starter going:
Do not refrigerate and do not use a meal spoon when stirring. On day 1 (the day you receive the starter) do nothing. Days 2, 3, 4 stir. On day 5 Stir in:
1c. Flour
1c. Milk
1c. Sugar
Cover lightly
Mixture will rise.
On days 6,7,8,9 stir
on day 10, stir in:
1c. Flour
1c. Milk
1c. Sugar
and stir.
Give 1 c to each of two friends, keeping 1c to make the bread and 1c for your starter.

Submitter: 
Angela Rhode Bizley Mondor
Description: 
Original recipe made without the use of yeast.
