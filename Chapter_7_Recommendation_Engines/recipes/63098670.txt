Title:
Mint Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownies:
2 oz square of unsweetened chocolate
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
1/4 tsp peppermint extract
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
Filling:
1 cup powdered sugar
1 Tbsp milk
3/4 tsp peppermint extract
Glaze:
1 oz unsweetened chocolate
1 Tbsp. butter

Directions:
Melt chocolate and butter in top of double boiler.
In medium bowl beat eggs until frothy.
Stir in sugar, chocolate mixture, and peppermint extract.
Add flour and nuts and mix well.
Pour into a greased 9" pan
Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes
For the filling, combine all ingredients and add green food coloring (optional) and mix.
Spread over cool brownies.  Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
For the glaze, melt chocolate and butter in double boiler.  Mix well.  Drizzle over cool, firm filling.  Tilt cake back and forth until glaze covers entire surface.
Cut into 1" bars and serve.

Submitter: 
Megan Fellows
Description: 
Delicious and rich brownies with a thin glaze of mint and frosted with a chocolate layer.
