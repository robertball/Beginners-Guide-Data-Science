Title:
Quinoa Breakfast Muesli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 cup water
1 cup rinsed quinoa
1 apple, finely chopped
raisins or craisins
pecans, chopped or almonds, slivered
yogurt
cinnamon
nutmeg
additional milk to serve
fresh berries or other fruit to serve

Directions:
Bring milk, water & quinoa to boil over high.
Reduce to low, cover & let simmer x 15 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed.
Remove from heat and let stand, covered x 5 minutes.
Stir in quantities of other ingredients to your taste and desired consistency.
Can be enjoyed right away, with milk and fresh fruit garnish, if using.
Stores well in fridge for several days; reheat to serve or eat it cold.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
I attempted to replicate a recipe from the Okanagan for a delicious, healthy breakfast dish. I think I came pretty close. Apples, almonds, yogurt and raisins make a tasty quinoa porridge.
