Title:
Chicken and Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Small whole chicken (giblets removed)
1 onion chopped
salt to taste
pepper to taste
3 carrots sliced
2 celery stalks chopped
8 chicken bouillon cubes
2 tsp parsley

Directions:
Cover chicken with water and Boil chicken, onion,chopped celery,sliced carrots, salt and pepper, parsley and bouillon cubes till chicken is tender enough to fall off the bone. Remove chicken from pot and cut from bone. Add chicken back to the stock.
Gravy: combine small amounts of flour and water mixed to thicken chicken stock to your desired gravy consistancy. add to stock and wisk till thick as you desire. Let simmer
Baking powder biscuits makes 12 biscuits
2 cups flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 c shortening
2/3 to 3/4 c milk
cut shortening into dry ingredients till coarse crumbs. add milk stir with fork till dough follows fork. Knead dough 10 to 12 strokes. Roll out and cut into biscuits bake 12 to 15 minutes at 450 degrees.
