Title:
Mississippi Mud Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup butter/margarine, melted
2 cups graham wafer crumbs
½ cup sugar
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 large tub of cool whip
1 large pudding mix

Directions:
Mix butter with graham wafer crumbs. Sprinkle sugar in bowl and mix well. Press into 9x13-inch pan. Place in freezer.
1st Layer: Combine cream cheese and ½ tub of cool whip and spread over base.
2nd Layer: Make pudding according to instructions and spread over top of 1st layer.
3rd Layer: Cover 2nd layer with rest of cool whip.
