Title:
Potluck Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 head of lettuce, shredded
3 Roma tomatoes, diced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 package taco shredded cheese blend
1 bag Nacho Cheese Doritos, crushed
1 16 oz. bottle Western salad dressing
1/2 of a 16 oz. bottle taco sauce

Directions:
Brown hamburger and onions over medium heat. Drain. Allow to cool. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, shredded cheese and cooled hamburger and onion mixture. Stir well. Add the salad dressing and taco sauce. Mix well. Stir in the crushed Doritos directly before serving.

Kristin McNeil

Simple, delicious taco salad everyone will love!
