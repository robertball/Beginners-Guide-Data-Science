Title:
Homemade Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup cold butter
1 cup milk or buttermilk

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the first three ingredients.
Cut in the butter using a pastry cutter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Stir in milk just until moistened.
Turn onto a lightly floured surface; knead 8-10 times
Pat or roll out to 1/2 inch thickness.
Cut with a floured glass or cookie cutter.
Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet.
Bake at 425Â° for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.

midwestchef
Description: 
I found this recipe in a magazine a few weeks ago and decided to try it when I didn't have any canned biscuits for the Chicken and Biscuit recipe.  I found the dough easy to work with and they were delicious.
