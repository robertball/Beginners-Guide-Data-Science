Title:
Keto Chicken Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6328878.jpg
Ingredients:
3 skinless boneless chicken breasts
1 cup crushed pork rinds
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp dried basil
2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
Mix crushed pork rinds, Parmesan cheese, sea salt, thyme, basil, and Italian seasoning in a large bowl.  This will be the breading.
Place melted butter in a separate bowl.
Cut chicken breasts into 3/4-inch thick strips (about 5 strips each breast)
Dip each strip into the melted butter, then coat with the breading mixture.
Place coated chicken strips on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Bake for 20 minutes, or until chicken is cooked thoroughly.

Description: 
Crunchy, juicy chicken strips without all the carbs!   These are delicious and sure to satisfy those carb cravings.
