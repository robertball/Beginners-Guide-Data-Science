Title:
Cool Whip Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C milk
1 (3 1/2oz)package instant pudding mix, any flavor
1/4 C powdered sugar
8 oz Cool Whip

Directions:
Pour milk into bowl, add pudding mix and sugar
Beat with wire whisk for 2 min.
Gently fold in whipped topping
