Title:
Nana's Amazing Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 tablespoons butter
2 1/2 cups finely chopped onions
3 tart apples, cored and chopped (do not peel)
1 cup dried cranberries
1 lb bulk breakfast sausage (Jimmy Dean)
3 cups coarsely crumbled corn bread
3 cups coarsely crumbled whole wheat bread
3 cups coarsely crumbled french bread
2 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp dried sage
1/2 cup chopped Italian parsley
1 1/2 cups pecan halves

Directions:
Melt half the butter in a skilled and cook the onions over medium heat, partially covered, until tender and lightly colored (about 25 minutes).  Transfer onions and butter to a large mixing bowl
Melt remaining butter in same skillet.  Add apple chunks and cook until lightly colored but not mushy.  Transfer apples and butter to same bowl.
Cook sausage in the skillet over medium heat, stirring until lightly browned.  Use a slotted spoon to transfer to mixing bowl.  Reserve the fat from the pan.
Add the remaining ingredients to the bowl and mix gently.
Bake covered at 325 for 30-45 minutes, baste occasionally.
