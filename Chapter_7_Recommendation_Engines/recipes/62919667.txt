Title:
scallop and clam chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bay (the small kind) scallops, rinsed and drained
2 cans whole baby clams (save about half the juice)
1 stick butter
2 bunch green onion, minced
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cup half and half
1 cup dry white wine
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
2 diced (cooked) potatoes
Salt and White Pepper to taste
1 tsp Thyme

Directions:
Wash and drain scallops.  In a medium stock pot, melt butter over medium-low heat. Saute onions and mushrooms until tender.
Stir in flour, half n half, and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly. Add wine, potatoes, clams, juice leftover from clams, scallops, Thyme, salt and pepper. Cook over medium heat until thickened. Simmer for about 5-8 min before removing from heat.
Serve topped with Swiss cheese and parsley.

Submitter: 
Pamela Cressey Gileno
Description: 
This is my first attempt at making a chowder...and using a few recipes on here, plus an addition of my own, I came up with the best chowder EVER!  If you prefer your chowder not to have the flavor of bacon...this is an excellent recipe.
