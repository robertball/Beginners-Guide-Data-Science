Title:
Quick Cream Cheese Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow cream

Directions:
Whip cream cheese and marshmallow cream together in a bowl until smooth.

Submitter: 
Karen Pugh
Description: 
Serve this quick and simple dip with your favorite fruit.
