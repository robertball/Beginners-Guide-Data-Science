Title:
Garlic-Veggie Pasta Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887710.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked pasta noodles, any shape desired
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 cloves garlic, finely diced
2 cups fresh or frozen peas & carrots, carrots cut into bite size pieces
garlic salt, to taste
fresh ground black pepper, to taste
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. oregano
2 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Boil the pasta according to package directions.
Add in the vegetables for the last 5-6 minutes of cook time, drain.
Meanwhile, heat the olive oil and garlic in a skillet over medium heat until garlic begins to turn a very light brown and become fragrant.
Toss the pasta with oil and spices. Top with cheese.
Serve hot.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick meal for Meatless Monday or a wholesome lunch.
To make it even healthier use a whole wheat or vegetable pasta.
