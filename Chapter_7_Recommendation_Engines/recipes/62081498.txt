Title:
Bacon Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs - week old eggs work best
4 tablespoons of ranch dressing
6 slices of cooked bacon - You can use precooked bacon if you like

Directions:
Place eggs in salted water and bring to boil. let boil for about 15 minutes so that eggs are hard boiled. Drain. Run eggs under cold water.
Peel eggs - week old eggs peel easier than fresh eggs. Slice eggs in half lengthwise.
Scoop out egg yolks. Place yolks in a bowl.
Add ranch dressing to yolks. Chop bacon into small pieces and add to yolks. Mash to ingredients with a fork. You can add a bit more ranch dressing if you desire a creamier texture.
Spoon about 1 tablespoon of yolk mixture onto sliced egg whites. Put onto a tray. Let chill in the fridge. Serve and enjoy!
