Title:
Philly Cheesesteak Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 large onion, diced
1/2 pound shaved rib-eye, chopped
1 (8 ounce) jar processed cheese spread (such as Cheese WhizÂ®)
2 fresh jalapeno peppers, sliced
1 (18 ounce) package restaurant-style tortilla chips

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion in the hot oil until tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Transfer onion to a bowl. Cook and stir rib-eye in the same skillet until meat is no longer red and liquid is evaporated, 5 to 10 minutes. Stir onion into cooked rib-eye.
Place a handful of nachos on a plate.  Spoon rib-eye mixture over hot dog; top with cheese spread and jalapenos
