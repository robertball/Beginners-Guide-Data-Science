Title:
Honey-Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts
French Dressing:
1/3 cup veg. oil
2 Tbsp. white wine vinegar
½ tsp. paprika
¼ tsp. dry mustard
¼ tsp. dried tarragon
1 garlic clove, minced
1/3 cup liquid honey
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper and place on foil on a cookie sheet.
Mix dressing ingredients together and pour over chicken.
Bake for 30 minutes.
