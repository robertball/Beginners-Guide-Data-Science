Title:
Rice with Chorizo (Arroz con Chorizo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/4 cup chopped onion
3/4 pound chorizo, removed from its casing and crumbled
1/4 pound roma tomatoes, peeled and chopped
3 1/2 cups cooked white rice
1/2 cup cojita cheese
salt to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil and saut√© the onion until it begins to soften. Add the chorizo and continue to cook, stirring, for 5 minutes.
Add the tomatoes and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
Add the rice, stirring to combine, and cook until heated through. Add salt to taste. Top with cojita cheese.
Serve immediately

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a quick mexican side dish, it can also be served as a main dish with a salad on the side.
