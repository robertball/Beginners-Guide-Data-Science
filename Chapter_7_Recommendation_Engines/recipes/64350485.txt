Title:
Sugar-Free Chiffon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Eggs, Large, Brown, Grade A__2 Each
Xylitol_________________2 Tablespoons
Cream Of Tartar_________½ Teaspoon
All-Purpose Flour_______1 ¾ Cups
Xylitol_________________½ Cup+2 Tablespoons
Baking Powder___________1 ½ teaspoons
Baking Soda_____________¾ Teaspoons
Vanilla Extract, Pure___1 teaspoons
Whole Milk______________1 Cup
Margarine, melted_______¼ Cup

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Separate the Eggs Between the egg yolks and the egg white; set the egg yolks aside.
Mix the ¼ cup of sugar together with the cream of tartar, add them together in a large mixing bowl with the egg whites; whisk the mixture into a stiff meringue.
Set the meringue aside; sift the cake flour, ½ cup of sugar, baking powder, and baking soda together into a large mixing bowl.
Beat the ingredients together in with the vanilla extract, milk, and margarine until the mixture forms into a smooth batter.
Fold the reserved meringue into the batter in two stages.
Grease and lightly flour the insides of a 9x5 loaf pan and pour the cake batter inside; bake at 350F For 45 Minutes or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
Remove the cake from the oven and allow it to rest for 10 minutes away from the heat; invert the pan onto a clean dry surface to remove the cake.
Trim, and frost as desired.

A sugar free variant of our basic chiffon cake
