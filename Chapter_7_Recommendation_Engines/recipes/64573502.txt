Title:
Roasted Vegetable and Ricotta Pita Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Whole Wheat Pita Bread
2 TBS Olive Oil (divided)
Salt/ Pepper
Carrots cut in large chunks
Parsnips cut in large chunks
1 handful fresh Kale stems removed
1/4 cup of low fat Ricotta cheese
Lunch meat ( optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375.
Toss the carrots and Parsnips in 1 Tbs of olive oil and place on a cookie sheet lined with foil or parchment paper.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper if desired.  Bake in oven for 10 minutes and then turn veggies and bake another 10-15 minutes.  Be sure to continue to check so you don't burn the veggies.  Time will vary depending on how big your chunks are
Toss the Kale with Olive oil and set aside.
In the mean time, spread 1/2 Tbs of the olive oil onto the pita all the way to the edges.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper as desired.
Once the vegetables are done roasting take out of the oven and let cool.
Place the Kale on the pita bread and bake in preheated oven for 8 minutes.
Take out of the oven and top with the roasted veggies.  Dollop the Ricotta cheese by the tsp over the top.
Top with lunch meat if using.
Put back in oven for 5 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Love2cook32
Description: 
This is an easy and healthy lunch or light dinner.  The sweet roasted veggies give way to the creaminess of Ricotta cheese.  You can change up the veggies or even add meat for more protein.
