Title:
Tomato Bacon & Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bacon
1 small onion, chopped finely
1 8 oz can of tomatoes, herbs and peppers
1 clove of garlic, crushed
3 tablespoons of tomato puree
1 8oz can tomato sauce
5 cups of chicken stock
1 bay leave
1 teaspoon of thyme
1 bouquet cube
1 teaspoon of Italian herbs
1 level teaspoon of sugar

Directions:
Fry bacon in a little olive oil until cooked – remove from the pan. Add a table spoon of olive oil and sauté the onions until transparent.
Add tin of tomatoes, tomato puree, tomato sauce and garlic stir well. Add all other ingredients, including the bacon and bring to the boil. Simmer for 20 mins then with a hand whisk process until you get the texture you want.
Serve with garlic bread.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
This is great with a grilled cheese sammy! Great for really cold winter days!
