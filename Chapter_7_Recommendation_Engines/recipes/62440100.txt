Title:
3 Musketeer's Mint Candy Bar® Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457105.jpg
Ingredients:
1(9 ounce)bag 3 Musketeer Mint® Mini Candies OR 4(2 ounce)3 Musketeer Mint Candy bars
1/4 cup butter
1 tbsp milk
2 tsp Pure Mint Extract
1 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar

Directions:
Unwrap mint candies from wrappers and place mint candies, butter, milk and extract in a microwaveable bowl.
Place bowl with contents in the microwave for 35 seconds. Remove and stir. Place back into microwave for another 35 seconds. Remove and stir once more.
Stir in confectioner's sugar, until well blended and smooth. Then either ice immediately or cool at room temperature, as desired to your favorite cupcake recipe
