Title:
Mom's Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
boneless pork chops
onion
garlic
celery
carrot
parsley
s/p
egg, beaten
dried bread crumbs

Directions:
Trim and flatten thick sections of porkchops.  dip first in flour, then egg and then crumbs. place in fridge at least 1 hour.  Fry in oil until brown on each side, seasoning with s/p while browning, add veggies and cook until tender.  Add water to covered porkchops and simmer 1 hr
