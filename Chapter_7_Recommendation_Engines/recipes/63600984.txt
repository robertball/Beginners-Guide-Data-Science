Title:
Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Vegetable Oil
1/3 pound ham cooked and cubed
1 onion diced
salt and pepper to taste
3 cloves garlic
2 inch piece peeled and finely chopped (optional)
3 whole scallions
1 can corn
3 carrots peeled and chopped
4 large eggs lightly beaten
4 cups dry long grain rice cooked, cooled and grains seperated

Directions:
Heat a large heavy bottomed skillet over high heat, when hot add 1 TBSP of the oil and cook the ham stirring constantly until lightly browned. Add the onions to the pan and season with salt and pepper, cook for 1-2 minutes. add garlic, ginger and the whites of the scallions. Fry for about 30 seconds, then add the corn and carrots. cook until the carrots are more tender but still crisp. Transfer the contents to a bowl and set aside.
2. Return the pan to the heat and add 2 more tablespoons of oil. Add the eggs and cook until almost set.transfer the egg to the bowl.
3. Return the pan to the heat and add the remaining oil. add the rice to the pan and use a spoon to break up any clumps. Season with salt and pepper and stir-fry the rice to coat evenly with the oil. stop stirring and then let the rice cook undisturbed until it gets slightly crispy. Let cook about two minutes and then repeat 1-2 more times. Add the scallion greens and the contents of the bowl, and cook while periodicly stirring for 3-4 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Robert Loughrey
Description: 
Excellent as a main dish or make a batch for the side dish of a larger meal. Bacon or chicken can be substituted for the ham, or leave it out for a vegetarian style dish.
