Title:
Chocolate-Cinnamon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/86838.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/2 tablespoons chocolate-hazelnut spread, such as NutellaÂ®

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese, marshmallow cream, and whipped topping with an electric mixer until smooth. Stir in cinnamon, vanilla, and chocolate-hazelnut spread. Pour dip into a serving dish and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
MMMGOOD!
Description: 
This light, creamy dip tastes great paired with strawberries, bananas, or apples. Try a spoonful on top of pound cake or angel food cake, it's delicious!
