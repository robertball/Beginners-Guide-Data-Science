Title:
Buttermilk Graham Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/428073.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup (14 squares) Honey Graham Crackers, crushed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon allspice
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
Buttermilk Glaze
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup buttermilk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/3 cup butter or margarine

Directions:
Blend oil and sugar thoroughly
Add eggs and vanilla extract, beat well
In separate bowl, mix together crumbs, flour, salt, baking powder, spices and baking soda
Add to creamed mixture alternately with buttermilk (beginning and ending with flour)
Stir in chopped walnuts (optional)
Pour into greased bundt pan and Bake at 300F for one hour, or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean
Cool in pan for 15 minutes
While cake is cooling, combine sugar, buttermilk, vanilla, and butter in a small saucepan and boil for one minute. Glaze will be very thin
Invert cake onto serving plate and use a baster to drizzle glaze over warm cake, re-using any run off until all absorbed into cake

Submitter: 
RioChick
Description: 
Old-timey and delicious! Very moist and just the right amount of spice, with a tangy vanilla Buttermilk Glaze to finish it off. Enjoy!
