Title:
Apple Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Apples
6 Tablespoons Peanut Butter
1 Cup Raisins

Directions:
Wash and core apples.  Slice in rings.  Spread peanut butter on two rings, place raisins on one side, sandwich two rings together.  Makes about 6 sandwiches.

Submitter: 
KESSIANNE
Description: 
Simple bread-less sandwiches made with apples, peanut butter and raisins
