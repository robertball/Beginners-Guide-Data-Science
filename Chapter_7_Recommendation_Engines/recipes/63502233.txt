Title:
3-Day Crock Pot Chicken (Whole) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt water (or enough to cover chicken)
1/8 cup salt
1/8 cup sugar
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon thyme
1 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 large roasting chicken (with pop-up timer if possible)
1 cup chopped onion (optional)

Directions:
(Day 1) Rinse chicken with water and place in large stainless steel or plastic bowl.
Mix 1/8 cup sugar and 1/8 cup salt into water and pour over chicken (using +/- 1 qt. of water to cover chicken completely).
Refrigerate overnight.
(Day 2) Put chopped onion into bottom of crockpot dish.
Remove chicken from liquid (discard liquid) and pat chicken dry.
In a small bowl, combine the spices.
Remove any giblets from chicken and clean chicken.
Rub spice mixture onto the chicken.
Place in crockpot dish, cover with lid and refrigerate overnight.
(Day 3) Place crockpot dish into warmer and cook on low for 4-8 hours (a pop-up timer is recommended).  There is no need to add liquid to the crockpot while cooking.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Nolan
Description: 
Brining, overnight seasoning and slow-cooking make this the most moist and flavorful slow cooker recipe for whole chicken.  With minimal prep-time, this recipe is incredibly easy.
