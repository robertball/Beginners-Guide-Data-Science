Title:
Spinach Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg frozen chopped spinach
3 Tbsp. shortening
3 heaping Tbsp. flour, sifted
1/2 small onion
clove garlic, chopped
2 cups cold milk
salt to taste

Directions:
Cook spinach, drain and puree in a food processor.
Melt shortening into saucepan.
Sift flour into pan and brown.
Chop onion and garlic and add to mixture.
Add spinach and blend thoroughly.
Add milk and bring to boil, stirring constantly.
Salt to taste.

Submitter: 
cdagirl
Description: 
My grandmother always served this with veal cutlets but this is great with just about anything.
