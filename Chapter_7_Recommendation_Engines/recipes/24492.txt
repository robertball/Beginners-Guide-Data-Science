Title:
Angie's Special Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1924764.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces deluxe Canadian whiskey
1 fluid ounce melon liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce pineapple juice

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer full of ice, combine whiskey, melon liqueur and pineapple juice. Shake vigorously and strain into shot glasses.

Submitter: 
ANG242424
Description: 
This is a great shot I made up one night while bartending. I combined Canadian Whiskey (I prefer Crown Royal) with Melon Liquor and a splash of pineapple juice.
