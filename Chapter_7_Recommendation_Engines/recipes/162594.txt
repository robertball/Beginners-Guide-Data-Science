Title:
Quick and Easy Steak Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985825.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups torn romaine lettuce
2 small plum tomatoes, sliced
1 yellow pepper, halved, grilled and cut into strips
1 pound beef flank steak, grilled, sliced
1/2 cup KRAFT Natural 2% Milk Reduced Fat Colby & Monterey Jack Cheese Crumbles
1/2 cup KRAFT Light Zesty Italian Reduced Fat Dressing

Directions:
Cover four salad plates with lettuce.
Top with tomatoes, peppers, meat and cheese.
Drizzle with dressing just before serving.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Salad Dressing
Description: 
Slices of grilled flank steak, plum tomato, and yellow bell pepper, topped with the kick of KRAFT Light Zesty Italian dressing, make this green salad a complete meal.
