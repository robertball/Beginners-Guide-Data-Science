Title:
Strawberry Freeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Oreo cookies, crushed (or graham crumbs)
Â¼ cup butter, melted
1 pkg.  (8 oz.) PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1/2 cup Sugar
1 can  (12 oz.) frozen berry juice concentrate, thawed
1 cup crushed strawberries
1 tub  (8 oz.) COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed
2 cups whole strawberries, cut in half

Directions:
Mix crushed cookies and butter; press firmly into bottom of a 9-inch springform pan (or 9-inch square pan).
Beat cream cheese and sugar with mixer until well blended. Gradually beat in juice concentrate. Stir in crushed strawberries. Whisk in COOL WHIP until well blended. Pour over cookies in pan.
Freeze 6 hours or until firm. Remove from freezer 15 min. before serving; let stand at room temperature to soften slightly. Top with berry halves just before serving.
