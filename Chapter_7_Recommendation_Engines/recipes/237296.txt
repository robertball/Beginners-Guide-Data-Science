Title:
Grapefruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1946239.jpg
Ingredients:
3 grapefruit, peeled and sectioned
1 cup cold water
3 ounces fresh spinach
6 ice cubes
1 (1/2 inch) piece peeled fresh ginger
1 teaspoon flax seeds

Directions:
Blend grapefruit, water, spinach, ice cubes, ginger, and flax seeds in a blender or NutriBullet(R) until smooth.

Submitter: 
LMJHEN
Description: 
A healthy, easy, and satisfying smoothie for those who enjoy grapefruit or are looking for a drink that is lower in sugar and calories. This is loaded with taste and is good for you!
