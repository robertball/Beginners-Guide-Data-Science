Title:
Strawberry Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984878.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 ounces Stirrings Strawberry Daiquiri Cocktail Mixer
2 ounces Stirrings Pina Colada Cocktail Mixer
2 1/2 ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay Coconut Rum
2 cups ice
1 slice strawberry
1 slice pineapple

Directions:
Add ingredients to a blender and mix for 15 to 20 seconds or until smooth.
Pour into daiquiri glass.
Garnish with a slice of strawberry and pineapple.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Captain Morgan Parrot Bay Coconut Rum is blended with ice, strawberry daiquiri mix and pina colada mix and then given a fruit garnish in this frosty tropical cocktail.
