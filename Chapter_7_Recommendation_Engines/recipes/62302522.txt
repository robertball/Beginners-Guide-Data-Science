Title:
K-Dub's Caesar Chicken Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole wheat tortillas
2 small sized boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 tablespoon of butter
salt and pepper
4 tablespoons of Caesar dressing
4 leaves of romaine lettuce, shredded
1/4 cup of freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a skillet.  Season both sides of chicken breasts with salt and pepper and cook in skillet for 15 minutes.  Shred each chicken breast.
Spread 2 tablespoons of Caesar dressing on each tortilla.  Add one shredded chicken breast to each tortilla, top with shredded romaine lettuce and freshly grated parmesan cheese.
Fold bottom of tortilla up and roll to make a wrap.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
K-Dub
Description: 
A great lunch or a hearty snack!! It's a yummy wrap you can take on the go, for lunch at work, or a late night munchie.
