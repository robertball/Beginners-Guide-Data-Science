Title:
Apple crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c butter or margarine
1 1/4 c lightly packed brown sugar
1/3 c flour
2/3 c oatmeal
1/4 tsp cinnamon
6 peeled apples

Directions:
Preheat oven 375
Cream butter and brown sugar
Stir in until crumbly flour oatmeal, cinsmmon mixture
Arrange apples in greased pan
Sprinkle with crumbs
Bake at 375 for 40 minutes
Makes 6 servings
