Title:
Mango Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large egg
1/2 cup sourcream (light works fine)
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
4 tbsp melted and cooled butter
1 1/2 cup diced mango
2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 tbsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
Topping:
1/3 cup chopped unsalted macadamia nuts
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsp butter softened

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Topping:
Combine all ingrediants except butter in a small bowl. Mash in butter with a fork till mixture is crumbly. Chill till needed.
Muffins:
Grease a 12cup muffin tin.  In a large bowl, whisk together egg, sour cream, milk, vanilla and melted butter.  Fold in mango.  In another large bowl stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Make a well in dry ingrediants and add mango mixture.  Fold together to just moisten and evenly blend.  the batter will be lumpy.
Fill muffin tins to tops.  Spinkle topping over and bake 25-30 mins.  Cool 10 mins and remove from pan.

These delicious muffins have a macadamia nut topping (other nuts could be substituted) they are fabulous served warm.
