Title:
Big Ray's Mexican Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101099.jpg
Ingredients:
12 cups double brewed coffee
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup half-n-half (or powdered creamer)
7 oz sweetened condensed milk (half a can)
1 package instant vanilla pudding mix
2 Tbsp chili powder
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup of Jameson's Whiskey (optional)

Directions:
Brew 12 cups of coffee with the twice the amount of coffee(double brew) or use 24 Tbs instant coffee with 12 cups water.
In a small bowl, combine sugar, chili, cayenne and dry pudding mix. Stir in condensed milk, vanilla and powder into coffee.  Drink hot or cold.
