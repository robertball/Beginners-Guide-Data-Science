Title:
Mild Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) pkg. cream cheese
1 cup butter (no substitutions)
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
2-4 Tbsp. milk or cream

Directions:
Beat all ingredients, except milk, together on medium-high speed until light and fluffy. If needed, add milk until desired consistency is reached.
