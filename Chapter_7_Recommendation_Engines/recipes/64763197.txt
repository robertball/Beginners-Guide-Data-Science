Title:
Chocolate Cherry and Pecan cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7213514.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp cornstarch
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/4 cup shortening
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp molasses
1 large egg
3/4 cup dark chocolate chips
1/2 cup dried cherries
1/2 cup chopped pecans
sprinkle coarse sea salt for topping

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt.
Beat together butter, shortening, and both sugars in large bowl until light and fluffy. On a low speed, add vanilla, egg, and molasses.
Graduallly add the flour mixture until just combined.
Using a wooden spoon, fold in the cherries, chocolate, and pecans.
Cover and place in fridge for at least 2 hrs.
Preheat oven to 350 F and line cookie sheets with parchment or silicone. Roll balls of cookie dough, 3 tbsp, and place on sheet 2 inches apart.
bake for 12-14 minutes, until golden brown on edges and slightly underdone in the middle.
Sprinkle with sea salt. Let cool on sheet for about 5 minutes, cookie will continue to cook. Transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
