Title:
Aw-some  Cole Slaw redux Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons  mayonnaise
1 Tablespoon white sugar
1 Tablespoon and 1 teaspoon milk
1 Tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 tablespoons Mirin
2 grinds Hawaiian rock salt or other grinding salt
3 grinds of fresh peppercorns, large output
3 cups shredded cabbage
4 medium carrots, thinly sliced with the mandolin
1 Tablespoon minced sweet onion
1-2 teaspoons dill
1 teaspoon caraway seeds.
2 teaspoons mini capers (optional)

Directions:
Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, milk, lemon juice, vinegar, and salt and pepper in a large bowl.
Toss the cabbage, carrots, and onion into the dressing; mix well. Best if refrigerated for at least 3 hours or up to overnight before serving.
