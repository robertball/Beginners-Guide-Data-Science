Title:
Tammy's Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1109044.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Box Barilla Elbow Noodles
1/2 Jar Hellman's Mayonnaise (Not Miracle Whip)
1 Bag Frozen Peas
16oz/1 pound Imitation Crab (Cut Into Chunks)
3/4 Block Mild Cheddar Cheese (Cubed)
Splash of Milk
1/4 Cup Sugar

Directions:
1. Boil water and cook noodles, drain, and set aside.
2. In the meantime, cut up crab and cheese.
3. Combine mayonnaise and sugar in a medium bowl.  Add a generous splash of milk to the mixture and stir until smooth and creamy.
4. In a separate large bowl, combine crab, noodles, peas, and cheese and stir together.  Then add mayonnaise mixture.
5. Let cool in the fridge for a few hours before serving.
6. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Ashley Rauschnot
Description: 
Barilla Elbow Noodles, Hellman's Mayonaise, Frozen Peas, Imitation Crab, & Mild Cheddar Cheese make for a quick and delicious cold salad that is sure to be a hit at parties!
