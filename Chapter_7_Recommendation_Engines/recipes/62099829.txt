Title:
Holiday Orange Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. can of frozen lemonade
12 oz. can of frozen orange juice
4 cans water
3/4 cup sugar
2 c. tea
8-12 oz. bourbon

Directions:
Mix and freeze for 6 hours.
Stir and refreeze.
Let it thaw a little before serving and use an ice cream scoop to serve.
I serve these in irish coffee glasses to make it look even more appetizing.

Submitter: 
Steph T.
Description: 
This is a refreshing slush that is perfect for entertaining during the holidays.  It can be made with or without alcohol depending on your needs.  Any extra slush can easily be stored in your freezer and pulled out whenever you have unexpected guests.
