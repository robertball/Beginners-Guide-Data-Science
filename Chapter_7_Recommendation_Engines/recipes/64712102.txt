Title:
Catalina Prosciutto Blueberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5618102.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups spinach
3/4 cup blueberries
3 oz of prosciutto
2 hard boiled eggs (cut into wedges)
2 1/2 tablespoons of Kraft Catalina dressing
1 mini-cucumber sliced
1 oz of goat cheese

Directions:
1. In a large bowl toss spinach, cucumber, blueberries, and Catalina dressing. Divide between two plates.
2. Cut prosciutto into thin ribbons. Heat a skillet on medium high heat. Crisp up prosciutto to desired crispiness.
3. Top salads with egg wedges, crisped prosciutto, and crumbled goat cheese.
4. Serve and enjoy!
