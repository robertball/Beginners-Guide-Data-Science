Title:
Magic cookie bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Butter ; melted
1 1/2 cups Graham crackers ; crushed
14 ounce Sweetened Condensed Milk ; 1 can
1 cup Walnuts ; chopped
2 cup Chocolate chips ; or butterscotch chips
1 1/3 cup Coconut ; sweetened flacked

Directions:
preheat oven to 350Â°
grease a 9x13 glass pan. Mix graham cracker crumbs with butter and press into glass pan.
Layer other ingredients on top of each other in order. Starting with condensed milk
bake 25 minutes untill lightly brown on top.
cool in pan 15 minutes then cut into bars.

Description: 
A versitly bar cookie. Use whatever chips and nuts you have extra from your holiday baking.
