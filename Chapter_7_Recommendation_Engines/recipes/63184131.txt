Title:
Amy's Israel Egg Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T diced red onion
1 clove garlic
1/2 cup fresh spinach
2 T fresh cream
4 eggs
1 T butter
salt and pepper

Directions:
Sautee onion in butter for about 4 minutes until tender, add garlic sautee for an addl minute.  Add spinach that you have thinly sliced sautee for a few addl seconds then add cream.  Simmer for about a minute then add the four eggs then cook until the desired doneness.  S&P to taste.
