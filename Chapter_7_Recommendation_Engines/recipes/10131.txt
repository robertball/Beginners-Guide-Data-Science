Title:
Sponge Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1101504.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon anise extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Beat six eggs.  Add sugar, oil, and anise flavoring and mix well.
Mix in the flour and baking powder.  Beat well.
Smooth batter into  a 10 x 15 inch  greased and floured pan.  Bake for 25 - 30 minutes.  Cool then cut in strips.
