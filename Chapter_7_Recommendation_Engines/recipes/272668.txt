Title:
Keto Chia Pudding Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
1/2 cup warm water
1/4 cup chia seeds
1 scoop vanilla-flavored plant-based protein powder (such as OrgainÂ®)
1 teaspoon maca powder

Directions:
Combine almond milk, water, chia seeds, protein powder, and maca powder in a bowl; mix well. Pour into two 8-ounce jars. Seal and refrigerate until set, 8 hours to overnight.
