Title:
Cheese Steak Subs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound Shaved Beef
4 Slices Cheese
1 Green Pepper Chopped
1 Medium Onion Chopped
12 oz. Mushroom Sliced
Sub Rolls
1/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
2 tbsp Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat skillet on medium heat
Add Oil and saute Beef with vegetables, Salt and Pepper
After beef has browned add cheese and cover for 1 minute.
Slice Rolls and place mixture in rolls.
Serve Immediately.
