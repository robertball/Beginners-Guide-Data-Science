Title:
Baked or Grilled Polenta Cakes in Muffin Pan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c polenta
3.5 c water (or stock)
salt/pepper

Directions:
Bring water to a boil, add salt.
Gradually add polenta, constantly stirring with a whisk.
Cook about 10-15 (or more -- 20-30) minutes on a low heat, stirring often. (cover, but leave adjar; polenta tends to spit
Meanwhile, grease muffin tin
Remove polenta from heat when done
Divide between the muffin cups
let cool
Bake polenta in muffin tin at 400F until golden brown (10-15 min). For crispy crust, fry in frying pan; or grill.
