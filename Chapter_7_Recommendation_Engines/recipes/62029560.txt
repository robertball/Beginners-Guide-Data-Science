Title:
Grannies Mock Lobster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
haddock
1 tbsp white vinegar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
pinch red pepper
2 or 3 bay leaves

Directions:
Start with frozen haddock.  Cut long pieces in half.  Place in frying pan on top of stove.  Cover haddock with cold water, bring to boil and add 1 tbsp white vinegar, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar, pinch red pepper, 2 or 3 bay leaves over the fish and cook 15 to 20 minutes, or until done.  Drain fish and dip or pour melted butter over it.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great recipe if you like fish.  My grannie used to make it alot.  She really was a good cook!
