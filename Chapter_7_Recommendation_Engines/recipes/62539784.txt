Title:
Chicken or Turkey Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/523126.jpg
Ingredients:
2 frozen pie crusts 9"
1 16 oz bag frozen vegetables, thawed
2 tablespoon unsalted butter
1/4 cup flour
2 1/2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1/4 cup heavy cream
3 cups cooked turkey breast meat or chicken, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Melt butter in large saucepan. Once butter is melted stir in the flour, this should make a clumpy mixture that's what you want . Increase the heat to high. Add the chickenbroth, cook until the mixture thickens,stirring constantly until flour mixture dissolves and the texture is thick about 5-10 minutes. Add the heavy cream,bag of vegetables, chicken or turkey, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until heated through and mixture is thick. Poor mixture into pie crust and top with remaining crust cut slits into top crust to allow venting. Bake at 400 for 10-15min or until golden brown.
