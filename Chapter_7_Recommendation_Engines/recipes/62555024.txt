Title:
Family Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/532510.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box supermoist white cake mix
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix cake mix, oil and eggs in a large bowl with a spoon until the dough forms(some dry mix will remain).  Shape dough into 1-inch balls.  Mix sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.  Roll balls in sugar mixture.  Place about 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 10 - 12 minutes or until set.  Remove cookies to wire rack to cool completely.
