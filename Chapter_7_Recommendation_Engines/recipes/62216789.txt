Title:
Chicken Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744501.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. wild rice
4 chk breasts, cubed
1 stick butter or 1-2 Tbsp olive oil
1 onion, chopped
1 c. chopped celery
1 c. chopped or shredded carrots
1 c. flour
1 can evaporated milk
1 can cream of chk soup
7-8 chk bouillon cubes or soup base
2 tsp. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Cube and brown chk breasts. Bring wild rice to a boil and simmer 1/2 hr.  During last 15 minutes, add chicken breasts.  Drain.
Melt butter and saute onion, chopped celery and chopped carrots.  Add flour and stir (will look lumpy).
To the wild rice, add 8 cups water, evaporated milk, cream of chk soup, chk bouillion cubes.
Add the sauteed items, lemon juice and pepper.
Stir and simmer one hour.
