Title:
Currant Affairs Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1655273.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk
1 frozen chopped banana
1/2 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
2/3 cup black currants
1/4 cup soft tofu

Directions:
Blend milk, banana, mango, currants, and tofu together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
A delicious affair between black currants and mangoes truly satisfies.
