Title:
Picnic Potato Salad with No Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds small new potatoes, cut in quarters
2 T balsamic vinegar
1/4 c extra virgin olive oil
1 T Dijon mustard
2 T chopped fresh basil
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 ounces (about 3/4 cup) crumbled blue cheese
2 T chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Cook potatoes until tender but not mushy.
In large bowl, whisk together the vinegar, olive oil, Dijon mustard, basil, salt and pepper.
When potatoes are done, drain well in a colander.  Add warm potatoes and onions to the dressing and toss gently, making sure to coat potatoes with dressing.
Let stand until cool, and then sprinkle with blue cheese and chives, fold in gently.
Best served at room temperature.

Submitter: 
It's A New Day
Description: 
This is a great potato salad if you're looking for one without mayonnaise.  Would hold up great at picnics.
