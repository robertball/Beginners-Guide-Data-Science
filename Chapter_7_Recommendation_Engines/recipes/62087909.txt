Title:
All Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of Bolthouse Farms Immunity Boost (Orange Juice can be substituted)
2 cups ice
1 peach, skin removed, cut into slices
1 banana, peeled, broken into thirds
10 medium sized strawberries, tops off, halved
10 large chunks of pineapple
12 raspberries
20 blueberries

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a blender, and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Chrisa
Description: 
This is a delicious and versatile smoothie.  It uses all real fruit, and is extremely healthy for you. (It does use a fruit juice and ice too) This is my favorite combination, but you can mix up the fruit for a different taste every time!
