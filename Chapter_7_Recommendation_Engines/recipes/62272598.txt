Title:
Pasta Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c onion, diced
3/4 c celery, diced
1 c carrot, diced
2 T olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 T parsley
1 t oregano
2 t basil
1 15-oz can tomato sauce
2 14-oz chicken broth
1 14.5-oz can petite diced tomatoes
1 15-oz can kidney beans
1 15-oz can white beans
5 oz frozen, chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
16-oz ditalini pasta
Grated parmesan cheese for garnish

Directions:
Sautee onion, celery and carrots in olive oil until onion is tender.
Add garlic, parsley, oregano and basil and stir until fragrant.
Add tomato sauce, chicken broth, tomatoes, beans and spinach and simmer several hours.
Cook pasta acording to package directions.
To serve: spoon some pasta into a bowl, top with soup and garnish with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
fullerla
Description: 
Tomatoes, beans, vegetables and spices combine to make a hearty soup that is served over ditalini pasta and garnished with parmesan cheese.  Paired with an Italian or wheat loaf, this soup makes a satisfying, nourishing meal.
