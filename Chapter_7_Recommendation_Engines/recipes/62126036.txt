Title:
Creamy Italian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breasts
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup butter
4 oz cream cheese
1 cup white wine, water, or chicken stock
1 pkg dry italian seasoning dressing mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together everything except the chicken breasts on the stove. Bring to a boil.
While preparing the sauce, put the chicken breasts in a greased oven pan.
Pour sauce over and bake uncovered for 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Nia
Description: 
This is so good and easy. The sauce is also excellent spooned over rice, noodles, or cooked veggies.
