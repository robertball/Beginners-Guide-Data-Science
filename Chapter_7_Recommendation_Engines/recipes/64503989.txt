Title:
U,S, 31 Bar-B-Que Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sweet pickle relish
1/2-1 cup catsup
1/2 cup yellow mustard
1-3 cups sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup white vinegar
5 cups shredded cabbage

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate for 48 hours or so.
