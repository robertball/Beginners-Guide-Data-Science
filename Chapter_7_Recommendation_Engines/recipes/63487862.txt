Title:
Chocolate Chunk and Walnut Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123279.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tbsp. unsalted butter
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
1/3 cup white flour
1/3 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 cups old-fashioned oats
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
3 oz. bittersweet chocolate, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter in small saucepan over low heat.  Remove from heat and add brown sugar; stir until smooth.
Combine flours baking soda, oats and salt in a medium bowl.
Combine butter mixture with the dry ingredients, and add egg and vanilla.  Fold in walnuts and chocolate.  Mix well, and spoon by tablespoonfuls onto lightly greased baking sheets.
Bake for 12 minutes or until tops are dry to the touch
