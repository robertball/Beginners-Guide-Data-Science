Title:
Cosmopolitian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 parts absolut citron
6 parts cranberry juice
1 part orange liqueor
1 dash of lime juice
lime

Directions:
Chill a cocktail glass with ice or in the freezer.
Pour absolut citron, cranberry juice and orange liqueor into a shaker.  Fill the shaker with ice cubes and shake it until the shaker is very cold.
Stain the drink into the cocktail glass.  Garnish with a lime wedge.
