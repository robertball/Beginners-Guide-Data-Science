Title:
Tzaziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 medium cucumber
2/3 cup plain yogurt(firm set)
2 tsp olive oil
1 tbsp chopped mint
1 garlic clove, minced
salt & pepper

Directions:
Dice cucumber, discarding seeds, place in bowl. Add yogurt and olive oil. Add the chopped mint and garlic. Stir well, adding salt & pepper to taste. Cover and chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
Use on sandwiches, or with pita bread, or veggies.
