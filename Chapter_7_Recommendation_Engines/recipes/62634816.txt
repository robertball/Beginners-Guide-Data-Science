Title:
Red Lobster Cheddar Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C biscuit mix
3/4 C cold whole milk
4 T cold butter
1/4 t garlic powder
1 C shredded cheddar cheese
TOPPING:
2 T melted butter
1/4 t dried parsley
1/2 t garlic powder
pinch salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
With pastry blender, combine butter w/baking mix until pea sized chunks form.
Add milk, garlic powder and cheddar and mix w/hands just until combined. Don't over mix.
Drop approx. 1/4 C dough onto ungreased baking sheet and bake for 15-17 min., until golden.
While still hot (don't remove from sheet), brush tops with topping mixture.
Serve immed., best warm.
