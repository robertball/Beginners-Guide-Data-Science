Title:
Cheese Strata Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 slices of white bread
1 lb cheddar cheese, sharp, shredded
2 tsp dry mustard
2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 small can green chilies, chopped, 4 oz
4 cups milk
6 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Trim bread; cut into cubes.
In a 3-quart baking dish, put in half the bread cubes, cheeses, dry mustard, salt, pepper, and green chilies.
Repeat a second layer.
Pour beaten eggs and milk over the layers.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
Bake (uncovered) in a 350-degree oven for 30-40 minutes or until bubbly and slightly brown on top.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
Refrigerate this egg and cheese casserole overnight, if desired, then pop it in the oven for breakfast.
