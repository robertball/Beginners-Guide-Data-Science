Title:
Quick & Easy Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Rotisserie Chicken (fully cooked)
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
3 cups chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
1 (12 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough (plain)

Directions:
Remove skin & debone chicken into bite size pieces, set aside.
In a large pot over medium heat add chicken broth, cream of celery and cream of mushroom.
Stir until lumps are gone, add salt and pepper to taste.
Flatten each biscuit with your palm on a cutting board and cut into 8 pieces(3 cuts vertically, 1 cut horizontally).
Once the dough is all cut start dropping them into the soup one or two at a time stirring occasionally to break up any that stick together.
Simmer over medium heat for 7 minutes, stir occasionally.
Add chicken, stir.
Return to simmer for another 3-5 minutes until dough is cooked through. Stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Amber Dawn
Description: 
Rotisserie chicken is deboned and combined with a can of cream of celery and cream of mushroom soup in this stew with refrigerated biscuits for dumplings.Modified from: Chicken and Dumplings III
