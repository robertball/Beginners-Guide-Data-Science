Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Peanut Butter Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/703010.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
7 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 cups old-fashioned oats
1 1/2 cups milk chocolate chips
3/4 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter, sugar, brown sugar and peanut butter.  Beat in eggs and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt; add to creamed mixture. Stir in oats, chocolate chips and peanut butter chips.
Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F for 13-15 minutes. Cool on wire racks.

Submitter: 
Yvonne Weston
Description: 
If you love peanut butter cookies and chocolate chip cookies, you will love these! A combination of peanut butter, milk chocolate chips and peanut butter chips make these cookies irresistible. My husband and 7 year old son ate the last batch in one day.Modified from: Oatmeal Chip Cookies
