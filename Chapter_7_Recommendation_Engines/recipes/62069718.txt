Title:
Tasty Beanless Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, crushed
1 green bell pepper, seeded & chopped
1 yellow bell pepper, seeded & chopped
2 stocks celery, chopped
1 lb. lean ground ground beef
3 cans (15 oz.) tomato sauce
1 can (14.5 oz.) diced tomatoes
2 tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. ground cumin

Directions:
1. In large stock pot heat olive oil. Add onion, crushed garlic, green & yellow bell peppers, and celery and cook about 5 minutes.  Add the ground beef and cook at medium heat untill meat is no longer pink.  Drain fat, if neccessary.
2. Add remaining ingredients (tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, chili powder, and ground cumin).  Stirring occasionally, bring to a boil, then simmer on low heat at least 1 hour. (The longer it simmers the better the flavor).

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a beanless chili with ground beef, onions, bell peppers, garlic, with a tomato sauce base.  Try it!  It's good!  Great served with shredded cheddar cheese melted on top and cornbread!
