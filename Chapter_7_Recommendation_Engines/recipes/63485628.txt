Title:
Zesty Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
juice of 2 limes
3/4 cup thick salsa
1/3 cup oil
2 cloves garlic
1 1/2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp salt
1 small red onion diced
2 celery stalks, chopped
1 green bell pepper chopped
1 lb can black beans
1 lb can garbanzo beans
1 lb can red beans
1 tomato chopped
lettuce

Directions:
mix first 5 ingredients.  add vegetables and beans, except tomato.  Chill.  add tomato just before serving on lettuce
