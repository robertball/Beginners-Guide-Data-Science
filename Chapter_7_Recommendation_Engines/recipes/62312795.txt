Title:
Jicama cucumber orange salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large (2 pounds) jicama
1  English (seedless) cucumber
4 medium (2 1/4 pounds) navel oranges
3 to 4  limes
1 cup(s) packed fresh cilantro leaves, coarsely chopped
1  tablespoon(s) vegetable oil
1/8 teaspoon(s) ground red pepper (cayenne)
Salt
Optional
1/2 avocado chopped
2 Tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Toss together the jicama, bell peppers, red onion, cucumber, orange, and cilantro in a large serving bowl. Pour lime juice over all. Sprinkle with a pinch of cayenne and paprika. Season generously with salt.
Let sit a half an hour before serving.
