Title:
Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon
1 cup diced sweet onion
2 cans minced clams
1 bottle of clam juice
3 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
4 tablespoons flour
3 cups light cream
1 teaspoons salt
Pepper to taste
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a large saucepot, gently fry the bacon (which has been diced) until crisp
Remove bacon
Add onion to the bacon drippings, fry gently until rich, golden brown
Remove onion
Drain the liquid from the clams; add to pot
Add bottled clam juice and potatoes; boil gently until tender
Gradually stir about 1 cup of cream into the flour; stir until smooth
Add to the potatoes, along with the rest of the cream
Add drained clams, salt and pepper
Cook over medium low heat, stirring constantly until thickened
Stir in milk, bacon and onion
