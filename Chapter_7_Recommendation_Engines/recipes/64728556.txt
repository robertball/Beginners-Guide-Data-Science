Title:
Spicy Cheeseburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cup water
2 Cups Peeled Potatoes (Cubed)
2 Small Carrots (grated)
1 Small Onion (Chopped)
1 1/4 C green peeper  (Chopped)
1 Jalapeno Pepper (Seeded & Chopped)
1 Garlic Clove (minced)
1 Tablespoon Boullion Granules
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1 Pound Ground Beef (Cooked & Drained)
2 1/2 Cup Milk (Divided)
3 Tablespoons All-Purpose Flour
1 (8 ounce) package process American Cheese (Cubed (Velvetta))
1/4 - 1 teaspsson Cayeen Pepper
1 jar picante sauce
Bacon

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine the first 9 ingredients; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes until potatoes are tender.
Stir in beef and 2 cups of milk; heat through.
Combine flour and remaining milk until smooth. Gradually stir into soup. Brin to boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened and bubbly.
Reduce heat; stir in cheese until melted.
Add Cayanne if desired.
Add picante sause if desired
Top with bacon just before you serve.

Submitter: 
Carrie Beth Rose
