Title:
Grasshopper Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 Oreo cookies, finely chopped
3 tbs of butter or margarine, melted
1 jar (7oz) marshmallow creme
1 tbs milk
3 cups softened frozen vanilla nonfat yogurt
1 container (8oz)frozen light whipped topping, thawed
1/2 tsp peppermint extract
8 drops green food coloring
additional chopped Oreos

Directions:
Finely chop Oreos and put in small bowl, add melted butter and mix well.  Press crumb mixture into bottom of spring-form pan and place in refrigerator
Combine Marshmallow and milk and whisk together, add frozen yogurt and whisk till well blended.  Fold in whipped topping, peppermint extract and food coloring.  Pour over crust.  Cover and freeze for 8 hours or overnight.  Garnish with additional chopped Oreos and serve
