Title:
7 Layer Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of jalapeno bean dip
1 package of avacado spread
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 package of taco seaoning mix
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1 can of black olives, drained and sliced
2 medium tomatoes diced
1 buncg of green onions, chopped with stems

Directions:
Place jalapeno bean dip at bottom of a 10" pie pan.
Spread avacado spread over the bean dip.
Mix together mayo, sour cream and taco seasoning as 3rd layer.
Add the grated cheese, followed by tomatoes, olives and scallions.
Serve with chip of choice - I preffer doritos
