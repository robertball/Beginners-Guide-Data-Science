Title:
Tiramisu Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2242213.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy whipping cream
2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
1 (8 ounce) container mascarpone cheese
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons grated semisweet chocolate

Directions:
Stir cream and coffee granules together in a bowl until dissolved. Add mascarpone cheese, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla extract; beat on low speed using an electric mixer until mixed. Increase speed to high and beat until soft peaks form; transfer to a serving bowl. Sprinkle chocolate over dip.

Submitter: 
TracyR
Description: 
A creamy dip that gives you a tiramisu flavor without the hassle! Serve with biscotti or thin cookies (such as Anna's Swedish cookies).
