Title:
Fall Wild Blend Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4588403.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c wild blend rice
1 orange, zested, peeled, and diced
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1 tbsp maple syrup
3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/3 c craisens
1/3 c toasted almond slices
1/3 c goat cheese, crumbled
2 c greens (romaine, spinach, kale, etc.
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare wild rice blend according to directions.
Meanwhile, combine orange zest, mustard, maple syrup, and vinegar in a medium size salad bowl.  Add olive oil, salt, and pepper, mix thouroughly.
Add cooked rice and remaining ingredients.
Chill at least one hour before serving.
