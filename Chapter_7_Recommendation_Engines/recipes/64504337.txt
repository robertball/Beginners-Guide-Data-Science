Title:
Spicy Jalapeno Cheese Pepper Potato Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups prepared mashed potatoes (salt and peppered and  much firmer than usually prepared)
1 egg
1 cup Tex Mex shredded Cheese
1 can (114 ml) Green Chiles
1 jar (250 ml) Pickled Jalepeno Peppers (only use 1/2 jar for milder flavour)
1/2 cup green onions chopped finely (white parts only)
3/4 cup panko bread crumbs
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp  chili spice
3/4 cup prepared salsa (mild to hot depending on your taste)

Directions:
1  Beat egg with onions, peppers, green chiles and cheese.
2. Add mashed potato and mix well , form patties and place on well greased baking sheet.
3. Mix bread crumbs with cumin and chili seasoning.
4. Dot each pattie with 1 1/2 tsp of salsa and sprinkle with 1 1/2 tsp of bread crumb topping.
5.  Cook @ 350 F for 20 minutes until cheese is melted and topping slightly browned.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
Excellent side dish for a meat loaf or other dish that needs a little accent.  As for myself, brunch side for scrambled eggs.
