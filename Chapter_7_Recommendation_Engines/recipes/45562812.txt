Title:
Jenny Allen's Homemade Handmade Bread/Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/314385.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups hot tap water
4 tablespoons granulated yeast
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup honey
3 teaspoons kosher salt
4 tablespoons oil (vegetable or canola)
8- 10 cups all purpose flour

Directions:
In a large bowl combine hot water, yeast, sugar, honey, salt, oil and 4 cups of the flour.
Stir with spatula until well combined.
Set aside and let double in size.  (It will grow and look like swiss cheese)
Once double add in the 4 more cups of flour and stir until well combined.
Turn out onto floured surface and knead dough till smooth and elastic adding more flour if necessary.
Form dough into a ball and place in a greased large bowl.
Cover with plastic wrap sprayed with cooking spray or damp towel.
Let rise until double.
Punch dough down and let rest about 2 minutes.
Divide dough into 3 - 4 portions and shape into loafs.
Place into greased 5x7 loaf pans.
Let rise until nearly double.
Bake at 325 degrees until golden brown.
Remove from oven and brush melted butter over crust to keep soft.
Turn out onto cooling racks.
Cool completely.

Submitter: 
Jenny Huntington Allen
Description: 
Easy recipe that creates a soft buttery crust.  Perfect bread to serve with homemade jam or grilled with your favorite cheese.
