Title:
Spicy greenbean casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14.5 oz. cans of Allen's Italian
cut greenbeans
2 cans of low sodium cream of mushroom soup
1 large can of French fried onions
1 large onion minced
1 to 2 small cans of mushrooms
1 tablespoon of minced garlic
1/2 a teaspoon of cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon of butter or olive oil
6 to 8 Velvetta cheese slices
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350. Heat butter or oil in skillet on medium heat. Saute onion, garlic and mushrooms until onion is tender. Season with salt, pepper and cayenne. In a deep casserole dish combine all ingredients except cheese. Once combined cover top with cheese slices. Heat in oven for 20-25 minutes or until cheese bubbles. Take out of oven and cover top with fried onions. Heat in oven for 5 minutes or until onions are browned.
