Title:
BLT Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 oz. macaroni, cooked as directed
8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 Cup Mayonnaise
1/3 Cup Salsa
1/4 Cup Lemon Juice
2 tsp. Instant Chicken Broth Seasoning
2 tsp. Sugar
green onion (optional)
1 large tomato, seeded and chopped

Directions:
In large bowl, combine mayo, salsa, lemon juice, broth seasoning, and sugar.
Stir in macaroni, tomato and onions.
Cover; chill.  Just before serving add bacon.

Submitter: 
TracyR
Description: 
The flavors of a BLT in an easy pasta salad.  The tangy dressing really adds to it!
