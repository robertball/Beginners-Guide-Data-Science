Title:
BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2   Cups Ketchup
1/4 Cup Molasses
3   Teaspoon Worcestershire (Lea & Perrins)
2   Tablespoons Lemon Juice
1/2 Teaspoon Tabasco Brand Sauce
3/8 Cup Dark Brown Sugar
1/2 Teaspoon Cayenne Powder
2   Cloves Minced Garlic (or to taste)
1   Tablespoon Course Ground Pepper

Directions:
Blend all together. Add while BBQ or as a dip after.
