Title:
Strawberry Guava Spritzer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6843957.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
1/2 cup strawberry guava flavored juice concentrate
1/2 cup club soda

Directions:
Fill a pint glass with ice. Pour juice concentrate and club soda into the glass and stir.

Submitter: 
abeauregard
Description: 
This an endlessly variable recipe. Try different kinds of juice concentrate or different carbonated beverages according to your taste. Use more or less club soda depending on the the level of sweetness you want.
