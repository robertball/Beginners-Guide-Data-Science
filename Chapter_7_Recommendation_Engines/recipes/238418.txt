Title:
Agave Almond Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1233055.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups unsalted natural whole almonds
1/4 cup agave nectar
1/8 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions:
Place almonds, agave nectar and salt in Oster(R) Versa(R) Performance Blender; PULSE on HIGH 2 to 3 times.
Blend on HIGH for 1-1/2 to 2 minutes or until creamy, using tamper to press ingredients into blade; transfer to a covered container.
Store airtight in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Oster® Versa®
Description: 
Almond butter sweetened with agave nectar makes a creamy spread for snacks or sandwiches.
