Title:
Cherry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979591.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 teaspoons honey
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
Salad:
5 cups cleaned torn spinach leaves, stems removed
1 cup bite-sized fresh pineapple wedges
1/2 cup dried tart cherries
1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
Crumbled feta cheese, if desired (optional)

Directions:
For the dressing, combine oil, vinegar, honey and pepper in a medium bowl; mix well.
For the salad, combine spinach with pineapple, cherries and onion in a large salad bowl.
Spoon dressing over spinach mixture; mix to coat salad with dressing. Serve topped with feta cheese, if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
Cherries add a special touch to salads.
