Title:
Fresh Homemade Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups quartered tomatoes, drained
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoon green canned green chillies
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice (one lime)
2 tablespoon minced fresh cilantro
2 cloves garlic, peeled
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a food processor, combine all ingredients; cover and process until chunky. Transfer to a small bowl. Refridgerate for at least an hour or so to help the flavors blend, or eat right away!

Submitter: 
Stephanie Roynestad Fuller
Description: 
I love Mexican food and this refreshing salsa is easy to prepare, notes Sarah Berger from Minneapolis, Minnesota. I think it tastes even better than the store-bought variety.Modified from: Speedy Homemade Salsa
