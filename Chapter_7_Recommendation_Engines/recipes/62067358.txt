Title:
Pink Cloud Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. cranberries (1 1/2 bags)
2 c. miniature marshmallows
2 c. sugar
1 c. chopped pecans
2 c. chopped celery
2 c. whipping cream, whipped

Directions:
Grind cranberries in blender or food processor.  Mix with sugar and let stand overnight in the refrigerator.  Add rest of ingredients one hour before serving.

Submitter: 
CHERI
Description: 
A sweet, creamy cranberry salad that is a nice compliment to a ham or turkey dinner.
