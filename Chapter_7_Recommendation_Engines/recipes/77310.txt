Title:
Crunchy Peppermint Candy Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4716425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups water
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 cups light cream
1/2 cup crushed peppermint candy
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine ingredients in ice cream freezer container; proceed according to manufacturer's instructions.  Garnish with additional crushed peppermint candy if desired.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Make your own peppermint holiday ice cream with this great recipe.
