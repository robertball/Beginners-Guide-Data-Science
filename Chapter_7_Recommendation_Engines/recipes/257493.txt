Title:
Blueberry Stevia Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4499410.jpg
Ingredients:
10 fresh organic blueberries, plus more for garnish
2 ounces fresh lemon juice
2 sprigs fresh thyme
2 packets Wholesome Organic Stevia, or more to taste
Sparkling water to taste
Lemon wheel for garnish

Directions:
Muddle blueberries with lemon juice in glass.
Stir in thyme sprigs and Wholesome Organic Stevia.
Top with sparkling water to taste.
Garnish with additional blueberries and lemon wheel.

Submitter: 
Wholesome
Description: 
A better-for-you spin on a classic recipe, sweetened with blueberries and organic stevia.
