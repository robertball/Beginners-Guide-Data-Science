Title:
Cowboy Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 Tbs mayonnaise
1 Tbs water
1/2 tsp ground cumin
4 Tbs hot sauce
1 Tbs freshly chopped parsley

Directions:
In a bowl whisk together the mayonnaise, water, cumin and hot sauce until well blended.
Add the eggs and whisk until the eggs become a uniform color.
Pour the eggs into the skillet and slowly fold the eggs onto themselves until cooked through but still moist.
Garnish with parsley and serve.
