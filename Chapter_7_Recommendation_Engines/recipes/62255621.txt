Title:
Lambrecht Family Kielbasa and Kraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 medium onion, diced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 teaspoon thyme leaves
1/4 teaspoon ground sage
Black pepper to taste
3 Yukon Gold potatoes
1 (16 ounce) bag sauerkraut
2lb beef kielbasa

Directions:
Put the cold butter in a cold pan with the diced onion, and slowly bring to a simmer on medium heat.
Simmer for about 10 minutes.
Add minced garlic and simmer 2 more minutes.
Add next sage, thyme and black pepper.
Combine with the sauerkraut (un-drained and not rinsed), kielbasa and potatoes.
Bring to a simmer on the stove top.
Cover loosely with foil and place in a preheated 225Â°F oven for 3 hours.
