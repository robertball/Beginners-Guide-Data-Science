Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 beaten egg

Directions:
Add flour,baking soda and salt in a bowl.Mix Well.In a separate bowl add butter,peanut butter,sugars,and egg. Mix till creamy.When creamy add the dry ingredients and mix well.Shape into 1 inch balls. Place 2 inches apart. Criss cross with fork.bake on an ungreased cookie sheet.Bake for 10 min at 375
