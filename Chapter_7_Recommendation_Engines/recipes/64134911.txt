Title:
Lemon Zucchini Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking powder
2 lg. eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1-1/3 cup sugar
2 tbsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/2 cup buttermilk
zest of 1 lg. lemon
1 cup grated zucchini
Glaze (optional)
3/4 cup icing sugar
2 tbsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
2 tsp.  milk

Directions:
Grease a 9" x 5" loaf pan
Preheat oven to 350
Mix flour, salt & baking powder.  Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl beat eggs.
Add oil, sugar, 2 tbsp. of lemon juice, buttermilk and lemon zest.  Beat until well mixed.
Fold in zucchini.  Add dry ingredients to wet in thirds, blending with a spoon to incorporate.  Do not over mix.
Pour into prepared loaf pan.
Bake 40 - 45 minutes or until a wood toothpick inserted into centre of loaf comes out clean.
Mix ingredients for glaze if using and set aside.
Cool loaf on wire rack for 15 minutes.  Turn out of pan and continue to cool on rack.  Drizzle glaze over loaf while it is still warm.
