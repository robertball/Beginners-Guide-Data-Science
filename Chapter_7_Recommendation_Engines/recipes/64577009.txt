Title:
Grilled Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head garlic, roasted
1 # ground dark turkey meat
3 T. mayo
4 tsp. finely chopped parsley
1 tsp lemon zest
1 tsp. Worcestershire
1/2 tsp. each salt and pepper
1/2 c. grated cheddar

Directions:
Preheat grill over medium high heat. While it's heating up, combine 1 T. roasted garlic paste, turkey, mayo, parsley, zest, worcestershire sauce, pepper and salt. Stir gently to blend the ingredients. Gently form into 3 or 4 patties. Form a 1/4 inch indentation in the center.
Lightly oil the grill and place the burgers directly over the heat. Cover and cook for about 7 minutes. Flip, top with cheese and continue to grill till no longer pink, about 7 minutes.
