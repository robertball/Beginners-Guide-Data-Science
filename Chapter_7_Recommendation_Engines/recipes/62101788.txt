Title:
Acorn Squash with Spinach and Ricotta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1.5-2lb. acorn squash
1 tbsp. walnut oil, may substitute your favorite.
1-2 cups cooked brown rice.
1.5-2 cups ricotta cheese
3 handfuls fresh baby spinach, washed/dried and chopped (may substitute 1/2 box of frozen-chopped)
3 tbsp. slivered almonds, toasted or roasted.
1/4 cup grated dried  parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground sage
1/2 tsp garlic powder, or to your taste. -Not garlic salt!!)
salt & pepper to taste
1/2-1/2 milk or cream

Directions:
Slice squash lengthwise n half.
Brush with oil of choice.
Sprinkle with pepper.
Place face down on baking sheet which has  @ 1/4 cp water on it so to preserve moisture.
Bake for @ 30 minutes @ 350, until flesh is easily speared 3/4 way with a fork.
Meanwhile: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, chopped spinach, parmesan, almonds, sage and nutmeg. Add 1/2 & 1/2 until the mixture is moist but still solid enough to hold shape.  GENTLY blend.  Add salt and pepper to your taste.
Score the almost cooked squash halfs with a knif on the diagonal and then again the other way.  Just enought to gather moisture and flavor while cooking.
Spoon in the stuffing, gently pressing into the squash, until @ 2 fingers worth are over the top . Sprinkle with extra parmesan and salt/pepper.
Bake @ 350 for 20 minutes, or until just toasty on top.

Description: 
Roasted Acorn Squash stiffed with a creamy ricotta,spinach, brown rice scented with sage and nutmeg.
