Title:
Christmas Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Pounded Chicken Breasts
1 container frozen spinach, thawed, and drained
1 chopped large tomato (or 1 can chopped tomatoes drained)
4 slices cooked and cooled bacon crumbled
1 cup Mozzerella crumbles
4 toothpicks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl mix together tomato, spinach, bacon, and cheese.
Layout pounded chicken breast, and scoop spoonfull of mixture onto center of each breast.
Roll over chicken and secure with toothpick.
Line up chicken rolls in the center of a shallow baking dish.
Spoon any remaining mixture over top.
Bake for 1 hr.
Serve with rice or your choice of sides.

Submitter: 
Tessa Miller-Schweder
Description: 
This chicken is SOO easy to make and very impressive. We call it Christmas Chicken because of the colors. But it is fabulous anytime!
