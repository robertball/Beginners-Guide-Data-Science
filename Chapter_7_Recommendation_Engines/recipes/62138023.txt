Title:
Platter Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 oz.) package of cream cheese
1 16 oz. jar picante sauce
green olives to taste
green pepper to taste
green onions to taste
2 16 oz. packages of shredded cheese

Directions:
Spread cream cheese on a large platter.  Layer picante sauce on top.  Sprinkle the chopped olives, pepper, and onions over the top.  Top off with shredded cheese.  Serve with chips.

Submitter: 
Jessy Sparks
Description: 
A chip dip that is great for parties!
