Title:
Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723360.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz mixed vegetables (your preference)
1/2 pound cubed uncooked chicken
1 tbsp. Paprika
1 Bay leaf
2 tsp. Parsley
2 tsp. Oregano
1 tsp. pepper
2 tsp. garlic
1 cup water
1 (10oz) can 98% fat free cream of chicken soup
2 cups  2% or fat-free milk
1 cup cooked medium egg noodles

Directions:
Cook cubed chicken over medium heat until white on outside. Add frozen vegetables and all seasonings including the bay leaf. Add 1 cup of water and stir until seasonings dissolve. Cook for 2 min or until simmering. Stir soup and milk into pan. Heat to a boil. Add cooked noodles. Reduce heat to low. Cook and stir for 5 min. or until vegetables are fully tender.
