Title:
Mom's Shrimp Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tomato soup
1 pkg cream cheese, 8 oz
1/2 cup green pepper, finely chopped
1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
2 envelopes gelatin
2/3 cup water
1 can tiny shrimp

Directions:
Heat together tomato soup and cream cheese. Mix until well blended.
Remove from heat. Add green pepper, celery, onion and mayonnaise.
Dissolve gelatin in water. Add to soup mixture along with shrimp. Mix well and chill. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
charlotte221
Description: 
Mom's classic party appetizer. Serve with Chicken in a Biscuit crackers! You don't have to love shrimp to love this dip!
