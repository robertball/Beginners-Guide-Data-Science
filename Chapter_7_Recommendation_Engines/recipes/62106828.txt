Title:
Gingery Garlicky Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Any type of a whole small fish
8 garlic cloves, diced
fresh ginger, 2 Tablespoons, sliced or minced
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 Tablespoon of sesame oil
5 sprigs of green onions, diced
2 tablespoons each of Light and regular soy sauce

Directions:
Add 2 cloves of garlic and ginger on the fish, skin side down, then steam the fish in a wok for ten minutes.
Add the remaining garlic in olive oil and place in a saucepan and saute until browned then place the garlic in a plate, leaving the olive oil in the pan.
Add sesame oil into the remaining olive oil as well as both the soy sauces and mix it up.
Add the diced green onions and return the garlic to the mixture, and salt and pepper to taste.
place this marinade on top of the fish and serve.
