Title:
Easy Vegetarian Corn Chowder 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702184.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup diced onion
1/2 cup diced celery
2 tablespoons shredded carrot
6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 (14.5 ounce) cans vegetable broth
2 (15 ounce) cans creamed corn
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 cup half-and-half cream
3/4 cup milk
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 pinch salt

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, melt butter.  Cook onions, celery and carrot in butter 3 minutes.  Whisk in flour and cook 6 minutes more, until light brown.  Whisk in broth and simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in creamed corn, corn, half-and-half, milk, nutmeg, pepper and salt.  Simmer over low heat 10 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Kim Carvalho
Description: 
Creamed corn, whole kernel corn, half-and-half and milk go into this quick corn chowder made with vegetable broth.Modified from: Easy Vegetarian Corn Chowder
