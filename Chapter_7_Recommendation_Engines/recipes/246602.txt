Title:
Cranberry-Brie Holiday Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3467879.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) round Brie cheese
1 (7.2 ounce) package Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
1 (8 ounce) can cranberry sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Place unwrapped Brie on parchment paper.
Bake until Brie is soft but not melted, 10 to 12 minutes. Cut into slices and place on top of Original Pretzel Crisps(R). Finish with cranberry sauce for a festive holiday app.

Submitter: 
Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
Description: 
Baked Brie is served on Original Pretzel Crisps® with cranberry sauce for a festive holiday appetizer.
