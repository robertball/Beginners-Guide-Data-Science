Title:
Deb's Mac N' Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2-5 c elbow macaroni (cooked for only 5 min)
1 - 16 oz Velveeta
1 - 8 oz cream cheese
1 3/4 c  whole milk
3 c (12 oz) shredded SHARP cheddar cheese
1 -12 oz can evaporated milk
2 eggs beaten
1/2 c butter

Directions:
Cook pasta in boiling and salted water for ONLY 5 min. Drain and set aside. In large dutch oven, add cut up Velveeta, cut up cream cheese on MED heat until cheese starts to melt (NOTE: Do not go any higher than MED heat or it will burn). Add milk and stir until cheeses start to melt about half way. Add 3 c sharp cheddar and evaporated milk. Stir well to combine. Beat eggs well, and while stirring, slowly pour eggs into the mixture. Continue to stir the cheese sauce and then add butter. Add macaroni, stir well to combine. Put into a lined crock pot on High for 30 min. then reduce to low or warm.
