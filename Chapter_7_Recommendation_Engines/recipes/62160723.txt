Title:
Quick and Easy Kimchi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads Napa Cabbage (approximately 2 lbs. each)
1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
2 green onions, cut into thin strips
1 medium asian pear (or apple), grated
1 tbsp crushed garlic
4 tbsp korean red chili pepper powder
4 tbsp korean red chili paste
4 tbsp sesame oil
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Cut napa cabbage into quarters and seperate without pulling leaves apart, rub salt and leave for at least 2 hours.  Drain all the liquid and pat dry.
Stir together green onions, grated pear, crushed garlic, red chili pepper powder, red chili paste, sesame oil, soy sauce and apple cider vinegar to form the sauce.
With gloved hands, rub chili sauce on and in between each leaf.  Roll the cabbage starting from the head towards the tip of the leaf.
Place in glass container with left-over sauce and carrot and leave at room temperature overnight.
Remove from sauce and cut into 2" pieces to serve as an appretizer/side dish, or as directed by recipe that calls for kimchi.

Submitter: 
moosie
Description: 
This kimchi can be made just one day ahead (rather than the usual 5 days to 2 weeks) and taste just as great!
