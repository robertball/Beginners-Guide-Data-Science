Title:
Sunshine Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 hard-boiled eggs
3 cups of diced cooked potatoes
1/2 c sliced celery
2 T chopped onion
1 T chopped green pepper
1/4 c mayonnaise
1-2 T prepared mustard
1 tsp salt
Dash pepper

Directions:
Combine ingredients, mix well.  Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
Egg-mayonnaise based potato salad with mustard.
