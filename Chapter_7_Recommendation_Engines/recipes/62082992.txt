Title:
Caroline's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093451.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts (deboned and skinned)
1 Can Mushroom Soup
1 Pkg. Dry Onion Soup Mix
1 Cup Dry White Wine

Directions:
Throw altogether in casserole dish.   Cook for three (3) hours at 325  degrees.  Start with lid on and remove for last 1/2 hour to brown chicken.  Great served with mashed potatoes and buttermilk tea biscuits.

Submitter: 
PTRICK
Description: 
Calling for only 4 ingredients of chicken, mushroom soup, white wine, and dry onion soup mix, the aroma & flavours produced from this dish are fantastic.  Your family will love it!
