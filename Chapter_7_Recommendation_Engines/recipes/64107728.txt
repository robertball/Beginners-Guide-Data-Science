Title:
Never Fail Strawberry Pie Crust II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose gluten-free flour
1/2 Teaspoon Xanthan gum
1 cups butter flavored shortening
1/2 tablespoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup ice water
1/2 egg
1/2 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
1 egg white

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450°
In a large bowl, mix flour, shortening, sugar and salt.
Add ice water, vinegar and egg. Beat to combine.
Put dough directly into glass pie plate
Generously brush dough with egg white
Prick the bottom and sides with a fork to prevent bubbling
Line the pie shell with a double thickness heavy-duty foil
Bake at 450° for 8 minutes
Remove Foil
bake 5-6 minutes or until dry and crisp
Refrigerate till cold before adding strawberries
