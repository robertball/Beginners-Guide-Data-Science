Title:
Choconut Caramel Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag milk chocolate morsels
2 T. shortening
14 oz. bag caramels
5 T. margarine
2 T. water
1 C. chopped nuts

Directions:
Using a double boiler, melt chocolate and shortening over hot (not boiling) water.  Remove from heat.  Pour 1/2 of mixture into 8" foil-lined square pan.  Refrigerate until firm. (15 minutes)  Return remaining chocolate mixture to low heat.  Using double boiler, combine caramels, butter and water over boiling water.  Stir until caramels melt.  Stir in nuts.  Pour into chocolate lined pan.  Refrigerate until tacky (15 minutes) Top with remaining melted chocolate.  Return to fridge at least 1 hour.
