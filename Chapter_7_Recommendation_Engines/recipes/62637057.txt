Title:
Hershey's "Perfectly Chocolate" Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1 3/4 c. flour
3/4 c. Hershey’s Cocoa
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
2 eggs
1 c. milk
1/2 c. vegetable oil
2 tsp. vanilla
1 c. boiling water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in order given.
Bake in 2 greased 9-inch round baking pans at 350F for 30-35 minutes.
