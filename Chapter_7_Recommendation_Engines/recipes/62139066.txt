Title:
Herbed Pot Roast with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 lb beef roast (your favorite cut)
6 red potatos
1 onion
3 stalks celery
1/2 lb thickly cut carrots.
3 tbsp fresh rosemary
3 tbsp fresh oregano
3 tbsp fresh basil
1 tbsp olive oil
3 cloves garlic
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Finely chop the herbs.
Chop Garlic finely keeping 1 clove seperate.
Slice new potatos into quarters.  Slice celerly into large peices.  Slice onion into wedges.  Place all in large bowl, and add carrots.  Add 1/2 herb combination and 1 clove of chopped garlic.  Add salt & pepper to taste.  Add Olive oil and mix to coat.
Place roast in pan.  Rub garlic on both sides.  Rub herbs on both sides.  Salt & pepper to taste.  Arrange vegetables around roast.  Cover with foil.
Bake 1 1/2-2 hours until roast is fork tender.  Goes will with cheesy bacon cabbage.
