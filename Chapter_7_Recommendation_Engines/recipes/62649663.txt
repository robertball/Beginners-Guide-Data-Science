Title:
Christmas Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/586260.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon
2 onions, chopped
2 cups fresh sliced mushrooms
1 tablespoon butter
4 cups frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
8 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1 pinch dried parsley
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place bacon in a large skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain and set aside. Add the mushrooms and onion to the skillet; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent and the mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
Grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish with the tablespoon of butter. Place potatoes in bottom of prepared dish.  Sprinkle with salt, garlic salt, and pepper. Top with  the onions and mushrooms.
In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs with the milk and parsley. Pour the beaten eggs over the casserole and top with grated cheese. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Crumble the cooked back over the top.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until set.

Description: 
Prepare this casserole, loaded with bacon, fresh mushrooms, hash browns, onions, and cheese on Christmas Eve. Pop it in the oven first thing on Christmas morning.Modified from: Christmas Brunch Casserole
