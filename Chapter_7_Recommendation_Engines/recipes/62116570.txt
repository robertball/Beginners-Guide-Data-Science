Title:
French Country Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fully cooked kielbasa, thinly sliced
1 can (16 oz) kidney beans, rinsed and frained
1 can (15 1/2 oz) great northern beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
3 medium carrots, thinly sliced
2 small onions, thinly sliced
1/2 cup beef broth
2 Tbs brown sugar
2 tsp minced garlic or 1 tsp garlic powder
1 1/2 tps dried thyme
salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl; transfer to an ungreased 3-qt. baking dish. Cover and bake at 375 for 60 to 70 minutes or until the carrots are tender.
