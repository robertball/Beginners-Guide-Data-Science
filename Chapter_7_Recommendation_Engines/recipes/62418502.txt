Title:
Chicken Junk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1069488.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen broccoli
2 cooked boneless chicken breast halves, cut up.
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
French's Fried Onion Rings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place broccoli in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with the chicken.
In a small bowl, blend the cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, mayonnaise,shredded cheese and lemon juice. Pour the mixture over the chicken. Top with Fried Onion Rings.
Bake 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.
