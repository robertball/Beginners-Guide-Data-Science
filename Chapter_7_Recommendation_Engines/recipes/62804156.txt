Title:
Copy Cat Domino's Pizza - Marinara Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666261.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz. crushed tomato (Hunts)
(OR 6 oz. can tomato paste + 6 oz. warm water)
2 TBSP extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp minced garlic (OR 1 tsp garlic powder)
2 teaspoons dried minced onions
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon basil
optional
1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper
(OR 1/8 teaspoon cayenne)

Directions:
Simmer garlic and onion in olive oil.
mix remaining ingredients together bring to a low boil, then simmer a few minutes until hot.
