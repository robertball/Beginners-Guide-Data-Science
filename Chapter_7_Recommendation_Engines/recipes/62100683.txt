Title:
Mom's Hush Puppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. self-rising cornmeal mix
1 egg, beaten
½ c. self-rising flour
½ c. grated cheddar cheese
1/3 c. chopped onion
1 hot pepper, chopped fine (optional)
½ tsp. garlic, minced or powder
Dash of salt
Dash of pepper
Enough buttermilk to make a stiff dough
Vegetable oil, for frying

Directions:
Add oil to skillet and heat on medium-high heat.
Beat egg in large bowl.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Using a spoon, drop batter by the spoonful into the hot oil.  Cook until golden brown, approximately 7-9 minutes.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
Mom's hush puppies are little bites of heaven!  These traditional puppies have bits of cheese and a hint of garlic that will have you begging for more!
