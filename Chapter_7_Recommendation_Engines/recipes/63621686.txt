Title:
Chocolate M & M cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cups of flour
¼ cup cocoa
½ tsp of baking powder
¼ tsp of baking soda
½ cup of softened butter
¼ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp of vanilla extract
1 cup of M and M’s

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350°and grease a cookie sheet.  In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa, baking powder and baking soda. Set the bowl aside. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter with the brown sugar and white sugar.  Add the eggs one at a time and then pour in the vanilla.  Sir in the M and M’s.  Scoop out dough and place on the greased cookie sheet. They should be about 1 inch in diameter.  Bake for 8 minutes.
