Title:
Hearty Oven Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds boneless beef chuck, (pot roast: arm, blade, eye)
4 cloves garlic, pressed
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups onions, coarsely chopped
2 cups green bell peppers, coarsely chopped
3 14 oz. cans diced tomatoes, undrained
2 8 oz. cans tomato sauces
1 15 oz. can kidney beans, pinto beans or black beans, drained and rinsed
8 tortillas, (optional)
1/4 cup sliced green onions, (optional)
1/4 cup sour cream, (optional)
1/4 cup sliced ripe olives, (optional)
1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded, (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Trim excess fat from meat.  Cut meat into 1/2 inch cubes. Place in Stoneware Baking Bowl.  Press garlic over meat. Sprinkle with seasonings; stir to coat meat.  Chop onions and bell pepper.  Add to baking bowl with tomatoes, tomato sauce and beans. Cover bowl with Deep Dish Baker. Bake 2-1/2 hours or until meat is tender.  Skim fat.
Serve with tortillas, shredded cheddar cheese, sliced olives, sliced green onions and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
This slow-cooked oven baked chili is a delicious hearty way to warm up the family on those cold winter nights
