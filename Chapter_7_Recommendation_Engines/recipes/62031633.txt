Title:
black bean chili with garlic parsley rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chili:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
3 onions chopped
1 28oz. can whole tomatoes in thick puree
2 jalepeno peppers seeded and chopped**
2 garlic cloves chopped
1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 cup water
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 14 1/2oz. cans black beans rinsed and drained
1 red pepper chopped
shredded cheddar cheese
Rice:
5 garic cloves chopped
1 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon Adobo seasoning
1 teaspoon olive oil
5 cups water
2cups uncooked long grain rice
***use gloves or make sure you wash hands after handling.

Directions:
Chili:
Heat oil saute onions 8-10min.Add garlic,jalepeno,cumin and chili powder stir for 2 min.Add water,sugar,salt and tomatoes break up tomatoes. Heat to a boil reduce heat simmer covered for 20 min. Add beans simmer 10 min. longer.
Meanwhile for the rice. Heat oil saute garlic for 2 min.Add water,parsley and adobo. Bring to a boil add rice. Reduce heat cover and simmer about 20-25 min until liquid is absorbed.
Take bell pepper microwave on high for  1 minute.
Serve chili over the rice and garnish with bell pepper and cheese
