Title:
Easy Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (29 ounce) can peaches in heavy syrup, drained & 1/2 cup syrup reserved
1/3 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons butter
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 cups all-purpose baking mix
2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/3 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a 9x13 inch baking dish mix together white sugar, 1/2 cup syrup and peaches.  Dot with butter.
In a separate bowl, whisk together brown sugar, baking mix and cinnamon.  Whisk in buttermilk until smooth.  Pour over peaches.
Bake in preheated oven 50 minutes, or until lightly browned and bubbly.  Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Kemberli Paes
Description: 
Canned peaches in heavy syrup are baked in a sweetened baking mix topping for an easy, comforting cobbler.Modified from: Easy Peachy Cobbler Bake
