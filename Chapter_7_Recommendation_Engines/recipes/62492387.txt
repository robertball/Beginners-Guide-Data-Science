Title:
Citrus Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4481926.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups pineapple juice
4 cups limeade
4 cups orange juice
4 cups orange pop
8 cups 7-Up
Fresh strawberry slices
Fresh orange slices

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a large punch bowl, adding 7-Up last.
Garnish with strawberry and orange slices.
