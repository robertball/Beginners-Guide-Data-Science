Title:
Minced Beef with Paprika, Mushrooms and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
450gr/1lb Beef Mince
2 sticks Celery - chopped
1 Onion - finely chopped
1 large clove Garlic - finely chopped
1tbsp Plain Flour
1½ tbsp ground sweet Paprika
50g/2oz Butter
1 Bay (Laurel) Leaf
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
100g/3 ½ oz fresh Mushrooms - sliced
300ml/10fl oz Chicken stock
250g/8oz long grain Rice - (Basmati is best)
1 tbsp Parsley - finely chopped (garnish)
150g/5oz carton Sour Cream

Directions:
Melt 25g (half) butter and heat with oil in large frying pan;
Add onion, garlic, celery and fry - low medium heat for 4 minutes - stirring occasionally;
Add mince & fry, stirring continually to break up and brown (no lumps) - about 8 minutes;
Add flour and Paprika to meat mixture, stirring constantly for 2 minutes more;
Add salt, pepper, bay leaf and stock;
Bring to boil, cover and simmer for approx 50 minutes
After some 30 minutes or so, in large pot, boil water with some salt and a large piece of butter to stop rice sticking, and cook rice;
Simmer until tender, 10 minutes or so, but not soft, drain in a colander and pour over with boiling water in colander to drain again;
In another pan, melt 25g (remains of butter) and fry the mushrooms for some 4 minutes - medium heat;
When meat is tender, remove bay leaf and add 3tbsp sour cream; stir together;
Dish onto large serving plate, cover with mushrooms and sprinkle with parsley;
Surround with rice and serve.
