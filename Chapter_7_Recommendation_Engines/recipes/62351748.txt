Title:
Non-fishy Fish Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp cooking oil
1 inch ginger root, minced
2 cloves garic, crushed and minced
2 green onions, chopped
2 lbs sole fillet, cut into large pieces
1/4 cup orange juice
1/4 cup port wine
1/4 red pepper, minced
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp thai basil

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan.  Stir-fry ginger, garlic, and onion for a minute.  Add in fish.  Fry until cooked on the outside.  Add liquids and stir to deglaze.  Simmer for 10 minutes.  Stir in rest of ingredients and cook another 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Victoria
Description: 
An Asian-European fusion stew, light but warming, perfect for a rainy spring day.
