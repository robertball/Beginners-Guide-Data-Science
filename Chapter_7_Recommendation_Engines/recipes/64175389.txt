Title:
Poppy Seed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
2 eggs
3/4 cup oil
1-1/2 cups sugar
1 cup milk
1 Tbsp poppy seeds
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp almond flavoring

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Mix all ingredients and beat for 2 minutes.
Pour into greased bundt pan and bake for one hour or until done.  Cool 5-8 minutes before removing from pan. Frost with lemon icing if desired.

Description: 
Almond flavoring makes this cake delicious!
