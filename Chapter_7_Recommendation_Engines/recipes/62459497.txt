Title:
Christine's Stuffed peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 bell peppers (halved with ribs and seeds removed)
1 sleeve saltine crackers
1 lb grated jack cheese
2 lbs hamburger
1/2 onion diced
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 small can evaporated milk
1/2 stick butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Par boil peppers 4-5 mins, removed from boiling set aside
brown hamburger,onions,garlic sat and pepper all together drain grease. To hamburger add can milk and butter mix together add crushed crackers mix add 1/2 the cheese mix together. stuff peppers with mixture and top with remaining cheese bake at 350 for 30-40 mins or until hot all the way though

Description: 
Different from your regular stuffed pepper this has no rice.
