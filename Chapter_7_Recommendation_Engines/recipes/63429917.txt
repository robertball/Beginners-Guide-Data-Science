Title:
Ron's Highly Addictive Taco Salad- WARNING! Did I say Addictive? Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2-3/4# of taco seasoned ground turkey, pork or beef.
1 Head of Lettuce
3 Tomatoes
1 Red Onion
1/2-3/4# Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 Can of Black, Pinto or Red Kidney Beans
1 Bag of Doritos- Nacho Cheese Flavor
1/2 Bottle of Thousand Island Dressing

Directions:
Prepare meat with favorite taco seasoning or use leftover meat from previous nights tacos
Chop lettuce, tomatoes and onion and place in large bowl with shredded cheese and toss well.
Add the Doritos starting with half to 3/4 of the bag and the meat (warm) and toss
Drizzle dressing over the top while tossing mixture in bowl. You may prefer more or less to taste.
Add remaining Doritos if desired and toss
Serve immediately!
