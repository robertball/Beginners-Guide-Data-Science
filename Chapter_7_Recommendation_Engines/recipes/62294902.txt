Title:
Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. yellow squash
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 onion
1 (8 oz) container of sour cream
1 stick of margarine
1 package Pepperidge Farm stuffing
Slivered sharp cheese

Directions:
Cut up squash and onion, then cook in margarine; drain. Mix all ingredients with 3/4 of stuffing mix. Sliver cheese on top of casserole, then add remaining 1/4 stuffing mix. Bake 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
