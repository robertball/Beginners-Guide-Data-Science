Title:
Wild Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup uncooked wild rice
2 cups water
1/2 cup chopped bacon
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
10 oz tin cream of mushroom soup
10 oz tin chopped mushrooms, drained
1/3 tin water

Directions:
Simmer wild rice in 2 cups salted water, approximately 1 hour. Rice is done when it is fully opened. Drain.
While rice is cooking, fry bacon, celery and onion until tender.
Place cooked rice and fried ingredients in casserole dish.
Add mushroom soup, and 1/3 tin water, and mushrooms.  Stir
Bake at 325 F for approximately 45 minutes (covered), stirring occasionally.
Remove casserole cover for last 15 minutes.
