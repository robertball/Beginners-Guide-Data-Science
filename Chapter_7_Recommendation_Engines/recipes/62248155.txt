Title:
Granpa's Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med. size cabbage
1 med. size carrot
1 med. size onion
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup salad dressing[Miracle Whip preferred]
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup buttermilk
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Shred cabbage and carrot
Finely chop onion
Place all other ingredients in large bowl.
Add cabbage, carrot and onion.
Toss to "mixed" consistency.
Chill before serving.
