Title:
Baked Chicken with Balsamic, Olives, and Herbs de Provence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade/Sauce
1/2 c olive oil
1/2 c balsamic
1/2 c white wine (Sauvignon Blanc)
3-4T herbs de Provence
4-5 garlic cloves chopped
1/4 c or more olive brine
Couple sprigs rosemary and thyme tucked under chicken skin and in marinade
2 whole chicken legs, bone in with skin
2 split chicken breasts, bone in with skin
1 1/2 c or more mixed Mediterranean olives (kalamata, etc)
4 or more shallots
1-2 cloves garlic
Little salt

Directions:
marinade chicken in sauce 2-24 hours
add olives, shallots, garlic little salt, fresh rosemary/thyme sprigs
bake 50-60 minutes 400 degrees
let sit 10 minutes
serve over arugula or other greens
