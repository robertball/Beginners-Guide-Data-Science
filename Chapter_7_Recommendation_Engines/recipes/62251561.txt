Title:
Apple Stuffed Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2 or 2 1/2 lb pork tenderloin
1 apple-cored and cubed
1 cup apple (or other fruit juices will work)
1 tsp butter
2 tsp maple syrup
1/4 cup brown sugar
Nutmeg
Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cut the tough white membrane off the tenderloin and peel off, discard.
Slice tenderloin lengthwise but do not cut all the way through, set aside in a glass baking dish.
In a skillet heat the butter on med and add the apple cubes.
Add 3/4 cup of the juice and continue to heat until apples are softened a bit.
Add maple syrup and brown sugar and stir to combine.
Add spices to taste.
Cook on med stirring frequently for about another 5 mins. If mix thickens too much add juice as needed.
Insert apple mixture into the tenderloin and close up with toothpicks.
*optional-Add more juice and brown sugar to the pan scrapings and pour this over the tenderloin.
Cook for 40-45 minutes, be careful not to overcook.

Submitter: 
CraZee A
Description: 
Simple to make, very forgiving of substitutions if you don't have an ingredient and always tender.  I didn't know what to do with a pork tenderloin but I always like fruit with pork, this is what I came up with using what I had on hand. A plus is two picky eaters loved it.
