Title:
Hot Seafood Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups medium shell pasta
2 1/2 cups cooked shellfish (lobster, crab, shrimp)
1 cup diced celery
3/4 cup coarsley chopped green pepper
3/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
1/4 cup toasted slivered almonds
1 1/2 cups shredded Parmesan or Swiss cheese
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 cup dry sherry
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2 tsp grated lemon peel
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp dry mustard
3/4 cup bread crumbs
2 Tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Cook pasta until tender. Rinse and drain well.
If using canned seafood, drain and rinse thoroughly in cold water.
In large bowl, combine pasta, seafood, celery, green pepper, onion, parsley, almonds and cheese.
In a mixing bowl, stire together mayonnaise, sherry, lemon juice, lemon peel, salt, pepper and mustard. Blend gently but thoroughly into the seafood mixture.
Pour seafood mixture into greased 8 cup casserole. Combine crumbs with melted butter and spread evenly over top. Bake in 350 degree oven for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
A hearty, warm seafood casserole that my Mom serves on cold fall & winter evenings - a nice change from chili!
