Title:
Geib Family Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5743600.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Lbs. Frozen Hash Browns, cubed, thawed
2 Cups Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
1 + ½ stick Butter, Salted
16 oz Sour Cream
.5 Cup Chopped Onion
1 – 10 oz Can Soy-Free Cream of Chicken Soup**
.25 C Milk
1 tsp Salt
2 Cups Crushed Corn Flakes

Directions:
Melt 1 stick of the butter in 13”x9” pan (You can do this in oven while it’s preheating)
Add thawed potatoes and toss in the melted butter.
In a separate bowl: mix sour cream, onion, soup, milk, cheese and salt together, then spread on top of potatoes.
Mix the remaining ½ stick of melted butter with corn flakes and spread over top of potatoes.
Bake uncovered in a 350* oven for 1 hour 10 minutes.
