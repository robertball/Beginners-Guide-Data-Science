Title:
Mom's Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 # leftover pork, cut into 1/2" cubes
1/2 # leftover beef, cut into 1/2" cubes
2 T oil
1 C celery, finely chopped
1 C sliced onions
1 C water
1 t salt
1/8 t pepper
#2 can of bean sprouts
4 T soy sauce
2 T cornstarch, dissolved in 1/4 C water
Mom also added canned Chinese vegetables or a 2 pack canned chop suey mix.

Directions:
Brown meats in oil, add celery, onions, water, salt and pepper. Cover and cook for 15 mins. Add bean sprouts and simmer for 3 mins. Add soy sauce. Thicken w/cornstarch mixture. Serve over rice or Chinese noodles. Can use addition soy sauce if desired.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
When I was a kid I had an aversion to anything cooked in 1 pot, but even I liked this and Chuck really loved this.
