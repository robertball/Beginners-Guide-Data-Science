Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef, browned and drained
28oz can crushed tomatoes
15.25 can corn, undrained
15oz can black beans, undrained
15.5 oz can red kidney or pinto beans, undrained
1 envelope ranch dressing mix
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 small onion chopped
Tortilla chips
Shredded cheese
Sour cream

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except last 3 in slow cooker.
Cover  and cook on low 4-6 hours.
Garnish with chips, cheese, and sour cream when serving.
