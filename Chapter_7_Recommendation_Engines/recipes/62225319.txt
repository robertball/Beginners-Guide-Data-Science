Title:
Peanut butter Kisses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup margarine
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup Light brown sugar
1 egg
2 cups all propurse flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon clear vanilla extract
1 bag foil-wrapped milk chocolate pieces, unwrapped

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degres F.
Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy (about 2-3 min)
Beat in the peanut butter
Add egg and vanilla extract and beat combine.
In a separet bowl, whisk together the flour and the baking soda.
And peanut butter mixture and beat until combine together.
Put dough on a plastic wrap; wrap the dough tight and secure.
Place on refrigerator for about 45 min allow to chill.
Roll about 1/4 in thickness ball and place it on a parchment paper about 2 in apart.
Using a fork flat them out, doing a criss-cross pattern.
Bake cookiess about 8-10 min or until cookies are golden brown.
Top each cookie immediately with an unwrapped chocolate piece, pressing down firmly so that cookie cracks around edge.
Remove from cookie sheets to cool about 5 -10 min
