Title:
Buffalo Chicken Sausage Tortellini Salad Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz package al fresco Buffalo Style Fully Cooked Chicken Sausage (2 links)
10 oz Cheese filled tortellini
2 Stalks of celery, diced.
1/2 cup Red bell peppers, chopped
1/2 cup Black olives, halved
1/2 Medium cucumber, diced
1/3 cup Low-fat creamy blue cheese dressing2 Tbs Crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
Place a medium sauce pan filled 1/2 way with water over high heat, bring to a boil and cook the tortellini according to package instructions. Drain and chill with cold water immediately.
Cut the al fresco Buffalo Style Fully Cooked Chicken Sausage into 1/4"" thick slices.
In a medium skillet prepared with non-stick pan spray, quickly saute the sausage slices over medium-high heat for 2 -3 minutes. Chill.
Meanwhile, in a medium mixing bowl, combine the chilled tortellini with the celery, red pepper, black olives, cucumber, salad dressing and 1 tbsp of the blue cheese crumbles.
When the sausage slices are chilled, toss in with the pasta mixture. Transfer to a serving bowl and top with the remaining blue cheese crumbles.
