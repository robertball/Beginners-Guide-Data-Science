Title:
Super Bowl Sunday Chili Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093561.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Jar of your favorite salsa
1 lb of ground turkey or beef
1 can of black beans
1 8oz bag of shredded cheese
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
In the crock-pot add black beans and salsa on low to stew.
Brown the meat in a large skillet.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add cooked meat to crock-pot and stir.
Add shredded cheese on top and turn to high for 1 hour.
Reduce to low or warm depending on when you will serve.
You can alter this recipe by adding virtually anything you enjoy, i.e.: jalapenos, corn, pinto beans, etc.
For vegans, eliminate the meat and your set!
