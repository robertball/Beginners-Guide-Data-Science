Title:
Lemon Drop Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 oz. Absolut vodka
3/4 oz. lemon juice
1 lemon
Sugar to taste

Directions:
Combine vodka and lemon juice in a shot glass.  Sprinkle sugar on top.  Slice lemon into wedges and dip in sugar.  Suck sugared lemon after taking shot.

Submitter: 
KATESWIFT
Description: 
A yummy alcoholic shot perfect for parties...tastes just like the candy!
