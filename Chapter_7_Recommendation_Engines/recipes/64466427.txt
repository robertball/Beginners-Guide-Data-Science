Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2441506.jpg
Ingredients:
Chocolate wafers
Marshmallows

Directions:
1. Melt the chocolate wafers in either a double boiler or the microwave
2. Using a fork or skewer, or your fingers if you're a heathen, dip the marshmallows in the chocolate
3. Let chocolate harden
4. Eat the Fluffy Bites while making dinosaur noises (VERY IMPORTANT)
5. Go do a random act of kindness
