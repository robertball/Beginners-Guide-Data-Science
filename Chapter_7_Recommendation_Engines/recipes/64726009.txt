Title:
Rose Water White Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6100023.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp rose water
1.5 cups water
1 tsp crystallized white sugar rocks
1 food grade rose petal (optional)

Directions:
Divide the rose water between two tea cups.
Add equal amounts of water between the two cups. Add 1/2 the rock sugar and half the rose petal to each cup. Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Lebanese white coffee, which isn’t coffee at all. It is hot water, rose water, and a touch of crystallized sugar. This is a beautiful after meal drink for anyone who doesn’t like coffee or for who may have over-indulged. Try this over ice for a refreshing drink on a hot day.
