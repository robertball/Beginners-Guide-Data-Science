Title:
Baileys Mint Chocolate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975693.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Mint Chocolate Irish Cream
1/2 ounce Smirnoff® Vanilla Vodka
Fresh mint
Chocolate stick

Directions:
Pour Baileys(R) Mint Chocolate Irish Cream and Smirnoff(R) Vanilla Vodka into a cocktail shaker with ice; shake cocktail and strain into a glass.
Garnish with fresh mint and chocolate stick to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Adding a touch of richness to any drink, Baileys® Irish cream liqueur mixes fine Irish whiskey, fresh dairy cream and a hint of chocolate flavors.
