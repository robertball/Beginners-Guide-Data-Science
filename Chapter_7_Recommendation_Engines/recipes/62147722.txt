Title:
Light And Spicy Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz rotini
1 cup mild shredded cheddar cheese
1 jalapeno, diced with seeds
1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1 tsp dry mustard
10 low-sodium Ritz crackers
ground pepper, to taste

Directions:
Prepare rotini according to directions on box.
Drain pasta and return to pot.
Stir in cheese until well blended.
Add peppers, mustard, Worcestershire and pepper and stir until all ingredients are mixed.
Crumble Ritz crackers on top of the macaroni.

Submitter: 
Cara
Description: 
Not-too-cheesy mac and cheese, created on a cold winter night.
