Title:
Chipotle Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T Olive Oil
1 Onion, chopped
4 Cloves garlic, chopped
1 16oz. can of black beans
1 16oz. can of kidney beans
1 lb Hamburg
1 Red pepper, chopped
1/2C frozen corn
1 28oz. can of Tomato Sauce
2 Chipotle Peppers in adobo sauce, chopped
2t Adobo Sauce
1T Chili Powder (or to taste)
Ground Black Pepper, to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees.
Heat 1T of olive oil in chili pot. Add onions and garlic and cook for about 10 minutes, or until garlic and onion browns.
Meanwhile, place chopped red pepper in baking dish and drizzle 1T of olive oil over it. Cook in oven for 10 minutes.
Add beef to onions and garlic and cook through.
Add chili powder and mix well. Cook for 10-15 minutes on medium heat.
Add tomato sauce to meat mixture and stir thoroughly. Turn heat down to medium low and cook for 5 minutes.
Add corn and red peppers. Sprinkle with ground pepper. Cook for 30 minutes. The longer it cooks, the better it tastes!
