Title:
Zucchini Noodles Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large zucchini
2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 tablespoons oyster sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons Asian chile pepper sauce, divided
1/4 cup chicken stock
2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
8 ounces shrimp peeled and deveined
12 ounces skinless boneless chicken breasts cut into 1" cubes
2 eggs beaten
3 cups bean sprouts
6 green onions chopped into 1" pieces
2 tablespoons chopped unsalted dry roasted peanuts
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1 ounce tamarind paste

Directions:
1.  Use a spiralizer to make the zucchini noodles and put aside.
2. In a small bowl combine soy sauce, worcestershire, lime juice, oyster sauce, sugar, chile sauce, chicken stock and tamarind paste. Set aside.
3. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat and add sesame oil. Stir in garlic when oil is hot for about 10 seconds. Add shrimp and chicken, cook about 5-7 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
4. Make a hole in center of wok and poor the eggs in the center and scramble them until firm. Add the noodles to the wok and the sauce. Stir and add water if needed for about 3 minutes. Add in 2 cups bean sprouts and green onions. Remove from the heat and garnish with chopped peanuts. Adjust any seasoning if needed.
5. Serve garnished with fresh basil and remaining bean spouts and extra lime wedges.
