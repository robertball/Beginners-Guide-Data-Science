Title:
Old Fashion Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C. diced apples (rather coarse diced)
2 Eggs
1/2 C corn oil
2 C. sugar
1 C. chopped nuts (coars chopped)
1 tsp. vanilla
2 C. flour
3/4 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Put the diced apples in a large mixing bowl and break the eggs over the apples. Add corn oil, sugar nuts and vanilla.
Mix well with wooden spoon.
Combine the dry ingredients and add to the mixing bowl and blend well.  Pour into a greased 9x12 baking pan.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.
Cool and serve with powdered sugar.
To make a little special: drizzle with melted butterscotch pieces and dust with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Tried and true! An old fashion cake recipe that has been handed down for many generations. I know you will pass this one on to family and friends.
