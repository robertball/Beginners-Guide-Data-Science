Title:
Holly's pasta fruit salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. pasta of your choice
1 large can of crushed pineapple
1 large can mandarin oranges
2 eggs, beatened
3/4 cups sugar
3 TB flour
8 oz. whipped topping

Directions:
Cook pasta.  Rinse with cold water and drain.  Drain fruit, reserving juice.  In medium sauce pan, cook fruit juice, beatened eggs, sugar and flour, stirring constantly until thick.  Cool well.  Mix pasta, fruit and sauce.  Refrigerate overnight, if possible.  Right before serving, add the whipped topping.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Although this has pasta in it, the pineapple and mandarin oranges makes this a refreshing summer fruit salad.
