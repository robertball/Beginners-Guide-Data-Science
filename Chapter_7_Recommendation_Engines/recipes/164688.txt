Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Savory Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985483.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
2 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
3/4 cup 2% milk
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 (10 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup red wine

Directions:
In large bowl, beat egg slightly. Stir in cereal, milk, onion, Worcestershire, salt, mustard and pepper. Let stand about 5 minutes, until cereal softens. Beat well.
Add ground beef; mix well. Shape into loaf. Place in shallow foil-lined baking pan coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 180 degrees C (350 degrees F) about 45 minutes or until cooked in centre. Serve with Mushroom Sauce.
Mushroom Sauce: In medium saucepan over low heat (or in microwave), heat mushroom soup and wine, stirring frequently, until mixture boils. Spoon sauce over sliced meatloaf.

Submitter:

Description: 
Here's a classic meatloaf--simple and satisfying--topped with a creamy mushroom sauce with wine.
