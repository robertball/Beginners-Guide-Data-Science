Title:
Bonnie's Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
8 ounces macaroni
1/2 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 sm can diced chilis
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes or medium salsa
1 can kernal corn, drained
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
2 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
2 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese
2 cups crushed tortilla chips
1/4 cup chopped green onions
lg can sliced olives
1/2 cup sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente.  Drain.
In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef, green pepper and chopped onion over medium heat until brown.  Mix in tomato soup, diced tomatoes, diced chilies, corn, and taco seasoning mix.   Stir in pasta.
Spoon beef mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle crumbled taco chips, olives and grated cheese on top.
Bake for about 30  minutes, until the cheese is melted.  Serve with chopped green onions and sour cream, if desired.
Add additional corn chips on top before serving.
