Title:
Creamy Chicken with Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breats
1/4 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1 T garlic powder
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cheddar cheese soup
1 10.5 oz. can Ro-Tel tomatoes
1 8 oz. bag egg noodles

Directions:
Place chicken in crockpot and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
Pour soups & tomatoes over all & cook on low for 8 hrs.
Add egg noodles pushing them down into sauce to cover.
Replace lid and cook for another 30 miutes on high.
