Title:
Best Ever Beef Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1083893.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1 clove garlic, minced
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk vegetable oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and garlic. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Shelli Brawner Schlafhauser
Description: 
This is the best beef marinade I have ever used. Everyone wants the recipe. My daughter always requests this.
