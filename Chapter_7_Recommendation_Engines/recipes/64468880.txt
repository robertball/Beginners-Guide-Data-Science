Title:
Heather's Dinomites Fluffy Bites - Pet Treat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2456604.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups whole wheat flour, or baby barley/oatmeal cereal
2 - 4 ounce containers your choice of meat and vegetable or combination of baby food
1/4-1/2 cup beef broth (as needed)
1 ½ Tbsp. minced kale

Directions:
Baby Dino Treats Directions:
Pour all ingredients except broth into a small bowl, and mix until thoroughly combined. Add broth a bit at a time, stirring until consistency is slightly sticky and holds together well when molded in your hand.
Roll into small ping-pong sized balls, place on a parchment lined or greased cookie sheet, and flatten with your thumb.
Bake in an oven preheated to 350° for 15-20 minutes until browned only slightly.
Cool and store in an airtight container in refrigerator or place in baggie to freeze
