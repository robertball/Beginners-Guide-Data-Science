Title:
Easy Refrigerator Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993097.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups thinly sliced cucumber
2 cups thinly sliced onions
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions:
Place cucumbers and onions in a large bowl; set aside. Combine remaining ingredients in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Cook and stir just until the sugar is dissolved. Pour over cucumber mixture; cool.  Cover tightly and refrigerate for at least 24 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Catherine  Seibold
Description: 
This is a great way to use cucumbers and onions from the garden. Here in Upstate New York, we have an abundance of cucumbers.
