Title:
Christmas Cranberry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
3/4 cup butter - softened
2 cups flour
10-12 oz. fresh cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Beat eggs and sugar in a large bowl until thickened and light in color - about 5-7 minutes.
Add butter and vanilla and beat for 2 more minutes.
Stir in flour until just combined then fold in cranberries.
Spread dough in a greased 13x9 pan.
Bake for 35-45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean in the center.
Dust with powdered sugar before serving.
