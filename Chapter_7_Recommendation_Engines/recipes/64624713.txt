Title:
Bourbon Cider Chicken Thighs with Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4462661.jpg
Ingredients:
2lbs chicken thighs bone in with skins
1lb baby red potatoes
1 cup apple cider juice
½ cup ketchup
¼ cup bourbon
1 Tbsp cornstarch
Salt
Pepper
Olive oil

Directions:
1. Using a paper towel pat dry chicken thighs and season with salt and pepper, then place on grill pan skin side up.
2. Wash the baby red potatoes and dry. Cut larger potatoes into halves, keeping them all similar in size. Place red potatoes in a medium sized bowl and drizzle with oil olive. Season with salt and pepper, then place in grill pan.
3. Place grill pan in oven and set for Auto Cook 1 for 2lbs chicken.
4. Combine ketchup and bourbon in a small saucepan on medium high heat.
5. In a small bowl whisk together apple cider and 1 Tablespoon cornstarch. Pour apple cider mixture into sauce pan and bring to a boil.
6. Allow sauce to boil for 10 minutes, whisking occasionally until thickened. Remove pot from heat, add 1/8 tsp. of pepper to sauce and allow to cool.
7. Once chicken is completed, remove from oven. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Serve chicken and potatoes with sauce.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Use Auto Cook on your Panasonic Countertop Induction Oven for your chicken and potatoes while making a simple, but flavorful sauce on the stove. Dinner will be ready in less than 30 minutes!
