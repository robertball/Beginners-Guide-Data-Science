Title:
Corn Bread Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14.75 ounce) can cream-style corn
2 eggs
1 (8 ounce) package dry corn bread mix
1 cup sour cream
2C Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a large mixing bowl, combine corn, eggs, corn bread mix, 1C cheese and sour cream; stir until blended. Pour batter into prepared pan; sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, until golden brown.
