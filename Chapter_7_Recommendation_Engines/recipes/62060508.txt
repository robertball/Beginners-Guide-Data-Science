Title:
Pineapple Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
2 T. pineapple juice
7 oz. jar marshmallow cream

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until smooth.  Serve with all kinds of fruit.  Our favorites are: bananas, apples, oranges, and grapes.

Submitter: 
CAROL46
Description: 
This light, sweet dip is a real hit with kids and adults.  The fruit platter is the first to disappear.
