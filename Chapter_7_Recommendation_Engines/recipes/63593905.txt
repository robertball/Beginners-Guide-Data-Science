Title:
Bourban Fig Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/999648.jpg
Ingredients:
7oz Dried Mission Figs, diced
1/4 cup White Sugar
1/4 cup Bourbon Whiskey
1/4 cup Balsamic Vinegar
1 Tbs Lemon Juice
Pinch Salt

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan.
2. Bring to a simmer and cook about 30 minutes. Liquid should reduce almost by half.
3. Let cool and serve with crackers, a nice Brie or Blue Cheese, and walnuts.

Submitter: 
James Ryan Schubert
Description: 
A sweet and tangy compote that it excellently paired with a Blue Cheese or Brie.
