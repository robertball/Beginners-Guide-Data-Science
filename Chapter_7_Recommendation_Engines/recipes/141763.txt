Title:
Italian Sausage and Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978703.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (19 ounce) package Bob Evans® Italian Sausage
4 ounces button mushrooms, diced
3 cups cubed stuffing
1 cup chicken broth or water
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  In nonstick skillet over medium heat, cook sausage and mushrooms until sausage is browned and mushrooms are tender.  Set aside. In 9-inch square baking dish, combine stuffing and broth.  Place sausage and mushrooms on top. Sprinkle with cheese. Cover and bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until sausage is fully cooked.

Submitter: 
Bob Evans®
Description: 
Browned sausage and mushrooms are baked over stuffing and topped with shredded cheese. A delicious main dish in under 45 minutes!
