Title:
Chocolate Pudding Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-package Keebler mini piecrusts
1 box instant chocolate pudding
Whipped cream, cubed fruit, & nuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix your pudding according to box. Spoon your pudding into piecrust. You should fill it almost all the way up. Next add your whipped cream, fruit, and nuts. Refrigerate. Eat cold with a spoon.
