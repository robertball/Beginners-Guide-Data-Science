Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs (beaten)
1 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup banana
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In medium bowl, cream butter and sugar.
Add eggs, and mix well.
Add flour, baking soda, salt. Mix well until dry ingredients are incorporated.
Blend in bananas, sour cream, vanilla extract.
Put in prepared (sprayed pan) or lightly greased loaf pan.
Bake at 350 F. for 50 minutes or until brown and knife comes out clean.
