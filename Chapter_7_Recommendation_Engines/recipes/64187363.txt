Title:
Walnut-Coconut Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1521659.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Mott's Natural Applesauce
1 cup sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped walnuts
confectioners' sugar

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the oil, sugars, eggs and vanilla; mix well. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon; add to the egg mixture alternately with buttermilk. Stir just until moistened. Stir in coconut and walnuts just until combined.
Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 45-55 minutes or until toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. Dust with confectioners' sugar if desired.
