Title:
Mini Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Coating:
1/4 cup plus 1 tbl cocoa powder
4 tbl maple syrup
3 tbl coconut oil
Filling:
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup natural peanut butter
1/4 cup icing sugar
1 tbl coconut oil
pinch of salt

Directions:
Sift cocoa in to a small bowl, mix all coating ingredients until well combined. If any of the ingredients have been refrigerated, put the mixture in the microwave for 15 seconds to loosen up, and then mix again.
Line the bottoms of 18 mini muffin cups with 1/2 the chocolate.
Put mini muffin cups in the freezer. Set remaining chocolate coating aside.
Meanwhile, sift icing sugar into a small, separate bowl, beat all filling ingredients by hand.
Take mini muffin cups out of the freezer and top with the filling.
Top with the remaining chocolate, spreading with a small spoon to the edges of the muffin cups.
Freeze until ready to serve.
