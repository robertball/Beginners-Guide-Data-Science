Title:
Mashed Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1 cup milk or cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
Fresh ground black pepper and salt to taste
4 tablespoons cream cheese
1 head roasted garlic
1 tsp olive oil

Directions:
Put 2 inches of water in a large stock pot.  Bring to a boil.  Add the cauliflower, cover, and steam until fork tender (about 30 minutes).
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Add milk, 1/4 cup cheese, salt and pepper, cream cheese, garlic, and cauliflower to a food processor.  Process until smooth.
Put mashed cauliflower in a baking dish.  Top with olive oil, remaining cheese, and salt and pepper.  Bake, uncovered, until top is starting to brown (about 20 minutes).

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
An tasty alternative to boring mashed potatoes.
