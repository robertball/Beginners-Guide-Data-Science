Title:
M.D.K's Famous Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/623471.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1 pinch salt and pepper

Directions:
In a medium size jar with a tight fitting lid, combine sugar, vinegar and salt and pepper.  Cover tightly and shake for approximately thirty seconds.  If necessary, add a few drops of water to thin out dressing.  Pour on your favorite salad.
