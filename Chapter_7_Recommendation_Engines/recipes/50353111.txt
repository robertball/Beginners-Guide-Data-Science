Title:
Italian Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
3 cups crushed tortilla chips
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (10 ounce) package mixed salad greens
1 (8 ounce) bottle zesty Italian dressing

Directions:
In a medium skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until evenly browned. Remove from heat, and drain.
In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, tortilla chips, mozzarella cheese, Cheddar cheese, and salad greens. Toss with Italian dressing until evenly coated, and serve.

Submitter: 
Janis Neill
