Title:
Quick & Easy Oatmeal Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pkg. Yellow Moist cake mix (18.25 oz)
3 eggs
1/3 cup canola oil
1 cup whole milk or buttermilk
3 tbsp. molasses
1/3 cup old fashioned oatmeal
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. gr. gloves
1/4 tsp. gr. nutmeg
Powdered sugar (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
Grease and flour 12cup Bundt pan.
Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
Beat on low speed until just moistened.
Beat on high speed for 2 minutes.
Pour mixture into Bundt pan.
Bake for 35 - 45 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
Cool in pan for 30 minutes.
Turn cake onto serving dish and cool completely.
Dust with powdered sugar if desired.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
This is a super moist cake that is made with a box mix and with just the right amount of spices added to it.
