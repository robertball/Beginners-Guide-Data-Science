Title:
Orange Jello Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box of white cake mix, prepared in 13x9" dish
1 large box orange jello
1 small box orange jello
1 small box instant vanilla pudding
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tub of cool whip topping (8 oz.)

Directions:
Bake cake as directed, cool.  poke several holes in cake using end of wooden spoon. Make up large jello box as directed and cool a little, pour over cake and put in fridge to set up.
Using a hand mixer, beat cream cheese until creamy. Add dry vanilla pudding mix, small box of dry orange jello mix, vanilla and milk. When well blended, fold in cool whip.
Top cooled jello cake with the frosting and keep chilled.
