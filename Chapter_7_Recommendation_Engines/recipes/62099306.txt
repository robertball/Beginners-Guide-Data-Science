Title:
aloha shrimp skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 large shrimp, peeled and deveined
8 bamboo skewers
2 cans of dole pineapple chunks
2 green peppers, cut into pieces
1 bunch cilantro, chopped
2 limes, juiced
1 cup of teriyaki sauce
1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
3 tablespoons shredded coconut
1/2 cup of oil
1 clove of garlic, chopped
1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes

Directions:
soak skewers in water for 2 hours. combine oil, teriyaki sauce, garlic, chili flakes, lime juice, cilantro, and shrimp in a bowl and marinate for 1 hour. alternate shrimp, pineapple and pepper on each skewer with 4 shrimp on each. grill for 4 minutes on each side, basting with hoisin sauce. serve with coconut sprinkled on top

Submitter: 
merri rosenthal
Description: 
this recipe is so tasty. wonderful hawaiian flavors combines to make a great meal. i serve skewers with rice and scallions.
