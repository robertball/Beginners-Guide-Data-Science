Title:
Michele's Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Ketchup
1/3 cup Dijon Mustard
1 TBL Worchestershire Sauce
1/4 cup Cider Vinegar
1 tsp Liquid Smoke
2 TBL McCormick's Mesquite Grill Seasoning
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
Tabasco sauce to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a medium bowl and stir.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
I created this tangy Texas style barbecue sauce when I was out of the bottled barbeque sauce.  The cupboard was empty, but I at least had these ingredients on hand.  Now my husband does want store-bought barbeque sauce anymore.  This is his favorite.
