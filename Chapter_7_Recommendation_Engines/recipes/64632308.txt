Title:
Super Peppermint white hot chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 bags crushed hard peppermint candies
3-5 containers peppermint extract
1 quart milk
6 bags white chocolate chips
Whipped cream { Optional }

Directions:
1. Take out 18 mugs or cups.
2. Fill up the mugs or cups.
3. Heat each of the cups or mugs of milk in microwave for 1 minute.
4. Add 1/4 cup of white chocolate chips to milk.
5. Mix white chocolate and milk until white chocolate is liquefied.
6. Add 3 tablespoons of crushed peppermint to the drink and stir well.
7. Add 1 1/3 tablespoons of Peppermint extract into each of the mugs.
8. Add whipped cream on top and cover the whipped cream with crushed peppermint candies.
