Title:
Take Out Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Kraft Asian Sesame Dressing
Zest of one orange
1/2 cup fresh orange juice
2 Tbsp Honey
2 tsp minced ginger root
1/8 tsp crushed chilis
1 tbsp sesame oil
450 gm skinless chicken breast, cubed
1 orange pepper (1 inch pieces)
1 red pepper (1 inch pieces)
1 yellow pepper (1 inch pieces)
2 green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:
Combine dressing, orange zest, juice, honey, ginger root and crushed red peppers.
Heat oil in wok on medium high heat.
Add chicken, peppers; cook for 5 to 6 minutes until chicken and peppers are done.
Remove chicken mixture from wok; cover to keep warm.
Add dressing mixture to wok and stir 3 to 4 minutes or until thickened.
Stir in chicken mixture.
Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until heated through.
Top with onions.
