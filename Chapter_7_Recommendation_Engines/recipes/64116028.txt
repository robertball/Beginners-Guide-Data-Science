Title:
Heavenly  Blueberry pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1285818.jpg
Ingredients:
Dry ingredients
3/4 cup of white flour
3/4 cup nutrablend flour or whole wheat
1 Tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
Wet ingredients
1/2 banana - mashed
1egg
1cup and 2 tablespoon of milk.
1 to 11/2 fresh blueberries.

Directions:
Preheat griddle to 350 dregrees and lightly oil.
Sift or stir dry ingredients in a bowl.
In a seperate bowl add and stir together wet ingredients.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until just blended.
Blue berries can be added to the batter at this time with a quick stir or added by hand on top of pancake when on the grill.
Drop 1/4 cup batter onto hot oiled griddle.
Cook until golden brown 4-5 minutes per side.  Butter and top with berry  syrup.

Submitter: 
Jeanna Carol Duke
Description: 
These are the most fluffy thick pancakes. This is the only recipe that I have used in years.  They do take abit of time to cook so be patience and you will be rewarded.  Use a berry syrup to enchance the blueberry flavour even more.
