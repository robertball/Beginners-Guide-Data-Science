Title:
Cold Smoked Chicken to Serve Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole cold smoked chicken
cooking oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a cast iron pan, heat cooking oil. Add whole cold smoked chicken and brown on all sides.
Place whole chicken, still in cast iron pan, into the oven and roast until done. About 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
ahoffort
Description: 
This not a recipe on how to cold smoke chicken, but how to prepare a cold smoked chicken for serving. This recipe is simple and basic.
