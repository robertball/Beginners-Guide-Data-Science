Title:
Ann's Calico Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef, browned with onion
1 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled
3 cans pork and beans
1 can small butter beans
2 tbsp vinegar
2 tbsp mustard
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Mix together ingredients in crockpot. Stir occasionally. Ready to serve when mixture is hot.

Submitter: 
Kristin McNeil
Description: 
Yummy, meaty beans to bring to those summer potlucks!
