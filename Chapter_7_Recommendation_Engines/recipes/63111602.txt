Title:
Jordan Pond Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C milk
4 lge. eggs
2 T vegetable oil
1 C all purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Wisk together milk, eggs oil.  Add flour to mixture, stirring until fully combined and smooth. Cover with plastic wrap. Let batter set 1-2 hrs (overnight is best).
Spoon batter into popover tins 1/2 full. Bake on middle rack of oven 16-20 min until evenly browned (do not open oven during baking cycle). Take out of oven, poke each a couple of times with toothpick. Place back in oven with door closed and heat off for about 5 min.
