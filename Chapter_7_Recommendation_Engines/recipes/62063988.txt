Title:
Sherry's Wild West Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups low-fat chicken broth
2 small cans chopped green chilies
2 cups cooked chicken or turkey, shredded
1 14 oz can garbanzo beans, drained
1 t. oregano
1 - 2 chipotle chile peppers, finely chopped
1 cup cooked white or brown rice, your preference
salt & pepper, to taste
1 avocado, sliced
1 cup Jack cheese, small cubes

Directions:
Heat the broth to simmer and add all of the ingredients.
Simmer for 30 minutes.
If soup is too thick for your liking, add a bit more broth.
Ladle into soup bowls and decorate each with avocado slices and cheese cubes.
Serve immediately.

Description: 
My mother-in-law, Sherry, comes up with the best soup recipes.  She is the queen of soup!  One of our family favorites is this hearty, rich and delicious soup.  A nice green salad on the side rounds out a perfect meal.
