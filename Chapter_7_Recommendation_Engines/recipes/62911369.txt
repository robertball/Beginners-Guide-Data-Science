Title:
Decadent Chocolate Fudge Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/771235.jpg
Ingredients:
Ganache:
12 ounces semi sweet chocolate chips
3/4 cup whipping cream
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
Frosting:
2 cups butter, softened
3 pounds powdered sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup dutch cocoa (Droste)
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons milk or chocolate non dairy coffee creamer
5 ounces whipping cream
1 tablespoon pure vanilla

Directions:
In a sauce pan add chocolate chips and the cream. Cook on low heat and stir until melted. Remove from heat and add the butter and vanilla. Stir until smooth. Let cool to room temp. In a large bowl cream together the butter and shortening. Add the remaining ingredients and the cooled ganache and whip until fluffy.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
This is a wonderful chocolate butter cream for decorating cakes and cupcakes. This is a crusting butter cream so it can be smoothed using the paper towel method. This recipe does take a little extra time but it is well worth the effort.
