Title:
Turkey Vegetable Chili Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 pound ground turkey breast
1 can (15oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (14-1/2oz.) Mexican-style stewed tomatoes, undrained
1 can (14-1/2oz.) no salt added diced tomatoes, undrained
1 cup frozen corn
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 gloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. Mexican seasoning
1/2 cup uncooked elbow macaroni
1/3 cup sour cream

Directions:
Spray large skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Add turkey; cook until browned. Combine cooked turkey, beans, tomatoes, corn, onion, garlic and seasoning in slow cooker. Cover and cook on LOW 4-5 hours. Stir in macaroni. Cover and cook 10 minutes; stir. Cover and cook 20-30 minutesor until pasta is tender. Serve with sour cream.
