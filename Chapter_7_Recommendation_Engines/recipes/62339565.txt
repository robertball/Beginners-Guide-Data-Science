Title:
Parmesan-Romano Crusted Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fresh shredded parmesan/romano cheese mix
2 Tbsp seasoned bread crumbs
1 Tbsp fresh chopped parsley
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp mayo
1 Tbsp lime juice
1/8 tsp dried basil
1/8 tsp ground pepper
1/8 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp Old Bay
1 pound tilapia filets

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except fish.
Broil fish 2-3 minutes.  Flip fish and broil 2 more minutes.  Divide the cheese mixture and press equal amounts onto each filet.  Broil 2 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Dutchgirl
Description: 
My East Coast friend made this for me when I was out for a visit.  She's been making this for years for all kinds of fish. Easy and delicious.
