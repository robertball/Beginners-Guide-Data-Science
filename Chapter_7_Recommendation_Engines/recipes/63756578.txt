Title:
Sugar Cookies, Soft (Mom's original) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2/3 cup shortening
2 eggs
1/3 cup sour cream
3 cups flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Beat sugar, shortening and eggs with an electric mixer on medium speed for about 3 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients into the sugar shortening mixture and mix until combined. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
Roll out 1/4 inch thick and cut into shapes. For fruit pizza, roll out on a pizza pan.
Bake at 350. Cookies 7-9 minutes, fruit pizza 9-14 minutes. Do not overbake.
