Title:
"Hawaiian" S'more Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1985346.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 Tbsp softened butter, or as needed
1 [12 oz/12 roll] package Hawaiian sweet rolls [such as King's]
3 [1.55 oz] milk chocolate bars
3 c mini marshmallows*
1/2 [12 oz] can Lite Spam, cut in 12 [2x1 3/4x1/4 inch] slices  - if desired

Directions:
Butter or spray baking dish.* *
If using Spam, brown slices in a skillet. Set aside.
Cut package of rolls in half horizontally.  Butter cut surface of top and bottom portions.
Heat griddle or large skillet. Place bottom of half of rolls , butter side down, on heated pan, till golden brown. Place in prepared baking dish, cut side up. Place top of loaf, buttered side down on pan, and brown. Set aside.
Break each chocolate bar in four sections. Place one piece of chocolate bar on each roll section of loaf in baking dish.
If using Spam, place one slice on each piece of chocolate.
Sprinkle marshmallows over chocolate [or Spam].
Set oven to "broil." Place baking dish six inches below heat source. Broil approximately two minutes, watching closely, until marshmallows are puffy and golden brown, being careful to remove pan before marshmallows burn.
Place top half of loaf, cut side down, on top of marshmallows. Allow to cool slightly, cut roll sections apart. Will be gooey and messy (=
Enjoy!
