Title:
Paula Deen's Chicken Georgia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter (1/2 stick)
4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
2 tablespoons minced shallots
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
4 oz. grated mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat in a non-stick pan.  Add mushrooms and shallots and sprinkle with salt & pepper.  Cook about 10 minutes.
Push mushrooms to the side of the pan and add chicken; cook 10 minutes on each side or until tender and cooked through.  Turn off heat.
Sprinkle chicken with grated cheese and top with the mushroom mix.  Cover pan and let stand until cheese is melted (about 5 minutes).
