Title:
Ravioli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986726.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (9 ounce) packages BUITONI速 Refrigerated Whole Wheat Four Cheese Ravioli, prepared according to package directions, kept hot
2 cups shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
2 tablespoons BUITONI速 Refrigerated Freshly Shredded Parmesan Cheese
16 ounces sweet or hot light turkey Italian sausage, casings removed
3/4 cup LIBBY'S速 100% Pure Pumpkin
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup fat-free chicken broth
1 (15 ounce) container BUITONI速 Refrigerated Roasted Garlic Marinara Sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese in medium bowl.
Heat large, nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add sausage; cook, stirring to break into pieces, until browned. Transfer sausage to paper towel-lined plate to drain.
Add pumpkin, wine and broth to skillet; cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until bubbly. Stir in sauce and sausage; heat through.
Layer ingredients as follows in ungreased 13 x 9-inch baking dish: 1/2 pasta, 1/2 sauce and 1/2 cheese mixture. Repeat layers. Cover with foil.
BAKE for 20 to 25 minutes or until cheese is melted. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Buitoni, courtesy of meals.com
Description: 
Layers of cheese ravioli, sausage and pumpkin-marinara sauce, and cheeses are baked for an easy savory casserole that's a sure crowd pleaser.
