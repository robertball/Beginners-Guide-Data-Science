Title:
That 70's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700161.jpg
Ingredients:
6 apples,sliced
f.g.cinnamon
1/4 cup margarine
2/3 cup flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
f.g.salt

Directions:
Mix flour,brown sugar,salt,cinnamon and margarine until it resembles bread crumbs.
Peel and core apples and slice into a greased baking dish.
Spread the margarine mixture on top.
Bake until apples are tender and top is golden brown.
