Title:
Beef Jerky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. seasoned salt
1/3 tsp garlic powder
1/3 tsp pepper (black)
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup soya sauce
1 tsp. liquid smoke
1 1/2 lbs sliced beef (sirloin)

Directions:
Marinate sliced meat in sauce over night.
Place on drying racks and dry until dry but not too dry.
