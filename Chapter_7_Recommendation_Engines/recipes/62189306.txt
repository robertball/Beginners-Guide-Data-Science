Title:
Creamy Garlic Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/428650.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked penne pasta
4 tablespoons butter
4 garlic cloves, crushed
1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 (15 ounce) can vegetable broth
2/3 cup sour cream
Salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste
Red pepper flakes, to taste (optional)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Set aside.
Meanwhile, melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until soft, about 5 minutes. Add flour and cook for 2 minutes. Add broth and stir frequently until mixture reaches a simmer.
Add the sour cream and stir until melted. Add salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if using.
Stir in cooked pasta until evenly coated with sauce. Let stand over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, until sauce reaches desired thickness.

Submitter:

Description: 
A rich vegetarian pasta dish. You can also mix in vegetables like spinach or broccoli, or cubed cooked chicken. Quick and easy!
