Title:
Jim's Pork and Saurkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. bag Saurkraut
1 sweet apple ( such as a red delicious )- peeled and chopped
1 lg. sweet onion (rough chop )
1 Tbs. Brown sugar
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. black pepper
2 lb. pork roast
1 Tbs. caraway seeds

Directions:
In crockpot layer The onion and apple, Sprinkle with brown sugar, 1/2  of the garlic powder and cover with saurkraut and caraway seeds.  Top with pork roast ( fat side up ) sprinkle roast with the other 1/2 garlic powder and black pepper.
Cook on low for 8 hrs.

Submitter: 
Terry Goff Tripp
Description: 
If you are not a kraut lover try this one... a little sweet apple, vidallia onion, and some brown sugar... sweetens this traditional german delicacy!
