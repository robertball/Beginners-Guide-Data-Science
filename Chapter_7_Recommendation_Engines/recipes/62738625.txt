Title:
Mamma Mia Misty's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/628158.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
3 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 egg substitute (1/4 cup)
1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
4 cloves of garlic, diced
1/2 onion, diced
1/2 bell pepper, diced
1-2 tsp. salt
1-2 tsp. ground pepper
1/2 tsp. paprika
2 tsp. italian seasoning

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients and roll into golf ball sized balls. Bake on a cookie sheet at 425 for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Misty Darnell
Description: 
A very healthy alternative to meatballs and you can't tell the difference! Great with pasta or in a meatball sandwich!
