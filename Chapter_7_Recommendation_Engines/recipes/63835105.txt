Title:
Pasta Pomodoro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065784.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 (10.75 ounce) can  low-sodium chicken broth
crushed red pepper to taste
pepper to taste
2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8  minutes or until al dente; drain.
Pour olive oil in a large deep skillet over high-heat. Saute onions and garlic until lightly browned. Reduce heat to medium-high and add tomatoes, vinegar and chicken broth; simmer for about 8 minutes.
Stir in red pepper, black pepper, basil and cooked pasta, tossing thoroughly with sauce.
Simmer for about 5 more minutes and serve topped with grated cheese.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Easy and light pasta with tomatoes and garlic. Cooked chicken breast chunks or shrimp can also be added to sauce for a great main dish!Modified from: Pasta Pomodoro
