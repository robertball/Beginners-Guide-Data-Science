Title:
Big Ray's Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1290573.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbl butter
1 Tbl olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 lb zucchini, sliced
2 lb yellow squash, sliced
3 eggs
1/4 cup sour cream
4 oz cream cheese, softened
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 Tbl fresh thyme
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Heat butter in large cast iron skillet.  Add onions and cook 2 minutes, add garlic cook additional 1 minute.  Add zucchini and yellow squash.  Cover and cook over medium heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Combine eggs, sour cream and cream cheese, stirring until smooth.  Add salt, pepper and thyme.  Pour over squash mixture and mix well.  Bake for 35 minutes.  Remove from oven and top with breadcrumbs and cheese.  Place under broiler and broil until brown, about 3 minutes.
