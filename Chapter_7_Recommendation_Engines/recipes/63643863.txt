Title:
Thai Salad with Teriyaki Ginger Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) al fresco Teriyaki Ginger Chicken Meatballs
2 tbsp honey
1 tbsp black bean sauce
1/2 cup pineapple, crushed with juice
1/4 tsp red chili pepper crushed flakes
1 1/2 cup broccoli, florets, fresh blanched & chilled
5 leaves romaine lettuce, fresh
5 napa cabbage leaves
1/2 cup carrots, freshly grated
3/4 cup soybean sprouts (may substitute pea shoots for a unique flavor & presentation)
1/2 cup red bell pepper, fresh, sliced
5 tbsp sesame ginger salad dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350&deg; F.
Using a small sauce pan, heat the honey, black bean sauce, crushed pineapple (with juice), and crushed red pepper flakes. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 2 -3 minutes.
Place al fresco Teriyaki Ginger Meatballs on a baking tray prepared with cooking spray. Heat for 15 minutes in oven, glaze with the pinepple, black bean sauce for the last 5 minutes of baking.
To arrange the salad, set-up 5 chilled plates. Line the plate with the romaine lettuce leaf followed by the napa cabbage leaf. Fill the lettuce cup with the bean sprouts, shredded carrots red pepper slices and broccoli florettes. Drizzle with the sesame & ginger salad dressing.
Top each salad with 3 glazed Teriyaki Ginger meatballs. Serve immediately.
