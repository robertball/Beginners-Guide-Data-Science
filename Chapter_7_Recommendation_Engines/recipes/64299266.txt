Title:
Slow Cooker Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream soup
1/4 cup water
1 1/2 pounds boneless pork chops
1 onion, chopped
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
Salt, onion & garlic powder to taste
14.5 oz can whole green beans
4 potatoes, peeled and cubed

Directions:
Place potatoes in bottom of slow cooker. Season chops, then brown in skillet with a bit of oil. Layer chops on top of potatoes. Stir in water to thin soup slightly and mix in skillet to get all the brown bits. Place chopped onions on top of chops, and pour soup mixture over everything. Cook on Low for 7 to 8 hours.
Add green beans and cook until heated - 45-60 minutes.R emove from heat.

Submitter: 
MaggieF60
Description: 
Use your slow cooker to make these creamy and comforting pork chops. Adding green beans and potatoes to lightly seasoned pork chops in cream of mushroom soup makes this a hearty one-pot meal.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chops
