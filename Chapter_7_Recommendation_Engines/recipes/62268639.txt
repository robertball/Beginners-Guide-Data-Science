Title:
Soft Mocha Chocolate Chip Cookies* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon Espresso powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
2 cups butter, softened
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 (3.4 ounce) packages instant chocolate pudding mix
4 eggs
2 Tablespoons Kahlua
4 cups milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Sift together the flour, espresso powder, baking soda, and salt and set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar.
Beat in the instant pudding mix until blended.
Stir in the eggs and Kahlua.
Blend in the flour mixture.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Drop cookies by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake for 10 minutes .
