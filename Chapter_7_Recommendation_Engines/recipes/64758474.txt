Title:
pantry potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 potatoes sliced 1/8 in thick
1/2 C mayonnaise
1/2 TBS sriracha
1 /2 C shredded white cheddar cheese
6 croutons crushed about 1/4 cup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise and sriracha fold in potatoes and1/4 C cheese.
Spray a casserole dish with cooking spray and pour potato mixture into it.
Cover and baked for 20 minutes.
Uncover and sprinkle with remaining cheese  and crushed croutons. Baked uncovered for 10 minutes or until browned and bubbling.
