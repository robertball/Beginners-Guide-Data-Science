Title:
curried lentils with sweet potatoes and chard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
oil
1/2 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 inch piece ginger root, peeled and grated
3/4 tsp. garam masala
3/4 tsp.curry powder
1 small jalapeno, diced
3 c. vegetable broth
3/4 # sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch cubes
3/4 c. dried lentils
1 small bay leaf
1/2 # greens (kale, chard, etc.), about one bunch, sliced thinly
1/2 tsp. Kosher salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
3 T. chopped cilantro
zest of 1 small lime; juice of 1/2 of it

Directions:
In large saucepan, heat oil and add onion; saute till translucent, 5-7 minutes.  Add garlic, ginger, garam masala, curry powder, and jalapeno.  Cook, stirring for 1 minute.
Stir in broth, sweet potatoes, lentils and bay leaf.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium, partially cover, and simmer till lentils are tender (about 40 minutes.)
Stir in chard and salt and pepper, and cook till the greens are tender, 5-10 minutes.
Just before serving, stir in cilantro, lime zest and juice.
