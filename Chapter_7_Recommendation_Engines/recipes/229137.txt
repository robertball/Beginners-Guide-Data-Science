Title:
Bath Water Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4575262.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon rainbow sherbet
1 (0.13 ounce) package black cherry-flavored fruit drink mix (such as KOOL-AID®)
1 cup white sugar
4 cups water
1 (0.13 ounce) package orange-flavored fruit drink mix (such as KOOL-AID®)
4 cups pineapple-flavored soda

Directions:
Scoop rainbow sherbet into a bowl; allow to sit at room temperature until it turns slushy..
Mix black cherry-flavored drink mix with sugar and water in a pitcher; stir until sugar is dissolved. Add orange-flavored drink mix; stir well. Pour into a punch bowl. Top with pineapple soda; stir well.
Stir the rainbow sherbet with a wooden spoon until it turns a grayish color. Scoop into the punch bowl. Stir slowly to break up the sherbet.

Submitter: 
Julianne
Description: 
A fruity Halloween punch that will make your guests cringe! Feel free to use a variety of food coloring to achieve the desired color of punch.
 Decorate with desired plastic flies, eye balls, or plastic fingers! For the adults: throw in some vanilla vodka! Yummy!
