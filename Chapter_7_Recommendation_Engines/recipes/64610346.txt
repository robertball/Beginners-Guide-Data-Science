Title:
Craisen  White Chocolate Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4155342.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c flour
1/2 c sugar
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
6 tbsp cold butter
1 c vanilla yogurt
1/4 c plus 2 tbsp milk, divided
1 1/3 c Craisens
2/3 c White baking chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and baking soda.
Cut in butter until mixture resembles course crumbs.
Combine yogurt and 1/4 c milk. Stir into crumb mixture until just moistened. Knead in craisens and chips.
On a greased baking sheet pat dough in a 9 in circle. Cut into 8 wedges and separate them slightly. Brush with remaining milk and sprinkle with sugar.
Bake 20-25 minutes.
Serve warm.
