Title:
Crock-Pot Beef Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5643725.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground chuck(you may use ground turkey if you wish)
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
1-14 1/2 ounce can petite diced tomatoes, drained slightly
1-15 1/4 ounce can whole kernel yellow corn, drained
1-15 1/2 ounce can pinto beans, drained(you can use kidney beans or black beans if you wish)
1 cup low-sodium beef broth
1 cup water
1-1 ounce packet taco seasoning mix(can use mild or spicy)
1-1 ounce packet dry ranch dressing mix(can use mild or spicy)
your favorite taco toppings for garnishing

Directions:
Place meat, seasonings, broth, water and vegetable mixture in a large Crock-Pot that's been lightly sprayed with nonstick cooking spray and mix until well combined.
Allow to cook for 8 hours on LOW. Stir occasionally.
Serve with your favorite taco toppings if desired.
