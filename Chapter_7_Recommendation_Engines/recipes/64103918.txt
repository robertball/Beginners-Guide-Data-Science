Title:
Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pie crust for 9 inch pie
2 eggs
1 can pumpkin (16 oz)
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp cloves
1-2/3 cups evaporated milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  Prepare pastry.
Beat eggs slightly with mixer; beat in remaining ingredients.  Pour into pastry.  Bake 15 minutes.
Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F.  Bake 45 minutes longer or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool.
