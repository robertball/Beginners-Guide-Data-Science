Title:
Grandma's Chicken 'n Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cut into thirds each)
1 pkg. Brownberry sage and onion stuffing mix (May substitute Stovetop chicken or turkey flavor)
1/2 onion, diced
1/2 cup prepared chicken bouillion
1/4 cup flour
6 tbsp. real butter
5 cups milk (not skim)
salt and pepper

Directions:
Spray a 9 x 13 casserole.
Lay raw chicken breast pieces on bottom of pan.
Prepare Brownberry stuffing according to directions.
Layer the stuffing on top of the chicken.
Set aside.
In 2 qt. saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.  Add onions and saute until transparent.  Slowly add flour, blending well with back of spoon.  Slowly add chicken broth, changing to a whisk to blend well.  Add milk slowly, continually stirring with whisk until mixture is heated through and thickened.  Season with salt/pepper to taste.
Pour sauce over chicken/stuffing mixture.
Bake in 350 degree oven for one hour, uncovered.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
A warm and comforting dish that complements potatoes or rice.  A touch of Thanksgiving without all the fuss!
