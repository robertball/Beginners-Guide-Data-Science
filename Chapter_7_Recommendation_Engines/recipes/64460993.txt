Title:
Lowfat Seaood Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs low fat mayonnaise
2 tbs fat free sour cream
2 tbs lemon juice
1 tbs Dijon mustard
1 tbs dry vermouth
2 tsp oil
2 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
1 lb peeled deveined medium shrimp
12 oz. lump crabmeat
3 tbs flour
1 15 oz can evaporated fat free milk
1 cup 1% milk
4 tbs Parmesan cheese
2 tbs bread crumbs
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
in a large bowl combine mayonnaise, sour cream, lemon juice, mustard and vermouth. Refrigerate. Warm oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic, cook 1 minute. Add shrimp and cook for 3 minutes, until opaque. Transfer to bowl with mayonnaise mixture. Add crab and stir. Coat a 2 quart casserole dish with cooking spray. Add shrimp mixture. Place flour in small saucepan. Gradually whisk in evaporated milk until smooth. Whisk in light milk. Cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly, until thickened. Whisk in 2 tbsp cheese and pour over shrimp. In a small bowl, mix bread crumbs, paprika and remaining cheese. Sprinkle over shrimp mixture. Bake 350 for 30-40 minutes.
