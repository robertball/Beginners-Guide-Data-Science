Title:
Jicama and Melon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/254969.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups thin slivers jicama
2 cups cubed honeydew melon
3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
3 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
1 teaspoon lime zest
2 teaspoons honey

Directions:
Toss together the jicama, honeydew, lime juice, mint, lime zest, and honey in a bowl; cover. Refrigerate 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Basil Babe
Description: 
This refreshing fruit salad is great with BBQ's.
