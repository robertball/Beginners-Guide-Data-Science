Title:
Thai hot Peanut sauce/dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small red chilli (seeded if required)
2 tbs light soy sauce
2 tbs fresh lime juice
2 tbs light brown sugar
6 tbs crunchy peanut butter
8 tbs coconut milk
1 tsp  Thai fish sauce (optional)

Directions:
finely chop the chilli
place in a pan with remaining ingredients, heat gently stiring constantly until well blended.
Adjust seasoning to taste (I use 1 tsp Thai fish sauce instead of salt, but salt is ok, the fish sauce is extra liquid though)
