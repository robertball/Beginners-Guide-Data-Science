Title:
Apfelkuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c soft butter
1 box yellow cake mix
1-2 large can apple pie mix or 2 1/2 c fresh apples(soak in lemon juice and water to prevent turning brown)
1t cinnamon
1/2c sugar or splenda
1c sour cream
1 egg

Directions:
Cut butter into cake mix until crumbly.
Pat mixture into 9x13 pan or 10x15 cookie sheet, building up slight edges.
Bake 10 minutes at 350.
Arrange apples on warm crust.
Mix sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle on apples.
Blend sour cream and egg; drizzle over apples.
Bake 25 minutes at 350, slightly less time for 10x15.

Submitter: 
Cammie Moon
Description: 
Easy dessert and you can use apples or peaches or chocolate or strawberrys....apples and pineapple chunks is a favorite of mine.  :>

You may also use a white or sour cream cake mix.
