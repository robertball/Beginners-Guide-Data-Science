Title:
Cool As A Cucumber Sauce (Tzatziki) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large cucumber, peeled and de-seeded
1 cup plain Greek yogurt
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop the cucumber in a food processor until pulpy.
Add yogurt to food processor and pulse until blended.
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and stir well.

Submitter: 
SlowcookerSeduction
Description: 
A mild sauce great for balancing a spicy dish or dipping chips and veggies. Best if chilled for at least one hour.Modified from: Molly's Mouthwatering Tzatziki Cucumber Sauce
