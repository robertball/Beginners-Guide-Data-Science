Title:
Tatertot Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Tater tots
1 LB Ground Beef
1 Can Cream of mushroom Soup
american cheese slices to cover top

Directions:
Cook tater tots accordng to package directions in oven
Brown Ground Beef and drain
Mix Ground Beef and can of Cream of mushroom soup
Pour mixture over tatertots in casserole dish
Layer american cheese on top.
Bake until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Melissa Combrink
Description: 
Tatertot and ground beef casserole easy and kids usually like this. It's also filling.
