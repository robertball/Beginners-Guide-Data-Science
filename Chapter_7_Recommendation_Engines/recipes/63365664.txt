Title:
Homemade Mohnfix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb poppy seeds
3/4 C sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 Tbl butter
1 egg
1/4 C honey
1/4 C candied orange peel
1 tsp lemon rind
1/4 C chopped walnuts
1/4 C chopped almonds
1/2 C golden raisins
2 egg whites

Directions:
Cover poppy seeds with water and bring to a boil. Remove and let stand until cool. Drain.
Blend seeds and nuts.
Melt butter. Add seed mix and sugar. Simmer 10-15 minutes. Add fruits.
Whip egg whites until stiff. Fold into poppy mix. Let cool.

Submitter: 
sixsnowflakes
Description: 
Use this poppy seed filling instead of cinnamon and sugar with a cinnamon roll dough to make poppy seed rolls.
