Title:
Golden Honey Date Nut Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/420788.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1 1/3 cup honey
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts
4 cups oats
1/2 cup oat bran
1/2 cup gound flaxseed
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/3 cup dried dates

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine oil, honey and sugar in a medium sauce pan, bring to a boil. Remove from heat, add vanilla.
In a large bowl combine oats, nuts, wheat bran, flaxseed, cranberries and dates.
Pour honey mixture over dry ingredients. Mix until completely combined.
Spread onto a foil cookie sheet with sides. Bake in preheated oven for 7 minutes. Stir granola on the cookie sheet and return to oven for 7 minutes more. Remove from heat and cool on cookie sheet. When granola is completely cooled store in an air tight 4 quart container.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
The perfect combination of chewy and crunchy. This honey kissed granola flavored with walnuts, cranberries and dried sweetened dates. Loaded with healthy ingredients, this granola is great with yogurt and fruit. Mix in chocolate chips for a healthy treat.
