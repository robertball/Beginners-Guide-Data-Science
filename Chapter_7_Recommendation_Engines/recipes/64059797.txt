Title:
Shrimp Olive Garden Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of shrimp OR 3 large chicken breasts
Italian seasoning and garlic
2 tablespoons of butter
12 ounce package of Spiral pasta
1 can sliced olives
1 cucumber, chopped into small pieces
1 medium tomato, chopped into small pieces
1 bell pepper, chopped
8 ounces Olive Garden dressing
1 cup Italian cheese, grated
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning for the pasta salad

Directions:
Melt the butter and add the shrimp. Sprinkle the shrimp well with granulated garlic and Italian seasoning.
Remove from the skillet when the shrimp turn light pink. Do not over cook them.
Cook the pasta according to the directions on the package.
Chop the cucumber and the tomato into bite size pieces.
Add all the ingredients including the Italian dressing and the shredded cheese.
I added an extra teaspoon of Italian seasoning at this point.
