Title:
Cheesy Potato Potato Chip Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 pint sour cream
10.75 oz. can condensed cream of chicken soup
2 c. shredded Cheddar cheese
Salt and pepper
2 lb. frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
2 c. crushed potato chips
1/2 c. melted butter

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
In a large mixing bowl, combine onion, sour cream, soup, cheese, salt and pepper.
Press the excess water out of the hashbrowns and then add them to the soup mixture and mix well.
Transfer to a 9x12 inch casserole dish.
Sprinkle potato chips on top, then drizzle with butter
Bake in preheated oven for 45-60 minutes, until golden brown
