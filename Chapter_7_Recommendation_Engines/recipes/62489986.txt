Title:
Pete's Creole & Blackening Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648756.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbl Onion powder
2 Tbl Garlic powder
3 Tbl Oregano, dried
2 Tbl Basil, dried
1 Tbl Thyme, dried
1 Tbl Black pepper
1 Tbl White pepper
1 Tbl Cayenne pepper
4 Tbl Paprika
1 Tbl Paprika, smoked
3 Tbl Salt

Directions:
Ensure that all leafy ingredients (i.e., Thyme, Basil) is crushed so that all ingredients will go through the salt shaker hole
Add all remaining ingredients and store in air tight container

Great seasoning mix, especially for Anna's Jambalaya
