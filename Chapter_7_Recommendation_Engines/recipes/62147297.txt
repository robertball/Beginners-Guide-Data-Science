Title:
Sam's Favorite Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Packaged Pork Tenderloin, trimmed of fat.
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. freshly ground pepper
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
2 tsp minced garlic
1/4 C. Apple Cider Vinegar
1/8 C. Worchestershire Sauce
1/2 C. Brown Sugar
2 C. Water
A few dashes Hot Sauce
For Sauce:
1/2 C. Mayo
3 Tbsp. White Vinegar
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
dash cayenne pepper
Lemon Pepper to taste

Directions:
Rub salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper onto tenderloin.
Brown all sides of tenderloin in a bit of oil over medium high heat.
Transfer meat to slow cooker.
Add minced garlic, cider vinegar, worchestershire sauce, brown sugar, water and hot sauce to slow cooker.
Set on low, cook 7 hours.
Remove tenderloin, shred meat. Reserve 1/4 to 1/2 cup of the cooking fluid, mix that with shredded meat back in the slow cooker.
If you wish, add about 1/4 C. prepared BBQ sauce.
For Sauce:
Combine ingredients in a bowl (all to taste).
Serve Pork on warm rolls/bread with a few dollups of sauce. Also great with sauteed onions.

Submitter: 
Morgan
Description: 
Pork tenderloin cooked all day in the slow cooker for an affordable, delicious supper!
