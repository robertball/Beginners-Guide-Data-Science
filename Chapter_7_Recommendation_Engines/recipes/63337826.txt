Title:
Chicken with Basil Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1/4  cup milk
1/4 cup bread crumbs
3 tbsp butter
1/2 cup chicken broth
4 oz jar pimentos, drained
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1/3 cup minced fresh basil
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Place milk and bread crumbs in separate shallow bowls.
Dip chicken in milk, then coat with crumbs.
In a skillet over medium-high heat, cook chicken in butter for about 8 minutes on each side or until a meat thermometer reads 170Â° Remove and keep warm.
Add broth to the skillet. Bring to a boil over medium heat; stir to loosen browned bits.
Stir in the cream and pimientos; boil and stir for 1 minute. Reduce heat.
Add Parmesan cheese, basil and pepper; cook and stir until heated through. Serve with chicken.
