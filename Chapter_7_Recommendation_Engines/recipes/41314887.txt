Title:
Texas Style Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp veg oil
1 lb. ground turkey
14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
1 small can mild green chilies
1 Poblano pepper, diced
2 jalapeno chiles, diced
1/2 cup good beer
1T chili powder
3 T dried onions
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a pot and add the ground turkey.
Once the turkey is browned, add it and the other ingredients to the crock pot and cook on low for 7-8 hours.
Serve with onions, cheese, and tortilla chip pieces.

Submitter: 
Lindsey
Description: 
I use ground turkey in my chili to make it a little more figure friendly.  Use beef if that is your favorite.
