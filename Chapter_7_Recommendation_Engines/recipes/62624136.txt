Title:
Irish Cream Liqueur II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle Irish whiskey
8 3/4 ounces DARK chocolate (Ghiradelli 60%)
2 (14 ounce) cans fat free sweetened condensed milk
2 (12 fluid ounce) cans fat free evaporated milk
2 cups heavy cream
1/2 cup skim milk
1/4 teaspoon instant coffee granules
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a bowl combine the Evaporated & sweetened condensed milk.  In the top of a double boiler, melt chocolate & cream together until smooth.  Heat skim milk in microwave & dissolve coffee granules.  Gradually stir in the chocolate cream and the cofffee mix into the other milks in bowl.  Whisk until well blended.  When cooled to room temp, add whiskey & vanilla.
Pour mixture into 3 (1 liter) bottles, seal and store in refrigerator. Shake bottle up every few days.  Best if kept at least 1 month before using. To serve: shake bottle well, serve in small glasses over crushed ice.
