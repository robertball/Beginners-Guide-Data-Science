Title:
Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Water
1 C. Milk
1 6 oz can orange juice
1 tsp. vanilla
1 egg
1/4 C. sugar
ice

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a mixer.  Blend.  As you blend drop in 8-10 ice cubes one at a time until all crushed

Submitter: 
Kelly Norton
Description: 
This is just as good as going and buying a Orange Julius at the mall.  Yum!
