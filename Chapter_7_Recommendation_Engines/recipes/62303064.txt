Title:
Cheesy Sausage Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1 lb. hot italian sausage, casings removed
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
8 ounces cream cheese
1 lb. Velveeta PepperJack Cheese loaf, cubed
1 small can green chiles, diced
1 can cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Brown ground beef, hot sausage and onion in large skillet over medium heat.
Add cream cheese and Velveeta, stir until cheeses are thoroughly melted.  Mix in remaining ingredients and simmer 3 minutes.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
DuffMan
Description: 
A high-fat, but oh-so-yummy warm dip.  Best served in a small crockpot so that it stays warm.  Serve with tortilla chips, pita chips or bagel chips.
