Title:
Low-Carb Fauxtato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower
4 chopped hard-boiled eggs
1/2 c. - 3/4 c. mayo
2 Tbls. dill relish
1 Tbl. prepared yellow mustard
2-3 Tbls. diced green onion
salt & pepper if desired

Directions:
Cut the cauliflower into bite-size pieces.  Steam for 5 min.  Once the cauliflower is done, mix with remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl.  Serve chilled or warm.

Submitter: 
SHAUNH
Description: 
Low-carb cauliflower salad that tastes just like a standard potato salad, but with only 3g carbs.
