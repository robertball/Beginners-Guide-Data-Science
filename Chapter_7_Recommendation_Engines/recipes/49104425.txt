Title:
Creamy Spinach sausage pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked rigatoni or large tube pasta
1 lb. bulk Italian sausage
1 cup finely chopped onion
1 can Italian diced tomatoes, undrained
2 pkg frozen creamed spinach, thawed
1 pkg cream cheese, softened
2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions.
Meanwhile, cook sausage and onion.
Drain and stir in tomatoes, spinach, cream cheese,cooked noodles and 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese.
Transfer to a 11x 7 baking dish
Cover and bake 350 for 35 minutes
Uncover; sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake 10 minutes longer
