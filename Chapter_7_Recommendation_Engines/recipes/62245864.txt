Title:
Mommy's Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 lb. ground pork
1/2 c. soft bread crumbs
1/2 c. sour cream
1 egg, slightly beaten
2 T. grated onion
1/8 t. allspice
1/4 t. pepper
1 1/4 t. salt
Sauce:
2 c. beef broth
1/2 c. sour cream
2 T. flour
dash allspice
1/2 t. grated onion

Directions:
Combine all meatball ingredients.  Form into 1" balls and broil.  Drain/blot off fat and set aside.
Combine all sauce ingredients and gently heat.  Pour over meatballs.
Serve with buttered noodles or rice.
