Title:
Pumpkin Pancakes (Whole Wheat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups milk
1/2 cup white vinegar
4- 4 1/2 cups fresh milled soft white wheat flour
1/4 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons salt
1 Tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
4 egg
1 cup pumpkin puree
cooking spray

Directions:
Start with 4 cups flour. Mix dry ingredients. Mix wet ingredients. Stir together until moistened. If too thin, add a little more flour. For large pancakes, use 1/3 cup scoop.
