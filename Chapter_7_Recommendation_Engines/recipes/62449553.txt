Title:
Simply Oven Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Long grain rice
3 cups water
1/4 cup soya sauce
1 pkg. onion soup mix
1/4 cup oil
1 tin sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and place in a covered casserole dish. Cover and bake 1 hour at 350 deg.

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
My friends and I have been making this rice dish for years.  It's so easy to make and everyone loves it.
