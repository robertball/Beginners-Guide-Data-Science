Title:
Sunrise Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536119.jpg
Ingredients:
1 container vanilla yogurt, low fat
1 small banana
1 cup whole strawberries
4 oz orange soda, low calorie
1 cup ice

Directions:
Add yogurt to blender.
Add banana in chunks, and blend until smooth.
Add fresh or frozen strawberries, and blend until smooth.
Add diet orange soda (like diet Sunkist), blend.
Add ice and blend until smooth.
