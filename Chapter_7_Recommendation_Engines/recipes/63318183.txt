Title:
Raspberry Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C raspberries
1 C water (divided)
3/4 C sugar
1/2 TBLS lemon juice
2 TBLS cornstarch

Directions:
1. Combine raspberries, lemon juice, sugar and 3/4 C water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil then turn the heat down. Simmer 15-20 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and strain through a fine mesh strainer to remove the seeds. Return liquid to the heat.
3. Combine remaining water and the cornstarch to make a slurry. Mix well then add to the raspberry mixture. Boil for 5 minutes then move to the fridge to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Alisa Provost
Description: 
A filling that can be used for cakes. You can substitute any berry you like: blueberry, strawberry, pear, blackberry. A filling with endless possibilities! I use this in a variety of cakes and its always a hit!
