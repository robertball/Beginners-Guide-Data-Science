Title:
Tuna Tango Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups spiral pasta, cooked (approx 8 ox uncooked)
1 can (6 1/8 oz) solid white tuna, drained
1 jar (2 oz) diced pimientos, drained
1/2 cup green onions, sliced
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp dill week
1/2 tsp ground mustard
1/2 tsp tarragon

Directions:
In a bowl, combine pasta, tuna, pimientos and green onions. Blend mayo with dill weed, ground mustard and tarragon; carefully stir into pasta mixture.
