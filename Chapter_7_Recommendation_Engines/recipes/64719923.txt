Title:
Chocolate Banana Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5885660.jpg
Ingredients:
Wet ingredients
2 large ripe bananas (for perfect ripeness, bake bananas peel on at 150 °C for 15 minutes)
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
Dry ingredients
1 & 1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup finely grated semi sweet chocolate (you can use chocolate chips or sprinkles)
For Filling
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate coarsely chopped
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
smash the bananas with a fork
add all the wet ingredients
mix well with electric mixer until all are homogeneous
sift  all dry ingredients over the mixture and continue mixing with the electric mixer until a thick sticky batter forms
add the chocolate grate and fold using a spatula or wooden spoon until fully incorporated.
scoop your batter into muffin molds using icecream scooper. fill each mold 3/4th its volume
muffin mold should be greased. muffin paper is better used.
bake in a preheated oven on 180 °C for 25 minutes. until a toothpick comes out clean of the muffins
remove from oven put aside to cool, dont remove from molds
heat the heavy cream (microwave for 40 seconds or heat over stovetop) until it starts scalding
pour the cream over the chocolate. let it rest for 5 minutes
whisk the cream and chocolate until fully dissolved, ganache forms.
with the back of a wooden spoon poke a hole in the center of each muffin all the way till the bottom
the hole should be around 1 cm in diameter
pour the ganache into the holes until its fills them completely
chill for 1 hour before serving
