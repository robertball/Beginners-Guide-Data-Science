Title:
Slow Cooked Taco Beef Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound (500g) rump, blade or chuck steak cubed
1 large onion diced
1 425g tinned tomatoes
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1/2 can of water (use tomato can)
2 teaspoons of beef stock powder (or 1 stock cube)
1 teaspoon garlic
a good slurp of worshitishire sauce
1 sachet of taco seasoning
1 tin of red kidney beans, well drained and rinsed

Directions:
Dice steak, not too small about 1.5cm (1/2 inch) and place in slow cooker
Dice onion and add to slow cooker
Add tomatoes, mushrooms, garlic, worshtishire sauce, water, stock powder and tace seasoning to slow cooker.
Turn on to high and cook for 4 hours (or on low for 6-7 hours)
Add the kidney beans 15 minutes before serving - any sooner and they will turn too moosh!
Serve with rice and corn chips (my husband likes it with sour cream as well)

Submitter: 
ELIZABETH101
Description: 
This recipe si so easy, but fool proof.  The secret ingredient is a sachet of taco seasoning.  Combine that with a few other ingredients and hey presto!! 4 hours later, a fantastic meal with almost no effort.
