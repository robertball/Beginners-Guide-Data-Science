Title:
Big Ray's Baja Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4137403.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound  tilapia
1/4 taco seasoning
juice of 1 1/2 limes, divided (plus additional lime wedges for serving)
8 taco-sized flour tortillas (6-inch tortillas)

Directions:
Grease a skillet and preheat over medium heat. Add fish pieces. Drizzle juice from 1 lime over fish, then sprinkle with taco seasoning and saute over medium heat 3-4 minutes until outside is browned and fish is tender.
Assemble tacos by adding toppings, fish, and sauce between tortillas. Serve with additional cilantro and lime wedges.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This recipe is so SO easy. The fish gets sautéed with seasonings and fresh lime juice, then wrapped in soft flour tortillas with toppings (creamy cabbage slaw) and topped with a mayo sauce! They’re absolutely insanely tasty and come together in less than 30 minutes. If you like fish tacos, you will LOVE these!!!
