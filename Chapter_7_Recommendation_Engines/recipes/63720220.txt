Title:
White Chicken Crockpot Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 frozen chicken breasts; 3 cans corn, DO NOT DRAIN; 3 Large cans of black beans, Drained and rinsed; 2 and a half packets Ranch Dressing mix; 2 and a half blocks Cream Cheese; 3 Tablespoons Cumin; 2 Tablespoons Chili Powder; 3 Tablespoons Onion Powder

Directions:
In crockpot, lay frozen chicken breasts at the bottom of the pot, top with corn, black beans, dry ranch dressing mix, cream cheese, cumin, chili powder and onion powder.  Cook on high for 6-8 hours.  When cooking is complete, stir thoroughly and shred chicken.
