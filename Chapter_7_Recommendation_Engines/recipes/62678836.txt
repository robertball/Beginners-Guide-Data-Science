Title:
Award Winning Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 large onions, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 tablespoon raw jalapeno, finely chopped
1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (14 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 (6oz) can tomato paste
2 tablespoons cumin
1 tablespoons chili powder
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon paprika
2 (14 ounce) can chili beans (don't drain)
2 (14 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can beef broth
Sour cream (optional)
Cheddar Cheese (optional)
Fritos or corn chips (optional)

Directions:
Fry hamburger and onion in large pan, drain oil and deglaze with beef broth.
Add all ingredients in large crock pot and cook on low for 3-4 hrs.
Top with sour cream, cheddar cheese and fritos or other corn chips (optional)
