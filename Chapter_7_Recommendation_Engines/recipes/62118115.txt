Title:
Old fashioned Crackling Cornbread stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
~CORNBREAD~
2 cups Cornmeal
2 teaspoons Baking powder
1 Tablesoon Baking Soda
3/4 Teaspoons salt
2 Beaten eggs
2 Cups Buttermilk
2 Tablespoons Vegatable Oil
rendered Pork Fat or bacon grease for greasing the pan
1 cup Craclkings
~DRESSING~
1 cup Celery
1/2 cup Onion
1 stick butter
1 lb Bacon, Cooked and Crumbled

Directions:
Mix cornmeal Baking Powder, Baking Powder and Salt in a large bowl.
Ad Eggs, Buttermilk, Vegtable oil and Cracklings and stir until moistened
Grease 10 in Iron Skillet generously with Rendered pork fat.  and place in oven 4 mins until hot
Remove from oven
Spoon batter into Skillet and bake at 450 for 25 mins until lightly browned
Cool and crumble into a large bowl
mix into cornbread bowl 1 lb cooked crumbled bacon and refrigerate overnight
Cook celery and onion in 1 stick of butter until tender.
add celery mixture to cornbread mixture
Use to stuff Turkey
