Title:
Lobster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole lobsters
melted butter
2quarts chicken stock

Directions:
Cover the lobster completley with chicken stock. Boil for 10min per pound. Take out and split. Place on broiler pan and top with butter then broil until brown!
