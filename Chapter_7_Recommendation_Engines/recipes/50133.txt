Title:
Dreamy Cinnamon Hazelnut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985364.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups hot brewed coffee or prepared instant coffee
1/4 cup Hazelnut Flavor NESTLE® CARNATION® COFFEE-MATE® Liquid Coffee Creamer
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons granulated sugar
Whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE coffee, Coffee-mate, sugar and cinnamon in pitcher; stir. Pour into 2 large mugs. Top with whipped cream. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Carnation® Coffee-mate®
Description: 
A lightly sweetened hazelnut coffee with a hint of cinnamon flavor.
