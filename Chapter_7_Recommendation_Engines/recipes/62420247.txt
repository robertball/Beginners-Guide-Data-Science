Title:
doggone good dog treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444136.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups flour
1-1/2 tbsp. corn syrup
1/2 cup and 1 tsp. water
1 tbsp. beef or chicken bouillon granulades
1/4 cup shredded cheese
pinch salt and pepper
1-3/4 cups rolled oats
3 tbsp. peanut butter

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
in medium bowl stir together dry ingredients
add wet ingredients and if not stiff dough add more flour
make flatten circle and place on greased cookie sheet. sprinkle extra cheese ontop of biskuit
place in oven for 18 minutes (cool before giving dog)
