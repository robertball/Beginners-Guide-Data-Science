Title:
TOMATOES  VINEGRETTE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. celery salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 cup vegetable oil
4 med. ripe roma tomatoes
2 med. cucumbers
2 med. burmuda onions

Directions:
In a jar with a tight fitting lid, shake together the vinegar, salt, celery salt and pepper. Add the oil and shake again vigorously. Set aside.
Wash and core the tomatoes, slice thin. Peel the cucumbers, slice very thin. Trim onions, slice into very thin rings and separate. Use a mandolin, if you have one, to make the thin slices.
In an 8x8x2" glass pan, arrange one of the onions in a single layer. Top with a single layer of one of the cucumbers. Top that layer with rows of tomatoes. Repeat with a layer of cucumbers; lastly a later of onions.
Shake vinegrette one last time and pour over the layered salad. Cover with plastic wrap, weigh down with a heavy plate. Refrigerate at least 12 hours.

Submitter: 
lgmarge
Description: 
Layers of tomatoes, cucumbers and red onions marinate
in a simple vinegrette to provide a tasty side dish.
