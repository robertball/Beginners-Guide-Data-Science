Title:
Bread Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups of softened butter
1/2 cup honey
1/2 tbsp sugar
1/2 tbs cinnamon

Directions:
Combine together in a bowl and spread on your warm bread. Very yummy tastes like cinnamon buns without all the work

Submitter: 
REDNECKGRL
Description: 
This is a spread i made to go on my amish sweet white bread. Its so easy and reminds me of cinnamon buns. My husband loves it and cant get enough of this on my freash warm bread.
