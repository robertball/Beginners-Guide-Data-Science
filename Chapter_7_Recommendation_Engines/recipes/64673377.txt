Title:
Ranch Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4589801.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons dried tarragon
2 tablespoons dried parsley
3 tablespoons dried dill
1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon sea salt
1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together and store in an air-tight container.
Can be used in dips, dressings, on chicken and eggs.

Submitter: 
Chef DJ
Description: 
Seasoning mix for ranch dressing, ranch dip or chicken.  Make it up ahead of time and have it on hand. Low-Carb, Ketogenic, and Paleo Diet friendly.  Total of approx. 2 g carbs per tablespoon.
