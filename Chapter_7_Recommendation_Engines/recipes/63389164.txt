Title:
Greek Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 tsp dried oregano
2 tsp finely grated lemon zest
2 garlic cloves, minced
8 bone-in chicken thighs
1/2 cup pitted and sliced Kalamata olives
salt and freshly ground pepper
1/2 cup coarsly crumbled feta cheese
2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
Combine the first 5 ingredients in a bowl large enough to hold the chicken.  Add the chicken and turn to coat.Cover, refrigerate and marinate the chicken for 4 hours, turning occasionally.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Place chicken in a baking dish.  Pour any marinade from the bowl over the chicken, then season with salt and pepper.
Bake for 35 minutes.
Remove from oven and baste chicken with pan juices. Top and surround chicken with olives and feta cheese.
Bake 15 more, or until chicken is cooked through.
Sprinkle chicken with parsley.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
A wonderfully easy family dish that appeals to everyone's taste!  I serve with fluffy white rice.
