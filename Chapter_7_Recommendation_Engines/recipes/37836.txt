Title:
Berry Lemonade Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package COUNTRY TIME Lemonade Flavor Drink Mix or COUNTRY TIME LEM'N BERRY SIPPERS Drink Mix , any flavor
1/2 cup water
3 cups ice cubes
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries or strawberry halves

Directions:
Measure drink mix into cap just to 1-quart line.
Place drink mix, water, ice and fruit into blender container; cover.  Blend on high speed about 10 seconds.  Turn off blender.  Mix with spoon; cover.  Blend about 5 seconds or until smooth.  Serve immediately.  Store leftover slush in freezer.
