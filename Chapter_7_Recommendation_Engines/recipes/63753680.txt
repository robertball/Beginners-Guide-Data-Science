Title:
Cheesy Tortellini Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (9 oz) packages cheese tortellini
18 oz marinara or spaghetti sauce
12 oz Alfredo sauce
6 oz fresh spinach
1/2 red onion, chopped
4 cloves minced garlic
1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup freshly grated Asiago or parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package; drain. While pasta is cooking, saute onion and garlic; add spinach to wilt it. Add sauces and Italian seasoning to pan and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes. Stir tortellini into sauce mixture. Pour in 8x8 pan. Top with cheeses and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
