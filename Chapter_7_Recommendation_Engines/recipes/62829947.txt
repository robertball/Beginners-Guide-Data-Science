Title:
Crunchy Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670319.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of flour
1 cup of Corn flakes
1 cup of oats
1/2 cup of butter
1/2 cup of sugar
1/4 cup of nutella/peanut butter
1 egg
1/2 tsp of vanilla
1/2 tsp of baking powder
1/2 tsp of baking soda
1/2 tsp of salt

Directions:
1)Poor all of the ingredients in a bowl,add the corn flakes and the nutella/peanut butter at the end.
2)Mix with your hands.
3)Scoop the cookie mix on a  cookie sheet .
4)Preheat the oven at 350*
5)Bake!
6)Let it cool down
7)Personnaly after baking them I put them in the freezer for an 1-2 to let it freeze so its like a cookie popsicle :P.
8)ENJOY!
