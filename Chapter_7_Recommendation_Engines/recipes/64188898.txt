Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot Ramen Noodle Thai Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3103368.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup hot salsa
1/4 cup chunky peanut butter
1 cup coconut milk
2 Tbsp lime juice
1 Tbsp soy sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp white sugar
2 Tbsp grated fresh ginger
2 tsp Sriracha chili sauce
2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
2 (3 ounce) packages ramen noodles
1 cup snap peas, cut in half
1/2 cup chopped peanuts, for topping

Directions:
Combine the salsa, peanut butter, coconut milk, lime juice, soy sauce, garlic, sugar, ginger and chili sauce in the crock of a slow cooker; mix well. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces and place the chicken in the sauce, spooning mixture over chicken to coat.
Place the lid on the cooker and set to LOW. Cook until chicken is very tender and sauce has thickened, 4 to 4 1/2 hours. Add ramen noodles (without spice mix) and snap peas and cook 1/2 hour Top with peanuts  before serving.
