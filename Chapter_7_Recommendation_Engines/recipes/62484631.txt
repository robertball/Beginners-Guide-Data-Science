Title:
Doubletree Post Oak Christmas Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks Butter, softened
1 cup Sugar
3/4 cup Brown sugar
4 Eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla
4 cups All-Purpose Flour
1/3 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 cup Rolled Oats
3/4 tsp. Soda
1 tsp. Lemon Juice
1 1/2 lbs White Chocolate Chips (use good quality)
8 oz. Craisins
8 oz Pecans, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°.
Cream butter & sugar together.  Add eggs & vanilla.  Beat at low speed.
In a separate bowl, mix flour, salt, cinnamon, oats, & baking soda.  Slowly fold flour mixture into butter mixture.
Add lemon juice and blend well. Fold in craisins, white chocolate chips & pecans.
Drop by rounded teaspoons (or tablespoon for large cookies) onto lightly greased cookie sheet.
Bake 12 to 15 minutes.
