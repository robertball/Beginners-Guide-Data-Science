Title:
French Onion Stuffed Mushroom Caps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 large fresh mushrooms
6 tablespoons butter or margarine
3/4 cup plain dry bread crumbs
1 envelope dry onion soup mix
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Remove stems from mushrooms caps, set caps aside and finely chop stems.
In skillet, saute' chopped mushrooms in butter until tender (approximately 6-8 minutes).
Remove from heat, stir in bread crumbs, soup mix & almonds.
Stuff bread crumb mixture into mushroom caps and place into greased 15x10x1 baking pan; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake, uncovered, at 425 degrees for 12 - 15 minutes or until tender.

Submitter:

Description:
