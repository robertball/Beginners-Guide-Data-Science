Title:
Cherry Cheesecake Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese, softened
2 tbsp sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup milk, plus 2 tbsp if needed
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 can cherry pie filling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees and prepare muffin pan with cooking spray.  Beat together the cream cheese, 2 tbsp sugar and 1 egg together until smooth. Set aside.  Pour cherry pie filling into a strainer and gently stir with a spoon until most of the glazed is strained off. Reserve the glaze for sticky tops if desiered. Set aside.
Beat together the melted butter and sugar to combine. Add the egg and beat until completly mixed in. Slowly add the milk and mix on medium-high until smooth.  In another bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder and salt. Slowly add the dry mixture to the wet and stir until just moist. Add extra milk if needed. Fold in the cherries.
Fill each muffin cup a little less than half way with batter. Spoon a heaping teaspoon of the cream cheese mixture into each cup and top w/ another teaspoon of batter.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes or untill toothpick test comes out clean.
For "sticky" tops, brush some of the strained cherry glaze on top of each muffin in the last five minutes of cooking.
