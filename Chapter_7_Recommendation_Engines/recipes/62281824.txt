Title:
Hearty Lasagne with Turkey Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 1/2 tsp coriander
1 1/2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
15 oz of part skim ricotta cheese
2 eggs
2 cloves of garlic minced
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves cut into slivers
2 medium yellow italian squash cut into thin coins
16 oz of mozzarella shredded
1/2 cup of freshly grated parmesan cheese
2 jars of spagehetti sauce
1 box of no boil lasagne noodles
dried Oregano

Directions:
In a medium bowl mix first 5 ingredients together and set aside to meld flavors- one hour to overnight.
In a medium bowl mix ricotta cheese, eggs, garlic and basil. Let rest to meld flavors while browning sausage.
Brown turkey sausage over medium heat; making sure to break up meat while cooking.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray a 9 x 13 baking pan with cooking spray.
Spread a cup of sauce on bottom of the pan. Layer with noodles.
Next, spread the entire ricotta mixture on to the noodles and on top add the layer of italian squash.
Layer on noodles and sauce.
Spread 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese and all of the parmesan cheese on top; followed by another layer of noodles and more sauce.
Add the layer of sausage followed by more noodles and sauce.
Finish with the remaining cheese and sprinkle generously with oregano.
Cover with foil and bake for 50-60 minutes.
Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and browned.
Let rest about 10 minutes before cutting and serving.

Thick lasagne with turkey sausage and rich cheese.  No boil recipe makes this super easy to make AND the Italian squash adds veggies with out altering the taste.  The leftovers are even better!
