Title:
Aunt Bonnie's Ham Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp ground cloves
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp flour
2 tbsp vinegar
1/2 cup fruit juice (orange juice preferred)

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together in a medium sauce pan over medium high heat until the sugar is fully dissolved and the sauce thickens and is like syrup. Pour over slices of ham.

Submitter: 
Kevin Gorton
Description: 
This recipe comes from my husband's Aunt Bonnie. Its a spicy sweet sauce that is the perfect compliment and finish to any Easter ham.
