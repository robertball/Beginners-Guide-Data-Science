Title:
Kalbase (Sauerkraut & Sausage) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 or 2 stalks of celery, diced
1 small head of cabbage
1 medium onion, diced
1 lg jar of sauerkraut
2 jars of water
1 stick of butter
3 cloves garlic, minced
dash or two of parsley flakes
dash of pepper
dash or two of Mrs. Dash
1 1/2 TBL brown sugar
1 pkg polish sausage, cut into 2" pieces

Directions:
Dump all into big pot. Cook and simmer 3 hours.
You may add some country style pork ribs.
