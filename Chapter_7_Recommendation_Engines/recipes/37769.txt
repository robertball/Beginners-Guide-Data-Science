Title:
Chicken Parmesan Pasta Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces linguine, uncooked
2 (6 ounce) packages LOUIS RICH Grilled Chicken Breast Strips
1 cup KRAFT Shredded Parmesan Cheese
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed on package, drain. Return to pan.
Toss with remaining ingredients.
