Title:
SEAFOOD SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PACKAGE FLAKE STYLE IMITATION CRAB
1/2 C MAYO
1 TABLESPOON LEMON JUICE
1 CHOPPED HARD BOILED EGG
1/4 TSP SUGAR
2 TBLS SWEET PICKLE RELISH
1/4 C FINLEY CHOPPED GREEN ONIONS
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
PAPRIKA

Directions:
REMOVE MEAT FROM PACKAGE AND FLAKE WITH A FORK. ADD ALL INDGREDIENTS, ENDING WITH THE SUGAR. SPRINKLE WITH A LITTLE PAPRIKA AND SERVE.

Description: 
THIS IS A GOOD DISH BY ITSELF, BUT WORKS WELL ON SANDWICHES.
