Title:
Marshmallow Coal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755609.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16oz bag of marshmallows (not minis)
1 pkg of chocolate bark
1 tsp vegetable oil
10 Oreo cookies

Directions:
Melt chocolate bark in the microwave for 1 minute, stir and continue microwaving in 30 second intervals until melted. Add 1t of vegetable oil to keep chocolate glossy when it hardens.
Finely crush Oreo cookies in a food processor and place in a shallow dish.
Dip each marshmallow in the melted chocolate until fully coated then roll in crushed Oreos.
Set on wax paper to cool and harden.
