Title:
Menage a Trios au Poulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lg Whole Chicken 4-5lbs or Chicken parts
2 Qts. Water or broth
1 Bay Leaf
4 Cloves Garlic(whole)
1 Whole Clove
3 Carrots(1/2" slice)
3 Ribs Celery(1/2"slice)
1 Lg.Onion(1/2 dice)
6 Mushrooms(quartered)optional
1 Stick Butter
2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. White Pepper
1/2 tsp. Cayenne Pepper
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1 Tbl. Herb de Provence
1 tsp. Tarragon(dried)
1 tsp. Rosemary(dried)
1/2 C. Flour
1/2 C. Milk or Cream
1 Pkg. Buttermilk Bicuits(8 pieces)

Directions:
Put chicken,water,bayleaf,garlic and clove in a large dutch oven and simmer covered 1 hr. Remove chicken and let cool. Strain broth and reserve. Saute carrots,celery,onion and mushrooms in butter for about 10 min. Stir in flour and cook for about 3-5 min.on med. heat until flour cooks a little. Add 2 cups broth and stir until smooth. Add rest of broth along with the peppers, paprika and herbs. Simmer for 45 min.uncovered. Check for salt; then add chicken and milk.Put biscuits on top of mixture and bake uncovered in a 400f. oven for 20 min.Cover and bake an additional 15 min.
