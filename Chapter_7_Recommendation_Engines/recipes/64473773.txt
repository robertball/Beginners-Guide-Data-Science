Title:
Creamy Seafood Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese (softened to room temp)
1 bag of seafood mix
6 Tbs of butter
1/4 cup of milk
3 oz of chopped spring onions
1/4 lb of Linguini
1/4 cup of lime and lemon divided into 2 1/8th portions
2 Tbs garlic
1 tsp of chili pepper
2 1/8 cup of water

Directions:
1. Thaw seafood or meat out and set into a pot with 1/8 cup lemon-lime mixture, set aside.
2. Melt cream cheese and butter, slowly whisk in milk, water, and onions.
3. In a deep skillet put pasta and salty water, enough just to cover the pasta, and boil until fully cooked, drain, and set aside in the refrigerator.
4. Add 1/8 cup of water, 1 tsp chili pepper, and 2 Tbs of garlic to the seafood (if you use different meat brown the meat with the seasoning, first).
5. Once meat is fully cooked slowly add to the cream and cook on a low simmer.
6. Combine the cram, pasta, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kitty
Description: 
This is a whatever was left in the fridge, recipe, but it aims to please! You can use whatever meat you like, but I would go with seafood.
