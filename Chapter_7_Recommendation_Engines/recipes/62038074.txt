Title:
Baked Potatoes For a bunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large baking potatoes
1 large bunch green onions, chopped
"Squeeze" butter such as Parkay
1/2 lb bacon, fried and crumbled or Bacos
Salt and Pepper
2 cups cheddar cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
Bake your potatoes like normal.  Remove from oven and cut into large chunks.  Place cut up potatoes in greased casserole dish.  Squeeze butter over potatoes to taste.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Top potatoes with cheese, bacon or Bacos and green onions.  Bake at 350 until throughly heated, about 30 - 50 minutes.  Top individual servings with sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
CATMANIACS
Description: 
Casserole version of baked potatoes. Great party dish that goes well with most meats.  Always a hit!
