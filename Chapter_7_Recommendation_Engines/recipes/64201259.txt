Title:
Semi's Stuffed Pork Chops with Onion Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1577689.jpg
Ingredients:
4	thick cut boneless pork chops
1	to 1 ½ cups stuffing (I used stove top pork stuffing mix)
2	tablespoons butter
1	tablespoon olive oil
½	cup seasoned flour
1	teaspoon Onion powder
1	teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Onion Gravy
1	large onion chopped or thinly sliced
1	tablespoon leftover seasoned butter
3	to 4 tablespoons flour
1	teaspoon chicken base
3 	to 4 cups chicken broth
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut a pocket in the chops large enough to fit ¼ cup of stuffing.  Salt and pepper  both sides and inside of pork chops.
Stuff pork chops with ¼ cup stuffing per chop, using a toothpick to close chop.
Dredge chops in seasoned flour and put straight into a hot skillet with 2 tablespoons butter and 1 tablespoon olive oil. Brown on both sides and move to a baking dish.  Cover and bake at 350 for 18 minutes.
Meanwhile in same skillet add 1 tablespoon butter and onion; cook over medium heat until onions are done.  add flour and stir cook 1 one more minute.  Using a whisk start slowly adding the chicken broth; stirring/whisking constantly.  Keep adding broth until you get the gravy consistency you like.  Add the chicken base/bouillon stirring until it is incorporated.  Taste and adjust seasonings.  Serve over pork chops.
