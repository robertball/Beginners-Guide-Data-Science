Description: 
This signature cocktail combines gin, pear brandy, lemon juice, orange juice, and simple syrup. A quick shake is all you need. Then strain the cocktail into a chilled Collins glass and garnish with an orange slice. It's a refreshing signature cocktail at JBird Cocktails in New York City.
