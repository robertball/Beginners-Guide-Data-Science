Title:
Oatmeal Banana Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c plant milk
1 c rolled oats (uncooked)
2 bananas (or you can use 1 c applesauce)
1/2 tsp guar gum (opt)
1/2 c brown sugar (not packed)
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 c walnuts, coursely chopped
1 T ground flax (opt)

Directions:
Preheat oven 425 degrees.
Soak oats in milk 15 minutes in medium bowl
Add banana to oats
In large bowl, mix remainder of ingredients
Add wet to dry ingredients, mixing just til moistened
Fill 12 muffin tins
Bake 25 minutes
