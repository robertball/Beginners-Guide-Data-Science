Title:
Apple Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar (28 ounce) fried apples, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup golden raisins
2 Tablespoons cider vinegar
1/4 or 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger, the amount that you like
1 or 2 dashes of cloves

Directions:
In medium saucepan put in choped apples, raisins, vinegar, ginger and cloves.  Heat through, stirring occasionally and serve warm with ham or pork.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
This is a simple chutney to make and put over ham or pork.
