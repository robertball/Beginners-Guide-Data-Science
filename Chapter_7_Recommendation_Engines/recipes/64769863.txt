Title:
Sausage potato casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7447407.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound sausage (hot or mild)
1 c Sour Cream
1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
2 c Cheddar Cheese
6 eggs
28 oz Hash browns w/onions & peppers (thawed)
Salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven 350
Brown Sausage and drain. Crumble
Mix - eggs, soup, sour cream and one cup of cheese
Add hash browns
Add Sausage and mix throughly
Place mixture in a 9x11 casserole dish
Top with remaining cheese
Cook 35-45 minutes
