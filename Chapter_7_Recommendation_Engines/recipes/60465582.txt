Title:
Tiramisu Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack 8oz cream cheese softened
3 cups cold milk
2 pack (4 serv size) vanilla instant pudding nad pie filling
1 tub (8oz) whipped topping, thawed, divided
48 nilla wafers
1/2 cup brewed strong coffee, cooled, divided
2 squares of bakers semi-sweet chocolate, grated
1 cup fresh rasberries

Directions:
beat cream cheese in a large bowl with electric mixer until creamy. Gradualy beat in milk. Add dry pudding mixes; mix well. Stir in 2 cups of the whipped topping.
Line bottom and sides of a 2-1/2 qt bowl with half of the wafers; drizzle with half of the coffee. Layer half of the pudding mixture over the wafers, and then top with half of the grated chocolate.
Repeat all layers starting with th wafers and coffee.  Top with remaining whipped topping and rasberries.  Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
