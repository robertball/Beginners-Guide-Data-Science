Title:
Parmesan Chicken II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken drumsticks
1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 garlic cloves smashed or 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
1 egg
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
2 Tablespoon of flour

Directions:
Parboil the chicken legs in salted water
In a shallow bowl, mix together salt, garlic powder, pepper, and cheese.  In a separate bowl, beat egg until lemon colored.  Place flour in a bag, add the legs and shake to coat the legs with the flour. Dip chicken in egg, coating well.  Roll in cheese.
Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes or until tender or until brown.
