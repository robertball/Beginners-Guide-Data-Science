Title:
Mom's Out of this World Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
2 cans (6 oz. each) tomato paste
3 cups water
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
In a large saucepan, brown the ground beef with the onions and garlic. Drain off the fat; return to saucepan. Add remaining ingredients, and simmer for at least an hour. Serve over hot cooked pasta.

Submitter: 
larkspur
Description: 
This is my family's favorite spaghetti sauce recipe. It came from my Oma, and it is the only sauce my family will eat!
