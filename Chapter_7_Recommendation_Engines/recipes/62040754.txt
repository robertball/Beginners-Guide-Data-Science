Title:
John's Famous Italian Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce, divided
1 egg
1 teaspoon oregano, divided
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
MIX: together ground beef, bread crumbs, half the tomato sauce, egg, 1/2 teaspoon oregano, salt, and pepper.  On wax paper, pat into a rectangle 1/2 inch thick.  Sprinkle with mozzarella.
ROLL: up by starting on short side.  Lift paper until meat begins to roll tightly.  Peel back paper to keep it free.  Continue lifting and peeling to complete roll.  Seal edges and place in loaf dish.
MICROWAVE: at high for 9 minutes, rotating dish after half of the cooking time.
REDUCE: power to 50% (Medium).  Microwave 11 to 14 minutes, or until meat loses most of its pink color, rotating dish after half the cooking time.  Drain.
COMBINE: remaining tomato sauce and oregano.  Pour over meatloaf.  Sprinkle with Parmesan.  Microwave 2 minutes to heat sauce.
TENT: with foil and let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Description: 
Meatloaf has never been so tasty.  This meatloaf will sure to be a family cheeser...I mean pleaser!  My wife and I sure love the taste.  It is so easy especially in the microwave.
