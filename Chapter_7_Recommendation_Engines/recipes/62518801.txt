Title:
Whipped Cream Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511360.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15.25 ounce) cans fruit cocktail, drained
2 large bananas, peeled and sliced
1 cup apples, diced
1 (10 ounce) jar maraschino cherries, halved
1 (10 ounce) jar mandarin oranges
1 cup pecans, chopped
1 pint heavy whipping cream
6 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine fruit cocktail, bananas, cherries, mandarin oranges, apples, and pecans. Reserve several maraschino cherries and pecans for garnish.
In a mixing bowl, whip the cream to soft peaks with an electric mixer. Add sugar and vanilla, and whip to combine.
Fold the whipped cream into the fruit. Garnish with maraschino cherries and pecans.
Refrigerate until served.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
This is a sweet, creamy recipe that has been handed down in our family. We have made it at Thanksgiving and Christmas for years and years.
