Title:
Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana
1/2 cup of lowfat plain yogurt
1/2 cup lowfat milk
1 tsp of vanilla
2 tsp of brown sugar
2-3 ice cubes
dash of cinnamon

Directions:
Put all ingredients above into the blender and blend until smooth.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Melissa Rapoza Fragoza
Description: 
I have this banana smoothie for breakfast most mornings.  It's a delicious and lowfat way to start the day.
