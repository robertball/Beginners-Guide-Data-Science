Title:
Lemon Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C flour
2 tsp cornstarch
1-1/4 C sugar
5 Tbsp butter, softened
2 Tbsp grated lemon zest
1/2 C lemon juice
5 lg eggs, separated
1-1/4 C whole milk, rm temp
2 qts boiling water

Directions:
1)Mix Batter- Adjust oven rack to lowest position and heat oven to 325 degrees. Grease 8 inch square dish. Whisk flour and cornstarch in bowl. Beat 1/2 C sugar, butter and lemon zest on md until light and fluffy, about 2 min. Beat in yolks, 1 at a time, until incorporated. Reduce speed to md-low. Add flour mixture and mix until incorporated. Slowly add milk and lemon juice, mixing until just combined.
2)Beat Egg Whites- Another bowl and whisk attachment- beat egg whites on md-high until soft peaks form, about 2 min. With mixer running, slowly add remaning sugar until whites are firm and glossy, about 1 min. Whisk 1/3 of white, 1 scoop at a time, until well blended.
3)Bake- Place kitchentowel in bottom of roasting pan and arrange prepared baking dish on towel. Spoon batter into prepared dish. Carefully place on oven rack and pour boiling water into pan until water comes half way up sides of dish. Bake until surface is golden brown and edges are set (cntr should jiggle slightly when shaken), about 1 hr. Transfer to wire rack and let cool at least 1 hr. Serve.
