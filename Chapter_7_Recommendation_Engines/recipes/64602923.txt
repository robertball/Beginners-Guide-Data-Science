Title:
Glenda's Gingerbread Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3970870.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
•	3 cups all-purpose flour
•	2 teaspoon baking powder
•	1/2 teaspoon baking soda
•	1 teaspoon salt
•	2 teaspoon ground dried ginger
•	2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
•	1/2 teaspoon pie spice
•       3 eggs
•	2 teaspoons vanilla extract
•	1/2 cup molasses
•	3 cups milk
•	1/2 cup coconut oil

Directions:
Directions
1.	In a medium bowl whisk dry ingredients together and set aside. In a large mixing bowl beat the egg then add all the wet ingredients and whisk together until blended. Stir the dry mixture into the wet mixture until combined a few lumps are okay.  Let batter thicken and puff up while the oiled griddle is warming up to a medium low heat - griddle should not be smoking.
2.	Drop batter by the ladle full onto the preheated griddle, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry. Flip, and cook until cake springs back to the touch.
