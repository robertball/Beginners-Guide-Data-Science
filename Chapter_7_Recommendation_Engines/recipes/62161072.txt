Title:
Taste like store brought HERSHEY'S CHOC SYRUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c cocoa
1 c sugar
1 c water
1 t vanilla
and a dash of salt

Directions:
Combine cocoa, sugar and salt ...mix well(in a sauce pan). Slowly add water and mix till smooth and bring to boil. Allow it to boil for 1 min after it becomes a rolling boil. If boils over it is messy so please watch closely. remove from heat after that minute ...when cooled add vanilla . It may look runny but will thicken as it cools .
