Title:
English Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sweet cream butter
2 cups sugar
12 oz. fine chopped pecans or almonds
4 square's sim. sweet chocolate

Directions:
Melt butter, when bubbley add sugar and 2/3 of the nuts. Cook on high heat stirring constantly until candy thermometer reaches 310 deg.
Pour in a cookie sheet and let set. Spread melted chocolate over candy and sprinkel with the rest of nuts. After it sets break into pieces.
