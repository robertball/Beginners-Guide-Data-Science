Title:
Spanish Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5787933.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb baby spinach
2 tbsp olive oil
Â½ cup raisins
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 dash ground ginger

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
Add the spinach to the pan and cook until it begins to wilt.
Add the garlic and ginger to the pan and mix well.
Stir in the raisins and continue cooking for 2-3 minutes.
Transfer to a serving bowl and enjoy!
