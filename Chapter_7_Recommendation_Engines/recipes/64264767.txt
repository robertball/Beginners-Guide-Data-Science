Title:
Walnut Snowballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup real butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup dark brown sugar
2 tsp real vanilla extract
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups flour
1 cup finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 375 degrees
2) Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla until fluffy.
3) Sift flour and salt together; add to creamed mixture.
4) Mix well; stir in walnuts.
5) Shape dough into walnut-sized balls.
6) Bake on a non-stick foil lined cookie sheet at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
7) Remove from cookie sheet.
8) When still warm but cool enough to handle, roll in powdered sugar.
9) When all cookies have been rolled in powdered sugar, roll them all a second time.
