Title:
Nana V's  Company Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
Sliced fresh fruit of your choice. Strawberries, blueberries and ripe peaches are our favorites!

Directions:
Prepare cake mix as directed on package. Let cake cool before frosting.
For the frosting: In a large bowl, beat softened cream cheese until smooth, then fold in whipped topping and mix until smooth. Spread on top of cooled cake.
Top with sliced fresh fruits, such as strawberries, kiwi, peaches, pineapple, blueberries, etc.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
My grandmother would whip this up whenever company dropped by in the summer, or for family dinners on Sunday afternoon. Top with sliced fresh fruit.Modified from: Nana V's  Company Cake
