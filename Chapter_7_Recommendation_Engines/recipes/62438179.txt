Title:
Pumpkin frozen yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can pumpkin
1 1/2 cups nonfat Greek yogurt
1/2 cup maple syrup
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp ground cloves

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients together in a large bowl. Refrigerate until chilled. Freeze following the directions of your ice cream maker.
