Title:
Broccoli Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/836301.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head broccoli flowerets
1 head cauliflower flowerets
1/4 cup purple onion, chopped
1/4 cup bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
----------------
Dressing:
1/4 cup sugar
2 TBSP apple cider vinegar
1 cup mayonaise (not Miracle Whip)

Directions:
Pour dressing over salad ingredients and mix well.  Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes or more before serving.
