Title:
Dracula's Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clear surgical rubber glove
2 cup(s) pomegranate juice
4 cup(s) chilled apple cider
1 cup(s) golden rum
2 cup(s) chilled lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Rinse out surgical glove and fill with pomogranate juice, tie and freeze overnight.
Pour ciderand run into a punch bowl. Add lemon-lime soda.
Cut glove off pomegranate ice hand; place in punch bowl. As hand melts, color will bleed into punch.
