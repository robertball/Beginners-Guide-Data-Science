Title:
Easy Veggie Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz pg. Vegetable Pasta spirals
1 4 oz. jar of diced pimentos drained
1 16 oz. pkg. Garden Blend Fiesta style Vegetables
1 16 oz. pkg. California Blend vegetables
1 small can of Black olives sliced
Any light Italian dressing

Directions:
Boil pasta according to directions, drain well when done.
While pasta is cooking Steam the frozen veggies or steam in microwave.
Drain your olives and pimento and set in a Large mixing bowl.
Now add your veggie pasta and vegetables .
Drizzle 3.5 oz. of dressing over mixture and toss to coat.
Cover & Refrigerate for an hour give it a toss and taste to see if you should add more dressing.
Best if marinated for 24 hours but can be eaten warm.

Submitter: 
Sonya
Description: 
Loads of Veggies in this easy to throw together Pasta salad. The longer you let it marinate the better it tastes.
