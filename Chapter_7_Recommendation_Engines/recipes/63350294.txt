Title:
Fresh Apple Cranberry Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/928227.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups chopped Granny Smith apples, peeled or unpeeled
2 cups fresh cranberries
3 Tbsp flour
1 cup white sugar
3 packets of Cinnamon Spice instant oatmeal
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine apples, cranberries, 3 Tbsp flour and sugar - Toss to coat well. Place into 2-quart casserole dish.
For the topping combine oatmeal packets, walnuts, 1/2 cup flour, brown sugar, and butter. Stir well.
Spoon the topping mixture over top of the fruit mixture. Press down lightly.
Bake for 45 minutes.
