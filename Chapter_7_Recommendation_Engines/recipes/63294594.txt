Title:
Greek Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 large leaves of romaine lettuce, chopped into bite-size pieces
4-5 sprigs of purslane leaves
1 cucumber, chopped into half circles
1/2 medium onion, cut into semicircular slices
3 small tomatoes, cut into wedges
or
12 cherry tomatoes, cut into halves
16 seedless black Spanish olives
1/2 cup low salt feta cheese, cut into small blocks
3 tbsp lemon juice
4 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tbsp salt
1 clove of garlic, minced
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
In a flat plate, layer the ingredients as follows: lettuce, purslane, cucumber, onion, tomato, olives, feta cheese.
In a small bowl mix dressing ingredients: olive oil lemon juice, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
Before serving, drizzle your salad with the dressing you prepared.

Submitter: 
Dana
Description: 
I love this salad. It's a perfect summer appetizer to be served for lunch or dinner. It's easy to prepare and full of flavor for those who want to eat something healthy, yet good tasting!
