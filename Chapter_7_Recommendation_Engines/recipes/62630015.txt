Title:
Chorizo with Prawns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 cloves Garlic - sliced
350g/12oz Chorizo cut into ½cm/¼in thick slices
Sea Salt
8 medium-sized Raw Prawns - shell on
60ml/2fl oz Dry Sherry (or Dry White Wine)
2-3 tbsp Flatleaf Parsley - chopped

Directions:
Heat a frying pan until medium hot, add the olive oil and garlic and fry for 1-2 minutes;
Add the chorizo and fry for a further 1-2 minutes
Add pinch sea salt and prawns and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the prawns are just pink;
Add the sherry (or wine) and deglaze the pan;
Pour everything into a serving dish and garnish with parsley
Serve with crusty bread to mop up the juices

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A great little Tapas, combining 2 of my favourite things. Tasty, a touch piquanty and full of flavour.
