Title:
Chicken Wings with Oyster Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken wings (divided in 2 pieces, discard ends)
1-1/2 Tbsp soy sauce
1/2 lb broccoli
1/4 tsp salt
1-1/2 Tbsp sesame oil
1/4 cup oil (for frying)
1 scallion, cut in 2 inch lengths
1 slice ginger, cut thin
1 clove garlic, minced
1 Tbsp sherry
1 cup water
2 Tbsp oyster sauce
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp cornstarch, dissolved in 1 tsp water

Directions:
Marinate chicken wings in soy sauce for 10 minutes, coating evenly.  Set aside.
Break broccoli into flowerets, cutting stems into 2" lengths.  Blanch in boiling water 1 minutes.  Rinse with cold water and drain.  Sprinkle with salt and 1 Tbsp sesame seed oil.  Set aside.
Heat oil to 400 degrees F in wok.  Stir fry chicken wings until golden brown.  Drain and set aside.
Reheat 1 Tbsp of oil.  Stir fry scallion, ginger and garlic until there is an aroma, about 1 minute.  Add chicken wings, sherry and water and bring to a boil.  Cover and cook at 250 degrees F for 15 minutes.  Add oyster sauce, sugar and 1/2 Tbsp sesame seed oil.  Stir fry 1 minutes at 425 degrees F.  Thicken with dissolved cornstarch.
Spoon wings onto platter.  Arrange broccoli around them.
