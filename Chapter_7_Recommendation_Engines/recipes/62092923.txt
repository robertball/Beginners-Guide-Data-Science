Title:
Super Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups orange juice
2 cups frozen strawberries
1 cup vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Put the orange juice and strawberries in the blender and blend until smooth.
Add yogurt and blend until incorporated. Serve.
