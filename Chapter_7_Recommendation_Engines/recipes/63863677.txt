Title:
Chicken fried Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 beef cube steaks
1/2 c flour
1/2 TSP pepper
1/2 TSP salt
1 egg
1 1/2 TSP hot pepper sauce
1/2 TSP garlic powder

Directions:
Put salt pepper and garlic powder in flour to dredge steak. add hot pepper sauce to egg and beat in bowl large enough to dip steak. dip steak in egg and dredge in flour. coat evenly and generously.
put in oven bake 350 for 45 minutes until done.
