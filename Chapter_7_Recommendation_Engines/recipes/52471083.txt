Title:
Unbelievable Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cider vinegar
2 tT prepared coarse-ground mustard
1 T dijon
3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
1 lime, juiced
1/2 lemon, juiced
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. salt
ground black pepper to taste
6 T olive oil
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves,
pounded

Directions:
In a large glass bowl, mix the cider vinegar, mustards, garlic, lime juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Whisk in the olive oil. Place chicken in the mixture. Cover, and marinate 8 hours, or overnight.
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Lightly oil the grill grate. Place chicken on the prepared grill, and cook 3 to 4 minutes per side, until juices run clear. Discard marinade.
