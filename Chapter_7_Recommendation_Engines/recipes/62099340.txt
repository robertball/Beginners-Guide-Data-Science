Title:
Beef and Veggie Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound stewing beef
3 small or 2 large onions
4 cloves garlic
1 zucchini
3 stalks of celery
6 large carrots
6 potatoes
2 tbsp taco seasoning
8 cups water
3 beef bouillion cubes
1 vegetable bouillion cube
oregano, salt and pepper to taste
flour to thicken
handfull whole peppercorns
2 bay leaves

Directions:
In large stock pot brown beef. Season meat with a little salt and some pepper. Add onions, carrots and potatoes and cook until onions are tender. (Only add 2 if using small onions or 1 if using large onions).
Add taco seasoning bay leaves, oregano and peppercorns and mix well. Stir in 3 tbsp flour and cook for a minute.
Add 7 cups of water, bring to a boil.  Add bouillion cubes and allow to simmer for an hour and a half.
In the last hour of cooking, add the rest of the onion, the garlic, zucchini and celery. Bring to a boil. Continue to cook until the veggies and meat are tender.
In a jar mix some flour and a cup of cold water together and add it to the pot.  Stir until thickened.  (add as much flour as needed to obtain desired thickness.).
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
