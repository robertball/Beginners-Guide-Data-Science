Title:
Romaine with Avocado Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium avocado, peeled and cubed
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup vegetable oil
3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
2 large garlic cloves, peeled
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp hot pepper sauce
1 bunch romaine lettuce, torn
3 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
1 cup shredded extra-old cheddar cheese
1 can sliced ripe olives, drained
4 medium green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:
To make dressing, place the first seven ingredients in a food processor.  Cover and process until blended.
In a large bowl, combine the remaining ingredients.  Drizzle with dressing to coat.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
Absolutely delicious salad that my mother shared with me.  A wonderful accompainment to a light meal of fish or chicken, or could be used on it's own as a luncheon salad.
