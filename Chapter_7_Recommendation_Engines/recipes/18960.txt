Title:
Pineapple-Orange Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pints vanilla ice cream
1 pint orange sherbet
7 cups pineapple juice
3 cups ginger ale

Directions:
In a punch bowl combine ice cream, sherbet, pineapple juice and ginger ale.

Submitter: 
JOYCEJJ111
Description: 
Ice cream, orange sherbet, pineapple juice and ginger ale makes for a really great punch! Double this recipe for a large punch bowl.
