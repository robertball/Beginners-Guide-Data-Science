Title:
Cheesy Spaghetti Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1 small garlic clove, crushed
2 tbsp margarine
2 tbs flour
1/4 tsp pepper
2/3 cup water
1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 cups spaghetti sauce (29oz jar)
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/3 cup carnation evaporated milk
1 cup shredded American cheese

Directions:
Brown beef, onion and garlic in skillet.  Drain fat.  Add spaghetti sauce, simmer, uncovered 10 minutes.  Break spaghetti into thirds.  Cook, rinse and drain.  Met butter in small saucepan; stir in four, salt and pepper.  Gradually add evaporated milk and water.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, just till mixture comes to a boil and thickens.  Add 1/2 cup American cheese and parmesan cheese; stir til melted.  Combine spaghetti and sauce mixture.  Spread half spaghetti mixture in bottom of 13 X 9 pan.  Spoon cheese sauce over spaghetti, top with remaining spaghetti.  Sprinkle with 1/2 cup American cheese.  Bake at 325 degrees for 20 minutes or till bubbly.
