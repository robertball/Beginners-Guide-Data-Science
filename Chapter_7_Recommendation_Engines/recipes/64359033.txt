Title:
Basic Taco Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion chopped
2 cloves garlic chopped
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup tomato paste
taco seasoning packet
12 corn tortillas, warmed
1/2 head small lettuce, shredded
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1/2 package shredded cheese
Salsa

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook until beginning to soften, 3 to 4 minutes.
Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
Stir in beef, tomato paste, and taco seasoning. Cook, breaking up with a wooden spoon, until browned, 8 to 10 minutes.
Fill each tortilla with the meat mixture, lettuce, tomatoes, & cheese. Serve with the salsa.
For a more "mexican" style taco, cook chicken with taco seasoning and shred. serve with sauteed/slightly charred onions, cilantro, lime, and salsa.

Description: 
Highly customizable taco. Serve with beans, rice, and/or chips and salsa.
