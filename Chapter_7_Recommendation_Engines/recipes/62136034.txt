Title:
Uncle John's Chicken Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oil
1 cup vinegar
1 1/2 tsp Poultry seasoning
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 egg

Directions:
Beat egg well then add ingredients. Keep putting on chicken with a paint brush as it cooks on the grill.

Submitter: 
Mel
Description: 
This is the sauce for the chicken you get at chicken bar-b-ques held alot of times by fire stations.
