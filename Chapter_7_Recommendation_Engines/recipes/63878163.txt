Title:
Big Ray's Crusted Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075841.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 Tbsp.  lemon juice
25 butter crackers, finely crushed (about 1 cup)
1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
2 tsp. dried basil leaves
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Heat oven to 400°F.
Mix mayo and lemon juice in bowl; set aside. Combine cracker crumbs, cheese and basil in separate bowl.
Dip chicken in mayo mixture, then roll in crumb mixture until evenly coated on all sides. Place on baking sheet.
Bake 20 min. or until chicken is done (165°F).

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick dip in a mayo-lemon juice mix and a roll in crushed butter crackers could make this Crusted Parmesan chicken breast recipe a new family favorite.
