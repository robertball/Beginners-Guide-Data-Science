Title:
Creamsicle Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz Triple Sec
1 oz Galliano
1 oz White Creme de Cacao
6 oz Whole Milk
Orange Peel and/ Orange Wheel for garnish

Directions:
Mix all liquid ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake vigorously.
Pour into a martini glass and garnish with a twisted orange peel (Zest only, no pith) or an orange wheel.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
A perfect summertime treat for adults. Brings back fond memories of the corner store on a sunny afternoon.
