Title:
Banana Pudding Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
35 Reduced fat Vanilla wafers
1/2 stick margarine, melted
8 oz. Philly low fat cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup powdered sugar
8 oz. lite whipped topping, divided
3-4 bananas, sliced
2 pkgs. vanilla sugar-free instant pudding
2 cups cold low fat milk
1/4 tsp. banana flavoring, if desired
1/2 square semi-sweet chocolate, grated

Directions:
Place vanilla wafers into food processor and process until finely chopped.  Place into a medium bowl.  Pour the melted margarine over the crumbs and mix with a fork until well combined.  Press into the bottom of a 9x13" baking dish and refrigerate.
Prepare the pudding with the milk; add the banana flavoring (if desired) and whisk for a couple of minutes.  Set aside.
Mix the cream cheese and powdered sugar in a bowl until well blended.  Stir in 1 1/2 cups of whipped topping and stir with a whisk until blended; spread over the crust.  Top with the sliced bananas.
Spread the pudding over the bananas and top with remaining whipped topping.  Grate the chocolate over the top.  Refrigerate unti serving.

Low sugar, low fat, good diabetic dessert.
