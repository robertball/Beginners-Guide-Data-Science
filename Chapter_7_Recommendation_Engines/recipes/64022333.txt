Title:
Easy Hollandaise Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg yolks
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup butter
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp. Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Melt butter until hot but not browned.
Place all other ingredients in blender and blend until well mixed.
With blender running VERY SLOWLY pour butter in a steady stream.
Serve immediately.
