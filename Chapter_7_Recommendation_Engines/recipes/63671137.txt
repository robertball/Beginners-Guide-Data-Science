Title:
Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup salt
1/2 cup Splenda
2 tablespoons lemon pepper
2 tablespoons ground black pepper
2 tablespoons paprika
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Place salt, white sugar, lemon pepper,  ground black pepper, paprika, chili powder and onion powder in a medium container. Seal container and shake vigorously to mix. Use as desired when grilling spareribs.
