Title:
Borderline Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/589793.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. flank or skirt steak
Large resealable plastic bag
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar or red wine vinegar
1/3 cup olive oil
1/3 cup tequila or lemon juice
Juice of 1 lime
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. chipotle pepper powder or chili powder
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. salt
Mesquite chips (for smoking packet)
Flour tortillas
Grilled onions and peppers
Pico de Gallo

Directions:
Place steak in bag.
Mix vinegar, olive oil, tequila, lime juice, garlic powser, chipotle peppper poder, onion powder and salt in a small bowl; Pour over steak.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour or overnight.
Preheat grill to medium-high heat using mesquite chips.
Remove steak from bag; grill 8 to 10 minutes per side or until done.
Remove and let meat rest several minutes; slice into thin strips.
Serve with grilled flour tortillas, grilled onions and peppers and pico de gallo

Submitter: 
Lance Hinkle
Description: 
This is a great fajita recipe.  If you prefer to use chicken instead of steak, feel free
