Title:
Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint of grape tomatoes
1 medium cucumber
1/2 red onion
1/4 cup chopped parsley
Salt & pepper to taste
1/4 cup Extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp red wine vinegar
8 oz flavored feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Cut the tomatoes in half and place in a large bowl. Dice up the cucumber to 1/2 inch cubes and add with the tomatoes. Slice the onion thinly and roughly chop and then add to the bowl.
In a small bowl, combine the olive oil, red wine vinegar, and salt and pepper.
Pour the dressing over the cucumbers, tomatoes, onion and parsley and toss to coat. Add the Feta cheese and toss lightly to coat.

Description: 
This yummy summer salad is a tasty side dish to any meal. It can easily be adjusted to meet your taste buds! Enjoy!
