Title:
Southern Pea & Pecan Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982634.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Shredded Cabbage
1 (10 ounce) package Frozen petite Peas, thawed and drained
4 Green Onions, thinly sliced
1/2 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon Curry powder
1 cup Pecans

Directions:
In large bowl combine peas, cabbage and onions; set aside.
In a small bowl, blend slaw dressing, mustard and curry powder.  Mix dressing and vegetables with 1/2 cup pecans.  Garnish with remaining pecans.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
Simple and delicious, this slaw combines a variety of flavors and textures.  Probably originated in Mississippi region... Louisiana and Arkansas are famous for their pecans.
