Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2011514.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon butter melted
1 medium onion chopped
1/2 cup butter melted
1/2 cup flour
2 cup half and half
1 cup milk
3 cups chicken broth
2 cup broccoli chopped
1 cup carrots julienned
3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
12 oz sharp cheddar cheese grated (ok to reduce to 8oz)
Salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Saut√© onion in 2 tbl butter and set aside.
In large pot add 1/2 c butter and 1/2 c flour, whisk together and cook gently for 3-4 minutes.
Slowly whisk in the half and half, milk and chicken broth. Let simmer about 20 minutes.
Add broccoli, carrots and onions. Let simmer on medium low for 25 minutes until broccoli and carrots are tender.
Add nutmeg and salt and pepper and cheddar cheese. Continue to heat on low until cheese is melted, stirring often.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
Adapted from a copycat recipe for Panera's Broccoli Cheese Soup. Absolutely delicious!! I increased the size of the recipe because I wanted more leftovers. I have added shortcut suggestions for a quick weeknight supper.
