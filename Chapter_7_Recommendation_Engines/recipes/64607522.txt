Title:
Gourmet Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Macaroni
4 Tbsp Butter
4 Tbsp Flour
2 Cups Milk
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Sugar
500 gm Processed Cheese cubed (Velveeta) - optional
2/3 cup Sour Cream
1 1/3 cups Cottage Cheese
2 Cups Shredded Old Cheddar Cheese
1 1/2 cups soft Bread crumbs
2 Tbsps Butter
1 tsp Paprika

Directions:
Cook and drain Macaroni and place in a greased 9 X 13 casserole dish.
Melt butter over medium heat, stir in flour stirring constantly till flour turns a pale yellow, then add milk stirring constantly until sauce thickens.
Add salt, sugar and cheese and mix well.
Mix sour cream and cottage cheese into the sauce.
Pour over the macaroni and mix well.
Sprinkle cheddar cheese and bread crumbs over the top and dot with butter.  Sprinkle the paprika as well.
Bake @ 350 for 45-50 minutes
