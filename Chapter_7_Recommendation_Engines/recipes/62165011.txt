Title:
Nalini's Dhal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup toor dahl
2 large (or 3 medium) tomatoes, finely chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 green chile, finely chopped
1 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 lemon
10 stems of fresh cilantro
1 thumb of fresh ginger, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder

Directions:
Rinse toor dahl in warm water.
Add 2 cups of water & salt to the dahl. Boil and cook over medium heat in a covered pot for 40 min.
In a skillet, add 1 teaspoon of olive oil and cumin, and heat until cumin swells and turns golden brown.
Add the turmeric, onion and ginger and saute until the onion is soft.
Add tomatoes, green chiles, cilantro and saute over medium heat for 10 min. Add the cooked dhal & simmer covered for 10 additional min.
Serve with rice. Yum!
