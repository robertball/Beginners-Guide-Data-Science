Title:
Italian Shrimp and Cherry Tomatoes Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/315017.jpg
Ingredients:
* 1 lb. spaghetti ( I like thin spaghetti for this dish)
* 1 lb. shrimp (either small or medium size)
* 2 tsp. lemon zest and juice of 1-2 lemons
* 6 cloves garlic
* 1/2 onion
* 2 green onions
* 1 pint cherry tomatoes
* 1/4 cup dry white wine
* 8 oz. can tomato sauce
* 1/4 cup heavy cream
* 1/2 bunch flat leaf Italian parsley
* about 20 fresh basil leaves
* Salt and Pepper

Directions:
1. Cook the spaghetti according to the box
2. Place shrimp in a bowl and add lemon zest, juice of 1 lemon and salt. Mix well.
3. Heat a large pan over medium heat with some olive oil. Cook shrimp until just pink and set aside. If needed add more olive oil to pan and cook the onions and garlic until soft. Add the white wine and let reduce for a couple of minutes. Then add the tomato sauce, heavy cream and tomatoes. Let cook a couple of minutes until desired thickness. Last add the herbs, salt and pepper to taste and and more lemon juice if desired. Mix in the shrimp and cooked spaghetti and cook until just heated. Then serve!!!
