Title:
Chokecherry Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. apple juice (1 pint can)
8 c. chokecherry juice
16 c. sugar
4 pkgs. (3 oz) MCP Pectin

Directions:
Put strained juices in saucepan, stir in pectin and bring to a boil.  Add pre-measured sugar and bring to boil again while continuing to stir.  Bring to full, rolling boil (until you cannot stir down the full boil).  Boil exactly 2 minutes.  Remove from heat, let sit until boiling subsides.  Skim carefully; pour into glasses.

Submitter: 
PacNWCook
Description: 
This recipe is an old pioneer recipe that leaves you craving for more.  It took alot of hard work to walk up the canyon to pick the chokecherries to make this recipe. My mom and aunt have wonderful stories about these excursions.
