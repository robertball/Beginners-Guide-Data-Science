Title:
Drunken Roasted Salsa II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large tomatoes
1 medium onion, sliced into 1/2 inch slices
3 jalapenos
1-2 large cloves garlic, unpeeled
1/2 teaspoon Mexican oregano
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 bottle of Mexican beer
1/3 bunch cilantro, chopped
salt to taste

Directions:
Roast the tomatoes, jalapenos and  garlic under the broiler on a baking sheet covered with foil.  Roast until dark brown and blistered - about 5 to 10  minutes. Keep a close on it, turning frequently with tongs.
Remove from oven and peel the garlic.  Coarsely chop everything and place in a bowl.
Add the spices, beer, cilantro and salt.
Let sit at least 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
CookBrooke
Description: 
This is my favorite salsa to serve to guests - since the roasting adds another level of flavor and therefore makes it a bit more special.  It is so easy and quick to make!
