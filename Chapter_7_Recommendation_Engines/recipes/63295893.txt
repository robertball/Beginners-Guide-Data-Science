Title:
Gluten-free Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/952737.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp. extra virgin olive oil
1-1 1/2 lb ground turkey
1 small onion, chopped fine
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbl. fresh basil, chopped
2 tsp. fresh thyme
Salt & Pepper (to taste)
1 cup frozen peas
2 cups green beans, *blanched and chopped
1 cup mushrooms, chopped
2 zucchini, chopped
2 cups crushed tomatoes
1 cup shredded mozarella
1/8 cup freshly grated parmesan

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Over medium-low heat, saute the onion in the extra virgin olive oil until translucent.
Add ground turkey and raise the heat to medium-high.  Salt and Pepper generously or to your liking.
When turkey is browned, reduce heat to medium and add garlic, basil and thyme. Stir to combine and cook for 3 minutes stirring occasionally.
Using a slotted spoon, remove turkey from pan and place into a 9x13 casserole dish.
Reserve about 2 Tbl. of liquid from pan and discard the rest.
Over medium heat and in the 2 Tbl. of liquid from pan, saute blanched beans, peas, mushrooms and zucchini.  Salt and Pepper generously or to taste.  Cook for 5 minutes and then add to the 9x13 casserole dish.
Combine with meat and then pour crushed tomatoes into dish and combine.
Sprinkle mozzarella over the casserole and then the parmesan.
Cook in preheated 350 degree oven for 20 minutes, and then let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
I had some things I needed to use in the fridge, so I decided to be brave and create my own casserole.  It turned out pretty good and my kids loved it!  This is a dish where you could substitute with different veggies and it would work great.
