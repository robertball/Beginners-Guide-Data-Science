Title:
Apple Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 apples, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 stick butter
1/2 cup walnuts
3 cups uncooked rolled oats
6 cups milk

Directions:
Layer apples in bottom of slow cooker.
Sprinkle with brown sugar, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
Dot with butter.
Scatter walnuts evenly over top.
Layer oatmeal over fruit and nuts.
Pour milk over oatmeal.  Stir together until well blended.
Cover. Cook on low for 6 hours.
