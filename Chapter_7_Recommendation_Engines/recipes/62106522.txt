Title:
Chili Beans with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of hamburger
2 cans of plain chili beans (no meat)
1 64 oz. campbells tomato juice
1 clove of minced garlic
1 small onion (chopped)
1 jar of brunos peppers (mild or nippy - depending how brave you are)
1 tsp. chili powder
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
brown the hambuger and then drain off the oil, season with the salt & pepper. Then combine all ingredients ( including all the juice from the peppers )and let simmer for 30 min.
Top with chedder cheese and maybe serve with some cornbread muffin Recipe recommeded (corn bread muffins 1) and enjoy

Submitter: 
Stephanie
Description: 
These quick and easy chili beans are fabulous! And the brunos peppers give it a nice bite!
