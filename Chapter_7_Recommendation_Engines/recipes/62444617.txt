Title:
Spaghetti Squash Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large spaghetti squash
1 packet taco seasoning
1 small onion diced
1 jalapeno diced
salt to taste as needed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Slice squash longways and remove seeds. place rind side up in oven safe pan and bake for 30 to 40 min.
Strip squash from rind with fork into short strands and set aside.
Saute jalapeno and onion until caramelized. Add squash and taco seasoning and saute for five minutes.
Salt to taste if needed and serve in your traditional taco style!

Submitter: 
jfresh
Description: 
An awesome new way to eat spaghetti squash! Great for the vegan, vegetarian, and meat-eater alike. Pair with salty fried corn tortillas, fresh salsa, and sour cream!
