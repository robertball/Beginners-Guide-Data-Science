Title:
Grilled Bacon Sushi Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 thick slices bacon
1/2 pound lean ground beef
2 fresh jalapenos
4 thin slices of prosciutto
2 sticks pepper jack cheese
BBQ rub
Your favorite BBQ sauce
1 cup fried onions

Directions:
On a sushi mat, lay the 6 uncooked slices of bacon side by side.
In a large bowl, mix the ground beef and the BBQ rub to taste.
Add the ground beef mixture in a thin layer evenly onto the bacon leaving approximately an inch of uncovered bacon on one end.
Cover the ground beef with the thin slices of prosciutto.
Slice the jalapeno into long strips and place in a line across the end.
Place the two cheese sticks in a line next to the jalapenos.
Roll tightly finishing with the uncovered end.
Grill on indirect heat at 350 F for 25 minutes.
Glaze with BBQ sauce.
Continue grilling for another 10 minutes, adding additional BBQ sauce at 5 minutes.
Remove from grill and coat evenly in dried onions.
Slice and serve with BBQ sauce (and chopsticks).

Submitter: 
The Gruntled Gourmand
Description: 
Do you love sushi but find that it's not giving you the Vitamin B (bacon and beef) that you need?  This delicious twist on sushi will provide just what the doctor ordered and will have you invited to every BBQ in your neighborhood!
