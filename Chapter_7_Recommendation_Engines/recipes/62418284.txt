Title:
Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz sour cream
1pkg taco seasoning
1 onion diced
1 jar salsa
1 8oz cream cheese(at room temp)
1pkg  shredded cheese
1 tomato, diced
1 small can minced black olives
1 large bag of tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sour cream together until creamed and then mix in the taco seasoning. Spread on large tray. Top with lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, black olives and onions. Sprinkle salas over the tray.
