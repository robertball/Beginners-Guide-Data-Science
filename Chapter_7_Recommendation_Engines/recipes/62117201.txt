Title:
Spicy chicken celery rice with sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups rice (not instant)
12 cups water
2 bay leaves
1/4 cup butter
20 peppercorns (crushed)
1 Tbsp. Onion powder
1 Tbsp. minced garlic
Picante sauce to taste
1 8 oz can of cream of celery
1 8 oz can of cream of chicken
4c water from rice boil
1tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Rice
Add all ingredients from rice list except rice in a pot and bring to a boil.
After water is boiling for a few minutes set aside about four cups of water with all ingredients from above for sauce.
Add rice to remaining water in pot.
Boil until rice is tender.
Sauce
Add all ingredients from sauce list.
Warm in a pan on stove top or microwave for 4 minutes.
Spoon rice into bowls and top with desired amount of sauce.

Submitter: 
Jason Schlauch
Description: 
This recipe is rice with a sauce. I do not really enjoy plain rice. I wanted to make a recipe that was appealing yet easy and delicious.
