Title:
Sweet Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1406224.jpg
Ingredients:
9 T dry white wine vinegar
4 cloves minced garlic
1/2 can tomato paste
1/4 c chopped fresh parsley
4 T honey
4--5 small red/yellow sweet chilies chopped
2 t arrowroot powder
2 t grated ginger
1/2 t salt
1/2 t cayenne pepper
1/2 t cinnamon
1/4 t ground allspice
Pinch ground cloves
Pinch xylitol (or sugar) to taste

Directions:
Combine vinegar, honey, and arrowroot powder in saucepan to dissolve arrowroot powder. Add remaining ingredients and bring to boil for1-2 minutes. Remove from heat.

Betsy B
Description: 
This is a great dipping sauce with seafood!! Seasoning amounts are a best-guess. I add as I go.
