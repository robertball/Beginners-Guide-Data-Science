Title:
Chicken Noodle Casserole by Deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken slow cooked
1 stalk celery
11/2 cup carrots
2 Tbsp minced  onion
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tablespoon olive or canola oil
1/2 teaspoon dried savory
4 tablespoons butter or stick margarine
4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
chicken broth from cooking chicken
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
2 eggs
1/4 c Greek yogurt
1 1/2 cups 2% milk
1 1/4 cups shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
8 ounces wide egg noodles, cooked and drained
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
In a large nonstick skillet, saute onion and garlic in oil until tender.  melt butter. Stir in the flour, salt and pepper until smooth.  Mix 2 eggs, yogurt and mix together.  Gradually add milk. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Remove from the heat; stir in cheese until melted. Add cut up chicken, carrots, celery and peas.  Add noodles; mix well.
Transfer to a 3-qt. baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until bubbly.
