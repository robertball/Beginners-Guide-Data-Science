Title:
Cookies and Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of chocolate chip cookies
Large container of whipped topping
Milk

Directions:
Dip chocolate chip cookies quickly in milk line in single layer in bottom of 9x13 pan. Apply thin layer of whipped topping repeat until all cookies and whipped topping are used alternating layers of cookies and whipped topping. Refrigerate for at least one hour or until ready to use.May be made several hours ahead just need to allow the hour to allow the cookies to soften and melt into the whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Livie's Mommy
Description: 
A sweet creamy confection that is quick to assemble and loved by kids and adults alike. With only three ingredients it is easy to have on hand.
