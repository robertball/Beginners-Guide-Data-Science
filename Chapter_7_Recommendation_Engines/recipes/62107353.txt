Title:
Tomato Orange Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter
2 cups sweet onion
2 cloves crushed garlic
3 tablespoons flour
3 14.5oz cans diced tomatoes
(or substitute for 2lbs. fresh)
4 cups chicken broth
3/4 cup orange juice
2 tsp. sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 tsp. hot sauce

Directions:
Melt butter, add onions and garlic, cook until onions are translucent (5min).  Stir in flour.
Add broth, tomatoes and OJ.  Bring soup to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes stirring occasionally.
Add sugar, salt, pepper and hot sauce.  Cool slightly.  Using an immersion blender, mix until smooth. (or desired consistancy)
Simmer for 5 more minutes, and serve!

Submitter: 
Kalex
Description: 
This is one of my favorite soups. It's my adapted version of a recipe I saw in a newspaper years ago. Perfect with grilled cheese.  It freezes really well, I've actually been told it's better after it's been in the freezer for a while.
