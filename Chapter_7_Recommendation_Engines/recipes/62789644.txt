Title:
Strawberry Pecan Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup pecan pieces
1/2 cup granulated sugar
6 cups fresh spinach
2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
3 oz. goat cheese/boursin cheese
Strawberry dressing:
3 Tbsp. olive oil
2 Tbsp. strawberry jam, warmed
2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Sugar pecans and set aside.
Arrange spinach on 6 salad plates. Scatter pecans, strawberries, and cheese on top of each.
Combine salad dressing ingredients and drizzle over each salad.
