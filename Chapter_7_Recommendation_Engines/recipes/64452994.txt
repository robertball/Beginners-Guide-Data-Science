Title:
Jerrye's Tomato Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bacon slices; 30oz Chicken Broth; 1/2 t salt; 1cup quick cook grits; 2 large ripe tomatos/choppoed; 1T green chilies; 1cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese; 3 cloves garlic chopped; 1 med. onion chopped

Directions:
Cook chopped bacon in large heavy sauce pan untill crisp; Add garlic & onion; saute till clear; add broth and salt, bring to a boil; Stir in grits, tomatos, chilies. return to boil stirring often.  Cover and simmer till grits are tender.

Submitter: 
Richard Ballenger
Description: 
Jerrye's Tomato Grits are a family favorite that is light and with fresh garden tomatoes DELICIOUS!
