Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Short Ribs Bourguignon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices thick bacon, cut into 1" pieces
2-3 pounds beef short ribs - deboned and cut into 2" cubes
1 medium onion sliced
2-3 carrots, cut into sticks
8 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 package frozen pearl onions
salt & pepper to taste
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1 cup of broth(chicken, beef, or vegetable)
1 Tablespoon tomato paste
1 cup red wine (I used Pinot Noir)
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 bay leaf
3 table spoons flour
1/2 cup cold water

Directions:
Cook bacon over medium-high heat until crispy
Remove bacon to a paper towel lined plate, leaving excess grease in pan
Blot meat dry with a paper towel then transfer to the pan with bacon grease
Sear beef cubes for 1-3 minutes on each side over medium  heat
As beef cubes are done, transfer them to the crock pot
Add bacon, onions, and carrots to the crock pot
Add broth
Sprinkle thyme, salt, and pepper over the beef and vegetables
Stir tomato paste, wine, and garlic together in a small bowl then pour over beef and vegetables
Tuck bay leaf into crock pot
Cook 6-8 hours on low or 4-5 hours on high
~30 minutes before serving time, stir together the flour and cold water
Add flour mixture to the crock pot and stir into the beef and vegetables.
Remove bay leaf and enjoy

Submitter: 
N8TE
Description: 
Beef short ribs, bacon, mushrooms, carrots, and pearl onions are cooked to perfection in a rich red wine sauce.  Adapted from a Julia Child favorite.  I served it over mashed potatoes, accompanied by Italian bread and a glass of Pinot Noir.
