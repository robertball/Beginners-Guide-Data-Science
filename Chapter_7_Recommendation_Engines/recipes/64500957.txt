Title:
Pumpkin Pie Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bananas
1 cup, Pumpkin Puree
1/3 cup, Honey
1.5 tsp, Pumpkin Pie Spice

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together well
Put in freezer and freeze, preferably overnight in a well-closed container
Enjoy!!!
