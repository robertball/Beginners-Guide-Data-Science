Title:
Penn Dutch Potato Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 fist-sized potatoes, pealed and cubed
1 stick butter
4 stalks celery, diced
2 fist-sized onions, diced
1 tbsp Bell's Seasoning
1 cup hot water
1 14oz bag seasoned stuffing

Directions:
Boil the potatoes until soft, drain and mash with butter.
Add celery, onions and seasoning and stir and set aside.
Mix stuffing with water until moistened.
Fold stuffing into potato mixture until well blended.
Cook at 325 degrees for about 1 hour (or until top is browned.)

Submitter: 
Sharon
Description: 
Very simple and hearty, just like the Penn Dutch side of the family that gave me the recipe.
