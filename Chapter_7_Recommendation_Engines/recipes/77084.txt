Title:
Beet, Bean and Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2682040.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8.25 ounce) can sliced beets, drained and diced
1 (15 ounce) can cut green beans, drained
1 large Granny Smith apple - peeled, cored and diced
1 teaspoon olive oil
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Combine the beets, green beans and apple in a serving bowl. Stir in olive oil and vinegar. Chill for 1 hour before serving. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Jodi Barney Larsen
Description: 
This brightly colored cold salad is a perfect compliment to any meal or a zesty vegetarian meal in itself.
