Title:
Milk and Cookies Ice Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5711215.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy whipping cream
1/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
12 chocolate chip cookies

Directions:
Mix heavy cream and sweetened condensed milk together in a bowl until combined.
Place 2 chocolate chip cookies in each ice pop mold. Fill with cream mixture and insert sticks.
Freeze ice pops until solid, at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Savannah Jean Simons
Description: 
Ice pops with cookies and milk all rolled into one! Use the cookies of your choice.
