Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Burgundy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon minced garlic (about 2 cloves)
2 pounds lean, boneless round steak, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
Vegetable cooking spray
3/4 cup dry red wine
3/4 cup canned no-salt-added beef broth
1 tablespoon tomato paste
1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme or 1 teaspoon dried thyme
8 ounces baby carrots (about 40 small)
1 large onion, cut into eighths
1 bay leaf
1 (8-ounce) package presliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 (12-ounce) package yolk-free medium egg noodles

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients in a large heavy-duty, zip-top plastic bag; add beef, and seal bag. Shake to coat beef.
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; place over medium-high heat until hot.
Add beef, and cook 10 minutes or until browned on all sides, stirring often.
Transfer beef to a 4-quart electric slow cooker coated with cooking spray.
Add wine and next 6 ingredients; toss well.
over and cook 6 hours on high setting or 8 to 10 hours on low setting until meat is tender and sauce thickens.
Add mushrooms 1 hour before cooking is completed.
Remove and discard bay leaf.
Stir thoroughly.
Ten minutes before sauce is done, cook noodles according to package directions.
Place 1/2 cup noodles on each of 8 individual serving plates.
Spoon beef mixture evenly over hot noodles.
