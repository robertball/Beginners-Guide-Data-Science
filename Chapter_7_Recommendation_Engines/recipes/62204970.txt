Title:
Beef Scallion Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb sirloin
8 scallion
1 1/2 T oil
2 T sake
2 T soy sauce
1 T sugar

Directions:
Cut beef into 2" lengths and 15 bundels, slice very thin and pound thin, roll around a few scallions
Add oil to skillet adn brown beef rolls then add rest and cook till beef is done
Reduce sauce to a glaze.
