Title:
Cream of Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups tomato juice or whole canned tomatoes you puree yourself
1/2-1 cup homemade yogurt
honey or saccharin to taste

Directions:
Make a paste with the yogurt and 1/4 cup tomato juice.
Slowly stir in the remaining juice.
Heat over low heat or in a double boiler, stirring occasionally.
Season to taste with salt, pepper, sweetener, and herbs of choice.
