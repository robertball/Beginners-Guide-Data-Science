Title:
Roasted Asparagus with Sea Salt and Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1362546.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
1/2 C shredded parmesan cheese
3 Tbsp butter, melted
1 tsp minced garlic
3/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp Diamond Crystal® Coarse Sea Salt
Juice and zest of 1 lemon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Cut trimmed asparagus into approximately 1 1/2" pieces.  Place asparagus pieces in large bowl and add parmesan, butter, garlic, black pepper, Diamond Crystal® Coarse Sea Salt, lemon juice, and lemon zest in small bowl; toss to coat.
Transfer asparagus to baking dish, spread out to single layer.  Bake for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter:

Description: 
Roasted asparagus makes for a simple yet elegant side dish on any holiday table but is just as easy and delicious for a weeknight family meal!
