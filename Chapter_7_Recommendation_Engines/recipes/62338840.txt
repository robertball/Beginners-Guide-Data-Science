Title:
Fried egg sandwhich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 or 2 fried eggs
mustard
butter
2 pieces of toast

Directions:
1. Fry the eggs until hardin the center.
2. Right before the eggs are finished, toast the bread.
3. Put butter and mustard on toast
4. Put egg(s) on the toast and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
A delicious hot sandwhich that you can have for breakfast, lunch or supper!
