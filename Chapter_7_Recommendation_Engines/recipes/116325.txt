Title:
Herbed Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/8150423.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups uncooked noodles
1 tablespoon butter or stick margarine
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
1/4 teaspoon minced chives
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a saucepan, cook noodles according to package directions; drain and return to saucepan. Add the remaining ingredients; toss well to coat.

Submitter: 
Edie  Farm
Description: 
This simple dish is a complement to just about any kind of meat of fish. We enjoy it often, no matter what the season.
