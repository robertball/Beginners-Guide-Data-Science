Title:
Rainbow Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-2/3 cups uncooked rice, preferably Jasmine
3-1/3 cups water
1 large red pepper finely chopped
1  large green pepper finely chopped
1 small red onion chopped
1 large ear corn boiled and husked or 1 cup canned corn
3 tablespoon butter divided
2 tablespoons plum sauce (optional)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Rinse rice thoroughly under cold running water
In a medium saucepan bring water to a boil. Add rice and tablespoon of butter and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 30 minutes or until craters form in rice.
While rice is cooking saute red and green peppers with a tablespoon of butter on medium heat for 10 minutes. Add onions and cook for another 10 minutes or until onions become translucent but not browned.
Combine cooked rice, corn, sauteed mixture, plum sauce and table spoon butter. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Note: Add grilled shrimp or chicken to make a main dish!

Submitter: 
Jolanta
Description: 
Quick, easy and colourful This delicious side dish goes well with just about any poultry dish.
