Title:
River Vally Swedish MeatBalls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crumbled rye crackers or bread
1/3 cup milk
1 pound lean ground beef
1 pound lean ground pork
2 egge
1/2 cup finely diced onion
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon cayenne or to taste
1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
Sauce:
4 tablespoons butter
5 tablespoons w/w flour
31/2 cups beef broth
1/2 tsp ground allspice
1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup parsley
2 tablespoons sour cream

Directions:
In a small bowel mix the crumbled crackers or bread and milk. Set aside. In a large bowel, mix the ground meats, eggs, onion,spices, salt/pepper and bread mixure. Roll into balls about the size of a walnut or scoop out with a tablespoon and place them in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Spray a large backing sheet with oil and place them without touching each other. Bake for 15 minutes. In the meantime melt butter and flour stirring continuously for a few minutes. Slowly whisk in broth,garlic power, worcestershire sauce and bring to a simmer. Cook till sauce had thickened and reduced. Add salt and pepper to taste and stir in the sour cream and parsley. Add the meatballs to the sauce and server warm over noodles or potatoes.

Great little meatballs to eat alone or over potatoes.
