Title:
Carne Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb round steak (1 inch cubes)
Salt/Pepper
3 tbsp butter
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 yellow onion (chopped)
3 tbsp tequila
1/2 cup beef broth
1 cup green chilis (roasted/chopped or 2 small cans)

Directions:
Cut round steak into 1 inch cubes and allow to come to room temperature.  Pat cubes dry and season with salt/pepper. In large pressure cooker, seer cubes in 2 tbsp of butter.  Set aside.
In meat juices, add remaining butter and saute onion and garlic. Deglaze pot with tequila.  Return beef to pot, add beef broth and green chili - stir to combine.  Beef broth should just cover ingredients.
Cook on high pressure for 30 minutes or Crockpot high for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
TheCattlemansWife
Description: 
Stewed beef with green chili - basic for tacos, burritos, etc...  Best if made in pressure cooker, but Crockpot will work as well.
