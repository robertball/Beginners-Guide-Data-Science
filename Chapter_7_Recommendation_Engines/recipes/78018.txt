Title:
Ginger-Cabbage Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
3/4 cup pickled ginger
4 cups shredded cabbage, green or red
1 cup shredded carrots
1/2 cup peanuts, crushed
1/4 cup mirin (sweetened Asian wine)
1/4 cup rice vinegar
4 scallions, sliced

Directions:
Combine all.
