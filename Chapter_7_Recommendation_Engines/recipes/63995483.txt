Title:
Peanut brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs of butter
1/2 a tsp of salt
1cup of sugar
1cup of peanuts

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat. Once melted and salt and sugar. Melt sugar until smooth. Take off the heat and add peanuts poor on to parchment paper spread quickly. Put in fridge and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Take parchment off and brake into pieces.
