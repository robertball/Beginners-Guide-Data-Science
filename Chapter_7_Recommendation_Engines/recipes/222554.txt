Title:
B-52 Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/936695.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 fluid ounce coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua®)
1/2 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur (such as Bailey's®)
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec

Directions:
Pour coffee liqueur into a shot glass. Carefully pour in Irish cream liqueur so it floats on top. Pour triple sec on top so it floats as well, to create a three-layered drink.
