Title:
Gravy for Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoon butter
4 Tablespoons all purpose flour
2 cups beef broth
1/2 container,8 ounces,of sour cream

Directions:
To make brown gravy, melt butter in saucepan and Whisk the flour into the melted butter until smooth, and gradually whisk in enough beef broth to total about 2 1/2 cups of liquid;
Bring the gravy to a simmer, whisking constantly until thick, about 5 minutes.
Just before serving, whisk in the sour cream and season to taste with salt and black pepper.  Serve the gravy with Swedish meatballs and mashed potatoes.
