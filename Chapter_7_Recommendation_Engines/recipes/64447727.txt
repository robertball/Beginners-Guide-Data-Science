Title:
Lisa's 1 Skillet Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP Olive oil
2 chicken breast, sliced into strips
2 TBSP. butter or margarine
1 pint mushrooms, sliced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tomato, diced
1 1/2 cups baby spinach
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil over medium-high heat, add chicken, sprinkling with salt and pepper to taste. Cook until chicken is cooked through. Remove from pan and set aside.
Add butter to skillet and allow to melt. Add mushrooms and garlic and saut√© until mushrooms begin to lightly brown. Add tomato and return chicken to skillet. Lightly salt and let cook 2-3 minutes. Add spinach and gently stir pan while cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes.
Can be served as is for low-carb meal or over angel hair pasta.

Submitter: 
Lisa Culbertson
Description: 
Created this recipe as a way to use leftover chicken and make a quick healthy dinner and it was a huge hit, even with my picky eaters!
