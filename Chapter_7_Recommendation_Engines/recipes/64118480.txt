Title:
Ribs, Crockpot Korean Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs beef short ribs
salt
pepper
1 med Asian pear, peeled, cored, coarsely chopped
1/2 c coconut aminos
6 garlic cloves, peeled
3 scallions, coarsely chopped
ginger, thumb sized, peeled, coarsely chopped
2 t Red Boat fish sauce
1 T coconut vinegar
1 c organic chicken broth
small handful cilantro, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Preheat broiler with rack 6 inches from element. Liberally salt and pepper ribs. Bone-side up on foil-lined baking sheet, broil 5 min. Flip and broil 5 more min.
Stack ribs on end in crockpot. Puree pear, aminos, garlic, scallions, ginger, fish sauce, and vinegar in blender until smooth. Pour over ribs.
Add broth to crockpot (down side to avoid disturbing sauce).
Cook on LOW 9-11 hours. Remove to plate. Sprinkle with cilantro.
