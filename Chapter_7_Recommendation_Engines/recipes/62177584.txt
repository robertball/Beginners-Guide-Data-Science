Title:
Turkey burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Diced turkey
chopped celery
chopped onions
chopped velveta
salt
pepper
sage
hamberger buns

Directions:
Combine turkey, celery, onions, cheese, salt,pepper and sage. Use as much of each ingredient to make as many burgers as needed. once all combined scoop the mix on hamberger buns and wrap in aluminum foil; then bake in oven for 30min at 350.
