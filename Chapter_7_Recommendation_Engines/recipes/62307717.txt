Title:
Oregon Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups boiling water
8 tea bags
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/3 cup white sugar
8 Lemon wedges (optional)

Directions:
1. Bring water to a boil, remove from heat, add tea bags and baking soda. Give it one light stir to mix the baking soda. Cover and steep for 15 minutes.
2.  Remove tea bags and discard.  Stir in sugar until dissolved.  Transfer tea to a pitcher and refrigerate until cold.
3.  Serve over ice with a lemon wedge.

Lots of tea flavor with a mild sweetness.
