Title:
Carole's Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) package frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed or hash browns from dairy case
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1/2 cup chopped onions
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
butter or oil for sauteing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Saute onions until soft. In a large bowl, combine hash browns, cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, cooked onion, Cheddar cheese, salt and pepper. Place mixture in a 3 quart casserole dish.
Bake covered in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
Uncover for a few minutes for a crisper top.
