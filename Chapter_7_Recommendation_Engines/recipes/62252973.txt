Title:
Poultry Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp. Butter or Margarine
4 tbsp. Flour
2 cups Hot Water
2 cups Chicken Boullion
1/4 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp. Ground Black Pepper

Directions:
On Medium-Low head, melt butter in medium sauce pan.
Sift flour into pan and wisk until a thick paste is formed.
Dissolve boullion in hot water and add to paste.
Whisk constantly to avoid lumps.
Heat until boiling.
Season with Garlic powder and Pepper.
