Title:
Laura's Frozen Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16-ounce can Dark sweet cherries
1 8.75-ounce can Crushed pineapple
1 11-ounce can Mandarin Oranges
1 8-ounce package Cream Cheese, softened
1 cup Sour cream
1/4 cup Granulated Sugar
2 cups Mini-marshmallows
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/2 cup Pecans, chopped

Directions:
Beat softened cream cheese until fluffy.
Blend in the sour cream, sugar, and salt.
Fold in the remaining ingredients and pour into a mold.
Freeze at least six hours.
Slice to serve.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This has now become a staple at family holiday meals. Some like it as a side, others insist it wait for dessert. Either way, it is delicious!
