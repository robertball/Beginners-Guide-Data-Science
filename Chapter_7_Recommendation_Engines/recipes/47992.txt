Title:
Italian Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976583.jpg
Ingredients:
12 TysonÂ® Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with Italian herbs
2 cups cubed zucchini squash
1 cup fresh pearl onions, peeled
1 cup fresh baby carrots
2 tablespoons tomato paste
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon salt
Hot baked potatoes or pasta

Directions:
PREP: CLEAN: Wash hands. Cut chicken into 1-inch pieces. Combine all ingredients in large slow cooker; mix well. CLEAN: Wash hands.
COOK: Cook on low setting at least 6 hours or until internal juices of chicken run clear. (Or insert instant-read meat thermometer in thickest part of chicken. Temperature should read 180 degrees F.)
SERVE: Serve over pasta or baked potatoes, if desired.
CHILL: Refrigerate leftovers immediately.

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods
Description: 
A tomato based chicken stew with Italian herbs.  Taste great served over pasta or potatoes.
