Title:
Cashew Chicken Rotini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (16 oz) spiral or rotini pasta
4 c cubed cooked chicken
1 can (20 oz) pineapple tidbits
1 1/2 c sliced celery
3/4 c thinly sliced green onions
1 c seedless red grapes
1 c seedless green grapes
1 pkg (5 oz) Craisens
1 c ranch salad dressing
3/4 c mayo
2 c salted cashews

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions, rinse and drain.
In large bowl, combine chicken, pineapple, celery, onions, grapes, Craisens and cooked pasta.
In small bowl, whisk ranch dressing and mayo. Toss over salad and toss to coat.
Cover and refrigerate for at least one hour.
Just before serving, stir in cashews.
