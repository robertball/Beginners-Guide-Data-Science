Title:
Marshmallow Peanut Butter Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978328.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup marshmallow creme
1/4 cup JIF® Creamy Peanut Butter
1 teaspoon lemon juice
2 teaspoons water
Fresh fruit or graham cracker sticks

Directions:
Combine first four ingredients in small bowl. Whisk until blended. Refrigerate 30 minutes before serving.
Serve with dippers such as fresh fruit, graham cracker sticks and pretzels.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
The kids will love this creamy peanut butter dip for their sliced fruit, pretzels or graham crackers.
