Title:
Pasta Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces linguine or fettuccine pasta
4 1/2 cups assorted vegetables -- I prefer baby carrots, snap peas, broccoli, and cauliflower (equivalent of 1 lb bag of frozen veggies)
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 teaspoon Lemon Juice
1 teaspoon Garlic Salt
1/4 teaspoon Thyme
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
-Cook pasta in large saucepan 12 minutes.
-Adding veggies during last 3 minutes of cooking.
-Drain well.
-Place broth, wine, lemon juice, garlic salt, thyme, cream, parmesan cheese and butter in same saucepan.
-Bring to boil, stirring constantly with small whisk.
-Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes.
-Add pasta and vegetables; toss gently to coat well.
-Top with additional parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Mary Thengvall
Description: 
A wonderfully light pasta, full of flavor and ready in less than 30 minutes. Perfect for hot summer evenings when you don't want to spend too long slaving over the stove.
