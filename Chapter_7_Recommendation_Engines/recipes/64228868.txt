Title:
Aunt Sarah's Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1 cup mayonnaise
dash black pepper
2 bunches broccoli (florets, cut small)
1/2 cup raisins
10-12 slices crumbled bacon

Directions:
Dissolve sugar into vinegar.
Add mayo n pepper and mix well.
Add all other ingredients and mix well.
Marinate only 1-2 hours.
