Title:
Grandmother's Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980842.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup milk
1 (15 ounce) can cream-style corn
1/2 cup sugar
5 slices day-old bread, crusts removed
1 tablespoon butter, softened

Directions:
In a bowl, beat eggs and milk. Add corn and sugar; mix well. Cut bread into 1/2-in. cubes and place in a greased 9-in. square baking dish. Pour egg mixture over bread. Dot with butter. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 50-60 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Susan  Brown Langenstein
Description: 
My grandmother always served this pudding for holidays and family reunions. Everyone loves it. Corn pudding is a popular side dish on Maryland's Eastern Shore.
