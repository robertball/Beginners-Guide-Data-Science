Title:
ONION CHICKEN AND RICE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup long grain rice (uncooked)
4 lbs chicken pieces (bone on and skin off can also leave skin on)
1 envelop dry onion soup mix (ie Liptons)
2 cups water

Directions:
In a roasting pan spread out rice
Lay chicken on top skin side up
Sprinkle the soup mix on top of the chicken
Add carefully water as to not wash the soup mix off the chicken
Bake in a 350 degree oven for about 1 hour or until the chicken runs clear and the rice is cooked.

Submitter: 
Nadine
Description: 
This is a fast and simple meal for the end of a busy day.  If you wish there is a recipe on site which you can make the onion soup mix which will have less salt if you wish.    I have picked up the soup mix from a health food store that worked great on this recipe.
