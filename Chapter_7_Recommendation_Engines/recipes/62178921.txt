Title:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes Secret Recipe Modified Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds unpeeled red potatoes, quartered
3/4 lb butter, room temperature
1 cup heated half & half; use more/less for desired consistency
5 ounces freshly-grated Romano cheese
2-3 heads roasted garlic (about 1/4 cup after baking)
3 teaspoons salt (to taste)
1 teaspooon white pepper (to taste)
2 teaspoons dried oregano

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm,  about 45 minutes; drain.  Stir in half & half, butter, cheese, garlic, salt, pepper and oregano.  Mash with a potato masher or with an electric mixer.

Submitter: 
Deb Blagg
Description: 
I modified the original recipe for 10 servings, roasted the garlic and also added white pepper and half & half.Modified from: Garlic Mashed Potatoes Secret Recipe
