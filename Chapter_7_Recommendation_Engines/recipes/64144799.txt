Title:
Elaine's Oil & Vinegar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T olive oil
1 T red wine vinegar
3/4 tsp season salt
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp dill
Accent to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and add to your favorite salad greens.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
This is the only salad dressing my kids grew up with thanks to my Mom-In-Law. They don't care for any other dressing with their salads.
