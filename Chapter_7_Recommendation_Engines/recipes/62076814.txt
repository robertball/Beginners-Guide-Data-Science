Title:
Cheesy Broccoli Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (10 oz.) frozen broccoli, chopped
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 jarred roasted red peppers, coarsely chopped
1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 package (16 oz.) frozen bread dough, thawed

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Grease 2 baking sheets. Cook broccoli according to package directions: drain well.
2) In a medium skillet, heat olive oil over low heat. Add garlic; saute for about 2 minutes. Add broccoli; cook, stirring until mosture has evaporated-about 3 minutes. Remove from heat: cool slightly.
3) In a medium bowl, combine broccoli mixture, mozzarella & parmesan cheeses, roasted peppers, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix Well.
4) On lightly floured surface, divide dough into 8 pieces: roll out each piece to form a 6-inch circle. Spoon an equal amount of broccoli mixture in the center of each circle. Fold dough over filling to form a half circle. Press edges with a fork to seal: prick a few holes in pocket tops.
5) Place pockets on prepared baking sheets. Bake until golden, about 25 minutes. Serve immedietly
