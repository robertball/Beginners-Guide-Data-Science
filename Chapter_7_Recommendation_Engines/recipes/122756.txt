Title:
Puddingwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978994.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cold milk
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1/4 cup peanut butter
15 whole graham crackers

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the milk, pudding mix and peanut butter. Beat on low speed for 2 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes. Break or cut graham crackers in half. Spread pudding mixture over half of the crackers; top with the remaining crackers. Wrap and freeze until firm. May be frozen for up to 1 month.

Submitter: 
Joanne  Zimmerman
Description: 
'Our daughter loves these fun chocolate and peanut butter snacks,' writes Joanne Zimmerman of Ephrata, Pennsylvania. 'I often defrost them in the microwave so they're a bit softer.'
