Title:
Overnight Strawberry Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/399347.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C. strawberries, halved, drained if frozen
1 loaf (12") French bread, cubed into 1" pieces
12 eggs
2 cups milk
1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, cubed into 1/2" pieces
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
2 T. maple syrup

Directions:
Put half of the cubed bread in the bottom of a 9x13 greased baking dish.  Spread 1 1/2 C. strawberries and cream cheese evenly over top.
Whisk eggs, milk, maple syrup and cinnamon until blended.  Pour mixture over bread and strawberries.
Top with remaining bread and sprinkle remaining strawberries.  Sprinkle cinnamon over top, if desired.  Cover with plastic wrap and transfer to fridge to sit overnight.
Preheat over to 350 degrees, bake, covered for 30 minutes.  Uncover and bake an additional 30 minutes or until center is done.

Submitter: 
Abbey
Description: 
French bread, strawberries and cream cheese sit overnight, and then bake to create a flavorful custard-filled dish perfect for brunch.
