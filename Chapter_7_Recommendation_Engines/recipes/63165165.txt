Title:
Mother in Law Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced chicken breast
1/4 tsp poultry seasoning
1/4 tsp garlic
1/2 tsp minced onion
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp white pepper
2 tbs olive oil
8 oz. thin spaghetti
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 c. milk
2 T. butter melted
1 4 oz. can mushrooms
2 T. pimentos
1 1/2 Tbs. parsley
3/4 c. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Sautee chicken in olive oil with poultry seasoning, garlic, minced onion, salt, white pepper.
Cook spaghetti until al denti and drain.
Mix milk, cream of mushroom soup, butter mushrooms, pimentos, parsley and parmesan cheese. Add chicken and spaghetti.
Pour mixture in a buttered 8 x 8 inch casserole dish and bake 400 degrees for 15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
My monther in laws recipe for chicken tetrazzini. This is a favorite among our family and its so quick and easy to make. I usually serve with a salad and garlic bread.
