Title:
Sedation Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 shot vodka
1/4 shot amaretto
1/2 shot peach schnapps
1/2 shot coconut rum
1/4 shot melon liqueur
1 shot of orange juice
1/2 shot pineapple juice
Splash of cranberry juice
Lemon Twist
Ice

Directions:
Fill a pounder beer glass with ice. Add all the alcohol, add orange juice, pineapple juice and float the cranberry juice on the top...garnish with a lemon twist.

Submitter: 
witchywoman
Description: 
This is a pretty potent drink...a couple of these will leave you definately sedated.
