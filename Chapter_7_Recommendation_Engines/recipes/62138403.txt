Title:
Spicy Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 2 1/2 pound boneless sirloin pork roast, or boneless pork butt...DO NOT use center cut pork roast.
RUB:
1/2 - 1 tsp. salt (to taste)
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 - 1 tsp. cayenne pepper (to taste)
1 tsp. garlic powder
MARINADE:
1 16 oz. jar hot chunky salsa
1/2 Cup white sugar
1/2 Cup cider vinegar
1 large yellow onion, chopped
6 cloves of garlic, sliced

Directions:
Lightly spray the ceramic liner of a slow cooker with non-stick spray.
Next, mix the RUB ingredients together in a small bowl.
Using your hands, rub this spice mixture into the meat.
Arrange the meat in the center of the slow cooker.
Next, in a medium bowl, mix the remaining ingredients.
Pour the salsa mixture over the meat.
Cover.  Cook on low for 8 hours.
When the meat is finished, gently pull the meat apart with two forks.
Serve on split rolls; add pickles and cole slaw for a more traditional taste.
