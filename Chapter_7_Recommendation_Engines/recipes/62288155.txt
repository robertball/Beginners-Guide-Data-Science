Title:
Curried Coconut Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs Boneless skinless chicken breast
(also great with 2lbs med shrimp)
1-teas.ea. salt and pepper
1.5-Tab veg. oil
2-Tab. curry powder
Lg.onion chopped
4-6 clove of minced garlic
2 Tab. butter
1-Lg carrot peeled and chopped
1-Lg potato peeled and chopped
2-ribs celery chopped
1-14oz can coconut milk
1-10oz can mild Rotel
1-8oz can tomato sauce
3-Tab. sugar
1-lime cut up in small wedges

Directions:
Cut chicken in 1/2 in. pieces and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Heat oil and curry powder in a large skillet for 5 min. Add onions and garlic and cook 1 min. more. Stir in chicken and cook till cicken is no longer pink, 7-10 min. Put chicken mix in a bowl.
Melt butter in skillet and add chopped carrots, celery and potato. Cook 5-7 min. till veggies start to soften. Stir in rotel and cook a 5-10 min. longer.
Add Coconut milk, tomato sauce, sugar and chicken mix and simmer for 20-30 min. Serve over steamed rice and a wedge of lime to squeeze for a complete tropical flavor.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
