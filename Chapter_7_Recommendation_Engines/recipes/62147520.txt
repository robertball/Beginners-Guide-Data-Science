Title:
So-Lo Orange Glow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1T	Unflavored gelatin
2 cup	Diet Orange Soda

Directions:
Sprinkle gelatin over 1/2 cup carbonated drink in saucepan.
Heat and stir until gelatin dissolves, about 2 or 3 minutes.
Remove from heat; stir in remaining beverage.
Pour into four molds.
Chill until firm.

Submitter: 
Candy
Description: 
A very low in sodium jello desert that is easy to make.
