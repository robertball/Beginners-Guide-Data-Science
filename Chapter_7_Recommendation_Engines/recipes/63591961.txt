Title:
Tortellini with ham and garlic cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/999139.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of olive oil
2 onions, finely sliced
250 gr of ham cubes
3 cloves of garlic, pealed
500 ml of cream
250 gr of fresh tortellini or 500 gr dried tortellini

Directions:
Heat the oil in a saucepan and fry the sliced onion until golden.
Add the ham cubes and fry for about a minute.
Use a garlic press to press the garlic, add to the mixture and fry for another 30 seconds.
Pour in the cream, bring to the boil and season with salt and pepper.
Leave to a slow boil for about 30 minutes, stir occasionally.
Prepare the tortellini according to the instructions on the pack. Serve the tortellini with the cream sauce.
