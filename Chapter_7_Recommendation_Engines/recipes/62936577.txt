Title:
Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 stick butter
2 cans cream of chicken soup
48 oz chicken broth (6 c. water and 1 1/2 T. chicken soup base)
16 oz Reames frozen noodles

Directions:
Place all ingredients, except noodles, in crockpot.  Cook on low for 4 hours.  Remove chicken and shred.  Return the chicken to the crockpot and add the noodles.  Cook on low for 1 - 2 more hours until the noodles are done.
