Title:
Roast Beef Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ciabatta rolls, sliced in half
1/2 pound deli roast beef, sliced
4 slices swiss cheese
4 tsp. light mayonnaise
4 tsp. Dijon mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix mayonnaise and mustard together in a small bowl. To assemble sandwiches, spread 2 tsp. mayo/mustard mixture on bottom half of roll. Top with roast beef and place cheese on top of that. Cover with top of ciabatta roll. Wrap each sandwich in aluminum foil and place on a baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until roast beef is warm and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
HARTMR
Description: 
Warm roast beef sandwiches with melted cheese and little bit of mayo and dijon mustard. Recipe is very versatile - would be tasty with any type of meat or cheese.
