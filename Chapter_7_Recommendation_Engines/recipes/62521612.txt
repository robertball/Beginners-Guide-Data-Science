Title:
Low Fat White Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/513002.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large egg whites
1 cup confection sugar
3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
1 tablespoon water
1/4 teaspoon salt
Additive options: all to taste
•	½ cup pureed banana or banana baby food or
•	1 teaspoon orange zest or
•	1 teaspoon lemon zest or

Directions:
Combine egg whites, confection sugar, vanilla extract, cream of tartar, water and salt in bowl above simmering water.  Stir continuously over heat for 3 minutes.
Remove bowl from heat and continue mixing for another 7-9 minutes.  Add in additive option  and mix until evenly distributed (about 10 seconds).  Cool in refrigerator for 30 minutes, then frost cupcakes prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Skinny Cakes
Description: 
This low in fat frosting can be tailored to your taste. Play with your frosting to see what additive option you like best or create your own. This base will give you the perfect low in fat topping for any cupcake!
