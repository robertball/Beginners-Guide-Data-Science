Title:
Double Cheese Corn Sandwich Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
50 grams canned corn
1 1/2 tablespoon mayonnaise
4 slices bread
20 grams butter
4 slices cheddar cheese
10 grams shredded cheese
little bit of pepper

Directions:
Drain the corn then mix it with mayonnaise.
Soften the butter then spread it on one side of each bread.
On the non-buttered side of bread put the cheddar cheese. Top it with the mayonnaise-corn mixture then add the shredded cheese. Sprinkle with some pepper then sandwich it with  a slice of bread w/ the buttered side up.
Preheat the sandwich maker. Once done, put the sandwich then toast and cook for 5 minutes or until crispy.
Slice in half before serving.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
For all the cheese corn lovers out there, this sandwich toast recipe is for you. I've made this extra cheesy by making use of two types of cheese.
