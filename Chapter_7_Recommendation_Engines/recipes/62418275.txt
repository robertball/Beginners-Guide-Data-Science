Title:
Monkey Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough
1/3 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 Tbsp butter
3 Tbsp packed brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9 inch pie plate.
Mix white sugar and cinnamon in a plastic bag. Cut biscuits into sixths. Shake 6 to 8 biscuit pieces in the sugar cinnamon mix. Arrange pieces in the bottom of the prepared pan. Continue until all biscuits are coated and placed in pan.
In 1c pyrex glass measure, combine butter with the brown sugar and microwave on high for 20 seconds; stir. Microwave on high for one minute. Spread over the biscuits.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a plate. Do not cut! The bread just pulls apart.
