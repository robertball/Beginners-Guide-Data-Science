Title:
Copycat Chipotle® Beef Barbacoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2436937.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs beef shoulder roast
5 large, dried chipotle peppers
½ medium yellow onion
¾ cup beef broth
¼ cup fresh lime juice
¼ cup tomato paste
1 ½ Tbsp apple cider vinegar
3 bay leaves
6 garlic cloves
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp salt
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp whole cloves

Directions:
Trim the roast of excess fat and place into a slow cooker.
Open the peppers and remove most of the seeds.
Place peppers, onion, garlic, lime juice, cumin, salt, oregano, tomato paste, and vinegar into a food processor and pulse until smooth. Spread mixture over meat.
Add broth, cloves, and bay leaves to the slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low for 7 hours.
Shred meat, then cover and continue cooking for an additional hour.
Serve in taco or burrito shells with your choice of accompaniments and toppings.
