Title:
Ed's Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7-9 red potatoes
2 eggs
1/2 onion
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3 tablespoons flour

Directions:
Shred potatoes and onion finely.  Add eggs, garlic powder, salt, and pepper into a mixing bowel.
Pour mix in pancake size amounts onto a skillet using medium heat until brown on both sides.
Enjoy.
