Title:
Cream of Chicken and Frenchs Onions Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken breasts, cream of chicken/ mushroom soup, green onions, sour cream, 1 container Frenchs onions, salt, pepper, nutmeg, paprika, shredded cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°. In a large casserole dish, spray with vegetable oil pan spray and place chicken. Combine 1/2 cup sour cream, 1 can soup, salt, pepper, nutmeg, paprika and a dash of onion powder to taste. Spoon mixture over chicken. Chop the green of the green onions and sprinkle over mixture. Then sprinkle 1 cup shredded cheese and then 2/3 can Frenchs onions over the chicken. Cover with tin foil and put in the oven. About 10 minutes before chicken is cooked through, drove the foil and add remaining Frenchs onions. Reinsert casserole into the oven and bake uncovered remaining 10 minutes.
