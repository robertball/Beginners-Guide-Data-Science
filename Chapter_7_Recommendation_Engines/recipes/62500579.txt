Title:
Cranana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521192.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large banana, cut into chunks
1 cup frozen cranberries
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
honey to taste (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and process until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
A festive healthy smoothie with one of my favorite flavors, cranberry!
