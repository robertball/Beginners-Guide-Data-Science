Title:
Herbed Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice3 cups water
1/2 cup finely chopped parsley
1/2 cup finely chopped green onions
1/8 tsp. dried thyme leaves
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes, or until rice is tender. Stir well before serving.
