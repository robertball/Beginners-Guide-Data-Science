Title:
Shrimp Scampi with Linguini by Tyler Florence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483956.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Linguini
4 T Butter
4 T Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 Shallots, finely diced
2 Cloves Garlic, finely diced
Pinch of Red Pepper Flakes
1 lb Shrimp peeled & deveined
Kosher Salt and Freshly Ground Pepper
½ C Dry White Wine
Juice of 1 Lemon
¼ C Finely Chopped Parsley Leaves

Directions:
Cook the Linguini
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, melt 2 T butter in 2 T olive oil over medium high heat. Saute the shallots, garlic and red pepper flakes until the shallots are translucent.  Season the shrimp with salt and pepper and add to the pan and cook until they turn pink, about 2 to 3 minutes.  Remove the shrimp from the pan, set aside and keep warm.  Add wine and lemon juice and bring to a boil.  Add 2 T butter and 2 T oil.  When the butter has melted, return the shrimp to the pan along with the parsley and cooked pasta.  Stir well and season with salt and pepper.Drizzleover a bit more olive oil and serve immediately.
