Title:
Grilled Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut lengthwise into 1/4-inch slices
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon Tony Chachiere's
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate. Toss potatoes with brown sugar, olive oil and spices. Grill directly on grill until browned and tender.
