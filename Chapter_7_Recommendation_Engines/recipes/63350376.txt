Title:
Healthier Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/957325.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/3 cups Splenda
1/2 cup rolled oats
4 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups pumpkin puree or 1 15 oz can pumpkin
2/3 cup packed dark brown sugar
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup applesauce
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup raisins (optional)
2 tablespoons rolled oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease or line 24 muffin cups with paper liners.
Combine 2 1/2 cups flour, 1/2 cup oats, pumpkin pie spice, baking soda, baking powder, and salt & splenda together in a bowl.
Whisk pumpkin puree, brown sugar,  vegetable oil, applesauce, eggs, and vanilla extract together in a separate large bowl.
Stir flour mixture into pumpkin mixture; mix well. Stir in raisins.
Pour the batter into the prepared muffin tin. Sprinkle each muffin with a few oats.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes.
