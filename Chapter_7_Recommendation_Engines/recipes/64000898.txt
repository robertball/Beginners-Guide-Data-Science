Title:
Homemade Whipped Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 can evaporated milk
* 3/4 cup to 1 cup granulated sugar
* 1/4 cup (more or less) lemon juice

Directions:
Put the evaporated milk in a glass bowl with electric beaters. Leave this in the freezer for 30 minutes to 45 minutes until ice crystals start around the beaters and edge of bowl. Take the bowl of evaporated milk out and beat on high for 1 minute or more until foamy and slowly add the sugar and lemon juice and keep beating until combined and fluffy.
Freeze for 30 minutes, put with fruit or pie or whatever you like whipped cream with.
