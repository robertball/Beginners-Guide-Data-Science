Title:
Apple Pie--No Sugar Added Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9" pie shells (2)
3/4 cup water
1 Tbl cornstarch
1/4 cup cold water
1 Tbl marg
20 packets Splenda
apple slices (2 cans--not pie filling) or 4 peeled, cored, diced apples
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
Bring 3/4 cup water to boil. In small bowl, whisk together cornstarch & 1/4 cup cold water. Whisk this mixture into boiling water. Reduce heat & stir until mixture is thickened, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat
Stir marg & Splenda into cornstarch mixture. Add cinnamon. Mix well. Place fruit in large bowl & pour mixture over fruit. Toss until fruit is thoroughly coated.
Pour fruit into pastry lined pie pan. Cover with 2nd pastry shell. Seal edges & cut steam vents in top.
Bake for 45min, until crust is golden brown
