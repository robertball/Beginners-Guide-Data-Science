Title:
Dad's Favourite Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lbs ground beef
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 cup onions, finely chopped
1 med celery stock, finely chopped
1/4 cup green pepper, chopped
1 clove of garlic, chopped
1 egg
1/2 cup ketchup
1/3 cups plain dry bread crumbs
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
Topping:
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup molassis (heated)
1/4 cup cider vinegar
2 Tbsp prepared mustard
1 Tbsp Worcestshire sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large skillet, melt butter.
Saute celery, onions, green pepper and garlic for ~ 1 minute.
Set veggies aside to cool.
In a large bowl, beat egg.
Add ground beef, ketchup, bread crumbs, salt & pepper and mix well.
Add sauted veggies to ground beef mixture.
Place in a 8x8x2 dish and cook for 1 hour.
