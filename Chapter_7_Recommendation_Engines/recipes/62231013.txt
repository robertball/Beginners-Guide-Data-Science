Title:
FRANK'S RedHot Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) cans chunk chicken, drained
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup Blue Cheese dressing
1/2 cup Blue Cheese crumbles
1/2 cup any flavor FRANK'S RedHot sauce
Assorted vegetables and/or crackers for dipping

Directions:
1) Stir cream cheese in deep baking dish until smooth. Stir in remaining ingredients.
2) Bake at 350F for 20 min until hot and bubbling. Stir. Serve with vegetables/crackers.
