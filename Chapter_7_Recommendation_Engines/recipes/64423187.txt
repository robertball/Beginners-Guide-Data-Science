Title:
Dry Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP Kosher Salt
1 TBSP Cracked Pepper
1 TBSP Garlic Powder
1 TBSP Paprika
1/2 TBSP Smoked Paprika
1/2 TBSP Onion Powder
1 TBSP Dried Oregano
1 Tsp Cayenne
1 Tsp Cumin

Directions:
Whisk to combine all the ingretients in a bowl, and use your hands to rub them onto meat. Let the rub stand on the meat for 1 hour before smoking or grilling.

Gives Great Flavor on pork, beef and chicken.
