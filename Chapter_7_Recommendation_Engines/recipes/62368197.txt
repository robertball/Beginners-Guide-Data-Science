Title:
Grandma Jordan's Chinese Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
4 Tbsp. oil
3 cups cold cooked white rice
1/2 cup finely chopped cooked bacon or ham
1 Tbsp. sliced green onion
2 Tbsp. soy sauce

Directions:
In small bowl, beat eggs with fork.
In frying pan, heat 1 Tbsp. oil.  Add eggs and scramble until well cooked.  Remove from heat and set aside.
In large frying pan, heat 3 Tbsp. oil.  Add rice and cook over medium high heat, stirring until rice is coated with oil.
Stir in meat, soy sauce, and scrambled eggs.  Stir until eggs are in small pieces mixed throughout rice.
Cook until heated through.  Top with green onion before serving.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This goes well with a green salad and soup.
