Title:
Horseradish Chicken, Butternut Squash, and Zucchini Sheet Pan Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6667181.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs
2 Tbsp. Horseradish
1 Tbsp. Dijon Mustard
2 tsp. Onion powder
1 tsp. Garlic Powder
2lb Butternut Squash- peeled, seeded, and cut into 1 inch cubes
2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/4 cup chopped Fresh Sage
1/4 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 medium Zucchini
1 Tbsp. Olive Oil
1 Tbsp. Balsamic Vinegar
1 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/8 tsp. Black Pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
2. Combine Butternut Squash, Olive Oil, Salt, Sage, And Parmesan into a medium bowl. Toss until coated completely and place on the right side of a large non stick baking sheet (17 1/4 x 11 1/2).
3. In another medium bowl combine Horseradish, Dijon Mustard, Onion Powder, and Garlic Powder. Mix until combine and coat chicken thighs completely. Place chicken thighs in the center of the baking sheet.
4. Slice zucchini into 16 equal size spears. Place zucchini spears on the left side of the baking sheet. In a small bowl mix olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Brush olive oil mixture on the zucchini and sprinkle with the garlic powder and black pepper.
5. Bake for 25 minutes or until chicken reaches 165 degrees.
