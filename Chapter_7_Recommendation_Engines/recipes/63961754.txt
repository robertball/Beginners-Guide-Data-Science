Title:
Chocolate Sapote Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1099399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 black sapote
2 handfuls of ice
1 cup chocolate almond milk

Directions:
Break open the sapote and pull out the pulp. Discard the seeds and skin. Combine sapote, ice, and almond milk into a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
claytical
Description: 
I've been experimenting with tropical fruit and sapote is really interesting. It's pulp is like chocolate pudding. This recipe is really simple and let's the black sapote shine. It's pure creamy chocolately goodness.
