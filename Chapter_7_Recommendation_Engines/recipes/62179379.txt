Title:
SPRITZ COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289888.jpg
Ingredients:
6 sticks Butter
2 1/4 cup Sugar
3 large Eggs
3 teaspoon Vanilla
1 1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon Baking powder
6 1/4 cups Flour
Sprinkles all kinds

Directions:
preheat oven to 375Â°
cream the butter and sugar in mixer at medium speed untill fluffy.
Add the eggs one at a time mixing well after each one.
Add vanilla, salt, and baking powder. mix well.
Add flour a little at a time or will over flow mixer. And mix well.
Use a cookie press and press out cookies into fun shapes and sprinkle with sprinkles
Bake on UNgreassed cookie sheet for 8-10 minutes till barely brown on edges.
