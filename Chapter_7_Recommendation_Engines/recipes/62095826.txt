Title:
Lisha's Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Veg. Oil
1 cup uncooked rice
1 small onion (chopped)
1/2 chopped green bell pepper
2 cups water
3/4 can petite diced tomatoes
1 small can diced Mild green chilies
2 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Saute Rice in Vegetable oil until brown.
Add rest of ingredients and bring to a boil.
Set on low and simmer for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Donna L Whitaker
Description: 
This is my sister in laws recipe and it is very good!!! Everyone loves it and usually fights over the leftovers!!
