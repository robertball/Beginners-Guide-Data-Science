Title:
Chocolatey Peanut Butter & Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Frozen Banana
1 Very Ripe, Room-temp Banana
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 tsps Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1 tbsp Natural Creamy Peanut Butter

Directions:
Break bananas into chunks, and place into food processor or blend. Blend until pureed. Add in vanilla extract, cocoa powder, and peanut butter. Blend until thoroughly combined.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
This is a great quick fix for a chocolate craving--without wrecking your diet! It's not a very sweet drink, so if you like sweet, you will probably have to adjust it to your tastes. It's quick and easy, and makes a perfect serving size for one person! Enjoy
