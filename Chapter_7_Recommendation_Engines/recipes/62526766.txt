Title:
Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6868459.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1 t. salt
2/3 c +2 T Crisco
1/4 c. water (may add more)

Directions:
Mix flour and salt together.
Cut in Crisco with a pastry blender until crumbly.
Add water and mix until forms a ball.
Roll out and put in pie pan.
