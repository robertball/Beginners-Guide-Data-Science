Title:
Cod Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBS BUTTER
1 CHOPPED ONION
4 TBS PLAIN FLOUR
1 PINT OF MILK
6 OZ SMOKED COD- COOKED & FLAKED
4 OZ GRATED TASTY CHEESE
4 OZ CORN KERNELS
SALT & PEPPER TO TASTE
1 TSP WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
TOPPING
2 SLICES OF BUTTERED WHITE BREAD
2 OZ GRATED TASTY CHEESE

Directions:
Melt butter in suacepan and saute onion until clear.
Remove from heat and add flour & mix until smooth.
Add milk slowly and cook stiring all the time.  Bring to boil and cook for 3 minutes.
Place fish, corn, cheese and flavourings to casserole dish.  Add the sauce to dish. Cut bread into cubes and place on top of dish. Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake in moderate oven for 20-30 minutes.

CZARDAS
Description: 
This is a old family recipe that my mother always used to make on Good Friday.
