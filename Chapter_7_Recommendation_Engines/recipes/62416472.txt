Title:
Roasted Red Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, cut in 1-inch cubes
1 medium onion, chopped
4 hard-cooked eggs, sliced
1 cup low fat mayonnaise
1/4 cup diced celery
1/4 teaspoon pepper
Paprika

Directions:
Place the potatoes in a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until tender and golden brown, stirring occasionally. Cool for 15 minutes. Transfer to a large bowl; add onion, eggs, low fat mayonnaise,celery and pepper. Toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight. Sprinkle with paprika if desired.
