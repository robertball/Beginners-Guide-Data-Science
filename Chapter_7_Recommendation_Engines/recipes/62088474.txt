Title:
Chocolate Orange Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
finely grated zest of 1 orange
1/2 cup fresh orange juice
6 tbsp brown sugar
2 cups all purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
3 oz cocoa powder
ICING
11 oz cream cheese, softened
2 oz icing sugar
2 tsp finely grated orange zest
6 tbsp orange juice
candied orange strips (for garnish)

Directions:
Preheat the oveb to 350 degrees.
Beat the butter and sugar in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until pale and creamy.
Beat the eggs in one at a time. Stir in the orange zest.
Stir the orange juice and brown sugar in a small saucepan over low heat until the sugar has dissolved.
Sift the flour, baking powder, and cocoa into the butter and sugar mixture gradually, alternating with the orange juice and brown sugar mixture.
Butter an 8-ince square cake pan and line with wax paper.
Spoon the batter into the pan and smooth the surface.
Bake for 45 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
Invert onto a cake rackand leave to cool.
ICING
Combine the cream cheese in a large bowl with the icing sugar, orange juice and zest, and beat until smooth and creamy.
When the cake is completely cool, spread the top and sides with icing. Deorate the top with strips of orange zest and, if liked, very thin slices of fresh orange. You can also add dark chocolate slivers aswell.
