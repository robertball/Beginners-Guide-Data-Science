Title:
Crunchy Cheese Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 medium tomato,diced
1 medium stalk celery, sliced
1 small green pepper,finely chopped
4 oz cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp salt
4 slices of bread
butter

Directions:
Add vegetables together in a medium sized mixing bowl. Toss until incorporated.  Add cheese. Mix in chili powder and salt.
Butter one side of each slice of bread.  Add one quarter of mixture to each slice of bread and spread to edges.
Place on cookie sheet or broiling pan.  Broil for 4-5 minutes until cheese melts. WATCH CLOSELY!!
Serve Hot.
