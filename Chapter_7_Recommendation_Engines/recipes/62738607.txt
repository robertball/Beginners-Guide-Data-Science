Title:
AAA Baked French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/628147.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf French or Italian bread sliced 1” thick
8 large eggs
2 ½ cups half & half, cream or milk
1 TBS sugar
¾ tsp salt
1 TBS vanilla
1 TBS maple syrup
½ cup Triple Sec (optional)
Butter
Nutmeg
Cinnamon

Directions:
Beat together eggs, milk/cream, sugar, salt, vanilla, syrup and triple sec(optional).
Place bread in buttered 9x13 glass dish.
Pour mixture over bread and soak overnight in covered baking dish.
Place a pat of butter on each slice of bread and sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg to taste.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown.
ENJOY
