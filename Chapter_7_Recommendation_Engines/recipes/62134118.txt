Title:
Mango Lassi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large ripe mango
2 cups fat free yogurt
1 cup fat free milk
2 tbsp sugar
2 tbsp mango pulp
5 ice cubes

Directions:
1) Peel and dice mango.
2) Blend all ingredients in a blender to a smooth pouring consistency.
3) Serve chilled.

Submitter:

A sweet refreshing mango yogurt drink
