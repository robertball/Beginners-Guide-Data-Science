Title:
Creamy Veggie Fettuccini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Fettuccini Noodles
3 tbs olive oil
3 carrots; diced
3 stalks of celery;diced
1 cup diced sweet onion
2 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp green curry powder
2 cups frozen broccoli
1 cup water
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 oz Velveeta Cheese cubed
4 oz sour cream
Black Pepper
Salt

Directions:
In a large skillet add oil and cook over medium heat.
When oil is hot add carrot, celery and onion.  Cook about 6 minutes.
While vegetables are cooking, break fettuccini in half and cook to package directions.
To the carrots, onion and celery mixture add the garlic and green curry powder- stir and cover. Cook about 5 minutes.
Add the water and the broccoli- Cover and cook until the broccoli is heated through.
Add soup and mix well.  Once the soup mix has heated add the cubed cheese.  Cover and reduce heat.
When cheese has melted mix well and then mix in the sour cream.
Add Fettuccini and season with salt and pepper to taste.
