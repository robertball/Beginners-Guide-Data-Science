Title:
Pacific Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4002800.jpg
Ingredients:
10 ice cubes
1 banana
4 teaspoons maple syrup
4 teaspoons brown sugar
1 cup eggnog
1/4 cup orange juice
1/4 cup vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Blend the ice, banana, maple syrup, brown sugar, eggnog, orange juice, and vanilla yogurt together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
BASTIANELLI
Description: 
A tropical delight that hits a sweet tooth, but reminds you of sunbathing in the tropics. After reading the ingredients it doesn't sound very good, but it is awesome. My favorite smoothie by far. Created by a friend of mine.
