Title:
Easy apple, pear and blackberry crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of washed Blackberries
3 medium apples (peeled, cored and cut in to wedges)
3 pears (peeled, cored and cut in to wedges)
2 cups of wholewheat flour
1 Cup of sugar (white or packed brown)
1 1/2 cups of low fat margarine (or butter if you prefer)

Directions:
Grease 3 9" cake or pie tins
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix flour butter and sugar together with fingers or in food processor to make crumble.
Layer apples and pears in dishes
sprinkle blackberries over the top of apples and pears
Evenly distribute crumble over top of fruit
Place in preheated oven for about 40 minutes or until fruit is soft and crumble is turning light brown.
Remove from oven and serve hot.
