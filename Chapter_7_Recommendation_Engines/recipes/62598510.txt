Title:
Steve's Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground turkey
1/2 lb 90% lean ground beef
1 onion chopped fine
2 eggs
1/4 cup Italian bread crumbs
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine turkey and meatloaf.  I like to use my Kitchen Aid mixer on "Stir".
Add onion, eggs, and bread crumbs until just combined.  Do not overmix.
Form mixture into a loaf.
Bake on convection bake at 375 degrees for 60 minutes.
Check for internal temperature of 185 degrees.
Rest for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
steveweis
Description: 
Combination of ground turkey and lean ground beef for a flavorful meatloaf that isn't too dry.
