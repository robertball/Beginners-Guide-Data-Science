Title:
Ultimate Breakfast Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tbs sugar
1 tbs baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp paprika
1 tbs herbs
1/2 cup of butter
3/4 cup of milk
1 tbs mayonnaise
1 cup of shredded cheese
1/3 cup of chopped up garlic sausage

Directions:
1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, paprika and herbs.
2. Cut in butter with a fork until mixture is crumbly.
3. Preheat oven to 425 F.
4. Stir in milk, mayonnaise, cheese and garlic sausage until a soft dough forms.
5. Form the dough into a ball and flatten to about an inch in thickness. Cut into 6 pieces and form triangles.
6. Lightly coat a cookie sheet with olive oil and place scones. Bake for 18-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marie Wishart
Description: 
These are so easy and delicious! You can use your favorite kinds of herbs and cheese.
