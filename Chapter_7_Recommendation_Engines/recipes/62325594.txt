Title:
Sausage Balls by Mrs.C Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork sausage
2 cups bisquick garlic/Cheddar mix
1 pound sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine sausage, biscuit baking mix and cheese. Form into walnut size balls and place on baking sheets. Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.
