Title:
Zesty Bloody Mary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990424.jpg
Ingredients:
4 fluid ounces Stirrings Bloody Mary Cocktail Mixer
1 1/2 fluid ounces Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
1 fluid ounce pickle juice
1 spear pickle
1 cup ice

Directions:
Mix together in a tall glass with plenty of ice.
Garnish with pickle spear.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
A simple but so refreshing bloody mary is made with mixer, vodka, a spear of pickle, and a little bit of pickle juice from the jar. It's served up ice cold and tangy.
