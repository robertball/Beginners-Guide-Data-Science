Title:
Jill Slayden's Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large chicken breasts
1 can cream of mushroom or cream of chicken soup
1 container of sour cream
1 roll of Ritz crackers
1 stick of butter

Directions:
Cook chicken and pull from bone.
Mix chicken, soup and sour cream together.
Put in baking dish.
Crush crackers and put on top.
Cut up stick of butter all over top of cracker topping.
Cook 40 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Buck Sommerkamp
Description: 
Delicious, buttery creamy chicken with a crackery crunch.
