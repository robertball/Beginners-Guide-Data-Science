Title:
Maple Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/726806.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) Unbaked Pie Shell
Filling:
1 (15 ounce) can Pumpkin
1 (14 ounce) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 Eggs
2 teaspoon Maple Flavor
1 teaspoon McCormicks Roasted Saigon Cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon McCormicks Roasted Ginger
1/4 teaspoon Fresh Grated Nutmeg
1 tiny dash of Cardamom

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
In large mixing bowl, combine pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, cinnamon, maple flavoring, ginger, nutmeg, cardamom and salt; mix well.  Pour into pie shell.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 15 minutes.  Reduce oven to 350 degrees F; continue baking 40 minutes. Cool.  Garnish as desired.  Store covered in refrigerator.
