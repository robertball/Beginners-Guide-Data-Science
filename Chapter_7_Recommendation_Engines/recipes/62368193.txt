Title:
Grandma Jordan's Chow Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. oil
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 onion, sliced
1 oz sliced mushrooms
1 cup bean sprouts
1 cup cooked chopped chicken
1/2 cup shicken broth
2 Tbsp. soy sauce
1 Tbsp. cornstarch

Directions:
Heat oil in large frying pan and over medium heat cook celery and onion for 3 minutes.
Turn heat to low and add mushrooms, bean sprouts, chicken and broth.
Cover pan and cook for 10 minutes.
Mix soy sauce and cornstarch in small bowl, then pour over chow mein.
Stir over low heat until mixture thickens.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
Serve over white rice or noodles.
