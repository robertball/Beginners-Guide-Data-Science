Title:
Winter Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Banana
1 Crisp Red Apple
1 Pear
1 Orange
1 Cup Grapes

Directions:
Chop fruit into bite sized pieces and toss gently, making sure the banana gets covered with orange juice to deter browning.  Serve right away, or let sit for up to 2 hours.
