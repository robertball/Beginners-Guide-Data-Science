Title:
Eddy's Famous Ceasar Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 eggs, cooked for one minute (use yolks only)
1 can anchovies, minced
Oil from the anchovy can
1 c. olive oil
1/2 c. vegetable oil
15-20 shakes of worcestershire
15-20 shakes of red wine vinegar
The juice of 1 lemon (if it's not real juicey, use 1 1/2 lemons)
10 shakes of hot sauce
1 1/2 t. salt
1 1/2 t. pepper
3 heaping T. Grey Poupon
2 heads of chilled romaine lettuce, torn into bite size pieces
6 oz. parmesan
Ceasan salad croutons

Directions:
In a large bowl add first 13 ingredients and mix well.  Add lettuce and toss to coat.  Add parmesan cheese and croutons and toss again.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
This is my father-in-law's recipe and it is consistently a favortie at family gatherings.  It's definitely the best I've tasted!
