Title:
Grandma Carol's Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 acorn squash
Water
Brown Sugar
Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Slice acorn squash lengthwise along stem into two pieces.  Scoop out seeds and scrape out excess flesh.
Place slices face down (skin side up) in baking dish.  Fill bottom of baking dish with 1/2 inch of water.  Bake for 1 hour.
Remove squash from oven and pour off water.  Flip squash over (skin side down).  Cover with butter and brown sugar as desired.  Bake for 15 minutes longer.
