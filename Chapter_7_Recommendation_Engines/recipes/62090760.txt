Title:
Lemon Olive Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium sized whole chicken
1 small can of sliced black olives
1/2 lemon, sliced
rosemary
pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Place washed chicken breast side down in a large pot with a lid.  Pour olives, including juice, over chicken.  Place lemons on top of chicken.  Sprinkle liberally with rosemary and freshly cracked pepper.  Cover and bake for approximately one hour.  Let stand for at least 5 minutes prior to cutting.

Submitter: 
Kayla
Description: 
This is a delicious recipe my mother makes with very minimal ingredients.  It's simple and tasty!  The olives give it just the right amount of salt and flavor, even if you're not a huge fan of olives!
