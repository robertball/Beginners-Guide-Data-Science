Title:
Genevieve's Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 cups of wide egg noodles
8 cups of water
1/2 teaspoon sage
1/2 teaspoon rosemary
1/2 teaspoon thyme
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoons of vegetable broth paste
2 2oz cans of white chicken meat

Directions:
In a dutch oven, cover the bottom with a very small amount water and add chopped onions, celery, spices, and carrots, cook until onions are translucent - about 5-8 minutes.
Meanwhile, in another large pot, cook egg noodles in 8 cups of water, when slightly before al dente drain but reserve water for the soup broth. Put noodle aside until the very end.
Add all reserved noodle water to Dutch oven, add 2 tablespoons of vegetable broth paste, and add 4 ounces cans of white chicken meat with juices. Simmer for 40 minutes.
Turn off heat, add cooked noodles to Dutch oven and serve.
