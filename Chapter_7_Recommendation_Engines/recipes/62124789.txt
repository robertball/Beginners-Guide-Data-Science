Title:
The Best Gluten Free Brownies Ever...Seriously Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick room temp. butter
1/4 c. tapioca flour
1/4 c. sorghum flour
1/4 c. teff flour
1/3 c. + 1 tsp cocoa powder
1/2-1 cup of chopped walnuts (optional)
1/2 bag semi sweet chocolate chips
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup sugar
1 tsp xanthan gum
2 eggs, beaten
dash of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ. Toss nuts and chocolate chips with a teaspoon of cocoa powder until coated. Set aside. Combine flours, cocoa, xanthan gum, and salt. In a separate bowl mix butter and sugar until incorporated. Mix in eggs and vanilla. Add dry mixture gradually until incorporated. Fold in nuts and chocolate chips. Spread in a greased 9x9 pan and bake approx. 25 minutes or until set.

Submitter: 
Lindsay L.
Description: 
I modified an old family favorite recipe upon going gluten-free. It is easy and super yummy and naughty. If you prefer to use a premade GF flour mix instead of the flours suggested it will work just as well.
