Title:
Rotini pasta with ground turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heaping cups whole grain rotini pasta
1 pound ground turkey
1 26.5 ounce can traditional style spaghetti sauce
2 tsp. brown sugar
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
ground black pepper to taste
salt to taste

Directions:
Brown ground turkey, season with pepper, salt and Italian seasoning. Drain grease from pan. Add can of of spaghetti sauce to the ground turkey. Add the brown sugar and stir into the meat sauce. Simmer on medium to low heat for 15 to 20 minutes. At the same time that the turkey and sauce are simmering, boil the pasta and drain. Add cooked pasta to the meat sauce and toss to combine. Serve in bowls with Parmesan cheese.
