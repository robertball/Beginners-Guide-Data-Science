Title:
Davy Crockett Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Oats
2 cups Self Rising Flour
3 eggs
1 cup Brown Sugar
1 cup White Sugar
1 cup Vegetable Oil
2 cups Chocolate Chips
2 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In large bowl cream the eggs, sugars, and vanilla.  Add oil.  In a seperate bowl mix flour, oats.  Gradually add flour mixture to the sugar mixture.  Stir untill completely mixed then fold in the chocolate chips.  Grease a cookie sheet and pour the mixture evenly into it.  Bake for 13 minutes (will be gooey).  Let cool for 3 minutes then cut into squares and cool on a wire rack for 10 mins.  Store in an airtight container.

Description: 
Yummy chocolate chip oatmeal cookies, passed down through our family that are a pleaser at any party, family reunion or Holiday mixer.
