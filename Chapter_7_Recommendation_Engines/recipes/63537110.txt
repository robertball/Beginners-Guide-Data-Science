Title:
Cheesy Creamy Au Gratin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled
6 tablespoons unsalted butter
2 tablespoons flour
1 1/2 cups milk, room temp (may use skim if cutting fat/calories)
1/2 cup half n half, room temp(may use fat free if cutting fat/calories)
3 slices American cheese, cut into 1 inch pieces
8 ounce sharp cheddar cheese spread, room temp (I prefer Merkt's brand)
8 ounces fine shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
1/4 teaspoon salt
fresh ground black pepper to taste
couple pinches garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Butter a 9 x 13 baking dish. Sprinkle bottom with a few pinches of garlic powder. Set aside.
Slice potatoes 1/4 inch thick, cutting larger slices in half. Place in large pot and cover with water. Add about 1/4 teaspoon salt. Bring to boil; remove from heat and let sit 5 minutes. Drain.
In meantime melt butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Sprinkle flour over butter and stir or whisk in til smooth. Heat, stirring occasionally, until bubbles. Slowly and gradually add milk and half n half, stirring or whisking after each addition to keep smooth. Continue to heat over medium high heat, stirring, until bubbles. Turn heat to low and add American cheese stirring until melted. Add cheddar cheese spread, Parmesan and 1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese. Continue to stir until melted. Stir in 1/4 teaspoon salt and fresh ground pepper to taste.
Evenly layer 1/2 of the potato slices in prepared dish. Sprinkle 1/3 of the leftover cheddar over the potatoes. Evenly layer rest of potatoes over and sprinkle 1/3 of cheddar over potatoes (reserving last 1/3 for last few minutes of baking). Pour or ladle cheese sauce evenly over potatoes. Bake uncover for 25 minutes. Sprinkle last 1/3 of cheddar over all and bake another 5 to 10 minutes until melted.
