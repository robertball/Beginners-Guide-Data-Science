Title:
Cori's Cheater Cheeseburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fiesta Deluxe Hamburger Seasoning
1.5 lb. lean chili meat
1 table spoon butter
1 bag of matchstick carrots
20 oz. Simply Potatoes and Onions fresh hash brown potatoes
4 cups water
3 teaspoons low sodium chicken bouillon
1 can Healthy Request Cheddar condensed soup
1 can Healthy Request Cream of Celery condensed soup
8 ounces 2% processed cheese food
salt and pepper to taste
8 oz. light sour cream

Directions:
Season beef with hamburger seasoning, and brown in a 4-5 quart saucepan. Drain and set aside. In the same saucepan, season carrots with hamburger seasoning and saut√© in 1 tablespoon butter until almost tender, about 5 minutes.  Add potatoes and onions and stir a couple minutes until the carrots are done.
Add water, bouillon, and beef and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover  and simmer for 5 - 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Add canned soups and cheese, broken up, to the pot and stir.   Cook and stir soup for 2 minutes. Reduce heat to low.
Add cheese, season to taste with salt and pepper.  Cook and stir until cheese melts. Remove from the heat. Add sour cream. Stir well.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
Bagged hash browns and onions from the refrigerated section and prepackaged shredded carrots are easy ways to add vegetables to this soup made with lean chili meat, soup, and processed cheese after simmering in chicken broth.Modified from: Cheeseburger Soup II
