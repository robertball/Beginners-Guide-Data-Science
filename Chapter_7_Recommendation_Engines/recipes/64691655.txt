Title:
Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Ricotta Cheese
16 oz Cottage Cheese
1 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
4 Cups Grated Mozzarella Cheese
1 egg
2 lbs ground beef
1 lb sweet Italian sausage
1 Jar Ragu Tomato, Garlic and Onion Sauce
1 Jar Ragu Sauteed Onion and Garlic Sauce
Pepperoni slices (optional)
1 Lasagna Noodles

Directions:
Start by combining Ricotta Cheese, 2 Cups Mozzarella Cheese, Parmesan Cheese, Cottage Cheese and 1 egg in a  large bowl.  Mixing thoroughly.  Place covered in refrigerator until needed.
Start water for Noodles, cook al dente.
Brown ground beef and sausage in a skillet.  Remove from heat, drain fat adding meat back into skillet.  Remove 1/4 cup of sauce from each jar and set aside.  Add remainder to the meat mixture.  Set to the side.
Mix the 1/2 cup of sauces set aside using half to coat the bottom of the lasagna dish.  This is important to help keep noodles from sticking to the bottom during cooking.  add your first layer of noodles, then a portion of the meat mixture.  At this time you can opt to add a layer of Pepperoni.  Another layer of noodles, then a portion of the cheese mixture.  Repeat.  Add your final layer of noodles, the remaining mixture of sauce, then remaining Mozzarella.  Bake at 400 degrees for 45 - 55 minutes or until bubbly.  Center must reach 160 degrees due to the egg used in Cheese mixture.
Let Lasagna rest for approximately 20 minutes.
