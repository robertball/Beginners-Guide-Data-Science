Title:
Spaghetti with Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 lb ground turkey
-1 White onion
- 1 Green pepper
- 1 cup sliced celery
- 1 medium zucchini
- 3-4 cloves garlic
- 2 T. EVOO
- 2 handfuls sliced crimini mushrooms
- 28 oz can tomato sauce
- 12 oz can diced tomatoes, drained.
- 1 T. cumin
- dash red pepper flakes
- 3 bay leaves
- 1 T. oregano
- 1 T. thyme
- 1 t. chili powder
- Worcestershire sauce
- Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute onions, celery and garlic with EVOO over medium-high heat for 4 minutes. Set aside. Use the same pan to cook turkey meat, adding Worcestershire sauce, salt & pepper generously. When turkey is done, add back in the onions, celery and garlic. Stir in chipped zucchini and mushrooms, then add in the tomato sauce and diced tomatoes. Mix well. Add cumin and other spices and simmer on low heat for at least 40 minutes to allow all flavors to meld together. Serve over your favorite pasta!

Submitter: 
Jenna Buescher-Hill
Description: 
Ground turkey with all kinds of veggies to bulk up a traditional spaghetti dinner.
