Title:
Seared Ahi Tuna with Grilled Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
3 red bell peppers, whole
1 yellow bell pepper (optional, for color), whole
1 red onion, whole
3 Roma tomatoes, whole
1 tablespoon chopped fresh garlic
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons capers
â€ 1/2 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes
Salt and pepper
Tuna:
2 tablespoons olive oil, plus extra for serving
1 pound Grade 1 ahi tuna

Directions:
Grill peppers, onion and tomatoes until their skins blister. Cool at least 5 minutes. Peel the skins off and discard seeds. Rinse, dry and cut vegetables into strips. Combine vegetables in a bowl with garlic, vinegar, 3 tablespoons olive oil, capers, red pepper flakes and salt and pepper to taste. Chill until serving time. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add 2 tablespoons olive oil. Sear tuna 1 minute on each side. Remove and keep warm. Place the vegetables in the center of a serving dish. Slice the tuna and place it on top. Drizzle with olive oil.
