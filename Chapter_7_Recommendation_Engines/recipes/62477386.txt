Title:
Chocolate Cookies with Cocoa Nibs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup cocoa (cacao) nibs
1 teaspoon sea salt
3/4 cup margarine, cold
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Combine the flour and cocoa powder in a small bowl.  Combine the nibs and salt in another bowl.
Cream the margarine and sugar with a mixer until light and fluffy. Add the vanilla and reduce the speed to low.  Add 1/2 of the flour cocoa mixture and mix well, the add the rest.  Increase speed for 1 to 2 minutes more.  Stop the mixer and fold in the nibs and salt mixture until well distributed.
Place dough on a piece of parhment paper.  Flatten with a rolling pin and roll out until 1/4 inch thinck. Using a biscuit cutter, cut out circles and place onto baking sheet lined with parchment paper.  Bake for 7 minutes on the top rack, then rotate to the bottom rack for another 8 minutes.  Let cool for 5 minutes and transer to a wire rack.
