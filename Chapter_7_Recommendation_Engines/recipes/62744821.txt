Title:
Sweet Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (12 ounce) microwavable frozen corn
1 cup 2% milk
2 eggs lightly beaten
2 tbsp. mayonnaise
3 tbsp. margarine, melted
2 tbsp. sugar
1 tbsp. flour
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare corn according to package directions and set aside to cool. Combine remaining ingredients in a large bowl with a wire whisk. Stir in corn. Turn into greased 1 quart shallow casserole dish. Bake 1 hour or until golden.

Submitter: 
Ray
Description: 
A delicious side dish that can be served anytime of the year, but is perfect for the holidays.
