Title:
Eggnog Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. white chocolate, divided
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
1/4 C confectioners' sugar
1/4 t ground nutmeg
1/4 t imitation rum extract
ground nutmeg for sprinkling

Directions:
Melt 8 oz. of the chocolate as directed on package.
Beat cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, nutmeg, and extract in large bowl on medium speed until well blended and smooth.
Add melted chocolate; beat until well mixed. Cover.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm.
Shape into 24 (~3/4-inch) balls. Place on waxed paper-lined tray.
Refrigerate until ready to dip. Coat only 12 truffles at a time to keep them firm.
Melt 4 oz. of the remaining chocolate in a small microwave-safe bowl on medium 1 1/2 minutes, stirring after 1 min.
Using a fork, dip 1 truffle at a time into the chocolate.
Place on waxed paper-lined tray. Sprinkle with nutmeg.
Repeat dipping process with remaining chocolate and truffles.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until chocolate is set.
Store in refrigerator up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These are amazing! If you like eggnog and you like white chocolate, you will LOVE these. They are like little chocolatey eggnog balls. A perfect holiday treat for a party or with a cup of hot chocolate next to the fire with the Christmas tree all lit up!
