Title:
Chocolate Almond Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5401912.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Almond Breeze Chocolate almondmilk
3 tablespoons toasted slivered or sliced almonds
2 large very ripe bananas
1 teaspoon ground flax seeds (optional)

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
This smoothie with Chocolate Almond Breeze almondmilk, bananas, and toasted almonds tastes similar to a chocolate-covered banana but is so much better for you!
