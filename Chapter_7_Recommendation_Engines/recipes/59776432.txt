Title:
Pork Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed pork stew meat
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
garlic powder to taste
2 tablespoons cornstarch, or as needed
8 red potatoes, diced
2 stalks sliced celery
1 sweet onion, diced
1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn
1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of mushroom soup
1 1/4 cups milk
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning

Directions:
Sprinkle pork on all sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder, and lightly coat with cornstarch. Place pork in the crock pot. Cover pork with potatoes, celery, onion, and corn in the slow cooker.
In a bowl, mix the tomatoes, cream of mushroom soup, milk, broth, and Italian seasoning. Pour into the slow cooker, stir to mix up and coat everything.
Cover, and cook 6-8 hours on low.

Submitter: 
Dawn Norby
Description: 
A savory stew of pork and potatoes to warm you inside and out. Great with crusty bread or corn muffins for dipping into stew and cleaning the bowls!Modified from: Savory Pork Stew
