Title:
Mississippi Mud Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup chopped pecans
1 8oz. package cream cheese
2 small packages instant chocolate pudding
1 stick melted butter
1 12oz cool whip
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Crust:
Mix flour and butter, then mix in pecans. Press into 9x13 pan. Bake at 325 degrees for 25 minutes then cool.
2nd layer - blend 1/2 of cool whip, cream cheese, and powdered sugar.
3rd layer - layer in pudding ( make according to box instructions).
4th layer - rest of cool whip and pecans
Chill and serve.
