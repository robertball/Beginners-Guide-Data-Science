Title:
Orange Roughy Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/69026.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) fillet orange roughy, red snapper, cod or haddock fillet
1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 dash garlic powder
1 tablespoon seasoned bread crumbs
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place fish in a greased 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Brush fillet with butter. Sprinkle with salt if desired and garlic powder. In a shallow bowl, combine bread crumbs and Parmesan. Coat fish with bread crumb mixture and transfer to prepared pan. Bake at 425 degrees F for 12-15 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.

Carole Davis

This simple fillet hits the spot, and it's quick to make, too.
