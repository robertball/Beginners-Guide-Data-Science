Title:
Boudreaux Margarita Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 oz Silver Tequila
1.5 oz Cointreau
1.5 oz Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice
1 oz Blue Agave

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Shake Well.
Serve on the rocks.
