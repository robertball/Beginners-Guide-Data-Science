Title:
Shandong  Chinese Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken leg quarters
1/2 cup of white vinegar
1/4 cup of water
1/2 cup of mayonnaise
2 tablespoons of prepared horseradish
3/4 teaspoons of ground black pepper
a pinch of salt
1 tablespoon of white sugar
butter or margerine

Directions:
Wash four chicken leg quarters and pat dry . Set aside .
In a large bowl , add the vinegar , the water , the mayonnaise , the horseradish , the black pepper , the salt and the sugar , making the sauce .
Mix all ingredients with a wooden spoon until blended .
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F .
Baste each chicken leg quarter , top side and bottom side with the sauce .
Line a baking pan with aluminum foil and brush the foil with butter or margerine to prevent the chicken from sticking to the pan .
Place the 4 leg quarters in the aluminum foil lined pan and bake for 45 minutes .
Periodically , baste the chicken with the remaining sauce .
Serve with rice or noodles and cole slaw or Korean kimchee .

Submitter: 
SONNYCHIBA
Description: 
A great chicken dish with a local Chinese Provincial flair . I learned this recipe while teaching English in mainland China . Ice cold beer is the beverage of choice with this dish .
