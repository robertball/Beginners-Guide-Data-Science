Title:
Deep Dish Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. Busquick, prepared according to directions for biscuits
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. oregano
1 c. mozzarella cheese, shredded
15 oz. can tomatoes, crushed
1 sm. can mushrooms, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Roll out the prepared buscuit mix and press into a 9-inch cake pan. Bring up the sides to make a crust border.
Brown beef and drain off any fat; add the salt, pepper, garlic and oregano. Spread over the dough.
Add crushed tomatoes, then cheese and top with the mushrooms.
Bake for 25 mins.
