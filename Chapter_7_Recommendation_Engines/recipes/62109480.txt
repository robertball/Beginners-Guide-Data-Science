Title:
Red, White & Blue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt fresh strawberries, cleaned
1 pt fresh blue berries, cleaned
1 loaf of buttered pound cake
16 oz contained of whipped topping

Directions:
Slice or dice strawberries in small-med pieces
Cube pound cake into 1 inch cubes
Layer small amount of whipped topping on bottom of trifle dish
Place layer of pound cake over topping
Layer more whipped topping
Layer strawberries and blueberries around edges and middle alternating fruit for color effect (red, blue, red, blue)
Add more whipped topping and continue until all ingredients are used!
You will have a beautifully easy, yummy and patriotic dish at the end!

Submitter: 
MEGSCOOKIN
Description: 
This is a basic, yet beautifully colorful trifle. Perfect for Memorial Day, Labor Day and the 4th of July, OR ANY DAY!
