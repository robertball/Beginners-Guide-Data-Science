Title:
Chocolate Pound cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c butter
3 c sugar
5 eggs
1 c milk
1 t vanilla
5 T cocoa
1 t baking powder
3 c flour
1/2 t salt

Directions:
Cream together the butter, sugar, and eggs.
Add the mild and vanilla, and mix.
Then add the remaining ingredients and mix again.
Bake in a bundt pan, buttered and floured for approximately 1 1/2 hours at 300 degrees.
