Title:
Quick and Awesome Pasta a la Norma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggplants (aubergines)
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup oilve oil
enough spagetti for 4 people (depending on how hungry they are!)
2 tsp salt
1 bay leaf and 4-6 peppercorns
8 tbsp virgin olive oil
4-8 cloves of garlic (depends on your taste!)
1 crushed dry red chilli pepper
10-12 medium sweet tomatoes, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1 block of feta cheese
some torn basil leaves

Directions:
Cut the eggplants into french fry-sized strips, salt lighly ands fry in olive oil until browned and fully done. Afterwards, drain on paper towels.
While your eggplants are frying, put on water for spaghetti, adding salt, bay leaf and peppercorns and cook per brand instructions.
Heat the virgin olive oil and lighlt fry the garlic and chilli flakes. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for a few minuted till soft but still holding shape. Season to taste.
Drain pasta and toss, while hot, with the fried eggplant, cooked tomatoes, crumbled feta and torn basil leaves. Serve immediately.
