Title:
Grandma Ruth's Snickerdoodle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015397.jpg
Ingredients:
For Cookies:
1 Cup Softened Butter
1.5 Cups Sugar
2 Eggs
2 3/4 Cups Enriched Flour
2 Teaspoons Cream of Tartar
1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
For Coating:
2 Tablespoons Sugar
2 Teaspoons Cinnamon

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. In medium bowl blend butter, sugar, & eggs until smooth.
3. Combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, & salt in seperate bowl.
4. Stir powder mixture (step 3) into liquid mixture (step 4) creating a dough.
5. In a small bowl combine sugar and cinnamon for coating.
6. Form dough into small balls (approx. 2 Tsp.) and roll into coating mixture.
7. Place dough balls approximately 2 inches apart onto lined or LIGHTLY greased cookie sheet.
8. Bake cookies on center rack in preheated oven for 7 minutes.
9. Remove cookies from oven, but allow cookies to remain on cookie sheet for 5 minutes, then promptly place onto cooling rack.
10. For storing cookies, place cooled cookies in a plastic container or plastic bag with a piece or two of bread to keep cookies soft.
