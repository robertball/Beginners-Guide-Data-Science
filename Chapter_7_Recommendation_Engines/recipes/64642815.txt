Title:
Quinoa salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa cooked in 2 cups water
3/4 tsp salt
1 tbs olive oil
1/3 cup olive oil
3 tbs vinegar
3 tbs fresh squeezed lemon juice
1-2 tbs lemon zest
fresh ground pepper
1 roma tomato, chopped
1/2 English cucumber, chopped into 1/2 inch pieces
1/2 cup green onion, chopped
1/2 red or yellow pepper, chopped into 1/2 inch pieces
1/2 cup lentils, cooked in 1 1/2 cups water for 15-20 minutes
1/2 cup mung beans, soaked overnight then cooked in 1 1/2 cups water for 25 minutes.
1/2 cup fresh, chopped parsley

Directions:
Put the lemon juice and vinegar in a large bowl and gradually whisk in the olive oil.Whisk in the lemon zest.When cooled add the quinoa, mung beans and lentils.Add the veggies and toss to coat.Taste and season with salt and pepper.
