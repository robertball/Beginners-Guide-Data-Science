Title:
Susan's Celestials Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1631184.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups roasted unsalted cashews, ground
1/4 cup finely chopped dates
1/4 cup finely chopped raisins
1/4 cup honey

Directions:
Mix cashews, dates, raisins, and honey together in a bowl; form into balls, about 1 tablespoon per ball.

Submitter: 
Susan Parrish
Description: 
Back in the '70s, these were popular in our health food store. When I couldn't find them anymore, I had to make my own.
