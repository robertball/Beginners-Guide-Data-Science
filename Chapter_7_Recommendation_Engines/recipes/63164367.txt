Title:
Twice Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Baking Potatoes
Salt and pepper
Butter
Milk
3/4 c. Grated Cheddar Cheese
3/4 c. Grated Mont Jack Cheese
1/2 c. Chopped Spinach(Optional)
1/4 c. Chopped onion(Optional)

Directions:
Prepare the potatoes in the oven at 400 degrees. for 1 hour.
While potatoes are still hot, cut in two. Scoop out pulp, leaving a thin shell.
Mash Potatoes adding salt, pepper, butter, and Milk to taste. Sir in Onions, Cheese, Spinach.
Spoon potato mix back into shells.
Bake in preheated oven(400 deg) for 20 min or until lightly brown.

Submitter: 
Cynthia
Description: 
Freezes well. Also doubles well.
