Title:
Beverly's Special Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-3 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
1 large onion
3 or 4 large garlic cloves, chopped
6-8 tbs chili powder
1 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 1/2 tsp oregano leaves
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp ground allspice
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1 bay leaf
2 cans beef gravy
2 14 1/2 oz cans stewed tomatoes
2 8 oz cans tomato sauce
1 6 oz can tomato paste
3 cans kidney beans
1/4 cup cornmeal

Directions:
In tall soup pot, brown beef in 3 tbs hot vegetable oil just until it changes color, then add onion and garlic and stir until the onion softens. Add chili powder, stirring constantly to mix well. Add cumin seeds, oregano, paprika, allspice, salt, and black pepper. Mix well stirring constantly so mixture does not scorch. Stir in tomato paste, bay leaf, tomato sauce, stewed tomatoes and canned gravy, then add about 30 oz of water. Cood on medium heat for 45 minutes to 1 hour, stirring very often. During last 10 minutes, add kidney beans with the juice and stir in the cornmeal to thicken.
