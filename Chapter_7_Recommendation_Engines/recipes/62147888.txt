Title:
Latino Style Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups crushed corn tortilla chips (not flavored)
1 Cup whole kernel corn (canned, frozen or even leftover)
1 Cup shredded white cheese (cheddar, jack or any mexican style cheese)
1 Cup salsa hot or mild

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except 1/2 of the cheese for the top)and  spread into a shallow 1.5 qut. casserole covered and bake at 375*F for 30 minutes.
Uncover and bake for and additional 15 minutes.
Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro and serve.

Submitter: 
Buffy Ehlers Whitney
Description: 
A blend of cruched corn tortilla chips, whole kernel corn, white cheese and salsa baked in a shallow casserole. A quick, easy and tasty side for any meal.
