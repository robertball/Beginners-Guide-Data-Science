Title:
Black Bean Hummus without Tahini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3071517.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can black beans, no salt added, drained & rinsed
1 jalapeno pepper, ends & seeds removed
1/4 cup fresh cilantro*
4 garlic cloves
1 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tbsp sesame oil**
4 tbsp lime juice

Directions:
To a food processor or blender, add all ingredients
Blend until ingredients are smooth
Remove & enjoy
Keep in the refrigerator in a storage container up to a week

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A Low Fat Spicy Black Bean Hummus Without Tahini lightened up by omitting the traditional ingredient without sacrificing taste. Pair with veggies & crackers for a healthy snack or spread on sandwich for extra protein & spice!
