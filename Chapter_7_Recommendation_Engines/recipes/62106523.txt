Title:
Chicken with a creamy marsala sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp butter
1/4 cup flour
coarse salt & pepper to taste
2lbs chicken tenders
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup marsala
1 cup heavy cream
1 tsp ground mustard

Directions:
Heat a large skillet over medium and add 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 turns of the pan and add 2 tbsp butter. Place flour in a shallow dish and season chicken with the salt and pepper then dredge the tenders in flour and add to the pan. Cook 7-8 min. until browned. Add the marsala and scrape up browned bits as the marsala cooks down. 30 seconds. Add chicken stock to skillet. Combine cream and mustard and add to stock. Let simmer for 10 min. and serve over rice
