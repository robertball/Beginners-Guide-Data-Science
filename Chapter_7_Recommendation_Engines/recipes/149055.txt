Title:
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats® Pumpkin Faces Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982785.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal or Kellogg's® Cocoa Krispies™ cereal
Candy corn
Jellied orange slices
Decorator frosting
Licorice

Directions:
Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Stir until well coated.
Using 1/2 cup measure coated with cooking spray, portion and shape mixture into pumpkins. Create eyes, nose, mouth and ears with candy corn, jellied orange slices and frosting. Use pieces of licorice stick for stems. Wrap individually in plastic wrap. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
For a fun Halloween activity, gather the kids around to adorn these Rice Krispies Treats® with pumpkin faces.
