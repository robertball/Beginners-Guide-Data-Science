Title:
Wonderful Soft Garlic Bread for the Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 9 oz. milk
- 3 cloves minced garlic (garlic lovers can add more)
- 3 cups bread flour
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 2 tbsp. butter or margarine

Directions:
Add ingredients to bread pan according to manufacturer's instructions.  Use "basic" bread setting.

Submitter: 
KATHYP100
Description: 
This delicious garlic bread has a wonderfully soft crust and will fill your kitchen with a mouthwatering aroma while baking.  The garlic can be adjusted to suit individual tastes.
