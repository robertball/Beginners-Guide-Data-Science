Title:
Tammy's Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz pkg. spaghetti
4 oz jar sliced mushrooms, with liquid
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 soup can of milk
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 cup grated Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
1 whole chicken, fully cooked and deboned; OR 3 large chicken breasts, fully cooked and diced
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Cook spaghetti according to package directions; drain.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl (using only half of the Parmesan cheese) and mix thoroughly.  Pour into a greased oblong casserole dish.  Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese.  Cover with foil.  Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Princess Tanya
Description: 
A yummy chicken-pasta dish.  Great with a salad.  Makes a lot so prepare for leftovers.
