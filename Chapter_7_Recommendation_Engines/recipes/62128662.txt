Title:
Grilled Potatoes, Onions and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large Red potatoes unpeeled
1 large onions sliced
1 medium Green Bell Pepper sliced
1 medium Red Bell Pepper sliced (may substitute another green or yellow pepper)
1 stick salted butter
1 tbsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
2 garlic cloves chopped finely (or 1 tsp garlic powder)
Heavy Duty Aluminum Foil

Directions:
Scrub potatoes and slice thinly.
Layer potatoes, onions and peppers on foil
Season with salt, pepper and garlic
Slice the butter stick over the top layer
Seal foil tightly on all sides.
Place on hot grill (or preheated 375 degree oven) for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Easy to make side dish for any Bar B Q! Sliced potatoes, onions and bell peppers wrapped in foil and cooked on a hot grill!  Ummm Good!
