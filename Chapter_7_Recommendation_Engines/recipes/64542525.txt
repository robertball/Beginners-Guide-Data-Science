Title:
Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups pumpkin
2/3 cup brown sugar
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
2 well beaten eggs
1/2 cup milk
2/3 cup evaporated milk or cream

Directions:
Blend together pumpkin and spices and stir in eggs and milk and cream
Pour into unbaked pie crust and bake 325 for about 1 hour
