Title:
Best Yet Banana Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup flour
1 t. baking powder
1 t. baking soda
3 bananas, mashed
3/4 c. sugar
1 egg beaten
1/3 c. butter, melted

Directions:
Mix together flour, baking powder and baking soda in a medium bowl, set aside. Blend bananas, sugar, egg and butter in a separate bowl. Combine flour mixture, stirring well; fill 48 greased mini muffin cups, 2/3 full. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
cathy
Description: 
When these muffins are used on a breakfast buffet table along with an array of other breakfast treats, they will steal the show! They are moist and full of banana flavor!
