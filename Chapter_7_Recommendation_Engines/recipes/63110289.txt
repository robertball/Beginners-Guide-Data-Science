Title:
Pineapple Habanero Hot Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs habanero peppers, stemmed
1 pint apple cider vinegar
3/4 cup lime juice
1 cup fresh pineapple
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tbs garlic powder

Directions:
Puree peppers and pineapple in a blender.
In a pot, combine pepper mixture with the other ingredients.
Slowly simmer for 30 minutes or until desired thickness.
Store in fridge for a month.  Stores longer when properly canned.
