Title:
Cinnamon Apple Cookies (Sugar Free!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097587.jpg
Ingredients:
3 green apples
1 tablespoon butter
1 1/4 cups hot water
3 tablespoons chia seeds
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoons liquid stevia
4 chopped figs
2 tablespoons cinnamon
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup oat flour
1 cup brown rice flour
2 teaspoons baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F.
Dice apples (leave skins)
Heat butter and diced apples in a sauce pan until mushy. (20 minutes)
In high speed blender add chia seeds to hot water.
Let sit for at least 5 minutes.
Add apples, stevia, figs, salt to chia seed mixture.
Blend until fully mixed.
In a bowl mix flours, baking soda, cinnamon and walnuts.
Add liquid mixture and blend together thoroughly.
Spoon out onto cookie sheet or parchment paper.
Bake for 12-15 minutes.
Let cool and store.
