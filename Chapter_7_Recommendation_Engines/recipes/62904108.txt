Title:
spot-on chocolate cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723440.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
2 1/4 cups cake flour
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 cup boiling water

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). line cupcake pans. in a medium bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder and salt. add the eggs, sour cream,  oil and vanilla, mix for 1 minute with a stand mixer. stir in the boiling water by hand. using a spouted measuring cup, pour into lined cupcake pan.
bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. cool for 15 minutes before removing from pans to cool completely.
