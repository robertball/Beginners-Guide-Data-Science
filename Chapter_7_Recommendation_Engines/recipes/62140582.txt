Title:
Chicken Veggie and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast cubed and cooked
1 small onion sauteed
1 bag mixed veggies
1 Cup cubed velveeta cheese
2 small cans cream of chicken soup with herbs
2 boxes rice a roni chicken & garlic rice
4 chicken bullion cubes

Directions:
cut chicken into bite sized pieces and saute until cooked through. make rice according to directions on box but cut the cooking time short. Make sure the sauce is still thin as this will continue baking in the oven. Add bullion cubes to rice while cooking. Mix all ingredients and pour into 9X13 pan. Cover with foil and Bake for 45min at 350. Top with crumbled ritz crackers if desired during the last 15 minutes. If crackers are added to top, remove foil for last 15 minutes of cooking time.

Submitter:

Description: 
Creamy and rich! So easy and fills a 9x13 inch pan easily.
