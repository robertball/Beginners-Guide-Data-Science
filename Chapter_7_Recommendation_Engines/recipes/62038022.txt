Title:
Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Combine in large bowl:
1 lb 14 oz bag of frozen shredded hashbrowns, thawed
1 10.75 oz can of cream of chicken soup
1 stick butter, melted
1/4 t. black pepper
3/4 c. chopped onions
1 pint sour cream
16 oz. sharp cheddar cheese, finely shredded
1/4 c. butter melted
2 cups crushed corn flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt one stick of butter in microwave in large bowl.  Combine soup, pepper and sour cream in bowl with butter blend until combined.  Add cheese and onions, blend well.  Add hashbrowns to mixture a little at a time and blend well.  Pour into butter 13x9 baking dish.  Crush corn flakes and pour over casserole evenly.  Drizzle 1/4 cup melted butter over top of corn flakes.  Cook in oven for 1 hour or until lightly brown on top and bubbly on the sides of dish.  Let stand 15 minutes then serve.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
This casserole is a great side dish for breakfast, lunch or dinner.  My family loves this dish and I usually have to double the recipe!
