Title:
Artisan Panini with Roasted Pepper and Asiago Chicken Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz al fresco RoastedÂ® Pepper & Asiago Fully Cooked Chicken Sausage
4 Ciabatta Rolls
1/4 cup Guacamole
2 Tomatoes, medium-sized, sliced 1/4 thick
2 tbsp Asiago Cheese, shredded
1 tbsp Oregano, fresh, chopped

Directions:
Pan fry or grill the al fresco Roasted Pepper & Asiago Fully Cooked Chicken Sausage over medium high heat for 10 - 12 minutes, (to an internal temperature of 165 degrees). Once cooked, cut in half lengthwise.
Slice the ciabatta bread in half lengthwise, spray with the olive oil pan spray, and pan fry or grill for 1 - 2 minutes to toast the bread.
Spread the ciabatta bottoms with guacomole, followed by tomato slices. Sprinkle with oregano and the Asiago cheese. Place the sliced sausage on top and finish with the with the ciabatta top.
Cut in half and serve immediately.
Serve with your favorite salad or soup.
