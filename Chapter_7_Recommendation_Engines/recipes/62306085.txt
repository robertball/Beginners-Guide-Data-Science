Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound cubed beef stew meat
2 (10.75 ounce) cans "condensed golden mushroom soup"
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup water
4 ounces cream cheese
salt,pepper,garlic                                 2 boullion cubes,                                                saute mushrooms,  and a dollup of sour cream

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine the meat, soup, onion, Worcestershire sauce and water.
Cook on Low setting for 5-6 hours, or on High setting for about 4 hours. Stir in cream cheese just before serving.

Submitter: 
Carla Mantzel
Description: 
Stew beef, condensed golden mushroom soup chopped onion and Worcestershire sauce are combined and slow cooked. Cream cheese a dollup of sour and saute mushrooms are stirred in just before serving.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I
