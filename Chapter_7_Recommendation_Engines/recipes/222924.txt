Title:
Best Ever Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe mangoes - peeled, pitted, and diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/3 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped

Directions:
Combine mangoes, bell pepper, onion, cilantro, sugar, lemon juice, and jalapeno pepper in a bowl; cover and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
