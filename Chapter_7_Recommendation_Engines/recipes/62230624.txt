Title:
Black-Eye Pea Bratwurst Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 16 ounce packages Bratwurst sausages
2 cans 15.5 ounce of Black eyed peas (or frozen)
2 packages frozen cut Green Beans
32 ounces Chicken Stock
1/2 Ts Thyme
2 Tbls Frank's Hot Wing Sauce (or to taste)
1 Tbls Worchestershire sauce
2 Tbls Ketchup (or tomato paste)
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves minced garlic
2 cups cubed potatoes
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop onion.
Cut Bratwurst sausages into thirds
Add all ingredients to a crock pot and cook on high for 3-4 hours. If desired make a slurry of 1/4 cup flour and water and add after cooked to thicken sauce.

Submitter: 
kimberly
Description: 
You can make it this dish in the crock pot or simmer on stove. Hearty, inexpensive and easy! Serve with rice or have plenty of bread on hand to soak up the juices!
