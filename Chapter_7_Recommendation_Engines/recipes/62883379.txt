Title:
Teriyaki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695348.jpg
Ingredients:
Four pieces of boneless thigh
Vegetable oil  4tablespoons
Japanese Mrin 1/2cup
soy sauce       1/2cup
sugar   4tablespoons
salt  1/2teaspoon
water   1cup

Directions:
In a small bowl,Mix Japanese mirin ,soy sauce,sugar, salt and water.
Prepare a pan,put salad oil  and pan-fried thigh by high flame until golden brown.
Add to 1, put the lid on and tune the flame low medium about 5-7minuts.
Take off the lid,and Continue about 3minutes.Be careful not to burn.

Submitter:

Description: 
This Teriyaki chicken is My mother's recipe.It's easy to cook,only pan-fried.but taste is deep.Your favorite way of eating? make it a sandwich? On rice or main dish? So delicious!!
