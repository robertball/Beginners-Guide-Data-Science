Title:
Best Clam Dip for chips and veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/925915.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8Oz package neufchatel or cream cheese
1 6.5 Oz can of minced clams, juice reserved
1/2 teaspoon dill
4 green onion stems, thinly sliced
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese,  clams, dill, and green onions. Slowly add clam juice until desired consistency. I usually add about half of the reserved juice. Pepper to taste.
Keep refrigerated in air right container.

Submitter: 
Heath
Description: 
My mom, grandma, and great grandma have been making this for decades! I've updated it a little to add some more flavor. Serve with potato chips, veggies, and summer sausage.
