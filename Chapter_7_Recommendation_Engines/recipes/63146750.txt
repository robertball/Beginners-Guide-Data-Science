Title:
Bashed Neeps & Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb turnips or rutabagas, peeled and chopped
2 lbs carrots, peeled and chopped
4 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons sour cream
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil turnips and carrots together in enough water to cover until tender (about 10-15 minutes). Drain water then add butter and sour cream. Mash as you would potatoes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Michael St. Laurent
Description: 
A traditional Scottish dish which is a delicious and healthy alternative to mashed potatoes.
