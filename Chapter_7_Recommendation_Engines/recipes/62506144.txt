Title:
Clear Potato and Bacon Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, sliced into small pieces
2 onions, diced
3 stalks of celery with leaves, chopped
1 large carrot, diced
3 yukon gold or other waxy potato, diced
6 cups chicken stock
2 cups water
4 cloves garlic, diced
2 bay leaves
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp rosemary
1/2 tsp coriander
pepper, and salt only if needed, the bacon adds a bit.

Directions:
In a dutch oven or heavy pot, brown bacon pieces in a bit of water or oil until slightly crisp.
Add chopped onions, celery, and carrots, saute in bacon fat until soft but not brown, about 6 minutes over medium-low heat.
Add the diced potato and garlic, saute another few minutes.
Add chicken broth, water, and seasonings, bring to a simmer, cover and cook for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove the bay leaves.  Enjoy!
