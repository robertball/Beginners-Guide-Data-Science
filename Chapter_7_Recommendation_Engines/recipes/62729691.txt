Title:
Prairie Fried chicken Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/709583.jpg
Ingredients:
5 boneless chicken breast
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1 cup flour
3 cups bread crumbs
1 Tbsp.paprika
1 tsp.each of the following:
oregano,sage, garlic powder,
onion powder,pepper,
margoram and chili powder
1/2 tsp.salt
4 cups oil for frying.

Directions:
Wash chicken and pat dry,cut into chicken finger size pieces.In mixing bowl,combine eggs and milk.In second bowl add flour.In third bowl blend bread crumbs and spices.
Coat chichen in flour,then into the egg mixture,and lastly coat well with bread crumbs.
Heat oil to 325F.Fry chicken in small batches till golden,OR bake at 350 for 20-30 min.Remaining crumbs can be kept in freezer till next time.
