Title:
Black Cherry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972052.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 OZ cream cheese
1 cup sugar
1 package frozen black cherries
1 can pineapple tibets
1 cup of nuts (I use pecan but you can use
walnuts)
1 8 oz Cool Whip

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and sugar. Drain cherries and pineapple. Mix in pineapple to cream cheese mixture, them add cherries. Fold in  Cook Whip and nuts.
Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
beveri15
Description: 
This recipe is easy to make. I got this recipe from a cook book I bought in a Tea Room in Eureka Springs, Arkansas. The Tea Room closed several years ago.
