Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
bottom:
1 lemon cake mix
4 oz butter, melted
1 egg
filling:
1 lb. cream cheese, room temp.
3 eggs
1 lb. confectioners sugar
topping:
14 oz confectioners sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350.  spray a 9x13 pan, line bottom with parchment, spray parchment.
mix cake mix, melted butter, and egg till well blended.  press mixture into the bottom of the pan.  bake for 10 minutes.
beat cream cheese until smooth.  add eggs and confectioners sugar.  mix just until well combined, but not fluffy.  pour cream cheese mixture on top of the baked crust.
reduce temp. to 300. bake lemon squares till batter is set, about an hour.  cool completely, cut (into 1 inch squares) and remove from the pan.  dredge with confectioners sugar.
freezes well.

Submitter: 
Woodbine United Methodist
Description: 
Fabulous cheese cake like lemony goodness.  plenty rich, so cut them small.
