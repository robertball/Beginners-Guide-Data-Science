Title:
crock pot macaroni and cheese with broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # uncooked macaroni
1 TBLS Vegetable oil
2 cans Evaporated milk(can use fat free )
3 cups Milk(can use 2 % or skim
4 cups Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
1 bag of frozen broccoli
Salt And Pepper to taste

Directions:
Spray crock pot with Pam.Put in uncooked pasta and the vegetable oil.Stir together.Mix all other ingredients together.Cook on low 4 to 5 hours.Stirring often because it will stick.Depending on crock pot it may be done sooner

Submitter: 
Lindalou
Description: 
And easy crock pot mac and cheese.Best part no boiling of the pasta.and can be made lower fat too.
