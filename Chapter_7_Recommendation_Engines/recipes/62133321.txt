Title:
Homemade Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh basil leaves, well-packed
1/4 cup italian parsley
2 to 4 cloves garlic, lightly crushed and peeled
1 cup pine nuts or walnuts (or combination of both)
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
1/2 to 1 cup extra virgin olive oil (for consistency you like)
1 T fresh lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place basil leaves, parsley, and garlic in food processor and process until leaves are finely chopped.
Add nuts and process until nuts are finely chopped.
Add cheese and process until combined.
With machine running, add lemon juice and olive oil (in a slow steady stream).
After the oil is incorporated, turn off the machine and add salt and pepper to taste.
Pesto will keep well in the refrigerator for a week or little longer.
Tip:  Freeze small portions in small air-tight containers and they will be handy to throw in soups, or sauces, etc.

Submitter: 
Peggy
Description: 
A fresh tasting pesto that can easily be used alone on pasta, crostini, pizza or add some to your favorite spaghetti sauce for a delicious special flavor!
