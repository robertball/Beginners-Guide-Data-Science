Title:
Broccolini with Chili Lime Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984428.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch Mann's BroccoliniÂ®
1/3 cup peaches or mangoes, pureed (fresh or bottled)
3 tablespoons mango chutney, finely chopped
2 tablespoons lime juice, fresh
1 teaspoon serrano chiles, finely chopped
2 tablespoons red chiles, finely chopped
3/4 teaspoon cumin, ground
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Blanch Mann's Broccolini. Set aside in refrigerator. Combine all ingredients for dip. Chill until needed and serve with whole Broccolini spears.

Description: 
Blanched Broccolini spears are served with a zesty dip. This fun combo will spice up any gathering.
