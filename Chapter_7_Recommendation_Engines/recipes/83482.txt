Title:
Hot Shrimp Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981429.jpg
Ingredients:
Salad Mix:
24 jumbo shrimp, cleaned and cooked
1 pound lettuce assortment
1 fresh pineapple, cored and cut into 8 one-inch pieces
1 large purple onion, cut into rings
2 avocados, sliced
1/2 pint fresh blueberries
Dressing:
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
1 pinch Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup olive oil
Juice of 1/2 lime
1 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
1 bunch mint, chopped (no stems)
1 tablespoon sugar

Directions:
Make dressing and set aside.
Mix together lettuce assortment, onion rings and avocado slices.
Heat skillet and grill pineapple rings.  Remove pineapple and grill the shrimp.
Place pineapple and shrimp on salad.  Top with dressing and blueberries.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
This colorful salad features flavorful sauteed shrimp and pineapple on a bed of greens topped with purple onion, avocados, and fresh blueberries; all jazzed up with a dressing starring lime juice, fresh mint, and a creamy slaw dressing.
