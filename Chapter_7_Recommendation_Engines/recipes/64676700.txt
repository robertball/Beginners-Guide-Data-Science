Title:
Fresh Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pounds fresh green beans
2 tbs unsalted butter
10 ounces button mushrooms
3 large shallots
1/3  cup all purpose flour
1 cup half and half
1 cup chicken broth
1 tsp soy sauce
Canola or vegetable  oil

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350*
Spray 2 1/2 quart casserole dish with cooking spray (butter flavor)
Trim green beans and halve crosswise
Bring pan of salted water to boil, add green beans and cook until tender but still crisp (4 min)
Drain, rinse and pat dry
Slice mushrooms
Mince 3 tbs of shallots
Melt butter in sauce pan and add mushrooms and cook until they are browned, stirring frequently (5-7min), add 3 tbs of minced shallots and cook until softened (2-3 min)
Add 3 tbs flour stirring
Slowly add half and half, broth, and soy sauce and bring to a boil while stirring
Reduce head to a simmer, stir until thickened ( 5 min) Stir in green beans, season with salt and pepper and transfer to casserole dish. (This can be prepared in advance, covered and refrigerated.)
Bake casserole for 20 mins (30 mins if refrigerated)
While casserole is baking slice remaining shallot into 1/8 in rounds. Place remaining flour in bowl and dredge shallot rings to coat evenly.
Fry rings in oil until golden brown, drain on paper towels
Remove casserole from oven and top with fried shallots.
