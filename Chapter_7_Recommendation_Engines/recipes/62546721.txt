Title:
Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bottom layer:
1 cup butter, melted
2 cups flour
1/2 cup powdered sugar
Topping:
4 eggs well beaten
2 cups sugar
6 TBSP. lemon joice
4 TBSP. flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together for the bottom layer.  Pat in 9 x 13 pan and bake for 20 minutes in preheated 350 degree oven.
Meanwhile, mix all ingredients together for the topping.  Pour over hot crust and bake for 20 - 25 minutes.  Dust with powdered sugar while hot.

Submitter: 
maggie
Description: 
My mother's lemon square recipe.  They are always requested and never last long whenever I make them.
