Title:
Slow-Cooker Posole, Nana's version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon canola oil
1 (2 pound) boneless pork loin roast, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 (28.5 ounce) can El Pato enchilada sauce
2 (15.5 ounce) cans white hominy,mexican style drained
1 onion, sliced
2 Poblano green chilies, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Heat the canola oil in a skillet over high heat. Add the pork; cook and stir just until meat is browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
Place the meat in a 4 quart slow cooker. Pour the enchilada sauce over the meat. Top with the hominy, onion, chilies, garlic, cayenne pepper, and oregano. Pour in enough water to fill the slow cooker.
Cover, and cook on High for 6 to 7 hours. Stir in the cilantro and salt. Cook on Low for 30 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Lara
Description: 
Made in a slow cooker, this streamlined version of classic Mexican Posole combines traditional ingredients such as hominy, green chiles, garlic, onions, oregano, and enchilada sauce with cubed pork.Modified from: Slow-Cooker Posole
