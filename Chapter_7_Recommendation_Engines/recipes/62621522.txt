Title:
Super Moist Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounce cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup firmly packed brown suger
2 eggs
1 to 1 1/4 cup over ripe bananas, peeled and mashed
1/2 teaspoon vaniila
1 1/3 cup flour
1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
1 teaspon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt (omit if using salted butter)
Streusel:
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 to 1 teaspoon cinnamon (to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. (To 325 if using dark coated baking pan.) Grease a 9 x 5 loaf pan.
In small bowl mix the streusel ingredients, the brown sugar and cinnamon and set aside.
In a mixing bowl cream the cream cheese and butter. Add both sugars and vanilla and beat until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time blending in well after each addition. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg and salt; stir in by hand using spoon just until blended. Stir in bananas using spoon till blended being careful not to over mix.
Spread 1/2 the batter in the prepared loaf pan. Sprinkle the top evenly with 1/2 the streusel mixture. Spoon the remaining batter evenly over the streusel, carefully spread evenly to cover. Sprinkle remaining streusel over top. Bake for 45 to 55 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Be sure to check 5 minute before end of bake time, you do not want to over bake.

Submitter: 
FunwithFood
Description: 
This banana bread is not a light and fluffy type, but more dense and moist. The cinnamon streusel filling and topping provide a wonderful taste addition!
