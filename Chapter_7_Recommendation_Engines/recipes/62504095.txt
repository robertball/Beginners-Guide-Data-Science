Title:
Spaghetti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. spaghetti noodles
1.5 lb. hamburger
1/2 onion
4 cups tomato sauce
2-3 cups sour cream
3 cups mozza cheese

Directions:
Saute onion and hamburger, and mix with tomato sauce.
In a 9 x 13 pan, layer saucy noodles, sour cream, mozza cheese, and then repeat. Bake at 350 F until cheese is melted and warmed through (about 40 minutes).
