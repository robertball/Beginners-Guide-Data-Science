Title:
Tangy Turkey Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Orange Marmalade
Flour tortilla
Turkey slices
Shredded Swiss cheese
Shredded carrots
Mustard

Directions:
Spread marmalade on tortilla.
Top with turkey, cheese and carrots.
Drizzle with mustard.
Roll into a wrap.
