Title:
Miso Marinated Black Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Black Cod (4 fillets)
1/4 cup sake
1/4 cup mirin
4 tblsp white miso paste
3 tblsp sugar

Directions:
Mix the marinade ingredients thoroughly and set aside.
Pat the fish fillets dry with paper towels and put in marinade for at least 24 hours
Preheat oven to 400 degrees and fire up the grill at the same time
Lightly wipe off (with fingers) any excess miso marinade clinging to the fish but do not rinse.
Place fish on the grill and lightly grill on both sides until the surface turns brown
Transfer fish fillets to the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes.
Optional: Heat extra marinade on the stove in a saucepan and drizzle on fish

Submitter: 
Emi Goya
Description: 
Sweet marinated black cod grilled to a soft buttery fish
