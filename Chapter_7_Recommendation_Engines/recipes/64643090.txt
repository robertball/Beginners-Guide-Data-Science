Title:
Egg on a bagel Hole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4519222.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons dried onion powder - 2 teaspoons dried garlic powder - 1 teaspoon salt teaspoon freshly ground black pepper - 6 sesame bagels , split in half - 2 teaspoons unsalted butter - 6 eggs -  1 cup cheddar cheese  - 1 teaspoons of Dry oregano

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.Using a 1- to 2-inch round cutter, cut a larger, more uniform hole in the center of the bagel - Put butter on every bagels put on a baking pan - Gently crack 1 egg in the hole of the bagel -  Sprinkle onion, garlic powder, sel and pepper and the cheddar cheese and dry oregano over the center of the egg - Place pan in the oven; bake at 375 5 to 10 minutes or until the white is set and the yolk is slightly runny - Serve immediately

Submitter:

Description: 
COOK YOUR EGG INTO A BAGEL HOLE AND VOILA! A MUCH SIMPLER AND FASTER WAY TO GET YOUR PERFECT BREAKFAST ON THE TABLE. EASY TO MAKE, DELICIOUS ALL YOUR FAMILY WILL LOVE IT .
