Title:
Quick Tuna and Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990525.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups water
1 (5.7 ounce) package Knorr® Rice Sides™ - Cheddar Broccoli
1 cup frozen green peas, partially thawed*
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
2 tablespoons I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!® Spread

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in 12-inch skillet.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
Reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, 12 minutes or until rice is tender.

Submitter: 
Knorr
Description: 
Tuna, green peas, and a savory cheddar and broccoli flavored rice mix make a quick, flavorful main dish in only 20 minutes.
