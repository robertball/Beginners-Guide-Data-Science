Title:
Unstuffed Cabbage Roll II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef (optional)
1 large onion, chopped
1 small head cabbage, chopped
1 cup rinsed Basmati white rice
2 (14 oz) cans diced tomatoes
1 (10 oz) can tomato soup (or tomato sauce)
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 cup water
1 tsp sugar (optional)
5 cloves garlic, chopped fine
2 teaspoons Mrs. Dash (Salt-Free | No MSG)
1 tbsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat a Dutch oven or large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and/or onion in the hot Dutch oven until browned, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease. Add cabbage, tomatoes, tomato soup, water, vinegar, garlic, sugar, salt, and pepper and bring to a boil. Cover Dutch oven, reduce heat, and simmer until cabbage is tender, about 30 - 35 minutes.
