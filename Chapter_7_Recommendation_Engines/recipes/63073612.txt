Title:
Mustard Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/779716.jpg
Ingredients:
4 to 5 bunches of fresh flat leaf mustard green (you can substitute frozen chopped mustard greens)
2 ham hocks
2 tablespoons seasoned salt, or to taste
2 tablespoons garlic powder, or to taste
1 tomato, sliced
1/2 red onion, sliced

Directions:
In a large stock pot, cover ham hocks with water.  Add seasoned salt and garlic powder.  Bring to a boil and simmer for 1 hour.
Chop greens and wash them.  Place them in the stock pot and add seasoning to taste.  Let simmer for 3 hours.
Garnish with sliced tomato and onion when serving.
