Title:
sweet and sour spareribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs sideribs(spareribs)
1/4tsp salt
2tsp pepper
4Tbsp brown sugar
4Tbsp cornstarch
1/2cup white vinegar
1/2cup cold water
2can tomato soup
1can pineapple tidbits with juice
2tsp soy sauce

Directions:
cut ribs into 2bone pieces. place on a cookie sheet,sprinkle with salt and pepper and bake at 350 for about 30min or browned.
Mix everything else is a roasting pan, then add browned ribs. Cover ribs with sauce.
With lid on pan, roast at 325 for at least 1 hr, but 2hrs the meat will fall off bone.

Submitter: 
Eirene
Description: 
These are your 'stick to your ribs' kinda ribs. so good with rice.
