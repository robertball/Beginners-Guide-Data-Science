Title:
home made ceasar dressing Recipe

Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp pepper
1 Tbls parmesean cheese
1/2 tsp worchestershire sauce
1/4 tsp finely grated garlic

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and serve.
