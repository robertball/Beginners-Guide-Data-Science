Title:
Chris Costello's Crawfish Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/348860.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. block of Velveeta blanco
1 bag of crawfish tail meat
1 can Rotel
Cajun seasoning to taste

Directions:
Melt the Velveeta in a crock pot with the Rotel. Turn it down to the low setting then add the crawfish meat and season to taste.
