Title:
Caramelized Onion Chicken Grillers with Onions and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-28 oz al fresco Caramelized Onion Chicken Grillers 2 cups Vidalia onion, thinly sliced
1 cup white mushrooms, thinly sliced 3 tbsp vinegar, balsamic 1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper 8 rolls, deli style, low sodium

Directions:
Spray a small saute pan with cooking spray, and saute the onions and mushrooms over medium high heat for 8-10 minutes or until the onions are caramelized. Add balsamic vinegar and season with black pepper.
Meanwhile, heat a skillet over medium heat and coat with cooking spray. Pan grill caramelized onion chicken patties for 6 minutes on both sides, to an internal temperature of 165 degrees .
To assemble: slice the deli rolls in half, top with the grilled patties, and top with the onion and mushrooms. Serve immediately.
