Title:
Vodka Soup with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985604.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken or vegetable broth
1 (15 ounce) container BUITONIÂ® Refrigerated Vodka Sauce
1 cup half-and-half
2 cups sliced mushrooms
2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Combine broth, sauce and half-and-half in medium saucepan. Cook over medium-high heat. When soup begins to simmer, add mushrooms; cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes. Stir in basil.
