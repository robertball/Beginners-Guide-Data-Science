Title:
WW Southern Style oven fried chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all purpose floor
1/4tsp salt
1/8 tsp cayenne
3 Oz low fat buttermilk
3/4 cups cornflake crumbs
4  - 4oz chicken breats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 365F.
Lightly coat pan  with non stock cooking spray
Combine floor,  salt and cayenne pepper
Roll chicken in flour,  dip chicken in milk and then roll in corn flakes
Bake until tender and no longer pink - approximately 20 minutes
