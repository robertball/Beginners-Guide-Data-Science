Title:
Merangue cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289864.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Egg white; room temp
1/4 tsp Cream of tarter
3/4 cup Sugar
~~ -- variation 1 -- ~~
1 cup Butterscotch chips
1 cup Walnut ; chopped
~~ -- variation 2 -- ~~
drops Mint extract
drops Green food coloring
1 cup crushed candy cains (optional)
~~ -- variation 3 -- ~~
1 cup Mini chocolate chips
1 tablespoon Instant coffee powder ; dissolved in 1 TBS hot water

Directions:
preheat oven to 250Â°
mix egg whites and cream of tarter in mixer at low speed till mixed and frothy.
slowley add sugar
beat at medium-high speed about 5 min till stiff peacks form and glossy
fold in one of the options above
put mixture in a quart sized ziplock bag. cut off one of the bottom corners. pipe onto lightly greased cookie sheets into quarter sizes drops.
bake in oven for 1 hour. turn oven off  and leave them in oven for 30 minutes
