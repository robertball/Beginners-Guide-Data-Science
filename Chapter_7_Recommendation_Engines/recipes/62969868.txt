Title:
Herbed Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/734302.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c Flour (all purpose)
4 t Baking Powder
1 t sea salt
1/2 t pepper
1 t dried parsley*
1 sprig fresh thyme*, leaves only, chopped
1T fresh chives* chopped
4T Butter - soft but not liquid
1 large egg
1 cup water

Directions:
Combine Flour, baking powder, salt, pepper, herbs - mix well.
Cut Butter into dry ingredients using a fork.
Beat egg and combine with water
slowly add liquid to the dry, mixing only to moisten and combine.. do not over mix.
drop by spoonfuls into simmering broth or soup as desired, cover and simmer for 15 minutes or until cooked through.
