Title:
Cheese Tortellini Caesar Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978856.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package BUITONI® Refrigerated Three Cheese Tortellini, prepared according to package directions, chilled
1 (10 ounce) package ready-to-use romaine lettuce
3/4 cup creamy Caesar salad dressing
1/3 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1 cup diced fresh tomatoes
1 cup Caesar-style croutons

Directions:
COMBINE pasta, lettuce, dressing and cheese in medium bowl. Toss to coat; top with tomatoes and croutons. Season with ground black pepper.

Submitter: 
Buitoni®
Description: 
The three cheese tortellini's cheese filling perfectly compliments the creamy and tangy Caesar dressing. Serve with your favorite warm bread and cool glasses of Perrier water.
