Title:
Hot & Spicy Chili Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced fat cream cheese
1 (15 ounce) can chili, any variety
1 tablespoon crushed red peppers
1 cup reduced fat shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Soften and spread cream cheese on bottom and sides of 9-inch microwavable pie dish. Mix chili with red pepper and spread over cream cheese. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
Microwave on high for 3 minutes or until mixture is hot and cheese is melted.
Serve hot with corn chips or tortilla chips.
