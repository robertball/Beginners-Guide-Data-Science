Title:
Jill's Dutch Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 potatoes
1 medium onion
2 pork chops
3 tbsp butter
salt to taste
pepper to taste
2 granny smith apple
2-3 cups mayonnaise
dutch zoetzuur pickles
dutch silver onions
1 medium tomato or 5-8 cherry/grape tomatoes
2 hard-boiled eggs

Directions:
Peel, quarter, and boil potatoes until tender, set aside in large mixing bowl
Salt and pepper pork chops
Melt butter in pan until light brown, and fry pork chops until done (do not drain pan)
Filet and dice pork chops, and re-fry in pan until crispy.
Top potatoes with diced pork chops and drippings
Refrigerate potatoes and pork for about 30 minutes, or until cool
Peel and dice the onion
Peel, core, and dice apples
Add to onions and apples  to bowl with 2 cups of mayonnaise
With two forks break apart potatoes, and toss thoroughly but GENTLY (do not overmix). salad will be slightly dry and chunky
Spoon onto platter or bowl, making a mound
"Frost" with mayonnaise to cover
Garnish with silver onions, slices of pickle, tomato and egg

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
This potato salad is great when you have leftover pork chops and boiled potatoes, or made from scratch. This is a meal potato salad, served during summer with buttered white toast and hot coffee.
