Title:
Garlic Chili Roasted Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg firm tofu
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp smokey papkrika
1 tsp chili powder
1 tblspn Worstershire

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°F - roast setting if applicable
Pat tofu dry and cut into 1/2- to 3/4-inch cubes.
Whisk together all oil and spices in a medium shallow dish or large sealable plastic bag.
Add the tofu; gently toss to combine.
(OK to marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour or up to 4 hours, gently stirring once or twice - but not necessary)
Spread out on a large baking sheet, making sure the pieces are not touching.
Roast, gently turning halfway through, until golden brown, about 20 minutes.
