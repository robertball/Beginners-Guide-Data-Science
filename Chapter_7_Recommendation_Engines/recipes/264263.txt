Title:
Paleo and Vegan Tropical Acai Bowl for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5490962.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen mango chunks
1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
7 ounces frozen unsweetened acai pulp (such as SambazonÂ®)
1 cup iced water, or as needed
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
1 teaspoon chia seeds
1 teaspoon agave nectar

Directions:
Place mango, pineapple, acai, water, and lime juice in a blender; pulse until smooth and creamy. Pour mixture into 2 bowls; sprinkle with shredded coconut and chia seeds. Drizzle with agave.

Submitter: 
Fioa
Description: 
Start the day with this quick and easy acai bowl made with pineapple, mango, and chia seeds. Made in just minutes and totally ideal for Paleo and vegan diets.
