Title:
Kitchen Cupboard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/625960.jpg
Ingredients:
2 parts crushed french fried onions
1 part crushed Ruffles
1 part Parmesan
-garlic powder
-onion powder
-salt and pepper *all -'to taste'
melted butter (cool before coating)
chicken breast
*I make this with oven fresh potato wedges' recipe from this site and use remaining par from that..

Directions:
Simple.
Preheat oven 375-400 (for boneless)
Crush Together- 2 parts french fried onions, and 1 part Ruffles (i.e. twice as many FF onions as ruffles)
In seperate bowl-
ADD to PARMESAN- garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper 'to taste'.
Combine the two mixtures.
Melt enough butter to coat all of your chicken
Coat chicken in butter then dry mixture. Bake approx. 30-45 min.
