Title:
Best Marinade for Anything in the World Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of soy sauce
3/4 cup of maple syrup
1/2 cup of Dijon mustard

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
This sauce gives your food the perfect kick of salt from the soy sauce, sweetness from the maple syrup, and a tiny kick of heat from the Dijon mustard. It will complete any meal!
