Title:
Mango- Coconut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1233102.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup unsweetened coconut milk
2 cups cubed ripe mango
1 cup green grapes
2 cups ice cubes
Diced mango and shredded coconut for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Place the first four ingredients in Oster(R) Versa(R) Performance Blender in order listed;
Add 1 cup water.
Select programmed SMOOTHIE setting.
Serve immediately. Top with diced mango and coconut, if desired.

Submitter: 
Oster® Versa®
Description: 
The taste of the tropics--coconut and mango--make a quick and refreshing smoothie.
