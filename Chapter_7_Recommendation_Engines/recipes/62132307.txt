Title:
The Best Turkey Supreme and Meat Lovers Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pk (2)pre packaged pizza crust (I prefer Kroger brand)
1 jar Ragu Homestyle pizza sauce
1 pkg Hormel turkey pep
1/4 lb turkey ham (diced)
2 Sweet italian turkey sausages
1/2 pack ground turkey
1 sm onion
1 small green pepper
1 4 cup package italian cheese
banana peppers (optional)
fresh mushrooms (optional)
McCormick Pizza grinder

Directions:
-Turn oven to 350 degrees
-chop veggies
-dice ham
-cut casing off sausages, place in skillet with other meats except turkey ham. Brown, drain if necessary.
-seperate crusts
-top with about 1/3 of the sauce on each crust.
-top with ground meats and ham.
-top with veggies
-top with half a pack of cheese
-grind pizza spices over the top of pizza
bake until golden brown on 350 degree oven.
Slice and enjoy.

Submitter: 
TIARA BRYANT-STOWE
Description: 
Pizza with everything made with turkey products.

There should be two pizza crusts per package.  Use 1 or both.  I normally make one with veggies and one for the kids just meat lovers.
