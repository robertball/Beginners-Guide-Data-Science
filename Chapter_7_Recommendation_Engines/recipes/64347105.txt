Title:
Superb crockpot Kung pao chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2104135.jpg
Ingredients:
-3 bones less skinless chicken breasts, chopped into one inch pieces
-1 12 ounce 'steam n' serve' edamame blend (or stir fry blend)
- 2 teaspoons of jar'd garlic
For the sauce
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce (I use less sodium kind)
1 tablespoon worchestshire sauce
-2 teaspoons of garlic
- 1 tablespoon of Siracha
- 1.5 tablespoons of white vinager
- 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
-1/2-3/4 cup of water
- 2 tablespoons of cornstarch + 2 tablespoons of water
-1/3 cup of peanuts (optional)
- 1 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)

Directions:
- drizzle a small amount ( 1 tablespoon) of olive oil and saut√© chicken over medium heat for 2-3 minutes, turning over once
- add chicken to greased crockpot (brush a small amount of oil along sides and bottom to avoide burning)
- in a med sized bowl mix together garlic, soy sauce, worchestshire sauce, siracha, vinegar, brown sugar, red chili flakes and 1/2 C - 3/4 C of water. Pour over chicken and cook on high 3-4 hours or low 5-6.
- about 15 minutes before serving, microwave veggies according to package instructions. Add steamed veggies along with 2 teaspoons of garlic to the crockpot and stir.
- add 1/2 the peantuts
- in a small bowl whisk together cold water and cornstarch until blended. Add to crockpot, stir, cover and cook an additional 10-15 minutes.
- serve on brown rice with remaining peanuts as garnish

Description: 
This is an awesome Chinese crockpot recipe that uses ingredients commonly found in your home!! Stumbled upon a version of this superb recipe online a while back, and yielded it according to what I had in my pantry. Can be made healthier by using less sodium ingredients and served on top of brown rice.
