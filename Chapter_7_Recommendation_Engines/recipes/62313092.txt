Title:
Easy Baked Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
2/3 cups milk
1 egg, beaten
2 Tbsp. butter, melted
1 Tbsp. cornstarch
1 Tbsp. sugar
1/2 teas. salt
1/4 teas. black pepper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender and blend for about 30 seconds.
Pour into 8 x 8 in. buttered baking dish or small casserole dish.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 50 minutes or until firm.

Submitter: 
Avcooker
Description: 
This is a great twist to the usual canned corn and very easy to prepare.  Make it your own with bell peppers, green chiles or bacon.
