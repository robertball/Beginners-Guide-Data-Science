Title:
Toffee Malted Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Soft Butter
½  C Sugar
½  C Packed brown sugar
2 Eggs
1 sm package Instant Vanilla Pudding
1 t Vanilla
2 ¼ C Flour
1 C. Quick Oatmeal
1 t Soda
½ t Salt
1 C Chopped Malt Balls
¾ C Toffee Bits

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, beat well. Add pudding mix and vanilla. Add dry ingredients, mix well. Add malt balls and chips. Drop by teaspoons onto ungreased baking sheet. Bake 350 for 12-15 min. Cool for 2 min. before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
Laura Dobbins Corbett
Description: 
These are heavenly! A great alternative to the normal cookies. Who would have thought to add whoppers to cookies. My sitter was making these one day and the smell was so incredible you knew they had to be geat cookies.
