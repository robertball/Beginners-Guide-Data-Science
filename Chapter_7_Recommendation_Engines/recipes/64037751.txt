Title:
Red & Green Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Lard (I use bacon grease)
2 tablespoons Flour
3/4 cup NM Red Chili Powder
2 cups water
2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 tablespoons salt
1/2 tablespoon Oregano
1 teaspoon cumin

Directions:
Brown the flour in the lard until a dark blond roux forms.  Add chili powder to "wake it up", once fragrant, slowly add the water.  Bring to a slow simmer and garlic, salt, oregano and cumin.
Once cool, freeze in ice cube trays for 1 oz servings.

Submitter: 
TheCattlemansWife
Description: 
Can be made with red or green chili powder - very easy and easy to freeze in ice cube trays for as needed servings.
