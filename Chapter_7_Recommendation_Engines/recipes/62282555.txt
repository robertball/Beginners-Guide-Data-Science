Title:
Shrimp Linguini with Sundried Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound linguini pasta
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup butter
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 pounds shrimp - peeled and deveined
1 (8 ounce) jar clam juice
1 jar chopped sun-dried tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon lemon zest
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/2 white wine
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a large skillet add the olive oil and butter. Heat until butter is melted. Add the garlic and saute until tender.
Add shrimp. Cook until shrimp is pink, about 10 minutes. Add clam juice, lemon juice, white wine, lemon zest, salt and pepper flakes. Cook for 3 minutes more.
To the cooked pasta add the tomatoes and parsley, toss. Pour seafood mixture over the linguini, serve immediately.

Submitter: 
CHUMMER
Description: 
Tender shrimp get a quick saute in hot, garlicky olive oil and butter before taking a brief clam juice simmer. Toss with hot pasta, bright strips of sun dried tomatoes and tangy bits of lemon zest for a refreshing entree or appetizer.Modified from: Linguine with Seafood and Sundried Tomatoes
