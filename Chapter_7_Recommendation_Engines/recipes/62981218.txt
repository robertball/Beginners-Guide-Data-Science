Title:
Robin's Cauliflower Cheddar Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 onion, chopped
1 Tbsp. garlic Potato Topper
1 tsp both salt and pepper
1 head cauliflower
1 carrot shredded
4 cups chicken broth
1 cup cheddar cheese
1/2 cup fresh parmesian cheese
1/2 cup cream cheese
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a stockpot, melt butter over medium heat. Cook onion and garlic in butter until softened.
Stir in cauliflower and carrot and cover with chicken broth.
Simmer until caulitflower is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
Reduce heat and stir in cheese until melted.
Mix in milk.
Puree with splunge mixer until thick and smooth
