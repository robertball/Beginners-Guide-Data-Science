Title:
Asparagus Ribbons with Lemon and Goat Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. large asparagus spears, trimmed
1 1/2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
2 tbsp. finely chopped fresh chives
2 tbsp. fresh  lemon juice
2 tsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. ground pepper
1/2 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup (2 oz.) crumbled goat cheese

Directions:
Holding tip end of asparagus spear, shave 3 cups of asparagus into ribbons with a vegetable peeler.
Reserve tips for another use.
Combine asparagus and tomatoes in a bowl.
Combine remaining ingredients except goat cheese and whisk to combine.
Drizzle over salad and top with goat cheese.
