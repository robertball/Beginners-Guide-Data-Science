Title:
Lemon Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c Lemon juice
1/4 c Oil
2 lb salmon fillet
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Mix lemon juice and oil. Place salmon in shallow dish and poor juice mixture over, making sure all is covered.  Sprinkle on salt and pepper.  Let marinate 20 to 30 minutes.
Broil in oven 15 minutes, or until salmon is no longer pink in the center.

Submitter: 
Noel Keen
Description: 
My favorite way to prepare salmon.  Served with a mayo, lemon juice and dried dill sauce.
