Title:
Orange Muffins with Honey Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/465642.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 t baking powder
1/2 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup honey
1 egg
1/4 cup orange juice concentrate
1/2 t orange zest
Orange Honey Butter:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup honey
1 T orange zest

Directions:
1. Combine all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
2. Cream the butter and honey with a whisk or electric mixer.
3. Gradually whisk in the egg, orange juice and zest; mix in the dry ingredients little by little.
4. Spoon into greased or paper-lined muffin tins; bake in a 180 C (350 F) oven for 25-30 minutes. Check for doneness with a skewer or the tip of a knife.
Honey and Orange Butter
Combine all the ingredients and serve on the side.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Light and airy muffins that won't weight you down - the perfect way to start your day!  May substitute orange juice for oj concentrate if you prefer a milder orange flavor in your morning muffin.  Do take the time to make the orange honey butter.
