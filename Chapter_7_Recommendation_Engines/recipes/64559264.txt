Title:
Black Bean Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3434167.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c black beans (1 15 oz can, rinsed and well-drained)
2 tsp cocoa powder
1/2 c regular or quick oats
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 pure maple sugar or agave
1/4 c coconut oil
2 tsp pure vanilla extract
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 c chocolate chips
Optional - more chips for presentation

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine all ingredients except chocolate chips in food processor, and blend until completely smooth.
Stir in chips.
Pour into greased 8x8 baking pan.
Optional: sprinkle chips on top.
Bake for 15-18 minutes. Let cool at least 10 minutes before cutting. May also want to firm them up in the fridge.
