Title:
Almond Sherbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(525g) tin sweetened condensed milk
1750 ml water
150g white sugar
1 tsp Almond essence
a few drops green food colouring

Directions:
Pour the water into a basin and add the condensed milk. Stir well.
Add the sugar, almond essence and colouring.Stir  until sugar is completely dissolved. Taste the sherbet and add a little more essence if necessary.
Chill and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Cuppycake
Description: 
This is a recipe commonly made in all Srilankan Muslim homes, every night of Ramadan and at most special occasions. It is best served chilled.
