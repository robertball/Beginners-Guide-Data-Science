Title:
Spicy Watermelon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of watermelon, cubed
1/4 teaspoon of cumin
1/4 teaspoon of coriander
1/4 teaspoon of chili powder
1/4 teaspoon of salt
1/8 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
juice of 1/2 a lime

Directions:
Mix spices together in a small bowl
Put watermelon into serving bowls, and sprinkle the spice mixture on top
Squeeze the lime juice on top of the watermelon pieces

Submitter: 
feverdream
Description: 
Traditionally enjoyed by Latino children, this dish makes a refreshing summer snack with a surprising combination of flavors that any adventurous eater can enjoy!
