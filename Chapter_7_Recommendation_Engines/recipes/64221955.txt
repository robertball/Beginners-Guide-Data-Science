Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream style corn
1 can whole kernel corn (undrained)
1 stick oleo
4 to 6 oz. sour cream
1 box Jiffy corn muffin mix

Directions:
Mix well.  Pour into a well greased square cake pan.
Bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description: 
I make this gluten free by using a gf corn muffin mix.
