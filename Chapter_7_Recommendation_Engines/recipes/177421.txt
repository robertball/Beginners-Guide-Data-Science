Title:
La Paloma Suprema Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Don Julio Reposado Tequila
4 ounces grapefruit soda
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Add Don Julio Reposado Tequila and grapefruit soda in salt-rimmed, ice-filled highball glass.
Garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Tequila and grapefruit soda garnished with a lime wedge make a perfect cocktail.
