Title:
Graham Cracker Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Line 9 x 13 pan with whole graham crackers.
In sauce pan:
1 stick butter
1 cup crushed graham crackers
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup coconut
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Boil above ingredients for 5 minutes. Spread on top of crackers. Add another layer of crackers on top of the filling. Frost with powdered sugar frosting. Refrigerate for several hours before cutting.
