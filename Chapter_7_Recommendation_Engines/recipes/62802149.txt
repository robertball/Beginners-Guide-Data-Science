Title:
Margarita Grilled Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
6 cloves garlic
The juice of 6 small limes
6 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup tequila
Splash of orange liqueur
1/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro
1/2 teaspoon ground red pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
5 or 6 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30minutes

Directions:
Combine the shrimp, garlic, lime juice, olive oil, tequila, cilantro, red pepper, and salt in a blender; place shrimp and marinade in a 1 gallon zip lock bag and refrigerate for a minimum of 1 hour
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate. Drain the marinade from the shrimp and discard; thread the shrimp on the skewers.
Cook on preheated grill until the shrimp turn pink, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
The shrimp can be marinated up to 3 hours before grilling. The amount of red pepper used can be adjusted according to how hot you like it. The marinade is also great on chicken.Modified from: Margarita Grilled Shrimp
