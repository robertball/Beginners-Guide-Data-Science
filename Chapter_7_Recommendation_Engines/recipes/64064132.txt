Title:
Cherry Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
1 box dark chocolate cake mix
1 large can cherry pie filling
2 eggs
1 tsp. almond extract
Frosting
1/3 cup milk
1 cup sugar
5 TBSP butter
6 oz. chocolate chips

Directions:
For cake; preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9 x 13 cake pan. Mix together cake ingredients with a wooden spoon until all are incorporated. Bake 40-45 minutes or until done. Let stand 10 minutes. Frost cake with frosting while still warm.
For frosting; combine milk, sugar and butter in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted. Pour over warm cake.
