Title:
Blueberry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
6 cups fresh blueberries (3 pints)
1/3 cup sugar
1 tbsp. cornstarch
1 tsp. fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp. salt
Topping:
3/4 cup flour
1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats (not quick or instant)
1/2 cup chopped nuts
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
6 tbsp. butter - softened
1/3 cup sugar
dash of cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Mix blueberries, sugar, lemon, cornstarch and salt.
Place in an 8" square baking dish.
Cream together sugar and butter, then add dry ingredients.
With hands, squeeze topping pieces together to form clumps.
Sprinkle topping evenly over blueberries.
Bake until filling is bubbling and topping is golden brown, about 45-55 minutes.
Cool about 30 minutes before serving.
Great with vanilla ice cream!
