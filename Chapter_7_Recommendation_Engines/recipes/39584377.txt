Title:
Slow Cooker Cabbage Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz tomato sauce
1 # ground beef, browned and drained
1 med. onion, sauteed
1 1/2 t garlic powder
1 t crushed oregano
dash pepper
1/2 t crushed red pepper flakes
small head cabbage sliced thin
1 1/2 C cottage cheese
1 beaten egg
shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Combine tomato sauce, browned beef, onion, garlic powder, oregano, pepper and red pepper flakes in saucepan. Simmer 15 min. Ladle some of meat sauce into bottom of crockpot. Combine cottage cheese and egg.
Layer in following order, repeat layers until all is used up(ending with mozzarella cheese) sliced cabbage-meat sauce-cottage cheese-mozzarella
3. cover; cook on high for 4-6 hrs
