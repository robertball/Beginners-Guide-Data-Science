Title:
Pineapple and Cottage Cheese Treat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/595299.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup boiling water
1 pkg. (0.3 oz.) JELL-O Orange Flavor Sugar Free Gelatin
Ice cubes
1/2 cup cold water
1 cup thawed COOL WHIP Sugar Free Whipped Topping
1 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN 2% Milkfat Low Fat Cottage Cheese
1 can (8 oz.) pineapple chunks in juice, drained

Directions:
ADD boiling water to gelatin mix in medium bowl; stir 2 min. until completely dissolved. Add enough ice cubes to cold water to make 1-1/4 cups. Add to gelatin; stir until slightly thickened. Remove any unmelted ice.
MIX COOL WHIP and cottage cheese until well blended; spoon into 8 dessert dishes. Top with pineapple and gelatin.
REFRIGERATE 2 hours or until gelatin is set.

Submitter:

Description: 
A refreshing blend of pineapple chunks, orange gelatin and cottage cheese.  A fun recipe any time of the year!
