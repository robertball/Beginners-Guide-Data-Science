Title:
Grilled Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one pineapple - cut off top, peel and leave core in.  slice into 8 rings.  set aside
1/4 cup dark rum (I used Bacardi anejo)
1/4 packed  brown sugar
1 Tbsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
For the sauce, mix together:
1/4 cup dark rum (I used Bacardi anejo)
1/4 packed  brown sugar
1 Tbsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
place pineapple in ziplock bag, pour in sauce, mush around and then place sealed bag in fridge for 2+ hours to marinate
remove from fridge and grill on lighlty oiled BBQ (med-high) for 15 min's turning once (wait for char marks to appear before turning)
