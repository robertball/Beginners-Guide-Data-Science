Title:
Gourmet Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982319.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup hot water
1/2 cup hot milk
1 cup Gourmet Roast TASTER'S CHOICE® 100% Pure Instant Coffee
Granulated sugar (optional)
Steamed milk foam (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE water, milk and Taster's Choice in large mug; stir until coffee is dissolved. Sweeten with sugar to taste. Top with steamed milk foam. Serve immediately.
