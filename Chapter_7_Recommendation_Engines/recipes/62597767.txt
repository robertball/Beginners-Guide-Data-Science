Title:
NANA'S SPINACH AND ARTICHOKE DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 oz.) boxes frozen spinach (follow directions)
2 (14 oz.) cans artichoke hearts (drained, partially chopped)
1 (10 oz.jar) Alfredo sauce
1 cup parmesan cheese
1 cup mozarella cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup milk
Dash of cayenne (optional)
Dash of garlic powder
Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a large stock pot boil spinach by directions. Once finished, add milk, mayo and Alfredo sauce, stirring slowly. Once all ingredients are combined, add artichoke hearts, continue to stir. Add cayenne pepper, garlic powder and salt/pepper to taste. Stir. Lastly, add parmesan and mozzarella cheese, stir. Place dip into a 9 x 11 oiled or sprayed baking dish for 20 minutes. Dip will be bubbly and browned around the edges. Serve hot! Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Nanasnthakchen
Description: 
This is a family favorite. We like to kick it up a bit but you may want to omit cayenne pepper. This dip is good for a family get together or a great appetizer for work or a party afterwards.
