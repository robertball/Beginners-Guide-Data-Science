Title:
Spicy Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw cabbage mixture
1 cup coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
2 small jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons minced chives
1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
Few drops of favorite hot sauce
1 cup Marzetti Slaw Dressing

Directions:
In a large bowl combine all ingredients and toss with Marzetti Slaw Dressing. Chill for 1 hour and serve. Salad can be prepared several hours in advance. Store remaining portion, covered in the refrigerator.
