Title:
Super-Simple Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup instant rice
1 cup water
1 tbsp margarine
2/3 cup diced tomatoes
2 tsp vinegar-based hot sauce (such as Tabascoâ„¢)

Directions:
Combine rice, water, margarine, and tomatoes in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
Cover and let sit for 7 minutes, or the maximum length of time directed on the instant rice package.
Add hot sauce, stir, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
hondafifi
Description: 
Easy, tasty, and low-fat - this is my favorite way to make Spanish rice. Use more or less hot sauce depending on how much you can take!
