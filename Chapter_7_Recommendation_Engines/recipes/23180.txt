Title:
Cuke Baguette  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4577459.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) loaf baguette
2 cucumbers, peeled and sliced
1 (16 ounce) jar creamy dill dip

Directions:
Thinly slice the baguette. Spread each slice with creamy dill dip. Top each with a cucumber slice. Arrange on a serving platter and chill in the refrigerator 1/2 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Auntie KK
Description: 
This is very easy! Cool cucumbers and a mild dip top fresh baguette slices. This was served at a party I attended and tasted very good.
