Title:
Toasted Marshmallow Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup marshmallow, sliced
1/4 cup heavy cream
1 cup high quality vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Slice marshmallows THIN.
Torch or broil marshmallows on both sides.
Add heavy cream and marshmallows to blender. Puree.
Add Ice cream, blend.
EAT!
