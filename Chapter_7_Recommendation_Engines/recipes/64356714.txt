Title:
Marie's Healthy Spicy Carrot and Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 oz can Tuna, packed in water
1 cup Shredded Carrots, grated with a grater
1/4 cup Salsa
2 Tbsp Light Mayonaise
1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain the tuna well and then plop into a medium sized bowl and then plop in the carrots, salsa, mayo, soy sauce, and pepper.
Mix until nicely combined and refrigerate up to 5 days.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
I love tuna, but I cannot stand the smell, so I make this very flavorful and healthy tuna salad. The soy sauce, salsa, and spices together hide the smell of tuna pretty well, so its perfect. I eat this for lunch and dinner sometimes. It is Delicious!
