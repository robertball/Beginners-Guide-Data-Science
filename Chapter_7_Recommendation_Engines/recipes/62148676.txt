Title:
Shrimp & Cheesy Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Grits:
11/2 cups chicken broth
11/2 cups canned milk
3/4 cup quick grits
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Shrimp:
1 cup chopped bacon
1 lb.shrimp,cleaned
1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
1/2 cup diced onion

Directions:
In saucepan,boil chicken broth & milk.
Stir in grits & salt; return to boiling.
Reduce heat to low & cover.
Cook 5 minutes until thick,stirring often.
Add cheese & stir; keeping it warm.
Cook bacon in skillet until crispy;remove set aside.
Drain all but 2 tbsps. of drippings from skillet.
Add shrimp,pepper,onions;cook over medium heat until
veggies are tender & shrimp is done.
Stir in bacon.
Place warm grits in serving dish,top with shrimp mixture.
Garnish with cheese.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Lowcountry style shrimp & grits at its best!
