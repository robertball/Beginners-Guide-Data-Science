Title:
Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436740.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup- all purpose flour
3/4 Cup- sugar
1 tsp- baking powder
1 tsp- ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp- baking soda
1/4 tsp- salt
1/4 tsp- ground cloves
2- beaten eggs
1 Cup- canned pumpkin (8 ounces)
1/2 Cup- cooking oil
Frosting
1- 8 ounce package of slightly softened cream cheese
1/2 Cup- powdered sugar
1 tsp- vanilla

Directions:
In large mixing bowl, with wooden spoon, stir together the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, salt, and cloves.
In same bowl (with electric mixer), beat together beaten eggs, pumpkin and oil until completely combined.
Spread batter in un-greased 8x8 inch baking pan.
Bake at 350 in oven for 25-30 minutes until toothpick (inserted in center) comes out clean.
Cool for 2 hours on a wire rack.
Cool spread frosting over bars. (Recipe for Cream Cheese Frosting can be found in my recipes).

Submitter:

Description: 
Perfect for the fall, this scrumptious dish adds to the unique feel of the weeks before Halloween. I'm not a fan of pumpkins, but this is delicious!
