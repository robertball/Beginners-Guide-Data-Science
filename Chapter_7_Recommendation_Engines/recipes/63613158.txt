Title:
Easy Chilaquiles Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006164.jpg
Ingredients:
6 corn tortillas, sliced into quarters
1 jar Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce
1/2 cup queso fresco*
1/4 cup crema mexicana or sour cream
1/4 white onion, sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Cover a baking sheet with parchment paper.  Place the corn tortilla pieces on the parchment paper.  Spray with cooking spray.  Bake for about 5 to 10 minutes until crispy.
In a skillet over medium heat, add the tortillas and the Tomatillo Verde sauce and simmer for about 5 minutes or until tortillas are softened.
Spoon the mixture into individual casserole dishes.  Top with crema mexicana,  queso fresco, sliced onions and serve.  Garnish with Cilantro, if desired.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This delicious quick and easy shortcut to Chilaquiles Verde gives you an authentic Mexican flavor without having to go to the effort of roasting tomatillos and peppers or frying tortillas.  It can be served with eggs or chicken for an excellent Mexican breakfast or brunch dish.
