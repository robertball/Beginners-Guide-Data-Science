Title:
Grilled Tuna Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cans Tuna
1 Can Mushroom soup
1/2 Cup Milk
2 Eggs
1TbS Salt
1 TBS Pepper
1 Box Stove Top Stuffing

Directions:
Mix Tuna, Soup, Milk, Eggs, Salt, Pepper in a large mixing bowl.
Add Stuffing Mix
Mix Throughly.
Make Patties till all mix is used
Chill in Ice Box 10 min
Grill over med/high for 15 min
Serve on buns or as a Main dish
