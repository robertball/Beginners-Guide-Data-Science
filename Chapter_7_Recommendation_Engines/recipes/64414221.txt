Title:
Christmas Breakfast Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 pound diced ham
1 TBL.& 3?4 tsp.  mustard powder
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tsp. pepper
20  eggs, beaten
4  cups milk
2 cups half & half
23 slices white bread, toasted and cut into cubes
2 pounds mild Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Crumble sausage into a medium skillet. Cook over medium heat until evenly brown; drain.
In a medium bowl, mix together mustard powder, salt, eggs and milk. Add the sausage, bread cubes, and cheese, and stir to coat evenly.  Pour into a greased full sheet baking dish. Cover, and chill in the refrigerator for 8 hours, or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cover, and bake 45 to 60 minutes. Uncover, and reduce temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Bake for an additional 30 minutes, or until set.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
Sausage, eggs, bread, and cheese snuggle down in a casserole, chill overnight, and bake in the morning. It's a hearty dish worth waking up for.Modified from: Christmas Breakfast Sausage Casserole
