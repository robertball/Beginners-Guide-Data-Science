Title:
Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) package of light cream cheese (room temperature)
1 cup light sour cream (not non-fat)
1/2 envelope of Old El-Paso Taco seasoning
1 cup medium salsa
1 small red pepper, diced
1 small yellow pepper, diced
2 green onions
1 cup medium cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine the cream cheese and sour cream. Mix in taco seasoning. Spread on a serving dish. Cover with salsa and then sprinkle with green onion, peppers and finally cheese.

Submitter: 
haywardsmom
Description: 
This has to be the yummiest dip for nacho chips I have ever tasted. It isn't really spicy, but certainly very tasty.
