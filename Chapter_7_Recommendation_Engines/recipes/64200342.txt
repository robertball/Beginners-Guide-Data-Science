Title:
Artichoke Parmesan Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14 oz cans artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 cups mayo
1/2 cup finely diced onion
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a slow cooker and mix well. Cook on low for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Hot and delicious restaurant quality dip.  Serve with warm pita or french bread.
