Title:
Crispy Cheesy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4  chicken half breast
1 cup cheese crackers (finely crushed)
2 eggs (beaten)
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease baking dish. Season breast with salt and pepper. Coat breast in eggs then  breading. Place on baking dish and bake for 30mins.
