Title:
Green Goddess Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289704.jpg
Ingredients:
12 OZ Mayonnaise
1 OZ Yogart
1 OZ White Wine Vinegar
2 OZ Flat Leaf Parsley
2 OZ Chives
1 1/2 OZ Tarragon Leaves
2 OZ Spinach
1/4 OZ Anchovy
TT Salt
TT Pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a food processor.
Pulse for about 30 seconds until the herbs are finely chopped and the dressing is smooth.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Chef Chris
Description: 
A rich and creamy dressing; perfect for dipping or any house salad.
