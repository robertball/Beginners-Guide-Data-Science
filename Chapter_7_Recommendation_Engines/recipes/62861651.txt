Title:
Nana’s Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A – 1 ½ cups butter
A – 1 ¼ cups sugar
A – 1 ½ cups brown sugar
A -  1 tsp. vanilla
A – 2 eggs
B – 4 cups flour
B – 2 tsp. baking soda
B – ½ tsp. salt
C -  4 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven at 350F.
In large bowl, mix all A’s until light and fluffy. Stir in B’s and C’s.
Bake for 11-13 minutes.
