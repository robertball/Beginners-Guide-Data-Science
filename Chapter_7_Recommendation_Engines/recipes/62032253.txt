Title:
Italian Bread and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed (1-inch) Italian bread
2 large ripe tomatoes, about 1 pound, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 small cucumber, peeled, seeded, and cut into 3/4 inch cubes
1/2 cup finely diced red onion
1 garlic clove
1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano or 1 teaspoon dried
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
1.  In large bowl, combine bread, tomatoes, cucumbers, and onion.
2.  In a small bowl, combine garlic, olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.  Beat with a fork to blend.  pour half the dressing over tomato combination and toss together.  Graduall add remaining dressing, tossing to season bread.  Use only as much as needed.  Allow to stand for about 15 minutes and toss again.

Submitter: 
punkypower
Description: 
This is really good in summer when tomatoes and cucumbers are in season.  I add the bread at the last minute so it doesn't get soggy.
