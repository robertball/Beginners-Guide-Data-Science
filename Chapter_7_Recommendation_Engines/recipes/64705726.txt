Title:
Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Balsamic and Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Brussels Sprouts, trimmed
3 Tbsp. Olive Oil (EVOO)
1 Tbsp. quality Balsamic Vinegar (or to taste)
2 Tsp. honey (or to taste)
1 Tsp. Kosher salt (or to taste)
1/2 Tsp. fresh ground black pepper (or to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Wash and trim Brussels sprouts.
Coat with olive oil, salt and pepper in large bowl.
Spread out evenly on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Put in oven for  approx. 30 minutes, turning sprouts half way to cook/brown evenly.
Remove from oven and return to bowl, adding balsamic and honey, mixing to coat evenly.
Serve and enjoy!
