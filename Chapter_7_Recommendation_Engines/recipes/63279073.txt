Title:
Zucchini Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds zucchini
1/2 pound onions
1/4 cup salt
2 cups sugar
2 cups cider vinegar
1 teaspoon celery seed
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
2 teaspoon mustard seed

Directions:
Wash zucchini and slice thin.  Quarter onions and slice thin.  Cover with salt and water.  Let stand 2 hours or longer; drain.
Bring sugar, vinegar, and spices to a boil and pour over zucchini.  Let stand 2 hours or more.
Begin sterilization process for 3 quart jars.
Return the zucchini and syrup mixture to a boil for 3 minutes.  Pack into 3 sterilized quart jars and seal immediately.

A great way to use all those zucchini your garden produced!
