Title:
Leftover Ham Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1875237.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces diced cooked ham
1 cup finely diced celery
1 cup finely diced red onion
1/3 cup mayonnaise, or more to taste
1 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves, crumbled
2 cups torn romaine lettuce, or to taste
1/4 cup ranch dressing

Directions:
Mix ham, celery, and red onion in a large bowl; add mayonnaise and rosemary and stir to coat.
Refrigerate ham mixture for at least 1 hour.
Put lettuce into a large bowl. Spoon ham mixture over the lettuce. Serve with ranch dressing on the side.

Submitter: 
John G
Description: 
We enjoy using leftover ham for salads, egg dishes, and other meals. This makes a healthy dinner for two. It's ideal for low-carb diets. Garnish with olives, tomato, and other favorites such as radish or peperoncini (mild yellow peppers called 'friggitello' in Italy), etc.
