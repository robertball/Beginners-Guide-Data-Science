Title:
Stove Top Fried Rice (No Fry) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2235855.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked Jasmine Rice
1 1/4 cup water
1/4 cup Teriyaki sauce
1 small onion chopped
1 small bag thawed uncooked peas & carrots
6 eggs
3/4 cup shredded MILD cheddar cheese
Soy Sauce to taste

Directions:
Add water, teriyaki sauce, onions to pan and bring to boil.
Add in rice, stir, bring back to boil.
Cover with tight fitting lid.
SET TIMER FOR 12 minutes!!
At 5 minutes left mark, add cracked and slightly beaten eggs.
Add cheese on top of raw eggs. Recover and leave on low on stove top.
At 12 min timer mark, SHAKE covered pan to mix remaining raw eggs and melty cheese and REMOVE from burner.
Add in thawed, frozen peas & carrots and recover.
Wait 10 minutes, then fluff all with fork. Ok if little crispie on bottom of pan. Scrape and mix that too.
Serve with soy sauce
