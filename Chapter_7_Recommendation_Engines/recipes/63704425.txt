Title:
Pear Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large box Jello (lime, raspberry, etc)
1 or 2 cans Pears
1 pkg. creams cheese (8oz.)
8 0z. cool whip

Directions:
Follow directions on the jello package for amount of liquid.  Bring liquid (I use the liquid from the pears and add water if needed) to a boil.  Add Jello.  Dissolve.  Put it in freezer for a little while to partially set - thick.  When Jello is partially set put in a blender with Pears and Cream Cheese.  Blend until smooth.  Add Cool Whip and put in Refrig. to set.
