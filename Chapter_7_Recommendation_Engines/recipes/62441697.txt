Title:
Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1 lb. hamburger
½ cup instant rice
¼ cup chopped onion
¼ cup cream
1 Tbsp. parsley
Salt and pepper
Sauce:
2 cans tomato soup
1 can water
1 ½ Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Make meatballs with first 6 ingredients and brown in frying pan. Pour sauce over meatballs and simmer for 20 minutes.
Or put meatballs in casserole dish, pour sauce over top and bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
