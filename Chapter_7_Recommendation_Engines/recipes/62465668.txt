Title:
Key Lime Cream Cheese Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/474876.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter or margarine, softened
3 cups sugar
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
6 eggs
1 teaspoon coconut extract
1 teaspoon rum extract
1/4 cup freshly squeezed key lime juice
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter, sugar and cream cheese. Add the key lime juice, coconut and rum extracts to mixture slowly beating. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add flour and baking powder; mix well. Pour into a greased and floured 10-in. tube pan. Bake at 325 degrees F for about 1 hour and 30 minutes or until cake tests done. Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing. Sprinkle lime zest over top of cake.

Submitter:

Description: 
This creamy pound cake melts in your mouth.  The key lime, coconut & rum extract blend nicely to form a rich pound cake.
