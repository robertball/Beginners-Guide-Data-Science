Title:
Ma Turner's Oatmeal Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Old Fashioned Oats
1 Tbs. Shortening (not oil)
2 Tbs. Salt
1/2 cup Molasses
1/2 cup Sugar
5 cups Boiling water
1 package regular Yeast
12 cups pre-sifted Flour

Directions:
Add boiling water to first 5 ingredients (which have been place in LARGE 'pail'). Stir and set aside until cool.
Dissolve yeast as directed on package, fill remainder of cup with water and set aside while measuring out the 12 cups of pre-sifted flour.
Into 'pail' with mixture, add the cup of water with yeast and stir.  Then add the 12 cups Flour all at once.
Now the fun part...Stir all together with HANDS... Then, knead (still in the pail and no extra flour). Cover pail with towel and let it rise overnight.
Next morning, push down, put in pans (5) and let rise again.  Bake 400 degrees for 3/4 hour.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Randi
Description: 
Best Served Warm from the Oven...Can also be toasted or cold...Serve with Butter and Honey.  Yum!
This freezes well when wrapped in aluminum foil.
Also can save in 'pail' for about 3 days at cool (not freezing) temp.
