Title:
The Juicy Frog Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3227378.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cubes ice
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger triple sec
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger lime cordial
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger sweet and sour mix
1/2 cup lemon-lime soda (such as 7-Up®)

Directions:
Place ice cubes in a glass and top with vodka, triple sec, lime cordial, sweet and sour mix, and lemon-lime soda, respectively. Mix well.

Submitter: 
juliejulie87
Description: 
The juicy frog is a delicious, not-too-sweet, not-too-tart drink, ready for any party! Approved by rowdy French-Canadian women! Add more 7up® to taste!
