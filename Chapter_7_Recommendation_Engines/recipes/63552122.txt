Title:
Ground Turkey and Vegetable Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground turkey breast
3 small zucchini
1 or 2 fresh peppers
4 medium tomatoes
1 Tbsp of flour (Sauce)
1 8oz can tomato sauce (Sauce)
1/2 c water
1 tsp salt (Sauce)
1 tsp each dry herbs: red chile pepper, garlic, oregano,  basil, thyme, onion powder (Sauce)
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Wash vegetables and cut into thick cubes.
Fry turkey over medium high in a very small amount of oil, around 10 minutes.
Put  chopped peppers and zucchini put in the pan with the meat and simmer 10 minutes until vegetables soften a little.
While stewing the vegetables, prepare the sauce: Mix all sauce ingredients together in a saucepan and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add chopped tomatoes to the vegetables and meat in pan and cover with the prepared sauce, stirring gently, cooking 2 minutes until thickened over medium heat.
Pour from the pan into a heat-resistant glass dish and sprinkle mozzarella on top.
Put dish in the oven preheated to 350 F for 20-25 minutes until cheese is browned. Serve with fresh-baked french bread.
