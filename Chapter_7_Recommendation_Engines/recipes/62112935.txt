Title:
Lemon Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tall can carnation condensed milk
2 small or 1 large package lemon jello
1 lemon
15 graham crackers
2 cups water

Directions:
Boil one cup of water, dissolve jello in it and mix with the additional cup of water and place in fridge to chill. In seperate bowl wisk condensed milk with electric mixer, add the juice and zest of the lemon. Wisk for about 2 minutes.It will be very foamy. When Jello has thickened combine with milk mixture with the electric mixer. Refridgerate for at least 1 hour. Garnish top with Graham Crackers.

Submitter: 
Sweet Southern Charm
Description: 
This is a wonderfully light and fluffy lemon flavored dessert that is topped with crushed graham crackers. It tastes a lot like lemon merinque pie but a lot easier. It was one of my favorites as a child and was passed down to me from my grandmother
