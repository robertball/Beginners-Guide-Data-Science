Title:
Pasta with White Bolognese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive plus more for garnish
1 lb. ground pork
1 lb. ground beef
1 cup finely chopped onion
1 cup finely chopped fennel
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 cup finely chopped parsnip
4 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tsp. red pepper flakes
2 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. salt
3 1/2 cups (1 bottle) dry white wine
4 cups chicken stock
1 cup whole milk
1 tbsp. chopped fresh sage, plus more for garnish
1 lb. lasagna sheets, broken into thirds
Grated Parmesan
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente, drain, and reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
Heat oil in large skillet, add meat and frown until meat is cooked through.
Remove meat and leave liquid in skillet.
Add vegetables, garlic, pepper flakes, salt and pepper to skillet.
Cook, stirring frequently, until browned - about 12-14 minutes.
Return meat to skillet, add wine, and cook until liquid is almost evaporated.
Add stock and cook until almost evaporated.
Add milk and sage, and simmer until thick - about 10 minutes.
Add pasta and pasta water to skillet and cook about 1 minute.
Serve, garnished with olive oil, Parmesan and parsley.
