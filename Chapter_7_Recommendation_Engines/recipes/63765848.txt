Title:
Herb Turkey & Wild Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045095.jpg
Ingredients:
6	slices bacon, cut into ½ inch pieces
1	pound turkey cut into ¾ inch pieces
¾ 	cup onion, chopped
½	cup carrot, chopped
½	cup celery, chopped
29	ounces chicken broth
1	can cream of chicken soup (or 10 ounces homemade cream of chicken soup)
¼	teaspoon dried marjoram
¼	teaspoon black pepper
1 ¼	cups uncooked wild rice
Sour cream, Optional

Directions:
In a 10 inch skillet over medium heat stirring occasionally cook bacon until crisp.  Stir in turkey; cook stirring occasionally until turkey is brown; stir in onion, carrot and celery.  Cook 2 minutes, stirring occasionally; drain.  If using leftover turkey add toward the end to brown a little and  warm through.
in the bowl of your 3 ½ to 6 qt. slow cooker add the soup and broth and whisk until smooth. Stir in marjoram and pepper.  Stir in turkey mixture and wild rice.
Cover and cook on high for 30 minutes; Reduce heat to low and cook for 6 to 7 hours.  If using sour cream stir in just before serving

one of my favorite dishes for using up my leftover cooked turkey.  A definite Family Favorite!
