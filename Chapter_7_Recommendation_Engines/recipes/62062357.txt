Title:
tomato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium tomatoes
1 medium onion, chopped
1 large green bell pepper, chopped
salt, lots of

Directions:
Cut tomatoes into bitesize pieces.  Salt each tomato after it's cut.  Add onion and bell pepper.  Mix well.  Add more salt as needed.  The salt will draw out the tomato juice as it sits.
