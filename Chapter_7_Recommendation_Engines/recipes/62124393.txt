Title:
Chili with a Twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. hamburger
1 lg. onion, chopped
2 lg. cans kidney beans
2 tsp. chili powder
2 lg. jars Ragu spaghetti sauce
1 pkg. hotdogs
1 bell pepper, chopped

Directions:
1.  Fry hamburger, onion, pepper and chili powder. Drain and rinse beans. Add spaghetti sauce and hotdogs. Simmer for 20 minutes. Then, put cover on pot and cook for 1-2 hours.
2. As chili cooks, the flavors meld together.

Submitter: 
Julie Durfee
Description: 
This chili recipe is really rich! Kids usually love it! It sounds different than most chili recipes but the finished product is yummy!
