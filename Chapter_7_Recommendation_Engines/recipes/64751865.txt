Title:
Summer Strawberry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6785520.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Quartz Water
5 lemons (JUICE)
6 large strawberries
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup honey

Directions:
Cut lemons and juice them into a pitcher
Cut strawberries into small pieces
Combine strawberries, sugar, and honey in a bowl and mash for 15-25 seconds
Pour strawberry sauce into the pitcher and mix.
Fill pitcher with bottled water and ice.
Wet rim of serving glass with lemon juice
Sugar rim of serving glass
Pour and serve, enjoy!
