Title:
Bacon Potato Salad w/Asiago Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2360597.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs New Red Potatoes
1 onion or 2 shallots or 1 bunch green onions
6 eggs (boiled)
1 lb bacon
2 stalks celery
1 cup shredded asiago cheese
1 cup mayo
1 cup sour cream
2 tsp mustard
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Boil to potatoes, drain, cool and cut up (about 18 minutes)
2. Boil eggs, drain, cool and cut up
3. Cook bacon, drain, cool and cup up
4. Mix all the ingredients together
5. Let stand for an hour
