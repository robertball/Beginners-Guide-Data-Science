Title:
Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
4 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained
1/2 cup chopped sweet onion
1 cup light mayonnaise
2 tablespoons dill pickle relish
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt

Directions:
Place the tuna, hard-cooked egg,  and onion in a large bowl. In a small bowl, stir together the mayonnaise,  relish, pepper and seasoned salt. Pour over the ingredients in the other bowl, and stir gently to coat. Serve immediately at room temperature, or chill until ready to serve.
