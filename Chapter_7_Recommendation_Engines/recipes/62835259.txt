Title:
Quinoa with Peaches & Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/672907.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Quinoa
2 cups Water
1 teaspoon of Nutmeg & Cinnamon each
1 Large Peach, diced
1/2 cup unflavored 0% fat Greek Yogurt
1/2 Lime
2 to 4 tablespoons Honey
Sprinkle of Nutmeg

Directions:
Rinse Quinoa very well, until all soapy residue is removed.
Add Quinoa to boiling water and cook for 15-20 minutes until light and fluffy.
Drain any excess water and add in nutmeg & cinnamon.
Mix Peaches & Yogurt together.
Place Quinoa in bowl and top with heaping spoonful of peach yogurt mix.
Drizzle honey over top, sprinkle with nutmeg and squirt lime juice over entire dish.
