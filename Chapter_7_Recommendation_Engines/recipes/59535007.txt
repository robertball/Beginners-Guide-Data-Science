Title:
Budget Chuck's Favorite Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/274678.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package elbow macaroni
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2 cups Jack cheese
3 cups milk
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup margarine
3 tablespoons all purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, add pasta, and cook until done; drain.
In a sauce pot melt the margarine and sprinkle over the flour.  Stir together until a slight nutty aroma occurs.  Slowly whisk in milk and bring to a boil until it begins to thicken.  Stir in cheddar and Jack cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
Mix together pasta and cheese sauce, pour into casserole dish and bake 30-35 minutes, or until top is golden and bubbly.

Submitter:

Description: 
I left out the sour cream and cottage cheese in this recipe and let the blend of cheeses do all the work.  I also sprinkled on the Parmesan cheese for the topping and left off the bread crumbs.  When the Parmesan bakes it melts into a crispy topping.Modified from: Chuck's Favorite Mac and Cheese
