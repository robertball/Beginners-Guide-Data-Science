Title:
CREAM PUFFS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 CUPS OF FLOUR
1/2 TSP WHITE SUGAR
1/4 TSP SALT
1/4 CUP UNSALTED BUTTER
1/2 CUP WATER
2 LARGE EGGS, LIGHTLY BEATEN
EGG WASH
1 LARGE EGG
PINCH OF SALT

Directions:
PREHEAT OVENTO 400 & PLACE RACK IN CENTER OF OVEN
IN A BOWL SIFT TOGETHER FLOUR, SUGAR & SALT
PLACE BUTTER & WATER IN SAUCEPAN OVER MEDIUM HEAT & BRING IT TO A BOIL.
REMOVE FROM HEAT & WITH A WOODEN SPOON OR SPATULA QUICKLY ADD THE FLOUR MIXTURE.  RETURNTO HEAT AND STIR CONSTANTLY UNTIL THE DOUGH COMES AWAY FROM THE SIDES OF THE PAN AND FORMS A THICK SMOOTH BALL (ABOUT A MINUTE OR TWO)
SPOON OR PIPE 12 MOUNDS OF DOUGH ONTO THE BAKING SHEET.
BEAT TOGETHER THE EGG & SALT FOR THE GLAZE AND WITH A PASTRY BRUSH GENTLY BRUSH ON THE TOPS OF THE DOUGH
BAKE FOR 15 MINUTES & THEN REDUCE THE OVEN TO 350 AND BAKE FOR A FURTHER 30-45 MINUTES OR UNTIL THE SHELLS ARE A NICE AMBER COLOR. TURN THE OVEN OFF AND WITH THE OVEN DOOR SLIGHTLY AJAR LET THE SHELLS DRY OUT FOR A FURTHER 10-15 MINUTES. LET COOL ON A WIRE RACK
