Title:
Pan fried chicken ceaser Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast sliced up into bite sized pieces
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 cloves minced garlic

Directions:
fry chicken till browned
Mix the cheese, sauce and garlic with the mayonnaise and put 1/2 in with the cooked chicken and use the rest for dipping or on the bun for sandwiches or salad
