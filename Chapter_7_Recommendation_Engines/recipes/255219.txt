Title:
Bambam Basil Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/3915830.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup unsalted butter, softened
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
3 cloves garlic, or to taste
10  fresh basil leaves
¼ teaspoon salt (optional)
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine butter, onion, Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, garlic, basil leaves, salt, and pepper in a food processor; pulse until smooth.

Submitter: 
Bambam
Description: 
I adore basil! Fresh basil leaves, onion, and garlic blended with butter is great on breads and vegetables. Refrigerate leftovers.
