Title:
Homemade Laundry Detergent Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 oz. bar of Zote ($0.97)
1 5 oz. bar of Fels Naptha ($1.19)
4 cups Borax ($3.99)
4 cups Washing Soda (NOT baking soda) ($2.69)

Directions:
First, either hand grate the bars or run through a food processor with a grater blade and S-blade until finely ground. (Slice each bar into 3-4 pieces first for best results). Then combine all four ingredients and mix well. Store in a large sturdy lidded container.  Use 2 Tablespoons per load.
