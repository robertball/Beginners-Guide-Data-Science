Title:
Karyn's Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine
1 cup peanut butter
1 lb powdered sugar
1/2 lb graham crackers crushed
12 oz semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix all except chocolate chips. Pat into an ungreased 9x13 pan. Top with melted chocolate chips. They are best if allowed to sit overnight or at least a few hours. Enjoy!
