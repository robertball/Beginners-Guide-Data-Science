Title:
Aunt Jeans Cucumber salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688287.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced.
1 small yellow onion, peeled and thinly sliced.
1/2 cup of vegetable oil
2/3 cup white vinegar
Dash of pepper
Dash of salt
Dash of sugar

Directions:
Mix oil, vinegar, and seasonings in a bowl. Add cucumber slices and onion slices. Toss and marinate. This can be served right away or refrigerated for 1 to 3 days.

An amazing salad that is perfect for all occasions, including barbequing, parties or as a side for an everyday meal.
