Title:
Broccoli, Rice, Cheese, and Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1.5 cups uncooked rice
1 lb cooked chicken breast (cubed)
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2 stick butter, softened
1 cup milk
1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
1 small white onion, chopped
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium sized bowl, whisk together the water, rice, cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, butter and milk.
Pour the mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.  Evenly sprinkle in the chicken cubes, broccoli and onion.  Add the shredded cheddar cheese on top.
Bake in the preheated oven for 60 to 70 minutes (or until rice is done), stirring once halfway through.
