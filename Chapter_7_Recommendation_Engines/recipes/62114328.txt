Title:
Cranberry Cinnamon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Flour 400g
Butter 240g
Sugar 240g
Egg 90g
Dried Candied Cranberry 80g
Cinnamon 2 g

Directions:
1. Combine room temperature butter and sugar, mix until creamy.
2. Add in two eggs one at a time. Mix thoroughly, and set aside as wet mixture.
3. Sift together flour and cinnamon.
4. Mix flour and cinnamon into the wet mixture slowly while gently stirring the mixture.
5. Mix in dried candied cranberry and gently stir everything together.
6. Shape the dough into long "cubic log", rap it in plastic rap and freeze for at least one hour. Preheat oven to 350 degree.
7. Use a sharp knife to cut the log into cookie dough slices, about 0.5 cm thick. Bake until the cookie turns slightly brown on the edge, about 10 min.
