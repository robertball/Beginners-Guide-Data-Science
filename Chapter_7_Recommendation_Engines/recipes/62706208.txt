Title:
Energy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/613152.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup quick cooking rolled oats
1/2 cup raw unsalted sunflower seeds
2 large eggs
1/3 cup pure maple syrup
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup powedered nonfat dry milk
1/2 cup minced pitted dated
1/2 cup rasins
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup chopped dried apricots
1/4 cup whole-wheat pastry flour
1/2 cup toasted wheat germ
1 large mashed ripe banana

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.  Coat a 9 by 13-inch baking pan with oil to prevent sticking.
Place all ingreadients in a large bowl and mix until well combined.
Spread the mixture evenly in the baking pan.  Bake for 20 minutes then allow to cool.  Cut into 9 bars and wrap in plastic wrap to store.

Submitter: 
Hungry in Vermont
Description: 
These bars are great before a workout or when you need a quick healthy snack to stave off hunger for a few hours.  They are easy to make and do not need refrigeration
