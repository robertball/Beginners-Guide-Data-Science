Title:
Skinny Girl Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960509.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of pureed prune
1/4 cup of honey
1/2 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
2/3 cup whole wheat pastry flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
Pinch of sea salt
1/2 cup of cocoa nibs or chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together and mix well
Use a cookie scoop to create perfectly portioned mounds
Bake for 8 minutes

Submitter: 
Sadie Melgoza
Description: 
Found this recipe and put my clean spin on it. They were amazing, chewy and chocolatey.
