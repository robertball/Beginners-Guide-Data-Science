Title:
Beef Tenderloin with Garlic Horseradish Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head garlic
1/3 cup prepared horseradish
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (3 lb) beef tenderloin

Directions:
Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Remove white papery skin from garlic head (do not peel or separate cloves).
3. Bake for 1 hour; cool 10 minutes.
4. Separate cloves and squeeze to extract garlic pulp.
5. Discard skins.
6. Mash garlic pulp and mix with horseradish, salt, basil, thyme and pepper until blended.
7. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
8. Trim fat from tenderloin; fold under 3 inches of small end.
9. Rub garlic mixture over roast.
10. Place tenderloin on a broiler pan coated with cooking spray.
11. Insert meat thermometer into thickest portion of tenderloin.
12. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes or until thermometer registers 145 degrees (rare) to 160 degrees (medium).
13. Place tenderloin on a platter and cover and let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
