Title:
Mom's Seafood Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cooked lasagna noodles
1 8oz package of cream cheese (softened)
1 1/2 cp of cottage cheese
2 tsp dried crushed basil
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
2 cans of cream of shrimp soup
1/2 cp of milk
1 lb. med - large shrimp
(cooked & halved)
1 lb. crabmeat (backfin or lump)

Directions:
Preaheat oven. Blend cream cheese with cottage cheese, basil, salt & pepper. Combine soup & milk; stir in shrimp & crab meat.
Lay 4 noodles in greased 13x9x2inch pan, spread 1/2 of the cheese mixture over noodles.  Spread 1/2 of soup mixture over top, & repeat layer.

Submitter: 
April R. Smith
Description: 
This is a family treat my mom fixed for us when we were little! It's easy and very good! Different from your traditional lasagna.
