Title:
No bake YUMMY Strawberry Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Keebler Graham Cracker Crust
1 (8oz) tub coolwhip
1 (8oz) philadelphia cream cheese
1/2 cup of sugar
For topping:  1 pt. fresh strawberries OR 1 Can premade strawberry topping (Comstock brand)

Directions:
Mix softened cream cheese and sugar in medium bowl until fluffy. Fold in cool whip. Pour into crust and garnish with fresh strawberries or strawberry topping. Refrigerate for 1 hour until firm.
