Title:
Red Pepper Parmesan Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup Water
1/2 cup Butter
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
4 Eggs
1 1/2 cups shredded Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1 1/2 table spoons finely chopped basil

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Set aside.
Place water and butterin a heavy 2-qt saucepan. Cook over medium heat until mixture comes to a boil (6 to 8 minutes). REDUCE HEAT TO LOW; stir in flour and garlic powder vigorously until mixture leaves sides of pan and forms a ball. REMOVE FROM HEAT. Add 1 egg at a time, beating well after each addition, until mixture is smooth. Stir in 1cup cheese, bell pepper, and basil.
Immediately drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls onto prepared baking sheets. Sprinkle each with a 1/4 teaspoon remaning cheese.
Bake for 20 to 22 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm
