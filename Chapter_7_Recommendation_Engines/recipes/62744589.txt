Title:
Sesame Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces pasta (linguine or spaghetti)
2 cloves garlic, microplaned
1 teaspoon ginger, minced
2 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 t oil
2 tablespoons rice vinegar
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 scant tablespoons sesame oil
1/2 teaspoons chili sauce
1/2 teaspoon chili paste
3 green onions, sliced
1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds, toasted
1 carrot, julienned

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions, cooking until just al dente.
Saute ginger, carrots, garlic, and the sliced *white part* of green onion in oil until fragrant.
Add sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, chili sauce and chili sauce.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves. Pour sauce over linguine, and toss to coat.
Garnish with remaining green onions and sesame seeds.
May add sauted veggies (brocolli, snap peas, peanuts, meats, etc) if desired.
