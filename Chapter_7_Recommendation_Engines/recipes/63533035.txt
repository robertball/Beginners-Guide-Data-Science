Title:
Mock Bananas Foster Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of salted butter
1/4 c. brown sugar
1/2 t. vanilla
dash of cinnamon
4 ripe bananas, sliced
2 T. rum, optional

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium sauce pan over medium heat.
Add brown sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon, (as well as rum, if desired) stir until well combined.
Add sliced bananas, simmer until bananas are very soft and sauce is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This is our favorite topping to serve with crepes. Fill crepes with vanilla ice cream and top with this sauce and some real whipped cream. If you prefer the grown-up version, add a little rum to your sauce.
