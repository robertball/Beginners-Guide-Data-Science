Title:
Red Pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 green bell pepper, minced (red)
1 cup minced hot peppers (combine jalapenos, serranos, and a habanero
1 1/2 cups cider vinegar
6 1/2 cups white sugar
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 (6 fluid ounce) container liquid pectin
8 drops red food coloring

Directions:
In a large, stainless steel saucepan, combine peppers, vinegar, sugar, and cayenne pepper. Cook over medium high heat. Stir frequently until mixture begins to boil for about 10 minutes.
Stir in pectin; boil 5 minutes longer, stirring constantly, and mix in food coloring. Skim off foam, and remove from heat.
Ladle into sterilized jars.  Seal, and process in a boiling-water canner for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
smbeatse
Description: 
This is a pretty, very medium hot jelly that is excellent to have on hand for the holidays,  as well as for unexpected visitors. Enjoy this with crackers and soft cream cheese.Modified from: Jalapeno Pepper Jelly
