Title:
South African Pap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
1 tsp salt
2 cups maize meal

Directions:
In large pot, boil water and salt.
Add maize meal and stir thoroughly.  Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour stirring occasionally.

Barbara Bartz
