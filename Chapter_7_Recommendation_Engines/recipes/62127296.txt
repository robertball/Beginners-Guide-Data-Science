Title:
Lemon Poke Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C oil
4 eggs
1 C boiling water
1 3.2 Oz. box Lemon Gelatin
1 box yellow cake mix
1 1/2 C powdered sugar
4 1/2 T lemon juice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients on low speed for 30 seconds then on medium speed for 2 minutes.
Pour into a greased and floured 9x13 pan.
Bake as directed on cake mix box.
While cake is hot poke full of holes using a fork or wooden skewers.
Slowly drizzle glaze over cake.

Description: 
A cake made with lemon flavored gelatin with a lemon glaze.
