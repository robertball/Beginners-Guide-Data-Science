Title:
Chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2178997.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup of all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1/2 Cup of butter
1/2 Cup of granulated sugar
1/4 Cup of brown sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon of vanilla
1 Cup of chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F
Lightly grease baking sheet
Sift together the first three ingredients
Cream together the butter with granulated sugar and brown sugar
Beat in the egg and vanilla until light and fluffy and then mix in dry ingredients
Fold in chocolate chips
Drop batter from a teaspoon about 2 inches apart onto baking sheet, bake in preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes

Submitter: 
Carly-Jay
Description: 
This is a great recipe if you're looking for a chocolate chip cookie that isn't too cakey and very simple to make.
