Title:
Asian Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb snow peas (generous 1 C) trim ends, remove strings
2 c napa cabbage, shredded
1/3 c carrot, julienned
1/3 c red bell pepper, julienned
1/3 c cucumber, julienned
3 tbs scallions, minced
2 tbs cilantro, minced
1 tbs mint, minced
3 tbs sesame oil
1 tbs wheat free tamari (soy sauce)
1 tbs rice vinegar
1 tbs lime juice
1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring a pot of water to a boil.
Add the snow peas and cook for 30 seconds, drain immediately and place in ice water.
Remove the snow peas and pat dry, put into a medium sized bowl.
Add to that the cabbage, carrot, red pepper, cucumber, scallion, cilantro, and mint.
In a small bowl whisk together the sesame oil, tamari, vinegar, lime juice, and pepper.
Pour the dressing over the vegetables and mix thoroughly.
