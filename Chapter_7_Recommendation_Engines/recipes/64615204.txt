Title:
Happiness Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Sirloin 90/10
1 lb Hot Italian Sausage
1 28 oz Can Diced Tomatoes
1 10 oz Can of Rotella Diced Tomatoes w/ Green Chiles
4 Roughly Chopped On The Vine Tomatoes
1 Can Dark Red Kidney Beans
1 Can Pinto Beans
1/2 Sweet Onion - Diced
1 Green Bell Pepper - Chopped
1/3 Cup Jalapenos - Diced
5 Cloves Garlic - Minced
2 tsp Salt - Divided
3 tsp Black Pepper
2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
3 tsp Chili Powder
2 tsp Cumin
2 Tbsp White Vinegar
3 Tbsp Honey
2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
2 Tbsp Hot Sauce (Optional)

Directions:
Brown all the meat separately adding 1 tsp of salt to each meat.
Add all ingredients, except the last 4 listed, into a large crock pot.
Cook on low for 6 hours
Add last 4 ingredients and cook for another 1 - 2 hours
Serve with shredded cheese and a dollop of sour cream if desired

Submitter: 
Al Coomes
Description: 
A sweet and mildly spicy chili that just makes you happy!
