Title:
Duchess Potatoes  /  pommes Duchesse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz of Baking Potatoes
2 Egg yolks
1 oz Butter
1/4 tsp Nutmeg
pinch of salt.

Directions:
Boil, Peel and Rice/mash the Potatoes. Quickly add the scrambled yolks, melted Buttter, salt and Nutmeg.  Thoroughly mix with a whisk and let cool.  Fill  a piping bag fitted with a large Star fitting and pipe onto parchment paper, on a baking pan;   golf Ball sized "rosettes". brush carefully with Butter and  Bake/broil until lightly brown.  keep warm for serving.
Serve with;   Fish and Chicken.

Alois
Description: 
Rosettes of Mashed Potato with egg yolk, Butter and Nutmeg
