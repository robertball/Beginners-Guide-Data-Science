Title:
White Chocolate Cranberry Krispies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7660649.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup White  chocolate chips
1/3 cup butter
16 large marshmallows
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
3  cups Rice Krispies
Â½ cup Cranberries
1/4 cup candy cane (Optional)

Directions:
In Microwave bowl, melt butter, marshmallows and white chocolate chips until smooth.  Add vanilla and mix again.
In a large bowl, combine krispies and cranberries.  Pour melted chocolate mix over krispies and cranberries. Mix quickly. Add candy cane if desired.

Submitter: 
K. Lea
Description: 
Adapted from No Bake Cookies IV.  The white chocolate make this cookie rice Krispies on Steroids. Cranberries add a surprising tart flavor.  Can also add crushed candy canes for a Christmas flair.
