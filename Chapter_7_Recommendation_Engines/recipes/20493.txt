Title:
Rebecca's Rockin' Vodka Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857046.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen raspberry lemonade concentrate
12 fluid ounces vodka
1 lime

Directions:
In a gallon pitcher prepare the lemonade concentrate according to package instructions, replacing one can of water with the vodka. Squeeze juice from one lime into mixture. Pour over ice and serve.
