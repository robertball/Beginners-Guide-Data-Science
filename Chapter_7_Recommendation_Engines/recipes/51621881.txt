Title:
Brown Sugar Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
3/4 cup milk
2 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 small onion, chopped
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
2 cup finely crushed saltine cracker crumbs (ritz crackers are great too).

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F  Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
Mix together ketchup and brown sugar.  Set aside.
In a mixing bowl, mix thoroughly all remaining ingredients and shape into a loaf.   Place in loaf pan and top with ketchup mixture.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.
**or, you can place in muffin tins and decrease cooking time.  It looks more impressive.
