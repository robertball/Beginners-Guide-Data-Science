Title:
Catalina Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-8 Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Worstershire sauce
Paprika
Lawry's Garlic Powder
Mrs. Dash original seasoning
1 1/2 bottles Kraft Catalina dressing
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees. Cover a pyrex pan in foil, and set chicken breasts spread out in the pan.  Sprinkle on worstershire sauce on both sides.  Then sprinkle as much as you want of the dry seasonings on both sides of the chicken.  Spread the chicken back out, and cover chicken and spaces with all of the Catalina dressing. Pour water around the edges of the pan (inside the foil) Bake covered with foil for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Hennes
Description: 
This is a sweet but tangy chicken dish that is super easy!
