Title:
Raspberry Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. safflower oil
2 tbsp. whole fruit raspberry preserves
1/4 cup freshly squeezed grapefruit juice (about half a grapefruit)

Directions:
Whisk oil, preserves, and grapefruit juice together in a small bowl. Drizzle over mixed greens or salad of your choice and refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
When you're looking for a recipe that's berry rich in appeal, don't shy away from this homemade raspberry dressing. With just three ingredients, it really doesn't get any simpler than this. You won't be able to bottle up your emotions for this fast favorite.
