Title:
Dianne's Pesto, Chicken and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284713.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen brocolli florets
1 cup frozen sliced carrots
1/2 cup frozen corn
1 Tbsp. water
2 Tbsp. prepared pesto sauce
1/2 cup cubed cooked chicken
1 cup dry pasta such as rotini or fusilli
2 Tbsp. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, place the frozen brocolli, carrots and corn in a covered skillet with the water.  Cover and cook on medium heat until the vegetables are thawed.  Add the pesto sauce and chicken and cook until veggies are tender and everything is heated through.
Once the pasta is done, drain and add to the veggies and chicken mixture and stir to combine.
Serve sprinkled with paremsan cheese.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
I created this recipe for an easy weeknight meal.  My inspiration was the bagged meals you find in the frozen section at the grocery store.  Mine is just as easy and less expensive!  Hint: Make your own pesto sauce and freeze in an ice cube tray.  Pop out two cubes for this recipe.
