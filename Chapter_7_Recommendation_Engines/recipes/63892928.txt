Title:
Hamburger balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pilsbury Crescent Rolls
Frozen meatballs thawed
Cheddar cheese slices
Mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Make 4 rectangles with dough, mushing perforated sides together. Cut each rectangle into thirds.
Cut Cheddar cheese into 4 pieces
Put one piece of cheese on the Dough slices
Put one meatball on cheese.
Wrap dough around cheese and meatball.
Place on cookie sheet in preheated oven for 11 minutes until lightly browned.
Serve with mustard.

Submitter: 
Frances Bell
Description: 
This recipe is easy to make and to involve the kids with. The kids really like eating these (and so do grown ups).
