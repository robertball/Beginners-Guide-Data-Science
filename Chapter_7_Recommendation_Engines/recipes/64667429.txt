Title:
Slightly Spicy, Super Yummy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4574592.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef
1 sweet onion diced
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
1½ teaspoons garlic salt
1-teaspoon salt (add more to taste if desired)
3 cups (2 cans) tomato sauce
2/3 jar of mild or hot Clint’s salsa (depending on preference)
1 packet mild chili seasoning
1-teaspoon ground cumin
1-teaspoon chili powder
2 cans light red kidney beans drained
1 can dark red kidney beans drained
1 4 oz can diced green chilis (don’t drain)
1-tablespoon sugar

Directions:
Combine ground beef and diced onion in a skillet and cook until meat is brown
Drain grease off of beef/onion mixture and put in large pot
Combine with remaining ingredients and mix together with large spoon.
Bring to a boil and reduce to a simmer for 45 minutes.
Stir occasionally to prevent burning on the bottom of the pot.
Serve hot topped with shredded Colby & Monterrey Jack cheese mixture, sour cream and crushed saltine crackers.

Submitter: 
Brent Clarkson
Description: 
Great recipe for a cold day. Love the slight kick that this chili provides. Delicious as is or when topped with shredded cheese, a dollop of sour cream and some crumbled saltine crackers.
