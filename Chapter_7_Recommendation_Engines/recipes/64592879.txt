Title:
Applescotch Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sliced, peeled apples
1/2 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup plus 1 tbsp. flour, divided
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup oats (or an oatmeal porridge blend)
1 136g COOK-&-SERVE butterscotch pudding mix (make sure not to use instant pudding mix)
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup butter or margarine

Directions:
Place apples in an ungreased 9"x9" baking dish or bundt pan.
Whisk brown sugar, 1 tbsp. flour, water and milk together; pour over apples.
Combine oats, pudding mix, cinnamon, salt and remaining flour; cut in butter till coarse crumbs; sprinkle over apples.
Bake @ 350 x 45-50 minutes or till topping is golden brown & fruit is tender.
Serve with ice cream, milk, cream or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Apples baked in brown sugar and topped with butterscotch/oat crumbs - served hot with ice cream = comfort food :)
Accidentally discovered that baking this in a bundt pan makes for an even crispier crisp!
