Title:
Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz pkg al fresco Sweet Apple Chicken Sausage
3 tsp Canola Oil
1 cup Leeks, chopped
1/2 cup Carrots, chopped
1/2 cup Celery, chopped
2 cups Mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup Red bell pepper
2 cloves Garlic, minced
2 tbsp Butter
1/4 cup Flour
1 cup Chicken broth
3/4 cup Low fat Milk
1/4 tsp Thyme
1 tbsp Dry Sherry
1/4 cup Frozen peas
1 tbsp Parsley
19" pie shell

Directions:
In a large pot, add one tsp of canola oil, leeks, celery, carrots and garlic and cook over medium-low heat for 15 minutes, stirring frequently and then transfer to a small bowl.
Heat another teaspoon of oil over medium heat and saute the mushrooms
Once the mushrooms begin to brown, add the red pepper
Saute the mixture for another 5 minutes and remove vegetables to the small bowl
Slice the chicken sausage into small pieces and saute in the pan over medium heat with the remaining oil until they are browned
Remove and place into the small bowl with the vegetables
Pre-heat the oven to 375 degrees
Heat the butter over medium-low heat and stir in flour for about 3 minutes
Heat the milk and chicken broth in a microwave for about a minute and a half on high
Remove from microwave and gradually add to the flour mixture whisking often, until the mixture comes to a boil, about 4 minutes
Add thyme
Pour the sauce mixture over the vegetables and chicken in the bowl
Add peas, sherry and parsley to the bowl and stir well.
Place mixture in the pie shell and bake for about 20 minutes, or until the top is bubbling and golden brown.
