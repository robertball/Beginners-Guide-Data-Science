Title:
April's Yummy Mimosa Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 12-ounce cans frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
2 quarts (8 cups) club soda, chilled
3 750-ml. bottles champagne, chilled
1 cup of triple sec
orange slices and maraschino cherries for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Empty the orange juice concentrate into a punch bowl.
Stir the club soda into the punch bowl.
Add the champagne and triple sec to the punch bowl and stir gently.
Float an ice mold in the punch bowl.
Serve immediately.
Garnish with cherries after serving in glasses if desired.

Submitter:

Description:
