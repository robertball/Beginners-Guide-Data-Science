Title:
Arroz con Leche (Rice with Milk) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked white rice
25 1/2 cups of water
1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt to taste
2 tablespoons canola oil
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla, to taste
1 to 2 tablespoons white or brown sugar to taste
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Place rice in a medium bowl with 4 cups of water. Water will be white and cloudy. Stir the rice with a wooden spoon. Carefully, drain water from rice. Repeat water 5 to 6 times until rice water is as clear as possible. Leave rice in water.
In a large pot combine 1 1/2 cups of water, salt, and oil. Bring to a boil.
Drain rice water from rice and pour soaked rice into boiling water. If water is still boiling after rice has been added reduce heat to low.
Allow rice to cook, stirring periodically, until all the water is gone.
Allow rice to cool slightly.
Combine milk, vanilla, sugar, and cinnamon and mix together.
Add milk mixture to warm rice and stir until rice has absorbed all of the milk.
Serve warm and garnish with cinnamon if desired.
