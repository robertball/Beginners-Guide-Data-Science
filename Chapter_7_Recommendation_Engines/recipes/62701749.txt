Title:
Russian Mushroom and Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tablespoons butter, divided
1 large red onion, chopped
2 large carrots, sliced
6 cups chicken broth
2 teaspoons dried dill weed
2 teaspoons salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 bay leaf
2 pounds potatoes, peeled and diced
10 oz fresh portabella mushrooms, sliced
1 cup fat free sour cream
1/4 fat free milk
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
fresh dill weed, for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Mix in onnion and carrots, and cook 5 minutes. Pour in broth. Season with dill, salt, pepper, and bay leaf. Mix in potatoes, cover, and cook 20 minutes, or until potatoes are tender but firm. Remove and discard the bay leaf.
Melt the remaining butter in a skillet over medium heat, and saute the mushrooms 5 minutes, until lightly browned. Stir into the soup.
In a small bowl, mix the sour cream, milk and flour until smooth. Stir into the soup to thicken. Garnish each bowl of soup with fresh dill to serve.
