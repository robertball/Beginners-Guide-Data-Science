Title:
Crunchy Kidney Friendly Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/945658.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups  Rice pillow type cereal
2 cups Crisp corn pillow cereal
3 cups bite-sized oyster crackers
1 cup unsalted pretzel twists
5 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1-1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons raw honey
1-1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
(optional - candy such as candy corn or covered chocolate candy can be added to the portion for those not on diet)
(optional - dried fruit can be added if not restricted also)

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 250°F.
2.Melt butter in a 10” x 15” pan. Stir in spices, Worcestershire sauce, honey and lemon juice.
3.Add cereals, crackers and pretzels. Toss to coat evenly.(make certain all honey is absorbed into cereals)
4.Bake for 45 minutes; gently mix every 15 minutes.
5.Spread on paper towels to cool.
6.Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Mooseinthekitchen
Description: 
A wonderful snack for all - but especially a welcome treet for those on a restrictive diet due to diabetes and kidney disease.
