Title:
Pork Chops with Cinnamon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lean pork chops
1 T butter
2 medium onions, sliced
1 c water
1 t chicken boullion
1/2 t cinnamon
1/2 t thyme

Directions:
Lightly brown chops in butter.
Add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer about 1 hour.
