Title:
homemade buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T. water
2 T. milk
1 T. oil
1 tsp. yeast
1 1/2 C. flour
1/2 T. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Heat water and milk till 105-115 degrees. Stir in yeast to dissolve.
In food processor with dough blade, mix flour, sugar and salt. With processor running, slowly add liquid and mix till dough forms a ball.
Place dough in greased bowl and let rise till double, about one hour.
Preheat oven to 375.  Place stone pizza pan in to warm.
Punch down dough and divide into 6 pieces. Roll out or shape into bun sizes.  Let rest, covered, for 10 minutes.
Place the 6 circles on the pizza pan.  Bake till golden.
