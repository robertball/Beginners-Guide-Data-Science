Title:
Cajun Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1299568.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken pieces
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 teaspoons onion powder
2 teaspoons dried thyme
2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 teaspoons sweet or smoky paprika
1/4 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine the garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, oregano, paprika, cayenne, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl.
Use one tablespoon of rub for every pound of meat.  Cook whole poultry or boneless skinless chicken breasts to an internal temperature of 165F.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
The Cajun rub for chicken is a quick and easy recipe for a weeknight dinner.  From The Mom 100 Cookbook by Katie Workman
