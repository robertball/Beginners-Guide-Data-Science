Title:
Quick Seafood Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 inch unbaked pie shell
6-8 oz canned crab, well drained and flaked or imitation crab
1/2 c shredded swiss or cheddar cheese
6 eggs
1 1/2 c light cream
1 tbsp minced onion
1 tbs dry white wine (optional)
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp dried tarragon, leaves, crushed

Directions:
Sprinkle crab meat and cheese in pie shell.
Beat together remaining ingredients.  pour over crabmeat and cheese.
Bake 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Seafood dish ready with ingredients you can keep on hand.  For quicker prep, use store bought ready-made pie crusts that already come in a pie pan.
