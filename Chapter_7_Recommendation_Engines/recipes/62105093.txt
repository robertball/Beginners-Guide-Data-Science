Title:
Simply Stated Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of tri colored rotini pasta
1 cup of cauliflower
1 cup of broccoli
1/2 lb of diced ham
1/2 lb of diced salami
1 cup of low cal colby and monterrey jack cheese (cubed)
1/4 cup chopped scallions
1 bottle of low cal italian dressing

Directions:
Boil the noodles for 8-10 minutes; drain
add all ingredients and 1/2 bottle of dressing.
refrigerate for approx 1 hour
before serving add remaining dressing

Submitter: 
Jen G
Description: 
This is a simple but fantastic recipe that is sure to impress.
