Title:
Dilled Shrimp (Dip-Spread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb cleaned baby shrimp
02 2 cans baby shrimp (drained)
1/4 - 1/3 cup Mayonnaise
11/2 tbs Dill seed
1 tbs dill weed
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 stalk celery (finely minced)
1/2 small onion (finely minced)

Directions:
Rinse and thoroughly dry baby shrimp.
Place shrimp in a mixing bowl with dry ingredients and finely minced onion and celery.
Mix in Mayonnaise to your desired consistency.
Chill or serve at room temperature with assorted cocktail breads or crackers.
You can adjust the dill, garlic and onion to your own liking.
I like mine "dilly" ...

Submitter: 
GRAYJETMAN
Description: 
If you love Shrimp and like Dill this recipe is for you ...
Dip or spread can be stretched by adding more Mayonnaise and celery.
Very good with cocktail Rye or Pumpernickel Breads.
