Title:
Glazed Cocktail Links Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991681.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) jar red currant jelly
1/2 cup Gulden's® Spicy Brown Mustard
2 (12 ounce) packages Hebrew National® Beef Polish Cocktail Links

Directions:
Stir together jelly and mustard in large saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat and cook 10 minutes or until sauce thickens, stirring several times.
Add links and stir to coat with sauce. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 10 minutes or until links are hot, stirring several times.

Submitter: 
Hebrew National®
Description: 
Polish cocktail links glazed with currant jelly and mustard.
