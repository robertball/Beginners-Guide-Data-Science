Title:
Emerald Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup green grapes, washed
1/3 cup canned crushed pineapple
1 small sliced, frozen banana
1/4 peeled and seeded, apple
Handful of spinach
8 ice cubes

Directions:
Directions: Add all of the ingredients to the blender and blend until nice and smooth.

Submitter: 
Holiday Baker
Description: 
This is a smoothie adapted from my Vitamix Blender book. It has a nice fruit juicy flavor. Enjoy!
