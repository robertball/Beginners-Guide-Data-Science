Title:
Easy Vegetarian Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4114699.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon almond butter, or to taste
2 slices whole-grain bread
1/2 Granny Smith apple, cored and thinly sliced
1/4 cup shredded carrots
1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Spread almond butter onto 1 side of each bread slice. Arrange apples neatly over almond butter on 1 slice of bread; top with carrots and Cheddar cheese. Place second piece of bread on top to make a sandwich.

Submitter: 
deedia
Description: 
It really a very simple sandwich that is very healthy. It's so yummy even the carnivores will enjoy it.
