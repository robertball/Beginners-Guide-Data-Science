Title:
Stuffed Celery Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986320.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1 tablespoon milk
2 teaspoons soy sauce
4 celery ribs, cut into serving-size pieces

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, peanut butter, milk and soy sauce until smooth. Transfer to a small resealable plastic bag. Cut a small hole in the corner of the bag; pipe mixture into celery pieces.

Submitter: 
Opal  Schmidt
Description: 
In Battle Creek, Iowa, Opal Schmidt mixes cream cheese and soy sauce into peanut butter to give her stuffed celery a rich, unique flavor. The filling is easy to blend together, and kids will have a ball squeezing it into the celery sticks before they start crunching away.
