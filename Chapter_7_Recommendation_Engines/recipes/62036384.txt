Title:
SAUSAGE & BEEF CON QUESO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Lbs. Medium Hot Sausage
2Lbs. Lean Ground Beef
4Lbs. Velveeta Cheese, cubed
2 {10oz. cans, each} Cream of Mushroom Soup
2 (16oz. jars, each} Med. Picante Sauce
2 {16oz. containers, each} Sour Cream
Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Brown sausage and beef in skillet, set on medium high heat, stirring until crumbly; drain well!
Place meat into crock-pot, set on low heat, add cheese, soup, picante sauce and sour cream. Simmer untill cheese is melted, stirring frequently.
Serve in crock-pot, warm and with Tortilla Chips!!

Submitter: 
Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
This a very hearty and savory dip!! great for a nice Tex-Mex party or a great sporting event party!!
