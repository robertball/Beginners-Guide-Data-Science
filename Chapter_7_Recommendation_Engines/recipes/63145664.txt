Title:
Pyrizhky- Filled Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. sugar
1/2 cup warm water
1 pkg.yeast
1 cup sour cream
3 eggs[beaten]
1/2 cup lard
1/2 tsp.salt
3 Tbsp.sugar
4 cups flour
Potato Filling:
2 Tbsp..chopped onion,sauted in
2 Tbsp.oil
2 cups mashed potatoes
salt and pepper to taste
Potato & cottage cheese filling:
2 Tbsp.chopped onion,sauted in
2 Tbsp.oil
1 cup mashed potatoes
1 cup cottage cheese
salt and pepper to taste
Sauerkraut Filling:
2 cups drained sauerkraut[chopped]
1 cup hamberger
3 Tbsp. onion chopped
lightly brown hamberger,add onion and saurkraut.Cook untill soft.
Dill cream sauce:
2 green onions,chopped
2 Tbsp.margarine
1 cup whipping cream
2 Tbsp.fresh dill,chopped
Saute onion in magarine untill just soft not brown.Add cream,bring to a gentle boil till thickened.Add dill,salt and pepper.

Directions:
Dissole sugar in warm water,add yeast,let stand 10 minutes.Combine sour cream and eggs.Mix together lard,salt,sugar and flour as if for a pie crust.Combine well with yeast,sour cream and eggs.Knead by hand to form a smooth ball.Cover closely with wax paper and refrigerate overnight.
Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thickness and cut into 1 1/2 inch circles.Place about 1/2 tsp.cooked filling in centre of circle,fold in half and pinch edges together,roll into a roll shape.Place on greased baking sheet with sealed side down.Let rise in a warm place.Bake in a 350 oven for 15-20 minutes till light brown.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
These can be filled with a sauerkraut and hamberger filling.The potato or potato and cottage cheese buns can be served with a dill cream sauce.
