Title:
Better Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tortillas, preferably homemade
1tablespoon cream cheese
1tablespoon butter
1/4 cup Goat cheese
1/2 cup mozzarella
1/2cup fresh spinach
1 portobello cap, diced small
1/4 cup artichoke hearts, diced

Directions:
Heat a non stick pan and add the portobello mushroom
Once the mushroom is soft, after about two minutes, add the fresh spinach, cream cheese artichoke hearts and butter
Spread goat cheese and separate shredded mozzarella into the four tortillas
Add vegetables and fold
Cook in the non stick for one minute per side or until the cheese is melted
Garnish with a fresh spinach leaf
