Title:
Easy Key Lime Pie I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg yolks, beaten
2 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup key lime juice
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Combine the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk and lime juice. Mix well. Pour into unbaked graham cracker shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes. Allow to cool. Top with whipped topping and garnish with lime slices if desired.

Submitter: 
Susan Best
Description: 
This is the 1999 American Pie Council 's National Pie Championship's first place winner in the Quick and Easy Category. Garnish each piece with a slice of lime and a dollop of whipped cream or whipped topping if you like.Modified from: Easy Key Lime Pie I
