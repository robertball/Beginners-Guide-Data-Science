Title:
Coconut Amino "Miso" Glazed Black Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb black cod, cut into 4 pieces
1/4 c avocado oil
1/4 c aminos
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp fish sauce
4-5 garlic cloves

Directions:
combine marinade ingredients; marinate 2 hours
Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
Heat an oven-proof skillet over medium high heat on the stovetop.
Wipe off any excess marinade
Film the pan with ghee or avocado or coconut oil
Place the fish skin-side-up on the pan and cook until the bottom of the fish browns and blackens in spots, about 3 minutes.
Flip and continue cooking until the other side is browned, 2 to 3 minutes. Transfer to the oven and bake for 5 to 10 minutes

Description:
