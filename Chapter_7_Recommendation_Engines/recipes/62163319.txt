Title:
Ground Beef Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 can tomatoes (15 oz)
1/2 cup yellow onion, chopped
1/4 cup green bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup carrots, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 can mushrooms (8 oz)
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 lb cheddar cheese
8 oz any small shaped pasta, cooked al dente (I use mini farfalle)
1 can tomato sauce (8 oz)
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Brown ground beef with salt & pepper; Add onion, pepper, carrots & garlic and simmer for about 15 minutes.
2. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, mushrooms, brown sugar & Worcestershire sauce. Season to taste with additional salt & pepper if needed.
3. Cook on low for approximately 45 minutes
4. Add cooked pasta to large casserole dish.
5. Spoon mushroom soup over pasta and then top with ground beef/sauce mixture.
6. Top with cheese.
7. Bake at 350 for approx 45 minutes.
