Title:
Amazingly Delicious Slow Cooker Pot Roast With Cognac Infused Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 pound beef roast such as chuck or shoulder
2 TBS EVOO
1 TBS course steak seasoning like Montreal
1 onion course chopped
3 cloves garlic minced
1/4 cup cognac
4 cups beef broth
1 bay leaf
1 TBS Worcestershire sauce
5 small red potatoes halved
3 celery stalk quartered
5 large carrots quartered

Directions:
Rub the roast with the EVOO.  Sprinkle the steak seasoning all over the roast pushing into roast to insure it sticks to the meat.
If your slow cooker has a sear options then sear the roast in the slow cooker on all sides until nicely seared.  About 4 minutes per side.  If your slow cooker does not have that option then heat a heavy skillet on med-high heat and sear on all sides.
Remove meat and set aside.  If using a skillet remove skillet from heat and deglaze slow cooker or skillet with the cognac scraping up the bits and pieces.  Let cook down for about 1-2 minutes.
Add the chopped onion and cook transparent or about 5 minutes.
Add chopped garlic and cook for 1 minute
Add the meat back to slow cooker or if using a skillet place all contents into the slow cooker
Add the beef broth, bay leaf and Worcestershire sauce.
Cook on low for 4 hours.
Add the potatoes, celery and carrots and continue to cook for another 3 hours on low

Submitter:

Description: 
This pot roast has tons of flavors and is so easy to make.  It's a cross between pot roast and stew.  Serve with warm buttered french bread for dipping in the gravy.
