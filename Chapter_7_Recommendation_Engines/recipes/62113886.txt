Title:
Coconut Rice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups long grain rice
1 can *creme of coconut
1/4 cup white sugar
1 mango
3 kiwis
15 strawberries

Directions:
Cook 3 cups of long grain rice in rice cooker
Blend 1 and 1/2 cups of creme of coconut with 1/4 cup of white sugar
As soon as the timer pops on the rice cooker, stir the creme of coconut and sugar mixture through the rice
Dump the rice into a rectangular glass baking dish and press firmly to pack with the back of a spoon while the rice is still hot
Place the dish in the refridgerator to chill
Slice the mango, kiwis and strawberries
Starting on the left at the top of the dish, lace one column of mango onto of the rice, top to bottom
Next place a column of kiwi, then a column of strawberries.
Continue the columns alternating the fruit untill the "cake" is fully topped.
Slice the cake to serve and use the remaining 1/2 cup of creme of coconut to spoon a small amount on top of each slice of cake.
* note: creme of coconut IS NOT the same as coconut milk. Creme of coconut can be found in the grocery store near the liquor mixes or fruit juiuces.

Submitter: 
MommyDiva
Description: 
This makes a great final dish to any Thai themed dinner or just for a special family treat.  Easy coconut rice is molded and topped with sliced fruit and served chilled.
