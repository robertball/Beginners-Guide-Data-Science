Title:
Tortilla Sausage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# pork sausage, browned slowly
12 oz. cottage cheese
3. T. chopped green onions
3T. chopped black olives
2 T. flour
1 jar spagetti sauce with peppers
3 oz. mozzarella, grated
flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine cottage cheese, onions, olives, flour sausage, about 6 oz. of sauce (enough to moisten).
Heat oven to 375.
Put 1/3 c. mixture onto tortillas.  Roll up, and place seam side down in 9x13 pan.  Spoon remaining sauce over.  Bake 15 minutes, then sprinkle with cheese, and bake till melted.
