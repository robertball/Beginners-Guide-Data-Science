Title:
Big Ray's Cracked Out Hot Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3537903.jpg
Ingredients:
2-1/2 cups cooked shreded chicken
1 Tbsp powdered ranch mix
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp granulated onion
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 cup mayonnaise
4 stips cooked bacon, crumbled
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup crushed potato chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly spray a 7x11-inch pan with cooking spray.
Combine chicken, ranch mix, lemon juice, Worcestershire, salt, granulated onion, celery, mayonnaise, bacon and cheddar cheese. Spread into prepared pan. Sprinkle with crushed potato chips.
Bake for 25 minutes.

Description: 
Chicken Salad is one of my favorite quick lunches. This Cracked Out Hot Chicken Salad is a fantastic change to my usual chicken salad sandwiches. It was so delicious baked and topped with potato chips. You can make the chicken salad mixture ahead of time and refrigerate it until you are ready to bake the dish. This is perfect for brunch, lunch or dinner! It is guaranteed to your new favorite chicken salad recipe.
