Title:
Awesome Sausage Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz package seasoned bulk pork sausage
3 tbps olive oil
1/2 c chopped onion
1/2 c chopped celery
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp dried rosemary leaves
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 cups herb seasoned stuffing mix
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/2 c hot water
1 1/2 tsp instant chicken bouillon granules

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Place sausage in large skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until no longer pink.  Drain.  Transfer to medium mixing bowl.
Heat oil in large skillet.  Add onion, celery and garlic.  Saute over moderate heat until celery is tender crisp.  Add mushrooms, worcestershire sauce, rosemary and pepper.  Cook, stirring constantly, 1 minute.  Add to sausage.  Mix well. Stir in stuffing mix and egg.  Mix hot water and bouillion granules in small bowl.  Stir into stuffing mixture.  Place into 1 and 1/2 qt casserole.  Bake at 325, 30 - 35 minutes or until heated through.
