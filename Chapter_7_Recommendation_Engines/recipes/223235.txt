Title:
Sweet and Crunchy Pea Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained
1/2 cup sweet pickles, halved and sliced
1/2 cup roasted Spanish peanuts
1/4 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle WhipÂ®), or to taste

Directions:
