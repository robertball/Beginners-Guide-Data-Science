Title:
Bacon and Cheese Fat Bomb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bacon (32 g) - 4 slices (see instructions: how to cook bacon)
Cream Cheese (3 oz) - a little less than ½ cup - at room temperature
Butter (2 oz) - ¼ cup - at room temperature
Cheese (1 oz) - 2 tablespoons
Garlic - 1 clove
3 serrano peppers (10 g) - seeded/finely chopped
Lime - ½ lime - zest
Cilantro - 4 sprigs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook bacon in oven @ 350 degrees for 15-25 minutes.
Mix together cheese, butter, cream.
Add garlic, lime zest and a squeeze of lime juice
Add serrano peppers.  Fold into mixture.  Place in refrigerator for 10 minutes.
Divide mixture into 6 equal parts.  Roll into balls.  Make sure the mixture is cold, so that it keeps its shape easier.
Chop bacon and cilantro.  Place in bowl.
Place one fat bomb at a time in the bowl to coat it in the bacon and cilantro.
Serve cold!
