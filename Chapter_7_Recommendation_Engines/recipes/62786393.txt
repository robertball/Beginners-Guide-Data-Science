Title:
Fresh Fruit-Pudding Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 Cup Cold Milk
1 Tbsp. Orange Juice
1 Pkg. (4 serving size) Vanilla Instant Pudding
1 8oz. tub Thawed Cool Whip
6 Cups Fresh Fruit

Directions:
Pour milk and juice into bowl; add pudding mix; beat slowly with hand beater or lowest speed electric mixer for 1 minute. Add 1 Cup Cool Whip; beat 1 minute more; just until blended. Arrange 2 Cups of fruit in a Large serving bowl. Add pudding mixture; top with remaining 3 cups of fruit. Garnish with Cool Whip and fruit pieces; if desired.
(Fresh Fruits: Blueberries, Bananas, Strawberries, Raspberries, Melon Balls, Peaches, Grapes) I am sure you could use canned too IF you drain them or use frozen too. Soooo good.
