Title:
Barbecue Cheese Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (6 ozs each)
4 slices fo cheese
1 cup barbecue sauce

Directions:
Carefully cut a pocket in each chicken breast half.  Fill with cheese; secure with meal or soaked wooden skewers. Grill.
