Title:
Grandma's Porcupine Meatballs in the Instant Pot® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7079361.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup uncooked white rice
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 egg
1 teaspoon diced garlic
salt and ground black pepper to taste
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Mix beef, rice, onion, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl until combined. Divide mixture into 8 equal portions and form into meatballs.
Place meatballs in a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot(R)). Mix tomato soup and water and pour over meatballs.
Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 10 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, 10 to 40 minutes.
