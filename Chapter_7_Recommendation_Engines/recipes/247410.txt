Title:
Delightful Peanut Butter Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/8273403.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup mascarpone cheese
3 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 pinch salt
5 tablespoons miniature semisweet chocolate chips
1 (7.2 ounce) package Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®

Directions:
Cream together peanut butter, mascarpone cheese, honey, vanilla and salt until well blended. Stir in chocolate chips.
Serve with Snack Factory(R) Pretzel Crisps(R) for dipping.

Submitter: 
stormybeach
Description: 
Peanut butter, mascarpone cheese, honey, vanilla and chocolate chips combine for a delicious fun dip for game night or the kids' after school snack. The mascarpone gives this a nice creamy texture.
