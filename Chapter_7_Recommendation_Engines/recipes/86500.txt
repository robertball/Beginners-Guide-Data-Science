Title:
Tailgating BBQ Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 medium red potatoes, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoons McCormick® Grill Mates® Barbecue Seasoning
1 large onion, cut into thin wedges

Directions:
Toss potatoes with oil in a large bowl. Add seasoning; toss to coat evenly.
Place potatoes on large, wide sheet of heavy-duty aluminum foil. Place onions on top of potatoes and fold foil end over end until tightly sealed.
Grill over medium-high heat 30-35 minutes; turning halfway through cooking time.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Whether you're tailgating at the stadium lot before the game or camping in the great outdoors, these no-fuss, sweet and smoky potatoes are guaranteed to satisfy.
