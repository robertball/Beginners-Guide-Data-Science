Title:
Bean and Bacon Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. dry mixed beans
1/2 cup dry brown lentils
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 carrot, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 green bell pepper, chopped
8-10 oz chopped tomatoes in juice
4-6 slices turkey bacon, chopped
10-11 cups water
1 tsp chili powder
2 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Saute carrot, onion, celery, and bell pepper in olive oil until softened.  Add garlic and turkey bacon and continue to saute until caramelized.  Pour in water, seasonings, tomatoes and beans, bring to a boil, simmer several hours or until beans are fully cooked and soft.
