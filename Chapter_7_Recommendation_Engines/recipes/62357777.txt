Title:
Angie's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup soy sauce
3/4 cup lemon juice
2 tsp garlic powder
2 TBS oregano leaves
1/3 cup oil
2 TBS white vinegar
bit of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix ingredients together in jar:
Shake and brush over chicken. You can marinate if you like.
Cook for 30 minutes at 350, then brush sauce over chicken again.
Cook 30 more minutes. Save sauce.
Cook alphabets with chicken bullion cubes.
Pour sauce from chicken over pasta. Serve with a green salad.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Fun and a bit different. This chicken has a bit of a tang thanks to the vinegar and lemon juice. Great flavor.
