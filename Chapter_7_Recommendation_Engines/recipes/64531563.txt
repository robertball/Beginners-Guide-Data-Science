Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Butter
1/2 cup Green Onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper, finely chopped
1/2 cup Red Bell Pepper, finely chopped
1 (15-oz.) can Whole Kernel Corn, drained
1 (15-oz.) can Cream Style Corn
1 pkg. Jiffy Cornbread MIx
1 Egg, beaten
3 Tbsp. Sugar
1 cu Cheddar Cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°.
In a large skillet, melt butter.  Add onions and peppers. Saute until soft.  Pour into a large bowl.
Add remaining ingredients in order listed.  MIx well.
Spray a 9 x 13 or 3-quart casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray.  Pour mixture into prepared dish.
Bake a 350° for 45 minutes, checking after 35 minutes to see if top is browning.  If so, cover loosely with foil.  Continue baking for the remaining 10 minutes.
Remove from oven and let stand 5-10 minutes, until the casserole had firmed up.
