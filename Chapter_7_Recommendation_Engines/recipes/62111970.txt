Title:
Easy Flavored Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp Instant coffee (regular or decaf)
1 Packet calorie free sweetener (splenda)
Hot water
3-4 Ice cubes
3/4 - 1 cup Milk
1 - 2 Tbsp Flavored Coffee Creamer

Directions:
In a tall 16 oz. glass, combine instant coffee and  sweetener.
Add a small amount of hot water to bottom of glass and stir until disolved.
Add ice cubes.
Stir in milk and coffee creamer of your choice. Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Pamela Frechette
Description: 
My mom has been making this for years. It's super easy and creamy! A great quick fix for coffee cravings! I like using french vanilla creamer! Adjust the sweetness and strength to your taste.
