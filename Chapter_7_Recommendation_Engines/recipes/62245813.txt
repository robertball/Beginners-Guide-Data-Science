Title:
Cherry Filled Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cherry pie filling
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vinilla
3 cups sifted flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup milk
2 cups sifted flour

Directions:
cream together the butter and the sugars. add eggs and vanilla. in separate bowl sift together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. add the dry ingrediants to the creamed mixture. then add the milk and the additional flour. blending it all together. this gets very thick. be sure to use all the flour. form into two rolls and chill over night. roll the chilled dough out and use a cookie cutter to make uniform cookies. add about 3 cherries to each cookie and then cover with another cookie, sealing the edges of the cookie with the cherry filling in the middle of the two. bake at 350 until cookie is done.
