Title:
Monster Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups peanut butter
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. corn syrup
4 1/2 cups rolled oats
2 tsp. baking soda
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 to 2/3 cup M&Ms or Smarties
No flour in this recipe!

Directions:
Cream PB, butter and sugars together.
Beat in eggs.
Mix in vanilla and corn syrup.
Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Note: there is no flour in this recipe!
Drop by tbsp. onto baking sheet. Flatten a bit with hand or fork.
Bake @ 350 x 7-8 min. (over-baking makes them hard).
About 5 dozen.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Classic cookies featuring Smarties or M&Ms, PB and oats. No flour in this recipe.
