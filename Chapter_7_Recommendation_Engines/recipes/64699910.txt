Title:
Hula's Buttermilk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Eggs
1/2 cup Butter softened
1 1/2 cups white sugar
2 Tblspn Flour
1 Tblspn Corn starch
1 cup Buttermilk
1 tsp Vanilla
1 Tblspn Lemon Juice
1/8 tsp Nutmeg
9" Pie Crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Beat Eggs till frothy.
Add butter, sugar, and flours.  Beat until smooth.
Stir in other ingredients and pour into pie shell.
Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, until center is firm.

Submitter: 
Virginia Keeley
Description: 
Can be frozen after baking
