Title:
Celery Blue Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 49.5 oz cans	Chicken Broth
2 - 49.5 oz cans water
24 oz Crumbled Blue Cheese
3 large shallots minced(approx a 1/2 cup)
3 celery hearts	diced (approx 50oz)

Directions:
In a large stock pot combine the chicken broth, water, shallots, and blue cheese.
Simmer on med to med high heat for an hour
Add celery and simmer for another hour.

Submitter: 
CCANDYLLAND
Description: 
This is one of my favorite comfort soups.  If you prefer a thicker soup, make a roux with flour and butter.
