Title:
Jameson Cured Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds fillet of organic salmon, skin on.
4 oz Irish whiskey
1/4 pound sugar
1/4 pound salt
3 tbs corsely ground black pepper.
Large bunch of dill, finely chopped

Directions:
line a lipped pan with plastic wrap. Combine salt, sugar, dill, and black pepper. Dust bottom of lined pan with 1/3 of mixture. Place salmon skin-side down in pan. Coat flesh of salmon with remaining mixture. Add irish whiskey and wrap tightly. Place in refrigerator for 24 hours. After 24 hours, turn salmon over and leave in refrigerator for at least another 24 hours. Remove from wrapping and rinse off the coating. Freeze for about an hour and slice thin. serve in whatever manner one would serve smoked salmon.
