Title:
Baked Potatoe and Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all purpose flour
2 cans of chicken brith, divided
3 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
2 cup broccoli florets, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1 1/4 cup milk
1 8oz sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
7 tsp fully cooked bacon, crumbled
7 tsp chopped green onions
4 tsp sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
1. Whisk flour and 1/3 cup chicken broth in a bowl and set aside.
2.In a dutch oven add remaining broth and add potatoes, green onions, and broccoli. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat and let simmer til potatoes are tender.
3.Gradually stir in flour mixture and cook stirring often for 5 minutes til it thickens.
4. Stir in milk and 8oz of cheese and let cheese melt.
5. When serving, fry bacon and drain and crumble on top along with remaining cheese.

Submitter: 
Cheryl's Cooking Now!
Description: 
WOW! I love this soup! I've tried other kinds of potatoe soups and they were okay but was bland and needed more flavor. The veggies, cheese, and bacon really makes this soup.
