Title:
Italian Onion Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970291.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, cubed
1 red onion, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 stalk celery, diced
1 tablespoon sea salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 (8 ounce) bottle zesty Italian-style salad dressing

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, red onion, bell pepper, and celery in a large bowl; season with sea salt and black pepper. Pour dressing over cucumber mixture.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
TinaK
Description: 
Everyone has these ingredients on hand when wanting a nice refreshing salad that is tasty, quick, and easy. Cubed roma tomatoes can also be added to this recipe; it's good either way. Serve as a side dish, afternoon snack, or midnight munchies.
