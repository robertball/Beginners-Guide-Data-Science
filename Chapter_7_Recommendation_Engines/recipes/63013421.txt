Title:
Pickled Quail Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
48 canned quail eggs
1 1/2 cups distilled white vinegar
1 1/2 cups water
1 cup beet water (optional)
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp peppercorns
1/2 tbsp allspice (or)
1 tbsp whole allspice berries
1 small onion, sliced thin
3 slices fresh ginger root
6 cloves garlic
2 bay leaves
4 whole cloves

Directions:
In saucepan, combine vinegar, water, sugar, and spices.
Simmer 10 minutes.
Pour vinegar mixture over eggs and seal containers.
Chill in refrigerator at least 2 days before serving.
If adding beet juice, do not add more than 24 hours before serving.
