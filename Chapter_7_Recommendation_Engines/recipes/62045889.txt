Title:
Chicken Simon and Garfunkel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
egg
bread crumbs
mozzarella cheese
1 stick butter
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp sage
1 tsp rosemary
1 tablespoon parsley
white wine (optional)

Directions:
Dip chicken in egg then bread crumbs
Slice mozz chesse and place in the middle of the chicken
Roll the chicken and place seams down in a baking dish or a toothpick through
In a saucepan place butter, thyme, sage, rosemary and parsley Stir for about 1 minutes and pour over the chicken
Place chicken in oven 350 for about 45 minutes
Optional:(After 30 pour some white wine over)

Submitter: 
Toni Losada
Description: 
This is the BEST breaded chicken with a superb sauce that takes minutes to make!!
