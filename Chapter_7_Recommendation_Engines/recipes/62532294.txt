Title:
Apple Cinnamon Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/518440.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 Tbsp cinnamon
2 eggs
1 jar of applesauce
1/2 c. oil
1/2 c. milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix together all dry ingredients, then add the wet ingredients and mix thoroughly. Do not grease muffin tray. Pour 1/4 c. of the mixture into each muffin cup. Bake for 30 minutes. Allow muffins to cool before removing them from the muffin cups.

Submitter: 
Tina Cochrane de Monroy
Description: 
I was trying to make banana muffins and then realized I had no bananas! So I went a different direction and it came out fantastic - these are the best muffins I've ever tasted and are now my weekly favourite to make for my family.
