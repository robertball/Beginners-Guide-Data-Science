Title:
Creamy and Comforting Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
12 oz boneless chicken breast, small cubed
2 onions, diced
3 tablespoons parsley, chopped
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 bay leaf
1 12 oz package frozen egg noodles (such as Reemes)
2 cups chopped carrot
2 cups chopped celery
1 cup frozen peas
1 1/2 cups milk & additional 1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons flour
salt and pepper

Directions:
Add the chicken broth to a large soup pot and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.  Add the chicken, onions, parsley, thyme, pepper and bay leaf and reduce the heat to a simmer 5 minutes.  Add the noodles, carrot, and celery and simmer an additional 25 minutes.  Add 1 1/2 cup milk and peas to pot.  In a separate cup mix 1/2 cup milk and flour.  Drizzle into chicken noodle mixture and stir until thick, about five additional minutes.  Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This is comfort food at it's best and easiest.  Tastes just like Grandma used to make with a alot less time and preparation.
