Title:
Homestyle Chili A+ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz Can diced tomatoes
2 lbs hamburger
1 lb flank steak( sliced into 1/2" cubes)
1/2 onion(diced)
1 Green pepper
1 Red Pepper
1 Stick Margarine or Butter
2 Tbsp Flour
2 Jars Tomato Sauce
2 Packets Mc Cormick Chili Seasoning
2 Potatoes (Cubed)
1 Tsp Sugar or Brown Sugar
2 Cans of Kidney Beans
1 Tsp Instant Coffee
Optional:
2 Jalapeno Peppers (Diced)
1/4 Cup Franks Red Hot
1/2 Tsp Chili Powder

Directions:
Dice up the onion, peppers and simmer in a frying pan with the butter for about 10 minutes.
Cut up the flank steak and roll in flower. Add the flank steak to the onions and peppers, cook for additional 10 minutes. To Spice it up a little more add some chili powder to the flour.
Next add the hamburger and cook all of the ingredients until the hamburger is fully cooked.
Next put the meat and peppers into a slow cooker along with the remaining ingredients, stir thoroughly and cook on low for at least 6 hours.
If you would rather make this in a traditional pot, cook the potatoes in the frying pan also and increase frying time by ten minutes and cook in the pot for about an hour.
