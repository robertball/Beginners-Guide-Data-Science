Title:
Family French Canadian Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white beans or 1 lb Great Northern Beans (if using dried beans soak overnight the evening before)
Vegetables stock as needed
1/4 lb Salt Pork
1 large white onion chopped roughly
Salt and Pepper
1 large Bay Leaf

Directions:
Heat Oven at 350
Drain beans but reserve  liquid. If using canned rinse and save liquid
Into bean pot place beans with Salt Pork, chopped onion, salt, pepper and Bay Leaf. Cover with reserved liquid or stock  to 1/4" above beans.
Cover with lid BUT leave slight opening on one side of Bean Pot.
Bake for 3 1/2  hours  checking occasionally and adding more reserved liquid as needed.
Serve with crusty bread and main dish of choice.Refrigerate left overs  and they are great next day in sandwiches with all you like

Submitter: 
caquilter
Description: 
Old family recipe, easy and delicious, just takes time - but anything great does! Our father was famous for this and all my siblings always talk about it.
