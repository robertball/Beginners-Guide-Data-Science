Title:
Jan's Creamsicle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/426490.jpg
Ingredients:
1 18 ounce orange cake mix
2 large eggs
1/3 cup canola oil
2 teaspoons orange extract
1 cup vanilla chips
powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
put cake mix, eggs, oil and extract in large bowl or mixer.  Combine well.
Add vanilla chips and mix until evenly distributed.
Cover dough and chill for 30 minutes.
Roll 1 tablespoon of dough into a ball and place on parchment lined cookie sheet.
Bake 10 - 12 minutes or until "set" but not browned.  Do not over bake!
Remove to a wire rack to cool.
Dust with powdered sugar lightly.
Store in an airtight container.

Description: 
An orange and vanilla cookie that's a throwback to childhood summer days - without the sticky fingers! Quick and easy to make!
