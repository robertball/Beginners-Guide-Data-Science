Title:
Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983950.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) jar roasted red peppers
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon minced garlic or roasted garlic
1/2 cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, cubed
Fresh parsley sprigs
Keebler® Town House® Original Crackers

Directions:
Drain peppers. Pat dry with paper towels. Set aside.
In food processor combine parsley and garlic. Cover and process until combined. Add peppers and Parmesan cheese. Process until smooth. Gradually add cream cheese through feed tube, processing until combined.
Spoon into serving bowl. Garnish with parsley, if desired. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Town House®
Description: 
A creamy blend of roasted red peppers, parsley, garlic, and Parmesan and cream cheese is garnished with chopped parsley and served with crackers.
