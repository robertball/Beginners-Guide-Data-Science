Title:
Casa Ole Green Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4	avocados
16	ounces sour cream
1	can hot rotel tomatoes
4	ounces green chile peppers
3	ounces cream cheese
1	teaspoon lemon juice
2	teaspoons salt
1	tablespoon garlic powder

Directions:
Add all ingredients to the bowl of your food processor and process until smooth.
