Title:
Jennah's Famous Peanut Butter Blossoms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084765.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Bisquick
3/4 Cup JIF Regular Peanut Butter
1 Can Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 Tsp Vanilla
Sugar
Hershey Kisses

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 Degrees
Mix together Bisquick, Peanut Butter, Sweetened Condensed Milk, and Vanilla.
Roll into 1" Balls
Pour sugar on a plate
Roll balls into sugar covering completely
Place on ungreased cookie sheet
Bake for 8 Minutes
While in oven unwrap Hershey Kisses
Remove from oven and immediately add kisses.
When done adding all kisses remove from cookie sheet onto cooling rack.
Enjoy :)

Submitter: 
JenRioux
Description: 
This is a recipe my mom and I have made every Christmas for almost 15 years. It is always a hit and has people begging for more and for the recipe!
