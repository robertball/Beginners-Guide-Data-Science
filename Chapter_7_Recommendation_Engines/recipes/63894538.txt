Title:
Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
2 cups sour cream
4 tablespoons butter, softened
4 tablespoons dried minced onion flakes
ground black pepper to taste
1.5  (2 pound) package frozen small cubed hash brown potatoes, thawed
2 cups  extra sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
3/4 package of Ranch Dressing
6 to 8 strips cooked bacon chopped
2 cup crushed cornflakes cereal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a large bowl mix together soup, sour cream, onion flakes, pepper, cheese, dry dressing, bacon. Mix together.  Add potatoes and mix until combined. Place into 9X13 pan and add crushed corn flakes, drizzle melted butter over top of flakes and place in oven.
Bake 45minutes covered, uncover and bake for another 35 to 40 minutes.
