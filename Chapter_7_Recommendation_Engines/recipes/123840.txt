Title:
Mixed Greens with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810084.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups mixed salad greens
1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
DRESSING:
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon thinly sliced green onion
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon sugar
1/8 teaspoon dried tarragon
Dash pepper

Directions:
Toss greens, tomatoes and mushrooms in a large bowl. In a small bowl, whisk together dressing ingredients; pour over salad and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sue  Walker
Description: 
This is a great year-round salad because you can use whatever greens are in season. The tarragon in the dressing is subtle but really adds to the flavor.
