Title:
Never Fail Caramel Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c brown sugar
1 can evaporated milk
1 beaten egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 c sugar
3/4 c butter
1/2 tsp salt
1 c chopped nuts

Directions:
Add everything but vanilla & nuts to a saucepan & boil for 4 min stirring constantly. Add vanilla & nuts & spread!

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a yummy icing you can use on everything from cakes to cinnamon rolls.
