Title:
Hobo Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can of Pork & Beans
1 regular can of kidney beans
1 regular can of lima beans
1 cp. Brown Sugar
2 onions
1 tsp. mustard
1/2 lb. of bacon
1 lb of hamburger
1 c. of Catsup

Directions:
Cook meat.  Put in crock pot with rest of ingredients.  Stir.  Cook several hours until hot
