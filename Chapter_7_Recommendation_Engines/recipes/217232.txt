Title:
John Collins Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
ice
2 fluid ounces bourbon whiskey
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/4 cup club soda, or as needed
1 slice lemon

Directions:
Fill a chilled cocktail glass with ice; add whiskey and lemon juice. Stir in sugar until dissolved. Top off with club soda. Garnish with lemon slice.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
Of the Collins siblings, John's gin-toting brother, Tom, is more famous. With whiskey, John is somewhat similar to a whiskey sour with club soda.
