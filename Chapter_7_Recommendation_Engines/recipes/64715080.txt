Title:
Cheesy Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1 HEAPING teaspoon of minced garlic
1 onion, chopped
16 oz Whole mushrooms, de-stemmed and chopped
2 12oz packages of frozen Chopped Spinach, steamed according to directions on bag
12 oz package of frozen Artichoke Hearts, steamed according to directions on bag and chopped
1 cup Mayo
2 8oz packages of cream cheese
6 oz package Shredded Parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese
Plenty of black Pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
In large skillet, melt the stick of butter over medium heat.
Add garlic, onion, and mushrooms and saute until soft.
Add the spinach and artichoke hearts.
Add the Mayo.
Add the cream cheese, stirring occasionally until melted.
Stir in Parmesan cheese.
Add plenty of fresh cracked black pepper and salt to taste.
Place in a large baking dish and top with Mozzarella cheese.
Cover with foil and bake at 375 till it's all nice a bubbly, or put it in a crock-pot on high until bubbly, then switch to warm to keep warm while serving.
