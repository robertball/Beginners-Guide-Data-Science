Title:
Quick n Easy, Smooth n Creamy Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998407.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup Brown Sugar (packed)
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
1/4 Teaspoon Ginger
1/4 Teaspoon Cloves
1/4 Teaspoon Nutmeg
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
1 1/4 Cup Milk
2 Eggs
1 15oz. Can Pumpkin Pie Filling (or 2 Cups cooked or canned Sweet Potatoes or Winter Squash)
9" pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
In small bowl, blend sugar, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, nutmeg, salt.
In a blender, add milk, eggs, and sugar mixture. Blend on low speed until well mixed.  While blending, spoon pumpkin into the blender. Increase blender speed as necessary to completely blend ingredients.
Pour pumpkin into pie shell.
Bake at 425 for 45 minutes or until center is set but not dry.
Serve cooled.
