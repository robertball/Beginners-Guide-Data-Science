Title:
Vegetable Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil (Gremolata infused)
20 ounces ground turkey, 92% lean
1 large yellow onion, chopped
2 teaspoons minced garlic
5 cups chopped cabbage
1 (16 ounce) can pinto beans, drained & rinsed
1 red bell pepper, cored and chopped
1 green bell pepper, cored and chopped
3 celery ribs, sliced
3 carrots, sliced
2 (16 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
3 teaspoons Penzey's beef soup base
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
1 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 Tablespoon dried parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in a large stockpot over medium high heat. Add ground turkey and onion, and cook until turkey is well browned and crumbled.
Add everything else, except parsley. Stir, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat, add parsley and stir.
