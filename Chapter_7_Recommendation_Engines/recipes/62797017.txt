Title:
Not My Mom's Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. elbow macaroni
2 Tbl. unsalted butter
2 Tbl. all purpose flour
1 1/2 c. milk
1/2 lb. american cheese

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to directions.
Melt butter in saucepan over med high heat, whisk in flour.
After it is all combined and bubbling, slowly add milk, stirring constantly.
After milk mixture has come to a boil for 1 minute, add cheese and continue stirring until all melted.
Add macaroni to cheese mixture and serve.

Submitter: 
Kate Prescott Steinke
Description: 
Never could master my mom's recipe, despite years of trying.  So, I went ahead and modified one for my family.  Simple cheese sauce requires no baking, and makes a great nacho sauce too!
