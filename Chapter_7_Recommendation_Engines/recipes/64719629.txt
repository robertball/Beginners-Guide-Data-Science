Title:
Spinach Mushroom Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR CREPES blend together:
2 C Flour
1 t Salt
2 Eggs
2 1/2 C Milk
1 C Fresh Spinach
Crepe Filling:
Sautee
2 T Butter
3 Cloves Garlic, minced
Add:
3 T Flour
1 Can Fri-Chick
4 C Sliced Mushrooms
1/2 Half and Half
Topping:
Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Place everything except the spinach for the crepes in the blender after blended mix in spinach.
Fill crepes with 2-3 tablespoons filling and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.  Roll up.
Heat covered for 15-20 minutes at 350 degrees F.
