Title:
Mediterranean Chicken & Orzo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRESSING:
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1/4 c red wine vinegar
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
3/4 c olive oil
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
SALAD:
12 ounces orzo
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1 cup kalamata olives, coarsley chopped
1/4 lb feta cheese
1.5 lb boneless, skinless chicken, chopped

Directions:
Whisk together all dressing ingredients except olive oil and italian seasoning.  Add olive oil in a thin stream, whisking constantly to create an imulsion.  Whisk in italian seasoning.  Cover and refrigerate.
Cook orzo according to package directions.
Combine orzo with other salad ingredients, add 3/4 cup dressing and mix well.  Drizzle remaining dressing over top of salad and serve.

Description: 
Can't remember where I got this recipe, but we absolutely love it for a light summer lunch or dinner.  Can be served in parboiled red bell pepper halves for an elegant presentation.
