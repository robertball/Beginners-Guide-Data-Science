Title:
Lemon peppermint green tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of dried peppermint
3/4 cup of dried green tea
3/4 cup of dried lemongrass
12 cups of water

Directions:
Put your 1/2 cup of dried peppermint in the coffee filter, add 4 cups of water, and turn your coffee maker on. Pour in a large pitcher. Throw away filter and peppermint.
Repeat process for green tea and lemongrass
Add honey to taste. I prefer this drink cold.

Submitter:

Description: 
This tea is made in any coffee maker. It is better for you than coffee and it tastes great.
