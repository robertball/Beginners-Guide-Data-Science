Title:
Wendy's Chocolate frosty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small container whipped cream
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 gallon chocolate milk

Directions:
combine all ingredients in ice cream maker or in bowl.
If bowl: Place in freezer and stir every 15 minutes till thick.
