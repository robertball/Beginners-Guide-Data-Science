Title:
Orange Date Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. dates, chopped
1/2 cup boiling water
1 teaspoon butter
1 egg
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup orange juice concentrate
1 1/2 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 1/2 oz. nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350
Pour boiling water over dates and butter, allow to cool slightly
Mix other ingredients into dates
Bake in loaf pan for 45-50 minutes
