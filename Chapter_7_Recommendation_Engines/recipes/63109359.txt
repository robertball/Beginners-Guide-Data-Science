Title:
Tasty Black Beans I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup finely chopped red onion
1/3 cup finely chopped bell pepper
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
5 cloves garlic, minced
1-1/2 tsp mild chili powder
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp ground cumin
2 - 3 cups chicken broth
2/15 oz cans of whole black beans, drained
Juice from 2 limes.
Salt & pepper optional
4 cups of cooked rice

Directions:
Combine onion and bell pepper in a large skillet and saute over medium heat in vegetable oil until completely softened, about 6 - 7 minutes.
Add garlic and next 3 ingredients, stirring constantly for 2 minutes.
Stir in chicken broth and black beans.
Simmer briskly, stirring often until sauce is thickened, about 10 - 15 minutes.
Add lime juice, and add salt & pepper if desired.
Serve over rice.  Garnish with chopped cilantro and cut limes.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
These dressed up black beans are a great side dish or can make a meal when served over rice.
