Title:
Roast Beef Vegetable Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364011.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
6 oz mushrooms, baby bella, chopped
6 oz roast beef, shredded, chopped
½ t sea salt
½ can artichokes, chopped
1 red pepper, roasted
6 slices bread, 16 grain
6 T greek yogurt

Directions:
heat oil over medium and add mushrooms. heat till soft.
add beef and heat until warm.
add salt and artichokes. toss and heat until warm. divide into three servings.
put mixture in a covered dish in a 170 degree oven to keep warm.
remove seeds from your peppers and cut into three or four sections. Heat over open gas flame, till bubbly and browned. Slice thinly. divide into three servings.
toast bread, let cool slightly, cover each with 1 tablespoon of yogurt.
place 1/3 of the peppers and beef mixture on each of three of the slices of toast. cover with remaining toast.
cut into 6 halves.
serve warm

Submitter: 
Rashel Fitchett
Description: 
A warm, shredded Roast Beef sandwich loaded with mushrooms, artichokes, and mushrooms on hearty whole wheat toast. Goes well with Black Bean Vegetable Soup.
