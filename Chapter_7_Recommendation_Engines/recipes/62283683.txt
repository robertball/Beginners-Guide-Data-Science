Title:
1 serving Cinnamon Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Oats
1 apple
3 T. powdered sugar
Add Cinnamon
Brown Sugar
MILK OR WATER

Directions:
In a bowl, add milk or water, oats, and chopped up apple...Put in Microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds...take out stir..add powdered sugar, cinnamon and brown sugar!!!Enjoy:)
