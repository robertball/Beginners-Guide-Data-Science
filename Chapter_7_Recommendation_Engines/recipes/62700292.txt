Title:
Big Red Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box white cake mix
2-3oz. strawberry jello
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1-12oz.can big red soda pop
1-3oz.pkg.instant vanilla pudding
3/4 cup milk
1-8oz. cool whip

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.Prepare cake as directed on the box.Grease 2x9x13-inch baking dish.After it bakes let it cool for 10 minutes.Poke holes in top of cake with wooden spoon.Mix jello and boiling water stir until it's dissolved then stir in big red soda pop.Pour over cake and refrigerate 1 hour or until firm.In mixing bowl add instant vanilla pudding,milk and cool whip mix well and then frost the cake.Keep it refrigerated.
