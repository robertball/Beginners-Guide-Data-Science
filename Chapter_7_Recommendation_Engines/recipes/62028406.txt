Title:
Sheppard's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 - Baking Potatoes
1 lb. lean ground beef
10 oz. can cream style corn
12 oz. can whole kernel corn (drained)
1/4 cp. milk
1/4 cp. butter
2 T. finely chopped onion
1 tsp. salt
1 egg, beaten
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain.  Turn into a 2 qt. baking dish to form bottom layer of pie.  Add cream style corn and then whole kernel corn to form second layer.  Peal, boil, drain and mash potatoes while still warm.  Add milk, butter, onion, salt and pepper to potatoes and beat until light and fluffy.  Turn into baking dish to form top layer.  Brush with beaten egg.
Bake in oven at 350 degrees until potatoes are slightly brown.  About 30 minutes.
