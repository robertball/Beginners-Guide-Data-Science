Title:
Mini Italian Meat Loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 pounds groung beef
3/4 cup tomato sauce
Shredded parmesan Cheese(optional)

Directions:
1. Combine egg, milk, bread crumbs, and salt in mixing bowl.
2. Crumbl the beef over the mixture and mix.
3.Shape into six individual loaves.
4. Place into a greased 13-in.x 9-in.x 2-in. baking dish.
5. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.
6. Pour tomato sauce over loaves.
7. Bake an additional 15 minutes.
8. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.(if desired)
9. It's ready to eat!
