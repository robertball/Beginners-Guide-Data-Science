Title:
Mom's Party Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds ground chuck
2 cup oatmeal
5 1/3 tablespoons milk; (16 tsp)
2/3 cup onion
1 1/3 teaspoon salt
1 1/3 teaspoon garlic salt
1 1/3 teaspoon pepper
2 8 oz. can tomato sauce
2 tablespoon vinegar
4 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix together hamburger, oatmeal, milk, onion, salt, garlic salt and pepper.
Roll into small balls. Broil until no longer pink.
Combine tomato sauce, vinegar, brown sugar and Worcestershire for the sauce. Heat and toss cooked meatballs in it.
Serve.

Submitter: 
HOWEWASS
Description: 
My Mom always served these meatballs at her Christmas party. 

This is double the original recipe my Mom used. I use this version for a party of about 75.
