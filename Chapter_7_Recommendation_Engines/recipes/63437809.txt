Title:
GooWa's Taco Soup... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/932356.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 cup of water
1 pkg taco mix
1 ranch dressing mix
1 can rotel
1 can pinto beans
1 can red kidney beans
1 can black beans
1 can ranch style beans
1 can whole kernal corn
Garnish:
tortilla chips
grated cheese
chopped onions
sour cream

Directions:
Brown gound beef & drain.
Add a cup of water
Add taco mix & ranch dressing mix.
Add rotel,
Add all the beans,
Add corn.
Simmer 15 minutes or so.

Submitter: 
Nauntie
Description: 
Super easy family meal. It is a wonderful recipe as is. 
*When I need to feed an army on a budget, I make dry beans and freeze leftovers.* 
Because you can use most any beans, I pull out my leftovers and start throwing stuff in, when I feed this recipe to an army.
