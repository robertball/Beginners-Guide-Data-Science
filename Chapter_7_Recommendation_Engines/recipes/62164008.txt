Title:
Tofu Hoagies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 block of extra-firm tofu, drained and frozen.
1/2 cup yellow curry sauce
1 small squirt (roughly 1/2 T.) of mayo
1 T. curry powder
1 t. ground cumin
1 t. coriander spice
1/2 t. onion powder
1/2 t. garlic powder
1/2 t. cardamom
1/2 to 1 t. cayenne pepper, depending on how spicy you like it.
2 T. vegetable oil
1 c. carrots, shredded
3 green onions, chopped
1/2 T. lime juice
small bunch of fresh cilantro, chopped
4 Hoagie buns

Directions:
cut the drained tofu up in cubes and put in a bowl.
pour in the yellow curry sauce and mayo.
add all of the spices, which can be adjusted according to your preferences.
mix everything well and let sit in a fridge for about 15 - 30 minutes to let it all soak in.
In a skillet add the vegetable oil and fry the tofu until the sides get golden brown.
Combine the shredded carrots, green onions, and lime juice.
Toast the hoagie buns in the oven for a few minutes, until lightly crispy.
Now you are ready to assemble your hoagie! Layer the cilantro, carrot mixture and tofu in the bun as you wish.
Enjoy!
