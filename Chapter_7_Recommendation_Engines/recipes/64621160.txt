Title:
Cream Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
8 TBS. butter
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. sugar
1 cup all purpose flour
3 -4 eggs (depends on the size)
Cream Filling
2 cups cream
1 package vanilla instant pudding
1/4 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
preheat oven to 425
Bring the water, butter, salt, and sugar to a boil in a medium sauce pan
Remove from heat and put into a mixer
Add flour mix at medium speed
adding one egg at a time stir on low speed
place a piece of parchament  paper on a 17 by 12 in baking sheet
scoop the mix on to the baking sheet placing them about an inch apart
stick in oven for 15 min.
reduce heat to 375 bake 20 more min
mix cream filling ingredients until peaks form
cut a slit in each puff after cooling  and fill them with the filling
serve after dinner or whenever
