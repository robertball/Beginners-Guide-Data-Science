Title:
rajma 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. cooked rice
2 T. coconut oil
2 c. cooked kidney beans
1/2 tsp. mustard and/ or cumin seeds
1 medium onion, chopped
1 pinch of garlic powder
1 small piece of ginger, minced
2 T. of tomato paste
1/4 tsp. turmeric
1 tsp. coriander
salt and chili powder to taste
1 tsp. garam masala.

Directions:
Heat up oil, add seeds and onions, and fry till onions turn golden brown.
Add tomato paste, ginger, garlic, and turmeric.  Mix thoroughly, then add salt, coriander, and chili powder.
Add beans and about 1 1/2 c. water (or bean water, if home-cooked, along with fresh tomatoes if desired). Let all simmer till consistency is good for serving over rice. Finally, add the garam masala.
