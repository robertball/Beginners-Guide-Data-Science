Title:
BLT Chopped Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c salad greens (romaine, spinach, butter lettuce, etc), chopped
2 c cherry tomatoes, halved
6 slices thick bacon, fried and crumbled
1 c sweet corn
1 avocado, diced
4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
Dressing...
1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp lime juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl combine all salad ingredients except bacon and avocado.
In small bowl, mix dressing ingredients and pour over salad.
Toss well, and gently fold in bacon and avocado.

Description: 
Like a BLT without the bread.
