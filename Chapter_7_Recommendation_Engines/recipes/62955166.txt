Title:
Classic Champagne/Cava Cocktail with Brandy Kicker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cube Sugar
2 dashes Angostura Bitters
1 dram Brandy or to taste
Champagne/Cava - chilled

Directions:
Drop the sugar cube in the bottom of a chilled champagne flute;
Add the bitters;
Pour in the brandy;
Top up slowly with the champagne/cava
