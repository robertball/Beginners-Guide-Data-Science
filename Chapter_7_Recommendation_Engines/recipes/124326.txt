Title:
Raspberry Mallow Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977535.jpg
Ingredients:
35 large marshmallows
1/2 cup milk
1 (10 ounce) package sweetened frozen raspberries
1 (8 ounce) carton frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (9 inch) graham cracker crust

Directions:
In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine marshmallows and milk. Cook on high for 1-2 minutes; stir until smooth. Stir in raspberries. Fold in whipped topping. Pour into crust. Refrigerate or freeze.

Submitter: 
Judie  Anglen
