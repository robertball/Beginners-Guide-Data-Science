Title:
Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3469665.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pack of ground turkey
1 Pack of spaghetti noodles
1 Green bell pepper
1 Yellow onion
4 Cans of ragu four cheese spaghetti sauce
Garlic and onion powder
Season all
Pepper
Italian seasoning
1 cup of sugar

Directions:
1. In a pot boil your noodles to they are fully cooked then drain them
2. In another pot cook your ground turkey. Season well with season all, pepper, and onion and garlic powder to your desire.
3. Add your sauces.Season well with season all, onion powder, garlic powder, pepper, and Italian seasoning. It contributes to how strong your flavor will be.
4. Add a cup of sugar.My family likes it sweet so we may add 2 1/2 cups .
5. Turn your pot down to a simmer. Pot a top on it and let it cook for 45 minutes continuously stirring it every once in a while.
6. Add your noodles and put your sauce on top(some people mix it)and add your Parmesan cheese and any other desired topping.
7. Enjoy!
