Title:
Spicy Sausage Pasta with Tomato Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rao's Arrabiata Sauce (3/4 jar)
1/2 yellow onion, diced
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 lb of hot italian sausage
1/4 cup of heavy cream
Red wine vinegar
Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes and garlic salt to taste
Serve with 3/4 lb of rigatoni

Directions:
Brown seasoned onion, garlic and red pepper flakes in olive oil in a dutch oven
Once the onions begin to caramelize, add the sausage to brown
Boil salted water for pasta
Once sausage is browned, add tomato sauce to pot and simmer
Add red white vinegar to sauve and continue to simmer while pasta boils
When pasta is almost done, turn off heat on sauce mixture and add heavy cream
Drain pasta and mix with sauce
Top with parmesan serve with bread and a green salad
