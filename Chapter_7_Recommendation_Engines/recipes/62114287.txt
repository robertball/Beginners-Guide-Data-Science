Title:
Grilled Basil Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound of medium fresh Shrimp, peeled, deveined and rinsed
5 T Basil Infused Olive Oil
2t Emeril's Bayou Blast seasoning
1 T Fresh basil, finely chopped
1 Large lemon, cut into wedges

Directions:
Mix together olive oil, fresh basil and Bayou Blast seasoning.
Coat shrimp with the oil mixture
Cover and marinate in the refridgerator for 1-2 hours
Place on preheated grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until shrimp turns pink
When removed from the grill, squeeze fresh lemon juice on the shrimp and sprinkle with Bayou Blast seasoning. Garnish with lemon wedges and fresh basil.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
This light, lowfat appetizer hits the spot every time. Make it a meal by doubling the marinade (set half aside) and tossing over fettucine noodles. Serve with a light salad and white wine!
