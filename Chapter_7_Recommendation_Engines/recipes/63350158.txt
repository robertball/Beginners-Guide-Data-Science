Title:
Mexican style Rice and Meat casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 thick slices leftover roast beef ( could use chicken breastcooked and cubed of ground hamburg cooked or leftover pork cubed)
2 cups cooked rice
1 can black beans drained
1 large tomato cut up
1/2 yellow or red sweet pepper cut up
may want to garnish with salsa, sourcream and limes
4 tablespoons apple cider vineger
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 teaspoon herbs de provence ( rosemary, thyme, lavender mixture)
salt and pepper
1 cup gratted cheddar cheese
1 cup tortilla chips

Directions:
in 8x8 deep casserole dish combine all ingredients but cheese and chips
mix well
cover and bake 350 for 45 min
remove cover and add cheese and break the chips up evenly over the top...cover again and bake 10 more min
may want to serve with sour cream and salsa on the side...and / or limes
