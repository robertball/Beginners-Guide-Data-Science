Title:
Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. white sugar
1 c. shortening
1 c. peanut butter
1 t. baking soda
1 t. baking powder
1 pkg. chocolate chips
2 1/2 c. flour

Directions:
Mix all together in the order given.
Roll into balls and flatten with a fork.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.
