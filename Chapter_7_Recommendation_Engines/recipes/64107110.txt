Title:
Chef Harry Estep's Angry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 (8 oz) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1/2 tablespoon chopped garlic
1/4 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon coriander
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon crushed red chilies
1/2 quart salad oil
1 tablespoon Cajun spice
4 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Place chicken in container and set aside.  In large mixing bowl, combine Dijon mustard, garlic, cilantro, red wine vinegar, salt, coriander, chili powder, red chilies and salad oil.  Mix well.  Pour marinade over chicken, cover and refrigerate 6 hours.  Remove chicken from marinade and sprinkle with Cajun spice.  Bake (or grill over white-hot charcoals), occasionally basting with butter, in a preheated 350-degree oven 25 to 30 minutes or until cooked.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
iammykidzmom
Description: 
This recipe appeared in the Pioneer Press newspaper some years ago following a request from a reader for Chef Estep's Angry Chicken of the Pointe in Tyme in Pointe Hilton Resort At Tapatio Cliffs in Phoenix, AZ.  It is excellent served with spanish rice and refried or black beans.
