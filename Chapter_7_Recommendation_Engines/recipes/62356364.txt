Title:
Fluffy Jello Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (4 serving size) vanilla instant pudding
1 1/2 c. milk
1 c. cool whip

Directions:
Beat milk and pudding until thickened.  Fold in the cool whip.  You can add food coloring if you like!

Submitter: 
Traci Caha Myers
Description: 
Great for a light frosting on cupcakes or Jello cake!
