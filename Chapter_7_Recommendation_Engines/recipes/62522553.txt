Title:
Hearty Garden Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/513611.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 tablespoons olive oil
1 small white onion; minced
3 cloves garlic; minced
1 large stalk celery; chopped
1 medium zucchini; chopped (or 14.5 oz can)
1 (8 oz.) can cut green beans
1 (14.5 oz.) can vegetable broth
1 (15 oz.) can red kidney beans; rinsed
1 (15 oz.) can small white beans; rinsed
1 (14 oz.) can diced tomatoes
2 large carrots; chopped
2 teaspoons italian seasoning
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Black pepper to taste
3 cups water
2 cups fresh spinach (or 8 oz. can)
3 oz. small pasta

Directions:
In large pot heat oil over medium heat.
Add onion, garlic, celery, zucchini and green beans. Saute for 5 minutes.
Add broth, beans, tomatoes, carrots and seasoning to pot. Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer for 20 minutes.
Add spinach and pasta. Simmer for additional 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
SlowcookerSeduction
Description: 
Your bowl will be bursting with a hearty blend of colorful vegetables, pasta and spices. Serve with crusty bread for a satisfying meal.
