Title:
dairy free mashed sweet potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sweet potatoes peel and chopped into wedges
sea salt
1/4 cup coconut oil
1/4 cup chicken broth
1 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
1. boil sweet potatoes in salted water until soft
2. drain and add coconut oil, broth, and cinnamon.
3. blend until smooth with an immersion blender.
4. add salt to taste
