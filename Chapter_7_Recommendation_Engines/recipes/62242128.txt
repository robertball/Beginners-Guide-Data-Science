Title:
Chicken Parmesan Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Chicken
1/2 Cup Italian Bread Crumbs
1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 Egg
1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
1 teaspoon Onion Powder
3 tablespoons tomato paste (Seperated 1 tbsp & 2 tbsp)
1 cup chicken stock
1 cup canned crushed tomato
1 medium onion
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 slices provolgne cheese
4 whole wheat burger buns
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients. Add 1 tablespoon of the tomato paste to ground chicken mixture.
Form into 4 patties. Make sure they are nicely flattened.
Cook in tablespoon of oil on medium for about 4 minutes on both sides. While cooking burgers cut up onion.
Once burgers are cooked through. Remove from pan and set aside.
Put onion in remaining oil and chicken juices. Cook until translucent. (About 3-4 minutes)
Add remaining 2 tablespoons of tomato paste, 1 cup of the tomatoes, and 1 cup chicken stock.
Cook until reduced by half and thickened. (About 5 minutes)
Add burgers and cover with tomato mixture cover for about 3 minutes.
Add cheese and cover for another 1 minute.
Place burgers on buns and spoon tomato mixture over. Enjoy!!!

This recipe tastes just like a chicken parm!! I was trying to find something that was healthy and tasty and this is it!!!
