Title:
Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 tbsp. butter
1/4 tsp. salt
1 2" cinnamon stick
1 cup Arborio or short grain rice
2 1/2 cups fat free milk
1/3 cup sugar
2 tsp. lime zest
1/2 cup raisins (optional)
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients in large pot and bring to a boil.
Stir in rice and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes or until liquid is almost absorbed, stirring occasionally.
Discard cinnamon stick.
Stir in milk, sugar and zest.
Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring frequently.
Reduce heat to medium low and cook 25 min. or until thick and creamy, stirring frequently.
Remove from heat.
Stir in raisins and vanilla.
