Title:
Nuts & Bolts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2884131.jpg
Ingredients:
9 cups Cheerios cereal
9 cups Shreddies cereal
6 cups small pretzels, any shape
454 g bag peanuts, optional
1 1/2 cups margarine, melted
2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
5 tsp. ranch dressing powder
3 tsp. taco seasoning powder (as spicy as you like!)

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients in a large roaster.
Stir together remaining ingredients and drizzle over first mixture, stirring to distribute well.
Bake, uncovered, @ 250 for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring every 20 minutes.
Cool.
Stores well in covered container or bags. Also freezes well.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Great crunchy treat. Can adjust the ingredients to your own taste. Try to eat just a handful!!
