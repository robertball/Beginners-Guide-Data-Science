Title:
Napoli Dough Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6903817.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs, 2, 0 oz. High Gluten (Bread) flour
26. 0 oz. Ice Water
1. oz. Salt
2 oz. Butter
.3 oz. Yeast

Directions:
Mix salt and water, until dissolved, then add the flour
Add butter, then yeast.
Allow mixer to mix for  12 minutes
Shape into balls. Small to medium pizzas = 8 oz. ball. Large pizza - 18 oz. ball
Wrap in plastic and rise in refrigerator at 41F for 24 hours.
Shape and top as desired
Back at 450 -500 F until lightly browned
