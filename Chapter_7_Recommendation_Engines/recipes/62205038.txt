Title:
Rosemary Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/306330.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 medium egg
1 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1 T. finely chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 tsp. baking powder

Directions:
Beat butter and sugar until creamy.  Add egg.
Add whole wheat flour, all purpose flour, rosemary and baking powder, combine until smooth.
Shape into 2 logs about 1 1/4" in diameter, wrap each in plastic.  Chill at least 2 hours in refrigerator or 1 hour in freezer.
Preheat oven to 350.  Slice 1/8"-1/4" thick, place on parchment papered baking sheets.  Bake 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Refrigerator cookies with my favorite herb, rosemary!  I developed the recipe for Christmas baking, but it would be wonderful any time of year!
