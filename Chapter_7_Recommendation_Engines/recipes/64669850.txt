Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4580443.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
4 slices of boiled ham
4 slices swiss cheese
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/2 c bread crumbs or more
3 T oil
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
Pound chicken to 1/4" thickness
Place a slice of ham on each chicken breast, then a slice of cheese.
Roll up and secure with a toothpick . Add salt and pepper to beaten egg
Dip rolls in egg and then bread crumbs. In a large skillet, brown rolls in oil about 5 min. Transfer to a baking pan and Bake at 375 for 30 min.
