Title:
Amped Up Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz can back beans
1 medium onion diced
5 slices thick bacon roughly diced
1 teaspoon ancho chili powder
1 teaspoon pasilla chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano(mexican oregano if you can find it)
2 garlic cloves minced
1 small red bell pepper diced
Kernals from 1 ear of corn(preferably roasted on BBQ first)
Juice of 1/2 lime
1 tablespoon orange juice
Salt
Cilantro and queso fresco(or feta cheese) for garnish

Directions:
Add the onion, bacon, both chili powders, cumin and cayenne to a fry pan over medium heat.  Cook until the bacon starts to crisp and the onions are very soft-about 10 mins.
Add the oregano,garlic and bell pepper and cook for another 2 minutes.
Add the beans(liquid included), corn, lime and orange juice to the pan.  Simmer on low heat for about 5-10 minutes.  If it dries out add a little water.
Salt to taste
Garnish with chopped cilantro and cheese.

Submitter: 
Ken from CA
Description: 
A great side dish for BBQs, taco night or whenever.
