Title:
Easy Italian Cannoli Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. container of good-quality whole milk ricotta (do not use skim)
1/2 cup powdered sugar
A sprinkle of cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Combine the ingredients well.
Fill the cannoli shells right before eating so they stay crisp.
Decorate with mini chocolate chips.
