Title:
NACHO CHEESE SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/4 cup chopped red pepper
4-5 cloves garlic,minced
2 scallions,minced
1 Tbsp.olive oil
2 Tbsp.butter
2 Tbsp.flour
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp.tabasco sauce
2 Tbsp.jalapeno pepper
1/4 tsp.salt
pinch of cayenne
1/4 tsp.paprika
nacho chips

Directions:
Mix together peppers,olive oil,garlic and butter in saucepan.Sprinkle in flour,add milk and stir untill mixture simmers.Do not boil.When mixture begins to thicken,stir in the cheese and remaining ingredients.
Simmer on low heat for 5 min.or untill cheese melts.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
Warm cheese dip.Serve with your favorite taco chips.
