Title:
Pampered Chef BLT Ranch Salad Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package refigerated pizza crust
2 cups thinly sliced lettuce
2 plum tomatoes
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup ranch salad dressing, divided
1-garlic clove, pressed
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup pitted olives, sliced
1/4 cup chopped red onion
6 slices bacon, crisply cooked, drained and crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 deg.
Unroll dough and press on lightly floured stone
Bake 15-18 min or until golden brown. Remove from oven and cool completely.
Thinly slice lettuce. Seed and chop tomatoes.
In Medium bowl mix cream cheese and 1/4 cup of ranch dressing and pressed garlic; mix well and spread evenly over cooled crust.
Top pizza with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, olives, onion and bacon. Drizzle with the remainder of the dressing.
Cut into squares and serve immediately.
