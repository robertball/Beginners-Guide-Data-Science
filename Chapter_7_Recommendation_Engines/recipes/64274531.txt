Title:
Jan's Maple Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1895532.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter room temp*
2 cups white sugar
2 large eggs room temp*
1 cup sour cream room temp*
2 teaspoons maple extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
For topping:
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons cold butter, diced
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 9 inch baking pans.
In a large bowl, cream together 1 cup butter and white sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, add in the sour cream and maple extract. Mix in flour, baking powder and salt. Spread 1/2 of the batter in each pan.
Prepare the topping: In a small bowl mix flour, brown sugar,  butter, cinnamon and nutmeg. Cut in/mash with forks until fine crumbs are present. Sprinkle evenly on tops of cakes.
Bake approximately 35 to 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
***use ingredients at room temp or baking times will be off and topping will harden.
Cakes also freeze very well.
