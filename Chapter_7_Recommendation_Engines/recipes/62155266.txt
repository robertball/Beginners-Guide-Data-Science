Title:
Easy CrockPot Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs fresh yellow squash
1 small onion
4 tbl spoons butter, cut in cubes
4 0z velvetta cheese
dont use to much cheese, you want to taste the wonderful
flavor of the yellow or summer squash

Directions:
Clean, slice, BOIL ,squash and onion
until cooked till desired texture,try not to over cook
layer in CROCK POT
butter,squash @ onion, velvetta, cheese
cook on low or warm
try not to stir to much as squash will fall apart

Submitter: 
floridascrubtech
Description: 
Yellow squash made easy, and put to the side to keep warm , so you can tend to other parts of the dinner, it is easy and delicious.
