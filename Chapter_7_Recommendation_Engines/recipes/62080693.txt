Title:
Cranberry Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup water
-----
2 cups fresh cranberries
1/2 cup raisins
1/4 cup finely chopped almonds
2 T. cider vinegar
1 T. brown sugar
1 T. finely grated fresh ginger
1/4 crushed red pepper

Directions:
Cook water and gr. sugar in stainless steel sauce pan, bring to boil.  Boil, stirring constantly until sugar dissolves.
Reduce heat, and add remaining ingredients.  Continue cooking, uncovered, for 10 minutes or until berries burst and mixure has thickened some (will continue to thicken as it cools).  Remove from heat and quickly ladel chutney into hot sterilized jars.
Cool, and serve with turkey or your favorite holiday meat, share with others as a holiday gift.  Excellent with ham and lamb!  Enjoy and happy holidays.
