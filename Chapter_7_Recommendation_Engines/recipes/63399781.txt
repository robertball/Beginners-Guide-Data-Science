Title:
Farfalle with Creamy Wild Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. uncooked farfalle pasta
1 tbsp. butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/3 cup finely chopped shallots
1 tbsp. minced garlic
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
12 oz. pre-sliced exotic mushroom blend (if this is unavailable, use all cremini)
1/4 cup dry white wine
2/3 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp. chopped fresh flat leaf parsley.

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente.
Melt butter in a large non-stick skillet over medium high heat.
Add onion, shallots, garlic pepper and 1 tsp. of salt to pan.
Cook about 12 minutes or until liquid evaporates and mushrooms are tender.
Add wine a cook about 2 minutes or until liquid evaporates, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and add cooked pasta, whipping cream and parsley to pan, tossing gently to coat.
Garnish with additional parsley before serving if desired.
