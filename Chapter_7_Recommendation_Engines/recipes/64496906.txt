Title:
Caramel Popcorn Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c Unsalted Butter, softened
1c Brown Sugar
2/3c Sugar
2 eggs
1t Vanilla
2 1/2c All purpose Flour
1t Baking Soda
1t Kosher Salt
4c Caramel Popcorn (200grams/3.5oz)
1c Chocolate Chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350.
2. Cream butter and sugars until well combined.  Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.  Mix in Vanilla.
3.  In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt and baking soda, and then add to the batter.
4. Blend the popcorn and chips into the batter, being careful not to overmix the batter.
5. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper and drop batter using a medium cookie scoop.
6.  Bake for 11-12 minutes at 350, let cool on the sheet for a few minutes and then move to a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Grace Gutberlet
Description: 
Great use for leftover caramel popcorn from the beach!   Great with or without the chocolate chips.  I use a stand mixer, as it helps to break up the popcorn pieces in the batter.
