Title:
Matcha Madness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2617406.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup low-fat soy milk
1/2 cup low-fat vanilla Greek-style yogurt
3/4 teaspoon green tea powder (matcha)
3 cubes  ice, or as needed

Directions:
Blend soy milk, yogurt, green tea powder, and ice in a blender on high until ice is entirely crushed, about 1 minute.

Description: 
This matcha shake is super, made with low-fat ingredients, packed with protein, antioxidants, and flavor!
