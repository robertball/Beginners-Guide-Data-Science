Title:
Pesto Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. penne pasta
1 1/2 C pesto
2 C Ranch dressing
4 Tbsp. Italian dressing
1 tsp. black pepper
1/4 C red wine vinegar
3/4 C chopped Greek olives
2 cucumbers, deseeded and diced

Directions:
Cook pasta until tender.  Drain and cool.  In a large bowl add all ingredients and mix well.  Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Tangy and flavorful, this pesto pasta is sure to delight.
