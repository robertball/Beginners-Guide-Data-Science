Title:
Cajun Wonder Rice Dressing / Dirty Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927732.jpg
Ingredients:
¼ lb chicken livers
2 T flour
2 T oil
½ cup onions diced
½ cup celery diced
2 Serrano peppers diced
1 clove garlic grated
1 tsp salt
¼ tsp black pepper
¼ tsp red/cayenne pepper
½ lb grnd beef
½ lb grnd pork
½ cup chicken broth
2 cups raw white rice
½ cup green onion tops, finely chopped
¼ cup parsley

Directions:
Pre-cook the white rice according to the package directions.
Rinse clean then boil chicken livers for 10 minutes.  Cool, dice, set aside.
Prepare all ingredients  – chop veggies, measure spices, etc.
In cast iron skillet, heat oil and flour making a brown roux over medium heat.  To make a roux, continuously stir the oil and flour mixture with a whisk until it turns a color a little lighter than an old penny.
Add first set of veggies
Cook until tender, stirring
Stir in all meats and spices
Cook on medium high heat until meats lose color, stirring often
Stir in broth and simmer covered for 25 minutes
Stir in cooked rice and green onions and parsley
Cook on low for 5 minutes

Description: 
Cajun rice dressing or sometimes called dirty rice is generally a side dish, but this recipe is so addicting and delicious you'll tend to eat more of it than the rest of the meal.  Rice dressing is also a traditional Thanksgiving dish instead of stuffing.
