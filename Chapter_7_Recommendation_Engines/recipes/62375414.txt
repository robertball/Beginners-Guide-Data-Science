Title:
Strawberry (or other fruit) Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Strawberry mixture:
1 bag frozen strawberries, thawed or 1 pkg. Fresh
1 C sugar
Juice of 1 lemon
Ice Cream:
1 C milk
1 cup sugar
3 C heavy whipping cream
1 T vanilla

Directions:
Strawberries: Put strawberries into blender
Add sugar and lemon juice
Let sit 1-2 hrs.
Blend on puree
Ice Cream: Blend milk and sugar until dissolved
Add cream and vanilla
Add strawberry mixture and stir together.
Freeze

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a no-cook, super smooth ice cream recipe.
