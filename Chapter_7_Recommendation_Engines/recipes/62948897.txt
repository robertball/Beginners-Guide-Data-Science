Title:
Garbanzo Extravaganzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (15.5 oz.) GOYA Low Sodium Chick Peas, drained
1 bag (6 oz.)baby spinach
1/2 cup medium red onion, finely chopped
1 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
3 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
GOYA Adobo with Pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together chick peas, spinach, and onions.
Pour vinegar into a small bowl.  Using a whisk, slowly drizzle in oil, whisking constantly, until combined.  Season with Adobo.  Pour dressing over salad.  Toss well to coat.
