Title:
Upside Down Pizza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985036.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (24 ounce) jar Classico® Tomato and Basil pasta sauce
1 1/2 (16 ounce) packages Johnsonville® Mild Italian Ground Sausage
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated biscuits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. In a large skillet cook Johnsonville(R) Mild Italian Ground Sausage until no longer pink, stirring frequently. Drain off fat. Stir in Classico(R) Tomato and Basil pasta sauce; heat through. Transfer mixture to a 2-quart rectangular baking dish. Sprinkle mixture with cheese.
Flatten each biscuit with your hands; arrange the biscuits on top of the cheese. Bake in the preheated oven about 15 minutes or until biscuits are golden.

Submitter: 
Classico
Description: 
This quick pizza casserole can be altered easily. Substitute Italian sausage for the ground beef, or stir sliced mushrooms or ripe olives into the meat mixture.
