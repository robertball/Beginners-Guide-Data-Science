Title:
Goya Culantro Shrimp Recipe

Ingredients:

1 1/4 fish
goya Achote y culantro
Tomato
onion
green pepper

Directions:
Season fish and shrimp wiht garlic, pepper and Goya seasoning.  Saute cut onions with tomatoe and green pepper.  Saute fish and cook.  Add shrimp til pink.  Serve over rice
