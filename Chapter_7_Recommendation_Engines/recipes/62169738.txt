Title:
Autumn Spiced Apple Yogurt Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large Granny Smith apples
2 large Gala apples
1/3 c. apple juice
1 tsp. lemon zest
1/2 c. dried cranberries
1/4 c. brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. fresh grated ginger
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
a few pinches of ground cloves
1/8 tsp. nutmeg
1/8 tsp. cardamom
1 c. vanilla yogurt
1/4 c. pecans, toasted and chopped

Directions:
Peel, core and dice the apples.
Place the apples into a large saucepan and add the apple juice, lemon zest and cranberries.
Bring up to a boil and then reduce heat to medium, cover pan and cook for about 8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until apples start to soften.
Stir in the brown sugar and all spices.
Turn off the heat and let apples cool to room temp.
Divide the apples into 4 dessert bowls or glasses.
Top each of the apple servings with a big dollop of vanilla yogurt and garnish with the toasted pecans.

Description: 
Chunky homemade applesauce dressed up with warming spices, topped with creamy yogurt and scattered with crunchy toasted nuts. A great and healthy breakfast, snack, or a delicious dessert after a fall dinner.
