Title:
Laura's Hawaiian chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fried Chicken
2 lbs boneless chicken breasts cut in to pieces
2 eggs
1 cup flour
salt and pepper to taste
Sauce
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup pineapple chunks
2 tbsp chopped onion
2 peppers cut into strips
1 tsp soy sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup pineapple juice
1 tbsp ketchup
2 tbsp cornstarch

Directions:
Dip cut chicken pieces in egg then dredge in flour.
Deep fry in deep fryer until golden yellow. Pat off excess oil on paper towel set aside.
In a pot, mix chicken broth, salt, pineapple chunks, chopped onion, and green peppers. Simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
Add soysauce, brown sugar, pineapple juice and ketchup.
Mix cornstarch with enough warm water to make a thin paste. Add to sauce to thicken. Put cooked chicken pieces into the sauce.
Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
Kevin Gorton
Description: 
This was another recipe given to me by my friend Laura at my bridal shower. The sweet pineapple sauce make it a favorite with the kids. Its very filling and cheap to make.
