Title:
Lemon Plum Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 Cups all-purpose flour
1/2  teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
3 eggs
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 1/2 teaspoons lemon zest, finely grated
1/2 cup plain yogurt
3 plums pitted and cut into eighths
2 tablespoons flour (for tossing plums)
powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Grease a 9-inch cake pan and line bottom with parchment paper round.
In a large bowl, whisk together 1 1/2 cups flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon
In a separate bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until light and fluffy. beat in eggs one at a time. Beat in vanilla and lemon zest.
Alternate adding flour mixture and sour cream until combined.
Spread batter into 9" round cake pan and smooth. In a bowl, toss plums with 2 tablespoons of flour and arrange on top of the batter in desired pattern.
Bake for 30 minutes or until browned. Loosely cover with foil and bake for another 30-40 minutes or until cake pulls away from side of pan and tester comes out clean.
Let cake cool completely in pan, run knife along edge and remove from pan. Dust with powdered sugar and serve.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kays
Description: 
This is a great cake for a bridal shower, baby shower, or tea party. It's light, fluffy, and not too sweet. The plums and lemon zest work very well together.
