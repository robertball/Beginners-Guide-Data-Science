Title:
Green Chile Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 (2 ounce) can sliced ripe olives
1/3 cup Hunt's® Ketchup
1/2 teaspoon Gebhardt® Chili Powder
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
8 slices County Line® Monterey Jack Cheese
8 hamburger buns, warmed

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except cheese and buns; mix well. Shape into 8 patties.
Grill patties 5 minutes on each side or until desired doneness. Top patties with cheese during the last 3 minutes of cooking.
Place cooked patties on top of hamburger buns; top with additional ketchup.

Submitter: 
Hunt's
Description: 
These burgers are packed with the distinctive flavors of Southwest cooking.
