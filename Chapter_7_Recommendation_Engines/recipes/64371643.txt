Title:
Creamy Lemon Cake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. lemon cake mix (regular size)
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 egg
Filling:
1 can (16 0z) lemon frosting
1 pkg. (8 oz. ) cream cheese, softened
2 eggs

Directions:
1.  In large bowl, beat the cake mix, butter and egg until crumbly.  Press into bottom of a greased 13 x 9 in. baking pan.
2.  In another bowl, beat frosting and cream cheese until smooth.  Set aside 1/2 cup for topping.  Add the eggs to remaining mixture;  beat on low speed just until combined.  Spread over crust.
3.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 min. or until filling is set.  Cool on a wire rack for 2 hour.  Spread reserved frosting mixture over top.  Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Cut into bars.  Store in the refrigerator in sealed container.
