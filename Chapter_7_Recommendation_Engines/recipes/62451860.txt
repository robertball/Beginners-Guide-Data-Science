Title:
Quick Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed golden mushroom soup
1 1/4 cups Marsala wine
1 box of slice white mushrooms, sauted in butter
1/8 cup to 1/4 cup heavy cream, added at the end.

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute the chicken breasts in butter. Once chicken is lightly browned on all sides, add the soup (undiluted) and Marsala wine. Add sauted mushrooms. Cover and simmer until chicken is no longer pink inside, about 20 minutes. Add cream and simmer 3 more minutes. Ready to serve!

Submitter: 
Samba
Description: 
This recipe gives you the basics--chicken, mushroom soup for the sauce, Marsala wine.  But you can enhance it with fresh sauteed mushrooms or onions if you have the inclination.Modified from: Quick Chicken Marsala
