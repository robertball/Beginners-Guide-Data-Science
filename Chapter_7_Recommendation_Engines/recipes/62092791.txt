Title:
Angel Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 cup sweetend coconut flakes
1 cup canned mandarin oranges, drained
1 cup canned chunk pineapple, drained
1 cup mini-marshmallows

Directions:
Gently mix all ingredients together in a mixing bowl.  Chill for 2 hours.  Transfer to serving dish and enjoy!

Submitter: 
PamN
Description: 
A delicious ambrosia-like salad that my mom used to make for Christmas and Thanksgiving.
