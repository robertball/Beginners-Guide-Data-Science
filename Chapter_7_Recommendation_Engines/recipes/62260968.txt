Title:
Garbage Plate Hot Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2 lbs Ground Beef 1/2 -Cup Chopped Onion 1/2 -Cup Butter or Margarine 4 -TBLS Chili Powder 2- TBLS Cayenne Pepper Or more you can always Add some at end if you want more heat 4- TBLS Black Pepper 2- TSP Paprika 1/8- TSP Ground Cloves 1/4- TSP Thyme 1/2- TSP Cinnamon 2- TBLS Yellow Mustard 2 -TSP Salt 2 -Cup of Water

Directions:
Directions
Brown ground beef and drain
If you have a food processor grind beef fine
Or mash while simmering
In a separate pan. Add onions and butter
heat until onions are soft
Mix together dry ingredients
Add Water, Beef and Dry ingredients
Simmer 2 hours check occasionally and add water if needed
Cook down to the consistency that you like
