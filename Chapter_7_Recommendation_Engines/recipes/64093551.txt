Title:
Vegan Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2250320.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chia seeds
1 cup hot water
1 - 1 1/2 cups hemp milk
1 cup spelt flour or quinoa flour
1 cup millet flour or brown rice flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups blueberries (more or less)
2 tablespoons oil or vegan margarine

Directions:
Heat pancake skillet.
Stir chia seeds in water. Let sit for 10 minutes
Add hemp milk ~ (amount may vary, make sure the mixture has enough liquid.)
Blend in a high speed blender.
Mix together flours, baking soda and salt.
A liquid mix together thoroughly.
Add blueberries.
Melt margarine.
Spoon pancake batter and cook. Flpping pancakes when needed.
