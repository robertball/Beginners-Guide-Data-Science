Title:
Super Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1912204.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups packed spinach
1/2 cup cooked quinoa
1/4 cup shredded carrots
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup chickpeas
1/4 cup edamame beans
2 Tbsp pumpkin seeds
Ginger miso dressing

Directions:
Toss all ingredients and enjoy!

Submitter: 
paralee
Description: 
I tried a similar salad from Trader Joe's and fell in love. This is my attempt to recreate it on a regular basis.
