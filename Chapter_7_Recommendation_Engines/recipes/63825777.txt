Title:
Steam Sauteed Sesame Green Beans or Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1061230.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound green beans or asparagus, cut into 2 to 3 inch pieces
1/3 cup diced onion
1/4 cup water, more as needed to keep skillet from drying out completely
2 teaspoons low sodium soy sauce
2 to 3 dashes sesame oil
2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Heat a large skillet with a lid over medium heat.  add water and veggies; cover and steam for 3 to 5 minutes or until almost your desired tenderness. adding 1 to 2 tablespoons water as necessary.
Remove lid and let the water evaporate.  Add soy sauce, and sesame oil, stir to coat and stirring frequently until the soy sauce evaporates; remove from the heat add sesame seeds toss and serve.
