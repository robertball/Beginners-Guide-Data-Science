Title:
Tuscan Tomato Bread Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1 large red onion, diced
4 15 oz. canned tomatoes
4 15 oz. vegetable or chicken broth
10 slices, 2 to 3 days old dense Italian or French bread
2 cups fresh basil, chopped fine
1/2 to 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.  Add onion and saute about 5 minutes until soft.
Add tomatoes and broth to onion and simmer for 25 minutes.
Add bread and simmer until soft. Optionally you can add basil at this point, then puree all ingredients.
Optionally you can add Parmesan at this point and stir well.  Serve soup with additional oil, slivers of basil, and Parmesan on top.
If you do not add basil and Parmesan per above, serve on top of pureed soup.

An Italian comfort food, which we have heartily adopted from our Pacific City friend, Frank.  Soup should be dense and tasty!!
