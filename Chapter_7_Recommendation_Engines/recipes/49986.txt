Title:
Vanilla Milkshake IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/76733.jpg
Ingredients:
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
1 cup milk
1/2 cup pure maple syrup

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice cream, milk and maple syrup. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Kate
Description: 
This is a cool refreshing drink. It is sweet but not too sweet. Kids love it.
