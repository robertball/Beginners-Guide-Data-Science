Title:
SF JELLO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 PACKAGES KNOX GELATIN
1PACKAGE FAVORITE FLAVOR KOOL AID
2 CUPS WATER
10 PACKETS SPLENDA

Directions:
2 PKGS GELATIN IN1 CUP COLD WATER
LET SIT 2 MIN. TO SOFTEN
ADD 1 CUP BOILING WATER
MIX IN KOOL AID & SPLENDA
STIR TO DISSOLVE
REFRIGERATE
