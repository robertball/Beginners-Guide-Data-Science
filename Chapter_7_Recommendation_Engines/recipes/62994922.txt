Title:
Caramelized Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds boneless skinless chicken
1/2 cup soy sauce
3/4 cup ketchup
3/4 cup honey
3-4 cloves garlic, minced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Mix together the soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, salt and pepper and cook in pan on stovetop until boiling. Reduce heat and simmer for a few minutes. Pour over the chicken.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes, or until sauce is caramelized.

Submitter: 
tara
Description: 
Soy and ketchup, laced with honey and garlic, make for a potent sauce.  This chicken is ideal for appetizers or a main course.Modified from: Caramelized Baked Chicken
