Title:
Chicken and Green Chili Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988959.jpg
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups shredded cooked chicken
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chiles, undrained
1 1/2 cups KNUDSEN Sour Cream, divided
1 (8 ounce) package KRAFT Mexican Style Shredded Taco Cheese, divided
16 (6 inch) corn tortillas, warmed
3 (10 ounce) cans red enchilada sauce, warmed

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine chicken, chilies and 1 cup each sour cream and cheese; spoon down centers of tortillas. Roll up.
Spread 1 cup sauce onto bottom of 13x9-inch pan. Add enchiladas, seam-sides down; top with remaining sauce. Cover.
Bake 20 minutes or until heated through. Sprinkle with remaining cheese; bake, uncovered, 5 minutes or until melted. Serve topped with remaining sour cream.

Submitter: 
KNUDSEN
Description: 
Get a hot, Mexican-inspired dish of chicken enchiladas on the table in under an hour. Sour cream, corn tortillas, Mexican-style cheese, and enchilada sauce add their rich flavors to the dish.
