Title:
Apricot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken parts (could be all breasts or combo - bone in)
1 16oz Russian or Thousand Island dressing
1 18oz jar apricot preserves
1 pkge of Lipton Onion Soup mix

Directions:
Spray baking pan with PAM or similar product or can line pan with foil for easy clean up
Pack chicken into baking pan
Mix ingredients
Pour half over chicken
Bake at 350 degrees
When half way done pour remaining sauce on chicken

Submitter:

Description: 
The ingredients don't make any sense but the chicken comes out great.  Serve over rice and top with remaining sauce
