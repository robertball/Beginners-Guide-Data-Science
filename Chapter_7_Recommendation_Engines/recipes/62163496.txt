Title:
Red Hot Applesauce Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red hot candy
1 cup water
3 oz Pkg cherry jello
2 cups applesauce

Directions:
In a pan on the stove dissolve red hots in water, and let simmer for a minute.  Dont boil.
Remove from heat, and add jello. Stir till dissolved.
Add applesauce and mix well.
put in serving bowl, and refrigerate until well set.

Submitter: 
tigershoes
Description: 
This is the only jello that tastes good to me.  I love it and serve it every holiday.
