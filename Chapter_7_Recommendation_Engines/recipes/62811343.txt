Title:
Mom's Hearty Cabbage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium Cabbage chopped
1 Medium Onion diced
3 Tomatoes diced (or 1 can diced tomatoes)
2 Stalks Celery diced
4 Chopped Potatoes (optional)
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce to taste
2 Slices Dried Wheat Bread diced
1 Tablespoon Toasted Sesame Seeds
1/2 Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
Salt to taste
6 Cups Water

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot and saute Onion, Celery, and Sesame Seeds over medium heat until Onion is soft. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alicia Taylor
Description: 
This is a great vegetarian/vegan dish for cold winter days. Can be made on stove or in slow cooker.
