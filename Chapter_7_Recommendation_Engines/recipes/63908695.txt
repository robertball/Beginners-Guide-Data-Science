Title:
Balsamic pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pork chops
1 tsp olive oil
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
2 tsp soy sauce
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
2 tbsp garlic
2 tbsp Dijon mustard

Directions:
Mix together and pour over meat and let marinade at least 2 hours. We usually do it much longer.
Grill on medium-high heat.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Murray
