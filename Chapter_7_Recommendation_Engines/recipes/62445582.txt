Title:
Baked Oatmeal Carole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups rolled oats
1/2 Splenda cup brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1/2 cup melted butter
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3/4 cup dried raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt. Stir in milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir in raisins. Spread into a 9x9 inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
