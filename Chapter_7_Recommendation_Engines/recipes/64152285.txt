Title:
Shrimp mould Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of small shrimp
1 - 8 oz pkg (large pkg) od phili cream cheese
1 can of campbells - ironic - cream of tomato soup
i spanish onion
1 celery stalk
2 pkgs of davis gelatin

Directions:
chop up celery and onions, each about 1/2 cup - use mini chopper
mash up shrimp
melt cheese and tomato soup - no water
stir over low heat until pink, beat until not lumpy
cool
mix 2 pkgs of david gelatin in 1/2 cup cold water
add to tomato soup mixture with onions/celery/shrimp
set in container you want to use for presentation - set in fridge
pour hot water into sink - immerse mould food side up in water for 30 seconds
put presentation plate on top, secure and flip
