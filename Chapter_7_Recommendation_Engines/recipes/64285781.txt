Title:
Black Eyed Pea Cakes (Cooking Light) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4C uncooked quinoa
1/2C veg broth
2C black eyed peas, rinsed, drained and divided
2 garlic cloves chopped
1/2C panko
1/2C red onion, finely chopped
1&1/2tsp ground cumin
1/2tsp black pepper
1/4tsp salt
1/8tsp ground red pepper
2 large eggs, slightly beaten
1Tbsp olive oil divided

Directions:
1. Combine broth and quinoa, bring to boil.  Cover, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.  Remove from heat and fluff with fork.
2. In food processor combine 1 Cup of black eyed peas and garlic.  Puree to a thick paste.
3. Combine pea paste with rest of peas, panko, and next 6 ingredients (through eggs) in large bowl. Stir in quinoa.
4. Divide into 8 portions and shape into a patty.
5. Add 1.5tsp oil to non stick skillet and cook 4 minutes per side.
