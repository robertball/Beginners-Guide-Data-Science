Title:
Godiva Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976920.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Godiva® Original Liqueur
1 ounce Baileys® Irish Cream
2 ounces cream

Directions:
Pour Godiva(R) Original Liqueur and Baileys(R) Irish Cream  into an ice-filled wine glass, stir, and top with cream to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add a touch of luxury to your drink with the velvety taste of Godiva® liqueur in Original Chocolate, White Chocolate, Cappuccino or Chocolate Cream.
