Title:
Noel's Famous Fruit Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small cantelope
1 small honeydew melon
Cilantro to taste
1/4 cup pickled jalepenos diced(like you put on nachos)
2 16oz cans of pinapple tidbits
juice from one can of the pinapple

Directions:
Cut the melon into salsa size pieces,
dice the jalepeno,chop the cilantro, and combine in a large bowl with the pinapple and juice. You can use a little juice from the jalepenos if you want to kick it up a little. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours stirring occasionally. Serve with salted tortilla chips. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
NOELLE
Description: 
Healthy and simple.I don't usually like fruit other than in fruit salad but this is an Awsome recipe. I haven't met anyone that doesn't love it. Give it a try you won't be disappointed. This is great for summer BBQ's.
