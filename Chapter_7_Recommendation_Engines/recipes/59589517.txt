Title:
Budget Brown Sugar Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277728.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar BBQ sauce
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1/4 cup milk
1 eggs
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup french fried onions
3/4 cup Italian bread crumbs
1/3 cup ketchup
1/4 cup brown sugar BBQ sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
In a bowl place ground beef, 1/2 cup BBQ sauce, bread crumbs, salt and pepper, garlic powder, french friend onions, milk, and egg. Fold together with hands until well combined, being careful not to over-mix. Place into prepared loaf pan and pack down, removing any air gaps.
In a small bowl mix together the ketchup and remaining 1/4 cup brown sugar BBQ sauce.  Spread over top of prepared meatloaf.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
Instead of using brown sugar in this meatloaf I used a brown sugar BBQ sauce to replace the ketchup and brown sugar.  Also cut back on the amount of milk and breadcrumbs and added a few french fried onions for a little texture.Modified from: Brown Sugar Meatloaf
