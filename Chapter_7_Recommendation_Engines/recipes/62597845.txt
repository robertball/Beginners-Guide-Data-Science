Title:
Bread Machine French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup water (80 degrees)
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
3 1/2 cup bread flour
1 tablespoon active dry yeast
Glaze:
2 tablespoons water
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into bread machine and set to kneed only.
Roll dough into rectangle. Starting at long end and roll tightly. Seal seams and ends. Let rise for 40 minutes ( or until doubled). Make three slashes on top of bread log.
Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
Add glaze after bread is removed from oven.
