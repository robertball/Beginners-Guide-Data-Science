Title:
Beef Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped scallions
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 tsp. butter
2 tbsp. soy sauce
3/4 cup sherry or dry red wine

Directions:
Saute scallions in butter then add remaining ingredients.
Simmer a few minutes then add any pan drippings you have.
