Title:
Hurricane Jessica Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1126141.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger passion fruit-flavored rum
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger mango-flavored rum
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger peach schnapps
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger whipped cream-flavored vodka
1 splash lime juice
1 splash grenadine syrup
1 splash seltzer water, or to taste
2 frozen mango chunks, or to taste
2 maraschino cherries, or to taste
1/4 cup ice cubes, or as desired

Directions:
Pour passion fruit-flavored rum, mango-flavored rum, peach schnapps, and whipped cream flavored vodka into a large glass; add lime juice and grenadine syrup. Float seltzer water atop the cocktail; garnish with mango and maraschino cherries. Add ice to cocktail as desired.
