Title:
Simple Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6201299.jpg
Ingredients:
refrigerated  pie crust like Pillsbury or store brand, Cambells Chicken Pot Pie soup , butter spray

Directions:
Butter spray baking pan with butter spray til well coated, then lay first pie crust on bottom of pan til flat and touches corners of pan, then fill with 1 can of Cambell's chicken pot pie soup or you can use chicken soup and then mixed canned vegetables, then top with 2nd pie crust , close together ends of pie crust and poke holes with fork in it place in oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes until brown and crust is done
