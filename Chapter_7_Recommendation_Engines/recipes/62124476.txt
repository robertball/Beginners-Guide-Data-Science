Title:
Beef & veggie skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef (prepared for Bolognese sauce if available)
1 onion, chopped
1 large zucchini, thinly sliced
3 small tomatoes, chopped
garlic powder, to taste
italian seasonings, to taste
salt, to taste
preferred oil (olive or vegetable oil)

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat the oil, add the onion and zucchini and salt, saut√© until the zucchini starts to become translucent.
When the zucchini becomes soft, make a space in the center of the skillet and place the meat inside.
Sprinkle the garlic powder and italian seasonings on the meat and mix until the meat is browned.
Add the tomatoes and mix everything together.
Cover and cook until the tomatoes have broken down into sauce.
Serve with crispy bread and a salad.
