Title:
Tilapia with Creamy Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
cooking spray
1 tablespoon margarine, melted
1 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill weed, or two teaspoons dried dill.
1 teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
1/4 teaspoon granulated garlic....whisk together.
2 tablespoons cream cheese
4 tablespoons lemon juice, or to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill weed or 3 teaspoon dried dill.
1/2 cup sour cream, whisked in.

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a baking sheet with cooking spray. Place the tilapia fillets on the sheet.  Mix the butter(margarine)lemon juice, fresh dill, and lemon-pepper seasoning together and brush on.
Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily.
While the fish is baking, combine the cream cheese, 4 tablespoons of lemon juice and 2 tablespoons of dill in a small bowl. Heat in the microwave for 1 minute and 10 seconds, then stir to blend and whisk in the 1/2 cup of sour cream.
Place fillets onto heated serving plates, and spoon sauce around them in a decorative fashion.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth Duckworth
Description: 
This dish dish is economy friendly. The fish is available just about everywhere.  I call it the Tofu fish as it seems to take on the flavor of what ever you put on it, which makes it one of the most versatile of fish and an economical wonder.Modified from: Tilapia with Creamy Sauce
