Title:
Maple-Orange Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519663.jpg
Ingredients:
9-inch prepared pie crust (thawed if frozen)
1 c. packed light-brown sugar
3 large eggs
½ c. maple syrup
2 tbsp. molasses
1 tbsp. freshly grated orange zest
1 tbsp. orange-flavored liqueur
1 tsp. white vinegar
¼ tsp. salt
2 c. shelled pecan halves toasted and cooled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Whisk sugar, eggs, maple syrup, molasses, orange zest, liqueur, vinegar and salt in large bowl.  Stir in pecans and pour into the pie shell.  Place pie on a rimmed baking sheet and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the filling is set in the center.  Cool completely before cutting.
