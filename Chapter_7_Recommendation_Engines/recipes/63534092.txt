Title:
Marsha's Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 Tbsp. oil
10-oz can enchilada sauce
8-oz can no-salt added tomato sauce
salt to taste
pepper to taste
8 corn tortilla shells
3 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
15-oz can ranch-style beans, drained
11-oz can Mexicorn, drained
4 ozs. reduced-fat cheddar cheese, grated
8-oz can of french style green beans, drained

Directions:
1. Saute onion and garlic in oil in saucepan. Stir in enchilada sauce and tomato sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Place two tortillas in bottom of slow cooker. Layer one-third of chicken on top. Top with one-third sauce mixture, one-third beans, one-third corn, one-third cheese, and one-third french style grean beans. Repeat layers 2 more times. Top with 2 tortillas.
3. Cover. Cook on Low 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Beth Reitberg Rossignol
Description: 
This is from the book FIX It and FORGET IT Diabetic Cookbook

It calls for a 2 1/4-oz. can of sliced black olives, drained but 
I substituted French style green beans. You can also substitute 1 lb. cooked and drained hamburger for the chicken.
