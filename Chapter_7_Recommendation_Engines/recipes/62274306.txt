Title:
Dutch Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/347395.jpg
Ingredients:
5 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced
1 cup white sugar, divided
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, divided
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup butter
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Place apples in a large mixing bowl.
Add 1/2 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons flour, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg, 2 teaspoons lemon juice and 1/4 teaspoon lemon peel.  Toss with apples until evenly coated.
In a medium bowl, cut 1/2 cup sugar, 1 cup flour, 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon salt together. Top pie with the mixture.
Place on a baking sheet, and bake in preheated oven for 40-50 minutes, or until topping is golden brown. Remove from oven, and cool pie on wire rack.
