Title:
Dijon Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 medium red-skinned potatoes, unpeeled
1 tbsp dijon mustard
2 tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp rosemary
salt
pepper

Directions:
Chop potatoes in 1 inch pieces. Toss in rest of ingredients and roast in greased pan at 375 degrees for 40-45 mins, turning occasionally. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
France C
Description: 
The dijon provides a slight tang that really kicks these potatoes to the next level! These are a family favorite at our house.
