Title:
Chess Pie Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For bottom crust:
1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup soft butter
3 tablespoons white sugar
For the chess filling:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon all purpose flour
3 eggs, beaten
3 tablespoons melted butter
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix  flour, soft nutter and white sugar.  Put in the bottom of an 8x8 greased pan to form pie crust.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
Beat eggs in one bowl.
Then, mix brown sugar, white sugar and flour in another bowl.  Add to beaten eggs.  Add melted butter and vanilla.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until done.
Yields 2 dozen 1 1/2 inch squares.
