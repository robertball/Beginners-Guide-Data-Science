Title:
Baileys Holiday Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990274.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Original Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1/4 ounce Godiva® Milk Chocolate Liqueur
Raspberry for garnish

Directions:
Add Baileys Original Irish Cream, Smirnoff No.21(TM) Vodka, and Godiva Original Liqueur.
Shake with ice and pour into martini glass.
Garnish with raspberry.

Submitter: 
Baileys®
Description: 
Baileys® Original Irish Cream, Smirnoff® No.21™ Vodka, and a hint of Godiva® Chocolate garnished with a fresh raspberry make a festive holiday martini.
