Title:
Raw 'Milano' Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of Cashews (soaked for 4 hours, then drained)
1/4 cup of raw organic honey
2 oranges, juiced
1/2 cup raw unsweetened shredded coconut
1/2 Tbs vanilla powder
sea salt to taste
Chocolate filling:
1 cup dates, pitted
1/4 cup raw cacao powder
1/4 cup cold-pressed coconut oil
3/4 cup water

Directions:
1. Blend the ingredients in your food processor until smooth.
2. Drop by using a Tbs of batter onto teflex sheets for your dehydrator.
3. Spread cookies into small oval shapes.
4. Dehydrate at 115 for 10-12 hours (flipping them half-way through). They should be dry but chewy.
5. In a blender, blend the ingredients for the icing for a looooong time. Start slow and work your way up.
6. Use icing between two cookies.
7. Enjoy your raw treat!

Description: 
Raw cashews are the base for these wonderful un-cooked 'Milano' cookies.
