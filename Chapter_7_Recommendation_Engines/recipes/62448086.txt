Title:
Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462738.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless and skinless chicken strips
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions:
1.	Coat chicken all over with Dijon mustard. Place Parmesan in a shallow dish or plastic bag, add chicken and turn (or shake bag) to coat.
2.	Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook 2-5 minutes per side, until cooked through.
