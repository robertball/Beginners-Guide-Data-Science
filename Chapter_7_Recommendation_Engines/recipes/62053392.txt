Title:
Mother's Spanish Pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops,
3 large green bell peppers,
6 cups rice,
2 small onions,
1 can tomatoes,
4 tsp butter,
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
Prepare rice as directed on package and set aside.  Brown pork chops in butter.  Cut green peppers into one inch circles; 1 per chop.  Place pork chops into a greased 9x12 casserole dish.  Put one bell pepper circle over each pork chop.  Fill peppers with rice, and put remainder of rice in between chops and peppers.  Cut onions in slices, and place one on top of each pepper.  Put remaining onion over rice in between chops.  Pour tomatoes over all.  Bake covered for 45 minutes at 350 degrees until vegetables are tender.

Rhonda Brock Fuller

A nice Sunday Dinner instead of the ordinary chicken.
