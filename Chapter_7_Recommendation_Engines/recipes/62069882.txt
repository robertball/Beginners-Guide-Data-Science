Title:
Easiest Meatballs Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 C. crushed seasoned croutons, any flavor
1 T. flour
1/2 C. and 1/4 C. milk
2 beef boullion cubes

Directions:
1. With hands, mix ground beef and crouton crumbs; roll into walnut-sized balls.
2. Cook over medium heat until thoroughly browned, rolling frequently to brown all sides.
3. Drop boullion cubes into milk in microwaveable container; heat for 30-45 seconds to disslove cubes. Stir in flour. Slowly add mixture to meatball pan (with meatballs still in it) stiring constantly. Add remaining milk, if necessary.

Submitter: 
PETBABYLAMBS
Description: 
This is a very easy basic recipe that I came up with in the middle of a snowstorm when the cupboards were nearly bare. It is wonderfully soothing served over mashed potatoes. Great comfort food!
