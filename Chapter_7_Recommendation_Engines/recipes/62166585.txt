Title:
Authentic Hot German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 6 Red Potatoes, cut and cooked with skins on
5 slices Bacon, sliced into small pieces
1 cup Chopped Onions
3 TB Flour
1 TS Salt
1/2 TS Ground Black Pepper
2/3 cup Sugar
2/3 cup Cider Vinegar
1 1/2 cup Water
1/3 cup Parsely
2 TS Celery Seed (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Fry the bacon in the pan until crispy.
Remove the bacon and reserve 1/4 cup of the fat.
In the remaining fat add the chopped onion and cook for 4 - 6 minutes.
Add the flour and pepper and cook for two minutes.
Add the cider vinegar, sugar, and water, and whisk until it comes to a boil.  Boil for 1 additional minute.
Add the Parsely, Celery Seeds and Bacon and combine.  Remove from heat.
Put potatoes into a casserole dish or a 13" x 8" pan, and pour the reserved oil on top of them.  Add the hot dressing and mix gently.
Bake for 45 minutes!

Submitter: 
HABERFAMILY
Description: 
Straight from my mothers mother, here is the hot german potato salad to top them all.  A perfect blend of sweet/sour, and goes very well with any kind of dish!!
