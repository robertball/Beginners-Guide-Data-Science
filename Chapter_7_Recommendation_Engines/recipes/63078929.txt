Title:
Raw Energy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup whole dates. Chopped
1/2 cup raisins, dried cranberries or dried cherries
2/3 cup almonds or walnuts
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Lay out 6 pieces of plastic wrap, about the size of a sandwhich bag.
In a food processor, grind dried dates,  fried fruits and cinnamon into a fine paste.  Scoop paste into a bowl. No need to clean processor bowl, add nuts. Grind to a fine texture, add to fruit paste and with clean hands mix well.  Potion into 6 equal portions, shape into bars and wrap one bar in each piece of plastic wrap. Store in refrigerator until ready to serve.
