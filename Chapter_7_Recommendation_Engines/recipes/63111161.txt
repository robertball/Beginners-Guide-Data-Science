Title:
Toasted Coconut and Pineapple Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1.5 cups shredded coconut, toasted (sweetened and unsweetened both work)
1 cup Quinoa
1 6oz can crushed pineapple
1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
Sea salt
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Spread coconut out on cookie sheet and bake in the oven for approximately 5-10 minutes until golden brown and toasted. Cook quinoa according to package directions. Add crushed pineapple,3/4 of toasted coconut and oil; then add sea salt and pepper to taste. Mix well and sprinkle with remaining toasted coconut to serve.
