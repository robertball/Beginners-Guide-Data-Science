Title:
Got a Craving ? Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/497563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter melted
1 1/2 cups of graham wafer crumbs
1 300 ml can of eagle brand sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
3/4 cups glazed marichino cherries cut in half
1/2 cup roughly chopped salted cashews
1/2 cup fine sweetened coconut
4 tbsp butter
200 gram package of chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt butter and stir in graham wafer crumbs.  Press into 9" square pan to make base.  Scatter cherries, coconut and cashews over the base evenly.  Cream together eagle brand milk and peanut butter with a spoon and pour evenly over the squares.  Bake at 350 for 25 minutes and set aside.  Melt butter and chocolate chips in a small pan, stirring constantly.  When melted, pour evenly over squares.  Once the pan is cool enough, chill in fridge.
