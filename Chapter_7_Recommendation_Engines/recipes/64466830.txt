Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2444152.jpg
Ingredients:
Dough:
250g  Flour
70g   Sugar Powder
100g  powdered Almond
200g  Butter
Overlay:
100g  Sugar Powder
2 Tablespoon Sugar

Directions:
Mix all the Dough ingredient to a smooth dough.
Put dough 1 hour in the refrigerator.
Meanwhile preheating the oven on 200Â°C.
Form little moons with the dough by hand.
Bake the moons 15 minutes in the oven.
Meanwhile mix the ingredient for the overlay in a bowl.
Put the warm cookies in the overlay and roll them until they are covered with sugar.
Let the cookies get cold and Dinomite's Fluffy Bites are ready. So fluffy he can't get enough of it.
