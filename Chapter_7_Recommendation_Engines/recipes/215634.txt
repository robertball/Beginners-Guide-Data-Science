Title:
Mmmm Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639568.jpg
Ingredients:
2 mangos (preferably honey mangos) - peeled, seeded, and diced
2 large roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and diced
2 large cloves garlic, minced
1 shallot, minced
1 green chile pepper, seeded and diced
1 lime, juiced
3 tablespoons pineapple juice
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Stir the mangos, tomatoes, garlic, shallot, green chile pepper, lime juice, pineapple juice, olive oil, and salt together in a bowl; refrigerate 1 hour to allow the flavors to mingle before serving.

Submitter: 
Patricia
Description: 
There's nothing so good and refreshing as mango salsa. I serve mine with tortilla chips or just eat it by itself as a side dish.
