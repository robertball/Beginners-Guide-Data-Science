Title:
Cinnamon Spiced Drizzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283933.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup powdered sugar
2 Tbls. 100% pure maple syrup
1/4 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 tsp. cinnamon powder
1/4 tsp. ground cloves

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Drizzle on cooled cookies or cake.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
This is a unique drizzle because it doesn't have a heavy powdered sugar taste. I love it on apple cookies or apple cakes!
