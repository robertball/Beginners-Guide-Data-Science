Title:
Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
·1 Cup of shredded Swiss cheese
·2 Eggs (Large)
·1 can flaky Hungry Jack (or other similar)  Biscuits (8-10)
·2 TBL spoons milk
·½ lb of bacon or crumbled sausage cooked
·1 bunch of green onions (cut into small pieces)
·8 oz Cream Cheese (brick not whipped) leave out to get soft
·Dash of Tabasco

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Mix ingredients well (blender or beater)
Spray muffin pan with Pam
Place biscuits in pan (be careful to remove air pockets)
Spoon mixture over muffins
Bake 20 minutes,  watch after 15

Submitter: 
Stephanie
Description: 
Got this from my dad of all places. Easily refrigerated and reheated.
