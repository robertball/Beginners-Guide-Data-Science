Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
125g melted butter
50ml milk
60g light brown sugar
40g white sugar
1 egg
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
200g self-raising flour
170g chocolate chips

Directions:
Blend the melted butter, milk, brown sugar and white sugar together.
Add in the egg and continue blending until smooth.
Blend in the vanilla extract.
Fold in the self-raising flour until batter is thick and gooey.
Fold in the chocolate chips.
Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheets or a non-stick tray. Make sure that they are well-spaced as the batter will expand quite a bit.
Bake at 175 degrees Celsius for 16 minutes. Remove and let cool.

Submitter: 
Jubilee
Description: 
Reduced sugar chocolate chip cookies for a healthier but no less delicious alternative.
