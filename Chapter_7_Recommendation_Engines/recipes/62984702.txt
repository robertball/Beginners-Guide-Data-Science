Title:
Creamy Corn with Bacon and Thyme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cream
1 (2 lb) package frozen corn, thawed
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
6 slices pre-cooked bacon
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp flour
1 c milk
2 tsp sugar
2 springs fresh chopped thyme
1/4 cup grated parmesan
s&p;, to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, heat corn and cream until hot.
In a large skillet, crisp bacon. Remove from skillet and cool on paper towels.
In the same skillet, melt butter and whisk in flour. Cook 2 minutes over medium-low heat. Slowly whisk in milk, stirring constantly. Add thyme and sugar.
Stir in corn mixture. Cook 5 minutes, or until thickened and bubbly. Remove from heat. Add parmesan cheese and season to taste with S&P.
