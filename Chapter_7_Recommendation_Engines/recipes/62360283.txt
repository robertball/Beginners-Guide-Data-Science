Title:
Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
oil for frying
3/4 pound cubed skinless, boneless chicken breast meat
chopped onion
garlic
i can green chilis
squirt of fresh lime juice
1/2 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (12oz) bag frozen southwestern corn
1 cup salsa
water as needed
1 cup shredded Mexican-style cheese
1 1/2 cups crushed plain tortilla chips

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium high heat, saute chicken in oil until cooked through and no longer pink inside, set aside. Saute onion and garlic until onion is partially soft.  Add taco seasoning, beans, corn, salsa and a little water to prevent drying out. Cover skillet and simmer over medium low heat for 10 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Transfer chicken mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with 1/2 cup of the cheese and crushed tortilla chips.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Add remaining 1/2 cup cheese and bake until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Eileen Jones English
Description: 
Chicken breast simmered with a spicy black bean and corn mixture, then topped with cheese and tortilla chip crumbs and baked. Easy and quick to fix weekday casserole with a Mexican flair.  Nutritious and kid friendly.Modified from: Mexican Casserole
