Title:
Ham & Gouda Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4023545.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 small onion, thinly sliced
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 (10 inch) flour tortilla
2 slices deli ham
8 dill pickle rounds
1/2 cup Borden® Gouda Shreds

Directions:
Spread mustard on one half of tortilla.
Layer with ham, caramelized onion, pickles and Gouda cheese; fold tortilla in half.
Cook in a dry skillet over medium high heat until browned on both sides and cheese is melted.
Cut into four pieces.

Submitter: 
Borden® Cheese
Description: 
Ham slices, shredded Gouda cheese with dill pickles and a dash of mustard make a delicious quesadilla.
