Title:
Instant Pot® No Stress Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5726664.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts (7-8 oz. each frozen) *
1 cup chicken broth
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 cup Sriracha or hot sauce of choice
1 tablespoon chili garlic sauce, optional
1/2 cup ranch style dressing
2 cups cheddar cheese (medium or mild), shredded
1 green onion, thinly sliced

Directions:
Turn on a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot®), add frozen chicken and 1 cup of chicken broth. Close, lock the lid and seal the vent. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 10 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
When cooking time is up turn off pressure cooker and allow for 10 minute natural pressure release according to manufacturer's instructions. Unlock and remove the lid, remove the chicken and shred.
Turn on the pressure cooker and select saute low. Add the cream cheese, hot sauces, ranch dressing and chicken. Slowly add the cheddar cheese, constantly stirring  until cheese is melted and the ingredients are well combined. Set the pressure cooker to the keep warm function until ready to serve.
Garnish with green onions and serve with cracker, chips or veggies.
*You can cook thawed chicken breasts by setting the time for 15 minutes high pressure and a quick release.

Description: 
Want to make buffalo chicken dip and all you have is frozen chicken? No stress, just cook the frozen chicken breasts in a pressure cooker such as an Instant Pot®, start to finish in 30 mins. Instructions for thawed chicken are included.
