Title:
Blazer's Creamy Chocolate Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319205.jpg
Ingredients:
5 pkgs. Chocolate pudding(cook & serve)NOT INSTANT
6 cans evaporated milk
8 oz. milk(I use this because I don't want to waste part of an evaporated milk can
2 pie crusts

Directions:
Bake pie crusts.
In a large pot, put all the milk in and add pkgs. of chocolate pudding. Stir constantly until it reaches a full boil. Pour into pre baked pie crusts. Cool, then refrigerate. Serve with whipped cream.
