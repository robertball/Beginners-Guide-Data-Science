Title:
All Day Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup tomato paste
2 1/2 cups crushed tomatoes (we like Furman's tomatoes in the large can; 3 cans)
3 carrots peeled
1 large onion peeled and halfed
1/2 teaspoon pepper
8 tablespoons butter, not margarine or spread!
1 tablespoon fresh basil
2 tablespoons fresh garlic, minced
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
1. Heat oil in large sauce pan.
2. Place carrots and onions in food processer and chop finely
3. Stir in garlic, tomato paste, carrot & onion mix, pepper and basil. Simmer for 30 minutes.
4. Add tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes. If sauce becomes too thick, add a little water slowly.
5. Stir in butter and salt to taste. Let simmer for 3 to 4 hours on extreme low, stirring often.
6. Serve with favorite pasta. YUM!
