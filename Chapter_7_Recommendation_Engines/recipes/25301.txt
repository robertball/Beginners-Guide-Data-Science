Title:
Laura's Lush Pasta Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4416807.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
2 teaspoons olive oil
1/2 pound lean steak, cut into strips
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
5 fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 tomato, chopped
1/4 onion, chopped

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.  Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Saute steak strips for 10 minutes.  Stir in green and red bell pepper, mushrooms, tomato and onion.  Cook for 10 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.  Serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
Laura Smith
Description: 
Pasta mixed with peppers, steak chunks, mushrooms, onions and tomato, topped with a sauce of your choice!
