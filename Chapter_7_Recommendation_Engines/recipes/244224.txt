Title:
Healthy Cocoa, Banana, PB Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2318489.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1/2 chopped frozen banana, or more to taste
2 tablespoons peanut butter
2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
Blend milk, banana, peanut butter, cocoa powder, and honey together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Dr. Cora Rivard
Description: 
This recipe is ideal for a post-workout protein drink, or afternoon pick-me-up. No supplemental protein mix needed!
