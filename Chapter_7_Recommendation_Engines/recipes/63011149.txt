Title:
Grandma's Crumb Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup flour

Directions:
Use a pastry blender or the whisk attachment on your mixer and blend until it looks like large peas.
Cover pie with topping and bake as directed by pie recipe.

Submitter: 
Noel Keen
Description: 
This is absolutely the best crumb topping for fruit pie.  It's super simple with super yummy results.
