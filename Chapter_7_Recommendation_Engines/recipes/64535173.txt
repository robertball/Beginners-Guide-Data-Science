Title:
Peanut Butter Banana Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana
½ cup natural peanut butter
¼ tsp baking soda
Pinch of salt
1 egg
3 Tbl honey
½ Tbl real, vanilla extract
½ cup of mini chocolate chips
Oil for greasing muffin tins

Directions:
Grease 1.5 mini muffin tins. Preheat oven to 400.
Put all but chocolate chips in to a blender or food processor and process until smooth. Add all but 2 Tbls chocolate chips and stir the mixture with a spoon (do not turn on the machine at this point).
Fill muffin tins to ¾ full. Top with remaining chocolate chips. Bake in preheated oven for 7-9 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Approximately 10 minutes after removing from the oven, pop them out of the muffin tins. You may need to gently run a butter knife around the tins to remove them.
