Title:
Mom's Moist Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Lbs ground turkey 7% lean
4 eggs or whites or equivalent egg substitute
1 1/2 C seasoned bread crumbs
2 C water
14 twists pepper
2 tsp salt
1 Tbs dill (optional)
1 handful chopped parsley (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 400
Line a loaf pan with 2 sheets of foil criss crossing with long overhangs on each side
Spray foil with non stick cooking spray
Combine all ingredients and put into the lined loaf pan
Spray meat with cooking spray to prevent it from sticking to the foil when done
Place the loaf pan into a larger roasting pan with about 1" of water
Bake in the water bath at 400 for about 2 hours
Let sit 15 minutes before removing from the pan
Serve with your choice of mushroom gravy, ketchup, BBQ sauce, or without any condiment.
