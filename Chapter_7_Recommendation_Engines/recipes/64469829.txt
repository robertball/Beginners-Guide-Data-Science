Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2461649.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups (12 oz) Chocolate chips
2 cups (12 oz) Butterscotch chips
1/2 cup Peanut Butter
3 cups (10 oz) Mini-Marshmallow
1 cup peanuts

Directions:
In a large Microwavable bowl, combine chocolate chips, butterscotch chips and peanut butter.
Heat for 1 minute and stir. Continue in 30 second increments until chocolate and peanut butter are thoroughly mixed. Please Note: Butterscotch chips will NOT completely melt. DO NOT worry about that, it only adds to the great taste!
Stir in mini-marshmallows and peanuts.
Drop by teaspoon into candy cups and let harden.
