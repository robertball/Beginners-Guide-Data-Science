Title:
Grandma Huggins' Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup shortening (or butter)
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup sour milk
1/2 chopped walnuts
3 ripe bananas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°
Grease and flour loaf pan.
Cream sugar and butter; add eggs and mashed bananas.
In a separate bowl, mix dry ingredients; add to banana mixture, alternating with milk while beating with mixer.
Pour into prepared pan and bake 40 minutes or until knife inserted comes out clean.
