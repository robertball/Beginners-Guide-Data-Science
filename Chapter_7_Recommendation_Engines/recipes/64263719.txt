Title:
Caramel Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (12 count) Rhodes frozen cinnamon rolls
1 c brown sugar
1 c chopped pecans (optional)
1 c whipping cream

Directions:
Spread brown sugar on bottom of 9"x13" baking pan, sprinkle nuts on sugar.
Arrange frozen rolls on sugar and nut mixture.
Cover with a towel and let raise overnight in oven (with light on for a little extra warmth).
In the morning, remove from oven and drizzle whipping cream over the top.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
Remove from oven and flip over onto larger serving tray or pan.
