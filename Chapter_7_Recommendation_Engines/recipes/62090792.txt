Title:
Avocado and Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 avocados, chopped
1 small red pepper, choppped
2 cups canned yellow corn, drained
2-3 shallots, chopped
1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
juice from 3 fresh limes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Gently mix all ingredients together in a bowl and salt and pepper to taste.  Serve immediately with favorite tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Irish916
Description: 
Super easy and very fresh summer salsa using avocado, corn, red pepper, shallots, cilantro and lime juice.
