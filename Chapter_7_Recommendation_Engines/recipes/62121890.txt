Title:
Bacon Cheeseburger Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1 small onion - diced
1 medium green pepper - chopped
4 slices thick bacon - chopped
2 cloves garlic - chopped
1 package fresh mushrooms
1 14 oz can diced tomatoes undrained
1 6 oz can  tomato paste
1/2 cup water
4 tbls barbacue sauce (Sweet Baby Rays)
1 can whole kernal corn
2 tsp of Italian Seasoning.
6 oz Valveeta Cheese
1/2 cup of milk
1 Cup Cheddar Cheese
1 lb Elbo Macaroni or Medium Shells

Directions:
Brown hamburger, bacon, onion, green pepper in a dutch oven.   Drain grease.
Add mushrooms, garlic & corn.  Cook down for 5 minutes
Mix tomatoes, paste, water, barbacue sauce and italian seasoning in a large bowl.  Add to dutch oven.  Simmer while cooking macaroni in a seperate pot.  Be sure not to OVERCOOK macaroni.Should be firm when drained.
Add Valveeta and milk to cooked and drained macaroni.  Simmer till melted and thickened.
Fold Macaroni & Cheese into dutch oven and spread Cheddar Cheese over the top.  Cover and cook on low till cheddar is melted or place uncovered in preheated oven at 325 for 15-20 minutes.
Serve with Salad & Garlic Bread.  Freeze leftovers for another meal.

Description: 
A twist on the Goulash Mom made for us as kids.  Hearty & Delicious with only two pans to wash after.
