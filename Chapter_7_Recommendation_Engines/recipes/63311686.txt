Title:
Beef Barley Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1/2 cup barley
1 bay leaf
2 tablespoons oil
3 carrots, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
4 cups beef broth
1 tablespoon Worcestershire
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (28 ounce) can chopped stewed tomatoes
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Brown beef in skillet, place in slow cooker liner. Add barley and bay leaf. Saute carrots, celery, and onion until tender. Add mixed veggies, broth, Worcestershire, sugar, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, chopped stewed tomatoes, and seasonings. Cook on Low 6 to 8 hours.  Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
