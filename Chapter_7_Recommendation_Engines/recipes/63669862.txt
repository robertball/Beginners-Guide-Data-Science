Title:
Susan's Pizza Dough! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/906923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast, prefer highly active
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
More olive oil

Directions:
Combine yeast, sugar, and water in a large bowl.  Set aside for 10-15 minutes.  Add flour and salt until dough forms a ball. Coat with olive oil.  Let rest in a bowl, covered with a dish towel, in a warm place for 15 minutes.
Roll out and place on a large pizza pan.  Brush with olive oil.  Bake at 425 for 10 minutes, then add sauce and toppings and finish cooking.
Bake at 400 to 425 degrees.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This one is a quick recipe that merely involves mixing a few basic ingredients and rolling out (or patting the dough into the pan). No need to wait for the dough to rise if in a hurry.Modified from: Our Favorite Pizza Dough!
