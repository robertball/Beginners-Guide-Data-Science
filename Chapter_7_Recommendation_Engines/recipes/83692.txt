Title:
Crunchy Chinese Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Chinese cabbage (napa or savoy), shredded
1 cup broccoli, broken into small florets
1/2 cup canned water chestnuts, drained
1/2 cup red radishes, thinly sliced
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
1 cup Chinese noodles, divided
1/4 teaspoon ginger
1/2 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing

Directions:
In mixing bowl, combine first five ingredients.  Add 3/4 cup Chinese noodles and mix lightly.  In small bowl, mix together slaw dressing and ginger; pour over vegetables and toss together.  Garnish with remaining 1/4 cup Chinese noodles.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
This colorful salad is made with fresh Chinese cabbage, broccoli, red radishes tossed with slaw dressing and garnished with Chinese noodles.
