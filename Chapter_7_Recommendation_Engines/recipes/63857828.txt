Title:
Orange Creamsicle Whoopie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box vanilla cake mix
1 cup of orange soda
1 container of cool whip

Directions:
In a bowl mix cake mix and soda together.
Drop into 1 tbs heaps onto a parchment paper lined cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.
Let cool and with a spatula remove from cookie sheet.
On the bottom of one cookie dollop 1-2 tbs of cool whip in the center.
Top with a 2nd cookie.
Store in the refrigerator.
