Title:
Cold Breakfast Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of old fashioned oats (not instant oats)
1/2 cup of skim milk
1/4 cup of raisins
1 tablespoon of flaked coconut
1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Directions:
In a cereal bowl, combine oats, raisins, coconut, and cinnamon together. Pour in milk. Make sure the liquid covers all the dry ingredients. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
windband
Description: 
A refreshing breakfast that wakes you up without making you sweaty on a warm summer morning! Very versatile. You can use dry fruits with the oatmeal to put in the fridge the night before, or add fresh fruits in the morning before serving.
