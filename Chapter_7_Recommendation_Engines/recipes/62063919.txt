Title:
Rebecca's Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium apples, cored, peeled and cut into small pieces
1/2 cup water
1/3 cup brown sugar (3 tbsp. if using white sugar)
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Place apples and water in 2 qt saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat.  Lower heat to medium/low and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Break up apples with spoon or fork as desired. Add remaining ingredients and simmer 5 minutes. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
rebeccalovestocook
Description: 
This applesauce is great and easy to make! You can make it more or less chunky depending on your taste. And don't worry, it's not thin or watered down at all! Does best with Macintosh apples.
