Title:
Cheesy Pasta Love Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz rotelli noodles
4 slices american cheese
1/4 c. parm cheese
1/4 c. milk
8 oz cream cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta until al dente.
Melt cream cheese and american cheese in separate bowl.
Add milk to pasta
Stir in cheese mixture.
Finally slowly add parm cheese.
Add a little more cheese if you like it very cheesey!

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
I wanted to step up the classic southern mac and cheese...my hubby named it! He loved it!!
