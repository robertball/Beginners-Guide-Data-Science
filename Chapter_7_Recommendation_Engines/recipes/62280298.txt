Title:
Pumpkin Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup quick oats
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup chopped nuts
1 3/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup melted butter
1 cup Libby's Solid Pack Pumpkin
3/4 cup undiluted Carnation Evaporated Milk
1 slightly beaten egg
1/3 cup of sugar
1/2 tsp. allspice
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Combine flour, oats, brown sugar, nuts, and 1 tsp. cinnamon. Add butter; mix until crumbly. Press into bottom of 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish. Bake in 350 degree oven 20-25 minutes. Remove from oven. Reduce oven temperature to 325 degrees. Combine remaining ingredients. Pour over crust. Bake in 325 degree oven 25-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely.
