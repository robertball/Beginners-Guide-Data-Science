Title:
Apricot Kielbasa Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. kielbasa, cut into small slices
1 - 12oz. jar apricot preserves
2 T. lemon juice
2 T. Dijon mustard
1/4 t. ground ginger

Directions:
In a saucepan or slow-cooker, combine all ingredients.
Heat throughout, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
An excellent way to prepare kielbasa, the flavors really marry well to create a scrumptious dish.
