Title:
Ridiculously Good Pork Chops and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs of olive oil
5-8 Pork Chops  Bone in
Garlic, chopped
Can of beef broth
Onion soup mix (like Lipton)
1 onion coarsely chopped
5 large carrots  peeled and cut into 1-2 inch pieces
1 large sweet potato  peeled and chunked
1-2 yukon gold potatos  scrubbed and chunked
½ lb of sliced mushrooms
Worcestershire sauce
Red wine
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat skillet add olive oil. Preheat oven to 325
Add chopped garlic sauté for a minute
Add pork chops. Sear pork chops until golden brown on each side – about 3-4 minutes a side
Remove chops
Deglaze skillet using red wine and broth. Scrape bits off bottom of skillet.
Pour the broth and wine into roasting pan. Add onion soup mix and ¼ cup of Worcestershire sauce and stir well
Add chops to the roasting pan
Cook for 2 hours
Add onion, carrots, sweet potatoes, Yukon gold potatoes, and mushrooms to roasting pan. Add salt and pepper to taste
Stir into liquid – if need more liquid add half cup of water, more wine and Worcestershire sauce
Cook for 1 hour and then serve
