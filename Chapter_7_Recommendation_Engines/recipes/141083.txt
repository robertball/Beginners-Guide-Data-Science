Title:
Strawberry Limeade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3580123.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup lime juice
2 cups cold water
1/4 cup white sugar
1 1/2 cups frozen sliced strawberries
5 cubes ice

Directions:
Place lime juice, cold water, sugar, and strawberries in blender and blend on high until smooth. While blender is running, add ice cubes one at a time.
