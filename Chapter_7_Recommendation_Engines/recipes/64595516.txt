Title:
Chocolate Walnut Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine
1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
3 cup walnuts, lightly chopped
1(12oz)bag chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
Butter four muffin pans.
Melt butter in bowl and add sugar.
Add eggs, flour, baking powder, and salt and mix thoroughly.
Mix in walnuts and chocolate chips.
Put one heaping scoop of batter into each muffin tin and divide any batter left into muffin tins.
Put in oven for 15 minutes.
Allow up to 5 minutes to cool.
