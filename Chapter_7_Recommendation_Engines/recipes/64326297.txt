Title:
Lace's Coconut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sifted coconut flour
1/2 cup dried coconut (unsweetened)
1 cup coconut yogurt
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 tsp. baking soda
3 eggs
zest of one lemon
pinch of salt
(Ghirardelli chocolate chips optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease 2 mini loaf pans with butter (pans are approx. 3 1/2 X 6 inches).
Mix together all dry ingredients including zest. Add yogurt and maple syrup and mix to incorporate all ingredients. Add optional chips at this time.  Pour or spoon into prepared mini loaf pans.  Bake for 25 minutes.  Cover with foil and bake another 5 minutes.  Remove from oven and allow to cool slightly before removing foil and turning out onto cooling rack.  Cool before slicing.

Submitter: 
Lace
Description: 
This coconut bread is somewhat cake-like. It's quick, easy, paleo friendly and never fails to satisfy. Adding chocolate chips turns it into a decadent treat.
