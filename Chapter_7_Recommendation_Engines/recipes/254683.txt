Ingredients:
6 ice cubes
1 (6 ounce) can orange juice concentrate
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup table cream (optional)
1 banana
1/3 cup white sugar
egg (optional)
1 tablespoon powdered dairy creamer
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place ice cubes in a blender; pulse until crushed to snow-like consistency. Add orange juice concentrate, milk, table cream, banana, sugar, egg, powdered creamer, and vanilla extract. Blend on high speed until smooth, about 1 minute.
