Title:
chicken soup with a kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg lipton chicken noodle soup (prepared according to pkg)
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 cup cubed tomatoes
1-2 cups bite sized tortilla chips (I have used leftover taco shells that I browned in oven first and then crushed)

Directions:
Serve the soup in to bowls. Add the cheese. Let melt for about 30 seconds. Add equal amounts of the tomatoes and then chips. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
BusyBeeTami
Description: 
This is an easy recipe, that is great for someone who has to eat soup due to illness, or for the kids.
