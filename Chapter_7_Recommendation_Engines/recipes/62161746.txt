Title:
Quick and Easy Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. Minute Rice
2 1/4 c. Chicken Broth
1 c. V8 Juice
1 Â½ tsp. Taco Seasoning
1/4 c. chopped onion
2 cloves minced garlic
2 TBSP. Oil

Directions:
In medium size skillet saute onion and garlic in oil.  Add rice and cook slightly.  Add chicken broth, V8 juice and taco seasoning.  Cover and simmer on low about 20 min.
or until the rice is tender not crunchy and liquid is absorbed.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Ruth Schwab
Description: 
This recipe is a simple spanish rice that is quick and easy and tastes delicious.  The V8 juice and the taco seasoning add more flavor to this dish.
