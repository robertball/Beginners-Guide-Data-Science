Title:
Dirty Snowball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970259.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger coffee flavored liqueur
1/3 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger creme de menthe liqueur
1/3 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Irish cream liqueur

Directions:
In a shot glass, combine the coffee liqueur, creme de menthe, and Irish cream liqueur. Drink all at once.

Submitter: 
embrcd
Description: 
Coffee, creme de menthe and Irish cream mingle in this shot that tastes like a chocolate mint candy.
