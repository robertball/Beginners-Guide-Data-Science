Title:
Roasted Sweet Potatoes and Arugula Salad with Balsamic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large sweet potatoes, peeled, chopped into 1 inch cubes
1 large red onion sliced
8-10 large Cremini mushrooms quartered
Olive oil
Salt
Salad:
1 5oz bag arugula
1 avocado (or use goat cheese)
3-4 Green onion chopped
Dressing:
3-4 cloves garlic
1/2 c Combination of balsamic and apple cider vinegar (used more balsamic)
1/2 c olive oil
1 tsp or to taste Dijon mustard
1-2 tsp or to taste honey
Salt

Directions:
toss vegetables with light coating of olive oil and salt
Roast vegetables 30+ minutes at 400 then 450 at end to Caramelize
meanwhile, prepare dressing and salad
blend all dressing ingredients until smooth and salt to taste
Toss greens, potato mixture, balsamic dressing
Could add goat cheese to salad or use goat cheese instead of avocado
