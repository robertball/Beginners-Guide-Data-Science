Title:
Chili Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 # lean ground beef
1 lg. onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 lg. cloves garlic, minced
2 cans chili-style diced tomatoes
2 cans mild or hot chili-style beans
2 T chili powder
1 T ground cumin
8 oz chredded colby, co-jack or cheddar cheese, divided
1 C dry bread crumbs
2 T butter, melted

Directions:
In lg. saucepan, cook beef, onion, green peper, and garlic over med-high heat stirring occ. until meat is browned and vegetables are tender, about 10 min.; drain any fat. Add tomatoes, beans, chili powder, and cumin; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 30 min. Remove from heat; stir in 1 C of shredded cheese.
Place chili in 2 1/2=qt. casserole. In small bowl, combine bread crumbs, butter and remaining cheese; sprinkle over top.
Bake in 400 degree oven until crumb topping is lightly browned, about 12-15 min.
