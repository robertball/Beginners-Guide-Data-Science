Title:
Ashley's Apple Cider Doughnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup apple cider
1 cup granulated sugar
3 1/2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
4 tablespoons butter (at room temperature)
2 eggs
1/2 cup buttermilk
Glaze
1 cup powder sugar
2 tablespoons apple cider

Directions:
Boil apple cider in a small pot until it reduces to 1/4 cup,set aside and allow to cool.With a mixer blend butter,sugar then eggs one at a time until well blended. Add buttermilk and reduced cider.
In a seperate bowl mix together flour,baking power,baking soda,cinnimon,salt and nutmeg.
Slowly add dry ingredients to wet mix just enough to combined. Turn the dough out onto lightly floured parchment paper sprinkle top with flour and roll out to 1/2 in thick. Use more flour if dough becomes too sticky.cut into doughnuts with a 3in doughnut cutter and place onto a sheet pan  and refrigerate for 20-30 mins. Start heating at least 3in of oil to 350 degrees. In a small bowl whisk together apple cider and powder sugar until smooth to make glaze. Cook doughnuts 2-3 at a time approx 1-2 mins each side or until golden brown. watch them close they can burn easily!Remove from oil and place onto paper towels to drain. While still warm pour glaze over doughnuts
