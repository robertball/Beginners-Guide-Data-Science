Title:
Mexican Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Packages Cream Cheese (softened)
1 1/2 Packages Taco Seasoning Mix
1 Small Jar Chopped Green Chilies
2 C Sour Cream
2 C Shredded Cheddar Cheese
3 Egg Yolks

Directions:
Mix and pour into spring-form pan
Bake @ 350 for 30-35 min
Pour Salsa over top.  Serve with Chips
