Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads fresh broccoli, cut into bite-size pieces
1 medium red onion, diced
1 lb. bacon, fried crisp and crumbled
1 cup sliced red grapes
1/2 cup crumbled walnuts
1 1/2 cups mayo
1/2 cup sugar
6 TB. red wine vinegar
1 TB. lemon juice
1/4 t. ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine broccoli, onions, bacon, walnuts and grapes in bowl.  Mix together mayo, sugar, vinegar, and lemon juice. Pour dressing over salad, toss well - refrigerate 3 hours or more before serving.
