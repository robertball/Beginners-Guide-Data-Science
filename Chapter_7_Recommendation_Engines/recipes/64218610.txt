Title:
Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 4 to 5 lb. chicken
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves of garlic, crushed
1 - 14.5 oz. can petite diced tomatoes with jalapenos
1/2 t. chili powder
1/2 t. cumin
salt and pepper to taste
1-2 cups grated cheddar or pepper jack cheese
1/2 bunch of cilantro
2 avocados
1 bag of tortilla chips
Hot sauce,such as Cholula or Tabasco, if desired

Directions:
Cook whole chicken in 6 cups water for approximately 1-1/2 hours or until legs break away and chicken is done. Remove chicken. Cool stock and skim grease. Add onions, garlic, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper. Simmer until hot.  Meantime, cut 2 avocados into chunks, clean and pick 1/2 bunch cilantro and grate 1-2 cups of cheese.  Add chicken to pot and simmer until hot.  To serve, top with avocado, cilantro, shredded cheese, tortilla chips and hot sauce if desired.

Submitter: 
DonnaT
Description: 
If you enjoy chicken tortilla soup, I think you'll like this. Simple, but very tasty!
