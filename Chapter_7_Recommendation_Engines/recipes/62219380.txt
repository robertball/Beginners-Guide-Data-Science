Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, boiled and diced
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup Blue cheese dressing
3/4 cup pepper sauce (such as Frank's Red Hot or to taste
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 bunch celery, cleaned and cut into 4 inch pieces
Crackers
Pita wedges

Directions:
Toss diced chicken and hot sauce in a bowl, add to a large skillet and add cubed cream cheese and blue cheese dressing. Cook, stirring until well blended and warm. Mix in the shredded cheese, and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker, and cook on Low setting until hot and bubbly. Or heat in skillet until bubbly and pour into a serving bowl. Serve with celery sticks and crackers or pita wedges.

Submitter:

Description: 
This tangy, creamy dip tastes just like buffalo chicken wings. It's best served hot with crackers and celery sticks or pita wedges. Everyone loves the results!Modified from: Buffalo Chicken Dip
