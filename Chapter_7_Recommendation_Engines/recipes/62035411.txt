Title:
Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14-oz. can artichokes, drained and chopped
2/3 cup parmesan cheese
2/3 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup half and half
1 Tablespoon chopped pimento
Assorted crackers

Directions:
Mix everything together. Bake at 325ยบ for 25 to 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
Great artichoke dip that is good even after it gets cold!  My family loves this with a variety of different crackers or bagel chips.  If you use a food processor the prepartation time is about 5 minutes.
