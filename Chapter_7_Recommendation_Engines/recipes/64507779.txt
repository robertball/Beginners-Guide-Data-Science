Title:
Zuppa Di Pesce, Cioppino, or Fish Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 large onion, minced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup fresh fennel, diced
1/2 cup carrots, diced
3 ounces shiitake mushrooms, diced
1 cup white grape juice
1/4 cup fresh parsley, minced
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground coriander seed
1 bay leaf
1 cups kale or spinach, rough chopped (or more, to taste)
56 ounces canned diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp lemon zest
1 pinch saffron
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
1.5 lb tilapia or cod fillets (white flesh fish)
1.5 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 lb scallops

Directions:
In a large pot, heat oil and add onion, garlic, celery, fennel, carrots and mushrooms.   Saute until onions are translucent (about 5 minutes).
Add grape juice and herbs.   Boil for 10 minutes.
Add kale, tomatoes, Worcestershire sauce, lemon zest, saffron, salt and pepper.
Add broth to pot and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients and simmer 10-15 more minutes until fish almost flakes apart.
Check for seasoning and add more salt and pepper if needed.
Garnish with fresh parsley and crushed red pepper flakes.
