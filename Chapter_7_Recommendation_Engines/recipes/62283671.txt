Title:
Chocolate Generations VI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Marshmallows (16 oz)
2 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 t. vanilla extract
5 cups strawberries
1/2 box rice Krsispies
3 T. butter

Directions:
In Saucepan, Put butter in, leave till melted, add marshmallows. keep on low.  Until melted.Add Vanilla.. Put rice Krispies in a dish...Smooth in Marshmallows, into the pan with Krispies. leave until firm... meanwhile, melt Chocolate chips..(in microwave) drizzle ontop of the rice krispies...add strawberries when firm..
