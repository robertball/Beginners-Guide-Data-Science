Title:
The Italian pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (pkg refrigerator) crescent rolls
8 oz mozzarella cheese, diced or shredded
1 Cup pepperoni, diced small
1/8 tsp. salt
2-3 Tbsp. Parmesan Cheese
3 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Grease or spray a 9 inch pie place or 8x8 square pan.  Place 5 rolls on the bottom and pat up the sides of pan. Press down with fingertips.
Mix all the remaining ingredients ina large bowl.  Pour mixture into the pan and make sure the pepperoni and cheese is fairly evenly distributed.
Cut the reserved rolls  into strips to form a lattice top.
Refrigerate, covered for 1 hour.
Bake 325 for 40-50 minutes. Use the clean knife test for doness but do not over bake.
Cut into small squares.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Makes a simple but delicious appetizer or serve as a brunch or lunch with a toss salad. A friend and fellow teacher gave this recipe to me about 20 years ago and I have been sharing it ever since. This is very rich and flavorful.
