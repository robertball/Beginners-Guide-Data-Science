Title:
Roasted Garlic Grilled Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977084.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon McCormick® Grill Mates® Roasted Garlic Montreal Chicken Seasoning
2 tablespoons oil
2 cups zucchini, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
2 cups fresh mushrooms
2 cups onion, cut into 1 1/2 -inch pieces
2 cups green bell pepper, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces

Directions:
Cut vegetables into 1 1/2-inch pieces.  Toss together Roasted Garlic Montreal Chicken Seasoning, oil and vegetables.
Place vegetables in grill basket, grill rack or place on skewers and grill directly over medium-hot coals.
Grill, uncovered, 12-15 minutes, or until vegetables are tender, turning occasionally.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Grilled vegetables are the hottest food of the summer!  McCormick® Grill Mates® Roasted Garlic Montreal Chicken Seasoning gives vegetables a robust flavor boost. Good with any of your favorite vegetables; try green beans, yellow squash, shallots, or eggplant.
