Title:
Bacon Cheddar Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For 1 1/2 pound loaf:
1 cup water
1 T dry milk
1 1/2 T margarine or butter
1 T sugar
3/4 t salt
3 cups bread flour
1 1/2 t yeast
2/3 cup shredded cheddar
2/3 cup bacon -- cooked and crumbled (or use real bacon bits)

Directions:
In a 1.5 lb. bread machine pan, place ingredients in order as directed by bread machine directions (mine calls for liquid ingredients in first, then dry). Select the regular bread bake time.

Submitter: 
Angie Fuller
Description: 
Quick and easy in the bread machine, this bread tastes even better toasted.
