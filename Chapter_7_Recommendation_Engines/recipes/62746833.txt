Title:
Sweettreats Strawberries and Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/632285.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pre made 9 inch vanilla wafer cookie pie crust
4 ounces cream cheese
1/8 teaspoon vanilla
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 pint strawberries sliced
8 ounce container Cool Whip
2 cups whipping cream
1 3.5 ounce pack instant vanilla pudding

Directions:
In a medium size bowl cream together the cream cheese,sugar and vanilla. Fold in 1 1/2 cups Cool Whip and spoon into the bottom of your prepared pie crust. Layer your sliced strawberries over the cream cheese mixture. In a medium size bowl add 2 cups whipping cream and sprinkle the entire pack of pudding. Whip until peaks form. Spoon vanilla mousse on top of the layered strawberries making sure the mousse completely covers the berries. Last take the remaining Cool Whip and completely cover the vanilla mousse. Top with a fresh strawberry. Let chill one hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
This is my favorite pie recipe. My family requests this pie for Easter and the Fourth of July. Quick and easy to make with a layer of fresh strawberries and creamy goodness. Enjoy.
