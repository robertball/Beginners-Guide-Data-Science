Title:
Caper and Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1012657.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 limes, juiced
2 tablespoons capers
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 clove garlic, minced
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk olive oil, lime juice, capers, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Tatiana
Description: 
It's easy and fast. It goes well with crab cakes, a fresh light salad, or fish.
