Title:
Graham Cracker bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg + 1 graham crackers
2 sticks marg
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup shredded almonds
3/4 cup walnuts

Directions:
Layer cookie sheet w/graham crackers. boil marg & brown sugar for 1 min. add nuts. pour over graham crackers.
Bake at 350 for 10 min.SLICE IMMEDIATELY & REMOVE
