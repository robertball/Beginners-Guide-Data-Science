Title:
Woopie Pies (Devil Dogs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup milk
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Filling:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup confectioners sugar
1 cup marshmellow fluff
1/2 tsp. vanilla
few drops milk

Directions:
Cream together margarine and 1 cup sugar, add egg.  Sift together 2 cups flour, cocoa, baking soda, 1/2 teaspoon salt and baking powder.  Add vanilla and milk.
Drop by tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheet and bake 7-8 minutes at 425 degrees F.  Cool completely and make sandwiches using following filling.
To Make Filling:  Combine all ingredients until smooth.
