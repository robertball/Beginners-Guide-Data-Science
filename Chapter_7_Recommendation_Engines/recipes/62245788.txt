Title:
Mommy's Johnny Marzetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
1/2 green pepper, diced
1 stalk celery, diced
1 med onion, chopped
16 oz. Hunt's tomato sauce
8 oz. pkg noodles, cooked
8 oz. cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown hamburger, green pepper, celery, and onion.  Drain off fat.  Add tomato sauce and noodles.
Pour into 2 qt. casserole, sprinkle with cheese, and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
