Title:
Cappuccino Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ounces freshly brewed espresso
8-10 ice cubes
1 1/2 cups extra creamy, low-fat, vanilla ice cream
1 tablespoon sugar (to taste)

Directions:
Place ice and ice cream in blender. Use pulse setting to chop ice cubes.
Add hot espresso and sugar. Blend until smooth.
Pour into two glasses. Top with whipped cream. Sprinkle with finely ground espresso or drizzle with chocolate syrup for garnish.

Submitter:

Description: 
This cool, refreshing beverage is a delicious treat for any coffee lover.
