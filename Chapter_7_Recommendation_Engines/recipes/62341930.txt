Title:
Marianne Worthington's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
4 1 pound cans pork & beans
1 1 pound can butter beans
1 1 pound can kidney beans
3/4 pound bacon
1 cup catsup
3 Tablespoons vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped yellow onion
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 Tablespoon Liquid Smoke

Directions:
Brown beef in skillet and drain.  Place in crock pot.
Cook bacon until crispy.  Place on paper towel to drain.  Crumble and add to crock pot.
Drain off drippings except 1 Tablespoon.  Cook onion in bacon drippings until tender.  Add to beef & bacon.
Add beans and other ingredients and stir well.
Cook on low in crock pot, 4 - 6 hours.
