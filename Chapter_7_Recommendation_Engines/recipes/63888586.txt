Title:
Al's Favorite Spinach Power Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. fresh lemon juice
2 T. extra virgin olive oil
1 lb baby spinach leaves, washed and trimmed
1 ripe avocado, chopped
1 10-oz. can mandarin orange sections, drained
1/4 cup toasted chopped walnuts or slivered almonds
optional feta cheese for topping

Directions:
Put lemon juice in a large mixing bowl and drizzle in the olive oil while whisking to form the dressing. Add the avocado and coat in dressing. Add the spinach, orange sections and nuts and toss to coat. Top with  feta cheese and serve.

Submitter: 
LivesToEat
Description: 
This salad with baked salmon and sauteed snap peas is our favorite weeknight meal. It all comes together quick and easy and the flavors really compliment each other. Best of all, it's very healthy AND delicious.
