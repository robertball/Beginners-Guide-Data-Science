Title:
Old School Home Made Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Vanilla Cook & Serve pudding
2 cans evaporated milk
2 C sugar
3 Eggs
Vanilla-to taste
2 Quarts milk

Directions:
1.  Combine pudding & canned milk in saucepan on stove.  Mix on low-medium until thick.
2.  Beat the eggs.
3.  Remove the pudding mixture from heat & add beaten eggs & sugar.
4.  Add vanilla extract to taste.
5.  Pour into ice cream cylinder from ice cream machine.
6.  Add milk to mixture (to fill line) and stir.
7.  Freeze according to your ice cream maker's directions.
Serve w/Mom's Homemade Peanut Butter Fudge Ice Cream Topping.
