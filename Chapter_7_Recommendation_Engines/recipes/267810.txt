Title:
Apple Cinnamon Cream Cheese Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5675974.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pancake mix
3/4 cup Almond Breeze Vanilla almondmilk
3/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon almond extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 cup peeled, chopped green apple
1/3 cup dried cranberries (optional)
1/2 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, torn into 1/2-inch pieces
chopped Blue DiamondÂ® Honey Roasted Almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F and line 12 muffin cups with paper liners.
Stir together pancake mix, Almond Breeze almondmilk, sugar, oil, extract, and cinnamon in a medium bowl. Lightly stir in apple, cranberries, and cream cheese. Spoon into prepared cups and sprinkle with almonds.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool, then store in a covered container with a loose fitting lid.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Sweet cinnamon muffins made with almondmilk and filled with apples and cream cheese. Muffins may be frozen. To reheat, leave wrapped muffins on the counter overnight, or microwave a frozen muffin on 50% power for about 20 seconds.
