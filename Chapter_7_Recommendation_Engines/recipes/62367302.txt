Title:
Creole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2  20 oz pkg of ground turkey
1/2  14 oz smoked sausage
1 green bell pepper, cored
1 red bell pepper, cored
1 pkg Uncle Ben’s Long Grain and Wild Rice, Quick Cook Recipe(plus seasoning packet), plus Minute Rice, to equal 2 cups
1 cup slivered almonds
1 tsp  1 tbsp crushed red pepper (to taste)
1 14 oz can chicken broth

Directions:
Cut sausage and bell peppers into chunks. Finely chop chunks in food processor in small batches, using “pulse,” Coat 5 qt pot with non-stick spray. Over high heat, brown turkey. Add sausage and bell peppers. Stir. Cook 5 minutes. Add rice, seasoning packet, almonds, and crushed red pepper. Stir. Add chicken broth. Stir. Bring to a boil. Cover, lower heat, and simmer 5 minutes. (If doubling, stir and simmer an additional 5 minutes.)
