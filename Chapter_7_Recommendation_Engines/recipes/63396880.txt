Title:
Coconut Black Bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1190449.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans black beans, rinsed
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can coconut milk
1 Tablespoon each ginger, cumin and turmeric
2-3 green onions, chopped
2-3 cloves garlic, diced
1 cup broth

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large sauce pan
Simmer 30-45 minutes.
Salt to taste.
