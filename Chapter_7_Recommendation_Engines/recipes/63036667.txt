Title:
Corned Beef Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 chopped sweet yellow or white onion
1 Tbsp oil
3 pkgs Budding corned beef -diced
1 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
8 oz sour cream
2 pkg cream cheese - softened
1 cup grated swiss cheese
1 tsp garlic powder
1 loaf Italian Bread
1 package crackers

Directions:
Saute onions is a pan until just tender
Put the onions into a large mixing bowl.
Mix in the remainding ingredients except the bread.
Hollow out the center of the Italian bread loaf to make a long bread bowl. Leave at least 1/2 inch on the sides.
Scoop the dip mixture into the bread bowl
Wrap in aluminum foil, place onto cookie sheet and bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 to 1.5 hours.
Serve on platter with crackers
