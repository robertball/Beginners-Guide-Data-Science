Title:
Mixed Berry Coconut Ice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana
2 cups frozen berries
1/4 cup coconut or almond milk

Directions:
Place ingredients into a blender or food processor and blend on high until combined. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This ice cream is refreshing and faster than driving to the store to pick up additive- and sugar-packed varieties.
