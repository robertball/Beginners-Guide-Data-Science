Title:
Roberta's Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed cream of mushroom (or celery) soup;
1/2 cup milk; 1/2 cup soy sauce; dash pepper; 4 cups cooked green beans; 1 1/3 cup fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix soup, milk, soy sauce, pepper, beans and 2/3 cup onions and 1.5 quart dish. Bake for 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Stir, then sprinkle remaining onions on top. Bake another 5 minutes.
