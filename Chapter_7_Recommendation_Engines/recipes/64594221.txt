Title:
Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp of cinnamon
4 cups of Peaches
3 Tbsp shortening
1 cup flour
1 Tbsp sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon baking power
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 400
Mix 1/2 cup sugar & cornstarch, cinnamon
Stir in Peaches.
Cook, stirring constantly until mixture thickens.
Boil and stir 1 minute.
Pour into ungreased 2 quarts casserole. Keep mixture hot in oven.
Cut shortening into flour, 1 Tbsp sugar, the baking powder, and salt mixture until crumbly.
Stir in milk.
Drop dough by 6 spoonfuls onto hot peach mixture.
Bake until topping is golden brown, 25-30 minutes

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
Easy and Delicious! Your can substitute the peaches with Cherry, Plum or Blueberries
