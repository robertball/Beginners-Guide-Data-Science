Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, chopped
2 stems fresh broccoli
1.3 Litres (quarts)chicken broth
1 cup processed cheese food such as Cheese Whiz
2 cups milk
2 cloves garlic crushed
2-3 Tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup water

Directions:
In a stockpot with chicken broth, Cook onion and garlic and broccoli until softened. Simmer until broccoli is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
Reduce heat, and stir in cheese cubes until melted. Mix in milk and garlic powder.
In a small bowl, stir cornstarch into water until dissolved. Stir into soup; cook, stirring frequently, until thick.

Submitter: 
Phyllis Trinke Funk
Description: 
This is a creamy soup made with broccoli florets and processed cheese food, and thickened with cornstarch.Modified from: Broccoli Cheese Soup
