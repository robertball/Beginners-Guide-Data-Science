Title:
Indoor S'mores 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups honey graham cereal
3 tablespoons margarine
6 cups miniature marshmallows (2cups aside)
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
1 Tbsp vanilla

Directions:
Coat a 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray.
Combine cereal and 2 cups marshmallows in mixing bowl.
In a double boiler, melt the chocolate chips and add vanilla.
In a medium saucepan over low heat, melt margarine. Add marshmallows and corn syrup and stir until melted and smooth. Remove from heat and add melted chocolate. Stir until completely mixed.
Pour over cereal/mallow mix; stir well to coat and press into prepared pan. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
