Title:
Campbell's Fiesta Chili Beef Soup - Copycat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup Ground Beef, browned
1 tablespoon Beef Fat (from Ground Beef)
1 ½ teaspoons Tomato Paste
½ teaspoon Garlic Powder
½ teaspoon Onion Powder
½ teaspoon Sugar
½ teaspoon Paprika
1 teaspoon Chili Powder
½ teaspoon Cumin
1 can Pinto Beans (with liquid), (15 oz.)
1 cup Beef broth
½ teaspoon Corn Starch
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Brown beef in a 2-qt. saucepan. Drain off fat and set beef aside, reserving 1 tablespoon fat. Add beef fat back to the saucepan over medium heat. Add tomato paste and cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes.
Combine dry seasonings in a small bowl. Add to fat/tomato paste mixture and cook for 30 sec to bloom the spices. Stir in beef broth.
Puree half the beans and liquid until smooth, add to soup along with remaining beans and liquid. Add ground beef. Mix cornstarch and water, add to soup and bring to a boil, then simmer for 10-15 minutes until flavors meld. Serve hot.
