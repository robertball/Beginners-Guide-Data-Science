Title:
Jim's Chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 seasoned rotisserie chicken ( seasoned)
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1 (15 ounce) cans mixed vegetables (drained)
1- Tablespoons minced garlic
3- Tablespoons Vegeta  seasoning
1 -Onion diced
2-Cups sliced mushrooms
2- Tablespoons chicken bouillion
1/2-teaspoon each salt and pepper (or to taste)
2-Tablespoons olive oil ( for cooking)
1-9 inch double crust pie

Directions:
Sautee the diced onion and garlic in the olive oil until the onions are translucent. Add the salt, pepper and Vegeta. Add the 2 cups of sliced mushrooms.
In a seperate bowl mix the cream of chicken soup and chicken bouillion.
Next take your rotisserie chicken ( or cut up chicken of your choosing) and tear it off into bite size pieces and place in a seperate bowl.
Take the thawed pastry crusts and place the chicken on the bottom. Add the  onion and garlic and chicken soup mixture then the mixed vegetables on top.
Take the 2nd pie crust and cover the top sealing the sides with a fork. Cut 5 slits on top of the crust to let it vent. Cook at 375 approx 40 minutes or until the crust is brown in the center.
