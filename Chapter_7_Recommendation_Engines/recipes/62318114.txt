Title:
Easy Parmesan Garlic Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C grated Parmesan cheese
1 envelope Italian salad dressing mix
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 t. garlic powder (or fresh garlic)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Combine cheese, Italian dressing mix and garlic powder.
Lightly moisten chicken with water or butter-flavored cooking spray, or dip into melted margarine.
Roll chicken in cheese mixture
Place chicken in lightly greased baking dish.
Bake for 20-25 min. or until chicken is done

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Boneless skinless chicken breasts, Italian salad dressing mix, garlic and grated Parmesan cheese makes for an easy to prepare, good tasting meal.
