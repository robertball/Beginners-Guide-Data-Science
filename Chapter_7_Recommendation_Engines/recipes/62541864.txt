Title:
Turkey Cranberry Wreath Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/524320.jpg
Ingredients:
2-8oz. pkgs. refrigerated crescent rolls
1/2 cup mayo, or as needed
2 tablespoons dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon coarsley ground black pepper
2 cups cooked turkey or chicken, chopped
1/2 cup celery, sliced
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1 cup shredded swiss cheese
1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
unroll crescent dough and seperate into 16 triangles.
With wide ends of the triangles towards the center, arrange 8 triangles in a circle on a 12" round pizza pan or large cooke sheet. Corners of wide ends will touch and points will extend beyond the baking sheet.
Arrange remaining 8 triangles in center, matching the wide ends.
Seal seams. Points will overlap in center; DO NOT SEAL.
Combine all remaining ingredients, except egg, in a mixing bowl and stir to mix well.
Spoon filling over seams of dough, forming a circle.
Beginning in center, lift one dough triangle across mixture.
Continue alternating with outer triangles to form a wreath shape, tucking ends underneath as you go along.
Beat egg in a small bowl and brush over dough.
Bake 20-30 minutes or until golden brown.
Cut, serve and enjoy!

One of my all-time favorite recipes adapted from The Pampered Chef. I did change slightly to suit my tastes. Also works great with chicken!
