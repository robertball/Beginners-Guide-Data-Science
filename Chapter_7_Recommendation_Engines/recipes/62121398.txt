Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 6-inch tortillas
1/2 Cup chopped onion
4 cloves minced garlic
1 taespoon ground coriander
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 Tablespoons butter
3 Tablespoons flour
8 ounces sour cream
4 ounce can diced green chilies; drained
1 Cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
2 Cups chopped cooked chicken breast
1 Cup sliced black olives
1 Cup chopped tomatoes
1 Cup sliced green onions

Directions:
Wrap tortillas in foil.
Heat in a 350 degree oven for 10- 15 minutes or until softened.
For sauce, in a large saucepan cook onion, garlic, corriander, and pepper in butter until onion is tender.
Stir flour into sour cream and add to onion mixture.
Stir in broth and chilies all at once.
Cook and stil until thickened and bubbly.
Remove from heat and stir in 1/2 Cup of cheese.
For filling, stir 1/2 Cup of sauce into chicken.
Place about 1/4 Cup of filling atop each tortilla and roll up.
Arrange tortilla rolls, seam side down, in a greased 12x8x2 inch baking dish.
Bake, covered, in a 350 degree oven for about 35 minutes or until heated through.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake, uncovered, about 5 minutes more or until cheese melts.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with black olives, tomatoes, and green onions.
