Title:
Coconut frosting white cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 white cake mix
2 sticks butter
2 13 oz cans unsweetened coconut milk
3/4 lb powdered sugar
vanilla extract
dash of salt
1/2 cup sweetened flaked coconut

Directions:
Prepare box cake according to directions making 2 9" layers and cool.
Boil coconut milk for about an hour, stirring occasionally, until it is less than 1 cup in volume; refrigerate.  Cream butter and sugar, add salt, as much vanilla as you like, and the cooled coconut milk.  Frost cake and sprinkle coconut flakes on top and side.
