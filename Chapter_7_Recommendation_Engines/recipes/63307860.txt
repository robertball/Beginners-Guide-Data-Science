Title:
Warm Cinnamon-dusted Apple Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small apples (any kind)
3 Tbsp. water
4 Tbsp. ground walnuts or almonds
dash of ground cinnamon
1c. cottage cheese 1% or 2%

Directions:
Cut each apple into eighths, discarding the core.
Coat a pan with nonstick spray and saute the apples in water until soft yet still crisp.
Top with cinnamon and nuts.
Serve with 1/2c. of cottage cheese.
