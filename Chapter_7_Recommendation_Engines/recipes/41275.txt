Title:
Bacon Hash Brown Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups grated cooked potatoes
12 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. Transfer to a greased 9-in. pie plate. Drizzle with butter; sprinkle with paprika.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
This tasty side dish has wonderful from-scratch flavor since it starts with fresh potatoes.
