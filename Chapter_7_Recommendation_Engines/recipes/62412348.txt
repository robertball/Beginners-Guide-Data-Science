Title:
Nana's Blueberry Ice Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C sugar
1 Tbsp corn starch
Pinch of salt
1/4 C water
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
2 C fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, mix together sugar, corn starch and salt. Stir in water and lemon juice. Add blueberries. Cook over med low heat,stirring occasionally, until thick and bubbling. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Serve warm over icecream. Can also be used for pancakes or waffles.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description: 
This is a delightful sauce for a summers day treat! Top with some fresh whipped cream...Mmmmmm...a little bit of heaven!
