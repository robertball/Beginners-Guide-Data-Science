Title:
Bacon, Asparagus, and Blackberry Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2307511.jpg
Ingredients:
Thin pizza crust
All amounts of following ingredients approximate:
2-3 Tbsp. ranch dressing
1 c.mozzarella
3-4 slices bacon, cooked semi-crisp
1/2 cup chopped grilled asparagus
1/3 cup sliced red onion
1/2 cup sliced blackberries
1/3 cup sliced kalamata olives
1/3 cup goat cheese

Directions:
Spread dressing on crust. Top with ingredients in order - amounts to taste.
Bake at 425 10 minutes or until cheese melts or to desired crust texture.
