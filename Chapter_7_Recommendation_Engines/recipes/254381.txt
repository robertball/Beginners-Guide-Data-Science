Title:
Insalata di Finocchi e Avocado (Fennel and Avocado Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4130013.jpg
Ingredients:
3 bulbs fennel, thinly sliced
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cubed
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
salt to taste

Directions:
Mix fennel and avocado together in a bowl. Stir in oil, nutmeg, and salt; mix until well combined.

Submitter: 
saretta
