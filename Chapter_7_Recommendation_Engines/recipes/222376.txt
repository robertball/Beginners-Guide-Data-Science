Title:
Creamy Strawberry Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970515.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (16 ounce) package fresh strawberries, hulled
1 loaf sliced white bread
1/2 cup chopped pecans, or more to taste (optional)

Directions:
Place cream cheese and strawberries in a food processor; pulse until smooth. Spread cream cheese mixture onto half the bread and sprinkle with pecans. Place remaining bread slices on top to create sandwiches.

Submitter: 
Rachel Winters
Description: 
These are very good. I served them at a bridal shower.
