Title:
Pineapple Upside-down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2305936.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/5 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp allspice
1 14 oz can pineapple or 8 fresh slice, drained reserving the juice
2 1/4 cups pineapple juice
3/4 cup cinnamon applesauce (to replace the eggs)
1/5 cup sugar
1/3 cup oil  chefmaster 52% oil margarine, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Combine all the dry ingredients flour cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda and allspice and sift them together into a bowl
Combine the wet ingredients pineapple juice, applesauce, margarine or oil and sugar
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and fold gently stirring to moisten add extra pineapple juice if too thick
Spray a 9" x 13" pan with Pam cooking spray until sides and bottom coated, then lay the pineapple  rings on bottom of pan, the pour cake batter over top
Bake in oven for 25-35 minutes or until center springs back when pressed

Submitter: 
Tony Carriere
Description: 
My version of the traditional dessert with the difference of no eggs substituted with applesauce.
