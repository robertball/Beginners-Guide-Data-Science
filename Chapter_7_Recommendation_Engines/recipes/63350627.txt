Title:
Vanilla Mousse Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 vanilla wafers, crushed - about 1 1/2 cups
3 tbsp. butter, melted
4 pkgs. cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 tbsp. and 1 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs
8 oz. tub Cool whip - thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Mix crushed wafers and butter.
Press into bottom of 9" springform pan.
Mix 3 packages of cream cheese, 3/4 cup sugar and 1 tbsp. vanilla until well blended.
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing at low speed until just blended.
Pour over crust.
Bake 50-55 minutes or until center is almost set.
Run knife around rim of pan to loosen cheesecake and cool completely in pan.
Beat remaining cream cheese, sugar and vanilla until well blended.
Fold in Cool Whip and spread over cooled cheese cake.
Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
Remove rim of pan before serving.
