Title:
Cole slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the slaw:
1 small head (2 to 2 1/2 pounds) cabbage (green, red, or a mix)
2 to 3 large carrots (3 to 3 1/2 cups shredded)
1 tablespoon salt, optional
1 1/4 cup coleslaw dressing
For the dressing (choose one):
Mayonnaise dressing:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup white wine vinegar or cider vinegar
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Shred the veggies, combine the ingredients and VOILA!
