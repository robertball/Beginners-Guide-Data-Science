Title:
Bruce's Best Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup ground baked almonds
2 teaspoons kirschwasser

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar. Beat in the egg, kirsch, and almonds. Gradually mix in the flour until well blended. Place by spoonful on cookie sheet, flatten slightly with the bottom of a glass.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cookies are lightly colored.
