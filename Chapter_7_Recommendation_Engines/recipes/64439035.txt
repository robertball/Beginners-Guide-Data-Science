Title:
The Best Chocolate Cake You Ever Ate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For cake:
1 & 1/2 cups white sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup butter
1 cup water
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup buttermilk
2 eggs
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
For frosting:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup milk
3 cups confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch pan or a cookie sheet.
Sift 1 & 1/2 cups white sugar and 2 cups flour together; set aside.
In a sauce pan; combine 1 cup butter, water, and 1/3 cup cocoa. Bring mixture to a boil, remove from heat and add to dry ingredients.
Dissolve baking soda in buttermilk; add with eggs, salt, cinnamon and 1 teaspoon vanilla to cocoa, flour mixture and stir well.
Pour batter into a 9 x 13 inch pan or large cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
To Make Frosting: Five minutes before cake is done combine 1/2 cup butter,1/4 cup cocoa and 1/4 cup milk in saucepan and bring to a boil.
Remove from heat and sift in confectioners' sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla. (Make sure you sift the powdered sugar into the cocoa mixture for the icing or you could get lumps of powdered sugar all over the cake when you pour the icing on top of it.)
Pour frosting over hot cake.  Cake remains moist and may be frozen. Serves 20.

Submitter: 
Scott Kallen
Description: 
I make this cake over and over.  It is easy to put together and the frosting of the cake is easy and fast.  It goes fast around my house.Modified from: The Best Chocolate Cake You Ever Ate
