Title:
Cranberry Sauce with Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5720523.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package fresh cranberries
2 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored, and diced
1 cup water
1 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Place cranberries, apples, water, sugar, and cinnamon in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer until cranberries pop and apples are soft, about 20 minutes.
Let cool completely before refrigerating.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
Fall flavors! Traditional cranberry sauce with the addition of Granny Smith apples. The pectin in the apples helps the sauce to set and brings another layer of flavor to a classic recipe. 
Make it several days in advance to relieve some of the holiday stress.
