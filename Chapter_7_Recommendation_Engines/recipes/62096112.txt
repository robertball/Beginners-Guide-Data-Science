Title:
Powdered Sugar Icing, 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Cups Powdered Sugar
2 tbsp. water
1 tbsp. butter, softened
1 tbsp. light corn syrup
1/2 tsp. vanilla or almond extract
food coloring if desired

Directions:
Combine powdered sugar, water, 1 tbsp. butter, corn syrup and 1/2 tsp vanilla in a small mixing bowl.
Mix until powdered sugar is moistened.
Beat at medium speed until smooth, adding water if necessary, to reach desired consistency for glazing.
May use a spoon or pipe from a bag to drizzle cookies.
Tint with food coloring if desired.

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
A nice shiny glaze that can be drizzled over sugar cookies or deserts. It will set (harden slightly) so it's nice if you wan't to stack your cookies.
