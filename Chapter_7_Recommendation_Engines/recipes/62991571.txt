Title:
Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb brussel sprouts
2 tbsp butter, melted
5 tbsp EVO
1 1/4" thick sliced pancetta, coarsely chopped
salt to taste
2 tbs blasamic reduction
1/4 cup penaces, toasted and chopped

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
cut ends off sprouts and slice in half, Rinse. Palce on baking sheet. Toss iwth butter and oil. Add pancetta. Sprinkle with salt.
Roast in oven for 15 - 20 minutes until cooked. Remove from oven and toss iwth balsamic and pecans. Return to over for 3 - 5 min until carmelized and brown
