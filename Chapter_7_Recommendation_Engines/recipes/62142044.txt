Title:
Dinner in a Dash Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 to 8 oz) box quick cook long grain and wild rice mix
2 (13 oz) cans chicken breast meat
2 cans peas and carrots, drained
1 can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (7 oz) jar diced fire roasted red peppers with liquid

Directions:
Prepare pilaf mix according to package directions in a large skillet or dutch oven.
Gently stir in remaining ingredients and heat through.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
Using a boxed pilaf rice mix and adding canned veggies and chicken helps me make a delicious dinner in under 20 minutes.
