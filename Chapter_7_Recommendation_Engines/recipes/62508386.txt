Title:
Delicious Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/505038.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces vodka
1 ounce sour apple schnapps
1 ounce apple juice

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, and pour in the vodka, sour apple schnapps, and apple juice. Shake well, then strain into a chilled martini glass.

Description: 
A perfect balance of sweet and sour in a glass.
