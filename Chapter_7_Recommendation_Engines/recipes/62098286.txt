Title:
Cajun Chicken Pasta LIGHT Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs skinless/boneless chicken breasts (or you can use chicken tenderloins)-chop into bite size pieces
4 TBSP light vegetable oil spread
1/2 cup chopped scallions
2 TBSP Cajun OR Creole seasoning
1 tsp lemon-pepper seasoning
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
2 TBSP hot pepper sauce
1 quart fat-free 1/2 and 1/2(large container)
8oz can mushroom stems/pieces (reduced sodium)
10 3/4 oz can reduced fat cream of mushroom soup
2-4 Tbsp flour
1/2 cup water
2lbs hot, cooked linguine
Grated parmesan/romano/asiago to taste

Directions:
Mix chopped up raw chicken with 2 TBSP of Cajun seasoning in plastic ziploc bag.  Shake well and then cook chicken in Dutch oven in melted vegetable oil-spread until chicken browned
Add mushrooms and scallions to pan
Add all of 1/2 and 1/2 and can of soup
Stir well over medium/high heat, gradually adding seasonings to taste (mixture will start to turn pale orange if you make it spicy)
When mixture hot, but not yet bubbling, mix together flour and warm water in coffee mug or jar and mix well until mixture looks milky
Add flour mixture to Dutch oven
Stir and bring to low boil, until mixture thickens, stirring frequently
Add grated cheese
Serve over hot linguine.

Submitter: 
JEN78RN
Description: 
Over the years I've perfected my healthier version of Cajun Chicken Pasta. It's VERY spicy, so play with the seasonings to your tastes. This makes a HUGE batch, which is great for entertaining.
