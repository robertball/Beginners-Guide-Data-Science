Title:
Jaay's Baked Spagetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounce package spagetti noodles, cooked
1(24 ounce)jar spagetti sauce
1 pound ground beef, browned and drained
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (adjust to suit taste or optional)
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1(12 ounce)can evaporated milk
3 cups sharp Cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine spagetti noodles, spagetti sauce, ground beef and crushed red pepper flakes in a large bowl and set aside.
Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat;add flour, Parmesan cheese, salt, and garlic powder, stirring constantly until smooth and bubbly.
Add evaporated milk and one cup of Cheddar cheese; stir until thickened.
Pour half of spagetti noodle mixture into a greased 9"x13" glass dish.
Pour cheese sauce mixture over top.
Pour remaining spagetti noodle mixture into dish.
Top with remaining 2 cups of Cheddar cheese.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jaay
Description: 
A delicious comforting recipe that dresses up store-bought spagetti sauce.  Perfect for when you're having company or just want to treat your family.
