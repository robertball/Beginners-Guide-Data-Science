Title:
Chicken Wings, chinese marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken wiings (with or without skin)
2 tlbs garlic paste
4 tlbs soy sauce
2 tlbs oyster sauce
2 egg whites
1 tsp ground black peppercorn
2 tlbs oil

Directions:
Mix, garlic, soy, egg whites, peppercorn and oyster sauce in a deep bowl
Make shallow knife slashes on the wings
Use hands to mix the wings well into the marinade
Set aside for 1/2 an hour
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
Place wings evenly in a shallow baking tray in the middle rack of the oven
Bake till meat is white and surface crispy to touch.
Best eaten with chilli-garlic dipping sauce or sweet chilli thai sauce, if adding extra chilli to marinade

Submitter: 
sugarFiend
Description: 
Extremely fast and easy to prepare. A new and flavourful version, using a simple method. However its an indulgence as the sauces contain a fair amount of salt, and chicken skin is extremely fatty.
