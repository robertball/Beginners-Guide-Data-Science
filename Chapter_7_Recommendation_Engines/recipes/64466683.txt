Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites (Lemon Blueberry) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2443080.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
6 tbsp butter or margarine (chilled)
1/3 cup milk
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1 tbsp lemon zest
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Measure first four ingredients in mixing bowl and combine. Cut in butter or margarine until crumbly. Stir in milk, lemon juice, lemon zest, and blueberries and mix until a soft dough forms. Do not over mix. Roll into 1.5" balls and place on parchment covered cookie sheet 2" apart. Brush lightly with milk and sprinkle with sugar. Bake for 15-20 minutes until tops start to turn golden brown.

Submitter: 
Wilene Parris Messex
Description: 
Quick breakfast on the go while doing gishwhes. Taste like the lemon blueberry scones you get at one of those fancy coffee shops.
