Title:
"Hashbrown" Casserole - No Carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Spaghetti Squash or Cauliflower
Chopped Onion
Greek Yogurt - a good dollop
Cream of Chicken Soup with Herbs
Shredded Cheese (Cheddar)
Spice - Mrs. Dash, seasoning salt, pepper, cayenne, etc. to taste

Directions:
Microwave the spaghetti squash/cauliflower in microwave until tender (5-10 minutes)
Shred/Mash cooked vegetable into a mixing bowl
Add in onion, yogurt, soup, cheese, and seasoning
Adjust to taste - nothing is "raw" so you can taste before cooking.
Place mixture into a baking dish, cover with additional cheese and/or paprika.
Bake at 350 until bubbly - maybe 45minutes.

Submitter: 
Colleen Hibbitts
Description: 
Trying to cut down on carbs? Easy, faux hashbrown recipe will make you forget all about potatoes. Tastes much like Cracker Barrel casserole
