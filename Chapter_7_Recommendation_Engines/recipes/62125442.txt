Title:
Swedish Kaldolmar (Stuffed Cabbage) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 cup boiled rice
1 pound ground pork
1 medium size onion, minced
1 head of cabbage
salt, pepper, allspice to taste

Directions:
Season meat, add rice and onion and mix.
Core cabbage, pluck off leaves separately and wilt in boiling water about 5 minutes.
Form meat mixture into patties and carefully wrap with cabbage leaves so as to cover well.
Fasten with toothpicks.
Butter pan and place patties side by side to keep shape while browning.
Baste with melted butter occasionally and bake about 1 hour in slow oven. (350 degrees)
Remove from pan.
Mix in 1 tablespoon flour and 1/2 cup cream add to juice in pan, cook until smooth.
Return rolls to gravy and reheat.

Description: 
This is a recipe that my great grandmother would make for the family.  This is the swedish version of stuffed cabbage.
