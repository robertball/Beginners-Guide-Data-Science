Title:
chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks unsalted butter
2.5 cups packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsps salt
4 tbls milk
2 tbls vanilla extract
3.5 cups all purpose flour
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Cream sugar into butter.  Add milk and vanilla and beat until incorporated and then blend in eggs.  Add remaining dry ingredients and mix well.  Stir in chips and nuts.
Put cookie dough on greased baking sheets and bake at 350 degrees until edges are lightly browned approx 12 minutes.

These cookies are fantastic.  Varying the ingredients and cooking time will personalize the cookies to your taste.
