Title:
Easy, Healthy Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs (or EggBeaters equivalent)
2 cups hash browns
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup mexican blend 2% shredded cheese, divided
2 Morningstar veggie sausage patties
1 teaspoon dried minced garlic
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
sour cream (optional)
hot sauce (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cook veggie sausages over medium heat in a covered skillet, flipping once, until heated through (about 7 minutes).  Remove from heat and dice.
If hash browns are frozen, thaw them by heating in microwave for one minute.
In a medium bowl, whisk eggs and milk together.
Add 1/4 cup of shredded cheese, garlic powder, salt, pepper, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes and mix well.
Fold in hash browns and diced sausage.
Pour mixture evenly into 9 inch glass pie plate and sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup of cheese over top.
Bake in 400 degree oven 25 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Serve with sour cream and hot sauce, if desired.

Description: 
This vegetarian breakfast casserole is quick and reasonably healthy.  Makes a great Sunday morning dish!
