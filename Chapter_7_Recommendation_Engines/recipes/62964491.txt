Title:
Meatloaf by Deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pound ground beef
8 oz fresh mushrooms, chopped
2 tablespoons steak sauce
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
4 eggs
1 1/2 cup instant oatmeal
4 teaspoon frahm seasoning
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix the ground beef, steak sauce, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, egg, oatmeal, seasoning and Cheddar cheese together in a large bowl until evenly mixed. Form into 2 loafs and place in pampered chef 4 loaf pan. Add mushrooms to remaining meat and mix throughly. Form into 2 loafs and fill last 2 spots in pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
