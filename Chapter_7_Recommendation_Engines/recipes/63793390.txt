Title:
Clara's Quick Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052397.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shortening
1 cup white sugar
1 large egg
4 tbsp. molasses
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 tsp. ground cloves
2 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 tsp. salt
extra white sugar to roll the dough in

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2.  Cream shortening, then add sugar, egg and molasses.
3.  Add remaining ingredients, stir until well blended.
4.  Scoop dough with rounded tablespoon and roll dough into small balls.
5.  Roll dough in white sugar and press down with fork, once vertically and then    again horizontally.  To keep fork from sticking, dip in cold water.
6.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for approx. 10 minutes*
7.  Wait approx. one minute before removing from cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
joanie bakes
Description: 
One of my earliest kitchen memories is baking these cookies with my mom, Clara.  She made these every Christmas for friends and family.  I now do the same.
