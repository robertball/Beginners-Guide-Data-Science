Title:
Black Bottom Banana Cream Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985141.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3.4 ounce) package vanilla or banana cream instant pudding and pie filling mix
1 cup cold milk
6 tablespoons hot fudge topping, heated according to package directions
1 (10 ounce) package Pepperidge FarmÂ® Puff Pastry Shells, prepared according to package directions
1 large banana, thinly sliced
1 1/2 cups sweetened whipped cream

Directions:
Beat the pudding mix and milk in a large bowl with a whisk for 2 minutes. Cover and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
Spoon 1 tablespoon fudge topping into each pastry shell. Reserve 6 slices banana. Divide the remaining banana among the pastry shells. Spoon the pudding mixture into the pastry shells. Top each with 1/4 cup whipped cream and 1 slice reserved banana.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Hot fudge, bananas, pudding, puff pastry and whipped cream...with those ingredients, these individual dessert shells are guaranteed to be delicious!
