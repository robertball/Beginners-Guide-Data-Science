Title:
Braised Chicken and Kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken legs
4 chicken thighs
1 tbsp. paprika
Salt and pepper
1 tsp. olive oil
1 medium onion - sliced
6 garlic cloves - sliced
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup dry white wine
2 sprigs of rosemary
2 sprigs of thyme
1 large bunch of kale, ribs and stems removed and cut into 1" strips
Lemon wedges

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with paprika and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
Add chicken, skin side down and cook, turning occasionally, until brown on all sides.
Remove chicken from pot and add garlic an onion.
Cook about 8-10 minutes or until softened.
Return chicken to pan and add broth, wine and herbs.
Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat.
Simmer for about 30-40 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Add kale to pot, cover and cook until wilted - about 5 minutes.
Discard herb sprigs.
Serve with lemon wedges.
