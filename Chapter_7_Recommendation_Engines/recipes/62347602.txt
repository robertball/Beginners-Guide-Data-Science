Title:
Tangy Cherryberry Sipper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1/2 cup sliced stawberries
1/2 cup pitted and halved cherries
1/2 cup lowfat plain Greek yogurt
2 teaspoons ground flaxseed
3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender in the order listed.  Blend on high speed until smooth.  Serve immediately.  If using frozen fruit instead of fresh, reduce the ice to 3/4 cup.
