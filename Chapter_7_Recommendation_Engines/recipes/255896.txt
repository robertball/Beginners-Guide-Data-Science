Title:
Roasted Raspberry Chipotle Grilled Cheese Sandwich on Sourdough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4061386.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter, softened
4 slices sourdough bread
8 BordenÂ® Mild Cheddar Slices
4 tablespoons roasted raspberry chipotle sauce

Directions:
Place a skillet over medium heat.
Butter 4 slices of bread with a tablespoon of butter each. Place 2 slices butter side down onto skillet. Place 2 slices of cheese on each.
Spread 2 tablespoons of the roasted raspberry chipotle sauce onto each sandwich, and top each with 2 more slices of cheese.
Top each with remaining bread, butter side up. Grill until bottom is lightly browned; flip sandwiches over. Grill other side until browned and cheese has melted.

Submitter:

Description: 
Smoky and sweet with a hint of heat to add a twist to a classic grilled cheese sandwich with roasted raspberry chipotle sauce.
