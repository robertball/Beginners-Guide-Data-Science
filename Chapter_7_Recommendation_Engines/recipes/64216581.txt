Title:
Vegan Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups unsweetened almond milk
2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
2 cups yellow corn meal
1 cup spelt flour
1/3 cup melted coconut oil
2 teaspoons maple syrup
½ teaspoon sea salt
2 teaspoons baking powder (aluminum free)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, line a large 9×13 baking
dish with parchment paper.
In a bowl, whisk together the almond milk and
vinegar.
Meanwhile, mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Add melted coconut oil and maple syrup to almond milk mixture and stir rapidly for 2 minutes
until a foam and bubbles start to develop.
Add the wet ingredients to the dry in the large bowl, stirring until well mixed.
Pour batter into baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes.
