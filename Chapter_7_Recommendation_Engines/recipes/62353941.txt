Title:
California Club Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c prepared guacamole
6 (8 inch) whole wheat tortillas
1 1/2 cups finely shredded iceberg lettuce
12 slices thinly sliced deli turkey
12 slices thinly sliced deli ham
3/4 cup shredded Swiss cheese
6 slices bacon, cooked and blixed

Directions:
Spread each tortilla with about 2 tablespoons of the guacomole to within 1/4 inch of the edge of the tortillas.
Arrange about 1/4 cup of shredded lettuce on each tortilla, and press the lettuce down into the guacomole mixture. Place 2 turkey slices per tortilla over the lettuce,then 2 slices of ham and sprinkle with 2 tablespoons of shredded Swiss cheese. Top each tortilla evenly crumbled bacon.
Roll each tortilla up tightly, and cut in half across the middle with a slightly diagonal cut.
Repeat as needed

Submitter:

Description: 
Thin deli turkey and ham is wrapped in a whole wheat tortilla with guacamole, Swiss cheese, and crumbled bacon for an easy lunch on the go.Modified from: Turkey Wraps
