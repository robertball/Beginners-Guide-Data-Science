Title:
Spicy Seafood Cajun Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (2 oz.) lobster tails, raw
1/4 lb. medium shrimp, raw
Sprinkling of crushed red pepper
1/2 cup light cream
Crushed tomatoes (about 5 or 6 tablespoons)
salt and pepper
sprinkling of cajun seasoning
1/2 box of fettucine
2 tbsp. Olive oil
1 tbsp. butter
2 cloves minced garlic
pinch of dried basil

Directions:
Boil water to cook pasta, cook pasta per box directions While pasta cooks, split tails down backside, pull out meat and chop into bite size pieces.
Shell and clean shrimp.
Heat olive oil & butter in skillet over med-high heat, when melted, add garlic & crushed red pepper
When softened, add lobster & shrimp, season with crushed basil, salt(s), and black pepper. Cook until pink.
Stir in tomatoes and cream and simmer until thickened.
Drain pasta and pour sauce over, mixing together.

Submitter: 
Trina Cosgrave
Description: 
A creamy spicy pasta with shrimp & lobster, goes great with a crisp white wine...casual enough for a home dinner, and simply elegant enough for entertaining guests.
