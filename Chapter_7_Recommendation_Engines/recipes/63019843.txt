Title:
Beef Stuffed Spuds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871621.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tbsp butter, divided
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 lb lean ground beef
4 large potatoes, baked
1 tsp milk
1 tsp salt
1/2 pepper
1 tsp minced parsley
1 cup (4 oz) shredded swiss cheese

Directions:
Saute 1 tablespoon butter, onion; add beef. Cook til brown and crumbly; drain. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise. Scoop out each potato, leaving about 1/4" shell, set shells aside. In large bowl, mash potatoes with milk, remaining butter. Add salt, pepper, parsley & meat; mix well. Fill potato shells & top with cheese. Place on cookie sheet. Bake at 400 for 15 minutes or til heated through.
