Title:
Mom's Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups rolled oats
1 cup canola oil
2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/4 cups AP flour
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cloves
1/2 tsp allspice
1 cup raisins, light and dark mix, plumped with boiling water, drained well

Directions:
Mis oatmeal and oil in large bowl.  Let sit overnight.  Preheat oven to 375.  Stir in eggs and vanilla and mix well.  In small bowl, mix dry ingredients.  Add to oatmeal and mix.  Add in raisins and mix.  Drop onto greased baking sheet and bake 8-12 minutes.  Do NOT over bake!
