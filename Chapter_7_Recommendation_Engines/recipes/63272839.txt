Title:
Butterscotch Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 t. baking powder
2 c. flour
1 1/2 t. salt
1 - 12oz. pkg. butterscotch chips
1/2 c. butter
2 c. brown sugar
4 eggs
1 t. vanilla
2 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
Melt together the chips and butter. Transfer to a different, larger bowl and add brown sugar.
Beat in eggs and vanilla. Blend in flour. Add nuts.
Spread in a greased 9x13 baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
