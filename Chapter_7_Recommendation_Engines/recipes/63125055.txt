Title:
Olive Garden Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet Good Seasons Italian dressing
oil
water
vinegar
1 tsp dried italian seasoning
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tbs mayonnaise (no miracle whip)
1/4 cup oil oil
2 tbs white vinegar
1 1/2 tbs water

Directions:
Make good seasons dressing and then add all other ingredients together and whisk together.  Put into container and serve with salad.
