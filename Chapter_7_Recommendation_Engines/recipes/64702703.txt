Title:
Beef Broccoli Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onions
1 clove garlic
3/4 teaspoon garlic salt (Can be just salt if you use garlic to brown)
1 1/4 cups milk
1 3-ounce package cream cheese
1 beaten egg
1 16-ounce bag frozen chopped broccoli cooked and drained
1 recipe of pie crust
4 ounces monterey jack cheese

Directions:
Brown beef, onion, garlic together, drain
In saucepan stir in flour, salt, or garlic salt.  Add milk and cream cheese.
cook and stir until thickened and smooth
Add a moderate amound of hot mixture to beaten eg
Return mixture to skillet
cook and stir for 1-2- minutes
Place mixture  in prepared pie crust, add monterey jack cheese sliced, brush crust with milk
Bake @ 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
