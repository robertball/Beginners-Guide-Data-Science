Title:
Baja Shrimp Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/569407.jpg
Ingredients:
Approx 12-15 medium sized shrimp, cooked w/ tails removed and deveined.
4 oz of cream cheese
10 grape tomatoes, halved
4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/2 avocado thinly sliced
4 sprigs of chives, chopped
4 flour tortillas
butter

Directions:
Butter one side of a tortilla and place butter side down in a non-stick pan. Layer half of the shrimp, tomatoes, bacon, avocado, & chives. Dot with 1/2 of the softened cream cheese. Top with another tortilla, butter side up. Grill til, it takes on a nice golden color, and carefully flip it to brown and cook the other side. Take out of the pan and cut into quarters. Repeat the same procedure with the remaining ingredients to make another one.
