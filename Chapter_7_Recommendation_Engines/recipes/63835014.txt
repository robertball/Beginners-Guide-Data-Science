Title:
Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065749.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle fruit punch, chilled
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle unsweetened pineapple juice, chilled
1 (2 liter) bottle ginger ale, chilled
1/2 gallon sherbet

Directions:
In a punch bowl, mix together fruit punch, pineapple juice and ginger ale.
Add scoops of sherbet into the punch.
Wait for the sherbet to begin melting, approximately 10 minutes, stir gently, and serve.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Cool and easy recipe. Fruit punch is enhanced with pineapple juice and ginger ale, then topped with sherbet.Modified from: Fruit Punch
