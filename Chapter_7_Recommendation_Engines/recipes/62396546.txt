Title:
Lemon Blueberry Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
1/2 cup short grained rice (rinsed)
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp vanilla
zest and juice of 3 lemons
1/2 cup blueberries (rinsed)
1 large container of balkan style yogurt plain
1 Tbsp lemoncello (optional)

Directions:
Put rice, milk, vanilla and sugar in a saucepan and bring to a boil....stir frequently..once it has boiled put on simmer and let the rice absorb the liquild...remember stir...
meanwhile..put lemon zest and juice in bowl.  Add blueberries and lemoncello.
Next empty container of yogurt into clean dish towel or cheesecloth and wrap around like a ball. Put into a sieve and then put the sieve over a bowl.  This will take all the bitter whey out. (takes about 1/2 hour)
Once rice is soft, take off heat.  When cool and the ingredients in the lemon mixture and stir. Then stir in dewheyed yogurt and blend till smooth. (You may want to add more sugar).  Pour into individual glasses. Cling wrap tops and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Debra Rose
Description: 
This smooth refreshing desert is perfect for the summer, can make a day ahead.
