Title:
Skinny Fudge Browinies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3051192.jpg
Ingredients:
2 farm fresh eggs
1 banana
1/2 cup melted chocolate
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1 cup Gluten Free Oat Flour
Pinch of Pink Himalayan Salt
1/4 tsp Gluten Free Baking Powder
or baking soda
1/4 chocolate chips

Directions:
Directions:
In s large mixing bowl, whisk eggs.
Add banana (ripe is better) and mash with a potato masher or fork.
Add melted chocolate, cocoa, and whisk.
Add oat flour, pink Himalayan salt, baking powder and whisk.
Add 1/4 chocolate chips, or as much as you prefer.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes but no longer than 25 minutes.
