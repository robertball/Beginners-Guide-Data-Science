Title:
Raleigh's Hush Puppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup self-rising cornmeal,
1/2 cup self-rising flour,
1 egg,
1/2-3/4 cup buttermilk,
1/2 cup finely chopped onions,
1/2 tsp black pepper,
1 quart vegetable oil for frying.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together well.  Note: a thinner batter will make a lighter puppy.  Spoon a TBSP at a time into hot oil, and deep fry umtil golden brown on all sides.  Do not over crowd the pot of oil.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
