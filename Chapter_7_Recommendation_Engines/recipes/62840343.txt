Title:
French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slc Bread
2 lg Eggs
¾ c Milk
¼ t Cinnamon
¼ t Nutmeg
1 t Vanilla
Salt (to taste)

Directions:
BEAT egg, milk, spices and vanilla.
HEAT a lightly oiled skillet at medium-high.
DUNK each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides.
PLACE in pan, and cook on both sides, until golden.
SERVE hot.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description: 
A basic French toast recipe that works with many types of bread - white, whole wheat, cinnamon-raisin, Italian or French. Serve hot with butter or margarine and maple syrup.Modified from: French Toast I
