Title:
Momma's Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3-6 pound) whole chicken, rinsed and giblets removed
Olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
1 onion, quartered
1 stalk of celery, cut into 4" pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Rinse chicken and remove giblets.
Pat dry with paper towels.
Place chicken in a 9x13 inch pyrex baking dish or roasting pan.
Drizzle olive oil on skin.  Rub in to evenly coat bird.
Stuff the cavity with the onion and celery.
Put remaining onion and celery around bird in pan.
Sprinkle salt and pepper over top and legs.
Roast in the preheated oven for 1 1/2 to 2  hours, or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Cooking time will vary a bit depending on the size of the bird.  Breast should read 160F and thigh should read 175F

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
Stuff the cavity of a whole roasting chicken with onion and celery for a simple and delicious baked chicken.  To change things up a bit, replace the celery with fresh rosemary and it will taste  just like the ones in Italian markets.Modified from: Roast Chicken with Rosemary
