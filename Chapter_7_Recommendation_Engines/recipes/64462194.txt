Title:
Hot Plate - Chinese Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-12 oz ground beef
2 cups Broccoli
1/2 cup carrots, slivered
1 1/2 cup mushrooms
1 Tbl olive oil
1/2 tsp sesame oil
Best Stir Fry Sauce Ever
Salt & pepper to taste
Optional:
Toasted sesame seeds
Scallions, chopped

Directions:
Heat large skillet over med-high. Add fat. Toss aromatic veggies and saute until crisp-tender.
Heat spices to pan until fragrant, 30 seconds.
Add protein and vegetables, sprinkle with salt & pepper.
