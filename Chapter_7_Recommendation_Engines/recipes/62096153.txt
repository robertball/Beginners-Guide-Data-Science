Title:
Oven Steak and Mushrooms #1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lbs. Sirloin Steak or Top Round, cut into one inch or bite size cubes
1/3 Cup Flour
1/2 Tsp. salt
1/4 Tsp pepper
1 Tbsp. Dry Mustard
2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
2 Cups Sliced mushrooms
1 Medium Onion, sliced or chopped
1 tbsp Brown Sugar
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1 (19 oz) can diced tomatoes w/juice
2 large potatoes, cut into 1 inch chunks

Directions:
Combine flour, salt, pepper and dry mustard
Toss beef cubes in flour mixture, coat evenly
Heat oil in a large frying pan and brown beef on all sides
Brown in batches and transfer to large casserole dish
Add remaining ingredients except potatoes
Cover and Bake at 350' for 45 minutes, add potatoes and stir well
Cook another 45 minutes, let sit for 10 minutes and serve
