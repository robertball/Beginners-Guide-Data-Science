Title:
Wasabi Cashews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg white
1 tablespoon water
1 pound whole natural Cashews
2 tablespoons wasabi powder
2 teaspoons kosher salt
2 teaspoons cornstarch

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees F. Line a sided baking sheet with greased foil or parchment paper.
Whisk egg white and water together until foamy. Add cashews and toss to coat. Transfer to a sieve; toss gently and let drain.  Stir together wasabi powder, salt and cornstarch in a large bowl. Add cashews and toss to coat.
Spread cashews on baking sheet in a single layer, and bake 30 minutes. Gently stir, reduce temperature to 200 degrees F and continue baking 20 more minutes. Let cool completely before serving. Serve, or store in an airtight container for up to a week
