Title:
Jlyn's Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Rice
2 Lb Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts
2 cans Cream of Chicken Soup
2C Milk
1 Tbsp Oil
1tsp each Salt and Pepper
Additional seasonings as desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Boil 3-4 Cups Water, add in 2 Cups Rice and cook til most of the water is absorbed and rice is al dente.
Slice Chicken breasts into small, bite-sized pieces.
Heat 1Tbsp oil in pan. Sear chicken pieces, but do not fully cook.
Combine rice, chicken, 2 cans cream of chicken soup, and 2C milk in 9x13 pan and mix until well blended.
Bake at 350F for 20-30 minutes, until chicken is cooked through.
