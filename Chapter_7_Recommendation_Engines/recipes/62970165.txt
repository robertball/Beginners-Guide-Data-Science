Title:
My Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/734429.jpg
Ingredients:
Bars:
2 c. flour
2 c. sugar
2 t. baking powder
1 t. soda
1 t. cinnamon
1 t. nutmeg
1 t. cloves
½ t. salt
1 c. oil
15-oz. can pumpkin
4 eggs
Frosting:
2 c. powdered sugar
1/3  c. margarine
3-oz. pkg. cream cheese
1 T. milk
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°.  Spray 15x10" pan with PAM.  In large bowl, blend all ingredients at low speed until moistened.  Beat 2 minutes at medium speed.  Pour into prepared pan.  Bake at 350° for 20-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool.
In small bowl, combine frosting ingredients; beat until smooth.  Spread over cooled bars.  Refrigerate leftovers.
