Title:
Vegetable Diet Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078340.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans of tomatoes (stewed or diced),
2 cans of green beans,
1 box beef or chicken broth (no fat),
1 package Lipton soup mix (ie: onion),
1 bunch celery (chopped)
3 large green onions (chopped)
2 green peppers (chopped)
2lb bag of carrots (chopped)
2 cups pot barley

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a large stock or soup pot except the pot barley
Season with salt, pepper, curry, parsley
Cover with water, boil fast for 10 minutes.
Simmer until all veggies are tender

Submitter: 
AnneP
Description: 
Inexpensive, freezes well and full of veggies! I sometimes throw in leftover chicken to cover off 3 food groups in one meal. Freezes very well in lunch sized portions
