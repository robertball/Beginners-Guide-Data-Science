Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 baked 9 inch pie shell
2 1/3 c. cold milk
2/3 c. chunky peanut butter
1 lg. box Jello chocolate instant pudding mix
1/2 tsp. vanilla, optional
1 8 oz. carton cool whip
2 tbs. chopped peanuts for garnish

Directions:
Mix and beat together well, milk, peanut butter, vanilla and pudding mix.  Pour into baked pie shell.  Chill.  Top with Cool Whip and sprinkle with finely chopped peanuts before serving.
