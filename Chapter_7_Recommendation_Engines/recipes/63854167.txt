Title:
Basic Bundt Cake Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070133.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ½ cups of confectioner's sugar
8 oz. of softened cream cheese
¼ cup of softened butter
3 T. of milk
2 t. of vanilla extract

Directions:
Beat confectioner's sugar, cream cheese, butter, milk and vanilla extract together in a mixing bowl using an electric mixer or a whisk. Mix the ingredients until smooth and creamy.
Place the cooled cake on a cake stand or plate.
Pour the icing over the top of the bundt cake evenly, allowing some to drizzle down the sides.
