Title:
Sour Cream Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1/4 teaspoon white vinegar
1 tbsp vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour (SIFTED)
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, beaten

Directions:
In a bowl, mix together milk, sour cream, yogurt, vinegar and vanilla extract.
Sift flour into a separate, large bowl; stir in baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Add sour cream mixture and eggs to flour mixture; blend until smooth.
Cook on a lightly greased waffle iron until golden brown; lift off with a fork.

Submitter: 
Marianna
Description: 
These waffles substitute sour cream and yogurt for milk. The inspiration came about one morning when there was no milk in the house.Modified from: Sour Cream Waffles
