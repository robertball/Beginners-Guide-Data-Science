Title:
Apricot Glazed Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Chicken Broth
1/2  (9 ounce) jar apricot preserves
1/2 large onion, chopped
1 tablespoons Dijon-style mustard or mustard powder
2 pounds boneless pork loin roast

Directions:
Mix broth, preserves, onion and mustard in 3 1/2-qt. slow cooker. Cut pork to fit. Add to cooker.
Cover and cook on LOW 8 to 9 hr.* or until done.

Submitter: 
Jacque
Description: 
Pork roast slow-cooks to tenderness under a tangy glaze of apricot preserves, mustard, onion and Campbell's® Condensed Chicken Broth.Modified from: Apricot Glazed Pork Roast
