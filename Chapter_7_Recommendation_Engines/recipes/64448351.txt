Title:
Chicken with Sherry Vinegar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken cutlets
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. butter
1 tsp. olive oil
1/2 cup minced shallot
3/4 cup chicken broth
3 tsp. sherry vinegar
2 tbsp. heavy cream
1 tbsp. fresh parsley

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.
Heat butter and oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
Add chicken and cook about 4 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
Remove from pan and keep warm.
Add shallots to pan and saute 1 minute.
Stir in broth and vinegar and cook for 2 minutes.
Stir in cream and cook 1 minute.
Pour sauce over chicken and sprinkle with parsley.
