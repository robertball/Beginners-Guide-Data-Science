Title:
Dirty Sue Bayou Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/401973.jpg
Ingredients:
3 oz Tito's Vodka
1/2 oz Dirty Sue premium olive juice
2 dashes Crystal Hot Sauce
Zatarain's Creole Seasoning
pickled okra or spicy pickled green beans

Directions:
In a mixing tin, add: Tito's, Dirty Sue and hot sauce. Add ice. Shake or stir well. Strain into a chilled martini glass that has the Zatarain's on half the rim. Garnish with the okra. Enjoy.
