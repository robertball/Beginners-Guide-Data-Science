Title:
Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
2 tsp ground cinnamon
4 cans flaky biscuits
1/2 butter, melted
3/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Grease bundt pan.
Mix sugar and cinnamon in 1 gallon baggie.  Cut each biscuit into quarters and shake in the bag to coat then place them in the pan.Mix butter and brown sugar and pour over biscuit pieces.
Bake at 350 for 40-45 min.
Turn upside down and serve.
