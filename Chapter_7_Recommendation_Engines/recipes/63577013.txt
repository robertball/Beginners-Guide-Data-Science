Title:
Grandma (Letha) Hoffman's Butterscotch Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c dark Karo Syrup
1 c light Karo Syrup
2 c sugar
4 tbsp butter
2 tbsp cream
1/3 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine the Karos, sugar, butter and cream.
Boil, stirring frequently to 258 degrees F on candy thermometer or when mixture is nearly brittle if a little is tried in cold water.
Add the vanilla and salt.
Pour into shallow oiled pan and cut into squares while still warm.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Old-fashioned candy that are likened to home-made caramels.
