Title:
Big Ray's Easy Honey Soy Garlic  Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3054108.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Chicken Wings , separated,(you can also use drumsticks, thighs, breast fillets etc.  NOTE: If using breast or thigh fillets, chop them up into even chunks to ensure even cooking.)
2 Tbsp of Honey
2 Tbsp of Dark Soy Sauce
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp olive oil

Directions:
In a large resealable bag, mix the honey, soy sauce and garlic.  Add chicken pieces and massage to mend flavous together. Allow to marinade 30 minutes (or more).
Heat a large skillet with oil over medium heat.  Add chicken pieces and cook, stirring occasionally until cooked.

Description: 
Easy Honey Soy Garlic Chicken is a simply but tasty dish that is bursting with flavour. Honey and dark soy sauce is combined to make a sweet, salty, sticky marinade, then glazed over fresh chicken pieces.
