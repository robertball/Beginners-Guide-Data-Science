Title:
Chili's Skillet Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz of Velveeta, cut into cubes
1 15 oz can Hormel Chili (No Beans)
1 cup milk
4 teaspons chili powder
2 teaspoons paprika
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan over medium low heat.
Stir frequently until cheese melts, then reduce heat to low or warm for twenty minutes.  Do not allow the mixture to boil.
Serve hot with tortilla chips for dipping.

Submitter: 
Thisni Caza
Description: 
Our appetizing cheese dip with seasoned beef.  Served with warm tostada chips.  From Top Secret Restaurant Recipes 2 by Todd Wilbur.  The ingredients are from the cookbook but the cooking method is slightly revised.
