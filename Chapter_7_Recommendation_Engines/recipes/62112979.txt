Title:
Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1/2 tsp cumin
dash red pepper
3 16-oz cans black beans
1 1/2 cups low sodium chicken broth
2 cups mild salsa
2 tbsp lime juice
1 can tomato sauce
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Cook onion, garlic, cumin, and pepper in olive oil till tender. Turn off heat.
Puree 1 can of beans with chicken broth and add to pot. Stir in remaining beans, salsa, lime juice, and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.

Description: 
My favorite summertime soup. Pretty easy to make and has lots of flavor. Enjoy!
