Title:
Mexican Style Deep Dish Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pkg (7.5oz) refrigerator buttermilk biscuits
1 1/2 cups salsa, divided
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1/3 cups chopped green pepper
1/3 cups chopped onion
1 small can sliced pitted ripe olives or 1/3 cup from
large can
1 garlic clove pressed
Hamburger that has been cooked and cooled. I used about a 1/2 lb and put in taco seasonind

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375'. Cut biscits into quarters into a large bowl. Stir in 1 cup salsa, 2 cups of cheese, bell pepper, onions, olives and the cooled meat along with the garlic, mix well. Spread into the greased 9 x 13 dish. Pour remaining 1 cup of cheese on top. Bake 30-35 mins.
