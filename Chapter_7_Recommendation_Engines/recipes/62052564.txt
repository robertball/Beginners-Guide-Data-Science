Title:
penne with pumkin and italian sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil, plus 1 tsp
1 pound bulk sweet italian sausage or casing removed from links.
4 cloves garlic, cracked and chopped
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 bay leaf, fresh or dried
4-6 sprigs sage leabes, chopped
1 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup canned pumpkin
1/2 cup heavy cream
1* tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
slat and pepper to taste
1 pound penne pasta
romano cheese for grating

Directions:
Heat a large, deep nonstick skillet over medium high heat.  Add 1 tbsp of olive oil to the pan and brown the sausage and crumble.  Transfer sausage to plate covered with paper towel to drain.  Drain fat from skillet and return pan to stove.  Add the remaining tsp oil, and then the garlic and onion.  saute 3-5 minutes until the onions are tender.  Add bay leaf, sage and wine to the pan.  Reduce wine by halr, about 2 minutes.  Add broth, and pumpkin and stir to combine, stirring sauce til it comes to a bubble.  Return sausage to pan, reduce heat,and stir in cream.  Season sauce with the cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and pepper to taste.  Simmer 5-10 minutes to thicken sauce.
Cook pasta to package directions, and add bay leaf.  Drain and remove bay leaf.  Return to pot.  Pour the sauce into pasta and toss over low heat for 1 minute.  Garnish with a lot of grated cheese and sage leaves.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
This is a lovely dish for the fall and you can't tast the pumpkin but it gives it a very smooth quality, very good.
