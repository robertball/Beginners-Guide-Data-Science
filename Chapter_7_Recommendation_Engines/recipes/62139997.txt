Title:
Angels Best Ever Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Flour (all-purpose or bread flour works well too)
1 Cup Corn Meal
1/3 Cup Sugar
2 Tsp. Baking Powder
1/4 Tsp. Salt
1/4 Cup Oil
2 Eggs (large)
1 14.75 Oz. Can Creamed Corn

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 and grease 8 or 9 inch pan.
Combine all dry ingredients.
Stir in milk, eggs, oil and creamed corn.
Pour batter in prepared pan.
Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Cookin_Queen
Description: 
Thick and moist cornbread that goes well with any soup or chili. A old recipe from my Grandma Bea that I have put my own twist on.
