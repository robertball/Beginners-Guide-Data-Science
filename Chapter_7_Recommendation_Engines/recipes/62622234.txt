Title:
Gluten Free Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/572223.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 small onion, diced
24 oz. Prego Traditional Spaghetti Sauce
8 oz. Hunts 100% Natural Tomato Sauce
4 oz. Schar GF Penne pasta
8 oz. Sargento Shredded Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook noodles as package directs; drain and set aside.
Brown ground beef with onion. (Drain grease if necessary).
Add spaghetti sauce, tomato sauce, and cheese.
Combine sauce mixture with pasta.  Pour into 2 qt. oblong Pyrex dish.
Bake uncovered at 350 for approx. 20 minutes or until bubbly along edges.

Our favorite GF spaghetti with good balance of sauce and noodles.
