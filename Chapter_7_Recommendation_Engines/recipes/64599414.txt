Title:
Savory Slow Cooker Grean Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 strips bacon, rough chopped
2 1/2-3 pounds fresh green beans, picked over and cut into 1 1/2 inch segments
5 cups chicken broth
1 large red  onion, sliced on the diagonal
3 tablespoons white vinegar
3 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon black pepper ( or more to taste)
1 table spoon seasoned salt, such as Lawrey's (or more to taste

Directions:
Sauté the bacon in a small pan over medium high heat until almost crisp, not fully cooked.
Place the green beans, broth, onions, vinegar, sugar, garlic, and seasonings into the crock pot.
Add the bacon and stir to fully incorporate.
Turn slow cooker on high and cook one hour.
Turn slow cooker down to low and cook another seven hours.
Taste and season accordingly.
Serve in a large bowl with some added broth.
