Title:
Italian Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 tsp. and 2 cups of cake flour
1/3 cup butter - softened
1 1/4 cups sugar
2 egg yolks
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup low-fat buttermilk
1/4 cup finely chopped pecans, toasted
1 tsp. coconut extract
1 tsp. vanilla extract
6 egg whites
Frosting:
1 tbsp. butter - softened
1/2 cup low fat cream cheese
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Prepare 2 (9") round cake pans by lining bottoms with waxed paper, spraying with cooking spray and then dust with 2 tsp. flour.
Place butter in a large bowl and beat until creamy.
Gradually add sugar and mix well.
Add yolks one at a time.
Combine flour, baking soda and salt.
Alternately add flour mixture and buttermilk to butter mixture, beginning and ending with the flour mixture.
Stir in pecans, coconut extract and vanilla.
In another bowl, beat egg whites on high speed until peaks form.
Fold whites into batter and pour into prepared pans.
Bake for about 23 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool 5 minutes on wire rack then loosen sides of cake from pan and remove.
Remove waxed paper and cool completely.
For frosting, mix butter and cream cheese until fluffy.
Gradually add powdered sugar, mixing until smooth but do not over beat.
Add vanilla and mix.
Spread one layer with 1/3 of frosting and top with other layer.
Spread remaining frosting on cake.
