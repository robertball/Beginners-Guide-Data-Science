Title:
Cucumber relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 small or 8 large cucumbers
12 medium sized onions
1 red and 1 green pepper
1 cauliflower
BRINE
5 cups vinegar
1 cup water
6 cups white sugar
1 tbsp celery seed
6 tbsps dry mustard
3/4 cups flour

Directions:
Grind together first four ingredients and soak one hour in 5 cups water and 1/2 cup salt.  Drain Well
COOK TOGETHER
vinegar, water white sugar, celery seed, mustard and flour.
Cook till thick then add vegetables and cook till tender. (Keep stirring)
