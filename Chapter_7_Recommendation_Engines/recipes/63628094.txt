Title:
Nectarine Arugula Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009085.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh arugula or baby spinach
4 cups torn Bibb or Boston lettuce
3 medium nectarines, sliced
2 TBS pine nuts, toasted
2 TBS crumbled blue cheese
2 TBS raspberry vinegar
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1/8 tsp salt
Dash of pepper
3 TBS olive oil

Directions:
In a large salad bowl, combine the first 5 ingredients.
In a small bowl, whisk the vinegar, sugar, mustard, salt and pepper until blended.
Gradually whisk in oil until dressing thickens.
Drizzle over salad; toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Sylvia Marchesi Walton
Description: 
Here is a summer salad that brightens any supper.  The homemade dressing with a hint of raspberry is perfect with arugula, nectarines and blue cheese.
