Title:
Cheesy Squash Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium zuchini squash, sliced
3 medium yellow squash, sliced
1 large white onion, sliced and separate the rings
2 large tomatoes, sliced
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1/2 block of velveeta cheese, sliced
salt and pepper

Directions:
In a 13 x 9 inch casserole dish, place one layer of zuchini squash, then top with one layer of yellow squash, tomatoes, and onion rings.  Top with pats of butter, then salt and pepper to taste.  Repeat with a second layer.  Bake at 375 for 30 minutes.  Remove from oven, drain off juice and top with slices of velveeta cheese, then return to oven until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Debbie
Description: 
This side dish always creates oohs and ahhs at mealtime.  The flavors of the squash and tomatoes baked together are a delicious combination and the cheese melting over the top leaves my kids wanting more and more...and they hate squash, lol.
