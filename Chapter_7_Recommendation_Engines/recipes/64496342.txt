Title:
Shaina's Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2609791.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Chuck roast- cut into chunks
2 white onions-cut into wedges
1/4 cup of Knorr powdered beef bouillon
1 1/2 T. Salt
1 1/2 T. Pepper
4-5 Idaho Potatoes cut into chunks
2 cans of green beans or fresh green beans  "if using fresh greens add to potatoes to boil"
1 can of carrots or 1 1/2 cups of fresh carrots
1 can whole kernel corn
1 can of peas
1 can of diced tomatoes
2- 15.25oz. Cans of tomato sauce
1 white onion "diced to add to potatoes while boiling"

Directions:
For the pressure cooker you will need:
2 lbs. Chuck roast- cut into chunks
2 white onions-cut into wedges
1/4 cup of Knorr powdered beef bouillon
1 1/2 T. Salt
1 1/2 T. Pepper
(Add cut carrots to pressure cooker if desired)
Pressure cook beef for 45mins.or longer depending on how large the beef is cut down.
When beef is finished cooking use about 6 cups of the beef broth in stock pot to boil your potatoes add a fresh diced onion to beef broth as well. Keep your beef inside of remaining beef broth inside the pressure cooker until ready to shred.
4-5 Idaho Potatoes cut into chunks
2 cans of green beans or fresh green beans  "if using fresh greens add to potatoes to boil"
1 can of carrots or 1 1/2 cups of fresh carrots
1 can whole kernel corn
1 can of peas
1 can of diced tomatoes
2- 15.25oz. Cans of tomato sauce
When potatoes are soft add all vegetables to remaining broth. Stir in tomato sauce and add shredded beef to your stock pot. Add salt and pepper to desired taste.
Simmer about 45mins stirring occasionally.
