Title:
Molded Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh cranberries
1 whole orange
1 cup sugar
1 pkg. lime jello
1 pkg. lemon Jello
1 cup hot water
1 cup diced celery
1 apple, diced
7 oz. crushed pineapple, not drained
1/2 cup pecans

Directions:
Grind cranberries and the orange in a meat grinder. Add sugar and let stand 2 hours.
Dissolve gelatin in hot water, cool. Add the rest of the ingredients. Pour into 9x13 glass dish or jello mold. Refrigerate until firm.
