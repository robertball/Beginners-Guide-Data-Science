Title:
Beef Ribeye Roast (bone in) With Mushrooms & Potatoes &Pan Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-4-5lb. ribeye roast with bone in
-1.5-2lbs. small potatoes
-1lb. small sliced mushrooms
-6 cloves of garlic
-2-14oz. cans of beef broth
-1-14oz. can of low sodium beef broth
-2tbsp. tomato paste
-2tbsp. butter
-3-4tbsp. olive oil
-3-6tbsp. olive oil
-Uncle Chris's Steak Seasoning(or whatever meat seasoning you prefer

Directions:
-cut slits in roast and insert sliced cloves of garlic all over the roast and then rub 3-6tbsp. of olive oil all over and sprinkle a generous amount of seasoning on all sides. (this can all be done up to 24hrs. before cooking)
-before cooking allow roast to come up to room temperature(approx. 2 hours to do this)
-preheat oven to 350
-pour can of low sodium beef broth in roasting pan and place roast on roast roasting rack of pan
-put thermometer in middle of roast
-put in oven for 40min.
-put potatoes in bowl and pour 3-4tbsp. of olive oil over them and then season them with same seasoning used on meat
-after 40min. of cooking roast, place potatoes in the pan below the meat
-cook 20min.
-after 20min. add mushroom slices in pan with potatoes
-cook 40-50min
-an internal temp. of 350 will give you a medium rare roast
-once desired internal temp is reached, remove roast from oven and wrap in foil and allow to rest for 15-20min.
-place potatoes and mushrooms in separate container
-pour the remaining juices that are in the pan into a small sauce pan (removing any fat) and add 2 cans of beef broth
-bring to a boil and then reduce heat and simmer for 3min.
-add butter and tomato paste and stir well
-slice roast and pour gravy on top of meat, potatoes, and mushrooms

Description: 
Delicious and fairly easy way to cook a roast in the oven with mushrooms and potatoes, as well as gravy.   You will need a roasting pan with rack and an electronic thermometer is very helpful.
