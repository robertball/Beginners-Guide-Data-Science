Title:
Cod with Italian Ragout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 6 Filets of Cod
4 Tbsp Olive Oil
4 Tbsp Butter
Kosher Salt & Fresh Ground Black Pepper, to taste
2 Tbsp Italian Seasoning, divided
1 Can Diced Tomatoes
1 Can Cannelini Beans, drained & rinsed
1 Can Quartered Artichoke Hearts, drained
1 Large Red Onion, chopped

Directions:
Heat large skillet over medium high heat. Add oil and butter, wait until butter is melted. Season butter mixture with salt, pepper and half of the Italian seasoning. Add fish to skillet and cook for four minutes. Turn cod over and cook four minutes more. Remove cod to a plate and cover with foil. Add remaining ingredients to skillet and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve immediately, place cod on plate and cover with bean mixture.

Submitter: 
Christine W
Description: 
I created this dish because my husband and I are trying to eat high protein and low carbs - similar to the South Beach diet. I knew that these ingredients would work well together and thus the birth of this meal.
