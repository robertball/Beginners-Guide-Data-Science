Title:
Big Ray's Tomatoes and Beans Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123833.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup virgin olive oil
4 cloves grlic, thinly sliced
2 cans cannellini beans, drained and rinsed (15 oz.)
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, seeds squeezed out
1 cup chicken broth
freash ground pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet over medium heat.  Add garlic and cook until golden, about 5 minutes.  Add beans and salt and saute for about 5 minutes.  Add tomatoes and saute until tomatoes begin to wilt, 5 more minutes.  Add broth and simmer until liquid is reduced by two-thirds.  Add pepper.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This can serve as a side dish, in place of potatoes, or makes a great main course.
