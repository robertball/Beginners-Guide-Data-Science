Title:
French Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066572.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/3 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
In a medium mixing bowl, stir together flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, nutmeg and salt.
Make a well in the center of the mixture.
Stir together egg, milk and 1/3 cup melted butter.
Add egg mixture to flour mixture; stir until just moistened (batter may be lumpy).  Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
Bake at 350Â° for 20 to 25 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine 1/4 cup sugar, cinnamon.
When muffins are finished baking, dip tops of muffins in 1/3 cup melted butter, and then in the cinnamon sugar mixture.
Serve warm.
