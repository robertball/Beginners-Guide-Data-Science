Title:
Canned Cucumber salad pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 large cukes
5 large onions
salt
1C sugar
2C vinegar
1tsp salt
1tsp pepper
1tsp mustard seed
1tsp celery seed.

Directions:
Slice and lay in large pan. Sprinkle with salt in layers. Soak one hour then drain and squeeze by hand. Then add sugar, vinegar, and spices. Let it all come to a boil.  Pour into 5pint jars that have been washed and sterilized. Process jars in hot water bath.
