Title:
Minty Mango-berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365638.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of frozen mango chunks, slightly thawed
1/2 cup of frozen sliced strawberries, slightly thawed
1 6 oz container of lime yogurt
1/4 cup 100% orange juice
10 torn fresh mint leaves
Crushed ice if desired

Directions:
Place the mango chunks, strawberries, yogurt,  orange juice, and mint leaves in a blender or food processor.  Blend or puree until smooth.  If desired, thin with some crushed ice until desired consistency is reached.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Baker
Description: 
This mint-flecked smoothie starts sweet and ends with a fresh mint flavor.  It's great for Spring & Summer, when garden mint is at its peak!  Add crushed ice to thin, if desired (I prefer thicker smoothies).
