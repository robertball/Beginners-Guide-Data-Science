Title:
Two Hour Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups warm water
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup oil
2 1/2 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. vinegar
2 Tbsp. Fermipan yeast
Flour to make a soft dough

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Knead well, oil top and let rise 15 min.
Punch down and repeat 4 times.
On fifth rise, make into loaves. Grease top and let rise 1 hour.
Bake at 350 for 40 -45 minutes.
