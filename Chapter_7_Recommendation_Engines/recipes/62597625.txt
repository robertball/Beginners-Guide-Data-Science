Title:
Spinach Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-8 large flour tortillas
16oz frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained well
1/4c mayonnaise
1/2c cream cheese, softened
1/4c sour cream
1/8c minced onion
1/4c bacon bits
1T Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Mix all filling ingredients.
Spread small amount of spinach ingredients on tortilla.
Roll tortilla up and place in plastic bag.
Continue until filling is used up.
Refrigerate 4 hours or longer.
Cut into slices to serve.
