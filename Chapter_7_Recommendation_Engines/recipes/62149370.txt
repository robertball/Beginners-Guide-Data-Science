Title:
Carolyn’s Passion Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/852513.jpg
Ingredients:
4 fish fillets (washed and patted dry)
¼ tsp. cinnamon
¼ tsp. cumin
¼ tsp. cardamom
¼ turmeric
¼ tsp granulated garlic
¼ kosher salt
¼ fresh ground black pepper
Sauce
1 stick of butter (not margarine)
Juice of 1 lemon
½ tsp. garlic- minced
½ c. leaks (cleaned thoroughly gown in sand)

Directions:
After fish has been cleaned and patted dry, mix the spices and sprinkle on both sides of  fish.
Melt butter and add garlic and leaks, first cut in half wash, then in thin slices horizontally, (making sure to only cut up to the bottom of the green leaves)
Cook until transparent, making sure to stir and don’t burn, add lemon juice.  Cook until sauce thickens, then pour over fish and bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes (depending on the oven).
Let rest for 5 minutes, then serve

Submitter:

Description: 
(Any flaky, white fish can be used i.e. Tilapia, Trout, Red Snapper, Catfish, Sea Bass etc) As always the spices make the dish different, tasty and yes stimulating. If prepared correctly this savory dish will promote a more healthy digestive system, as well as excite the palate.
