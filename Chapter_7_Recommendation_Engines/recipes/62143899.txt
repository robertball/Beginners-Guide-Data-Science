Title:
Easy Buttery Breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 cups flour
1 tbs baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 cup milk
1 tbs parsley flakes (optional)

Directions:
Melt butter in microwave
Pour melted butter into 9X13 pan
Combine flour, baking soda, salt and parsley in bowl and mix in milk.  If too sticky add more flour
Drop dough onto floured surface, knead a few times
Roll dough out into 9x13 rectangle
Place dough in buttered pan
Using a knife or pizza cutter cut dough into strips
Bake in 425 oven for about 12 minutes or until nice and brown.
