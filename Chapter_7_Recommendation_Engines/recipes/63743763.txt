Title:
Creamy Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg light Philly cream cheese
1/2 cup dry white wine
2 green onions finely chopped
1/4 tsp each....tarragon leaves
....freshly chopped garlic
....dijon mustard
....fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and heat over low heat stirring constantly until blended
Pour over chicken breast, fish or steaks
Garnish with parsley

Submitter: 
Margaret Byrne
Description: 
Easy to make sauce to dress up any main dish meat
