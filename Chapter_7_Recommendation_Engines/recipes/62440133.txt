Title:
Focaccia Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 cup warm water
2 pkg. active dry yeast
¼ cup Epicure focaccia bread spices
1/3 cup olive oil
¾ cup hot water
2 tsp. salt
4 cups flour

Directions:
Dissolve 1 Tbsp. sugar into 1 cup warm water. Stir in 2 pkgs. of active dry yeast. Let sit 5 minutes.
Soften ¼ cup focaccia spices in 1/3 cup olive oil and ¾ cup hot water. Cool to lukewarm, add 2 tsp. salt and combine with yeast mixture.
Stir in 4 cups flour, one cup at a time. Knead dough on a floured surface until smooth. Let sit 5 minutes.
Brush baking sheet with olive oil. Roll dough onto pan (cut into 2 loaves). Brush wih olive oil and let rise 40 minutes. Add a drizzle of oil and brush with focaccia spices and sea salt (optional).
Bake at 425 for 20-25 minutes until golden.
