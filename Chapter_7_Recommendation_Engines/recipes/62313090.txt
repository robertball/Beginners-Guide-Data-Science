Title:
Best London Broil Ever! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) top round London broil
2 12 OZ. CANS OF DICED tomatoes
garlic PEPPER, OREGANO, BASIL, PEPPER, HERBED GARLIC SALT.

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place the roast in a roasting pan, pour DICED tomatoes over it, and season with garlic salt.
Roast for  3 hours, until fork tender. Let stand for about 10 minutes at room temperature before carving and serving.
