Title:
Cola Pot Roast I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1 cup Coke
1 packet dry spaghetti sauce mix
1 pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
3 pounds beef chuck roast
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
In a large bowl, break up tomatoes in their juice.  Stir in cola, spaghetti sauce mix, and onion soup mix. Stir until spaghetti sauce mix is dissolved.
Brown roast in oil about 10 minutes on each side. Drain off all fat. Place in large crock pot.  Pour tomato mixture over meat. Cover, and cook on low for eight hours.

Submitter: 
Suzanne Goodenow
Description: 
Tomatoes, cola, and spaghetti sauce mix are the starring ingredients of this pot roast, which may be cooked in advance and reheated.Modified from: Cola Pot Roast I
