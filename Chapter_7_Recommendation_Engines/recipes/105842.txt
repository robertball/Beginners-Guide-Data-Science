Title:
CB Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 cup chocolate liqueur
1 cup coffee flavored liqueur
1 cup vodka

Directions:
In a container with a lid, stir together the chocolate liqueur, coffee flavored liqueur and vodka. Cover and store in the freezer. Stir before serving chilled in shot glasses.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chocolate liqueur, coffee liqueur and vodka blend into a smooth and chocolaty shot.
