Title:
Simple Salmon Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4569800.jpg
Ingredients:
4 oz fillet of salmon
1/4 c Italian bread crumbs
minced garlic
salt & pepper (optional)
flour for dusting
2 T olive oil
burger buns
mayonnaise
fresh greens such as romaine or spinach

Directions:
Cut salmon into chunks and place in food processor. Add to processor the minced garlic, bread crumbs, and salt and pepper as desired. Pulse until very finely chopped. Form into 2 burgers and chill in fridge for 20 minutes. Then dust both sides of burger with flour and fry in oil at medium high high for 2-3 minutes per side. Add to bun and add mayo and lettuce.

Submitter: 
DOMMETT
Description: 
Cooked at the right temperature, this burger is ever so slightly crispy on the outside and delicious on the inside
