Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2453194.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz canned oysters
2 eggs
Milk
Flour
Salt
Baking Soda
Cooking oil (such as canola or vegetable oil)

Directions:
1. Fill deep pan with oil, heat medium high
2. Cover plate with flour (no particular amount, this is for dredging)
3. Pour flour into mixing bowl.  Add a pinch of salt and a small amount of baking soda.
4. Drain juice from oysters into mixing bowl.  Set oysters aside on cutting board
5. Add eggs to mixture. Mix well
6. Cut oysters in half
7. Dredge oysters in the flour on the plate, cover with batter, place in hot oil and fry until golden brown.  BE CAREFUL!

Submitter: 
Lon Bacon Teter
Description: 
Whip batter quickly, this helps it remain fluffy.  Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
