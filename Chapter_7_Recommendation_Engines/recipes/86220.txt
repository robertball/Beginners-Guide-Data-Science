Title:
Craig's Cocktail Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6020285.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (36 ounce) bottle ketchup
3 tablespoons steak sauce
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
6 tablespoons lemon juice concentrate
3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
15 drops hot pepper sauce, or to taste
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Squeeze ketchup into a bowl. Pour in steak sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice. Stir in horseradish, hot pepper sauce, and salt, and mix well.

Submitter: 
CDSN99
Description: 
This is a cocktail sauce I've been making for over 20 years. Hope you enjoy. Use a kitchen funnel to pour the sauce back into the ketchup bottle -- it will last a long time.
