Title:
Lite Bananas Foster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982582.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon trans-fat free margarine
1 tablespoon sucralose sweetener and brown sugar blend
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Pure Vanilla Extract
1/4 teaspoon Spice Islands® Ground Saigon Cinnamon
1 pinch Spice Islands® Ground Nutmeg
3 firm, ripe, 7 to 8-inch bananas, peeled, halved and sliced lengthwise
1/4 cup Karo® Lite Syrup
1 tablespoon rum OR banana liqueur (optional)
Vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Melt margarine in a large skillet over medium heat.  Stir in brown sugar blend, vanilla, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Add lite syrup when mixture begins to bubble; gently stir in bananas.  Cook bananas for about 1 minute per side until hot.  Remove from heat and serve immediately over ice cream.

Submitter: 
Karo® Lite
Description: 
An elegant, classic dessert, these subtly spiced bananas are cooked quickly in syrup then served over vanilla ice cream.
