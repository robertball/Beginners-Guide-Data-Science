Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/866340.jpg
Ingredients:
4 bananas, sliced
3 cups milk
1 box Nilla Wafers
2 small boxes instant FRENCH vanilla pudding
1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
1 14oz. can sweetened condensed milk
1 12 oz. container Cool Whip

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the milk and pudding mix and blend well using a handheld electric mixer.
Using another bowl, combine the cream cheese and condensed milk together and mix until smooth. Fold the whipped topping into the cream cheese mixture.
Add the cream cheese mixture to the pudding mixture and stir until well blended.  Pour the mixture over the cookies and bananas.
Cover with the remaining cookies.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
