Title:
chicken cheddar stuffed tomato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium-large tomato
1 cooked boneless skinless chicken breast, cubed
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, cubed
1/2 cup creamy salad dressing(low fat is fine)
salt and pepper to taste
optional:
breadcrumbs

Directions:
Cut tops off tomatoes and a thin slice off the bottom so they stand upright in the oven. Hollow out the tomato and reserve pulp. Chop the reserved pulp into cubes and place in bowl with cubed, cooked chicken and cheddar cheese. Add the dressing (ranch, caesar etc.) and salt and pepper. mix and stuff into tomatoes. If using breadcrumbs, sprinkle on top. Place in baking dish(lightly oiled if not non-stick) and bake at 350 for 15 minutes. Turn to broil and continue till golden brown.
