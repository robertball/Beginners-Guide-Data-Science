Title:
Peanut Butter Balls - AKA Fat Pills Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
2 c. crunchy peanut butter
3 1/2 c. powdered sugar
2 c. ground special K cereal
12 oz. chocolate chips
1/4 bar of paraffin wax

Directions:
Mix all of the above and roll into small balls.
Chill.  Dip balls into melted chocolate.
