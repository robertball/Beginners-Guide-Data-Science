Title:
Zero-Cream of Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1111988.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. Olive oil
2 C. onions chopped
1 stalk celery chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. salt
1 large head broccoli, par boiled
5 c. chicken or vegetable broth
1/3 c. quick cook oatmeal
1 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. fresh dill
1/2 tsp. ground pepper
Optional:
Fresh chives minced for garnish
Thinly sliced lemons for garnish

Directions:
In large stock pot, heat oil and saute chopped celery and onions until tender, about 10 minutes over medium heat. Stir often.
Chop broccoli into small chunks (including small stems)
Reserve about 1 cup of chopped broccoli and 1/2 cup chicken broth and set aside. Add 4 cups of chicken broth to sauted vegetables and bring to boil. Lower heat and let simmer about 5 minutes.
Add oatmeal (or other thickening agent) and simmer gently 10 minutes or until broccoli is tender and soup has thickened. Remove from heat and add lemon juice, fresh dill and pepper.
Puree soup in small batches in blender (or use an emersion blender in stock pot)until soup is smooth. Microwave reserved broccoli in the chicken broth until tender. Stir in and warm soup to serving temperature over low heat.
Serve with chive and lemon garnish!

Submitter: 
Dylan's Nana
Description: 
This soup is creamy and delicious without all of the fat and calories of traditional Cream of Broccoli soup. If you're gluten free, use a thickener that suits your diet. 
Enjoy!
