Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Beef and Bacon Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982444.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 1/2 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal, crushed
1/4 cup finely grated onion
1/4 cup chili sauce
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
6 slices bacon
6 burger buns

Directions:
In large bowl, mix together all ingredients, except bacon and buns, until well combined. Shape into 6 patties.
Wrap bacon slice around each patty, overlapping at top. Place on broiler pan. Broil about 6 minutes per side until meat is cooked to desired doneness and bacon crisps. Serve immediately on toasted buns. Garnish as desired.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Seasoned with mustard, Worcestershire and chili sauce, these burgers are wrapped in bacon and broiled for a juicy, flavorful lunch or dinner treat.
