Title:
Chicken and Wild Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 box fast cooking long grain and wild rice
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
12 oz Mountain Dew
1/4 cup margarine or butter
small package slivered almonds
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix together soups, soda and the rice mix, including the seasoning packet.
Pour into a 9 x 13 greased baking dish.
Lay chicken breasts on top of rice mixture.
Drizzle melted margarine/butter rice/chicken.
Sprinkle slivered almonds across rice/chicken.
Sprinkle rice/chicken with parmesan cheese.
Bake at 300 for 45-60 minutes, checking at for doneness at 45 minute mark. Chicken should be golden brown on top.
Serve chicken breasts with rice as a side dish. Goes great with fresh green beans
