Title:
Spinach Pierogi Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz can pizza crust
1 9 oz. pkg frozen spinach, thawed, and squeezed to drain
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 tbs butter
1 1/2 cup mashed potatoes
2 tbs oil
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 small onion, slice and separated into rings

Directions:
Heat oven to 425. Grease 13x9 inch pan. Unroll dough and place in greased pan, starting in center, press out with hands. Bake at 425 for 7-9 minutes, until golden brown. Meanwhile, in large skillet cook spinach and chopped onion in butter until onion is crisp tender. Stir in potatoes,  mix well. In small bowl, combine oil and garlic salt. Brush over partially baked crust, top with spinach mixture. Sprinkle mozzarella and cheddar cheese. Top with onions. Bake at 425 for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust edges are golden brown.
