Title:
Seafood Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1020281.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups raw potatoes, cubed
2 cups carrots, diced
1 cup celery, chopped
2 tablespoons onion, finely chopped
2 teaspoons parsley flakes
Seasoning salt and pepper, to taste
1 pound Velveeta cheese, cubed
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cups milk
12 ounces crab meat, chopped
12 ounces lobster meat, chopped
12 ounces baby shrimp

Directions:
Combine potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, parsley and seasonings in a large pot with water and cook until tender.  DO NOT LET BOIL DRY.
When tender and most of the water is gone mash gently with a potatoe masher.
In a separate pan over low heat, melt cheee with soup and milk.  Once all the cheese is melted, add crab/lobster meat, and shrimp, stir thoroughly.
Combine vegetables and cheese sauce together, add more milk if soup is thicker than you would like it to be.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Serve with warm brotchen on a cold winter night.
