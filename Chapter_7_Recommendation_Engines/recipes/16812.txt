Title:
Easy Lovely Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F103341.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
4  lemons, halved
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon curry powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place chicken pieces in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Squeeze lemon halves over the chicken, then sprinkle with the garlic, curry powder and salt and pepper to taste.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Stephanie Lewis
Description: 
This makes a lovely, lemony chicken. It bakes in one dish, and comes out juicy and tender.
