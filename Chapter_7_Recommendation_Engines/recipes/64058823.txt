Title:
Basil Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages fresh basil leaves, no stems
3/4 cup EVOO
1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
2 cloves crushed garlis
1/4 cup pine nuts

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bender until smooth. Toss with warm, cooked pasta for a simple and delicious dinner
