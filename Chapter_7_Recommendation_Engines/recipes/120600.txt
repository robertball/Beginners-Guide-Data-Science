Title:
Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981051.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups broccoli florets
1 medium carrot, shredded
2 cups shredded red cabbage
1/2 cup raisins
1 small sweet onion, chopped
1 (16 ounce) bottle coleslaw dressing

Directions:
In a serving bowl, combine all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
Konny  Thomas
Description: 
Here's a new twist on traditional coleslaw. It's easy to make and so delicious.
