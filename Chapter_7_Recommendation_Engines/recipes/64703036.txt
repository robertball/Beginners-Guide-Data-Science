Title:
Apple Pecan Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5410375.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. salt
4 eggs
3 1/2 cups flour
1 Tbsp. baking powder
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup milk
4 cups apples
1 cup pecans
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
In large bowl, cream butter, sugars, vanilla, salt and eggs together.
In separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder and cinnamon.
Alternate adding flour mixture and milk to creamed butter mixture.  Do not over mix!
Fold in apples and pecans.
Fill prepared muffin cups 3/4 full.
Make topping by combining sugar, cinnamon, flour and butter until crumbs form.
Sprinkle topping over muffins.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, until done in the centre.
