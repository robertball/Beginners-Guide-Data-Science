Title:
Pasta with Tomato, Andouille & Bocconcini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz Andouille Sausage
2 cup? of Red Wine
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
4 sliced green onions
2 cloves minced garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
4 cups cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
1/4 cup fresh basil or parsley
4 cups scoobi doo or penne pasta
1 cup pearl bocconcini cheese

Directions:
Pierce sausages and place in skillet, add enough red wine to cover the sausages 1/2-3/4 of the way, boil until wine is evaporated
Brown sausages and remove from skillet
In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta until tender but firm
Meanwhile, Heat oil over medium heat and fry onions, garlic, salt & pepper until softened (about 3mins)
Add tomatoes and cook until softened (about 8mins)
Add tomato mixture, sliced sausages, bocconcini and basil/parsley to drained pasta and toss to coat
