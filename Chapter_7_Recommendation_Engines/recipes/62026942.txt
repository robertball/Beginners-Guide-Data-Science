Title:
Mocha Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
1 c margarine
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 c white sugar
2 tbsp chocolate syrup
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp mocha or cappucino flavored powder
mixed with 2 tbsp hot water
18 ounces chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions:
350 degrs. Mix dry ingredients and set
aside. Cream margarine, sugars, chocolate
syrup and vanilla until smooth. Add eggs
one at a time, beat for 5 minutes. Stir
in dry ingredients, coffee mixture,
chocolate chips and nuts. Mix with wooden
spoon. Bake 12 - 14 minutes
