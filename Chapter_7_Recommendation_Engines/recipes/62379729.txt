Title:
Two Berries and a Grape Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/421277.jpg
Ingredients:
16 whole almonds
1/4 cup oats
1 tablespoon flaxmeal
1 ripe banana, peeled and frozen
1 cup blueberry yogurt
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
1/2 cup sliced fresh or frozen strawberries
1/2 cup concord red grape juice
3/4 cup 1% buttermilk
1/2 teaspoon agave syrup or 1 tsp honey *(optional)

Directions:
Place almonds, oats and flaxmeal in a blender and pulverize. Break banana into chunks and add to blender along with remaining ingredients. Blend until smooth. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Alberta Rose
Description: 
Featuring blueberries and strawberries, this smoothie mixes concord red grape juice (which is high in disease fighting antioxidants) with oatmeal, flax and almonds for a mildly sweet smoothie with a lot of flavor and a nutritious boost.
