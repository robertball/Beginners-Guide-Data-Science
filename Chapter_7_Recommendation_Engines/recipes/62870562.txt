Title:
Grilled Tuna Santa Fe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c fresh lime juice
1/2 c beer
3 T oil
4 t cumin
4 t dijon mustard
1/2 t salt
4 cloves garlic, minced
4 tuna fillets
salsa fresca
2 ripe tomatoes
1/2 onion
1/2 c green bell pepper
3 garlic cloves
1 jalapeno
2 aneheim chilis,
salt
1 bunch cilantro

Directions:
Combine lime juice, beer, oil, cumin, mustard, salt and garlic in a large shallow dish. Marinate fillets for 2 hours, turning occasionally. Preheat grill. Grill fillets for 5 minutes on each side. Serve with salsa fresca.
Mix ingredients for salsa fresca and store in airtight container for up to 5 days.
