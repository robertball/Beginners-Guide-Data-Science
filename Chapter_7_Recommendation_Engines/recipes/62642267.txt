Title:
Valentine Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 white cake mix
eggs
oil
water
1 can prepared frosting
Pam baking spray
1 8" ROUND cake pan
1 8" SQUARE cake pan

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350&#0176;
Spray the cake pans
Mix up the cake following package directions
Pour batter evenly into the pans and bake in 350&#0176; oven as directed on package.
Cool in pans for 5 minutes, then remove and place on racks to cool completely.
Get a large platter, tray or cover a piece of cardboard with heavy duty foil.
Place the square cake on an angle like a baseball diamond, cut the round cake in half and place 1/2 on each side of square cake.
Frost with the can of icing, adding a few drops of red food coloring if desired.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Here's an easy way to make a heart-shaped layer cake for your Valentine celebration.
