Title:
Christmas Cane Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4233293.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (.55 ounce) package instant hot chocolate mix
1/2 cup hot water
2 fluid ounces vanilla-flavored vodka
1 fluid ounce peppermint schnapps
1 fluid ounce creme de cacao
ice

Directions:
Empty hot chocolate mix into a cup. Stir hot water gradually into the mix. Chill hot chocolate to room temperature, about 10 minutes.
Mix chilled hot chocolate, vanilla-flavored vodka, peppermint schnapps, and creme de cacao together in a bowl. Pour into a glass filled with ice.

Submitter: 
soon2bmrsdmh
Description: 
This is the perfect drink for your Christmas cocktail parties! I serve it pre-mixed in a pitcher and let the guests pour it over ice. It is so simple to make, and very cost effective for a crowd.
