Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, diced
1 Tbsp chili powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
Lettuce
Corn chips
Cheese sauce
Sour cream
Tomato wedges
Black olives

Directions:
In large frypan, brown ground beef, onion and spices until done.  Do not overcook.
Serve over shredded lettuce and corn chips.  Top with cheese sauce.  Garnish with sour cream, tomato wedges and black olives.
