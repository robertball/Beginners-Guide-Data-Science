Title:
Sam's Favorite Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef (80% lean or leaner)
1/3 c. italian style bread crumbs
1 egg
1 T. worchestershire sauce
1 tsp. each of onion powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well (with hands yields best results).
Form into 4-6 firmly packed patties, depending on desired thickness.
Grill, or if desired, place on sprayed cookie cooling rack on baking sheet.  Bake in 400 degree oven for 18-22 minutes.  We use this recipe for meatballs as well, yielding about 20 meatballs.  Bake them in the oven in the same manner.

Submitter: 
Linda In Da Kitchen
Description: 
I named these burgers for my son Sam . . . who wouldn't eat burgers before I came up with this recipe.  When we can't grill them due to weather or time factor, I bake them in the oven on a cookie cooling rack on a baking sheet.
