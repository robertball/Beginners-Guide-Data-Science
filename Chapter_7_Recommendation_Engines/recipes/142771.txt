Title:
Garlic and Basil Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/253484.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
2 tablespoons chopped fresh garlic
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2/3 cup olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Combine the basil, garlic, balsamic vinegar, and Parmesan cheese in a blender. Blend until ingredients form a paste. Slowly pour olive oil into the mixture and continue to blend until smooth.
Add salt and pepper to taste. Whisk before serving.
