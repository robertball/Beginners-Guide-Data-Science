Title:
Mom's Mexican Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
24 oz jar Southwest Salsa
8 oz cheddar cheese
16 oz corn bread mix (My mom uses Marie Calendars Corn Bread Mix Original)
sour cream

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400
Brown ground beef over medium high heat.
Mix ground beef and whole jar of salsa in a greased 13x9x2 glass baking dish.
Sprinkle cheese evenly over mixture.
Mix corn bread as directed on back of box, and poor evenly over cheese.
Place in heated oven for 30-35 minutes or till a toothpick poked in center comes out free of cornbread.
Serve with a dollop of sour cream on top.

Submitter: 
SHACKL
Description: 
VERY quick to prepare! Everyone in the family loves it. Ground beef and Southwest Salsa (it has corn and black beans in the salsa) with a layer of cheese over it and topped with Corn bread. MMM MMM tasty!
