Title:
Three (or Four) Ingredient Oatmeal Banana Breakfast Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4417027.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripened bananas
1 3/4 cup instant oats
1/4 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1/4 cup walnuts or almonds (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mash bananas in a medium bowl.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
Drop by rounded tablespoon onto a baking sheet lined with wax paper.
Bake for 15-20 minutes.
