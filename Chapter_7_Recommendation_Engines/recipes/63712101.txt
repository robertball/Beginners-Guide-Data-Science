Title:
mustard zucchini relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups zucchini, grated & packed tight
4 cups onions, shredded & packed tight
1 large red pepper, shredded
5 cups sugar
1 tablespoon turmeric
2 1/4 cups cider vinegar
1 tablespoon nutmeg
2 teaspoons celery seeds
1 tablespoon mustard seeds
2 tablespoons ground mustard
1/2 tablespoon black pepper

Directions:
Place vegetables in a large bowl and cover with cold salt water (3 Tablespoons salt). Let stand overnight. Drain off water and rinse with cold water, drain well.
2
In large pan, combine sugar, vinegar, and spices. Bring to a boil; add vegetables and cook 30 minutes.
3
Fill prepared jars with hot relish. Seal and process 15 minutes in hot water bath. Makes 5
