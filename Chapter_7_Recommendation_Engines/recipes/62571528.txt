Title:
Shrimp or Clam Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of cream cheese
1 med. container sour cream
1 T. lemon juice
1 T. worcestershire sauce
1 can of med. deveined shrimp or 1 can clams

Directions:
Blend cream cheese and sour cream till smooth.
Add lemon juice and worcestershire sauce.
Fold in shrimp or clams.
Serve with potato chips.

Submitter: 
Julie Moon
Description: 
The most simple and tasty chip dip.
