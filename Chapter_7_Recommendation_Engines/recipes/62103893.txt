Title:
3 packets and a roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet Ranch style dressing mix
1 packet italian dressing mix
1 backet brown gravy mix
1 3 pound beef roast
3 cups water
Cut up veggies (optional)

Directions:
Throw everything into a crockpot and cook on low for 8-10 hours.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
This slow cooker roast is combined with 3 packets of special seasoning mixes to create a delicious gravy and a tender roast.
