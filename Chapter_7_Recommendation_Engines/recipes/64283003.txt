Title:
1-2-3 Complete Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Greek yogurt
1/4 cup steel cut oats - uncooked
1/4 cup water, almond or soy milk or low-fat milk
1/2 cup frozen mixed berries (blueberries, blackberries, strawberries, raspberries, cherries - in any combination)
1 scoop flavored or unflavored protein powder
honey, sugar, Splenda, stevia to taste

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a blender jar and puree until smooth.

Submitter: 
AnnInLondon
Description: 
An easy, fast, high fiber and high protein breakfast or anytime smoothie.  Choosing your fruits, yogurt and protein powder flavors will make this your personal customized recipe.  This smoothie will keep you going until lunch time!
