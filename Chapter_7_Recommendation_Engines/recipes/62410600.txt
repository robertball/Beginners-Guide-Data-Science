Title:
Melon Maple Frozen Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced very ripe cantaloupe
1 cup diced fresh pineapple
2 tablespoons maple syrup
1/2 cup turbinado or granulated sugar
3 cups low fat vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.  Freeze in an ice cream freezer according to manufacturer's instructions.  Serve immediately or place in freezer.
