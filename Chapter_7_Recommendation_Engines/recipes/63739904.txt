Title:
Home-Style Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (40 oz.) can Bush's © Pork and Beans
1 (16 oz.) can great northern beans, with juice
1 (16 oz.) can stewed tomatoes
1 medium onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
1 cup dark brown sugar
1 cup ketchup
1 lb. bacon

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients in a baking dish. Top with the bacon. Bake in a 350° preheated oven, uncovered, for 3 hours.

Description:
