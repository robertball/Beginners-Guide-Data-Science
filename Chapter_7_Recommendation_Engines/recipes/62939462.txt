Title:
Snicker Bar Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725637.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cup flour
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup water
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup snicker bars,very finely chopped
Frosting:
1(8oz) pkg cream cheese, softened
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup butter softened
2 oz. unsweetened chocolate, melted & slightly cooled
1(8oz) box of powered sugar
Optional: Additional 1/2 cup of coarsly chopped snicker bar to garnish top of cake

Directions:
Sift together flour, salt, baking soda and baking powder. Set aside
In a large mixer bowl,cream together butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time and mix well.
Add the flour mixture alternating with water and buttermilk.
Mix on low until combined, then on high for 3 minutes.
Mix in vanilla flavoring.
Stir in 1 cup of finely chopped snickers bars.
Pour into 2 greased and floured 9 inch round pans. *Do not over fill pans if using 8" pans. The cake will not cook well in the middle.  Use a bundt or tube pan instead or make cup cakes with the extra batter.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Prepare frosting while cake is cooling.
Melt 4 oz. unsweetened chocolate over low heat stirring constantly. Set aside to cool.
Cream together the cream cheese, butter, vanilla flavoring and melted chocolate.
Gradually add the powered sugar and beat until smooth and fluffy.
Frost cake once it is completely cooled.
Optional: Garnish with additional snicker bar pieces.
