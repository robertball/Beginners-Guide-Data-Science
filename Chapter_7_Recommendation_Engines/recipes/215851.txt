Title:
Easy Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993326.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 pound lean ground beef
16 ounces Classico® Tomato and Basil Sauce
1 pound spaghetti
1 cup shredded mild Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In large skillet, cook hamburger until brown.  Mix pasta sauce into skillet.  Reduce heat and simmer.
Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Mix in pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Mix together spaghetti and meat mixture, pour into 9x13 pan.  Top with cheese and bake for 30 minutes, or until heated through and cheese is bubbly.
