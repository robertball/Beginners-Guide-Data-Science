Title:
Macadamia nut Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage finely chopped or pre-packaged coleslaw mix
2 large carrots shredded
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 chopped Macadamia nuts
2 cups pineapple(optional)
pinch of pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and let sit in refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
Garnish with extra Macadamia on top before serving.
