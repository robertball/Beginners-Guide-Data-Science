Title:
Avocado and Ham Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1104227.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded Romaine lettuce
1/2 cup mixed salad greens
1/2 cup arugula
1/2 cup cooked mixed beans, drained
1/2 cup chopped cucumber
1/3 cup chopped sweet red pepper
3 radishes, sliced
1 tomato, sliced
1 small avocado, peeled, pitted, and chopped
1 slice ham, chopped

Directions:
Toss Romaine lettuce, mixed salad greens, arugula, mixed beans, cucumber, sweet red pepper, radishes, tomato, avocado, and ham together in a large bowl. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
PaulaM11
Description: 
This is really light and refreshing. It's also very filling. The mixed beans I used were left over from the night before, but if you are making them fresh, soak them overnight in water, then boil for at least 20 minutes. Leave to cool before adding to the salad.
