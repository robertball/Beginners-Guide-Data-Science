Title:
Brown Sugar Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 TBLS Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1/2 cup milk
2 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 small onion, chopped or 1 packet of onion soup mix
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1 cup of seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
In small bowl mix ketchup, brown sugar and worcestershire sauce, set aside.
In a mixing bowl, mix thoroughly all remaining ingredients and shape into a loaf.
Place loaf in pan and top with the ketchup mixture.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.
If top starts to burn cover with tin foil.
