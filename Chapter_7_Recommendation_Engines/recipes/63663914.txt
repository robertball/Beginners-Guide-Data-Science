Title:
Worlds Greatest Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz. Bush's baked beans
1lb. Lean ground beef (cooked and drained)
6 strips of bacon (cooked and crumbled)
1tsp. White vinegar
1Tbsp. Prepared yellow mustard
1 1/2 c ketchup
1 cup light brown sugar
1/4 c barbeque sauce
1 tbsp. Liquid Smoke
1 tbsp. A1 steak sauce (optional)
1 envelope dried onion soup mix
1 tsp. garlic

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Marinate 24 hours. Bake for 1 hour uncovered at 350 degrees.

Submitter:

Description: 
This simple, hearty baked bean recipe gets rave reviews whenever and wherever you bring it! It's an awesome side dish and lots of my friends eat it all alone....sometimes cold!
