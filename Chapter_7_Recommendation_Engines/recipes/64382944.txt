Title:
Three Week Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb cabbage, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
2 med. onions, chopped
2 c. sugar
1 c. cooking oil
1 c. cider vinegar
2 Tbsp celery seed
1 Tbsp salt

Directions:
Combine cabbage, pepper, onion, and sugar; mix well.  Mix oil, vinegar, celery seed, and salt in a pan, and bring to a boil.  Pour over cabbage mixture and stir until very well mixed.  Store in refrigerator.  Let stand for 2 or 3 days before serving.
