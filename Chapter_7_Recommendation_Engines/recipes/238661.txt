Title:
Strawberry Peach Parfait Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1306673.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped fresh peach
1 cup chopped old fashioned donuts
1 (6 ounce) container strawberry yogurt

Directions:
Divide about 1/4 cup chopped peach into two small cups or bowls, followed by a layer of about 1/4 cup donuts. Spread about 1/2 of the yogurt over donuts, then repeat layers once more.

Submitter: 
Ali1999
Description: 
Super tasty for a breakfast, a quick snack, or even dessert. Great for those days were you can't seem to stop thinking about the ice cream in your freezer.
