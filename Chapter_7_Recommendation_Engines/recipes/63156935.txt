Title:
BEEF BARLEY VEGETABLE SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-3 lb. beef chuck roast
1/2 cup barley
1 bay leaf
1 onion chopped
3 stalks celery chopped
1 16oz. frozen mixed vegetables
1 48 oz. can beef broth
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
28 oz can stewed tomatoes

Directions:
In a slow cooker roast meat  until very tender about 4 hours on high.  Remove meat and set aside.
Drain drippings into a measure cup and refrigerate or freeze.
In the crockpot add broth and return meat cut up in bite size pieces.  Add celery, onion, mixed vegetables until tender. About an hour.  Add pepper, salt, stewed tomaotes and barley.  Remove broth from refridgertor and take off the risen fat and discard.  Replace the broth and heat to a simmer.
