Title:
Easy Raspberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of frozen raspberries
1/3 cup of water
1 tablespoon of granulated sugar
1 tablespoon of instant clear gel or cornstarch

Directions:
Put a saucepan on medium-high heat and add raspberries
Stir raspberries until heated through and mushy
Stir in water and sugar until well combined and syrupy
Add gel or cornstarch and stir until thicker and turn heat to low
Continue stirring until thick

Submitter: 
Kidchen
Description: 
A quick and simple raspberry jam for cookies, cakes, sandwiches and more!
