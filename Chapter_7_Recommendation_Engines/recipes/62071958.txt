Title:
Fruity Pudding Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 ounce bowl Whip Topping
1 3.4 oz. Box Cheesecake instant pudding and pie filling
1 12 oz. Bag Berry Medley Frozen Berries (Blueberries, Strawberries, Blackberries, Red Raspberries)
1 10.5 oz. Bag Jet-Puffed Mini Marshmallows (Multicolored)
1 20 oz. Can crushed Pineapple
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In Medium Bowl stir Whipped topping and pudding mix together.
Add in Bag of Frozen Berries and stir till fruit is coated.
Add in Bag of Mini Marshmallows and stir till coated.
Add in can Crushed Pineapple including juice and stir thoroughly. Stir in chopped walnuts.  Refrigerate 30 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Dawn Raegene Mendenhall Scott
Description: 
Creamy and fun to make for the kids.  It is a great summertime treat and very sweet for satisfying a sweet tooth.
