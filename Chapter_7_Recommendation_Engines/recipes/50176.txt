Title:
Friday Night Special Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1117937.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces citron vodka
1 teaspoon deluxe Canadian whiskey
1 dash lemon juice

Directions:
Pour the vodka into a shaker, cover, and shake well. Pour the whiskey into a well chilled martini glass, followed by the vodka. Squeeze in a dash of lemon juice. Enjoy and savor.
