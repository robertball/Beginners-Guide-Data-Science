Title:
Mandarin Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Cottage cheese, small curd
1 lg box Orange Jello, dry
1 pkg Dream Whip, prepared as directed (or use 8 oz Cool Whip, slightly thawed)
1 sm can Mandarin orange segments, well drained
1 sm can crushed pineapple, well drained (opt)

Directions:
Whip cottage cheese with blender or food processor until smooth. (Optional - just for smoother texture). Set aside.
In small bowl, blend prepared Dream Whip (or Cool Whip) with dry jello.  Mix well. Fold in cottage cheese.  Add *well drained* mandarin orange segments and pineapple, if desired.
Refrigerate 45 minutes to 1 hr or until set.
