Title:
Vegan Apple Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(Dough)
2 cups all purpose flour
2-1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
4 tbsp earth balance original buttery spread (or any other vegan margarine of your preferance)
1 cup water
(Apple Marinade)
2 apples (yellow apples taste best with recipe)
1/2 cup of sugar/brown sugar
1/2 cup water
4 tbsp earth balance butter (melted)
1 tsp vanilla extract
tsp ground cinnamon

Directions:
(Dough)
1. whisk together all dough ingredients. wait until mixture is in fine crumbs to add water.
2. seperate dough into 10 pieces
(Apples)
1. preheat oven to 350 F
2. peel apples and slice into eighths
3. boil apples for 5 min, or until soft but not mushy
4. cool apples, add lemon to prevent browning (optional)
5. in small bowl, combine all apple marinade ingredients and microwave for 30 seconds, or until butter has dissolved in mix
6. soak apples in marinade for 10 min
7. take 1/10 of dough and and flaten into even circle
8. 1 apple slice cut into fourths to one dumpling, wrap dough around apple pieces
9. butter baking pan and place all 9-12 dumplings on
10. drizzle remaining apple marinade onto dumplings on pan
11. bake for 15-30 min until golden brown
12. serve hot, and enjoy!
