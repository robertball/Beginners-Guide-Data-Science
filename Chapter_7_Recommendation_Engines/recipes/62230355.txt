Title:
Calves Liver with honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of calves liver (about 6 ounces each and 1/2" thick)
flour for dusting
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
2 Tbsp of sherry vinegar or red wine vinegar
2-3 Tbsp of chicken broth
2 Tbsp of honey
salt and pepper for seasoning
watercress sprig to garnish

Directions:
Wipe the liver slices with a damp paper towel, then season both sides with a little salt and pepper and dust the slices lightly with flour, shaking off any excess
In a large heavy frying pan melt half the butter with the oil over high heat and swirl to blend
Add the liver slices to the pan and cook 1-2 minutes on each side. Transfer to a warm plate and keep warm
Stir the vinegar, broth, and honey into the pan and boil for about 1 minute, stirring occasionally then add the remaining butter, stirring until melted and smooth. Spoon over the liver slices and garnish
