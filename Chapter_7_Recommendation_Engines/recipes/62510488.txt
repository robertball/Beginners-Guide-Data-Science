Title:
Hello Dolly Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566989.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 cup shredded coconut
1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup walnuts, chopped
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a 13 by 9 inch baking pan mix together butter and graham cracker crumbs. Press into bottom of pan.
Pour 1/2 of the milk over the crust. Sprinkle the coconut, chocolate chips, and walnuts in layers evenly over. Gently press all the ingrediants down with your hands. Then drizzle the rest of the milk over the top.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool completely before cutting into squares.
