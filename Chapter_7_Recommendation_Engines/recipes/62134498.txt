Title:
Amazing Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1/2 stick of butter or margarine
1 tsp of butter flavoring
3-3 1/2 cups of powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream shortening and butter together.
Add butter flavoring to mixture.
Add powdered sugar about 1/2 to 1 cup at a time.  Use up to 3 1/2 cups until desired consistency for decorating.
Add frosting to disposable decorating bags, and begin decorating!

Submitter: 
jody
Description: 
This is the best buttercream frosting recipe that is great for decorating, as it holds its shape and tastes delicious!
