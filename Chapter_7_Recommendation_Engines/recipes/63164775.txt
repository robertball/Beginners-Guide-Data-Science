Title:
Julie's basil-y marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans stewed tomatoes
large can diced tomatoes
yellow onion
2 cloves elephant garlic
4 tbs basil
1 tbs oregano
tsp marjoram
tsp parsley
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp rosemary
tbs olive oil
3/4 cup red cooking wine
1/2 cup chicken stock
tsp brown sugar

Directions:
dice onion and mince elephant garlic
mix with basil, oregano, marjoram, parsley, thyme and rosemary. olive oil, red wine and chicken stock. Simmer until wine is reduce by 50%.
Stir in tomatoes and brown sugar.
cook on low for 3 hours. Blend in food processor and serve with favorite pasta
