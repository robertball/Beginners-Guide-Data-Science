Title:
Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. flour
dash pepper
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. white pepper (optional)
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Make a white sauce and cook over hot water in a double boiler until the mixture has thickened to desired consistency. Add seasonings and cheese, stirring until cheese has melted.
This is a good sauce to use over vegetables or add to elbow macaroni for mac and cheese.
