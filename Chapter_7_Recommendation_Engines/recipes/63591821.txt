Title:
Roasted Rosemary Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic, smashed and peeled
2  pounds red potatoes, cut in 1/4ths
3 tablespoons olive oil
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
2 tablespoons fresh rosemary leaves (about 2 sprigs)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Rub a cookie sheet with the smashed garlic the discard. One the sheet, toss potatoes, olive oil, salt and pepper together. Roast for 25 to 30 minutes. Turn potatoes  so they release from the pan and sprinkle the potatoes with the fresh rosemary.  Continue to cook the potatoes until golden brown and tender (10 to 15 minutes).
