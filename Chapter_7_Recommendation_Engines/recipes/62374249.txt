Title:
Grandpa's Stuffed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 to 5 lb pork loin
1/4 c Old Bay Seasoning
1/4 c Olive oil
2 boxes of seasoned pork stuffing
6 large carrots, cut into spears
4 stalks celery, cut into spears
2 large onions, chopped
3/4 c. chicken broth
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Prepare ahead: Pork chop stuffing mixture according to the box directions.
Cut loin into chops one inch thick; slice to halve the chop nearly through creating the pocket for the stuffing.
Sprinkle both sides of chops with Old Bay seasoning.
Brown chops in Roti pan with olive oil.
Fill the chops with stuffing.  Lay any remaining stuffing around chops.
Add carrot and celery spears with chopped onion.
Pour 3/4 c chicken broth over top.   Salt and pepper to taste.
Bake covered with foil 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Remove the foil, bake an additional 30 minutes.
