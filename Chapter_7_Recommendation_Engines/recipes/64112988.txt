Title:
Piquant Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
1/4 tsp pepper
1 can chicken broth
3 tbsp lime juice
1 tbsp sugar
1 tsp dijon
4 tsp cornstarch
2 tbsp water

Directions:
Sprinkle chops with pepper. Cook chops until browned on both sides.
Stir in broth, lime juice, sugar and mustard. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low. Cover: simmer 10 minutes or until chops are no longer pink.
Transfer chops to platter. In cup, stir cornstarch and water until well blended, gradually stirring into skillet. Cook over high heat until mixture boils and thickens, stirring often.
