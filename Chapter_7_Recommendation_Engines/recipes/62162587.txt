Title:
Famous Restaurant Alfredo Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated parmesean cheese
1/2 cup grated romano cheese
6 jumbo egg yolks

Directions:
1. Pour milk and cream in saucepan and heat until it begins to simmer. Turn off heat. Slowly whip in cheese then remove from heat.
2. Place egg yolks in seperate bowl and slowly whip in a portion of the hot milk and cream mixture. Slowly add egg yolk mixture back into the remaining cream mixture. Place back on very low heat and continually stir until simmering. Take sauce off heat to thicken. (This will tempure the egg yolks)
3. Season to taste with salk and pepper and serve over your favorite pasta.
