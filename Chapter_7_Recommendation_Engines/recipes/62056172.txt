Title:
Italian Spinach Soup with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 quarts chicken broth
2 stalks celery, cut into chunks
2 carrots cut into chunks
1 large onion, quartered
teaspoon salt
40 frozen Italian meatballs
1 (16 ounce) can crushed Italian style tomatoes, undrained
1 (16 ounce) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons dries basil
4 garlic cloves, crushed

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large pot.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, uncovered 30 minutes.  Remove vegetables and discard.  Bring broth to boil; add meatballs.  Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.  Stir in tomatoes and remaining ingredients and simmer 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
halehp64
Description: 
This hearty soup is easy to make and always a big hit.  Serve it with a nice crusty bread.
