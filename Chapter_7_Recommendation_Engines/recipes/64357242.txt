Title:
Chinese Oven-Fried Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
3 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp sherry (or water)
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp garlic powder
4 pork chops
1/4 cup cracker crumbs (or bread crumbs)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In small bowl, beat egg, soy sauce, sherry, ginger and garlic powder together.
Take the pork chops and dip in the egg mixture, then coat with cracker crumbs.  Place on a greased cookie sheet.
Bake for 30 minutes.  Turn and bake 20 minutes longer or until chops are tender and show no pink.  Do not overcook.
