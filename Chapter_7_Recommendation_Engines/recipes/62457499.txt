Title:
anything quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1/2 cup baking mix
1/4 cup melted oleo or butter
2 cups cottage cheese
1 cup shredded cheese
1 cup browned & drained meat
1 cup steamed veggies

Directions:
mix together with hand mixer  eggs
baking mix
butter
set aside
fold together                 cottage cheese
cheese
meat
veggies
fold cottage cheese mixture into egg mixture
spray 2 8 inch pie plates or 1  9 X 13
fill pans and let rest 10 minutes
bake @ 350 degrees  42-45 minutes

Description: 
you can start fresh or use up the leftovers in this makes-its-own-crust quiche:-)
