Title:
Vanilla Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water, plus a pot of water to sterilize jars
2 cups milk powder
¼ cup sugar
½ Tbsp. vanilla
¼ cup plain yogurt

Directions:
Boil 1 cup water.
Add 2 cups milk powder, 2 cups cold water, sugar, vanilla, plain yogurt.
Stir very well till all lumps are gone.
Bring to 115 F.
Swirl glass jars in a separate pot of boiling water to sterilize them.
Fill sterilized jars with yogurt mixture.
Wrap jars in towels on counter, or place in pan of warm water for about 5 hours or until thickened.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This delicious recipe is healthier than most store-bought yogurts.
