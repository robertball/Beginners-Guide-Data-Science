Title:
Creamy Banana Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4457033.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 scoop vanilla ice cream
1 ripe banana
1 pinch ground cinnamon, or to taste
2 tablespoons whipped cream, or more to taste
1 tablespoon caramel topping (optional)

Directions:
Combine milk, ice cream, banana, and cinnamon in a blender; blend on low speed until consistency of a thick milkshake. Pour milkshake into a cup and top with whipped cream and drizzled caramel.
