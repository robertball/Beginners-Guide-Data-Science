Title:
Easy Tiramisu Modified LAF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 egg yolks
1/3 cup white sugar
3 tablespoons vanilla extract
16 oz. mascarpone cheese
1 angel food cake
1 1/4 cups brewed coffee
1.5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
5 egg yolks 1/3 cup white sugar 3 Tbsp vanilla extract 16 oz Marscarpone cheese 1 angel food cake 1 cup strongly brewed instant coffee 1.5 Tbsp cocoa powder

Directions:
In a medium bowl, beat yolks with sugar and vanilla until smooth and light yellow.  Fold mascarpone into yolk mixture.  Set aside.
Place slices of angel food cake in the pan, then spoon 2 teaspoons of coffee per piece over the bottom layer.  Put a layer of filling, and then a layer of cake, this time with 3 teaspoons of coffee per piece.
Spread remaining mascarpone mixture over the cake.  Cover and chill 1 hour.  Sprinkle with cocoa just before serving.

Description: 
Mascarpone is sweetened with sugar and vanilla and made richer with egg yolks, then layered with coffee soaked angel food cake and chilled in this no-cook tiramisu. 9 by 13 pan with angel food cake substitutionModified from: Easy Tiramisu
