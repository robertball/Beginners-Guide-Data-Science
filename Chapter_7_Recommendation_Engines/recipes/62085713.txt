Title:
Supper Subs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 submarine or French rolls, cut in half lengthwise
Miracle Whip or Mayonnaise
1 pkg. (16 oz.) sliced meats, any variety
12 slices of American Cheese
Lettuce Leaves
2 large tomatoes, sliced

Directions:
Spread roll halves with mayonnaise or miracle whip.
Layre bottom half of rolls with remaining ingredients. Cover with top half of roll.  Serve with Macaroni and Cheese or with Cottage Chips.
Note:
You can also serve this with carrots and celery sticks complemented by ranch dressing.
