Title:
Pasta Fagioli Soup by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3530904.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef  or turkey or sausage
1/2 onion, chopped
1-2 clove garlic, minced
Garlic powder, Alpine Touch, salt, pepper
3/4 cup carrots, sliced
1/2 cup celery
3/4 cup squash
3/4 cup bell pepper
1/4 bulb fennel
1 can cannelloni beans
3/4 cup diced tomatoes (or 1 can)
1 can or jar of ready made spaghetti or marinara sauce (24-26 ounces)
4 cups beef broth
1 1/2 teaspoon ground oregano
3/4 tsp Tabasco
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 1/4 c dried pasta, cooked as directed

Directions:
Brown beef or tukey with onion, season to taste, add garlic towards the end
Mix all together except pasta, simmer on low 30-60 minutes or 4-6 hours in crockpot
Add cooked pasta when ready to serve
