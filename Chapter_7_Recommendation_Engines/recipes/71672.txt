Title:
BUSH'S® Italian Bean Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984041.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15.8 ounce) can BUSH'S® Great Northern Beans, rinsed and drained
1 large ripe tomato, diced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon black pepper or red pepper flakes
24 slices (1/2-inch thick) French or Italian bread, grilled or lightly toasted*

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients except bread; mix well.
Spoon mixture on bread and serve.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
A healthy twist on traditional bruschetta that is full of flavor.
