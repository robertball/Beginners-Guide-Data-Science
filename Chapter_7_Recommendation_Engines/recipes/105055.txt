Title:
Vitamina Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2384934.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups diced mango
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup milk, or as needed

Directions:
Place condensed milk, mango, and vanilla extract in the bowl of a blender.  Blend until smooth, adding milk as needed until the consistency is a little thinner than a milkshake.

Submitter: 
BrazilCook
Description: 
This drink is good for hot and cold days. It is very Brazilian.  All the measurements are approximate but even if they are a little off it is still delicious.
