Title:
Campfire Pizza Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices white bread
2 tsp canned pizza sauce
5 pepperoni slices
2 Tbsp shredded mozzarella cheese
butter flavored cooking spray
Pie Irons (hobo pies...campfire pies)

Directions:
Spray the inside of each pie iron with cooking spray.
Place a slice of white bread inside each.
Top each slice with 1 tsp pizza sauce.
On one side only, place pepperoni and mozzarella cheese.
Close pie iron.
Hold above coals in campfire to toast bread and melt cheese.
Be careful--the pizza pie is hot inside!

Submitter: 
Debbie B.
Description: 
Easy pizza in a pie iron--kids can make it themselves! A favorite around our campfires!
