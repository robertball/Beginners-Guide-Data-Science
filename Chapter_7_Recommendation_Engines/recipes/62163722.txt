Title:
Mrs. Wixom's Heavenly Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
3 Tablespoons cocoa
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 cups buttermilk
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 Tablespoons flour
1 cup heavy cream
2 eggs, beaten
1 Tablespoon vanilla
1 cup pecans
1 can angel flaked coconut
1/4 cup butter, melted (1/2 stick)

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla.
Add buttermilk to baking soda set aside.
Add all dry ingredients together and beat in alternating with buttermilk until all ingredients are incorporated.
Pour into greased and floured 9x13 inch baking pan.
For frosting, mix sugar and flour together.
Add cream and eggs.
Cook on low heat until mixture is thick, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and add nuts, coconut, vanilla, and 1/2 stick of melted butter.
Cool mixture and spread on cake.

Submitter:

Description: 
My best friends mom made this cake and I fell in love with it.  Before I left for college I made sure to get the recipe.  I love the coconut in the frosting.  This is one of my most cherished recipes.
