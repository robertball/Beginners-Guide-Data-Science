Title:
Butter Cheese Drop Bisquits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
10 tablespoons butter
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 tablespoons fresh parsley
1 tablespoons fresh chives
2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
Parchment paper

Directions:
Heat oven to 425*
Place flour,baking powder,baking soda and salt into food processor
Pulse to combine
Cut 6 tablespoons of butter into cubes
Place cubed butter and cheddar cheese into food processor and pulse 6 times.Mixture will still be coarse and lumpy
Pour in buttermilk, pulse enough to moisten
Drop ice cream scoop sized balls of dough onto parchment paper lined cookie sheet
Mix parsley and chives with 2 tablespoons of butter
Brush  Chive Parsley mixture on biscuit drops
Place in oven for 10-15 minutes
Mix remaining two tablespoons butter with two tablespoons Parmesan cheese
When golden brown remove from oven, brush with remaining two tablespoons of butter and Parmesan cheese
Serve!

Submitter: 
POPS OHIO
Description: 
Buttermilk biscuits in less than 30 minutes. Perfect with any meal. Warm from the oven, rich butter and Parmesan flavor.
