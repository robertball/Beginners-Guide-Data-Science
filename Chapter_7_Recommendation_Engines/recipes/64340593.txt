Title:
Vegan Butter Bean Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can butter beans, drained
1 small onion, chopped
1 tablespoon finely chopped jalapeno pepper
1/2 c bread crumbs (gluten free preferably)
1 sub (3 T chia seed in 1/4 water) or 2 T corn starch in 1 T water
1/2 cup nutritional yeast opt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mash butter beans. Mix in onion, jalapeno pepper, crumbs, egg sub, yeast, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Divide into 4 or 6 equal parts, and shape into patties.
Cook in a large skillet over medium-high heat until golden, about 5 minutes on each side or bake at 400 for 15 min on each side.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Butter beans mashed and combined with onion, jalapeno pepper, bread crumbs, then shaped and fried into vegetarian burgers. You can substitute bell pepper for the jalapenos for a milder taste.Modified from: Butter Bean Burgers
