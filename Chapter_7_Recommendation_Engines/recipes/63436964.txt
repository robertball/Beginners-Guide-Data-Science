Title:
VEGGIE BARS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large tubes crescent rolls
8 oz. cream cheee
1 cup cottage cheese
3/4 c. mayonnaise
1 pgk Ranch dressing mix
1/2 chopped green pepper
1 c. finely chipped broccoli
1 c. cinely chopped couliflower
1 tomato, seeded and choppped
1/2 c. chopped green pepper

Directions:
Unroll dough and spread on ungreased jelly rool pan or 1 or 2 cookie sheets.
Bake 400 degrees for 10 minutes.  Cool.
Mix cream chees, cottage cheese, and mayonnaise with ranch dressing mix.
Spread on baked dough.  Top with veggies. Chill overnight.
Cut into bars.
