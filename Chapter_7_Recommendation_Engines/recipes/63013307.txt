Title:
Frozen Orange Grand Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Envelopes plain gelatin
1/4 Cup boiling water
1/4 Cup cold water
1 1/3 Cup orange juice
6 Egg yolks
2 Cup Granulated Sugar
2/3 Cup Grand Marnier Liquor
2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream

Directions:
Soften gelatin in cold water, then stir in boiling water and stir until dissolved.  Beat OJ with egg yolks.  Beat in sugar and Grand Marnier.  Stir in gelatin.  Beat until it starts to thicken. Beat cream to soft peak stage; fold in carefully to thickened gelatin mixture.  Blend, don't let orange juice settle. Pour into a glass trifle dish; freeze ovefr night.  Thaw before serving; garnish with strawberries, orange slices or sliced Kiwi.
