Title:
Chicken Veggie Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 lasagna noodles
1 lb. chicken
1 24 oz. jar marinara sauce
1 24 oz. container fat-free cottage cheese
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 egg
2 Tbsp minced garlic
1 yellow onion
1 5 oz. bag of baby spinach
1 zucchini
15 baby carrots
1/2 cup chopped broccoli
1 red bell pepper
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tsp dried basil
1 Tsp dried oregano
1 Tsp onion powder
black pepper to taste
optional - crushed red pepper flakes to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Poach chicken in salted water (about 20 minutes).
While chicken is poaching, dice the onion, pepper, carrot, and zucchini, and chop the spinach.
In a large saucepan, saute garlic, onion, spinach, pepper, carrot, and zucchini in olive until onions are translucent.
In a large bowl, mix cottage cheese, 1 cup of shredded mozzarella, 1 egg, 1 Tsp each dried onion powder, basil, and oregano, and black pepper and red pepper flakes if desired.
When chicken is cooked through, roughly chop and mix in with vegetables.
Prepare lasagna noodles according to package directions.
In a 9x13 baking dish, spread each layer evenly as follows: 1/4 cup marinara sauce , 3 noodles, 1/2 of veggie and meat mix, 3 noodles, 1/4 cup sauce, 1/2 cheese mixture, 3 noodles, 1/4 cup sauce, remaining veggie and meat mixture, 3 noodles, 1/4 cup sauce, remaining cheese mixture, 3 noodles, remaining sauce, 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes until bubbly.
Remove foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes.

Description: 
This lasagna recipe is a family favorite.  It is absolutely packed with veggies, but even picky eaters will love it.  For other variations, ground turkey or sausage can be used in the place of chicken, and alfredo sauce can be used in place of marinara.
