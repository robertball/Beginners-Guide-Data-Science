Title:
Chocolate Ganache S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/620622.jpg
Ingredients:
Bottom Graham Cracker Layer:
14 full-size Honey Graham Crackers (crumbled finely)
1 stick of butter (melted)
1/4 cup of white sugar
1/2 tsp. of cinnamon
Middle Layer Chocolate Ganache:
1 cup of heavy cream
9 oz. of bittersweet or semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 Tbsp. dark rum (optional)
Top Layer Marshmallows:
10oz. bag of large Marshmallows

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cover 9x13 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
2. In mixing bowling stir together all Graham Cracker Layer ingredients. Mash any large crackers. Spread mixture evenly on bottom of baking dish pressing down flat. Bake layer for 5 minutes.
3. In saucepan over medium heat bring heavy cream to a boil. Remove from heat immediately once it starts boiling. Whisk in chocolate chips and stir until well mixed (add rum optional). Pour evenly over graham cracker layer.
4. Evenly place all marshmallows on top of Ganache layer. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until marshmallows have expanded to cover the Ganache layer.
5. Turn oven to broil mode. Broil for 1 minute or until marshmallows are lightly toasted.
6. Let cool for 15-20 minutes. Then refrigerate for 45 minutes (or until chocolate has hardened). Cut into bars using a pizza cutter spray with non-stick cooking spray. Serve and enjoy!

Description: 
A scrumptious oven baked dessert to bring on the taste of those yummy campfire graham cracker, chocolate, marshmallow s'mores!
