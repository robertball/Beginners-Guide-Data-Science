Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
7 tbsp. milk
2 tbsp. shortening
2 tbsp. margarine
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, milk, shortening, margarine and salt. Bring to a rolling boil, stirring constantly to prevent scorching. Allow to boil for 2 minutes, without stirring.
Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla and peanut butter. Beat until smooth and frost Peanut Butter Brownies. Wrk fairly quickly, as it sets up quickly!

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
THE Peanut Butter Fudge frosting for the Peanut Butter Brownies! Enjoy!
