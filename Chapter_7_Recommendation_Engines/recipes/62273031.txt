Title:
Slightly sweet light wheat bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/345802.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup warm water
1 egg
1/8 cup honey
1 TB butter
2 TB sugar
1/4 cup vegetable shortening
1 tsp. salt
3 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup bread flour
1 1/2 tsp. vital gluten
2 tsp. bread machine yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients in your bread machine according to manufacturer.
Hit the "dough" cycle button
When bread machine has finished, remove dough and punch down
Roll out and form into loaf
Place in a greased 8x4 loaf pan
Cover and bake in preheated 375 degrees oven for 30 minutes

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This bread is slightly sweet and very moist.  It's a high riser.  It's so good warm with jelly or great toasted.
