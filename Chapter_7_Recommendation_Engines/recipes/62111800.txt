Title:
Strawberry Banana Protein Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c skim milk
1 scoop of vanilla flavored whey protein powder
2 c ice
1 c fresh or frozen strawberries
1 large banana
1 tbls natural peanut butter

Directions:
In blender, add ingredients in order mentioned. Blend until creamy and smooth. serve immediatly in a shaker bottle or glass cup.

Submitter: 
TEVANS211
Description: 
This is a healthy, versatile protein milk shake perfect for a post work out meal, or healthy alternative to an ice cream shake for the kids. Strawberrys and banana's could be substitued with kiwi's, pineapples, pears, mango's, blueberries, etc. Get creative and enjoy!
