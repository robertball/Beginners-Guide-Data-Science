Title:
Hot Wing Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pkgs. cream cheese
8 oz. bottle of Ken's Blue Cheese Dressing
6 oz. Frank's Red Hot Sauce (use more or less depending on personal preference)
3 cooked boneless chicken breasts, shredded
1 bag shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese, dressing and hot sauce.
Stir in chicken.
Spread in large baking dish and cover top with cheddar cheese.
Bake at 350 until bubbly - about 20-30 minutes.
Serve with Triscuits.
