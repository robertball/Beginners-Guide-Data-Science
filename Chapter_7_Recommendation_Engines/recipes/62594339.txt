Title:
Braised Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
ground black pepper to taste
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tsp olive oil
1/2 onion, thinly sliced
2 tbsp 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
1/2 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (6.5 ounce) can mushrooms
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried thyme

Directions:
Season chicken breasts with ground black pepper.
Heat olive oil in a medium skillet, and brown the onion, garlic, mushrooms and seasoned chicken breasts.
Pour tomatoes and balsamic vinegar over chicken, and season with oregano, and thyme.
Simmer until chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear, about 15 - 25 minutes depending on breast thickness.
