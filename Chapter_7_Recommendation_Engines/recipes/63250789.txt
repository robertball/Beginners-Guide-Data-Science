Title:
Stuffed Rolled Round Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of very thin beef round steak
2 cans of spinach
12 dried apricots chopped
4 gloves of chopped garlic
1 cup chopped onion (red is good)
4 inch block of feta cheese
1 cup bread crumbs
2 chopped jalapeno peppers
red wine and rosemary sprigs

Directions:
Drain spinach and squeeze out all the water.  Mix all other ingredients. make six equal piles and spread the mixture on each slice of meat.  Roll up tight and secure with a toothpick.  Place each roll up on top of a rosemary sprig, Pour wine over to about 1/2 inch depth. Bake at 350 for about an hour, basing often.  You can also thicken sauce with a little butter
