Title:
Raspberry Jell-O Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt. Frozen raspberries, thawed and drained (keep juice)
or 1 pkg. Frozen raspberries, thawed
1 pkg rasp. Jell-O (large)
1 reg. Tub cool whip

Directions:
If using jarred raspberries, use juice and add water to equal 2 C.
Heat liquid and add Jell-O.
Let partially set (do not over set) 30 min in fridge or 15 in freezer
Add in raspberries
Fold in cool whip last and let set for at least 2 hrs.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
There is nothing that can beat this recipe when it comes to Jell-O concoctions. It is especially delicious when make with Raspberries from my Grandma's garden.
