Title:
Swiss Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793774.jpg
Ingredients:
5 medium yellow potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1 small onion, thinly sliced
3 garlic cloves, minced
8-10 pieces of bacon (crumbled)
2 cups shredded Swiss cheese, divided
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
2 tablespoons butter or margarine

Directions:
1.
In a greased shallow 3-qt. baking dish, layer a third of the potatoes, onion, bacon, garlic and Swiss cheese; sprinkle with 1/4 teaspoon salt and a dash of pepper. Repeat layers once. Top with remaining potatoes, onion, pimientos, garlic, salt and pepper. Pour broth over the top; dot with butter. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees F for 1 hour.
2.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake 30 minutes longer or until liquid is absorbed and cheese is melted. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
