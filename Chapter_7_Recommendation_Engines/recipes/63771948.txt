Title:
Spicy Sour Stir Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T dark soy sauce
2 T rice wine vinegar
1 T lime juice
quarter red chilli, sliced
1 T Thai minced spices
1 t minced ginger
1 t powdered ginger
1 small bunch fresh coriander leaves, chopped
2 t cornflour
1 T honey (or Splenda)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a mug.
Add to stir-fry at the end of the cooking cycle.
Cook, stirring all the time, until the cornflour has thickened the sauce and the whole stir fry is covered.

Submitter: 
Raven
Description: 
A spicy sour stir fry sauce that will light up any stir fry you make.
