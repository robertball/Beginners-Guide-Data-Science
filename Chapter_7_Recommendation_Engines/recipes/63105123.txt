Title:
Endorphin Releasing Thick Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787976.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c Butter
1/2 c Vegetable Shortening
3/4 c Sugar
1 3/4 c Brown Sugar
3 Large Eggs
1 t Vanilla
3 3/4 c Flour
2 t Baking Soda
1 t Salt
1 12oz bag of Milk Chocolate Chips
1 c Semisweet Chocolate Chips
1/2 to 3/4 c chopped pecans or walnuts if you want

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Lightly grease baking sheets or use silicon mats.
Using an electric mixer, blend the softened butter and shortening until creamy; add sugars and beat until fluffy.  Add eggs and vanilla.
Add flour, baking soda and salt and beat until completely blended.  Fold in chocolate chips and drop by 1/4 cupfuls spaced about 3 in apart.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned.  Let cool a couple of minutes on baking sheets then transfer to wire rack to finish cooling.
