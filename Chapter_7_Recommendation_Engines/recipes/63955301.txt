Title:
Ranch Dressing (pioneer woman modified Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Greek Yogurt plain
1/4 cup chopped parsley (flat or curly leaf)
2 tbsp fresh chives or green from green onions)
1 tsp dried dill weed
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup buttermilk (more to reach desired consistency)
2 tbsp white vinegar

Directions:
Place all ingredients into a bowl and stir. Place in fridge and allow flavors to meld.

Description: 
Ranch dressing without the mayo and sour cream.
