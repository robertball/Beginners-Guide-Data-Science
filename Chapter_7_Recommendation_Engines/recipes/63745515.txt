Title:
Helen and Werner Vogel's Sherried Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 Tbsp butter
1 small onion, chopped
1/4 tsp garlic powder
2- 10.5 oz cans chicken broth
3-10.75 cans cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 cup milk
2 Tbsp sour cream
1 Tbsp parsley
1 tsp chopped thyme
1 cup quick brown rice
1/2 cup dry sherry
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Melt butter over medium high heat
Saute mushrooms, onions and garlic powder
Add broth and reduce heat, simmer 5 minutes
Add mushroom soup, milk and sour cream.  Whisk until incorporated.
Add brown rice, parsley, thyme and pepper
Cook 20-30 minutes or until rice is tender and soup is thick

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
This soup was something that I couldn't get enough of.  I have introduced this to numerous people who love it.  This is great served in the fall and winter.
