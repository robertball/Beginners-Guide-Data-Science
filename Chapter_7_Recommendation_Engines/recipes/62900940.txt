Title:
Poor man's soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/703152.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium white potatoes, peeled and diced
1 lb of ground beef
1/2 cup of chopped celery
1 medium chopped onion
1 tablespoon of minced garlic
1 tablespoon of parsley
1 tsp of rosemary
1 tsp of thyme
1 tsp of oregano

Directions:
In a large saucepan warm 1 tbs of minced garlic and add beef.
Once beef is browned, add 3 cups of water and combine potatoes, chopped onion, celery, parsley, rosemary, thyme, and oregano into the mixture.
Cook until potatoes are tender.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Durham1222
Description: 
A simple meat and potato soup that my husband's father used to make for him when he was a child.
