Title:
One Pot Chicken Fajita Penne Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp oil
3 chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 red bell pepper, sliced
i green bell pepper, sliced
1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
1 onion, sliced
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp cummin
1 tbsp garlic powder
6 cups milk
4 cups penne pasta
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.  Heat oil in a large pot.
2.  Add the chicken and cook until the chicken is thoroughly cooked. Remove chicken from the pot.
3.  Add the sliced peppers and the sliced onions and cook until the onions are translucent.
4.  Add the cooked chicken back into the pot. Add the salt, pepper, chili powder, cumin and the garlic powder and stir well to coat everything evenly.
5.  Add the milk and the penne pasta to the pot. Make sure you stir constantly to prevent the pasta from sticking to the pot.
6.  Cook for 10 to 12 minutes until the pasta is cooked and the milk boils down to a nice thick sauce.
7.  Remove from heat, add the cheese and stir until cheese is thoroughly melted. Spoon into bowls. Garnish with sliced green onions if desired and serve.

Submitter: 
Rob Dickinson
Description: 
A nice thick pasta dish that will stick to your ribs on a cold evening!
