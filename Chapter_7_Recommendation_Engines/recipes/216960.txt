Title:
Grilled Corn with Lime Ponzu Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990896.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ears fresh corn
8 tablespoons butter, melted
1/4 cup Kikkoman Lime Ponzu
4 cloves garlic, crushed
Salt and cayenne pepper

Directions:
Prepare fire in charcoal or heat gas grill. Strip corn husks to the stem without removing them; remove silk to expose kernels. Soak corn in cold water at least 10 minutes. While corn is soaking, combine butter, ponzu and garlic. Remove corn from water; wrap husks back over kernels. Grill corn, turning occasionally, 15-20 minutes or until steamed through. Unwrap husks; spread butter mixture on corn and season with salt and cayenne pepper.

Submitter: 
Kikkoman
Description: 
Fire up the grill for this tasty corn steamed in the husk and spread with citrus seasoned garlic butter.
