Title:
Apple Spice Drops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
•	2/3 cup sugar
•	2/3 cup packed brown sugar
•	1 egg
•	1/4 cup apple juice
•	2 cups plus 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
•	1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
•	1/2 teaspoon baking soda
•	½ teaspoon baking powder
•	1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
•	1 cup finely chopped peeled tart apple
•	1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
•	FROSTING:
•	1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
•	3 cups confectioners' sugar
•	1 teaspoon vanilla extract
•	3 tablespoons apple juice, apple juice concentrate or apple cider
•	Garnish with nutmeg

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Beat in egg and apple juice. Combine the dry ingredients; gradually add to the creamed mixture. Fold in apple and walnuts or pecans.
Drop by teaspoonfuls 2 in. apart onto greased baking sheets. Bake at 375 degrees F for 12-14 minutes or until golden brown. Remove to wire racks to cool.
For frosting, cream butter, sugar, vanilla and enough apple juice, concentrate or cider to achieve spreading consistency. Frost cooled cookies. Garnish with nutmeg as desired.
