Title:
Pumpkin chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/450014.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks butters ( softened )
1 Cup white sugar
1 cup light brown sugar
2 Large eggs
1tsp vanilla extract
1 cup canned pumpkin puree
3 cups all purpose flour
2tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground cloves
2 cups ( one 12oz bag ) milk chocolate chips
non stick cooking spray or parchment paper

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees f . Spray cookie sheets or line with parchment paper .
Using a mixer beat the butter till smooth . Beat in the white and brown sugar until mixture is fluffy . Beat in eggs adding 1 at a time . Then add the pumpkin and vanilla and mix till smooth . In a large seperate bowl whisk together flour ,baking soda ,salt,cinnimon,ginger,nutmeg,and cloves .Slowy beat in the dry mixture into the wet adding a little at a time till smooth and creamy . Stir or fold in chocolate chips . Put bowl in fridge for 5 minutes .  Use small ice cream scoop or tablespoon and scoop out cookie doough onto cookie sheets .  Bake for 15-20 minutes . remove and let cool before removing from sheets or they will stick to each other .
