Title:
Potato Marguerite Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium white rose potatoes, boiled and sliced
6-8 hard boiled eggs, boiled, peeled and sliced
5-6 Tbsp fine bread crumbs
Lawry's seasoning salt
2 pints sour cream
3 Tbsp all-purpose flour
5-6 Tbsp green onions, finely sliced
Butter

Directions:
Mix together the sour cream, flour and green onions.
Spray a 9x12 inch casserole pan with nonstick spray. Sprinkle with 1/2 the bread crumbs and Lawry's salt to taste. Dot with butter.
Add a layer of 1/3 of potato slices and 1/3 of egg slices. Cover potatoes and eggs with about 1/3 of the sour cream mixture.
Repeat with layer of 1/3 of potatoes, salt, dots of butter, 1/3 of eggs and sour cream mixture. Repeat layering.
Top last layer of sour cream with remainder  of breadcrumbs and dot with butter.
Bake at 350 degrees until bubbly.

Submitter: 
morcar
Description: 
Casserole of layers of potato and boiled egg with butter and sour cream. Thanksgiving and Christmas staple at with my husband's family. Great for holiday potlucks. Great way to use up too many eggs.
