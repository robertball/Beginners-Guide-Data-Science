Title:
Spicy Lentil Quinoa Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil or cooking oil
1 cup diced onion
1/2 cup chopped carrots
1 cup chopped mushrooms
3 garlic cloves, minced
4 cups vegetable broth
1 cup water
1 cup dry green lentils (or any kind)
1/2 cup quinoa
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon cayenne
2 tablespoons curry powder
1 tablespoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon tumeric
1 tablespoon cumin
1 teaspoon garam masala (indian spice)
1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
Dash of black pepper (or to taste)
1/4 cup milk or cream
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.
Add diced onion and saute until cooked and translucent (approximately 3 minutes).
Add garlic, carrots, and mushrooms and cook for 2 additional minutes.
Add lentils, quinoa, broth, water, tomato paste, and spices.
Reduce heat to medium-low, cover with lid, and cook for approximately 40 minutes.
Add butter and cream and stir in.
Put lid back, and continue cooking for 5 more minutes, or until lentils are tender

Description: 
Lots of flavor packed in this indian-spiced curry! This recipe is very adaptable and can be made to fit your preferences. You can also change up the veggies you use, alter the amount of spice, etc.
