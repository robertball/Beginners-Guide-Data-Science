Title:
Tangy Vegetable Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip
1 c. cottage cheese
3/4 c. chopped onion
1 1/2 Tbs prepared horseradish
1 1/2 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tsp caraway seeds
1 1/2 tdsp celery seeds
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp salt
1 tsp Tabasco
1/2 tsp seasoned salt

Directions:
Beat until smooth.  Keeps in refrigerator a long time.

Submitter: 
Sue Clark
Description: 
A cowoker at school shared this many years ago.  it is always a hit as a vegetable dip.  When i take it to work, it and the veggies are GONE!
