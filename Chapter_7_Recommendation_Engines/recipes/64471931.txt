Title:
Five-Layer Veggie (and Turkey) Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 grated carrots
2 T olive oil
2 T fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp black pepper
3/4 cup chopped and drained giardiniera (mixed pickled vegetables)
1/4 cup olive tapenade
6 oz sliced roasted turkey
1 sliced tomato
1 thinly sliced cucumber
1 bunch arugula
4 halved soft whole-grain rolls

Directions:
Combine carrots, olive oil, and lemon juice.
Season with pepper.
Divide carrots, giardiniera, tapenade, turkey, tomato, cucumber, and arugula among rolls.
