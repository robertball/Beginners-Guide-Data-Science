Title:
Aunt Carole's Almond Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 & 1/2 c sugar
3/4 c melted butter
2 eggs
1 & 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp almond
1 & 1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp salt
slivered almonds and sugar for garnish

Directions:
Mix all together.
Spread in 9" pan or large tart pan.
Sprinkle with almonds and sugar.
Bake at 350 for 30-35 min.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
Thin, dense, delicious.  My mom makes these by the dozen to sell at a church fundraiser every summer.  People place orders for them in advance, so she hardly has any to sell!
