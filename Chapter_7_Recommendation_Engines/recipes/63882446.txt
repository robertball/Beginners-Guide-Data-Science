Title:
Baked Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077022.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c cooked macaroni
2 1/2 t butter, divided
1 1/2 t all purpose flour
1 1/2 c milk
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 1/2 c cheddar cheese
1/2 c dried breadcrumbs
A few shakes of smoked paprika

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions. Drain; set aside.
Melt 1 t of butter in a small pan. Add breadcrumbs. Cook & stir until toasted, about 3 mins. Set aside.
In a med pan, melt remaining butter. Add flour. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly.
Cook, stirring constantly, until thickened. Stir in salt & pepper. Remove pan from heat.
Add cheese. Stir until cheese is melted. Stir macaroni into cheese sauce.
Spoon onto individual serving plates, then sprinkle with bread crumbs, paprika & scallions & serve.
