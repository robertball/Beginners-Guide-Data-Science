Title:
Ham and Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups finely chopped ham
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 tablespoons prepared mustard
dash of cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine the ham, onion and celery. Add cheese , mayo, mustard & pepper. Mix well and serve on rolls.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
After making your own ham salad you'll never buy it prepared again. I first tried adding cheese to ham salad when I had to stretch a small amount of ham to make sandwiches for my children's lunches. It worked and they loved it!Modified from: Ham and Cheese Salad
