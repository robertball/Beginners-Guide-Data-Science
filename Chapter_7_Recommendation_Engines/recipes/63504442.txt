Title:
Hearty Vegan/Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Zucchini
1 Small Tomato, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1 small handful of baby spinach, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
2 jalapeno peppers, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
2 stalks kale, chopped
1 heaping handful fresh cut green beans
4 cloves garlic minced
1 large carrot chopped
1 Medium Russet Potato, chopped
1 15 oz can Black Beans
2 15 oz cans pinto beans
3 cups tomato juice
2.5 cups veggie broth
2.25 cups water
4 tbs molasses
1 lb vegan burger crumbles
Salt, Pepper, Cumin, Chili Powder to taste

Directions:
1. Add all ingredients to Crock Pot or Large Pot
2. Cook on medium-high heat for 1.5-3.5 hours depending on how cooked you want your veggies to be
3. Pour and serve
