Title:
Chicken salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
one head of lettuce, or one back of greens
one cucumber
one tomato
one pepper
spinach, if you have some
one chicken breast

Directions:
cook chicken till done, about 20 minutes
cut up tomato, cucumber, pepper, and lettuce.
combine ingredients, add your favorite dressing.
