Title:
FB roasted potatoes with blue cheese and walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb thin skinned baby potatoes, halved
1 1/2 t. OO
1/4 t. freshly ground pepper
1/8 t. salt
1/2 C coarsely chopped walnuts
2 oz crumbled blue cheese
2 scallions, thinly sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Coat a 9"x9" baking dish with cooking spray or line with parchment paper. Place the potatoes in the prepared dish and toss with oil, salt and pepper. Turn cut side down in the pan. Roast for 30-35 minutes or until very tender and lightly golden on the underside.
Meanwhile, put the walnuts in in a small baking pan or skillet and toast for 6-8 minutes in the oven. Tip onto plate and let cool. Add the blue cheese and scallions and crumble with your fingers.
When the potatoes are done, turn them over and sprinkle evenly with blue cheese walnut mixture. Bake for 5 minutes longer or until cheese has melted.
