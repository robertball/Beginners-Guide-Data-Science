Title:
Microwave Corn Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen corn kernels
1/3 cup old-fashioned oats
1/2 cup egg substitute or egg whites

Directions:
Put frozen corn into a bowl and defrost by microwaving for 1 minute.
Add corn, oats, and egg into a blender. Blend until it reaches your desired consistency.
Place this batter back into the bowl and microwave for 2-3 minutes, stirring after each minute.
Let cool and enjoy!
