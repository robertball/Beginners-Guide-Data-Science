Title:
italian chicken breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast
2 tablespoons of olive oil
1 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon of pepper
8 ounces of goat cheese softened
6 sundried tomatoes chopped finely
6 tablespoons of chopped fresh basil
6 teaspoons of onions minced
6 teaspoons of garlic minced
12 tablespoons of salted butter
1/3 of a cup of fresh lemon juice

Directions:
preheat a grill outside for the best flavor
brush the chicken with the olive oil on both sides
season with the salt and pepper
grill until the internal temperature is(165 F)
while thats cooking prepare your sauce for the chicken
add about two tablespoons of butter to a sauce pan and saute the onions and garlic for about 5 to 7 minutes
then add the rest of the butter and lemon juice
add your chopped sundried tomatoes and basil
heat the sauce up until nice and hot then set aside
cut your goatcheese in 1/2 inch slices
and at the end of the chicken cooking
add two slices to each peice of chicken
let set on the grill for about 3 minutes
bring inside and put chicken on a serving platter
pour about 3 or 4 tablespoons of sauce on each peice of chicken.

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
this has a wonderful flavor of sundried tomatoes,fresh basil,goatcheese,and a lemon butter sauce that is to die for.
