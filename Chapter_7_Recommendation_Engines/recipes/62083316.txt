Title:
Spinach Artichoke Dip (Cold) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch fresh spinach, stems removed, torn into small pieces
1 bunch green onions diced
1 can artichokes in water - drained and chopped
1 (8 ounce) container non-fat sour cream
2 c non-fat plain yogurt
1/2 cup mayonnaise (non-fat okay)
1 (.4 ounce) packet dry vegetable soup mix
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and let sit in fridge overnight before serving.  Serve in breadbowls, with inside bread removed and torn in small pieces for dipping.

Submitter: 
Crystal Miller
Description: 
This is a fabulous spinach artichoke dip with less of the heavy fattening ingredients that make you wish you hadn't had that last bite.  Enjoy GUILT FREE!
