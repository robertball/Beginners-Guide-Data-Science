Title:
Hot Mushroom Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Fresh Sliced Mushrooms
3 Tbsp. Butter
1 Tsp. Garlic Powder
1 Pkg. Dry Onion/Mushroom Soup Mix
1 Tbsp. Flour
8 Oz. Sour Cream
1 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Saute mushrooms in butter and garlic
Add flour and remove from heat.
In a large mixing bowl, mix sour cream and soup mix.
Add Mushrooms to bowl and mix
Pour mixture into a 9" inch pie plate, add cheese.
Bake for 10 minutes.
