Title:
Simply Scrumptious Southern Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1612945.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C sharp cheddar cheese, finely shredded
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 C mayonnaise
4 oz diced pimento, drained
1 Tbsp minced pickled jalapeño
1 tsp brine from pickled jalapeños
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp cayenne
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt, or to taste

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in stand mixer or food processor until well combined.  Allow to chill for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
This tasty version of Southern pimento cheese is sure to please!  Serve by itself with chips or celery for dipping, spread on bread for a tasty grilled cheese, or dollop on burgers for melt-in-your mouth cheesy flavor!
