Title:
Lime-Cilantro Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670045.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup lime juice
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Blend the lime juice, vinegar, cilantro, brown sugar, garlic, salt and mustard until smooth. Turn the blender on low and slowly pour the olive oil into the dressing mixture in a thin stream; blend until thoroughly combined.
