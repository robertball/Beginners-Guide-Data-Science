Title:
Very Berry Blueberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/652705.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, chopped
1 kiwi, sliced
3/4 cup blueberries
1 cup ice cubes
1 (8 ounce) container vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Combine the banana, kiwi, blueberries, ice cubes, and vanilla yogurt in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Michaela
Description: 
If you love blueberries, this is the smoothie for you! It's quick, easy, and enjoyable! It's a definite 'Yummy in the Tummy!'
