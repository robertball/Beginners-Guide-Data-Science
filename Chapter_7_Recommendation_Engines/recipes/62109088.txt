Title:
Big Sister's Savory Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
3/4 cup cheese- colby jack
1/8 cup diced red onion
1 stalk chopped green onion
1/2 tablespoon fresh chopped basil (optional)
1 tablespoon butter or margarine

Directions:
Melt butter into a medium sized frying pan.
Break eggs into a bowl and mix until yolks are blended.
Pour eggs into the pan and cook for 1 minute, stirring frequently.
Add in cheese and all chopped ingredients, folding well into the egg mixture.
Cook until eggs are the desired texture.
*Serve with buttered toast and extra cheese.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Chocolate
Description: 
These eggs are delicious and a welcoming change to the original morning breakfast egg.  They have a bold flavor and moist texture.
