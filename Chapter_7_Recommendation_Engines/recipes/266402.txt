Title:
Corn on the Cob in the Microwave Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5541546.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ears fresh corn, shucked
4 dampened paper towels
1 storage bag

Directions:
Wrap each ear of corn in a damp paper towel. Place corn in a storage bag; do not seal the bag.
Place in the microwave oven and cook on high for 4 minutes. Flip the bag over and cook on high an additional 4 minutes.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
Summer time and corn is in season. 
Sometimes it is too hot bring a pot of water to a boil.
 Short on time? This is when the microwave comes into play.
