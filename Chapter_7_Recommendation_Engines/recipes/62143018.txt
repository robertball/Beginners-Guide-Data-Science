Title:
3 ingredient slow cooker meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 16 oz bag of frozen meatballs
2-3 cans of condensed cream of onion soup (matter of taste)
1 can of condensed cheddar cheese soup
1/4 cup of water

Directions:
1. Add meatballs, Cream of Onion & Cheddar cheese soup to slow cooker
2. Stir together until meatballs are covered in soup (Add a little water to thin soup out if needed)
3. Cook on low setting for 3-4 hours or until meatballs are thoroughly heated.
NOTE: These can also be made in a roasting pan in the oven.

Submitter: 
Jen Camilleri
Description: 
This recipe always receives rave reviews yet noboday can believe how easy these are to make. These are delicious on mashed potatoes or noodles or just by themselves.
