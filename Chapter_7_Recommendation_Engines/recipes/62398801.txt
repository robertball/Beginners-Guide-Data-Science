Title:
Green Chili and Cheese Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter, room temp
1/2 c sugar
4 eggs
4 oz green chilies
1 16 oz can creamed corn
1/2 c grated Jack cheese
1/2 c mild cheddar cheese
1 c flour
1 c yellow cornmeal
4 t baking powder
1/4 t salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Brease and flour a 9x13 in pan. Cream 1/2 cup butter with sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time. Mix in chilies, corn and cheeses. Combine flour, cornmeal, baking powder and salt. Add dry ingredients to corn mixture and blend well. Pour batter until golden brown and beginning to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 55 minutes
