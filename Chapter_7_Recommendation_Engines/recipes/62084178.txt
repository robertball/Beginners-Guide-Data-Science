Title:
Tuna Mushroom Melt Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups minute rice
2 cups water
2 cans tuna
1 26 oz. can cream of mushroom soup
3/4 cup italian style bread crumbs
2 tablespoons melted butter
2 cups shredded mild cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Bring water to a boil.  Stir in rice, remove from heat, cover and let stand for 5 minutes.  Mix prepared rice with tuna and cream of mushroom soup.  In a separate bowl, combine melted butter and bread crumbs.  Spread rice mixture into a 9 X 13 baking dish and top with bread crumb mixture.  To finish, top with cheddar cheese and bake for approximately 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Submitter: 
OLIVIAC15163
Description: 
This is a quick and simple recipe for anyone who is on the run. It consists white rice with cream of mushroom soup and tuna, with melted cheddar cheese on top.  Serve with bread sticks and a green vegetable and you have dinner in no time!
