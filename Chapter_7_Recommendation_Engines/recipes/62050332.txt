Title:
Grilled Eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Eggplant, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch slices
1/4 cup part olive oil
1/4 cup part balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup part good quality soy sauce

Directions:
Heat grill to medium high. Mix liquids in small bowl. Coat eggplant pieces in sauce and carefully place on hot grill (they will flame up). Cook about 5 minutes per side, or until deep brown. Serve hot, room temp., or cold.
