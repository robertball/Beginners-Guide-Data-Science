Title:
Kaylee's Classic Breadmachine Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz skim milk
1 teaspoon salt
1 Tablespoon olive oil
3 1/4 cup bread flour
1 1/2 Tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon yeast

Directions:
Add ingredients to the bread machine. Bake using regular and light setting (3).

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Super, traditional bread in the bread machine... and healthy too! I named it after Kaylee because she helped me set out the six ingredients many times when she came to visit.
