Title:
Gluten Free Almond Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 cup of milk
1 tbsp of agave
1 tbsp Organic Almond Butter
1 tbsp coconut oil
1 cup Rice Flour
1/4 teaspoon Gluten Free Baking Powder
Pinch of salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
1/4 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk almond butter and coconut oil.
In a large bowl, whisk egg, then add agave, milk, and almond butter mixture.
Whisk in remainder ingredients.
Pour mixture into muffin tins about 3/4 full. Sprinkle sliced almonds on top.
Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes and enjoy.
