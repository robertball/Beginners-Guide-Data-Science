Title:
Zucchini Fritters with Fresh Mozzarella and Tomato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded zucchini
2 cups all purpose flour
4 large eggs
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp each of garlic powder, dried oregano, marjoram and basil
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
1 tsp kosher salt
1/4 cup grated parmesean cheese
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 lb fresh mozzarella cheese
2 large heirloom tomato
finishing salt such as fluer de sel
vegetable and olive oil for frying

Directions:
slice the mozzarella into 1/4 inch slices and set aside
Slice the tomato into rounds and set aside
In a large skillet heat a 50/50 ratio of vegtable oil and olive oil to about 375 degrees.  The oil should be about 1/2 inch deep
Place the shredded zucchini in a large bowl and add the flour, baking soda, seasonings, salt, pepper and cheese.
Stir to combine
in a small bowl slightly beat the eggs and add the buttermilk.  Stir to combine
pour the liquid ingredients into the dry and mix to combine
drop fritter batter into the skillet using about 1/4 cup for each fritter
fry until golden brown and turn to brown other side
drain on paper towel and assemble each fritter with a slice of cheese and top with tomato.  Salt with finishing salt and serve.
