Title:
Chocolate Syrup Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Hershey's chocolate syrup
1/3 cup Sweetened condensed milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
few grains of salt
2 cups of whipped heavy cream (2 1/2 pints whipped)
2 egg whites stiffly whipped

Directions:
Combine syrup, milk, vanilla and salt
Blend well and chill for a bit (15 min or so)
Remove from freezer and add whipped cream
Whip till mixture is spongy and light
Fold in whipped egg whites
Freeze in 8x8x2 pan
Scrape sides after 1 hour
Freeze again
Serve next day or make in the morning and serve that night
