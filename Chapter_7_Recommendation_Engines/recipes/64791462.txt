Title:
Air Fryer Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb beef tenderloin,  room temperature
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tap cracked pepper
1 tsp oregano

Directions:
Preheat an air fryer to 400째F. Pat dry the roast with paper towels.
Place the roast on a plate. Drizzle the oil and sprinkle the salt, pepper, and oregano and rub it all over the beef, covering the whole roast. (You can do this an hour before if desired.)
Place the roast in the air fryer basket by folding it to make it fit in the basket. Close the basket. Reduce heat to 390째. Cook for 22 minutes.
Reduce heat to 360째F. Cook for another 10 minutes. Check the temperature of the meat with a thermometer. It should read 135째F for medium.
Remove from the basket and place on a plate. Allow to rest, uncovered, for 10 minutes or more before serving.

Description: 
Beef tenderloin with a lovely crispy crust and soft inside made in your air fryer. This is perfect to serve Italian style, room temperature with a squeeze of lemon a drizzle of olive oil.
