Title:
Cocoa Mounds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Oats
1/2 cup Peanut Butter
2 cups Sugar
1/3 cup Cocoa
1/2 cup Butter (1 stick)
1/2 cup Milk
2 teaspoons Vanilla

Directions:
Mix sugar and cocoa in a saucepan.
Add butter, milk, and vanilla.
While stirring continuously on medium heat, bring to a rolling boil and boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat.
Mix in oats and peanut butter.
Let set in pan for about 4 minutes, then put spoon fulls on sheet of wax paper, and let cool. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
MMS
Description: 
A combination of all the great cookie ingredients...oats, peanut butter, and of course chocolate.
