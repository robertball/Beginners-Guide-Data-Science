Ingredients:
1 Pint Strawberries-Sliced
1 Mango-Diced
2 Bananas-Sliced
3/4 Cup Mexican Crema or Sour Cream
1/4 to 1/3 Cup White Sugar (you may add more to your taste)

Directions:
Remove the stem from strawberries, then slice into small slices and place into a bowl. Use Potato masher to mash the Strawberries into a thick paste and most chunks are gone.
Stir in the diced mango. Add Sour cream and sugar to strawberries and mix.
Dice bananas and add them to the mixture. Stir together.
Refridgerate about 30 minutes or until cold and serve.
