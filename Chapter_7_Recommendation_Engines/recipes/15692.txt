Title:
Chili Cheese Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3201922.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (15 ounce) can chili with beans
1 pinch chili powder
1/2 tablespoon white sugar
1 (16 ounce) package corn chips

Directions:
Cover cream cheese with chili in 1-1/2 quart microwaveable casserole dish. Cover and microwave until the chili and cream cheese stir together easily and become hot and bubbly.  Add chili powder and sugar to taste.  Serve hot with chips.

Submitter: 
Susie Booth
Description: 
My husband, Trac, makes this terrific dip.  He adds spices and a bit of sugar to take the bite out of the dip. He samples with corn chips until it's just right!
