Title:
Greek-Style Zucchini Quinoa Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Quinoa:
1.5 cups quinoa
1/2 bunch fresh parsley or cilantro
1 12oz can tomato paste
1/4 cup soy sauce
Casserole:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 onions, sliced
5 garlic cloves, minced
7 zucchini, sliced into half-moons (or a substitute vegetable)
2 teaspoons oregano
2 teaspoons basil
1 teaspoon marjoram
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 lb feta cheese
2 lb cottage cheese
1/2 lb cheddar cheese, grated
3 tomatoes, thinly sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly oil the pan.
Cook the quinoa.
Saute onions and garlic in oil. When softened, stir in zucchini, herbs, and pepper, and cook on medium until zucchini are just tender.
Once quinoa has cooked, stir in the parsley, tomato paste, and soy sauce into the cooked quinoa.
In a large bowl, mix the feta and cottage cheese.
Assemble the casserole with quinoa on bottom, sauteed mix, then fetta and cottage cheese.
Bake covered for 45 minutes. Uncover and top with grated cheddar cheese and tomato slices. Bake for 15 minutes. Let cool for 30 minutes.
