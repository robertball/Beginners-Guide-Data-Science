Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2442206.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
12 strips bacon
1/2 cup cheddar cheese shredded
½ cup shredded kale

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix eggs in a bowl.
Add shredded cheese and kale.
Line each muffin tin with crisscrossed strip of bacon(cut in half) and fill with egg mixture.
Bake in oven for 8 to 10 minutes

Submitter: 
Rose Killion
Description: 
These 'Dinomite's Fluffy Bites” are fluffy and flavorful. So good they are “Dinomite!”
