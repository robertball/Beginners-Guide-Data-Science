Title:
Greek Style Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large red bell peppers
6 red potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
2 tbsp olive oil
1 1/2 lbs medium ground beef
2 onions, chopped
1 cup long grain rice, raw
1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes, with juice
1 (7 1/2 oz) can tomato sauce
1 handfull fresh dill, finely chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Remove tops of peppers, and clean out the insides. (Do not discard the pepper tops)
In skillet, brown onions in olive oil. Add ground beef and brown until no longer pink. Drain off fat.
Stir in salt, pepper and dill. Add canned tomatoes (with juice), tomato sauce, and rice.  Stir until well blended.
Place peppers in a large casserole dish. Stuff each pepper with the meat mixture and cover with pepper tops.  Surround the peppers with any remaining meat mixture as well as the potato wedges.
Cover and bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.
