Title:
Philly Steak And Cheese Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Sirloin steak sliced thin
2 cups canned sliced mushrooms
1 thin sliced onion
1/2 cup finely diced bell pepper
Splash of olive oil
Italian spice blend to taste
Dash of meat tenderizer
Black pepper to taste
8 slices provolone cheese
Hawaiian style dinner rolls 12 pack

Directions:
Slice steak 1/8 to 3/16 strips bite size, add tenderizer and mix
Heat olive oil in medium fry pan
Saute onion, bell pepper 3 minutes over medium high heat
Add steak to pan with onion and peppers saute additional 3 minutes
Slice through entire loaf of dinner rolls, do not pull apart, set aside on baking sheet or shallow baking pan
Add black pepper and Italian spices to taste in pan
Add drained mushrooms to pan saute 3 more minutes
Meat should be medium done
Lay meat mixture across entire lower half of dinner roll loaf edge to edge, include some of the juices from the pan
Lay cheese across top of meat and dinner rolls
Place in broiler until cheese melts
Remove from broiler and add top layer of dinner rolls
Place back in broiler and toast the top of the rolls.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
James Dillard
Description: 
Quick and easy take on a Philly steak and cheese sandwich.
