Title:
Zucchini Marmalade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Zucchini or Summer squash
juice of 2 lemons
1 tsp lemon zest
1 13.5 oz can crushed pineapple, drained
1 pkg Certo (or other pectin)
5 cups sugar
2-3 Tbsp candied ginger, finely minced
5-6 1/2 pint jars

Directions:
Wash and sterilize pint jars.
Wash Zucchini/squash, peel and slice.
In a non aluminum pan, combine zucchini/ squash the lemon juice, zest, and pineapple (drained). Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes; stir occasionally.
Stir in pectin and boil again. Add sugar and ginger; continue to boil for one more minute, stirring continuously.
Remove from heat; skim off foam. Allow to cool 5-10 minute.
Ladle into hot sterilized jars and seal (water bath method; process for 5 minutes).
