Title:
Buckeyes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups peanut butter
2 cups confectioners sugar
1 stick butter softened
3 cups rice krispes
1/2 package chocolate almond bark

Directions:
Mix peanut butter and butter together, then add the sugar, then the rice krispes. Form all dough into balls placing on waxed paper lined cookie sheets. Chill for 1 hour . Melt bark in top of double boiler. Dip balls 3/4 of the way into melted bark. Place back on wax paper linted sheets till set.
