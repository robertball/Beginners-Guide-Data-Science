Title:
Crunchy Chinese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small cabbage, grated
1/2 carrot, grated
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 pkg chicken Ramen noodles
3 Tbsp sesame seeds, toasted
1/2 cup sliced almonds, toasted
DRESSING:
1/2 cup oil
3 Tbsp cider vinegar
2 Tbsp sugar
1 chicken flavor packet (from Ramen noodles)
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl mix cabbage, carrot, green onions, noodles, sesame seeds and almonds.  Set aside
In a separate bowl combine oil, vinegar, sugar, chicken flavor packet, salt and pepper.  Mix well.
Just before serving, pour dressing over cabbage mixture.  Mix well and serve immediately.
