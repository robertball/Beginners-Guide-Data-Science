Title:
Salsa Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992471.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 cup salsa
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
1 cup frozen corn, thawed
1 cup chopped green pepper
Lettuce Leaves

Directions:
In a bowl, combine yogurt, salsa and pepper; mix well. Add tuna, corn and green pepper; toss to coat. Serve in a lettuce-lined bowl if desired.

Submitter: 
Jennifer  Harris
Description: 
In Skellytown, Texas, Jennifer Harris perks up her fresh-tasting tuna salad with flavorful salsa. Bright corn and green pepper add pretty color to the mixture. Served on a bed of lettuce, it makes a great light lunch.
