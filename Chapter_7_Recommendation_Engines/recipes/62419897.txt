Title:
Rainbow Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chopped cabbage
8 chopped carrots
12 chopped onions
2 chopped red peppers
2 chopped green peppers
6 cups sugar
1/2 cup salt
2 quarts vinegar
2 tbsp mustard seed
1 tbsp celery seed

Directions:
Mix together and put in jars.
Store in refridgerator.
