Title:
Teriyaki Pork Kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. reduced sodium soy sauce
1/3 c. packed brown sugar
1 T. minced fresh ginger
2 garlic cloves, pressed
2 tsp. dark sesame oil
2 lb. pork tenderloin
2 med bell peppers (green, red, orange, or yellow)
fresh pineapple

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, minced ginger, oil, and garlic.  Mix well.
Reserve about 1/2 c. marinade& set aside.
Cut pork into bite sized pieces.
Add pork to marinade, tossing to coat.
Refrigerate 30 minutes.  Don't marinate too long or pork will get mushy.
Cut peppers and pineapple into bite sized pieces.
Remove pork from marinade and discard.
Thread pork, pineapple, and peppers on skewers.
Brush kebabs with some of the reserved marinade and grill until pork is no longer pink, brushing occasionally with reserved marinade.
