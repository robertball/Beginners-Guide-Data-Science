Title:
Mom's Christmas Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg yeast
1/3 cup warm water
1 cup scalded milk
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup shortening (or oil)
1-1/2 tsp salt
5 cups flour (or more)
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 cup raisins
1/2 pkg fruit cake mix (or more)

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in water and proof.
In separate bowl combine milk, sugar, shortening and salt.  Cool to lukewarm.
Add 1-1/2 cups flour and stir.  Add eggs.  Stir in yeast, vanilla and nutmeg.  Add raisins and fruit.  Add remaining flour to make a soft dough.
Knead until smooth.  Place in greased bowl, cover and let rise until double.
Punch down and divide in half.  Make 2 round loaves.  Cover and let rise until double.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes.
