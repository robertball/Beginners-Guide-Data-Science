Title:
Party Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Original Cooking Spray
3 Tbs butter
1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 lbs Boneless Chicken Breast Fillet
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 (10.75 oz) can Campbell's Healthy Request Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
1/2 cup light mayonnaise
1 1/2 cup Panko Bread Flakes (Japanese Bread Crumbs)
1 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  Coat 2-quart baking dish with cooking spray.   Cut butter into small pieces and place in medium bowl to soften.
Place flour in zip-top bag.  Season chicken with pepper; add to bad.  Seal bag tightly; shake to coat.  Remove chicken from bag; shake off excess flour.  Arrange chicken in baking dish
Combine soup and mayonnaise in second bowl; spread mixture evenly over chicken.  Stir pank and cheese into mixture until crumbly.  Top chicken with cheese mixture; cover dish with foil.  Bake 10 minutes.
Remove foil.  Bake 10-15 minutes more minutes or until top is golden.  Serve
