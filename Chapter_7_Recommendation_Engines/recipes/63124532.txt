Title:
FiberOne Herman Sourdough Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796171.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c FiberOne cereal, crushed fine (crush cereal and then measure 1 cup of that). A few coarse bits are ok
1 1/2 c all purpose flour
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
6 tbsp butter
1/2 c buttermilk
1 c Herman sourdough starter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Place all dry ingredients in a bowl.
Cut in the butter until it ts the size of peas.
Add buttermilk and starter, mix to combine well but don't knead.
Roll to desired thickness and cut with biscuit cutter.
Place in cookie sheet and bake for 10 min.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
Substitute flour with crushed up cereal to make a tasty high-fiber morning treat.
