Title:
Paloma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 ice cubes
1.5 oz tequila
grapefruit soda to taste such as Fresca or Squirt
2 lime wedges

Directions:
Pour tequila over ice in a tall glass.  Roll the glass between your palms, then squeeze juice from one lime wedge into glass.  Pour grapefruit soda over tequila mixture.  Garnish with the other lime wedge.

Submitter: 
CLOUDSSUNRAIN
Description: 
I learned about this combination in Mexico.  It goes down really easy for a tequila drink!
