Title:
Peanut Butter or Chocolate Oatmeal No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Sugar
½ cup of Milk
½ cup of Peanut Butter
¼ cup of butter
1/3 cup of Unsweetened Cocoa Powder (optional it makes chocolate cookies)
3 cups of Rolled Oats (Uncooked)

Directions:
In sauce pan combine milk, sugar, butter and cocoa (just leave out the cocoa for peanut butter cookies). Bring to a boil for one minute. Remove from heat and mix with other ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Drop by 1 tbsp. full on wax paper and let them sit in fridge until they harden and are cold. Always store them in the fridge.
