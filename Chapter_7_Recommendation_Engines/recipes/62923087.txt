Title:
** Mulled Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gal. burgandy wine
1 qt. cranberry juice
2 c. water
1 c. sugar
6 whole cloves
6 cinnamon sticks
2 lemons, sliced thin

Directions:
Boil sugar & water on low heat until sugar is disolved (10 min.).
Add juice, cloves & cinnamon sticks to sugar water & heat through. Steep lemon slices 10 min.
Add wine & heat - do not boil.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
My old recipe.  Easy to heat and serve in a crockpot.
