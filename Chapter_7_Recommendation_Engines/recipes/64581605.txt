Title:
Healthy Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3654881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large orange
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tbsp orange zest
2 handfuls ice
sweetener of choice

Directions:
Blend all ingredients until the ice and orange are broken down and becomes frothy.
Then transfer to a large glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
This Healthy Orange Julius Smoothie is made with 6 ingredients and in under 5 minutes. A refreshing spin on a classic favorite that's lighter and waist-friendly!
