Title:
Devil's Fudge Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cocoa
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup sugar dissolved in 1 Tbsp hot water
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup hot water
1 cup icing sugar (or more)

Directions:
Cream butter and cocoa  2 mins add dissolved sugar, salt and vanilla. Beat 2 mins. Add hot water and then icing sugar till the right consistency .
