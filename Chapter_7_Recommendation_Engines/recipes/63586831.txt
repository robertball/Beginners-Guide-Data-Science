Title:
Gmama's griddle scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups buttermilk
2 level tsp baking soda
4 cups flour
2 level tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup margarine
extra flour

Directions:
put baking soda and buttermilk in a bowl. set aside.
sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Rub margarine into this mixture, then add milk all at once.Cut it through the flour with a knife. Dough should be soft. take a little bit out  on too a floured board. roll it out slightly into a round then cut it into 4. place on a warmed griddle about five minutes or until browned, then turn on other side until brown , then stand on each side to make sure they are done, then put them on a tea bowl and cover, this keeps them soft.
