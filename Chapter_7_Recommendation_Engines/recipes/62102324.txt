Title:
Garlic Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound thin spaghetti
2-3 Tbsp Olive Oil
1/4 C butter
2-3 cloves fresh garlic, chopped
2-3 Tbsp. fresh chopped parsely
1/4 tsp. crushed cayenne pepper
1/4 C. parmesan cheese
Optional: 1 cup diced chicken breast or half a pound of cooked shrimp, or oil packed sun dried tomatoes, cut into strips.  Add option of choice just before adding pasta.  Toss all together.

Directions:
Cook pasta according to pkg. directions. In skillet heat olive oil. Saute garlic and pepper in olive oil until garlic starts to brown. Add parsley and butter. Drain and rinse pasta and toss with the garlic mixture in skillet. Transfer to serving dish and top with parmesan cheese and additional sprinkling of parsley.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
My husband loves this pasta by itself.  Add chicken, shrimp, or sundried tomatoes for a different flavor.  Very fast and easy, yet elegant looking.
