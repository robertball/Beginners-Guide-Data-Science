Title:
Pasta with Ham and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz farfalle
2/3 c frozen peas
1 stick butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup ham, diced
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Begin cooking pasta as directed.  During the last 2 minutes, add the peas.  Drain when al dente.  Put pan back on burner and melt butter.  Add cream, ham, and Parmesan.  Heat through.  Add pasta and peas.  Toss to coat.
