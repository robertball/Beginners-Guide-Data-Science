Title:
Garlic Shrimp Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound uncooked linguine
1 1/2 sticks butter
3/4 cup white wine
4 cloves garlic, minced
drizzle olive oil
2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1 teaspoon chopped fresh basil (garnish)
grated fresh Asiago cheese (garnish)  Asiago a must
1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium low heat; add wine, garlic, olive oil, pepper flakes, basil and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer over low heat for 3 to 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Increase heat to medium high and add shrimp to saucepan; cook for about 3 to 4 minutes or until shrimp begins to turn pink. Do not overcook.
Divide pasta into portions and spoon sauce on top; garnish with asiago and fresh basil.
