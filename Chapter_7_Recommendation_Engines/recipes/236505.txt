Title:
Dandy Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes, or as desired
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger bourbon whiskey
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger herbal red wine (such as Dubonnet®)
1/2 fluid ounce orange-flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
1 dash bitters (such as Angostura®)
1 (1 1/2 inch) piece lemon peel, or more to taste
1 (1 inch) piece orange peel, or more to taste

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour bourbon, herbal red wine, orange-flavored liqueur, and bitters over ice; add lemon peel and orange peel. Cover shaker, shake vigorously to release oils from peels, and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Description: 
This one's like a Manhattan with Dubonnet® and Cointreau® in place of sweet vermouth. Dubonnet® is a fortified red wine, flavored with herbs and fruit and cinchona bark. Rye whiskey can be used in place of bourbon if desired.
