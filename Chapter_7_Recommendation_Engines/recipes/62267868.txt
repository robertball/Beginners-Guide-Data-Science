Title:
Beef Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce
1 cup ketchup
1 cup soy sauce
6oz pineapple juice
6tbsp sugar
1/2 tbsp pepper
3 green peppers
2 red peppers
2 medium onions
Marinade
6oz pineapple juice
1/2 cup soy sauce
2 cloves chopped garlic
1/2 tbsp pepper
3 tbsp cornstarch
3 lbs cubed beef

Directions:
1. Combine marinade ingredients in Tupperware container, and leave overnight.
2. When ready to cook stir fry beef until seared on each side.
3. In a separate pan Stir fry onions for 3 minutes.
4. Add peppers and stir fry an additional 3 minutes.
5.Add 2 cups water, bring to boil and then reduce to a simmer.
6. Combine vegetables with beef, and add in prepared sauce.
7. Add an additional 2 cups water and 2 tbsps cornstarch.
8. Boil and then simmer for 10 minutes.
9. Serve over rice.
