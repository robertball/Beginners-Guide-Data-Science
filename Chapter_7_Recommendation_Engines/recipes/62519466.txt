Title:
Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 egg
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup water
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon mustard powder

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together 1/3 cup condensed French onion soup with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, and black pepper. Shape into 6 oval patties.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown both sides of patties. Transfer patties to an oven proof dish.
In a small bowl, blend flour and remaining soup until smooth. Mix in ketchup, water, Worcestershire sauce and mustard powder. Pour over meat in baking dish. Cover with foil and bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Amy Thomas Link
Description: 
Ground beef is mixed with condensed onion soup, bread crumbs, and egg, then formed into patties which are browned and simmered in a sauce made with more soup and seasonings.Modified from: Salisbury Steak
