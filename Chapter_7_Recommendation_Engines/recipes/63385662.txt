Title:
Salt and Pepper Grilled Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c extra virgin olive oil
1/2 c coarse salt
2 T freshly ground pepper
1 large fesh or thawed frozen turkey (about 22 lbs), rinsed and patted dry, neck and giblets reserved for Giblet Gravy

Directions:
1.  Combine oil, salt and pepper to make a paste.  Rub over outside of turkey.  Tie legs together with kitchen twine.
2.  Place about 50 coals in a chimney starter, and ignite, heat until just gray.  Place a 9x13 disposable aluminum roasting pan in the center of the bottom rack.  Pour coalsonto rack on either side of pan, dividing them evenly.  Replace top grill rack.  Place turkey on center of rack over pan, and cover.  Gill, adding 8 coals to each pile every 45 minutes to maintain heat, until ameat thermomoeter inserted into the thickest part of the thigh (avoiding boe) registers 165 degrees (begin checkng after about 3 hours; cooking may take up th 5 hours depending on the exact size of bird).  Tent with parchment lined foil.  Let stand 30 minutes.  Reserve drippings from roasting pan for Giblt Gravy.
