Title:
Roasted Red Pepper Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium yellow onions, chopped
2 T extra virgin olive oil
1 jar (7oz) roasted sweet peppers, save the juice and chop the peppers
1 can (4oz) chopped green chilies, drained
2 T ground cumin
1 t salt
1 t ground coriander
3 C diced potatoes
3 C chicken broth
2 T minced fresh cilantro
1 T lemon juice
1/2 C milk (original recipe calls for cream cheese)

Directions:
1. In your soup pot, saute onions in oil until tender. Stir in the roasted peppers, chilies, cumin, salt, and coriander. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Stir in potatoes and broth, bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat and cover. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until potatoes are tender. Stir in cilantro and lemon juice. Pull off heat.
3. Pour half the soup into a blender and puree with milk until smooth. Pour back into the soup pot with the other half of the soup. Heat through again on medium. Serve warm.
