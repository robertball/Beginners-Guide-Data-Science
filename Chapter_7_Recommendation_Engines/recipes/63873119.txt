Title:
Hobbit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese - softened
1 1/2 heads roasted garlic
2 tsp. prepared horseradish
8 oz. goat cheese
1/2 tsp. lemon juice
1/8 to 1/4 cup toasted pistachios, crushed

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and goat cheese and mix until creamy.
Mash roasted garlic and add to cheese mixture with other ingredients.
Mix until well blended.
Spread into greased dish and bake at 350 until heated through and golden brown around the edges.
Serve warm with crostini, apple slices or pita chips.
