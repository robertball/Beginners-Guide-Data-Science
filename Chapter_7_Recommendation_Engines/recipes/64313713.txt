Title:
Kale and Butter Beans Soup with Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1992813.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cloves 	Garlic, pressed
1 pc.		Onion, cut in tiny cubes
1 pc. 		Carrot, medium, cut in tiny cubes
1 pc.		Tomato, medium, cut in tiny cubes
4 branches	Kale, hard stems removed, cut roughly
2 pcs.		Spicy Pork Sausage or Merguez
500 g. 		Butter beans, cooked and drained
4 tbsp		Virgin Olive oil
1 cup		Water
2 tbsp		Basil Pesto
10 pcs.	Fresh   Basil leaves

Directions:
1.  Heat half of the olive oil in heavy cooking pan at medium fire, add the garlic, onion and carrots. Stir until the onion turns transparent, about 3 mins.  Remove the sausage skin by sliding the meat out.  Add the sausage meat in the pan, breaking it roughly into small pieces and cook until it turns slightly golden.
2.  Add the tomatoes, beans and water. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes.
3.  Add the pesto, kale and stir until kale is slightly wilted.
4.  Add the fresh basil leaves.  Drizzle the remaining olive oil.
5.  Serve hot with shaved parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Patty Gay
Description: 
This is perfect for cold weather, one of the best ways to cook with Kale. It's a soup dish with the most wonderful crunchy texture from kale. With the addition of basil pesto takes this traditional lentil-base soup to the next level.
