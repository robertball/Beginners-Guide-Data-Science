Title:
Cranberry-Orange Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985770.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package fat-free cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons Tropicana速 Light n Healthy Orange Juice Beverage
1 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon grated orange peel
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup dried cranberries
2 tablespoons chopped toasted pecans
Quaker速 Cinnamon-Sugar Multigrain Mini Cakes

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, orange juice beverage, sugar, peel and cinnamon in medium bowl; beat with electric mixer until smooth.  Gently stir in all but 2 teaspoons cranberries.
Transfer to serving dish.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
Sprinkle with pecans and remaining cranberries.  Dip or spread each Cinnamon-Sugar Multigrain Mini Cake with spread.
