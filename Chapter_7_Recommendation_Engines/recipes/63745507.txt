Title:
Orange Cranberry Pecan Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, room temp
3 tbsp frozen orange juice concentrate
1 cup crainraisons, soaked in water
1 tbsp orange zest
1/2 cup toasted pecans

Directions:
Mix all ingredients into cream cheese, using mixer. Refrigerate overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
This is a great spread served with bagels, waverly wafers or club crackers.  This is most refreshing at a brunch!
