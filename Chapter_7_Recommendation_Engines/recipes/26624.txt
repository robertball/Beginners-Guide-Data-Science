Title:
Mexican Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979209.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces ground beef
3/4 cup water
1 (1.25 ounce) package ORTEGA® Taco Seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) can ORTEGA® Refried Beans, divided
1 package (10) ORTEGA® Tostada Shells
2 cups SARGENTO® Nacho & Taco Blend Shredded Cheese, divided
Toppings: shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, sliced ripe olives, sliced avocado, chopped fresh cilantro, sliced green onions, sour cream, guacamole, whole kernel corn, black beans, ORTEGA® Thick & Chunky Salsa

Directions:
BROWN beef; drain.  Stir in water and seasoning mix.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 to 6 minutes or until mixture is thickened.
SPREAD 2 tablespoons beans over each tostada shell.  Top with 1/2 cup meat mixture and 1/4 cup cheese.  Broil for 1 to 2 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Garnish with desired toppings.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Tired of the same old pizza? Spice up your routine the Ortega® way -- and please everyone in your family.  Tostada shells make it easy to prepare individual and creative pizzas. It's also a great recipe for buffets.
