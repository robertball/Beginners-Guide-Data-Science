Title:
Chocolate Almond Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 cups crushed vanilla wafers
1 cup finely chopped almonds
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter - melted
Filling:
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
1/2 cup milk
2 8oz. pkgs. cream cheese - softened
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 to 1/2 tsp. almond extract
1 pkg. (11 1/2 oz.) milk chocolate chips - melted and cooled
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream - whipped

Directions:
In large bowl, combine cookie crumbs, almonds, sugar and butter.
Press onto bottom and sides of grease 9" springform pan.
Cover and refrigerate 1 hour.
In small pan, soften gelatin in milk and let stand for 5 minutes.
Cook and stir over medium heat until gelatin is dissolved.
Cool to room temperature - about 5 min.
In large bowl, mix cream cheese, sour cream and almond extract until smooth.
Beat in melted chocolate and gelatin mixture.
Fold in whipped cream.
Spoon into crust.
Cover and chill for 6 hours or overnight.
Before serving, loosen edge from pan with a knife.
