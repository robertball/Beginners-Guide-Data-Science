Title:
Zucchini Chile Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups tomato sauce
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
2 cups crispy tortilla strips
3 cups thinly sliced zucchini
1 1/2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese, divided
1 cup corn kernels
1 can (4 ounces) diced green chiles, drained
1 cup sour cream, or to taste
3 green onions, chopped

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, F.  Oil 13x9 - inch baking dish.
2. Combine tomato sauce, chili powder, tomato paste, vinegar, cumin, salt, garlic powder and red pepper in medium saucepan.  Bring to a boil over high heat; reduce heat to low and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Steam zucchini 5 minutes; drain.  Transfer to large bowl.  Add 3/4 cups cheese, corn, chilies and tortilla strips.  Toss lightly to combine; spoon into prepared baking dish.  Spread tomato sauce mixture over zucchini mixture and top with remaining 3/4 cup cheese.  Bake 30 minutes or until heated through.
4. Spread sour cream over top and sprinkle with green onions.  Serve immediately.
