Title:
Low Cal Broccoli Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1 large or 2 sm heads broccoli
1/2 to 1 onion (use lesser amount if it is a strong onion)
1/2 cup each red and orange pepper
Dressing:
3/4 cup light salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip)
2 Tbsp artificial sweetener (such as Splenda)
3 Tbsp seasoned rice vinegar
2 Tbsp garlic flavored red wine vinegar
1 tsp.  creamy style horseradish (such as Inglehoffer)

Directions:
Trim and finely chop veggies into a big mixing bowl.
Separately, mix dressing ingredients until smooth.
Pour dressing over veggies and blend well.
Refrigerate for at least an hour or more to let flavors blend and develop.

Submitter: 
LaDonna Lonberger Sanders
Description: 
This salad is tasty and low calorie/low points.  IT keeps well for several days--the cauliflower stays crisp!
