Title:
Roasted Chicken Sauteed In Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812292.jpg
Ingredients:
2 15 ounce cans of tomatoes
5 Lbs of Roasted\Baked Chicken (White or Dark Meat)
1 Tablespoon Kosher Salt (To Season chicken)
1 Teaspoon Pepper (To Season chicken)
1 Large Onion, Diced
1 Teaspoon of Basel
1 Teaspoon of Oregano
1 Teaspoon of Rosemary
1 Teaspoon of Thyme
4 Ounces of Grated Parmesan
8 Ounces of white wine (You Can Use Red Wine)
Prior to serving Kosher Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Roast the chicken seasoned with salt and pepper, in a oven at about 450F on A wire rack to allow the fat to melt off of the chicken, cook the chicken until it reaches 165F internal temperature, And the chicken is nicely browned.
In a frying pan fry add Olive oil, and fry the onions until browned.
add tomatoes, wine, basel, oregano, rosemary, thyme, and chicken.
Just prior to serving salt and pepper to taste.
