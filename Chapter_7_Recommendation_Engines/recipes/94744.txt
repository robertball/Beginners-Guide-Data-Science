Title:
Coney Island Chili Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 HORMEL® Hot Dogs
8 hot dog buns, split
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili No Beans
Yellow mustard
Sweet pickle relish
Chopped sweet onion
Shredded Cheddar cheese
Chili sauce

Directions:
Grill hot dogs. Heat chili. To assemble dogs, place hot dogs in buns. Top each with chili and favorite toppings such as yellow mustard, pickle relish, chopped sweet onion, shredded Cheddar cheese and chili sauce.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Grilled hot dogs are piled high with chili, relish, onions, and cheese.
