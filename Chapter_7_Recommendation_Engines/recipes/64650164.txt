Title:
Amish Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of broccoli, chopped
1 head of cauliflower, chopped
1 cup mayonnaise  (lite I you want)
1 cup sour cream (lite if you want)
1/2 cup of sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 pound of bacon, fried and crumbled
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese or more ( fat free if desired)

Directions:
Mix all together by mixing the mayonnaise, sour cream and sugar together then pouring over the rest of the ingredients.
