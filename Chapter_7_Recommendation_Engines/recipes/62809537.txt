Title:
Mexican Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large potatoes
1 c. shredded Colby Jack cheese
1/2 c. Mexican salsa
1/2 c. lettuce
1 lb. ground beef
1 pkg. taco mix
1/4 c. sour cream

Directions:
Cook potatoes in microwave for 15 minutes; let cool.
Peel the skin off and cut in half. Dig out some of the potato in the shape of a boat. Set aside.
Brown ground beef; add taco mix, 3/4 cup water, and 1/4 cup Mexican salsa.
Deep fry the potatoes that are shaped like a boat until golden brown.
Add meat mixture; cover with cheese. Add lettuce, sour cream, and salsa to liking.
