Title:
Chef John's lighter chicken fettuccini alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lage chicken breast
2 cups chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
1 lb. fettuccini
2 cups heavy cream
4 cloves garlic,finely chopped
2 large egg yolks
1/4 cup fresh parsley
2 cups parmigiano-reggiano cheese

Directions:
Bring broth just to a boil.Place chicken in stock pot.Cover,lower heat and simmer 5 minutes.Turn over and simmer another 5 minutes.Turn heat off DO NOT LIFT LID FOR 15 MINUTES.Remove chicken and let cool slightly.Bring pasta water to a boil,salt well.
Meanwhile boil chicken broth down to 1 cup.Add garlic,salt,pepper,parsley and cream.Crack egg yolks in bowl,slowly add heated cream to warm egg yolks,add to cream mixture.Cook pasta 1 minute less than directions.Drain,return back to pot.Add sauce to pasta,cover let sit another 1-2 minutes.Add thickly sliced chicken,let sit another minute.The pasta will cook a bit more and suck up the cream.Sprinkle with a little more parsley and serve.
