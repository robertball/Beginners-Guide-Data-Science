Title:
The Best Pumpkin Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 14 oz pkg gingerbread mix
1- 5.1 oz pkg cook and serve vanilla pudding mix
1- 30 oz pumpkin pie filling
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 tsp cinnamon
1- 12-16 oz container whip topping
2 cups crushed ginger snaps

Directions:
Make gingerbread and pudding as directed on packages. Set pudding aside to cool. When pudding is cool, mix pumpkin, brown sugar, cinnamon into pudding. In bottom of trifle bowl or big tupperware container, crumble gingerbread and then layer pumpkin mix, then whipped topping and then crushed ginger snaps. Repeat ending with ginger snaps. Refrigerate and serve. ENJOY!!
