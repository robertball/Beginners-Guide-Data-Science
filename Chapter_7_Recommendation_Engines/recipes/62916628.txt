Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean Ground Beef
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
-- OR--
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
Tater tots
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine ground beef, salt & pepper. Press into the bottom of a casserole dish.
Spread soup evenly over the ground beef.
Top with a single layer of tater tots.
Bake at 350Â° for 30 minutes or until bubbly and tater tots are golden brown.
