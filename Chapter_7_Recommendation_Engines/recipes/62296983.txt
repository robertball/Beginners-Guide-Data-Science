Title:
South Plains Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5-7 lb Boston Picnic Pork Shoulder
1 large yellow or white onion sliced in rings
1 1/4 cup water
2 cups bbq sauce

Directions:
Layer onions on bottom of slow cooker.
Place the pork shoulder on top.
Add water.
Cook on high for 6-8 hours.
Remove pork shoulder from cooker and shred.
Strain 1/2 liquid out of cooker, leaving onions and a little juice.
Mix in bbq sauce and then the shredded pork.
Cook for another 25-30 mins and serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
This is a great no-brainer recipe. Put it on before work and when you get home, dinner is almost ready!
