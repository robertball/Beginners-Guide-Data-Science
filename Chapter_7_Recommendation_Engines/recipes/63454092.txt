Title:
Greens with White Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 teaspoons garlic powder
2 cube beef-flavored vegetarian bouillon
1 bag frozen spinach
3 parsnips,
3 carrots,
1/2 chopped onion
1 1/4 cups water
salt and ground black pepper to taste
3-4 cups  great Northern beans, cooked
2 Tablespoons Maple Syrup

Directions:
Cook Beans for 1 hour, covered with water. Taste beans to see if they're done.  Drain lots of water, make sure the beans are barely covered with water.
Add rest of ingredients. Cook for another hour. . Adjust seasoning as needed.

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
This low-fat beans and greens vegan dish uses onion, garlic, and canned tomatoes for flavor.Modified from: Collard Greens with White Beans
