Title:
Brian's Favorite Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
6 tablespoons olive oil
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
4 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
2 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons white sugar
1 pinch cayenne pepper (don't have to use if using hot Italian sausage

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat saute chopped onion and garlic in the oil. Break up sausage into small pieces and brown. Add tomato sauce, ground black pepper, dried oregano, dried basil, chopped tomatoes, salt, sugar and cayenne pepper; stir well. Let simmer uncovered 15 to 30 minutes or place in crock pot and let cook for many hours.
