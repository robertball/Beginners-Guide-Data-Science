Title:
General Tso's Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Approx. 3.5-4 pounds of chicken drumsticks or wings
Simply Asia Sweet Smoke Hibachi Seasoning
House of Tsang TSO'S Stir Fry Sauce

Directions:
Generously sprinkle chicken with seasoning and mix.
Over indirect heat, grill chicken at 450 for 30 minutes, turn and grill for another 20 minutes.
Remove chicken and pour Stir fry sauce over.
Garnish with sliced green onion if desired.
