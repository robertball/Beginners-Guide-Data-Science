Title:
French Cherry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (8 oz.) cream cheese
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 pint (1 cup) whipping cream
1 Keebler's graham cracker crust (9")
1 can (21 oz.) cherry pie filling

Directions:
Soften cream cheese to room temperature.  Stir in sugar & vanilla; mix well.  Fold in whipping cream & mix well.  Pour mixture into pie shell; refrigerate about 1 1/2 hours, or until firm.  Top with cherry pie filling; spread evenly to all sides.  Refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
SARA R
Description: 
My stepmother served this as one of a number of pies at Thanksgiving and Christmas...similar to cherry cheesecake, but smaller portions...
