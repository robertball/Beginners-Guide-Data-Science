Title:
20-Minute Green Bean Chicken and Rice Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980888.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cooking oil
4 small boneless skinless chicken breast halves
2 cups frozen cut green beans, thawed
1 3/4 cups milk
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 cups Minute® White Rice, uncooked
1/2 cup French fried onion rings

Directions:
Heat oil in large non-stick skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken; cover. Cook 4 minutes on each side or until cooked through (170 degrees F). Remove chicken from skillet; cover to keep warm.
Add beans, milk, soup, and seasonings to skillet; stir. Bring to a boil.
Stir in rice. Top with chicken; cover. Reduce heat to low. Cook 5 min. or until rice is tender. Sprinkle with onion rings.

Submitter: 
Minute® Rice
Description: 
Simmer chicken breasts, green beans, cream of mushroom soup, paprika and pepper, serve over Minute® Rice, and top with French fried onions to create a full meal in 20 minutes.
