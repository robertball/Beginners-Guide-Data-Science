Title:
Kielbasa with Honey Mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound kielbasa sausage
1 1/2 cups ginger ale
3-4 teaspoons brown sugar
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
1 onion sliced
honey mustard for dipping

Directions:
Preheat oven to broil. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil. Slice kielbasa.
In a medium-size skillet saute kielbasa in ginger ale until they are heated through. Remove kielbasa, set aside, add mushrooms and onion to sauce , reduce; cooking veggies till soft. Arrange the kielbasa and veggies on the prepared baking sheet, then sprinkle them with brown sugar.
Broil the kielbasa until the brown sugar melts and begins to crystallize, check after 3 to 5 minutes. Serve the kielbasa with honey mustard for dipping.

Description:
