Title:
Company French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups brown sugar
1 Cup butter
2 TBSP cinnamon
3 Cups Milk
12 eggs well beaten
1/4 cup sugar
2 tsp vanilla
bread  Texas toast works well, needs to be a couple of days
old.

Directions:
Melt butter and add brown sugar and cinnamon. I add a little water to make it thinner.
In greaseed 9x13 pan drizzle the syrup (butter,brown sugar)to cover the bottom. Add layer of bread. Then alternate the syrup mixture with the bread. End with syrup mixture on the top.
Beat the sugar , milk, eggs, and vanilla.
Pour over bread until covered. Cover and refridgerate over night.
Bake 350   1-11/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Carla Calhoun
Description: 
make the night before and just bake and eat.
