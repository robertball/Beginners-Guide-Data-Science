Title:
Taco Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz ground beef, cooked and drained
1 oz taco seasoning mix
4 oz cheddar cheese, shredded
2 pkg crescent roll dough
2 T water
1/2 head lettuce, shredded
1 small onion, chopped
1 c salsa
1 bell peppeer
1 tomato, cubed
1/2 cup black olives, sliced
Sour cream

Directions:
Oven to 375 - use baking stone
Combine cooked meat, taco seasoning, cheese and water in bowl and mix well.
Arrange crescent triangles in a circle on baking stone with bases overlapping in center and points facing out.  There should be apx 5 ince diameter circle in the center of stone.
Scoop meat mixture over crescent rolls
Fold points of triangles over filling and tuck under base at center (filling will not be completely covered)
Bake for 25 to 25" or until golden brown.
in meantime, cut top off bell pepepr and remove interior membrane.  Fill with salsa.
When crescent ring is done, place bell pepper in the center of ring.  Mound lettuce, onion, tomato and olives around bell pepper and garnish with sour cream.
