Title:
Pineapple Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) package fat free Cream Cheese
1 (3 oz) package sugar-free , instant cheesecake pudding
1 cup cold fat free milk
1 (8oz) container sugar-free Cool Whip, thawed
1 lg. can pineapple , drained
1 lg. graham cracker pie crust or 2 purchased graham cracker crust
4  Almond cookies, crumbled

Directions:
Beat cream cheese until fluffy, add pineapple and stir.  In another bowl beat  milk and pudding 1 Â½ minutes, add to cream cheese mixture. Add 1/3 of the cool whip, mix well. Fold in the rest of the cool whip,  crumble almond cookies on top. Freeze for 1 hour ,then refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Rosalie
Description: 
A simple to make recipe, but delicious. Makes 2 pies with purchased graham cracker crust, or 1 pie if you make a large graham cracher crust.
