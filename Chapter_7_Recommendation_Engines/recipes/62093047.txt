Title:
Restaurant Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup milk
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1 teaspoon celery seed
pepper to taste
4 cups shredded cabbage
1 small carrot

Directions:
mix the mayonnaise, sugar, buttermilk, milk,lemon juice,vinegar and celery seed in a medium bowl.
Stir in shredded cabbage and shredded carrot. Let sit for a few hours, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Gina
Description: 
This is a very good old fashioned cole slaw, similiar to many you find in diners. Simply delicious everytime.
