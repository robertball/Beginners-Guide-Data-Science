Title:
Baked Macaroni with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. Rotini Pasta
6 T. Margarine
5 T. Flour
3.75 cups Milk
4 T. Cheese
Diced Ham
Salt and Pepper
Paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Make a roux with margarine and flour. Add milk to roux. Add salt and pepper to taste. Cook pasta per package directions. Add mixture to pasta. Put half of mixture in casserole dish and sprinkle with cheese. Put the rest of mixture on top of first layer and sprinkle with cheese. Sprinkle casserole with paprika and add little dabs of margarine throughout the casserole. Bake 375 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
