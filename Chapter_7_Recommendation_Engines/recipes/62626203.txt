Title:
Supreme Banana Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 8 or 9 inch graham cracker crust (either pre-made or made from scratch)
1 - 4 serving size instant banana or vanilla pudding mix
1 cup heavy whipping cream, well chilled
3/4 cup milk, well chilled
1 to 2 ripe bananas, peeled and sliced
8 ounce Cool Whip, thawed

Directions:
Pour milk into mixing bowl. Using whisk gradually whisk pudding mix into the milk. Using electric mixer gradually beat in the whipping cream. Beat on highest speed until soft peaks form. Spoon 1/3 of the mixture into pie crust. Evenly layer half of the bananas over pudding, spoon 1nother 1/3 of pudding over bananas, layer rest bananas and finish with last 1/3 of pudding. Spoon and spread the Cool Whip over all. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
FunwithFood
Description: 
Super easy! Super decadent! I created this pie for my son who loves banana cream pie. I am not a very big fan of traditional banana cream pie, but I thoroughly enjoy every bite of this one!
