Title:
Cut-out Cookies (vegan) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cup organic flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt (a little less if using sea salt)
1 cup baker's sugar
1/2 cup Soy Garden buttery spread
1/2 cup soy milk
1/2 Tbl. vinegar
1 Tbl. cornstarch
2 Tbl. soy milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. flavouring (almond, rose water or other)

Directions:
In medium bowl combine flour, baking soda, powder, and salt.
In mixing bowl, cream sugar and buttery spread.
Whisk vinegar into the 1/2 cup soymilk.
In another bowl, whisk cornstarch into 2 Tbl. cold soymilk.
Beat soured soymilk and cornstarch/milk in mixing bowl.
Add vanilla and flavouring.
On a low speed, add flour slowly to mixture.
Divide dough into two balls.
Wrap dough balls in saran/plastic film.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
Un-wrap one ball of dough and roll out on floured wax paper.
Roll out to 1/4 inch thickness.
Cut out desired shapes and place on air-bake cookie sheet.
Bake6-8 minutes until edges are firm.
Cool completely before frosting.

Submitter: 
dreaming*vegan
Description: 
A delicious yet vegan version of the classic cut-out/roll-out cookie.  Not just for the holidays! As a bonus, this recipe is dairy, egg, cholesterol and trans-fat free!
