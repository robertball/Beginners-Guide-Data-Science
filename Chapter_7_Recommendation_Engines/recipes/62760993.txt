Title:
Camarones al Ajillo (Garlic Shrimp) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/638516.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound large shrimp, raw and shelled
1 large onion, sliced
1/2 green bell pepper, sliced
10 garlic cloves, pressed or minced
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup butter
1 Tablespoon dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
1/2 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat skillet to medium heat
Add olive oil and butter
When butter melts, add onions and green bell peppers, cook until softened, about 3 to 5 minutes
Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute
Add shrimp and cook about 2 minutes per side until opaque and cooked through, do not overcook.
Add tomato sauce, parsley and crushed red pepper.
Season with salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
LatinaCook
Description: 
Quick, easy and delicious shrimp that can be served over white rice or mofongo.
