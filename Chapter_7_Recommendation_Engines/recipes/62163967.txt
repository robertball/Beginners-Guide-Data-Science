Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Volcano Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c chopped peanuts
1 box chocolate cake mix
1/2 c peanut butter
8 ounce pkg cream cheese, softened
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
8 chocolate peanut butter cups, chopped
4 T butter
1/4 c cocoa powder
1/2 c milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Spray the bottom and sides of a 9 x 13 pan with non-stick cooking spray
Spread chopped peanuts on the bottom of pan
Make cake mix according to directions on box
Stir chopped chocolate peanut butter cups into cake mixture
Pour cake mixture into greased pan
Cream cheese mixture:
Mix softened cream cheese, peanut butter and 2 cups of powdered sugar in large bowl with mixer until smooth
Drop cream cheese mixture by tablespoonfuls onto top of cake mixture
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until cake is done
Glaze mixture:
Melt butter in small saucepan
Add cocoa powder and milk and bring to boil
Reduce heat to medium
Add 1 1/2 cups of powdered sugar and stir
Take off of heat and let cool somewhat
Glaze mixture will be thin
Pour over prepared cake
Glaze will pool in cream cheese mixture craters
