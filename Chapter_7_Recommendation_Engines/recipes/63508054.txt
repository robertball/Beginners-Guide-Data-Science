Title:
Mark Bittman's Amazing Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 medium onions chopped
2 carrots chopped
2 celery stalks chopped
2 cups of hard vegetables like potatos, parsnips, or turnips
12 cups of vegetable broth
2 cups crushed tomatoes
1 cup of green beans, cut in inch pieces
1 cup of zuchinni squash, chopped
1 cup of parsley chopped
4 cups of cooked beans
1 cup small pasta (optional)
Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a large, deep stock pot over medium heat.
Saute the onion, carrot, and celery until onion softens, about 5 minutes.
Add the hard vegetables, cook stirring for 2 minutes.
Add the broth and tomatoes.  Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to a low simmer and mixture is gently bubbling.  Stir occasionally and cook for 15 minutes.
Add the soft vegetables and parsley and bring to a low simmer. Stir occasionally and cook for 15 minutes.
Add remaining olive oil and serve immediately.
Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
