Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
3 tablespoons of smooth peanut butter
10 oz. (about 40) regular-sized marshmallows
1 cup cooked kale
6 cups Kellogg's Rice Krispies cereal

Directions:
Microwave butter with marshmallows on high setting for 2 minutes in a microwave safe mixing bowl. Stir together when melted.
Add in the peanut butter and microwave on high for 30 seconds.
Stir in kale. (This may summon a wild Misha, do at your own risk.)
Stir in cereal, mixing until well coated.
Press the mixtute evenly into a pan which has been buttered or sprayed with non-stick spray or lined with wax paper.
Cut into squares when the mixture cools.

Submitter: 
Abby Huang
Description: 
After a long day of  gishwhes, Dinomite loves to chomp down on these yummy treats and now you can too!
