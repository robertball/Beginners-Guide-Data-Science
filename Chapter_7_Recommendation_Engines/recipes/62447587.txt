Title:
Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz spaghetti
1-½ cups shredded cheddar cheese, low fat
1-½ cups shredded Monterey jack cheese
1-½ cups onion, diced (or 1/2 Tbsp. dried minced onion)
1 10.75oz. can cream of chicken soup, condensed
1 10.75oz. can cream of mushroom soup, condensed
10 ounces milk, 2% low fat (1 soup can)
3 cups chicken – diced
8-10 oz. bag frozen sweet peas

Directions:
Cook spaghetti until al dente, drain.
Sauté onions with cooking spray.
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
Divide into bags and freeze.
To serve: Thaw Tetrazzini and put in baking dish. Bake uncovered in preheated 350F oven until bubbly, about 30 to 40 minutes. You can top with bread crumbs and more grated cheese.
