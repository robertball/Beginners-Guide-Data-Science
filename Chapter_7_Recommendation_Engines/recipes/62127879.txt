Title:
Flourless Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200 gms best quality bittersweet chocolate
125 gms butter, softened
125 gms caster sugar
4 eggs, separated
200 gms peeled and finely ground almonds

Directions:
Braek the chocolate into pieces in a bowl and set the bowl over simmering water to melt the chocolate, stirring occasionaly.
Cream the butter abd suag until light and fluffy. Separate the eggs, and add the yolks to the butter mixture together with the ground almonds and melted chocolate. Mix well to combine.
Whisk the egg whites until stiff and add to the batter, mixing gently.
Put the batter into a 8inch round pan, and baker in a preheated oven at 180 degrees centigrade for about 20 minutes or until done.
Cool in the pan for about 15 minutes before inverting onto a plate.

Submitter: 
Priyanka
Description: 
The addition of ground almonds sets this cake apart from others, and gives it a a rich and tender crumb. Serve warm with a dollop of cream for a truely luxurious dessert.
