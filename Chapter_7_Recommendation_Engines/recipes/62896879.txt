Title:
Caprese Salad Sandwhiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/701058.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Heirloom or Beefsteak tomato or 3 roma tomatoes still somewhat firm
1 package/handful fresh basil leaf
Enough White truffle oil or olive oil to drizzle over 8 pieces of bread
8 Pieces of heavy/dark artisan loaf bread.
Salt/Pepper to taste
1 Package buffalo mozzarella or bocconcini

Directions:
Lay out each piece of bread and drizzle lightly with truffle or olive oil.
Place thinly sliced mozzarella or bocconcini until most or all of the bread is covered.
Cover with basil leaves.
Top basil leaves with thinly sliced tomatoes.
Salt and Pepper to taste.
Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Chef Noir
Description: 
Everyone knows there's no salad like a caprese salad on a hot summer day. Fresh picked basil and tomatoes with just a hint of seasoning. Muah!
