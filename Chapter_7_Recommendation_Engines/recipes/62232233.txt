Title:
Cracker Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. package of cream cheese, softened
4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
8 oz. cooked and chopped ham or crab meat
1 small onion, chopped
1 jalapeno or serano pepper, seeded and chopped
snack crackers

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together the cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, ham or crab meat, onion and pepper.
Bake for 30 minutes or until the dip is heated throughout.
Serve with crackers.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jeanne Mason
Description: 
This super easy crowd pleaser can be made with either ham or crab meat. I used some left over ham from a holiday dinner.
