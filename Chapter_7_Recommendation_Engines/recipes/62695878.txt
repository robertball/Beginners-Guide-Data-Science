Title:
Cioppino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup olive oil
1 medium onion (finely diced)
2 ribs of celery (finely diced)
6 cloves of garlic (minced)
1 lemon (juiced)
2 bay leaves
2 tsp dried basil
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp oregano
2 tsp worchester
3/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp salt
dash of pepper
dash of cayenne
dash of paprika
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 14 oz can tomato sauce
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
1 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken broth
1 lb cod
a mixture of favorite seafoods (cleaned and raw) (Costco has a seafood medly "mixture" that works great)
scallops
shrimp
clams or mussles
salmon
calamari

Directions:
In large pot, over medium heat, saut√© onion and celery until tender (approx 10 minutes).
Turn heat to low and add garlic, and all other ingredients, EXCEPT THE SEAFOOD.  Simmer covered  for about 2 hrs over low heat add more broth if it gets to thick (I didn't need to).
Add seafood and cook for about 10 additional minutes over med/low heat until seafood is cooked. Serve hot.
