Title:
Celebration Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/21228.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups double strength brewed tea
3/4 cup white sugar
2 1/2 cups orange juice
1 1/2 cups grapefruit juice
2/3 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup lime juice
6 cups ginger ale, chilled

Directions:
Brew tea for 3 minutes. Stir in sugar; set aside to cool.
When tea is cool, stir in orange juice, grapefruit juice, lemon juice and lime juice. Just before serving, stir in chilled ginger ale.

Submitter: 
Mitzi
Description: 
Non-alcoholic, not too sweet punch. My friends request it for every party!
