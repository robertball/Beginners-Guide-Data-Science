Title:
Parmesan and garlic crust sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices sourdough bread
1/4 cup freshly shredded parmesan
1 pressed garlic clove
1 c margarine, softened
1 lb. meat of choice (turkey, ham, roast beef or combination)
1 lb. cheese of choice (pepperjack, colby, cheddar etc)
1 large tomato
1/2 lb. bacon

Directions:
Preheat frying pan over med low heat.
Sweat the bacon in the frying pan until crispy.
In a small bowl, mix margarine, garlic and parmesan cheese.
Spread margarine mix over one side of each slices of bread.
Place one slice of bread, margarine side down, in frying pan.  Layer with cheese, meat, tomato, bacon and another layer cheese, then another slice bread, margarine side up.
Fry each side until nicely golden brown and sandwhich is warmed through.
I use a very large spatula to flip these large sandwhiches.

Submitter: 
Brandon Willard Gossman
Description: 
This is a wonderful grilled sandwich that my family loves.  It combines a number of cheeses, meats, tomatoes, bacon and sourdough bread.
