Title:
Nana Daniele's Italian Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C. Balsamic or Red Wine Vinegar*
1/2 C. LIGHT olive oil or vegetable oil*
1 Tbl. white sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried Italian herb mix
3 cloves of garlic, peeled and gently pressed and skewered onto a tooth pick

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients into a jar, seal with the lid and shake.  Refrigerate the dressing overnight or longer before serving.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
This simple, never-written-down salad dressing recipe, was always in my Nana's and my mother's refrigerator.
