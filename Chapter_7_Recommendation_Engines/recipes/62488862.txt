Title:
Deep Fried Chimichangas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
1 teaspoon of garlic powder
1 (1 oz) package of taco seasoning
1 (10.5 oz) can of cream of chicken soup
1 (8 oz) package of cream cheese
1 (8 oz) package of shredded cheddar cheese
Seasoning Salt (to taste)
Flour tortillas (Buritto size, at least 8)
Toothpicks
Vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Boil chicken breasts until cooked thoroughly. Shred chicken with two forks, place in a bowl.
Mix in garlic powder, taco seasoning, cream of chicken soup, cream cheese, shredded cheese, and finally your seasoning salt until well-blended.
Heat oil in skillet.
Place mixture in flour tortillas, roll and secure with toothpicks, fry evenly on all sides until golden-brown, take out toothpicks and serve.

Submitter: 
Samantha
Description: 
Chimichangas may be frozen to enjoy at a later date!
