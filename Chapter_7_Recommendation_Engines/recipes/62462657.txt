Title:
Restaurant Style Hamburger Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1 egg, lightly beaten
2 egg yolks, lightly beaten
2 tablespoons fine dry bread crumbs
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine and blend the ground beef, chopped onion, beaten egg and egg yolks, bread crumbs, salt, and pepper. Mold the meat mixture into six 1/2-inch thick oblong steaks. In a large skillet, heat 3 tablespoons butter over medium-high heat. Add the beef and brown on both sides, until inside is no longer pink.

Submitter:

Description: 
Very tender hamburger steak with great flavor.  Also good with A-1 Steak Sauce.
