Title:
** Microwave Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. raw peanuts
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. light corn syrup
dash salt
1 Tbl. butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Mix peanuts, sugar, corn syrup, & salt in glass mixing bowl.
Microwave on HIGH for 7-9 min. Mixture should be bubbling & peanuts browned.
Quickly stir in butter & vanilla. Cook 2-3 min. longer.
Add baking soda & stir quickly until mixture is foamy.
Pour onto greased baking sheet & let cool. Break up brittle & store in air-tight container.
