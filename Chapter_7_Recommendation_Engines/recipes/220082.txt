Title:
Johnsonville Easy Sausage Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988203.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (19.76 ounce) package JohnsonvilleÂ® Mild Italian Sausage Links or Ground Sausage
1 12-inch pre-baked packaged pizza crust
1 cup pizza sauce
1 1/4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped

Directions:
Remove sausage from casing and pinch into dime-sized pieces.
Spread sauce over crust; sprinkle with 1 cup cheese.
Add Italian Sausage pieces and green pepper.
Top with the remaining cheese.
Bake according to crust package directions or until sausage is no longer pink (160 degrees F) and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
The Kitchen at Johnsonville Sausage
Description: 
Pre-baked pizza crust, topped with sausage, green peppers and cheese makes a quick and easy dinner.
