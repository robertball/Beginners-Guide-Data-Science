Title:
Coconut Shrimptastrophy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2511121.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Cups	Flour, divided
5 Cups Cold Club soda (or Beer)
1 Tablespoon +1 Teaspoon Baking powder
1 Tablespoon +1 Teaspoon Baking soda
Â½ teaspoon Salt
8 Pounds Shrimp, Tail On, Peeled and Deveined
8 Cups Shredded Unsweetened Coconut
2 Quart	Canola Oil

Directions:
Place two thirds of the flour (4 cups), club soda, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Whisk the ingredients until batter is not quite smooth.
Pour canola oil into deep-fat fryer. Heat oil to 350F. Dust Shrimp with remaining flour. Dip shrimp into the batter, then place it on a baking sheet to drain for 5 minutes. Place coconut on a rimmed baking sheet. Roll battered shrimp in coconut.
Pour canola oil into deep-fat fryer. Heat oil to 350F.
Place an uncrowded single layer of shrimp into the heated oil and fry until shrimp float in the oil and are golden brown. Remove shrimp and drain on paper toweling.
It usually takes about 10 batches in a two quart fryer to complete the frying.
