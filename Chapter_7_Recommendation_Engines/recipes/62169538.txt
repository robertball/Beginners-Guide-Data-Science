Title:
Real Canadian Butter Tarts, eh? Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/285040.jpg
Ingredients:
12 unsweetened tart shells
½ cup packed brown sugar
½ cup maple syrup
¼ cup butter
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 t vanilla
¼ t salt
¾ cup raisins
Walnuts (to top)

Directions:
Melt butter.
Mix everything in bowl except egg.
When mixture is not too hot, add egg (if too hot, egg will scramble).
Add raisins into tart shells (if you add them last, you will overflow your tarts).
Pour mixture into tarts. Sprinkle each tart with chopped walnuts.
Bake for 425 C for 12 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
mrsmambo
Description: 
These butter tarts are traditionally Canadian, made with sweet maple syrup.
