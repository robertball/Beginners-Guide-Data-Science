Title:
Messed up chocolate balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup graham crackers, crushed.
1/2 cup chocolate powder (sweeten cocoa).
1/2 cup sour cream.

Directions:
Stir together the crushed crackers and sweeten cocoa.
Add sour cream slowly, until you have a rich and black dough. Depending on the crackers, you may use a bit less or more than 1/2 cup.
Get ready for the fun part! Just let your children use their hands to form beautiful (multi-sized) balls. Place them in a nice tray.If you wish, decorate with confectioner´s sugar and then put into fridge.Allow to chill for about 40 minutes and your kids can enjoy them, this time without getting sticky fingers.(as if that was possible, ha!)

Submitter: 
Charlie cheesecake
Description: 
I hope that you get at least 12  of this delicious balls, but I can´t guarantee that your kids won´t eat the whole dough before you manage to make the first one! This recipe is perfect for preparing with the help of your little ones, or just guide them if you don´t want to end up with chocolatey dirt in your ten fingers.
