Title:
Spicy Lime Crockpot Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Pound Chuck Steak cut in thin slices
1 14.5 oz can Diced Tomatoes with Green Chilies
1 Onion, diced
1 TBSP Chili Powder
1/2 tsp Garlic Granules
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 Lime
1 tsp Cornstarch mixed
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
Finely zest the lime and squeeze out the juice.
Place the beef, tomatoes, onion, chili powder, garlic, salt, pepper, lime zest and lime juice in the Crockpot. Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours.
Mix cornstarch with water and mix into beef, breaking the beef up some. Cook for 20 minutes more.
Serve over rice or mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Mark E Morley
Description: 
This is a spicy Lime slow cooked beef that I made up and my family loved it. I used Chuck Steak that had all ready been cut Carne Asada Style. You can use any Chuck Roast or steaks and just slice thinly.
