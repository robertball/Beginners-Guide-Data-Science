Title:
Ham Macaroni and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c milk
1/2c butter
1tsp pepper
12oz velveeta cheese cubed
2c. leftover, cubed or sliced ham
1 head broccoli chopped and steamed
16oz Macaroni cooked
Bread crumbs
Parmasan cheese

Directions:
Heat milk, butter and pepper on Med/Low until it begins to simmer. Add velveeta to it until melted through. Mix in broccoli and ham and pour over macaroni in 9x13 baking dish and sprinkle w/ bread crumbs and parmasan cheese. Bake at 350F for 15 min. or until cheese is bubbling.
