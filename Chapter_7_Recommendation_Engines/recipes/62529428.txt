Title:
Rackhouse Pub Beer-Baked Mac n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup amber beer
2 cups half and half
1/2 lb brie
16oz cream cheese
1 1/2 cup gorgonzola chese
2 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, divided
1 (16) oz box penne pasta, cooked
salt and pepper
1/2 cup panko or bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degees.  In medium pot, melt butter, whisk in flour to form a roux.  Slowly add beer and half and half.  Add cheeses one at a time until incorporated, only using one cup of the parmesan.  Stir in penne pasta.  Season to taste.  Pour mixture into 9x13 pan.  Top with remaining parmesan cheese and bread crumbs.  Bake for one hour.  Let cool slightly before serving.

Submitter:
