Title:
Honey and Garlic Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
3 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
5 Tablespoons Honey
4 Cloves of Garlic, minced
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Ginger
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Pepper
2-3 pounds Chicken (I prefer thighs)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425°F
Mix olive oil, soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, ginger and ground pepper in a large ziploc bag. Add the chicken to the plastic bag. Make sure to coat all of the chicken pieces. Marinate overnight and/or
Place chicken (skin side down) and sauce in a baking dish, bake at 425° for 25 minutes. Turn the chicken and cook another 15 - 20 minutes depending on the size of your chicken pieces (internal temperature reads 165°F).
