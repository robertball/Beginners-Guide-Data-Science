Title:
cranberry relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. orange juice
1/3 to 1/2 c. sugar or other sweetener
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
dash cloves
3 c. fresh cranberries
1/2 c. raisins
1/2 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
In a saucepan combine first five ingredients.  Cook and stir till sugar is dissolved, then add berries and raisins.  Bring to boil, reduce heat and cook and stir till berries pop (about 4 minutes).  Remove from heat and stir in pecans.
Cover and chill at least 2 hours

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
Twist on plain cranberries--tastes like a dessert! (fromBH &G;)
