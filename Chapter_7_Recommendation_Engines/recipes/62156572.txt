Title:
crab cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# of crab meat
3oz of sour cream and onion pringles chips CRUSHED.
1/4cp of minced onions
10 crackers. crushed
2tbsp of mustard
5tbsp of dill mayo dip
1tsp of hot sauce
1tsp of cracked blacl pepper
1 tbsp of olive oil
2tsp of lime juice
3tbs of parmesan cheese
1tsp of seasalt.
2 eggs
2tbsp of worcestershire sauce

Directions:
mix all ingregients BUT THE CRAB.in bowl. taste it. ajust it.
add crab mix gently!!
make patties let set in frig for a 1hr
add to hot pan with oil. cook till brown on one side flip when brown, squeeze a little lemon juice on before eatiing.
