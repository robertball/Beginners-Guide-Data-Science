Title:
Crispy Grilled Buffalo Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3# chicken wings, cuth into drumettes and flats
1 TBL baking powder
1 tea cayenne pepper
1 tea salt
Sauce:
4 TBL unsalted butter
1/2 cup Frank's hot sauce
2 TBL "other" hot sauce
1 TBL brown sugar
1 TBL honey
2 tea cider vinegar
1/4 tea cayenne pepper

Directions:
1.  Pat wings dry.  Place wings in a large bowl and sprinkle with baking powder, cayenne, and salt.
2.  Arrange the wings in a single layer on a wire rack over a baking sheet.  Refrigerate uncovered for at least 8 hours
3.  Place butter in small saucepan and melt over low heat.  Whisk in remaining ingredients.  Keep on low heat until needed.
4.  Place the wings skin side up over the cool side of the grill.  Cook until skins are crisp and starting to brown....about 40 minutes.
5.  Flip wings over and move to the hot side of the grill and continue to cook until the skins just begin to char....about 2-3 minutes.
6.  Remove wings to bowl and pour sauce over wings until coated evenly.
