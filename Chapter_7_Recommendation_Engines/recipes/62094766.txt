Title:
Creamy Orange-Soy Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup light mayonnaise
1/2 cup orange marmalade
1/4 cup Low Sodium Soy Sauce
2 or 3 tablespoons sugar, to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a jar, fasten lid, and shake, shake, shake! Allow to sit in refrigerator for one hour before use, so that flavors can blend.

Submitter: 
Janna
Description: 
This dressing is excellent for summer salads, when you want something creamy and slightly fruity. Goes well with my Summer Strawberry Salad, posted on this site.
