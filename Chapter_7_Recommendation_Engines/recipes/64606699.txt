Title:
Baked Eggplant Turkish Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Small eggplant
Salt
1/4 Cup EVOO (Olive Oil)
1 Large onion, chopped
1 Garlic clove, crushed
1 Red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
2 Tablespoons tomato paste
2 Oz. oil packed sun dried tomatoes , drained and chopped
1/2 Teaspoon  sugar
1 Teaspoon wine vinegar
Black pepper
Toasted pine nuts
Cilantro leaves

Directions:
1. Cut the eggplants into 1/4 inch thick slices, sprinkle with salt and set in colander to drain for 30 minutes.
2. Preheat the oven 350 F.
3. In a skillet, heat 1 tablespoon EVOO, add onion, garlic and bell pepper and cook 10 minutes until onion is soft.
4. Stir in the tomato paste, sugar, vinegar and black pepper and set aside.
5. Pat the eggplant slices dry with paper towels and arrange slices in an lightly oiled baking pan.
6. Drop a teaspoonful of the mixture onto each eggplant slice then drizzle with remaining  EVOO over and around.
7. Cover  with foil and bake 40-50 minutes until the eggplant slices are tender.
8. Garnish with toasted pine nuts and cilantro leaves.
9. Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
The dish is simple to prepare, but it does take a little time to cook. It is served at room temperature  so do allow time for it to cool properly.
