Title:
Ham and Potato Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. heavy cream
1 c. half & half
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tsp. salt
2 1/2 lb. red potatoes, sliced 1/8 in. thick
3/4 lb. ham, diced into 3/4 in. cubes
1 1/2 c. Gruyere cheese, shredded
pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine cream, half & half, garlic, salt, & pepper in large saucepan.
Add sliced potatoes and stir gently to completely coat.
Bring to simmer and cook for 25 min. or until potatoes are tender.  Do not boil.
Gently stir in ham.
Adjust seasonings and pour into a baking dish.
Sprinkle with cheese and bake in preheated 425 oven until golden and liquid has been absorbed, about 20 min

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
The ham makes this hearty enough for a main dish, but it makes a really fancy side.
