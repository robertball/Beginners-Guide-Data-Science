Title:
Peppered Cilantro Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985661.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped sweet red pepper
1/2 cup finely chopped sweet yellow pepper
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tablespoon olive or canola oil
2 cups water
1 cup uncooked long grain rice
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro or parsley

Directions:
In a saucepan, saute the onion, peppers and garlic in oil for 2-4 minutes or until crisp-tender. Add the water, rice, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 18-22 minutes or until the rice is tender. Remove from the heat. Add cilantro; fluff with a fork.

Submitter: 
Laura  Perry
Description: 
This colorful confetti rice is a traditional dish in Puerto Rico. We enjoy it in the summer alongside grilled shrimp kabobs, but it is good with most any entree.                                   -Laura Perry of Exton, Pennsylvania
