Title:
Ybor City Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the salad:
4 cups iceburg lettuce, broken into 1 1/2" x 1 1/2" pieces
1 ripe tomato, cut into eighths
1/2 cup baked ham, julienned 2" x 1/8"
1/2 cup Swiss cheese, juliennne 2" x 1/8"
1/2 cup pimento stuffed green Spanish olives
dressing (see below)
1/2 cup Romano cheese, grated
2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
1 lemon
For the dressing:
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
4 garlic cloves, minced
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1/8 cup white wine vinegar
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
For the salad:
Place the lettuce, tomato, ham, Swiss, and olives in a large serving bowl.  Immediately before serving add the dressing, worcestershire, Romano, and the juice of one lemon.  Toss and serve.
For the dressing:
Place the olive oil, garlic, and oregano in a bowl and mix with a wisk.  Wisk in the vinegar until an emulsion forms.  Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This wonderful salad is inspired by the Cuban and Spanish flavors found in the restaurants in the Ybor City area of Tampa, Florida.  Serious garlic lovers only!  Add grilled shrimp for a terrific main course salad.  The dressing is best made several days in advance.
