Title:
The Best Southern Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10oz. pkg. frozen peas & carrots
1/3 C. butter
1/3 C. all-purpose flour
1/3 C. chopped onion
1/2 ts. salt
1/4 ts. pepper
1 3/4 C. chicken broth
2/3 C. buttermilk
2 1/2 - 3 C. cooked, chunked chicken
Pastries for double crust pie (I buy mine from the store to save time)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Rinse peas and carrots in cold water to defrost and set aside.
Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat.
Stir in flour, salt, peper, & onion.
Stirring constantly, cook until mixture becomes bubbly and remove from heat.
Stir in the buttermilk and broth, return to heat and bring to the boil.
Stirring constantly, boil for 1 minute.
Stir in chicken and veggies.
Roll 2/3 of the pastry into a 13-inch square.
Place into a 9x9x2 ungreased square pan.
Pour mixture into the shell.
Roll remaining pastry into 11-inch square.
Place over top of chicken mixture.
Turn pastry edges under and flute.
Cut several slits into the top.
Bake about 35 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Donna Bertheola LeMaster
Description: 
A rich, delicious version of this classic American dish. Fattening? Absolutely! It wouldn't be southern if it wasn't!
