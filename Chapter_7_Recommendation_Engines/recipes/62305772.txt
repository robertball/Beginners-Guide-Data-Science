Title:
Portobello Pesto Egg Omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 portobello mushroom, sliced
1/4 cup red onion
1 tsp olive oil
4 egg whites
1 tsp water
salt and pepper, to taste
1/4 cup shredded light mozzarella
1 tsp prepared pesto

Directions:
1. Saute the sliced mushroom, red onion, and olive oil in a medium pan over medium heat, until the mushroom is soft.
2. In a small bowl, whisk the egg whites and teaspoon of water together.
3. Add the eggs to the pan.
4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
5. Let the eggs cook until no longer runny.
6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top and add the pesto.
7. Fold the omelette in half in the pan and allow the cheese to melt. Remove from heat, and serve!

Submitter:

Description: 
Serve with whole wheat toast and you've got yourself a balanced, healthy breakfast!
