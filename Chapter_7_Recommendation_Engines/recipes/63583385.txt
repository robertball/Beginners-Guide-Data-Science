Title:
Deeli's Spicy Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen jumbo eggs
8 tbls mayonnaise
1 tbls Dijon mustard
2/3 cup stuffed Manzanilla olives, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
Tabasco sauce to taste
Garnish of choice

Directions:
Pierce large end of eggs with a needle to release cooking pressure, place in large pan and cover with cold water.
Bring to boil, simmer 12 minutes, pour off hot water, shake vigorously in pan to break shells, immediately give eggs an ice bath and allow to rest.
Shell eggs and slice in half lengthwise, carefully remove yolks, place yolks in a bowl and mash.
Add mayonnaise, mustard and olives to the yokes, stir well, season with salt, pepper and Tabasco to taste and stir again.
Pipe yolk mixture into the center of the egg whites, garnish and serve.

Submitter: 
Deeli
Description: 
No boring taste here; the ingredients in these eggs put the meaning of Devil back into deviled eggs!
