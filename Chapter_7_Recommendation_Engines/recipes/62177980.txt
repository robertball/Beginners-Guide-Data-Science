Title:
Jean's Zucchini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad
1 1/2 cup unpeeled, zucchini, sliced thin
1/2 cup celery, sliced thin
1/4 onion (red for color) sliced thin
1/4 bell pepper (any color) sliced thin
Marinade
1/4 cup rice vinegar
3 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
1 tablespoon wine vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
2 tablespoon water
approx. 6 tablespoon sugar or sweetener (liquid sweet substitute works well)

Directions:
1.  Slice the salad ingredients, mix and set aside
2.  Mix marinade ingredients
3.  Combine the two and mix well
4.  Refrigerate and serve cold

Submitter:

Description: 
My sister, Jean, found this recipe years ago - I always love it.  Great for pot lucks and BBQ's.
