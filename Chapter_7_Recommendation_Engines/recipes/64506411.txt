Title:
Quick and Easy Creamy and Smokey Risotto-Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 inch circle handfull of Angel Hair or Spaghetti broken into 1 inch long pieces
3 tbs olive oil
1 1/2 cups instant rice
1 cup milk
3/4 cup water
2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp chopped onion
1 tsp Parsley
3 tablespoon chicken bouillon
1 tsp pepper
4 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
3 tablespoon smoked cheese ( American or Gouda works great) * Key ingredient

Directions:
Combine oil and Angel hair in a medium pot over medium heat until pasta is browned (3 minutes)
Add remaining ingredients and cook over low heat covered until rice is done (approx 6-8 minutes)
Add parmesan and smoked cheese pieces, cook on low for 2 more minutes covered or until cheeses are melted.
The smoked cheese is what makes this dish!

Submitter: 
Holleta
Description: 
Somewhere between a Pilaf and Risotto. A quick and easy creamy rice dish that is gourmet quality with a subtle smoked cheese and herbs finish!
