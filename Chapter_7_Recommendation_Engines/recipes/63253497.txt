Title:
Peppered Beef w/ Flat Rice Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
marinade:
1 Tb. oyster sauce
1 Tb. soy sauce
1 tsp. black pepper
2 Tb. sherry
1 1/2 tsp. cornstarch
12 oz. sirloin stk, cut into thin strips
Sauce:
1 Tb. soy sauce
1/4 cup chicken stock
1 lb. flat rice noodles or fresh fettucine
2 Tb. vegtable oil
3 Tb. chopped shallots
1 Tb. finely chopped ginger
1 Tb. finely chopped garlic
3/4 cup slice green pepper
3/4 cup sliced red pepper
1 cup bean sprouts, packed down
fresh ground pepper

Directions:
In a med. bowl, combine ingredients for marinade. Add beef and marinade for 20 min.
In a small bowl, prepare sauce and set aside.
If using fresh rice noodles, break them up by placing in a colander, running hot water oer them and serarating the strands w/ your fingers. If using fettucine, prepare according to packcage directions, drain, and coat with a bit of oil.
in a large non-stick wok or skillet, heat oil over high heat for 30 seconds. Add shallots, ginger, garlic, stir fry 30 seconds. Add beef and stir fry for 2 min, add peppers and stir fry 2 min. Add noodles and sauce. add bean sprouts last and season with pepper.
