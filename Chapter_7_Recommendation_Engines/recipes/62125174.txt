Title:
Ginger and Rosemary Pork Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fresh lime juice
1 1/2 teaspoons rosemary
1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 clove garlic

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in bowl, stir and pour into a zipper bag.
Add pork and cover with marinade.
Place in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours.

Submitter: 
Cara
Description: 
A delicious way to zing up your pork chops or cutlets. Allow to marinate up to 24 hours for maximum flavor.

All measurements are approximate because I usually just throw them together! Adjust for your taste.
