Title:
Quiche from Laura Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded swiss
2 Tablespoons flour
1 1/2 cup half & half
4 eggs slightly beaten
3/4 cup meat (shrimp, ham, turkey, chicken)
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup olives
1/2 cup mushroom
1/2 cup onion
1 pastry shell

Directions:
Toss cheese with flour.
Add remaining ingredients mixing well.
Pour into pastry shell.
Bake at 350.
