Title:
Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter melted
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 3/4 cups flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2/3 cups sugar
1/3 cup shortening
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Grease 8 x 8 pan
Melt butter and stir in brown sugar and cinnamon
Set aside for top of cake
Measure flour without sifting into large mixing bowl, add baking powder, salt and sugar, and stir well to blend
Cut in shortening with pastry blender or 2 knives
Make a hollow and add egg, milk and vanilla, then combine with dry ingredients, mixing just until moistened
Turn into pan and sprinkle with brown sugar mixture
Bake 30-35 minutes
