Title:
Pizza Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Ground Beef
1 lg. Onion, chopped
2 Jars Spaghetti Sauce (16 oz. each)
1 pkg. Spiral Pasta, cooked and drained (16 oz.)
4 C Shredded, Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese
8 oz. Sliced Pepperoni

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
In a large skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and pasta.
Transfer to two greased 13x9x2 in. baking dishes. Sprinkle with cheese. Arrange pepperoni over the top.
Cover and freeze one casserole for up to three months.
Bake the second casserole, uncovered, for 25-30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Lainie Vansant
Description: 
This recipe makes two yummy casseroles - one can be kept in the freezer for up to three months. Submitted to the PUMC cookbook by Leandra Costello
