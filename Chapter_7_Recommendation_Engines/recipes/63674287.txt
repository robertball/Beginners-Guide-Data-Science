Title:
Chicken Yakisoba Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023238.jpg
Ingredients:
16 ounces of fresh soba noodles
2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
2 carrots peeled and cut into matchstick size pieces
3/4 cup green onions, cut into matchstick size pieces
1 red bell pepper core removed and cut into matchstick size pieces
2 cups of shredded green cabbage
8 ounces of boneless chicken breast cut into 1/2" slices.
1/2 cup of water
3/4 cup of stir-fry or teriyaki sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl, soak the noodles until softened.
Add 1 Tbsp. of oil to a large wok or fry pan over medium-high heat.
Add Carrots, onion and bell peppers, and cook til just softened, 3-5 min.
Add cabbage and cook for 1-2 minutes.
Remove vegetables from pan and set aside.
Add another Tbsp of oil to pan, and sautee chicken over medium-high heat until browned.
Add the water and softened noodles and put lid on pan.
Steam Chicken and noodles for 2 minutes.
Return Vegetables to pan until heated back through.
Add stir-fry or teriyaki sauce, and stir to thouroughly coat all ingredients with sauce.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
Yakisoba means fried noodles in sauce or, as most people call it, stir fry. Yakisoba noodles are very similar to ramen.
