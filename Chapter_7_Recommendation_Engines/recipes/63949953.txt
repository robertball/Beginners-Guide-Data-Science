Title:
Creamy White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1095676.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound of boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into 1/2 inch cubes
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon of canola oil
2 cans (15 oz each) great northern beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (14 oz) of chicken broth **(Can substitute chicken bouillon)
2 cans (4 oz each) of chopped green chilies
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon of pepper
1/2 dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon of cumin
1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
2 cups (8 oz) of sour cream
2 cups of heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In a large sauce pan, saute the chicken, onion and garlic powder in oil, until chicken is no longer pink. Add the beans, broth, chilies and seasoning. Bring to boil. Reduce the heat; simmer, undercover, for 30 minutes.
Remove from the heat; stir in the sour cream and cream.
