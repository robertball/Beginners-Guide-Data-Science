Title:
Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ground beef
Taco seasoning
Cream cheese
Sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Get out baking pan, (Glass.)
2. Cook ground beef, drain, mix in taco seasoning.
3. Spread cream cheese on bottom of pan, then add ground beef, then top with cheddar cheese.
4. Wait until 5 minutes before you plan on putting it out, and stick in oven at 350 degrees.
