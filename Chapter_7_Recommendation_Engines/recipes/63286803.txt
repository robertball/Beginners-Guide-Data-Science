Title:
Split Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups all purpose Flour
1 Cup Split Flour
1¾ Cups Milk
¼ Cup Butter
3 Tablespoons Wheat Gluten
1 teaspoon salt
1 package yeast

Directions:
Combine all ingredients knead for 15 - 20 minutes.  Let rise until doubled. Form into 16 rolls or 4 small loaves.  Let rise in 200° oven for about 15 minutes.  Bake at 325° for about 33 minutes.
