Title:
Spiced and Sweet Butternut Squash Pie Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/471017.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large butternut Squash
1 cup brown sugar (or more if desired)
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 Tbsp Vanilla
2 Tbsp Pumpkin pie spice (or more if desired)
1 tsp of cinnamon
1 stick of butter unsalted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Prepare squash; slice lengthways and scoop seeds.
Leaving skin on, put slices stood up in 1 inch of water in a large caserole dish and bake for approx 45 mins -1 hour - the softer it is the easier to blend. Discard water and cool. Once cooled remove skins.
Mash or whip squash until smooth
add all other ingreadients and mix thoroughly.
