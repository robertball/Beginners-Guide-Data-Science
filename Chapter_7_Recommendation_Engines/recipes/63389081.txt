Title:
Parmesan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/912191.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Real Mayonnaise
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
4 tsp. Panko bread crumbs
1 tbsp Dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°.
Combine Real Mayonnaise with cheese in medium bowl.
Arrange chicken on baking sheet. Evenly top with mayonnaise mixture, then sprinkle with bread crumbs, and dried parsley.
Bake 20 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
Mayonnaise transforms your chicken into a juicier, crispier, more delicious meal.
