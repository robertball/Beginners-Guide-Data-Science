Title:
Northern Buttermilk Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2/3 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 cup buttermilk
1 to 2 cups of corn
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Pre heat to 375*F
Grease your 2  8x8 pan + double the recipe.
Melt your butter set aside
Measure out buttermilk, sugar, 2 eggs and corn-mix it all together(if using powered buttermilk add 1 cup water at this time) - add butter.
Mix all dry ingredients (if using buttermilk powder add this in now).
Gently fold it all together, make sure to get to the bottom, but don't over mix.
Pour equally into both pans, cook 30-40 min or so;
