Title:
Powdered Sugar Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C sifted powdered sugar
1 T butter
1 or 2 T milk
1/2 t vanilla

Directions:
In a small bowl blend ingredients until smooth.  Spread or drizzle on slightly warm coffee cake, cinnamon rolls, etc.

Submitter: 
munchmn
Description: 
Confectioners sugar glaze for coffee cakes and sweet rolls, love it because it's easy, sets up nicely and I always have the ingredients!  
Sometimes I make 1 1/2 times the amount if more glaze is desired.
