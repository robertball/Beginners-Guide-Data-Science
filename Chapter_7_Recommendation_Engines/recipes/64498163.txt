Title:
Kale and Quinoa Patties with Herb Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2611839.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoon Olive Oil, divided
1/2 onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
1 bunch of Kale, stems removed and roughly chopped (about 1 1/2 cups)
1 1/4 cup cooked Quinoa
1/4 cup cooked Lentils (optional)
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup shredded (or crumbled) feta
1 tablespoon parsley
1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
Salt & pepper to taste
Sauce:
3 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
6 Fresh Basil leaves
1 Tablespoon Fresh Parsley
2 teaspoons Dill (dried)
1 Teaspoon Lemon Juice
Salt & Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large cast iron skillet.
Add onion and garlic and cook until translucent, stirring often about 4 minutes.
Stir in kale and cook for 4-5 minutes or until wilted.
In large bowl, add quinoa, lentils, eggs, feta, parsley, salt, pepper and bread crumbs. Mix well.
Stir kale mixture into quinoa mixture and combine. Once combined form into patties.
Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in skillet over medium heat. Add patties leaving space between each patty. Cover and cook for 5 minutes or until patties are golden brown.
Flip patties with spatula and cook for another 5 minutes or until brown. Transfer skillet to oven and broil for 3-5 minutes to crisp up the top.
In the meantime, make the sauce. Combine all ingredients and mix well.
Serve patties with dollop of sauce. Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
I needed a recipe to use up leftover Quinoa and Lentils, this is what I came up with using what I had on hand. Very versatile, quick and easy. Feel free to substitute spinach for the kale and omit the lentils.
