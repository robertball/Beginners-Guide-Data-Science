Title:
Air Fryer Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups pumpkin seeds
2 tsp Avocado oil
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Place pumpkin seeds in a small colander and rinse well.
Lay two sheets of paper towels on a plate, put the seeds on the paper towels and place another two sheets on top of the seeds. Press down to remove most of the water. Let them dry for a minimum of 15 minutes.
Place the seeds in a medium bowl and add the oil, paprika and salt. Use a med spoon to mix.
Preheat the Air Fryer to 180
Place the seeds in the basket of the Air Fryer and cook for 35 minutes, shaking frequently.
See cooks notes.
Cooks notes: Olive oil may be subbed for Avocado oil.
Watch carefully, last 5 minutes, they can go from perfectly cooked to burned quickly.
They are best when cold.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Wonderfully crunchy with just a hint of smokey flavour.
