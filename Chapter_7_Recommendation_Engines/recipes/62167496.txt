Title:
Steves Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/682348.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup lt. olive oil
8-10 cloves garlic, minced
2 Bay leaves, 1 sprig of rosemary
5 hot peppers seeded and 4 chopped
8-12 large very ripe garden tomatoes, peeled, seeded and chopped
1 large sweet onion chopped
1 cucumber, peeled, seeded and chopped
1 cup sweet peppers chopped
½ cup mix of Fresh basil, oregano and parsley chopped
1 cup wine vinegar
½ cup balsamic vinegar
1 small can tomato paste
1 lemon and 1 lime juiced and zest
¼ c Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbls. tamari or soy sauce
2 Tbls. honey
1 Tbls sea salt
Ground black pepper
2-4 cups V8 juice
Italian or French bread
Olive oil spray
Garlic cloves

Directions:
In med sauce pan heat oil on low add minced garlic, bay leaves, rosemary and 1 hot pepper
Bring just barely to simmer and set aside to cool
In large mixing bowl add chopped veggies and herbs
In small mixing bowl add vinegars, tomato paste, zest and juice of lemon and lime, Worcestershire, soy, honey and seasonings
Remove bay leaves, hot peppers and rosemary from cooled oil and add to large bowl
Add 1-2 cups veggie juice
Stir, Taste and adjust
Soup should be very chunky, add juice to thin
Refrigerate overnight stir and serve cold w/ garlic croutons
Note oil may set up in fridge. It will melt when served
To make croutons
Slice bread ¾“
Put on cookie sheet, spray w/ oil on both sides
Bake @350* until crisp
Rub w/ garlic clove
Cut into cubes
