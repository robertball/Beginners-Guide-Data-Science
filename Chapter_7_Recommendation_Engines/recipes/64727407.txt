Title:
Coconut Cream Cheese Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese
1 can of coconut milk
1 can of evaporated milk
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
1/3 cup of sugar
5 eggs
1 tsp of vanilla
1 1/2 cups of sugar for caramel
Shredded coconut for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place 13 x 9 baking pan in a bain-marie and place ib the oven.
Melt sugar over low heat until clear amber in color.
Pour melted sugar in preheated baking pan. Make sure to cover entire bottom.
Return to oven.
In a blender, first blend together coconut milk,  sugar  and cream cheese until smooth.
Add eggs one at at time through hole in the lid top while machine is running.
Add milks slowly while machine is running.
Add vanilla. Blend 30 seconds.
Pour mixture into preheated pan on top of the caramel.
Bake for 50 minutes.
Cool on baking rack for 30 seconds.
Refrigerate overnight.
