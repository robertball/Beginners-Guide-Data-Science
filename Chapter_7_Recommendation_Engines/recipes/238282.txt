Title:
'Get Loose' Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle gin
12 lemons, juiced
1 cup confectioners' sugar
ice cubes as desired
4 (750 milliliter) bottles chilled Champagne

Directions:
Mix gin, lemon juice, and confectioners' sugar in a punch bowl until the sugar is dissolved; add ice to chill. Pour Champagne into the bowl and lightly stir to blend.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple and lemony refreshing summer drink that just might sneak up on ya!
