Title:
Buttermilk Cole Slaw with Splenda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups shredded cabbage (mix green and red cabbage if desired)- shred using a 2 mm blade on a food processor
1 cup shredded onion  yellow or red (shred with 2 mm blade)
¼ cup (or more as desired) shredded carrot  (use the fine shredding blade)
½ tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
¼ cup milk
¼ cup buttermilk
½ cup mayonnaise
1 ½ Tbsp white vinegar
2 ½ Tbsp lemon juice
Splenda to taste (start with 1 Tbsp)

Directions:
Place shredded cabbage, onion, and carrot in a large bowl.
Whisk together the salt, pepper, milk, buttermilk, mayonnaise, vinegar, and lemon juice until smooth.  Add one or two Tablespoons of Splenda.  Mix.
Pour over cabbage mixture and mix well.  Taste for additional sweetener, if needed,
Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.  (Additional Splenda can be added to reach desired level of sweetness.)
Will keep in sealed container, in refrigerator, for up to 10 days.
