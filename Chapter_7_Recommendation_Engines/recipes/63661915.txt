Title:
Mushroom Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017430.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. butter, divided
1 Tbsp. olive oil
2 cups mushrooms, diced
1 small white onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups brown rice, rinsed
3 cups water
1/4 tsp. salt (more to taste)
1/4 tsp. freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Add 1 tbsp. butter and 1 tbsp. olive oil to saute pan over medium heat, and add onions and mushrooms with a pinch of salt.  Saute until they are well browned.
Add garlic and brown rice and cook until garlic is fragrant (do not let it brown).
Add water, salt and pepper and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer until water is absorbed (around 40 minutes).
Add remaining 1 tbsp. of butter and combine.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
A nice way to spruce up brown rice and makes a great side dish!
Serve with steamed veggies and grilled chicken breasts.
