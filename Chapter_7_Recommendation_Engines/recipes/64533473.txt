Title:
GF Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
Grass Fed ground beef
Sweet potato
Blue cheese
Picked vegetables

Directions:
Directions:
Form small patties with the grass fed ground beef to match the size of your sweet potato.
Slice sweet potato into 1/2 inch slices.
Grill patties and sweet potato slices until well done (or preference.)
Once cooked, add blue cheese and picked vegetables to your slider.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an easy fun way of keeping your grill day Gluten Free.
