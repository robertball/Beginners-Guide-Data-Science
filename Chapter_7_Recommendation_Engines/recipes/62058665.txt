Title:
Alfredo Plus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C butter
3/4 C milk
1 C freshly grated parmesean cheese
1/2 of a white onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 T dried parsley
10 pepperoni slices, diced
flour as needed to thicken
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  Melt butter over medium-high heat.  Add milk, onions, garlic, parsley, and pepperoni, then simmer until onions soften (2-3 minutes).
2.  Add the parmesean cheese, and allow it to melt.  Thicken with flour.  Mix with a hand-held blender to incorporate all flour and blend the onions into the liquid.
3.  Add salt and pepper to taste, and serve it over tortelloni, fettuccini, or pasta of your choice

Submitter: 
Glori Lynn
Description: 
Based on plain alfredo sauce, with some delectable add-ins, this sauce will add color and depth to your next pasta dish.
