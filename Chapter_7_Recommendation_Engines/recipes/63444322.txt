Title:
Apricot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chicken drumsticks and wings
1 TBS. oil
1 (12 oz.) jar apricot preserves
2 TBS. peeled, minced ginger root
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/8 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Brown chicken in oil and drain.  Place in a 13x9x2" pan.  Pour rest of ingredients over chicken.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until done.
