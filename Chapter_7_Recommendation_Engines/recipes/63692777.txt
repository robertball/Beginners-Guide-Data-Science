Title:
Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Imitation Crab meat
12 oz. Cream cheese
5 Tbsp. Mayo
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
Onions, minced (sprinkled)
Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except for the cheese.  Refrigerate over night.
Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top and bake at 350 for 20 min.
