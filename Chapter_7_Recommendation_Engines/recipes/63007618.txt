Title:
Stoney's Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4C AP Flour
1/4# Butter
1/2C Honey or brown sugar
2T   Worcestershire Sauce (Lee & Perins)
1/4C Tabasco

Directions:
Coat wings lightly with flour. Melt the butter and your choise of sweetener together, but do not boil. Add Worcestershire. Then add the Tabasco and simmer but do not boil or burn. Fry the wings and toss in wing sauce.
