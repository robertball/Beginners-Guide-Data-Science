Title:
German Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483019.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 1/2 cups milk
9 eggs
1 1/2 cups flour
3/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Place stick of butter in 9 x 13 pan and put over oven to melt.
Mix together milk, eggs, flour, and salt.
Pour batter into pan and bake at 450 for 25 minutes.
Serve with fresh fruit on top (bananas, strawberries, and blueberries).
