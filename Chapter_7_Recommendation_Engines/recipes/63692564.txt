Title:
Pizza and Mozz Cheese Bread Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1026643.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 cup warm water (110 degrees)30 secs in microwave

Directions:
DOUGH
Combine flour, salt, sugar, and yeast in a large bowl. Mix in oil and warm water. Turn out on flour board, cut in half. using one half, divide in two and knuckle in two sprayed 9" pie pans, add toppings. using the other half of dough, roll out flat and cut into 6 equal parts, roll each part with finger size cut mozz cheese, brush with melted garlic butter and sprinkle with parm cheese. bake at 375 degrees for 25 minutes.
