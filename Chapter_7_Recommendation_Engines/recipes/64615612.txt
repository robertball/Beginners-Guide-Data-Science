Title:
Sinful Chocolate Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4296883.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup cocoa powder
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon baking powder
3 eggs
1 cup of mini chocolate chips
1 tablespoon vanilla
1 tablespoon Espresso powder (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 and line 2 pans with parchment paper.
In a small bowl combine vanilla and Espresso powder. Mix till dissolved.
In a medium bowl sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Stir in chocolate chips.
In a large bowl whisk eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla mixture.
Add flour mixture to the egg mixture and combine to form a heavy dough.
Divide dough into two pieces and place on cookie sheets.
Wet hands and shape dough into a roll as long as cookies sheet and press down to about 1/2 thickness.
Bake for 25 minutes
Remove from oven and let cool 5 minutes.
When cool enough to handle move biscotti to a cutting board and slice each one diagonally crosswise into 1/2 inch long slices. Place back on cookie sheet and return to oven for 6 minutes on each side.
Remove from oven and let cool on wore racks.. They will harden up more one cool.
Drizzle with more chocolate if desired.
Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
A brownie like biscotti that tastes great with a cup of coffee. Especially popular in the winter time when coffee and chocolate taste so much better.
