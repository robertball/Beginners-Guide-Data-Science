Title:
Carolina Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounce Sothern Comfort
2 ounces Cranberry juice
1/2 ounce Orange juice
1 lime wedge

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice. Add all ingredients and stir. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Kristen Boesenberg
Description: 
Refreshing cocktail with a southern twist to cool you down on a hot day.
