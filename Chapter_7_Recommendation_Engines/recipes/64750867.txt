Title:
Keto Instant Pot Chicken with Bacon, Onions, Mushrooms, Cheese with Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Frozen Chicken Breasts
6 Slices of Bacon
1/2 Medium Yellow Onion
1 Package of Sliced Mushrooms
1/2 Cup Cheese
6 Cups of Broccoli

Directions:
1. Cook Frozen chicken in Instant Pot for 14 minutes with a cup of water, seasoned with olive oil
2. Release steam
3. Cook Broccoli for 8 minutes instant pot add cup of water and olive oil
3. On a frying pan(1) add olive oil then add sliced onions until almost cooked, and afterwards mushrooms
4. Grate the Cheese
5. Other frying pan add bacon until cooked
6. Add Chicken to frying pan(1)  and crisp it
7. Let Broccoli finish
8. Then put chicken on a plate with ingredients
9. Put bacon on top then cheese
10. Serve
