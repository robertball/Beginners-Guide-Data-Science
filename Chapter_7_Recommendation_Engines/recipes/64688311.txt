Title:
My Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 med onion, diced
1/4-1/2 tsp. red chili flakes
1tsp dried oregano
2 celery stalks, sliced
1 large potato, peeled and chopped
1 14oz tin, diced tomatoes
1 quart or  liter chicken stock
1 tin Romano or Kidney beans, drained and rinsed
Salt and pepper to taste
Grated parmesan for serving

Directions:
Heat oil on medium heat in a medium sized pot or dutch oven.
Add onion, garlic , chili flakes and dried oregano, cook until onion is soft and translucent, careful not to burn the garlic, about 5 mins.
Add vegetables and cook for another 5 mins.
Add stock and beans and bring to a boil, then simmer for about 40 mins until potatoes are tender.
Serve hot with a sprinkling of parmesan.
Optional....you can add a small pasta (previously cooked) just before taking off the heat.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
This is a hearty flavourful soup. It is also very versatile in that you can add any vegetables that you have. This is how i was taught to make it, but honestly it's different everytime i prepare it. Serve it with some crusty bread and you have yourself a meal!
