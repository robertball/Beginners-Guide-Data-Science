Title:
Champ Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Archer Farms™ chorizo sausage
2 cups Archer Farms™ salsa
2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, drained
2 (14 ounce) cans diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Cook sausage as directed on package, using a large saucepan.  Drain and cut into bite-sized pieces.
In same large pan, mix sausage and remaining ingredients, except cheese.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
Ladle chili into bowls and top with shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Chorizo sausage gives this chili a spicy south-of-the-border flair.
