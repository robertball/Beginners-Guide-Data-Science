Title:
Citrus salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons sesame oil
2 tablespoon sesame seeds (toasted)
2 tablespoons poppyseeds
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup canola or vegetable oil
1/4 cup apple cider vineagar
1/2 teaspoon paprika
2 tablespoons minced onions
1 teaspoon garlic - minced
12oz. mixed greens
8oz. can - mandarin oranges - drained
1/4 - scallions chopped
1/2 cup pear tomatoes
1/2 cup sliced almonds - toasted
1/4 cup gorgonzola cheese - crumbled

Directions:
In a jar mix the first 9 ingredients.  Mix well.  In a salad bowl, lay mixed greens in and pour in half of dressing.  Toss salad.  Neatly place the mandarin oranges around the salad, sprinkle the tomatoes, scallions, cheese and dress the almonds on top.  Serve immediately

Submitter: 
Tina Andre' Fox
Description: 
A very refreshing light salad with a delicous light dressing.
