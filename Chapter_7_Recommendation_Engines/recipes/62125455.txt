Title:
Ham Loaf with Red Currant Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ham Loaf:
One pound ground ham (can use food processor to grind up a pre-cooked ham)
one pound ground pork
one cup soft breadcrumbs (in container)
1/3 cip chopped onion
two beaten eggs
1/2 cup milk
Red Currant Sauce: (double this)
One jar red currant jelly
one and 1/2 tsp. grated orange peel
Two Tbsp. orange juice
One Tbsp. prepared creamy horseradish

Directions:
For Ham Loaf: Combine ground ham, ground pork, soft breadcrumbs, finely chopped onion, eggs, and milk in a large bowl. Shape into loaf with hands. Place in shallow baking dish. Bake @ 350 degrees for one and 1/4 hours, or until done and top is browned.
For Red Currant Sauce (I double this):
One jar red currant jelly
one and 1/2 tsp. grated orange peel
Two Tbsp. orange juice
One Tbsp. prepared creamy horseradish.
Combine ingredients into saucepan. Heat over medium heat and stir until smooth, and jelly melted.   Serve in a bowl or gravy boat. Serves 4-6 people. Great for holidays. The sauce makes the loaf.

Submitter: 
ALISON19
Description: 
Ham loaf becomes fancy with a flavorful currant sauce. Great use of left-over ham, good holiday dish.
