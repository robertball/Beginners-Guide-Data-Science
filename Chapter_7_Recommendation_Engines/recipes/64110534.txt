Title:
Three Packet Slow Cooker Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
1 (.75 ounce) packet dry brown gravy mix
1 (3 pound) boneless beef chuck roast
3-4 potatoes, chopped
3 carrots, chopped
1 small onion, chopped

Directions:
Whisk together the water, Italian dressing mix, ranch dressing mix, and brown gravy mix together in a bowl until smooth. Put veggies in the bottom of the crock pot. Cut any fat off of the roast. Place the beef roast over them, and pour the sauce over top.
Cook on Low until the roast is easily pierced by a fork, 6 to 8 hours.
