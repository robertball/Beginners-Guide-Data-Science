Title:
Cream of Chicken Soup Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1014467.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon flour
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup milk
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
1.  Melt the butter in a small sauce pan over medium-low heat.
2.  Whisk in the flour.  Continue whisking until smooth.
3.  Slowly whisk in the chicken broth and milk. Bring to a slow boil over medium heat.  Keeping it at a low boil, whisk constantly until the soup thickens, about 7 to 10 minutes.
4.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Lisa Altmiller
Description: 
My husband does not like the taste of cream of chicken soup.  But I love casseroles.  I decided I needed to find a way to make cream of chicken soup from scratch.  After a few attempts, I came up with version.  It's easy and much tastier (and healthier) than the canned stuff!
