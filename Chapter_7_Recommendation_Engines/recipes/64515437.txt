Title:
Deep Dish Peach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14oz cans sliced peaches, drained
3/4 cup sugar (white or brown - as per your taste preference)
1/4 cup flour
1 tbsp. butter or margarine
2 tbsp. water (or juice from peaches)
1 tbsp. lemon juice
almond flavoring
9" unbaked pie crust (or double if covering top)

Directions:
Line bottom 9" deep dish pie plate.
Mix sugar, flour and butter to make crumbs.
Put 1/2 mixture in bottom of crust.
Arrange peaches over crumbs.
Cover with rest of crumbs.
Combine liquid and pour over pie.
Add top crust, if using.
Bake @ 375 x 40 - 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This delicious fruit pie can be made open face (no top crust) with a crumb topping or with a top pastry crust.
