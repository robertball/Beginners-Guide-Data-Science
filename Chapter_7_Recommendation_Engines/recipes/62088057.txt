Title:
Chicken with stout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken pieces (thighs or legs)
4 medium potatoes cut into approx 1 inch pieces
2 large carrots sliced
1/2 white cabbage sliced
3/4 pint of beef stock
3/4 pint stout (guiness is good)
1 dessert spoon brown sugar
1 dessert spoon of corn starch/flour
seasoned all purpose flour for coating

Directions:
Place the potato, cabbage and carrots in the bottom of a large casserole.
coat the chicken in the flour and fry in a little oil until brown on both sides (about 5-8 minutes)
Place the chicken on top of the vedgetables.
Combine the warm stock, stout and sugar in a jug.
Mix the corn flour with a little water and add to the stock mixture.
Pour over the chicken.
Cover and cook in oven at 170c for approx 1 & 1/4 hours.
Uncover and cook for a further 3/4 hour.
Serve and enjoy!
