Title:
BLT With Attitude Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR TOMATOES - MAKE AHEAD
2 medium tomatoes, sliced
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil (the good stuff)
2 T red wine vinegar
1 large garlic clove, minced
1 t parsley
pinch salt
fresh ground pepper
FOR SANDWICH
8 slices bacon, cooked and drained
4 crispy leaves of lettuce
4 slices bread
mayo

Directions:
Slice and lay tomatoes in a large shallow.
In a bowl, whisk the oil, vinegar, garlic, parsley and salt. Pour over tomatoes and let sit at room temp at least an hour if possible.
When making sandwiches, toast bread, spread with mayo, cracked pepper, then layer bacon, followed by tomatoes (remove from vinegarette and lightly shake), and then lettuce.
