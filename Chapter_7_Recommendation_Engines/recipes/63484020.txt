Title:
Chicken and Black Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 cups chopped cooked chicken
1 1lb can black beans drained
1 10oz can tomatoes and green chilies
1 onion chopped
1 glove garlic, minced
1 tbs olive oil
1 green bell pepper
corn optional
cumin
tobasco
s/p
lemon juice
tortillas
shredded cheese

Directions:
saute onion in olive oil.  add garlic, black beans, tomatoes and chicken.  add seasoning.  simmer to reduce liquid. wrap in warm tortillas with cheese
