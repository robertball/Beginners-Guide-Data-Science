Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Creamy Herb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1035115.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup minced carrots
1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin medallions
2 1/2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/2 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon beef bouillon granules
2/3 cup whole milk
1/4 cup dry white wine

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook carrots in oil for 5 minutes, stirring often.  Add pork, and cook until lightly browned.  Remove only pork, and keep warm.
In the skillet, stir together flour, basil, parsley, pepper, garlic, onion, Italian seasoning, and beef granules.  Whisk in milk, stirring until thick.  Stir in wine.  Return pork to pan, reduce heat to low, and cover.  Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
