Title:
Charlotte's Pumkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped dates
1 cup chopped pecans
1 1/3 cups flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup pumpkin pie filling
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 water
2 eggs
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 ground nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325F and lightly grease your bread pan.
Mix nuts and dates in a bowl, set aside.
Sift all remaining dry ingredients into a bowl.
In a large mixing bowl add water, eggs, oil, pumpkin, and eggs. And mix at a low speed.
While mixing, slowly add dry ingredients.
Gently fold in dates and nuts.
Pour mix into bread pan and bake for 1h 15min
Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.
You may now serve the bread, or chill it overnight for a great cold desert!
