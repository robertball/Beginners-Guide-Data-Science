Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/52518.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
1 cup mashed ripe bananas
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine cake mix, egg and milk.  Add bananas; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.  Stir in pecans.  Pour into two greased 8-in. x 4-in. x 2-in. loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.  Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Marie  Davis
Description: 
A yellow cake mix streamlines assembly of this moist golden bread from Marie Davis. 'I searched a long while for a banana bread that was easy to make,' explains the Pendleton, South Carolina cook. 'This one takes no time at all, and makes two loaves, so one can be frozen to enjoy later.'
