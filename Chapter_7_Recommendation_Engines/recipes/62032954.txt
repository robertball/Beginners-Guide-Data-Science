Title:
Chicken and Pear Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
3 Tbs. red wine vinegar
2 Tbs. honey Dijon mustard
salt and pepper
3 cups chopped, cooked chicken
1/2 cup walnuts, toasted
1/2 cup Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
2 pears, halved, cored and sliced
1 lb. mixed salad greens

Directions:
In bowl, whisk together oil, vinegar, salt and pepper to taste. Add chicken, walnuts and cheese and toss lightly. Place greens on salad plates. Arrange sliced pears in fan shape along one side atop greens. With slotted spoon, divide chicken mixture evenly among plates. Drizzle any remaining dressing over and pears and greens.
