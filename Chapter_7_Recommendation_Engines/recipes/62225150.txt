Title:
Cocktail Links in Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lil smokies
12 ounces chili sauce
10 ounce grape jelly

Directions:
Heat chili sauce and jelly, stirring constantly, until jelly is melted.
Add sausages and stir until coated.
Simmer uncovered 30 minutes. Serve hot in a chafing dish.
