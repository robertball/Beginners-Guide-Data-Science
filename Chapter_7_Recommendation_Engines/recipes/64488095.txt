Title:
Creamy Tomato Tortellini and Sausage Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2564199.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp of EVOO
1 lb sweet Italian sausage, casings removed
1 pkg of fresh spinach
1 pkg of fresh sliced mushrooms
1 lg red pepper, sliced into thin strips
1 sm sweet onion, chopped
3 cloves of minced garlic
2 tbsp of butter
1 4 oz can of diced tomatoes
1/2 can of tomato soup, condensed
1/4 cup of light cream
1 pkg of cheese tortellini, cooked
2 tsp basil 2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp of crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
2 mozzarella cheese sticks, cut into pieces
1/4 cup of shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Cook tortellini according to pkg directions
2. Add EVOO and 1 tbsp to a large skillet
3. Add in crumbled sausage, onions, peppers, and garlic and saute'.
4. Add in remaining butter,  your mushrooms and spinach leaves. Saute' until spinach is wilted and mushrooms are browned.
5. Add in spices. Cook about 3 minutes and then add in diced tomatoes, grated cheese and mozzarella sticks. Incorporate the ingredients and slowly add the tomato soup and cream. Cook for about 5 minutes.
6. Turn heat down to simmer and add the cooked tortellini. Cover and continue to simmer for 5-7 minutes.
7. Serve sprinkled with shredded Parmesan cheese.
Bon Appetit!!
