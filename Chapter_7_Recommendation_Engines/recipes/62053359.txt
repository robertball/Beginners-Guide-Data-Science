Title:
Daiquiri Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 baked pie shell,
8 ounces softened cream cheese,
1 can sweetened condensed milk,
1 can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed and not diluted,
1/3 cup light rum, or 2 tsp rum flavoring,
1/2 ounce yellow food coloring,
1 small container whipped topping.

Directions:
In large mixer bowl, beat cream cheese til light and fluffy.  Add milk, and lemonade; beat until smooth.  Add rum; stir in food coloring.  Fold in whipped topping; pour into pie shell.  refrigerate 4 hours or until firm.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
A great little pie to impress that special someone.
