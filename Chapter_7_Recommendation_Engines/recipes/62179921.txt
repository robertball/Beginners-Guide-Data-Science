Title:
Brown Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs pinto beans
2 cloves garlic
1 bunch celery
2 large onions
3 leeks
1/2 carrots
1 bunch italian parsley
2 beef bullion cubes
2 cups beef broth
1 tsp cloves
1 TBS nutmeg
1 ham bone
1 smoked sausage
1/3 C cider vinegar
14 cups water
salt and pepper

Directions:
place beans in pot and let soak over night  do not drain chop onions, leeks, carrots and celery and add to pot.  Add ham bone
cloves and nutmeg cider vinegar and water bring to a boil, cover and simmer for about 2 hours. remove ham bone.  Cut all excess meat off bone and add to pot.  thinly slice smoked sausage and add to pot.  Simmer until beans are done.  Remove about 3 cups and puree and return to pot. Right before serving  add finely chopped parsley.  Taste and then season with salt and pepper to taste.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Glenda Morrison de Kruif
Description: 
This is great for a cold winters night
