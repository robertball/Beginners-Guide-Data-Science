Title:
Grandma's Hot Fudge Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 scant cup sugar
1 1/2 Tbsp. cornstarch
6 Tbsp. baking cocoa
12 oz. evaporated milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan.
Cook and stir over medium heat until bubbly.
Serve over ice cream.

Submitter: 
scarlett
Description: 
Whenever we stayed overnight at grandma's house, we had vanilla ice cream with this yummy topping!
