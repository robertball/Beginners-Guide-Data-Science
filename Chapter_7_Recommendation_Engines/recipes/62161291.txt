Title:
Sausage & Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box long grain wild rice
1 onion, chopped
8 oz mushrooms
8 oz water chestnuts
1 pound sausage
3T soy sauce
3 oz almonds

Directions:
Cook rice, per box instructions.  Brown meat and onion together.  Mix all ingredients (reserving almonds).  Sprinkel almonds on top, Bake at 350 for 50 minutes.

We use this as a breakfast casserole.
