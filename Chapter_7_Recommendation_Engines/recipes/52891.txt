Title:
Minted Mocha Fusion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/134457.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Rich Chocolate NESTLE速 CARNATION速 Hot Cocoa Mix
1/4 cup TASTER'S CHOICE速 100% Pure Instant Coffee
1/4 teaspoon peppermint extract

Directions:
PLACE cocoa mix, Taster's Choice and peppermint extract in food processor or blender; cover. Process until ingredients are combined. Store in airtight container.
TO MAKE ONE SERVING: Place 3 tablespoons Minted Mocha Fusion in mug; stir in 1 cup hot milk or water. Serve immediately.
