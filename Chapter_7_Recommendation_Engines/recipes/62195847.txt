Title:
Slow Cooker Lentil and Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/300690.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup dry lentils (soaked overnight)
1 cup Pinto Beans (soaked overnight)
1 cups uncooked Basmati Rice Medley
1 14 ounce can of stewed tomatoes
2 cup chopped carrots
1 cup chopped celery
4 cups water
4 cup vegetable broth
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tsp. ground Cumin
1 tsp. Paprika
1 tsp. Chili Powder
1/2 tsp. Red Pepper Flakes
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms

Directions:
Place the lentils, rice, carrots, celery, onion, water, broth, garlic powder, ground black pepper and salt in a slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low setting for 7 to 8 hours. Stir in the mushrooms 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Gitano
Description: 
Dried lentils and uncooked rice mix are combined with vegetables, seasonings and broth in this easy and nutritious soup.  Chopped mushrooms are added in the last of the hour of cooking time.Modified from: Slow Cooker Lentil & Rice Soup
