Title:
zucchine patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup bisquick
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/8 tsp salt
2 eggs beaten well
2 cups shreded zucchine
2 t onion powder or diced onion
5 slices bacon

Directions:
cook bacon till crispy take out of pan lay on paper towel, leave bacon greese in pan ,mix all in large bowel  crumble bacon in mixture and , spoon heaping table spoons of mixture in skilet on med fry 3 to 5 min on each side place on paper towels to drain till all gone
