Title:
Noodles Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
8 oz. wide egg noodles
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup light cream (20%)
1 cup grated parmesean cheese
1 tablespoon parsley flakes
1/4 tsp. salt
a dash of pepper
Cook noodles as directed...While the noodles cook, heat butter and cream in a small saucepan...over low heat until butter is melted.Stir in cheese, parsley,salt and pepper...keep warm over low heat..Return drained noodles to saucepan..Pour sauce over noodles and stir gently until noodles are well coated.
