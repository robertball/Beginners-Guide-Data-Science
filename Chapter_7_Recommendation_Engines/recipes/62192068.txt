Title:
Chinese Chicken Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken tenders
1-1/2 lb shredded green cabbage
1/2 lb shredded red cabbage
1/3 lb browned sliced almonds
1/4 lb browned sesame seeds
1 ramen noodles, broken
1 bunch green onions, diced
1/2 C canola oil
2 T sugar
2 T cider vinegar
1 T rice wine vinegar
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Dice chicken tenders into bite sized pieces.  Saute.
Mix chicken, cabbage, almonds, sesame seeds, ramen noodles and green onions.
Serve with dressing.
