Title:
Italian Chicken ala Genovese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken with bone, skin is optional to cut on calories) chopped into 8 pieces
1 large carrot, minced
1 celery stalk, minced
1 medium onion, minced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 cup olive oil and 2 tbsp butter
1 bullion cube
1 bay leaf
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp fresh rosemary
as much water as needed

Directions:
Begin by warming old and butter in a large, deep skilled that has a cover to go with it.
add your minced carrot, celery, onion, and garlic.
sautee a bit until half way cooked on a medium flame.
add your chicken pieces and continue to cook all together for about 2 minutes.
add the bullion cube, water, salt, pepper, bay leaf and rosemary and cover with water.
let it come to a boil and turn the chicken pieces around.
let it continue to cook on a medium flame until chicken is fully cooked.
as the water cooks down, add more as needed until chicken is done and you are left with a carrot sauce that is not too thick or too thin.
serve with white rice and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Felicia Manocchio
Description: 
A easy and flavorful chicken dish that goes great on white rice.
