Title:
Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3046342.jpg
Ingredients:
Start with:
2 c sugar
1/2 c brown sugar
7/8 c butter
1 c peanut butter
Then add:
4 eggs
2 c flour
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1 t vanilla
Frosting:
2 c powdered sugar
1/4 c cocoa
1/4 c butter
add milk 1 T at a time until creamy (about 3 to 4 T)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. Cream together sugars, butter, and peanut butter. Add eggs, flour, baking powder, salt, and vanilla.
Place batter into a greased 10 x 15 pan, and bake for about 25 minutes.
Frost before completely cooled to adhere frosting to bars.
