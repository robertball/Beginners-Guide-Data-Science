Title:
Pineapple Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2362241.jpg
Ingredients:
1 english cucumber,quartered and chunked
1/2 pineapple,peeled and chunked
2 green onions,sliced thinly
3 TBSP cilantr,finely chopped
3 TBSP fresh mint,finely chopped
3 TBSP sweet chili sauce
3 TBSP fresh lime juice, and zest from one lime
2 TBSP sesame oil
1 TBSP fish sauce
1 TBSP brown sugar ,added 1tsp at a time

Directions:
Combine chili sauce, lime juice,zest,oil and fish sauce. Whisk to blend , add Brown sugar 1 tsp at a time  (you may not need all of it depending on your personal taste) to balance flavors.
Add cucumbers, pineapple, onions and herbs to bowl with dressing, and toss well

Submitter: 
Ann Freele
Description: 
Light, refreshing summer salad that works through out the year
