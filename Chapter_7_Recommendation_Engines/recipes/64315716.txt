Title:
Mediterranean Dirty Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2001731.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oil
10 garlic cloves, minced
1 cup chopped onion
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
2 teaspoons annatto or achiote powder or one sazon with achiote. (Optional)
1 teaspoon ground cumin
4 cups uncooked medium white rice
4 cups water
1 (plum) tomatoes, chopped
1 small can cups whole corn kernels
1 can black beans
1 red onion, thinly sliced
2 tablespoon fresh lime juice
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 lime, cut into wedges
Salt to taste
1/2 freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
In a heavy saucepan, heat the oil over medium heat, the garlic and the chopped onions; saute for 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Mix in the bell pepper, crushed pepper, ground annatto, cumin, corn, black beans, 1 tablespoon of cilantro and tomatoes. Saute for 2 minutes.
Add the water and salt, and bring to boil over high heat. Add rice stir and wait until it absorbs all the water reduce to low. Simmer the rice for 30 minutes.  When the mixture is heated through, add lemon juice and stir spoon it onto plates and top with the sliced red onion and cilantro. Serve a wedge or two of lime with each plate to squeeze over the rice.
