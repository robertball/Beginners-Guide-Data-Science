Title:
Pork Chops With Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Pork Chops
2 Tablespoon onion, chopped
1/4 cup water
1 can (10.5 ounce)cream of mushroom soup
1 can (4 ounce) of mushrooms (stems & pieces, drained
1/2 cup sour cream
3 cups soft bread crumbs (stuffing) - 1 pack of Mrs.   Cubbisons = 3 cups
3/4 cup melted butter
1/4 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1/3 cup of white wine

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients except for pork chops.  Put in baking dish, add pork chops on top.
Bake in a COVERED dish for 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
I've been fixing this for years.  I like it and hear no one else complain, in fact my family really likes the taste of it and have asked for it several times.
