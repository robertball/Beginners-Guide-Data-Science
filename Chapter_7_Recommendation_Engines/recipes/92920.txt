Title:
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats® Candy Canes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975940.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal or Kellogg's® Cocoa Krispies™ cereal
Red string licorice
Prepared frosting (optional)

Directions:
Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Stir until well coated.
Divide warm mixture in half and shape each portion into 12-inch candy cane. Wrap red licorice around canes for stripes and secure with frosting. Cut each candy cane into 6 slices. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
Make this fun dessert a holiday centerpiece for your table, then dig in! These giant candy canes are sure to inspire a festive mood.
