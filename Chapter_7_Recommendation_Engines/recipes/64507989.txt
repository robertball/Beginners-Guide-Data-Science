Title:
Braided Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf frozen bread
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup chopped nuts
1 can apples
Frosting:
powdered sugar
1-2 tbs butter
milk (added until frosting is right consistency)

Directions:
Thaw frozen bread over night.
Roll out to approximately 12 X 15" rectangle
Sprinkle brown sugar and nuts over dough
Spread canned apples over dough
Cut into three strips lengthwise.
Pinch filling inside strip of dough.
Braid dough strips on cookie sheet covered in foil.
Let rise for another 20-30 minutes.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 20-30 minutes. Let top become golden brown.
Remove from oven and remove from cookie sheet immediately. Pull foil sheet on to a platter.
Mix frosting until good consistency and spread on top. May decorate with nuts or colored sugar.
