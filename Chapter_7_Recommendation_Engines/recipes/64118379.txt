Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 4 cups white sugar
* 1 cup milk
* 1/2 cup butter
* 1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
* 12 ounces peanut butter
* 2/3 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:
1)Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish, set aside.
2)In a saucepan combine sugar, milk, and butter. Bring to a boil, and cook 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in the marshmallow creme and peanut butter. Gradually stir in the flour. Spread into the prepared pan, and let cool.
