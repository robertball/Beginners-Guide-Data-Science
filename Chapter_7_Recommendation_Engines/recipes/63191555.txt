Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/823037.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. water
1 c. raisins
3/4 c. shortening
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 c. flour
1/2 tsp a baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup pecans (optional)

Directions:
Boil raisins in water for about 20-25 minutes. Set aside to cool .
Mix shortening, sugar, eggs & vanilla together. Drain raisins and reserve liquid. Measure amount of liquid on the raisins and add enough water to make 1/2 cup. Mix in raisin liquid with sugar/egg mixture.
Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add wet and dry mixes together and mix well. Add rolled oats, raisins and 1/2 cup of nuts if desired. Drop onto well sprayed or parchment lined pan. Bake at 400Â° for 8-10 minutes
