Title:
Simple and Easy Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/801386.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 Chicken Breasts
8 oz Sour Cream
1 bag(8oz) of Sharp cheese
1 can(8oz) of water chestnuts
1 small bag(12oz) of broccoli
1 cups of crushed Potato Chips
1 can(10.75oz) of Cream of chicken or mushroom
1 can of mushrooms
1 stick of Butter

Directions:
Pre-Heat oven for 350 degrees.
Chop up the chicken in small pieces and cook.
Melt the butter.
Mix the chicken, sour cream, 1 1/4cup of cheese, water chestnuts, broccoli, cream of chicken/mushroom, mushrooms and butter in a bowl.
Spray baking dish with cooking spray then add mix to a baking dish.
Once smoothed out, mix Potato chips and rest of cheese then add to the top of the casserole.
Cook for 25 minutes.
Let stand for 5 minutes and Enjoy!
