Title:
Party Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese softened
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 4oz can chopped green chilis
1 4oz can chopped black olives
1 cup sour cream
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
7 10 inch flour tortillas

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until well blended. Spread mixture thinly on flour tortilla and roll up tightly.   Wrap each roll up in saran wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours or overnight. Cut into 1/2 inch pieces and arrange on large plate or platter. Serve with salsa for dipping.
The colored and flavored tortillas can make these very festive for any occasion.

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Cream cheese, sour cream, olives and chilis rolled in a flour tortilla. Great for any Party!
