Title:
Emeril's Baked Redfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon salt •
½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper •
Six 8-ounce redfish fillets or other white-fleshed fish fillets, such as snapper •
Garlic Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven  to 350F
Lay the redfish fillets on a work surface and season on both sides with ½ teaspoon salt and ¼ teaspoon pepper.
Using a flat metal spatula, smear both sides of each fillet liberally with the garlic butter.
Lay a fillet on top of a sheet of aluminum foil
fold up to enclose the fillets, and tightly crimp the edges to seal the pouches.
Place on a large baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes.
