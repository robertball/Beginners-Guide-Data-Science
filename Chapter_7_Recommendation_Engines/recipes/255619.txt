Title:
Farmhouse Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4013372.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pound petite potatoes
1 pound bacon, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 onion, sliced
1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 lemon, zested

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Mix olive oil, garlic powder, parsley, and salt together in a large bowl; add potatoes and stir until coated. Mix bacon and onion into potato mixture. Spread potato mixture onto a baking sheet.
Roast in the preheated oven until potatoes are tender and crisp, about 30 minutes. Toss potatoes with Parmesan cheese and top with lemon zest.
