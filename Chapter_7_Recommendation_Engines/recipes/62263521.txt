Title:
Dry Ranch Dressing Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried chives
1/4 cup dried parsley
1/4 cup dried dill weed
2 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons onion powder
1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in an airtight container. Mix, and store with your spices.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
I keep this on hand, and it is great for making ranch dressing, ranch dip, adding as seasoning to entrees, baked potatoes, soups...the possibilities are endless!
