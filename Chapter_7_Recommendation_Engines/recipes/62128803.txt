Title:
Beefy Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*  12 baby portobello mushrooms, cleaned
* 2t butter or oleo
* 1/2t minced garlic
* 1/2lb lean ground beef
* 1/2t salt
* 1/4t onion powder
* 1/4t black pepper
* 8oz cream cheese(1 pack), softened
* 1/4c Parmesan cheese + some to sprinkle

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350.
2. Remove stems from mushrooms.
3. Set mushroom caps aside on a baking sheet.
4. Chop stems and set aside
5. In a large skillet, over medium heat, melt butter, saute garlic.
6. Add grown beef, chopped stems, salt, pepper, and onion powder. Brown through completely.
7. Remove from heat and drain fat
8. Add cream cheese and Parmesan, stir until melted.
9. Fill mushroom caps until almost overflowing, about 1 1/2 Tablespoons, depending on the size of the caps.
10. Place on baking sheet, bake for 15 minutes, or until mushrooms begin to weep.
11. Remove caps, sprinkle with remaining cheese.
12. Turn oven to Broil, broil mushrooms for 2-3 minutes, or until cheese is crispy.

Submitter: 
Heather Maki
Description: 
Stuffed mushrooms that could be served as a main meal if using bigger mushrooms.
