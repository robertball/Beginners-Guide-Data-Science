Title:
English Two in One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 cup milk
1/4 cup cold water
3 eggs slightly beaten
1 pound of mild sauage, cut into 1-2 inch pieces

Directions:
preheat oven to 450
in a large bowl, combine flour and salt - add milk a little at a time and beat with an electric mixer until smooth - add water and eggs and beat until bubbly.  Cover loosely and set aside for 30 minutes in a cool place but not the fridge
meanwhile place sausage in a 9 x 13 inch baking pan and bake for 20 minutes, remove from oven and pour batter over sausage
bake in centre oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until puffy, well browned and crisp

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
This is a cross between yorkshire pudding meets sausage and a breakfast cassarole
