Title:
BREAKSTONE'S Cucumber-Dill Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987611.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup BREAKSTONE'S Reduced Fat Sour Cream
1/4 cup MIRACLE WHIP Light Dressing
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 large cucumbers, sliced
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Mix sour cream, dressing, dill and lemon juice.
Pour over cucumbers in large bowl; toss to coat.
Sprinkle with pepper.

Submitter: 
BREAKSTONE'S
Description: 
Sliced cucumbers are tossed in a creamy, tangy dill dressing for an easy, quick salad.
