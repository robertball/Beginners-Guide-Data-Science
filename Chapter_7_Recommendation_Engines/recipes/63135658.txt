Title:
HE Friendly Laundry Detergent Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz plastic container
1/2 cup washing soda
1/2 cup borax
1 bar lavender castile soap (grated)
1/4 cup baking soda
15 drops lemon essential oil

Directions:
Add washing soda, borax, grated soap, baking soda and oil to the lidded container. Cover and shake well.
How to use: Use 3 Tbsp detergent per load, or slightly more for very soiled clothes.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
High efficiency washers use less water than their conventional counterparts, so this detergent produces less lather and easily rinses out.  Costs just $4.03 for an 18oz tub (enough for 12 Loads)
