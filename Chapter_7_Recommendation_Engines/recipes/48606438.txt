Title:
Chicken Wing Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound of chicken wings
1 8oz. jar of apricot jam
3 T soy sauce
2 T horseradish
1 T mayonnaise
Dash hot pepper sauce ( Tabasco)
flour for coating chicken

Directions:
Joint the wings, discarding the tips. Place the flour in a bag and shake the wings in the flour. Place in a single layer on shallow baking pan. Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes. Turn wings and bake for 15 more minutes. Meanwhile, mix sauce together. Spoon sauce over the wings. Bake for 15 minutes more or until done. Serve warm.
