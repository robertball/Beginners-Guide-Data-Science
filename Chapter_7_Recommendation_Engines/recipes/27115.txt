Title:
Chocolate Martini III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/965182.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers vodka
5 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers creme de cacao liqueur
2 teaspoons chocolate syrup

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer full of ice, combine vodka, creme de cacao and chocolate syrup. Shake vigorously and strain into 4 cocktail glasses.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
For those who like a little candy in their drinks - creme de cacao and chocolate syrup are shaken with vodka and ice.
