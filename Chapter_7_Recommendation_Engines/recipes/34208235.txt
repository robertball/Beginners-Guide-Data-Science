Title:
Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs cubed chicken
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
2 cups taco sauce
1 cup sour cream
4 tsp chili powder
1 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup water
1 lb monterey jack cheese
2 cloves minced garlic
1 1/2 tsp salt
12 small flour tortillas

Directions:
Cook meat and onion, drain. Add cumin, garlic, chili powder, salt, pepper and water. Simmer uncovered untill liquid is evaporated. Pour 1/2 cup taco sauce into greased 9x13 pan Arrange 6 tortillas on bottom. Pour 1/2 cup taco sauce over tortillas. Spread meat mixture over toco sauce and top with sour cream and 1/2 of the cheese. Arrange 6 tortillas over cheese. Cover with remaining toco sauce and top with cheese. Cover with foil and bake 40 min @350. Uncaver and bake 5 min more. Let stand % min and top with lettuce cheese or salsa.
