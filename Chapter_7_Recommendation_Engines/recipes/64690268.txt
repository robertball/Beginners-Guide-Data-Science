Title:
Ground Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. lean ground beef
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1/2 tsp garlic powder
can mushroom soup
1/2 c reduced fat sour cream
4 oz can of chopped mushrooms (drained)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet.
Add in onion and garlic and cook together for 3 min.
Add in remaining ingredients bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes with lid on.
The wonderful thing about this recipe is that EVERYTHING can be toyed with.
Make it your own and enjoy.
