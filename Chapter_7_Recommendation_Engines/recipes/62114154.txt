Title:
Electric Skillet Meat Loaf Casswerole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 egg,slightly beaten
1 c. soft bread crumbs
1/4 c tomato sauce
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 c flour
1 tbls crisco
1/4 c beef stock
8 small potatoes
2 zucchini,cut in strips
1 tsp grated onion

Directions:
Combine beef, egg, bread, tomato sauce, worcestershire, salt and pepper; form in compact loaf to fit in middle of skillet.
Dredge in flour.
Heat skillet to 300 degrees.
Add crisco, and when heated,put in loaf and brown on all sides.
Add stock,cover pan,a,d simmer 15 minutes.
Put in potatoes, cover again and cook about 30 minutes longer until potatoes are just about tender, then tuck zucchini aroubd pan, spinkle with onion., cover and simmer 15 minutes longer until tender.
