Title:
Quick and Easy Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856078.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups biscuit mix
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup vegetable oil
7 fluid ounces lemon-lime soda

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine biscuit mix, eggs and oil. Add soda and mix well. Bake in a preheated waffle iron according to manufacturer's directions until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Phyllis McCosh
Description: 
These fast-to-prepare waffles are perfect to whip up at the last minute, and the recipe can easily be doubled. I often made them when our six children were growing up. And now when they come back to visit, they always request them.
