Title:
Creamy Tomato Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup beef broth
1/2 dry white wine
1/4 cup tomato paste
6 sprigs fresh thyme
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
950 g boneless beef blade pot roast, trimmed
1/4 cup whipping cream (35%)
3 tbsp all purpose flour
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In slow cooker, combine broth, wine, tomato paste, thyme, salt and pepper.  Add beef, pushing to submerge.  Cover and cook on low, 8 to 10 hours.
Using 2 slotted spoons, remove beef to platter; remove any twine.   Keep warm.  Discard thyme.  Skim fat from surface of cooking liquid.
In small bowl, whisk cream with flour; gradually whisk into slow cooker.  Cover and cook on high until thickened, about 15 minutes.   Spoon onto beef.   Spring with parsley.
Per serving: 340 cal, 30 g pro, 20 g fat, 10 g carb, 1 g fibre, 4 g sugar, 97 mg chol.
