Title:
sweet and spicy sweet potato chips and dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/441254.jpg
Ingredients:
potatoes
*1 lb. sweet potatoes peeled and cut to thin slices
*3/4 tsp. all purpose sugar
*3/4 tbsp. lemon juice (better from real lemons)
*3/4 tsp. nonfat butter or margarine melted
*1/2 tsp. cinnamon
marshmallow
*13 large marshmallows
*1/4 tsp. cinnamon
*1/4 tbs.nonfat margarine
*2 tbsp. skim milk

Directions:
potatoes:
toss all ingredients in a bowl to coat potatoes
scoop potatoes in a casserole dish that has been greased with non fat cooking spray
cover casserole dish and place in over on 350 for 30-35 minutes
sauce:
1.put all ingredients in pot
2.turn on medium until marshmallows are melted
3.turn off heat and stir
