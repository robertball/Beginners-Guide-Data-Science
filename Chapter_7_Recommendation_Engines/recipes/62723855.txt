Title:
Cheesy Pork and Rice Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups leftover pork loin
8 cups (basil/garlic) Rice cooked
1 can of Mushroom soup
1 can of Cheddar Cheese soup
Approx 250mls of black bean salsa
1/2 cup corn
2 cups Tex Mex (any) cheese

Directions:
Place cooked rice in a 9x12 cassarole dish. Add all ingrediants and mix. Top with cheese (Tex Mex mix would be a good choice)

Submitter: 
Vickie Smalls
Description: 
I made up this dish myself from suggestions on this site for what to do with pork and rice. I never know what to do with leftover pork. You can customize this anyway you like but this is really yummy. I made one of these and my family asked for another, immediately!
