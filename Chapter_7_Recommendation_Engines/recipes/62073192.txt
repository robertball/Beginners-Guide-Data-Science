Title:
Beezy white chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups flour
2 eggs
3/4 cup honey
3/4 cup sugar
1 t baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup light of ff sour cream
12 oz of white choc. chips
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
1. In one bowl mix flour, salt, and baking soda. Set aside.
2. In second bowl, mix eggs, sugar, sour cream, vanilla, and honey.
3. Mix the mixtures together. Add white chocolate chips.
4. Scoop in small heaps onto cookie sheets and bake for about 10 minutes at 350 degrees

Submitter: 
JESSAMINE689
Description: 
These are almost little cakes, they're light and oh-so-moist. You could add any kinds of chips to them.
