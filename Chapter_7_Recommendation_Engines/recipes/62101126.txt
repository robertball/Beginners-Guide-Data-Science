Title:
Artichoke & Roasted Red Pepper Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ½ cups chopped bagels (about 3 bagels)
2 tablespoons olive oil
Vegetable cooking spray
2 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
1 container (15 ounces) ricotta cheese
1 can (10 ¾ ounces) condensed cream of celery soup
2 eggs
2 tablespoons chopped green onion
1 tablespoon dried Italian seasoning
1 clove garlic, minced
1 can (14 ounces) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 jar (12 ounces) roasted red bell peppers, drained and chopped, divided
1 cup chopped fresh basil, divided

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine bagels and oil; mix well. Spray 9 X 2 ½-inch spring form baking pan with vegetable cooking spray. Press bagel mixture into bottom of spring form pan. Bake at 375ºF 15 minutes; cool.
With electric mixer, combine cheeses, soup, egg substitute, green onion, Italian seasoning and garlic; mix well. Spread half cheese mixture over bagel crust. Top with artichokes and half each of peppers and basil. Spread remaining cheese filling over basil; top with remaining peppers. Bake at 375ºF 1 hour or until set in middle; cool. Refrigerate 6 to 8 hours or overnight. Run knife around edge of torte; remove side of pan. Top with remaining basil. Slice thinly and serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Anna B
Description: 
This torte is not only easy to make it is a beautiful presentation for any holiday gathering. I am asked time and time again to make this great tasting appetizer!
