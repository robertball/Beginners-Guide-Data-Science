Title:
Brussels Sprouts Pan Fried with Bacon and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved lengthwise
1/4 cup bacon bits
1 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup chopped onion
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoon butter
7 Shiitake mushrooms,cut in half
10 sprigs fresh thyme, stemmed and diced
1 large sprig rosemary, stemmed and diced
1 tsp fennel seed
1 tsp kosher salt
1/4 cup white wine or cooking sherry

Directions:
Steam Brussels sprouts for 6 minuets, drain and place on paper towels
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the onion and garlic until soft. Stir in the bacon bits and the butter, cook until bacon is heated through. Add the Brussels sprouts, mushrooms, thyme, rosemary, fennel seed, and salt. Stir and cook until sprouts are browned, 7 to 10 minuets.
Sprinkle with wine and toss to coat.
