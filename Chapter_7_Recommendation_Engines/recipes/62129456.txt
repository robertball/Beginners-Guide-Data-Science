Title:
Nutricious Low-fat Pumpkin Breakfast Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup all purpose flour
1/3 cup ground flax seed
2/3 cup natural bran
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups splenda (or other sugar substitute)
1/2 cup brown sugar (or brown sugar/splenda blend)
1 egg plus 3 egg whites, lightly beaten
1 large can pumpkin puree (795 g)
1 Tbsp baking powder
2 Tsp baking soda
1 Tbsp cinamon
1/2 tsp allspice
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cloves
2 cups raisins
1 cup chopped nuts or sunflower seeds (optional)
Topping: (optional)
1/2 c icing sugar
6 Tbsp water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except icing sugar in a large bowl and mix until just combined.
Drop by large tablespoons onto a lightly greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350C for approx 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
Cool.
Mix icing sugar with water, 1 Tbsp at a time until you reach drizzling consistency.
Drizzle cookies cookies with thinned icing and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Angela Bergerman
Description: 
Healthy, low-fat, low-sugar, high-fibre breakfast bars - Good for a quick, yet nutricious breakfast, and a great addition to a bowl of soup at lunch or dinner.
