Title:
Quick Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
4 tsp baking powder
1/3 cup butter
2/3 cup milk

Directions:
1) Mix dry ingredients
2) Cut in softened butter
3) Add milk and stir until dough follows fork
4) Drop from spoon onto greased cookie sheet
5) Bake at 425 for 9 minutes
