Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small bag Mahatma yellow rice
1 can Mexicorn
1 can cream of celery soup
1 10 oz bag shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook rice according to directions on package.
Mix rice, soup, and corn in a 3-quart casserole dish, and top with cheese.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Ashley
Description: 
This corn casserole is made with Mexicorn and yellow rice. Absolutely delicious!
