Title:
Swirl Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 tea. vanilla extract
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 graham cracker crust (9 inch)
1 can (21 oz.) pie filling, divided

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Add eggs, beat on low until just combined. pou into crust. Drop 1/2 of the pie filling by heaping teaspoonfulls onto cream cheese mixture, and cut through with a knife to create swirl. 350 for 35-40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool on wire rack for one hour. refrigerate for 2 hours. top with remaining pie filling. Refrigeraste leftovers
