Title:
Pesto Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984489.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) container BUITONI® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
1 baguette, cut in half lengthwise
1/4 cup BUITONI® Refrigerated Freshly Shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
Spread pesto over bread; sprinkle evenly with cheese. Place on baking sheet.
Broil for 1 to 2 minutes or until bread is crisp and cheese is melted. Cut into pieces.
