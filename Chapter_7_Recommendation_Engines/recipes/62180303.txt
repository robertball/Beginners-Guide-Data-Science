Title:
Mom's Cranberry-Orange Nut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c cranberries, sliced thickly
2 c flour
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 c butter, room temp
1 c sugar3/4 c orange juice
1 tbsp grated orange peel, fresh
1 egg
1 c pecans or walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Slice or coarsly cut cranberries; set aside.
Whisk together flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.
Blend egg, butter,juice peel. Add egg mixture, cranberries, nuts to flour misture. Combine until moist.
Fill paper lined muffin tins 3/4 c full. Bake in a 375 degree oven for 17 min or until golden brown.
let cool on wire racks.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A moist and fruity-tart version of the quick bread.
Huge family favorite during the fall when the cranberries are coming into season.
