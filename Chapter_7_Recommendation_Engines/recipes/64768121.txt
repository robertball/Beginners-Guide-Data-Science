Title:
Pancake Noodles  / Pfankuchen Streifen   "celestine" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz Eggs  /  2ea.
6 oz Flour
9 oz Milk/Water.  ( half and half)
1 oz Finely Chopped Parsley
ground; Muscat
salt

Directions:
Make a Finely mixed Batter of all the Ingredients and to include the Parsley and muscat.
using a very lightly oiled Pan, bake (both sides) Thin, whole Pan bottom sized Pancakes.
when they have cooled.  Roll 2 or 3 together into a roll and slice about 1/8 inch thick the width of the entire Pancake. Finish with the rest.  Toss  the slices a bit and set aside .  When you are ready to serve your Stock or Broth add a Large pinch of the Slices.  !!

Alois

Sliced Pancake for serving with Hearty Stock.
