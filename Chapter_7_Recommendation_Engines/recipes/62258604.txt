Title:
Cinnamon Mocha Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/337000.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
1/2 cup white sugar
1 HEAPING Tablespoonful instant espresso or instant coffee granules
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons BAKING POWDER
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup chopped pecans **(See footnote)
1/2 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup cinnamon baking chips (If cinnamon chips are unavailable in your area, use an additional 1/2 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Combine butter, sugars and espresso or coffee granules in a large bowl and beat with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Add vanilla and mix briefly.
Mix flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon together.  Add to creamed mixture until well blended.
Fold in pecans, miniature chocolate chips and cinnamon chips.
Dough will be sticky, form 2-3 ropes about 1 1/4" wide.
Place on a greased cookie (2 per sheet)
Bake 25 minutes (loaves will expand at least double in size during baking)
Remove from oven and cool (5 minutes)
Use a serrated knife to cut loaves diagonally into 1/2-inch slices.
Place slices on its side and return to oven and bake for 8 minutes
Turn slices to the other side and bake 8 minutes more.
Remove from oven and cool completely.
Store in an airtight container.
