Title:
Bountiful Basket Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sesame Oil
Olive Oil
Can of V8 juice
Red Wine Vinegar
White Rice Vinegar
Sweet chili sauce
Hoisin sauce
Soy Sauce
Chicken breast, chopped
Bok Choy, head chopped
Napa Cabbage, head chopped
fresh onion, chopped
ginger, diced
garlic, diced
zucchini, sliced
tomatoes, diced
snow peas
curry powder or other spices to taste

Directions:
Fry chicken breast in oil and vinegar for 5-8 minutes.
Add onion and garlic and cook for 5-8 minutes.
Add other ingredients and cook for 5-8 minutes.
Simmer adding water as needed.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
I used the ingredients we received in our fresh veggie basket to make some great Asian stir fry
