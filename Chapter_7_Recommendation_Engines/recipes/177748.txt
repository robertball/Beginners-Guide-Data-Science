Title:
Creamy Chicken Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976736.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut up
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's速 Condensed Cream of Mushroom with Roasted Garlic Soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's速 Condensed Chicken Broth
3/4 cup water
1 small carrot, chopped
2 green onions, sliced
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup uncooked regular long-grain white rice

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned, stirring often.
Add soup, broth, water, carrot, green onions and cheese. Heat to a boil. Stir in rice. Cover and cook over low heat 25 minutes or until done.
