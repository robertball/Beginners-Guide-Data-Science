Title:
Pumpkin Scones with Cinnamon butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cinnamon Butter:
1/2 stick of butter at room temperature
1 tsp. maple syrup
3/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
Scones:
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. baking soda
2 cups flour
3/4 cup chilled butter
1/2 cup chopped fresh or thawed cranberries
1 large egg
1/2 cup pumpkin
1/4 cup buttermilk plus more for brushing
2 tbsp. raw sugar

Directions:
Cinnamon Butter:
Mix all ingredients in a small bowl, cover and chill until ready to use.
Scones:
Whisk sugar, baking powder, ginger, nutmeg, cinnamon, salt, cloves, baking soda and flour in a large bowl.
Using the large holes on a box grater, grate chilled butter into flour mixture, tossing to coat as you go.
Toss in cranberries.
Mix in egg, pumpkin and buttermilk.
Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and pat to a disk of 1 1/2" thickness.
Cut into 8 wedges and transfer to a parchment lined baking sheet.
Freeze until firm, about 25-30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400.
Brush scones with buttermilk and sprinkle with raw sugar.
Bake until golden brown - about 25-30 minutes.
Serve with cinnamon butter.
