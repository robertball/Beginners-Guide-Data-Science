Title:
Hot Mulled Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon apple cider
1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
1-1/2 tsp. balsamic or cider vinegar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cinnamon stick
6 whole cloves
1/2 cup applejack or buron

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in slow cooker. Cover and cook on low 5-6 hours. Discard cinnamon stick and cloves.
