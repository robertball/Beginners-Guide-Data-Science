Title:
Slow Cooker Chops & Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sweet potatoes, peeled, sliced 1" thick
1/2 c brown sugar
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice or cinnamon
2-3 apples (we like Granny Smith) cored, peeled, quartered
4-6 pork loin chops
1/4 tsp chili powder
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 c barbeque sauce
1/4 c orange juice or water

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and pie spice.
In a slow cooker, toss sweet potatoes and brown sugar mixture.
Place apples on top. Drizzle orange juice over apples.
Combine chili & onion powders and paprika, sprinkle on chops.  Brown chops in a skillet. Place chops on top of apples in the slow cooker.
Spoon barbeque sauce over chops.
Cover, cook on low 8-10 hours, on high 3-4 hours or until chops and potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
REALCOOKIE
Description: 
My kids favorite combo - sweet potatoes and apples - topped off with tender pork chops for an easy family meal.
