Title:
Ina's Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/820330.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese (room temp)
1 1/2 sticks butter (room temp)
1 t vanilla
12 oz powdered sugar, sifted

Directions:
In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, on low speed, cream together the cream cheese, butter, and vanilla extract.
Add the confectioners' sugar and mix until smooth.
