Title:
Herb Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb chicken cut in pieces (cut breasts in half)
1/4 cup flour
1 Tbsp Season-All seasoning salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp Herbes de Provence
1/4 cup butter
1/2 a large onion minced
2 tsp fresh parsley minced
1/2 tsp dried thyme leaves
1/2 cup dry white wine

Directions:
Combine Season-All, pepper, paprika and Herbes de Provence in a bowl.
Roll/press chicken pieces into Season-All mixture.
Bread seasoned chicken pieces with flour.
Brown chicken with butter in a skillet.
Sprinkle onion, parsley and thyme over chicken and add wine.
Cover and cook on stove top at simmer for 45 minutes or until chicken is done.
