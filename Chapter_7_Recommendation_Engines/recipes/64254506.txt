Title:
Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1793640.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 Tbls baking powder
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
½ pound butter (2 sticks)
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 cup milk
2 tsp vanilla
2Tbls mayo
flavors/spices (optional): Cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger
Orange rind, pineapple Juice, Lemon rind, Lemon Juice, Apple Sauce, Almond extract, Brandy etc....

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
Grease and flour cake pan
Combine dry ingredients in bowl
Cream butter, sugar and vanilla in bowl add 1 egg, ¼ of milk, and 1 cup of flour at a time while mixing on high until batter is light colored, smooth and fluffy.
Bake for 22-25 minutes for layers or 40-50 minutes for Bundt or loaf.
