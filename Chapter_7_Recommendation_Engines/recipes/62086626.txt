Title:
Crisp Stuffed Chicken in Marsala Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-- Main Ingredients --
6 chicken breasts
6 slices prosciutto
6 slices Fontina cheese
Spinach
10 ounces sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup Marsala wine
4 Cloves of garlic
Grated Parmesan Reggiano cheese
-- Spices --
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Thyme
Basil
Parsley
(Pre sorted Poultry Herb Packs will work fine)
-- Other Ingredients --
Olive oil
2 cup chicken stock
1 table spoon butter
2 eggs beaten
Seasoned Bread crumbs

Directions:
** Chicken Preparation **
Place chicken breasts between two pieces of wax paper and pound lightly.
Cut pockets into chicken.
Dice 2 cloves of the garlic and rub on outside of chicken.
Season chicken (inside and out) with the rest of the spices.  Let marinate.
Stuff chicken with Fontina cheese, spinach, and prosciutto.
Dip chicken breasts in beaten eggs and dredge in the seasoned bread crumbs.
Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat and brown both sides of chicken
breast.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Remove chicken and place on oven tray.
Top with grated cheese and drizzle olive oil on top.
Place the chicken in the oven for 10-20 minutes until cook through.
** Marsala Sauce **
Add more oil to the same pan if needed.
Add the rest of the garlic diced.
Add sliced mushrooms when garlic has browned.
Cook for approx. 5 minutes until pan is deglazed and add the Marsala wine.
Reduce for about 5 minutes.
Add chicken stock to pan.
Thicken sauce my adding butter and slowly adding flour.
** Finishing **
Prepare bowtie pasta or canned white potatoes.
Plate chicken and starches and pour sauce over everything.

Submitter:

Description: 
I love Chicken Marsala sauce, but I am not a fan of the soggy chicken that usually goes along with it.  This recipe is my interpretation of Chicken Marsala.  One taste and you will never go back to the Old World version.
