Title:
Quick Croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 and a half pounds (by weight) of your favorite potato salad
1 cup of shredded chedder cheese
1 teaspoon black pepper
A pinch of garlic salt
3 and a quarter cups of italian style bread crumbs (plain would work too)

Directions:
With a potato ricer, mix up the potato salad until a coleslaw consistancy. Then add 1/4 cup of bread crumbs, 1 cup of cheese, and garlic salt and pepper. mix well, take a spoon and form medallion on a non-stick sheet. cook 8-10 minutes on each side

Submitter: 
StevenRN
Description: 
This is a really quick and easy croquette recipie that can be made for an appitizer in a pinch.
