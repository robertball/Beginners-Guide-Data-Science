Title:
Nurnberger Lebkuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lg eggs
1 c sugar
2 tbsp vanilla sugar
2 1/3 flour, sifted
1 tsp baking powder
1 1/3 c almonds, chopped finely
1/2 c citron, chopped finely
1/8 freshley grated lemon peel
1/8 pinch clove
1/8 allspice
1/8 pinch cardamom
1 tsp cinnamon
oblaten (70 mm)
glasur , or rum chocolate recipe

Directions:
In a bowl sift flour and baking powder together, set aside.
Chop the citron and almonds finely, setr aside.
Cream eggs and sugars until light and fluffy. Add flour mixture, chopped citron and almonds. Mix until moist.
Place tbsp of dough onto oblaten, bake in a 350 degree F oven for 13 min or until lightly golden brown on the edges.
Cover with glasur or schokoladenglasur, deorating with almond slivers in a star pattern.
Can be stored for a week in a tight container.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Lebkuchen (or Pfefferkuchen) is a traditional German product baked for Christmas, somewhat resembling soft gingerbread. This is a recipe translated fathfully from a german cookbook passed down 2 generations.
