Title:
Orange-Basil Sparkler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6866374.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sprig basil
2 slices navel orange
4 slices lemon
1 1/2 cups chilled sparkling water
2 packets Emergen-C® Super Orange vitamin drink mix
Ice

Directions:
Place basil sprig and orange and lemon slices in a pitcher and gently muddle (press from different angles) using a wooden spoon until basil is fragrant and citrus releases juices.
Add sparkling water. Stir in Emergen-C(R) Super Orange vitamin drink mix. Using a spoon to prevent fruit from splashing out, pour drink over ice and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Emergen-C®
Description: 
Emergen-C® Super Orange sparkles in this refreshing drink, which features citrus slices and fresh basil for a punch of flavor.
