Title:
Cowboy Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
12 ounces bacon
2 16 ounce cans great northern beans (may substitute favorite canned beans of any type)
1 16 ounce can pork and beans
1 cup barbecue sauce, any flavor
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1 envelope dried onion soup mix
1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce (optional)
1/2 tsp dried oregano (optional)
1/4 tsp cumin (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
Fry bacon until crunchy, and crumble when cool.
Brown ground beef, drain well.
In large bowl, mix together beans, barbecue sauce, brown sugar, and onion soup mix.
Add crumbled, crispy bacon and drained ground beef, along with spices and hot sauce.
Pour into 3 quart casserole dish and bake for 40 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description: 
These hearty baked beans are excellent for summer picnics or for a winter's meal.  Switch the ingredients around to suit your taste, including or leaving out spices as you like.  Also, use your favorite barbecue sauce for a custom flavor.
