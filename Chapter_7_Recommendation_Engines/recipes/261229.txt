Title:
Apple Carrot Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4576320.jpg
Ingredients:
3 carrots, chopped
2 apples, cored and chopped
1 navel orange, peeled and sectioned
1 tablespoon flaxseed oil

Directions:
Press carrots, apples, and orange slices through a juicer and into a large glass. Stir in flaxseed oil.

Submitter: 
MisterHealthy
Description: 
Apple and orange are added to this carrot juice which makes for a healthy, tasty beverage. If you add flaxseed oil, you'll add some omega-3 fatty acids to your morning drink.
