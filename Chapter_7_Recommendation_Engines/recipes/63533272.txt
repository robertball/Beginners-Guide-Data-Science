Title:
Herb Buttered Chicken and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/966486.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 Chicken Breasts, boneless, cut in bite-sized chunks
1 1/2 stks Butter
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
2 tsp Salt
1 tsp dried Parsley
1 tsp dried Thyme
1 can Chicken Broth
20-30 Baby Carrots (cut into threes)
1 Tbsp Mrs. Dash
1 med Green Pepper, julianned
3-4 Celery Stalks, chopped
1/2 cup Bread Crumbs
1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
To make herb butter: Melt 1 1/2 sticks of butter, then stir in Garlic Powder, Parsley, Thyme and 1 tsp Salt.
Add 2-3 Tbsp of Herb butter to a pan and saute chicken chunks, over medium heat, til chicken is slightly browned.
Transfer to plate and cover.
Over medium heat, bring 1 can of Chicken Broth, Carrots, Mrs. Dash and 1 tsp Salt to boil.
Reduce heat, cover and cook til carrots are tender (not mushy).
Add Green Peppers and Celery. Cook till tender.
In meantime, combine Bread Crumbs, grated Parmesan and remaining butter in a small bowl. Heat in microwave.
Stir Chicken chunks and Buttered Bread Crumbs into vegetables.
Stir until Bread Crumbs are evenly distributed and Chicken is heated through.
