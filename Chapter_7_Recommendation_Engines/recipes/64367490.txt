Title:
Cinnamon Acorn Squash Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons milk
1 egg
3/4 cup soft bread crumbs (about 2 & 1/2 slices bread)
1/4 cup cornmeal
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 large acorn squash (1 & 1/2 pounds), cut crosswise (NOT lengthwise)into 1/2 inch slices and seeded
1/3 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Mix brown sugar, milk and egg.
Mix breadcrumbs, cornmeal and cinnamon.
Dip squash slices into egg mixture, then coat with bread crumb mixture; repeat.
Place in ungreased rectangular 13x9x2 pan.  Drizzle with melted butter.  Bake uncovered at 400 for 30-35 minutes or until squash is tender.
