Ingredients:
1/2 cup bottled Steak Sauce
1/4 cup Bourbon Whiskey
1 tablespoon Honey
2 teaspoons prepared Mustard
4 steaks

Directions:
1. Combine all the ingredients except the steaks in a baking dish or resealable plastic storage bag
2. Mix well.
3. Add the steaks
4. Cover (or seal) and refrigerate for 2 hours, or overnight.
5. Remove steaks and grill as desired.  Discard marinade.
