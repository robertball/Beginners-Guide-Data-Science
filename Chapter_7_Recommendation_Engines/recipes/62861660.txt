Title:
Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup butter or all-vegetable shortening
1 ¼ cups brown sugar
2 Tbsp. milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 egg
1 ¾ cups flour
1 tsp. salt
¾ tsp. baking soda
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 375F.
Combine butter, brown sugar, milk and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat at medium speed of electric mixer until well blended. Beat eggs into creamed mixture.
Combine flour, salt, baking soda. Mix into creamed mixture just until blended. Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop by rounded spoonfuls 3-inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet.
Bake on baking sheet at a time for 8-10 minutes for chewy cookies (Cookies will appear moist. Do not over bake.) or 11-13 minutes for crisp cookies.
Cool 2 minutes on baking sheet place sheets of foil down. Remove cookies to foil to cool.
