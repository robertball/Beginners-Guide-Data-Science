Title:
Hot Crash Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 red potatoes
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 tablespoon Greek/Turkish seasoning

Directions:
Boil Potatoes to fork tender stage.
Drizzle cookie sheet with olive oil.
Smash each potato with potato masher.
Sprinkle with more olive oil, salt, pepper, rosemary and seasoning.
Bake in 450F oven for 20-25 minutes until crispy.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
Originally from the Pioneer Woman site, these potatoes ROCK!  A little like latkes, but easier!
