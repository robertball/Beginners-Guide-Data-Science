Title:
Bold and Spicy Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4572876.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
several stalks of celery, chopped
1/2 package cabbage slaw mix
1 small green pepper, chopped
4 cups fresh mixed vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, radishes, carrots)
2 cups kale/spinach combo
1/2 pkg frozen peas, Lima beans or mixed vegetables
2 cups cooked, chopped chicken
coconut oil for sauteing
1 quart beef or chicken stock
1 large can petite diced tomatoes
1 quart water
1 T. lemon pepper
1 T. Weber's Bold & Spicy Chipotle Seasoning
1 T. organic garlic & herb seasoning
1 t. sea salt

Directions:
Saute the raw vegetables in the coconut oil until slightly softened.
Add the frozen vegetables.
Add seasonings and salt.
Add the tomatoes, stock and water.
Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 1-2 hours.
Adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
Use leftover vegetable and stock for a delicious, easy soup.
