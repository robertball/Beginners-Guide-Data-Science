Title:
Spicy Mexican Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2197738.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, diced
1 tablespoon crushed garlic
1 1/2 cups tomato juice cocktail
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce, or to taste
salt to taste
1 chopped cucumber
1 ripe avocado - peeled, pitted, and chopped

Directions:
Mix shrimp, red onion, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, and garlic & cucumber in a large bowl; add tomato juice, lime juice, horseradish, and hot pepper sauce and stir. Season mixture with salt. Gently stir avocado into the mixture taking care to not mash the avocado.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours before serving.

Description: 
This is perfect for a light summer meal. Cooked shrimp are chilled in a tomato-juice cocktail with avocado, red onion, and cilantro. The addition of jalapeno chiles brings the heat.Modified from: Spicy Mexican Shrimp Cocktail
