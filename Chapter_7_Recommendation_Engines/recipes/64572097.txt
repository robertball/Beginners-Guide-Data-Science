Title:
Big Ray's Garlic-Roasted Chicken and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3915376.jpg
Ingredients:
8bone-in chicken thighs (about 2-1/4 pounds)
6 medium red potatoes (about 2 pounds), cut into 1-inch cubes
24 garlic cloves, peeled
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 tsp salt, divided
1/4 cup maple syrup

Directions:
Place the chicken, potatoes and garlic in a large roasting pan. Drizzle with butter; sprinkle with 3/4 teaspoon salt. Toss to coat. Bake, uncovered, at 400° for 40 minutes.
Combine the syrup and remaining salt; drizzle over chicken. Spoon pan juices over potatoes and garlic. Bake 20 minutes longer or until a thermometer reads 170°-175° and potatoes are tender.
