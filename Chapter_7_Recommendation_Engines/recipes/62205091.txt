Title:
Vanishing Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup (2 sticks margarine) or butter
1 Cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 Cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 Cup all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt (optional)
3 Cups Quaker Instant Oatmeal
1 Cup raisins
1 1/2 Cup coconut (Optional)
1 1/2 Cup nutmeats (Optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Beat together margarine and sugar until creamy. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well. Add combined flour, baking soda, cinnamon, salt; mix well. Stir in oats, raisins (coconut and nutmeats if adding them too); mix well. Drop by rounded Tblsp. onto ungreased baking sheet.(I flatten them out with a wet fork) Bake 10-13 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 1 minute on cookie sheet, remove to wire rack.
