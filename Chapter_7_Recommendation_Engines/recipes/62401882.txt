Title:
Don's Cocktail Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/431864.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup of Ketchup
3 Tab Chili Sauce
3 Tea Lemon Juice
12 Drops of Tabasco Sauce
1 1/2 tea Worchestershire Sauce
3 tea Horseradish

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and chill
increase or reduce horseradish to your taste, we usually add more.
Makes enough for 2.5 lbs of large shrimp

Submitter: 
KENDRU58
Description: 
My father in law made this all the time for family gatherings. We recently received the recipe and now prepare it for our family.  It requires a few more ingredients than most, but they are common items that have a long shelf life, and are definitely worth it.
