Title:
parmesan roasted cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6039980.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
1 sliced onion
4 thyme sprigs
4 unpeeled garlic cloves
3 tbs olive oil
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut cauliflower into florets. toss on a large rimmed baking sheet with  sliced onion, thyme and garlic cloves and olive oil. season with salt and ground pepper. Roast, tossing occasionally until tender, 35-45 mins. Sprinkle with parmesan , toss to combine and roast until cauliflower to tender, 10-15 mins longer
