Title:
Mom's Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 lb beef, cut up
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1 onion, chopped
2 cubes Beef Bouillon
2 cans cream mushroom soup
1 large container sour cream
Kitchen Bouquet (desired amount)
1 pkg wide egg noodles

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions.
Heat oil in large saucepan; saute onions and garlic.
Add beef and bouillon, cook until beef is no longer red.
Add soup, sour cream and Kitchen Bouquet.
Simmer for hour.
Server over noodles.
