Title:
Bill's Spicy Refrigerator Dill Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 3 to 4 inch long pickling cucumbers
2 cups water
1 3/4 cups white vinegar
1 1/2 cups chopped fresh dill weed
2 tbsp white sugar
8 cloves garlic, chopped
1 1/2 tablespoons coarse salt
1 tablespoon pickling spice
1 1/2 teaspoons dill seed (optional)
1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
4 sprigs fresh dill weed

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the cucumbers, water, vinegar, chopped dill, sugar, garlic, salt, pickling spice, dill seed, and red pepper flakes.
Stir, and let stand at room temperature for 2 hours, until the sugar and salt dissolve.
Remove the cucumbers and liquid to a 1 1/2 quart container with lid. Refrigerate for 7 days before eating. Use within 1 month.

Bill Alexander
