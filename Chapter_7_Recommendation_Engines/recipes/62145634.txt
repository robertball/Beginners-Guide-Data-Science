Title:
Cucumber Carrot Salad (Raita) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Half cucumber
Half carrot
One and a half cups of yogurt
Salt
Chopped cilantro (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, shred cucumber and carrot.
Add yogurt and mix well. Adjust amount of yogurt according to preference.
Add salt to taste.
Garnish with chopped cilantro.

Submitter: 
PB
Description: 
This is a side dish often referred to as raita in Indian cuisine.  It consists of a combination of shredded cucumbers and carrots mixed in yogurt.
