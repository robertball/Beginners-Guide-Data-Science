Title:
Garlic Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/609635.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions:
Rinse beans under cold water to remove any residue.
Snap off the ends of each bean. Discard these.
Place beans and enough water to cover in a saucepan; bring to a boil.
Cook, uncovered, for 8-10 minutes or until crisp-tender; drain.
Toss beans with garlic, salt and pepper. Drizzle with oil.
Serve immediately.
