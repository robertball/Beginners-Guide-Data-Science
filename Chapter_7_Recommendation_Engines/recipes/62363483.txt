Title:
Floyd's Favorite Chicken-Broccoli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. butter
1 chicken, cut up (or an equivalent amount of drumsticks, wings, etc.)
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
4 cups broccoli
3 cups potatoes
2 cups cream of chicken soup (other cream soups work fine too)
1 1/4 cup mushroom pieces, cooked
1/4 cup water or broth
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce (optional)
1/2 tsp. dried thyme leaves

Directions:
Melt butter in a 9x14 pan in a 400 degree oven.
Place chicken in pan and turn to coat with butter.  Arrange skin sides up.
Sprinkle garlic salt over chicken.
Bake uncovered at 400 for 1 hour.
Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, boil potatoes and broccoli until almost tender.
Mix next five ingredients separately.
Remove chicken from oven and drain fat from dish.
Arrange broccoli and potatoes along sides of chicken.
Spoon soup mixture over all.
Return to oven and bake uncovered at 350 about 20 minutes.
Garnish with paprika if desired.
