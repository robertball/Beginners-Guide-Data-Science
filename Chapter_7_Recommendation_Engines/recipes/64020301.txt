Title:
Italian Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound carrots, peeled and cut diagonally into 1/2-inch pieces
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup chicken broth
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon marjoram
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 dash freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine butter, broth, galic, oregano, and marjoram in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add carrots; reduce heat to medium-low. Cook, covered, for 10 minutes, or until carrots are crisp-tender. Raise heat to high, and add vinegar. Continue to cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly, until carrots are glazed and liquids are reduced. Season to taste with black pepper and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Laurel Patton
Description: 
This recipe came from the Greensboro News & Record. It is an excellent substitute for a salad if you want something different to serve alongside a pasta dish.
