Title:
Vichyssoise, Potato, Onion, Leek Cream Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6095843.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 sticks butter
1 large onion, diced
3 leeks, cleaned with ends trimmed and both green and white parts sliced
6 large potatoes, cleaned with peels still on and sliced
1/4 C chicken mckays seasoning
1 t ground black pepper
1 quart half and half

Directions:
In a medium pot melt butter
Sauté onion until it is translucent
Add sliced leeks, sauté until they are wilted
Add chicken mckays seasoning and mix well
Add water
Bring pot to a boil
Cover pot turn heat down to low and simmer for 30 minutes
Test potatoes with a fork making sure that they are completely cooked
Using an immersion blender purée mixture
Slowly pour in half and half while using immersion blender
Adjust salt and pepper to taste
Serve
If not hot enough gently reheat

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
A delicious thick Cream Soup made with Potatoes, Onions, Leek, and half and half
I leave the potato skins on since that is where all the vitamins and minerals are, having said that this is a delicious decadent soup that is not low calorie
