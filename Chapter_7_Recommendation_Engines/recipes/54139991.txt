Title:
Fish in a Sack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Parchment paper
Green beans or mixed vegetables
Anchovy paste
Roasted red peppers
Basa fish
Pitted clamata olives
Garlic and sauted onions
Dried parsley
Fish seasoning (epicure)
Lemon garlic spice

Directions:
Cut parchment paper into sheets to fit serving (will need to be big enough to fold into bag shape)
Stack the meal in the order of the ingredients listed above
Fold the parchment and make a pouch
Bake at 350 degrees F

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
Steamed fish that melts in your mouth (or fish in a sack)
