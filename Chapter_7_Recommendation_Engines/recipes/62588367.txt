Title:
Winter Lentil Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup red or green lentils
1 chopped onion
4 stalk celery, chopped
2 cans tomatoes or 4-6 fresh diced tomatoes
6 cups chicken broth
4 carrots, chopped
2 clove garlic, crushed
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 tsp cayenne
1 small bag Fresh Spinach

Directions:
Place the lentils into a stockpot or a Dutch oven and add water to twice the depth of the lentils. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and let simmer for about 15 minutes or until just underdone. Drain and rinse lentils; Put in slowcooker
Add onion, celery, tomatoes, chicken broth, carrots and garlic to the pot and season with pepper, basil, thyme, curry and cayenne (as desired). Cook in slowcooker on high for 2-3 hours or until desired tenderness is achieved.
