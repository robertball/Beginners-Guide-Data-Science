Title:
Cheese Stuffed Jalapenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz cream cheese (softened)
1/2 cup grated sharp cheddar
Salt
Pepper
6 large jalapenos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450F.
Halve the six jalapeno peppers lengthwise and remove seeds and ribs.
In a small bowl, mix cream cheese and cheddar.
Season with salt & pepper to taste.
With a small spoon, fill each jalapeno with cheese mixture.
Place peppers on parchment-lined baking sheet and bake until cheese is browned and bubbling (~10 minutes).

Description: 
Jalapeno peppers stuffed with cheese and then baked (or grilled) to perfection.
