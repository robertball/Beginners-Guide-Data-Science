Title:
Slow Cooker Chocolate Banana Steel Cut Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1969063.jpg
Ingredients:
cooking spray
10 cups water
2 cups steel-cut oats
2 pounds ripe bananas, mashed
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/3 cup granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as SplendaÂ®) (optional)

Directions:
Lightly spray a 5-quart or larger slow cooker crock with cooking spray.
Mix water, oats, mashed bananas, cocoa powder, and sweetener in prepared slow cooker.
Cook on Low for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
DebbieKat
Description: 
Just another way for oatmeal to be enjoyed! Makes plenty of leftovers to freeze for later.
