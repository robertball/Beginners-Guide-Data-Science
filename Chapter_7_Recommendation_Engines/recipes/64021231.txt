Title:
Sweet Irish Soda Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
1 1/4 cups sugar
1/4 tsp. baking soda
3/4 cup milk
2 tsp. cinnamon
4 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. nutmeg
3 large eggs
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Add milk and eggs and stir until just blended.
Place dough on a floured surface and shape into 6 evenly sized loaves.
Place loaves on a greased cookie sheet then cut a cross into the top of each loaf.
Bake for 30-35 minutes or until loaves test done.
