Title:
Miso, Chicken adn Green Onion soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups chicken breast cut into cubes or Tofu or turkey breast
1 onion peeled and chopped
5 green onions, trimmed and chopped
1 1/2 portobello mushrooms, peeled and sliced
8 cups low sodium chicken or vegetable broth
2 Tbsp miso
sea salt and pepper

Directions:
1. In a large skillet saute onions until golden. Add slice mushrooms and cook 5 minutes more. use 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth- continue  cooking onions and mushrooms. let liquid disappear.
2. In a small bowl whisk miso paste with 2 Tbsp broth. Pour into skillet and cook. Season with seal salt and pepper. Add remaining broth and cooked chicken or turkey or tofu. cook for 30 minutes more on low hear.

Submitter: 
Micaella
Description: 
Great homemade miso soup. It's loaded with protein or if you're vegan you can substitute firm tofu for the chicken.
