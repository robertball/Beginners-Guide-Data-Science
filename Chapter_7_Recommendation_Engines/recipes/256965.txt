Title:
Super Shrimp Dip  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4466231.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked shrimp meat
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise (such as Best Foods®)
1/2 cup cocktail sauce
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped cooked bacon
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped green onions
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon hot sauce
2 teaspoons seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay®)
1 large green bell pepper, cored and seeded

Directions:
Mix shrimp, cream cheese, mayonnaise, cocktail sauce, celery, bacon, Cheddar cheese, green onions, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and seafood seasoning in a large bowl.
Chill shrimp mixture until flavors combine, at least 1 hour. Spoon into green bell pepper.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is sure to please all your guests. You can also serve this dip warm in a hollowed-out baguette. Serve with baguette slices, cold veggies, and crackers.
