Title:
Fruit Soup/Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fruit (frozen will make it thicker) - blueberries, strawberries, or peaches
1/4 cup plain yogurt or buttermilk
2 packets of Splenda  (or sweetener of choice)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender or food processor.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This is a quick fix for something healthy and fruity.  Use your favorite frozen fruit for an ice cream-like treat, or use thawed fruit for a fruit soup!
