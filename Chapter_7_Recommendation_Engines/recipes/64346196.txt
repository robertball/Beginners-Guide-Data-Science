Title:
Penne Chicken and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound penne pasta
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1/4-1/2 cup finely chopped red pepper
1/2 onion chopped
2/3 cup heavy cream
1 tablespoons butter
1 cup chix stock
1 cube chicken bouillon
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1 tablespoons cornstarch
2 large heads broccoli, steamed & chopped
2 breaded and cooked skinless, boneless chicken breast

Directions:
In a large pot of salted boiling water, place pasta and cook(abt 10-12 minutes), reserve 1 cup h2o from pan, then drain. Put aside
In a large skillet, saute garlic, onion, & red pepper in butter over medium heat till soft (5 min). Add chicken broth, stock and bouillon, and reduce  by abt 1/2(5-10 min). Stir in the heavy cream, cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Stir well, then add chix, pasta and broccoli. Simmer all together for about 5-10 minutes. Add parmesan and serve.
