Title:
Artichoke Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1128705.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts sliced horizontally to make 4 thinner pieces
1 small onion-chopped
2 cloves garlic-minced
3 T. Olive oil
1 small bag pre- washed baby spinach
3 5-oz. Jars marinated artichoke hearts-drained and chopped (reserve 1/4 cup marinade)
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 cup mayonnaise/sour cream (1/2 of each to equal 3/4c)
Pinch garlic pepper
Pinch of cayenne

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Butter a shallow glass baking dish. Add spinach evenly to dish.  Place chicken in single layer on top of spinach.
3. Caramelize onions in olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook additional 1 minute.
4. Spread onion/garlic mixture over chicken.
5.  In a medium bowl, mix together the artichoke hearts, parmesan cheese, mayo/sour cream, and garlic pepper.
6. Cover chicken evenly with artichoke mixture.  Lightly sprinkle with cayenne pepper.
7. Bake, uncovered, for 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear and top is browned.
