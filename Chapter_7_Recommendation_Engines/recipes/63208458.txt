Title:
Tropical Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pineapple
2 ripe mangoes
2 ripe papayas
2-4 bananas, depends on preference

Directions:
Cut pineapple into small 1/2 inch cubes. Peel the mango and papaya and cut small as well. Slice in bananas. Mix and serve.

Submitter: 
Devin
Description: 
A bright, beautiful and delicious fruit salad. This goes well with jerk chicken or another island meal.
