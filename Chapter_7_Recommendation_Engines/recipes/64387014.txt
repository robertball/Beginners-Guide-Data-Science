Title:
Day Before Payday Top Ramen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Serves two
Two Packs Top Ramen
1 can tuna-oil or water packed
water as directed on Ramen pkg
2 drops Tabasco sauce (optional)
vegetable oil
Sesame oil
Sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Prepare Top Ramen as directed on package breaking noodles before boiling and separating as they cook.
Drain half the water when noodles are done.
Add seasoning packages and Tabasco (optional) with one can Tuna. If using oil packed Tuna do not drain.Flake Tuna well.
Mix all seasonings well and serve with a dollap of Sour Creme (optional).

Description: 
Yes, we all survived on this through college, but now that some of us have worked our fingers off at jobs to pay off college loans and please bosses AND earn a retirement of sorts AND are retired indeed, while we wait for the retirement checks (well-earned I may say) to arrive, here is a quick, cheap and easy dish.
