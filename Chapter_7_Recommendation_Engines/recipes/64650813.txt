Title:
Charles' Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Jalapenos, diced
a pot of melted Cheddar and Gouda
crumbled bacon
half an onion, diced

Directions:
put the diced onion in the melted cheese
simmer until onions are soft
toss in the diced jalapenos and simmer
stir occasionally
then add bacon
stir to evenly mix the ingredients
enjoy

Submitter: 
Charles Bullock
Description: 
A cheese sauce that is splendidly amazing
