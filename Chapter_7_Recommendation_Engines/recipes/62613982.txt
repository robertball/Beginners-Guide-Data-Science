Title:
Italian "Palmiers" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/568072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet of puff pastry
2 tbsp mayonnaise
7 grams of very thinly sliced Proscuitto
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spread mayo over the entire surface of puff pastry.
Lay strips of Proscuitto to cover the surface with out over lap.
Sprinkle evenly with Parmesan.
Roll parallel edges toward each other to meet at the center.
Slice double barreled log into 12 equal slices which will be about 1/2" think.
Bake slices on cookie sheet for 20 minutes until golden and crisp. Cool.

Submitter: 
Sheila Tastula
Description: 
As this is a Gang-Up challenge I am taking great license with this recipe.  My theme was Italian dessert appetizers. This is an Italian version of a Palmier pastry.
