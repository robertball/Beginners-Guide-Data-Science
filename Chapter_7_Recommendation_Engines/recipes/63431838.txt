Title:
Salty Dog Cocktail and Holiday Giveaway + AMEX gift card Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/929838.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounce Vodka
1 1/2 ounce fresh Grapefruit juice
2 tablespoons of Vanilla Bean Salt
Grapefruit wedge

Directions:
Cut a grapefruit in half, juicing one half.  In a martini shaker add ice, juice, vodka and shake to mix.
Cut Grapefruit wedge
Rub rim of glass over other 1/2 of grapefruit and then sit glass onto salt to coat rim.
Pour cocktail in glass, add Grapefruit wedge and enjoy!
