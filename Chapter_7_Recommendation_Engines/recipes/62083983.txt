Title:
Half-Healthy Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup milled flax seed
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup soy flour
1 Tbl baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 3/4 cup lowfat milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup safflower oil
Cooking oil spray

Directions:
1. Preheat waffle iron.
2. In a large bowl, combine all-purpose flour, flax seed, whole wheat flour, soy flour, baking powder and salt.
3. In a seperate bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and oil. Add them to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
4. Lightly spray preheated iron with cooking oil. Cook waffles in batches until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Wannabe Gardener
Description: 
This delicious nutty flavored recipe was developed by my husband so we could eat our waffles and feel good about it! Best served with warmed maple syrup.
