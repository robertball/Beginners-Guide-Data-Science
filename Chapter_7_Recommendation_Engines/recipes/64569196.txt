Title:
Provencal Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large tomatoes (1 lb), cut crosswise into 1/2 in thick slices
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
3 Tbs olive oil, or as needed
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup grated asiago cheese
2 Tbs dried seasoned bread crumbs
2 Tbs dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 deg F
Arrange tomatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet or in a flameproof baking dish, sprinkle with   salt and pepper and drizzle with 1 Tbs oil.
Stir together cheeses, bread crumbs, parsley and remaining 2 Tbs oil in a small bowl.  Top tomatoes with cheese mixture and bake until tomatoes are tender, 5-7 min.
Heat broiler with rack 6 in from heat source.  Broil tomatoes until tops are crisp about 1 min.

Submitter: 
Sylvia Marchesi Walton
Description: 
These delicious fresh tomatoes broiled with a cheese toping pair well with chicken, meat or fish.
