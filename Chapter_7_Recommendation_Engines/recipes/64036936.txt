Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 Cups Frozen Apples
1/2 Unsalted Butter
1 Cup Flour
1 tsp Cinnamon
1 lb Brown Sugar
10 Cups Crumble Topping

Directions:
1. Place Apples on sheet pan and put in oven for 8-10 minutes or until Apples are thawed.
2. In large bowl, add all ingredients: butter, flour, cinnamon, brown sugar, and apples, toss well.  Place mixture in 4 inch Hotel Pan- spray first. Top with Crumble topping and bake for 35-45 minutes or until bubbling all around edges.
