Title:
Shrimp with Lime Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 egg
2 tbsp. water
1 c. Panko breadcrumbs
4 tbsp. light oil, divided in half
2 tbsp. butter
juice of one lime

Directions:
Mix egg and water to make a light egg wash.
Dip the shrimp into the egg wash and toss in breadcrumbs.
Brown shrimp in 2 tbsp. of oil in a fry pan on the stove on medium heat.
Remove shrimp and keep warm.
Rinse out the pan and melt remaining 2 tbsp. of oil and the butter in the pan.
Once well melted, add the lime juice into the pan and let cook until the mixture starts to boil.
Boil for 1 min.
Place each piece of shrimp back in the pan momentarily until both sides are coated with the lime sauce and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Miss Amy
Description: 
Panko coated pan searerd shrimp with a delicious lime butter that is tasty enough but does not overpower the flavors of the shrimp.  Serve over a mixed greens salad or with angel hair pasta with cream sauce and a fresh vegetable for a larger meal.
