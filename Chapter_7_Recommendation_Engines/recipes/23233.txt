Title:
Holiday Pepper Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982508.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup LAND O LAKES® Light Sour Cream
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup chopped pitted ripe olives
2 cups LAND O LAKES® Hot Pepper Monterey Jack Cheese, shredded
1 (2 ounce) jar diced pimento, drained
1 tablespoon sliced green onion
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Combine sour cream  and cream cheese in small bowl. Beat at medium speed, scraping bowl often, until smooth.
Stir in all remaining ingredients by hand except tortilla chips. Cover; refrigerate at least 2 hours. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Land O'Lakes, Inc.
Description: 
Hot pepper cheese brings pizzazz to this great party dip.
