Title:
Creamy Orange and Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup orange juice.
1 cup frozen strawberries.
1/2 cup Lit n Fit Vanilla yogurt.
ice (opt. or if using fresh strawberries)

Directions:
Blend orange juice and strawberries in blender on high until mixed(will be sorbet consistency)
then add in yogurt and blend until creamy.
Enjoy the cold, sweet, slightly tangy, smoothie.

Submitter: 
Isabela14
Description: 
Orange juice and strawberries blended with lit n fit vanilla yogurt making this smoothie delicious and a lower calorie breakfast option.(160 calories)
