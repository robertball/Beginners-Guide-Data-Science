Title:
George's Critters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. vegetable oil
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. lemon pepper
1 tsp. dill weed
1 envelope Ranch dressing (dry)
2 pkgs. oyster crackers (12 oz. ea.)

Directions:
Mix all together. Bake at 200 for 1 hr., stirring every 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description: 
Spicy crackers great for snacking on during the holidays.
