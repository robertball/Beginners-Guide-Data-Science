Title:
Watermelon and Jicama Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C orange juice
1 T orange zest
1/4 C lime juice
1 T lime zest
2 T honey
1 T olive oil
1 T fresh ground pepper
1 T sea salt
1 small seedless watermelon, peeled and cubed (4-6 C)
1/2 small jicama, peeled and cubed (1/2-1 C)

Directions:
Add the juices, zest, honey, oil, pepper and salt in a large bowl and whisk together. Add the cubed watermelon and jicama, and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
WILKBROWN
Description: 
This pretty, easy salad goes great with shrimp for a light summer meal.
