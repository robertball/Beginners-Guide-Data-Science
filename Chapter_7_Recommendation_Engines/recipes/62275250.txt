Title:
Fruit Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/347189.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup softened margarine/butter
1/4 cup oil
3 Tbsp. cold water
3/4 cup softened cream cheese (or use thick, cooled pudding)
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
strawberries, kiwis, or any sliced fruit
1 tsp. cornstarch
2 Tbsp. water
1/3 cup orange juice or other fruit juice
3 Tbsp. powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine first three ingredients with fork until it resembles coarse meal.  Stir in oil with fork, then add 3 Tbsp. water a little at a time until dough leaves sides of bowl.
Grease baking sheet well.  Shape dough into a ball on baking sheet and flatten to an 11-inch circle.  Cook at 375 F. for 12-14 minutes.  Cool 10 minutes, then carefully remove to wire rack to finish cooling.  When cool, slide onto serving plate.
Combine cream cheese, 1/4 cup sugar, and vanilla in bowl and blend until smooth.  Spread mixture over crust.  Arranged any combination of sliced fruit on top in concentric circles.
Combine last four ingredients in small sauce pan.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly; boil one minute.  Cool.  Spoon over fruit.  Refrigerate no longer than two hours before serving.
