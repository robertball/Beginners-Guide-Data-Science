Title:
Blackberry Pastry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c warm milk (105-115)
1 pkg (1/4 oz) active dry yeast
1/2 c butter, soft
1 T sugar
1 t salt
3 1/2 c flour
1/4 c sugar
1 c whipping cream
1 c blackberry, sweetened
3 egg yolks

Directions:
Combine milk, yeast and 1 T sugar.
Mix in egg yolks and whipping cream.
Set aside 10 minutes.
Blend flour, 1/4 c sugar and salt.
Cut in butter until pieces are size of peas.
Pour yeast mixture over.
Fold until ingredients are mixed.
Do not overstir.
Cover with plastic wrap.
Refrigerate 4 hours.
Turn dough onto lightly floured board.
Divide into three portions.
Dust each portion with flour.
Roll one at a time to make a 12X9 rectangle.
With knife or pastry wheel make cuts one inch apart on edges.
Spoon 1/3 c blackberry in the center.
Crisscross strips over filling to look like a braid.
Place on greased cookie sheet.
Let rise until almost double.
Bake at 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Brandon Willard Gossman
Description: 
This is a wonderful sweet bread that wraps up sweetened blackberries.  I have made this recipe for my family for over 15 years.
