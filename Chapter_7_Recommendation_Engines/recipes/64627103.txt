Title:
Vegan gluten free chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4515021.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cup Pillsbury Best gluten free all purpose flour blend
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup organic coconut oil
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup white sugar
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 tsp vanilla extract
Enjoy brand choc. chips

Directions:
1. Mix sugars and oil until fluffy2. Add applesauce and vanilla and mix well.3.Add the flour, salt and baking soda. Mix until combined.4. Fold in chips5. Bake at 350 for about 10 minutes. Check after 8 minutes, the bottom will be brown and tops will still look a little uncooked. Let them sit 5 min on the pan and another 10 on a wire rack
