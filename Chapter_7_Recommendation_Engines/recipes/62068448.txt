Title:
Picadillo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat mixture:
2 1/2 lbs. ground beef
2 medium yellow onions
2 green peppers
6-8 cloves of garlic
salt and pepper, to taste
1 c. raisins
1 c. green olives
1 c. red wine
2 14 oz.cans stewed tomatoes
Black Bean:
2 cans black beans
1 can black bean soup
1 onion
3-4 cloves garlic
2 T. ketchup
2 T. vinegar
2 bay leaves
2 T. olive oil
Serve over yellow saffron rice.

Directions:
Chop the onions, peppers and garlic and saute until softened.
Add meat and cook thoroughly, drain
Add the next 5 ingredients.
Cook slowly for 30-40 minutes.
In another pot saute one onion and 3-4 cloves of garlic with the olive oil.
Add Black Bean soup and 2 cans of black beans.
Add the next 3 ingredients to taste.
Simmer for 15-20 mins.
This recipe is the best if made ahead of time and reheated.  Flavors have time to meld.
Serve with yellow saffron rice.  Layer the beans on the rice and the meat mixture on the top.

Submitter:

Description: 
My sister-in-law gave me this easy and delicious recipe.  There are so many wonderful flavors and textures-   yellow saffron rice, black beans and then a flavorful meat sauce.  This meal is sure to impress!
