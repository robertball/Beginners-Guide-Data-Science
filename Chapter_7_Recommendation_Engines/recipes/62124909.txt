Title:
Pineapple Carrot Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz cans crushed pineapple
3/4 cup softened butter
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup shredded carrots
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup raisins
1 1/2 cup flour
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Drain pineapple
Beat sugars and butter until fluffy
Beat in egg and vanilla
Beat in pineapple, carrots nuts and raisins
Combine remaining ingredients, mix into pineapple mixture
Drop by 1 tablespoon onto greased cookie sheet
Flatten top with spoon
Bake 15 - 20 minutes
