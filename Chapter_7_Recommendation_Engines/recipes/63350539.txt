Title:
Hot German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 potato's peeled
6 slices bacaon
1/4 c diced onions
2 T flour
1 T sugar
2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. celery see
1 c. water
1/3 c apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Boil the peeled potatoes in salted water.
Cook until tender but still firm.
Drain and cool.  When able to handle chunck potatoes
While the potatoes are boiling place bacon in a large deep skillet.  Cook bacon until crisp, drain and crumble.  Set aside.
In baclon drippings, saute onions until golden brown.
In a small bowl, mix together the flour, salt, sugar, celery seed and pepper.
Add to the onions and cook for several minutes.
Remove from heat.
Add water, vinegar and cook until bubbly continusly stirring. Add the potatoes.  Add more water if needed then Adjust the taste adding salt, pepper and vinegar as needed.add the crumbled bacon.
Serve with a smile.
