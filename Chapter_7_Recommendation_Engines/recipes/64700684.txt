Title:
Tortilla Crusted Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 thin sliced chicken cutlets
8 tbsp. jarred salsa
8 tbsp. grated Monterey Jack cheese
15 tortilla chips
1/2 c. chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Arrange chicken cutlets on a foil-lined baking sheet. Spread 1 tablespoon jarred salsa on each cutlet.
Sprinkle each with 1 tablespoon grated Monterey Jack cheese. In a bowl, combine crushed tortilla chips and fresh cilantro.
Sprinkle the chip mixture on the chicken and roast at 400 degrees F until cooked through, 10-15 min
