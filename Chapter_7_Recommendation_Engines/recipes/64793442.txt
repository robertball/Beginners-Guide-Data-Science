Title:
Green (Raw) Corn Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups corn
1 egg
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tbl butter, melted
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
pepper to taste
butter for frying

Directions:
Combine corn, egg, sugar, salt , pepper and melted butter. Mix. Sift together flour and baking powder. Add to corn mixture and mix well. Melt a small amount of butter in a pan and drop spoonful amount of batter in pan. brown on both sides. Serve imediately.
