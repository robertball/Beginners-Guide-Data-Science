Title:
Healthy Lower Sodium Mac N' Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces whole wheat elbow macaroni
1 (7 ounce) package shredded reduced-fat medium Cheddar cheese
4 oz reduced fat sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 a boiled pureed sweet potato (approx 6-8 oz)
12 ounces low-fat, low sodium cottage cheese
8 ounces chopped spinach
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1/2 cup whole wheat bread crumbs
2 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
Stir together macaroni, shredded Cheddar cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, sweet potato, spinach and salt and pepper in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Mix together bread crumbs and melted butter in a small bowl; sprinkle topping over macaroni mixture.
Bake until top is golden in the preheated oven, 30 to 35 minutes.
