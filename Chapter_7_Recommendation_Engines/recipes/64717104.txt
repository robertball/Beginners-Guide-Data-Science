Title:
Quick Chicken & White Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken Broth
Cream Cheese
Chicken Breast
White Beans
French Style Green Beans
Green Chiles
Celery
Carrot
Onion
Green Pepper
Garlic Powder
Black Pepper
Adobo
Butter

Directions:
Sautee chopped vegetables in a pat of butter
In large pot melt cream cheese, slowly whisk in broth while melting
Chop sautéed chicken breast and add in
Add sautéed vegetables, remainder of the broth, spices

Submitter: 
trishwa77
Description: 
Throw this together quickly after a long day of work.  Grab a loaf of bread from your bakery and dinner is complete!
