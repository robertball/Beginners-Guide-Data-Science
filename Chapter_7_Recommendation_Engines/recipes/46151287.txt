Title:
Pecan Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7.5 oz tub "I Can't Believe It's Not Butter"
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups sifted flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar and vanilla until fluffy.
Sift flour and salt and add to the creamed mixture, blending thoroughly.
Add chopped pecans and mix well.
Shape into 1 inch balls, place on baking sheet and flatten.
Bake at 325 degrees for 15 minutes.
Do not brown.

Submitter: 
Evelyn
Description: 
Light, buttery, pecan cookie with no cholesterol
