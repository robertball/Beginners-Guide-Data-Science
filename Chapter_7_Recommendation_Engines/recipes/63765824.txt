Title:
Hearty Potato Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 c chopped onion
1/2 c. chopped green pepper
1 tbsp poppy seed
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 pkg Au Gratin Potatoes
1 c hot water
2 c kidney beans, undrained
2 c. stewed tomatoes, undrained
4 oz can mushroom pieces, undrained
Sliced black olives (opt)
Fresh grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown together first five ingredients.
Drain, if necessary.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
Cover and simmer, stirring occasionallyfor 40-45 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and potatoes are tender.
If desired, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and dollops of sour cream just  before serving.

NJHoffman
Description: 
A hearty meal-in-one enjoyed on a frosty fall or winter day served up with a freshly baked crusty home made loaf offf bread and a crisp salad.
