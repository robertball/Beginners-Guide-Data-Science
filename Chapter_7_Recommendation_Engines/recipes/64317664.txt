Title:
Homemade Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2006241.jpg
Ingredients:
300 g Stewing beef, cut up small
2 Large carrots, sliced
3 large celery, sliced
1 ½  red onion, cut into large dice
170 g bag Mann’s snow peas
4 Mini bell peppers assorted colors, cut in half, sliced
225 g fresh mushrooms, sliced or about 1/2 package
3 garlic cloves, chopped
250 ml whole wheat flour, divided in half
15 ml black pepper
15 ml Mrs. Dash original
10 ml oregano leaves
10 ml dried parsley
90 ml Selection Steak Spice Marinade
45 ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil
30 ml Low sodium beef bouillon
2 l water

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a dutch oven or other large pot, add garlic and let brown, place beef in a ziplock bag with 125 ml of the flour, then shake till coated, then add it to the oil stirring until meat is browned.
Add the vegetables, except potatoes and stir fry in pot for about 5 minutes, add the water, seasonings, marinade, and bouillon, then let simmer about 1 hour or so, stirring every 15-20 minutes. Add the potatoes during last 20 - 30 minutes of cooking.
Take the last 125 ml, flour mix with cold water and make a paste, then add to stew 15 ml at a time until desired thickness is attained, then let simmer another 10 minutes,
