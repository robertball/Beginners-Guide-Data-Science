Title:
Thai Sweet Corn Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sweet corn kernel
1 egg
1 tsp finley crushed garlic
2 tsp wheat flour
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt

Directions:
mix all ingredients together
shape the mixture into small patties about 1 1/2 inches in diameter and deep fry in oil until golden and cooked.
place on paper towel until drained and serve immediately with chili sauce or ketchup.

Submitter: 
Abbi Saesong
Description: 
This is a popular thai apetizer or snack. They usually dip it in sweet thai chili sauce.
