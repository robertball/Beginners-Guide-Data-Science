Title:
Popcorn Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup honey
½ cup corn syrup
2/3 cup sugar
½ cup butter
15 cups popped corn

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except corn in a large saucepan. Heat until it cooks to softball stage of 230 degrees on a candy thermometer.
Pour mixture over popcorn. Mix and make into balls.
Use a wooden spoon to mix syrup over corn – this is VERY hot! Dip hands in cold water to shake off excess corn when making the balls.
*Most important thing is to watch the temperature.
