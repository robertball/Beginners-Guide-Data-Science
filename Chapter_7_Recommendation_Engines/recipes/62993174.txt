Title:
Pattie's Christmas Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Rice Chex
2 Cups coconut
2 Cups Mixed Nuts
1 Cup Raisins
1 Cup Karo Syrup
1 1/2 Stick of Butter
1 Cup Sugar
Sprinkle of Salt

Directions:
Mix Rice Chex, coconut,raisins, and nuts together, and set aside.
In a pot mix syrup, butter, sugar, and salt. Heat to boil for 2 minutes.
Then add 1 tsp Vanillala
1/2 tsp Soda
Quickly pour over Chex mix.

Submitter: 
deltasky
Description: 
My wonderful neighbor makes this for treats at Christmas, and so now it has become ours, with a little tweaking.
