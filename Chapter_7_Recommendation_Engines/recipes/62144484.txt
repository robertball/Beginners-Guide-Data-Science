Title:
Aunt Bea's Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups white sugar
2 sticks butter (NOT MARGARINE)
12oz can evaporated milk
2 cups peanut butter
16oz marshmallow creme

Directions:
Mix 4 1/2 cups of sugar, 2 sticks butter, and 12oz can of evaporated milk in a medium heavy sauce pan.
Bring to a boil for 20 minutes.
Don't mistake escaping air bubbles for the boil.
Remove from heat.
Beat in the 2 cups peanut butter and 16oz of marshmallow creme.
Pour into 2 (8") buttered pans or 1 (9"X13") pan.
Cool completely before cutting.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Jo Lomax
Description: 
This recipe takes a little more time, but it is well worth the effort, your family will love you for it!
