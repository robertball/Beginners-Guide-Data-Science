Title:
Big Daddy's (The Works) Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh strawberries
1 banana, sliced
1 cup fresh peaches
1 cup apples
2 scoops of Mila
1 1/2 cups vanilla ice cream
1 1/2 cups ice cubes
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
In a blender combine strawberries, banana, peaches, apples, Mila and ice cream. Blend until smooth. Add ice, pour in milk and blend again until smooth. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
Strawberries, bananas, peaches, and apples blended with vanilla ice cream for a delightful fruit smoothie.Modified from: Bananerberry Smoothie
