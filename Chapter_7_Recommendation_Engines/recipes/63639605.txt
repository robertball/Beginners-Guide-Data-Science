Title:
Vegetarian Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white rice
1 1/2 cups water
1 Tbs olive oil
4 spicy black bean burger patties - thawed & cut into small pieces
1 (15 ounce) can of chili ready diced tomatoes
1 small sweet onion diced
1 green pepper dice
1 4 oz. can of diced green chili's
2 tsp. of cumin
1 tsp. chili powder
white corn tortillas
1 (14.25 ounce) can vegetarian refried beans, divided
2 fresh jalapeno peppers - seeded, sliced, and divided (optional)
1 1/2 cups salsa
2 1/2 cups shredded mexican blend cheese

Directions:
In a saucepan bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
Add diced tomatoes and salsa to rice and mix well.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Saut√© onion and green pepper in olive oil over medium heat until tender.
Add  black bean burger crumbles, green chili's, cumin and chili powder to pan and cook until heated through (about 3-5 minutes)
Lay corn tortilla's in a lightly greased 8x8 inch baking dish. Layer with one half of the beans, jalapeno slices, black bean burger mixture with rice on top.
Sprinkle with 1/3 of the mexican blend cheese.
Repeat layers with remaining ingredients, beginning with the corn tortilla, and top with remaining shredded cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until heated through and cheese is melted. Serve immediately.
