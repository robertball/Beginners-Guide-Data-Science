Title:
Three Loaf Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water
3 cups sugar
1 cup shortening
1 large 24 oz. container of raisins
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. cloves
4 beaten eggs
6 cups flour
1 cup nuts (walnuts or pecans or both)
1 tsp. salt
1 T. baking soda
1 T. vanilla

Directions:
Mix sugar, shortening, water, raisins, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves and boil for 3 minutes.
Completely cool this mixture-I cool it overnight.
Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
Pour into greased bread pans and top with 3 to 4 pecans.
Bake at 325' for 1 to 1 1/4 hour.
Cool, remove from pans and serve or wrap in foil/ziploc freezer bags and save for later.

Submitter: 
Julie Moon
