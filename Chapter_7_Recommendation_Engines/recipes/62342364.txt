Title:
Brownie Sophisticate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/392177.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package brownie mix (14 oz)
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 large egg, beaten
1 cup milk chocolate chips
* * *
1/3 cup sugar
1-1/2 tsp vanilla
1 cup sour cream
pinch of salt
* * *
1 quart fresh strawberries, stems removed, quartered
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix strawberries with 1/2 cup sugar.  Set aside in refrigerator.
Mix brownie mix, water, oil and egg.  Mix well.
Stir chocolate chips into batter.
Spread better into greased 13X9" pan.
Bake at 350 F for about 25 minutes.
While the brownies bake, mix 1/3 cup of sugar, 1-1/2 tsp vanilla, and 1 cup sour cream.  Stir in a pinch of salt.
When brownies are done, remove from oven, spread the sour cream mixture over the top, put it in the oven for 10 minutes at 300 F.
When brownies are cooled, remove sugared strawberries from refrigerator and spread over the top of the sour cream topping.

Submitter: 
TLW
Description: 
Liven up a package of brownie mix with a bit of sophistication- strawberries and sweetened sour cream!
