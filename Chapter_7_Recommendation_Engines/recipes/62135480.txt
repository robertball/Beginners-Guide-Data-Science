Title:
Mother's Perfect Paella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large red onion
5 clove of garlic
1 small green pepper
1 small can of red peppers roasted
parsley fresh and if possible mediterranean style
4 pieces of chicken thighs
small can of peas
small can artichoke hearts
2 tablespoon capers
half lb medium size shrimps
small can of baby clams
1 cup of small grain rice (rinse well to remove starch)
colorante o curcumen (zafran or Sazon seasonsing)
1/2 cup olive oil
1 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken broth
2 medium ripe tomatoes

Directions:
In oil at medium heat, sauted onions, garlic, green pepper and tomato.
After the veggies are tender add chicken and sauted by stirring constantly until chicken is cooked through.
Add the peas, baby clams artichokes, parsley until heathed through.
Add the already rinsed rice, capers, shrimps, red roasted peppers,wine and chiken broth and finally the colorante.
Stir gently, bring to a boil and then cover and simmer until rice is done about 20 minutes more or less. Just taste for salt and for rice to be done.
Que aproveche!!

Submitter: 
Vanessa Moore
Description: 
Get ready for a burst of seafood flavors in this wonderful authentic Spanish recipe. My mother born and raise in Sevilla taught be the Andalusian version of this classic Spanish dish. Try it with your favorite meats, pork, chicken or veal and add in whatever seafoods you like best!
