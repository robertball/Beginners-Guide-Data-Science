Title:
Sweet Potato Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Sweet Potatoes
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Coconut Oil
Salt
Pepper
Cinnamon
Chili Powder
Garlic Powder

Directions:
- Set oven to 350 degrees.
- In a large container with a lid mix EVOO, coconut oil, salt, peper, cinnamon, chili powder & garlic powder.
- Slice sweet potatoes as thin as possible (I use the SliceOMatic) and place in container with seasoned oil mixture.
- Once all potatoes have been sliced, attach lid and shake until all slices have been evenly covered with seasoned oil mixture.
- Place potato slices on a sheet of aluminum foil and put in oven.
- Bake for approximately 50 mins of total baking time flipping slices every 10 mins.
