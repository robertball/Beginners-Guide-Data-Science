Title:
Pete's Poultry Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648753.jpg
Ingredients:
2 gallons water
1 1/2 c kosher salt
3 Tbl minced garlic
1 Tbl ground black pepper
1/4 c Worcestershire Sauce
1/3 c Brown Sugar
2 Tbl Pete's Poultry Seasoning
Olive Oil

Directions:
In a large enough container to hold your bird, mix together all ingredients except for vegetables.   Store in refrigerator based upon size of the bird (1-2 days).  When preparing to smoke the animal, fill the cavity with vegetables and rub with olive oil.  Smoke until bird is complete.

Submitter: 
Memphis B&Q;'
Description: 
This is an excellent brine that works well for chicken or Turkey.  Chicken should be done only for 24 hours while turkey should be done for 48 hours.
