Title:
Joyce's Tomato Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 quarts tomato juice
4 onions, peeled and halved
Equivalent of one bag of shredded carrots
1 package abc pasta
1/4 cup salt
2 1/2 Tbsp. dried sweet basil
1/2 Tbsp. black pepper
1/2 cup sugar
1 lb. butter

Directions:
Put onions and carrots in blender with 1 cup tomato juice.  Puree.  Add to the remaining tomato juice.  Simmer for 15 minutes.   Add sugar and spices.  Simmer 5 more minutes.  Add butter.  After butter melts, pour into bottles and process for 30 minutes

Description: 
This is my favorite soup to bottle from the garden tomatoes!
