Title:
Turkey & Apple Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/20777.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package HORMEL速 NATURAL CHOICE速 Oven Roasted Deli Turkey
4 (10 inch) tomato basil flavored tortillas
1/2 cup whipped cream cheese
1 green-skinned apple, thinly sliced
1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1 cup watercress

Directions:
Spread each flour tortilla with 2 tablespoons cream cheese.  Top with sliced turkey, apples, tomatoes, cranberries and watercress.  Fold edges inward and roll-up.  Slice in half diagonally and serve.

Submitter: 
HORMEL速 NATURAL CHOICE速 Deli Sandwich Meats
Description: 
Sweet, tart, crisp, and creamy, these sumptuous roll-ups have it all! Apples, sun-dried tomatoes, cranberries, and watercress are the perfect combo with turkey and cream cheese.
