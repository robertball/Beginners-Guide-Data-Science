Title:
Hamburger Supreme with Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1/2 cup Onion, chopped
3 tbsp flour
16 oz can tomatos
8 oz corn, canned
8 oz green beans
1 clove garlic
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup flour
1.5 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp dry mustard
2 tbsp shortening
2/3 cup milk
3 oz shreaded cheese (optional)

Directions:
Fry hamburger, garlic and onion till brown and drain fat
Blend flour,salt and pepper
Stir in tomatos and cook slightly till thickened
Drain corn and beans and add to meat mixture
heat through and place in a casserole dish
Dumplings
Sift flour, baking powder and mustard.
Cut in shortening with pastry blender
Blend in milk and half cheese to form soft dough
Drop dough on top of meat mixture
bake uncovered at 350 for 30 minutes
