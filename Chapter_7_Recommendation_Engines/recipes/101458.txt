Title:
Oven-Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978263.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 pound) roasting chicken
2 tablespoons Bertolli ® Classico™ Olive Oil
2 teaspoons Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
1 teaspoon Lawry's® Seasoned Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In 13 x 9-inch roasting pan, with or without rack, place chicken breast-side-up. Rub chicken with Olive Oil, then sprinkle Lawry's(R) Seasoned Salt and Lawry's(R) Seasoned Pepper over outside and inside cavity of chicken. Roast 60 minutes or until meat thermometer inserted in thickest part of the thigh reaches 180 degrees F.  Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Serve it for dinner, and any leftovers can be used in chicken salads, enchiladas, and soups.  What could be more versatile than whole, roasted chicken?
