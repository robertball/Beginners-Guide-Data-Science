Title:
Crock Pot Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken, cut up
3T flour
1/3c orange marmalade
1/3b barbecue sauce
2T soy sauce
1T ginger

Directions:
Toss chicken and flour in crockpot.
Mix other ingredients and pour over.
Cook on low 6-8 hours.
