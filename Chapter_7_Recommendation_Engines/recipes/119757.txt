Title:
Frozen Fruit Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 medium firm bananas, sliced
1 (29 ounce) can peach halves, drained and diced
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (8 ounce) carton frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the bananas, peaches, pie filling and milk; mix well. Fold in whipped topping. Pour into a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. dish. Cover and freeze for 8 hours or overnight. May be frozen for up to 1 month.

Submitter: 
Deb  Palmer
Description:
