Title:
Stay in Bed Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chuck stew meat
1 large onion, chunked
2 potatoes, peeled and diced large
4 carrots, peeled and sliced
10 3/4 oz. can tomato soup
1/2 soup can of water
dash of pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1 bay leaf
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Put all ingredients except peas in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on low 5-6 hours, or until vegetables are as tender as you like them.
Add peas 1/2 hour before serving.
Remove bay leaf before serving.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner

Perfect for those days you just don't feel like cooking too much!
