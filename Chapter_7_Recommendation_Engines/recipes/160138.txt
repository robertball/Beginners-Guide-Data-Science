Title:
Simply Potatoes® Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982874.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Shredded Hash Browns
3 pounds lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups chopped onions
2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1 tablespoon salt
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups ketchup
4 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F.  In large bowl combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup ketchup.  Mix well, using hands if necessary.  Divide mixture evenly into two 8x4- or 9x5-inch loaf pans.  Spread 1/4 cup ketchup on top of each meatloaf.
Bake 70 to 75 minutes or until center of meatloaf reaches 160 degrees F.  Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
