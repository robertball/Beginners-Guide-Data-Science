Title:
Italian-Style Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups instant brown rice
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
1 pkg (16 oz) frozen stir-fry vegetables, thawed
1/2 cup dressing

Directions:
Cook rice. Heat a large nonstick skillet sprayed with cooking spray over medium-high heat. Add chicken; stir-fry for 2 to 3 minutes. Add vegetables and dressing; stir-fry for 3 to 4 minutes or until chicken is cooked and vegetables are crisp-tender. Serve over rice.
