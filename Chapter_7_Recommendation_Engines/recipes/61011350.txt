Title:
Classic Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277611.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 cup favorite jelly or jam

Directions:
Cream together butter, peanut butter and sugars. Beat in eggs.
In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and   salt. Stir into batter. Put batter in refrigerator for 1 hour.
3. Roll into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets. Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F oven for 8 minutes. Remove from oven and carefully spoon about 1/4 teaspoon jelly or jam into the center of each cookie. Return to oven for about 2 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
