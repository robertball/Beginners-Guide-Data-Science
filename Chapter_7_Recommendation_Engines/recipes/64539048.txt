Title:
Protein-Packed Vegetarian Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3141572.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
2 carrots, peeled and diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1/4 onion, minced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 block firm tofu, cubed
Salt and pepper, to taste
3 cups cooked white rice
4 eggs, beaten
1 8oz package spinach, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
Sriracha sauce, to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil over medium-high heat. Cook the carrots, celery, onion, garlic, and tofu over medium-high heat in the oil until soft but not mushy, about 5-10 minutes.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add rice, eggs, and spinach to the vegetables and cook until egg is no longer runny, about 5 minutes.
Add salt, pepper, and sriracha sauce to taste, and enjoy warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
This vegetarian fried rice is delicious and packed with protein; a nutrient that many vegetarians have trouble incorporating into their diets.
