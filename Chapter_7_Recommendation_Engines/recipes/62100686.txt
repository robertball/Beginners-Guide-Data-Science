Title:
Venison Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-pounds venison roast
oil
1 1/2 cup beef broth
1/2 cup dry white or red wine
5 carrots, cut in 2-inch peices
3 celery stalks, cut in 3-inch peices
4 potatoes, quartered then halved
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Mix 1/3 cup flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder and 1 teaspoon parsly. Coat meat with the flour mixture and place in a Dutch oven. Brown it on all sides in a little oil. Add the broth, wine and any remaining flour mixture and stir. Cover and bake the roast for about 1 1/2 hours. Add vegetables and bake for another hour. A delicios meal for the family.
