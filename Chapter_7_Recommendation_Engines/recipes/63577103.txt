Title:
Stacy's Creamy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c cooked chicken, coarsely shredded
2 c sour cream
1 (7oz) can diced green chilies
3 (10 oz) cans cream of chicken soup
3 c grated cheddar cheese
3/4 c. chopped green onions (or sweet onions diced, or powdered onion - to taste)
12 flour tortillas

Directions:
Mix sour cream, chillies and soup to make sauce.
Add half of the sauce, 2 cups of shredded cheese and onions to the chicken.
Mix well.
Spoon chicken mixture into tortillas and place seam side down in greased 9x13" pan.
Cover with remaining sauce and cheese.
Bake at 350*F for 20-30 min.
Serve with salsa if desired.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Easy and good - comfort food.  Works well to make a day ahead and refrigerate for baking the next day.  Sometimes you just must have this and not look at the calorie count!
