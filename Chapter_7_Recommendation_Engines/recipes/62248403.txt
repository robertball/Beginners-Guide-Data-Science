Title:
Chicken Thighs with Honey-Ginger Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup honey
2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp soy sauce
4 tsp grated fresh ginger
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
Dash or two of hot sauce
2-3 tsp vegetable oil
6 boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients in small bowl.
Heat oil in a non stick skillet over medium high heat.
Add chicken and cook for 7 minutes on each side or until brown.
Add sauce, cover, reduce heat and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes or until done.
Remove and spoon sauce over the chicken.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
Serve over steamed rice with vegetables for a healthy, well balanced, meal packed full of flavor.  Perfect for the busy parent!
