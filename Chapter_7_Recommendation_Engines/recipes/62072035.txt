Title:
Mom's One-Egg Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup shortening
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 egg
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons baking powder.

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar together. Add egg and flavoring.  Mix.
In another bowl combine flour, salt, baking powder.
Add alternately with milk to the creamed shortening and sugar.
Creat for strawberry short cake. Bake in well oiled muffin tins for individual cakes.
Pour into well oiled pan.
Bake ate 375 degreees for 35 minutes.
