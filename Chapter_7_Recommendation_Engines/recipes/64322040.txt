Title:
Hearty Tossed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup(s) lettuce, mixed greens
1 large cucumber(s)
1 medium radish(es)
10 medium tomato(es), cherry
1 large pepper(s), red, bell
2/3 cup(s) beans, garbanzo (chickpeas) drained and rinsed
2 teaspoon vinegar, balsamic
1 tablespoon oil, olive
salt to taste
peppercorns, black freshly ground, to taste

Directions:
Place mixed greens in a bowl large enough to toss the salad. Thinly slice cucumber. Peel if desired. Thinly slice radishes. Cut tomatoes in half. Remove top, core, seeds and ribs of bell pepper and cut into dice or julienne. Add chickpeas. Toss ingredients with vinegar, oil, salt, and fresh ground black pepper. Place on a plate and serve.
