Title:
Goat Cheese and Bacon Scrambled Egg Brunch Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8251262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg scrambled
1 6 inch flour tortilla (fajita style)
2 slices cooked turkey bacon
1 oz honey infused goat cheese
1 tbs chopped kalamata olives (about 5)
4 pieces of  baby spinach

Directions:
Heat the tortilla in the microwave for 15 seconds to make it soft and pliable.
Spread goat cheese on tortilla. It may crumble- just spread it across the tortilla evenly.
In the center of the tortilla layer olives, one slice of bacon, eggs, spinach and top with remaining slice of bacon.
Wrap up and enjoy.

Description: 
We love scrambled egg sandwiches and I’m always putting new combos together.  This one’s flavor profile is perfect for brunch. It’s sweet, savory, salty , tart, and fresh.   You can make one or a bunch.  We use turkey bacon but you can sub regular.
