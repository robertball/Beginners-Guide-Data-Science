Title:
Mom's Spiced Eggnog Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 Tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (or to taste)
2 eggs, lightly beaten
2 cups old-fashioned dairy eggnog
1/4 cup cooking oil
1/2 tsp rum extract (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the flour with the baking powder, salt and spices.
In a second bowl, combine the remaining ingredients.
Stir eggnog mixture into flour mixture until slightly lumpy.
Heat a lightly greased griddle over mdeium heat.
For each pancake, pour 1/4 cup batter onto griddle.
Cook until golden; turn when tops re bubbly and edges are slightly dry (1-2 minutes.)
Serve with maple syrup OR with warm appelsauce.
