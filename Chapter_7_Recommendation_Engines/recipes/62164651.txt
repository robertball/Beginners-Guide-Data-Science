Title:
triple decker BLT sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices of bread toasted
2 1/2 tablespoons of mayo
3 slices of cooked bacon,cut in half
2 lettuce leaves
2 slices of tomato
and a dash of pepper

Directions:
lay your three slices of toasted bread out on a cutting board
spread mayo on all three slices
put slices of the bacon on the first piece of bread
top the bacon with the second piece of bread mayo side down
spread mayo on the top of that piece of bread and top that with the lettuce and tomato
put the last piece of bread on top and slice in half to make two triangles

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
this is the classic BLT sandwich but with three slices of toasted bread with lots of bacon,a bit of lettuce,tomato,and mayo...
