Title:
Stinky's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/963838.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
3 hard-boiled eggs
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon lemon pepper
salt to taste (optional)

Directions:
Mash avocado and hard-boiled eggs together in a bowl until evenly combined. Stir in lemon juice and lemon pepper. Season with salt.
