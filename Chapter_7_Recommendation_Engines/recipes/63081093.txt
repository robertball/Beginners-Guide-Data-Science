Title:
Chicken Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778475.jpg
Ingredients:
5 strips of bacon
1 onion diced
1 red bell pepper diced
6 stalks of celery chopped
3-4 chicken breast cut in bite size pieces
2 cans creamed corn
6 potatoes
1 1/2 cups milk
1 tbs cornstarch
4 cups chicken stock
pepper to taste

Directions:
1. fry up bacon to crispy reserve bacon grease
2. Bake 3 potatoes in a potato bag in the microwave or bake as you would in oven take out insides and mash.
3. in big pot add 2-3 tbs of bacon grease, diced onions and peppers, chopped celery and chicken.  Cook over heat about 5 min until chicken is not longer pink and onions are getting translucent.
4.add remaining 3 potatoes (cut into bite sized pieces) mashed potatoes, chicken stock, and creamed corn heat to boiling.
5. mix cornstarch in with the milk and add to soup simmer until bite sized potato pieces are soft.
6. top soup with crumbled bacon and enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Carolyn Showman
Description: 
A warm and hearty soup that will hit the spot with everyone in the family!
