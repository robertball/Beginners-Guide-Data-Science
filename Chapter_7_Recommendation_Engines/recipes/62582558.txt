Title:
Crockpot Cheesy Ham and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 potatoes, sliced
About 2 C.+ ham, diced
8 oz + American or Velveeta Cheese, cubed
half a small onion, diced
1 can of cream of chicken soup (I use the healthy request, reduced fat stuff)
1/2 C. milk

Directions:
Layer potatoes, ham, cheese, and onion in the crockpot. Add the soup and milk on top.
Cover and cook on low for about 6 hours.

Submitter: 
arrion
Description: 
Great for ham leftovers, the house smells wonderful as this dish slow cooks all afternoon.
