Title:
Shauna's Zucchini-Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup apple sauce
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 cups grated zucchini
2 bananas-mushed
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F ( 165 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8x4 inch loaf pans. Or line 24 muffin cups for muffins
In a large bowl, beat eggs until light and frothy. Mix in oil, apple sauce, brown sugar and sugar. Stir in zucchini, bananas and vanilla. Combine flour, cinnamon, soda, baking powder and salt; stir into the egg mixture. Divide batter into prepared pans or muffin cups.
For loaves bake for 45-60, or until done.  For muffins bake 25-30 min.
