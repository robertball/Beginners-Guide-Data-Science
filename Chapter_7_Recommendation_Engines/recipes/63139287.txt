Title:
Pork Adobo (slow cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/802199.jpg
Ingredients:
4 yellow onions, halved and sliced
4 cloves garlic, crushed and divided
1 bay leaf
1 tsp. black pepper
3 lbs. boneless pork shoulder or but, cut into 1-inch cubes
1/2 c. apple cider vinegar
1/2 c. shoyu
1 Tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Spread half of onions in bottom of slow cooker. Add 2 garlic cloves, bay leaf and pepper. Arrange pork in a single layer on top of onions. Top with remaining onions and garlic.
Drizzle vinegar and shoyu over ingredients and sprinkle with sugar. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours.
Discard bay leaf before serving.
