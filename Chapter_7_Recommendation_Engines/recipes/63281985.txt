Title:
No-Egg Cheesy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground hamburger (minced) meat
2/3 cups evaporated milk
1/3 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup ketchup
1 tsp salt
2 tsps Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp black pepper
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
4 carrots, cup into slices
3 big baking potatoes cut into pieces
2 yellow onions, cut into chunks

Directions:
Directions
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a bowl, blend hamburger, evaporated milk, bread crumbs, ketchup, worcestershire sauce, salt & pepper and form into a loaf. Slice off the top of the loaf, form a slight 'ditch' across the top and fill with cheese; cover with removed meat.
Place in the center of a 9"x13" pan.
Place carrots, potatoes and onions around the loaf. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour.
Remove foil and complete last 10 minutes of cooking uncovered to brown

Submitter: 
AMI13
Description: 
This is an old family recipe which uses evaporated milk instead of eggs. Make it low carb by sustituting crushed pork rinds for the bread crumbs.
