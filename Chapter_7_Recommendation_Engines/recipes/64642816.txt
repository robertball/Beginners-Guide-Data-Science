Title:
Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4525681.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag of frozen meatballs or make your own
4 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons flour
2 cups beef broth
1 cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon dijon mustard
salt and pepper to taste (I use white pepper)

Directions:
Bake the meatballs according to directions. Use the butter and flour to make a roux. Add the beef broth, dijon mustard and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer while stirring frequently. Simmer for 10 minutes or until thickened. Add meatballs and simmer for 1-2 more minutes. Serve or egg noodles.
