Title:
Rich Cherry Pear Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
1 large can cherry pie filling
1 15 oz can pears, chop these up
2 sticks butter, melted
1 cup whole or halved pecans

Directions:
Spray bottom of a 9 x 13 baking dish with pam. Drain can of pears, chop them if they are too big, pour pears onto bottom of pan. Pour cherry filling over. Sprinkle cake mix, make sure everything is covered, but do not stir. Pour butter on cake mix, sprinkle pecans on top.
Bake in a 350 oven for about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Kathryn Weidenheimer
Description: 
This is one of my families favorite cakes, so easy to make that my nine year old helps. Its very rich and moist and taste great with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whip cream.
