Title:
Tim's Downhome Andouille Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5263414.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, cut into 1/2 inch dice
1 large onion, cut into 1/2 inch dice
6 links precooked andouille sausage, cut into 1/2 inch dice
1  bag precooked shrimp deveined, tail on.
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 stick butter
Optional: three eggs
We like Cheese, so 8 oz Munster and 8 oz Colby Jack or Montery Jack.
Half an Avocado

Directions:
Begin by cutting dicing the sausage, potatoes, onions, and shrimp into aproximately 1/2 inch sizes.
If you opt for the cheese, also dice that.
Turn a large skillet up to 375F.
Add the Butter, spread it around to coat the whole skillet.
Add the Potatoes, cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add the other ingredients except for, cheese, and egg.
Cook for 8 more minutes. Stirring occasionally.
If you use the eggs add them when the timer gets to 5 minutes, scramble them in.
At one minute left, add the cheeses. Also add the Avocado if desired.
Serve and Enjoy.
