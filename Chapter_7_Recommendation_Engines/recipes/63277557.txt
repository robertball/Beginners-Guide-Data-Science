Title:
Fresh Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 fresh pineapple
3 kiwi fruit
4 apples
1 package of fresh strawberries (washed and cut up)
1 bunch seedless grapes (washed)
1 half bag of bing cherries (washed and pitted)
1 package of fresh blueberries
1 package of fresh raspberries
1 package of fresh black berries

Directions:
Peel and cut up the pineapple, kiwi, and apples and add to bowl.
Add remaining fruit to bowl and mix. Sugar can be added if desired

Submitter: 
Liz Westfaul
Description: 
This is great on a hot summer day!  Just get the fresh fruit and berries your grocery store has on hand and enjoy!
