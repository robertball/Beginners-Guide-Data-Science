Title:
BOCA® Breakfast Biscuits and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 frozen BOCA Meatless Breakfast Patties
1 (2.64 ounce) package country gravy mix
4 English muffins, toasted or baked biscuits, split

Directions:
MICROWAVE patties as directed on package.
MEANWHILE, prepare gravy mix in medium saucepan as directed on package.  Crumble patties into saucepan; mix well.
PLACE 2 muffin halves on each plate; top with generous 1/2 cup of the gravy mixture

Submitter: 
BOCA®
Description: 
Start your day off warm and wonderful with this down-home easy country breakfast.
