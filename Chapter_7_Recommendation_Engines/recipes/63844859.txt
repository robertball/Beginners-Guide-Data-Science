Title:
Sweet Basil and Oregano Bruschetta Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068036.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
1 package McCormick® Gourmet Sweet Basil & Oregano Bruschetta Chicken
2 pounds plum tomatoes, quartered lengthwise
1 pound chicken tenders, or boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch strips
8 ounces angel hair pasta
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
2 cups Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Mix oil and Seasoning Mix in small bowl until well blended.
Reserve 3 tablespoons.
Place tomatoes in single layer on one side of large shallow foil-lined baking pan. Place chicken on other side of pan.
Brush tomatoes then chicken generously with remaining oil mixture.
Bake at 425° for 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and tomatoes are tender.
Meanwhile, cook pasta as directed on package.
Drain well.
Spoon tomatoes into large bowl; coarsely chop.
Add pasta, chicken, pan juices, reserved oil mixture and vinegar; toss to coat. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Roast plum tomatoes to bring out their sweetness. Add chicken tenders to the baking pan and you'll have a flavorful sauce to toss with pasta.Modified from: Sweet Basil and Oregano Bruschetta Chicken
