Title:
Italian Roasted Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound salmon, wild caught
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
2 teaspoons Italian seasonings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees F.
Place salmon skin side down in a baking dish.  Drizzle olive oil then season with salt, pepper and Italian seasonings.
Roast for 12-14 minutes; let rest for a few minutes then enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
I love salmon so when we have it during the week I get a pound for the 2 of us.  Then there is enough for me to add into my eggs in the morning or over a salad for lunch.  

Super easy, super healthy and super yummy!  Enjoy
