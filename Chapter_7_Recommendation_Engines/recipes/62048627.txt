Caribbean-Style Garlic Jerk Wings Recipe

Description: 
These sweet, sticky wings have a decidedly Caribbean flavor. They're the first to go when served as a party appetizer and can even be used as a main dish for a Caribbean menu of Steamed Cabbage, Rice and Peas, Fried Plantains and Cornbread. 

Feel free to spice things up with a 
little Cayenne or Red Pepper flakes....Yah,mon!
