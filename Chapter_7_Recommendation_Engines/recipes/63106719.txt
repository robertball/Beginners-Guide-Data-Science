Title:
Old Fashioned Rhubarb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry for double crust pie
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
dash salt
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
3 to 5 cups rhubarb, cut up
2 TBL butter

Directions:
Prepare rhubarb and cut into 1" pieces. Stir together sugar, flour, salt and vanilla. Add sugar mixture to rhubarb pieces and toss to coat. Let mixture stand 15 minutes.
Fit pastry into 9" pie plate and fill with rhubarb mixture. Dot with butter. Cover with top crust or lattice top, seal and flute edges, cover edges of crust with foil.
Bake in a 375 degree oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 25 minutes more or until golden brown. Serve warm.
