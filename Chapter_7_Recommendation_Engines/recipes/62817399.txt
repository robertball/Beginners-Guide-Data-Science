Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Covered Pretzels Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1 quart half and half
1 1/2 cups crushed chocolate covered pretzels
1 1/2 cups peanut butter
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except pretzels and pour into ice cream freezer.  Freeze until it is of the consistency you prefer.  After it is frozen, mix in the crushed pretzels before serving.
