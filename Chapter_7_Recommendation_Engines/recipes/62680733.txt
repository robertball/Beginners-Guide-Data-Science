Title:
Mom's Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/601043.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
2 tablespoons butter
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 bag frozen green bell pepper
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/2 cup white wine
1 can carrots
2 cups fresh mushrooms, quartered
2 potatoes chopped
3 tablespoons hot sauce
1 can Chicken Broth

Directions:
Put diced tomatoes, raw chicken, onion, garlic, butter, chicken broth, white wine, potatoes, hot sauce, salt &amp; pepper in slow cooker. Cook on low 8 hrs or high 5 hrs. Add Mushrooms carrots, green peppers 30 minutes or so before eating.
