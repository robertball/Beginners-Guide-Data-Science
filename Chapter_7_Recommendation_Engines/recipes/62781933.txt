Title:
Rich and Tasty Pasta (with Philly Cooking Creme) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/728411.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz pappardelle egg pasta
1 small tomato chopped
1 small onion diced
1 clove garlic minced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tbs butter
1/2 cup water
8 oz prepared pizza sauce (about 1/2 a jar)
5 oz (1/2 tub)Philadelphia Cooking Creme- Italian Cheese and Herb
1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
In a medium pot heat olive oil over medium heat; add onion and cook for 2 minutes; add tomato and garlic, stir and cover.  Cook 5 minutes.
Stir in water and butter, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
Add pizza sauce to the pot stir and add in cooking creme.  Stir until smooth, cover for 5 minutes.
Toss with pasta; serve immediately with Parmesan Cheese.
