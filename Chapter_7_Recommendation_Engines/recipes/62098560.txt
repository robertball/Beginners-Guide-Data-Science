Title:
Leftover Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 & 1/2 cups of leftover meat{chicken steak, pork, or even hamburgers}
1 4 oz can diced green chiles
1 15 oz can beans {any kind you have on hand}
1 cup cooked rice
2 cups shredded cheese
1 14 oz can of enchilada sauce
10 tortillas

Directions:
Combine the first four ingrediants in large mixing bowl.
Add 1 cup of the cheese and 2 tablespoons of the enchilada sauce to the mixture and stir.
Spoon mixture into tortillas and roll, then place in large greased baking dish.
Top with the rest of the cheese and enchilada sauce, cover with tin foil.
Bake in 350 degree preheated oven for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Todi
Description: 
I hate to waste food so I have a couple of recipes that I created for leftover meat and this one is a family favorite.
