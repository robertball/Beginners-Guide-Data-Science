Title:
Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce
1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 tablespoons water
3 tablespoons brown sugar
3 tablespoons tomato paste
3 tablespoons rice vinegar
Noodles
14 oz rice noodles
1 medium crown of broccoli
1/3 cup sliced shallots or onion
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup sliced green onions
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
2 teaspoons oil
tofu
Toppings
2 cups thinly sliced cabbage
1 carrot, thinly sliced into matchsticks
1 cup bean sprouts
chopped peanuts
lime wedges

Directions:
mix together all the stuff for the sauce in medium glass
cook noodles according to package directions. rise with cold water
Heat oil over medium heat. Add shallots and stirfry for 2 minutes. Add broccoli and fry 2 minutes. Add garlic.
Gently add the noodles and 1/3 cup of the sauce and toss. Keep stirring and fold in the tofu. Cook for 30 seconds and make sure sauce is absorbed. Keep adding sauce until all gone. Turn off heat and fold in green onions and cilantro.
Serve right away piled high on a plate next to the cabbages, carrots, bean sprouts, and topped with the peanuts
