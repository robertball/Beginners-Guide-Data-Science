Title:
Confetti Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream corn
2/3 c Kellogg's Croutettes (seasoned croutons)
1/2 c milk
2 Tbsp. butter
2 Tbsp. sweet green peppers, minced
2 Tbsp. sweet red peppers, minced
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Saute peppers in butter until soft.  Combine all ingredients in a greased casserole.  Bake uncovered at 350 for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
My grandmother always had this for Thanksgiving and it has been a tradition for over 30 years.  We have it every year.
