Title:
Kid's Favorite Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
3 Tbs. olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 medium onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 3/4 cups dried split peas
2 cans chicken broth
bay leaf
2 oz ham or salt pork
2-3 potatoes, peeled and diced
2 carrots, peeled and sliced
2 teaspoons dried chervil
salt & pepper to taste
In a pressure cooker, saute the garlic, onion, and celery in the olive oil until translucent.  Add the peas, chicke broth, bay leaf and ham.  Cook under pressure for 10-12 minutes.  (If you do not have a pressure cooker, you can cook the peas 3-4 hours or use a slow cooker.)  Once the peas are soft, add the potatoes, carrots and chervil.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  You can add water as needed to adjust the thickness of the soup.
