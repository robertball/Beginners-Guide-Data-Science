Title:
Easy Skillet Pimiento Mac 'n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (16oz) pack of penne pasta
2TBS all purpose flour
1-1/2 cups 1% low-fat milk
1 cup (4oz shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1(4oz) jar diced pimento drained
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
pinch of paprika

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Whisk together flour and 1/4 cup of milk. Add flour mixture into remaining milk, whisking until smooth.
Bring milk mixture to a boil in a large skillet over medium heat; reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer, whisking constantly, 3-5 minutes or until smooth. Stir in cheese and next 4 ingredients until smooth. Stir in pasta, and cook 1 minute until thoroughly heated. Serve immediately.
