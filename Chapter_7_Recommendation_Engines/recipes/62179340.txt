Title:
peanut butter or butterscotch fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 ounce Marshmallow fluff ; 1 jar
1 1/2 cup Sugar
2/3 cup Evaporated milk
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/2 stick Butter
12 ounce peanutbutter chips or Butterscotch chips
1 teaspoon Vanilla

Directions:
in medium sauce pan add marshmallow fluff, sugar, evaporated milk, salt, and butter.
bring to a full boil and boil 5 minutes stiring constantly.
add butterscotch chips and stir untill smooth
pour into lined 8 inch square pan and let sit on counter over night.
