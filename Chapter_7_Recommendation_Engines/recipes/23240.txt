Title:
Honey Dijon Pasta and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package farfalle (bow tie) pasta, uncooked
2 cups LAND O LAKESÂ® Light Sour Cream
1/4 cup Dijon-style mustard
2 tablespoons honey
12 ounces 97% Fat Free cooked ham, cubed
2 tablespoons finely chopped green onions
1 (16 ounce) package package frozen vegetable combination (broccoli, carrots and cauliflower)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.  Drain.
Meanwhile, combine sour cream, mustard and honey in small bowl; mix well.  Set aside.
Place ham and green onions in 12-inch nonstick skillet.  Cook over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until ham is lightly browned (3 to 4 minutes).  Add vegetables.  Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are crisply tender (5 to 7 minutes).
Reduce heat to medium-low.  Add cooked pasta and sour cream mixture; stir gently to coat.  Cook until heated through (1 to 2 minutes).

Land O'Lakes, Inc.
Description: 
Honey adds a touch of sweetness to this quick and easy 20-minute entree.
