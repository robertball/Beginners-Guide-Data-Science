Title:
Karli's Ultimate Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688770.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup walnut halves
1/4 cup pecan halves
1/4 cup cashews
1/4 cup almonds
1/4 cup raw pumpkin seeds
1/2 cup sesame snack sticks
1/2 cup golden raisins
1/2 cup dark raisins
1/2 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Combine the walnuts, pecans, cashews, almonds, pumpkin seeds, sesame sticks, golden raisins, dark raisins, and cranberries in a bowl. Store at room temperature in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Karli
Description: 
A little bit of all your favorite snacks. Granola can also be added for a little extra flavor.
