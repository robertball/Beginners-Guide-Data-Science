Title:
Cherry Chocolate Pumpkin Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
2 cups white sugar
2 cups pumpkin puree
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup dried cherries, coarsely chopped
3/4 cup chocolate chips
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1 additional tsp flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 2 9x5 inch loaf pans (or 4 mini loaf pans).
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking powder and salt.  Set aside.
Combine eggs, sugar, pumpkin and oil in small mixing bowl, beat until just blended. Stir the wet mixture into the dry with a wooden spoon until batter is just moistened.
Sprinkle the cherries, chocolate chips and nuts with 1 tsp flour, tossing to coat.  Fold the cherries, chocolate chips and walnuts into the batter. Spoon the batter into the prepared loaf pans.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes. (If using mini loaf pans, begin checking bread after 40 minutes.)
