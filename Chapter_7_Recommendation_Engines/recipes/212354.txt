Title:
Hot Ham and Swiss Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976238.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded Swiss cheese
1 1/2 cups finely chopped ham
1/2 cup sliced green onions
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
2 tablespoons spicy brown mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons caraway seeds, slightly crushed
56 KEEBLER® TOWN HOUSE® FLIPSIDES® Original Crackers or KEEBLER® TOWN HOUSE® FLIPSIDES® Cheddar Crackers

Directions:
In medium bowl stir together cheese, ham, green onions and flour. Add mayonnaise, mustard and caraway seeds. Mix until combined. Spread in 9-inch pie plate.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F about 25 minutes or until heated through and bubbly around edges.
Serve warm with KEEBLER(R) TOWN HOUSE(R) FLIPSIDES(R) Original Crackers.

Submitter: 
Town House
Description: 
Ham and Swiss cheese aren't just for sandwiches. They make a great hot dip for entertaining family and friends.
