Title:
Barbie Shot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1071038.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice cubes, or as needed
1 fluid ounce coconut-flavored rum (such as Malibu®)
1 fluid ounce vodka (such as Absolut®)
1 fluid ounce cranberry juice
1 fluid ounce orange juice

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour coconut-flavored rum, vodka, cranberry juice, and orange juice over ice; cover and shake. Strain drink into a glass.

This pink drink is delicious! 
You can order it in shot form or drink form. It comes with sugar on the rim. Happy drinking, Lovelies!
