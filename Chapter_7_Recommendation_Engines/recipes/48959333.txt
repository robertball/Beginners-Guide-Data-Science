Ingredients:
3 T flour
1 1/4 cups sugar
1/2 t salt
1 egg, beaten
1 cup sour cream
1 t vanilla extract
1/2 t lemon extract
1 9" unbaked pie shell
3 cups chopped rhubarb*
***CRUMB TOPPING***
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup flour
1 t ground cinnamon
1/4 t salt
1/4 cup soft butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In large bowl, combine
the flour, sugar, and salt. Stir in egg, sour cream, vanilla, and lemon extract. Mix well. Fold in rhubarb. Pour into pie shell and bake 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake 25 more minutes. Meanwhile, prepare topping by combining all ingredients with a fork until crumbly. Sprinkle over pie and bake 15 minutes or until topping is browned. Cool. Serve with whipped topping.
