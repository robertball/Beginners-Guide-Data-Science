Title:
Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup vegetable oil
¼ cup vinegar
3 Tablespoons lemon juice
1 clove garlic, crushed
¼ teaspoon sugar
¼ teaspoon salt
Dash of pepper
6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 pound of fresh spinach torn into bite size pieces
2 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
3 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Combine the first seven ingredients in a jar.  Cover tightly and shake vigorously.
Combine spinach, bacon, eggs and green onions in a serving bowl.  Pour dressing over salad, tossing slightly

Submitter: 
Brooke129
Description: 
Spinach, hard boiled eggs, bacon and vinegar make this salad really pop with flavor.
