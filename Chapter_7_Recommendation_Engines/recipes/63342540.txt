Title:
Nutty Sauteed Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tsp. olive oil
1 garlic clove - minced
1/3 cup fresh breadcrumbs
3/4 lb. Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. fresh ground pepper
1 1/2 tsp. finely chopped walnuts, toasted
1/2 oz. shaved Asiago or Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat 1 tsp. of oil in large non-stick skillet.
Add garlic and cook about 1 minute or until just golden, stirring constantly.
Add breadcrumbs and cook 1 minute or until lightly browned, stirring constantly.
Transfer mixture to a small bowl.
Separate leaves from Brussels sprouts and quarter cores.
Hear remaining 1/2 tsp. oil.
Add sprouts and cook about 8 minutes or just until leaves wilt and cores are crisp-tender, stirring frequently.
Remove from heat and toss with breadcrumbs, salt and pepper.
Top with walnuts and shaved cheese before serving.
