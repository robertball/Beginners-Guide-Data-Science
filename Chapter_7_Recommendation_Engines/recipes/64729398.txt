Title:
Dad's Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Preheat oven to 350* with rack on upper position
5 Tbs Softened Butter
12 Slices of Day Old Bread, dry, not toasted
5 Lg Eggs, Lightly Beaten
3/4 C Granulated Sugar
4 C Whole Milk
1 tsp Nutmeg, Fresh Grated if possible
1 tsp cinnamon
1 Tbs Pure Vanilla Ext
1/2 C Raisins

Directions:
Spread butter on one side of the bread slices
Place butter side down in a buttered 9"x 12"x 2" baking dish adding Raisins between layers of buttered bread
Place 3 qt of water in a pot and bring to a boil.
Beat sugar and eggs together in a medium bowl, add milk, nutmeg, cinnamon and vanilla
Mix lightly and pour over bread slices and allow to stand for 30 minutes
Lightly sprinkle additional granulated sugar and nutmeg
Place baking dish into a larger baking disk and pour in boiling water to come up  1" on the side of the inner baking dish
Bake for 30 minutes until a knife comes out clean after being places in pudding 2" from the edge. After cooling  to room temp sprinkle with Powered Sugar
Enjoy with company

Description: 
My Dad, Leonard S. Sweeney, (LSS) shrunk this recipe from a much larger recipe he used in his restaurant, Lenny's J&M;, in Pleasantville NJ in the 60's, for us to make for our families.
