Title:
Sugar Snap Peas (or Snow Peas) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sugar snap peas, trimmed
1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
1 teaspoon oriental sesame oil

Directions:
Steam sugar snap peas until crisp-tender, about 3 minutes.
Transfer to a bowl, toss with seeds and oil.
Season with salt if desired.
Place on a small pretty serving platter.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This makes a delicious & pretty serving at Easter or for any spring meal.  Use either sugar snap peas or snow peas.
