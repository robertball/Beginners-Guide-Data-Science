Title:
Cheesey Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/459860.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of cooked pasta
2 Cups Pasta Sauce
2 Cups of Shredded Marble Cheese
Sauce:
1 Can of Crushed Tomatoes
1 Can of Diced Tomatoes
1 Chopped onion
5 Sliced Mushrooms
2 Tbsp Minced Garlic
2 Tbsp of Italian Seasoning
1 Tbsp Chili Flakes
1/2 Tsp of Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Sauce: Sautee onions on medium heat in olive oil for about 6 minutes or until clear, add garlic and mushrooms and saute for about 3 minutes.
Add to sauce, with seasonings, cover and simmer for about 20-30 minutes.
Cook pasta for a bout 7 minutes, drain and place in baking dish.
Cover with sauce and mix together so noodles are well coated. Coat evenly with cheese, and then cover with tin foil.
Bake at 385 for 20 minutes, take tinfoil off and bake for another 10 minutes or until cheese is browned and bubbly.
Serve with salad or bread!
