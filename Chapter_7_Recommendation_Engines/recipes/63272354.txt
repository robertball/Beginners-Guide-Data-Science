Title:
Zucchini Spaghetti alla Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/859267.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinara Sauce
1/2 can 8oz of tomato paste
2 medium ripe tomatoes chopped
2 tablespoon of simple syrup
2 tablespoon of garlic
1 tablespoon of olive oil
2 tablespoon of Balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon of sea salt
1/2 cup water , as needed
For Spaghetti
4 medium Zucchini, peeled  and spiralized

Directions:
Combine all sauce ingredients including 1/2 water , in a high speed blender and blend to combine. Add more water if needed but sauce should be thick
In a large bowl, toss the zucchini noodles that you have spiralized and add sauce .
Add cheese of your choice if you like
