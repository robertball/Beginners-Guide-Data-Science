Title:
Hearty Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977699.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package DOLEÂ® Field Greens
1 large large tomato, cut in thin wedges
1/2 cucumber, sliced
1 cup Parmesan cheese-flavored croutons
1/2 cup enoki mushrooms
1/2 cup walnut halves, toasted
8 pitted kalamata olives, cut in half
Bacon, Onion and Mustard Salad Dressing (recipe below)

Directions:
Combine salad, tomato, cucumber, croutons, mushrooms, walnuts and olives in large bowl. Toss with Bacon, Onion and Mustard Salad Dressing, to taste. Refrigerate any remaining dressing.

Submitter:

Description: 
Crisp cucumber, enoki mushrooms, and tomatoes are topped with a rich bacon, onion, and mustard salad dressing in this hearty salad.
