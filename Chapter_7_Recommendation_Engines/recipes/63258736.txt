Title:
Cannoli Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. container Part Skim Ricotta
1/3c powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/3c mini chocolate chips
3 T Cream Cheese
Waffle Bowls - broken up

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients.  Break up Waffle Bowls into chip-size pieces.  Enjoy!
