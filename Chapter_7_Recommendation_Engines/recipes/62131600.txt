Title:
French Bean and Potato Sabzi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. French beans (chopped into half inch pieces)
2 Medium Russet Potatoes (peeled and cubed)
1 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1/2 tablespoon chopped ginger
1/2 teaspoon turmuric powder
1 1/4 teasopoon salt (or to taste)
2 tablespoon oil

Directions:
Heat the oil in a rounded nonstick skillet (such as a wok).  Heat the cumin seeds until slightly browned.  Add the ginger and fry for a few seconds.  Then add the potatoes and green beans.  Sprinkle the turmuric powder and salt over the vegetables and cook until potatoes/green beans are warm to touch but still raw.  Lower heat and cover, stirring occasionally until vegetables are soft.
