Title:
Michele's Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boned/shredded rotisserie chicken (this is my secret)
1 cup sliced carrots
1 cup diced celery
1 diced onion
2 tsp original Mrs Dash (her secret ingredient)
chicken broth to cover plus 2 cups
1 tsp celery salt
1 pound homestyle wide egg noodles (white hearty type)

Directions:
In crockpot or saucepan
mix all ingredients except noodles
bring to boil then simmer until vegies tender
return to boil and add noodles
cook until noodles tender
serve

Submitter: 
Michele Weyland
Description: 
Years ago as a home care nurse a breast cancer patient taught me how to make homemade noodles and told me the secret ingredient for good homemade noodle soup. Well I have three kids now and have had to make my own recipe but the secret ingredient still makes this good soup great.
