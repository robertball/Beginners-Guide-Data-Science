Title:
Fried Potatos! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
bag of potatos
enough oil to cover the bottom of pan
2 swt onions or reg
3 to 4 eggs
cook over medium heat (I use a griddle  to cook them easier to turn)

Directions:
Cut potatos up into slices of half inch thick  (one of those things not to think and not to thin)cut up about 6 or 7 potatos, put them to the side.
slice onion up into thin long slices put those into the oil first then add potatos cook until potatos are brown and crunchy, finally when they are brown and cooked add the eggs crack over top in diff sections of potatos and let them cook. Eat and serve!!
