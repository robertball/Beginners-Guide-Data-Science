Title:
Jim's Chipotle Aioli Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/738859.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg yolk
2 teaspoons Gray Poupon Dijon mustard
1 clove garlic minced
2 tablespoons sherry vinegar
3/4 cup grapeseed oil
1/4 cup Spanish olive oil
1 canned Chipotle pepper minced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk together the yolk,mustard,garlic and vinegar in a bowl. Slowly drizzle in the oils while whisking vigorously. The sauce should have the consistancy of mayonnaise.

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
Delicious dipping sauce for many dishes but particularly mushrooms!
