Title:
Vietnamese Lemongrass Beef and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. thinly sliced flank steak
1/3 cup minced fresh lemongrass
3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
2-3 tablespoons of Maggi soy sauce
1 tablespoon of dry sherry
1 tablespoon of brown sugar
1 bunch of Thai basil leaves
1 bunch of cilantro
Fresh bean sprouts
Cooked Vermicelli noodles
Sweetened fish sauce
Sweetened fish sauce ingredients:
2 cloves finely minced garlic
juice of half a medium lemon
2 thai red chilis, minced
2-3 tablespoons of warm water
2 tablespoon of sugar
1/4 cup of fish sauce

Directions:
Marinate sliced flank steak in lemongrass, soy sauce, garlic, sherry and brown sugar. Toss evenly and let sit for 30 min.
On medium high heat, quickly saute beef until medium rare to well. Arrange vermicelli noodles in a medium bowl for one serving.  Add hot beef on top with fresh sprigs of cilantro, bean sprouts and basil leaves. Add 1-2 tablespoons of sweetened fish sauce over the top and serve.
To make fish sauce:
Add sugar and lemon juice to the warm water and mix until sugar has dissolved.  Add remaining ingredients and stir well until mixed.  Add more fish sauce or sugar based on preference.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a classic Vietnamese dish that is so simple to make. Sauteed beef marinated in lemongrass and garlic and tossed with cold vermicelli noodles and fresh herbs. Great on warm days or makes a quick weeknight meal.
