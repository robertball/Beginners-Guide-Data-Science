Title:
Honey Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 egg
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup liquid honey
1 1/2 cups bran cereal
1 1/2 cups flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Grease 12 large muffin tins
Combine milk and lemon juice-set aside
Blend in oil, honey, and milk
Combine remaining ingredients in other bowl
Stir liquid  mixture into dry ingredients
Fill muffin cups and bake 15-20 minutes
