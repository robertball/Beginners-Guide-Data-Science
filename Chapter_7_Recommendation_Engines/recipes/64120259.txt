Title:
Sprouted Buckwheat Pumpkin Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pumpkin
1/3 cup sprouted buckwheat flour
2 egg whites
1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 - 1 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt
1/2 heaping tsp cinnamon
1/2 heaping tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Combine the pumpkin, sprouted buckwheat flour, vanilla, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and pumpkin pie spice. Then add the egg whites and almond milk.
Cook on a griddle greased with a bit of coconut oil.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
You can also make these your own by adding blueberries, chocolate chips, or crushed walnuts to the batter.
