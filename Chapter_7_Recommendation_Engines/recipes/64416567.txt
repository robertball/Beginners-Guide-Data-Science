Title:
Roasted chicken with lavender & provencal herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp dried lavender flowers
2 cloves of garlic
1/2 tsp salt
6 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp died marjoram
2 tsp chopped rosemary
1 tbsp thyme
1 4 lb chicken
1 tbsp honey
1/2 tsp crousely cracked pepper

Directions:
using a mortar and pestle, lightly crush the lavender flowers, add garlic and sale and work to a paste,  stir in butter, marjoram, rosemary and thyme.  remove half the butter to a small saucepan and set aside
arrange rack to middle position, preheat to 400
pat chicken dry, lift breast skin at cavity and using the other half of the butter mixture, work butter under the skin dividing it as evenly as possible and place on a rack on a roasting pan
roast for 30 minutes, meanwhile, add honey ad pepper to butter in a saucepan, melt over low heat.  brush chicken with butter mixture, continue roasting for an additional 35 - 40 minutes more, basting with butter mixture twice more, until chicken is cooked through and internal temperature reads 165
tent with foil and rest for 10 minutes before carving

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
seriously tasty roast chicken to serve alongside of refreshing summer salad
