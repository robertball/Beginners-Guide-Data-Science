Title:
Cilantro Lime Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup plain Greek yogurt
3-4 TBS cilantro
Juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in blender, mix well.  Chill and serve.
