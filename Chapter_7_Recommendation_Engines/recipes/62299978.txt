Title:
Famous Mini Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364073.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground chuck
1 (8 ouce) can beef broth
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
24 dinner rolls
1 (16 ounce) jar dill pickle slices, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a medium baking sheet with aluminum foil or grease lightly if preferred.
In a mixing bowl mix together ground beef, beef broth & dry onion soup mix.
Press mixture out flat on cooking sheet and prick in several places with a fork.
Cook for about 10 minutes or until beef is evenly brown.
Remove from heat, drain juices and cut into 24 evenly sized pieces using a knife or pizza cutter.
Place cut pieces on the dinner rolls. Garnish with pickle & cheese slices if desired.

Submitter: 
vettdj
Description: 
In this crowd pleaser, ground beef is flavored with onion soup mix and beef broth, then browned in the oven. The mixture is cut and laid onto dinner rolls, and garnished with a pickle to make two dozen miniature burgers.Modified from: Favorite Hamburger Bites
