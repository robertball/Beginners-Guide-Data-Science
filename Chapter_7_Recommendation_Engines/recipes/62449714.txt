Title:
Hearty Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/463928.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Tbsp olive oil
1 sm pinch of lite salt
1 medium onion chopped
3 cloves garlic minced
1 8 oz pkg sliced mushrooms
1 12 oz pkg broccoli slaw
1 1/2 lbs cabbage, coarsely chopped
1 10 oz can dices tomatoes and green Chile
8 oz frozen sliced carrots
1 32 oz carton chicken, beef, or vegetable (vegetarian version) broth
3 1/2 cups boiling water
2 Tbsp Mrs. Dash garlic and herb seasoning

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat, add onions and pinch of salt, saute for about 2 minutes, add garlic. when onions and garlic are soft add mushrooms and cook for five more minutes, add to crock pot.
Add broccoli slaw, cabbage, tomatoes, carrots, pour boiling water over vegetables, add broth and Mrs. Dash. Stir to mix, taste for flavor, cover and cook on high til veggies are tender. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Nancy Childress
Description: 
This easy vegetable soup is easy to make and delicious. I make a big pot in my slow cooker, but it can easily be made on the stove top. (I didn't use much salt in this soup because we are trying to lower our sodium intake.)
