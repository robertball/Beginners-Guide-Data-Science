Title:
Sunshine Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick butter
8 raw pre-made biscuits in a tube(Pillsbury, etc)
1 c. white sugar
cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Remove raw biscuits from tube
Melt butter in microwave in a glass bowl
In a separate bowl, mix the cinnamon and sugar together until as cinnamonny as you like
Immerse each biscuit in the melted butter then immediately roll in the cinnamon sugar mixture.
Place on greased baking sheet and bake according to directions on biscuit tube

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
A sweet twist on your run-of-the-mill store-bought refrigerated biscuit.  Simply dip the pre-made raw biscuits in melted butter and roll in cinnamon-sugar, then bake for a delightful Good Morning meal!
