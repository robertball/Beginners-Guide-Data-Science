Title:
Chicken Tetrozzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 can (10 3/4 oz) cream of mushroom soup
1 can (10 3/4 oz) cream of chicken soup
1 jar (4 1/2 oz) sliced mushrooms, drained (optional)
2 tablespoons chopped pimento (optional)
2 cups or more cut up chicken
4 oz spaghetti, cooked and drained
1 cup sour cream
Grated Parmesan Cheese
Paprika

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt butter over low heat.
Add soups and mushroom.
Stir until well blended.
Remove from the heat.
Add pimento, chicken, spaghetti, and sour cream.
Pour mixture into a 13x9x2 inch baking pan.
Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and paprika.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
