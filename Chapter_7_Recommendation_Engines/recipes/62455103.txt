Title:
Pear and Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lettuce, torn in pieces
1/2 cup glazed walnuts
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup feta cheese
1 Bosc pear, peeled and cut in thin slices
Asian dressing

Directions:
Tear lettuce and place on individual salad plates
Place sliced pear among the four plates
Sprinkle the walnuts and cranberries atop the plates.
Lightly sprinkle the asian dressing on each serving
Top with the feta cheese.
