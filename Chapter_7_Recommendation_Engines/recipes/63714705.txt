Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole Chicken (approx five lbs)
1 tbsp paprika
3 tbsp steak seasoning
salt and pepper

Directions:
Roll up foil into balls.
Place them on bottom of Slow cooker.
Season chicken with the ingredients listed.
Place Chicken on top of foil in slow cooker.
Cook on high for 4.5 hours or until insides are 165 degrees.

Submitter: 
Nichoel Casey
Description: 
This chicken comes out moist and delicious every-time.
