Title:
The Falcon (smoothie) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bananas
8 strawberries
16 blueberries
1/2 cup almond milk
1/2 cup orange juice

Directions:
Prep fruit and place in freezer for at least 30 minutes.
Blend all ingredients.
