Title:
Best Whole Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Pkgs of Yeast
1/2 C Warm Water
2 1/2 C Hot Water
3/4 C Brown Sugar
3 Eggs
1/3 C shortening
4 C Whole Wheat Flour
3-4 C of White Flour

Directions:
Soften yeast in 1/4 C warm water.
In a large bowl mix hot water, sugar, salt, and shortening.
Cool to lukewarm.
Stir in whole wheat flour and beat with a spoon or mixer.
Add yeast and enough remaining flour to begin kneading.
Knead dough until smooth, adding flour as needed, but leave dough slightly moist.
Shape into balls and let rise in greased bowls, covered, for about 1-1/2 hours.
shape into loaves.
Place in greased bread pans.
Let rise until double.
Bake at 350* for 35 minutes.
