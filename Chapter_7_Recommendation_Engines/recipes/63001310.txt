Title:
Gooey Caramel Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts of popped popcorn
1/2 c of Karo syrup
1 1/3 c sugar
1 c margarine
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Boil syrup, sugar, vanilla, and margarine until thick (softball stage). Pour over popcorn. Spread on was papered cookie sheets. Store in ice cream buckets.
