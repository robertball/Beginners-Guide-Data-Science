Title:
Sweet & Zesty Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts.
1 small onion
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup chunky salsa
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp Garlic Powder

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350. Place chicken in a rectangular oven safe dish with sides. Chop onion finely. Combine the onion, brown sugar, ketchup, salsa, and spices in a bowl. Stir together. Pour over chicken. Bake in hot oven uncovered for 50 min.

Submitter: 
JulsCampbell
Description: 
A surprisingly simple yet delicious meal that can easily be expanded to serve more. I like to double the amount of sauce as it goes wonderfully with rice.
