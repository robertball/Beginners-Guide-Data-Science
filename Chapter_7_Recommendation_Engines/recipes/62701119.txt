Title:
Ethiopian Cabbage & Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup niter kibbeh, Ethiopian spiced clarified butter
4 carrots, thinly sliced
1 onion, thinly sliced
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
1/2 head cabbage, shredded
1 large potato
1 can garbanzo beans, drained

Directions:
Heat the butter in a deep skillet over medium heat.
Cook the carrots and onion in the hot butter about 5 minutes.
Stir in the salt, pepper, spices, garlic, ginger and cabbage and cook another 15 to 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, prick the potato and microwave for 8--10 minutes; remove and dice into 1" cubes.
Add the potatoes and garbanzo beans to the skillet.
Reduce heat to medium-low and cook and stir until beans and potatoes are heated through.
