Title:
Hardee's Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. self-rising flour
6 Tbs. shortening
1 c. buttermilk
1 Tbsp. baking pwder

Directions:
Mix flour with baking powder. Work shortening in thoroughly with hands. Add buttermilk and stir. Turn onto floured board and knead a few times. Press dough to about 1/2 inch thickness and cut with biscuit cutter (or drinking glass works just fine). Bake at 375 degrees for about 20 minutes or until browned. Remove from oven and brush with butter.
