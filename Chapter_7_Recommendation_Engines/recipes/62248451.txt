Title:
Andrew's Favorite Grilled Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1/3 cup low salt soy sauce
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tsp fresh minced rosemary
2 green onions sliced
4 boneless center cut (thick cut) pork chops

Directions:
Combine 1st five ingredients and mix well.
Pour into a sealable plastic bag and add pork chops.
Marinate overnight (the longer they marinate the more flavorful they are).
Grill over medium heat for 30 minutes - pork will give slightly when cooked.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
My son's very favorite grilled pork chops.  I serve this with nutty basmati rice and mixed spring greens!  A winner everytime!!!
