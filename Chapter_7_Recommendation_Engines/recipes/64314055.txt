Title:
Chicken Gumbo Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1995059.jpg
Ingredients:
8 C. Chicken Stock
1/2 med Onion, diced
1 Garlic clove
2 lg Carrots, sliced
12 oz Okra, sliced
1/4 C. uncooked Rice
1 boneless Chicken Breast
2 Chicken Thighs
1 1/2 cups Rotini pasta
Tony Chachere's "More Spice" Seasoning to taste
ground Black Pepper to taste
3 Green Onions, sliced

Directions:
Bring the Chicken Stock to a boil and cook the Chicken.  Remove cooked Chicken, cool, and cube. Put the carrots, okra, rice, and cubed chicken into the pot of water.
Saut√© Onions and Garlic in butter and add to pot. Turn heat to low, and cook for three hours.
Add the spiral pasta, and cook for 20 minutes.  Add salt and pepper to  taste.  Serve hot, garnished with green onions.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
Okra is cooked with chicken breast, wild rice and spiral shaped pasta in this stew which is seasoned with hot pepper sauce and garlic.Modified from: Chicken Gumbo Soup
