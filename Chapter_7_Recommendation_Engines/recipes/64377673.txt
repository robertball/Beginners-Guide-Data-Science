Title:
Chinese Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Casserole Ingredients:
1 cup cooked chicken, (I use rotisserie chicken)
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup cooked long grain white rice, (If doubling the recipe, then use 3 cups cooked rice for two 9x13 pans)
3/4 cup light mayonnaise
1 can sliced mushrooms, (reserve juice from can)
1 can water chestnuts, dice  (reserve juice from can)1 cup water OR preferably use the reserved juices  from the 2 cans of reserved juices
1 can bamboo shoots, sliced (julienned)
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon salt
1 can cream of mushroom soup
Topping Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup slivered almonds, toasted
1 and 1/2 cups cornflakes, lightly crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Spray pan with non-stick.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl; place in a 9x13 baking pan.
Meanwhile prepare topping ingredients; combine all topping ingredients in a bowl.  (If you are freezing the casserole don't put topping on until ready to bake)
Top casserole with topping; bake for 35 minutes if casserole is at room temperature.
Removed from oven and let casserole sit for at least 10 minutes same as you would a lasagna to let it setup.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This casserole freezes well.  Double the ingredients to bake one now and freeze one for later.  Or you can make one for yourself and share the second one.  If you double the recipe follow the rice directions by changing it from one cup cooked rice to three cups cooked rice.
