Title:
RaRa's Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 small tub whipped topping
1 box confectioners sugar
1 small jar marshmallo cream
food coloring(optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together until smooth.  Add food coloring if desired and mix well.  Chill 1 hour and serve with your favorite fruit.  Great with strawberries and melon.  Food coloring makes a pretty presentation for showers.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I make this all the time.  It is extremely rich, so it doesn't take much to satisfy your sweet tooth.  Great for a baby/bridal tea.
