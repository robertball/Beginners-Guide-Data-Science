Title:
Christmas Eve Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream  cheese
8 1/2 oz. Drained crushed pineapple
1 t. Lowery's Season Salt
1 T. Finely Chopped Onion
1 T. Green Pepper chopped
1 1/2 t. Brown Sugar

Directions:
Mix well and roll in any kind of chopped nuts.

Submitter: 
Mary Guilds
Description: 
Wonderful easy cheese ball to make.  Won't believe how good it is to make.  We use it for crackers but just experiment on what other things it will go great with.
