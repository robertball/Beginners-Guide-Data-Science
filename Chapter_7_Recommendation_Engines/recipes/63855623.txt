Title:
Lois's Frozen Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Frozen Chicken (legs or thighs)
Worchestershire Sauce
Pepper

Directions:
Take frozen chicken out of wrapper. Remove backside paper (may have to use a butter knife to pry off, but usually comes off easily)
Place frozen chicken (don't break apart) in 9x13 pyrex
pepper with seasoned pepper
Worchestershire sauce; I give it a good covering.
Oven at 350 degrees. Turn in 1 hour. Bake total of two hours.Do not cover.
