Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 cups chocolate chips (dark or semi-sweet)

Directions:
Cream together shortening, sugar, eggs and vanilla. In a separate bowl, blend flour and baking soda. Combine bowls and add chocolate chips.
Place on cookie sheet 1/2" thick, pressed flat. Bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes. Doesn't brown much
