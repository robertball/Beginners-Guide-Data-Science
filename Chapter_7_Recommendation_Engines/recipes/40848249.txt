Title:
New Year Black Eyed Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablesppons olive oil or butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 large yellow onion, diced
2 (32oz) carton chicken broth
1 lb dry black-eyed peas
1 lb ham hocks
1 bay leaf, whole
1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
5 pepperoncini peppers
1/4 tsp ground thyme
1/2 tsp garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-soak peas overnight as per package directions.
In a large stock pot over medium high heat, saute onions and garlic in olive oil until onion becomes translucent.
Add chicken broth and an equivalent amount of water (by re-filling containers) and bring to low simmer as add ingredients.
Stir in ham, peas, tomatoes, peppers, and bay leaf.
Season with thyme, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
Cover the pot and simmer on low heat for around 3 hours, or until the peas are tender, the broth is thickening, and the meat is falling off of the bones.

Submitter:

Description: 
A delicious New Year's Eve recipe to bring you luck in the new year! I sometimes just use the ham bone and some of the surrounding meat from the traditional Christmas  ham that I make each year, but the ham hocks also add a nice, smokey, rustic flavor of their own.
