Title:
Banana Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. rolled oats
1 c. milk
1/4 t. cinnamin
1/8 t. nutmeg
1 t. vanilla extract
2 ripe frozen bananas - peeled and cut into chunks
2 T. crushed walnuts (optional)
4 ice cubes

Directions:
put all ingredients in blender and blend till smooth.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
A creamy thick smoothie that tastes like a banana milkshake. Great for a quick easy breakfast and with no added sugars and wholesome ingredients it's an ideal filling snack for both kids and adults.
