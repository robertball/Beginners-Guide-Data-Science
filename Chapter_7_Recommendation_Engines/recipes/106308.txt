Title:
Spooky Halloween Brew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985801.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (.13 ounce) envelope unsweetened grape soft drink mix
1 (.13 ounce) envelope unsweetened orange soft drink mix
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
3 quarts cold water
2 (12 fluid ounce) bottles Ginger ale sweetened with Splenda

Directions:
To make a frozen hand, wash a disposable glove, fill with water, seal with a rubber band and freeze until hard.
Stir together grape soft drink mix, orange soft drink mix, SPLENDA(R), and water until solids are dissolved.  Combine with chilled ginger ale just before serving.  Dip the frozen hand briefly in warm water, then peel off the glove.  Float the prepared hand in the punch bowl for a ghastly effect.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This spooky Halloween brew has an eerie color from grape and orange soft drink mixes.  The frozen hand adds a terrifying touch.  Children and adults alike will find this punch shockingly delicious!
