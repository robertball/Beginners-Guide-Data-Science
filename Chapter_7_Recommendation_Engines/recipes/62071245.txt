Title:
Thanksgiving-in-a-Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces
1 14 oz. can condensed cream of chicken & mushroom soup
2 14 oz. cans cut green beans, drained
Salt and pepper to taste
1 8 oz. package StoveTop chicken or turkey stuffing
1 small can French fried onions
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
1.	In a 9x13 casserole dish, combine chicken, soup, green beans, salt & pepper, cranberries, and most of the French fried onions.
2.	Prepare stuffing according to the directions on the box.
3.	Sprinkle cheese and the remaining onions lightly over the top of the chicken mixture.
4.	Spoon stuffing over the cheese.
5.	Bake, covered, for 30 minutes at 375Â°.

Submitter: 
Amy Cothran Gray
Description: 
Mingling the holiday tastes of green bean casserole, stuffing, and cranberries, this easy dish has become a family favorite year-round.
