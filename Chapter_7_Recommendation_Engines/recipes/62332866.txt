Title:
French Toast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hotdog buns (pinched into 1" pieces)
4 eggs
1/2 to 1 cup half & half (depends on how you like your french toast)
1 tsp vanilla extract
chopped pecans (optional)
1/4 c brown sugar (more if needed for your taste)

Directions:
place 1" pieces of hotdog buns into round cake pan.  Beat together 4 eggs and half and half and add vanilla extract.  Pour liquid over hotdog bun pieces.  Crumble brown sugar onto top and top off with chopped pecans.  Bake at 375 degrees for 15 mins.

Description: 
This makes a really good breakfast.  Quick to make and very tasty!
