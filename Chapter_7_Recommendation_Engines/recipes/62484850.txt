Title:
Cream of Asparagus Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
6 cups liquid vegetable stock
1 potato, peeled and diced
1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and coarsely chopped
3/4 cup plain philadelphia cream cheese
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil a heavy cooking pot.  Add onions and chopped celery; saute until tender, about 4 minutes.  Stir in flour, mixing well.  Cook for about 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Do not burn, or let it go lumpy.  Add vegetable broth; stir until smooth.  Bring to a boil.  Add diced potatoes and chopped asparagus.  Reduce heat, and simmer for about 20 minutes.
Puree soup in a food processor or blender in batches.  Return to pot.
Stir in phili cream cheese, soy sauce, and black and white pepper.  Bring soup just to boil.  Adjust seasonings to taste.  Serve hot.
