Title:
Honey Caramel Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Honey Caramel Sticky Buns
2 1/2 teaspoons yeast
2 tablespoons warm water
1/3 cup milk
2 tablespoons shortening
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 egg, beaten
2 cups all-purpose flour
Topping:
1 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons butter
1/3 cup chopped nuts
Filling:
1/4 cup butter softened
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
1.	Soften yeast in warm water. Heat milk to scalding. Remove from heat and stir in shortening, sugar and salt. Cool to lukewarm. Stir in softened yeast, egg and flour to make a moderately stiff dough. Knead slightly. Allow dough to rest while preparing topping.
2.	In a small saucepan combine brown sugar, honey and butter. Heat, stirring, until sugar is dissolved, Spread on bottom of a greased 8-inch square pan. Sprinkle with nuts; set aside.
3.	Roll dough into a 10 x 12-inch long about 1/4-inch thick. Dot surface with butter; sprinkle with sugar, cinnamon and raisins. Starting with long side, roll up as for a jelly roll and pinch edge of lengthwise seam together. Cut into 9 equal slices. Place rolls cut side down in prepared pan. Flatten each slice with fingers until barely touching each other. Cover and let rise until light and double in size, about 1 hour.
4.	Bake at 375Â°F for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven and invert pan immediately on a large plate. Let cool.
