Title:
Carmel chocolate icecream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c sugar
1 1/2 c water
6 c cream
2 T vanilla
3 c pecans, toasted
7 oz chocolate

Directions:
Mix sugar with water and cook till dark
add cream and cook 5-7 min
Mix all and fallow icecream maker directions
