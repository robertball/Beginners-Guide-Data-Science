Title:
BBQ Apple Pork with Cheddar on a bun Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764129.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground pork or three cups of shredded leftover pork roast
1 apple, peeled and chopped
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 cup bbq sauce ( I use honey garlic)
Cheddar cheese for melting
Buns warmed in oven

Directions:
Saut√© onion and apple until the onion is starting to brown. If using ground pork then brown it at this time. Add the onions, pork and apple to the crockpot and pour in the 1/2 cup bbq sauce. Cook on low for 2-4 hours
Assemble meat and then cheese on warm buns and broil the cheese until it browns. About three minutes.
