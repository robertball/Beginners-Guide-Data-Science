Title:
Microwave White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 whole heads of garlic, unpeeled
3/4 tsp salt, divided
3 Tbsp olive oil, divided
2 Poblano peppers
1 onion
1 1/2# boneless, skinless chicken
1 (16 oz) jar salsa Verde
2 Tbsp Southwestern Seasoning mix (Pampered Chef)
2 cans (15.5 oz) Great Northern beans, drained

Directions:
1. Cut about 1/4 inch of the top of the garlic heads to expose cloves. Place in bowl. Sprinkle with 1/4 tsp salt and drizzle with 2 Tbsp oil.
2. Cover and microwave on HIGH for 3 minutes or until soft. Set aside to cool.
3. Dice peppers; chop onions. Combine in Deep Covered Baker.
4.Trim and dice chicken and add with seasoning mix, remaining oi and remaining salt to baker, mix well. Microwave, covered,on HIGH 4 minutes, stir to separate chicken, microwave another 4-6 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
5. Gently squeeze cooled garlic from heads into bowl (discard skins) and mash. Add beans and salsa. Transfer to baker and mix well.
6. Microwave, covered, on HIGH 5-7 minutes, or until heated through.
