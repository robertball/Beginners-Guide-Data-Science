Title:
Mom's Johnny Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3 tsp baking powder
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/4 cup shortening

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Pour into an 8X8 pan. Bake at 425 for 20-25 minutes or until set and toothpick comes out clean from the centre of the cake.

Submitter: 
J
Description: 
My mom made this for our family when I was a kid. It was always a breakfast favorite when we poured a little milk and maple syrup over the top.
