Title:
Chia Seed Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chia seeds
Tito's Vodka (or 151 rum -1:2 substitution)

Directions:
Add 3 tablespoons of chia seeds to a freshly cleaned glass
Add 100 mL of 80 proof vodka, or 150 proof rum
Stir to combine, chase with more chia seed cocktail

Submitter: 
Wistopher Walken
Description: 
A healthy and digestively stimulating combination of your favorite super food and alcohol. Best consumed before lunch, but after brunch.
