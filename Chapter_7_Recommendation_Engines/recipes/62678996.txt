Title:
Cordon Bleu Style Chicken Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 large boneless skinless chicken breasts, about 1.5 pounds, cut into 3-4 strips each (or 1.5 pounds chicken tenders, about 12)
6 slices smoked deli ham, cut in half lengthwise
1/2 package Baby Swiss flavor Cheez-its, crushed into coarse crumbs
1 egg
1 tablespoon water
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°F
Spray a large rimmed baking sheet with no-stick spray, or line with no-stick foil.
Place egg &amp; water in a shallow dish, blend with fork until well mixed.
Place cracker crumbs in another shallow dish and stir in pepper.
Wrap each chicken strip with a half piece of ham, secure with toothpick if needed.
Roll in cracker crumbs, then in egg mixture, then again in cracker crumbs.
Place on cookie sheet.
Bake 15-25 minutes, turning halfway through. Chicken should register 170° on instant read thermometer.

Submitter: 
LARANEFF
Description: 
The flavors of Cordon Bleu in a oven-baked, breaded chicken strip.  Easy enough for a weeknight, can be prepped in the time it takes the oven to preheat, make a quick salad or vegetable while the chicken cooks for an easy meal.
