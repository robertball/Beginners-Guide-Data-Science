Title:
Big Ray's Comeback Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3597053.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp granulated garlic
1 tsp granulated onion
1/4 cup chili sauce
3/4 tsp Sriracha
1/4 cup ketchup
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp creole seasoning blend
1 tsp pepper
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Blend all ingredients well.  Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Comeback Sauce is a southern-style sauce that is so versatile, you can use it as a condiment, sauce or salad dressing.
