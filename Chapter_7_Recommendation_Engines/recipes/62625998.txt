Title:
Banana Walnut "Brownies" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/574639.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup melted butter
1 cup packed light brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/4 cup mashed banana
1 3/4 cups flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x13 baking dish or spray with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine butter and sugar, mixing well. Mix in beaten eggs. Stir in mashed banana.
In a medium bowl, combine flour and salt. Mix well.
Add dry ingredients to wet, stirring until just combined. Stir in walnuts.
Bake in preheated oven 35-40 minutes, until edges are lightly brown and center is set, (it may look moist, but is not sticky when touched.)
Cool completely.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
This is not your mamma's banana cake. This is denser, more brownie-like bar.
