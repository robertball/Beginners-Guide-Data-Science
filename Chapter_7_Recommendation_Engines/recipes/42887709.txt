Title:
Wassil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922200.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 Cup Sugar
1 Quart Water
6 Whole Cloves
4 Sticks Cimmamon
4 Whole Allspice
3 Cups Orange Juice
2 Cups Lemonade
2 Quarts Apple Cider

Directions:
Boil Sugar and water for 10 min.
Add Spices and Cover.
Let Stand for 1 hour.
Add the rest of the ingredients.
Heat and serve.
