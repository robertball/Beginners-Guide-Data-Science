Title:
Norweigen Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 tbsp white sugar
3 tbsp melted shortening
1 3/4 cups milk
1 1/2 cups flour
3 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Heat waffle iron. Beat together eggs and sugar until fluffy. Add melted shortening and milk mix with an electric mixer until well blended. Add salt. In another bowl mix together flour and baking soda. Add flower mixture to the egg mixture. Add vanila. Mix well. Pour about 2/3 cup at a time into waffle iron. Cook until brown. Repeat until there is no more batter left.
