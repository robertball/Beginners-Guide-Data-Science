Title:
Garlic Herb Marinade for Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. Soy Sauce
1/4 c. Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 c. Vegetable Oil
1 tsp. Dried Thyme
1 tsp. Dried Marjoram
1 tsp. Rubbed Sage
1 tsp. Ground Ginger
2 garlic cloves - minced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a plastic zip lock back. Marinate meat overnight turning once. Discard left over marinade and grill meat to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
Jessica53214
Description: 
I created this recipe by combining what I like from other receipes. It gives the meat a wonderful flavor and makes it tender. It also works well on cheaper cuts of meat like skirt steak and flank steak. It's so flavorful!
