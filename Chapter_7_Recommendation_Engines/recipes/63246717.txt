Title:
Apple Butter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 1/4 cups apple butter
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar in a mixing bowl. Add the egg and apple butter; mix well. Combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, nutmeg and cloves; slowly add to the creamed mixture until combined. Fold in raisins and nuts. Pour into a greased 8-in. x 4-in. x 2-in. loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 40 - 50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Bruce Stone
Description: 
The wonderful taste of apple butter in a very easy to make bread. Your family will love it!
