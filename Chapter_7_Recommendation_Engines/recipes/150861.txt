Title:
Bourbon Apple Manhattan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979432.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 fluid ounce Bulleit Bourbon™
1/2 fluid ounce apple schnapps
1/2 fluid ounce sweet vermouth
1 pinch ground nutmeg

Directions:
Pour Bulleit Bourbon(TM), apple schnapps, and sweet vermouth into a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
Shake with ice and strain into a martini glass.
Add a sprinkle of nutmeg.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The hints of vanilla and honey, and long, smoky finish of Bulleit Bourbon™ Kentucky straight bourbon whiskey make it ideal for classic cocktails.
