Title:
Southern Macaroni and Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups macaroni noodles (cooked)
1 stick of butter
24 oz evaporated milk
1 tbsp black pepper
3 eggs
1 tsp salt
4 cups cheddar cheese (shredded)
Paprika (optional)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees farenheit. Cook two cups of macaroni noodles, drain and set aside.
Slowly melt 1 stick of butter in a large sauce pot over low heat. Add evaporated milk and gently stir until butter is fully melted. Add 1 Tbsp black pepper to the mixture.
Temper 3 eggs and add them to the mixture. Then add 1 tsp salt and the macaroni to the mixture and combine.
Lightly grease a 9x13 casserole dish with cooking spray.
Pour a thin layer of macaroni mixture intot he casserole dish and then top with some shredded cheese. Continue creating layers of macaroni mixture and cheese until you've used all of the mixture and cheese and then top with paprika to taste.
Bake at 425 degrees farenheit for 20 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
