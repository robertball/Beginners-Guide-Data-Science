Title:
Refried Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Vegetarian Refried Beans
3 - 4 Tbs Canned Evaporated Milk
1/2 C Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Heat beans on medium heat, add in canned milk and cheese and cook until all is blended together smoothly.  Stir frequently!

Submitter: 
Dragonfly
Description: 
Vegetarian refried beans from a can get some flavor from canned milk and cheddar cheese.
