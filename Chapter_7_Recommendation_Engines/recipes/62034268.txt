Title:
Tangy Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
1/4 tsp pepper
1 boneless beef chuck roast (2-3 pounds)
2 tbsp. cooking oil
1 cup water
1 can (14-1/2 oz)tomatoes with liquid, chopped
1 envelope dry onion soup mix
2 bay leaves
GRAVY:
1/4 cup cold water
3 tbsp water

Directions:
Combine flour and pepper.  Dredge pot roast.  In a dutch oven, brown roast in oil.  Drain.  Add 1 cup water, tomatoes, onion soup mix, and bay leaves.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover tightly and simmer 2-3 hours or until roast is tender.  Remove to a warm platter.  Discard bay leaves.  For gravy, stir together water and flour.  Add to the cooking liquid; cook and stir until thickened.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
Tender pot roast with tangy chopped tomatoes and onion soup!
