Title:
Baked Eggplant Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051380.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggplants, sliced lengthwise into 1/4 inch slices
2 carrots, julienned
1 red bell pepper,julienned
1 tbsp. olive oil
Sea salt, to taste
Cilantro leaves
For Lemon Dressing:
2  3 tbsp. olive oil
½ fresh lemon juice
1 clove garlic, minced
½ tsp. black pepper
Sea salt, to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 450°F. In a baking dish, arrange your eggplant slices. Salt and drizzle with some olive oil. Bake for 5 minutes, then turn them over and bake for another 10 minutes. Let eggplant cool and slice crosswise into 1/8 inch slices.
Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice vinegar, pepper and salt.
Toss the baked eggplants, julienned carrots and julienned bell pepper with the lemon dressing.
Finish with a torn cilantro leaves.
