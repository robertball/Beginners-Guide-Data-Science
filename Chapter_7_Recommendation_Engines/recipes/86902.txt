Un Monstruo! Recipe

1 (8 ounce) container vanilla low fat yogurt

Directions:
Plop the peanut butter onto a plate. Cover it with vanilla yogurt. Place two chocolate chips onto the blob for 'eyes'. Stick pretzels into the blob for arms, legs or antennae. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar over the top.

Muy delicioso!  Very good if you want a quick and sweet snack.  And there is no need to use the oven, microwave, or stove! If you want, use whipped cream in place of the yogurt.
