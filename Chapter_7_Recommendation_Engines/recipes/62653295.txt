Title:
Reuben Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound sliced corned beef
1/2 pound sliced swiss cheese
1 16 ounce can saurkraut
1 loaf pumpernickle rye bread
butter for bread slices

Directions:
Place ingredients in order on 1 slice of bread.  Top with other slice of bread.  Butter both sides of the sandwich and fry in a pan until cheese melts and bread is browned.  Serve with potato chips.

Jacqueline Allison
Description: 
A hearty sandwich that is easy to prepare.
