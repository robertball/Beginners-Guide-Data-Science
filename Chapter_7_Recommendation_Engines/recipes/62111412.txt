Title:
mexican slow cooked beef (version of barbacoa) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 4 pound chuck roast, (tri tip works well also)most fat trimmed off.
2 garlic cloves cut in half
1 jalepeno pepper seeded and sliced
1 1/2 tsp of salt
1 tsp of pepper
1/4 cup of chile powder/ should be enough to cover all meat.
1 1/2 cups of water

Directions:
Cut meat into big cubes. About 6 pieces total.  Place in slow cooker. Season well with above seasonings.  Add garlic and jalapeno. Add water. Cover and cook on high for 5 hours. Shred meat well with two forks, and at this point if it needs more water add some. It should be moist, but not like soup. Check seasonings and adjust to your liking. Cover and cook for another hour or so until very tender. Enjoy

Description: 
I make this at least once a month for our family who just absolutley loves it. Its great served with rice and beans, or in quesadillas, scrambled eggs, and even tacos..Enjoy.
