Title:
Better than sex cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
German choclate cake mix
Eagle, condencened milk
1 jar, Mrs. Richardsons carmel-butterscotch (ice cream topping)
2 health bars

Directions:
Prepare cake as box directs, cool 15 minutes
W/ end of wooden spoon poke holes all over cake
Pour condencened milk over cake and into holes
pour icecream topping on top of cake
refridge overnight
top w/ coolwhip and sprinkle crushed health bar ontop

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
This is an AMAZING german choclate cake that my mom gave me the recipe for.
