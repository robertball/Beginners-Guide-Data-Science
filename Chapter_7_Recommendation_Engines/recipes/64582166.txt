Title:
Matcha-Berry Swirl Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3661993.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh spinach
1 cup frozen peaches
1 cup frozen pineapple
1 cup orange juice
1 frozen banana halved
2 teaspoons ceremonial or premium matcha powder
1 cup frozen raspberries
1 cup frozen strawberries
1 cup orange juice
1 frozen banana halved

Directions:
Blend the first six ingredients to make a green smoothie, and pour smoothie into a separate glass.
Rinse out the blender.
Blend the last 4 ingredients (raspberries, strawberries, orange juice, 1 banana) to make a berry smoothie
Pour the berry smoothies into a separate glass.
Get out four serving glasses, and pour in both smoothies at the same time from opposite sides.
