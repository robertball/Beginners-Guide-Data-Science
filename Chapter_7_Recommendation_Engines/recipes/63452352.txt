Title:
Macadamia Nut Brittle with Blueberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/938346.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Turbinado, or sugar
1/2 Cup Corn syrup, or homemade simple syrup
1/2 Cup Macadamia nuts, salted, chopped in half
1/2 Cup Pecans, chopped
1 teaspoon Coconut oil, or butter
1/2 teaspoon ground Cinnamon
Pinch ground Nutmeg
1 teaspoon Baking soda
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1/2 Cup dried Blueberries

Directions:
You will need a candy thermometer and all your ingredients measured and ready to be added quickly.
Butter a 15 X 10 -inch pan. Set aside.
Add sugar and corn syrup (or simple syrup) to a saucepan.  Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Just before the temperature reads 300 degrees (275)F, add the nuts, coconut oil or butter, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Stir, and just as the temperature reaches 300, remove from the heat.
Quickly add the baking soda, vanilla, and blueberries.  Stir to combine.
Immediately pour all of the candy onto the greased pan.  Spread to desired thickness.  Cool completely before breaking into pieces.
