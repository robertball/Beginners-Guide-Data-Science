Title:
Rich and Cheesy Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One (1) small box Velveta Cheese product -- sliced into 1/4 inch sections
One (1) 15 oz. jar Cheese Whiz
Two (2) cans Cream of Mushroom soup
Two (2) cans evaporated milk
Two (2) small Broccoli crowns

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except broccoli in a slow cooker and whisk until smooth. Set cooker on low and let heat for 2 hours, or until cheese, soup and milk mixture is a uniform consistency.
Carefully cut broccoli crowns into bite-sized pieces, including the smaller and more tender stems if desired. Place cut broccoli into a steamer bag and microwave according to bag directions. Once broccoli is cooked and tender, add to soup mixture stirring well.
Good served with a nice warm crusty bread for dipping.
