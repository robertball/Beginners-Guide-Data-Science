Title:
hungarian goulash II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb beef stew meat cut into cubes
1 small yellow onion chopped
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1 1/2 tsp paprika
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp salt
3 tbsp worchestershire sauce
3/4 tsp white or cider vinegar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 1/2 cup water
a bag of egg noodles

Directions:
brown meat in a medium to large pot that has been sprayed with cooking spray
add onion to same pot as meat saute for about 5 minutes then add all other ingredients stir and then cover and let simmer over low heat for about 2 1/2 hours
serve over the cooked egg noodles that have been prepared per their package directions
