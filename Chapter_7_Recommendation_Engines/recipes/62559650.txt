Title:
Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535515.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Lean ground turkey
4 bell peppers- cut in half
10 ounce package sliced mushrooms
2 cups tomato sauce
1 cup diced onions
3/4 cup brown rice-Uncle Ben’s instant brown rice
¾ cup egg beaters
Salt/pepper

Directions:
Chop up approximately 7 ounces of the sliced mushrooms, add to large bowl- reserve remaining sliced mushrooms to add to pan. In same bowl add turkey, rice, onions, egg beaters, salt/pepper. Mix until well blended and make 8 meatballs to add to each ½ pepper. Place peppers in a baking dish, cover with tomato sauce and add remaining mushrooms to pan. Bake covered for one hour at 400 degrees.
