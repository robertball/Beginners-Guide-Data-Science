Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:
1 c. milk
1 egg
3 tsp. baking powder
1 T. sugar
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 c. melted shortening
1 c. flour

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. together, starting with milk. Add flour last.
Beat with rotary beater.
