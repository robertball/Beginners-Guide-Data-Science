Title:
Sausage Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pork sausage
1 lb. sour cream (regular; not lite or fat free)
1 lb. pepper cheese

Directions:
Cook sausage and drain grease.  Add sour cream and pepper cheese to sausage and cook over medium heat until cheese is melted.  Place dip in a crockpot on low and serve with tortilla chips.
