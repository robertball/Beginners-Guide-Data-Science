Title:
Hot and sweet cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I package cornbread mix
I egg
1/3 cup milk
1 cup fresh pineapple chopped in 1/2 inch chunks (approx.)
I roasted jalapeno pepper diced.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Grease 8" x 8" baking dish.  In medium mixing bowl combine cornbread mix, egg and milk.  Stir just until combined.  Add chopped pineapple and diced jalapeno.  Pour into prepared pan.  Bake in 375 degree oven for about 20 minutes or until golden brown on top.

Submitter: 
Avcooker
Description: 
I had extra fresh pineapple on hand so I came up with this version of cornbread.  I used a box of prepared cornbread mix and kicked it up with roasted jalapeno's.  Sounds strange but it is really delicious.
