Title:
Triple Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz unsweetened chocolate
3 C choc. chips
7 T butter
2 t instant coffee
2 t vanilla
1/2 C flour
1/2 t salt
1/2 t baking powder
3 eggs
1 C sugar

Directions:
In microwave or on stove, melt unsweetened choc., 1 1/2 C chips and butter until smooth. Set aside.
Mix coffee with the vanilla until well combined. Set aside.
Combine flour, salt and baking powder.
Beat eggs with sugar for 4 min. Add coffee mixture, beat, then add choc. mixture.
Fold in flour; mix well.
Add remaining 1 1/2 C choc. chips.
Batter will be soft. Let sit at room temp for 30 min. Will stiffen up.
Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
Bake 350 degrees for 11-16 min. Bake 5 min, then switch sheets in oven. Cookies will look underbaked. Let sit on baking sheet until fully cooked-about 30 min.
