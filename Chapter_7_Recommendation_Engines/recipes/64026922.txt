Title:
Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbs cold butter, divided
2 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup snipped fresh chives
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 tbs vegetable shortening
1 cup buttermilk

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 450. Melt 2 tbs butter set aside. Combine flour, chives, baking powder, salt, and baking soda in large mixing bowl; mix well. Cut remaining butter and shortening into flour mixture until resembles coarse crumbs. Make a well in center and pour in buttermilk. Mix just until dough comes together.
2. Turn out dough onto lightly floured surface; sprinkle with flour and gently fold dough over onto itself 5-6 times.  Pat into a 1/2 inch thick round. Cut out biscuits with biscuit cutter. Arrange on cookie sheet. Brush with melted butter. Bake 10-12 munuts
