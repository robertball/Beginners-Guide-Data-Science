Title:
Easy Fancy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package boneless skinless chicken breast (thawed)
1 cup water
1-1/4 cups Dry Vermouth
Olive Oil
Garlic Salt
Fajita Seasoning
Chicken Seasoning
Oregano
Italian Style Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Trim any excess off chicken and rinse.
In large rectangular baking dish add water.
Put in chicken breast.
Put in Dry Vermouth.
Drizzle chicken with olive oil.
Sprinkle on Fajita seasoning, garlic salt, chicken seasoning and oregano making sure there is enough to cover chicken and season the water/vermouth.
Coat each chicken breast with Italian Bread Crumbs.
Bake at 375* for about 30 mins, or until chicken is cooked through.
You can use the leftover juices to spoon over each breast.
Great with a side of pasta and green peas or broccoli!

Submitter: 
olivia13
Description: 
This is a quick easy chicken dish I make at home about 2 to 3 times a week. It becomes a favorite to everyone who has tried it!
You can make just one for yourself or as many as needed.
