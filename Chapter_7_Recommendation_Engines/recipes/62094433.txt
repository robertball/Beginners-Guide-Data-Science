Title:
Calabacitas (little squash) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Mexican gray squash diced in 1/2" pieces
1/2 large onion, diced in large chunks
small red bell pepper, diced in large chunks
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/4 cup frozen corn
grated monterrey jack cheese (optional)
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup water or chicken broth

Directions:
Heat oil in 2 qt. saucepan.
Add onion, saute over low heat until almost translucent.
Add bell pepper and heat thoroughly.
Add diced squash and stir until squash, onion, and bell pepper are mixed.
Let squash heat for about 1 minute, then add 1/4 cup tap water.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add corn and stir.
Cover and cook over med-low heat for about 5 minutes.  Squash should be tender but not mushy.
Remove from heat.  Add cheese if desired.  Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
KGNEP
Description: 
Mexican gray squash lightly sauteed with onion and other vegetables.  Use what you have in your refrigerator to make this dish more interesting!
