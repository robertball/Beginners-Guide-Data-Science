Title:
Baked Ziti with Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups ziti pasta
1 c zucchini, diced
1 c summer squash, diced
1 c red pepper, diced
1 c carrot, diced
1 c  1% low-fat cottage cheese
2 c marinara sauce, low sodium
1/2 c part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded
4 T Parmesan Cheese, grated
Vegetable cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Place vegetables in a single layer on 2 baking sheets sprayed with cooking oil.
Roast for 15-20 minutes, stirring once.
Cook ziti until done and drain.
Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees.
When pasta and veggies are cooked, fill 4 individual casserole dishes with 1 c of the ziti.
Top each casserole with 3/4 c of the roasted veggies.  then layer 1/4 c cottage cheese, and 1/2 c marinara sauce.
Sprinkle 2 T mozzarella cheese and 1 T Parmesan cheese on the top of each casserole.
Bake casseroles at 350 degrees for 20-30 minutes until heated thoroughly and cheese is melted.
