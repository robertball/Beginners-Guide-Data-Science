Title:
Sweet and Savory Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 mini sweet rolls (like King's Hawaiian) split
1 lb lean ground beef
1 egg
1/4 cup panko bread crumbs
1 small onion chopped
2 large cloves garlic chopped
1/4 cup shaved parmesan cheese
freash ground black pepper to taste
6 slices muentster cheese optional

Directions:
Mix beef, egg, onion, garlic in a medium bowl.  Fold in bread crumbs and cheese.
Heat a large electric skillet to 300 degrees.
Roll meat mixture into 12 balls and flatten out slightly.
Place on hot skillet.  After 4 minutes flip burgers and turn down heat to 200- add skillet cover and cook an additional 4 minutes.
Once cooked through turn off skillet.
Fold cheese in half and in half again.  Place two little squares of cheese on each burger replace skillet top until cheese is melted.
Add to sweet rolls.
