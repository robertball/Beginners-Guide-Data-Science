Title:
Mom's Date Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 c. sugar
8 oz. dates, chopped
1 egg, well beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. chopped nuts
2 1/2 - 2 3/4 c. rice krispies
powdered sugar

Directions:
Melt butter in frying pan, remove from heat.
Add sugar and dates, mix well.
Add egg, stirring fast so it won't scramble.
Return to heat, cooking 6 min on low, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat.
Add vanilla, nuts, and rice krispies; stir well.
Let cool until it is safe to touch.
Butter hands and roll mix into balls.
Roll balls in powdered sugar.
