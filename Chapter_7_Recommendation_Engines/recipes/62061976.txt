Title:
Chinese Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 c. soy sauce
1/2 c. white sugar OR heaping cup of brown sugar
1/4 c. vinegar
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 1/2 tsp. fresh ginger OR 1/2 tsp. powdered ginger
1 Tbsp. cornstarch
1 Tbsp. cold water
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Mix all of the above ingredients in a small mixing bowl.  Pour into a square baking dish and marinate chicken in it for one hour, covered in the refrigerator.
Bake in sauce at 325 degrees until juices run clear, turning midway through.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
A sweet marinade that is just so easy to make!  I use this recipe whenever we bring dinner to another couple's house... I marinade it at home then just cook it there.  It's great with white rice.
