Title:
Super Simple Spicy Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131249.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken breast strips
1/4 C olive oil
1/4 C sirracha
2 tsp ground black pepper
1 1/2 tsp seasoned salt

Directions:
Place chicken breast tenders in resealable bag and add remaining ingredients, toss to coat thoroughly.  Cook on outdoor grill until internal temperature of 165 degrees F.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
This chicken is delicious and always turns out perfectly- marinate it if you want to get a head start or don't, it is always wonderful!  I love serving it in a wrap with coleslaw- delicious!
