Title:
Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/559858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
1 1/4 cups beef broth
5 1/2 pounds pot roast

Directions:
In a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix and beef broth. Flour the pot roast and fry in a small amount of oil, until brown on all sides. Then place meat in slow cooker and coat with soup mixture.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
