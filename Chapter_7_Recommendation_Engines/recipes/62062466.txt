Title:
Luscious, moist chocolate bundt cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups flour
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup cocoa
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 1/4 cup 1% milk
1 cup light brown sugar
2 eggs
1/4 vegetable oil
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup strong, hot black coffee

Directions:
1. Preheat over 350 degrees.
2. Grease and flour bundt pan.
3. Mix first 6 ingredients
4. Add all but coffee. Beat medium for about 2 minutes
5. Whisk in coffee (batter will be thin).
6. Bake 35-40 minutes (till toothpick comes out clean)
7. Let cool 15 min before removing from pan.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an ultra-moist, springy and flavorful cake recipe that does not need any frosting to be delicious!
