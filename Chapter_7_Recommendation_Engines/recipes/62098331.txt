Title:
Macadamia Nut Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
salt and pepper to taste
1/3 cup white flour
1/2 cup of coconut milk
1/2 cup crushed macadamia nuts
Optional- you can add 1/3 cup shredded coconut to the nuts for more coconut flavor.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Lightly grease a baking sheet or 9x19" pan
Put each ingredient in a seperate bowl except the chicken.
Take the chicken breasts and sprinkle with salt and pepper , dredge in the flour.
Dip the dredged chicken breast into the coconut milk and then into the crushed macadamia nuts. (Make sure that each breast is well coated.
Place on greased baking sheet.
Repeat with the remaining breasts.
Place in preheated oven and bake for 30 minutes or until juices run clear.
Remove from oven and serve with ORANGE LIME SAUCE
1 cup orange marmalade
2 tablespoons dijon  mustard or any spicy mustard
1/2 teaspoon grated lime zest
2 tablespoons lime juice
Combine all sauce ingredients in a sauce pan and warm over low heat until marmalade is melted.

Submitter: 
MARGARET N
Description: 
This quick, easy chicken recipe will take you to the Hawaiian Islands for dinner. It's a favorite of my kids and their friends! The coating is also great on shrimp.
