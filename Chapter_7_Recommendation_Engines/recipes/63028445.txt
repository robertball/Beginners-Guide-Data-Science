Title:
Popeye's Cajun Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886764.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1/2 cup bell pepper, finely diced
1/3 cup green onions, diced
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon celery flakes
1 teaspoon creole seasoning
1/4 teaspoon red pepper (cayenne)
4 cups long grain rice, cooked and drained
1/4 cup water (more as needed)
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In fry pan,  stir in ground beef and bell pepper.
Cook over medium high heat until beef loses its pink color and bell pepper is soft.
Remove excess grease.
Turn temperature down to medium or medium low.
Add remaining ingredients; stir and cook together until ground beef is completely cooked and liquid is gone.  (about 25-35 mins.)
More creoloe seasoning and red pepper may be added for taste.
