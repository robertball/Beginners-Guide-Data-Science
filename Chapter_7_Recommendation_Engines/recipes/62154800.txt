Title:
Guilt-Free Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast, diced
1/2 cup black bean
1 cup fresh spinach
2 tbsp creole dijon sauce, divided
1 tsp water
1/4 cup unsalted cashews
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
3 tbsp low-fat chicken broth
1/4 diced red bell pepper
1/4 cup red onion

Directions:
Mix water and 1 tbsp creole sauce. Put into baggie with chicken, shake well to coat and marinate for 1 hour.
Cook chicken on each side in a non-stick skillet until golden brown and no longer pink inside.
Add spinach, cook for 5 minutes on low heat, and then add pepper flakes, beans, pepper and onion.
Cook for another 8 minutes on low heat, transfer into large bowl. Pour broth into skillet, and swish around to loosen flavours off the skillet. Mix chicken back in, cook for 2 minutes. Put vegtabables onto a plate, topping with the chicken. Sprinkle with cashews, and drizzle with remaining suce.
