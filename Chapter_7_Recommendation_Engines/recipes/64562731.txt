Title:
Beet Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups finely chopped cooked beets
2 cups finely chopped raw cabbage
2 tbsp. horseradish
1 1/2 tsp. salt
pinch pepper
1 cup sugar
1 1/4 cups cider or blended vinegar (regular would work too)

Directions:
Mix beets, cabbage and horseradish well.
Pack lightly into clean canning jars.
Combine salt, pepper, sugar and vinegar in med. saucepan.
Heat to boil & immediately pour over packed vegetables in jars till almost full.
Seal & store in cool place.
Makes about 2 pints or 1 litre.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
My Grandma's recipe for a ruby red relish with some kick from horseradish!
