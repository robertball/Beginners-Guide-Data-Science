Title:
Chipotle Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
4 TBL lime juice
4 TBL sugar
1/2 tsp chipotle chili powder (add more or less to adjust the heat to your taste)
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
In a small bowl mix all ingregients together.  Refrigerate until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This sweet and tart vinaigrette has an added spicy smokiness from the chipotle chili powder and an earthiness from the cumin.  The combination of flavors is delicious on any salad or in a marinade.
