Title:
Minnesota Booya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2651334.jpg
Ingredients:
3# Beef chuck/arm roast
4# Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
10# Potatoes Peeled and Cubed
2# Onion Chopped
2 - 28 oz. Canned Crushed Tomatoes
1 Rutabaga Chopped
1 Celery Bunch Chopped
2# Frozen Carrot Chips
2# Bag Frozen Corn
2# Bag Frozen Peas
1# Bag Frozen Green Beans
1 12oz Jar Orrington Farm Makes 56 Cups Chicken Broth Base and Seasoning
6oz  Orrington Farm Makes 56 Cups Beef Broth Base and Seasoning
1/4 C Salt
1/8 C Pepper
1/4 C Onion Powder
1/2 Bottle Liquid Smoke
Turkey Fryer Pot or Something Similar
Big Ladle or Paddle for Stirring

Directions:
Takes 8 Hours to make start to finish.
Start Fire and Bring 6 Gallons of Water to A Boil
Add Meat, Celery and Onions,
COOK FOR 3 HOURS
Remove Meat and Chop Up and Return to Pot; Add Bouliion,  Potatoes, Rutabaga, Carrots and Crushed Tomatoes
COOK FOR 3 HOURS
Add Veggies, Salt, Pepper, Onion Powder, Liquid Smoke
COOK FOR 2 HOURS
Serve
