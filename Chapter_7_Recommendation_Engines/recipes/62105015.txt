Title:
Apple Honey Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. apple jelly
1 T. honey
1 T. dijon or regular mustard
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
4 bonless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Heat grill.
Mix all ingredients except chicken
Brush chicken with glaze.
Put chicken on grill and brush with glaze throughout grilling.
