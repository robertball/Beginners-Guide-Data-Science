Title:
Fall Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/517231.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Strongly Brewed Pumpkin Spice Coffee
3/4 Cup Milk
2 Tablespoons Vanilla Syrup
1/4 Teaspoon Nutmeg
1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
Whipped Cream (optional)

Directions:
1.) Brew Pumpkin Spice Coffee and set aside.
2.) Pour vanilla syrup into the bottom of the coffee mug and add the cinnamon and nutmeg spices. Pour milk on top, stir, and heat until the milk is very warm without having it boil. Using a milk frother, frother the milk. Add the coffee on top and topped which whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
