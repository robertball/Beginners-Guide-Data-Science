Title:
Moms Lula Kababs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. Lean ground chuck (or lamb/beef mixture)
1 Large onion, chopped
¼ Cup chopped green pepper
3 T chopped fresh dill
1 T chopped fresh basil
1 T Seasoned salt
¼ tsp. black pepper
Fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Mix all ingredients very well.  Take about a cupful and squeeze on a skewer.  Keep working meat on a skewer for about 5 minutes or until it stays on good and is shaped like a long sausage.
Oil your grate and then when the grill is med/hot put on skeweres; grill turning when each side is golden brown or about 8 minutes.  Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and serve.
