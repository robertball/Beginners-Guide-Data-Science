Title:
Lean and Green Laughing Cow Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast, ~ 8 oz., pounded to ¼” thin
Seasoned salt
1 wedge garlic & herb light laughing cow cheese
One sun-dried tomato, slivered
One scallion, green part only, chopped
Dried basil
Lemon zest (~ 1/2 teaspoon)
½ package Parmesan cheese puffs, crushed
Chocolate balsamic vinegar (or regular balsamic)

Directions:
Pound the chicken thinly, pat dry with a paper towel and sprinkle with seasoned salt.
Mix together the cheese, tomato, scallion and lemon zest, spread over one side of the chicken breast. Roll up and secure with toothpicks. Roll in crushed puffs.
Place in a 400º oven and bake for 30-35 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from oven, let rest for a few minutes and slice in half. Drizzle with chocolate (or regular) balsamic vinegar.
