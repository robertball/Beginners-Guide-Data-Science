Title:
Grilled Pineapple with Mascarone cream. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/405066.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp crushed haselnuts
1 lemon
2 cups of mascarpone
3 tbsp honey
1 pineapple

Directions:
In a pan cook haselnuts until golden brown
Rub the zest off of the lemon, and then press the lemon juice out. Mix with mascarpone and honey.
Cut the pinapple and slice it about a cm thick.
Cook pinapple slices on both side on the grill for about 2 or 3 min.
Places slices on a plate, put some mascarpone cream over it and sprinkle with haselnuts.
serve

Submitter:

Description: 
Perfect desert on a hot summer night infront of the grill and pinapple lovers.
