Title:
irene’s (should be) famous tuna salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/890901.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans white tuna in water, drained
2 tsp balsamic vinegar
2 tbsp red onion
1 tbsp capers, chopped
1 tbsp sun dried tomato, chopped
1 tbsp dijon mustard
2 tbsp mayo
salt and pepper

Directions:
Put the drained tuna in a bowl, drizzle over the balsamic vinegar, then mix in all remaining ingredients with a fork, add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
comfortablefood.com
Description: 
This tuna salad is so different and flavorful  it has many strong flavors but none of them overpower, and it ends up being just perfect.
