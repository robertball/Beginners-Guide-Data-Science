Title:
Stuffed Poblanos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Poblano Chilis
1/2 cup shredded cheddar/jack
1/2 cup sour  cream
4 strips of bacon
1 tblspoon dehydrated diced onion
1 teaspoon garlic powder
pinch salt and pepper

Directions:
Blister Poblanos then place in freezer for 20 minutes. Peel skin off and slit one side to remome seeds.
Cook the bacon until very crisp
blend the cheese, sour cream onion, garlic powder, salt and pepper and crispy bacon.
Stuff the poblanos with the mixture.
Bake in the oven at 375 for 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
marinza
Description: 
Pablanos stuffed with a sour cream, cheddar/jack blend, and bacon. Then baked in the oven.
