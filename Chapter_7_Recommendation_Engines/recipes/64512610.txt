Title:
Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon cider
6 oz. frozen orange juice concentrate
6 oz. frozen lemonade concentrate
1/2 -1 cup brown sugar (to taste)
1 tsp. whole nutmeg
1 tbsp. whole cloves
1 tbsp. whole allspice
orange slices
cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Combine cider, orange juice, and lemonade concentrates with brown sugar in slow cooker.  Mix well.
Place nutmeg, cloves, and allspice in cheesecloth or spice ball.   Add to juices in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on low 2-8 hours until the Wassail is as hot as you'd like it.
Float orange slices and cinnamon sticks on top.
