Title:
Shrimpcargot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
seasoned bread crumbs
6 peeled and deveined large shrimp (21 to 25 per lb), chopped into bite-sized pieces
6 mushrooms, stems removed
2 tablespoons shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Put mushrooms on cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes.  in the meantime,heat the butter and garlic in a skillet over medium heat until the butter begins to bubble. Stir in shrimp and cook until done, then remove.  Stir in enough breadcrumbs to make a nice consistency. Place shrimp into each mushroom cap, and place into a small baking dish. Spoon the garlic butter, breadcrumb mixture into the mushroom caps, and sprinkle each with a pinch of mozzarella cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven until the mushrooms are tender and the cheese is golden and bubbly, 10 to 15 minutes. Turn broiler on and brown the tops for 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
After having these shrimp as an appetizer at a restaurant I have been trying to duplicate them. Serve with bread to mop up the extra yummy garlic butter!Modified from: Shrimpcargot
