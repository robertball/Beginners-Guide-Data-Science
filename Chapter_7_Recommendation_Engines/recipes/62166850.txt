Title:
Creamy Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy whipping cream
2 cups whole milk
1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
4 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine all ingrediants.  Chill until cold.  Pour into ice cream maker and freeze.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
A simple classic that tastes so creamy.  Perfect for ice cream sundaes or with pies.
