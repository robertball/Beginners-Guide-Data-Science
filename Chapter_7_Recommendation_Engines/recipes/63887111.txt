Title:
Ugly Duckling Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078376.jpg
Ingredients:
CAKE
1 pkg. 2 layer size yellow cake mix
1 can 16 oz. fruit cocktail
2 1/2 cups angel flake coconut
2 eggs
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
TOPPING
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup evaporated milk

Directions:
CAKE
Turn on oven to 325 degrees.
Combine cake mix, fruit cocktail with syrup, 1 cup of coconut, and eggs in a large mixer bowl.  Blend, then beat for 2 minutes at medium speed.
Pour mix into greased/buttered 13 x 9 inch pan.  Sprinkle with brown sugar.
Bake at 325 degrees F for 45 minutes or until cake springs back when lightly touched.
TOPPING
Bring butter, granulated sugar, and milk to a boil in a small saucepan.  Boil for 2 minutes.
Remove from heat.  Stir in remaining coconut.
Spoon over hot cake in pan.
