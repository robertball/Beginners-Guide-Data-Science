Title:
Toasted Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323030.jpg
Ingredients:
10 frozen cheese ravioli
2 tablespoons Italian salad dressing
2 tablespoons seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup marinara sauce, warmed

Directions:
Cook ravioli according to package directions; drain.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Put Italian dressing in small bowl and bread crumbs in a separate bowl.
Dip each ravioli in Italian dressing, then coat with the bread crumbs.
Place on a baking sheet coated with cooking spray.
Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
Serve with marinara sauce.

A healthier version of fried ravioli.
