Title:
Chipotle Burgers with Avocado Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground hamburger
1 can Chipotle chilies in adobo sauce
1 cup chopped mushroom
1tbsp worcestershire sauce
1 avocado
1 onion
1 tomato
2 fresh jalapenos
1 bunch cilantro
1 lime, squeezed (juice only)
salt and pepper to taste
Bread of choice- onion rolls are best

Directions:
Chop cilantro, set aside. Mix ground beef, worcestershire, chipotle peppers, mushrooms, and half of the cilantro. Season with salt and pepper. Form patties. Meat can be grilled or browned in skillet.
Dice avocado, tomato, onion, and jalapenos. Mix with lime juice and remaining cilantro. Salt and pepper to taste.
Place cooked hamburger patty on onion roll (sourdough is nice for this recipe, as well). Top with avocado mixture.
More spiciness? Try adding pepperjack cheese on top of the meat before adding the avocado salsa.
