Title:
Essanaye's Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/617200.jpg
Ingredients:
2-8 oz packages cream cheese at room temperature,  1 cube (1/2 cup) butter at room temperature,  2 tsp vanilla extract,  3 cups powdered sugar (sifted).

Directions:
mix the cream cheese and butter together until smooth.  Add vanilla extract and mix well, add powdered sugar in and mix well with hand mixer until creamy.   Load into piping bags (or use spatula) and frost up a storm.    Makes enough to frost 30+ standard cupcakes.

Submitter: 
Essanaye
Description: 
A little sweeter than standard cream cheese frosting.
