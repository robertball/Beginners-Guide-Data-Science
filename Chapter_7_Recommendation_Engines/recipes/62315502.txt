Title:
Bacon Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/375467.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 cup biscuit/baking mix
Dash pepper
8 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
8 oz of chopped spinach thawed and drained

Directions:
In a blender, combine eggs, milk and butter. Add biscuit mix and pepper; cover and process for 15 seconds. Pour into a greased 9-in. pie plate. Top with bacon and cheese and spinach.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Tom
Description: 
Enjoy the traditional flavor of a bacon quiche without the effort by preparing this quick version from Helen Hoppes of Wabash, Indiana. Using baking mix means there's no need to fuss over a pastry crust.Modified from: Bacon Quiche
