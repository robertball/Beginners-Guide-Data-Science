Title:
Blue Grapefruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/998270.jpg
Ingredients:
1 grapefruit - peeled, seeds removed, and white pith removed
1 cup rice milk
1 cup blueberries
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup rolled oats
1 banana
1 kiwi
3 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:
Mix grapefruit, rice milk, blueberries, yogurt, oats, banana, kiwi, and sugar together in a blender until smooth.

Description: 
This grapefruit smoothie is a satisfying, perky combo!
