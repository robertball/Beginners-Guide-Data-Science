Title:
Carmel apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
49 kraft carmels (unwrapped)
2 Tbsp water
5medium apples
wooden sticks

Directions:
Melt carmels with water in top of a double boiler. Wash and dry the apples. Insert sticks in stem end and dip into the melted carmel sauce. Place the apples on waxed paper and chill until firm
