Title:
ALICIA'S MEXICAN RICE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2333071.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups INSTANT Rice
1 can Ortega Tomatoes w/Peppers
1 1/2 cups hot water
1 or 2 chicken (or vegetable) bouillon cube(s)
1 fresh bell pepper of choice, diced
1 medium yellow onion, diced
1-15 ounce can Mexican style corn
1 teaspoon bacon grease (or shortening)
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon medium chili powder
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/4 teaspoon cumin

Directions:
Bring everything (except rice) to a full, rolling boil for 2 minutes.  Add rice and stir; then remove from heat and allow to rest, covered for 5 minutes.  Fluff with fork and serve.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
Not authentic, but yummy just the same, this rice dish is excellent served with boneless  pork chops or shredded pork roast and enchilada sauce.  Add  sharp process cheddar cheese and corn bread or flour tortillas, and you've got quick comfort food at its finest!
