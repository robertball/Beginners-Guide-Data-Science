Title:
Dr. Pepper Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box German Chocolate Cake mix
1 box (3 1/2 oz.) instant vanilla pudding mix
2/3 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs
1 can Dr. Pepper
1 tsp. vanilla
--------------------
FROSTING
1 pkg. (8 oz.) cream cheese
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 tsp. vanilla
1 lb. powdered sugar
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Comgine the first three ingredients and mix until well blended.
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each.
Add Dr. Pepper and vanilla, mix well.
Grease and flour three 9-inch cake pans and pour equal amounts of batter in each.
Bake for 35 minutes.  Cool for 10 minutes in pans before removing.  When cool enough frost.
