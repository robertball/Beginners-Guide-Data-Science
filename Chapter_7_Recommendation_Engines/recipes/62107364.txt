Title:
Oatmeal Crusted Chicken Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless chicken breasts (cut into small strips) yields @ 25 pieces
2 eggs beaten with salt and pepper to taste
1-2 cups of oatmeal
2 tbsp grated parmesian cheese
(optional)canola oil to fry
Cajun spice (optional)
Optional Sauces:
2 chipotles blended with 1/4 cup mayo
1/2 dijon mustard and 1/2 honey (amount vaires on how much you want)
1/2 sour cream and 1/2 salsa (amount vaires on how much you want)

Directions:
Use scissors to cut the chicken breasts into strips (much easier than a knife).
Blend the oatmeal and cheese together (add cajun spice if you like) until it looks like flour with some oatmeal chunks.
Put the oatmeal mixture into a large ziplock bag.
Dip chicken strips into the egg.
Toss half of the chicken in the large ziplock at a a time and shake until coated.
Heat about 2tbsp of oil in a frying pan over med-high heat.
Fry both sides of the chicken until golden (about 3-5 minutes per side).
*For a healthier version you can bake the strips in the oven on 350 for about 15-20 minutes
