Title:
Danish Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 medium cucumbers
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1/4 teaspoon white pepper

Directions:
Cut cucumbers into paper thin slices.  Toss with salt.  Marinate in salt at room temperature for one hour.
Drain liquid from cucumber.  Add remaining ingredients and stir.  Cover and refrigerate three hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Thin slices of cucumber absorb sweet and sour flavors from a marinade.
