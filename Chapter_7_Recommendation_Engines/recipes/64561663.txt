Title:
Low Fat Quick Mango Rabdi - Indian Sweet Rabri Recipe Just in 2 minutes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3472770.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Mango Pieces,
1 Cup Mango Small Pieces
1 Cup Cashew,
4 Bread Slices,
3 Tbsp Sugar,
3/4 Cup Milk,
1 Tbsp Almond & Pistachio for garnishing.

Directions:
Take cashew, bread slice, sugar & add little milk and grind all ingredients.
Than add mango pieces and milk and again grind it.
Remove this mixture in a bowl and add small chopped mango pieces. mix all well.
Garnish with chopped almond & pistachio.

Submitter: 
Manisha Bharani
Description: 
Rabdi or Rabri is a traditional Indian sweet.The making of authentic rabri recipe is a long process & it is very rich and full of calaories. But this recipe is an instant recipe is low cal and yummy. In recipe uses of bread & cashews that gives thickness to recipe. It reduces the time & low fat also. Mango gives very nice taste to the recipe
