Title:
Drew's 30 min Easy Enchiladas (Cooks) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/761426.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of rotisserie chicken
3/4 cup refried beans
1 Anaheim chili chopped
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup cilantro
10-12 corn tortillas
20 oz green enchilada sauce
avocado, for serving
sour cream, for serving
lime wedges, for serving

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Shred 2 cups (about 1/2 a rotisserie chicken) in a glass bowl that can be microwaved
Add 3/4 cup of refried beans, chopped chili pepper, 1/2 cup of enchilada sauce and combine- microwave for 1-2 minutes
Microwave tortillas for 50 seconds
Add 1 and 1/2 cup of shredded cheese and cilantro to the mixture
Arrange tortillas on a work surface and divide meat mixture evenly (about 1/4 cup each). Tightly roll and place in greased baking dish with seam side down
Pour 1 cup more enchilada sauce over enchiladas. Sprinkle remaining cheese over enchiladas
Cover baking dish with foil and bake for 10 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes.  Heat last cup of sauce in microwwave for 1.5 min. Serve with warmed sauce, lime wedges, sour cream, and avocado slices
