Title:
Baked potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium yukon gold potatoes, sliced
1/2 pound sliced bacon
1 pound grated cheddar
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
salt and pepper to taste
Tomatoes, diced

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender, then drain and mix with cheese, mayo, sour cream, and green onions. Top with bacon and bake for 1 hour at 325.
