Title:
Old Fashioned Rock Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups sugar
3 cups water
3/4 tsp cream of tartar

Directions:
Cook these ingredients quickly without stirring to 290 degrees.  Substitute fruit juice for liquid or add flavored extract and desired food coloring.  Pour quickly into greased square pan.  Chill until firm.  Turnout of pan.  Crack into desired pieces.
