Title:
Chocolate-Coffee Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
3-1/4 cups confectioners' sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 teaspoon of instant coffee
1/8 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons half & half

Directions:
Have all ingredients at room temperature. Melt the chocolate in a double boiler or microwave oven. Once melted whisk in instant coffee.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Pour melted chocolate/coffee into cream cheese and mix on medium speed until well blended. Add vanilla and pinch of salt. Slowly mix in the confectioners sugar.
Scrape the sides of the bowl and increase mixer speed to medium-high. Slowly add half & half until the frosting is spreading consistency.
