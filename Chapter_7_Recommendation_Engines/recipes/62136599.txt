Title:
Pineapple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20 oz. can pineapple chunks(save juice)
1/2 cup sugar
3 tbps. flour
1 cup grated sharp chedder cheese
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 to 1 cup cracker crumbs

Directions:
Drain pineapple and save 3 tbsp.juice.
Combine sugar, flour, and juice.
Add cheese and pineapple. Put in 1 quart casserole dish.
Combine butter and cracker crumbs and spoon over pineapple mixture.
Bake at 350 for 20 to 30 minutes (until top is crispy)

Submitter:

Description:
