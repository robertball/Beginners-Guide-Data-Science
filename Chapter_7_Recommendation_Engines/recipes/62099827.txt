Title:
MAKE AHEAD MASHED POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 large potatoes, peeled & cut in chunks
1/2 c. butter
12 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
1/2 c. sour cream
1/4 t. pepper
1/2 t. salt

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender.
Put potatoes in mixing bowl, beat until fluffy.
Beat in remaining ingredients.
Transfer to a heavily buttered 9X13 casserole dish.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
On the day you would like to serve this, bring the potatoes to room temperature.
Bake uncovered in a 350 degree F oven for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Steph T.
Description: 
Fantastic rich and creamy mashed potatoes that can be prepared a day or two ahead of time and then just thrown in the oven for a few minutes before you enjoy them.  Tasty and convenient for large dinner parties.
