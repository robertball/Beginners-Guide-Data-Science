Title:
Leek Potsy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chopped potatoes
Leeks
Heavy cream
Chopped onion
Cream of celery (condensed)
Fresh dill
Butter
Chopped garlic
Vegetable bouillon cube
Water

Directions:
Equal parts (or roughly equal) of chopped potatoes, leeks, and heavy cream.
Half that quantity and use that measurement for equal parts chopped onion, cream of celery condensed, and fresh dill (or any fresh spice).
Throw in some butter, chopped garlic, a vegetable bouillon cube, and cover it with water for desired consistency.
Cook in slow cooker for whatever length you choose. When nearing the end, ladle the soup into a blender (several cycles will be necesary; don't overfill blender). Blend well. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Em Bea
Description: 
Slow-cooker soup perfect for brunch. Light, fresh, and flavorful but with the indulgence of heavy cream.
