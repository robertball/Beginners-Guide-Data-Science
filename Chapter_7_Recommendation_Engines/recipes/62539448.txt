Title:
Caramelized Brussels Sprouts and Chestnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522895.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups jarred vacuum-packed whole cooked chestnut
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and cut in half
Salt and freshly ground black pepper
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup turkey stock (homemade or canned)

Directions:
Melt butter and oil in a large saute pan set over medium-high heat. Add brussels sprouts; season with salt and pepper.
Cook, stirring occasionally, until golden, 20 minutes.
Add the roasted chestnuts.
Cook, gently stirring occasionally, until Brussels sprouts are tender and spotted deep brown, 20 to 25 minutes.
Add cider vinegar, sugar, and turkey stock. Cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid has been reduced to a syrup, 4 to 5 minutes.
Transfer to a serving dish and enjoy!

Description: 
This side dish has appeared on our Thanksgiving table for years! Yet it a great side dish for any fall/ winter dinner!
