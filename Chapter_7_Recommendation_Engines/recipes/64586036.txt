Title:
Cucumber Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3717489.jpg
Ingredients:
large seedless cucumber
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup apple cider
water

Directions:
chop cucumber into pitcher
add sugar and apple cider
add water to 1 quart
blend with submersion blender
add water to 2 quart
stir
