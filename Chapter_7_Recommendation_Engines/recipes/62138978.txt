Title:
Decadent Turtle Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Oreo cookie crumbs
6 Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted
1 14oz. bag caramels
1/2 cup milk
1 cup chopped pecans
3 8oz. packages cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 Tbsp. vanilla
3 eggs
2 squares semi-sweet baking chocolate

Directions:
Mix crumbs and butter. and press into bottom and 2 inches up side of a 9" springform pan.
Place caramels and milk in small microwavable bowl. Microwave on high 3 minutes or until caramels are completely melted, stirring after each minute. Stir in pecans.
Pour half of the caramel mixture into crust, and refrigerate 10 minutes. Set remaining caramel mixture aside for later use.
Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed after each addition just until blended. Pour over caramel mixture in crust.
Bake at 325 for 1 hour 5 minutes to 1 hour 15 minutes or until center is almost set if using a silver springform pan. If using a dark nonstick pan, bake at 300 for same amount of time or until center is almost set.
Run knife around side of pan to loosen cake and cool before removing side of pan.
Refrigerate 5 hours or overnight.
Top with remaining caramel mixture just before serving. Melt chocolate as directed on package and drizzle over top.
