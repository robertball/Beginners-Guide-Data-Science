Title:
Instant Pot Pumpkin Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4550416.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
1 cup water
1-1/4 cup vanilla almond milk
1/3 cup pumpkin puree
1 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 cup chopped pecans
2 teaspoons maple syrup

Directions:
Place old-fashioned rolled oats, water, almond milk, pumpkin puree, butter, brown sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg into the Instant Pot®.  Stir the mixture to help hydrate the oats.
Place the lid on the Instant Pot®, turn pressure valve to Sealing,  Select the Manual button, high pressure, and set the timer for 4 minutes.
When the cooking cycle is complete, do a Quick Pressure Release.  Open the Pot, remove the inner pot, and stir the oatmeal.
Divide between 2 bowls and garnish each with 2 tablespoons pecans and 1 teaspoon maple syrup.

Description: 
Start your day with a quick, easy, and warm bowl of pumpkin oatmeal.  Just mix everything together in the Instant Pot®,  and in no time, you’ll have a delicious breakfast on the table.
