Title:
Ambrosia Fruit Salad with Greek Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 Cans Tropical Fruit with juice
2-3 Apples
Misc other fruits in season, grapes, bananas (only if firm), etc.
1 cup shredded, sweetened coconut
1-2 cups Vanilla Honey Greek Yogurt (or other favorite)
2 tsp cinamon
2 tsp vanilla (or 1tsp vanilla and 1/2 tsp almond extract)
Refrigerate at least two hours to overnight before serving.  Fold once more before placing in serving dish, and sprinkle with coconut.

Directions:
Chop apples and other fresh fruit into large bite size pieces.  Place fresh and canned fruit (reserving liquid in separate container) in bowl.  Add coconut, and extracts.  Stir gently to combine.  Add Yogurt 1/2 cup at a time with a few tablespoons of reserved fruit juice, gently folding after each addition, until desired (fully coated and covered) consistency is reached.
Refrigerate at least two hours to overnight before serving.  Fold once more before placing in serving dish, and sprinkle with coconut.

Chunky fresh and canned fruits in a creamy cinnamon dressing, with coconut shavings
