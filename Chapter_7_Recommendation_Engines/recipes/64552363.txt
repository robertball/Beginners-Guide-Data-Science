Title:
Candied Yam Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large yams, peeled, cooked and mashed
1 cup water (use water from cooking yams & top up if needed)
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 - 3 tbsp. maple or pancake syrup
approx. 1 cup mini white marshmallows

Directions:
Peel and cook yams.
Reserve cooking water drained from yams.
Measure 1 cup cooking water, topping up with drinking water if needed.
Stir sugar and syrup into water.
Mash yams, gradually adding sugar mixture till very soft, but not runny.
Put into shallow casserole dish (ungreased).
Cover with a layer of mini white marshmallows.
Bake @ 350 x approx. 15 min., till marshmallows are lightly toasted.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A delicious toasted marshmallow topped yam / sweet potato casserole.
