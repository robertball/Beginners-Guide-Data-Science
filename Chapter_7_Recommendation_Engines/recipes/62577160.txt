Title:
Peach Salsa Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. jar "Das Peach Haus" Peach Salsa [from Fischer and Wieser]
1  8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened

Directions:
Pour salsa into a small sauce pan.
Bring to a boil,reduce heat to simmer and cook until about 1/2 remains
Run the hot mixture thru the food processor until smooth
Pour into dish/or shallow pan and chill.
When cool, mix into the cream cheese and beat together until well incorporated.
Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Discovered this delicious treat when cleaning out the cupboard...simple but good.  Use any salsa you like to make your own favorite spread.
