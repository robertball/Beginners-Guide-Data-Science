Title:
BBQ 'Pulled' Tuna Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6291260.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sliced red onion
3 cloves garlic, diced
1 tablespoon water, or as needed
3 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon steak seasoning
1 cup barbeque sauce
1 tablespoon butter
4 large soft rolls, warmed

Directions:
Cook and stir onion, garlic, and water in a skillet over medium heat until tender, about 5 minutes. Add tuna, Worcestershire sauce, and steak seasoning; mix briefly to combine. Add the barbeque sauce. Simmer on low heat to allow the flavors to combine, about 5 minutes.
Spread butter on soft rolls. Heap tuna mixture onto the rolls and serve.

Description: 
Pulled pork sandwiches are one of my favorite things in the world and I made this recipe to sort of capture the taste with less time and work! Goes excellent with a nice crisp coleslaw!
