Title:
Tex-Mex Taco Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs.ground beef, browned and drained
16 oz. can refried beans
10 oz. can enchilada sauce
1 1/4 oz. pkg. taco seasoning mix
16 oz. jar salsa
25 hot dog buns, split
Garnish: shredded Cheddar cheese, shredded lettuce,
chopped tomatoes, sour cream

Directions:
Place beefr in a slow cooker.  Stir in beans, enchilada sauce, seasoning mix and salsa.
Cover and cook on low setting for 4 to 6 hours.
To serve, fill each bun with 1/3 cup beef mixture; garnish as desired.
Makes 25 sandwiches.
