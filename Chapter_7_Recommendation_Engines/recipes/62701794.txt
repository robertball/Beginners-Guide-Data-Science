Title:
Strawberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T.  white vinegar
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
4-5 strawberries, sliced
1/2 - 3/4 c. sugar
1 t. dry mustard
½ t. salt
1/3 cup cider vinegar
1 c. olive oil
1½ T. poppy seeds

Directions:
Mix all ingredients into a blender until thickened.
