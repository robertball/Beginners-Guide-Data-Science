Title:
Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices hard salami
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
6 green onions (can also use sweet or dill pickles)

Directions:
Lay the hard salami slices flat on a serving plate and spread with cream cheese. Place a cut green onion stalk at one end of each slice, and roll the slices into cylinders around the onion; secure with toothpicks. Chill in refrigerator until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
jabaran
Description: 
Yummy salami, onion,and cream cheese roll ups for an appetizer or a party! Secure the roll ups with toothpicks, and cut them into bite size pieces.Modified from: Yummy Roll Ups
