Title:
Very Gooood Cocoa Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. confectioners sugar
5 T. cocoa
1/8 tsp. salt
3 T. soft butter
4-5 T. milk

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients together. Add butter and milk. Beat well.
