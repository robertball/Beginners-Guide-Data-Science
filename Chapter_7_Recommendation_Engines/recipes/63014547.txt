Title:
Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754000.jpg
Ingredients:
- Shredded Chicken or Shredded Beef
- Tortillas
- Enchiladas Sauce or seasoning package
- Shredded Mexican Cheese

Directions:
Cook chicken (breast preferably) after cooked shred up chicken to be stuffed in tortillas.
Lightly heat up tortillas in microwave for they are soft enough to fold and wont fall apart, stuff chicken in tortilla and roll it up!
Add sauce on top of the enchiladas covering it completely and the sides, add shredded cheese.
Cover with foil paper and put in oven 350 degrees for about 25 min until sauce starts to bubble and cheese is melted. SERVE & ENJOY
