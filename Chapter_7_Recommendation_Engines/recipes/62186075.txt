Title:
APPLE PUDDING LIKE COBBLER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 COOKING APPLES,PEELED AND SLICED,ALMOST ENOUGH TO FILL YOUR 8 OR 9-INCH BAKING PAN.
1 1/2 C SUGAR,DIVIDED
3/4 TSP CINNAMON,DIVIDED
1/2 CUP BUTTER OR MARGERINE,SOFTENED
1 EGG
1 1/4 CUPS FLOUR
1 TSP BAKING POWDER
1/4 TSP SALT
1/4 TSP NUTMEG
ABOUT 2/3 CUP MILK
1/2 TSP VANILLA EXTRACT
IS DELICIOUS SERVED WARM,WITH VANILLA ICE CREAM,WHIPPED CREAM OR THE OPTIONAL SAUCE,FOR WHICH THE RECIPE FOLLOWS BELOW:
OPTIONAL SERVING SAUCE:
1 TB BUTTER
1 TB FLOUR
1/4 C SUGAR
1/4 TSP NUTMEG(MORE/LESS)TO TASTE.
MELT BUTTER IN SAUCEPAN.COMBINE FLOUR,SUGAR AND NUTMEG.STIR QUICKLY INTO BUTTER.ADD WARM WATER TO MAKE THIN SAUCE.STIR AND SPOON OVER PUDDING WHEN YOU SERVE IT,IF DESIRED.

Directions:
COMBINE 1/2 CUP SUGAR MIXED WITH 1/2 TSP CINNAMON.POUR OVER APPLES AND MIX TILL COATED.PLACE IN BAKING PAN.IN BOWL,CREAM BUTTER AND 1 CUP OF SUGAR.ADD EGG.BEAT UNTIL LIGHT.ADD FLOUR,BAKING POWDER,SALT,NUTMEG AND 1/4 TSP CINNAMON.GRADUALLY ADD MILK,WHICH HAS THE VANILLA MIXED IN IT.MIX ALL WELL.BATTER MAY APPEAR THIN,BUT IS FINE.POUR OVER APPLES,COVERING COMPLETELY.BAKE AT 350 FOR ABOUT ONE HOUR.

Submitter: 
Barbara Hale
Description: 
DELICIOUS BUT NOT DIFFICULT DESSERT,WITH CINNAMON AND SUGAR SPRINLKED APPLES,THEN TOPPED AND BAKED WITH AN OLD FASHIONED CINNAMON,NUTMEG SEASONED COBBLER TYPE TOP.
