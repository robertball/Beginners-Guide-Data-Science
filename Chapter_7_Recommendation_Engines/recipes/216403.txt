Title:
Caesar BLT Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990587.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups romaine lettuce leaves, torn
1 medium tomato, chopped
1/3 cup bacon, crisply cooked, crumbled
1/4 cup Caesar salad dressing
1 1/2 cups Sargento® Artisan Blends® Shredded Parmesan Cheese
4 (10 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine lettuce, tomato, bacon and salad dressing in large bowl. Add cheese; toss.
Spoon mixture evenly onto tortillas; roll up tortillas burrito-style. Wrap sandwiches in plastic wrap and refrigerate until serving time.

Submitter: 
Sargento Shreds
Description: 
You can make these delicious BLT wraps with Caesar salad dressing and shredded Parmesan cheese ahead of time for a quick lunch or light supper.
