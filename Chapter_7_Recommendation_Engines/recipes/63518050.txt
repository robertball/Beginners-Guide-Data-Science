Title:
Spicy Garlic Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960691.jpg
Ingredients:
19oz. of Garbanzo Beans, juice reserved
1 Lemon, juiced = about 4 tablespoons
1 whole garlic bulb, pealed and minced - OR - 11 of 1/2 tsp of minced garlic.
1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Paprika
1/2 tsp Curry
4 tbsp Cilantro or Parsley, freshed and chopped (optional)
1/3 cup Tahini
2 tbsp Olive Oil

Directions:
Peel and mince the bulb of garlic, set aside
Prepare all your dry spices in one bowl, set aside
Chop your fresh Cilantro or Parsley if using, set aside
Rinse your Garbanzo Beans, drain well and put in the blender.
Add a little reserved juice
Add your Lemon Juice
Add Olive Oil
Add Tahini
Add all your dry spices
Add Fresh spices is using any
Blend & Taste!

Submitter: 
Sadie Melgoza
Description: 
Some like it hot as the saying goes and so do we! Hummus, hummus, everyone has got to love some Spicy Garlic Hummus. This recipe can easily be changed to satisfy anyone's taste as you will see.
