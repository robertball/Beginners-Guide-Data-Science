Title:
Black Gold Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986248.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup finely chopped onions
6 large cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
6 onion rolls, split

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium heat. Cook and stir onions and garlic in margarine in medium skillet on medium heat until tender but not brown; stir in sugar. Reduce heat to low; cook 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in steak sauce; keep warm.
Shape meat into six patties.
Grill patties 5 minutes on each side or until cooked through (160 degrees F). Place burgers on bottom halves of rolls; cover with sauce and tops of rolls.
