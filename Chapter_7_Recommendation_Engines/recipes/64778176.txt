Title:
Carnivore Butter Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg yolks
1/2 c. melted unsalted butter (or bacon grease or tallow or other animal fat)
garlic clove (if desired)
lemon juice or apple cider vinegar (as needed/desired)
salt to taste
1 T hot water if needed

Directions:
put 3 egg yolks in bottom of jar or pyrex measuring cup
add garlic and little salt
use hand immersion blender to blend
slowly add butter while using hand immersion blender
add salt and lemon juice or apple cider vinegar to taste
add 1 T or more hot water as needed to thin to desired consistency
