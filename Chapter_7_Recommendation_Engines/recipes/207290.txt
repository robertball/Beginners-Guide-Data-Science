Title:
Kahlua Espresso Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991472.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Kahlua
1 fluid ounce vodka
1/2 fluid ounce fresh brewed espresso

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake vigorously, strain into a chilled martini glass.

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
Iced coffee with a Kahlua and vodka kick! The perfect martini for the coffee fanatic in your life.
