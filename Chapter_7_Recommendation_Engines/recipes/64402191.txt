Title:
Gluten and Lactose Free Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5943098.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 slices bacon
1 medium white onion chopped
2 garlic cloves finely diced
3 cups cheddar goat cheese, grated
2 tablespoons earth balance spread(or becel vegan margarine)
3 tablespoons gluten free flour
2 cups So Delicious soy free coconut milk for coffee(or lactose free milk)
8 ounces gluten free elbow macaroni(Catelli has good ones)
salt
1/2 to 1  teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook bacon on medium heat till nicely browned, when cooked drain on paper towel and crumble and set it aside.
In a medium frying pan saute the onion and garlic about 5 minutes. Take off the heat and add the crumpled bacon and set aside. In a large pot bring the water to a boil and add
a good pinch of salt, add pasta and cook till al dente then drain.
For the sauce
melt the earth balance spread over medium heat, once melted add the flour and stir for about 1-2 minutes, add the milk slowly and keep stirring until it gets thick. Once it thickens add 2 cups of the cheese and stir until melted.
add nutmeg and stir it in.
Combine the pasta with the onion, garlic and bacon and mix until blended. Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish and sprinkle the rest of the cheese over all.
At this point sprinkle a bit of paprika on top for a nice golden color.
Bake uncovered until the cheese is melted and a nice golden
brown. 15-20 minutes.Serve warm with a green salad and some crusty bread.
