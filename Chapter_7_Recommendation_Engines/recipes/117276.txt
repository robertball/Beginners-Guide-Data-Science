Title:
Cranberry Orange Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987285.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon grated orange peel
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 cup orange juice
1 cup chopped fresh or frozen cranberries
1 cup golden raisins

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Beat in egg and orange peel.  Combine the dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately with juice.  Fold in cranberries and raisins.  Pour into a greased 9-in. x 5-in. x 3-in. loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 60-65 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.  Cool for 10 minutes; remove from pan to a wire rack to cool completely.
