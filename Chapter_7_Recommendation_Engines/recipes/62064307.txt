Title:
Ginger soy salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 1/3 lb salmon fillets
2 cloves garlic
1tbsp. sesame oil
2/3 c. light soy sauce
1/2 tsp. wasabi paste
1 1/2 tsp. ground ginger
3 tbsp. rice wine
3 tbsp. rice vinegar

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients except garlic and sesame oil.  Mince and saute garlic in sesame oil.  Add to mixture.
Marinate fish for 20 minutes.
Bake at 350 10 min. per " thickness
