Title:
Iced Coffee Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cold strong brewed coffee
2 scoops frozen vanilla fat free yogourt
2 cups ice
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 envelopes sweet and low sweetener

Directions:
Mix coffee, vanilla extract and sweetener in blender.
Add ice and frozen yogourt. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
BramptonMommyof2
Description: 
I decided to whip this up after wanting a really cold drink but I wanted coffee too. Best of both combined. Wonderfully refreshing on a hot day.
