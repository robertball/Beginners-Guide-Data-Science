Title:
Mexican Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1061213.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (11 ounce) cans corn with red and green peppers (such as Green Giant® Mexicorn®), drained
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons shredded extra-sharp Cheddar cheese, or to taste
1 teaspoon grated onion, or to taste

Directions:
Stir corn, sour cream, mayonnaise, Cheddar cheese, and onion together until evenly mixed.
