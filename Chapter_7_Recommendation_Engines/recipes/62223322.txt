Title:
Oven Baked Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breast halves (4 to 6 oz. each)
1/4 cup (1 stick) butter or margarine, melted
1 can (6 oz.) frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1/4 cup honey
3/4 cup fine bread crumbs
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. thyme
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Brush chicken breasts with butter.
Combine undiluted orange juice and honey in shallow dish.
Mix brad crumbs, flour, paprika, thyme & salt.
Roll Chicken in honey mixture, then in bread crumb mix.
Place chicken in a large greased baking pan and drizzle with any ramining butter or honey mixture.
Bake at 375 F for 45 to 50 minutes or until tender and no longer pink.
