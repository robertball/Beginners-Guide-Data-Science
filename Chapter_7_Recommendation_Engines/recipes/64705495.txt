Title:
3 Eggs, Scrambled Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5471958.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large eggs
2 T Butter
2 T Milk
Pinch Salt

Directions:
Heat pan on Medium-High heat with the butter
Beat the Eggs, Milk, and Salt until well mixed
When butter starts to bubble, pour the egg mixture in
Stir frequently to distribute the liquid eggs mixture until it reaches the level of firmness
