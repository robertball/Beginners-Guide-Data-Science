Title:
My favorite Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 tablespoons vegetable oi
1 large onion, chopped
1-2 stalks celery
2 large cloves garlic minced
1 green bell pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 to 2 pound ground beef
1/2   pound spicy Italian sausage, casing removed
1 large (28 oz  can peeled and diced tomatoes with juice
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle dark beer
1 cup strong brewed coffee
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
1/4 cup  Chili powder
2 1/2  teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon sugar
1  1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 basil teaspoon dried basil
1- teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon paprika
2-3 dashes of hot sauce
1/4 t. cayenne pepper ( if needed  don't add until it has cooked a couple of hours and you taste it.
2 (15 ounce) cans kidney beans drained
2 (15 oz. can( chili beans)
2 Anaheim chile peppers, chopped
1 serrano pepper, chopped (seeds and membrans removed, can leave a few seeds
1 habanero pepper (seeds and membranes removed)

Directions:
Heat a large stock pot over medium-high heat. Crumble the ground beef and sausage into the hot pan, and cook until evenly browned. Drain off excess grease.
In dutch oven over medium heat, heat remaining oil. Cook onions, peppers, garlic, celery, Anaheim, serrano and habanero peppers in oil with 1 t. salt until just tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
Stir in all other ingredients except beans and cayenne pepper and simmer with lid on about 2 hours.
Add beans and adjust seasoning.  Add cayenne pepper if you want it more spicy.  Adjust salt if needed.  Simmer another 45-1 hr. with lid off
