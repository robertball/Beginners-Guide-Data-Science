Title:
Amy’s Quick Pan Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5433603.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 28oz cans of good whole tomatoes. I use Cento San Marzano.
1/2 bulb of garlic.
Bunch of fresh basil.
Crushed red pepper.
Olive oil.
**Optional:  to add depth, sauté a couple anchovy filets until they disappear before sautéing the garlic.
**Optional: to add richness, add a couple tablespoons of tomato paste.

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large pan.
Smash and peel garlic leaving whole.
Add smashed garlic cloves to oil and sauté for a few minutes. Do not let garlic burn.
Add red pepper flakes.
Pour all contents of canned tomatoes into pan.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Carefully smash whole tomatoes with potato masher.
Tear the basil and add to sauce.
Simmer on high while your spaghetti or macaroni cooks.
Serve with Parmesan or Peccorino.

Submitter: 
Amy Standaert
Description: 
Sam likes this sauce. Simple, light, fast and easy.
