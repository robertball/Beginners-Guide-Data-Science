Title:
Sour Cream Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. shredded jack or hot pepper jack cheese, divided
3 c. cooked, shredded chicken
1 med. onion, chopped
17 oz. can green chili sauce
1 can chopped green chilies
8 oz. lite or regular sour cream
8 soft taco sized tortillas (preferrably "Gordita" style)
9 x 12 x 2 in. glass casserole dish

Directions:
Combine 2 cups cheese, chicken, onions and green chilies in a bowl and set aside.  Pour enough green sauce to coat the bottom of the casserole dish.
Lay tortilla flat onto workspace and fill with chicken mixture.  Top mixture with sour cream.  Roll once, tuck in sides and roll to close.  Place seam side down into sauced dish.  Repeat until all tortillas are filled.
Pour remaining green sauce over filled tortillas and top with cheese.
Bake at 375 for 15 - 20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
