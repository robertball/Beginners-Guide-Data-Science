Title:
rueben/pierogie casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box pierogie 2 tbl sp of butter quater pound corn beef sliced and choped  8oz sauerkraut 8 slices swiss CHESSE 1 cup thousand island dressing rye or pumpernickle bread crumbs 1/2 cup

Directions:
pre heat ovean bake @ 350* butter 19x13 pan, line bottom with frozen pierogie add sauerkraut,then meat then chesse, pour dressing over top. top with bread crumbs, bake 30-40 minutes until it bubbles.( i like 2 cover the first 20 minutes)
