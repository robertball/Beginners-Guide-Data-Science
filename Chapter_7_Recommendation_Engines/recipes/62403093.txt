Title:
Easy Seafood Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage, diced
1 head celery, diced
3 green onions, thinly sliced
2 lbs cooked, deveined, peeled shrimp
1 lb imitation crab meat
1 cup mayo
2 tbsp sugar or Splenda

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl.  Chill for 3 hours to allow cabbage to soften and flavors to blend. Use more or less mayo and sugar to taste.

Submitter: 
Jen K
Description: 
A restaurant quality seafood salad - no pasta, just veggies and whatever seafood you choose to add.
