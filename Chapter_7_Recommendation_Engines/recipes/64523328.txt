Title:
Spicey Cheese Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb sharp Cheddar
5 ounces of Parmesan
1 1/2 Cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cut in chunks
1 1/2 teaspoons Colman's Mustard powder
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1 1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper, divided
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbs ice water

Directions:
In food processor: Combine Cheddar, 4 oz of Parmesan,
Add Flour, butter, 1/2 tsp mustard,salt and 1/4 tsp cayenne.
Pulse to small pellets
Add Worsestershire & ice water and pulse just until combined
Divide into 2 balls
Shape into 2, 12 inch logs
Wrap in plastic wrap
Refrigerate at least 1 hour
Heat oven to 325 degrees
Line  a pan with parchment paper to be baked on the center rack
Combine 1 tsp mustard and 1 tsp of cayenne.
Unwrap the dough and roll in the spice mix.
Slice dough about 1/3 inch thick
Sprinkle each round with a pinch of the seasoning
Place on parchment paper until golden brown.
Transfer to a rack to cool
