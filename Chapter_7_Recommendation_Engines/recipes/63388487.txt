Title:
SouthWest Not-so-Funereal Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sour cream
1 c plain 2% greek yogurt
2 11.5 oz cans cream of mushroom or cream of chicken soup
2 cups shredded pepper jack cheese
1 32 oz package shredded hash browns, thawed
2 14.5 oz cans white navy and/or black beans (optional)

Directions:
Combine first four ingredients then stir in potatoes and optional beans. Pour into 9x13 casserole, resist the temptation to cover, and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to an hour.

Submitter: 
SarahWearsHeadbands
Description: 
A twist on funeral potatoes, (so named for their ubiquitous presence at Mormon funerals). These would belong more at a Salsa club than a funeral, with pepper-jack cheese for kick and beans and plain greek yogurt to decrease their tendency to cause the event they so infamously accompany...
