Title:
Beet Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups cooked and finely chopped beets
4 cups sugar
2 cups vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. lemon jc.
2 pkg Knox gelatin

Directions:
Cook and finely chop beets in food processor
Boil all ingredients together x 20 min.
Mix gelatin with 1/2 cup water.
Add to beets & boil another 7 min.
Put in jars.
Makes 4 pints

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Just the right hint of sweetness in this relish
