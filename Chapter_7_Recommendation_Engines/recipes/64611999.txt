Title:
Grandma's Peach cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can of peaches in heavy syrup
1, 1/2 stick of unsalted butter
1, 1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup all purose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 pinch of salt
Teaspoon of Cinamon
Teaspoon of nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Melt 1 stick of butter and put into empty casserole dish.
In a large bowl mix canned peaches, remaining butter,  1/2 cup of white sugar, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon  and nutmeg together. Place inside buttered casserole dish
(For the crust) In large mixing bowl place 1 cup flour, 1 cup sugar, pinch salt, 1 tablespoon baking powder and 1 cup milk together. Mix until well blended.
Pour crust mixture over peaches.
Bake at 350 for 45min until golden brown
