Title:
Sweet & Spicy Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659123.jpg
Ingredients:
-12 ounces of cooked chicken (shredded or cubed)
-5 Tablespoons fat free mayonnaise
-6 Tablespoons barbecue sauce
-1/3 Cup chopped onions (can caramelize for fuller flavor)
-1/2 Tablespoon Brown Sugar
-1/4 Teaspoon of crushed red pepper
-1/4 Teaspoon black pepper
-dash of Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Storing in fridge overnight will allow flavor to reach its maximum potential. This recipe makes approximately six 1/2 cup servings.

Submitter: 
Sarah Meedel
Description: 
A quick and easy and flavorful twist on an old favorite. Great for a quick lunch on warm days, pairs great with low-fat/low carb bread with lettuce and onion.
