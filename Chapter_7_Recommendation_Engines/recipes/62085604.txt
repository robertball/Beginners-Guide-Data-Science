Title:
Craig's Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 package fully cooked sausage, sliced
1 diced onion
1 15 oz. can of tomato sauce
1 tablespoon cajun seasoning, or to taste
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
2 teaspoons worcester sauce
1 15 oz. can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat.  Add onion and sausage and cook until onion is transparent.  Add tomato sauce and spices; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer covered for 10 minutes.  Add rice and heat through.

Submitter: 
Stephanie Zartman
Description: 
This was one of my husband's favorite things to cook back in his bachelor days.  It's not fancy, but it's simple to make. Great with cornbread on a cold day.  I've also used browned ground sausage for this recipe.  I've had to omit the cajun seasoning before and it was still good.
