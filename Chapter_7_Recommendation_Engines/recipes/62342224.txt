Title:
deep fried asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
bunddle fresh asparagus
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups biscuit mix
1/2 cup grated parmeasan cheese
tsp salt and pepper
ranch dressing
2 cups of oil

Directions:
heat oil in frying pan large enogh to hold asparagus. mix milk and eggs together in 1 bowl and biscuit mix, parmeasan cheese, salt, pepper in another. dip asparagus in milk mixter then in the other. repeat. and put in oil cook until golden brown. remove and serve with ranch for dipping.

Description: 
a fancier version of the other fried asparagus on here more like for an appetizer.
