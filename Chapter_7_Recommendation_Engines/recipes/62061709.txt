Title:
Cobb Salad with Brown Derby French Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 head iceberg lettuce, chopped
1 bunch watercress, leaves only chopped
3 medium tomatoes, chopped
3/4 pound slice turkey breast from deli, diced
3 hardboiled eggs, chopped
3/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
2 avocados, diced
1/2 pound lean bacon, fried and crumbled
Dressing:
1/2 cup water
1/2 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/3 cup olive oil
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine dressing ingredients.  Place chopped lettuce and watercress in large wide salad bowl.  Put other ingredients in strips on top of lettuce.  When ready to serve, add desired amount of dressing and toss.

Description: 
A great main dish salad recipe that has a pleasing presentation.  Serve with crusty bread for a filling meal.
