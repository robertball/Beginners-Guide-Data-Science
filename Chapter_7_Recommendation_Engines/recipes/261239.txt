Title:
Easy Pumpkin Pie Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4610328.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin pie filling
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Beat sugar and cream cheese together in a bowl using an electric mixer on medium speed. Add pumpkin pie filling, cinnamon, and ginger. Beat well. Cover with plastic wrap and chill for 8 hours to overnight.

Submitter: 
Tracy
Description: 
I received this recipe from a friend. And the dip was fabulous. Hope you all love it too! Serve with ginger snaps and crisp apple and pear slices.
