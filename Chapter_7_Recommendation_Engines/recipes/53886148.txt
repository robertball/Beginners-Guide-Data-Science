Title:
Kelly's Chinese Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage, shredded
1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles, uncooked, crushed
1 cup sliced almond or cashew pieces, toasted
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 large can mandarin oranges
Dressing
1/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1/3 cup rice vinegar
1 Tbs. soy sauce *optional
pepper to taste

Directions:
In a preheated 350 degree F oven (175 degree C), toast the crushed noodles and nuts until golden brown.
In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, green onions, and almonds.
To prepare the dressing, whisk together the sugar, oil, vinegar and soy sauce. Pour the dressing over the salad, toss.
Top with mandarin oranges and ramen noodles.
