Title:
Curried Sweet Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Sweet Potatoes, peeled and cubed.
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cups baby carrots
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
3/4 teaspoon of cinnamon
1 teaspoon red curry powder (any curry powder will do)
5 cups chicken broth
2 tablespoons real maple syrup
1 cup of half and half or light cream

Directions:
Place all ingredients (except for maple syrup and cream) in a crock pot (slow cooker)
Stir well and cover
Cook on low for 7 hours
Puree soup in a blender
Return to crock pot
Add cream and maple syrup
Add salt and pepper, to taste
Cover and cook on high for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
Food Eater
Description: 
A delicious blend of sweet potatoes, onion and carrots with just the right spices for a perfect autumn soup.
