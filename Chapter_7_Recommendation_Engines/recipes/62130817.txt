Title:
Marinade for Grilled Meat, Shrimp or Poultry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 ounce can of tomato paste
1/2 cup red wine, more if thinner marinade is desired
6 cloves of garlic finely minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1 t olive oil
Cracked black pepper
Sea Salt

Directions:
Mix tomato paste, wine, garlic, basil, olive oil and cracked black pepper together. Coat meat with marinade and refrigerate for 4- 6 hours.  Coat seafood with marinade and place in refrigerator for 1 hour.  Before grilling remove some of the marinade from meat or seafood with a paper towel, leaving a thin coating of marinade. Season with salt to taste. Grill according to your favorite recipe.

Submitter: 
Juanita/Jane
Description: 
This simple marinade is perfect for chicken, lamb, beef, pork or shrimp. Marinate a butterflied leg of lamb, split cornish hens or shrimp on a skewer, or any of your favorite grilled recipes will be fabulous with this marinade. Use  a heavy coating for hearty meats and a lighter application for delicate seafood. Enjoy!
