Title:
Old as the hills-Baking Powder Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798004.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sifted flour
4 tsp.baking powder
1/2 tsp.salt
4 Tbsp.shortening,chilled
2/3-3/4 cup milk or water

Directions:
Sift together the dry ingredients-twice.
Cut the chilled shortening finely into the dry  ingredients.
Add the liquid slowly,stirring untill soft ball forms.
Turn onto lightly floured board,toss till sparingly coated with flour.
Knead lightly for a few seconds.
Roll out 1/2-3/4 inch in thickness.
Cut with floured biscuit cutter or desired shape.
Place on greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 450 for 12-15 min.
