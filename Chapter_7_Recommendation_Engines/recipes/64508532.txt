Title:
Italian Ragu  Bolognese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 large sweet onion, diced
1 cup shredded carrots
3 tablespoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon dried oregano
2 teaspoons dried crushed thyme
1 1/2 tablespoons dried basil
3 tablespoons dried parsley
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons black pepper
1 cup roasted red pepper, diced
2 pounds boneless chuck steak--cut into 1/2 cubes
2-28 ounce cans petite diced tomatoes, drained
1-28 ounce can tomato puree
1-6 ounce  can tomato paste
2 tablespoons beef base
3 bay leaves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a large dutch oven, cook the onions, carrots, garlic, dried herbs, salt, and pepper in the olive oil over medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes or until translucent and softened.  Add the roasted red peppers and cook for another 5 minutes.  Add the remaining ingredients and stir until combined.  Cover with an oven proof lid or aluminum foil.
Bake in the oven for 2-2.5 hours or until the beef is tender.  Remove the bay leaves.  Serve over your favorite pasta.

Submitter: 
JRS_cook
Description: 
Rich and hearty, this version utilizes beef cubes as opposed to ground meat.  Once made any leftovers can easily be frozen and reheated for use later on.
