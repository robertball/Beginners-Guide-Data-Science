Title:
Chicken Enchiladas I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277528.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground chicken
1 onion, chopped
1/2 pint sour cream
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 clove garlic, minced
8 (10 inch) flour tortillas
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium, non-stick skillet over medium heat, cook chicken. Drain excess fat. Add the onion, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, parsley, oregano and ground black pepper. Heat until cheese melts. Stir in salt, tomato sauce, water, chili powder, and garlic.
Place even amounts on each tortilla and fold in half.  Arrange in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with 3/4 cup Cheddar cheese. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven 20 minutes. Cool 10 minutes before serving.
