Title:
Old Post Office House Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic
1-1/2 cups vegetable oil
1/2 cup honey
1/2 apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
chives (dried)
sunflower seeds, several tablespoons

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, blend all ingredients except for chives and sun flower kernals.
When well blended, stir in chives and sun flower kernals.
Place in airtight container and refrigerate, until well chilled or overnight before using.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
This sweet and tangy was the star of the salad bar at The Old Post Office restaurant and bar in Fayetteville, AR, back in the 70's and 80's.  It's just as wonderful drizzled over a fruit salad as it is over mixed greens.
