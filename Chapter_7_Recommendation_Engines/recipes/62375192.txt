Title:
Green Bean and Cheese Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can French fried onions
2 cans regular or French cut green beans, drained
1 box of Velveeta cheese (or 2 C. shredded cheddar)
1 can of cream of celery soup
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°
Mix green beans, cheese, soup, and milk in med. Mixing bowl
Pour ingredients into baking dish using only 1/3 of the can of onions,
1/2 of all other ingredients
Bake for 30 minutes. Remove from oven.
Add remaining beans, milk, soup, and most of cheese to make another layer.
Top with cheese then pour the remaining 2/3 the can of onions on top.
Bake for ten minutes. Let stand and cool for about 5-8 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This recipe takes an old favorite and adds cheese. And what recipe can't be improved by adding a little cheese? :)
