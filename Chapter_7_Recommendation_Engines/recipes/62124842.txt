Title:
Bakery Graham Cracker Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
2 T sugar
2 T melted butter
Custard:
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/3 tsp salt
2 1/2 cup scalded milk
4 egg yolks
2 T butter
1 tsp vanilla
Meringue:
4 egg whites
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Crust:
Mix ingredients well and press into 9" pie plate, reserving a couple tablespoons for garnish.
Custard:
In double boiler, blend sugar, cornstarch, & salt.
Slowly stir in hot milk until mixture thickens.
Add 1/3 of mixture slowly to beaten egg yolks.
Add remaining mixture and cook 2 minutes.  Cool
Stir in butter and vanilla
Pour into graham cracker pie shell.
Meringue:
Beat ingredients until stiff peaks form.
Spread over custard and sprinkle with reserved crust.
Brown meringue in 300 degree oven for approx 10 min
