Title:
No Bake Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Sugar
4 tblsp Cocoa
1 Stick Butter
1/2 Cup Milk
1Cup Peanut Butter
1 tblsp Vanilla
3 Cups Oatmeal
Waxed Paper

Directions:
In heavy saucepan bring to a boil...Sugar, Cocoa, Butter and Milk.  Let boil for 1min then add Peanut Butter, Vanilla and Oatmeal.  Drop teaspoonful of mixture on waxed paper till cooled and hardened.
