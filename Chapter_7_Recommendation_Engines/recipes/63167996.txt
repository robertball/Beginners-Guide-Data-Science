Title:
Best King Ranch Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 precooked chicken breasts (or a skinned and deboned store bought rotisserie chicken)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 can rotel
1 can chopped green chilies
6-8 corn tortillas torn into strips
2 - 3 cups shredded monterey jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together the 3 soups, onion, undrained rotel, chilies and shredded or cubed chicken.
Layer tortillas, soup mixture then cheese.
Do this twice ending with cheese on top.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until goden brown and bubbly around the sides.
If it's runny, just let it sit for a few minutes and it will firm up a bit as it cools.
