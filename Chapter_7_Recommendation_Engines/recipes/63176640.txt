Title:
So Easy So Yummy Scallop Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp.margarine
10 large potatoes
1 cup chopped onion
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can cheddar cheese soup
1 cup cheddar cheese,grated
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Spread margarine all over casserole dish.
In large plastic bowl combine the three soup,mix well.Warm in microwave for 3 minutes,stir well.
Peel and wash  potatoes.Cut length wise and slice.Layer half the sliced potatoes into casserole,top with half the chopped onion and half of the soup mixture.Sprinkle with pepper.Layer the rest of the sliced potatoes with the remaining chopped onion and the soup mixture.Sprinkle with pepper and the grated cheddar cheese.
Bake for about 2 hours.

Description:
