Title:
Apple Brunch Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/821698.jpg
Ingredients:
Ready to use pizza crust
4 baking apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
2 Tbsp flour

Directions:
With a pastry blender mix the butter, sugar, cinnamon, and flour together. Set aside.
Grease the pan and press pizza dough into it.
Arrange apple slices on crust, then cover with topping mixture.
Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
I started making this when I was in highschool and wanted something sweet and easy when my friends came over. All these years later I still make it for my own kids. It's wonderful for a late morning brunch or a special late night treat to share with your children.
