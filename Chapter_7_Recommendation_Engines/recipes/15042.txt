Title:
Ambrosial Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/82681.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups water
3/4 cup chocolate-covered crispy peanut butter candy
1/3 cup honey
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3 tablespoons dry milk powder
4 cups bread flour
2 3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients into the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Sweet Bread setting or White Bread setting.  Start.

Submitter: 
Johnny Williams
Description: 
This is a sweet, dense bread. It goes good with almost anything.
