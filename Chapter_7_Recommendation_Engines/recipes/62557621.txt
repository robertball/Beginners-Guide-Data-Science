Title:
Teriyaki Pork Chops! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup Teriyaki Sauce
3/4 tbls. garlic salt
1/2 tbls. ground black pepper
1 tbls. extra vigin olive oil
3 pork chops

Directions:
Start by mixing first 3 ingredients together in a bowl. Unthaw pork chops and place on a plate. Pour 3/4 mixture over pork chops, flip and do the same to the underside. Let sit for 30 min. Heat up saucepan and pour in oil. Warm up oil and add pork chops. Place lid over pan. After 15 min. flip pork chops over. After the next 10 min. lift lid and pour remaining mixture on top of pork chop. Put lid back on let simmer for 5 min. Ta Da!
