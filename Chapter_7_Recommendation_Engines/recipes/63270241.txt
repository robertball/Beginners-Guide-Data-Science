Title:
Sun Dried Tomato Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbs sun dried tomatoes
1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
1 lb organic ground turkey
1 tbs balsamic vinegar
3 tbs fresh basil, chopped
1 tbs garlic, minced
1 1/2 tsp dijon mustard
pinch of sea salt & pepper

Directions:
Cover the sun dried tomatos in warm water to soak until soft, about 10 minutes.
Drain and chop the tomatoes into small pieces.
Combine with remaining ingredients and form into 4 patties.
Grill, pan sear, or bake in the oven @375* for 8 minutes. Serve with a mixed greens salad.
