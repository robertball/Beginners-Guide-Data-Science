Title:
Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/552638.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Yukon potatoes
1/4 cup sour cream
1/3 cup milk
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Clean, peel, and chop potatoes.
Boil potatoes in pan for 20 minutes. In the meantime, prep milk, sour cream, salt and pepper.
Drain potatoes, mash and add all other ingredients. Enjoy!
