Title:
Rhubarb Sour Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups rhubarb (in 1 inch lengths)
1 unbaked 9 inch pie shell
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1/3 cup flour
1 cup sour cream
Topping:
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened

Directions:
Place rhubarb in pie shell.
Stir sugar and flour together in small bowl. Mix in sour cream. Pour over rhubarb.
Mix topping ingredients together until crumbly. Sprinkle over pie.
Bake on bottom shelf in oven at 425 F for 10 minutes.
Reduce heat to 350 F and bake about 40-50 minutes (until rhubarb is cooked and topping is browned).
