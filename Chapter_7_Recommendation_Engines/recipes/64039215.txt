Title:
Kami's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lasagna Noodles
2 cans tomatoes
2 cans tomato sauce
oregano
italian seasoning
1 lb. ground beef
2 bags Mozzerella Cheese

Directions:
1. Blend tomatoes and tomato sauce until it is slightly chunky.
2. Brown meat and then add tomato sauce.  Cook until bubbly.  Add oregano and italian seasoning.  Cook about 5 more minutes.
3. Layer lasagna in 9x13 pyrex.  Sauce, Noodles, Cheese, Sauce, Noodles, Cheese.
