Title:
Microwave Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 can condensed milk
6 squares semi-sweet chocolate

Directions:
Put butter, brown sugar, condensed milk and vanilla in a microwavable bowl.
Microwave on high for a total of 11 minutes, but stirring every 3 minutes (the last time will only be 2 minutes). Make sure to stir the butter into the mixture each time.
Remove from microwave and stir in the chocolate squares. Keep stirring until melted.
Use a mixer and beat on high for 2 minutes.
Pour into buttered container(s).
Refrigerate until set.
