Title:
Sundried Tomato Turkey Panini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of Ciabatta bread
2 slices of provolone cheese
2-3 slices of deli turkey
2 tbs. of Kraft Sun-dried Tomato Vinegarette Dressing
2 tbs. of mayonaise
enough olive oil to coat outside of bread

Directions:
Spread inside of bread slices with mayo and dressing; 1 tbs. on each side.
Top with 1 slice of cheese, then turkey, then another slice of cheese.
Top that with other slice of bread.
Coat outside of slices with olive oil and grill in a panini press until browned.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is a simple sandwich I came up with one evening when I was tired of plain old turkey and cheese.  It's absolutely delicious and rivals those made in those upscale sandwich shops!!
