Title:
Roast Pork Loin with Apple Topping (or Stuffing!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mustard Caraway Rub
1/2 tsp. sugar
2 tbsp. flour
1-1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. caraway seeds
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. ground sage
4-5 lb. pork loin roast
Topping:
1-1/2 cups minced apple
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. mace
1/4 tsp. salt
small amount of water for gravy

Directions:
To make rub - Combine ingredients and rub into pork.  Place fat side up in a roasting pan and bake at 325 for 1.5 hours.
To prepare topping - Combine ingredients and spread over roast.  Bake 1 hour longer, adding a little water to pan to make excellent gravy
