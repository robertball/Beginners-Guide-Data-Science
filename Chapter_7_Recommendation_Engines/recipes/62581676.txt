Title:
Okra Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 lbs small okra
1/3 c canning salt
2 tsp dill seed
3 c H2O
3 c vinegar
4 garlic cloves
2 small hot red peppers cut in half

Directions:
Trim stems off okra. Mix salt, dill, water & vinegar & bring to a boil. Pack okra into prepared jars leaving 1/4 in head space. Put 1 clove of garlic & 1/2 pepper into each jar. Ladle liquid to 1/4 in head space. Remove air & process 15 min.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
So yummy & the perfect way to store all those beautiful okra from your summer garden.
