Title:
Best Bread Machine Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1108623.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 1/4 tsp bread machine yeast
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 cups bread flour
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place the water, sugar and yeast in the pan of the bread machine. Let the yeast dissolve and foam for 10 minutes. Add the oil, flour and salt to the yeast. Select Basic or White Bread setting, and press Start.

Submitter: 
Mike
Description: 
This bread recipe for your bread machine is very easy-to-follow to the point of being foolproof, delivering a soft bread with a flaky crust.Modified from: Best Bread Machine Bread
