Title:
Texas Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground chuck
1/2 lb chili meat
1/2 lb coarsely chopped stew meat
1 white onion chopped
6 large heads of garlic finely chopped
1/2 cup chopped celery
3 roasted chili peppers - chopped
4 sundried tomatoes - chopped
3 red jalapenos - chopped
1 capful apple cider vinegar
1 heaping tablespoon oregano
2 heaping tablespoons cumin
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon paprika
2 tablespoons chili powder - or to desired color
6 bay leaves
2 cans of rotel tomatoes
Shredded cheddar cheese
Crackers

Directions:
Brown ground chuck, chili meat and coarsly chopped stew meat.  Add onion and garlic and sautee until onion is translucent.  Stir in one capful of apple cider vinager.  Drain meat and transfer to large sauce pan.  combine all other ingredients and, for best results, simmer for several hours.
Top with cheese and serve with crackers or fritos for frito pie.

Submitter: 
Colleen Kain
Description: 
This is a Texas recipe is not for the faint of heart, it is very spicey, but you can adjust the seasonings to your taste.
