Title:
Italian Dijon Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 T Dijon mustard
1 1/2 T olive oil
1 clove garlic= minced
1/2 t tarragon
1 c. bread crumbs
2 chicken breats
salt & pepper

Directions:
Lightly spray baking pan. Whisk mustard, olive oil, garlic & tarragon. Season chicken with salt & pepper and spread over both sides of chicken, dip in bread crumbs. Bake in oven for 10 minutes then  turn. Take out when juices runs clear
