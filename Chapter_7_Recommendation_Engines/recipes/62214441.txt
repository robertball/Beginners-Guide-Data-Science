Title:
Twisted Hot Dog Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hot dogs , cut into 1/4-inch slices
2 (15 ounce) can maple baked beans
1 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
1 (9 inch) deep dish pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Bake empty pie shell for 10 minutes.
In a microwave bowl, combine hot dogs, beans, and mustard. Microwave on high for 2 to 3 minutes.
Spread bean mixture into baked pie shell.
Bake for 5 to 10 minutes, or until steamy.
