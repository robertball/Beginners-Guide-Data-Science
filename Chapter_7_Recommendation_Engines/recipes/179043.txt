Title:
Skillet Sweet and Tangy Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981556.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 pork chops, 1/2 inch thick
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Tomato Soup (Regular or 25% Less Sodium)
1 tablespoon packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
4 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.  Add chops and cook until browned.
Add soup, sugar, vinegar and Worcestershire.  Heat to a boil.  Cover and cook over low heat 10 minutes or until done.  Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Sear and simmer pork chops to tender perfection in an easy, flavorful sauce.
