Title:
Coconut Banana Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1960245.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup wholemeal (wholewheat) flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 medium eggs
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup coconut cream
1 1/2 very ripe bananas, mashed
1/2 cup fine desiccated coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200C (400F), fan-forced 170C (340F) and grease muffin tins.
Lightly beat eggs and add in coconut cream and butter.
Mix in flour, baking powder, mashed banana and fine desiccated coconut. Mix until combined.
Spoon into prepared muffin tins.
Bake for 15-17 minutes or until muffin tops are golden and toothpick comes out clean. Cool on rack.
