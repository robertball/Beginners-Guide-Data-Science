Title:
Michele's Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668742.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower
1 small red onion (thinly sliced)
1 cup frozen peas
1 small jar (15 oz) of hellmans mayo
1 tbsp of McCormick Salad Supreme seasoning
1 16oz package of shredded chedder cheese
1 lb of crumbled bacon

Directions:
layer ingredients in order in a medium sided bowl.
place in the fridge for at least an hour before serving.
Mix the salad once ready to be enjoyed by friends and family.

Submitter: 
Kelli Jo
Description: 
This recipe I got from my mother-in-law they make it a lot for bbq's and holidays. Now I make this all the time. a very simple layered salad easy to make and taste great.
