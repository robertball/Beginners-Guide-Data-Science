Title:
Easy and Delicious Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/655471.jpg
Ingredients:
1 270 ml jar oil packed sun dried tomatoes
2 28 ounce cans ground plum tomatoes
1 300 ml jar roasted red peppers, drained
1 large onion
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp minced garlic
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp salt
1 tbsp brown sugar
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 tbsps minced fresh basil OR 2 tsps dried oregano

Directions:
Drain sun dried tomatoes, place in a bowl and cover with boiling water, let sit for 5 minutes.
Drain sun dried tomatoes.
Place the onion, sun dried tomatoes, canned tomatoes and drained red peppers in a food processor, process until smooth.
In a large saucepan heat the olive oil over medium heat, saute the garlic for 1 minute.
Stir in the tomato mixture and cook for 5 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients and cook for another 5 minutes or until desired thickness.
Remove from heat and let cool. Sauce can be refrigerated for up to 2 weeks or frozen for future use.
I use Pizza Dough I and prepare it in a 11x15 inch pan.

Description: 
Easy to make homemade pizza sauce, enough for 4 pizzas, freeze extra for pizza at any time.
