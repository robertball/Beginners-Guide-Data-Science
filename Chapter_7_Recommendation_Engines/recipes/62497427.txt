Title:
Spicy Pasta with Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet potato, peeled into 3/4 inch cubes (2 cups)
1/2 tsp each sugar, chili powder, and cinnamon
8 oz dried rigatoni
1/3 cup PB
3oz cream cheese cut up
2 tsp Asian chili sauce
1 tbsp soy sauce
6 green onions sliced thinly

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 450.  Oil a rimmed baking pan:  set aside. Place sweet potato cubes in bowl. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, the sugar, chili powder, and cinnamon.  Spread in pan and bake 20 mins or until tender.
2. Cook pasta.  Drain and reserve 1 cup pasta water.
3. In saucepan combine PB, cream cheese, chili sauce, and soy sauce; whisk in 3/4 cup of the hot pasta water.  Stir over medium heat until heated through.  If too thick, stir in additional water.  Stir in green onions.  Serve over pasta with sweet potatoes.  Also try with shrimp or chicken.
