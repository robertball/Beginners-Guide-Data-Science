Title:
Savory Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. Italian sausage links
2 lg. onions, chopped
6 garlic cloves, minced
2 cans (28 oz. each) tomato sauce or puree
2 tsp. dried basil
1 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
4 tsp. sugar
Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, cook ground beef and sausage until meat is browned; drain. Slice sausage into thick slices and set aside. In same pan, saute onion for 5 or 6 minutes or until semi-translucent. Add garlic and saute 2-3 minutes. Add the meats and remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 45 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
Sue S.
Description: 
This sauce is a snap to make and freezes very well. The recipe doubles or triples easily, and can be varied with any number of ingredients - green pepper, pork ribs, neck bones, roast beef, meatballs, a splash of wine. You can even add some chili powder, cumin, green chilies and beans for a quickie southwestern pasta sauce.
