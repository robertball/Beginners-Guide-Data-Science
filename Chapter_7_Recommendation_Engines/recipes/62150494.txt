Title:
Stacie's Awesome Fruity Grilled veggies. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Zucchinis
3 Squash
1 Med onion
1 lrg. orange.
1 lemon
1 stick of butter
Lemon pepper seasoning
salt
pepper
Aluminium foil

Directions:
Slice veggies and fruits
slice butter into little pieces
Pull 2 large pieces of heavy duty aluminium foil
Lay the foil out in a criss croos shape.
Place veggies and fruits in the middle of foil.
Sprinkle with salt , pepper, and lemon pepper- to taste.
Wrap and place on grill while cooking your meat.
Enjoy
