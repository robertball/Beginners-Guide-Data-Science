Title:
Grandma's Five Cup Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/210392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
2 cups miniature marshmallows
1 cup flaked coconut
6 ounces sour cream

Directions:
Combine the oranges, pineapple, marshmallows, coconut, and sour cream. Stir till mixed well. Refrigerate till cold. Serve.

Description:
