Title:
Easy Chicken Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472564.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. all-purpose flour
3/4 t. seasoned salt
1-1/4 c. crushed cornflakes
1/3 c. butter, melted
1-1/2 lbs. boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch strips

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, combine flour and seasoned salt. Place conrflakes and butter in separate shallow bowls. Coat chicken w/ flour mixture, then dip in butter and coat w/cornflakes. Transfer to an ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 400 for 15-20 minutes.
