Title:
Baked Elbow Pasta with Italian Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/892164.jpg
Ingredients:
Pasta, two packages, cooked and drained
1 medium jar of prepared pasta sauce
1 package Al Fresco Natural Sweet Italian Chicken
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
Olive oil
Butter
Salt

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.  Drain and add butter and salt to taste.
Cut sausage into cubes and fry in 1 tablespoon of olive oil, until browned.
Add sauce to pasta, and about 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese.  Mix in sausage.
Put pasta mixture in a deep baking dish.  Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.  Cover with shredded mozzarella cheese.
Bake in the oven for 30 - 45 minutes until cheese is melted and lightly browned.
