Title:
Best Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans green beans, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (6 ounce) can French fried onions
3/4 cup milk
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix all ingredients, except fried onions, well and put in a casserole dish; sprinkle with French fried onions.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes.

Description: 
This great variation of the traditional green bean casserole is topped with French fried onions and Cheddar cheese.Modified from: Best Green Bean Casserole
