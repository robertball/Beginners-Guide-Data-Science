Title:
Fluffy Hot Chocolate - Quick version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoons baking cocoa
1 cup milk
10 miniature marshmallows
1 teaspoon chocolate syrup

Directions:
In a microwave-safe mug, combine the first 3 ingredients. Microwave about 1 minute.  Stir in marshmallows and chocolate syrup.  Microwave about 30 more seconds or until hot.
