Title:
Raspberry Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-14oz can sweetened condensed milk
2/3 cup frozen concentrate, thawed, undiluted
1-8oz whipped cream, thawed
1 cup fresh raspberries, divided
1-6oz graham cracker crust

Directions:
1. In a large bowl, combine sweetened condensed milk and lemonade; mix well. Fold in whipped cream.
2. Spoon 1/2 cup raspberries into bottom of crust. top with lemonade filling. Freeze 6 hours.
3. Top with remaining raspberries prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Samantha Oaks
Description: 
Creamy no-bake pie with fresh raspberries.
