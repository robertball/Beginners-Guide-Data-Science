Title:
Ground Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 tsp minced garlic
3 Tbsp butter
2 cups beef broth
1 cup sour cream
1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz chopped mushrooms
1/4 cup flour
Salt and pepper to taste
1 pkg egg noodles

Directions:
Brown ground beef; drain and set aside. Melt butter and saute onions, mushrooms and garlic. Add flour to pan and stir. Add beef broth, stir well to remove lumps. Bring to boil for 2-3 minutes until thickened slightly. Stir in sour cream, soup and pepper. Add beef and cook until hot. Cook egg noodles according to package. Serve beef over noodles.
