Title:
The Real Deal Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. Ground Round
1 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
1 cup ketchup
1 (8 ounce can) tomato sauce
1 1/2 Tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 Tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 Tablespoons prepared mustard
1 Tablespoon brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
Hamburger buns

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add meat, onion and green pepper.  Cook untgil meat is brown.  Drain.  Add ketchup and next 7 ingredients; stir well.  Cook uncovered, over medium heat, 10 minutes or until thoroughly heated and slightly thickened, stirring frequently.
Serve on toasted hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
My mom got this recipe from her home economics teacher when she was in high school. There is no comparison to this and anything canned. We love it for a weekend hot sandwich with baked sweet potato fries or with potato chips.  It's a crowd pleaser and well worth the effort.
