Title:
ROMAS BBQ SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 CUPS KETCHUP
2 1/2 CUP TOMATO SAUCE
2 TBSP.PREPARED MUSTARD
1 CUP BROWN SUGAR
2 TBSP.PEPPER
1 CUP RED WINE VINEGAR
5 TBSP.STEAK SAUCE
1 TBSP.TABASCO SAUCE
6 TBSP.WORCESTERSHIRE
6 TBSP.SOYA SAUCE
1/2 CUP CANOLA OIL
1 1/2 CUPS FINELY CHOPPED ONION
3 TBSP.LIQUID SMOKE
1 TBSP.SALT
1 CAN BEEF BROTH

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large sauce pan.
Bring to a boil,reduce heat and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
Strain,store in fridge or can be frozen.
Makes 7 cups.
