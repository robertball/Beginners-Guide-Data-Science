Title:
Lentil Quinoa Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown lentils
1 cup quinoa, rinsed
1 Red Bell Pepper chopped
1 Onion chopped
4 cups water
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Put all the ingredients in the slow cooker, cook for 7-9 hours. Taste and add more seasoning if desired.

Submitter: 
Lindsay Thorpe
Description: 
An easy to throw together crock pot meal, vegan and gluten free and yummy.
