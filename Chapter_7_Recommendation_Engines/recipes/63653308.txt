Title:
Banana Bread with Spent Grain Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Spent Grain
1/4 cup vital wheat gluten
1/4 cup oat flour
1/4 cup flaxseed meal
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
5 tablespoons butter
3/4 cup xylitol
2 very-ripe bananas, peeled
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup pecans or walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together the flours, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
In a mixing bowl, cream the butter and xylitol together. Add the eggs and banana and stir to incorporate
Add the flour mixture to the batter, in two or three parts.  Mix until well combined. Fold in the nuts.
Pour batter into a well-buttered loaf, cake or muffin pan. Bake one hour or until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean, but don't over cook.
Let cool. Enjoy!
