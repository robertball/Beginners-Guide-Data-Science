Title:
Aunt Rocky's Low Carb Peppermint Mocha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4459263.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz. strong coffee
2 Tbsp. heavy cream (or Almond/Coconut milk for dairy free)
3 Tbsp. Walden Farms Chocolate Syrup
2-3 drops Peppermint Extract (or to taste)
1-2 drops liquid Sucralose (or sweetener of choice)

Directions:
Mix all together in a large coffee mug.
Adjust any of the ingredients to your own taste.
Enjoy.
