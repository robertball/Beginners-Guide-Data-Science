Title:
Chicken Broccoli Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz cooked broccoli
4 cups cooked rice
1 onion sliced and cooked soft
10 mushrooms sliced and cooked
1 1/2 cups frozen corn
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 1/2 cup water
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Warm up soup and water and add the cheese till melted in a large bowl. Add all the other ingredients and mix well. Pour in 9x13 inch casserole dish and bake in 350 degree oven for 45 min.
