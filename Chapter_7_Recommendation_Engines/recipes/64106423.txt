Title:
Szechuan shredded pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5lbs pork shoulder blade steak
1 can cream of mushroom
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup dry sherry
1 tbsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 cup diagonally sliced carrot
1/2 medium sweet red or green pepper, cut into 2" julienne strips
Rice

Directions:
Remove bone from steak; thinly slice steak, discarding excess fat. Set aside.
In small bowl, combine soup, water, sherry, soy sauce, ginger and crushed red pepper.
Stir fry carrot and pepper strips until tender crisp. Transfer to bowl.
Stir fry pork half at a time, until pork is no longer pink. Stir in cooked vegetables and soup mixture. Heat to boiling. Serve over rice.
