Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
filling:
2 cans cream of chix soup
3 cups cooked diced chix
1 bag frozen peas and carrots mix
1 cup frozen cubed hashbrowns or can of potatoes
crust:
1 cup flour
1 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1 c milk
1/3 c melted butter

Directions:
spray 9 x 13 pan with pam
mix filling and pour in pan
mix crust and pour over filling
bake 1 hour at 350 degrees
