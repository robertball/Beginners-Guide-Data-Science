Title:
Irish Whisper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2828550.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces whiskey
1/2 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1 splash peppermint schnapps
1 splash club soda

Directions:
Fill a glass with ice, and pour in the whiskey, coffee liqueur, peppermint schnapps, and soda; stir to mix.
