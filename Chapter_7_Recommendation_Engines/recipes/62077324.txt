Title:
Gluten-Free Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling
7-9 baking apples (Granny Smith, Johnathan, McIntosh, Rome, or Winesap)peeled, cored and thinly sliced
3 Tablespoons Minute Quick Tapioca (in the red box, by the pudding mixes)
3/4 white sugar
1-2 teaspoons (to taste) ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
Topping Ingredients
3/4 cup Oats
3/4 cup Brown Sugar
3/4 (1& 1/2 stick) margarine
dash of cinnamon
dash of salt

Directions:
Prepare apples, making sure to peel, core and thinly slice them. Put apples and the rest of the filling ingredients into a 13x9 baking dish. Stir to combine and let sit for 15-20 minutes, while you make the topping.
For topping, combine the oatmeal, brown sugar, margarine and dashes of salt and cinnamon. Cut mixture together until well blended, and almost crumbly. Sprinkle over top of fruit filling and bake at 350 F for about 50 minutes, or until thick bubbles appear in juice from apples.

Submitter: 
GENELLEB
Description: 
Cooking gluten-free goodies can be tough. Here's a simple recipe for a great apple crisp, that's wheat-free, and full of great apple flavor.
