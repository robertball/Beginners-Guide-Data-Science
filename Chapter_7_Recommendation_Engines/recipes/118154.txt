Title:
Cheesy Drop Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987555.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising flour*
1 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients until blended. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto lightly greased baking sheets. BAke at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Marla  Miller
Description: 
It's hard to believe that these buttery bites call for only a few items. 'I keep the ingredients for these biscuits on hand because my sons just love them,' writes Marla Miller from Englewood, Tennessee.
