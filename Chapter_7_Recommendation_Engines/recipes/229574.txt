Title:
Christmas Cosmo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/994194.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces vodka
1 fluid ounce cranberry juice
1 fluid ounce triple sec (orange-flavored liqueur)
2 teaspoons fresh lime juice
1 lime wedge
2 cranberries

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add vodka, cranberry juice, triple sec, and lime juice. Cover and shake. Strain cocktail into a martini glass. Garnish with a lime wedge and cranberries.

Submitter: 
Sheila Parcell Fisher
Description: 
A festive twist on a favorite drink: the cosmo.
