Title:
Michelle's Ultimate Beef Stroganoff Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 cans of cream of mushroom soup
1/2 - 3/4 cup of milk
1 8 oz. brick of cream cheese
1 11 oz. can of either diced carrots OR peas
1 16 oz. package of small egg noodles

Directions:
Season ground beef with salt, pepper and if you like, garlic powder, and fry it in a large skillet. While meat is frying, cook egg noodles per package directions, and drain. When beef is browned,add the cream of mushroom soup and cream cheese and milk and cook til cheese is melted and mixture is saucy. Add this to the noodles and add the can of either peas or carrots, and stir to combine all ingredients and coat the noodles. Serve with garlic bread or bread and butter.

Description: 
Ultimate comfort food that includes egg noodles, cream cheese, cream of mushroom soup, ground beef. Very rich, creamy, and totally satisfying on a chilly night. Super easy and fast for the working mom!! My husband and sons always ask for seconds!!
