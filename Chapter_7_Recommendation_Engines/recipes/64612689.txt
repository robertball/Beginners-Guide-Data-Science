Title:
Gail's Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040856.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of white vinegar
3/4 cup of oil
1tsp salt
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp dry mustard
2 bags of coleslaw

Directions:
Bring all ingredients to a boil
cool and add to the two bags of coleslaw
add 1 cup of sugar on the top of slaw and put in
fridge for 24 hours. Shake and toss during the 24 hrs
