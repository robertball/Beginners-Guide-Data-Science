Title:
Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound uncooked chicken
2 cans Mexican Rotel
1 (15 ounce) can fire-roasted enchilada sauce
1 package of frozen corn, onions, peppers, black beans ("Fiesta" or "Santa Fe" blends)
2 cloves garlic, minced
30 oz. water
3 table spoons chicken bouillon
1 tablespoon cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Pour in water and chicken bouillon, and season with cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and bay leaf.  Place chicken, rotel, enchilada sauce, frozen vegetables, and garlic into a slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low setting for 6 to 8 hours or on high setting for 3 to 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
A quick, no-fuss version of chicken tortilla soup! All you do is put everything into the slow cooker, and turn it on.Modified from: Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup
