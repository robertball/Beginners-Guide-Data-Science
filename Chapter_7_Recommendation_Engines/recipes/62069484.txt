Title:
Happy  cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup melted butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 cups oatmeal
2 cups rice krispies
1 cup coconut

Directions:
Mix well. Drop by teaspoonful on a baking sheet. Bake at 350Ffor 10-12 minutes or until nicely browned.
