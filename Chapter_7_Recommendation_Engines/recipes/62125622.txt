Title:
Blueberry Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 pkgs. Graham crackers, crushed
½ C butter, melted
½ C sugar
8 oz. Cool Whip
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
1 C powdered sugar
1 (21 oz.) Can blueberry pie filling

Directions:
Mix together Graham crackers, butter, and sugar; press into the bottom of a 9x13 pan.
Chill until firm.
Beat cream cheese and powdered sugar together.
Fold in Cool Whip.
Spoon over chilled crust.
Top with blueberry pie filling.
Chill at least 4 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
FoodFan
Description: 
This is a wonderful cheesecake-type dessert that's great for holidays or potlucks.
