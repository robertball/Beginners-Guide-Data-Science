Title:
Pam's Amazing Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 slices bacon
1 pound lean ground beef
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons fancy molasses
1 19oz can tomatoes
1 14oz can red kidney beans
1 14oz can baked beans in tomato sauce
1/3 cup vinegar
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon Worchestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce

Directions:
In Dutch oven, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Crumble and set aside. Drain fat from pan, increase heat to medium high. Add beef and onion. Cook, breaking up meat with a spoon, for 10 minutes, or until no longer pink.
Return bacon to pan. Add remaining ingredients and stir well. Cover and bake in 300 degree oven for 2 hours. Uncover and bake 1 hour more or until thickened.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
This is the most amazing chili I have ever had!  Recipe was handed down to me by my best friend's mother, and is now the only chili we make - always served with homemade cornmeal muffins on the side!
