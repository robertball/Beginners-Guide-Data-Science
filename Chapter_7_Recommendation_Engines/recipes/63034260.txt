Title:
Sweet, Sticky and Spicy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves square
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup diced onion
1 cup thinly sliced carrots
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup cashews (optional)
Sauce
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1 tablespoon brown sugar
2 tablespoons honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 cloves minced garlic
2 tablespoons Sriracha Hot Chili Sauce (Rooster Sauce)*

Directions:
Cut chicken in to bite-size pieces. Put flour, salt  and pepper in a quart-size plastic zip-lock bag; shake to coat. Heat oil in fry pan over medium heat; add chicken and brown on both sides; remove chicken from pan and set aside. In same pan, add onion, carrots and garlic; saute until onions are transparent, adding a little more oil, if necessary. Return chicken to the pan; temporarily remove pan from heat. Mix together brown sugar, honey, soy sauce, ginger, garlic and hot sauce in a small bowl. Return pan to heat; pour sauce over chicken; simmer uncovered until the sauce thickens, 8 to 10 minutes; remove from heat. Add cashews.

Submitter: 
Marlene Osborn
Description: 
This delicious dish uses skinless boneless chicken breasts cooked in a gingery, spicy, soy  sauce. Serve it over rice along with a green vegetable to  make a great meal.Modified from: Sweet, Sticky and Spicy Chicken
