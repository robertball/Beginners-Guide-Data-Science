Title:
Kapusta Soup [sauerkraut] Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/795379.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs.pork hocks
1 lb.spareribs,separate
half onion, cut in chunks
2 celery,cut in chunks
1 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 cloves garlic,minced
1 tsp.oregano
1 jar sauerkraut
salt and pepper
1/2 tsp,chili seeds
enough water to cover meat

Directions:
In large dutch oven add meat with  onion and celery chunks,garlic,oregano,salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil,reduce heat and let simmer for 4-5 hours,add more water if needed.
Strain broth and keep to the side,save meat.
Add jar of sauerkraut to large pot,add broth little at a time,the soup shouldn't be thin.Add chopped onion, celery and chilies.Simmer 1 hour.Add meat and cook another 15 min.
