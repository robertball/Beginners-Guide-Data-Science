Title:
Meat Bolognese w/Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Good sized carrots
2 Large stalks of Celery
1 large Onion
1 Can (28 ounces)diced tomatoes
1 Tablespoon vegetable oil
1 1/4 lb (20 ounces)ground beef, or ground chicken, or ground turkey, or ground sausage or sausage in casings, casings removed.
1 Cup dry white wine
1 Cup milk
1 Box (13.5 ounces)Linguine
Grated Parmesan or Romano Cheese

Directions:
Rough chop Carrots, Celery and Onion. Place in food processor and pulse until finely chopped, 5-6 pulses
Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add vegetable mixture and cook, stirring for about 6 minutes
Rinse food processor bowl and set aside
Crumble meat into separate skillet and cook for about 5 minutes. Cool slightly, about 5 minutes
Add meat to food processor and pulse about 3-5 times
Add meat to skillet with vegetables
Pour in wine and simmer for about 10 minutes
Add tomatoes and liquid, reduce heat and cook for about 20 minutes or until most of the liquid has been absorbed.
Stir in the milk and cook for another 8 minutes.
Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions in salted, boiling water for about 12 minutes.
Drain pasta and serve with meat sauce and grated cheese.

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
A hearty meat & pasta dish that doesn't use alot of meat. It's so good, my Carnivore husband doesn't have a clue that there's lots of veggies in it. The subtle layers of flavor will keep your family coming back for helping after helping. And, on top of all this, it's EASY to prepare.
