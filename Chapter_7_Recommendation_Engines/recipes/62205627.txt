Title:
Zesty Manhatten Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans (6.5 oz) minced clams
2 12 oz cans V-8 juice( spicey hot V-8 optional)
2 carrots diced
2 potatoes, cleaned and chopped
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp Thyme
1 can (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes

Directions:
Add clams including juice ,potatoes,carrots, chopped green onions, thyme and black pepper in the slow cooker on low and come home to a treat!

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This Manhatten Clam Chowder has flavor! I make it in a slow cooker but you can do it faster on the stove.
