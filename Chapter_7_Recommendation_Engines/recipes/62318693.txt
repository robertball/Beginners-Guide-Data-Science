Title:
Dijon-Tarragon Cream Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup flour seasoned with salt, pepper, & cayanne
1 shallot finely diced
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup half & half
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
2 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon
1 teaspoon chopped fresh tarragon

Directions:
Melt the butter and heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper, dredge in seasoned flour, and place in the skillet. Brown on both sides. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and continue cooking until chicken juices run clear. Set aside and keep warm.
Stir shallots into pan and saute for 2 minutes.  Add wine and deglaze pan.  Add half & half to the pan, scraping up brown bits. Mix in mustard and 2 teaspoons of tarragon. Cook and stir 5 minutes, or until thickened. Return chicken to skillet to coat with sauce.
Place chicken breasts on bed of rice and pour sauce over chicken. Top with 1 tsp of tarragon to serve.

Submitter: 
John Gallo
Description: 
Chicken breasts bathed in a delicate mustard tarragon sauce. Serve over a bed of white rice.

A quick and simple recipe that you can serve on a weeknight but tastes like a French chef came to your house!Modified from: Dijon-Tarragon Cream Chicken
