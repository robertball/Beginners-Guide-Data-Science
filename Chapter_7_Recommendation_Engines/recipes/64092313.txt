Title:
Anzac Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c shredded coconut
1 c rolled oats
1 c flour
1 c granulated sugar
1 tsp baking soda
pinch salt
1/2 c butter
2 T molasses
2 T water

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
grease cookie sheet
mix dry ingredients
melt butter in medium saucepan and add molasses and water
pour butter mixture into flour and mix well
drop by teaspoon onto cookie sheet
bake 12-15 min
remove from pan and let cool
