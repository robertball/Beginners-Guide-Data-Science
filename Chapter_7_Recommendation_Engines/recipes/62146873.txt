Title:
Sour Cream Chocolate Frosting I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter (melted)
2/3 cup cocoa powder
3/4 cup sour cream
1 to 2 tablespoons milk
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
3 to 5 cups of confections sugar

Directions:
Melt the butter.  Add butter and cocoa powder to bowl and mix.  Add in sour cream and continue to mix.  Add in confections sugar one cup at a time to taste, as frosting thickens, add milk in 1/2 tablespoon increments. Add vanilla and spread on your favorite cake.

Submitter: 
prell2k4
Description: 
A nice rich chocolate frosting.  I don't tend to like frosting made with melted chips, I think cocoa makes for a more chocolate taste.
