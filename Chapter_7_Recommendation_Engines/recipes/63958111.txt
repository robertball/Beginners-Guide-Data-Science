Title:
Tamara's Slow Cooker Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
26 oz. bag of hash browns
28 oz. of fat free chicken broth
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) can of cream of chicken soup.  (98% fat free
1/4 cup onion (I dice half of an onion)
-------
1 pkg.  (8 oz.)  "1/3 less fat" cream cheese
-------
1 cup fat free milk

Directions:
Cook first five ingredients for 4 hours in a crock pot.
Then add the cream cheese (diced) and cook for one more hour
Add the fat free milk and cook for another 15 to 20 minutes

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
Although the ingredients make this a lower fat dish it is a delicious, satisfying soup.
