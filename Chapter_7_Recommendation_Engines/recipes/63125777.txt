Title:
Herbed Peasant Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796613.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Medium Onion, Chopped
3 Tablespoons Butter
9 Ounces Milk, Warmed
1 Tablespoon Sugar
1 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Dill Weed
1/2 Teaspoon Basil, Dried
1/2 Teaspoon Rosemary, Crushed
2 Teaspoons Yeast
3 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour

Directions:
In a skillet over low heat, saute onion in butter until tender, about 8 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes.
Place in Bread Machine container and add other ingredients; place settings on dough.
When machine completes cycle; shape dough into ball and place on a greased baking sheet. Cover and let rise until doubled (approx. 45 minutes).
Bake at 375Â° for 25-30 minutes.
Remove to a wire rack; brush with melted butter. And let cool.
