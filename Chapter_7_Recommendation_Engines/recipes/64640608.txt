Title:
Everybody In The Pool Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4513240.jpg
Ingredients:
½ 7.5 ounce Peaches Canned
½ 7.5 ounce Mandarin Orange Canned (Optional)
½ 7.5 Pineapple Chucks Canned (Optional)
1 Apple
1 Banana
3 Kiwi
1 Mango
10 Strawberries
½ cup Blueberries
½ cup Mandarin Orange (Optional)
½ cup Pineapple (Optional)
¼ cup Stevia Sweetener (Optional)

Directions:
Cut all the fresh fruit.
Add canned fruit to a bowl with liquid.
Add all fruit to bowl and the Stevia Sweetener.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
I use a combination of fresh and canned fruits to make a fruit salad at any time of the year. It depends on what is available either locally or seasonally from other locations.
