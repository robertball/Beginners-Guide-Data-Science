Title:
Shrimp Skewers with Curry Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126194.jpg
Ingredients:
18 to 20 jumbo shrimp (1 to 1 1/2 pounds) peeled and deveined
Olive oil
Lime or lemon wedges
Fresh dill or parsley sprigs
For the Curry Butter
1/2 cup butter
2 tablespoons very finely chopped onion
1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
1 to 1-1/2 teaspoons curry powder
1 clove finely minced garlic

Directions:
To prepare curry butter.
Melt the butter in a small pan over medium heat.
Stir in minced onion, dill, curry powder, and minced garlic,
Cook 5 - 8 minutes and remove from heat.
Thread deveined and peeled shrimp on skewers.
Mist the shrimp with olive oil very lightly.
Grill over medium heat until the shrimp turn pink and are tender, approximately 4 to 5 minutes depending on size.
Brush with the curry butter periodically while shrimp is grilling.
Arrange shrimp on serving plate with lemon or lime wedges.  Enjoy!
