Title:
Cauliflower Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups of cauliflower florets
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
3 extra large eggs
1 pkg. Dry Italian salad dressing mix (0.7 oz)
Olive Oil

Directions:
Add cauliflower florets to your food processor and process until ground.  Pour into a large bowl.   Add next four ingredients and stir together.  Pour olive oil to cover in frying pan and heat over medium heat.  When hot, add heaping tablespoons of mixture to pan and fry until golden brown, turn fritters and brown other side.

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
These cauliflower fritters are easy to make.  So delicious and tasty.  I've made these a number of times for my family & friends.  A different way to have cauliflower.
