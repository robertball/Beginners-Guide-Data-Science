Title:
Nutty Bar Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303499.jpg
Ingredients:
24 Nutty Bars Wafer Bars
2-8 Oz. Pkgs Cream Cheese
1-4 Oz Pkg. Inst. Vanilla Pudding
2- Cups Chunky Peanut Butter
2- Cups Milk
1- Cup Whipping Cream
1- Tsp. Vanilla Extract
1/2- Cup Sugar
1/2- Cup Butter

Directions:
1. Unwrap 12 Nutty Bars, chop in blender until finely crushed. Pour into bowl.
2. Add butter to crushed bars and mix well. Press into bottom of a 12" spring form pan. Set aside.
3. Unwrap remaining Nutty Bars. Cut in half. Line rim of pan with bars as shown in photo.
4. In a separate bowl, whip cream cheese, milk, peanut butter and sugar together until fluffy.
5. Add whipping cream,pudding mix and vanilla to cream cheese filling. Mix 2 minutes.
6. Pour filling into crust. Chill overnight.

Submitter:

Description: 
I got this recipe off the back of a Little Debbie Nutty Bars snacks and since I really love anything with peanut butter, I thought I'd give this a try and I'm so glad I did...IT WAS GREAT and very easy to make...enjoy!!
