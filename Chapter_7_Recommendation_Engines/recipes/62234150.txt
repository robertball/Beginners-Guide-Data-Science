Title:
Mediterranean Dry Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Ground Cumin
1 Tbsp. Paprika
1 1/2 Tsp. Ground Ginger
1 1/2 Tsp. Ground Coriander
1 Tsp. Ground Black Pepper
1 Tsp. Salt
1/4 Tsp. Cayenne
2 Tbsp. Olive Oil

Directions:
Mix first 7 ingrediants in small bowl.  Add olive oil and mix thoroughly.  Rub mixture completely over steaks.  Chill for at least 3 hours or overnight.  Grill over medium high heat.  Cook to desired doneness.  5 min. per side will be medium rare.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
This dry rub makes the best grilled steaks.  Will cover 4 - 1 pound steaks 1 thick.  I use rib eye steaks.
