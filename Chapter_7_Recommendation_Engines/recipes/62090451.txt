Title:
Low-Fat Cheesy Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown rice
1/2 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1/4 red onion, chopped
1 cup fat free cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook one cup of brown rice as directed on package, while, in the meantime chopping onions and bell peppers.
Put chopped onion and pepper into a skillet coated with "PAM" and cook until lightly browned.
Once rice is finished cooking, combine pepper, onion, and fat free cheese in with rice.  Stir until cheese is melted and then serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Chrisa
Description: 
This magnificent recipe is healthy because it has veggies, calcium and rice.  Cheesy because it has fat free cheddar cheese.  And delicious because all of these flavors blend together to make a delicious side dish!
