Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken & Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sweet onion, Whole Chicken, Jar of Salsa (I prefer Southwestern Style)

Directions:
Slice sweet onion, separate rings and place at the bottom of the slow cooker to create a base.  Lay whole chicken breast side down on top of the onion.  Pour large jar of salsa over the chicken.  Cook on high for 5 hours.

Submitter: 
CorindaRose
Description: 
Easy & Quick Recipe!  Chicken comes out moist and sweet.  Slice breast to serve as main course or shred and use in tacos.  Very versitale.  Enjoy :)
