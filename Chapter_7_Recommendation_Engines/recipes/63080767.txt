Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies (Crisco) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup (1/2 stick) Butter Flavor Crisco
1 1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
3 T. milk
1 T. vanilla
1 egg
1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp. salt
3/4 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Preheat over to 375 degrees
Combine peanut butter, Crisco, lgt. brown sugar, milk and vanilla in large bowl
Beat @ med. speed until well blended.  Add egg.  Beat till blended.
Combine flour, salt and baking soda.  Add to creamed mixture @ low speed. Mix well until blended.
Drop by heaping tsp.-fuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheet.
Flatten lightly in crisscross patterns with fork.
Bake @ 375 degrees for 7-8 min. till set and beginning to brown
Cook 2 min. on baking sheet.  Remove cookies to foil or rack to cool.
