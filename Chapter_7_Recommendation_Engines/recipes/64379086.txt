Title:
Dairy-Free Spelt German Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2197205.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. coconut oil
7 eggs
1 ¼ cup unsweetened coconut milk (or almond milk)
1 cup spelt flour
½ tsp. Himalayan salt (or sea salt)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
Place the coconut oil in a 9x13 and place in preheated oven for 3 minutes, or until melted.
Combine eggs and coconut milk in a large bowl.
Add salt and spelt flour to bowl. Combine.
Pour batter into the hot 9x13 pan, and place in the oven.
Cook for 18 minutes, rotating the pan once halfway through.
Top with pure maple syrup.
