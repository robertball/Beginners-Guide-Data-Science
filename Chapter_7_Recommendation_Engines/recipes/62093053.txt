Title:
Melody's Raisin Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
3 tablespoons flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup sour cream
3/4 cup of raisins
3 egg yolks
Meringue
3 egg whites
6 tablespoons sugar
9 inch prebaked pie shell

Directions:
Soak the raisins for a few minutes in hot water to soften and plump while cooking the filling. Drain.
In a nonstick saucepan cook the brown sugar, flour, cinnamon and nutmeg till thick, stirring constantly.
Stir the egg yolks then add to the mixture and whisk in quickly, continue cooking till really thick.
Stir in the raisins.
Add to a prebaked 9 in pie shell.
Meringue
Beat the egg whites till foamy, add the sugar, and beat till very stiff, spread on pie filling and bake at the 375 degrees until the meringue is golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an old fashioned pie recipe my dear friend gave me. It is a classic. Have made it many times and it is full proof, and delicious.
