Title:
Wedge Salad Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese
1 cup of sour cream
1 pk of ranch dressing mix
11/2 cup of chopped  romaine lettuce
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped cooked bacon bits
2-4oz crumbled blue cheese
1 tbs of chopped chives
1 tsp garlic powder
1tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and sour cream, ranch dressing mix, garlic powder and garlic salt in mixer and mix well.
Spread into the bottom of 9in pie plate of anything of the same size
Top with lettuce, tomato, bacon, blue cheese and chives
Enjoy with Fritos or toastitos

The best dip you could have for a crowd.
