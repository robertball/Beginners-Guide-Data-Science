Title:
South Side Slopes Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Kielbasa (1 package)
1 yellow onion, sliced
Salt and Pepper
2 tbs. butter
3 Pierogi (whichever kind you prefer, I used cheddar)
3 BallPark gourmet sandwich buns
3 slices American Cheese
Horseradish sauce

Directions:
Slice onion.
Sautee in 2 tbs. of butter until soft.  Add salt and pepper as desired.
Cut kielbasa into thirds, slice lengthwise and spread apart making sure they're still attached.
Grill kielbasa until slightly charred.
Add pierogi to the onions, sautee until done.
Remove pierogi and onions.
Grill buns, inside part down in remaining butter until slightly browned.
Apply horseradish sauce to bottom bun, amount is as much as desired.
Place kielbasa on bun.
Top with American cheese, grilled onions and pierogi.
Top with remaining top bun and serve.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is a copycat of a sandwich I had at Fat Heads Saloon in Pittsburgh, PA.  It was so good that I had to try to duplicate it.
