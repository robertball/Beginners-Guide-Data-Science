Title:
Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
3-4 cups powdered sugar
1/4 tsp table salt
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2-4 tablespoon milk (or heavy cream)

Directions:
Beat butter with a mixer.   Add 3 cups powdered sugar and beat on low speed.   When mixed add vanilla extract, salt, and milk (start with 2 tbsp).   Add more milk (to thin) OR more powdered sugar (to make sifter) to achieve desired consistency.

Submitter: 
jodygreiner
Description: 
My girls love this frosting.   It is great for piping on cupcakes.
