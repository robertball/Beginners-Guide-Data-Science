Title:
Dark Chocolate Almond Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
3 cups of flour
1 1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 cup of butter (2 sticks)
1 cup of sugar
3/4 cup of stevia (truvia blend)
4 oz of crumbled almond paste
1/2 teaspoon of almond extract
6 eggs
8 oz greek yogurt
2 cups of dark chocolate chips
Chocolate coating
1.5 cups of dark chocolate chips
1 stick of butter (1/2 cup)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Mix flour, baking powder and soda in a large bowl. In a separate bowl mix 1 cup of softened butter, sugars, almond paste eggs and almond extract.
Mix butter mix with flour mix and then stir in yogurt. Once blended put chocolate chips in a baggy and a little bit of flour and coat chips to prevent them from sinking then mix chocolate chips in large bowl.
Pour mix between 2 9 inch cake pans and bake at 325 for 45 minutes to an hour (until slight browning on top and toothpick comes out clean).
Once cool, cut on cake so it is flat and start to mix chocolate chips and butter. Out a layer of chocolate in-between the two cakes and layer. Then coat the rest of the cake so it is covered in chocolate coating. Keep cool.
