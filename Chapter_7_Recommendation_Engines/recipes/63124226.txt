Title:
Cream Soup Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. cornstarch
2 c. dry milk
1/4 c. dry chicken bouillon
1 t. onion powder
1/4 t. thyme
1/4 t. basil
1/4 t. pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and store in a jar.
To use dry:  1/3 c. = 1 can soup.
To reconstitute:  Blend 1/3 c. mix with 1 1/4 c. cold water.  Microwave on high for 2-3 minutes or until thickened, stirring twice.  Cool.
Special additions:  1/4 - 1/2 c. cooked chicken, mushrooms, celery or onion.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
Use in place of canned cream soup.
