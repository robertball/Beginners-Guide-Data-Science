Title:
Mango Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981943.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Margarita Mix
1/2 mango, diced
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
5 cups ice
Mango or lime for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Margarita mix, mango and BACARDI(R) Rum.
Add ice and blend for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with mango or lime.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Mango and ice are blended with margarita mix and rum in this refreshing tropical margarita. A fun treat on a hot day.
