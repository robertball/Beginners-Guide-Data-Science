Title:
Jane Heartland Meatloaf Recipe Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/4 - 1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 - 3/4 cup ketchup
1 1/2 lbs lean ground beef
3/4 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup cracker crumbs
1 small onion, chopped
1 teaspoon salt & pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
*Mix brown sugar and ketchup together and press into bottom of loaf pan.
*In a bowl, combine remaining ingredients and mix well.
*Place meat mixture in pan over brown sugar and ketchup.
*Bake uncovered for 1 hour at 350Â°.
*Turn loaf out onto serving platter.
*Slice and spoon sauce over meat.
