Title:
Sunny Day Sunflower Dog Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/585157.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Whole wheat Flour
1/3 Cup Oatmeal
½ Cup Shelled Sunflower seed kernels
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
½ Cup Low Sodium Chicken Broth
2 Eggs
¼ Cup Milk
2 Tablespoons Flax seeds
1 teaspoon Baking Powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheets (I use my Pampered Chef large stoneware bar pans.
In a large bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, oatmeal and seeds.
Beat together the eggs and milk, add to the flour bowl.  Also add the oil and chicken broth.  Mix well.
Kneed dough on a floured surface for a good minute.  Roll the dough out until ¼ inch think.  Cut with your favorite cookie cutter.
Bake in the hot oven for 30 minutes, once the initial 30 minutes is up.  Turn off the oven but leave the biscuits in the warm but off over for a further 30 minutes to crisp.
