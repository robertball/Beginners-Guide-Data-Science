Title:
Egg Noodles with Shrimp and Asparagus - Lite Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh asparagus, chopped into 1-1/2 inch pieces
1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup dry white wine
3/4 cup chicken broth
2 large eggs
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
1 pound fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 Tbs grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to full boil, place the pasta in the pot and return to a rolling boil; cook until al dente. Drain well.
In a large saucepan, saute garlic in the olive oil over medium-low heat until the garlic is golden brown.
Place the shrimp in the saucepan and cook until the shrimp turns pink, about 3 min.  Add wine and bring to a boil, add broth.
Add asparagus and mushrooms.
Beat eggs, 1 Tbs Parmesan, and pepper in a small bowl; slowly whisk in 1/2 C of hot broth mix. Add warmed egg mix into skillet, whisking constantly. Reduce heat and stir in pasta. Cook, stirring constantly, until sauce is thickened and pasta is heated through, about 3 min.
Serve with sprinkle of Parmesan in warmed pasta bowls.
