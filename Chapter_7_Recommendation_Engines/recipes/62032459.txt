Title:
Cole Slaw - Greek Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of shredded cabbage OR
1 small head of cabbage shredded in food processor
1 bottle of greek salad dressing (Ken's is preferred)
4 oz. package crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Place shredded cabbage in large bowl. Pour on salad dressing and toss.  Add feta cheese and gently fold into the slaw.  Refrigerate at least one hour for the flavors to meld.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
MLT
Description: 
This is the easiest & quickest slaw you will ever make.  My husband is the pickiest of eaters...this is the first and only cole slaw he will eat.  Great as a cookout side dish, or with fried fish.  My friends all ask for this one!
