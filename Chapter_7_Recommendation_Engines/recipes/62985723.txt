Title:
Short Rib Beef Wellington Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 lbs. boneless beef short ribs cut into 1" cubes
2 tbsp. olive oil
1/4 lb. prosciutto - diced
3/4 lb. cremini mushrooms, quartered
8 tbsp, (1 stick) unsalted butter, cubed
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup red wine
1 1/2 tbsp. beef demi-glace
3 cups beef stock
1 tsp. fresh thyme - chopped
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 cups pearl onions
1/4 cup fresh flat leaf parsley - chopped
1 sheet puff pastry
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 tsp. water
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Season beef with salt and pepper.
In a Dutch oven, heat olive oil and add beef.  Brown on all sides.
Remove beef from pot.
Add prosciutto and cook until crisp then add to beef.
Add mushrooms and cook about 8 minutes or until tender. Add to beef/prosciutto.
Pour off excess fat.
Add butter to pot, melt and add flour, stirring constantly for 2-3 minutes.
Whisk in demi-glace and wine and cook for 1 minute.
Slowly whisk in stock and bring to a simmer.
Add thyme, bay leaf, onion, and beef mixture.
Lightly season with salt and pepper.
Cover pot, place in oven and cook until beef is fork tender, about 2 - 2 1/2 hours.
Discard bay leaf and spoon off excess fat.
Stir in parsley.
Increase oven to 400.
Place puff pastry on lightly floured work surface.
Using a sharp knife, score pastry with diagonal lines about 2 inches apart, forming a diamond pattern.
Brush edge of pot with water then brush pastry with egg mixture.
Place pastry, egg side up, over pot and press edges to seal.
Trim overhang to 1 inch.
Bake until pastry is puffed and golden brown, about 20-25 minutes.
Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
