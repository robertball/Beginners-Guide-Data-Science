Title:
Benja's Mango Coconut Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/906113.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups coconut cream
pinch of salt
3/4-1 cup sugar
4 cups dried sticky rice
8 mangos
roasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Mix sauce. Cook rice in steamer basket for 2 hours and flip it into sauce mixture. Let sit for 5 minutes. Turn over and let sit 5 more minutes. Spoon rice and sauce mixture onto plate. Peel and slice mango (spread like flower on to plate--on top of rice). Drizzle with coconut cream (with sugar and a pinch of salt) and sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
