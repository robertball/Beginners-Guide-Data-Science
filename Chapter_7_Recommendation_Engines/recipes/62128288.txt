Title:
Cinnamon Spice Instant Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c quick oats
1/4 t salt
1/4 t cinnamon
1/8 t nutmeg
1/8 t allspice1
1/2 c boiling water
2 T real maple syrup, or other sweetener to taste

Directions:
Place oats, salt, and spices into a large breakfast bowl and mix. Boil water in a saucepan or kettle and add to instant oatmeal. Adjust water amount for thicker or thinner oatmeal. Add sweetener to taste.

Submitter: 
SarahWearsHeadbands
Description: 
Tasty oatmeal without the packet. I wanted to avoid the cost, refined sugar, and dairy in the store-bought oatmeal packets, so I developed this tasty, simple recipe.
