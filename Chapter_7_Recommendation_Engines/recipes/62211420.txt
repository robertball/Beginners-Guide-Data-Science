Title:
Potato Casso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 bacon strips, diced
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon pepper
6 cups chicken broth
1 bag of chopped potatoes (Homestyle Hashbrowns)
2 cups half-and-half cream
Shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large saucepan, cook bacon, onion and garlic  until bacon is crisp.
Drain, reserving 2 tablespoons drippings.
Stir in flour, salt, basil and pepper; mix well. 4.  Gradually add broth. Bring to boil; boil and stir for 2 minutes.
Add the potatoes and cream
Heat through until you get desired thickness. Garnish with cheese.
