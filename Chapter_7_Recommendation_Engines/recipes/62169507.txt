Title:
Chicken and Dumplins the Cheatin Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast cut into bite size chunks
2 TBS olive oil
1 16 oz package frozen peas and carrots
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can large refrigerated biscuits

Directions:
In stock pot heat olive oil over medium heat .
Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
Add peas and carrots and cream soups.
Stir to combine and bring just to a boil.
Arrange individual biscuits in a single layer on top of the mixture.
Cover and reduce heat to low.
Cook for 15 to 20 minutes until biscuits cooked though.

Submitter: 
Todi
Description: 
This is a quick and super easy Chicken and Dumplin recipe that still has all the essential flavors with some veggies thrown in to make it a meal!!! Absolutely delicious!
