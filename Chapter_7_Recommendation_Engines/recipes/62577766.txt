Title:
Corn Tamalitos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tablespoons butter or margarine, 1/4 cup masa harina flour, 1/3 cup sugar, 2 cups corn (frozen, fresh or canned), 1/2 cup cornmeal, 1/2 cup water, 1tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp salt, 2 Tbls milk, 1 tsp milk

Directions:
Mix the margarine, masa and sugar in a small bowl with mixer until light and fluffy, about 1 minute.
Blend 1cup of the corn kernels with the water in a blender until smooth.
Combine with margarine mixture, stirring well.
Bring a medium saucepan of water to a boil.
Pour the mixture into an 8-inch baking dish.
Tightly cover with plastic wrap and set atop the saucepan of boiling water.
Keep pan tightly wrapped and steam for 50 minutes to 1 hour.
Check water level often, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
