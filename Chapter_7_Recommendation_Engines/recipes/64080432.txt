Title:
Black Beans with Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups black beans (cooked or 16 oz can)
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp ground cumin
1/3 cup tomato juice
1/4 tsp salt
2 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
1. Rinse black beans and drain.
2. In a skillet cook garlic and cumin in oil over moderate heat, stirring, until fragrant.
3. Add black beans, juice, and salt.  Cook, stirring, until beans are heated through. Stir in cilantro.
