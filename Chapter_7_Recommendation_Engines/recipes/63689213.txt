Title:
Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. box of bow tie pasta
6 slices bacon
1 c. frozen peas
1 1/2 c. bacon ranch salad dressing
1 small red onion, minced
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package direction. Drain and rinse with cold water. Place in bowl.
Cook bacon until crisp. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.  When cool enough to handle, crumble into small pieces.
Mix together pasta, dressing, bacon, peas and onion.  Chill and serve.

Description: 
Cool and creamy pasta salad, the perfect side for you next cookout!
