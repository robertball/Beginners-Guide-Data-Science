Title:
Classic Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large leek, thinly sliced
2 carrots, chopped
1 zucchini, thinly sliced
4 oz. whole green beans, halved
2 stalks of celery, thinly sliced
3 T. olive oil
6-7 cups stock water (use vege buillon)
1 - 14 oz. can chopped tomatoes
1 T. fresh basil
1 tsp. fresh thyme or 1/2 tsp. dried
salt and black pepper
1 - 14 oz. can of kidney beans
1/3 cup tri-colored vege spiral pasta

Directions:
Put all fresh veges into a large saucepan with olive oil. Heat until sizzling and cover, lower the heat and sweat veges for 15 minutes.
Add the stock water, tomatoes, herbs and seasoning. Bring to boil, replace lid and simmer gently for about 30 minutes.
Add the beans and their juices and pasta, simmer for another 10 minutes. Check the seasoning and serve.
