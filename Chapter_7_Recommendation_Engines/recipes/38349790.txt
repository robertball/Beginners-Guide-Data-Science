Title:
Chicken Holiday Wreath Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1/2 cup red pepper, chopped
1/2 cup broccoli, chopped
1/4 cup water chestnuts, chopped
2 Tbsp onion, chopped
1 can (5 oz) chicken, drained
2/3 cup cream chicken soup
1 cup (4 oz) cojack cheese, shredded
2 cans (8 oz ea) crescent roll dough

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Chop red pepper, broccoli, water chestnuts and onions with food chopper.
Flake chicken with pastry blender.
Combine all filling ingredients in batter bowl.
Arrange crescent triangles in a circle on a 15 inch pizza stone with bases overlapped in the center and points to the outside.  Scoop filling mixture into widest portion of each crescent.
Fold points of triangles over filling and tuck under base at center.
Filling will not be completely covered.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
