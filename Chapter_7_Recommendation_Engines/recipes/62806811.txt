Title:
Italian Spinach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659089.jpg
Ingredients:
-16 ounces of 2% Cottage Cheese
-3-4 cups of chopped fresh spinach (or 10 ounces frozen spinach, thawed, well drained)
-1 cup shredded part skim mozzarella cheese
-4 eggs lightly beaten
-1 jar (7 oz) red peppers, well drained & chopped (or 1/2 cup chopped fresh red peppers)
-1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
-1 tsp dried oregano leaves

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Mix all ingredients and pour into greased 9-inch pie plate
Bake 35-50 minutes until center of pie is set
You may sprinkle additional cheese on top of pie for last 5 minutes for a cheesy crust.

Submitter: 
Sarah Meedel
Description: 
A quick and easy dish to serve as a main course or side. Also works great for breakfasts and brunches with its egg base. Get in your protein and veggies all in one recipe. Great for those watching carbohydrate intake as well.
