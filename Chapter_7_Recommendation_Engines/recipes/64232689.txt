Title:
You'll never know it's Gluten Free Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup coconut oil (not melted)
3/4 cup coconut sugar (white sugar is ok)
2 eggs
11/2 cups of mashed bananas
2 cups Pamela's gluten free baking mix
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease & flour one 9x5 inch pan
Cream butter, coconut oil & sugar until smooth, beat in eggs, then bananas. Add baking mix & soda stirring until just combined.
Pour into prepared pan & sprinkle with pecans.  bake at 350' for 1 hour or until toothpick comes out clean. Remove from oven & let cool, store in refrigerator or freezer

Submitter: 
Jill Tall
Description: 
This is the moistest & most delicious not to mention healthy banana bread recipe & its foolproof, my family loves it & always wants more.  I always double the recipe.. enjoy!
