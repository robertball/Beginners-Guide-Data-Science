Title:
Bonnie's Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Eggs
6 Bread slices (cubed)
2 cups Milk
2 cups Shredded cheese
1/4 tsp Ground mustard
1 Onion diced
Meat (your choice, optional)
Veggies (your choice, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix all ingredients.
Thoroughly spray casserole dish with cooking spray.
Put mix into casserole dish.
Bake 45 minutes - 1 hour
