Title:
Kay's Gluten Free Banana Date Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6454312.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups gluten free all purpose flour (I use Krusteaz)
3  ripe bananas
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
3 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
2/3 cup cinnamon applesauce
2/3 cup chopped dates
3/4 cup chopped walnuts, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Spray 9x9 baking dish with cooking spray (do not use loaf pan).  Mash bananas.  Add sugar and applesauce.  With a spoon, mix in eggs, baking soda and baking powder.  Add flour, a little at a time.  Add in dates and nuts (if desired).  Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour, testing with toothpick until done.  Toothpick will come out clean.  Let cool at least 5 minutes before cutting.
