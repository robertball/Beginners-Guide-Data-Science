Title:
Fantastic Lemon Fluff Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
2/3 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
2 table spoons lemon juice
1 1/2 cups nondairy whipped topping
1 (5 ounce) package instant lemon pudding mix
3 cups cold milk
1 1/2 cups frozen whipped topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, mix together flour, softened butter, and 3/4 cup chopped nuts until a soft dough forms. Press dough into bottom and sides of a 9 inch deep-dish pie pan. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Allow to cool completely.
In a medium mixing bowl, beat together softened cream cheese, lemon juice, and confectioners' sugar until fluffy. Fold in 1 1/2 cups nondairy whipped topping. Spread mixture into cooled crust.
In a medium bowl, whisk Taste - add lemon extract if needed.  Allow to set up 5 minutes, then spread over cream cheese layer. Top with remaining whipped topping and sprinkle with remaining nuts. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

Cori
Description: 
The flavors of tart lemon, rich cream cheese, and buttery, nutty pastry mingle in this delicious dessert the whole family will love. Easy 3-steps to prepare. You can even experiment with different pudding flavors if you like!  Choose any nut you like to add to crust.Modified from: Fantastic Pie
