Title:
Meme's Potato Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052391.jpg
Ingredients:
8 yellow potatoes
5 eggs
2 tbsp matzo meal
1 and 1/2 large onions
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 tbsp garlic powder
1 and 1/4 teaspoon salt
9 X 9 inch baking pan

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Peel and wash potatoes and cut into eights. In a large pot, cover potatoes with water, add one teaspoon salt and bring to a boil.  Turn down to medium heat, add garlic powder and continue boiling for 15 minutes.
Meanwhile, peel and finely chop onion in food processor and fry in oil until golden.
Drain potatoes and mash until there are no lumps.  Mix in fried onions, 1/4 teaspoons salt and pepper and wait until cool.
Once cool, add 4 eggs, a pinch of salt and matzo meal. Add mix to a greased pan and then use brush to coat top with about half of the last egg.  Put in oven at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes or until knife inserted into the Kugel comes out clean.
