Title:
Korean Bulgogi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
2-3 Tbsp. sesame oil
1 Tbsp. sesame seeds
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
2-3 Tbsp. white sugar
Asian pear or kiwi, chopped
1-2 Tbsp. apple cider vinegar
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 lb. thinly sliced beef
1 carrot cut in thin strips
2-3 green onions, chopped
1/2 yellow onion, chopped

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, sesame seeds, garlic, sugar, salt, fruit, vinegar, and pepper in blender.
Pour over meat and veggies
Marinate at least 2 hours - overnight is better.
Cook in skillet over high heat until meat is just cooked through
Best served with rice, seaweed, steamed broccoli, and pineapple!
