Title:
Chipotle Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004905.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Boneless skinless chicken breasts (2-2-1/2 lbs)
1 Red Onion - cut into 1/4" slices
1 Large Red Bell Pepper - cut into 1/4" slices
1 (1.12 oz.) Package Fajita Mix Seasoning
1 (14.5 oz.) Can Drained Mild Diced Tomatoes with Green Chiles
1 (12 oz.) Jar Herdez Traditional Chipotle Cooking Sauce
1 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
3 Tbsp. Vegetable Oil - divided
1/4 Cup Water
1/2 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Mix 1/4 cup water and fajita mix.  Pour 1/2 of mixture over chicken strips. Set aside.
In separate bowl add remaining 1/2 of fajita mix with 1 tbsp. lemon juice and 1 tbsp. vegetable oil.  Mix Well.  Add red bell pepper and red onions to mixture. Set aside.
Prepare pasta according to pasta package directions and drain well.
While pasta is cooking, heat large skillet to medium high and add remaining 2 tbsp. vegetable oil.  Add chicken strips and sauté for 5 minutes or until chicken is browned. Remove from pan and set to the side.
Add vegetable mixture to pan and sauté for 5 minutes or until just softened.
Add drained tomatoes and Herdez chipotle cooking sauce to vegetables in skillet.  Reduce heat and simmer over medium low heat for 5 minutes.
Add chicken to pan and continue to simmer for 6-8 minutes until chicken is fully cooked and sauce has slightly reduced.
Remove from heat and toss sauce in serving bowl with well drained pasta.
Let rest for 5 minutes for sauce to absorb into pasta.
Top with 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese or more if desired.

Description: 
This dish was inspired by a pasta dish my husband and I ate at one of our favorite restaurants.  It is a spicy Tex-Mex pasta using Herdez chipotle cooking sauce.  It would also be great with the addition of corn or black beans
