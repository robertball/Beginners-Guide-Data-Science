Title:
Spicy Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small okra (not more than 2" long)
1 small zucchini (about 6" long)
3 eggs
1 TBSP milk
3 TBSP Wasabi Tartar Sauce         (Allrecipes.com recipe)
nonstick spray
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Spray 10" saute pan. Thinly slice the okra and zucchini. Saute about 5 minutes over medium-high heat. In a blender mix eggs, milk and wasabi sauce. Pour over vegetables, then sprinkle cheese on top. Cook over medium-low heat until eggs are set and cheese is melted.
