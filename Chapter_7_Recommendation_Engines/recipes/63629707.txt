Title:
Corn and Jalapenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups frozen corn, thawed
1 large jalapeno pepper, minced
2 Tbsp onion, diced
2 tsp olive oil
1 Tbsp fresh cilantro
salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil over medium low heat. Add minced pepper. Saute for 5 min. Add onion and saute for an additional 2 min or until tender. Add corn, salt and pepper. Saute 5 min. Add cilantro and stir for 30-60 seconds. Serve.
