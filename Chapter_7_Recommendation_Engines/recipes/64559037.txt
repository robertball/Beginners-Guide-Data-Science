Title:
Tropical Snack Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup sweetened shredded coconut
1 cup buttermilk
6 tbsp butter, melted and cooled
Â½ ripe banana, mashed
zest of 1 lime
1 cup chopped pineapple

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350F. Grease and flour a bundt pan.
In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, salt, sugar and shredded coconut.
In a medium bowl, whisk together buttermilk, melted butter, mashed banana and lime zest. Add to flour mixture and stir to combine, mixing only until no streaks of dry ingredients remain. Stir in chopped pineapple.
Spread batter evenly into a bundt pan.
Bake for 45-55 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Cool cake on a wire rack for at least 15 minutes before inverting and serving.

Submitter: 
Matt Wencl
Description: 
When you have leftover citrus and other tropical ingredients after your Tiki Party, bake 'em all up in a snacking cake. Also a great way to use up drained pineapple after infusing rum!
