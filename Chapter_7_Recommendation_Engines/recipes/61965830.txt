Title:
Sun Dried Tomato and Goat Cheese Tartlet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces Pillsbury Grands buttermilk biscuits
1/2 Cup mayonnaise
2 ounces goat cheese, crumbled
1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, oil-packed, chopped, drained
1 teaspoon dried onion flakes
1 teaspoon dries pesto seasoning
1 tablespoon parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.  Separate dough into 10 biscuits;separate each biscuit evenly into 2 layers making 20 dough rounds.  Press each dough round into an ungreased mini muffin pan.  In a small bowl. mix the mayo, goat cheese, tomatoes, onion flakes and pesto seasoning.  Spoon about 1 1/2 teaspoons of the mayo mixture into each dough line cup.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 375 for 10-16 minutes or until golden brown.  Remove from muffin cups.  Cool 5 minute before serving.
