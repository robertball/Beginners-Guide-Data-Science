Title:
Raspberry Cantaloupe Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3103002.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen raspberries
1 cup chopped cantaloupe
1 cup vanilla soy milk
1/4 cup silken tofu

Directions:
Blend raspberries, cantaloupe, soy milk, and tofu together in a blender until smooth.

Sarah-May
Description: 
A simple dairy-free smoothie.
