Title:
Grilled Margherita Pizza with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter, cut up
3 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup fresh basil, minced
1 tube refrigerated pizza dough
1 chicken breast, fully cooked, chopped
1 Roma tomato, seeded and diced
1/2 cup diced red onion
8 slices whole milk mozzarella

Directions:
Melt butter in a small saucepan with the garlic and basil.
Pour butter mixture into small, chilled metal bowl; place in refrigerator and allow to set.
Preheat grill for low heat and lightly oil grate.
In a medium bowl, mix together chicken, tomatoes and onion.
Roll out pizza dough and shape as desired.
Place dough on grill for 3-5 minutes; flip over.
Spread set butter mixture evenly over dough.
Top with mozzarella cheese, tomatoes, onions and chicken.
Close grill cover and cook for 5-10 minutes more or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
A Turtle in the Kitchen
Description: 
We eat a lot of frozen pizza, but I decided to try this on our new grill as a healthier alternative. This pizza is quick to make if you use leftover grilled chicken. I'd like to experiment with different crusts.
