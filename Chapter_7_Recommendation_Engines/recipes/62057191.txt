Title:
Spicy Bread Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
1 teaspoon dried basil or oregano
1/2 teaspoon tarragon
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Let stand overnight in a cool, dry place to blend flavors.  Serve with sour dough or French bread.  Store in a cool, dry place for up to one month.

Submitter: 
maricam C
Description: 
This spicy dip and your choice of bread is a wonderful alternative to bread n' butter and makes a tasty appetizer, side dish, or light lunch.
