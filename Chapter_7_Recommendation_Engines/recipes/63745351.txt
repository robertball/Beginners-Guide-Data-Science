Title:
Lasagna Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1039456.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. Warm water (95 - 110 deg F)
1 1/2 tsp. Active dry yeast
1 tsp. Granulated sugar
1 Tbsp. Canola oil
1 tsp. Salt
1 Egg
3 C. All purpose flour
2 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
2 Tbsp. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Cottage cheese
3 Tbsp. Tomato sauce
7 Spinach leaves, chopped finely
1 C. Cheddar cheese (grated)
1 Onion, chopped finely

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water and stir in sugar. let stand for 10 minutes until it has proved
Stir in salt, canola oil, sugar, egg,  cottage cheese, tomato sauce and spinach leaves. Mix in two cups of all purpose flour to make a sticky dough. Add whole wheat pastry flour  and enough remaining flour to make a stiff dough. Punch down dough and place in a bowl lightly coated with oil. Oil top of dough lightly and let rise for 1 hour in a warm location.
When dough has risen shape into two separate loaves.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 9" by 5" by 3" loaf pan and cookie sheet.
Place dough into loaf pan and cookie sheet. Allow to rise for 45 minutes until doubled in bulk
Sprinkle grated cheese and onion on top of loaves and bake in oven for 20 minutes until loaves are browned
With a pastry brush apply a light coating of butter on top of loaves to keep crust from getting dry.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light bread with added cottage cheese, tomato and spinach to make a savoury loaf for lunch or dinner
