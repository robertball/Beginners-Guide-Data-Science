Title:
Pluot Lime Gallette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2580464.jpg
Ingredients:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
4 pluots
1/3 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar
2 tsp lime juice
1 tbs corn starch
1 1/4 cup  flour
1 stick cold butter, cubed
1/4 tsp  salt
1/4 cup cold water
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Make gallette crust
Place  2 tablespoons  sugar, salt, and flour in food processor.
Pulse to combine.
Add butter and pulse until the butter is about pea sized.
Add water and pulse until the mixture starts to form together.
Shape into a circle and wrap with plastic wrap.
Refrigerate for about 30 minutes.
Pit and thinly slice the pluots, I use a mandoline.
Place in a medium size bowl.
Add 1/3 cup sugar and corn starch.
Add 2 tsp lime  juice.
Toss to combine.
Flour a work surface and roll dough out to a 12" diameter circle.
Place dough on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
Starting from the outside in (leave about 1" of dough on the outside) place pluots in a circle
Fold the edges over the pluots and brush with the egg wash.
Sprinkle sugar over dough.
Bake 45 minutes or until golden brown.
Let sit 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
This gallette is very easy to make.   It is very rustic looking and very delicious.  Perfect for company.  Pluots are a cross between a plum and an apricot. Try different fruits, any of them will work.
