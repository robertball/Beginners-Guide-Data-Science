Title:
Revised Yummy Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 sweet potatoes, baked at 375 degrees for 1 hour
1/4 cup white or brown sugar (optional)
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 to 1 cup evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. cinnamon
Topping: Double recipe - optional (may also drizzle 1/4 c. maple syrup over top and sprinkle with 1/3 c. finely chopped pecans)
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup flour
3 tablespoons cold butter
1/2 tsp. cinnamon, optional
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  In a large bowl, mix together the sweet potatoes, white sugar, eggs, salt, butter, milk and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In medium bowl, mix the brown sugar and flour. Cut in the butter until the mixture is coarse. Stir in the pecans. Sprinkle the mixture over the sweet potato mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven 30-45 minutes, or until the topping is lightly brown.
