Title:
Indian Tomato Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
***Dressing***
1 T. ginger-garlic paste
pinch of salt
1/3 cup vinegar (flavored is best)
1/3 cup canola oil
***Salad***
2 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
1/2 bell pepper, chopped
1 cucumber, chopped
1/4 onion, chopped
handful of fresh cilantro, finely chopped
2 T. chopped fresh basil

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine and wisk ginger-garlic paste, salt, vinegar and oil.  In medium bowl, mix tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, cucumber,  cilantro, and basil. Add amount of dressing to your preference; toss lightly to coat.
