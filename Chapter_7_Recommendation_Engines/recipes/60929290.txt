Title:
Holiday Brownie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19.8oz)pkg Market Pantry dark chocolate brownie mix
1/2 cup butter, melted
3 TBS water
1 egg
1 cup chocolate-flavored crisp rice cereal
1 cup M & M candies for the holidays
1/2 cup quick-cooking rolled oats
1/3 cup market Pantry white baking chips, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Mix brownie mix, butter, water and egg until well blended. Stir in cereal, candies and oats; mix well. Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 350 for 9-12 minutes or until edges are set. Cool one minute on cookie sheet; remove from cookie sheets. Cool 10 minutes or until completely cooled. Drizzle with melted baking chips.
