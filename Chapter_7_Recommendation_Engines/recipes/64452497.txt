Title:
Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 pounds ground turkey
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
1 (10 ounce) Ro*Tel diced tomatoes and green chilies
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
1 cup more or less grated cheese
2 tablespoons chili powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brown the ground turkey in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add chili powder, refried beans, R0*TEL tomatoes and soup; mix well and heat thoroughly.
Line a 13x9 inch dish with tortillas. Then make a layer with the meat mixture. Make another row of tortillas, then finish off with rest of meat mixture.  Top with grated cheese.
Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes.
