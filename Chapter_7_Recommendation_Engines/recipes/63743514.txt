Title:
Light Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 envelopes Dream Whip
1 8 oz package philly cream cheese softened
3/4 cups sugar
splash lime juice
Strawberry Glaze

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sugar and lime juice together
Pour into ready made pie crusts
Top with strawberry glaze or any fresh fruit
Refrigerate a few hours before serving
