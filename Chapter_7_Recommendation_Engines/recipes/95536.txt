Title:
South of the Border Dip (or Spread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986657.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup non-fat sour cream
1 cup salsa
2 tablespoons chopped green peppers
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro or parsley (optional)
1 (3 ounce) package Quaker® Quakes® Cheddar Cheese Rice Snacks

Directions:
Combine first four ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
Dip or spread each Quakes Cheddar Cheese Rice Snack with 2/3 teaspoon of the mixture.

Submitter: 
Quaker® Rice Snacks
Description: 
Add some zing to your snacking with this delicious and low-fat dip or spread!
