Title:
Chimichurri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 tsp ground blk pepper
1/2 tsp chilli flakes
1 tsp smoked paprika
2 tsp finely chopped fresh oregano
Large handful of fresh parsely finely chopped
3.5 oz olive oil
5 Tbs red wine vinegar
Kosher salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together salting to taste. Leave for 3-4 hours before using. Taste before use to reseason.
