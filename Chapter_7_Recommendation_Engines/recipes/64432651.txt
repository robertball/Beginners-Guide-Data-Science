Title:
Double delicious cookie bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup melted margarine
1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
1 cup milk powder
1/3 cup boiling water
3 Tbsp melted margarine
2/3 cup sugar
2 cups chocolate chips
1 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
Add melted margarine to crumbs and press into a 9x11 greased pan
In blender combine milk power, melted margarine, and sugar then pour in boiling water to make sweetened condensed milk. If needed warm up in microwave to make sure the mixture is smooth.
Pour over crumbs then sprinkle on chocolate and peanut butter chips and press down.
Bake 325 till golden brown (25 mins)
