Title:
Creamy Chicken Cheddar Perogie Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 Cheddar Potato Perogies
3-4 pieces Boneless Skinless Chicken
1 jar Alfredo Sauce
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
1 teaspoon Olive Oil
1 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Boil perogies as directed on package.  Lay flat in the bottom of a 9"x13" pan.
Boil chicken until fully cooked.
Cut chicken into small pieces, season with garlic powder, and brown in skillet with olive oil for about 3-4 minutes.
Combine chicken and alfredo sauce and pour over top of perogies.
Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over top and Bake at 350 degrees for about 25 minutes.
