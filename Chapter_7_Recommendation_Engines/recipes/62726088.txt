Title:
Breakfast Pita Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pieces Bacon
4 Scrambled Eggs
2 Tbs. Pesto
1/2 c. shredded Cheese
1/4 c. chopped Mushrooms
1/4 chopped and sauteed Onions
1/2 chopped Tomato
1/2 c. chopped spinach
1 sliced Avocado

Directions:
Cook bacon until crispy
Scramble eggs
chop and dice veggies
Spread pesto on Pita bread
Add scrambled eggs, bacon, mushrooms, Onions, tomato, spinach.
Top with Cheese
Bake on 350 for 10 min. or until cheese is melted.
Serve Avocado on the side with fresh fruit.
