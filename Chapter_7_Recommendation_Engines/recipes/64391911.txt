Title:
Sherried Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs stew beef
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 packet lipton onion soup mix
1 cup sherry
1 jar mushrooms

Directions:
Combine ingredients in dutch oven and bake at 325 degrees for 3-4 hours or until beef is fork tender.  Or cook in slow cooker on low for the same amount of time.
Serve over cooked egg noodles.
