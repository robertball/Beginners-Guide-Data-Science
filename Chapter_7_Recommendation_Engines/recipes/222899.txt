Title:
Stinger Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/995328.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces brandy
1/2 fluid ounce white creme de menthe
1 cup ice

Directions:
Combine brandy and creme de menthe in a cocktail shaker. Add ice; cover and shake until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
