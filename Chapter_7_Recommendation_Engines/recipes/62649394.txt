Title:
Mom's Chow Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1/2 tbsp garlic powder
3 cups celery, cut on the bias
1 cup diced onion
1 envelope Mrs. Grass® or Lipton's®onion soup mix
1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 15oz can low sodium chicken broth
1 12oz bag fresh bean sprouts (mung beans)
1 5oz can sliced water chestnuts, drained and diced
1 package of chow mein noodles
Cooked rice (optional)
Red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
Brown ground turkey and onions with garlic powder until no longer pink.
Drain fat from meat, and return to pan.
Add onion soup mix, chicken broth, soy sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add water chestnuts and celery until heated stirring constantly.
Add bean sprouts, stir
Top individual servings with red pepper flakes to taste.
Serve over chow mein noodles and or rice.
