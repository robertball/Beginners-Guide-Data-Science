Title:
Big Ray's Sweet Saucy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2429066.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp oil
6 small chicken breasts
1/2 cup dressing (see below)
1/2 cup apricot jam
3 Tbsp onion soup mix (about 1/2  of 1 oz pk.)
Dressing:
1 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1 pinch paprika
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp celery seed
1/2 tsp dry mustard
grated onion
1/2 cup vinegar
2/3 cup ketchup
1 cup canola oil

Directions:
Dressing: Place all ingredients in blender and mix until smooth.  Store in refrigerator.  Shake before using.
Heat oven to 350.
Heat oil in large skillet medium high heat, and brown breasts on both sides (about 4 min each).  Transfer to 3 qt casserole dish.
Mix dressing, jam and soup mix, pour over chicken.
Bake 35 to 40 min until juices run clear.
