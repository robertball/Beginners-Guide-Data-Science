Title:
Sue's Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages imitation crab meat, flakes
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/4 cup chopped radishes
1/2 cup Helman's mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp Bon Apetit or Beaumonde seasoning

Directions:
Shred crab into thin coleslaw-like texture using a small, sharp knife.  Place in a large bowl with other vegetables.  In a separate bowl, combine Helman's, sour cream and Bon Apetit or Beaumonde seasoning until well mixed.  Add to crab mixture.  Refrigerate 6 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
SUSANHOR
Description: 
People look at this dish and assume it is coleslaw until they try it.  There is never any left!  Great in a pita, on crackers, on a plate!  Always made for special occasions in our home.
