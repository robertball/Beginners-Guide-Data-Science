Title:
Dragon Eye Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2439442.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup dragon eyes (longan) with peels and seeds removed
3/4 cup liquid (milk, coconut milk, fruit juice, etc.)
3/4 cup butter, softened
1 1/4 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
2 1/4 cups flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter and sugar.
Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.
Mix flour, baking powder, and salt separately, then add to butter mixture.
Mince dragon eyes finely.
Add liquid and chopped dragon eyes to butter mixture and mix well.
Spoon mixture into greased muffin pans.
Bake 15-20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
Dragon eyes, also called longans, are small, round tropical fruits a little larger than grapes. You could try substituting lychees if you can't get dragon eyes.
