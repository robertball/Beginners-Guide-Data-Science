Title:
Chicken with Portabello Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/586051.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 tablespoons onion powder
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup heavy whipping cream
10-15 Portabello mushrooms, sliced
1/3 cup white wine
1/2 teaspoon chicken base stock
Mozzarella cheese
Parmesan cheese
Salt & pepper
Parsley flakes
1/2 teaspoon cornstarch (optional)

Directions:
Bake, boil, or grill chicken.
Saut√© mushrooms and garlic with butter in skillet. About 3-5 minutes. Once mushrooms have softened, add heavy whipping cream, white wine, chicken base stock, onion powder, and salt and pepper. Let simmer and thicken 3-5 minutes.
Place cooked chicken with cream sauce and turn heat to low. Place lid on the skillet and simmer 5-10 minutes stirring occasionally.
Top with cheeses and parsley flakes. Enjoy!
