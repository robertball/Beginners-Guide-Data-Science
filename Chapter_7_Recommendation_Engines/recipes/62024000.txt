Title:
Uncle Ed's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. skinless, boneless chicken breasts
2 large egss
1 sleeve regular Saltine crackers
3/4 tsp. poultry seasoning
2 sticks butter or margarine

Directions:
Fillet the chicken breasts lengthwise.  If desired cut in half crosswise for smaller pieces.
Melt the butter in a large frying pan.
Beat the eggs in a small bowl.
In a gallon size ziplock bag place the saltines and poultry seasoning. Press out air and seal.  Crush saltines WELL with a rolling pin.  Pour into medium bowl.
Dip the chicken pieces into the egg and then press into the saltine mixture.  Fry at medium high heat until golden brown then turn, adding additional small amounts of margarine as needed.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description:
