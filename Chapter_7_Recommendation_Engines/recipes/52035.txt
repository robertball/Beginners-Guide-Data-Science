Title:
Cardinal Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4620550.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces cranberry juice
2 fluid ounces ginger ale
1/2 fluid ounce lemon juice
1 fluid ounce orange juice
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
In a glass, blend the cranberry juice, ginger ale, lemon juice, orange juice, and honey. Serve with ice.

Submitter: 
DEESEBREE
Description: 
This cool, non-alcoholic summertime drink is a little sweet, a little tart, and entirely refreshing!
