Title:
Instant Pot ® Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5750098.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cold half and half
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup chicken broth, divided
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1/2 cup chopped onions
8 ounces sliced button mushrooms
1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
6 ounces crispy fried onions, divided

Directions:
1. Make soup by whisking half and half, cornstarch, Worcestershire, 1/4 cup chicken broth, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
2. Turn on a multi-functional cooker (such as Instant Pot (R)) and select Saute function. Add  olive oil, butter, and onions; cook for 2 minutes. Add mushrooms and 1/4 cup chicken broth and cook 3 minutes more. Turn pot off.
3. Add remaining 1/4 cup chicken broth, green beans, 1/2 cup crispy fried onions, and soup mixture. Stir to combine.
4. Close and lock the lid. Select Manual; set timer for 1 minute. Allow 10 minutes for pressure to build.
5. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacture's instructions, about 5 minutes. Carefully release remaining pressure manually. Remove the lid. Transfer to a serving dish and let thicken for 5 minutes. Top with remaining crispy fried onions.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Save oven space this Holiday season by letting your Instant Pot ® do some of the work. This recipe uses fresh green beans and homemade soup for a healthier take on a classic.
