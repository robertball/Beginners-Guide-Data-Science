Title:
Lemon Potatoes, Greek Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695045.jpg
Ingredients:
Potatoes, peeled & cut any size (Yukon Gold)
4 lemons, for juicing
1/2 stick salted butter, in slices
1/4 cup olive oil
salt
pepper
dried oregano

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350. Add all ingredients in a roasting pan and mix well. Season to your taste (salt, lemon, pepper, oregano). Depending on how many people you're cooking for will determine how many potatoes to use. This dish can be made for 1-100.
Add 2 cups water into pan and put into over with loose tinfoil on top. Remove foil after 30 minutes to brown potatoes and mix all ingredients at this time. Add any additional water if needed if pan is dry (1 cup).
Adding more salt, pepper, oregano and lemon juice at this time is ok too. Even another slice of butter is fine. It's all seasoned to your taste prefence. Potatoes are done when they are soft.

Submitter: 
GreekMuse
Description: 
Soft buttery roasted potatoes with lots of lemon kick. These are commonly served with most Greek meals.
