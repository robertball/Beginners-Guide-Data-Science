Title:
Red Ribbon Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can ranch beans
1 can black beans
1 can kidney beans
1 can diced fire roasted tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, smashed
1 medium onion, diced
2 tbsp ground cumin
2 tbsp chili powder (mild)
1 1/2 tbsp Hot Madras curry powder
2 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
2 tsp Lawryâ€™s season salt
1/2 tsp ground cayenne pepper
1 cube chicken bouillon
1/2 small can tomato paste (6oz.)

Directions:
1.Brown the ground beef with garlic and onions.
2.While the meat is browning, combine all dry spices in a small bowl.  Add this mixture, along with the bouillon, to the meat and cook for two more minutes.
3.Do not drain any cans.  Add all beans, tomatoes, and tomato paste.
4.Let the chili simmer for at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Keiko
Description: 
This is a thick chili with a spicy twist.  The secret ingredient in the chili is hot madras curry powder, which adds a smokey, gentle spice that is warm, but not overpowering.  Best of all, this chili is easy!  It can be on the table in 40 minutes.
