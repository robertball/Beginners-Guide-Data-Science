Title:
Creamy White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cans great northern beans (not drained)
2 lbs cooked, diced chicken breast
3 cans chicken broth
2 small containers diced green chili peppers
1/2 white onion, diced
8 oz sour cream
1/2 cup half and half
1 1/2 TBS ground cumin
1 tsp salt
1 TBS chili powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1 tsp ground white pepper
1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
1 TBS garlic powder

Directions:
Combine chicken broth, chicken, onion, beans, and peppers in a large slow cooker.
Cook for 1 hour on high.
Turn down to low and cook for 1/2 hour.
Add seasonings, sour cream, and half and half.
Cook on low for another 1/2 hour or until heated through.
Serve hot with crackers, pepperjack cheese, or corn chips.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Toast
Description: 
White chili just isn't quite right unless it's WHITE! Sour Cream and half and half make this a tasty easy fall dish.
