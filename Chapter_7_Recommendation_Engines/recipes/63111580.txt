Title:
Spinach Salad (best ever) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C sugar
1/3 C honey
1 clove mashed garlic
3/4 C salad oil
1/3 C vinegar/lemon juice
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. salt.               1 tsp paprika

Directions:
Mix all ingredients above well and pour over torn raw spinach. Top with crumbled bacon!

Submitter: 
Retired house husband
Description: 
This is an old family favorite. I have served this for gourmet club as well as casual dinners and it always goes over well!
