Title:
Italian Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 2/3 cup sugar
6 eggs, separated
1 cup buttermilk
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups flour
3/4 tsp. vanilla
2 cups coconut
1 cup pecans
1/2 cup quartered maraschino cherries (large bottle)
1/4 tsp. cream of tartar
Frosting
1/2 cup butter, room temperature
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
4 cups powdered sugar
3/4 tsp. vanilla
1 cup pecans

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg yolks, one at a time, and beat well after each addition. Add buttermilk alternately with dry ingredients, ending with flour. Stir in vanilla, coconut, pecans and cherries. Beat egg whites with cream of tartar until stiff. Fold into cake mixture. Bake in 3 greased 9 inch layer pans. Cool 10 minutes, remove from pan.
Frosting
Cream butter and cream cheese. Gradually add sugar and vanilla. Beat until smooth and creamy. Add pecans or sprinkle on top of frosted cake.
