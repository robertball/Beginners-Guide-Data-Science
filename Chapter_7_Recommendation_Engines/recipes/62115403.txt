Title:
Best Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion chopped
2 TBS. Olive Oil
6-8 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans (14.5 oz) Diced tomatoes, undrained
1 can (4 oz.) Diced Green Chiles, undrained
3 tsp. ground cumin
2 cans (14.5 oz.) Chicken broth
2 cans Black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup Red wine
*corn, optional
Salt and pepper to taste
Garnish with tortilla strips, sour cream and diced avocado.

Directions:
Heat oil in pan.  Saute onion and garlic until tender.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jello Girl
Description: 
This is a fantastic and super easy soup.  Give it a try.  You won't be disappointed.  The flavor is similar to chicken tortilla soup.  Try the garnishes, they really take the soup over the top.
