Title:
Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T butter
1 T minced onion
2/3 c bread crumbs
1 c water
3/4 lb ground pork
3/4 lb ground beef
2 egg yolk
1/4 t pepper
1/4 t nutmeg
1/4 t allspice
4 T butter
2 T flour
2 c beef stock

Directions:
Cook onions in butter, put rest of ingredients in mixer. Beat on low until smooth, then on high for 10 min.
Form into meatballs and Fry in pan with 4 T butter for 15-20 at a time. Cook till mostly cook through
Add flour to pan and brown. Add stock and make gravy, put meatballs back in gravy and finish cooking
