Title:
Charity's Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
5 cloves garlic, grated
4 oz cream cheese
1 1/2 cups 1 % milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
salt
pepper
5/6 oz parm cheese
parsley

Directions:
Melt butter, and add grated garlic
Mix in salt and pepper and softened cream cheese
On low heat add a little milk at a time until bubbly.
Slowly add parmesan cheese and cook on low until desired consistency
