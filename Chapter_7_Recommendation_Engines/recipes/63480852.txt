Title:
Marie Rose Style Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250g Mayonnaise
80g Tomato Ketchup
Worcestershire Sauce - to taste
Tabasco Sauce - to taste
Brandy - to taste

Directions:
Mix the Mayonnaise and Tomato Sauce together in a small bowl;
Add a few drops each and/or more - to taste of the Tabasco, Worcestershire Sauce and Brandy and mix well;
Cover with cling film and chill in the refrigerator

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
The Perfect, Supreme Sauce and/or Dip for Prawns/Shrimp and Seafood. Simple to make - totally personal - simple and delicious to eat. What more can you ask for.
