Title:
Classic Taste Of Wonders Platter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
60 Green Apple Slices
3 Containers Of Creamy peanut butter { Warmed Up }
1 Bag Of Potato Chips
3 Sleeves Of Ritz Crackers

Directions:
1. Use Apple Corer To Cut Apples Into 60 Slices.
2. Heat Each Serving Of Peanut Butter Up For 30 Seconds Each.
3. Pour Potato Chips Out Of Bag & Put Them On The Outside Of The Platter.
4. Place The Ritz Crackers On The Inner Part Of The Platter Right Before The Chips.
5. Then Place The Green Apple Slices Right Before The Ritz Crackers.
6. When Peanut Butter Is Warm, Put The Creamy Peanut Butter In The Center Of The Platter.
7. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
Sweet green apples & a creamy spread plus potato chips make a wonderful classic snack of wonders!
