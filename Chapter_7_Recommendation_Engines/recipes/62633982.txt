Title:
OFFICIAL HENRY BAIN'S SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 17 oz. jar Major Grey' chutney
1/2 9 oz. jar imported pickled walnuts
1 14 oz. bottle ketchup
1 11 oz. bottle A-1 sauce
1 10 oz. bottle Worcestershire sauce
1 12 oz. bottle chili sauce
Tabasco to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour into pint jars. Refrigerate until use. Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
RedHotTarheel
Description: 
Henry Bain was a maitre de' at Louisville's all male Pendennis Club in the early 20th century. This recipe was obtained through the current President of the club, a personal friend.  The recipe yields 4 pints and may be quartered. This is a steak sauce.
