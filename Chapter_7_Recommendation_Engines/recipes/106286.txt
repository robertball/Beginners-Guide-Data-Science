Title:
Soothing Warm Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984262.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups 1% low-fat milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 tablespoon SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
In two large mugs, stir together the milk, vanilla and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener. Heat in the microwave on full power for 2 minutes. Stir in nutmeg, and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Have a glass of this calming warm milk to wind down from a busy day of work, school, or play.
