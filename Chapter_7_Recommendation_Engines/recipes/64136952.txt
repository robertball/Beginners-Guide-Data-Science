Title:
breakfast casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground maple sausage
1 bag of croutons (any flavor)
6 eggs
2 cups milk
1 tablespoon prepared Dijon mustard
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Brown sausage in a skillet. Drain off grease
Combine eggs, milk and mustard in a large bowl & whisk until foamy
Add croutons, sausage and 1 cup of cheese and fold until coated
Pour into greased 9x13 pa, cover  and refrigerate until croutons are softened (min 8 hours)
Preheat oven to 350 and bake, covered  for 45 mins
Uncover and coat with remaining cheese. Bake uncovered another 15-30 mins, when cheese is bubbly.
Let stand 5 mins and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Ashley Kirker
Description: 
Easy, plan-ahead breakfast. Sure to be a crowd pleaser for any brunch! Reheats nicely in the microwave for quick, hot breakfasts!
