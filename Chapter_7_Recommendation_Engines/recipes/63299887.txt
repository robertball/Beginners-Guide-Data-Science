Title:
a1 meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tomato sauce
2 lb ground beef
1 cup bread crumbs
1/3 cup a1 sauce
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
reserve 1/2 can of tom sauce
mix remaining sauce and all ingredients
bake in 9x5 pan for 50 min
pour off fat, top with reserved tom sauce
bake 10 min
