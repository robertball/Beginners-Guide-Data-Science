Title:
Creamy Crock-Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white wine
1-10oz can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
1/4 flour
1 cup sour cream
6 boned chicken breasts
salt and pepper
paprika

Directions:
Mix wine, soup, and mushrooms together. Stir flour into sour cream and add to soup mixture. Place mixture together with chicken in freezer bag. Freeze.
Serving Day:
Place chicken and sauce in crock-pot. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and paprika over top. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
