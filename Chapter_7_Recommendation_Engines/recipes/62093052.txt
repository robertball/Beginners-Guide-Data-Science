Title:
Heavenly Vegetable Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large tomatoes, sliced
2 zucchini, sliced
1/4 cup cottage cheese
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, shredded
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons Ranch dressing seasoning (powdered)

Directions:
Slice the tomatoes and zucchini into small, bite size pieces.
Layer a 9x9 baking dish with half the tomatoes and zucchini.
Combine remaining ingredients in a medium bowl except for half the mozzarella.
Spoon entire mixture over the tomatoes and zucchini.
Top with remaining tomatoes and zucchini.
Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the top.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes until the cheese is melted and it is bubbling around the sides.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
This creamy tomato and zucchini baked dip smells heavenly and tastes even better! Spread on warm pita bread or dip with tortilla chips for a starter that everyone will rave over! You can vary the vegetables according to the season and what you have on hand.
