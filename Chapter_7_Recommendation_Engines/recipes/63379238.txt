Title:
Banana Pumpkin Nut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/904637.jpg
Ingredients:
2 mashed bananas
1/2 cup pumpkin
1 cup vanilla almond milk (unsweetened)
2 eggs
1 t Vanilla Extract
1 T Baking Powder
3 cups Oats
.5 cup chopped walnuts
1 t pumpkin spice
1 t cinammon

Directions:
Beat eggs.  Fold in rest of wet ingredients.  Stir together dry ingredients.  Fold dry ingredients into egg mixture.  Line or spray muffin tins.  Pour into 12 muffin tins.  Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes or until firm when pressed.

Submitter: 
Kris Frame Bethune
Description: 
These muffins are gluten, sugar, dairy free and yummy!
