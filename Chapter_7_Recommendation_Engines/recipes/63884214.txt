Title:
Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077590.jpg
Ingredients:
3 oz unsweetened baking chocolate
1 1/2 c water
1/3 c sugar
dash of salt
4 1/2 c milk

Directions:
In 1 1/2 qt saucepan, heat chocolate & water over med heat, stirring constantly, until chocolate is melted & mixture is smooth.
Stir in sugar & salt. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer uncovered 4 mins, stirring constantly. Stir in milk. Heat just until hot (don't boil because skin will form on top.)
Beat with hand beater until foamy, or stir until smooth. Serve immediately with jumbo marshmallows!
