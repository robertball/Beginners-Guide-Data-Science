Title:
Squashed Lemon Coconut pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. sugar
3 eggs
1 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. lemon flavoring (not juice)
1 tsp. coconut flavoring
1/2 stick butter, melted
1-1 1/2 c. squash, grated
1 pie crust

Directions:
You can use zucchini or yellow squash for this pie.  If using zucchini and truly want to disguise it, peel off the green before shredding it.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix first seven ingredients together.
If using a deep-dish pie crust use 1 1/2 c. of grated squash.
If using a standard pie crust, use 1 c. of grated squash
Pour into prepared pie crust.
Bake for 30-45 minutes or until set and barely golden browned on top.

Submitter: 
Dawn Eberly Wampler
Description: 
Great way to use up that extra squash ... and in a dessert!  If you don't tell them it's there, they'll never know.
