Title:
Homemade Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2603469.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 Cups of Organics Unbleached All Purpose Flour
2 Cups Granulated Sugar
3/4 Cups Unsweetened Cocoa
1/2 Tsp Baking Powder
1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
2 Tbl Spoons Pure Vanilla Extract
2 Eggs
1 Cup Almond Milk or Milk of your choice
1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
1 Cup Boiling water OR Coffee

Directions:
Turn oven on to 350Degrees and depending on your preference grease, butter, flour or spray cooking pan to prevent your cake from sticking.
In a bowl mix together all dry ingredients. Flour, Sugar, Cocoa,  Baking Soda, Baking Powder.
Once you have mixed together all of the dry ingredients add your wet ingredients starting with the eggs, vanilla, milk, and vegetable oil. SLOWLY mix in the hot water or coffee last.
Your cake mix will be RUNNY this is normal!! It still bakes like a regular ole' cake but will be super moist and delicious!
Add your Cake mix into your already greased pan.
Place in oven and bake your cake for 30-45mins
After 30-45mins insert a toothpick in the center of the cake to check that your cake is complete. If the toothpick comes out clean your cake is ready to cool down then eat or decorate!
