Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-	4 eggs
-	½ cup flour
-	½ cup asiago cheese (shredded)
-	4 oz. portabello mushrooms (chopped)
-	4 oz. finely chopped kale
-	1 tsp pepper

Directions:
1)	Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
2)	Add the eggs, cheese, pepper and flour (so that you can have a slight crust) to a bowl. Whisk thoroughly.
3)	Place cupcake papers into cupcake pan and then add mixture to pan, spreading it evenly across each position.
4)	Add mushrooms and kale to each cupcake
5)	Once oven is preheated, cook for 8 to 10 minutes then let stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Lorne Cook
Description: 
This easy on the go recipe can be made for any meal and is also ideal for parties and gatherings.
