Title:
Quick and Easy Shrimp Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984984.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds shrimp*, peeled and deveined, 24-30 count
1/2 cup McCormick速 Golden Dipt速 Garlic Herb Marinade
1 lemon (optional)

Directions:
Combine shrimp and marinade in resealable plastic bag; turn to coat.  Refrigerate 15 minutes.
Cook and stir shrimp and marinade in a large skillet for 5 minutes.
Serve over pasta or rice.  Garnish with lemon slices, if desired.
