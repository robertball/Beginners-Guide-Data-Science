Title:
Fruity Cream Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/61766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup sliced fresh strawberries

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, pineapple, and strawberries. Stir until well blended.

Submitter: 
Mary48
Description: 
This spread is easy to make, and is great for parties. Serve with bagels or ginger snaps.
