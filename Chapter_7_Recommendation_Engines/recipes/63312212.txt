Title:
Authentic Japanese Scallop Soup with Ramen Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875971.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3 oz. package ramen
1 1 and 1/3 tsp package of powdered dashi stock
2.5 cups water
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp mirin
1 tsp rice vinegar
1 tsp minced fresh ginger
2 sliced scallions
5 shiitake mushrooms
1 tbsp butter
8 fresh scallops

Directions:
Cook ramen according to package directions, drain, rinse with cold water, and set aside.
Boil the water and add the powdered dashi stock, soy sauce, mirin, and rice vinegar.
Reduce the heat to a simmer and add ginger, scallions, and mushrooms. Keep at a simmer.
Melt butter in frying pan over medium high heat. Add scallops and cook until opaque in the middle, about 3 minutes.
Divide noodles into two bowls. Pour half the stock over each bowl, top with 4 scallops each.

Submitter: 
Seth Kolloen
Description: 
Adapted from an authentic Japanese recipe, an easy and delicious soup. Look for dashi stock in a Japanese grocery store or order it online!
