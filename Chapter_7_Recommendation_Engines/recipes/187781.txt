Title:
Chimichurri Muy Pefecto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/772350.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups chopped Italian flat-leaf parsley leaves
1 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon hot sauce (such as TabascoÂ®)
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Place the Italian parsley, olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, cumin, salt, garlic, hot sauce, red pepper flakes, and lemon juice in the bowl of a food processor. Pulse until the parsley has been minced.
