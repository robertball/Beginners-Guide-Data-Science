Title:
Crockpot Rosemary Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can pinto beans in chili sauce. (Bush's Best Chili Beans)
1 15 oz can seasoned diced tomato sauce for chili (Hunt's)
1-1.5 lbs ground beef.
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 teaspoon minced garlic
About 4 sprigs of fresh rosemary, pull off the leaves and discard the stems.

Directions:
Put everything in the crock pot on low for 4 to 6 hours. When the meat is cooked, you can start eating. You can even use frozen beef, just make sure to stir about once per hour until it is cooked through.

Submitter: 
Samantha
Description: 
The rosemary really gives this chili great flavor.
