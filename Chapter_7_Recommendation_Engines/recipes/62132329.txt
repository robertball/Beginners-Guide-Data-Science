Title:
Easy Taco Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion
1 can diced tomatoes
1 cup converted rice
1 package taco seasoning
2 cups water
1 cup shredded taco cheese
1 cup lettuce

Directions:
Chop onion.  Add to beef and cook in skillet until browned.  Drain beef.  Add diced tomatoes (including juice), water, taco seasoning, and rice.  Stir.  Turn heat up until mixture starts to boil then reduce, cover, and simmer for 25 minutes or until rice is tender.  Top with shredded cheese and lettuce.

Description: 
Easy recipe that uses only one pan. This recipe can be made for under $10!
