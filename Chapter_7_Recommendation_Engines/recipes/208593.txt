Title:
Dark Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987947.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (1 ounce) squares BAKER'S SELECT Bittersweet Chocolate
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed
1/2 cup fresh raspberries

Directions:
Microwave chocolate in microwaveable bowl on HIGH 1-1/2 min., stirring with whisk every 45 sec. until melted.
Add 1 cup COOL WHIP; stir until blended. Stir in remaining COOL WHIP.
Spoon into dessert dishes; top with raspberries.

Submitter: 
Kraft
Description: 
An elegant dessert has never been so easy--whipped topping blended with melted chocolate is topped with fresh raspberries.
