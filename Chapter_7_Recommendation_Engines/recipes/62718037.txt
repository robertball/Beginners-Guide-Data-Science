Title:
Venison Meatballs with Silver Fox Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871660.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dry mustard
1 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup white vinegar
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 tsp salt
2 large eggs
1/2 cup whole milk
1 cup fresh breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tsp chopped fresh parsley
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1 lb ground venison
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
1. Make the Silver Fox mustard sauce: In a small bowl, combine the mustard, sweetened condensed milk, vinegar, vegetable oil, and salt. Makes about 1 1/4 cups. Keep refrigerated for up to one month.
2. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs and milk. Add the breadcrumbs, cheese, parsley, garlic powder, salt and pepper. mix thoroughly. Add the venison and knead with your hand until well blended. Form into golf ball size meatballs.
3. In a large skillet with a tight fitting lid, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the meatballs and brown them on all sides, about 8 minutes.
4. Add 1/2 cup of the mustard sauce, reduce the meat to low and simmer, covered for about 30 minutes. Serve the meatballs with the remaining mustard sauce on the side.
