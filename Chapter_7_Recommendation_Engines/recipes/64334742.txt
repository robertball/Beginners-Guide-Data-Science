Title:
Valentines Day Shrimp Creole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. prepared rice
1/2 c.chopped green bell pepper from freezer section
1/2 c chopped onion
1/2 c chopped celery
3 T butter
1 T Cornstarch
10 oz can diced tomatoes with chili's
8 oz can tomato sauce
1T Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. chili powder
1 dash cayenne pepper
1 dash Franks hot sauce
1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Prepare rice
In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat.  Add onion, pepper, celery and cook until tender.
Mix in cornstarch.  Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, cayenne pepper and Franks.  Bring to a boil, stir frequently.  Stir in shrimp and cook until shrimp turns pink.
