Title:
Ricks, French broiled chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbs melted butter
2 tbs. olive oil
________________
6 tbs mustard
3 tbs shallots
1/2 teas. basil
1/8 tea pepper
pinch cayenne
1/2 of the juices from chicken
________________
2 cups bread crumbs
2 chickens or 10 lbs of leg quarters with spine cut off.

Directions:
Put butter & oil on chicken and broil for 10 min. Turn over and baste the other side. Broil for 10 min.
Beat the mustard mixture. Brush on chicken. Coat with bread crumbs & dribble one half of the other half of juice put to broil for 10 min. Dribble other 1/4 of drippings on other side and broil for another 10 min or more. Check with temp guage to see if done, 180 degrees
