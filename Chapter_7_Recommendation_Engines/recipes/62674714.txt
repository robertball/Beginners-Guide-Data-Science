Title:
Cheesy Garlic Spread on French Loaf or Baguette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 French loaf
1/4 cup cream cheese
2 cloves grated or crushed
60 ml parsley leaves chopped
60 ml green onion chopped
1 tomato finely chopped
60 ml grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
Spread on sliced bread or baguette
Bake approximately 3 minutes or till toasted
