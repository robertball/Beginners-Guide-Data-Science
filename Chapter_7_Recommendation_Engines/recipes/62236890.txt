Title:
Buffalo Chicken Wings II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken wings, split and tips discarded OR chicken thighs
3 tablespoons butter, divided
1/3 c breadcrumbs
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 T distilled white vinegar
3 T Tabasco
1/4 t salt
1/2 cup blue cheese salad dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a 9x13 inch baking dish in preheated oven.
Coat chicken with flour, shake off excess and place in dish.
Bake thighs uncovered in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Turn pieces and bake uncovered for another 30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink inside when thickest pieces are cut in the center.
If baking wings, cook 20 minutes on one side than 25 - 30 minutes on the other side.
Drain on paper toweling.
In a small bowl combine the remaining 1 tablespoon butter with the 3 T vinegar, 3 T pepper sauce and 1/4 t salt and mix until well blended.
Add chicken and toss until evenly coated with mixture.
Serve with bleu cheese salad dressing.
