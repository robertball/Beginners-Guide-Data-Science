Title:
Holiday Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie crust
1/2 cup chopped prime rib
4 stalks of steamed asparagus, chopped
1/4 cup diced onion
2 strips of bacon crumbled
1/4 c. shredded cheddar
1/4 c. shredded parmesan
3 eggs
1 cup milk
2 tbs cornstarch
1/2 tsp garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
layer 1st 6 ingredients into unbaked pie shell
mix eggs,milk, cornstarch and seasonings with wire wisk.
pour over layers
cover the pie crust edges with foil (to keep crust from burning)
bake in preheated oven for 60 min.

Submitter: 
Terry Goff Tripp
Description: 
I use my leftovers from my holiday meal... since we have the same christmas dinner every year the quiche always turns out the same... just perfect!
