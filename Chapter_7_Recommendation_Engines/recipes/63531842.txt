Title:
Sour Cream Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/964539.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce
2 Cans Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Small can diced green chilis
1 Container Sour Cream
Filling
1 dozen corn tortillas
1 Large can White Meat Chicken (or 1 cup cooked chicken shredded)
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 small onion diced.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a saucepan combine the soup, green chilis, and 8 oz of sour cream. Heat through.
Spray non-stick coating on 9x13 casserole dish then dollop sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of tortillas. Add all the chicken and dollop more of the sauce, Tortillas, Cheese and onion next, sauce, Tortillas, cover the top with sauce.
Bake 20 minutes until heated through. Serve with corn chips and salsa.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
This one is a kid pleaser and quick to make after work. NOTE: The longer you heat the sauce the spicier it will become.
