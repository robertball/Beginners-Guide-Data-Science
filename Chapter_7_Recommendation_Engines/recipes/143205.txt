Title:
Fresh Grapefruit Juice Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/212140.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups fresh red grapefruit juice
8 large strawberries
2 medium bananas, sliced
1 (8 ounce) container strawberry-banana yogurt
2 tablespoons honey
1 cup crushed ice

Directions:
Place the grapefruit juice, strawberries, bananas, yogurt, honey, and ice into a blender. Cover, and blend until smooth.
