Title:
Liquid Chocolate Covered Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz whipped cream flavored vodka
4 oz hazelnut liqour, such as Frangelico
Fresh ice
shaker of salt

Directions:
Place fresh ice to a cocktail shaker and add the vodka and liqour.  Shake, and strain& pour into 8 shot glasses.  To enjoy, moisten the back of your hand and sprinkle with salt (as you would for a tequilla shot).  Down the chilled shot, and then lick the salt off the hand to finish.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
Yes, it's a shot, and yes, they REALLY taste like a chocolate covered pretzel!
