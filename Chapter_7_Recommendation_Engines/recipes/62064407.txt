Title:
CURRIED CHICKEN RICE SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large carrots, diced
2 celery ribs, diced
1 small onion, chopped
3/4 cup margarine or butter
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/2 to 1 teaspoon (to taste)of curry powder
3 (12oz) cans evaporated milk
4 cups chicken broth
2-3 cups cooked, cubed chicken
2 cups cooked long grain rice

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute carrots, celery and onion in butter for 2 minutes.  Stir in flour, seasoned salt and curry until smooth.  Gradually add milk.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly for 2 minutes or until thickened.  Gradually add broth.  Stir in chicken and rice.  Return to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring often to avoid scorching.

Submitter: 
Graden
Description: 
This is a delicious soup, unique in flavor and addictive!  Very creamy, restaurant quality and it freezes well.  Enjoy!
