Title:
Kim's Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/765593.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 pound ground beef
•	1 small onion, chopped
•	1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
•	2 cups water
•	1 (20 ounce) can mild chili beans (pinto beans in chili sauce)
•	1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained (or 1 cup frozen corn)
•	1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
•	2 cups salsa
•	1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies
•	1 (1 ounce) package ranch salad dressing mix or dip mix
•	juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Brown ground beef with chopped onion.  Once browned, add taco seasoning and mix well.  Stir in all remaining ingredients. Simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
Top with sour cream and shredded cheddar cheese!

Submitter: 
NurseKimmi
Description: 
Quick easy soup and delicious!  I love using up my left-over homemade salsa in this soup! (when there actually is any salsa left!)
