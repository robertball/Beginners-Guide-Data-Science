Title:
Cornbread to Die For! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes Jiffy corn bread mix
2 eggs
1 l/2 cups Mayonnaise (I use Hellman's light)
1 14 oz. can cream of corn
l/4 to l/1 cup of sugar (optional)

Directions:
Stir together, adding corn last.
Bake in 9 x 12 dish 350 for 30 to 40 minutes or until done.

Submitter: 
PATHEFLIN
Description: 
This has been a constant hit and I'm always asked for the recipe.  Excellent with chile and stew or anything for that matter!
