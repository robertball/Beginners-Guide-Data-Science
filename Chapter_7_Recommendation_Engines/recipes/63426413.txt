Title:
Imitation Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups orange juice
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1 cup ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
Pour into glasses and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Michael Doyle
Description: 
Pretty close to the famous Orange Julius.
