Title:
Korozot (pronounced ket i zit) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323009.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg(8oz) cream cheese, or Neufchatel works fine
4 oz butter
anchovy paste, 1 big tablespoon or to your taste
2 heaping tablespoons grated onion, and can add some larger chunks if you like
1 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp caroway seeds
1 tsp Dijon mustard
Sour dough bread to serve it on

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together except for the sour dough bread.
Chill and serve with chunks of sour dough bread

Submitter: 
Blazer
Description: 
This is a hungarian appetizer given to me by my DIL. I never would have tried it if I knew there was anchovy paste in it, glad I tried it.
