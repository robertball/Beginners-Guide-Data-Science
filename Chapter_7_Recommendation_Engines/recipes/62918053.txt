Title:
Mac and Cheese in the Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Elbow Macaroni, cooked and drained
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. salt
1-12oz can evaporated milk
16 oz. shredded cheddar cheese divided
1 1/2 c. milk
2 eggs beaten

Directions:
In a 3 1/2 quart crockpot that has been sprayed with vegetable soil spray, add the evaporated milk, regular milk, eggs, salt, pepper and 3 c. of the cheese.  Mix very well.  Add the cooked maccaroni.  Mix well again.  Place in the crockpot sprinkling the remaining cup of cheese on top.  Cook on low for 5 hours or until the mixture is firm and golden around the edgs.  Do Not Over Cook.  Do Not Lift Cover or stir until cooking time is up.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
A great creamy side dish for those hot dog and hamburger summer dishes without putting your oven on.
