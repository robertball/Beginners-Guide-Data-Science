Title:
Kelly's Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
2 tablespoons of real mayonnaise
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
2 tablespoons pickle juice
Paprika to taste

Directions:
Place eggs in a pot of salted water. Bring the water to a boil, and let eggs cook in boiling water until they are hard boiled, approximately 10 to 15 minutes. Drain and cool.
Cut eggs lengthwise in half and scoop out yolk.  Combine yolk, mayo, mustard and pickle juice in a small bowl.  Spoon into egg halves.  Sprinkle with paprika.

Submitter: 
LUSYRSGIRL
Description: 
Finally, a tasty way to use that pickle juice left over in your jars in the refrigerator!  These are a big hit at potlucks.
