Title:
Pate a Choux (for popovers, or with sugar for cream puffs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/2 cup butter
1 tbsp sugar (use for desserts only)
1 cup flour
4 eggs

Directions:
In saucepan, combine water and butter (and sugar if making desserts). Bring to boil over high heat.
Add flour all at once, stirring quickly until mixture pulls away from pan sides and masses totether. Remove from heat and stir until flour is incorporated and mixture is smooth, let cool about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Stir in eggs one at a time, incorportating until dough is smooth and shiny.
Use dough fro popovers, or as desserts such as cream puffs or eclairs.
