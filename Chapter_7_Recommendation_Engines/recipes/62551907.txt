Title:
Oven Baked Apple Walnut Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups old-fashioned oats
1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
1/3 cup ground flax seed
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
1 tablespoon orange juice
3 large eggs
1/4 cup canola oil
2 cups peeled, finely diced apples
3/4 cup finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F.  Coat a 10X15 rimmed baking sheet with cooking oil.  Whisk all dry ingredients together in a large bowl.  Whisk buttermilk, orange juice, eggs and oil together in a smaller bowl.  Stir lightly into dry ingredients until just combined; don't overmix.  Lightly stir in apples and walnuts.  Spread batter into prepared pan.  Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out nearly clean.  Cut into squares and serve with favorite topping.
