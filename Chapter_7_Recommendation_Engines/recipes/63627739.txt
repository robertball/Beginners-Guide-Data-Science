Title:
Herbed Oven-Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs chicken pieces
1 pkg (9oz) Triscuit brown rice & wheat crackers w/sweet potato & roasted sweet onion flavor
1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 TBL onion powder
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 1/2 tsp dried marjoram
3/4 tsp dry poultry seasoning
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 eggs
1/2 cup plain low-fat greek yogurt

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Line rimmed backing sheet w/foil; coat w/cooking spray. In food processor, process crackers until fine crumbs form. Transfer to large bowl; stir in 3/4 cup flour, onion & garlic powders, marjoram, poultry seasoning, 1/2 tsp salt & 1/4 tsp pepper. Reserve. In large resealable bag, combine remaining 1 cup flour, 1/2 tsp salt & 1/4 tsp pepper. In separate large bowl, lightly beat eggs w/yogurt & 1/2 cup water until blended.
2. Add chicken to bag w/flour; seal. Gently shake until chicken is coated w/flour. One at a time, remove chicken pieces from bag, shaking off excess flour; dip into egg mixture, then coat in cracker mixture. Place in single layer on baking sheet.
3. Coat chicken w/cooking spray. Bake 20 minutes; reduce oven temperature to 375 degrees. Back 20-25 minutes or until chicken juices run clear when pierced with fork.
