Title:
Quick Easy Chicken Barley soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts chicken broth
2 ribs celery
2 medium carrots
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 medium onion
1/3 cup rice
2/3 cup barley
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put broth in large pan on medium high heat. Chop celery, carrots, onion and chicken. Add all ingredients to boiling
broth. When rice is done so is everything else.(About 20 min). Salt and pepper to taste.
