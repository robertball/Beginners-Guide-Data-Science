Title:
Taco Braid Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871606.jpg
Ingredients:
* 2 1/2 cups Unbleached White Bread Flour
* 2 tbsp Sugar
* 1/2 tsp Salt
* 1 tsp Rapid Rise Yeast
* 1 tsp Soy Lecithin Granules
* 1 cup Tap Water
* 4 oz Cream Cheese (light)
* 1/2 cup Medium Salsa
* 1/2 lb Ground Turkey (Cooked)
* 2 tsp Mild Taco Seasoning
* 15 oz Black Beans - Canned
* 1 cup Natural Shredded Cheddar Cheese 2% Milk

Directions:
*** The yeast is really SAF-instant yeast, and the soy lecithin is "Easy Squeezy Soy Liquid Lecithin" from bread made easy. And the water should be warm.
Mix dry ingredients. Make a dimple in dry ingredients with tsp. squeeze lecithin into the bottom of the dimple about the size of a quarter. Add water slowly. Mix for 1 minute and check consistency. The dough mixer should be sticky and pull itself off bowl. Mix/knead in mixer on medium setting for 5 minutes. Spray counter and hands with cooking spray and k nead for a few seconds to form smooth ball. Roll dough into a 15x12 inch rectangle. Place on cookie sheet.
Season the meat with the taco mix while cooking. Drain the beans.
Spread cream cheese down the center of the rectangle. Then layer salsa, seasoned meat, beans and cheese.
On each long side, cut 1" strips about 2 1/2" into center. Starting at one end, fold alternating trips at an angle across filling. Pinch ends to seal and tuck under. Brush with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic salt. Cover and let rise for 20 minutes.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
