Title:
Teriyaki Chicken Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871605.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb	lean ground chicken
1 cup	corn flakes
1 tsp	gingerroot - grated
1 tsp	soy sauce
1 tsp	sesame oil
2	green onions - chopped
1	garlic clove - minced
2 tsp	sugar

Directions:
In large bowl, ombine all ingredients. Mix well. Shape mixture into 6 patties. Cook over medium heat until burgers are no longer pink in center, turning once. If desired, serve on burger buns; garnish with pineapple slices and sliced green onions.
