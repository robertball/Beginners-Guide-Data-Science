Title:
My G'ma's Pisketti (Spaghetti) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422398.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green pepper, chopped
1 small can tomato paste
1 small can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
1 can (14.5 oz.) can Italian style diced tomatoes
1 can (8 oz.) sliced mushrooms
2/3 cup water
1 Tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground oregano
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown ground beef, breaking up with spoon; add onions, green pepper and cook til meat is no longer pink.  Drain.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer on low for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.  Serve with favorite pasta.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
Makes no difference when they're here, they always ask to help me make pisketti. Fast, Easy, bellyfilling and heartwarmingly prepared for kids but loved by all.
