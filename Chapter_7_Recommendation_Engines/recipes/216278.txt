Title:
5-Layer Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992209.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15.5 ounce) can refried beans
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 cup BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream
1 cup KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese
3 green onions, sliced
1/3 cup sliced black olives
1 tomato, chopped

Directions:
Mix beans, chili powder and cumin; spread onto bottom of 9-inch pie plate.
Top with layers of remaining ingredients.
Refrigerate several hours or until chilled. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
BREAKSTONE'S
Description: 
On a base of spicy refried black beans, layer BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream, shredded cheese, sliced green onions, black olives and chopped tomato for a quick and tasty crowd-pleasing dip.
