Title:
Jennifer's Dill Pickle Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces Cream Cheese - softened
16 ounces Full Fat Sour Cream
1/4 Cup Finely Chopped Fresh Dill
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 teaspoon Onion Powder
Pinch Salt/Pepper to taste
1 Cup Chopped Pickles (Claussen)

Directions:
1 - Mix cream cheese and sour cream until combined.
2 - Add all other ingredients and mix together.
This can be served immediately but it gets even better after 1-2 hours of refrigeration.  Tastes best with wavy lays.
