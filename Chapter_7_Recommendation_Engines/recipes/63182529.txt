Title:
Spinach Cashew Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/818885.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Fresh Baby Spinach
1/2 cup Cashews
6 cloves Garlic
1 cup Parmesan Cheese
1 tbsp Lemon Juice
1/2 cup Olive Oil
1/4 tsp Sea Salt
1/8 tsp Black Pepper

Directions:
Blend Spinach in a food processor
Add everything except olive oil and blend
Finally, add a little olive oil at a time and blend, you may not need all of it.
Add to your noodles, vegetables, or meats and cook until warm.
