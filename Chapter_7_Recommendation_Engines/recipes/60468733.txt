Title:
Crunchy Coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/3 cup french fried onions
1/3 cup flaked, sweetened coconut
1 lb. large shrimp, shelled and deveined
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 egg whites, beaten

Directions:
Crunch french fried onions with coconut in plastic bag, and shake. Coat shrimp with flour, then dip in egg whites. Dip shrimp into onion mixture to coat. Bake 10 minutes at 400 until shrimp are cooked.
