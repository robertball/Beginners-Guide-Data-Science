Title:
Whole Wheat Loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2 TBS> honey
1 TBS. butter softened
1 tsp. salt
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup wheat flour
1/3 cup nonfat dry milk powder
1/3 cup wheat germ
1-1/2 tsp. active dry yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients in bread pan.  Program basic cycle and desired crust setting; press start.  Immediately remove baked bread from pan, cool on wire rack.
