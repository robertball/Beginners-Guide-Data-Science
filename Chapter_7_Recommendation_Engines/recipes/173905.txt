Title:
Southwestern Stuffed Green Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985601.jpg
Ingredients:
8 medium green bell peppers
1 pound lean ground beef
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Southwest Style Hash Browns
1 (15 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes
1 (15 ounce) can Italian seasoned tomato sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.  Cut off top of peppers.  Remove seeds and spine. Place 1 inch of water in shallow Dutch oven or 10 quart saucepan.  Bring to a boil over high heat.  Reduce heat to low.  Add peppers.  Cover and steam 4 to 5 minutes or until peppers are crisp tender.  Drain on paper towel.  Place peppers, tops side up, in 13 x 9 inch baking pan.
Meanwhile, cook ground beef in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat until no longer pink.  Stir in Simply Potatoes(R) and diced tomatoes.  Continue cooking until Simply Potatoes(R) are tender.  Cool mixture 10 minutes.
Stuff each pepper with 3/4 cup meat mixture.  Pour tomato sauce in pan around stuffed peppers.  Cover; bake 20 to 25 minutes or until peppers are tender.
To serve; spoon sauce over peppers.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
Stuffed green peppers are a real treat for guests. This recipe serves a crowd. Just divide the recipe in half to serve a smaller family.
