Title:
Pistachio Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 3oz pkg instant pistachio pudding
1 can crushed pineapple (including juice)
1 - 9oz container of cool whip
1/2 c. miniature marshmallows
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix pudding and pineapple together (this will set up while you mix the other ingredients).
Mix cool whip and marshmallows together.
Cover pudding mix with cool whip mix and sprinkle with chopped nuts.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
