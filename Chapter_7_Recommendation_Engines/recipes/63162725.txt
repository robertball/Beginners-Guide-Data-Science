Title:
Orange Glazed Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/898054.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups All Purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. gr. cloves
1 tsp. gr. cinnamon
1 tsp. gr. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. gr. allspice
1/4 tsp. gr. ginger
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups solid pack pumpkin
1 cup oil
3 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
1 cup raisins
Orange Glaze:
1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar
Grated orange peel from 1 orange
10 tsp. orange juice
Additional chopped walnuts (optional)
Non stick cooking spray.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray (3) 8" X 4" loaf pans with a non stick cooking spray.
In a medium bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.  Set aside.
In a large bowl, combine the pumpkin, oil, sugar, and eggs. Mix together with an electric mixer until well blended.
Add dry ingredients and mix well.
By hand, fold in the walnuts (optional) and raisins  until blended.
Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 - 55 min. or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Cool in pans on a wire rack for 15 min.
Remove from pans and cool completely.
Orange Glaze:
In a small bowl, combine confectioners sugar, orange peel, and orange juice.
Stir with spoon to blend.
Spoon on top of cooled loaves, letting the glaze drip down the sides.
Add additional chopped waltnuts (optional)on top of glaze before it hardens.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
This bread has just the right amount of spices and the orange glaze just gives it that extra flavor that makes it special. It has always been a favorite especially around the holidays, but is good anytime of the year!
