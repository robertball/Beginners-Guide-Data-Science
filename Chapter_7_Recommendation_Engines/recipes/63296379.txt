Title:
Veggie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/869569.jpg
Ingredients:
1 artisan pizza crust
3 tablespoons cream cheese spread
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
Pepperoni
sliced carrots
cherub tomatoes
Broccoli heads
black pepper
lemon pepper seasoning

Directions:
preheat oven to 425.
Take cream cheese and spread it all over the pizza crust.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese all over the pizza.
Take the carrot slices and spread them evenly along the pizza, and the same with the pepperoni.
Take the broccoli heads and sprinkle the tops along over the top of the pizza. Then sprinkle just a little bit of the lemon pepper seasoning all over, and the same with the pepper.
Place in oven and bake 14 minutes or until golden brown.
