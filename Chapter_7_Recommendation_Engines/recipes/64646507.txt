Title:
Nutty Carrot Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz dry roasted, unsalted almonds (about 1/2 cup)
3 oz. unsalted pumpkin seeds (about 1/2 cup), toasted
2 3/4 oz. dry roasted, unsalted sunflower seed kernels (about 1/2 cup)
21 oz. carrots (about 5 large), cut into 1/2-inch thick slices
1 tsp. chopped fresh oregano
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. sea salt
2 large eggs
2 large egg whites

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
Pulse almonds in food processor until very finely chopped. Add pumpkin seeds and sunflower seeds and pulse 5-6 times until coarsely chopped. Transfer mixture to large bowl.
Place carrots in food processor and process until very finely chopped. Add carrots and remaining ingredients to almond mixture, stirring to combine.
Press mixture in a thin layer onto a rimmed baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 25 minutes or until bread is firm and golden. Cool in pan on a wire rack. Cut bread into 12 pieces and serve warm or at room temperature.
