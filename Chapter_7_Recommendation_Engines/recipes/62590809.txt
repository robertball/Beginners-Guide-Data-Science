Title:
Super Good Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Avacados; Dark skins Ones
1-2 lemons, juiced
Salt to Tast
Half Onion, finely chopped
Chopped Tomato, small pieces
Lots of Cilantro, Chopped
1-2 Dash Hot Sauce
1-2 Tablespoons Sour Cream
LOTS of Black Pepper

Directions:
This is so easy, and all ingredients are approxmiate, according to your taste.  Its the ingredients that are important.  They all just go so well to make wonderful Guacamole. Just mix it all together and cool in fridge till ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
This is the best recipe for Guacamole i have ever eaten.  If you put the pit from the avacado in the guacamole it will keep ot from turning brown.
