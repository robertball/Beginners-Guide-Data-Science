Title:
Hamburger Potato Casserole for 40 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pound lean ground beef
20 cups peeled and thinly sliced potatoes
3 cup chopped onion
6 tsp garlic
6 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
6 cup sour cream
4 1/2 cup milk
salt to taste
freshly ground pepper, to taste
6 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef and onion, then add garlic; drain fat.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, milk, salt and pepper to taste.
Mix the soup mixture with the potatoes, then combine all ingredients in in 2 large church baking pans. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 1/2 hours, or until potatoes are tender. Plan to have ready at 11:30. They can cook longer if need extra time, or just turn off oven and let them stay warm until noon.
