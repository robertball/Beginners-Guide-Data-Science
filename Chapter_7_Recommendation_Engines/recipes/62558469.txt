Title:
Peppermint Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/539554.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 lb powdered sugar (approx 4 cups)
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp peppermint extract
1/4 cup finely crushed peppermint candies

Directions:
Cream the butter and cream cheese until smooth.  Add the extracts and mix well.  Add the powdered sugar and mix until fluffy, about 3 minutes. Add the candies.  Use as desired.

Submitter: 
Soifua
Description: 
This is a lovely, minty frosting made with peppermints that are easily found over the winter holidays.
