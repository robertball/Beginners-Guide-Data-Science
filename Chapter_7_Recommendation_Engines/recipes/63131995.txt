Title:
Grape Salad with Blue Cheese and Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/808850.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds grapes (mixture of red and green seedless)
4 ounces walnut pieces (lightly toasted)
4 ounces Maytag blue cheese
1 tablespoon honey
2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
2 tablespoons Walnut Oil or Rice Bran Oil
1 teaspoon sea salt or Kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper, freshly ground

Directions:
Wash, drain and remove from stems the red and green grapes.
Add walnut pieces to grapes.
Crumble blue cheese and add to grape-walnut mixture.
Whisk honey, salt and vinegar until blended; whisk oil into the honey-salt-vinegar mixture, whisking until mixed and creamy.
Pour over grape mixture, lightly toss to coat. Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Chris Diaz
Description: 
A different and delicious salad that tastes just like the one served at Central Market with just a touch of honey and white balsamic vinegar.
