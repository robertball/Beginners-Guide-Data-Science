Title:
bob's dry bbq rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tbls white sugar
5 tbls light brown sugar
2 tbls kosher salt
1 tbls onion powder
1 tbls fresh ground black pepper
2 tbls paprika
1 tbls garlic powder

Directions:
combine all ingredients together in an air tight container and shake well to mix.
allow 1 day for flavors to blend.
always rub meat then wrap in plastic and place in the fridge for at least 2 hours to blend in the meat before cooking

Submitter: 
bobthecook1
Description: 
this is a rub for pork, chicken and even beef. it has a wonderful flavor
