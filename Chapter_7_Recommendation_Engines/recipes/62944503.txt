Title:
Pecan Banana Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723281.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8oz package of cream cheese.
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/3 cup apple suauce
1/2 cup canola oil
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 cups ripe bananas smashed
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
3 teasppons cinnamon
1 cup chopped pecans
Frosting:
4 cups powder sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 teaspoons vanilla
8oz package cream cheese

Directions:
Cupcakes: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cream the butter and cream cheese. slowly add both brown and white sugar, mix until light and fluffy.  add eggs one at a time mixing well.  add the applesauce and oil mix well.  add the flour, baking soda, baking powder salt and cinnamon.  mix until all ingredients are blended well.  mix in the bananas and vanilla.  fold in nuts.  fill cupcake papers 2/3 full, bake at 350 degrees for 28-33 min or until brown on top and toothpick is clean. Let cool completely.
Frosting:Mix soften butter and cream cheese with 4 cups of powder sugar and 2 teaspoon vanilla.

Submitter: 
Linda Kaye
Description: 
Very tasty alternative to banana bread.  My husband and kids like these with or without frosting!
