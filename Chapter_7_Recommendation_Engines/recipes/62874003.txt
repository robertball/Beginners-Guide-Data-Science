Title:
Chilled Strawberry Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(2) 16 oz packages of frozen strawberries, unsweetened
1 can frozen apple juice concentrate (undiluted)
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp cornstarch
1 cup low fat vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Combine strawberries and apple juice over medium heat.  Bring to a boil.  Boil for 5 minutes.  In another bowl, combine water and cornstarch until smooth.  Add cornstarch/water mixture to strawberry/juice mixture.  Stir for 1 minute or until thick and clear.  Cool.  Puree in blender 5-10 seconds or until desired consistency.  Chill at least 2 hours.  Add yogurt when ready to serve by mixing into the strawberry mixture, until light pink in color. You can add honey or sugar if it's not sweet enough for you.

Submitter: 
Carolynn Squares Hartzell
Description: 
Delicious, refreshing, chilled soup for the hot summer months.
