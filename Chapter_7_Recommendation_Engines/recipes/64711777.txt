Title:
FALL OFF THE BONE BACK RIBS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 RACKS BABY BACK RIBS
1 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1 TABLESPOON SALT
1/2 TABLESPOON BLACK PEPPER
3 CUPS HOT WATER
1 TEASPOON GARLIC POWDER
1 CUP BBQ SAUCE

Directions:
Mix brown sugar with spices and set aside.
Preheat oven to 275 degrees
Use a 2pc broiler pan...the lower half filled with very hot water, then layer the whole racks of ribs on the top. (Up to 6 racks ok)
Rub the brown sugar mixture liberally over each rack.
Using heavy duty foil, cover the pan so that no heat escapes. (double wrap)
Set in the oven for 3-5 hours
Remove foil carefully and cut ribs into a manageable length to grill them.
Brush with favourite BBQ sauce
Grill over very hot grill for 2-3 minutes per side :)

Submitter: 
Margaret Byrne
Description: 
THESE RIBS COME CLEAN OFF THE BONE AFTER COOKING IN THE OVEN OVER LOW HEAT...THEN A QUICK BRUSH WITH YOUR FAV BBQ SAUCE AND GRILLED FOR 5 OR 6 MINUTES.
