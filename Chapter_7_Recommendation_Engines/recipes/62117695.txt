Title:
Peanutbutter & Banana Burritto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8" flour tortilla
2 tablespoons peanutbutter
1 small banana
1 tablespoon honey or jelly [optional]

Directions:
Spread tortilla with peanutbutter.
Top with honey or jelly if using.
Place peeled banana in center of tortilla, you might need to trim the ends of banana to fit the tortilla.
Roll up burritto style, cut in half.

Submitter: 
Billie Miller
Description: 
It's a twist on ordinary pb & j.
