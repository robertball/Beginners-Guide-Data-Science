Title:
Creamy, spicy, chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 heaping teaspoon of minced garlic
1 onion, chopped
1-2 cups fresh, sliced jalapenos (I had some in the freezer)
1 can diced tomatoes
3 cans chicken broth
1-2 cups pulled chicken (I had some in the freezer)
2 8oz blocks of cream cheese
1tsp cumin
1tsp seasoned salt, start small and adjust to taste
1tsp cilantro

Directions:
Saute garlic, onion, and jalapenos in soup pot over medium heat until onions are clear.
Add diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and pulled chicken and let it get hot.
Add cream cheese, stirring occasionally until all melted.
Season to taste.
Simmer for 30 minutes and serve!
