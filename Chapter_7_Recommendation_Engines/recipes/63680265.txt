Title:
Tomato Mint Passion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tomatoes
2 nectarines, peeled, seed removed
1/2c. fresh mint leaves
Pulp of 4 passion fruit
1c. crushed ice

Directions:
Process tomatoes, nectarines, & mint leaves through Juice Fountain™ using speed 1 & 2.
Stir in passion fruit pulp.
Scoop ice into 4 glasses, pour in juice, mix well to combine. Serve Immediately.

Submitter:
