Title:
Baked Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz bacon
2 cloves of garlic smashed
24 med mushrooms,clean and separate caps and stems
1 large onion well well chopped
butter and bread crumbs to make stuffing

Directions:
fry bacon to crisp,  then chopand reserve, in bacon fat add smashed garlic cloves, finely chopped onion,cook until light brown, when onions are done add chopped mushroom stems and cook soft about one minuteminute, add 2 to 4 oz of butter, add enough bread crumbs to make a firm stuffing, stuff caps, and bake at 325 for 10 to 15 minutes
