Title:
Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 small onion chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cups picante sauce or salsa
1 Can diced tomatoes
1 small green & red pepper chopped
1 TB. lime juice
1 1/2 tb. pepper & chili powder
12 corn tortillas
1 cup sour cream
3/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
3/4 cup shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
or additional cheese to your taste
shredded lettuce and sliced olives (optional)

Directions:
Brown meat with onion and garlic, drain. Add picante or salsa, diced tomatoes, peppers,lime juice, pepper and chili powder.  Simmer uncovered 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Arrange 6 tortillas on bottom and up sides of lightly greased 13x9 by 2" baking dish, overlapping as necessary.  Top with half the meat mixture.  Arrange remaining tortillas over, overlapping as necessary.  Spread sour cream over tortillas. Top with remaining meat mixture and 1/2 of the shredded cheese.  Bake in 350 degree F oven 30 minutes, top with remaining cheese.  Let stand for 10 minutes.
Garnish with lettuce and olives, if desired.

A crowd pleasing casserole hit with a Tex-Mex taste all made in one dish.
