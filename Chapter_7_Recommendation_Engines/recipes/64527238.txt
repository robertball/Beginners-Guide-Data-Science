Title:
Hot Broccoli Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh broccoli florets, chopped fairly fine
2 tbsp. onion, chopped
2 tbsp. red pepper, chopped
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic, pressed
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayo
1/8 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Bake @ 350 in greased casserole dish or 8" x 8" pan x 25 minutes.
Serve with scoop chips or bread.
Leftovers keep well in fridge.
Tastes best if reheated to serve leftovers.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Cheesy, creamy appetizer, loaded with broccoli, with a garlic kicker. I've also used this to top chicken, ham, etc. and bake together. I usually double this!
