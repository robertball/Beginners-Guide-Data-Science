Title:
Gramma Hubbard's Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup shortning
2 1/2 Tbs. water

Directions:
Mix salt, and flour in a large bowl.
Cream shortning into salt, and flour, then add water.
Mix well, untill completely blended.
Roll out on floured wax paper.
Lay in pie pan, and fill with your favorite filling.

Submitter: 
Tiffanie DeMayo
Description: 
A flacky, homemade, old-fashioned pie crust.
