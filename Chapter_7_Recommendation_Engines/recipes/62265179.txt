Title:
Texas Sweet Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unbleached flour
1 cup yellow cornmeal
2/3 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon baking powder
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/4 cup butter (1/2 stick), melted

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Spray or lightly grease a 9 inch square or round pan.
2. In a large bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt and baking powder. Stir in egg, milk and butter until combined. Don't over stir. Pour into prepared pan.
3. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until golden brown and pulls away from edge.

Submitter: 
LKVESELKA
Description: 
My families favorite sweet cornbread recipe. Goes great with soups, sausage and cabbage, or your favorite comfort food.
