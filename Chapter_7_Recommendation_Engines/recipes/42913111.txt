Title:
Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cube butter
1 cube margerine
4 squares unsweetened baking chocolate
2 Cups Sugar
4 Eggs
1 Cup Flour
1 tsp Vanilla
1/2 tsp Almond extract
1/2 Cup broken walnut pieces

Directions:
Melt butter, margerine, and chocolate over medium heat stirring constantly
Remove from heat
Mix in sugar until completely incorporated
Mix in eggs one at a time, beat after each
Mix in flour
Add Vanilla and Almond
Mix in nuts.
Pour into greased 7x11 baking dish
Bake @ 350 for 20-25 min
