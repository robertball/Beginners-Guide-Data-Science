Title:
Mom's Spring Garden Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large zucchini squash, 1 yellow squash chopped
2-3 cups crumbled flavored crackers
1 medium chopped onion
4 stalks chopped celery
4 large white mushrooms sliced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can milk
2 green tomatoes sliced
1 cup cubed cheese
several good shakes Worcestershire sauce and paprika
Olive oil, about 2 T. to fry onions
1/2 teaspoon Mrs. Dash
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease and flour a medium baking dish.
In a large soup pan fry onions till golden, then add green tomatoes, squash, celery and mushrooms in that order about 2 minutes apart stirring frequently. When everything is soft and wilted, add soup and milk, when bubbling add crackers and cheese, stir to mix and dissolve cheese. Remove from heat. Add seasonings salt, and pepper. Transfer to the prepared baking dish.
Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven.
For a fancier look, top with shredded cheese and more cracker crumbs, and continue cooking 15 minutes, or put under a low broiler heat until cheese is melted and bubbly, top is browned.
