Title:
Garlic Oven Potatoes Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, quartered and sliced
1 onion, peeled and sliced
2 tablespoons Bertolli速 Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 1/2 teaspoons Lawry's速 Garlic Salt
1/2 teaspoon Lawry's速 Seasoned Pepper

Directions:
In large Ziploc(R) Brand Bag, shake together all ingredients until evenly coated.  Spray baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray; spread potato mixture out on baking sheet.  Bake in preheated 400 degrees F oven for 45 minutes, stirring after 30 minutes.
