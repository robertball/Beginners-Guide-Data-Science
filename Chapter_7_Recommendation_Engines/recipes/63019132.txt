Title:
Easy Lasagna II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef or 1/2 lb each ground beef and sausage
1 jar (26 to 30 oz)spaghetti sauce
1 can 14 1/2 oz diced tomatoes
15 oz can tomato sauce
15 oz ricotta cheeese
1/4 c grated Parmesan cheese
1 egg, beaten
10 uncooked lasagna noodles
2 c shredded mozzarella, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Brown ground beef, drain fat.  Add spaghetti sauce, diced tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Stir well.
In medium bowl combine ricotta, parmesan, and egg.
In 13x9 baking dish spread 2 cups of beef mixture.  Arrange 5 noodles on top.  Press noodles into sauce.
Spread entire ricotta mixture over noodles.  Sprinkle with 1 cup mozzarella.  Top with 1 c beef mixture.
Arrange 5 noodles and press lightly into sauce.  Top with remaining sauce.
Bake 45 minutes.  Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella.  Tent lightly with foil and let stand 15 minutes.
