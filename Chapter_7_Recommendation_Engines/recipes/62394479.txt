Title:
Turkey Twist Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/426915.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups (8oz.) Cooked Turkey Breast meat, torn into pieces
8 oz. rotini pasta, cooked
1 large cucumber, peeled, seeded, and diced.
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 small white onion diced
1 1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup prepared Italian dressing
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
Salt and Pepper to Taste.

Directions:
Combine turkey, pasta, cucumber, celery, and onion in a large bowl.
In a small bowl combine mayonnaise, Italian dressing, lemon juice, mustard, salt and pepper.
Add the mayonnaise mixture to the turkey mixture and toss lightly.
Chill well before serving.

Submitter: 
Andrea Luhman
Description: 
A great summer dish to serve as a pic-nick main or a BBQ side.
