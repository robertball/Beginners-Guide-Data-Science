Title:
Green Beans with Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans green beans
1 tbls sugar
¼ tsp pepper
2 cup cherry tomato halves (optional)
¼ cup butter
¾ tsp garlic salt
1 ½ tsp dried basil

Directions:
Heat green beans.  Drain thoroughly and set aside.  Melt butter in a skillet; stir in sugar, garlic salt, pepper and basil.  Add tomatoes and cook, stirring gently, just until soft.  Pour tomato mixture over green beans.  Toss gently to blend.  If using fresh green beans:  Cook 1 ½ lbs beans (trimmed and cut into 2” pieces) in 1 ½ cup water; cover and bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until tender, about 10 minutes; drain.
