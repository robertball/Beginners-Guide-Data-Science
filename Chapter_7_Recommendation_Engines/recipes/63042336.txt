Title:
Beefy Cabbage Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound extra-lean ground beef
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 onion, minced
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
2 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (11.5 ounce) can tomato juice
1 1/2 cups water
1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix
1 teaspoon Greek-style seasoning

Directions:
In a large pot over medium-high heat, cook onion until translucent.  Add garlic and cook 5 more minutes.  Add beef and cook until brown; drain.
Return meat to pot with pinto beans, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato juice, water, coleslaw mix and Greek seasoning.  Simmer over low heat 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Christine W
Description: 
Ground beef is simmered with pinto beans, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato juice and coleslaw mix and seasoned with Greek seasoning on the stove in this easy stew with no prep time!Modified from: Beefy Cabbage Stew
