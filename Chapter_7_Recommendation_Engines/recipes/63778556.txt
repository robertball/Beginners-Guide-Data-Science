Title:
Spicy Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 green peppers, tops and seeds removed
1 pound 90% Lean Ground Beef
1 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup cooked rice
1 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
Dash cayenne pepper
1 jar (8 ounces) picante sauce

Directions:
1.  Cook peppers in boiling salted water for 3 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water. Combine ground beef, onion, rice and seasonings; spoon into peppers. Place in a greased baking dish; top with picante sauce. Bake, uncovered, at 375Â° for 35-40 minutes or until meat is no longer pink.
