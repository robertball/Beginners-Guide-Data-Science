Title:
Swiss Chard with Garbanzo Beans and Fresh Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoons olive oil
1 Tablespoon bacon grease
1/2 C chopped sweet Onion
2 cloves Garlic, minced
3" of a garlic scape minced
1 can garbanzo beans, drained
salt and pepper to taste
2 bunches red Swiss chard, rinsed and chopped
1 large tomato, chopped
1 small lemon, juiced
Sea Salt to taste
To try - finish with goat cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil and bacon grease in a large skillet. Stir in onion, scape, cook and stir for 3 to 4 minutes, or until soft and fragrant, add garlic, saute for 1 minute. Stir in garbanzo beans, and season with salt and pepper; heat through. Place chard in pan, and cook until wilted. Add tomato, squeeze lemon juice over greens, and heat through. Plate, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Top with goat cheese, optional.

Submitter: 
Vanessa C
Description: 
Beans and greens are a perfect combination; earthy and satisfying. This recipe for Swiss chard with garbanzo beans, onion, and fresh tomato is brightened with lemon juice and makes a perfect vegetarian main dish or a tantalizing side dish for fish or meat.Modified from: Swiss Chard with Garbanzo Beans and Fresh Tomatoes
