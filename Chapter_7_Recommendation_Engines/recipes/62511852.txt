Title:
An Authentic Spaghetti alla Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
120g Pancetta (or Unsmoked Streaky Bacon) - cut into narrow strips about 1 inch long
½ tsp Garlic - finely chopped
2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 tbsp Butter
150ml dry White Wine
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
500g Spaghetti or to own requirement
1 to 2 tbsp flat-leaf Parsley (to taste), - finely chopped
3 tbsp Parmesan Cheese (Reggiano)  grated (or 4 tbsp if only using Parmesan)
1 tbsp Pecorino Romano - grated
4 to 5 Egg Yolks or 2 whole eggs + 2 egg yolks (Just yolks makes it richer and you can use less or no cream)
140ml carton double cream (Optional but more creamy and delicious with)

Directions:
Fill a large pan with water with tbsp salt and tbsp olive oil, place over high heat, and bring to a boil;
Add the spaghetti, and stir until all the strands are submerged; Cook until al dente (about 10 mins)
Put the olive oil, butter, and garlic in a frying pan and cook gently until garlic begins to sizzle;
Add the pancetta and cook on a slightly higher heat, 2 to 3 minutes until the pancetta and garlic begin to brown, but not crisp;
Add the wine and cook until it has reduced by half; Remove from the heat and set aside;
While the pasta is cooking, put the parsley and the grated cheeses in a bowl large enough to serve the pasta in;
Add the yolks and/or whole eggs, cream (if using) and season lightly with salt and generously with pepper; Mix thoroughly.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
An authentic delightful, creamy Italian dish, suitable for all occasions. The key is the hot pasta and pancetta mixing well with the uncooked egg mixture.
