Title:
Mother's Date Dips from 1940's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tbsp. butter
1/3 C. sugar
1 egg
1 C. Flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4. C. heavy cream
1 tsp. vanilla
3 C. pitted dates

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees F.
Either grease or spray with cooking release two cookie sheets.
Using a mixer, Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Add the egg and continue beating.
Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt.
Add alternately to creamed mixture with heavy cream.
Dip Dates into batter one at a time and place on greased baking sheet.
Bake one sheet at a time in the center of the oven.
Bake 375 for 10 minutes

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
My mother used to bake these cookies for  a special occasion, Christmas or for weddings. This recipe has been in the family for 100 years and was passed down from my Grandmother to my mother. Hope you enjoy it.
