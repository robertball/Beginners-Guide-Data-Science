Title:
Turkey Sausage Button Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979145.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh pizza dough
1 (19.5 ounce) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Sweet Italian Sausage
1/2 cup marinara sauce
1 yellow bell pepper, cut into strips
1 orange bell pepper, cut into strips
1 small red onion, cut into thin wedges
2 cups mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Split pizza dough into two equal portions and let rise at room temperature for 1 1/2 -2 hours.
Preheat oven and pizza stone to 400 degrees.
In a large skillet, brown the sausages according to package directions.  Remove sausages from the skillet, let cool slightly and cut diagonally into 1/4 inch slices.
Lightly flour a wooden pizza board.   Stretch one pizza dough to desired shape and spread with 1/4 cup marinara sauce.  Evenly sprinkle 1 cup mozzarella and top with bell pepper strips, onion wedges and half of the sliced sausage buttons.  Slide the pizza onto the pizza stone and bake for 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
Pizza is fun to prepare at home! This pie is topped with colorful pepper slices and cute 'buttons' made with flavorful Italian sausage slices.
