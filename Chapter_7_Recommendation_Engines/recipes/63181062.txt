Title:
Baked Chicken with Dressing LGLS 174 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 skls, bnls breast
3 cloves peeled minced garlic
1 lime juice whole
3 tbsp evoo
2 tbsp fresh tarragon chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1 garlic clove peeled
1 egg yolk
1/2-1 cup evoo
juice of 1 whole lime
2 tbsp fresh tarragon, chopped
4-6 cups of mixed greens
black pepper

Directions:
1. 350
2. Baking dish -rub chicken with--garlic, salt, pepper, lime oil and tarragon
3. bake covered 30 cool then cut into 1 inch pcs
4. mince puree garlic, tarragon, salt and set aside
5. beat egg yolk and whisk into 1/3 of evoo
6. whisk in tsp lime juice to thin and alternate lime and oil until smooth and mixed
7. stir in tarragon mix and remaining tarragon
8. spread greens and arrange chicken atop and drizzle dressing and garnish with pepper

Submitter: 
Mary Landis
Description: 
Chicken seasoned with tarragon served over fresh salad of greens.Modified from: Baked Chicken with Dressing
