Title:
Sesame-Miso Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 T. sesame seeds, toasted
2 T. white miso (soybean paste or lower-sodium soy sauce
1 T. rice vinegar
1 T. honey
1 T. hot water
1 t. crushed red pepper
2 t. dark sesame oil
4 cups thinly sliced and seeded cucumbers

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients in a large bowl, stirring with a whisk.  Add cucumber; toss to coat.
