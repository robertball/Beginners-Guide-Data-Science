Title:
Vegetarian Stuffed Zucchini Boats on a Bed of Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium Zucchini
2 T Olive Oil
1 Onion finely chopped
1 head garlic minced
6 Celery Ribs chopped
1 lb sliced mushrooms
4 Medium tomatos chopped into small pieces
1 Tablespoon chopped Fresh Parsley
3 Tablespoons chopped Fresh Basil
2 Tablespoons chopped Fresh Oragano
2 Tablespoons Fresh Lemon Juice
1 teaspoon Herbamare or just Sea Salt
1 teaspoon Fresh ground Black Pepper
2 Cups grated Cheddar Cheese
1 Cup Seasoned Bread Crumbs
1 Cup Corn Flake Crumbs
1 Tablespoon Seasoning Salt
1 Tablespoon Sweet Paprika
4 Tablespoons Melted Butter
Fresh Herb leaves for garnish
Parmesan Cheese, freshly grated
Pasta Al Dente for base.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375F degrees
Cut zucchini lengthwise
Use spoon to scoop out zucchini seeds and extra pulp, set aside
Brush zucchini shells with olive oil
Chop up zucchini pulp and place in large bowl
Sauté onions in pan until translucent
Add minced garlic continue to sauté for about 1 minute do not brown garlic
Add sliced cleaned celery
Add sliced cleaned mushrooms, sauté until soft about 5 minutes
Add zucchini pulp, tomatoes, lemon juice, and salt
Mix and bring to simmer, turn stove off.
Allow mixture to cool
Add fresh herbs and black pepper
In casserole dish lined with parchment paper line hollowed out zucchini's, in single layer
Scoop mixture into hollowed out zucchini's, pile on in mounds
Cover dish with Aluminum foil
Bake in pre-heated oven for 30 minutes, until zucchini shells are cooked and tender
In another bowl place bread crumbs and cornflake crumbs and combine
Add 1 tablespoon Seasoning Salt and Paprika, combine into crumb mixture
Melt butter
Pour over bread crumb and cornflake crumb mixture
Mix in grated cheese into buttered crumbs
Remove stuffed zucchini from oven when cooked
Cover with crumb/cheese mixture
Return to oven bake for 10 more minutes
Turn oven to broil
Watching Zucchini's brown toppings
Remove from oven.
Serve over pasta al dente use juices in reserve to pour over pasta.
Garnish with Fresh Herb Leaves and Freshly grated parmesan cheese
Enjoy!
