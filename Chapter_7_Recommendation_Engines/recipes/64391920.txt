Title:
Mom's Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small package sliced mushrooms
1 lb ground beef
1/4 cup flour
dash of pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 small carton sour cream

Directions:
Sautee meat and mushrooms in lg saucepan over medium heat.  Stir in flour and seasonings.  Cook 5 more minutes.  Add cream of mushroom soup and cook for 10 minutes.  Add sour cream just before serving.
Serve over noodles or rice.
