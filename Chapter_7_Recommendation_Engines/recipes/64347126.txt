Title:
Chicken, Wild Rice & Broccoli Au Gratin Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh broccoli florets
1.5 lbs of cooked, cubed boneless chicken (cooked with rotisserie seasoning)
2 boxes of Long grain and Wild Rice (I use Farmhouse)
2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Prepare the wild rice mix per the box instructions on the stovetop.  When about 3/4 done, remove from stove and pour into a large mixing bowl.
Add broccoli to the rice in the bowl.  Add in the chicken, cheese and the cream soup, garlic and onion powders.  Mix all to combine well.
Pour into a buttered 13x9x2 baking dish.   (optional:  top with crushed Ritz crackers or panko bread crumbs)
Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes or until bubbly.
