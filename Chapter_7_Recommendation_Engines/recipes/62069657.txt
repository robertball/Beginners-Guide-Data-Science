Title:
Breakfast Fruit Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tortilla
3 tsp strawberry jam
3 tbsp ricotta cheese
1/2 cup sliced fresh strawberries
1 tbsp sliced almonds, toasted

Directions:
Spread preserves on tortilla. Top with ricotta cheese and then with sliced fruit. Sprinkle with almonds. Starting from one end, roll tightly. Wrap in foil and keep in fridge until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
LISALC98
Description: 
A very healthy and delicious breakfast for those on the go! I like to make it the night before and keep it in the fridge until I go to work.
