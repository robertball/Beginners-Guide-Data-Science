Title:
Chicken with garlicy sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
6 chicken breasts
1/4 cup olive oil (for frying)
8 oz cheddar cheese: sliced to put over chicken
Galic Sauce:
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 cloves of "real" garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degree.
Combine bread crumbs and parmesan cheese together.
Dip chicken breasts in milk, then in bread crumb mixture.
Fry chicken in olive oil about 4 minutes on each side.
Transfer chicken into oven
Blend the garlic sauce ingredients (mayo, sour cream, lemon juice and garlic) together in a blender or majic bullet.
Spoon the sauce over each chicken breast and top with cheddar cheese.
Leave chicken in about 5 more minutes or until the cheese melts and chicken is done (160 degrees inside).

Submitter: 
Toni Losada
Description: 
I love this sauce to use as dipping or for falafel so I decided to try it on chicken and it was delicious!
