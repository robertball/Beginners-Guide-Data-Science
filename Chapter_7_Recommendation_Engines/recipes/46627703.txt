Title:
Sui Gow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/813369.jpg
Ingredients:
Pork
Chinese cabbage
Green Onion
Sesame Oil
Salt
Ginger
Corn Starch
1 egg ( or white only)
Sui Gow wraper

Directions:
Ground Chinese cabbage in food processor and squeeze out the water.
Ground the ginger
Use water to close the Sui Gow.
Boil:
Put in Sui Gow after the water is  boiling. After boiling, add 1 bowl of cool water and let it boil again until the Sui Gow are on the top
