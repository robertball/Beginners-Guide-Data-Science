Title:
Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. rotini macaroni
1 green pepper
1 onion
4 carrots
1 can eagle brand milk
2 c. hellman's
3/4 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Cook macaroni until done.  Drain and cool.  Add vegetables.  Mix sweetened condensed milk.  Hellmans's mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper.  Pour over macaroni mixture. Let set overnight.
