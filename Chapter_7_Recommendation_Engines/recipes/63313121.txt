Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup flour
2/3 cup sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup ripe banana, mashed
1/3 cup butter
2 Tbs. milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
In a large mixer, combine 1 cup of flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add banana, butter, and milk.
Beat with an electric mixer on low until blended; then on high for 2 minutes.
Add eggs and remaining flour. Beat until blended. Stir in nuts.
Pour batter into a greased 8x4x2- inch bread pan. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool.
Wrap and store overnight before slicing.
