Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Butt Steak Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pork butt steaks, trimmed of fat and cut into 1 inch cubes
1 medium zucchini, cut into 1/2 inch rounds
1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch cubes
1 large bell pepper (any color), cut into 1/2 inch pieces
2 medium onions, chopped and diced
2 leeks, white parts only, washed well and cut into 1/2 inch rounds (another onion can be substituted)
2 stalks celery cut into 1/2 pieces
2 1/2 cups salsa (heat of your choosing)
1 10 ounce can of diced tomatoes with green chiles
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
1 cup frozen corn kernels
1 cup instant rice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in slow cooker on low heat  with the exception of the corn and rice.  Stir in corn and rice the last 30 minutes of cooking and turn slow cooker to high heat.  Add a tiny bit of water or tomato juice should stew be too thick.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
Purchased too much produce at the farm market's close out sale and it was starting to spoil so I hastily put this meal together.  A cheaper cut of meat turns tender in this stew with a kick and you determine the level of heat you desire.
