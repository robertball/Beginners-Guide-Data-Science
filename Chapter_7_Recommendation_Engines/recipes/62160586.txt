Title:
Grilled Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 long skinny russet potatoes
Olive oil for brushing
1/2 Tbs. Paprika
1 tsp Kosher salt
1/2 tsp Black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Wash and quarter the potatoes length wise leaving the skins on.
Par boil the potato wedges for ten minutes.
Drain the potato wedges.
Brush with olive oil on all three sides.
Mix the paprika, salt, pepper and garlic and sprinkle on cut side of the potatoes.
Grill on cut edges 5 - 8 minutes each side.

Submitter: 
noogie01
Description: 
Like big steak cut fries, but only on the grill. They are also great reheated with a little cheese.
