Title:
Herbed Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c buttermilk baking mix
1/2 c shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 t thyme leaves, crushed
1/4 t basil leaves, crushed
1 t ground black pepper
2/3 c milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  In bowl, stir together baking mix, cheese, thyme, basil and pepper.  Add milk and stir just until blended.  Place dough on lightly floured board; knead 10 times.  With rolling pin, roll dough to 1/2-inch thickness.  Cut dough into shapes using 2 1/2 inch biscuits.  Reroll scraps and repeat.  Place on ungreased baking sheets.  Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
It's A New Day
Description: 
When my girls went to Girl Scout camp and had to pack a non-perishable lunch, I made these for them every day. You can add more cheese if you want them cheesier but may need to add more milk if mix gets too dry.
