Title:
Crockpot Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Eggs
1/2 bag frozen shredded hashbrowns
1 tsp. olive oil
1 Medium Onion, diced
1 Red Pepper, diced (optional)
1 small can chopped Green Chilies (optional)
1 pkg brown and serve suasage links
2 Cup shredded Cheddar Cheese, seperated
Salt & Pepper to taste
Salsa (Optional)

Directions:
Break all eggs into a large bowl and beat to an even consistency, stirring in 1 cup of cheddar and can of chilies. Cut sausage into bite size pieces and place in the bottom of the crockpot. Place the hashbrowns over the sausage.
Heat the oil in a large skillet, add onion and pepper, sautee for 3 minutes. Add the eggs and scramble until ALMOST firm, adding salt and pepper to taste. Place egg mixture over the hashbrowns and cook on low for 8 hours. Add last cup of cheddar 15 minutes before serving.  Serve with salsa on the side.
