Title:
Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups raw cranberries, ground
1 cup sugar or 14 packets Equal sweetener
1 11 ounce can mandarin oranges;drain, reserve juice
1 8 1/4 ounce can pineapple tidbits;drain and reserve juice
1 cup coconut flakes
1 large banana,mashed,optional
2 envelopes of plain gelatin
1 cup sour cream
1 cup whipped cream or frozen whipped topping,thawed

Directions:
In a bowl, mix together the ground cranberries and sugar. Let stand until sugar dissolves, about 10-15 minutes. Drain orages and pineapple;reserve 3/4 cup of juice. Add oranges and pineapple to cranberries. Mix in coconut. In a saucepan, sprinkle gelatin over fruit juice and heat until warm and gelatin is dissolved. Add to cranberries. Add sour cream and whipped cream or whipped topping. Mix in mashed banana,if desired. Mix thoroughly. Pour into a 8 x 9 x 2 inch dish. Refrigerate until set. "Overnight is best."
