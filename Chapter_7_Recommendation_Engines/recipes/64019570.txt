Title:
Sausage Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1118072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoons butter
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
1/4 cup chopped onions
2 cups shredded pepper jack cheese
16 ozs. Johnsonville mild ground sausage or 13.5 ozs. Johnsonville smoked sausage (diced fine)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Use the butter to grease a tube pan.
If using the mild ground sausage, brown it in a skillet with the onions and set aside to cool.
Quarter biscuits. Mix biscuits, salsa, cooled sausage mixture (or diced smoked sausage) and cheese in a large bowl. Pour into prepared tube pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes.
Allow to cool for 10 minutes, then turn upside down onto a serving plate.
OPTIONAL...top with sour cream and green onions before serving.
