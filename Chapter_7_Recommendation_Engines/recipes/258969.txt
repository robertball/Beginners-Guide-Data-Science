Title:
Paleo Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4561549.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/4 cup coconut milk
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Beat eggs in a bowl. Stir in coconut milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
Heat a skillet over medium-low heat; pour in egg mixture. Cook and stir until fluffy and firm, 5 to 7 minutes.

Delicious; add anything else you want to toss in, like chives, dill, or parsley.
