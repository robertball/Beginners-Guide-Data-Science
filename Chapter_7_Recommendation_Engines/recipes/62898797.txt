Title:
Mom's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. Hamburger
1 cup dry Bread Crumbs
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 small onion (chopped)
1 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard (or 1 tsp prepared mustard)
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp ground sage
1/2 cup ketchup

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except ketchup.  Spread in ungreased loaf pan, 9c5x3 inches.  Spread ketchup generously over top of loaf.  Cook uncovered in 350 degree oven until done, about 1 1/2 hours.
Individual Meatloaves:  Shape meat mixture into 6 small loaves; place in ungreased oblong pan, 13x9x2 inches.  Spread ketchup over loaves.  Cook uncovered 45 minutes.
