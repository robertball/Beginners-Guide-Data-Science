Title:
Peanut Butter Puppy Poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c whole wheat flour
1 TB baking powder
1 c peanut butter
1 c milk

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 F. combine dry ingredients in bowl. mix wet in another bowl, then add to dry ingredients and mix well. place dough on lightly floured surface and knead. roll dough to 1/4" and cut shapes.
Bake and cool on rack completely
