Title:
Black Bean Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 carrots, chopped fine
1 stalk celery, chopped fine
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
2-3 cups chicken stock
2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
1 cup frozen corn (or one 8.75 ounce can whole kernel corn)
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes (see footnote)

Directions:
In large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat; cook onion, garlic, celery, corn and carrots, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until onion is softened. Add chili powder and cumin; cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add stock, 1 can of the beans, and pepper; bring to boil.
Meanwhile, in food processor or blender, puree together tomatoes and remaining can of beans; add to pot. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes or until carrots are tender.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Half of the black beans in this spicy soup are pureed with tomatoes, the other half are simmered with carrots, onion, celery and kernels of corn.Modified from: Spicy Black Bean Vegetable Soup
