Title:
Old-Fashioned Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507339.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds beef top round or Sirlon steak
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon pepper
4 tablespoon all-purpose flour
4 tablespoon canola oil
1 large onion, chopped
2 6 oz. pkg. portabella mushrooms coarsely chopped
21 ounces can tomato juice
2 cans diced canned tomatoes
2 1/2 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 1/2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
2 teaspoon prepared mustard
1 slice per serving mozzarella cheese (optional)

Directions:
Cut steak into serving pieces; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Using a mallet, pound flour into the meat. In a large skillet, brown meat in oil on both sides. Transfer to a shallow 3-qt. baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray.
In the same skillet, saute onion and mushrooms in drippings until tender. Stir in the remaining ingredients. Pour over meat. Cover and bake at 300 degrees F for 2-2 1/2 hours or until tender.
Serve over wide egg noodles. Suggested side: green beans.
