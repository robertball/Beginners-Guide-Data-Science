Title:
White Chip Orange Dream Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup (2 sticks) butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
1 egg
2 to 3 tbsp. grated orange peel
2 cups (12 ounce package) White chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine flour , baking soda and salt in a small bowl.
Beat butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar in large mixer bowl until creamy. Beat in egg and orange peel.
Gradually beat in flour mixture.
Stir in morsels.
Drop dough by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are light golden brown.
Let stand for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
OH MY GOD, these are so delicious!!!  This is my favorite cookie recipe.  Similar to the traditional chocolate chip cookie recipe but substituting white chocolate chips and adding grated orange peel.
