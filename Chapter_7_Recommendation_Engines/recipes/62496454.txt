Title:
Hamburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. ground lean hamburger
1/2 cup pasta stars (or alphabet pasta)
1 bullion cube
1 T tomato paste
1 small can salsa
6 cups water

Directions:
Brown the hamburger in a sauce pan on the stove. Add the other ingredients and cook until the pasta is done.
