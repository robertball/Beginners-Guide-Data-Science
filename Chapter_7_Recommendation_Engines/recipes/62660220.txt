Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 onion, chopped
1 teaspoon minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 (32 ounce) package tater tots
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown the ground beef with the onions and garlic. Drain excess fat, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Spread the beef mixture evenly over the bottom of a 2 quart casserole dish. In a small bowl, stir the soup into the milk until smooth; pour over beef. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese evenly over the top. Arrange tater tots evenly over beef layer.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until tater tots are brown and crispy.

Submitter: 
Heidi Chambers
Description: 
A kid favorite meal of ground beef, sauteed onions and mushroom soup topped with cheddar cheese and baked under crispy tater tots.Modified from: Tater Tot Bake
