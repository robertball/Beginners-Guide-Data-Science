Title:
Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 onions chopped
6 cups chicken broth
2 chicken breast halves
1 (29 ounce) can/jar diced tomatoes
2 - 4 oz. cans green chilies
1 tablespoon cumin
2 tablespoons mixed chilli powder
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 teaspoon salt or to taste
1 cup shredded cheddar
1 avocado
tortilla chips

Directions:
Saute onions in oil 2-3 minutes.
Add chicken broth and chicken.
Bring to a boil.
When chicken is cooked remove, shred and return to soup pot.
Add tomatoes, green chilies, cumin, chilli powder, cilantro and salt.
Simmer about 30 minutes.
Serve with cheese and tortilla chips and avocado.

Description: 
Easy soup to make and like many soups it tastes even better the next day.
