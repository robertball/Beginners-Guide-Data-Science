Title:
Easy Lemon Chiffon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz pkg lemon gelatin (or any flavor)
1 c boiling water
1 small container non-dairy whippped topping
1 graham cracker crumb crust

Directions:
Mix the gelatin in the boiling water, stir to dissolve.  Allow to cool to room temperature.  Beat the gelatin mixture into the non-dairy whipped topping.  Pour into the graham cracker crust.  Chill 3 hours or till firm.

Submitter: 
SHECOOKS2
Description: 
This pies is too easy and so luscious!  You can substitute any flavor of gelatin that you prefer!!
