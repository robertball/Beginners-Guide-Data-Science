Title:
Original Gooey Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1	cup all-purpose flour
3	tablespoons sugar
1/3	cup butter
Filling
1-1/4	cups sugar
3/4	cup butter
1	egg
1	cup all-purpose flour
2/3	cup evaporated milk
1/4	cup light corn syrup
1	teaspoon vanilla
Powdered sugar

Directions:
1. Combine flour and sugar for crust in mixing bowl. Cut in butter until mixture resembles fine crumbs which begin to cling together.
2. Pat into bottom and sides of greased 9-inch square baking pan.
3. Beat sugar and butter together until light and fluffy.
4. Beat in egg until well combined.
5. Add flour alternating with evaporated milk, mixing each addition in well before adding next.
6. Add corn syrup and vanilla. Mix at medium speed until well blended.
7. Pour into prepared crust. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
8. Bake at 350°F for 25 to 35 minutes or until cake is nearly set in middle. Do not overcook.
Cool in pan.

Submitter: 
Gregg Hollander
Description: 
This is the original recipe from the St. Louis Junior League cookbook, St. Louis Days … St. Louis Nights.
