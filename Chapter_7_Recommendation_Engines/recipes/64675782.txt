Title:
Pasta e Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
12 ounces chopped onion
14 ounces slivered carrots
14 ounces diced celery
48 ounces canned diced tomatoes
2 cups cooked, drained red kidney beans
2 cups cooked, drained white kidney beans
2 3/4 cups beef broth
1 tablespoon dried oregano
2 1/2 teaspoons black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons Tabasco sauce
2 24 ounce jars of marinara sauce
8 ounces small macaroni
5 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook the beef in the oil in large stockpot.
Add onions, carrots, celery, and tomatoes and simmer; 10 minutes
Add the beans, broth,seasonings, and marinara sauce
Simmer; 45 minutes
Add pasta and simmer
