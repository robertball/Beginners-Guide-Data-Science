Title:
One Bowl Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
210 ml vegetable oil
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 3/4 cup	flour
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
150 g dark chocolate - (chopped)

Directions:
Preheat over to 180 C
Combine eggs, oil and sugar. Mix well.
Sir in vanilla.
Add the dry ingredients.
Sir with wooden sppon no more than 50 strokes (don't overmix or they will be tough)
If desired, add chocolate pieces. Stir a few more times to mix in.
Spread batter into a greased 22 x 23 cm pan/ 9x13 in
Bake at 180 C for 28-35 min
