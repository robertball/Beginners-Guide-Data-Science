Title:
Pressure Cooked Pinto Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds dried pinto beans, washed, sorted and drained.
2 tablespoons oil
1 tablespoon salt
Enough water to cover beans

Directions:
Add all ingredients to pressure cooker.  Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
Great flavored beans made easy in an electric pressure cooker.
