Title:
Chicken Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Chicken breast cut into 1/2" cubes
1/2 onion
4 cups frozen mixed vegatables
1 12oz jar chicken gravy
2 medium potatoes, peeled & cut into 1/2" cubes.
1/2 bag frozen potatoe rounds
Spice to taste.  I used onion powder, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.  Also a dash ot thyme.

Directions:
saute the chicken breast with the onion until cooked.  Cut into 1/2" cubes and set aside.
Boil the peeled & cubed potatoe for 5 minutes to pre-cook. .
Thaw the frozen veggies so they are not frozen or icy.
In a 2qt mixing bowl, combine the chicken, veggies, potatoes, and gravy.  Add spices and stir until well mixed with gravy.
Pour into a 9" square pryex baking dish.
Arrange a single layer of frozen potatoe rounds on the top of the mixture.
Bake at 375' for 45 minutes.  Mixture should be bubbly around edges.

Submitter: 
Cindypray
Description: 
I had Tater Tots in my freezer, but every recipe seemed to use ground beef, so I took the Savory Tater Tot Recipe and revised it for chicken.
