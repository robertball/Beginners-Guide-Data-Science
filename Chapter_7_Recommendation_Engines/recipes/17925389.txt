Title:
Green Curry Stew With Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup finely chopped onion
4 large garlic cloves, minced
4 tsp freshly chopped ginger
2 Tbsp finely chopped cilantro stems
1 1/2 tsp green curry paste
1 1/2 tsp brown sugar
1 tsp turmeric
1/4 cup water
1 Tbsp fish sauce
6 Tbsp olive oil
1 cup coconut milk
2 cups chicken stock
1 1/2 lbs. large uncooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro leaves
2 cups cooked basmati rice

Directions:
In a food processor, combine oinion, garlic, ginger, cilantro stems, 1/2 tsp of curry paste, brown sugar, turmeric, water and fish sauce.
Pulse until a smooth paste forms, taste to increase curry paste, then process until smooth.
Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat until shimmering, then cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until fragrant and just beginning to stick to the pan, about 6 - 7 minutes.
Whisk in coconut milk and stock and cook for 10 minutes.
Add shrimp and cook another 3 - 4 minutes, until shrimp are cooked through.
Stir in cilantro leaves.
Serve over cooked rice.
