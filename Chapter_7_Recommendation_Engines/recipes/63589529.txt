Title:
Meatballs (for Spaghetti Sauce) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 c. bread crumbs
1/4 c. grated parmesan (grated and/or powdered)
2 eggs
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 c. minced parsley
1/4 c. minced onions

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and roll into balls.
Put them in sauce to cook, or bake at 350 degress 20-30 minutes.
