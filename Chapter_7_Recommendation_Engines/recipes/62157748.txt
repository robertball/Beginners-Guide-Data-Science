Title:
Pom-Straw-Ana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284715.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pomegranate juice
1 cup frozen whole strawberries
1 banana
1 cup plain yogurt

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
A healthy and refreshing smoothie.  Enjoy for breakfast or as an energizing snack before a workout!
