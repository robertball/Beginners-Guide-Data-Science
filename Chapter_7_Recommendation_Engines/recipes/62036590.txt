Title:
Grannies Pickled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
1 can beets juice and beets
2 tbsp pickling spices

Directions:
Combine vinegar, sugar, and spices in a small pan.  Heat, stirring several times to boil about 10 minutes.  Add red food coloring if desired.  Add beets and juice.  Store in refrigerator in a sealed jar.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This is the only pickled egg recipe that I like.  I love the beets especially!  My grandmother used to make this for me when I was growing up.  She truley was a very good cook in her time!
