Title:
Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds chuck roast
1 packet dry onion soup mix
1 cup water with 2 Beef Bouillon cubes.
1 tbs Worcestershire sauce
parsley
garlic minced.
baby carrots, chopped
1 onion, chopped
6 potatoes, quartered red potatoes
1 stalk celery, chopped

Directions:
Make rub using ground pepper, kosher salt, garlic powder and paprika. Rub into meat then spray with olive oil spray.
Brown on all sides in a large skillet over high heat.
Place in the slow cooker and add the soup mix, bouillon water, and onion.
About 1/3 way through add carrots, potatoes and celery.
Cover and cook on low setting for 8 to 10 hours.

Submitter: 
Sheri
Description: 
Browned chuck roast is slow cooked for 8 to 10 hours along with potatoes, carrots, onion, celery, and dehydrated onion soup mix.Modified from: Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast
