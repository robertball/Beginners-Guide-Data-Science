Title:
Almond shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups of cake and pastry flour
3/4 cup of ground almonds
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
1 cup icing sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350F (180C)
2. In medium bowl stir together flour, almonds and salt. Set aside.
3. In large bowl, beat butter and 1/2 cup icing sugar using electrical mixer. Beat on high until smooth and stiff. Beat in vanilla.
4. Add flour mix in thirds to the butter mixture, using electrical mixer or a spoon. Mix together until evenly smooth.
5. Pinch piece of dough to make about 1 inch ball. Roll it in your hand and then flatten it out into disc. Put piece or two of chocolate chip and roll it into ball again.
6. Place it on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
7. Repeat until dough is finished.
8. Bake in center of oven until it just starts to get sun kissed (around 10 minutes).
9. Place remaining icing sugar in sifter and spread it evenly over the cookies. Best done wile cookies are still worm.
10. Enjoy!
11. For interesting twist, try placing maraschino cherry or candied fruit in center.
