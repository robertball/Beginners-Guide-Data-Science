Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz cream cheese
1 tbsp soft butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups sifted powdered sugar

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, combine cream cheese, butter and vanilla. Blend with electric mixer at low speed until smooth and fluffy.
2. Gradually add sugar, continue beating at medium speed until fluffy.
3. Spread on cake.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
This frosting goes well with the Applesauce Date Cake.
