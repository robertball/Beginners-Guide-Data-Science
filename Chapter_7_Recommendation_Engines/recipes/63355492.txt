Title:
Sweet Breakfast Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/892804.jpg
Ingredients:
3 medium russet potatoes, diced
1/2 medium sweet onion, chopped
1 golden delicious apple, diced
1 package al fresco Sweet Apple sausage links, diced
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp ground fennel
1/2 tsp rosemary
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 cup dried cranberries (optional)

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large saute pan.  Add potatoes and onions and cook until potatoes are slightly brown and onions and translucent.
Heat small saute pan.  Saute apple, sausage, salt, pepper, fennel, rosemary, red pepper, and cranberries if desired for 5 minutes.
Add contents of small pan to potato mixture and serve.

A nice accompaniment to eggs or pancakes!
