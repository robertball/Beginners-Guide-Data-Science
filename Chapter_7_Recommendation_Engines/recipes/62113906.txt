Title:
Japanese Style Homemade Apply Honey Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pack (125g) House Vermont Curry Sauce Mix Cube - Mild
1/2 pack (125g) House Vermont Curry Sauce Mix Cube - Medium Hot
500g Chicken tenderloin, cut into bite size
2 medium onions, chopped
2 medium potatoes, cubed
1/2 carrot, cubed
2 tablespoons cooking oil
1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
7 cups water
Chicken marinade:
4 tablespoon light soy sauce
2 tablespoon Chinese cooking wine
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon tapioca starch or potato starch

Directions:
In a medium bowl, marinate chicken cubes with soy sauce,  cooking wine and sugar. Sprinkle tapioca starch over chicken and massage chicken cubes to mix well. Marinate for 20 minutes
In a Chinese wok, heat cooking oil. Saute onions until tender.
Add chicken to onions and stir fry till lightly brown. Discard remaining marinade. Add white wine to moist chicken and onions if desired.
Add potatoes and carrots. Continue stir-frying for 3 minutes
Add water and bring to boil. Turn heat to medium and cook covered for 10 minutes or until chicken is tender.
Remove the wok from heat. Break House Vermont Curry Sauce Mix Cubes into pieces and add to wok. Mix until sauce cubes completely disolved. Bring back to low heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes or until desired consistency is reached
Serve over multigrain rice or white rice and freshly boiled vegetables

Submitter: 
Anita Chen
Description: 
Delicious Japanese style chicken curry with a touch of apple and honey flavor. Super easy to prepare. Serve with multigrain rice or white rice and freshily boiled veggies. Bon Appetit!
