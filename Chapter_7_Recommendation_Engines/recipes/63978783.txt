Title:
Aye Aye Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c mayonnaise
2 cloves garlic, crushed
2 tsp Dijon mustard
1 egg (pasturized)
1/2 c olive oil
1/2 c vegetable oil
2 tsp lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. In a mixing bowl whisk mayo, garlic, mustard and egg yolk.
2. Slowly add vegetable oil while whisking until sauce emulsifies
3. Repeat 2nd step with olive oil
4. Stir in lemon juice, salt and pepper
5. Refrigerate in container with lid at least 30 mins
Sauce can be used up to 3 days

Submitter: 
Tiare Tierney
Description: 
Serve the kids fishsticks, fries or nuggets with some southern French flavor.
