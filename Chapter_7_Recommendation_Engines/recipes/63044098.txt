Title:
Peanut Butter Ice Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One can sweetened condensed milk
One half cup butter
One half to three quarters of a cup of peanut butter - your choice of smooth or chunky
One teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a heavy saucepan and cook over medium heat until well combined.  Store in the fridge for up to two weeks.

Submitter: 
Jeanie
Description: 
When my medical provider said that I had to eliminate chocolate, I immediately thought of peanut butter sauce for my ice cream.  This is quick, easy and tastes great.
