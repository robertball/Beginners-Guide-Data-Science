Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 dozen eggs, hard boiled
salt, to taste
1/2 cup chopped pimento-stuffed green
olives
Dried parsley
Paprika
Miracle Whip
Prepared mustard

Directions:
Hard Boil eggs. Peel and cut in half length-wise. Scoop out yolks into mixing bowl.  Use hand mixer to crumble yolks.  Add the green olives and salt.  Add Miracle Whip, 1/2 cup at a time until mixture is slightly creamed.  Add 1 tablespoon of prepared mustard at a time until desired taste.  (Amount of Miracle Whip and mustard is according to personal taste and texture.)  Blend with mixer until mixture is soft enough to stuff egg white halves.  Stuff egg halves and sprinkle with paprika and crushed dried parsley. Arrange on platter and watch them disappear.

Submitter: 
Barb Wahnsiedler Klein
Description: 
This recipe is always requested by my family for Easter dinner.  I have to hide the dish until dinner time or it would be gone ahead of time.
