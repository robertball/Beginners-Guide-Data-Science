Title:
Colorful Taco Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984478.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 cup baking mix
1/3 cup cold water
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
Toppings:
Sour cream
Shredded lettuce
RED GOLD® DICED TOMATOES or REDPACK® DICED TOMATOES in JUICE

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.  Grease an 8x8 inch baking dish.  In a skillet cook ground beef and taco seasoning according to package directions.
In a mixing bowl combine baking mix and cold water.  Mix until soft dough forms; spread in prepared baking dish.  Spread beef mixture over dough.
Bake, uncovered, 25 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean; do not over bake.  Sprinkle with cheese and let stand 1 to 2 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Cut into 6 equal pieces and serve with toppings.

Submitter: 
Red Gold®, Inc.
Description: 
Get ready for a new dimension to the ever-popular taco.  With this recipe you can prepare the recipe in the oven and apply your favorite topping before eating.
