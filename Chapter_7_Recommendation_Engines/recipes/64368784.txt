Title:
Chicken & Biscuit Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stewing chicken (4-5 pounds)
2 ribs celery, chopped
1 small onion,
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
4 & 1/2 tablespoons flour
Biscuits (recipe follows)
2 cups sifted flour
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
4 teaspoons baking powder
3/4 cup shortening
milk

Directions:
Simmer chicken, celery and onion in 4 cups water with salt & pepper until chicken is done.
Let chicken sit in broth to cool enough to handle; remove chicken discard skin and bones.
Cut chicken into medium size pieces.
Reduce stock to 2 & 1/2 cups by boiling; remove excess fat.  Thicken with flour mixed with 6 tablespoons water, salt and pepper to taste,, will be a gravy.
Arrange pieces of chicken in bottom of a 12x8x2 baking dish or 2 quart casserole.  Pour gravy over chicken and let cool.  ---  Meanwhile make the biscuits to have ready to use.  And preheat oven.
Biscuit directions:
Sift dry ingredients and cut in shortening with a pastry blender.  Moisten with milk.               Roll out 1/2 inch thick on lightly floured surface; cut with 2-inch biscuit cutter.
Place Biscuits on top of cooked chicken mixture and bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes or until biscuits are done.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This recipe comes from Colonial Williamsburg Virginia.  It's a one pan dish with the chicken, gravy and biscuits baked on top.
