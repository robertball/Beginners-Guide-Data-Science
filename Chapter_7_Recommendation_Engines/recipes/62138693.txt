Title:
Pasta Cheese & Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/374143.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium head of Cauliflower.
1+1/2 cups Rigatoni or similar.
1+1/2-2 cups of béchamel, with 3 Tbs of grated Parmesan.
1+1/2 cups grated Emmental.

Directions:
Parboil cauliflower florets in salted water, drain & set aside to cool.
( Should remain crisp )
Cook pasta in the same water until 'al dente', drain & set aside to cool.
Spray casserole dish with 'No stick cooking spray'. Approximate size of casserole dish, 10"x4"x3".
Prepare 1+1/2-2 cups of béchamel combined with 3 Tbsp of finely grated Parmesan.
Layer in Rigatoni, Cauliflower, béchamel, grated Emmental until full.
Top with grated Emmental & Parmesan.
Bake in oven 30 minutes-should be 'crusty' on top.

Submitter: 
Ronald
Description: 
Layers of Cauliflower tubular Pasta béchamel & Grated Cheese, baked until crusty on top.
