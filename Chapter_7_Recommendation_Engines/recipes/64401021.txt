Title:
Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (2 sticks), softened, room temperature
½ tablespoon heavy whipping cream
1 teaspoon real vanilla extract
½ cup semi-sweet chocolate , melted and cooled
½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
2½ cups powdered sugar, sifted (spoon into measuring cup to measure)
pinch of salt

Directions:
1. Cream butter until fluffy
2. Add in heavy cream and mix until light and fluffy
3. Mix in vanilla extract
4. Mix in melted chocolate
5. Mix in cocoa powder and powdered sugar until light, fluffy and whipped
6. Stir in salt
7. Frost cupcakes
Makes enough for 30 cupcakes

Jackie
Description: 
Delicious chocolate buttercream icing that stays fluffy.
