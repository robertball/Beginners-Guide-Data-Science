Title:
Spicy Veggie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, chopped
3/4 onion, chopped
3 large carrots, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 lb. frozen green beans
4 cups low sodium chicken broth
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tbsp. oregano
2 tsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. cilantro, chopped
1/2 habanero pepper, chopped
1 tsp. fresh ground pepper
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
In a large pot combine all ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hr or until vegetables are tender.

Submitter: 
Jordan VanderLaan
Description: 
This is a healthy vegetarian and/or diet soup that has a nice kick to it due to addition of habanero pepper.
