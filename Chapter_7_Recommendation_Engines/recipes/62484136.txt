Title:
Herbed Skillet Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can Swanson® Natural Goodness™ Chicken Broth
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
12 small new potatoes, cut into quarters
2 medium carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 stalks celery, cut into 2 inch pieces
1/2 large red onion quartered and separated
2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Mix broth, thyme, pepper, salt, garlic, onion powder, onion, potatoes, carrots and celery in a deep skillet. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 20-30 minutes or until tender.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
Taste the difference Swanson® Chicken Broth can make when potatoes, carrots and celery are cooked in broth instead of water!Modified from: Herbed Skillet Vegetables
