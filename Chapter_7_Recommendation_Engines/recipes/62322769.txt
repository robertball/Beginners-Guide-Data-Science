Title:
Soft Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. active dry yeast
1 1/2 cup warm water
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
4 cups flour
1 egg, beaten
coarse salt, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Dissolve yeast in water in mixing bowl.  Add sugar and salt.  Blend in flour.
Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth.
Cut dough into 10-12 pieces. Roll each into a long rope.  Twist into pretzel or desired shape.
Arrange pretzels on a greased baking sheet.  Brush tops with egg. Sprinkle coarse salt on top.
Bake for 15 minutes or until brown.
