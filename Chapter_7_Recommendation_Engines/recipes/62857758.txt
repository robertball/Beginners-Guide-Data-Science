Title:
Gluten Free Chocolate Seed and Nut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup natural peanut butter, almond butter or Tahini
½ cup honey, brown rice syrup or pure maple syrup
1 tsp pure vanilla
½ tsp salt
2 eggs (or egg replacement)
¼ cup coconut oil
¼ cup ground chia seeds
¼ cup ground almonds
¼ cup ground pumpkin seeds
¼ cup cocoa
¼ cup ground flax seeds
½ cup rice or quinoa flour
½ cups chopped dried fruit (dates, apricots, raisins, apples)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Combine all ingredients.
Roll into balls and place on cookie sheet
Bake 8-10 minutes
