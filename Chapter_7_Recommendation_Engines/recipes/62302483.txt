Title:
Delicious Baked Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 medium russet potatoes
1/2 cup sour cream
1 8oz pkg cream cheese (softened)
Salt, Pepper, & Butter to taste
Fried bacon or bacon bits
Chives
1/2 block grated Colby Jack cheese

Directions:
Wash, peel, and dice potatoes.
Boil until very soft and drain.
Pour hot potatoes over sour cream and cream cheese.
Mash and mix well.
Place a square of butter or margarine on top.
Bake uncovered in 375 degree oven for about 35 minutes.
Remove from oven and sprinkle desired amount of cheese on top.
Bake for about 10 more minutes in the oven.
When potatoes are finished baking, sprinkle bacon/bacon bits and chives on top.

Submitter: 
StarGazer
Description: 
I am not normally a mashed potato fan but I always loved when my mom or grandma would make these for the holidays.  Here is my grandma's recipe with a couple little things I've added in.
