Title:
Big Ray's Pork with Sweet Potatoes Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3321099.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 lb boneless  pork chops (about 1 inch thick)
1 Tbsp canola oil
2 medium sweet potatoes (about 1 pound), peeled and cubed
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1 can (14-1/2 ounces) reduced-sodium chicken broth
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 medium apple, sliced
4 green onions, chopped

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, mix flour, salt and pepper. Cut pork chops into 1 inch cubes.. Dip pork in flour mixture to coat all sides; shake off excess.
In a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray, heat oil over medium-high heat; brown pork in batches. Remove from pan.
Add sweet potatoes, cranberries and broth to same pan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, covered, 4-6 minutes or until potatoes are almost tender. Stir in mustard.
Return pork to pan; add apple and green onions. Return to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, covered, 4-6 minutes or until pork and sweet potatoes are tender. Yield: 4 servings.

Submitter:

Description: 
A perfect combination, sweet potatoes, apple and pork blend perfectly in this simple skillet meal. The cranberries lend a burst of color, making this dish merry and bright.
