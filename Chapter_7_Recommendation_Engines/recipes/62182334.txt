Title:
Twiced Baked Potatoes (Lite) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large baking potatoes
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1/4 cup skim milk
1 cup shredded lowfat cheddar cheese

Directions:
Scrub potatoes and bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour or until tender. Allow potatoes to cool until they can be handled.
Cut baked potatoes in half and carefully scoop out the potato pulp to within 1/4-inch of the skin.
Add butter or margarine, milk and 1/2 cup cheese. Mash potatoes and add salt and pepper to taste.
Fill potato shells with filling and top with remaining cheese.
Bake another 10-15 minutes or until heated through.
