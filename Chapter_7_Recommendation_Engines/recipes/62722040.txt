Title:
Broccoli Beef Stroganoff Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Oven Ready Lasagna Noodles
1 lb ground sirloin or 93% fat free hamburger
2 cans cream of mushroom
4 cups fresh or frozen broccoli florets
1 cup grated cheese
1 cup sour cream
1/2 milk
1/2 cup water
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown & drain meat. Mix in 1 can cream of mushroom soup and sour cream.
Boil broccoli until soft. Drain & add 1/2 c. shredded cheese. Mix well until spreadable.
In bowl, mix 1 can cream of mushroom, milk and water. spread in bottom of 9x13 baking dish or lasagna dish.
Add first layer of noodles leaving space between each noodle and space between dish and noodles. (noodles will expand in baking time.)
Spread thin layer of meat mix.
Spread thin layer of broccoli mix.
Then start over with the noodles and continue layering until you end up with just meat mix on top.
Sprinkle 1/2 cup shredded cheese on top.
Cover with foil.
Bake at 350 degrees, for 35 mins OR let sit for 2-4 hours, then bake at 425 degrees for 20 mins.
Serve with garlic bread or toast.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
I was tired of the normal everyday stuff and didnt want to go out to eat. So I made do with oven ready lasagna noodles, beef and broccoli. Its awesome! A new favorite for my family! Great make ahead dish for busy families on a budget.
