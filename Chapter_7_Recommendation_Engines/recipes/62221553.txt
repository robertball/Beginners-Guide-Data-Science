Title:
Brownie Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Brownie Mix - prepared per instructions
1/2 cup Hersheys Chocolate Syrup
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips (optional)
2 Boxes Frozen Prepare Phyllo Dough cups (30)
Optional fillings
Raspberry Jam
Caramel Ice Cream Topping
Peanut Butter
Prepared Cheesecake filling (from the dairy department)

Directions:
Prepare brownie mix per box directions, add 1/2 cup chocolate syrup and 1/2 cup chocolate chips (these are optional). Place frozen phyllo dough cups on a cookie sheet, if using any type of optional filling add 1/2 teaspoon then fill each approximately 3/4 of the way with brownie dough. If not using any filling just add the brownie dough.  Bake approximately 30 - 40 minutes at 350 degrees or until tops of the brownie start to crack.  Place on a wire rack to cool.
