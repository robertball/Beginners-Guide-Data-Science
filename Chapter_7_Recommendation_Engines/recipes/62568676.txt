Title:
Jacobsen's Sweet stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices torn up white bread
1 red apple, chopped
5 Tablespoons applesauce
1/2 c. raisins
1/8 c. milk
1/4 c. sugar
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 Degrees.
Mix together bread, chopped apple, applesauce, raisins, milk, sugar and egg in a medium sized bowl. Then trasnfer to a Corningware style dish and bake for about 45 minutes.  If it is browning too much on top, then cover with Corningware lid or foil.
Remove from oven and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Nicole Jacobsen
Description: 
This is a recipe from my Husband's family. I believe I first tried it 10 years ago and thought it was great.  Nothing like your traditional stove top stuffing.  I wouldn't reccomend putting this in your turkey.
