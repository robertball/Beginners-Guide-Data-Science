Title:
Delicious Cheese and Rice Filled Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown rice
2 cups water
4 large green or red peppers
1 8 oz package of low fat 4 Cheese Mexican cheese
1 14.5 can of diced tomatoes with green chilies, drained.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook rice in water.
Slice peppers in half and seed. Blanch in boiling water for about 4 minutes or until just soft. Cool immediately in cold water. Place in casserole dish sprayed with cooking oil.
Mix cheese into cooked rice.
Fill peppers with cheese and rice mixture. Top with diced tomatoes.
Bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Peppers filled with a cheese and rice filled mixture, topped with diced tomatoes, make for a delicious and hearty meatless dish that is quick and easy to make.
