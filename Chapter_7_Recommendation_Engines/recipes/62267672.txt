Title:
White Chocolate Covered Dog Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1lb. package almond bark
1 box dog bones (or homemade)

Directions:
Line a sheet pan with wax paper, parchment or tinfoil.
Break the bark into small chunks and put in microwave safe bowl.
Microwave in 30 second increments until totally melted.
Dip half of each bone into the chocolate and place on sheet pan.
Let harden completely and feed to your pooches!

Jessica
Description: 
A wonderful treat for your spoiled pooches!
