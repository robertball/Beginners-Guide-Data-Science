Title:
"Dr. No" Vodka Gin Dirty Wonder Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/864791.jpg
Ingredients:
3 parts Vodka
1 part Gin
1 part dry vermouth
1 part olive brine
cubed ice
green olives

Directions:
Put the liquid ingredients into a shaker
Add ice
Shake, not stir - until the shaker is very cold
Filter into martini glasses
Garnish with olives

Submitter: 
Wonder Becky
Description: 
I once found a recipe for a James Bond martini.  It used more gin than vodka.  I changed it up to use more vodka than gin and made it dirty.  Since it's the reverse of the James Bond, I call it the Dr. No Martini.  It's just the right combo and very smooth.
