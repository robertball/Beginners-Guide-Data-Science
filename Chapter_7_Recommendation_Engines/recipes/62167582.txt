Title:
STEVES CRABBY GRILLED CHEESE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 SLICES SOFT RYE
4 OZ GRUEYERE CHEESE SLICED (OR AMERICAN SLICES)
SPRAY OLIVE OIL OR BUTTER
½ lb. COOKED COOLED SNOW CRAB
½ lb. COOKED COOLED BLUE CRAB
½ c. MAYO
4 SCALLIONS CHOPPED
½ tsp LIME JUICE AND ZEST
½ tsp COUNTRY DIJON MUTARD
DASH WORCHESTERSHIRE SAUCE
MD BAY SEASONING
4 MINT LEAVES CHOPPED
SEA SALT AND PEPPER

Directions:
MIX CRAB AND THE LAST INGREDIENTS LIGHTLY W/ FORK
HEAT GRIDDLE/LG PAN OVER LOW-MED HEAT
SPRAY RYE BREAD WITH OIL OR BUTTER ON 1 SIDE
PUT 4 SLICES BREAD ON GRIDDLE OIL SIDE DOWN
PUT 1 SLICE CHEESE ON EACH BREAD SLICE AND BROWN SLIGHTLY
DIVIDE CRAB MIX ON 2 SLICES
TOP W/ OTHER TOASTED CHEESE SLICE
REMOVE TO PLATE
AND REPEAT FOR 2 MORE SAMMIES
SLICE IN HALF AND SERVE
SERVE WITH FRESH TOMATO BASIL SOUP AND GLASS OF CHARDONEY
