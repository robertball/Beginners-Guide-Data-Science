Title:
raisin-pineapple sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. firmly packed light brown sugar
2 tbsp. corn starch
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. ground cloves
1/2 c. raisins
1 15 1/2 oz. can pineapple shunks
1 3/4 c. pineapple juice
2 tbsp. soft butter
1/4 c. cooking sherry
6 maraschino cherries(cut in 1/4ths)

Directions:
combine brown sugar, corn starch, dry mustard and cloves into saucepan. blend in next 4 ingredients--stir until smooth. cook over low heat until it boils and thickens, stirring frequently. add sherry and cherries. continue simmering for 10 minutes. serve hot over baked ham.

Submitter: 
ladderman229
Description: 
The perfect compliment to baked ham, made with pineapples, raisins, maraschino cherries and sherry.
