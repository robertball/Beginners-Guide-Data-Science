Title:
Simple Jerk Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp olive oil
3 Tbsp lime juice
1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
2 green onions
2 garlic cloves
1 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp dried ginger
1 Tbsp brown sugar
3/4 tspground allspice
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1 teaspoon kosher salt plus more
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 habanero pepper, seeded

Directions:
-  blend all ingredients until smooth and marinade chicken in the sauce for at least an hour.
-  preheat oven to 425F
- roast in oven for approx 40 minutes, turning once.

Classic flavour with ingredients that are usually on hand
