Title:
Margarita Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/533806.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Box - Yellow Butter Recipe Cake Mix (Betty Crocker)
3/4 Cup - Lemon Lime Soda
1.5 Shots - Jose Cuervo Tequila
1/2 Cup - Jose Cuervo Margarita Mix (Classic Lime)
3 - Whole eggs
1 - Egg yolk
1/2 Cup - Margarine or butter

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 325 degrees.  In a small bowl, melt margarine or butter.  Add cake mix to large mixing bowl.
Add margarine, all eggs, and margarita mix. Stir just a little.  Add soda next, and stir by hand until foam is gone.  Last add tequila and mix completely.
Place cupcake cups in a tin.  Pour batter into cups, until 3/4 full.  Bake for 30 minutes or until toothpick (inserted into cakes) comes out clean.
