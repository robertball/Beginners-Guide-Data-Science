Title:
Steak Marinade Extraordinaire Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1097695.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
1 tablespoon Sriracha hot chili sauce
1 tablespoon steak seasoning

Directions:
Combine olive oil, vinegar, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, onion flakes, Sriracha sauce, and steak seasoning in a resealable bag.

Submitter: 
Bonnie Stewart
Description: 
I have been making this marinade for years. Everybody raves over how flavorful the steak always tastes. You can adjust vinegar and chili sauce to taste. Amazing!
