Title:
Nesquik brownie in a mug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons Nesquik
1 pinch salt
3 tablespoons water
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Mix together flour, Nesquik and salt in a mug. Add water and olive oil. Mix well.
Put in the microwave for 1 minute 55 seconds. Let it cool until ready to eat. (about 2 minutes)  Enjoy!
