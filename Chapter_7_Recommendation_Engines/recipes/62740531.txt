Title:
Mom's Jumbo Date Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
½ cup margarine
1½ cups flour
½ tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
½ lb dates finely chopped
1 cup golden raisins
½ cup chopped walnuts
1tsp vanilla
Spices
1½ tsp Cloves, 2tsp Cinnamon, and pinch of Nutmeg

Directions:
Cream together brown sugar and margarine.  Add eggs and vanilla. Mix flour, soda, salt and spices together then blend into creamed mixture. Stir in dates, raisins and walnuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto baking sheet. Bake at 325 F about 11-13 min. Cookies are quite soft when removed from oven, but will firm up a bit as they cool. Place on rack to cool for 10 min. Do NOT over bake. Cool and keep covered in cookie tin to keep fresh

Submitter: 
Carol
Description: 
Soft spicy date cookies are very filling and great to take on a hike or in the car on a long trip.
