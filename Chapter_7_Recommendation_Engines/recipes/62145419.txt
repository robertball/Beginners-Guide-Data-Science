Title:
Tempeh Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T Olive Oil
2 red peppers, halved and seeded
4 oz. Garden Vegetable Tempeh, crumbled
1 Small Onion, chopped
1/2 C Baby Spinach
1/4 C Vegetable Broth
1 C Cannellini Beans
2 T Nutritional Yeast
1/2 C Feta Cheese
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Pre-Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Place pepper halves in steamer for 5 minutes.
In the meantime, heat olive oil in cast iron skillet on medium heat.
Cook onion and tempeh for 5 to 10 minutes, or until tempeh is crispy.
Add vegetable broth, spinach and beans and cook for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat and add yeast, feta cheese and salt and pepper.
Fill pepper halves and bake for 30 minutes, uncovered.
