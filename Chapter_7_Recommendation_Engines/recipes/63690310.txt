Title:
Asian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded thin
2 T. butter
1 c. teriyaki sauce
1/2 c. water
1/2 c. orange marmalade
1 t. minced garlic

Directions:
Brown chicken on both sides in the butter in a saucepan.
Combine remaining ingredients and add to pan.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat.
Cover and simmer until juices run clear (appx. 10-15 minutes)

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
A tasty main dish in a snap. I usually serve over wild rice.
