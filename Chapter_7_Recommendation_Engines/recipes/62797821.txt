Title:
VEGETABLE SALAD IN THE RAW Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups broccoli-cut into bite size pieces
4 cups cauliflower-cut into bite size pieces
1 container cherry tomatos
1 pkg.snow peas
1 red pepper-cut into chunks
1 cup bean sprouts
1/2 cup Italian dressing

Directions:
Mix all vegetables in large bowl.Add Italian dressing,stir well.Store in fridge for at least an hour.
