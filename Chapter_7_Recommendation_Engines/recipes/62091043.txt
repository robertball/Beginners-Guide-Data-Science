Title:
Easy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Advocados
1 Lemon
1 8 oz Can "Rotel" Tomatos
1 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Peel advocados and place in bowl.  Cut and juice lemon over advocados.  Add can of tomatoes and mash mixture until at desired consistancy.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Ready to serve once completed.

A quick and easy Guacamole recipe.
