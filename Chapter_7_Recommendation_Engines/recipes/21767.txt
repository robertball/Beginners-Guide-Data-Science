Title:
Baked Rockfish   Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pesto
3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 carrot, finely chopped
1 leek, chopped
2 pounds drawn rockfish

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 . Grease rectangular baking dish, 13x9x2 inches.
Mix pesto, lemon juice, carrot and leek. Place fish in dish. Spread vegetable mixture over fish.
Cover and bake about 40 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork.
