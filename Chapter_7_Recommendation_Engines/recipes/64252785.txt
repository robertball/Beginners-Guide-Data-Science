Title:
Southwest Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1786410.jpg
Ingredients:
4 scallions (green onions), chopped
2 avocados
1 (10 oz) package frozen corn, thawed
1 (10 oz) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 (15 oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 (4 oz) can of green chiles
1 Tbsp lime juice
1½ tsp ground cumin
1½ tsp salt (optional)
1 tsp chipotle chili pepper
½ tsp black pepper
¼ tsp cayenne pepper
¼ tsp onion powder

Directions:
Dice the avocados and place in a large mixing bowl (I use a 2.5 qt) with lime juice. Mash together using a fork until you have a chunky guacamole consistency.
Add remaining ingredients and stir to thoroughly combine everything.
Place in a serving dish and enjoy with your favorite tortilla chips!

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a super easy and delicious dip for any occasion! It also happens to be vegan and a healthy alternative for spinach artichoke dip.
