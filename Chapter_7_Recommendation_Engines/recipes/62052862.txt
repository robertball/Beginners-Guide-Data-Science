Title:
Granny's Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising corn meal
1 cup flour
6 raw eggs
1 1/2 cup buttermilk
2 quarts chicken stock
3 TBSP sage
2 TBSP poultry seasoning
salt and pepper to taste
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 medium onions, chopped

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, mix together flour, meal, 1 egg, and buttermilk to a cornbread consistency.Pour batter into greased 9 x 13 baking pan and cook until golden brown.In a large bowl, crumble corn bread, and add eggs, onion, soup and spices.  Mix together well.  Add chicken stock until mixture is looser than a cake batter.  Pour mixture back into baking pan and cook until browned on top.  Serve with giblet gravy and cranberry sauce.  YUMMY!
