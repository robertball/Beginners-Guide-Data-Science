Title:
Mom's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* Hamburger meat (1-2 pounds)
* Diced cut up onions (1 onion)
* Diced cut up green bell pepper (1- 1 1/2 cups)
* 2 packets mild chili seasoning
* 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste (more less depending on how you like your chili)
* Salt to taste
* Sugar to taste
* Pepper to taste
* Water
* Saltine crackers

Directions:
1)Brown ground beef in a skillet until done, then drain off the fat.
2)Boil the onion and pepper in water until tender.
3)In a large cooking pot add i 6 oz can of tomato paste and add some water and stir well, cook for a few minutes. Then add the browned hamburger, 2 packs of chili seasoning, onion, bell pepper, pepper to taste, salt to taste, sugar to taste and water. Bring this all to a boil and cook on medium-high heat until all flavors have combined and paste is no longer clumpy. Taste and add the other 6 oz can of tomato paste to desired liking and taste.
