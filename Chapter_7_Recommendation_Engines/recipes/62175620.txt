Title:
Traditional Czech Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs of diced stew beef
3 garlic cloves
1 tbs caraway seeds
2 large potatoes, diced
2 tbs vegetable oil
1 tbs paprika
3 large tomatoes, diced
2 tbs butter
1 large white onion, diced
1/2 tsp marjoram
2 tbs flour

Directions:
Heat the vegetable oil on medium-high in a large, deep skillet. Brown the beef, adding the chopped onion and the garlic immediately after. Stir occasionally until the vegetables are evenly browned. Add paprika and about 5 cups of water, then mix in the caraway seeds, marjoram and tomatoes. Season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce the heat and let simmer till tender, about 1-1/2 hours.
Add the potatoes and cook until tender, about 20 additional minutes.
To thicken the sauce, add melted butter and flour. Serve with knedlicky and warm bread!

Traditional Czech comfort food. Excellent with a side of knedlicky!
