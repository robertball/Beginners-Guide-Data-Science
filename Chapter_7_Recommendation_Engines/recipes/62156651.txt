Title:
Chicken steamed/oyster sauce (chinese) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole frying chicken
2 teaspoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons rice wine
4 cups vegetable oil
1 ounce chinese black dried mushrooms, soaked & sliced
2 ounces of bamboo shoots sliced
5 Tablespoons ginger, sliced
2 ounced Yucan preserved vegetables (salted mushrooms), sliced
2 Tablespoons oyster sauce
2 1/2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoons salt
1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
2 Tablespoons msg optional

Directions:
Mis, soy sauce & rice wine & rub  all over the chicken, inside & out. Heat oil in wok. Add, the chicken & deep fry  until brown. Remove & drain. Pour oilout of wok. Blanch mushrooms & bamboo shoots, slice & put in boiling water.  Drain, & set aside. Pour 3 1/2 ounces oil back into wok, add scallions, ginger & stir fry. Add, remaining rice wine, soy sauce, & mushrooms, bamboo shoots, preserved vegetables, dates, stock, oyster sauce, sugar, salt, peppercorns & chicken. Boil, then reduce heat, simmer. Remove mushrooms, bamboo shoots, dates &  place in a serving dish. remove chicken, chop 1 by 2 inch chuncks & stack in the serving dish. Discard preserve vegetables. Stir in cornstarch mix, add to the wok, stirring until thick. pour over chicken & serve.
