Title:
Lemon Tarragon Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Ts white wine vinegar or sherry vinegar
Kosher salt
Black Pepper
2 teaspoon lemon zest
1.5 Tablespoon Fresh Tarragon. if using dried use 1 TS.
5oz Olive oil.
Granulated sugar to taste.

Directions:
Place vinegar and seasonings in a bowl. While whisking quickly and constantly slowly add olive oil. You can cheat and use a stick mixer or blender. Taste and refrigerate.
