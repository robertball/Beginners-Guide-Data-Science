Title:
Glennie's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Venison or Beef
Minced onions (addition)
1 can Bushes Chili Magic (blue label)
1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
1 can (reg size) tomato sauce (addition)
1 can (reg size) chili beans
Chili powder (addition)

Directions:
Brown meat and chop into small pieces in stew pot with onions.  Drain meat if needed.
Stir in chili magic, tomatoes, sauce, and beans.
Add chili powder, if desired.  Bring to boil then turn to low and simmer with a lid.

Submitter: 
Jeanine Miller
Description: 
Glennie Wingates recipe with the Millers additions.
