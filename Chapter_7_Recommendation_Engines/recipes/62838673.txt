Title:
Salted Caramel Party Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package crispy corn and rice cereal
1 1/3 cup pecans
1 1/3 cups almonds
1 1/3 cup cashews
1-2 cups sweetened flaked coconut
Optional- 3/4 cup raisins or dried cranberries
1 stick plus 3 TBS Butter
1 1/2 cups of brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Place Cereal, nuts and dried fruit into a large brown paper bag or a large bowl and stir or shake to mix.
In a saucepan over medium heat- Melt Butter, Brown Sugar and salt.
Bring to a slow boil then simmer 5 minutes stirring frequently.
Remove from heat and add vanilla
Pour over the cereal mixture and stir to coat.
Microwave the bag or bowl for 1 1/2 minutes on high three times for a total of 4 1/2 minutes, stirring between each microwaving. Add as much or as little coconut as you like.
Spread out to cool and once cooled, break apart.
This freezes really well.

Submitter: 
MARGARET N
Description: 
This has been a family favorite for over 20 years. It's the perfect combination of sweet and crunchy. It's sure to please the whole crowd with no corn syrup and no hot oven! This makes a great gift for the holidays.
