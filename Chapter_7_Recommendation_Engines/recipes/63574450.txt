Title:
Gurkensalat (German Cucumber Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers, sliced thin
1/2 onion, sliced thin (optional)
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon Hellman's Mayonaise
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons white vinegar
2 Garlic Cloves, minced
1 teaspoon Olive Oil
1 teaspoon dried dill
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Spread cucumbers and onion on a platter; season with salt and let rest for 30 minutes. Squeeze excess moisture from cucumbers.
Stir mayonaise, sugar, vinegar, garlic, olive oil, dill, and parsley together in a large bowl.
Fold cucumber and onion slices into the mixture.
Refrigerate 8 hours to over night; garnish with paprika to serve.

Submitter: 
Jessica Onorati
Description: 
Cucumber and onion slices marinate in a mayonaise and vinegar dressing for maximum flavor in this German cucumber salad recipe.Modified from: Gurkensalat (German Cucumber Salad)
