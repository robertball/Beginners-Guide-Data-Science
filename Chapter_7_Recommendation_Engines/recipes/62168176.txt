Title:
Mexican Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of Lean Ground Beef
2 packs of Taco Seasoning - Regular
1 1/2 cups water
1 teaspoon of Chili powder
8 oz bottle of Taco Sauce
2 cups of Penne or Spiral Noodles
3/4 cup of Salsa
3/4 cup of crushed Tortilla chips chips
1 cup grated Mexican blend cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
Brown ground beef in pain, and then drain
add taco seasoning, chili power, and water to meat and simmer for 10-15 min.
Drain meat again to remove excess water, and add pasta, taco sauce and chips to meat and mix well.
Place mixture in a baking dish and top with grated cheese
Bake for 20-30 min
When done serve in a bowl and top with you favorite toppings like sour cream.
