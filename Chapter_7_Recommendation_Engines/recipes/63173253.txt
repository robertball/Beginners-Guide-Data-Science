Title:
The Classic 1936 Original Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/814937.jpg
Ingredients:
Salt, for rimming the glass (optional)
Ice
1 1/2 oz. tequila blanco
1 1/2 oz. freshly squeezed lime juice
1 1/2 oz Cointreau

Directions:
If using salt, place in a shallow dish. Moisten the rim of a rocks glass with a dampened paper towel or with lime wedge, then dip in salt.
Fill Boston shaker with ice, tequila, lime juice, and Cointreau.
Shake vigorously until the shaker is iced over
Strain in to rocks glass with ice or cocktail glass without ice
Garnish with lime wedge.
Serve immediately.

Description: 
The one true original recipe from Puebla, Mexico, 1936
