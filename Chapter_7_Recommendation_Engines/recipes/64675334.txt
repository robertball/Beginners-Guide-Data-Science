Title:
Big Ray's Skillet Beef Tamales/Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4646373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 chopped sweet red green pepper
1 chopped large onion
2 cups salsa
1 cup frozen corn
1 can black beans, drained
2 tablespoons water
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
5 tablespoons  sour cream
1 cup shredded lettuce

Directions:
In a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray, cook beef, onions and peppers over medium heat 6-8 minutes or until beef is no longer pink and vegetables are tender, breaking up beef into crumbles; drain. Stir in salsa, corn, beans and water; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; simmer, covered, 10-15 minutes or until tortillas are softened. Sprinkle with cheese; cook, covered, 2-3 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Serve with sour cream & shredded lettuce. Use chips as your spoons.
Freeze option: Freeze cooled meat mixture in freezer containers. To use, partially thaw in refrigerator overnight. Heat through in a saucepan, stirring occasionally and adding a little water if necessary. Serve with sour cream  shredded lettuce.

Description: 
This is perfect for a quick supper or for a great appetizer.  Make sure you have thick chips if you want to use it as a dip.
