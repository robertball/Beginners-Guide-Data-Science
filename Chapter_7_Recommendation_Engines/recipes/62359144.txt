Title:
Southern Macaroni & Cheese w/Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403093.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Mild cheddar cheese
3 cups elbow macaroni noodles
1/2 pkg of Large Velvetta cheese
6 cooked Thick slices of bacon
8 oz sour cream
1 1/2 sticks of butter
1 Tbsp sugar
2/3 cup milk
Dash of salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cook macaroni according to directions on the box.  Drain macaroni.  Cut Velvetta into small 1" cubes and cut Bacon in 1" pieces. Place macaroni in Large bowl then add Velvetta,Bacon,Sour cream,Sugar,Milk, Salt,1/2 of the butter and mix well. Pour into 13x9 casserole dish.  Cut slices of remaining butter and place on top, sprinkle with pepper.  Bake for approximately 30 minutes or until it gets a nice golden brown crust. Remove from oven and add 2 cups of mild cheddar cheese to the top and place back into the oven and broil until nice brown cheesey crust is obtained. Remove from oven and let cool for about 15 minutes
