Title:
Healthier Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups peeled, cored, sliced apples
1/2 cup light vegetable oil spread, melted
1 cup quick cooking oats
1/4 cup firmly packed Splenda brown sugar blend
1/3 cup flour
1 packet of Splenda original
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place apple slices in 8x8 in square glass pan coated with cooking spray
Sprinkle cinnamon and Splenda packet over apples
Combine melted spread, oats, brown Splenda, and flour and stir with fork or pastry mixer.  Sprinkle oats mixture over apples.
Bake for 40 min. or until apples are tender and topping is browned.  Serve with frozen yogurt or light whipped cream if desired.

Submitter: 
JEN78RN
Description: 
I revised a much loved apple crisp recipe so that it fit into my goal of more healthful eating! It's warm, comforting, and great leftover for breakfast.
