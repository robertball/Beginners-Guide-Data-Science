Title:
Cream of Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons butter
1 Tablespoon canola Oil
1/3 Cup Green Onions
2 minced garlic cloves
Small sweet potato grated
4 Cups Spinach
1 teaspoon salt
3 Tablespoons Flour
1 teaspoon Marjoram
4 Cups hot vegetable Broth
1/8 teaspoon of white pepper
½ Cup light cream or to taste

Directions:
Heat oil and butter until hot
Add green onion and garlic, sauté for 5 minutes
Add grated sweet potato and stir
Add flour, salt, Marjoram and stir
Add Spinach and then vegetable broth
Bring to a boil and simmer until cooked
Add pepper
Puree mixture
Add cream and heat
