Title:
Buitoni's Garden Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992710.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package BUITONI® Family Size Three Cheese Tortellini
1 cup Italian salad dressing
2 cups chopped tomatoes
2 cups broccoli florets
1 cup chopped green bell pepper
3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced ripe olives, drained

Directions:
PREPARE pasta according to package directions. Chill.
POUR dressing into large bowl. Add pasta, tomatoes, broccoli, bell pepper, cheese and olives; toss well to coat. Refrigerate or serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Buitoni®
Description: 
Here's a traditional, easy-to-make pasta salad that's full of flavor. Make ahead and store in the refrigerator for easy entertaining or brown bag lunches.
