Title:
Pecan Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup Butter (DO NOT substitute)
2 Eggs
1/2 C Flour
2 Cups Brown Sugar
2 Cups Pecans

Directions:
Melt butter, add eggs.  Chop pecans real fine. (I cheat and do it in my osterizer), in seperate bowl add dry ingrediants.  Add butter and eggs to dry ingrediants.  Spray mini muffin pans with Pam, fill 2/3 full.  Bake at 350 for 23-25 minutes.  Immediately remove onto wire racks to cool.

Submitter: 
Melody R. Fales
Description: 
This has been a long secret recipe.  But, so many people love it.  I make it so often for people, thought I would finally share it. I do it batches of 4, trying to do it single is challenging.
