Title:
TEX-MEX PASTA BAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pound ground turkey ~ Browned with the onion
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 #10 can Salsa  undrained
1/2 C taco seasoning
2#bag whole grain pasta half cooked and rinsed
1# bag of Corn
1 C fresh Cilantro

Directions:
Bring a  saucepan of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until almost tender, about 4 minutes. Rinse with cool water and set aside to drain.
Crumble the turkey in a large skillet set over medium heat. Cook and stir until evenly brown; drain.  Add onion to browned turkey meat and cook until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in Salsa, and chopped Cilantro and Taco seasoning until combined then mix in the corn and pasta in a deep baking pan.  Bake covered for 30 min. at 350 until internal temp. of 165 is reached.

Submitter:

Description: 
Ground meat, salsa and corn with a little spice creates a quick meal everyone loves.Modified from: S.O.B. (South of the Border) Casserole
