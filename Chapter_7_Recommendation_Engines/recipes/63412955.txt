Title:
MOM'S  CHOCOLATE ICE BOX PIE   1954 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS
1/2  lb		Vanilla Wafer Cookies
1/4 cup	Butter
1/2 cup	Butter
1    cup	Powder Sugar
3		Egg Yolks
2    Sq.	Bitter Chocolate
1   cup 	Whipping Cream
optional
1   cup	Chopped Walnuts

Directions:
PREPARATION
Crush the 1/2 # vanilla wafers.  Mix them with 1/4 cup of BUTTER.  Use at least 3/4 of them to line a PIE TIN, depending on the size of the pie tin.  Heat the pie tin in the oven to set the vanilla wafer pie crust.
Beat 1/2 cup of BUTTER with 1 cup of POWDERED SUGAR,  than add one at a time 3 EGG YOLKS,  beating very well after each yolk.   Melt 2 squares BITTER CHOCOLATE,  mix the chocolate into the mixture.
Optional -
Add 1 cup of CHOPPED WALNUTS to mixture.
Pour pie filling into the pie shell.
Beat 1 cup of WHIPPING CREAM adding sugar and vanilla to taste.   Layer the whipped cream over the top of the pie filling.
Sprinkle a few remaining cookie crumbs on top of the whipped cream.
Refrigerate overnight.
Serve small pieces, very rich.
