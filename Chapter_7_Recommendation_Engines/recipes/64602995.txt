Title:
GG's Lemon Lime Refrigerator Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3oz package lime Jell-O
1 package Duncan Hines Lemon Supreme cake mix
Oil (as directed for cake mix)
Eggs (as directed for cake mix)
1 envelope whipped topping mix (2-2.5 cup size)
1 small package lemon instant pudding mix
1.5 cup COLD milk

Directions:
Dissolve Jello in ¾ C. boiling water.  Add ½ C. cold water; set aside.  Mix and bake cake as directed for 9x13 pan.  Cool 20-25 minutes.  Poke deep holes through top of warm cake with handle of wooden spoon, spaced 1” apart.  Pour Jello into holes.  Refrigerate while preparing topping.
In a chilled deep bowl, blend and whip topping mix, pudding, and milk until stiff (3-8 minutes).  Immediately frost cake.  Store in refrigerator or freeze.
