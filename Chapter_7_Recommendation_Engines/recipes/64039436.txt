Title:
The Best Rolled Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
.5 cup unsalted butter, softened
2.5 cups white sugar
*3 eggs / 1 egg white
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 TBSP powdered sugar
5 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
.25 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until smooth. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Stir in the  flour, baking powder, and salt. Cover, and chill dough for at least one hour (or overnight).
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Roll out dough on surface(using powdered sugar for nonstick surface) 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick. Cut into shapes with any cookie cutter. Place cookies 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 6 to 8 minutes in preheated oven. Cool completely.
