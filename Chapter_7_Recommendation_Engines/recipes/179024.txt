Title:
Glazed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403034.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
6 pork chops, 3/4-inch thick
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed French Onion Soup
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar

Directions:
Mix cornstarch and water.
Melt butter in skillet. Add  chops and cook until browned.
Add soup and sugar. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat  10 minutes or until done.
Remove chops and keep warm. Add cornstarch mixture. Cook and stir until mixture boils and thickens. Serve over chops.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
These fabulous pork chops taste like more than their simple ingredients.  Sauteed chops are served with a savory-sweet glaze of French onion and brown sugar. Serve with roasted potato wedges and green beans simmered in chicken broth.
