Title:
Grilled Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup water
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
dash Worcestershire sauce
1 clove garlic smashed
2 Tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon ginger powder
Chicken breasts or chicken tenders

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except the chicken, in a gallon ziplock bag.  Add chicken, and remove as much air as possible from bag.  Place in fridge to marinate at least an hour, or up until four hours.
Grill on barbecue grill until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Bedsaul Johnson
Description: 
my aunt shared this basic teriyaki type marinade with me 40 years ago. Her original recipe called for a lot more sugar and soy sauce.  I have tweaked it over the years to get the flavor we love.
