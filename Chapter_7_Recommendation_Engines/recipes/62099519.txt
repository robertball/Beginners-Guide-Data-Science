Title:
Protein Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tea water, boiled
1/2 c. quick cooking oats
1-1/2 tablespoons wheat germ
1-1/2 tablespoons flaxseed meal
1/2 scoop vanilla flavored whey protein powder
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Boil water in tea kettle until whistling.
Meanwhile: In small bowl, combine oatmeal, wheat germ, flaxseed meal, whey protein powder, and cinnamon. Add boiling water to taste, mix well and enjoy.

Submitter: 
TEVANS211
Description: 
Don't have time to prepare a meal before heading out to the gym? Try this fast, easy, heart healthy whole grain protein oatmeal to give you that extra boost to sustain your morning workout.
