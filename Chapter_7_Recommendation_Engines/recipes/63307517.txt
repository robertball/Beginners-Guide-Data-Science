Title:
Bryan's Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Velveeta cheese (original)
1 lb. Velveeta "hot" Mexican cheese*
1/2 can condensed cream of mushroom soup (undiluted)
1 small can Rotel chopped tomatoes w/ green chilies

Directions:
Cut cheese into chunks and add to pan with other ingredients.  Cook and stir over medium-low heat (or in microwave) until cheese mixture is smooth.
*If you prefer milder dip, substitute 1 lb. "mild" Mexican Velveeta for "hot".

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is great dip that stays creamy even after is begins to cool.  Don't leave out the cream of mushroom soup; it's not the same without it.
