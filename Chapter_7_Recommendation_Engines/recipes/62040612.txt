Title:
Logan's Yummy Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds Ground Beef
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp. Italian Seasoning
8 oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced
8 oz. sour cream
2 cans undiluted, condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/4 tsp. crushed thyme
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. paprika
Coarse grind black pepper, to taste
2 cups shredded, sharp cheese
32 oz. frozen tater tots

Directions:
Brown the ground beef, onion, italian seasoning and garlic, drain. Place in 13x9 baking pan, sprayed with non-stick spray. In a large bowl, add the soup, mushrooms, sour cream, thyme, garlic powder, paprika, pepper and 1/2 cup shredded cheese, mix well. Spread over the beef layer. Top with tater tots, covering the entire soup layer. Sprinkle remaining cheese over tater tots. Bake in a preheated 425 degree oven for40- 45 minutes or until tater tots are crisp.

Submitter: 
SABRYSON
Description: 
This is named after my son, who proclaimed it his favorite dish. I have tried many variations, and this is the best.
