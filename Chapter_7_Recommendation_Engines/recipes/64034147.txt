Title:
Roasted Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125706.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Olive oil
2 Tbsp dried dill
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp grated lemon zest
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 large head cauliflower,cut into floweretts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Add all the ingredients in a large zip lock bag to combine. Put into greased 9" x 13" baking dish. Bake for approximately 25 minutes, turning half way through.
