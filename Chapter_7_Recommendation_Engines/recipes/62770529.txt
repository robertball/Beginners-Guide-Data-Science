Title:
Tuna Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Chopped Onion
1 Tbsp. Butter
1 Can (10 3/4 oz) Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 Can Water
1/2 C. shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 Can (7 oz.) Tuna, drain and flake
2 Tbsp Pimento, Diced
1 Tbsp. Parsley, chopped
2 C. cooked spaghetti

Directions:
Saute onion in the butter until tender.  Blend in soup, water and cheese.  Cook over low heat until sauce melts; stir occasionally.  Add Tuna, Pimento, parsley and spaghetti.  Heat and serve.

PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Very good fast pasta dish with canned tuna.
