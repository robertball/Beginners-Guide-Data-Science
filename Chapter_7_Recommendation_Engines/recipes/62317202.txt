Title:
Modified Easy Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1/2 cups water
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1 teaspoon dried parsley flake
1 tablespoon minced onion (or 1 teaspoon onion powder)
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 whole bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake

Directions:
Mix together the tomato paste, water, and olive oil.  Add remaining spices and mix well.  Simmer on low for 20-30 minutes.  Discard bay leaf before spreading on pizza.

Submitter: 
Sue Henderson
Description: 
A modified version of the other 'Easy Pizza Sauce' recipes.  This is a thicker sauce with less oil.  Simmering it on the stove allows the spices to blend together in a shorter period of time.Modified from: Easy Pizza Sauce I
