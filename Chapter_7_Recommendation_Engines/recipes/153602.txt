Title:
Curried Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces fresh cranberries
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup port wine
3/4 cup orange marmalade
1/3 cup raisins
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Curry Powder
2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Pumpkin Pie Spice
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Wash and sort cranberries.
Combine sugar, orange marmalade, vinegar, port, raisins, curry powder, pumpkin pie spice and salt in a large bowl. Add cranberries and mix well.
Spray a 2-quart baking dish with cooking spray. Pour in cranberries and cover with foil.
Bake for 1 hour until cranberries are soft.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
These spicy cranberries are excellent warm or chilled!
