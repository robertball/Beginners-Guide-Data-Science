Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3874334.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup oat flour (make by using old fashioned or large flake oats and blending in the blender)
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 cups mashed over-ripe bananas (4 medium bananas)
2 eggs
1/2 cup natural crunchy peanut butter
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup unsweetened apple sauce
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 9 x 5" loaf pan
In a large bowl, mix together the flours, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
In a separate bowl, mix together the bananas, eggs, peanut butter, oil, applesauce and vanilla.  Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and still until just combined.  Add chocolate and peanut butter chips and stir gently to incorporate.
Pour the batter into the loaf pan.  Bake in 350 degree oven for about 1 hour, or until tester inserted in centre come out clean.  Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool on a cooling rack.
Recipe easily doubles.  Loaves freeze well.
