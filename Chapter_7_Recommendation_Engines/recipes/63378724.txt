Title:
"Good Cookies" by Aunt Mary Ellen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. margarine
1 c. granulated sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. vegetable oil
1 c. oats (not instant)
1 c. crushed corn flakes (not frosted flakes)
1 c. flaked coconut
1 c. chopped pecans
1 beaten egg
3 1/2 c. plain flour
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1 t. pure vanilla (I never use imitation vanilla)

Directions:
Easy Recipe
Cream margarine & sugar, add beaten egg , then remaining ingredients all at once and mix well.
Roll in small balls the size of a walnut, press down with a fork (dip your fork in water to make it not stick).
Preheat oven to 350
Bake on ungreased baking sheet 12-14 minutes until lightly browned around edges...do not over cook.
