Title:
Old fashion Coconut Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup white karo syrup
3 egg whites
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a double boiler.
Beat until stif while on eye.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
My mom makes this icing for coconut cakes. The whole family enjoys this recipe. It was one that's been handed down in my family through many geneerations.
