Title:
Omi's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. ground beef
1/2 lb. italian sausage
1 medium onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
16 oz. ricotta cheese
1 egg
16 oz mozzerella, shredded
25 oz spaghetti sauce
10 lasagna noodles
1 T black pepper
1 T Italian seasoning

Directions:
Brown ground beef and sausage.
Add onion and garlic half way through cooking.
Add spaghetti sauce.
Heat through.
Set aside.
Mix ricotta, egg, Italian seasoning and pepper in medium bowl.
Set aside.
Boil lasagna noodles for 11 minutes.
Drain.
Pat dry with paper towels.
Spread 1/2 cup of the beef/sausage/sauce mixture in botton of 9x13 pan.
Layer noodles on top.
Layer ricotta/egg/seasoning mixture on top.
Layer mozzerella.
Repeat layers ending with mozzerella.
Spread parmesan as top layer.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
hils
Description: 
A simple, tasty lasagna that my dear friend Naomi came up with and can be easily prepared ahead of time.
