Title:
Wet Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/512604.jpg
Ingredients:
3.5-4 lbs regal rump roast
1 envelope Lipton onion soup mix
2 envelopes taco seasoning mix
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/2 cup milk
l lg can re-fried beans
Shredded cheddar cheese
Pkg 10 burrito shells

Directions:
Place roast in crock pot filled half way with water.
Add onion soup mix.
Cover and cook on high for at least 8 hrs or till toast is tender and easily shredded.
Remove roast from crock pot transfer contents of pot into dutch oven. On stove top bring to boil then reduce medium heat.
Add taco seasoning.
In a separate bowl mix cornstarch and milk, add to mixture stirring over medium heat until gravy consistency is achieved. at gravy point reduce to simmer. Add water if to thick.
Shred beef and place in gravy mixture.
Heat re-fried beans in a separate pot.
To plate this dish set flour tortilla down first and add beans. Spoon on beef'n'gravy and sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
Carefully roll into a burrito and top with more gravy. Add desired toppings like green onion, sour cream, salsa and whatever else you like.

Submitter: 
RobBoiarski
Description: 
Slow cooked beef with a Mexican flare! We got this recipe from an old college friend. Dress them up with sour cream, salsa, green onions and shredded cheddar. Add Spanish rice and you have a fiesta, Enjoy
