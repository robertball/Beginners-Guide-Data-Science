Title:
Zucchini Sausage casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 whole zucchini
1 lb sausage
1 onion
1 pkg  stuffing mix
2 eggs
1 Tbs melted butter/margarine
1/2 C water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Dice zucchini, place in a pot with 1/4 C of water, cover and bring to a boil. Boil 2-3 minutes, drain, return to the pot and set aside.
Brown sausage breaking it up into small pieces.  Drain and set aside.
Dice onion and cook in hot skillet from sausage. Cook until just translucent.
Mix the melted butter and 1/2 of the stuffing. Add to zucchini and mix well.
In a large mixing bowl beat the 2 eggs . Add a small amount of the hot sausage to the eggs to temper them. Stir well. Once the eggs are fully tempered add all of the cooked ingredients and mix well.
Pour filling into a 8 x 11 casserole pan that has been sprayed with cooking oil.
Combine the remaining stuffing with remaining 1/4 C of water and mix well. If it is still too dry add more water until it is the consistency desired. Spread stuffing mix over the top of the casserole.  Place in oven and cook for 30 min.
Serve and enjoy!

Incredibly rich and flavorful.  I developed this recipe in a quest for a meal that has plenty of flavor, is relatively low in calories and can be eaten by my son who has a milk allergy.
