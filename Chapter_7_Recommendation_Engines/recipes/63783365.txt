Title:
Awesome Apple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c oil
3 eggs
2 c white sugar
1 tsp vanilla
3 c apples, diced
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 c chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat Oven at 300 degrees
Combine and set aside the oil, eggs, sugar, and vanilla. Sift flour, cinnamon, soda, and salt. Add dry ingredients to the oil mixture gradually. Add apples and nuts. Bake in 2 regular loaf pans or 3 foil pans for 1 1/2 hours. Cool 10 minutes in the pan. Sprinkle with sugar, if desired.
