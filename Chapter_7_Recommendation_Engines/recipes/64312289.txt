Title:
Mocha Ricotta Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup(s) cheese, ricotta, part-skim
1/2 teaspoon cocoa powder, unsweetened
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 packet(s) sugar substitute
1 dash(es) espresso, instant

Directions:
Mix together the ricotta, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and sugar substitute in a dessert bowl. Serve chilled with a dusting of espresso powder.
