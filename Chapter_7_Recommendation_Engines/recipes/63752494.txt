Title:
Smoked Salmon Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. packages cream cheese
1/3 cup finely chopped red onion
1 T. dried dill
2 T. drained capers
1 T. lemon juice
6 oz. smoked salmon

Directions:
Using an electric mixer, blend all ingredients.  Cover and chill for at least 4 hours, or up to 3 days.
Serve with crackers or baguette slices.

Submitter: 
Jo Bice
Description: 
Easy and elegant.  The trouble with this recipe is that I can't stop eating it!  Recipe is easy to double if you are having a big party.
