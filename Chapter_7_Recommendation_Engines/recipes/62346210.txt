Title:
(ellenc) Mascarpella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027528.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mascarpone
1/2 cup chocolate-hazelnut spread (such as Nutella)
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Beat mascarpone, Nutella, and almond extract until smooth; set aside.
Whip heavy cream in a chilled metal or glass bowl until stiff.
Fold mascarpone mixture into whipped cream.
Cover and refrigerate at least one hour.
If using as a pie filling, spoon into prebaked or crumb crust before refrigerating.

Submitter: 
evian257
Description: 
This can be used as a pie filling, or a filling for crepes or layer cake. Also good spread on slices of pound cake, and garnished with fresh raspberries or strawberries.
