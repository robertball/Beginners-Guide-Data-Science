Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/4 cup flour
1 can tomato soup
2 lbs. stew beef
4 cubed potatoes
3 carrots, sliced
2 stalks celery, sliced
1 large onion, big chunks
2 beef bouillon cubes
1 Tbsp. Italian seasoning
salt 'n' pepper
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Shake water and flour together.
Combine everything into crockpot and cook on low 4-6 hours.
