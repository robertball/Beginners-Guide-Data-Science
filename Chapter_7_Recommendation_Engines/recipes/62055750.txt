Title:
Cabbage and noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 package egg ( or egg free) ribbon noodles
1/2 small cabbage cut into strips or shredded
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 lb. of ground beef
1 small onion chopped

Directions:
fry ground beef with onion on stovetop until pink is gone,  drain fat then, add strips of cabbage, it will seem like a lot but will cook down considerably, cook until cabbage is crisp tender. Meanwhile cook noodles until al dente in boiling water. Drain noodles. Add noodles to beef and cabbage, stir in mushroom soup and season with salt and pepper, heat through and serve. If you want you can add milk to make it more gravy-like.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great quick supper for busy nights, better for you than hamburger helper and I think it tastes better too! It's really easy to make and my family loves it with biscuits and butter.
