Title:
Nana's Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortning
1/2 cup softend butter
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups old fashioned  oats
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
In a large bowl cream shortening, butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar and vanilla  Add eggs and mix thoroughly.
Combine the baking soda, salt and flour and stir into creamed mixture
Add oatmeal and chocolate chips and stir until well blended.
Drop heaping tablespoons of dough onto greased cookie sheet or sheet covered in parchment paper.  Bake for 15 minutes.
