Title:
Clean Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup good quality chocolate chips (dark or milk)
6 Medjool dates, pitted
1/2 cup pure seed or nut butter

Directions:
Melt chocolate in a microwave stirring every 20 seconds until chocolate is melted.
Using a pastry brush, coat melted chocolate on the bottom and sides of a candy mold or mini cupcake liner. Put mold or cupcake liners in freezer to set.
While chocolate is setting, process peanut butter and dates in a food processor until well combined. Spoon peanut butter mixture into the candy molds over the set chocolate. To finish brush or spoon the remaining chocolate over the peanut butter and again place in freezer to set.
After 10 to 15 minutes, pop the cups out of the mold and enjoy. Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
Clean version of the Reeses peanut butter cup that is just as irresistible as the original.

Serving size 1.3 oz (1 cup) = 200 calories, 25 carbs, 11 g fat, 5 grams protein. Compared to Reese's peanut butter cups 1.5 ounces (2 cups) = 210 calories, 24 g. carbs, 13 g fat, 5 g protein.
