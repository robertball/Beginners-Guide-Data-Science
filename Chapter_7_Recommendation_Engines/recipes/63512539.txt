Title:
Beef Barbeque a la Nancy Schumacher Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. left-over shredded pot roast
2 Tbs. vinegar
2 Tbs. brown sugar
1 C. Ketchup
3 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 Tbs. prepared mustard
1 C. water
1/2 C. finely chopped celery
pinch of salt and cayenne pepper, to taste

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients and simmer for 1/2 hour.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
A simple and delicious use for left-over pot roast.
