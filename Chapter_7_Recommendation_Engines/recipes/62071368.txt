Title:
Middle Eastern Dip - Australian Measurements Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup split red lentils
Olive oil
2 garlic cloves
1 1/2tsp grated ginger
1-2tsp mild curry paste (to taste)
1/4 cup mango chutney
1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
1/2 cup greek yoghurt
2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander

Directions:
Rinse lentils and boil in saucepan in approx 2 cups of water. Cook on low heat for 20 minutes or until soft, should be of a thick but slightly dripping consistently. If too thick a a little more water. If too thin cook for a bit longer. Remove lentils and rinse pan. Add olive oil to pan and cook garlic and ginger on low heat for about 1 minute. Add curry paste and cook for 30 seconds. Remove from heat, stir in lentils and chutney, cool and then add mint. Serve dip on a plate topped with yoghurt and coriander. Delicious!

Submitter: 
XANTHE
Description: 
Lovely easy dip made with split red lentils, mango chutney, greek yoghurt and mint. If you like bean style dips this is refreshingly different from boring old hummus, its sweeter and so much more tasty.
