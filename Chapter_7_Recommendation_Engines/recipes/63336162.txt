Title:
Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup agave or maple syrup
1 16 oz can pumpkin
1 cup canola oil
1 cup ground flax seed
3 cups white whole wheat flour
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp baking powder
1 - 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup craisins

Directions:
Whisk together eggs, sugar, syrup, oil, and ground flax in medium bowl.  In large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.  Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and fold together with chocolate chips and craisins.  Bake at 375 for 18-20 minutes.
