Title:
Keto Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c peanut butter
1/4 c coconut aminos
1/2 tsp fish sauce
1/2 T rice wine vinegar
1 garlic clove
1/3 to 1/2 lime, juiced
1 tsp tamari
1/4 c water or to desired consistency
pinch red pepper flakes
[add ginger next time]

Directions:
blend all ingredients in blender or with hand mixer
