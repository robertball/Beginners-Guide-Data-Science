Title:
Vegetarian Chili With Cashews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons oil
1 1/2 cups chopped celery
1 1/2 cups chopped green pepper
1 cup chopped onion
2-3 cloves minced garlic
2 28 oz. cans ground and peeled tomatoes
3 16 oz. cans kidney beans drained and rinsed
1/2 cup raisins
3/4 cups cashews
12 oz. beer
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon chopped parsley
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. basil
1 1/2 tsp. oregano
1 1/2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. allspice
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Cook celery, pepper, onion and garlic in oil until tender.  Stir in tomatoes, kidney beans, raisins, vinegar, and spices.  Simmer covered 1 1/2 hours.  Stir in beer and cashews. Bring to a boil and then simmer for another half hour. (if you wish, you can put all the ingredients together in a crock pot on low and cook 6-8 hours, adding beer and cashews at the end).

Submitter: 
Nancy Toth
Description: 
A great tasting vegetarian chili that even meat lovers love! Do not let the amount of ingredients fool you, it looks harder than it is. The flavor is wonderful and well worth it!
