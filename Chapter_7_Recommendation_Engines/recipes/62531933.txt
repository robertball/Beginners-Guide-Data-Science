Title:
Pumpkin Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Make Libby's Famous Pumpkin Pie Recipe.  Fill pie shell(s) only 1/3 to 1/2 full.  Cook and cool.
8 oz. cream cheese at room temperature.
1 can sweet condensed milk
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/2 tsp. orange extract or bitters

Directions:
Make pie recipe, cook, and cool.
Beat cream cheese until fluffy.
Add milk until well blended.
Add lemon juice and orange and blend.
Pour over pie to top, and chill (1 hour, estimated).
Sprinkle nuts, chocolate pieces, or other, as decoration.
