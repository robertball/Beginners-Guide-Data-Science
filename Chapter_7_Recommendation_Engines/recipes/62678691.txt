Title:
Mango Chicken Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/600149.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lb. boneless skinless chicken thighs ($2.98)
about 3/4 cups Spicy Mango Marinade from KC Masterpiece ($.50)
2 mangoes, diced ($1) On sale for $.50 each latelyâ€¦how to select and cut a mango
1 celery stalk ($.10)
2 garlic cloves ($.10)
1 Tbsp Spicy Mango Marinade ($.05)
Lettuce leaves from the garden
Optional: Use tortillas instead of lettuce wraps
1 bag Steamers frozen peas ($.67)

Directions:
Marinate the chicken thighs in the Spicy Mango Marinade for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
While the chicken is marinating, cut the mango and chop the celery.  Crush the garlic and add it into the mix. Season with some salt and pepper.
After the chicken thighs have marinated, grill them until they are cooked through. Then slice and dice them&#8230;and add them to the mango mixture.  Toss and add into lettuce leaves or tortillas.
Cook the Steamers peas as directed on package.
Serve Mango Chicken Lettuce Wraps with peas.
