Title:
Creamy Jalapeno Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397261.jpg
Ingredients:
16 ounce sour cream
1/2 cup pickled jalapenos
1 packet ranch dip/dressing mix
1/4 cup fresh cilantro

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until creamy.  Refrigerate for at least an hour.  Serve with chips or spoon over tacos.  The possibilities are endless!

Submitter: 
amylrowe
Description: 
There is a restaurant in Houston, Chuy's which serves this amazing dip.  We request it every time we dine there.  This is my take on the dip.  I think this recipe is pretty darn close and is a huge crowd pleaser.  It's a perfect dip or sauce for any meal.
