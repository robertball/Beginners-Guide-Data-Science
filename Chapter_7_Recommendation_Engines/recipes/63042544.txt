Title:
2 step chicken- mushroom Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
8 ounces cream cheese
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
2 cups milk
8 ounces grated parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup light sour cream
1-2 tablespoons flour
1 13 ounce can chicken breast
1-2 4 ounce cans of mushrooms

Directions:
Put all ingredients into medium sauce pan over medium heat
Stir with a wisk until hot and smooth.

Submitter: 
live_love_cook
Description: 
This is one of our old family favorites. It is best when served over linguine or fettucine.
