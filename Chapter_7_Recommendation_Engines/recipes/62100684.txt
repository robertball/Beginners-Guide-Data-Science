Title:
Pam's Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
2 c. sugar
2 Tbsp. corn syrup
1/8 tsp. salt
4 Tbsp. peanut butter
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Butter a 9 x 9-inch baking pan.  Combine milk, sugar, syrup and salt in a 3-quart sauce pan.  Cook slowly, stirring until sugar is dissolved. Increase heat and bring mixture to boil, stirring occasionally. When it reaches 234°, let it cool to 110°.
Add peanut butter and vanilla. Beat vigorously until mixture is dull in color. Quickly turn into the buttered pan without scraping bottom or sides of sauce pan. Spread evenly.  Cut into desired size.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
Caro syrup, peanut butter and vanilla help make the a sweet treat for any time of the year!
