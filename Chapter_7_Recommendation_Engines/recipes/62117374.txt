Title:
Pinto beans and Spam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups clean pinto beans, (no need to soak overnight, not me, nor my mother, or my grandmother ever soaked the beans).
9 cups water, (can substitute beer for all or part).
1 quartered onion
1 bulb peeled garlic, (or less if you care : ).
1 can SPAM cut in half and sliced into 1/2" slices.
1 tsp black pepper.
1 tsp baking soda.

Directions:
Place all ingredients in the slow cooker on high in the morning and they are ready in 6 hours.

Submitter: 
TOM TROTTIER
Description: 
Great comfort food that my mom used to make.
