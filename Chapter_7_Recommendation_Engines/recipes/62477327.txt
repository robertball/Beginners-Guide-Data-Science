Title:
Powen's Mozzarella And Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488828.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large tomato
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste
4 ounces mozzarella cheese, thickly sliced
4 leaves fresh basil, torn into strips

Directions:
Slice tomato and lightly salt; arrange on a plate.
Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over each serving.
Lay slices of cheese over tomatoes, lightly salt and pepper, then layer strips of basil on top.
Cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
Easy, delicious and fresh. Plump tomatoes, thick slices of cheese, good dressings, and fresh basil.Modified from: Owen's Mozzarella and Tomato Salad
