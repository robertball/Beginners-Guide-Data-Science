Title:
Refrigerator Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c - old fashioned oats
1/3 c  unsweetened vanilla almond milk
1/2 c - vanilla Greek yogurt (Dannon’s lite & fit recommended)
1/2 c  frozen fruit

Directions:
Add each ingredient (in order) to mason jar or cup.  Mix together.
Place in fridge at least overnight or up to 3 days.

Submitter: 
Sarah Massey
Description: 
This recipe is great as a quick breakfast or snack. My whole family loves it. It can be heated up (with no lid) in the microwave or eaten cold.
