Title:
Sparkling Orange Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can mandarin oranges in juice -drained with juice reserved
1 cup cold orange soda
½ cup frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (3 oz) package orange gelatin
Prepared graham cracker crust

Directions:
Measure reserved juice from orange can. If necessary add enough water to make ¾ cup. Boil.
Stir boiling juice into gelatin and stir to dissolve. Refrigerate 10 minutes.
Gently stir soda into gelatin.
In a small bowl, add ½ cup gelatin to ½ cup whipped topping and whisk until smooth. Pour into crust. Refrigerate 15 minutes.
Gently arrange orange segments on top of gelatin mixture in crust. Refrigerate 10 minutes.
Spoon remaining gelatin into crust and refrigerate 3 hours or until firm. Garnish as desired.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
This dessert is simple and refreshing and great for an easy Summertime treat.
