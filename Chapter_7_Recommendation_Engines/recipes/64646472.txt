Title:
Garlic Shrimp Black Bean Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4528746.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package black bean pasta
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 pound large shrimp, shelled and deveined
4 tablespoon unsalted butter
6 -8 cloves garlic, minced
1 lemon, juiced
2 tablespoon white wine
3/4 cup low sodium chicken broth
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions:
Cook pasta in large pot of boiling salted water until for 6 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
Heat a large saut√© pan over medium heat.  Add In oil then shrimp.  Cook for about 3 minutes.
Add in butter and garlic.  Once the butter has melted and begins to lightly bubble add in lemon juice, white wine, chicken broth and red pepper flakes.  Let simmer for about 2-3 minutes.
Take the black bean pasta and lay on the bottom of a serving bowl or platter.
Add the shrimp along the platter then pour over the liquid.  Garnish with fresh parsley and serve to enjoy.
