Title:
Lemon Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lemon
1/2 C chicken broth
3 T soy sauce
2 t cornstarch
1 T canola oil
1# boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-in pieces
1 T chopped garlic
10 oz mushrooms, halved or uartered
1 C carrots, diagonally sliced (1/4 IN)
2 C snow peas (6oz)
Bunch scallions, cut into 1-in pieces, separate green parts

Directions:
Grate 1 t lemon zest and set aside. Juice lemon and whisk 3 T of juice w/broth, soy sauce and cornstarch in sm. bowl.
Heat oil in lg. skillet over med-high heat. Add chicken and cook, stirring occ., until cooked through, 4-5 min. Transfer to plate. Add mushrooms and carrots to pan and cook until carrots are just tender, about 5 min. Add snow peas, scallion whites, garlic and lemon zest. Cook, stirring, until fragrant, 30 sec.
Whisk broth mixture and add to pan; cook, stirring, until thickened, 2-3 min. Add scallion greens and chicken with any accumulated juices, cook, stirring, until heated through, 1-2 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Colorful mix of snow peas, carrots and scallions in a zesty lemon sauce. You can substitute veggies with bell peppers or zucchini. Serve over rice
