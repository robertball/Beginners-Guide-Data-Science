Title:
Black Bean Salsa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen corn
2 cups chopped tomatoes
2 cups chopped seeded, peeled cucumbers
1 large chopped orange bell pepper
3/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1 15 oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
3/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro
1/2 cup cider vinegar
3 Tbsp olive oil
2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Cook corn.  Drain.
Combine all vegetables in a large bowl and mix well.  Let stand at room temperature to blend flavors.
Just before serving, combine oil, vinegar, sugar and salt and mix well.  Pour over salad.  Toss.

Submitter:

Description: 
Great summer salad.  Fresh, colorful and appetizing and feeds a crowd!
