Title:
Taco Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup seeds from freshly cut pumpkin, washed and dried
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 to 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
1/4 to 1/12 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
In a skillet, saute pumpkin seeds in oil for 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Using a slotted spoon, transfer seeds to an ungreased 12x10x1 in baking pan. Sprinkle with taco seasoning and garlic salt; stir to coat. Spread into a single layer. Bake at 325 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until crisp. Remove to paper towels to cool completely. Store in airtight container for up to 3 weeks.

Submitter: 
Jessica Schultz
Description: 
Taco Seasoning and garlic flavor these fresh pumpkin seeds just right. Recipe from Taste of Home, Ultimate Halloween Edition 2012.
