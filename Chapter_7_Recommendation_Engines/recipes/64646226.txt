Title:
Meal Prep Salmon dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tbsp garlic
1 lb of salmon
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 carrot
5 oz green beans
5 oz asparagus
Olive oil

Directions:
Cut salmon into 4 equal pieces
Mix soy sauce, balsamic vinaigrette, olive oil and garlic in a bowl. Place 2 salmon in bowl with mixture and chill for 30 minutes.
Mix paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper in a bowl and sprinkle over  remaining 2 pieces of salmon.
Cut carrot, green beans, asparagus into medley. Put on cooking tray and glaze with olive oil, salt, pepper.
Cook in oven 450 degrees for 11 minutes
