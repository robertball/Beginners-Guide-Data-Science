Title:
Pizza Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990695.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
3/4 cup chopped pepperoni
1/2 cup pizza sauce
4 slices Market Pantryâ„¢ Deli-sliced Mozzarella cheese
4 hamburger buns

Directions:
Heat grill.
Mix ground beef, pepperoni and 1/4 cup of the sauce in medium bowl.  Form into 4 (1/2-inch thick) patties.
Place burgers on grill over medium heat.  Cook 6 to 8 minutes or until thoroughly cooked.  During last minute of cooking, top with cheese.
Meanwhile, heat remaining pizza sauce.  Place burgers on buns.  Top with heated sauce.

Description:
