Title:
Touch of the Tropics Tea Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup fresh finely chopped mint leaves
1 1/2 tsp lemon zest
1 tsp lime zest
1 T fresh lemon juice
2 1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt, set aside.
Cream butter and sugar together.  Mix in eggs, mint, zest and lemon juice.  Add in flour mixture.
Chill at least one hour.
Roll out 1/8" thick and cut into desired shapes.  Place on ungreased baking sheet and bake until edges turn lightly brown, 6-8 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cat Hill
Description: 
A nice light cookie with hints of mint and citrus, perfect with tea or lemonade.
