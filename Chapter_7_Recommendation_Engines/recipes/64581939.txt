Title:
Curry Mango Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1/2Cup plain yogurt or reduced fat sour cream
1/2Cup reduced fat mayo
1/3Cup mango chutney (I like more so I add another large dollup)
1tsp turmeric
1/4tsp cinnamon
pinch cayenne
1Tbsp curry powder (pref Madras)
Salad:
12oz  pasta shells or rotini or similar
4Tbsp slivered almonds
1/4Cup chopped red onion
2 large carrots grated
1 large bunch of broccoli cut into small pcs
1/2 red pepper chopped
1/2 green pepper chopped

Directions:
1. Heat pan and toast almonds until fragrant, remove almonds and let cool
2. Place curry powder in hot pan for 1 min until fragrant
3. Mix mayo, yogurt(or sr cream), mango chutney and spices in small bowl
4. Mix veggies, pasta, almonds in salad bowl
5. Top with curry sauce mixture and fold in.
6. Chill for 1 hr and enjoy!
