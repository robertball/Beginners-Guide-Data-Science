Title:
Hot Potato Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. of new potatoes quartered
2 tbsp. rosemary
2 tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. sea salt
1/2 tsp. freshly ground pepper
1 pkg. of center cut bacon*
1/4 c. vegetable oil
1/4 c. red wine vinegar*
1 tbsp. sugar*
2 garlic cloves pressed*
1 10oz. pkg of baby spinach (fresh)
1 10oz. pkg of romaine
Grated parmesean (optional)

Directions:
Combine first six ingredients and toss to coat.  Place in a single layer in a pan and roast at 400 for 30-40 minutes.
Meanwhile, cook bacon in batches reserving 1/2 drippings.  Whisk oil and next 6 ingredients into drippings and cook over medium heat for 3-4 minutes.
Combine greens and parmesean, if desired.  Add warm dressing and toss well.  Allow potatoes to cool slighly then top greens with potatoes and crumbled bacon.
*If avoiding bacon dressing whisk balsalmic, oil and toss with greens.  Crumble precooked turkey bacon over all and serve.

Submitter: 
Miss Amy
Description: 
This is a perfect summer main dish salad or a great winter salad to serve with a warm soup.  Can be modified to be made with crumbled turkey bacon and balsalmic vinegar if you are saving calories.
