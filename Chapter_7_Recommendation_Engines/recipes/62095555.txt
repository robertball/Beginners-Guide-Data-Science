Title:
Fresh Blueberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup shortening
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1 cup blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease an 8 x 8-inch pan.
Sift together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
Cream shortening and alternately add to flour mixture with milk.
Beat for 2 minutes.
Add egg and beat for 1 minute.
Combine lemon peel with berries and fold into batter.
Pour into pan.
Bake for about 50 minutes.

This is a DELICIOUS blueberry cake made with fresh blueberries! It is great with confectioners sugar or whipped cream on top!
