Title:
Fancy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (10.75oz) cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 cup mayonnaise
1 pint sour cream
1/3 cup chopped green onions
1 pkg (30oz) frozen hash browns
2 cups corn flakes, crushed
3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Butter 9x13in pan.
In a large saucepan over medium heat melt together cream of mushroom soup, cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream and green onions.
In a large bowl mix together the hashbrowns with 1/2 of the cheese mixture.  Pour hashbrown mixture into pan and spread evenly.
Pour the rest of the cheese mixture over hashbrowns and spread evenly.  Top with corn flakes.  Drizzle corn flakes with melted butter.
Cover with foil and cook in oven for 45 minutes.  Remove foil and cook an additional 20 minutes.
