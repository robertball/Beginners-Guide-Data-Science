Title:
Crab Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Phillips crab meat
2 Tbsp. basil, finely chopped
4 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. grainy Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper, freshly ground
1/2 tsp. white sugar
8 tomato slices, 1/2” thick
8 fresh mozzarella slices, 1/2” thick
4 crusty sourdough bread slices, 1” thick
13 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
1. Mix the basil, lemon juice, mustard, salt, pepper and sugar in a bowl.
2. Slowly whisk in 12 Tbsp. of olive oil, mix well.
3. Take 4 slices of bread and cover with remaining olive oil and grill on one side until golden brown. You can grill outside, in a pan or broil in oven.
4. Place one tomato, then cheese and repeat. Until each slice has 2 slices of tomato and 2 slices of cheese.
5. Place 4 oz of crab on top of the tomato and cheese slices.
6. Drizzle desired amount of dressing on top and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
It is a very light, elegant meal. Great for a summertime lunch.
