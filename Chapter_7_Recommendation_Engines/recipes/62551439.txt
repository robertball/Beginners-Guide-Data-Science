Title:
Whipped Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (softened)
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl combine all ingredients.
Beat until the batter resembles whipped cream (about 10 minutes).
Drop by small spoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet.
Bake 12-15 minutes until bottoms are golden.

Submitter: 
Dan
Description: 
Tender and delicate, rich and light; these shortbread cookies will melt in your mouth, and warm your heart!
