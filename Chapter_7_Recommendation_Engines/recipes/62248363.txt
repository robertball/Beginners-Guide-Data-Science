Title:
Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large Bell Peppers
1 lb Ground Beef
1/2 - 1 lb shredded Mozzerella cheese
1/2 C brown Rice, instant is fine
2 tbs italian seasoning separated
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 sm onion
1/2 c chopped mushroom
2 green onions chopped
1-2 tbs minced garlic
2 tbs olive oil
1 14.5 oz can tomato sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Prepare your bell peppers by cutting off tops and removing seeds. Boil for 10 minutes or just until they change color. remove from water to allow them to cool for handling. They may be soft. Boil for less time if you want a crisper pepper.
prepare rice per packaging directions.
heat olive oil in pan and add onions, green onions, and garlic. Let cook till onion is tender then add the chopped mushrooms.
mix ground beef into the onion mixture and add 1 tbs of the italian seasoning, salt and pepper then  cook until brown stirring occasionally to mix all together. Drain.
add rice and about 1/3 of cheese to meat mixture mixing well.
in a small sauce pan on med heat mix tomato sauce, 1 tbs Italian seasoning, salt and pepper, and approx 1/4 C mozzerella cheese and cook until  warm and the cheese is partially melted into the sauce.
pour enough of the tomato sauce mixture to cover the  the bottom of a baking dish.
Cut the Peppers in half and place them into the baking dish. stuff with the meat mixture.
pour remaining tomato sauce over the top of the peppers and top with Mozzerella cheese
bake in oven approx 10 min or until cheese is melted and slightly browned
