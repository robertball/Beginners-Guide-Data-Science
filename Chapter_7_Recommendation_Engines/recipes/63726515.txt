Title:
Easy No Cook Nutella Smores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Graham crackers
2tbsp Marshmallow fluff
2tbsp Nutella

Directions:
1) Break graham crackers in half
2) Put 1/2 tbsp of Nutella onto 4 of the graham crackers and 1/2 tbsp marshmallow fluff on the remaining 4.
3)put smores together and enjoy

Submitter: 
Amber
Description: 
You no longer have to have a campfire to eat a good smore. These smores use nutella and marshmallow fluff so there is no cooking involved.
