Title:
Ham ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2, 8 oz packages of cream cheese
2 tablespoons of parsley flakes
1/2 cup of mayonaise
1 teaspoon of garlic powder
1, 6 oz can of chunk ham o 6 oz package of chopped ham (luncheon meat)
1 cup of chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except the pecans. Pour mixture onto waxed paper. Chill, form into a ball and roll in the chopped nuts.
