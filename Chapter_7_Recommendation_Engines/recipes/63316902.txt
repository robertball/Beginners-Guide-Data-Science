Title:
Yam and Kale Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole grain tortilla wraps
2 medium yams
4 large leafs of kale
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 can drained black beans
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette
2 tbsp soy sauce
1/2 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Cut kale into bite sized pieces and mix with balsamic vinaigrette and soy sauce.  Let marinade in the refrigerator for about an hour.
Cut yams into quarters and boil for 15-20 minutes or until soft when pierced with a fork. Cut into cubes.
Drain black beans and heat until warm.
Saute kale quickly for about 2 minutes in oil until dark green.
Combine yams, kale, beans, in feta cheese in a warm tortilla. Enjoy.
