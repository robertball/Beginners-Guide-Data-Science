Title:
Pickeld Eggs and Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Whole Beets (Reserve Juice)
2 Whole Cloves
1 Bay Leaf
1/2 C Vinegar
1/2 C Beet Juice
3 Whole Black Peppercorns
1 Med Onion - Thinly Sliced
1/2 t Salt
2 T White Sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients (except Beets) and bring to a rolling boil and stir.  Remove from heat and cool to room temp.
Pour into a glass jar (non-reactive), add beets and 6 Shelled Hard Boiled Eggs.  Insure that Eggs are covered. Allow eggs to pickle for about 1 week.  Keep Refrigerated.
