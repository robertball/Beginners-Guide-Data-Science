Title:
California Aged Gouda With Balsamic Reduction Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982077.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup balsamic vinegar
4 teaspoons sugar
12 (2/3 ounce) spears California aged Gouda cheese
Watercress sprigs, for garnish

Directions:
In small saucepan over high heat, bring vinegar to simmer. Continue cooking until vinegar is reduced to 1/4 cup.
Whisk in sugar; set aside to cool.
For each serving, plate 3 spears cheese. Drizzle over 1/4 of the reduction. Garnish with watercress sprigs.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
The nutty richness of aged gouda is a perfect match for a sweet and tangy balsamic vinegar reduction. Serve this beautiful dish as an appetizer or cheese course.
