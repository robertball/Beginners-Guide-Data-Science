Title:
Quick and Easy Cincinnati Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package McCormick速 Mild Chili Seasoning
1 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Cinnamon
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
8 ounces spaghetti, cooked
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped onions

Directions:
Cook ground beef in large saucepan over medium-high heat until beef is no longer pink, drain.
Add chili seasoning, cinnamon, and tomato sauce to beef mixture. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
Serve chili: 1) over spaghetti, 2) topped with beans, 3) topped with Cheddar, 4) topped with onions, or 5) combination of all 5.
