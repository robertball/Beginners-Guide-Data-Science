Title:
Slow Cooker Creamy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 onion, finely chopped
2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed chicken broth
2 cups water
5 large potatoes, diced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups fat free half-and-half cream
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk

Directions:
Place bacon and onion in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until bacon is evenly brown and onions are soft. Drain off excess grease.
Transfer the bacon and onion to a slow cooker, and stir in chicken broth, water, potatoes, salt, and  pepper. Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 7 hours, stirring occasionally.
In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and half-and-half. Stir into the soup along with the evaporated milk. Cover, and cook another 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Sylvia Marchesi Walton
Description: 
This is a very rich and creamy soup.  A great family favorite. It may be cooked on the stove or in a slow cooker. Garnish with chives, if desired.Modified from: Slow Cooker Creamy Potato Soup
