Title:
Chelsea's Chicken Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbls unsalted butter
4 tbls all purpose flour
1 tsp cumin
2 cups homemade or store bought chicken broth

Directions:
Melt butter in pot and whisk in flour, add cumin and let simmer while whisking 2 minutes to cook the flour.
Add broth slowly while whisking and then let simmer for 20 minutes stirring occasionally.
Taste, then add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Chelsea Clerke
Description: 
This is a simple but amazingly delicious gravy that goes great with Chelsea's Bacon Roast Chicken, or pretty much any chicken you can think of!
