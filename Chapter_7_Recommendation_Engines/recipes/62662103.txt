Title:
Gluten Free, Dairy Free, Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup egg white, egg substitute
1 tsp extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Combine ingredients and drop by teaspoonfuls on cookie sheet.  Bake for 8-12 minutes until starting to brown.  Let cool on the cookie sheet for at least 10-15 minutes before removing.
