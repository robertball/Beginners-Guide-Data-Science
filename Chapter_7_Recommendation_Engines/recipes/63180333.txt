Title:
Honey Ginger Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound carrots, sliced
1/2 pound snow pea pods
2 tablespoons butter
2 1/2 tablespoons honey
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Bring a pot of water to a boil.  Add carrots and snow peas and cook until tender but still firm, about 5 minutes. Drain.
In a large skillet over low heat, melt butter with honey.  Stir in ground ginger and lemon juice.  Stir in carrots and simmer until heated through.
