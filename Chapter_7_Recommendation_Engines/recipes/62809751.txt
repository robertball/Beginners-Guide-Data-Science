Title:
Tofu Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665363.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1 300g pkg soft Tofu
- 3 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
- 4 Tbsp. Olive Oil
- 3 Cloves Garlic (minced)
- 5 tsp. Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb
- 1/2 c. Almond Milk
- 2 tsp. Sea Salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender / food processor and puree till blended and smooth.  Allow to sit for 2-3 hours for flavors to blend.  Serve with any salad dressing.  Stores for up to 2 weeks in refigerator.

Submitter: 
Poetic_thing
Description: 
I created this when I couldn't find a dressing that was free of vinegar/dairy.  We use it in place of mayonnaise and sour cream too.
