Title:
HEDGEHOG BREAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1087678.jpg
Ingredients:
1 round loaf ofbread, about 2 lbs
8 oz. Shredded swiss cheese
1/2 stick of butter, melted
Chopped garlic to taste (1/4 cup)
1/2 cup cooked and chopped bacon
8 oz. Raclette cheese

Directions:
Cut bread into a checkerboard pattern of 1 inch squares not cutting through the bottom crust.
Mix shredded swiss, butter, bacon and stuff into the cut areas of the loaf.
Tear the raclette into pieces and stuff between the cut areas.
Drizzle olive oil over the top and sprinkle lightly with salt if desired.
Place loaf on a pizza stone and bake for 35 minutes at 350 until golden crusty brown
Serve hot, pull apart with your fingers.
