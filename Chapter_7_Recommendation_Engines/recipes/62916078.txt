Title:
Cream Cheese Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tub (12oz) light cream cheese (room temp)
1 (1.4oz) sugar-free vanilla instant pudding
3 C skim milk
20 oz lite cherry pie filling or sugar-free preserves

Directions:
In sm. mixing bowl, combine pudding mix and milk. Beat on low. Add cream cheese. Increase speed and beat until smooth and thickened. Pour into lg serving dish. Top with filling or preserves and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Like a cheese cake but made with sugar free or light ingredients. Only 9 carbs
