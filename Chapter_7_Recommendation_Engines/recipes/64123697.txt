Title:
Chocolate Chip Sheet Cookie (Dairy Free!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups dairy free margarine (Earth Balance)
6 cups brown sugar (loose)
1 Tbsp vanilla extract
6 eggs
4.5 cups flour
2 Tbsp baking powder
1.5 tsp salt
1 cup mini dairy free chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Melt marg.
3. Add sugar & vanilla & stir well to blend.
4. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each.
5. Combine flour, baking powder & salt.
6. Add flour mixture to saucepan & mix well.
7. Spread on a greased 1/2 sheet cake pan.
8. Sprinkle chocolate chips over surface, then press lightly.
9. Bake for 25-30 mins - turn pan 1/2 way thru baking & check doneness along the way!  It will be browner & won't be risen up when it's done.
10. Cut when slightly cooled, before it hardens!

Submitter: 
Kerri
Description: 
This is a fairly simple sheet cookie!  Makes a ton but goes together quick!  Originally contained dairy... substitutes worked well! :)
