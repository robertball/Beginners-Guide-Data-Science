Title:
Apple Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chopped cabbage
1 unpeeled red apple, cored and chopped
1 unpeeled Granny Smith apple, cored and chopped
1 carrot, grated
1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
2 green onions, finely chopped
1/4 of a red onion diced small
3/4 cup of assorted dried fruit. (Apricots, cherries, golden raisins, cranberries etc...)
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 T Apple Cider Vinegar
1 tablespoon lemon juice, or to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cabbage, red apple, green apple, carrot, red bell pepper,green onions and dried fruit. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, brown sugar, and lemon juice. Pour dressing over salad toss.
