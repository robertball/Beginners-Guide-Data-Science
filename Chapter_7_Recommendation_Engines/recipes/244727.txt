Title:
Cucumber Ranch and Feta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497406.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Light Cucumber Dressing
4 cups romaine lettuce leaves, torn
1 cup green grapes, halved
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 cup croutons

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss all of the ingredients with the Hidden Valley(R) Original Ranch(R) Light Cucumber Dressing until well coated. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
This light and refreshing green salad with grapes, feta cheese, bell peppers, and croutons is complemented perfectly with Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Light Cucumber Dressing.
