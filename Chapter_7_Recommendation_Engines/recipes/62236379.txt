Title:
Philly 3-Step Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 oz) pkgs cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
1 graham cracker crust

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sugar and vanilla at medium speed with electric mixer until blended.
Add eggs, mix until blended.  Pour into crust.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until center is almost set.  Cool.
Refrigerate 3 hours or overnight.
