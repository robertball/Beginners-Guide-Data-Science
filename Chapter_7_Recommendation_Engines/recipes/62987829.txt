Title:
Harvest Pumpkin Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cupcakes:
4 eggs, slightly beaten
3/4 cup MazolaÂ® Vegetable Plus! Oil
2 cups sugar
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup Corn Starch
4 teaspoons Pumpkin Pie Spice
2 teaspoons Baking Powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
Frosting:
2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, cold
6 tablespoons butter OR margarine, cold
2 tablespoon orange juice
4 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
1 Tablespoon freshly grated orange peel
8 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
To make cupcakes: Blend the eggs, oil, sugar, and pumpkin in a large mixing bowl; set aside. Stir together dry ingredients in a separate bowl. Add dry ingredients to pumpkin mixture and beat until well blended.   POUR into lined muffin tins.  Fill about 2/3 full.  Bake in preheated 350 degrees oven for 20 minutes or until center springs back when touched. Cool 30 minutes. Spread with frosting.
To make frosting: Beat cream cheese and butter until fluffy. Add remaining ingredients and beat until smooth.  Spread over cooled cupcakes.
