Title:
Vegetarian Roll Up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 leaves lettuce, red leaf on the larger side
4 tablespoon pepper(s), red sweet, roastedcut into strips
4 leaves basil, fresh
4 tablespoon sprouts, alfalfa
2 tablespoon cheese, cheddar, low-sodium shredded or grated
4 teaspoon pumpkin seeds, shelled roasted, unsalted

Directions:
Lay lettuce leaves out flat
In the top third of one leaf, layer half the remaining ingredents and roll toward the stem end, Secure with a toothpick if necessary.
