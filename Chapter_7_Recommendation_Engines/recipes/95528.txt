Title:
Maple Praline and Peaches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fat-free cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons Aunt Jemima® Light Syrup, divided
2 Quaker® Maple Brown Sugar Multigrain Cakes
1 small ripe peach, peeled, sliced
2 teaspoons chopped toasted pecans

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese and 1 tablespoon syrup in small bowl until smooth.
Spread mixture over Maple Brown Sugar Multigrain Cakes, dividing evenly.
Top with peach slices.  Drizzle with remaining syrup; sprinkle with pecans.

Submitter: 
Quaker® Rice Snacks
Description: 
A delicious and nutritious way to start your day!
