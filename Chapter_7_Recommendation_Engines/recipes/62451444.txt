Title:
Molasses Grilled Peaches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 peach per person(this recipe is for 4 people, so 4 peaches)
~ 2 tbsp Molasses per person, or 1/2 cup
Ground cinnamon

Directions:
Cut peaches in half and remove pit.  Place flesh side down on a medium hot grill( 375F) and cook for 4-5 minutes.  Flip peaches over, dust with cinnamon, and fill each peach with 1 tbsp molasses.  Cook for 3 more minutes.  Remove and let peaches rest for 3-5 minutes,taking care to keep the 'well' of molasses intact.
