Title:
Cheesy Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068332.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) package frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
2 cups Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Spray one 9x13 inch pan with non-stick cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, butter, salt, pepper, onions, soup and cheese.
Gently mix and pour into prepared pan or dish.
Bake at 350Â° uncovered for 35 minutes.
