Title:
Secret Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
First
8 oz cream cheese @ room temp
8 oz cool whip
1 c sugar
1 tsp vanilla
Second
1 c mashed strawberries
1 c sugar
1 tsp cornstarch
3/4 c hot water
Crust
1 1/2 c flour
1 tsp sugar
dash of salt
1/4 c milk
1/2 c oil
1 pint strawberries

Directions:
Mix First ingredients with a mixer & pour into the cool whip bowl, chill until cold
Mix Second ingredients together & cook in microwave for 3min. Stir & cook 3 more min. Place in fridge to chill.
Cut 1 pint of strawberries into quarters & chill to keep from bleeding.
Mix Crust ingredients together & press into pie pan. Cook @ 450 for 12 min.
After crust is cool, place the FIRST mixture into the bottom & spread evenly. Mix the strawberry quarters into the strawberry mixture & spread over the cream cheese layer. Chill & serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is the recipe for the most famous pie in my hometown. They sell out of it everyday at the local sandwich shop.
