Title:
Cheese spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1, 2 cup package of 4 Cheeses
1, 8 oz package of cream cheese
1, 15 oz jar of Cheez Whiz, plain
1/2 package of Original Ranch Dressing Mix
3/4 cup of mayonaise
3/4 cup of sour cream
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix 4 Cheese, Ranch dressing mix, and cream cheese until well blended. Add all other ingredients one at a time. Mix untl smooth and refrigerate. The cheese spread will last about a week in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Angela Morris
Description: 
Great cheese spread for bread and rolls.
