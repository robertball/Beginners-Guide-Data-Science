Title:
Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 tablespoons cocoa
2 ounces liquid red food coloring
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup buttermilk
2 1/2 cups cake flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon vinegar
Frosting:
5 tablespoons flour
1 cup milk
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 2 9-inch layer pans. Cream shortening and sugar until fluffy. Beat in eggs. Make a paste of cocoa and food coloring; add to creamed mixture; beat well. Add salt and vanilla. Add flour alternately with buttermilk, beating well. Combine soda and vinegar; stir in gently until combined; do not beat. Divide into prepared pan. Bake 30 minutes or until cakes test done.
Cool in pan for 5 minutes; turn out onto rack and cool completely.
For frosting: Combine flour and milk in a medium-size saucepan. Cook and stir over medium heat until thick and raw flour taste disappears. Remove from heat; cool. Beat in sugar, butter and vanilla. Beat until it reaches spreading consistency.
