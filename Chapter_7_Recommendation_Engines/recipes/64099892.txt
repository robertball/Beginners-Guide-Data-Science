Title:
Twice Baked Potatoes (to die for) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1227205.jpg
Ingredients:
5 russet potatoes, cleaned and poked with a fork
5 slices of bacon, browned and chopped (reserve 1/3 for topping)
1 medium onion, diced
8 to 10 green onions, chopped (about 1/4 cup chopped)
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1.5 Tbsp. butter
1.5 Tbsp. olive oil
3 Tbsp. milk or greek yogurt
1.5 cups of shredded cheese, cheddar or other (reserve 1/3 for topping)
Salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste
TOP WITH
1/3 of chopped bacon
1/3 of cheese
1/3 of chopped green onions

Directions:
1. Clean potatoes and poke holes in all sides.  Bake in oven at 450 degrees for 45 to 1 hour.  Allow to cool (but keep the oven on), slice long ways and scoop out "meat" into a large mixing bowl.  Set skins on a baking sheet.
2. Brown bacon and dice. Reserving the bacon fat.   Throw 2/3 of bacon into mixing bowl with potato meat. Save remaining 1/3 for topping.
3. Throw diced onion and olive oil into the bacon fat and cook until browned.  Add garlic for one minute.  Add mixture to mixing bowl.
4. Add butter, milk or yogurt, cheese, salt and pepper to mixing bowl and combine ingredients.  Taste and add salt if necessary.
5. Fill potato skins with approximately 2 tablespoons of mixture, they should be slightly over-filled.  Top with the reserved cheese.
6. Bake 20 minutes and top with reserved bacon and chives.
