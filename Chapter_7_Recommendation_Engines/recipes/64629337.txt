Title:
Tuna and Egg Sandwich Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bread
20 grams butter
1 canned tuna (small can)
1 Tbsp mayonnaise
1/4 pc onion, minced
salt
pepper
2 hard boiled eggs
chopped parsley

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, mix the tuna, mayonnaise, onion, salt and pepper.
2. Take the yellow part of the hard boiled eggs then lightly mash it.
3. Take the bread crusts if you prefer then spread the tuna mixture on each side of two breads.
4. Add the egg yellows on top of the tuna mixture then add the chopped parsley on top of it. Add the other slice of bread in top.
5. Preheat the sandwich maker. Once done preheating, spread melted butter on the sandwich plates. Put the sandwich then close it. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the sandwich are crispy.
6. Take the sandwich toasts then cut in half diagonally before serving.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
You'll never go wrong with a tuna sandwich. Whatever you do with it it's always good and this sandwich toast is what I do when I want some color in my tuna sandwich. It's simple and tasty.
