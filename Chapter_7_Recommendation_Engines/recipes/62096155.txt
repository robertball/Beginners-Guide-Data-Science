Title:
Oven Steak With Mushrooms #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Lbs. Top Round Steak
1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tbsp. oil
1 (10.5 oz) can beef consomme
3 (6oz) Cans Mushroom steak sauce (Make sure it's mushroom steak sauce, not just mushrooms)

Directions:
Trim excess fat from steak and cut into 6 equal sized pieces
Pound each piece on both sides to tenderize
In a shallow pan, combine flour, salt and pepper and dredge each piece of steak lightly in flour mixture
In a large skillet, heat oil and brown meat on both sides
Arrange meat in a roaster pan, cover.
Bake in preheated oven at 325' for 45 minutes
Combine steak sauce and beef consomme and pour over meat
Return to oven and roast, covered, until meat is tender (another 45 minutes to 1 hour)

Description: 
A tasty way to prepare steak without needing a grill.
