Title:
Shulie Krinkles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.25 oz) pkg Fudge Brownie mix
1/2 (1 stick) butter, softened (can use margarine too)
1 lg egg
1 tsp vanilla
2 c powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients in a large bowl.
Cover and chill for at least an hour.
Form into tablespoon sized balls and roll in powdered sugar.
Bake at 350* for 12-14 minutes.
They should be soft and chewy and krinkle-y.
Let cool on baking sheet for 5 mintues before removing to cooling rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
RONNIEBETH13
Description: 
My Aunt Julie and I were making Christmas cookies and we tried to make cake-mix cookies with brownie mix.  This is the result which we named by combining our names. Our families ate them all before Christmas!
