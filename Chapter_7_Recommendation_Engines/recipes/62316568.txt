Title:
Kathy's Scrumpdelicious Layered Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small head of iceberg lettuce
2 stalks celery, sliced
1 small red onion, sliced thin
8 slices bacon, cooked until crispy
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup mayonnaise, thinned with 3 T white vinegar
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Layer lettuce, celery, and sliced red onion in a large bowl. Mix sugar and sour cream until well blended, pour over layers.  Next pour on mayonnaise mixture. Top with crumbled bacon, then shower with parmesan cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
I always get so many requests for this wonderful salad.  The dressing ingredients are a little unusual, but you've just got to try it, it's fabulous!
