Title:
Crispix Mix® Maple Cinnamon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 cups Kellogg's® Crispix® cereal
3 cups dry roasted peanuts
3 cups dried cranberries or dried cherries
1 cup butter or margarine
2 cups firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup maple syrup
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 cups candy-coated chocolate candies

Directions:
. In large bowl combine KELLOGG'S CRISPIX cereal, peanuts and cranberries. Spread on two 15 x 10 x 1-inch baking sheets coated with cooking spray. Set aside.
2. In large saucepan melt butter. Add brown sugar, maple syrup and salt. Bring to boiling over medium-high heat, stirring constantly. Boil for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Stir in cinnamon, baking soda and vanilla.
3. Drizzle over cereal mixture. Stir until evenly coated.
4. Bake at 250° for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes. Stir in candies. Cool completely. Store in airtight container.
