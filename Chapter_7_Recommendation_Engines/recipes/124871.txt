Title:
Ice Cream Sundae Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992640.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers, crushed
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 quarts vanilla ice cream or flavor of your choice, softened

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, melt chocolate chips with milk and salt; cook and stir until thickened, about 25 minutes. Remove from the heat; set aside. Combine wafer crumbs and butter; set aside 1 cup. Press remaining crumbs into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. pan. Chill for 10-15 minutes. Pour chocolate over crumbs. Cover and freeze for 10-15 minutes or until firm. Spread the ice cream over chocolate. Sprinkle with reserved crumbs. Freeze at least 2 hours before serving.
