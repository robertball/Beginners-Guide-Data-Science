Title:
Almond Ricotta cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup ground almonds, separated
1/4 cup sliced or halved almonds
6 heaping tbsp sifted all purpose floud
1 tsp baking powder
4 heaping tbsp butter
6 tbsp fine sugar [white or brown]
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup ricotta or khoya
2 eggs
1 tbsp golden syrup or honey

Directions:
- coat 8" pan in generous amount of softened butter, then cover bottom and sides with 1/2 cup ground almonds decorate bottom of pan with sliced almonds.
- mix six heaping tbsp sifted all-purpose flour with 1 tsp baking powder and 1/2 cup ground almonds
- in another bowl, cream 100g butter [4 oz/4 heaping tbsp] with 6 tbsp fine sugar and 1 tsp vanilla
- mix in 1/2 cup ricotta, 2 eggs, and 1 tbsp golden syrup
- fold flour and butter mixtures together. If too thick due to cold weather, add 1 tsp milk
- pour in pan and bake at preheated 350 degree oven for 1/2 hour
- let cool several minutes and flip upside down onto plate
