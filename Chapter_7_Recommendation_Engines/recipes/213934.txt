Title:
Harvest Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989448.jpg
Ingredients:
6 SARGENTO® Reduced Sodium Colby-Jack Sticks
1 apple, cut into wedges, skin-on, cored
1 pear, cut into wedges, skin-on, cored
dried apricots (optional)
1/4 cup orange juice
1/2 cup caramel ice cream topping
1/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Cut each cheese snack crosswise in half. Dip apple and pear wedges in orange juice to prevent browning. Arrange on platter. Stir spice into caramel and serve as dip for cheese and fruit.

Submitter: 
Sargento®
Description: 
Using apples, pears, caramel sauce and Sargento Reduced Sodium Colby-Jack cheese, it takes no time to whip up this tasty treat.
