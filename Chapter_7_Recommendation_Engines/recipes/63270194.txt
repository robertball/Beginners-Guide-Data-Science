Title:
Buttery Garlic Shrimp Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound small to medium fresh shrimp (about 15-18)
3 TBS Butter
1 tsp seasoned salt Like Lawry's
1 clove fresh garlic, minced
vegetable oil

Directions:
If using wooden skewers soak in water for 20 minutes.
Heat grill on medium heat. Peel, leaving tails on and devein shrimp.  Dry on a paper towel.
Thread onto skewers.
In a microwave safe bowl, place butter and garlic.  Cook on high about 30 seconds or until melted.  Mix in seasoned salt.
Season grill with oil and place shrimp skewers on grill.  Brush with butter. After 4 minutes, flip and brush again.
Continue cooking until shrimp are plump and white with pink tails.
Serve immediately.
