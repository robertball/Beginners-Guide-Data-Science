Title:
Suzie's Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 cup butter
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
Filling
2 1/2 pounds cream cheese, softened
1 3/4 cups white sugar
3/4 teaspoon. vanilla
1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
5 eggs room temp.
2 egg yolks room temp.
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Lightly spray bottom of a 10 inch springform pan with Pam.
Cream butter and baking powder together then add confectioners' sugar and mix until light and fluffy.
Add flour and stir until well blended.
Pat into a 10 inch springform pan and prick all over with a fork.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until edges are lightly browned.
Allow to cool.
Increase oven temperature to 500 degrees F
In a large bowl, combine softened cream cheese, 1 3/4 cups sugar, 3 tablespoons flour mix until smooth. Stir in the eggs and the yolks and stir until well blended.
Add cream and mix only enough to blend.
Pour filling over crust and bake for 10 minutes at 500 degrees F. Reduce temperature to 200 degrees F  ) and continue to bake for one hour and 40 min.  DO NOT OPEN OVEN Turn oven off, but leave cake in for another hour.
Chill FOR 6 hours. This is very important so that it firms up all the way! If desired, top with your favorite fruit or serve plain.
