Title:
Drunk Turkey Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 links of turkey kielbasa cut into bite size pieces
1/2 cup diced onion
1 cup of lager style beer
1 good squeeze of Dijon mustard
2 tbsp brown sugar
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
On medium heat, saut√© kielbasa and onion in oil until brown and starting to soften. Whisk beer, brown sugar and mustard together until combined then pour mixture in skillet. Boil until liquid reduces by half and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
