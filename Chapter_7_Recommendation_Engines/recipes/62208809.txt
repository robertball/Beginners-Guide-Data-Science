Title:
Matt and Lulu's Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bag of idaho potatoes, peeled
1 stick of butter
3-4 tablespoons of sour cream
6-8 cloves of garlic, minced
4 sprigs of rosemary, crushed
3 dashes of dried parsley or chives
3 dashes of garlic salt or powder
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil the peeled potatoes until soft
Mix all ingredients but the potatoes in a mixing bowl (melt the butter if needed)
Mash potatoes with masher, and fold in the flavored ingredients.
