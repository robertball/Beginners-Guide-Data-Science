Title:
Coconut Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups coconut
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup milk*
1/3 cup lime juice**
2 tsp. grated lime rind***
4 medium eggs
1 8" pie shell
* Soy milk may be used instead
** Juice of two limes
*** Grated rind of two limes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine coconut, sugar, milk, lime juice, lime rind, and eggs in a medium bowl
Pour into pie shell.  Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes.

Description: 
Delicate fragrant lime tastes great in this coconut pie!
