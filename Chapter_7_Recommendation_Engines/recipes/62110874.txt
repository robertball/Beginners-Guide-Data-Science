Title:
Uncle John's Butter Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
Juice of one lemon
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp Worchestershire
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
1 1/2 tsp paprika
1 tsp tarragon

Directions:
Melt butter
Add all other ingredients
Stir and gently heat to combine flavors
This amount will take care of four to six chicken breasts nicely. The recipe can be doubled for larger barbeques. The paprika gives the chicken a lovely color.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
No tomato! Doesn't burn! Can be used through the entire cooking process.

This is best on poultry as the tarragon mixes nicely with the flavor of the bird.
