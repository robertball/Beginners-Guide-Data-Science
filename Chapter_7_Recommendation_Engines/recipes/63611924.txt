Title:
Healthy Strawberry Banana Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of ice
6 Tbs plain, nonfat yogurt
1 banana, sliced
3 strawberries, sliced

Directions:
Wash strawberries and slice. Peel and slice the banana as well.
Put ice, yogurt, strawberries, and banana in a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Snowgirl1224
Description: 
Just a little recipe I concocted when I started my health food kick. Tastes just like strawberry banana yogurt! Best of all, not only does it taste oh so good, but it's oh so good for you!
