Title:
Quinoa with Toasted Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked quinoa
2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons finely chopped shallots
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 1/4 cups organic vegetable stock
1 tsp fresh thyme
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/4 cup walnuts, toasted
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
1 tablespoon toasted walnut oil
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Rinse and drain quinoa.
Heat a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add 2 teaspoons olive oil to pan; swirl to coat. Add shallots; saute 1 minute or until tender.
Add garlic; cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Add quinoa; cook 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
Add vegetable stock, thyme, and kosher salt; bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 12 minutes or until the liquid is absorbed and quinoa is tender.
Combine quinoa, walnuts, chives, walnut oil, and pepper; toss.
