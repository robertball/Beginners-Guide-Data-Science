Title:
Undercover Broccolini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987419.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon honey mustard
8 ounces Mann's BroccoliniÂ®, blanched
3 ounces prosciutto or ham, thinly sliced

Directions:
For dipping sauce: In a small bowl, mix sour cream and honey mustard until well blended. Place in a small bowl and set aside.
Blanch Broccolini, pat dry with paper towels. Trim off ends of Broccolini stalks. Cut proscuitto or ham slices lengthwise into 1-inch strips. Tightly wrap 1 or 2 strips diagonally around each Broccolini stalk, leaving about 1-inch of stalk showing at the bottom. Arrange on a platter; adding dipping sauce. (This can be covered with plastic wrap and refrigerated up to 24 hours.) Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Mann's Fresh Vegetables
Description: 
Broccolini stalks are wrapped in thin slices of prosciutto and served with a creamy mustard sauce.
