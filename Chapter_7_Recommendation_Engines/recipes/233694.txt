Title:
KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS Chicken Bruschetta Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034322.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups farfalle (bow tie) pasta, uncooked
1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
1 (16.4 ounce) package KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS Chicken Bruschetta Pasta
2 tablespoons KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Cook 3 cups pasta as directed on package, omitting salt.
Cook and stir chicken in half the Simmer Sauce in large skillet until done.
Drain pasta.  Add to skillet with remaining Simmer Sauce; mix lightly.
Top with Finishing Sauce and Parmesan; cover.  Cook on low heat 2 to 3 min. or until heated through.

Submitter: 
KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS
Description: 
Chicken strips are cooked in a basil garlic sauce, then simmered in a savory finishing sauce with farfalle and Parmesan cheese for a quick weeknight meal.
