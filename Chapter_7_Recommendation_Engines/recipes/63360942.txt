Title:
Apple Cider Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/895201.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups apple juice
2 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tbsp. cornstarch
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ground cloves

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients and cook over medium heat just until thickened and clear. Refrigerate the leftovers for up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Serve over pancakes, waffles, or french toast.
