Title:
Greek style mahi mahi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4LB mahi mahi fillets
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup olive oil
3 tbsp. chopped fresh oregano
3 tbsp. chopped fresh min (speariment)
1/3 tsp. fresh minced garlic
1 tsp. finely shredded lemon peel
1/4 salt

Directions:
clean fish and dry with paper towels. cut fish if needed in to 4 servings. place all ingredients together  in a small baking dish,stir together then lay fish in  (make sure it is not overlaying each other. marinate each side for 30 min.
coat grill with nonstick cooking spray heat grill to high heat sear in juices each side 15 seconds then lay skin side down and turn to medium heat (keep fish away from flames)and cook for 12-15 minutes
or until fish flakes with fork
to remove from grill use 2 spatulas one in front and one in back
sides for this dish
small red potatoes chopped with garilc,onions and olive oil and butter 2 tbsp. (bake 45 min 400 deg. broil 4 min)
grilled squash
qrilled zucchini
