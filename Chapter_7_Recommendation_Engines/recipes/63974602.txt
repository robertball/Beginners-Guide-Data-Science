Title:
Ginger Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1102938.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
3 Tbsp. Premium Dry cocoa
3 Tbsp. Dry ginger
1/2 C. Butter
1/2 C. Brown sugar
1/2 C. Maple sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Quick oats
1 C. Chocolate chips
2 tsp. Maple syrup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, B. powder, B. soda, cocoa and ginger together in a small bowl. Set aside
In a large mixing bowl combine butter and sugars together and mix until butter dissolves into the sugar.
Add eggs to the butter/sugar mixture and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and creamy
Mix in oats and chocolate chips continuing to beat on medium speed and stir in maple syrup to make a soft dough that comes away from the sides of the bowl
Stir in the dry ingredients and mix all ingredients well to make a smooth dough
Take a small amount of dough and roll it in your hands and place 2 inches apart on lined cookie sheet. Press cookies down with a fork and bake in oven for 10 minutes
Remove from pan and set on wire rack to cool completely before storing away.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A delicious taste of chocolate with the zest of ginger to make a good snack cookie for tea time or a healthy snack on the go.
