Title:
Monster Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/2 c. margarine
1 c. sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. peanut butter
2 t. soda
1 T. vanilla
4 1/2 c. oatmeal
1/2 c. M & M's
1/2 c. chocolate chips
1/2 c. nuts
Bake on greased cookie sheet at 350Â° for 8-10 minutes.  Makes 5 1/2  dozen cookies.
This is Mom's recipe.  It is a great way to use up Holiday M & M's.

Directions:
Cream together sugar, brown sugar, peanut butter, eggs, margarine, and baking soda.  Add 1 T. vanilla; mix well. Stir in remaining ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
