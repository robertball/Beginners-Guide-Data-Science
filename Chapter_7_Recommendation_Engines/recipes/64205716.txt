Title:
Pork Chop Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp Fennel Seeds
1 and 1/2 Tbsp Coriander Seed
1/2 Tbsp Peppercorn
Salt as desired

Directions:
Place fennel seeds, coriander, and peppercorn in pan over medium heat.  Roast seeds in the pan, tossing seeds in the pan often. Watch carefully, remove when seeds are lightly brown and fragrant. Remove from heat. Let cool on a plate for a few minutes. Pour the mixture in a grinder or food processor.  Add desired salt. Blend to a fine powder.
Store in tightly sealed container or freeze.
