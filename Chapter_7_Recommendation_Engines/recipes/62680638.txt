Title:
Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast cut in cubes
Sauce:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white vinegar
2/3 cup water or pineapple juice/water
2 tbsp cornstarch
1 tin pineapple chunks (drained)
Green pepper, cut into chunks (optional)

Directions:
Brown chicken, turning frequently.
While chicken is cooking, mix sauce ingredients together in large mixing bow.
Pour sauce over cooked chicken. Add pineapple and pepper. Serve over cooked pasta or rice.
