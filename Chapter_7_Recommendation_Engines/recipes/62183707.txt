Title:
Sausage Italian Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sweet or hot Italian sausage, casings removed
1 large onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, pressed
2 14.5 oz cans low-sodium chicken broth
4 small zucchini, chopped
4 cups kale, chopped
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon marjoram
Dash crushed red pepper flakes, to taste

Directions:
In a large, heavy saucepan or Dutch oven, brown and break up sausage over medium-high heat.
Add onion and garlic.  Cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Lower heat and simmer for about 8 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Amy Cothran Gray
Description: 
Sausage, kale, zucchini and onion make a yummy soup.  Originally from Leanne Ely's Menu Mailer.
