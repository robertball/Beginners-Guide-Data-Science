Title:
King Ranch Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1 large green pepper, chopped
2 C. diced, cooked chicken meat
1 can chicken soup
1 can celery soup
1 - 10 oz can Rotel tomatoes
1 t. chili powder
1/4 t. garlic powder & 1/2 t. pepper
8 oz. shredded cheddar
8 tortillas

Directions:
Saute onion and pepper.
Stir in all ingredients except tortillas and cheese.
Preheat oven to 375.
Grease 9 X 13 in. dish.
Cut tortillas into 1 in. strips.
Form single layer in bottom of dish.
Top w/ 1/3 of the mixture.
Do two more layers and spread the cheese on the top.
Bake at 375 until bubbly - about 45-50 minutes.
