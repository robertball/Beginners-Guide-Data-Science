Title:
Greek Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* Romaine lettuce
* 1 cucumber, sliced
* 2 large tomatoes, cut up to liking
* 1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
* 1/2 cup thinly sliced green bell pepper (optional)
* 1/2 cup whole, pitted Kalamata olives
* 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
* 2 Tablespoons dried oregano
* 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
* 2 Tablespoons olive oil
* salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, layer Romaine lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, Kalamata olives and feta cheese. Sprinkle with oregano, then drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Note: I used zesty Italian dressing instead of oil and lemon juice.
