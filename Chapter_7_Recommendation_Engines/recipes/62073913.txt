Title:
Greasy Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. wide egg noodles
2.5 cans of chicken broth
2 sticks butter or margarine
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring chicken broth and 1 stick of butter to a boil. Add noodles and other stick of butter. Boil until nooldes are tender and broth is absorbed. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
KENDRAJEAN1
Description: 
If you're looking for a low-fat recipe, this is not for you. These noodles are boiled in nothing more than chicken broth and margarine and taste awesome!
