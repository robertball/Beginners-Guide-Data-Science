Title:
Slow cooker hunny barbecue ranch wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken wings
Hunny
Sweet Baby Ray's barbecue sauce
3 packets dried Hidden Valley ranch dressing.

Directions:
In a large bowl combine 1 1/2 cups barbecue sauce with 1 cup hunny and 3 packets ranch dressing.
Stir together, mixing well.
Dip wings in to mixture, coating wings.
Transfer to slow cooker.
Pour any remaining mixture over wings.
Cook on high for 4 hours, or until done.
Cook's note: a 6 quart slow cooker was used for this recipe, you may need to adjust quantity and cooking times.  I used Hidden Valley ranch, use what you like.  Adjust recipe as needed, and to your liking.

Submitter: 
Michael Rodgerson
Description: 
Moist and tasty chicken wings that can go with anything, I could see thees going well with rice.
