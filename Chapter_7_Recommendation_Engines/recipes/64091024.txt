Title:
Gerry's Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1190240.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lime, 1/2 Lemon, White Sugar, Reposado Tequilla, Grand Marnier, salt, ice, cocktail shaker.

Directions:
Juice the 1 lime, and 1/2 lemon into the cocktail shaker.
Add two heaping tablespoons of white sugar. (Don't be shy)
Add two shots(3oz) of a Reposado Tequila(ex: gold tequila like Jose Cuervo)
Add ice, and shake it baby.
Let shaker rest for 1 or 2 minutes (very important)
Use left over lemon to salt serving glass (optional)
Serve over ice.
Top with Grand Marnier (optional)
Enjoy!
