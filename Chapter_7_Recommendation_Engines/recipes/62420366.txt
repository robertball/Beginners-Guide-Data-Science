Title:
Easy Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
1 cup Lemon pie filling
1/2 cup chicken broth/stock
1/4 C chopped, grean onions
salt & pepper to taste
non-stick cooking spray OR 1/8 Cup of Canola/Vegetable Oil

Directions:
Heat large skillet on med-high add non-stick cooking spray or oil and heat though. Cut chicken breast into peices, season with salt and pepper and add to skillet, cook until nearly done.
Add 1 cup Lemon pie filling and the 1/2 cup chicken broth to the pan, combine well, add chopped green onions, stir and cover. Let simmer about 6-8 minutes, until desired thickness. Serve over hot white rice, to complete.

Submitter: 
Ana Pallarez
Description:
