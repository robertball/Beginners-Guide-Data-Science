Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless chicken breasts,  cooked and shredded
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup Blue Cheese dressing
3/4 cup pepper sauce (such as Frank's Red HotÂ®)
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar or Jack cheese
1 bunch celery, cleaned and cut into 4 inch pieces
1 (8 ounce) box chicken-flavored crackers or corn chips

Directions:
Cook and shred chicken, Combine Chicken and hot sauce in a skillet over medium heat, until heated through. Mix softened cream cheese and Blue Cheese dressing,  stirring until well blended . Layer Cream Cheese mixture. Then Chicken and hot sauce mixture, Sprinkle shredded cheese over the top, Heat in oven until hot and bubbly. Serve with celery sticks and crackers or chips

Submitter: 
Mia Corriea
Description: 
This tangy, creamy dip tastes just like buffalo chicken wings. It's best served hot with crackers and Celery sticks. Everyone loves the results!Modified from: Buffalo Chicken Dip
