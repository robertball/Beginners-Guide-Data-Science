Title:
Scalloped Corn (aka Corn Casserole) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/416554.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can Creamed Corn
1 cans Kernel Corn (drained)
1 c Sour Cream
2 lg Eggs
¼ c Margarine
½ c Sugar
1 pkg Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350°.
GREASE Corningware.
MIX together corn, sour cream, eggs, margarine and sugar.
MIX in corn muffin mix.
POUR into prepared baking dish.
BAKE for 45 minutes or until a knife inserted midway into pan comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description: 
This is a great, traditional side dish.  We especially like to serve it with Mexican food and at Thanksgiving.
