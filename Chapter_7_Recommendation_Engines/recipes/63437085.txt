Title:
BARK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Saltine crackers
2 sticks butter
1 c. sugar
12 oz. pkg. chocolate chips
Nuts, sprinkles, etc.

Directions:
Line cookie sheet with foil
Fill sheet with 1 layer saltines
Boil butter and sugar 2 minutes
Pour over saltines and bake 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
Immediately spread melted chips over top
Add chopped nuts, etc.
Refrigerate 24 hours
Break into pieces.
