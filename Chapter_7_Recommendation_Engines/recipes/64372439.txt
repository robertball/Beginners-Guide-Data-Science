Title:
Tri-Color Slaw with Lime Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Head Green Cabbage, Cored & Shredded
1/2 Head Red Cabbage, Cored & Shredded
1 Lb Carrots, Peeled & Shredded
1 Bunch Cilantro, Roughly Chopped
1/3 Cup Lime Juice (approx. 3 Limes)
2/3 Cup Peanut Oil
2 tsp Sugar
Kosher Salt, to taste
Ground Pepper, to taste

Directions:
Toss together Green & Red Cabbage, Carrots & Cilantro
Whisk together Lime Juice & Peanut Oil.  Once combined, whisk in Sugar.
Toss Slaw and Dressing together.  Season generously with Salt & Pepper.
Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
Use within 3 days.
