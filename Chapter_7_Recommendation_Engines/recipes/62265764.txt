Title:
Sweet Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120265.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Egg
1/2 C Milk
1/4 C Oil
1 1/2 C Flour
1/2 C Sugar
2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F and spray muffin pan. Mix, batter will be lumpy, 2/3 full in muffin pan, bake 20 to 25 minutes.
