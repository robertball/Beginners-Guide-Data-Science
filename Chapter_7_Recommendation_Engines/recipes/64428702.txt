Title:
Mini Pumpkin Bunt Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2323407.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boxes of Betty Crocker Super Moist Cake Mix Spice
1 30oz can of pumpkin pie mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine cake mix and canned pumpkin together until smooth.
Press the batter into mini bunt cake tins; fill to the top.
Cook for about 15-20 minutes in oven.

Submitter: 
Chef Lizzie
Description: 
This simple 2 ingredient bunt cake will blow your mind and mouth! Perfect for holiday desserts and breakfast.
