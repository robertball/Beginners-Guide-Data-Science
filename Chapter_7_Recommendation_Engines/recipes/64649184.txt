Title:
Flourless Peanut Butter Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup natural organic peanut butter
2 very ripe bananas, mashed (about 1 cup)
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
3 whole eggs
1/2 tsp. sea salt
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp. cinnamon
optional add-ins:  1/2 cup choc. chips, 1 c. raspberries, 1/2 c. raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and prepare muffin tin with 12 paper liners.
In a medium bowl, combine all of the ingredients and mix until smooth.
Fold in optional if desired.
Put in liners and bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees.  Muffins should be firm and light brown.
Allow to cool for 15 minutes.
Store in sealed container in the fridge for up to a week.
