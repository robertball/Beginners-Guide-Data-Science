Title:
Chocolate Zucchini Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra light olive oil
1/4 cup of unsweetened applesauce
1  cup white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 1/2 cups shredded zucchini
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup chocolate chips
6 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup margarine
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour(use olive oil spray & dust pan with  dash cocoa powder instead of flour) a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, mix together the oil,applesauce,  sugar and 2 teaspoons vanilla until well blended. Combine the flour, 1/2 cup cocoa, baking soda  and salt; stir into the sugar mixture. Fold in the zucchini and walnuts. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake for 23 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until brownies spring back when gently touched.
Sprinkle chocolate chips over warm brownies when pan comes out of oven.
