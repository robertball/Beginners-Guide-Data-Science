Title:
2 ingredient fudge sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs. Hershey's Chocolate Syrup
1/4 Cup Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips in microwave on 50% power.
Stir in Hershey's syrup.
Heat on 50% power until drizzling consistency, stirring often.
Serve over ice cream.

Submitter: 
ACHOCOCAT
Description: 
I've always wanted a thicker ice cream topping than plain Hershey's syrup. I made this up one day and it is fast and super easy!
