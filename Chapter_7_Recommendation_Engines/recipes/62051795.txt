Title:
Chocolate Malt Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c. chocolate milk
2c. chocolate ice cream
3 tablespoons malted milk powder
handful of malte milk balls
chocolate sprinkles

Directions:
Carefully smash the malted milk balls into little pieces.Set aside. Use a blender to blend the milk, ice cream, & powder. Blend until well mixed. Pour into chilled glasses and add the malted milk balls and sprinkles.

Submitter: 
CHRISTCOOKS
Description: 
A magnificent mix of chocolate and malt that makes a great kids milkshake. Only for chocolate lovers.
