Title:
thin pork chops with mango salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large thin pork chops
4 T flour
2 T olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
Mango salsa:
1 mango, cubed
2 T red onion, chopped
2 fresh tomatoes, seeds removed, cubed
juice of 1 large lime
2 T fresh cilantro, chopped
1/2 jalapeno pepper, diced (optional)

Directions:
Make the salsa: combine all ingredients and adjust taste with salt and pepper and/or jalapeno.
Season pork chops with salt and pepper and dredge in flour. In a large frying pan heat the oil over medium high heat and saute one side of the porkchops until brown, about 3 minutes. Turn chops over and add salsa mix to the pan. Cook another 2-3 minutes. Serve immediately.  Serves 2.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
This is a quick and refreshing way to serve thin pork chops. The salsa is wonderful with chicken as well.
