Title:
Festive Brownie Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 squares of baking chocolate
4 tbsp butter/margerine
4 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 cups of granulated sugar
2/3 cups of all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt
1/2 cup of crushed candycanes

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 F, grease and flour the cookie sheet.
melt chocolate with butter in the micowave and let cool slightly.
beat eggs vanilla and sugar untill very thick and then add melted chocolate.
sift flour baking powder and salt, mix into batter and add candy. (the batter will seem very wet)
Drop 'small'(1tbsp) spoonfulls of batter onto cookie sheet and bake for 10min.
let set on cookie sheet for 3min and then carefully transfer on to wire racks to finish cooling.   Enjoy with a cup of hot cocoa!

Submitter: 
EBSEPKE
Description: 
This cookie is like the crunchy top of a brownie with crushed candycanes in it.
