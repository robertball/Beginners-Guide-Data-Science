Title:
Portobello Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1/4 cup olive oil
4 tsp. grated orange peel
1 tsp. fennel seed
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 pound sliced baby Portobello mushrooms
6 oz. baby spinach
11 oz. can mandarin oranges
1 thinly sliced red onion
1/4 cup slivered almonds

Directions:
Combine orange juice, olive oil, grated orange peel, fennel seed, pepper and salt; pour into large resealable plastic bag.  Add mushrooms and refrigerate for 15 minutes.  Drain mushroom and discard marinade.  Grill mushrooms until tender; cool slightly.
Combine spinach, mandarin oranges, red onion, almonds, mushrooms and toss.
