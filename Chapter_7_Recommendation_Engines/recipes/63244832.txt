Title:
Lattice Chicken and Peppers Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup salsa
1/2 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons chili powder
7 ounces whole roasted sweet peppers,drained and cut into strips
4 medium green onions, sliced (about 1/2 cup)
3 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 package (11 ozs.) refrigerated bread twists

Directions:
Stir the soup, salsa, sour cream, chili powder, peppers, green onions and chicken in a 12x8x2 inch shallow baking dish
Bake at 400 for 15 minutes. Stir.
Separate the bread twists into 16 strips.  Arrange the strips lattice fashion over the chicken mixture, overlapping stips as necessary to fit.
Bake for 15 minutes or moer until the bread is golden.
