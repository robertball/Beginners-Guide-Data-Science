Title:
Quick and Easy Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
17 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
cooking spray
1 T  garlic Powder, minced
2 tsp. pinch salt
1 T ground black pepper
1 T ground cumin
1#10 can salsa
1 5# bag  shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
grease a 2in. deep pan big enough to hold the chicken breasts. Rub chicken pieces with garlic, salt, pepper and cumin to taste; place in greased pan. Cover with #10 can of salsa.Cover pan. Bake chicken covered for 2 hours (if frozen ) internal temp of chicken must be 165.
Cover with the cheese the last 20 min. of baking.
Serve over black Beans and Rice

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Seasoned chicken bakes under a crown of salsa and cheese. Served over rice or buttered noodles, dinner is ready in a snap!Modified from: Quick and Easy Mexican Chicken
