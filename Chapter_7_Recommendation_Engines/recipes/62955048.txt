Title:
Orange-Ginger Madeleines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744650.jpg
Ingredients:
Butter Mixture:
1/4 Cup Unsalted Butter (1/2 Stick)
1 TBS Honey
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
Flour Mixture:
3/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
3/4 tsp Ground Ginger*
1/4 tsp Salt
Sugar Mixture:
1/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
3/4 Cup Confectioners' Sugar
2 Eggs
Glaze: **
3/4 Cup Confectioners' Sugar
1 tsp Grated Orange Zest
2 TBS Orange Juice/Juice from an Orange

Directions:
- Butter Mixture: melt butter in a saucepan over low heat; remove from heat and stir in honey and vanilla
- Flour Mixture: whisk flour, baking powder, ginger, and salt in a bowl; set aside
- Preheat oven to 325 degrees with rack in center
- Sugar Mixture: mix sugar and eggs in a bowl
- Fold flour mixture into sugar mixture; add butter mixture and fold until combined. Cover in plastic wrap and refrigerate 30 minutes.
- Spoon batter into buttered brownie singles pan filling each section halfway. Bake until cookies are puffed and golden on the edges - about 20-25 minutes. Transfer to wire rack; cool slightly; unmold onto rack and cool completely.
- Glaze**: Mix Confectioners' sugar, orange zest, and orange juice together well; spread onto cookies with pastry brush when cookies are completely cooled. Let stand 15 minutes.

Description: 
These . . . cakey cookies are a french treat traditionally served with tea. The buttery madeleines are spiced with ground [ginger] and coated with a sweet citrus icing.
