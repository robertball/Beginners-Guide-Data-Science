Title:
Slow Cooker BBQ Flat Iron Steak Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs flat iron steak, cubed or thin sliced
1 T dried chopped onion
1/2 c ketchup
1/2 c Italian salad dressing (oil based)
2 T soy sauce
1 T molasses or honey
4 hoagie buns, split and toasted
1 c cole slaw (optional)

Directions:
Place steak in clow cooker.
Sprinkle with dried onion, and turn cooker on high.
Mix ketchup, dressing, soy sauce, molasses or honey, and pour over steak.
Cook on high for one hour.  Reduce to low heat until meat is tender.
Serve on hoagie buns, and top with 1/4 cup cole slaw, for a southern bbq flavor.  Also good with sharp cheddar.

Submitter: 
Linda
Description: 
Ridiculously easy sauce and slow cooking make this savory steak sandwich a family favorite.
