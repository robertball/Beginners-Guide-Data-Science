Title:
Strawberry-Banana-Peanut Butter Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6911001.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup nonfat plain yogurt
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 banana
4 fresh strawberries, hulled
10 ice cubes

Directions:
Place yogurt, peanut butter, banana, strawberries, and ice cubes into a blender. Puree until smooth.
