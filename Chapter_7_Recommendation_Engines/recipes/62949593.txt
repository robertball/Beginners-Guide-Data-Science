Title:
MEL'S SWEET ITALIAN SAUSAGE TOMATO  PASTA SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725522.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBSP. WORCESTERSHIRE
1 TBSP. STEAK SAUCE
1/2 TSP. ROSEMARY
1/2 TSP. DRIED CHOPPED ONION SEASONING
PACKAGE OF SWEET ITALIAN SAUSAGE
1 LB. OF HAMBURGER MEAT
HUNTS PASTE (BASIL,GARLIC&OREGANO;)
1 10 OZ. CAN ROTEL (MEXICAN LIME&CILANTRO;)
2 24 OZ. JARS OF PASTA SAUCE(AS IN CLASSICO)(SPICY TOMATO&BASIL;)AND(FIRE ROASTED TOMATO&GARLIC;)(THESE I HIGHLY RECOMMEND FOR THIS SAUCE, BUT UP TO YOUR PREFERENCES)

Directions:
BROWN, HAMBURGER MEAT ON MEDIUM-HIGH HEAT,DRAIN PAN,THEN CUT UP SAUSAGE(TO YOUR LIKING)ADD TO PAN AND BROWN.
ADD ROSEMARY,DRIED ONION SEASONING,WORCESTERSHIRE & STEAK SAUCE.STIR AROUND,
THEN ADD ROTEL&PASTE.THEN ADD YOUR JARS OF TOMATO SAUCE.
THEN SIMMER, TILL ITS HEATED THROUGH.
