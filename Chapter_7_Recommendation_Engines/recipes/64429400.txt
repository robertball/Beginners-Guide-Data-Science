Title:
Grape Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups broccoli
6 green onions
1 cup diced celery
1 cup green grapes
1 cup red grapes
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sugar
1 Tablespoon cider vinegar
1/2 lb. sliced bacon cooked and crumbled
1 cup slivered almonds, toasted

Directions:
combine vegetables and grapes.  In another bowl, whisk the mayonnaise, sugar, and vinegar. Toss these together and chill. Stir in bacon and almonds just before serving.
