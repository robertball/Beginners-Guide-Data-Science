Title:
Mexican Bow Tie Pasta with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. cooked bow tie pasta
1 1/2 lbs. boneless chicken cut into bite sized pieces
2 tbsp. butter
2 cloves garlic - crushed
1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup milk
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup salsa
8 oz. package of cream cheese cut into 1/2 inch cubes

Directions:
Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat and add chicken.
Cook about 5 minutes.
Add garlic and cook until chicken is no longer pink.
Stir in flour then slowly add milk and broth, stirring constantly.
Add salsa and cream cheese and stir until sauce is hot and cream cheese is melted.
Add cooked pasta and stir until coated with sauce.
Serve immediately.
