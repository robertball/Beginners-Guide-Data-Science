Title:
Kalenutsco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1959192.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups coconut water
4 leaves kale
1 cup walnuts
1/2 cup shredded sweetened coconut

Directions:
Blend coconut water, kale, walnuts, and coconut in a blender until smooth; refrigerate up to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Edward Fine
Description: 
I found this recipe in my house and made it, I liked for its sweet taste.
