Title:
Mom's Old Fashioned Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 to 10 Med. Red Potatoes, boiled, cooled, and diced (or 5 -6 russets)
4 stalks celery, chopped fine
1 medium red onion, diced
3 or 4 Tblsp. Dill Relish
1 Can sliced Olives,
6 Hard boiled eggs, chopped
2 tsp celery seed
1-1/2 cups Best Foods Mayo
½ cup Original Ranch Dressing
2 Tblsp. Dijon Mustard (French’s regular mustard may be substituted if desired)
Seasoned Salt and Pepper to taste
½ Tbsp. Garlic Powder
Pinch of cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes for a little extra kick

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large bowl…mix well, taste for seasoning, adjust if needed.  Sprinkle top with Paprika for looks and chill at least 2 hours before serving.  Serves 8.

Submitter: 
Beverly Burton
Description: 
I usually make this potato salad whenever we have a BBQ and am always asked for the recipe.  Most often it's the favorite of everyone.  This is my mother's southern recipe....Enjoy
