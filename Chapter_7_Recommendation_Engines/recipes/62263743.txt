Title:
Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked, diced chicken breasts
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup reduced fat mayo
2 tablespoons cream (1/2 & 1/2)
2 tablespoons vinegar
1/4-1/2 teaspoon lemon pepper
Salt (I salt chicken when cooking)

Directions:
Cook and largely dice chicken breasts
Dice celery
Mix together remaining ingredients for dressing
Combine dressing w/ chicken and celery
Chill
Serve on lettuce leaves.

Submitter:

Description: 
This chicken salad recipe is a revamped version of my original chicken salad added to my recipe box last year.
