Title:
Chopped Ham Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1112346.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cooked ham, diced
1 cup finely diced celery
1 cup finely diced red onion
1/3 cup mayonnaise (or to taste)
1 tsp rosemary, crumbled
1/4 cup dressing
2 Cups, +/- lettuce or romaine

Directions:
Dice the ham, celery, onion
Combine
Blend in mayonnaise and rosemary
Chill for 1 hr or more
Serve on bed of lettuce and/or romaine
Garnish with olives, tomato, other favorites such as radish, peperoncini (mild yellow peppers, called friggitello in Italy), etc
Serve the dressing on the side.

Submitter: 
John G
Description: 
We enjoy using leftover ham for salads, egg dishes, other meals.
