Title:
Pumpkin Pecan Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521907.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can pumpkin pie filling
1 C evaporated milk
1 C sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 (18.25 oz) Pillsbury Moist Supreme Golden Butter cake mix
1 C chopped pecans
1 C melted butter
whipped cream or cool whip
ground nutmeg

Directions:
Stir first 5 ingredients together.  Pour into lightly greased 13 X 9 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle dry cake mix evenly over the pumpkin mixture.  Sprinkle evenly with pecans.  Drizzle the butter over the top.  Bake at 350 degress for one hour.  Let stand at least 10 minutes before serving. Serve warm or cold and topped with whipped cream or cool whip. Sprinkle with nutmeg if desired.

Submitter: 
Kathleen Duncanson Heseltine
Description: 
This is a delicious desert that is a cross between pumpkin pie and pecan pie.  The great news?  No piecrust!  Quick and easy to prepare and delicious hot or cold.
