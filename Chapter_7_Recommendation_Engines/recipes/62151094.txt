Title:
Sweet Italian Chicken Parmesian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts.
1 bottle italian dressing.
1 cup parmesian cheese.
2 Tbs. honey
8 oz. mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Marinate chicken breasts in half of the italian dressing,overnight if possible.
Drizzle oil in bottom of pan.
Place chicken in pan. Do not use drippings from marinate overnight.
Use rest of the italian dressing and pour over pan.
Cover all chicken with parmesian cheese.
Bake for 20 minutes.
Take out chicken and coat lightly with honey and mozzarella cheese.
Bake for 5 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Few ingredients but big on taste. Sweet, succulent variation on Chicken Parmesian.
