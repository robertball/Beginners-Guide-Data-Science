Title:
cantaloupe salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cantaloupe, cubed
2 tsp. lime zest
3 Tbls. lime juice
1 tsp. ground ginger
2 Tbls. sugar
2 Tbls. honey
3 Tbls. mint leaves, chopped
1/2 tsp. ground pepper
1 bag of fresh romaine, or salad blend

Directions:
Place cubed cantaloupe in large bowl.  Add all other ingredients, except salad blend.  Mix thoroughly with tongs or spoon.  Add salad and continue to toss, until all flavors are blended.  Refrigerate before serving.
