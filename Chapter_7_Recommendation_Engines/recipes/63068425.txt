Title:
Hard Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123127.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1/2 c. light Karo
1/2 c. water
dash salt
candy oil
food coloring
powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients.
Boil to hard crack stage(using candy thermometer).
Add oil and colorings.
Pour onto flat buttered pan.
Cool and break up.
Roll in powdered sugar.
