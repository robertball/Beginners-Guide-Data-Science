Title:
C'S Burbon Pumpkin Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/781066.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
2 large eggs
1 can (15 ounces) LIBBY'S® 100% Pure Pumpkin
1 can (12 fluid ounces) NESTLÉ® CARNATION® Evaporated Milk
2 tablespoons bourbon

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 425° F.
MIX sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger and cloves in small bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture. Gradually stir in evaporated milk and bourbon.
POUR into ramikins.
BAKE for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350° F; bake for an additional 40 to 50 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Serve immediately with Bourbon Cool Whip or refrigerate.
FOR BOURBON COOL WHIP:
WHIP 1 cup Fat Free Cool Whipo in large mixer bowl with 2 tablespoons light brown sugar, 2 teaspoons bourbon and 1 teaspoon ground ginger.

Submitter: 
Carolyn Vaught
Description: 
I just love this!  It's just the Libby's recipe poured into ramikins and topped with fat free burbon Cool Whip.  Less calories and delicious!
