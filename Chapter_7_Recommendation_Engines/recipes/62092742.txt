Title:
Creamy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken breast (seasoned to taste)
4 cups torn fresh spinach
2 green onions, sliced thin
1 8 oz carton nonfat sour cream
1/4 cup plain nonfat yogurt
2 tbsp whole grain flour
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup 1% milk
1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
6 whole wheat tortillas (6-8" diameter)
1/2 cup low fat shredded cheddar cheese
salsa

Directions:
In medium sized sauce pan, cook spinach with small amount of water and dash of salt for about five minutes.
In large bowl, combine chicken, spinach and green onions.  Mix well and set aside.
In medium sized bowl, combine sour cream, yogurt, flour, cumin and salt.  Add milk and jalapeno.  Mix well.
Pour 1/2 the sauce into chicken mixture.  Divide filling among tortillas.  Place seam side down in large baking dishes.  Spoon remaining sauce over tortillas and bake for 20 minutes.
Sprinkle with cheese and cook until melted, about five minutes.  Top with salsa and serve.

Delicious, low-fat version of a classic.  You and your family will love this recipe!
