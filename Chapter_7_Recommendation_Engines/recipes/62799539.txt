Title:
Sage Brown Butter Sauce with Cranberries & Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (2 sticks)
1/4 cup chopped fresh sage leaves or 1 tsp dried sage
1 tsp pumpkin spice (cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, allspice)
2/3 cup toasted walnuts, chopped
2/3 cup dried cranberries
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground white pepper
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat. Add the sage, pumpkin spice, walnuts and cranberries. Cook until the butter starts to brown, about 3 minutes.
Turn the heat off, stir in sugar until melted and add salt and pepper. Spoon sauce on pasta. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over pasta
