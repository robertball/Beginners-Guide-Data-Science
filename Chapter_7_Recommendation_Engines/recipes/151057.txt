Title:
Honduran Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986047.jpg
Ingredients:
Turkey Mixture:
1 pound smoked turkey, cubed
1/2 cup red wine
1/4 cup Spice Islands® Cilantro
1 tablespoon Spice Islands® Ground Coriander
1 tablespoon Spice Islands® Cumin
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® Medium Grind Black Pepper
1 medium lime
Chili Mixture:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 large onion, diced
1 medium green bell pepper, diced
1 jalapeno pepper, minced
1 tablespoon minced garlic
2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, undrained
1 (15.5 ounce) can light kidney beans, undrained

Directions:
Combine turkey, wine, cilantro, coriander, cumin and black pepper in a medium sized bowl. Squeeze lime and add juice to turkey mixture. Marinate 10 minutes.
Heat olive oil in a large pot on medium heat. Add onion, peppers and garlic. Saute until vegetables are softened. Stir in beans. Cook until chili begins to simmer. Add turkey mixture, and simmer 1 to 2 hours stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
This turkey and black bean chili is a terrific lunch or dinner option, especially during the colder months.
