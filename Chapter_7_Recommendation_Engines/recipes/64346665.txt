Title:
Brownie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups oatmeal
1 ½ cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Spray 13 x 9 pan with Pam Cooking Spray.
Mix together:
1 ½ cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup granulated sugar
Add to dry ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
Mix thoroughly.
Stir in:
2 cups oatmeal
1 ½ cups chocolate chips
Once ingredients are mixed together, spread mixture into prepared 13 x 9 pan.  Bake for 25 minutes.*

Submitter: 
Michelle Metzger
Description: 
A tasty dessert that is a cross between a cookie and a brownie.
