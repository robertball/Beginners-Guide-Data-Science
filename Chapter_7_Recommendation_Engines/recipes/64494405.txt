Title:
Creamy Stove-Top Alfredo with Bacon and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices thick-slice bacon, coarsely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
8 oz uncooked rigatoni, penne, or rotini pasta
1 14.5 oz can chicken broth
1 c water
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 c frozen peas
1/4 c to 1/3 c whipping cream
1/4 c grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In extra-large skillet, cook bacon until crisp.
Add garlic and cook 30 seconds more.
Drain off fat.
Stir in pasta, broth, water, salt, and pepper.
Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 12-15 minutes or until pasta is tender.
Stir in peas, whipping cream,and cheese. Cook and stir about one minute more.
Serve immediately. If desired, sprinkle with crumbled bacon.
