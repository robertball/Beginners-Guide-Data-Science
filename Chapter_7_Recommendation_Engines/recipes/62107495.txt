Title:
Pina Colada Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box yellow cake mix
1 Can of cream of coconut
1 can sweet eagle brand milk
1 can of crush pineapple -drained
1 cool whip.

Directions:
Mix cake to box directions cook and when done poke holes in cake all
over
then pour sweet eagle brand milk all over
then pour cream of coconut all over
then  pour 1 can of drain pineapples all over
*when its COOL* THEN top off with cool whip.
