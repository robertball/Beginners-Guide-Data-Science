Title:
warm cheesy spinach dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz) pkg frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 cup mayo
1/3 cup romano cheese, grated
1/2 cup parmesean, grated
1 cup fresh shreaded mozzarella
2 Tablespoons french onion soup mix

Directions:
pre-heat oven to 350
in a large bowl, mix together the ricotta and mayo.
Add in the oniom soup mix until well blended.
Now, add the parmesean and the romano.
Finally, add the spinach and the mozzarella until well blended together.
Spoon mixture into a glass dish, preferably a round pie dish, but anything will work.
Bake intil the top is lighty brown and bubbly.
Serve while warm with bread slices or crackers.

Submitter: 
Melissa Younger Lucchi
Description: 
This was a dip that i made with a mix of two recipes in one; cold spinach dip and hot artichoke dip. Becuase it was an experiment, the measurements might need tweaking.
