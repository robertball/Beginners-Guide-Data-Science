Title:
Mince Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Jar Crosse & Blackwwod Mince (I used two small jars for a 9 inch deep dish pie)
1 sm Can crushed Pineapple (drained)
1 dozen marschino Cherries (Quartered)
1/2 cup Broken Pecans
1/2 cup Brown Sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
2 Tbsp Honey
1 stick butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except butter) together
Pour into uncooked pie crust.
Dot with slices of butter
Cover with top crust (be sure to vent)
Bake at 425 for 15 min then turn down to 350 for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
This pie has won awards at various fairs and church events. It was Gramma Lois's favorite holiday pie.
