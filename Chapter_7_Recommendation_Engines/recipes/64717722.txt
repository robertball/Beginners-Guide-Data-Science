Title:
Big Ray's Zucchini Onion Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5806956.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large eggs
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup canola oil
1 Tbsp minced fresh parsley
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
3 cups sliced zucchini
1 cup biscuit/baking mix
1 small onion, chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk the first seven ingredients. Stir in the zucchini, baking mix and onion. Pour into a greased 11" x 7" baking dish. Bake at 350Â° for 25-35 minutes or until lightly browned.
