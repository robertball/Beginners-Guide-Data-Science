Title:
Crock pot pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork chops
1 box Stove Top stuffing
1 egg
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 tbsp olive oil
1 cup water

Directions:
Beat egg.
Put stuffing in separate bowl.
Dip pork chops in egg then into stuffing.
Cook chops over medium heat until brown.
Place in bottom of crock pot sprayed with PAM.
Mix celery and onion into remaining stuffing and sprinkle mixture over pork chops.
Pour 1 cup water over stuffing.
Cook on high for 3 hours.
