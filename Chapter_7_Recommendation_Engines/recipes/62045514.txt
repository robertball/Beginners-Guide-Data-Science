Title:
Marinated Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. brussels sprouts
1 large bottle Zesty Italian Salad Dressing
Several dashes hot sauce, if desired

Directions:
Wash and trim the brussels sprouts.
Place in a pot of boiling, salted water and cook until tender, approximately 15 minutes.
Drain and place the sprouts in a bowl.
Pour the Zesty Italian Dressing (adding hot sauce, if desired) over the top of the sprouts making sure they are covered with dressing.
Marinate at least 4 hours, in the refrigerator, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Diana S.
Description: 
Very simple but delicious way to serve brussels sprouts. If you have time you may want to make your own  Italian dressing, if not the bottled kind works just fine. We like them as a side dish or even mixed into a tossed salad.
