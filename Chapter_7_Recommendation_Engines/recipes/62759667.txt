Title:
Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups bread flour
1 cup reg. flour
2 1/4 tsp yeast or one packet REGULAR yeast.
1 cup + warm water
1 tsp salt
2 tsp olive oil
1 tbsp sugar
I grind Italian seasoning into the dough and
you can also add shredded Parmesan Cheese to the dough too.

Directions:
Knead dough, let rise, punch down, let rise again, punch down and roll out dough to fit pan.
Add pizza stuff
Hot Oven at 450
I have a pizza stone on the bottom of the oven.  I cook the pizza on the lowest rack.
