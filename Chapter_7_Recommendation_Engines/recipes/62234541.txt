Title:
Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup potato, cubed
1/4 cup butter
1 T green onion, diced
2 T flour
1 can clams (6.5 oz)
1/2 t salt
8 oz half & half
1/4 cup milk
2 T plus 2 t clam juice

Directions:
Place potatoes in saucepan and cover with water, cook on medium heat until tender.
Remove potatoes from heat, drain and set aside.
In 1 quart saucepan on medium low heat melt butter, add diced onions, cook until onions are slightly clear in color.
Add flour to butter and stir briskly. Mixture will start to thicken.
Add half and half a little at a time while stirring briskly. Mixture will be creamy and will look like thick cream.
Place salt, clams and juice, potatoes, bottled clam juice and milk with mixture.
On medium low temperature bring chowder to a slow boil. Turn temperature down to low and let chowder simmer gently for 15 minutes, stirring frequently.
