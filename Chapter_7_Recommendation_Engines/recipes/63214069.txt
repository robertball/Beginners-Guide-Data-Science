Title:
Classic Huevos Rancheros Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tb. olive oil
1 15 oz. can tomato sauce
1 10 oz. can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 tsp. fresh oregano, chopped
1/2 tsp. fresh basil, chopped
8 eggs
Corn tortilla chips, toasted in oven
1 large Haas avocado, halved, seeded, peeled and cubed

Directions:
In a large skillet cook onion and garlic in oil until tender but not brown. Stir in tomato sauce, can tomatoes, basil and oregano. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer for 4 minutes.
Carefully break 1 egg into a small dish. Gently slide the egg into the sauce int he skillet. Repeat with remaining eggs, allowing each egg an equal amount of space.
Cover skillet and simmer gently for 7-8 minutes or until yolks are just set. Serve eggs and sauce with tortilla wedges and cubed avocado.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Miskiewicz
Description: 
This Mexican classic is quick, cheap and easy! The leftovers heat up great in the microwave for a great, healthy protein-packed breakfast.
