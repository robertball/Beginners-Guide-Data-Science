Title:
Cherry Fluff n' Stuff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup miniature marshmellows
1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the cherry pie filling, sweetened condensed milk, drained crushed pineapple, marshmellows, pecans, and whipped topping. Chill in the refrigerator at least 2 hours before serving.

Description:
