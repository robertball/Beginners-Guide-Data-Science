Title:
Slow Cooker Creamy Chicken Italian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 envelope dry Italian dressing mix
1/4 cup water
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
10 3/4 oz. cream of chicken soup
4 oz can of mushrooms

Directions:
Place chicken in greased slow cooker.
Combine salad-dressing mix and water in bowl.  Pour over chicken.
Cover.  Cook on low 4-5 hours.
In saucepan, combine cream cheese and soup.  Heat slightly to melt cream cheese.  Stir in mushrooms.  Pour over chicken.
Cover.  Cook an additional 1 hour on low.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Serve over noodles or rice.   Add frozen vegetables in step 4 with mushrooms if desired.
