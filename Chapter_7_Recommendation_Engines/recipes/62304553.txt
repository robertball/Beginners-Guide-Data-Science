Title:
chicken and chorizo tostada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385104.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken cutlets
salt
pepper
olive oil
4 small flour tortillas
4 ounces thinly sliced spanish chorizo (cured sausage)
2 cups grated cheddar
sour cream
cilantro leaves

Directions:
season 4 chicken cutlets with salt and pepper. cook in olive oil over medium-high heat until golden; slice. broil 4 small flour tortillas until crisp; top with the chicken, 4 ounces thinly sliced Spanish chorizo, and 2 cups grated cheddar. broil until cheese melts. serve with sour creame and cilantro leaves.
