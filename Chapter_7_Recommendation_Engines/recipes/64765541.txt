Title:
Sproutin'  Shrimp n Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-12 large prawn or shrimp peeled and deveined
6-8 Brussels Sprouts trimmed and quartered lengthwise
2 C Stock ( see notes for shrimp stock)
1.5 cups 1/2 &1/2
4T Butter
3/4 c grits
1/2 c shredded cheese
1 shallot (1/4c) minced
2-3 cloves garlic minced
3 T lemon juice
1 t lemon zest
1/4 t pepper flakes
1/4 c chopped parsley
1 t Old Bay seasoning
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
toss shrimp and sprouts together with 2T olive oil, lemon zest, 1 T parsley, pepper flakes and Old Bay seasoning
Bring stock, 1 cup  1/2 &1/2 and 2 T butter to a boil,  add grits gently, stir, reduce heat and cover. Stir occasionally to keep grits loose ( 15-30 minutes depending on cornmeal grain cut) when soft fold in cheese and remove from heat
In a large saute pan heat 2T olive oil, add shallots and garlic and saute till softened( 6-8 minutes)
remove solids from pan reserving as much oil as possible.
bring the heat up high and add remaining butter. toss in shrimp and sprouts and sear undisturbed for 2 minutes. stir or toss and add lemon juice and cook for 2 more minutes.
Reduce heat, add shallot/garlic saute mixture and remaining 1/2&1/2 and simmer for 2-3 minutes uncovered. Add parsley and remove from heat.
Plate with grits and shrimp and sprouts on top. Garnish with cheese and parsley
*note    Shrimp Stock
2.5 C water
shrimp skins
1 bay leaf
1 t salt
1t pepper
scraps of onion, celery and garlic
simmer for 30 minutes and strain off reserving stock
