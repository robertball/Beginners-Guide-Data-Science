Title:
Creamy Tomato Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb of pasta
1 small yellow onion, chopped
2 large tomatoes, diced
1 Large tomato, crushed
2 cups of fresh basil
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup pine nuts or crushed walnuts
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
4 cloves of garlic
1/2-3/4 cup of dry white wine (preferably Riesling)
Salt and Pepper
Pinch of Allspice
1/4 cup heavy creme
1/4 cup sour cream
2 Tbs olive oil

Directions:
Place basil, olive oil, pine nuts, garlic, and parmesan cheese to food processer or blender and pulse until smooth and combined. Set aside
In large skillet heat 2 Tbs olive oil. Ad onion and cook over medium heat until soft and golden, stirring ocassionaly.
Add the diced tomato, crushed tomato, and wine. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer and cook until the sauce is reduced.
Pour the pesto into the skillet and stir until combined. Add the heavy creme and sour cream and simmer until reduced. Add allspice, and salt and pepper to taste.
Pour over pasta, sprinkle with a little more parmesan, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Liv
Description: 
This delicious spin on your average pesto goes great with the noodle of your choice or in your favorite lasagna! Use fresh ingredients, it's always better!
