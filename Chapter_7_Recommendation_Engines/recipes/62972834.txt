Title:
UNCLE RICK'S DEER CAMP CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 LBS BEEF OR VENISON, 2-29OZ CANS OF TOMATO SAUCE, 1-12OZ CAN TOMATO PASTE, 2-15OZ CANS STEWED TOMATOES, 2 SMALL CANS OF CHOPPED CHILES, 3-4 TBS SUGAR, 1/2 CUP CHILI POWDER, 1 TBS BUTTER, 3 LARGE ONIONS CHOPPED, 3-4 BELL PEPPERS CHOPPED, 3 STALKS CELERY CHOPPED, 1 CLOVE GARLIC, 2TBL TABASCO SAUCE, 1TBS OREGANO, 1 tsp BASIL, 1 tsp SALT, 1/2 TBL COURSE BLACK PEPPER.

Directions:
COOK MEAT WITH 1 TBL TABASCO SAUCE, DRAIN OFF FAT. IN LARGE PAN ADD BUTTER, ONIONS, CELERY, BELL PEPPERS, GARLIC, AND ALL SPICES EXCEPT SUGAR AND CHILI POWDER. COOK VEGIES UNTIL TENDER. ADD MEAT AND ALL TOMATO PRODUCTS, CHILIES, SUGAR, AND CHILI POWDER, COOK 1 HOUR AND SERVE.

Submitter: 
Rick Falsetta
Description: 
A GREAT TASTING CHILI THAT GOES WITH ANYTHING FROM GRILLED CHEESE TO PASTA TO PUT OVER YOUR POTATOES AND EGGS.
