Title:
Crack Slaw or Egg Roll in a Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb  ground pork (or chicken or beef)
2-3 c red or yellow cabbage
1/4 c plus more coconut aminos
5-6 garlic cloves
1 tsp ground ginger
2 T rice wine vinegar
1-2 T sesame oil
1/2 c green onion
red pepper flakes
salt

Directions:
saute pork over medium-high heat until almost cooked
add garlic and ginger
add coconut aminos, rice wine vinegar
add cabbage
saute until cabbage starts to soften
add green onion
add sesame oil
serve with lime, avocado, sesame oil, red pepper flakes
