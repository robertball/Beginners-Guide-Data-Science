Title:
Bush's BBQ Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
3 TBL BBQ sauce
3/4-1 cup shredded colby-jack or cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded chicken
1 (9oz)thin & crispy ready-made pizza crust
1 can bush's southern pit bbq grillin' beans

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Lightly brush crust with olive oil and spread with bbq sauce.
2. In medium bowl, mix chicken and beans, placing mixture on pizza round and topping with cheese.
3. Bake on cookie sheet (middle position) for 8-10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
