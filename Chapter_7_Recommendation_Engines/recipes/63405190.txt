Ingredients:
8 oz uncooked spaghetti
Thai Salad Dressing:
1/2 C Rice Vinegar
1/3 C peanut oil or canola oil
2 T lime or lemon juice
1 T Sugar
1-1/2 tsp minced fresh gingeroot
1-1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce
1-1/2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp salt
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
Salad:
1 package (5oz) spring mix salad greens
1 small sweet red pepper, thinly sliced
4 green onions, chopped
1 lb. deli roat beef, cut into strips
2 T chopped salted peanuts (Optional)

Directions:
-Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a call bowl whisk te dressing ingredients.
-Drain spaghett and rinse in cold water; toss with 1/2 C dressing.
Plae in a large serving dish.
