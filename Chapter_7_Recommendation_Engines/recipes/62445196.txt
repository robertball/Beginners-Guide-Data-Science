Title:
The LTH Anti-EggNog Holiday Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 C water
2 C sugar
4 tea bags
2 C water
1 large can unsweetened pineapple juice
1 12 oz can frozen lemonade
2 C whiskey
Ginger Ale

Directions:
Combine water and sugar in large saucepan.
Bring to a boil, remove from heat, and allow to cool.
While the sugar water is boiling, boil 2 C of water w/4 tea bags.  Cool.
Combine sugar water, tea, pineapple juice, frozen lemonade, and whiskey in large freezer safe container.
Place in freezer.  Stir occasionally during the freezing process.
Scoop/scrape into serving glasses of your choice.
Pour ginger ale into glasses to create slush.
Serve with a straw and a spoon.
