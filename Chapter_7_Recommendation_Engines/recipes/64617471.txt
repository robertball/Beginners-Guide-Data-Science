Title:
Easy Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup yellow cornmeal
2/3 cup white sugar
3 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/3 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
In a medium mixing bowl combine all dry ingredients (flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt and baking powder)
Stir in egg, milk and vegetable oil until well combined
Pour batter into lightly 9 x 7.5" greased pan
Bake for 20-25 minutes until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean

Submitter: 
Janessa Pendleton Rivera
Description: 
I never buy the boxed corn bread mixes because this is so easy and quick. I usually have all the ingredients on hand.
