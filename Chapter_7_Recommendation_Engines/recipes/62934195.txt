Title:
Balken Brei III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. Cracklings
4 Tbs. Fat
2 C. Water
2-1/2 C. Flour
1 tsp. Ground Cloves
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Ginger
1/4 tsp. Pepper
3/4 tsp. Salt

Directions:
Bring cracklings, fat, and water to a boil.
Mix remaining ingredients pour slowly into boiling mixture, stirring constantly.
When well mixed, put in 5-1/2" x 10" pan.
When cold, cut into thin slices and fry until brown on both sides in skillet.
Eat Warm with syrup.
