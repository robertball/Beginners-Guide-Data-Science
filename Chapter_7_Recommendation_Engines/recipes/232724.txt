Title:
Fresh and Fruity Cranberry Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cranberries
2 oranges, peeled and segmented
2 large apples, peeled and cored
2 1/2 cups white sugar
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple
1/2 cup walnuts

Directions:
Coarsely grind cranberries, oranges, and apples in a food mill or grinder; put in a bowl. Stir sugar, pineapple, and walnuts through the cranberry mixture.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.
