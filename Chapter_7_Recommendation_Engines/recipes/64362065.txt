Title:
Feta Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced cooked chicken
1/2 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
1/4 red onion, diced
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
2 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
2 teaspoons dried dill weed
1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Mix all and pour over chicken, and stir to blend. Taste, and season with salt and pepper as needed. Serve immediately, or refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Ewolfe
Description: 
A basic chicken salad recipe with red bell peppers and feta cheese. Try using tomato-basil feta for added flavor!Modified from: Feta Chicken Salad
