Title:
Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes with onion & peppers
16 ounces cooked, diced ham
1 can cream of potato soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
2 cups shredded  Cheddar cheese
combine for topping
1  cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup buttered bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a large bowl, mix hash browns, ham, soups, sour cream, and Cheddar cheese. Spread evenly into prepared dish. combine & Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese & bread crumbs.
Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until bubbly and lightly brown. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
I mostly use this as a breakfast casserole, but it's great anytime. May be served with or without diced ham. Quick and easy to make, not to mention delicious!Modified from: Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole
