Title:
Kartoffel kroketten /  Potato croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Baking Potato
3 egg yolks
1 tsp. starch
3 oz flour.
2 oz butter
ground white pepper, Salt, Nutmeg
2 egg-yolk
1/2 cup of water
1 cup flour
1 cup of Bread crumbs

Directions:
Boil, peel and mash the potatoes.  while still hot mix with the 2 scrambled egg yolks, Butter, and spices.   Next while still hot; fill a piping bag fitted with a smooth Large Fitting and onto a Parchment paper on a Pan;   pipe,  consecutive lines of the Potato.  let completely cool and carefully cut 1 1/2 inch Logs.  Next;   Flour them;   dip them in the mix of egg yolk and water and then in Bread crumbs.  coat the ends.   In a med heated fryer. Deep fat fry until golden.
Keep warm until ready to serve:
Serve with:  Grilled Meats, and with traditional Main dishes; Roasts, Etc.

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
Mashed potatoes with egg yolk, butter, Pepper, salt, and Nutmeg.
