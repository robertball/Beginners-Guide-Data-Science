Title:
Amazing Simple Thai Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) package firm tofu, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
I small red bell pepper chopped
1 c. Chopped broccoli , optional
1 tsp. red pepper flakes or to taste
1/3 cup chopped green onion
1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
1 T soy sauce
2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger root
1/4 cup chunky peanut butter
Coconut milk, about 1/2 cup
sesame seeds

Directions:
Combine pb and coconut milk till saucy.  Set aside. Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium,  add bell paper and broccoli; cook for a minute, then add green onions and pepper flakes and cook  for one minute. Add tofu, and continue cooking 4 minutes more, sprinkling with soy sauce halfway through. Gently stir in the peanut butter mixture and ginger, being careful not to break the tofu, until well incorporated.
Remove from heat.. Transfer to a serving dish, and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
Vegan. you'd never guess it's Tofu!  Peanut and ginger flavors combine to create a wonderful Asian-flavored dish that everyone will love. Serve over white rice.Modified from: Amazing Simple Thai Tofu
