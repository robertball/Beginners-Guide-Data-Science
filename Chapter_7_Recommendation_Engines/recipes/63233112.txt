Title:
Maple Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup cornmeal
1 1/3 cup flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup maple syrup
8 tablespoons margarine melted and cooled
2 eggs beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Mix first four ingredients together
Add the next three ingredients to the mix
Stir so that it is well mixed- but do not beat
Pour into 9 inch pan or into muffin tins and bake until lightly brown on top

Submitter: 
kmy
Description: 
This is a recipe from my mom, it is a great side dish. It is a delicious cornbread with a twist of maple!
