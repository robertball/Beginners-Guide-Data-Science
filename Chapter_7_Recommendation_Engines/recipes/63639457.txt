Title:
Wild Rice & Orzo Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup uncooked Wild Rice
1 Cup uncooked Orzo (rice shaped pasta)
3 Tbsp Butter
2 Cups chopped Onions
1 1/2 Cups thawed Frozen Peas
1/2 tsp each of Salt & Pepper
1/3 Cup sliced Scallions/Green Onions

Directions:
Cook wild rice & orzo separately according to package directions.
Meanwhile, melt 1 Tbsp of the butter in a large nonstick skillet. Add onions: cover and cook over medium heat 15 Stir in remaining 2 Tbsp of butter, peas, Salt and pepper; cook 1 min.
Drain rice and orzo; pour into serving dish. Stir in onion mixture and scallions (aka green onions).

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
A friend of mine served this side dish for a group dinner. We all loved & devoured it. It's so very easy and versatile that you can personalize it to suit your own taste.
