Title:
Cheeseburger Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups bow tie pasta, cooked and drained
1 tbsp cooking oil
1 lb. lean ground beef
1/4 yellow onion, chopped
1/4 teaspoon garlic
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon yellow mustard
8 slices cooked bacon, chopped
1 can evaporated milk (5 oz)
1 and 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cook bow tie pasta according to box directions.  Heat oil in large nonstick skillet.  Cook onions until transparent.  Add ground beef and cook until brown.  Sprinkle with garlic powder.  Add remaining ingredients and stir to combine.  Pour into rectangular 7x11 baking dish and bake for 15 minutes until bubbly.
