Title:
Hell's Kitchen's Wild Rice Porridge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked wild rice
1/2 cup roasted, cracked hazelnuts
1/2 cup dried blueberries
1/4 cup Craisens
1/4 cup pure maple syrup
1 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In heavy saucepan, add all ingredients except cream and cook over medium-high heat for about 3 minutes. Add cream and heat through, stirring constantly. Serve immediately.
