Title:
Elsa's Sunflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. pkg coleslaw
2 pkg BEEF flavored ramen noodles (uncooked)
1 c. salted sunflower seeds
1 bunch green onions
1 c. slivered almonds (lightly toasted)
Dressing:
2/3 c. oil
1/2 c. sugar
2 seasoning packets from the ramen noodle bags
1/3 c. tarragon vinegar

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together, crushing the noodles while still in the package.
Right before serving put all dressing ingredients in a jar and shake to mix.  Pour over dry ingredients and stir.
