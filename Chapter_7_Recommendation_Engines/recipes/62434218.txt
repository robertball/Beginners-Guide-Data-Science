Title:
Low-Fat Poppy Seed Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/453176.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1/2 cup fat free milk (skim)
•	1 cup poppy seeds(you can use black or white ones. I used white in the pic above because the grocer didn't have the black ones)
•	1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
•	1 teaspoon baking soda
•	1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
•	1/2 cup vegetable oil
•	1 lemon, juiced
•	2/3 cup maple syrup
•	1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Over medium heat combine cream and poppy seeds. Once warm set aside.
3. In a medium bowl combine flour, baking soda and cinnamon. Stir in oil, lemon juice and syrup. Mix well and then add the milk and poppy seed mixture.
4. Grease pan.
5. Drop by spoonfuls onto a cookie sheet.
6. Bake for 18 minutes in the preheated oven.
7. Allow cookies to cool completely and then serve.

Submitter: 
luckycharm416
Description: 
These cookies are healthy!! Poppy seeds and maple syrup both contribute to a healthy heart. I had some Hashasli (poppy seed cookies) at a Turkish restaurant, so I decided to make my healthy version of it. Enjoy!
