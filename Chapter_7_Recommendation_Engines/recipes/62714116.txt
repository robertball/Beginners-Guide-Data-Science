Title:
Mexican (salsa) Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can sliced black olives
1 small jar green olives, sliced
1 medium onion chopped
1/4 to 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
2 tablespoons canola oil
2 32 oz. jars Pace Picante Sauce,medium heat

Directions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is a great dip to make for a large crowd.  Serve with scoop chips to really get in there and dip.
