Title:
Thai Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken legs, thawed, skin on
marinade:
1/2 c honey mustard
1/2 c sweet chili sauce
2 eggs, beaten
4 c panko bread crumbs (for breading)
vegetable oil for frying
Kosher salt for seasoning

Directions:
Place legs into large ziplock baggie
Cover with marinade
Let rest in fridge for at least 4 hrs
Heat oil to 350 degrees F.
Heat oven to 324 degrees F.
Remove legs from the baggie and dredge completely in panko
Gently place into fully heated oil until golden brown, turning half-way through if necessary
Place on cooling rack set above a jelly roll pan
Repeat all steps until chicken is all fried
Place chicken into oven
Cover with aluminum foil
Bake for 30 minutes or until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
MERRIPAN
Description: 
Sweet chili sauce and honey mustard marinade creates a great twist on an old favorite.  The crunch of panko bread crumbs makes a great combo.
