Title:
Pressed Pepper & Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup grated monterey jack cheese
1 cup red, green and yellow pepper (minced fine)
1 egg
salt and pepper (season to taste)
paprika
1 contianer button mushrooms

Directions:
Combine cheese, red-green-yellow peppers, egg, and salt/pepper. Mix together until smooth. Set aside until ready to use.
Take each mushroom and wash thouroughly. After drying, pull stem off and rinse the inside of mushroom.
Insert the cheese mixture into your Cookie Press. Use the star tip to fill each mushroom. Sprinkle the top with paprike (if desired). Place mushrooms on a cookie sheet and bake at 350* F for 7 minutes or until cheese is melted and the filling is hot.
