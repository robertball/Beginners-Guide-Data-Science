Title:
Instant Diet Russian Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/709107.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups orange-flavored drink mix (e.g. Tang)
2 cups granulated splenda
1/4 cup instant tea powder
3/4 cup lemon instant tea powder (unsweetened)
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine orange drink mix, splenda, tea powder, cinnamon and cloves. Mix well and store in an airtight container.
To serve, put 3 teaspoons of mix in a mug. Stir in 1 cup boiling water. Adjust to taste.
