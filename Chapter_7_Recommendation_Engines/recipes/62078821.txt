Title:
Traditional Indiana Persimmon Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint persimmon pulp
1 C sugar
1 Tbsp. butter
4 C flour
2 teas. baking soda
2 eggs
6 C milk

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients. Beat eggs and pulp with wire whisk. Add milk alternately with dry ingredients to the pulp mixture. Pour into large buttered (oven-safe)crock. Drop butter on top. Bake at 350 for 2 hours, stirring every 15 min. Pudding will be dark brown when done.

Submitter: 
KerryT
Description: 
This recipe has been handed down in our family since 1850. Its a Thanksgiving favorite, hot or cold!
