Title:
Melt-in-your-Mouth Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/594011.jpg
Ingredients:
1-3/4 c. Flour
3/4 c. Rolled or Quick Oats
2 t. Powdered Milk
1 t. Baking Powder
1/2 t. Baking Soda
1/2 t. Salt
Heaping 1/4 t. Nutmeg
1 Egg
1/2 c. Orange Juice Concentrate, thawed
1/4 c. Water or Milk
2/3 c. Vegetable Oil
1/4 t. Vanilla
1 c. Frozen Blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease a 12-muffin tin.
In a large bowl, mix together the dry ingredients.
In a separate bowl, beat the egg, then add and mix the following four ingredients.
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix gently just until combined.
Fold in the blueberries.
Divide the batter equally into the twelve muffin cups.
Bake for 17 minutes or until golden and cooked through.  Let cool in the tin for a minute before removing.

Submitter: 
MessiaenTheKitchen
Description: 
We experimented with making a sugarless, naturally sweetened muffin and found the perfect comfort food! Sweetened with orange juice concentrate and blueberries, it's a delightful blend of fruitiness.
