Title:
Kaeng Khua Curry Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 dried chilies, soaked in hot water for 15 min.
3 T chopped shallots
2 T garlic
1 t chopped galangal
1 T chopped lemon grass
1 t chopped kaffir lime rind
1 t chopped coriander root
2 t salt
1 t shrimp paste

Directions:
In a blender put all ingredients except shrimp paste, blend to mix well. Add shrimp paste and blend until a fine textured paste.
Store in a glass in the fridge for 4 months.
