Title:
Peach Dessert/Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Dessert/Tart Shells
1/2 Cup Sugar
1 Pinch of Salt
1 1/4 Tablespoon Cornstarch
3/4 Cup Lemonade
1 1/2 Peaches

Directions:
Peel and cut all of the peaches.
Stir together sugar, cornstarch, and salt. In saucepan ad lemonade and cook on medium heat. Stirring constantly until thick and smooth. Let cool 10 minutes.
Put a small amount of lemon glaze in the bottom of all of the dessert/tart shells. Then put an even amount of peaches into the shells. Evenly distribute the rest of the glaze on top of the peaches.
Chill 2-4 hours. Garnish if desired.
