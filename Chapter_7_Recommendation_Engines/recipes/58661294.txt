Title:
Mom's Best Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup quick-cooking oats
1/8 cup cornmeal
1 cup dried bread crumbs
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons white sugar
1 egg, beaten
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
1 tablespoon ketchup
1 dash liquid smoke flavoring (2 drops)
1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
1/3 cup ketchup
2 teaspoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
1 drop liquid smoke flavoring

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion, oatmeal, cornmeal, bread crumbs, salt, sugar, egg, water, barbecue sauce, 1/8 teaspoon liquid smoke and vinegar. Mix together thoroughly and place mixture into a lightly greased 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a separate small bowl, combine the ketchup, brown sugar, mustard and 1 drop liquid smoke and mix thoroughly. Spread over the top of the meatloaf.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for approximately one hour.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Mom made this every Sunday and we looked forward to cold sandwiches the next day, if there was any left!! Homemade breadcrumbs work best.Modified from: Mom's Best Meatloaf
