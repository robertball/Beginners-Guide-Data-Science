Title:
Deep Fried Crab Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Bisquick
1/3 Cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 Cup Finely chopped green onion
1 Cup (8oz) Cooked Crab
1 Egg
1/3 Cup water
1 Tsp Worchestershire Sauce
1/4 Tsp Tobasco
Salad Oil
1/2 Cup Sour Cream
2 tbsp Dijon Mustard
1 Tsp Lemon Juice

Directions:
Stir together Bisquick, cheese & onion.
Shred Crab and add
Beat together Egg, Water, Whorchestershire, & Tobasco
Add to mix and stir just till blended
Can be frozen
Drop by spoonfuls into hot oil
Serve with mustard Dip
Mix toether, sour cream, Dijon Mustard and Lemon Juice
