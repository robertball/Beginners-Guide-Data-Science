Title:
Versatile Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup white sugar
5 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup butter
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup orange juice, approx.
1/4 cup milk
Orange zest or orange extract, if desired
Dried cranberries, if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in butter. Mix the egg in a measuring cup; add orange juice and milk to equal one cup of liquid total, and stir into flour mixture until moistened, along with orange zest or extract (if you want a stronger orange flavor).
Add dried cranberries, if desired.  Drop onto greased cookie sheet and bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Wendy Darling
Description: 
A simple scone recipe that can be customized in a myriad of ways.  Add whatever liquids you prefer to egg to equal one cup liquid.  Can use pumpkin puree and cinnamon instead of OJ and milk, etc., etc.Modified from: Scones
