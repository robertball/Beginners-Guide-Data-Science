Title:
Lazy Sunday Afternoon Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup whole wheat flour
1 ½ cup of crushed bran cereal
2 tbls. wheat germ
1 cup fat free milk
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
½ cup blueberries
½ cup raisins
½ cup chopped walnuts
½ cup applesauce
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. In a small bowl combine milk and crushed bran cereal, set aside and let it sit for 10 minutes.
In a large bowl, combine whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, wheat germ, and cinnamon. Mix together. Set aside.
In another large bowl, mix together applesauce, egg, and brown sugar. Mix in flour mixture. Then combine bran mixture as well.
Fold in blueberries, raisins, and chopped walnuts.
Bake in oven for 20 minutes or until muffins appear golden brown.

Submitter: 
Kate Hanson
Description: 
One Sunday afternoon I was really not sure what to do with my time...so I decided 'why not make up a muffin recipe!' These super moist muffins have been getting raved about!
