Title:
Sweet and Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 cup pineapple juice (from can of pineapple chunks)
1/2 cup water
1 Large green pepper
1- 13 oz. can of pineapple chunks
2 tablespoons cornstarch
2 tablespoons vinegar

Directions:
In saucepan combine sugar, cornstarch, soy sauce, vinegar, water, and pineapple juice. Stir constantly, cook until thickens and boils.  Add green pepper, pineapple, and meatballs to crock-pot.  Cook on high until hot (about 2 hours) Turn to low while serving.
Meatballs can be made from scratch or buy precooked meatballs in your freezer section.
