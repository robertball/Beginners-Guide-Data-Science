Title:
Roll-ups Roast Beef-Pastrami-Corned Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* flour tortillas
* roast beef
* pastrami
* corned beef
* cut up lettuce
* cut up tomatoes
* ranch dressing

Directions:
On the flour tortillas, place meat, cut up lettuce, tomato and ranch dressing. Roll up and cut into 2 inch pieces, then plate.
