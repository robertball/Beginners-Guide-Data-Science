Title:
Best Milkshake Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5238587.jpg
Ingredients:
3 scoops your fav. ice cream flavor
1/2 cup milk(any kind will work)
Whipped Cream(optional)

Directions:
Put half the ice cream in a blender with the milk and blend for 15 seconds. Add the rest of the ice cream, and use the tuck'n'fold stir with the ice cream to ensure thorough blending. Pulse until blended to your fav. consistency.
Pour in a serving glass and enjoy. If wanted, pipe or spoon on whipped cream.
Enjoy!
