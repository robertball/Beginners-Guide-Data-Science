Title:
Felicity's Faux Fried Fowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole boneless chicken breasts
1/4 yellow mustard
2 tbsp parmesean cheese
1 tbsp garlic italian dressing
1 tbsp chipotle marinade
1 1/2 cups crushed cornflakes

Directions:
Mix parmesean cheese, mustard, and sauces together.
Dip chicken in sauce and coat in crushed cornflakes.
Bake in preheated 450 degrees until chicken is done.

Submitter: 
Felicity
Description: 
This chicken is easy and delicious, better than fried chicken in my opinion. Used Dianna's Marinades, fantastic and flavorful!
