Title:
Easy Thumbprint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 cup margarine or butter, softened (not melted)
1/2 tsp. salt
1 TBSP. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 cup finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and chill dough for about 1-2 hours.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease baking sheet.
Form into small balls and press hole in center using little finger or handle of a wooden spoon.
Bake for 15 minutes or until browned.
Fill with grape jelly when serving.

Submitter: 
Kristin McNeil
Description: 
This is my grandma's recipe! These little cookies are super easy and delicious!
