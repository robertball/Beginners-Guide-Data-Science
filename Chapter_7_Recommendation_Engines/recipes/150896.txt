Title:
Mango Mai Tai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978171.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ Mango
1 1/2 ounces pineapple juice
1/4 ounce orgeat syrup
1/4 cup grenadine
1 slice pineapple
1 stemmed cherry

Directions:
Pour Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) Mango, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix, pineapple juice, orgeat syrup, and grenadine into a shaker; shake with ice and pour into glass.
Garnish the mai tai with a pineapple slice and cherry.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add fruit flavor to your drinks without all the slicing by using Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ in Coconut, Mango, Pineapple or Passion Fruit.
