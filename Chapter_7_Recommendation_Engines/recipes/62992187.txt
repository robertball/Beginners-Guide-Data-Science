Title:
Asian Sweet and Spicy Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744432.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T low sodium soy sauce
2 t Splenda
1/4 cup water
1 t tomato paste
1 T sherry
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 t fresh ginger, minced
red pepper flakes to taste
1 green onion, finely chopped
1 t cornstarch
1 T water
olive oil spray
1/2 lb chicken tenders

Directions:
Mix the first nine ingredients until well blended.  Place the chicken in a skillet sprayed with a bit of olive oil spray.  Pour the sauce over the chicken and simmer, covered for 8-10 minutes or until chicken is done, turning once.  Remove chicken and keep warm on a plate covered with foil.  Mix the cornstarch and water together in a small bowl and pour into the sauce.  Cook for 2-3 minutes until the sauce is thickened.  Pour sauce over chicken and serve.
