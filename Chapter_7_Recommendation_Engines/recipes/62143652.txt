Title:
Yummy Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quick oatmeal(or 2 packages plain instant oatmeal)
2 TBSP. chopped dates
2 TBSP. golden raisins
1 medium sized banana,sliced
2 TBSP chopped Pecans
11/2 cups water (or apple cider-optional)

Directions:
combine ingredients into a cereal bowl and microwave for 2 minutes and 30 seconds. Remove from microwave and stir until all ingredients are mixed together. The banana will"melt" away into your oatmeal thickening it up. ENJOY!!!

Submitter: 
Jaci Jirik
Description: 
Super easy to make and full of antioxidants! I make this every morning in the winter here in WI and it keeps me satisfied till lunch and it gives me energy needed to clear the snow. LOL
