Title:
Jalapeno Rice Cassserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c uncooked rice
1 onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
1 c celery, chopped
1/3-1/2 c butter
1 T sugar
2 cans cream style corn
1 c grated Cheddar or Mexican cheese

Directions:
Cook the rice and set aside.
Saute the vegetables in the butter until tender. Combine everything and pour into greased 2 quart casserole.
Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jeanne Mason
Description: 
This recipe is quick and easy to make. Additional spices can be added to make it a true south of the border dish.
