Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123154.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks margarine
1/2 lb Velveeta cheese
1 c. peanut butter
1 T. vanilla
2 lb powdered sugar

Directions:
In microwave, melt margarine & Velveeta for 2 1/2 min. @ level 5 (medium).
Stir.
Add peanut butter & vanilla.
Stir in powdered sugar.
Press in 13x9 pan.
