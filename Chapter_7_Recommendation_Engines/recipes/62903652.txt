Title:
Veggie Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Uncooked lasagna noodles
1 yellow squash sliced
1 zuccini sliced
2 portabella mushroom sliced
1/2 onion
2 cloves garlic
1 Tbl. oregano
1 Tbl. italian seasoning
1 tsp. garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
15 oz. part skim Ricotta cheese
1/2 c. parmesian cheese
2 eggs
8 oz. mozzarella cheese shredded
8 oz. mild cheddar cheese shredded
6 oz. tomato paste
8 oz. tomato sauce
14.5 oz. stewed tomoto
2 tsp. olive oil
In a sauce pan dice the two cloves of garlic and add the onion, cook until the onions are translucent.  Add the tomato sauce, paste and stewed tomatoes, salt,pepper, oregano, italian seasoning and galic powder. Bring ingrediants to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes.
Slice the zucchini, squash and mushrooms.
Mix the 2 eggs, parmesian cheese and ricotta cheese together.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven 375
Layer the pan first with a bit of sauce then lay down the first layer of lasagna noodles, breaking them to make them fit the pan.  Spoon ricotta cheese all over the noodles, layer with the squash and mushrooms, cover with a layer of sauce and cover with both kinds of cheese, put down your second layer of noodles and continue with veggies and cheese and sauce till you finish off the top with cheese and bake in oven.
Note:
Cover the pan with lid or tin foil and bake for the first 30 minutes, cook last 15 minutes uncovered to brown the top.
The lasagna is done when a knife inserted in the middle comes out easy.

Submitter: 
Stacie Shelton-Mainard
Description: 
This is a veggie lasagna made in a 8 inch cast iron skillet.
