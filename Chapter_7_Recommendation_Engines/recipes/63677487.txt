Title:
Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. ground beef
1 envelope of taco seasoning
16 oz. cream cheese
8 oz onion dip
8 oz salsa
2 c shredded cheddar
1 c shredded pepper jack cheese
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown and drain ground beef, add taco seasoning and prepare accoring to package directions.
Spray a 9x13' pan with non-stick spray.
Soften cream cheese and combine with onion dip. Spread in bottom of 9x13" pan.
Layer beef on top of the cream cheese mixture.
Pour the salsa over the beef layer.
Top with cheeses.
Bake at 350 until hot and bubbly, about 20-30 minutes.
Serve with tortilla chips!

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
My mom made this to serve at my daughter's graduation party. It was a big hit and I have made it several times since then!
