Title:
Chicken Curry Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/823277.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups fresh broccoli florets
3 chopped chicken breasts
1/2 cup of butter
4 chopped garlic cloves
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup of grated cheddar cheese
1 can creamed chicken mushroom soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon curry
1 teaspoon garlic
1 dozen crushed saltine crackers

Directions:
Cook broccoli until tender by steaming or place in a microwave for approximately 4 to 5 minutes. Set aside.
Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic, pepper, salt and chicken. Cook until chicken is no longer pink, approximately 6 minutes, stirring often.
Lay broccoli in the bottom of a 2 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with the cheese. Place the (undrained) chicken, garlic mixture over the cheese.
Combine the soup, mayo, curry and garlic. Spread over the chicken. Sprinkle crackers over the sauce.
Bake at 350 degrees 35 minutes and serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Elyssa Luria
Description: 
Garlic sauteed chicken, fresh broccoli along with curry and cheese make this recipe an irresistible, home cooked meal. Nicely paired with a side of rice.
