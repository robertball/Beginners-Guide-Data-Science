Title:
Rapture Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3 oz. pkgs. rasberry Jello
1 env. unflavored gelatin
1 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
1 c. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese (softened)
1 20 oz. can cherry pie filling
1 to 2 c. whipped cream

Directions:
Dissolve one package of rasberry Jello in 1 cup boiling water, add 1 cup cold water, stir, pour into medium sized bowl, and refrigerate until set (about 2 hours). Dissolve envelope of plain gelatin in 1/2 cup cold water. Heat sweetened condensed milk and sugar in small saucepan but do not boil. Add plain gelatin mixture, vanilla, and softened cream cheese. Mix until smooth. Cool, then pour on top of first layer. Refrigerate until set (about 2 hours). Dissolve other package of rasberry Jello in 1 cup boiling water. Stir in pie filling and pour over second layer. Chill until set (about 2 hours). Before serving cover with whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Joannayarbrough
Description: 
I've made this for potlucks multiple times and it's always one of the first things to go! It can be easily made low sugar for diabetics if you substitute artificial sweetener for the sugar and use sugar free jello, whipped cream, and pie filling.
