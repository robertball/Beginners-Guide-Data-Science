Title:
Garlic And Herb Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-boiled eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1-2 Tbsp. prepared mustard
1 Tbsp. 'Mrs. Dash' Garlic and Herb flavor salt free seasoning (or to taste)
Paprika (optional)

Directions:
Hard-boil, cool and peel eggs.
Cut in half lengthwise, carefully remove yolks and place in a separate small bowl; mash with fork.
Add mayonnaise, Mrs. Dash and mustard.  Blend well.
Scoop or pipe yolk mixture into egg halves.
Garnish with additonal Mrs. Dash or paprika if desired.

Kimberly
Description: 
A simple deviled egg recipe using 'Mrs. Dash'.
