Title:
Deb's Mediteranian Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of bacon, cut into small pieces.
1 shallot, sliced
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup red wine
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 clove garlic
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
8 oz cavatapi pasta
1/2 cup 2% milk
1 1/2 teaspoons flour
1/4 cup fresh grated parmesian cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut bacon into small pieces and fry.  Remove the bacon from grease and drain on a paper towel.  Add shallots and mushrooms to grease and saute 2 minutes.  Add red wine and undrained tomatoes.  Bring to a boil and cook til liquid is reduced.
Add milk and flour to small saucepan.  Stir with a whisk until thick.  Add cheese, stir well.  Add cheese sauce to tomatoe mixture.  Stir well to mix.
Cook pasta 10 minutes.  Drain.  Add the pasta to the tomatoe mixture.  Toss well to mix.  Serve with french bread and a salad.
Makes 4 servings.
