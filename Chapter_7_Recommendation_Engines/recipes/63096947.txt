Title:
Beef Stew - Untraditional Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (25 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
2 cups water
4.5 pounds beef stew chunks
1 12 oz bag of carrots
3 potatoes cut to 1 inch x 1 inch approx blocks

Directions:
In a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix and water. Place pot roast in slow cooker and coat with soup mixture.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
Place the potatoes & carrots in and cook for 1-2 hours (per the setting selected previously)
