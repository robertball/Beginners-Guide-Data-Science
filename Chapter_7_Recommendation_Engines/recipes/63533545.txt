Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10, medium; Beef Short Ribs (or equivalent of larger size)
5 lbs; Beef Chuck ... trimmed, cut to 3/4" cubes (include meat from Bones)
1 lb; Mushrooms ... chopped
1 lb; Red Potatoes ... cut to 3/4" pieces (with skin)
5 med.; Carrots ... cup to 3/4" pieces
1/2 cup; Onions .... diced
1/2 cup; Shallots ... diced
2, 14 oz cans; Tomatoes ... chopped (retain juice)
1/2+ cup; Olive Oil
2 TBL; all-purpose Flour
2 TBL; fresh Thyme ... chopped fine
2 tsp;  Allspice
5 cloves; Garlic
3+ cups; Beef Broth
2 cups; Marsala Wine
1, 14 oz can; Tomato Paste
Kosher Salt & fresh-ground White Pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine Beef, Flour, half of the Thyme, Allspice.
In large pot, heat 1/4 cup of Oil on medium heat.  Add half the Garlic & bones.  Allow to brown for five minutes.
Add 1/4 cup of Oil & remaining Garlic.  Stir in Beef mixture & allow to brown.
Mix in Mushrooms & continue cooking until Beef is thoroughly brown.  Remove Beef & Mushrooms to separate bowl; leave Bones in pot.
Reduce heat to low.  Add Potatoes to bones and stir for three minutes.  Add Carrots & continue stirring additional three minutes.  Add Onion & Shallots & finish stirring an additional three minutes.
Return Beef and juices to pot & stir thoroughly.  Add Tomatoes & remaining Thyme.  Stir for five minutes.
Stir in Marsala & three cups of Broth.  Bring to boil; cover & reduce to simmer for 15-20 minutes.
Add Tomato Paste & stir until blended; recover & simmer 15 minutes.
Transfer all contents to Crock-Pot (or any slow-cooker).  Continue cooking on low temperature.  Check every 20-30 minutes until mixture begins to thicken.
Remove bones for Fido.
Continue cooking until desired thickness is achieved (at least 45 minutes).  If it gets too thick, add mixture of 1/2 Broth with 1/2 Tomato juice, in 1/4 cup increments.
Serve with Saltine or Oyster crackers.
