Title:
Waikiki Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1.5 lbs ground beef
2/3 cup bread crumbs
1/3 cup minced onion
1 egg
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ginger
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon shortening
Sauce:
2 tablespoons cornstarch
13 oz tin of pineapple chunks (reserve liquid)
1/2 cup golden brown sugar
1 tablespoon soya sauce
1/3 cup chopped green pepper
1/3 cup vinegar

Directions:
For the meatballs, mix all the ingredients except the shortening. Shape into meatballs and fry until brown in shortening.
For the sauce, mix cornstarch and sugar in a small bowl. Stir in reserved pineapple liquid, vinegar and soya sauce until smooth. Pour into skillet and cook over medium heat stirring until it boils and thickens. Add meatballs, pineapple, peppers and heat through, about 5 minutes.
Serve over rice
