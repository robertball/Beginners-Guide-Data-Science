Title:
Chicken, Broccoli, and Rigatoni in Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
½ box rigatoni pasta
2 cups broccoli florets
½ pint heavy cream
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 T olive oil
4 T butter
½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 T. cream cheese
¼ tsp paprika
½ tsp garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Flour and Italian seasoning for dredging

Directions:
Butterfly chicken breasts and lie between two pieces of saran wrap and pound flat. Cut into bite size pieces.
Mix Italian seasoning and flour and dredge chicken pieces, set aside.
In skillet heat 2T of olive oil and 2T of butter and saute chicken till golden brown, set aside
In large skillet slowly heat 2 T butter, heavy cream, chicken broth, paprika, garlic powder and salt and pepper until bubbly.
Add Parmesan cheese and cream cheese until melted.
Meanwhile boil rigatoni until al dente (about 8-9 minutes).
Add chicken and broccoli to dish and simmer until sauce thickens and broccoli is cooked to desired tenderness.
Add rigatoni to dish and let cook for a minute or two longer.
Serve with your favorite Italian bread.
