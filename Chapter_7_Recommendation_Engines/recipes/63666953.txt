Title:
Fried Cornmeal Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs catfish fillets
1 cup yellow cornmeal
1/3 cup flour
1 tsp Cajun seasoning or Old Bay
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp paprika
Vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Heat 1/2 inch deep oil in large skillet to 350. Combine dry ingredients in large zip-loc bag. Place fillets into the bag 1-2 at a time and shake until thoroughly coated. Fry in batches, 5-6 minutes per side, turning once, until fish is golden brown and flakes easily with fork. Drain on paper towels.
