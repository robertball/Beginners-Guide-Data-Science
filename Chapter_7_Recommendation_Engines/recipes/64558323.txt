Title:
JEGS Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Elbow Noodles (dry)
Sauce:
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
4 tbsp milk + 1/2 to 1 cup milk
12 oz Velveeta Cheese
2 tsp parseman
1/4 cup of cheddar cheese
optional - pinch of Oregano

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cook noodles according to directions.   While noodles are cooking make the cheese sauce by melting the butter in a sauce pan.  Add flour to the butter to make a roux.   Then add 2 tbsp milk to the roux.   Cut up Velveeta, add it to the roux, and stir until melted.   Add parmesan, oregano, and cheddar cheese to the cheese sauce.   Add approximately 1/4 to 1/2 cup additional milk to make desired thickness (gravy like thickness).    Drain noodles.   Mix noodles and cheese sauce in 2 qt Pyrex dish.   Cook at 350 for 10 minutes.
