Title:
Kevie's Mom's Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. shredded imitation crab
16 oz. container sour cream
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 bunch green onion, chopped
1 shakes tobasco
1/2 T. lemon juice
1 T. barbecue sauce
optional, 2 shakes accent
milk if needed

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and add milk to thin if needed.
Best prepared 1 day ahead.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Kevie's mom's Krab dip, great on Ritz.
