Title:
Pineapple Ginger Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (20 ozs) unsweetened pinapple tidbits
1 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
4 bone-in pork loin chops
1 tablespoon butter
1/2 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Drain pinapple, reserving 1/4 cup juice; set aside.  Combine pepper and ginger; rub over both sides of pork chops.  In a skillet, brown chops in better for 2-3 minutes on each side.  Add orange juice, 1 cup pineapple and resevered pineapple juice.  (Remaining pineapple may be used for something else.)
Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until meat is tender.  Remove pork chops and keep warm.  In a bowl, combine the cornstarch, salt and water until smooth.  Stir into pan juices.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.  Serve with chops.
