Title:
Yummy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6  8 MEDIUM SIZED RED POTATOES
3 CUPS SLICED FRESH MUSHROOMS
6 TBSP MELTED BUTTER DIVIDED
2 CUPS SHREDDED CHEESE (MEXICAN)
+ CUP SOUR CREAM   (FAT FREE)
+ CUP RANCH        ( LIGHT)
-  CUP BACON BITS
1 BUNCH SLICED GREEN ONION
1 CUP  BREAD CRUMBS  (ITALIAN)

Directions:
1.  PREHEAT OVEN TO 350*  BOIL POTATOES (SEE NOTES)  IN LARGE SAUCEPAN UNTIL TENDER ABOUT 20 MIN.  DRAIN AND CUT POTATOES INTO SLICES.
2  SAUTE MUSHROOMS IN 2TBSP BUTTER IN A LARGE SKILLET OVER MEDIUM-HIGH HEAT UNTIL TENDER.  REMOVE FROM HEAT.
3.  IN A LARGE BOWL MIX CHEESE, SOUR CREAM, RANCH BACON BITS.  (SEE NOTES)  I ADD ABOUT 6 CRACKS FRESH PEPPER AND 6 SHAKES GARLIC SALT TO THIS MIXTURE.
4.  SPRAY A 9X13 PAN WITH PAM AND PLACE POTATOE SLICES EVENLY ACROSS THE BOTTOM OF THE PAN.  SPREAD MUSHROOMS ACROSS POTATOES.  LAYER CHEESE MIXTURE ON TOP.
5.  SPREAD GREEN ONIONS ON TOP OF CHEESE MIXTURE AND TOP WITH BREAD CRUMBS.
6.  DRIZZLE 4TBSP MELTED BUTTER OVER THE TOP.
7.  COVER WITH FOIL AND BAKE FOR 20 MIN.
8.  REMOVE FOIL AND BAKE FOR 10-15 MIN MORE.  (LIGHT BROWN)

Submitter: 
MARCYY
Description: 
These potatoes are layered with bacon,mushrooms and cheese.  Very Creamy and Delicious
