Title:
Paleo Peach Cobbler with Coconut and Slivered Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3780049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16 oz bag frozen peaches
2 tablespoon coconut sugar, divided
1 1/2 cups almond flour
1/2 cup coconut flakes
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 tablespoons unsalted butter, cubed
1/4 cup silvered almonds
1 tablespoon coconut oil, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place frozen peaches in a pie plate or other baking dish.  Place in the oven for 5 minutes.  Remove then break apart and spread out in the dish.  Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of coconut sugar.
In a food processor add in almond meal, remaining coconut sugar, coconut flakes, baking powder and salt. Pulse about 5 times to incorporate together.  Then add in vanilla and butter.  Pulse a couple more times.  Remove from processor and top over peaches.  Top with silvered almonds then drizzle the top with the melted coconut oil.
Bake for 22-25 minutes or until topping is golden brown.  Serve warm!

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
I always have frozen fruit and vegetables handy.  Tonight I pulled out a 16ounce bag of frozen peaches and decided to make this yummy Paleo Dessert.  I hope you enjoy.
