Title:
Fudge Brownie Tassies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tassie Dough:
1 c. butter, softened
6 oz. cream cheese
2 c. flour
Tassie Filling:
1 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
4 T. butter
2 eggs, slightly beaten
2/3 c. sugar
2 t. vanilla
Pecan halves

Directions:
Combine dough ingredients in food processor and pulse until dough forms large ball.  Roll into 1 inch balls and press into mini muffin pans.
For filling: In a small saucepan, combine chocolate and butter. Cook and stir over low heat until melted.  Remove from heat.  Stir in eggs, sugar, and vanilla.
Place about 1 1/2 t. of the filling into each shell.
Top with a pecan half.
Bake at 325 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until pastry is golden and filling is puffed.  Cool slightly in pan.  Carefully remove tassies from pan; cool on wire rack.
