Title:
Blue Cheese Horseradish Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1008978.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) jar mayonnaise (such as Hellmann's®/Best Foods®)
10 ounces sour cream
6 tablespoons milk
1 (4 ounce) package crumbled blue cheese, or more to taste
1 dash hot pepper sauce
1/8 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise, sour cream, milk, blue cheese, hot sauce, horseradish, and garlic powder in a bowl. Refrigerate 1 to 2 hours to let flavors blend.

Submitter: 
jeastman331
Description: 
This family recipe comes from a small town restaurant owner who was known for his sauces/dressings. I made a few tweaks. It's amazing!
