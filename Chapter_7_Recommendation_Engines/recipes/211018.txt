Title:
1-2-3 Cheddar Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) jar Ragu® Cheesy Double Cheddar Sauce
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen broccoli florets, thawed
1/4 cup plain or Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1 tablespoon Shedd's Spread Country Crock® Spread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine Double Cheddar Sauce with broccoli in 1-1/2-quart casserole.
Evenly top with bread crumbs combined with Spread.
Bake uncovered 20 minutes or until bread crumbs are golden and broccoli is tender.

Submitter: 
Ragu®
Description: 
Serve this casserole over hot baked potatoes for a delicious, light meatless meal.
