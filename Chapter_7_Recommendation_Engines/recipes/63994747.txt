Title:
Best Ever Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. yellow fleshed potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes
6 whole cloves of garlic, peeled
1 1/2 cups of water
1 tsp salt
pepper to taste
3/4 cup half and half, warmed
1/3 cup butter
2 tbsp chopped fresh chives

Directions:
In a 4-6 quart slow cooker, combine potatoes, garlic, 1 tsp. salt and water, pushing garlic down to ensure that it is submerged in water. Cover and cook on low for 5 to 6 hours or on high for 2 1/2 to 3 hours, until potatoes and garlic are tender. Drain potatoes and garlic and return to slow cooker. Add 1/4 tsp pepper, cream and butter; mash to desired smoothness, stir in chives. Add more cream to reach desired consistency. Adjust seasonings with salt and pepper, if desired.
