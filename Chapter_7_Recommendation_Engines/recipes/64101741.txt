Title:
lime cilantro marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp canola oil
3 tbsp fresh lime juice
1 garlic clove, minced
2 tbsp fresh chopped cilantro
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp onion powder
dash of hot sauce
pinch of black pepper

Directions:
put all ingredients into a small bowl and mix well. place your chicken, pork, or fish into a plastic bag with a seal, pour marinade over meat, seal bag and refrigerate for 30 mins. remove meat from bag and grill, broil or saute. discard any unused marinade.
