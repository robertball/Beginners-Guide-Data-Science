Title:
Ritz Cracker Chicken Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Chicken breasts
1-2 rolls of crackers crushed up
1 cube butter (melted)
8-12 Slices of ham
4-6 pieces swiss or provolone cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°.
Cut chicken lengthwise (butterfly) almost all the way through
Soak chicken in melted butter
Coat chicken in crushed Ritz crackers
Place in 9x13 pan. Cover with foil. Bake 25 min. covered at 350°
Stuff chicken with ham and cheese using a fork to separate at cuts.
Bake an additional 5 min. uncovered or until cheese is melted and crackers are crunchy.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is one of the many variations to the classic Ritz Cracker chicken recipe. I love to eat this with homemade alfredo.
