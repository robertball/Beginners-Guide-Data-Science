Title:
Quick Raspberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985769.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (12 ounce) packages frozen raspberries, thawed
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in food processor or blender and mix until smooth.  Store covered in refrigerator.
