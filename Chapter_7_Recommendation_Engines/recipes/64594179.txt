Title:
Big Ray's Colorful Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3867781.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa, uncooked
1 can (14.5 oz.) chicken broth
1 can (15 oz.) black beans, rinsed
1 can (11 oz.) corn, drained
1 red pepper, chopped
5 green onions, sliced
6 slices deli ham, finely chopped
1/2 cup Italian Dressing

Directions:
Bring quinoa and broth to boil in medium saucepan; cover. Simmer on low heat 15 min. or until quinoa is tender and broth is absorbed; spoon into medium bowl. Cool.
Add remaining ingredients just before serving; mix lightly.

Submitter:

Description: 
Add some color—and some great tastes—to your life with our Colorful Quinoa Salad that includes red peppers, rich ham and a zesty dressing.
