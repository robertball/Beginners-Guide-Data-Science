Title:
Reese's Peanut Butter Temptations Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
About 40 Reese's Peanut Butter Cup Miniature
1/2 cup- butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup- sugar
1/2 cup- packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup- Reese's Creamy Peanut Butter
1 egg
1/2 tsp- vanilla
1 1/2 cups- flour
3/4 tsp- baking soda
1/2 tsp- salt

Directions:
1. Heat the oven to 375. Remove wrappers from candies. Line small muffin cups with paper baking cups.
2. Beat butter, brown sugar, sugar, peanut butter, egg, and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy.Stir together flour, baking soda, and salt; add to butter mixture, beating until well blended. Shape dough into 1 inch balls; place one in each prepared muffin cup. Do not flatten.
3. Bake 8-10 minutes until puffed and lightly browned; remove from oven. Immediately press peanut butter cup or piece onto each cookie. Cool completely on muffin pan.
