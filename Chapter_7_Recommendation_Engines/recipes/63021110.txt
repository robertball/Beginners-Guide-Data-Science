Title:
Josh's Chicken Flavored stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 pound butter, melted
1 (15 ounce) Cream of Chicken Soup
1 (15 ounce) can asparagus tips
1 (8.5 ounce) package corn bread mix
1 tsp. dry sage
1 cup of diced cooked chicken breast
1 ounce sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a mixing bowl, combine butter, Chicken soup, asparagus tips, sage, chicken breast, corn muffin mix and sour cream. Fold all ingredients together, pour into 2 quart casserole dish. Bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
It takes 5 minutes to combine the ingredients and have a whole meal in one dish that taste like Thanksgiving.Modified from: Cornbread Casserole
