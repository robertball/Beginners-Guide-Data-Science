Title:
chicken & feta roll ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken
2 oz feta
2 TB chopped fresh oregano
2TB chopped fresh basil
1 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 lemon halved and thinly sliced
1 1/4 cups whole wheat orzo cooked to package directions
2 TB chopped parsley

Directions:
heat oven to 400.  pound chickent thin.  place 1/4 of the cheese on short end of each chick breast.  Sprinkle and equal amount of oregano and basil over each breast and roll up tightly.
Place chicken, seam side down in 11x7x2 glass dish.  Pour broth and wine over chicken.  Season with salt and pepper. place a slice of lemon on each.  Looslely cover with foil and bake at 400 for 30 min.
cut chick in to 1inch slices and sercve with cooked orzao.
