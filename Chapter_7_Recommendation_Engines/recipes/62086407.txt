Title:
Buttermilk Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
2 cups Sugar
2 cups All-purpose flour
4 tbsp cocoa
1 cup cold water
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
Frosting
1/2 cup margarine
1/4 cup cocoa
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 lb powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 400 F, Grease a 15x11 cookie pan.
Mix together flour, sugar, and cocoa. Place cold water in a sauce pan and bring to a boil. Add margarine and oil and stir till margarine is melted. Pour over dry ingredients, beat until creamy. Add buttermilk, baking soda and eggs, beat until creamy. Note: batter will not be thick. Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 18 minutes.
Frosting:
Bring to a boil over low heat margarine, cocoa and buttermilk, boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in powdered sugar and vanilla extract. Stir until suger melts. Add nuts. Then pour over the brownies immediately after they come out of the oven.
