Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce, chopped
1 head cauliflower, chopped
1 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled
Dressing...
1 c Mayo
1/4 sugar
3/4 c shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In large bowl, combine, lettuce, cauliflower, and bacon.
Combine remaining ingredients in a smaller bowl and blend well. Add a little milk if mixture is too stiff.
Pour dressing over salad mixture and mix well. Refrigerate at least an hour before serving.
