Title:
Italian Style Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg spagetti noodles
2Tbsp shortening
1pd ground beef
1 15oz can tomato sauce
1 6oz can tomato paste
3/4C water
1/4C chopped parsley
1/2C chopped green peppers
1/4tsp garlic powder
1 1/2tsp salt
1/4tsp pepper
1/8tsp oregano
1/8tsp basil
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat shortening in skillet and meat. Cook until brown. Add all ingredients except spagetti and parmesan cheese. Simmer slowly uncovered, 1 hour or until sauce becomes thick. Meanwhile, cook spagetti as directed on package. Chill in cold water, then re-heat in hot water for 3 minutes before serving.
