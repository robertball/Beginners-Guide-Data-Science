Title:
WW Italian Skillet Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp Olive Oil
2 medium onions, chopped
1 medium carrot, chopped
1 stalk celery, sliced
3/4 Lbs. chicken, breast, raw, without skin & bone, trimmed of all visible fat and cut into strips
14 1/2 oz. canned crushed tomatoes
1 Tbsp basil, fresh, chopped or 1 tsp dried
1/2 tsp table salt
1/4 tsp black pepper, freshly ground

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Add the onions, carrot and celery; cook, stirring ocassionallyl, until softened, about 10 minutes.  Add the chicken and cook, stirring occasionally, until opaque, about 5 minutes.  Stir in tomatoes, basil, salt & pepper.  Reduce the heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until the flavors are blended and the chicken is cooked through, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
Chicken strips simmer in a simple tomato sauce with lots of geggies and fresh basil.  Either chicken breasts or chicken tenders can be used.
