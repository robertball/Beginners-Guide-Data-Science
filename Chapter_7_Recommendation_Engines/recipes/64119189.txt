Title:
Granny's Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 Tomatoes cut in quarters
3 Green Peppers chopped
2 Onions chopped
1 Cup Sugar
1.5 Cups Vinegar
3 tsp Salt
1 tsp Cloves
1 tsp Allspice
1 tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a heavy saucepan and simmer until thickened.  Pour into sterilized jars and seal.

Submitter: 
ellie
Description: 
Granny's Chili Sauce was a staple in her home.  She loved this on everything from eggs to toast and potatoes. Try it with seafood and poultry too.  The warm spices make this salsa like sauce a timeless favourite in our family.
