Title:
Grandma's Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Frozen Hash Browns
1 - 500ml Carton of Sour Cream
2 Cans of Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 Cup of Grated Onion
2 Cups Grated Cheddar Cheese
1 Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Pepper
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Thaw potatoes slightly, mix all ingredients except parmesan cheese.  Place in greased 9x13 inch pan.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
Bake at 350, for 1-1.5 hours.
Should be golden brown and bubbly when done.

Submitter: 
Shauna Hubbard
Description: 
My Grandmother used to make this for her ham dinners, and I still make it quite regularly in my own house!  Great side dish that is completely comfort food.
