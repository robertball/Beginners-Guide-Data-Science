Title:
Sante Fe Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/732460.jpg
Ingredients:
2 hard boiled eggs
2 oz cottage cheese (I use lowfat)
2 TBSP your favorite red salsa (I use one w/ black beans in it)
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop cool, boiled eggs into your preferred consistency for an egg salad and then mix in cottage cheese and salsa. I usually refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
Turnin40EatinBetter
Description: 
A spicy take on a stand alone egg salad. Makes a great breakfast w/out all the heaviness and calories! I sometimes have it as a side instead of chips w/ a sandwich too.
