Title:
Garlic & Shrimp Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
1/2 lb shrimp
1 tsp chopped garlic
1/4 cup balsamic & basil dipping oil
1 can diced tomatoes with oregano basil & garlic
optional-1 chili pepper and/or a shake of red pepper flakes
1/4 cup olive oil
Pasta (angel hair)

Directions:
Saute shrimp in olive oil with garlic.
Add balsamic basil & garlic dipping oil and the tomatoes.
Add 1/4 cup fo olive oil
Cook pasta and cool.
Serve mixture over pasta.
