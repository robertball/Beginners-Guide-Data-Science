Title:
Bamia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup of olive oil
1 arge onion finely chopped
1 pound of lamb(from the shoulder or leg)
1 8oz can of sauce
1 package of frozen okra

Directions:
Sautee onions in olive oil until translucent.
Rinse meat well.  Then add to pot.  Salt and pepper meat lightly and let fry in onions for 5-10 minutes.
Add sauce and water until meat is covered.  Salt and pepper some more.
Let water come to a boil and then turn on low.
Allow meat to cook for at least 1 hour on low.
Then add okra.  Add some more water and bring to a boil for a couple of minutes.
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Pour okra mixture into a clay baking dish if available.  if not, any deep baking dish is fine.  salt and pepper to taste for the last time and cover with foil.
45 minutes in the oven.  Uncover for the last 10 minutes.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Felicia Manocchio
Description: 
This is a great recipe for making during the month of Ramadan.  It is an Egyptian dish.  Best served with rice.
