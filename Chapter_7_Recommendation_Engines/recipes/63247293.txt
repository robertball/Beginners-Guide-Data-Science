Title:
Lawn Mower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/848253.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. water
1/2 c. oil (canola or vegetable)
1/4 c. Balsamic vinegar
2 seasoning packets from the Ramen noodles
2 pkgs. Chicken Ramen noodles (uncooked)
1 pkg. broccoli slaw (12 - 16 oz.)
1 bunch green onions - sliced
1 c. cashews
1/4 c. sunflower seeds

Directions:
Mix in a small bowl or shake in a jar: sugar, water, oil, vinegar and 2 seasoning packets from Ramen noodles.
Crumble Ramen noodles in a large bowl and add the broccoli slaw and onions.
Shake/stir dressing very well before adding to the salad.
Add the dressing 1/2 hour to 4 hours before serving.  Depends on how crunchy you want the Ramen noodles.
Add the cashews and sunflower seeds right before serving.
