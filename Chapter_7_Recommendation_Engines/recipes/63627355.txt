Title:
Summer Corn and Cod Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices center cut bacon, halved crosswise
8 scallions, thinly sliced, whites and greens separated
1.5 tsp finely chopped garlic
2 tbsp all-purpose flour
2 med Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled with a .5inch dice
2 cups 2% milk
2 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
2 tsp finely chopped fresh thyme
.75 tsp kosher salt
.25 tsp fresh ground pepper
.75 lb cod, skin removed, chopped
2.5 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels
.25 cup fat free half and half

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook bacon over med heat until crisp (6 mins). Transfer to a paper towel lined plate and crumble.  In same pan, saute scallion whites for 2 mins.  Add garlic: saute 1 min. Add flour; cook, stirring frequently, 2 mins.  Stir in potatoes, milk, broth, thyme, salt, and pepper: bring to a boil. Reduce heat to med-low, simmer 10 mins.  Stir in fish, corn and 2/3 of the bacon; simmer 5 mins.  Stir in half and half and 3/4 scallion greens; simmer 2 mins. Garnish with remaining scallion greens and bacon.
