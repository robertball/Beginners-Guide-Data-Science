Title:
Extra-Easy Spinach Lasagna -- Krysie's Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) container of no or low fat ricotta cheese
1 (16 ounce) package of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
2 (8 ounce) packages of no or low fat mozzarella cheese, shredded
2 eggs
2 (26 ounce) containers of Italian Sauce
9 uncooked lasagna noodles
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Mix together ricotta cheese, spinach, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese and eggs.
Spread 1/2 container pasta sauce in 13 X 9 X 2 inch baking dish. Top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 container pasta sauce and half the spinach mixture. Repeat layers. Top with last 3 lasagna noodes and remaining pasta sauce. Slowly pour water around inside edges of baking dish. Cover with foil.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 50 minutes. Uncover. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese. Bake 10 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted. Let stand 10 minutes.

An easy way to make an extra cheesy spinach lasagna. No or low fat cheeses reduce fat and cholesterol. Use your favorite pasta sauce.Modified from: Extra-Easy Spinach Lasagna
