Title:
Indian Spiced Lentil and leftover Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4384768.jpg
Ingredients:
Leftover Turkey Carcass
6 C water
1/4 C chopped green beans
6-8 small new potatoes halved
1/2 C baby carrots
1 onion quartered
3 garlic gloves smashed
3 whole cloves
1 T Garam Masala
1 T Curry powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 C Lentils
pepper to taste

Directions:
add water to large pot
put in turkey carcass (fill water to cover if needed)
add next 9 Ingredients
bring to slight boil then simmer for 40 minutes
remove turkey carcass but keep vegetables in broth
de-bone any meat add to pot
add lentils
bring to a simmer 20-30 minutes or until lentils are desired texture
