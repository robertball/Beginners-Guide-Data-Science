Title:
Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Corn tortillas
1# ground beef
Taco seasoning
Refried beans
Cheddar cheese
Shredded lettuce
Diced tomatoes
Green onion, chopped
Black olives, sliced
Salsa
Sour cream
Guacamole

Directions:
Warm tortillas one at a time in a hot cast-iron skillet, 2-3 minutes on each side.
Brown ground beef, season according to directions.
Fill tacos as desired.
