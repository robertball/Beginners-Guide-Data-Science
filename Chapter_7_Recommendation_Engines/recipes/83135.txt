Title:
Barbecue Pork On Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983450.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) boneless pork loin
1 onion, chopped
3/4 cup cola carbonated beverage
3/4 cup barbecue sauce
8 sandwich buns

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except buns in a 4-quart slow-cooker; cook, covered, on high for 5-6 hours, until very tender.
Drain and slice or shred pork; serve on buns with additional barbecue sauce, if desired.

Submitter: 
National Pork Board
Description: 
Want the flavor of barbecued sandwiches, but don't want to get the grill out or turn on the oven? Fix this slow cook version of barbecue pork for sandwiches.
