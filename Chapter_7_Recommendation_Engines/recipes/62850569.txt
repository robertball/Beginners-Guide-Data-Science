Title:
Cranberry Walnut & Gorgonzola dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Water
1/4 cup cranberries
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
3 T sugar
2 T cheese (gorganzola, parmesan or feta)
2 T walnuts
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
Place everything, except oil in blender and blend until sugar & salt dissolve (about 1 minute)
Drissle oil into blender slowly.
Blend 1 more minute.
Store in bottle and chill.
