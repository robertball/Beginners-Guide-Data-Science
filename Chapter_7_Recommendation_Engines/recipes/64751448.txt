Title:
Plum Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4lbs plums
5 cups sugar
2T lemon juice
1/2 cup water
2 1.75 oz boxes of regular/full sugar pectin
1/2tsp vanilla, optional

Directions:
Cut plums into bite sized pieces and discard pits.
In large pot over medium high heat combine plums, lemon juice and water.
Bring to rolling simmer, cover, and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add sugar and bring to a rolling boil, adjusting heat as necessary, stirring constantly.
Add pectin and return to a full rolling boil, stirring constantly for 1 minute.  Remove from heat.
Check consistency by placing a small amount of jam on a frozen plate and allow to cool.  If it isn't thick enough, return to a boil for 1 minute.
Remove from heat and skim any foam that appears on the top
Allow jam to cool slightly and fill jars to within 1/8 of the rim.  Close and finger tighten rings and lids.
Wipe threads as needed and process in water bath for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Arizona Desert Flower
Description: 
Use with any fresh plum you have on hand.  Makes 10 8 oz jars.
