Title:
Andre's Creamy Mushroom Marsala Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mushrooms chopped in small pieces
1/8 cup marsala wine
2 tbsp bisto gravy mix
1 cup water
1/2 tsp basil leaves
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp ground peper
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp maple syrup
10% cream - to taste
1

Directions:
Fry mushrooms in butter
Add marsala wine and bring to a boil
mix Bisto with water and add to mushroom mixture
Bring to a boil and stir for 2 minutes
Add spices (basil, celery salt, pepper and worcestershire sauce)
Add maple syrup and cream.
Serve over steak or chicken.
