Title:
Lobster Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 Pillsbury thin crust pizza dough
* Melted butter
* Garlic powder
* salt
* 2 (12 0z.) cans diced tomatoes
* 1/4 to 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese shredded
* 1- 2 cups pizza blend cheese
* 1 to 2 lobsters (lobster meat chopped up)
* fresh basil or dried basil

Directions:
1) Spread the pizza dough on a cookie sheet.
2) Melt some butter and add desired amount of garlic powder and salt. Mix well and brush over the crust. Bake crust in oven (350 degrees F) until lightly browned.
3) Take crust out and let it sit until cooled and then top with parmesan cheese, diced tomatoes, lobster, fresh or dried basil, then top with some parmesan and pizza blend cheese.
4) Bake in oven until completely done and cheese is melted. Once done cut into squares.
