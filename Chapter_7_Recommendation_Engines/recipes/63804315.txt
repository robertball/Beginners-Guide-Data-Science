Title:
Barbeque Sauce, Memphis-Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T unsalted butter
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 c tomato sauce
1/2 c cider vinegar
1/3 c rice vinegar
1/3 c molasses
3 T Worcestershire sauce
2 T dark brown sugar
2 t yellow mustard
1 t Kosher salt
1 t freshly ground black pepper
1/4 t cayenne pepper

Directions:
Saute onion in butter until soft. Add garlic and saute additional 30 seconds.
Add remaining ingredients. Simmer 15-20 minutes until slightly thickened.
Blend until smooth.
Store in refrigerator up to a month.
