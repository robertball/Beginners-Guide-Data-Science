Title:
Autumn Gold Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 extra large eggs
¼ tsp. salt
½ cup sugar                                                1 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup cooked pumpkin (1 can is fine)
½ tsp. ginger
¼ cup dark corn syrup
½ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix ingredients with beater.  Mix in walnuts last if you are using them (yuk!).  Pour in unbaked pastry lined pan (I use refrigerated dough– works great).  Bake 45 to 50 minutes at 375 degrees.  Enjoy!!
