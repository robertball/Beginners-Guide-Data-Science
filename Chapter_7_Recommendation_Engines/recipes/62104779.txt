Title:
Kahlua cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups flour
1/4 cup powdered cocoa
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup butter, slightly softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 teaspoons Kahlua coffee liquer
2 eggs
1 12 ounce package of chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt in small bowl. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and Kahlua in large mixer bowl until creamy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in morsels and nuts. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake for 9 to 11 minutes. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Steph T.
Description: 
An adaptation of the traditional chocolate chip cookie with a slight Kahlua kick and extra chocolate to savor.
