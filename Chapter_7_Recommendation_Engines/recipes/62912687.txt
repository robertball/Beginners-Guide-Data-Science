Title:
Suspiciously Easy and Addictive Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans red kidney beans
1 packet chili or taco seasoning
1 can red enchilada sauce
10 small tortillas [any kind]
2 chicken breasts, chopped
handful chopped green chile peppers
3 cups shredded marble cheese
2 cups cottage cheese

Directions:
1. Chop and saute chicken breast [if you prefer, in cumin and chiles]
2. Saute beans with 1/2 packet seasoning and one cup water. Mash until smooth.
3. In tortilla, place a few spoonfuls of beans, chicken, and cottage cheese down the center. Roll and place in tray.
4. Cover all enchiladas equally with enchilada sauce. Coat with cheese. Sprinkle chopped chile peppers on top. Bake at 400 degrees until cheese is bubbly, but not browned.

Submitter: 
Amna Kazmi
Description: 
These are simple, forgiving and versatile enchiladas that always draw raves and demands, and over the years I've become known for them! The beauty of this recipe is how adaptable it is to different ingredients and amounts. But this simple version always comes out spectacular with perfectly blended flavors!
