Title:
Big Ray's Spicy Drunken Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3196597.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound shrimp, peeled & de-veined
1 can of beer,an  IPA works best
3 green onions, sliced
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 tsp red pepper flakes, or to taste
1-2 bay leaves
fresh chopped parsley
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Add shallot, garlic, red pepper flakes, bay leaves and beer in a large saucepan and turn heat on high. Bring to a boil and reduce beer sauce by half, stirring occasionally.
Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 3-4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, if needed.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe has a nice kick to it, so alter the heat to your taste.  Serve with crusty bread or on top of rice.
