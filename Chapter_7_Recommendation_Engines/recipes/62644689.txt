Title:
Baby Super food Latke Hors d' oeuvres Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds white potato
2 medium onions
2 eggs
1/4 cup wheat germ
1/4 fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Coarsely grate in food processor 1 1/2 pounds white potato and 2 medium onions add to beaten eggs, wheat germ and parsley. Mixture will be wet, Bake or fry, making sure the egg gets throughly cooked.
