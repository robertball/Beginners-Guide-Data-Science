Title:
Cake Vodka Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c vanilla chips
1/2 c granulated sugar
3 tbls of light corn syrup
1/2 c of UV Cake Vodka
1 box of vanilla wafers, crushed
Powdered sugar to roll the vodka balls

Directions:
Melt the vanilla chips, add sugar, corn syrup, 1/2 vodka and mix.  Add vanilla wafers, refrigerate for 1 hour.  Make balls out of the mixture and roll in powdered sugar. The size of the candy is optional, I make them 1 inch balls.
Also, another option is to mix some cocoa with the powdered sugar and roll the vodka balls.
