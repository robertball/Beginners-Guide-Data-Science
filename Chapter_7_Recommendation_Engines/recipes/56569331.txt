Title:
Honey Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Honey Bars:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup oil
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg
1 3/4 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
dash salt
1/2 cup pecans (optional it says on the recipe card but I officially declare them to be required)

Directions:
Mix together first 5 ingredients. In a large bowl , mix together flour, salt and soda. Combine the two and add nuts. Spoon into 9x13 pan (batter will be thick) Bake at 325 for 15-20 minutes.
