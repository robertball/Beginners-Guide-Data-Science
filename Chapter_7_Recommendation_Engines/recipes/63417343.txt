Title:
Ed's Sticky Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12            Boneless skinless chicken thighs
1/2 cup    Barbecue sauce
1/4 cup    Soy sauce
1/4 cup    Red wine vinegar
1/4 cup    Maple syrup or liquid honey
1 tbsp       Chili powder
1 tbsp      Dijon mustard
1 tbsp      Grated gingerroot
2 tsp        Minced garlic
1 tsp        Ground Cumin
1 tbsp      Cornstarch

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Spray a 9x13 inch baking pan with cooking spray.  Arrange thighs in a pan in a single layer
Sauce-whisk together remaining ingredients EXCEPT CORNSTARCH in a medium bowl.  Pour evenly over chicken.  Coat both sides of chicken with sauce.  Bake uncovered for about 40 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink in middle.
Remove chicken from pan and keep warm.  Carefully pour sauce from pan into a small pot.  Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.  Combine cornstarch with a equal amount of water and mix until smooth.  Add to sauce.  Cook until sauce is bubbly and has thickened about 1 minute.  Serve hot chicken with extra sauce on top
