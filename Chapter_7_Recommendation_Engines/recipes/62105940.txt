Title:
Simple & Easy Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef (or turkey)
1 large onion-diced
3 tsp. sugar
3 tsp. white vinegar
2 Tbs. prepared mustard
1 cup ketchup
dash pepper

Directions:
Brown meat, drain and set aside. Saute onions. Combine meat, onions, sugar, vinegar, mustard, ketchup and pepper. Simmer for about 45 minutes. Serve on rolls.
