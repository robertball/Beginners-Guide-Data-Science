Title:
Dessert Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 4oz can mild green chilies
1  16oz can creamed corn
1/2 cup grated cheese
1 cup flour
1 cup cornmeal
4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Beat butter and sugar together add eggs one at a time until blended, add creamed corn. Mix together the flour, cornmeal, baking powder and salt and to sugar mixture, stir in the cheese.  Pour into a 9x13 greased pan and bake at 350 till done.
