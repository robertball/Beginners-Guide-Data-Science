Title:
Butternut Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large butternut squash
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cups milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 pinch salt
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
3 eggs
1/4 cup butter, melted
TOPPING
8 ounces cereal, crushed
1/8 butter melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Cut butternut squash in half, scoop out seeds, plact half side down and roast until soft. Scoop out and mix with all ingredients until blended.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until set.
In a medium bowl combine crushed cereal and melted butter.  Crumble over top of cooked casserole and return to oven to brown.

Submitter: 
Heather
