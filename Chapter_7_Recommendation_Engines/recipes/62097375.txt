Title:
Best Rhubarb Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups rhubarb, diced
Thickening:
1 cup white sugar
2 Tbsp. water
2 tsp. vanilla
3 Tbsp. tapioca
Crispy:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup flour
3/4 cup oatmeal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine all crispy ingredients together and mix until crumbly.  Place 1/2 this mixture in the bottom of an 8x8 pan.
Combine all thickening ingredients in a medium sauce pan and cook over medium heat until sauce begins to thicken.
Add the sauce to the rhubarb and stir.
Pour rhubarb sauce over crispy layer on the bottom of the pan and then sprinkle the top with the remaining crispy mixture.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes, or until the top is brown and the juice bubbles.

Submitter: 
PULLEYHEATHER
Description: 
I always look forward to rhubarb season so I can make this delicious crisp.  It never lasts long in our house!  Tastes great with vanilla ice cream.
