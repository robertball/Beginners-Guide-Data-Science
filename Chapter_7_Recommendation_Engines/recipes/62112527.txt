Title:
Vanilla Cupcakes with Chocolate Swiss Meringue Buttercream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups butter, room temperature
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 large eggs
3-4 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups milk
5 large egg whites
1 1/4 cups sugar
2 cups butter, room temperature
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon coarse salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Line cupcake pan with paper liners; set aside.
In a medium mixing bowl, mix dry ingredients.
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.
Add eggs one at a time.
Add vanilla.
Add flour mixture and milk alternately.
Fill liners 2/3 full.
Bake for 15-23 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rae
Description: 
Moist Vanilla cupcakes are baked until golden brown and frosted with chocolate swiss meringue buttercream.
