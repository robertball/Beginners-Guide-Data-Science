Title:
Onion Quiche with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large Vidalia onion, thinly sliced
2 cups mushrooms, sliced
1 TB olive oil
4 eggs
1 cup whipping cream
1 1/2 TB chopped fresh thyme
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 9-inch deep-dish pastry shell

Directions:
Saute onion and mushrooms in hot oil in a large skillet over medium heat until onions are soft and tender.  Stir together eggs, cream, thyme, salt, pepper and nutmeg in a large bowl.  Stir in the onion mixture and pour this mixture into the pastry shell.  Place pie on a baking sheet.  Bake on lower oven rack for 45 minutes or until done at a temperature of 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Everyone loves this!  Any type of mushroom could be used by I prefer button mushrooms.  Use sweet vidalia onions too.  A store brought pie crust could be substituted.
