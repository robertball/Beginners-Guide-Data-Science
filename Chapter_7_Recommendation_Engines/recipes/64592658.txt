Title:
Quick Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. 3.4oz instant vanilla pudding mix
2 cups cold milk
1 cup cold cooked rice
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Mix pudding mix and milk; beat 1-2 minutes until well blended.
Stir in rice and raisins.
Chill.
Can be served with a sprinkle of cinnamon and/or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Using cold cooked rice & instant pudding, this dessert is quick and easy.
