Title:
Summer Squash Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large summer squash
approx 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup of butter

Directions:
Wash and peel squash, and slice approximately 1/4 inch slices
Layer in a baking dish (single layer)
Dot with butter
Sprinkle parmesan cheese (grated)
Bake @ 350 degrees until bubbly (golden yellow-brown)

Submitter: 
Peg
Description: 
An easy summer recipe that you are sure to enjoy!
