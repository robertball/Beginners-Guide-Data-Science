Title:
Creamy Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981548.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into strips
3 cups cut-up vegetables (broccoli, carrots, green beans and red pepper)
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Healthy Request® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
4 cups hot cooked rice, cooked without salt

Directions:
Spray skillet with vegetable cooking spray and heat 1 minute. Add chicken and stir-fry until browned and juices evaporate. Push chicken to one side of skillet.
Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
Add soup, milk, soy and garlic powder and heat through. Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Sauteed chicken and colorful vegetables are served over hot rice in a savory, cream sauce.
