Title:
Baked Brussel Sprouts and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
brussel sprouts
broccoli
olive oil
kosher salt
black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Cut Brussel sprouts in half
Toss veggies with oil, salt, pepper
Arrange on baking sheet
Bake 25 minutes
