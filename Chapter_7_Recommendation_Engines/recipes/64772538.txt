Title:
Macaire Kartoffeln / Potato Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Baking potato
2 egg yolk
2 oz butter
1 tbsp. Finely chopped Parsley
1/4 tsp Nutmeg
*( 2 oz of finely minced Bacon or Ham if desired).
1

Directions:
Boil, peel and mash the potatoes.  While hot add the scrambled yo;ks, melted Butter, Nutmeg and Parsley.  Mix thoroughly and place onto a moist clean dish towel.  carefully roll and form a log about
2 inches Diameter.  make sure the ends are flat and has consistent shape.  Chill  at least 2 hours.  Cut into 1 inch slices;   coat in bread crumbs or flour and carefully fry in a lightly oiled Pan.
keep warm for serving.
serve with.  Chicken,  Duck, goose,  Game and Grilled meats

Alois
Description: 
Potato cakes of Mashed Potato, Egg yolk, butter, Nutmeg and Parsley.
