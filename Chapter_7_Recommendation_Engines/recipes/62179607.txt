Title:
Salsa Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. beef chuck roast
1/2 cup chunky salsa
1/8 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
1 tablespoon dried Cilantro
2 10 oz. cans Rotel tomatoes
1 clove garlic, diced
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Directions:
Place roast in a slow cooker.
Mix together the remaining ingredients.
Pour over the meat and cover.
Cook for 8-10 hours on low, or until the meat is fork tender.
Remove meat and shred when cooled.
Skim fat from broth and thicken, if desired to use with meat.
Use meat as filling for tortillas, chimichangas, tacos or eat as is with beans and rice.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This beef can is used as a main dish with rice and bean, as a filling for tacos or chimichangas, or as the meat in a taco salad.  My family loves it and I hope you do as well.  Spice it up or down to satisfy your family's taste buds.
