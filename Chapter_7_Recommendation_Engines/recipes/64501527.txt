Title:
Baked Chicken Chimichangas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2616054.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Shredded Chicken
1/4 cup Chopped Green Onions
1 Diced Tomato
1 cup Salsa
1 Tbsp Cumin
1 C Shredded Cheese
1/2 cup Cream Cheese
8 Flour Tortillas
Oil

Directions:
1) Combine all ingredients, through Cream Cheese together into one bowl. Stir together to create filling.
2) Scoop about 1 cup of filling in the center of each tortilla and fold up burrito style. Place seam side down on a baking rack in a cookie sheet. Spray or brush oil onto the top and bottom of each tortilla.
3) Bake at 450 degrees for about 8 to 10 minutes on each side.
