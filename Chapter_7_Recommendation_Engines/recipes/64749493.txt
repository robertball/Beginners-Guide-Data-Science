Title:
Moraccan Couscous with Rhubarb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6700025.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 cup chopped dried apricots
1/2 cup finely chopped rhubarb
1 cup couscous
1 1/4 cups chicken stock
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
Pinch ground cloves
1/4 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Heat oil in a saucepan. Add onions and cook, until they begin to color and soften.
Add apricots and rhubarb and cook 2 minutes more. Add couscous, stock, salt and spices. (Mixture well be thick) Stir, bring to a boil, then cover and remove from heat.
Let sit for 10 minutes, until all the liquid is absorbed.
