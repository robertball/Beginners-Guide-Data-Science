Title:
Cheesy Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless chicken breasts (about 4), cubed
Vegetable Oil or nonstick cooking spray
1 cup frozen corn kernals
1 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
2 cups chunky salsa (mild or medium)
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (can substitute reduced-fat cheddar)
8 - 10 Flour tortillas, warmed according to package directions

Directions:
Brown cubed chicken breast in non-stick skillet coated with non-stick cooking spray or vegetable oil.  Cook through, about 5 minutes.  Add frozen corn, black beans and salsa, cook until heated through. Add shredded cheddar and cook, stirring constantly, until cheese is melted and mixture is hot and bubbly.  Spoon into flour tortillas heated according to package directions and roll up burrito-style.

Submitter: 
Christine Hanson
Description: 
Full of chicken, cheese and veggies - on the table in 20 minutes! Easily modified to low-fat without sacrificing taste, and a hit with kids and men.
