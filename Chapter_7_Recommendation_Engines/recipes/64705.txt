Title:
Shortcut Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts water
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
1 (10 ounce) package frozen BOCA Meatless Italian Sausage, cut into 1/2-inch slices
2 medium carrots, sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup bow tie pasta, uncooked
Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
PLACE all ingredients except Parmesan cheese into stock pot or large Dutch oven.
BRING to a boil on medium-high heat, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 15 minutes or until carrots are tender, stirring occasionally.
SERVE sprinkled with Parmesan cheese, if desired.
