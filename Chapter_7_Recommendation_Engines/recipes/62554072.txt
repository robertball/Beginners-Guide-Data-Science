Title:
Mixed Fruit Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups mixed fruit
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 red tomatoes
1 tea spoon lemon juice

Directions:
Use leftover fruit from the breakfast table : such as apple,papaya,guava,grapes etc. Add a tomato or two and a little water . Cook on low heat or microwave till soft. When cool, Puree using a liquidizier and sieve through a strainer
to get soft pulp.
For one cup of this pulp,use 1 1/2 cups of sugar.
Take sugar in a heavy bottom pan. Add 1/4 cup of water. Mix well and boil till 2-thread consistancy. Add the mashed fruit and cook further for 5 minutes till mixure looks thick. Remove from heat Add lemon juice to adjust sweetness. Store in glass jar. Use within a week
