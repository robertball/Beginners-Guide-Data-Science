Title:
Gobble-Tini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987504.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Smirnoff Cranberry Vodka
1/4 ounce raspberry liqueur
1/2 ounce cranberry juice

Directions:
Fill shaker with ice.
Add Smirnoff Cranberry Vodka, raspberry liqueur, and cranberry juice.
Shake.
Strain into a martini glass.
Garnish with 3 cranberries on a stick.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Use Smirnoff Cranberry Vodka in this festive martini for the holidays.
