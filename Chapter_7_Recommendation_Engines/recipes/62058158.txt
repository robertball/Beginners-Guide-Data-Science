Title:
slow cooker wild rice chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup uncooked wild rice with seasoning packet (like uncle bens)      1/2 cup shredded carrot                 1 lb. cooked and shredded chicken       1/2 cup chopped onion                   6 cups or 3 cans 14 oz ea. chicken broth                                   1/2 cup diced celery                    1 cup sour cream                        1/2 cup flour

Directions:
Stir all except sour cream and flour into a slow cooker and cook low 6 hours. Rice and vegetables should be tender. In a separate pan take 2 cups of the broth from slow cooker and add the sour cream and flour to this. Whisk until smooth and return to the crockpot.  Cook on high 20 more minutes.  Freezes well.   If you want thinner just add more chicken broth!

Submitter:

Description: 
A warm and easy soup to make that I use often with leftover chicken and even turkey!
