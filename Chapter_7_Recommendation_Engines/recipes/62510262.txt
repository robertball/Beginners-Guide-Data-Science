Title:
SCD Skillet Chuck Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chuck roast
salt and pepper to taste
2 cloves garlic
2 bay leaves
2 sprigs rosemary
3 Carrots cut in large chunks
1 lb Mushrooms cut in halfs
2 onions quartered

Directions:
Salt and pepper roast to taste. Sear on all sides in electric skillet. Throw in carrots and onions around roast. Add water to about 1/2 way up in the skillet, throw in garlic, bay leaves and rosemary. Simmer for 1-2 hours and add mushrooms. Simmer 1-2 hours longer. Remove roast and vegetables from the pot and slice. Strain out bay leaves and rosemary and toss. Reserve broth for dipping if desired.
