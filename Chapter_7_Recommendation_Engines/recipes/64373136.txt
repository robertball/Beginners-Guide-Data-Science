Title:
Chocolate Buttermilk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick melted butter
2 eggs beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup oats( regular or quick)
3 1/2 tablespoons cocoa
pinch salt
2 unbaked regular 9 inch pie crusts

Directions:
mix together dry ingredients- add liquids - mix well(whisk is fine)- pour into unbaked pie shells- bake 25 minutes  @ 325- rotate pies- bake another 25 minutes- cool on wire rack- may top with ice cream- or whipped cream

Description: 
old fashioned buttermilk pie- only chocolate with a slightly crunch to the top-
