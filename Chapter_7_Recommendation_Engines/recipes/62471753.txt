Title:
Chinese Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/559695.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. shredded cabbage
4-5 green onions, chopped
2 Tbsp. sesame seeds
1 small package sliced almonds
1 package Ramen (any flavor)
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3 Tbsp. vinegar
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. pepper
Ramen seasoning packet

Directions:
Salad:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Crush Ramen noodles and place on a cookie sheet with sesame seeds and almonds; brown in the oven for 5 minutes.
Add noodles, seeds, and almonds to a bowl with cabbage and green onions.
Dressing:
Mix oil, vinegar, sugar, pepper and seasoning packet together.
Don't add dressing to salad until just before serving.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
This crunchy salad is a great with cabbage or bok choy.
