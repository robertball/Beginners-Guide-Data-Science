Title:
Pistachio Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 small package instant pistachio pudding
1 (8 oz) package cram cheese, softened
1 large carton cool whip, softened
2 cups miniature marshmallows
1 small can crushed pineapple
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix milk with pudding until well blended.  Add cream cheese and mix well.  Fold in cool whip until mixed well.  Add remaining ingredients and keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
My grandma used to make this salad at holiday time when I was little.  It's an old recipe but still just as good.
