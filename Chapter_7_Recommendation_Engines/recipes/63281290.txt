Title:
Olive Oil Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. large shrimp, peeled and de-veined
3 tbsp. chopped garlic
1/2 cup olive oil
4 tbsp. lemon juice
1&1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. red pepper flakes
3 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
1/4 cup white wine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Combine all ingredients in an oven safe saute pan and bake for 10 - 15 minutes or until shrimp is cooked through.  Can also be cooked on top of stove.
