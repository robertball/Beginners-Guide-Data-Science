Title:
Classic French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 1" slices of stale whole grain bread
3 eggs
1 cups of milk
2 Tbsp honey
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
2 Tbsp butter

Directions:
1. Heat honey in the microwave for about 15 seconds to loosen it up.
2. Beat honey with eggs, then add milk, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
3. Soak each slice of bread in the milk mixture for about 10 seconds on each side, set onto cookie sheet while you soak the other slices.
4. Cook over medium-low heat until brown on each side (about 4 minutes on each side)
5. Finish by laying on the rack in a 300* oven for 10 minutes to make sure the middle of each slice is cooked.

Submitter: 
paralee
Description: 
Always use this recipe when I find a nice chunk of stale bread in my pantry. Easy to make and people always rave about the rich and wholesome flavor.
