Title:
RITZ Egg Salad-Tomato Bites Recipe

Ingredients:
24 RITZ Crackers
3/4 cup egg salad
8 grape tomatoes, each cut into 6 slices
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Top crackers with egg salad, a slice of grape tomato, and sprinkle with chopped chives.
