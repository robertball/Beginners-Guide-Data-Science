Title:
Amy's Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/320068.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy Peanut Butter
1 cup salted butter (2 sticks)
1 lb confectioners sugar (3 3/4 cups)
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Line 8x8 pan with wax paper. Grease wax paper with unsalted butter.
Put the peanut butter and butter in a microwave approved bowl. Cook on high for 2 minutes. Stir and cook for 2 more minutes.
Remove from microwave. Add vanilla and confectioners sugar. Mix together till creamy.
Spoon into pan, spread out in pan, and let it cool. Enjoy!

Description: 
A super easy way to make peanute butter fudge using the microwave. Very rich and creamy. I make this fudge for every holiday. If you need a quick and easy fudge recipe, this is it!
