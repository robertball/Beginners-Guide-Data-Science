Title:
Herbed Florentine Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/346183.jpg
Ingredients:
1(16 oz.) bag frozen Florentine-style vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, carrots)
1/2 cup water
1/4 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
1/2 tsp. Italian herb seasoning
1/4 tsp. fresh ground black pepper
1 1/2 Tbsp. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, bring vegetables, water and salt to a boil over high heat.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer 6 - 8 minutes, or until vegetable are tender.  Drain.
In a small bowl, combine olive oil, herbs and pepper.  Pour mixture over vegetables, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and toss gently.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
This is a tasty way to fix a bag of frozen mixed vegetables.  It's healthy, fast and easy.  A perfect side dish for a hectic weeknight meal!
