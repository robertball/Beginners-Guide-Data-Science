Title:
Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Large Portobello Mushroom Caps
8 Cherry Tomatoes (diced)
1 Red Pepper (Diced)
1/2 Green Pepper (Diced)
1/2 a Red Onion (Diced)
4 Tbsp Italian Bread Crumbs
1 Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1/4 Cup Fresh Italian Parsley
To garnish
Marinade:
4 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
4 Tbsp Worchester sauce
4 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
4 Tbsp Red Wine
4 Cloves Garlic
2 Tsp. Fresh or Dry Basil
2 Tsp. Fresh or Dry Thyme
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the mushroom caps in a glass baking pan. Cut out the stems and set aside.
Prepare the marinade in a small bowl, Wisk together. Spoon mixture onto mushrooms, reserving about 1/4 cup for the filling.
Chop up the mushroom stems, peppers, and onion. Add veggies to a medium saucepan and saut√© on medium-high for about 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Then Add diced tomatoes and reserved marinade, simmer for an additional 5 minutes or until most excess liquid has cooked off.
Spoon mixture into mushroom caps. Sprinkle 1 Tbsp of bread crumbs, then 1/4 mozzarella cheese onto each cap.
Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees. Garnish with Italian parsley. Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
Portobello mushrooms are marinated with Italian herbs then stuffed with veggies and topped with cheese. This delicious gourmet inspired dish is sure to wow your guests and please your family. A great vegetarian option that is quick and easy to make. You could easily customize the filling with different vegetables like olives, artichokes, or sundried tomatoes. Yummm!
