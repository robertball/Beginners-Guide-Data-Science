Title:
Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Sugar
1 1/2 Cup Oil
4 Eggs
2 Cups Flour
2 tsp Baking Soda
2 tsp Cinnamon
3 Cups Grated Carrots

Directions:
Mix sugar & oil together. Add eggs mixing in one at a time. Add remaining dry ingredients and then the carrots. Mix until all is incorporated.
Pour batter into two 8" round paper lined & greased baking pans.
Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes. Test for doneness by inserting a knife into the center. If it comes out clean, it's done.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
One of my favorite carrot cake recipes. My family loves it so much that my kids ask for it for their birthday cake.
