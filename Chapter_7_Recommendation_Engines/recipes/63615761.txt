Title:
Quick Shrimp Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1 1/2 lb. Medium Fresh Shrimp With Tails
1 Stick Butter
1 or 2 Sliced Lemons
1 pkg. Italian Salad Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Line a baking sheet or jellyroll pan with foil.
Melt and spread butter over foil.  Arrange sliced lemons over the butter.  Arrange sprimp over the lemons and sprinkle the Italian Seasoning over all.  Bake for no longer than 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
ttkc
Description: 
So quick and summery.  No need to heat up the kitchen.  This shrimp is good as is, or used to top a ceasar-like salador serve as an appetizer.
