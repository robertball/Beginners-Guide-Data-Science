Title:
Cucumbers for freezer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123028.jpg
Ingredients:
7 cups peeled and sliced cucumbers
3 medium onions , sliced
1 green pepper, optional
1 cup white vinegar
2 cups sugar
1 T salt
1/2 t celery seed

Directions:
Combine 1st 3 ingredients in bowl.
Heat next 3 ingredients to boiling.
Mix all ingredients together and marinate for 3 to 5 hours.
