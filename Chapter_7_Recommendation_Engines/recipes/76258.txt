Title:
Spiced Apple Mulled Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984614.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups unsweetened apple cider
1/2 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
16 whole cloves
6 whole allspice
5 (3 inch) cinnamon sticks
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1/2 cup dried cranberries
8 thinly sliced oranges
8 thinly sliced lemons

Directions:
Combine cider and next 4 ingredients in a stock pot.   Cook over low heat 30 to 60 minutes. Do not boil. Add fruit slices 10 minutes before serving. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This delightful spiced apple cider will fill your home with a tantalizing smell. It is easy to make, and is a great hot drink for gatherings in the cold months.
