Title:
Savory Paella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988099.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (40 ounce) bag STOUFFER'S® frozen Skillet Sensations Savory Chicken & Rice Family Meal
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
1/8 teaspoon ground red pepper
1/2 teaspoon crushed saffron threads (optional)
1/2 pound fully cooked, cleaned medium-sized shrimp
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained

Directions:
HEAT olive oil in deep 12-inch skillet over medium heat. Add garlic; cook until aromatic. Add Savory Chicken & Rice, saffron and red pepper; prepare according to package instructions. Add shrimp and tomatoes; cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes or until hot.

Submitter: 
Stouffer's®
Description: 
The signature dish of the Spanish city of Valencia, paella brings together the most tender rice, the richest of stock and the freshest of seafood and chicken. Kissed by a few threads of saffron this Savory Paella will bring Valencia to your table.
