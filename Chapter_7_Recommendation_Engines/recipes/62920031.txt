Title:
Gourmet Beer Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz. Cream Cheese
10oz. Velveeta Cheese
1 Bottle Oktoberfest Beer room temperature
2 tsp ground mustard
3 cloves Garlic
4oz Smoked Gouda Cheese (4 Slices)
4oz Mozzarella Cheese (4 Slices)
4oz Smoked Provolone Cheese (4 Slices)
1TBSP Horseradish (or to Taste)
1 TBSP Worcestershire Sauce
1 TBSP Brown Sugar
Salt and Pepper to taste.

Directions:
Melt cream cheese and Velveeta over low heat.
Slowly stir in beer until thoroughly mixed.
Add remaining cheese while stirring continually.
Add reaming ingredients.
Serve with soft pretzels.

Submitter: 
Dustin Jay
Description: 
Perfect Beer Cheese Recipe for Oktoberfest!  Smoked Gouda adds the perfect touch.
