Title:
Italian Style Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of hawaiian rolls or italian bread
1/2 lb of honey ham (shaved thin)
1/2 lb of cooked ham (shaved thin)
1/2 lb of smoked ham (shaved thin)
1/2 bag of shredded cheese (more if desired)
1 stick of butter
1 package of Italian dressing mix (dry)

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees
Melt butter with the Italian dressing mix on the stove in a small pot.
Cut tops off bread and lay aside while putting the bottom pices on a baking sheet.
Layer different hams on bottom piece of bread then top with shredded cheese.
Replace the tops of bread and pour melted butter mixture on the bread.
Put in the oven until the cheese melts or bread is golden brown.
