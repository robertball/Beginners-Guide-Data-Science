Title:
Chicken Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken
3 eggs, sliced or mashed
1 cup chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 stick melted butter
1 cups bisquick
1 cups milk
1 egg
1 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Mix the chicken, eggs, broth, black pepper , and cream of chicken soup in a 2 quart or 8x8 casserole dish.
Mix the butter, bisquick, milk and egg together.
Pour this over the mixture.
Bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Bisquick mix and a few other ingredients make for a quick, warm meal.
