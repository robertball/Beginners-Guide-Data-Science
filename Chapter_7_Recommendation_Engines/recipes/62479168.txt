Title:
Olio Di Oliva Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/484446.jpg
Ingredients:
1 5oz. can Albacore Tuna packed in water
1/4 c. diced tomato
1/4 c. diced celery
1/8 c. diced Kalamata olives
1 t. capers
1/4 t. dry basil
1/4 t. dry oregano
1/4 t. dry parsley
1 T. olive oil
1 1/2 T. red wine vinegar
salt and cracked pepper to taste
2 t. pine nuts optional

Directions:
Drain canned tuna well.
Place in bowl and add rest of ingredients.
Toss gently to mix.
Chill or eat immediatley.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Tuna salad made with Olive Oil and Italian seasonings portioned to serve one that is easily increased to feed as many as desired. Quick and nutritious for a brown bagged lunch or summer dinner when it's too hot to cook. Enjoy over mixed greens or with a thick slice of crusty wholegrain bread.
