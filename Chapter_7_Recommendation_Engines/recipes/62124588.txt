Title:
Apple Crunch A la Mode Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759651.jpg
Ingredients:
6- peeled and thinly sliced tart apples (Granny Smith, Jonathan)
Syrup
1 C. sugar
1 C. cold water
1 TBSP cornstarch
1 tsp vanilla extract
Topping
1 C. flour
1 C. firmly packed brown sugar
3/4 C. old fashioned or quick-cooking rolled oats
1/2 C. butter, melted
1/2 C. chopped walnuts
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon

Directions:
Place sliced apples in greased 13x9 baking dish. In small saucepan, combine all syrup ingredients. Cook over med heat until clear. Pour hot syrup over fruit. In medium bowl, stir together all topping ingredients until crumbly. Spoon over fruit mixture. bake in 350 oven until top is brown and fruit is bubbling, about 1 hour. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.
