Title:
Chocolate Banana Split Drizzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3659822.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
In a microwave-safe bowl, combine cocoa, sugar, milk and butter.  Microwave 2 minutes, stirring 3 times.  Serve over ice cream.
