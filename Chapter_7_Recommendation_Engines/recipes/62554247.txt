Title:
Feta cheese ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I 8 oz. package cream cheese, room temperature
5 oz. feta cheese, crumbled
1/3 c. butter
3 green onions, chopped
About 20 black olives, sliced
! small package sliced almonds

Directions:
In a bowl, combine feta cheese, butter and cream cheese. It is easiest to use your hands to combine the ingredients.
Add black olives and green onions to the mixture.
Form mixture into a ball.
Sprinkle almonds onto ball and pat ball to secure almonds.
Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Sabryna Cornish
Description: 
This is decadent cheese ball that showcases the flavors of feta cheese. It always gets rave reviews when I bring it to parties.
