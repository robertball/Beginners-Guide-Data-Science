Title:
Iceberg Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Bottled Caesar or Creamy Peppercorn Dressing
6 Tablespoons Dill Pickle Relish
2 Tablespoons Cumin
2 Tablespoons Chili Powder
4 Cups Shredded Iceberg Lettuce
1 Cup Shredded Red Cabbage
1 Cup Shredded Carrots
Salt to Taste

Directions:
Combine the salad dressing and relish in a bowl. Add the cumin and chili powder and mix well. Add the lettuce, cabbage, carrots and salt; mix lightly. Chill for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sheila S.
Description: 
Looking for something different and lighter from your everyday slaw? This is it. Delicious and fresh - you won't have to worry about the leftovers because there won't be any! Best part - no mayo.
