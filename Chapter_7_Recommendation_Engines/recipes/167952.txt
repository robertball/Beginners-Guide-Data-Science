Title:
Texas-Style Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984388.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce, divided
1/3 cup chopped onion
1/3 cup chopped green chilies
6 hamburger buns, split
1/2 cup guacamole
3/4 cup KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese
3/4 cup chopped tomato

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium heat. Mix ground beef, 1/4 cup of the steak sauce, the onion and chilies; shape evenly into 6 (1/2-inch-thick) patties.
Grill patties, uncovered, 11 to 13 minutes or until cooked through (160 degrees F), turning patties occasionally and brushing with the remaining 1/4 cup steak sauce.
Serve in buns topped evenly with the guacamole, cheese and tomatoes.
