Title:
ABSOLUTELY THE BEST BEEF TENDERLION Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) beef tenderloin roast
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup melted butter
1 lb baby white mushrooms or baby portabela mushrooms
2 cloves garlic sliced
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup honey (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place roast into a shallow, glass baking dish. Pour soy sauce , melted butter over the tenderloin. place the sliced garlic on the tenderlion and drizzel the honey over the whole thing.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, then turn the roast over, and continue cooking 35 to 40 minutes, basting occasionally until the internal temperature of the roast is at 140 degrees F (60 degrees C) for medium. Or, cook to your desired degree of doneness. Let meat rest for 10 to 15 minutes before slicing.
