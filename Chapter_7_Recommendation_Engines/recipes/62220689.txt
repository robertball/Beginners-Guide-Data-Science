Title:
Cauliflower Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped celery
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed tomato soup, undiluted
1 1/4 cups water
1 1/4 cups chicken stock
2 cups fresh cauliflowerets
1/2 cup frozen peas
1/2 teaspoon dill weed
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a sauce celery in butter until tender. Stir in the remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 12-16 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

Submitter: 
Shauna Rhoads
Description: 
We usually have tomato soup at least once a week, so I've tried to find ways to dress it up. This recipe is one of my favorites.Modified from: Cauliflower Tomato Soup
