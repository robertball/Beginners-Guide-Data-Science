Title:
jello 7-up salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small pkg. lemon or lime jello
2 cups boiling water
2 cups 7-up
1 large can crushed pineapple( save juice)
2 cups mini marshmellos
2 large bananas thinly sliced
1 cup whipped cream
1/3 cup sugar
2 tablespoons flour
1 cup pinapple juice
2 egg

Directions:
dissove jello into 2 cups hot water. add 2 cups 7-up,let cool.chill but not firm in a 9x 12 pan.then add the banana, pineapple and marshmellows and let set.
to make top layer: in a small sauce pan mix sugar, beaten eggs, flour,and pinapple juice. heat at med heat until thick. cool completely
whip up the the whipping cream and fold into the pinapple juice mixture. spread on top of set jello salad
