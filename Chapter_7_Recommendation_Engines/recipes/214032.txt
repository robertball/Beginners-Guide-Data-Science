Title:
Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988477.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts water
1 1/2 cups Stevia Extract In The Raw® Cup For Cup
1 1/2 cups fresh squeezed lemon juice with pulp, seeded
Lemon slices
Fresh mint leaves for garnish
Ice cubes

Directions:
In 2 quart pitcher combine water, Stevia Extract In The Raw and lemon juice.  Stir well and chill.
Fill each tall glass with ice and lemonade.  Add lemon slices and fresh mint and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Stevia Extract In The Raw®
Description: 
A delicious, low calorie, easy-to-make drink for those hot summer days or any day.
