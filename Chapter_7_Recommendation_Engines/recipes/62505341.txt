Title:
Chocolate-Caramel Shortbread Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1+1/3 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup Splenda
2+1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup butter
1/2 cup apricot-pineapple (or other flavor) jam
1 cup packed light brown sugar
4 tablespoons light corn syrup
1 cup sweetened condensed milk
2+1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 C)
Line a 9 x 13 inch baking dish with nonstick foil and spray the foil with Pam for Baking.
In a medium bowl, mix together 1+1/3 cup butter, Splenda and flour until evenly crumbly.
Press into the baking dish.
Bake for 20 minutes.
Poke holes in shortbread and let cool completely in the freezer for about 10 minutes.
Microwave the jam until jam gets runny.
Spread jam over shortbread; let cool.
In a 2 quart saucepan, combine 1 cup butter, brown sugar, corn syrup, and sweetened condensed milk.
Bring to a boil, stirring constangly with a wooden spoon.
Continue to boil for 5 minutes, still stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and beat vigorously with the wooden spoon for about 3 minutes.
Pour over cooled crust.
Cool until caramel begins to firm.
Place chocolate and butter in a microwave-safe bowl.
Heat for 1 minute, then stir and continue to heat and stir at 20 second intervals until chocolate is melted and smooth.
Pour chocolate over the caramel layer and spread evenly to cover completely.
Let cool just until chocolate has set up but is still a bit soft.
Remove from dish, using the foil to lift it out.
Cut into 1 inch x 2 inch bars.
