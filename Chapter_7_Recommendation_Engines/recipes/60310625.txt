Title:
Zesty Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed tomato soup
1/4 cup instant rice
1/4 cup grated onion
salt and  pepper
1/2  pound lean ground turkey
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Lightly beat egg with a fork, then add a heaping tablespoon of the soup and mix lightly. Mix in rice, onion, , onion and pepper. Stir in the ground turkey and mix well with hands. From mixture into 1 1/2 inch round meatballs.
Bake in oven until meatballs are done.
In a skillet, combine remaining soup with Worcestershire and stir until smooth. Then add meatballs to skillet.. Cover with lid and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jodie Hoffman Crowe
Description: 
These meatballs are easy to make and a favorite around our house. Serve over hot rice.Modified from: Zesty Porcupine Meatballs
