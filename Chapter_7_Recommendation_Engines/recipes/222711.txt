Title:
Cream Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5107055.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, cubed
1 3/4 cups milk
1 tablespoon dry sherry
2 teaspoons dry mustard powder
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 pinch ground black pepper
1 pinch ground nutmeg

Directions:
Cook and stir cream cheese and milk in a saucepan over medium heat until cheese is almost melted, 5 to 7 minutes. Stir in sherry, mustard, garlic, pepper, and nutmeg; continue to cook and stir over medium heat until cream cheese is completely melted, about 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mei Xiang
Description: 
A fondue for cream cheese lovers! A dash of nutmeg and a splash of sherry make this fondue extra tasty.
