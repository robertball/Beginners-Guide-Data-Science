Title:
Texas Pit BBQ Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 to 5 pound center cut brisket
1 cup marinade (1/2 c. olive oil, 1/2 c. balsamic vinegar, 2 teas. Dijon mustard, 1 clove garlic, salt and pepper, 1 teas. dried parsley, 1 teas. dried cilantro) or Italian dressing
1 1/2 cups Texas Pit BBQ Sauce
1-2 teaspoons fajita seasonings OR salt and pepper
1 tablespoon flour
1 large onion, sliced
1 large browning bag

Directions:
Pour 1/2 cup marinade in shallow (9x13) dish
Place brisket in dish, pour remaining marinade over it
Cover tightly and place in fridge for 12 hours, turning several times
After marinating, remove brisket from dish, discard marinade and wash dish
Set oven to 250Â°F
Put flour in bag and shake; place in 9x13 dish
Pour 1/2 cup of BBQ sauce into bottom of bag, mixing w/ flour
Layer onion slices on top of sauce
Place brisket in bag; pour remaining BBQ sauce over brisket and sprinkle with seasonings
Close bag and puncture with fork as directed on package.
Place in oven and bake 4-6 hours or until brisket is fork tender.
Slice across the grain
