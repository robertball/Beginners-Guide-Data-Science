Title:
Broccoli & Cauliflower Hamburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce)package frozen broccoli & cauliflower mix
1/2 pound ground beef
1 Tbsp minced onions
1 (8 ounce) package fresh mushrooms
salt & pepper to taste
2 Tbsp Greek Yogurt
1 large tomato
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Prepare the broccoli/cauliflower according to package directions.
In a skillet over medium heat, mix the ground beef, and onion. Cook until beef is evenly brown; drain grease. Season with salt and pepper and set aside.
In saute pan, melt 1 tbs butter and add mushrooms.  Cook til tender - add cut up tomato and cook til soft.  Add greek yogurt til creamy.
Mix hamburger and vegetables together and pour into casserole dish.
Top with cheese.
Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly brown.
