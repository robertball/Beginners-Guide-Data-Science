Title:
Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Cups Yellow Squash
1 Large Vidalia Onion
2-3 Med-Large Tomatos
3 Tbsp. Olive Oil
1 Tsp. Italian Seasoning
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Chop Yellow Squash into bite-size pieces.  Dice the Onion (small)
Dice Tomato into small pieces.
Place all three vegetables into a mixing bowl.  Pour in the olive oil, italian seasoning and salt and pepper.
Mix Well
Pour into a casserole dish and cover with heavy duty aluminum foil.
Place in oven and cook for 2-3 hours.  The longer you cook it, the better it turns out!! This recipe is totally goof-proof!

Description: 
mmm, comfort food at its best!!  Seasonal yellow squash, Fresh Vine Tomatos, Vidalia Onions and Olive Oil all bake together for a delicious and hearty side dish.
