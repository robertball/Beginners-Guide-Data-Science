Title:
Naan Margarita Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pieces Naan or Garlic Naan
Crushed garlic
Olive oil
3 Campari tomatoes (or 1 regular tomato)
salt & pepper
8 slices of fresh mozzarella (about 3" in diameter and 1/8" thick)
fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Place Naans on cookie sheet and spread on 1 Tsp crushed garlic on each piece. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
Place tomato slices on and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place mozzarella on top.
Put under hot broiler for 5 - 10 minutes until cheese has melted and naan is golden brown near edges. Top with torn basil leaves as soon as you remove from oven and add a last drizzle of olive oil.
