Title:
Mindless Meatball Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14.5 ounce cans chicken broth
1 14.5 ounce can beef broth
1 pound frozen mixed vegetables
1 18 ounce bag frozen meatballs
1 14.5 ounce can stewed tomatoes
1/2 cup dry macaroni
1 15 ounce can light red kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
6 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large Dutch oven or stock pot, heat the chicken and beef broth. Add the frozen vegetables and meatballs while they are still frozen. Add canned tomatoes and macaroni. Cover the pot and cook until it boils (about 10 minutes). Uncover and stir well. Add kidney beans and Italian seasoning. Reduce heat to medium and until macaroni is tender (about 6-7 minutes). Stirring frequently. Garnish with Paremesan cheese and serve. Makes 6 single-bowl servings.
