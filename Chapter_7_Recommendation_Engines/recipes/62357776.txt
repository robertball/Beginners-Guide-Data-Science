Title:
Caramel Apple Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1.5 cups sugar
1 1/4 cups vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cups Silver Palate Oatmeal
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
4 cups chopped, peeled apples
1/2 cup Kraft Carmel bits
TOPPING:
1/2 cup Kraft Carmel bits

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat eggs until foamy; gradually add sugar. Blend in oil and vanilla. Combine flour, Oats, salt and baking soda; add to egg mixture. Stir in apples and Carmel Bits. Pour into a greased 10-in. tube pan; bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until the cake tests done. Cool in pan on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Remove cake to a serving platter.
For topping, Sprinkle Carmel Bits over warm cake
