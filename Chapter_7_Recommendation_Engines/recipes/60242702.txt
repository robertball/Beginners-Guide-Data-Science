Title:
Orginal Marbled Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. cream cheese (I use 8 oz. pkg.)
5 T. butter
1/3 c. sugar
2 eggs
2 T. flour
3/4 t. vanilla
1 pkg. family size brownie mix
+ the ingredients called for on the mix box

Directions:
Soften the cream cheese and butter; beat together.
Agg the sugar, eggs, flour and vanilla; beat until smooth and set aside.
Preheat oven to 350&#0176;
Prepare the brownie batter as directed on the package for the cake-like brownies.
Pour half the brownie batter into a greased 9x13 pan.
Pour all the cream cheese mixture over the brownie layer.
Spoon the remaining brownie batter in spots over the top.
Swirl the two mixtures together w/ a knife.
Bake at 350&#0176; for 35-40 minutes or until done.
Cool and frost with Easy Milk Chocolate Frosting:
Melt 3 T. butter in a medium saucepan.  Stir in 2 T. cocoa until dissolved.  Add 1 1/2 c. confectioner's sugar, 2 T. milk and 1 t. vanilla. Stir until smooth.  Add more milk if needed to make a soft spreading consistency.  Frost brownies and let set until firm.
