Title:
Carlene's Gooey Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/967008.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Yellow Cake Mix
1 stick Margarine
3 eggs
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 lb Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Mix yellow cake mix, margarine, and 1 egg. Press into a 9x13 pan.
In a seperate bowl mix sour cream, powdered sugar (minus 2 tbsp), and 2 eggs. Pour this on top of cake mixture.
Bake on 350 degrees for 45 minutes. When you pull it out of the oven sprinkle with 2 tbsp powdered sugar.
