Title:
Poor Man's Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. polish sausage (or your favorite kind)
1 medium onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
2 qts. low sodium chicken broth/stock
1/2 lb. carrots, sliced thick
1 (14 oz.) can kidney beans
1 small head cabbage, coarsely chopped

Directions:
In a large stock pot, brown sausage over medium-high heat.
Add onions, continuing to cook over medium heat until they begin to caramelize.
Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 20 minutes or until carrots and cabbage are tender.

Description: 
A quick and satisfying dish with just a few ingredients.  Easy to prepare and tastes great served with cornbread or crusty rolls.
