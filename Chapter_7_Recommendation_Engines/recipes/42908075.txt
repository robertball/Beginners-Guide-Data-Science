Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lb Chuck or other inexpensive cut of steak
2 med onions
2 16oz Can's of stewed tomatoes
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut the steak into palm sized pieces and place in crock pot
Lightly Salt and Pepper (don't need much in a crockpot)
Slice onions and place on top
Pour cans of tomatos over the top
Stir
Cover and cook on low for 8-12 hours or on high for 6-8. Until steak is tender.
(optional)You can thicken the gravy by using 1/4 cup milk and 1/4 flour in a shaker to blend, and then pour into the mixture about an hour before completion.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
Made up in a crockpot this is an easy to through together main meal.
