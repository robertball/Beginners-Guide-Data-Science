Title:
Party Potatoe Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-5 lb. bag of potatoes
2-8 oz. pkgs. of cream cheese
1-16 oz, container of sour cream
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper

Directions:
Prepare potatoes as you would for mashed.
Drain and mash. Add next 6 ingredients and mash well. Pour mixture into a greased 3 qt. casserole dish. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Just before baking, sprinkle with paprika and dot with butter. Bake @ 350 for 45 minutes.
