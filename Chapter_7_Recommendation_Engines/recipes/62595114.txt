Title:
Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb roast
1T garlic- powder or minced
1T oregano
5T lemon juice
2T seasoned salt
1T salt
5 bay leaves
1c water
1T worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Place roast in crock pot.
Cover with bay leaves.
Combine all other ingredients and pour over roast.
Cook all day on low.
