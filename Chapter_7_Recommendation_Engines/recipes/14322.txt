Title:
Coleslaw with a Difference! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/254568.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix
1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup salsa

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine coleslaw, seasoning salt, lemon juice, olive oil, mayonnaise and salsa. Toss well and serve.

Submitter: 
Susan Mercy
Description: 
Coleslaw like you've never had it before.  You'll come back for more! A perfect accompaniment to any Mexican or Tex-Mex meal.
