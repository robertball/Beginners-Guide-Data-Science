Title:
Sweet Onion Potatoes Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519136.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter
1 large or 2 medium sweet onions, thinly sliced
Salt & pepper
½ tsp. ground thyme (or dried if ground isn’t available)
1 bay leaf, fresh or dried
2 lbs. baby Yukon Gold potatoes or fingerling potatoes
½ to 2/3 c. cream
½ c. grated Parmigiano-Reggiano chees
½ lb. grated Swiss cheese

Directions:
Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.  Add onions.  Seaon with salt, pepper, thyme and bay leaf.  Cook until very soft, sweet and lightly caramelized, about 20 minutes.  Remove bay leaf.
While onions cook, place potatoes in pot and cover with water.  Bring to a boil, salt the water and cook until tender 12-15 minutes.  Drain and return potatoes to pot.  Mash the potatoes with cream, season with salt and pepper and mix in the Parmigiano cheese.
Preheat broiler.  Arrange the potatoes in shallow casserole dish.  Top with onions and cheese.  Brown cheese under hot broiler until bubbly, about 2 minutes.
