Title:
Lamb Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Linquini
4 Lbs. Ground Lamb (8 Fat Patties)
1 head fresh garlic (peeled and crushed)
1 T. Onion Powder
3 T. Lemon Juice
1/2 Tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 Tsp. Oregano
4 sm. or 3 md. or 1 Xlg potato (peeled and chopped into 1/4 dice)
Water
Salt and Pepper to taste
Olive Oil

Directions:
Brown ground lamb in olive oil for 10 minutes while adding the crushed garlic and onion powder. Add the lemon juice, cinnamon and oregano. Continue to brown until the liquid that has been created cooks off and the meat begins to stick to the pan. BROWN WELL Add water and diced potatos. Simmer until potatos are mushie and start to thicken the broth. Salt and pepper to taste at this point. Crush the potatos against the side of pot to hasten the thickening process.
Cook linguini al dente.   Serve lamb gravy over serving of linquini.

Submitter: 
Summer
Description: 
White sauce, Lamb based served over Linguini. Something that came from my Grandfather's family.
