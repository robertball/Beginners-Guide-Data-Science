Title:
Instant Pot Pinto Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb dried pinto beans
7 cups water (or chicken broth)
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp thyme
1 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
other seasonings of choice
1/2 onion

Directions:
Put water (or broth), beans, onion and seasonings into Instant Pot. Set to manual high pressure for 45 min. Let the pressure naturally release. Take the lid off and allow to set for about 10 min.
