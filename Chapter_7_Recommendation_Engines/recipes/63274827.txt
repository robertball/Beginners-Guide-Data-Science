Title:
Natalie's Perfect Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large lemons, juiced
5 ½ c. cold water
2 T. lemon concentrate
1 c. sugar
Ice cubes

Directions:
Mix together lemon juice and concentrate & add ½ of water. (2 ¼ c.)
Mix in sugar.
Add rest of water and desired number of ice cubes. Enjoy!
Note: if you would prefer not to add ice cubes, refrigerate for 1-3 hours.

Submitter: 
Natalie Herr
Description: 
This is the perfect lemonade for any occasion!
