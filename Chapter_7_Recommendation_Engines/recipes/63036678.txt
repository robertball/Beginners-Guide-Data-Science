Title:
Cowboy Oatmeal Cookies - High Altitude Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup canola oil
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 1/2 cups quick cooking oats
1/2 cup butterscotch chips
1 cup walnut pieces

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
In a medium bowl, cream butter, oil, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time. Gradually stir in the sifted ingredients until well blended. Mix in oats, nuts and butterscotch chips. Drop from a teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in preheated oven, or until edges are golden. Let set up on the cookie sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
