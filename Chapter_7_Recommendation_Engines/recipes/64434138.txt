Title:
Moroccan Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2340793.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbl lemon juice
2 Tbl olive oil
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp chill powder
4 large carrots
1/2 cup raisins
1 16 oz. can garbanzo beans, drained & rinsed
salt & pepper to taste
1/4 cup feta cheese

Directions:
Peel carrots and either thinly shave or grate.  Add raisins and beans. Whisk remaining ingredients in a small bowl.  Add to to carrots and allow to marinate 2 hours.  Serve with crumbled feta cheese.
