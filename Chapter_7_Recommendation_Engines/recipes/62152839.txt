Title:
Ramen Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pk uncooked ramen noodles w/ season package (original flavor)
½ head cabbage - sliced
1 med red bell pepper  chopped
½ c toasted walnuts  chopped
4 green onions  chopped
2 TB sesame seeds
½ c soy beans
½ c olive oil
1 tsp sesame oil
5 TB sugar
3 TB rice wine vinegar
1 tsp soy sauce

Directions:
Mix the olive oil, sesame, oil, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, and ramen seasoning packet and chill until serving
Toss the remaining ingredients with dressing right before serving.

Submitter: 
tristin
Description: 
This salad is addictive.  It is the perfect balance of sweet, sour, and savory.
