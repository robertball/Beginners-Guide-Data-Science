Title:
Cappuccino Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups plain flour
1 cup caster sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon mixed spice
1/4 cup strong coffee
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180 degrees C
Place all ingredients into a large bowl and mix well.  Turn onto a floured bowl and knead until smooth.
Roll dough into a log 20 cms long and place on a lined baking tray.  Bake 35 mins.
Cool slightly and cut into biscuits 1cm slices.  Place cut side down on tray and bake again for 15 mins or until golden.  Cool and serve.
