Title:
Chocolate Cherry Christmas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2833246.jpg
Ingredients:
crushed ice
2 fluid ounces vanilla-flavored vodka
2 fluid ounces black cherry juice
1 fluid ounce triple sec
1/2 fluid ounce chocolate liqueur (such as GodivaÂ®)
1 cherry

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add vodka, cherry juice, triple sec, and chocolate liqueur. Cover the shaker and shake until cocktail is chilled. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a cherry.

Submitter: 
J2HAYNES
Description: 
This drink tastes just like a chocolate-covered cherry! Very simple to make. I used organic unsweetened black cherry juice.
