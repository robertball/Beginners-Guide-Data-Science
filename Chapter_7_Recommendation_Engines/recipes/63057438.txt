Title:
Garlic Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (6 ounce) salmon fillets
garlic oil to marinate
1 red onion, sliced
1 teaspoon dried dill
cayenne to taste
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to Broil. Arrange the salmon skin side down in the prepared pan,Place onion slices on the salmon fillets. Season with dill, cayenne, salt, and pepper.
Bake 4 to 6 minutes each side, or until salmon is easily flaked with a fork.
