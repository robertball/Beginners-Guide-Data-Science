Title:
Taco Seasoning by Deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T dried minced onion
2 T chili powder*
1 T cornstarch
1 T minced garlic
1 T ground cumin
1/2 T dried oregano
1/2 T  cayenne pepper
1/2 T crushed red peppers

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine all of the ingredients. 1 T = taco mix package.  YStore in an airtight container in a cool dry place for up to 1 year.

Submitter: 
jakerm1985
Description: 
Our Test Kitchen developed this spicy, salt-free alternative to packaged taco seasoning. Stir it into dips or ground beef, sprinkle on chicken or wherever you want a Southwestern flair.Modified from: Taco Seasoning
