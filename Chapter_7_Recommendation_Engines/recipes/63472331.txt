Title:
Chicken Caesar Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cups of pasta of choice, cooked and drained
2 cups of cooked, shredded chicken, store bought rotisserie works well
1 cup of creamy style Caesar salad dressing
1/2 cup of chicken broth
1/3 cup green onion, finely sliced
1/4 cup red bell pepper, finely diced
1 1/2 cups shredded Italian cheese blend
1 cup of croutons
1/4 cup of shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mx all ingredients EXCEPT the croutons and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl. Turn into a greased 2 quart casserole dish. Top with the croutons and shredded Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for about 20 minutes, or until casserole is heated thru and top is golden.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
A quick Caesar style chicken casserole.
