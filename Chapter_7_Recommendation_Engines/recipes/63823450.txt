Title:
Easy Hamburger Mushroom Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef, browned and drained
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 c. macaroni
Seasoning to taste (herbs, salt, pepper, cayenne)
1 c. frozen peas
1 1/2 c. water

Directions:
Brown and drain hamburger.  Cook macaroni until tender, drain.  Combine all ingredients in a glass casserole dish and stir well, seasoning to taste.  Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes or until heated completely through.
