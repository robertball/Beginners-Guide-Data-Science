Title:
Summer Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 packages imitation crab chunks
1 cup celery, chopped
1 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
1/2 cup radish, chopped
1 cup Helman's mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon Bon Apetit or Beaumonde seasoning

Directions:
Using fork or small knife, shred crab chunks into a large bowl.  Add celery, onion, green pepper and chopped radish and mix well.  In separate bowl, mix Helman's, sour cream and seasoning with whisk.  Add to crab mixture and mix well.  Refrigerate overnight.  Can be served on lettuce leaf for presentation.

Submitter: 
SUSANHOR
Description: 
Everyone always assumes this salad is a coleslaw and are really surprised to find out it is a crab salad.  I always have requests for the recipe.  Best served if prepared the day before.
