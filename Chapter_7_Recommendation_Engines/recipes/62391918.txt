Title:
Chicken and Green Chili Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/425416.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup of all purpose flour
1 teaspoon of salt
1/2 teaspoon of pepper
2 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken breast
1/4 cup of olive oil
5 4.5 oz cans of green chilies
1/2 cup of salsa
12 8 inch flour tortillas
shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cut the chicken breast into small cubes using kitchen sheers or knife.
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the flour, salt and pepper.
Add chicken cubes and shake until evenly coated.
In a large saucepan or skillet, cook chicken in oil over medium heat until thoroughly cooked.
Add the chilies and salsa.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover and let simmer for 30 minutes.
Spoon about 1/2 cup on each tortilla.
Sprinkle with cheese and roll into burrito.
Serve alone or with desired sides.
