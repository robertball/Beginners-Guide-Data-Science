Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz package of button mushrooms
1 8oz package of cream cheese
1/2 pound sage sausage
1 small onion, diced
1/4 cup italian style bread crumbs
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.
Fry sausage until cooked through and crumbled.
Add diced onion to skillet.
Remove stems from mushrooms and chop.
Add chopped stems to sausage.
Sautee for 1-2 minutes, reduce heat to low, add cream cheese and remove from heat.
Mix well, transfer to mixer if necessary.
Spray a 9x13 pan with non-stick spray, fill each mushroom cap until slightly mounded.
Place stuffed caps in pan close together.
Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Spray aluminum foil with non-stick spray and place over mushrooms.
Bake, covered 25 minutes, remove foil and bake for an additional 8-10 minutes, or until bubbling and mushrooms are cooked through.
