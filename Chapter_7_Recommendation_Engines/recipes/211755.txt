Title:
Mixed Greens with Mandarins and Thai Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991672.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/4 cup VH® Sweet Thai Chili Sauce
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon VH® Soya Sauce
8 cups mixed greens
1 cup canned mandarin oranges, drained
1 cup red pepper, finely diced
1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
1/2 cup slivered almonds, toasted

Directions:
In a large bowl whisk together lime juice, chili sauce, vegetable oil and VH(R) Soya sauce.
Add the rest of the ingredients, toss well to coat with vinaigrette and serve.

Submitter: 
VH® Sauce
Description: 
This salad has loads of flavours and the kids will love the sweet and tangy vinaigrette.
