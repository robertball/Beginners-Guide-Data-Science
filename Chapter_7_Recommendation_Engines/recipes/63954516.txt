Title:
Scrambled Eggs with Assorted Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097001.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup peppers (green, red and/or orange), chopped
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
2 eggs
2 teaspoons water
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
2 teaspoons snipped chives
ground black pepper, to taste
ground sea salt, to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium high heat. Add chopped peppers and garlic. Stir fry for about 2 to 3 minutes, until peppers just begin to soften.
Wisk together the eggs, water, Parmesan cheese, chives, pepper and salt.
Add eggs to peppers. Reduce heat to medium. Cook, lifting cooked eggs, until eggs are set but still slightly moist.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Assorted peppers add flavor and color to this simple, tasty and healthy scrambled egg dish.
