Title:
Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bag Orida Southern Style Hashbrowns
1 Small Onion (Diced
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 16 oz Sour Cream
2-3 Cups Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
Topping
1 Stick Butter (Melted)
3 Cups Crushed Corn Flakes

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Place in a greased glass baking dish and cover overnight. Bake at 350 Degrees for approximately 45 to 1 hour then add the corn flake butter topping and bake for another 15 mins.
