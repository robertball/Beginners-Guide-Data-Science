Title:
Baked Curry Sweet Potato Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/340802.jpg
Ingredients:
4 sweet potatoes (scrubbed clean)
3 Tbs olive oil
2 Tbs curry powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Cut potatoes lengthwise and then quarter each half.
In a large bowl combine olive oil and curry powder.  Add potatoes and toss to coat.
Spread potatoes on a foil lined cookie sheet.  Drizzle any remaining oil evenly over potatoes.
Place in preheated oven and cook for 15 minutes before taking potatoes out of oven to flip.  Cook for an additional 15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Curry goes really well with sweet potatoes.
