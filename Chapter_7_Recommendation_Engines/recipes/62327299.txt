Title:
beef chimichanga Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 coles garlis
1/2 taco seasoning
1 tsp. oregano
1 4oz can green chilies
1/4 c sour cream
2 tbs. vinegar
1/4 c margerine
1 c cheddar cheese
1 small onion chopped
6 large tortillas

Directions:
Brown hamberger with onions and garlic, taco seasoning,oregano.  Stir in chilies, sour cream, and vinegar.  Melt margarine in large fry pan, Dip tortillas one at a time untill soft usually about 30 second.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet and fill 1/3 full with beef and cheese mix, fold envelope style and place on cookie sheet seam side down.  Bake at 450 for 15 minutes until crisp.  Top with shreddded cheese, lettace, tomato, salsa and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
The family requests this all the time.  It is easy for my husband to make when I have to work late.
