Title:
Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon fresh cilantro
4 (6 ounce) salmon fillets
2 teaspoons olive oil
2 garlic cloves, chopped
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a small dish, combine the Old bay, pepper and cilantro. Sprinkle over salmon. In a nonstick skillet, cook salmon in oil over medium heat for 4 minutes on each side. Add the garlic and cayenne pepper. Reduce heat; cover and cook 3-4 minutes longer or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
