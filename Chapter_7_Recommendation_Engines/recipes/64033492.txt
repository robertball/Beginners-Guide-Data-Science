Title:
Ratatouille Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C. olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
3 ribs celery, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 large eggplant, peeled and chopped
1/2 lb mushrooms, sliced
1 lb. zucchini, sliced
1 28oz can chopped tomatoes (or 6-8 fresh)
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp tomato paste if tomatoes are not sweet enough

Directions:
1. In a deep Dutch Oven, Sauté onion, celery, bell pepper and jalapeno until crisp tender (about 10 minutes.  Add garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
2. Add eggplant and mushrooms and cook 10 minutes .  Add zucchini and cook 5 minutes.
3.  Stir in herbs, salt and pepper.
4. Scald and peel tomatoes, chop and add to pot. Add broth if necessary.  Cover and cook on low heat 20-30 minutes (stirring frequently).
5. Alternative: place in casserole and top with cheese.  Bake 350 for 20-30 minutes.
6.  I serve with polenta.

Submitter: 
Ruth Rae
Description: 
Great vegetarian dish for summer.  Use the veggies from the garden.
