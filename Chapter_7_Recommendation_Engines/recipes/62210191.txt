Title:
Kaub Hawaiian Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1-inch pork chops
1/4 cup olive oil
1 medium onion cut into strips
2 tablespoons garlic
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 cup vinegar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 9oz can crushed pineapple
1 tablespoon soy sauce
3 tablespoon brown sugar
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Mix vinegar, ketchup, pineapple, soy sauce and brown sugar in bowl & set aside.
Dredge pork chops in flour.
Heat oil in skillet, and brown pork chops in oil. Remove from pan & set aside.
In skillet, saute onions & garlic until onions are soft approx 5 min.
Add white wine to scrap off soux from bottom of pan. Reduce liquid to half.
Return pork chops to pan, then pour ketchup mixture over pork chops.
Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour & 15 min. Check internal temperature of pork to reach 160 degrees.
If more liquid is needed, add chicken stock to mixture.
