Title:
"Casey Dias" Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/406979.jpg
Ingredients:
6  10inch flour tortillas
1 can of Frito Lay bean dip
1 package shredded cheddar cheese
Taco sauce
garlic and onion powder, to taste
2 tsp. butter, margarine or oil

Directions:
Prepare 3 tortillas with a thin layer of bean dip.
On top of the dip add a little taco sauce,garlic and onion powder.
Sprinkle with enough cheese to cover.
Add another tortilla to the tops.
Over medium heat melt butter in skillet.
Work it around so it covers skillet surface.
Place each quesadilla in skillet and brown on both sides.
You may have to add more butter to brown each quesadilla.
Remove from skillet and cut into triangles(like a pizza) and serve.
