Title:
Quick Mango Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6815394.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen mango chunks
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
2 packets artificial sweetener (such as TruviaÂ®), or to taste
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine frozen mangoes, almond milk, sweetener, and vanilla extract in a high-capacity blender, such as a Vitamix(R). Blend until mixture has the consistency of ice cream, 45 to 60 seconds.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Cool and refreshing. Ready in less than 5 minutes and you don't need an ice cream maker. Plus you can make a mango smoothie with this same recipe! And it's 'skinny'.
