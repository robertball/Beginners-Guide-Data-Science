Title:
Muscovy Duck Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup diced onion
1/3 cup diced carrot
1/3 cup diced celery
1/3 cup diced mushrooms
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup cooked Muscovy duck, diced
1 medium potato, peeled and diced
4 cups chicken stock
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 Table spoon olive oil

Directions:
Heat oil in bottom of small stock pot.  Saute onion, carrot and celery until onion starts to turn a little translucent.  Add garlic and mushroom. Saute for 2-3 minutes.  Add in Muscovy and potato.  Add stock.  Bring to a simmer and cook until the carrots are done, About 30 minutes.
