Title:
Cheesy Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. whole-wheat penne
2 C 1/2-in. cauliflower florets
1 T extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 C finely chopped onion
1/2 C dry white wine
3 C low-fat milk
3 T flour
3/4 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1 C shredded Gruyere or Swiss cheese
3 C shredded cooked chicken or turkey (12 oz)
1 t Dijon mustard
2 T chopped fresh chives or scallion greens

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 5 min. Add cauliflower florets and cook until pasta and florets are tender, about 4 min. more. Drain, rinse and return to pot.
Meanwhile, heat oil in large saucepan over med. heat. Add onion and cook, stirring, until tender, 2-3 min. Add wine and cook until reduced slightly, about 1 min. Whisk milk, flour, salt and pepper together in med. bowl and add to pan. Bring to boil over med-high heat, stirring frequently. Cool, stirring, until thickened, about 1 min. Reduce heat to low and stir in cheese until smooth. Stir in chicken and mustard into cheese sauce; cook until heated through, about 2 min.
Stir sauce into drained pasta and cauliflower. Serve sprinkled with chives.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This ooey-gooey pasta dish is a crowd-pleaser-think rich and creamy macaroni-and-cheese tossed with cauliflower and chicken. Serve with a spinach salad.
