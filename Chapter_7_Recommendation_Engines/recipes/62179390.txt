Title:
Hermit cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289907.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shortning
2 cups light brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
1/2 cup cold coffee
3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 1/2 cup raisins
1 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
cream shortning and sugar in a mixer until light. Add eggs and coffee and mix well.
add flour, baking soda, salt, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Mix well and scrape down sides of bowl.
Add rasins and nuts. Mix well.
Put cookie dough in refrigerator for several hours or overnight.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
grease cookie sheets
Drop dough by rounded teaspoon full 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheets.
Bake 8-10 minutes until just set.
Let cool on wire racks.
