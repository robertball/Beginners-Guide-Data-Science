Title:
Creamy Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6502255.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. box orzo pasta
1 T olive oil
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 c. heavy cream
1/2 c. shredded parmesan or parmesan/asiago/romano cheese
Cracked black pepper and sea salt to taste
1 cup reserved cooking liquid from pasta, or chicken broth

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and rinse, reserving pasta liquid.
Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat. Saut√© garlic. Stir in cream, bring to near boil.
Remove from heat and add orzo and cheese.  Stir until thoroughly blended.  Add some of the reserved pasta water or broth a bit at a time until it reaches desired creaminess.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
