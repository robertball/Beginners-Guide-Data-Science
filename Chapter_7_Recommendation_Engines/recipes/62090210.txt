Title:
Creamy Sausage Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box bow-tie noodles, boiled as   directed and drained
1 jar of alfredo sauce
4-5 Zummo's sausage, sliced
1 green bell pepper
1 med. yellow onion,chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 can chicken broth
1 can Rotel tomatoes, mild
1 tsp. olive oil
1 tsp of dried rosemary
1/2 tsp itailian seasoning
1 tbsp garlic powder
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium pan, heat oil and saute the onion garlic and bell pepper over medium heat. Add sausage,stir until well blended. Add the chicken broth, and can of rotel tomatoes. Let simmer for about 2-3 minutes.
Add the rosemary, italian seasoning,and Garlic powder. Stir until well blended. Let it simmer on medium heat until sausage is tender.
Then, slowly pour the sausage mixture over the noodles and mix. Once mixed, slowly add the alfredo sauce and mix well. Season with the salt and pepper to taste.
