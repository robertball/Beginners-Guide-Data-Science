Title:
French Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C water
1/4 C oj concentrate
1/2 C apple juice concentrate
1/2 C lemon juice
3 Tbls tomato puree
1 tsp. each: dill weed
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. paprika
1 1/2 Tbls salt (?? tsp ??)
1 1/2 Tbls cornstarch dissolved in 1/4 C water.

Directions:
Put all except cornstarch water in pan, bring to boil, add cornstarch water and cook 1 min. Chill, lasts for several months in fridge. I just made this. Really good. From Best Gourmet Recipes.
