Title:
Pork Tinga Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/634743.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds boneless pork spareribs
1 medium onions, quatrered
2 bay leaves
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 meduim onion, sliced
5 roma tomatoes, diced
4 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, 4 tablespoon adobo sauce (freeze extra)
8 guajillo chiles
1 chicken bouillon cube
1/2 teaspoon mexican oregano
7 cups of water
1/2 teaspoon latin inspired spice mix (or cumin)
salt and pepper to taste
Toppings:
22 tostada shells
shredded lettuce
sliced radishes
sliced avocado
queso fresco
mexican crema

Directions:
In a medium sized pot: place pork, 7 cups of water, chicken bouillon, 2 garlic cloves, bay leaves and 1 quartered onion. Cover and simmer for about 1-1/2 hours on medium to high heat. Reserve the stock, discard bay leaves. Remove pork and to cool and then shred it.
While pork is cooking place guajillo chiles into warm water and let soak.  Once pork is done remove stems and seeds from chiles and place them into the blender.  Add the chipotle chiles and adobo.
Add diced tomatoes and sliced onion to stock and cook about 10 minutes until tomatoes are tender.  Add remaining stock and tomatoes to the blender.  Blend until smooth.  Add puree to stock pot.
Shred pork and add to the puree.  Add latin spice mix and mexican oregano.  Simmer on low heat for about 30 minutes.  Salt and pepper to taste.
To assemble place meat mixture onto tostada, top with queso fresco, mexican crema, lettuce, avocado and radishes.

Description: 
This authentic Mexican recipe I picked up while having my in laws stay with me. It has a spicy, smoky flavor from chipotle peppers. It is perfect for a buffet style lunch or dinner! You can adjust the peppers to your taste.
