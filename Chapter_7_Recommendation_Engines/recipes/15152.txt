Title:
Whole Wheat Bread III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5210399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
1 1/2 tablespoons instant powdered milk
1/3 cup honey
3 tablespoons margarine, softened
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3 cups whole wheat flour
1/4 cup cornmeal
2 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients into the pan of the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Press Start.

Submitter: 
BENNETT.MAYO
Description: 
Sweet whole wheat bread with a crunch of corn meal, dark crust and best of all, my kids love it!
