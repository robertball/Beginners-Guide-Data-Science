Title:
My  Sweet Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups cold mashed sweet potatoes (without added milk and butter)
1/4 cup white sugar
1 cup milk
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
5 eggs
2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
TOPPING:
1 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
4 tablespoons cold butter or margarine
mini marshmallows

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat sweet potatoes, sugar, milk, butter, eggs, salt and vanilla until smooth. Transfer to a greased 5-qt. baking dish. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, pecans and flour; cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over potato mixture. Then sprinkle marshmallows over the top Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees F for 50 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Julie Hansen Ostler
Description: 
Mom loves sweet potatoes and fixed them often in this creamy, comforting casserole. With its nutty topping, this side dish could almost serve as a dessert. It's a yummy treat!Modified from: Mom's Sweet Potato Bake
