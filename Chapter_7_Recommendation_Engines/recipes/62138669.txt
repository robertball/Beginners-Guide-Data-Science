Title:
Herbs de Provence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp savory
1/2 tsp basil
1/4 tsp rosemary
1/4 tsp sage

Directions:
These are all dried herbs. Mix together and store in an air tight container.
I very often double this recipe.

Submitter: 
DECAR48
Description: 
A blend of dried herbs that is found in a number of recipes. Sometimes you can't find them in the grocery, so here's a good recipe I've used for many years.  Lots cheaper too!!!
