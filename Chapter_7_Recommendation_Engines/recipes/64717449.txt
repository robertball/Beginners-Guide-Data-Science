Title:
Chicken Orzo Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T. Oliver oil
1 sweet onion, chopped
4 medium carrots, chopped
4 celery stalks, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1.5 lbs chicken breast, uncooked
8 C. chicken broth (or more if preferred)
1.5 C. dry orzo
2 lemons
2 T. Parsley

Directions:
In large stock pot, add onion, carrots, celery, and olive oil and sautee for 5 mins.
Add garlic and sautee additional 2 mins.
Add diced chicken, broth, and dry pasta.  Bring to boil and simmer for 20 mins or until chicken and pasta are cooked.  Remove from heat.  Add lemon juice and parsley.  Serve hot.

A zesty spin on regular chicken noodle soup.
