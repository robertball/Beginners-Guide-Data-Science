Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp onion - finely chopped
1 stick butter
1 small jar cheez whiz
1 can cream of celery soup
2 cups cooked rice
1 pkg frozen broccoli spears (thawed / uncooked)
Bake: 45 minutes at 350 degrees

Directions:
2 tbsp onion - sauteed in 1 stick butter
Stir in 1 small jar cheez whiz and
1 can cream of celery soup
Add 2 cups cooked rice
Add 1 pkg frozen broccoli spears (thawed / uncooked)
Bake: 45 minutes at 350 degrees
