Title:
Cranberry Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  3/4 cups sugar
1  1/2 cups margarine or butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teapsoon grated orange peel
6 eggs
3 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
8 ounces sour cream
1  1/2 cups cranberries. (rinse and dump in)

Directions:
The batter is REALLY dense.
Grease and flour the bundt pan.  Bake 350  65-75 minutes.
(it will make one large bundt and one loaf pan - or 3 loaves.  If the top browns, cover in foil, but let cook the suggested time)
