Title:
Chocolate Cheerio Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Cheerios (preferably chocolate variety)
25 caramels
1 1/2 T water
1 cup miniature marshmallows
1/4 cup dry roasted peanuts
TOPPING:
1/4 cup chocolate chips
2 t shortening

Directions:
Put Cheerios in large bowl.
In medium sized bowl, combine caramels & water.  Mircowave on high for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring each minute, or until smooth.
Pour melted caramels over Cheerios and carefully stir
Add marshmallows, and stir
Press into a buttered 8x8" pan (similar to making Rice Krispy treats).
Sprinkle peanuts over mix and press in.
Combine topping ingredients - microwave on high for 45 sec to a minute, stir until smooth, and drizzle over cereal mix.  Cut into bars, then chill before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Couldn't stand the chocolate Cheerios I'd purchased, but didn't want to throw them away.  These outrageously tasty treats are a combination of Cheerios, caramels, marshmallows, peanuts and chocolate.
