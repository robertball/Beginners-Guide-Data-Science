Title:
Mexican Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 & 1/2 pounds extra lean - ground beef or ground round or ground turkey (I use very lean ground beef from meat market)
1 cup prepared packaged cornbread stuffing
1 medium size onion, chopped (1/2 cup)
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped or 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chilies
1 egg
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce

Directions:
Combine the ground beef, cornbread stuffing, onion, garlic, chilies, egg, chili powder and half the tomato sauce in a large bowl.
Shape into a loaf and place in a shallow baking dish.
Bake in a preheated 375 oven for one hour.
Drain and discard any fat from baking dish.
Spread the remaining tomato sauce over the meatloaf; bake 5 minutes longer.  Let meatloaf stand for 5 minutes before slicing.
