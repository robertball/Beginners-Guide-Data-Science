Title:
Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup butter, softened
1+1/2 cups Splenda
1 cup Chess cookie crumbs
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix ingredients by hand.
Refrigerate mixture for 30 minutes.
Roll mixture into balls.
Roll or dip into the following ingredients:
Coconut
Cocoa
Sprinkles
Icing Sugar
Cinnamon Sugar
Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
No baking, and use Splenda to lower the carbs.Modified from: Whitney's Peanut Butter Cookie Balls
