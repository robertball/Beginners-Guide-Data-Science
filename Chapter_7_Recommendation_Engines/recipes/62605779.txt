Title:
White Wine and Mushroom Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 small onion, finely chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups white wine
1 (14.5 oz.) can chicken broth
16 oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced
3/4 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 cup water
1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions:
In a deep 12 x 16" electric skillet, heat oil at 300 degrees.
Put chicken breasts in a large ziploc bag and pound until thin.
Coat chicken with flour, add to skillet and cook until cooked through, turning after a few minutes. Remove chicken breasts to a platter and keep warm.
To the same skillet, add the onions and garlic, saute for 1 minute.
Add mushrooms and saute until they give off their juices.
Add wine and salt, heat until reduced by half.
Add broth and heat for 3 minutes.
Mix water and cornstarch, add to sauce.  Heat 2 more minutes to thicken.
Return chicken to skillet for 3-5 more minutes.
Serve over rice, pasta or mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
Description: 
A white wine sauce with onions, garlic and mushrooms bathes chicken in this delectable presentation.Modified from: Port And Mushroom Sauce Chicken
