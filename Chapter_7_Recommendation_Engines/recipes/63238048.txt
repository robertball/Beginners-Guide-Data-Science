Title:
Loretta's Green Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 small can evaporated milk
1/2 lb. soft processed cheese (Velveeta)
1 small can green chilies, chopped
1/2 lb. longhorn cheese, grated
1 cup onions, chopped (optional)
1 lb. ground beef (I put Lawry's seasoning in it)
1 dozen flour tortillas

Directions:
Heat soup, milk, and processed cheese in top of double boiler until cheese is melted.  Add chopped chilies.  Set aside.
Cook ground beef and onions.  Fill each tortilla with meat mixture and grated cheese.  Roll tortilla and place in baking pan.  Pour cheese sauce over tortillas.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
