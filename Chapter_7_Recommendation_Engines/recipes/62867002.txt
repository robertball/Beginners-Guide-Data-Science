Title:
Macadamia Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. soft butter (2 sticks)
1 c. sugar
2 c. flour
1/2 c. choc chips
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 c. ground macadamia nuts
1/4 c. chopped mix nuts

Directions:
Grind 1/4 c. macadamia nuts with cornstarch in grinder. Beat sugar and butter until creamy. Blend in ground nuts. Add flour and salt until well combined then knead. Add chopped mixed nuts and chips. mix more with hands. Press into an ungreased 8x8 pan or 11x7. Bake at 325 degrees until very light brown for 20 to 35 min. Cool for 10 mins and cut into 1 inch squares. Cool for 10 mins more before removing
