Title:
Cuppa Joe Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992052.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 fluid ounce Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
3/4 fluid ounce hazelnut liqueur
1 fluid ounce cold espresso

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, add Smirnoff No.21 Vodka, hazelnut liqueur, and espresso.
Mix well and serve in a chilled martini glass.
Garnish with lemon zest twist.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
A martini with hazelnut and espresso flavors gets a lemon twist for garnish.
