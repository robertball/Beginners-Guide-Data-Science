Title:
Spicy Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C Corn Chex
3 C Rice Chex
3 C Wheat Chex (or replace with other Chex)
1 C mixed nuts
1 C pretzels
1 C cheese-crackers
1/4 C butter
1 TBSP Worchestershire sauce
1 1/4 tsp seasoned salt
2 1/2 tsp red pepper sauce

Directions:
Mix cereal, nuts, pretzels, and crackers; set aside. Microwave butter until melted. Stir in Wor. sauce, seasoned salt, and red pepper sauce. Pour over cereal mixture, stir until evenly coated.
Microwave uncovered on High 5 to 6 minutes, stirring every 2. Spread on paper towels to cool.
OR Bake in a roasting pan 1 hour at 250, stirring 15 minutes.)

Hannah Barrett
Description: 
Traditional Chex mix with an extra little kick
