Title:
Dried Beef Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
2 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 (4 ounce) jar dried beef, finely chopped
4 green onions, chopped
Assorted crackers

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, lemon juice and garlic powder until smooth. Beat in beef and onions. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Laura Martin
Description: 
'A friend shared this thick cracker spread with a sister a while ago, and we all agree it's delicious,' writes Laura Martin of Indianapolis, Indiana. 'Be ready to hand out the recipe whenever you make it.'
