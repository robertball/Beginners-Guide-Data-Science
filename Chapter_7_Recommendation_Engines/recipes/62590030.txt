Title:
Amy's Roasted Broccoli and Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen broccoli and Cauliflower mix
2 T vegetable oil
2t  Taragon Vinegar
1 tsp Brown Sugar
Salt
Pepper
Season Salt

Directions:
On a baking sheet add the bag of broc/cauli mix.  Add all other ingredients and toss to coat.
Bake at 400 for 20 minutes or until nicely roasted. I take out and stir halfway thru roasting time.

Submitter: 
ALD0014
Description: 
Roasting gives a nice texture and nutty flavor.  Love this!
