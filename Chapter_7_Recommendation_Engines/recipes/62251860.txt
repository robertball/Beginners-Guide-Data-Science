Title:
Meatballs & Brown Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1/2 Cup Plain Bread Crumbs
1 Egg
2/3 Cup Milk
1/8 tsp Nutmeg
Salt & Pepper to taste
1/4 tsp Minced Onion
1 14 oz. Can Franco American Beef Gravy

Directions:
Mix togeth meat, bread crumbs, egg, milk, nutmeg, salt, pepper.
Form into small meatballs.
Fry or bake approx. 1/2 hour until browned.
Place in frying pan w/gravy and simmer for 1/2 hour.

Submitter: 
dang1570@gmail.com
Description: 
Meatballs in a savory brown gravy. Served well with mashed potatoes and fresh vegetable.
