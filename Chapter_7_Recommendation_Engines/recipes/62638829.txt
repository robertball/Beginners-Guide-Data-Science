Title:
Blackberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound blackberries (fresh or frozen)
2/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Heat all to boiling in medium saucepan
Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until berries are tender - 8 to 10 minutes
Cool, then process in blender until smooth.
Strain and serve either chilled or at room temp
