Title:
Pickled Eggs & Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans reb beets; liquid drained and reserved
18 hard boiled eggs; peeled
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups white vinegar
1 Tbsp salt
20 whole garlic cloves

Directions:
Layer beets and eggs in 1/2 gallon jar.
Combine remaining ingredients including reserved beet liquid in a pot over medium high heat.  Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes.
Pour the liquid over the beets and eggs to cover.  Close and refrigerate 24 hours.
