Title:
Hearty Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656662.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Hamburger buns
2 pounds lean ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 teaspoons  yellow mustard
1-1/2 cups ketchup
2 tablespoons brown sugar
salt and black pepper to taste
Top with grated cheese (optional)

Directions:
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion, and green pepper; drain off liquids.
Stir in the garlic powder, mustard, ketchup, and brown sugar; mix thoroughly. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper and serve on buns.
