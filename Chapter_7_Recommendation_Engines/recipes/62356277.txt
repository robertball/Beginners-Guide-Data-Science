Title:
Russian Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 pieces chicken (breast or otherwise)
1 bottle Russian dressing (8-16 oz, depending on how much sauce you like)
1 pkg dry onion soup mix
3 Tbsp apricot jam

Directions:
Lay out chicken in 9x13" dish. In separate bowl, mix together dressing, soup mix, and jam. Pour over chicken. Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees. Great with rice.
