Title:
Deep Fried Lasagna Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4085606.jpg
Ingredients:
8 lasagna noodles, cooked to package instructions
1 jar of your favorite pasta sauce
1 tub of ricotta cheese (around 15oz)
3 eggs
box of Panko breadcrumbs
1.5-2 cups of flour
2-3 Italian sausages, casings removed (squeeze the meat out!)
Spinach, mushrooms, onions, garlic, any of your favorite pasta goodies
Italian seasoning, as desired
toothpicks needed!
Oil for frying

Directions:
Boil lasagna noodles according to box instructions
Cook spinach, mushroom, onion, garlic, Italian seasoning, and sausages in separate pan, after all cooked, add 1/2 jar of sauce to mixture
Lay out lasagna noodles flat, spoon ricotta down first, spoon on mixture from step 2 evenly down the noodle (leave a small space at the end to seal roll)
ROLL ROLL ROLL it up! toothpick it down in place (REPEAT x 8)
Freeze for 20 min (meanwhile... set up your 3 bowls of flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs)
Okay, get them puppies out and begin the breading process: dip each roll into flour, then eggs, then breadcrumbs) BE MINDFUL OF TOOTHPICK!
Heat 2 inches of oil in pan for 2min, remove toothpicks, & begin frying baby!
heat up extra sauce for dipping
ENJOY! :)
