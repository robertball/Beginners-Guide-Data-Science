Title:
Easy Hearty Sausage Soup/Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5118594.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground Italian sausage
1 large onion, chopped into chunks
3 carrots, sliced
2 celery sticks, sliced
2 tsp. chopped garlic or 3 cloves
2 large (796ml) cans Diced tomatoes (with juice)
3 cups beef broth
1 cup each of chopped broccoli, cauliflower
1 tbsp. parsley
Pepper to taste

Directions:
In Dutch Oven or large pot:
Cook ground sausage until well done. Do not remove the fat.
Add tomatoes, celery, carrots, onion, garlic, parsley, pepper.
Add the beef bouillon/broth to the pot.
Simmer for 1 hour.
Add Broccoli and Cauliflower, simmer 30 mins more.
Allow to sit for 2 hours, or better yet, serve the next day, this soup like most soups is better the next day.

Submitter: 
TC the Cookin' Cableguy
Description: 
This satisfying, hearty soup uses ground sausage, it is comfort food at its best.
You can vary vegetables based on what you have at hand.
