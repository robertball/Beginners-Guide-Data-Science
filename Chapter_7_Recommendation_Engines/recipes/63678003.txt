Title:
pistachio frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 8 oz cream cheese packages softened
1 cup powder sugar
1 enveloped sugar free pistachio instant pudding
1/2 cup cold milk or as much as u need.
1 cup cool whip

Directions:
Beat in a medium speed cream cheese until creamy about 3 minutes , add sugar little by little , add pudding , use as much milk that u need for your desired  consistency .Fold in cool whip.
