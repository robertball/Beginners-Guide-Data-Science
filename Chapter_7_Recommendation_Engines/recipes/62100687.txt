Title:
Swiss Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 c. shredded Swiss cheese, divided
2/3 c. club crackers(about 16)
1 small onion, chopped
2 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. rubbed sage
1 lb. lean ground beef

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the egg, crackers,  onion, garlic, salt, sage and pepper to the ground beef. Add the cheese, reserving 3 Tbsp. to sprinkle on the top.  Mix well.  Shape into a loaf.  Placed in a greased loaf pan.  Bake uncovered at 375Â° for 1 hour or until done.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese; bake for 2 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
This is a tasty twist to the traditional meatloaf.  It is one of my families favorites!
