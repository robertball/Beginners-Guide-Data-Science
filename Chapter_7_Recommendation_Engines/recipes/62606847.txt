Title:
Spiced Applesauce muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563217.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup oat bran
1/4 cup flax seed
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp allspice
1/2 tsp cloves
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 large egg
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup apple sauce
3/4 cup vanilla yogurt
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix together flour, oat bran, flax seed, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and spices and set aside.
In a seperate bowl beat together egg, sugar, applesauce, yogurt and oil.
Make a well in the flour mixture and add the wet mixture.  Stir togther until just combined.
Gently fold in nuts.
Divide batter evenly into a greased or paper lined muffin tin.
Bake 18-22 minutes, until muffin tops are springy to the touch.
