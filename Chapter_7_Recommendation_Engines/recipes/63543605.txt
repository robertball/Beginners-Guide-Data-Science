Title:
Spicy Garlic Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 breasts, halved
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon dried parsley
1 small garlic clove sliced
2 TBSP butter
1 TBSP olive oil
2 TBSP lime juice

Directions:
Combine all spices (not garlic) into bowl and mix together.  Rinse breasts and sprinkle spice mix over both sides.
Heat skillet to medium.  Add butter, olive oil and garlic.
Cook each breast approx. 3 mins. on first side.
Flip to 2nd side, adding lime juice to pan.  Cook another 3 mins. or until done.  Remove to plate and spoon pan sauce over finished breasts.
