Title:
BBQ Cornbread Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shredded chicken or browned ground beef
can black beans, drained
can whole kernel corn, drained
chopped onions, garlic
BBQ sauce
cheddar cheese
cornbread mix (egg n milk to make)

Directions:
Make cornbread mix per box directions.
Pour into casserole dish.
Saute onions n garlic.
Brown beef if needed or simply add chicken.
Add beans and corn and BBQ sauce. Simmer a few mins to blend flavors.
Pour over cornbread.
Bake uncovered 350.
Top with cheese and brown.
