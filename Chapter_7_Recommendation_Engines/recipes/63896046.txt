Title:
Dave's Favorite Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked elbow macaroni
shredded carrots
1 green pepper, shredded
Sauce:
1 can Eagle Brand Milk
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 cups Miracle Whip

Directions:
In very large bowl, mix all ingredients well.
Refrigerate overnight and stir before serving.

Description: 
Very quick, easy, and tasty macaroni salad.
