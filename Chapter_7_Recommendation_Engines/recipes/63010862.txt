Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
1 (14.5 ounce)can chicken broth
dash pepper
dash garlic salt
dash poultry seasoning
dash dried minced onion
rolled dumpling recipe from this site

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker, and broth to cover.
Cover, and cook for 5 to 6 hours on low. About 30 minutes before serving,shred chicken place the dumplings in the slow cooker. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center.

Jamie Lowe
