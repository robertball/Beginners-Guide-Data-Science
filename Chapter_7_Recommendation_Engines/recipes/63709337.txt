Title:
Sun Dried Tomatoes & Pesto Artisan Break Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030771.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon yeast
2 1/2 teaspoons salt
3 cups water
2/3 cups sun dried tomatoes
2 heaping tablespoons pesto
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Directions:
In large bowl mix flour, yeast, salt, Italian seasonings, then sun dried tomatoes (cut into 1/4 to 1/2 inch pieces), and pesto.
Add water and mix well.
Cover bowl with plastic film.
Let set at room temperature (in warm room) for 12-18 hours.
Remove from bowl to floured counter.
Turn dough over several times until very soft ball forms.
Place in parchment lined 10-inch fry pan.
Cover with plastic film and a moist towel.
Let rise for 2 hours.
At the beginning of 2nd hour of rising heat oven to 450F and place 4 quart size dutch oven covered baking pot in oven to heat through for one hour.
After final hour of rising pick up dough by the parchment paper and place into hot dutch oven.
Cover.  Bake for 30 minutes.
Remove cover and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes to desired golden brown color.
Remove from oven by lifting up on the corners of the parchment paper and cool on wire rack in draft-free area.

Description: 
Crusty flavorful Artisan no-knead bread.  Great served with pasta dishes and red wine.
