Title:
Big Ray's Rhubarb Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1128936.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups rhubarb, chopped (frozen or fresh)
1/2 cup  sugar
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. ginger
1 pkg. (3 oz.) raspberry gelatin
1 pkg.  (18.25 oz.) lemon cake mix
1 cup  water
1/3 cup  butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish
Spread the rhubarb evenly in the bottom of the buttered baking dish. Mix the sugar, gelatin, nutmeg  and ginger and sprinkle the mixture over the rhubarb.  Mix the cake mix, butter and water and pour on top of that.  Do not stir.
Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the rhubarb is tender. Can be served as is or with ice cream or whipped cream.
