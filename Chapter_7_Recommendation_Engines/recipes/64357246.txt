Title:
Egg Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
1-1/4 cups heavy whipping cream
2-3/4 cups whole milk
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
Nutmeg

Directions:
Separate eggs and beat whites until stiff.  Beat yolks in a separate bowl.  Fold whites into yolks.
Add cream and milk.  Stir well.  Add sugar and vanilla.
Ladle into small cups or glasses.  Sprinkle with nutmeg before serving.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description:
