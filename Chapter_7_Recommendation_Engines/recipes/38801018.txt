Title:
Grandma's Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz packages cream cheese, softened
1 8oz package mild cheddar cheese, shredded
1 tablespoon onions, minced
2 tablespoons Wosterchire sauce
dash salt
2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, mix all ingredients except pecans.
Form into a ball shape.
Chill minimum of two hours.
Roll in chopped pecans.
Serve with crackers or crusty bread.
