Title:
Zucchini Hamburger Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med Chunked Zucchini
1 medium onion chopped
1 3/4 lb ground beef
1 can corn
Jalapeno Pepper ( to taste)
1 small block Velvetta Cheese

Directions:
Fry hamburger, onion in pan on medium heat. Add zucchini. Stir occansionally until cooked. Add cheese and jalapeno pepper. Stir to melt.
