Title:
Lemon pepper grilled chicken salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Half head of iceburg lettuce
2 cucumbers
2 tomatoes
4 carrots
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
green onions (optional)
2 lemons
2 limes
1 tblsp lemon pepper seasoning

Directions:
Roll lemons and limes on cutting board then cut in half and squeeze juice onto chicken breasts. Sprinkle with half of the seasoning, and let marinate for about 5 minutes. Meanwhile chop lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers and carrots and toss in a large salad bowl.
When chicken is done marinating and the juice has been absorbed by the chicken, turn over and sprinkle other side with seasoning and grill until juices run clear. You can squeeze a little juice on the chicken while it is grilling if you like. cut chicken into long strips and place on salad. Squeeze remaining juice on salad at last minute and you can also sprinkle additional seasoning on salad.
