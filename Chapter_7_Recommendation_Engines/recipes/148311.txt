Title:
Slippery Frog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1474859.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger coffee flavored liqueur
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Irish cream liqueur
3 fluid ounces half-and-half
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger creme de menthe

Directions:
Fill an 8 ounce tumbler with ice. Pour in the vodka, coffee flavored liqueur, Irish cream, and half-and-half; stir. Drizzle creme de menthe on top to garnish.

Submitter: 
Bethany L. Firem
Description: 
This is an alcoholic drink for the White Russian lover. The twist is the addition of creme de menthe which not only give it a nice green color but makes the whole drink taste like a mint cookie!
