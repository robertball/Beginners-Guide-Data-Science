Title:
Skillet Lamb Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988295.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) lamb shoulder blade chops
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup warm water
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon dried minced onion
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown lamb chops in oil. Add the remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30-35 minutes or until meat juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Alpha  Wilson
Description: 
These mildly seasoned chops are so satisfying, particularly if you enjoy lamb for holiday dinners. Field editor Alpha Wilson of Roswell, New Mexico shares the recipe.
