Title:
Buffalo Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/824910.jpg
Ingredients:
One package ground turkey (approx 20 oz)
one small onion, grated
salt, pepper to taste
1/2 cup Frank's red hot original sauce
1/4 cup butter
garlic powder/onion powder to taste
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup blue cheese crumbles

Directions:
Combine ground turkey, grated onion, salt and pepper. Make the loaf and bake for 45 minutes at 350.
Meanwhile, melt butter in a pan, add hot sauce and garlic and onion powders. Mix and keep warm but not boiling.
Combine sour cream, mayonnaise, blue cheese and garlic powder/onion powder/black pepper to taste. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
After 40 minutes of baking, pour buffalo sauce over meatloaf. Return to the oven for 5 mins. (Check to make sure the turkey is cooked through)Remove and serve with blue cheese dressing.

Description: 
Combining two favorite dishes to make one great meal, this baked meatloaf topped with homemade or your favorite bottled buffalo wing sauce and served with an easy blue cheese dressing is sure to be a hit.
