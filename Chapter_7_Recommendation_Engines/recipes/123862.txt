Title:
Oatmeal Banana Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/30278.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup mashed ripe bananas
3/4 cup honey
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup quick-cooking oats
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, bananas and honey; mix well. Combine dry ingredients; stir into creamed mixture just until moistened. Fill paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 18-20 minutes or until cupcakes test done. Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Louise  Skinner
Description: 
Oatmeal Banana Cupcakes are moist, nutritious and require no frosting. They're handy for traveling, assures Louise Skinner of Hamilton, Ohio.
