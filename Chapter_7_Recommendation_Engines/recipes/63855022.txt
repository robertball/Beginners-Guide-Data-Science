Title:
Green Goddess Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 medium onions, chopped
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 medium bell pepper (green or yellow), finely chopped
1 green chili (anaheim or other), chopped
1 jalapeno, chopped
1 lb tomatillos, chopped
14.5 oz chicken broth (I use Better than Bullion)
1/4 teaspoon dried coriander
1/4 teaspoon cayenne

Directions:
Saute onions and garlic until translucent. Add the rest of the ingredients. Cook for 10-20 minutes until tomatillos have softened and flavors have started to blend. Puree with a blender until desired consistency is achieved.
