Title:
Summer Seafood - Balsamic Ahi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 6 oz ahi tuna fillets
3 medium vine ripened tomatoes, diced
14 oz can black olives, chopped
1/8 cup of lemon juice + 2 T more
1/8 cup of balsamic vinegar
garlic powder and fresh ground black pepper to taste
1 bag salad of choice

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium.
Combine tomatoes and olives with balsamic vinegar and 1/8 C lemon juice, refrigerate
Rub garlic powder and pepper on both sides of ahi.
Grill tuna to sear each side about 4 minutes each.
Serve tomatoes and olives over tuna on salad bed
