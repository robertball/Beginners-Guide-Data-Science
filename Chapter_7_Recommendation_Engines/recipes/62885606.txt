Title:
Elnora's Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup of milk
1 cup of self rising flour
1 cup of sugar
4 cups of sliced peaches
sugar to taste

Directions:
1. Melt the butter and set aside to cool.
2. Mix the milk, flour and sugar until blended. add melted butter.
3. Butter 11" x 17" baking dish and add sliced peaches. Add sugar to taste if necessary. Cover peaches with batter mixture (do not stir).
4. Bake for 40 minutes in a 375Â° oven until browning is noted.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
This is my grandmother's simple recipe. Only peaches and cobbler in Pennsylvania, no hiding the peach flavor with too many spices.
