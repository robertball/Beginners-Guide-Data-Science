Title:
Apple Filled French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 loaf Texas Toast bread
2. 1 21 ounce can apple pie mix
3. 8 large eggs
4. 2 cups milk
5. 1 cup sour cream
6. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
7. 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
8. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
topping
9. 1/2 cup butter
10. 2 tablespoons coorn syrup, white
11. 1 cup brown sugar
12. 1 cup chopped nuts ( pecans or walnuts)
13. 2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Butter 9x13" baking pan
Layer 1/2 of 1. in the bottom of the pan.
Mix 3. 4. 5. 6. 7. 8. together then add 2.
pour 1/2 of mixture over the bread...cut or tear remaining bread and place on top...pour rest of egg mixture over the torn bread
make topping by blending 9. 10. 11. 12. together with 13....spread over the bread
Bake 50 min to 1 hr 350* till puffed and golden
