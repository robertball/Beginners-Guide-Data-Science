Title:
Chicken Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Ground Chicken
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp thyme
1sp Asian Spice
1 tbsp Asian Sauce
bibb lettuce
apricot sauce
red pepper sauce

Directions:
Mix chicken with spices and sauce.
Grill until cooked through.
Serve on fresh buns with lettuce and other sauces.
