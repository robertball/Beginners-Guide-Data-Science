Title:
Blueberry Stuffed French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Slices bread
8 oz cream cheese
2 1/2 cups blueberries
10 slices cubed 3/4" bread
3 cups milk
3 eggs
1/4 maple syrup
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cup sugar, divided
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
2 T. cornstarch
1 cup water
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Spread cream cheese on the 6 slices of bread and place in a 9 by 13 pan, cheese side up.  Sprinkle 1 1/2 cups of blueberries and bread cubes over the top.
Whisk together milk, eggs, syrup, vanilla, and 1/4 C sugar and pour over bread mixture.  Cover with aluminum foil and refrigerate overnight.
Remove foil and mix cinnamon and 1/2 cup sugar together and sprinkle over the top.  Recover and bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
Meanwhile make sauce.  Combine cornstarch, water, and remaining sugar and cook until thick.  Add remaining blueberries and butter and stir.
Cut french toast into squares and serve with sauce drizzled on top.

Description: 
An easy, prepare-ahead hearty holiday-worthy brunch dish or a special family treat anytime. Other fresh or frozen fruit can be used.
