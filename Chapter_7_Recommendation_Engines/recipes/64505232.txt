Title:
July - Lite Apron - Teriyaki Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb oz uncooked ground turkey breast
4 item(s) light hamburger roll(s) or bun(s)
1/2 lb uncooked string beans
3 medium uncooked scallion(s)
1 large English cucumber(s)
one large bunch fresh cilantro
2 T rice vinegar
2 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp minced ginger
2 Tbsp dried sesame seed kernels
1/4 cup(s) panko breadcrumbs
1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
2 T olive oil

Directions:
preheat oven to 450
thinly slice scallions
thinly slice cucumber
pick cilantro leaves off stems
mince the garlic
clean and cut ends off beans
in large pan heat one T olive oil in medium high until hot.
add white bottoms of scallions and ginger.
season with salt and pepper.
cook 2-3 minutes and transfer to a large bowl.
to the bowl of scallions and ginger add the ground turkey, teriyaki sauce, breadcrumbs, green tops of scallions and half the sesame seeds. season with salt and pepper.
mix to combine then form patties.
add 1 T olive oil to the pan and heat to medium high.
add the patties.
Cook 4-6 minutes per side until cooked through and browned.
transfer to a plate.
add 1 T oil to the pan and heat on Med. High.
add the beans. season with salt and pepper.
Cook 7-9 minutes or until tender.
turn off the heat.
stir in half the sesame oil and the remaining sesame seeds.
transfer to serving dish.
toast buns and serve dish
