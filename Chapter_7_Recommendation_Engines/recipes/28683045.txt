Title:
Homestyle Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875899.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1/2 lb mushrooms
1 1/2c. chopped (or half slices) onions
4 cans kidney beans (undrained)
3 cans stewed diced tomatoes (undrained)
1/2 T. chili powder
1t salt
4 cans campbells tomato soup

Directions:
Brown meat and onions.
Drain.
Add all remaining ingredients and simmer for 1/2 an hour.
Serves best with saltines or corn chips

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
a basic chili for the picky eater. Not too spicy either.
