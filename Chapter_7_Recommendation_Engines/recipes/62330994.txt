Title:
Cheesy Jalapeno Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/384727.jpg
Ingredients:
2 8 ounce packages of cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 package dry ranch seasoning
1/4 cup freshly chopped cilantro
1 4 ounce can diced jalapenos, with juices
1 4 ounce can green chili, with juices
1 8 ounce package shredded cheddar cheese, divided in half
1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
2 jalapenos with seeds; one diced, one sliced into rings

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sour cream, ranch seasoning, green chili, canned jalapeno, diced jalapeno, and cilantro. Add half of the shredded cheddar and half of the Parmesan cheese. Mix well. Spread in pan and top with jalapeno rings, remaining cheddar and Parmesan. Bake covered for 25 minutes at 350 degrees or until top is warm and bubbly.

Submitter: 
KimberlyClaire79
Description: 
AKA Diablo dip, this is creamy, spicy, gooey, crunchy, and so tasty!  Great served with chips or spread on a bacon cheeseburger.

I make mine with low fat cream cheese and sour cream and no one can tell because there is so much other taste going on.  Wonderful!
