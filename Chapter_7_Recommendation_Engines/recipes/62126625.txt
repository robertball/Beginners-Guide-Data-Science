Title:
Greek Farmer's Bread (Xwriatiko Psomi) for Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/3 cups bread flour
1/2 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 cups water
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in bread machine pan according to manufacturer's instructions. Select 1 1/2 pound basic white bread setting with medium crust.

Submitter: 
tignor
Description: 
This recipe is the traditional Greek table bread--chewy crust with a soft, pillowlike inside--that is served at most restaurants and on tables at homes in Greece. I have adapted this for use in the bread machine. It has a texture not found in other white breads.
