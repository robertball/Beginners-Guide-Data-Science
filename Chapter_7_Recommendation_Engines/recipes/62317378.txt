Title:
Rich "Beef" Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup tomato sauce
1 cup red wine, cabernet
2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
2 cups vegetable broth (knorr), hot
1/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon braggs liquid aminos
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1 bay leaf
1-1/2 teaspoons oregano, dried
1-1/2 teaspoons thyme, dried
2 baking potatoes, cubed
2 large carrots, sliced
1 ounce porcini mushrooms, dried
2 tablespoons onion soup/dip mix
2 packages gardin "beef" (*note at bottom if not using)
2 tablespoons crushed garlic
fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Heat 2 cups of vegetable broth very hot and add dried porcini mushrooms. Cover with plastic wrap. Set aside.
In crock pot add in tomato sauce, wine, soy sauce, worcestershire, flour, braggs, pepper, bay leaf, oregano, thyme, potatoes, carrots, 2 tablespoons onion soup mix. Stir.
Coat a large frying pan with oil on high heat. When oil is hot add 2 packages of  gardin "beef" and saut√© until brown on one side. Add chopped garlic, fresh ground pepper and stir to brown other sides. When all sides are browned, add to stock pot. Add vegetable broth with the porcini mushrooms and stir.
Cover. Cook on high until it starts to simmer then turn to low and cook for 7 1/2 to 8 hours.
Remove bay leaf and serve by itself or with rice.
