Title:
Easy Chicken Roll-up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/948535.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts
4 teaspoons of Basil
4 teaspoons of Salt
4 pieces of String Cheese
3/4 cup of Italian Bread Crumbs
4 pieces of Bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cook bacon until desired crispiness and set aside.
Pound chicken until its 1/2 inch in size.
Sprinkle on basil and salt
Roll the string cheese and bacon into the chicken and seal with toothpick if needed.
Coat the chicken in Italian bread crumbs.
Place in a 8 x 8 baking dish and cook for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Brent Berkeypile
Description: 
This is an easy go to chicken dinner that I like to make.  I usually always have ingredients on hand.
