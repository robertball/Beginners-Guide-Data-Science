Title:
Spicy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs ground beef (or turkey)
4 cups cubed, peeled potatoes
1 chopped onion
3 cans (8 oz) tomato sauce
4 cups water
2 teaspoons salt (or to taste)
1 tablespoons hot pepper sauce (or to taste)
1 1/2 tablespoons black pepper (or to taste)

Directions:
In a large pot brown ground beef; drain. Add potatoes, onions, and tomato sauce. Stir in water, salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for one hour until potatoes are tender and soup has thickened.
