Title:
Cilantro Serrano Cream Sauce! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 bunch cilantro, coarsely chopped
3 serrano chile peppers, seeded and chopped
3 cloves garlic
1 cup mayonnaise
3/4 cup cup milk
juice & zest of 1 lime
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place cilantro, serrano peppers, garlic, mayonnaise, milk and lime with zest into a blender; season with salt and pepper. Puree until smooth, about 15 seconds.

Submitter: 
Dawn Norby
Description: 
This is a great sauce for chicken tacos and fish tacos. It's also great as a dip!Modified from: Cilantro Serrano Cream Sauce
