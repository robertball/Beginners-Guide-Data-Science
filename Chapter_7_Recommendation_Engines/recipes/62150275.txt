Title:
Cauliflower, Carrot & Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/285245.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups Cauliflower Florets, steamed
1 Cup Baby Carrots, steamed
2 Tsp. Olive Oil
2 Cloves Garlic, crushed
1/4 Cup Chopped Green Onion
2 Cups Sliced Mushrooms
1 Cup Frozen Peas
3 Cups Low-Sodium Vegetable Stock (Knorr)
1 Tsp. Nutmeg
1 Tsp. Dried Parsley
Fresh Cracked Pepper to taste
Fat-Free Sour Cream for Garnish (optional)

Directions:
Steam Cauliflower and Carrots, set aside to cool and then puree in a food processor or use a stick blender.
In a medium frypan, saute crushed garlic and green onion until soft, add mushrooms and saute until mushrooms are just starting to turn golden.
Bring 3 cups of vegetable stock to a boil, add garlic, onions and mushrooms, nutmeg, cracked pepper and parsley, stir well to combine.
Add pureed vegetables and frozen peas, reduce heat to low and simmer until peas are no longer frozen and soup is heated through (about 10-15 minutes).
Serve with a dollop of Fat-Free Sour Cream if desired

Submitter: 
Gitano
Description: 
Steamed Cauliflower & Carrots are pureed and infused with sauted garlic, mushrooms and green onions, serve with a dollop of fat-free sour cream for a healthy, waist-line friendly meal!
