Greek Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
