Title:
Caramel apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 honeycrisp apples(or any other kind)
4 caramel wraps(circle shaped caramel disks)
4 wooden sticks
1 microwavable plate

Directions:
Wash the apples. Gently wrap one caramel around each apple, so none of the skin of the apple is exposed. Stick a wooden stick through where the stim is. Place in the microwave on a microwavable plate for 15 to 25 seconds(one or two apples at a time.). Be sure to make sure the caramel has cooled before eating!!!Enjoy!!
