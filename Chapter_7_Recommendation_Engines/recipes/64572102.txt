Title:
Big Ray's Catalina Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3608497.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups sugar
4 tsp celery seed
4 tsp salt
4 tsp paprika
2 cup ketchup
1 cup cannola oil
1 cup vinegar
Juice of 3 lemons
2 medium onions, minced well
1 clove garlic, minced well

Directions:
Blend all ingredients well. Keep in the refrigerator up to 3 months.
