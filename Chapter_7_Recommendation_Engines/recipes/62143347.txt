Title:
Aunt Lou's Pumpkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/294472.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can pumpkin
1 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 8 oz brick cream cheese
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 13x9 inch cake pan.
Beat together eggs, oil, and sugar. Stir in remaining ingredients until evenly mixed.
Pour into waiting pan. Bake for 30 minutes or until cake springs back when touched. Cool completely
In separate bowl, mix together cream cheese, vanilla, and powdered sugar. More sugar may be added to taste. Frost cake.
