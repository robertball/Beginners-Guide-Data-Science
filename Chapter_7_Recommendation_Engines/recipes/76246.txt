Title:
Mix Ahead Hot Cocoa Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992864.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9.6 ounce) box instant nonfat dry milk powder
1 1/4 cups SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2/3 cup powdered non-dairy coffee creamer
2/3 cup Dutch process cocoa

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, stirring well. Store in an airtight container.
Combine 1/4 cup Hot Cocoa Mix with 3/4 cup boiling water for an individual serving. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This easy hot cocoa mix is great for keeping in the pantry. Make some when you need a rich chocolate pick-me-up.
