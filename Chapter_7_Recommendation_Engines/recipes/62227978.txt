Title:
Chavra (Indian Chex Mix) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319211.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C Chex Cereal (mixed flavors)
1 C mixed nuts
1/2 C Shaved coconut
3 TB Sesame Seeds
3/4 tsp Ground cumin
1/2 tsp Garlic powder
1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
1/4 C Melted butter
3/4 tsp tumeric
Salt to taste
Raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200 degrees
Combine all ingredients (except raisins) in a large bowl and mix well.
Place mixture on a cookie sheet in oven.  Stir every 15 minutes for 1 hour - don't let mixture borwn.
Remove from oven and stir in raisins.
Best made 2-3 hours prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Karen Packer
Description: 
This is an Indian take on the traditional Chex Mix.  The spicieness of the tumeric, cumin & cayenne are offset by the sweetness of the raisins.
