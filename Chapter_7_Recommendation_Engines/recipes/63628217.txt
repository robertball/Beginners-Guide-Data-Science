Title:
Pistachio-Cherry Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Unsalted butter for greasing pan
5 cups pitted cherries (2lb whole or 1 1/2 lb), thawed or frozen
1/2 cup plus 1 tbsp light brown sugar, divided
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1/3 cup plus 1 tbsp all-purpose flour, divided
1/2 cup shelled, unsalted pistachios
1/3 cup rolled oats
4 walnuts, shelled
1/4 tsp ground cardamom
1/4 tsp salt
4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F. Butter a 9" pie pan. In pan, mix cherries with 1 tbsp sugar, lemon juice, and 1 tbsp flour.
In a food processor, pulse remaining 1/2 cup sugar, remaining 1/3 cup flour, pistachios, oats, walnuts, cardamom and salt until nuts are finely chopped, 1-2 mins. Stir in butter; sprinkle topping over cherries.
Bake until juices bubble thickly and topping is browned, about 25 mins. Serve warm or at room temp.
