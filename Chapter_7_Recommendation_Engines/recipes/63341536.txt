Title:
Satiny Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887373.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
3 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon light corn syrup
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a double boiler over hot, but not boiling water, combine chocolate chips, butter, and corn syrup.
Stir until chips are melted and mixture is smooth, then add vanilla.
Spread warm glaze over top of cake, letting it drizzle down the sides.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
A glossy chocolate glaze to drizzle over a Bundt cake. It's also great on cookies and doughnuts.Modified from: Satiny Chocolate Glaze
