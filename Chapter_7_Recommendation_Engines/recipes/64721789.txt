Title:
Meaty Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9-12 Lasagna noodles, cooked
1 lb ground beef (or sausage)
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c. onion, chopped
1 tsp oregano
1 Tbsp parsley flakes
1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
3 (8 oz) cans tomato sauce
3/4 c. hot water
1 egg
1 (16 oz) container small curd cottage cheese
3 c. shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Boil the noodles in a pot of salt water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
Brown the beef, garlic & onion until soft and cooked thoroughly. Add the seasonings, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water. Combine and set aside.
Combine the egg, cottage cheese and 1 c. of mozzarella cheese together.
Layer together in a greased 9x13 as follows:  1/4 meat sauce, 3 noodles, 1/4 meat sauce, 1/2 cheese mixture, 3 noodles,  1 c. mozzarella cheese, 1/4 meat sauce, 1/2 cheese mixture, 1 c. mozzarella cheese, 3 noodles, then top with the rest of the meat mixture and 1 more layer of cheese. Sprinkle with dried parsley or basil.
Bake at 350 uncovered for 30-40 minutes.
