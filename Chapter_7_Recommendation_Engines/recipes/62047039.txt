Title:
Justine's Artichoke Chicken Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-10oz package Frozen chopped spinach
1-14oz can Artichoke Bottoms
1-13oz can can chunk chicken
1-Cup Mayonnaise
2-Cups Shredded Mozzerella Cheese
2 cloves crushed garlic
1-tsp liquid smoke (optional)

Directions:
Precook and drain chopped spinach.  Drain and chop artichoke bottoms.
Chop chicken.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix together.
Spoon entire mixture into a glass pie or casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until edges become golden brown

Submitter: 
JustineCosley
Description: 
A variation of the regular artichoke spinach dip.  I first tasted this at a local restaraunt and have been trying to perfect it for over a year.  I've finally done it and would like to share it with you.
