Title:
Jewish Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125328.jpg
Ingredients:
BATTER
3 cups flour
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups sugar
2½ tsp vanilla
1/3 cup orange juice
3 teaspoons baking powder
4 eggs
pinch of salt
TOPPING
4 TBSP sugar
3 TBSP cinnamon
4 large apples,sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. Grease and flour tube pan. Mix cinnamon and sugar for topping. mix all other ingredients (except apples) in large mixing bowl. Layer with half of batter~apples~topping. Repeat next layer. When cake tester comes out clean it is done. Allow to cool. Release from tube pan.

Submitter: 
ChezSue
Description: 
My mother-in-law gave me this recipe.  It never lasts more than dessert.  A comfort dessert that warms tummy, heart and soul.
