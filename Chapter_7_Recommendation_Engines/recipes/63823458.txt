Title:
Pizza Spaghetti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060697.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground beef
1 16 oz. box uncooked spaghetti noodles
½ teaspoon Oregano
½ teaspoon garlic powder
½ Cup Milk
1 Egg
2 ounces sliced pepperoni
1 (26 ounce) jar pasta sauce
1 16 oz. can diced Italian style tomatoes
¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Italian cheese blend
Any other pizza toppings, you can add like Black olives, sausage, onions, green peppers, etc...

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook Spaghetti noodles according to directions.
Brown meat in a separate frying pan.
Once noodles are cooked, drain and put in a well-greased casserole dish.
In a separate bowl, whisk together milk & egg. pour over pasta & add pasta sauce, tomatoes, garlic powder, & oregano. Mix together well.
Layer browned ground beef on top of spaghetti mixture, then add layer of pepperoni slices.
Sprinkle top with Italian cheese, and another layer of pepperoni.  Sprinkle Parmesan over top of pepperoni.  Bake for 30 minutes or until cheese is browned and bubbly.
