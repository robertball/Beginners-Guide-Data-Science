Title:
Chipotle Pork Naanwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778963.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Naan Bread
3 generous size pieces of pork cutlet (or any other meat, already cooked)
3 tbsp chipotle mayo (or more)
lettuce
green onions
3 slices gouda cheese

Directions:
Chipotle Mayo:  mix 3 tbsp mayo with 1 small chipotle pepper, diced up.  You can add more or less, depending on taste.
Heat cutlet up in oven or microwave
Add cheese and heat until cheese is melted
Meanwhile, heat up Naan Bread so it is flexible
Remove Naan Bread, spread with chipotle mayo, top with cutlet,onions, lettuce, and anything else you would like.
Fold over and enjoy

Submitter: 
Babbs
Description: 
I created this while trying to use up leftovers.  It was made with pork cutlets, but any meat would be good.  Hope you enjoy !
