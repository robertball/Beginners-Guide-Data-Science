Title:
Mini Apple Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/732262.jpg
Ingredients:
2 refrigerated ready-made pie crusts
3 cups diced granny smith apple
2 Tblespoons minute tapioca
1/2 cup of sugar
juice of one lemon
1/8 teaspoon of nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
2. Cut 4 mini pie crusts out of the 2 crusts
3. Place in mini pie plates
4. Stir together the apples, tapioca, sugar, lemon and spices in a bowl.
5. 5. Let the mixture stand for 5 minutes.
6. Meanwhile, cut remining pie crust into 1/8 inch strips to make a small lattice top on each pie.
7. Pour the apple mixture into the pie shell.
8. Lay the strips across the pie in a lattice pattern, and pinch the edges to seal.
9. Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
10. Allow to cool before serving.

Submitter: 
Lisawas
Description: 
I love these mini pies because I can serve several different types together and everyone can select their favorite.  This mini apple pies use granny smith apples.  I bought the mini pie dishes online and they are really fun!
