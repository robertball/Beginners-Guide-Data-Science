Title:
Bayou Bunny al la Antoine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 flour tortillas
grated mozzarella cheese
pizza seasoning
spaghetti or pizza sauce

Directions:
Heat a small frying pan to medium high.  Spray with Pam.  Lay tortilla in pan.  Sprinkle on cheese and top with tortilla.  Spray top tortilla with Pam.  Sprinkle pizza season on top.  Flip tortilla when cheese begins to melt and tortilla is lightly brown. Brown other side.  Remove from pan and slice.  Dip slices into warmed spaghetti sauce.
