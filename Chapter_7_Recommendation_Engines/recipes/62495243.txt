Title:
Perfect Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 1/4 cups milk(we like a thinner pancake)
2 tablespoons melted butter, slightly cooled
a lot of cinnamon, & i do mean a lot

Directions:
Mix ingredients together, best on a griddle.

Submitter: 
Melissa Wilson Martin
Description: 
I make this using egg substitute and soy milk.  These are  delicious.
