Title:
Mushroom Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 uncooked lasagna noodles
1 lb sliced fesh mushrooms
2C chopped baby portobella mushrooms
1 large sweet onion, chopped
3Tbsp olive oil
1/2C minced fresh parsley
2 thin slices prosciutto or deli ham chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
2/3C white wine or chicken broth
2 Cans (14 1/2oz) diced tomatoes, drained
1 C shredded part skim mozzarella cheese
1C parmesan cheese
1/2C heavy whipping cream
1/4C milk

Directions:
Cook noodles according to pkg directions. Meanwhile, in a dutch oven, cook and stir the mushrooms and onion in oil until tender. Add the parsley, prosciutto, garlic, and seasoning; cook 2 minutes longer.  Add the wine; cook and stir until liquid is evaporated. Add tomatoes and heat through.
Spread the sauce in a greased 13x9 pan.  Layer with three noodles,1 1/3C sauce, add a scant of each cheese. Repeat with next two layers.
In a small bowl, combine the cream, milk and remaining cheeses;spoon over the top.
Cover and bake 350 for 30 minutes.
Uncover; bake 10 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.
