Title:
Best Ever Rugelach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Cottage Cheese
1 1/4 Cups  Unsalted Butter Softened (divided)
2 Cups Flour
3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
1/4 Tsp Cinnamon
3/4 Cup Nuts - chopped--(pecans or walnuts work well)
1 egg white
2 Tbsp Water
****
1/2 cup raisins chopped (optional)
3 Tbsp Orange Marmalade or Raspberry or Apricot preserves (optional)

Directions:
1. Combine cottage cheese and 1 Cup of the Butter in medium bowl. Mix throroughly until creamy.  Gradually add flour, beating until the dough forms a ball. (It will be thick and sticky)  Divide into thirds, wrap in waxed paper and chill for at least 2 hours.  Roll each piece into a 9" circle on a WELL floured surface. (Let the others stay chilled until ready to use)
2.Combine remaining 1/4 cup of softened butter, brown sugar, cinnamon and nuts and  optional raisins. (If you would like to add the preserves, brush the dough with a thin layer of the preserves.)
3.  Spread each circle with 1/3 of the nut mixture. Press gently into the dough. Using a pizza wheel, cut heach circle into 16 pie shaped pieces. Roll each piece tightly starting with the wide end.  Place point side down on a lightly greased cookie sheet.
4.Beat egg white and water, brush on cookies. Bake at 400 degress for 20 minutes. (Check them at around 18 minutes)- Cook until golden brown.  Enjoy!
