Title:
Sparkling Sea Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4487830.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger cranberry juice
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger grapefruit juice
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers sparkling water
1 lime wedge

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add vodka, cranberry juice, and grapefruit juice. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted.
Pour sparkling water into a glass. Pour in vodka mixture. Garnish with a wedge of lime.

Submitter: 
emvcook
Description: 
This variation of a Sea Breeze gives it a bit of fizz, a touch more vodka, and it's easy to tweak the sweetness.
