Title:
Cheesecake Topper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar
1 cup thawed Cool Whip
10 Graham crackers, broken in half to make 20 squares
1.5 cups fresh raspberries

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl until well blended. Stir in cool whip.
Spread 1 Tbsp topping onto graham square just before serving.
Top with 2-3 raspberries per square.
