Title:
Brine Solution Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 QRT Water, Cold
1/4 CUP Kosher Salt

Directions:
Stir the Kosher salt in the cold water until it is dissolved.
Place poultry in the solution and let brine for 30 minutes to 1 hour, depending on the size of the poultry.

Submitter: 
Chef Chris
Description: 
Brining poultry before cooking serves two purposes:  it helps the flesh retain moisture and it seasons all the way through.
