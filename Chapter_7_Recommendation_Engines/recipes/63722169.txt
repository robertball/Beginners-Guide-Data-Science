Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2-3 very ripe bananas
2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1/2 cup oil (or applesauce)
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease loaf pan or muffin tin.
Peel bananas and mash with fork in a small bowl.
In a different bowl, mix sugar, oil and egg until creamy yellow.
Blend in mashed bananas.
Add flour, baking soda, salt and spices.
Destroy any lumps.
Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 1 hour for loaf pan (20-30 minutes for muffins) or until toothpick comes out clean.
