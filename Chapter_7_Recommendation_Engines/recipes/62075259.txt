Title:
Peanut Butter Pokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Peanut butter
1 cup granulated sugar
2 egg whites

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Liberate 2 egg whites and slip them into a med. bowl. Fold in peanut butter and sugar.
Roll into 1" balls and place on a lightly greased cookie sheet.  Next take a fork and press gently to make a lattice impression on the dough.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
Makes 1 DZN

Submitter: 
Lady Lerandia
Description: 
Easy 3 ingredient recipe.  This is the absolute best peanut-butter cookie recipe you have ever tried. It's beauty is in it's simplicity.  My husband LOVES this recipe.
