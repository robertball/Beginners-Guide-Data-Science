Title:
Pasta and Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. butter
2 medium zucchini, quartered
lengthwise and thinly sliced
2 medium onions, chopped
2 carrots, sliced
2 cups tomato or vegetable juice
1 box(32 oz.)vegetable broth
1/2 cup macaroni, uncooked
dash black pepper
2(16 oz.)cans cannellini beans,
rinsed and drained
1 cup corn kernels

Directions:
In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add zucchini, onion and carrot. Cook 3 to 4 minutes, stirring frequently. Add tomato juice and broth. Bring to a boil.
Add macaroni and pepper. Cook for 6 minutes, or until tender. Stir in the beans and corn; heat through.
