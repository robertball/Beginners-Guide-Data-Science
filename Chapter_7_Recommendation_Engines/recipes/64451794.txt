Title:
Pasta Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T vegetable oil
bow tie pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta in boiling water for half of the cooking time listed on the package. Drain. Immediately pan fry the pasta in 2 T. oil in large skillet over medium-high heat, turning, until golden brown and crisp.
Drain on paper towels.
Immediately sprinkle with seasonings of your choice.
Serve hot.
