Title:
Firecracker Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. cranberry juice
4 c. pineapple juice
2 qt. ginger ale
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 T. almond extract

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except the ginger ale.
Stir until sugar is dissolved.
Chill.
Add ginger ale just before serving.
