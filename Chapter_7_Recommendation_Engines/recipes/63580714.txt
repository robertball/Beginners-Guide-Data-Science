Title:
Restaurant Style Chicken Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995858.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons oil
4 boneless chicken breast minced
2 cup water chestnuts
1 1/2 cup mushrooms(straw, morel or whatever you like)
1 chopped onion
4 cloves garlic
Iceberg lettuce or other for wrapping chicken
Special Sauce
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup water
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
4 tablespoons hoisin sauce
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
1/4 teaspoon chili oil
2 tablespoon hot mustard(Chinese style)
2-4 teaspoons garlic and red chili paste
2 tablespoons Green onions
Stir Fry Sauce
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar

Directions:
Make the special sauce by dissolving the sugar in water in a small bowl.
Add soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, hoisin, lemon juice, mustard,water, garlic chili sauce, green onions, chili and sesame oil.Mix well and refrigerate this sauce until you're ready to serve. Note-add mustard and garlic sauce little by little till taste fits yours.
Bring oil to high heat in a wok or large frying pan.
Saute chicken breast for 4 to 5 minutes till done.
As chicken cools mince water chestnuts and mushrooms to about the size of small peas.
Prepare the stir fry sauce by mixing the soy sauce, brown sugar, and rice vinegar together in a small bowl.
With the pan still on high heat, add another Tbsp of vegetable
Add chicken, garlic, onions, water chestnuts and mushrooms to the pan.
Add the stir fry sauce to the pan and saute the mixture for a couple minutes then serve it in the lettuce"cups".
Top with special sauce
