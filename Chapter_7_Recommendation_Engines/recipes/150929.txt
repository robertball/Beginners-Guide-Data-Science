Title:
TEN Fresh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979437.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Tanqueray No.TEN® Gin
1 ounce fresh lime juice
1 ounce simple syrup
1 teaspoon sugar
1 slice lime

Directions:
Pour Tanqueray No.TEN(R) Gin, fresh lime juice, simple syrup, and sugar into a shaker with ice.
Shake cocktail well to blend syrup and strain into chilled martini glass; garnish with lime slice.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
For a cocktail with an aroma and flavor that marries fresh citrus to mature juniper, use Tanqueray No. TEN® Gin.
