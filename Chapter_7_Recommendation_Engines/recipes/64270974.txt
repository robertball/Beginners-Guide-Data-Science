Title:
Jerk seasoning marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large onion chopped
4-6 habenero peppers chopped
1 head garlic cloves chopped
1dozen seeds pimento grain crushed
2Tbs ginger chopped
3tbs sugar
3stalks escallion chopped
2sprig thyme
1tsp cloves
2TBScelery leaves chopped
2basil leaves chopped
Salt to taste
3tbs honey (optional)

Directions:
In a sauce pan add all ingredients with two cups water and bring to boil for an hour until all the seasonings are cooked store in a sterilized glass bottle then allow to cool.use as marinade for your chicken or pork.when using on pork allow meat to boil in jerk seasonings for about an hour.then cool re-season and place on Char coal  grill basting repeatedly with marinade and beer of your choice cook until juices run clear and meat is tender. The same is done for the chicken except it doesn't need to be boiled first just add to grill and cook until done basting at regular intervals with marinade and beer.

Description: 
A spicy marinade used to make the world famous jerk if you like hot this is for you.
