Title:
Linda's Baked Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. box pasta (elbows, rotini, etc.)
4 Tablespoons butter
4 Tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon salt
cracked black pepper
4  cups whole milk or evaporated milk or half and half or any combination thereof
1 1/2 lbs. shredded cheddar cheese (NOT pre-shredded)
1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat large pot of water to boiling and cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and rinse and put back in pot and set aside.
While pasta is cooking, melt butter in heavy sauce pan over medium high heat. Add flour and stir, mixing well.  Allow to bubble, stirring frequently for at lease 1 minute, watching carefully that it doesn't burn.
Add milk and mix well with wire whisk.  Heat and stir until mixture thickens.
Add 3 cups of shredded cheddar, 1 cup at a time, and whisk until melted and completely incorporated. Pour mixture into pot with cooked pasta and mix well.  Add 1 cup of shredded cheddar and stir.
Pour into a 13" x 9" pan that has been sprayed with cooking spray.  Spread remaining cheddar and parmesan cheese on top. Bake in 375Â° oven for 25-30 minutes, or until hot and bubbly and cheese is melted.
