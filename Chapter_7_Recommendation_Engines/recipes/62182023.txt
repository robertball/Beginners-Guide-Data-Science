Title:
French Dip Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
1 beef bouillon
1/2 pound thinly sliced deli roast beef
4 slices provolone cheese
2 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Open the hoagie rolls and lay out on a baking sheet.
2.Heat beef broth & bouillion in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat to make a rich beef broth. Place the roast beef in the broth and warm for 3-4 minutes. Arrange the meat on the hoagie rolls and top each roll with 2 slices of provolone.
3.Bake the sandwiches in the preheated oven for 5 minutes, or until the cheese just begins to melt. Serve the sandwiches with small bowls of the warm broth for dipping.
