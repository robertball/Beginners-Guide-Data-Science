Title:
Parfait Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989545.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped strawberries
1/2 cup frozen yogurt
1/2 Pop-Tarts® Strawberry Milkshake toaster pastry

Directions:
Place the strawberries and frozen yogurt into a blender. Blend until ingredients are mixed, about 15 seconds. Pour into a glass. Serve with a toasted Pop-Tarts(R) Strawberry Milkshake toaster pastry.

Submitter: 
Pop-Tarts
Description: 
Serve this refreshing strawberry and frozen yogurt drink with your favorite flavor Pop-Tarts®.
