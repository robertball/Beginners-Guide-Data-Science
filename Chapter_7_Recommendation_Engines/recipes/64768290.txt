Title:
Herring in Sourcream / Lady of the House  " Matjes Hausfrauenart" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Herring Filets
1 Large Dill Pickle
1 small Onion
1 small Apple
6 oz of Sour Cream
Salt, Pepper,
Fresh Dill Sprigs
2 Tsp Butter.

Directions:
Peel the Pickle and Dice,  same with the the Onion and Apple (remove core).  Blanch the Vegtables in about a 1/2 cup of water until Soft, Drain and then "sautee" in Butter.   Let Cool and Pat Dry.  Mix with the Sour-cream,  Salt & Pepper.  Roll 1 Filet ea. on two plates,  Ladle this Sauce over the Herring.  Serve the rest Sauce "apart"  in a "Sauciere" .  Garnish with Dill Sprig.  Serve with Toast.
