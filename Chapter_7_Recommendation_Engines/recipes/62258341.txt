Title:
Pasta E Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dried navy beans
1 T. oil
1 c. chopped onions
1/2 c. sliced carrot
1/2 c. chopped celery
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 (28 oz) can tomatoes
9 c. water
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 bay leaf
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/4 tsp. dried rosemary
2 garlic cloves, minced
6 oz. elbow macaroni
1 c. chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
1/4 tsp. black pepper
3 oz. grated fresh parmesan

Directions:
Sort and wash beans and place in a large Dutch oven.  Cover with water to 2 inches above beans; bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat, cover and let stand for one hour.  Drain beans in a colander.
Heat oil in pan over medium heat till hot.  Add onion, carrot and celery and saute for 5 minutes or till tender.  Add 2 cloves garlic, cook one minute.  Add tomatoes.  Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add beans, water, red pepper and bay leaf, and bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce
heat, and simmer for 2 hours.  Add salt, oregano, thyme, rosemary and 2 garlic cloves; cover and simmer till beans are tender (1/2 hour?)  Discard bay leaf.
Place 2 c. bean mixture in a food processor; process till smooth.  Return bean puree to pan, stir well.  Add pasta; cook 7 minutes or till done.
Remove from heat; stir in parsley and pepper.  Ladle soup into bowls and sprinkle with cheese.
