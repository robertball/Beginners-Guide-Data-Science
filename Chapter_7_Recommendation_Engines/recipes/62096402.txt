Title:
All Purpose Hawaiian Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups unsweetened pineapple or pineapple coconut juice
2 cups soy sauce
1 cup dry sherry
1/2 cup rice vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon fresh ginger root, grated
1 tablespoon Sesame Oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 Green Onions, diced
2 teaspoons garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon Chinese 5 Spice Powder
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
*Meat or Poultry of your choice

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix all ingredients together. Allow meat to marinate in zip top baggies for 12 to 48 hours. Remove meat from bags and pour marinade into sauce pan.  Bring to boil over medium high heat and for 10 minutes.  Baste meat with marinade in between flips on the grill.

Submitter: 
SAVVYHOSTESS
Description: 
I originally concocted this recipe for a wedding shower luau I hosted.  I used it on chicken thighs and legs, but since then, I've tried it beef and pork, as well.
