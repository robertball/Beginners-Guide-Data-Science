Title:
Veggie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable Pizza
2 pillsbury jumbo crescent rolls
2 packages of cream cheese (lite)
1/2 cup of mayonnaise (lite)
3 tsp. dill
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 jar mild salsa
2 large carrots - grated
finely chopped cauliflower (enough to cover)
finely chopped broccoli (enough to cover)
grated sharp cheddar cheese (enough to cover the top)

Directions:
Bake the crescent rolls until lightly browned - about 10 minutes. Mix together in a bowl the cream cheese, mayonnaise and spices. Spread onto the cooled crust. Over top spread a layer of salsa.
Add a layer of carrots, then cauliflower and broccoli. Finally add the shredded cheese. Refrigerate overnight. This will give the flavors a
chance to blend. Cut into slices and enjoy. To this pizza, you can also add a layer of shrimp15 , cut up for a more festive touch.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
Easy, delicious and never any leftovers. This pizza is very festive for the holidays and great for when its too hot to cook.
