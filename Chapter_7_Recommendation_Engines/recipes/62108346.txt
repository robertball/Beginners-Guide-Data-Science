Title:
Fig Stuffed Baked Brie Party Wheel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large wheel Brie, cut in half from side to side so there is a top and bottom.
1 jar fig preserves
1 cup sliced or slivered almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Open halves of Brie like a sandwich.  Slather jar of fig preserves on one half of the Brie.  Sprinkle with almonds, and carefully, replace 2nd half on top of fig preserves, cheese side down.
Transfer wheel to a shallow foil lined baking sheet and bake 25 minutes. Let cool about 5 minutes before serving.
Arrange Brie in center of a large platter, Place cheese knives and spreaders for cutting bite size pieces on platter and serve warm with crackers or Melba toast rounds.

Submitter: 
Linda Glenn
Description: 
My brother came up with this idea and it's been a winner every time it's been served at family functions!
