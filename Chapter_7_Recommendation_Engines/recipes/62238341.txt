Title:
Red Pepper Hummus Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup red pepper hummus
2 Tbl dry ranch dressing mix
2 Tbl parsley flakes
2/3 cup lowfat milk
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  Chill for at least 30 minutes.  Serve over salad.

Submitter: 
reneeBcookin
Description: 
I was at a loss for a healthy, tasty salad dressing.  So I came up with this recipe.  There's a lot of room to play around with this one.
