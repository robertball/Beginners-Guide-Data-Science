Title:
Cheesy Tomato & Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034186.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. grated cheddar cheese
1/2 c. grated mozzarella cheese
1/3 c. grated parmesan cheese
1/2 tea. dried oregano
1/2 tea. chopped fresh basil
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 onion diced
salt & Pepper to taste
1 medium zucchini thinly sliced
1 medium yellow squash thinly sliced
3 tomatoes thinly sliced
bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Lightly butter casserole dish.  In large bowl, combine cheeses, oregano, basil, salt and pepper.  Saut√© onions in butter until soft and add bread crumbs.  In casserole dish layers.. zucchini, 1/4 of cheese mixture, tomatoes, 1/4 cheese mixture, yellow squash,  1/4 cheese mixture, tomatoes and rest of cheese mixture.  Sprinkle bread crumb/onion mixture as top layer.  Bake for 25 min foil covered, remove foil base until brown.
Hint... place tomatoes on several layers of paper towels to drain.  Salt and pepper each layer of vegetables.

Submitter: 
Lynn Mauk
Description: 
Great use of summer vegetables.  A dish that goes great with BBQ chicken.
