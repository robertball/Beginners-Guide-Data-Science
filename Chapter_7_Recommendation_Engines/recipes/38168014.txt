Title:
Pumpkin Coconut Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sugar for caramel
1 small can pumpkin puree
1 14 oz can coconut milk
6 large eggs
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt or to taste
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Prepare caramel for baine Marie
Mix the rest of the ingredients in blender and pour over prepared mold.
Bake in Baine Marie until done, 350 F
