Title:
Ranch Cheddar Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. ranch dressing
1 T. flour
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/4 c. shredded sharp cheddar
1/4 c. grated parmesan

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Mix together dressing and flour.
Coat each chicken breast with mixture and place in a casserole dish.
Combine cheeses and sprinkle over the chicken.
Bake 25 min. or until cooked through.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
The dressing makes the chicken juicy and delicious.
