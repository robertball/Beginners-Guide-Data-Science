Title:
THE Vanilla custard ice cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125069.jpg
Ingredients:
16 egg  yolks
2 cups castor sugar or honey
4 cups milk
4 cups heavy cream
8 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Beat egg yolks with sugar until thick and well blended
Pour milk in sauce pan and scald
Pour some hot milk into the egg mixture while stirring rapidly to temper the eggs, while still stirring add egg mixture back into pan of hot milk
Heat until custard thickens and leaves a film on back of spoon. DO NOT LET IT BOIL OR IT WILL CURDLE
Chill overnight.
Right before putting in ice cream freezer, stir in heavy cream and vanilla.
Churn according to directions on churn.

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
The richest ice cream on the planet. 
I use fresh eggs, Jersey cream and homemade real vanilla.
Goatsmilk would be good too.
