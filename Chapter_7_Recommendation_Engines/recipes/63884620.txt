Title:
Baking Powder Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077668.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c all purpose flour
1 t sugar
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 c shortening
3/4 c milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 450.
In med bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder & salt. cut in shortening, using pastry blender, until mixture looks like fine crumbs. Stir in milk until dough leaves side of bowl.
Place dough on lightly floured surface. Knead lightly 10 times. Roll or pat 1/2" thick. Cut with floured 2 1/4" round cutter. Place on ungreased cookie sheet about 1" apart for crusty sides, touching for soft sides.
Bake 10-12 mins or until golden brown. Immediately remove from cookie sheet. Serve warm.
