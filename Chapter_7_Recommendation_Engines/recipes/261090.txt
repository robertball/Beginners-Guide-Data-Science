Title:
Pop Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4578162.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2.9 ounce) bag microwave popcorn
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup melted butter
1 cup savory baked cereal snack mix (such as Bits & Bites)
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place packaged popcorn in the microwave; cook on high until popping begins to slow down, about 2 1/2 minutes. Mix in Parmesan cheese. Drizzle melted butter over popcorn. Mix in savory snack mix and salt.
