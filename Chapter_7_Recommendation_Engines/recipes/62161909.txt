Title:
Texas Glazed Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg white
4 tsp. water
3 tsp. pure vanilla extract
3/4 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup sugar
1 lb. pecan halves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
In a medium size mixing bowl, beat egg white until frothy.
Wisk in water and vanilla.
Add salt, cinnamon, and sugar then stir.
Add pecans and stir until completely coated.
Spread a single layer of the coated pecans on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
Roast for 1 hour while stopping to stir every 15 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes.  ENJOY!!

Submitter: 
Texas Gal
Description: 
Glazed cinnamon and vanilla roasted  pecans taste just like the state fair!!
