Title:
Italian Style Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556750.jpg
Ingredients:
1  2 Pound Roasted Chicken
1  Fennel Bulb (Cored And Sliced Thin)
3  Celery Stalks (Slice thin and use leaves too)
1  Red Onion (Slice Thinly)
1/2  Cup Good Extra Virgin Olive oil
3  Cloves of garlic (Chopped)
2  Lemons (Juiced and 1 Zested)
1/2 Cup Fresh Parsely (Chopped)
1/2 Cup Of Mayonnaise
Salt and Pepper to taste
Bunch of argula to use as a bed for the salad

Directions:
You can either roast your chicken ahead of time or just buy a pre roasted bird if you want to save time. Break down the chicken into small bite size pieces into a large bowl. Add thin sliced fennel, celery, and red onions and give a toss to incorperate. Set Aside.
In a pan add olive oil and heat to a moderate temperture. Once Hot add in the garlic and saute till soft and golden brown, Just watch that it is not overcooked. As soon as ths is ready add all of the lemon juice to the pan. Remove from heat and allow to cool to room temperture.
Add to chicken mixture. Then add your parsley mayonnaise, lemon zest, and pepper and salt.
Now you can lay a few pieces of arugula on the plate in a form of a bed and top with the chicken salad. Then Enjoy!!

Description: 
Warm roasted Chicken and garlic adds flavor, Fresh fennel and onion and celery give nice crunch. Served on a bed of arugula. You are going to love this one!
