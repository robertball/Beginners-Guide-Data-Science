Title:
Maria's Chicken Tamale Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/491221.jpg
Ingredients:
12 chicken tamales (we use handmade ones from our friend Maria)
1 cup uncooked rice
1 3/4 cup water
1 (10 oz) can diced tomatoes with green chilies (do not drain)
1/2 t garlic salt
1/2 t onion powder
1/4 t chili powder
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup sour cream
1 (7 oz) can Salsa Verde (Mexican Green Sauce)
2 cups shredded cheese of your choice (we use a blend of Cheddar, Monterey Jack, Asadero, and Queso Quesadilla)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray the bottom and sides of a 9" x 13" pan.  Unwrap chicken tamales and set aside.
Cook rice in water according to package directions.  When rice is ready, fluff and turn it into a large bowl.
Add the diced tomatoes with green chilies, garlic salt, onion powder, chili powder, salt and pepper.
Blend well and spread in bottom of pan.
Lay chicken tamales on top of rice mixture.
Mix sour cream with Salsa Verde and pour evenly over tamales.
Sprinkle top with cheese.
Bake covered for 20 minutes then remove cover and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until thoroughly hot.  Let sit for 10-15 minutes before serving.

Description: 
Handmade chicken tamales on a bed of Spanish rice with a creamy tomatillo sauce and topped with a blend of Mexican cheeses.
