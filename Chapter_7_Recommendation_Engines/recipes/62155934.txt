Title:
Kickin Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rice
1/4 tsp salt
2 TBSP butter
3 dried chile peppers
1 chicken breast, diced
1 TBSP sesame oil (you can use vegetable oil in a pinch)
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
1/8-1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tsp basil
1/2 tsp ground mustard
1 tsp ground white pepper
pinch turmeric
1c green peppers, diced
1c red bell peppers, diced
1/2c onion, diced
1 1/2c broccoli
1 tsp lemon juice
1 TBSP butter

Directions:
Add 2 TBSP butter, 1/4 tsp salt and 3 dried, crushed chile peppers to 6 cups of water. Heat to a boil. Add rice, lower heat to medium, and cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
While the water for rice, warm oil in a sautee pan or wok. Add garlic and sautee for a minute before adding half of the soy sauce.
Add chicken and spices and lightly brown. Add the rest of the soy sauce.
In a separate pan, add 1 TBSP butter and vegetables. Gently sautee and add lemon juice.
Combine vegetables with chicken and stir. Add soy sauce if desired.
Once rice is done, scoop 1/2 of it onto each plate. Scoop chicken and vegetables on top of the piles of rice and serve.
For garnish, sprinkle sesame seeds on top.

Description: 
A great, easy stir fry with a good, spicy kick. This is a great way to stretch your protein while enjoying an awesome meal for two!
