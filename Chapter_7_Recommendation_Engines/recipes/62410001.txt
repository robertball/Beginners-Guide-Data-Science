Title:
Spicy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436967.jpg
Ingredients:
4 onions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound ground beef
1 pound Hot Italian sausage, casing removed
1 (14.5 ounce) can peeled and diced tomatoes with juice
12 fl oz dark beer
1 cup strong brewed coffee
2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
1/2 cup chili powder
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1 teaspoon salt
1 (1.5 oz) tube of wasabi paste
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, liquid drained
2 (15 ounce) cans pinto beans, liquid drained
3 Anaheim chile peppers, chopped
1 serrano pepper, chopped
1 habanero pepper, sliced

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat brown onions, garlic, beef, sausage and 1/4 cup chili powder.  Transfer to 5 qt Crockpot.  Pour in the tomatoes, beer, coffee, tomato paste and broth.  Season with 1/4 cup chili powder, cumin, brown sugar, oregano, cayenne, coriander, salt and wasabi.
Cook on low heat for 4 hours.
In a large skillet over medium heat, 2 tbs oil.  Cook Anaheim, serrano and habanero peppers in oil until just tender, 5 to 10 minutes.  Add to Crockpot with 3 cans of beans and cook 2 hours more.
