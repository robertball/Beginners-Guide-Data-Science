Title:
Maplenut Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/770093.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup unsalted natural peanut butter
a big pinch of salt

Directions:
Start with room temperature peanut butter and maple syrup, sprinkle on your salt and stir until well mixed. Spread and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Beyker
Description: 
A quick-and-easy luscious butter to spread over baked sweet potatoes and toast.
