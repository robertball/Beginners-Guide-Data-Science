Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium cucumber(s)
1 tablespoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1 tablespoon vinegar, balsamic
1/2 teaspoon peppercorns, black, Freshly Ground

Directions:
Arrange the cucumber in a rotating pattern on a large serving plate.
Combine the oil and vinegar and drizzle over the salad. Sprinkle the pepper over the top.
