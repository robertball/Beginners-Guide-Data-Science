Title:
Lactation Friendly Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut oil
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
4 tbs water
2 tbs ground flax
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
2 cups plain, uncooked oats
1-2 cups cacao nibs (or chocolate chips)
4 tbs brewers yeast

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Mix flaxseed and water and let sit 3-5 minutes
Beat coconut oil and sugars, add eggs and mix
add flax seed and vanilla
Mix in all dry ingredients except oats and cacoa until well incorporated.
Stir in oats and cacoa nibs (or chips, nut)
Bake 10-12 minutes
