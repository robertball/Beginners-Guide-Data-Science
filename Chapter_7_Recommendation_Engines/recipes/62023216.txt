Title:
Perfect Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups softened butter
1 lb box confectioner sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla or 1 tsp. lemon & 1 tsp. vanilla
6 large eggs
3 cups sifted plain flour

Directions:
Combine butter and sugar, beat medium speed 5 minutes until creamy. Add vanilla. Alternately add eggs and flour to the creamed mixture. Bake 325 60-70 minutes or until test done with a toothpick.

Submitter: 
Maybelle Miller
Description: 
Simple to make, and melt in your mouth delicious makes this cake perfect.
