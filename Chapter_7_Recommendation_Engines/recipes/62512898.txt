Title:
Ridgeway Dinner Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 Tbsp. yeast
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
2 cups warm water
Just over 1/4 cup oil
2-3 cups flour

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well. Add enough flour to make a sticky, but manageable dough (about 2-3 more cups). Let dough rise 1 hour.
Roll out rolls and place them on a greased pan. Let rolls rise until they have doubled (about 30-45 minutes).
Bake at 425 for 15 minutes.
