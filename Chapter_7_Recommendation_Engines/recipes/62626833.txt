Title:
Salsa Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tomatillos
1 jalapeno
1 heaping T minced garlic
1/2 yellow onion
1/3 bushel de-stemmed cilantro
1 t sea salt
1 small tomato
1 t raw sugar
2 t dried oregano
4 avocados
Juice of a lime

Directions:
Boil tomatillos for 5-7 minutes adn core. Take tomatillos and blend with all ingredients except avocados and onion until smooth.  Add onion and pulse until onion's size is small, but even in consistency.  Cut avocados into crouton-sized portions and add to blended ingredients.  Chill before serving.
