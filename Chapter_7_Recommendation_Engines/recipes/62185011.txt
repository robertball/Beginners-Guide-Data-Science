Title:
Bacon Ranch Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon
1 quart water
1 (16 ounce) package frozen green peas
1/3 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup Ranch dressing
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
Â½ cup pimento
12 carrort sticks

Directions:
Place bacon in a skillet over medium-high heat, and cook until evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
Bring the water to a boil in a pot. Boil the peas 1 minute, until just tender, and drain. Cool peas under cold running water.
In a bowl, toss together bacon, peas, pimento, onion, Ranch dressing, and Cheddar cheese. Refrigerate 30 minutes or until chilled before serving.  Top with carrot sticks

Description: 
Peas, bacon, cheese, onions, and Ranch dressing are tossed together and chilled in this great summer salad.Modified from: Bacon Ranch Pea Salad
