Title:
Sesame Ginger Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 16oz package of shredded cabbage
1/2-3/4 cup sliced almonds
1-2 green onions sliced
4 T low fat sesame ginger dressing (Newmans Own)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Ready to serve.
