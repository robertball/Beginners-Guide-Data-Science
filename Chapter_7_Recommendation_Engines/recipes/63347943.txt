Title:
Sauteed Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
4 large tart apples - peeled, cored and sliced 1/4 inch thick
2 teaspoons arrowroot powder
1/2 cup cold water
1/2 cup raw cane sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a large skillet or saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; add apples. Cook, stirring constantly, until apples are almost tender, about 6 to 7 minutes.
Dissolve arrowroot in water; add to skillet. Stir in sugar and cinnamon. Boil for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Alex
Description: 
Sweet, sauteed apples are great for breakfast, but you can serve them at any meal. The syrup from the apples is delicious on homemade pancakes or waffles.Modified from: Sauteed Apples
