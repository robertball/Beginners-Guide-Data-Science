Title:
Spicy Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Sun-dried Tomatoes
3/4 cup 2% Greek Yogurt
2 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
1/2  tsp Chile Powder
1/4 tsp Cayenne Pepper
2 Tbsp Diced Red Onion
1 head Romaine Lettuce, finely shredded
1 1/2 cups Diced Cooked Chicken Breast
1 Cucumber, diced
1 pint Cherry Tomatoes, halved
1 3/4 cups Chickpeas
4 oz Cheddar, diced

Directions:
Soften tomatoes in 1/2 cup hot water. Puree in blender with yogurt, vinegar, chile and cayenne; place in bowl. Stir in onion and cornichons. Toss with remaining ingredients. Serve.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
In my opinion, Chicken goes so well with vegetables, so whenever there is leftover chicken breast, I enjoy making a chicken salad. The Greek yogurt adds a nice creamyness.
