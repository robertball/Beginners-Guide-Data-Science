Title:
Race Day Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/590399.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ears corn on the cob, husk on
2-15 oz. cans no-salt black beans, drained and rinsed
6 italian plum tomatoes or 4 regular tomatoes
1 med. green pepper, seeded and diced
1 med. red onion, diced
2 jalapeno or other variety, chopped with the seeds
1 lime, juiced with pulp
2 tsp. fresh cilantro, chopped
2 cloves fresh garlic
1 12 oz. can tomato juice
1-14 oz. can tomato sauce
Kosher salt and fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Roast corn in husk on grill.  Let cool and then remove from cob.  Place in large glass bowl.
Chop/Dice all vegetables, cilantro and lime placing in a the bowl with the corn.  Fold together.
Drain and rinse black beans, place in bowl and add tomato sauce and juice.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Fold together carefully to avoid mashing the beans and tomatoes.
Let sit for at least one hour, preferably overnight.  Can be frozen for up to 3 months with some loss of texture.

Submitter: 
VH
Description: 
Extremely fresh, healthy and crunchy, 'not your typical' salsa!  This is my husbands special treat for NASCAR Race Days.
