Title:
Crockpot Mac n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/761109.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. macaroni, cooked & drained
1 stick of butter
3 cups cheese, grated (your favorite)
1/2 cup of velveeta
1 egg
2 cups milk
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Grease crock pot w/ Pam. Mix all ingred. Cover and cook on low for 3 hours.
I use cheddar and about 1 1/2 hour into cooking I cover the top with more cheddar and then continue to cook. It is very yummy!!!!!!
