Title:
Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 egg
3 tablespoons crushed buttery round cracker crumbs
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon poultry seasoning
.
2 pats butter
.
3 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons Wondra
3 cups milk
1 can (14 oz) beef broth
.
2 (4 ounce) cans sliced mushrooms, drained

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, cracker crumbs, onion, salt, pepper and poultry seasoning using your hands.
Shape into 6 patties about 1 inch thick.
Melt 2 pats of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat and saute the patties for 3 to 4 minutes per side, or until browned.
Remove patties to a platter; keep warm.
Melt three tablespoons butter in the same skillet.
Sprinkle the Wondra over, and whisk until blended.
Cook the Wondra mixture for two to three minutes on medium heat, stirring constantly.
Stir in the milk and beef broth. Cook and stir over medium heat until smooth and thickened.
Stir in the mushrooms.
Return the patties to the gravy and cook over low heat, uncovered, for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
