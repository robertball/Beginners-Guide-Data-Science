Title:
Instant Pot Coconut Fish Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1.5 lb  Fish steaks or fillets, ( I use Halibut or tilapias)
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 14 oz can chopped tomatoes
2 large sweet potatoes
1 crown broccoli
2 medium onions
2 garlic cloves
1 Tbsp Ginger powder
6 Basil Leaves
1 Tbsp ground coriander
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp ground turmeric
1 tsp chili powder
2.5 blocks of S & B Golden Curry sauce mix
1 13 oz can coconut milk or coconut cream
1 cup Coconut milk
Salt to taste

Directions:
In the  Instant pot  on saut√©, add a swirl of oil and then drop in the curry leaves and lightly fry them until golden around the edges (about 1 minute).
Then add the onion, garlic, and sweet potato and saute until the onion is soft.
Add all of the ground spices: Coriander, Cumin, Tumeric, Chili Powder and ginger and sautee them together with the onions until they have released their aroma (about 2 minutes).
De-glaze with the coconut milk making sure to un-stick anything from the bottom of the cooker and incorporate it into the sauce.
Add the, Tomatoes, fish pieces, and golden curry sauce mix. Stir to coat the fish well with the mixture.
Close the lid and set the valve to pressure cooking position.
Cook for 10 minutes on pressure cook.
When time is up, release pressure using the Normal method - release vapor through the valve.
Add salt to taste.
Serve alone, or with steamed rice.
