Title:
Potato Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mashed potatoes
1/2 cup AP flour
3 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together
Flatten on a floured board.
Cooked on a pre-heated, with oil, cast iron griddle until browned on each side
