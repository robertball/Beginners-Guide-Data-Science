Title:
Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C sugar
1/2C crisco
2 eggs
3-4Tbsp. molasses
2C flour
1tsp baking soda mixed in 1C sour milk
1tsp cinnamon
1/4tsp ginger,allspice,and cloves
1tsp salt

Directions:
Cream the sugar and shortning, add the eggs, molasses. Sift the flour and spices together and add alternately with the sour milk. Once combined bake in the oven at 350 until knife comes out clean from the center of the cake.
