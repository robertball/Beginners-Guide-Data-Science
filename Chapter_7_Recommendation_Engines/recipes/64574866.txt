Title:
Grandmia's Italian Skillet Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3590523.jpg
Ingredients:
1 white onion
1 zucchini squash
1 summer squash
8 oz. fresh mushrooms, siiced (can use 4 oz. canned mushrooms, drained)
1 pint grape tomatoes
6 ounces pepperoni mini slices
Pepper (to taste)
Sea salt (to taste)
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. dried basil leaves
3 Tbsp. olive oil
1 Tbsp. butter
1 - 14 ounce jar spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Spiralize the zucchini and summer squash.  Place in colander and sprinkle with approximately 1 teaspoon of sea salt.  Allow to sweat for about 30 minutes.
In the meantime, slice onion thinly.  Heat olive oil and butter over medium high heat.  Add onion and allow to cook until caramelized (about 10 minutes).
Slice mushrooms (or drain canned mushrooms).  When onions are caramelized, add spiralized zucchini and summer squash.  Cook for 5 minutes over medium heat.  Halve grape tomatoes.  Add to skillet with mushrooms and pepperoni.
Season with pepper, 1 tsp. dried oregano, and 1 tsp. dried basil leaves.  Cook for about 5 minutes.  Add pasta sauce and heat thoroughly.
Serve with garlic bread/toast.
Serves 6.
