Title:
Frocaccia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe pizza dough
2 Tbsp.olive oil
1/2 cup mozzerella
2 Tbsp.fresh rosemary or
1 tsp. dried
1 tsp.coarse salt

Directions:
Roll out dough to 1/2 inch thick.Poke with fork.Brush with olive oil.Spread with cheese,rosemary and salt.Bake in oven at 400 for 20-25 min.
Dip into:
1/4 cup each of olive oil and malt vinigar.one crushed garlic clove and sprinkle oregano on top.
