Title:
Italian Easter Meat Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (9 inch)  unbaked pie crusts
2 pounds ricotta cheese
8 eggs, hard boiled and sliced
8 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 pound cooked ham, sliced thin
1/2 pound Genoa salami, sliced thin
1/2 pound prosciutto, sliced thin
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Place ricotta in a mixing bowl, mix in mozzarella.
Line two 9 inch pans with pastry. In each pie pan...Layer one layer of chopped eggs to cover bottom.  Layer with ricotta cheese.  layer sliced ham slami and prosciutto.  Repeat layers.
Brush water on edge of crust...Put on top crust and pinch edges to seal.  Brush with egg white until just covered.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour, until crust is golden brown. Cool on racks.

Description: 
This meat pie is a winner every Easter. Stuffed full of ham, salami, and prosciutto, as well as ricotta, Parmesan, and mozzarella cheese. This pie is requested many times during the year.!Modified from: Easter Meat Pie
