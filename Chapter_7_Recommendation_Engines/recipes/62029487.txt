Title:
Spicy Shrimp in Beer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, chopped
4 - 6 cloves garlic, chopped
1 can beer
1 to 3 pounds unpeeled fresh shrimp
1/3 jar (or to taste) spicy seafood spices such as Paul Prudhomme's Seafood Magic
ground red, black and white peppers to taste

Directions:
Melt butter over low heat.  Add oil, onion and garlic.  Cover and cook over low heat to soften onion approx. 15 minutes.  Add beer and seasonings and bring to boil.  Add shrimp, reduce heat and simmer uncovered until pink,  about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
MARYSTEVE
Description: 
Peel & eat shrimp.  Perfect al fresco meal!  Cover the table with newspapers and serve in a bowl with lots of crusty French bread for dipping up the sauce. Provide bowls for discarded shells.
