Title:
Mexican Chicken Gnocchi Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037584.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 C butter
1 large yellow onion, sliced thinly
1 anaheim pepper, diced
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1 Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp thyme
1/3 C flour
2 C Half and Half
3 C Chicken broth
3 dried chile peppers (optional)
3 chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
2 large carrots, sliced
1 1/2 C whole kernel corn
1 large tomato, diced
1 lb package gnocchi

Directions:
Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat.  Add onion, anaheim pepper, garlic, chili powder, black pepper, salt, red pepper flakes, oregano, cumin, paprika, and thyme.  Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Stir flour into vegetable mixture and stir to combine thoroughly.  Slowly add half and half and broth into vegetable mixture.  Add dried chiles, chicken, carrots, corn, and tomato.  Heat thoroughly, stirring often.
Add gnocchi to soup and serve when cooked through, approximately 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
This tasty recipe is a great spin on chicken gnocchi soup, and so easily adaptable!  Great served as-is with tortillas, or leave the gnocchi out entirely and serve with drop dumplings or over biscuits.  Wonderful, deep flavor and a filling meal on a chilly night!
