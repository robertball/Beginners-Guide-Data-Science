Title:
Carrot Raisin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 carrot sticks
1 cup raisins
3-4 Tablespoons mayo or salad dressing
1 small can crushed pineapple, drained with 1 tablespoon juice reserved

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill until time to serve!
The longer this salad chills, the juicier the raisins get!
