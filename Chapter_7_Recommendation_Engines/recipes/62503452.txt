Title:
Val's Cinnamon Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups warm water
1 cup milk
3/8 cup oil
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 Tbsp. salt
3 1/2 cups flour
2 Tbsp. yeast
Butter, brown sugar and cinnamon
Sauce:
1/4 cup corn syrup
1 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar

Directions:
In a separate mixing bowl, combine water, milk, oil and eggs. In another bowl, combine sugar and salt, then mix in with liquids. Add flour and yeast and mix together. Add flour and let rise in bowl for 1 hour (in a warm place). Put oil on top of dough. When finger impression stays, it is done rising.
Roll out 2 rectangles. Spread butter on dough, then brown sugar and cinnamon. Mix sauce ingredients and cook in a saucepan until sugar has dissolved. Pour into bottom of two 9 x 13 pans. Bake for 22-25 minutes as 350 F.
