Title:
California Snack Roll Up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 leaves lettuce, red leaf
4 ounce(s) turkey, deli-style, smoked, fat-free, sliced
4 slice(s) tomato(es) thinly sliced
4 teaspoon watercress chopped
4 teaspoon avocado cubed
2 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
Whisk lemon juice and olive oil together, set aside. Combine all the other ingredients, mix in the dressing. Serve
