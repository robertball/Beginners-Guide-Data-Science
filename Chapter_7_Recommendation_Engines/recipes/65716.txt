Title:
Saucy Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound medium-size shrimp
1/4 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Baste & Glaze
2 tablespoons dry sherry
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon sliced green onion
3 drops hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Shell, devein and butterfly shrimp. Combine teriyaki baste & glaze, sherry, lime juice, green onion and pepper sauce; add shrimp, stirring to coat well. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour.
Remove shrimp and place on rack of broiler pan. Broil 2 to 3 minutes, or until opaque and cooked. Serve immediately with wooden picks.

Submitter: 
Kikkoman
Description: 
A zesty glaze makes these shrimp wonderful appetizers for any party.
