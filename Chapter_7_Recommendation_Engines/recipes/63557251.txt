Title:
Pulled pork salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pulled pork
Coleslaw mix
Lettuce
Thin sliced carrots
Thin sliced green and red pepper
Pineapple
Onion
Celery
Cucumber
BBQdressing:
1/2cup lite mayo
1/4 cup habenaro BBQ sauce
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp honey
1tsp minced onion
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Make pulled pork- in a slow cooker, use pork shoulder butt or picnic, in beef broth, 8 hours. Pull, add 1/2 original bullseye, 1/2 presidents choice smokin habenaro. Make salad, add meat and dressing.
