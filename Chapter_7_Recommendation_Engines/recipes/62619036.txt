Title:
Sweet Potato French Toast, Vegan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mashed sweet potatoes
3 0z tofu per egg (4 eggs)
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
8 slices whole wheat bread

Directions:
Whisk together the sweet potato, tofu, cinnamon, and nutmeg until smooth. Dip the bread into the mixture on both sides for several seconds until the bread is moist all the way through.
Heat a large, lightly-oiled skillet over medium heat. Cook the French toast in batches until golden brown on each side and no longer wet in the center, about 4 minutes per side.
