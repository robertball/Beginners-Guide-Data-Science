Title:
Memphis Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup paprika
1 tablespoon packed dark brown sugar
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons tenderizer
1 teaspoon celery salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
In a small airtight container, mix paprika, dark brown sugar, white sugar, salt, monosodium glutamate, celery salt, ground black pepper, cayenne pepper, dry mustard, garlic powder and onion powder. Seal and store in a cool dark place until ready for use.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
This zesty rub will bring out the best in ribs and other meats. Try it on chicken!Modified from: Memphis Rub
