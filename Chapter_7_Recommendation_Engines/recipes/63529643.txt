Title:
Salt Free Sazon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/963804.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp of Ground Annato (Achiote) or Turmeric
1 Tbsp of Ground Coriander
1 Tbsp of Ground Oregano
1/2 Tbsp of Ground Cumin
Optional: 1 Tbsp of Powdered Garlic
Optional: 1 Tbsp of Powdered Onion

Directions:
Mix everything into a small container, such as a jar with a lid. Use only 1 Tbsp at a time for recipes that call for Sazon.
