Title:
Blue Cheese Garlic Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/179595.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, cubed
1 cup crumbled blue cheese
2 garlic cloves, peeled
Assorted crackers

Directions:
In a blender, combine the milk, cream cheese, blue cheese and garlic; cover and process until blended. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Lillian Nardi
Description: 
This thick, creamy dip is my mom's recipe and a family favorite for ringing in the season. It also makes a tasty substitute for mayonnaise on chicken and turkey sandwiches.
