Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436743.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 ounce package of slightly softened cream cheese
1/2 Cup- powdered sugar
1 tsp- vanilla

Directions:
With electric mixer, blend together the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla.
(For the recipe "Pumpkin Bars") Frost the cooled cake using a knife or metal spatula.

Submitter: 
R H
Description: 
Delicious, creamy frosting to top almost any dessert. This is specifically for my other recipe Pumpkin Bars but is not limited to that. This is delicious!
