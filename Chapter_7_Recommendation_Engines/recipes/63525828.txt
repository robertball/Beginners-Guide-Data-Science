Lean and Green Celery Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup chopped celery, leaves and all (2 greens)
1 tablespoon chopped mint or sweet basil leaves (1/2 condiment)
1 teaspoon lemon juice (1/2 condiment)
1 teaspoon olive oil (1 healthy fat)

Directions:
