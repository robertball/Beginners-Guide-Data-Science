Title:
Serbian Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can spinach
8 ounces grated cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons margarine, melted
3 ounces cottage cheese
3 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Blend margarine and flour.  Add remaining ingredients.  Stir together.  Bake for 35 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This is a great way to get your family to eat spinach.  I don't like spinach, but love this casserole.
