Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage
1/2 pound ground beef
1 cup shredded carrots
1 cup chopped water chestnuts
1/2 cup chopped onion
TOPPING:
2 T sesame oil
3 T soy sauce
1/4 cup grated ginger
1 clove garlic
1/3 cup shopped cilantro

Directions:
Separate leaves of cabbage. Cook cabbage 5 mins in water. Place inside leaves of cabbage in 9x13 casserole pan.
Brown meat. Add carrots, water chestnuts, onion cook another few minutes. Wrap meat mixture in outer leaves and place in casserole.
Mix topping ingredients together and pour over cabbages. Brush with EVOO. Bake 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
