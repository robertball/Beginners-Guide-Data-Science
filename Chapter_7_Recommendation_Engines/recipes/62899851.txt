HANGOVER HELPER Recipe

Ingredients:

3 CUPS VANILLA ICE CREAM
1 1/2 CUPS MILK
6oz.RASPBERRY LIQUEUR

Directions:

Serve in empty pepto-bismol bottes or bottle of your choice,don't forget the straws!
