Title:
One Month Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of green cabbage
1 bag shredded carrot
1 bag shredded broccoli
1 chopped onion
---
2/3 cup canola oil
1 cup sugar
1 cup apple cider vinegar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp mustard

Directions:
1. Cut the cabbage into quarters and slice thinly with a chefs knife. Grate carrots and onions and put into a large bowl. 2. Bring non-veggies to a rolling boil then remove from heat. Once room temperature, pour over cabbage mixture.

Submitter: 
AnneP
Description: 
Easy recipe for coleslaw that can last a month in the fridge...if it lasts that long with the family!
