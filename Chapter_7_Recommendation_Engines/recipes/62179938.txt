Title:
MsChristy Au Gratin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 (10.75 ounce) condensed cream of chicken soup
24 oz. Ore-Ida hashbrowns Southern style (thawed)
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2 garlic clove
1/4 teaspoon of Cayenne pepper
1/2 c. chopped celery
1 small chopped onion or green onion
2 cup cornflakes cereal or 3 cups Ritz crackers
crushed & mixed 1/3 stick melted butter
Top 1/2 cup Romano cheese for added flavor (optional)
sprinkle with paprika
1 tsp. black pepper & salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray one 9 x 13 inch baking dish with vegetable cooking spray.
Saut√©ed onion, garlic and celery with butter.
In a bowl, stir together the sour cream and soup, garlic, celery and onions with spices. Mix well.
Layer half of potatoes in dish; spoon half of sauce over potatoes.  Top with half of cheese.  Repeat layering with remaining potatoes, sauce and cheese.  Sprinkle with paprika, if desired.
Cover the dish with a lid or aluminum foil.
Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven. In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter and cornflakes. Sprinkle over the top of the potatoes, and return to the oven.
Bake uncovered, for an additional 20 minutes or until bubbly and corn flakes are golden brown. Remove from the oven and serve!

Submitter: 
MsChristy
Description: 
This creamy potato dish sidles up nicely to beef, poultry or pork.  The corn flake topping adds crunch or Ritz crackers while the paprika provides additional color.Modified from: MsChristy's Au Gratin Potatoes
