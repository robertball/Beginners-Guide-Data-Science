Title:
Big Ray's Killer Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006255.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1 can chili style diced tomatoes(14.5 oz),drained with liguid reserved.
1 cup chunky salsa
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium green pepper, diced
1 cup diced celery
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbl brown sugar
1 Tbl Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbl white wine vinegar
1 tsp hot sauce
2 Tbl corn starch
8 hard rolls

Directions:
Brown ground beef until no longer pink.  Drain fat and mash with potato masher until no lumps remain.
Puree the diced tomatoes in a food processer or blender until smooth.
Put ground beef into slow cooker  and add remaining ingredients, stirring to mix together.  Cook on low for 5 and 1/2  hours. Remove cover and turn to high for remaining 1/2 hourto allow to thicken.  If still too soupy, mix remaining liquid from tomatoes with corn starch to thicken. Serve on toasted hard rolls or hamburger buns.
