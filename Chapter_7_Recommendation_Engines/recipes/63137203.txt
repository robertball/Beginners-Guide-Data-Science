Title:
Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/802116.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Carrots
2 Celery stalks
2 large cloves of garlic
1 Large Onion
Pulse the above in food processor for soup base.
1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
2 15 oz cans diced tomatoes with jalaperios
1 10 oz can enchilada sauce
1 15 oz can of whole corn
2 15 oz cans black beans, drained and rinsed.
3 cups of water
1 1/2 tsp chili powder
3 corn tortillas, torn into 1" pieces.
1-2 TB lime juice.
2 TB fresh cilantro.

Directions:
Saute the first four ingredients in a little oil olive until wilted.
Add everything, except the cilantro and lime juice in a slow cooker or heavy soup pot.  Slow Cooker:Cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8-10 hours. On the stove-top for 2 hours.
Remove the chicken and shred with two forks, return to pot with the cilantro and lime juice. Taste and add extra seasonings if desired.
Serve with optional garnishes such as diced avocado, lime wedges, sour cream, shredded cheese and toasted tortilla strips.

Submitter: 
dablacks77
Description: 
Omitted Chicken stock to cut out the sodium. Used a veg. base of diced onion, carrot and garlic, celery for soup base. This veggy soup base can be used for any soup and is very healthy, as there is no animal fat.
