Title:
Pear Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads romaine lettuce
1 head lettuce (your choice)
1/2 cup toasted pecans, chopped
3-4 pears, sliced
1/2 lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
4 oz. feta or Gorgonzola cheese
Dressing:
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 Tbsp. minced, dry onion
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup oil
1 tsp. poppy seeds

Directions:
Combine dressing ingredients in blender and blend in oil. Pulse in poppy seeds. Chill (this dressing freezes well).
Combine salad ingredients and top with dressing just before serving.
