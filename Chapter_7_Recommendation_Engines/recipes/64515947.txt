Title:
Cook'n mandy-Blackforest cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
chocolate cake mix
1/2 can of soda
1 can cherry pie filling
whipped cream

Directions:
mix up the cake according to package directions
at the same time, dump the pie filling into the Dutch Oven (or regular sized cake pan)
pour soda onto the pie filling (it's okay if it looks watery)
pour the cake mix on pie filling
bake 250 F until toothpick comes out clean or push down gently on the middle and it springs back up.
