Title:
Chocolate Brownie Cherry Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
~~~For the 1st layer~~~
Recipe for your favorite brownies or a box of brownie mix to fit an 8 x 8 baking dish
~~~2nd layer~~~
1 8 ounce pagkage of cream cheese, room temperature
1 cup powdered sugar
1 cup whipped cream or Cool Whip
~~~3rd layer~~~
1 can of your favorite cherry pie filling.
Chocolate Syrup for drizzling over top of cherries
~~~Ingredients: turning any canned fruit into pie filling~~~
1 large and 1 small can or three small cans of canned fruit.
1/2 cup sugar
2 1/2 TBS cornstarch
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 TBS butter or margarine

Directions:
1st layer:
Using your favorite brownie recipe or a box of brownie mix make a batch of brownies for an 8 x 8 baking dish.
Spray baking dish with non-stick spray and pour in the brownie batter and spread evenly.
Bake as directed and then refrigerate until completely cooled.
2nd layer:
Cream cheese must be at room temp. or it will take a very long time to get blended.
Add all the ingredients into a bowl and cream together, using a small mixer, do not over mix.
Spread cream cheese mixture over completely cooled brownie layer, smoothing top and return to refrigerator.
Open your can of cherry pie filling and spread the cherries over top of the cheesecake layer.
If you would like, drizzle a small amount of chocolate syrup over cherries.
Top with whipped cream or Cool Whip and drizzle a tiny amount of syrup over the whipped cream mound.
When plating the brownies, drizzle attractive swirls in the dessert plate and place the brownies in the center of the plate.
Directions: Turning canned fruit into pie filling.
Pour fruit into colander which is sitting in large bowl to catch juices.
Set fruit aside and save the juice.
Mix sugar, cornstarch and cinnamon together well.
Pour reserved juice into heavy sauce pan, Using some of the reserved juice, whisk into cornstarch mixture until smooth then add to remaining juice in sauce pan.
Add butter and heat juice mixture stirring constantly until juice thickens.
Add reserved fruit to juice mixture and cook until fruit is hot and bubbly.
Remove pan from heat and pour fruit into bowl to cool; you may refrigerate if you would like to speed up cooling time.
Your pie fllling is now made and you can procede with dessert.

A yummy bottom layer of your favorite Brownies, covered by a rich (and, Oh, so easy) cheesecake layer and then covered in a layer of sweet cherries.
