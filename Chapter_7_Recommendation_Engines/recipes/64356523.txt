Title:
Zesty Pizza sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 29oz. Can Hunts tomato sauce
8oz. Hunts tomato paste
1 Tbsp. Minced garlic
1 Tbsp. Dried minced onion
1/2 Tbsp. Onion powder
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp. sea salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper
1/8 tsp. red pepper flakes

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in large sauce pan.  Cook over medium-low heat for 15 minutes.  Remove from heat and cool.
