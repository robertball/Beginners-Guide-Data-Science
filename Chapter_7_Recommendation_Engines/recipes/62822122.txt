Title:
Bobbie's Twice Baked Spud Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666697.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Medium sized Red Skinned Potatoes
1/2 tsp Olive Oil
4 TBS Heavy Cream
1/2 oz very thinly sliced and finely chopped Pork Tenderloin
1 Shallot finely chopped
1/4 C Cheddar Cheese grated

Directions:
Fry the pork pieces in the Olive Oil, set aside.
Pierce the Potatoes on all sides with a fork and microwave on high for 4 minutes then turn them and microwave for a further 4 minutes.
Set aside to cool for 10 minutes.
Cut potatoes in half and scoop out the potato from the skin making sure not to break the skin.
Mix the potato with the cream, most of the cheese and pork pieces and spoon back into the skins.
Sprinkle remaining cheese over tops.
Bake in a preheated 350*F (175*C) oven for 15 minutes
