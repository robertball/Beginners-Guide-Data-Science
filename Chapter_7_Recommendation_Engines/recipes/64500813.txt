Title:
Low Sugar Fruit Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen strawberries
1 green apple, sliced
2 cups spinach, loosely packed
1/3 cup no sugar added white grape juice
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup greek yogurt
1 long stalk celery, chopped
1 Tbsp flax seed
1/2 Tbsp  chia seed
1 cup no sugar added almond coconut milk
3-4 ice cubes

Directions:
Add all ingredients to blender. Blend 1 minute until smooth. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Terri Christiansen Bateman
Description: 
Healthy smoothie that isn't sweet and won't load you up on carbs for the day.
