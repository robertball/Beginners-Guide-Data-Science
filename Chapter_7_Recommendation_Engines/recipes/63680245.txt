Title:
Sparkling Pear & Apricot Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large apricots, halved & seeded
3 large pears, stalks removed
1c. crushed ice
1c. mineral water

Directions:
Process apricots & pears through Juice Fountain™ using speeds 2 & 5.
Scoop ice into 4 tall glasses.
Divide estracted juice between the glasses.
Top with mineral water, stir well to blend. Serve Immediately.
