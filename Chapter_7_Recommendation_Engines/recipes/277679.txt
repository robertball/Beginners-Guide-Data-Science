Title:
Cranberry Arctic Freeze Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7457266.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1/2 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple
1/2 (14 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
2 tablespoons chopped pecans
1/2 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool WhipÂ®)
1/2 cup miniature marshmallows
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Directions:
Blend cream cheese, sugar, and mayonnaise together in a bowl. Add pineapple, cranberry sauce, and pecans; mix to combine. Fold in frozen whipped topping and mini marshmallows.
Pour mixture into graham cracker pie crust. Freeze until firm, about 2 hours.
