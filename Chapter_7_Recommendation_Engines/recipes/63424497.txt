Title:
Six Layer Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 cup chopped nuts
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup Butterscotch Chips, (optional)
1 1/2 cups coconut flakes
1 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk, (Not evaporated)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F.
In 13 x 9 inch pan, melt butter/margarine while heating oven.
Combine graham cracker crumbs with butter/margarine; press on bottom of pan.
Sprinkle with layer of nuts, chocolate chips (butterscotch chips, if used) and coconut.
Drizzle condensed milk over top.
Bake at 350F for 20 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned.
Cool; cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
For those who like a little cookie to have a very big taste, this rich little concoction will sweep your taste buds right off of your tongue.
