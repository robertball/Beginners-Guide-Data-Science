Title:
Excellent Veggie & Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package basmati & wild rice mix
2 cans vegetable stock OR
4 cups vegetable boullion stock
2 large carrots, sliced
1 medium green zucchini, sliced
1 medium yellow zuccini, sliced
1 portobello mushroom, sliced
4 shallots, diced
1 tbps olive oil
1 tbsp butter
garlic powder
salt
pepper

Directions:
Heat a medium sized skillet on med-high, and add olive oil.  Take 2 shallots and add to hot pan.  When they begin to brown, add mushrooms and sprinkle with garlic powder and pepper to taste.
In a 4 quart stock pot, heat 2 cups stock to boiling and add the rice mix, following the cooking directions for the time indicated.
Turn down heat on the skillet to medium and add carrots.  Cook until just tender.  Add zucchini and butter to pan and toss until completely heated.
Add vegetables and the rest of the stock to the pot and cook until rice is done.

Submitter: 
MERRIPAN
Description: 
This is more a stew than a soup, and is wonderfully filling.  It's great as leftovers, too!  Try adding additional veggies to help increase servings.  You can make this completely vegan by ommitting the butter.
