Luscious Lemon Parfait Recipe

3/4 cup lemon curd, prepared

1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Place six 10-ounce parfait-style glasses on a clean work surface. Into each parfait layer:                                       1/3 cup yogurt; 2 tablespoons lemon curd; 2 tablespoons crushed graham crackers; 1 tablespoon coconut.
Repeat process and serve.
