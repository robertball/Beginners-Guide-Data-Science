Title:
Chicken Spinach Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground chicken
1/3 bag of frozen spinach thawed but don't drain
1/2 spoon of salt
1/2 spoon  of basil
1/2 cup of Asiago Cheese
1/4 cup of Feta cheese (optional)
Roughly process the below ingredients.
2 jalapeno peppers (seeded)
½ cup onion or quarter of a large onion
2 garlic cloves
1 inch ginger cube

Directions:
Preheat the oven at 375 degrees.  Mix all the ingredients and mixture gently.  Don’t over mix it and form balls and arrange them on greased (spray) cookie sheets about ½ inch apart and bake them for 15 minutes or check them for doneness.  They freeze great.

Submitter: 
Radhika
Description: 
These chicken spinach meatballs are to die for.  I make these for my kids lunch boxes and everyone at their school wants them.  I have made these meatballs with Turkey, but they taste so much better ground chicken.
