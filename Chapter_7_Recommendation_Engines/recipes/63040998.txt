Title:
Hashbrown Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded american or cheddar cheese
1 10 3/4oz can of condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 32oz package frozen loose-pack hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 cup crushed cornflakes
1 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions:
Grease a 13x9x2-inch baking dish; set aside. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
In a large mixing bowl, combine shredded cheese, chicken soup, sour cream, onion, the 1/4 cup melted butter, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Fold in thawed hash brown potatoes.
Put the potato and cheese mixture into the baking dish. Toss together the cornflakes and the 1 tablespoon melted butter. Sprinkle it over the mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour or until the casserole is golden and the potatoes are tender.
