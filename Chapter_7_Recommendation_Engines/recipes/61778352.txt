Title:
Best Ever Popcorn Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup margarine
2 teaspoons cold water
2 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 cup marshmallows
1/2 tsp. salt
add vanilla as soon as you take it off the burner
1/4 tsp. vanilla
5 quarts plain popped popcorn   (3/4 cup + 2 TLB.) unpopped

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the corn syrup, margarine, cold water, confectioners' sugar and marshmallows salt, . Heat and stir until the mixture comes to a boil. add vanilla stir, Carefully combine the hot mixture with the popcorn, coating each kernel.
Grease hands with vegetable shortening and quickly shape the coated popcorn into balls before it cools. Wrap with cellophane or plastic wrap and store at room temperature.
