Title:
Bacon Bombs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can (8ct) Pillsbury Grands Flaky Layers Biscuit
Cubed Mozzarella Cheese (1-1" cube per Bomb)
2lbs of Bacon (1 slice per)
Oil for Frying

Directions:
Cut Cheese into cubes
Cut each Bisquit into fourths
Place one piece of cheese inside a Bisquit Quarter and roll it up tightly.  Wrap each bomb with a piece of Bacon and secure w/ toothpick.
In a med/large pot, heat up approx.2" of Oil (to 350) and fry them up in small batches. One or two.  Oil will expand so two at a time and stay close.  Drain on paper towel.  Best if served warm.
