Title:
Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb green asparagus, ends cut off
1 lb white asparagus, ends cut off
3 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Approximately
Several Dashes of Kosher Salt
Several Dashes of Fresh Ground Pepper
1 Lemon

Directions:
Heat Oven to 425.
Drizzle asparagus with oilve oil, season with Kosher Salt and Fresh Ground Pepper.
Place on baking Sheet.
Roast until crisp tender, about 10-15 mintes.
Lightly squeeze lemon over the top before serving.

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
These are so good. - Roasting Asparagus in the oven brings out the flavor; compared with boiling them which washes away the flavor and taste bitter. - NOTE:  You can use just green if you cannot find white. - I never have used  the white.
