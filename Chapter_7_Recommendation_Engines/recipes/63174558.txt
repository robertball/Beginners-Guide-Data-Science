Title:
Philly Cheese Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/815470.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz prime rib, thinly sliced
2 slices provolone cheese
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 small yellow onion, chopped
Squirt of mayo
Minced garlic
Salt and Pepper
2 sub or hoagie roll, toasted

Directions:
In a skillet on medium heat, saute onions and peppers in some olive oil until soft and fragrant.
Add sliced meat to the pan, and fry until pieces are slightly browned. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Lay cheese slices on top of the meat/veg mixture, and allow to melt slightly. Stir mixture to evenly distribute melted cheese among the meat/veg. Take skillet off heat.
Mix mayo and garlic. Spread on toasted bun.
Slice your toasted roll in half, and spoon meat/veg/cheese mixture into the middle.
