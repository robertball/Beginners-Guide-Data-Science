Title:
Home Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 potatoes
1 1/2 tablespoons canola oil
2 diced shallots
1 teaspoon rosemary
salt
pepper

Directions:
Fill a large saucepan with water; bring to a boil.
Add the potatoes and boil until soft.
Once cooled, cut the potatoes into cubes.
Pour the oil into a frying pan, add the potatoes and shallots
Fry until the potatoes turn golden
Add rosemary and enough salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
My favorite part of any restaurant breakfast dish is the potatoes. Every time I tried to make it, though, it wouldn't turn out right. Then I discovered the boiling. It really works! I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as I do. Happy cooking!
