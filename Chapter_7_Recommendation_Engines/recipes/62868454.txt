Title:
Left over Pork Tenderloin and Stuffing casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688693.jpg
Ingredients:
One cooked pork tenderloin sliced
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1 box of stuffing mix
1/2 package of sliced mushroom, chopped
1/2 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/tsp. poultry seasoning

Directions:
i.n baking pan lay the sliced pork on the bottom. Spread undiluted mushroom soup on top. Saute onions, celery mushrooms and poultry seasoning. Add dressing mix add melted butter and hot water like the dressing instructions say. Mix well and spread over the top of the mushroom soup. Cover pan with foil and bake 30 to 40 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Nancy Gibson
Description: 
Came up with this out of necessity. I had a whole pork tenderloin left over from dinner last night and wanted to use it up. This turned out so good I decided I should share with all of you.
