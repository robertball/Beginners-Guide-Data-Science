Title:
Connie's Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
1/2 cup shortening
5 eggs
1 cup milk
3 cups plain flour
3 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. lemon
1 tsp. almond
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking powder

Directions:
Cream butter, shortening and sugar together.  Add eggs one at a time, add milk, and dry ingredients alternately, then add flavoring.  Bake at 325 for 1 hr. for a loaf pan and 350 for 1 hr. for tube or bundt pan.

Submitter: 
Exhibits
Description: 
Finally got this recipe from my daughter's mother-in-law!  This is a great tasting pound cake and one of my favorites.
