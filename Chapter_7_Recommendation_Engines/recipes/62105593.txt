Title:
Cheesy Broccoli and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen broccoli florets
1/4 cup roasted red pepper strips
1 jar Olde English Sharp process cheese
1/2 milk or light cream
8 0z. fettucini (or your favorite pasta)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring large pot salted water to a boil.  Cook pasta according to directions on box.  While cooking, in a large sauce pan, melt the cheese and milk together, add broccoli florets and roasted red peppers.  Drain pasta when finished and add to mixture.  Toss well and serve immediatley!
