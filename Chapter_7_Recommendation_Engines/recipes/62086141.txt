Title:
Grandma Larson's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs Lean Ground Beef (can use ground chicken or turkey, instead)
1 - 12 Oz Bottle Chili Sauce
1 - Can Chicken Gumbo Soup, undiluted
1 Medium Yellow Onion, chopped
1 - Green Pepper, Seeded and Diced
1 Tbsp Yellow Mustard
1Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
1 clove garlic, minced
Salt and Pepper to taste
Hamburger Buns

Directions:
In large pan, brown and drain beef.
Add onion, green peppers and garlic. Saute 3 - 4 minutes.
Add chili sauce, soup, mustard, worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, salt and pepper.
Mix well. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat  and simmer about 45 minutes to 1 hour. Stir occasionally.
Can make ahead.  Freezes well.

Submitter:

Description: 
My grandma made a mean Sloppy Joe in her day, and here is her recipe.  You may be wrinkling up your nose at the odd ingredient...chicken gumbo soup.  Honestly, it's what makes this recipe so good!
