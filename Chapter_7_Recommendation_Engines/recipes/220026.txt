Title:
White Pizza Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3036549.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 (8 ounce) container ricotta cheese
3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup chopped pepperoni
1 (1 ounce) envelope herb and garlic soup mix (such as LiptonÂ®)
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 loaf Italian bread, sliced

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Stir the sour cream, ricotta cheese, 3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, and herb and garlic soup mix together in a 1-quart casserole dish.
Sprinkle 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the mixture.
Bake in preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes.
Serve with the Italian bread.
