Title:
Avon's, End of Summer Sunday Morning Peach Coffee Cake. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451305.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C. brown sugar
1/4 C. maple syrup
3/4 C. peach yogurt
1 over ripe banana (mashed)
1/4 C. butter (melted)
1 egg
1/2 C steel cut oats
1 C.flour
3/4 C. whole wheat flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
1 C. chopped ripe peaches
1/2 C. pecans (chopped)
1/2 C. whole wheat flour
1/4 C. brown sugar
1/4 C. butter (melted)
pinch cinnamon

Directions:
Mix the brown sugar, maple syrup, yogurt, banana, butter, egg and  oats in a bowl and let it sit for 10-20 minutes while you prepare the rest.
Mix the flours, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt in a bowl.
Mix the wet and dry ingredients along with the peaches until just mixed.
Pour the mixture into a greased 9 inch square baking pan.
Mix the pecans, flour, sugar, butter and cinnamon until they form crumbs.
Spread the streusel over the mixture.
Bake in a preheated 375F oven until a toothpick pushed into the center comes out clean, about 40-50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Avon- status quo PRO
Description: 
Picking peaches with my daughter and watching her create a peach/blueberry muffin inspired me to make this really nice and flavorful coffee cake with a tender crumb.Peaches and bananas are great together. The aroma while baking is heavenly. Not really restricted to Sunday mornings!
