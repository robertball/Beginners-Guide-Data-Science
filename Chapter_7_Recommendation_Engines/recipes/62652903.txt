Title:
Swedish Toscas with Jam (Almond Tarts) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter, room temperature
1/4 cup white sugar
1 cup all-purpose flour
. . . . . .
1/3 cup slivered almonds
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons butter
1 1/2 tablespoons cream
2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
. . . . . .
6--8 tablespoons raspberry jam

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ and spray a cupcake pan well with Pam For Baking.
Cream the butter with the white sugar in a mixing bowl.
Mix in 1 cup flour and stir to make a smooth dough.
Divide the mixture by placing even amounts into the cups of a 12-cup muffin tin.
Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of each cup.
Regrigerate the shells for 5 minutes before baking.
. . . . . . . .
To make the filling, mix the slivered almonds, brown sugar, and 2 tablespoons butter in a small pan.
Stir in the cream, and 2 teaspoons flour.
Cook and stir the mixture constantly over medium heat until it boils, about 10 minutes.
Spread about 1/2 to 3/4 tablespoon of the jam in each shell.
Divide the almond mixture evenly and spread over the jam in the shells.
Bake in preheated oven until tops and crust edges are light brown, about 10 to 15 minutes.
Remove from the oven, and cool 10 to 15 minutes before removing the tarts from the muffin pan.
Cool completely on a rack.
