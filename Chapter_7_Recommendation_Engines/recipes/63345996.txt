Title:
al fresco Fiesta Beans & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889065.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans (15.5oz) Black Beans, rinsed
1 medium Vidalia Onion, chopped
2 cloves of Garlic, chopped
1 Red Pepper, chopped
1 Yellow Pepper, chopped
3 Green Onions, chopped
1 package al fresco Jalapeno Chicken Sausage, chopped
2 tbs Cilantro, chopped
2 tbs, Vegetable Oil
1/2 c water
salt & pepper to taste
Yellow Rice, preferred

Directions:
Rinse black beans and set aside. Saute garlic & onions in oil, salt & pepper to release flavor. Add red & yellow pepper, and cook until onions are translucent. Add black beans, green onions & al fresco Jalapeno Chicken Sausage to pan. Add water and simmer & stir for 20-30 minutes until a slight gravy has formed. Serve of yellow rice & sprinkle with chopped cilantro.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
This southwestern take on traditional red beans & rice can be made with the Jalapeno or Mango Adobo chicken sausages. It can also be used in a seven layer dip or as a burrito filling.
