Title:
Grilled Cheesy Corn-On-The-Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989320.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet (18x24-inches) Reynolds Wrap® Heavy Duty Aluminum Foil
4 ears corn on the cob, husked
1/4 cup butter, softened, or olive oil
2 tablespoons KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves, crushed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 ice cubes

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium-high.
Center corn on sheet of Reynolds Wrap(R) Heavy Duty Aluminum Foil.  Spread butter on corn.  Sprinkle with Kraft Grated Parmesan Cheese and spices.  Top with ice cubes.
Bring up foil sides.  Double fold top and ends to seal making one large foil packet, leaving room for heat circulation inside.
Grill 15 to 20 minutes in covered grill, turning packet over once.

Submitter: 
Reynolds Wrap®
Description: 
Wrapped in foil, fresh ears of corn are grilled with butter, Parmesan cheese, and rosemary.
