Title:
BUSH'S® Grilled Potato Packets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
6 large red potatoes*
6 green onions, cut into 1-inch strips
1 (28 ounce) can BUSH'S® Country Style Baked Beans
2 tablespoons butter
Worcestershire sauce
Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Wrap potatoes in paper towels. Microwave for 10 minutes on high, until firm but tender. Cut into 1-inch cubes.
Cut 6, 12-inch squares of heavy duty aluminum foil. Spread potatoes evenly among the foil pieces. Season with salt and pepper. Mix onions, BUSH'S Country Style Baked Beans and Worcestershire sauce in a bowl and divide mixture among foil packets. Dot each packet with butter. Seal potatoes in foil and place on the grill. Cook 20 minutes over indirect heat.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
Red potatoes grilled in foil packets with baked beans and green onions are a quick and easy cookout side dish.
