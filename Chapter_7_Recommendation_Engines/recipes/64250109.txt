Title:
Basic Chicken Salad jodys way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tsp mrs. dash
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped in food processor
1/8th cup relish
2 hard boiled egg whites chopped
2 tbl chives

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mrs dash, and pepper. Toss with chicken, relish, celery, eggs.
Store and give 4 days shelf life

Submitter: 
Jody Comerford
Description: 
Sometimes the best approach is the simplest one, as in this mix of chicken, mayonnaise, almonds and celery. Use herbed chicken for extra flavor.Modified from: Basic Chicken Salad
