Title:
Stir-Fry Pork with Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut oil
2 tbls grated ginger
1 pound thinly sliced lean pork into bite size strips
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup water
2 tsp corn starch
1/2 tsp crushed pepper
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1 green onion, chopped
1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine
6 green onions cut in one inch lenghts
2 cups cabbage
1 clove garlic , minced

Directions:
Blend soy sauce into cornstarch; stir in water, sesame oil, sugar and pepper; set aside. Preheat stir fry pan with the peanut oil on medium-high heat. Stir fry ginger and garlic in hot oil for 30 seconds. Add Chinese cabbage, stir fry for 2 minutes. Add green onion and bamboo shoots, stir fry 1 minute more. Remove vegetables.
Add half of pork to hot oil (add more oil, if necessary) and stir fry for 2 to 3 minutes. Remove pork from pan. Stir fry remaining pork for 2 to 3 minutes. Return all meat to pan. Stir soy sauce mixture and then stir into pork. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Stir in Chinese cabbage, green onions and cook for 1 minute. Serve at once. Serves 4.

Description: 
A simple Chinese dish.  The wine and ginger gives the dish its fragrant smell.  Best served with warm rice.Modified from: Stir-Fry Pork with Ginger
