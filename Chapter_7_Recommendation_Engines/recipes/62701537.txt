Title:
Maple Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/611080.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake: 2 Stickes Butter, 1 Cup water, 2 Cups Flour, 1 Cup White Sugar, 1 Cup Brown sugar, 2 Eggs, 1/2 Cup sour cream, 1 tsp soda, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp maple flavoring.
Frosting :  1 Stick Butter, 6 Tbs Milk, 1/2 tsp Salt, 1 Cup White sugar, 1 Cup Coconut, 1 Cup Walnuts, 1 tsp Maple Flavoring.

Directions:
PRE HEAT OVEN TO 350
Cake: Boil 2 Sticks Butter & 1 Cup water, Pour over 2 Cups flour, 1 Cup White Sugar, 1 Cup Brown Sugar, Mix well. Add 1/2 Cup Sour Cream, 2 Eggs, 1 tsp Soda, 1 tsp Salt, 1 tsp Maple Flavoring, Bake 350 deg 20-22 min
Frosting: Boil Butter, Milk, & Salt. Pour over Sugar, Coconut, Walnuts, & Maple Flavoring. Pour over Hot Cake.
