Title:
Grilled Ranch Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium red potatoes, quartered
2/3 cup	sour cream
4 tsp ranch salad dressing, powdered
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat grill.  Place quartered potatoes in microwave-safe bowl and microwave on high for up to 5 minutes, stirring once during cooking.  Remove bowl from microwave and let potatoes cool.  Meanwhile, mix together sour cream, salad dressing, pepper, and salt.
Thread quartered potatoes onto four metal skewers.  Brush sour cream dressing mixture on all surfaces of the potatoes.  Place skewers over medium heat/coals on grill and grill for about 5-6 minutes on each side, turning and brushing with more dressing mixture as needed, until crispy and done.

Description: 
A great side dish for steak or chicken with a tossed salad!
