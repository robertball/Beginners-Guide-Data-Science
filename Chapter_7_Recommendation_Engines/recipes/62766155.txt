Title:
Raspberry Rhubarb Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
4 cups frozen raspberries
2 cups frozen rhubarb pieces
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix berries, sugar, and cinnamon in the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch pan. Place in preheating oven to thaw, then remove from oven. Cover berries with dry cake mix.  Pour butter over cake mix, do not stir.
Bake for 30 minutes (after 15 minutes use fork to make sure cake mix is evenly distributed with butter), or until light brown.  Serve warm or cold. top with ice cream.
