Title:
Best Crisp Chocolate Chip Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444981.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 cup butter
1 cup oil
1 egg
1 tsp coconut flavoring
2 tsp vanilla
3 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cream of tartar
1 cup coconut flakes
1 cup oatmeal
1 cup crisp rice cereal
6 oz chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix ingredients in order listed.
Roll into 1" balls
Place on baking sheet, flatten with hand
Bake for 12-15 minutes until edges are golden brown.

Submitter: 
AliciaMarie
Description: 
My mom has made these since I can remember and now when I make them for gatherings, everyone begs for the recipe! The coconut adds a surprise without being over powering!
