Ingredients:
10 ounces frozen chopped spinach (do not thaw)
1 14 ounce can quartered artichoke hearts in water, drained
8 ounces cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup chopped white onion
1/2 cup chicken broth
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon crushed red chili flakes *optional*
2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
3 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
1. Add all ingredients except for the parmesan cheese and monterey jack cheese to the Instant Pot.
2. Close the lid and set to manual pressure for 4 minutes.
3. Do a quick release and remove the lid.
4. Add parmesean and monterey jack. Whisk until smooth.
5. Serve immediately with your choice of chips, crackers, or toast.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
A classic appetizer made in just minutes in the Instant Pot. This has all of the creamy and cheesy goodness you'd expect in a spinach dip without the hassle.
