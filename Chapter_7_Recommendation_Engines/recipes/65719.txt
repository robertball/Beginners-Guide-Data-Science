Title:
Spicy Party Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups whole natural almonds
1 1/2 cups blanched slivered almonds
6 tablespoons Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
1 egg white, beaten
4 teaspoons sugar
1 1/4 teaspoons ground red pepper (cayenne)
1 1/4 teaspoons onion powder
2 1/2 cups unsalted dry roasted peanuts
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 1/2 cups seedless or golden raisins

Directions:
Toast almonds together on ungreased large shallow baking pan in 350 degrees F oven 10 minutes, without stirring, or until slivered almonds are golden brown. Cool in pan on wire rack.
Meanwhile, reduce oven temperature to 275 degrees F. Combine teriyaki marinade & sauce, egg white, sugar, red pepper and onion powder in large bowl. Stir in almonds and peanuts until evenly coated. Add oil and toss nuts to coat; turn out onto same baking pan, spreading mixture out evenly.
Bake 30 minutes, or until nuts are browned, stirring and turning nuts over every 10 minutes. Stir nuts and spread in single layer; cool completely in pan on wire rack. Stir in raisins. Store in tightly covered container.

Submitter: 
Kikkoman
Description: 
Toasted nuts, plenty of spice, and the sweetness of raisins form a zesty combination you're sure to enjoy!
