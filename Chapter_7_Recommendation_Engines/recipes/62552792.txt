Title:
Sues Earthquake Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
1 18 oz box german chocolate cake
1 stick butter
1 8oz pkg cream cheese
1 16oz box powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
spray 9x13 pan, preheat oven to 350 deg.
sprinkle bottom of the pan with coconut and pecans.
Prepare cake mix as directed and pour over nuts and coconut.
Beat cream cheese and butter till smooth. Add powdered sugar and vanilla and beat till smooth.
Dallop cheese mix on top of cake batter. Do NOT stir!
Bake at 350 deg. for 40 to 50 min.
