Title:
Dark Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/926372.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz dark chocolate (chips or bar)
1 can Marshmallow Creme
1 pint whipping cream - divided
Pie crust - prepared
3/4 cup confecioners sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Put chocolate and 1/3 cream in microwavable bowl and heat for a total of 2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds.. Let cool to just warm and add marshmallow cream and 1/2 cup confectioners sugar and vanilla. I blend in mixer but you could do by hand. Let sit until completely cool. Then mix remaining whipping cream and remainder of confectioners sugar until stiff and fold into chocolate mixture. Pour into pie shell and refrigerate for at least 2 hours

Description: 
Dark chocolate, marshmallow creme and whipping cream make this a simple and yummy pie!
