Title:
Herb Crusted Sirloin with Cheddar Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978347.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 pounds beef loin top sirloin steak
2 teaspoons Archer Farmsâ„¢ beef rub
1/4 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

Directions:
Heat grill.  Sprinkle rub evenly over both sides of steak, pressing in.
Place steak on grill.  Cover and cook steak over medium-high heat 8 to 10 minutes for medium rare or to desired degree of doneness.
Meanwhile, stir cheese, butter and mustard together. Cut steak into serving-size portions and top with a dollop of Cheddar butter.

Submitter:

Description: 
The steak topper, a blend of butter and cheddar, also makes a great embellishment for baked potatoes or steamed vegetables.
