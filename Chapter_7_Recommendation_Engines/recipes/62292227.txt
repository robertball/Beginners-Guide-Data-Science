Title:
Quick and Light Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 oz) can premium chicken breast meat
1/4 cup Miracle Whip Light
1-2 T. spicy brown mustard
2 T. chopped green onion
2-3 T. dill pickle relish
1/2 t. garlic powder
1 dash salt
1/2 t. black pepper

Directions:
Shred chicken into a medium bowl.  Add remaining ingredients and stir.  Chill.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
I came up with the recipe when I was trying to make a healthier lunch option without making it boring!  The garlic and spicy brown mustard give it a surprising kick!
