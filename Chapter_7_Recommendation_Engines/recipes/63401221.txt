Title:
Cornbread Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs ground beef
1/2 medium bell pepper
1/2 medium onion
1 can diced tomato
1 can pinto beans - or Ranch Sytle
1 4oz can chopped green chiles
1 small can of corn
2 tbs Chili powder
1 tsp garlic powder
Salt & pepper to taste
1 box corn bread mix (such as Martha White made occording to package directions)

Directions:
Brown ground beef, bell pepper and onion together. Drain off fat. Mix together with next 7 ingredients, through seasonings. Pour into greased 13x9 dish. Mix corn bread mix according to package directions. Pour over beef mixture. Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes or until cornbread topping is done.

Submitter: 
LREYNAL
Description: 
When I want a ground beef dish that isn't a meatloaf or hamburger, this is my go to recipe. It's versatile and easy. The combinations of veggies and spices can be adjusted to your liking.
