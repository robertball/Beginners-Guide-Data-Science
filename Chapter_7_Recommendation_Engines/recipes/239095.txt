Title:
Orangey Pink Lemon Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4554318.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ice cubes
1 cup pink lemonade
1 clementine, peeled and segmented
1/2 lemon, peeled
1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, pink lemonade, clementine, lemon, and sugar in a blender until smooth.
