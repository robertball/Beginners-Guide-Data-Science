Title:
Paula's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl. olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 med onion, chopped
2 lb. ground beef
2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. basil
1 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. rosemary
24 oz. tomato paste
5 cups hot water
1 lb. lasagna noodles, cooked
8 oz. ricotta cheese
1 lb. mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large skillet.
Add garlic and onion.  Saute until soft.
Add ground beef.  Cook until no longer pink.
Mix in salt, pepper, basil, oregano and rosemary.
In a separate bowl, blend tomato paste and water.
Combine liquid mixture with meat mixture and simmer uncovered approximately 1 hr.
Meanwhile, cook and drain lasagna noodles.  Rinse in cool water.
Set aside 1 cup mozzarella for topping.
In a 9 1/2 x 15 pan place ingredients in order as follows:  sauce, noodles, ricotta and mozzarella.
Repeat, finishing with a layer of sauce.
Bake 25 min.  Sprinkle remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese on top.
Bake an additional 15 min. or until hot and bubbly.
Let stand 10 min.
