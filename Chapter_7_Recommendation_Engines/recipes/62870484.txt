Title:
Light and Easy Chicken a la King Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Lean Cuisine Chicken a la King
2 large, cooked chicken breast
1 package sliced mushrooms
1 onion, diced
1 T butter
4 Pepperidge Farm puff pastry

Directions:
In a skillet, mely butter and saute mushroom and onions. Add extra chicken, mushrooms and onion to Lean Cusine and heat thoroughly. Prepare puffed  according to directions. Serve over the puffed pastry.
