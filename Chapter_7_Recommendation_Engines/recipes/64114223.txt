Title:
oatmeal cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks melted butter
1 cups brown sugar
1cups granulated sugar
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. nutmeg
2 cups flour
12 oz bag sweetened dried fruit
1 1/2 cups rolled oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt butter in a large bowl, add sugars, and let cool a bit. Add eggs, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and vanilla. Add flour; mix well. Add cranberries and oatmeal and mix together.
Drop by teaspoons on a greased cookie sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes; cool on sheet for 2 minutes before removing to wire rack to cool completely.
