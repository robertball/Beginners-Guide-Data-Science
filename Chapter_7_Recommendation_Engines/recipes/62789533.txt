Title:
Glazed Balsamic Italian Sausage Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4499073.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (500 g) Mild or Spicy Italian Sausage
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 red or yellow pepper
2 cups fresh spinach
Penne

Directions:
De-link sausage and sauté (with red pepper) in frying pan until almost cooked.
Add balsamic vinegar and brown sugar and sauté until meat is thoroughly cooked (should form a glaze on the meat).
Boil penne and strain.
Combine sausage mixture, pasta, and spinach and serve.
