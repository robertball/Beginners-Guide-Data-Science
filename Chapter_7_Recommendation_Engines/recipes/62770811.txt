Title:
Nan's Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
flour tortillas
3 c cooked, chopped chicken
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 - 1 c chopped pecans
1/4-1/2 c chopped onions
2-3 cloves garlic
3-8 pz cans tomato sauce
1 small can chopped green chiles
4 oz sour cream
4 oz jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Combine chicken, cream cheese, and pecans.
Fill tortillas with chicken mixture, roll, and line up in 9 X 13 pan.
Saute onions and garlic in a little oil. Add sauce and chiles. Pour over filled tortillas.
Bake at 350F until bubbly.
