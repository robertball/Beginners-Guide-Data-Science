Title:
Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
300ml warm water
1 tbsp sugar
2 pkg or 2 tbsp yeast (Fleischmann's Traditional Active Dry Yeast 8g pkg)
3 cups all purpose (hard) flour (use 2.5cups for mixture and 0.5cup for kneading)
1 tsp heaping salt
For best results use pizza pans with air holes

Directions:
mix water & sugar. pour yeast on top and let stand for 10 mins
stir yeast then add to flour, salt and blend till it forms a sticky ball
it is not necessary for all flour to be absorbed
turn out on floured board and knead for 10 mins adding flour as required
return to bowl and cover. let rise for 1 hour until it double in size
Use a little oil on hands to remove dough from bowl
divide dough in half
place dough in centre of pizza pan and press down onto pan with palm of hand
press dough out to edges of pan
let pressed dough sit for 15min to rise
top with favorite toppings and bake in preheated oven at 475 for 18 mins

Submitter: 
Amy Lee
Description: 
Wanda's pizza crust
makes 2 12 pizzas
Preheat oven and bake at 475
