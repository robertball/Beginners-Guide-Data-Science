Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Father's Day Mug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985061.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
Prepared frosting
Red food coloring
Chocolate syrup

Directions:
In a large microwave-safe bowl, melt margarine or butter and marshmallows in a microwave set on HIGH for 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. Stir until mixture is smooth.
Allow cereal mixture to cool slightly. With buttered hands, shape cereal mixture into mugs, reserving small portions of cereal mixture for handles. Attach handle to mug with frosting. Add chocolate syrup for 'coffee' colour.
Decorate as desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
Make dad a sweet treat coffee mug decorated just for him on his special day.
