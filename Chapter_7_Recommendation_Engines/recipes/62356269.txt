Title:
Chicken Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 pieces chicken (breast or otherwise)
2 cans cream of chicken soup
8 oz cream cheese
8 oz cream cheese--green onion and chives flavored
1 pkg dry Italian dressing mix

Directions:
Lay out chicken in 9x13" dish. Sprinkle dry Italian dressing mix over the top of the chicken. In separate bowl, mix together the soups and cream cheeses (will be lumpy). Pour over chicken. Bake uncovered for 1 hour at 350. Great with rice or noodles.

Submitter: 
Chmeliar
Description: 
A rich, creamy dish. If you like alfredo, this will hit home with you.
