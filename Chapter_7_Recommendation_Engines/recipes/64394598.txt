Title:
Zesty Chicken Quinoa Salad served over Spinach with Lemon Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2234387.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
½ Medium Red Onion,
2 Tbsp garlic
½ cup Sun Dried Tomatoes
1 cup Chicken broth
2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 cup dry organic Quinoa Grain
1½ tsp sea salt
1 cup broccoli florets
½ cup sliced almonds
2 tbsp Ground pepper
2 lemons
1 ½ tbsp dried basil
¾ Cup grape seed oil
1 garlic clove

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Place chicken strips in a greased 13×9 baking dish. Bake uncovered for 20-25 minutes or until chicken is cooked through
Heat the olive oil over medium heat in a saucepan and sauté the onion(fined chopped)  and garlic (minced) for 3 minutes. Add the sun dried tomatoes, chicken broth and lemon juice and bring to a boil. Stir in quinoa and salt. Reduce heat and simmer covered about 15 to 20 minutes. Add chicken and the broccoli (in bite size pieces) on top and simmer an additional 5 minutes.
Remove from heat, toss gently until combined. Add ground pepper and additional salt, if desired, to taste. Garnish with almonds before serving over spinach. Dress with Lemon Sauce.
Lemon Sauce
2 lemons, squeezed (about ½ cup)
1 ½ tsp dried basil
¾ cup grape seed oil
¾ cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp pepper
1 garlic clove, minced
Combine all ingredients and whisk vigorously. Sauce will slightly thicken.  Store in an air-tight container.
