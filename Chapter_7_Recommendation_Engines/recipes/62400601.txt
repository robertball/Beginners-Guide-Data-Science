Title:
Nacho Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. package cream cheese
1 package sour cream
1 package taco seasoning mix
Salsa
Lettuce; shredded (about 1/2 head)
Tomatoes; seeded & chopped (1 large)
Onions; chopped (1 large sweet onion)
Small can chopped black olives
Grated cheddar cheese
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sour cream & taco seasoning together and spread over large platter.
Pour salsa over top of cream cheese mixture
Layer lettuce over salsa, top with tomatoes, onions and black olives.
Sprinkle grated cheese over everything
Serve with round tortilla chips.
