Title:
Pineapple Fried Rice with Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
• 1 can chopped pineapple
• 2/3 cup white rice
• 14 oz can lentils, rinsed & warmed
• 1 tbsp cooking oil
• 2 eggs, beaten
• 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 2 green onions, chopped
• 1 grated carrot
• 1 tbsp yellow curry paste (sub green or red)
• 1 tbsp soy sauce
• 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
• 1/2 cup cashews
• 1/2 lime, juiced

Directions:
Open can of pineapple and drain juice into a measuring cup.
In a medium saucepan, combine the liquid from the can of crushed pineapple with water to a total of 1 1/3 cups liquid and bring to a boil.
Add rice and bring mixture to boil. Cover and reduce heat to simmer.
Cook 25 to 30 minutes or until rice is tender.
In a non-stick pan heat the oil. Add the eggs and cook without stirring, until set. Slide eggs out of the pan to a plate & cut into short, narrow strips. In the same pan, heat the oil and stir fry the garlic, green onions, carrots, curry paste, soy sauce, and brown sugar for about 4 minutes. Stir in cooked rice, pineapple, and egg strips and cook 5-10 minutes.
Combine with cooked lentils and top with cashews and lime juice.
