Title:
Fresh Salsa II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ripe tomatoes, seeded and diced
1 green bell peppers, seeded and diced
1 small onion, diced
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1/2 bunch fresh cilantro chopped, for garnish
2 tablespoon fresh lime juice
1 jalapeno chile pepper, seeded and diced
1 tablespoon Splenda White Sugar Blend
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon Kosher salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix tomatoes, green bell peppers, onion, garlic, cilantro, oregano, cumin, salt, black pepper, lime juice and jalapeno chile pepper. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.
