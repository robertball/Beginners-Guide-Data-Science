Title:
Barbequed Chicken with Citrus Yogurt Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/215392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lemon, juice and zest
1 orange, juice and zest
1 cup DannonÂ® Plain Yogurt
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup parsley
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon chopped garlic
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
12 boneless chicken thighs
1 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions:
In a large bowl combine citrus juices and zests, yogurt, olive oil, garlic, spices and herbs, mix well. Pour all but  1/2 cup marinade over chicken and toss to coat completely. Let chicken marinate 1 to 2 hours (or overnight).
Salt chickens. Place chicken skin side down on barbecue/grill and cook with lid up 10 to 12 minutes. Turn chicken and baste with reserved marinade. Continue to cook, basting occasionally, lid down for 10 to 12 minutes.
Serve chicken over rice or couscous.

Submitter: 
DANNON
Description: 
A tangy marinade with yogurt, lemon, and orange flavors gives this grilled chicken its zesty flavor.
