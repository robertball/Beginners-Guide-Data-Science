Title:
Skinny Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983951.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1-inch x 1/2-inch) strip lime zest
1 packet Sweet'N Low granulated sugar substitute
3/4 ounce fresh lime juice
1 1/2 ounces light rum
1 slice lime, for garnish

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, muddle the lime zest with the SweetN Low. Add the lime juice and rum. Fill halfway with cracked ice and shake vigorously.  Strain into a chilled stemmed glass. Garnish with the lime slice.
