Title:
No Bake Cherry Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Graham cracker crust
1 cup powdered sugar
2 Tablespoons milk
1 8 oz. pkg cream cheese
1 cup chopped nuts
1 can cherry pie filling
Whipped cream for topping (optional)

Directions:
Soften the cream cheese, add 2 Tbls of milk and 1 cup powdered sugar, cream together and spread on crust.
sprinkle half portion of the nuts on the cream cheese mixture
spread one can of cherry pie filling on top of that
sprinkle the other nuts on top for presentation. Add whipped cream if desired. Keep in the refridgerator.
