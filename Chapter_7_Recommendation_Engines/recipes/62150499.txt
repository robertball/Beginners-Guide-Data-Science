Title:
Dylan's Peanut Butter Cup Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. peanut butter sandwhich cookies
1 1/2 sticks butter
1 C milk chocolate chips
1 C peanut butter chips
2/3 C light corn syrup
1 T. Vanilla
2 C Reeses peanut butter cups candies (unwrapped)
2 C Chopped peanuts
2 C Rice Krispies cereal

Directions:
Pulverize peanut butter cookies in food processer.  Melt 1 stick of butter (save the other half for later).  Combine melted butter with cookie crumbs and press into foil lined, greased, 9x13 pan.  Bake for 15 min. in a 350 degree oven (until golden brown).  Melt chocolate and peanut butter chips with corn syrup and remaining butter to a creamy fudgy consistancy.  Pour 1/2 of chocolate mixture over cookie crust and spread evenly.  Coursely chop Reeses peanut butter cups and distibute evenly over the bars.  Mix remaining chocolate mixture with chopped peanuts and Rice Krispies.  Spread evenly and gently press over peanut butter cups.  Chill throughly and cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Janean Yates
Description: 
After searching, without success, for a PB Cup candy recipe, I decided to make my own.  This has the chopped up Peanut Butter Cups, chocolate, and Peanut Butter sandwhich cookies that my son-in-law Dylan loves.  It was created just for him but has become everyone's favorite.
