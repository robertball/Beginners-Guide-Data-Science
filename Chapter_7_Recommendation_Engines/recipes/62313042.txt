Title:
Eggplant Ratatouille Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small eggplant, sliced 1/4-inch thick
course kosher salt
1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
1 medium yellow bell pepper, cored and sliced crosswise 1/4 inch thick
1 medium zucchini, halved and thinly sliced lengthwise
1/2 c. chopped kale
1 can or 1 1/2 c. great Northern beans, drained and rinsed
1 Roma tomato, cut into 1/4 inch slices
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. each: oregano, thyme and marjoram leaves
1 T. extra-virgin olive oil
grated Asiago cheese (about 3/4 c.)

Directions:
Sprinkle both sides of eggplant slices liberally with the coarse salt; let drain in a colander for an hour.  Rinse off excess salt and squeeze out water.
Preheat oven to 375.  Coat a 9 x 9 pan or casserole with spray oil.  Layer half of the eggplant, onion, peppers and zucchini in pan.  Cover with all the kale and beans, and spread with half of the tomatoes.
Sprinkle with half the garlic and half the herbs.  Repeat layers process (except kale and beans.)  Press down gently and drizzle with oil.  Cover loosely with foil and bake for an hour or till veggies are very tender.
Remove foil.  Sprinkle cheese over vegetables and cook about 5 more minutes.
