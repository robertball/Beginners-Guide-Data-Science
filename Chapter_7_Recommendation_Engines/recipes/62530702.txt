Title:
Apple-Banana Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Apples, chopped
2 bananas, sliced
Mayonnaise
Milk
Cinnamon & Sugar
8 servings

Directions:
Combine Mayo, Milk and Cinnamon & Sugar until a smooth, creamy sauce.  Start with the mayo and cinnamon and sugar.  Add milk slowly until desired sauce texture is achieved.
