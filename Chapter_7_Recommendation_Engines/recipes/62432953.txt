Title:
Jan's Crumb Topping or Streusel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/452355.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup white sugar
2/3 cup brown sugar
2-3 tablespoons cinnamon (use to your own taste)
2 sticks butter
3 cups flour

Directions:
Blend butter and sugar until creamy.
Add in cinnamon and flour and mix until fine crumbs.
Store in airtight container in the freezer until needed.  Use as much as you want to top cakes, muffins or even cookies then put back in the freezer for next time.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
I add this topping to muffins, pie and cakes so often I decided to make it in a large amount and freeze.  Now I have it ready whenever the mood strikes!
