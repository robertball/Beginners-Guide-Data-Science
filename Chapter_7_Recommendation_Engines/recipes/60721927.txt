Title:
Chicken Salad Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/305944.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound small red potatoes, quartered
1 pound green beans, halved crosswise
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons coarsely chopped capers
3 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley
Grated peel and juice of 1/2 lemon
Salt and
pepper
1/4 cup flour
Two 6-ounce skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, lightly pounded
1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
toss potatoes with  PAM and season to taste(maybe lemon pepper) place on cookie sheet and bake til tender and golden brown stiring every 10 minutes. For about 30 min.
in a saucepot bring salted water to a boil and Add the green beans to the boiling water and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes. Drain in the colander let cool slightly.
In a large bowl, whisk together capers, 3 tablespoons parsley, the lemon peel and the lemon juice.  set aside.
In a shallow bowl season the flour with salt and pepper. In a large nonstick skillet, heat the 2 tablespoons olive oil over medium-high heat. Coat the chicken breasts with the seasoned flour Add to the skillet and cook, turning once, until golden, about 8 minutes. Transfer to a cutting board and cut into strips.
Add the chicken broth to the skillet; cook over medium heat, scraping up any browned bits. Add the lemon caper mixture.Remove from the heat and stir in the chicken, green beans and potatoes, stir to coat.
