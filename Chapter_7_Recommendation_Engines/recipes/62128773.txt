Title:
Hot Pastrami & Swiss Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. beef pastrami
4 slices swiss cheese, or to taste
4 slices light rye bread
brown deli mustard to taste

Directions:
Bring cheese to room temperature
Simmer pastrami in water (or saute in pan) for 15 minutes Slather all the bread slices with mustard
Remove pastrami when done, place on 2 slices of the bread Add 2 slices swiss, top with other slice of bread
Increase recipe as needed

Submitter: 
Sharpshooter
Description: 
This sandwich reminds me of my school daze and the late-night munchies. The recipe lives on. It ain't rocket science.
