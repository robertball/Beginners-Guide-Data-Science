Title:
Irish Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Whole Eggs
1 tsp. instant coffee granules
1 tsp. chocolate syrup ( I use Hersheys )
3/4 tsp. Almond extract
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 pint Whipping Cream
12 Oz. Whiskey

Directions:
Blend eggs, coffee, chocolate syrup, and almond extract in blender.
Add sweetened condensed milk, whipping cream and blend.
Add Whiskey and blend, put in fridge overnight and serve cold. Keep any unused portion in fridge.

Submitter: 
Bethany Bambenek
Description: 
This is a recipe I have made for years and everyone seems to love it.
