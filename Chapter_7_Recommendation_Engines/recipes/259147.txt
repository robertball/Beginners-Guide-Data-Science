Title:
Low-Sugar Sherbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup granular sucralose sweetener (such as SplendaÂ®)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix orange juice, milk, granular sweetener, and vanilla extract together in a bowl. Pour into a freezer-safe container.
Place orange juice mixture into the freezer for 30 minutes. Stir well with a fork. Smooth out and level the top. Return to the freezer. Repeat, stirring every 30 minutes, until sherbet is frozen, 1 to 2 hours.
