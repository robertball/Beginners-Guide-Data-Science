Title:
Kodiak Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
4 cups diced onions
2 garlic cloves, minced
3 medium green peppers, diced
4 cups diced celery
1 jar (5 3/4 oz.) stuffed green olives, undiluted
1 can (4 oz.) mushroom stems and pieces, undrained
1 can ((10 3/4 oz.) condensed tomato soup, undiluted
1 jar (8 oz.)picante sauce
1 bottle (18 oz.) barbecue sauce
2 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
3 to 4 cups medium noodles, cooked and drained
1 cup (4 oz.) shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, brown ground beef with onions and garlic; drain.  Add remaining ingredients except cheese; mix well.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until hot and bubbly.
Sprinkle with the cheese just before serving.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
This recipe has a little kick and an interesting and tasty mix of ingredients.  Perfect for potluck.  It is an Alaskan recipe from the early 1950s
