Title:
Turkey and Brie Croissant with Cranberry Orange Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/653824.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Mayonnaise
Juice from 1/2 small orange
1/8 c. dried cranberries, roughly chopped
2 large (sandwich size) bakery croissants
1/4 - 1/3 LB thin sliced deli turkey
4 slices brie cheese
8 thin slices Granny Smith apple

Directions:
For aioli: In a small bowl, mix together mayo, orange juice and chopped cranberries. Set mixture aside.
Warm non-stick skillet or panini pan on medium heat.  Cut croissants in half. Place 4 apple slices on each croissant bottom. Top apple with sliced turkey, the sliced brie, divided equally. Spread aoili mixture on the inside of each croissant top, and top each sandwich. Place assembled sandwiches into heated pan, and lightly toast on each side, until desired color is reached and cheese is slightly melted. Serve warm.

Description: 
Perfectly warm brie compliments this tangy, flaky croissant sandwich.
