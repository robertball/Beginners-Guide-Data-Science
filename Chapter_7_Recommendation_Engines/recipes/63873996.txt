Title:
Slow Cooker Barbeque Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 2-3lb. Eye Round Roasts
48oz. Dr. Pepper
1 yellow onion, sliced into rings
4 cloves of garlic, minced
1tablespoon salt
2 splashes of hot sauce
54oz. barbeque sauce

Directions:
Put eye round roasts in large slow cooker (mine is a 6 quart oval).
Add Dr. Pepper, onion, garlic, salt, and hot sauce.
Cook on low for 6 hours.
Drain liquid, shred the beef, and then cover with barbeque sauce.
Cook on low for 2 more hours.
