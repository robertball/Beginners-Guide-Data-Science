Title:
Annette's Cheese Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 heaped tsp. baking powder
pinch of salt
2 tbs. butter or margarine
1 egg broken into 1 cup measuring cup, lightly beaten
Enough milk to top above cup up to top
1 1/2 cups grated cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
2. Put flour, baking powder, salt and butter/margarine into mixing bowl, rub the butter into the dry mixture with fingers until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
3. Add in egg/milk mixture and cheese.  Mix with fork 'til all dry ingredients are moist.  Mixture will be thick and sticky.
4. Spoon into greased muffin pans, bake for 12 minutes on top rack.

Submitter: 
Petal
Description: 
British style scones with cheese in, great for an afternoon tea!  Be sure to use the spray to grease your muffin pan or you may not get them out :)
