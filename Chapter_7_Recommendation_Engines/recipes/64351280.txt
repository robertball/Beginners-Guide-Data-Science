Title:
Bourbon Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 tbsp butter
2 cloves garlic
8 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground cloves
(can add 1 1/2 tsp apple pie spice instead of cinnamon and cloves)
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
melt butter over medium heat
add garlic and cook for 1 minute
add bourbon and remaining ingredients and simmer over high heat
whisk well until thick and syrupy, 2 to 4 minutes

Submitter: 
Rick Kleinhans (kokodiablo)
Description: 
delicious caramel glaze for pork tenderloin or even fruit
