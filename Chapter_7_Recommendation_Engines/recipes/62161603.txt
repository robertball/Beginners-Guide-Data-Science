Gin Bucket - 01 Recipe

Ingredients:
1 handle of gin
2 2-Liter Fresca
Lots of Ice
1 Grapefruit
or
2 Limes and Lemons

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mix well, and serve!

Description: 
Delicious adult punch for the summer.  Drink it as is with friends out of a bucket... or dress it up with some frozen fruit and put it in a punch bowl.
