Title:
Portuguese Stuffing Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 loaves white bread
1 large egg
1/2 cup water
2 medium vidalia onions
1/2 cup freshly chopped parsley
1/3 cup cider vinegar
2-3 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 1/2 cup lard

Directions:
Break up bread into a large pan or bowl, when finished, pour the water over the bread pieces and add beaten egg. begin pressing the mixture down with fist like kneading. turn and press until it feels moistened and broken up like a stuffing should. Cover and let sit
Preheat oven to 325
In large skillet, melt lard on medium heat.
Add chopped onions and parsley, lower heat to above simmmering. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes or until onions are tender turning heat all the way to low. Add salt, both peppers and vinagar letting ingredients stand for 5 minutes. Pour over the breading mixture and turn in bowl/pan as you pour. Mix completly until all ingredients are evenly combined.
Spray non stick cooking spray on 2 baking sheets and make small (softball) sized balls with the stuffing mixture, place on sheets 1 1/2" apart and cook for 18-20 minutes or until golden brown.
