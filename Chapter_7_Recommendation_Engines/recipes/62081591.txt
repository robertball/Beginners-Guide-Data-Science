Title:
Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tbsp Butter
1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup flour
3/4 cup sugar
2 Tbsp flour
1/4 tsp finely shredded lemon peel
3 Tbsp lemon Juice
1/4 tsp baking powder
powdered sugar

Directions:
Beat butter till soft. Add 1/4 cup sugar and 1/4 tsp salt. Beat till fluffy. Stir in 1 cup flour. Press dough onto a 8 X 8 X 2"greasd baking pan. Bake at 350 Degree for 15 min
In same mixing bowl, beat 2 eggs. Add 3/4 cup sugar, 2 tbls flour, lemon peel, juice and baking powder. Beat 3 mins or till slightly thickened. Pour over baked layer. Bake 25-30 min more or until center is set. Cool. Sift powdered sugar over cookes. Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Lois Anderson
Description: 
I have made this recipe for several years. It is well loved by all that taste it.
