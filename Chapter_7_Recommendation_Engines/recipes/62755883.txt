Title:
Dad's Sausage Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/4 lb Italian Sausage or ground venison
4 Tbsps of all purpose seasoned flour
1 1/3 cup of milk
1/2 cup of Water

Directions:
Heat Sausage (or ground venison) over medium heat.
Once meat has browned slowly sift flour over the meat.
Mix meat and dripping into flour to form clumps.
Add water and knead the clumps to loosen until the water is no longer clear.
Slowly pour milk into the mixture and continue kneading.
Reduce heat and serve.

Submitter: 
Durham1222
Description: 
An awesome gravy recipe my dad shared with me when I was younger.  The seasoned flour makes it spicy and it's fairly easy to make.  It can be made with either sausage or venison.
