Title:
Evil Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 blocks cream cheese (8 oz each)
1 stick butter (1/2 cup)
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 packet Hidden Valley ranch dressing mix (dry)
Freshly cracked black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients until smooth.  May wish to use a blender to achieve whipped consistency.
Spread onto serving platter (I use a pie plate).
Crack/grind black pepper over the surface.
Serve with pretzels, crackers or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
orangechickie
Description: 
I first learned of this from a coworker who termed it Whipped Fat.   As that nickname indicates, this is not the healthiest recipe...but it is addictive and soooo good.   Your best bet is to make it for a large party so as to limit your individual consumption.
