Title:
Easy Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons butter
1 cup chopped carrots
1/2 cup chopped onions (if tolerated)
2 small potatoes, diced
1 teaspoon thyme
1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
4 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
2 teaspoon chicken bouillon
4 ounces egg noodles, uncooked
2 cups cooked chicken breast, chopped

Directions:
Melt butter in large pot and saute the carrot and onion for 2 minutes.
Add potato, thyme, poultry seasoning, chicken broth and bouillon and bring to a boil.
Add noodles and chicken and cook on low for 20 minutes.
