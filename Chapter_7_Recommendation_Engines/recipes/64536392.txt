Title:
Super Garlicky Paleo Tzatziki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 -4 cloves garlic
1/2 cucumber
2 cups plain coconut milk yogurt
1/2 tsp dill
1 tsp sea salt
juice of 1 lemon

Directions:
Put everything in a food processor and blend until well mixed.

Submitter: 
Natasha Farrell
Description: 
This is like garlic sauce times tzatziki sauce. To the power of garlic. I like mine super garlic but you may adjust your garlic to taste. This tastes amazing on everything.
