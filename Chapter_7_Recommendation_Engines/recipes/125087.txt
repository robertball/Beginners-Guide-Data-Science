Title:
Raisin Bran Chewies Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/689983.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs, lightly beaten
2 tablespoons honey
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 cups raisin bran cereal
3/4 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening and sugars. Add eggs, honey and vanilla; mix well. Combine flour, baking soda and salt; add to creamed mixture. Stir in cereal. Fold in raisins and walnuts. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F for 12-14 minutes or until done.

Submitter: 
Ione  Perkins
Description: 
'I created this recipe when I was trying to use up some Raisin Bran cereal,' explains Ione Perkins of Rowlins, Wyoming.
