Title:
Grilled Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Salmon
2 t. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2t. Fresh Lemon Juice
1/4 t. Salt
Pinch of fresh ground Pepper
2 Cloves Garlic
2 t. fresh Rosemary chopped or 1 t. dried Rosemary
Sprig or fresh rosemary for garnish (optional)
Capers (optional)

Directions:
Cut the salmon in 4 equal portions (optional).  Combine the olive oil, lemon juice, salt pepper, garlic and Rosemary in a bowl.  Spread this mixture on fish.
Spray grill with cooking spray or use grilling basket sprayed with cooking oil.  Grill over med. heat until fish flakes about 4 to 6 min. per 1/2 inch of thickness.  If the fish is more than 1 inch thick turn gently halfway through grilling.
You may also use the broiler and use the same cooking times as above.
