Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/2 cup powdered sugar
2 cups flour
pinch of salt
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
4 tbsp flour
6 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Mix together first 4 ingredients and pat into 9x13 pan and bake at 350 degrees for 15 min.
Mix last 4 ingredients together and pour on crust. Bake 20 min at 350 degrees
Sprinkle with powdered sugar while warm. Cool and cut into bars.

Super delicious and rich. One of my mom's favorite desserts.
