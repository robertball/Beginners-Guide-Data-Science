Title:
Sour Cream Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup finely chopped green onions
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1 dash white pepper
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Sprinkle the cucumbers with salt. Let stand 20 minutes. Drain liquid and rinse cucumbers.
Blot to remove excess water.
Combine onions, vinegar, pepper and sour cream.
Stir into cucumbers.
Chill.
