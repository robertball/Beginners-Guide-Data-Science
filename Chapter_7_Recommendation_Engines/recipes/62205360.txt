Title:
Buffalo Wontons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz package of WonTon Wraps
1 egg white
2 cups cooking oil
Filling:
2 cups shredded chicken
1 cup buffalo sauce  (recipe follows)
1 8oz package cream cheese
Buffalo Sauce:
1 1/2 cups melted butter
12oz bottle of hot sauce
6 teaspoons white vinegar

Directions:
Melt butter in a sauce pan
Add hot sauce and vinegar
Whisk until mixed well
Set aside and cool for one hour
After sauce is cooled (should be a bit thicker now), mix  1 cup of sauce with chicken; let sit for 15 minutes
Lay out several wontons
Place 1/2 teaspoon of filling in center of each wrap
Top each one with 1/2 teaspoon on cream cheese
Brush 2 edges of each wrap with egg white
Fold over into a triangle shape, lightly pressing edges to seal
Deep fry wontons in oil heated to 375 degrees for 1 minute, turning once; or until golden brown
Place fried wontons on a paper towel to drain oil
Serve with blue cheese or ranch dipping sauce

Submitter: 
Krystal
Description: 
Shredded chicken with buffalo sauce and cream cheese, wrapped up in a warm, crunchy wonton.  Easy, delicious.  The perfect appetizer for a party or at home!
