Title:
Spaghetti al Forno Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 T olive oil, plus more for drizzling
1 sm. onion, finely chopped
2 carrots, peeled and finely chopped
1 stalk celery, finely chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste
1/2# whole wheat spaghetti or linguine
2 T chopped flat-leaf parsley
1 lg. pinch red pepper flakes
3/4 C grated Parmesan cheese
2 T whole wheat breadcrumbs

Directions:
To make sauce: In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, celery and garlic. Cook, stirring frequently, until vegetables begin caramelizing, about 12 min. Turn heat to low; cook until very soft, 10-15 min. Raise heat to medium; add tomatoes. Bring to a simmer and partially cover. Cook 30-40 min, stirring occ. Season with salt and pepper.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Bring large pot of water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions, subtracting several minutes off cooking time so it comes out very al dente.
Drain well; add to sauce. Add parsley and red pepper flakes; toss to combine. Transfer to 2 qt. baking dish. Spread level. Sprinkle w/cheese and bread crumbs. Drizzle w/olive oil. Bake until top is browning and sauce is bubbling, about 15 min. Serve immed.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Whole wheat pasta, veggies in a tomato sauce are first cooked on stove and then heated in very hot oven until top is browned and sauce is bubbling
