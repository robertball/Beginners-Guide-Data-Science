Title:
Sugar Snaps Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 1/2 tablespoons flour
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon celery seed, ground
1/2 cup Mann's Stringless Sugar SnapÂ® Peas
3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, grated
1 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Set oven to broil. In a small pan, melt margarine or butter. Stir in flour; blend. Remove pan from heat and stir in milk, onion powder and ground celery seed. When well mixed, return to heat and cook, stirring constantly, until thickened.
Divide and arrange Mann's Stringless Sugar Snap Peas in bottoms of two individual baking dishes. Divide and pour sauce over tops of Mann's Stringless Sugar Snap Peas. Sprinkle tops with grated Parmesan and paprika.
Put 6 inches under broiler and broil for 4 minutes or until sauce is bubbly and top is browned.

Submitter: 
Mann's Fresh Vegetables
Description: 
Convenient stringless sugar snap peas are prepared in a creamy sauce, then quickly broiled with a Parmesan cheese crust.
