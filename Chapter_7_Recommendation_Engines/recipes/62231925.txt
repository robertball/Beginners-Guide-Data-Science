Title:
Campfire Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1            pound  extra lean ground beef -- crumbled
1/2         cup  onion -- chopped
3            tablespoons  dry taco seasoning mix
14 1/2    ounces  Mexican tomatoes (discard 2 tablespoons liquid) -- chopped
2            15-ounce cans  pinto beans
1/3         cup  ripe olives -- sliced
3            tablespoons  fresh parsley -- chopped
4            ounces  Monterey jack cheese -- shredded
1            package  cornmeal mix -- prepared
1            tablespoon  water

Directions:
Saut√© beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; drain and blot with paper towels to absorb extra grease.
Add taco seasoning, tomatoes, beans, olives and parsley. Sprinkle with cheese.
Top with prepared cornmeal mix.
Bake at 350 for 15 minutes, or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
Great casserole for camping because it can be made in stages ahead of time and cooked in a cast iron pot.
