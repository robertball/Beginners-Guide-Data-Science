Title:
Barley Black Bean Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS (Nutrition)
1/2 cup uncooked quick-cooking barley
1 large egg
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup mushrooms, minced
1/4 cup fresh parsley, minced
1 (16 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 red bell pepper, minced
1/2 onion, minced
3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
1 egg
1/2 cup bread crumbs
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat grill for high heat, and generously oil a sheet of aluminum foil.
Alternatively, preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C), and oil a baking sheet.
Cook barley according to package directions. Set aside.
Mash black beans with a fork until thick and pasty.
Stir together all ingredients and mix in bread crumbs until the mixture is sticky and holds together.
Divide mixture into four to six patties.
If grilling, place patties on foil, and grill about 8 minutes on each side.
If baking, place patties on baking sheet, and bake about 10 minutes on each side.

Submitter: 
Troy Meyer
Description: 
These veggie burgers will firm up as they cook on the grill.  Great with all the trimmings.
