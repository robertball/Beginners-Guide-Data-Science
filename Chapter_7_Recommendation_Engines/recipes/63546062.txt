Title:
Chicken Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed, cooked rotiserie chicken
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup slivered almonds, toasted
1/4 cup dill pickle relish
2 tablespoons chopped onion
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
6 English muffins, split and toasted
6 lettuce leaves
6 thin slices  tomato

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first 11 ingredients. Top muffin halves with lettuce leaves; spread with chicken salad. Top with tomato slices and remaining muffin halves.
Can also be served on sandwiches, crackers or on apple slices.

Submitter: 
Ciara Gavin
Description: 
This is a great recipe to use up leftover baked or rotisserie chicken.  I've served at baby showers and fr a quick casual lunch.Modified from: Chicken Salad Sandwiches
