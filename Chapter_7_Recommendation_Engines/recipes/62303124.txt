Title:
Layered Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head iceberg lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
1 red onion, chopped (or green onion)
1 c. chopped celery
1 c. chopped green pepper
1 (10 ounce) package frozen green peas
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/4 cups mayonnaise
Bacon bits (1/2 lb?)

Directions:
In a large flat bowl, place the chopped lettuce and top with a layer of onion, celery, green peppers,peas. Spread mayo over to seal. Sprinkle shredded cheese over top. Refrigerate overnight. Top with bacon before serving.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
Works well in a 9 X 13 glass pan. Yummy!Modified from: Seven Layer Salad
