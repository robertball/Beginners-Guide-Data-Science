Title:
Raisin Bran Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups raisin bran cereal, crushed
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
3/4 cup margarine
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs

Directions:
Stir cereal, flours, soda together.
Beat margarine and sugars till light and fluffy.
Add eggs to margarine mixture. Beat.
Add flour mixture. Mix well.
Roll into balls and press to flatten to about 1/2" thick.
Bake @ 325 x 15 min. or till done.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Healthier cookies! featuring raisin bran cereal and whole wheat flour.
