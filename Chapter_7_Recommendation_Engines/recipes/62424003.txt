Title:
Pan-Fried Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs. butter
3 Tbs. olive oil
1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed

Directions:
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Cook garlic in mixture for 10-20 seconds, but do not brown. Add asparagus, and cook for 10 minutes, turning asparagus to ensure even cooking.
