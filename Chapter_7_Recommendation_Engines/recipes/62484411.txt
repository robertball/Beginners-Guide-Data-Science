Title:
Marne's Texas Chocolate Cookie Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C flour
2 C white sugar
2 sticks butter
1 C water
4 Tbl cocoa
1/2 C Buttermilk
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
For frosting:
1 stick butter
4 Tbl cocoa
6 Tbl milk
1 box powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 C nuts (optional)

Directions:
Sift flour and sugar together, set aside
Bring butter, water and cocoa to boil.  Pour over flour and sugar while hot.  Add buttermilk, eggs, soda and vanilla. Stir to combine.  Pour into prepared sheet pan.
Bake 20 minutes at 400Â°
While cake is baking bring 1 stick butter, 4 tablespoons cocoa and 6 tablespoons milk to a boil.  Remove from heat.  Add one box powdered sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla and 1 cup nuts (optional).  Spread over cake while both are hot.

Submitter: 
Sharon Springer Andrews
Description: 
A moist, delicious mix between frosted brownie, bar cookie and chocolate cake.  Makes me think of my Grandmother every time I mix it up.
