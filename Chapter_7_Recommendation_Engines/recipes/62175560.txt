Title:
Meat loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb hamburger
1 egg
medium onion cut fine
1 1/2tsp salt
14 crackers(crushed)
1/4C BBQ sauce
1Tbsp mustard
1Tbsp pepper
(add milk if it seems dry;if using bread and not cracker)

Directions:
Mix all together and Bake at 300 for 1hour and 30minutes. To make a gravy use the drippings from the meatloaf once done. Put dripping in a pan on the stove and add enough water to make a enough gravy for you meal. Once water comes to a boil add a flour and water mixture to thicken to the right consistancey.
