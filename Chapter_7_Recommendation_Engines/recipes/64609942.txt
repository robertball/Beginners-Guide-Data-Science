Title:
LaVergene's Green Tomato Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4137315.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 cup sugar
2 cup diced green tomatoes
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cup chopped nuts
1 cup canola oil
1 Tbsp vanilla
3  cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 Tbsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease and flour 2  8" x 4" loaf pans, set aside.
Beat first 7 ingredients together in medium bowl.
In separate bowl sift dry ingredients together and add to egg mixture, mix well.
Pour into 2 loaf pans, bake for 1 hour or until knife inserted in center comes out clean (3 small pans bake for 55 minutes).
Cool on wire rack before serving.
