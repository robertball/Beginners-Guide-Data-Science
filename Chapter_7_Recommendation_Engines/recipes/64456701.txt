Title:
peanut butter protein balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 scoops of whey protein isolate
3 tsp agave syrup
4 tbsp chopped mixed nuts (or just peanuts)
2 tsp almond milk
2 tbsp peanut butter
1 tsp maple extract
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
2. Shape the dough into 10 peanut butter bombs.
3. Place in fridge overnight
Optional instructions
1. Drizzle self-hardening chocolate, butterscotch or caramel sauce over bombs. (butterscotch rocks)
2. Sprinkle nuts over sauce before it hardens.
3. Dip bombs in a low fat sugar free jelly or jam.
