Title:
Big Ray's Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130806.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef (or turkey)
1 cup salsa
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 cup sour cream
1 (14 oz) can refried beans
1 (14 oz  can Mexican corn, drained
2 Tbl chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp Adobo seasoning
2 cups crushed tortilla chips, divided
4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
4 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese, divided
chopped tomatoes
chopped lettuce
sour cream
cilantro
chopped jalapeños

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cook and stir ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat until crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink adding onions last few minutes to soften. Drain and discard any excess grease. Stir salsa, sour cream, cayenne, Adobo seasoning, chili powder, beans , corn, and cumin into the beef. Remove from heat.
Spread about 1/3 the ground beef mixture into the bottom of a 9 x 13 dish. Spread about 1/3 the tortilla chips in a layer atop the beef mixture. Layer about 1/3 of each of the Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses over the tortilla chip layer. Repeat layers with remaining ingredients, ending with Monterey Jack cheese. Cover dish with aluminum foil.
Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is melted in the middle, about 30 minutes.
Serve with chopped tomatoes, chopped lettuce, sour cream, cilantro and  chopped jalapeños

Description: 
A quick and easy taco entrée for a last minute meal. Change the flavor of the chips ie: guacamole, lime , nacho or spicy, to fit your family's tastes.
