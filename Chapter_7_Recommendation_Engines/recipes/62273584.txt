Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cubed chicken breasts
1 onion, chopped
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 T oil
2 - 15 ounce cans Great Northern Beans, rinsed and drained
3/4 cup chicken broth
2 - 4 ounce cans chopped green chilies
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup whipping cream

Directions:
Saut√© chicken, onion, and garlic powder in oil, drain. Add beans, broth, chiles and seasonings. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Remove from heat.  Stir in sour cream and whipping cream. Reheat on low if necessary. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jen Welch Lackey
Description: 
This is creamy chicken chowder and is a nice change from regular chili. It warms you up and will soon become a family favorite!! It's my MOST requested recipe!
