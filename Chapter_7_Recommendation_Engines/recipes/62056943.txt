Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15-oz cans black beans
1 15-oz can garbanzo beans
1 15-oz can sweet corn
2 14.5-oz cans petite cut diced tomatoes
2 cans diced green chiles
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 sweet red pepper, diced
1 vidalia or other sweet onion, diced
1 large bunch of cilantro, chopped
juice and pulp of 5 limes
1 to 3 (to taste) jalepenos, chopped
3 avocados, diced

Directions:
1. Drain beans and corn and pour into large bowl.
2. Add diced tomatoes, with juice from cans.
3. Mix in remaining ingredients, gently adding avocados last.
4. In air-tight container, refrigerate overnight to allow flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Randi
Description: 
A delicious relish with Mexcican dishes or serve with tortilla chips as a snack or appetizer.
