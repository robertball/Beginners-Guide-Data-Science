Title:
Greek Lemon Chicken and Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843345.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken legs
1 small bag of smaller potatoes halved or whole baby potatoes (any kind you like)
2 whole lemons
2 tablespoons of basil
2 tablespoons of oregano
1 tablespoon of salt
1 tablespoon of pepper
2 tablespoon of your favourite lemon seasoning
1/2 cup of oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
In a bowl mix potatoes with 1 of the juiced lemons, basil, oregano, salt, pepper,lemon seasoning, and oil. Toss to coat.
Place chicken on greased pan, place potatoes all around chicken, and with all the drippings in bottom of bowl pour over chicken.
squeeze the last lemon all over .
Place in oven and reduce temperature to 400
cook for about 1 hour. shake potatoes a bit half way through to prevent sticking.
