Title:
Quick Kale and Turmeric Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3251267.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ice cubes
1 cup almond milk
1 banana
3 leaves kale, large stems discarded, leaves chopped
1/4 cup flax seed meal
2 tablespoons chopped fresh ginger
2 tablespoons chopped fresh turmeric root
1 tablespoon almond butter
1/4 teaspoon stevia
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, almond milk, banana, kale, flax seed meal, ginger, turmeric root, almond butter, stevia powder, cayenne pepper, and ground black pepper together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Katrina Waite
Description: 
This is a quick meal replacement, detox vitamin-packed drink. You will love it!
