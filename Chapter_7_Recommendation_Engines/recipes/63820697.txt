Title:
Fennel leek cannellini bean pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fennel stems
1 leek
5 garlic cloves
1 can cannellini beans
Lemon zest
Parm cheese or goat cheese
12-16oz pasta
Parsley to serve

Directions:
Cook garlic fennel leek low heat to soften/carmelize
Add beans
Add lemon zest
Warm with pasta and pasta water
Salt pepper to taste
Serve with parm or goat cheese, lemon, parsley

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Simple fennel leek bean pasta. Good with garbanzo beans too
