Title:
White Russian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978595.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
3/4 ounce Godiva® Cappuccino Liqueur
2 ounces heavy cream

Directions:
Pour Godiva(R) Cappuccino Liqueur and Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka into an ice-filled old-fashioned glass, stir, and top with cream.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Clean and ultrasmooth Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka gives this cocktail a clear, pure taste unmuddied by impurities or harshness.
