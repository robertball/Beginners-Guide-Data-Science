Title:
Jerky Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/662220.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Yoshida's sweet and savory marinade
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup teryaki sauce
1 tsp liquid smoke
1/4 cup granulated garlic
3/4 cup white wine
3/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Mix together all ingrediants in a large bowl or pot with a lid
Marinate 4lbs of thinly sliced beef for 1 -2 days in a covered vessel in the fridge stirring often. Meat will absorbed most of liquid
