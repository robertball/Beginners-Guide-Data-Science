Title:
Sweete Potatoe Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs cresent rolls
8 frozen sweet potatoe patties
1 1/2 cups water
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 stick margerine
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 cup  finely chopped nuts (walnuts - pecans)

Directions:
Unroll the cresent rolls and lay flat on clean surface.  Cut potatoes in half and roll up into cresent roll stretching to cover all potatoe.  Put nuts in a plate and dip rolls into nuts pressing down to coat top side. Place in 9 X 12 glass baking dish, Nut side up.  In heavy saucepan put in all other ingredients and heat just until sugar and butter are disolved.  Pour over rolls and Bake for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Belinda Owens McMath
Description: 
This can be a veggie dish or a desert.  They are so yummy and easy to make..  I tried these at a family reunion and searched the room to find out who made them and begged the recipe!
