Title:
Sweet Potato Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lg. eggs
1/2 C EVOO
3/4 C sweet potato puree
3/4 C brown sugar, packed
1 t vanilla
1/2 t cinnamon
1 t pumpkin pie spice
1 C stone ground cornmeal
1 C flour
1/2 t baking soda
1 1/2 t baking powder
2 pinches sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven 350. Grease bottom of 7 or 8-in cake pan and dust w/cornmeal or use spring form pan.
In lg. bowl, whisk eggs till frothy and add oil; whisk to combine. Add sweet potatoes and whisk well. Add brown sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and pie spice and whisk to combine.
In separate bowl whisk together cornmeal, flour, baking powder, soda and salt.
Using rubber spatula add dry ingred into wet; and stir by hand just enough to make batter smooth. Pour into prepared cake pan.
Bake center rack for 45 min or until cornbread is firm to touch and golden (or until toothpick test shows clean toothpick). Cool for 10 mins and then remove from pan.
