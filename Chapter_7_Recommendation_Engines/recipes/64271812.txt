Title:
Peach Creamsicle Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1875874.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups sliced peaches
1 cup vanilla yogurt
1/3 cup fresh orange juice
3-5 ice cubes

Directions:
Blend peaches, yogurt and orange juice until smooth. Add ice cubes and pulse until fully combined.
Leftovers can be frozen in popsicle moulds.
