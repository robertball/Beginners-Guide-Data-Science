Title:
Chili Sauce for Rellenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T oil
1 white onion, diced small
2 cloves garlic, minced small
1 red or green bell pepper cut into bite size pieces
1 Tbls Flour
1 can chicken broth
1/2 tsp salt or more to taste
1/2 tsp Cumin or more to taste
2 - 3 Tbls. California Chili Powder

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, saute the onion, garlic and red or green bell peppers in the oil.  Cook unil just soft and fragrant.  Add the salt.
Sprinkle the flour over the mix and stir to create a roux.  Cook for a few minutes....do not brown.
Slowly add the chicken broth and continue to stir so you don't get lumps.
Add the cumin and California Chili powder.
Bring to a slow boil and then turn down and simmer for a few minutes.  Taste, adjust the salt and add more chili powder if desired.
Serve over Chili Rellenos or any mexican inspired egg dish.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This is a light, tasty sauce that is served with Chili Rellenos.
