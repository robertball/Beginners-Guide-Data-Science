Title:
Vichyssoise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. butter
1 cup sliced onions
2 cups thinly sliced potatoes
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup hot milk
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/8 tsp. paprika
2/3 cup light cream
2 Tbsp. chopped chives

Directions:
Saute onion in butter until golden.
Add potatoes and broth and cook until potatoes are tender.
Puree in blender.
Add rest of ingredients.
Serve topped with chives.

Submitter: 
food-dancer
Description: 
A simple, creamy potato and onion soup that's blended to a smooth richness.  Great hot or cold.
