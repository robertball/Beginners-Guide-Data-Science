Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2448141.jpg
Ingredients:
400 g dark chocolate, chopped
250 g  marshmallows
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup desiccated coconut

Directions:
Place chocolate in a medium heatproof bowl.
Stand the bowl over a small pan of simmering water and stir until the chocolate has completely melted and is smooth.
Mix in peanut butter
Roughly spread about 1/4 of the melted chocolate over the base of the tin.
Place the marshmallows, over the chocolate and press lightly to stick, sprinkle the coconut over.
Pour remaining chocolate over the ingredients in the tin, tapping tin on work bench to ensure chocolate is distributed evenly.
Refrigerate to set.
Cut into squares.
