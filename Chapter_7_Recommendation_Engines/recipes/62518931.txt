Title:
Warm Crab Parmesan Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese, divided
12-16 oz lump crabmeat, drained and flaked
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon red Tabasco Sauce
.
Sliced Baguette
Blue Corn Taco Chips
Ritz Crackers or Club Crackers

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small baking dish, mix 1 cup Parmesan with the remaining ingredients.
Bake uncovered in the preheated oven 45 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
Remove from oven and dust with remaining 1/2 cup Parmesan; serve warm with baguette, taco chips or crackers.
