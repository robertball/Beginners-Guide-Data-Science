Title:
Vanilla Winter White Russian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970792.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces vanilla flavored vodka
2 fluid ounces coffee flavored liqueur
2 fluid ounces Irish cream liqueur
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
5 fluid ounces milk

Directions:
In a small pitcher, stir together the vanilla flavored vodka, coffee liqueur, Irish cream, and milk. Place one scoop of vanilla ice cream into each of two highball glasses. Pour the liqueur mixture over the ice cream, and serve immediately.
