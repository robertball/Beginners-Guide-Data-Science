Title:
Marinated Steak with Caramelized Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4c balsamic vinaigrette dressing
3T A1 Steak Sauce
2T brown sugar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 beef sirloin steak (1 lb)
1T olive oil
1 large onion, thinly sliced

Directions:
Mix dressing, 2T steak sauce, sugar, and garlic until well blended.
Pour over steak, marinate 1 hour.
Heat oil in large skillet on medium-high.
Add onions, cooking and stirring 5 minutes.
Cover and cook medium-low 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Heat broiler. Remove steak from marinade and discard marinade.
Place steak on rack of broiler and broil 8 minutes per side until desired doneness.
Remove from heat and cover.
Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
Stir last T of steak sauce into onions and serve with meat.
