Title:
beef kabaab Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Rib steak cut into cubes
1tsp salt
Ground black pepper
Dash of cardamom, cinnamon, all spice, corriander
1tsp lemon juice
2tbs red wine vinegar
3tbs olive oil
A sprig of rosmary
1/2tsp of 7 spice

Directions:
Mix all.ingredients except steak
marinate steak cubes in mixture in fridge for 4+ hrs
Put on skewers alternating with sweet onion & bbq
