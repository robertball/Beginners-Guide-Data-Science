Title:
Heritage Meatball Stew Slow-Cooked Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5187584.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds prepared meatballs
1 can 16 ounce B&M; Beans
1 can 15 ounce Corn Beef Hash, crumbled up
1/2 cup onions, chopped
1 jar 24 ounce Medium Chunky Salsa
1 can 6 ounce black olives (optional)
1 egg

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients into a slow cooker and cook on low for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This was a recipe from a family cookbook on my wife's side. I found the ingredients mixed together were very interesting and chose to change it to a slow cooker recipe. It's simple and a quick way to prepare a pot luck dish that will impress everyone in your next dinner party.
