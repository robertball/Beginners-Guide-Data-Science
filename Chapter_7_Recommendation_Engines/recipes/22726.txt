Title:
Salty Dog II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472678.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger dry gin
5 fluid ounces grapefruit juice
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a highball glass over ice, combine gin, grapefruit juice and salt. Stir well.

Description: 
This simple salty concoction is made with gin, grapefruit juice and salt.
