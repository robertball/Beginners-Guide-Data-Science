Title:
Vanilla Banamango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1009535.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup orange juice, or to taste
1 mango, sliced
1 frozen banana, sliced
2 baby carrots
1 (6 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
2 ice cubes, or as desired
1 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Pour orange juice into the pitcher of a blender and add mango, frozen banana, carrots, vanilla yogurt, ice cubes, and ginger. Pulse several times to crush ice, then blend until smooth, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
