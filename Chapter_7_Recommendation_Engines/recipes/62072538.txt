Title:
Rotel Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large chicken breasts, cooked, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup olive oil
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1 can cream of mushroom soup
16 oz. can diced tomatoes
cooked rice

Directions:
Cook onion in olive oil.  Add rotel, soup and tomatoes; bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 20 minutes.  Uncover and add chopped chicken; simmer 20 more minutes.  Serve over rice.
