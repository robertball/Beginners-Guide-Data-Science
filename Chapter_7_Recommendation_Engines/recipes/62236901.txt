Title:
Brownie Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Butter
1 Cup chocolate chips
4 eggs
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In saucepan over low heat, melt butter and chips. Stir until smooth. Cool. In a bowl combine eggs, sugar and beat. Add flour and vanilla, fold into choc mixture. Fill paper cups 1/2 full. Bake 325 for 30 minutes.
