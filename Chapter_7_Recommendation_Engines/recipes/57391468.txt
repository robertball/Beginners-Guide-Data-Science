Title:
Yummy Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup light Italian-style salad dressing (or however much it takes to cover chops, along with the soy sauce)
1 sliced onion
1 tsp. minced garlic
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon pepper
4 boneless pork chops

Directions:
In a bowl, mix the Italian-style salad dressing, soy sauce, and pepper.
Trim all fat from the chops.
Place the pork chops in a skillet over medium heat, and cover with the dressing mixture. Cover skillet, and cook pork chops 25 minutes, turning occasionally. Remove cover, reduce heat to low, and continue cooking 15-20 minutes.
