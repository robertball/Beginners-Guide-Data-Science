Title:
Zesty Caesar Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 cups DannonÂ® Plain Yogurt
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon chopped garlic
Salt and cracked black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl and mix well. Season with salt and cracked pepper.

Submitter: 
DANNON
Description: 
This yogurt dip based on creamy Caesar salad dressing is made with parmesan, lemon juice, garlic, and cracked pepper and is great for dipping veggies or chips.
