Title:
7UP Holiday Orange Spice Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987448.jpg
Ingredients:
1 quart orange sherbet
1 (2 liter) bottle 7UP®
12 ounces Canada Dry® Ginger Ale
6 ounces orange juice
6 ounces cranberry juice
1 pinch pumpkin spice
1 cup Canadian whiskey (optional)

Directions:
Add ingredients to a 1 gallon punch bowl.
Garnish the punch bowl with lemon, lime, and orange slices, cinnamon sticks, and a touch of pumpkin spice.

Submitter: 
7UP
Description: 
Orange sherbet, fruit juices, 7UP, ginger ale, and pumpkin spice make a festive holiday punch.
