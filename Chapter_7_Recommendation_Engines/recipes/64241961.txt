Title:
Horseradish Vinaigrette (Thanksgiving 2014) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T prepared horseradish (Batampte brand, plain)
1 large clove garlic, peeled
1 small shallot, peeled
1 t honey
2 T pinot grigio vinegar
1/4 c olive oil
1/2 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1/4 c loosely packed parsley leaves
2 T sour cream

Directions:
Process all ingredients in blender until smooth. Refrigerate until needed.

Submitter: 
meghanmacrae
Description: 
One of the best dressings ever. Mix a double batch for the Autumn Salad, some to dress and some to pass.
