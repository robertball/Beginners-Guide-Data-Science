Title:
Bayou Shrimp Etouffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs fresh shrimp, peeled and deviened
1/4 lb oleo or 3 T oil
1 cup onions, chopped fine
1/2 cup celery, chopped fine
1/2 cup bell pepper, chopped fine
4 cloves garlic, pressed
1 T cornstarch (arrowroot)
1 1/2 c wter
salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper (don't skimp) to taste
cooked rice

Directions:
Split shrimp and season generously with salt, black and cayenne pepper.  Set aside
Melt oleo or oil and add onions, celery, bell pepper and garlic until onions are wilted.
Add seasoned shrimp and simmer lightly, stirring occassionally for 20 minutes
Dissolve cornstarch (arrowroot) in water and add to mixture.  Cooke another 15 minutes, stirring occassionally
Serve over rice
