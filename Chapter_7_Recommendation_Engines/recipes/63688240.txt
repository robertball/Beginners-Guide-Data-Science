Title:
Mediterranean Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of garbanzo beans
1/2 cup chopped sundried tomatoes OR a pint of cherry tomatoes quartered
1 small read onion finely chopped
1 red bell pepper finely chopped
1 small zucchini chopped
1 small yellow summer squash chopped
2-3 minced gloves garlic
1/2 cup chopped FRESH parsley OR cilantro
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons FRESH lemon juice
1/4 cup EVOO
1/2 teaspoon oregano
salt and pepper to taste
Feta or fresh shaved parmesan cheese for garnish.

Directions:
In a small bowl whisk the red wine vinegar, lemon juice, oregano, EVOO and S&P together well.  Set aside.  Have a large bowl handy, glass is preferred.  Open and drain the 2 cans of garbanzo beans in a strainer.  Chop, mince and dice all of the vegetables and add to the bowl.  Add the drained beans and then the dressing.  Toss gently and refrigerate up to 3 hrs.  Serve over a bed of crisp greens with a good sprinkle of the cheese of your choice.

Submitter: 
Mary P.
Description: 
There is a bowl of this salad in our refrigerator all summer long.  It's great served cold over a bed of lettuce or stuffed in a pita for a quick lunch.  It's the perfect summer side with any grilled meat, fish or chicken.
