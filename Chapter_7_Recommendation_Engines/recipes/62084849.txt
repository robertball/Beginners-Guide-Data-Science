Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4 lb pork loin roast
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 package golden onion soup mix
three yellow onions
5 red potatoes
2 - 3 cups baby carrots

Directions:
quarter the onions and potatoes and put them in the bottom of the slow cooker along with the baby carrots.  Place pork roast on top of vegetables.  In a bowl combine the cream of mushroom soup and the golden onion soup mix.  Pour mixture over the roast.  Cover and cook on high for 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
mam62505
Description: 
This slow-cooker recipe is perfect for a nice Sunday meal.  It couldn't be easier and the result is so tasty!
