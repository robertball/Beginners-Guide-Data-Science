Title:
Chili Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs
2 c cottage cheese
1 lb shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 c butter
1 4 oz can green chilies
1/4 c flour
1 tsp baking powder
2-3 tomatoes, sliced
Bunch green onions, diced

Directions:
In large bowl combine all ingredients except tomatoes and green onions and mix until smooth.
Pour in greased 9x13 inch baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
Layer Sliced tomatoes and sliced green onions on top and bake for additional 15 minutes.
