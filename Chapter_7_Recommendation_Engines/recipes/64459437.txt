Title:
Sweet Potato Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large sweet potatoes, scrubbed, dried and sliced very thin
2 tbs olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 and spray baking sheet with cooking spray. In a large bowl toss sweet potatoes, oil and seasoning to coat. Transfer to baking dish in a single layer. Bake about 25 minutes flipping once. Outside should be crisp and inside should be soft.
