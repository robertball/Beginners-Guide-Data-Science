Title:
Traditional Italian Knot Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998035.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Butter softened
1/2 Cup Shortening
3/4 Cup Sugar
6 Large Eggs
1 Teaspoon Anise Oil (Not Extract)
7 Cups of All Purpose Flour
6 Tablespoons Baking Powder
Extra flour is needed for rolling.
Icing
2 Cups Powdered Sugar
1/4 Water
2 drops Anise Oil or Lemon Extract

Directions:
Mix flour and baking powder and set aside.  In an electric mixer cream butter, shortening, and sugar.  Add anise flavor and eggs 1 at a time.  Add flour mix gradually.  Dough will be sticky. Refrigerate over night.  Prehat oven to 350 degrees. Turn dough out on floured surface adding flour as needed.  Make 1" balls, then roll into a log shape about 3" long. Tie in a knot and place on an ungreased baking sheet about an inch apart.  Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
Cool completely before icing.
Add small amounts of water to the powdered sugar until it is a nice smooth consistency. Add a couple of drops of the Anise oil or Lemon Extract. You can also add some food coloring at this point.  Dip cookies one at a time a place on a wax paper to dry.  If desired, before the icing dries, sprinkle with rainbow colored sprinkles.

Submitter:

Description: 
There are many variations to these cookies.  Every Italian family seems to make there own version. This is ours!
