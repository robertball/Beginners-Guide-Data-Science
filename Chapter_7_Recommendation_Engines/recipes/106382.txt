Ingredients:
1 head Tanimura & Antle Broccoflower速, cut into 1-inch florets
1 cup chopped red bell pepper
1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted
Sauce:
1/4 cup orange juice
1 1/2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions:
Combine sauce ingredients in a small bowl; reserve.
Heat 1/4 cup water in wok or skillet over medium-high heat.  Add Broccoflower(R) and cover for 1 to 2 minutes.  Add red pepper and sauce.  Stir-fry 2 to 3 minutes or until Broccoflower(R) is crisp tender and sauce is thickened.  Stir in sesame seeds.
