Title:
Surf & Turf Grilled Ranch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz steak sirloin tops, 1" cubes
8 oz shrimp, 21-25 peeled & deveined
bamboo skewers
1 lg bag mixed greens
1 lg red pepper, 1/4" slices
1 med. vidalia onion, 1/4" slices
ranch dressing
vegetable oil
salt
ground black pepper
grape tomatoes

Directions:
Soak skewers in water for 30 minutes or more. Skewer beef and shrimp seperately. Toss in vegetable oil and add salt and pepper.
Preheat grill. Grill beef to desired doneness. Grill shrimp until opaque. On a large serving plate, place greens and top with red pepper, onion and grape tomatoes. Drizzle with ranch dressing. Serve immediately.
