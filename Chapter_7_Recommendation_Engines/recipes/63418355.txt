Title:
Warm Kale and Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/923369.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
2 cups water
1 large bunch curly kale, stemmed and chopped
1/2 cup toasted, sliced or slivered almonds or toasted pine nuts
1/2 cup pomegranate arils or sections from 2 navel oranges cut into small pieces
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil
sea salt to taste
fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine 1 cup quinoa and 2 cups water in a medium pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork. Add chopped kale to the quinoa and cover for a few minutes until kale wilts.
Combine lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper to a small jar and shake well.
In a large bowl, toss kale and quinoa with remaining ingredients and dressing. Serve warm or cold.
