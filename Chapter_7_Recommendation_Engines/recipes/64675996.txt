Title:
Peach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup water
4-5 cups peaches
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Combine sugar, cornstarch, salt
Mix into peaches and water
Bring to boil for 1 minute, cool
Pour into pie shell
Bake 30 minutes
