Title:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes Secret Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 pounds unpeeled red potatoes, quartered
1 pounds butter, room temperature
8 ounces Romano cheese, grated
2 1/2 whole cloves of  chopped garlic
1 Tbs salt
1 Tbs dried oregano

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm,  about 45 minutes; drain.  Stir in butter, cheese, garlic, salt and oregano.  Mash with a potato masher or with an electric mixer.

Submitter: 
Jack Pleugers
Description: 
Red potatoes mashed with butter, Romano cheese, garlic, salt and oregano.  A new twist on a familiar favorite!Modified from: Garlic Mashed Potatoes Secret Recipe
