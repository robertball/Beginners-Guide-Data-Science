Title:
Baked Potatoe Rounds. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/504244.jpg
Ingredients:
3   Large Baking Potatoes
1/3 Cup Of Sweet Bbq Sauce I use (PC Smokin Stampede)
3/4 cup of Shredded Old Chedder Cheese
1/2 Pound Bacon Cooked-Diced
1/2 Cup Sour Cream- Thick Restaurant Style
3/4 Cup Of Chopped Green onion.
1 tsp Butter- Not Margarine. Just not the same.

Directions:
Scrub,and Pierce potatoes & Cook Either in the microwave or Oven. Let Cool.When Cool enough to handle Cut each potatoe width wise into circles about a little less than 1/2 inch thick. Place on a Buttered Cookie sheet.Brush Potatoes with bbq Sauce Gererously. Sprinnkle with cheese and Cooked Bacon.
Bake For 10 minutes until cheese is melted. remove from oven carefully place on your serving dish if you dont like the look of your pan. top withsour cream and then sprinkle with green onion. Serve Hot.
