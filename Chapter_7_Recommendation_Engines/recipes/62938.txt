Title:
Grenadine Half-Mast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659273.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice
6 fluid ounces sweet and sour mix
3 fluid ounces tequila
1 1/2 fluid ounces grenadine syrup
1 fluid ounce triple sec (orange-flavored liqueur)

Directions:
Fill a large glass with ice. Pour sweet and sour mix, tequila, grenadine, and triple sec over the ice; stir.

Submitter: 
elaffint
Description: 
This modification of the standard half-mast recipe uses grenadine instead of cranberry juice and a tad more tequila.
