Title:
Roasted Potatoes with Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb small new potatoes, halved
1 head garlic, cloves separated but not peeled
2 T olive oil
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Arrange potatoes and garlic cloves in a 9x13" pan.  Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Bake 30-40 minutes until potatoes until tender and garlic is soft.  Squeeze the roasted garlic from the peels onto the potatoes.  Mix together.
