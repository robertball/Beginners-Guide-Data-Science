Title:
Spinach Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/628757.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boxes frozen chopped spinach thawed and squeezed
2 cups herb stuffing mix
2 cups onion chopped fine
6 eggs beaten
1/2 cup melted margarine
1/2 cup parmesean cheese
1 tablespoon garlic powder
2/3 teaspoon thyme
1/2 tablespoon pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and let sit for 1/2 hour.
Form into 1" balls and put on ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pam Earl-Vienneau
Description: 
Got this recipe from a caterer years ago. Even people who don't like spinach like these!
