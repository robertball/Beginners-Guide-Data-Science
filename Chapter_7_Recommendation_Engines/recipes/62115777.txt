Title:
Fudgy Peanut Butter Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookies
1 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix
1 stick (1/2 cup) margarine, melted
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 (10 ounce) package peanut butter chips, divided
Glaze
Reserved 1/2 cup peanut butter chips
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 tablespoon milk

Directions:
Measure 1/2 cup peanut butter chips and set aside for the glaze.
In a large bowl, combine cake mix, melted margarine, and eggs; mix well. Add all but the reserved 1/2 cup of peanut butter chips; stir to mix.
Drop dough by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake 9-11 minutes at 350Â° F. Cool 1 minute. Remove to a wire rack and cool completely before glazing.
Glaze: In a small saucepan over low heat, melt glaze ingredients, stirring constantly till smooth. Drizzle over cookies. Let stand until set.

Description: 
Who would ever guess that cookies this fudgy and rich could come from a cake mix? Incredibly delicious, deceptively simple. Don't forget to have some ice-cold milk on hand!
