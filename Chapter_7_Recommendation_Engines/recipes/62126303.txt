Title:
Potato Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857670.jpg
Ingredients:
Parmesean Cheese
paprika
garlic salt
salt
pepper
2 pounds chicken breasts, skinless and with majority of fat removed
6T melted margarine or butter
mashed potato flakes

Directions:
grease a pan and preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place breasts bone-up on a cutting board and sprinkle on cheese, paprika, garlic salt, salt, and pepper.
Place breasts in pan bone-side down and coat this side with cheese, paprika, garlic salt, salt, and pepper.
Coat breasts lightly with potato flakes, then distribute half of the butter or margarine on top.
Re-coat the chicken with another sprinkling of flakes on top, and put rest of butter or margarine on. Place in oven and cook for 35-50 minutes, or until chicken is done.
