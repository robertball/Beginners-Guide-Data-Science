Title:
Persimmon Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1641959.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups almond milk
1/3 cup plain kefir
1 1/3 cups chopped kale, or as needed
1 1/3 cups chopped fresh spinach, or as needed
1 1/3 cups chopped Swiss chard, or as needed
3 persimmons, peeled
1 1/2 frozen bananas
1/2 avocado, peeled and pitted
1 tablespoon chia seeds
1 teaspoon honey, or more to taste (optional)

Directions:
Blend almond milk and kefir together in a blender; add kale, spinach, and Swiss chard. Blend until smooth and the mixture reaches the 4 cup mark. Add persimmons, bananas, avocado, chia seeds, and honey; puree until smooth.
