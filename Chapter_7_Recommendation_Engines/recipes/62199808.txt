Title:
Christmas "Odor" Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt pineapple juice
1 qt water
1 qt apple cider
4 pieces ginger
3 3-inch sticks cinnamon
16 whole cloves
1 tsp allspice
1 or 2 tsp pickling spice

Directions:
Combine in large kettle or teapot; bring to boil. Boil several minutes. Simmer. Add water as needed.

Submitter: 
Buck Sommerkamp
