Title:
Cheesy Fiesta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
2 C. cubed frozen potatoes (hashbrowns)
1 can tomatoes with chiles (Rotel)
1 can nacho cheese soup
1 or 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or colby jack)
1 tbsp. taco seasoning
1/4 tsp. onion salt
1 1/2 cups taco chips (about 3/4 cup crushed)

Directions:
Brown hamburger with taco seasoning.  Drain.  While meat is browning, combine potatoes with Rotel (do not drain) and onion salt. Spray casserole dish with cooking spray.  Spread potato mixture in casserole dish.  Spread nacho cheese soup over potato mixture.  Top with seasoned browned beef. Sprinkle cheese over beef.  Crush taco chips and sprinkle over cheese.  Bake for 30 min. at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
Cheesey, beefy potato casserole really hits the spot! This just got thrown together one night with pretty tasty results.
