Title:
Mom's Red Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. olive oil
1/4 c. butter
6 medium cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 c. chopped fresh parsley (or 1T. dried)
1 T. dried basil
3 T. parmesan cheese
6 oz. can tomato paste
3/4 c. water
1 can minced (or chopped) clams with juice
pinch red pepper flakes (or to taste)

Directions:
Melt the oil with the butter in a small skillet.
Add the crushed garlic and saute until soft.
Add the remaining ingredients and using a whisk gently stir until all ingredients come together to form a sauce.
Bring to a low boil.
Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes.
Serve over 8 oz. of hot, cooked pasta.

Submitter: 
Diana S.
Description: 
To satisfy my father's love for Italian food, my Mom came up with this delicious (and amazingly easy) red clam sauce. I love to make it on busy nights because I can have dinner ready in under half an hour.
