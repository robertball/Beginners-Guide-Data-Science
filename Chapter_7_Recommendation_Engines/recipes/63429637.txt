Title:
Baked Haddock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon Old Bay
3/4 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup fresh parsley (finely chopped)
4 haddock fillets
1/4 cup butter, melted
lemon, cut in wedges

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
In a small bowl, combine the milk, salt & Old Bay.  In a separate bowl, mix together the bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, and parsley.  Dip the haddock fillets in the milk, then press into the crumb mixture to coat. Place haddock fillets in a glass baking dish, sprinkle with fresh lemon juice and drizzle with melted butter
Bake on the top rack of the preheated oven until the fish flakes easily, about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
JMASTRON
Description: 
Haddock (or other white fish) is lightly breaded and baked.  Parmesan adds a nice flavor.  Quick and easy to prepare, it's a nice alternative to deep frying.Modified from: Baked Haddock
