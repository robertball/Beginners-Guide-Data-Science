Title:
Overnight mashed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 medium potatoes, peeled & diced
2 Tbsp butter
8 oz cream cheese
1/4 cup sour cream
salt
pepper
Butter
paprika

Directions:
Boil potatoes until done, drain.
Mash potatoes with 2 tbsp butter, cream cheese, sour cream, and salt & pepper to taste.
Place in 2 quart casserole dish (coated in cooking spray).
Dot top with additional butter and sprinkle paprika.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes, uncovered

Submitter: 
BOILERMISH
Description: 
These mashed potatoes taste best after sitting overnight.  Boil/mash the night before then bake the next day to have with dinner.
