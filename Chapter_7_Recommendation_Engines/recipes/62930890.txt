Title:
Bockwurst (Hot Dogs In Natural Casing) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/717307.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Bockwurst (Or Hot Dog) In Natural Casing
1 Brotchen Rolls
1 Tablespoon of Lowensenf German Mustard
1 oz of onions, chopped
2 Oz of sauerkraut, shredded (Optional)

Directions:
Slice Roll
Boil water, and then turn down to low, simmer hot dogs for 30 mins.
Note: I let my hot dogs cook for longer to allow some of the salt to be removed.
Heat Roll In A 400F preheated oven for 10 mins
Add Bockwurst (Or hot dog), Mustard, Onions, And sauerkraut
