Title:
Crustless Mushroom-Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz frozen chopped spinach, cooked, drained and pressed
1 tsp oil or butter
2 scallions
1 cup mushrooms
1 cup whipped cream
3 eggs
pinch of nutmeg
1/4-1/2 tsp sweet basil
a pinch of dill
1/4 tsp tarragon
1 cup grated cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cook spinach, drain and press out extra moisture
Place spinach in bottom of pie plate
Sautee scallions and mushrooms in oil or butter
Place scallions and mushrooms on top of spinach
Mix well whipped cream and eggs and add spices
Pour egg mixture over vegetables in pie plate
Grate cheese (your choice) and place over egg mixture
Bake 1/2 hour or until soft-set
