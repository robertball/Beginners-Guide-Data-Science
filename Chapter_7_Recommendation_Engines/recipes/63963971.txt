Title:
Real Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 clove garlic
1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, rinsed & drained
2-3 tablespoons lemon juice
2-4 tablespoons tahini
1/2 - 1 teaspoon salt
1 tsp cumin (optional)
2-3 tablespoons water, as needed

Directions:
In a blender, chop the garlic. Pour garbanzo beans into blender, reserving about a tablespoon for garnish. Place lemon juice, tahini, chopped garlic and salt in blender. Blend until creamy and well mixed.
Transfer the mixture to a medium serving bowl. Sprinkle with additional paprika.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
A traditional hummus recipe includes tahini and makes a wonderful snack or appetizer. Serve with warm pita bread or veggies.Modified from: Real Hummus
