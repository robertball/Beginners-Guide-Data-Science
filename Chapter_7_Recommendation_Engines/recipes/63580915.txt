Title:
Southern Corn Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T butter, room temperature
1 1/2 t Old Bay
1/4 cup chives
1/2 t salt
1/4 t black pepper

Directions:
Mix all well with a fork or whisk.  Spread on corn on the cob before serving.

Submitter: 
WEEBRIE
Description: 
I'm from the South, live in GA, and we love a good low country boil.  However - this spread compliments corn regardless of what your serve it with!
