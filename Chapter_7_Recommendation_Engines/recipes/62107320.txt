Title:
Sour Cream Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
4 squares (1 oz.) unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled.
1 cup water
3/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup shortening
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 1/4 tsp. baking powder
Frosting:
1/2 cup butter, softened
6 squares (1 oz.) unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled.
6 cups confectioners sugar
1/2 cup sour cream
6 Tbsp. milk
2 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the first six ingredients and mix well.
Combine the dry ingredients in a separate smaller bowl, gradually add to large bowl mixture.
Beat misture on low speed until smooth and consistent.
Beat on high for 3 minutes.
Pour into two greased and floured 9 inch round baking pans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.
In a mixing bowl, combine frosting ingredients.
Beat until smooth and creamy.
Spread over cake.
Store in refrigerator until ready to serve. Enjoy!
OPTIONAL: For a nice touch, use a potato peeler and shave dark chocolate on top of the cake.
