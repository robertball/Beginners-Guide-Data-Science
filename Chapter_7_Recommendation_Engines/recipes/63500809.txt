Title:
Pistachio Tortoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups cold half-and-half or milk
1 package (4-serving size) JELL-O Pistachio Flavor Instant Pudding & Pie Filling
2 cups thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping
1/2 cup chopped maraschino cherries (drained)
1/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Pour half-and-half (or milk) into medium bowl.  Add pudding mix.  Beat with a ire whisk for two minutes.  Gently stir in whiped topping, cherries, and chips until blended.  Spoon mixture into 10 paper-lined muffin cups.
Freeze for a minimum of 3 hours.  Serve immediately after removing from freezer.

Submitter: 
HDN
Description: 
A quick, easy dessert - very refreshing and low-fat when made with milk.  Tastes similar to pistachio ice cream.

Recipe originally from Favorite Brand Name Country Italian cookbook.
