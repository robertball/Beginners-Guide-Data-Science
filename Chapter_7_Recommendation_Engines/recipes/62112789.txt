Title:
Royal Blue Velvet Bundt Cake With Blueberry Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 packages 9oz each white cake mix
1 1/4 C water
1/4 vegetable oil
3 eggs
1/4 oz blue food coloring
1 cup powdered sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons blueberry preserves
1/2 cup fresh blueberries(optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Spray inside of bundt cake pan with non stick cooking spray. In a large bowl, mix cake mix with water, eggs, oil and food coloring. Pour into pan and bake for 40-45 minutes or until done. Remove from oven and let pan cool on wire rack for 15 minutes. Remove from pan onto cake plate.
In small bowl, add powdered sugar, blueberry preserves and lemon juice, mix well. Pour glaze over top of cake. Garnish with blueberries if desired.
