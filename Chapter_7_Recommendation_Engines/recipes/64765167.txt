Title:
Frank Parra’s Ragatoni Pollo for 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shallots
1 C sherry
1 C chicken stock
1/2 quart heavy cream
Pinch of tarragon
Pinch of salt and pepper
2  pats butter
1 container of sliced fresh mushrooms
2 chicken breasts
2 handfuls of rigatoni

Directions:
In hot skillet sauté shallots, sherry, chicken stock
Reduce heat add cream, tarragon, salt, and pepper
In another skillet, chicken breasts cut into strips sautéed in butter, shallots, mushrooms, salt and pepper.  Cook thru, then add sauce
In another pot 1 t olive oil, 2 handfuls of ragotoni (only add pasta when water is boiling) 8-10 min. Chill pasta w cold water and add some oil so it won’t stick.
Add sauce to pasta
