Title:
PB Chicken Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c hot water
1/4 c Teriyak BBQ sauce
1/4 c creamy peanut butter
1/4 c soy sauce
1/4 c chopped cilantro
2 TBL honey dijon
1 lb boness chix breasts cut in strips

Directions:
Mix ingredients with wire whisk, pour 1.2 in plastic bag. Add chicken, marinate in fridge 2 hours.
Grill 5-7 minutes & serve with reserved sauce
