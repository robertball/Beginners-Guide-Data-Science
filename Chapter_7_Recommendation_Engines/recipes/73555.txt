Title:
Tomato Pesto Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet puff pastry, thawed according to package directions
1/2 cup prepared pesto
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt'sÂ® Whole Tomatoes, sliced

Directions:
Unfold puff pastry on floured surface; crimp edges.  Place on baking sheet sprayed with PAM(R) cooking spray.
Spread pesto evenly across pastry; sprinkle with 1/2 cup cheese, tomato slices, and remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
Bake in preheated 400 degrees F oven 17-20 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve immediately.
