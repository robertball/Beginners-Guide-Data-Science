Title:
Mexican Ham And Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 of a 32 oz pkg of frozen hashbrowns(thawed)
5 oz cooked diced ham
1 can(10 3/4 oz) southwest style pepperjack soup
6 oz sour cream
3-4 Tbs milk
1 cup mexican blend shredded cheese plus a little extra for topping
1 small can chopped green chilies
1/2 of a small onion(chopped)
1/4 of a green pepper(chopped)
1 1/2 cups crushed tortilla chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. spray an 8x8 baking dish with nonstick spray.
In a small pan over medium heat saute onion and green pepper in a small amount of olive oil until softened, let cool a bit.
Meanwhile in a meduim bowl mix the soup, sour cream and milk, stir to combine. Add the can of chiles, the onion and green pepper mixture,ham,cheese and the potaotes, mix to combine.
pour into the greased casserole, cover and cook at 375 for 1/2 hour, uncover and sprinkle with crushed tortilla chips. Bake an additional 30-45 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.
About 5 minutes before done sprinkle with about 1/2 cup of cheese
Let stand 5 minutes before serving
ENJOY!
