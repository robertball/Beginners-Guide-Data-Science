Title:
Cheesy Potato Corn Chowder with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. Butter
1 c. chopped celery
1 c. chopped onion
4 (14.5 oz.) cans chicken broth
4 cups shredded cooked chicken
8 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
2 (15 oz. cans whole kernel corn  lightly drained)
1 (4 oz.) can diced green chilies
2  - 2.5 oz. packages Country Style Gravy Mix
3 cups milk (or 2 cups whole milk and 1 cup ½-n-½)
2 cups shredded Mexican-style cheese

Directions:
1.In a large saucepan, melt butter over med. high heat.  Add celery and onion; cook and stir until tender – about 5 min.
2.Add chicken broth, bring to a boil.  Add potatoes, cook over low heat for 20 min. stirring occasionally.
3.Stir in corn, chicken and chilies; return to boiling.  Dissolve gravy mix in milk; stir into boiling mixture.  Add cheese; cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
I started with the original Cheesy Potato Corn Chowder recipe on Allrecipes.com and modified it a bit.  Very hardy and delicious.  Hope you like it too.  Good with a tossed salad and good bread.
