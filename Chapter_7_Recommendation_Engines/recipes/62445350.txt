Title:
Baked Ziti II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package ziti pasta
24 ounces ricotta cheese
1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
2 eggs, beaten
4 Cloves Garlic. minced
9 oz bag Spinach, lightly steamed
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
1 lb hamburger
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and rinse.
Brown 2 garlic in EVOO, add mushrooms and brown. Remove to a bowl and brown beef in pan, add salt & pepper, drain and add to mushroom mix. Mix in Spaghetti sauce and Italian Seasoning.
In a large bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1/3 mozzarella & eggs, mix in ziti
Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish and spoon in small amount of sauce, then spread 1/2 of ziti mixture, 1/2 of meat mixture, spinach, top w/ 1/3 Mozzarella, top with remaining ziti, meat sauce, and mozzarella, followed by Parmesan cheese on top.
Cover  w/ foil and  Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, removing foil after 20. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
wendi
Description: 
Rich and creamy ricotta cheese is combined with mozzarella, beaten egg and spaghetti sauce to make a rich and filling sauce for cooked ziti. Top with Parmesan cheese and bake for a delicious, classic Italian casserole.Modified from: Baked Ziti II
