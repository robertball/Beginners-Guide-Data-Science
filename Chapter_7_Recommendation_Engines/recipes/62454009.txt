Title:
Easy Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced (don't peel)
1 small yellow onion, diced
2 large jalapenos, seeded, deveined and diced (wear gloves)
1/2 cup chopped up fresh cilantro
1/2 teaspoon salt
juice of one lime ( I used two frozen ice cube-sized pieces)
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and store, tightly covered, in fridge.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Simple, delicious - eat with tostito chips,use on roast beef sandwiches, or as a garnish for grilled chicken.  It's also great on coneys!
