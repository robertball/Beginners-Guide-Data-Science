Title:
Taco Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (16 ounce) can Black Beans
1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning
1/4 teaspoon cumin (or to taste)
1 can diced tomatoes
Toppings
2 Cups Shredded Lettuce
2 Cups Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese
1 Cup diced Bell Pepper
soft hamburger buns

Directions:
Place the ground beef into a skillet over medium heat. Once it starts to brown sprinkle in 1/2 taco seasoning. Cook, stirring to crumble, until completely browned, drain grease.
Mix in the rest of the taco seasoning. Reduce heat to low and stir in the black beans and diced tomatoes, simmering until thoroughly hot.
Serve on hamburger buns with desired toppings.

Submitter: 
Amanda Nye
Description: 
This is quick taco version of the sloppy joe. My boys love this tasty sandwich. It is a favorite in my house. Leftover meat is great on a salad too!!Modified from: Taco Bean Burgers
