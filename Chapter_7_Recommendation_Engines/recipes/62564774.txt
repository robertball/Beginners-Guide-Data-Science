Title:
Cajun Goolash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of smoked sausage
1 14 oz can of petite diced tomatoes
1 8 oz can of tomato sauce
2 tsp. of dried onions
1 15 oz can of corn
2 cups of raw egg noodles
1 cup of water
3 tsp. of chili powder
1 tsp. of garlic powder
1 tsp. of cajun seasoning
8 oz. of velveeta cheese cut into cubes

Directions:
Cut the sausage into 1/2 inch slices.  Brown in a pan on medium heat.  Meanwhile, cook the noodles as directed on the package.  Drain the noodles and place into a large pot.  Add all ingredients except the cheese.  Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes.  Add the cheese.  Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, stirring often.

Submitter: 
hungrydad
Description: 
This is my modified version of my mother-in-law's goolash.  Cheesy, spicy and really good on a cold winter night.
