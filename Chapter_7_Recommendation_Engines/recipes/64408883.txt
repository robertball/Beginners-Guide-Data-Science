Title:
vegan cheese sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c potatoes, diced
1 c carrots, diced
1/2 to 1 c reserved cooking water
1/3 c olive oil
2 tsp salt
1 Tbl fresh lemon juice
1/2 c nutritional yeast
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
dash of cayenne (optional)
1 Tbl chili powder (optional)

Directions:
Boil potatoes and carrots until extremely soft. Reserve 1 cup of the cooking water.
Put all ingredients except 1/2 cup of water in a blender and blend until extremely smooth.
Add more water until mixture reaches desired thickness.

Submitter: 
BusyBeeTami
Description: 
This can taste just like nacho cheese if you add the chili powder. I have added fresh garlic and onion in place of the powdered, and have added salsa at times. Very versatile!
