Title:
Southwest Corn & Black bean salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. corn
1/3 c pine nuts
1/4 c lime juice
2T EVO
1/4 c cilantro
2 (14.5 oz.) black beans, rinsed
2 c shredded red cabbage
1 large tomato, diced
1/2 c minced red onion

Directions:
Cook pine nuts in a dry skillet until fragrant & lightly browned (2-4 min).
Whisk lime juice, oil, cilantro,salt, & pepper in a large bowl. Add remaining ingredients. Refridgerate.
Add salt & pepper before serving
