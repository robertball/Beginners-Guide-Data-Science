Title:
Cinnamon Toast Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons butter, melted
2 Tablespoons light corn syrup
2 teaspoons granulated sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 cups (about 1 lb) pecan halves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
Stir together first 5 ingredients.
Add pecans and toss to coat.
Spread nuts in a single layer on a large pan.
Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Let cool.
Store in airtight container for up to 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
Jeanne Mason
Description: 
A real crowd pleaser and super easy to make. You can make ahead of time but you might want to make yourself an extra batch because you won't be able to wait.
Enjoy!
