Title:
Amazing Cottage Cheese Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs (beaten)
1 large carton small curd cottage cheese
1 1/3 c. flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 T fresh chopped chives or 1 small bunch (2-3) chopped green onion
(splash of milk if batter looks dry)

Directions:
Drop by heaping spoonful into salted boiling water.
Boil until they float to the top and roll over.
Strain with slotted spoon and fry in butter.

Submitter: 
my4teengirls
Description: 
These fried dumplings are a great way to use up that cottage cheese in the fridge and wonderful comfort food. I like to make it as a side for a change from potatoes or rice.
