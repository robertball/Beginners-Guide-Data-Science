Title:
Dijon Crusted Halibut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2  tablespoons prepared Dijon-style mustard
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/4 cup Panko bread crumbs
1 1/2  tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
4 (4 ounce) halibut steaks
3/4 cup Panko bread crumbs
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
Olive Oil PAM

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mustard, horseradish, and lemon juice. Stir in 1/4 cup bread crumbs and 1 1/2  tablespoons Parmesan cheese. Arrange fish on the prepared baking sheet. Spread mixture evenly over fish.
In a small bowl, mix 3/4 cup bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Press onto the coated halibut.  Spray top generously with Olive Oil PAM.
Bake 25 minutes, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.  Crisp up in broiler for several minutes.
