Title:
TexMex Shark & Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 thawed Blacktip Shark steaks (About an inch thick, 5 inches long) cut in half.
tsp olive oil
Small bag frozen pre-cooked shrimp.
1 14.5oz can Diced tomatoes and green chili's.
4 servings wide egg noodles (follow directions on bag)
2 cups shredded mozarella
pepper to taste

Directions:
Start noodles in seperate pot.
Run shrimp under cold water in strainer until ice is off of them. (Use the amount you like)
Warm olive oil in skillet on medium heat. Add 4 pieces of shark, shrimp and can of diced tomatoes with green chili's. Simmer with lid on about 15 minutes. Make sure shark is thoroughly cooked. It should be flaky and white.
When noodles have been cooked and drained, put on plate. Add cooked shark/shrimp mixture on top. Sprinkle with Mozarella and serve. Add black pepper to taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great spicy change for shark and shrimp.  You can probably get non-seafood eaters to enjoy this! I did!
