Title:
Turkey Pot Pie II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 refrigerated pie crusts (1 box for double crust pie)
2 cups cubed cooked turkey
1 cup frozen corn, thawed
1 cup frozen peas, thawed
1 stalk fresh celery
1 diced potato
2 tablespoons dried minced onion
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom w/roasted garlic soup
1/2 cup milk
1 cup shredded cheese
1/2 t poultry seasoning
1/2 t paprika
1/2 t dried parsley
1/2 t garlic powder
1/2 t garlic salt
1/2 t oregano
1/2 t thyme
1/2 t rosemary
1/2 t black pepper
1/2 t sage
1 t chicken bouillon granules
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line bottom of pie pan with crust.
Mix together the turkey or chicken, vegetables, onion, soup, cheese, seasonings and milk. Pour into pie crust, cover with top crust and crimp edges.
Egg wash and poke holes in top crust.
Bake for 40 to 50 minutes.
