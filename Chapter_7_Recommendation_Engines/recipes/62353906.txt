Title:
Balsamic Vinaigrette for Spinach/Strawberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c Balsamic Vinegar
1/4 c Olive Oil
2 Tablespoons Sugar
1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients well. Pour over salad or keep in the fridge - shake before use.

Submitter: 
MK!
Description: 
Delicious dressing for a Spinach and Strawberry Salad - add whatever other ingredients you have/want in your salad.
