Title:
Contadina速 Classic Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package dried lasagna noodles
1 pound mild Italian sausage, casings removed
1 (15 ounce) can CONTADINA速 Extra Thick & Zesty Tomato Sauce
1 (14.5 ounce) can CONTADINA速 Diced Tomatoes, undrained
1/2 cup beef broth
1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package direction; drain.
Meanwhile, cook sausage in large skillet until no longer pink, stirring to break up sausage; drain.
Add tomato sauce, undrained tomatoes and broth; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes. Combine ricotta cheese, egg and salt.
Layer 1/3 noodles, 1/2 meat sauce, 1/3 noodles, all ricotta cheese mixture,  1/2 mozzarella cheese, remaining noodles, remaining meat sauce and remaining mozzarella cheese in 13x9 inch baking dish.
Cover and bake in preheated 350 degrees F oven, 30 minutes. Bake, uncovered, 15 minutes or until bubbly.

Here's an easy recipe for preparing a classic lasagna with Italian sausage and lots of cheese.
