Title:
Barbecue Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup panko bread crumbs, seasoned
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup Sweet Baby Ray's Cola barbecue sauce
1 tablespoon McCormick's Grill Mates Molasses Bacon
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 1/2 teaspoon Adobo with pepper (con piemento)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking pan.
Place the ground beef, eggs, panko, and 1/2 cup ketchup in a large bowl. Mix by hand, and season with McCormick's Grill Mates Molasses Bacon, garlic powder, onion powder, and Adobo with pepper . Form into a loaf shape, and place on the prepared baking pan. Drizzle with 1/4 cup barbecue sauce. Sprinkle McCormick's Grill Mates Molasses Bacon over the top.
Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, to an internal temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

Submitter: 
Kat
Description: 
Ground beef, ketchup, and Japanese bread crumbs (panko) are mixed with a couple of eggs and some seasonings to yield an easy, delicious meatloaf.Modified from: Jackie's Special Meatloaf
