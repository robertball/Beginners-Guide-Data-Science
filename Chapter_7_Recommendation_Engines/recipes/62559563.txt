Title:
High Protein Breakfast Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535464.jpg
Ingredients:
Non-fat cooking spray
3 egg whites
1/3 cup quick cooking oats
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 cup frozen blueberries

Directions:
Coat pan with non-fat spray- set over med. high heat. Whisk together egg whites, oatmeal, and cinnamon, then fold in the blueberries. Pour into heated skillet and cook until puffed up slightly and brown on one side- flip and cook other side, just like you would a pancake. Serve with sugar free syrup.
