Title:
Cabbage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tsp olive oil or cooking spray
1/2 onion, chopped
2 stalks of celery
20 baby carrots cut up
2 cloves garlic, chopped
optional: 1 tsp cayenne pepper, 1/2 tsp italian dressing, 1 potato
2 quarts water + 1.5 cups water
4 tsp chicken bouillon
1 tsp salt, or to taste
1/2 tsp black pepper, or to taste
1/2 head cabbage, cored and coarsely chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes, drained and diced vs tom with juice

Directions:
In a large stockpot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Stir in onion and garlic; cook until onion is transparent, about 5 minutes.
Stir in water, bouillon, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then stir in cabbage. Simmer until cabbage wilts, about 10 minutes.
Stir in tomatoes. Return to a boil, then simmer 15 to 30 minutes, stirring often.
