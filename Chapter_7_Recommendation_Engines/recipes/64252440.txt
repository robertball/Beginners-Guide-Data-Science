Title:
Buffalo Chicken Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages reduced-fat cream cheese
3/4 cup (6 ounces) cayenne pepper sauce (Crystal or Frank's RedHot)
1/2 cup ranch dressing or bleu cheese dressing
1 1/4 - 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cooked and chopped (3 to 4 chicken breast halves or about 3 cups chopped)
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese or mozzarella
Tortilla chips, crackers, or vegetables, for serving

Directions:
1Preheat the oven to 350Â° F. Spray a 1 1/2-quart casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray.
2Combine cream cheese, pepper sauce, and ranch dressing in a small saucepan. Heat over low heat, stirring frequently, until melted.
3Stir in chicken and about half of the cheddar cheese. Stir until combined.
4Spoon into prepared dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
5Bake 15 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted.
6Serve warm with tortilla chips, crackers, or vegetables

Submitter: 
RDG
Description: 
you can make the recipe ahead of time and just warm it up in the oven just before the game starts. Bonus: if the dip needs a little more heat at halftime, just throw it in the microwave for a minute to bring it back to its creamy state.
