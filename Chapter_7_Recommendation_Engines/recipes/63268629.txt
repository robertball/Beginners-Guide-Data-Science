Title:
Candied Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 t. cornstarch
1 T. lemon juice
1/3 c. butter
1/2 c. hot water

Directions:
Heat all ingredients in a saucepan until sugar is dissolved.
Pour over sweet potatoes that are peeled, sliced/diced, and bake at 350 degrees until the potatoes are done.
