Title:
Pork Chops for the Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless pork chops
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup ketchup
2 cloves garlic, crushed
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place pork chops in slow cooker. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over pork chops.
Cook on Low setting for 4 - 6 hours, until internal temperature of pork has reached 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
