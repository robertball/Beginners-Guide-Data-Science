Title:
Slow Cooker Beer and BBQ Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Beer
2 Bottles of your favorite 12 oz. BBQ Sauce
1 Chicken bouillon cube
2 lbs pork butt
1 lb peeled baby carrots

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in slow cooker
Cook low for 10 hours or high for 5 hours
Shred pork with fork
Serve

Submitter: 
ANGELBEBEBOO
Description: 
Easy and tasty pulled pork recipe.  The beer adds a subtle nutty flavor and goes so well with BBQ flavors.  This is my honey's favorite bbq recipe.  Serve alone or as a sandwich with corn, potatoes, and/or coleslaw.  Yumm!
