Title:
Peanut Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup Jiff creamy peanut butter
2 cups confectioners sugar (powdered sugar)
3 Tablespoons cream, or more as needed (Heavy whipping cream or Half and Half)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, beat together butter and peanut butter until light and fluffy. Slowly mix in 1 cup of  the powdered sugar. Mix in the cream. Add the remaining powdered sugar, beating well. If necessary, whip in a little more cream until the frosting reaches a good spreading consistency. Frost completely cooled CHOCOLATE cake or cupcakes. Enjoy!!!
