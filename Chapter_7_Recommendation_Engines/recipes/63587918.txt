Title:
Chunky Curry Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 carrot- sliced up
1 Tbsp curry powder
4 cloves minced garlic
3 cups water
chicken base to taste
1 can chick peas
cauliflower- cut into small pieces
fresh cilantro for garnish
feta cheese for garnish

Directions:
In a large pot, heat a little bit of olive oil or coconut oil.
Saute the carrots. Add the curry powder. Stir frequently.
Add the garlic and saute for 30 seconds being careful not to scorch it.
Add the water and the chicken base.
Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
Add Cauliflower and simmer 5 more minutes.
Spoon into bowls. Add feta and cilantro before serving.
(I left out the feta since I had none.)

Submitter: 
Sherillynn
Description: 
Hearty and flavorful. Great on a cold windy day.
