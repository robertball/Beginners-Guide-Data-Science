Title:
Fajita K-Bobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1128983.jpg
Ingredients:
K-BOBS:
1LB OF CHICKEN AND/OR BEEF-CUBED OR CUT INTO STRIPS
1 ONION-CUT INTO BITE SIZED CUBES
1 GREEN PEPPER-CUT INTO BITE SIZED CUBES
MARINADE:
3 TABLESPOONS-OLIVE OIL
1 1/2 TABLESPOONS RED WINE VINEGAR
1 LIME JUICED
1/2 CUP CHOPPED FRESH CILANTRO
1-TSP-CHILI POWDER
1/2 TSP-PAPRIKA
1/2 TSP-ONION POWDER
1/2 TSP-GARLIC POWDER
1/4 TSP-CAYENNE PEPPER OR TO TASTE
12-14 WOOD OR METAL SKEWERS-IF USING WOOD, SOAK IN WATER FOR 1 HOUR BEFORE ASSEMBLING K-BOBS.
12-14 6 INCH TORTILLAS-WARMED
SOUR CREAM
GUACAMOLE-*OPTIONAL
SALSA

Directions:
IN A BIG MIXING BOWL COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS.  TOSS VIGOROUSLY, UNTIL ALL VEGETABLES  AND MEAT ARE COATED WITH THE SEASONINGS.
COVER AND REFRIGERATE FOR AT LEAST ONE HOUR TO 4 HOURS.
ASSEMBLE K-BOBS ON SKEWERS-WITH 3 PCS OF MEAT OPPOSITE THE VEGETABLES.
ON MEDIUM PRE-HEATED GRILL, BRUSHED WITH OLIVE OIL, COOK K-BOBS, CONSTANTLY TURNING TO PREVENT BURNING.  COOK 7-10 MINUTES UNTIL BEEF COOKED TO DESIRED DONENESS OR CHICKEN IS NO LONGER PINK AND IS DRAINING CLEAR JUICES.
SERVE IMMEDIATELY  BY PLACING THE K-BOB WITH A PREPARED TORTILLA WITH DESIRED ADD ONS SUCH AS SOUR CREAM AND/OR GUACAMOLE.  AND TOPPED WITH FRESH OR STORE BOUGHT SALSA.
SERVE WITH HOMEMADE PINTO BEANS AND AUTHENTIC MEXICAN RICE.

Submitter: 
Sydnee Davis
Description: 
Great recipe to go with a cold margarita or strawberry water.  Served this meal for Cinco de Mayo this year and it was a hit.  Great way to combine a Mexican food favorite with the grill outside.
