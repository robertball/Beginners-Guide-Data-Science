Title:
Spicy Shrimp Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs Olive Oil
1 medium onion coarsely chopped
2-3 cloves garlic minced
1 medium green pepper coarsely chopped
4 oz button mushrooms quartered
25 oz jar spicy marinara sauce
1 tsp - 1 Tbs cajun seasoning - to taste
12 oz peeled de-veined raw shrimp
8 oz cooked drained penne noodles
shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In large skilled saute garlic, onion, green pepper, and mushrooms in olive oil on medium-high heat until crisp-tender and onions turn translucent.
Stir in marinara sauce and cajun seasoning.  Cover, turn heat to med-low and let simmer (approximately 10 minutes) while cooking noodles to package directions.  Stir in shrimp, cover and simmer additional 5-7 minutes until shrimp are opaque and cooked througout.  Stir in warm drained cooked penne noodles.  Top each serving with parmesan cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
A super easy shrimp and pasta dish with a kicked up tomato sauce.  Cubed cooked chicken can also be substituted for shrimp.
