Title:
Barbecue Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1085645.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 inch) prebaked pizza crust or Italian bread shell
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
2 cups Tyson® Grilled and Ready® Fully Cooked Frozen Grilled Chicken Breast Strips, thawed
1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Place pizza crust on a large greased baking sheet. Bake for 5 minutes; remove from oven.
Spread barbecue sauce over pizza crust to within 1/2 inch of the edge. Sprinkle evenly with Grilled & Ready(R) Grilled Chicken Breast Strips, onion and cheese. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until cheese is melted and toppings are heated through. Garnish with cilantro. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Tyson Chicken
Description: 
Grilled chicken breast strips, barbecue sauce, sliced red onions and cheese on a pre-baked pizza crust make a quick weeknight meal.
