Title:
Warm Chipotle Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (8 oz)     Philly Cream Cheese
2 Tbsp     Sour Cream
2 Tbsp     Mayonnaise
1 pkg     Finely Shredded Sharp Cheddar
1 packet    Dry Ranch Seasoning
1/2 tsp (or to taste)     Chipotle Tabasco
1 pkg     Frozen Chopped Spinach, Thawed
1/2 pkg     Frozen Artichoke Hearts, Thawed

Directions:
Drain as much liquid as possible from spinach and artichokes
Roughly chiffonade the artichoke hearts removing any rough pieces.
Mix all ingredients together with the paddle attachment of an electric mixer until very well incorporated.
Optional: Top with extra cheese before you bake if desired
Bake in a 350* oven for 15-20 minutes
