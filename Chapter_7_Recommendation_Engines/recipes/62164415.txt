Title:
Swiss Chard,Sausage, Pasta, and Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 bunch Chard
1 15.25 oz can beans (kidney, black, pinto or garbonzos)
1/2 box Fusilli pasta
2  Italian sausages
1T butter
1/8 t red pepper flakes
2T Basalmic Vinegar
1 med onion chopped
3-4 cloves garlic crushed
1 T chicken boullion
1 ripe tomato chopped
6 oz Feta Cheese
1 t salt
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Bring 5 cups water + 1t salt to boil and cook pasta according to directions. Halfway through cook time add chopped chard stems and chicken boullion and continue cooking. Drain pasta reserving 1 cup of cooking broth and set aside.
Meanwhile, brown sausage and set aside. In browning pan add butter and pepper flakes. When melted add onions and garlic saute 5-7 minutes, stir and add sliced chard leaves top with basalmic vinegar cover and cook 5 minutes over medium heat.
Add drained beans and tomato and cook for 3 minutes more. Add noodles and chard stems and 1/2 of the reserved liquid. toss and taste for flavor salt and pepper as needed.
Add remaining liquid if too dry.
Arrange mixture in casserole dish and top with sliced sausage and feta cheese pressing mixture down.
Bake for 15 minutes and broil for 3-5 to brown cheese.
