Title:
Crumb-Coated Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. dry bread crumbs
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. paprika
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. curry powder
1/4 tsp. pepper
4 boneless pork chops, cut thick

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the first seven ingredients. Add pork chops, one at a time, and shake to coat. Place on a baking sheet coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until chops are no longer pink. Turning them over about half way through the cooking time.
