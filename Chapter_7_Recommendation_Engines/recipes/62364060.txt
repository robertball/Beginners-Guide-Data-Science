Title:
Chocolate Fudge Marshmallow Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C sugar
1 5 oz. can evaporated milk
10 large marshmallows
1/2 C butter
1 C semisweet chocolate chips
1 t vanilla

Directions:
Place sugar, milk, and marshmallows in a large saucepan over medium heat and let come to a boil, stirring with a wooden spoon.  Reduce heat and let simmer for 6 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the butter, chips, and vanilla.  Continue stirring until the frosting is thick and smooth and the chocolate and butter have melted, about 5 minutes.  Use immediately; it sets up quickly.
