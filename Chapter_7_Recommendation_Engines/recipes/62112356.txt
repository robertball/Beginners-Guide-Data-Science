Title:
Quick and Easy Hot Dog Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion (chopped)
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
2 tablespoons white vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons water
1 teaspoon worchestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1/4 teaspoon tabasco sauce (more or less as desired)
1/4 cup ketchup

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion, drain off excess liquid.  Add remaining ingredients - simmer 10 -15 minutes to allow flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Nonna
Description: 
This sweet sauce is quick and easy to whip up and tastes better on hot dogs than the canned chili sauces.
