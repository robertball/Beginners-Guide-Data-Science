Title:
Venison Potato Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef (or venison)
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. instant onion
1/4 c. bread crumbs
1/2 c. milk
1 egg
3/4 c. cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except for cheese. Spread in 9 inch pie pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes. Make mashed potatoes for 4 servings. Spread on baked meat and top with 3/4 cup grated cheddar cheese. Bake 10 minutes longer or until potatoes are hot and cheese is melted.
