Title:
Grandmas Homemade IRISH Potato Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups Hot Mashed Potato
2 Cups of Italian Bread Crumbs
1 Teaspoon of fresh ground pepper
1 Teaspoon of salt
2  Teaspoons of Sage
1/2 stick of unsalted Butter
4 tablespoons of Onion Juice

Directions:
Step one Mix Hot Mashed Potatoes and bread crumbs and all  ingredients slowly  evenly.
Step Two . Serve Hot  and enjoy

Submitter: 
Brian Callahan
Description: 
This is a Old  Irish Family recipe that has been passed down from generation to generation in my family . Every one that has had it Loves it and its very simple and easy to make . Can't have a Turkey or chicken roaster without it . I hope you Enjoy it .
