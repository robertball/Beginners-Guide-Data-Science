Title:
Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T Olive Oil
1 Small Onion(finely chopped)
2 Cloves Garlic(finely chopped)
1 28oz Can Tomato Puree
2T Fresh Basil(chopped)
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add onions and cook until soft, 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add tomatoes and simmer for about 20 minutes or until thickened. Stir in basil;season with salt and pepper.
