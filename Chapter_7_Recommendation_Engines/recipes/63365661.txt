Title:
Rhubarb Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
250 grams Flour
125 grams Butter
50 grams Sugar
1 Egg
Filling:
1 kg Rhubarb or Apple
3 Eggs
250 grams Sugar
600 mL Creme Fraiche
Vanilla
Sliced Almonds

Directions:
Mix the ingredients for the crust and press into a cheesecake form. Mix the filling ingredients and place into the form. Top with sliced almonds. Bake 50-60 minutes at 170degC. Cool before serving.
