Title:
5-Minute Cheesy Broccoli Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975825.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups frozen broccoli florets
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 pound VELVEETA Pasteurized Prepared Cheese Product, cut up
1 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Combine broccoli, mustard and VELVEETA in large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat.
Cook 5 min. until broccoli is crisp-tender and hot, stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle with the Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Kraft Kitchens
Description: 
It only takes a few added ingredients to transform frozen broccoli into this delectable side dish.
