Title:
Crosby Cowboy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2lb hamburger meat
-3 jalapeños diced
-2 cups cream cheese
-1 cup shredded sharp cheddar
-1 tsp salt
-1 tsp pepper
-1 tsp garlic powder
-2 eggs whisked
-2/3 cup breadcrumbs or crushed crackers
-1 tsp salt
-1 tsp pepper
-1 tbsp parsley
-bbq sauce

Directions:
-Preheat oven to 350
-Mix cream cheese, salt, pepper, jalapeños, and garlic powder
-Mix hb, eggs, salt, pepper, breadcrumbs, and parsley
-Line greased pan with part of burger
-Insert cream cheese mix
-Cover with remaining burger meat
-Bake 40 minutes
-Remove from pan and coat with bbq sauce
