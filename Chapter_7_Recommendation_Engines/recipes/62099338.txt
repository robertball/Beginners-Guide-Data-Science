Title:
Iced coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tray frozen coffee cubes
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cold coffee
4 tsp powdered hot chocolate mix
2 tsp white sugar
3 tsp brown sugar

Directions:
Pour cold coffee, milk and coffee cubes into blender.  Pulse a couple times.  Add the rest of the ingredients and blend. Taste for sweetness.

Submitter: 
babybluangel
Description: 
This is a good way to use up leftover coffee.  I pour some into ice cube trays and the rest goes into the fridge.
