Title:
Mississippi Mud Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2155890.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Duncan Hines® Chocolate Decadence Brownie Mix
2 large eggs
1/3 cup water
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow cream
1 1/4 cups Duncan Hines® Creamy Home-Style Milk Chocolate Frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease bottom of 13x9-inch pan.
Combine brownie mix, eggs, water and oil in large bowl. Stir with spoon until well blended, about 50 strokes. Spread in pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 27 to 30 minutes or until set.
Spread marshmallow cream gently over hot brownies. Place frosting in microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high power for 15 to 30 seconds. Stir until smooth. Pour frosting over marshmallow cream. Swirl with knife to marbleize. Cool completely. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Duncan Hines® Canada
Description: 
By adding a layer of marshmallow cream to these Duncan Hines Chocolate Decadence Brownies, you create a layer of heaven. Swirl in milk chocolate frosting and you deserve a halo for these Mississippi Mud Brownies!
