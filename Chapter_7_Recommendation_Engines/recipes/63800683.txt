Title:
Bacon/Ranch Beer-Can Turkey Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bone-in whole turkey breast
1/4 C. butter, softened
1 packet Ranch Dressing Mix
Six strips of thick-cut bacon
1 Cup beer (not dark beer)

Directions:
Spray the crock of a large slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray
Rinse and pat dry the turkey breast
Mix the butter and 1/2 packet of the Ranch Dressing mix in a small bowl
Carefully, loosen each side of the skin from the breast of the turkey and spread the butter/ranch mixture beneath the skin
Place the turkey inside the prepared slow cooker
Sprinkle the remaining dressing mix evenly over the top of the turkey breast
Lay the bacon strips evenly across the top of the turkey breast
Pour the beer along the side of the breast, not over the top
Cook on high for one hour
Reduce the temperature to low, and cook for 6 to 8 hours

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
This is a delicious and tender turkey breast.  The recipe fuses the traditional flavors of bacon/turkey and ranch seasonings with the moistness of my beer-can chicken.
