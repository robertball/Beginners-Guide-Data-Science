Title:
Pioneer Woman's Onion Strings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg. onion
2 c. buttermilk
2 c. flour
slightly less than 1 T. salt
black pepper - lots
1/4-1/2 t. cayenne pepper
1-2 qts. oil for frying

Directions:
Slice onion very thin.  Place in baking dish and cover with buttermilk for at least one hour.
Combine dry ingredients and set aside.
Heat oil to 375.
Grab a handful of onions, throw into flour mixture, shake off excess, plunge into hot oil.
Fry for a few minutes - remove when golden brown.
