Title:
Pineapple Rocket Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 pineapple, cored, and chopped
1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
4 tbsp cilantro
4 cups washed and torn arugula
dressing:
3 tbsps apple cider vinegar
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp maple syrup
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine greens with pineapple, onions, and cilantro in a large salad bowl.
In a small bowl, add dressing ingredients and stir until mixed.
Add dressing to salad and toss.
