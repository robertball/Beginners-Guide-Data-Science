Title:
Quick Creamy Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980404.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Chicken and Mushroom Soup
1/2 cup milk
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey
3 cups medium egg noodles, cooked and drained
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat the soup, milk, black pepper, cheese, chicken and noodles in a 4-quart saucepan over medium heat until the mixture is hot and bubbling, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with the parsley before serving.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Campbell's® Cream of Chicken and Mushroom Soup provides the base for a wonderful dish featuring chicken, egg noodles, and Parmesan cheese.
