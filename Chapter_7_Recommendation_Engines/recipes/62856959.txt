Title:
Southwestern Three Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/683823.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb wax beans, cut in 1" pieces
1/2 lb green beans, cut in 1" pieces
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 15-oz can black beans, drained & rinsed (or 1 1/4 cups)
1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
1/3 cup chopped cilantro
1/4 cup lime juice
2 tbsp cider vinegar
1 tbsp honey
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp Mexican oregano
1/2 tsp ancho chili powder
1/4 tsp cayenne
1/2 tsp chipotle sauce (ie Tabasco)

Directions:
Steam wax and green beans for about 5 minutes or until tender-crisp.  Rinse under cold water to stop cooking process.
Combine beans, vegetables and cilantro.  Whisk remaining ingredients and pour over salad.  Toss to coat.  Cover and refrigerate at least one hour.
