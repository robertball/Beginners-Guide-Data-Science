Title:
Creamy Triple Caramel Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Duncan Hines Caramel cake mix
4 eggs
1 cup real buttermilk
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 small pkg (4 servings size) French vanilla Jell-o instant pudding mix
3/4 cup Mrs Richardson's caramel ice cream topping
1 tub Duncan Hines Creamy Homestyle Caramel flavored frosting
8 ounce Cool Whip thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Grease and flour 9 x 13 cake pan. In large bowl combine cake mix, eggs, buttermilk, milk, vegetable oil and pudding mix. Beat on high for 2 minutes scraping sides of bowl as needed. Pour into prepared cake pan and spread evenly. Bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until toothpick poked in middle comes out clean. Immediately poke holes in cake using the handle of a wooden spoon. Push handle straight down to bottom of cake each time. Make holes about 1 to 1 1/2 inches apart. Heat the ice cream topping in microwave as directed on jar. Pour into holes until holes are filled. Fold frosting into Cool Whip; chill in frig while cake cools completely. Once cake is cooled spread frosting evenly over cake. Chill in frig at least 2 hours. Store in frig.

Description: 
Moist and creamy caramel flavored cake holds pockets of gooey caramel and topped with a light and  creamy caramel frosting. I created this cake because of my love for caramel and I'm so glad I did! Easy to add your own twist to it.
