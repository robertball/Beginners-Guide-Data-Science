Title:
Turkey Italian Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb turkey Italian sausage
2 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
3 stalks celery, diced
1 large yellow onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
3 medium carrots, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
12oz baby spinach
1 lemon
2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
pinch red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 cup white wine
1 quart low sodium chicken broth
8oz short pasta, cooked (can use any short pasta, penne, rigatoni, shells, etc)

Directions:
Heat oil in heavy stockpot over medium high heat
Brown and crumble Italian sausage in oil
Add onion and celery as soon as meat is browned, cook about 2 minutes
Add bell pepper, carrots and garlic, cook about 2 minutes
Add spices and herbs then wine. Let cook about 2 to 3 minutes until wine is almost cooked away.
Add broth, reduce heat to medium and let cook about 30 minutes covered.
Zest and juice lemon, add to soup.
Add spinach and cook just until wilted.
Add pasta and remove from heat.
Serve with crusty bread.

Submitter: 
Michelle Black Thompson
Description: 
Italian Sausage with veggies and pasta in a lemony chicken broth.
