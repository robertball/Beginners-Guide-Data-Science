Title:
sweet bread cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 pound lard
1/4 pound margarine
1/4 pound sugar
1 and 1/2 pound self raising flour
1/4 pound blanched almonds
1/4 pound raisins (stoned)
1/4 pound sultanas
1/4 pound pine nuts (seed)
1/4 pound candid peel
4 eggs
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 wineglass aniseed (anis)
1 wineglass gin

Directions:
rub margarine and lard into the flour.
add the basking powder, sugar, fruits, almonds and seeds (pine nuts).
Stir in beaten eggs, gin and anis.
Knead and form into 3 loaves.
Cook in a moderate oven for 1 and 1/4 hours.
