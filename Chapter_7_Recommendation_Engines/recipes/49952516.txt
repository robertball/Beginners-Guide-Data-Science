Title:
WHITE CHOCOLATE CAROUSEL CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 		eggs
1 tsp.		vanilla
1-1/4 cups 	flour
1 cup 		sugar
2 tsp. 		baking powder
1/2 tsp. 	salt
1/2 cup 	butter, softened
1/2 cup 	milk
Mousse:
1 pouch 	Knox unflavored gelatin
1/4 cup 	cold water
2 		eggs yolks
3/4 cup 	sugar
1/2 cup 	hot milk
8 oz. 		white chocolate (or squares)
1-1/2 cups 	whipping cream
3 cups 		fresh strawberries

Directions:
CAKE: Preheat oven to 350.  Butter a 9 inch spring form pan.  Beat eggs and vanilla.  Set aside.  In large bowl, mix together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Beat butter into flour mixture for 1 minute.  Beat in eggs; gradually add milk.  Beat until fluffy.  Scrape batter into prepared pan and bake for 30 minutes. Cool in pan.
MOUSSE:In blender, place gelatin and water.  Let stand for 1 minute.  Add egg yolks, sugar and milk; blend well.  Transfer to saucepan and cook, stirring, over medium heat for about 5 minutes or until thick enough to coat back of spoon.  Stir in chocolate until smooth.
Cool to room temperature.
Whip cream until stiff.  Gently fold into chocolate mixture.  Cut berries in half and arrange some in a row, cut side facing out, along side of spring form pan on top of baked cake.  Scatter remaining strawberries over cake.  Spoon mousse on top of berries; spread to cover evenly.  Refrigerate for 2 hours or until mousse is set. Run a knife around edge of pan and remove ring.
