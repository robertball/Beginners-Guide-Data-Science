Title:
Cauliflower Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of cauliflower
2 eggs beaten
2 tablespoons melted butter
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook cauliflower in boiling salted water till tender about 8 minutes. drain well and chop. Mix together egg, butter, salt and pepper and cream. stir into cauliflower. mix in cheese. Pour into a buttered casserole dish. Bake 45 minutes to an hour.
