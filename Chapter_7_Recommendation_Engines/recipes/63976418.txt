Title:
Wacky Cake (from Aunt Inga) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c flour
2 c sugar
1/2 c cocoa
2 tsp soda
2 T vinegar
1/2 c cooking oil
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
2 cups water

Directions:
Sift together dry ingredients twice.  Put in 9 x 13 baking pan.  Make 3 holes in dry ingredients.  Put 2 T vinegar in one hole, 2 tsp vanilla in second hole, and 1/2 c cooking oil in third hold.  Pour 2 c cold water over ingredients and mix together in pan.  Bake at 350F for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
A quick, adaptable, inexpensive eggless, milk-free cake.
