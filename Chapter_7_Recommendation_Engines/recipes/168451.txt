Title:
Steakhouse T-Bone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982075.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1 pound) beef T-bone steaks, 1 inch thick
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/3 cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce

Directions:
Preheat grill to high heat. Add steaks. Grill 7 to 8 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
Meanwhile, cook and stir mushrooms in hot oil in skillet until tender. Stir in steak sauce; cook until heated through, stirring occasionally.
Cut each steak into 3 equal portions. Serve topped with the mushroom mixture.
