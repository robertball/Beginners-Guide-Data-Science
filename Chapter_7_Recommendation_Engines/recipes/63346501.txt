Title:
Shires Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Eggs
1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
2 Teaspoon Pickle Juice
Paprika for Garnish
3 Bacon Strips
1/2 Teaspoon Yellow Mustard
1/2 Teaspoon Dijon Mustard
4 Tablespoons Mayonnaise

Directions:
Hard Boil 6 eggs and peel. Cut lengthwise and remove the yolks. In a bowl combine Worcestershire Sauce, Pickle juice, half the crumbled bacon, mustard, mayonnaise and the egg yolks in a separate bowl and mash with a fork. Add the mixture to the eggs whites and garnish with paprika and the remaining bacon.

Andy Dillon

Yet another spin on deviled eggs.
