Title:
German Chocolate Candy Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box German chocolate cake
1 (14 oz) can of sweetened condensed milk
1 (12 oz) jar of caramel topping
1 (8oz) container of non-dairy whipped topping
4 Heath or Skor candy bars (crushed)

Directions:
Bake cake as per instructions in a 13 x 9 inch pan.  When done baking, while cake is still warm, poke holes into top using the end of a wooden spoon. Pour condensed milk and caramel topping over the cake filling holes. Top with 1/2 of crushed candy mixture.  Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.  Top with whipped topping and sprinkle with remaining candy.

Submitter: 
Leonda
Description: 
A rich, decadent dessert that's easy to make.  It's an instant hit for family gatherings or potlucks.
