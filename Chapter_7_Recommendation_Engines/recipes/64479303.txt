Title:
Gluten Free Blackberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2518514.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups fresh blackberries
1/4 cup coconut sugar
1/8 cup gluten free flour
(combine and place in no stick sprayed 2 qt. square casserole dish)
Cobbler portion:
1 cup gluten free flour (Bob's Red Mill is good)
1/4 cup coconut flour, brown rice flour, millet or buckwheat
1 cup coconut sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon xanthan gum
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1 cup low fat buttermilk
1/4 cup melted butter, ghee, or margarine
1/2 teaspoon gluten free vanilla
dash of nutmeg

Directions:
Mix berries, 1/4 cup coconut sugar and 1/8 cup of gluten free flour and pour into a non-stick sprayed 2 quart square casserole dish, set aside.  Another bowl, mix the GF flours, coconut sugar, baking powder, salt, xanthan gum and nutmeg, stir well, adding the buttermilk and melted butter or ghee and vanilla to mixture.  Spread over the berries and bake in 350 degree oven about 45 minutes until done and golden. Let cool down some before devouring.

Description: 
A healthy twist on an old favorite, a bit tart. We have SO many wild blackberries in our neck of the woods, hence this recipe and lots of jams also this time of year get made.
