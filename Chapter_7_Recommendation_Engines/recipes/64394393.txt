Title:
Chocolate Almond Oat Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients:

1-2 tsp Almond Butter
8-10 oz. Almond Milk
2 Scoops Chocolate Protein
1 tablespoon ground flax seed

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender for your preference.
