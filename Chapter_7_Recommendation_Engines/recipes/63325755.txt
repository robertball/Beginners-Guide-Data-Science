Title:
Sausage & Rice Casserole 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Sausage
½ cup Celery, chopped
1 (4 oz) jar Chopped Pimento
1 (6 oz) box Uncle Ben's Long Grain & Wild Rice
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 cup Cheddar Cheese, grated
½ cup Bell Pepper, chopped
1 small Onion, chopped
½ tsp. Marjoram
1½ cups Hot Water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325°.
Brown sausage and drain.
Add celery, bell pepper & onion.  Cook a few minutes until vegetables are tender.  Remove from heat.
Add pimento, rice (with packet), soup, cheese, marjoram, & hot water.  Mix well.
Pour into 2 quart baking dish & cover with foil.
Bake for 1½ hours.
