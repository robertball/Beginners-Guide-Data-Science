Title:
Blueberry Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/288950.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packs of blueberry JELL-O gelatin dessert
2 cups of milk
3 cups heavy cream
1/2 cup white sugar

Directions:
Go by the instructions on the packs of JELL-O, except do NOT put mix into fridge.
in a large pitcher mix together JELL-O mix, milk, heavy cream, sugar, and salt. Once mixed together pour into plastic container and let it freeze over-night.
