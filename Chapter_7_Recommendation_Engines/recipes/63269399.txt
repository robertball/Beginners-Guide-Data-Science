Title:
E.T. Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. margarine
1 c. peanut butter
2 c. powdered sugar
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. vanilla
1 c. graham cracker crumbs
6 oz. chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips and set aside.
Mix all other ingredients together and pat into a 8x8 pan.
Spread melted chocolate chips on top.
Cool, cut, and serve.
