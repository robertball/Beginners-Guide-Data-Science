Title:
Coconut Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1/2 lb. fine noodles
- 4 eggs
- 3 c. milk
- 1 c. sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 stick butter
- 3/4 c. coconut

Directions:
Cook noodles.
Butter 9 x 13 inch dish.
Mix ingredients well (not butter or coconut), then add noodles.
Pour into dish and pour melted butter on top.
Top with coconut.
Bake at 350 for 1 hr or until set and coconut is nicely browned.
