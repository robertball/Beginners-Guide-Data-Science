Title:
Slow Cooker Barbecue Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843674.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds pork baby back ribs
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups BBQ sauce (Sweet Baby Rays)
1 cup tomato sauce
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
6 tablespoons vinegar
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1 dash cloves
1 dash allspice
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1 dash hot sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Season ribs with salt and pepper. Place in a shallow baking pan. Brown in oven 15 minutes. Turn over, and brown another 15 minutes; drain fat.
In a medium bowl, mix together remaining ingredients. Place ribs in slow cooker. Pour sauce over ribs, and turn to coat.
Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours, or until ribs are tender.

Submitter:
