Title:
Orange Jell-O Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large (6-serving) package Orange Jell-O
8 oz. Cool Whip
8 oz. Sour Cream
1 small can Mandarin Oranges, drained

Directions:
Place Cool Whip and sour cream in a bowl.  Mix together well.
Add orange Jell-O. Mix together well.
Fold in drained Mandarin oranges.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.  Refrigerate any leftovers.

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
This is great as a side dish or dessert.  We usually make it with our holiday meals, though it also makes a great summertime treat
