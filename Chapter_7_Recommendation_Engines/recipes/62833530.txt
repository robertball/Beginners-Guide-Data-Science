Title:
Blackberry Jell-O Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boxes Blackberry Jell-O
3 cups boiling water
1 can crushed pineapple with juice
1 can blueberry pie filling
1/2 pint sour cream
8oz cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 small container cool whip

Directions:
Dissolve the Jell-O in the boiling water and add the pineapple with juice and the blueberry pie filling.  Place in a 9x13 pan and let it set up in the refrigerator.
While it is chilling, beat the cream cheese and sugar until fluffy.  Beat in the sour cream and the vanilla.  Fold in the cool whip and spread onto the set Jell-O salad.  Chill for about an hour and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a delicious combination of summer flavors and a creamy topping.  Always a big hit at family picnics!
