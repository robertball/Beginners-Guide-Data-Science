Title:
Mama Burtner's Red Earth Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c unsalted butter
1 1/2 c sugar
2 eggs
4 tsp cocoa
1 tsp re cake coloring
1 c butter milk
2 Tbsp hot coffee
2 c flour
1/2 tsp salat
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
Frosting:
2 c sugar
2 tsp cocoa
2 Tbsp Karo
1 c heavy cream
2 tsp vanilla
3 tsp unsalted butter
Filling:
1 c toasted chopped pecans
6 tsp heavy cream
2-2 1/2 c powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare 2 layer cake pans. For the cake mix all wet & dry ingredients in separate bowls & then combine them together. Bake for 20 min or until cake bounces back when touched. Toast pecans in 2 Tbsp butter on the stove. While cake is baking prepare frosting. Cook 1st four frosting ingredients to the softball stage (use a candy thermometer). Add a large lump of unsalted butter & 2 tsp vanilla. Let cook then beat till it's a spreading consistency.
Let cake cool completely & place on a serving platter. Top the 1st layer with the pecan filling. Top with 2nd layer of cake & then frost. For a finishing touch arrange whole pecans on top or sprinkle with chopped pecans.
