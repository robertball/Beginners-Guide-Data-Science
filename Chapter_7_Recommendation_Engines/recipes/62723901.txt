Title:
Eggplant Casserole #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant, about 1 1/4 #
6 slices bacon
1/2 # mushrooms, sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 T brown sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
1 1/2 to 2 C shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/3 C oil
Lg. onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
1 # fresh or canned tomatoes, cut into chunks

Directions:
Wash and peel eggplant. Cut into 1/2 inch slices and brush all sides with oil. Arrange in single layer on baking sheet; bake uncovered at 450 degrees for about 10 min or until soft.
Cook bacon in lg. skillet until crisp; drain and crumble. Discard all but 2 T of drippings. Add onion, green pepper, mushrooms and garlic. Saute until limp. Stir in tomatoes and their liquid, sugar, salt and pepper. Cook over med high heat, uncovered, until most of liquid has evaporated. Layer eggplant, bacon, and sauce in shallow 2-qt casserole. Top w/cheese and bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees until bubbly, about 15 min.
