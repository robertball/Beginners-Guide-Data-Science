Title:
Spinach and Mandarin orange salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoons water
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
1 egg
Salad:
10-16 oz fresh baby spinach, washed and dried
6 oz sliced fresh mushrooms
15 oz canned mandarin oranges, drained
1/3 cup toasted slivered almonds
4 slices crispy bacon, crumbled
Red onion rings (optional)
Croutons (optional)

Directions:
Mix first five salad dressing ingredients together in a small saucepan.  Heat until all sugar disolves and mixture boils for 1 minute.  Cool for approximately 1 hour at room temperature.  With wire whisk, add 1 well-beaten egg to cooled dressing. Return mixture to low heat and slowly cook until dressing thickens.
Mix all of the salad ingredients together and top with warm salad dressing.  Serve soon after dressing salad for a crisp spinach salad.

Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe that is sure to impress guests.  It's not your ordinary spinach salad.
