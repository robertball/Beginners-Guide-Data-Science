Title:
Green Salad Plus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1010945.jpg
Ingredients:
Salad:
6 cups mixed baby greens
2 medium pears, sliced
1 large bulb fennel, chopped or sliced
1/3 cup toasted walnuts
Dressing:
5 tablespoons olive oil
3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup orange juice
2 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
Berries with Yogurt:
1 cup blueberries
1 cup strawberries
1 cup raspberries
1 cup fat-free yogurt, lemon or vanilla flavored

Directions:
Combine salad ingredients. Whisk together dressing and add to salad. Toss.
Combine berries. Serve with a dollop of yogurt.

Submitter: 
USA WEEKEND columnist Jean Carper
Description: 
This meal for six is hearty and nutritious. Eat one serving of each dish and you'll get incalculable amounts of antioxidants.
