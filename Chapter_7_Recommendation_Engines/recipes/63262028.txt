Title:
Kahlua Pumpkin Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c flour
1/4c powdered sugar
1/2c cold butter
1c pumpkin
1 8oz cream cheese, softened
2 eggs
1/4c sugar
1/4c Kahlua
1c walnuts, chopped
3/4c brown sugar
1/4c butter, melted

Directions:
Cut butter into flour and powdered sugar, press into bottom of 8" square pan. Bake 350* for 15 min.
Process pumpkin, cream cheese, egg, sugar and Kahlua until smooth. Pour pumpkin mixture over warm crust and return to oven for 20 min.
Cool, cover and refrigerate.
Mic nuts, brown sugar and melted butter and sprinkle over pumpkin filling.
