Title:
Strawberry Mini Paninis with NUTELLA速 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044635.jpg
Ingredients:
8 whole grain mini Panini buns or firm dinner rolls, halved
1/4 cup NUTELLA速
2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
1/4 cup low-fat ricotta cheese
4 teaspoons butter, softened, or non-hydrogenated margarine

Directions:
Preheat Panini press to high.
Spread cut side of Panini bottoms with NUTELLA(R). Top with strawberries and ricotta cheese. Replace Panini tops and butter the outside. Place buns on Panini press and grill for 5 minutes or until warm and cheese has started to melt.
