Title:
Paleo Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2377007.jpg
Ingredients:
-3 eggs, whisked
-1/2 can pumpkin
-1 small sweet potato
-1/2 cup honey
-1/4 cup coconut oil
-1/2 cup dairy free chocolate chips
-1/4 cup almond flour
-1/2 tsp baking soda
-1/2 tsp baking powder
-3 tbs cocoa powder
-1/4 tsp cinnamon
-1/2 tsp pure vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cook sweet potato in oven till tender. And mash till smooth.
Melt coconut oil and combine with pumpkin, egg, honey and vanilla. Add sweet potato and mix till smooth.
In a separate bowl mix dry ingredients together, then add to the wet mixture.
Fold in chocolate chips
Pour into a 9x9 baking dish and spray with non stick spray.
Bake for 50-60 minutes, checking half way through. It will be ready when toothpick comes out clean!
