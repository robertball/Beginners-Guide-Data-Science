Title:
Picnic Bento Box Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4535364.jpg
Ingredients:
Deli roll-ups:
1 Tbsp mayonnaise
1/2 tsp mustard
1/4 tsp sriracha
1  large flour tortilla
1/2 C baby spinach leaves
2 slices muenster cheese, torn into strips
4 very thin slices tomato
4 slices honey ham
1 Tbsp sliced pepperoncini (optional)
The Best Potato Salad (http://allrecipes.com/recipe/169084/the-best-potato-salad/)**Ingredients below are for a full batch as per site**:
6 eggs
10 red potatoes
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1/3 cup dill pickle relish
2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon celery seed
1 onion, chopped
1/4 cup pepperoncini (optional)
1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
1/2 C fresh cherries
1 graham cracker, broken into rectangles
2 snack-size milk chocolate bars
1/4 C mini marshmallows

Directions:
For wraps:
1. Mix together mayonnaise, mustard, and sriracha.  Lay tortilla on a flat surface and spread with mayonnaise mixture all the way to edges.
2. Top tortilla evenly with spinach, cheese, tomato, ham, and pepperoncini all the way to edges of tortilla.  Roll tortilla tightly and place seam-side down on cutting board.  Slice into 1 1/2" sections.  Transfer to bento box.
For potato salad:
3. Follow directions for The Best Potato Salad (http://allrecipes.com/recipe/169084/the-best-potato-salad/).  Place 1/2 C of potato salad in bento box.
For cherries:
4. Place cherries in bento box.
For s'mores:
5. Place graham cracker rectangles, snack-size milk chocolate bars, and mini marshmallows together in bento box.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
Whether you're packing a picnic lunch of bringing the flavors of summer to school with you, this playful box is full of fun and flavor too!  Deli roll-ups, potato salad, fresh cherries, and s'mores for dessert!
