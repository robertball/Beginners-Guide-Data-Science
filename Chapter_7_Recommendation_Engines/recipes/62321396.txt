Title:
Jim's Tapioca Pudding slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/378644.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C Whole milk
1 C Half and Half ( or cream)
3/4 C sugar
2 Eggs whisked
1/2 C Tapioca pearls

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in your slow cooker and whisk. Cover and cook for 4 hours on low.
