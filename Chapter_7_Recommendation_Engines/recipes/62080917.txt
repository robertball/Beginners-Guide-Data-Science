Title:
AMAZING AND EASY BEEF STROGANOFF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 pound of hamburger
1 8-ounce package cream cheese
1 15-ounce can peas OR carrots
1 cup of milk
1 10-ounce package of noodles
Salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Cook the noodles per package directions until al dente. Season hamburger with salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste and brown the meat. Drain the meat if necessary, then add brick of cream cheese, can of mushroom soup, and milk and simmer on low heat until all is thick, creamy and well-blended. Stir this mixture into drained noodles. Then fold in peas OR carrots. Serve warm with dinner rolls or crusty bread.
