Title:
Yummy Honey Ham and Swiss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991075.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices toasted country bread
2 leaves butter lettuce
2 slices lean skinless ham
1 slice Swiss cheese
1 teaspoon honey mustard
1 tablespoon Hellmann's® 1/2 the Fat mayonnaise-type dressing

Directions:
Spread honey mustard on one slice of bread and spread mayonnaise on other slice. Layer other ingredients between both slices of bread and serve.

Submitter: 
Hellmann's®
Description: 
The classic ham and Swiss gets a sweet boost with honey mustard and butter lettuce.
