Title:
Pumpkin Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C roasted pumpkin
1 C apple juice
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground allspice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together in a food processor until smooth
Pour into warm medium sauce pan over low heat
Cook uncovered and stir occassionally until thickened to your preference (30-90 minutes depending on the wetness of your apples or pumpkin
