Title:
Cranberry Poppy Seed Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/231160.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup whole berry cranberry sauce
1 teaspoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Whisk together mayonnaise and cranberry sauce in a large bowl.  Stir in the poppy seeds. Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
tahoegirl
Description: 
This spread is delicious on turkey sandwiches or as a dressing for chicken salads.
