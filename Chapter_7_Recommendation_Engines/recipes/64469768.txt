Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2470189.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 tbsp. cream cheese
1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
3 strips turkey bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a medium sized bowl, beat eggs until fluffy.  Soften cream cheese in microwave and add to eggs.  Incorporate shredded cheese.
Line a muffin tin with 1/2 strip of turkey bacon, and fill to just below the top with egg mixture.  Bake 18-20 minutes, or until eggs have puffed and look golden.
