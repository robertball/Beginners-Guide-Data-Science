Title:
Salt & Burn Loves Passionfruit's Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2443673.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag mini marshmallows
3/4 cup peanut butter
1 bag semi-sweet chocolate chips
7 tbs butter
1 stalk kale, chopped finely

Directions:
For easy clean-up, line a 8x8 pan with foil and spray with non-stick spray.
Put the marshmallows in a bowl and set aside.
combine the rest of the ingredients, except the kale, in a pot and melt over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally. When melted, remove from heat and let sit 5 minutes, still stirring a few times.
When mixture is cool, combine with the marshmallows, folding it in until all marshmallows are well covered.
Pour into the 8x8 pan.
Top with chopped kale.
Refrigerate for 2 hours or until cool.
Cut into squares and enjoy.
