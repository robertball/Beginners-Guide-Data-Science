Title:
Sandy's Sweet Heat Asian Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. brown sugar
1/8 c. soy sauce
1 jalapeno diced
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp lime juice
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
1/2 tsp sesame oil
1/2  tsp fish sauce
Couple dashes of red pepper flakes

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a small sauce pan.
Bring to a boil over medium heat.
Reduce to heat and simmer 15-20 minutes.
Toss fried wings in sauce and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sandy Schoon
Description: 
A great alternative to teriyaki wings-with a kick!
