Title:
German Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 tablespoons flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
6 medium potatoes, peeled and shredded
1/2 cup diced onion
vegetable oil

Directions:
In large bowl, beat eggs, flour, baking powder, salt and pepper.  Mix in potatoes and onion.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  In batches, drop heaping tablespoonfuls of the potato mixture into the skillet.  Press to flatten, cook 3-5 minutes per side until crisp.  Drain on paper towels.  Serve with sour cream and apple sauce.
