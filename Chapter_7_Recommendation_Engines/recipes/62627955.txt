Title:
Shallot Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/591698.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp. extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp. red wine vinegar
1 tsp. chopped fresh or 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1 tbsp. minced shallots
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Whisk all the ingredients together until blended.

Submitter: 
moaa
Description: 
Shallots can be very strong for your palate but this vinaigrette has a depth of flavor. I love to drizzle it over cherry tomatoes!
