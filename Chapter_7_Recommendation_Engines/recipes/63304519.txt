Title:
Best Baked Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, slightly beaten
1/3 cup sugar
dash salt
1 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 cups milk, scalded
Nutmeg

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Blend eggs, sugar, salt, and vanilla.  Gradually stir in milk.  Pour into 6 oz. custard cups.  Sprinkle with a little nutmeg.  Place cups in a baking pan, 13x9x3; pour VERY hot water into pan to within 1/2 inch of tops of cups.  Bake about 45 min. or until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean.  Remove cups from water.  Serve warm or chilled.
DON'T OVERCOOK! Your custard will curdle.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
My mom used to make this custard when I was little and I always love it.  It's simple but so good!
