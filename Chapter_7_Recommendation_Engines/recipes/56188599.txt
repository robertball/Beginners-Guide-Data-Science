Title:
Mama's Tomato Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup bacon drippings
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups water
1 can stewed tomatoes
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat bacon drippings in a skillet over medium-high heat. Stir the flour into the bacon drippings and cook, stirring constantly, until lightly browned. Slowly pour the water into the flour mixture while whisking. Stir in the tomatoes. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture  begins to thicken. Reduce heat to low and simmer about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
peggy
Description: 
Serve over buttermilk biscuits for a real Southern treat.  When our family would gather at our Mississippi home, this was a weekend breakfast staple.Modified from: Mama's Tomato Gravy
