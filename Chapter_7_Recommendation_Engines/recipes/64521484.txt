Title:
Vegan Thai stirfry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz uncooked whole wheat linguine
1/3 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
1/4 cup lime juice
3 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tbsp Thai chili sauce
2 tbsp peanut oil, divided
1/2lb firm or extra firm tofu
1 cup fresh snow peas
1 medium sliced sweet red pepper
4 garlic cloves minced
1/3 cup chopped, unsalted peanuts

Directions:
- Cook linguine according to package directions.
- Mix soy sauce, lime juice, brown sugar, vinegar and chili sauce.
- In a skillet, heat 1 tbsp oil over medium-high heat. Add Tofu if you prefer it fried When done remove Tofu from pan. Add snow peas and red pepper to pan and cook until crisp, add garlic and cook for 1 more minnute. Stir
-Drain linguine and add to vegetable mixture.  Stir soy sauce mixture and add to pan. Bring to a boil and add Tofu. Sprinkle with Peanuts
-

Submitter:

Description: 
Original recipe called for chicken and eggs, but I simply substituted the chicken for Tofu and scrapped the eggs.
