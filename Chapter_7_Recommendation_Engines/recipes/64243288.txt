Title:
Double Delicious cookie Bars     2/3 recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Make Sweetened Condensed Milk
2/3 cup Powered Milk
1/4 cup boiling water
1/2 cup white sugar
2 Tbsp butter
Melt 1/3 cup butter in 10" pan, sprinkle 1  cup graham cracker crumbs evenly over butter. pour on condensed milk evenly, Top with 1 1/3 cups chocolate chips, 2/3 cup peanut butter chips or butterscotch chip, press down firmly. Bake 20-25 mins
