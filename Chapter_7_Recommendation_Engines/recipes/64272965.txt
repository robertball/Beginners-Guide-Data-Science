Title:
Mom's Russian Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1879829.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups of water
1 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 small can orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 cup sugar, dissolved in 1 cup boiling water*
teaball with 6-8 whole cloves
2 cinnamon sticks or 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
4 Family size Lipton or Luzianne black tea bags

Directions:
Bring to rolling boil 8 cups of water in large stock pot
Boil a cup of water in microwave or stove top and dump 1 cup sugar in boiling water, stir well and place in water mixture in stock pot
Add 1/2 cup lemon juice, 1 cup pineapple juice an thawed o.j. concentrate. Stir mixture.
Place 6-8 whole cloves in teaball and place in pot.
Insert 2 cinnamon sticks or ground cinnamon in pot.
Do NOT boil, but cook over medium heat for 10 minutes and turn heat down to very low and let simmer for 15-20 minutes. Enjoy.
*Adjust sugar or sweetener to your liking. Our family prefers regular white sugar.

Description: 
Nothing powdered or instant here. Just pure natural flavors to sip on a cold winter day. Smells great while it's brewing too. Vitamin C never tasted so good !
