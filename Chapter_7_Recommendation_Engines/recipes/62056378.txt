Title:
Mushroom Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 uncooked pie crust
1 pound browned ground beef
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mayonaise
1/2 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten
1 tbs cornstarch
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 small cans mushrooms

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour into unbaked crust in 9-inch pie pan. Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
MRSMTAYLOR
Description: 
This is an easy quiche. Delicious for a no-fuss dinner. I also make an alternative version with green chiles and olives.
