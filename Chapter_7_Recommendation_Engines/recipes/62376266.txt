Title:
Grilled Salmon I - modified Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds salmon fillets
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
1/3 cup low-sodium soy sauce
1/3 cup dark brown sugar
1/3 cup pineapple juice
1/3 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together garlic powder, soy sauce, brown sugar, pineapple juice, and vegetable oil until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag with the soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Preheat grill for medium heat.
Lightly oil grill grate. Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade. Cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
