Title:
Hawaiian Potato and Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110600.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large red and/or gold potatoes, cooked til tender, peeled and cut into cubes
3 Cups cooked shell or elbow macaroni, cooked til tender
1/4 C chopped red onion, finely chopped
3 green onions, chopped
3 hard cooked eggs, chopped
1 cup frozen peas, defrosted
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
2 carrots, grated
2 Tbsp sweet pickle relish
2 Cups mayonnaise (I prefer Helman's)
2 Tbsp cider vinegar
2 Tsp granulated garlic powder
1 Tbsp sugar
Sea salt and pepper to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
In large bowl, toss together potatoes and next 8 ingredients. Lightly salt & pepper; set aside
In small bowl, mix mayo, vinegar, sugar, garlic powder, salt and pepper.  Lightly toss with potato/mac mixture.
Refrigerate over night, or for a minimum of 4 hours.  Taste, and add more mayonnaise or seasoning as needed.

Submitter: 
empebi
Description: 
First tasted this at a restaurant in Kauai.  It is often included in box lunches.  I've taken a couple of online recipes and combined and or added things to make it like we remembered in Hawaii.
