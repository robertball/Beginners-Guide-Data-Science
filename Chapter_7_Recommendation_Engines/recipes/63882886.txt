Title:
Strawberry White Chocolate Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two 6oz packages white chocolate*
1 1/2 cups heavy or whipping cream, divided
One 3-oz package of cream cheese, softened
36 ladyfingers, split. (3 3-oz packs)
1 1/4 cups cooled espresso or strong coffee
2 tbsp brandy(optional)
2 pints strawberries, divided.

Directions:
Melt white chocolate with 1/4 cup cream over hot (not boiling) water, stirring until smooth. Cool completely.
In a large mixer bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Stir in melted chocolate mixture.
In small mixer bowl, beat remaining 1 1/4 cup cream until stiff peaks form. Fold into cream cheese mixture.
Line side of 9x3" springform pan with ladyfinger halves, cut sides in. Arrange half of remaining ladyfingers on bottom of pan.
In small bowl, combine espresso/coffee and brandy(if used)and brush half over ladyfingers in bottom of pan.
Cover ladyfingers in the bottom with half of the cream cheese filling. Slice 1 pint of strawberries arrange over filling.
Repeat ladyfingers, espresso mix and filling layers. Halve remaining 1 pint of strawberries and arrange decoratively on top. Cover and refrigerate until set, 4 hours or overnight. Remove side of pan before serving.
