Title:
jagic refreshing cream cheese and yogurt spread assyrian style! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package cream cheese
8 ounces plain yogurt
8 ounces cottage cheese
2 green onions sliced thinly
1/2 cup fresh dill chopped
1/2 cup cilantro chopped
1/2 cup fresh basil chopped
1 jalapeno seeded and diced
salt to taste

Directions:
.combine yogurt,cottage cheese and creamcheese together till mixed well .Add herbs onions jalapeno and salt mix well until combined you can serve room temp. or chilled.serve with pita chips, pita bread or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
sharon younan
Description: 
jagic is a dish that my mother has been making for year's and that has been passed on for decades! it is very refreshing to the sences and has fresh herbs like dill and basil. This dish is a great appetizer your party will love this guaranteed enjoy!Modified from: jagic refreshing cheese and yogurt spread assyrian style!
