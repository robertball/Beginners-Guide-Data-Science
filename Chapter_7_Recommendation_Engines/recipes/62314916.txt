Title:
Wonderland Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter, softened
1 c sugar
2 eggs
2 T cream
1 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp soda

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, cream and vanilla.  Sift together dry ingredients, add to creamed mixture. Mix until dough forms. Chill dough 4 to 5 hours or overnight.  Roll out dough to 1/8" inch and cut as desired (may use cookie cutters).  Bake for 6 to 8 minutes at 375F to 400F on an ungreased cookie sheet.

A soft sugar cookie.
