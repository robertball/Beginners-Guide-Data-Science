Title:
Catch-A-Man Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups crumbled cooked bacon
1 lb. extra sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 bunch green onions finely chopped
2 cups mayonnaise
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 cup toasted slivered almonds
Strawberry Jam
Crackers or French  bread

Directions:
Combine bacon, cheese, green onions, mayonnaise and cayenne pepper in a medium bowl
Place almonds in the bottom of a lightly oiled ring mold. Press cheese mixture into mold. Refrigerate overnight. Unmold cheese ring onto a serving dish. Place a small cup full of jam in the center of the mold. Serve with crackers or French bread. Place cheese on cracker first followed by a bit of jam..
