Title:
Mom's Famous Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754004.jpg
Ingredients:
- Lasagna Noddles
- Ground Beef
- 2 cans Tomato Sauce
- Cottage Cheese
- Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
- Dried Oregano & Parsley

Directions:
In a skillet cook ground beef thoroughly, drain excess fat then add the tomato sauce! (Season with Oregano & Parsley) About 1 Tbsp
Cook noddles, once noddles are cooked drain and place in a buttered baking pan.
1st Layer: Pasta & Meat Sauce, 2nd Layer: Pasta, Cottage Cheese & Mozzarella, 3rd Layer: Pasta, Meat Sauce & Mozzarella, 4th Layer: Pasta, Cottage Cheese & Mozzarella, Top Layer: Add any extra sauce on top and Top with lots of Mozzarella Cheese!
Cover with foil paper, place in oven 350 degrees for about 45 min, until cheeses are melted and all settled down! SERVE & ENJOY!
