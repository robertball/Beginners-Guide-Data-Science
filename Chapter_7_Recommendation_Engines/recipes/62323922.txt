Title:
Mom's Potato Pie - Two crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/380159.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Medium potatoes
Salt and Pepper to taste
Butter (margerine can be substituted)
Milk
Cheese - American, Muenster, Harvarty, or Brie can
be used, or a combination thereof
Pastry Crust for a 9 inch pie (your favorite or a
box of pie crust mix can be used.

Directions:
Peel and boil your potatoes until tender;
Mash thoroughly and add
4-5 tablespoons of butter
Milk - enough to make the potato mixture a medium consistency
Salt and Pepper to taste
Add 1/2-3/4 Cup of cheese - any of the above or a mixture
Add mixture to pie plate; add top crust and crimp edges; Make vent holes in top of crust and brush with milk and melted butter mixture.
I place aluminum foil stips around edges of pie so that they do not burn; remove foil and bake last 20 minutes without.
Bake at 375-400 degree oven (depending on how "hot" your oven runs for about 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sandra G
Description: 
I grew up in a Catholic family, when Catholics could not eat meat on Fridays. Fish and a side dish were Friday meals. One particularly favorite side-dish was this recipe. Hearty, filling and delicious!
