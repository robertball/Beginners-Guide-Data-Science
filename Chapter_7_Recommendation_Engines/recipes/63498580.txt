Title:
Mediterranean Mini Burgers with  Rich Onion Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BURGERS
750g/1½lb Mixta - 70% minced Beef to 30% minced Pork
1 clove Garlic - finely chopped
1 Spring Onion - finely chopped
4tbsp Basil - finely chopped
2tbsp flat leaf Parsley
Liberal splashes Worcestershire Sauce - to taste
1 Egg Yolk - lightly beaten
45g/1½oz Butter
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
ONION GRAVY
1 large Onion - sliced
60g/2oz Butter
1 clove Garlic - lightly crushed - but still whole
2 to 3 sprigs fresh Thyme
3tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
200ml/7fl oz Red Wine
200ml/7f oz Beef Stock
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
BURGERS
Apart from Butter, mix all ingredients together, season to taste and form into small balls;
Flatten the balls, shape into mini - burgers about 1½in/4cm in diameter and place on baking tray;
Put tray in fridge and chill burgers for 30 minutes to an hour or so;
Melt butter in frying pan, add the burgers and cook on medium heat;
When sufficiently browned on one side, turn over and cook steadily until meat just cooked through with juices running freely and clearly;
Serve.
ONION GRAVY
Heat half(1oz) butter in a frying pan on a low heat, add the onion and cook for some 30 minutes or so, stirring occasionally, or until the onion is golden-brown;
Season with pepper and add the garlic and thyme;
Continue cooking for a further minute or so, stirring occasionally;
Pour in the balsamic and stir until it has evaporated to syrup;
Pour in the wine, increase heat to high, and cook until it has reduced by half to two thirds;
Pour in the stock and bring to the boil;
Turn down the heat and simmer until reduced to consistency required;
Remove the garlic and thyme;
Season to taste and whisk in the butter;
If available,add any juices from the meat.
