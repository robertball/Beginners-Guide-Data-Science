Title:
White Almond Sour Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/888531.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boxes white or yellow cake mix with pudding in the mix
2 cups all purpose flour
2 cups granulated sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
8 egg whites or 1 1/3 cup egg substitute
2 2/3 cups water
4 tbsp vegetable oil or 4 tbsp applesauce
2 cups sour cream
2 tbsp clear vanilla flavoring
2 tsp almond extract
1 small box instant pudding mix

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients by hand using whisk in very large mixing bowl
Add rest of ingredients and beat on low speed for 2 minutes
Bake at 325 until done
