Title:
Artichoke Dip V Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4 oz crean cheese at room temp
2  12oz cans artichoke hearts in water, drained and chopped
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon bay seasoning or more for spicier flavor
1/2 tspn white pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, thoroughly mix the mayonnaise, Parmesan, artichoke, mozzarella, bay seasoning, pepper and garlic. Transfer the mixture to an 8x8 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with mozzarella & parmesan.
Bake 25 minutes, or until the surface is lightly browned and bubbly.
Serve warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick and easy hot dip that goes well with crackers or french bread.Modified from: Artichoke Dip I
