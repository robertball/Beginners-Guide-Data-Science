Title:
Flour Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677716.jpg
Ingredients:
4 C white flour
1 1/2 C water
1/2 C lard or shortening
1 1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix flour and salt; cut in lard.  Add enough water to make a pliable dough.
Kneed the dough until it is soft.  It should stretch.
Grease hands and form dough into small balls about the size of an egg.
Cover and let stand 1/2 hour.
Roll or pat dough paper thin, about 12" across.  Bake in a med-hot skillet or griddle.  When they start to bubble, flip them and bake a few seconds longer.
