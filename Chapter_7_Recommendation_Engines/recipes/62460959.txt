Title:
Husband's Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
1 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon of sugar
1 teaspoon of pepper
2 small cans of tomato sauce
1 small onion
3 ounces of cream cheese
1/2 pint of sour cream
8 ounces thin egg noodles, cooked
4 ounces cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Brown and drain ground beef.  Add salt, sugar, pepper and tomato sauce.  Simmer 15 minutes.  Cream together onion, cream cheese, and sour cream.  Using 9x13 inch baking dish layer noodles, meat mixer and sour cream mixture.  Repeat.  Top with cheddar cheese.  Bake.  Temperature 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Nannette Turner
Description: 
Easy and delish.  Not only will your husband love it but it is a smash for the entire family and friends.  So good you'll be making this again and again!
