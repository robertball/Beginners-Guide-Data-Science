Title:
Kahlua Mintslide Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989554.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces Kahlua
1/2 fluid ounce Hiram Walker White Creme de Cacao
1/2 fluid ounce Hiram Walker Green Creme de Menthe
1/2 fluid ounce milk

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a blender, add ice, and blend until smooth. Serve in a highball glass garnished with a sprig of fresh mint

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
Kick your mudslide cocktail to a new level with Kahlua and chocolate and mint liqueurs.
