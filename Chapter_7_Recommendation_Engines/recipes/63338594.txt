Title:
Potato Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. olive oil
2 red onions, peeled and sliced
4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1 cup sliced leeks, white and pale green parts only
4 Idaho potatoes, peeled and shredded on box grater - about 2 1/2 lbs.
Salt and pepper
1 tbsp. butter, melted
1/2 cup bread crumbs
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups heavy cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Rinse shredded potatoes in strainer until water runs clear, about 2 minutes.
Heat olive oil in large pan.
Add onions, garlic and leeks, and saute until onions are translucent - about 10 minutes.
Brush 9x13 baking dish with melted butter.
Sprinkle half of bread crumbs on bottom and sides of dish.
Alternate layers of potato and onion mixture, ending with potatoes.
Sprinkle cheese and remaining bread crumbs on top.
Pour half of the cream over the potatoes.
Bake for 10 minutes then pour remaining cream over the top.
Cook until potatoes are tender and top is golden brown, about 35 minutes.
