Title:
Jan's Dark Chocolate Swirl Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1374090.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups cake flour
3 stick butter, unsalted (softened at room temp)
8 oz cream cheese, room temperature
3 cups sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 tsp almond extract
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
6 large eggs room temperature
1/3 cup cocoa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Grease a 10 inch tube pan. (see note about using parchment paper)
Beat butter and cream cheese until well combined.
Add in sugar and beat until fluffy.  Approximately 3-4 minutes.
Mix in salt, baking powder and extracts.
Add 2 eggs and blend until combined.
Add 1 cup flour and mix until combined.
Add 2 more eggs and blend until combined.
Add 1 cup flour and blend until combined.
Add the last 2 eggs and completely combine.
Add remaining 1 cup of flour and mix until well combined.
Remove 2 cups of batter and place in separate mixing bowl.
Add in cocoa powder and blend thoroughly.
Grease and flour a large 10 inch tube pan.
Add 1/2 of the plain batter to the pan.
Add 1/2 of the chocolate batter on top and swirl with a knife.
Repeat with the remaining plain batter and chocolate batter swirling together being careful not to over mix the bottom layer.
Place pan on middle rack in the oven.
Bake for 90 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
