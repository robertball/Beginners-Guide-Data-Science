Title:
Classic Country Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1109919.jpg
Ingredients:
50 oz can cut green beans, undrained
2 oz piece fatback
2 cubes chicken bouillon
1 1/2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp seasoned salt
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Empty can of green beans into large saucepan.  Add enough water to cover to just cover beans.  Place remaining ingredients to pan and stir to combine.  Bring liquid to a boil and allow to boil for 10 minutes; reduce heat to low and cook for an additional 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
I love green beans in any form, but this is my family's absolute favorite way to eat them- there are never any leftovers!
