Title:
Marshmellow Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of carmel candy (About 18)
1/2 can of Eagle Brand Condensed Milk (14 oz)
1/2 cube of butter
1 bag of large marshmellows
1 box of rice krispies

Directions:
Melt caramel and milk in double boiler
Dip Marshmellows in caramel mix
Roll in rice krispies and cool on wax paper

Submitter: 
Tracy Kathleen
Description: 
Simple and delicious.It doesn't get any better than caramel rolled marshmellows with a rice krispy coating.
