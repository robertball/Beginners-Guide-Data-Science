Title:
Cream Cheese-Filled Peach Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (29oz) can of sliced peaches
2 TBS cornstarch
1/4 cup orange juice
2 TBS lemon juice
2 TBS margerine
1 cup sifted flour
1 TBS sugar
1/8 tsp salt
2 eggs lightly beaten
1-1/2 cups of milk
Cream Cheese Filling
2(3oz) packages of cream cheese, softened
3 TBS sugar
2 TBS milk
1/2 tsp grated orange peel
1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
For sauce, drain peaches, reserving juice.  In a large skillet over medium heat gradually blend cornstarch into reserved peach juice.  Add orange juice, lemon juice, and margerine, stirring constantly 10 minutes until thickened and bubbly.  Reduce heat to low to keep warm.
For crepes, combine flour, 1 TBS of the sugar, salt, eggs and 1-1/2 cups of milk in large bowl until mixture is smooth.  Pour 1/4 cup batter into lightly greased 6" skillet or crepe pan over medium heat, rotating pan so batter covers bottom evenly.  Cook 3 minutes.  Remove by inverting pan, loosening edges with spatula if necessary; place crepes on paper towels.  Repeat with remaining batter, lightly greasing skilllet occasionaly.
For filling, beat together cream cheese, remaining 3 TBS sugar, remaining 2 TBS milk, orange peel and vanilla.  Spread unbrowned side of crepes with 2 TBS filling each; fold in quarters.  Place crepe on serving plate and top evenly with sauce.
