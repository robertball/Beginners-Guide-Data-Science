Title:
Quick Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (about 4oz each)
1/2 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
1 oz fresh parmesan cheese, grated ( about 1/4 cup)
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
1 egg
1 tbls pluss 2 tsp olive oil, divided
1 garlic clove, pressed
1/3 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 can (14 1/2 oz) italian- seasoned dried tomatoes, undrained
1/3 cup pitted rip olives, cut in half

Directions:
1. Place each chicken breast between 2 pieces of parchment paper. Flatten chicken to at least 1/4 inch thickness by alternately pounding and rolling with dough and pizza roller
2. In mixing bowl, combine bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, salt and black pepper. In smaller bowl lightly beat egg with wisk. Dip chicken breasts in egg; coat with bread crumb mixture. Sprinkle with any remaining bread crumbs; set aside.
3. Heat 1 tlbs oil in skillet until hot. Add Chicken breats; cook 3-5 min per side or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear. Remove chicken breasts from skillet; keep warm. * Keep chicken warm in oven set on warm setting. To keep coating crisp, do not cover.
4.Heat remaining 2 tss oil in Skillet. Press garlic into skillet. Add bell pepper; cook and stir until pepper is crisp-tender, about 2 min. Add tomatoes and olives; stir. Simmer 5 min. Serve tomato sauce over chicken.
