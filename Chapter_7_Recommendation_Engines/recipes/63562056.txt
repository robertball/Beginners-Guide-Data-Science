Title:
Italian Dressing Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of sweet corn
2 cans of black eyed peas
2 cans of black beans
4-6 red, yellow, orange, green peppers chopped
4-6 serrano peppers
1 large bunch of cilantro
1 bottle of zesty Italian dressing
4-6 Roma tomatoes

Directions:
Chop cilantro and peppers into small pieces.  Soak all veggies and beans in Italian dressing overnight then strain. Cut up tomatoes after straining and add to salsa.
