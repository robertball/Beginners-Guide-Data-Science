Title:
Lebanese White Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6100016.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp orange blossom water, divided
1 cup boiling water
1 tsp acacia honey
2 orange blossoms (optional)

Directions:
Divide the orange blossom water between two tea cups. Add one-half cup boiling water to each cup and stir. Stir in 1/2 tsp honey to each cup. Float a piece of orange blossom in each cup. Serve hot.
