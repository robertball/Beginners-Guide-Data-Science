Title:
Baked Ham and Cheese Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4558746.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
2 cups grated sharp Cheddar cheese
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 cup chopped smoked ham
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, sour cream, ham, and green chilies in a baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until bubbling, about 30 minutes; serve hot.
