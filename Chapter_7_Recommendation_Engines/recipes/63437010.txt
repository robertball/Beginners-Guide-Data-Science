Title:
SPINACH SPREAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Knorr's Swiss Veg. soup mix
1 can sliced water chestnuts, chopped
1 small onion
1 c. miracle whip
1 frozen chopped spinach, defrosted and drained

Directions:
Mix all together and put in a round rye bread that has been hollowed out in bite sized pieces.
Use pieces you took out to put around the bread to dip into the spread.
