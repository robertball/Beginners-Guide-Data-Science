Title:
Favorite Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. macaroni, uncooked
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, finely chopped
1 can tomato soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
9 slices American cheese

Directions:
Prepare macaroni according to package directions, then drain.
Meanwhile, cook ground beef and chopped onion in a large skillet over medium heat.  Once the meat is browned and onion is tender, drain the fat and add the soups, garlic powder and Worcestershire sauce.  Stir well to combine.
Add the cooked macaroni to the skillet and stir to coat evenly.  Place slices of cheese over top.  Turn off heat and cover skillet with a lid or foil.  Allow cheese to melt, then serve.

Description: 
Cheap, quick, easy and filling - our favorite weeknight meal.  Tastes like Hamburger Helper but doesn't have all of the preservatives.
