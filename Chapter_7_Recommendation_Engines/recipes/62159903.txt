Title:
Family Favorite Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of elbow macaroni
4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups shredded colby jack
2/3 cup grated parmesan
2/3 cup cottage cheese (small curd)
2/3 cup sour cream
1/3 cup milk
2 Tablespoons Dijon Mustard
Black Pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil water and add macaroni.  Cook until done to desired tenderness.  Drain well.  Mix in large bowl- cottage cheese, sour cream, mustard, milk and Parmesan and black pepper.  Add Shredded cheeses.  Mix well.  If looks too dry add a tad more milk.  Mix in cooked macaroni.  Place in greased 9 X 13 casserole dish. If desired can dab top with butter for extra crispy top.

Submitter: 
Lindsay
Description: 
I made this up after reading tons of recipes and deciding I wanted to get creative on my own... the result has been wonderful!  My family can't get enough of this.  It's a four cheese blend and has tons of cheese in it, so its really cheesy.
