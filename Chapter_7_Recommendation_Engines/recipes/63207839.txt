Title:
THE vanilla ice cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
? 16 egg yolks
? 2 cup castor sugar
? 4 cup milk
? 4 cup heavy cream
? 8 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Directions:
beat yolks and sugar til thick
pour milk in sauce pan and scald. Pour some hot milk into yolks to temper them, then pour back into pan.
Heat entry, stirring until custard thickens. DO NOT BRING TO BOIL OR IT WILL CURDLE
look for a film on the back of spoon.
chill
right before freezing, stir in cream and vanilla

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
The BEST custard based ice cream. Great to add crushed peppermints, or oreos before freezing
