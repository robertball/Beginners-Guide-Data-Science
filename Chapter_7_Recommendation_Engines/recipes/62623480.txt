Title:
Boozy Steak with Creamy Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/583433.jpg
Ingredients:
2 6-7 ounce steaks
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 tsp. soy sauce
2 tbsp. fresh ground peppercorns
3 tbsp. butter
6-8 mushrooms, sliced
1 large garlic clove, pressed or minced
1 ounce good bourbon
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Coat steaks with olive oil, soy sauce and peppercorns. Allow to rest, unrefrigerated for 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 425. Heat iron skillet medium high heat. Place 2 tbsp. butter in skillet, allowing it to melt completely, and begin to foam. place steaks in butter. Sear steaks on each side for 2-3 minutes. Place sliced mushrooms around steaks, and stir till coated in pan drippings. Put steaks in skillet in to preheated oven. Bake for 6-8 minutes for medium rare steaks. Remove steaks from skillet and allow to rest. Return skillet to medium heat, and add remaining butter and garlic. Sautee garlic for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add bourbon (careful if using a gas stove!) and cream. Allow to simmer for about 2 minutes, until slightly reduced. Serve pan sauce over steaks.

Description: 
Peppered steaks seared in cast iron, with a quick, creamy pan mushroom sauce. Simple, elegant dinner for 2.
