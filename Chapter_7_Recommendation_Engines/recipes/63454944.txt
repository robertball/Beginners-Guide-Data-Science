Title:
Vanilla Cinnamon Dry Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup instant nonfat dry milk
1 small box (1 oz) sugar free instant vanilla pudding
1/3 cup packed dark brown sugar
2 heaping teaspoons cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon cloves

Directions:
Mix all of these ingredients together. I use 1 Tablespoon per 8 oz cup of coffee. You can use more or less according to your taste buds.

Submitter: 
Scotdog
Description: 
I have fun coming up with homemade versions of things we buy at the store. This made a delicious cup of coffee that smells so wonderful.
