Title:
Old-fashioned Butterscotch Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
6 tablespoons milk
1 stick butter or margarine
7 tbsp flour
2 eggs, well-beaten
2 cups milk
1 9-inch pre-baked pie crust

Directions:
Brown sugar, butter and milk together. While sugar mixture browns, mix flour and eggs together. Gradually add milk to flour mixture
When sugar mix has desired color, add flour mixture (stirring rapidly and constantly) to bubbling sugar mixture.
Pour mixture into pre-baked pie crust and either eat warm or chill in refrigerator until ready to serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
You won't find any butterscotch flavoring or melted butterscotch discs in -this- butterscotch pie recipe! Scratch-made butterscotch can't be beat, and this pie has been an all-time favorite.
