Title:
Beurre Blanc Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large Shallots - finely chopped (although mild onions will do)
100ml/3 ½ fl oz White Wine Vinegar
100ml/3 ½ fl oz dry White Wine
200ml/6 ½ oz Unsalted Butter - cut into small cubes and chilled
Salt and Pepper.
Plus I added small amount finely chopped Chives or can use some Lemon Zest.

Directions:
In a heavy based pot, cook shallots, vinegar, wine and, if using chives or lemon zest, over med/high heat and reduce to 2 tbsp liquid;
Turn down heat to low and whisk in butter bit by bit to smooth consistency;
Add seasoning and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A delicious, famous, classic sauce to serve with fish dishes. Poached Trout and Salmon are magic with it.
