Title:
Lowfat Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground turkey
1 chopped onion
1 diced green and / or red pepper
1 grated zucchini
3 minced garlic cloves
32 oz chicken broth
16 - 24 oz salsa
1 can corn - drained
1 can black beans - drained
1 can white beans - drained
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes (not drained)
1/4 cup lime juice
2 - 3 Tablespoons fresh cilantro - diced (or 2 tsp dried)
tortilla chips

Directions:
Spray large soup pan with vegtable oil.
Add ground turkey, season with salt and pepper and cook until done
Stir in onion, pepper, zucchini and garlic until cook until soft.
Add broth, salsa, corn, beans, tomatoes, lime juice and cilantro.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
In soup bowl, place chips on bottom.
Pour soup over chips and enjoy!
