Title:
Fluffy Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg, beaten
2 TBSP orange juice
2 TBSP orange zest
2 TBSP lemon zest
3 oz cream cheese, softened
1 12 oz container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the first six ingredients. Cook and stir over low heat until sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat.
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add in egg mixture, beating until smooth.
Cool to room temperature. Fold in whipped topping.
Refrigerate until ready to serve. Sprinkle with additional orange and lemon zest if desired.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Fluffy, sweet, and delicious dip for fresh fruit.
