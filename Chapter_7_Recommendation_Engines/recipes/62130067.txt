Title:
Golden BBQ Taters! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium Cooked Potatoes (with skin still on!)
Butter for spreading
Garlic Mayo
Grated Chedder Cheese

Directions:
Cut the potatoes lengthways and lightly spread with butter. Leave to sit for 10 mins.
Place potatoes butter side down on a medium grill.
Remove when the potaoes are a nice golden brown colour.
Heap a tsp of garlic mayo on top.
Sprinkle some grated Chedder cheese on top to finish!

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
These golden BBQ potatoes are so easy to make and a wonderful side for any BBQ meal!
