Title:
Creamy Vegetables in Pastry Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981239.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package Pepperidge Farm® Puff Pastry Shells, prepared according to package directions
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/3 cup milk or water
1 (16 ounce) bag frozen vegetable combination (broccoli, cauliflower, carrots), cooked and drained

Directions:
Heat the soup and milk in a 1-quart saucepan over medium heat until the mixture is hot and bubbling, stirring occasionally. Divide the vegetables among the pastry shells. Spoon the soup mixture over the vegetables.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
This stunning side dish is made with convenient products like puff pastry shells, frozen vegetables and canned soup. This side goes beautifully with seafood, beef or poultry.
