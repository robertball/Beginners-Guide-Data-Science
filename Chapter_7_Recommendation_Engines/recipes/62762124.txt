Title:
Five Minute Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen sliced strawberries
1/2 cup sugar
2/3 cup plain or vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Combine the frozen strawberries and sugar in a food processor or blender. Process until the fruit is roughly chopped. With the processor running, slowly pour in the yogurt until fully incorporated. Serve immediately, or freeze for up to one week.

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
A fast way to make delicious ice cream without compromising quality. Use any frozen fruit in place of the strawberries. This is a quick recipe to WOW company who drop by.Modified from: Five Minute Ice Cream
