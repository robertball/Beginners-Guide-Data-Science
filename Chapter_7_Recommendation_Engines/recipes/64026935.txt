Title:
Quinoa Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1118873.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. light brown sugar
2 Eggs, beaten
1 C. Quinoa flakes
1/2 C. Oat bran
1 C. Pure chocolate chips
1/4 C. Light cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
In a large mixing bowl combine butter and sugar together and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until sugar dissolves into the butter
Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture and beat until light and creamy
Mix in oat bran, quinoa flakes, chocolate chips and cream, mixing all ingredients together
Stir in dry ingredients and coat evenly over the mixture to form a smooth cookie dough
Take small amounts of dough and roll into balls placing them 2 inches apart on parchment paper
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes until lightly browned
Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool completely before storing in a container

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very chocolatey flavoured cookie with added quinoa and oat bran to make a nice cookie for tea or coffee time.
