Title:
Eggplant Supper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 pound ground turkey
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 pound eggplant, diced
3/4 cup sliced celery
1 red bell pepper, chopped
2 (14.5 ounce) cans Italian diced tomatoes
2 (14 ounce) cans beef, turkey OR chicken broth
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2/3 cup dry macaroni
2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the onion, ground turkey, and garlic until turkey is evenly brown. Mix in eggplant, celery, red bell pepper and tomatoes. Pour in broth. Mix in sugar, and season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir until heated through.
Mix macaroni into the soup, and continue cooking 12 minutes, or until macaroni is al dente. Mix in parsley. Top with shredded cheese to serve.
