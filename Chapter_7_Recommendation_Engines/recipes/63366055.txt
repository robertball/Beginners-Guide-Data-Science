Title:
Roasted Red Pepper Hummus with a twist from Nidal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15oz cans garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1/3 cup tahini
the juice of 1 lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cloves garlic
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 pinch paprika
1/4 -1/2 cup roasted red peppers
1-2 T. white vinegar

Directions:
Place the garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice from 1/2 lemon, roasted pepper, white vinegar,salt and garlic in a blender or food processor. Blend (adding the other half lemon juice and water as needed, until smooth. Transfer mixture to a serving bowl.
Drizzle olive oil over the garbanzo bean mixture. Sprinkle with paprika and parsley and chopped roasted red pepper if you have extra.

Submitter: 
bekagreen
Description: 
As a spread, dip or garlicky pita filling, roasted red pepper hummus is both delicious and satisfying. Best of all, it's whipped up in seconds in a blender or food processor.Modified from: Hummus III
