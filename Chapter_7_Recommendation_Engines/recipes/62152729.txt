Title:
Huge Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937823.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter
1 1/4 cup brown sugar
3 eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
1 cup walnuts
1 12oz bag chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375*
In mixing bowl add the butter softened, brown sugar, baking soda and salt then mix until well combined. Add eggs one at a time and continue to mix add the flour a little at a time scraping bowl constantly stir in nuts and chocolate chips drop 2 tablespoon size round (ice cream scoop) onto cool parchment covered cookie sheets then bake in oven for 15-18 minutes. Cool on pan for 5 minutes then transfer to cooling racks, makes about 15 very large soft cookies

Submitter: 
Vince Jones
Description: 
I made these cookies after a challenge from my wife to make large high chocolate chip cookies that didn't go flat and crunchy when baked. It worked and now I am forced to always make them this way.
