Title:
Pancit Canton Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 onion, sliced
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1 lb port shoulder, thin sliced
1 bonless chicken breast, thin sliced
1/2 lb shrimp peeled and deveined
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 cups chicked broth
1 cup cabbage, shreaded
1 cup green (baguio) beans, julienned
2 carrots, julienned
3/4 cup fresh sliced mushrooms
1 package pancit canton noodles (1 lb)
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
fish sauce (patis), to taste
4 green onions, sliced
1 lime, cut into wedges

Directions:
Soak mushrooms in warm water for 30 minutes, set aside.
Heat oil over medium-high heat in wok or large skillet.
Saute garlic and onions until tender.
Add pork, chicken and shrimp, and cook until browned.
Stir in soy sauce.
Add chicken broth and bring to boil.
Add cabbage, green beans, carrots and mushrooms.
Cook 5-8 minutes until vegetables are tender.
Add noodles, mixing gently to prevent them from breaking.
Cook until liquid is absorbed and noodles are done, about 10 minutes.
Remove from heat.
Season with black pepper and patis.
Garnish with green onions and lime wedges.
