Title:
Brown Rice & Kale Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown rice
4 cups vegetable broth
3 tbs butter
1/2 cup onion, diced
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup carrot, diced
1/2 cup mushrooms, diced
2 cups kale, chopped and stems removed
1/4 cup almonds, chopped
salt to taste

Directions:
Cook brown rice in vegetable broth until tender and fluffy
Melt butter in large saucepan. Saute onion, carrot, celery and almonds until vegetables begin to soften. Add mushrooms and cook a minute more. Salt to taste.
Add cooked rice to saute pan. Toss to coat with sauteed vegetables.
Add kale and cook until completely wilted. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
AFulton
Description: 
Great way to use up leftovers. Perfect alone for a hearty lunch or as a side to a nice steak.
