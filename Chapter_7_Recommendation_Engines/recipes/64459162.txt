Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 c oil
2 c flour
2 c zucchini, peeled and grated
3 tsp vanilla
3 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 c chopped nuts

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mix well and pour into two 5x9 inch dark loaf pans that have been greased and floured. Bake at 350 for one hour.

Submitter: 
Noel Keen
Description: 
Great, moist bread to use up your zucchini!
