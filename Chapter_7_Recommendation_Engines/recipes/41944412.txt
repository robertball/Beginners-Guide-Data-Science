Title:
Lentils and Italian Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBL + 3TBL olive oil
1 lb dry lentils
1 large onion
3 TBL minced garlic
1 pkg Italian Turkey Sausage
1 16oz Italian diced tomatoes
1  32 ounce reduced sodium chicken broth
2 cups water
2 bay leaf
1 tsp thyme
6-8 peppercorn

Directions:
Heat oil in large frying pan and cook sliced sausage 3-5 minutes.
Set aside.  Heat olive oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Stir in lentils, onion,and garlic; cook until tender.  Stir in sausage and tomatoes.  Add chicken broth and water.  Stir in bay leaf, thyme and peppercorns.  Bring to a boil; cover, simmer until lentils are tender, about 30 minutes.  Season with salt if necessary.
