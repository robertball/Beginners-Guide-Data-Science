Title:
Chicken Wing or Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 chicken wings or 6 steaks
1 c soy sauce
1 c water
2 tbsp brown sugar
2 tsp ground ginger
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped

Directions:
Mix all non-meat ingredients in large plastic bag.
Add meat and marinate overnight.
Bake wings on large baking sheet at 350 degrees for 1 1/2-2 hours; grill steak.
