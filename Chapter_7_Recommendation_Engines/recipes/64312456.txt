Title:
Chicken Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 spray(s) cooking spray
6 ounce(s) chicken, breast, boneless, skinless
1/2 teaspoon taco seasoning mix
1/8 cup(s) pepper, bell, any color chopped
1/4 cup(s) tomato(es) chopped
1 tablespoon cheese, Colby-Jack, reduced fat, shredded
1/4 medium avocado
1 tablespoon onion(s), red chopped
1/2 teaspoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1/2 tablespoon lime juice, fresh

Directions:
Heat skillet with cooking spray to medium heat.
Season chicken with taco seasoning. Once pan is hot cook chicken for about 5 minutes per side until the center is no longer pink, then remove from heat.
Combine all other ingredients, topping it with the olive oil and lime juice and enjoy.
