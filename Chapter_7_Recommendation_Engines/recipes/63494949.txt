Title:
Creamy Maple Mustard Vinagrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup walnut oil
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup mayo
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp dijon mustard
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Put in mason jar and use immersion blender.  Stores for a few weeks in refrigerator.
