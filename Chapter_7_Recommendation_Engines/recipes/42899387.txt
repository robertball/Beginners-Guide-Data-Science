Title:
Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Your favorite sauce doctored the way you like it
Lasagne Noodles
1 1/2 Lb ground sirloin
2 Sm Containers of Cottage Cheese
2 eggs
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/2 Tsp Fennel Seed
2 cups mozzerella Shredded

Directions:
Precook the Lasagne Noodles
Mix the meat, cottage cheese, eggs, parmesan, and fennel seed together
Layer; Sauce, noodles,
Drop meat into layer by slicing it off the spoon in small chunks
Sprinkle generously with Mozzerella
Sauce
Noodles
Meat
Cheese
Repeat until pan is full
Top with Noodles and Sauce
Bake covered for 45 min, remove cover and brown slightly
