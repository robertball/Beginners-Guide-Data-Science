Title:
Peppery Coleslaw with Cucumbers and Celery Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head small cabbage, shredded
1/2 cucumber with peeling on, chopped
1 stalk of celery including top, chopped fine
2 tbsp fresh chopped cilantro
2 tbsp fresh chopped Italian flat-leaved parsley
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tbsp white vinegar
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp fresh coarsely ground black pepper

Directions:
Prepare vegetables.  Mix mayo, salt, pepper, onion powder & vinegar.  Pour over vegetables and mix well.  Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Susan Bray Garrison
Description: 
Tired of sweet coleslaw?  Try this for a nice change.
