Title:
Glazed Pepper Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983917.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons sugar
4 teaspoons Spice Islands® Fennel Seed
4 teaspoons Spice Islands® Crushed Red Pepper
2 teaspoons salt
2 cups whole nuts, unsalted (almonds, cashews or pecans)
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with foil; spray with cooking spray.
Combine sugar, fennel seeds, crushed red pepper and salt in a medium-sized bowl. Mix in nuts and water. Stir to evenly coat. Spread mixture on prepared baking sheet in a single layer. Bake about 15 minutes, stirring frequently, until nuts are a deep golden brown. Cool. Separate nuts with fork.
