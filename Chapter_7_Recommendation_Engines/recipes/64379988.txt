Title:
vegan fluffy waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3450566.jpg
Ingredients:
4 1/4 c organic all purpose flour
2tbsp baking powder (Hain pure food brand for corn free)
1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
1 tbsp white sugar
1/2 tsp salt
4 1/4c almond milk (I use vanilla flavor)
1/2 c canola oil
1 tbsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix dry ingredient
Make a well, and poor in wet ingredients
Mix well, poor into waffle iron
Cook until iron stops steaming.
(I even used chocolate almond milk and it tasted great)

Submitter:

Description: 
With multiple allergies in our house hold,I had create something our whole family could enjoy. dairy,egg,soy and corn free
