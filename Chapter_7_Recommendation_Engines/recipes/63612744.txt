Title:
Skinny Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004867.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
6 oz fat free Greek yogurt
1 tbsp light mayonnaise
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, mash blue cheese and yogurt together with a fork. Stir in mayonnaise, lemon juice, vinegar, and garlic powder until well blended. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Makes 1 cup.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
The perfect dip for buffalo wings without the fat!
