Title:
Leftover Turkey Soup (Crock Pot) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts chicken broth
1 turkey carcass, all meat/fat/skin removed
1 onion, quartered
2 carrot, halved lengthwise
3 cellery stalks, halved lengthwise
2 bay leaves
1 cup chopped carrots
1/3 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 cups uncooked pasta
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup (optional)
3 cups turkey meat

Directions:
Put chicken broth, turkey, onion halves, carrot halves, celery stalks, and bay leaves in crock pot. Cook on HIGH for 4 hours
Carefully strain broth and transfer to bowl. Discard solids. Rinse out crock pot.
Transfer broth back to crock pot. Add celery, onion, and mushrooms. Cook on LOW for 6 hours. Add pasta halfway through cooking (otherwise it may be too soft). Add turkey meat and condensed soup 30 minutes before done.
