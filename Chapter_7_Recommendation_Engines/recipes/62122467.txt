Title:
Bourbon Barbequed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast, boiled
1 small onion, minced
3 garlic cloves, minced
3/4 cup of bourbon
2 cups of ketchup
1/4 cup tomato paste
1/3 cup cider vinegar
1 tablespoon liquid smoke
2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
dash of hot sauce
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil chicken breast, and set aside to cool. In the meantime, in a large skillet on med-high heat, combine onions, garlic and bourbon. Simmer until the onion is tender. While this is simmering, shred chicken breast with a fork and place in slow cooker on low heat.
To the onion, garlic and bourbon, add ketchup, tomato paste, vinegar, liquid smoke, worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, hot sauce and salt & pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes. Pour over shredded chicken in slow cooker and stir. Cook on low for two hours.

Submitter: 
Janine
Description: 
This recipe never fails to get a two thumbs up from family and friends. It doesn't necessarily have to be finished in the slow cooker, it can be done stove top. We just enjoy the aroma of the simmering barbeque in the slow cooker. I hope you enjoy!
