Title:
Slow Cooker Cilantro Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
1 (1.25 ounce) package dry taco seasoning mix
1 lime, juiced
1/2 - 1 teaspoon dried cilantro
1.5 pounds frozen skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Stir together the salsa, taco seasoning and lime juice. Place frozen chicken breasts in the slow cooker and pour mixture over the top. Cover the cooker, set to High, and cook until the chicken is very tender, about 4 hours. If desired, set cooker to Low and cook 8 hours. Shred chicken with 2 forks and add back into the slow cooker.  Add cilantro and stir.  Allow to simmer for another 30 minutes.  Serve on tacos or over rice.

Submitter: 
Linda In Da Kitchen
Description: 
Chicken breasts slow-cooked in Mexican-inspired salsa and seasonings are great to shred and serve on tacos with your favorite toppings.Modified from: Slow Cooker Cilantro Lime Chicken
