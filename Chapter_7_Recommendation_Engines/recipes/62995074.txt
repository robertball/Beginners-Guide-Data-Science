Title:
Vodka Slushies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 C water
1 C sugar
12 oz can frozen orange juice
12 oz can frozen lemonade
2 C vodka
Club soda (for mix)

Directions:
In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add sugar. Simmer for 15 minutes to disolve sugar completely. Let cool.
Add orange juice, lemonade & vodka to sugar water and mix until incorporated.
Pour into a gallon ice cream bucket and place in freezer. Stir occassionally during the freezing process (approximately 24 hours)
To serve, add vodka sluch to glass and top off with the desired amount of club soda. Stir.
