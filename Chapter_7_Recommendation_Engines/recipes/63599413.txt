Title:
Coconut Korma (BIR style) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS
3 Tablespoons olive oil
1 lbs diced chicken thigh â€“ skinned
3 Tablespoons chopped cashew nuts
2 Tablespoons desiccated coconut
3/4 cup honey greek yogurt
3 cups curry gravy
1/2 teaspoon coconut extract
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon garam masala
1/2 cup coconut milk
1 Tablespoon cumin powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
salt and black pepper to taste
3 tablespoons chopped coriander

Directions:
Method
Heat the olive oil over medium heat and add the chicken pieces and cashew pieces/broken up.
When the chicken pieces are almost cooked through, add the remaining curry gravy and then spoon the yogurt mixture in one spoonful at a time. Add coconut milk.
Stir continuously.
Add the extract, cayene, cumin, coriander powder and salt an pepper to taste.
To serve
Top the chicken korma with chopped coriander and serve with plain boiled white or wild rice.
