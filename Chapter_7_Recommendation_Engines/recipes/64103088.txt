Title:
Big Ray's Spicy Sesame Buffalo Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1238076.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup  sesame oil
1 Tbsp.  granulated garlic
1 Tbsp.  ground red pepper (cayenne)
2 tsp.  ground ginger
2 lb.  chicken
3/4 cup  barbecue sauce
1  green onion, sliced

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients in a resealable plastic bag;add chicken pieces. Refrigerate 30 min to overnight to marinate, turning to coat.
Heat grill to medium-high heat. Remove chicken from marinade; discard marinade. Grill chicken 15 min., turning occasionally. Brush lightly with some of the barbecue sauce. Continue grilling 4 to 5 min. per side or until chicken is done (165ÂºF), turning and brushing frequently with remaining barbecue sauce.
Place chicken on serving platter; top with onions.

Description: 
It is a good thing if no one is talking while eating these spicy, sweet and garlicky chicken. This Asian twist on the classic will be your new game-night staple.  Use whatever chicken pieces you want: wingd, thighs or whole chicken cut up.
