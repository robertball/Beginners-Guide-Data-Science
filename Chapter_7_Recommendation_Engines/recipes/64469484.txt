Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites: Fluffy Dino Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2459720.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (softened)
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg; 2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 cup chocolate mocha mix
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
several handfuls of mini-marshmallows or about 12 large ones;

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
2. Mix butter and sugar in bowl together. Then, add eggs and vanilla.
3. Add in cocoa/mocha powder and flour and baking soda to the bowl. At this point you may have to use an electric mixer or your hands.
4. Take a small ball of dough, smoosh down the center and sort of poke the sides up a bit. Cook for 12 minutes. Remove from heat.
5. Place marshmallows in the center (if using large marshmallows, cut in half) and cook for about three more minutes.
