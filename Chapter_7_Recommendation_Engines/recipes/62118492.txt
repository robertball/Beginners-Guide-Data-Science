Title:
Simple Deep Fried Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 - 15 pound turkey - completely thawed
3.5 gallons of peanut oil
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Ensure turkey is COMPLETELY thawed.
Remove inards.
Cut extra skin away from the neck area and make sure neck hole is at least an inch in diameter.
Liberally salt and pepper the turkey, inside and out.
Cook the turkey for 3.5 minutes per pound of bird.
Let cool for 15 minutes, carve and serve.

Submitter: 
sgtsquarepants
Description: 
If you like a more traditional tasting turkey, this is the one for you.  No injecting, no marinades, no complicated rubs.
