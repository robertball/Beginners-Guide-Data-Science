Title:
Strawberry-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (250 mL) frozen unsweetened strawberries
1 medium banana, halved
3/4 cup (175 mL) milk
1/3 cup (75 mL) vanilla-flavored yogurt

Directions:
Place strawberries in bowl. Microwave on HIGH for 15-20 seconds until slightly thawed. Combine strawberries, banana, milk, and yogurt in food processor; cover and process until smooth.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Start the morning with this lightning-fast smoothie, which makes a great breakfast for two.
