Title:
Strawberry Lemonade With a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/822790.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup water
Grated zest of one lemon
1 pint fresh strawberries, cleaned & hulled
1 cup fresh lemon juice
2 cups cold water
1 cup cold vodka
ice
strawberries & lemon wedges for garnish, if desired

Directions:
Make a simple syrup by combining sugar and lemon zest with 1 cup of water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat, and stir until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and let cool.
Puree strawberries in a blender.
In a large pitcher, combine strawberry puree, simple syrup, lemon juice, and vodka. Stir to combine.
Add cold water, stir.
Pour into chilled glasses with ice and garnish with lemon wedge and strawberry, if desired.

Description: 
Everyone loves strawberry lemonade. This one is delicious and has a kick of vodka. Serve it at your next BBQ. It's sure to be a crowd pleaser.
