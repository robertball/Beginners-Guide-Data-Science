Title:
Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound powdered sugar,
1/2 cup butterm
1 tsp vanilla,
3 TBSP milk.

Directions:
In large bowl, beat together sugar, butter, vanilla and milk until smooth.  If necessary add more milk til frosting is the right spreadable consistency.  If desired, add a few drops of food coloring, stirring to blend.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
A tasty addition to almost any cake recipe.
