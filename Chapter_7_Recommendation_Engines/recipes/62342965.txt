Title:
Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts or Chicken Tenders
1 8 oz. bottle of Italian Dressing
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
1 16 oz. bottle Hidden Valley Ranch

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a 12" baking pan, place chicken breasts, Italian dressing, chopped onions and green peppers into pan and bake for 25-35 minutes.
When chicken is almost done, drain Italian dressing and add ranch dressing over chicken breasts. Bake another 5-10 minutes and enjoy.
