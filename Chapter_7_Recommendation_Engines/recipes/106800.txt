Title:
Blackberry Twist Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983495.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh blackberries, or unsweetened frozen blackberries, thawed
1 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
4 cups cold water

Directions:
Combine blackberries, lemon juice, and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener in a blender; process until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides. Press mixture through a sieve into a pitcher; discard solids. Stir in water. Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Blackberries give a new twist and a bright purple color to a summertime favorite.
