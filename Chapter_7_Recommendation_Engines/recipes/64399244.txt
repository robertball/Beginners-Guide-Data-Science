Title:
Bacon,  Tomato and Avocado Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2245553.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Medium Size flour Tortilla for soft fajitas (any size will work)
1 Cup grated Cheddar Cheese
1 small tomato, seeded & diced
1/2 cup onion, diced
3-4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
1 Avocado, pitted & sliced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp Cilantro, chopped (optional)
Drizzle Ranch and buffalo ranch dressing (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
Spray large cast iron skillet with cooking spray and let warm up on medium heat for 30 seconds. Add flour tortilla, cook for 1 minute to brown and harden the bottom. Top tortilla with half of the grated cheese, tomato, onion and bacon. Season with salt and pepper. Cook for 1 minute until cheese starts to melt.
Transfer cast iron skillet to oven for 2-3 minutes until cheese is melted.
Top tortilla with avocado slices. Season with salt, pepper & cilantro. Drizzle top in a zig-zag motion with ranch and buffalo ranch dressing.
Repeat with remaining tortilla.

Submitter: 
tcasa
Description: 
A quick and easy recipe with bacon, tomatoes and avocados. A great base to add a variety of topping and get the kids involved in the kitchen. We love making these for lunch and as appetizers with whatever leftovers we have available; chicken, steak, vegetables. Enjoy!
