Title:
Apple Cardamom Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/62842.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Ground Cardamom
1/3 cup butter, melted
Glazed apples:
4 medium cooking apples, peeled and cored
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup butter
3/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Ground Cardamom
Cheese Filling:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
2/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons McCormick® Pure Vanilla Extract
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel
4 eggs

Directions:
Crust: Mix all ingredients.  Press firmly on bottom and 1 inch up the sides of 9-inch springform pan.  Set aside.
Glazed Apples:  Cut apples into 16ths.  In large skillet, combine apples with remaining ingredients.  Cook until apples begin to caramelize, turning often. Arrange over bottom of crust.
Cheese Filling:   Beat cream cheese until fluffy.  Gradually beat in sugar, vanilla, and lemon peel.  Beat in eggs, one at a time.  Pour over apples.
Bake in preheated 325 degrees F oven 60-70 minutes, or until lightly browned.  Turn oven off and allow cheesecake to remain in oven 1 hour with door slightly ajar.  Cool in pan.  Chill.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
This is a spectacular cheese cake with a hidden surprise. Glazed apples accented with cardamom are the surprise that adds a fresh, fruity taste, too.
