Title:
Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can corn, drained
1 tomato, diced
1 onion, chopped
1/4 cup lime juice
2 tsp chopped cilantro
1/2 tsp cumin
1 pkg italian seasoning dressing mix

Directions:
Mix all together and chill overnight in fridge. Serve with corn or tortilla chips.

Description:
