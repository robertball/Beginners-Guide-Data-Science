Title:
Crock Pot Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large chicken breasts, uncooked and cubed
2 large cans cream of chicken soup
1 C. celery
1 small bag baby carrots
10 medium sized red potato's cut in 1/2 or 1/4ths depending on size
1 bag of frozen mixed veggies
1 tsp celery salt
2 tsp garlic salt
1 tbsp pepper
6 chicken bullion cubes or bullion powder is fine too

Directions:
cube chicken, add rest of ingredients and cook on high for about 6 hours or until potato's and carrots are tender. If you want the frozen veggies to retain the fresh color then wait to add them until the last hour of cooking. Serve over split buttermilk biscuits.
