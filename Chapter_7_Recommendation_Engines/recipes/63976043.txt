Title:
Sausage and Spinach Rice Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown rice cooked (or an 8.8oz pkg Uncle Bens ready cooked rice)
1 tablespoon olive oil
16 ounces hot turkey Italian sausage, casings removed
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper
5 garlic cloves, minced
1 (6-ounce) package fresh baby spinach
1 ounce Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, shaved (about 1/4 cup)

Directions:
Heat rice according to directions.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add sausage and pepper; cook 4 minutes or until sausage is browned, stirring to crumble. Add garlic; cook 30 seconds, stirring constantly. Add spinach; cook 30 seconds or until spinach begins to wilt, tossing constantly. Stir in rice; cook 1 minute or until heated. Sprinkle with cheese.

Easy one pan dinner. Double the recipe for lunch the next day.
