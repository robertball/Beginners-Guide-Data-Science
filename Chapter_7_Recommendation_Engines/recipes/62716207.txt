Title:
Extra Tasty Cream Dried Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
2 10 oz pkg Buddig original beef
1/2 C flour
4 C milk
2 tsp worcestershire sauce
salt & pepper

Directions:
Melt 1 stick butter in electric skillet about 300 degrees
Use kitchen scissors to cut beef into bite size pieces
Cook beef until crispy, use a cooking fork to stir & separate the pieces of beef
Use slotted spoon to remove beef to a large bowl
Melt 1 stick butter, stir in flour, add milk
Stir until thickened, add worcestershire sauce, salt & pepper to taste.
Stir in cooked beef. Continue heating a little while for flavor to cook through.
Serve on top of baked potato or toast.
