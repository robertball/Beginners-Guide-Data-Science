Title:
Brined Chicken Breast (for Grilling) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup salt
4 cups water
2 tbsps of sesame oil
1 tsp Montreal Chicken spice

Directions:
Mix sugar and salt with enough water to cover chicken breasts.
Brine breasts for up to 1 hour in refrigerator.
Pound to an even thickness (about 3/4 inch)
Dry Chicken breasts
Rub Sesame oil over breasts
Season with Chicken spice and push spice into the meat with the back of a spoon.
Preheat the Grill to about 400. Use 2 burners and leave one burner off.
Grill for about 5 minutes per side on heated side of grill. Watch carefully as it does not take much to overcook the chicken.

Submitter: 
Ron Lloyd
Description: 
Brined chicken breast turns out moist.
