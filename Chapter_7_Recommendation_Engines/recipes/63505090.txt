Title:
Super Chocolate Peppermint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Spelt Flour
1/2 Cup Oat Flour
1/4 cup Raw Sugar
1/2 Cup Cocoa Powder
1 teaspoon Baking Soda
2 Chia Seed Eggs
1/2 Cup Agave
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 teaspoon Vanilla
1 Cup Chocolate Chips
1 Cup Chopped Walnuts
1/2 teaspoon Peppermint
6-8 drops Peppermint Essential Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F
Blend Chia Seed Egg, Agave, Olive Oil, Vanilla, Peppermint - In Blender
Combine Sugar, Flours, Baking Soda, Salt, Choc Chips, Walnuts, Cocoa Powder
Add Wet ingredients to dry Ingredients.
Spoon on greased Cookie sheet or parchment paper.
Flatten and shape the cookies with damp hands.
Bake for 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
SO GOOD! These cookies have the spark of peppermint in chocolate. A winning combination. Sweetened with agave and raw sugar,  made with Chia eggs, spelt and oat flour.
