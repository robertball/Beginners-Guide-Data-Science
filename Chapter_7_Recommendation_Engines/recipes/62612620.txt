Title:
Picadillo(ground beef) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup tomato(finely chopped)
1/2cup tomatillo(finely chopped)
1/2cup onion(finely chopped)
1/4cup jalapeno(finely chopped)
1 1/2cup patato(diced 1/2 inch)
1 1/2cup carrot diced 1/2 inch(baby carrots cut 1/3's)
1 1/2lbs ground beef
2tbs vegtable oil(optional)
2 1/2 tsp salt divided
1 1/2tsp ground pepper
2tsp cumin
2 red california chili peppers(red chili pods)soaked

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat add oil(optional)and ground beef,brown(season with 1 1/2tsp salt & ground pepper) untill no longer pink;add tomato,tomatillo,onion,and jalapeno;Stir occationaly until veggies are almost done;add carrots and patatoes;meanwhile in blender blend soaked red peppers with 2cups water,cumin & remaining salt;add to meat simmer on low for 20-30 minutes or untill carrots are soft
