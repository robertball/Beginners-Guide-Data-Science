Title:
Homemade cranberry sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag fresh cranberries
1 pint frozen OJ
sugar to taste
1 15 oz can madarine oranges
1/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
wash cranberries and separate out old ones
Boil cranberries in sugar and OJ until they pop
drain the oranges
mix oranges with cranberry mixture, add peacans
refrigerate overnight
