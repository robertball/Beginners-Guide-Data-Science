Title:
Bonnie Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/678936.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter, softened (no substitutes)
1 3/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups sifted cake flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 350ยบ. Grease and flour one 9x13-inch pan, three 8-inch round layer pans or two 9-inch round layer pans.
Cream butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until fluffy. Beat 5 minutes on high speed.
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Mix dry ingredients into creamed mixture alternately with milk using low speed on mixer.
Pour into prepare pans and bake 9x13-inch pan 45-50 minutes and layers 25-35 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Let cake cool for 10 minutes, then invert onto cooling rack and cool completely. Frost as desired.
