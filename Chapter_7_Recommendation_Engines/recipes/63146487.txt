Title:
The Best Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 - 2 pounds lean ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (14.5 ounce) can green beans, drained
1 (32 ounce) package tater tots, thawed
1 (8 oz)can sliced water chestnuts (optional)
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1-1.5 Cups shredded cheese
salt to taste

Directions:
Saute onion in a large skillet until soft; add beef & garlic; cook until browned. Drain set aside.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spread soup, water chestnuts and green beans in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Slowly add meat, then top with tater tots and season with salt to taste.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30-35 minutes. Add 1-1.5 cups of cheese (depends on how cheesy you want it) and cook for 10 more minutes until cheese is completely melted. Enjoy!
