Title:
Corn Chip Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 can yellow kernel corn, drained
1 can white kernel corn, drained (or 2 cans of yellow if you can't get white)
1/2 red bell pepper, sliced and chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, sliced and chopped
1/2 purple onion, chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 package of Ranch Salad Dressing Mix
1 cup shredded chedder or colby cheese
1/2 bag of Chili cheese flavor Frito corn chips

Directions:
Combine 1 cup mayonnaise and 1/2 Package of Ranch Salad Dressing Mix in large bowl
Add yellow corn, white corn, red pepper, green pepper, and onion
Refrigerate before serving
Just before serving, fold in the Chili Cheese Fritos

Submitter: 
Kimberley Drake Smith
Description: 
This is a yummy dish that I had over at a friends house. It looked a little strange but it was delicious! Would go great on any summer menu!
