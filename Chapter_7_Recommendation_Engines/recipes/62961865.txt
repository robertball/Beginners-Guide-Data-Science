Title:
Downeast Maine Pumpkin Bread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin puree
4 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2/3 cup applesauce
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
2/3 cup agave nectar
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup oat bran
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
2 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground ginger
1 vanilla bean (scraped)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 7x3 inch loaf pans.
In a large bowl, mix together pumpkin puree, eggs, oil, applesauce, ginger, vanilla bean, water and sugar until well blended. In a separate bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients. Stir the dry ingredients into the pumpkin mixture until just blended. Pour into the prepared pans.
Bake for about 50 minutes in the preheated oven. Loaves are done when toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
