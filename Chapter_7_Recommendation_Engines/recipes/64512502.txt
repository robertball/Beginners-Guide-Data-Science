Title:
Rice & Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. loose Italian sausage, hot or sweet
2- 32 oz. boxes chicken or vegetable broth
28 oz. can diced tomatoes
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1 cup long-grain rice, uncooked
15 oz. can white beans, rinsed and drained
4 carrots, sliced

Directions:
Brown sausage in a skillet.  Drain off drippings.
Put all ingredients in slow cooker.  Stir until well blended.
Cover.  Cook on Low 2 hours.  Stir soup.
Cover and cook an additional 1 1/2 to 2 hours on Low, or until vegetables and rice are tender but not mushy.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Italian sausage makes this pop on a cold day!
