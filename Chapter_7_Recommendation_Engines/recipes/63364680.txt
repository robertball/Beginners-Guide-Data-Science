Title:
Carols Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123172.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs hash brown patties (14)(thawed and shredded)
1/4 cup onion, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 cups cheddar cheese (shredded)
1 stick butter (melted)(put 3/4 in recipe and save rest)
16 oz. sour cream
3/4 cup crushed corn flakes

Directions:
1. Mix all except 1/4 stick of butter and corn flakes. Put in 9x13 baking dish.
2. Mix 1/4 stick of butter and corn flakes and sprinkle over top of potato mixture.
3. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. (Can make ahead and set overnight.)
