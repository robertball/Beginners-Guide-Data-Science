Title:
Mary's Chicken Black Bean Chile Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101097.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 Large Onion Chopped
1 Green Bell Peppers seeded and cut in squares
1 Red Bell Peppers seeded and cut in squares
2 Cloves of Garlic
1 Cup of Chicken Broth
3 Tablespoons Chili poweder
1 Tablespoon Cumin
1 1/2 Teaspoons Salt
1/4 Teaspoon ground Pepper
1 Chicken Cooked
3 Cans Black Beans
3 Cans Mexican Stewed Tomatos

Directions:
Heat olive oil in Dutch oven or deep skillet.  Add chopped onion, green pepper, red pepper and garlic.  Saut√© slowly until vegetables are tender but do not let them brown.
Add chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper.
Cover and let simmer 30 minutes.
In a separate pot pour in black beans, mexican stewed tomatoes and chicken.
When the vegetables are done mix in with beans, tomatoes and chicken and heat for 30 mins or until at desired temp.
If needed add water to keep runny but not too much as to make sure it is not swimming.
