Title:
Cucumber Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1500177.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cucumbers
2 Apples
2 Limes
Lemon Pepper

Directions:
1. Peel and seed cucumbers.
2. Peel apples and course chop both.
3. Zest both limes over cucumbers and apples.
4. Juice both limes over cucumbers and apples.
5. Lightly spinkle with Lemon Pepper.
6. Toss and refrigerate 15 mins before serving.
