Title:
Baked Oatmeal II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups rolled oats
1 cup brown sugar - REDUCED to 1/4
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1/2 cup melted butter - SUBSTITUTE 3/4 cup flax meal and 1/4 cup butter instead
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3/4 cup dried cranberries
Needs more liquid with flax substitution - maybe increase milk or add apple juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt. Beat in milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir in dried cranberries. Spread into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
