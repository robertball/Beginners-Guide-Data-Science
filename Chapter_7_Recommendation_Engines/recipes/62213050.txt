Title:
Chicken with Peaches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pieces of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. curry
1 tsp. ginger
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1 can of peaches
1 carton of heavy cream

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingredients together in a large ziplock bag, shake each piece of chicken in mixture until evenly coated.  Brown chicken in frying pan.  Melt 2 Tbs. of butter in a glass ovenproof dish, add chicken and can of peaches (drain first).  Bake at 325 for 30 minutes.  Last 10 minutes of baking, pour in cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
A coated chicken with a peaches and cream sauce.
