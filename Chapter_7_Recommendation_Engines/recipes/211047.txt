Title:
Cheesy Calzones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992023.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound refrigerator pizza dough
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 egg
1 cup RAGÚ® Old World Style® Traditional Sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Roll dough into two 7-inch circles; set aside.
Mix cheeses and egg in medium bowl. Spoon cheese mixture onto centers of each circle. Fold each circle in half to form semicircle; press edges together to seal. Beginning at one end, stretch sealed edge and fold over, pinching to seal. Arrange on greased baking pan; poke 5 holes in top of each with toothpick.
Bake 20 minutes or until golden and puffed. Let stand on wire rack 15 minutes before serving. Serve with Pasta Sauce, heated.
