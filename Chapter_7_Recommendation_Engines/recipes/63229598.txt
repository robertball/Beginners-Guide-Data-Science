Title:
Strawberry Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840082.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz lime juice (juice from 1 lime)
1 1/2 oz tequila
1/2 oz triple sec (or other orange flavored liquor)
1/4 cup sliced strawberries (1 large strawberry)
1 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
1 cup ice cubes
lime wedge, optional
sugar, optional

Directions:
For sugared rim, place a small layer of sugar in a small place. Rub lime wedge around the rim of a glass. Dip glass rim in sugar to cover. Combine lime juice, tequila, triple sec, strawberries and sugar in blender and puree for 10 seconds.
Add ice cubes and blend on highest setting for 15 seconds.

Submitter: 
Patrick Washburn
Description: 
A refreshing variation on a classic frozen margarita.
