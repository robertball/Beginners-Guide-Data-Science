Title:
Dads chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 lb chili meat
1/2 lb breakfast sausage
1/2 large sweet onion
1/2 cup chili powder (less to reduce spice)
1 tablespoon paprika
1 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 clove garlic
2 cans rotel
1 can ranch style beans
1 can kidney beans
1 large tomato juice (46 oz)

Directions:
Brown all the meat with onion. Then put in crock pot and add all other ingredients.
