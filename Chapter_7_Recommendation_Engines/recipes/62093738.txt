Title:
Oreo Cookie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/410110.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oreo cookie or chocolate pie shell
16 ounces mini oreo cookies
1 quart vanilla ice cream
8 oz. cool whip (optional)

Directions:
Save 8 cookies for garnishing.
Coarsley crush cookies in a food processor or in a plastic bag.
Stir crushed cookies into softened vanilla ice cream.
Put ice cream mixture into pie shell and freeze until set, about one hour.
Pipe cool whip around edge of pie and top with reserved cookies standing on end.
Freeze several hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
cookin'mama
Description: 
My daughter requests this pie for her birthday each year.  It is embarrassing how easy it is, despite it's popularity with both adults and children!
