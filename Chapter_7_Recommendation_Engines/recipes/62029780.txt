Title:
Spicy Fish Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 T chili powder
1 1/2 cups fat-free or low-fat chicken broth
1 4oz. can chopped green chilies
1 t. cumin
1 1/2 cups canned, diced tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/2 cup tiny frozen cooked shrimp (like salad shrimp)
1/2 pound cod fillets, partially thawed if frozen, and cut into 1" pieces
3/4 cup plain nonfat yogurt
1.  Spray a large saucepan with nonstick cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat.  Add onions and cook, stirring, about 5 minutes.  Stir in garlic and chili powder and continue to cook 1-2 minutes.
2.  Stir in broth, chilies and cumin.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes.
3.  Stir in tomatoes, bell pepper, shrimp,and cod pieces.  Return to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 5 minutes.
4.  Gradually stir in yogurt and heat just until hot.  Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
CHEFETTE
Description: 
A delicious fish soup that's virtually fat free!  Having grown up on the Texas - Mexico border, I like foods spicy, but you can adjust the seasonings to taste.  I also splash in a drop of Tabasco or similar hot sauce in each bowl.  :)
