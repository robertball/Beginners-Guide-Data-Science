Title:
Siple's Chicken Bacon Ranch Pizzaz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2247036.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Bags Better Crocker Pizza Crust Mix
2 lbs Bacon
1/2 Jar Alfredo Sauce
1 Bottle (smaller sized) Hidden Valley Ranch
1 Red Onion
2 Can Cooked Chicken
1 Large Bag Mozzarella Cheese
Optional Heather Ideas:
Add Spinach leaves, chopped into smallish pieces
Add jar of mushrooms
YUM!  :)

Directions:
Dice 2 lbs of Bacon
Fry and crumble it up so it is crispy
Take 2 bags of crust prepared with water per directions and flatten out on one large-sized cookie sheet
Take 1/2 jar of alfredo sauce and spread onto crust
Take ranch dressing and spread on it top also(1/2 a small bottle)
Dice red onion, spread on sauce
Take 2 cans of cooked chicken, drain juice, crumble on pizza
Put bacon on top of chicken
Drizzle remainding ranch on top of that
Put entire bag of mozzarella cheese on top
Bake per directions on pizza crust bag
and VOILA!!!

Add Spinach leaves, chopped into smallish pieces
