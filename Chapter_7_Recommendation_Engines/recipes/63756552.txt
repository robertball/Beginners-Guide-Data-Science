Title:
Big Ray's Grilled Moroccan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042524.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup chopped scallion (white part only)
1/4 cup chopped parsley
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon minced garlic
2 teaspoons paprika
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon turmeric
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
squeeze of lemon
dash of cinnamon
4 boneless skinless chicken thighs

Directions:
Combine oil, scallions, parsley, cilantro, garlic, paprika, cumin, salt, turmeric, cayenne pepper, lemon juice and cinnamon in the container of a food processor.
Process until smooth.
Place all items into a gallon freezer bag, then added chicken, and let it marinate for 30 min to 2 hours. Rub the mixture on both sides of the chicken thighs thrugh the bag every 10 min or so.
Preheat the grill to medium hot.
Grill chicken thighs 5-7 minutes on each side, or until done.
