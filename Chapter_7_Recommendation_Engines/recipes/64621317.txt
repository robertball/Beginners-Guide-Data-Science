Title:
Irene's Famous Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 slightly beaten eggs
1 2/3 cups of flour
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup water
1 cup canned pumpkin
1 cup chopped dates or raisins
1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Oven 325 degrees
Mix ingredients well, pour into a large greased bread pan.
bake 1 1/2 hours.
