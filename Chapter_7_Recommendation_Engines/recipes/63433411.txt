Title:
Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 -2/3 c rice, cook as directed
2 c milk
1/3 c sugar
2 1/2 tbsp corn starch
Dash salt
1/3 c milk
3 eggs, divided
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cook rice as directed.
In separate large sauce pan, bring milk to a boil.
Separate eggs, reserving 2 whites for meringue.
Mix 1 egg and 2 yolks with all remaining ingredients, then add to milk.
Boil until thick.
Pour into oven-safe serving dish.
Prepare meringue, spread on top of pudding, and brown.
