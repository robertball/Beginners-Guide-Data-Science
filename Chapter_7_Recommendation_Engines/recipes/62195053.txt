Title:
Grandma's Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/342843.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Over medium-low heat, melt butter in a medium saucepan and then stir in sugar, cocoa powder and milk.  Stir and bring to a boil and boil for 1 minute.  Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
My Grandma made this recipe for decades.  It is so rich and chocolaty.  Serve over vanilla ice cream for a simply perfect dessert!
