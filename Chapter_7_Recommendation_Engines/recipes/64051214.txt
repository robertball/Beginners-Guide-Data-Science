Title:
Healthy Vegan Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126406.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1 Cup Whole Wheat Flour
- 1 Cup Oat Flour
- 1/2 Cup Unsweetened Coca Powder
- 1 tsp. Baking Soda
- 1/2 Cup Coconut Oil (or any vegan oil/butter alternative)
- 1/2 Cup Vegetable Shortening
- 3/4 Cup Raw Cane Sugar
- 3/4 Cup Natural Brown Sugar
- 2 flaxeggs (2 Tbsp. ground flaxseed and 6 Tbsp. water)
- 1 tsp Vanilla
-2 Cups Vegan Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F.
Combine the flours, coca, and baking soda
In a separate bowl, stir the coconut oil, shortening, and sugars until smooth. Then add your FlaxEggs and vanilla
Gradually add your flour mixture into your oil mixture, then add in your vegan chocolate chips.
Scoop onto prepared cookie sheet with ice cream scoop and flatten tops of cookies with the palm of your hand.
Bake for about 10 minutes and once out of the oven let cool on cookie sheet for approx. 5 minutes then let cool completely on cooling rack.
