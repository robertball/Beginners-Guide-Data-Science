Title:
French Toast II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggs
3 Tablespoons Milk
2 1/2 teaspoons  brown sugar
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
2 slices white bread

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat the egg. Add the milk, brown sugar and cinnamon; stir well to combine.
Soak bread slices in the egg mixture until saturated.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Brown slices on both sides, and serve hot.

A simple French Toast recipe that anyone can make. Use your favorite type of bread and serve with your favorite syrup.Modified from: French Toast II
