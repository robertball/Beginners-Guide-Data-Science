Title:
Nanas Spaghetti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow pepper cut small pieces
1 red pepper cut small pieces
1 med onion chopped fine
2 stalks celery chopped fine
2 cans cream mush soup
1 lb spaghetti broken in pieces
package sharp cheddar shredded cheese
whole chicken or pkg thighs

Directions:
in large stock pot cook chicken, onion, celery...season with salt and pepper...onion powder, garlic powder...remove chicken when cooked and set aside...save broth...
cut chicken into small pieces
place peppers in small bow and cook covered in  microwave for 2 min to soften them
cook spaghetti in the chicken stock ( reserve one cup broth) till spag done - drain spaghetti
in lagre bowl mix soup cheese chicken, peppers and cooked spaghetti and 1 cup broth...bake in casserole dish 350* 35-45 min

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
great flavor...cook the spaghetti in chicken broth for flavor
