Title:
Prosciutto Chicken Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2/3 cup ricotta cheese
1/3 cup frozen spinach, thawed, drained and squeezed dry
2 tbs grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbs chopped drained sun-dried tomatoes in olive oil
6 slices prosciutto
1 3/4 cup marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Tenderize chicken until its 1/4 inch thick. Combine ricotta cheese, spinach, Parmesan cheese and sun-dried tomatoes. Place 1 slice of prosciutto on top on each chicken half, spread cheese mixture evenly on top. Roll up chicken in a jelly roll fashion and secure with toothpicks. Place chicken in a 13x9 in baking pan coated with cooking spray. Spoon 1 cup marinara over chicken. Cover and bake for 15 minutes. Uncover and spoon liquid from pan over chicken and bake an additional 5 minutes. To serve, slice chicken into 4-5 slices. Spoon remaining marinara over chicken and serve.
