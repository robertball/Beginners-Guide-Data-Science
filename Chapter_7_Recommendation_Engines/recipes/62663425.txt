Title:
Sweet Vanilla Wheat Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/595614.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat flour
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tbsp. baking powder
2 tsp. salt
1/4 cup granulated sugar
4 eggs, beaten
3 cups milk
1 cup low fat vanilla yogurt
2 tsp. pure vanilla extract
1 1/2 sticks butter, melted

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, sift flours, sugar, baking powder and salt. In a separate bowl, mix beaten eggs, milk, yogurt, vanilla and melted butter. Slowly add wet to dry ingredients, whisking until just combined (batter may be slightly lumpy). Allow to rest for 5 minutes, while waffle iron heats. Cook according to waffle iron instructions.

Submitter: 
metread
Description: 
These light and fluffy waffles are perfectly sweetened and moist, with the addition of vanilla yogurt to the batter.
