Title:
Refrigerator Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 1/2 cups warm water
2 pkg yeast
3/4 cup sugar
2 tsp salt
4 eggs
7 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients. Beat with dough hook 4 to 5 minutes. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Dough can be kept up to 3 days.
3 hour before serving remove and roll on well oiled surface. Shape as desired. Let rise until double. Bake 375 degrees, 15 - 20 minutes
