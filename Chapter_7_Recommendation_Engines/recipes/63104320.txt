Title:
Honey Curried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cut up chicken pieces
1/8 cup prepared mustard
1/2 cup honey
1 1/2 tablespoons curry powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (150 degrees C).
Lay chicken pieces in a 9x13 inch baking dish. In a small microwave safe bowl mix together the mustard, honey and curry powder. Microwave on HIGH (full power) for 1 minute.  Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Turn chicken pieces after 1/2 hour of baking. When done, chicken will be a beautiful golden brown.
