Title:
BANANA CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP SUGAR
1 CUP (3) RIPE BANANA'S
1/3 CUP OIL
2 EGSS
1 CUP FLOUR
1 TSP BAKING POWDER
1/2 TSP BAKING SODA
1/2 TSP CINNAMON
1/4 TSP SALT
1/4 CUPPED CHOPPED NUTS (OPTIONAL)
CREAM CHEESE FROSTING
1 PKG (3 OZ) CREAM CHEESE
1 3/ CUP BUTTER (SOFT)
1 TSP VANILLA
2 CUPS POWDERED SUGAR

Directions:
HEAT OVEN TO 350, GREASE 9 X 13 PAN
BEAT SUGAR, BANANA'S, OIL & EGGS IN A BOWL.  STIR IN FLOUR, BAKING POWEDER, BAKING SODA, CINNAMON & SALT. STIR IN NUTS. SPREAD BATTER INTO PAN
BAKE FOR 25-35 MINUTES OR UNTIL TOOTHPICK COMES OUT CLEAN.
COOL COMPLETELY AND PREPARE CREAM CHEESE FROSTING
FROSTING
BEAT CREAM CHEESE, BUTTER & VANILLA UNTIL SOFT. ADDED POWDERED SUGAR. PUT ON TOP 0F CAKE
