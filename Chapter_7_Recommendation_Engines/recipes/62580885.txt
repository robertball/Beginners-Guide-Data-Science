Title:
Divinity Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c sugar
1 c Karo
3/4 cup water
1 tsp vanilla
3 egg whited beaten stiff
1 c nuts

Directions:
Cook all but the egg whites, vanilla & nuts to 255 degrees. Pour in a thin stream into the egg whites while beating slowly, add vanilla. Beat until the candy loses it's gloss. Add nuts & spoon onto wax paper or pour into a glass cake pan.
