Title:
Easy pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of Tyson chunky shredded chicken
1 bag of steamfresh mixed vegetables
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 canister of pilsbury biscuits (recommend small biscuits)

Directions:
Mix together the soup, vegetables, and chicken in baking dish (I use 8 x 8). Put biscuits on top until covered. Put into the oven on 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until biscuits are brown.
