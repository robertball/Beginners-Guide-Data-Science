Title:
Velveeta Ultimate Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Velveeta, cubed
1 can Ro*Tel Tomatoes and Chilies
Cut up vegetables
Wheat Thins
Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Mix 1 lb cubed Velveeta and 1 can Ro*Tel Tomatoes and Chilies in a microwaveable bowl. Microwave for 5 minutes or until Velveeta is completely melted, stirring after 3 minutes. Serve with cut-up vegetables, Wheat Thins Snack Crackers or tortilla chips.
