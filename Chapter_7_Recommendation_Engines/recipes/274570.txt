Title:
Fresh Strawberry Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1050557.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon fresh mint leaves
2 ripe strawberries
1/2 tablespoon agave nectar, or more to taste
3 ounces silver rum
1/4 lime, juiced
1/4 cup lime-flavored sparkling water (such as La CroixÂ®), or as needed
1 cup ice, or as needed

Directions:
Place mint into the bottom of a cocktail shaker and muddle for 15 to 20 seconds. Add strawberries and muddle until juicy. Pour in rum, agave, and lime juice. Add ice and shake.
Pour into a glass and add sparkling water. Stir together using a straw.
