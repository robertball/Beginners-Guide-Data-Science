Title:
Dad's Famous Creamy Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound macaroni shells (or noodles)
1 (26 ounce) can family-sized cream of mushroom soup
3-3/4 cup whole milk
4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cook noodles according to package directions.  Drain.
Mix all ingredients together and cook for 30 minutes, or until cheese is fully melted and bubbly.

Submitter: 
CrissyCK
Description: 
This is my all time favorite creamy macaroni, compliments of my Dad.  There's never any left at a luncheon.
