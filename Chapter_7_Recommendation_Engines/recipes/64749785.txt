Title:
Crispy Chicken Thighs with Roasted Carrots & Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6710033.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken thighs
Kosher salt and black pepper
2 tsp. canola oil
12 oz. small carrots
1 (5.8-oz.) box of olive oil and herb Couscous or any kind of Couscous
1/3 c. chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
1/3 c. roasted salted pepitas
1/4 c. Chopped fresh mint
1 finely chopped garlic clove (or more for the garlic lovers)
3 tbsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. lemon zest

Directions:
1).Preheat oven to 450Â°F. Season chicken thighs with kosher salt and black pepper. Cook, skin side down, in canola oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat until skin is golden brown, 6 to 8 minutes; transfer to a plate.
2).In same skillet, add carrots and season with kosher salt and black pepper; toss to coat. Top with chicken, skin side up. Roast until chicken is cooked through, 8 to 10 minutes.
3).Meanwhile, prepare couscous according to package directions.
4).Toss together chopped parsley and roasted salted pepitas, fresh mint, chopped garlic clove, olive oil, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Season with kosher salt and black pepper. 5).Serve chicken, carrots, and couscous topped with herb mixture.
