Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/334243.jpg
Ingredients:
Eggplant
1 jar tomato sauce
1 cup mozzarella chees
1/4 cup milk
2 eggs
breadcrumbs
Spices: salt, parsley, pepper, oregano, garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Batter eggplant with milk & eggs, and breadcrumbs & spices.
Lightly oil foil.  Cook for 15 minutes on each side (re-oil when flipping).
Reduce temp to 375.  Layer sauce, eggplant, cheese in baking dish.  Bake for 20 minutes.
