Title:
Gyro Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 frozen BOCA Meatless Original Burgers
1/2 cup fat free sour cream
1/4 medium unpeeled cucumber, chopped
1 clove garlic, peeled
4 foldable pita breads
2 plum tomatoes, sliced
1 cup shredded lettuce

Directions:
HEAT burgers as directed on package.
MEANWHILE, place sour cream, cucumber and garlic in blender or food processor container; cover. Blend 10 to 30 seconds or until almost smooth.
COVER pita breads evenly with tomatoes and lettuce; drizzle with sour cream sauce. Top each with 1 burger, cut in half.
