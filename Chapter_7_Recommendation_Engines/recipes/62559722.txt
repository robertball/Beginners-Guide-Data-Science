Title:
Pepper and Egg sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535557.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion- sliced
2 small bell peppers (I used 1 red, 1 green)
2 cups Egg Beaters
Salt & Pepper- to taste
PAM spray

Directions:
Spray PAM in skillet-add onions and peppers. Cook until soft over medium heat-about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add in Egg Beaters stir until cooked through. Serve on rolls, toast, or just eat the mix cold out of the fridge for breakfast.

Description: 
onions, bell peppers, and egg beaters, scrambled and eaten on toast or in a tortilla wrap if you need something on the go.
