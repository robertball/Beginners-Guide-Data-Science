Title:
Crown Roast of Pork with Garlic Mustard Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 pound) 12-rib crown roast of pork
salt and pepper to taste
4 garlic cloves, minced or finely grated
zest of 2 lemons
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
olive oil
coarse salt
freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Combine all ingredients except the pork and stir to blend. Place the roast in a roasting pan; rub with garlic mustard mixture until well covered. Place roast in preheated oven.  Roast for 20 minutes.
Reduce heat to 325 degrees.  Continue roasting meat until the internal temperature of the meat reaches 160 degrees F. Remove from oven, and allow to rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
