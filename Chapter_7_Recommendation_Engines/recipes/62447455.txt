Title:
Potato Swiss Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. potatoes (approximately 5 potatoes)
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
3 tbsp. butter
1 cup shredded swiss cheese
3/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 F and butter a 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
Peel potatoes and slice into thin slices.
Spread half of the potatoes on the bottom of the baking dish and sprinkle with half the salt and pepper. Dot with half of the butter cut into small pieces. Then add another layer with the rest of the potatoes, salt, pepper and butter.
Top with the shredded cheese and pour chicken broth over the top.
Bake until bubbly and golden brown or approximately 40 minutes.  Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Joan Gerland Crabtree
Description: 
I found this recipe years ago in an old church cookbook.  It is now a family favorite.  It is a twist on the typical scalloped or au gratin potatoes.  Try it, you will not be disappointed.
