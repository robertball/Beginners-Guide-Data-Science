Title:
Press Box Vegetable Salsa Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (15.5 ounce) can yellow hominy, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can white hominy, drained
1 (15 ounce) can black-eyed peas, drained
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped, seeded tomato
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
1 (8 ounce) bottle zesty Italian-style salad dressing

Directions:
Mix yellow hominy, white hominy, black-eyed peas, onion, red bell pepper, tomato, and garlic together in a bowl. Stir Italian-style dressing into the mixture. Allow to marinate in refrigerator for 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Mark Lackey
Description: 
Great vegetable-bean dip for any party or appetizer event. Serve with scoop chips or any tortilla chip you like. Enjoy!
