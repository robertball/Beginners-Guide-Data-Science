Title:
High Altitude Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2/3 cups brown sugar
2/3 cups white sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 1/2 cups high altitude flour
1 cup pecans
2 tsp water
2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix sugars and butter well, add eggs, vanilla, baking soda, salt and flour. Stir in water, then add nuts and chocolate chips.
