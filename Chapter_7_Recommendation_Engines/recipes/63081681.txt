Title:
Mushroom Chicken in Sour Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
serve over noodles
I doubled the sauce and mushrooms!

Directions:
Mix the salt, pepper, paprika, onion powder and garlic powder together in a bowl; rub evenly onto all sides of the chicken breasts. Place the seasoned chicken into the bottom of a slow cooker. saute mushrooms in skillet with butter until lightly browned.  mix the cream of mushroom soup, sour cream,  chicken broth, add mushrooms mix and add to the slow cooker. cover  the slow cooker and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. depending if you leave breast whole or cut up

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
Chicken breasts seasoned with paprika, lemon pepper, and garlic powder are slow cooked with white wine, sour cream, mushrooms, and cream of mushroom soup in this easy-to-prepare recipe that cooks while you go about your day.Modified from: Mushroom Chicken in Sour Cream Sauce
