Title:
Marzetti® Red, White and Blue Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/982231.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups green cabbage, coarsely shredded
1/2 cup bacon bits
3/4 cup bleu cheese, crumbled
1 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Cherry tomatoes

Directions:
Combine green cabbage, bacon bits, 1/2 cup bleu cheese.  Add slaw dressing and toss gently to coat well.  Chill to blend flavors.
When you're ready to serve, garnish with cherry tomatoes and another 1/4 cup bleu cheese.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
Not just for Independence Day, this slaw features blue cheese, bacon, and cherry tomatoes. It's a summer sensation.
