Title:
Vegetarian Stuffed Jalapeno Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/912336.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Bell Peppers assorted colors for presentation (I used large Bells)cleaned, cut in half, cleaned out seeds and insides discarded (Approximately 800 grams of peppers)
6 oz Extra Firm Tofu Frozen for 72 hours then boiled for 20 minutes to sort of flash thaw and then shredd
2/3 cup chopped onion
2/3 cup mushrooms finely diced
2 cups wild long grain rice cooked
1/2 cup vegetable marinara spicy sauce
1 cup shredded colby-montery jack cheese
4 fresh Jalapenos cleaned, cut in half, cleaned out seeds and insides discarded.
1/2 cup cottage cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cover cookie sheet with tin foil and spray with non stick cooking spray such as Pam (Set aside)
In a large medium frying pan coated with cooking spray such as pam, place the Shredded tofu the onions, mushrooms, cooked rice, marinara 1/2 cup shredded cheese. Cook on warm just to mix the flavors and heat the mixture and soften the onions
While the mixture is heating prepare the Bell Peppers and Jalapenos Cut each of the Peppers in half, clean out stems and seeds and unnecessary parts of all the peppers.
Place the cleaned out Jalapeno halves aside for now while placing the Bell Pepper halves on the prepared cookie sheet Sprinkle a pinch of remaining cheese into the bottom of the halves approximately 1/4 cup into the bottoms of all total.
Spoon in equal amounts of the rice tofu mixture into each Bell Pepper Half
Bring on the Jalapeno halves and cottage cheese, fill as best you can each half of Jalapeno with cottage cheese after that is done divide the rest of the cottage cheese into each Bell Pepper half mixing does or does not have to be done.
Then, Shove the stuffed Jalapeno into the rice and tofu mixture already in the Bell Peppers (Sprinkle remaining cheese over the tops of all)
Bake uncovered for 30 minutes, lay foil loosely over top of peppers and bake an additional 30 minutes
Serve, Eat, and Enjoy
