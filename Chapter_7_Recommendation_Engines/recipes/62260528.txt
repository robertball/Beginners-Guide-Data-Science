Title:
Swiss Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless, center cut pork chops, trimmed
salt
pepper
prepared mustard
flour
2 Tbsp butter or margerine
1 10 3/4 oz. can cream of chicken or mushroom soup
1/2 can of milk
1 4 oz. can sliced mushrooms
1 cup Swiss cheese, shredded

Directions:
Salt and pepper both sides of pork chops, rubbing seasonings into chops. Spread mustard on each side.
Coat chops in flour. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat. Brown chops in butter. Drain.
Mix soup, milk, and mushrooms. Pour over chops. Simmer, covered, for 30 mins. Sprinkle with cheese. Simmer until cheese melts.
