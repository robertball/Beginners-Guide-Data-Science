Title:
Apple Walnut Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter or margarine
2 squares unsweetened chocolate
2 eggs
1 c. sugar
1 c. sifted cake flour
1/4 t. baking powder
1/4 t. salt
1 c. chopped walnuts
1 c. finely chopped apples
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Melt butter/margarine and chocolate (be careful not to scorch the chocolate!!)
Beat eggs until light and lemon colored.
Add sugar gradually while continuing to beat. Stir in chocolate mixture and beat hard for 1 minute.
Mix flour, baking powder and salt. Stir into the first mixture.
Stir in remaining ingredients. Spoon into a greased 8x8 cake pan, and bake at 350 degrees for 40 mins.
