Title:
Antipasto Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Red Wine Vinegarette:
2/3 cup red wine vinegar
1 clove garlic
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
Salad:
1 lb fusilli pasta
1/2 cup hard salami, cut into strips
1/2 cup pepperoni, cut into strips
1/4 cup provolone cheese, cut into strips
1/4 cup grate Asiago cheese
3 tbsp chopped olives (your choice)
3 tbsp roasted red peppers, cut into strips
1 sm jar marinated artichoke hearts, quartered
1 bunch fresh basil, cut into strips (about 1 1/2 cups)
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
Optional topper:
Toasted seeded Italian bread, cut into cubes

Directions:
Mix ingredients for vinegarette and shake or blend well.
Bring a large pot of salted water to boil over high heat. Add past and cook until tender but still firm to the bite. Drain pasta.
In a large bowl, toss cooked past with remaining salad ingredients. Drizzle with dressing. Add croutons or other toppings as desired.

Submitter: 
Christine Foley Wanner
Description: 
This is a quick and easy side dish. Perfect for a BBQ, potluck or any kind of party. Delicious!
