Title:
asian porkchops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2  large onion cut large dice
3 cloves minced garlic
2 green bell peppers sliced into strips
1 red and/or yellow bell pepper sliced into strips
1/4c hoisin sauce
1/4c soy sauce (low sodium is the best)
3 Tbsp olive oil
4 to 5 pork chops ( 1/2lbs prefered)

Directions:
put all the ingrediants in i large storage container in the refrigerator. so the meat can marinate
Chill for 3-4 hours in the refrigerator
half an hour before you want to start cooking set out the pork chops
Put pork chops on the grill for 5min each side
I sautee my vegtables so i can put it on my pork chops but you can do what you want to do
