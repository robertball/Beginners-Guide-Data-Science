Title:
Nutella Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of butter, soften
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 egg
2/3 cup Nutella
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Mix together flour, cocoa, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Set aside dry ingredients.
Cream together butter, egg, granulated sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
Mix in Nutella and vanilla extract.
Add flour mixture and mix until just blended.
Drop small 1 inch balls of dough on a cookie sheet.
Bake for 10 minutes and cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes.
