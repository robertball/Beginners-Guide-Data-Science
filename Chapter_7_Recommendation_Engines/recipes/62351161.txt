Title:
Slow Cooker Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans with liquid
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, with liquid
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 cups water
2 (14.5 ounce) cans peeled and diced tomatoes
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chile peppers
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
shredded cheese
sour cream
tortilla chips

Directions:
In a medium skillet, cook the ground beef until browned over medium heat. Drain, and set aside.
Place the ground beef, onion, pinto beans, corn, tomato sauce, water, diced tomatoes, green chile peppers and taco seasoning mix in a slow cooker. Mix to blend, and cook on Low setting for 8 hours.

Description: 
Browned ground beef  seasoned with a package of taco seasonings is combined in a slow cooker with canned beans, tomatoes, and green chilies.  Serve with tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Slow Cooker Taco Soup
