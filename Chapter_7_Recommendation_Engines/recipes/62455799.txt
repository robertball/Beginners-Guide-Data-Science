Title:
Fresh Tomato Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup finely diced onion
4 fresh jalapeno peppers, finely chopped
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 teaspoon salt
the juice of i medium lime

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together tomatoes, onion, jalapeno peppers, cilantro, salt, and lime juice. Chill for one hour in the refrigerator before serving.

Submitter: 
Paula S.
Description: 
Finely chopped jalapeno chilies make this salsa nippy and nice. Combine with tomatoes, onions, cilantro and lime juice, and this salsa is ready for dipping.Modified from: Fresh Tomato Salsa
