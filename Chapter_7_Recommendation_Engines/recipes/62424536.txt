Title:
Oven-roasted Baby Potatoes with Creamy Lemon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Creamy Lemon Dressing
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
½ tsp finely grated Lemon Zest
2 tsp fresh Lemon Juice
½ tsp Dijon Mustard
¼ tsp Sugar
1/8 tsp Salt
2 tsp Mayonnaise
Potatoes
1 Lb Red or Yellow Baby Potatoes, halved
2 ¾ tsp Salt
¼ cup Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
1 tsp fresh Lemon Juice

Directions:
For Creamy Lemon Dressing: combine olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, ½ tsp mustard, sugar and salt in small bowl and wish thoroughly until emulsified. Whisk in 2 tsp mayonnaise until dressing is smooth. If making ahead, refrigerate until ready to serve (you may need to rewhisk before serving).
For Potatoes: Preheat oven to 350. Put potatoes and 2 tsp salt in large saucepan and cover with water by at least 1 inch. Bring to a boil, then simmer and cook until tender (20 to 25 minutes). Drain and rinse lightly with cool water. Allow potatoes to cool in refrigerator for at least 10 minutes.
In a large mixing bowl whisk together mayonnaise, 1 Tbsp mustard, and lemon juice. Put potatoes in bowl with mayo mixture and ¾ tsp salt. Toss and coat potatoes with mayo mixture. Spread potatoes on a baking sheet and place in the oven for 15-20 minutes. Lemon dressing can be served in a small bowl on the side or poured over the potatoes. Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Alyse Ellienne
Description: 
Potatoes flavored with creamy lemon dressing and oven-roasted. Lemon gives the recipe a nice, subtle zing.
