Title:
Plachenka (Super Easy Crepes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6065776.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
2 1/8 C Milk
1 1/4 C Flour
1 t Salt
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Put all ingredients into blender and blend until smooth.  Preheat a skillet on medium setting a spray with cooking spray.  Pour a small amount of batter into pan and immediately swirl to cover bottom.  Cook until edges begin to pull away from pan and crepe is slightly browned.  Flip on to plate.  These are great filled with cottage cheese, bananas, strawberries, cinnamon sugar, lemon curd, Nutella or what ever you choose.
