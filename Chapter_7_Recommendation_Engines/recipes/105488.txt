Title:
Creamy Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/8 teaspoon salt
6 tablespoons cold butter, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
Filling:
4 large eggs
1/2 cup half-and-half
1 1/4 cups SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon grated fresh lemon peel
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
Optional garnish: powdered sugar

Directions:
Crust Directions: preheat oven to 350 degrees  F. Lightly spray an 8-x-8-inch baking pan with vegetable cooking spray; set aside.
Combine flour, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and salt in food processor bowl; pulse 2 to 3 times. Add butter; pulse until mixture resembles coarse meal. Press mixture into prepared pan; bake 20 minutes or until crust is lightly browned.
Filling Directions: whisk together eggs and half-and-half in a medium bowl; gradually whisk in SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and flour. Whisk in lemon peel and lemon juice; pour over hot crust. Bake 15 minutes or until center is set and edges are lightly browned. Cool completely in pan on a wire rack; dust with powdered sugar, if desired. Cut into 2-inch squares.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
A crisp, buttery crust forms the base for these creamy lemon treats. Cool completely for easier cutting.
