Title:
Bourbon Glazed Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4-5 pound boneless ham
1/3 cup bourbon, any brand
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 8 oz. can crushed pineapples
1/2 cup honey
2-4 garlic cloves
1 8 oz can whole sliced pineapples
toothpicks

Directions:
Score ham with x's or prick several times with fork.
In a large resealable plastic baggie combine ham, bourbon, brown sugar, crushed pineapples, honey, and garlic and marinate overnight turning occasionally.
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Place ham and marinade in baking dish with 1-2 cups of water if needed.
Secure whole pineapples with toothpicks on ham.
Bake for 1 hour and 30-45 minutes until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees basting occasionally.
