Title:
Quick and Easy Chili Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810070.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans chili with beans
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup chunky salsa
1 (13 ounce) can roast beef, shredded
1 (14.5 ounce) package tortilla chips

Directions:
Place chili, cream cheese, salsa, and roast beef in a large saucepan. Heat slowly, stirring occasionally until the mixture comes to a slow boil. Serve with chips and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Valerie B
Description: 
For an easy, tasty dip to make for a crowd, try this one. Rather than preparing this dip on the stovetop, you could heat it in a slow cooker without fuss.
