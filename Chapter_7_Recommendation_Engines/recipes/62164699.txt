Title:
Banana Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 white cake mix
1 small box of banana instant pudding
3 eggs
walnuts (optional)
1/3 cup oil
1 1/3 cup water
1 1/2 cups milk
1 pkg dream whip
1 small box banana instant pudding

Directions:
mix first 6 ingredients together and pour into well greased bunt pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
When cake is done baking let cool and then chill in refrigerator.
Once chilled start the frosting by mixing milk, 1 box of pudding, and 1 pkg of dream whip together.
Beat till soft peaks form.
Spread all over cake and serve.  Keep in refrigerator when not serving.

Description:
