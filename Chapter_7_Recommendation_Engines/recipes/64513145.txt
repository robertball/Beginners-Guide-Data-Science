Title:
Banana Cookie Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine, softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 cup mashed ripe bananas (about 3)
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. allspice
1/3 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
Glaze:
1/4 cup margarine, softened, but not melted
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
approx. 2 tbsp. boiling water

Directions:
Cake:
Blend margarine and sugar together well.
Add eggs, bananas, milk and vanilla.
Stir in dry ingredients till moistened, don't over-mix.
Bake in greased 10" x 15" jelly roll pan @ 350 x 20 - 25 minutes.
Glaze:
Mix ingredients together until smooth.
Spread over cooled cake.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This large batch of moist (and not too sweet) glazed banana bars is perfect for a potluck contribution.
