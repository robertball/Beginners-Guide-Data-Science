Title:
Fruit Salad Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 cups fresh fruit (strawberry, grapes, blackberry, etc)
3 envelopes unflavored gelatin
3 cups white grape juice
3/4 cup sugar

Directions:
Fill bundt pan with fruit.
Soften gelatin by sprinkling over 1/2 cup grape juice.
Dissolve sugar in 1/2 cup grape juice over medium heat.
Remove from heat and add gelatin mixture. Stir until gelatin is dissolved.
Add remaining grape juice and pour over fruit.
Refrigerate at least 4 hours. Remove from mold and serve.
