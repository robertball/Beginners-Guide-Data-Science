Title:
Chocolate Chip Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 cup oil
3 cups zucchini, grated
1 cup coconut, flaked
2 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In large bowl, beat eggs and sugar.  Add remaining ingredients in order given.  Pour into 2 greased bread loaf pans.
Bake for 1 hour.
