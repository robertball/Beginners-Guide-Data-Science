Title:
Almond Butter and Apple Sandwich Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 slices whole wheat bread
1 tablespoon almond butter, or to taste
1 small apple - peeled, cored and shredded

Directions:
Spread a thin layer of almond butter onto one side of each slice of bread. Place shredded apple onto the peanut butter, and place the other peanut buttered side of bread on top. Serve immediately.
