Title:
Mexican Stuffed Peppers by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6435763.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
Seasoning: lowrys, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin
1/2 medium onion, diced
1-2 cloves garlic
1 can diced tomatoes with chilis (like Rotel)
1 small can diced green chilis
1 can black beans
1 c cooked rice
Package taco seasoning with 1/2 c water
4 large peppers, cut off tops and remove ribs and seeds
1c shredded cheese
Serve with sour cream (mix with lime juice and cilantro)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Prepare peppers by cutting off tops and remove ribs and seeds. Place in covered pan with about 1‚Äù of water.  Bake/steam in oven for 20 minutes until they begin to soften.
Micro-cook rice (instant rice, approx 5 minutes).
Meanwhile, brown turkey with seasonings such as lowrys, garlic powder etc.
When turkey is about half done, add onion and cook until tender and meat is done.
Add garlic, beans, tomatoes, chilis and  cooked rice.
Add taco seasoning and water, simmer on low 3-4 minutes.
Remove peppers from oven and stuff with meat mixture. Bake covered for 30 minutes, until peppers are tender. Add cheese on top and return to oven uncovered for an additional 10 minutes.
Or cut peppers in half and lay out  on baking sheet and fill with meat mixture. Loosely cover with foil and bake 20 minutes until peppers are tender. Add cheese and return to oven for an additional 10 minutes.
Serve with salsa and sour cream (mix in a little lime juice, garlic salt and cilantro).
