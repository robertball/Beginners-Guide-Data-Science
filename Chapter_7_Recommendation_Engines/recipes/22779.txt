Title:
Casey and Leigh's Delightful Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970916.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces cream cheese
1 1/8 cups marshmallow fluff
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1 dash food coloring

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, marshmallow creme, yogurt and food color until smooth.  Serve with fresh fruit.

Submitter: 
MissyR
Description: 
Sweet creamy dip for fresh fruit -- strawberries, cantaloupe, pineapple, apple -- This is a great one for the kids to eat and to make.  My girls love making and serving this to company.
