Title:
My Favorite Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
•	1/2 cup cornstarch
•	¼ cup finely ground hazelnuts or pecans
•	2 tablespoon baking powder
•	1/2 teaspoon salt
•	1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
•	3 eggs
•	2 1/2 cups milk
•	2 teaspoons vanilla
•	3 tablespoons vegetable oil
•	1/3 cup butter, melted
•	3 tablespoons honey
•	2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Directions:
1.	Preheat a waffle iron
2.	In a large bowl, mix flour, cornstarch, ground nuts, baking powder, salt and nutmeg.
3.	In a medium bowl, beat together eggs, milk, vanilla, vegetable oil, butter and honey.
4.	 When mixed well add lemon juice and immediately Wisk vigorously into the flour mixture to form a batter. Don’t over whip just mixed well and set aside 5 minutes
5.	Spray waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray, or lightly brush with oil. Ladle the batter onto preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Steve Fairhurst
Description: 
I added hazelnuts for a nutty flavor, and use a trick I learned, using baking powder and lemon juice - makes them puff up, for a light and crispy, nutty heaven. I like topping with chocolate whipped cream and fresh strawberriesModified from: World's Best Waffles
