Title:
Warm Italiano Spread (Kraft) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444444.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (8 oz.) Neufchatel Cheese
1/4 cup  Pesto
1 Plum tomato or red bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup Finely Shredded Italian* Five Cheese Blend TRISCUIT Cracked Pepper & Olive Oil Crackers
You can subsitute the tomato for bell pepper or jalapeno if you like some heat!

Directions:
HEAT grill to medium heat. Place unwrapped Neufchatel cheese on 8-inch square sheet of heavy-duty foil; top with pesto, tomatoes or pepper and shredded cheese.
PLACE foil on grill; cover with lid. Grill 8 to 10 min. or until shredded cheese is melted and Neufchatel is softened, but still holding its shape.
TRANSFER foil to platter. Serve spread with crackers
