Title:
Chicken Pomodora Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken cutlets, pounded to 1/4 inch thick
2 Tbsp. oil
1/4 C vodka
1/2 C. low-sodium chicken broth
2 Tbsp. fresh lemmon juice
1/2 C. tomatoes, chopped small
2 T. heavy cream
1/3 C. scallions, minced

Directions:
1. Pound the chicken breast in a zip-lock bag to 1/4 in thickness. Season the cutlets with salt and pepper and dust with flour.
2. Saute cutlets in oil. Transfer to a platter to keep warm.
3.  Deglaze pan with vodka away from heat and cook until vodka is nearly gone.  Add broth and lemon juice.  Return cutlets to pan and cook each side 1 minute. Transfer to warm plate.
4. Finish the sauce with tomatoes and cream.  Heat through, then pour over cutlets.
Garnish with scallions

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A gorgeous presentation and a delightful fresh flavor.  Be sure to deglaze with the vodka off heat.
