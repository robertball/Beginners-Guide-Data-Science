Title:
Vegan Kitchen Sink Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
2 large cloves garlic
1 C chopped onion
1 C chopped carrots
1C chopped celery
2 C chopped spinach
6 red potatoes - cubed
2 cans vegetable broth
1 6 oz. can tomato paste
1 12 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 Can Cannalini beans
6 oz. Silken tofu
1/4 C Nutritional Yeast
1 t. liquid smoke
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
2 t. oregano flakes
2 t. basil flakes
1 t. crushed rosemary

Directions:
Heat a large soup pot and add the olive oil and crushed garlic.  Add the onions, carrots, and celery and cook till onions are translucent.
Pour in the tomatoes, tomato paste, and 1 can of broth.
Put silken tofu, nutritional yeast, and 1 can of broth in the blender and process until smooth.  Add this mixture to the soup pot and stir.
Add the potatoes, spinach, and seasonings.  Let simmer for about 30 minutes. Adjust seasonings as needed, and serve with crusty bread.  This tastes even better the second day.
