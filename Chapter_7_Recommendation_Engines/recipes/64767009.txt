Title:
Nanny's Sour Cream Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 cup of shortening
3 cups of sugar
6 eggs
3 cups of plain flour, sifted
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp butter & nut flavoring

Directions:
Mix sour cream and shortening together.  Slowly add sugar.  Add one egg at a time, mixing thoroughly after each one.  Add flour, baking soda, salt and flavoring.
Bake at 325 for 1 hour and 10 minutes.
Let cool.

Carrie Sumner
Description: 
Southern and sweet, just like Nanny!
