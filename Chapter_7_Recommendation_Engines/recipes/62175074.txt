Title:
Pecan Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups AP flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cream of tartar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
1 cup Fisher pecan cookie pieces (really fine chop)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
First bowl, combine: flour, baking soda, cream of tartar and salt.
Second bowl: cream together butter, oil, sugars, egg and vanilla. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in pecans by hand.
Make 1 Tbsp balls of dough, place 2" apart on cookie sheet. Flatten cookie balls with the bottom of a glass dipped in sugar.
Bake 10-12 minutes, cool 3 minutes on sheet then remove to cooking rack. Will not be brown, and that's good!

Submitter: 
Lisa Provost
Description: 
Kind of like a pecan sandie. Pretty good - very crumbly.
