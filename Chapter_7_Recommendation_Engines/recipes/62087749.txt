Title:
The best beef stew you'll ever taste! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Stew Meat
2 tbsp olive oil
1 bag baby carrots
8 medium potatoes
1 onion
2 cloves garlic
2 beef bouillon cubes
1 1/2 cup Ketchup
Salt / Pepper to taste
1 15oz can sweet peas (drained)
6-8 cups water
4 tbsp cornstarch
4 tbsp water

Directions:
In a large pot, heat olive oil and fry the stew meat, onion, garlic, salt and pepper. Do not drain.
Add water, carrots, ketchup, beef bouillon cubes and more salt and pepper to taste. Should be a reddish brown. If not, add a little more ketchup.
Bring to a boil and then simmer on medium to low heat for an hour or until carrots are starting to get soft.
Add potatoes and cook for another hour or until all vegetables are to the desired tenderness, then pour in the peas.
While peas are cooking, bring the stew to a boil.
Mix cornstarch with 4 Tbsps COLD water and mix into the stew to thicken it.

Submitter: 
Becky Mader Wisniewski
Description: 
This beef stew combines beef, carrots, potatoes, onions and peas and slowly cooked to perfection with beef bouillon and a bit of ketchup!
