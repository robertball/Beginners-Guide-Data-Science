Title:
Lime Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg lime koolaid
2 cups real lemon
1 large can unsweetened pineapple juice
2 cups white sugar
2 cups water
2 large (750 ml) ginger ale

Directions:
Mix the first five ingredients together in large punch bowl. Mix in gingerale and some ice cubes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
A nice refreshing drink. The green colour makes it great for St. Patrick's Day.
