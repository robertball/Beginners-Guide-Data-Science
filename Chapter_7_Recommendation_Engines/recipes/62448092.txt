Title:
Paprika Chicken & Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/896230.jpg
Ingredients:
½	cup all purpose flour
2	teaspoons paprika
1	teaspoon ground black pepper
1	teaspoon garlic powder
1	teaspoon cayenne pepper
4	boneless skinless chicken breasts
3	tablespoons butter or margarine
2       tablespoons EVOO
1       cup chicken broth, divided
1	can Campbell’s condensed cream of chicken soup
¼	cup sliced green onions

Directions:
1.	MIX flour, paprika, garlic powder, black pepper and red pepper on plate. Coat chicken. in a small bowl combine half the broth, soup and green onions; set aside.
2.	Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil, and 1 tablespoon butter in skillet over medium heat.  Cook 1/2 chicken 10 minutes or until brown.  Set chicken aside. repeat with remaining chicken.
3.	Add remaining broth stir and cook for 1 minute. stir in soup and green onions.  Bring to a boil.  Return chicken to pan. Cover and cook on low heat for 10 minutes or until chicken is done.   and serve
