Title:
All American Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 T flour
1 t cinnamon
Dash of nutmeg
6 cups peeled, cored and thinly sliced cooking apples
1 ready made pie crust
1 T butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Combine the sugar, flour, sugar cinnamon and nutmeg.
Add sugar mixture to the sliced apples and toss to coat.
Line a 9 inch pie plate with the piecrust, and fill it with the apple mixture. Dot the mixture with butter.
Add the top crust and pinch the edges.
Cover the edges of the pie with foil and back for 40-45 minutes.
