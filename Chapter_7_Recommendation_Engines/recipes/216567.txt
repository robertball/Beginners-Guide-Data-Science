Title:
Hot and Creamy Swiss Almond Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988009.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (250 g) package Philadelphia Brick Cream Cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups shredded Kraft Swiss Cheese
1/3 cup Miracle Whip Dressing
1 green onion, finely chopped
1/3 cup sliced almonds, toasted

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix all ingredients except nuts in ovenproof serving dish or pie plate.
Bake 15 min., stirring after 8 min.
Top with nuts.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia Cream Cheese
Description: 
A hot and bubbly cheese spread--nobody will believe it's just PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, Swiss cheese and MIRACLE WHIP. This one is a Kraft Kitchens favourite.
