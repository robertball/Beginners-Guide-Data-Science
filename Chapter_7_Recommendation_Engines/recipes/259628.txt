Title:
Stars and Flowers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4548559.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (7 inch) whole wheat tortillas
2 slices Cheddar cheese, or to taste
2 thin slices ham, or to taste
2 tablespoons pimento cheese spread, or to taste
4 celery sticks, or to taste
4 baby carrots, or to taste
5 small black olives
2 tablespoons mixed nuts with sweetened cranberries (such as CraisinsÂ®)

Directions:
Cut tortillas and Cheddar cheese into star and flower shapes using cookie cutters. Place ham between tortilla and cheese shapes to make sandwiches.
Spread pimento cheese over celery sticks and cut into smaller pieces.
Assemble bento box with sandwiches, celery pieces, baby carrots, black olives, and mixed nuts.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Stars and flowers are the hit of this bento lunch with the added plush toy. What child doesn't like putting black olives on the ends of their fingers? This will make the little ones happy.
