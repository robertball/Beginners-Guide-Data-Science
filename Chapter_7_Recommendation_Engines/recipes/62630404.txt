Title:
Blueberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 T cornstarch
4 cups blueberries
1 t lemon juice
1 cup flour
1 T sugar
1 1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
3 T shortening
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Oven to 400 = 2 quart casserole bowl, ungreased
Blend 1/2 cup sugar and cornstarch in saucepan
Stir in blueberries and lemon juice
Cook, stirring constantly, until mix thickens and boils
Boil for 1 minute then pour into casserole and place in oven while preparing topping
Combine flour, 1 T sugar, baking powder and salt into bowl
Add shortening and milk
Cut through shorteing 6 times, then mix until dough forms a ball
You should have enough dough for 6 large spoonfuls
Drop onto hot fruit
Return to oven and bake, uncovered, for 25 to 30 minutes or until topping is golden brown
Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream
