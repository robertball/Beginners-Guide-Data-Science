Title:
Marge's Angel Food/Jello Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 angel food cake, torn or cut into pieces
1 large raspberry jello mix, mix according to directions less 1/4 c water - refrigerate @ 30 minutes
8 oz cool whip
Topping...
Raspberries
Cool whip

Directions:
After jello mixture has been refrigerated @ 30 minutes, fold all ingredients together.
Spread in 9 x 13 pan.
Refrigerate at least two hours and serve with extra raspberries and cool whip.
