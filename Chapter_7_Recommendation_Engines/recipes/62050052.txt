Title:
Butter Cream Bon Bons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks of soft Butter
2 boxes of powder sugar
small amount of milk
6 sqs. chocolate
1/2 block parafin wax (grated)

Directions:
Cream together butter and powder sugar, add milk a little at a time until it forms a ball, be sure it's not too dry. Form into one inch balls and chill for  2-3 hours.
Melt in double boiler chocolate and parafin.
Dip balls in melted chocolate and cool.
Varations:
Chocolate Buter Creams
Add 7 Tabls.  of cocoa powder to powder sugar mix.
Peanut Butter
Add 9 Tabls. peanut butter to cream mixture.
