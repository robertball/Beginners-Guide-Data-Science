Title:
Waffle Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2	cups milk
1/3	cup sugar
2	tbsp cornstarch
1	egg
1/3	cup milk
Vanilla
Butter

Directions:
Sprinkle sugar onto milk and heat on medium heat.
DO NOT STIR TILL MILK IS HOT enough to add cornstarch mixture.
Beat cornstarch, egg & milk together and add to hot milk.
Add butter & vanilla last.
Stir over low heat until sauce is thick enough to coat a spoon.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
Aunt Marge's waffle sauce recipe. Not too sweet. Just right.
