Title:
Rempeyek Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472181.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 4 Garlic
2. 4 Candle nut (Kemiri)
3. 1 Tsp ground Coriander (Ketumbar
4. 1 Egg
5. 50 cc Coconut Milk( Mix with spice) +
5a 250 for later use
6 Salt
7 Peanut - Raw Spanish nut, cut in halve
8. 200 gr. rice flour
9. 1 Tsp tapioca

Directions:
1. Mix flour and blend spice from 1-6.
2. Add 250 cc light waterly coconut milk

Submitter: 
JFKR
Description: 
Rempeyek / Peyek ini bisa di isi apa aja, mulai kedelai sampai teri. Ada juga yang ditambah irisan daun jeruk biar harum.

Bisa tahan tetap krispi selama seminggu kalo di simpan di baskom kedap udara.

You can keep this crackers for a week in an air tight plastic ware. 

You can substitute peanut with soy or shrimps or dried anchovy.
