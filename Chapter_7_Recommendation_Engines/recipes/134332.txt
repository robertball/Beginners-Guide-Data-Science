Title:
Piscola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6369628.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cubes ice
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger pisco
3 ounces cola, or to taste
1 slice lime

Directions:
Fill a highball glass with ice, pour in pisco, then fill with cola. Garnish with a slice of lime.

Submitter: 
damasio
Description: 
Here's the Chilean version of rum and cola, made with pisco, the national liquor. Call it a 'Chile Libre'!
