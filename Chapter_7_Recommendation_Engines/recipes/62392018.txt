Title:
Easy Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 16oz cans refried beans
2 4oz cans green chilies (you can use jalapenos if you want it hotter)
8 oz shredded cheddarcheese
8 oz sour cream
1/2 tsp cilantro

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and microwave for 1 minute.  Stir, and keep microwaving in 30 second increments until cheese is melted.  Serve with chips.

Submitter: 
CANDUS_P
Description: 
A yummy and quick bean dip that you can make in minutes!  I serve it with homemade tortilla chips, but the ones from the bag are fine.
