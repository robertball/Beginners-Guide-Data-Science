Title:
Applesauce Raisin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 extra large egg
1/2 cup melted butter or coconut butter
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
2 cups flour
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup raisins
1/2 cup crushed walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Use muffin liners or spray the muffin tin with nonstick spray
Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
Melt butter in small bowl
Beat egg into melted butter and pour into the dry mix
Mix in the applesauce
Add crushed walnuts
Mix until it reaches a doughy batter consistency
Using an Ice cream scooper, scoop batter into muffin liners
Sprinkle extra sugar and ground cinnamon on top of the muffins
Bake for 18-20 minutes
Let cool for about 5 mins
