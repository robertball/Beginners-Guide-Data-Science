Title:
Fruit and Oat Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup uncooked quick oats
1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted
3/4 cup apricot, cherry or other fruit preserves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degree. Spray a 9-inch square baking pan with non-stick cooking spray and set aside.
Combine flour, oats, brown sugar, baking soda, salt and cinnamon in a medium bowl; mix well. Add margarine; mix with fork until mixture is crumbly.  Reserve 3/4 cup crumb mixture for topping.  Press remaining crumb mixture evenly onto bottom of prepared pan.  Bake 5 to 7 minutes or until lightly browned. Spread preserves onto crust; sprinkle with reserve crumb mixture.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Cut into 16 squares.

Description: 
A quick, tasty treat for kids which freeze really well making it an ideal snack for a packed lunch.
