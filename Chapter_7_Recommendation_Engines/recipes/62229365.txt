Title:
Pakistani Curried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2   tablespoons   olive oil
1   large         onion chopped
4   tablespoons   curry powder mix or more to taste
1   tablespoons   chicken bouillon powder or 1 cube
salt and pepper to taste
1/8 teaspoon      cayenne or more to taste
1 lbs  fresh tomatoes chopped or a 14 oz can diced
2 - 2.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs cut in half
1   28 oz can tomato sauce
3   cloves garlic chopped
1/2 can  water to rinse tomato sauce can

Directions:
Saut√© onions in oil until translucent over medium heat.
Add the curry powder, salt, pepper, cayenne and chicken bouillon powder or cube. Stir for about 30 seconds to mix spices.
Add fresh tomatoes or can of diced tomatoes. Add chicken and cook for 5 minutes.
Add tomato sauce, water, bay leaves and garlic. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer covered for 30 minutes.
Remove lid and continue to slowly simmer another 40 minutes until sauce has reduced and thickened.
Remove Bay Leaves and serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Scook
Description: 
Boneless skinless pieces of chicken slowly cooked in a tomato and curry sauce delicious over rice. Can be as spicy as you would like. Everyone of all ages loves this recipe even most people who say they don't like curry.
