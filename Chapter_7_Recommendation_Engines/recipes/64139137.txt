Title:
Mango Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 3/4 oz can Mango
1 8oz package cream cheese
2 3oz box orange flavored gelatin
1 3oz box lemon flavored gelatin
2 cup boiling water

Directions:
Put Mango and cream cheese in a blender until smooth. Dissolve gelatin in hot water. Add mango and cheese. Chill.
