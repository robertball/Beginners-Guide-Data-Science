Title:
Strawberries and Cream Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5 cups Almond Vanilla Milk
1 cup strawberries
Dash salt
2 cups oats
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
4 tablespoons ground flax
2 tablespoons maple syrup

Directions:
Bring almond milk, frozen strawberries and salt to a low boil, then add oats. Stir frequently. After a few minutes, add cinnamon, nutmeg and flax and continue cooking until desired consistency. Stir in maple syrup.
