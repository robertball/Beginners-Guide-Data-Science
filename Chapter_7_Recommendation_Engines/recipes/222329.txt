Title:
Refreshing Mango Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4508997.jpg
Ingredients:
1 red onion, chopped
1 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
1 medium tomato, chopped
2 sprigs fresh cilantro, chopped, or more to taste

Directions:
Mix red onion, mango, tomato, and cilantro together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Elliot Porter
Description: 
Great topping for schnitzel (fried chicken cutlet) ! Adds a sweet and spicy flavor.
