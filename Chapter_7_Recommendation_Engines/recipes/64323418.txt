Title:
Millet Stuffing with Cranberries to use with Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups COOKED millet
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped dried cranberries
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 small yellow onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 cup vegetable broth
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp all purpose seasoning
1 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Cook onions in a frying pan over medium heat for 7-10 minutes (use water to saute)
Add garlic and celery and cook for 3-5 more minutes
Add cranberries, walnuts, thyme, all purpose seasoning, sea salt and black pepper and continue stirring for a few more minutes
Add millet and broth and cook for 5 more minutes, or until liquid is absorbed
Makes enough for 3 Delicata Squash. Once squashes are cooked, flip over and stuff with millet mix. Or use as a side dish alone.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
A gluten-free pilaf great for stuffing squash or as a side.
