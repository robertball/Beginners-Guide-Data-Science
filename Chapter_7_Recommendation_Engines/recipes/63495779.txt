Title:
Lemon Coconut cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954029.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C.  Butter  (softened)
1 C.  Granulated sugar
1 C. Medium shred coconut
3 Lemons (juice and zest)
1 C. Quick Oats
1 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Ginger
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
In 2 small bowls obtain the juice and zest from the lemons and set aside
In a large mixing bowl add butter and sugar and cream together until soft and smooth
Add the zest to the creamed mixture and stir until well combined
Slowly add the juice to the mixture (some juice will not be absorbed right away)
Add the dry ingredients stirring until most of the lemon juice is absorbed by the flour
Stir in the quick oats and make a smooth drop dough
On a large cookie sheet covered with parchment paper drop dough by teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart
Put in oven for 7 to 9 minutes when cookies are lightly browned on top
Remove cookies from oven and let cool in pan for a few minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very tangy lemon flavour with the chewy coconut to make a very healthy cookie for a snack or lunchtime.
