Title:
Pumpkin and Bitter Melon Tuna Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 pc kabocha pumpkin
1 pc bitter melon
1 tomato
1 small can of tuna
1/4 pc yellow onion
1 Tbsp lemon juice
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp water
salt

Directions:
Drain the canned tuna
Dice the tomato and onion then put in one bowl.
Combine 1/4 tsp of salt, lemon juice and olive oil, mix then pour it to the bowl with the tomato and onion. Put in fridge until use later after steaming the vegetables.
Cut the bitter melon along its length. Take the seeds at its center using a spoon. Cut along its width thinly then soak in water with salt. Drain before using.
Slice the kabocha thinly.
Put the kabocha and bitter melon inside the microwave pressure cooker. Add 1 Tbsp of water then cover. Microwave for 5 minutes using 600W. Keep covered and wait for the pressure to be released before opening.
Place the vegetables in a serving plate then add the salsa sauce and tuna on the side.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This is a refreshing and delicious dish. A colorful and healthy salad which can be served as lunch or side dish.
