Title:
Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1 cup uncooked rice
1 cup milk
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2 tsp salt
1 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp pepper
3 tbsp cooking oil or drippings
2 15 oz cans tomato sauce
2 cups water
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix beef, rice, milk, onion, and seasonings and form into 16 medium balls.
Brown on all sides in oil over medium heat.
Add tomato sauce, water and Worchestershire to pan; stir and bring to simmer.
Simmer over low heat for 45 minutes, covered.  If water cooks down too much add a little more.

Submitter: 
Jo Bice
Description: 
I got this recipe from Betty Crocker's Cooking for Two cookbook when I got married in 1971.  My kids still request porcupines!  Obviously, I've had to change the quantities from the original!
