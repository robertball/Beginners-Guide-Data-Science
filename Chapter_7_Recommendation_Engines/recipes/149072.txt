Title:
Baked Cinnamon French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982792.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs, slightly beaten
2/3 cup fat-free milk or evaporated fat-free milk
2 tablespoons sugar, divided
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
3/4 teaspoon cinnamon, divided
8 (1 inch thick) slices French or Italian bread (about 3- to 4-inches in diameter)
1 cup Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal
Topping:
2 medium bananas, sliced
1/2 cup maple syrup, warmed

Directions:
In shallow dish stir together eggs, milk, 1 tablespoon of the sugar, vanilla and 1/2 teaspoon of the cinnamon. Dip bread slices into egg mixture, turning to generously coat both sides. Place on 15 x 10 x 1-inch baking pan coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 450 degrees F about 11 minutes or until light brown.
Meanwhile, in small bowl stir together remaining 1 tablespoon sugar and remaining 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon.
Turn bread slices. Sprinkle with Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Lightly press cereal into bread. Sprinkle sugar-cinnamon mixture over cereal. Bake at 450 degrees F about 5 minutes more or until light brown.
Top each slice with banana slices. Serve with syrup.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
This easy, baked French toast is topped with Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® for a nice crunch, then is served with sliced bananas and warm syrup.
