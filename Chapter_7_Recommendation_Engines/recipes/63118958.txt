Title:
Chicken, Broccoli, and Rigatoni with a Creamy White Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 - 1 lb quality, Italian rigatoni
2 tbsp olive oil
400g chicken breasts, boneless & skinless, cut into strips or thinly pounded
flour for dusting chicken
2 cups broccoli florets
2 tsp garlic, chopped
2 lemon slices
1 cup creme fraiche (or reduced fat creme fraiche)
1 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 - 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring a pot full of water to a rapid boil. Cook rigatoni according to package instructions.
Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet.
Dust chicken with flour and cook until golden on both sides.
Add broccoli florets, garlic and lemon slices. Stir and cook for 1 minute.
Turn up the heat and add the wine. Cook for 1-2 minutes then add the chicken broth and creme fraiche.  Cook until liquid is reduced by half (about 2 minutes). Season to taste.
Add cooked and strained rigatoni to sauce, toss until well mixed and serve at once, reserving lemon slices as garnishes.  Serve with freshly grated parmesan cheese.
