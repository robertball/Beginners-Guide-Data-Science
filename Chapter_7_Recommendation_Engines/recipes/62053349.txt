Title:
Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground round,
1 onion, diced,
3 tsp. canola oil,
6 cups cooked cabbage,
1 can tomato soup,
1 sleeve of cracker crumbs,
1 cup grated cheese.

Directions:
Cook beef, onion, and oil together, until meat is brown and crumbly; drain.  Put layer of cabbage in a 9x9 baking dish.  Add layer of meat, can of soup, cracker crumbs, then cheese.  BAke at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe that the whole family can enjoy.  I even have requests to bring it to family gatherings.
