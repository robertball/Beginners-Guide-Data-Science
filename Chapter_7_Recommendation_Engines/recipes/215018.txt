Title:
Speedy Chihuahua Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1313121.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lime wedges
kosher salt for rimming glasses
ice cubes
4 fluid ounces white tequila
1 fluid ounce orange flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
2 limes, juiced
8 fluid ounces grapefruit juice
3 splashes club soda

Directions:
Rub a lime wedge around the rims of 3 margarita glasses, and dip each glass rim into salt. Place ice cubes into each glass.
Mix together the tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice, and grapefruit juice into a pitcher, and pour into the glasses over the ice. Add 1 splash of club soda to each glass, and serve.

Submitter: 
KIERSA
Description: 
A refreshing mix between a margarita and a greyhound. Not too sweet, and oh-so refreshing! Try experimenting with different grapefruit juices -- Ruby Red, fresh-squeezed, and Simply Grapefruit® all work great!
