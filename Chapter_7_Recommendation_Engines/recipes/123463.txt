Title:
BLT Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995231.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded lettuce
1 1/2 cups diced fresh tomatoes
12 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/8 teaspoon pepper
4 (10 inch) flour tortillas, warmed

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first five ingredients; mix well. Spoon down the center of each tortilla; fold the ends and sides over filling and roll up. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Dorothy  Anderson
Description: 
'This fun variation on the traditional bacon, lettuce and tomato sandwich is a breeze to fix,' assures Dorothy Anderson, Ottawa, Kansas. 'Flour tortillas make them easy to eat on the run.'
