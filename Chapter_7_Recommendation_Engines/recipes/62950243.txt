Title:
Pecan Crusted Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 (4 oz) salmon fillets
2 cup pecans
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet on medium heat.
Place pecans, garlic powder & pepper in food processor, grind until fine.
Roll salmon in pecan powder.
Saute in oil until done, about 5-6 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
limegreendog
Description: 
A breaded fish dish with out the breading. This has a nice crunch to it.
