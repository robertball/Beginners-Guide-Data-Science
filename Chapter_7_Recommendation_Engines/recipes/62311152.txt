Title:
Italian Spaghetti Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1 pound lean ground beef
1 cup fresh bread crumbs
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup of milk
SAUCE
3/4 cup chopped onion
5 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 (28 ounce) can of crushed tomato
1 (28 ounce) can of tomato puree
1 (8 ounce) can of tomato sauce
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons white sugar
2 bay leafs
3/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.  Stir in tomatoes, salt, sugar and bay leaf. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 90 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, milk, parsley, Parmesan, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, garlic powder and beaten egg.  Mix well and form into balls. Place on baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes. When meatballs are done stir in  basil, 1/2 teaspoon pepper and meatballs and simmer 30 minutes more.  Serve.
