Title:
Homemade Chicken Nuggets with Honey Mustard Dipping sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless/skinless chicken breasts
2 cups Flour
1/2 cup seasoned salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup water
1 cup vegetable oil
Honey Mustard Sauce
1 cup honey
1/2 cup spicy mustard

Directions:
1. Wash chicken, pat dry.  Cut breasts into 1 to 2 inch bite size pieces.
2. In a large skillet heat oil over medium heat.
3. Mix flour and seasoned salt. In a separate bowl, crack eggs and stir with a fork until well blended. Add 1/2 cup water and stir until well blended.
4. With a fork dredge chicken pieces through flour mixture, then egg mixture, and back through flour mixture.
5. Add coated pieces to hot oil and cook about 3 mintues per side. Work in batches of about 12, depending on your skillet size.  Drain on paper towels.
6. For dipping sauce, mix together honey and mustard until well blended.
