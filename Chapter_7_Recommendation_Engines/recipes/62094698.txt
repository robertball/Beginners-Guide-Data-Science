Title:
Chicken Scampi and Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
WHITE SAUCE:
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoons flour
3/4 cup milk, hot
SCAMPI SAUCE
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons of minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 teaspoon dried cilantro
1 1/2 teaspoons oregano
1 teaspoon basil
2 teaspoons of black pepper
3/4 cup white wine
1 cup chicken broth
prepared white sauce
REMAINING INGREDIENTS
1 lb linguine, cooked and drained
1 red onion chopped in small pieces
10-12 skinless chicken tenders
olive oil

Directions:
1. For white sauce: heat 1 tablespoon of butter in sauce pan, when melted add hot milk and then add flour slowly while stirring. Milk must be hot and flour must be added last to ensure sauce does not get lumpy.
2. For scampi sauce: Heat butter over low heat. In seperate bowl mix dry ingredients of garlic, red pepper, cilantro, oregano, basil and black pepper then add to melted butter in saucepan. Cook together for about a minute then add wine and chicken broth. Stir well then allow to come to rolling boil. Simmer for 20 minutes. Add white sauce and cook till slightly thickened.
3. In large skillet saute chicken tenders in olive oil till nearly done. Add onions and saute for 2 minutes. Add sauce to skillet and cook on med-low for 30-40 minutes so flavor permeates chicken.
4. Pour entire contents of skillet over cooked and drained linguine and toss together well.

Submitter: 
Malina
Description: 
Flavorful and tender chicken cooked in a light garlic butter sauce full of flavor and served tossed with linguine.
