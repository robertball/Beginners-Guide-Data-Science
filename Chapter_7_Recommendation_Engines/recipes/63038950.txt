Title:
Pan Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small red potatoes, quartered
1/2 lb. parsnips, quartered
3 celery stalks, cut into 3" pieces
4 large carrots, cut into 2" chunks
4 small onions, 1 chopped, the rest quartered
4 garlic cloves, unpeeled
2-3 Tbsp olive oil
Salt, pepper
1/4 tsp thyme
2 bay leaves
1/4 tsp dill weed
4 parsley sprigs

Directions:
Cup up vegetables; place in a bowl with the oil and garlic. Toss.
Season with salt, pepper, half of the thyme and dill.
Put 2-3 Tbsp water and 1 Tbsp oil in the bottom of a roasting pan. Scatter chopped onion ovr the pan with the rest of the herbs, bay leaves, and parsley. Place vegetables in the pan; cover.
Cover and bake 30 minutes at 425 degrees. Turn the vegetables.
Bake 20 minutes longer and check for doneness.
Uncover and roast 10 minutes longer or until done.
