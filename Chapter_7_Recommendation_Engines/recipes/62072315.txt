Title:
Bromley Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 lb bag red potatos
1 jar of mayonaisse
1 bunch of fresh parsley chopped thin
3 large red onions chopped thin
1 head of celery chopped thin
1/4 cup of half and half
salt and pepper

Directions:
Boil,cook and cool potatoes. Refrigerate 1 hour. In a bowl mix mayonaisse, parsley, onion, celery, and whisk in half and half.  Cut up potatoes into 12 cuts and stir.
