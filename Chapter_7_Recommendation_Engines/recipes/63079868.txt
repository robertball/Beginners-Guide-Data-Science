Title:
Death By Chocolate Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg. (16 sqs.) semi-sweet baking chocolate
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup flour
1/4 tsp. baking powder
2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Coarsely chop 8 squares of chocolate; set aside
Microwave remaining 8 squares chocolate in bowl 1-2 min.
Stir until choc. is melted and smooth.  Stir in sugar, butter, eggs and vanilla.
Stir in flour and baking powder.
Stir in reserve chocolate and nuts.
Drop by 1/4 cupfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet
Bake 12-13 min. or until cookies are puffed
Cool on cookie sheet 1 min.  Transfer to wire rack or foil to cool completely
