Title:
Best Guacamole Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 avocados
6 Tablespoons, 2 teaspoons cilantro, chopped
6 Tablespoons, 2 teaspoons red onions, chopped
1 Tablespoon, 1 teaspoon jalapenos, diced
1 Tablespoon, 1 teaspoon Lemon-lime juice
1/2 Tablespoon Kosher salt
Tomatoes, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Mash peeled/cored avocados with lemon-lime juice.  Add remaining ingredients and stir well.
