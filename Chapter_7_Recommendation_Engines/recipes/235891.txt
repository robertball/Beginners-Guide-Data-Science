Title:
Auntie's Chocolate Chip Cookies with Truvia速 Baking Blend Recipe

Ingredients:
1 1/8 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup Truvia速 Baking Blend, plus
2 tablespoons Truvia速 Baking Blend
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 large egg
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 tablespoon molasses* (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine flour, salt and baking soda in a small bowl.
Beat butter, Truvia(R) Baking Blend and vanilla (and optional molasses) in a separate large mixing bowl. Mix in egg.
Gradually stir in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop rounded tablespoon on cookie sheets and press down to slightly flatten.
Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until lightly browned.
