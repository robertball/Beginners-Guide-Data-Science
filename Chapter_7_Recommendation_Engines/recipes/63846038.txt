Title:
Pumpkin Pecan Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FILLING
1 15 oz. can pumpkin
1 12 oz. can evaporated milk
3 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
4 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
TOPPING
1 box vanilla cake mix
1 stick salted butter, melted
2 cups toasted pecans, chopped

Directions:
Preheat often to 375ยบ.
Whisk together all the filling ingredients until smooth. Pour into a greased 9 x 13 pan.
Sprinkle entire box of cake mix evenly over the filling. Sprinkle pecans evenly over cake mix, and carefully pour melted butter evenly over the entire thing.
Bake at 375ยบ for about an hour, or until edges are a deep golden brown.

This recipe combines the flavors of pumpkin pie, pecan pie, and cake all in one dessert. It also travels well, so it's the perfect holiday recipe for sharing!
