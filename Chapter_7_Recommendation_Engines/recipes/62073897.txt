Title:
Avocado Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of milk
4 large ripe avocados, halved, peeled and pitted
1/2 cup of sugar
4 cups of ice
whipped cream

Directions:
1. Put all ingredients into blender.
2. Blend on medium for 3-5 minutes, until mixture becomes smooth.
3. Pour into glasses and serve with whipped cream on top.
If there are any left over, simply put into your freezer and microwave for 15-30 seconds when served.

Submitter: 
FOODFORYOUFOODFORME
Description: 
When I first entered this coffee shop with some friends, they suggested the Avocado milkshake.  It had sounded at gross at first but I found that it was actually very sweet, smooth and creamy. Don't pass this smoothie up.
