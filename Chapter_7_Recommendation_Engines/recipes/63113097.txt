Title:
Helene's BBQ ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 slabs babyback ribs
lemon juice
garlic juice
black pepper
lemon pepper
garlic salt
onion powder
Texene chili powder
1 bottle Kraft Hickory BBQ sauce
1 bottle Woody's Bourbon BBQ sauce
4-5 dashes worchestire sauce
1/4 -1/2 c. ketchup
2 drops liquid smoke
3-4 dashes tabasco

Directions:
Parboil ribs for 2-3 hours in water, lemon juice, black pepper, and garlic juice.
Sprinkle ribs, front and back, with lemon pepper, garlic salt, onion powder, and Texene.
Combine both BBQ sauces, Worchestire, ketchup, liquid smoke, tabasco, 2-3 dashes lemon juice.
Pour over ribs, wrap in foil, and marinate 12-24 hours.
Grill ribs 4-5 minutes each side, or until slightly charred.
