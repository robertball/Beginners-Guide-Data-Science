Title:
5 ingredient Banana Oat PB Breakfast cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 mashed ripened bananas
2 cups oats
2 tbsp PB
1/2 tsp salt
touch of cinnamon (optional)
protein powder (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake cookies for 15 minutes. Let cool. Freezes well.
