Title:
Guinness Cupcakes w/ Guinness Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cupcakes
1 (12-ounce) Bottle of Guinness Stout
1/2 cup Milk
1/2 cup Vegetable Oil
1 Tablespoon Pure Vanilla Extract
3 Large Eggs
3/4 Cup Sour Cream
3/4 Cup Unsweetened Cocoa
2 Cups Sugar
2 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
1 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Soda
Frosting
1/4 Cup, Heaping Cocoa Powder
1/4 Cup Guinness Stout
1/2 Cup Butter, softened
1/2 Cup Cream Cheese, softened
2 1/2 Cups Powdered Sugar, sifted

Directions:
***Cupcakes***
Preheat the oven to 350F / Place 24 cupcake liners into cupcake tins
In a large mixing bowl, combine the Guinness, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Mix in the sour cream.
In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the cocoa, sugar, flour, and baking soda. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet Guinness mixture.
Beat with mixer on low until mostly smooth.
Pour mixture into cupcake liners 3/4 full.
Bake for 25 minutes, until risen and set in the middle but still soft and tender. Cool before turning out of tins.
***Frosting***
Whisk the cocoa powder and Guinness together in a medium sized bowl.
Add in the butter and cream cheese and use a hand mixer to blend completely.
Mix in the powdered sugar cup at a time.
Feel free to add more sugar by the 1/4 cup if you like your frosting sweeter.
