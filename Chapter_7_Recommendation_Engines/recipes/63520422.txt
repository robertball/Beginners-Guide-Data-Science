Title:
Cashew "Cream Cheese" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. raw cashews, soaked for a few hours or overnight
1/2 c water
2 T. lemon juice
1 T. nutritional yeast
1 T. onion powder
1-1/2 T. finely diced chives
1/4 c. finely minced parsley
1-1/2 finely chopped shallots or red onion
1/2 t. sea salt
Fresh ground pepper, to taste

Directions:
Blend cashews with water and lemon in blender until smooth. (You might need to add a little extra water.)
Transfer to a bowl and stir in the rest of the ingredients.

Submitter: 
squawk93
Description: 
Use this as a spread, dip or filling. The recipe is suitable for vegetarians, vegans and those following a Paleo diet.
