Title:
Brussels Sprouts, Eggs + Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsps avocado oil, or olive oil
2 fresh cloves garlic, minced
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
2 lbs. brussels sprouts, halved
2 sweet red bell peppers, diced
4 slices nitrate-free bacon, chopped
4 large free range eggs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 370 degrees f.
Place Brussels sprouts, bell pepper and bacon in a single layer onto the prepared baking sheet. Add garlic and a drizzle of oil.
Season with salt and pepper and stir to combine.
Place into oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until tender.
Remove from oven and create 4 wells, gently cracking the eggs in.
Return to the oven and bake until the egg whites have set, an
additional 7-9 minutes.
Serve immediately!
