Title:
Mexican Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tuna fish, drained
2-3 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, chopped
1-2 tbl mayo
1 cup drained frozen or canned peas
garlic salt, onion flakes, and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix the above ingredients together and serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
AMYB9
Description: 
Spice up your tuna fish with this easy modified recipe that one of my student's families made for me - authentic Mexican!
