Title:
Pull Apart Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Grands biscuits
1/4 c olive oil
2 t garlic powder or salt
1 T italian seasoning
8 oz shredded cheese
Pepperoni, mushrooms, black olives, peppers, onions, hamburger, sausage or other toppings of choice

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.  Cut biscuits into quarters. Toss quarters in with oil and spices til well coated.  Add other ingredients as desired until all are mixed.  Spray bundt pan.  Bake 25 min or so, til dough is cooked through.  Remove from oven and immediately flip on to cooling rack or plate.  Let cool several minutes then invert onto serving tray.  Serve with favorite dipping sauce:  pizza sauce, ranch, bbq, honey, etc.
