Title:
Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1 (10.5 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 4 ounce can sliced mushrooms
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 egg
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dry thyme leaves
3 tablespoon all-purpose flour for dreging
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour for gravy
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
1 medium onion sliced

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together dry onion soup with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg,1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce,thyme, and black pepper. Shape into 6 oval patties. Dredge patties in flour
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown both sides of patties. Pour off excess fat.
In a small bowl, blend flour and 2 cans of soup until smooth. Mix in ketchup, water, 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce, sliced onions, sliced mushrooms and mustard powder. Pour over meat in skillet. Cover, and cook(simmer) for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally (just to keep from sticking on the bottom).

Description: 
Ground beef is mixed with dry onion soup, bread crumbs, and egg, then formed into patties which are browned and simmered in a sauce made with more soup and seasonings.Modified from: Salisbury Steak
