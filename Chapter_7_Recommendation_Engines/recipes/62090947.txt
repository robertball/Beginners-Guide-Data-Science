Title:
Shortcrust Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup plain flour
1/2 cup cold butter, chopped
1 egg yolk
3 teaspoons iced water (approximately)

Directions:
Sift flour into bowl, rub in butter.
Add egg yolk and enough water to make ingredients cling together.
Press dough into a ball, knead gently on lightly floured surface until smooth.
Wrap in plastic wrap, refrigerate 30 minutes.
Use pastry for tarts, pasties, quiches or other savoury goodies.

Submitter: 
Joanna
Description: 
Shortcrust is probably the most widely used of all pastries, and is good with sweet or savoury fillings.
