Title:
Bakery Style Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lbs. All Purpose Flour
2T & 2t Soda
2T & 2t Salt
2 lbs. Butter
2 lbs. Margarine
6 C. granulated sugar
6 C. light (preferably) brown sugar
2T 2t Vanilla
16 Eggs
16 C. Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in a seperate bowl.
Blend softened Butter and Margarine with your sugars until smooth and fluffy.
Pour in eggs and vanilla and mix until combined.
Add flour while mixing on low.
Add chocolate chips to batter.
Scoop a generous amount (about 1/2 cup) onto parchment-lined pans.
Bake at 400 degrees for about 15 minutes, rotating trays half-way through.  Edges of cookies should be brown, middle should be whitish.
Allow cookies to cool on pans to cook through middle, and remove when needed.
