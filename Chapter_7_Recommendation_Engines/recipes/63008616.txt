Title:
PATTY'S CHRISTMAS BUTTER SPRITZ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup butter
1 1/4 c sifted 10x sugar
3 egg yolks
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
Food coloring if desird

Directions:
Sift flour with salt.  Set aside.
Cream butter in mixing bowl.  Gradually add confectionrs sugar, continue creaming until light and fluffy.  Blend in egg yolks, extract and coloring.  Add dry ingredients gradually  mix.  Press a small amount of dough at a time through cookie press onto ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake at 400 degrees for 8 minutes or until delicately browned.
