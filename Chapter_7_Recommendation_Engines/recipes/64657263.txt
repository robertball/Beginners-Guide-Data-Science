Title:
Alexander's Sweet Chocolate Cubes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bags Of Each Of Every Kind Of Chocolate Chips
Kitchen Items Needed To Create This Recipe's Product:
1 Empty Ice Cube Tray
1 Microwave
1 Freezer
1 of every size of measuring bowls
1 of every size of measuring cups
1 microwave-safe bowl

Directions:
1. Open 1 bag of chocolate chips.
2. Pour 1 half of the bag into a microwave-safe bowl.
3. Microwave the chocolate chips until they have become liquid.
4. Pour the melted chocolate into each of the ice cube tray's slots.
5. Freeze the chocolate filled ice tray for 2 hours.
6. When the chocolates have hardened, eat them up!
