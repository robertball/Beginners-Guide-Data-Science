Title:
Quick Kraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Head Cabbage - cut up
2 cups Wood Chuck Amber hard cider
1 1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/4 tsp ground pepper
1 1/2 tsp kosher salt

Directions:
In a dutch oven, combine all ingredients.
Simmer covered for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until cabbage begins to take on a little darker color.

Submitter: 
duboo
Description: 
This is a great mellow kraut recipe.   Fresh kraut on a dog is the only way to go!
