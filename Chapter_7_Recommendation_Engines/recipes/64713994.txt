Title:
Caprese Stuffed Avocado's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5677675.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
8 oz baby mozzarella balls
4 tablespoons basil pesto (homemade or store bought)
2 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 cup olive oil
Salt and pepper to season
4 ripe avocados peeled, seeded and halved
Fresh basil leaves to serve (optional)
4 tablespoons balsamic glaze reduction to drizzle
4 tablespoons fresh basil chopped

Directions:
1.  Combine tomatoes, mozzarella balls, pesto, garlic, olive oil, salt and pepper in a bowl. Toss well to evenly combine all of the flavours.
2.  Arrange prepared avocado halves onto a plate with fresh basil leaves.
3.  Spoon the Caprese filling into each avocado halve and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
4.  Top with chopped fresh basil. Serve immediately
* I use Shanda's Wild Edibles and Basil Pesto
(https://www.allrecipes.com/personal-recipe/64715175/shandas-wild-edibles-and-basil-pesto/)

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Take creamy avocados to a different level with this stuffed avocado recipe! Sweet and juicy grape/cherry tomatoes with fresh mozzarella balls are tossed in basil pesto with a touch of garlic, then stuffed into avocado halves! Drizzle with a balsamic glaze for an incredible flavor combination.
