Title:
Pasta Beans Sausage and Escarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I pound italian sausage
1 onion chopped
4 garlic cloves chopped
1 red pepper chopped
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 head escarole cleaned and coarsley chopped
1 can 16oz. cannellini beans drained
1 pound pasta ziti or  medium shells cooked and drained
fresh parmesan cheese grated

Directions:
*Poke the sausage a few times with a fork. Place sausage in a large skillet cover with water and bring to a boil. Cover reduce heat and simmer 20 min. Remove from water and let cool then slice. Clean skillet then heat olive oil saute onion, garlic, red pepper, seasonings and salt and pepper for 5 minutes. Stir in sausage slices and continue to saute for 2-3 minutes. Add broth and escarole simmer for 5 minutes.stir in beans heat  through toss with pasta. Top with Cheese
* You can cook the sausage any way you want. Whatever you are comfortable with.

A change from the average red sauce. I like to serve with a salad and garlic bread.
