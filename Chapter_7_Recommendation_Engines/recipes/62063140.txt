Title:
sopnge roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces egg yolks
4 ounces sugar
6 ounces cake flour
9 ounces egg whites
1/4 teaspoon salt
6 ounces sugar

Directions:
Combime egg yolks,4 ounces of suar and salt in a metal bowl. Put bowl over hot water bath and stir until mixture is warm not hot. (do not stop stirring or eggs will scramble)After egss are warm place in mixing bowl with wire whip attachment and beat eggs at high speed until light and thick.10 to 15 minutes this might take. Whip egg whites until they form soft peeks,then add suger and beat until firm peeks.
Fold in whites alternately with sifted dry in gredints. Fold in with hand  not in mixer. Put in pan and bake at 425 degrees for 7 minutes.
After cooled sprinkle with some sugar and spread raspberry or strawberry jelly and roll up. If desired frost with whipped cream
