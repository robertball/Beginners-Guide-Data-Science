Title:
South Carolina Mustard BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup yellow mustard
3/4 cup cider vinegar
1 tablespoon light brown sugar (or more to taste - i add two splenda packets to cut carbs)
1 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter
2 teaspoons salt
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 1/4 teaspoons fresh ground black pepper
2 teaspoons louisiana hot sauce	 (to taste)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in small saucepan. Whisk to combine and occasionally while simmering for 20-30 minutes.
Let cool before using.
Save remainder in a sealed container in the refer.

Submitter: 
margieatk
Description: 
I grew up with SC mustard sauce.  Really perks up pork or chicken.
