Title:
SUN DRIED TOMATO VINAIGRETTE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup sun-dried tomato pieces
1/4 cup chopped green onions
2 teaspoons minced shallots
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 turns freshly ground black pepper
3/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
Combine the vinegar, tomatoes, green onions, shallots, garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
Slowly whisk in the oil until the dressing is thoroughly emulsified. Serve immediately or store, refrigerated, in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
Whisk before serving.
