Title:
Quinoa and Honey Mustard Chicken Slow Cooker Meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white quinoa
1 1/2 tsp sea salt
3 TBSP nutritional yeast flakes (do not substitute)
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp dill weed
1/8 tsp turmeric
1 TBSP chives (dried or fresh)
3 large or 4 small chicken breasts (frozen)
1 3/4 cup water
1 tsp red wine vinegar
1 TBSP butter
1 TBSP honey
3 TBSP spicy brown mustard

Directions:
1. Soak 1 cup quinoa in enough hot water to cover for 5 minutes while assembling seasonings.
2. In a small dish, mix together 1 1/2 tsp salt, 3 TBSP nutritional yeast flakes, 1 tsp onion powder, 1/4 tsp dill weed, 1/8 tsp turmeric and 1 TBSP chives.
3. Drain quinoa and place in slow cooker and stir in 1 3/4 cup water, 1 tsp vinegar, and seasoning mix. Dot with 1 TBSP butter.
4. Place 3 large or 4 small frozen chicken breasts on top of quinoa, season lightly with salt.
5. Stir together 1 TBSP honey with 3 TBSP spicy brown mustard. Spread over just the chicken breasts.
6. Cook on high for 3 hours. Serve with a green salad and some steamed veggies.

Submitter: 
Bestfoodist
Description: 
This goes into the cooker quickly, with no precooking and chicken straight from the freezer! I like to put it in before we leave for church so I can come home to a nice meal.
