Title:
Classic Italian Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 8 ounces rotelle or spiral pasta, cooked and drained.
* 1/3 cup sliced pitted ripe olives
* 1 cup Italian dressing
* 1/2 cup shredded cheddar or mozzarella cheese
* 1/3 cup (more or less) chopped up pepperoni
* 2 1/2 cups assorted cut-up vegetables (broccoli, carrots, tomatoes, bell peppers, cauliflower, onions and mushrooms).

Directions:
1)Combine all ingredients except the Italian dressing in a large bowl. Add dressing and toss well. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
