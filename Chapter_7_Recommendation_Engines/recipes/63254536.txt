Title:
big mac pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6913522.jpg
Ingredients:
1 refridgerated pizza dough
1 cup thousand island dressing
1 small onion, diced
2 cups finely shredded lettuce
2 cups hamburger, cooked and drained
13-15 dill pickle slices
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
pre-heat oven to 425, roll out pizza dough on a lightly floured pan. top with thousand island dressing, shredded cheese and hamburger. place in oven for 10-15 minutes or until dough is cooked. top with the lettuce, onions and pickles. enjoy!
