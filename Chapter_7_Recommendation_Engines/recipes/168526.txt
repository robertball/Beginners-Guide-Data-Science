Title:
Cilantro Lime Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plain low-fat yogurt
2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
1 tablespoon sliced green onion tops
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
3/4 teaspoon lime zest
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Stir together all ingredients in a medium bowl.
Cover and chill for at least 1 hour for flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
Make this fresh and zesty dip for dipping vegetables, pita triangles, or tortilla chips.
