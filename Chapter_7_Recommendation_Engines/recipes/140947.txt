Title:
Garlic Turkey Focaccia Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 garlic clove, minced
1 (10 ounce) loaf Archer Farms™ Asiago focaccia
2 cups Archer Farms™ sliced oven roasted turkey breast
12 fresh basil leaves
1 large tomato, sliced

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise and garlic in small bowl.  Cut focaccia horizontally in half.
Layer turkey, basil and tomato slices on bottom half of focaccia.  Spread cut side remaining focaccia half with garlic mayonnaise.  Place mayonnaise side down on tomato.
Cut into 4 sandwiches.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Deli turkey gets a taste-makeover with garlic and basil, and the hearty texture of focaccia.
