Title:
Britton Family Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup condensed tomato soup
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup yellow mustard
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup butter
3 egg yolks, beaten

Directions:
In a medium-small saucepan over medium heat mix all ingredients except egg yolks.
Whisk until butter melts and sugar is dissolved.
Lower heat to simmer and incorporate egg yolks until sauce thickens.
Remove from heat and let cool prior to refrigerating.

Submitter: 
BOILERMISH
Description: 
This mustard sauce recipe was my great Grandmother's recipe; we make it whenever we have a large gathering with ham.  It is also great the next day on sandwiches or for dipping pretzels or kielbasa.  The vinegar and tomato soup give it some tartness to counteract the sugar.
