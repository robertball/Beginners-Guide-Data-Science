Title:
Elotes-Style Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
POTATOES
1 lb of redskin potatoes, cubed to 1.2 inch pieces (enough to fill a baking sheet)
1/2 yellow onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
2 red chili peppers
EVOO
Salt and pepper
DRESSING
2 tomatillos, finely diced
2 red chili peppers, minced
4-5 cloves of garlic
1/2 cup of cilantro, chopped
3/4 cup of mayonnaise
Juice of two limes
1/2 tablespoon of hot Mexican chili powder
1 cup of cotija cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees
Prep potatoes and vegetables for roasting
Place potatoes on baking sheet lined with parchment paper
Toss potatoes with EVOO, salt and pepper
Roast for 10 minutes, then add the onions and peppers
Cook until crispy (roughly 25 minutes)
While potato mixture roasts, combine all dressing ingredients in a small bowl (save the cheese until the potatoes are done)
When potatoes are done, toss first with cheese, then toss with dressing
Serve immediately

Description: 
Crispy and warm potato salad, Mexican street corn style.
