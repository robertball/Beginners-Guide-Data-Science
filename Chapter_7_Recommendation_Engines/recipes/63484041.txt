Title:
Turkey Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbs margarine
2-4 tbs flour
1 1/4 cup milk
1/4 tsp dry mustard
broccoli
turkey
s/p
1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese (2 oz/1/2 cup make white sauce)

Directions:
arrange hot cooked broccoli and sliced turkey in casserole.  pour sauce over all
sprinkle with 2 tbs grated parmesan cheese
broil 4" from heat until cheese is browned
