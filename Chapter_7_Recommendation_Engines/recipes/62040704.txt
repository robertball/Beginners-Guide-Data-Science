Title:
Parmesan Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 stalks chopped celery
4 tablespoons butter
4 chicken bouillon cubes
1/4 cup flour
2 cups water
1 Tbsp. thyme
1-2 cups milk
2 cooked cubed chicken breasts
6 cups (dry measure) cooked egg noodles
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
If not cooked, begin cooking egg noodles and chicken.  While they cook:  In large saucepan cook celery in butter and bouillon cubes until soft. Mix flour with water and add to pan with 1 Tbsp. thyme.  Boil for about 5 minutes or until very thick. Slowly add milk and continue to simmer. Add chicken, stir, and pour mixture over cooked egg noodles. Pour into casserole dish and generously sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Serve immediately or cover and bake in microwave or oven until hot and bubbly.

Submitter:

Description: 
A very tasty casserole.  The celery is cooked in butter and bouillon cubes and absorbs a wonderful flavor.
