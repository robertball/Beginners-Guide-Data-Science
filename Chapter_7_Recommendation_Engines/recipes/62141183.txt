Title:
Sweet and Sour Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts cut into desired cubes
1 C. ice water
1 egg
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
2 C. flour
2-4 C. vegetable oil
Sauce
1/2 C. ketchup
1/2 C. vinegar
1/2 C. water
2 tsp. soy sauce
1 C. sugar
1/2 C. orange marmalade
1 1/2 tsp. minced ginger
1 tsp. minced garlic
1/4 tsp. pepper flakes (or hot sauce)
2 Tbsp. pineapple juice
1/2 C. chopped pineapple (optional)
4 Tbsp. cornstarch mixed with 3 Tbsp. water (for thickening)

Directions:
Put in Wok or frying pan. Let the oil heat up on medium high heat while mixing the batter. To check the oil put a drop of water in it and is should start crackling. Beat together water and egg. Add baking soda and salt. Add 1 C. flour to the water mixture. It should be blended but lumpy. Put 1 C. flour in a separate bowl. Cover each piece of chicken in flour then in batter. Let the excess batter drip off then put in oil. Cook until golden brown. You may have to flip the chicken to get both sides golden brown. Should take 3-4 min. at the most if the oil is the right temperature.
In medium sauce pan combine all ingredients except thickening agent. Bring to a boil then add cornstarch mixture. Reduce heat and simmer stirring frequently until desired thickness is reached. Make sure to bring mixture to a boil otherwise it with be starchy.
