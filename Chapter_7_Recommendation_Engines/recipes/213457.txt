Title:
Salsa Verde Corn and Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990006.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups PaceÂ® Salsa Verde
1 large red pepper, diced
1 cup whole kernel corn
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 mango - peeled, seeded and diced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves

Directions:
Stir the salsa, pepper, corn, beans, mango and cilantro in a medium bowl. Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Red pepper, mango, corn and black beans are mixed with a zesty green salsa for this refreshing corn and bean salad.
