Title:
Beet, Radish, Apple, Olive, Avocado, Quinoa Salad with Citrus Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1121295.jpg
Ingredients:
salad
2 beets, thinly sliced or matchsticks
2-3 large radish, thinly sliced or matchsticks
1/3 green apple, thinly sliced or matchsticks
1 avocado, diced
1/2 c or more olives
8-10 beet greens/leaves (as desired)
1 c parsley (or more)
2 c cooked quinoa (cook with garlic/toasted before)
goat cheese or feta to serve (optional)
vinaigrette
1/2 c apple cider vinegar
juice from 3-4 mandarin oranges
juice 1/2 to 1 lemon
little olive juice
salt/pepper
pinch paprika (to taste)

Directions:
cook quinoa
combine all salad ingredients
toss
combine all vinaigrette ingredients
combine with salad
serve with goat cheese or feta if desired
