Title:
Goblin's Favorite Ranch Taco Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983560.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 cup TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Thick 'N Chunky Salsa, divided
1 (16 ounce) package salad greens
1 cup KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 cup KRAFT Ranch Dressing

Directions:
Cook chicken in 1/4 cup of the salsa in large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat 8 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Toss chicken with greens and cheese in large bowl.
Top with remaining 3/4 cup salsa and dressing just before serving.
