Title:
Low Calorie Veggie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500405.jpg
Ingredients:
64 ounces Organic Vegetable Broth
2 cups of White Wine
1 large onion
2 leeks
5 carrots
1/2 large green cabbage
2 yellow crookneck squash
2 tbls Tarragon
1 teas Salt
1 teas pepper
1/2 bottle Saffron Threads
4 cups water
Tools
Cuisinart slicer and grater
Wand blender
If you dont have these tools the time to prepare will be greatly increased.

Directions:
Pour liquids into large pot. Add spices to pot. Start temp is med. With cuisinart slicing blade, slice leeks and cabbage, with grating blade, grate rest of veggies. Add all veggies to pot then add water to cover. Turn temp to Hi and put on lid for 30 mins. Stir occationally. Next turn heat down to warm and simmer for 1 hour. Take off heat and use wand blender in an up and down motion. Blend till smooth, no chunks. Proteins for this would be chicken, keilbasa, hotlinks, portuguise sausage.
Other spice options
Fresh basil and oregano with Sweet Italian Sausage.
Rosemary, Garlic, red wine with Lamb Shanks

Submitter: 
KLemons
Description: 
Very easy vegan soup that can be seasoned in many ways. It can be eaten for a low calorie snack or add protein for hearty main course.
