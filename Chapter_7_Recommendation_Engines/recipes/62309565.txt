Title:
Irish Soda Bread and whiskey butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raisins
1/2 cup whiskey
3 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
1tbs baking powder
1tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1tbs grated orange peel (optional)
1 1/3 cups buttermilk
4 tbs melted butter
1tbs carroway seeds (optional)

Directions:
soak raisins overnight in whiskey
in large bowl combine flour sugar baking powder salt and baking soda
Stir in drained raisin (reserve leftover whiskey) carroway seeds and orange peel
blend in buttermilk and butter
put in 2 qt casserole dish
cut x across top with floured knife
bake in a 350 degree oven for 1:10 min or until toothpick comes out clean
Whiskey butter
Mix desired amount of leftover whiskey with softened butter for topping
