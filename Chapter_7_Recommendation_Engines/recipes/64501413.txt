Title:
Mashed Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
2 cups chicken broth, low sodium
1 small sprig rosemary (1/2 tsp dried rosemary leaves)
3 garlic cloves, smashed or 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Remove green stems from cauliflower and cut cauliflower in fourths, keeping each piece connected to the center core. Place the cauliflower in a the steaming portion of a steamer.
For the portion of the steamer which holds the liquid, combine broth, rosemary and smashed garlic. Place the steamer basket filled with cauliflower over the portion with liquid. Bring liquid to a boil and allow the cauliflower to steam for 15 minutes. When steaming is complete move cauliflower only to a blender jar and blend until smooth. Add salt and pepper and butter to taste and serve.
