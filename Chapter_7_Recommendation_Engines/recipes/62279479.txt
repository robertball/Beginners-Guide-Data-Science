Title:
Bleu Cheese Celery Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Bleu Cheese crumbles
1 ½ teaspoon Peppercorn, freshly crushed
1 cup Sour cream
½ cup Mayo
1 ½ tablespoon Worcestershire
½ teaspoon Seasoned Salt
1 teaspoon White pepper
1 ½ teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
2 stalks Celery, sliced

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients EXCEPT celery and refrigerate for at least an hour.  Just before serving, add celery and mix thoroughly.
This can be served with hot wings or used as a dip with chips.

Submitter: 
AndAQT2
Description: 
If you like Bleu Cheese Dressing and celery with your hot wings, you'll love this.  It can also be used as a chip dip.
