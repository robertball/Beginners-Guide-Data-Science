Title:
ROQUEFORT GRAPES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. grapes, green or red seedless
1 10 oz. pkg.walnuts, pecans or almonds, coarsely chopped
2 tbl.  heavy cream
1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
1/8 lb. Roquefort Cheese or Gorgonzola

Directions:
Rinse and dry grapes. Set aside.
Spread nuts on a baking sheet and toast at 275 degrees until nuts are a light brown color.  Cool
Chop nuts coarsely and spread on a platter.
In a bowl, mix heavy cream, cream cheese and Roquefort cheeses.
Beat until smooth.
Drop grapes into the cheese mixture and fold gently.
After coating grapes generously with cheese, roll in toasted chopped nuts.
Place on wax paper - lined tray.  Chill until ready to serve.
Serve as is or skewered into a freah pineapple along with strawberries and other fresh fruit.

A very nice combination of flavors, textures and colors.
