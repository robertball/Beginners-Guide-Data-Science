Title:
Big Ray's Fruity Baked Oatmel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5402746.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
2 tsp vanilla
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup milk
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 medium chopped peeled tart apple
1/3 cup chopped fresh or frozen peaches
1/3 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large bowl, combine oats and milk and allow to soak, about 10 minutes,then add brown sugar, baking powder, salt vanillaand cinnamon. Combine eggs, milk and butter; add to the dry ingredients. Stir in apple, peaches and blueberries.
Pour into an 8-in. square baking dish coated with cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, 35-40 minutes or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cut into squares. Serve with milk if desired.
Note: This can also be made in a cast iron skillet.  Canned peaches may be substituted for the fresh/frozen and add the juice from them as well.

Submitter:

Description: 
If you have been trying to find an alternative to boring oatmeal and this is it!  It is so versatile, you can change fruits to seasonal. I sometimes throw in a handful of nuts to change it up. Can be frozen in individual servings. Sometimes I will serve this as a desert!
