Title:
Tasty Twisted Mac 'n Cheese Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018066.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. ground beef
1/2 lb. Italian sausage, casing removed
1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasonings
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
2 16 oz. cans Italian (diced) tomatoes with liquid
1 6 oz. can tomato paste
4 cups cooked rotini
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
4 slices American cheese

Directions:
Brown and drain the meats.
Add onion, garlic and seasonings, cooking until onion is clear.
Add diced tomatoes.
Pour tomato paste into tomato can and add 1 6 oz. can of water.
Stir together and add to the skillet.
Simmer just until heated through.
Preheat oven to 350Â°
Combine skillet ingredients with the cooked pasta in a greased, large oven-safe baking dish.
Add the shredded cheese and toss together.
Bake for 20 minutes, then top with cheese slices
Return to oven and bake for 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
Serve with a green salad and crusty Italian bread.
