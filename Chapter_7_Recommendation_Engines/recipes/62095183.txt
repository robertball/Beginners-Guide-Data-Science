Title:
cool big shrimp dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
two bags of frozen cooked baby shrimp thawed.
one 8 oz package -chive and onion cream cheese.
1/2 a cup of sour cream
1/2 a cup of cocktail sauce with horseradish
2 cups of finely chopped celery
1 cup of finely chopped onion
1 tsp of salt
1 tsp of pepper

Directions:
clean and rinse shrimp with cold water set aside in large bowl
in another bowl mix cream cheese, sour cream,cocktail sauce and salt and pepper till combinde.
add cream cheese mixture to larger bowl with shrimp, and celery and onion.  fold everything together and transfer to a casserole dish and smooth out.   chill for one hour.  enjoy with a mixture of crackers.
