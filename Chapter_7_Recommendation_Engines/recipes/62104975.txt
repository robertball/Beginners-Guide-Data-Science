Title:
Individual Apple Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie crust(enough to fill eight 3" muffin cups)
4 cups apples, peeled and thinly sliced
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup cream
2 slightly beaten eggs
2 Tablespoons melted butter
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line eight 3" muffin cups with pie crust.
Fill muffin cups with the sliced apples.
Combine remaining ingredients and pour over the apples.
Bake for 40 minutes. Very good served with thick whipped cream or vanilla ice cream, with a pinch of cinnamon sprinkled on top.

Submitter: 
Emma J
Description: 
These little pies are great for an easy and delectable desert. They also reheat well in the microwave!
