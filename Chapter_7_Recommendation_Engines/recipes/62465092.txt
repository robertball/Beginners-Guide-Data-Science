Title:
Slow Cooker Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup water
8 boneless pork chops
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (14.5 ounce) can green beans
6 potatoes, peeled and cubed
onion powder
garlic powder
salt
olive oil
flour

Directions:
Season chops with salt, onion and garlic powder; dip in flour and brown with olive oil in frying pan.
Dice potatoes.
Layer potatoes, soup/water mixture/green beans/chops in slow cooker until gone, seasoning potatoes with salt/pepper as you go.
Slow-cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Sylvia Marchesi Walton
Description: 
Use your slow cooker to make these creamy and comforting pork chops. Adding green beans and potatoes to lightly seasoned pork chops in cream of mushroom soup makes this a hearty one-pot meal.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chops
