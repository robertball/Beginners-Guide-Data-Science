Title:
Layered Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup Miracle Whip
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
1/2 pint sour creme
Romaine or iceberg lettuce
1 box frozen peas, slightly cooked and cooled
2 small onions, sliced thin
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 can water chestnuts
1/2 lb. bacon, cooked and diced

Directions:
To make the dressing, mix together the sugar, miracle whip, vinegar, and sour creme. Set aside.
In a nonreactive 9x13 baking dish, layer the remaining ingredients as follows: lettuce (until about 1 1/2" thick), peas, onion slices, chopped celery, water chestnuts, and bacon. Pour the dressing over top, and refrigerate over night.
