Title:
Kishk El-Omarrah (Royal Almond Pudding ) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c milk
1 c ground almond plus 1-2 T for topping
1/2 c cornstarch
1/2 c water
1 1/4 c sugar (to taste)
1/2 t mistika, finely crushed (optional) found at the mediterranean stores

Directions:
1. Heat milk on stove.
2. When it starts to simmer, add almond, sugar and mixture of cornstarch and water. Bring to a simmer until thickened.
3. Take pudding off the heat, add mistika and stir.
4. Pour into serving cups/bowls, sprinkle some ground almond on top, cover with plastic wrap and cool in the refrigerator.
