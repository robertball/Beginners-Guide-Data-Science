Title:
Crock Pot Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium bell peppers
1.5 lbs ground beef
3/4 C of uncooked rice
28 oz jar of spaghetti sauce
1/2 C of water
8 oz can tomato sauce
8 oz can of mushrooms
2 T dried minced onion
2 T dried minced garlic
1 T ground black pepper
1 T salt
1 T parsley
Parmesan Cheese to taste

Directions:
Cut tops off of peppers and clean out seeds and membranes.  Set peppers in crockpot.
Mix ground beef with mushrooms, 8 oz can of sauce, onion, garlic pepper, salt, parsley and rice.
Fill pepppers with mixture and sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
Pour tomato sauce and 1/2 C of water (use water to get any sauce stuck in jar!) over top of peppers.
Cook on low for 6-8 hours in crock pot.
Enjoy!
