Title:
poussin with a Redcurrant Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 poussin(Little Chickens)
125g Butter
1 Garlic clove Crushed
1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
1 teaspoon paprika
1 tablespoon Honey
(sauce)
8 tablespoon Red wine
2 teaspoon White wine vinegar
4 tablespoon redcurrant jelly
4 teaspoon cornflour
8 tablespoons water

Directions:
pre heat oven to 180 degrees
In a saucepan melt the butter, honey,cumin,paprika and garlic.
Put the poussin in a roasting tray.
Pour the butter mixture over the poussin.
Roast in the oven for 45 mins (untill cooked)
Towards the end of cooking make the redcurrant sauce.
In a sauce pan melt, untill smooth the Red wine, white wine vinegar and redcurrant jelly.
blend the cornflour and water together.
Add the cornflour mixture to the redcurrant mixture.
stir untill the sauce thickens and becomes darker in colour
Serve with potatoes and vegetables pouring the sauce all over the meal.
