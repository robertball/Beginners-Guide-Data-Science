Title:
Superfood Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4458751.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup baby spinach leaves
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/2 cup chopped honeydew melon
1/4 cup chopped pear
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Combine baby spinach, Greek yogurt, honeydew melon, pear, milk, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.

Description:
