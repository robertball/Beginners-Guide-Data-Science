Title:
Amy's Chili Rellenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Anaheim chile peppers, charred and peeled or large can of whole green chilies
1 pound Monterrey Jack cheese, cut into long strips
1/2 cup milk
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Wash the chilies and remove and seeds. Make a small slit at the top of the chili and insert the cheese strips. Be careful not to rupture the chili membrane.
2.  In a small bowl mix the egg (scrambled) and milk.
3. Take the stuffed chili and dredge in flour then the egg/milk batter and then flour again.
4. Fry in oil on medium heat until browned on both sides. After removing from the grease sprinkle with salt to taste and serve while warm.

Description: 
Jack Stuffed Anaheim Peppers fried in a golden batter. I am always asked for this recipe. It's simple and very wonderful.
