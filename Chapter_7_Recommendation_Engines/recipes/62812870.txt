Title:
Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice of 1/4 of an orange
Juice of 1/2 of a lime
Pinch of kosher salt
1/2 tsp chopped cilantro
1 avocado
2 tsp roasted Roma tomatoes/Serrano peppers mixture (roast tomatoes and
peppers over open fire or in the oven @ 450 degrees until charred, remove
skins on tomatoes, and chop both - one pepper to 4 or 5 tomatoes)
1 tsp chopped red onions

Directions:
Squeeze juices into bowl. Add avocado and dice. Add kosher salt and
cilantro. Dice again. Add onions and tomato/Serrano mixture and fold into
guacamole.
