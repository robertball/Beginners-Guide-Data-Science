Title:
Sweet Potato Pie Georgia Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/739237.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
2 cups cooked and mashed sweet potatoes, (about 4 potatoes)
4 tablespoons butter, softened
3 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
2/3 cup (1 can) evaporated milk
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon allspice
whipped cream,(optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
To cook sweet potatoes, place on a baking sheet and cook for one hour, or until easy to mash when touched. Allow to cool, then scrape pulp from skin and mash in a large bowl.
In a medium bowl, beat together butter, sugar, and brown sugar until creamy. Add eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, ginger,salt nutmeg, and allspice. Add flour, evaporated milk and stir mixture into sweet potatoes. Beat together with mixer until smooth and pour into unbaked pie shell.
Bake at 350 degrees on bottom rack of oven for 1 hour or until center of pie is firm. Remove from oven and allow to stand for 10 minutes. Serve warm. Add dollop of whipped cream to each slice, if desired.
