Title:
Sloppy Joes II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271322.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
3/4 cup ketchup
3 teaspoons brown sugar
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
12 mini rolls

Directions:
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef; drain off liquids.
Stir in the garlic powder, mustard, ketchup, and brown sugar; mix thoroughly. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
Cut roll in half and place sloppy joe mix on the bottom piece. Cover with top of bun.

Submitter: 
Cooks4Kids
Description: 
My kids love this recipe when I tone down the spice for them and make them on mini rolls. They love their kid-sized sloppy joes and usually want to eat way too many.Modified from: Sloppy Joes II
