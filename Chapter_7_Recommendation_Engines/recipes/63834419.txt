Title:
Poetic Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065648.jpg
Ingredients:
CRUST
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 stick butter, melted
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
FILLING
1 15 oz. can pumpkin
1 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
2 large eggs
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp lemon zest
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/8 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp salt
BROWN SUGAR WHIPPED CREAM
1 cup heavy whipping cream
2 heaping Tbsp brown sugar
1tsp vanilla

Directions:
FOR THE CRUST: Preheat oven to 375ยบ. Combine all crust ingredients in an 8" pie pan and mix well with a fork. Press lightly into bottoms and up the sides of the pie pan. Bake about 7 min to set it.
FOR THE FILLING: Turn oven up to 425ยบ. Combine the filling ingredients and whisk until smooth. Pour into cooled crust and bake for 15 min. Lower temp to 350ยบ and bake another 35-40 min until filling is set and edges of crust are dark golden brown.
BROWN SUGAR WHIPPED CREAM: In a med bowl, combine ingredients and beat with an electric mixer on med speed until stiff peaks form; spread generously over cooled pie and lightly dust with nutmeg.

Description: 
I like pumpkin pie, but have always found it a tad boring in its usual form. The cinnamon graham cracker crust and brown sugar whipped cream breathe new life into traditional pumpkin pie and make it, well, poetic!
