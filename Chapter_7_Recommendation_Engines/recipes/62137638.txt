Title:
Chicken in beer and cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 boneless chicken breasts halves
2dl of heavy cream
1 cube of chicken broth
150g of sliced caned mushrooms
1 package (60g) of instant onion soup powder
33cl of beer of your choice
2 tablespoons of olive oil

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a casserole and dip in the breasts halves.Salt is not needed.
Cook in low/medium fire until the breasts are tender and the beer/cream sauce is thick, stiring ocasionally during the cooking. Accompany with mashed potatoes, rice or some fries and a salad.

Submitter: 
Rita Machado
Description: 
It's so delicious, and so simple to make, but it's true!My family and friends love this!There's no way it can go wrong, even if you are a beginner!
If using a chicken in pieces just double the quantity of beer and cook some more time!
