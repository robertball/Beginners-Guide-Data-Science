Title:
Quesadilla Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Burgers:
1 lb of hamburger:
2 Tbsp. egg beaters
2 Tbsp. catsup
2 Tbsp. mustard
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/2 tsp. of seasoning salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. dry onion flakes
1/4 cup of italian bread crumbs
Garnishes:sliced tomatoes, lettuce, green chili, sliced dill pickles, 1 large onion sliced
flour tortilla-cut in 1/2
6  slices of pepper jack cheese-one for each burger
Condiments: any of the following mustard, ketchup, or mayonnaise

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together.
Wipe cast iron skillet with oil.
Add meat mixture to cast iron skillet.
Use a spatula to spread hamburger evenly in the skillet.
Bake for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, prepare flour tortillas by cutting 1/2.
Cook 6 pieces of bacon.
Prepare garnishes-sliced tomatoes, lettuce, green chili, sliced dill pickles, and sliced onions.
Add  1/2 of the sliced onions around edge.
Bake for 10 minutes more.
Drain grease from hamburger. Place cooked onions on a paper towel to drain.
Cut hamburger into 6 pieces.
Add 1/6 of burger to tortilla.
Add i piece of cooked bacon and 1 slice of pepper jack cheese for each quesadilla.
Warm in microwave for 10 seconds on a microwave safe plate.
Add other garnishes and condiments.
