Title:
Spaghetti sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 to 2 lbs ground beef
1 onion
1 green pepper
2 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp celery
3 C ketchup
2 - 29 oz. cans of tomato puree

Directions:
1.  Brown hamburger, peppers, onion and garlic in a skillet
2.  Put tomato puree and ketchup in a crock pot.
3.  Add garlic powder, cayenne pepper, chili powder and celery salt and stir.
4.  Add browned hamburger mixture.
5.  Cook in crockpot a few hours or best all day on low.
Enjoy!  Freezes well too!

Submitter: 
ASTEINOUR
Description: 
My Dad was a cook in a restaurant for 20 years and
served this as a regular menu item.  I make in the crock
pot and use for spaghetti, lazagna, Zita, whatever.  Its great!
