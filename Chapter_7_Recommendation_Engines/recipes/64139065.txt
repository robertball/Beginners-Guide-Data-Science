Title:
Easy Enchilada Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-12 corn tortillas
1 pound beef or turkey
cilantro (to taste)
chopped onion
1 small can diced green chilies
Black olives
1 large can enchilada sauce
shredded chesse
1 can corn

Directions:
preheat oven to 325, grease 10x8 baking dish
Put 4-6 tortillas in bottom of dish
brown meat, cilantro and onion together. stir in the corn, diced green chilies and olives
Put a layer of meat mixture on tortillas, pour some of the enchilada sauce over the meat
sprinkle some cheese over all.
layer down some more tortillas and repeat ending with cheese.
bake uncovered 35 mins
