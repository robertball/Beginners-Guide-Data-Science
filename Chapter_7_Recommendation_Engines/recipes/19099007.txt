Title:
Jean Salter's Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup natural bran
2 heaping Tbsp wheat germ
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 egg
1/3 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour, bran, wheat germ, sugar, salt and baking powder.
In another bowl, beat together egg, oil, milk and vanilla.
Add liquid to dry ingredients and mix just enough to moisten.
Drop unto greased muffin tins and bake at 375F for 18 minutes.

Submitter: 
cdagirl
Description: 
These are not your conventional muffin.  They are rather heavy and very full of fiber, but at the same time, tasty and moist.  A breakfast treat!
