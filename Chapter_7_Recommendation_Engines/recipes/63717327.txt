Title:
Hawaiian Lavender Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb - Pappardelle's Lavender Pasta (or equivalent)
12 Macadamia nuts, salted
2 tsp. Chervil
1 tbsp Butter
1 tbsp EVOO (Extra Virgin Olive Oil) Masia al Altet or other high quality oil.
Pinch of Hawaiian Sea Salt

Directions:
Boil the noodles 6-8 minutes or until al dente, with a bit of bite, but not hard
In a food processor or chopper, fine chop the macadamia nuts. Set aside.
In a large work bowl, put the butter.
Drain the pasta. Add to the work bowl.
Sprinkle chervil and macadamia nuts on the pasta. Add salt to taste.
Toss and serve.

Submitter: 
The Jazz Chef
Description: 
This is a delicate pasta with an amazing taste to it if you let them come out. The rich nuts accentuate the flavor, as does the Chervil without overwhelming it.
