Title:
Apple Pie Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 8oz Packages of Cream Cheese
1 250g Tub Mascarpone Cheese
3 eggs
2/3 Cup Sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
2 Large Apples
1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon
1 Teaspoon Brown Sugar
1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg
2/3 box Graham Crackers
1 1/4 Sticks of butter, melted

Directions:
Preparation:
1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees
2. Line a 9" springform pan with pachment paper or grease with butter.
Crust:
1. Use a Food Processor or a rolling pin and plastic bag to finely crumble the graham crackers. Add to large bowl.
2. Stir in melted butter to make grainy paste-like crust.
3. Reserve Approx 1/4 Cup for topping.
4. Distribute crust mixture evenly to base of a greased or parchment-paper lined springform pan, let cool.
Topping:
1. Peel, core and slice apples into 1/4" slices. Place in large bowl.
2. Add Brown Sugar, Cinnamon and Nutmeg. Toss to coat.
Filling:
1. Add Cream Cheese, Marscarpone, sugar and vanilla extract to large bowl. Mix with electric mixer.
2. Add eggs one at a time.
3. Beat 2-3 minutes until well-integrated.
4. Pour out into crust-lined springform pan. Smooth with spatula.
5. Top with apple slices in circular "deck of cards" arrangement.
6. Sprinkle with reserved crust mixture.
7. Bake at 350 degrees for approx 30-40 minutes, or until edges begin to firm up, but the center remains "jiggly", and the apples begin to soften.
8. Cool for 6-8 hours and serve.
