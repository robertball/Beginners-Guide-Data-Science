Title:
Pond Scum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/911582.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 liter) bottle grape soda
1 (2 liter) bottle orange soda
1 quart lime sherbet

Directions:
Pour grape and orange soda into a punch bowl. Scoop small portions of lime sherbet into the soda mixture. Let the sherbet melt into a foamy green layer on top.

Submitter: 
Kay
Description: 
Disgusting-looking punch for children's Halloween parties. Very easy and inexpensive. It is hard to convince kids to drink it, but they love it once they've tried it. It's too sweet for most adults. It helps that we live on a lake and say we've just drawn it from the lake.
