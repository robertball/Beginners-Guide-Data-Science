Title:
Marvelous Dill Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint sour cream
3 Tbsp. low fat Hellman's mayonnaise
1 Tsp. Beau Monde (spice)
1 Tbsp. Dill Weed
1 Tbsp. Parsley Flakes
3/4 Tbsp. Onion Flakes

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a medium bowl.
Use whisk and mix it well.
Chill for 1/2 - 1 hour

Submitter: 
fishinwidow
Description: 
This dill dip goes great with cut up veggies!  Kids love this dip.
