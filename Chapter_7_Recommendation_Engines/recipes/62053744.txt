Title:
Creamy Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs acorn or butternut squash
1 can cream of chicken soup, undiluted
1 cup sour cream
1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 medium carrots, shredded
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2-1/4 cups herb-seasoned stuffing mix, divided

Directions:
Cut squash in half, remove seeds, peel and cut into 1/2 inch cubes.  Cook in a small amount of water 3 minutes; drain and set aside.  In bowl, combine soup, sour cream, butter, carrots and onion; stir in 2 cups stuffing mix.  Fold in squash.  Transfer to greased or sprayed 7 x 11 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with remaining stuffing mix.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 25 minutes, or until squash is tender.
