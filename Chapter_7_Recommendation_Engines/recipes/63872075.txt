Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz white chocolate chips; 8 oz semi sweet chocolate chips; 1 1/2 cups peanut butter; 1 tsp vanilla (optional)

Directions:
1. Grease a cookie sheet and line with parchment paper. 2. Mix white chocolate chips with peanut butter and melt in microwave until half melted, then stir; set aside. 3. Melt semi sweet chocolate chips in microwave until half melted, then stir; set aside. 4. Spread peanut butter mixture evenly on baking sheet. 5. Evenly distribute the melted semi sweet chocolate on top of peanut butter. 6. Using the tip of a sharp knife, marbleize the chocolate into peanut butter. 7. Refrigerate for 1/2-2 hours; cut into pieces, then store in air tight container.
