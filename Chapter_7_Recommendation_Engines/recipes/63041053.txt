Title:
Angel Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter - softened
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp cream of tartar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour

Directions:
Conbime ingredients in order given.  Roll into balls and pat with a fork.  Bake at 350 for 8-9 minutes until light golden.

Submitter: 
Sheila Kampman
Description: 
A recipe from my childhood. Ingredients are always on hand for these rich, delicious cookies.
