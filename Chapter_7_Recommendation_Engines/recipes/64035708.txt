Title:
Banana Blondie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup skim milk
2 eggs
4 ripe bananas, mashed
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.  Toss nuts and chips in 1 table spoon of flour.
In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, stevia, baking powder and baking soda. In a separate bowl, stir together the applesauce, vanilla, milk, eggs and bananas; pour into the dry ingredients and mix well. Stir in nuts and chips.  Spread evenly in the prepared pan.
Bake for 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
