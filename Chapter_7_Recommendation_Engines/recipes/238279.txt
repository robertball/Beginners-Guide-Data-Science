Title:
Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free Vanilla Banana Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1226771.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large very ripe bananas
1 cup unsweetened vanilla-flavored almond milk (such as Almond BreezeÂ®)
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Blend bananas, almond milk, and honey together in a blender until smooth. Pour banana mixture into ice pop molds and freeze until firm, 8 hours to overnight. Run warm water over the mold to release ice pops.

Submitter: 
Allie
Description: 
A gluten- and dairy-free vanilla or chocolate banana pop. Perfect for the summer, these pops are incredibly easy to make and use only 3 to 4 ingredients. Experiment with different fruits such as peaches, strawberries, blueberries or a combination of your choice. Your kids will love these, as will you! Enjoy!
