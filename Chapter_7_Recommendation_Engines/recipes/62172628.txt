Title:
Chocalate-chip Zucchini Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C butter
1 3/4C sugar
1/2C cooking oil
2 eggs
1tsp vanilla
1/2C sour milk
1/2tsp ground cinnamon
1/2tsp ground cloves
2 1/2 C flour
1/4c cocoa
1tsp baking powder
1tsp baking soda
2C finely grated zucchini
1C cholalate chips
1C chopped peacans

Directions:
In mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add the oil, eggs, vanilla and sour milk;beat well.
In another bowl, combine cinnamon, cloves,flour,cocoa, baking powder and baking soda. Add to liquid mixture. Add zucchini, mix well. Pour in grease 13x9 baking pan. Sprinkle chocolate chips and nuts on top. Bake for 45min.Cool in pan on rack.
