Title:
Raspberry Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup heavy cream
1/3 cup red raspberry preserves
2 1/4 tsp sugar
1 1/2 tsp raspberry liqueur

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat heavy whipping cream until it begins to thicken.
Add raspberry preserves, sugar and raspberry liqueur; beat until stiff peaks form.
Spread on tops of bottom and middle cake layers.
