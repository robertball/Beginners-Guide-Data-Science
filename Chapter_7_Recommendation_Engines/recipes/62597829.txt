Title:
Oatmeal Cinnamon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3 cups oats
1 cup cinnamon chips (I can only find these around December but they are WORTH it)

Directions:
Beat butter and Dugger, add eggs and vanilla extract, add flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Stir in oats and cinnamon chips.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes.
