Title:
Mom's Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
both sauteed in butter
2 cans of chream of chicken soup( cream of celery or cream of mushroom soup or a combination of these may be used)
Salt and pepper to taste
1 pound box shells pasta
2 small cans tuna drained
dried bread crumbs (bread left out for day)
milk

Directions:
Dry bread.
Cook pasta al dente.
Sautee onions and celery in butter in skillet.
Add 2 cans cream of chicken soup and ~1/2 can milk(or more) to onions and celery to thin out mixture.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add drained tuna.
Warm mixture.
Place cooked and drained pasta to separate casserole dish.
Add sauce mixture to casserole dish.
Melt some butter and mix into bread crumbs until damp.
Sprinkle bread crumbs on top.
Cook 30-45 mins on 375 degrees.

Submitter: 
Lisa Ann Critchfield
Description: 
Tuna and pasta just like mom made it!
If the kids don't like tuna, just tell them it's chicken! Works every time!
