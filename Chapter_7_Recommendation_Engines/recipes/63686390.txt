Title:
Aunt Bea's Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz med egg noodles
8 oz lite cream chees
6 Tbl soft marg
3 large eggs
1/2 cup sugar (splenda)
1 cup milk
1 can fruit cocktail, drained
topping:
6 Tbl marg
1 cup crushed corn flakes
1/2 cup sugar (splenda)
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Cook noodles according to pkg directions. Drain & add cream cheese & marg. Toss until melted. Add eggs, sugar, milk & canned fruit.
Turn into buttered oblong dish
Topping: Melt marg, stir together with other ingredients. Top casserole
Bake at 350 for 45 min
