Title:
Cherry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976247.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon dried tart cherries
1 small onion
1 clove garlic
3 tablespoons fruit-flavored vinegar
2 tablespoons orange juice
1 tablespoon honey
1/3 cup olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop cherries, onion and garlic in food processor; pulse until finely chopped.
Add fruit-flavored vinegar, orange juice and honey; puree.
With food processor on, slowly add olive oil; mix well.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
The Cherry Marketing Institute
Description: 
This vinaigrette dressing is perfect over a salad of butter lettuce, blue cheese, crispy bacon bits and toasted pine nuts. Garnish with additional dried tart cherries, if desired. Add grilled chicken for an entree salad.
