Title:
Mini Cheesecakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 8 oz packages cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
20 cupcake liners
20 vanilla wafers
20 oz can pie filling (cherry or blueberry)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Beat together cream cheese, sugar, eggs, lemon juice and vanilla until light and fluffy.
Line muffin pan with liners.  Place one vanilla wafer in bottom of each liner.  Fill liners 3/4 full with cream cheese mixture.
Bake for 15-20 minutes.  Top with pie filling and chill.
