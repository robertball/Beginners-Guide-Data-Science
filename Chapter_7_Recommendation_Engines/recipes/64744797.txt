Title:
Stuffed Turkey Meatloaf by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6528356.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, diced
1 lb ground turkey
3/8c breadcrumbs (Italian)
2 tbsp ketchup
1 large egg
1/2 tsp salt or  Lowrys
Garlic powder, onion powder
Filling
3/4 c shredded mozzarella (or any)
1 oz chopped fresh baby spinach
Sauce
2 tbsp ketchup
1 tsp brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp Dijon
2 tbsp Worcestershire
Garlic powder
Variation: add Italian seasoning to meat mixture and sauce.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In large bowl mix turkey, onions, breadcrumbs, egg, seasonings. Mix well.
Spread meat mixture over parchment paper to form a large square or rectangle.
Sprinkle cheese and spinach over half of the meat mixture.
Gently fold other half of mixture over the filled side. Press together and shape into a log.
Spray cookie sheet with Pam and carefully transfer meatloaf onto it.
Bake 35 min.
In the meantime mix up the sauce. After 35 minutes add glaze to meat and return to over for an additional 10-15 minutes.
Let rest 5 minutes before serving.
