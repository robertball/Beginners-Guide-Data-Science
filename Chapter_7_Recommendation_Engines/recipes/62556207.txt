Title:
Herb and Cheese Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp sage leaves, crumbled
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 Tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 1/4 cup butter/sour milk
1 ea large egg

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease bottoms of 12 muffin-pan cups. Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup. In large bowl, combine flour sugar, baking powder, baking soda, sage, parsley and cheese, blend well. Add butter/sour milk, butter and egg; stir just until moistened. Fill prepared muffin cups 2/3rds full. Bake at 400 for 15 - 20 minutes.
