Title:
Grandma's Jam Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cups blackberry jam or jelly
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup buttermilk
1 tsp cloves
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
3 eggs
Quick Caramel Frosting:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
1 3/4 cups confectioners sugar
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Cake:
Cream shortening and sugar
Add eggs and blackberry jam and beat thoroughly
Sift the flour, measure and resift with soda, salt and spices
Add alternately with milk to the first mixture
Beat well
Pour into well greased tube or bundt cake pan
Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes
Frosting:
Melt butter
Add sugar and boil for 2 minutes
Add milk and stir until it comes back to a boil
Remove from heat and let cool
Add confectioners sugar and beat until a good consistency to spread

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
A delicious fruit and spice flavored cake that Grandma used to make for us at least once a month.  She frosted with Quick Caramel Frosting.
