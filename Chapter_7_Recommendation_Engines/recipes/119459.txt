Title:
Baked Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985845.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch slices
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
1 tablespoon minced fresh oregano
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a bowl, toss the zucchini, butter and oregano. Arrange in a single layer on a greased baking sheet or shallow baking dish; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes or until golden brown. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Betty  Castleberry
Description: 
I've been preparing zucchini this way for years. It's a great side dish with many entrees. -Betty Castleberry, Livonia, Michigan
