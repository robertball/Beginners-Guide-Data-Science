Title:
Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cooked and cut in cubes
1 10 oz can chicken broth
1 10 oz can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 jar salsa
1 large bag corn chips (or tortilla chips)
2 cups cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Line 9 x 13 inch pan with corn chips.  Layer with chicken.
Combine chicken broth and mushroom soup and pour over chicken.
Next layer with salsa, corn chips and cheese.  Bake for 30-40 minutes.
