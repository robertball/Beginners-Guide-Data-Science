Title:
Tiramisu Angel Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/387243.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 box angel food cake mix
(add 1 1/4 cups water)
Kahlua 50ml (mini bottle)
1 package Starbucks VIA instant coffee
6 oz water
Filling:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1 small package French Vanilla pudding mix
Frosting:
1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
2 tablespoons heavy cream
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine cake mix and water as directed on box. Bake in 9 x 13-inch cake pan. Allow to cool. Cut cake into two layers.
Combine instant coffee and water. Mix until disolved. Drizzle the bottom layer of the cake with coffee mixture and mini bottle of Kahlua
To make filling, beat together cream cheese, heavy cream, butter and vanilla pudding powder until smooth. Spread mixture over bottom layer of the cake. Place top layer on the cake.
To make icing, blend together the cream cheese, heavy cream and vanilla until light and fluffy. Slowly mix in confectioner's sugar. Beat until smooth. Spread icing on top and sides of the cake.
