Title:
Bubble Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground meat
*1 can/jar pasta sauce
1 to 2 containers of refridgerated biscuits
1/2 lb mozzarella
1/4 lb cheddar
*Manwich seasoning or taco seasoning can be added to sauce, depending on taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, lightly spray cassarole dish with non-stick cooking spray.  Cut refridgerated biscuits in 1/4's and throw into dish, covering the bottom.
Brown ground meat and drain, add pasta sauce. Pour meat sauce on top of biscuits, covering them completely.
Top with cheese.
Bake in oven for 30-35 minutes.
