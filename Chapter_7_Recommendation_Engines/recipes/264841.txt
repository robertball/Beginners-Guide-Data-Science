Title:
Basil Cucumber Smash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5418699.jpg
Ingredients:
3 slices cucumber, plus more for garnish
3 basil leaves, plus more for garnish
1 1/2 teaspoons superfine sugar
1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
1 cup crushed ice

Directions:
In a Collins glass, muddle cucumber, basil, and sugar until sugar is dissolved. Add vodka and crushed ice. Stir until well chilled. Garnish with an additional cucumber slice and basil leaf.

Submitter: 
Juliana Hale
Description: 
Crushed ice, and plenty of it, is key to this and other smashes. It's what, in pre-freezer days, made this 
cocktail style seem so very indulgent.
