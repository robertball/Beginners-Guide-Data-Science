Title:
Grilled Shrimp Po' Boys Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 French rolls, split and hinged
4 tablespoons melted butter
1 teaspoon minced garlic
2 tablespoons Creole seasoning
2 pounds jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 cups shredded lettuce
1 tomato
Remoulade sauce:
1/2 cup mayo
1 tablespoon horseradish
1 teaspoon pickle relish
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 tablespoons Kikkoman Ponzu Lime

Directions:
Combine butter and garlic, spread on rolls and put on grill until brown. Toss shrimp in creole seasoning and marinade for a bit then throw on the grill until cooked.  Add shrimp to roll, followed by shredded lettuce and tomato. Drizzle with remoulade to taste.
Remoulade sauce: Mix mayo, horseradish, pickle relish, minced garlic, cayenne pepper, and ponzu in a bowl.
