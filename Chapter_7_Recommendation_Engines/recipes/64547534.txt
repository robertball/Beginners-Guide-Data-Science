Title:
Spicy Fiesta Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3249905.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs boneless chicken breasts or chicken thighs
1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 3/4 oz) kernal corn, rinsed and drained
3/4 (28 oz) can of diced tomatoes, undrained
8 oz of La Victoria Salsa Victoria or salsa of your choice
1 pkg McCormick slow cooker fiesta chicken seasoning mix
1-2 TBS New Mexico Hot Chili Powder
1 small onion, diced
1/2-2/3 cup chopped cilantro
Chopped New Mexico Hatch green chili or chopped fresh jalapenos
1 small can of sliced black olives

Directions:
1.  Place Chicken in slow cooker
2.  Mix beans, corn, tomatoes, seasoning mix, chili powder together in a bowl.
3.  Add onion, cilantro, green chili ( I use about a cup of Hot Hatch chili or 1/3
cup chopped Jalapenos), and black olives to the mixture and stir.
4.   Pour mixture over chicken and cover.
5.  Cook 8 hours on low or 4 hours on high.
6.  Remove chicken from slow cooker to a large bowl.
7.  Using 2 forks shred the chicken and return to slow cooker, mix well.
8.  Serve in tacos with your favorite toppings, over rice, or in a burrito

Submitter: 
Snow Borden
Description: 
A delightfully spicy version of Fiesta Chicken.  A delicious shredded chicken recipe with bright flavors and wonderful seasonings.  Great in tacos or served over rice!  Spice it up and enjoy!
