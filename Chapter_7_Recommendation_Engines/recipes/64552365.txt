Title:
Steakhouse Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, cubed
1 lb fresh mushrooms, sliced or 1/4'd
2 tsp. dried basil (or less if using fresh)
1/2 tsp. dried oregano (or less if using fresh)
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
1/4 tsp. garlic powder (or a minced or crushed clove)
1 tsp. browning sauce

Directions:
In large skillet, melt butter.
Add mushrooms and garlic clove (if using); saut√© until tender.
Stir in seasonings and browning sauce.
Reduce heat
Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, for 3-5 minutes to allow flavors to blend.

Deena Kordt
Description: 
Yummy mushroom & herb topping for steaks, cutlets or potatoes.
