Title:
Serrano Ham and Havarti Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/641040.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large flour tortillas
1/2 lb 227 g finely sliced Serrano Ham
(or any other air cured ham like Westfalian or Prosciutto)
1 cup 250 mL DofinoÂ® Havarti Cheese, grated
Your favourite dip ~ we used a Red Pepper Dip

Directions:
Divide the ham and Havarti evenly among the 4 tortillas. Fold the tortillas in half. Brush with olive oil and place in a medium skillet and toast until brown on both sides. Repeat with remaining tortillas. Slice and serve immediately.
Makes 4 - 6 servings.
