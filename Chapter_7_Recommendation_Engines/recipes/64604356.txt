Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp EVOO
4 cloves of garlic
3 Tbsp all purpose flour
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup milk
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt
pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large saute pan over medium-high heat
Add garlic and saute one minute
Sprinkle with flour, stir to combine
Saute for an additional minute
Slowly add chicken broth, whisking to combine until smooth
Whisk in milk
Cook an additional minute to thicken
Stir in Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper until the cheese is melted
