Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Butter
1 1/2 Cups White Rice
1/2 Cup Chopped Onion
1/2 Cup Chopped Green Pepper
1 Clove Garlic
2 Cups Water
1 14oz. Can Rotel With Chilies
1 Tespoon Chili Powder
1 Cup Salt
2 Teaspoons Sugar
1 Cup Shredded Cheddar

Directions:
In large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.  Add rice, onion, pepper and garli.  Cook until rice is brown and vegetables are tender.  Stir in water, tomatoes, chili powder, salt and sugar.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat.  Simmer covered until rice is tender (about 15 -20 minutes).  Stir in cheese and transfer to serving bowl.
