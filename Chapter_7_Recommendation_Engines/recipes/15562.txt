Title:
Peanut Butter Balls VII Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/568042.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup honey
1 1/2 cups dry milk powder

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together the peanut butter and honey. Add the powdered milk and mix until well blended. Roll into walnut sized balls and serve.
