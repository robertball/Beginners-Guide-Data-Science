Title:
THE BEST Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
2/3 cup shortening
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Mix flour and salt together; take out 1/3 cup and set aside. Work shortening into the first flour/salt mixture until crumbly. With 1/3 cup of second mixture, add 1/4 cup water and blend. Add flour/water mixture to flour/shortening mixture and stir lightly,  until a pastry ball can be formed. Refrigerate for 20 minutes. divide in two pastry balls. Roll out each as needed. On floured surface, roll out 8 or 9 inch crusts.
