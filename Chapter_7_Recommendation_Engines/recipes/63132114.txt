Title:
Banana, Oatmeal Breakfast Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups Oats
2-3 super-ripe Bananas
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
to taste Cinnamon

Directions:
Mix everything together.  Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 35 minutes.
