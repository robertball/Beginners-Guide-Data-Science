Title:
Eggnog Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C graham cracker crumbs
3T sugar
1/4t ground ginger
1/4t ground cinnamon
1/4t ground nutmeg
3T unsalted butter, melted
12oz cream cheese, softened
1/3C sugar
1 egg
3/4C eggnog
1T bourbon (opt)
graham cracker crumbs and/or crystalized ginger (opt)

Directions:
For crust: Combine graham cracker crumb, 3T sugar, ginger, cinnamon and nutmeg in med. bowl. Mix in melted butter w/fork. Transfer crumbs to an 8x8x2-in baking pan lined w/foil or parchment paper. Press flat and even. Bake in 375 over for 5 min. Remove from oven; reduce oven temp to 325.
For filling: In lg. bowl, beat cream cheese on med-high speed until smooth, about 1 min. Add 1/3C sugar; beat 1 min more. Add egg; beat until smooth. Add eggnog and, if desired, bourbon. Beat on med. speed 1 min. more, until mixture is light and smooth.
Pour filling over crust in pan. Bake for 28-30 min. or until center is just set. Cool in pan on wire rack for 1 hour. Cover and refrig. at least 4 hours before serving. Garnish if you like.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Long cooling and chilling time but can easily be made to low sugar and fat dessert.
