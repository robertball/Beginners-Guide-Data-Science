Title:
Sticky Bun Breakfast Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube Pillsbury grands buttermilk biscuits
3 T butter, melted
1/2 cup maple syrup
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 t cinnamon
1/4 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Spray a fluted bundt pan with non stick spray. Combine the melted butter and syrup in a bowl and set aside. In another bowl, combine the brown sugar, cinnamon, and nuts. Place about half the syrup into the bottom of the pan. Then sprinkle half the brown sugar mixture on top of that. Lay the biscuits in pan overlapping the edges to form a circle around the pan. Top with remaining syrup and sugar mixtures. Bake at 375 for about 20 minutes until golden brown.
Let cool for a minute, then turn it out onto a plate and serve.
