Title:
Halloween Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen cheese pizza
1 can (6oz) pitted ripe olives, drained and halved
1 medium sweet red pepper
1 small green pepper

Directions:
Place pizza on pan.  Arrange olives in a circle around edge of pizza.  Cut a nose, eyes and mouth from red pepper.  Cut a stem and eyebrows from the green pepper.  Make a jack-o'-lantern face on pizza with the peppers.  Bake according to package directions.
