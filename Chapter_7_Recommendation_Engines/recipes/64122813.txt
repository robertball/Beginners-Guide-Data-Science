Title:
Blue Cheese & Chive Crisps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. Blue cheese
1/2 C. unsalted butter
1 egg
1 egg yolk
2 tsp. chopped fresh chives
black pepper
2 C. all-purpose flour

Directions:
The day before serving, beat together the cheese and butter until well blended. Add the egg, egg yolk, chives and pepper to taste and beat until just blended.
Add flour in three batches, folding in well between each addition.
Divide dough in half and shape each half into a log about 2 inches in diameter. Wrap in waxed paper and chill overnight.
Preheat oven to 375. Lightly grease 2 baking sheets. Cut dough logs about 1/8 inc thick and place on baking sheets.Bake about 10 minutes, until just golden around the edges. Transfer to wire rack to cool.
