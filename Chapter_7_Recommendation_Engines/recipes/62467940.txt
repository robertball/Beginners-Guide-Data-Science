Title:
Broccoli Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/496126.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped broccoli
3/4 cups instant rice
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk
8 oz cheddar cheese
1/4 cup parmesean cheese
1 tablespoon butter
1 bouillon cube
1 large onion, chopped
1 small grated carrot
salt and pepper to taste
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Cook broccoli according to package directions.
Cook rice - sautee onions, butter, buillon cube and rice - then add water - cook accordingly - after complete add grated carrot to rice.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium saucepan over low heat, mix cream of chicken soup and milk. Gradually stir in cheeses until melted.
In a large mixing bowl, combine broccoli, rice, soup and cheese mixture. Season with salt and pepper, garlic salt, garlic powder. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, until bubbly and lightly brown.
