Title:
Taco Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030789.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 packet low-sodium taco seasoning
3/4 cup water
1 16 oz. can tomato sauce
1 can low-sodium black beans, drained
2 cups cooked white rice
Tortilla chips/corn chips/tortillas
Your favorite taco toppings - salsa, cheese, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, etc.

Directions:
Brown meat in skillet and drain.
Add taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
Stir in tomato sauce, black beans, and rice.
Heat in skillet for 5-10 minutes.
Serve over tortilla chips/corn chips/tortillas.
Top with your favorite taco toppings
