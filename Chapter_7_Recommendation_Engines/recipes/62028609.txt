Title:
Pineapple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans crushed pineapple, drained
1 C. flour
1 C. brown sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 C. melted butter

Directions:
In a 9 inch square pan, spread well drained crushed pineapple. In a bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and egg until smooth. Spoon evenly over pineapple, then pour butter over mixture and sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes. Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Bobby Darapureddi
Description: 
Excellent served warm with vanilla ice cream.
