Title:
Chicken and Spaghetti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c flour
6 T butter
3 cup  canned chicken broth
1 cup milk
1 small can sliced mushroom's
8 oz. miracle whip
3 or 4 boneless chicken breast
1 lb. spaghetti. broken into small pieces

Directions:
Cook chicken & shred  (save broth)
add flour to the canned chicken broth and whisk until smooth, add melted  butter, milk and liquid from jar of mushrooms cook until thick stirring to keep smooth
remove from heat and add miracle whip
cook spg. in chicken stock from cooked chicken
mix all the above ingredients, put in 9/13 buttered casserole dish, cover with crushed potato chips.
bake 350' for 30 minutes or until bubbly
