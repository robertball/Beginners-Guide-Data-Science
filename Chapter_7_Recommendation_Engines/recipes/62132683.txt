Title:
Berrylicious Blast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup fresh blueberries
1/2 Cup sliced fresh strawberries
ice
7 oz sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp honey
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Fill blender 3/4 full with ice
Add berries, sweetened condensed milk, honey and vanilla extract.
Pulse until ice and berry mixture combines.
Whip for 2 minutes
Pour into glasses and add whipped cream
