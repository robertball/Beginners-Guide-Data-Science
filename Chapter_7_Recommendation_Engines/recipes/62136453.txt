Title:
Smokey Hearty Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground hamburg or sausage
1 chopped onion
3 minced garlic cloves
1 tsp basil
2 tsps oregano
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1/8 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
28oz can diced fire roasted tomatoes
28oz can crushed fire roasted tomatoes
12 lasagna noodles, cooked
(2) 15oz cottage cheese
Â½ cup Parmesan cheese
1 tsp Italian seasoning
10oz box frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1 large egg white, lightly beaten
2 cups mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook hamburg or sausage until no longer pink, then drain fat. Add onions and garlic and cook until onions are done. Add basil through tomatoes to make sauce. Simmer for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, combine cottage cheese through egg white in a separate bowl.
Spread thin layer of sauce on bottom of 9x13in baking dish. Arrange 4 noodles on top, then spread half the cottage cheese mixture, followed by about 2 cups of sauce, and ending with 2/3 cup mozzarella cheese. Repeat these layers finishing with the sauce. Cover dish and bake at 350 degrees for one hour. Uncover dish, spread remaining 2/3 cup mozzarella and bake for 10 minutes. Removed from oven and let set for about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Suzie Toulouse Satterfield
Description: 
Fire roasted tomatoes, ground hamburg or sausage, and spinach bring out the flavor in this healthy and delicious lasagna that even the kids will love!
