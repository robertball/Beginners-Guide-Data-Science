Title:
Cowboy Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground beef
1 onion chopped
1 can of corn
4 pieces of bacon chopped
1 bottle of  barbecue sauce
2 boxes of corn bread mix
1 box of Velveeta cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
mix cornbread mix in large mixing bowl set aside
In large skillet brown ground beef and bacon drain grease then mix in onions, corn and barbecue sauce on medium heat.  While that's cooking grab 9x9 or 13x9 cake/casserole dish and grease the bottom pour a thin layer of the corn bread mix in the bottom and spread evenly place in oven for about 10 minutes.  Cut thin strips of Velveeta cheese and set aside. Remove pan from over make sure bread is firm pour mix from pan into cake pan place thin strips of cheese over mix then pour remaining balance of corn bread mix make sure to spread and cover the top layer of beef.  Cook for an additional 20 minutes or until top is golden brown.
