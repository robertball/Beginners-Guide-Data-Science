Title:
Massaman Curry Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 dried chilies soaked in hot water for 15 min
3 T chopped shallots
2 T garlic
1 t galangal
1 1/4 T chopped lemon grass
2 cloves
1 T coriander seeds
5 pepper corns
1 t shrimp paste
1 t salt

Directions:
Fry first 7 until golden brown and grind into a powder.
Blend all but shrimp until blended. Add shrimp paste and grind to a fine paste.
Store in the fridge for 4 months.
