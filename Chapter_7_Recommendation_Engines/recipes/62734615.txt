Title:
BANNOCK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/783868.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups flour
1 cup lard or marg.
3 Tbsp.baking powder
1 Tbsp. salt
3 1/2 cups water

Directions:
Mix flour and lard by hand till small pellets form.Add baking powder and salt.Then add water,knead into dough for 3 min.
Campfire bake:
Divide dough into 4 sections,wrap around 4 inch stick.Prop over coals and bake 40-45min.
Oven bake:
Spread dough on grease 16 inch pan.Bake at 425f for 20 min. till golden.
