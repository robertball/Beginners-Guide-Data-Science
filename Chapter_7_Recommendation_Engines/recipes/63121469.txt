Title:
Italian Restaurant-Style Salad Dressing II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoons corn syrup
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons grated Romano cheese
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon Italian-style seasoning
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tsp sugar

Directions:
In a blender, combine the mayonnaise, vinegar, oil, corn syrup, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, garlic, Italian seasoning, parsley and lemon juice and sugar.  Blend until smooth. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Pam Winders McCain
Description: 
This dressing is delicious and just like the creamy dressing served at a popular Italian restaurant. You may add sugar to taste if it seems too tart.Modified from: Italian Restaurant-Style Salad Dressing II
