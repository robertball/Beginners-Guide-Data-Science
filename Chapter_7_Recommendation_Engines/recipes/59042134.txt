Title:
Budget Best Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/265891.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup vegetable shortening
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cream together the butter, shortening, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.  Stir in flour, baking soda, salt, and chocolate chips. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
By purchasing store brand or sale priced baking ingredients as well as reducing the amount of sugar and substituting half of the butter with vegetable shortening, these cookies bake up into the perfect combination of crisp and chewy.  These won't last long in the cookie jar.Modified from: Best Chocolate Chip Cookies
