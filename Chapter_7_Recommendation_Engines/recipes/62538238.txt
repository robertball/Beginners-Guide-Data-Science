Title:
Louisiana Yam Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522148.jpg
Ingredients:
Yams
2 Cans (30 oz) Yams
1/4 cup butter
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp nutmeg
Topping
1/4 c butter
3/4 c brown sugar
3 tsp flour
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Yams
Drain yams, Mix all ingredients together with mixer. Spoon into casserole dish.
Topping
Melt butter and add brown sugar, flour and pecans. Spinkle topping over casserole.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
MSLIB5
Description: 
I originally got this recipe from a cookbook done by the Louisiana Chapter 24 of the Pioneers of America cookbook entitled Pots, Pans, and Pioneers.  I had the book for over 20 years and it fell apart, but I saved the recipe.

We serve it every Thanksgiving.
