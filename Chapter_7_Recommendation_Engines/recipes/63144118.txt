Title:
Bevy's Butter Bean Soup, Crock pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/804249.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cans butter beans
1/2 can water
1/2 chopped Poblano Pepper
1/2 chopped Vidalia Onion
1/3 cup chopped raw bacon
1 t. cumin
1 t. savory
1 t. adobo

Directions:
Put evertyhing in crock pot. Cook on high 5-6 hours. Serve with dollop of sour cream.
