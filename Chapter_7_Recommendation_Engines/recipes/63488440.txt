Title:
Chicken Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground chicken breast
1/2 cup of finely minced onions
1/2 cup of finely diced red bell pepper
2 cloves of minced garlic
1 egg
1 cup of whole wheat bread crumbs
1/4 tsp of paprika
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp of dried thyme
1/4 tsp of dried basil
1/4 tsp of dried sage
1/4 tsp of red pepper flakes
1/2 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
1. Begin heating grill or grill pan.
2. Heat oil in a small skillet.
3. Saute onions and peppers until the onions are translucent...about 6 minutes.
4. Mix sauteed veggies with remaining ingredients.
5. Form into 4 burger patties.
6. Grill burgers for 5-6 minutes on each side.  If you are adding cheese, add after 3 minutes of cooking the second side.
7. Remove from grill and serve as desired.

Submitter: 
MalsGTown
Description: 
My wife and I were in the mood for burgers but wanted something a little healthier.  So we came up with this after reviewing a few other chicken burger recipes.  They aren't anything spectacular, but they are an easy, healthy, and tasty dish.
