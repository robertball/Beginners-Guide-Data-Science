Title:
Garlic Parsley Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 16oz. package angel hair pasta
4 minced garlic cloves
1/2 c. olive oil
1/2 c. fresh minced parsley
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions on package.
Drain.
In a separate pan, mix together oil and garlic cloves. Lightly simmer until cloves turn golden brown.
Add the pasta and toss gently.
Add parsley and toss again.
Season to taste.
