Title:
Snappy Balsamic Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon
1 cup chopped onions
3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 (16 ounce) bag frozen cut green beans

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside.
While bacon is cooking, prepare green beans in a pan according to package directions, drain.
Saute onions in bacon grease left on bottom of the pan; add vinegar, and saute until onions are tender.
Add green beans and cook until heated through.
Crumble the bacon on top.
