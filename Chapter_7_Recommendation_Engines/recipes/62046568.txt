Title:
Easy Cheesy Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pasta, cooked al dente
2 cups mushrooms, sliced
6 back bacon rashers, 1/2 inch pieces
6 cloves garlic, chopped
1 cup spring onions, chopped
1 1/2 tubs cream
1 chicken cube, crushed
1 cup freshly shredded Parmesan cheese
Extra Parmesan Cheese for topping

Directions:
1. In a saucepan, fry up bacon until slightly crsipy. Add in mushrooms, cook until soft then toss in the spring opnions, chicken cube, garlic, cheese and cream. Mix well, lower heat and let simmer for about 5 mins. Serve over your favourite pasta.

Submitter: 
justluv2cook
Description: 
This is a simple and easy to make pasta dish. The smoky aroma of fried bacon makes the cream sauce more flavoursome. The garlic and spring onions just make it even betterrr!
