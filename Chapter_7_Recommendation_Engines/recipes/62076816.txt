Title:
Amy's Famous Orange Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small tub cool whip
1 small container cottage cheese
1 package (6 serving size) Orange JELLO
1 small can crushed pineapple, drained
1 small can mandarine oranges, drained

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl; Mix Well. Store in refigerator over night to let the JELLO set.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description:
