Title:
Skillet Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992492.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Chicken Broth
3/4 cup water
1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves, crushed
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 cup uncooked regular long-grain white rice
2 cups fresh OR frozen broccoli flowerets
Paprika

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned. Remove chicken.
Add broth, water, basil and garlic. Heat to a boil. Stir in rice. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes.
Stir in broccoli. Top with chicken. Sprinkle with paprika. Cover and cook 15 minutes or until rice is done.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Use your family's favorite vegetable combination in this fragrant and delicious chicken and herb-seasoned rice skillet dinner. Toss a salad while the ingredients simmer undisturbed, and voila: Dinner is served.
