Title:
Bisquick Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon (pinch) salt
1 tablespoons shortening, oil, or melted butter

Directions:
Sift flour into medium bowl.
Add baking powder using and salt using a whisk or large spoon to fully incorporate.
Cut in shortening, oil, or cold butter using a pastry cutter.  If using melted butter, be sure to evenly disperse the butter as much as possible

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
Quick and easy substitute for Bisquick.
