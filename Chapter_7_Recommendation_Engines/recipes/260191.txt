Title:
Tito's Classic Gimlet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4557424.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces Tito's Handmade Vodka
1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
1 ounce simple syrup
Garnish:
Lime wedge or wheel

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice.
Shake well, and strain into a chilled martini glass.
Garnish with lime wedge or wheel.

Submitter: 
Tito's Handmade Vodka
Description: 
A classic gimlet recipe with vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup gets fancy with Tito's Handmade Vodka.
