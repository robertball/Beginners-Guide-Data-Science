Title:
Hot Peppermint Patty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz peppermint schnapps
1/2 oz dark crème de cacao
1 tsp crème de menthe
hot chocolate
whipped cream
shaved chocolate or chocolate sprinkles for garnish

Directions:
Pour the liqueurs into an Irish coffee glass.
Fill with hot chocolate.
Top with whipped cream.
Garnish with shaved chocolate or chocolate sprinkles.

Submitter: 
Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
You have got to love spiked hot chocolate and this is one of the best recipes out there for it. Both peppermint schnapps and crème de menthe add that minty snap to this intensely chocolate warm drink.
