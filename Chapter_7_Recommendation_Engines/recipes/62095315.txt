Title:
bacon wrapped bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bananas (ripe)
1 tbsp. honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
10 bacon slices cut in half

Directions:
Cut bananas into chunks.
Blend the soy sauce and honey and marinate bananas for half an hour.
Wrap each piece of banana in bacon.
Use a toothpick to secure.
Dip in the remaining marinate mixture and broil for about 8-10 minutes.
