Title:
Easy Coleslaw Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. mayonnaise
1/8 c. sugar
1 1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
1 tbsp. vinegar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.  Serve right away over coleslaw, or chill in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
GarlicQueen
Description: 
A creamy coleslaw dressing that can be made with ingredients you already have!
