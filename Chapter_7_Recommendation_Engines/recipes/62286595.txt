Title:
Garlic Alfredo Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/383546.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 tilapia fillets
2-3 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp creole seasoning (or more, if you desire)
3 tbsp butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup alfredo sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F.
Place fillets in shallow baking pan. Coat them with olive oil and sprinkle creole seasoning (more or less, to your taste).
Bake for 10 minutes.
In small pan, melt butter and add minced garlic.
Stir in alfredo sauce.
When fish is ready, top with the sauce and if desired, add a dusting more of creole seasoning.
