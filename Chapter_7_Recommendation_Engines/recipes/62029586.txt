Title:
Jackie's Easy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 onion
2 14.5 oz cans kidney beans
2 14.5 oz cans pinto beans
1 can rotel chopped tomatoes and green chiles
1 can chopped tomatoes
2 8 oz cans tomato sauce
1/4 cup chili powder

Directions:
Brown the ground beef and onion salting and peppering to taste.  Drain.  Transfer into dutch oven.  Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer for about 20 minutes.  DELICIOUS!  Even better the next day.  This recipe can also be put into a crockpot on low for about 6 hours for an easy leave it and go recipe.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Allison
Description: 
This is a super easy and delicious chili recipe.  I get compliments on it all the time!
