Title:
Cauliflower Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large cauliflower
1/2 pint creme fraiche
2 cups mature cheddar cheese, grated
3 heaped tbsp finely grated parmesan cheese
1 large egg, beaten
2 cloves garlic, crushed
2 shallots, finely diced
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 cup unsalted crackers, finely crushed
2 tbsp fresh thyme
2 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
A drizzle of olive oil
Salt and black pepper to season

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Cut cauliflower into florets then steam until just tender. Allow to cool, then chop roughly.
In a large bowl, mix together the creme fraiche, beaten egg, cheeses, garlic, shallots, nutmeg, and cayenne pepper. Season with a little salt and plenty of black pepper.  Fold in cauliflower.
In a small bowl, toss together crushed crackers, thyme, and parsley.
Pour the cauliflower mixture into a small baking dish and spread out evenly.
Sprinkle with the cracker mixture and drizzle with a little olive oil.
Bake in a preheated oven for approx. 30 - 40 mins or until nice and golden.
Allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
