Title:
Tuna Souvlaki Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 red onion, chopped bite-size
2 stalks celery, chopped bite-size
1 sprig parsley, minced, stem removed
4 basil leaves, rolled into a cigar shape and sliced in ribbons
8 oz colorful pasta, such as tricolor rotini or spinach wheels
1/2 cup roasted red peppers, jarred or fresh, drained, OR 1/2 cup diced bell pepper, raw
1/4 cup smoked sun-dried tomatoes OR 1/2 cup fresh diced roma tomatoes
2 tsp capers
1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled, plain OR seasoned
1 can light tuna in water, drained and flaked
1 cup Greek vinaigrette OR balsamic (not sweet)

Directions:
Chop onion and soak in ice-cold water in the refrigerator for no less than 30 minutes (to remove some of the bite).
Chop remaining raw vegetables.
Boil pasta, rinse in cold water.
Mix all ingredients. Be sure to drain and rinse your onions again.
Allow to rest in refrigerator for 4 hours to overnight.

Submitter: 
JARRIE
Description: 
A non-traditional dinner-style pasta salad for those favoring a vinaigrette dressing. Keep some minty gum handy for afterwards!
