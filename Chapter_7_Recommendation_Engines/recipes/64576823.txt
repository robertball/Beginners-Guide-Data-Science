Title:
Butterscoth Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 tsp. vanilla
3/4 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts, optional
Frosting:
2 tbsp. butter or margarine
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp. milk
1 cup icing sugar

Directions:
Melt butter.
Add sugar and mix.
Add egg and vanilla and mix.
Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Spread in greased 8" x 8" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 20 - 22 minutes, until sides show signs of pulling away form pan.
Frost.
Frosting:
Mix first 3 ingredients in saucepan.
Bring to boil & simmer 2 minutes.
Remove from heat. Cool.
Add icing sugar and stir well. Adjust icing sugar and milk as needed to make a smooth consistency.
Frost brownies.

A chewy brownie with a caramel flavor
