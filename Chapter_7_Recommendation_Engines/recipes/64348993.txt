Title:
Vietnmese Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons white sugar
2 tablespoons fish sauce (or soy if don't have)
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 stalks lemon grass, minced
2 teaspoons sesame seeds
1 pkg firm tofu, drained, pressed
12 leaves romaine lettuce
fresh cilantro for garnish
fresh basil for garnish
fresh mint for garnish
thinly sliced green onion for garnish

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the sugar, fish sauce, pepper, garlic, lemon grass, and sesame seeds. Place the tofu (or tempeh) in the dish, and stir to coat. Cover, and refrigerate for 4 hours.
Dry fry or bake tofu til browned.
Serve on lettuce leaves with rice vermicelli and bowls of cilantro, mint, basil, and sliced green onions. Let people add what they like to their roll. Have a dipping sauce or two (peanut?) available.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Traditional Vietnamese ingredients flavor this marinated tofu or tempeh, which is served in lettuce leaves with fresh basil, green onions, and mint.Modified from: Bo Nuong Xa
