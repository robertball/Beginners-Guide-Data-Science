Title:
Supremo Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535453.jpg
Ingredients:
1 low fat, low carb tortilla
2 ounces Lean ground turkey-w/taco seasoning
1/4 cup fat free refried beans
1 teaspoon light sour cream
1 tablespoon sliced black olives
1 tablespoon diced tomatoes
hot sauce to taste

Directions:
Brown and drain turkey.
Spread refried beadn over the tortilla, leaving 1/4 an inch of space from the edge of the tortilla. Layer turkey, sour cream, olives, tomatoes, and hot sauce into the center of the burrito.
To wrap, fold in the left and right rides and roll up like a jelly roll.
