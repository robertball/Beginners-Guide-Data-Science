Title:
Low 'N Slow Mushroom Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup pearl barley
1 cup water, boiled
2 Tbsp canola oil
1 onion, finely chopped
1 stalk celery, finely chopped
1 carrot, finely chopped
6 cups white/button mushrooms, cut in half if large
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp tomato paste
6 cups beef stock
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 Tbsp Worstershire sauce

Directions:
Place barley in a bowl and pour in boiling water.  Set aside for 10 minutes.
In a large saucepan, heat canola oil over medium heat.
Saute onion, celery and carrot until softened (5-7 minutes).
Add mushrooms and saute until they start to soften and brown (about 5 minutes).
Drain barley (discard soaking water) and add to pan.
Add garlic and tomato paste then stir well.
Add beef stock, oregano, salt, pepper and Worstershire sauce.
Bring to a boil then lower heat and simmer for one hour.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
This soup hits the spot all year round!  Serve with soft warm rolls and your family will be cheering!
