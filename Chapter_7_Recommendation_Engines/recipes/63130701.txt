Title:
Whole Wheat Cinnamon Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798785.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups quick cooking oats
1 cup whole wheat flour
2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
2 medium eggs, room temperature, lightly beaten
2 cups buttermilk
1/3 cup 2% milk

Directions:
Mix Buttermilk & Eggs & Milk with a whisk until blended & add in oats & whisk for 30 seconds. Let this mixture sit for 5 minutes so the oats can absorb the liquid. Add Flour, Sugar, Salt, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, & Cinnamon. Whisk until batter is completely blended.
Lightly grease pan with butter before cooking.
Cook over medium high heat in a frying pan.
