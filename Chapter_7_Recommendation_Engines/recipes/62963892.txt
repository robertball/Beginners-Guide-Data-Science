Title:
Brie Pesto Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/731514.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1 Baguette Loaf (Cut to 2-inch each)
- Brie Cheese
- Small Jar of Pesto Sauce
- 2 Cups of Sun-dried Tomatoes (Marinate in Tomato Sauce to keep moist during baking)

Directions:
1) Heat oven to 350 degrees
2) Spread Pesto Sauce onto Baguette Pieces (Top only)
3) Add thin slice of Brie Cheese on top
4) 2-3 Pieces of Sun-dried Tomatoes
5) Place in oven and Bake until edges of bread are golden brown and until cheese is melted. (Note: Make sure the Sun-dried Tomatoes dont burn)

Submitter: 
Mrs Sarah
Description: 
These are great appetizers for a get together or small starters before dinner!
