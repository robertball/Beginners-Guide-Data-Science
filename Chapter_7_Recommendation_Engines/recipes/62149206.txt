Title:
SS Roll - Spicy Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb imitation crab (krab) meat
3 scallions chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
lemon juice to taste
1tbsp hot chili sauce (Sriracha or Tiger Sauce)
salt to taste

Directions:
Shred the imitation crab meat in a large bowl. Add all remaining ingredients together, stir. Flavor more or less to taste. Chill for at least an hour to let flavors mingle.

Submitter: 
Colleen Hibbitts
Description: 
A spicy crab meat salad - perfect for party dip, or add to sushi rice for an incredible roll!
