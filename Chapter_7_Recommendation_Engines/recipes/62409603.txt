Title:
Chicken and White Bean Soup Mexican Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 large chicken breast halves, cubed (or two cups cooked chicken, add after cooking onion)
1 small white onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
3 cups chicken broth
1 heaping teaspoon chili powder
1 heaping teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon oregano
2 jalapeño peppers, seeded and chopped
2 cans white beans, rinsed
salt and pepper to taste
Garnishments:
Tortilla chips
Shredded Jack or Cheddar cheese
Sour cream
Green onions, chopped
Chopped cilantro

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large saucepan or stock pot. Add chicken, onion, and garlic. Sauté for 4 or 5 minutes.
When onion is tender, add chicken broth, chili powder, cumin, cayenne pepper, oregano, salt and pepper.
Simmer uncovered for 15 minutes.
Add white beans and jalapeño.
Add more spice or seasoning if desired.
Cover and simmer for 10 more minutes or so.
Serve with chips, shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions and cilantro if desired. Olé!

Submitter: 
KirklandCook
Description: 
Chicken, white beans and jalapeños make a tasty and savory soup. Serve with tortilla chips, shredded cheese and sour cream and you can't go wrong!
