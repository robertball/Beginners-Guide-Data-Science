Title:
Killer Mint Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package brownie mix, prepared as directed
2 sticks butter
2 tablespoons milk
2 cups powdered sugar
1 teaspoon peppermint extract
dash green food coloring
1 package semisweet chocolate chips, 12 oz

Directions:
Bake brownies as directed on box.  Put in refrigerator for 20 minutes when done.
Mint filling:    Cream together 1 stick butter, milk, powdered sugar, peppermint extract, and food coloring.  Spread over cooled brownies.  Put in refrigerator for 20 minutes.
Chocolate topping:   Melt together 1 stick of butter and chocolate chips.  Beat with mixer.  Pour over brownie and mint.  Cool.  Cut before too cool or chocolate will break.
