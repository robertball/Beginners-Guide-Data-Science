Title:
Not the Classic Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of bacon
16 oz. green beans
2 tbs water
2 tbs butter, room temperature
15 oz corn
8 oz cream cheese, room temperature
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 cup French fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Cook bacon until crispy, chop coarsely and set aside. Cook green beans, drain and cut in half. In medium bowl combine all ingredients except onions. put in a greased 9x12 dish. Top with onions. Cover and bake for 35-40 minutes.

Try this instead of the same old green bean casserole.
