Title:
TexMex  Beef and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/872579.jpg
Ingredients:
BEEF
2 lb. sirloin, thinly sliced
1 tsp southwest rub mix
1 tsp granulated garlic
1/2 tsp granulated onion
1 1/2 tsp Mrs. Dash southwest chipotle
1/4 tsp black pepper
RICE AND VEGGIES
1 c brown instant rice
1 c beef stock-low sodium (preferably homemade)
1 green bell or pablano pepper, sliced
1 sm sweet onion, sliced
2 clove garlic, minced
3 sm tomatoes, halved and sliced
1 jalapeno, seeded and sliced
1 tsp smoke paprika
1/2 tsp Mexican oregano
1/4 tsp cumin
Gouda chipotle cheese-or your favorite

Directions:
In a nonstick skillet place  pepper slices, onion slices, sliced jalapeno, minced garlic and slice tomatoes with about a tablespoon of olive oil. Add smoked paprika, cumin, Mexican oregano, and a dash of salt if youâ€™re a salt eater. Simmer for about 10 min. Add c. dry instant rice and 1 c. of beef stock. Stir together well and simmer about 5-10 min. longer till rice is tender.
Meanwhile, thinly slice beef and place on flat cooking surface, I use an electric. Griddle. Sprinkle with southwest rub, garlic gran. onion gran. And black pepper, mix around with tongs. Cook over med. high heat for about 10 min till done.
Serve beef over rice and veggies and top with grated cheese
