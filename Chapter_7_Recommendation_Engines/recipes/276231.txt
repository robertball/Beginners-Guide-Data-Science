Title:
Raspberry-Mint Mocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raspberry puree
1/3 cup fresh mint leaves, minced
1/4 cup lime juice
1 tablespoon maple syrup
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine raspberry puree, mint, lime juice, maple syrup, and salt together in a blender or food processor and blend.

Submitter: 
kate aspen
Description: 
This raspberry-mint mocktail is just the thing you need! The mint and lime juice give it a little zing to the sweet fruity fleshiness of the drink. An all-time classic, really. Wouldn't recommend consuming it after 5pm, however, since the sugar hits you like a train at full speed.
