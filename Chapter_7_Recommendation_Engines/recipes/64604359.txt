Title:
Chicken Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box ziti noodles
2 cups shredded, cooked chicken
1 batch alfredo sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Cook pasta al dente, drain
add pasta, chicken, and alfredo sauce to pan
Gently tossed to combine
Pour half of the pasta in a greased baking pan
Sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella
pour other half of pasta
Sprinkle with remaining cheese
top with parmesan cheese
Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese is melted and starting to turn brown
