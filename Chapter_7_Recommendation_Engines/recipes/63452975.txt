Title:
Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dried split peas
12 cups water
4 bay leaves
Sea Salt
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 large yellow onion
4 ribs chopped celery
3 chopped carrots
2 cups low-sodium veg/chicken broth
6 cups water

Directions:
In a crock pot combines all ingredients and cook on medium until consistency is thick.  Add ham bone and leave ham chunks in soup for better flavor.
