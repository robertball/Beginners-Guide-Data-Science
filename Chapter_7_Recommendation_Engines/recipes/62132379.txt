Title:
Banoffi Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Graham cookie base/pre-baked pie shell (or make your own)
1 can condensed milk
2 bananas, sliced
1 cup cream, whipped

Directions:
Place unopened can in a large pan of water. Boil for 2 hours over a med-low heat making sure that the can stays submerged (needs to be topped up regularly) Let can cool before opening.
Pour milk(now caramel)into crust.
Arrange sliced bananas over caramel.
Top with whipped cream.
