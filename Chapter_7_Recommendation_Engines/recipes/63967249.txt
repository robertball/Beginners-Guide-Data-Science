Title:
Rocky Road Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101006.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup white vinegar
2 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 cup mini marshmallows
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Combine milk and vinegar in large bowl and set aside for about 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in medium bowl. Whisk egg, vanilla and butter into milk mixture.  Add flour mixture to wet mixture and whisk until lumps are gone. Gently fold in marshmallows and walnuts.
Pour 1/3 cupfuls of batter into preheated skillet coated with cooking spray.  Flip when tops begin to bubble.  Cook until browned on other side.

Description: 
Serve with maple syrup or indulge with chocolate syrup and whipped cream.  Yummy either way!
