Title:
Brownie Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sugar
2 cups flour
1 1/3 cup cocoa
2 t baking powder
2 t salt
1 cup shortning

Directions:
Mix all but shortening.
Cut in shortening until looks like chocolate cornmeal.  Seal in plastic bag or jar at room temp.
Makes 8 cups of mix (4 pans of brownies)
To use:
Mix 2 eggs, 1 t vanilla, and 4 T melted butter in large bowl.
Lightly spoon in 2 cups of mix (do not pack mix while measuring).
Beat well w/spoon.
Bake in greased 8x8 pan @ 350 for 30-35"
