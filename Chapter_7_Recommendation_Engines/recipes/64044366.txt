Title:
Mixed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mixed Salad
Yield: 4  servings
1 head romain lettuce
1 tomato
1/2 can corn
1 carrot - shredded
1/4 cup Olive oil
1/4 cup Balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Wash and dry lettuce. Cut up and place in bowl.
Dice tomatoes and add to salad.
Sprinkle corn over salad
Finally shred the carrot over the salad
Put out the olive oil and balsamic for each person to add as desired.
