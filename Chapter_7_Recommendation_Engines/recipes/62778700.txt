Title:
French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Soup:
2 32 oz beef stock or broth
5 cloves of garlic, chopped
4 large sweet vidalia onions
2 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp italian seasoning
salt and pepper to taste
Topping:
Bread-frnech bread or focaccia bread,cut into 1 inch cubes
fontina cheese
goat cheese

Directions:
Heat a soup pot to medium heat, add onions and garlic. Saute until they become tender. Add all other soup ingredient and allow to simmer for about 15 minutes.
To serve: spoon soup into a bowl and top with bread and cheeses. Put under broiler and cook until cheese is melted, about 3 mins.
