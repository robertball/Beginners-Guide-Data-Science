Title:
Insanely Hot Pickled Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/555777.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs jalapenos - sliced
2 lbs habanero peppers - sliced
6 cups white vinegar
3 cups red wine vinegar
3 cups water
8 cloves garlic - minced
5 cloves garlic - halved
5tsp pickling spice
5tb bhut jolokia powder
1 medium onion - chopped
3 tb olive oil
5 qt canning jars

Directions:
In a large saucepan, heat oil, add onion and 8 cloves minced garlic saute' until soft add vinegars and water and bring to a boil.
While bringing the vinegar mix to a boil, pack peppers into jars and add 1 clove halved garlic, 1tsp pickling spice and 1 tb bhut jolokia powder to each jar.
Fill jars with vinegar/water mixture.
Seal with lids.
Store in cool dark area for 8 weeks and enjoy

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
These pickled peppers are insanely hot using bhut jolokia powder ( formerly the hottest pepper in the world) AKA the white ghost chili pepper. Hot but great!
