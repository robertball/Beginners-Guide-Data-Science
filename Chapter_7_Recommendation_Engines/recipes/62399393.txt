Title:
Slow Roasted Cherry Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cherry tomatoes
olive oil
kosher salt
unpeeled garlic cloves
ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 225 degrees.
Place a layer of foil on cookie sheet.
Slice grape or cherry tomatoes in half, drizzle with olive oil, salt & pepper.
Toss and place cut side up on sheet.
Throw in a few unpeeled garlic cloves if desired.
Bake for 2 1/2 to 3 hours depending on oven.  Mine took a full 3 hours.
