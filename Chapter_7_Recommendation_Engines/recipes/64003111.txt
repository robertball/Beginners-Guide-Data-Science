Title:
ATK Classic Cream of Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
48oz whole tomatoes packed in juice (drained, juice reserved)
1 1/2 tsp dark brown sugar
4 T butter
2 shallots, minced (apx 1/2 cup)
1 T tomato paste
pinch ground allspice
1 3/4 c chicken broth
1/2 c heavy cream
2 T brandy or dry sherry

Directions:
Note:  you need 3 cups tomato juice.  Use juice from cans as well as any juice from baking tomatoes.
Oven to 450.  Line baking sheet with foil.
Squeeze seeds from tomatoes over strainer (to save juice).  Put tomatoes on sheet and sprinkle with brown sugar.  Bake apx 30 minute.  Cool then remove to bowl and set aside.
Melt butter in large saucepan.  Add shallots, tomato paste, and allspice.  Turn heat to low, cover, and simmer stirring occasionally 7 to 10 minutes.
Add flour and continue to cook stirring constantly 30 seconds.
Whisk in broth, tomato juice, and tomatoes.  Cover, increase heat to medium, and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low and simmer (stirring occ) for 10 minutes.
Strain mix to remove solids and transfer them to blender.  Add 1 cup liquid and puree until smooth.  Return all to pan, add cream and warm over low heat until hot.
Off heat, stir in brandy or sherry and season with salt/pepper.
Can be refrigerated for up to 2 days.  When reheating, do not bring back up to boil.
