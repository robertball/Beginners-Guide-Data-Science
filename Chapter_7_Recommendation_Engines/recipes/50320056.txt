Title:
Brandy Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon grated lime zest
Juice of 1 lime
1/4 cup brandy
Plus your choice of flavors:
lemon zest
coconut extract
almond extract
rum

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine water, sugar and lemon zest. Bring mixture to a boil and continue boiling for 5 minutes. Strain into a sealable container, removing the lemon zest. Allow to cool, then cover and store in refrigerator.
When ready to use, mix in brandy. Brush onto cake layers before frosting. Or pour over warm cakes and let cool before frosting.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
Simple Syrup is brushed on cakes for added moisture and flavor, or poured hot over cooled cake. This one can be flavored with brandy or rum.Modified from: Brandy Simple Syrup
