Title:
Quinoa Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
#5  can    Tuna
2   Cups   Quinoa
2          Onions, sliced
1          Egg
2  Tbs     Flour
2  Cups    Mushrooms, sliced
6  Tbs     Butter
6  Cups    Stock (chicken or vegetable)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute mushrooms and onions until soft and set aside.
Make Quinoa Risotto: Melt 2 tbs of butter in a pot over medium heat. Allow quinoa to lightly brown then pour enough stock to just cover the quinoa. Allow the liquid to reduce to half. Continue this process until quinoa is soft. Salt through out process.
Add mushrooms, onions, and tuna to the quinoa.
Add fresh pepper, the rest of butter, flour and egg. Put in a casserole dish and top with bread crumbs.
Put in 325 degree oven for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
mariehipple
Description: 
My father is on a low carb diet, but I love tuna casserole so I decided to try to make it with quinoa. This is also kind of a recipe for quinoa risotto as that is how I cooked the quinoa.
