Title:
Pepperoni Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen bread dough balls, thawed
1 egg, beaten
8 ounces sliced pepperoni
1 cup shredded mozzarella or three cheese mix
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese plus some for topping
1 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Lightly grease a baking sheet.
Mix all cheese and spices together
Hand flatten a dough ball press 4 sliced peperoni inside top with cheese mixture close the dough makeing a round ball. Brush with egg and sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Place seam sides down, on prepared baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes, or until golden.
