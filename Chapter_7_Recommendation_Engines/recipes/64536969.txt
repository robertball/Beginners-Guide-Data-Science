Title:
Easy Raw Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6122998.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond butter (room temp)
1/3 cup coconut oil
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/4 cup pure maple syrup
1/2 tsp fine sea salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In medium bowl, mix nut butter and coconut oil until smooth.
Add in cocoa powder, maple syrup, sea salt and vanilla and stir until smooth and creamy.
May add pecans if desired.
Transfer mixture to dish lined with plastic wrap.
Freeze.
