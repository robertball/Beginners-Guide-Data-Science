Title:
Big Ray's Caribbean Spiced Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015686.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb Country Style Ribs
2 Tbl Dried Minced Onion
1 tsp Ground Mustard
1 tsp Crushed Red Pepper
1/2 tsp Ground Allspice
1/4 tsp Nutmeg
1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1 Large Onion, sliced
1/2 Cup Dark Rum
1/2 Cup Barbecue Sauce
1/4 tsp Liquid Smoke
2 Tbl Brown Sugar

Directions:
Mix first 7 dry ingredients and rub well into ribs.
Layer ribs and onions in slow cooker.  Pour rum over ribs.
Cover and cook on low for 8 to 9 hours.
Remove ribs from slow cooker and discard liquid.
Place ribs in shallow bowl and cover ribs with barbecue sauce. Return ribs to slow cooker and sprinkle brown sugar over ribs, cook 1 hour longer.
