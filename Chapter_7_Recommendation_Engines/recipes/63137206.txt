Title:
Pate Spice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon bay leaf, crushed
1 tablespoon thyme, dried
1 tablespoon mace, powdered
1 tablespoon rosemary, dried
1 tablespoon basil, dried
2 tablespoon cinnamon, powdered
1/2 teaspoon clove, ground
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg, ground
1/2 teaspoon allspice, ground
1 teaspoon white pepper, ground
2 teaspoons paprika

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well in a spice mortar or coffee grinder, or crush them together in a deep bowl with the bottom of a cup. Sift through a fine sieve then repeat with that which does not go through the sieve until everything goes through. Keep in a tightly covered jar.

Submitter: 
Michael St. Laurent

Also known as Parisian spice.
