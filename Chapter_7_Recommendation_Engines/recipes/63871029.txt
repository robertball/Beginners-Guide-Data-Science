Title:
Amanda's Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon canola oil
1/2 onion minced
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 teaspoon cumin
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 head romaine lettuce- rinsed, dried and chopped
2 large tomatoes, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
1-2 Cups salsa (as desired)
12 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (14.5 ounce) package corn tortilla chips

Directions:
Put the oil in your pan for browning the hamburger and saute the onions until clear. Then add the beef and brown with cumin and taco seasoning, drain and set aside to cool.
In a large bowl, combine romaine lettuce, tomatoes, green pepper, red or yellow pepper and black beans. Add desired amount of salsa and cooled beef. Toss well and place in the refrigerator.
When ready to serve top with desired cheese and chips. Enjoy! This recipe is easy to make your own I will use different cheese or beans or change up the seasonings.

Submitter: 
Amanda Nye
Description: 
This is a delicious mix of taco flavors that is easy to make your own. In my family we all take the salad and top it with our own mix of things. The recipe states to mix together but this is easily altered to suit everybody.Modified from: Joy's Taco Salad
