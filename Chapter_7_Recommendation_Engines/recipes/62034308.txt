Title:
Meatloaf Cordon Bleau Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. extra lean ground chuck
1 Cup italian bread crumbs
2 eggs beaten
1 small onion chopped
1/8 tsp. garlic
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
1/4 lb. boiled ham
1/4 lb. provolone cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients (except for ham and cheese) Blend well.
Pat out on wax paper to 1/2 inch thickness.  Lay slices
of ham and cheese on top of meat mixture.  Pick up edge of wax paper and roll like a
jelly roll.  Seal edges, place in a meat loaf pan.  Bake 1 hour 15 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a recipe that my grandmother handed down to me
and now it is a family favorite! My kids love it as
well as everyone that has tried it.
