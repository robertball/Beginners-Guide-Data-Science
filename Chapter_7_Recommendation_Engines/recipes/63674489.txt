Title:
David's Sister's Slaw Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1020514.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag of slaw mix (12 oz.)
1 small onion, finely  diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
For the dressing:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup oil
2/3 cup white vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Heat the water and sugar, stirring until sugar dissolves.
Add vinegar and oil, salt and pepper and let cool
Stir well and pour slaw mix, onion and bell pepper.
Let stand for six hours before serving.
Just before serving, drain off 1/2 of the dressing.
Will keep for 1-2 weeks in fridge
