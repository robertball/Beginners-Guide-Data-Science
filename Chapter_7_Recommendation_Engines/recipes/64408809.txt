Title:
Cheesy Hamburger Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
6 medium potatoes (peeled and sliced thin)
1 can tomato soup
10 oz milk (fill soup can)
1 lb package shredded cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
add soup and milk to a 13 x 9 in. backing pan, stir.  Add potatoes.
place in oven for 30 min.
Brown hamburger
Remove pan from oven add hamburger and cheese, mix
Place pan back in oven for 15 min. or until potatoes are done.
Remove pan and stir

Submitter: 
Jessica Skiles
Description: 
My favorite dish.  Creamy, cheesy, potato dish for the whole family.
