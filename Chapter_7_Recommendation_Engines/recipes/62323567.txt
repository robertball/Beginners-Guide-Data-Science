Title:
Hannah Banana Cream Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant banana pudding mix
2 very ripe banana, mashed
3/4 cup milk
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a medium bowl or food processor, mix pudding, bananas and milk until very thick. Gently fold in whipped topping. Enough filling for a 12 inch cake.
