Title:
Scrambled Eggs, Cheese, Avocado, Tortilla Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2554453.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
1/2 tsp Milk (any kind)
1/2 tsp butter/margin (optional)
1/4 to 1/2 cup of Mexican Cheese Blend
1/2 avocado
1 soft flour tortilla
1 small tortilla mold / muffin tin
Pinch of Sea-Salt
(Optional) Paprika
(Optional) Choice of Salsa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F (or follow instructions for your tortilla mold)
Crack eggs into microwavable bowl and add milk and butter/margin and scramble well.
Add most of the cheese to the mixture in the bowl and mix  again. (Save a little cheese for topping if you wish)
Add soft tortilla to mold and place in oven for 5 minutes (or per mold instructions)
Microwave egg mixture for 30 seconds to 1 minute (in most microwaves)
Remove and remix egg with a fork and return to microwave for another 30 seconds (and repeat until cooked if needed)
Let eggs sit in microwave while cutting avocado for topping.
Remove tortilla bowl from oven when crispy, remove from mold and cool slightly for a minute.
Add eggs in tortilla bowl, top with (optional) salsa and avocado, sprinkle with (optional) paprika, sea salt, and cheese.

Submitter: 
Shelley West
Description: 
Scrambled eggs and cheese topped with sea-salt dusted avocados all in a tortilla shell bowl. Paprika and salsa optional.  Yummy protein packed breakfast! Easy to change to ingredients for your health and taste preferences...
