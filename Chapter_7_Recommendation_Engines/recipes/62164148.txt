Title:
Lime Fluff II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sugar free whipped topping
1 (16 .oz) small curd low fat cottage cheese
1 medium can crushed pineapple, drained
1 pkg. sugar free lime Jell-O

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese and and dry Jell-O mix. Drain and add pineapple. Stir. Add whipped topping and store in the fridge.
