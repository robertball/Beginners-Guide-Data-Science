Title:
Raspberry White Chocolate Doodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/733970.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter softened, 1/2 cup shortening, 1 1/2 cups white sugar, 2 eggs, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour, 1 tsp baking power, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 3/4 cup raspberry tidbits, 3/4 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F, Cream together butter, shortening, 1 1/2 cups sugar, add eggs and vanilla.  Mix in the flour, soda and salt.  Dough will be very firm.  Add raspberry tidbits and white chocote chips.  Using regular size ice cream scoop shape dough and place on cookie sheet.  Bake 12 - 14 minutes, or until bottoms are slightly browned. Remove immediately from baking sheets.
