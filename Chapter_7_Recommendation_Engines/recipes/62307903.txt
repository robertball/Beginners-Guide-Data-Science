Title:
Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound ground beef
1/2 pound sweet Italian Sausage
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 small red bell pepper, diced
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 teaspoons dried basil
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1-2 teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Combine ground beef,Italian sausage, onion, garlic, and red pepper in a large saucepan.  Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Drain grease.
Stir crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, sugar,salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Rich and meaty spaghetti sauce is surprisingly easy to make, and ready in just over an hour.  Serve over any variety of hot cooked pasta.Modified from: Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef
