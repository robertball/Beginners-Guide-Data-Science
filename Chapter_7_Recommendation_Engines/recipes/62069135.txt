Title:
Best London Broil Ever! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lb. top round london broil
1  28 oz. can of stewed tomatoes
garlic salt to taste

Directions:
Place london broil in a roasting pan.  Pour on stewed tomatoes and sprinkle with as much garlic salt as desired.  Bake at 350 for about 2 1/2 to 3 hours.  Serve with mashed potatoes and your vegetable of choice!  Yummy!

Submitter: 
LSERAMONE
Description: 
Very easy, very tasty, very tender london broil.  A great meal with hardly any effort!
