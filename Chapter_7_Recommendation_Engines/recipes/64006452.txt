Title:
Pumpkin Maple Baked Beans Recipe

•1 large garlic clove, minced

•1 tbsp ketchup
•2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
•1/2 cup canned pumpkin
•1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
•1/2 cup dried cranberries
•1 tsp kosher salt, or to taste
