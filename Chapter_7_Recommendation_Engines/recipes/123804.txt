Title:
Oatmeal Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/577881.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/4 cup molasses
1 egg
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup quick-cooking oats
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon salt
Additional sugar

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening, molasses and egg. Combine dry ingredients; stir into creamed mixture. Roll into 1-in. balls; roll in sugar. Place on greased baking sheets. Flatten slightly with a flat-bottomed glass. Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes (do not overbake).

Submitter: 
Sherry  Harke
Description: 
I always get compliments on these delicious chewy cookies. The spicy aroma fills my kitchen when they're baking and never fails to set a warm holiday mood.
