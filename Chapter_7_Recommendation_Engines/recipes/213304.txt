Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Tomato-Basil Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993373.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Tomato Soup
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves, crushed
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
4 cups hot cooked medium tube-shaped pasta (ziti)

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned.
Add soup, milk, cheese, basil and garlic. Heat to  a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 5 min. or until done. Serve with pasta.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
This speedy sauteed chicken dish features a delicious tomato sauce flavored with basil and Parmesan cheese.
