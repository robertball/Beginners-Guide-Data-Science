Title:
Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2171483.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, room temperature
4 cups powdered sugar, or to desired consistency
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
4 ounces semisweet chocolate squares (such as Bakers), melted
Pinch of salt
1/4 cup heavy cream

Directions:
In an electric mixer bowl combine butter and powdered sugar until well mixed.
Add vanilla extract, chocolate, and salt and mix until thoroughly combined.
Add cream and mix until light and fluffy, up to five minutes.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
Because I prefer a chocolate frosting made with chocolate rather than cocoa, I fashioned this one for myself.
