Title:
Homemade Sweet Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5057953.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cornmeal
1 1/2 cups flour
2/3 cup sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup oil
3 tablespoons melted butter
1 tablespoon honey
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/4 cups milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ.
In a bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
Add the oil, melted butter, honey, eggs, and milk.
Pour into a greased 8-inch square pan.
Bake for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chris Mosley
Description: 
A great quick recipe when you've got a little extra time to bring an extra layer of homemade goodness to a meal!
