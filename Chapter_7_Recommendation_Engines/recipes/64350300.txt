Title:
Avocado Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 avocado
2 tsp lemon juice
1/2 cup greek yogurt
1 tsp hot sauce
1/4 cup EEOV
2 garlic cloves, minced
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In blender or food processor, mix all ingredients.
Serve over salad.
