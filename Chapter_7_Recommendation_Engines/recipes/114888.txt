Title:
Southwestern Pepper Ranch Turkey Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987971.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) package Borden® Shred Medleys™ Southwestern Style Shredded Cheese, seasoned according to package directions
1 cup prepared peppercorn Ranch dressing
8 burrito style flour tortillas
16 slices deli-style sliced turkey breast
4 cups shredded iceberg lettuce
1 cup sliced black olives, drained
1 cup tomatoes, chopped

Directions:
COMBINE seasoned cheese and dressing in bowl. Stir to blend; reserve.
SHINGLE turkey slices evenly down center of each tortilla; top with seasoned cheese mixture, shredded lettuce, olives and tomatoes.
FOLD both sides of each tortilla over filling. Then fold lower end of each wrap over filling and roll closed.
DIAGONAL slice each wrap.

Submitter: 
Borden® Shred Medleys™
Description: 
These tasty wraps filled with sliced turkey, Southwestern-style cheese, and peppercorn Ranch dressing make a great meal on-the-go.
