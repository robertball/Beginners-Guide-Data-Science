Title:
Cran-Apple Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 + cup peeled, chopped apples
2 cups fresh cranberries
2 TBS flour
1 cup sugar
3 pkgs instant oatmeal cinnamon/spice
3/4 chopped pecans
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Combine 1st 3 ingredients tossing to coat fruit.
Add sugar, mix well.
Place in 2 qt. casserole dish.
Combine oatmeal, pecans, 1/2 cup flour, & brown sugar. Add butter and stir. Spoon over fruit mixture.
Bake uncovered at 350.
