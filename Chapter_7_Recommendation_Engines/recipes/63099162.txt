Title:
Tunisian Spiced Turkey Breast (slow cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/785589.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs Flour
1 Tsp Chipotle Chili Powder
1/2 Tsp Ground Coriander
1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
1/2 Tsp Salt
1/4 Tsp Ground Black Pepper
1/2 Turkey Breast (4lbs), skin removed
1 Tbs Olive Oil
2 Medium Red Onions, peeled and cut in quarters
3 Large Carrots, peeled and cut into thirds
6 Garlic Cloves, unpeeled
1 Acorn Squash, seeded and cut into quarters (OR)
4-6 Potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters

Directions:
Combine flour, chili powder, coriander, garlic powder, cinnamon, salt and pepper in a plastic bag.  Add turkey and shake to coat well.  Heat oil in large fry pan and sear the turkey breast on both sides until nicely browned.  Put onions, carrots squash (or potatoes) and garlic cloves on bottom of ceramic slow cooker.  Place the turkey breast on top.
Cover and cook on low for 7 to 8 hours (3 1/2 - 4 hours on high).
Remove turkey and allow to cool before slicing.  Spoon the pan juices over turkey and surround with vegetables.  Peel the garlic cloves and remove skin from squash before serving.
