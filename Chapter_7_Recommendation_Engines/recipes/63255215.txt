Title:
Summer Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bottles Moscato
1 pink lemonade concentrate
3 C of Sprite (more or less, depending on your taste)
Fresh Strawberries

Directions:
Empty all of the liquid ingredients into a pitcher and stir.  Then mash about half the strawberries and put them in.  Place the rest of the berries in the glasses and served.
