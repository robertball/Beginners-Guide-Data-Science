Title:
Turkey Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-10 oz cans of chicken broth
2 cups of water
1 box of Uncle Bens Wild Rice
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
2 cups of 1/2 & 1/2
1 1/2 cu turkey or chicken
8 slices of cooked bacon

Directions:
Add chicken broth and water to large stock pot.
Add wild rice and seasoning packet to broth and cook 45 minutes.
In seperate saucepan,melt the butter and add the flour. Then slowly add the 1/2 & 1/2 till smooth. Add this mixture to broth slowly, stirring until soup thickens. Add the turkey cook for 15-30 minutes and add crumbled bacon.
