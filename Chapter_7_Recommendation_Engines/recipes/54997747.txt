Title:
Zucchini Green Chile Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 thick pie crust
3 C. grated zucchini (3/4#)
4 oz. gr. chiles, large dice
3/4 c. gr. onions
1 1/2 T. butter
3 eggs
1 to 1/2 c. half&half/evap.milk
1 c. cheddar, 1/2 c. jack cheese
1 T. flour
salt & pepper

Directions:
Grate zucchini on foil and sprinkle with salt.  Let stand 30 minutes then squeeze dry.
Cook onions in butter about 1 min. then stir in zucchini and heat just a few minutes till it's glazed in fat.
Blend in flour then spread in pie shell. Sprinkle with chiles and half the cheese.Pour egg/milk mix over and add remaining cheeses.
400 for 15, then 350 for 25.
