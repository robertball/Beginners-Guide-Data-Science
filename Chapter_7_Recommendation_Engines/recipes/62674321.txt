Title:
Foolproof White Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp butter
1 cup long grain white rice
2 cups water
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add rice, and stir to coat. Cook 1-2 minutes, until rice grains begin to turn opaque white (do not brown).
Add water and salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for exactly 15 minutes, but do NOT lift the lid.
Keeping pan covered, remove from heat, and let stand for 5 minutes.
Fluff with a fork before serving.

Description:
