Title:
Michelle's Pumpkin Ricotta Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
4 Tsp. baking powder
2 Tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup pure pumpkin puree
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
2 large eggs
1 1/3 cups milk
Maple syrup
Whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Combine the flour, baking powder, pumpkin pie spice, and sugar in a medium bowl.
In a large bowl, mix the pumpkin puree, ricotta, eggs and milk on medium with an electric mixer until smooth.
Add the flour mixture to the pumpkin mixture, and stir until combined.
Lightly coat a large skillet or griddle with cooking spray, heat over medium heat.  Once the pan/griddle is hot, pour batter into medium-sized pancakes.  Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
Serve with maple syrup and/or whipped cream.
