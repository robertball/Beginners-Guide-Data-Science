Title:
Curried Chicken Pitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4c cilantro, chopped
1/4c sour cream
2T mayonnaise
1T fresh lime juice
1t grated fresh ginger
1/4t curry powder
1/4t ground coriander
2c chopped cooked chicken breast meat
5 radishes, sliced
1-1/2c chopped cantaloupe
1/4 small red onion
3T roasted cashews, chopped
4 toasted pitas

Directions:
Whisk cilantro, sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, ginger, curry powder, coriander, and 1/8t salt until blended.
Combine chicken, radishes, cantaloupe, and onion.
Toss chicken mixture with half of dressing.
Sprinkle with cashews.
Serve with pitas and remaining dressing.
