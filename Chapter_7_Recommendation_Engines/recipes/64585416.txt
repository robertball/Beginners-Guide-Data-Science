Title:
Orange Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
22 oz filtered or sparkling water
4 orange slices
10 grapes, halved
4 fresh basil sprigs

Directions:
Place ingredients in 32-oz mason jar or pitcher.
Stir gently, cover, and refrigerate at least 1 hour (longer for more intense flavor).
Store in refrigerator up to 3 days.
