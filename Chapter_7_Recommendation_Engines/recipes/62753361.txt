Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium bananas, mashed (1 cup)
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
2 cups flour
1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
In following order, combine: bananas, sugar, eggs, sour cream, baking soda, salt. Mix thoroughly.
Add flour, nuts.
Pour into greased loaf pan. Let stand ½ hour.
Bake for 1 hour (may be done in 45 minutes).
