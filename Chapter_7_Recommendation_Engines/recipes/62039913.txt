Title:
Kiddie Casserole    Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 can green beans -- drained
1 can tomato sauce
2 cups mashed potatoes
1/4 cup shredded lowfat cheddar cheese

Directions:
-Brown ground beef in skillet, drain, and put in casserole dish.
-Top beef with green beans, then tomato sauce, then mashed potatoes.
-Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
-Cover and bake in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.

Robbie Rice
