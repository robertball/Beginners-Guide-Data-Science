Title:
Healthy Peanut Butter Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. old-fashioned oats
1 c. roasted, non-salted peanuts
1 c. sliced almonds
1/2 c. wheat germ
1/2 c. milled flax seed
2 t. ground cinnamon
1/4 c. canola oil
1/2 c. honey
heaping 1/2 c. creamy peanut butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine oats, peanuts, almonds, wheat germ, flax, and cinnamon.
In a small saucepan over low heat, combine oil, honey, and peanut butter. Stir until mixture is completely combined, but do not allow to simmer.
Pour into dry ingredient bowl and mix until well incorporated.
Spread the granola in the pan and bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
Allow to cool COMPLETELY then break into chunks.
Transfer to air-tight container(s). Stores longer in fridge. Freezes well, too.

Submitter: 
jandjwhite
Description: 
I make a double batch and freeze some! Perfect by itself or mixed with milk, yogurt, or ice cream!
