Title:
Baby Bells with Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4537909.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12.5 oz. can chunk chicken breast, drained and flaked
2 oz. jalapeno, diced.
3 green onion, thinly sliced; divided use
2 tablespoons light mayonnaise; more or less to taste
3 baby bells
1 cup cottage cheese, low fat
1 cup fresh cherries

Directions:
Thoroughly mix the chicken, jalapeno and two sliced green onions with the mayonnaise.
Slice each baby bell in half lengthwise, remove any seeds and stuff with chicken mixture.
Mix half of the remainder sliced green onion with the cottage cheese and garnish the baby bells and cottage cheese with the other half.
Refrigerate any remaining chicken mixture for later use.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
This bento box style lunch is not only tasty but healthy too. Baby bells stuffed with a chicken salad and it comes complete with fresh cherries and cottage cheese.
