Title:
Roasted Garlic and Red Pepper Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Red Peppers
4 Garlic Cloves
2 Cans (15.5 oz) Chic Peas, drained
1/4 C. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/3 C. Tahini
Juice of one Lemon
Salt to taste

Directions:
Roast peppers under broiler until black. Immediately seal in a plastic bag for ten minutes. Clean peppers and remove seeds. Cut into chunks.
Place unpeeled garlic cloves in a foil pouch. Drizzle with a touch of olive oil and seal edges of pouch. Place in a dry saute pan, over medium heat, for 15 minutes, turning pouch over every 3-4 minutes. Set aside for 5 minutes. Peel garlic.
Place all ingredients in a food processer. Process until very smooth. If Hummus is a little thick, add a little more olive oil.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description: 
Roasting the garlic and red peppers gives this hummus a unique and mellow flavor. Serve with whole pita rounds, just tear and dip. This recipe makes enough for a party.
