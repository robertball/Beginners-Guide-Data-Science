Title:
Buffalo Hot Wing Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One rotisserie chicken, skinned and cubed, 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese,
1/2 cup ranch dressing,
one cup of Franks "Original" Red Hot Sauce,
8 oz. cream cheese, cubed.
"Scoop Fritoes".

Directions:
Put all ingredients in casserole dish, microwave until hot. Serve with "Scoop" fritos.

Submitter: 
Jen
Description: 
If you like buffalo wings then you'll love this dip. This easy-to-make dip offers less mess than hot wings but doesn't skip on the taste!
