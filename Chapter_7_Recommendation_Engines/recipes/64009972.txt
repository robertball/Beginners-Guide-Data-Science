Title:
grenadine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1113302.jpg
Ingredients:
4 C pomegranate juice (divided)
1 C sugar

Directions:
Combine sugar and 3 C juice in a sauce pan. Heat to 244F/118C (firm ball stage on a candy thermometer). Cover and let cool until pan is just warm to the touch. Mix in remaining 1 C juice. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
T. Edward Foster
Description: 
This rich, tawny syrup will elevate your cocktails from pretty in pink to pretty spectacular.
