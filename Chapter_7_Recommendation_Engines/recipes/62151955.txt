Title:
Scrumptious Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded thin between 2 sheets of wax paper or parchment
1 cup flour
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 eggs
2 tablespoons water
1 1/2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup freshly grated (ground) parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Mix flour, salt and pepper in a plate or pie tin.
Beat eggs and water in a second plate or pie tin.
Mix bread crumbs and parmesan in a third plate or pie tin.
Dredge both sides of chicken in the flour mixture and shake off excess.
Next, dip each chicken breast in the egg wash.  Turn and dip the other side.
Then dredge chicken in the bread crumb mixture...both sides.
Fry chicken (2 breasts at a time)for 3 minutes on each side over medium heat or until well browned.
