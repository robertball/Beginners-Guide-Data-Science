Title:
Basic Egg Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 tablespoon margarine
3 cups bread flour
2 tablespoons sugar
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon active dry yeast (Red Star)

Directions:
Layer all ingredients with flour then yeast last.
Cook on basic in bread machine.
