Title:
Kamikaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988816.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 fluid ounces Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
1/4 fluid ounce triple sec
1/4 fluid ounce lime juice

Directions:
Add lime juice, triple sec and Smirnoff No.21 Vodka.
Shake and strain into a shot glass.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
The tangy, refreshing tastes of lime juice and orange liqueur team up with vodka in this classic cocktail.
