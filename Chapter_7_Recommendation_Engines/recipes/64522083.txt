Title:
Sunday Slow Cooker Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs chuck stew meat
¼ c flour
2 Tbsp season salt
Vegetable oil
3 large stalks celery (large diagonal dice)
3 large carrots (large diagonal dice)
4-5 gold potatoes (large cube)
2 large onion (large cross slice)
1/2 Cup red wine
2 Tbsp tomato paste
2 bay leaf
1 packet onion soup mix
3 cups boiling water
1 Tbsp beef bullion (or two cubes)
¼ cup Worcestershire
1 cup frozen peas
3 Tbsp corn starch
3 Tbsp cold water
salt/pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, coat beef with flour/season salt.
Working in batches, brown in oil in medium hot pan (cast iron or stainless steel) and add to slow cooker.
While meat is browning cut vegs and add to slow cooker.
Cube potatoes and set aside (in cold water).
When beef is finished reduce heat to medium, add onions to the pan with some salt, cook 5- 10 minutes until they pick up some color.
Deglaze with wine.
Add tomato paste and reduce to concentrate flavors.
Add to slow cooker with bay leaf.
Boil three cups of water.
Add onion soup mix, stir and add hot liquid to slow cooker.
Add bullion and Worcestershire.
Cook on low 5 hrs.
Add cornstarch slurry to thicken sauce.
Boil potatoes until tender, add to slow cooker with peas.
Season to taste with SP.
Cook until heated through.
