Title:
Curry Coconut Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1103253.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken cut into 1/2 chunks
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons oil
2 tablespoons curry powder
1/2 onion thinly sliced
2 garlic cloves
1 14 oz. can coconut milk
1 14 oz. can stewed diced tomatos

Directions:
Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat for 2 minutes. Stir in onions and garlic. Cook for 1 minute then add chicken and curry. Reduce heat to medium and cook 7-10 minutes or until chicken is done and juices run clear. Pour in coconut milk and tomato sauce. Stir to combine. Cover and simmer over medium-low or low heat stirring occationally for 30-40 minutes.
Serve over rice.
