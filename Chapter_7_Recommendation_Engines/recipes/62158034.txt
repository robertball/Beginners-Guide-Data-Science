Title:
Lomi Lomi Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Five firm tomatoes
One round onion
1/4 C chopped green onions
1/8 C sea salt
1/2 lbs salmon
2 C Crushed ice

Directions:
1/4 inch Cube the tomatoes
1/4 inch Cube the salmon
Chop the round onion
Lomi, which means massage all ingredients together
Taste to see if there is enough salt
Add a layer of crushed ice over the top
Sprinkle more sea salt over the crushed ice
Chill over night

Submitter: 
Cooking from the Hip
Description: 
This is a side dish at almost ALL celebrations and luaus.
