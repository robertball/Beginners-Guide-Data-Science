Title:
Hamburger Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground turkey
2 onions, chopped
1/2 Tablespoon minced garlic
5 cups chicken broth
3 large red potatoes, chopped
6 stalks of celery, sliced
8 carrots, sliced
1 cup frozen green beans
1 cup frozen peas
15 oz. can corn, drained
2 bay leaves
1/2 Tablespoon dried basil
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2 Tablespoons tomato paste
1 Tablespoon soy sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook ground turkey with onions in a large stockpot over medium-high heat until turkey is no longer pink and onions are translucent.  Drain off the fat. Add garlic, cook a few more minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.  Add veggies and spices.  Return to a boil, then cover and simmer on low heat for about 30 minutes until veggies are soft.
