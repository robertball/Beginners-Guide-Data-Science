Title:
Sweet 'n' Sour Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1121993.jpg
Ingredients:
7 tomatoes
1 small yellow onion
1/2 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Thinly slice the tomatoes.  Cut the onion in half through root end,  then thinly slice into half-circles.  In a large bowl, toss together tomatoes, onion, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper.  You may adjust the amounts of vinegar and sweetener according to your taste. Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Bold sweet and sour flavors compliment fresh tomatoes and slivered onions in this easy-to-prepare salad.
