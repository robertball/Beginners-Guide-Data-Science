Title:
Sour Cream and Chive Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb potatoes, peeled and cubes
1 tbl freeze dried chives
1 sticks butter, melted
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup milk or cream
salt to taste

Directions:
1. Soak cubed potatoes in water overnight.
2. Drain water from potatoes.  Add cubes to new pot of boiling water.  Boil until tender (about 20 minutes).
3. Drain potatoes into large bowl.  Lightly mash and gently fold in melted butter, sour cream, milk, and chives.
4. Using a hand mixer blend potatoes.  Test just before completely creamy, and add salt as desired.  Do not over-whip the potatoes.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
These potatoes are so creamy and good. This makes enough for a side dish for 6 people, but can be easily double or tripled for a large party.
