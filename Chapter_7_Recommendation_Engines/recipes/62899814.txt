Title:
Honey Almond Oatmeal Bath Bar Soap: Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup distilled water
4 white glycerin bar soap
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup Sweet almond oil
4 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup powdered milk
1 tablespoon Grapefruit Seed Extract

Directions:
Shred the white glycerin bar soap and set aside. Heat the water in a heavy saucepan over low heat. Stir in the shredded soap until it forms a sticky mass.
Add honey and stir in well. Remove the pan from the heat and add the powdered milk, Sweet almond oil, Grapefruit Seed Extract, oatmeal; stir until well-blended.
Spoon the soap into its mold and let set for four hours or until hardened.

Description: 
My Aunt always made her own bath soap because she had allergy to some products that was in commercial ready made soap. Her Dermatologist help her come up with this recipe over 40 years ago.
