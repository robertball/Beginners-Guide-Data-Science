Title:
Baked Radicchio and Herbed Goat Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium heads radicchio cut into 1/4" strips
4 oz of goat cheese cut into 1/2" pieces
1/4 c olive oil
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 tsp dried basil and marjoram
1/2 tsp dried rosemary
Slice italian bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400*
Place radicchio in shallow baking dish
Arrange goat cheese over top.
Heat oil in heavy saucepan over medium heat.
Add garlic and saute until golden (about one minute). Then discard garlic.
Add herbs to oil and drizzle over cheese.
Season with pepper.
Bake until cheese begins to melt (15 min)
