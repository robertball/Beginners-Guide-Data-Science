Title:
Semigourmet's Tuscan Chicken and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients 8 ounces Barilla Rotini ¼ cup chopped dry packed sun dried tomatoes 2 medium zucchini, cut into matchstick strips 1 tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil 26 ounces Barilla Tomato and Basil Pasta Sauce 1 cup cooked chicken strips ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Directions 1. Begin cooking rotini according to package directions. Add sun dried tomatoes to pasta during last 5 minutes of cooking; Drain rotini and tomatoes. 2. Meanwhile, combine zucchini and oil in large microwave safe bowl; cover with plastic wrap. Microwave on high 4 minutes stirring twice. 3. Stir in pasta sauce and chicken. Cover with plastic wrap; microwave on high 4 minutes, stirring twice. Combine sauce mixture, hot drained rotini with sun dried tomatoes and cheese; toss to coat. Notes I didn’t use the microwave on this dish. I cooked it in a sauté pan, or wok. It is very tasty and pretty easy.
