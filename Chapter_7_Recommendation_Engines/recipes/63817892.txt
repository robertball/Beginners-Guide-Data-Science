Title:
Lynch Mob Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1058949.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb stew beef cubed
4 medium potatoes
4 medium carrots
4 stalks of celery
1 can of peas
1 medium onion (whole or sliced)
1 can drained corn nibs
1 can drained green beans
1 package (or 1 can) sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup olive oil
4 Tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 whole bay leaf (or crushed leaves)*remove if whole

Directions:
in a large spaghetti/stock pot, place 1/4C oil, heat on high. In a plastic bag put flour, shake in beef cubes until coated. Place coated beef cubes in pot and sear all sides of the meat. Add 6 Cups hot water and onion to cover meat. Bring temp up to boil for 5 minutes, then turn to low. Add vegetables and spices. Simmer for 3 hours.
