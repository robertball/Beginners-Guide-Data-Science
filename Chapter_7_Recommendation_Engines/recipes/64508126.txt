Title:
Spiced Pecan Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tbs sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 3/4 cups milk
3 eggs, separated
6 tbs unsalted butter, melted and cooled
pinch of cream of tartar
1/2 cup finely chopped pecans, lightly toasted

Directions:
Preheat waffle maker.
In a bowl, sift together dry ingredients.
In a small bowl, whisk milk, egg yolks and melted butter.
In another bowl, beat egg whites and cream of tartar to stiff peaks.
Stir milk mixture into dry ingredients until smooth.  Stir in pecans.   Gently fold in egg whites until just blended.
Cook in waffle iron.
