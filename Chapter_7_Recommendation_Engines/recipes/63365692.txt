Title:
Sessions Procupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb beef
1/3 C uncooked rice
1/4 C chopped onion
1/4 C water
1 tsp salt
pepper
1 can condensed tomato soup
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 C water

Directions:
Combine ingredients through pepper into balls. Place in a saucepan. Combine soup, chili powder, and water. Cover meatballs with the sauce and simmer for 1 hour.
