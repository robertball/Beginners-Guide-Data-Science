Title:
Vanilla Sesame Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289405.jpg
Ingredients:
250g butter
250g sugar
6 large eggs
250g self-raising flour
1 tablespoon of vanilla essence
100g black sesame seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
Blend the butter and sugar together until creamy.
Add in the eggs one at a time and continue blending until batter is smooth.
Stir in the vanilla essence.
Fold in the self-raising flour and sesame seeds
Pour into a baking pan and bake for 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Submitter:

Description: 
Pair it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a perfect afternoon snack.
