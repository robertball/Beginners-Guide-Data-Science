Title:
Taco Cornbread Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980825.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8.5 ounce) package corn muffin mix
1 pound ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Taco Seasoning Mix
1 (8 ounce) package KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese, divided

Directions:
Prepare corn muffin mix as directed on package. Spread batter into greased 12-inch pizza pan. Bake at 400 degrees F for 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
Brown meat; drain. Add Seasoning Mix and 3/4 cup water; prepare as directed on package. Sprinkle 1 cup cheese over baked crust. Top with meat mixture and remaining cheese.
Bake 4 to 5 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serve with your favorite taco toppings such as shredded lettuce, TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Thick 'N Chunky Salsa and BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream.
