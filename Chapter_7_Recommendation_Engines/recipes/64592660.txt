Title:
Easy Rhubarb Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 18-1/2 oz pkg white cake mix
Ingredients for making cake mix, as listed on pkg.
5 cups diced fresh or frozen rhubarb
1 cup sugar
1 cup heavy (whipping) cream

Directions:
Mix cake as instructed on package.
Pour batter into a greased 9" x 13" pan.
Sprinkle rhubarb over batter.
Sprinkle with sugar; pour cream over top. Do not mix.
Bake @ 350 x 35-40 min. or until cake tests done.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Using a cake mix speeds this dessert up. The recipe calls for fresh or frozen rhubarb, but I like to use fresh if available. This is a fairly light dessert, cake mix makes it fluffy and the cream makes it rich.
