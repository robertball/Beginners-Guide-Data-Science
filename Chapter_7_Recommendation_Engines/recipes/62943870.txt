Title:
Creamed Spinach II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 Onion, finely chopped
2 Cloves Garlic, minced
1 cube Chicken Bouillon
1 tablespoon butter
2 tbsp Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup Milk
garlic salt to taste

Directions:
Prepare spinach according to package directions; drain well.
Saute onion & garlic in butter. Add mushroom soup, bouillon, spinach, cheese, & milk
Bring to a low boil.  Season with garlic salt to taste.
Can be topped with breadcrumbs and baked for 20 minutes.

Spinach is simmered with cream of mushroom soup, butter and garlic salt.  It doesn't get much easier than this!Modified from: Creamed Spinach II
