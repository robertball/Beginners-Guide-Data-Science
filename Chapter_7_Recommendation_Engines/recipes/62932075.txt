Title:
Healthy Coconut Crusted Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/721251.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (4 oz.) tilapia filets
4 tbsp. cornstarch
1 egg
1/2 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
2 tbsp. coconut sugar or cane sugar
1 tsp. creole or cajun seasoning
1/4 cup flour
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400ยบ degrees.
Measure cornstarch into a small bowl. Whisk the egg in a separate small bowl. Combine coconut, sugar, creole seasoning and flour in another bowl. Set aside.
Coat a glass baking dish with cooking spray or a small amount of oil.
Evenly coat tilapia filets in the cornstarch. Dip cornstarch coated filets into the whisked egg and finish by coating them in the coconut mixture. Place filets into prepared baking dish and spray the top with cooking spray. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes. Enjoy!
