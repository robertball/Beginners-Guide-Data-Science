Title:
Fake-Out Takeout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 tbsp olive oil
1 pkg. frozen stir-fry vegetable blend thawed
1/4 cup apple juice
1/4 cup of soy sauce
1/4 cup of peanut butter
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/4 cup cayenne pepper
Hot cooked Rice

Directions:
In a large skillet or wok, stir-fry chicken in oil for 3-4 minutes or until no longer pink.
Sitr in vegetable blend
Cook,uncovered, for 4-6 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Meanwhile in a small bowl, combine the apple juice, soy sauce, peanut butter, brown sugar, garlic powder, ginger and cayenne. Stir in chicken mixture
Heat through.
Serve with rice.
