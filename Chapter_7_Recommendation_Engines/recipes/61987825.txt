Title:
Crockpot Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. stewing meat, cubed
2 large onions, chopped
1/2 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
1 Tbsp. chili powder
2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 cup vinegar(pref. apple cider)
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1(6-oz.)can tomato paste
16 hamburger buns

Directions:
In a crockpot, combine all ingredients. Cook on Low for 8-10 hours, or until meat is tender. Stir with a fork to break up pieces.
Serve on hamburger buns.
