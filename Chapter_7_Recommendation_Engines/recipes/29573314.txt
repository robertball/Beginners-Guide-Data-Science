Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pie shell
1 stick butter (melted)
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup corn syrup
2 eggs
pinch salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1-2 cups pecans
1 heaping teaspoon flour.

Directions:
Mix all ingrediants and pour into pie shell.
Bake 325 1 hour
