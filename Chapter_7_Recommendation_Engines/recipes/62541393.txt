Title:
Eggcellent Eggnog Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup Flour
2 tsp. Baking Power
1 tsp. Nutmeg
1/4 cup sugar
1 Egg beaten
1/2 cup Milk
1/2 cup VERY thick Eggnog
1 Tbs. Oil

Directions:
Sift and stir dry ingredients together in a bowl.
Whisk Milk, Eggnog, Oil, and Egg, in a separate bowl.
Pour Milk mixture into flour mixture and stir to moisten. Batter will be slightly lumpy.
Heat griddle on medium hi (about 350). Ladle or scoop batter onto a hot griddle. Wait until edges are dry and bubbles form on top but do not fill when they pop. Turn. Cook another minute then remove from griddle and serve warm with butter and syrup.
