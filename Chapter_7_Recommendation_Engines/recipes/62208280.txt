Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cans of pork and beans
2 T. mustard
1 medium finely chopped
1 T. apple cider vinegar
1/2 C. brown sugar
1/2 C. catsup
1/2 C. BBQ sauce mesquite works best
package of bacon

Directions:
mix ingredients together.
Bake in glass cake pan if you have one if not a metal pan will do.
top with strips of bacon
*350 degrees for 30 to 35 min.*

Submitter:

Description: 
My Aunts recipe. We always had it during Thanksgiving. Now that I have the recipe I make it all time!
