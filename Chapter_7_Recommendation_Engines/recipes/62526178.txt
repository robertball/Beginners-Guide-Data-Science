Title:
Sweet Potato Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
#1
4 sweet potatoes (3 cups mashed)
1 cup flaked coconut (optional)
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup white sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 stick butter
#2 Topping:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup plain flour
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Combine ingredients from #1, mix well.  Do not clean beaters (strings from potatoes will be on beaters).  Pour in casserole dish.  Cut ingredients from #2, then sprinkly on top of casserole.  Bake in 350 oven about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Exhibits
Description: 
This is an all time favorite with my family.  It was handed down to me by Annie Laurie Teal about 30 years ago and I still make it at Thanksgiving.  Fresh sweet potatoes are best, but if you can find canned packed potatoes, they work fine.
