Title:
Slow cooker Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large Roast
2 packets Italian dressing
2 packets Ranch dressing
2 packets Brown Gravy
Large onion
1 C. Water
4. oz Mushrooms

Directions:
cut onion into rings, slice mushrooms.  Put rings of onions in bottom of slow cooker. Put roast in slow cooker, add seasoning.  add sliced mushrooms,  and Water.  Cook for 8 hours on low

Submitter: 
Christal Hudson Ellard

Roast with Italian & Ranch dressing, and brown grvy.
