Title:
Banana Berry Bliss Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520588.jpg
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana, cut into chunks
1/3 cup strawberries
1/3 cup pitted cherries
1 cup Almond Breeze Unsweetened Original Almondmilk

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
This banana, strawberry, cherry, and almondmilk smoothie makes a great mid-day snack or quick, refreshing breakfast.
