Title:
sweet and sour pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs boneless pork, cubed
15 1/4 oz can pineapple in chunks, reserve juice
3/4 cup BBQ sauce
1 1/2 cups water
vegetable:
green bell pepper
chinese peas
flat green beans
sliced carrot

Directions:
Fry pork in 2 tbs oil until brown. add 3/4 cup reserved pineapple juice, BBQ sauce, and water.  Cover and simmer for 40 mins
Thicken sauce with 1 tbs cornstarch combined with 2 tbs water.  add 1 cup pineapple chunks and preferred vegetables.  Simmer until vegetables are tender and sauce is thick.
Serve over rice.
