Title:
Savory Brisket Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3lb beef brisket cut into 3 pieces lengthwise
1 medium butternut squash (3lb) peeled, cut into about 1 in cubes
1 medium onion, cut into 1/2 in wedges
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 28oz can crushed tomatoes, undrained
2 cups beef broth
1 cup dry red wine (or additional broth)
14 cups (28oz) uncooked wide egg noodles
1/4 cup quick-cooking tapioca

Directions:
1. Spray 5-6 quart slow cooker with cooking oil.  Arrange brisket pieces fat side up. Add remaining ingredients except noodles and tapioca in order listed above.
2. Cover, cook on low heat 8-10 hours
3. Cook and drain noodles as directed on package. Meanwhile, remove brisket from cooker and place fat side up on cutting board.  If necessary, scrape off any fat from brisket and discard. Using 2 forks, coarsely shred brisket.
4. Stir tapioca into liquid in cooker, return shredded brisket to cooker. Increase heat setting to high. Cover, cook about 15 minutes or until thickened.  Serve in soup bowls over hot noodles.
