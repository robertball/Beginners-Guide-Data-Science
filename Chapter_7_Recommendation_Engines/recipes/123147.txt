Title:
Ranch Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323855.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 tablespoons dry ranch salad dressing mix
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, halved lengthwise

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, combine butter, dressing mix and garlic powder; beat until combined. Spread over cut sides of bread. Place on a baking sheet. broil 4-6-in. from the heat for 3-4 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
John  Palmer
Description: 
'I've worked as a manager of a fast food restaurant for 12 year,' relates John Palmer of Cottonwood, California. 'At home, I like to cook up different things using everyday ingredients.' John gives a buttery loaf of French bread plenty of flavor simply with salad dressing mix and garlic powder.
