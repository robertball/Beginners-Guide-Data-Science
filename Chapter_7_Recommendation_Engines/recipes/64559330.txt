Title:
Quinoa and Kale salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. quinoa
1 can garbanzos, drained, rinsed
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 c. diced cucumber
1 c. crumbled feta
1/4 c. finely chopped shallots
2 T. red wine vinegar
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 tsp. honey
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 c. olive oil
3 c. baby kale, roughly chopped
1/4 c. pine nuts, toasted

Directions:
Cook quinoa and drain; cool completely. Transfer to a large bowl and add garbanzo beans, tomatoes, cucumber, feta, and shallots.
In a separate bowl, whisk together vinegar, lemon, honey, salt and pepper. Slowly add oil, whisking as you go. (or use hand blender) Pour over quinoa mixture, then add chopped kale, tossing to combine. Top with pine nuts.
