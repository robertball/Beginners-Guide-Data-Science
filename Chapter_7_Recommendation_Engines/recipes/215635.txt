Title:
Easy Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5417360.jpg
Ingredients:
2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and diced
2 green onions, chopped
1 clove clove of garlic, minced, or to taste
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon orange juice

Directions:
Stir the mangos, green onions, garlic, cilantro, lime juice, and orange juice together in a bowl. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
ArizonaAmy
Description: 
A very tasty and simple mango salsa.  It can also be used over chicken, with fajitas, or with quesadillas. I chop up the ingredients in my food processor to save time.
