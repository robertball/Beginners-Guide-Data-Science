Title:
Apple Cider Brine for Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts apple cider
1/2 cup brown sugar - can substitute with maple syrup or honey
1 1/2 cups Kosher Salt - can substitute with 3/4 cup table salt
1 Tablespoon black peppercorns
20 juniper berries
10 sprigs of fresh thyme
2 heads fresh garlic
5 quarts cold water
2 quarts ice

Directions:
Combine apple cider, brown sugar, salt, peppercorns, juniper berries, and fresh thyme in a large pot.  Slice the heads of garlic crosswise, and add to the pot.  Bring the mixture to a boil, then cover and set aside to steep and cool.
Before brining, add 5 quarts of cold water and 2 quarts of ice to the mixture.  Now you're ready to brine up to 15 pounds of turkey, chicken, or pork.

Submitter: 
notajenny
Description: 
This brine is a sweet, low salt brine, suitable for many types of meat including turkey, chicken, and especially pork tenderloin.
