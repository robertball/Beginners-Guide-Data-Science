Title:
Warm Yam Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cubed yams
3 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
10 oz. bag of spinach, torn into pieces
1 - 2 garlic cloves, minced
3 Tbsp. olive oil
1 cup crumbled feta
1/4 cup pine nuts
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Combine yams, first amount of olive oil and brown sugar in a medium bowl.
Transfer to ungreased baking sheet, and bake in 350F oven for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Transfer to large bowl and set aside.
Saute mushrooms, spinach and garlic in the second amount of olive oil in a large frying pan on medium-high for 3 to 5 minutes until mushrooms are soft.
Add to yam mixture.
Sprinkle feta cheese and pine nuts on top.
Drizzle with vinegar and toss until well coated.
Serve warm.
