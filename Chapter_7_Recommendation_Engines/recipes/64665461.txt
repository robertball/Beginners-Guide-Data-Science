Title:
Gluten Free Mascarpone Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5221761.jpg
Ingredients:
1.75 cups white sugar
1 cup mascarpone
5 medium eggs
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups gluten free all-purpose flour
1.5 t baking powder
1 cup whole milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 175°C. Grease 12-cup Bundt® or 10-inch angel food cake (tube) pan; sprinkle with gluten-free flour blend. Set aside.
Combine sugar and mascarpone in the bowl of a standing mixer. Beat at medium speed, scraping bowl often, until creamy. Add 1 egg at a time, beating well after each addition. Add vanilla; beat until well mixed.
Stir together gluten-free flour blend and baking powder in bowl. Lower the speed to the low setting. Gradually add flour blend mixture alternately with milk to the butter mixture until well mixed.
Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake 50-60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pan.
