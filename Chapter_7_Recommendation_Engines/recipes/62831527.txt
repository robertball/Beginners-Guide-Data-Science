Title:
Ranch Onion Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/671870.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds lean ground beef
2 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
2 package Lipton Dry Onion Soup Mix
2 egg, lightly beaten
16 Slices Cheddar Cheese
32 Mini Hamburger Buns

Directions:
Preheat the grill for high heat.
In a bowl, mix the ground beef, ranch dressing mix, Lipton Onion Soup Mix and egg. Form into 32 miniature slider sized hamburger patties.
Lightly oil the grill grate. Place patties on the grill, and cook 5 minutes per side, or until well done.  Top with half a slice of cheese and place on bun.
