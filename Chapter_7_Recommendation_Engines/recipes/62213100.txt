Title:
Gluten Free Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dark brown sugar, firmly packed
3/4 cup teff flour
3/4 cup brown rice flour
1/2 cup arrowroot starch (or tapioca flour)
1-1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 large eggs
1/3 cup vegetable oil
2/3 cup water
1/2 cup mashed banana, if desired
1/2 cup chopped walnuts, if desired
OR
1/2 cup frozen blueberries, if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly coat muffin pans with non-stick spray.  Combine sugar,flours, arrowroot starch (or tapioca starch if substituting), baking powder, cinnamon and salt.  Mix eggs, oil and water and add to the flour mixture, mixing quickly.  Add bananas and walnuts OR frozen blueberries, if desired.  Fill muffin pans 3/4 full.  Bake for 20-25 minutes, checking at 20.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a recipe I got from Bob's Red Mill which I use as a base for many flavor variations from blueberry to banana walnut.  What started as an experiment for my Celiac daughter is now requested by everyone in the family.
