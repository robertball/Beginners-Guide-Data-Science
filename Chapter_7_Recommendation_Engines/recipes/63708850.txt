Title:
Sweet Corn Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ears sweet corn, kernels removed and cobs chopped
4 cups whole milk
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup sugar
5 egg yolks, lightly beaten

Directions:
Cook corn kernels, cobs, milk, cream, and sugar in a large saucepan over low heat, stirring until sugar dissolves.  Simmer one hour, uncovered.  Discard corn cobs.
Puree corn mixture in batches in a blender until smooth.
Put egg yolks in a large bowl.  Slowly whisk in hot milk mixture.  Return mixture to saucepan.
Cook over low heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon until it thickens (170 degrees).  Do not allow to boil.
Immediately strain custard through a fine mesh sieve.  Chill for at least 6 hours.
Transfer custard to ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.  Put in an airtight container and freeze until firm, at least 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
An unusual and delicious ice cream that makes good use of ripe late summer corn.  Serve with fresh fruit.
