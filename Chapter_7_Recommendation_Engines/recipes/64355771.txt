Title:
Chicken & Gnocchi Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz gnocchi
1 cup frozen peas
1 pound boneless skinless chicken, cut into 1 inch pieces
1/3 cup flour
2 T flour
1/4 t salt
1/2 t pepper
2 cups diced carrots
1 cup sliced celery
1 medium onion diced
1 t dried thyme
1 3/4 c chicken broth

Directions:
Boil water. Add gnocchi & cook, stirring frequently, for 2 minutes. Stir in peas and cook until gnocchi are tender 2 more minutes.
Toss chicken with flour, cook in 1 T oil over med-high heat, sprinkle with salt & pepper to taste until lightly brown 5-8 minutes. remove to plate.
Add remaining 1 T oil, add carrots, celery, onion, thyme, salt & pepper. Cook until crisp tender, 5-7 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining flour, stir to coat, stir in broth & chicken cook until thick, about 3 minutes. Add gnocchi & peas, cook, stirring gently until gnocchi are hot, about 3 minutes.
