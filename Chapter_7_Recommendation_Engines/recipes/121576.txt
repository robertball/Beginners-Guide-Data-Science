Title:
Plum Quick Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 (6 ounce) jars plum or plum with apples baby food
1 cup vegetable oil
1 cup buttermilk
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups sugar
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the eggs, baby food, oil and buttermilk.  Combine dry ingredients; add to the egg mixture and beat just until moistened. Transfer to two greased 9-in. x 5-in. x 3-in. loaf pans.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 60-65 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.  Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks.

Submitter: 
Deb  Thierwechter
Description: 
Friends and family will rush to get another slice of this quick bread shared by Deb Thierwechter, who bakes these tasty loaves in her Clayton, Delaware kitchen. Mild plum and cinnamon make a mouth-watering combination. No one will guess the secret ingredient is baby food.
