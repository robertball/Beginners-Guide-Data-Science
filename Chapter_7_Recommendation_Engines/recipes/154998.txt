Title:
Cranberry-Orange Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982115.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole berry cranberry sauce
1/2 orange, zest and juice
1/4 cup onion, finely diced
1/4 cup diced celery
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 pound pork tenderloin, trimmed
1 (16 ounce) bag Birds Eye® Frozen Broccoli Florets, Cauliflower & Carrots

Directions:
In medium mixing bowl, combine cranberry sauce, orange zest, orange juice, onion, celery, salt, pepper and cornstarch; mix well. Place tenderloins in a 9x13 inch greased pan. Pour cranberry sauce mix over pork. Cover and bake in a preheated 350 degrees F oven for 45 minutes. Remove cover and place vegetables around tenderloin. Return to oven uncovered and bake 45 minutes or until tested done.

Submitter: 
Birds Eye®
Description: 
Pork and vegetables are delicious with this tangy fruity marinade.
