Title:
Fruit & Wheat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mixed dried fruit, chopped (dates, peaches, apricots or pre-blended fruit mix)
1 1/2 cups whole wheat self-rising flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup oat bran
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 1/4 cup skim milk
1 egg
1 tbsp vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat to 350
Soak dried fruit mix in a bowl with boiling water for 5-10 minutes.
Combine flour and baking powder in a large bowl.
Stir in sugar and oat bran.
Combine milk, eggs, oil and fruit in a separate bowl and mix.
Make a well in the dry ingredients and add the wet ons, then fold in gently until combined.
Divide evenly into 12 muffin cups or pre-greased pan.
Bake 20 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Ms. Robinson
Description: 
So bursting with goodness that you feel like they're healing you as you eat them. Packed with dried fruits in a hearty wheat and oat base. Happy morning!
