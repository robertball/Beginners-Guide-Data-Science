Title:
DebB's Chayote Chicken Casserole (encore appearance) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded or cut thin
1/2 tsp aleppo pepper flakes (or crushed red pepper flakes)
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp Mexican oregano
1 TB olive oil
1/2 C chopped yellow onion
2 cloves of minced garlic
1 or 2 chayote squash, peeled and sliced
zest of one lime
juice of one lime
7 oz. roasted, chopped green chiles
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 C reduced-fat, shredded Mexican cheese blend

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Sprinkle chicken with aleppo pepper, cumin and oregano and set aside. Heat olive oil in a dutch oven and saute onion and garlic till just tender. Add chayote slices, lime zest, & juice and saute/simmer a few minutes longer. Slide chicken pieces under the chayote/onion mixture, then top chayote with green chiles and tomatoes. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, cover and place in oven for 35 minutes. Remove from oven, sprinkle with cheese and return to oven, uncovered, five more minutes or until cheese is melted.
