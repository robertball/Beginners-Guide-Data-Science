Title:
Blackened Halibut With Remoulade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1/3  cup  low-fat mayonnaise
2  tablespoons  chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
1  tablespoon  chopped fresh chives
1  tablespoon  finely chopped cornichon
1  tablespoon  whole-grain Dijon mustard
1  teaspoon  fresh lemon juice
1/8  teaspoon  salt
1  garlic clove, minced
Fish:
2  teaspoons  paprika
1  teaspoon  ground cumin
1/2  teaspoon  salt
1/2  teaspoon  sugar
1/2  teaspoon  garlic powder
1/2  teaspoon  dried oregano
1/4  teaspoon  dried thyme
1/4  teaspoon  ground red pepper
4  (6-ounce) halibut fillets
2  teaspoons  canola oil

Directions:
1. To prepare sauce, combine first 8 ingredients in a bowl. Cover and chill.
2. To prepare fish, combine paprika and next 7 ingredients (through red pepper). Sprinkle fish evenly with seasoning mixture. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add fish to pan; saut√© 4 minutes on each side or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork or until desired degree of doneness. Serve with sauce.
