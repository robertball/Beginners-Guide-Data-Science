Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6720879.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box of banana cook and serve pudding mix
1 1/2 cups cold milk
14 oz sweetened condensed milk
16 oz cool whip, thawed
4 bananas
1 12 oz box vanilla wafers

Directions:
1. Mix together milk, and pudding mix  and cook  in pot until thicken.
2. Pour in bowl Chill in Freezer for about 10 minuets.
3. Add in condensed milk and mix until well combined.
4. Add in 8 oz of cool whip to pudding mix. Fold into pudding mix .
5. Layer 1/3 of pudding mix, topped with wafers, then banana slice, and repeat 3 times.
6. Top with the remaining whipped cream  and Chill for an hour or overnight  before serving.
