Title:
Mango peach avocado spinach shrimp salad with Asian orange vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 mango (cut into bite size pieces)
1 avocado (cut into bite size pieces)
1-2 peaches (cut into bite size pieces)
1/2 small-medium onion (cut into thin ribbons)
small bunch of cilantro (remove stems)
1 bag of spinach
2 lb wild shrimp (shelled, steamed, and seasoned with Old Bay)
.
Dressing:
1 clove garlic (crushed)
1 t sea salt (crushed)
5-10 pepper corns (crushed)
1 heaping T of orange Marmalade
1 T raw sugar
1 t dijon mustard
1 t soy sauce
1-3 T Sesame oil

Directions:
Combine vinaigrette
Combine mango, onion, avocado, peaches, cilantro
Heavily dress the above ingredients with vinaigrette
Plate spinach, and top with above
Place shrimp around edge or on top
eat
