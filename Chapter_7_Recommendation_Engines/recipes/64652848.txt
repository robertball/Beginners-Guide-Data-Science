Title:
Barbecue Fritos® Dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4542981.jpg
Ingredients:
8 hot dogs
8 hot dog buns
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup barbecue sauce, or more to taste
1 cup corn chips, such as Fritos®, or more as needed
1/2 cup mild or hot  jarred banana pepper rings
1-2 fresh jalapeno peppers sliced into rings

Directions:
Cook hot dogs according to package directions.
Place 1 hot dog onto each bun and top with some barbecue sauce.
Layer on the onions, cheese, Fritos(R), pepper rings and jalapenos.
Top with extra barbecue sauce.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
We love trying different hot dog combinations and this one was a winner. Hope you enjoy as much as we do! :)
