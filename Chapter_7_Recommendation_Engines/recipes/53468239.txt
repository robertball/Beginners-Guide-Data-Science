Title:
Sirloin Steak Dianne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) beef filets
Salt, pepper & dry mustard
3 TBS clarified butter
2 TBS shallots, chopped fine
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup button mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup red wine
2 TBS Worcestershire sauce
1 TBS lemon juice
1 TBS dijon mustard
1/2 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground white pepper
1 TBS parsley, chopped
1 TBS chives, chopped
1/4 cup brandy

Directions:
Rub steaks with salt, pepper and dry mustard.  Preheat a 12&#34; skillet over med heat.  Add butter and heat for 1 min.  Add steaks and cook the steaks for 2 minutes on each side.  Remove steaks from the pan and set aside.
Add the shallots, mushrooms and garlic over medium heat until they soften (2 - 3 min.  Stir in the red wine,  Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, dijon mustard and thyme; mix well.  Bring to a simmer.
Gently return the steaks to the sauce in the skillet.  Cook 2 to 3 minutes per side.  Add salt, pepper, parsley and chives.  Add brandy and ignite.
When flames die out.  put steaks on plates and spoon sauce over them.  Serve immediately.
