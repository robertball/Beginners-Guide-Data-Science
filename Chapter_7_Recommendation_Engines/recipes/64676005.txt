Title:
Seared Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Yellowfin tuna steaks (approx. 1 1/2" thick)
Thai marinade (Whole Foods)
Sesame seeds
Olive oil

Directions:
Marinate tuna in Thai marinade, turning several times; 40 minutes
Sprinkle with garlic powder
Sour sesame seeds onto a plate
Roll tuna steaks in sesame seeds, coating all sides
Heat olive oil to hot in frying pan

Submitter:

Description: 
Allow about 1/3 pound per person
