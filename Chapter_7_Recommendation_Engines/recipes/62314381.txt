Title:
Layered Beef Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
2(15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
3/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 1/2 cup chopped onion
3 cups sliced carrots
3 pounds lean ground beef cooked and drained
1 can spaghetti sauce
salt and pepper to taste
2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar

Directions:
In a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish, layer potatoes, corn, green pepper, onion and carrots. Crumble beef over the vegetables. Pour spaghetti sauce over top. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
