Title:
1-2-3 Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983634.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6 ounce) cans Hunt's® Tomato Paste
3 cups hot water
2 (14.5 ounce) cans Hunt’s® Diced Tomatoes with Basil, Garlic and Oregano, undrained

Directions:
Combine tomato paste and water in medium saucepan; stir in diced tomatoes with their liquid.
Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low; simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve over hot cooked pasta.

Submitter: 
Hunts.com
Description: 
Diced tomatoes seasoned with basil, garlic and oregano add an Italian flavor to this simple sauce.
