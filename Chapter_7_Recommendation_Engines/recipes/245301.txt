Title:
Lazy Green Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2780147.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen peas
1 tablespoon shredded Italian cheese blend (such as WegmansÂ®)
1 teaspoon nutritional yeast, or more to taste
1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat frozen peas in a small microwave-safe bowl on high in the microwave until hot, about 3 minutes.
Stir Italian cheese blend, nutritional yeast, and garlic powder into peas; season with salt and black pepper.
