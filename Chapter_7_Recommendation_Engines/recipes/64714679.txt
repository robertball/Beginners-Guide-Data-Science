Title:
Southwest Chicken Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken, chopped
1 1/2 cups coleslaw mix
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1/2 cup lite ranch dressing
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup grape or cherry tomatoes, quartered
Freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Sarah Massey
Description: 
A delicious Southwest Mexican side dish! Serve at a BBQ or picnic and also great served in wraps for lunch! (Southwest Chicken Coleslaw Recipe from Hot Eats and Cool Reads)
