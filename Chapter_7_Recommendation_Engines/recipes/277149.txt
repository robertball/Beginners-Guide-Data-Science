Title:
Sausage Balls without Bisquick® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7488595.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cornbread stuffing mix
3/4 cup hot water
1 pound bulk pork sausage
1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese
1/8 cup chopped green onion
1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Cover a large baking dish with aluminum foil.
Mix stuffing mix and water in a bowl until moistened. Add pork, Cheddar cheese, green onion, and baking powder. Mix well.
Shape into 1-inch balls and place into the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until browned and no longer pink in the centers, 18 to 20 minutes.
