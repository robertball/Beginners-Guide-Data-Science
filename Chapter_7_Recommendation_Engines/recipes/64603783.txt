Title:
Bechamel sauce for lasagna layer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp butter
1/4 cup flour
2 1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/4 each salt, pepper, nutmeg

Directions:
Melt butter in sauce pan and add the flour slowly whisking in to brown. Add milk 1/2 cups at a time making sure each addition thickens up. When all milk is added and sauce has thickened, add the cheese and spices.
Use as needed or for a layer of lasagna
