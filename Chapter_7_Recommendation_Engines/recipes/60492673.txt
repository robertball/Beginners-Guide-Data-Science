Title:
Taco Seasoning I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/274493.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon lemon pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together chili powder, garlic powder, oregano, paprika, cumin, salt, pepper, and lemon pepper. Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Cooks4Kids
Description: 
My kids mostly prefer plain hamburger meat on their tacos but they liked this recipe when I toned down the spice and added lemon pepper.Modified from: Taco Seasoning I
