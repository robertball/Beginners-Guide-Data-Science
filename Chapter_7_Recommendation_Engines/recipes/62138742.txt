Title:
Bug's Favorite Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 1/3 cups Vegetable Oil
1 Cup White Granulated Sugar
1 Cup Brown Sugar
2 tsp. Madagascar Bourbon Vanilla
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. Ground Cinnamon
2 cups Grated Carrots
1 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup raisens

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 9 x 13 inch pan.
In a large bowl, beat together eggs, oil, sugars and 2 teaspoons vanilla. In a serpate bowl mix dry ingredients then incooperate into wet ingredients and blend and mix well. Fold in Carrots, pecans and raisens and pour into prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven for 40 - 50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool in pan and frost with your favorite cream cheese frosting.
