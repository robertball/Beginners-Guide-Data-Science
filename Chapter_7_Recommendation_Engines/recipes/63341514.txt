Title:
Italian Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887354.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons basil
2 tablespoons oregano
2 tablespoons thyme
2 tablespoons marjoram
1 tablespoon rosemary
1 tablespoon sage

Directions:
In a food processor, combine basil, oregano, thyme, marjoram, rosemary and sage. Blend for 1 minute, or until desired consistency is achieved.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Better than already prepared types because you can adjust it to your taste. I use the bulk baggie spices in the Mexican food aisle, they're so much less expensive and taste great!Modified from: Italian Seasoning II
