Title:
Cucumbers in Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982565.jpg
Ingredients:
3 medium cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
1 medium onion, sliced
2 cups water
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In a bowl, combine cucumbers, onion, water and salt. Let stand for 1 hour; drain and rinse. In a serving bowl, mix cream, sugar and vinegar. Add cucumbers and onion; toss gently. Sprinkle with parsley if desired.

Submitter: 
Phyllis  Kirsling
Description: 
'WE HAD an enormous vegetable garden, so we enjoyed freshly picked cucumbers in summer. After the cucumber were sliced and soaked awhile in salt water. Mother added thick cream, which she carefully scooped from the top of some fresh milk.'
