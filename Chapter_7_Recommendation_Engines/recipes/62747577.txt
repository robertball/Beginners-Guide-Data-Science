Title:
Black Forest Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/632693.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can cherry pie filling
1 chocolate cake mix
ingredients needed to make cake
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
Optional crust
1 chocolate cake mix & 2 sticks butter
or
1 package chocolate Oreo cookies, crushed

Directions:
Line dutch over with tin foil. Spray well with cooking spray.
Mix cake mix, set aside.
Optional crust: 1. Melt butter and mix with chocolate cake mix. Spread on bottom of dutch oven. This will create a gooey crust. -OR- 2. Crush Oreo cookies and spread on bottom of dutch oven. This will create a firmer crust. -OR- no crust is necessary.
Pour cherry pie filling into dutch oven.
Spread cake mix on top.
Layer chocolate chips on top of cake mix.
Cook at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes or until cake is done. Check every 15 minutes and turn lid quarter turn.
Enjoy!
