Title:
Microwave Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanuts
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup corn syrup (light or dark - I always used dark)
1 tsp. margarine
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Mix peanuts, sugar, salt and corn syrup in a GLASS!! microwave safe bowl.
Microwave, uncovered, on high x 5 minutes.
Add margarine and vanilla.
Microwave, uncovered, another 1 minute on high.
Add soda. Stir till light & fluffy.
Pour into a greased 10" x 15" cookie sheet.
Cool. Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A quick, easy way to enjoy this crunchy classic peanut treat.
