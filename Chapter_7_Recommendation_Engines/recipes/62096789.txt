Title:
Purple Passion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 sticks butter, melted
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup chopped pecans
3 bananas, sliced
1 8 oz. cream cheese
16 oz. Cool Whip (divided)
1 cup confectioners sugar
1 can blueberry pie filling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Combine butter, flour, & pecans. Press into a 9x13 pan. Bake for 15 minutes or until crust is golden. Cool.  Layer sliced bananas onto cooled crust.  Mix cream cheese, 8 oz. of Cool Whip, & confectioners sugar until smooth. Spread cream cheese mixture on top of bananas. Spread blueberry pie filling on top on cream cheese mixture.  Top with remaining 8 oz. of Cool Whip. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours & enjoy!

Submitter: 
BAYOU GAL
Description: 
A buttery pecan crust layered with bananas, cream cheese, blueberries, and whipped topping keeps them coming back for more! (Better keep the recipe handy too!)
