Title:
Sauteed Swiss Chard with Mushrooms and Roasted Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4462489.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 bunch Swiss chard, chopped
1/2 roasted red pepper, chopped
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1/2 cup white wine

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms, onion, and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent and mushrooms soften, about 5 minutes. Stir in Swiss chard, red pepper, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper. Cook until Swiss chard is wilted, about 3 minutes. Stir in white wine; cook until Swiss chard is tender, 3 to 4 minutes more.

Description: 
Made this up when I had Swiss chard and did not know what to do with it. Added whatever I had in the fridge. Added some white wine and voila. Yummy. 
You can also use other veggies or seasoning you have on hand.
