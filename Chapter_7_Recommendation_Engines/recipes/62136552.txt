Title:
Granny's Old Fashioned Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Sugar
1/2 t. Nutmeg
1/2 t. Allspice
1 3/4t Cinnamon
2 c. Whole Milk
2 T. Butter
1 t. Vanilla
16oz. Pumpkin
3 Large Eggs, Beaten
2 Deep Dish Pie Shells

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Combine sugar and spices.
On stove mix milk and butter and cook on medium high heat until butter is melted.
Remove from heat and add sugar spice mixture, vanilla, pumpkin and beaten eggs.
Mix well.
Pour into 2 unbaked deep dish pie shells.
Bake at 325 degrees for 15 minutes. Increase temperature to 350 degrees and continue cooking for 45 minutes or until set and lightly brown.
Cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
This pumpkin pie pie recipe was always requested by those who tried my Grandmother's pumpkin pie.   Granny's pumpkin pie was always a tradition at Thanksgiving.
