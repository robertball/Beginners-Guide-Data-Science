Title:
Sweet, Sticky and Spicy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon brown sugar
2 tablespoons honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 teaspoons chopped fresh ginger root
2 teaspoons chopped garlic
2 tablespoons hot sauce
salt and pepper to taste
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1/2 inch strips
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
flour for dredging chicken

Directions:
Mix together brown sugar, honey, soy sauce, ginger, garlic and hot sauce in a small bowl.
Lightly salt and pepper the chicken strips.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add flour dredged chicken strips and brown on both sides, about 1 minute per side. Pour the sauce over the chicken. Simmer uncovered until the sauce thickens, 8 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Hana
Description: 
This delicious dish uses skinless boneless chicken breasts cooked in a gingery, spicy, soy  sauce. It's great as an appetizer or as a main dish served with rice and a veggie.Modified from: Sweet, Sticky and Spicy Chicken
