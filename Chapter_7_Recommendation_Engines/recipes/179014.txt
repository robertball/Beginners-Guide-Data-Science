Title:
Easy Tomato Soup Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977835.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package spice cake mix
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Tomato Soup
1/2 cup water
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease and lightly flour two 8-inch or 9-inch round cake pans.
Mix cake mix, soup, water and eggs according to package directions.  Pour into prepared pans.
Bake 25 minutes or until done.
Cool on wire racks 10 minutes.  Remove from pans and cool completely.
Fill and frost with your favorite cream cheese frosting.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Adding tomato soup could makes a spice cake mix surprisingly moist and delicious.
