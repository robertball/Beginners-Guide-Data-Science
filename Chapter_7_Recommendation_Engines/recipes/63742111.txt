Title:
My Favorite Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1038774.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
3 celery stalks, diced small
1/2 C mayonaise
3/4 C pecans, roughly chopped
2 Tbsp yellow onion, minced
1 1/2 Tbsp sweet pickle relish
2 tsp prepared mustard
1 1/2 tsp black pepper
1 1/2 tsp dill
1 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp white sugar
1/2 tsp paprika

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large mixing bowl and mix thoroughly to combine.  Refrigerate 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
I'm a huge fan of chicken salad and have tried so many different variations over the years, but this one is by far my favorite.  Simple, refreshing flavors and great texture make it wonderful on a sandwich or just served over a bed of lettuce!
