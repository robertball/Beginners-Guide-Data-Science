Title:
Stuffed Avocados Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One large avocado sliced in half.
1/3 pound of flaked (poached) halibut, or canned white chunk albacore.Drain well.
2 heaping tablespoons of red onion chopped into 1/4" bits.
A few drops of truffled olive oil.
Freshly grated lemon zest - teaspoon full...
Kosher salt to taste.

Directions:
Slice Avocado.
Mix Stuffing (halibut, red onion, truffled olive oil,lemon zest.
Stuff avocados.
Rain with kosher salt to taste.

Submitter: 
the dean
Description: 
A super easy, heart friendly hot weather delight.
One large avocado cut in half and stuffed with a mix of flaked poached halibut, red onions, a few drops of truffled olive oil, and lemon zest.
