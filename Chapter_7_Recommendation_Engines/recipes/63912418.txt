Title:
Lemon Risotto and Garlic Lemon Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1085128.jpg
Ingredients:
Risotto
6-8 c chicken stock
1 T olive oil
1 T butter
1/2 yellow onion, diced
6-8 small green onions, whites/light green only, chopped
5-8 garlic cloves, chopped
2 c arborio rice
1/2 c white wine
salt
1/4 to 1/3 c lemon juice
zest from 1-2 lemons
1/4 c. parmesan reggiano cheese, shredded
parsley
Green Beans
3/4 to 1 lb green beans
1 T olive oil
2-4 garlic cloves
squeeze of lemon juice
sea salt

Directions:
Risotto
heat chicken broth in sauce pan
heat olive oil and butter over medium heat
saute onion, garlic
add rice and saute until glistens
add wine until absorbed
add warm stock 1/2 c at time until absorbed and rice cooked 20-30 min
add lemon juice/zest
add cheese at table
garnish with parsley
Green Beans
boil green beans in water 3 min; drain and put on ice; drain again
coat with olive oil, garlic, salt, lemon
saute in butter until warm, slightly browned and crisp/cooked
