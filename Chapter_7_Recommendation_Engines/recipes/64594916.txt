Title:
Chili Brick Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I brick of frozen chili like Dolores
8 oz of Mexican velveeta
1 cup salsa verde

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a crockpot. Heat on low.  Heat until hot and bubbly.  Stir often.
Serve with tortilla chips.
