Title:
Eggs Benedict Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 English muffins - diced into 1/2 inch cubes
10-12 ounces Canadian bacon or ham - diced into 1/2 inch cubes
8 large eggs
2 cups  milk
1 teaspoon onion powder
3 tablespoons scallions or chives - chopped
1 teaspoon salt
nonstick cooking spray
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 envelope hollandaise sauce - (made according to package directions)

Directions:
Whisk the eggs in a large bowl, add milk, salt, scallions and onion powder.
Mix well.
Spray a 9x13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Add 1/2 of the Canadian bacon, followed by English muffin cubes, distribute remaining Canadian bacon on top.
Pour egg mixture evenly over the top.
Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
Preheat oven to 375Â°.
While oven is heating, sprinkle cassarole with paprika.
Cover with foil and bake for 45-55 minutes or until the eggs are set.  Remove foil the last 15 minutes.
Drizzle with the hollandaise sauce or serve on the side if desired.

Description: 
Overnight Eggs Benedict!  Easy to make ahead, perfect for brunch or a special occasion or overnight house guests.
