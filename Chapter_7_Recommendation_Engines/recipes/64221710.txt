Title:
Jim's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1654980.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Pounds Beef Stew Meat
1 Pkg Lawrys Beef Stew Mix
3 large potatoes cut in 1/4 th
1 1# package Carrots
1 T minced garlic
1 12oz can corn incl juice
1 12oz can peas incl juice
1 12oz can tomato sauce
1 12oz can whole tomatoes
2- 4oz cans mushrooms
1 T Worcester sauce
2 beef bouillon cubes
4 med onions 1/4 erd
1/2 onion diced
3 C water
1/2 C instant mashed potatoes
2 12oz Mr. & Mrs. T Bloody Mary Mix
1 T El Yucateco Habanero Sauce Red
1 t red pepper flakes
Salt and pepper to taste
4 T vegetable oil to brown the meat

Directions:
Brown the meat in the vegetable oil. Add all the other ingredients and let simmer for 3 hours on low-med heat. You can also transfer this to a crockpot on high for 3 hours
