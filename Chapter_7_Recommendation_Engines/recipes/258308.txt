Title:
Refresh & Reboot Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520595.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fresh spinach
1/2 pear, cored
1/2 lemon, peeled, seeded and chopped
1 cup Almond Breeze Unsweetened Original Almondmilk

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Fruit, citrus, and spinach come together to make a refreshing almondmilk smoothie.
