Title:
Mashed Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Butternut Squashes
1/2 cup Butter
3/4 cup Sour Cream and 1 tbsp of Sour Cream to Garnish
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tbsp brown sugar
A Few Sprigs of Fresh Parsley to Garnish
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Cut Squashes in half, and pierce skin with small knife before placing squashes cut side down in a baking dish (medium size) With enough water to make sure it does not burn; usually about 1/2 inch. Cover, and bake until tender. Scoop squash from skin into a bowl, add Sour Cream, Cinnamon, brown sugar, and salt and pepper. Mash mixture until smooth.
