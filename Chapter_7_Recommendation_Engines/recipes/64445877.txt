Title:
Cold Peanut Soba Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2374633.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces of uncut soba noodles
1/4 cup of peanut butter or 1/4 cup of PB2 with 3 tablespoons of water
2 tablespoon of toasted sesame oil
1/4 cup of rice vinegar
1 tablespoon of soy sauce
2 tablespoons of chili sauce (optional)
Chopped peanuts to garnish (optional)
Fresh raw or blanched vegetables

Directions:
Boil soba according to package instructions with a generous amount of salt. Once cooked completely, rinse under cold water in a mesh strainer.
Meanwhile, mix the peanut butter, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and chili sauce (optional) in a large bowl. Then coat the noodles and chill for at least one hour.
When serving, mix in the toasted sesame oil and vegetables, or serve vegetables on the side. Enjoy!
