Description: 
This is a simple yet tasty garlic spread that's quick to make and can be adapted in many different ways. It's a good starter for the beginning cook to try out and then improve! Serve hot with your favorite meal! You can use whatever combination of herbs and spices you like, or even fresh roasted garlic, the works! Try adding fresh chopped herbs and veggies; spread on top of anything from simply sliced white or Italian bread, or specialty artisan breads. Get creative!
