Title:
Ovaltine Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c sugar
1/2 c butter, softened
1 t vanilla extract
2 eggs
2 3/4 c A/P flour
1 t cream of tartar
1/2 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
2 T sugar
2 T cinnamon
2 T ovaltine

Directions:
Heat oven to 400.
In large bowl combine sugar, butter, extract and eggs.  Blend well.
Add flour, cream of tartar, soda, salt.  Mix thoroughly.
Shape dough into 1" balls.
Combine 2 T each sugar, ovaltine and cinnamon.
Roll balls into sugar/ovaltine/cinnamon mixture.
Place 2" apart on cookie sheet.
Bake 8 mins.
Remove from cookie sheet immediately.
