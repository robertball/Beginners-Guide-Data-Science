Title:
Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices bacon, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup onions, diced
1 cup carrots, diced
6 cups water
4 cups beef broth
1/2 cup tomato paste
1-1/2 cups dry lentils
2 cups potatoes, diced
2 bay leaves
2 cloves
1 tsp salt
2 cups Smokey links sausage, sliced
1-2 Tbsp vinegar (to taste)

Directions:
Saute bacon, garlic, onions and carrots.
Add remaining ingredients EXCEPT sausage and vinegar.  Simmer 1- 1/2 hours.
Add sausage and vinegar.  Cook 1/2 hour more.

Barbara Bartz

Sausage and lentils- great combination!
