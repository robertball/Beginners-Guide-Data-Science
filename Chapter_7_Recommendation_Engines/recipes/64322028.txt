Title:
Fruity Muesli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup(s) nuts, walnuts, chopped
1/4 cup(s) oats, rolled
2 tablespoon wheat germ
1 tablespoon pumpkin seeds
1 tablespoon sunflower seeds
2 teaspoon flax seeds, ground
1/2 cup blueberries
1/2 cup(s) milk, fat-free

Directions:
For the muesli: Heat the oven to 275Â°F. Spread walnuts on a baking sheet and bake until fragrant and lightly browned, about 12 minutes. In a large bowl, combine walnuts, oats, wheat germ, pumpkin seeds, and sunflower seeds.
For each serving, place 1/2 cup muesli in a cereal bowl. Top each with 3/4 teaspoon flaxseed and 1/4 cup apricots. Add 1/4 cup milk and serve.
