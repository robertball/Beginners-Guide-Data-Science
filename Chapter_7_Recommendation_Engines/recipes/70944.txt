Title:
Southwest Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 pound lean ground beef
1 cup water
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (6.0 ounce) package PASTA RONI® Four Cheese Flavor
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 cup chunky salsa
1 cup frozen whole kernel corn
1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
Chopped cilantro (optional)

Directions:
In large skillet, brown ground beef until thoroughly cooked; drain. Add water, milk, and margarine or butter to skillet with beef. Bring to a boil.
Slowly stir in pasta, seasonings and chili powder. Return to a boil. Reduce heat to medium. Gently boil uncovered, 7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Stir in salsa and corn; return to a boil. Boil 1 to 2 minutes or until pasta is slightly firm, stirring frequently. Remove skillet from heat. (Sauce will be thin.) Sprinkle cheese and Topping over pasta. Let stand 3 to 5 minutes for sauce to thicken. Sprinkle with cilantro, if desired.
