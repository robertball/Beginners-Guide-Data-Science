Title:
Bodacious BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs Pork Baby Back Ribs1 (10 2/3 ounce) jar honey1 1/3 teaspoons paprika1 1/3 teaspoons chili powder2/3 teaspoon garlic powder2 2/3 tablespoons Old Bay Seasoning2/3 teaspoon onion powder1/3 teaspoon celery salt2/3 cup dark brown sugar1/3 teaspoon fresh ground pepper1 1/3 medium onion, grated or finely chopped16 ounces of your favorite bbq barbecue sauce1/3 cup white sugar

Directions:
Place all ingredients together in large roasting panCut ribs apart for easier serving.Mix together making sure to coat all ribs with this paste.Spread ribs out evenly on bottom of pan.cover lightly with foil.Bake at 375 degrees for approximately 1 hour.  If you have more time, reduce to 350 for 2 hrs or 325 for 3.Turning or stirring occasionally.Finish ribs on grill to firm up a bit and caramelize the sauce.

Submitter: 
Rick Kleinhans (kokodiablo)
Description: 
A really really good bbq sauce.  Sticky, tacky, bbq goodness.
