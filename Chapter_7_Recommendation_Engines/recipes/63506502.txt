Title:
Apple Jack Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless pork chops or 1 tenderloin cut into medallions
1 Granny Smith apple peeled and chopped large
4 Tbspn butter divided
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup brandy
heavy cream
flour for dredging

Directions:
Dredge pork in the flour
Brown well both sides in 2Tbspn of the butter and remove from the pan
Add the remaining 2 Tbspn butter and brown sugar.  Cook and stir until the sugar melts.
Add the apple and saute about 10 minutes
Add the brandy and stock and bring to a boil
Return the meat to the pan turning to coat, cover and simmer 10 to 15 minutes or until done.
remove the meat to a serving dish
Add the heavy cream (judge the amount for a nice sauce) and heat thru
Pour the sauce over the the meat and serve
