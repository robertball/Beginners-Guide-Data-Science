Title:
Rich & Crunchy Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. Slivered Almonds
3 cans French Cut Green Beans, drained
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/3 c. Half & Half
1 can Water Chestnuts, chopped and drained
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp. pepper
French Fried Onions to top

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2.  Dry Roast almonds in small saucepan, stirring constantly until golden brown.  Remove from saucepan immediately and let cool slightly.
3.  Stir green beans, cream of mushroom soup, half & half, water chestnuts, shredded cheddar cheese,  pepper, and roasted almonds together in a large bowl.  Transfer mixture to a lightly greased casserole bowl.  Top with french fried onions.
4.  Bake 40 min. in preheated oven.  French fried onions will turn a dark brown and casserole will be bubbly.
