Title:
Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
2 eggs
1 cup oatmeal
1 envelope onion soup mix
3 cans vernors
1 375 ml ketchup
1/2 cup applesauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix beef, eggs, oatmeal and onion soup mix in a bowl and form into meatballs
Combine Vernors, ketchup and apple sauce to form sauce.
Place meatballs in a baking pan, pour sauce over them and bake for 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
