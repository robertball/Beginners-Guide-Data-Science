Title:
Key Lime Basil Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
3 lb bone-in short ribs
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
6 small fresh red chiles, stemmed and halved
2 cloves garlic
1 3 inch piece ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
1 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 cup lime marmalade
1/2 cup low sodium chicken broth
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
2 Tbsp finely chopped fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet and brown ribs on all sides.  Remove from skillet and set aside.
Add onion to skillet and saute until soft.  Add chiles, garlic, giner, thyme, allspice, and nutmeg.  Stir until fragrent.  Add marmalade and stir until melted.
Put onion mixture in slow cooker.  Stir in broth and soy sauce.  Nestle ribs in sauce and cook on high 5-6 hours or low 9 hours.
Use tongs to remove ribs.  Pour sauce through a strainer into a medium saucepan.  Add vinegar and bring to a boil.  Boil until reduced by half.  Add basil, and boil an additional minute.  Pour sauce over ribs and serve.
