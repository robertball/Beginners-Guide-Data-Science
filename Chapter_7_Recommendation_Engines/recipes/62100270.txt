Title:
Barbecue Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large chuck roast  about 4-5 lbs.  cooked until very tender. Shred meat
1 cup onions  diced (fry in 1 T. butter) then add
¾ cup catsup
¼ cup Barbecue Sauce (more if needed, depending on size of roast)
½ cup brown sugar
¼ cup cider vinegar
2 cups water

Directions:
Bring sauce to boil.  Add shredded beef, reduce heat and let simmer 1 hour – uncovered.  Serve on buns or dinner rolls.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
This recipe is for 1 large chuck roast.  I usually make 3 or 4 roasts at a time. It freezes very well.  So you may want to double, triple… this recipe.  It is the best I have ever 
           had.
