Title:
Caprese Salad with Balsamic Reduction Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/447921.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large ripe tomatoes cut into 1/2 inch slices
1 pound fresh mozzarella cut into 1/4 inch slices
1 cup of balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup basil leaves
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Add vinegar and honey to a small saucepan.
Over high heat, stir vinegar mixture until soft boil is achieved.
Reduce heat and simmer until mixture has thickened and reduced to 1/3 cup.  Set aside and allow to cool.
On a platter alternately arrange tomato and mozzarella slices.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Top with basil leaves.
Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic reduction.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tomatoes, fresh mozzarella and basil drizzled with a sweet balsamic reduction and olive oil.  Easy, impressive and delicious!
