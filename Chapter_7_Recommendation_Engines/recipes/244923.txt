Title:
Moscato Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4880787.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (750 milliliter) bottles Moscato wine
1 (6 ounce) can frozen strawberry lemonade concentrate
1/2 cup strawberry-flavored vodka

Directions:
Mix Moscato wine, lemonade concentrate, and strawberry-flavored vodka together in a pitcher.

Submitter: 
Pumpkin
Description: 
The bubbly, crisp acidity of Moscato marries with the sweet and tangy strawberry lemonade to create a perfect cocktail. Serve over ice.
