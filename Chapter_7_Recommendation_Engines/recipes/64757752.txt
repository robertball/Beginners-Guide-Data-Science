Title:
Baked bean burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6990974.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1 white onion
1 bunch green onion
2 (12 oz) cans baked beans
1 (12 oz) can sliced mushrooms
1 bag of shredded cheese

Directions:
This recipe serves four.
1) Split hamburger into four patties and fry them as desired
2) Chop up white and green onion
3) Heat up baked beans on stove or microwave
4) Open can of sliced mushrooms
5) Put hamburger patty down, then heated baked beans, then mushrooms, white onions, green onions, and cheese on top
Total comfort food, enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jessica Myers
Description: 
This is a comfort food, child-friendly recipe based on the traditional southern-California Chili Size, except instead of chili-over-a-burger, it's baked-beans-over-a-burger.  And equally as delicious, cheap, and easy to make.
