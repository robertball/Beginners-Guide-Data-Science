Ingredients:
1.5 pounds ground beef
1 egg
1/4 c. Fiber One (original flavor) cereal
1/4 c. onion, finely diced
1/4 c. green pepper, finely diced
1/2 tsp of garlic powder
1/4 tsp celery salt or ground celery seed
salt and pepper to taste.
1/2 carrot, shredded (optional)

Directions:
Rinse Fiber One cereal well to remove sweetener coating. Soak in water to cover for 10 minutes to soften.
Combine all ingredients and mix til evenly combined.
Shape into 2 inch meatballs.
Spray a fry pan with a spray oil and sautee the meatballs on medium for 10 minutes, turning once.
Drain. Add your favorite jarred spaghetti sauce and simmmer for 30 minutes,serve over pasta.
