Title:
Pistachio White Cheddar Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1040031.jpg
Ingredients:
1   cup Original Bisquick mix
1   cup shredded white Cheddar cheese (4 oz)
1   cup shredded mozzarella cheese (4 oz)
1   cup chopped pistachio nuts
1   tablespoon Dijon mustard
1   teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
2   eggs
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 400Â°F. Lightly spray cookie sheet with cooking spray.
In large bowl, stir together all ingredients. Roll into 1-inch balls, and place on cookie sheet.
Bake 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through. Serve immediately.
