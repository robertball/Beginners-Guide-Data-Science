Title:
Yummy Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
3/4 cup cocoa
1 cup melted butter
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/4 flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix together sugar, cocoa, then butter eggs and vanilla. Add flour and salt and mix well. Fold in chocolate chips. Spread batter in 9x13 pan and bake at 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes.
