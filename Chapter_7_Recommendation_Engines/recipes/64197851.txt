Title:
Chocolate Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1564137.jpg
Ingredients:
For the cream cheese:
1 cup Heavy Cream
8 oz Philadelphia cheese
Gelatin (soaked in lukewarm water)
1 tsp vannila extract
1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
For the chocolate:
1 cup heavy cream
2 cups of semi sweet/dark chocolate
For the base:-
1 cup Marie Gold Biscuits ( available in ALL indian stores) or you can use graham crackers )
1 1/s tbsp melted butter
1 tbsp sugar

Directions:
Step 1 - to prepare base
In a food processor put your BISCUIT/CRACKER and blend it to get a smooth powder.
Then add melted butter and sugar.
Mix well
and with a measuring cup or anything press the base into the bowl/plate and make a smooth surface.
Step 2 - to make chocolate
Boil the heavy cream and then pour over the chocolate let it sit for a while and with a whisk/electric whisk on a high speed for 2-3 min and then pour the mixture over the biscuit base.
Step 3 - to make cream cheese
Whisk the heavy cream on a high speed for 5mins and add sugar slowly and then add philadelphia cheese and then whisk it for 5 min for high speed and then add gelatine mix (in a bowl take 1 cup of boiling water and add the sachet of gelatine powder and 1tbsp of sugar by adding sugar it helps to dissolve  fast) and add a 3-4drops of vanilla essence and pour over the chocolate.
1st base will be biscuit base
2nd base will be chocolate mixture
3rd base will be cream cheese mixture
and keep it in a refrigerator not in the FREEZER over night or you can also do the same thing in a mason jar or small cups.
And its ready
