Title:
Slow Cooker Dump and Go Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 can condensed cream of Cheddar cheese soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Spray slow cooker with cooking spray. Place chicken breasts inside. In a medium bowl mix together soups and milk, and pour mixture over chicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste and garlic powder.
Cook on High for about 6 hours. Note: Do not lift lid while cooking!

Submitter: 
Dawn Norby
Description: 
A creamy, Cheddar cheesy soup sauce cooked with chicken and a little seasoning in a slow cooker. This is an easy way to enjoy a great chicken dinner, even when you run out of time!! The trick is do not lift lid while cooking! Make with rice, pasta, or potatoes.4Modified from: Slow Cooker Dump and Go Cheesy Chicken
