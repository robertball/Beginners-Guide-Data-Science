Title:
Easy Tasty Ham Steaks with Maple Glaze For 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Ham Steaks (cooked)Sliced
1 cup Brown sugar
1 cup Pure Maple Syrup (maybe a tad more if you like)
a handful of whole Cloves (about 15)
2 tablespoons dijon mustard
1 1/2 tsp white vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350, put ham steaks in baking dish, in a seperate bowl mix brown sugar, maple syrup, mustard and white vinegar. Then pour over steaks, then for the taste of home, sprinkle the cloves all over both ham steaks. Let cook until browned on both sides, turning every 15 minutes or so. After about 45 minutes, add to your plate, spoon some sauce over ham or make a gravy with it, but don't eat the cloves!

Submitter: 
Chriss Pooler
Description: 
This tastes like Moms easter ham, and only a handful of ingredients! It's designed for 2, but you could alter it for a bigger ham.
