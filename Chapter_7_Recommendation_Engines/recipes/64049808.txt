Title:
Caramelized Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken wings
2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
1/2 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons ketchup
1 cup honey or 1/2 cup honey & 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tblsp molasses
1 clove garlic, minced, 1 squeeze ginger
Lemon juice
Cornstarch
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix together all ingredients except chicken and cornstarch. Cook for 10 mins on low hear, then add cornstarch.
Cook until caramelized. Pour over the chicken.  Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven for one hour, or until sauce is caramelized.

Submitter: 
Shelley Noelle's Garden Moise
Description: 
Soy and ketchup, laced with honey and garlic, make for a potent sauce.  This chicken is ideal for appetizers or a main course.Modified from: Caramelized Baked Chicken
