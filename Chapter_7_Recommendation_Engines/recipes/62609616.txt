Title:
Johnny Appleseed Apple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 C. Shortening
2 Medium Eggs
1 C. Sugar
1 t. Vanilla
1/2 C. Milk
2 C. All Purpose Flour
1 T. Baking Powder
1 t. Cinnamon
1 t. Salt
1/2 C. Applesauce
2 Medium Granny Smith Apples-peeled, cored, and finely chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Cream together shortening and sugar and then mix in eggs and vanilla. Stir in milk.
In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
Stir the dry ingredients into the shortening mixture a little at a time, just until moistened.
Fold in the applesauce and chopped apples.
Fill 15 prepared muffin cups to almost the top of the rim. Place muffin pan in oven and immediately reduce oven temperature to 375 degrees F/190 degrees C. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes or until muffins test done. Remove from pan and cool on wire rack.
