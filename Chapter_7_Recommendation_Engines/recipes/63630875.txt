Title:
Tomato Soup with Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009647.jpg
Ingredients:
14 oz. canned tomatoes, chopped
2 tbsp. tomato paste
3-4 oz. vermicelli or egg noodles
½ large onion, chopped
4 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
¼ tsp. spicy paprika
3 tbsp. olive oil
2-3 cups boiled water
½ tsp. marjoram
Thyme
Basil
Salt

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a saucepan. Add the onion, garlic and  thyme and cook over a medium-low heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes, stirring.
Add tomatoes ( I used organic sweet tomatoes), marjoram, paprika, salt and cook for 3 minutes, then stir in the water and cook for another 2 minutes.
Stir in the noodles and cook. Check the label on the noodle package for cooking times. Transfer to bowls, garnish with basil and parmesan cheese and serve immediately.
