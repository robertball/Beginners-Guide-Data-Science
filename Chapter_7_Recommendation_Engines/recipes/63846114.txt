Title:
Buffalo Blue Cheese Burger Casserole. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups crumbled blue cheese,divided
1 (16 ounce)package uncooked pasta shells
1 pound ground beef
3/4 cup chopped red onion
1 cup wing sauce
3/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon seasoned pepper
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
8 slices white american cheese
1 3/4 cups chunky blue cheese dressing
3/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.Sprinkle with 1 cup crumbled blue cheese over the baking pan.
Cook shell pasta according to package directions.
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.Cook and stir ground beef and onion until browned.Drain off excess grease.Stir in wing sauce,oregano,crushed red flakes,garlic powder,seasoned pepper,onion powder,cooked pasta and sliced white american cheese.Pour into prepared baking pan,spoon with  blue cheese dressing over beef/pasta mixture and cover with aluminum foil.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.Remove the casserole from the oven and sprinkle with remaining crumbled blue cheese and shredded parmesan cheese.Return to the oven and bake until the cheeses melts,about 5 minutes.
