Title:
AMAZING! White chocolate chip pistachio cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036663.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of butter (melted)
3/4 cups of brown sugar
3/4 cups of granulated sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups of flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp of salt
1 cup pistachios (pealed and crushed)
1/2 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Line cookie sheets with parchment paper
2. Cream together butter and sugars in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time beating after each addition and stir in the vanilla extract.
3. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking soda and salt and gradually stir it in the mixture.
4. Fold in the pistachios and white chocolate chips
5. Drop spoonfuls onto the cookie sheets.
6. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Let cool
7. ENJOY =)
