Title:
Walnut Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) unbaked pie shell
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, divided
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  In small mixing bowl, combine nuts, sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon; sprinkle evenly over bottom of pastry shell. In large bowl, combine pumpkin, EAGLE BRAND(R), eggs, remaining 1 teaspoon cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and ginger; mix well.  Pour filling over nut mixture in pie shell.
Bake for 15 minutes.  Reduce oven to 350 degrees F; continue baking 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted 1 inch from edge comes out clean. Cool.  Garnish as desired.  Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This delicious pumpkin pie has a layer of spiced walnuts on the bottom to add intrigue. Top servings with a dollop of whipped cream, and enjoy this fall classic.
