Title:
Adventure Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. (1 stick) butter
3/4 c. sugar
1 Tbls. milk
1 c. chopped dates
1 egg, well beaten
1 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. salt
2 c. Rice Krispies cereal
1/2 c. walnuts, chopped
1 pkg. shredded coconut (like Angel Flake)

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the butter, sugar, milk and dates and cook over medium low heat until thick. Set aside to cool.
Once the date mixture has cooled, add the egg, vanilla and salt. Stir to mix.
Add the Rice Krispies and nuts; mix very well.
Shape into balls and roll in the coconut.

Submitter: 
Jacki_In_Simi
Description: 
This is another recipe that mom had and made for us frequently, especially in the summer because they are a 'no-bake' cookie and freeze well. We'd eat them straight out of the freezer and, man, were they good!
