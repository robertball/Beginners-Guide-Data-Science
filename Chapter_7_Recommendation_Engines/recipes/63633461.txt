Title:
Caesar-Style Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 TB dijon-style mustard
2 TB Worcestershire sauce
2 TB lemon juice
2 TB pepper
1 TB Penzy's Italian Vinegar and Oil Salad Base
1/4 cup  freshly-grated Parmesan cheese
3 cloves fresh garlic, minced (or 1/4 tsp minced garlic rehydrated in 1 TB water)

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients together. Blend well.
Toss with bite size pieces of romaine lettuce.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This is a great egg-free Caesar dressing!
