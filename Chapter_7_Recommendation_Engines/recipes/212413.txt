Title:
Mocha Soymilk Frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989429.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup warm water
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa or carob powder
2 teaspoons powdered instant coffee
1 packet Stevia Extract In The Raw®
1/2 cup light vanilla soymilk
2 cups double-churned vanilla ice cream or soy non-dairy frozen dessert

Directions:
Place water, cocoa, coffee and Stevia Extract In The Raw in the blender. Cover and process briefly on low speed.
Add soymilk, cover and process on high speed until well blended. Add ice cream, cover and process until smooth. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Stevia Extract In The Raw®
Description: 
This refreshing ‘pick-me-up' drink saves calories and carbs by using Stevia.
