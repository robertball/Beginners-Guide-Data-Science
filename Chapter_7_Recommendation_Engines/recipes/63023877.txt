Title:
Chive-Goat Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Goat Cheese (room temp)
4 oz Cream Cheese (room temp)
6 tbsp fresh chives (minced)
1 clove garlic (pressed)
12 whole chives
1 baguette sliced

Directions:
Combine cheeses, minced chives, and garlic. Mix with fork.
On a flat surface, spread plastic wrap.
Arrange whole chives in criss-cross pattern atop center of plastic.
Spoon cheese mixture over and folk into 4-5 inch square.
Lift edges of plastic, wrapping chives around completely.
Refridgerate overnight.
