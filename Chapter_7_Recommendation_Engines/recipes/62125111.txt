Title:
Chicken Chili Sausage Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground chicken sausage (hot or mild)
1 egg
1/2 c chili sauce
1/2 c sour cream
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
1 T worsteshire sauce
1 c crushed tortilla chips
1/4 c finely chopped cilantro
dash of chili powder
2 T mustard
2 T brown sugar
1/2 c ketchup
2 T water
1 t chili powder

Directions:
Mix ground sausage, egg, chili sauce, sour cream, onion, worsteshire sauce, tortilla chips, cilantro, and chili powder in a large bowl.  Form into loaf in a sprayed 13x9 pan.  Bake uncovered at 350 for 45 minutes.
Pour mixture of mustard, ketchup, brown sugar, chili powder on loaf and bake for 15-20 minutes more.

Meatloaf made with spicy chicken sausage and hints of chili.
