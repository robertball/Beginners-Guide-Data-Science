Title:
Creamy keto bacon hamburger instant pot soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5865007.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces lean ground beef
8 ounces organic pork sausage
2 strips thick cut bacon
1/2 cup diced onion
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp gulden’s spicy brown mustard
1 cup water
1/2 cup chopped, fire roasted tomatoes
1 cup whipping cream
4 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, grated
2 ounces cream cheese
1/2 tsp dried minced garlic
1/2 tsp savory
2 tsp McCormick Montreal steak seasoning

Directions:
Set instant pot to sauté mode. Add butter and sauté onions and bacon. Add sausage and ground beef. Stir and break up the meat until crumbly and cooked throughout. Add tomatoes, water, Worcestershire sauce, and mustard. Add the whipping cream, cream cheese, and cheddar cheese. Stir to incorporate until cheese is melted. Add the remaining seasonings, stir to combine.
Garnish each serving with tomatoes, bacon, cheese, and onions if desired.
