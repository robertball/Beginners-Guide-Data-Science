Title:
Flank steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4-5 lb. flank steak
1 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 Tbsp. onion flakes
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/2 cup white wine

Directions:
Diagonally score both sides of flank steak.
Mix together soy sauce, lemon juice, onion flakes, Italian seasoning, and white wine in a large ziplock bag or marinade container.
Marinate meat for 2-4 hours in the refrigerator.
Preheat oven broiler to high.
Broil flank steak for 5 minutes on each side or until meat is medium-rare on an ungreased broiling pan.
Slice thin against the grain and serve.
