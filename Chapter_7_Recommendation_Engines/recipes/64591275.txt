Title:
KC's Fried Kingfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3790420.jpg
Ingredients:
-Fresh kingfish cut into chunks
-Italian dressing
-Package or box of fish fry (I like Zatarains brand)
-Vegetable oil

Directions:
-Let kingfish pieces marinate in Italian dressing for 3-4 hours.
-Heat oil to 375 degrees.
-Cover fish pieces in the fish fry and place in oil.
-Fry until golden color (usually takes less than 2 minutes).
-Enjoy!!!!!!!!!

Submitter: 
Kevin...lost
Description: 
We often catch kings when we troll around the rigs here in the Gulf and this is an excellent and easy way to cook them.
