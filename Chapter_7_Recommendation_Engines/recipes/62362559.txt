Title:
Strawberry White Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/405316.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 18.25oz Box Strawberry Cake Mix (ex:Pillsbury)
1/2 Cup Oil
2 Eggs
3/4 Cup White Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix cake mix, oil and egg together.  Add chips to mixture and mix again.  Form balls about 3/4 inch and place them onto baking sheet.
Place cookies into oven for about 10-12 minutes watching them closely so they dont burn.
Remove and cool about 5-10 minutes.  Then enjoy or place in airtight container or bag for later time.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
I came up with this recipe when I had a box of strawberry cake mix to use up and we didnt want cake.  My kids love these simple cookies.  They are quick and easy to make.
