Title:
Peppered cold cuts sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. of thinly sliced peppered turkey breast
1/2 lb. of thinly sliced pepperoni
1/2 lb. of thinly sliced peppered monterey jack cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon basil
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
large loaf of fresh Italian or French  bread

Directions:
1.  Mix the mayonnaise, basil, oregano,  and pepper in a bowl and set aside.
2.  Slice the loaf of bread lengthwise and open it up.
3.  Spread the mayonnaise mixture on both sides of the bread.
4.  Layer the cheese, turkey and pepperoni on the bread.
5.  Close the bread and slice it up.

Submitter: 
LISATEACHER
Description: 
A collection of peppered cold cuts make this sandwich spicy and delicious.  Try it and you won't be disappointed.
