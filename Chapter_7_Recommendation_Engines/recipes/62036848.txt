Title:
Strawberry Jiffy Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. washed and hulled strawberries (frozen strawberries may be substituted)
1 C. cold water
1 4-oz. package sugar-free strawberry flavored gelatin

Directions:
Crush strawberries in a large saucepan, add water and gelatin, mixing well.  Heat mixture to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly. Lower heat, simmer for 2 minutes.  Pour into jars, cool, then cover.  This jam keeps well in refrigerator about a week, or frozen for several weeks. 3 calories per tbsp.

Submitter: 
Bobby Darapureddi
Description: 
This jam is not only easy and flavorful, it's also sugar free.
