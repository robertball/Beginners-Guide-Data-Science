Title:
Apple-Stuffed Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium acorn squash
1 cup sliced, peeled apple
4 teaspoons brown sugar
2 teaspoons butter (melted)
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Cut squash in half; discard seeds. Place squash cut side down in a lightly gressed baking pan. Bake at 350 F for 30 min.
Mix together peeled sliced apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves with the 2 teaspoons of melted butter. Coat apples.
Remove squash from oven and turn squash over.
Fill centers of the squash with apple mixture.
Cover and bake at 400 degrees F for 30 minutes.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with a little dash nutmeg.
