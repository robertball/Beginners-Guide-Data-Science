Title:
Jim's Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/415637.jpg
Ingredients:
5- 6oz cans white tuna
8- Tablespoons Miracle Whip
1/4- teaspoon Hot sauce
1/8- teaspoon garlic powder
1- teaspoon dill seed
1- tablespoon parsley
1/4- teaspoon curry powder
1/4- teaspoon onion flakes
2- tablespoons pickle relish
2- tablespoons parmesean cheese
2- stalks celery diced

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors meld! Enjoy!
