Title:
Chicken, Peppers and Rice Skillet Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 1/2 cups rice
1 cup chopped onion
3 or 4 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/4 tsp chili powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/4 tsp pepper
1 bay leaf
1 red pepper, cut into strips
1 green pepper, cut into strips
2 green onions, chopped
1 tsp parsley
1/2 tsp each basil and thyme
2 tbsp. butter
1 cup either frozen peas or corn
1 pound chicken breasts

Directions:
1. In large skillet or Dutch oven, bring broth, rice, onion, garlic, chili powder, salt and pepper, and bay leaf to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is tender.  Discard bay leaf.
2. Meanwhile is skillet over medium high heat, saute the peppers, green onions, parsley, basil and thyme in butter for until peppers are crisp/tender.  Add the frozen vegetables and heat through.  Toss this in on top of the rice and cover.
3. Stir chicken in that skillet with a bit more butter or olive oil until no longer pink.
4. Add to rice mixture, stir and serve.
