Title:
KC's  Slow Cooker Beef Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-4lbs. beef stew meat
-2 cans of cream of mushroom soup
-4 packs of brown gravy mix (.8 ounce packs)
-2 small onions (diced)
-2 beef bullion cubes
-2 cups water
-white rice

Directions:
-dice onions then throw in crockpot
-throw meat, soup, gravy mix, bullion cubes, and water in crockpot
-mix well
-cover and cook on low for 7 or 8 hours
-serve over rice
