Title:
Irish Creamy Cafe Amish Friendship Bread Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769190.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Amish Friendship Bread Starter
3 eggs
1 cup oil
1/2 cup half and half
1 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
2 small (4 ounce) boxes french vanilla instant pudding
4 tablespoons International Cafe Irish Cream mix
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1 can (12 ounce) of whipped cream cheese frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325° F (165° C).
In a large mixing bowl, add all the ingredients and mix well.
Grease muffin tins or put in cupcake liners.
Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Let cupcakes cool and top with cream cheese frosting.
ENJOY!
