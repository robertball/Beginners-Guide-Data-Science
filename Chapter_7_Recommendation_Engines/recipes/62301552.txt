Title:
Maitre D Hotel Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter softened and divided
1 tbsp minced shallots
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp chopped rosemary
2 tbsp chopped basil
1 tbsp chopped tarragon
1 tsp chopped oregano
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp lemon zest
pepper and garlic salt to taste

Directions:
In a saute pan, melt 1 tbsp softened butter over medium high heat.  Add shallots and minced garlic and saute 3-5 min.  Add herbs and saute 2 min. Pour contents of skillet including butter into a bowl and allow to cool.  Mix remaining butter into herb mixture and blend well.  Season to taste with pepper salt and granulated garlic.  Allow butter to remain soft until ready to serve.  Do not melt.  After serving, you may wish to place any remaining butter in the center of a 12" square of plastic wrap and roll into a cigar shape, tightly squeesing the end of wrap to seal well.  Place in refrigerator for later use.  It's perfect for steaks or seafood.
