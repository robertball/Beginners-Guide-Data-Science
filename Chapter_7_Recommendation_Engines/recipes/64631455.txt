Title:
KK Cornbread Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040811.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 1/40z) can of whole kernel corn, drained
1 (14 3/4oz) can cream style corn
1 8 oz package of Jiffy corn muffin mix
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup butter, melted
1-1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease a 9x9 baking pan or 2 qt casserole dish
Mix together the corn, Jiffy, sour cream and butter in a large bowl.
Stir in the cheese.  Pour into the prepared ban. Bake for 55 mins , or
until golden brown and set. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
