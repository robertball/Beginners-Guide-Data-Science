Title:
Southwestern Winter vegetable soup with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 onion, chopped
3/4 cup shredded carrots
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 head cauliflower, coarsely chopped
2 cans chicken broth
3 sliced potatoes
1 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 teas dill weed
2 teas salt
1/2 teas black pepper
1 table spoon chicken soup stck
1 small head of brocolli
8 oz of cooked ham cubes
Shredded cheddar or cheese of your choice

Directions:
In a large pan over medium heat, saute the celery, onion and carrot in olive oil for 5 minutes, or until onion is translucent.
Add chickn broth, potatoes, chopped cauliflower, curry powder, dill weed, chicken stock, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minuets. Add water if need and brocolli stalks and simmer 5 more minutes. Add brocolli heads and simmer 3 minutes.
Use stick bender to cream soup. Add ham and warm. If thin, add instant mashed potatoes or shredded cheese
Serve with cheese.
