Title:
Pumpkin Seed Pesto with Basil and Parsley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 c pumpkin seeds (raw, sprouted or combination)
6 garlic cloves, chopped or smashed with salt rubbed in
1/2 to 3/4 c olive oil
3-4 oz basil
1 c (large handful) parsley
juice from 1/2 lemon
1/2 T sea salt (to taste)
1/2 to 3/4 c pasta water
parm cheese to taste

Directions:
mix all ingredients in blender, depending on blender --
grind/chop pumpkin seeds
add basil, parsley, garlic, lemon, salt and pulse, add olive oil and pasta water to desired consistency
add parm cheese if desired
pulse to combine all
