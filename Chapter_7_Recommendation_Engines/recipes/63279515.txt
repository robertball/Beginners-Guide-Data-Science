Title:
Chickpea and Tomato Stirfry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Tomatoes cut into one inch chunks
1 Can (15oz) Chickpeas
3 cloves of garlic, chopped
2 Tbs Olive Oil
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat Olive oil in skillet until a drop of water fizzles when dripped on pan.
Add garlic and saute about 30 seconds.
Add tomato and chickpeas.  Saute until tomato is desired consistency (about 5 min).
Salt to taste

Submitter: 
venymae
Description: 
Fast, easy, tasty, healthy and hearty. With 5 ingredients and a total of 15 minutes maximum prep AND cook time this dish is good as a side or main dish any time. (and it's vegan!)
