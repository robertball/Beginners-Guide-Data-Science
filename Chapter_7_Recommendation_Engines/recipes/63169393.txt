Title:
boiled eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
eggs

Directions:
put eggs in pan, cover with water one inch above eggs,bring to rapid boil and cover and turn heat off.let stand 14 to 17 minutes. dump hot water from pan and add cold water. after cooled finely crack eggs and let sit in pan for 1 hour for easy peel.
