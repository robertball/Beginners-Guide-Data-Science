Title:
My Chewy Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c any granola
1/4 c honey
1/8 c chopped peanuts, other nuts, or chocolate chips
1 egg

Directions:
Combine all ingredients well and press into a greased 8x8 baking pan, about 1/2 inch thick.  Bake at 350' for 20 minutes.  Slice into bars after it cools.
