Title:
Snickerdoodle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Granulated Sugar, Divided
1 Cup Land-O-Lakes Butter
2 Eggs
2 3/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
3/4 Teaspoon Crisco Shortening
3/4 Teaspoons Baking Soda
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
2 Tablespoons Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 325.
In mixing bowl, Beat 1 1/2 cup sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
Blend in eggs, scrape down bowl.
Add four, shortening, baking soda and salt, blend well.
Roll mixture into 1" balls, then roll in 2 tablespoons cinnamon and 1/2 cup sugar combined.
Place on a parchment lined or nonstick cookie sheet.
Bake at 325 degrees for approximately 10 minutes, cool on a wire rack.
