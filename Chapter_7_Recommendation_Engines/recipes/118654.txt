Title:
Veggie Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984552.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound hard vegetables, sliced into sticks (carrots, red peppers, zucchini, green beans, asparagus)
1 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® dressing, plus extra for dipping
5 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Blanch vegetables in salted boiling water for 3 minutes to soften.
Mix Ranch, olive oil and lemon juice. Pour mixture over vegetables to coat.  Marinate for 1 to 3 hours.
Put 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large frying pan. Fry vegetables over medium heat until cooked through and brown on all sides. Serve with Ranch for dipping.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
'Vegetable fries' served with creamy Ranch dipping sauce are a terrific alternative to potato fries.
