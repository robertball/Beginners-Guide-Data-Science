Title:
Jamie's Sweet and Easy Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 corn on the cob
1 Tb lemon juice (or squeeze all juice from 1/2 lemon)
2 Tbs sugar

Directions:
Fill a large stock pot 3/4 of the way with water. Add lemon juice and sugar and heat on high. When water boils, place corn in water and turn heat off. Cover. Leave stock pot on burner. Let sit for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
heatherjane
Description: 
My mother-in-law gave me this quick and easy recipe for the sweetest, tastiest corn cobs!
