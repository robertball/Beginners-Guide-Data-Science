Title:
Karen's Lemon Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion chopped
2 Tablespoons butter
1 pound zucchini
1 clove garlic
dash of oregano
dill to taste
Salt and pepper
1 Tablespoon fresh lemon juice or to taste

Directions:
Chop the onion into 1/2 inch pieces
Melt butter in pan
Sauté the onion for 3 - 5 minutes
Slice the zucchini into 1/4 - 1/2 inch rounds
Add zucchini, garlic, oregano , dill, salt and pepper to onions
Sauté' until tender
Add lemon juice and serve

Submitter: 
Pickles
Description: 
Sautéed zucchini and onions with dill, garlic and a splash of fresh lemon
