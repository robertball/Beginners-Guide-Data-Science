Title:
Spinach Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3201244.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch spinach, 1 carrot chopped into small slices, 1/2 white onion chopped small, 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 can (14.5oz) diced tomatoes, 1 can (14.5oz) white beans, 1 can (14.5oz) sweet peas, 3 medium garlic cloves chopped small, 10 cups of water, 2 teaspoons salt, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1/2 teaspoon paprika, 1/3 cup white vinegar, 5 ounces Fideo egg noodles, 2 tablespoons of chicken bouillon

Directions:
in a large pot heat olive oil
add garlic, onion and carrot and saute for 2 minutes
add tomatoes and saute for another 2 minutes
add water, chicken stock, beans, peas and all spices and cook until water boils
add noodles and vinegar
when it boils again reduce heat to medium and add spinach
soup will be ready in 5-10 minutes when the noodles are cooked to taste

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a quick and easy healthy soup that i can eat whenever i want to eat light and healthy. I can eat a lot of it to feel full without a lot of calories. I like to pair it with a chicken salad sandwich.
