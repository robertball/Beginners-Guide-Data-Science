Title:
Chocolate Chip Pudding Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
1 (4-serving size) package instant vanilla pudding
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon lemon juice
2 eggs
1 package chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
Cream the butter and sugars. Add vanilla, lemon juice and eggs, one at a time. Beat well.
Add pudding and mix well.
Add baking soda and mix well.
Gradually add flour, mixing between each addition.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop onto ungreased baking sheets and bake for 8-10 minutes.
Also good kept frozen for up to 3 months!

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
A fun twist on ordinary chocolate chip cookies. These scrumptious treats are extra moist and slightly heavy, leaving your sweet tooth completely satisfied!
