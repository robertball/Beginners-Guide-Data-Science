Title:
Baked Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoon virgin olive oil
1 large green onion, finely chopped
6 medium cloves garlic, finely minced
1 Pound ground veal
1 Pound ground sirloin
1 Pound ground pork
1 Cup grated Parmesan cheese
4 large eggs
1 Teaspoon salt
1/2 Teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
f possible, have the butcher grind together the three different meats. Be sure to wash your hands thoroughly after handling raw pork.
Heat oven to 375°F.
In a skillet, over high heat, cook onion 5 minutes, stirring frequently, until softened. Add garlic and cook 1 minute more.
Transfer to a bowl and mix in ground meats, cheese, eggs, salt, and pepper. Roll into golf ball-size meatballs. Place on a jelly roll pan.
Bake 20–25 minutes, until browned and cooked through.
