Title:
Vegan Royal Icing with Aquafaba Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup aquafaba (chickpea water)
4 cups confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place aquafaba in the bowl of a stand mixer; beat on high speed until foamy, about 1 minute. Add confectioners' sugar and vanilla extract; beat on medium speed until shiny and creamy, about 7 minutes.

Submitter: 
Fioa
Description: 
A simple vegan royal icing made with aquafaba instead of egg whites. It's so tasty and works really well to decorate gingerbread houses and cookies, and dries shiny and hard. Make sure to store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator until ready to use.
