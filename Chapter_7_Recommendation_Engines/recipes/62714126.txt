Title:
One Minute Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10oz.) can diced Rotel tomatoes with green chiles, undrained - medium heat
1 (14.5 oz.) can stewed tomatoes, undrained

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender, process for 30 seconds.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Use a food processor or blender, with two cans on hand you'll have salsa.
