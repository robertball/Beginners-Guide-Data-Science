Title:
Four-Cheese Italian with Garlic Basil Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982083.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (19.76 ounce) package JohnsonvilleÂ® Four-Cheese Italian Sausage Links
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 tablespoon minced garlic
3 tablespoons olive oil
3/4 cup frozen peas
1 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 (14 ounce) can reduced-sodium chicken broth
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
Hot cooked angel hair pasta

Directions:
Cook sausage according to package directions; cut in half lengthwise and into thin slices.
In a skillet, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until tender. Add peas. Combine the cornstarch, broth and pepper until smooth; stir into onion mixture. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Stir in sausage and basil. Serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
The Kitchen at Johnsonville Sausage
Description: 
This simple Italian dish is a flavorful and cheesy take on an old favorite. Garlic and basil complement four-cheese sausage in a dish no one can resist.
