Title:
Matcha Coconut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3319927.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 cup frozen mango chunks
2 leaves kale, torn into several pieces
3 tablespoons white beans, drained
2 tablespoons unsweetened shredded coconut
1/2 teaspoon matcha green tea powder
1 cup water

Directions:
Combine banana, mango, kale, white beans, coconut, and matcha powder in a blender; add water. Blend mixture until very smooth.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
Matcha green tea powder and kale make this superfood smoothie extra green. White beans add a little extra protein too!
