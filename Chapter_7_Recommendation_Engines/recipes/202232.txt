Title:
Cuervo Tradicional Tamarindo Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991898.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Tradicional
3 ounces tamarindo syrup
1/2 cup crushed ice
1 tablespoon sugar
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Rub the rim of a rocks glass with lime and dip into sugar.
In cocktail shaker mix all ingredients.
Shake well and pour into rocks glass on the rocks.
Garnish with lime wedge.
