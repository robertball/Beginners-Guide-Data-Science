Title:
Slow Cooker Red Beans and Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2142652.jpg
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups water
14 ounces smoked sausage (such as Healthy Choice®), quartered lengthwise and cut into 1/4-inch pieces
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 red onion, chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
2 cubes chicken bouillon (such as Knorr®)
1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 cup long-grain rice

Directions:
Combine water, sausage, kidney beans, onion, celery, chicken bouillon, Cajun seasoning, garlic salt, and thyme in a slow cooker.
Cook on High for 4 hours. Add rice and cook until rice is tender, about 1 hour more.

Submitter: 
Peach822
Description: 
If you love the flavors of red beans and rice, combine the ingredients to make a filling and healthy soup!
