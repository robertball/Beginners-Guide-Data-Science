Title:
Mom's Potato Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large spuds, cut into cubes, peeled/not peeled
1 large box chicken broth
2 leeks, sliced white parts only
1 8-oz container sour cream
2 cups sharp white or yellow cheddar cheese, shredded
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
3 tablespoons butter
1 cup water

Directions:
In a large stockpot, boil potatoes in chicken broth and water.
In a skillet, saute leeks in butter until soft; transfer butter and leeks into pot with potatoes, water and stock.
Boil potatoes until just softened.
When potatoes are done, remove 2 cups of the potato mixture and puree, either in food processor or with a mixer.
Mix until smooth.
Return the puree back into the pot.
Add salt, pepper, sour cream and cheese.
Continually stir and simmer for approx 15 minutes.  Enjoy!!
