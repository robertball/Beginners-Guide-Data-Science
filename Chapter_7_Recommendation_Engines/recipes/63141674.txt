Title:
Ed's Macaroni Jumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/803309.jpg
Ingredients:
Elbow macaroni                     2 cups              500ml
Cooking oil                            2 tsp.               10ml
Lean ground beef                  1 lb.                 454g
Chopped onion                     1/3cup             75ml
Frozen kernel corn                1 1/2 cups       375ml
Can of Tomato sauce            7 1/2oz            213ml
Ketchup                                1/2 cup            125 ml
Water                                    1/2 cup           125 ml
Chili powder                 2 tsp            10 ml
Granulated sugar          1/2 tsp        2ml
Seasoned salt               1/2 tsp        2ml
Grated medium Cheddar 1/2 cup     125ml
Cheese

Directions:
Cook macaroni in boiling salted water in large uncovered saucepan for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender but firm.  Drain.  Set aside.
Heat cooking oil in large frying pan on medium.  Add ground beef and onion.  Scramble-fry for about 10 minutes until beef is no longer pink.  Drain.
Add the next 7 ingredients.  Heat and stir for about 1 minute until boiling.  Reduce heat to medium low.  Simmer uncovered for about 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.  Add macaroni.  Heat and stir for about 5 minutes until heated through.
Sprinkle with cheese.  Remove from heat.  Let stand for about one minute until cheese is melted.  Serves 4
