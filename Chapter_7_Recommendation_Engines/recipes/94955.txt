Title:
Halibut Dijonaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986123.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (6 ounce) fillets halibut
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/2 cup dried plain breadcrumbs, divided
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, divided
2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Adjust oven rack to lower-middle position and preheat oven to 400 degrees. Coat a foil-lined baking sheet with vegetable cooking spray. Arrange fish fillets on the prepared baking sheet.
Combine mayonnaise, mustard, horseradish and lemon juice in a small bowl. Stir in 1/4 cup bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon cheese. Spread mayonnaise mixture evenly over fish. Combine butter, remaining breadcrumbs and remaining cheese in a small bowl; sprinkle over fish.
Bake until cooked through, about 18 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
This mouthwatering baked halibut dish with a crusty parmesan cheese topping would be an elegant meal for guests.
