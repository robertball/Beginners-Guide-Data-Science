Title:
Raw Sprouted Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sprouted beans ("Organic Crunchy Sprouts" are fast and easy)
2 organic red bell peppers, chopped
2 organic ears of corn, kernals only
1 organic red onion, chopped
1/2 cup organic extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup coconut vinegar
1 organic lime, juiced
1 small organic lemon, juiced
3 tablespoons raw organic honey
1 tablespoon sea salt
1 clove organic garlic, crushed
1/4 cup fresh organic parsley, chopped
1/2 tablespoon organic ground cumin
1/2 tablespoon organic ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon organic cayenne

Directions:
1. In a large bowl, combine beans, bell peppers, corn, and onion.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, lime juice, lemon juice, honey, sea salt, garlic, parsely, cumin, black pepper, and cayenne.
3. Pour olive oil dressing over sprouts and veggies
4. Mix well.
5. Chill, and serve cold.
