Title:
Greek Chicken and Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 to 5 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into large pieces
2 cups tiny red potatoes, cut in half
2 cups baby cut carrots
1 onion, cut into big pieces
1 small basket cherry tomatoes
1 cup virgin olive oil
1 cup balsamic vinegar
2 Tbsp. Cavender's Greek Seasoning (or comparable)

Directions:
Lightly cook potatoes and carrots in a steamer until almost soft.  In a 9x13 pan place the chicken pieces, steamed vegetables, onion pieces, and tomatoes.  Pour olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the top.  Sprinkle on the Greek seasoning.  Place uncovered in the oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to an hour.

Submitter: 
drjwall
Description: 
This makes a delicious, yet easy Sunday dinner!  Serve with a rustic loaf of artisan bread for dipping!
