Title:
Auntie Em's Sweet and Spicy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup dried onions
1 15 ounce can Chili beans
1 15 ounce can dark red kidney beans
1 15 ounce can Pinto beans
4 Table spoons Chili powder (add more or less to taste)
2 LBS. Ground beef
1 Large can of Tomato juice
Salt and pepper (to taste)
1/8 cup brown sugar (more or less to taste)
1 small can chopped or diced tomatoes

Directions:
in a med.-large pot brown ground beef, once brown add in beans(Do not drain them), onion, tomato juice, tomatoes, salt and pepper, and chili powder. cook on low- medium heat for 30 minutes stirring every few minutes. Add brown sugar. then cook till desired temperature.
