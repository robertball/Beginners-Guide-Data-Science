Title:
Cinnamon and Sugar Popped Sorghum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2223426.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons popping sorghum
1 teaspoon canola oil
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Pour sorghum into a small brown paper bag and fold shut. Microwave on High until the majority of sorghum is popped,  1 to 1 1/2 minutes. Transfer popped sorghum into a bowl and drizzle with canola oil; sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. Toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
Popped sorghum is an earthy spin on popcorn. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar onto the freshly popped sorghum for a sweet treat.
