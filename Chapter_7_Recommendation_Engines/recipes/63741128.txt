Title:
Strawberry Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bananas
1 cups Strawberries, halved
1 cup ice
1 cup vanilla yogurt
1/4 cup 2% milk
1 tsp agave
a pinch of cinnamon

Directions:
Throw all the ingredients in the blender and blend until all are combined. Makes 6 8 ounce servings.
