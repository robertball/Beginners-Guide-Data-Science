Title:
Butter Spread for Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. garlic, chopped
2 C cheddar cheese, shredded
1 C parmesen cheese, shredded
2 lbs. butter, softened
4 C mayonnaise
1 Tbsp. seasoning mix

Directions:
Put butter and mayonnaise in a food processor and mix briefly.  Add seasoning, cheddar, parmesen, and garlic and mix until incorporated.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Melts right down into french bread, making a delicious and flavorful cheesy garlic bread.
