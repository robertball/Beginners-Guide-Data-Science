Title:
Grilled Turkey Pesto "Italiano" Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-lb Foccacia bread (un-sliced)
Basil pesto
6T olive oil
4 slices cheese such as, provolone, mozzarella or Swiss
Romaine lettuce leaves, washed and dried
Deli-sliced turkey breast, warmed

Directions:
Cut the foccacia bread in 4 even pieces then split them in half for a top and bottom.
Heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan; when hot add 4 halves of the bread, flat side down and grill until nicely browned.  Place on plates or a platter, spread some of the basil pesto on each then top the pesto with 1/2 slice of cheese.  Repeat with the remaining slices of bread.
Add turkey and lettuce to each and assemble the sandwiches.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
The pesto turns ordinary grilled turkey cheese sandwich into a gourmet sandwich so good you'll say it's delizioso.  You can choose your favorite cheese.  Any way it's awesome!
