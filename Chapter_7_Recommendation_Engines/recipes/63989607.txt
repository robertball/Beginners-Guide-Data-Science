Title:
Super Delicious Cinnamon Crumb Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2/3 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
In a large bowl, stir together the cake mix and instant pudding mix. Add the eggs, sour cream and oil, mix until well blended. In a small bowl combine the sugar, cinnamon and nuts.
Pour half of the batter into the prepared pan, then sprinkle with half of the cinnamon nut mixture. Pour the remaining batter over the top and repeat with the cinnamon nut mixture.
Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven. Cool for 1/2 hour before removing from pan to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
An easy cake mix cake with a beautiful layer of cinnamon in the middle. Serve warm with a scoop of ice cream.Modified from: Easy Apple Coffee Cake
