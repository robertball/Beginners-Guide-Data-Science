Title:
melted wtches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-teaspoon water
4-drops yellow food coloring
1&1/2 cups flaked coconut
2-cups(12 ounce) semisweet chocolate chips
6- table spoons shorting divided
36 cream filled sandwich cookies
36 bugles
4 cups vanilla or white chips
36 pretzel "sticks"

Directions:
In a large plastic bag add water and food coloring then coconut.Seal bag & shack to tint;set aside.In microwave melt chocolate chips & 2tables spoons shorting until smooth.
For witches hat place 1/3 cup chocolate mix in resealable bag;cut a small hole in a corner of bag.Pipe a small amount of chocolate on top of a cookie;dip bugle in some of the remaining chocolate ,allow excess to drip off,Position on cookie forming a hat. Set on wax paper to dry.Repeat with remaining cookies.
For brooms&puddles melt white chips&remaining shorting,stir til smooth.Place mixture in plastic bag,cut hole in one end,Pipe mix on to wax paper lined cookie sheet in the shape of a puddle,immediately place a hat on puddle.Place pretzel stick alongside hat sprinkle
coconut at the end of pretzel stick repeat with remaining hats .Let stand 15 minutes.Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Suzann Stephens
Description: 
you have a cookie &bugle; sitting on top of melted white chocolate that looks like a witch that melted.
