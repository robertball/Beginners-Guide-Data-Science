Title:
Rhonda's London Broil and Pineapple Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3lb flank steak
Marinade:
20oz can sliced pineapple drained(reserve juice)
1/2cup brown sugar
1/2cup soy sauce
1teas.dry mince garlic
2 Tabls. prepared mustard

Directions:
Place meat in a flat dish that will be used for marinading.
Put pineapple slices in a sealed container and refrigerate. You will need them at the grill!
Marinade: In a medium bowl wisk together pineapple juice, brown sugar, soy sauce, garlic and mustard.
Pour the marinade over the meat, cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
Heat the grill till hot. Remove meat from the marinade letting the excess marinade run off and place on the grill. Reduce heat.
Place the pineapple slices in the marinade for the steak and allow to soak while the meat is grilling.
Grill meat for about 10-12 min on each side till meat thermometer reads 145deg. for medium well to 160deg. for well done.
When the steak is done move to a cutting board and allow meat to rest for 15 minutes.
Slice in thin slices accross the grain.
While the meat is resting place each pineapple slice on the grill individually if you have enough room and grill till golden brown grill marks are seared into the surface of the rings.
Plate up the sliced steak and surround the meat with the grilled pineapple!

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is an economical way to have delicious steak for the budget minded yet is tasty enough to serve to company!
