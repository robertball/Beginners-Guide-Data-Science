Title:
Artichoke Vinnaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz. jars marinated artichoke hearts, drained
1/4 c. balsamic vinegar
2 T. chopped chive or green onion tops
1 T. fresh lemon juice
1 T. course grained Dijon mustard
2 tsp. honey
5 T. mayonnaise
1/4 c. extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Mince 1 jar artichoke hearts in processor and add balsamic vinegar, chives, lemon juice, mustard, and honey.
Transfer mix to a large bowl and add mayo; mix until smooth.
Gradually whisk in oil.
Cover and chill at least 30 min.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Delicious creamy dressing, fabulous if you want to make home-made.
