Title:
Cold Smoked Salmon Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986647.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
4 ounces smoked salmon, chopped
3 tablespoons finely chopped chives
1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
2 tablespoons capers
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine cream cheese with smoked salmon in medium mixing bowl. Add remaining ingredients. Spoon into small serving bowl or plate. Garnish with fresh dill and lemon peel, if desired. Chill and serve with Summer Town House(R) crackers.

Submitter: 
Keebler® Kitchens
Description: 
This simple, savory spread combines the classic flavors of smoked salmon, cream cheese, and dill. The ideal accompaniment for a platter of buttery Town House® crackers.
