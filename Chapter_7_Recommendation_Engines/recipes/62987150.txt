Title:
Cornstarch glaze for bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp cornstarch
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Mix the cornstarch with a few drops of water to form a paste then add the rest of the water and whisk until smooth
Cook until in the microwave until it comes to a boil and becomes glossy.
Allow to cool.
Brush on loaf, add seeds and bake as usual.
Store any leftover in a covered container for up to 2 weeks in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
This will give breads that smooth professional look and help seeds adhere.
