Title:
Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes, peeled, cut and boiled
1 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 pt. sour cream
1/4 cup margarine, melted
1/3 cup onion, finely chopped
1 can cream chicken soup
2 cups corn flakes

Directions:
Mash potatoes slightly and mix with remaining ingredients except corn flakes.  Spread into 9x13 baking pan.  Sprinkle corn flakes over top.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until hot.
