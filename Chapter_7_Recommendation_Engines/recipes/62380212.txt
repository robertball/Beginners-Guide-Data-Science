Title:
Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C Flour
Pinch of Salt
3 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 C Milk
2 T melted butter
1/2 t. vanilla

Directions:
Place liquids in blender and blend until well mixed.
Add dry ingredients and blend until creamy
Lightly grease skillet
Pour into skillet and cook 2 min. on one side.
Flip the crepe and cook 30 sec.-1min. on other side. Serve hot.
