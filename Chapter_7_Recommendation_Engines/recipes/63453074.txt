Title:
Quinoa Tabbouleh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa well rinsed and drained
2 cups water
Sea Salt
1/2 cup edamame
1 fresh chopped tomato
1/2 cucumber chopped
1 tbsp fresh lime juice
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
Fresh minced chives, parsley, thyme

Directions:
1. Quinoa, salt and water to a boil and simmer 20 minutes
2. Place edamame on top, sit for 5 minutes - fluff with fork
3. Put all ingredients in a glass serving bowl
