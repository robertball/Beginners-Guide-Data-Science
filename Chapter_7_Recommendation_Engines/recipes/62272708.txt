Title:
BLT French Bread Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of cooked bacon
1/2 cup of mayo
1 teaspoon of basil
1 teaspoon of garlic powder
1 teaspoon of onion powder
2 cups of shredded cheese
2 roma tomatoes chopped
1 cup of shredded lettuce
1 loaf of French Bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Mix mayo,basil,garlic, and onion together in small bowl.  Cut bread in half and place cut side up onto a cookie sheet.  Spread mayo mixture on the bread.  Top with crumbled up bacon and cheese.  Cook in preheat oven for 10-12 minutes until cheese is melted.  When pizza is done top with lettuce and tomatoes.
