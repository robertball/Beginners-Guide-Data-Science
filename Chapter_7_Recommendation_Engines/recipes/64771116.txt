Title:
Amy's Traditional Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7493406.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds Meatball mix (Ground beef, veal & pork)
2 eggs
1 Cup bread crumbs
1 Cup Parmesan or Romano grated cheese
A lot of garlic.
A lot of basil.
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Mince garlic
Chop basil
In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs.
Add the breadcrumbs, cheese, basil and garlic.
Mix to combine.
Add meat.
Combine well but do not overmix.
Bake on a lightly greased cookie sheet for 20 minutes if you'll be finishing them in the sauce.
Bake longer if you will not be cooking in the sauce.

Submitter: 
Amy Standaert
Description: 
This recipe is how my mom taught me. It's not hugely different from my own meatball recipe but it is just another version that is equally delicious.
