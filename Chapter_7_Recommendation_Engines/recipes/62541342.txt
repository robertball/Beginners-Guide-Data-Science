Title:
SCD Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. almond flour
4 eggs
4 Tbl honey
2 Tbl scd vanilla
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 Tbl butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in the blender and fry in skillet on low heat. Takes a little longer than a regular pancake.

Submitter: 
lovetoeat
Description: 
These are delicious pancakes. I topped mine with some candy that I had made that was too sweet to eat alone. It was made with mac nut butter, honey and pecans. The combination was perfect.
