Title:
BruenKager (Piffinuese) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup molasses
1 cup granulated sugar
1 egg
3 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1.  Cream together first 4 ingredients.
2.  Mix in the egg.
3.  Add flour and baking soda and mix well to form a nice dough.  Chill for 3 hours or overnight.
4.  Heat oven to 275 degrees.
5.  Cookies can be rolled out thin and cut into shapes for decorating and baking.  They can also be rolled into small snakes and cut into little "nuggets" and baked.
6.  Bake on cookie sheets for 23 minutes.
