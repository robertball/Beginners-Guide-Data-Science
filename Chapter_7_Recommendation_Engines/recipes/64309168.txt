Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1/4 cup green pepper, chopped
1 Tbsp oil
4 cups cooked rice
1 cup tomato sauce
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Saute onion and green pepper in 1 Tbsp oil until tender.
Add rice, tomato sauce, Worchestershire sauce, salt and pepper.  Simmer for 15 minutes.

Barbara Bartz

A nice side dish with Mexican meals.
