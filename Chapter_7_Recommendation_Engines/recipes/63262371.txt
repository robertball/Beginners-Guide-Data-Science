Title:
Butter Pecan Ice cream, Pumpkin pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt butter pecan ice cream (extra yummy if you make it)
9 in gingersnap crust-baked and cooled
1 can pumpkin
1/2c sugar
1/4t cinnamon
1/4t ginger
1/4t nutmeg
1c whipped cream-real is better

Directions:
Spread ice-cream in pie crust, freeze 2 hours.
Combine pumpkin with spices, fold in whipped cream. Spread on ice-cream, freeze 2 hours. Top with your choice and warm up for 15 min.
