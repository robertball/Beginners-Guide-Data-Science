Title:
Boerenjongens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Golden Raisins
1/2 cup dark raisins
2 cup of sugar
3 cups of water
1 cup of Brandy
1 cinnamon stick

Directions:
Add the raisins and heat with  the sugar along  with 3 cups of water and bring to a boil.
Simmer for 2 minutes and cool.
Add the raisins to a glass jar.
Mix the cooled sugary syrup, with the brandy, stir.
Pour over the raisins.
If there isn't enough liquid to cover the raisins, make another batch of the syrup.
Add the cinnamon stick.
Cover, and store in a cool dark place for 4 weeks
