Title:
Healthy Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15-ounce can chick peas, drained and rinsed
2 eggs
1 small onion, quartered
1 15-ounce fat-free ricotta cheese
1/4 cup dried parsley
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
30 jumbo pasta shells
1 32-ounce jar marinara sauce
2 cups shredded Italian cheese blend

Directions:
Process chickpeas and eggs until smooth.  Add the next six ingredients and process until onion is uniformly and finely chopped.  Cook shells according to package directions, making sure they do not get too soft.  Drain shells with cold water.  Spread 1 cup of the sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 glass baking dish.  Spoon about 2 T of the chickpea mixture into each shell.  Arrange in dish in a single layer, split sides up.  Top with remaining sauce.  Bake for 30 minutes at 350.  Remove from oven and top with cheese.  Let stand for about 10 minutes before serving.
