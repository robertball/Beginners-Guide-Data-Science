Title:
Tofu Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb firm tofu, diced
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
pepper, to taste
salt, to taste
handful, dried parsley flakes
1 1/2 tbsp curry powder
1 frozen deep dish pie shell or 2 regular shells
2 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Sautee veggies and spices in frying pan with a little vegetable oil.
Place in frozen pie shell.
Pour beaten eggs over pies.  Brush edges of shell with any remaining egg in bowl.
Bake in preheated 400 degree oven for 15-20 mins or until golden.

Submitter: 
MICHELLE_F
Description: 
This is a recipe from Argentina.  Anybody who has ever said yuk to tofu has been surprised by this recipe.  Remember tofu takes on the flavour of what it's cooked with...you don't have to be a vegeatarian to enjoy this recipe.  Some have described to taste like quiche!
