Title:
Chicken scarpariello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/12 lb chicken pieces
1 lb hot Italian sausage cut into 1" pieces
1/2 cup olive oil
1 large onion thinly sliced
1/4 cup hot slice cherry peppers
5 cloves garlic thinly sliced
1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
1/4 c of white wine
1/2 cup of chicken broth 1/2 cup fresh parsley chopped

Directions:
Wash and pat dry chicken. Cut into serving pieces. Season with salt and pepper. Heat 4TBS olive oil in skillet over med heat for 1 min until hot add the chicken skin side down until brown. keep warm on platter.using the same skillet add the sausage until brown. remove and place on platter with chicken.
Pour off fat from skillet and add the remaining oil and add onions until well browned, add cherry peppers and stir to combine. Add garlic cook 2mins until golden. Add lemon juice wine and allow both to reduce by half before adding the chicken broth. when broth simmers add chicken and sausage back to pan. cook about 12 mins until done. Season with salt and pepper for taste. serve over noodles.
