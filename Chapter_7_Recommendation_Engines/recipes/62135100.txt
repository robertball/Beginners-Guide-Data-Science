Title:
Tender Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Flank steak (whole)
1 Tbsp chopped garlic
4 Tbsp Soy sauce
4 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Place whole piece of steak into a large ziplock bag or marinating container.
Add garlic, soy and worcestershire sauce.
You may want to add a pinch of salt if you are using light soy sauce.
Let marinate over night.
Grill on med med/high bbq for 7 min each side for medium cooked steak.
Let rest for 5 min.
Slice against grain.
Serve!

Submitter: 
Renee Manz
Description: 
Marinated flank steak cooked on the Bbq.  The most tender and flavourful steak!!
