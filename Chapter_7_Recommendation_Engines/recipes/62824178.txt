Title:
Chicken Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breasts, cooked and chopped
Oil
1 Onion, diced
2 Bell Peppers, diced
1 Zucchini or Yellow Squash, diced
1 pkg. Fajita Mix
Water
Flour Tortillas

Directions:
Saute onions, pepper and zucchini till tender.  Add chicken and seasoning and water as package directs.  Simmer 10-15 minutes.
