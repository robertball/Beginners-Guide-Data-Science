Title:
Best Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tillapia filets, thawed, halved and trimmed to 4" by 1-2" sections
Enough bread crumbs to coat fish - season crumbs with plenty of chipolte pepper and cayenne and a pinch of salt.
Olive oil 1/4" deep in a non-stick skillet.
Coat fish and fry in hot oil until browned (about 3-4 mins per side)
Place fish on a warm tortilla and add 2-3 Tbs grated cheese, lettuce, salsa and sour cream. Taco Bell Ranchero sauce is recommended!
