Title:
Black Label Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976918.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Johnnie Walker® Black Label® blended Scotch whisky
1 ounce fresh lime juice
2 ounces ginger ale
1/2 ounce simple syrup
1 tablespoon sour mix
1 twist lime

Directions:
Pour Johnnie Walker(R) Black Label(R) blended Scotch whisky, fresh lime juice, ginger ale, simple syrup, and sour mix into a shaker with ice. Shake and serve over ice in a rocks glass; garnish with a lime twist.
