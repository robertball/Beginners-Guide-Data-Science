Title:
Mexican Chicken Corn Chowder 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
3 tablespoons butter
2 cubes  chicken bouillon
2 cups hot water
3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon leaf oregano
1 cup canned milk
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 (14.75 ounce) can cream-style corn
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 dash hot pepper sauce
1 tomato, chopped
fresh cilantro sprigs, for garnish (optional)

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, brown chicken, onion, and garlic in butter until chicken is no longer pink.
Dissolve the bouillon in hot water; Pour into Dutch oven, and season with cumin & oregano. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 5 minutes.
Stir in canned milk, cheese, corn, chilies, and hot pepper sauce. Cook, stirring frequently, until the cheese is melted. Stir in chopped tomato. Garnish with cilantro.

Description: 
This creamy chicken and corn chowder, made spicy with ground cumin, oregano, green chilies, and hot pepper sauce, will warm you right through.  Shredded Monterey jack cheese and canned milk make this soup rich and delicious.Modified from: Mexican Chicken Corn Chowder
