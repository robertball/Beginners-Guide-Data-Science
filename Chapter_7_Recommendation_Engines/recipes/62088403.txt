Title:
Favorit Country Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups of diced peeled potatoes
1 cup of chopped onion
1 1/2 cups diced celery
6 chicken buillion cubes
3 cups of water
3 cups of sour cream or Chivo
6T of flour or cornstarch
1 1/2 tsp salt
6 cups of milk

Directions:
In a large pot combine water, chicken buillion cubes, potatoes, onion and celery and the salt, if desired. Cook until veggies are tender, about 20 minutes. Add 3 cups of milk to the pot and bring back to hot temperature. In a medium bowl, gradually combine sour cream (or Chivo)cornstarch or flour, and another 3 cups of milk. When the mixture in the pot is hot again, gradually stir the sour cream mixture into it and then cook over low heat until as thick as desired.

Submitter: 
GENELLEB
Description: 
A thick, creamy potato soup, perfect for vegetarian diets, or those who must go meat-free on Fridays for Lent. We've usually eaten this soup during Lent.
