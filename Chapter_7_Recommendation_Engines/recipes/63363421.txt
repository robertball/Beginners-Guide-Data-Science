Title:
Fresh Fruit Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tablespoons butter
2 1/2 cups fruit
1 1/4 cups sugar, divided
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Prepare fruit by peeling and slicing.
Preheat oven to 350*.
Put butter in 1 1/2 quart baking dish and place it in oven to melt.  Remove when melted.
Combine fruit with 3/4 cup of the sugar and the cinnamon.
Mix together flour, salt, baking powder and remaining sugar.
Add milk and vanilla to dry ingredients, mixing well.
Pour over melted butter.  Do not stir.
Top with fruit.  Don't stir this, either.
Bake 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Use any fresh fruit in season and follow directions as written.  Serve hot with ice cream or cold with whipped cream.
