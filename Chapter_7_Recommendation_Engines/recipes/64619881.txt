Title:
Sour Cream Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
1/2 lb (2 sticks) butter
1 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
3 cups flour
6 large eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
In a large mixing bowl, using mixer combine and cream the butter and sugar until pale and fluffy. Add the sour cream.
Sift the baking soda and flour together.
Mix in the sifted flour to the creamed mixture alternating with eggs, beating each egg one at a time by hand.
Add the vanilla and pour the mixture into a greased and floured 10- inch tube pan.
Bake for 1 hour 20 minutes.
