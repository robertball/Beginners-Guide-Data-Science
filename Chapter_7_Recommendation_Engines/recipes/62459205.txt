Title:
Chicken Curry Pineapple Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boneless Chicken Breasts (cut in bite-sized pieces)
1 Tablespoon Minced Garlic
1 Â½ Cups Long grain Rice
3 Cups Water
Olive Oil
1 Green Pepper, cut into strips
1 Red Pepper, cut into strips
1 Large Can Chunk Pineapple and juice
2 teaspoons Curry
1 Tablespoon Cornstarch
2 Tablespoons Water
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut Chicken into bite sized pieces.
Cook rice in 3 cups of water until done and tender to bite.
Place enough olive oil in the bottom of a wok or large pot to stir fry.  Add chicken and garlic and cook until chicken is done.  Add pepper strips and stir fry until tender crisp.  Add pineapple chunks and juice, curry powder and salt and pepper to taste.  Heat all through thoroughly and bring to a slow boil.
Mix cornstarch with cold water until dissolved.  Slowly add the cornstarch to the stir-fry allowing it to come to another slow boil and thickening.
Serve over steamed rice.
