Title:
Best Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 cup light corn syrup
1 cup sugar
3 lg eggs, beaten
1/2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
1 dash of salt (I omitted)
1 cup chopped pecans
9" unbaked pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Brown butter in saucepan until it is golden, but do not scorch.  Let cool.
In a separate bowl, mix syrup and sugar, then add eggs, lemon juice, vanilla and salt.
Stir in pecans.
Blend in browned butter and mix well.
Pour in unbaked pie shell and cover edges of crust with foil.
Bake at 425 for 10 minutes.
Lower temperature to 325 and bake for 45 minutes more.
