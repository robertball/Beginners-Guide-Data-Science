Title:
Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757953.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. cranberries- chopped
1 can crushed pineapple- drained
1 pint heavy whipping cream
1 apple chopped
1 cup seedless grapes- cut in half
1/4 cup pecans- chopped
1 cup miniature marshmallows
1 cup sugar

Directions:
chop cranberries and mix with cup of sugar and let marinate overnight in the refrigerator.
whip, heavy whipping cream
mix remaining ingredients into whipping cream.
add cranberries and sugar mixture and mix well.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
A delicious cranberry salad that's good enough to be served as a desert.
