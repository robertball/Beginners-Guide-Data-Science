Title:
WW Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
1 white cake mix
3 egg whites
2 1/2 c diet 7-up
Frosting
1 pkg SF instant pudding (any flavor)
8 oz FF Cool Whip
1 c diet 7-up

Directions:
Mix dry cake mix with egg whites and diet 7-up.  Pour into 9 x 13 greased pan.  Bake for 30-35 minutes @ 350 degrees.  Test cake top by lightly touching center, which should feel slightly firm and will slightly spring back to the touch.  Cool completely.  Mix together frosting ingredients and spread onto cooled cake.  Refrigerate until time to serve.  Should stay fresh in refrigerator for up to two weeks.
