Title:
Big Ray's Dijon-Bacon Dip for Pretzels Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1067862.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup Dijon mustard
1/2 cup real bacon bits or crumbled cooked bacon, divided
1 to 3 teaspoons prepared horseradish
Pretzels

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, mustard, 1/2 the bacon and horseradish. Cover and chill until serving. Sprinkle remaining bacon on top. Serve with pretzels

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
With just four ingredients that you probably already have, this quick appetizer comes together in a snap. If you like the zip of horseradish, start with one or two teaspoons and add more to your taste.
