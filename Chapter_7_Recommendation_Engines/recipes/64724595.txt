Title:
Sous vide simple syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6055654.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
3 cups water
1 gallon Sous vide bag

Directions:
pour Water and sugar into a sous vide bag take as much air as possible out
Heat water to 140 degrees Fahrenheit
Add bag for 2 hours
Let cool and enjoy
