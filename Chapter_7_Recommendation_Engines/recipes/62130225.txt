Title:
Slow Cooker Angel Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breast
1/2 cup butter
1 package dry Italian salad dressing mix
1 can Campbell's golden mushroom soup
1/2 cup white wine (cooking wine or regular drinking wine)
4oz onion & chive cream cheese
pkg. angel hair pasta (cook according to box directions)

Directions:
Place chicken in crock pot.
Melt butter over low-medium heat in a small saucepan.
Stir in Italian salad dressing mix, soup, cream cheese & wine.
Pour over chicken. Cook on low for 4-5 hours.
Serve over cooked angel hair pasta or any kind of noodle you desire. (rice works well also). (Makes 6 breasts)

Submitter: 
Kristin DeLooff
Description: 
Saucy chicken breasts best served with angel hair pasta or rice.
