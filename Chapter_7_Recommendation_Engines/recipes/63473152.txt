Title:
Black Bean and Molasses Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946710.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can  ( 19 oz ) Black Beans, pureed
1 C. Fancy Molasses
1 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
1/2 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Cinnamon
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs
1/4 C. Canola oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a  12 cup muffin pan and set aside
In a large saucepan cook black beans in liquid until heated through
Cool the beans and then drain all the liquid from the beans
Mash the beans with a potato masher until nicely pureed and smooth
In a large mixing bowl combine molasses and beans together and stir until beans are fully dissolved into molasses
With a wire whisk add eggs and oil to molasses mixture and stir until eggs and oil are fully incorporated into the batter
In a medium size bowl combine flours, Baking Powder, Salt, Spice and Sugar. Stir dry ingredients until no lumps remain in the flour.
Add the dry ingredients to the molasses mixture and stir until all the flour is mixed into the batter
Fill muffin cups 3/4 full with batter and bake in oven for 20 minutes or until lightly browned

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A change from your usual bran muffin with added bean fibre and molasses to  add something more to these muffins for a nice dessert
