Title:
Simple Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz Box of Jumbo Shells
1 4lb Container of Ricotta
2 8oz bags of Mozzarella
1 1lb 10oz Jar of Patsa Sauce

Directions:
Boil the shells until tender, Drain and rinse with cold water. Mix the ricotta and the mozzarella. Stuff into each shell and layer shells into a mircowave safe dish with the bottom covered in a little bit of the pasta sauce. Make one layer of shells then pour more sauce on.. Make another layer and so on. May need to use 2 dishes. When ready to serve wrap in seran wrap and put into the mircowave for around 10mins more time depending on your microwave. Serve and Enjoy!
