Title:
** Bisquick Impossible Coconut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  cup flaked or shredded coconut
3/4  cup sugar
1/2  cup Original Bisquick® mix
1/4  cup butter or margarine, softened
2  cups milk
1 1/2  teaspoons vanilla
4  eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°F. Grease 9-inch pie plate with shortening or cooking spray.
In medium bowl, mix all ingredients until blended. (Can also mix in a blender.)
Pour into pie plate.
Bake 50-55 minutes or until golden brown and knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cover and refrigerate any remaining pie.
