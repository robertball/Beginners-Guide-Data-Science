Title:
Apple Tea Barbecue Sause Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Ketchup
1/2 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
1/2 cup Frozen Apple Juice concentrate
1/4 packed Brown Sugar
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tsp. yellow mustard
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. lemon pepper
1 tsp. paprika
1/3 cup of fresh bacon bits ground in spice grinder
1/2 cup grated onion
1/2 cup Black Tea leaves
2 dry bay leaves

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine all the ingredients except the grated onion and black tea bring to boil over medium - high head.
Stir in onion and Tea Reduce the heat and simmer uncovered for 10 to 15 minutes or until slightly thickened stirring often.
Strain and let cool
to serve bring back up to temp and serve on side or small amount over smoked ribs
