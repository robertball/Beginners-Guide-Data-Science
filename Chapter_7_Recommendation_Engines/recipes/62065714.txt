Title:
Cranberries n' Cream Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh cranberries
3 cups miniature marshmallows
3/4 cup sugar
2 medium apples (try Gala, Braeburn, or Fuji)
1 cup seedless grapes, green or red
1/2 cup walnuts or pecans
1 (8-oz.) whipping cream

Directions:
Pick cranberries over and toss out soft or blemished berries. Coarsely chop cranberries and measure 2 cups.
Mix cranberries, marshmallows, and sugar in a large non-metal bowl. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours or overnight.
Core and dice apples, cut grapes in half lengthwise, and chop nuts. Add to cranberry mixture.
Whip cream till stiff peaks form. Fold into fruit/nut mixture. Refrigerate till serving time.

Submitter: 
RCKim
Description: 
A sweetened mixture of fresh cranberries, apples, grapes, mini marshmallows, and nuts are folded into whipped cream for a light and dreamy holiday salad.
