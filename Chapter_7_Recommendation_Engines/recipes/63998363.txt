Title:
The Perfect Rustic Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1109505.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 oz) cans tomato sauce
1 head minced garlic
1/2 cup red wine
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 large tomato
1/3 cup minced onion
2 links Italian sausage
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp chopped basil
1 tbsp oregano
1 tbsp thyme
1 tbsp black pepper
1 tsp chili flake
1 tsp salt
1 tsp brown sugar

Directions:
Put all ingredients in blender
Blend to chosen thickness
Simmer on stove until wine cooks off, stirring occasionally

Submitter: 
kylewassell
Description: 
Making marinara sauce is literally one of my favorite things in the world to do. There is almost no way to go wrong other than adding too much salt or too much sugar. Just blend, simmer, and let the ingredients speak for themselves.
