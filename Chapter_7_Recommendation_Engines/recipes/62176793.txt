Title:
Crunchy brownie bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crunchy layer
3/4 c regular flour, sifted
1/4 t bakin soda
1/4 t salt
1 1/2 c quick cooking rolled oats
3/4 c bown sugar
3/4 c butter or margarine, melted
Brownie layer
1 package Duncan HInes brownie mix (family size)

Directions:
Combine flour, soda, and salt; stir in oats and brown sugar.
Add the 3/4 c melted butter and pat mixture in bottom of an ungreased 9 x 13 baking pan.
Bake at 350 for 10 minutes; at this time it will not be completely baked.
While bottom layer is in the oven, prepare brownie mix as directed on package.
Spread batter in pan over the crunchy layer.
Return to oven; bake fudgy style for 40-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
Imagine a cross between brownies and oatmeal cookies, yum!
