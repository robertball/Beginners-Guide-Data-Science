Title:
Max & Erma's Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cans  Cream of Mushroom soup
1 cans  Cream of Chicken soup
1 cans  Cream of Celery soup
1 cans  Cheddar Cheese soup
1 (15 ounce) cans chicken broth
1/2 (15 ounces) can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup salsa
1/2 (4.5 ounce) can green chiles
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
1 teaspoon red chile powder
Salt and pepper to  taste
1/8 cup fresh, chopped cilantro
2 cooked chicken breasts, cut into
small chunks
1/2 package flour tortillas
1/4 pound Colby cheese

Directions:
In a large stockpot whisk together  all eight cans of soup until smooth. Add chicken broth, whisk. Add tomatoes, salsa, green chiles, onion, garlic, chili powder, salt and pepper.
Bring all ingredients to a  boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 1 hour.
Add cilantro and chicken breast. Simmer another hour.
While soup is simmering, cut flour tortillas into small thin strips and deep fry in hot oil until lightly browned. Shred the cheese.
When serving, ladle soup into bowls, top with shredded cheese and fried tortilla strips. Great comfort food!

Submitter: 
KR
Description: 
This is very close to what is served at Max & Erma's restaurant and it's a big hit when I make it for a crowd or my family.
