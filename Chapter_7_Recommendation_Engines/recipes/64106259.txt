Title:
Spicy shrimp and noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
2 packages Ramen chicken noodle soup
1 lb med shrimp, shlled and deveined
1 cup green onions cut in 2" pieces.
Chopped fresh parsley, green on ion brush, orange peel and lemon slice for garnish

Directions:
1. Over high heat, combine water, soy sauce, ginger, pepper, garlic powder and half of one seasoning packet. Heat to boiling.
2. Add noodles, shrimp and green onion pieces. Return to boiling. Reduce heat to low. Cook 5 minutes or until shrimp are pink and opaque and noodles are tender, stirring occasionally to separate noodles.
