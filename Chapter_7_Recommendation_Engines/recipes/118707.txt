Title:
Roasted Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563879.jpg
Ingredients:
8 medium ears corn, husks and silks removed
Refrigerated butter-flavored spray*
2 tablespoons butter or stick margarine, melted
2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Spray eight 12-in. x 10-in. pieces of foil with nonstick cooking spray. Place one ear of corn on each piece of foil; spritz corn evenly with butter-flavored spray. Fold foil over corn and seal tightly. Grill, covered, over medium indirect heat or bake at 400 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until corn is tender. Combine the butter, horseradish, mustard, garlic, salt and pepper; brush over corn. Sprinkle with paprika.

Submitter: 
Sandy  Szwarc
Description: 
'I've been grilling corn on the cob for years this way...our friends always insist on it!' writes Sandy Szwarc from Albuquerque, New Mexico.
