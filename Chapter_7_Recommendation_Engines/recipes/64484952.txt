Title:
Oreo Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2542968.jpg
Ingredients:
15.25 ounces Oreo Cookies (finely crushed)
4 ounces Cream Cheese (softened)
6 ounces Candy Wafers or Chocolate Chips (melted)
Candy or Nut Sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and cookies until blended
Divide and shape dough into 24 balls
Freeze 10 minutes
Insert candy stick into the center of each ball
Dip ball into melted candy
Decorate with sprinkles as desired.
Place on waxed paper-covered baking sheet
Repeat
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm
