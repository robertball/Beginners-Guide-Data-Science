Title:
Jacolyn's Herbed Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2288209.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
2 teaspoons dried thyme
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Whisk olive oil, vinegar, garlic, basil, thyme, sugar, black pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jacolyn
Description: 
This is a quick and easy dressing. Perfect for your favorite green salad.
