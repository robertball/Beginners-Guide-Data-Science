Title:
Blue Lagoon Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 oz. Tequila
1 oz. Blue Caracao
1/2 oz. lime juice
1 1/2 oz. pinapple juice
2 oz. sour mix
1-2 cups ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender. Pour and serve.
