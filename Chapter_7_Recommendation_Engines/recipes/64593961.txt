Title:
Green Beans with a kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 onion grated
1 large clove of elephant ear garlic(grated)
1 can tomatoes with green chiles use juice
1 lb green beans snapped or you can use canned
1/4c  chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
2Tbsp olive oil

Directions:
If using fresh beans wash and snap them. In a medium on low heat coat pan, just enough for the onions to cook sprinkle with salt,saute until translucent. Add the green beans and grated garlic,tomatoes with green chiles,salt and pepper to taste, turn heat to med hi, and cook until almost all liquid is gone. Leave only about 2 to 3tbs of liquid. top with 1 to 2 tbs of olive oil. to your on taste I use a little over 1tbs. I hope you can enjoy these as much as me!

Submitter: 
Wanda Parker Davenport
Description: 
I love a little heat and wanted a side with my meatloaf, and my green beans came to be with onion,garlic and tomatoes with green chiles
