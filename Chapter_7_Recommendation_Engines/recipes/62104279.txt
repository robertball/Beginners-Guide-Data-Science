Title:
Fudgy Chocolate Mints Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 Tbsp butter or margarine
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 Tbsp peppermint flavoring (more or less to suit your taste)

Directions:
Combine chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, and butter in a saucepan and heat until melted.
Stir in vanilla and peppermint flavoring.
Pour into a foiled 9 inch baking pan and chill until set.
Cut into small pieces and serve.
