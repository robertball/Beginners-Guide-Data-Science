Title:
Easy and Tasty Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2233733.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of Spinach
1 Naval Orange
1/2 of Banana
1 Cup of water
1 Tablespoon of flaxseed

Directions:
In a blender add water, banana, naval orange (peeled), spinach and flaxseed. Blend for about 30 seconds to 1 minute until it's smooth.

Submitter: 
Joseph Alvarado
Description: 
If your looking for a tasty green smoothie. This is it. Enjoy this healthy snack.
