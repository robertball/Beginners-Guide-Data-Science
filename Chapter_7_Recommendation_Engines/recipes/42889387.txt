Title:
Bishop's Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4983824.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Flour
1 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder
3 Eggs
1/2 tsp Salt
1 Tsp Vanilla
1 6 oz package Chocolate Chips
1 Cup Chopped Dates
1 Cup Marischino Cherries (cut in half)
2 Cups Chopped nuts
1 Tbsp Butter
1 Cup Sugar

Directions:
In small bowl combine flour, baking powder, Salt, Chips, Dates, Cherries, & Nuts.
Stir untill coated
In large bowl: Cream butter & sugar together
Add Eggs and Vanilla, mix well
Fold Fruit into mixture
Place into greased and floured loaf pan(s)
Bake at 375 1 to 1.5 hours
