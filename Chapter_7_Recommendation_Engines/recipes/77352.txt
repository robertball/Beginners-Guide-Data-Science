Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978597.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1 ounce) squares semi-sweet baking chocolate
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/4 cup Jif® Creamy Peanut Butter
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker pie crust
1 (8 ounce) container frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a large bowl, melt chocolate squares in microwave for 1 minute, stirring after 30 seconds or until completely melted. Add sweetened condensed milk and peanut butter to melted chocolate; mix well.
Fold in whipped topping. Spoon into crust.
Freeze overnight. Garnish as desired. Serve immediately. Store leftovers covered in freezer.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This pie combines peanut butter and chocolate in a rich fluffy pie. Garnish with whipped topping or shaved chocolate.
