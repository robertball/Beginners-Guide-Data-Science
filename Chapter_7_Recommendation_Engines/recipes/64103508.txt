Title:
Roasted Kale and Chickpea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of Kale
1 15-oz can of chickpeas, drained
1 cup walnuts, chopped and toasted
2 tbs fresh parsley, chopped
2 tbs Sunflower Oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2  tbs lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 225 degrees Fahrenheit.
Rinse kale, remove stems, and tear the leaves into strips.
In a medium size bowl mix together oil, salt, and pepper. Toss kale and chickpeas in oil then, spread out on a roasting pan.
Bake kale and chickpeas until kale is wilted and just a little crunchy, appx. 25 minutes.
Remove kale and chickpeas from roasting pan and into a serving bowl. Add the walnuts, parsley, and lemon juice, then toss it all together.
