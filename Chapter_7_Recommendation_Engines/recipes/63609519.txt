Title:
Enchilada Casserole with Red Guijillo Chile Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004212.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb 96% extra lean ground beef
3 tablespoons taco seasoning (or 1 packet taco seasoning)
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup shredded carrots
1 (12-oz) jar Herdez Red Guijillo Chile Sauce
2 tablespoons sugar
1 (15-oz) can reduced sodium black beans, rinsed and drained
6 (6-inch) corn tortillas
2 cups 50% reduced fat cheddar cheese, shredded  (Such as Cabot)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Brown the ground beef in a large skillet.  Sprinkle with taco seasoning and pepper and stir until the meat is browned.  Add in the onions, red and green bell peppers, and shredded carrots and saut√© about 5 minutes.   Pour the sauce into a large measuring cup and stir in 2 tablespoons of sugar into the sauce.  Then pour the sauce over the meat mixture.   Stir in the drained black beans.
Place 3 corn tortillas on the bottom of a casserole dish.  Spoon half of the meat mixture on top.  Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese on top of that.  Then repeat the layers ending with the cheese on top.
Bake at 375 degrees for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
My family loves this delicious easy, budget friendly Mexican enchilada casserole with lots of vegetables.  The Herdez Red Guijillo Chile Mexican Cooking Sauce adds a deep earthy robust flavor to this family favorite.
