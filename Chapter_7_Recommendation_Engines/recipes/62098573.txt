Title:
Marsala Cream Chicken with Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons butter, divided
1 whole shallot, finely chopped
1/4 cup chopped sun dried tomatoes
1 cup sliced baby portabella mushrooms
1 cup fresh thin asparagus spears, prepped and cut into 3rds
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup marsala wine
1 Tablespoon all purpose flour
1 cooked rotisserie chicken, deboned and cut into bite size slices
3 Tablespoons whipping cream
8 oz cooked penne pasta
shredded parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Melt 2 Tablespoons butter in large saute pan on med high. Add shallots and saute for approx 5 minutes. Add mushrooms, asparagus and sun dried tomatoes. Saute for approx 10 minutes or until asparagus has started to soften but is not mushy.
Add chicken broth and marsala wine and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to med low and add cream. Add in chicken slices.
Mix remaining butter and flour to a paste and add to mushroom/asparagus mixture.  Stir for approximately 1-2 minutes until flour has cooked through and thickened.  Serve over hot penne pasta with shredded parmesan cheese.

Description: 
I use a deli roasted chicken from our local grocery store to speed up prep time on this dish. You can change the vegetables to suit your tastes. Serve with a loaf of crusty french bread and a glass of wine.
