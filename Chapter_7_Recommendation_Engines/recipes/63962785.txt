Title:
Mini Croissants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup boiling water
1 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 eggs

Directions:
Melt butter in boiling water.  Add flour and salt.  Cook and stir until pulls away from sides of pan.  Let cool in mixing bowl.
Add eggs, one at a time.  Beat well after each addition.  Will be smooth.  Drop by scant teaspoon on greased baking sheets.  Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
