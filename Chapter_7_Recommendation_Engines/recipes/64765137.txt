Title:
Shake n Bake Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 1/2cup bread crumbs
-1 tsp paprika
-1 tsp pepper
-1 tsp minced garlic
-pinch dried basil
-pinch dried oregano
-1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
1. Combine dry ingredients in a large zip top bag. Mix thoroughly
2. Add oil and chicken. Zip bag. Shake vigorously to coat
3. Bake on greased cookie sheet at 450 for 25-30 minutes
***Goes great with roasted red potatoes. Can both bake on the same pan
