Title:
Oven Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1105773.jpg
Ingredients:
3 - 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast
1/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
4TBSP cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°.
Wash chicken and pat dry with paper towels.
In a pie pan, combine flour, paprika, salt and pepper.
Coat chicken with the flour mixture.
Put butter on baking sheet; place in oven to melt.
When butter is melted, arrange chicken in the baking pan in a single layer.
Bake 10 minutes; turn and bake 10 minutes longer, or until chicken is tender and done.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
This simple oven fried chicken is coated with a seasoned flour mixture and baked with a butter.
