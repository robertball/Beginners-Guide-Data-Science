Title:
Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Graham Pie Crust
2 12oz. pkg of Cream Cheese
1 8oz. pkg of Cream Cheese
2 Eggs
1 Tsp. Vanilla extract
1 8oz. can of Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/8 tsp. Lemon Juice

Directions:
Blend all ingredients except condensed milk until soft.
Add condensed milk and blend again.
Pour mixture into graham pie crust.
Cook for 1 hour or until firm.
