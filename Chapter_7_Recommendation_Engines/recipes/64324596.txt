Title:
Couscous and Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon oil, olive
plus 1/2 tablespoon, divided
1/2 cup(s) couscous, 100% whole-wheat
3/8 cup(s) beans, garbanzo (chickpeas) drained and rinsed (reserve the liquid)
1/4 cup(s) peas, frozen thawed
1/2 medium carrot(s) coarsely shredded
1/2 small tomato(es) chopped
1/2 small pepper(s), red, bell or yellow pepper, chopped
1 1/4 tablespoon currants, fresh
1 1/4 tablespoon chives, fresh finely chopped
3/4 tablespoon nuts, pistachio or pine nuts
1/2 medium lemon juiced (use 3/4 tablespoons)
1/8 teaspoon thyme, dried
1/8 teaspoon marjoram, dried

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over high heat, bring 1 1/2 cups of water, salt, and 1 teaspoon of the oil to a boil. Stir in the couscous. Remove from the heat, cover and let stand for 5 minutes or until the liquid is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.
2
Transfer the couscous to a large bowl. Add the chickpeas (without the liquid), peas, carrots, tomatoes, peppers, currants, chives, and nuts. Toss gently until mixed.
3
In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, thyme, marjoram and the remaining oil. Add 3 tablespoons of the reserved chickpea liquid; discard the remaining chickpea liquid. Mix and pour over the salad. Toss to mix well.
4
Cover and let stand at room temperature for 30 minutes to blend the flavors.
