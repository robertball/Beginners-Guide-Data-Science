Title:
Rhonda's Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups  sugar
3/4cup  butter
2/3cup  canned milk
12oz    semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 jar   marshmallow creme
1 teas  vanilla

Directions:
Line a 15"x10"x3/4" pan with foil(thin fudge).
Melt butter, add milk and sugar to a heavy large saucepan. Bring to a full boil and stir constantly for 4 minutes. Add chocolate and stir till melted. Add marshmallow creme and stir till melted and combined. Stir in vanilla and pour into prepared pan. Allow to cool and set.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
