Title:
Gluten Free Honey Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1133785.jpg
Ingredients:
DRY INGREDIENTS:
2 CUPS GLUTEN FREE FLOUR MIX
2 TABLESPOONS COCONUT FLOUR
2 TEASPOONS BAKING POWDER
1 TEASPOON BAKING SODA
1/2 TEASPOON SEA SALT
1/2 TEASPOON XANTHUM GUM
WET INGREDIENTS:
1/2 CUP VERY COLD BUTTER CUT UP IN CUBES
1/2 CUP FAT FREE PLAIN YOGURT
1 EGG
1/4 CUP RAW LOCAL HONEY IF AVAILABLE

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 400 DEGREES. LINE WITH PARCHMENT PAPER A STANDARD COOKIE SHEET.  MIX DRY INGREDIENTS IN ONE BOWL, AND WET IN ANOTHER LARGE BOWL. COMBINE BOTH, STIRRING GENTLY, CUTTING IN COLD BUTTER TO DRY FIRST WITH PASTRY TOOL.  MIX ALL INGREDIENTS INTO THE LARGER BOWL UNTIL COMBINED. DO NOT OVER MIX. PAT THIS DOUGH ONTO A LIGHTLY FLOURED (GLUTEN FREE TYPE) PIECE OF PARCHMENT PAPER WHICH IS ON COOKIE SHEET.  LARGE ROUND DOUGH ABOUT 3/4 INCH THICK. SCORE WITH LARGE KNIFE INTO 8 TRIANGLES, BAKE ABOUT 17 MINUTES UNTIL DONE. REMOVE FROM OVEN AND CUT ALL THE WAY THROUGH THE BAKED DOUGH TO COMPLETE PERFECT TRIANGLE SCONES.

Submitter: 
gem bee
Description: 
THIS IS A PERFECT GLUTEN FREE AND NOT TOO SWEET HONEY BISCUIT FOR STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE, OR EVEN SAUSAGE GRAVY AS A BREAKFAST TREAT. THINKING ON HOW TO THICKEN GRAVY?  WE USE BROWN RICE FLOUR, WORKS QUITE WELL.
