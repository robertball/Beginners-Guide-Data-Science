Title:
Chicken Gumbo Over Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoon olive oil, divided
1/2  pound Italian sausage, cut into 1/4 inch slices
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1/2 inch slices
1 cup chopped onions
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup diced green bell pepper
2 tablespoons minced jalapeno or serrano peppers
1 teaspoon paprika
1 1/2 cup fresh or frozen okra, cut into1/4 inch slices
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup white wine
2 cups cooked white or brown rice

Directions:
1. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage; cook about 10 minutes or until browned. Transfer to paper towel lined plate with slotted spoon.
2. Heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in same skillet. Add flour; cook until dark brown but not burnt, whisking constantly. Add chicken, onions, celery,  Bell pepper, jalapeno peppers and paprika, Cook and stir 7 to 8 minutes or until vegetables softened. Transfer to crockpot.
3. Add sausage, okra, broth and wine to crock pot. Cover; cook on low 7 to 8 hours or on high 4 to 6 hours. Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
CG Joseph
Description: 
This is a great dish served over your favorite white or brown rice.
