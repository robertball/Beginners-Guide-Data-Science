Title:
Teriyaki Pork with Garden Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless pork tenderloin, cubed
2 4.5 oz. pkg. Lipton's Chicken fried rice
1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (corn, peas, carrots and green beans)
2 cups water
1 tablespoon sesame seed oil (or you can use olive oil)
1 tablespoon ground ginger
1 small onion, chopped
3/4 cup Lawry's Teriyaki marinade
1 8 oz. can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 8 oz. can sliced bamboo shoots, drained
Garden Vegetable Soup:
4 1.6 oz. Thai Kitchen Garden Vegetable Rice Noodles
2 32 oz. carton Chicken Broth
1 8 oz. can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1/3 cup snipped chives, dried or fresh
2 celery stalks sliced thinly
2 cups water
1 8 oz. can sliced bamboo shoots, drained
1 cup of Leeks, cleaned and chopped

Directions:
Put stove on medium heat under large skillet pan, add sesame seed oil (can be substituted for olive oil) add in the cubed pork tenderloin, onion and ginger, cook meat until not pink.
Add in frozen vegetables, water chestnuts, bamboo shoots, rice, water and teriyaki sauce.  Cover with lid to cook and stir occassionaly.
Meanwhile in medium sauce pan on medium heat add Chicken broth, water and chives; stir until just mixed. Add thinly sliced celery, water chestnuts, bamboo shoots and leeks; until mixed.  Add the rice noodles with seasonings in package.  Cover to boil.  It will take about 3-5 minutes for noodles to be cooked and it will take 10 minutes for the teriyaki to finish cooking.  So under 20 minutes you will be sitting down with your family for a lovely dinner. Bon Appetit.
