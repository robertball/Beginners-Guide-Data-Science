Title:
Raspberrry Infused Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798422.jpg
Ingredients:
(6) Packages Rrozen Raspberries
4 1/2 Cup Sugar
4 Cups Water
2 1/2 gallons Vodka

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large container (non metallic)
Press/Mull/Squeeze Raspberries till incorporated into mixture
Stir mixture for 10-15 minutes every day for 5 or 6 days
Let mixture stand till lees/dregs from raspberries have settled
Siphon concoction till you have a clear liquid
ENJOY!
