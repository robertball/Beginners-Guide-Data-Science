Title:
Beef and Broccoli Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1Pound ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 tbsp flour
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1 1/4 c milk
3 oz pkg. cream cheese
1 beaten egg
10 oz pkg. frozen broccoli, cooked and drained
Pie shells top and bottom
1 package shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Brown onion and beef.
Add flour, salt and garlic salt.
Add milk and cream cheese, simmer until thickened.
Add beaten egg and stir.
Stir in broccoli.
Pour into pie shell.
Cover mixture with Monterey Jack cheese and top with upper crust.
Perforate crust and brush with milk.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes.
