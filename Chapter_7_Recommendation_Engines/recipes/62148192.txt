Title:
Summer Doritto Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs ground beef
1 taco seasoning packet
1 head of lettuce (chopped)
2 cups of shredded chedder cheese
1 chopped tomato
1 medium bag of nacho cheese dorittos
1 bottle of California French Dressing
1/2 cup of water

Directions:
1.Brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat. Mix in the taco season packet and 1/2 cup of water. Stir over medium heat untill sauce thickens. Remove from heat and let cool to room temp.
2.Take the bag of Dorittos, and crunch them up.
3. In a large bowl, toss the lettuce, the beef mixture, tomato, cheese, dorittos and dressing.
