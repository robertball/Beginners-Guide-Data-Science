Title:
Creamy Almond Butter with Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup toasted almonds
2 teaspoons olive oil
honey until creamy

Directions:
Place the almonds in a food processor. Process on high until ground almonds begin to form a ball. Drizzle olive oil over almonds and continue to process, pulsing and stopping occasionally to scrape sides of the bowl with a spatula as needed. Store in an airtight container.

Description: 
This is a quick, simple way to make healthy, all-natural basic almond butter. Add sugar, spices, or other ingredients to taste if desired.Modified from: Almond Butter
