Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. broccoli, chopped
1 boned, skinned chicken breast, cubed
8 oz. fettuccine
3/4 - 1 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup freshly shredded parmesan cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook broccoli until tender and bright green, 2 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, transfer to a colander, leaving water in pot. Add chicken to pot; cook until opaque, 2to 4 minutes. Transfer to colander with broccoli.
Cook pasta in same pot of water until just tender about 10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup posta water; drain pasta.
Simmer cream, reserved 1/2 cup water and 1/2 cup parmesan in same pot over  medium heat, stirring often. Add broccoli, chicken and pasta, stirring to combine. Season to taste with sat and pepper. Serve with additional parmesan on the side.
