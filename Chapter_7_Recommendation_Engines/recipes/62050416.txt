Title:
Almost Candy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 devils food cake mix
1 stick margarine, softened
1 can sweetened condensed milk (Eagle Brand)
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch chips
1 cup coconut
1/2 to 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix cake mix and butter until crumbly and lightly press into 9 x 13 inch pan.  Pour sweetened condensed milk over crust.  Sprinkle with chocolate chips, butterscotch chips, coconut, and nuts.
Bake at 350 for 20 to 25 minutes or until coconut is browned.  Let cool and cut into squares

Submitter: 
P Hutch
Description: 
Easy bar cookies that are so yummy.  You can experiment with different toppings to suit your fancy.
