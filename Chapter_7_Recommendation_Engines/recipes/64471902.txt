Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2476425.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks unsalted butter
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla
2 10 oz bags of mini marshmallows
10 cups rice Krispy cereal
1 1/2 cup candy coated sunflower seeds
2 bags dark chocolate or white chocolate chips (I have used both)
1 bag coconut flakes

Directions:
-In a large pot heat butter, salt, and vanilla until it melts
-Add in marshmallows
-Remove from heat once everything is melted together
-pour in rice Krispy cereal (stir until evenly coated in marshmallow)
-pour in candy coated sunflower seeds and mix well
-form into balls and place in parchment paper
-melt dark chocolate
-place coconut flakes into a bowl and have it ready
-Dip each rice Krispy treat ball into the dark chocolate and immediately rollbin coconut flakes.
-set aside to cool
- eat and enjoy!
