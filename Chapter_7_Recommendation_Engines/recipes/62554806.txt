Title:
Simple Tasty Burgers! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/532347.jpg
Ingredients:
2lbs. ground chuck
Season Salt
Garlic Salt
Garlic Pepper
5 Hamburger Buns

Directions:
Thaw out your burger. Place it in a bowl, add season salt and garlic salt ( I don't know exact measurements its just by look)coat spices. Stir around. Make into patties, season both sides of patties with garlic pepper. Cook on grill for 5-6 min., flip to other side, cook for another 5-6 min. Take off grill. Add cheese or any other condiments. ENJOY!
