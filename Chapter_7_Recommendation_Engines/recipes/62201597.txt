Title:
Basic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups lukewarm water
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
2 pks yeast
1/4 cup soft shortening
61/2 - 7 cups flour

Directions:
Mix water, sugar and salt
Add yeast - stir until dissolved
Add egg & shortening
Mix in flour with spoon & knead
Let rise (1 - 1 1/2 hrs) until doubled in size
Punch down and rise again  (in bread pans)
Bake at 375 for 45 min (approx)

Very basic white bread
