Title:
Peanut Butter-Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces unsweetened chocolate
1 cup peanut butter (or a mix of other butters: 1/4 c almond is nice)
3/4 cup maple syrup

Directions:
Melt the chocolate with the peanut butter and maple syrup in a double boiler over medium heat, stirring continually until smooth (or melt chocolate in the microwave and stir in PB and syrup till smooth). Pour into a dish lined with parchment paper; refrigerate 6 hours to overnight before cutting to serve.

Susie
