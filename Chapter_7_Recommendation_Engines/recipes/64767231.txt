Title:
Suckling Pig in Chestnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 suckling Pig 5-6 lbs
1 Lb. of Chestnuts
3.5 oz. Butter
2 oz. Sweet Red Wine
Grapes, Pears, and Apples  (  marinated preserved if possible ).

Directions:
Clean and scrape the Suckling white. Salt and Pepper the Inside carcass. before you do. Take the
Chestnuts and make a cross cut on the point end, then bake at 400 until you hear popping.
Remove the Chestnuts Hull them and saute them in butter and Wine until Tender.
fill the Suckling with these Chestnuts and close with a dressing Needle and Yarn or with
many small needles.  Score the Skin carefully with a sharp Knife. Then in a Roasting Pan
Begin Roasting at 400 for 30 minutes per Pound including Filling.  Baste frequently.
Suggested to serve with;  Small par-boiled potatoes along with the Fruit which will be added 5 minute before done.  When Done remove the needles.  Cut the suckling in Portions and Serve on a large Dish or "Plat-russe" along with the Chestnuts, Potatoes and Fruit.
being done.

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
Month old Savory Suckling Pig stuffed with Chestnuts and served with Fresh or marinated Fall Fruits.
