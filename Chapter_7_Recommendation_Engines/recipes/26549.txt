Title:
Marinated Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992786.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup ORTEGA® Thick & Chunky Salsa
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cups chopped fresh tomato
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 medium ripe avocado
1 cup chopped zucchini
1 (4 ounce) can ORTEGA® Diced Green Chiles
lettuce leaves

Directions:
COMBINE salsa, lime juice, parsley and olive oil in large bowl.  Add tomatoes, kidney beans, avocado, zucchini and chiles.  Toss well to coat.  Cover; refrigerate for at least 2 hours.  Serve over lettuce.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Mexican cuisine is very creative when it comes to salads. A century ago, ensalada was the name given to practically any accompaniment to the main meat course. Here 's an Ortega salad that carries on that tradition and goes with anything.
