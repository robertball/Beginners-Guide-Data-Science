Title:
Chery's Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Chick peas (drain & save the juice)
3 cloves of Garlic
1 Tsp Salt
4 Tbsp Tahini
3 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 Tbsp Olive oil

Directions:
Mash Garlic and salt together (with a mortar and pestle). Put chick peas, garlic salt mixture, tahini in a food processor. Mix a bit. Add lemon juice, 3 Tbsp of the chick pea juice and the olive oil. Mix all together until smooth. Taste- it may need a bit more lemon juice if it needs salt- add a bit more chick pea juice instead.

Deborah Thompson
