Title:
Awesome Chili Mac With a Healthier Twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. extra lean ground turkey (93/7)
1 large onion, chopped
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
1 (16 oz.) can kidney beans, drained
2 c. (8 oz.) whole grain elbow macaroni
1 c. water
8 oz. low fat cheddar cheese, grated
Louisiana Hot Sauce, to taste

Directions:
Brown mean and onion in a dutch oven or 6 qt. pan.  Drain excess fat.
Add all other ingredients except cheese.  Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
Stir in cheese until melted.
Serve with Louisiana Hot Sauce if you want a little extra kick.

Submitter: 
J. Rentsch
Description: 
This is an adaptation of a recipe I received from a friend.  Wanting to make it healthier, I made several substitutions without sacrificing the taste.  I think you'll agree it's delicious.
