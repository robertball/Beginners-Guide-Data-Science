Title:
Hearty Winter Casserole Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/596283.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. turkey Italian sausage
2 onions, chopped
1 qt. chicken broth
2 cups water
3 cans diced tomatoes with juice
12 oz vegetable juice (like V-8)
1 cup sliced carrots
2 cups chopped celery
2 cups sliced cabbage
2 cups uncooked whole wheat pasta
2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
4 cups washed fresh spinach
1 can black beans, drained
1 can white beans, drained
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup grated low fat cheddar

Directions:
In a large Dutch oven, break up sausage and brown with onion.  Drain fat, then add broth, water, tomatoes, juice, carrots and celery.  Cook, covered, 5 minutes.  Add cabbage and pasta and cook uncovered 10 minutes.  Stir in horseradish and vinegar, then add beans and spinach.  Boil 1 minute.  Serve garnished with cheese, warm bread on the side.
