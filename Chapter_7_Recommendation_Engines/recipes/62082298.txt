Title:
Harper Family salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 roma tomatoes chopped
1/2 red onion diced
1 seranno pepper diced
1 red bell pepper diced
1 10oz.can of diced tomato & green chili's
1 clove of garlic pressed
4 tbs.of fresh cilatro
1/4c. lime juice
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
blend all ingredients in blender until desired consistantcy.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Laymance
Description: 
This is a great salsa recipe you can make in the blender our whole family loves it!! You can add more peppers for spice.
