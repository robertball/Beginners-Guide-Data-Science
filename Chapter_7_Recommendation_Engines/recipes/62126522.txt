Title:
Cheese Enchiladas Kids Love Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP finely chopped onion
2 cups shredded colby/jack cheese
1 cup mild taco sauce, divided
8 oz. light sour cream, divided
8 oz. Velveeta cheese, cubed
2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. garlic powder
8 flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a 9x11 baking dish with cooking spray.
For enchilada filling, combine onion, shredded cheese, 1/4 cup taco sauce, and 1/4 cup sour cream in a medium bowl.
In a medium microwavable bowl, melt Velveeta in microwave, stirring often.
In the same bowl, add remaining sour cream, remaining taco sauce, cumin, and garlic.
Roll about 1/4 cup of the filling mixture in each tortilla.
Pour Velveeta mixture over the top and bake uncovered for 20-25 minutes.
