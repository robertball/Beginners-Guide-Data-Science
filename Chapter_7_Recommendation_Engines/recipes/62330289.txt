Title:
Caramel Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 frozen cinnamon rolls
1c        sugar
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 c     Milk
3/4c      butter
1/4c      brown sugar

Directions:
1. Let cinnamon rolls rise as instructed on package
2. Put the rest of the ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil
3. Pour mixture over raised rolls and bake at 400 for 10-15 min.
4. Remove from oven and let sit 5 min. before turning out on a plate.
