Title:
Old  Fashioned  Porridge and Molasses Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5858301.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups boiling water
2 cups rolled oats
1/4 cup shortening
2 teaspoons white sugar
1 cup lukewarm water(100F)
2 envelopes active dry yeast
2/3 cup molasses
4 teaspoons salt
8 and 1/2 cups all purpose flour.
more flour if needed.

Directions:
Pour the 3 cups boiling water over the 2 cups rolled oats and the 1/4 cup shortening. Stir until shortening melts and let stand for 20 minutes, giving it a stir once in a while. Meanwhile dissolve  the 2 teaspoons of sugar in the cup of lukewarm water, add the 2 envelopes of yeast, let stand 10 minutes then stir briskly with a fork.  Stir into the partially cooled rolled oat mixture the 2/3 cup molasses and 4 teaspoons salt. Cool to lukewarm. Add softened yeast mixture to the rolled oat mixture and stir. Beat in 2 and 1/2 cups flour, then gradually add in 5 and 1/2 to 6 more cups of flour. Turn dough on to floured surface and knead 8-10 minutes. Shape into a smooth ball and place in a greased bowl, rolling the dough so the top is greased. Cover and let double in size(about 1 and 1/2 hour. Punch down and shape into 4 loaves. Place in greased bread pans 8and 1/2x 4 and 1/2 inch. Cover and let rise again for about 1 hour until doubled. Bake in preheated 400* degree oven for 30-35 minutes. See notes.*****
