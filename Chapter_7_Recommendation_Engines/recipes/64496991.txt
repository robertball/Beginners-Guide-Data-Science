Title:
Not your Mothers Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground lean white meat chicken
1 lb ground lean Pork
3 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic minced
2 onions
2 cups chopped celery
5 carrots
6 cups chicken stock
1 jar spaghetti sauce (about 4 cups)
1 - 14.5 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 cup white wine
2 rinds of Parmesan Cheese ( we grind our own and save the rinds in the freezer to use later)
1 can cannellini beans drained and rinsed
1 cup frozen corn
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tps salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 zucchinis quartered and diced
1/2 half a bag frozen cut green beans
2 cups spinach
8 ounces elbow pasta (we use Barilla Gluten Free)
grated Fresh Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Directions:
Heat stock with oil over medium high heat, add the ground chicken and brown about 4 minutes, add the ground pork and garlic and continue cooking until cooked through. While the meat is cooking use your food processor and mince up the onions, celery and carrots to a mince.  Add to meat and simmer 5 to 7 minutes stirring occasionally.
Add broth, wine, sauce, crushed tomatoes,  Parmesan rinds, corn, beans, oregano, basil salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil and turn down heat to a simmer and cook 20 minutes. Heat a pot of water and cook pasta as directed and drain.   Remove Parmesan rinds and add Zucchini and Frozen green beans, cook 5 minutes add fresh spinach and cook 3 minutes. Spoon cooked pasta into a bowl and ladle soup over the top.  Sprinkle wit Parmesan cheese if desired. Serves 10 to 12
