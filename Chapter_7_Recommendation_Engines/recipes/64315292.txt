Title:
Refried Beans Without the Refry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, peeled and chopped
3 cups dry pinto beans, rinsed
12 cloves minced garlic
2 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 3/4 teaspoons fresh ground black pepper
4 oz. green chilies
1 teaspoon ground cumin
Chicken Broth
To make Dip Add:
2 C salsa
4 C shredded mexi-blend cheese
8 oz. cream cheese
16 oz. sour cream
2 T chili powder
1/2 t ground cumin

Directions:
Place the onion, rinsed beans, garlic, salt, pepper, chilies and cumin into a slow cooker. Pour in just enough chicken broth to cover and stir to combine.  Cook on High for 8 hours, adding more broth as needed.  Note: if more than 1 cup of water has evaporated during cooking, then the temperature is too high.
Mash the beans with a potato masher, then hand mixer adding broth if needed to attain desired consistency.
To make a large batch of bean dip, after mashing the beans add: 2 C salsa, 4 C shredded mexi-blend cheese, , 8 oz. cream cheese, 16 oz. sour cream, 2 T chili powder, 1/2 t ground cumin...mix and let cook a couple hours longer. Freeze in small batches.

Description: 
Flavorful refried beans seasoned with garlic, chilies and cumin are simple to make when cooked in a slow cooker.Modified from: Refried Beans Without the Refry
