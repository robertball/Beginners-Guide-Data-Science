Title:
Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. lemon juice
1 tbs. vanilla
1 graham cracker crust
2 - 8 oz. packages cream cheese, softened
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Beat cream cheese until fluffy
Slowly add milk and beat
Stir in vanilla and lemon juice
Pour into crust
Chill 2 - 3 hours

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
This pie unites cheesecake lovers and non-lovers. Perfect blend of creamy sweetness. This recipe was given to me by a friend and has been a family favorite since then.
