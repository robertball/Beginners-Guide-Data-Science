Title:
Household Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580053.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 egg
2 Tbsp. water
1/2 cup Stove Top Italian Stuffing Mix
1/4 cup minced onion
1/4 cup minced green pepper
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
2 Tbsp. A1 Steak Sauce
4 Tbsp. Ketchup
1 jar Marinara Sauce (Optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In large bowl beat together egg and water.
Add stuffing mix, onion, green pepper, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, pepper, A1, and Ketchup mixing well.
Add ground beef, broken into chunks, and combine well using hands to mix. (If necessary, add up to 2 Tbsp. more stuffing mix for proper texture, depending on moisture.)
Form into meatballs about 1" in diameter and place on a broiler pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes until meatballs are no longer pink in center. Enjoy.. or add hot marinara sauce (optional) and enjoy.
