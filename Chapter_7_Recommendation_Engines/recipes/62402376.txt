Title:
Bean and Barley Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4C quick cooking barley
1Can kidney beans rinsed and drained
1Can  whole kernal corn drained
1 large sweet red pepper, finely chopped
6 green onions chopped
1/3C fresh minced cilantro
Dressing:
3/4C olive oil
1/3C red wine vinegar
2 galic cloves minced
1 1/2tsp chilli powder
3/4tsp  salt
1/2tsp crushed red peppers
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Prepare barley according to package directions. Transfer to a large bowl; stir in the beans;corn, red pepper, onions, and cilantro. In a small bowl, combine the dressing ingredients. Pour over salad; toss to coat. Chill until serving.
