Title:
Onion Ring Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box long grain and wild rice
2 small cans cream of chicken soup
1 c + 2 T mayonnaise
1 small can sliced water chestnuts
1 small onion, chopped
1 c celery, chopped
3 whole chicken breasts, cooked and diced
1 bag or box frozen onion rings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Prepare rice as directed. While rice cooks, prepare and mix together all other items except onion rings.
Mix cooked rice into chicken mixture and turn into 9x13" casserole.
Top with onion rings. Bake for 1 hour- cover with foil for the first 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
wilco
Description: 
Whenever our family wants a little something different, this is a great and easy recipe! It also goes over well at potlucks.
