Title:
Herbed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBL olive oil
1 green onion, chopped
3 TBL chopped fresh rosemary, sage and thyme
1 LB frozen white bread dough, thawed
2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in heavy small skillet over medium-low heat. Add green onion, rosemary, sage and thyme and saute until aromatic, abt 1 minute. Remove from heat. Place dough in medium bowl. Add herb mixture and pepper and knead until combined.
Grease 8 1/2x4 1/2 inch loaf pan. Pat dough into rectangle, roll up into loaf shape and place in prepared pan, seam side down. cover and let rise in warm, draft-free area, until doubled, 45 minutes or less.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Bake bread until golden brown and loaf sounds hollow when tapped on bottom; abt 25 minutes. Turn bread out onto rack and cool completely before serving.
