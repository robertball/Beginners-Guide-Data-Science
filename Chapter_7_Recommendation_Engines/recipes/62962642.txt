Title:
Carmel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 cups light butter popcorn (about 4 bags)
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
remove kernels, add popcorn to lrg bowl, set aside
In glass dish combine: sugar, butter, syrup & vanilla
microwave 3 minutes, remove & stir till blended
return to microwave for 1 min 30 seconds
remove from micro and add soda, stir till bleanded
pour syrup over popcorn and stir to coat
return to micro for 1 minute
remove and stir
return to micro for 1 minute
remove and place on wax paper or cookie sheet to set
store in an airtight container
