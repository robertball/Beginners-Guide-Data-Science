Title:
Chipped Beef on Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. cornstarch
1 T. margarine or butter
1 c. milk
1 package beef deli meat (like Buddig beef)
4 - 6 slices bread

Directions:
Melt margarine in small saucepan.  Add cornstarch and cook until thick and bubbly.  Slowly stir in milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps.  Chop beef into bite sized pieces and add to sauce.  Add salt and pepper to taste and warm through.  Toast bread and serve sauce over toast.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
A quick and easy version of the long time favorite.
