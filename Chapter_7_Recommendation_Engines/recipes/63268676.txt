Title:
Microwave Twice Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, cleaned and dried
1/2 c. butter or margarine
1/2 c. grated cheddar cheese
1 T. grated onion
1/2 t. salt
1/8 t. pepper
3/4 c. milk
paprika

Directions:
Prick potatoes once. Bake on high power in the microwave until done.
While still hot, slice potatoes lengthwise. Scoop out insides, saving shells.
Whip together potatoes and remaining ingredients except paprika. Spoon mixture back into shells. Return to the microwave (or a hot oven) for 5 more minutes.
