Title:
Chef Kiran's Okra Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs. Vegetable Oil
1 Lb. young small Okra cleaned
1/2 tsp. Turmeric powder
1 clove Garlic chopped
1/2 tsp. fresh peeled ginger chopped
2 onions chopped into quarters
2 Roma Tomatoes chopped into quarters
1 Tb. chopped Cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil in Wok or large skillet
Stir-fry Okra until tender and golden brown about 3 minutes - set okra aside.
Add Turmeric powder to oil let turmeric release its aroma and oils add garlic, ginger, onions and tomatoes. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender add okra.  Garnish with cilantro and serve.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
Okra, sweet and delish... Stir Fry turns Okra golden brown and tender.
