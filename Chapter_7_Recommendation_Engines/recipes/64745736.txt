Title:
Cheese Crusted Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
6 large eggs
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1 cup shredded Colby Monterrey jack cheese
1 cup cooked and chopped broccoli florets
1 Tbsp dried, minced onion
Salt & Pepper, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
Spray glass pie pan with non-stick spray and line the bottom shredded sharp cheddar cheese.
Place in the oven for 12-14 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
Set aside to cool slightly.
Reduce oven temperature to 325 degrees Fahrenheit.
In a mixing bowl, combine eggs, cream, shredded cheese, broccoli, onion, salt and pepper and whisk until well blended.
Pour mixture over the cheese crust, and return to the oven (now 325 degrees) for 35-40 minutes, or until fully baked through.
Remove from oven and let cool slightly before slicing up to serve.
