Title:
Keto Sausage Pizza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8567596.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c chicken stock or bone broth or water
1/2 head cauliflower, chopped into florets
1 lb Mild Italian Sausage-ground
2 T butter, ghee, or tallow
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp sea salt
1 c fresh Basil chopped
1 T oregano, dried
1 tsp Fennel seeds
3 Tbsp Canned Tomato Paste
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 cup heavy cream (or more to get creamy consistency)
1 T Balsamic Vinegar
3 c Shredded Mozzarella, divided

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
boil cauliflower in chicken stock or water until tender; drain and set aside
brown sausage in tallow/ghee/bacon grease (salt lightly and add some fennel seeds), set aside
soften onions and garlic in butter/ghee/tallow
add spices and salt
add tomato paste
add balsamic vinegar
add cream cheese
add heavy cream
add 1 c mozzarella
add 1/2 c basil
season sauce to taste; use extra chicken stock to thin or cook down to make thicker
in greased pyrex or casserole pan 9x11 layer: meat, cauliflower, 1 c mozzarella cheese.
repeat layers once
top with mozzarella and  basil
bake 45-60 min at 350 until cheese is golden and bubbly
