Title:
Grammies Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium potatoes
1 cup of water
1 can carnation milk, or 2% milk
1/4 cup butter or a few stripes bacon grease (2 tbsp)
1 large onion
1 large can creamed corn
Salt and pepper for seasoning

Directions:
Chop potatoes in cubes, and finely chop the onions. If using bacon, fry onions with it.
