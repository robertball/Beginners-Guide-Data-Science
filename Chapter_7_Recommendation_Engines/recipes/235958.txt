Title:
Winter Refresher Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1093662.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup fresh spinach leaves, or to taste
1 apple, cored and quartered
1 orange, peeled
1 cucumber, peeled and quartered
1/2 cup cranberries
1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger root, peeled

Directions:
Place water, spinach, apple, orange, cucumber, cranberries, and ginger in a blender. Blend on low until just combined; increase speed to high and blend until smooth and liquefied, about 1 minute.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
A bright and fresh green smoothie sure to blast away the winter blues! Serve over ice.
