1	(19 oz/540 mL) Can red kidney beans, drained
1/2 cup	Corn niblets (frozen or canned)

Directions:
Directions
HEAT olive oil in large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cook gently for 2 minutes.
ADD turkey and cook for 8-9 minutes, breaking up turkey with the back of a wooden spoon.
ADD remaining ingredients; bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 35-40 minutes.
