Title:
Cinco de Mayo Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef, browned and drained
1 small to medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 can Rotel tomatoes, drained
1 can Mexican corn, any size, drained
1 cup salsa, drained
1 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheese of your choice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 cups shredded cheddar or four-cheese blend.

Directions:
Pour browned and drained meat into your casserole baking dish.
Sautee the onion and garlic and add to dish.
Add drained tomatoes, corn and salsa.
Stir in salt and pepper, sour cream and 1 cup shredded cheese.
Top with 2 cups shredded cheddar or four-cheese blend.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.
Let sit for 10 minutes before serving it with a dollop of sour cream, sliced black olives, more salsa and warmed tortillas.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
A simple, satisfying main dish that our family enjoys.  You can use reduced fat cheeses and sour cream, but don't use the fat-free choices.
