Title:
Gluten-free Pie Crust-CAG-AR Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c pecans
1/2 c hazelnuts
4 T butter
pinch salt

Directions:
1 c. pecans 1/2 c. hazelnuts 4 Tbsp. butter a pinch of salt. Preheat oven to 350F Process nuts in food processor to almost a flour consistency. Be careful not to process too much and get butter instead. In a bowl mix the ground nuts with the butter and then spread the crust mixture in a pie pan and bake for 10 min. Then add your pumpkin pie filling.
