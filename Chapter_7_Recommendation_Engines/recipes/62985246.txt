Title:
Apple Crisp - Perfect & Easy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/743542.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups crisp cooking apples, pared, cored & sliced
1 cup sifted all-purpose flour
1 cup sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup soft butter (room temp., not melted)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Lightly grease an 8-by-8-by-2-inch baking dish.
Wash, core, peel, & slice tart cooking apples.
Spread apples evenly in prepared baking dish.
Sift flour with sugar, salt, & cinnamon into med. bowl.
Use pastry blender or 2 knives to cut softened butter (not melted) into flour mixture until mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.
Sprinkle mixture evenly over apples.
Bake 40-45 minutes or until topping is golden (not brown).
Cool slightly.
Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
JoAnn Hague
Description: 
I adapted this from a 50-year-old Peach Crisp recipe.  Although it works well with peaches, it works even better with fresh, crisp cooking apples
