Title:
Cucumber-Orzo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. dried orzo pasta
1 small cucumber, peeled and chopped
1/2 c. chopped red sweet pepper
1/4 c. chopped red onion
1/4 c. olive oil
1/4 c. white balsamic vinegar
1 T. honey
1 t. finely shredded lemon peel
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/4 c. snipped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Directions:
Cook orzo according to package directions, drain.  Rinse with cold water; drain again.
In large bowl, combine orzo, cucumber, sweet pepper, and red onion.
In a small bowl, whisk together remaining ingredients and combine with other ingredients.
Cover and chill for at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Amy Hague Miller
Description: 
This is really good in the summer.
