Title:
WW Roasted Asparagus and Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/589457.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb asparagus trimmed
1 large red bell pepper cut into thick strips
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1 tsp grated lemon zest

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Spray nonstick baking sheet with nonstick spray
Put asparagus and bell pepper in large bowl.
Add lemon juice, oil, sald & pepper flakes; toss to coat.
Spread veggies in single layer on baking sheet.
Roast (shake pan occasionally) until tender (about 15mins)
Transfer to serving bowl and sprinkle with lemon zest.
