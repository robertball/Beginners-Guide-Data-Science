Title:
Zesty Sausage Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988762.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) package Bob Evans® Original Recipe or Zesty Hot Sausage Roll
1 (8 ounce) jar taco sauce
12 taco shells
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 large onion, chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
1/4 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 cup fresh cilantro sprigs and bell pepper triangles (optional)

Directions:
Crumble sausage into medium skillet; cook over medium-high heat until browned, stirring occasionally. Drain off any drippings. Stir in taco sauce. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; simmer 5 minutes.
To assemble tacos, place 2 tablespoons sausage mixture in each taco shell and top evenly with cheese, onion, tomatoes and lettuce. Serve hot. Refrigerate any leftover filling.

Submitter: 
Bob Evans®
Description: 
Try something new the next time you have tacos, and use sausage in place of the beef. These tacos are great, and everyone will love to make their own so there is less work for the cook.
