Title:
Sweet Southern Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head green cabbage,shredded
1 cup carrots, shredded
1 medium green pepper, diced
1 small onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
3 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon vinegar
3 tablespoons sweet pickles chopped, or sweet relish, drained
salt to taste
black pepper to taste
paprika to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the cabbage, carrots, green pepper, onion and garlic.
In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, sweet pickles.
Add the mayo mixture to the cabbage mixture, stirring until blended.
Season to taste with salt, and pepper.
Garnish with paprika.
Best to chill at leat 2 hours.

Submitter: 
JOYCE
Description: 
A creamy, slightly sweet cole slaw that will accomany any BBQ dish or even a good ol' burger and fries.
