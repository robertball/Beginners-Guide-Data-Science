Title:
Pineapple Cracker Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/333828.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can unsweetened crushed pineapple
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
2 green onions, chopped
Assorted crackers

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving 1 teaspoon juice. In a small mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Beat in the pineapple, green pepper, onions and reserved juice. Cover and chill for 1-1/2 hours. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Gloria  Wark
Description: 
Gloria Wark has been serving this thick mild-tasting mixture with snack crackers for more than 20 years. 'I usually have everything for the simple yet delicious dip on hand, so I can prepare it in a snap and refrigerate if for parties and get-togethers,' explains the Houston, Texas cook.
