Title:
Jan's Wheat-N-Honey Sandwich Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/492062.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups warm milk
1 cup warm water
1/4 cup honey
1 tbsp yeast
1 tsp salt
3 tbsp. butter
2 cups bread flour
4 cups whole wheat flour
4 tbsp. vital wheat gluten
3 tablespoons liquid lecithin (optional) This natural product is healthy for you and helps bread retain its freshness longer.
VARIATION:
Reduce wheat flour to 3.50 cups
Add in its place:
1/4 ground flax meal
1/4 cup wheat germ

Directions:
Place all ingredients in to bread machine and choose dough cycle.
When dough is finished, place on lightly oiled table and knead for a few minutes.
Place dough in a Wilton long loaf pan (16x4) or 2 8x4 loaf pans, to rise for about an hour.  Dough should reach the top of pan before baking.
Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 30 minutes or until an insta-read thermometer reaches 200 - 210 degrees.
When bread is finished, brush top of loaf with melted butter. Cool 10 minutes in the pan then invert onto rack to cool completely.
Cool completely before slicing.  Store in airtight bags.  Freezes well!

Description: 
Moist crumb, soft crust, lightly sweet and full of wheat goodness!  The perfect sandwich loaf!
