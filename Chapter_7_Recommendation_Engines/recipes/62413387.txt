Title:
Fresh Berry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Qt Berries
1 1/2 C. Sugar
1/4 C. Flour
1/4 tsp Salt
3 Tbls Butter

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 425F
Wash and prep Berries. Mix Sugar, Flour, & Salt.  Sift several times and pour over Berries.  Brush pie shell with butter. Fill pie shell with Berry mix and pour remaining Butter.  Add Lattice crust or Full top.
Bake 15 Mins and reduce heat to 350F and bake another 35-40 mins
