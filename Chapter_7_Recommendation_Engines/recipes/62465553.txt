Title:
Laurel's Mini Chocolate Chip Cheesecakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 chocolate chip cookies (medium size)
2 8 ounce packages cream cheese
2/3 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp almond extract
2 eggs
1/2 cup mini chocolate baking chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Place 24 paper or foil cupcake liners into muffin tins.
Place a chocolate chip cookie into the bottom of each cupcake cup.
Beat cream cheese, sugar, vanilla and almond extracts together until smooth and creamy. Add eggs and beat until combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
Fill each cupcake cup 2/3 to 3/4 full.
Bake for 17 minutes. Remove and cool.

Description: 
I made these wonderful little cheesecakes for my daughter's 16th birthday. They are delicious with or without a topping.
