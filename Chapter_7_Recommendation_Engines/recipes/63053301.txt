Title:
Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/897643.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package rice noodles
2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 cup vegetable or sesame oil
2 eggs
2 tablespoon white wine or rice vinegar
4 tablespoons fish sauce
4 tablespoons white sugar
1/4 tablespoon crushed red pepper
2 cups bean sprouts
1/4 cup crushed peanuts
3 green onions, chopped
1 lime, cut into wedges
fresh cilantro

Directions:
Pour boiling water over noodles, cover and let soak for 7 minutes until soft. Rinse with cold water. Drain, and set aside.
Heat sesame oil in a wok or large heavy skillet. Saute chicken with garlic until browned. Remove, and set aside.
Combine vinegar, fish sauce, sugar, & crushed red pepper mixture.  Set aside.
Heat oil in wok over medium-high heat. Crack eggs into hot oil, and cook until firm. Stir in chicken, and cook for 5 minutes. Add softened noodles, and vinegar, fish sauce, sugar and red pepper. Adjust seasonings to taste. Mix while cooking, until noodles are tender. Add bean sprouts, and mix for 3 minutes.
Add lime and cilantro after serving.
