Title:
Guatamalan Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb. Monterey Jack cheese, cut in strips
3 cups sour cream (or cottage cheese/ yogurt)
2 cans chopped green chiles
3 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.  Butter 1.5 quart casserole.
Mix cream and chiles together.  Season rice with salt and pepper.
Layer rice, sour cream and cheese strips in that order, with rice on top.
Bake for 30 minutes.  Sprinkle with extra cheddar cheese that last few minutes of cooking.
