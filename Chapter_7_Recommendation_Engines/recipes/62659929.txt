Title:
French's Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs ground beef (90% or leaner)
2 onions, chopped
2 cups ketchup
1 cup mustard
1/2 cup Worcestershire
24 slider buns

Directions:
Cook meat and onions. Drain. Mix in remaining ingredients, bring to a simmer for 5 mins or until thickened. Serve on (toasted?) slider buns.
