Title:
Gram's Best Date Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/722314.jpg
Ingredients:
Top & Bottom Crust:
2 1/4 cups quick oatmeal
1 1/2 cups AP flour
1 cup light brown sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup margerine or softened butter
Date Sauce:
1 lb. pkg. pitted baking dates (chopped)
1 cup light brown sugar
1 cup water
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
For the top & bottom crust:
In a large bowl mix all ingredients together into a crumb mixture.  Press half the mixture into a 10" X 10" square pan.
For the date sauce:
In a sauce pan bring water to a boil.
Add dates, brown sugar, and vanilla to the boiling water.  Lower heat to med. low.
While stirring, cook for about 5 min. or until sauce has a thick consistency.
Remove sauce from heat and spread evenly over the bottom crust.
Add remaining oatmeal crust mixture on top of sauce and press down slightly covering all date sauce.
On center rack, bake at 350 degrees for 45 min. or until slightly browned.
Remove from oven and cool competely.
Cut the squares into 16 servings.
To store:
Cover the cooled squares until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
Growing up this was my favorite treat that my Gram would make for us. These date squares have a gooey sweet date sauce that is centered between an oatmeal crumb crust and is so easy to make.
