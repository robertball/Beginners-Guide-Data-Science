Title:
7 Layer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c. spinach or salad greens
2 c. grape tomatoes, halved
1 c. red onion, chopped
1 1/4 c. frozen peas, thawed & drained
1 c. cheddar cheese, shredded
6 slices bacon, cooked crisp and chopped
3 hard-boiled eggs, quartered

Directions:
1. Layer in order listed above in a 2-quart serving bowl.
2. Garnish with bacon & eggs.
3. Serve with store bought Poppyseed or Ranch dressing OR with homemade dressing (listed below).

Submitter: 
Chevette
Description: 
This salad is one of my favorites. It's easy and pretty...perfect for your get-together!
