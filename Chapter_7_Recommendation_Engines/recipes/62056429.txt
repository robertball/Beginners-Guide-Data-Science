Title:
cheesy eggplant cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large eggplant peeled and diced
1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
1 cup dry stuffing mix
1 clove garlic crushed
2 tbsp diced onion
1 tsp italian herbs
1 egg
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel and dice the eggplant. Place in a microwave safe dish and cook for 3 minutes.  Stir well and cook 3 minutes more. Add 1/2 cup of cheese, garlic, onion, egg, italian herbs, salt and pepper.  Mix well.  Add the dry stuffing and stir well till the stuffing mix is thoroughly moistened.
Place in a 9x9 inch baking dish and bake at 350 for 15 minutes.  Add the remaining cup of cheese and bake 15 minutes more or until the cheese is completely melted.

Submitter: 
Renee' Levins
Description: 
I am not real big on eggplant but I cook it for my husband.  This dish was thrown together in a hurry and it turned out to be DELICIOUS.  I even ate the leftovers.
