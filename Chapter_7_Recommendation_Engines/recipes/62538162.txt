Title:
Pumpkin Ice Cream Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2 quarts vanilla ice cream
Crust:
3 cups crushed gingersnap cookies
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Mix crust ingredients and press into 9x13 pan.  Save some crumbs for the top of dessert. Chill crust until firm before filling.
Combine filling ingredients well, then stir in ice cream. Pour filling into chilled shell.  Keep frozen until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Shawn Hoffman Zimmerman
Description: 
This is a family favorite and a must- have over the holidays. The gingerbread cookie crust mixes perfectly with the pumpkin and spice ice cream.
