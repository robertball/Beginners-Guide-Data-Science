Title:
chicken tortilla casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. butter/oil
1 medium green pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can each: cream of mushroom, cream of chicken
2 cans (10-oz) rotel, or 1 can (16-oz) tomatoes and 4 oz green chiles
2 cups cubed cooked chicken (about 14 ounces)
12 corn tortillas, torn into bite-sized pieces
2 c. shredded cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Cook pepper and onion in butter for 5 minutes. Add soups, rotel, chicken, stirring.
Make 3 layers: tortillas, soup mix, cheese.
Bake for 40 minutes.
