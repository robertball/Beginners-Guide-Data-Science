Title:
Nancy's Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup vegetable oil
2 cups granulated sugar
2 cups grated raw zucchini
2 cups flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
1 Tbsp pure vanilla
1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Butter and flour 2 loaf pans.
Beat eggs, sugar, oil and vanilla till thick, mix in zucchini.
Sift dry ingredients together and add to zucchini mixture, mix until incorporated, add nuts mix well.
Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool in pan on rack for 15 minutes. Remove bread from pan, and completely cool

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
Light and fluffy zucchini bread, that's been a family favorite for over 50 years.
