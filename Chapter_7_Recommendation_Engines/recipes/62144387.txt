Title:
Southern Oyster Rockefeller Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen whole fresh oysters
1 1/2 cups chopped yellow onion
1/4 cup finely chopped celery
3 tbs butter or margarine
1 3/4 teas spoon sugar
1/2 teas spoon salt
1/2 teas spoon cayenne peper
1/4 cup pernod
1/2 pound frozen chopped spinach thawed and drained
1 1/2 tbls spoon finely chooped anchovies
2 1/2 tbls half and half
1/4 pound of velveeta cubed
5 tbls bread crumbs divided

Directions:
boil or stem whole oysters for 3 to 4 minutes. Set aside. Ina medium sauce pan, saute onions and celery in butter.Cover and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes over low heat. Add sugar, salt cayenne pepper and Pernod. Stir well.Add spinach and anchovies.Gradually add half and half.Ater spinach and milk had heated add velveeta cheese. Add 3 tbls of bread crumbs.Stir well.Spoon over whole oysters that have been placed in cleaned oyster shells.Sprinkle with remaining bread crumbs and bake at 450 for 15 to 20 minutes.If you are not serving right away refrigerate and bake for 45 minutes.Sprinkle bread crumbs right before baking.
