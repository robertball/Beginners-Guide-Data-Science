Title:
Fig and Jalapeno Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2387106.jpg
Ingredients:
2 jalapenos, chopped
4 tablespoons of fig jelly
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
4 corn tortillas

Directions:
Spread fig jelly on one side of a tortilla.
Place tortilla on griddle at low heat.
Add chopped jalapenos and shredded cheese to tortilla.
Cover with another tortilla and cook for about 5 min.
Flip and do the same.
