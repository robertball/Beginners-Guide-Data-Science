Title:
Soy-Free Chicken Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Chicken Stock
2 Tbsp Coconut Aminos
1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
¼ Cup Peanut Butter
1 Tbsp Lime Juice (Or juice from half of a fresh Lime)
1 Tbsp Rice Wine (I use Aji-Mirin Brand)
1 Tbsp Thai Chili Sauce
1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
1 tsp Garlic, Minced
4 oz Rice Noodles, cooked using directions on package minus 1 minute and rinsed
1 Tbsp Canola Oil
1.5 Pounds Chicken Breast, Diced
½ Cup Bean Sprouts
? Cup Carrots, Shredded or Julienne
2 Green Onions, Chopped
Chopped Peanuts (Optional)
Cilantro (Optional)
Sriracha (Optional)

Directions:
Whisk together Chicken Stock, Coconut Aminos, Brown Sugar, Peanut Butter, Lime Juice, Rice Wine, Thai Chili Sauce, Ground Ginger and Garlic and set aside.
Heat a skillet over medium heat and add Canola Oil.
Add carrots and cook for one minute.
Add chicken breast and cook until chicken is cooked through.
Add the precooked noodles to the skillet and then pour the sauce over the noodles.
Slightly reduce the heat to medium-low to not overcook the noodles.
Let it cook while stirring frequently until the mixture begins to thicken (about 3-5 minutes)
Stir the Bean Sprouts and Green Onions and remove from the heat.
Optional: Serve and top with Chopped Peanuts, Cilantro, or Sriracha.
