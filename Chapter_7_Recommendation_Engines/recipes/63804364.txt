Title:
Crock Pot Pumpkin Angel Food Cake with Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. pumpkin, canned
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
16 oz. angel food cake mix
14 oz. sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
1.Preheat crock pot on high.
2.Prepare the angel food cake mix according to package.
3.In another bowl, mix together pumpkin, spice, and vanilla.
4.Fold pumpkin mix into angel food mix until well incorporated.
5.Spray crock pot with baking spray to avoid sticking.
6.Place unopened clean paperless sweetened condensed milk can in the center of a 5 qt or larger crock pot.
7.Pour cake mix into crock pot around can.
8.Cover. Cook on High for 2 hours.
9.After 2 hours, check cake with a toothpick to make sure it is cooked through.
10.It should also be pulling away from the sides of the crock pot.
11.Carefully remove the sweetened conensed milk can and gently flip the cake out on to a baking rack to cool.
12.Return the can and 2 cups of hot water (surrounding the can) to the crock pot for the caramel to continue to cook for 2 hours, while the cake cools.
13.When done, remove the can and allow to cool. Carefully open to reveal the caramel. Drizzle caramel over the cake to serve
