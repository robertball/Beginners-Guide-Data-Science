Title:
Rainbow Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup finely chopped apple
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups white sugar
2 cups shredded zucchini, yellow squash and carrots
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
1 pint fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease 4 mini-loaf pans.
In a large bowl, beat together the eggs, oil, vanilla, and sugar. Fold in the apples and vegetables. Beat in the flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, pumpkin pie spice.  Gently fold in the blueberries. Transfer to the prepared mini-loaf pans.
Bake 50 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a knife inserted in the center of a loaf comes out clean. Cool 20 minutes in pans, then turn out onto wire racks to cool completely.
