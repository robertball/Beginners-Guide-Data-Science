Title:
Hawaiian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/29216.jpg
Ingredients:
1 broiler/fryer chicken (3 to 4 pounds), cut up
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup ketchup
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1 pinch ground ginger

Directions:
Place chicken in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish; drizzle with butter. Drain pineapple, reserving juice; set pineapple aside. In a saucepan, combine brown sugar, cornstarch and salt; stir in reserved pineapple juice until smooth. Add the ketchup, vinegar, chili powder, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce and ginger. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Pour over chicken.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour. Uncover and bake for 30 minutes. Top with reserved pineapple; bake 15 minutes longer or until chicken juices run clear and pineapple is heated through.
