Title:
Comforting Crockpot Chili Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1 large onion, finely chopped
1 (5-oz. can pinto beans, rinsed, drained
1 (8 1/2 oz) can corn, rinsed, drained
1 (15-oz) jar tomato sauce
1 (14 1/2 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 (10-oz) can diced tomato and green chillies
1 TBSP Chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Cook ground turkey until no longer pink.  Drain and transfer to crockpot.
Add remaining ingredients and stir to combine
Cover, cook on high 4 hours.  Give one quick stir halfway through cooking time.

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
This recipe makes 8 servings.  Each serving is 220 caolories and 6 grams of fat.
