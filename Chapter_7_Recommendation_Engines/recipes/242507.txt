Title:
Sneak-Em In Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2368167.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
2 slices Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup chopped broccoli
1/4 cup chopped zucchini
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 tablespoon chopped jalapeno pepper
3 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Arrange bread slices on a work surface. Place Cheddar cheese slices on top of 1 bread slice. Layer broccoli, zucchini, green bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper on top of cheese. Place the second piece of bread on top of vegetables, creating a sandwich.
Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Melt 1 1/2 tablespoons butter in the hot skillet. Add sandwich to skillet, cover with a lid, and cook until bread is golden, about 45 seconds. Flip sandwich, add remaining butter, and cook until bottom is golden, about 45 seconds more.

Submitter: 
Pegah Motaleb
Description: 
Are your kids not eating their vegetables? Sneak them in their favorite snack. Add any of your favorite vegetables. If you do not like spice, do not add the jalapeno.
