Title:
Chocolious Ice Cream (dairy free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1183568.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups hemp milk (original )
3 Tablespoons chia seeds
1/2 cup cacao powder
1/2 cup agave
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 teaspoons almond extract
2 ounces semi-sweet chocolate (baking bar)
1/2 teaspoon salt
Dash of pepper

Directions:
Heat milk in a sauce pan on medium flame for 5 minutes until warm to touch.
Stir chia seeds thoroughly into milk making sure the seeds do not clump together.
Let milk/chia seed mixture sit for 2-3 hours, stir occasionally throughout the hour.
Add remaining ingredients.
Blend thoroughly in Blendtec or Vitamix blender.
Cool in refrigerator for 3 hours.
Pour into ice cream maker machine and blend 25-30 minutes according to directions.

Description: 
Chocolate ice cream with healthy rich chia seeds and hemp milk! A healthy alternative ice cream for vegans and lactose intolerant individuals.
