Title:
Balsamic Cherry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 cups dry red wine
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 teaspoons fresh rosemary, minced
1 cup dried cherries
salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
Reduce until liquid thickens and becomes syrupy.

Submitter: 
AJ
Description: 
This sauce is perfect for peppered beef, pork, and lamb.
