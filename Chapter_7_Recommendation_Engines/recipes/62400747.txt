Title:
Bananas Foster ala JIM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/431129.jpg
Ingredients:
4 bananas cut in 1/4 inch diagonal slices
4 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 cup 151 dark rum
Vanilla bean ice cream

Directions:
1) Melt the butter and brown sugar in a large sautee pan over medium heat. Stir until smooth
2)Add the bananas coating them well. Sautee gently for 3-5 mins until tender
3) Sprinkle the rum over and carefully flambe. Allow alcohol to burn off.
4) Spoon the bananas and sauce into bowls and top with the ice cream
