Title:
Spaghetti Sauce (Better Homes & Garden Recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic
1 large onion, chopped
2 Tbsp cooking oil
2 cans (28oz) crushed tomatoes
1 1/2 c. water
1 can (6oz) tomato paste
1 Tbsp sugar
2 tsp instant beef bouillon granules
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp dried oregano, crushed
1/2 tsp dried basil, crushed
1/4 tsp pepper
1 large bay leaf (removed before serving)
1 can (4oz) sliced mushrooms, undrained (optional)

Directions:
Peel & mince garlic.  In a 3-quart saucepan, cook the garlic and the onion ini the oil until onion is tender.
Add rest of ingredients except mushrooms.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer uncovered for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
Remove & discard bay leaf.
Stir in can of mushrooms and simmer another 15-30 minutes.
