Title:
GRILLED TURKEY W/LEMON HERB MARINADE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb breast of turkey (from deli--4 1/2" slices)
1/3 vegetable oil
1/4 c white wine vinegar
1/4 c lemon juice
1 Tbl instant minced onion
1/2 tsp rosemary
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
combine marinade ingred & pour over turkey. cover & refrig 4hrs or overnight. grill for 10min, turning once
