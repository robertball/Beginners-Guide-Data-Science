Title:
K-Dub's Chopped Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of romaine lettuce, finely chopped
1 16 oz can of kidney beans
1 16 oz can of garbanzo beans
1 cucumber, peeled and diced
2 tomatoes, diced
3/4 cup diced Monterey Jack cheese
3/4 cup diced Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup of balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 cup of olive oil
Salt and freshly ground pepper for taste

Directions:
Toss all salad ingredients together in a salad bowl.
In a separate bowl mix together the balsamic vinegar and mustard. Slowly whisk in the olive oil and season with salt and pepper.  Toss the salad with the vinaigrette and serve.

Submitter: 
K-Dub
Description: 
A light and refreshing salad. Good for you and tasty!
