Title:
Lite Italian Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
3oz. corn oil
1 tbls.sugar
1/4 tsp.black pepper
1/4 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Wisk all ingredients together and pour into a shaker bottle.
Do not refridgerate because it may thicken.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
A Refreshing Lite Dressing made from scratch that is Great on any Tossed Salad or Pasta Salad!Try my Easy Tortellini Salad recipe with this.
