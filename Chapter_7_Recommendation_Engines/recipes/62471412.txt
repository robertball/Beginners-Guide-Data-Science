Title:
McCormick Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. McCormick Swedish Meatball Seasoning and Sauce Mix
1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
1/3 cup Water
2 tbsp. Oil
2 cubs Milk
10 oz Egg Noodles

Directions:
Pour Sauce Mix into bowl and set aside.
Pour Seasoning Mix into large bowl and mix with beef and water.
Shape into 1 1/2 inch meatballs.
Heat oil in large skillet on medium heat.  Add meatballs; cook 8 minutes or until cooked through, turning frequently.  Remove meatballs; drain fat.
Stir milk and reserved sauce mix into skillet.  Stirring frequently, simmer on medium heat and simmer 1 minute or until thickened.  Add meatballs to sauce; cook until heated through.  Serve over noodles or rice.
