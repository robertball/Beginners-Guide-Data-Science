Title:
Stawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb stawberrys
1 tub cool whip (I use light)
1 Package stawberry gel
1 graham cracker crust

Directions:
Cut stawberry's into pieces
Add stawberry's and gel in a big bowl
Mix well
Add to pie crust
Top with Cool whip

Submitter: 
Lynnette Darnall
Description: 
Very light pie for a hot summer day everyone will love
