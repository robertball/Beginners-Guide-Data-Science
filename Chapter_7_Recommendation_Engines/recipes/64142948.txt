Title:
Chocolate Chip Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1373446.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening (Crisco)
1/2 c. butter (softened, no substitutes)
2 large eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1-1/2 c. granulated white sugar
3 Tbsp. dark molasses
2-1/4 c. all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 12-oz. pkg. semi-sweet chocolate chips (may substitute milk chocolate or white chocolate chips)
1 cup slivered **almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine flour, baking soda and salt in bowl.  Set aside.
Beat butter and shortening until light and fluffy.
Add eggs and vanilla.  Beat well.
In a separate bowl, combine sugar and molasses.  Blend well with spoon, then add to butter mixture.  Whip for 2-3 minutes until well incorporated.
Gradually beat in flour mixture.
Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
Drop by rounded tablespoon onto sprayed or greased cookie sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
Allow to stand on pan for 2 minutes once removed from oven for a crispier cookie.
Remove to wire rack to cool completely.
