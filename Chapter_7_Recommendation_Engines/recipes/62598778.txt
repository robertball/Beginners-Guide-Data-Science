Title:
Basil Chicken and Angel Hair Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package angel hair pasta
2 t olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1/2-3/4 c white wine
2 (15 oz) cans diced tomatoes
1 (4 oz) can tomato paste
1/2 c water
4 small boneless skinless chicken breast
1/4 c dried basil
1 T dried oregano
1/2 t salt
1/4 c parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat.
Saute the onions and garlic for 2 min.
Add white wine, simmer 2 min.
Add basil and oregano, simmer 2 min.
Stir in the tomatoes, tomato paste & water, mix well.
Top with chicken breasts.  Reduce heat to medium, and cover skillet. Simmer, stirring ocaisionally, until chicken is cooked through.
While chicken is cooking, boil pasta to al dente.
Shred chicken, return to sauce.  Simmer 2 min.
Toss sauce with hot cooked angel hair pasta to coat. Serve with Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
Description: 
Shredded chicken breast is simmered in a white wine tomato-basil sauce and tossed with fine strands of angel hair pasta.Modified from: Basil Chicken over Angel Hair
