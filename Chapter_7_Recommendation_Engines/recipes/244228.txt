Title:
Skinny Almond Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2313916.jpg
Ingredients:
15 ice cubes, or as desired
3/4 cup almond milk
1/3 banana
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, almond milk, banana, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.
