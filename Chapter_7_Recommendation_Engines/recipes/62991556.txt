Title:
Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup heavy cream
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate
2 tsp vanilla extract
Toppings:
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup slated pistachio pieces
1/2 cup shredded coconut
2 tbsp Hawaiian sea salt or other specialty salt

Directions:
Bring cream to a low boil. Remove from heat. Stir in chocoalte until melted and smooth. Stir in vanilla. Pour into ungreased backing dish, cover and chill.
Remove from fridge 15 - 20 minutes before you want to form truffles. With a melon scooper or sppon, scamre 1 - 2 tbsp of chocolate out of the pan and roll into balls. Place on  tray lined with parchment paper. Repeat until out of chocolate. Cover with plastic wrap and chill for 15 minutes or until firm again.
Roll each truffle in the topping of your choosing and place in parchment -lined storage container. Keep chilled
