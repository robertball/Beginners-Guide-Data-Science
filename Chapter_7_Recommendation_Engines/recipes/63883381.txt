Title:
Best Ever Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077333.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c all purpose flour
2 tsp dried thyme
2 tsp paprika
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
2 tsp lemon juice
1/2 c buttermilk
8 bone-in chicken pieces
oil for frying

Directions:
In a Ziplock bag, combine flour, thyme, paprika, & salt. In a shallow bowl combine egg, milk, & lemon juice.
Place chicken pieces in flour mix, seal bag, & shake to coat. Put chicken pieces in egg mix. Put back in flour mix. Fry.
Dip chicken in buttermilk. Dip in flour mix. Place bone-in side up in pans. Bake uncovered at 425 for 25 mins. Turn & bake 10 more mins.
In sauce pan, add remaining buttermilk to soup. Stir & heat through. Serve with chicken.
