Title:
Pineapple Chicken Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz can crushed pineapple
1/2 cup chili sauce or hot style ketchup
2 T brown sugar
1 T prepared mustard

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving 1/4 cup of syrup.
Combine pineapple, reserved syrup, chili sauce or ketchup, sugar and mustard.
Brush or spoon onto grilled chicken last 5 to 10 minutes of cooking.

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
This is a delicious glaze for grilled chicken.
