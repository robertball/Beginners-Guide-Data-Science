Title:
Cabbage Soup & Spaetzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Tabatchnik brand potato soup
1 box Tabatchnik brand cabbade soup
1c water
14oz frozen baby lima beans
salt to taste
14oz box spaetzle
water

Directions:
Prepare the spaetzle according to package instructions and drain.
Meanwhile, combine both soups, 1c water, lima beans, and salt in a large pot.  Bring to a boil, and then back down to a simmer.
Stir in spaetzle and serve.
