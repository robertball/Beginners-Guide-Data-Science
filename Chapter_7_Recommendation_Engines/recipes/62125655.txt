Title:
Chili Lime Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz pkg of frozen corn, thawed
2 Tbl butter
1 1/2 tsp Chili powder
1 Lime, juiced
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in medium sauce pan over medium-high heat.
Add corn and stir.
Add chili powder and stir.
Add lime juice and stir.
Add salt and pepper to your tastes.

Submitter: 
Wannabe Gardener
Description: 
This easy side dish adds a zesty kick to your meal. We like to eat it with grilled beef.
