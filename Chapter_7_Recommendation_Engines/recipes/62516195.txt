Title:
Super Cheesy Double Cheese Mac'n'Cheese. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. elbow macaroni, uncooked
1 pkg. (12 oz.) creamed cottage cheese
1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup flour
2 cups milk
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. white pepper
1/2 cup soft bread crumbs (optional)
2 Tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Cook and drain macaroni according to directions.
Alternate layers of macaroni and Cheddar and cottage cheese in greased 2-quart baking dish, beginning with macaroni and ending with cheese.
Melt the 1/4 cup butter in saucepan; stir in flour to make a smooth mixture. Blend in milk gradually. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.
Cook on low until sauce has thickened, about 2 minutes. Add salt and pepper.
Pour sauce over macaroni and cheese.
Mix bread crumbs with melted butter. Sprinkle butter or mixture over macaroni and cheese.
Bake at 375 F for 30 to 35 min.
