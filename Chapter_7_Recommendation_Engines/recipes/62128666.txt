Title:
Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs. lean ground turkey
1 packet low-sodium taco seasoning
1 (16 oz) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15 oz.) can diced tomatoes with jalepenos
1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1.5 cups biscuit mix
1 cup milk
2 eggs, beaten
1 (2.25 oz.) can sliced black olives, drained
1/4 cup sliced, pickled jalapenos, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Brown meat, and add taco seasoning, kidney beans, and tomatoes.
Place mixture in an 8-inch square baking dish and top with the cheddar cheese.
In a bowl, combine the biscuit mix, milk, and eggs. Fold in the olives and jalapenos.  Pour this mixture over the meat and cheese.
Bake for 20 â€“ 25 minutes, or until lightly browned and a toothpick inserted in the center of the topping comes out clean.
