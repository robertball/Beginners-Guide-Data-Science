Title:
Cilantro Lime Basmati Brown Rice (Chipotle Copycat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Unbooked basmati brown rice
4 cups vegetable broth
2 garlic cloves
1/4 cup lime juice
1/2 - 1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 - 1 tsp salt
1-1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp ground white pepper
1-1/4 cups fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Add rice and vegetable broth to 3 quart panbring to boil over high heat, give it a quick stir, cover and lower heat to lowest simmer setting. Continue to simmer on low for 45 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand covered for 5 minutes, or until any remaining liquid in bottom of pan is absorbed. Fluff with fork.
While rice cooks, whisk together  the garlic, lime juice, olive oil, salt, oregano, cumin, and pepper. Pour over cooked rice, add the cilantro, and lightly toss with fork until combined. Taste and add salt, if desired.
