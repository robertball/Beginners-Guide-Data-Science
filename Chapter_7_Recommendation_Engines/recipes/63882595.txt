Title:
Creamy Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
3/4 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup butter
2 cups light cream

Directions:
Butter a 8x8 square pan.
Combine sugar, corn syrup, butter and half of the cream into a large saucepan.
Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Stir in remaining cream.
Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until a candy thermometer reads 245F(118C) or until a small amount dropped into very cold water forms a firm ball.
Immediately spread mixture evenly in pan. Cool and cut into 1 inch squares.
