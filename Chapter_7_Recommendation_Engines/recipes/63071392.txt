Title:
Baked Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887251.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dried bread crumbs
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
1 egg, beaten
5 skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
In a medium bowl mix together the bread crumbs, Italian seasoning and Parmesan cheese.
Dip chicken breasts in egg, then in bread crumb mixture to coat.
Place coated chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake at 350ยบ  for 30 to 35 minutes or until juices run clear, turning over chicken 5 minutes before removing from oven.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Using Parmesan cheese gives a little more texture to breaded chicken.Modified from: Simply Parmesan Chicken
