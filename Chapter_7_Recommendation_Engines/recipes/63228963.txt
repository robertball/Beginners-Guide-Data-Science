Title:
Tangy Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can[46 ozs.]pineapple juice
1 can[12ozs.]frozen orange juice concentrate,thawed
3/4 cup lemonade concentrate,thawed
1 cup water,divided
1/2 cup sugar
2 large ripe bananas
1 pkg.frozen unsweetened strawberries,thawed
2 liter bottle gingerale,chilled

Directions:
Combine pineapple juice,orange juice concentrate,lemonade concentrate,1/2 cup water and sugar.
Place bananas,strawberries and remaining water in a blender,process until smooth.Stir into juice mixture.Refigerate.Before serving add chilled gingerale to juices.
