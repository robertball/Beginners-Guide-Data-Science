Title:
Oven Fried Chicken III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 to 3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
1 cup panko breading or cracker crumbs
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
In a medium bowl or gallon size resealable plastic bag, mix together the breading, garlic powder, salt, pepper, thyme and paprika.
Coat the chicken pieces with mayonnaise. Place chicken pieces in bowl/bag with breading mixture and coat/shake until well coated. Place chicken pieces in a lightly greased cookie pan on a cookie cooling rack,  and bake for 45 minutes to one hour, or until juices run clear.
