Title:
Tasty Black Beans II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups of water
500 grams of uncooked black beans
1 tsp onion powder
1 jalapeno chile, finely minced
1 tsp salt (optional)
1 cup finely chopped red onion
1 cup finely chopped bell pepper
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
6 - 9 cloves garlic, minced
1-1/2 tbsp mild chili powder
1 tbsp ground coriander
1-1/2 tsp ground cumin
3/4 cup tomato sauce (or 210 gram box/175 ml can)
2 cups water
2 large or 4 small cubes of chicken or veggie bullion
Juice from 2 limes.
Salt & pepper to taste
4 cups of cooked rice

Directions:
Rinse and sort beans.  Place in large pot and soak over night in 8 cups of water.
Add onion powder, jalapeno, and salt to beans. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to a simmer.  Cook beans for 1 hour or until soft. Set aside un drained.
Combine onion and bell pepper in a large skillet or dutch oven and saute over medium heat in vegetable oil until completely softened, about 6 - 7 minutes.
Add garlic and next 3 ingredients, stirring constantly for 2 minutes.
Add water and vegetable or chicken bullion cubes and black beans to skillet.
Simmer briskly uncovered, stirring often until sauce is thickened, about 10 - 15 minutes.
Add lime juice, and add salt & pepper if desired.
Serve over rice.  Garnish with chopped cilantro and cut limes.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
These dressed up black beans are a great side dish or can make a meal when served over rice.Modified from: Tasty Black Beans I
