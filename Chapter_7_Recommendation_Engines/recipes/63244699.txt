Title:
Lingonberry Sauce for Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon salted butter
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 jar of Ikea's Lingonberry jam
1/4 cup dry red wine
2 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1 tsp. fresh thyme leaves (may substitute 1/2 tsp. dried thyme instead)

Directions:
1. Saute the onion in the butter until translucent but firm.
2. Add the jam, wine, and vinegar. Stir and simmer on low heat for 10 to 12 minutes until thickened. Stir occasionally to prevent scorching.
3. Add the thyme and simmer for 5 minutes more, stirring occasionally.
4. Spoon over a pork roast or grilled chops.

Submitter: 
qwertycook
Description: 
Made with Ikea's Lingonberry jam. The wine, vinegar, and thyme really bring out the unique taste of the lingonberries. Yummy!
