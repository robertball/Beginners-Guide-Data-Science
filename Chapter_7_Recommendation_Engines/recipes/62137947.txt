Title:
Chocolate Chip Pecan Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
3/4 c. white sugar
1 c. brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 t. vanilla
1 t. ginger
1 heaping spoonful of sour cream
2 c. flour
1 bag (12 oz.) chocolate chips
1/4 t. salt
pecans

Directions:
Peheat the oven to 375.
Cream the butter and the sugars. Beat in the eggs and vanilla. Add the ginger and sour cream. Mix well. Slowly add in the flour until you have achieved the desired consistency. Mix in the chocolate chips.
Scoop onto a cookie sheet in approximately 1 tablespoon sized spheres. Press a whole pecan into each cookie.
Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown around the edges. Do not overbake.
Pan will be hot when removed from the oven. Allow to cool for 6 minutes before serving. Serve with milk.

Submitter: 
pianocook
Description: 
The ginger adds an unexpected flair to this semi-traditional chocolate chip cookie, allowing the taster to experience a galvanization of their inner passion for this eastern spice fuzed with a classic family favorite.
