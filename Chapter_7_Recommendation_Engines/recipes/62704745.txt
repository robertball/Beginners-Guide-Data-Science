Title:
Mom Dem's REAL Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup butter
2 tbsp light corn syrup
2 (2oz) envelopes Nestle chocolate (chocobake)
dash salt
1/4 cup milk
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In a small saucepan combine sugar, butter, chocolate, corn syrup and salt.  Mix well. Cook over medium heat stirring constantly until sugar dissolves.  Add milk. Bring to a boil and continue stirring. Remove from heat.  Add vanilla.

Submitter: 
Donna Fary Rice Bobchin
Description: 
This recipe is from my BFF's mom. It reminds me of when I was a kid. She always made the best ice cream sundaes...This recipe is why!
