Title:
Chipits Chewy Milk Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup melted butter
2 cups lightly packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 Tbsp. hot water
2 2/3 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups Chipits milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Beat butter, brown sugar, eggs and hot water; mix well.
Stir in flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Mix in chocolate chips.
Drop from spoon onto cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 min.
