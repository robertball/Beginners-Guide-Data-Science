Title:
Chicken, Spinach & Mango Salad with Warm Tomato Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces boneless skinless chicken breasts, bite-size pieces
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt'sÂ® Whole Tomatoes, coarsely chopped
1/4 cup French salad dressing
4 cups fresh spinach leaves
2 mangoes, cut into cubes
2 medium avocados, cut into cubes

Directions:
Brown chicken in hot oil until golden.
Add and mix tomatoes with chicken; bring to a boil. Reduce heat.
Simmer uncovered for 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink in center. Add French dressing; heat through. Arrange spinach, mangos and avocados on serving dishes. Top evenly with chicken mixture; toss gently to coat.

Submitter: 
Hunt's
Description: 
This luscious, colorful salad is full of interesting flavors and textures.
