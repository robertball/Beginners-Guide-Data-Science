Title:
Baked Spaghetti Squah and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/2 cups cooked spaghetti squash (about 2 small)
1 TBS butter
1 TBS olive oil
1/4 cup minced onion
1/4 cup flour
2 cups milk
1 cup broth
8oz mild cheddar
salt and pepper
4 cups baby spinach
1/8 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 375 degrees
2. cut squash in half lengthwise; remove and discard seeds. Place squash on baking sheet.  Bake until tender, about 1 hour.  Separated strands of squash with fork and place in a medium bowl.
3. Heat butter and oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add onions and cook about 2 minutes. Stir in flour. Reduce heat to low and cook, stirring continually 3-4 minutes.
4. Add milk and broth and continue whisking, raising heat to medium-high until it comes to a boil and becomes smooth and thick, about 2 minutes stirring; season with salt and pepper.
5. Once it becomes thick, remove from heat, add cheddar cheese and mix well until cheese is melted.  Adjust salt and pepper to taste, add cooked spaghetti squash and baby spinach, pour into a baking dish and sprinkle parmesan cheese on top. Bake until bubbly and golden about 25-30 minutes.
