Title:
Chokecherry Liqueur Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chokecherries
3/4 cup sugar
375 ml vodka

Directions:
Sort and wash berries
Place in a bottle with a tight-fitting lid.
Add sugar and vodka
Let stand one month in a warm, dark location, tipping bottle daily until sugar dissolves.
Allow to stand one more month.
Strain well. Serve.
A liquor bottle with 4 flat sides works well. Lay the bottle on its side and turn to a new side each day

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Wild berry flavored liqueur!
