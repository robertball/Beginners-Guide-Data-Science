Title:
Orange Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7893562.jpg
Ingredients:
2.5 beef roast
2 Tbsp neutral oil (canola, corn, peanut)
2 tsp Salt, divided
1 medium white onion, coarsely chopped
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1/2 Tbsp Allspice
1/2 Tbsp Cinnamon
1Tbsp Ginger
1 cup sweet red wine
2 cups water
1 can tomato paste
1 Tbsp honey
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes.
2 Mandarin oranges peeled, pieces halved
1/3 cup Craisins
9 dried prunes
1/2 cup orange juice

Directions:
Season beef with 1 tsp salt
In a 4 quart Dutch oven, heat the oil until shimmering
Sear beef 3 minutes per side. Remove.
Soften onions and garlic. 5 min.
Add allspice, ginger, and cinnamon. Stir 1 min.
Deglaze with wine.
Add water, tomato paste, honey, red pepper, remaining salt.
Cut the beef into 2cm cubes and return to pot.
Boil and reduce to simmer 1 hour.
Add orange juice, oranges, and dried fruit and simmer another 20 minutes without lid.
Best served hot over rice.

Submitter: 
JohnCrane
Description: 
A Moroccan-inspired dish of warming and citrus flavors that that really ended up better than I expected.
