Title:
Bachelor's Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound groumd meat (beef, turkey, sausage, chicken)
3 grated potatoes, good size potatoes,
4 grated carrots
1 can or 1 1/2 cups whole kernal corn
1 can or 1 1/2 cups green beans
1 cup chopped onions
8 to 10 slices bacon

Directions:
Brown ground meat and spread in the bottom of the baking dish.
Spread 1/4 cup chopped onions over meat layer.
Peel and grate potatoes, you want about 3/4 of an inch of potatoes. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Spread 1/4 cup chopped onions over potatoes.
Peel and grate carrots and layer the carrots over the potatoes. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Spread 1/4 cup chopped onions over carrots.
Spread 1 can or 1 1/2 cups whole kernal corn over carrots. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Add 1/4 cup chopped onion.
Spread 1 can or 1 1/2 cups green beans over corn.
Cover the top with slices of uncooked bacon.
Lay a piece of foil over top and bake 375* for 40 minutes then uncover and bake until complely hot and the bacon, potaotes and carrots are cooked.

Submitter: 
Kitten
Description: 
This is so easy and inexpensive and quick to put together. I use a glass 8 X 8 or a small rectangle baking dish. The layers show through the glass and look very inviting. If you want to use a 9 x 13 baking dish, just double everything.
