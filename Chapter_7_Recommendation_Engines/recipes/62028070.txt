Title:
angel hair pasta with chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil divided
1 pound boneless skinless chicken cut into 1 inch pieces
1 carrot sliced
1 onion diced
1 red pepper diced
1 cup mushrooms sliced
1 (10 ounce) package frozen broccoli florets thawed
4 garlic cloves minced
12 ounces  angel hair pasta cooked and drained
1 14 1/2 ounce can chicken broth (you might need more  broth if it is dry or you are reheating it)
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon of oil. Add chicken cook 5 minutes or until no longer pink. Remove to bowl. In same skillet heat remaining oil add carrot,onion,and pepper cook 4 minutes then add mushrooms, broccoli and garlic cook 2 minutes. Add broth, basil and cheese to skillet and return chicken to skillet simmer 4 minutes. Top pasta with sauce, and additional cheese if desired.
