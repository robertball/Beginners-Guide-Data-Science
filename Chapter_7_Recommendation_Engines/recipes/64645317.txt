Title:
Easy & Dairy-Free Spicy Mustard Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 red potatoes, diced
3 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 red onion, minced
1/4 cup spicy brown mustard, or to taste
Fresh dill to taste
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Minced garlic (optional)

Directions:
Chop up potatoes and place them in a large pot. Cover potatoes with water. To taste, add salt, pepper, and garlic (optional). Boil over high heat.
Once boiling, adjust heat to medium-low and wait for potatoes to soften, about 15-20 minutes. Once soft, drain potatoes in collander and rise with cool water.
In a large bowl, mix together remaining ingredients. Cover and refrigerate overnight to soften onion flavor.
