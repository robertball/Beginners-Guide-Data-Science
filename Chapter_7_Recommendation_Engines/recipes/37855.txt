Title:
Sparkling Champagne Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981624.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups boiling water
1 (6 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Sparkling White Grape Flavor Gelatin
2 cups cold orange juice
3 cups cold ginger ale
1 (750 milliliter) bottle cold champagne
ice cubes

Directions:
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved.  Stir in cold orange juice.  Cool to room temperature.
Just before serving, pour into punch bowl.  Add cold ginger ale, cold champagne and ice cubes.
