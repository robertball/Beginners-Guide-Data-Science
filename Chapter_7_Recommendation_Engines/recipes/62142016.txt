Title:
Holiday Pineapple Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can pineapple slices
1 pkg. of any fruit flavored sugar-free gelatin
1 cup boiling water

Directions:
Drain juice from pineapples, leaving the pineapples in the can.  Dissolve gelatin in boiling water, then pour into the can over the pineapples.  Refrigerate until firm.  Use a knife to loosen the gelatin and get it out of the can.  Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Allison Buckley
Description: 
A very quick & simple extra something for holiday meals.  My mother-in-law gave me this super easy recipe and I love it!
