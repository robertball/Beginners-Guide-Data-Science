Title:
Special Edition Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1059364.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Brown sugar
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. Quick oats
1/2 C. Peanut butter
1/2 C. Medium shredded coconut
1/2 C. Chocolate chips
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
Mix butter and sugars together combining them to form a smooth paste
Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture and beat well after each addition until smooth and creamy
Combine quick oats, chocolate chips and coconut adding each amount separately to wet ingredients and mix until thoroughly combined
Stir in dry ingredients and mix to form a smooth cookie dough
Roll small amounts of cookie dough in your hand and place on cookie sheet 2 inches apart
Flatten each round with a fork dipped in flour and press out to flatten on cookie sheet
Bake in preheated oven for 10  minutes until lightly browned
Remove from oven and place on a wire rack to cool completely

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
An old favourite with the added goodness of gooey chocolate chips and coconut  to make a delicious snack for lunch time.
