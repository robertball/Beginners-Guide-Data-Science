Title:
Noodle Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb egg noodle, cooked
5 eggs
4 c half and half
1/4 c brown sugar
2 t vanilla
1/2 t cinnamon
1 1/2 T kosher salt
1/2 t pepper
1 c ricotta cheese
1 c golden raisins

Directions:
Cook egg noodles, whisk all the rest together.
Mix and bake 350 for 45 min.
