Title:
Watermelon Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3566072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 center-cut slice watermelon, about 2 inches thick
Toppings:
seedless red grapes, halved
cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
cherries, pitted and halved
crumbled feta cheese
chopped fresh mint

Directions:
Arrange watermelon on large plate or cutting board. Sprinkle with desired toppings. Cut into wedges to serve.

Submitter: 
Target Test Kitchen
Description: 
A slice of watermelon cut into wedges is the 'crust' of this summery pizza. Top with fresh fruits and veggies for refreshing flavor and crisp texture.
