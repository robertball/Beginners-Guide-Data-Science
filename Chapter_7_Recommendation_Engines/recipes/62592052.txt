Title:
Four Decker Coconut Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup flour
2 Tablespoons Sugar
2 (8 oz.) packages Cream Cheese
1 cup confectioners sugar
4 cups of Cool Whip
1 large package of lemon Jell-O pudding/pie filling mix
3 cups Milk
Toasted Coconut
Dark chocolate (optional)

Directions:
Mix the butter, flour, and sugar. Pat into a 9-by-13 inch pan.  Bake 15-18 minutes @ 350 degrees. Cool. Mix together the cream cheese, confectioners sugar, and one cup of cool whip. Spread on cooled crust and chill.  Cook filling (on stove), using only 3 cups of Milk.  Cool. Spoon over cream cheese layer.  Chill until firm, several hours or overnight.  Spread the remaining 3 cups of Cool whip over pudding layer and sprinkle with toasted coconut.  Optional: grate dark chocolate on top, but be careful not to use too much. Refrigerate.
