Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Fantabulous Fruit Square Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993009.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
3/4 cup chopped mixed dried fruits (apricots, tart cherries, blueberries, pineapple, raisins, dates)
1/2 cup quick-cooking rolled oats
1/2 cup lightly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup corn syrup

Directions:
In a large bowl combine cereal, dried fruit and oats. Set aside.
In a small saucepan over low heat, stir brown sugar and syrup until sugar is completely dissolved. Pour over cereal mixture. Stir until well coated.
Press firmly into lightly-buttered 21 (8-inch) square pan.
Cool (do not chill). Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
These snack squares with rolled oats are loaded with dried fruits like apricots, cherries, raisins and dates.
