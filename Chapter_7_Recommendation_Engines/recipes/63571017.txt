Title:
Savory Dinner Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c 2T all-purpose flour
3 eggs
1 3/4 t baking powder
2 dash salt
2T olive oil, hot
1/3c canola oil
1/2c milk, hot
1c frozen or densely packed young shepherd's purse greens - may substitute other seasonal dark greens*
1T fresh parsley leaves
2T minced chives, preferably onion grass / wild chives
2 2.5oz cans chopped black olives, drained
1 16oz can white beans, drained and mashed
1T pignoli
1T sesame seeds

Directions:
Grease a casserole dish and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine the flour, eggs, baking powder, and salt.  Mix until well-combined.
Add in the olive oil and milk.  Mix then whisk until smooth.
Add in the canola oil.  Whisk until well-combined.
Add in the beans, greens, parsley, and chives.  Stir.
Pour this mixture into the casserole dish.  Place in oven for 10 minutes.
Remove from the oven and sprinkle the pignoli and sesame seeds on top.  Replace into the oven for about 20 more minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Remove and drizzle with olive oil.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is a dense variation of French savory cakes, and can be eaten as a main course.
