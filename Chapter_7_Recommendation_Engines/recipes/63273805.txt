Title:
Grilled Tomato Basil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tomatoes
3 garlic cloves with skins
2 T onions
2 T whole fresh basil leaves
2 cups chicken broth
2 t olive oil
1 tsp salt
dash pepper
1/4 cup cream
basil garnish

Directions:
Grill or roast tomatoes,onion, and garlic cloves until
skin easily comes off tomatoes, onion is turning clear, garlic cloves are soft and mushy
grill basil right before adding to soup mixture
Add all ingredients except cream to pot and mix with immersion blender
or
Add ingredients except cream to blender, puree, then put in a pot
Heat until just boiling
simmer 5 min
add cream
garnish with basil leaves
