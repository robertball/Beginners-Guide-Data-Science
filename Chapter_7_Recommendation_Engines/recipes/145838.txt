Title:
Jesse's Ginger Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6394077.jpg
Ingredients:
1 carrot, peeled and chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons ground ginger
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:
Combine carrot, mayonnaise, ground ginger, soy sauce, and white sugar in a food processor. Blend until smooth.
