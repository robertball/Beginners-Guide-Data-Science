Title:
Whole Wheat Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. whole wheat, unground
1 C. Milk 1/2 C. Milk (separated)
1/2 t. salt
1/2 C oil
2 eggs, separate whites from yolks
3 t. baking powder

Directions:
Blend together wheat and 1 C. milk for 2-3 min.
Add 1/2 C. milk and blend for 2 more min.
Add salt, oil, and egg yolks and blend. Add baking powder
Beat egg whites and fold into batter.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These are a great way to use some of that food storage wheat. Also great for fiber and lowering cholesterole (unless you top them with a tablespoon of butter!)
