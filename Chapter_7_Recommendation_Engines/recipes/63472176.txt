Title:
Kahlua Candied Yams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium yams
1/4 c butter
1/3 c brown sugar
1/4 c Kahlua

Directions:
Peel and cut yams into bite size pieces.
Steam, boil or microwave until yams are tender but firm. Don't over cook.
Melt butter in skillet and add sugar and Kahlua. cook for 1 min.
Add yams, turning until all sides are coated. Add more Kahlua to taste.
Pour into 8" baking dish, cover and heat in oven until for 15 min. at 350'.

Submitter: 
Debi
Description: 
Recently found my Grandmother's recipe. Tried it for the first time this Christmas. It will now be a staple year round, and perfect for traditional Turkey Dinner.
