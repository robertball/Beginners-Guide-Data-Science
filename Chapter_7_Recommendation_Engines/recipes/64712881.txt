Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 large boneless skinless chicken tenders, cooked, cooled and shredded
1-10 3/4 ounce can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1-16 ounce container sour cream
1-10 ounce can diced tomatoes with green chiles(Rotel), drained slightly
1 small yellow onion, chopped
1 small green bell pepper, chopped
1 small red bell pepper, chopped
1 small yellow bell pepper, chopped
1 cup of your favorite thick and chunky salsa
3 cups shredded cheddar jack cheese
10 flour tortillas(soft taco size)
Optional garnishes: chopped romaine lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped green onions, sliced black olives, extra shredded cheddar jack cheese, guacamole, sour cream, pico de gallo, extra salsa, pickled jalapeno pepper slices, lime wedges, lemon wedges

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine shredded chicken with the onion, bell peppers, soup, sour cream and rotel and mix until well incorporated. Set aside.
Spray a 9x13 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
Place one tortilla in dish and fill with 1 1/2 tablespoons filling, top with cheese, then roll under. Repeat process until all tortillas are used and dish is full.
Spread salsa and remaining cheese on top.
Bake for 35 minutes. Serve with your favorite garnishes.

Submitter: 
Ashley Holman
Description: 
One of my favorite recipes to make and I love that it makes a lot of filling that I can freeze for later use!
