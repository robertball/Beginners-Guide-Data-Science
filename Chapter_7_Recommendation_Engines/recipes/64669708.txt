Title:
Egg Roll Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
2 green onions, green included
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 Tbs. vegetable oil
2 tsp ginger root
2 Tbs soy sauce
8oz salad shrimp
1/4 cup water
14oz cole slaw mix
1 1/2 tsp Sesame oil
1/2 cup sweet and sour sauce
friend wonton strips (optional)

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil in large skillet add diced garlic, green onion and ginger root.
Cook on Med/high heat for 5 min
Add chicken pieces, soy sauce.
Cook 12 min or until done.
Add coleslaw mix and water, stir until wilted
Cook for 5-6 min Add sesame oil
Serve with Sweet and Sour sauce and Fried Wonton strips and cooked rice if desired.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Curtis Mallory
Description: 
This is  a great recipe when you are craving the egg roll without all the grease. I this over rice with wonton strips. Everyone loves it!
