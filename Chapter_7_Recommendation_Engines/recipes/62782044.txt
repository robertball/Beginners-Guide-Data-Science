Title:
Asian Brown Rice Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz raw broccoli florets
1 c. cooked brown rice
3 thiny sliced green onions
1 small can of water chestnuts, drained and diced
2 T, soy sauce
2 T. rice wine vinegar
1 T. sesame oil
fresh ginger, red pepper flakes - to taste

Directions:
1. Steam the broccoli til tender and bright green or leave raw - your choice
2. mix broccoli, cooked rice, green onions and water chestnuts in large bowl
3. in seperate small bowl, mix soy sauce, riec wine vinegar, sesame oil, red pepper flakes and ginger.
4. Toss soy sauce mixture with vegetables.
5. Serve warm or chill and serve cold
