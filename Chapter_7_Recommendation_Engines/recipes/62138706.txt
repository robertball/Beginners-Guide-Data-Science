Title:
Shan's Amazing Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef or turkey
1 green pepper chopped
1 onion chopped
1 can dices tomatoes with juice
1 can chilli flavored diced tomatoes with juice
2 pkgs chilli seasoning(mild)
1 large can Bush's mild chilli beans
2 tsp chilli powder

Directions:
In a large pot, brown beef or turkey,green pepper and onion
when meat is cooked and vegies are tender pour in a strainer and drain any grease aand place back in pot.
Add all remaining ingredients to the meat mixture along with 2-3 cups water and let simmer over medium heat for 1and1/2 hours stirring occasionally. when chilli is done serve over cooked pasta and top with cheese
