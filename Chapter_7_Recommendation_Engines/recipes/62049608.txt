Title:
Pop's Fruit Kabob's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana, peeled and cut into 1inch chunks
1 orange, peeled and cut into half an inch slices
1 apple, cored and quartered
1 firm pear, cored and quartered
lemon juice
2oz. black grapes, washed and seedless
2oz. white grapes, washed and seedless

Directions:
1. Toss the banana and apple in lemon juice to prevent them browning
2. Thread the fruit on to 4 skewers, alternating the grapes with the other fruit
3. Place the kebabs on a hot barbecue for 10 minutes turning frequently
4. Serve when the pear and apple singe slightly

Submitter: 
Knightcraft
Description: 
It's the perfect ending to the perfect barbeque! So easy to make, you'll have time to have one yourself.
