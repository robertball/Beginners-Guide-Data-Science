Title:
Ruthie's Mango Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 mangoes, slightly under-ripe (3 lb/1.5 kg)
2 cups brown sugar or 2/3 cup brown sugar and 1 1/3 cups of Splenda
2/3 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoons coriander seeds
1/4 teaspoon cardamom
1 teaspoon red pepper (cayenne)
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
1.5 teaspoon ginger paste  or ginger powder
1/2 teaspoon cloves
16 fl oz (400 ml) white vinegar
2 teaspoon minced garlic
1 onion (finely chopped)

Directions:
Peel the mango with knife (good result with potato peeler).
Dice Mango.
Evenly sprinkle the sugar and/or Splenda over mango and leave for sometime in cool place (for better result leave 12 hours).
Heat a skillet over medium low heat.
Roast the cumin, coriander and cardamom.
Crush the seeds after toasting.
Add everything else and bring to simmer for about 2-3 hours on low heat, stirring from time to time, until the mango becomes translucent and the liquid has almost evaporated, leaving behind a thick syrup. Remove from heat.Let it cool and put into jars, using a funnel. Store in fridge.
