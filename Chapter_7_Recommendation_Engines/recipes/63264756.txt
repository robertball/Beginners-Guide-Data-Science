Title:
Peanut Butter Marshmallow Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup peanut butter
300 g bag butterscotch chips
200 g bag coloured, mini marshmallows

Directions:
Grease a 11x13 baking dish. Melt butter, peanut butter and chips in a large saucepan on low heat, stirring frequently until melted and smooth. Take off heat and let cool until it thickens up quite a bit (very important). Stir in marshmallows. Transfer to baking dish and put in freezer for 1 hour. Take out 5 minutes before serving. Cut in to squares and serve.
