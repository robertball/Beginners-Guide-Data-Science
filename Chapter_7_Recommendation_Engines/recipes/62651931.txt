Title:
Herb butter scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the scallops:
1 lb dry large sea scallops
1 Tbs butter
1 Tbs olive oil
salt and pepper
For the sauce:
3 Tbs butter in six pieces
2 Tbs finely diced shallots or red onion
1/4 cup dry white wine
1/4 cup finely chopped mixed herbs
1/4 tsp grated lemon zest
salt and pepper
2-3 lemon wedges for serving.

Directions:
Pat scallops dry with paper towels so they will brown.
Heat 10-12 inch pan over medium heat add olive oil and butter and heat till hot.  Season scallops with salt and pepper and add to pan in an uncrowded layer.  Sear untill browned and crisp 2 to 4 min.  Flip and sear on otherside 2 to 4 min until almost firm to the touch.
Return the pan to medium add a piece of butter and the shallots saute until they begin to soften, about 1min. Add the wine and simmer until reduced by about half.  Add the herbs and lemon zest.  Reduce the heat to low, add the remaining butter, and wihisk constantly until the butter melts in the sauce.  Return the scallops and any accumulated juices to the pan.
