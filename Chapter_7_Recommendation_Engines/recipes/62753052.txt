Title:
Banana Sour Cream Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter (150g)
1 1/2 cups white sugar
3 eggs
6 very ripe bananas, mashed (3 cups)
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream (10oz)
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons baking soda
2 1/2 cups all-purpose white flour
2 cups brown flour
1 cup chocolate chips(optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Grease three 7x3 inch loaf pans.
In a large bowl, cream butter and 1 1/2 cups sugar. Mix in eggs, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla and cinnamon. Mix in salt, baking soda and flour.  Divide into prepared pans.
Bake for 1 hour, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
Less sweet and a bit healthier (brown flour) than the original version. But you'll never know because of the chocolate chips!
Makes 3 loaves.Modified from: Banana Sour Cream Bread
