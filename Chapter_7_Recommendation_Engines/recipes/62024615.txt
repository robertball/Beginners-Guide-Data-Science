Title:
Rosemary and white wine chicken breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast (with bone, with or without skin)
sprinkle of rosemary
smaller sprinkle of parsley
1 clove of garlic
1 onion (minced, halved, however)
2 tblsp of chicken Bovril (liquid chicken concentrate)
enough white wine to cover chicken (or almost)
a little water (varies according do how much wine you put...) (about 1/4 cup)
and some salt and pepper

Directions:
Add all ingredients in an oven proof casserole dish and cooks for about 1hr and 30min, covered. Remove lid for the last 30min. Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
SarahCooks
Description: 
This is a very good easy meal to prepare when you have other things to take care of (laundry and whatnot). Very easy, very good. Chicken comes out very nice and moist.
