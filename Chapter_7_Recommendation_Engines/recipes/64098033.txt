Title:
Amy's Barbecue Sauce x 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. vegetable oil
3 c. chopped onion
3 c. ketchup (cheap is fine)
3 c. water
1 1/3 c. lemon juice
3/4 c. sugar
3/4 c. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c. prepared mustard
2T & 2t. salt
2 t. pepper

Directions:
Brown onions in oil until soft.  Add remaining ingredients and cook slow until thick

Submitter: 
Amy Hague Miller
Description: 
This is the best barbecue sauce.  I always keep in the refrigerator for grilling season.
