Title:
Big Ray's Biscuits  and Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070062.jpg
Ingredients:
1 roll refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
8 eggs
1 cup milk
1 can (7 oz)  mexicorn whole kernel corn with red and green peppers, drained
6 strips bacon, cooked, crumbled
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F.
In medium bowl, beat eggs and milk with wire whisk until blended. Stir in corn, bacon and cheese.
Spray 9" x 13" casserole with cooking spray. Cut each biscuit into 8 pieces; arrange evenly in casserole. Pour egg mixture over biscuits. Press down with back of spoon, making sure all biscuit pieces are covered with egg mixture.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until edges are golden brown and center is set. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Description: 
This egg and bacon bake is topped with refrigerated biscuits for a quick 'n easy version of the classic breakfast dish. Make a white gravy with the bacon dripping for an added bonus.
