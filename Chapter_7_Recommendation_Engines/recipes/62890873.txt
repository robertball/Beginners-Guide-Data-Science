Title:
Fried Green Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/698456.jpg
Ingredients:
3 medium, firm green tomatoes
Salt
1 cup all-purpose flour
pinch cayenne
1/2 teaspoon each white pepper, black pepper, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder.
1/2 cup soymilk mixed with 1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 egg
1/3 cup cornmeal
1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
oil for frying

Directions:
1 Cut unpeeled tomatoes into 1/2 inch slices. Sprinkle slices with salt. Let tomato slices stand for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, place in separate shallow bowls: the flour and seasoning, buttermilk and egg, and bread crumbs and cornmeal.
2 Heat the in a skillet on medium heat. Beat the egg and the buttermilk together. Dip tomato slices in the flour-seasoning mix, then buttermilk-egg mixture, then the cornmeal-bread crumb mix. In the skillet, fry half of the coated tomato slices at a time, for 3-5 minutes on each side or until brown. Set the cooked tomatoes on paper towels to drain. These are fantastic with a little a little goat cheese.
