Title:
Roasted Pepper Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7-8 roma tomatoes, diced (about 3 cups)
1 small onion, diced
2 poblano peppers
2 jalapeno peppers
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c. cilantro, chopped
1 lime, juiced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat broiler.  Roast peppers until blistered and charred all over, about 8 min.  Place peppers in a glass bowl and cover with plastic wrap.  Let rest 5 minutes.
2. Peel, seed, and chop poblano peppers.  Peel and chop jalapeno peppers.
3. Combine tomatoes, onion, peppers, garlic, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Stir until combined.  Chill at least 2 hours.  Serve with tortilla chips.
