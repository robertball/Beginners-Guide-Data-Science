Title:
Cornbread Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* Half a batch of cornbread (2 to 3 cups when chopped).
* 1 packet buttermilk ranch dressing mix
* 1 cup mayo
* 1/2 cup buttermilk
* 1 14.5 oz. can of pinto beans, drained and rinsed
* 1 16 oz. can whole kernel corn, drained
* 1 small green bell pepper, finely chopped
* 1 small sweet onion, finely chopped
* 2 ripe tomatoes, seeded and chopped
* 2 cups finely shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
1) Cut cornbread into 1 inch cubes and place in the bottom of a large bowl or trifle bowl.
2) In a small bowl, combine ranch dressing mix, mayo and milk. Mix well and refrigerate.
3) Topped cornbread layer with rinsed pinto beans, next layer drained corn, top with chopped green pepper, then top that with chopped sweet onion, next add chopped tomatoes and then top off with shredded cheddar cheese.
4) Pour ranch dressing mixture on top; refrigerate for several hours to allow the flavors to develop.
