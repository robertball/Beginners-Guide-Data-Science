Title:
Green Pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large green bell peppers
1 1/2 cups white vinegar
6 1/2 cups sugar
3 Tbl crushed red pepper
4 drops green food coloring
1 6 oz packet certo or other pectin

Directions:
Blend bell pepper and some of the vinegar in a blender or food processor.  Pour in a large pot, add sugar and rest of the vinegar and bring to a rolling boil, continue a low boil for 5 minutes.  Add certa and stir one minute.  Bring to a boil again.  Skim foam after it boils one minute and add food coloring. (continue a low boil for approx 5 minutes) Stir and put into jars.  Makes 7 - (1/2 pint) jars

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
Delicious! Has a kick.  Serve with crackers and cream cheese.
