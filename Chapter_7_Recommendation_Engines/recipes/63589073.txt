Title:
Peaches 'n Cream Light Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
1.5 cups frozen peaches
1 fresh or frozen banana, broken in chunks
1 tbs. honey
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Add all ingredients into high-powered blender.  Blend until thoroughly combined. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jennifer C. Martin
Description: 
This is a great morning smoothie that tastes like a milkshake.  I used low-fat milk, but any milk (whole, soy, almond) would work.
