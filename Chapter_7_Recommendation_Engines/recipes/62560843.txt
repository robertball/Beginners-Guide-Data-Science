Title:
Celery and Pear Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536274.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ½ T butter
6 cps thinly sliced celery with leaves, about 12 stalks, plus chopped leaves (for garnish)
2 1/2 cps unpeeled ripe Bartlett pears, cored, diced plus ½ cp finely diced (for garnish)
1 ½ cps chopped dark green leek tops
3 small Turkey bay leaves
1 ½ t chopped fresh thyme
1 ½ T all purpose flour
3 cps (or more) low-salt chicken broth

Directions:
Melt butter in pot over med-high heat.
Add sliced celery, 3 cps diced pears, leek tops, bay leaves, and thyme.
Cover; cook until celery softens, stirring occasionally, about 8 min.
Toss in flour.
Stir in 3 cps broth; bring to boil.
Cover, reduce heat to med-low, and simmer until celery is tender, about 20 min.
Remove bay leaves from soup.
Puree soup in batched in blender until smooth.
Return puree to same pot. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Thin with more broth by the ¼ cupfuls, if desired. Re-warm briefly.
Divide soup among bowls; garnish with ½ cp finely diced pear and celery leaves.

Submitter: 
Jen
Description: 
This would be great paired with grilled chicken! It's a light soup that won't leave you feeling too full.
