Title:
Hot Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked chicken
3/4 cup mayonnaise
3/4 cup cream of chicken soup
2 cups celery, chopped
4 eggs, hard boiled and chopped
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp onion
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup cheddar cheese, grated
1-1/2 cups potato chips, crushed
1/2 cup almonds, toasted and sliced

Directions:
In large bowl, combine all ingredients except cheese, chips and almonds.  Place in 7-1/2" x 11" baking dish.  Top with cheese and chips.  Refrigerate overnight.  Take out about 1 hour before baking.  Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes.  Don't overbake.  Add almonds and serve.
