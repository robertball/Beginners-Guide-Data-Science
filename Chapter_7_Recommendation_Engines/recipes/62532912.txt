Title:
Christmas Caramel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cup brown sugar
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 c butter
1 c karo syrup
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped nuts of choice optional

Directions:
cook on stovetop on medium heat until 236 degrees
add vanilla and nuts
pour into 13x9 foil pan
cool, cut and wrap
