Title:
New York Style Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup warm water, 110 to 115 degrees
1/2 tsp salt
2-1/4 cups all-purpose or bread flour
1 tsp. sugar
2 tsp. active dry yeast
1 TBS. cornmeal

Directions:
Place all ingredients except cornmeal in bread machine - program dough cycle setting; press start.  Remove dough from bread machine pan; let rest 2-3 minutes.
Pat and gently stretch dough from edges until dough seems to not stretch anymore.  Let rest 2-3 minutes.  Continue patting and stretching until dough is 12-14" in diameter.  Spray pan with cooking spray, sprinkle with cornmeal.  Press dough into pan.
Follow topping and baking instructions for individual recipes, baking pizz on bottom rack of oven.
