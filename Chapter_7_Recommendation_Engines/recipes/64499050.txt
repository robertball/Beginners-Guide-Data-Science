Title:
Rockin Robin's End of Season Squash Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sliced zucchini
2 cups sliced  summer squash
2 cups sliced vegetable marrow
2 cups sliced thin eggplant
2 cups sliced tomatoes
2 cups sliced onions
8 cloves garlic
1 cup packed fresh basil
1 cup packed fresh parsley
1/2 cup packed fresh oregano
250 gm pkg of cream cheese
250 gm pkg of ricotta cheese
680 ml tomato sauce
1 cup Italian bread crumbs
1 cup grated or shredded  parmesan cheese
salt and black pepper

Directions:
Spray a 4 quart casserole heavily with cooking spray.
In food processor, mix garlic cloves, basil, parsley and oregano.  If dried herbs are being used, skip this step.
On slow heat, melt cream cheese and add herbs, tomato sauce, ricotta and stir well.
Slice onions, eggplant, zucchini , summer squash, tomato, and vegetable marrow into 1/2 inch slices using a sharp knife or mandolin WITH a guard.
Layer eggplant into casserole and sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper.  Add zucchini, summer squash, vegetable marrow, onions and tomato slices.  Cover with half of sauce.
Repeat layers and cover with remaining sauce.   Sprinkle with Italian bread crumbs and parmesan cheese, remove cover the last 15 minutes of cooking.  Casserole will be bubbling and slightly browned when ready.
