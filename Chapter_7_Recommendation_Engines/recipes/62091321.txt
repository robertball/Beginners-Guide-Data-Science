Title:
Cranberry Cream Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/3 C. minced onion
½ C. sugar
2 T. prepared horseradish
½ t. salt
8 oz. cream cheese
triscuits

Directions:
Bring cranberry sauce, sugar, salt, onion, and horseradish to a boil in a small saucepan, stirring frequently.  Put in a container and chill for at least 1 hour.  (This will keep in the refrigerator up to 3 days.)  Spread cream cheese on serving dish and cover with cranberry salsa.  Serve with triscuits.
