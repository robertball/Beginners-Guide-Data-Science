Title:
White Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 cups milk
2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
In medium saucepan,combine cornstarch,salt and pepper; using whisk, stir in milk until smooth.  Add butter.  Stirring constantly with rubber spatula, bring to boil or medium-high heat and boil one minute.  Remove from heat.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
This sauce is used in several freezer cooking recipes.
