Title:
Chicken Escabeche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6754461.jpg
Ingredients:
12.5 ounces skinless, boneless chicken breast
3.5 ounces carrots, julienned
3.5 ounces white onion, thinly sliced
3.5 ounces red onion, thinly sliced
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
2 bay leaves
1/4 red wine vinegar
1/4 cup dry white wine
1/4 cup water
3 Tbsp evoo
1 tsp achiote powder
1/2 tsp ground cayenne chili powder

Directions:
1. Heat the oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Brown the chicken on one side, about 5 minutes. Turn over the chicken. Add the carrots, onions, garlic, bay leaves, salt, and pepper. Mix everything until the vegetables become soft and fragrant and the chicken has browned. About 8 minutes
2. Pour in the vinegar, wine, and water. Sprinkle the achiote powder and chili powder over everything and stir well. Reduce the heat to medium. Cover and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken is 165Â°.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Chicken breasts, onions, and carrots stew in vinegar and wine for a tangy South American meal. This is delicious served over rice or quinoa. This dish is delicious if served immediately but even better if served at room temperature after the flavors have had some time to meld.
