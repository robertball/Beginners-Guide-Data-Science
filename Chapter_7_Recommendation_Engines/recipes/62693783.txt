Title:
Veggie Taco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados
1 (16 ounce) jar of salsa
1 (15 ounce) can of flavored black beans
2 (15 ounce) cans of rinsed black beans
1 (16 ounce) package corn tortillas
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 (16 ounce) package of brocolli slaw or cole slaw
1 (15 ounce) container crumbled feta cheese
2 tablespoons cumin
2 garlic cloves
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel and smash avacado.  Stir in jar of salsa.  Set aside. Mix beans, slaw, feta, cumin, and garlic in a bowl.  Place a small amount of olive oil on a skillet.  Place corn tortillas on skillet with a scoop of the slaw mixture in the middle of each. Fry until brown, fold, and place on plate. Top with avacado mixture and eat.
