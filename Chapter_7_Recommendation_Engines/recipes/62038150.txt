Title:
fiesta rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked white rice
1/2 cup frozen corn
1/2 cup black beans
2-3 tablespoons taco seasoning
OPTIONAL for top:
shredded cheddar cheese
sour cream
green onions

Directions:
1) Prepare 2 cups of uncooked rice according to package. Befor simmering add 1/2 cup frozen corn and 1/2 cup black beans.
2) After rice is completely cooked, add taco seasoning.
3) Top with shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, or green onions, if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
Great for a sidedish, or enough for a vegetarian meal. I discovered this recipe by accident when I had little groceries left in the house! ENJOY!! :)
