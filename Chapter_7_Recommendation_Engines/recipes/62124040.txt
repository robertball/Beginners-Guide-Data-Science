Title:
Monkey Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 inch Flour Tortilla
1 Banana
2 to 3 TBSP. Peanut Butter

Directions:
Spread Peanut Butter on Tortilla
Place whole banana on end of Tortilla, roll tortilla around banana.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cheesecakemama
Description: 
This is a quick snack for the kids after school or whenever.  My son makes these himself as a quick snack.
