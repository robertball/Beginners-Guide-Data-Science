Title:
Maple Butter Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588512.jpg
Ingredients:
*****Dough*****
1 cup sifted flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 Cup chopped walnuts (or Pecans)
1/3 cup butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 Tablespoon Vanilla extract.
1/2 cup white chocholate chips (or butterscotch chips)
*****Maple Butter Sauce*******
5 oz Cream Cheese
1/2 cup Real Maple Syrup (not imitation)
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar (or less to taste)
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease 9x9 square baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
In Medium Bowl, Sift flour, add baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Sift again with whisk. Add chopped nuts and set aside.
In Mixer bowl, add melted butter, and brown sugar. Mix well. Add eggs and vanilla extract. Blend well. Add flour mixture, a little at a time, mixing well. Stir in white chocolate (or butterscotch) chips. Spread in 9x9 pan. Bake for 20 t0 25 min until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean or with slightly fudgy crumbs. Serve with ice cream and Maple Butter Sauce.
Maple Butter Sauce
In a medium saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Increase heat to Med-Low, add syrup and cream cheese. Stir in brown sugar until dissolved. Heat while stirring constantly until bubbly. Add Walnuts if desired.
Pour over individual servings of Blondies immediately and enjoy.
