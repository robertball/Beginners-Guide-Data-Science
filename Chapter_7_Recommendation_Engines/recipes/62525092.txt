Title:
White Chocolate Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup + 2 TBS white sugar
1/4 cup + 2 TBS brown sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp ginger powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup crystallized ginger, chopped
8 oz. white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
In a medium bowl, cream together the butter and sugars. Mix in the egg and vanilla.
Combine the flour, ginger, baking soda and salt.  Stir into the wet ingredients. Mix in the white chocolate chips and crystallized ginger.
Drop cookies by tablespoon onto an ungreased cookie sheet, about 2 inches apart.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned. Cool on baking sheet and then removie to wire racks after a few minutes.
When completely cool, store in an airtight container.
