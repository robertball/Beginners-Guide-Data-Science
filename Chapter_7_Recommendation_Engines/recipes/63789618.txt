Title:
Nachos Grande Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051384.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 large onion finely chopped
3 medium hot peppers sliced
1-2 tablespoons Taco seasoning (or to taste)
1 medium tomato diced
2-3 green onions sliced
1 can (2.25 oz.) sliced black olives
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (16 oz.) can refried beans
1/2 cup sour cream
1 bag corn tortilla chips

Directions:
In a large frying pan, brown ground beef, with onion and sliced hot peppers over medium heat. Break the meat into very small pieces while it is cooking. After the meat is thoroughly cooked, drain off the grease and season with taco seasoning.
Heat the refried beans.
Bake tortilla chips at 350 degrees F for 8-10 minutes
Layer taco chips. Spread beans over the ships Layer with 1/2 the cheese, the ground beef mixture, and the remaining cheese.
Top with the sliced olives, diced tomatoes, sliced green onions, and a dollop of sour cream.
Serve immediately.

Description: 
This recipe is great for a quick meal, and works for an appetizer. It's easy to make, and can be easily modified for ingredients that you have on hand. I use a homemade taco seasoning that is on this site.
