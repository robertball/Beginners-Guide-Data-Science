Title:
Frosted Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4  cup           butter
1/4  cup           milk
1      cup           powdered sugar
1      teaspoon      baking powder
1      each          egg -- separated
1/2  teaspoon      vanilla
2      cups          flour -- sifted

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar;  add unbeaten egg yolk and beat well.  Add milk and vanilla.  Add flour and baking powder which have been sifted together.  Fold in stiffly beaten egg white.  Drop mix. on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 for 10 min.  (They burn easily)  Frost w/red and green frosting for Christmas or pastel colored frosting for Easter.

Submitter: 
Carol Keller
Description: 
I have been making these for 50 years and my Mom before that.  They are always the most asked for on the cooky platter.
