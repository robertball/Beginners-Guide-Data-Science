Title:
KFC Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
Enough milk for soaking chicken
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tbsp season salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp paprika
1/2 c flour
3 tbsp butter

Directions:
Soak chicken in milk for at least 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Melt butter in pan and spread to coat bottom.
Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
Shake excess milk from soaked chicken and dip chicken into seasoning mixture to coat.
Place in buttered pan.
Cook for 40 minutes, turning once halfway thru.
