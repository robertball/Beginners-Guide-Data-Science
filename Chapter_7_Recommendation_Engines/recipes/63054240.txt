Title:
Sweet Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium carrots, peeled and sliced diagonally
1/2 cup catalina dressing
2 tbsps. brown sugar
2 tbsps worchestershire sauce
2 tbsps chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook carrots in  boiling water until crisp tender; drain. Return to saucepan add dressing, sugar and worchestershire sauce; stir until evenly coated with the glaze. Add parsley.
