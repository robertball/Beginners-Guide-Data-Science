Title:
Red Cabbage Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 had red cabbage
2 tsps red wine vinegar
1 1/2 tsps Sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tbls Mayonnaise
Fresh ground pepper
1 green onion or scallion sliced thin
Toasted sesame seeds, about 2tsps.

Directions:
Cut the red cabbage into quarters.
Slice cabbage as thinly as possible.
In a bowl, mix red wine vinegar, sugar, salt, mayonnaise and pepper.
Add cabbage to bowl and incorporate with mixture.
Taste for seasoning and correct if necessary.
Top with scallions and toated sesame seeds.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
Nice as side dish or try it on a sandwich!
