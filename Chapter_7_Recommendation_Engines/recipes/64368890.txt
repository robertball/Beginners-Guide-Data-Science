Title:
Gluten free mashed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 white potatoes
1 tablespoon of yellow organic mustard
1 hard boiled egg
1/4 teaspoon of salt and black pepper
1 tablespoon gluten free bread crumbs
1 tablespoon of gluten free mayo

Directions:
Boil potatoes and egg until cooked.  Cool and mash together with the rest of the ingredients, then sprinkle over bread crumbs and bake in oven at 350 for 5-7 minutes.  Cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Priscilla Akrong
Description: 
One of my favorites, a little different than traditional mashed potatoes-sure to please!
