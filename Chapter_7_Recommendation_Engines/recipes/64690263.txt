Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Beef and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean beef stew meat
1 can low-fat cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup water
1 packet dry onion soup mix
8 oz fresh mushrooms (whole or sliced, your preference)

Directions:
Brown meat in skillet over med-high heat. (You can skip this step to save time and the recipe will still be great.)
Place meat in 4 quart crock pot.
Place mushrooms on top of beef.
Combine soup, water and soup mix and pour over mushrooms and beef.
Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
Tastes great over brown rice or hot cooked noodles.

Submitter: 
Alexis Redden
Description: 
Easy slow cooker meal for your busiest nights. Tastes great over brown rice.
