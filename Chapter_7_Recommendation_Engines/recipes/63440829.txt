Title:
Holiday Fruit Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Butter, Softened
2 Cups Brown Sugar
2 Eggs
1/2 Cup Soured Milk
3 1/2 Cups Flour
1 tsp Soda
1 tsp Salt
1 1/2 Cup Chopped Pecans
2 Cups Candied Cherries, Cut in Half
2 Cups Chopped Dates

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar and eggs.  Stir in milk.  Mix flour, soda and salt.  Stir in.  Mix in nuts and fruit.
Chill.
Drop onto greased baking sheet, press one half pecan into each.
Bake in 400 oven for 8 to 18 minutes.
