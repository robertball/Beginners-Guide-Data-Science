Title:
Southwestern White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
1/4 cup onion, chopped
2 cups low sodium chicken broth
2 oz green chiles, diced
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp Oregano
1 tsp fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
1/4 tsp red pepper
2 (19oz) cans white kidney beans, undrained

Directions:
Heat oil in 5 qt saucepan over medium heat.  Add chicken and cook 4 to 5 minutes, stirring often.  Remove chicken with slotted spoon; cover and keep warm. Add onion to saucepan and cook 2 minutes. Stir in broth, green chiles and spices; simmer 30 minutes.  Add chicken and beans to broth and heat through. Serve in small bowls with desired garnishes (like Monterey shredded jack cheese and chives).

Submitter: 
Julie Glosenger
Description: 
Delicious chicken chili dinner the whole family will enjoy!  This is the only chili recipe my husband and children will eat.  Enjoy! To make quicker, get the broth simmer and then in another pan cook the chicken while it simmers.  One serving size is roughly 7 oz of chili
