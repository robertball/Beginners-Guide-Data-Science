Title:
Tropical Ricotta Fruit Whip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639108.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup (250 mL) Tre Stelle® Traditional Ricotta Cheese
1 tbsp (15 mL) freshly squeezed lime juice
1 container (100 g) banana or coconut yogurt
1/4 cup (50 mL) canned coconut milk (optional)
1 ripe banana, peeled and sliced
1 mango, peeled and cut into Â¼” cubes
1 tsp (5 mL) lime zest, grated
1/4 cup (50 mL) sweetened shredded coconut
Garnish: 1 tbsp sweetened coconut, toasted, for garnish if desired

Directions:
Combine Ricotta, lime juice, yogurt, coconut milk, shredded coconut and process in a blender and until smooth. Stir in banana slices. Refrigerate for one hour or until ready to use.
Spoon into small serving dishes; top with mango cubes and lime zest. Garnish with shredded coconut if desired.
