Title:
Chattanooga Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz package of extra sharp cheese
1 8 oz package of cream cheese
1 T mayonnaise
1 medium onion, minced finely
1 dash salt
2 T dried chopped chives
paprika

Directions:
Mince the onion, grate the cheddar and mix all ingredients except paprika. Shape into ball or log shapes. Roll in paprika and store overnight in plastic wrap. Serve on a cheese board with crackers.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
This is great for a party. Always part of our New Year's dinner buffet!
