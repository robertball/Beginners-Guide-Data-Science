Title:
Candice's Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/940589.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
2/3 cup sugar
1 large egg
1 T. light corn syrup
1 T. vanilla extract

Directions:
Whisk together the flour and salt.
In another bowl, mix the butter and sugar.
Add the egg, corn syrup, and vanilla extract.
Stir in the flour and salt mixture.
Divide dough into 2 portions and chill in the refrigerator 1-2 hours.
Working with one disk at a time, roll between 2 sheets of wax paper till 1/4 inch thick.
Cut into shapes.
Place shapes on an ungreased baking sheet.
Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 8-10 minutes.
Frost with Candice's Sugar Cookie Icing.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
My daughter makes the best iced sugar cookies.  This is the recipe she uses.  They are called Big Daddy's Sugar Cookies and she got them from her daughter's Tiana's Cookbook.
