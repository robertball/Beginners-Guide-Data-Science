Title:
mini chip snowball cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks of butter, softened
3/4 C. powdered sugar
1/2 t. salt
1 T. vanilla extract
3 C. All-Purpose Flour
1 pkg semi-sweet mini morsels

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Cream together butter, sugar, vanilla and salt. Gradually beat in flour. Stir in mini morsels. Shape into 1-1/4 inch balls onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake for 10 minutes or until set. Sprinkle powdered sugar over top of cookies while still hot. Cool on baking sheets for 10 minutes then remove to wire rack to finish cooling.
