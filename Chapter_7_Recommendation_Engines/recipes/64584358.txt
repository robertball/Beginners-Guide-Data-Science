Title:
Spicy Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3690859.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast - cubed
1 small onion - chopped
1 large zuccini - sliced and quartered
1 can Rotel tomato
2 stalks celery - sliced
4 cloved garlic - minced
1/3 can chicken broth (14.5 oz)
1 tbsp crushed red pepper
3 tbsp sofrito
2 tbsp cajun seasoning
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large  pot.  Cook chicken until almost done then add onions, garlic, celery and zucchini.  Saute until onions are translucent.  Add tomato, broth, sofrito, and cajun seasoning.  Bring to a boil then let simmer 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Thillyre
Description: 
This is my version of a spicy low carb chicken stew I created while starting a flat belly cleanse.  I don't measure so the measurements are approximate - adjust to your taste!  It just turned out so good I had to share!
