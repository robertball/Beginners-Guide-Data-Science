Title:
Honeymoon Teriyaki Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lb flank steak
1 1/4 c. packed brown sugar
1 c. soy sauce
5 shakes or 1/2 " fresh grated ginger
1/2 garlic clove-crushed/chopped
1/8 tsp sesame oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a ziploc bag, pierce the steak with a fork beforehand and marinate overnight...can even stay in fridge 2-3 nights
Grill over medium high (cooks quickly).  Slice across the grain slightly angled.
