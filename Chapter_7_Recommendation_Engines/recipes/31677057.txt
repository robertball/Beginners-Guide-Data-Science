Title:
Pasta with Tuna and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can of Italian Tuna, packed in olive oil
1/4 of a red onion, diced
1/2 10 oz package of baby portabello mushrooms, diced
1/4- 1/2 bottle of good marinara sauce
2 1/2 handfuls of strozzapeti or cavatappi pasta
salt and pepper

Directions:
Boil salted water and cook pasta to desired doneness
Dice mushrooms and onions
Drain olive oil from tuna can into another pan, once heated, add mushrooms, salt and pepper and saute
Once mushrooms are tender, add red onions and saute until tender
Add marinara sauce and flake in tuna with a fork.
Season sauce with salt and pepper
Cook sauce until heated through
Drain pasta and combine with sauce
Serve with grated black pepper and parmesan cheese
