Title:
Country French Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices Bacon
3 lb boneless beef chuck roast, cut in to 1" pieces
1 large onion, cut in 1/2" wedges
3 cups ready to serve baby carrots
1 cup red wine (Zinfandel)
3/4 cup beef broth
3 T flour
1 t dried basil
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
8 oz sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 c sun dried tomatoes
Egg noodles
chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
1.  Spray slow cooker with cooking spray.  Cook bacon until crisp and cut in small pieces.  Save 1 T bacon fat.  Cook beef in the bacon fat in batches 2-3 minutes.  Stir onion into browned beef, cook 1 minute.  Put in slow cooker.
2.  Stir in carrots, wine, broth, flour, basil, thyme, salt, pepper, and canned diced tomatoes.
3.  Cover and cook on low 7-8 hours.
4.  Stir in muchrooms and sun dried tomatoes.  Cover and cook on low 20 to 30 min.
5.  Cook noodles.  Serve over noodles with parsley.
