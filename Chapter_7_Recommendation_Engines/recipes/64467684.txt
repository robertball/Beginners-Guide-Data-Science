Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2451867.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 stick butter
2lbs kale
1 large container marshmallow fluff

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Melt butter on stovetop or in microwave
Add melted butter to flour and mix well
Roll mixture out with a rolling pin
Spread fluff evenly
Pile kale on top of fluff
Roll the mixture up (like a cinnamon roll)
Place in oven and bake for 10 mins
Let cool then slice and serve with kale juice (optional but highly recommended)
