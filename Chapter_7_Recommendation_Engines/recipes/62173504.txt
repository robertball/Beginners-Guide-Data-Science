Title:
"Adult" Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large lemons
1 Cup sugar
1 pint-sized bottle of vodka
4 trays of ice
A large, empty, glass pickle jar and a kitchen hand-towel

Directions:
Fill the jar with ice
Cut lemons in half and squeeze juice into jar
Add sugar and vodka
Put the lid on the jar and wrap it with the hand towel, now shake that jar like crazy until the towel sticks to the jar (because of the condensation freezing).

Submitter: 
Whitney
Description: 
A simple adult-lemonade that takes some elbow grease!
