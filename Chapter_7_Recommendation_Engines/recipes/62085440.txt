Title:
Crockpot Potato-Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 potatoes, cubed
1 tbsp. chives, chopped
1 1/2 cs. chopped celery
1/3 c. chopped parsley
1/2 c. chopped onion
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. celery seed
1 tsp. savory
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. milk
2 tbsps. flour
2 tbsps. butter
2 1/2 cs. grated chedder cheese

Directions:
Place potatoes, chives, celery, parsley, onion, paprika, clery seed, savory and salt in a slow cooker and add just enough water to barely cover.
Cook on high one hour.
Turn heat to low and cook 4 to 5 hours or until potatoes are done.
Combine milk and flour in a tightly covered jar and shake to blend evenly.
In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
Add flout mixture slowly, and stir constantly 3 to 4 minutes.
Add cheese; stir until melted.
Turn crock-pot to high setting.
Add cheese mixture to soup and cook until slighly thickened.
Serve hot in bowls and garnish with chives, sour cream or bacon bits.
