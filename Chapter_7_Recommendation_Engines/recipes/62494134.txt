Title:
Amy's chicken noodle soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken ( or the equivalent in chicken breasts)
5-7 cans of chicken broth
1 sm bag of baby carrots
1 sm bag of celery
1 white onion
1 bag of frozen homestyle egg noodles
6 eggs
3 cups of flour

Directions:
Cook chicken in 5 cans of broth for 30 min
Remove from heat, cool n debone
Put broth back on stove til near boiling add veggies n noodles
Cook for 20 min on med
Add cut up chicken bring to boil
Mix up eggs really well
Slowly add 3 cups of flour while continually stirring
Stir dumplings in soup 1 tea at a time
Turn on low
Cover with lid
Let stand 20 min ( no peaking or stirring)
Put a lid on
