Title:
5 Quick Cabbage Side Dishes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shred 1 head cabbage.
THEN:

Directions:
Stir into CANNED STEWED TOMATOES. Heat until cabbage wilts. Add CIDER VINEGAR and BROWN SUGAR to taste.
Stir-fry in small amount of oil with GARLIC and fresh ground GINGER.
Toss cabbage with 1 thinly sliced ONION, 1/4 cup CIDER VINEGAR, 1 Tbsp. sugar, salt, and pepper. Drain and serve.
Cook along with NOODLES during the last 5 minutes in the same pot. Drain and toss with oil, LEMON JUICE, POPPY SEEDS, salt and pepper to taste.
Mix with thinly sliced RED AND GREN PEPPERS and PURPLE ONION. Stir a bit of DIJON MUSTARD, DILL, HOT PEPPER SAUCE, and pepper into your usual coleslaw dressing.
