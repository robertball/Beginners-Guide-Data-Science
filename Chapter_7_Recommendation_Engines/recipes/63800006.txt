Title:
Warm Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. ground beef
1 packet taco seasoning
16 oz. cream cheese
8 oz. onion dip
8 oz. salsa
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown hamburger and mix with taco seasoning.
Soften cream cheese, combine with onion dip.
Mix cheeses together.
Spray 9x13 dish with non-stick spray.
In the 9x13 pan, spread the cream cheese mixture. Next, layer on the ground beef, then salsa. Top with shredded cheeses.
Bake for 20-30 minutes at 350. Serve with chips and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This is a delicious and easy dip that will disappear before your very eyes! I usually double the recipe unless it's a very small gathering.
