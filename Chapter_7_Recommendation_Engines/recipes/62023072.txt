Title:
Sour Cream Peach Kuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yellow cake mix
1/2 cup toasted shredded coconut
4 oz butter
26 oz can sliced peaches
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tea ground cinnamon
1 cup sour cream
1 egg

Directions:
In bowl, combine cake mix and coconut.  Cut in butter until mixture crumbles.  Press mixture into bottom and up sides of a well greased 8X12 pan.  Bake at 325 for 10-15 min.  Arrent well-drained peaches evenly over crust.  Combine sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over peaches.  BLend sour cream and beaten egg and pour over peaches.  Bake 10-15 min til topping is set.

Submitter: 
JUNEBUFF1
Description: 
An easy peach kuchen starts with a cake mix.  Sour cream adds tang and makes each bite creamy.
