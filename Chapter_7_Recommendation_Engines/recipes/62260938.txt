Title:
Baked Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 1/2 Tbsp. flour
1 can cream-style corn
2 eggs (slightly beaten)
1/2 cup chopped bell pepper (red, green, or yellow)
1 Tbsp. melted butter
1/3 cup milk
paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
In a small bowl, combine sugar and flour; set aside.
In a large bowl, combine corn and eggs.
Stir in sugar mixture; add bell pepper, melted butter, milk; mix well.
Spoon into small greased baking dish.
Bake 1 hour or until light brown.
Sprinkle with paprika before serving.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
A tasty corn dish that's nice and colorful - even Christmasy - if you use a combination of red and green bell pepper.
