Title:
Cheesy Baked Shells and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb medium pasta shells
1 tbsp butter
2 tbsp all purpose flour
2 c whole milk
2 c grated cheddar or gruyere
1/8 tsp nutmeg
3/4 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
16 oz bag frozen broccoli, thawed

Directions:
Heat broiler. Cook pasta according to package directions
Meanwhile, heat butter in lg pot over med heat. Add flour and cook, stirring for 2 minutes. Whisk in milk and cook, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened, 4-5 minutes
Add 1 1/2 cups of the cheese and stir until melted. Stir in nutmeg, salt and pepper
Add pasta and broccoli, toss to combine.
Tansfer to broiler proof 8" square dish or other 1 1/2 qt baking dish. Sprink with remaining cheese.
Broil until golden, 3-4 minutes.
