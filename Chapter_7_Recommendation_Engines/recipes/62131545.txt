Title:
EASY PEASY  HUMMUS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284435.jpg
Ingredients:
1 19 oz Can Chickpeas (drained, reserve liquid)
1/4 Cup Liquid from drained chickpeas
4 Tbsp Lemon juice
2 Tbsp Tahini (sesame paste)
2-4 Cloves of garlice crushed (or more to taste)
1/2 Tsp salt (or a good grind of sea salt)
3 Tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
1/4 Tsp Paprika (or to taste)
1/4 Tsp Cumin (or to taste)

Directions:
In food processor pour in chickpeas, liquid from chickpeas, lemon juice, tahini, garlic, salt, paprika, and cumin if desired and start blending together. Blend for several minutes, while food processor is going add your olive oil slowly, continue blending until desired consistency.  Shorter time for coarse texture and longer for a smoother texture.
Garnish with a sprig of fresh parsley and a sprinkle of paprika.  Serve with pita bread, toasted or plain, or crackers, and/or vegetables for dipping.  Can be stored in refrigerator for up to 3 days.    Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
LOBSTER
Description: 
AN absolutely yummy, easily made hummus you can play with. Add what you like and omit what you don't.  Serve with vegetables, toasted pitas, tortillas, or just a spoon!!
