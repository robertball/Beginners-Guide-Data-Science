Title:
Beet Infused Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1896895.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds beets, peeled and cubed
1 liter vodka

Directions:
Place beets in a sealable container. Pour vodka over beets, seal container, and shake vigorously. Place container in a cool, dark place and store for 3 days, shaking twice per day.

Submitter: 
JonahLuck
Description: 
This is a full-flavored and earthy vodka that will keep you coming back.
