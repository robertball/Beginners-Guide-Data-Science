Title:
Mojito with Mojo (low carb) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413390.jpg
Ingredients:
2 leaves fresh mint
1 tablespoon Splenda mixed with 2 T water
crushed  ice
1 1/4 fluid ounces rum
1 fluid ounce carbonated water
1 sprig fresh mint
Lime wedge

Directions:
In a cocktail glass, muddle (crush) mint leaves with Splenda mixture. Add ice and rum. Squeze in some fresh lime juice to taste. Top with carbonated water. Garnish with a sprig of fresh mint.

Submitter: 
Parizienne
Description: 
Muddled mint and rum. Similar to a mint julep, but made with rum instead of bourbon. This cocktail has recently become popular again.Modified from: Mojito Cocktail
