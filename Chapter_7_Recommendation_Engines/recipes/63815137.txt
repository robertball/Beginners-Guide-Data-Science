Title:
Sparkling Cranberry Brie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 round wheat crackers
3oz Brie cheese, thinly sliced
Sugared Cranberries (recipe follows)

Directions:
Place crackers on serving platter. Top each cracker w/thin slice of Brie, and garnish w/3-4 sugared cranberries. Serve immediately.
Sugared Cranberries:
Place a wire rack w/sm. openings (so cranberries don't fall through) on foil-lined baking sheet; set aside. In sm. saucepan, combine 1/2C water and 1/2C sugar. Heat over med-low heat, stirring occ. until sugar is fully dissolved. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Add 1C fresh cranberries to saucepan and stir gently to coat. Using slotted spoon or strainer, remove cranberries from sugar and let drain. Place cranberries on rack. Let stand about 1hr. Roll cranberries in additional sugar.
