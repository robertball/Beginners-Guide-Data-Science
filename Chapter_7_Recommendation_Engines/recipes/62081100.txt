Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Servings of Instant Rice
1 Can of Del Monte diced tomatoes with zesty jalapenos
1 TBS of vegetable oil
1/2 of a medium green pepper
1/2 of a small onion
Salsa to taste
cumin, salt, pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Prepare rice according to package directions
2. While rice is cooking, dice green pepper and onions and saute in vegetable oil.
3. Add cumin, salt and pepper to taste
4. Add can of tomatoes to pepper and onions until heated through.
5. Add rice to vegetables and let simmer on low for 10 minutes.
6. Add salsa and serve
Great with tacos!!
NOTE: I've also added shredded cheese in the past, or even black beans and baked it as a casserole with tortilla shells!
