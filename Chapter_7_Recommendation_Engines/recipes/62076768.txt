Title:
sesame sugar snap peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of frozen sugar snap peas
1.5 Tablespoons black sesame seeds (white are fine, black are very pretty)
2 or 3 Tablespoons olive oil
Fresh cracked salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a microwave heat a bowl of water to boiling.  Drop the frozen sugar snap peas into the water and place back in the microwave for 1 minute.  Drain snap peas, add all the other ingredients and mix.

Submitter: 
Dan'ish
Description: 
This is a nice summer side dish for pretty much any kind of meat, or as a vegetarian meal.
