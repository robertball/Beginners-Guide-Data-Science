Title:
Easy Turtle Bars (no bake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups quick cooking oats
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
30 caramels, unwrapped
2 tbls cream or half & half or evaporated milk
1/2 cup pecans, chopped

Directions:
Grease a 9x9 inch square pan.
Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat. Stir in brown sugar and vanilla. Mix in the oats. Allow to cook on low heat 2 to 3 minutes, stirring frequently, until oats begin to smell toasted.
Press 2/3 of oat mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan. Reserve the other 1/3 for topping.
Sprinkle the chocolate chips evenly over oat layer.
Melt caramels & cream/milk in a small heavy saucepan over low heat, stirring frequently until smooth.
Pour the caramel mixture evenly over the chocolate layer.
Mix the remaining oat mixture with the pecans & crumble over the caramel layer, pressing in gently.
Cover, and refrigerate 2 to 3 hours or overnight.  Cut into 36 squares.

Submitter: 
LARANEFF
Description: 
These are easy to make in the summer when you dont want to turn on the oven, but want a good simple dessert.
