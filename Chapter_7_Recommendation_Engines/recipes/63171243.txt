Title:
Chicken Orzo Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole rotissere chicken(cooked)
1 med onion minced
1c carrots sliced
1c celery sliced
1/4c dry lentils
1/2c dry orzo
5 boullion chix or veg
1tsp salt
1tsp onion powder
1tsp garlic powder
1tsp dehydrated onion
1/2tsp black pepper
5c water

Directions:
Add water and chicken to pot.  Covered, let it come up to a boil and then immediately drop to a simmer for 10 min. Remove Chicken to cool some. Add onion, carrots,celery and all seasonings and lentils to simmer covered for 15 to 20 min.  DO NOT allow broth to boil.
Take all the meat off the chicken, carefully removing all bone, fat and skin.  break into small pieces and add back to the pot with the orzo.
Will be finished in 5 min or so.
Add the juice of 1/2 a lemon just before serving.

Yummy classic soup brightened up with a big squeeze of lemon and lentils!
