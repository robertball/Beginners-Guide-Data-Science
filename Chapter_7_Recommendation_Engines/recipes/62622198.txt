Title:
Venison Cottage Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C. Potatoes, peeled, cubed and boiled soft
1 T. Sea Salt
4 T. Butter
1/4 C. Milk
2 Leeks, diced
1 1/2 C. Carrots, diced
1 1/2 C. Frozen Kernel Corn
1 lb. Ground Venison
1 (14 oz) can Beef Broth
4 T. Corn Starch
2 T. Water
1/4 lb. Bacon, chopped

Directions:
In a large deep saucepan, fry bacon.  Drain, reserving 3 T. grease.  In the same saucepan, saute leeks and carrots in bacon grease for 2-3 minutes, crumble in venison.  Cook until venison is browned.  Add beef broth and corn, stir and simmer until boiling.  Mix cornstarch and water, pour into meat mixture and stir until thickened.  Mix in reserved bacon.  Mash potatoes, butter, salt and milk until smooth and creamy.  Pour meat mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.  Smooth mashed potatoes over top.  Bake at 425 for 15 minutes until mashed potatoes are lightly browned on top and mixture is bubbling.

Submitter: 
Abbey
Description: 
Vegetables and bacon are mixed with ground venison to make a different, delicious cottage pie.
