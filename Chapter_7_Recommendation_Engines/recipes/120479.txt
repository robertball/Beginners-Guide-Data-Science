Title:
Hot Tortellini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990960.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package refrigerated cheese tortellini
2 cups fresh broccoli florets
4 green onions, sliced
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
6 ounces fully cooked ham, julienned
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a 2-qt. microwave-safe bowl, combine the broccoli, onions and butter. cover and microwave on high for 2-1/2 minutes; stir. Cook 2-1/2 minutes longer or until broccoli is tender.
Drain tortellini. stir tortellini, ham, sour cream and basil into broccoli mixture. Cover and microwave on high for 1-2 minutes or until heated through. Let stand for 2 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Catherine  Allan
Description: 
Once you've cooked the tortellini, the rest of this salad is a breeze to finish in the microwave. Sour cream provides the mild coating over this memorable medley of pasta, ham and broccoli shared Catherine Allan of Twin Falls, Idaho.
