Title:
Beach Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Sub Roll, cut in half, lengthwise
3 Tbsp Bleu Cheese Dressing
1 Tomato, Sliced
1/4 C mozzarella

Directions:
Place sub roll halves open face up. Spread bleu cheese dressing on them, evenly. Top with tomato slices. Heat in 350 degree oven about 5 min. Top with mozzerella. Broil to lightly brown. Slice. Serve.

Submitter: 
Lisa Harmon
Description: 
I don't know if this is indigenous to Ft Myers Beach, but that's the only place I've had it.  Good Stuff!
