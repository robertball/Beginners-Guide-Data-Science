Title:
Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. soy sauce (optional)
dash of pepper
4 cups cooked cut green beans (3 cans)
1 1/3 cups (2.8 oz. can) french fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray  1 1/2 quart casserole dish with Pam.
Place beans in the casserole dish. Mix soup, milk, soy sauce, pepper and 2/3 cup onions together. Pour over beans.
Bake 25 minutes or until hot.
Stir. Sprinkle remaining 2/3 cup onions over bean mixture. Bake 5 minutes longer or until onions are golden.
