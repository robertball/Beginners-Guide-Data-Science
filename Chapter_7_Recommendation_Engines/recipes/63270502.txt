Title:
Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can pink salmon
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 egg
1 tablespoon spicy mustard
2 teaspoons dill
salt to taste
1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
Combine salmon (discard skin and bones), bread crumbs, egg, mustard, dill and salt.
heat olive oil in pan
form mixture into small patties
cook in pan over medium high heat until brown and crispy on both sides
