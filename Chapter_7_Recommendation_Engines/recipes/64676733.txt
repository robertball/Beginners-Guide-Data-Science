Title:
Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 lb ground lean beef
1/2 lb ground sweet Italian sausage
1/ cup cooked (canned) spinach, chopped fine
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 cloves garlic, finely  chopped
2 small onions, finely chopped
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2 small eggs
Dough:
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon oil (canola oil, not olive)
Yolks of 2 eggs, slightly beaten, reserve whites
1 cup lukewarm water
1 cup flour at first, then add 1/2 cup at a time until dough is coming away from the bowl

Directions:
Filling:
Mix very finely chopped ingredients and let stand overnight to ripen in refrigerator
Dough:
Mix above ingredients with a fork, except for flour, until well blended
Add flour, one cup at a time until batter is stiff enough to handle
Knead dough with flour
Cover bowl and let stand in warm room about 30 minutes to ripen
Roll on a floured board or table until dough is very thin
Spread half of the dough with meat filling, using a fork
Brush the other half of dough with beaten egg whites
Fold dough in half, sealing around outside edge
Roll over the dough with ravioli rolling-pin
Cut into squares with cutting wheel
Lay on cookie sheets lined with foil, in layers
Freeze thoroughly; bag after frozen
Boil:
Drop frozen ravioli into boiling salted water (add a drop of olive oil in water)
The ravioli will float when cooked.
