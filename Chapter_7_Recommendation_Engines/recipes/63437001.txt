Title:
TEX-MEX Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans frito bean dip
3 avocasos, mashed with 2 tsp. lemon and dash pepper
1 c. sour cream
1/2 cup mayo.
1 pkg. taco seasoning mix.
lettuce, tomato, green onion, chopped olives, and shredded longhorn or cheddar cheese.

Directions:
Mix together sour cream, mayo and taco seasoning.
On large flat dish, spread the bean dip
Top with avacado.
Top with sour cream mayo, taco mixture.
Sprinkle with chopped tomato,green onion, chopped oives, shredded cheese
Serve with Scoops or tortilla chips.
