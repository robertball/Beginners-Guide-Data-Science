Title:
Cheesy Margherita Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988163.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can Pillsbury速 refrigerated crescent dinner rolls or Pillsbury速 Crescent Recipe Creations速 refrigerated seamless dough sheet
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 1/2 cups shredded Italian cheese blend
3 medium plum (Roma) tomatoes, sliced
2 tablespoons fresh basil strips

Directions:
Separate or cut dough into 2 long rectangles (if using crescent rolls, press perforations to seal). Place on ungreased cookie sheet; press into 12x8-inch rectangle. Brush with oil.
Top with cheese and tomatoes.
Bake at 375 degrees F 12 to 15 minutes. Sprinkle with basil.

Submitter: 
Pillsbury速 Crescents
Description: 
A classic pizza trio -- fresh tomatoes, basil and mozzarella, tops a flaky Crescent crust, ready in less than 30 minutes.
