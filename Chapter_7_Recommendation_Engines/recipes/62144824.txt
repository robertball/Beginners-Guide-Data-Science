Title:
Cranberry Pear Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups mixed baby greens
3 medium pears, sliced thinly
1 cup dried cranberries
1 cup pecans: toasted, glazed or honey-roasted
3/4 cup cubed havarti cheese
1/2 cup sliced almonds, toasted
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
3/4 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon dark brown sugar
3/4 tablespoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
1. Whisk together vinegar, oil, sugar, garlic, salt and pepper. Set aside.
2. In large bowl, combine greens, pear slices, cranberries, pecans and cheese.
3. Add dressing to salad, toss well. Garnish with almonds. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
ChrissyCoole
Description: 
This delicious salad works well as a side or on it's own. Especially good in fall and winter months when fresh summer salad fare is harder to come by.
