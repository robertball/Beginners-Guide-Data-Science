Title:
Chicken tomato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 yellow onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
4 cups chicken broth
1 can (15oz) diced tomatoes
1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken, diced into bite sized pieces
3 tbs lime juice
1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat
Add onion, garlic, cumin, and cayenne. Cook and stir until onion is softened, about 4 minutes.
Add chicken broth and tomatoes. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
Add chicken and return to a simmer. Cook until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
Remove pan from heat and add lime juice.
Season with salt and pepper as desired.
Ladle into bowls and sprinkle with cilantro.
