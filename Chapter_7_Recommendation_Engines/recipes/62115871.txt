Title:
Chicken Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 roasting chicken, split into 2 halves
3 stalks of celery (with leaves) diced
1 medium onion diced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup of water
5 oz. Carlo Rossi Sangria
1 tablespoon dried parsley
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons black pepper

Directions:
preheat oven to 375
arrange chicken halves in a small roaster
top with onions and celery
in saucepan combine all other ingredients
heat on low until smooth
pour over chicken
bake at 375 for 1.5-2 hours until done

Submitter: 
Neicie
Description: 
Half chickens are simmered in a seasoned red wine and cream of mushroom sauce
