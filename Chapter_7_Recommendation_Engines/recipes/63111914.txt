Title:
Chicken and Brown Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793505.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups chicken broth
2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1 cup diced celery
1 cup diced onion
1/4 cup diced carrots
1/4 cup corn
1/4 cup black beans
1 tsp sage
1 bay leaf
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup brown rice

Directions:
In a large pot, bring chicken broth to a boil. Add chicken breasts and boil until cooked. Approx. 20 minutes.
With a fork, shred the chicken. Add the celery, onion, carrots, corn, black beans and spices. Cook another 20 minutes.
Add the brown rice and let simmer for 1 hour, or until the rice is soft.
