Title:
Cranberry Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/494392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) boneless rolled pork loin roast
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (16 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/4 cup honey
1 teaspoon grated orange peel
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
Cut roast in half and place in a slow cooker; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Combine the remaining ingredients; pour over roast. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or until a meat thermometer reads 145 degrees F (63 degrees C). Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Kimberley  Scasny
Description: 
'I love to serve guests this moist flavorful pork,' informs Kimberley Scasny of Douglasville, Georgia. 'You don't have to slave away in the kitchen to prepare it, yet it tastes like a gourmet meal.'
