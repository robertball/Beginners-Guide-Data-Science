Title:
Zuchinni Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups zucchini
2 red bell peppers
2 green bell peppers
4 cups onion
5 tbsp canning salt
1 tsp tumeric
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp celery seed
1/2 tbsp corn starch
2 1/2 cups white vinegar
4 1/2 cups sugar

Directions:
Grind zucchini, peppers, and onion.  Sprinkle salt over mixture and let stand overnight in the fridge.
Rinse in cold water.  Add the rest of the ingredients and cook over medium-low heat for 20-30 minutes.
Pour into canning jars and process in a hot water bath for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
A great way to rid yourself of extra zucchini.  Goes great on everything pickle relish does.
