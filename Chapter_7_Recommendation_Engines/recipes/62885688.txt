Title:
Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken bouillon cube
1/2 cup boiling water
1 cup pancake mix (not the complete kind)
1 egg
1 tbsp vegetable oil
Outer slices from 2 large onions

Directions:
Dissolve bouillon cube in boiling water.
Mix all remaining ingredients except the onions, adding the bouillon at the end.
Coat sliced onions in batter before dropping into 375Â° oil in a deep fat fryer.
When they float to the surface, turn once. Cook another 2 minutes or so and drain on paper towels.

A quick way to enjoy home-made onion rings.
