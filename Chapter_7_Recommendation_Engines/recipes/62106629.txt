Title:
No Cook Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of Elbow Macaroni cooked
2 cans drained Tuna
1 cup Mayonnaise (just enough coat)
1 1/2 tsp. salt (or to taste)
1 tsp. black pepper (or to taste)
1 tsp. celery seed (don't skip this)

Directions:
Combine well and refrigerate until cold.

Submitter: 
cmccreight
Description: 
Delicious, easy-to-make cold Tuna Noodle Casserole.  I put some on a bed of Bibb Lettuce and bring it for lunch at work.  My son shakes Louisiana Hot Sauce all over his and eats it that way. I'm suggesting spice amounts but it's really to taste.
