Title:
EVOO Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3063915.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2/3 cup EVOO
1 cup sugar
2 cups Manitoba flour, sifted
1 pinch of salt
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 185Â°C and prepare a baking sheet.
Cream together the eggs, oil, and sugar until smooth and become a pale golden color.
Add flour, salt and baking powder and mix until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips, if using.
Refrigerate the doigh for half an hour before baking.
Using a small spoon, drop a spoonful of dough on the prepared baking sheet approx 2 inches apart (these will spread).  Bake for 8 minutes or until just golden around the edges. Cool for a minute or two before transferring to a cooling rack. Refrigerate leftover dough while the cookies bake.
Proceed with the rest of the dough.
The cookies and dough freeze well. The dough will resist in the refrigerator up to 24 hours.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Extra virgin olive oil replaces butter in a simple sugar cookie. The results is a rich, dense cookie to sink your teeth into. These are great for dunking in your tea.
