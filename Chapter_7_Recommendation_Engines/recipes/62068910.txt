Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp. Oil
1 small onion chopped
2 Cloves Garlic Chopped
1 pound Ground Beef
1 Tbsp. Paprika
1-3 Tbsp. Flour
Salt & Pepper to taste
1/2 to 1 Cup Water
1 10.5 oz. can Cream of Chicken soup
1 8 ounce container Sour Cream

Directions:
1.  In a deep skillet add oil, chopped Onion & Chopped Garlic simmer on medium till onion and garlic is golden.
2.  Put ground beef into skillet and brown.
3.  Drain off any excess grease from beef and return to skillet.
4.  Add in Flour, Paprika, Salt & Pepper and brown.
5.  Add in water and stir quickly.
6.  Add can of Cream of Chicken soup and simmer 10 to 15 minutes just till it bubbles slightly.
7.  Just before serving stir in Sour Cream.
Serve over steaming hot Rice.  Also good served over Noodles.

Submitter: 
Dawn Raegene Mendenhall Scott
Description: 
Made with Cream of Chicken soup wrather than Cream of mushroom this is a recipe that will be a winner for years to come.  We have ours over rice.
