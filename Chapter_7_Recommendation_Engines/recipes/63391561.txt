Title:
White bean, kale, and grilled sausage soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1046507.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sweet onion, largely diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans navy or cannellini beans, drained
4 cups chicken broth
1/2-3/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
salt
1 large bay leaf
1 large bunch kale, ripped into 2" pieces
6 chicken-apple sausages
grated parmesan

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot and saute onions until soft and clear. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
Add beans and broth to pot and season with red pepper flakes and salt, then add the bay leaf.
Bring to a simmer.
While soup is simmering, lightly oil a griddle and grill sausages over med-high heat (about 12 minutes).
Let sausages rest about 5 minutes, then slice.
Stir the kale pieces and sausage into soup and simmer until kale is wilted (about 2-3 minutes).
Serve soup topped with parmesan.

Submitter: 
paralee
Description: 
Great hearty soup! The kale and beans are a little spicy, the sausage is a little sweet, and the parmesan tops it all off perfectly.
