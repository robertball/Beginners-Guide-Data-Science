Title:
Maple Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 salmon fillets
1 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 tsp. ginger
2 whole garlic cloves

Directions:
Combine syrup, soy sauce and ginger in a baking dish.
Place salmon in marinade and top with garlic cloves.
Marinade salmon for 30 minutes, flipping once half way through marinading time.
Place marinaded salmon on greased baking pan and bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes or until salmon is flaky but still moist.

Submitter: 
Hunter's Mom 2008
Description: 
A sweet and savoury combination that works great with salmon.
