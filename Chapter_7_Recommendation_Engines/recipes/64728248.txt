Title:
Hawaiian meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1/2tsp ginger
1/4tsp pepper
1/4tsp garlic powder
1 chopped green pepper
1 tin pineapple chunks
Sauce:
1/4c brown sugar
2 tbsp corn starch
1/4c white vinegar
1tbsp soy sauce

Directions:
Mix beef and spices.
Form into 1-1/2" meatballs.
Saut√© in a large frying pan (about 10min).
Remove once cooked and set aside.
To drippings, add the juice from the tinned pineapple plus water to make 1 cup.
Mix sugar and cornstarch.
Add to drippings.
Add vinegar and soy sauce.
Heat until mix thickens and boil for 2-3 minutes.
Add meatballs, pineapple and green pepper.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
