Title:
Cauliflower and olives Italian style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium size cauliflower
2 tablespoons of chopped Italian parsley
1 teaspoon of garlic
8 oz of green kalamata olives
3-4 tablespoons of olive oil
salt

Directions:
Wash the cauliflower and trim the bottom leaves.  Sit it in pot with a lid, drizzle the olive oil on top of the cauliflower.  Spread the olives on the bottom of the pot, all around the cauliflower. Chop the parsley and the garlic and spread on top of the cauliflower. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt.
Cook over medium to low heat until center is soft (test it with skewer).  Add a couple of tablespoon of water during cooking to avoid burning.
