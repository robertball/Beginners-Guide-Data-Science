Title:
Vegan Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3652992.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups coconut water
1 cup baby spinach
1 banana
6 sliced fresh strawberries
5 dates, pitted

Directions:
Blend coconut water, spinach, banana, strawberries, and dates together in a blender.
