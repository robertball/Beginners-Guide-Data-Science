Title:
Chick Pea Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of chick peas
1 green pepper
1 red pepper
1/4 red onion
1/4 jalapeno pepper
2 cups Catalina dressing
pepper to taste

Directions:
drain the can of chick peas
place chickpeas into a tupperware bowl with lid
dice red pepper
dice green pepper
chop onion into medium size chunks
finely dice jalapeno pepper
place all ingriedients on top of chick peas
add catalina dressing to bowl
add pepper if desired (not needed)
mix ingriedients
add more dressing to make more dip like if required
refridgerate for at least 1 hour to allow flavors to blend
place in a serving bowl and eat with tortilla chips just as you would serve a salsa. Also great with corn chips!
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Deanna
Description: 
A great, easy dip to make that's different from salsa or the usual dips you would have if company is coming over.  Also great to bring camping.  All vegetarian!
