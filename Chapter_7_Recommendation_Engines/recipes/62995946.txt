Title:
Pumpkin Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dried elbow mac
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
1 cup whipping cream
1 cup whole milk
4 oz Fontina cheese
1 15oz can of pumpkin
1/2 tsp sage
1/4 bread crumbs
1/4 Parmesan cheese
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350.  Cook pasta and return to pot.
2. In a medium saucepan, heat butter over medium heat.  Stir in flour, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper.  Add cream and milk all at once.  Cook on medium until slightly thickened and bubbly.  Stir in Fontina, pumpkin and sage until cheese is melted.  Stir into pasta and transfer to a baking dish.
3. In Bowl, combine bread crumbs, Parmesan and oil.  Sprinkle over pasta.  Bake uncovered 30 mins or until bubbly.  Let stand 10 mins before serving.
