Title:
Raspberry Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups sifted powdered sugar
1 1/2 t. raspberry extract
3 drops red coloring
1 drop blue food coloring

Directions:
Using an electric mixer, cream the butter and the cream cheese until fluffy.
Add the raspberry extract and food coloring. Mix until combined.
Add the powdered sugar slowly. Beat the frosting on medium-low until combined.
Be sure to stop the mixer and scrap down the sides of the mixing bowl with a spatula to make sure everything is getting mixed.

Submitter: 
Alisan
Description: 
I needed a raspberry cream cheese frosting for my raspberry cupcakes and couldn't find any recipes! So I made one up by myself. And it is delicious! I could probably just eat the frosting by itself. The food coloring is not mandatory, just if you want it to be a pretty purple color.
