Title:
Super coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 t. salt, 1/2 t. dry mustard, 2 T. sugar, 1 T. red pimento (or 1 T.red pepper), 1/3 C. vinegar, 1/4 t. pepper, 1 t.celery seed, 1/4 C. green pepper, 1/2 t. grated onion, 3 T. salad oil, 3 C. shredded cabbage.

Directions:
Place ingredients in large bowl in order given. Blend well, cover and refridgerate minimum of 1 hour. Better flavor if refridgerated overnight. Very good flavor and freezes beautifully.

Description: 
A recipe which was requested at all church potlucks for my grandmother to make.
