Title:
Christen's Energy Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dry oats
2/3 cup coconut flakes
1/2 cup of peanut butter
2 tablespoons flax seed
1 tablespoon chia seed (optional)
1/2 cup mini-chocolate chips
1/4 cup honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
dash of cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in medium sized bowl. Mixture will form one large ball. Divide, by hand, into 12-15 1 inch balls. Refrigerate for 2 hours in a sealed container. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Christen Cline-Schultz
Description: 
A family pleaser! Kids love as well as adults. Makes a great mid-morning, after a work out, after school snack. Fun to make!
