Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup oil
3 cups sugar
2 cups peeled and grated zucchini
3 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
3/4 tsp. nutmeg
3 tsp. cinnamon
(1 cup nuts and rasins (optional))

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl, and spead  in a greased loaf pan(s).
Bake for 1 hour.
