Title:
Tuna Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups noodles(the no boil kind)
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can mixed vegetables (do not drain)
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 cups plain potato chips (crushed)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix soup with milk and vegetables.
Place noodles in bottom of baking dish.
Pour soup mixture over top.
Sprinkle on the cheese and salt & pepper.
Top with potato chips.
Bake for 30 mins.
