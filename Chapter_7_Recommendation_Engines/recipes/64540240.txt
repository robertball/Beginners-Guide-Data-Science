Title:
Instant Pot Potato & Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs bacon grease or butter
2 leeks, cleaned and sliced
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 cup onion, diced finely
3 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
4 cups chicken broth
1 lb bacon
1 cup heavy cream
1 Tbs cornstarch

Directions:
Select Saute. Saute leeks, garlic and onion and bacon grease or butter in inner pot until onion & leek are softened, about 3 minutes. Select Keep Warm/Cancel
Add potatoes and broth.
Close and lock lid and shut vent. Select Manual and 7 minutes cook time.
Cook bacon while soup is cooking.
Let the pressure come down naturally for 5 minutes then release the remaining pressure.
Add the cornstarch to the cream and pour mixture into pot.
Select Keep Warm/Cancel and then select Saute. Let cook until thickened, about 5 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
Garnish with cooked bacon.
You can also garnish with cheddar, green onion, sour cream.
