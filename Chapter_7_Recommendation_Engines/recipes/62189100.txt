Title:
Kimmie's Chile Colorado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  lbs stew meat cut into chunks
2 tablespoosn flour
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 large can red enchilada sauce
1 large can chile colorado sauce
1 onion cut into slices
6  small potatoes cut into chunks
2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cut meat into chunks and place in a plastic bag with flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon of garlic powder. Heat pan with oil, saute meat and onion until browned, add the rest of the garlic powder, oregano and both cans of sauce.  Bring down to a simmer, add potatoes and cook for 75 to 90 minutes, until meat is tender, add salt to taste and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Camille
Description: 
A slightly spicy, warm and delicious chile, filled with beef, potatoes and onions. A hit at every get together, that goes great with spanish rice!
