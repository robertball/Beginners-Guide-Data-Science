Title:
Dal Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups lentils, boiled until soft
1 whole yellow onion, chopped
1 bunch cilantro, finely chopped
1 large tomato, diced
14 ounces lite coconut milk
8 ounces tomato sauce
1 tsp curry powder, or to taste
1/4 tsp turmeric, or to taste
1/2 tsp ground ginger, or to taste
1/4 tsp ground clove, or to taste
1/2 tsp ground cumin, or to taste
1/4 tsp cayenne, or to taste
1 tsp salt, or to taste

Directions:
Saute onion until tender.  Add cilantro and tomato and let get a little soft.
Add rest of ingredients and let simmer for about 30 minutes.
