Title:
Grilled Curry Chicken Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken breast cut into thick strips
2 tbs sesame oil
2 tbs vegetable oil
1tsp yellow curry
1tsp garlic powder
1tsp thyme
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix oils, curry, garlic powder, salt and thyme in a bowl.  Place chicken in the marinade bowl and mix unti we'l coated.  Place in refrigerator for an hour.
Soak 10 wooden skewers in water for 60 minutes.
Preheat a grill on low heat for 30 minutes.
Thread chicken on skewers. Place on heated grill and cook 8 minutes per side.

This chicken is full of flavor and super juicy.  The yellow curry gives the chicken a gorgeous golden hue. I like to serve this with my peanut sauce recipe, baked potatoes and steamed asparagus. It would taste great over rice or in pita.
