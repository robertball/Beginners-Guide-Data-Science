Title:
Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 3/4 cups milk
3 Tbsp sugar
1 1/2 cups sifted flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3 tsp oil
1/4 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Beat egg with milk and sugar.
Add dry ingredients in three siftings.
Stir in oil and vanilla.
Drop batter onto hot griddle.
