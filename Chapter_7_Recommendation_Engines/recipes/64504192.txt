Title:
Ed's Sweet and Sour Meatball Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2  Cups           Brown sugar
6 Tbsp.                           Corn Starch
1/2 Cup                          Vinegar
4 Tbsp.                           Soy Sauce
1/2 Cup                            Ketchup
2 .5 Cups                              Water
1/2 Cup                           Pineapple  juice

Directions:
In a large saucepan combine all the ingredients and bring to a boil and mix for ten minutes.
Pour into a slow cooker containing approx. 60 already cooked meatballs enough to cover.
Mix evenly and keep warm until served.
