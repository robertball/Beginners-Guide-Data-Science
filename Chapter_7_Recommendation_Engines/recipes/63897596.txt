Title:
Buffalo Chicken Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground chicken
1 egg
1/2 cup unseasoned regular or panko breadcrumbs
1 carrot
1 stalk of celery
2 green onions
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 oz. crumbled blue cheese
1/2 cup Frank's Hot Sauce, divided
1 tbsp. butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Line cookie sheet with tin foil and spray with cooking spray.
Cut celery, carrot and green onion into chunks then place in food processor and pulse to chop into small bits.
An a large bowl, combine vegetable mixture, 1/4 cup of the hot sauce and except for the butter, add remaining ingredients.
Gently fold to combine, but don't over mix.
Form into small meatballs and place on cookie sheet.
Bake for 20 minutes.
In a microwave safe bowl, combine butter and remaining hot sauce.
Microwave about 30 seconds, or until butter melts.
Stir well to combine.
Remove meatballs from the oven, cool slightly and then place on serving dish.
Drizzle with butter/hot sauce mixture and serve.
