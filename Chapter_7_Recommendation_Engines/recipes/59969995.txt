Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (14 oz) beef broth
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon onion salt
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 bay leaf
2 (.7 ounce) pkgs dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1 (5 pound) rump or chuck roast
Italian rolls/hoagies, toasted
Slices provolone cheese

Directions:
Combine broth with salt, ground black pepper, oregano, basil, onion salt, parsley, garlic powder, bay leaf, and salad dressing mix in a saucepan. Stir well, and bring to a boil.
Place roast in slow cooker, and pour salad dressing mixture over the meat.
Cover, and cook on Low for 10 to 12 hours, or on High for 4 to 5 hours. Anytime after 8 hrs, remove meat and bay leaf.  Shred meat and return to cooker for 1/2 hour.
Toast rolls, add meat, then place under broiler with cheese.

Submitter: 
FrogsMom
Description: 
This makes a sandwich similar to one I used to get at a local restaurant when I lived in a suburb of Chicago. My all time favorite! Serve on crusty rolls with roasted sweet or hot peppers, if desired.Modified from: Slow Cooker Italian Beef for Sandwiches
