Title:
Oh Henry Butterscotch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
whole graham crackers
3/4 cup butter or margarine
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
Icing:
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
3 tbsp. butter or margarine
1 1/2 tsp. water
1/2 tsp. vanilla or almond extract

Directions:
Line ungreased 9" x 9" pan with whole crackers, trimming to fit.
Mix butter, sugar, milk and vanilla in saucepan.
Bring to boil and simmer x 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
Stir in crumbs. Pour over crackers in pan.
Cover with another layer of whole crackers, trimming to fit.
Cool. Ice.
Icing:
Beat ingredients together until smooth, adjusting water and sugar to reach desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Layers of butterscotch filling between graham crackers - this tastes better if it sits awhile as the crackers soften.
