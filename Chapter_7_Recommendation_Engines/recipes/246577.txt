Title:
Pasta Rybna (Mackerel Spread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7054343.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ounces canned mackerel fillets in oil
2 hard-boiled eggs, diced
1 onion, chopped
1 tablespoon mayonnaise (optional)
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste (optional)
1 pinch ground black pepper

Directions:
Drain mackerel and place in a large bowl. Add eggs, onion, mayonnaise, parsley, and black pepper; mix well to combine.

Submitter: 
Jola
Description: 
This quick and easy mackerel spread is easy to whip up in a hurry and serve as a starter or as a party dip with crusty bread or crackers.
