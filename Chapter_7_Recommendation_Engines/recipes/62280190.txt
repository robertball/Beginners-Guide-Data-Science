Title:
Mayonnaise Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of all-purpose flour
1 cup of sugar
4 Tbsp. cocoa
2 tsp. baking soda
1 cup of mayonnaise
1 cup of water
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly. Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour or until a cake tester comes out clean. Bake in a greased Bundt pan or angel food cake pan.
