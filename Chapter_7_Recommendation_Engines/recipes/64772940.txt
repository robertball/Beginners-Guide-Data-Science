Title:
Black Bean Burgers with Lime Cabbage Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion
1 can black beans
1 chipotle pepper
Bread crumbs
Greek yogut
Vinager
1 lime
red pepper
grated cabbage

Directions:
1. Saute onion in olive oil until browned. Remove and set aside.
2. Make burgers. Add beans to a bowl and smash until almost smooth. Add onion, chipotle pepper, bread crumbs and combine. Form into two patties. Chill in freezer for 5-10 minutes.
3. Roast pepper. Roas in the broiler until charred. About 10 mins. Flip halfway through. Cool 5 mins then mince.
4. Make slaw. Combine half of greek yogurt, vinegar, lime zest, juice of half lime, and 1 tablespoon olive oil. Add salt and pepper as needed. Add carrot and cabbage. Toss to coat. Set aside.
5. Make spread. Toast buns. Meanwhile, combine red bell peepper, remaining yoagurt, and 1 tablespoon of adobe sauce. Taste and salt.
6. Cook burger. Wipe pan from onion and add a little olive oil over med-high heat. Add burgers and cooke until warmed through and browned. About 4 min per side. Place burgeres on bun, slalther on sauce and add tops. Serve with slaw.
