Title:
The Best Chili! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 slices bacon
2 T. fresh parsley
1 small or ½ medium onion, finely chopped
1 stalk celery, finely chopped
½ each red and green pepper, chopped into small pieces
1 lb. ground beef
1 15oz. can regular diced or stewed tomatoes, pureed smooth in food   processor or with stick blender
3-4 8oz. cans tomato sauce
1 10 oz. can diced tomatoes with green chiles
1 c. salsa
1 15 oz. can EACH of kidney beans and black beans
8 oz. frozen corn
1-2 cloves garlic
1 T. chili powder
¼ tsp. cumin
dash worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Fry bacon in dutch oven.  Remove and set aside.
Sauté parsley, onion, celery, and green pepper in bacon drippings until onions are translucent and other veggies begin to soften.
Add ground beef and brown.  Drain off fat.
Add pureed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomatoes with  chiles and salsa. Add kidney beans right from the can, but rinse and drain black beans before adding. Add corn, garlic and remaining spices.  Bring back to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer until veggies are tender, about 30-40 minutes.
Top with desired items, such as sour cream, cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon, and/or green onions.
Enjoy!
