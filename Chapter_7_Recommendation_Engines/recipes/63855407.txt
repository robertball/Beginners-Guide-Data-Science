Title:
Dr. Jen's Kale Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of kale leaves, washed, stems removed
1/2 cup basil leaves, washed, patted dry
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup walnut pieces
2 cloves garlic, skins removed

Directions:
Add all ingredients to food processor, blend until smooth. Add 1 tbsp of water if you want a thinner pesto.

Submitter: 
skonkle
Description: 
A version of pesto with a healthy twist, created by my naturopathic doctor friend Jen.
