Title:
Colorful Zucchini Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, sliced
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tomato, chopped
1 zucchini, peeled and cut into 1 inch thick slices
1 red bell pepper, chopped
salt black pepper to taste
1/4 cup uncooked white rice
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Heat oil in a saute pan over medium heat.
Add onion and garlic, and cook for about 3 minutes.
Add tomatoes, zucchini, and red pepper.
Stir in rice and water.
Season with salt and black pepper.
Cover, and simmer over low heat for 20 minutes.
