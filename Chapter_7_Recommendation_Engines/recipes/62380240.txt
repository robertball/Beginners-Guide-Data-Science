Title:
Zucchini Orange Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
1 C. veg. oil
1 1/2 C. sugar
1 3/4 C Unpeeled zucchini, grated
1 1/4 C Orange juice
2 T. grated orange peel
1 C. chopped nuts
1 t. vanilla
3 C. flour
1/2 t. salt
1 t. baking soda
1/4 t. baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Mix first eight ingredients together. Add remain ingredients.
Grease and flour pans.
Bake at 350° for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a very moist and flavorful bread recipe.
