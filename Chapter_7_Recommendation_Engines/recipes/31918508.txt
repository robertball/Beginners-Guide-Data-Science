Title:
Mock HOLLANDAISE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour
3/4 cup scalded milk
3 egg yolks
1 tablespoon strained lemon juice

Directions:
Melt 2 tablespoons butter, and blend will with an equal amount of flour. Stirring constantly, and add 3/4 cup scalded milk, salt, pepper, and a few grains of cayenne to taste. Bring this mixture to the boiling point, beating all the while; then remove from the fire, and stir in 3 egg yolks and, bit by bit, 1/2 cup butter. Finish with 1 tablespoon strained lemon juice.
