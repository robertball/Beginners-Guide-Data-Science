Title:
Steak Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4503103.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 teaspoon Montreal steak seasoning
4 hamburger buns, split

Directions:
Mix ground beef and Montreal steak seasoning together in a bowl. Form mixture into 4 patties.
Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Grill patties until browned, 5 to 6 minutes per side.
Serve grilled patties on hamburger buns.

Description: 
I enjoy making these hamburgers because they have that steak flavor in it. I just serve them right off the grill, they are bursting with flavor.
