Title:
Pecan Crescents 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 cups flour
2 cups chopped pecans
5 Tbsp sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1 Tbsp water
1/2 tsp salt
Powdered sugar to coat

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Cream butter
Stir sugar, water, and vanilla into the creamed butter
Sift flour and salt together and add gradually to butter mixture
Add chopped pecans
Roll mixture in hands into crescent shape
Bake at 325 for 15-25 minutes (top should not get brown but bottoms will be golden brown)
Let cool slightly, then toss in powdered sugar (works best if the cookies are still a bit warm)

Submitter: 
Melissa Hoffmann Rung
Description: 
Got this recipe from a colleague who made these for a cookie exchange.  They were light and crumbly and absolutely perfect!
