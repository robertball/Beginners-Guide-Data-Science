Title:
DVO-Thruwurkin Creamy PB Dip for Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
2 TB minced crisp bacon
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp Prepared Horseradish
sliced, unpeeled red eating apple (red delicious?)

Directions:
combine peanut butter and bacon
beat in sour cream
stir in horseradish
serve as dip for apples

Submitter: 
KirbyZmom
Description: 
came from Cook n'/DVO. i would be making this with low fat sour cream and unsalted peanut butter. also, i would be using honeycrisp apples instead of red delicious.
