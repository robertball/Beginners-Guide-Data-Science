Title:
Easy Sausage Muffins! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of Bisquick
1 lb cooked sausage
4 eggs beaten
1 cup of shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix all together...scoop into greased muffin tins
bake  350 degrees 20 minutes. YUM! Great for a cold night
