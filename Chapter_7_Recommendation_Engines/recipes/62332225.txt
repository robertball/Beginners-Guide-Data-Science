Title:
Caribbean Shrimp over Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb shrimp
2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 mangos, peeled and cubed
1 papaya, peeled and cubed
1/4 cup pineapple juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp lime juice
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
1/2 tsp cornstarch
3 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
2 cups cooked jasmine/basmati rice

Directions:

3. Mix mango, papaya, pineapple juice, sugar, salt and lime juice together in the skillet. Add vinegar and cornstarch mixture.
4. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Cook for one minute or until mixture thickens.
5. Add shrimp and reduce heat to low. Simmer for one minute, stirring constantly.
6. Remove from heat. Serve over rice and top with cilantro.
