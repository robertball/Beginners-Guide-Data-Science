Title:
Juicy Mustard Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless pork loin chops
Olive oil cooking spray
1 tablespoon yellow mustard powder
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon white pepper
2 tablespoon light soy sauce
50g dried parsley leaves
1/2 onions, slices

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, mix together soy sauce, sugar and white pepper. Dip pork chops into the mixture and turn the chops several times to coat. Sprinkle mustard powder and parsley leaves on both sides of chops. Marinate for 30 minutes
Heat the saucepan coated with olive oil cooking spray. Saute onions with high heat
Add pork chops to onions. Saute each side for 2-3 minutes or until it's golden brown. Reduce the heat to low and cook for another 3 minutes or until center is no longer pink.
Serve with Thai rice and freshly boiled broccoli

Submitter: 
Anita Chen
Description: 
Yummy pork chop marinated in soysauce and mustard powder then sauted with onions. An easy & quick meal for all working moms!
