Title:
Chicken Ala King Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup green pepper
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
Salt and Pepper
1 3/4 cup chicken broth
2 cup cream
2 cups cubed chicken
1 jar pimento

Directions:
Saute mushrooms and green pepper in butter for 5 minutes
Blend in flour and salt and pepper
Cook until bubbly over low heat
Remove and stir in cream, broth and mushroom liquid
Heat till boiling stirring constantly
Boil and stir 1 minute
Stir in chicken and pimento
Serve over Peppridge Farm Pastry Shells
