Title:
Turkey or Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c cubed swiss cheese
2 c chopped vegies (often broccoli)
2 c chopped cooked chicken or turkey
2 c cubed buttered bread
sauce:
1 cup mayo
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 T lemon juice

Directions:
spray cake pan with pam
mix sauce and add meat, vegies and cheese, stir to coat with sauce
pour mixture into 9 x 13 pan
cover with cubed buttered bread
do not cover
cook 350 degrees 45 min
