Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces BACARDI速 Superior Rum
12 fresh spearmint leaves
1/2 lime
2 tablespoons simple syrup
Top with club soda

Directions:
Muddle mint leaves and lime in a tall glass. Cover with simple syrup and fill glass with ice. Add BACARDI(R) Rum and club soda; stir well. Garnish glass with lime wedge and sprigs of mint.
