Title:
English Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sugar
1 c butter
3 tbsp water
1 tsp vanilla
1 c finely chopped walnuts or pecans
1 1/2 c semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Butter the bottom of 10x15 sheet pan and set aside.
Place sugar, butter, water, and vanilla in 2-3 qt heavy-bottomed saucepan.
Stirring constantly, boil mixture over med-high heat, until golden brown and glossy - approximately 10 minutes. Watch closely to prevent scorching.
Mixture is ready when candy thermometer reaches 305 degrees or when a drop placed into a cup of cold water immediately hardens to crack stage.
Pour hot mixture onto prepared sheet pan and spread to make thin layer.
Immediately and evenly pour chocolate chips over hot mixture. As chips soften, spread chocolate like frosting and sprinkle with nuts.
Place in a cool place for candy to harden.
Break into pieces.
