Title:
Betty Crocker Bread Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chopped celery
3/4 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup margarine or butter
9 cups soft bread cubes
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground sage
1/2 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
The day before you stuff the bird:
Melt butter in kettle and add onion and celery.   Cook on medium-low until mostly tender.
Cube bread and add salt, sage, thyme and pepper.
Add butter/celery/onion mixture and stir until well blended.
Cover and refrigerate over night.
May be stuffed or put in a crock pot (add 1/4 cup of chicken broth and 1/8 cup of butter per single batch) for 4 hours on low or baked at 325 for 30 minutes covered and 15 minutes uncovered.
