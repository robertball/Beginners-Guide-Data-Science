Title:
After the Holiday Stove Top /Slow-Cooker Turkey Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound shredded, cooked turkey/chicken
1 (796 ml) can whole peeled tomatoes, mashed
1 jar enchilada sauce
1 medium onion, chopped
1 (127 ml) can chopped green chilies
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 cups turkey/chicken broth
1 1/2 - 2 cups water
1 cup salsa
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 bunch fresh chopped cilantro
crumbled corn tortilla chips

Directions:
Place turkey/chicken, tomatoes, enchilada sauce, onion, green chiles, and garlic into the pot.  Add turkey/chicken broth, water and salsa. Season with chili powder, salt and pepper.  Simmer about 2 hours. A few minutes before it's done, add the cilantro and cook unti wilted. Serve with sour cream, guacamole and tortilla crumbs.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
A quick, no-fuss version of chicken tortilla soup! All you do is put everything into the pot, and turn it on. Then garnish with sour cream guacamole and crushed tortilla chips!Modified from: Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup
