Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
3-1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
3 Tbsp oil
3/4 cup milk
1 cup mashed ripe banana (2-3 medium)
1 egg
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease one 9x5x3 inch loaf pan or two 8-1/2x4-1/2x2-1/2 inch loaf pans.  Measure all ingredients into a large mixer bowl.  Beat on medium speed 1/2 minute.
Pour into pans.  Bake 55-65 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.  Remove from pan and cool.
