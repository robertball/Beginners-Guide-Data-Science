Title:
Caramel Cream Coffee Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 to 1/2 cup of Half n half (or 2 % milk)
1 cup Vanilla ice cream
2 tablespoons Caramel Syrup
1 teaspoons instant coffee of choice
2 tablespoons hot water
2 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
Combine instant coffee, hot water and sugar in sealable jar.
Shake until foamy.
Combine ice cream, caramel syrup, half n half, in a blender.
Add coffee mixture, slowly.
Blend to desired thickness.
Add less coffee mixture, if you like it thick.
Add more coffee mixture, if you like it thin.
Pour into a glass, top with whipped cream and caramel.
(If you would rather have it, on the rocks)
Omit ice cream.
Prepare instant coffee mixture, add 2-4 tablespoons sugar.
The sweeter, the better.
Add 1 cup half n half or milk to coffee mixture.
2-4 tablespoons of Caramel syrup.
Shake until your satisfied.
Serve on ice.
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Suzanne Frideres
Description: 
This is for frozen coffee drink lovers.
I used to tend a bar.
I wanted a sweet coffee shake to taste like those at expensive coffee places.
My family LOVES this.
