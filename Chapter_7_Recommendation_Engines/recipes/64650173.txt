Title:
Franzeya's Quick Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 ounces button mushrooms, cleaned and stems removed
1 box Instant stuffing mix
1 cup or less of celery chopped up
Half of a large onion diced
2 eggs
1 can or more of cream of mushroom
sage to taste
1-2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
chicken broth to taste and moisten stuffing mix
1-2 sticks melted butter

Directions:
Melt butter and place in a large baking dish. Place the mushrooms in pan.
Mix the chopped up mushroom stems, with 1 cup of cheese, set aside. Boil the celery and onion and set aside.
Make the stuffing according to package. Mix in onion and celery, mix in cream of mushroom, eggs, sage, mix in the mushroom stems and 1 cup of cheddar cheese. Mix in chicken broth. Once mixed to liking , stuff the mushrooms with stuffing and top with cheddar cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees until mushrooms are tender and cheese is melted.
