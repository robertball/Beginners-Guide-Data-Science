Title:
Country Style Vanilla Homemade Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
5 Cups Milk
2 Tablespoons Vanilla
2 1/2 Cups Sugar
4 Cups Whipping Cream
1/4 Teaspoon Salt

Directions:
In a large mixing blow beat eggs until foamy.  Slowly add sugar, beat until thickened.  Add cream, vanilla and salt and mix well.  Pour into can.  Add milk to fill line on can and stir well.  Turn in ice cream freezer until thick.  Makes 4 Quarts.
