Title:
PINEAPPLE CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cans pineapple chunks, drained
2/3 cup sugar
5 tbs. plain flour
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 stack Ritz crackers, crushed
1 stick butter, melted (or marjarine)

Directions:
Place pineapple in 9" x 9" baking dish.  Mix sugar & flour.  Sprinkle  over pineapple.  Then sprinkle on cheese.  Then, the cracker crumbs.  Drizzle melted butter over the dish evenly.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.  Serve hot.
