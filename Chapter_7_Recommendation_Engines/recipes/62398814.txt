Title:
Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf french bread
Margarine
Cheddar cheese, grated
Jack cheese, grated

Directions:
Cut loaf length wise and and then into strips. Spread bread with margarine. Mix grated cheeses together and cover bread heavily. Put in oven at 400 until cheese is melted.
