Title:
Sourdough Poppy Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sourdough
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1 teaspoon salt
4 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in bread machine in the order listed. Select dough cycle and start. When cycle is completed, divide dough into 2 pieces, shape into round loaves, and place on a greased cookie sheet. Cover with clean towel and let rise until doubled. Bake at 350 degrees F for 25 minutes or until browned.

Submitter: 
Sally
Description: 
This recipe makes two beautiful round loaves.
