Title:
Italian Red Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
**MEATBALLS**
2 pound lean ground beef
1 cup fresh bread crumbs
1.5 TBSP dried parsley
2 TBSP grated Parmesan cheese
1/8 tsp salt
1/2 tsp(just under) ground black pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp onion powder
1.5 egg, beaten
pinch fresh ground italion season-6turns/shakes in a fine grinder
2TBSP water to moisten(optional)
**SAUCE**
1 small onion chopped
1 rounded TBSP jar minced garlic,(5-6cloves)
1/4 cup olive oil
2 (28 oz) cans 'kitchen ready' tomatoes
1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 TBSP white sugar
2 bay leaf
3/4 tsp dried basil
1 whole carrot peeled
fresh ground italian season to taste(10 turns/shakes in a fine grinder)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix beef, eggs, bread crumbs, parsley, parm.cheese, salt, pepper, garlic+onion powder,H20+it. season.  Mix well and form into 2-3inch balls. It is important to roll well. Store, covered, in refrigerator until needed.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.  Stir in 2 lg tomato cans+ 1/2 can H20, salt, pepper, basil, italian season, sugar, bay leaves, and carrot(carrot neutralizes the acid).  Cover, heat to low, and simmer 30 minutes. Add  Stir in tomato paste,and simmer 30 minutes more. Add meatballs and simmer untill cooked 2 1/2-3hours. Discard the carrot and serve over pasta.
