Title:
Slow Cooker Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts
3 tbsp. flour
1/3 c. orange marmalade
1/3 c. barbecue sauce
2 tbsp. soy sauce
1 tbsp. grated fresh gingerroot

Directions:
Toss chicken with flour in slow cooker.  Stir in all remaining ingredients and cover with lid.  Cook on LOW for 6 to 8 hours or on HIGH for 3 to 4 hours.
