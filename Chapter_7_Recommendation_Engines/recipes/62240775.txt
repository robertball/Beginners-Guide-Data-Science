Title:
Chicken Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/327009.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (16oz) of rice noodles
2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced in strips
1 tbsp oil
4 carrots cut thin
1/2 lbs bean sprouts
1 leek sliced thin
4 green onions cut 1/2 inch long
1 tbsp salt or to taste
ground red cayenne pepper to taste (i like it spicy)
1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
1 package (both inner envelopes) of "Taste of Thai peanut sauce mix"
4 oz spanish peanuts beaten to little pieces

Directions:
1.Soak rice noodles in  warm water for 45 minutes until tender yet still firm.
2.On high heat add oil in large pan or wok.
3.Add chicken breast when oil is hot. Add carrots, leek, green onion and sprouts; saute 5 minutes or until all chicken is whitened and cut carrots are softened
4.Add coconut milk and sauce mix, stir and bring to a boil while stirring for 3 minutes.
5.Add drained noodles, mix everything together.
6.Add red cayenne pepper to taste and mix in ground peanuts
7. Cook until noodles are firm yet tender to taste. About 3 minutes.
