Title:
Lemon Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. beef flank steak or bonless beef top sirloin steak
1 tsp grated lemon peel
1/3 cup lemon juice
2 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp tsp oregano
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Score meat in 1-inch intervals across steak. Place meat in freezer bags. Stir together marinade ingredients. Place marinade in freezer bag.
Serving Day:
Thaw. Place meat on grill for 12-14 minutes for medium doneness. Turn once during cooking and brush with marinade halfway through cooking process. (Always boil marinade 2-3 minutes after removing meat and prior to basting grilled steak.
