Title:
Sweet Potato Carrot Spiced Life Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 large sweet potatoes, peeled
3 large carrots
1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root, peeled
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 pinch ground nutmeg

Directions:
Juice sweet potatoes, carrots, and ginger in a juicer according to manufacturer's instructions. Stir ground cinnamon and nutmeg into juice.

Submitter: 
Ety Deutsch
Description: 
This spicy, fresh-pressed juice will give you the energy you need for the holidays!
