Title:
Mixed Berries fruit pocket - diabetic and kidney friendly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/945684.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups fruit as available eg:raspberries, blackberries, strawberries, huckleberries, or even cherries
4 6" pie crusts
3 tablespoons splenda divided
2 tablespoons of sugarfree berry preserves

Directions:
reheat oven to 425° F.
2.Roll dough into 4 6” circles. Place on a baking sheets lightly coated with cooking spray.
3.In a medium bowl, combine berries and 2 tablespoons Splenda® sugar substitute.
4. melt the preserves and add berries and mix well - add 1 tablespoon of splenda to mixture.
5.Arrange 3/4 cup mixture in center of each dough leaving a 2” border. Fold edges of dough across the center, pressing gently to seal.
6.Bake at 425° F for 10 minutes; reduce heat to 350° F and bake an additional 20 minutes.
7.Remove from oven brush with butter, and sprinkle with Splenda®.
8.Serve warm or at room temperature.
