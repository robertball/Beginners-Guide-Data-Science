Title:
Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
29 oz can peaches in syrup
1/3 cup sugar
1 tbsp cornstarch
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup flour
1/3 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 9 inch pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Poke holes in crust with fork and prebake according to package directions. Drain peach slices, reserving 3/4 cup syrup.  Combine sugar and cornstarch in small pan.  Slowly add reserved peach syrup.  Stir until smooth.  Add vanilla and 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon.  Cook over med-low, stirring, until thickened.  Set aside. Combine brown sugar, flour, oats, butter and cinnamon in small bowl.  Stir until crumbly, set aside.  Layer peaches sauce and crumb topping in crust.  Bake 45 minutes.
