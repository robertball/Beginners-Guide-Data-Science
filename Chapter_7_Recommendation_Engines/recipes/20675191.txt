Title:
The Apple Pie To End All Apple Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
3 Tbsp flour
1 tsp ground cinnamon
6 large tart apples, pared, cored and sliced thin
unbaked 9-inch pie shell
2 Tbsp butter or margarine
Topping:
1 cup lightly spooned all purpose flour
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 cup butter or margarine

Directions:
Combine sugar, brown sugar, flour and cinnamon ina large mixing bowl.
Add sliced apples and toss well to mix.
Fill pie shell with mixture and and dot with 2 Tbsp butter.
For topping, combine flour, brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly and scatter thickly over apple filling.
Bake at 400F for 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
This mile-high apple pie is a cross between a pie and a crumble. So easy and delicious!
