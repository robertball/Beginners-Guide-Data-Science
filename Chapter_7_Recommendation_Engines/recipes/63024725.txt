Title:
Lime Sherbert Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757994.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts lime sherbert
2 liters ginger ale
1 (46oz) can pineapple juice
lemon slices
lime slices
maraschino cherries

Directions:
In a punch bowl, add 2 quarts of lime sherbet. Then add ginger ale and pineapple juice. Decorate with lemon and lime slices and some maraschino cherries.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
A holiday favorite that everyone looks for year after year. You'll learn to buy the lime sherbert in advance since the stores sell out around the holidays.
