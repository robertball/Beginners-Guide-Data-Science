Title:
Fettuccine w shripm, garlic & broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
1 med sweet onion diced
1 med carrot, peeled & diced
1 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
1/4  tsp red pepper flakes
1 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
3/4 lbs frozen med shrimp
8 oz whole wheat fettuccine
3 C broccoli florets

Directions:
1.  Heat olive oil in lg skillet over med heat.  Add onion & carrot, saute 5 min.  Add italian seasoning & red pepper flakes.
2.  Remove tails, set aside.  Prepare  pasta according to package directions, adding broccoli florets tot he pot for the last 2 min of cooking.  Drain & set aside.
3.. Add shrimp to the tomato sauce cook 2 - 3 min until opaque.
4.  Serve shrimp & sauce over pasta & broccoli
