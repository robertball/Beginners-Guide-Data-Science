Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/2 cup 10x
1 cup marg
4 eggs beaten
2 cups granulated sugar
1/3 cup lemon juice and rind (juice and peel 2 lemons= 2 teas zest)
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking powder

Directions:
sift flour 10x and add melted margarine.  press into 13x9" pan. bake 350F for 20-25 mins until golden
beat eggs, sugar and lemon. wisk sifted dry ingredients into egg mixture.  pour over baked crust.  bake 25 mins.  when cool sprinkle  with 10 x sugar and cut into bars
