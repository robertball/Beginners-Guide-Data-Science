Title:
Instant Pot Sliced Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ham slice
1 1/2 cups water

Directions:
Wrap the ham tightly in heavy duty aluminum foil (make sure it will fit in your cooker
Pour 1 1/2 cups of water into the Instant Pot
Place rack in bottom of the pot
Put the wrapped in on top of the rack
Lock the lid in place
Select High Pressure and set time for 12 minutes and press start
When the beep sounds turn off the Instant Pot
Use a natural pressure release for 10 minutes then  to a quick pressure release to release any remaining pressure
When the valve drops carefully remove the lid
Remove ham from aluminum foil, cut the slice away from the bone and serve.
