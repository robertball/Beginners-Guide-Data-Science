Title:
Pineapple Upside Down Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4533281.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp brown sugar
5 Tbsp butter, melted, divided
2 cans (8oz each) pineapple slices, well drained
7 maraschino cherries, well drained, stemmed
1 C Graham cracker crumbs
3/4 C plus 3 Tbsp granulated sugar, divided
3 packages cream cheese (8oz each), softened
3/4 C sour cream
2 tsp vanilla
3 eggs

Directions:
Mix brown sugar and 2 Tbsp butter in a 9 inch round cake pan (not springform) until blended ; spread to cover bottom of pan evenly.
Top with pineapple slices.
Place cherries in centers of pineapple slices.
Combine graham cracker crumbs, 3 Tbsp granulated sugar and remaining butter.
Press gently over pineapple slices.
Beat cream cheese and remaining granulated sugar with mixer until blended.
Add sour cream and vanilla, mix well.
Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed after each until blended.
Pour over pineapple.
Bake 55 min to 1 hour or until center is almost set.
Rub knife around side of pan to loosen cake and cool completely.
Invert Cheesecake onto a plate and carefully remove pan.
Refrigerate for 3 hours.
Enjoy!!!!!!
