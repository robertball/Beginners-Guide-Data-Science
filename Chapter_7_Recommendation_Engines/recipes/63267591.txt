Title:
Simple Baked Porcupine Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857197.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs Ground Beef
1 Small Onion (whichever one you prefer)Chopped Small
1 Green Pepper - Chopped Small
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Pepper
3/4 tsp. Italian seasoning
3 Eggs
1 1/2 Cups Seasoned Bread Crumbs
2 Cups - Cooked White Rice(2 Boil in bag rice works great too)
1 Jar of your favorite sauce
Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees. Boil white rice until just cooked, should not be mushy.
2. While rice is cooking, chop or dice onion and pepper and put aside.
3.)In large bowl, mix uncooked ground beef, eggs, bread crumbs, garlic, salt, pepper and italian seasoning. Then add in onion and pepper.
4.)When rice is done, add into ground beef mixture and mix until evenly combined.
5.) In a 9x13 baking dish, thinly cover bottom with sauce. Form meat mixture into 6 softball sized balls. seperate evenly into dish. Use remaining sauce to top each ball.
6.)Cover with foil, and bake at 400 degrees for 45 min or until no longer pink.
7.)Top with Mozzarella cheese and bake uncovered until cheese is slightly browned and melted.

Submitter: 
MaidMommy
Description: 
Delicious, simple and yummy. You can use ground turkey and brown rice to make this a healthier meal. Even my picky eaters love it!!
