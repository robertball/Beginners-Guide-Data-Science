Title:
Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 soft tortillas
1 pd hamburger
1/4 c chopped onions
1/2 c sour cream
1 c shredded cheddar cheese
1 T parsley
1 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1 can (15 oz) tomato paste
2/3 c water
1/3 c chopped green peppers
1 T chili powder
1/2 t oregano
1/4 t cumin
1 t minced garlic

Directions:
Cook and drain hamburger and onion. Stir in sour cream, cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Cover and save.
Heat remaining ingredents to a boil, stirring occasionally; reduce heat. Simmer for 5 minutes. Pours sauce inot uncreased 8-9 inch pie plate.
Dip each tortilla into sauce, coating both sides. Spoon about 1/4 cup of hamburger mixture onto tortilla, roll and arrange in 9 x 13 baking dish. Pour remaining sauce over tortillas. Back uncovered for 15-20 minutes. Sprinkle cheese over top.
