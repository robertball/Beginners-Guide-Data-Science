Title:
Spinach Artichoke Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken shredded/chopped
6 ounce package frozen spinach thawed and well drained
7 ounces artichoke hearts (plain) chopped
4 ounces cream cheese at room temperature
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese grated
3/4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/3 cup sour cream
1/8 cup mayonnaise
pinch garlic salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
In a mixing bowl, add all ingredients, except for the mozzarella cheese.
Place mix in  baking dish that has been sprayed with nonstick spray.
Top with the shredded mozzarella cheese and bake for 25 minutes until the cheese is slightly golden and bubbly.
