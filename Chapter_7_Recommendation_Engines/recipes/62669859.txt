Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 small onion, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan or Gruyere cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add onion and saute until tender. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes, then add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly, heating through. Stir in parsley and serve.

Submitter: 
pweimer
Description: 
Cream, butter, onion, garlic, parsley and Parmesan cheese make a classic Alfredo sauce for chicken dishes.Modified from: Alfredo Sauce
