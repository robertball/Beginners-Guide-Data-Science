Title:
Salmon and veggies in parchment Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (8oz) pieces of Atlantic salmon
Green beans
Olive oil
Garlic powder
Salt and pepper
Parchment

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Par boil green beans for 4 min, then soak in an ice bath.
Place parchment on counter and spread light layer of olive oil on both sides. Place 1 8oz salmon filet in center of the parchment. Place semi cooked green beans next to salmon. Sprinkle with garlic powder, salt and pepper. Fold over parchment to hold air.
Bake in preheated oven for 13 min. Remove and let sit for 5 min to finish cooking.
