Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cakes:
1 box vanilla cake mix
3 eggs
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup vegetable oil
several drops of blue food coloring
Filling:
1 package cream cheese (softened)
1/2 cup butter (softened)
2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 jar Marshmallow fluff
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup mini marshmallows
Red food coloring

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
In a mixer, combine the cake mix, eggs, water, blue food coloring, to reach the desired shade, and oil; mix 2-3 min.  Using an mini scoop, scoop out mounds of batter onto the cookie sheet.  Bake for 8-10 minutes.  Allow to cool completely.
Beat together the butter and cream cheese on high until smooth.  Add in the powdered sugar a little at a time, then the marshmallow fluff, red food coloring, until you reach your desired shade, and vanilla.  Stir in the mini marshmallows.  Spread onto one of the cooled cookies, topping with another to make sandwiches.
