Title:
Cucumber Crunch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/432091.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
1 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 teaspoon seasoning salt

Directions:
Combine cucumbers and red onion in a salad bowl.
In a small bowl, stir together water, rice wine vinegar, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and seasoned salt. Pour over vegetables.  Chill for 1 hour.  Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home cooks
Description: 
No oil is used in this cool and refreshing summer salad made with cucumbers, red onion, and rice wine vinegar.
