Title:
4 minute fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-12oz package chocolate chips
1- 12oz package peanut butter chips
(if you don't want choc/peanut butter just use all chocolate)
1 stick butter
1 can sweet condensed milk
Nuts are optional. (Walnuts are great in these!)

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a microwave safe bowl and micro. for 2 mins. Stir and then micro. for 2 more mins. Whisk mixture until nice and smooth.
Pour into a buttered 8" x 8" square pan and cool in fridge for at least 2 hours.
Cut into squares.
**You can also make white chocolate fudge too! Cut the time in the microwave down to 1 1/2 mins, then whisk. Back in the microwave for another 1 1/2 mins, then whisk until smooth. This one is great with crushed candy cane sprinkled on top!
