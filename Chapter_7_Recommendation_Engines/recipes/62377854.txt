Title:
Tzatziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 English cucumbers
4 ounces sour cream
8 ounces low-fat or fat non-fat Greek yogurt
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
1/2 teaspoon dry dill
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Peel cucumbers and grate into a colander.  Squeeze out excess water.
Mix together remaining ingredients in a large bowl.  Stir in cucumbers.  Chill at least 30 minutes and up to 2 hours before serving.
