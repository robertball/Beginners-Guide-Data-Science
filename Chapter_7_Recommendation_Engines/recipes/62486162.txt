Title:
Chili Dog Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large, regular thickness, ready-made pizza crust (like Boboli)
Can of chili (can be vegetarian or all-meat)
3 reduced-fat, all-beef hot dogs (uncooked)
1-1 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar jack cheese (or your favorite)
1/4- 1/2 cup red onion (or your favorite), coarsely chopped
Sweet Pickle Relish (the squeeze bottle type works best)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450F
Remove pizza crust from wrapper and place on circular pizza pan
Open can of chili and spread onto pizza crust out to 1/2 inch from edge
Cut hot dogs into 1/2 inch buttons and spread evenly over chili
Distribute onions and cheese evenly over pizza
Bake for 8-10 minutes or until cheese completely melts
Remove from oven at let cool for ~ 3-5 minutes
Cut and serve with pickle relish

Submitter: 
Jane Guzauskas Ruby
Description: 
This is the best comfort food to come out of an oven! Most ingredients can already be in your fridge or pantry: Hot dogs, canned chili, sweet pickle relish and anything else that you'd have on hand for a quick chilidog dinner. All you need is ready-made pizza crust and an imagination!
