Title:
Sausage Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2202109.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean sausage meat
6 slices American cheese
6-8  eggs
12 slices white bread
2 cups skim milk

Directions:
Brown sausage and drain.
Spray 9x11 pan with cooking spray (pam)
Butter 6 slices bread and place in pan butter side down.
Sprinkle sausage on bread. Layer cheese.
Put 6 slices bread -- butter side up --  on cheese.
Mix eggs and milk; spoon over bread
Cover with foil, let stand overnight.
Bake uncovered at 350F for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
HOWEWASS
Description: 
This is the recipe my mom used when I was a kid. It is easy to prepare the night before for a very yummy breakfast.
