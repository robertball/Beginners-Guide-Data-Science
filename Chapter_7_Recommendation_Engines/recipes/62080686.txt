Title:
Lomi Salmon-Hawaiian Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb salted salmon
2 stalks green onion, cut in 1" pieces
1/2 onion sliced (any will do but sweet onions tastes better)
2 tomatoes, sliced

Directions:
Remove bones and skin from salmon and shred into small pieces with fingers.  Add green oniion, sliced onion, and tomatoes.  Mash mixture with a fork or lomi (crush/massage) with fingers.  Put 6-8 ice cubes in mixture and chill in refrigerator until ready to serve (about 1 hour or longer).  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
ISLANDGIRL05
Description: 
Great side dish or appetizer.  In Hawaii, it's always at a luau or big gatherings.  Enjoy!
