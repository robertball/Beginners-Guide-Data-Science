Title:
Best Baked Wings! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Chicken wings halved
2 table spoons olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp no salt added grill seasoning of your choice
1/3 tsp cayenne pepper
wing sauce
1/2 cup Franks red hot sauce
2 tbsp no salt added butter
1/2 tsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp worcestshire sauce
1 1/2 tsp light brown sugar
1/2 tsp honey
3/4 tsp sricracha
3/4 tsp chili garlic paste
1/4 tsp vinegar
1 garlic clove smashed

Directions:
Mix cleaned halved raw wings with olive oil, salt, pepper, cayenne and grill seasoning. Line cookie sheet with non stick foil or spray with non stick spray. Evenly space out wings and bake on 400 degrees for half hour to 45 min. Turning over halfway through. Broil for the last 5 min to crisp up skin.
In a saucepan combine sauce ingredients and simmer until thoroughly combined.
Once wings are cooked through mix with sauce and serve immediately.

Description: 
These baked wings deliver the same great flavor for the slightly health conscious. Your kitchen stays clean and you'll love the sauce.
