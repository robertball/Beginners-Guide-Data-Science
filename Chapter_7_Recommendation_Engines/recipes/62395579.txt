Title:
Coconut Daiquiris Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 very ripe pineapple, cut into chunks
Juice of 1 lime
2 tablespoons Coco Lopez cream of coconut
71/2 ounces Bacardi light rum
20 ice cubes.

Directions:
Combine the ingredients in a blender and whiz until smooth.
Serve immediately without garnishes. Makes about 5 daiquiris.
