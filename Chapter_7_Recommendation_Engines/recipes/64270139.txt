Title:
Sweet Condensed Milk Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
1 c Sugar
1 TBSP butter
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Pour the sugar into the milk in a saucepan.
Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Cook 20 minutes at a slow rolling boil.
Pull off heat and whisk in butter and vanilla.
Remove from the heat and allow to cool before using.
