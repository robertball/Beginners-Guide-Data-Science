Title:
Mac & Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C uncooked elbow macaroni (or mix in some gnocchi
2 TB butter
1/4 C finely chopped onion
4 oz mushrooms, sliced
2 TB chopped green bell pepper (about half of a small bell pepper)
2 TB chopped pimiento-stuffed olives
4 oz (1 cup) shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 C sour cream
1 tsp seasoned salt
1 tsp pepper
1 1/2 C diced cooked chicken
1/4 C milk
1/2 C soft bread crumbs, tossed with a few teaspoons melted butter - for topping chicken casserole

Directions:
Cook and drain macaroni, following package directions; place in a large mixing bowl.
In a large skillet, melt butter over medium low heat. Add chopped onion, mushrooms, and chopped green bell pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, for 3 to 4 minutes, or until onion is softened and mushrooms are browned.
Add skillet mixture to macaroni with chopped olives, shredded cheese, sour cream, salt and pepper, chicken, and milk.
Transfer chicken and macaroni mixture to a shallow 1 1/2-quart baking dish.
Sprinkle buttered bread crumbs over the top of macaroni and chicken casserole;
Bake at 350Â° for 25 to 35 minutes, or until chicken casserole is bubbly and breadcrumbs are lightly browned.
Macaroni and chicken casserole serves 6.
