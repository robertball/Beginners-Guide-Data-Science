Title:
Mini Pizza's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package of 6 Plain English Muffins
3 Cups Pizza Sauce
3 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese or your favorite
36 Slices Pepperoni

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Split english muffins in half, so you have 12 halfs. Spray baking sheet with cooking spray and place 6 halfs per baking sheet.  Add about 1/4 cup pizza sauce, cheese and 3 pepperoni slices to each english muffin half.
Place in oven and bake for about 15-20 watching until cheese is bubbly and done.  Remove and let cool for 3-5 minutes and serve.
