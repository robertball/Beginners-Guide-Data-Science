Title:
Light Sesame Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C white or brown rice
1/3 C honey
1/4 C soy sauce
2 coves garlic minced
3 tbs sesame seeds
1/2 inc piece of ginger minced or grated
3 lg egg whites
1/3 C cornstarch
1 = 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast cut into 2 inch chuncks
olive, peanut or vegetable oil for frying
4 scallions chopped

Directions:
1.  Cook rice according to package
2.  in sm bowl whisk honey, soy sauce, garlic, ginger & sesame seeds together.  set aside
3.  In lg bowl, whisk egg whites, & cornstarch until they come together
4.  Season chicken w salt & pepper
5.  In lg nonstick or cast iron skillet, heat 1 tbs oil over med heat.  Add 1/3 of chiicken to egg whites & coat pieces throroughly.  Remove 1 piece at a time with tongs & allow excess to drip off.
6.  Cook, turning the pieces every 2-3 min with thin, sharp metal spatula until browned on all sides & cooked through the centers.  Transfer to a platter
7.  Repeat adding more oil to pan eatch time
8.  Once all chicken is cooked add sauce to the pan & bring to a simmer

Gina Edwards

a lighter version of sesame chicken w a sweet & savory sauce that you will love
