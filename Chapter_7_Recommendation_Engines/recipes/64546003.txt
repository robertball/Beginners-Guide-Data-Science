Title:
Apple Peanut Butter Cookies With Flax & Chia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3229185.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium apples, cored & diced
1 flax egg
2 tbsp coconut oil
1/2 cup brown rice syrup
1/4 tsp almond extract
1 cup GF rolled oats
3/4 cup brown rice flour
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
dash of sea salt
2 tbsp Powdered Peanut Butter
1.5 tbsp warm water
1 packet sweetener

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Using a hand or standing mixer, add flax egg, coconut oil, almond extract, brown rice syrup & mix then add diced apples & coat with mixture
Then add spices, oats & flour & combine until everything is mixed well
To a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, add 1 are spoonful of batter to form a cookie
The batter will be loose & you may need to form them a bit with your hands, they will harden when they bake
Bake 15 minutes until edges are slightly browned
Remove from the oven & place on a cooling rack to cool
In a small ramekin mix the drizzle together, you may need a little more water or PB to get the desired consistency
Using a 1/4 tsp pour drizzle on top of cookies and enjoy warm

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A healthy, gluten free cookie made with oatmeal, brown rice and powdered peanut butter that's low calorie and vegan. Turn the oven on because these Apple Peanut Butter Cookies With Flax & Chia are a delicious & nutritious sweet treat!
