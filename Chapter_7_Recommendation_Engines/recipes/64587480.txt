Title:
THE BUSHWACKER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
Ice
1 1/2 jiggers dark rum
1 jigger light rum
1/2 jigger Creme de Cocoa
1/2 jigger Kahlua
Dash of 151 rum (optional)

Directions:
Put the ice cream and liquors in a blender and pulse to blend.
Add the ice to an inch or two to the top of the blender container and blend until smooth.
If the mixture freezes, add a few drops of water.
Pour into an old-fashion glass and top with a dash of the 151 rum.
