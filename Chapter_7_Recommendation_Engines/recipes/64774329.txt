Title:
Mexican chicken soup - instant pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of chicken breast
2 celery stalks
1 chopped onion
1 diced jalapeno
2 tbsp olive oil
4 crushed garlic cloves
14 oz can diced tomatoes with chilies
6 cups chicken broth
1 lime, Juiced
1 teaspoon cumin
1/2 Bunch cilantro chopped
1 sliced avocado, garnish
One sliced lime, garnish

Directions:
turn on instant pot saute setting
saute celery, onions, jalapeno, and garlic in the olive oil until tender
Add chicken, Tomatoes, broth, and cumin
cover and lock the lid. Cook on manual high pressure for 10 minutes. Natural release 15 minutes and quick release after.
Remove the chicken breasts from the soup and Shred
Return the shredded chicken. Add lime juice and stir. Add in the fresh chopped cilantro.
Serve soup with a slice of avocado and lime wedge.
