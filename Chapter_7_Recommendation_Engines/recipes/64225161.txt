Title:
Station 18 Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Pounds Ground Beef
1 Pound Pork Sausage
6 Strips Bacon, Diced
2 Large Onions, Chopped
8 Cloves Garlic, Minced
1 Large Celery Stalk, Diced
1 Medium Bell Pepper, Diced
1 12 Ounce can Tomato Paste
2 14.5 Ounce cans Chopped Tomatoes w/green Chili, Undrained
1 14.5 Ounce can Chopped Tomatoes w/Olive Oil, Basil & Balsamic VInegar, Undrained
1 14.5 Ounce can Tomato Wedges or 1 Can Whole tomoatoes cut into wedges, Undrained
1 28 Ounce can Diced Tomatoes, Undrained
1 4.5 Ounce can Green Chili, Diced
1 15 Ounce can Pinto Beans, Drained
2 15 Ounce Small White Beans, Drained
1 15 Ounce can Black-eyed Peas, Drained
4 Cups Water
4 Tablespoons Chili Powder
1 Tablespoon Cumin
2 Teaspoons Mexican Oregano
1 Tablespoon Salt
4 Tablespoons Sugar
4 Tablespoons White Vinegar
1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
4 Teaspoons Beef Boullion Granules
Several Dashes Black Pepper

Directions:
Brown beef, sausage, bacon and onion in heavy skillet. Drain and transfer to large pot. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 3 hours.
