Title:
Cherry Coke Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup water
1 can cherry pie filling
1 cup coke
2 sm boxes cherry jello
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1-2 cups crushed pineapple (drained)

Directions:
Bring sugar and water to a boil.
Add pie filling and bring to boil again.
Add jello, pineapple, peacans and coke (last).
Pour into serving bowl or container.
Stir well and chill.

Submitter: 
Kristin
Description: 
This is a yummy dessert that my grandma has passed down.  Easy dish to take somewhere.  Great with whip cream too. Yum!
