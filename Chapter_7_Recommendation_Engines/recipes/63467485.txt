Title:
Chai Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tbsp cardamom pods
2 tbsp whole cloves
1 tbsp coriander
8 2inch sticks cinnamon, broken into pieces
1/4 tsp black peppercorns
2 whole star anise
1 tsp ground ginger

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients except ginger in a skillet. Stir over medium heat 2 to 3 minutes to toast spices. Add ginger and then crush spices. A mortar is best but if you don't have one a rolling pin in a zip lock baggie. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
To make the tea: 1 cup milk, 2 rounded tsp of mix, and 2 tsp brown sugar. Heat to boiling. Boil 2 cups of water with tea. Mix together. Drink.
