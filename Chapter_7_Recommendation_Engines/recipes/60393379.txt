Title:
Pumpkin Crunch Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cold milk
1 pack (3.4oz) of instant vanilla pudding mix
2cups whipped topping
1 cup canned pumpkin
1/2 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
32 gingersnap cookies, crushed (about 1-1/2 cups)
additional whipped topping

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat milk and pudding mix on low speed for 2 minutes.  Stir in whipped topping, pumpkin and pumkin pie spice; mix well.
Spoon half the mixture into parfait glasses, top with half of the gingersnap crumbs. Repeat layers. Top with additional whip cream.
