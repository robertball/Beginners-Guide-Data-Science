Title:
Sweet Hot Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. white vinegar
1 c. dry mustard powder
2 eggs, beaten
1 c. sugar (or Splenda)
1 ½ tsp margarine (or EVOO or butter)

Directions:
Day 1 – mix together vinegar and dry mustard.
Day 2 – add beaten egg to dry mustard/vinegar mixture.  In a medium saucepan, melt butter.  Add egg/dry mustard/vinegar mixture to butter.  Stir constantly until mixture comes to boil and cook until it coats spoon.  Cool and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Smooth and creamy classic sauce good with seafood or barbeque pork loin served with roasted sesame seeds. To reduce calories use Splenda and olive oil instead of sugar and butter.
