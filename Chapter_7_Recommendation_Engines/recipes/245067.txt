Title:
Hidden Valley Potato Skins Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2938326.jpg
Ingredients:
4 baked potatoes, quartered
1/4 cup sour cream
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley速 Original Ranch速 Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
Sliced green onions (optional)
Bacon pieces (optional)

Directions:
Scoop the potato out of the skins; combine with sour cream and dressing mix. Fill skins with mixture. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 375 degrees F for 12-15 minutes or until cheese is melted. Garnish with green onions and/or bacon bits, if desired.
