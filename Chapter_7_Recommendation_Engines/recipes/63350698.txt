Title:
Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Stove Top Stuffing mix
2 cups chopped, cooked chicken
1 can evaporated milk
1 stalk celery, diced
1 pkg. broccoli florets, thawed
1 small onion, chopped
8 oz. shredded Swiss cheese
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Cook Stove Top according to package directions then place in bottom of greased 13x9 baking dish.
Combine evaporated milk and soup.
Add remaining ingredients, reserving 1/4 of the Swiss cheese.
Pour over stuffing and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake for 45 minutes.
