Title:
Simple Soy Sauce Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pieces of chicken, skinned if preferred
1/2 c. Italian salad dressing
1/4 c. water
1/4 c. soy sauce
1 t. onion flakes
1 t. brown sugar
1/2 t. garlic powder
1/8 t. black pepper
4 oz canned mushrooms
2 c. rice, cooked

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except rice in a covered skillet or dutch oven and simmer on low heat for 45 minutes.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
springermom
Description: 
My boys always begged for this chicken.  It literally falls off the bones and the sauce gives a rich flavor to the rice.  Made with pantry staples, it's easy to throw together too!
