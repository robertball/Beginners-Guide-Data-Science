Title:
Super Easy Vegetarian Black Bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can or 2 cups vegetable broth or stock (can be made from boullion)
1 can vegetarian refried beans
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can corn (or fill one of the other cans with frozen corn)
1 can ro-tel (or tomatoes with chiles)

Directions:
Warm the broth on medium to medium high heat and stir in the refried beans until a smooth creamy texture.
Add the beans, corn, and ro-tel.
Heat until warmed through.
Can be served with sour cream, guacamole, shredded cheese, chopped green onions, cilantro, or hot sauce.

Submitter: 
Denise McCabe
Description: 
Easy to make and super tasty.  Nice for a weeknight meal.
