Title:
Buttermilk Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*2 1/2 to 3 pounds of chicken
pieces
*1 1/2 cups buttermilk
*2 cups all-purpose flour
*3â€“4 cups corn oil
*2 teaspoon salt
*1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in zip-lock bag with buttermilk, 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours and up to 24 hours.
Add flour, 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper into a large paper bag, shake to combine. Place half of the chicken pieces into flour mixture and shake to coat. Remove chicken from bag and shake off excess flour. Repeat coating with remaining chicken pieces.
Heat oil to 350 degrees. Place chicken skin side down and cook covered for 10 minutes. Turn and cook uncovered until chicken is golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes longer. Place on wire rack to remove excess oil.
