Title:
Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ripe plum tomatoes, seeded, finely chopped (about 1 pound)
1/2 large white onion, finely chopped
2 green onions, finely chopped
1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 fresh jalapeños, seeded, finely chopped
1/2 lime, juiced
Kosher salt to taste

Directions:
Mix tomatoes, onion, garlic, green onion, cilantro, jalapeños and lime juice. Add salt to taste. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Best if served the same day made.

Submitter: 
WISEREADER
Description: 
A classic Pico de Gallo recipe. Great as a side, on fajitas or tacos, or with nacho chips.
