Title:
Stovies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Left over Roast Beef - diced or can use Chicken or Corned Beef - shredded
4 large Tatties (Potatoes) partially boiled and sliced
1 Onion - thinly sliced
1 tbsp Dripping (from cooked beef)or Lard
A wee touch of Beef  Stock
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Add a wee bit of lard to a pot and gently cook the onions until soft;
Add the beef or other and add salt and pepper.
Layer over with tattie slices and cover with the stock to taste (depending on how moist you prefer the meal); Suggestion - add a wee bit and top up as required.
Simmer on the hob for about an hour or cook in the oven at 190c/380f/gas 5 for about 50 minutes.
Serve piping hot with oatcakes, beetroot and skirlie

Submitter:

Description: 
NOT Mine except, I've added the modern Corned Beef - An old traditional Scot's recipe. In the big houses, used by the staff to make left over food from the table of the rich go further. Great comfort dish.
