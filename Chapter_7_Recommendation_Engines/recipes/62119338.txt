Title:
Flatbread With Fresh Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb.store-bought pizza dough
1 Tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 Tablespoon chopped fresh thyme leaves (any herb will work)
1 teaspoon good quality coarse salt (or to taste)
Flour, for handling

Directions:
Roll out the dough on a lightly floured work surface to 1/2-inch thick, or less for thinner flatbread.  If dough won't roll - stretch and pull it while on the pan - pressing as you go.
Transfer the dough to a baking/pizza sheet. Using a fork, pierce the dough several times.
Drizzle the oil over the dough and gently sperad with pastry brush (or your hand).
cover loosely with plastic and let sit for an hour or over night.
Sprinkle with the thyme and salt.
Bake in a 450 degree oven until golden, about 15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
This easy recipes uses store-bought pizza dough and takes just minutes.  Results are a gorgeous and gourmet looking appetizer or weekend snack.
