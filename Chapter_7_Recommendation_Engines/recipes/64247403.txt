Title:
Turkey kofta kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I package  ground turkey
1 small onion, minced
2 cloves garlic. minced
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1 whole egg
1/4 tsp chopped green chill
1/4 tsp coriander
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp chili powder
salt (to taste)

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the ground turkey, onion, garlic, cilantro, egg, spices, salt and pepper until evenly blended. Divide into a heaping 1/4 cup portions so you get 12; roll into log shaped ovals. Place on a cookie sheet or baking tray Grill for 25 to 30 minutes on indirect heat turning occasionally, until meat is no longer pink @ 350 degrees in the middle rack.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
My Persian neighbor introduced me to kofta about 20  years ago. His were made of beef and I never actually got his recipe but I'm pretty good at replicating flavors and these came pretty  close... and of course using turkey instead of beef makes these a lot lighter.
