Title:
Peceto with Green Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium peceto
50 grs. butter
1 TBSP olive oil
2 cloves garlic
5 green onions finely chopped
Salt and Pepper
1/2 cup white wine
a pinch of nutmeg
1 cup vegetable broth

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350. Grease a baking pan.
Chop the green onion finely, and cut the cloves of garlic in 4 parts.
Make several deep cuts in the peceto, and place part of the green onions and all of the garlic on the meat. Salt and pepper to taste and cook for 30 to 35 minutes.
While the meat is cooking, brown the rest of the green onions in 1 TBSP. of butter and a little olive oil over medium heat. Add the vegetable broth and the white wine and allow it to thicken. Sprinkle it over the meat when it's done.
