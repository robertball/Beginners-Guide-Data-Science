Title:
Chili Rellenos Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (7oz) Can of Whole Green Chilies, drained
1 lb block Montery Jack Cheese, sliced
5 eggs, beaten
2c Milk
1/2c Flour
1t Salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 350*F and spray or butter 9x13 baking dish. Glass works best.
Cut chilies so they are open and lay flat. Layer bottom of dish with chilies. Lay next layer of sliced cheese. Sprinkle with pepper to taste.
Alternate layers until all the chilies and cheese is gone.
Add milk, flour, and salt to the beaten eggs and pour over chilies and cheese. Sprinkle with more pepper if desired.
Bake in preheated over for 45-60 minutes, depending on type of pan used.
