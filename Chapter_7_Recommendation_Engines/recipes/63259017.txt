Title:
Shrimp Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds raw fresh shrimp
4 TBL. cayenne pepper
6 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 cup 1/2 & 1/2
1/2 cup chopped parsley
3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1 (16 ounce) package linguini pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta until al dente according to package directions.
While linguine is cooking, Boil Shrimp for 3 minutes with cayenne pepper. drain put in ice water to prevent further cooking.   Melt butter or margarine in a large skillet,  add garlic, and saute briefly to release flavor,  add cream; heat just to boiling, stirring frequently.  Lower heat, add parsley, basil, and thyme, cayenne pepper.  Add shrimp Continue cooking for one minute, Remove sauce from heat.
Drain the pasta, and toss lightly with the sauce.  Toss again with cheese, and salt and pepper to taste.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
Shrimp, parsley, basil and thyme simmer in a dreamy garlic and cream sauce for a few brief moments before being tossed with hot pasta and Parmesan cheese. Season to taste and serve to a happy dinner party.Modified from: Shrimp Linguine
