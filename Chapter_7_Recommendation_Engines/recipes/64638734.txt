Title:
Neil's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Avacados
2 Tsp. Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb Seasoning
1 Tsp. Lawry's Seasoning Salt
2 Tsp. Frank's Hot Sauce

Directions:
Cut the avacados in half and scoop out the pulp into a fairly flat bowl.  Take a fork and smash the avacado.  Add the Mrs. Dash, the Lawry's, and the Frank's and mix well.  Voila, you have delicious guacamole!  Serve with tortilla chips or ruffled potato chips.

Submitter: 
LonestarNR
Description: 
I learned from Rick Bayless that guacamole should be simple and not diluted with things like tomato, onion, cilantro, etc.  Since I read his cookbook, I have kept my guacamole simple and everyone has loved it.
