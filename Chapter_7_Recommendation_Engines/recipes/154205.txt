Title:
Holiday Apple Side Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4578379.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) cans dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
2 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1 bunch red globe grapes, halved and seeded
1 1/2 cups creamy salad dressing, e.g. Miracle Whip â„¢

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together the kidney beans, celery, and grapes. Stir in the creamy salad dressing a little at a time. Adjust the amount of creamy salad dressing to suit your taste.
