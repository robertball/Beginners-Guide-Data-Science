Title:
Pumpkin Almond Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1049321.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon baking powder
¾ teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon (ground) ginger
¼ teaspoon cloves
¼ teaspoon allspice
½ teaspoon salt
4 eggs
1 cup sugar
¼ cup packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
¾ cup pumpkin puree
¾ cup coconut oil
¼ cup sliced almonds
1/4 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Spray mini muffin pan with canola oil
Combine the flour, soda, powder, spices and salt in a bowl
Beat the eggs, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla together with an electric mixer until well blended.
Add the pumpkin and oil, mix well
Add the dry ingredients and mix well
Pour mixture 1/4" from the top of each mini muffin tin and sprinkle with almond slices
Place pan in oven, bake 11 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean
Let cool in pan for 2 minutes, and then remove and place on cooling rack
Let cool for 5 minutes on rack and then using a sifter, sprinkle powdered sugar on top of each muffin

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
If you enjoy pumpkin you will love these mini muffins. Perfect for popping in your mouth and just the right size for kids!
