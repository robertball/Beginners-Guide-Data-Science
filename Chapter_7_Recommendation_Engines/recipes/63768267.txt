Title:
Apples & Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup honey
1/4 cup white wine
2 Tbl lemon juice
1/4 tsp cinammon
apples--

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Quarter apples & put in baking dish
Mix the 1st 4 ingredients. Pour over apples. Bake uncovered 45min until soft. baste every 10min.
