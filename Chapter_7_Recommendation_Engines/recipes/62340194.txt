Title:
Turtle Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. plus 2 tbsp. caramel ice cream topping, divided
1 graham cracker pie crust
1 c. cold milk
1/2 c. plus 2 tbsp. pecans (if desired)
2 pkg (3.4oz each) Jello Vanilla instant pudding
1 c. canned pumpkin
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tub (8oz) cool whip, divided

Directions:
Pour 1/4 c. caramel topping into crust and sprinkle with 1/2 c. pecans.
Beat milk, pudding mixes, pumpkin and spices with whisk until blended.  Stir in 1 1/2 c. cool whip and spread into crust.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour.  Top with remaining cool whip, caramel and pecans before serving.
