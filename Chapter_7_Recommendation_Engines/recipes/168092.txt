Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Smudgy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985063.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
5 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
1/2 cup peanuts
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
In large saucepan over low heat, melt margarine. Add marshmallows; stir until melted and well blended. Remove from heat.
Stir in vanilla. Add cereal, peanuts and raisins, then chocolate chips, stirring until thoroughly coated.
Using lightly-buttered spatula, press into buttered 3.5 L (13 x 9-inch) pan. Cool. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
These crispy, chewy treats are packed with peanuts, raisins, and chocolate chips for an extra flavor boost.
