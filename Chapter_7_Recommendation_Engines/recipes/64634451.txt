Title:
Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 lb potatoes
-6 slices bacon
-1 small white onion or 2-3 stalks green onion
-32 oz chicken stock
-4 tbsp butter
-1/3 cup flour
-1 can evaporated milk
-1/2 cup sour cream
-1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
-salt & pepper

Directions:
1. Fully cook a bacon
2. Cube potatoes, chop onion, chop bacon. Combine in crockpot.
3. Add chicken stock (do not add this until you are ready to actually cook the soup)
4. Cook on high for 3 hours
5. Near the end of the cooking, melt butter in a small saucepan. Wisk in flour.
6. Once flour/butter is smooth, slowly add in evaporated milk, wishing until smooth. Bring to a simmer to thicken.
7. Once thick, wisk in sour cream.
8. Add this mixture and the cheese to the soup and combine thoroughly. Add S&P to taste
**to thicken soup, mash some of the potatoes before serving
**Top soup with extra cheese, sour cream, bacon, and/or green onion to serve
