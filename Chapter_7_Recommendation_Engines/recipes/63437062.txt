Title:
ARTICHOKE DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can artichoke hearts packed in water, drained (not marinated kind)
Mayonnaise (not low fat.) 2 or 3 good dollops!
1 Tbs. lemon juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 c. grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 pkg. chopped spinach (optional)
crusty bread or crackers

Directions:
Chop hearts in Cuisinart, or mash them up with the back of a fork.
Don't chop them too finely.  Better if dip has some sizable chunks in it.
Place artichokes in bowl
Mix in 2 or 3 dollops of mayo, lemon juice, garlic, cheese and spinach
Is the dip creamy?  If not, add more mayo
Turn into a small casserole dish
Bake 20-25 minutes or until dip is bubbly and golden brown on edges
Serve with crackers or bread
