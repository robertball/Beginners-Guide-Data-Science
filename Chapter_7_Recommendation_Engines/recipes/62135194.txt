Title:
Cornell Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 egg

Directions:
1. Place all ingredients into a blender.
2. Blend for 2 1/2 minutes.
3. Use on meat and marinade overnight.
(Can grill meat or roast with marinade in pan)

Submitter:

Description: 
Marinade perfect for chicken, pork, or turkey
