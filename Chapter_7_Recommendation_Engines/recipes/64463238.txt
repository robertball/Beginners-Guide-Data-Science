Title:
Anniversary burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
! Tbsp each cream and milk
1 slice bread torn up
1/2 Tbsp dried minced onion
3/4 tsp salt
1 tsp horseradish
1/8 tsp each thyme and pepper
1/4 tsp dried mustard
1 pound ground beef
1/8 cup each butter and ketchup

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except beef. Let stand 10 mins then stir well again. Add beef.
Cook and baste with 1/8 cup butter mixed with 1/8 cup ketchup
