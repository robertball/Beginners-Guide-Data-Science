Title:
Mini Caprese Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cherry Tomatoes, Cut in Half
Fresh Mozzarella, Bocconcini (sized)
Fresh Basil Leaves, cut in half (if large)
Balsamic Glaze, for Drizzle
Skewers or Toothpicks

Directions:
Thread a tomato half, mini mozzarella cheese ball, and a basil leaf onto a tooth pick.
Repeat with remaining ingredients.
Drizzle cooled balsamic reduction on top.
