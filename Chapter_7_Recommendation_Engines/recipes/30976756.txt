Title:
Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365226.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 jar pasta sauce
1 container ricotta cheese
9 oven-ready lasagne noodles
1 onion
1 block mozzerella cheese
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook hamburg and onion in skillet until meat no longer pink. Mix with pasta sauce.
In lasagne pan place 2tbsp sauce on bottom. Top with 3 noodles, a layer of sauce mixture, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese, 1/3 of the mozzerella cheese, sprinkle of parmesan. Repeat layers.
Top with remaining 3 noodles, remaining sauce mixture and the mozzerella cheese.
Bake at 350 F for 1 hour.
