Title:
Kahlua Chocolate Puding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/694275.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Chocolate Cake Mix (wtih out pudding if possible)
1 4 Oz package of vanila instant pudding
4 Eggs
1 pint (16 Oz.) of Sour Cream
3/4 Cup Vegetale Oil
1/3 Cup of Kahlua
6-12 Oz package of chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and bake at 350 for 45 minutes
