Title:
Sim's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/309141.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Italian sausage
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, pressed
2 (14.5 ounce) cans Italian stewed tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 tablespoon dried basil
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook Italian sausage with onion and garlic until golden brown; drain. Stir in remaining ingredients. Mix well, breaking up stewed tomatoes with a wooden spoon. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer one hour. Serve over cooked spaghetti.

Submitter: 
SIMZGIRL
Description: 
This is my husband's favorite spaghetti sauce. My three-year old daughter even loves it! We enjoy eating it with crusty bread and a fresh salad.
