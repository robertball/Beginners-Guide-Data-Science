Title:
Best Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922099.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
2 Eggs
2-4 crushed bananas
1 tsp. baking soda
2 Cups sifted flour
1/2 cup walnuts

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in the order given, stirring well after each addition
Pour into buttered and floured loaf dish
Bake in slow oven at 325 for 1 - 1 1/4 hours

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
Truly the best.  Be sure to mix together in the order given
