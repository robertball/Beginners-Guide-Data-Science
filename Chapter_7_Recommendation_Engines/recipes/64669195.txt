Title:
Pan Fried Breaded Boneless Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork tenderloins, 1/2 to 1 inch thick
2 tbsp vegetable oil. Substitute EVOO for healthier option
1 cup white all purpose flour
2 tsp salt
2 tsp oregano
2 tsp paprika
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp ground sage
1 tsp ground thyme

Directions:
Heat skillet or frying pan on medium high heat.
Mix all ingredients EXCEPT pork chops into a shallow plate or dish. Press each pork chop firmly into flour and spice mixture, coating both sides, set aside.
Pour oil into frying pan/skillet.
Place pork chops in pan/skillet. Cook for 4 minutes then flip to other side in pan. Cook 6 minutes per side or until golden brown for thicker pork chops.
Serve, enjoy.
