Title:
Big Ray's Cheesy Mushroom-Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119148.jpg
Ingredients:
4 frozen tilapia fillets (1 lb.)
2 Tbsp. Italian Dressing
1 cup  sliced fresh mushrooms
1 medium onion, sliced
1/2 cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1 Tbsp.  chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat oven to 450Â°F.
Place frozen fish in 13x9-inch baking dish; drizzle with dressing. Bake 25 min.
In a medium skillet, melt butter and add onions. Heat until limp.  Add mushrooms and heat until mushrooms start to brown.  Remove from heat and set aside.   When fish have cooked top with onions, mushrooms and cheese; bake 5 min. more or until cheese is melted and fish flakes easily with fork.
Sprinkle with parsley.
