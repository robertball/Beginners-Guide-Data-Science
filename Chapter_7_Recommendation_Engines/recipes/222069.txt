Title:
Easy Raspberry Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/840149.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime soda
1 gallon fruit punch
1 pint raspberry sherbet

Directions:
Mix lemon-lime soda and fruit punch together in a large punch bowl. Scoop raspberry sherbet into punch mixture.

Submitter: 
Chrissy DeCosmo Fessler
Description: 
A tasty easy punch perfect for parties and showers. Garnish with cherries and raspberries if desired.
