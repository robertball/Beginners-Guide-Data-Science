Title:
Roasted Potato, Tomato & Onion Tian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695133.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Medium Tomatoes, very thinly sliced
3 Medium Potatoes, very thinly sliced
1 Medium Sweet Onion, very thinly sliced
2 T. Olive Oil
1 t. Chives
1 t. Garlic
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Drizzle half of the olive oil in the bottom of a shallow baking dish coating thoroughly.  Arrange the vegetables in rows, overtlapping and alternating.  Drizzle with remiaining olive oil.  Season the chives, garlic, salt and pepper.  Roast at 425 degrees F for 25 to 30 minutes.  (Potatos should be tender and edges slightly browned.)

Submitter: 
Shannon Felgner
Description: 
Need a new potato side dish for the next gathering?  (Enough cheesy party potatoes already!!)  Try this lovely layered potato side dish that is light yet satisfying. I created it when I had a bumper crop of potatoes and tomatos. It is fairly easy to make and has a lovely presentation!
