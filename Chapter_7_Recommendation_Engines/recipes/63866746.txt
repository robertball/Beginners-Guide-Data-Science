Title:
Roasted Hazelnut Chocolate Chip Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb (3 sticks) unsalted butter at room temperature
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup powdered sugar
2 Tablespoons honey
2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
3 cups all purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
2 cups roasted Hazelnuts finely chopped *
1 cup semisweet mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Sift together flour and salt. Set aside
In the bowl of a mixer, cream together butter and sugars
Add honey and vanilla, beat until well incorporated
Add flour 1 cup at a time and blend on medium speed just until moistened
Stir in chopped nuts and chocolate chips into cookie dough. Do not over mix
Scrap dough onto plastic wrap, cover with another piece of plastic and form into a 1/2 inch rectangle
Refrigerate for at least 1 hour
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cut or shape dough into same size/ weight cookies. I weight dough into .5 ounces and press into a cookie cutter to shape, then remove
Place on ungreased, unlined cookie sheet and bake 12 - 14 minutes depending on the size of your cookies and your oven. Start checking at 10 minutes.
