Title:
Blueberry Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/875951.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon chia seeds
1 (6 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup mango juice
1/4 cup fresh blueberries
1/4 cup fresh mango chunks
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Grind chia seeds in a food processor until pulverized; add yogurt, mango juice, blueberries, mango, and vanilla extract and blend until smooth.
