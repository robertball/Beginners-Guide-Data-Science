Title:
Fast yet Yummy Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Elbow Macaroni
1/2 Cup Real Mayo
1 tbs dry dill weed
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1/4 cup chopped red bell peppers
1/4 cup chopped green bell peppers
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring pot of macaroni  to rapid boil. Cook till al dente
In large bowl combined all other ingredients and chill while pasta is cooking.
When pasta is done drain and rinse in cold water drainning well
Add to mixture and stir well.

Description: 
This recipe is very easy fast and always a delight to share. Using just a few ingredients.
