Title:
Cheesy Butternut Squash Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 Tbsp. butter
1/3 c. onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 c. apple, peeled and diced
2 c. cooked butternut squash
1 c. chicken broth
1/3 c. milk
1/3 c. parmesan, asiago, romano cheese blend
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Over a medium-low heat, saute onion, garlic, and apple in butter and olive oil till softened and brown, about 15 minutes.
Add cooked butternut squash and chicken broth, raise the heat until the mixture is bubbly.
Lower the heat and add cheese and milk.
Stir till cheese is melted.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Serve over whole grain pasta or ravioli.

Submitter: 
SPEARL20
Description: 
I had some leftover cooked butternut squash and and made this savory pasta sauce.  It is tasty over ravioli or any whole grain pasta.  Your squash should be very soft to begin with.  I generally steam my squash in a slow cooker for 4-5 hours.
