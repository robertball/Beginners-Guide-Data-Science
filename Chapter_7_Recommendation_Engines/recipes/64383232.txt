Title:
Think N' Tangy Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2208752.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Egg Yolks
1/2 Teaspoon of Mustard Powder
2 Tablespoons of Distilled White Vinegar
2 Teaspoons of Lemon Juice
2 Teaspoons of Sugar
1/4 Teaspoon of Salt
2 Cups of Vegetable Oil

Directions:
Chill a small mixing bowl in the fridge beforehand to help with emulsion
Combine Mustard Powder, Salt, Vinegar, Lemon Juice, Sugar, and Egg Yolks in the mixing bowl and  thoroughly mix together with a hand mixer.
Slowly incorporate the Vegetable Oil into the bowl while mixing at high speed.
Store in a glass mason jar and refrigerate for an hour before using for best consistancy and taste.
