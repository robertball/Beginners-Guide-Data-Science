Title:
Vanilla Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 ounces sweetened condensed milk, 2 cups half n' half or whole milk, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Place ingredients in a clean container and shake vigorously. Store in the refrigerator for 1 week.
