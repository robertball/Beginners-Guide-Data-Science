Title:
Ethiopian Spicy Mixed Vegetable Stew (Yetakelt Wat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Onions, finely chopped
2 Garlic cloves, pressed
2 Tablespoons Berbere
1 teaspoon ground cloves
1 Tablespoon sweet Hungarian paprika
¼ Cup Spiced Butter
1 Cup green beans, cut in thirds
1 Cup Carrots, peeled and chopped
1 Cup Potatoes, unpeeled and cubed
1 Cup Tomatoes, chopped
¼Cup Tomato paste
2 Cups vegetable stock
Salt and pepper to taste
¼ Cups Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
aute the onions, garlic, berbere, ground cloves, and paprika in the Spiced Butter for 2 minutes.
Add the beans, carrots, and potatoes and continue to saute for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, and vegetable stock.
Bring to a boil and then simmer for 40 minutes, or until all of the vegetables are tender.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Mix in the parsley.
ä Serve with
Injera
yogurt or cottage cheese.
