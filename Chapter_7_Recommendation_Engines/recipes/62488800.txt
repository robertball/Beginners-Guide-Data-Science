Title:
Pineapple Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chicken wings
1 can pineapple tidbits, undrained
1 cup sweet & sour bbq sauce

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Place wings in greased 9x13 baking dish. Bake for 30 minutes
2) Drain pineapple, reserving 1/4 cup juice. Mix juice, sauce and pineapple. Spoon over wings; toss to coat.
3) Bake 30 minutes more or until wings are cooked through.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
Awesome as an appetizer, part of a meal, or for potlucks.
