Title:
Scrumptions Old Homemade PB&J Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/863489.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf of bread (8 slices)
1 jar of peanut butter
1 jar of jam
measuring spoons

Directions:
Take 2 slices of bread.
Spread 2 tablespoons (or more) of peanut butter on one slice of bread.
Dollop jam on the other slice.
Smash your messy masterpiece together and enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
Slices of bread with gooey jelly and sticky peanut butter smashed together! An old favorite!
