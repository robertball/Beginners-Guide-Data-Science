Title:
Crock Pot Cuban Mojo Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb. boneless pork butt, trimmed of excess fat, then cut into big hunks
3/4 Cup chicken broth
1/2 Cup Orange Juice
1/4 Cup Lime Juice
1/4 Cup Lemon Juice
1 onion, cut into big chunks
1 Jalapeno, sliced in half
1 Head Garlic, cloves separated and peeled
1 1/2 tea salt
1 tea dried oregano
1/2 tea pepper
1/2 tea cumin
1/2 tea smoked paprika
2 bay leaves

Directions:
1.  Add all ingredients to large crock pot then stir to combine.  Cook on low for 8-10 hours or until pork shreds easily with a fork
