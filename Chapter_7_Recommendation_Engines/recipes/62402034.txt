Title:
Eggs Fantastic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 lb sausage
1 c salsa
1 onion (I use minced onion)
1/4 cup mushroom (Tiff used 1/2 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz sour cream
8 oz shredded velveeta
8 oz shredded cheese

Directions:
Wisk eggs, add sour cream pour in 9x13 pan and bake at 350 until set (15-20 min) meanwhile cook sausage, onion, and mushrooms, mix the Velveeta and the soup in sausage first, spoon sausage mixture then salsa top with cheese, cook til cheese is melted and bubbly
