Title:
Green Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. oil
1 onion cubed
1 onion finely chopped
2 T. finely chopped garlic
3 stalks finely chopped celery
1 t. ground cummin
1 t. chili powder
1 1/2 T. dried basil
1 (49-oz)can chicken stalk
1 (19-oz)can your favorite "green enchilada sauce"
3 large boneless skinless chicken breast cubed
1 can regular corn (do not drain)
1 can black beans (do not drain)
2 (4-oz)cans medium dices green chiles
1 C. cilantro, caursely chopped
1 1/2 T. Honey
salt and pepper to taste
-Topping-
sour cream
tortillas
crunched tortilla chips

Directions:
In a large pot saute the first onion and one stalk chopped celery.
add all ingredients to pot, except toppings.Bring to a simmer until chicken is cooked through and the onions are transparent. top with sour cream and tortillas or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Jamie Jensen
Description: 
Simple to make recipe that isn't simple to the taste. It has lots of flavor and is a unique tortilla soup.
