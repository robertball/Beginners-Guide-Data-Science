Microwave Drying Place a single layer of herbs onto a paper plate and place the plate inside the microwave. The herbs may take up to 2 minutes using this method. Check to see how they are drying at 30 second intervals. Ttry drying basil, mint, oregano, rosemary, tarragon and thyme in the microwave. Culinary herbs might not work too well with this method, as they may become too dry and lose most of their aromatic essential oils. Herbs being used for dried floral arrangements or decorative potpourri can be successfully dried using this method.
