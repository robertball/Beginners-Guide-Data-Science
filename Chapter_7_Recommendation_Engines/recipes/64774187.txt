Title:
BBQ Pineapple Flatbreads Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large flatbreads
1 c. canned or fresh pineapple
1/4 c. bbq sauce
1 c. Monterrey jack cheese
1/4 c. fresh mozzarella cheese
1 roasted green pepper or poblano pepper
1 red onion - cooked to caramelize
olive oil
red wine vinegar
cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven 450 with baking sheet in oven covered in foil OR use baking mat.  *Important step!
Pickle 1/4 of the onion by chopping and pouring red vinegar over it to soak
Cook rest of onion until caramelized, then add pineapple
Saute or roast pepper until cooked through
Coat each side of flatbread with olive oil
Assemble pizzas
Bake 10-12 mins
