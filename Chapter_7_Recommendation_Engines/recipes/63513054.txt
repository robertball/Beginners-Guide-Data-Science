Title:
Sausage Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 pound pasta
1 pound spicy Italian sausage
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes (Del Monte Diced Tomatoes with Basil, Garlic & Oregano)
1/2 of 6 oz bag of fresh spinach, chopped
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3 Tbl Italian Mozzarella Spice (Homemade Gourmet)

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown sausage with onion and garlic. Cook through until no longer pink.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain over chopped spinach and reserve.
Add basil, italian mozzarella spice, salt and tomatoes with liquid to the meat mixture.
Cook over medium heat for 5 minutes to slightly reduce. Add pasta and chopped spinach, reduced heat and sprinkle with cheese and serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description: 
Very simple pasta recipe tossed with sausage, tomato, onion, garlic and spinach to make a perfect and easy week night meal.Modified from: Sausage Pasta
