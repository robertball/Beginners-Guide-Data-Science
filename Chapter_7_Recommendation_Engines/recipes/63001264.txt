Title:
Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
tube of crescent rolls
1 1/2 lb of hamburger
pkg of taco seasoning
15 oz tomato sauce
1/2 c sour cream
2 c cheeder and mozzerella cheese
Dorritos or Tostidos

Directions:
Roll out the crescent rolls on a greased 9x13 inch pan. Fry the hamburger, drain and mix the taco seasoning, tomato paste and sour cream together. Put on the cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes. Top with chips.
