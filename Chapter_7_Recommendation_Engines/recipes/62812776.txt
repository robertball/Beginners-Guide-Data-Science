Title:
Linda's Mango Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups peeled & sliced mangoes
3/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp lime juice
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup instant tapioca
1 tbsp butter
9" pie crust, top & bottom

Directions:
Combine above ingredients except butter and place in a deep dish un-baked pie shell.
Dot with butter and cover with top pie crust.
Bake 1 hour at 400*

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
A great fruit pie.  Top with whipped cream.
