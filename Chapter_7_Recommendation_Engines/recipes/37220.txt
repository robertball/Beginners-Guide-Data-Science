Title:
Pasta with Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992707.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package fettuccini, uncooked
1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/3 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted

Directions:
COOK fettuccine according to package directions; drain and set aside.
COOK spinach according to package directions; drain and set aside. Saute garlic in olive oil in a large skillet until tender but not brown. Stir in cooked spinach, salt, and pepper; cook until thoroughly heated.
Toss together spinach mixture, fettuccine, Parmesan cheese, and pine nuts in a large serving bowl. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
For a speedy cooktop supper, serve this meatless main dish with sliced tomatoes and breadsticks.
