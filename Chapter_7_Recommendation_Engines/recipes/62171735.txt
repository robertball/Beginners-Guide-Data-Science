Title:
Simply Delicious Sausage, Potato and Onion Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium sized potatoes
1 large onion
1 1/2 - 2 lbs. sweet italian sausage
salt
pepper
olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Peel potatoes and cut into chunks.  Slice onion.
Brown sausage about five minutes on stove-top in about 1-2 tbsps. of olive oil.  Remove from pan and slice sausages.
Combine potatoes, onion and sausage in a 9x13 inch baking pan.  Season with salt and pepper.  Drizzle with about 2 tablespoons of olive oil and mix well.
Bake in oven 45 minutes.  After 45 minutes, turn heat up to 400 degrees for fiftenn minutes. This helps make the potatoes brown and crispy.
