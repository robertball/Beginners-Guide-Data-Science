Title:
No-Cook Pizza Sauce  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce (such as Muir Glen™ Organic)
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste (such as Muir Glen™ Organic)
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
1 teaspoon dried basil (optional)
3 pinches garlic powder, or to taste
1 pinch red pepper flakes
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine tomato sauce, tomato paste, olive oil, Italian herbs, basil, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, and salt in a medium bowl. Stir until thoroughly combined.

Submitter: 
Kristen M
Description: 
I like to avoid prepared and processed food, but with two young children, anything I make has to be easy and worth the effort (i.e. tasty!). Note: I usually use Muir Glen tomato products.
