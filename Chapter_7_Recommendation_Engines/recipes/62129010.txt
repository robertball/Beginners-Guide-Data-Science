Title:
Oyster Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Evaporated Milk + 4 cans water
2 sticks butter or margarine
1 container of fresh oysters (drained)
or 2 cans canned oysters (drained)
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring water and evaporated milk to slow boil.
Add margarine or butter.
Add salt & pepper.
Stir til all margarine or butter is completely melted.
Add oysters.
Cook on medium low, stirring often for 15 minutes.
serve with oyster or saltine crackers.
other suggestions: serve with hot sauce

Submitter: 
Chris Braddy
Description: 
A quick - but yummy - oyster stew!
