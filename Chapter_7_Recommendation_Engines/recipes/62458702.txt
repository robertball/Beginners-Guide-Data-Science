Title:
Cranberry Pineapple Molded Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small pkg Raspberry jello (or one large) Can subsitute sugar-less also)
2 cups hot water
1 can whole cranberry sauce
I can crushed pineapple (remove most all juice and put in measuring cup)
3 oz of cream cheese
1/3 cup mayonnaise
Dissolve the jello in hot water. Add juice drained from the pineapple.  Cool until syrupy. Add cranberry sauce and pineapple.  Pour a little more than ½ of the mixture into the mold.  LET SET.  Mix cream cheese and mayonnaise.  Spread over jello mixture. Add the remaining (room temperature) jello mixture.  Return to the refrigerator.  When set- unmold to serve.  Great thanksgiving side dish.
