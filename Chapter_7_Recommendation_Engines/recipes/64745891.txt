Title:
Simple Sauteed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags (10 oz) baby spinach
2 tsp minced garlic,
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp water
salt, to taste

Directions:
In a medium pan, heat olive oil and garlic on medium heat until sizzling.
Add spinach and water; Stir until leaves are coated.
Cover, cook 5-10 mintues or until leaves are wilted, stirring occasionally.
Drain excess water and oil.
Add salt to taste, serve.
