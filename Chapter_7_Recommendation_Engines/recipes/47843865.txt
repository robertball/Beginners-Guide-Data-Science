Title:
NEWFIE APPETIZERS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Vienna Sausages (little baby wieners)
1/3- 1/2 lb. Medium or Sharp Cheddar Cheese
8-10 baby dill pickles

Directions:
Slice Vienna Sausages and Baby Dills cross ways approximately 1/4 inch thick. Cube the cheese into similar sized pieces. On a round toothpick, skewer 1 slice of Vienna Sausage, 1 slice of pickle and 1 piece of Cheddar Cheese. Arrange on a platter and serve.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
This is a traditional Newfoundland appetizer and can be found at most Newfoundland “Kitchen Parties” as well as “Newfie” get-togethers across Canada.
