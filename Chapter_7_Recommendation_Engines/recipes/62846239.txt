Title:
BBQ Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 thick center-cut loin pork chops, 1 1/2 lbs. (BBQPC)
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. ginger
1/4 c. soy sauce
1/4 c. oil
1/4 c. steak sauce
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 T. brown sugar

Directions:
Place chops in a glass casserole dish. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over chops. Cover and refrigerate. Marinade overnight, turning several times. Grill over medium coals and baste occasionally with the marinade until done.
