Title:
White Bean Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/865722.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth (made my own)
3 small fresh tomatillos, chopped
1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes w/green chiles
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon ground coriander seed
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
1 cup frozen corn, thawed
1-1/2 cup diced, cooked chicken meat
1 (15 ounce) can white beans, drained & rinsed
1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil, and cook onion, then add garlic until  both soft.
Stir in broth, tomatillos, tomatoes w/chilies, and spices. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes.
Add cilantro, corn, chicken, and beans; simmer 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Julie Holden
Description: 
Canned white beans and cooked chicken meat are incorporated in chicken stock with tomatillos, green chilies, chopped tomatoes and corn kernels in this chili seasoned with cilantro, garlic, cumin, coriander and oregano.Modified from: White Bean Chicken Chili
