Title:
First Aid Lunch Kit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4537955.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12.5 oz. can chunk chicken breast, drained and flaked
1 green onion, thinly sliced
1 celery stalk, diced
2 tablespoons light mayonnaise; more or less to taste
Mixed green salad
Vile of salad dressing to represent peroxide
Red bell pepper cut into the shape of a red cross
Jicama sliced to represent splints
Organic 7 grain crackers to represent eye patches
Cheese squares with heart tooth picks
Whole wheat tortillas cut out in the shape of band-aids
Cheese squares to top the band-aids
Fruit roll ups to represent the gauze
Fruit jello to represent swabs soaking in iodopovidone
Yellow chocolate candies to represent aspirin
Magic grow pill capsules that transform into dinosaurs when dropped in water

Directions:
Thoroughly mix chicken, green onion, celery and mayonnaise together.
Place chicken salad and other ingredients in a first aid looking container and serve.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Check the heartbeat of this bento box style lunch that can be easily made for those young ones aspiring to go into the medical fields. This first aid kit comes complete with edible rolls of gauze, splints and aspirin among other things including capsules that transform into dinosaurs.
