Title:
Crispy and Tender Baked Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin on)
1/4 tsp of garlic salt
1/4 tsp of onion salt
1/4 tsp of oregano
1/4 tsp of thyme
1/4 tsp of paprika
1/4 tsp of black pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Line the a cookie sheet/broiler pan with foil and spray with cooking spray.
Put all of the spcies into a small container with lid and shake them together.  Sprinkle liberally over the chicken thighs.
Bake the chicken for 1 hour until the skin is crispy and the juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Nicole Burdett
Description: 
A simple spice blend give tons of flavor to the crispy skin and moist, juicy thigh meat.  This is in the oven in just minutes for a delicious (and budget-friendly!) main dish.
