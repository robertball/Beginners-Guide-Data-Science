Title:
Banana and Black Bean Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Cooking oil
1 banana, chopped
1/8 cup honey
3/4 can of black beans
Juice of 1/2 a lime
Pinch of salt
1 Can of coconut milk
Rice to serve

Directions:
Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
Add banana pieces and saute until hot.
Stir in honey.
Mix in black beans, heating for a couple of minutes.
Add lime juice and a pinch of salt. Stir.
Mix in 1/2 to 3/4 of the can of coconut milk and heat to reduce.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Lin Snow
Description: 
Given to me by my friend Amanda, this is a variation of one she used to make at a restaurant. Delicious served on rice!
