Title:
Slow Cooker Ropa Vieja Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797044.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15-oz can crushed tomatoes
3 T ketchup
1 T apple cider vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 t ground cumin
1 jalapeno pepper, thinly sliced (w/o seeds)
1 1/2 lbs skirt or flank steak
2 bell peppers, 1 red 1 green, sliced 1/2in thick
1 small onion, thinly sliced
3 T chopped pimiento-stuffed green olives
1 T brine from olive jar
Cooked white rice for serving

Directions:
Combine tomatoes, ketchup, vinegar, garlic, cumin and 3/4 t salt in slow cooker. Add steak, peppers and onion and toss to coat. Cover and cook on low, undisturbed, for 8 hours.
Uncover and skim off any excess fat. Coarsely shred the meat with 2 forks, then sir in olive and brine.
Serve over rice.
