Title:
Carribean Sunrise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Part Run
1 Part coconut rum
5 Parts Pineapple Juice
1/4 Part Grenedine
Crushed Ice

Directions:
Mix crushed ice, both rums, and pineapple juice in a Boston Shaker and shake.  Pour in your favorite cocktail glass.  Slowly add grenedine allowing it to settle to the bottom.
