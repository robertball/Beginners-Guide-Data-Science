Title:
Vegan Tofu Bok Choy Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package firm tofu or tempeh opt
1/4 cup all-purpose or rice flour
2 cups thinly sliced bok choy
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup red bell pepper, cut into 1/4 inch strips
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup veggie broth
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon dry sherry
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Drain and press firm tofu, cut into 1 inch pieces. Combine flour and tofu in a resealable bag, seal and shake well.
Add tofu to a heated non stick skillet, cook 3 minutes or until browned. Remove from pan and keep warm.
Add bok choy, celery, red pepper, mushrooms, water chestnuts and garlic; stir fry for 3 minutes, adding liquid if needed. In a bowl combine broth, soy sauce, cornstarch, sherry and ginger. Stir well. Combine tofu and broth mixture in skillet, cook 1 minute or until thick.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This is a very easy, tasty recipe.  Serve over rice and sprinkle with chopped green onions.Modified from: Pork Chop Suey
