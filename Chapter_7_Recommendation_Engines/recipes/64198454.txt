Title:
Kielbasa with Peppers and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 package kielbasa sausage,sliced, diced or chopped
3 medium red potatoes, diced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
1/2 Sweet Onion, chopped
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 cup fresh green beans
Sea Salt and Grinder Pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Place  potatoes in the saucepan. Cover, and cook 15 minutes, stirring occasionally, add kielbasa and continue cooking until potatoes are tender.
Mix red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, onions, garlic and green beans into the saucepan, and continue cooking 10 minutes, until peppers are just tender.
