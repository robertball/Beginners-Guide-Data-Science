Title:
Orange Rice Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/371265.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 green onion, chopped
1/2 cup chopped sweet red pepper
2 teaspoons olive or canola oil
1 cup uncooked brown rice
1 1/2 cups low sodium chicken broth
1/2 cup orange juice
1 tsp grated orange zest
1/4 teaspoon salt
Dash pepper

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and pepper in oil until tender. Add rice; stir until lightly browned. Add broth, orange juice, pepper and zest; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 50 minutes or until liquid is absorbed. Serve sprinkled with green onions.
