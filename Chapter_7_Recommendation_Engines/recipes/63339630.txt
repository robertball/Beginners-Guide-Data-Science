Title:
Pescado a la Sharon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6/6 oz. fillets of Corbina
Veggie Sauce to taste (see recipe below)
Mango/Ginger Sauce to taste (see recipe below)
Thinly sliced onion
Thinly sliced tomato
Salt & pepper to taste
Veggie Sauce
1/4 cup olive oil
Lots of finely minced or pressed garlic
Coarse salt to taste
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Mango/Ginger Sauce
Fresh mango
Soy sauce
Seasoned rice vinegar
Garlic
Fresh ginger
Raw almond chunks

Directions:
Wash and dry fish fillets.
Place fillets in banana leaves, one fillet per leaf
Season fillets with Veggie and Mango Sauce to taste.
Salt to taste.
Place one slice of onion and tomato atop each fillet.
Wrap fillet in banana, then wrap each fillet in tin foil, pinched and folded to seal.
BBQ at 400* for 8 minutes a side.
Serve hot

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Moist and flavorful recipe for Corbina, wrapped in banana leaves and bbq'd to perfection.
