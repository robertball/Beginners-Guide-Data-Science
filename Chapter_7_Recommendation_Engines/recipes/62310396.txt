Title:
Three Amigos Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole Hass Avocado
1 whole mango
1 medium grapefruit (or half a large one)
1/2 half of a lime

Directions:
Cut all three of the fruits into bite size pieces and mix together in a bowl
Squeeze the half lime over the salad and toss
Refrigerate if making ahead, keeps 2 days
Options: serve with cottage cheese for a complete diet meal
