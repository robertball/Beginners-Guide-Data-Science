Title:
Mango Tango Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985899.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (7 ounce) can whole kernel corn with peppers, drained
1 medium mango, peeled, seeded and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients. Serve with baked tortilla chips, if desired.

Submitter: 
Canned Food Alliance
Description: 
This colorful bean salsa is a great dip with tortilla chips - a fun and flavorful alternative to the usual bean dip.
