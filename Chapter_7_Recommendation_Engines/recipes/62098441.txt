Title:
Quick Green Beans & Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cut green beans (juice reserved)
3 med potatoes (peeled and cubed in 1 inch pieces)
4 slices thick bacon (chopped)
1 med onion (chopped)
2 cloves garlic (minced)
black pepper (to taste)
dash of garlic salt

Directions:
Place chopped bacon in large pot over med or med-hi heat. Once bacon begins to render and crisp, add onion and garlic.  Cook till onion is tender.
Add cubed potatoes & mix to coat.
Add juice from green beans to just cover potatoes and cook over med-hi, stirring occationally, till potates are just tender.
Add grean beans, pepper and garlic salt, mix & heat through.
Cover pot to let flavors blend till cool enough to serve.

Submitter: 
AVASEWELL
Description: 
A very simple version of an old Southern favorite. Everyone will think it took all day, but it's ready in no time with simple things we all have on hand. Thank you Grandma-ma...I remember you every time we gobble this up!
