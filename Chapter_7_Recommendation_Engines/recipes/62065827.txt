Title:
Grandma's Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/4 lb. butter (1 stick)
3T vegetable shortening
1 C sugar
juice of one orange
3 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt
about 4 C flour
for the filling:
3 lbs. MacIntosh apples
lemon juice
2-3 handfuls sugar
matzah meal

Directions:
mix ingredients for crust and add enough flour for good consistency (when it sticks together and not to you). Divide into 4 equal parts, roll out with flour underneath.
2) preheat oven to 300 degrees. sprinkle bottoms of pie crusts with matzah meal. core, peel, and slice apples (about 8 slices per apple). put into bowl of water with lemon juice, until all apples are ready. layer in crust with matzah meal and 2-3 handfuls of sugar. dot with a few peices of butter. cover with top crust and pinch edges together. bake for 1 hour or more until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Greg
Description: 
An old family recipe that uses a cookie crust, making it stand out from other apple pies. It works best if you don't try to halve it, and just make the 2 pies. you can freeze them until ready to bake.
