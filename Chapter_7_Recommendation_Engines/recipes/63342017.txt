Title:
The Best Asian Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rice vinegar
5 tablespoons honey
1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
1/8 cup Asian (toasted) sesame oil
3 tablespoons Asian chili paste or chili garlic sauce
3 tablespoons minced garlic

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together in saucepan.  Bring to boil and let reduce 3 to 5 minutes or so.  Lovely.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
This stuff is the absolute bomb!  Use on saute'd veggies, as glaze for meats, in woks, on rice, as dipping sauce - you name it.  It is spicy, so if kids are around, cut the chili paste down a bit.
