Title:
Thanksgiving Turkey Brine & Aromatics Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brine:
1 cup kosher salt
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 gallon vegetable stock
1 Tbs Black pepper corns
1/2 Tbs Allspice berries
1/2 Tbs candied ginger
1 gallon ice water or chicken broth
Aromatics:
1 red apple, sliced
1/2 onion, sliced
1 cinnamon stick
4 sprigs rosemary
6 leaves sage
1 cup water

Directions:
Brine:
Combine all ingredients, except ice water in stockpot. Bring to boil. Stir to dissolve all solids. Cool to room temperature and refrigerate until chilled. The night before combine brine and ice water in a 5 gallon bucket. Place thawed turkey breast side down in brine. Cover and refrigerate or place in cool area (basement or outside). Turn turkey over once halfway through brining. Remove from brine, rinse and pat dry.
Aromatics:
Bring all aromatics to boil. Remove from heat and place in turkey cavity.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This is the best brine! It will make your turkey incredibly tender and juicy.
