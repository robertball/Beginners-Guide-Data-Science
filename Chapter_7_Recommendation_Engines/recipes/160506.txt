Title:
Italian Vegetable Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985093.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup KRAFT Mayo Real Mayonnaise
1 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 (.7 ounce) envelope GOOD SEASONS Italian or Zesty Italian Salad Dressing & Recipe Mix
1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped red pepper

Directions:
Mix mayo, sour cream and salad dressing mix until well blended. Stir in peppers; cover.
Refrigerate several hours or until chilled.
Serve with NABISCO Crackers, fresh vegetable dippers or chips.

Submitter: 
Breakstone's and Knudsen Family of Products
Description: 
Mayonnaise, sour cream, Italian salad dressing mix and chopped bell peppers make a savory appetizer to serve with crackers, chips or vegetable dippers.
