Title:
9 Layer Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1:  1 large can refried beans mixed with package of taco seasoning
2:  2-3 cups guacamole
3:  1 large can chopped green chilies
4:  2 bunches of green onions chopped (reserve a few for garnish)
5:  1 large can pitted ripe olices, slice (reserve a few for garnish)
6:  2 diced tomatoes
7:  2 cups mild salsa
8:  2 cups grated cheese
9:  32 ounces sour cream

Directions:
Layer the ingredients in order given on large platter.
