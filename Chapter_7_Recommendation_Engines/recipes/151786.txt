Title:
Minute® Rice Tex Mex Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Minute® White or Brown Rice
1 pound browned ground beef
1 (15 ounce) can chili beans
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 pound prepared cheese product, cut into cubes, divided
1 (12 ounce) package crushed tortilla chips
1 bunch sliced green onions (optional)
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Prepare rice according to package directions; set aside.
Brown ground beef. Mix in cooked rice, beans and tomatoes with green chilies. Cover baking dish bottom with chips. Layer half of the cheese over the chips. Top with rice mixture. Top with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted. Garnish with sour cream and green onions, if desired.

Submitter: 
Minute® Rice
Description: 
Prepare this hearty beef and cheese main dish for a quick weeknight supper.
