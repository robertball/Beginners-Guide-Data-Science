Title:
Roasted Pork loin with Sweet red wine glaze *Crock pot & Oven* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106729.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon rubbed sage
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (5 pound) boneless pork loin
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon flower
1/4 cup vinegar
2oz red wine

Directions:
OVEN
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a bowl, combine sage, garlic salt, pepper. Rub thoroughly all over pork. Place pork in an uncovered roasting pan on the middle oven rack.
Bake in the preheated oven approximately 3 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C), depending upon your desired doneness.
Meanwhile, place sugar, flour, vinegar, and wine in a small saucepan. Heat, stirring occasionally, until mixture begins to bubble and thicken slightly. Brush roast with glaze 3 or 4 times during the last hour of cooking. Pour remaining glaze over roast, and serve.
CROCK POT
Rub seasoning thoroughly all over pork as above On low temp. for 2hrs then Brush roast with glaze 3 or 4 times during the last hour of cooking. Pour remaining glaze over roast, and serve. or until the internal temperature reaches at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C), depending upon your desired doneness.
