Title:
Best sausage,hash brown,egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Spicy ground breakfast sausage
1 30 oz. pkg.  frozen hash browns
1/2 c. Softened butter
16 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
1 16 oz. container sour cream
1/2 c. Onion, diced
1-2  jalapeño diced super fine (to taste)
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Cook sausage until brown and crumbly. Set aside.
Mix the softened butter with the hash browns
Press into a 9 x 13" pan, lightly greased.
I mix together, egg, sour cream,salt, pepper,sausage, jalapeño and cheese.
Pour over hash brown crust.
Bake for 1 hr or until toothpick comes out clean.
Let stand for 5 min.
