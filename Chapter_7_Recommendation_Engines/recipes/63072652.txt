Title:
Slow Cooker Cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777148.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces
1 onion, quartered and thinly sliced
4 large cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 (15 ounce) cans cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 pound turkey kielbasa, cut into small pieces
1/2 tsp. liquid smoke
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken broth - canned or homemade
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
4 oz. cream cheese - I used fat free

Directions:
Place the chicken into the bottom of a slow cooker. Stir together the onion, garlic, parsley, salt, pepper, cannellini beans, liquid smoke, chicken broth, white wine and turkey kielbasa in a large bowl. Pour the mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker, Cover and cook on low for 5 hours. Then stir in the cream cheese and shredded cheddar cheese and continue to cook for another hour. Serve over egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
