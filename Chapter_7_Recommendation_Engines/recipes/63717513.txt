Title:
Big Ray's Broiled Tillapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074268.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fat-free Italian salad dressing
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon each salt, garlic powder, curry powder, paprika, red pepper  and pepper
4 tillapia fillets (6 ounces each)
2 teaspoons butter

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the dressing, sugar and seasonings. Add the fish; seal bag and turn to coat. Refrigerate for 10-30 minutes.
Drain and discard marinade; place fillets on a broiler pan coated with cooking spray.  Broil 3-4 in. from the heat for 10-12 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork. Place 1 teaspoon butter on each fillet; let stand until melted.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This is the easiest and tastiest fish you'll serve. Even finicky eaters who think they don't like fish will love it because it lacks a fishy taste and is beautiful and flakes easily.
