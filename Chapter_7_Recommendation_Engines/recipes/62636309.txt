Title:
Not so Sloppy Hot Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. lean ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 can (10.75 Oz. can) condensed tomato soup
2 1/2 Tbs ketchup
1/2 Tbs  white vinegar
1/8 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1 Tbs taco seasoning
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 package Hot Dog buns
1 package your favorite Hot Dogs

Directions:
Place ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook until evenly browned,stirring to crumble. Use a potato masher or other implement to even out lumps. Add onion and celery, cover and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Drain off any excess grease.
Stir the tomato soup (undiluted), ketchup, brown sugar and Worcestershire sauce into beef mixture. Add salt, garlic powder and taco seasoning. Heat to a simmer over low heat and cook until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently to prevent it from burning on the bottom.
Cook your Hot Dogs by your favorite method.
Warm or toast your buns, (Hot dog buns) place hot dog into bun and add beef mixture, to your desired amount.
Enjoy, I really find this makes a great Hot Dog!
