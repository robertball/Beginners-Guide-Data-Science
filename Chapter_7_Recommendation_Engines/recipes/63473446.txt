Title:
Zucchini Appetizer squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946893.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups of thinly sliced zucchini
2 cups Bisquick
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup chopped Italian parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
cracked pepper to taste
2 gloves of chopped garlic
1 cup canola oil
5 large eggs, slightly beaten

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease an oblong 9x 13 pan. Mix all ingredients and spread in pan.  Garnish with a paprika grid design if desired. Bake until golden brown for about 25 min. or until toothpick comes out clean.  When cool, cut into 2x2 inch squares
