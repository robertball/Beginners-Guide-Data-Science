Title:
Julie's corn relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of cream cheese
1 stick of butter
jalapeno diced to taste
1 can of whole kernel corn
onion and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
melt butter and cream cheese until smooth
mix in onion powder, jalapeno and garlic powder
fold in corn
bake at 350 for about 30 min
