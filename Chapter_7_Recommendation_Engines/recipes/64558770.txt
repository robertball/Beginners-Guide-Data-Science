Title:
Kickin' BBQ Sriracha Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3426789.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon lemon pepper
1 tablespoon garlic pepper
1/2 cup sriracha sauce
1 bottle of Kansas City BBQ Sauce (or similar sauce)
honey
5-6 chicken strips

Directions:
Poke small holes into the strips in a large container. Pour ingredients into the container, marinate for 1-2 days.
Place half of the strips onto a preheated skillet (medium high heat)
Turn after 15 minutes.
Check the temp, remove.
Repeat for the remaining strips.
Drizzle honey (not too much) onto the strips.
Enjoy!
