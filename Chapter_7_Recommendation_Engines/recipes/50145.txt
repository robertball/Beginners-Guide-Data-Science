Title:
Iced Neschai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981942.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups water
1 cup Green Tea & Honey Flavor NESTEA速 Sweetened Iced Tea Concentrate
1/2 cup NESTLE速 CARNATION速 Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
2 cups ice cubes
6 leaves fresh mint (optional)

Directions:
PLACE water, Nestea, sweetened condensed milk, cinnamon and cardamom in large pitcher. Stir until combined. Serve over ice. Garnish with mint.
