Title:
Beefy Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2   pounds  ground beef, browned
1   medium  jar salsa, add to ground beef 1   package  corn tortillas, softened in hot oil or oven
2  cans evaporated milk
2  cans chopped green chilies
12  ounces American cheese
2  cans cream of chicken soup
2  packages green onion soup mix

Directions:
In the microwave, melt together milk chilies, cheese, soup and soup mix. Set aside.
Roll tortillas with ground beef mixture, and place in 9 x 13 pan. Pour melted mixture over rolled tortilla's.  Top with shredded cheese if desired.  Bake in oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
This makes 2 pans.  Eat one and freeze the other for later!

Submitter: 
Brenda Laughlin Sumner
Description: 
Rich with cheese and a cream sauce, this recipe is a family favorite and GREAT for parties!
