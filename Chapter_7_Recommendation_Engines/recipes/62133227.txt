Title:
Easy Homemade Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. vegetable oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
2 c. mashed ripe bananas (about 3-4)
Optional ingredients:
3/4 c. chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)
dash of cinnamon

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F
Grease a regular size loaf pan
Mix dry ingredients together in a separate small bowl.
Mix wet ingredients together in a medium-large bowl
Slowly add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Let cool for 10-15 minutes before serving.
