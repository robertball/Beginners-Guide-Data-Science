Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's速 Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular, 98% Fat Free or Healthy Request)
1 (2 ounce) pouch Campbell's速 Dry Onion Soup and Recipe Mix
6 small red potatoes, halved
6 medium carrots, cut into 2-inch pieces
1 (3 pound) boneless beef bottom round roast or chuck pot roast

Directions:
Stir the soup, onion soup mix, potatoes and carrots in a 4 1/2-quart slow cooker. Add the beef and turn to coat.
Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours* or until the beef is fork-tender.
