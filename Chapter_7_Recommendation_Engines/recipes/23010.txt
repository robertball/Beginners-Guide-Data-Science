Title:
Cranberry Salad VII Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4186728.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package cranberry flavored Jell-OÂ® mix
2 cups boiling water
1/2 orange, peeled and chopped
1/2 pound fresh or frozen cranberries, chopped
3 apples - peeled, cored and chopped
1 cup white sugar
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Refrigerate 1/2 hour, or until it begins to gel.
Mix together orange, cranberries, apples and sugar. In a 9x13 inch dish, mix the gelatin, fruit mixture, celery and walnuts
Chill in the refrigerator 3 to 4 hours, until firm.

Submitter: 
Valorie
Description: 
This recipe is a farm family favorite that's wonderful any time of year. Strawberry or raspberry Jell-O works just as well as cranberry in this shimmering salad that's both sweet and tart.
