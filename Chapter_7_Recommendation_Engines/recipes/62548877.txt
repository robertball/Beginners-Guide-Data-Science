Title:
Grandpa's  Famous Beer Can Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521766.jpg
Ingredients:
1. One turkey, sized to your family needs.
2. Can of cooking spray.
3. Lowery's Seasonings
4. Large roasting pan
5. Turkey rack to hold turkey.

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 450*. After thawing turkey, remove what ever is inside and rinse inside and out. Wipe outside with paper towel. Spray with cooking oil. Cover with as much seasoning that suits your taste. Pour beer in holder until it comes out of the holes and pour rest in roasting pan. Put turkey on holder, tie legs and wings together, put in pan, and put in oven. Add 2 quarts of water in roasting pan. Bake 20 minutes an 450* and reduce heat to 325* and continue baking for 3-3 1/2 hours until inside temperature is 180*. Remove from oven and let cool 15-20 minutes before slicing.
