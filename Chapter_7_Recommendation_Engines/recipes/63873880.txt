Title:
Poppy Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
2 1/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 Tablespoon poppy seeds
3 eggs
1 1/8 cup oil
1 1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1 1/2 teaspoon butter flavor
GLAZE:
1/4 cup orange juice
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1/2 teaspoon butter flavor

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Put every thing in a mixing bowl and beat together until smooth. Pour batter into 2 greased & floured 4x8 bread loaf pans and cook for  1 hour.
While bread is cooking make glaze by stirring all ingredients together. When bread is out of the oven, stir glaze then brush 2 coats on bread while bread is in the pan and still hot.

Description: 
A quick, traditional bread with a glaze that makes it out of this world.
