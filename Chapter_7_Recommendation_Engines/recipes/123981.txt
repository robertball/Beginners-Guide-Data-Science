Title:
Sausage Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (7 ounce) packages fully cooked sausage links, cut into slices
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
2 1/2 cups milk
2 cups fresh corn
2/3 cup sliced green onions
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
Crumble sausage into a large saucepan or Dutch oven; brown over medium heat.  Drain.  Add soup, milk, corn, green onions and hot pepper sauce.  Cook until corn is tender.  Reduce heat to low; add cheese and heat until melted.

Submitter: 
Sharon  Wallace
Description: 
This hearty soup is a meal in itself when served with a salad and bread. For a spicier flavor, I sometimes substitute Mexicorn for the whole kernel corn.
