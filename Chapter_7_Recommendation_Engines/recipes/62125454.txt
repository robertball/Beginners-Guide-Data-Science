Title:
Pumpkin Layer Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
First Layer:
1 3/4 c. graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. butter or margarine--melted
Second Layer:
2 eggs, beaten
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. sugar
Third Layer
3/4 c. milk
2 pkg. (4 oz.) instant vanilla pudding
1-16 oz. can pumpkin
1/2 t. pumpkin pie spice
1 c. cool whip

Directions:
Combine and mix ingredients for the first layer.  Mix well.  Spread on the bottom of a 9x13 pan.
Combine and mix the ingredients for the second layer.  Pour over graham cracker crumbs and bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Cool.
Combine ingredients for third layer.  Fold in one cup cool whip and pour over second layer.  Spread with cool whip and sprinkle with nuts.  Chill for several hours.

Submitter: 
J. Roderick
Description: 
A delicious, light pumpkin dessert.
