Title:
vegetable & egg muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2079800.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded cheese
1 1/2 cup white whole wheat flour
1/3 cup liquid egg white
1 1/2 cup skim milk
1/2 cup corn
1 cup chopped spinach
1/2 tomato

Directions:
Preheat oven to 356 degrees.
Mix ingredients in a large bowl until combined well.
Spoon into a greased muffin tin.
Cook for 20 to 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chef Lizzie
Description: 
This is a great recipe that you can make for those busy mornings. Can store in freezer
