Title:
Chinese Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cooked, shredded chicken
2 cups bean sprouts
1 cup sliced water chestnuts
1/4 cup sliced celery
1/4 cup sliced green onion
bag of crispy chinese noodles
Dressing:
1 cup mayonnaise (Best Foods)
4 T soy sauce
lemon juice to taste
touch of ginger-to taste

Directions:
toss green onion, sprouts, water chestnuts, celery and chicken. Mix dressing ingredients and mix well with previous. Chill
Mix with noodles right before serving, so they don't get soggy. Serve on chilled plates.

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
A really good cold salad, no idea of recipe origin. Got it from Mom who got it from my sister.
