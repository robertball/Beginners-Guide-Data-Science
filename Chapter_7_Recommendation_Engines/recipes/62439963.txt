Title:
Top Ramen Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage
2 large chicken breasts
8 green onions
1 cup sliced almonds
2 packages Top Ramen - chicken
4 Tablespoons sesame seeds
Dressing
1 cup oil
6 Tablspoons rice vinegar
2 teaspoons pepper
1 package seasoning from Top Ramen

Directions:
Shred cabbage (or buy it already done).
Cook chicken and cut into bite size pieces.
Toast almonds, sesame seeds and top ramen noodles (broken up) for 10 minutes.
Mix dressing.
Toss it all together... best if refrigerated over night.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
Crunchy, filling salad for lunch or as a light dinner
