Title:
Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can yellow wax beans
1 can green beans
1 can red kidney beans
1 can garbanzo beans
3 chopped salad onions
DRESSING
3/4 cup vinegar
2/3 cup salad oil
2/3 cup sugar

Directions:
Drain beans and onions and marinate in dressing for 12 hours or more.
