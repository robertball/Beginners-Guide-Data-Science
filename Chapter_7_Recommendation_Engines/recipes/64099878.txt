Title:
Ooey Gooey Cheesy Chicken Crockpot Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1227143.jpg
Ingredients:
5 to 7 large boneless skinless chicken breast tenderloins (or 2 to 3 large breast)
1 - 12 oz jar of salsa, heat of your choice
1 - 4oz. can diced green chilies
1 handful of spanish olives, diced
1 medium onion, diced
1/2 cup chicken broth or water
1.5 cups shredded monterey jack cheese
2 Tbsp. tapioca flour, plus extra if sauce needs to be thickened
Tortillas (optional)

Directions:
1.  Add salsa, chicken broth, tapioca flour to crock pot and stir until flour is dissolved.
2. Add chilies, olives, onion, shredded cheese and stir, then add chicken.
3. Cook on high for 3 to 4 hours, or on low for 6-8.
4. Shred the chicken with a fork.
5. Serve in a bowl, or with tortillas and fixings.

Submitter: 
Jess
Description: 
These burritos taste like heaven, the smell is almost irresistible and the entire cooking process is done in a crock pot!
