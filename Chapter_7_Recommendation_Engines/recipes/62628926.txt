Title:
Rhubarb Strawberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123182.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups diced rhubarb
3-3 1/2 cups sugar
1 small strawberry jello

Directions:
Cook rhubarb in microwave with no water until tender.
Add sugar and cook a short time just to dissolve sugar.
Add jello and mix well.
Pour into containers and freeze or refrigerate.
