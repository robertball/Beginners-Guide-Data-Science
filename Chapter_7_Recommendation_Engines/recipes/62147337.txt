Title:
Chimichurri Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chimichurri:
1-2 cups fresh parsley
3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
2-4 tablespoons dried oregano
2-4 teaspoons ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
1-6 tablespoon minced garlic
1/2 tablespoon hot sauce, such as Tabascoâ„¢
Soup Base:
1 whole chicken
2 cups beef broth
4 cups chicken broth
1 large red onion
4 bell peppers
3 jalepenos - with or without seeds
4-8 small fresh tomatoes
2 - 6 large cloves garlic
4 Parsnips
3 carrots
4 med potatoes
1 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
Boil whole chicken in broths along with rough chopped 1 large red onion
4 bell peppers
3 jalepenos - with or without seeds
4-8 small fresh tomatoes
2 - 6 large cloves garlic.
Prepare Chimichuri in food processor:  Place the parsley, olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, cumin, salt, garlic and hot pepper sauce into the container of a blender or food processor. Blend for about 10 seconds on medium speed, or until ingredients are evenly blended.
Medium chop 4 Parsnips
3 carrots
4 med potatoes
1 cup chopped cilantro
When chicken is done, place chicken in bowl to cool.  Take all stock and using blender, puree.
Bring puree back to heat and add chopped 4 Parsnips
3 carrots
4 med potatoes
1 cup chopped cilantro.
Once chopped vegetables are tender, add pulled chicken.
Final add is 1/4 - 1/2 cup chimichurri.
Salt/Fresh ground black pepper to taste.
May be served with a streak of creme fresh/cilantro/fresh jalepeno as garnish on top
