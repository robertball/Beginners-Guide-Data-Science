Title:
Skillet Apples with Cinnamon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1124805.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
8 apples, cored and chopped
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Melt butter in a large, heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in hot butter until tender and golden, 10 to 15 minutes.
