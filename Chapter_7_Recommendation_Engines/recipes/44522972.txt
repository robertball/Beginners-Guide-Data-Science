Title:
Feta Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon canola oil
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup egg substitute
1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
2 tablespoons crumbled fat-free feta cheese
Pinch of oregano
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saute onions until translucent. Pour in egg substitute. Cook, stirring occasionally to scramble. When egg substitute appears almost done, stir in chopped tomatoes and feta cheese, and season with oregano, salt and pepper. Cook until cheese is melted.
