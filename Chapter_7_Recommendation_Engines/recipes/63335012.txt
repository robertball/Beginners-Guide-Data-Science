Title:
bourbon vanilla extract Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (8oz) cups bourbon
9 vanilla beans, split

Directions:
Put bourbon into a clean (preferably sterilized) jar and add the vanilla beans. Store in a cool, dark place for 8 weeks, shaking occasionally.
