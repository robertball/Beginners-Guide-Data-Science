Title:
Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 2 (8oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened (fat free if desired)
-1 cup sour cream (fat free if desired)
-1/2 cup granulated sugar
-1 tbsp. vanilla extract
-1 lb. green grapes, washed and rinsed
-1 lb. red grapes, washed and rinsed
-1 cup brown sugar
-1 chopped walnuts

Directions:
Beat the cream cheese, sour cream, sugar, and vanilla until thick and smooth.  Fold in the grapes and spread into a 9x13 pan.  Mix the brown sugar and walnuts together in a separate bowl and spinkle over the grape mixture.  Cover with plastic wrap and refridgerate until you serve, at least 4 hours.  It's best to just leave it overnight!

Submitter: 
Eric
Description: 
This is a wonderful blend of cream cheese and grapes with walnuts that your guests will fawn over!  The brown sugar melts into the cream cheese and makes this delicious dessert spectacular!  Make sure you bring plenty of copies of this summertime comfort treat!
