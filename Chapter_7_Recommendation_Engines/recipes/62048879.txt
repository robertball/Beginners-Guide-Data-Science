Title:
Sweet & Sour Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg English cucumber, peeled
1/4 cup red onion, chopped
2-3 T. fresh cilantro
3-4 T. peanuts, chopped
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup white vinegar
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
To make dressing:  Combine water, vinegar, sugar & salt in a small saucepan.  Heat over med-high heat, stirring occasionally, until gently boiling.  Let boil until slightly thickened and clear (about 2 mins.)  Remove from heat and let cool.
Cut cucumber lengthwise in half, then in quarters.  Then cut horizontally to form little triangles.
Combine cucumber, onion and cilantro in a med. bowl and pour cooled dressing over everything.  Cover and refridgerate until ready to serve.
Pour off most of dressing and sprinkle salad with chopped peanuts. Yummy!

Submitter: 
Jackie Cree
Description: 
A delightfully light tangy side dish that goes wonderfully with grilled dishes, particularly Asian-inspired ones.  Hope you enjoy it!
