Title:
30 Minute Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Archer Farms™ organic penne rigate
1 pound lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 (4 ounce) can Market Pantry™ diced chiles
1 (8 ounce) package sliced button mushrooms
1 (14.5 ounce) can Market Pantry™ stewed tomatoes
1 (25.5 ounce) jar Archer Farms™ organic Burgundy marinara
1 (8 ounce) package Market Pantry™ shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup Market Pantry™ grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Cook penne rigate according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in large skillet, brown ground beef with onions; drain.
Reduce heat to medium-low; add chiles, mushrooms, stewed tomatoes and marinara. Cook about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Spray 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. Add cooked pasta. Pour meat sauce over pasta. Sprinkle with cheeses.
Bake 15 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted.
