Title:
Mahi Mahi with orange and rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz mahi mahi
Olive Oil
2-4 Garlic cloves, minced
1-2 T rosemary (dried -- or use more fresh)
zest of 1/2 orange
juice of 1/4 to 1/2 orange
salt

Directions:
coat shallow baking dish with olive oil
put little olive oil on fish, both sides
create paste of garlic, rosemary, orange zest and little olive oil and apply to fish
add orange juice to pan
Bake 350 to 400 degrees for 15-20 min

Submitter:

Description: 
zesty citrus and rosemary; could also use basil or lemon
