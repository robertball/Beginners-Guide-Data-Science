Title:
Moscato Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
1 pint fresh raspberries
2 oranges, sliced
2 lemons, sliced
3 limes, sliced
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime soda (such as SpriteÂ®)
1 (750 milliliter) bottle moscato wine
1 (750 milliliter) bottle sparkling moscato wine
3 fluid ounces peach schnapps

Directions:
Combine strawberries, raspberries, oranges, lemons, and limes in a large container. Pour in lemon-lime soda, moscato wines, and peach schnapps. Mix and chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Sandy Wall- Krusinski
Description: 
This delicious moscato sangria is the perfect drink for a hot summer day.
