Title:
Grandma Rose’s Jam-jams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup margarine, softened
6 Tbsp. white corn syrup
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
3 ¼ -4 cups flour
Raspberry jam

Directions:
Beat eggs, sugar, margarine, and corn syrup together.
Blend together soda, vanilla and flour, and mix with previous ingredients.
Roll out dough. Use a glass to cut circles.
Put on cookie sheet and bake at 350 F for 8 minutes.
Once cookies have cooled, put ½ tsp. raspberry jam in between (on the face that was on the cookie sheet).
Make sandwiches and enjoy!
