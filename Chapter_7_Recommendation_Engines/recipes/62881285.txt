Title:
Better than sex Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
TOPPING
1 1/2 Cup Panko Bread Crumbs (Oriental section of your grocery store)
4 Tablespoons Butter
1 Dash Salt
1/4 Teaspoon Paprika
MAC & CHEESE
1 Lb. Box Elbow Macaroni (Or whichever you prefer)
2 12 Oz. Cans Evaporated Milk (Whole Milk)
2 Eggs
2 Teaspoons Mustard Powder
1 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Black Pepper (ground)
1 Teaspoon Tabasco
1 Lb. Sharp Cheddar Cheese (Shredded)
1/2 Lb. Velveeta Cheese (Cubed)
1/2 Lb. Parmesan Cheese (Grated)
1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter

Directions:
Shred, cube, grate whatever cheese you have to and combine them in a bowl, mixing evenly.
In a small pot melt 4 Tbsp butter over medium heat
Add Salt, Paprika, and Breadcrumbs stirring until browned (Approx 6-7 minutes).
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a bowl; combine the Milk, Eggs, Mustard, Salt, Pepper, And Tabasco and whisk until well blended.
Boil pasta and cook until "al dente".
Drain pot, then return to pot with the stick of Unsalted Butter and mix around evenly.
Now add the milk mixture to the pasta along with half the cheese and stir until cheese is melted.
Add Remaining cheese, stir until melted thoroughly.
Move this to a 9X13 in. pan, coat with bread crumbs and heat for 25 minutes in the preheated oven.
Remove and enjoy :)
