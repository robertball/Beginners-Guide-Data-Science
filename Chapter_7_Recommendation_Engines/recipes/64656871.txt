Title:
Easy Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick of Butter (unsalted)
16 oz. Heavy Cream
3 oz. Cream Cheese
3/4 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1-3 cloves of Garlic
Sea salt to taste

Directions:
Heat the butter, heavy cream and cream cheese over medium heat until melted and blended stirring constantly.
Add the parmesan cheese and garlic. Adjust the garlic to taste.
Simmer on low for 30 minutes stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Jack K Maxfield
Description: 
The creamiest Alfredo sauce that can made on the go.
