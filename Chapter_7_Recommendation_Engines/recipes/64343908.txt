Title:
MACE CAKE from Sandy Everett Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup BUTTER
2 Cups SUGAR
(cream butter and sugar together)
5 Large EGGS (Add 1 at a time beating 1 min. after each)
2 Cups FLOUR
1 TBS. MACE
(Sift together 3 times)
1 Cup chopped NUTS
(Folded in)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease Bundt pan generously with 2-3 TBS. shortening
Bake for 45-50 minutes.
Turn out of pan onto rack immediately.

Submitter: 
Shirley
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by my good friend Sandy Everett and it has been a family favorite for over 30 years.
