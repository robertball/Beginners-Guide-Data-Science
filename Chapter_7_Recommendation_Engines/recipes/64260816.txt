Title:
Cocoa Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup oil
2 cups brown sugar
2 cups grated zucchini
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
3 Tbsp cocoa
1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In large bowl, beat the eggs.  Add oil, brown sugar, zucchini and vanilla and mix well.   Add remaining ingredients in order given.
Pour into 2 greased bread pans.  Bake at for 1 hour.
