Title:
Kebab Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef or lamb
1 onion, minced
1/2 c cilantro
1/4 c ginger
1 garlic
2 T mint
2 jalapeno minced
1 T coriander
1 t pepper
1 t salt

Directions:
Mix all and make into 9 flat sausages. Place on skewers and grill
Serve with tzaki sauce
