Title:
Bison Meatballs with Caramelized Onion Gravy and Cauliflower Mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.29 lbs ground bison
1/3 c almond flour
1 tsp sea salt
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp cloves
3/4 tsp cacoa powder
1/2 tsp cumin
2 tsp oregano
1 egg
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 T avocado oil
1 T ghee
1 T butter
1 T ghee
1 red onion
1/2 yellow onion
1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
1/4 c balsamic vinegar
1/4 c red wine
1 c beer (gluten free Omission Lager)
2 c low sodium beef stock
2 T butter
1 T ghee
4 garlic cloves
1 head cauliflower
2 c low sodium chicken stock
2 T ghee

Directions:
Meatballs:
combine almond flour, salt, pepper, and spices
combine egg and Worcestershire sauce
combine bison with dry mixture and egg mixture
make meatballs (19 in this recipe)
brown meatballs in avocado oil and ghee on medium high heat for about 2-3 mintues on each side; set aside
Gravy:
in same pan, add butter, saute garlic, onion until golden and starting to caramelize
deglaze pan with vinegar, wine and beer
add stock
return meatballs to pan and simmer on low for 20 minutes
season to taste
add 2 T butter at end before serving
Cauliflower Mash:
saute garlic in 1 T ghee until softened
add cauliflower
add stock
bring to boil and cook until very tender
strain cauliflower, reserving liquid
put cauliflower in vitamix with 2T ghee
blend until smooth with salt/pepper to taste
