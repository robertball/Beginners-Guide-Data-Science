Title:
Turkey n’ Stuffing Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519106.jpg
Ingredients:
4 c. herb-seasoned stuffing cubes, divided
1 10 oz. package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 c. diced cooked turkey or chicken
½ c. shredded cheddar cheese
3 tbsp. thinly sliced green onions with tops
6 egg
1 ½ c. skim or low-fat milk
2 tbsp. sliced blanched almonds

Directions:
Evenly coat 8x8 baking pan with spray.  Sprinkle 2 cups of stuffing cubes in bottom of pan.  Layer broccoli, turkey, cheese and onions over cubes.  Top with remaining 2 cups of cubes.  In large bowl, beat together eggs and milk until well blended.  Pour evenly over stuffing mixture.  Cover.  Refrigerate several hours.
Uncover.  Sprinkle with almonds.  Bake in preheated 350 degree oven until golden brown and knife inserted near center comes out clean.  50-60 minutes.
