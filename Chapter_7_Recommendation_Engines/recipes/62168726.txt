Title:
Aromatic pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pork chops
Tsp. aromatic bitters
Meat seasoning
salt
pepper

Directions:
Spray pan with canola or olive oil,and after thirty seconds or so, put in pork chops. To let the pork chops cook in the bitters, put the bitters in after one minute. put a pinch of seasoning on one side then flip and put seasoning on that side, too. Add salt and pepper, and enjoy!
