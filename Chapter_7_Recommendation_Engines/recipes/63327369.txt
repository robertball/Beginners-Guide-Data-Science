Title:
Anniversary Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 pieces boneless chicken breasts
small bottle Ranch salad dressing
1cup grated cheddar cheese
1/4cup chopped green onion (to taste)
1tsp teriyaki sauce per piece of chicken

Directions:
Coat chicken in flour and fry until each side is light brown. Place chicken in 9x11 dish and put 1tsp teriyaki sauce on each piece. Cover with ranch dressing. Sprinkle on cheese and green onion. Bake at 375C for 45min. Serve HOT. Enjoy!
