Title:
Sweet-Hot Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cups water
1 6oz Can tomato paste
1/2 Cup mild-flavored molasses
1/2 Cup dark-color corn syrup
1/4 Cup vinegar
1/4 Cup honey
1 Tablespoon whiskey or bourbon (Optional)
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. paprika
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. liquid smoke
1 tsp. bottled hot pepper sauce (Franks Red Hot)
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1/4 to 1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp. onion powder

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine all the ingredients, and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low, and cook uncovered for about 45 minutes or until mixture reaches desired consistency, stirring frequently.
