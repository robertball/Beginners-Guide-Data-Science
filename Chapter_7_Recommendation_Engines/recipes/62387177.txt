Title:
Orange Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound baby carrots
1/4 cup orange juice
3 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons butter
1 pinch salt
1 pinch cinnamon
1 1/2 TBL. corn starch

Directions:
Place carrots in a shallow saucepan, and cover with water. Boil until tender. Drain, and return carrots to pan.
In another saucepan, combine orange juice, brown sugar, butter, salt, cinnamon, and corn starch. Simmer over medium heat until hot and sauce thickens up, stirring frequently.  Pour sauce over carrots, and mix well. Simmer over medium heat for about 5 minutes, until carrots are nicely glazed.

Submitter: 
Floyd Joswick
