Title:
Ms Honey's Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pre-baked pie shell
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 ounces cocoa (dry cocoa to 4 oz line of meas. cup)
1/2 tsp. salt
2 and 1/2 TBsp cornstarch
1 TBsp flour
2 and 1/2 cups milk
3 eggs, separated
1 Tbsp butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. cream of tartar
6 Tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in saucepan and stir in milk. Cook until thick. Stir 1 cup mixture into 3 egg yolks. When mixture is thick, remove and add butter and flavoring. (If too thin, add little more flour.) Pour into pre-baked pie shell
Make meringue with 3 egg whites, 1/4 tsp. cream of tartar. Beat and gradually add 6 TBsp. sugar until peaks form. Brown in oven.

Dark chocolate pie with meringue.
