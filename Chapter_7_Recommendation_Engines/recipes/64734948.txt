Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
8 oz of Sour Cream
1 small can of green chilies
8 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
8 oz Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
1 can of Corn
1 1/2 lbs  Cooked Chicken Breast, cubed
12 Flour Tortillas, (The bigger ones, Burrito)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ÂºF, prep a 9x13 with cooking spray.
Combine soup, sour cream, chilies, and 1/2 of the cheese.
Divide into two bowls and add all of the chicken and corn to one of the bowls.
Fill tortilla's with Chicken mixture and place in prepared pan seem side down.
Cover enchiladas with the remainder of the mixture and sprinkle the rest of the cheese on the top.
Bake for 30-40 minutes until top is bubbling and cheese has started to brown.
