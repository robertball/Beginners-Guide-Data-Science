Title:
Stuffed Green Pepper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef, cooked and drained
1 family size can tomato soup and 1 can of water
3 med green peppers
1 med onion
1 can diced tomatoes
2 beef boullion cubes
2 tsp sugar
sprinkle of pepper to taste
1 cup prepared rice

Directions:
Add all ingredients (except rice) together in large pot. Cover and simmer over medium heat until peppers/onion are tender.  Stir in rice and serve.

Submitter: 
Jen K
Description: 
My friend makes this delicious soup during colder weather and it's wonderfull.  Tastes just like stuffed peppers.
