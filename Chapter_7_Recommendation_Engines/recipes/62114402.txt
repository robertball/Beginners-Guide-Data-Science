Title:
Preacher Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 regular size pkg of cream cheese
1/4 cup powdered sugar
Tub of cool whip
1 pkg. of vanilla instant pudding
1 pkg. of chocolate instant pudding
3 cups milk

Directions:
Crust:
Mix 1 cup flour, melted butter, and pecans together.  Spread crust in either a 13x9 in. greased pan.  The crust will be thin, and it dosent have to be smooth and pretty.  Then bake for 15 min at 350 degrees.  Let cool when done.
1st layer:
In a med bowl beat in cream cheese (softened), and powdered sugar.  Then fold in 2 cups of whipped topping.  Then spread onto cooled crust.
2nd layer:  Beat vanilla and chocolate pudding with 3 cups milk, and spread on to 1st layer.
put in fridge for 10 min. So pudding sets a little bit.  Then top with cool whip. You can shred chocolate, or sprinkle with pecans for a nice look.

Choclate pudding dream with cream cheese center baked on a pecan crust.  This is soooo good!!
