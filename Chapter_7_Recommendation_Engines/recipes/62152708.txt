Title:
Chile Rellenos Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs, beaten
1 cup sour cream
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
4 cups shredded four-cheese mix
1 cup chopped green chiles*

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Grease a 9 X 13 inch dish.
Combine all ingredients.
Pour into prepared dish.
Bake 45 minutes or until golden brown and set in the center.
Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
Serve with warm tortillas, sour cream, salsa, black olives, or toppings of your choice.
*Buy a large can of green chiles - hot, medium or mild, dice enough for 1 cup, and freeze the remainder for next time. Saves money and you'll get no seeds, stems, etc.)

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
If you like chile rellenos, you will love this dish for it's ease of preparation...all the taste without the work!
And it reheats well IF there are any leftovers.
