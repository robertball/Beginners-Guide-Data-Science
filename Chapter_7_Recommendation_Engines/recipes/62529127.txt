Title:
Classic Turkey and Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For Stock:
1 turkey carcass, picked over
1 large onion, cut in half, skin left on
1 large carrot, roughly chopped
1 stalk celery, roughly chopped
1 head garlic, sliced in half
2 bay leaves
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp thyme
salt and pepper
enough water to fill the pot
For Soup:
2 large onions, diced
2 carrots, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp onion powder
2 cups cooked rice of your choice
salt and pepper

Directions:
Add all of the ingredients for the stock into a large pot, and bring to a simmer.   Cook, covered, for about an  hour over low heat, then remove the carcass and all of the vegetables from the stock.  Remove all turkey meat from carcass, and set aside. Discard the vegetables.
Add the onions, carrots, celery, garlic, and the seasonings to the stock, bring to a simmer.  Cook, covered, for 20-30 minutes or until veg are tender.  Add the cooked rice and the turkey meat, and check seasonings, adding more salt and pepper to taste.  Enjoy!
