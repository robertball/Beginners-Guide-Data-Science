Title:
Honey Oatmeal Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz skim milk
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon raw honey
1 Tablespoon honey
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup oats
2 1/2 cup bread (or wheat) flour
1 1/2 teaspoon yeast

Directions:
Add each ingredient into the bread maker. Use the regular and light cycles (3).
