Title:
Chicken OMG !! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3LBS CUBED CHICKEN
2 cups cubed bell peppers
1/2 cups sliced carrots
1/2 cups sliced squash
1 cup cut green beans
1 small jar sweet red roasted peppers
2 packages savory lipton soup mix
water
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crockpot
Cover with water
Let cook on high 4-6 hours or on low 8-10 hrs
