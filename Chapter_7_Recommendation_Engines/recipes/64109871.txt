Title:
Seared Salmon with Lentil Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T and 2 tsp olive oil, divided
1 1/4 lb skinless salmon fillet, cut into 4 pieces
Kosher salt and pepper
2T lemon juice
2 T Dijon mustard
2 stalks celery, thinly sliced
1 small seedless cucumber, cut into 1/2 in. pieces
1/2 small red onion, finely chopped
15 oz. can of lentils, rinsed
1/2 cup fresh flat-leaf parsley or cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Heat 2 tsp oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Season the salmon with 1/4 tsp each salt and pepper and cook until opaque throughout, 5 to 6 minutes per side; transfer to plates.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, mustard, remaining 2 T oil and 1/2 tsp each salt and pepper. Add the celery, cucumber and red onion and toss to coat. Fold in the lentils and parsley. Serve with salmon.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This dish can be switched up for a vegetarian meal by omitting the salmon and folding baby spinach or arugula and one Granny Smith apple into the lentil mixture.
