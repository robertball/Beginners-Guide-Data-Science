Title:
Super Simple Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) jar salsa
1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened

Directions:
Combine salsa and cream cheese in a bowl or blender (blender if you like a smoother texture).  Beat or puree until thoroughly mixed.  Serve immediately with tortilla chips or refrigerate for a colder dip.
*Tip: Try playing around with different salsas for various flavors.  Can also be garnished with diced tomatoes or olives.
