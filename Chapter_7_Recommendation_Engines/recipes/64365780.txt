Title:
Bulgarian Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 white onion chopped, 1/2 red bell pepper chopped, 1 carrot chopped, 1 bunch Italian parsley, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 3 cubes chicken Bouillon, 1 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon Bulgarian oregano, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 4 cups Fideo egg noodles, 2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts, 10 cups water, 1 lemon

Directions:
in a large pot boil chicken breasts in water till cooked through
take out chicken and shred (easily done using 2 forks pulling in opposite directions)
while chicken is out, chop up the parsley but keep the stems chopped up separately from the rest
add parsley stems, onion, bell pepper, carrot, tomatoes and all spices
when the water boils again, add noodles and the shredded chicken
in about 10 minutes, when the pasta is cooked to your liking, turn off the oven and add the rest of the parsley
let cool for ten minutes and squeeze a fresh lemon 8th into individual serving bowl before serving

Submitter:

Description: 
This is my favorite chicken noodle soup recipe. It tastes nothing like the stuff you get in the store. It's my mom's recipe and is a favorite in my family.
