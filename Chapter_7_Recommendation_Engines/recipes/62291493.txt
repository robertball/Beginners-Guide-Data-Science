Title:
Low Sodium All Purpose Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436550.jpg
Ingredients:
Try these adding more or less according to your families taste buds
1 T granulated garlic or garlic powder
2 t onion powder
2 t paprika
2 t black pepper
1 t white pepper
1 t cayenne
1/2 t thyme
1/2 t basil
1/2 t celery salt, seed or flakes (depending on what you have on hand)
1/2 t italian oregano (was from my garden & dried in the microwave)
couple of turns of my sea salt grinder

Directions:
Mix them all together in a bowl. Store in an airtight container or baggie. I like to use empty spice containers.

Submitter: 
Scotdog
Description: 
My husband was diagnosed with high blood pressure & diabetes. I'm constantly trying to find recipes that fit the low sodium criteria. When you can't have salt, you have to punch up the other flavors. This is a flavorful mix of Cajun and Creole flavors.
