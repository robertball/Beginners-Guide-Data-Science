Title:
California Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen BOCA Meatless Burger (any variety)
1 whole wheat bun, toasted
1 lettuce leaf
4 strips roasted red/green pepper
5 slices cucumber
3 slices onion rings
3 slices peeled avocado
1 tablespoon reduced fat Ranch or your favorite salad dressing (optional)

Directions:
GRILL a frozen BOCA Meatless Burger (any variety) as directed on package.
SERVE on a toasted whole wheat bun, with lettuce.  Top burger with grilled or roasted peppers, cucumber and onion slices and fresh avocado.  Drizzle with reduced fat Ranch or any favorite salad dressing, if desired.
