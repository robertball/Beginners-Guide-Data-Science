Title:
"Cherry, Almond Cinnamon Granola" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5280639.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Old fashioned rolled oats
2 cups sweetened, shredded coconut
2 cups sliced almonds
1 1/2 cup dried cherries, chopped
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/3 cup honey
2 tsp. ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Toss oats, coconut, almonds and cherries in a large bowl.
Pour the oil and honey over the oat mixture.  Add the cinnamon and stir with a wooden spoon until oats and nuts are coated.
Pour onto a sheet pan with sides.  Bake, stirring occasionally with wooden spoon until the mixture turns a nice even golden brown, about 25-30 min.
Remove pan from the oven and place on wire rack and cool undisturbed until room temperature about 1 1/2 hours.
Store in airtight container.
FOOTNOTES:
Other dried fruits and nuts can be added as desired.
