Title:
SPAGHETTI PUTANESCa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
9 anchovies (opt)
8 cloves garlic, whole
1/4 tsp dried red pepper flakes
1-28oz can chopped tomatoes w/juice
12 Calmata olives, pitted & halved
2 tsp capers, drained
5 cloves garlic, chopped
1/3 cup chop fresh parsley
1 Tbl chop fresh basil
3/4 lb fresh pasta

Directions:
Heat oil in saute pan. Add anchovies & whole garlic cloves. Cook until the garlic cloves brown & the anchovies fall apart. Add red pepper flakes & cook few seconds more. Add tomatoes, olives & capers. Simmer for 30 min. Add chopped garlic, parsley & basil to sauce & simmer a bit longer
Cook pasta according to pasta directions. Toss with 1/2 sauce until well coated. Arrange pasta on platter & pour remaining sauce on top.
