Title:
Libby's® Pumpkin Apple Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984524.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can LIBBY'S® 100% Pure Pumpkin
1 medium apple, peeled and grated
1 cup apple juice
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Combine pumpkin, apple, apple juice, sugar and pumpkin pie spice in medium, heavy-duty saucepan. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 1 1/2 hours. Serve with buttermilk biscuits, breads, corn muffins or hot cereal. Store in airtight container in refrigerator for up to 2 months.

Submitter: 
Libby's® Pumpkin
Description: 
Take the chill off a winter day or delight in a sunny morning! Pumpkin Apple Butter on flaky buttermilk biscuits makes the perfect combination!
