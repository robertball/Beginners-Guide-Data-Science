Title:
Sweet Potato and Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
3 cups sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
3 carrots, peeled and sliced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp. fresh gingerroot, minced
1 tsp. cumin
1.2 tsp. coriander
1 tsp. turmeric
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. dried thyme
5 cups vegetable broth
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes
3 bay leaves
1 16-oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
2 tbsp. natural style creamy peanut butter

Directions:
Heat oil in a large soup pot or Dutch oven over medium heat; add onions, celery and peppers and saute 2 to 3 minutes.
Add sweet potatoes and saute for about 4 minutes or until potatoes just begin to soften slightly.
Add carrots, garlic, ginger, spices and thyme.  Cook, stirring often, about 4 minutes until potatoes and carrots are just tender.
Add broth, tomatoes and bay leaves; bring to boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Add black beans and cook 10 minutes or until beans are heated through.
Stir in peanut butter until combined and remove from heat.
Discard bay leaves before serving.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
A touch of peanut butter adds a rich smoothness to this exotically spiced soup, chock full of chunky vegetables and hearty black beans.
