Title:
Homemade Cranberry Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977277.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large baking apples, peeled, cored, and chopped
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle Cranberry Apple Flavor LIBBY'S速 JUICY JUICE速 100% Juice
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 (8 ounce) package dried cranberries

Directions:
COMBINE apples, Juicy Juice, cranberries, brown sugar and cinnamon in stockpot. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, for 35 to 40 minutes or until apples are very tender. Serve warm or chilled.
