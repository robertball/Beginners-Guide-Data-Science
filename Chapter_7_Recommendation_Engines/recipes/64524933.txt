Title:
Rolls and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2970639.jpg
Ingredients:
1 container of frozen bread roll dough (12 pieces)
8 ounces of cream cheese
1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup of Romano cheese
1/4 cup of sour cream
1 1/2 cups of artichoke hearts, chopped
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon of basil
3/4 cup spinach, chopped
1/2 teaspoon of red pepper (optional)

Directions:
Coat a 10 inch oven-safe skillet with olive oil. Place the Dinner Roll Dough Balls in a ring around the skillet. Cover and let rise until thawed and doubled in size.
Mix together remaining ingredients until well blended. Place mixture in the middle of the skillet with the thawed rolls.
Brush the rolls with olive oil and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Bake for 25 minutes at 375Â°.
