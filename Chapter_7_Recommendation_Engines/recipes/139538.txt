Title:
Easy Iced Cappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/862459.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
1 teaspoon SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
3 tablespoons warm water
6 fluid ounces cold milk

Directions:
In sealable jar, combine instant coffee, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and warm water. Cover the jar and shake until it is foamy. Pour into a glass full of ice. Fill the glass with milk. Adjust to taste if necessary.

Description: 
Here is a sugar-free shortcut to a frothy cappuccino-like drink.
