Title:
Taste of Morocco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 zucchini, cubed
1 sweet potato, cubed
1 clove garlic, minced
1 T. oil
1 (15 oz) can chick peas
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. each:  allspice, ginger, turmeric, paprika
1/4 tsp. each: salt, cayenne, cinnamon
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1 c. uncooked couscous
1/2 c. raisins

Directions:
Precook the cubed sweet potato in the microwave for a minute or two.
Saute the zucchini, sweet potato, and garlic in the oil until partially cooked, about 5 minutes.  Use water as necessary to keep the vegetables from sticking.  Drain and rinse the chick peas.  Add the spices, chick peas, and peppers to the pan.  Cover and cook for about 5 minutes.
Cook couscous according package directions, including raisins.  Serve veggie mixture over couscous.
