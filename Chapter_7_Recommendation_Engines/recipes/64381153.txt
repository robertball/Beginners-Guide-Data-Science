Title:
3 Cheese Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces elbow macaroni, cooked according to package
2 cups reduced-fat milk
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
4 oz asiago
4 oz romano
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
1. cook noodles according to package
2. Shred the cheeses
3.Add all the ingredients to the strained hot noodles, put back in the pot stirring until combined
