Title:
Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound chicken pieces
KFC Chicken Spices recipe
Wax paper
3 quarts peanut or veg. oil
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oil to 325 degrees.
prepare a baking sheet with rack.
In a shallow bowl lightly beat egg.
In another shallow bowl mix the KFC chicken spices recipe with flour.
Coat chicken pieces one at a time with the flour mixture then the egg mixture and then back into the flour mixture.  Place chicken pieces on wax paper let sit for 5 minutes before frying.
Place 2-3 pieces of chicken into oil and fry for 15 minutes.
Remove chicken from oil and place on rack in backing sheet to drain excess oil.
If you are cooking a large batch place into warm oven until ready to serve.
