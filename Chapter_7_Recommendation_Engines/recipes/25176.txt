Title:
Simple Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/9597.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup white vinegar
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
2 1/2 tablespoons prepared Dijon-style mustard
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a jar with a lid, combine lemon juice, vinegar, water, cheese, mustard, garlic powder and pepper.  Cover and shake well.  Refrigerate until ready to use.

Submitter:

Description: 
This flavorful vinaigrette takes the work out of Caesar salad dressing!
