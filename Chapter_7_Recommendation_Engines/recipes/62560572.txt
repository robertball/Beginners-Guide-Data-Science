Title:
Tuna Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536114.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6 ounce) cans water packed tuna, drained and flaked
3 egg whites
3 tablespoons seasoned dried bread crumbs
4 scallions, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons minced parsley
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the tuna, egg whites, bread crumbs, scallions/onions, parsley and
pepper. Shape the mixture into 4 equal patties.
Spray a large nonstick skillet with nonstick cooking spray and preheat it. Add the patties and
cook until golden brown and heated through (approximately 5 minutes on each side).

Submitter:

Description: 
Simple tuna cakes using canned tuna and seasoned bread crumbs. quick, easy and healthy.
