Title:
Mommy's Molasses Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. shortening
1 c. sugar
1/4 c. Brer Rabbit Molasses (gold label)
1 egg
2 t. baking soda
2 c. flour
1/2 t. cloves
1/2 t. ginger
1 t. cinnamon
1/2 t. salt

Directions:
Melt shortening in a 3-4 qt saucepan over low heat.  Remove from heat; let cool.
Add sugar, molasses, and egg; beat well.
Sift together flour, soda, cloves, ginger, cinnamon, and salt; add to first mixture and mix well.  Chill dough.
Form in 1 inch balls and roll in granulated sugar.
Place on cookie sheets lined with parchment paper.
Bake in moderate oven 350 - 375 for 8-10 minutes
