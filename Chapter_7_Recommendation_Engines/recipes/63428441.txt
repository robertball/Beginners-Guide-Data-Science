Title:
Cranberry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb chicken fryer
1 can cranberry sauce
1c French dressing
I pkg dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Layer fryer in 9x13 baking dish.
Mix other ingredients and pour over fryer.
Bake 350 degrees for 90 minutes. May have to cover toward the end of baking time.
