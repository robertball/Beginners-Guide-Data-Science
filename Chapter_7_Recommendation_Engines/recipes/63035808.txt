Title:
Chocolate Covered Cherry Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
equal parts
cherry vodka
Godiva chocolate liqueur
Dark cream de cacao
Rasberry liqueur (ie chambord)
Optional garnish - chocolate syrup and cherries

Directions:
Chill your glass with icewater while you mix the rest.
Place in shaker with ice.
Swirl chocolate syrup oon the side of the chilled glass.
pour in the strained alcohol mixture
garnish with cherry if desired
Enjoy
