Title:
Shrimp & Sausage & Chicken Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/320412.jpg
Ingredients:
½  cup flour
2 tbls vegetable oil
2 cups chopped onions
2 cups chopped green bell pepper
1 pound cooked shredded chicken
2 cups chicken broth
4 tsp Creole or Cajun seasoning
salt and pepper to taste
6 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tsp dried thyme
2 bay leaves
1 pound Italian sausage
1 ½ cups spicy V8 juice
44 oz diced tomatoes in juice
1 pound shrimp, no tails

Directions:
Sprinkle flour over bottom of a heavy pot. Stir flour continuously over medium-low heat until the flour turns golden brown (NOT BURNED) for about 15 minutes. Pour browned flour into bowl and set aside.
Heat oil in the same pot over medium heat. Add onion & bell pepper and sauté for about 7 minutes.
Add garlic, thyme, and bay leaf. Stir for 1 minute. Add sausage and sauté until brown, breaking up with the back of a spoon. Add cooked chicken, browned flour, tomatoes w/juices, V8, chicken broth, & Creole seasoning.
Bring to a boil then reduce heat. Cover and simmer on low for 20 to 30 minutes stirring often.
Add shrimp to pot and simmer just until the shrimp has turned pink.
Locate and discard both bay leaves.
Season with salt & pepper as desired then serve.

This hearty Indiana Gumbo is great anytime of year but especially when it's cold outside and you and your friends are huddled around the tube watching the game.
