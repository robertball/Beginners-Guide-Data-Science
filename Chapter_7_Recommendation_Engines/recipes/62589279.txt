Title:
Curry Chicken and Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs. boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 lb. cookked, devained shrimp ( optional)
2 cups broccoli
1 medium onion
1-2 jalapanos ( optional)
1 red bell pepper
4-6 cloves garlic
1 can of coconut milk
1 packet of curry paste ( start with half then add more to taste)
salt, pepper,crushed red pepper, chilli powder and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Cut chicken into bite sized chunks sautee in EVOO over medium heat with all the vegtables except broccoli and all the seasoning.
When chicken is fully cooked add the coconut milk and curry paste
Add broccoli and shrimp
Cover and simmer on medium- low heat untill the coconut milk reduces down to a thick sauce.
Serve over white or Jasmin rice.
