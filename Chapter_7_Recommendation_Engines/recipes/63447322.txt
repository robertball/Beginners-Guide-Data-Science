Title:
Ice Cream Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c Rice Chex cereal, crushed
1 c butter, melted
1 c brown sugar
1/2 gallon vanilla ice-cream, softened
1 can cherry pie filling

Directions:
In large bowl, combine butter and brown sugar.
Add cereal, and mix thoroughly.
Pat 1/2 cereal mixture in 9x13 pan.
Spread ice cream in pan.
Top with remaining cereal mixture and pat down.
Freeze.
Serve with dollop of pie filling
