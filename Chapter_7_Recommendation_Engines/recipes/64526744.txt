Title:
Big Ray's Black Bean 'n' Squash Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2998136.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium sweet yellow pepper, chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 cans (15 ounces each) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 ounces) solid-pack pumpkin
1 can (14-1/2 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
3 cups chicken broth
1 cup chopped sweet potatoes
1 cup chopped butternut squash
2 lb ground turkey
2 tsp dried parsley flakes
2 tsp chili powder
1-1/2 tsp ground cumin
1-1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt thinly sliced green onions, optional

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Brown ground turkey until no longer pink. Add onion and pepper; cook and stir until tender. Add garlic; cook 1 minute longer.
Transfer to a 5-qt. slow cooker; stir in the remaining ingredients. Cook, covered, on low 4-5 hours. If desired, top with green onions.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This slow-cooked recipe is especially good on cold days were you can set it and forget it until mealtime. It's a wonderful variation on standard chili that freezes well and tastes even better as leftovers.
