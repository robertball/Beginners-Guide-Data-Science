Title:
Dirt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large, family sized boxes of instant vanilla pudding mix
5 cups of whole milk
2 eight ounce packages of cream cheese, very well softened
1 eight ounce tub of Cool Whip
1 large, family sized package of Oreo cookies, or two smaller sized packages

Directions:
Mix the pudding mix Witt the milk until smooth and thick. In a separate bowl, with an electric mixer, blend the softened cream cheese well to eliminate lumps. Mix the cream cheese and the pudding together. Fold in Cool Whip. Add crushed Oreo cookies, reserving some for sprinkling on the top if desired.

Easy dish that is always a huge hit at potlucks!
