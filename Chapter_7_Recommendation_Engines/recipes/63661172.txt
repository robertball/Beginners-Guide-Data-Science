Title:
Cool Crabmeat Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg. Imitation Crabmeat
2 stalks celery chopped fine
1 apple chopped fine
1 cucumber, peeled and chopped fine
1/4 Italian dressing
mayonnaise

Directions:
Shred imitation crabmeat with two forks until the pieces are the size you prefer.
Add chopped celery, apple, cucumber, Italian dressing and enough mayonnaise to moisten all ingredients.
Serve on lettuce, in a sandwich, in a pita pocket or on a tortilla for a roll-up.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cari Wensley
Description: 
The perfect recipe for a summer lunch or dinner, when you want to have something cool and refreshing, without being heavy. My family loves it when I serve this and it doesn't require any exotic ingredients.
