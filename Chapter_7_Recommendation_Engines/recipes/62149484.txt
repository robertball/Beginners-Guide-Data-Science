Title:
Southern Cabbage Patch Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs. cooking oil
2 lbs. ground beef or turkey
2 med onions chopped
1 small cabbage head chopped into bite-sized pieces
2 stalks of celery chopped
1 16oz canned tomatoes (I use 1 qt. of my home canned tomatoes)
2 Tbs. sugar
4 cups of broth or water (more if needed)
2 15oz cans Kidney beans
2 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. ground cummin
1/4 tsp ground red pepper
1 tsp. garlic powder
Black pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
Brown meat in large pot with oil and drain most of the fat.
Add onions and celery and saute for 5 minutes over medium heat.
Add tomatoes, water or broth, beans, chili powder, red pepper, cummin, garlic powder, and sugar.
Bring to a boil and simmer for about 30 minutes on medium heat.
Add cabbage and cook 10 minutes or until cabbage is tender.
Add salt & pepper to taste while soup is cooking.

Submitter: 
Shelby Snider
Description: 
Even if you don't like cooked cabbage, you'll love this soup! Quick and easy, great with southern cornbread.
