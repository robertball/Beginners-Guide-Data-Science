Title:
Spicy Lime avocado soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinned/washed chicken breasts
3 large just ripe avocados
2 jalapenos,split and sliced very thin
1 cup cilantro, split into 1/2 cups
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 large white onion, chopped
juice of 3 limes
2 tbsp powdered reduced sodium chicken boullion
4 cups water
olive oil
queso fresco or monty jack cheese
sour cream or creme fraiche (optional)

Directions:
Remove as much fat from chicken breasts as possible. Boil in water over med-high heat for 7 minutes until cooked through.
Meanwhile, sautee garlic, 1/2 of chopped onion, jalapeno, cilantro and lime juice in olive oil until onion starts to turn slight greenish color. Blend boullion with water and pour over lime/onion mixture.  Let cook until just heated through.
Remove chicken from boiling pot, run cool water over chicken to speed cooling process. Shred or finely slice chicken breasts, add to soup pot.
Seed and peel avocado, chop into chunks and set aside.
Ladel soup into four bowls.  Add additional fresh chopped cilantro,the fresh onion and avocado to bowls. Top off with mozzarella or monty jack cheese.
Serve with warm tortilla chips and a nice large mojito.
