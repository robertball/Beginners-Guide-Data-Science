Title:
Thrive - Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs Olive or Coconut oil
1.5 lbs Ground Turkey
1 medium Onion, chopped
3 cloves Garlic, minced
1 medium Red Bell Pepper, chopped
1 (16 oz) can Pumpkin Puree
1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes
3 Tbs Apple Cider Vinegar
1 Tbs Tomato Paste
1 tsp ground Cumin
1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1 tsp Paprika
1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
1 or 2 cans Tomato Sauce (optional)

Directions:
Heat the oil in Dutch Oven over medium-high heat.  Add turkey and cook through.  Discard liquid.
Add remaining ingredients.  Stir thoroughly and bring to a boil.
Optional:  Add tomato sauce to provide more soupiness/more liquid.
Lower heat and allow to simmer for about 1 hour.
Serve hot - optionally add cheese topping.

Submitter: 
Cyndi Martin McAlpine
Description: 
Excellent chili without using chili powder! The pumpkin puree gives a wonderful smooth taste to this chili!
