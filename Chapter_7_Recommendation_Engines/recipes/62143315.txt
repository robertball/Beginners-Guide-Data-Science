Title:
Grilled Shrimp Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds Roma tomatoes cut up,
20 medium fresh basil leaves
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
Salt
2 tablespoons butter or olive oil
1/2 cup white wine
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
1 pound angel hair pasta
2 cups  Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Combine shrimp and basil pieces, extra-virgin olive oil, minced garlic, Italian seasoning and salt into a large bowl and blend thoroughly.
Cover, set aside and leave shrimp to marinate for at least 1 hour.
Then, heat a large, nonstick skillet over medium heat.
Add butter or olive oil and let melt.
Stir in white wine and bring to a boil.
Add heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese and bring to a simmer.
Let sauce reduce to desired consistency.
Add cooked drained pasta and tomatoes and mozzarella cheese to skillet.
Stir to thoroughly coat pasta with sauce.
Grill or saute shrimp until internal temperature reaches 150°F [65.5°C] and set aside.
Top pasta with cooked shrimp, and serve.

Submitter: 
Gretchen Huyck Paulson
Description: 
Grilled shrimp over angel hair pasta with melted mozzarella, basil and tomatoes in a garlic butter sauce.
