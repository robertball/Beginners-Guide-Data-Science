Title:
Cream Cheese Filled Criossants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz Cream Cheese
1 pkg of 8 Crescent Rolls
1/4 c Vanilla Flavored Yogurt
1/6th cup white sugar
3 large strawberries cut up

Directions:
Mix in a bowl, Cream Cheese, Yogurt, Sugar and Strawberries.  Blend well until real creamy.  Take the Crescent rolls out of the package roll out.  You can do this in triangles or rectangles.  Place the filling on the crescent roll and roll up.  Bake at 375 for 13 to 15 mins.(depending on your oven and how it cooks)

Description: 
My daughter wanted something cream cheese filled for breakfast and just threw it together.
