Title:
Guinness Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground chuck
1 1/2 cups chopped onions
1 serano chili pepper, seeds removed, chopped
1 jalapeno, seeds removed,chopped
1/4 cup flour
1 14.9 oz. can of Guinness beer
1 can of diced tomatoes
1 small can of tomato paste
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp white pepper
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (or to taste)
1 tsp cumin
1/4 cup chili powder
1 can red kidney bean undrained
1 can pinto beans, undrained
1-2 cups beef broth, to taste
2 Tbsp butter
Cheddar Cheese, chopped green onions, crackers, sour cream, crushed tortilla chips, etc. for toppings.

Directions:
Brown ground chuck in a dutch oven or large pot. Drain off most of the fat. Add onions & peppers and cook until translucent.
Add flour and stir constantly for one minute. Add Guinness and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and stir in tomatoes & tomato paste. Add salt, spices, & beans. Add beef broth to your desired consistency (add more if you like your chili thin, less for thick chili).
Simmer for at least 30 minutes or move to a slow cooker & cook on low for at least 2 hours.
Serve with desired toppings.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
An adaptation of a classic chili recipe using Guinness beer (or any other dark stout). Goes great with Beer Bread or Cornbread for dinner on a cold night.
