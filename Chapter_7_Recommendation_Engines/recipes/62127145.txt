Title:
Coconut Curry Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups basmati rice
1 cup cubed pineapple
1 red pepper, julienned
1 carrot, peeled and julienned
1 cup chopped apple, peeled
1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup golden raisins
1/2 lb cooked shrimp or prawns
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Dressing:
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 cups light coconut milk
2 tbsp curry powder
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp chili powder
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 clove garlic, minced
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 tsp oregano
chopped cilantro to garnish

Directions:
Cook the basmati rice according to package directions. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-low heat. Add the onion, pepper, carrot, pineapple, apple, raisins and shrimp. Cook until onions are tender, about 3-5 minutes.
In a separate bowl, whisk together the oil, coconut milk, curry powder, chili powder,cumin, lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper and oregano. Blend well. Pour about one quarter of the dressing over the mixture in the skillet. Cook until heated through, about 3-4 minutes.
In a different bowl, add the basmati rice. Once the skillet mixture is heated through, add it to the rice. Stir well and serve into plates. Over top of each serving, evenly garnish with the remaining dressing and add a pinch of chopped cilantro over top.
