Title:
Apricot-Mustard Grilled Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) pork tenderloin
6 tablespoons apricot preserves
1/4 cup mustard
1 tablespoon honey
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Season tenderloin with salt and pepper. Stir together the preserves,mustard honey and cayenne pepper in a small bowl. Place pork over a medium-hot fire and grill for about 25 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 150 degrees F., brushing with mustard mixture in the last few minutes.
