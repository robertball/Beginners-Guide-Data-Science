Title:
Boston Coolers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
Strong ginger ale (or ginger beer works best)

Directions:
Put two scoops of ice cream in a tall glass and pour enough ginger ale (or ginger beer) over it to cover.
Stick a straw in it and enjoy!

Submitter: 
The Magpie
Description: 
A favorite in our family for generations -- our classic summer cool-off!
