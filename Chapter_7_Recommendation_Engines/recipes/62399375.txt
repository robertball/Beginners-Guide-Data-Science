Title:
Chewy Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/481162.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup of soft butter
2 cups of sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups of unsifted flour
3/4 cup of cocoa
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1 cup of chocolate chips

Directions:
1. set oven to 350F
2. Cream butter and sugar; add eggs and vanilla- blend well.
3.in a small bowl mix together flour, cocoa baking soda and salt. blend into creamy mixture.
4.put in chocolate chips.
5.drop by teaspoons onto a slightly greased cookie sheet. bake for 8-9  minutes
6. let set for one minute

Submitter: 
girl's best friend
Description: 
I made these cookies for a lemonade stand, and they were a big hit. By the time we were done, there were only 5 cookies left.
