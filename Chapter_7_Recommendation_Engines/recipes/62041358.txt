Title:
PIRATE PUNCH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cans (46 oz. each) of pineapple juice, 1 fifth of good coconut rum, med. can of pineapple chunks - undrained, 3 cans of mandarin oranges - drained, or 3 fresh oranges - supremed, 3 lemons - sliced, lg. jar marachino cherries - drained and rinsed, 1 14 oz can of pink grapefruit - drained, if desired 2 (14 oz.) cans of (drained) mixed tropical fruit can be added.

Directions:
Place all fruits in bottom of non-plastic container large enough to hold all ingredients. Cover with rum. SLIGHTLY mash fruits to incorporate rum. Add rest of rum. Let sit at least 2 hours - the longer the better - up to 2 weeks. Add the 2 cans of pineapple juice, stir well. Put in freezer till very cold. Serve with spoon to eat the fruit after you finish the drink. Very mild, but can sneak up on you!!

Submitter: 
RILEYBAMA
Description: 
A fantastic punch - light- refreshing - wonderful on a hot Southern summer night!!! It has a slight pina colada flavor. The fruit is as good as the punch!
