Title:
Hawaiian Bread II* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
1 egg
1/2 cup crushed pineapple with juice
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup Splenda
1/4 cup Benecol
2 cups bread flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast

Directions:
Put in bread machine in order given and set to "dough" cycle. Remove from machine and put in a greased 5x9 loaf pan. Cover with greased waxed paper & a clean cloth. Let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until bottom of a loaf sounds hollow when tapped.
