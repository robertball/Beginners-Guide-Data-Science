Title:
Split Peas Soup w Cottage Roll Stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Stock from Cottage Roll
1-1/2 cups yellow split peas
2 stalks celery, chopped
3 carrots chopped, or 1/2 bag mini carrots
1 medium onion, chopped
1 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp salt
water
* 1 tsp chicken bullion * if needed
* diced cottage roll * optional

Directions:
Skin off fat from cottage roll stock and add to slow cooker.
Add to pot split peas, celery, carrots, onion, thyme, and pepper.
(if you feel your stock is not flavorful enough add the chicken bouillon)
Add water to leave 3 inches from top of slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low 10 - 12 hrs or alternatively on high for 6 hours and then:
Puree soup and either add back to slow cooker (will be a little thin
or
Add to pot on stove and simmer on medium/high for 30-45 minutes to thicken the soup
Add salt to taste.
20 minutes before serving add diced cottage roll to soup if you want to.
Enjoy with crusty bread

Submitter: 
Camille Watson
Description: 
This soup is flavoured with the water left over from a cottage roll made the night before
