Title:
Cream cheese alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz pkg softened cream cheese (diced)
1 1/2 cups 1/2 and 1/2
1 cup fresh grated parmasian cheese
1 tablespoon coarse ground garlic
1 lb pkg. fettichini noodles

Directions:
Put a  sauce pan over low to medium heat, add cream cheese, ,1/2 & 1/2 and garlic. Stir until cream cheese melts completly add 3/4 cup Parm Cheese and stir until melted. will be thin at 1st but will thicken as it sits.
Serve over noodles and top with  with remaining cheese.
You can add cooked chicken, shrip, crab or even artifical crab meat to sauce.

Submitter: 
Val-Flowers
Description: 
A creamy garlic alfredo that you can add chicken,shrimp or crab .An easy to prepare meal that looks like it took a longtime.
