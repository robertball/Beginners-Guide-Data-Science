Title:
Jean Ann's Corn and Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. frozen whole corn, thawed
15 oz. can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 jalepeno pepper, seeded and chopped
1 large tomato, seeded and chopped
1/2 C. sweet onion, chopped
1/2 C. sliced green onions
1 clove garlic, minced
DRESSING
3/4 C. Prepared Good Seasons Italian Dressing
3/4 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1 T. lime juice
2 T. cilantro, chopped

Directions:
In a serving bowl, mix all of the ingredients, except the dressing. Next, put the dressing ingredients together into a jar with a tight lid.  Shake vigorously and pour over the vegetables.  Stir and chill, covered, for at least six hours or overnight.  Serve with your favorite chips.
