Title:
Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs round steak cut into strips
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup oil
1 lb can whole tomatoes
1 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup onion chopped
1 clove garlic minced
1 1/2 tsp worchestershire sauce
2 large green bell peppers cut into strips

Directions:
coat meat with flour, s/p.  fry in oil.  add next 4 ingred, reserving tomatoes and adding juice.  simmmer 1 hour covered. stir in worchestershire sauce, pepper strips, and tomato chopped.  simmer 5 mins
spice with tobasco sauce
can serve with rice
