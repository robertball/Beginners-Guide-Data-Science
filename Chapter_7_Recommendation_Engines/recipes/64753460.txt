Title:
Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean grnd beef
1/3 cup (or less) chopped onion
1/2 cup long grain rice
1 tsp beef bouillon
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 1/2 cups tomato juice, divided
1 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp brown sugar

Directions:
Put first 6 ingredients into bowl.
Add ~ 1/2 cup tomato juice to moisten, but still manageable to make meatballs.
Shape into 1 1/2" meatballs (or smaller).
Arrange in baking dish or roaster.
Stir remaining tomato juice, soy sauce and sugar together. Pour over meatballs.
Cover. Bake @ 350 x 60 - 70 min. until rice is tender.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These fun meatballs have rice 'quills'! Baked and delicious
