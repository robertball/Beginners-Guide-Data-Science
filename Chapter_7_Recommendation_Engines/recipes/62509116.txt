Title:
Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter slightly softened (NOT melted!)
1 cup white granulated sugar
1 cup light brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
12 oz chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine softened butter and both sugars in a large mixing bowl. Add eggs and vanilla. In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Add to the butter mixture. Add chocolate chips. Spray a cookie sheet with cooking spray for the first batch. Form dough into approx. one-inch balls and drop onto cookie sheet. Bake at 375 degrees for 8 minutes.
For a double batch, double all the ingredients except for the salt.
