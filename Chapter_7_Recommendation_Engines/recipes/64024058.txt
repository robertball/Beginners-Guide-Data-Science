Title:
Stovetop Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups dried elbow macaroni (or pasta of choice)
2 TBS butter
2 TBS flour
1 cup milk
6 oz cheese, cheddar
3 oz greek yogurt, plain

Directions:
1. Cook pasta as directed on package
2. Make roux with flour and butter, whisk in milk. Stir until milk thickened and bubbly. Cook 1 minute more.
3. Remove pan from heat and add cheese. Stir until smooth. Stir in yogurt. Mix sauce and pasta
