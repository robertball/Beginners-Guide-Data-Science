Title:
Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-15 oz can of garbanzo beans, with some liquid
1/4 cup tahini
juice of 3/4 of a lemon (about 1/2 cup)
1 teaspoon salt
4 large garlic cloves, pressed
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/8 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Pour off some of the liquid from the garbanzo beans.
Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth. If necessary, add more bean liquid until desired consistency.
Transfer mixture to a serving bowl.
