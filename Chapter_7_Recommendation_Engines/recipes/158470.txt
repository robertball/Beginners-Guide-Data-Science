Title:
Champion Cherry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine, softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 (12 ounce) package vanilla milk chips
2 cups dried tart cherries
1 cup coarsely chopped pecans
Nonstick spray coating

Directions:
Combine margarine, granulated sugar, and brown sugar in a large mixing bowl. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add eggs and vanilla.
Combine flour and baking powder; gradually add to mixture. Stir in vanilla milk chips, dried cherries and pecans. Spread dough evenly in greased 15x10x1-inch baking pan.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree F oven 20 minutes, or until light golden brown.
Do not over bake. Cool in pan, and then cut into bars. Store in airtight container. These bars freeze well.

Submitter: 
The Cherry Marketing Institute
Description: 
Prepare and freeze these extraordinary bars in advance of the party.
