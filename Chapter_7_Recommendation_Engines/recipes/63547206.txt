Title:
Dani's Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb meat (meat loaf mixture)
1 cup grated cheddar and gruyere
4 scallions thinly sliced
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1 tbs garlic powder
Course salt and ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix all ingredients with your hands, but save some cheese for the top.  Place in a non-greased pan.  Cook for approx. 40 minutes, add cheese on top and cook 20 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Erin Douglas
Description: 
My best pal made this for me once and I had to write down this recipe.. This is a carb free meat loaf that is sooooo good!
