Title:
Crawfish Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7758326.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 sticks butter
2 cups chopped yellow onions
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
2 tablespoons minced garlic
2-3 tsp salt
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp black pepper
1 pinch white pepper
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 16oz can diced tomatoes, drained
2 pounds peeled crawfish tails
2 tbsp chopped parsley
Up to 1 cup vegetable stock (or chicken stock)
2 9 inch prepared deep-dish pie crusts

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet or dutch oven over medium heat. Stir in the onion, peppers, celery, garlic, salt, cayenne, black and white pepper. Cook, stirring, until the vegetables are tender and the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add the flour and cook, stirring constantly for another 5 minutes.
Stir in the tomatoes and cook for another 5 minutes.
and crawfish tails and parsley. Reduce heat to medium-low, and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and allow to stand for 20 to 30 minutes to finish thickening.
Split mixture between the two pie crusts. Bake in the oven at 400 ยบ F. for 35 minutes or until the edges are brown and the filling is bubbly.
Remove the pies from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes to cool slightly.
