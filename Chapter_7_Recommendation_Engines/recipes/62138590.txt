Title:
Hot Pumpkin Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cups water
1/3 C cream of wheat or malt-o-meal
1/2 to 3/4 cups pumpkin, depending on your preference
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
2 Splenda packets or 4 teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Mix the cereal and water in a microwave-safe bowl.
Heat on high power 3 minutes, stirring after every minute.
When cereal is hot and thickened, mix in the remaining ingredients.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
BCORTE
Description: 
I was looking for a way to use up a little bit of canned pumpkin I had leftover from another recipe.  I love Cream of Wheat and Malt-O-Meal, so I thought I'd find a way to combine them!
