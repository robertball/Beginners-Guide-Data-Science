Title:
Caramel Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
¾ cup white sugar
1 small pkg butterscotch cook and serve pudding
1 pkg frozen rolls – yeast rolls that will rise
¾ c chopped pecans
½ c brown sugar

Directions:
Butter a large bundt pan
Literally dump in the sugar and the butterscotch pudding
Place the rolls around the pan
Cover with pecans and brown sugar
Drip the melted butter over top
Place the Bundt Pan in a cold oven and let it sit overnight
In the morning, turn the oven to 350 and back for 25 to 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an easy and delicious dessert you can make in minutes and serve in a variety of meal situations.
