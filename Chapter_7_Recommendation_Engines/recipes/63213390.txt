Title:
Orzo with Zucchini, Dill, and Feta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for grill
3 medium zucchini, halved lengthwise
1 large shallot, thinly sliced
1 pound orzo
1 tablespoon lemon zest
1/2 cup lemon juice (about three lemons)
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1/3 pound feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
1. Preheat the grill or grill pan to medium and oil liberally. Season zucchini with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Grill until tender with golden-brown marks, about 5 minutes per side. Transfer zucchini to cutting board and slice into 1/2 inch pieces. Place in a a large bowl and sprinkle with shallot. Cover to keep warm and set aside.
2. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook orzo following package instructions. Drain and add to reserved zucchini. Add olive oil, lemon zest and juice, and dill; toss to combine. Top with feta and gently toss. Season with salt and pepper.
