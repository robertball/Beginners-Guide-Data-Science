Title:
Oven Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Peaces Chicken
2 Cups Butter Milk or Sour Reg Milk with 2 tsp Vinegar.
2 Cups Flour
1 1/2 Tbs Paperika
1 1/2 Tbs Season Salt
1/2   Tbs Black Pepper
1/2   Tbs Garlic

Directions:
Mix Dry ingredents
Drench Chicken in Milk then in Dry ingredents Pan Fry to Sear Chicken
Place on oven proof pan cook 1 hour or till juices run clear.
