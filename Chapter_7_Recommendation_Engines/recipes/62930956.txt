Title:
Chicken Enchilada Slow Cooker Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained (may use 1 3/4 c. frozen whole kernel corn)
1 can original Rotel chopped tomatoes and chilies
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 (10 ounce) can enchilada sauce
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 white onion, chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 bay leaves
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon Penzey's bold taco seasoning
1 zucchini, quartered lengthwise and sliced
1 yellow squash, quartered and sliced

Directions:
Rinse and pat dry the chicken breasts, then place into the bottom of a slow cooker. Add the corn, Rotel, chicken broth, enchilada sauce, green chiles, onion, bay leaves, garlic, cumin, chili powder, and Penzey's bold taco seasoning.
Cook on Low for 3 hours. Add zucchini, yellow squash and cilantro.  Continue cooking on low for another 3 hours.  Transfer the chicken to a large plate, then shred the meat with two forks. Return the chicken to the slow cooker and continue cooking for 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Bibi
Description: 
Chicken breasts cook up tender and flavorful when slow-cooked in this enchilada-inspired soup. Toss all the ingredients into your slow cooker and let it simmer all day long while you're at work. The scent of dinner will come wafting through before you even get through the door!Modified from: Chicken Enchilada Slow Cooker Soup
