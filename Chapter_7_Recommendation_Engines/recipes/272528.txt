Title:
Pandan Coconut Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2 pandan leaves
2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
1 cinnamon stick, broken in half
2 coconuts, grated
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Combine water and pandan leaves together in a blender; blend until smooth. Strain through a piece of cheesecloth and set aside juice.
Heat ghee over medium-low heat in a saucepan. Add cinnamon. Cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add grated coconut, sweetened condensed milk, and pandan juice. Cook and stir until thoroughly combined and coconut sticks together, about 25 minutes.
Place mixture in a round or rectangular pan. Let cool for 10 minutes. Cut into pieces before transferring to a refrigerator. Serve chilled.
