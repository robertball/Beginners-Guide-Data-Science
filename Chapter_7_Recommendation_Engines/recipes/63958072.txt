Title:
Meemaws Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 lb thinly sliced sirloin cut into 1 inch strips
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup cooking oil
3/4 cup chopped onion
3 cloves pressed garlic
1 and 1/2 to 2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 cup dry red wine
2 cups Daisey Sour Cream (Kosher cooks can substitute Kosher soy or Kosher almond yogurt)
1 tsp. Ground nutmeg
1 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp. Dill
1 lb. Egg Noodles or "no yolk egg noodles"

Directions:
1) Flour meat (put flour in large baggie add beef seal and shake until meat is coated)
2) Heat oil in large non-stick skillet add meat and brown, stirring frequently
3) Add onions, mushrooms, and garlic and continue browning until onion is tender (about 2 minutes)
4) Stir in red wine and nutmeg, simmer to release browning on pan (deglaze the pan)
5) Stir in 1/2 of the sour cream and reduce heat as low as possible while noodles cook.
6) Prepare noodles according to package directions
7) When noodles are drained stir in remaining sour cream.
Serve mixture atop noodles. Garnish with a sprinkle of dill.

Submitter: 
AnniecatMT
Description: 
This Stroganoff requires moderate cooking skill, but is a family favorite for cold winter nights. The combination of flavors are unique.
