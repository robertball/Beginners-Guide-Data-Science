Title:
Simple Greek Yogurt Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1127556.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon brown sugar
8 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped green onion
2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
2 tablespoons yellow mustard
1/4 teaspoon dill weed
1/4 teaspoon paprika
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk yogurt and brown sugar together in a bowl. Add eggs, celery, green onion, relish, mustard, dill, paprika, and black pepper; mix well.

Submitter: 
CookingFreak
Description: 
Greek yogurt gives this dish a healthy kick, but the brown sugar makes sure no one even knows it's in there! Very kid-friendly.
