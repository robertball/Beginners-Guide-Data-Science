Title:
Baked Oatmeal II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups rolled oats
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 cup apple sauce
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3/4 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together oats, sugars, spices, baking powder, and salt. Beat in milk, eggs, melted butter, apple sauce and vanilla extract. Stir in raisins and pecans. Spread into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
