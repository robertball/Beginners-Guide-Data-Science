Title:
Summer Seafood - Greek Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb salmon fillets
4 medium vine ripened tomatoes
3 oz feta cheese
1/4 C balsamic vinegar
salt/pepper to taste
2 T lemon juice
1 bag of salad (of choice)

Directions:
Preheat grill for medium heat
Dice tomatoes and combine with feta and balsamic vinegar, refrigerate
Place salmon in cooking spray lined foil.
Pour lemon juice, salt and pepper on fish
Make packet and grill for 25 minutes or until fish flakes easily.
Serve tomatoes/feta/balsamic dressing over salmon on top of salad

Submitter: 
MEGSCOOKIN
Description: 
A light topping to this hearty fish makes a great summer dinner!
