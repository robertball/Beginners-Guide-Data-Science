Title:
Chocolate Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Baked Pie Crust
3/4 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup milk
8 oz. bittersweet chocolate
1 egg, lightly beaten

Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring cream and milk to a boil.
Place chocolate in a bowl and whisk in hot milk mixture.
Let cool slightly then whisk in egg.
Pour into tart shell and bake about 10 minutes.
Let cool then chill.
Bring to room temperature about 1 hour before serving.
