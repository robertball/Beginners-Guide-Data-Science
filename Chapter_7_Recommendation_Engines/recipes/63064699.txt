Title:
Sweet and Sour Fish Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773845.jpg
Ingredients:
Fish:
4 tablespoons pineapple or orange juice
1 lb cod fillets, cut into 2 inch chuncks
Sea salt and ground white pepper
Sauce:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon peeled and grated ginger
1 red pepper, cut into 1/2 inch chunks
1 yellow onion, cut into 1/2 inch chunks
One 8 ounce can pineapple chunks in juice
2 tablespoons rice vinegar
2 tablespoons light soy sauce
1 teaspoon brown sugar
1 heaping tablespoon cornstarch
1 scallion, sliced for garnish
Serving suggestion: steamed rice
Special equipment: bamboo steamer basket

Directions:
Bring wok filled halfway with water to a boil & reduce to a simmer.  Divide the fish between 2 plates that will fit into the basket layers of a bamboo steamer.  Sprinkle fish with sea salt & ground white pepper.  Drizzle 1-2 tbsp pineapple juice over each plate of fish. Steam fish until flesh turns white & pulls apart easily, 7-8 minutes. Keep fish covered.
Sauce: when the wok is hot on high heat, add the oil.  Add the garlic and ginger and cook, 1min.  Add red peppers & onions cook til tender. Add pineapple chunks & juice.  Add vinegar, soy sauce & brown sugar.  Cook til sauce thickens 1-2 min.

Submitter: 
Hawn Easterling
Description: 
Easy sweet and sour dish which can be used for all meats, not just fish.  Recipe courtesy of Ching-He Huang, 2010.
