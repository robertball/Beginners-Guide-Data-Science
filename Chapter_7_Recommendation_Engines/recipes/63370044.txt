Title:
Breakfast Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123075.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. sausage
1 pkg. refrigerator crescent rolls
1 c. frozen hash browns, thawed
1 c. sharp cheddar cheese, grated
5 eggs
1/4 c. milk
salt and pepper to taste
2 T. Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Fry sausage until done.
Separate crescent rolls into triangles on ungreased 12-inch pizza pan; points to the center. Seal perforations; bring dough up the sides.
Sprinkle on sausage, potatoes and cheddar cheese.
Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper; pour over everything.
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake at 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
