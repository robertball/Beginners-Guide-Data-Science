Title:
Best Buttermilk Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
3 cups sugar
6 eggs separated
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 cup buttermilk
2 tsp lemon extract
a capful almond extract

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.
Add individually each egg yolk until all are mixed in.
Add flour alternately with buttermilk.
Beat eggwhites until stiff and fold into cake batter.
Bake at 350 F for 1 hr. and 15 min.
