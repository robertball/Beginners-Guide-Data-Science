Title:
"Blasted" Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pounds broccoli crowns, cut into florets (about 8 cups)
3 tbsp. olive oil
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 garlic cloves, minced
Large pinch of dried crushed red pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°F.
Place broccoli in a large baking sheet.
Drizzle with the olive oil and then sprinkle with a large pinch of salt and a few grinds of pepper.  Roast 15 minutes.
Remove sheet from oven and sprinkle with the garlic and red pepper flakes.
Roast until broccoli is beginning to brown, about 5-8 minutes longer.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Serve immediately.
