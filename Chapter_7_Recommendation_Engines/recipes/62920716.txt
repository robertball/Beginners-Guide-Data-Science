Title:
White Chocolate Cranberry Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/4 cup + 2 tblspn Splenda / sugar blend
3 tbsp baking powder
1 tspn cinammon
1/2 tspn ginger
1/4 tspn salt
1 cup pumpkin
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Spray muffin pan with fat-free cooking spray or ine with paper baking cups.
Combine flour, splenda blend, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger, and salt in a large bowl.
Add pumpkin, applesauce, yogurt, cranberries, and white chocolate chips.  Whisk just until moistened.
Fill muffin cups 3/4 full.
Bake for 17 - 20 min until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
