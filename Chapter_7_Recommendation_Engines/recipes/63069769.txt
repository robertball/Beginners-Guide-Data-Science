Title:
Bussels Sprout Bacon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773730.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Brussels Sprouts (about 20)
4 slices of Bacon
2 tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar
4 tablespoons Blue Cheese
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper
Housemade Croutons
- 2 cups cubed bread of choice
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- galic powder to taste
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cook bacon in skillet until crisp. Remove bacon onto paper towels reserving 1 tablespoon of the bacon grease in the pan.
Cube a few slices of your chosen cread for croutons. toss with olive oil, salt, pepper and bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes or until crunchy.
Cut bottoms off of the Brussels sprouts and discard. Cut each Brussels sprout in half and then cut each half into shreds (approximately 5 times each half). Toss shredded Brussels into olive oil to coat lightly and salt and pepper. Pour brussels onto baking sheet lined with parchment paper and roast on 350 until wilted, 10-15 minmutes.
Meanwhile, crumble blue cheee.
Remove Brussels from oven and immmediately toss in pan with bacon grease, blue cheese, red wine vinegar and croutons. Distribute to serving bowls and top with crunchy bacon.
Makes 4 small servings.
