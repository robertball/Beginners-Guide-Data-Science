Title:
Blood Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2060461.jpg
Ingredients:
BOUDIN - (BLOOD SAUSAGE)
10 oz beef blood (Asian Mkt, freezer section)
10 oz fresh whole milk
1/4 c. chopped onions fine (food chopper)
1/2 c. salt pork fat back (white only) chopped
3 tbs flour (mixed, diluted with milk)
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp. cumin
Salt to taste

Directions:
1)Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
2)Simmer chopped onions to translucent
3)In a sauce pan bring blood and milk to a simmer
4)Add onions and salt pork - stir constantly
5)Add all spice and salt to taste (1 to 2 ts)
6)Simmer & stir, thicken with flour and milk mixture (substitute with roux or corn starch and water)
7)Transfer to a 9 X 9 casserole dish. Place in another larger dish and use as a double boiler. Bring water to the same height as the mixture.
8)Bake in oven for 45 minutes. Check with cake tester 'till it comes out clean or top to be firm like a custard as it starts to pull from the edges.
9)Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
10)Cut into 2" X 2" squares
11)Serve with cream sauce with potatoes and sliced hard boiled eggs, roasted brussel sprouts w/ garlic.

Submitter: 
Conrad Crepeau
Description: 
Normally store bought as Blood Sausage in a casing. You can enjoy the same or or better Boudin as a pudding - homemade. The sausage can sometimes be dry. Not so with this recipe. Always moist and delish! Enjoy some French Canadian cuisine.
