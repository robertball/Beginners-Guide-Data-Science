Title:
Buttermilk Whipped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 2 inch chunks
1/2 cup buttermilk, warmed
2 Tbsp. butter or margarine, softened
1 tsp. salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes in a large pot, for 15 minutes, or until they are pierced easily with a fork.
Drain potatoes and put in a large mixer bowl.
Beat potatoes with an electric mixer on low, gradually adding buttermilk, butter, salt, and pepper.
Increase mixing speed to medium once ingredients are blended in, and mix for 1 minute.
Mix on high for 2 to 3 minutes.
