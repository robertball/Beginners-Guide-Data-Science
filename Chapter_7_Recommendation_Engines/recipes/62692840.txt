Title:
Colcannon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound potatoes
1 pound (4 cup)  green cabbage grated
1 large onion chopped (1 cup)
½ cup skim milk or sour cream (IMO)
1 T butter
3 oz. cream cheese
Fresh ground pepper and salt to taste.

Directions:
Boil potatoes in salted water (until soft not mushy) drain and save the liquid.
Cook cabbage and onion in liquid for 5 minutes
Drain vegetables
Peel potatoes when cool enough to handle.
Add milk or sour cream and butter then mash until smooth.
Add vegetables to potato mixture
Fold in cream cheese, salt and pepper.
Put in greased casserole and Bake @ 350 – 425 degrees until browned.

Submitter: 
katt
Description: 
This is a great side dish for Corned Beef on St. Patrick's Day. It is very smooth and rich! The recipe came from my mother in-law, who is first generation Irish.
