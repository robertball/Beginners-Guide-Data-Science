Title:
Rosemary Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2345484.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 Russet potatoes
1 teaspoon of dried rosemary
1 teaspoon of garlic salt
2-3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Dice potatoes into 1/2 inch cubes, peel if you wish.
Place on cookie sheet or in a casserole.
Drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with the rosemary and garlic salt.
Place in a 350 degree oven for at least 20 minuets or until the potatoes begin to brown slightly.
