Title:
Mushroom Weinburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground burger
12 oz canned mushrooms, do not drain
1 onion
1/8 c half and half
12 oz beef boillion, mixed with water to make beef broth
3 tbl flour
1/2 cup butter
1/4 c parsley
1/2-3/4 cup burgendy wine
Salt and Pepper
1 bag of egg noodles

Directions:
Take out burger.  Mince the onion, add to the burger then proceed to add 1 1/2 tsp salt and 1 tsp pepper and all of the  half and half. Mix together.  Shape into small burgers 1-2 inches wide.  Pan fry in large saute pan on medium heat.  Make small batches and cook until well done, about 5 minutes for each batch.  Place burgers on paper toweled plate to help drain extra oil.
With left over grease, place butter in pan and melt.  Add flour and stir, like gravy and slowly add beef broth.  Let boil to thicken and slowly add wine and mushroom.  Let thicken.  It should thick, not like gravy, but like a nice white sauce. Add parsley and salt and pepper to taste.  Lastly, add burgers and let simmer for at least 15 minutes.
Boil egg noodles.  Drain and place sauce over them.  Enjoy.

Submitter: 
meg
Description: 
This recipe is my old family recipe from when we came over from Ireland in the 1930s and found ways to make ends meet.  Over the years, it has been changed to make less and incorporate greater flavor.  My fiance loves this recipe, as does my whole family.
