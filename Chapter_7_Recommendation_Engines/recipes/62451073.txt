Title:
Thai Salmon Cakes with Hot and Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans salmon, drained
1 T. oil
3/4 c. diced onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. minced jalapeno
1 1/2 tsp. lemon grass, minced
juice and zest of 1/2 lime
4 tsp. soy sauce
2 tsp sugar
1 1/2 c. finely sliced green oinions
1/4 c. chopped cilantro
2 eggs
3 T. cornstarch
hot and sour sauce:
2 T. sugar
2 T. plus 1 tsp. rice vinegar
2 T. chili sauce (sambai or roasted)
1 T. chopped cilantro

Directions:
saute onion in oil for 3-5 minutes.  Add garlic, jalapeno, lemon grass and cook 1-2 more minutes.  Cool.
In a large bowl, combine lime juice and zest, soy sauce, sugar, green onions, and cilantro.  Mix well.
In a small bowl, whisk eggs.  Add cornstarch and salt and pepper.s
Add egg and cooked onion mixture to large bowl; fold gently.  Add salmon and fold.  Form into 8 cakes (3 oz each) and chill for 30 minutes or more.
Pan fry salmon cakes.  Serve with hot and sour sauce:
