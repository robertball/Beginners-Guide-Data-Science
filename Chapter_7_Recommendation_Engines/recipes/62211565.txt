Title:
Oven Fried Chicken III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 to 3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
1 cup dried bread crumbs
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 cup lite Miracle whip

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In a medium bowl or gallon size resealable plastic bag, mix together the bread crumbs, garlic powder, salt, pepper, thyme and paprika.
Coat the chicken pieces with lite miracle whip. Place chicken pieces in bowl/bag with bread crumb mixture and coat/shake until well coated. Place chicken pieces in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish and bake for 1hr or until juices run clear.
