Title:
P.B. Chips Brownie Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986152.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 cups white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
4 eggs
3/4 cup HERSHEY®'S Cocoa Powder
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (10 ounce) package REESE'S® Peanut Butter Chips, divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F.  Line 18 muffin cups (2-1/2 inches in diameter) with paper or foil bake cups.
In large microwave-safe bowl, place butter.  Microwave at High (100%) 1 to 1-/2 minutes or until melted.  Stir in sugar and vanilla.  Add eggs; beat well.  Add cocoa; beat until well blended.  Add flour, baking powder and salt; beat well.  Stir in 1-1/3 cups peanut butter chips.  Divide batter evenly into muffin cups; sprinkle with remaining 1/3 cup peanut butter chips.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until surface is firm; cool completely in pan on wire rack.
