Title:
Quick Peanut Butter Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 of an 8 ounce block of cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
1 cup confectioner's sugar
1 8-9 ounce container whipped topping, thawed
1 prepared chocolate crumb crust (the brand available here is Oreo), or make your own

Directions:
Beat softened cream cheese and peanut butter until well blended, then beat in confectioners suger until fluffy. Fold in whipped topping. Pile into the crumb crust and refrigerate for 3 hours or until firm.
Top with more whipped topping and serve.
You can drizzle with chocolate sauce or sprinkle with cocoa powder to "fancy it up".

Submitter: 
Louise Bridges Kelley
Description: 
Light & cool, chocolate & peanut butter - what more could you ask for?  A similar pie sells for $3.95 a slice at a local restaurant,  but it's so easy to make at home!
