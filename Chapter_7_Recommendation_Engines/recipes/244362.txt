Title:
Banana Chocolate PB Oat Bran Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6369578.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 banana
1/3 cup oat bran
1 teaspoon powdered peanut butter
1 teaspoon cocoa powder
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Mash banana in a bowl; add oat bran, powdered peanut butter, and cocoa powder and stir to combine. Stir milk into banana mixture until blended.
Microwave banana mixture until hot, about 2 minutes. Let cereal rest until set, about 30 seconds.
