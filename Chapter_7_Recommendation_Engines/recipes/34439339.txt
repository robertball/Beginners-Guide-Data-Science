Title:
Lemon pudding ice box cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 sleeves of club house butter
crackers
2 pkgs. of lemon flavor instant
pudding
3 cups of milk
8 oz. of whipped cream cheese
Half cup sour cream
8 oz. container of whipped cream

Directions:
In a 13 by 9 pan layer bottom
with crackers
Mix pudding with 3 cups of milk
Before set mix in cream cheese
and sour cream
Add half of pudding mixture over
crackers
Cover with half of the whipped
cream
Then cover with a layer of crack-
ers, and then the rest of pudding
Cover with the rest of crackers
and whipped cream.
Cover pan and chill over night
to let the crackers soften.

Submitter: 
Kristine
Description: 
No bake ice box cake.  Salty and sweet, and tangy lemon flavor.
