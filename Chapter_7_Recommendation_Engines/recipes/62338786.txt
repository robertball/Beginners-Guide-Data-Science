Title:
Baking Powder Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup butter
3/4-1 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Heavily grease a small pan.
In a bowl mix the flour, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the butter until the mixture has only pea sized lumps. Add milk and mix until dry mixture is absorbed.Cut into 6-8 pieces. Place in greased pan and pour some melted butter over the top.
Bake for 12 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown.
