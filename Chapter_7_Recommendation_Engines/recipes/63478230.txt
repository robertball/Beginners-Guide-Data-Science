Title:
Vegan Bean Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups cooked adzuki beans (or any beans)
1 cup quick oats
1 1/2 cup water
2-4 cloves garlic
1 onion
2 carrots, peeled.
1 stalk celery
2 Tablespoons water
1 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup fresh parsley
2 -3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1/2  cup Brown Rice Flour

Directions:
Boil water.
Add quick oats, stir on low heat, until cooked. (about 1-3 minutes)
Chop garlic, onion, carrots, celery.
Saute celery, carrots, garlic and onion -
Add 2 tablespoons of water, basil  and salt cover and cook stirring occasionally. (about 5-10 minutes)
Combine all ingredients and blend in a food processor.
Transfer fully blended mixture to a bowl.
Add flour to mixture, stirring in flour slowly.
Form into patties and saut√©.

Description: 
This versatile burger can be made with almost any beans you have around. It stands up well to condiments and is a healthy alternative for those seeking to cut down on meat.
