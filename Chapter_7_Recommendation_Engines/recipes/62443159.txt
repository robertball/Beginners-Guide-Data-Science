Title:
Low Guilt Fudge Truffle Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460252.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup chocolate chips
1 cup plain 0% yogurt
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease an 8x8 inch glass baking dish and set aside.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa and baking soda. Set aside.
In a small saucepan over low heat, stir chocolate until melted and smooth. Remove from heat.
Place yogurt in a medium bowl and whisk in the sugar and water. Pour a few tablespoons of the warm chocolate into the yogurt mixture, whisking constantly until well blended. Stir in the remaining chocolate and mix thoroughly. Pour the chocolate mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
Pour into the prepared pan and bake for approximately 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
