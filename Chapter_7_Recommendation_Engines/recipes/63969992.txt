Title:
Bacon Hashbrown Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups grated cooked potatoes
12 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first seven ingredients.  Transfer to a greased 9-inch pie plate.  Drizzle with butter; sprinkle with paprika.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes or until lightly browned.
