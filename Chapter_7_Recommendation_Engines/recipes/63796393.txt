Title:
Chatfield'd Vegan Carb Cupcakes with Vanill Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cupcakes
1 1/2 Cups All purpose flour
3/4 Cup Sugar
1/3 Cup Chatfield's carob powder
1 teaspoon Baking soda
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/4 Cup Vegetable Oil
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1 Cup Water
Frosting
1/3/4 Cups Confectioner's sugar
6 Tablesoons Vegan non-dairy buttery spread
2 teaspoons Apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract

Directions:
Cupcakes
Preheat over to 350 F.  Sift together the flour, sugar, carob powder, baking soda and salt.  Add the oil, vanilla and water.  Mix togerther, beating well.  Place cup liners in a muffin pan and fill 3/4 full.  Bake for 20-22 minutes.  Cool before frosting
Frosting
In a large mixing bowl, cream together the confectioners sugar and the vegan non dairy buttery spread until smooth.  Add the apple cider vinegar and the vanilla extract and beat until frosting holds stiff peaks.  Store in a covered container in the refgertor until ready to use.
