Title:
BUSH'S® Best Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980589.jpg
Ingredients:
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup diced red onion
1 (15.25 ounce) can corn, drained
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon cilantro
1/4 cup olive oil
4 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon lime juice
1 (15 ounce) can BUSH'S® Black Beans, rinsed and drained
Salt and pepper to taste
Tortilla chips

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine peppers, onion, corn, garlic and cilantro. Add olive oil, vinegar, lime juice and salt and pepper to taste. Add BUSH'S BEST Black Beans and toss well. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
This colorful bean salad is dressed with a light, lime vinaigrette and only takes a few minutes to prepare. Serve with tortilla chips or as a side dish.
