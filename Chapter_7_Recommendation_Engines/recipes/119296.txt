Title:
Onion Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/815967.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted
1 1/2 cups biscuit/baking mix
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the milk, egg and butter. Add biscuit mix, 1/2 cup cheese and onion. Spoon into six greased muffin cups. Sprinkle with poppy seeds and remaining cheese. Bake at 400 degrees F for 12-14 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.

Submitter:
