Title:
Smirnoff White Grape Cosmo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983999.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Smirnoff White Grape Vodka
1/4 ounce triple sec
2 ounces cranberry juice
1 ounce lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1 wedge lemon

Directions:
Combine in a cocktail shaker with ice.
Shake, strain and serve in a martini glass.
Add a squeeze of lemon wedge.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Smooth and elegant -- this version of the cosmopolitan provides a flavorful twist on the sophisticate's sip of choice.
