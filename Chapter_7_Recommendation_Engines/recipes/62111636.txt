Title:
Mexican Tortilla Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refried beans
1 pk. McCormick taco seasoning
2 lb. ground beef
1 bag Kraft shredded taco cheese
1 jar Kraft cheese whiz
1 cup water
1 bag tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown ground beef.
Drain.
Add all ingredients to a slow cooker, reserving one cup of shredded cheese.
Cook on low for 2 hours.
Serve individually over a bed of tortilla chips.
Top with cheese and other favorite toppings

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
An easy way to make a taco treat everyone can enjoy.
