Title:
Lemon Cream Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1000461.jpg
Ingredients:
Topping:
5 Tbsp butter, softened
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup All-Purpose flour
1/4 cup sliced almonds
Filling:
6 oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp lemon extract
zest of one lemon
Cake:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp lemon extract
zest of one lemon
1 1/4 cups plain non-fat yogurt

Directions:
Make the topping by combining all ingredients in a small bowl with a pastry blender until mixed together and crumbs form. Set aside.
Make the filling: In a medium bowl beat the cream cheese with the sugar until fluffy. Mix in the lemon extract and lemon zest. Set aside.
To make the cake: Preheat oven to 350. Use shortening to grease a 9x13 pan.
In a small bowl whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until fluffy.
Add the eggs one at a time, beating until just combined.
Mix in the lemon extract and lemon zest.
Gradually add the dry ingredients in 4 additions, mix until just combined.
Fold in the yogurt.
Pour batter into prepared pan. Smooth with a rubber spatula.
Spoon dollops of cream cheese mixture on top of cake and swirl into batter with rubber spatula.
Sprinkle topping evenly over cake and press in just slightly.
Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
