Title:
Spicy Beef and Bean Enchilada Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20oz can black refried beans
1/2 c. diced onion
1 packet taco seasoning mix
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 lb ground beef
1 15oz can tex-mex style fire-roasted tomatoes
6 (8 inch) flour tortillas
2 cups pepper jack cheese
2 cups monterey jack cheese sour cream
2 10oz cans medium Old El Paso red enchilada sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Brown ground beef and saute onions together til done. Add taco seasoning mix and can green chiles. In separate mix together warmed refried black beans and 1 can enchilada sauce. Stir together til creamy. Lightly brown each tortilla in a cast iron skillet over medium heat til slightly crisp. About 15sec each side. Place one tortilla on bottom of  springform pan. Spread generous layer of refried beans, then generous layer of meat. Add one cup of pepperjack cheese. Repeat layers until beans and meat mixture are all spread between tortillas, adding about 1/4 can enchilada sauce between layers. Sprinkle remaining cheese and enchilada sauce over top and bake covered with foil for 40 minutes. Remove and let cool for 15 minutes before cutting. Serve with sour cream on top.

Submitter: 
Karla Smith
Description: 
This is a scrumptious, filling Mexican dish that is easy and loved by everyone I've served it to. Lots of flavor and a spicy kick of chiles and pepperjack cheese will make this a family staple any night of the weekModified from: Seven Layer Tortilla Pie
