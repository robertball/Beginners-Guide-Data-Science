Title:
EAGLE BRAND® Lemon Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976363.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tablespoons grated lemon rind
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
5 cups all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
Lemon Drizzle:
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
2 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
In large bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, EAGLE BRAND(R), lemon juice, lemon rind and vanilla; mix until smooth.
In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt. Add to creamed mixture; mix well. Divide dough into 3 balls. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate at least 4 hours.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. On lightly floured surface, roll each ball out to 1/4-inch thickness and cut into desired shapes.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes. Cool on wire rack. Garnish with Lemon Drizzle (optional). Store leftovers covered at room temperature.
Lemon Drizzle: Combine confectioners' sugar, water and lemon juice; mix well. Drizzle over cooled cookies.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Lemon juice and zest add a bright note to these sugar cookies. Cut into your favorite shapes, then drizzle with more lemony goodness after baking.
