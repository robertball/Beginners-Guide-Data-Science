Title:
Steel Cut Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tb butter
1 C steel cut oats
3 C boiling water
1/2 C whole milk
1/2 C buttermilk
1/2 ts salt
1/2 C raisins
1 Tb brown sugar
1/2 ts cinnamon

Directions:
In a large saucepot, melt the butter and add the oats. Stir for 2 minutes to toast. Add the boiling water and reduce heat to a simmer. Keep at a low simmer for 25 minutes, without stirring.
Combine the milk and half of the buttermilk with the oatmeal. Add raisins, cinnamon & salt. Stir gently to combine and cook for an additional 10 minutes. Spoon into a serving bowl and top with brown sugar.
