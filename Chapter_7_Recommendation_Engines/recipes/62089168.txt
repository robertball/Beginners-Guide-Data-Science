Title:
Zingy Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole tomatos (cubed)
1/2 medium white onion (chopped)
1/4 medium red onion (chopped)
2 tablespoons hot sauce
1 tablespoon minced garlic
4 cups tomato juice
1 tablespoons chopped basil
1 teaspoon allspice
1 cup heavy whipping cream
2 tablespoons real butter
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
1. simmer 2 cups of tomato sauce, onions, garlic and 2 cubed tomatos in a pot for 25 minutes.
2. in a seperat pot, simmer 2 cups of tomato sauce and 2 onions at the same time.
3. after 25 minutes, puree or blend contents of the first pot with basil, then add puree to pot 2.
4. add hot sauce, allspice, whipping cream, butter and salt and pepper. Stir and heat until butter is melted.
Serve with a hot deli sandwich or fresh bread.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kays
Description: 
This is a great twist on a classic tomato and basil soup. The onion and hot sauce give it a great taste.
