Title:
Chocolate Zucchini Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. oil
1/2 c. margarine
1 3/4 c. sugar
1 t. vanilla
2 eggs
1/2 c. sour milk (see directions)
2 1/2 c. flour
1 t. baking soda
1/4 c. cocoa
1/2 t. salt
1/2 - 1 t. cinnamon
2 c. grated zucchini
1/2 c. mini chooclate chips
1/2 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
To sour milk put 1 teaspoon of lemon juice in the bottom of a 1/2 cup.
Fill with milk and let sit for 5 minutes until it thickens.
Beat oil, margarine and sugar together.
Mix flour, baking soda, cocoa, salt, and ??? together in a bowl.
Alternately add 1/3 of the dry ingredients then 1/3 of the milk.
Add eggs one at a time;continue until well mixed.
Stir in Zucchini.
Pour into 9"x13" square baking dish.
Sprinkle with chocolate chips and chopped pecans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes or until the cake tests done.
