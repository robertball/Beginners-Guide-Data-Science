Title:
Mushroom Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
approx 1 T olive oil
several cloves of garlic, chopped
1 yellow/white onion, chopped
4-6 c mushrooms (cremini, white, etc), sliced
chicken, mushroom, or other stock
white wine (sauvignon blanc)
3 c arborio rice
3-4 c water, as needed
herbs de provence (basil, oregano, rosemary, etc) or other herbs
fresh basil, chopped
parmesan or reggiano cheese, grated
salt, pepper

Directions:
heat olive oil
add garlic, onion to soften
add mushrooms and stock/wine to soften
add onion/mush to rice cooker, leaving a little in pan
add rice to pan to brown/soften
add rice to rice cooker
add stock, white wine, water (totaling about 4 c)
season with salt, herbs de provence
turn to cook on rice cooker
stir as needed, add stock/water/wine as needed
stir fresh basil in before serving
serve with fresh basil and parm/regg cheese
