Title:
Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sugar
2 cups water
1 quart lemon juice
3 cups orange juice
1 quart grape juice
3 1/2 quarts water

Directions:
Make syrup of sugar and water.  Cool.  Add strained fruit juices and water.  Pour over block of ice in punch bowl.  Makes about 50 small servings.
