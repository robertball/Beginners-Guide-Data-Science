Title:
Holiday Chocolate Nut Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup sugar, divided
1 1/4 cups flour
2 Tbsp heavy cream
1 3/4 cups pecans, coarsely chopped
1 cup flaked coconut
4 oz semi-sweet chocolate, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Beat 1/2 cup shortening and 1/4 cup sugar until creamy.  Blend in flour.  Press on bottom of ungreased 9x9x2" baking pan and bake at 350 degrees for 18 minutes, or until edges are lightly browned.
Heat 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup shortening, and cream in saucepan, stirring until mixture is blended.  Stir in pecans.  Set aside.
Sprinkle coconut and chocolate over baked crust.  Top with pecan mixture and bake for 20 minutes more, or until golden.  Cool and cut into squares.
