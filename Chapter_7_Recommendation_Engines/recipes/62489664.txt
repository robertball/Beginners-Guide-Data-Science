Title:
Vanillla Syrup for Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. water
1 c. sugar
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Mix sugar and water in saucepan.
Bring to a boil over medium heat.
Boil 5 min.
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Cool completely and store in a bottle for easy pouring!

Submitter: 
Molly-Blue
Description: 
Great for homemade lattes, chai tea or coffee!
