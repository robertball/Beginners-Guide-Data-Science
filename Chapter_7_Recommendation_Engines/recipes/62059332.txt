Title:
Hot Dog Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Chuck
1 medium Onion-finely chopped
3 cloves of minced Garlic
1/2 C Ketchup
1/4 C BBQ Sauce
1T Chili Powder
2T Yellow Mustard
1/2t Black Pepper
2 t Salt-more or less to taste
2 t heaping Cumin
1 T Worshestershire Sauce
Pinch Red Pepper Flakes-to taste
1 C Water

Directions:
Brown beef and onions til meat is no longer pink.  Drain meat of fat.  Put slightly cooled meat mixture into food processor and pulse for 5 or 6 spins, or until meat/onion mixture is the size of small tiny crumbles.  Return meat to fry pan, add garlic, cook for 3 minutes making sure not to burn garlic, add remaining ingredients, stir and cook with out a cover on medium heat for 10 minutes or until most of the water has cooked out and left you with a thick rich sauce.  Taste for salt at this time.  Cool slightly,  Take a hotdog in a bun, smother it with Hot Dog Sauce, top with shredded cheddar cheese and chopped raw onions.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
GOGOBLUES
Description: 
A flavorful meaty sauce with a slight kick that is guaranteed to liven up any hotdog!
