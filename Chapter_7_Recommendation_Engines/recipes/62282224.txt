Title:
Crock Pot Beef Stew I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
1 Tablespoon vegetable oil
1/3-1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 clove garlic, minced(jar=1tsp)
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon paprika
1 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1 onion, chopped
2 cups beef broth
3 potatoes, diced
4 carrots, sliced
1 stalk celery, chopped
turnip
1 pckt McCormick bf stew
pinch fresh ground italian seasonings

Directions:
In a small bowl mix together the flour, salt;
Try to cut excess fat off meat
Pepper meat to taste and add about 2Tbsp of flour mix to cover.Heat 1Tbsp oil in frying pan add meat and brown lightly(refrigerate till ready to cook)
In a med size mixing bowl add broth, worcester sauce, McCormicks, &paprika, then set aside.
Then cut onion,garlic,potatoes,carrots,celery,and turnip and add to the crock pot with bay leaves.Pour rest of flour mix over veggies and add wet ingr(beef broth mix). Pour beef into slow cooker trying to keep beef on top of veggies.You should be able to see a little broth, but meat won't be covered.Wait to add any other spices, salt or pepper 'till 1/2 hour before it's finished.Otherwise, don't open the lid once started.
Cover, and cook on Low setting for 8-10 hours, or on High setting for 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
S Sconi
Description: 
Cubed meat is seasoned with pepper and sauteed to light brown with vegetables, beef broth, garlic,extra herbs, Worcestershire sauce, and paprika. Under 30 min to prepare, put it in the crock pot & you can go to work and come home to a house filled with mouth watering aromas and a fully cooked meal!Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Stew I
