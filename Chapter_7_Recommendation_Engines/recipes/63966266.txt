Title:
Mom's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 tablespoon sour cream
2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
3 bananas, mashed
1/2 cup nuts, chopped, optional

Directions:
Beat eggs.  Add all ingredients EXCEPT bananas and nuts.  Mix.  Add mashed bananas.  Add nuts.
Bake for 1 hour in large loaf pan at 325 degrees.
Alternatives:  Can use thawed, frozen bananas.  For more less moist and more cake like bread cook in 9x13 pan.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
My Grandma Holbrook used this one!  It is a gem.
