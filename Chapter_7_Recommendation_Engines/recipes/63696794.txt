Title:
Homemade Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/ 2 cup of butter
3/4 cup of brown sugar (6 tablespoons of brown sugar splenda blend)
1/2 cup of granulated sugar (1/4 cup of stevia domino blend)
2 egg whites or 1 egg
1 teaspoon of vanilla
1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
3 cups of old fashioned oats (uncooked)
1/2 cup of almonds (optional)
1 cups of raisins

Directions:
Put oven to 350 degrees.
Mix first 3 ingredients then add eggs and vanilla. Mix in cinnamon, oats and raisins well. (Add almonds if using)
Press dough onto a 13x9 inch pan (can spray Pam down before just incase).
Bake for about 25 minutes or until looks and smells ready.
Cool and break granola up and place in container or bag to store.

Submitter: 
Nicole Marie
Description: 
Enjoy your homemade granola rather than the high calorie granola from the store.
