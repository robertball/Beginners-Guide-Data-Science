Title:
pomegranate dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pomegranate
1/8 cup water
2 tbsp pomegranate juice(pomegranate blueberry)

Directions:
muddle pomegranate seeds with water. pour into a bowl.
add the 2 tbsp of pomegranate juice. mix. pour on top of any salad enjoy.

Submitter: 
Elle Mcgrath
Description: 
this is a great salad dressing. it is so easy to make. it goes good with any salad.
