Title:
Coconut Coffee Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup softened butter (1 stick)
1 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
4 tbsp cocoa
3 tbsp cold coffee
1 1/2 cup oats
1 cup coconut flakes

Directions:
Combine butter, sugar and vanilla and mix until blended
Mix in oats
Slowly mix in coffee until it becomes a firm dough
Form dough into small balls approximately 1" in diameter
Roll balls through coconut shreds

Submitter: 
Michael Zick Doherty
Description: 
Small coffee flavored oat balls coated in coconut shreds
