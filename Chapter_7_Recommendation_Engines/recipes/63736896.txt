Title:
Pineapple and Pecan Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037463.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups of all purpose flour
2 Cups of sugar
2 Teaspoons of Baking Soda
2 Eggs
20 ouz can of crushed pineapple with juice
1 cup of chopped pecans
1 stick of butter softened
1 8 oz cream cheese softened
2 cups of confectioners sugar
1 tablespoon of vanilla

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees.Mix the dry ingredients(flour and sugar ) and stir. Add in pineapples along with its juice and eggs. Put pecans on pan and spray with canola oil and roast for 5 min. Add into mixed ingredients. Stir until all ingredients are mixed well. Pour into a buttered 9x13 inch baking pan. Bake in oven for 35 minutes or until tooth pick comes out clean. Cool completely.
Making Icing: Combine cream cheese,butter,confectioners sugar,and vanilla. beat until smooth.
Frost cake using all the frosting.
