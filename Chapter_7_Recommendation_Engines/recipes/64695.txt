Title:
Grilled Italian Sausage and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 medium bell peppers (red, green and/or yellow), cut into strips
1 small onion, sliced
1/4 cup fat-free Italian dressing
4 frozen BOCA Meatless Italian Sausages
4 hot dog buns or French bread rolls, partially split

Directions:
PLACE peppers, onions and dressing in heavy-duty foil bag or foil pouch; close bag tightly. Grill 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender, turning bag occasionally.
MEANWHILE, grill sausages as directed on package. Fill buns with sausages; top with pepper mixture. Makes 4 sandwiches.
FILL buns with sausages; top with pepper mixture.
