Title:
Tuna Fish Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714260.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Cans Of Tuna fish
1/2 Of A Onion, diced
1/8 teaspoon of salt
1/8 teaspoon of Pepper
1 Hoagie roll 12 inches
6 Level tablespoons of mayonnaise

Directions:
preheat oven to 375F
Cut the sub down the side
Place the roll on a baking try on a rack for 10 to 15 mins
Take the tuna and drain, then mix it with mayonnaise, onions, salt, and pepper.
when you take out The roll it should have a nice crispy crust.
Scoop out the tuna mixture onto the roll

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
Me and my wife love Tuna Fish Sandwiches, I think we have about 30 cans right now in the closet, and buy some when they go on sale.
