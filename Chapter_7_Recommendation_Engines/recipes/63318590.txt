Title:
Fluffy Fall French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup flour
1/2 cup half & half
2 eggs
1 Tbsp brown sugar
3 Tbsp honey
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp dried orange peel
1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients (except bread) in a large bowl. Heat skillet to about 325 degrees. Grease skillet with butter. Dip bread slices into batter, coating both sides of bread until bread is mostly soaked. Fry bread on both sides until it is a mottled brown. Serve with maple syrup or orange marmalade and a sprinkle of pumpkin pie spice. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Samuela
Description: 
I've been searching and searching for a good french toast recipe that is comparable to the french toast from my favorite restaurant. After mixing ingredients from a couple recipes, I think I've finally found it!
