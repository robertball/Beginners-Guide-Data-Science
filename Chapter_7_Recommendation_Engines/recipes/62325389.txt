Title:
Milk Chocolate Ganache Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/609923.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
1.) Pour milk chocolate chips into a medium size bowl and set aside.
2.) Pour heavy whipping cream into a small saucepan and heat medium high for about 2 minutes until it is very warm, yet be careful so it doesn't burn.
3.) Once heavy whipping cream is throughly warm, take off of the stove and pour over the milk chocolate chips. Let the warm cream, sit over the milk chocolate chips for a minute to allow the warm cream to melt the chips.
4.) Using a whisk, begin to stir mixture until creamy.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Milk chocolate ganache for those who don't want to use semi-sweet or dark chocolate. Great topping for a cheesecake!
