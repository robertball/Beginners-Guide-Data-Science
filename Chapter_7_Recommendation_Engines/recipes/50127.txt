Title:
Chocolate Peanut Bunny Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981940.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large scoops HAAGEN-DAZS® Vanilla Ice Cream
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons Chocolate Flavor NESTLE® NESQUIK™ Syrup
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter

Directions:
PLACE ice cream, milk, Nestle Nesquik and peanut butter in blender; cover. Blend until smooth. Divide between 4 chilled glasses.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Nesquik™
Description: 
Shake it up Bunny! This yummy concoction is just too hard to resist.
