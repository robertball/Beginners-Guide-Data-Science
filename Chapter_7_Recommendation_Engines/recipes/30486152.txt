Title:
White Chocolate Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) loaf French bread - cut into 1 in. cubes
1 cup white chocolate chips
4 cups milk
1/4 cup butter
1 cup sugar
5 eggs
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp cinnamon
Caramel Sauce:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
1 cup brown sugar
1 Tb. vanilla

Directions:
Pudding: Grease 9x13in. pan, add bread to pan and sprinkle with white chocolate chips.
Heat milk and butter. Add sugar. Mix until melted
Pour small amount of milk mixture into eggs.  Stir constantly, add eggs to milk mixture. Add salt, vanilla and cinnamon. Pour over bread and white chocolate chips. Bake 45 minutes in 350 degree oven.
Sauce: Combine butter and brown sugar.  Cook until sugar melts.  Stir in milk.  Bring to a boil.  Stir in vanilla.
