Title:
Refried beans and ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooked Pinto beans about 3 cups
Bacon Grease about 1/2 cup
1 cup chopped ham
1/2 cup water (optional)
1 Cup mixed shredded cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat up pan, add bacon grease till hot, add chopped ham and cook till starting to brown dont forget to stir, add cooked beans ( b careful sometimes splatters when you add the beans) with a potato masher mash the beans until there are no solid beans left making sure to scrap the bottom so it doesnt burn, add 1/2 cup water if too thick. Add salt and pepper to taste and add the cheese. Heat all till bubbles and thickens then turn off.

Submitter: 
Starr
Description: 
My family is Mexican so we make Beans every which way you can imagine but my kids favorite will always be this one. My 4yr old will ask to lick the pan. I dont make them like this all the time but there are never any left. Hope you enjoy
