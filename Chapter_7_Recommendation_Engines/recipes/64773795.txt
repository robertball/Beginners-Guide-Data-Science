Title:
Coconut Lime Rum Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7563072.jpg
Ingredients:
11 oz vanilla wafers, crushed
1/2 cup sweetened coconut flakes
1/2 cup coconut rum
1 lime zested
1 Tbsp. lime juice concentrated (such as Rose's)
1/8 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine vanilla wafer crumbs, coconut flakes, coconut rum, lime zest, and lime juice concentrate. Shape dough into 1 inch balls; roll in confectioners' sugar.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
A no bake rum ball recipe just like Grandma used to fix, but with a coconut lime twist!
