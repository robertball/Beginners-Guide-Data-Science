Title:
Pumpkin Poop Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 bag pumpkin spice marshmallows
5 to 6 cups oat circle cereal
1 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/4 cup chopped toasted pecans, optional
Cooking spray

Directions:
Place a large parchment paper lined baking sheet in the freezer.
In a nonstick Dutch oven, melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows & stir frequently until melted. Add cereal & stir until coated evenly.
Remove from heat, stir in mini chocolate chips & pecans, if desired.
Lightly spray 2 soup spoons front & back with cooking spray. Use sprayed spoons to scoop mixture & push onto chilled baking sheet into irregular mounds. Let cool to set.
