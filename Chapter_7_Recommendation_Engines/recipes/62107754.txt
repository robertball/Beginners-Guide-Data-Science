Title:
English tea cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 egg, slightly beatened
1 1/2 cups sour pie filling cherries, drained
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
Sauce:
1/2 cups butter
1/2 cups margarine
1 cup sugar
1/2 cups evaporated milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Stir together sugar, salt, flour and baking soda
Add egg.
Stir in cherries and nuts.
Pour into greased 8 inch square baking dish or ramekins.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
To make sauce:
Cook together butter, margarine, sugar and milk until bubbly.
Add vanilla and simmer for 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
These moist,petite tea cakes are great for a luncheon.  Served with a rich cream sauce, they are sinful!  I've also substituted walnuts for the pecans.
