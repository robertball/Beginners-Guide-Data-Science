Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2445515.jpg
Ingredients:
2-2 1/2C all purpose flour
2C warm (90F) milk
1/2C cocoa
1/2C sugar
1/4C +1TBSP vegetable oil
2 1/2tsp active yeast
1/2tsp salt

Directions:
1. Add warm milk and sugar to a bowl and mix well.
2. Add yeast and stir gently, then set aside for 5-10 minutes.
3. Add half a cup of flour, cocoa, 1/4C oil and salt. Stir gently with a fork.
4. Add flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the mixture forms a ball and is no longer sticky.
5. Lift the dough from the bowl, pour in the remaining TBSP of oil and return the dough to the bowl, rolling to coat well.
6. Cover and allow to rise until at least double in volume (1 hour in warm weather, more in colder)
7. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
8. Grease a cookie sheet.
9. Divide the dough into balls about 1-2 inches in diameter.
10. Place balls on cookie sheet about 1-2 inches apart.
11. Reduce heat to 400 and place sheet in oven.
12. Bake for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jamie Gardner
Description: 
Chocolate bread bites that can be sweet or savory, depending on how you serve them. It's abnosomely delicious. I make it to serve during G.I.S.H.W.H.E.S. and to feed all the little DinoMites, Elopuses, Fograts, and Woosters that might stop by.
