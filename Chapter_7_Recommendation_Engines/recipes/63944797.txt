Title:
Big Ray's Zucchini Oven Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093528.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium sized zucchini
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/8 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp paprika
1 tsp kosher salt
2 tsp olive oil
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Cut zucchini into 3" sticks and mix all dry ingredients in medium bowl.  Add olive oil and mix with fingertips until combined.  In a separate bowl beat egg until frothy.  Toss zucchini sticks in egg mixture.  Working in batches, transfer sticks to bread crumbs mixture and press onto sticks.
Place on greased baking sheet and bake until golden and tender, about 12 min.
