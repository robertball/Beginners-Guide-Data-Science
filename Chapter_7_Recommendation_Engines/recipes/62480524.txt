Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breast
16 oz. package wild rice
2 c. shredded carrots
3-4 stalks celery, chopped
2 cups frozen chopped onion
1 Tbs. minced garlic
1 Tbs. kosher salt
2 tsp. thyme
1 tsp. celery salt
2 tsp. Rosemary (I used Pampered Chef Rosemary herb mix)
2 bay leaves
8-9 cups of broth (canned or bouillon with water; I used a combination)

Directions:
Put everything in a 6 qt. crockpot, except chicken.
Stir.
Add chicken.
Cook for 5 hours on high.
After a couple hours, we took out the chicken to shred/dice and added about 2 cups of water.
