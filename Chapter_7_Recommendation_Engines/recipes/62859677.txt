Title:
Hungarian Drop Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts water + 1 cup
3 teaspoons salt
2 1/3 cups sifted flour
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Boil 2 quarts water with 2 teaspoons salt.
Meanwhile, sift together and set aside 2 and 1/3 cups sifted flour and 1 teaspoon salt.
Mix together slightly beaten egg and 1 cup water.
Gradually add flour mixture to egg mixture, stirring until smooth.  Batter should be very thick and break from the spoon.
Spoon batter into the boiling water by 1/2 teaspoonfuls, dipping spoon into water each time.  Cook only one layer of noodles at a time.
After noodles rise to surface, boil gently 5-8 minutes or until soft when pressed against side of pan.
Remove from water with with slotted spoon. and place into a warm bowl.
Toss dumplings with 1/4 cup of melted butter.

Description: 
This is the recipe passed down through my family from my Hungarian immigrant grandparents.  It's the ultimate comfort food!
