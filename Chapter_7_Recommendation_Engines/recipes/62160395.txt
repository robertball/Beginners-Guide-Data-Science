Title:
Life of the party Taco Salad... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces lean turkey meat
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix (I prefer taco bell)
1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1/2 medium onion thinly sliced
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, drained
2 medium sized tomatoes, chopped
2 avocados- peeled, pitted, and chopped
8 ounces reduced fat Cheddar cheese
1/2 bag (16 ounce) package tortilla chips (baked if you can find)
1/3 bottle light Balsamic Vinigrette dressing.

Directions:
1.  Prepare the ground turkey meat as directed by the taco seasoning package and set aside.
2. In a large bowl, combine the turkey, lettuce, onion, pinto beans, tomatoes, avacado and cheese.
3.  Before serving, pour in the Balsamic Vinaigrette and crush up the tortilla chips and mix well.  Serve immediately.
