Title:
Pineapple Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 French baguette, cut into 1/3 inch thick slices
1/2 medium sweet onion finely chopped
4 pineapple rings drained and squeezed of juice and chopped
1 can italian diced tomatoes drained
6-8 mild banana pepper rings (optional)
5 tbls mayo or miricle whip (your choice)
1 slice provolone cheese torn into small pieces
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup olive oil
italian seasoning
basil

Directions:
1.Preheat broiler
2.Cut french baguette into 1/3 inch thick slices. Place them on a cookie sheet. Mix Olive oil and italian seasoning in a small bowl. Lightly brush the olive oil and seasoning mixture over the tops of the baguette slices. Place in the oven under the broiler for 2 minutes or until the tops are just golden brown.
3.Remove from the oven. Spread mayo or miricle whip lightly over each slice. Top with diced tomatoes,chopped onion,chopped pineapple,chopped pepper rings,provolone cheese and finally mozzarella cheese.
4.Place in the oven under the broiler for 2 minutes or until the cheese is lightly brown and bubbly.

This is a all time favorite with my family and friends. The pineapple gives it a hint of sweet among the rich italian flavor of a classic bruschetta.
