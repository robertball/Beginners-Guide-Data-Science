Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2, 24 oz. cans chicken, drained
1, 15 oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
1, 4 oz. can diced green chilies
1/2 c. salsa
flour tortillas
1 1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Grease 13x9 pan and set aside.
In large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions and cook until translucent. Add garlic and saute 1 min.
Add chicken, beans, chilies and salsa. Simmer 5 min.
Fill tortillas with mixture, roll and place, seam side down, in greased baking dish. Cover generously with cheese.
Bake 15 min, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
