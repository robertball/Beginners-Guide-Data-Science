Title:
Cherry bing candy *fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4362587.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C Sugar
2/3 C Evaporated Milk
1/8 tsp salt *optional
12 Marshmallows or 1/2 of a 10 oz bag of mini's
1/2 C Butter
1 - 10oz pkg. Cherry chips
1 tsp vanilla
1 - 12oz pkg. Chocolate Chips
3/4 C chunky Peanut Butter
1 C crushed salted peanuts ( ie. Planter's roasted, salted peanuts)

Directions:
Combine sugar, milk, salt, marshmallows and butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Boil for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and add cherry chips and vanilla.
Pour into a 9x13 buttered pan and smooth out.
Cool well
Melt chocolate chips. Add peanut butter and nuts. Spread over top of Cherry layer and chill until set. Cut into 1" cubes and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Andrea Shreve Wood
Description: 
A two layer fudge recipe with a cherry layer and a peanutty chocolate top layer. This has always been a family favorite.
