Title:
Peaches and Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 package of vanilla pudding mix (3 1/4 oz. - not instant)
3 tbsp. butter
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
15 oz. can of peaches (or pineapple) drained - reserve juice
8 oz. package of cream cheese - softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, pudding mix, butter, egg and milk.
Beat for 2 minutes and then pour batter into a greased 9-10 inch pie plate.
Place peach slices on top of batter.
In a small bowl, combine cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar and 3 tbsp. peach juice and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
Spoon mixture over peaches to within 1 inch of the edge of the pie plate.
Combine cinnamon and remaining 1 tbsp. of sugar and sprinkle over cream cheese mixture.
Bake for 30-35 minutes.
