Title:
Chocolate Snowswirl Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981807.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
4 tablespoons butter or margarine, divided
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Dash salt
1 cup chopped nuts
2 cups miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Melt chips with sweetened condensed milk, 2 tablespoons, butter, vanilla and salt in a heavy saucepan over low heat.  Remove from heat; stir in vanilla and nuts.  Spread evenly into wax paper-lined 8- or 9-inch square pan.
In medium saucepan, over low heat, melt marshmallows with remaining 2 tablespoons butter.  Spread on top of fudge. With table knife or metal spatula, swirl through top of fudge.
Chill at least 2 hours or until firm.  Turn fudge onto cutting board; peel off wax paper and cut into squares.  Store covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Melted marshmallows add a decorative swirl to chocolate fudge.
