Title:
My Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
3/8 cup olive oil
2 (6 ounce) can canned crabmeat, drained
2 egg
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 teaspoon mustard
6 ounces buttery round crackers, crushed (20 crackers)
1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon garlic
1/4 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning TM
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a skillet over high heat.
Combine crabmeat, eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, crushed crackers, cayenne pepper, garlic,  Old Bay seasoning, salt and pepper. Form into 1/2 inch thick patties. Coat the patties with bread crumbs.
Heat 1/2 cup oil in a skillet over medium high heat. Cook cakes until golden brown on each side. Drain briefly on paper towels and serve hot.
