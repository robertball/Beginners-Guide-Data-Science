Title:
FB Chickpea Ragu with Polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T OO
1 onion finely chopped
1 t. dried oregano
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 can chickpeas, ringed and drained
1 can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup tapenade
2 cups 1% milk
1 cup water
1/2 t salt
1/2 cup instant polenta
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large non-stick skillet. Add onion and oregano. Cook until onion begins to soften. Stir in garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Stir in chickpeas and cook for 3 minutes. Stir in the tomatoes and cook for about 3-4 minutes until mixture starts to thicken. Reduce heat to low, stir in tapenade and keep warm.
Combine the milk, water and salt in a medium sauce pan. Bring mixture to a boil over medium high heat, and whisk in polenta in a steady stream. Cook, stirring occasionally until polenta thickens. Remove from heat, stir in cheese. Serve chickpea ragu on top of polenta.
