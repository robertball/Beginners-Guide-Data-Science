Title:
Almond Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1 2/3 cup flour
13 cup cornstarch
1/2 tsp almond extract
50 whole almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
Cream together butter, confectioners' sugar and almond extract until fluffy. Stir in flour and cornstarch. Fill cookie press with dough and press onto baking sheet. You can also roll the dough into balls and press them flat on a cookie sheet.
Press a whole almond into the centre of each cookie. Bake for 8-10 minutes in preheated oven, or until edges are gold.

Submitter: 
Abbi Saesong
Description: 
These cookies turn out wonderful with a cookie press, and are a great for holidays or special events.
