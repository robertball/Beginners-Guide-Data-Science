Title:
Southwest Chicken Chopped Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c diced rotisserie chicken
1 green pepper, diced
1 c black beans
1 c corn (canned or defrosted from frozen)
1 c cherry tomatoes, halved
1 large romaine heart, chopped
2 avocados, diced
2 c crushed tortilla chips
Dressing...
1/2 c mayo
1/2 c sour cream
1/2 tbsp ranch seasoning
1/2 tbsp taco seasoning
1/2 tbsp lime juice
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In large bowl, stir all salad ingredients together except chips and avocado.
In small bowl, mix all dressing ingredients together.
Toss salad and gently add avocado and chips before serving.
