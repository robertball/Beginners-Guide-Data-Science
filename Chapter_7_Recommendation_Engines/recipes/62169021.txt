Title:
Bruschetta Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/297920.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large skinless, boneless chicken breasts, butterflied
1/2 cup of bread crumbs
2-3 tbsp of light garlic cream cheese
1/4 cup of light mayo or light sour cream
4 leaves of fresh basil
1 tsp of italian seasoning
1 tsp of grated parmesan
1 tsp of garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
Bruschetta Mix
1 roma tomato, seeded and finely diced
2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
1/2 tbsp of pre-made pesto
1 tbsp of olive oil

Directions:
Mix all your bruschetta ingredients together and let sit in the fridge overnight.
Preheat oven to 400F
Spread garlic cream cheese on one side of each chicken breast. Lay out 2 leaves of basil on the cream cheese on each chicken breast. Spoon bruschetta mixture over the top of the basil.
Fold over the chicken and brush with light mayo or sour cream.
In a medium bowl combine breadcrumbs, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, garlic powder and Parmesan.
Dip each chicken breast into the breading until it is well coated.
Cook on a foil lined baking sheet for 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Mixing a great appetizer with chicken to make a very flavourful and delectable entree.
