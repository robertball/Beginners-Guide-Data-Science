Title:
Orange and Garlic Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/334050.jpg
Ingredients:
-2 bags of frozen green beans or 1lb fresh trimmed green beans
- Julliened Peel and juice of 1 orange
-5 garlic cloves sliced
-5 tbsp. sesame oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook green beans for 5 minutes. Drain and shock in ice water. Dry the pot of any liquid and heat the seseame oil over med-hi heat. Add garlic and orange peel and stir for approx 3 minutes to flavor the oil. Add the orange juice and  green beans and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes stirring occasionally and then cover and let cook for another 10 minutes stirring every few minutes until green beans are cooked but still green and crisp.
