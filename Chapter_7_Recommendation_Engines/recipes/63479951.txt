Title:
Creamy Alfredo Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium size red potatoes, cubed
1 small onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup diced carrots (or 12 baby carrots sliced)
2 stalks celery, diced
1 32 oz. box organic beef broth
3/4 lbs. kielbasa sausage, diced
1 tbsp. dried parsley
Â½ cup Alfredo sauce (bottled or see recipe below)
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a small Dutch oven over medium heat combine potatoes, onion, garlic, carrots and celery; cook until onions have softened and veggies are just tender. Add beef broth and bring to a boiling point then reduce heat to simmer for 15 minutes. Stir in sausage and parsley and continue to simmer until heated through, about 10 minutes. Add Alfredo sauce just before serving, mix well; season to taste.

Submitter: 
Dhyana
Description: 
Alfredo sauce adds a delectable creamy flavor to this hearty soup. Perfect for a winter's day!
