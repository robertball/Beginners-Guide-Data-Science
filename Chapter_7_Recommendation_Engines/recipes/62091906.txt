Title:
Cooked Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Cake:
2 cups wheat flour
2 cups sugar
2 cups chocolate powder
1 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon sodium bicarbonate
2 cups boiling milk
For the covering:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup chocolate powder
1 tablespoon butter
3 tablespoon cold milk

Directions:
For the Cake: Mix all the ingredients, except the milk. When it gets completely homogeneous, add the boiling milk and mix evenly. The dough will get very liquid. Put it into a retangular medium pan buttered and floured. Put it in the ovem for 180 Celsius degrees. Qhen it is done, pour the covering over the cake while it is still hot.
For the covering:
Mix all the ingredients. It is not necessary to heat it. Use it when the cake is done.

Submitter: 
Cacau
Description: 
A delicious chocolate cake, prepared with hot milk. The texture is similar to a brownie, very wet and tasty. The covering is crispy and marvelous.
