Title:
Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Center:
2 oz. semisweet or bittersweet chocolate
1/4 cup heavy cream
Cake:
4 oz. bittersweet chocolate
1 stick unsalted butter
2 eggs
2 egg  yolks
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup cake flour
handful of raspberries
1/2 cup confectioners sugar

Directions:
Making Centers:
Melt chocolate and cream in dbl boiler.
Stirring constantly.
Refrigerate for 2 hours.
Form into 6 balls & refrigerate until later
Making the Cake:
heat oven to 400 degrees.
Spray 6 ramekins with non stick spray.
Melt chocolate and butter in dbl boiler.
Constantly stirring.
In large bowl mix eggs/yolk/sugar/vanilla.
Mix on high speed for 5 minutes.
Fold chocolate with flour.
Then gently mix that mixture with egg mix.
Spoon cake mix into ramekins.
Place a chocolate ball in middle.
Bake for 15 mins.
take out of oven & let sit for 5 mins.
Garnish w/ confectioners sugar & raspberry
