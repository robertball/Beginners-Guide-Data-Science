Title:
Sandy's Apple Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
dash of salt
1 - 2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 tablespoon butter, melted
1 egg
1/3 cup milk, more if needed
1-1 1/2 cups apples, chopped
Oil for frying
Milk and powered sugar for a glaze or powdered sugar for dusting. whichever you prefer.

Directions:
Glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar and 1 tablespoon of milk mix well. add more powdered sugar or milk as needed.
A large sauce pan used for heating the oil.
Mix all dry ingredients together, slowly add the wet ingredients minus the apple. Carefully mix until well until combined. Do not over mix. Gently fold in apple pieces. the batter should be the consistence of a light cake mix.
Once the oil is ready, using a large spoon carefully drop by spoonful into oil a few at a time. do not over crowd pan. Once the underside is golden brown turn over and continue until done.

Submitter: 
Sandy Lafleur
Description: 
Tried this recipe when my family went apple picking. I had plenty of apples to make this recipe a few times. I think they came out very good.
