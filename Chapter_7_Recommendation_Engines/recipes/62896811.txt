Title:
Apple Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
2 Tbsp. honey
1 Tbsp. butter (no substitute!)
¼ tsp. salt
½ tsp. cinnamon
1 cup dry old-fashioned oats
1 cup chopped apples
½ cup chopped walnuts
2 Tbsp. brown sugar

Directions:
1. Mix together all ingredients in greased slow cooker.
2. Cover. Cook on Low 5-6 hours.
Serving suggestion: Serve with milk or ice cream.
Variation: Add ½ cup light or dark rasins to mixture.
