Title:
Quick, Low-Sodium Red Enchilada Sauce w/Jalapeno Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070311.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon dried minced onion
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
2 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon dried jalapeño
2 teaspoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
3 cups water

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together onion, oregano, chili powder, basil, ground black pepper, cumin, jalapeño, parsley.  Mix together in a medium pan the tomato paste and olive oil.  Stir in small bowl of spices. Cook for two minutes and stir in the water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
VH
Description: 
Looking for a low sodium option to replace canned sauce.  Using all spices, this allows for a smooth sauce with plenty of taste.  Use spice amounts according to your personal preference.   Idea:  Mix up the spices and make packets with directions for holiday gifts.
