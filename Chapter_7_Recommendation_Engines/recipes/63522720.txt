Title:
Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks real butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
2 cups sifted flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter and sugar well.
Add sifted flour and knead well.
Roll out and cut.
Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes, until light brown.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description:
