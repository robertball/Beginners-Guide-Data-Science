Title:
Christmas Spiced Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1802568.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
3/4 cup cocoa
1 1/4 cup sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/2 cup unsweetened apple sauce
3 cups oats
1 1/2 cups shredded coconut
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1 pinch of salt

Directions:
measure out oats, coconut, and cloves, and mix together.
place butter, milk, cocoa,sugar,applesauce,vanilla, and salt into pot and bring ingredients up to boil
and continue to boil for 1 minute
take off heat, and mix in your dry ingredients.
scoop tablespoon fulls onto parchment lined tray.
place in fridge until set, and transfer to an airtight container and place in freezer.
enjoy!

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
They taste great from the freezer, and are great to pull out when company comes..or when you need a little snack. The ground cloves makes them taste like Christmas.
