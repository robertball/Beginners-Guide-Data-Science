Title:
Almond Royal Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons meringue powder
4 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
5 tablespoons water
2 teaspoons almond extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Beat all ingredients at low speed  for 7 to 10 minutes, or until icing forms peaks.  Tip: Keep icing covered with a wet kitchen towel at all times. Icing can dry out quickly.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
Meringue powder and confectioners' sugar are the base of this icing.Modified from: Royal Icing II
