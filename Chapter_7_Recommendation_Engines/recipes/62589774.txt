Title:
Nancy's Grandma's Kasha (Chicken Wings & Rice) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Roaster
3 cups chicken broth
4 chicken wings
2 large carrots cut
2 large celery cut
small whole onion with the skin
1 tsp salt
pepper
1/2 tst sweet papricka

Directions:
Add from broth to papricka - cook 1 hour
take out chicken wings
add 1 cup long grain rice - cook until broth absorbed 35 minutes - add chicken meat from wings
Take out cooked roaster - drizzle off excess fat from bottom of pan
Pour rice mixture into roasting pan - let sit several minutes
Then mix to get brownings and serve
