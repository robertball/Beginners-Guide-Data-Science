Title:
Hungarian Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar (1 brown, 1 white)
2 sticks butter, softened
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 cups flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease 9 x 13 pan and set aside.
Mix together first 5 ingredients until it forms crumbs.  Set aside one cup for topping.  Mix the remaining ingredients with the dry mixture until smooth.
Pour into greased pan and add crumb mixture to top.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.
Bake for 45-50 minutes or until tests done.  Is best when served warm out of the oven.

Submitter: 
Camille
Description: 
This is my Mom's recipe and one of my favorites for breakfast...love it warm out of the oven.
