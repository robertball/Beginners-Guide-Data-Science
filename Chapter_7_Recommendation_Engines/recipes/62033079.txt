Title:
Summer Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 ounce package spinach (8-10 cups)
1 11 ounce can mandarin oranges, drained
1 cup sliced strawberries
1/2 cup blueberries
1/4 cup sliced toasted almonds
Poppy Seed Dressing
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 tsp salt
1 Tbls poppy seeds

Directions:
Wash spinach if it hasn't been prewashed and tear into small pieces.
Use a large salad bowl and combine spinach, mandarin oranges, strawberries, blueberries and almonds. Top with Poppy Seed Dressing and toss.
Poppy Seed Dressing
Combine olive oil, sugar, vinegar, onion and salt in a blender.  Cover and blend until smooth.  Add poppy seeds and blend for a just a few seconds more.  Makes 1+ cups.  Serve over spinach salad.
