Title:
Tito's Red, White & Boozy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/8268189.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Tito's Handmade Vodka
3 ounces sparkling water
2 ounces cranberry juice
Blueberries to garnish

Directions:
Add Tito's Handmade Vodka and cranberry juice to a glass with ice. Slowly pour sparkling water for a layering effect.
Drop in a few blueberries and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Tito's Handmade Vodka
Description: 
America's Original Craft Vodka, a splash of color, fresh blueberries, all topped with bubbles. No matter how you're celebrating the season, raise a Tito's Red, White & Boozy for a summer well spent.
