Title:
Texas-New Mexico Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
1 medium onion, chopped
3-4 cloves garlic, minced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 lb. ground beef (not chili meat)
2 Tbs. ground cumin
1 Tbs. chili powder
1 teaspoon oregano
1 bay leaf
4 cans chicken broth
1 can chopped tomatoes
1 can pinto beans
1 can light red kidney beans
2-3 Tbs. corn meal
Saute the onion, garlic, pepper and meat.  Add the spices to the meat mixture and 1-2 minutes.  Add the chicken broth and tomatoes.  Cook 30 minutes more.  Add the beans and cook 10 minutes more.  Add the corn meal and cook until the chili thickens.

Submitter: 
SHECOOKS2
Description: 
This is a hybrid of two chili recipes and it is wonderful.  I serve it with tortilla chips and cheese.  Makes great nachos!!
