Title:
Chocolate Cookies N Cream Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups half-and-half cream
1/2 cup eggnog
1/4 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 tablespoons vanilla extract
1 pinch salt
6 crushed oreo cookies, plus 3 additional coarsely chopped oreos (separated)

Directions:
Heat the half & half and sugar to a simmer (whisking periodically).
Remove from heat, add the eggnog, heavy cream, salt and vanilla to the mixture, whisk a little more, let it cool & refrigerate overnight, 2-4 hrs is sufficient.
Pour batter into ice cream freezer, add the 6 crushed oreo cookies and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
Five minutes before freezing process is complete, add remaining cookies.

Description: 
Not your regular Cookies N Cream Ice Cream.  I used the Vanilla Ice Cream V recipe to make Chocolate Cookies N Cream Ice Cream and it was absolutely FABULOUS!! The first 6 crushed cookies make it Chocolate Ice Cream, the last 3 cookies make it Cookies N Cream, making it Chocolate Cookies N Cream Ice Cream.Modified from: Vanilla Ice Cream V
