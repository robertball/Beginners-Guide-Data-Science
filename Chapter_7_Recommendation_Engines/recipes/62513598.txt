Title:
da Mooch's Meaty Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/508342.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed unground beef(sirloin/round/tri-tip)
5 pounds lean ground beef (freshly ground chili meat)
3 (10 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 large yellow onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 cup chili powder
1 cup water or beer

Directions:
Place 1 tbsp. olive oil in large pot.  Place cubed beef in pot over medium-high heat and evenly brown/sear cubes. Set aside browned cubes. Brown chili ground beef in same pot. Cook, stirring to crumble, until evenly browned. Drain off grease(optional). Place the pot over medium heat, and add the onion, garlic, green pepper, red pepper and kidney beans. Cook and stir for a few minutes. Stir in the tomato sauce and water/beer, and season with chili powder. Simmer over low heat for 40 minutes uncovered.
