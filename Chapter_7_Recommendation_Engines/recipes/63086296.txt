Title:
Nestle Toll House Chocolate Cookie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked 9-inch deep dish pie pastry
2 large eggs
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar , packed
3/4 cup butter , softened
1 cup Nestle semi-sweet chocolate chip
1 cup chopped walnuts (or your preference)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Beat eggs in large mixer bowl on high speed until foamy.
Beat in flour, granulated sugar and brown sugar. Beat in butter.
Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
Spoon into pie shell.
Bake for 40 minutes or until knife inserted half way between outside edge and center comes out clean.
Cool on wire rack.

Description: 
If you love soft chocolate chip cookies, you'll love this pie. Served warm, this gooey pie brings back childhood memories. If you have kids they will be demanding Santa try it!
