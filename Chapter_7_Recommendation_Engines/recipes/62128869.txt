Title:
Denver Omelet Breakfast Taco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter
1/4 c. chopped ham
1/4 c. chopped bell pepper
1/2 c. sliced or chopped onion
1/2 c. shredded cheese (monterey jack, colbyjack, cheddar)
4 eggs
tortillas
salsa

Directions:
Melt butter in a skillet over medium-low heat, saute bell pepper, ham, and onion. Scramble eggs in a seperate bowl, after bell pepper, onion, and ham are done sauteeing, pour eggs into skillet and scramble until done. Spread cheddar cheese and stir into the egg. Serve on tortillas, with salsa or any other garnish you like.

Submitter: 
Lindsey Watson
Description: 
Great way to start the morning, quick and healthy alternative to an actual omelet when you don't have the time to make one.
