Title:
Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 lb ground beef (can use ground chicken or turkey)
1 4oz can mushrooms, drained
2 tbsp flour
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp paprika
1 tsp salt
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
wide egg noodles, cooked and drained

Directions:
Saute onion and beef until beef is no longer pink and is in small pieces.
Add mushooms, flour, garlic, pepper, paprika, and salt.
Stir well and cook until flour is well mixed in.
Add soup and simmer for 10 minutes.
Just before serving, stir in sour cream.
Serve over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
Beth Mower Dodge
Description: 
Turn a pound of ground beef into a simple to make, creamy stroganoff. This is great served over egg noodles. This was one of my mom's go-to recipes for weeknight dinners when we were kids, and it's still one of my favorites today.
