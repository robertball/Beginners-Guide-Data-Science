Title:
Velveeta Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz.. cream cheese
16 oz. Velveeta, cut into cubes
1 T. lemon juice
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
2 T. green onions, minced
1 to 2 C. chopped pecans
assorted crackers

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and Velveeta with hands.  Incorporate lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and green onions.  Form into ball.  After chilled, roll in pecans and serve with assorted crackers.

Submitter: 
Miss Moneypenny
Description: 
Smooth and simple, this cheese ball sets itself apart from the others.
