Title:
Leslie's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# beef stew meat
1/4 cup oil
1 1/2 cup chopped onions
16 oz canned diced tomatoes
3 Tbls. cornstarch
1 glove (or more) minced garlic
1 Tbls. dried parsley
2 1/2 tsp. salt (watch if also using beef base)
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 bay leaf
1-2 bags baby carrots, cut up
4-5# potatoes
1/2 cup celery
1 can beef broth

Directions:
Brown beef on all sides in hot oil.  Add onions, tomatoes, corn starch(mix into beef broth before adding),garlic, parsley, salt, pepper, and bay leaf.
Bring to boil. Put into larger baking pan, cover and bake at 350* for 1 1/2 hours.  Add carrots, potatoes, and celery, continue baking, covered for 1 hour or until vegs are tender.
