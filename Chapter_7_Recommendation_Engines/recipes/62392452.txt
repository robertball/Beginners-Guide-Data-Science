Title:
Best Ever Chocolate Chip Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large ripe bananas
3/4 c. sugar
1 egg lightly beaten
1/3 c. butter melted
1 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp. soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
6 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 deg.  Mash bananas than add sugar and egg.  Add melted butter to the banana mixture, then stir in flour, soda, baking powder and salt.  Stir in chips.  Fill greased 12 cup muffin tin and bake for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Melissa Burnett Mandrona
Description: 
Great muffins for kids, especially if baked in mini-muffin tins.
