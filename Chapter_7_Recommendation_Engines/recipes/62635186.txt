Title:
Orange Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/606150.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces vodka
1 1/2 ounces triple sec
1 1/2 ounces orange juice

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, and pour in the vodka, triple sec, and orange juice. Shake well, then strain into a chilled martini glass.
