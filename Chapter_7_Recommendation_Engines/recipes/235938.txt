Title:
Chipotle-Infused Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4592886.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle vodka
1 (4 ounce) can chipotle peppers, drained and rinsed

Directions:
Pour vodka into a pitcher with a lid. Wrap peppers in a clean cotton cloth to dry; add to vodka. Allow to sit until vodka changes color, at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Digiman
Description: 
Vodka with a pepper spice and flavor. Great in a bloody mary! The darker the color, the spicier the vodka.
