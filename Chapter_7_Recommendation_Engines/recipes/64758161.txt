Title:
AirFried Mahi Mahi with Brown Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 6oz  Mahi Mahi fillets
Salt and pepper to taste
Canola oil spray
2/3 C butter

Directions:
Preheat an air fryer at 350 degrees. Season The fish with salt and pepper.
Spray each side of the fillets with cooking spray and place into the basket of the fryer making sure to leave space between.
Set timer for 12 minutes.
While fish is cooking, place butter in a small sauce pan over low-medium heat.  Bring to a simmer until butter turns frothy and rich brown. Remove from heat.
The fish will be done when it’s flaked easily with a fork and has a golden hue.
Place fish on a plate and drizzle with butter.

This is a super simple but super tasty fish dish.  You’ll want to check your fish halfway through to gauge how much more time you’ll need to keep cooking, depending on the thickness of your fish.
