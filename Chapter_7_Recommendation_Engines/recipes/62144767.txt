Title:
Yellow Rice with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups veggie broth
1 1/2 cups uncooked rice
1/2 cup chopped broccoli florets
1 carrot, diced
1/4 cup diced red pepper
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 packet Goya Sazón with Coriander and Annatto
sprinkle of Goya Adobo with pepper
1 tsp vegetable oil

Directions:
In medium saucepan, heat oil.
Add all veggies and garlic.
Cook and stir with fork until garlic is done.
Add veggie broth and heat to boiling.
Add rice, Goya Sazón with Coriander and Annatto and sprinkle of Goya Adobo with pepper (if you don't have Adobo add a sprinkle of salt and pepper).
Stir well and bring to a boil.
Cover, reduce heat to low and cook for 25 minutes, until rice is tender and all water is absorbed.
Fluff with fork and serve.

Submitter: 
Angie Duffy Hernandez
Description: 
Spanish yellow rice with fresh veggies that everyone loves.
