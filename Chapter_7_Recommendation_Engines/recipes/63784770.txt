Title:
Taco Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp chili powder
1 1/2 tsp paprika
1 1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp onion powder
3/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp sea salt
dash cayenne pepper

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together.  Quantity is equivalent to one package taco seasoning.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
Add to one pound of ground meat to make tacos or shredded meat for fajitas.
