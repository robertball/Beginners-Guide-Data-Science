Title:
Winter Pasta with Brown Butter, Squash and Arugula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound dried long flat pasta (linguini)
4 cups of arugula, washed
1 butternut squash, peeled, seeded cut into 1 inch pieces
1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup pine nuts
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Places cubed squash on a cookie sheet and drizzle with olive oil, 1/2 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper. Roast in oven for 30 minutes until soft and browned, keep warm.
In a large pot, cook pasta per package directions and don't forget to salt water.
Once cooked, drain pasta into colander reserving 1 cup of the pasta water.
In the same pot, over medium heat, melt butter and cook until bubbly and slightly brown.
Add garlic and pine nuts and saute for 2 minutes.
Add cooked pasta, arugula, cheese, pepper and enough reserved pasta water to combine the mixture and melt the cheese.
Transfer pasta to plates and top with roasted squash and more parmesan cheese.
