Title:
Jalapeno Bacon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•2 jalapeños
•16 ounces cream cheese
•6 strips bacon, cooked and chopped
•1-1/4 cups shredded Parmesan cheese, divided use
•1/2 cup sour cream
•1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
•1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
•Tortilla or potato chips, for serving

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2.Chop the jalapeños, leaving in seeds and membranes as desired to adjust heat level.
3.In a food processor add the chopped jalapeños, cream cheese, 1 cup Parmesan cheese, sour cream, bacon (reserve a tablespoon for garnish, if desired), smoked paprika and garlic powder. Process until well combined.
4.Pour into a baking dish. Garnish with remaining Parmesan cheese and bacon.
5.Bake for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve warm with chips.
