Title:
"Mexican" Baked Bean Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can of your favorite baked beans
1 tbls of fresh cilantro chopped
2 green onions sliced thin
1 tsp of ground cumin
1 tsp of chili pepper (optional)
cilantro leaves for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a heavy pan.  Cook on high until boiling.  Lower heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes covered.  Garnish with cilantro and serve.

Submitter: 
Melissa Rapoza Fragoza
Description: 
Here is a quick and easy side dish that uses already prepared baked beans.  Serve this with enchiladas, tacos or your favorite mexican dish for a complete meal.
