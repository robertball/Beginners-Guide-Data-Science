Title:
Spicy Garlic Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cup Franks Red Hot Sauce (I started with one cup and added more at the end to fill up blender)
1 Stick Butter, melted
3 TBS Minced Garlic (I think this could take one more TBS)

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in bullet blender.
