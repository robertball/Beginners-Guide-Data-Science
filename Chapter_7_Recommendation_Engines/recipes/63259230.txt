Title:
Buttermilk Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups white sugar
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup butter
2 tablespoons corn syrup
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
In a saucepan, stir together the sugar, buttermilk, butter, corn syrup, and baking soda. Bring to a boil, and cook for 7 minutes. Remove from the heat, and stir in the vanilla.

Submitter: 
Anita Newhart
Description: 
This is a great homemade syrup that we found at a little B&B; in South Dakota.  This syrup is wonderful on hot, fresh blueberry pancakes.Modified from: Buttermilk Syrup
