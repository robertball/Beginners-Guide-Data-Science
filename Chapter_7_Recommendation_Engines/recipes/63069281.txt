Title:
Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter
1/2 c. shortening
3 c. granulated sugar
5 eggs
3 c. all purpose flour
1/2 c. cocoa
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 c. buttermilk
1 T. red food color
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream fats and sugar in mixer.  Add eggs, mixing after each.
Combine in a separate bowl the flour, cocoa, leavening agents, and salt.
Mix red food color, vanilla and buttermilk together.  Add dry and wet ingredients to mixer alternately by fourths.
Pour into three wax paper lined 8 inch round heavy baking pans.
Bake 40-45 minutes at 325 degrees.
