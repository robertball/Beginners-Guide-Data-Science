Title:
Cream Cheese Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. Idaho potatoes, peeled, chopped
1 pint heavy cream, hot
1 stick butter, soft
1 8oz. block cream cheese
Salt and pepper as needed

Directions:
Boil the potatoes in salted water until fork tender. Remove and drain.
In a mixer, or using a potato masher, add half the heavy cream, all the butter and cream cheese.
Mix until smooth adding more cream until the desired consistency is achieved.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
sd061507
Description: 
I love these mashed potatoes and make them for Thanksgiving every year. Just a tip, using a hand mixer I mix the butter and cream cheese in first and then add the cream a little at a time until I get the consistency I want.
