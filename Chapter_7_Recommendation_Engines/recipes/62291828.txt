Title:
Easy Whoopie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake -
1 (18.25 ounce) package Devil's Food cake mix (make per directions EXCEPT only use 3/4 cup of water).
1 small package of Jello Devils Food instant pudding mix
Filling -
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/4 cup confectioners sugar
1 tsp vanilla
Dash of almond extract
2 tbsp milk
1 cup marshmallow fluff (Add more fluff to make it thicker)

Directions:
To make the cakes: Prepare mix as directed on package, use same amount of oil and eggs - but ONLY use 3/4 cup of water. Blend cake ingredients together and beat 1 minute on medium speed. Add Jello Devils Food Instant Pudding mix and beat for 1-2 more minutes on medium speed. This should make your batter thicker. Spoon onto greased cookie sheet in 2 inch dabs (circles).
Bake for 10-12 minutes at 350 or until toothpick inserted in the center of a cake comes out clean.
Let cakes cool before adding filling.
To make the filling: Blend all ingredients together, beating until desired thickness and texture (2-3 minute). Recipe is great doubled.
Turn the cakes over to the flat side and spoon desired amount of filling (recipe makes appx. kitchen tablespoon per sandwich) onto the flat side of the cake. Place another cake's flat side on top of the filling to make a sandwich.

Submitter: 
Allison W.
Description: 
Small chocolate cakes (cookie size) with fluffy, creamy icing in the middle. This easy dessert tastes like it takes a long time. Perfect as gifts, for parties (individual desserts with no cutting), or to win over coworkers. Taste even better 2nd day so can easily be made the night before a big event.Modified from: Whoopie Pies
