Title:
QUICK POT PIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix 1/2 cup of Bisquick,
1/2 Cup of Milk,
2 eggs together for a base
any filling you want

Directions:
Top with about 1/4 cup of any "fillings" you want. - Cheeseburger, Pizza, BLT, Chicken and Veggies, etc.... You can mix it up in the pan (any meat should already be cooked)
Put about 1 T. of mixture in each muffin cup
Then top with one more tablespoon of the "Bisquick Mixture" - and bake at 375 for 25-30.

Submitter: 
Julie Jane Anderson
Description: 
It's a great way to get rid of your leftovers! These can also be frozen!
Just pop them into the microwave for a quick meal on the go!
Recipe from Betty Crocker.
