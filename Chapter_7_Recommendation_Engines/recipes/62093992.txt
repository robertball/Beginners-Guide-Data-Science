Title:
Cherries in the Snow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cherry pie filling
1 angel food cake (store-bought)
1 8 oz. package cream cheese, softened
1 tub cool whip
1/2 c white sugar

Directions:
Cut the angel food cake into bite sized pieces, divide in half.  Mix with an electric mixer (for about 10 minutes) together cool whip, cream cheese and sugar until light and fluffy - no lumps.  Place ingredients in the bottom of trifle dish or other glass serving bowl in the following order: 1/2 of the cut-up angel food cake, 1/2 of the cool whip mixture, 1 can of cherry pie filling.  Repeat.
Serve immediately or allow to chill in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Nicole M.
Description: 
This is an easy to make, very impressive looking summer dessert!  Looks beautiful layered in a trifle dish - great for company or just for fun.
