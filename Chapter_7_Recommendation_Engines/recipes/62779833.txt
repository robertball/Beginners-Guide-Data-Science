Title:
Mozzerala  & Herb Stuffed Chicken Breast - Sous Vide Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/646963.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breast halves
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
1/2 medium onion, diced
4 ounces sliced mushrooms
1 rib celery, diced
1 egg, beaten
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons fine dry breadcrumbs
2 teaspoons grated parmassan cheese
3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Cajun Seasoning

Directions:
Flatten breasts to a suitable thickness for rolling
Saut√© onions and celery in butter and oil with seasoning.
Combine all ingredients sans chicken in food processor...process until mixture is a paste.
Put paste on breast and roll, use toothpicks or string to maintain roll
Smother breasts with remaining paste and season breasts lightly with Cajun Seasoning
Vacuum seal individual breasts
Cook 2 hours in 141 degree Fahrenheit water bath
Serve immediately with side dishes of your choice

Moist and tender chicken breast stuffed with a mixture of cheeses and herbs
