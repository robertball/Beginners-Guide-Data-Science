Title:
Weenie Water Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart of water
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup of chives
2 tbsp fresh celantro
1 medium sized carrot, diced
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 pkg beef hot dogs

Directions:
In 1 quart of water, boil a package of hot dogs (preferably all beef). When complete remove hot dogs and place pot of remaining water on stove - low simmer.
Add all other ingredients stir well and let simmer for 15 minutes. Ready to serve!

Submitter: 
Cookin' Canuck
Description: 
Don't throw out that weenie water - make a light and tasty soup! All the flavour of hot dogs in a soup!
