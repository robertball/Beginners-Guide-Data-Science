Title:
Ricotta and Red Pepper Crostini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Slices Baguette
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 clove of garlic cut lengthwise
1/2 tsp each salt and pepper
1 cup ricotta cheese
1-1/2 cups of thinly sliced roasted red peppers
1 small clove of garlic minced
2 tbsp chopped Italian parsley
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
Fresh small basil leaves

Directions:
Brush baguette with 2 tbsp of the oil.  Toast or grill baguette slices until golden and crisp.  Rub garlic over baguette while still warm.  Sprinkle with half each of the salt and pepper; set aside.
In bowl combine roasted peppers, minced garlic, parsley, vinegar and remaining oil, salt and pepper.
Spread ricotta over toasted baguette and top with peppers and basil.

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
Creamy ricotta cheese is the background for sweet roasted peppers.  Add some peppery arugula for a different flavour and a salute to the Italian Flag.  Great appetizer for parties.
