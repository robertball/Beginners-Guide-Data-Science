Title:
Pineapple Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz package coleslaw mix
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/3 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
3/4 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 small (4-6 oz)can pineapple juice

Directions:
Combine the coleslaw, onion and celery in a large bowl.
In a separate bowl, combine remaining ingredients; mix until smooth.
Pour mixture over the coleslaw, onion and celery.
Stir well and referigerate at least 2 hours.
Taste and add aditional sugar or pineapple juice if needed.

Submitter: 
atomicdogs
Description: 
My best attempt at copying a popular local restaraunts recipe. They put the dressing on just before serving , so it stays crunchy. I like the flavors to blend.
