Title:
Sad Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Bisquik
1 box light brown sugar
4 eggs
3/4 cup oil
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup coconut

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together; pour into a greased and floured 9x13 inch pan.  Bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes to one hour.
