Title:
Freezer Bulk Pumpkin Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 1/2 cups pumpkin puree
3 cup evaporated milk
2 cup white sugar
1 1/2 cup self-rising flour
6 eggs
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup butter
1T pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine the pumpkin, evaporated milk, sugar, flour, eggs, vanilla, melted butter and ground cinnamon to taste. Spoon into 4 8x8 pans.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
