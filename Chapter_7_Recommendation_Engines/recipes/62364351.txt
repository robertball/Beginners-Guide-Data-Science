Title:
Piri-Piri Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade;Favorite prepared hot sauce,garlic powder,chopped onion,ginger,lemon juice,vegetable oil,salt,pepper and chopped cilantro.
Chicken quarters,halves or pieces,as desired
Glaze;freshly prepared Marinade and melted butter,combined.

Directions:
Marinade chicken for two hours prior to cooking. Cook on grill over indirect heat. Baste with glaze often while grilling.
