Title:
Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz cream cheese
1/4tsp accent
1/4tsp onion powder
1/4tsp lawry's salt
5oz chopped budding ham or beef
1/4C scallions

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Form into a ball. Save some ham or beef and the scallions to roll ball in.
