Title:
Really Quick Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 22-oz. can cling peaches, heavy syrup
1/2 stick butter/margarine
2 tbsp. flour
3/4 cup sugar
2 frozen pie crusts
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together sugar, flour, nutmeg and cinnamon.
Pour peaches in bottom of glass pan, 8x8 square or 13x9 rectangular, your choice.
Sprinkle dry mix throughout peaches.  Mix well.
Cut up 1/4 stick butter/margarine all over mixture.
Cut thawed pie crusts into 1-inch strips.
Lay criss-cross or striped over peach mixture.
Sprinkle sugar, nutmeg and cinnamon over crust strips.
Cut up remainder of butter/margarine over strips.
Bake for 30-40 minutes or until crust is golden, crisp brown.
