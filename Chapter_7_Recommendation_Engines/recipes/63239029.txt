Title:
Baked Kale Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844329.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Cups Kale
1 Teaspoon Oil
Desired Seasonings (Salt, Pepper, Garlic Salt, etc.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
Rinse, and dry Kale leaves.
Remove the inner stem from each leaf, slice into strips, and then again into 1" chunks.
Lay your greens onto a baking sheet, drizzle with oil, and sprinkle desired seasonings.
Bake at 300 degrees for 15 minutes, or until dry and crisp.

Description: 
Extremely healthy option to your every day crunchy chip.
