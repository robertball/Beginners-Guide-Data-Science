Title:
Cajun Chicken Caesar Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323063.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 pound bacon
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips
1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
1 head romaine lettuce- rinsed, dried and chopped
1/2 cup Caesar salad dressing
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Crumble and set aside.
Spray baking sheet with non-stick cooking spray. Sprinkle both sides of chicken breasts with Cajun seasoning. Broil 6 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Slice and set aside.
In a salad bowl, combine Romaine, enough salad dressing to coat, Parmesan cheese and bacon. Toss and place on individual salad plates. Top with the sliced chicken and serve.
