Title:
Hawaiian Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/612948.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5c whole wheat flour
1/2c wheat bran
1/2c evaporated cane juice
1/2c unsweetened shredded coconut plus some to top muffins
1t baking soda
1t baking powder
1/2c melted coconut oil
1/4c applesauce
3 beaten eggs
1 20 oz can crushed pineapple

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Mix together flour, bran, cane juice, shredded coconut, baking soda, and baking powder.  In a separate bowl, mix the remaining ingredients.  Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just moistened.  Spoon batter into a prepared muffin tin. Top muffins with some extra shredded coconut.
Bake for about 15 minutes, or when a toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
CrunchySarah
Description: 
I was looking for a healthy pineapple muffin, but couldn't find anything so I decided to just make something up.  These bran muffins are low-sugar and dairy-free.  My family loved them so much the whole batch was gone in one day!
