Title:
Ann Romney's M & M Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2645465.jpg
Ingredients:
2  cups granulated sugar
2  cup light brown sugar, packed
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
6 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups crunchy peanut butter
1/4 cup light corn syrup
9 cups old fashioned oats
4  teaspoons baking soda
12  oz. chocolate chips
19 oz. package M&M;'s

Directions:
Heat oven to 325°.
In a large bowl, cream sugars and butter.
Beat in eggs 1 at a time until light and frothy.
Add vanilla extract, peanut butter, and corn syrup. Add baking soda and mix well.
Gradually stir in oatmeal. Add chocolate chips and M & M's.
Using a standard-size ice cream scoop, drop dough onto greased baking sheets (2 or 3 inches apart).
Bake at 325° for 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
Cool 2 minutes, then transfer cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.
