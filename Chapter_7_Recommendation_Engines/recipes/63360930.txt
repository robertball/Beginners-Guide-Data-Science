Title:
Tropical Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/895198.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups peach-mango juice, divided
1 (15 oz) can cream of coconut (not coconut milk)
5 cups pineapple juice
1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice (about 3-4 limes)
1 (1 L) bottle of Sprite

Directions:
Combine 3 cups of peach-mango juice with the can of cream of coconut in the blender. Blend until completely smooth.
Combine blended mixture with all other ingredients, except for the Sprite, in a serving bowl or pitcher. When ready to serve, add Sprite and serve over ice. Garnish with slices of lime, coconut, and/or pineapple if desired.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Perfect for tropical-themed parties. You can freeze the leftovers and then allow the drink to partially thaw and blend it for a slushy treat.
