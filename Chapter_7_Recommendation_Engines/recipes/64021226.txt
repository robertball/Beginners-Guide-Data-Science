Title:
Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box instant vanilla pudding (3 1/4 oz.)
8 oz. cream cheese - softened
1/2 tsp. vanilla
graham cracker crust

Directions:
Prepare instant pudding as directed.
Mix in cream cheese until smooth then add vanilla.
Pour into crust then refrigerate at least 2 hours.
