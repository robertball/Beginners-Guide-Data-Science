Title:
Focaccia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups bread flour
1 cup water @ 80 degrees
1 TBS. sugar
1 TBS. olive oil, plus additional for brushing
2-1/4 tsp. red star active dry yeast
1 tsp. salt
Suggested toppings of: sun-dried tomatoes, grilled bell pepper slices, sauteed onion rings, fresh and dried herbs, grated hard cheese

Directions:
Place ingredients, except toppings, in pan in order specified by owner's manual.  Select dough/manual cycle.  Check dough consistancy after 5 minutes of kneading making adjustments, if necessary.
Place dough in lightly oiled bowl and turn to grease top.  Cover; let rise until indentation remains after touching.  Turn dough onto lightly floured surface, punch down to remove air bubbles.  Shape dough into a ball; place on greased cookie sheet.  Flatten to 14" circle.  With knife, cut circle in dough about 1" from edge, cutting almost through to cookie sheet.  Pierce center with fork.  Cover; let rise about 15minutes.  Brush with oil and sprinkle with desired toppings.  Bake in preheated oven 375 for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.  Remove from cookie sheet to cool.
