Title:
ham gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cup ham dripping(must be baked in water)
1 cup flour
1 1/2 cups warm water
pepper to taste

Directions:
pour 3 cups liquid in saucepan on med heat
mix flour and water and pepper until thick and smooth
turn heat to low slowly add flour mixture to pan
wisk until smooth and thick
