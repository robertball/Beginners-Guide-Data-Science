Title:
Harvest Apple Cake With Caramel Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake Ingredients:
2 Eggs
1 ½ Cups Sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla
1 teaspoon Salt
1 ½ teaspoons Baking Soda
½ Cup Shortening
½ Cup Water
2 Cups Flour
1 teaspoon Cinnamon
4 Cups Apples (peeled and diced)
½ Cup Chopped Walnuts
Frosting Ingredients:
½ Cup Butter
1 Cup Brown Sugar (packed)
¼ teaspoon Salt
¼ Cup Milk
2 Cups Confectioners Sugar

Directions:
Cake Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Mix first 6 ingredients well.  Add flour, water and cinnamon; beat well.  Fold in chopped apples and nuts.  Pour into prepared 13 x 9 inch pan.  Bake.  Test by inserting toothpick in center until it comes clean. About 50 – 55 minutes.  Cool completely on wire rack.
Frosting Directions:
Melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat.  Stir in the brown sugar, salt and milk.  Bring to a boil over medium heat and boil for 2 minutes; stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and allow to cool until lukewarm.  Stir in confectioners sugar, beat until smooth.  If frosting is too thick; add a bit more milk.

Submitter: 
Randy
Description: 
This moist delicious cake is perfect for fall get togethers.  An abundance of apples and cinnamon topped off with a sweet caramel frosting.  Makes a perfect ending to a delicious meal on a cool fall day.
