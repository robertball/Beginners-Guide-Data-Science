Title:
Sicilian Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken cut up into 8 pieces.
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp oregano
cooking spray

Directions:
preheat oven to 425F
spray 9 x 13 inch baking pan with cooking spray
place chicken parts in pan
sprinkle , salt, papper, paprika, garlic powder and oregano on both sides of chicken.
Bake 1hr or until juices run clear when pierced and chicken is nicely browned.
