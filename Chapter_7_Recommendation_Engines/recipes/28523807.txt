Title:
STUFFED PORK CHOPS WITH CRANBERRIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops 1 in thick
1 cup sun dried cranberries
2 tablespoons pine nuts,toasted
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
2 tablespoon brown sugar
Salt
4 cups chicken stock
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 cup red wine
1/4 cup cranberry juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400deg.
Cut pocket into side of pork chop 3/4 way through.
Season with salt and pepper. Stuff with cranberry stuffing. Recipe below
Heat 2 tablespoons of olive in pan. Place chops in oil and sear. Place chops and 1 cup of sauce, recipe below in oven. roast until  meat is firm to touch, 8-10 minutes.
Spoon cranberry sauce sauce on plate. Place chops on top, garnish with pine nuts and dried cranberries.
