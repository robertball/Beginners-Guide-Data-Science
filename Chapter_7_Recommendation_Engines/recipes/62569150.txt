Title:
Easy Spumoni Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14oz.) can sweetened condensed milk, divided
1 1/2 C. dark chocolate chips
3/4 C. shelled pistachio's, chopped
1 1/2 C. white chocolate chips
3/4 C. cherry flavored dried cranberries
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
Line bottom of 8" square pan with parchment or wax paper. Set aside.
In small pot over low heat, combine half of the condensed milk with all the dark chocolate chips.Cook until melted, stirring often.
Remove from heat and stir in pistachios. Spread evenly in prepared pan. Refrigerate until set, about 15 minutes.
Meanwhile, melt white chocolate over low heat with remaining condensed milk, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and stir in cranberries, vanilla, and salt.
Pour over dark chocolate layer. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Cut into squares.
