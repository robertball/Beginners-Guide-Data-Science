Title:
Chicken with Tomatillo Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lbs Chicken
1/4 c oil
lime juice
1 T garlic
cumin
1 1/2 t kosher salt
1/2 t pepper
Tomatillo Salsa
2 1/2 thick slices onions
5 tomatillos
1 ear of corn
1 poblano
1/4 c cilantro
1/2 t sugar
1/2 c sourcream
extra lime and cilantro

Directions:
Reserve 2T oil and 2T lime juice-mix remaining marinaid ingredients. Marinade chicken for 2 hours. Grill. Mix reserved with sour cream.
Oil corn, onions, pepper and grill-cut corn off cob-process all but corn in food processor. Add corn and serve with chicken.
