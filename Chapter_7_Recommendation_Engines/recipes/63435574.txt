Title:
Shortbread Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups butter at room temperature
1 cup sugar
4 cups plus 2 T flour

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl with your hands until completely mixed.
Put mixture into a greased (with butter)13x9 inch pan.
Cover with waxed paper and press firmly into the pan.
Prick dough with a fork through the waxed paper and then remove waxed paper.
Bake at 275 for 1 1/2 hours.
Turn off oven and remove pan from oven. Cut shortbread into 1 inch squares. Return pan to oven and leave until oven is cool.
Sprinkle shortbread squares with colored sugar, remove from pan and store in airtight containers.

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
My favorite Christmas cookie!  They absolutely melt in your mouth!
