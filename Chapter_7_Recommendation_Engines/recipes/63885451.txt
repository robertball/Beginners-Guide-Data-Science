Title:
Glaze Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1096026.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. powdered sugar
1/2 cup corn syrup
3 tsp or 1 T almond extract
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together on lowest mixer setting. Mix well for at least one minute.

Description: 
Great recipe for icing sugar cookies.
