Title:
Taco Dip - Plain OR Fancy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-oz. pkg regular cream cheese
1 8-oz. pkg. neufchatel cream cheese
1 cup chunky salsa (mild, medium or spicy - your choice)
1/4 cup sour cream
IF MAKING FANCY, ADD THE FOLLOWING TO INGREDIENTS:
2 c. chunky salsa
4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
4 cups finely shredded lettuce
1 c. finely chopped, or sliced, black olives

Directions:
Place both blocks of cream cheese into mixing bowl and whip on high speed until blended.  Add sour cream and salsa.  Mix on low until well blended.  Mix on medium one minute.  Whip on high until fluffy (approximately 2 minutes).  Serve with tortilla chips or wheat thins type crackers.
FANCY VERSION:
Spread above mixture into 8 x 12 pan or on large pizza pan. Top with remaining ingredients in the following order:  2 c. salsa, lettuce, cheese, black olives.
Serve immediately or refrigerate no more than 6 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Two recipes in one!  This recipe can satisfy any party need.  Dress it up, or dress it down.  Top it off, or serve it up light and fluffy.  You choose the venue!
