Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies IX/CHOCOLATE AND WHITE VERSION Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819754.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup peanut butter
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup water
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup whole wheat flour
2/3 cup cocoa.
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, white sugar and peanut butter until smooth. Beat in the egg and vanilla. Combine the flour, wheat flour, baking soda, cocoa and salt; stir into the peanut butter mixture- add water.  Finally, mix in the oats and white chocolate chips. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake for 8 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are golden. Remove from cookie sheets to cool on wire racks.
