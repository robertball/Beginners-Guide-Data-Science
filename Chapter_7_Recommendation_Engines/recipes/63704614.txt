Title:
Taco Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030790.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
8 oz small dry pasta
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 cup salsa
1 packet low-sodium taco seasoning
3 oz cream cheese
4 oz Greek yogurt
Pepper
Shredded cheese for topping

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package.
Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water and set aside,
Cook turkey and drain.
Add in garlic, onion, salsa, taco seasoning to turkey.
Stir turkey mixture into pasta along with cream cheese and Greek yogurt.
Season with pepper to taste.
Top with shredded cheese if desired.
