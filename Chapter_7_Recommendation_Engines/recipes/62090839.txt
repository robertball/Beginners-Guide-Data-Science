Title:
Grilled Bruschette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 loaf of good crusty french bread.
2. 4-6 T. of Pesto
3. olive oil, to drizzle.
4. 2 chopped roma tomatos
5. 5 blend italian cheese.

Directions:
1st, take the loaf and cut it in to 3 pieces (could be more depending on the size of the bread.
2. Then cut the bread in half the long way.
3. Drizzle olive oil lightly on both sides of bread.
4. Place bread on grill crust side up.
5. flip in about 4 min. long enough to get nice grill marks.
6.  after flipping, spread pesto on each piece of bread.
7. top with tomatos and cheese.
8. grill until cheese is melted, takes about 4-6 min.
9.  Enjoy! be careful though it will be hot!

Submitter: 
Jenna
Description: 
This grilled bruschette is a great twist off the original.  It is also super simple!
