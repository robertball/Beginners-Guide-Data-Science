Title:
Panettone Italino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005755.jpg
Ingredients:
7 grams instant yeast
230 grams lukewarm water
100 grams white sugar
425 grams ,bread flour or white flour
125 grams white whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon salt
23 grams non-fat dry milk powder(1/3 cup)
130 grams sour cream
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon lemon oil
1 tbsp. vegetable oil
50 grams dark raisins
50 grams golden raisins
50 grams collocate
1/4 cup, orange juice
1 teaspoon orange liqueur

Directions:
In a medium bowl: combine yeast, water and sugar and cover and let it sand for 10 to 15 minutes ,or until foamy.
In a small jar : and the collocate,orange liqueur or orange juice and let it stand until ready.
In a bowl of a stand mixer: with a flat beater add the flours, dry powder milk, and salt until dough is mixed, Now pour in the yeast mix and mix with a dough hook on medium low #2, until just mixed for 5 to 8 minutes (you may need more flour) then add the eggs, sour cream, pure vanilla extract, lemon oil, orange or lemon zest and mix.
Place dough in a large, lightly greased bowl and cover with plastic wrap, and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 2 hours.
Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and Punch down dough gently and knead the fruits for 5 to 10 minutes, until dough is soft and pliable, but not sticky, Form dough into a ball, place in prepared greased Bundt pan, cover loosely with dish towel, and let rise 2 hours more. (Loaf will rise above the pan sides.)
Brush with melted butter, if desired.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. and Bake for 35 to 45 minutes or until loaf is golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
