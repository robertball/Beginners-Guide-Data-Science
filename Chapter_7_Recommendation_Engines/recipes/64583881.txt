Title:
Caprese Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3765263.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts (@4 breasts.)
Kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1/4 c balsamic vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
2 tbsp shredded basil
4 slices mozzarella

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil.
Liberally season chicken with salt and pepper and cook until golden and cooked through, @ 6 minutes per side. Transfer chicken to plate.
Add balsamic vinegar to skillet to deglaze, then add garlic and stir til fragrant, @ 1 minute.
Add tomatoes and season with salt. Simmer until soft, 5-7 minutes.
Stir in basil.
Return chicken to skillet and nestle amongst tomatoes. Top with a slice of mozzarella and cover with lid to melt.
Spoon tomatoes over chicken and serve.
