Title:
1865 Cafe's Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1lb box pasta (elbows, shells, rotini)
10 boiled eggs, sliced or diced
1 onion, diced really small
10 chops off head of celery, diced small
3-4 cups salad dressing
1 cup coleslaw dressing
1 cup sugar
salt & pepper & a bit of garlic
1/4 c chopped chives (optional)

Directions:
Boil pasta, drain & rinse with cool water.  Add eggs, onion, and celery.  Mix the remaining ingredients.  Add to pasta mixture.  Chill & serve.  We quadroupled this recipe to serve at my wedding and added chopped cooked chicken and diced cheese.  What a hit!

Submitter: 
Torrerizor
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by one of our cooks before she moved to PE.  Our cafe still serves it today!
