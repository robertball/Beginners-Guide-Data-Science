Title:
Seafood Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/483160.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 oz.) container sour cream
3 green onions, chopped
1 tsp. lemon juice
1/4 cup barbeque sauce
couple drops of Tobasco, optional
1 1/2 lb. imitation crabmeat, chopped

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and sour cream together until smooth.
Stir in green onions, lemon juice, barbeque sauce and Tobasco.
Add imitation crabmeat and gently fold in.
Refrigerate for a couple hours before serving.

Submitter: 
wannabe chefette
Description: 
This has won the favorite dip contest many times by family and friends.  It's great with crackers or cut up veggies.
