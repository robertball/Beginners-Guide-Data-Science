Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1/2 cup diced red onion
1 1/2 cups chopped carrots
1 cup sliced celery
1/4 t basil
1 t parsley
6 cups no salt added chicken broth
20 small Italian meatballs, preferably home made
1/2 pastina
6 ounces baby spinach
4 t grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Press saute and add the oil to the pot
When hot add the onion, celery and carrots and saute until tender, 4 or 5 minutes.
Add the pepper, basil and parsley, stir
Add the chicken broth and meatballs,  stir
Place the lid on the pot, close the valve and press soup/stew. Adjust the time to 6 minutes.
When the timer goes off do a quick release, this will take about 3 minutes.
Add the pasta and stir.
Place the lid back on the pot, close the valve and press soup. Adjust the time to 3 minutes.
Do a quick release when the timer goes off, this will take about 3 minutes. Add the spinach and stir.
Allow this to sit for 5 minutes, the pasta will be fully cooked and the spinach will wilt.
Taste and add salt if needed.
Ladle into bowls, top with the cheese and serve.
