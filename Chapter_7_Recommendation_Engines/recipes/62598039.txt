Title:
Grape Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c broccoli florets
3/4c halved seedless red grapes
1/3c chopped celery
1/3c chopped green onions
1/3c sliced water chestnuts
1/4c raisins
1/2c fat free plain yogurt
2T low fat mayonnaise
1t honey

Directions:
Combine yogurt, mayonnaise, and honey.
Pour over other ingredients and combine.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
