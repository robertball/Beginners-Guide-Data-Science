Title:
Easy Sweet N Sour Kielbasa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 2 lbs. kielbasa, cut into 3/4 inch diagonal slices
1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
1 jar (about 12 oz.) plain yellow mustard

Directions:
Place kielbasa in crockpot and cover with brown sugar and mustard.
Set slow-cooker on low and cook for between 1 1/2 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Chunks of kielbasa in a sweet and tangy sauce - no one will believe this recipe is so simple!  Can also be cooked in the oven by baking for about 40 minutes at 350 degrees, stirring occasionally.
