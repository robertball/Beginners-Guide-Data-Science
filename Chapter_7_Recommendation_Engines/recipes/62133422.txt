Title:
(Slightly) More Figure Friendly Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb frozen shredded hashed brown potatoes
1/2 C melted butter
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 C chopped red bell pepper
1/2 C chopped orange bell pepper
1/2 C chopped yellow bell pepper
1 C chopped broccoli
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
10 oz can cream of chicken soup
1 C  fat free sour cream
2 C 2% shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350.
2.  Combine potatoes, butter, onions, peppers, broccoli, salt, and pepper in bowl.  Mix well.
3.   Add soup, sour cream, and cheese.  Mix well.
4.   Pour into 9X13 baking dish (spray w/non stick cooking spray, first).
5.  Bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
lovestohost
Description: 
I love my aunt's Cheesy Potatoes, but they are such a high calorie investment for a low nutrient return. I played around with her recipe and came up with a lighter, more nutrient dense,  happy medium.
