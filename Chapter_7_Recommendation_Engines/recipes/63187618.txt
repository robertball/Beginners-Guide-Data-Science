Title:
Garlic Ginger Tofu with Veggie Stir Fry over Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons canola oil
3 tablespoons minced garlic
3 tablespoons grated fresh ginger root
1/2 juice of a lemon
3 tablespoon tamari or soy sauce, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 pounds extra firm tofu diced

Directions:
Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Stir in garlic and ginger, and cook for 1 minute. Add tofu to the pan with tamari, and stir to coat. Cook on high heat until Tofu has a nice light brown seared color to it.
Sample to test flavors.  If desired, add more garlic and/or ginger.
Add Stir Fry veggies. (I used Costco Brand that I defrosted in the microwave first).
Serve over White or Brown Steamed Rice

Submitter:

Description: 
Tofu simmered in its own juices and seasoned with garlic, fresh ginger, and a little Lemon juice - this pairs well with steamed rice and veggies to make an easy weeknight meal.Modified from: Garlic Ginger Tofu
