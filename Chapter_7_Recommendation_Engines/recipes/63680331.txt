Title:
Cool Cucumber Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, peeled if desired
1/2c. fresh cilantro leaves
1/2 small pinapple, peeled & halved
1c. crushed ice

Directions:
Process cucumbers, cilantro, & pineapple through Juice Fountain™ using speeds 4 & 5.
Scoop ice into 4 glasses, pour in juice, mix well to combine. Serve Immediately.

Submitter:

Description:
