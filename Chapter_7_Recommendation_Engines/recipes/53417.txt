Title:
Chili Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991998.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package McCormick® Chili Seasoning
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, cut up
1 (14.5 ounce) can pinto or kidney beans, undrained
8 (10 inch) flour tortillas
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef; drain. Stir in seasoning mix, stewed tomatoes and beans; cook 5 minutes.
Arrange tortillas in a circle on a 12-inch pizza pan; overlap edges where needed.
Spoon ground beef mixture onto tortillas. Top with cheese. Bake in 400 degree F oven 5-10 minutes or until cheese melts. Garnish with sliced black olives and green onion, if desired.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
For a great weekend meal, combine chili and pizza for a real treat that children will love.
