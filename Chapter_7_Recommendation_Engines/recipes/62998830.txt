Title:
Spinach Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil, divided
8 oz. frozen chopped spinach
1 15 oz. can white beans, cannelloni or similar, drained and rinsed
2 cloves garlic
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
juice of one lemon
1/2 tsp salt or to taste
1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne or to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute minced garlic in one tablespoon olive oil for about one minute.  Add spinach and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes. Allow to cool for a few minutes and transfer to food processor. Add remaining ingredients and process until smooth. Adjust seasonings and chill until ready to serve. Garnish with red peppers if desired.
Serve with veggies or crackers.

Submitter: 
SJRJA
Description: 
Low in fat, but full of flavor! Serve this colorful dip with whole grain crackers, pita or veggies. I garnish with diced red pepper for a festive look!
