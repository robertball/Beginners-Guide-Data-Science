Title:
Thai Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons coconut oil
1 bell pepper, seeded and chopped
3 tablespoons ginger/lemongrass/garlic/sesame oil blend
2 cup uncooked quinoa
4 cups vegetable broth or water
4 tomatoes chopped
Fresh basil chopped

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the peppers; cook and stir for about 2 minutes. Add the ginger blend, and cook for about 2 more minutes. Stir in the quinoa, vegetable stock, and tomatoes.
Return to a boil, then cover and simmer over low heat  for 15 minutes, or until quinoa grains are soft. Stir in the basil. Cook until heated through, then serve.

Submitter: 
KESSIANNE
Description: 
Onions and peppers are sauteed, then simmered with quinoa and tomatoes to make a delicious whole grain side dish. This colorful quinoa dish is easy to cook, and my toddler loves it!Modified from: Mediterranean Quinoa
