Title:
Pineapple Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 x 13 pan
1 1/2 cup flour
2 x  3/4 cup margerine or butter
2 1/4 cups icing sugar
3 tbsp brown sugar
1 egg
19 oz can pineapple crushed (drained)
2 pkg. dream whip

Directions:
Base:
Mix flour, brown sugar, and 3/4 cup butter well
Put in pan and pat down
Bake at 350 degrees until golden brown
Let cool 1/2 hr to 1 hr
Middle:
Beat together icing sugar, 3/4 butter, and 1 egg until stiff
Spread on base
Top:
Drain pineapple and prepare dream whip
Mix together and put on top
Refrigerate
