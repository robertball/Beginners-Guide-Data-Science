Title:
Blueberry Vanilla Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. sugar
1 tbsp. cornstarch
1/4 c. water
1/4 c. light corn syrup
2 tsp. lemon juice (or more, to taste)
2 c. blueberries
1 whole Vanilla bean

Directions:
Combine sugar and cornstarch in large pot.  Add water, syrup, and lemon juice.  Stir in blueberries.  Drop in vanilla bean.  Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes or until thickened and infused with vanilla flavor.   Remove from heat.  Pour into hot jars through a sieve, leaving 1/4 inch head space. Adjust caps. Process 10 minutes in a boiling water bath.

Description: 
A lovely, fresh blueberry syrup with mellow undertones of vanilla.
