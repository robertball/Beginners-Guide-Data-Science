Title:
Toast with ricotta a warm balsamic caramel apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Six 3/4 inch thick slices of white bakery brad
4 tbsps unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 pounds granny smith apples, peeled cored and cut into wedges
3/4 cup sugar
2 tbsps balsami vinegar
1/4 cup plus 2 tbsps water
3/4 cup ricotta cheese
2 tbsps chopped macron almonds

Directions:
Preheat the broiler. Brush the bread with 1 1/2 tbsps of the butter.Broil the bread 4 inches from the heat for one minute, turning once, until browned. Transfer to plates.
In a large skillet, melt the remaining 2 1/2 tbsps of butter. Add the apples and cook over moderately high heat, stirring occasionaly, until carmelized, 2-3 minutes. Add the balsamic vinegar and water and bring to a boil. simmer over low heat until the apples are tender and the sauce is syrupy, about 4 minutes.
Using a slotted spoon, spoon the apples over the toasts and dollop with ricotta cheese on top.Drizzle with the balsamic-caramel syrup and sprinkle with the almonds.Cut each toast in half and serve right away.
