Title:
Asparagus with Roasted Shallots and Cayenne Pepper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 shallots, left whole
2 tbs EVOO
1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp frsh thyme
1 tsp frsh basil
1/2 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.
In an oven safe pan add the whole shallots and cover with oil.
Roast for 25-30 min or until they begin to brown and get tender.  Stir as need so they brown evenly.
Remove and slice into large rings and place in a bowl and keep them warm.
Bring a small pot of water to a boil and cook the asparagus for 3-4 minutes or until tender.
Drain and arrange on a plate.
Add the remaining ingredients to the bowl of shallots and blend gently.
Pour over the asparagus and serve.
