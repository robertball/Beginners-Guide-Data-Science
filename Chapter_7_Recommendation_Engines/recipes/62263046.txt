Title:
Homade No paste turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb Ground turkey meat
2 cans chili beans
1 can dark red kidney beans
2 lg. onions (diced)
1 can mushrooms
2 cloves garlic (minced)
2 Jalapeno' peppers (minced)
2 cans diced tomato's
1/2 cup chili pwd.
2 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1tbs.cumin
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
1 can tomato soup
1/2 cup ketchup

Directions:
In large pot,crumble and cook ground turkey. Add all spices, diced onion, jalapeno' pepper,tomato's, garlic,mushrooms and beans.lastly stir in soup and ketchup. stir frequently let cook on low heat 1 hr..serve.
for a thicker chili add 1-2 tsp cornstarch to your chili after the soup and ketchup..

Submitter: 
Cathy Adams-Kane
Description: 
This chili is a mix of a couple different recopies,I did not have any tomato paste or sauce..It has a bite to it.adjust the spices to your liking..Enjoy
