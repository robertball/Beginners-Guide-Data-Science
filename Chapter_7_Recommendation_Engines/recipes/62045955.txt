Title:
Dirty Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Sourdough hard pretzels
3/4 cup oil
1 tsp. lemon pepper
1 tsp. dill weed
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 envelope ranch seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300.
Break up pretzels in a large bowl.  In a smaller bowl, stir the seasonings into the oil.  Pour over pretzels and mix thoroughly.  Place pretzels on baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes.  Remove from oven, stir, and bake for remaining 15 minutes.  Allow to cool before serving.

Submitter: 
Joni Zalot
Description: 
These seasoned pretzels are addictive and will disappear FAST!!!  Make plenty!
