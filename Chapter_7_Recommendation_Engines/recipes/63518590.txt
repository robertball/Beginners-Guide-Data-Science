Title:
Hot Artichoke/Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cream cheese (8 oz. pkg)
1 c. mayonnaise
1 clove garlic, pressed
1 artichoke heart in water (14 oz) drained and chopped
1 1/2 c. crab meat
3/4 c. parmesan cheese
1/3 c. green onions, sliced
1/2 c. plain bread crumbs
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/3 c. chopped red pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Combine cream cheese and mayonnaise. Mix well. Add garlic into the mixture. Add artichokes, crab, cheese onion, cayenne and red pepper. Mix well. Place in pie plate. Bake 20-30 minutes or until golden. Sprinkle additional red pepper and onion on top for garnish. Serve warm with baguette slices.
