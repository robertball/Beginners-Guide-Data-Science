Title:
Red Onion Poppy Seed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons dry mustard
2 teaspoons salt
2/3 cup white vinegar
1/2 medium to large red onion* - divided, use several slices for the salad, and chop up the rest
2 C. oil
3 tablespoon poppy seeds
Slivered almonds, toasted, for salad topping
Romaine and Red Leaf Lettuce

Directions:
Combine the sugar, dry mustard, salt, vinegar, chopped onion and oil in a blender, blend for 1 minute.
Add poppy seeds and blend just a few seconds.  (makes 4 cups)
Pour into a jar with a tight fitting lid.  Store in a jar in refrigerator.
Make the dressing one day in advance, if possible.
To Serve:
Start by using about Â½ of this dressing for 1 large group salad.
Toss romaine & red leaf lettuce, sliced red onions and slivered almonds.
Add dressing just before serving and toss.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Make the dressing to this pretty spring salad one day in advance & store the dressing in a jar in the refrigerator until serving.  
This pretty salad will look like it has a raspberry dressing on it because of the red onion and poppy seeds.
