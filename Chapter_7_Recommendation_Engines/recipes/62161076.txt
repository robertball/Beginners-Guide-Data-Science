Title:
Redo Jelly recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c water
4 t pectin
1/4 c sugar

Directions:
Use 3 qt pan and stir 1/4 c water and 4 t pectin together . and boil 1 qt of jelly into mixture and add 1/4 sugar. stir till you have a rolling boil .for about 30 seconds. skim the top and pour back into jars and place back into your water bath or pressure cooker .

Submitter: 
jessmomof3
Description: 
This is for when you are canning and your jellies dont jelly . I found this to be the best way for me to fix the problem.
