Title:
Perfect Italian Meatball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/752282.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chuck
1 cup Ricotta
1/2 cup Parmesan
1/4 cup total shallots & garlic
1 cup bread crumbs
1 egg
dash of salt
dash of fresh ground pepper
Olive Oil
1 cup of water (divided)
fresh parsley, garnish

Directions:
Put first 8 ingredients in a bowl. Mix together.
Best to mix with one hand and keep the other one clean. After mixed, grab Olive Oil with clean hand and pour some onto hand you used to mix with. Rub hands together.
Grab some meat & roll between hands.
As you make each meatball place in a pan containing your favorite marinara sauce/tomato sauce.
After all meatballs are in pan, turn heat to medium.
Add about 1/2 cup of water (it will cook off).
Cook for about 15-20 minutes.
Carefully turn meatballs over so bottom is now on top.
Add another 1/2 cup of water.
Spoon sauce over meatballs.
Allow to cook another 15 minutes.
Place meatballs on plate. Cover with remaining sauce.
Sprinkle with fresh parsley.
Drizzle with olive oil.
Sprinkle with fresh ground pepper & Parmesan.

Submitter: 
Loretta Ann Zazo
Description: 
Totally awesome meatballs. They are a meal in themselves. No pasta necessary. Just a glass of Cianti on the side.
