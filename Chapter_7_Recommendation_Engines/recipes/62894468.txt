Title:
The Best Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700042.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Potatoes, Cubed
2 Tablespoons Of Butter
2 Tablespoons Of Heavy Cream
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon of pepper (optional)

Directions:
Boil Potatoes in a pot, till your fork goes Thu it easily
drain potatoes
Put potatoes Thu your potatoes ricer
Add Butter
And Cream
Add Salt
Add Pepper (Optional)
Stir With A Fork Till Blended
Mashed Potatoes, Done

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
The Best Mashed Potatoes Are Done In A Potatoe Ricer, Not with a Blender. After Years, And Years Of Mixing Potatoes In A Bowl, And Using A Blender I Found Out Using A Ricer Renders The Ultimate Mashed Potatos.

When You Use A Blender You May Over Blend Your Potatoes And Create Nothing More Than A Lump Of Paste.
