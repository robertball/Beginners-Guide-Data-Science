Title:
Chopped Sesame  Salad in Bread  bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Salad-
4 cups chopped hearts of romaine
1  cup finely diced plum tomato
1 1/2 cup finely diced red onion
1 cup corn kernels (you can use frozen)
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
1 tbsp sesame or olive oil
For the dressing-
3/4 cup white wine vinegar
1 1/2 cup seasme oil
4 tbsp lightly toasted sesame seeds
4 tbsp garlic powder
8 tbsp sugar
4 tsp oregano
salt and pepper to taste
For the bread bowl
4-6 individual size hearty bread rounds or you can use this recipe and make them at home (makes 8)
http://bread.allrecipes.com/az/ItlinBrdBwls.asp

Directions:
For the dressing-
Combine the vinegar, sugar, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a bowl. Dribble oil slowly and whisk. Add seasme seeds.
Heat a skillet and dribble 1 tbsp of olive/sesame oil. Add the corn kernels and stir-fry until slightly blackened.
To Assemble-
Cut the top quarter of the bread
Scoop the centers of the bread round to create a hollow bowl. Brush the insdies and outsides with sesame oil.
Toss all salad ingredients excpet blue cheese in a large bowl. Pour dressing and toss to coat well.
Fill the bread bowls with salad and top with crumbled blue cheese.

Submitter: 
Talking Head
Description: 
Who says that only soups can be served in bread bowls. Try this zesty salad with a nutty sesame flavor served in a hearty bread bowl. This summertime salad is perfect for you next buffet or potluck. The little extra dressing can be dribbled on top of the salad to add flavour to the bread bowl. Makes a nice light lunch!
