Title:
Sarah's Mexican Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bell peppers, whatever colors you like
1 cup cooked rice seasoned with cilantro, salt, and lime juice
1 lb ground beef, turkey, or chicken
1 cup Mexican shredded cheese, plus more for topping
1/2 package taco seasoning
1 jar salsa
1 can diced green chiles
1/2 cup corn
1/2 cup black beans
1/4 cup diced onion
1/4 cup diced tomato
1 garlic clove

Directions:
Cook meat until it is no longer pink. Cut off top of peppers and hollow out the inside. Then mix all remaining ingredients together and fill the peppers with it. Top with cheese and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
