Title:
Biscuits With Turkey Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 of a finely diced onion
3 T butter
3 T flour
4 C turkey or chicken stock
salt and pepper to taste
refrigerated or home-made biscuits
Leftover turkey

Directions:
Saute garlic and onion in the butter over med-high heat. Cook 5 min., until onions are soft. Stir in the flour. Add the stock slowly and bring to a boil. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Serve over biscuits w/leftover turkey.
