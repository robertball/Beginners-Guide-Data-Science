Title:
Easy Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  1/3 cups self-rising flour
8 tsp. sugar
1 stick frozen butter, grated
1  1/3 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Mix flour and sugar.  Grate butter into mixture and stir.  Add buttermilk and stir gently until mixture forms soft sticky ball.  Divide dough into about. 9 equal parts, lightly for biscuit and place in 9 x 9 greased pan.  Bake at 400* until light golden brown, about 20 minutes.  Enjoy
