Title:
Hugs Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 13 oz. bag Hershey's Hugs
1 bag round pretzels
2 1.69 oz. bags M&Ms; or other candy-coated chocolates

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place pretzels on baking sheets covered with aluminum foil or parchment paper.
Unwrap candy Hugs and place one in the center of each pretzel.
Put baking sheets in preheated oven 2 minutes, until Hugs are mostly melted.
Remove from oven and squish one M&M in the center of each pretzel.
Chill on baking sheet until set.
Note: Substitue Reese's Pieces or other candy-coated peanut butter pieces in place of the M&Ms for an added twist.

Submitter: 
Cassie Merritt
Description: 
A slight variation on Mel's Chocolate Pretzels recipe, this recipes uses Hershey's Hugs for a swirly, neat-looking effect.  These candies are quick and delicious!
