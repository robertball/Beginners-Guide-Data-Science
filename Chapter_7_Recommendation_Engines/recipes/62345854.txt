Title:
Fresh Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz Lime Juice (freshly squeezed)
1 oz Agave Nectar (organic)
2 oz Tequila
Kosher Salt for rimming

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker 2/3rds full with ice cubes. Wet the rim of an old fashioned or margarita glass by running a lime wedge all the way around. Salt the rim using the kosher salt then fill the glass with ice. Shake all ingredients and strain over glass. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jose Napoleon Cuasay Artiaga I
Description: 
This margarita uses only three ingredients making the freshest margarita you have ever tasted!
