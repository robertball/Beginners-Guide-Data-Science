Title:
Furikake Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box corn chex mix
1 box rice chex mix
1 bottle Furikake (try the Asian aisle in grocery stores)
1 stick of butter
2 Tbsp of soy sauce
1/2 cup of brown sugar
1/2 cup of cane sugar
3/4 cup of corn syrup

Directions:
Heat oven at 250F.
Mix the two boxes of corn and rice chex mix in 2 large baking pan of 2" depth.
Melt butter, sugars, corn syrup, soy sauce on low heat in a pot.
Pour mixture over the chex mix in the pan.
Pour the Furikake over the mixture.
Mix lightly in the pan.
Bake for 45 mnutes to 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Let cool and store in airtight container.
