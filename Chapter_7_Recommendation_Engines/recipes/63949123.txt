Title:
Prune Yogurt Spice Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1095385.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Quick oats
1 1/2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 tsp. Nutmeg
1/4 tsp. Cloves
1 C. Dried prunes, chopped finely
1 C. Plain yogurt
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs
1 tsp. Maple syrup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 9" by 5" by 3" loaf pan. Set aside
Blend flour, B. powder, spices and quick oats together in a small bowl. Set aside
In a large bowl combine yogurt, eggs, prunes and sugar mixing all ingredients well
Stir dry ingredients into wet mixture and fold maple syrup into batter and mix until just combined
Pour batter into greased loaf pan and bake for 40 minutes in preheated oven
Loaf is done when cake tester inserted into the centre comes out clean.
Remove loaf from oven and allow to cool for 5 minutes before removing from loaf pan
Gently tip loaf pan over and tap against counter and place on wire rack to cool completely before slicing.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very delicious loaf with prunes and oats adding fibre with maple syrup essence to make it a wonderful addition to your tea or coffee time.
