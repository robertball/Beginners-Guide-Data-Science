Title:
Pancakes Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Dry
1 1/4c - All Purpose Flour
2T - Granulated Sugar
2t - Baking Powder
1/2 t - Salt
Wet
1 - Egg (beaten)
1c - Milk
1T - Cooking Oil

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients. In separate bowl mix all wet ingredients. Combine both mixtures until blended.
