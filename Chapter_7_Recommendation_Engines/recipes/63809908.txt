Title:
Nanas Beef Steak Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- Beef Steak - preferably with a bone ( the ones with a round bone is what she used)
2- Large onion sliced
3- 3 cloves garlic minced
4- 3 stalks celery with leaved diced
5- large can plum tomatoes  or diced tomatoes
6- 4 carrots peeled and sliced
7- package frozen spinach
8- box of beef broth or 8  beef bouillon cubes
9- 1 cup elbow dry pasta or any small pasta
10- salt and pepper
11- water

Directions:
in a large soup pan add 1/2 cup water and place steak to brown both sides med heat...add cut up onion, celery, garlic & salt & pepper... flip over steak a few times ( might have to add more water to keep from sticking) till onions brown or semi cooked..add tomatoes, carrots 10-20 cups of water...add boullion...simmer medium heat 2 hrs...take beef out and cut to smaller bite size pieces ..add spinach and cook 10 min..add pasta and cook 10 min
