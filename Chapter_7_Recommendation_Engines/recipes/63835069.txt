Title:
Cajun Spice Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065774.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 1/4 teaspoons oregano
1 1/4 teaspoons thyme
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
Stir together salt, garlic powder, paprika, black pepper, onion powder, cayenne pepper, oregano, thyme, and red pepper flakes until evenly blended. Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This is a mildly hot Cajun spice mix you make yourself with common spices from your pantry. For those who like it hotter, add the optional crushed red pepper. This makes a great oven Cajun hashbrowns!Modified from: Cajun Spice Mix
