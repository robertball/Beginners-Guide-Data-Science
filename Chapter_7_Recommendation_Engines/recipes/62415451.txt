Title:
Sandy's Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag dry black beans (or a can of prepared beans)
1 bag dry kidney beans (or a can of prepared beans)
1 onion
1 bell pepper
1 package cream cheese
2 Tomatoes diced
green onions
4 oz Cheddar cheese
1 package whole wheat tortillas
salt (to taste)
pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Prepare beans according to package (or buy prepared beans) only 1 cup of each is needed for recipe.
Preheat oven to 350
Chop onions and pepper.
In large pan saute onions and pepper (in lightly greased pan).
Mix in beans and cream cheese.
In 9x13 pan place tortillas filled with bean mixture.
Top with cheese then green onions and tomatoes.
Bake until heated through the cheese is melted (15 mins)

Submitter: 
MeSprague
Description: 
A bean recipe for those who are just beginning to like beans.  The beans have a smoother texture with the cream cheese and are flavored by onion and bell pepper.   Sandy cooked it for a ministry meeting.  Simple, inexpensive, healthy, and tasty.
