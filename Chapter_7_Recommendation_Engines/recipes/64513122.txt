Title:
Fudgie Thumbprint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 squares unsweetened chocolate
1/4 tsp. salt
2 cups flour

Directions:
Cream butter and sugars till well blended.
Mix in egg and vanilla.
Stir in melted chocolate.
Add flour and salt, but don't overmix or dough will be tough.
Shape 1 1/2 tsp. portions into balls. Indent each with thumb.
Bake @ 350 x 12 - 14 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Little fudge bites that are a nice addition to a baking tray. Freeze well.
