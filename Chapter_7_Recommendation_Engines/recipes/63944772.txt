Title:
Big Ray's Pizza Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093523.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups baby spinach, finely packed
4 oz. mozzarella cheese, shredded
6 sun-dried tomatoes halves, finely chopped
1/4 cup fresh basil, finely chopped
1 16 oz. can chopped tomato, very well drained (or 1 large tomato thinly sliced)
1 Tbs corn meal
1/2 lb. pizza dough, thawed
3/4 ricotta cheese
1 egg, lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Sprinkle work surface with corn meal.  Roll pizza dough into 10" x 16" rectangle.
Spray baking sheet with cooking spray and transfer dough.
Spread ricotta cheese along middle of the dough, leaving a 3" border on each long side and a 1/2" border on top and bottom.  Top with spinach and sun-dried tomatoes.  Layer chopped tomatoes on top, then add mozzarella cheese and basil.  Brush egg on top of dough and filling and roll strudel up lengthwise like a jellyroll.  Press to seal firmly.  Position the roll seam side down.
Brush dough with remaining egg and cut four slits into the top of the dough about 2 inches apart.
bake until crust has browned and the cheese bubbles slightly from the slits, about 15 minutes.  Let cool slightly and cut using a serrated knife.
