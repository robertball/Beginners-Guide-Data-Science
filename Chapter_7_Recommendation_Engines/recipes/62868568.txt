Title:
Mocha Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. powdered sugar (approximately 4 3/4 c.)
2 T. milk (approximately 1/4 c.)
6 T. butter, melted
1 1/2 t. vanilla extract
1/4 c. cocoa
1/2 t. instant coffee powder

Directions:
In a bowl, warm the milk and the coffee powder until it dissolves.
Add remaining ingredients and stir until clumps are gone and the desired consistency is achieved.
Eat with a spoon, or use to frost a cool cake.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Delicious mellow coffee flavor makes this frosting a favorite on chocolate cake.
