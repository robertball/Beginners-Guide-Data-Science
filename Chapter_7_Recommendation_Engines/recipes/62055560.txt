Title:
Cheesy Brussle Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 16oz packages frozen Brussle Sprouts
2 - 10 3/4 cans cream of mushroom soup
8oz sour cream
1 1/2 cup shredded cheese
pinch salt / pepper (optional)

Directions:
Steam the brussle sprouts until tender and place in a medium cassarole dish.  In a seperate bowl mix the soup and sour cream until smooth then fold in the cheese. Pour over the brussle sprouts and spread evenly.  Bake at 375 degrees for  approximately 1 hour or until bubbling in the center.

Submitter: 
Renee' Levins
Description: 
This recipe is actually REQUESTED for all family and social gatherings.  Even EX-brussle sprout haters have taken to this dish. Super fast and easy to make.
