Title:
Frozen Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of cherry Jello
1 large can of pineapple juice
1/2 cup of sugar
1 small can of frozen lemonade
4 cups of hot water
2 bottles of ginger ale

Directions:
Dissolve jello in hot water. Add sugar, lemonade and pineapple juice. Blend to mix well. Pour into gallon jug and let freeze until 1 1/2 hours before using. Pour gingerale over frozen punch before serving.
