Title:
black pepper onion rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
2 large red onions- peeled and cut into thin rings
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup corn starch
1/2 packet powder black pepper white gravy mix
salt
garlic powder-to taste

Directions:
1. add onions to eggwhites and coat well.
2. put flour, cornstarch, gravy mix, salt, and garlic powder in a large plastic bag. add onions.
3.add onions to bag, seal and shake to coat, separating onions a little as you shake. (its okay if a few stick together)
4. repeat steps 1 and 2. (re dip in eggs and breading)
5. deep fry at 375 degrees until golden.
