Title:
Smooth and simple deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large eggs
1/4 tsp yellow mustard
1/4 C mayonnaise (or more to taste)
Salt to taste
Paprika fro garnish

Directions:
Place eggs In a sauce pan as cover with cool water.  Place on a burner with medium high heat. Once the water starts to boil, cover an turn heat to off.
Let sit for 15 minutes.  Remove from heat and place in an ice bath until cool.
Peel eggs, and cut in half.
Place egg yolks, 2 at a time , in a wire mesh strainer over a large bowl. With a spoon, firmly press the egg yolk through the mesh. Gently scraping  the egg from the underside if th strainer into the bowl. Repeat until all yolks are pressed.
Add the mustard, mayonnaise and salt to the yolk and mix well.
With a spoon pick up a tsp of egg filling and drop into egg white.
Sprinkle with paprika.

I’ve tried every deviled eggs recipe.  This very simple recipe has been the family favorite- hands down.  Running the yolk through the sieve makes for a glorious smooth egg.
