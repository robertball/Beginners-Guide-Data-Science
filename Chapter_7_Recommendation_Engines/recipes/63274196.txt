Title:
Red Leaf Lettuce, Pear and Walnut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBL apple cider vinegar (rice vinegar can be substituted)
1/2 cup apple juice or apple cider
1/4 cup canola oil
4 tsp dijon mustard
1 TBL honey
1 tsp poppy seeds
salt and pepper to taste
10 cups rinsed and well drained red leaf lettuce, coarsely chopped, packed
1/4 cup coarsely chopped toasted walnuts
4 pears, peeled, cored & cut into 3/4-inch pieces

Directions:
Add vinegar, apple juice, canola oil, dijon mustard and honey to food processor or blender and pulse until well combined. Stir in poppy seeds and add salt and pepper to taste, if desired. Reserve 1/4 cup of the dressing and set aside.
Drizzle remaining dressing on sides of large bowl. Add red leaf lettuce and walnuts; toss to coat well.
Divide lettuce among 8 individual salad plates or bowls.
In separate bowl, combine pears with reserved 1/4 cup dressing and toss well. Spoon pears evenly over the 8 salads.
