Title:
Chicken & Veggies over Spaghetti Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3oz chicken breast sliced
1 cup baked spaghetti squash
1 cup zucchini, chopped
1/2 tbs EVOO
1/2 of raw marinara sauce
3 roma tomatoes
12 pieces sun dried tomatoes
1 clove garlic
5 basil leaves
1/2 tbs oregano
1 medjool date

Directions:
bake spaghetti squash.
Cook shicken and veggies in olive oil.
Blend ingredients for marinara sauce.
serve 1/2 marinara sauce over chicken
