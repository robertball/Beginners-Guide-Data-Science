Title:
Kim's Spicy BBQ Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sirloin steaks
1/4 cup olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
3/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
3/4 tsp. lemon pepper
1 tsp. black pepper
2 tsp. paprika
1 1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
3/4 tsp. salt
3/4 tsp. ground cumin

Directions:
Brush the meat with olive oil and rub the meat with garlic. Stir together remaining ingredients in bowl and rub meat with the spices.  Place in plastic freezer bag and seal and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.  Cook on hot grill or sear in skillet till desired doneness.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This spicy rub will get your taste buds working.  It's great on any kind of meat.  I used sirloin steaks and cooked them on my charcoal grill.  So flavorful!
