Title:
ALEXANDER'S SWEET BIRTHDAY CAKE FRAPPUCCINO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz milk
½ oz. vanilla syrup
½ oz hazelnut syrup
5 ice cubes
scrapings of about ? of a vanilla bean
1-pint heavy whipping cream
½ oz vanilla syrup
¼ oz raspberry syrup
sprinkles

Directions:
Blend the milk, vanilla, hazelnut syrups with ice cubes and the vanilla bean scrapings.
Whip the whipping cream with vanilla and raspberry syrup until stiff peaks form. Top the drink with the whipped cream and sprinkles.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes a frappuccino that has been through the final perfection stages!
