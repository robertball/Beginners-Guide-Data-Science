Title:
Quick and Easy Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/962786.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
2 tablespoons tahini
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Blend garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Cocoanut
Description: 
Serve with a drizzle of olive oil and a dash of paprika; this middle-eastern dish is irresistible!
