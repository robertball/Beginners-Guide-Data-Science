Title:
Rachael's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup butter
1 t dried thyme
1 t rosemary
salt/pepper
1 1/2 (21 oz total) can chicken broth

Directions:
Heat a medium skillet over medium-high heat.  Sprinkle chicken with garlic salt, pepper. Cook chicken quickly 3-4 min each side.
To the skillet add the butter.  When the butter has melted, sprinkle thyme & rosemary over the breasts and pour in the broth.  Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer breasts, turning occasionally.  Cook until desired liquid consistency is reached, and chicken is no longer pink and its juices run clear.
