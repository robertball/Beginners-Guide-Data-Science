Title:
Classic Rotelle Dip with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 __ block velveeta
1 can rotello tomatoes
1 lb lean ground beef
1 pkg taco seasonong
1 sm onion finely chopped

Directions:
cube velveeta and put in the bottom of a crock pot
pour rotelle over cheese
brown beef and onion and add to cheese
after the cheese has melted... fold in the taco seasoning
yummy

Submitter: 
Pam Rosterman
Description: 
This dip was made popular in the 70s and still remains a classic today!  Server it with Tortilla chips.  Also awesome as a topping for any mexican dish.
