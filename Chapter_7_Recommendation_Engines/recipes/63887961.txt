Title:
Rich Red Wine Onion Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large Onion - sliced
60g/2oz Butter
1 clove Garlic - lightly crushed
3 sprigs Thyme
3tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
200ml/7fl oz Red Wine
200ml/7f oz Beef Stock
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Heat half(1oz) butter in a frying pan on a low heat, add the onion and cook slowly for some 30 minutes or so, stirring occasionally, or until the onion starts to turn golden;
Season with pepper and add the garlic and thyme;
Continue cooking for a further minute or so, stirring occasionally;
Pour in the vinegar and stir until it turns into a syrup;
Pour in the wine, increase heat to high, and cook until it has reduced by about half to two thirds;
Pour in the stock and bring to the boil;
Turn down the heat and, still stirring occasionally, simmer the gravy until reduced to consistency required;
Remove the garlic and thyme;
Season to taste and whisk in the butter;
If available, add any juices from the meat.
