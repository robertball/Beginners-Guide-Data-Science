Title:
Authentic Crawfish Etoufee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/934170.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs peeled crawfish tails
3/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp red pepper
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
1 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
2 Tbs crawfish fat (if fresh and available)
2 cups cold water
2 Tbs cornstarch
1/4 cup onion tops
1/4 cup chopped parsley
4 cups steamed rice

Directions:
Season crawfish tails with salt and peppers and set aside.
Melt butter in heavy 2-3 quart pot.  Add onions, bell pepper, and celery; cook over medium high heat until onions are wilted, stirring constantly.
Add crawfish fat, 1 1/2 cups of water, and crawfish tails.  Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer over low heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Dissolve cornstarch into 1/2 cup cold water and then stir into pot until etoufee thickens.
Add onion tops and parsley, and cook on low heat for another 5 minutes.
Serve over steamed rice.
