Title:
Super Berry Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/791673.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen mixed berries
2 cups milk
1 tablespoon honey
1/4 cup crushed sliced almonds

Directions:
In a blender, combine the mixed berries, milk, honey and almonds. Cover, and blend until somewhat smooth. Spoon into bowls, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Chicken
Description: 
A yummy, easy-to prepare dessert that tastes fab! Frozen mixed berries are blended with milk, honey and almonds to make a guilt-free treat.
