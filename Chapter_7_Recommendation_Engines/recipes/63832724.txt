Title:
Grandma's Best Chicken Noodle Soup for class Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup wide egg noodles
1/4 teaspoon vegetable oil
4 cups chicken broth (made from 3 boullion cubes and 4 cups water)
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/3 cup chopped celery
1/3 cup chopped onion
2 Tablespoon
1 1/2  Tablespoon water
1 cups diced, cooked chicken meat

Directions:
1.Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and oil, and boil for 8 minutes, or until tender. Drain, and rinse under cool running water.
2. In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, combine 4 cups water, bouillion cubes, salt, poultry seasoning and garlic powder. Bring to a boil.
3. Stir in celery and onion. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
4. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and water together until cornstarch is completely dissolved. Gradually add to soup, stirring constantly.
5. Stir in noodles and chicken, and heat through.
