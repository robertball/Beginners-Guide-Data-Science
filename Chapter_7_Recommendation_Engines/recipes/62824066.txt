Title:
Easy Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Dozen Eggs
1 Roll Of Sausage (I prefer Jimmy Dean Sage)
1 Small Diced Onion
1 Bunch Diced Cilantro
Medium Tortillas
Salt and Pepper to taste
Salsa

Directions:
Brown sausage and set aside in separate bowl. (You may add the onion during cooking of sausage if you prefer it not raw).
Scramble all of the eggs.
Add sausage to egg and mix.
Add diced cilantro and onion if you did not cook with sausage.
Serve with tortillas and roll into a burrito.
Add salsa to your burrito before you roll if you like.

Submitter: 
amberrlp
Description: 
Super easy breakfast burritos.  Serve them hot and fresh, or wrap in celephane and freeze for later!
