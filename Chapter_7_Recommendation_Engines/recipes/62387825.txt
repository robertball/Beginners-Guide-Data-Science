Title:
Salmon Spread the Love Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz FF cream cheese, room temp
5 oz can pink salmon in water, drained & finely flaked
2 wedges laughing cow original cheese
1 1/2 Tbl dijonaise
1 Tbl minced onion
1/2 tsp lemon juice

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, cheese wedges, dijonnaise, & lemon juice. Mix thoroughly until ingredients are completely integrated.
Add salmon & onion & stir thoroughly, until a very smooth consistency.
Refrigerate until ready to serve
