Title:
Magic Bullet Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 avocado
5 cherry tomatoes
Juice of half a lime
1 T cilantro leaves
Salt and pepper to taste
a little water to loosen it up (if needed)

Directions:
Throw all ingredients in  the bullet.  Blend until you reach the consistency you desire.

Description: 
Quick and easy guacamole - can be prepared in magic bullet or blender.
