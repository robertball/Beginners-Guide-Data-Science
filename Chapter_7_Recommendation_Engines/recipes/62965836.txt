Title:
Honey Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs Chicken leg quarters, skin removed
1 stick butter (no substitute)
1 cup honey
1/2 cup yellow mustard
1 tbs + curry powder (I never really measure the curry, I make it and taste as I go to get the perfect curry taste)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees
In a sauce pan combine butter, honey, mustard, curry and salt and pepper heat until melted and well combined
Pour over chicken arranged in a 9x13 baking pan
cover with foil
bake for 35-45 mins (or until chicken is done)

Submitter: 
DaraLynn Ketcham
Description: 
This chicken dish is so yummy and is great easy dish for company. Your whole house will smell delicious! Goes great with mixed vegies and the sauce tastes heavenly over rice.
