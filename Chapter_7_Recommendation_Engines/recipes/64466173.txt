Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dino Fluff:
1 1/2 cups half and half
1/2 cup sugar
2 large eggs
1 large egg yolk
2 tablespoons all purpose flour
2 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Cookie Base
2 2/3 cups AP white flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 c unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 tsp grated lemon zest
1 lg egg
2 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Chocolate sprinkles

Directions:
Dino Fluff (Can be made up to 1 day in advance):
Hear half and half in medium saucepan, bring to a simmer
In a medium bowl, whisk together sugar, eggs, egg yolk and flour. Gradually whisk in warm half and half.
Continuously whisk over medium heat until mixture thickens and comes to boil. Boil 1 minute. Transfer to medium bowl.
Cover; chill until cold, about 4 hours.
Cookie Base:
Preheat oven to 350 and grease several baking sheets.
Stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt set aside.
With an electric mixer on high, beat butter sugar and lemon until light and fluffy
Add egg, 1tbsp water vanilla and lemon zezt. Beat until incorporated.
Reduce speed and gradually beat in flour mixture.
With greased hands, shape dough into balls (approx 1 1/2 to 2 inches) and place on baking sheets approx 2 1/2 inches apart.
Press a deep well into the center of each ball.
Bake 10-13 minutes until cookies are lightly browned.
Let cool slightly and pipe on Dino Fluff
Top with chocolate “mites” (sprinkles)

Submitter: 
Jennifer Mullins
