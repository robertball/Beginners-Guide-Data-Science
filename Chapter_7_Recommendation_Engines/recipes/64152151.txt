Title:
Batter Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp sugar
1/2 warm water
1 pkg dry yeast
1-1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup shotening
2 tsp salt
2 eggs
3-3/4 cups flour

Directions:
disolve sugar in warm water - sprinkle in yeast - let stand for 10 minutes, then stir well.
combine milk sugar and shortening and salt in saucepan and heat until lukewarm and shortening is melted
add to yeast mixture
add 2-3/4 cups of flour to the yeast mixture, mix with electric mixture for 3 minutes - gradually add the remaining flour
cover and let stand for 1 hour - cover with tea towel
stir down and let stand for 10 minutes
fill greased muffing tins 1/2 full and let rise again for 45 minutes
bake at 375 for 20-25 minuted or until golden brown -
