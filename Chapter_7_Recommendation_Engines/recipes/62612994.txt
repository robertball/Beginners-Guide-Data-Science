Title:
Brown Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14T butter
2c dark brown sugar
1/4c white sugar
2c + 2T flour
1/2t baking soda
1/4t powder
1/2t salt
1 large egg + 1 yolk
1T vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix white sugar with 1/4c brown sugar.
Melt and brown 10T butter.
Add melted butter to 4T cold and cool for 15 minutes.
Add 1-3/4c brown sugar and salt to melted butter.
Add eggs and vanilla.
Add flour and mix.
Dough will be very firm.
Firm into rectangle and cut into 24 pieces.
Roll each piece in sugar mix.
Place 12 on parchment paper.
Bake 6 minutes, turn, and bake 6 minutes.
Leave on parchment 5 minutes then cool on rack.
