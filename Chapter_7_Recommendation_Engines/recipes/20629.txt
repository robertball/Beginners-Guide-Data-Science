Title:
Kool Ade Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1272165.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4.6 ounce) package cherry flavored sweetened soft drink mix
1 (46 fluid ounce) can unsweetened pineapple juice
1 liter lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed

Directions:
In a punch bowl prepare drink mix according to package instructions. Stir in pineapple juice and lemon-lime soda. Mix in the concentrated orange juice and lemonade. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Pat
Description: 
Makes a delicious drink and you can also float ice cream or sherbet on top.
