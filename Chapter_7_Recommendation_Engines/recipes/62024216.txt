Title:
Lemon Rosemary Slo-Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole 4lb. baking chicken
1 lemon halved
4 sprigs fresh rosemary
3 cloves whole garlic
1/2 red onion
1 tbl olive oil
1 tbl lemon pepper
3/4 cup sake
2 chicken boulion cubes

Directions:
Remove giblets and discard.  Rinse chicken inside and out.  Rub outside with olive oil. Sprinkle with lemon pepper. Insert halved lemon, garlic, and onion into chicken cavity.  Place in slow cooker, pour in sake and add boullion cubes.  Cover and cook 6-8 hours on setting 4.
Remove chicken, thicken gravy if desired.

Submitter: 
CHRISPENTINA
Description: 
I made this up when I couldn't find the slow baked chicken recipe I wanted.  It's great! Makes marvelous gravy.  I servied it with mashed potatoes and fresh broccoli.
