Title:
Fruit Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, melted, plus more for greasing pan
2 cups sugar
2 cup self-rising flour (or 2 cups flour + 1 t. salt + 1 T baking powder)
2 cups milk
6 cups fresh (or frozen) fruit

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease an 11x13" baking dish with butter.
In a medium bowl, whisk 1 cup sugar with the flour and milk. Whisk in the melted butter.
Rinse the blackberries and pat them dry. Pour the berries into the baking dish. Pour the batter into the baking dish. Sprinkle the blackberries evenly over the top of the batter on top. Bake until golden brown and bubbly, about 1 hour. When 10 minutes of the cooking time remains, sprinkle the remaining 2 tablespoons sugar over the top.
