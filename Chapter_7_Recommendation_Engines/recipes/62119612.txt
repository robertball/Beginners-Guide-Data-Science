Title:
A Fabulous Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown sugar
3 cups powdered sugar
1 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Measure out all ingredients, so they are ready to be added. Place the powdered sugar into a bowl. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stie in brown sugar and milk. Bring mixture to a boil, and boil for approxiamately 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Remove pan from heat and add vanilla and peanut butter, stir. Pour mixture over the powdered sugar and Beat until smooth. Pour into pan and smooth out. Chill until firm. Cut into squares.
