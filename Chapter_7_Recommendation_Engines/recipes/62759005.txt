Title:
HONEY GARLIC RIBS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/733864.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 lbs.pork side ribs,cut in pieces.
1/2 cup honey
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup soy sauce
4 cloves crushed garlic

Directions:
Cook ribs in boiling salted water untill tender.Drain and put in baking dish.Combine honey,ketchup,soy sauce and garlic.Pour over ribs.Bake at 350 for 45 mins.till sauce thickens and ribs are glazed.Great in a crock pot to.

This is such a easy recipe-and every one loves it.
Great for pulled pork too.
