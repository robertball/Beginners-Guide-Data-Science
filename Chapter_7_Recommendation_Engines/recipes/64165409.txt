Title:
Italian Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 lb sweet italian sausage
1 1/4 c italian bread crumbs
2 tbl italian seasoning
garlic to taste
4 tbl grated parmesan cheese
salt
pepper
2 eggs

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
mix all ingredients, until combined
shape into balls of uniform size, can make large or small, the size of meatball, determines serving size

Submitter: 
Ann Beagle
Description: 
this is great for meatball subs and for spaghetti and meatballs
