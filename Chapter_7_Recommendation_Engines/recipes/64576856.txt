Title:
Hard Sauce for Christmas Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter, softened
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups icing sugar

Directions:
In small bowl with electric mixer, cream butter on high till light and fluffy.
Add other ingredients; beat until smooth.
Makes about 1 1/2 cups.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Traditional 'hard' sauce vs traditional hot, clearer, less sweet sauce - guess which one was my favorite on Christmas pudding?!
