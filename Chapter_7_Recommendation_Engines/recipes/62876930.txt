Title:
Roasted Pork Belly for Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pork belly, skin removed
2 tablespoons chili pepper flakes
2 tablespoons black peppercorns
2 talbespoons dried fennel
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup sea salt
1/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup fresh minced garlic
1 bunch thyme leaves, whole sprigs
1 tablespoon fresh grated nutmeg

Directions:
Grind chili flakes, peppercorns, and fennel together.
Mix with remaining seasonings, and rub all over the top of the pork belly.
Top with whole thyme springs and rub over the top of the seasonings.
Wrap tighly with cellophane wrap, and let pork marinate in dry rub for three days.
