Title:
Baked Hungarian Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. fine noodles
4 c. cream-style cottage cheese
4 c. sour cream
1 c. minced onion
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
4 T. worcestershire sauce
4 dashes tabasco sauce
4 T. poppy seeds
2 t. salt
ground pepper to taste
paprika
grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook noodles in boiling, salted water until tender. Drain. Combine the noodles with the remaining ingredients except the paprika and parmesan cheese.
Bake in a buttered dish at 350* until hot.
Sprinkle with paprika and serve with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
tjmallick
Description: 
This recipe has been written to serve 24 guests because it is so suitable for Beef Bourguignon, but it can easily be divided by 4 to serve 6. Do note that it may be made well in advance of the party.
