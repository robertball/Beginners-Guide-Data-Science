Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Baked Custard with Caramel Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983182.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
4 eggs
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups skim milk
3/4 cup caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Place cereal in 20 cm (8-inch) square glass baking dish.
In medium bowl, beat together eggs, sugar, salt, cinnamon and vanilla until well combined. Stir in milk. Pour over cereal.
Bake at 160 degrees C (325 degrees F) about 30 minutes or until knife inserted in centre comes out clean. Serve warm drizzled with about 30 mL (2 tbsp) caramel syrup.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Served with a drizzle of warm, caramel syrup, this creamy custard has a crunchy KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal crust.
