Title:
Strawberry-Banana Freeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large frozen container sliced strawberries in syrup (thawed)
2 bananas sliced
Large container cool whip

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well. (You may not want to use all the syrup in the strawberries so that consistency is not too watery.  Add more syrup until desired consistency is reached.)
Pour mixture into glass serving dish and freeze.
Cut into squares and serve!

Submitter: 
BAYOU GAL
Description: 
Quick, easy, & refresingly cool fruity dessert for the sweltering days of summer!
