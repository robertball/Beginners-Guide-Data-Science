Title:
Easy Mini Pumkin Pie Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056904.jpg
Ingredients:
Box of yellow cake mix
One can of pumpkin (15oz)
One egg
2 tsp. pumpkin spice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a bowl. Mix with electric mixer for 2-3 minutes. Butter or grease mini muffin tin. Put one slightly heaping TB of mix in each cup. Bake at 350 degrees for 13-16 min. Frost with cream cheese frosting. Cream cheese frosting: 4oz cream cheese (1/2 block 8oz cream cheese)(30 sec. in microwave), 2 Tb. softened butter, one tsp. vanilla, 2-3 cups powdered sugar. Add 2 cups at first and then add more as needed. Mix well.

Submitter: 
Caramia
Description: 
Using only 4 ingredients makes this a super easy snack or dessert. This recipe is very kid friendly as you can tell by viewing the picture. My four year frosted the mini cupcakes. Without the frosting, they could be considered a healthy snack, maybe.
