Title:
A Different Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/705893.jpg
Ingredients:
46 oz Tomato Juice
1 can Diced Tomatoes with Green Chilies
1 can Chili Ready Diced Tomatoes
2 cans Hot Chili Beans
1 can Refried Beans
2 Pounds 80/20 Ground Beef
2 cups Elbow Macaroni
3 TBS Onion Flakes
2 TBS Chili Powder
1 TBS Nutmeg
46 oz Water
Salt to taste

Directions:
Brown and Drain Ground Beef.  Place all ingredients, except for the elbow macaroni, into a 6 qt slow cooker. Cook on low for 8 hours.  Add elbow macaroni and continue cooking for half an hour (or until noodles are done)

Submitter: 
samiammy
Description: 
This is a recipe the whole family loves.  The yield is quite alot so it is great for family gatherings or game days.  It also freezes well.
