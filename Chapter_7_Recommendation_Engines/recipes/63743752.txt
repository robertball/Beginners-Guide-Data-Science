Title:
Tea Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cream of tartar
1/2 cup cold butter
1 cup cold milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375degrees
Mix all dry ingredients
Cut in butter, add milk and mix
Knead four....8-10 times
Cut into squares....Bake 12-15 minutes
