Title:
Stuffing Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp vegetable oil
3/4 cups finely diced bacon
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
3/4 cup diced celery
6 cups fresh brown bread crumbs (about 12 slices)
1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp each - dried thyme, rubbed sage, dried savoury, salt and black pepper
1 egg, beaten
3 tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Heat oil in frying pan over medium heat. Sautee bacon 2 minutes until lightly browned. Add onion and celery to pan and sautee 3-5 minutes until softened.
Combine bread crumbs, bacon, sauteed vegetables, parsley, seasonings and egg in mixing bowl. Adjust seasoning to taste.
Divide into 30 equal portions and shape into balls. Arrange on greased baking sheet. Using pastry brush, brush each with melted butter.
Bake uncovered in preheated 350 F oven about 20 minutes until golden. Cool. Wrap and freeze.
The day before serving, thaw in the fridge overnight. Reheat covered in preheated 350 F oven 20-25 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
These can be made ahead of time and frozen, then reheated when needed.
