Title:
Just Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. 75% / 25% ground beef
1 med. onion diced
chili powder to taste
garlic powder to taste
black pepper to taste
2 TBS catsup
1/2 cup water
my additions...
1/4 tsp celery seed
1/2 tsp spanish paprika
dash of worstershire sauce

Directions:
Brown ground beef and diced onions
add spices sprinkles at a time. ( I make a pass of the width of the pan with the spices once then taste)
drain
return to skillet.
pass spices over meat and onion mix... lightly!
add 2 tbs catsup, 1/2 cup water, and worstershire sauce
bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until water is reduced, simmer on low stirring occaisionally.
