Title:
The Ultimate Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices hearty white bread, torn
1 cup whole milk
3 eggs, beaten
11/2 cup onion, minced
1 cup roma tomatoes, pureed
1/2 cup celery, minced
1 Tbs garlic, minced
1/2 fresh parsley, minced
2 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
1 Tbs salt
2 tsp black pepper
2# ground chuck
2# ground pork
Chunky BBQ Topping:
2 strips thick-sliced bacon, diced
1/2 cup onion, diced
1/2 cup green bell pepper, diced
1 Tbs garlic, minced
1 cup roma tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup prepared yellow mustard
2 Tbs red wine vinegar
2 Tbs brown sugar
salt to taste
1 tsp Tabasco, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Prepare two loaf pans
Combine bread, milk, and eggs in a large mixing bowl; soak 20 - 30 min
Then, lightly, mash to a paste consistency
Saute onion, tomato puree, celery, and garlic in butter over med-high heat for 3 min; cool for 5 min
Add parsley, Worcestershire, salt, pepper, and the tomato mixture to the bread mixture
Fold in ground beef and pork
Spoon meatloaf into loaf pans
Prepare the topping
Saute bacon in skillet over med-high heat
Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic; saute 3 min
Stir in remaining ingredients and simmer for 3 min
Spread over top of each loaf
Bake 11/4 hrs or until internal temp reaches 160
Let set 15 min before serving
