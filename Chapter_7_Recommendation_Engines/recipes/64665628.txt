Title:
Brown Rice  Broccoli Cheddar Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4570032.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked brown rice, cooled
2 Large beaten Eggs
2 Tablespoons melted Butter
1 cup shredded cheddar
1 1/4 cups  broccoli gently cooked and chopped small
2 Tablespoons minced yellow onion or green onion
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 Tablespoon Penzeys Fox Point seasoning
3 Tablespoons Coconut oil for frying

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together with spoon in a medium bowl.  Warm up iron skillet to medium low heat with one Tablespoon coconut oil.   Shape mixture into 6 same size balls.  Place  two balls into preheated skillet and press down with spatula to about 1/2 inch thickness and cook about 3 mnutes on each side.  Repeat.  *Cooks note -  You can also make these with mushrooms.
