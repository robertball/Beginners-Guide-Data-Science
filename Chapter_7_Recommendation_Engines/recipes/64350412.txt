Title:
Dark Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable Shortening________½ Cup
Powdered Sugar______________4 ½ Cups
Cocoa Powder, Dark__________¼ Cup
Salt, Kosher, Fine__________¾ Teaspoon
Heavy Cream_________________1 Cup
Canola Oil__________________1 ½ Tablespoons
Vanilla Extract, Pure_______1 Teaspoons

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Add the vegetable shortening to the mixing bowl, then sift the powdered sugar, and salt into it.
using the paddle attachment, beat the three ingredients together on medium low speed until the mixture forms a coarse texture.
Continue to beat the mixture; Combine the heavy cream, canola oil and vanilla extract, and add it to the blend.
Increase the speed to medium high and continue to beat the mixture until it forms into a smooth frosting.
