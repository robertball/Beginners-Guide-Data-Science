Title:
Mo's Bro’s Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6127441.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 cup Italian Bread Crumbs
1/2 Tablespoon Steak Sauce (ex: A1)
1/2 Tablespoon Liquid Smoke
4 slices Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat grill to medium high heat.
2. In a medium bowl, mix the ground beef,   Worcestershire sauce, bread crumbs, steak sauce, and liquid smoke using your hands. Form the mixture into 4 hamburger patties.
3. Lightly oil grill. Grill for 5 minutes, then flip and grill for an additional 4 minutes, or to your desired degree of doneness. The internal temperature should be at 180 degrees F (82 degrees C) when taken with a meat thermometer.
4. Removed burgers from heat and place on sliced cheese. Allow to rest and serve on buns with the usual trimmings.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Simple smoked flavored burgers that will melt in your mouth. You’d swear they were expensive gourmet.
