Title:
Chicken with Whiskey-Orange Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boned and skinned chicken breast halves
salt and pepper, to taste
Vegetable cooking spray
1/2 cup Irish whiskey
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup half and half

Directions:
Salt and pepper chicken breasts on both sides.  Heat a nonstick skillet sprayed with vegetable cooking spray until hot.  Add chicken and brown quickly on both sides.
Meanwhile, combine whiskey and honey together until blended.  Add mixture to chicken in pan, cooking on medium heat for about 3 minutes, turning chicken a few times.  Cover and cook chicken for 5 more minutes, adding orange juice halfway through.  Continue cooking until chicken is done; remove from pan.
Add half and half, blending into sauce mixture, and reducing by half over medium heat.  Spoon over chicken breasts and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
LINDA W.
Description: 
When I first made this recipe,  I used a mini-bottle of mead bought in Ireland, which dates back to Biblical times and was very popular in Great Britain in medieval times.  The mixture of whiskey and honey used in the recipe is a good substitute.
