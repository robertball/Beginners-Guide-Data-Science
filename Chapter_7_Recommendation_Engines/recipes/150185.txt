Title:
Maple-Poppy Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/45661.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 cup real maple syrup
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Whisk together mayonnaise, maple syrup, vinegar, sugar, and poppy seeds in a bowl until blended.

Submitter: 
Sue Cantara
Description: 
This simple recipe  was handed down to me from a very dear friend, and is great on any tossed green salad. You can add a few drops of red food coloring to make it pink if you wish.
