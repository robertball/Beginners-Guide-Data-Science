Title:
Grandma Wallace’s Jam Jams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup brown sugar
1 cup margarine/butter
¾ cup white sugar
3 eggs + 1 egg yoke (save the egg white to brush top of cookies)
2 Tbsp. corn syrup
4 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
Strawberry or raspberry jam

Directions:
Cream margarine; add sugar, eggs, syrup and vanilla, beating well.
Add dry ingredients and mix.
Drop a spoonful of dough on cookie sheet, make a depression in the center and fill with jam. Put ½ tsp. dough on top to cover jam hole.
Bake at 350 until golden brown (about 10 minutes).
Brush with egg white (that has been beaten until frothy).
