Title:
Cranberry Apple Crockpot Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3194117.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups unsweetened apple sauce
6 ounces cranberries, fresh or frozen (thawed if frozen)
1 medium apple, cored & sliced
2 cups Gluten Free rolled oats
2 tbsp pepita (pumpkin) seeds
1 tbsp chia seeds
1 tbsp hemp seeds
2 large egg whites
1/8 cup Truvia brown baking sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 cup unsweetened almond milk

Directions:
Spray inside of a crockpot with nonstick cooking spray
Place all ingredients inside crockpot
Cook on low 3 hours or high 1 hour until oats are cooked and cranberries and apples are softened
Serve in a bowl topped with Greek yogurt or reduced fat whipped topping

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Packed with protein and super seeds, this thick & creamy Cranberry Apple Crockpot Oatmeal makes a delicious, no fuss breakfast. Throw the ingredients in a crockpot & have yourself dessert for breakfast!
