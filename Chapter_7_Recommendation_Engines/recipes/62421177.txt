Title:
Crockpot Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can beef broth
8 oz sliced mushrooms, fresh
1 cup dry red wine
4 cups mini carrots
2 potatoes, cubed
1 bag frozen peas
2 onions, sliced

Directions:
Place beef stew meat, condensed French onion soup, condensed cream of mushroom soup, dry red wine and sliced onions into a slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours.
Add carrots, potatoes, mushrooms and peas.  Continue on low for 2 to 3 more hours.
