Title:
Cranberry Festive Sparkler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990208.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces Ocean Spray® Cranberry Juice Drink, any flavor, chilled
2 ounces sparkling water
Lime wedge, garnish

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice. Add cranberry juice drink. Top with sparkling water. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Ocean Spray®
Description: 
Combine cranberry juice, sparkling water and a lime wedge garnish to make a refreshing beverage that is perfect any time of year.
