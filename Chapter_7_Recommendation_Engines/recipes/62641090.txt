Title:
Country Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh haricot verts, trimmed
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped cooked ham
1/4 cup "I Can't Believe It's Not Butter"
1/4 cup water
2 garlic cloves, minced
Ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt "I Can't Believe It's Not Butter" in a sauce pan then saute onion & garlic until tender; add remaining ingredients. Cover and simmer for 5-10 minutes or until beans are tender.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mom
Description: 
This deliciously different way to dress up green beans is sure to become a family favorite at your house, too. The garlic, chopped ham and onion blend so well with the beans. It's a beautiful and tasty side dish that has real country appeal. -Linda Gaido, New Brighton, PennsylvaniaModified from: Country Green Beans
