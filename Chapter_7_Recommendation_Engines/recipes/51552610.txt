Title:
Licorice Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/739822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 c. light corn syrup
2 c. sugar
1 cup butter (not margarine)
1 t. anise extract
1/2 t. black paste food coloring

Directions:
Press foil onto the bottom and sides of an 8x8-inch square pan.
Butter the foil.
Melt butter.
Add sugar, corn syrup, and sweetened condensed milk, stirring to combine.
Continue to stir over medium heat until mixture reaches the firm ball stage, 244 degrees. This will take at least 15-20 minutes.
Remove from heat and add extract and food coloring.
Pour into prepared pan.
Let cool.
Cut into SMALL squares.
Wrap individually to keep them from sticking together.

This is a delicious blend of black licorice flavor (anise extract)and caramel. It is a tradition to make it for Thanksgiving in our family. The Wright family loves it! Picture from TasteofHome.com
