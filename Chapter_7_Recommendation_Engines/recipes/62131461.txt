Title:
Sweet 'N' Sour Chicken With Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves with skin or without
1 (8 ounce) can of crushed pineapple, undrained
1/3 cup of maraschino cherries
1 green bell pepper, cleaned and chopped
1 small or medium onion, chopped
1/2 cup honey
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 Tablespoon ginger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place the chicken breast halves in a medium baking dish.  In a medium bowl place the undrained pineapple, the maraschino cherries, the chopped bell pepper, the chopped onion and the cayenne pepper.  Mix ingredients well.
Pour the ingredients over the chicken breast halves and sprinkle with ground ginger.
Cover the dish and bake for 1 hour.  Serve this over rice.
Have salad as a sidedish.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
My Mother used to fix this ever since I was 10 years old, so the years have passed, but I still have her recipe and changed it just a little.
