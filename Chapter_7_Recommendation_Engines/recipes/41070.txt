Title:
Savory Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986649.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons white wine Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 teaspoons snipped fresh thyme
1 teaspoon snipped fresh rosemary
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground red pepper
2 cups macadamia nuts, broken walnuts, and/or unblanched almonds

Directions:
Combine Worcestershire sauce, olive oil, thyme, rosemary, salt, and ground red pepper. Spread nuts in a 13x9x2-inch baking pan. Drizzle with oil mixture. Toss gently. Bake in a 350 degree F oven for 12 to 15 minutes or until nuts are toasted, stirring occasionally. Spread on a large sheet of foil; cool. Store in an airtight container up to 1 week.
