Title:
Peanut Butter Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. sugar
1/2 C. corn syrup
1/2 C. peanut butter
1/2 t. vanilla
8 C. popped popcorn

Directions:
In a heavy saucepan, combine the sugar and corn syrup.
Cook to a rolling boil, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and stir in the peanut butter and vanilla.
Stir until creamy.
Pour over the popped popcorn.
Stir to coat.

Submitter: 
myfoursonsks
Description: 
My kids beg for this popcorn.  It is like caramel corn, only for peanut butter lovers. Since it uses ingredients that you will most likely have on hand, you can make this cheap, easy treat anytime.
