Title:
Baked Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
1 cup water
3 tbsp. quick-cooking tapioca
2 tsp. sugar
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 lbs lean beef stew meat, cut into 1" cubes
4 medium carrots, cut into 1" chunks
3 medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
2 celery ribs, cut into 3/4" chunks
1/2 medium onion, cut into chunks
1 slice bread, cubed

Directions:
In large bowl, mix ingredients well.
Pour into a greased 9" x 13" or 3 quart baking dish.
Cover and bake @ 375 for 1 3/4 to 2 hours or until meat and veggies are tender.
