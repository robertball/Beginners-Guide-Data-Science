Title:
Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium size ripe tomatoes (quartered)
1/4 medium size onion
4 sprigs cilantro (parsley)
2 cloves of garlic
2 tbsp. lemon juice
1 jalepeno (1/3 for mild, 2/3 for medium, all for hot and spicy)

Directions:
Put all the ingredients in your KITCHEN PLUS 2000 and mix to consistency desired.
Serve with tortillas.
Note: Can also put in food processor.
