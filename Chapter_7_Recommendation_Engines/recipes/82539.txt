Title:
Chicken  Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/16577.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small sweet onion, peeled and diced
1 (10 ounce) can canned chicken, drained
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup light mayonnaise
16 slices SARA LEE® Soft & Smooth™ Whole Grain White Bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a mixing bowl, combine sweet onion, drained chicken, mozzarella, and mayonnaise. Mix thoroughly.
Spread chicken mixture on 8 slices of  bread, and top with remaining slices to form 8 sandwiches. Place sandwiches on a cookie sheet.
Bake in a preheated oven for 10 minutes, until filling is hot and bubbly and bread is lightly toasted.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee Soft & Smooth™
Description: 
A combination of chicken and mozzarella is the filling for a delicious hot sandwich.
