Title:
Big Ray's Garlically Pull-Apart Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2598005.jpg
Ingredients:
16.3 oz can Refrigerated  buttermilk biscuits
½ c. finely grated Parmesan
4 tbsp. butter, melted
4 cloves garlic, minced
½ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Halve each individual biscuits horizontally – you will have 16 slices. In a bowl, combine cheese, butter, garlic, salt, and black pepper.
Using a pastry brush, brush the buttery mixture on both sides of each biscuit slice. Arrange biscuits in an 8 ½" loaf pan.
Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
