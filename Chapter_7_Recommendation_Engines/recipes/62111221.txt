Title:
Tuna and Goat Cheese Stuffed Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large tomatoes, hollowed out and drained, reserve ½ cup of insides and dice fine
4 ozs crumbled goat cheese
8 ozs mascarpone cheese, softened
1 large can albacore tuna, drained and shredded with a fork
½ cup red onion, diced fine
½ cup cucumber, diced fine
½ cup mushrooms, diced fine
½ cup red pepper, diced fine
½ cup celery, diced fine
1 tsp minced garlic
3 tsps toasted sesame seeds
2 tsps sunflower seeds
2 tbsps shredded parmesan cheese
Garlic salt and onion powder to taste
Salt & pepper to taste
Dried parsley for garnish
Worcestershire sauce for garnish

Directions:
Season insides of hollow tomatoes with a little salt & pepper.
In a large bowl, hand mix reserved diced tomato and next 10 ingredients (up to and including the sesame seeds).  Mix well.
Scoop mixture into tomato shells even with top of tomato.  Place on a foil lined baking sheet.
Sprinkle each tomato evenly with sunflower seeds, parmesan cheese, garlic salt, onion powder and salt & pepper to taste, place a pinch of parsley and a few drops of Worcestershire sauce on top and bake at 350F for 25 minutes and then broil for 1-2 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Glenn
Description: 
My neighbor delivered a very large bag of his summer tomatoes, and I didn't want them to go to waste. I experimented with the stuffing mixture until my husband loved this combo.  Easy, tasty, satisfying, and a good choice for hot summer nights!
