Title:
Cream of Celery Soup for Eight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups (approx 1 can stock) chicken stock
1 tsp. Better Than Bouillon - Chicken or Mushroom
6 oz celery, coarsely chopped
1 oz carrots, julienned
1 oz onions, chopped
2 Tbs all-purpose flour
1/3  tablespoon salt (or less)
1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper
1.5 cups hot milk
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp dill
1/2 tsp parsley

Directions:
Pour the chicken stock into a large pot, and bring to a boil. Add the celery, carrots and onion to the pot.  Boil for 20 minutes or until veggies are soft; then strain out the vegetables by pouring through a colander or strainer. Put in blender and puree. Add back into stock. Whisk together the flour, seasonings, and milk; add to the pot along with the margarine and cook gently until thickened.

Submitter: 
Judith
Description: 
Creamy celery soup for eight. This is a variation on the Cream of Celery Soup by Wonderfalk. Veggies are blended and returned to soup.Modified from: Cream of Celery Soup
