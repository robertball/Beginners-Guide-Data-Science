Title:
Italian Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/617605.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups popped popcorn
4 1/2 teaspoons butter, melted
3/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
2 tablespoons grated Romano cheese

Directions:
Place the popcorn in a bowl.  Combine the butter, Italian seasoning and garlic salt; pour over popcorn and toss to coat.  Sprinkle with cheese; toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Nancy  Reichert
Description: 
In Thomasville, Georgia, Nancy Reichert jazzes up plain popcorn using this 10-minute recipe you'll want to make time and again.  Simply add butter, seasonings and Romano cheese for a fast and flavor-packed snack.
