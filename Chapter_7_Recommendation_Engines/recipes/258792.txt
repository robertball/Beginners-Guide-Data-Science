Title:
Basil Pesto Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4531999.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup tahini
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/2 cup basil pesto
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, basil pesto, olive oil, garlic, and salt in a blender until well combined.
