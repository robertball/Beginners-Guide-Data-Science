Title:
Lentil-vegetable stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¼  c. lentils
4 c. veggie or chicken broth
1 c. water
2 c. peeled and cubed sweet potatoes - 9 oz.
½ c. chopped onion
½ c. chopped celery
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. soy sauce
2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. coriander
salt to taste
1 ½ c. sliced zucchini
1 ½ c. broccoli florets

Directions:
In a large pot or Dutch oven, combine lentils with 3. c. of stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer till lentils are barely tender (about 30 minutes).
Add remaining ingredients except zucchini and broccoli and cook, partially covered, for about 40 minutes on medium heat.
Add zuc and broccoli and cook about 10 minutes longer, till veggies are tender.
