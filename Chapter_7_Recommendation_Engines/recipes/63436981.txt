Title:
HOT CLAM SPREAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans minced clams (7 1/2 oz. each) with juice
1 c. dried bread crumbs
1/2 lb. melted butter'
1/2 ts. oregano
1/2 tsp parsley flakes
Garlic salt to taste
Dash of salt and pepper
4 oz. mozzarella cheese (grated)
1/2 c. Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except cheese, until gloppy.  Put in a 9 inch oven proof dish
Sprinkele with Parmesan cheese and the grated mozzarella cheese
Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
Serve with crackers.
