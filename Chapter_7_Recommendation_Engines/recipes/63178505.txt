Title:
LeighAnne's Famous Beaver Dam Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
1/3 c. dry unsweeted cocoa
1/2 c. milk
1 c. coconut
2 c. white granulated sugar
3 1/2 c. quick dry oats (oatmeal)
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 c. chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Melt butter and combine the rest of the ingredients in a medium-large saucepan over medium heat.
Stir and bring to a rapid boil.
Drop balls of mixture onto waxed paper.
Freeze for about 30 mins-1 hour.

Submitter: 
Natalie Herr
Description: 
This recipe is from the back of my mom's 4th grade reading book. These are more of a candy rather than a cookie because they are frozen rather than baked. Tip: read through the entire recipe before making so that you have an idea of what to do.
