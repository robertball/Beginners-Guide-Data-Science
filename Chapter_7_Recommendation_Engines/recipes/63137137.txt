Title:
Sure-Fire Steak Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. Corn Starch
2 tbsp. Soul Food Seasoning or Seasoned Salt
2 tbsp. Ground Kosher Salt
2 tsp. Garlic Salt
4 tsp. Ground Black Pepper
1 tsp. Red Pepper Flakes
1 tsp. Lemon Pepper
1 tsp. Red Cayenne Pepper
2 tsp. Dried Oregano
2 1/2 tbsp. Garlic Powder
2 tsp. Paprika
2 tsp. Beef Bullion (or Granulated Beef Stock)

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients into large measuring bowl.  Stir rigorously to ensure ingredients are mixed thoroughly and evenly.
Funnel rub mixture into large spice shaker (I use recycled spice containers).
Makes approximately 1 cup.
Use this rub to coat steaks, burgers, beef ribs, briskets, or any other cut of beef and ENJOY!

Submitter: 
webbbilly
Description: 
I have been experimenting with steak rubs for a few years, and I finally settled on this one.  It's extremely versatile and can be used to season almost any kind of beef, any way you cook it.  I don't cook steaks or burgers anymore without it!
