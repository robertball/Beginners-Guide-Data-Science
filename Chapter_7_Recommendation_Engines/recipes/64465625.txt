Title:
Sara's Slow Cooker Chicken-a-la-king Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs chicken breasts cut into bite sized pieces
1 (10 oz) can condenced cream of chicken soup
1/2 c. milk
1 tsp dried minced onion
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp paprika
salt and pepper to taste
1 c. frozen peas and carrots
2 c. left over mashed potatoes, heated

Directions:
Combine the first 8 ingredients (through salt and pepper) in slow cooker.
Cook on low for 6 hours or high for 3 hours.
Add peas and carrots and continue cooking for an additional 30 minutes.
Serve over mashed potatoes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chicken and vegetable gravy. Can also be served over rice.
