Title:
Adobo Chicken Tacos (Nick’s favorite) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bone in, skin on chicken breasts
1 t. salt
½ t. black pepper
¼ t. red pepper flakes
¼ c. olive oil
4 garlic cloves, minced
28 oz. can whole tomatoes
2 chipotle peppers (canned in adobo sauce), finely diced
2 T. of the adobo sauce

Directions:
Season chicken with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes. Brown chicken in olive oil in Dutch oven over medium heat just until all sides are lightly browned.
Cover and place in preheated 375 degree oven for approximately 45 mins. Debone chicken and set aside.
Place same pot with drippings over medium heat and add garlic, tomatoes (you may want to break the tomatoes up a bit), chipotle peppers and adobo sauce, then add the chicken. Simmer for 30 minutes Serve in taco shells or tortillas of your choice with toppings such as sour cream and grated Monterrey Jack cheese. Serves 4-6.
