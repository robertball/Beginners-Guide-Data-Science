Title:
Pasta with Green Vegetables and Crock-Pot Pesto Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1/2 large onion
1/3 cup pesto
2 1/2 cups chicken broth
12 oz linguini uncooked
salt
pepper
2 cloves of garlic; minced
3 tbls olive oil
1 small green pepper
1 green italian squash
1/2 bunch fresh spinach washed and dried
1/4 cup white wine
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Slice the onion into thick rings.
Spread half of the pesto in the bottom of the crock pot; layer onion rings on top.
Place chicken breasts on top of onion and spread remaining pesto on chicken.  Cover and turn to high- cook 2 hours.
Add 1/2 cup of chicken broth to the crock pot. Turn to low cook 4 hours.
Once chicken is done;
Chop green pepper and italian squash into small cubes- set aside.
Chop ends off of spinach; discard.  Coarsely chop leaves. set aside.
Start boiling water and cook linguini as package directs.
In a large skillet heat olive oil over medium heat and add garlic.  Cook until golden.
Add green pepper cook until soft about 5 minutes.
Deglaze pan with wine- scrape pan and add remaining chicken stock turn heat to high and boil for 5 minutes.
Place on medium heat and add Italian squash; salt and pepper to taste.
Continue cooking until squash is soft.
Drain pasta.
Add spinach to skillet.  Cook until wilted.
Meanwhile remove chicken from crockpot and set aside.
Place pasta into large serving dish and with a slotted spoon remove onion from crock pot and place onto pasta in servng dish.
Pour sauce and green veggies over pasta, mix well.
Place chicken on top and shred with a fork; if desired.
Sprinkled with parmesan cheese.
Serve immediatley.
