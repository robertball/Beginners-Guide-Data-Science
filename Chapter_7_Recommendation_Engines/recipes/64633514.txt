Title:
Airfried Arancini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rice
615 g water
28 g evoo
42 g butter
5 g zafferano powder
8 g salt
3 g pepper
280 g arborio rice
Bay leaf (whole-not crushed!)
14 g white onion
Ragù with peas
Flour/salt/water
Bread crumbs

Directions:
Add all ingredients except rice, bring to a boil.
Add rice and cook until liquid is absorbed. Wooden sppon should stand on its own. Remove bay leaf.
Cool and refrigerate
Put some rice in the palm of your hand and flatten it. Put in a walnut sized amount of ragù and close the rice around it.
Roll it in the flour, salt, and water mixture and smooth it out with your hands.
Roll it in the bread crumbs.
Cook for 10 minutes on 200°C
