Title:
Baked Mini Lemon Cake Doughnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460512.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cups cake flour, sifted
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon zest
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 1/2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F, spray mini doughnut pan with nonstick cooking spray.
In a large mixing bowl, sift together flour, sugar, baking powder, lemon zest and salt.  Add buttermilk, egg and butter and stir until just combined.  Fill each doughnut cup approximately 1/2 full.
Bake 4-6 minutes or until the top of the doughnuts spring back when touched.  Let cool in pan 4-5 minutes before removing.
Finish the doughnuts with glaze or sprinkles.
