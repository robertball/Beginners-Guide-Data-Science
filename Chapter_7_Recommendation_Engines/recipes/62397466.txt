Title:
Healthy chocolate breakfast shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/428811.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup quick cooking oatmeal
1 ripe banana, peeled and cut into chunks
1 tbsp cocoa powder
1 tbsp smooth peanut butter
1 cup skim or soy milk

Directions:
Pulse the oatmeal in a blender to a fine meal.
Add remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
A nutritious way to fix that chocolate craving, this healthy shake has no sugar added and packs a punch of protein.
