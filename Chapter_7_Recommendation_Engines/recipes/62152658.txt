Title:
Here's To Good Friends Bahama Mama Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz Siced Rum
1 oz Malibu Rum
1 oz Banana Liqour
2 oz Pineapple Juice
2 oz Orange Juice
A splash of Grenandine

Directions:
Fill a Mertini Shaker with ice cubes. Add all ingerdients except Grenandine to the shaker. Shake vigorously until the shaker is very cold. Strain into a cocktal or Martini glass. Add Grenadine and garnish with a slice of Pineapple.
