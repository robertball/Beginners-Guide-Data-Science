Title:
Baked Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951280.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can crushed pineapple
3 TBSP tapioca
1/2 c white sugar
1/2 c milk
1 egg
Cinnamon

Directions:
Whisk egg, add all ingredients together.
combine well and pour into 8x8 baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Top with cinnamon after backing.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
This side dish is a family favorite with our Easter ham.
