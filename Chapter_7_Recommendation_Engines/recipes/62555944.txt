Title:
Cheese Filled Chicken Cutlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
flour
1/4 tsp. salt
butter or margarine
1 8 oz. pkg. mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
1/8 tsp. pepper
2/3 c. water
2/3 c. milk
1 chicken flavored bouillon cube

Directions:
Cut each chicken breast in half, yielding 12 pieces for this recipe. With meat mallet, pound each chicken cutlet to about 7/8‚Äù thickness. On waxed paper, mix salt with flour; coat chicken. In skillet over medium heat, melt 4T. butter.  Add cutlets, a few at time; cook until lightly browned, adding more butter as necessary. Remove cutlets. Arrange cheese slices on 6 cutlets; top with the remaining cutlets. Skewer cutlets together with toothpicks and set aside. In drippings remaining in skillet, melt 2 more T. butter. Stir in pepper and 1 T. flour until blended. Gradually stir in water, milk and bouillon. Heat to boiling, stirring to loosen brown bits from bottom. Return cutlets to skillet; heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer for 5 min. Discard toothpicks.
