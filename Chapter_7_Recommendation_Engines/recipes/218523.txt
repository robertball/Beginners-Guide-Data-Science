Title:
BBQ Chicken Pizzeria Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982080.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 recipe Perfect Parmesan Pizza Dough (enough for 1 crust)*
1/2 cup pizza sauce
3 tablespoons KRAFT Original Barbecue Sauce
1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
2 thick red onion slices, separated into rings
2 cups KRAFT Mozza-Cheddar Light Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees F.
Roll Perfect Parmesan Pizza Dough into 12-inch circle on lightly floured surface. Press onto bottom of 12-inch pizza pan sprayed with cooking spray, stretching dough as necessary to fit. Let stand 10 min.
Mix pizza sauce and barbecue sauce; spread onto dough. Top with remaining ingredients.
Bake on bottom oven rack 17 to 18 min. or until crust is golden brown.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
No need to order in when you can make gourmet-style pizzas at home--and with delicious barbecue sauce and rotisserie chicken, this pizza's ready in less than an hour!
