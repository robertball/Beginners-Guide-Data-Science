Title:
Blueberry Buckle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. butter
3/4 C., 1/2 C. Sugar, separated
1 egg.
2 C., 1/2 C. flour, separated
2 1/2 t. baking powder
1/4 t. salt
1/2 C. milk
2 C. fresh blueberries
1/2 t. cinnamon
1/4 C. butter

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350°
Cream butter, 3/4 C. sugar well.
Add egg and beat until light and fluffy.
Mix together 2 C. flour, baking powder, and salt.
Add alternately with the milk a little at a time to butter mixture.
Spread in greased 11x7 pan. Top with berries
Mix 1/2 C. sugar, 1/2 C. flour and cinnamon.
Cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over berries.
Bake at 350° for 45 min. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A Dowdle Family classic fruit dessert similar to a cobbler. (Just make sure you don't confuse your sugar for your salt!)
