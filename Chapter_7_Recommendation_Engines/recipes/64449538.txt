Title:
Cranberry Thokku Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Olive oil
12 oz. Cranberry (Chopped Finely)
1 tsp. mustard seeds
1 tsp fenugreek seeds
1/8 tsp. turmeric powder
1/8 tsp. asafoetida
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp achar masala

Directions:
1. Heat oil, mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, turmeric, and asafoetida over medium
2. Mix in cranberries
3. Add salt and achar masala
4. Stir until it forms a cohesive ball
5. Remove from heat and let cool before serving.
