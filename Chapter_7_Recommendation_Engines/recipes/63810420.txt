Title:
The Best Slow Cooker Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes of chicken or vegetable stock.  1 can of potato soup (your choice) 2 chicken breasts.  3 stalks of celery, chopped.  1/2 head of cabbage, chopped.  3 medium carrots, chopped.  1 bunch fresh parsley, destemmed. 2 cups of water.

Directions:
Add chicken/vegetable stock, potato soup and chicken breast to the crock pot.  Cook on medium for 4 hours or until chicken is cooked thoroughly.  Remove chicken and shred.  Place back in crock pot.  Add the rest of the ingredients and water.  Cook on medium/ high for 2 hours or until vegetables are cooked.

Submitter: 
Bizzle
Description: 
I have perfected this recipe to replicate my Hungarian grandmothers homemade soup.  It's delicious, hearty and healthy!
