Title:
"Heavenly" Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10-12" angel food cake (store bought or homemade)
8 large eggs
1 cup heavy cream
1 tsp. vanilla or almond extract
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup real maple syrup (may substitute store bought)
pinch salt

Directions:
Spray a 9 x 13 pan.
Break angel food cake into bit-sized pieces and place in pan.
Combine remaining ingredients, whisking until well blended.
Pour liquid over angel food cake pieces.
Cover and refrigerate overnight, or place in refrigerator two hours.
Remove cover and place in preheated 350-degree oven.
Bake for 40 minutes or until golden brown and toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Remove from oven and cut into squares.
Serve hot with maple syrup, jam, or fresh berries and whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Light and fluffy, not too sweet, easy to make. Treat your family to this breakfast made in heaven. :-)  This may be made a day ahead or allowed to stand in the refrigerator 2 hours before baking.
