Title:
Grilled Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/872611.jpg
Ingredients:
10 	ears corn on the cob
1 	red bell
1 	yellow bell
1 	Anaheim pepper
1/2 	red onion
1 tsp 	Mrs. Dash
2 tsp 	olive oil
4 oz. 	cheese, cubed (mozzarella)

Directions:
Remove husks and wash corn. Cut red and yellow bell in half and remove seeds.
Grill till slightly charred. Remove corn from cobs. Remove skin from Anaheim pepper and remove seeds. Chop peppers. Finely dice red onion. Cube cheese. Place all ingredients in large bowl. Add Mrs. Dash and olive oil and stir till well blended.

Submitter: 
Lynns Kitchen
Description: 
A wonderful way to utilize fresh corn on the cob, and the backyard grill.
