Title:
Muhummara Roasted Red Pepper Walnut Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7165347.jpg
Ingredients:
3 red bell peppers
1 1/2 cups raw walnuts, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon Aleppo Pepper
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2 1/2 tablespoons pomegranate molasses
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
Kosher salt and pepper to taste
Coriander to garnish

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350F.
Place bell peppers on a baking sheet, and roast them for 10 min. Half way through, change the setting to broil rotate frequently until skin is slightly blackened.
Peel the red peppers using a paring knife and discard the peels, stem and seeds. Throw the roasted red peppers, walnuts, garlic, paprika, cumin, red pepper flakes, lemon juice, pomegranate molasses and olive oil into a food processor with a teaspoon of salt, and run until the dip is smooth.
Garnish with coriander.
Cool and serve.
