Title:
Easy  Cheesy Ground Beef Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. ground beef - browned
2 Cups cooked white rice
7-8 baby carrots - diced
3 stalks celery - diced
3 cloves garlic - diced
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 small cans tomato sauce
1 can chicken broth (14 oz)
5 small tomatoes cut into  chunks
tsp. italian seasoning
tsp. cumin
tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. ground red pepper
pepper to taste

Directions:
Begin to brown ground beef on medium heat.
When beef is half way browned, add carrots, celery and garlic.
Finish browning beef and drain grease.
Add tomato chunks, tomato sauce, and seasonings. Heat over low - medium heat to warm thoroughly and soften tomatoes.
Add can of chicken broth to cooked rice. Stir.
In a large casserole dish, evenly disperse beef mixture and rice. Move around until completely mixed.
Top evenly with cheese and cover dish with foil.
Put dish in oven set at 375 degrees until cheese is completely melted.
Let cool for 5-6 minutes.
