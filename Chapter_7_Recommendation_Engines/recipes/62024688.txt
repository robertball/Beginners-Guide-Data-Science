Title:
Onion Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves
1 1/2 cups white rice, uncooked
2 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 packet dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Spread rice evenly on the bottom of a 9x13x2 inch baking dish.
Pour chicken broth over rice.
Place chicken breasts on top of rice.
Spoon cream of mushroom soup over each chicken breast to coat.
Pour dry onion soup mix over chicken and rice.
Bake for one hour or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.  During cooking time, stir rice to make sure the rice on top stays moist.

Submitter: 
Michelle A
Description: 
Chicken breast and creamy onion flavored rice make a great meal! My sister-in-law gave me this recipe that she got from her mother-in-law.  I thought it should be shared with everyone because it is so quick, easy and delicious.  It tasts just as good the next day!
