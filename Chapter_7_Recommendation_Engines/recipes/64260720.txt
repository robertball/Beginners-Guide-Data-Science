Title:
Sweet and Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 egg
2 Tbsp onion, minced
2 cups breadcrumbs
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp marjoram
2 Tbsp parsley
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
Oil for frying
SAUCE:
1 cup catsup
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 Tbsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Combine ground beef, egg, onion, breadcrumbs, 1/2 tsp salt, marjoram, parsley, 1/4 tsp black pepper and 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce.  Make small balls and brown in hot oil.  Drain on paper towels.
Combine remaining ingredients and cook until well blended. Pour sauce over meatballs and serve.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Tangy twist for meatballs.
