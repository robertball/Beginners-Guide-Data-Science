Title:
Classic Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 carrots, diced
2 ribs celery, sliced
1 large onion, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
salt and pepper
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 cup small shell pasta or macaroni
1 cup shredded escarole  or kale
1 15.5 oz can cannellini or navy beans, drained
Grated Parmesan, for serving, optional

Directions:
1. Combine carrots, celery, onion and garlic in slow cooker. Power in broth and stir in tomatoes with their liquid, Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper. Cover and coook on low for 4 to 6 hours.
2. About 30 minutes before serving, stir in pasta, escarole and beans. Cover, increase heat to high and cook until pasta is tender, about 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot, topped with Parmesan, if desired.
