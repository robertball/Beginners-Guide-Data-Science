Title:
Strawberry Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart fresh strawberries
1 pint heavy whipping cream
6 egg whites
2 tbsp sugar
extra berries to top

Directions:
Chop strawberries in food processor, still leaving some chunks, add sugar. Set aside. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Set aside.  Whip egg whites until stiff peaks (may use a bit of cream of tartar to speed up process.
Gently fold together strawberries and heavy cream.  When blended gently fold in egg whites.
Put in serving cups (parfait glass) and top with a fanned strawberry.
