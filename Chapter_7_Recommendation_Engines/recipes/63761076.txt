Title:
Connin Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 lb of ground pork (or sausage)
3 eggs
1/2 c milk
1/2 package of crackers
2 T Worcestershire
1/2 onion, chopped super fine
1/2 c ketchup
2 T brown sugar
5 cloves garlic
1 t savory
1 c ketchup
1/2 c brown sugar
1 T Worcestershire

Directions:
Preheat over to 350.
Mix all ingredients, careful not to over mix.
shape into 2 loaves
mix last 3 for glaze, pour over loaves
bake for 1 hour
