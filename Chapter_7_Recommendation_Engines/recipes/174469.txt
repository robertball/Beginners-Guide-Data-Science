Title:
White Bean Pesto Dip with Pita Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 (15 ounce) cans cannellini (white kidney) beans, rinsed and drained
1 (7 ounce) container BUITONIÂ® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
3 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
Pita chips

Directions:
Place beans in food processor; cover. Process until smooth. Stir in pesto. Add lemon juice to taste. Season with ground black pepper. Serve with pita chips.
