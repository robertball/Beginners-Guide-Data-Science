Title:
Cranberry Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4993779.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon white sugar, or as needed
1 teaspoon salt, or as needed
ice cubes
1 orange, sliced
1 lime, sliced
4 ounces 100% cranberry juice (such as Ocean Spray® Organic)
2 ounces silver tequila (such as Patron®)
1 ounce triple sec
1 orange, juiced
1 lime, juiced
8 ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage (such as Squirt®)

Directions:
Rim 2 wine glasses with sugar and salt. Fill with ice. Add an orange slice and lime slice to each glass.
Pour cranberry juice, tequila, triple sec, orange juice, and lime juice into a shaker. Shake well.
Pour mixture evenly over ice in rimmed glasses. Top with lemon-lime beverage.

Submitter: 
Ashleigh Evans
Description: 
Switch up your margarita with this delicious cranberry variety. No sugary margarita mix needed, just fresh fruit!
