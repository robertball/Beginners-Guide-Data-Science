Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2442992.jpg
Ingredients:
5 ripe bananas
1 bag large marshmallows
1 cup dark chocolate chips
2 teaspoons coconut oil
1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)
1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional)

Directions:
1.  Cover a baking sheet with parchment or wax paper.
2.  Slice bananas into 1 inch pieces.  Slice marshmallows in half.
3.  Place a toothpick in a marshmallow half and then into each banana slice. Freeze for 15 minutes.
4.  Mix chocolate chips and coconut oil in a double boiler. Cook over medium heat, stirring until smooth. 5.  Remove banana slices from freezer, dip each slice in melted chocolate, and place on the lined baking sheet.
6.  (Optional) Dip in shredded coconut or chopped nuts.
7.  Place in freezer for at least 15 minutes.
Serve frozen.

Submitter: 
Gabriel Vincent
Description: 
When you have to make snacks for a whole brigade of monkeys and you don't have time to make a batch of pasta with jam sauce, these bites are perfect to tame even the hungriest insect/prehistoric dinosaur hybrid.
