Title:
Strawberry Pretzel Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups crushed butter gold pretzels
4 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
3/4 cup butter, melted
1 cup white sugar
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (6 ounce) package strawberry flavored Jell-OÂ®
2 cups boiling water
1 (16 ounce) package frozen strawberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix together the pretzels, 4 1/2 tablespoons sugar and melted butter.
Press into the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan. Bake for 10 minutes only.
Set aside to cool completely.
In a medium bowl, beat the sugar,  cream cheese and vanilla, until smooth.
Fold in whipped topping. Spread evenly over the cooled crust.
Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
In a medium bowl, stir together the gelatin mix and boiling water. Mix in frozen strawberries, and stir until thawed. Let cool until slightly thickened.
Pour over cream cheese mixture in pan. Refrigerate until completely chilled, at least 1 hour.
