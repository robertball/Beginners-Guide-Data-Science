Title:
Roasted Grape, Blue Cheese, & Honey Crostini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound red grapes, removed from the stems
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 French baguette, thinly sliced and toasted
4-6 ounces Blue cheese
Honey, for drizzling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Place grapes on a large baking sheet. Drizzle grapes with olive oil and gently toss. Roast for 15-20 minutes or until grapes are soft and slightly shriveled. Let the grapes cool to room temperature.
Place 3 grapes on top of toasted baguette slice.
Top with a little blue cheese and drizzle with honey.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
From: two peas and their pod.
Crostini with roasted grapes, blue cheese, and a touch of sweet honey. This easy appetizer is perfect for parities.
