Title:
Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons olive oil
1 1/2 pounds ground turkey (or what ever ground meat you like)
1 jalapeno (finely chopped no seeds)
3 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
1 Tablespoon ground cumin
1 Tablespoon ground chili powder
Salt and Pepper to Taste
3/4 cup water

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet over medium/high heat.  Add ground turkey to pan and season with salt.
After turkey is browned drain if necessary.
To the browned meat add jalapeno, onions and garlic.  Cook until onions are transparent.
Stir in cumin, chili powder and pepper.  Let cook for 3 minutes then add water and adjust heat to low.
Adjust salt to taste if necessary.
Serve with taco shells, lettuce, tomato, olives, refried beans etc.
