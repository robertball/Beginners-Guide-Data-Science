Title:
Kahlua® and Club Soda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060426.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Kahlua
3 fluid ounces club soda
Lime wedge (optional)

Directions:
Pour Kahlua and club soda over large ice cube or crushed ice in an old-fashioned glass. Garnish with a lime wheel or wedge, if desired.

Submitter: 
Kahlua®
Description: 
Kahlua and club soda on the rocks or over crushed ice makes a delicious cocktail; garnish with a lime wedge if you like!
