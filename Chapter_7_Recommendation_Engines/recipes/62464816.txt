Title:
Pumpkin Harvest Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/475889.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/4 c. chicken broth
3/4 c. pumpkin puree
1 apple, diced
2 tbsp. coconut oil or butter
1 tsp. honey
1 tsp. balsamic vinegar
1/2 tsp. grated fresh ginger
4-6 fresh sage leaves, diced finely
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 c. orzo pasta
1 tbsp. heavy cream

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine chicken broth, pumpkin, apple, coconut oil or butter, honey, balsamic vinegar, ginger, sage, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Add orzo.
Reduce heat to low, and simmer, stirring frequently, for about 15 minutes, or until orzo is tender.
Remove from heat and stir in cream. Serve immediately.
