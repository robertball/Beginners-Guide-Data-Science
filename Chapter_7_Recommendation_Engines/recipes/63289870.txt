Title:
Thousand Island Dressing Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish (More if you like.)
1/2 - 1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1-3 teaspoons prepared horseradish, depending on taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Chill.
