Title:
Cucumber Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/134485.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium cucumber, peeled, seeded, and diced
1 medium tart apple, chopped
1 cup halved green grapes
1 cup halved seedless red grapes
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the cucumber, apple and grapes. In a small bowl, combine the sour cream, sugar and parsley. Pour over cucumber mixture and toss to coat. Stir in walnuts if desired. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Trudie  Hagen
Description: 
From Roggen, Colorado, Trudie Hagen sends this tasty twist on a traditional Waldorf salad. Cool cucumber and juicy grapes give this summer salad its light and refreshing difference.
