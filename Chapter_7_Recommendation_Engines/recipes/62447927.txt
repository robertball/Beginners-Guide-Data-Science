Title:
Baked Orange Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 Cup Splenda Brown Sugar
2 Tbsp Asian sweet chili sauce
1 Tbsp Asian garlic chili sauce
2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
2 C orange juice, reduced sugar
4 T light butter, divided
2 T flour
1/2 Cup sliced green onions

Directions:
Combine mustard, orange juice.
Lightly brown the onions in non stick pan, sprayed with olive oil spray.  Remove from pan, and lightly brown the chicken.
Spray 9X13" pan with olive oil spray. Place chicken in pan, cover with the onions.  Sprinkle with the brown sugar and dot each breast with 1/2 Tbsp light butter.
Bake at 375 for 45 minutes.  Remove chicken and cover with foil.
Melt last 1 Tbsp of butter, add flour to make a roux.  Pour in pan juices, and bring to boil to thicken.
Serve chicken in sauce.  This is great with fresh broccoli and brown rice.

Submitter: 
empebi
Description: 
This is a lightened and reduced sugar version of some of the orange chicken recipes I've used in the past.  It may be light, but it is still delicious.
