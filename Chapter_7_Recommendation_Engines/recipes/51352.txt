Title:
Saganaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces feta cheese
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon minced fresh oregano
1/2 cup all-purpose flour for coating
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium tomato, thickly sliced
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 lemon, cut into wedges

Directions:
Cut feta into 8 slices. In a small bowl, mix together egg and oregano. Dip feta slices in egg, shake off excess, and coat with flour.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry cheese until golden brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels. Arrange on a plate with tomato slices, and sprinkle with pepper. Serve with lemon wedges.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth Chorley
Description: 
Greek appetizer consisting of fried breaded feta cheese served with tomatoes and lemon wedges.
