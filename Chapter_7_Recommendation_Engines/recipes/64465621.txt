Title:
Pulled Pork Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. shredded cooked pork roast
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 c. barbecue sauce (e.g. Baby Ray's)
1 Tbsp hot sauce (e.g. Chalula)
salt and pepper
sandwich buns
sliced dill pickles

Directions:
Melt butter in medium sauce pan. Stir in shredded pork.
Add barbecue sauce, hot sauce, salt and pepper, stirring over medium low heat until heated through.
Serve on sandwich buns with pickles.

Description:
