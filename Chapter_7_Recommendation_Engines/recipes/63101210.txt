Title:
Streusel Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Streusel:
2 1/2 Tab. packed light brown sugar
2 1/2 Tab. all-purpose flour
1/2 tea. cinnamon
pinch of salt
2 Tab. cold butter
Bread:
1 cup canned unsweetened pumpkin puree
1 1/2 cups packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup veg. oil
2 eggs
1 tea. vanilla
1 1/2 cup all-purp. flour
2 1/4 tea. pumpkin pie spice
1 tea. baking soda
1 tea. salt
1/2 tea. baking powder
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Place oven rack to the bottom third of the oven and grease a loaf pan with butter, oil, or spray. Streusel: In med. bowl whisk together all but the butter. Then cut in the butter with pastry blender or hands until it resembles course meal. Set aside.
Bread:
In a large bowl whisk together pumpkin, sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla. In a med. bowl whisk together the flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking soda, salt, and baking powder. Gently fold the flour mixture into the pumpkin mixture just to combine. stir in the raisins. Pour into loaf pan and smooth the top. Top with the streusel. Bake at 350 55-60 min. Until toothpick comes out clean. Let cool in the pan on wire rack for 10 min. Then pop it out and let it cool completly on the rack before slicing.
