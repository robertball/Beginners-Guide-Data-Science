Title:
Strawberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh spinach
2 c fresh strawberries, sliced
1 orange, peeled and diced
1/2 c fetta cheese, crumbled
1/4 c pecan halves
1/2 c kiwi, peeled and sliced
1/2 c Strawberry kiwi diet green tea
1/4 c apple cider vinegar
1 T honey
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/4 c olive oil

Directions:
Mix spinach, strawberries, orange, fetta cheese, pecan halves, and kiwi.
For dressing: Mix tea, vinegar, honey, garlic salt, black pepper,mustard, and olive oil.
