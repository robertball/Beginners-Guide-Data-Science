Title:
Raspberry Chocolate Bailey's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566222.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz Bailey's Irish Cream
1 oz Chocolate Syrup
1 oz Raspberry Syrup

Directions:
Pour chocolate syrup into the bottom of a Bailey's glass.
Pour Bailey's over chocolate syrup.
Top with raspberry syrup.
Add 2 ice cubes and serve with a swizzle stick so your guests can stir and combine the flavours!

Submitter: 
Gitano
Description: 
I created this for the Jan 2011 GangUp Challenge ~ Appetizers & Cocktails Edition. This is my cocktail creation! A yummy blend of chocolate syrup, raspberry syrup and smooth Bailey's Irish Cream!
