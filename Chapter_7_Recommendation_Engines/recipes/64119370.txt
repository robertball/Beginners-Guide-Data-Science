Title:
Combat Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 tsp cinnamon
1 cup oats
1 tsp baking powder
2 egg whites

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients until you achieve a pancake-like consistency.
Preheat a pan over medium heat, then spray with nonstick cooking spray.
Place mix into pan and cook both sides until fully cooked through. Test with a toothpick.
