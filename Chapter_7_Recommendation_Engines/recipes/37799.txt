Title:
Parmesan Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979311.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces spaghetti
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Prepare spaghetti as directed on package. Drain.
Cook and stir garlic in butter in same pan on medium heat until tender. Add spaghetti; toss lightly.
Sprinkle with cheese; toss to coat. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley, diced tomatoes and additional cheese, if desired. Serve immediately.
