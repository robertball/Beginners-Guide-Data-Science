Title:
Chicken Pasta Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 4 large boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1" cubes
- 12 oz. shell pasta
- 2 small zucchini, chopped into 1" pieces
- 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped into 1" pieces
- 1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped into 1" pieces
- 2 medium carrots, chopped
- 2 cups sliced mushrooms
- 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
- 1 cup fresh basil leaves, shredded
- 1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes in oil, drained & diced (reserve oil)
- 1/4 cup oil from sun dried tomatoes
- 2 tsp. dried basil
- 1 tsp. garlic powder
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 6 large cloves garlic, diced
- 3 tsp. chicken boullion granules
- 1/2 cup Half and Half
- 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese.
- salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the chicken in airtight container and add reserved oil from sundried tomatoes as well as dried basil and garlic powder.  Mix well, seal with lid and marinate in refrigerator for 2-4 hours.
Cook pasta in boiling water for 8-10 minutes until al dente.  Drain and reserve.
Preheat large skillet over medium high heat.  Add chicken and cook until done all the way through and browned on outside.  Remove chicken from skillet.
Add 1/4 cup olive oil to skillet over medium high heat.  Add garlic and carrots and cook 5 minutes.  Add peppers and zucchini and cook additional 5 minutes.  Add mushrooms, spinach leaves, fresh basil and sun dried tomatoes and continue cooking 5 minutes more.  Add pasta and chicken and stir to combine.  Sprinkle chicken bouillion over top and add half and half.  Stir to combine.  Add parmesan cheese, stir and heat through.  Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve.
