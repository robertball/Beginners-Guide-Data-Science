Title:
Kansas City Style Chili Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978752.jpg
Ingredients:
8 HORMEL速 Hot Dogs
8 hot dog buns, split
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL速 Chili No Beans
2 cups preshredded coleslaw blend *
1/3 cup coleslaw dressing
Favorite barbecue sauce

Directions:
Grill hot dogs. Heat chili. In bowl, combine coleslaw blend and dressing; toss to coat. To assemble dogs, place hot dogs in buns. Top each with chili, coleslaw and favorite barbecue sauce.
