Title:
Lentil Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2117565.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c uncooked black lentils
1 T Olive oil, ghee or coconut oil
1/2 red onion, finely diced
1/2 yellow onion, finely diced
4-8 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 carrot large, finely diced
1/2 red pepper, finely diced
1-2 T cumin plus more
1-2 T chili powder
1-2 T smoked paprika plus more
oregano to taste
3-4 T cider vinegar
BBQ seasoning
Smoked salt
2 14.5oz cans diced fire roasted tomatoes
1-2 c vegetable stock
1 can tomato paste
2 T maple syrup (or honey? or brown sugar?)
1-2 tsp mustard (optional)
1/2 red onion, sliced
1/2 yellow onion, sliced
hamburger buns

Directions:
pick over/rinse lentils
put lentils in pot, cover with water, bring to boil and reduce heat to simmer, cover and cook with lid cracked until cooked al dente
meanwhile, heat oil, saute onion, garlic, carrots, red pepper until softened
add spices
add tomatoes, tomato paste, cider, broth, mustard
simmer 30 min or to taste
fry onions for serving
