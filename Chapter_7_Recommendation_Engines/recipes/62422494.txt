Title:
Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
lime juice
salt and pepper
cod filets (appox. 1 pound)
1/2 cup plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 cup lowfat sour cream
1/4 cup lowfat mayonnaise
1 lime, juiced
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1 teaspoon minced capers
2 Tablespoons coarsely chopped cilantro
1 Tablespoon chopped fresh dill
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon ground ancho chili
1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
1/4 medium head green cabbage, finely shredded
2 cups baby spinach leaves, chiffonaded
1 avocado
1 tomato

Directions:
Stir together yogurt, sour cream, mayo, jalapeno, capers, herbs and spices. Thin with lime juice until slightly runny.
Thinnly shred cabbage and spinach. Slice avocado. Dice tomato.
Coat fish with oil and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper. Grill. Warm tortillas briefly on grill.
To serve, place fish in a tortilla. Top with sauce, avocado, tomato, and shredded greens.
