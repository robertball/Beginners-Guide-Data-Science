Title:
Oatmeal Fudge Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/728114.jpg
Ingredients:
Cookie Mixture:
2 cups brown sugar
3/4 cup butter
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 cups Bisquick
3 cups quick oats
Chocolate Sauce:
1 pkg milk chocolate chips
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 tsp vanilla
2 Tbsp margarine
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Mix together butter, eggs, and 2 tsp vanilla.  Then add brown sugar, Bisquick, and oats.
In saucepan melt and stir chocolate chips, condensed milk, vanilla, margarine and salt.
Spread 3/4 of oat mixture on sprayed cookie sheet.  Pour chocolate mixture over top of oat mixture.  Place small pieces of remaining  1/4 of oat mixture randomly over chocolate.
Bake for 15-20 minutes.
