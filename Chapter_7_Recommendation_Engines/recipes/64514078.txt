Title:
Simple Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs ground turkey
1 large onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, smashed and diced
4 cans stewed tomatoes
1 can tomato sauce
1 can beans
2 teaspoon dried oregano
3 tablespoons chili powder

Directions:
Soften onions in olive oil, add salt
Brown turkey
Add garlic
Add stewed tomatoes, squeezing as they are being poured into the pot
Add tomato sauce and spices
Simmer for 1 hour
Add beans and cook for additional 20 mins
