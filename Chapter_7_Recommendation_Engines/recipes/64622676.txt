Title:
Cauliflower Mash "Potatoes" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 T ghee
5-8 cloves garlic
1 head cauliflower
2-3 c low sodium chicken stock
salt and pepper

Directions:
saute garlic in 1 T ghee until softened
add cauliflower
add chicken stock and bring to boil
reduce to simmer and cook until very tender
strain cauliflower, reserve stock
puree cauliflower with 1-2 T ghee
salt and pepper to taste
add stock if too thick
