Title:
Gina's Cheesy Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow onion, minced
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 Tbsp butter
1 can creamed corn
1 can corn niblets
1 package Jiffy dry corn muffin mix
1 1/2 cps Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
1  Preheat oven to 350F.
2  Melt butter in pan and saute green pepper and onion til tender (apprx 5-10 min).
3  Pour creamed corn, niblets, and dry muffin mix into 2 qt casserole dish.  Mix thoroughly.
4  Add peppers and onions and stir thoroughly.  Cover casserole with shredded Cheddar--okay if cheese is thick.
5  Place in oven and bake at 350F for 1 hour.  Remove when cheese is bubbling.  Serve immediately!

Submitter: 
DOCTOR KITTEN
Description: 
My stepmother always used to make this awesome and easy corn casserole.  A great side dish that satisfies even the pickiest eaters!
