Title:
Tarter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c Miracle Whip
2 tablespoons relish
1 tablespoon ranch dressing
1/8 teaspoon paprika
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients into a small bowl and stir until everything is combined.
Refrigerate for 5-10 minutes.
Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
Yummy tarter sauce that goes great with fish sticks.
