Title:
Monster Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C butter (1lb.)
2 C dark brown sugar, packed
1 1/2 C sugar
3 eggs
1 T vanilla
1 1/2 t. vanilla
1 t. salt
6 C flour
1 1/2 t. baking soda
4-6 C chocolate chips
2-3 C broken pecans
1/2 C secret ingredient

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Cream butter, sugars.
Add eggs and vanilla.
Add secret ingredient, then dry ingredients, ch. Chips, and nuts.
Cover cookies sheet w/ waxed paper (change each time)
Use scoop to make large or small balls depending on preference.
Bake 350° 8-10 mins for small, 10-12 for large. Do not over bake!

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These are the most disgustingly tasty cookies you will ever try. Sadly, if you are not a Jones (or married to one) you cannot have the secret ingredient. Even without it, they make pretty darn good cookies.
