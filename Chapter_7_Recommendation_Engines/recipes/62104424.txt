Title:
Easy Banana Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Basic Pie Pastry:
1-1/8 cups All Purpose Flour
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/8 tsp Baking Powder
1 Stick of Butter, chopped
2-3 tablespoons Water
Pie:
Pie Pastry
8 Large Egg Yolks
1/2 cup Sugar
1/4 teaspoon Salt
3 tablespoons Cornstarch
2-1/2 cups Whole Milk
1-1/4 cups Heavy Cream
2 tablespoons Butter, cut into pieces
1 tablespoon Vanilla Extract
2 Bananas, sliced

Directions:
Roll out pie pastry into 13-inch circle, 1/8 inch thick. Fit into 9-inch pie pan. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit. Prick dough with fork. Line pie shell with parchment paper and fill with pie weights or dried beans(so that pastry won't puff) and bake 15 mins. or until light brown, removing paper and beans 3 mins. before fully baked. Set aside.
Whisk yolks, sugar, and salt until well blended. Mix in cornstarch.
Scald milk in saucepan over medim-high heat. Whisk into egg mixture until smooth. Return mixture into saucepan over medium-high heat, whisking 3 mins. Take pan off heat and whisk in 1/4 heavy cream, butter, and 2 teaspoons vanilla.
Place bananas in pie crust. Pour mixture in and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate overnight. Whip remaining cream and vanilla until it holds shape. Spoon over pie and serve.
