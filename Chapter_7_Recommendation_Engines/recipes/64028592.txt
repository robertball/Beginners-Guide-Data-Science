Title:
Chop Salad with Lemon Basil Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
Juice from 1/2 lemon
1/8 cup fresh basil - chopped
Seasoned salt to taste
Garlic pepper seasoning to taste
Salad:
4 cups chopped iceberg lettuce
1/2 cup radicchio - julienned
1/2 cup fresh spinach - julienned
1/4 cup green olives - chopped
1/6 cup red onion - chopped or very thinly sliced
3 oz. fresh mushrooms - sliced
2 hard boiled eggs - chopped
7 oz. chilled hearts of palm - drained, rinsed and sliced
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
1/4 lb. crisp bacon - chopped
1/4 cup plain croutons
Cherry tomatoes - halved

Directions:
Dressing:
in a medium bowl, combine all ingredients and refrigerate until serving.
Salad:
In a large bowl, combine lettuce, radicchio, spinach, olives, onion, mushrooms, eggs, hearts of palm and bacon.
Toss well.
Add blue cheese, croutons and 1/2 to 3/4 of dressing.
Toss until well mixed.
Garnish with tomatoes and serve.
