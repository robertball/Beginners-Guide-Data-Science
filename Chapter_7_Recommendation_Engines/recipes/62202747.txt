Title:
Perfect Punch (non alcoholic) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754045.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bottle of cranberry-raspberry juice or coctail
1 bottle of 7 Up or Sprite
1 tray of fresh raspberries (I have used blackberries as well)
1 or 2 trays of ice

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and serve in punch bowl or jug. Great for kids!

Submitter: 
Jason F.
Description: 
I make this punch every New Years Eve and it's always a big hit with everyone!
