Title:
Red Snapper with Tomato Sauce (peppers, celery & onions) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 (6 ounce) fillets red snapper
Dash black pepper
Dash Old Bay seasoning
1 cup sliced onions
1 cup celery (chopped)
1 cup green pepper (chopped)
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon flour
1 14 oz. can tomato sauce

Directions:
1.Season all fillets with black pepper and Old Bay seasoning. Place aside. 2.Heat olive oil and place onions, celery and peppers into oil and saute slowly. 3.Once transparent, remove from oil and place the fillets in olive oil. 4. Adjust temperature to sear fillets on both sides (3 minutes) per side.  5. Remove fillets. (If there is excessive oil in pan, place tablespoon of flour into saucepan and stir around until flour has browned making a roux.) 6.Now add tomato sauce and continue to stir until smooth consistancy. 7. Now place all ingredients into tomato sauce (fillets/veggies). Allow to marinate (10 minutes).Prepare to serve!

Submitter: 
Nanasnthakchen
Description: 
This is a family favorite. Red snapper is a meaty fish and stands up well with this recipe. I have tried with other fish and found that any fillets turn out very well!Modified from: Shrimp-Topped Red Snapper
