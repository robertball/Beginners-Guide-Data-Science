Title:
Stuffing Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5lb ground round
1 egg
1 small white or yellow onion chopped
3/4 cup stuffing (I use chicken Stoufers)
Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray coat 1 loaf pan or 4 mini loaf pans.
Hand mix all ingredients except cheese. Press in to prepared pan(s). Bake 35-40 minutes for large loaf or 25-30 minutes for mini loaves.
Remove from oven and top with shredded mozzarella. Let rest while cheese melts for ten minutes. Slice and serve.
