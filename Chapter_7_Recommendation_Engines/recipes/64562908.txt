Title:
Simple Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 small pork chops
2 tbsp. oil (canola or other)
salt & pepper, sprinkle
284 ml can condensed cream of mushroom soup
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. paprika

Directions:
Brown chops in oil in frying pan.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place in small roaster.
Stir together soup, Worcest. sauce and paprika.
Spoon over chops.
Bake, covered, @ 350 for about 1 1/2 hours, until tender.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
An old classic for a reason - easy, tasty comfort food that you most likely the ingredients on hand for. A creamy mushroom sauce baked over chops.
