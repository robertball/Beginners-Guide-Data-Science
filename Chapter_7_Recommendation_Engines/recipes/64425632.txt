Title:
Cranberry Yogurt Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2314450.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup yogurt (plain, vanilla, or strawberry)
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 cup flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup cranberries (fresh or frozen, chopped in half)

Directions:
Soak oats in yogurt.
Add oil, sugar, and egg- beat well.
Stir in flour, salt, soda, and baking powder.
Stir to blend. Add cranberries.
Fill muffin tins and bake at 400 for 20 minutes.
