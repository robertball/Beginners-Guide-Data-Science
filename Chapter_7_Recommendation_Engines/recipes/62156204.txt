Title:
Brooke's Italian Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1 c. celery hearts, chopped
1/2 c. shredded carrots
1 c. cucumbers, chopped (I prefer persian)
1 c. canned artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
2/3 c. italian dressing (I prefer Good Seasons mix prepared with rice vinegar)

Directions:
1.  Combine all ingredients and refrigerate at least one hour before serving.
