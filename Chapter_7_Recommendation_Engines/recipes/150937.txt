Title:
Vodka Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978594.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle rose wine
6 ounces Ciroc® Vodka
3 ounces Grand Marnier liqueur
2 ounces white grape juice
2 ounces lemon juice
2 ounces simple syrup
3 ounces pomegranate juice
3 ounces strained orange juice
4 ounces club soda
1 pint blackberries or raspberries

Directions:
Pour rose wine, Ciroc(R) Vodka, Grand Marnier liqueur, white grape juice, lemon juice, simple syrup, pomegranate juice, and strained orange juice in a large pitcher half filled with ice (entire mixture should be light pink).
Placea small amount of ice into serving glasses. Pour the wine mixture over the ice, top each glass with about 1/2 oz. of club soda; garnish with blackberries or raspberries.
