Title:
Cheesy Fiber Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/785811.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup crushed Fiber One cereal
1/2 Cup corn meal
1/2 Cup flour (all purpose)
1 Cup milk
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 egg
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic salt
half a can of diced green chiles
2 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a muffin tin with 9 paper liners.
In a mixing bowl stir together the flour, corn meal, cereal, baking soda and powder, garlic salt and onion powder. Slowly add the cup of milk until everything is coated. Stir in egg, chiles, brown sugar, and cheese.
Pour the corn muffing batter into the liners, 2/3 the way full.
Bake in preheated oven for 12 minutes or until the tops are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
These are moist corn muffins with a slight spice to them and lots of hidden fiber! Great to serve with a bowl of spicy chili as these are mild and gooey!
