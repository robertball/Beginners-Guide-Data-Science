Title:
Stuffed Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium sized zucchini
1 tsp olice oil
2 jalapeno peppers, seeds removed and finely chopped*
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup fresh whole kernel corn
1 small tomato, chopped
1/3 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup shredded Monterrey jack or cheddar cheese
1 tablespoons fresh cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Trim ends of zucchini and cut each in half, lengthwise. Scoop out and discard pulp, leaving 1/2-1/4 inch shells. Place zucchini cut side up in a shallow baking pan.
In a large skillet heat olive oil and add chile peppers and onion. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute more. Stir in corn, tomato and black beans. Cook for about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and add 1/2 of the cheese and the cilantro.
Divide mixture evenly among the zucchini halves and cover with the remaining cheese. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.
