Title:
Black Eyed Pea Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 14 oz. cans black eyed peas (do not drain)
1 can rotel tomatoes
1 14 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 tbl. olive oil
1 med onion chopped
5 celery stalks chopped
2 cloves garlic chopped fine
1 med. bell pepper chopped
1 cup brown rice
2 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Sautee onions, garlic, bell pepper and celery in 1 tbl. olive oil until just tender. Add remaining ingredients. Cook until rice is done. Add water if soup is to thick. Serve with a tossed salad and corn bread. A winter time favorite at our house.
Submitted by: Lynn Patterson

Submitter: 
Lynn Blackwelder Patterson
Description: 
A great winter time soup.  We always have it for New Years Day while we watch football!
