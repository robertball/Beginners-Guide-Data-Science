Title:
Sweet & Sour Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into bite sized pieces
2 Tablespoons of sesame oil
one clove minced garlic
Thin noodles (rice noodles, kimran, etc.)
Sauce:
one can of low sodium chicken broth
1/4 cup of fish sauce
1/4 cup of low sodium soy sauce
3 Tablespoons of sweet chili sauce
1 Tablespoon of Worsteshire sauce
juice of 1/4 lime
1 Tablespoon minced ginger
pinch of crushed chili flakes
1 Tablespoon of smooth peanut butter
Veggies:
can of bean sprouts
can of baby corn pieces
can of shoestring bamboo
1/2 cup of shredded carrots
1/2 cup of chopped white onion
1/4 cup of diced green onion

Directions:
Add all of the sauce ingredients into a measuring cup and heat in the microwave for 2 minutes, stir and set aside
Brown the chicken and garlic in sesame oil in a wok on medium heat
While the chicken is browning, boil the noodles until just about ready (do not overcook them), drain and set aside.
Add all of the veggies except the green onion into the chicken and let cook for 2-3 minutes, stir often.
Add all of the sauce into the veggies & chicken, turn the heat up a bit and let simmer for 5 minutes stirring often.
Turn down the heat to low and turn in the noodles gently until fully combined, let sit for 5 minutes stirring every minute or so.
Add the green onions, stir, and serve! Enjoy!

Submitter: 
ShannonMcK
Description: 
This sweet & spicy noodle dish is a favorite of my family! Add more spice with extra crushed chilies, or more nutty flavour by adding in crushed cashews or peanuts with the noodles!
