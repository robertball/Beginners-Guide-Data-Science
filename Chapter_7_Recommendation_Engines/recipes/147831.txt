Title:
All-Bran速 Yogurt Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup rye flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon caraway seeds
1 teaspoon anise seeds
2 cups low-fat yogurt
2 eggs
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/2 cup light molasses
1 teaspoon orange flavoring
1 cup All-Bran速 Original

Directions:
Stir together flour, rye flour, baking powder, soda, salt, caraway seeds and anise seeds. Set aside.
In large mixing bowl combine yogurt, eggs, applesauce, molasses and orange flavoring. Beat well. Add Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal. Let stand 2 minutes or until cereal softens. Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined. Portion batter evenly into two 8 1/2x4 1/2x2 1/2-inch loaf pans coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 350 degrees F about 50 minutes or until wooden pick comes out clean. Let cool 10 minutes and remove from pan. Cool on wire rack. Wrap in plastic wrap.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's速 All-Bran速
Description: 
Caraway and anise seeds, molasses, and a hint of orange add distinctive flavors to this robust quick bread.
