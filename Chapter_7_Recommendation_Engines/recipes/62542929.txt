Title:
Doggie Delight Turkey Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground turkey
1/2 cup cooked vegetables (I use broccoli)
1 tsp garlic powder
1 egg
1/2 cup quick cooking barley
1 cup quick cooking oats
4 oz. fat-free gravy

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease a 9"x5" loaf pan
In a mixing bowl mix turkey, vegetables, garlic powder, egg, barley and oats.
Mix together thoroughly.
Spoon mixture into prepared loaf pan and press down until level.
Spoon gravy over top.
Bake in preheated oven for 1-1.5 hr.
Cool and slice into individual portions.
Refridgerate.

Submitter: 
Laurie Connors
Description: 
I wanted to make our canine crew (5 dogs) a healthy and tasty meal treat with some ground turkey that I had in the fridge. I slice the cooled loaf and cut each piece into individual bites that I add to their kibble. They all agree that it's a Bow Wow WOW!
