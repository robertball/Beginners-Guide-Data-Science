Title:
Strawberry Sparkler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup strawberries, hulled and chopped
2 tbsp cold water
1 slice of lime
sugar
Lime slice or Strawberry for garnish

Directions:
Put strawberries and water into your blender and puree/liquify for about a min. Pour strawberry liquid into small sauce pan. On medium heat, bring to a simmer  then turn heat to low and simmer and stir constantly til reduced. Take off of heat and cool.
If you are using a sugar rim, run a slice of lime around the rim of your glass, turn glass, rim down, into a small pile of sugar and turn to coat all of the rim edge.
Add 2 tbsp of reduced strawberry puree to your glass, fill with ice and pour in your favourite sparkling water. Garnish with a strawberry and or slice of lime.
This recipe makes enough to make two tall glasses.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
If you're not into sweet drinks, this one's for you. A  nice refreshing drink made of reduced strawberry puree and your favourite sparking water. A sugar rim is optional!
