Title:
Microwave Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 cup salted peanuts
1 teaspoon butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda

Directions:
Butter a cookie sheet. Combine sugar and corn syrup in a 2 quart glass bowl and microwave on high 3 minutes. Stir in peanuts and microwave on high 2 1/2 minutes more, then stir in butter and vanilla and microwave for 1 minutes.
Stir in baking soda until light and foamy.  Pour onto warm cookie sheet and spread thin.  Cool completely and break into pieces and serve.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
Salted peanuts in a sugary, crunch candy.  All the cooking is done in the microwave, then just pour it onto a cookie sheet, let it cool, break it off and eat it.Modified from: Microwave Peanut Brittle
