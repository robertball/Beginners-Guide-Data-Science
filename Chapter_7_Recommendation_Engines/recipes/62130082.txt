Title:
Low Fat Sausage and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound Package 99% fat free or 93/7 Ground Turkey
2 Large Stalks Celery
1 Medium Onion
1/2 Bell Pepper (any color will work)
1 Can Water Chestnuts, drained
1 Can Reduced Fat Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Can Reduced Fat Cream of Celery Soup (cream of Mushroom may be substituted here)
3 Cups Cooked Brown Rice
Dash Red Pepper
1 Tbsp. Dried Parsley (2 Tbsp. if using fresh)
1 tsp. Ground Sage
1/2 tsp. Ground Nutmeg
1/2 tsp. Ground Black Pepper
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
**1 tsp. crushed red pepper (optional for a more spicy sausage)

Directions:
Cook brown rice according to package.
Add rice to a large baking dish.
Chop Celery, Bell Pepper, and Onion add to baking dish.
In large skillet combine ground turkey, Red Pepper, Parsley, Sage, Nutmeg, Ground Black Pepper, Garlic Powder, and **Crushed Red Pepper.
Cook turkey mixture until brown and crumbled.
Drain.
Add Turkey mixture to baking dish.
Mix well
(If preparing a day or two in advance stop here and chill.)
Dice Water Chestnuts and add to mixture.
Add Cream of Celery and Cream of Chicken soups. Mix well
Bake 350 Degrees Covered for 40-45 minutes or until heated through and bubbly.
Let stand uncovered 5 minutes before serving.
