Title:
Raisin Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raisins
1 1/2 cup water
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 Tablespoon cornstarch
1 Tablespoon vinegar
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1/4 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Boil raisins in the water for 5 minutes.
Add sugar mixed with cornstarch, spices, mustard and salt.
Cook, stirring until thickened.  Blend in vinegar
Serve hot over ham.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
A great sauce for ham from a church friend
