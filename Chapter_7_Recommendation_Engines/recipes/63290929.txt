Title:
Healthy Banana Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups oats (old fashioned kind, not quick cooking)
1 single serving cup of plain low fat greek yogurt
2 eggs
3/4 cup sweetener of choice (sugar) or 1/2 cup pure maple syrup...or try honey
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 bananas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease muffin tins (buttter/spray).  Do not use paper liners.
Place all ingredients, including bananas in a blender or food processor and blend until oatmeal is smooth
Divide batter among muffin tin and bake for 20-25 mins or until toothpick comes out clean.
Enjoy!
