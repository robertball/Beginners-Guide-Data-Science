Title:
Coconut oil Banana Bread with Chocolate Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs, slightly beaten
3 mashed medium size ripe bananas
4 Tablespoons milk
1/2 cup coconut oil
1 cup sugar
1 3/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (optional)
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs slightly.  Add the bananas, milk, coconut oil and sugar and beat until combined.  Mix the flour, soda and salt together then add it to the banana mixture.  Beat until smooth and fully combined.  Stir in the chocolate chips and the nuts if desired.  Bake for one hour, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.  Cool on a rack.

Submitter: 
SueHoo
Description: 
A simple, super-moist loaf of fresh banana bread with the added decadence of chocolate!  I never make my banana bread with regular butter or shortening anymore since discovering how delicious it is when made with coconut oil.
