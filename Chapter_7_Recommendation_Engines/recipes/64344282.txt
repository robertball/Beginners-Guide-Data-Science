Title:
Ginger Chicken Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2093567.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb ground chicken, preferably dark meat
1/2 cup plain dry bread crumbs
1/3 cup minced scallions, plus thinly sliced scallions for garnish
3 tablespoons minced peeled fresh ginger
1 large egg
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
2 teaspoons soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
canola oil for brushing

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450Â°
In a large bowl, mix together all of the ingredients
Form the mixture into 1 1/2" balls and arrange on a parchment lined baking sheet
Brush the meatballs with canola oil and bake for about 13 minutes, until browned and cooked through
Transfer the meatballs to a platter and serve

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
This is a twist to the traditional meatball and healthier too.  Enjoy!
