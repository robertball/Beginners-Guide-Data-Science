Title:
Famous Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. frozen Southern Style Hash Browns
1 can cream of Chicken Soup
1/2 cup Butter or Margarine
1/2 cup Onion, chopped
8 oz. Sour Cream
10 oz. Sharp Cheddar Cheese
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place potatoes into a greased 9 by 13 pan.
Pour remaining ingredients on top except for the cheese.
Bake uncovered at 350 for 25 minutes and then stir and add the cheese.
Bake for another 35-45 minutes until lightly brown on top.
Enjoy!
