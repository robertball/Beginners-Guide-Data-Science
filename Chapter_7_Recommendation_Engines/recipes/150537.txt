Title:
Broccoli and Cheddar Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978914.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Minute® White or Brown Rice
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
1 (10.75 ounce) can reduced fat cream of mushroom soup
3 cups 2% milk
1 pound 2% processed cheese, cubed

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions.
In a medium saucepan, combine broccoli, soup and milk.  Bring to a simmer over medium heat.  Add cheese and stir until melted.  Remove from heat and stir in rice.

Submitter: 
Minute® Rice
Description: 
This quick and easy broccoli cheese soup recipe is made with Minute® White or Brown Rice, frozen broccoli, cream of mushroom soup and cheese.
