Title:
Hidden Valley Ranch Crunchy Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen petite peas
1 cup celery, diced
1 cup cauliflower, chopped
1 cup cashews, chopped
1/4 cup green onions, diced
1/4 cup crisp-cooked, crumbled bacon*
2 tablespoons pimentos, chopped
3/4 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard

Directions:
Rinse peas in hot water (or steam, if fresh); drain. Combine peas, celery, cauliflower, cashews, onions, bacon and pimientos in a large bowl. Whisk together dressing, sour cream and mustard in a small bowl; pour over salad mixture. Toss gently. Chill.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Crunchy vegetables, crumbled bacon, and cashews coated in a creamy Ranch and sour cream dressing.
