Title:
Cheesy Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen peas or a few handfuls of fresh.
2 tbls. butter
1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1 tbls. of lemon juice
1/4 tsp. white pepper

Directions:
Boil the peas until cooked, then drain, saving a small cup of the cooking water.
Put the peas back in the pan with some of the reserved water, and add butter.
Stir, and then sprinkle in the Parmesan cheese.
Squeeze lemon juice.
Sprinkle white pepper.
