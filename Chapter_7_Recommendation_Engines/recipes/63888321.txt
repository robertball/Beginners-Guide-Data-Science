Title:
Chris's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078713.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups mashed bananas
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup butter at room temp
1/2 cups white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1//4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
Topping:
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
2 tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9x5 inch pan.
Combine banana and sour cream. Set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
Beat in eggs one at a time, vanilla and banana mixture.
Combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt; stir into the banana mixture.
Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.
Mix topping ingredients until well combined. Sprinkle on top of batter.
Bake for 60 minutes in preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the bread comes out clean.
