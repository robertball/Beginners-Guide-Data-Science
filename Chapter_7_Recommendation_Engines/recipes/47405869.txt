Title:
Soup Lover's Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBSP BUTTER
2-3 CUPS SLICED SHALLOTS
8 OZ SMALL ELBOW MACARONI
1 1/4 CUP MILK (I USE LOWFAT)
2 TBSP HOT SAUCE (I USE SALSA VERDE)
2 CUPS PACKED GRADED CHEESE MIX OF SHARP/EXTRA SHARP AND WHITE CHEDDAR
1 1/2 TBSP ALL PURPOSE FLOUR
2/3 CUP CRUMBLED FETA OR GOAT CHEESE

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 400 SPRAY CASSAROLE DISH MELT BUTTER -ADD SHALLOTS SALT AND PEPPER AND CARMALIZE UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN COOK MACARONI, DRAIN, SET ASIDE. BRINK MILK AND HOT SAUCE TO A SIMMER..TOSS GRADED CHEESE IN FLOUR TO COAT, AND ADD TO MILK/HOT SAUCE, STIR UNTIL SMOOTH AND MIXTURE RETURNS TO A SIMMER, ADD MORE SALT AND PEPPER IF NEEDED, POUR INTO CASSAROLE DISH TOP WITH CARMALIZED SHALLOTS AND CRUMBLED FETA CHEESE. BAKE UNTIL HEATED THUR ABOUT 15 MINS(I USUALLY LEAVE IN DOUBLE LONG SO ITS BROWN ON TOP
