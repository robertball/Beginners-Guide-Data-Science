Title:
Grandma's Corn Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamed corn (14-15 oz)
4 eggs, separated
1/2 cup flour, noted as scant
1 cup milk
1/2 cup butter, melted
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Original Directions:  Mix together adding stiffly beaten eggs last. Bake 1 Hour at 325, serve immediately.
Updated:
Preheat oven to 325Â°F, grease a souffle dish.
Beat egg whites to stiff peaks.
In another bowl mix corn, egg yolks, and flour until no lumps remain.  Add milk, butter, salt and pepper.
Add 1/4 of egg whites, fold gently to lighten the mixture.  Add remaining egg whites and fold gently until just combined.  Pour into prepared dish.
Bake one hour, serve immediately.
