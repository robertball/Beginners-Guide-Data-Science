Title:
Excellent Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 C Flour
3/4 C Margarine(1 1/2 sticks)
2/3 C chopped pecans
Filling:
8 oz soft cream cheese
1 C Powdered sugar
16 oz Cool whip(1/2 for cream cheese layer, 1/2 for top)
2nd Filling:
6 oz instant pudding made

Directions:
Blend flour, margarine and nuts. Press into pan and bake 350F until browned. Cool. Mix cream cheese, sugar and 1 C Cool whip, spread on crust. Make pudding, spread over cream cheese layer. Top with rest of Cool whip. Garnish as desired with nuts, chocolate curls, cherries.

Description: 
Layered with flour/nut crust, cream cheese filling, chocolate pudding and cool whip
