Title:
Spicy chicken noodle soup with jalapeño Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042764.jpg
Ingredients:
Use as many jalapeños as you want.
1 tbs butter
2 cloves garlic minced
1/2 c chopped onion
1 lg jalapeño  sliced (seeded if you desire)
1/2 lb cooked chicken breast, chopped
32 oz chicken broth
1 package of cooked thin spaghetti. about 1 1/2 cups
juice of one lime
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
lime wedges for garnish

Directions:
In a large pot over med heat melt the butter. Add the garlic and cook about 1 min or just until it's fragrant. do not let it brown.  Add the onion and jalapeño and sauté until just tender about 5 min. Stir in the cooked chicken. Pour in the broth and bring to a simmer. Stir in the noodles and heat through.
Add a squeeze of lime juice, cook about 1 non, taste and adjust seasoning adding salt and pepper to taste. Just before serving stir in the cilantro.
Serve with topped lime slices.
