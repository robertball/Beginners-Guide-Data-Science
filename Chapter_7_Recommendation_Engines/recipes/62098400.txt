Title:
Mom's Creamy Cheese Sandwich Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. butter
1 Raw egg, beaten
1 Tbsp. vinegar
1/2 Cup heavy cream
2 Hard boiled eggs, chopped
1 Small jar chopped pimentoes, drain liquid
1/2 Cup shredded American cheese
1 Tbsp. onion, chopped fine
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients in a medium saucepan, cook and stir over medium heat till thickened. Take from burner and stir in remaining ingredients. Refrigerate till cold for sandwiches.
This recipe can be doubled easily if desired.

Submitter: 
Kaye Lynn
Description: 
My mom made this for us when we were growing up and we loved it. It's creamy, rich and oh, so good! It's also good served open-faced on cocktail rye bread as an appetizer.
