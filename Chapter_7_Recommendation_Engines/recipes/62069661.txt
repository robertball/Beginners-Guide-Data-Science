Title:
Slow Cooker Fettucine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cube of butter
2 chicken breasts, cubed
1 envelope of "Good Seasons" Italian dry mix
8 oz. of cream cheese
1 can cream of chicken
1 /2 can of water
Fettucine noodles

Directions:
Combine the butter, chicken, and dry mix in the slow cooker and cook on low for three hours. Then add the cream cheese, cream of chicken, and water. Continue cooking this for another hour.
After this is cooked, let the alfredo sit and thicken while you cook the fettucine noodles. Serve once alfredo is thickened and the noodles are cooked al dente.
