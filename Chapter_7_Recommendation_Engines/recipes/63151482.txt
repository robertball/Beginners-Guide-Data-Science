Title:
Jenang Grendul - Cassava Porridge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/807340.jpg
Ingredients:
1500 ml water
250 gr palmsugar
1 pandan leaf
500 gr cassava
150 gr Tapioca flour
Bahan-bahan saus:
ingredients sauce:
750 ml santen cair (thin coconut milk)
1/2 tsp salt
1 pandan leaf

Directions:
1. Slice the steamed cassave into small cubes.
2. Roast the sagon dry in a skillet or Oven. Disolved in part of the water or bake at 400 for 10 min
Heat in a pan the water. add the palm sugar, the pandan leaf and the cassava and cook it slowly until tthe cassava is done and the palmsugar is dissolved. Mix Sagon with Water, add to brown sugar mixture with high heat. Remove it from the pan and serve it with the sauce.
preparation/method sauce:
Heat is a pan the santen together with the salt and the pandan leaf and cook it slowly until the sauce thickens.
