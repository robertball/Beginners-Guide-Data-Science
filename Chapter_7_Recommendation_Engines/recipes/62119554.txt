Title:
Peanut Butter Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough
INGREDIENTS:
2 C all-purpose flour
1 C whole wheat flour
1 (.25 ounce) package active
dry yeast
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 cup warm water (110
degrees F
Topping:
1/4 -1/3 C peanut butter
2 T honey
1 C shredded mozzerella cheese

Directions:
Combine flour, salt, sugar, and yeast in a large bowl.
Add oil and water.
Spread out on a large pizza pan building up the edges
Let sit in a warm place for 15min.
Bake for 15 min at 375
Spread a thin layer of peanut butter
Drizzle with honey
top with cheese
Bake for 10-15 min or until cheese is melted.
You may use pitas (not pocket) instead of pizza dough, for individual pizzas.

Submitter: 
jalexandropoulos
Description: 
My little sisters would eat this at preschool 30 years ago.  We liked it so much, we got the recipe and baked it at home for a special treat.  It makes a great lunch that kids love.  This is not a desert, as you may assume.
