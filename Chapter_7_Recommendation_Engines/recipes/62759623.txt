Title:
Sweet and Sour Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3lbs lean pork cut into pcs
2T oil
1/4c water
1/2c br sugar
2T cornstarch
1t salt
1/4 c vinegar
1T soya sauce
1 can pineapple chunks reserve juice
1 gr pepper julienned
2 tomatoes cut into eigths
1 peeled orange in chunks

Directions:
Brown meat in oil and drain.  Combine water w brown sugar, cornstarch, salt, vinegar and soy sauce w liquid from pineapple.  Stir to blend.  Add browned meat and stir to coat.  Cover and cook on low 6-7 hours.  Add remaining ingredients and cook for 1 hr.  Serve over cooked rice
