Title:
Thanksgiving Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 c. butter or margarine
3/4 c. evaporated milk
21 large or 2 1/2 c. miniature marshmallows
6 ounces white chocolate (or bark coating)
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/4 tsp. cloves

Directions:
In a saucepan, mix together the sugar, salt, butter, evaporated milk and marshmallows. Bring to boil over high heat, stirring constantly with a long-handled spoon to avoid burns. Turn heat down to medium and continue boiling for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Stir in white chocolate until melted. Add spices and stir until mixed. Pour in a greased 9x13 pan. Cool.

Submitter: 
Cyndy C.
Description: 
Like an iced spice cake without the crumbs! This velvety fudge is a quick and no fuss treat alongside your pumpkin pie. Try adding nuts!
