Title:
Lime Cilantro Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
1 cup rice (basmati)
1 teaspoon lime zest
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
Heat butter/oil in a saucepan over low heat. Add rice and lime juice. Stir for 1 min.
Add water and salt, bring to full boil. Cover, turn down to simmer over low heat until rice is tender and water is absorbed (approx 25 mins).
Fluff with fork and toss with cilantro.
