Title:
Bread Machine Focaccia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup lukewarm water
4 tablespoons olive oil, divided ( 2 tbl, 2 tbl)
1/2 teaspoon Kosher salt, plus more for top
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
2 teaspoons dried crushed rosemary, divided (1 tsp, 1 tsp)
1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes
Parmesan cheese to taste
3 cups bread flour
1 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place water, 2 tablespoons olive oil, salt, garlic,  1 teaspoon rosemary, sun-dried tomatoes, bread flour and yeast in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Dough cycle; press Start.
Remove dough from bread machine when cycle is complete.  Pat dough into either a 7x11 inch baking pan or 12 inch pizza pan. Use your fingers to dimple the dough every inch or so. Brush with remaining olive oil and sprinkle with salt, Parmesan cheese and remaining rosemary.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Cover foccacia with plastic wrap while oven preheats.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until golden brown. let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
