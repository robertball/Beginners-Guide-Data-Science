Title:
Mushroom Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup long-grain rice
1 cup water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 pound mushrooms
1/4 cup onion
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1/4 cup white wine

Directions:
In a 1 1/2-quart heavy saucepan bring rice and water with salt to a boil.
Stir rice once and cook, covered, over moderately low heat 15 minutes.
Remove pan from heat and let rice stand, covered, 5 minutes.
While rice is cooking, cut mushrooms into 1/4-inch-thick slices and chop onions.
In a skillet heat butter over moderately high heat until foam subsides and saut√© mushrooms and onions with salt to taste until golden and any liquid mushrooms give off is evaporated.
Add wine and simmer, stirring occasionally, until wine is evaporated.
Fluff rice with a fork and stir in mushrooms and scallions.
