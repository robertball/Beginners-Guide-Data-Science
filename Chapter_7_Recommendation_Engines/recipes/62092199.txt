Title:
Apple Crisp Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 granny smith apples
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/3 cup quick cooking oats
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 10 in. graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Peel and slice apples removing core. Place slices in lemon water so apples do not brown.  Set aside.
Combine cinnamon and 1/3 cup sugar.  Set aside.
In medium bowl mix together cream cheese and 1/4 cup sugar until well combined.  Add egg and vanilla.
Place apples in bottom of pie crust.  Sprinkle cinnamon sugar and quick oats over top of apples.
Pour cheesecake mixture over top of apples and bake in the oven for 40 mins at 375 degrees or until top is just beginning to brown.  Cool on a rack for approx. 1-2 hours and refrigerate for 3-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Miriam M.
Description: 
A great dessert I came up with combining 2 favorites, apple crisp and cheesecake.  It's been a hit with my family and friends. Enjoy!
