Title:
Chicken with Artichoke Pan Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chicken stock
2 Tbsp. flour
2 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
1 1/2 tsp. grated lemon rind
2 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice, divided
1 tsp. garlic powder
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 tsp. kosher salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
Cooking spray
1/2 cup thinly sliced shallots
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh rosemary
2 oz. pancetta, finely chopped
1 Tbsp. chopped garlic
1/2 cup dry sherry
1 cup frozen artichoke hearts, thawed and halved
4 tsp. fresh chopped parsley

Directions:
Combine stock and flour in small bowl, whisk
Combine 1 T. of oil, rind, t tsp juice and garlic powder in a medium bowl
Add chicken, turning to coat.  Let stand at room temp for 30 minutes
Remove chicken from marinade and discard marinade
Sprinkle chicken with salt/pepper
Heat a large skillet; coat with cooking spray
Add chicken to pan; cook 5 minutes on each side; remove from pan
Reduce heat. Add remaining 1 Tbsp oil to pan; swirl to coat
Add shallots, rosemary and pancetts; cook 3 minutes
Add garlic; cook 30 seconds
Add sherry to pan; cook 4 minutes until liquid is almost evaporated
Stir in stock mixture; bring to a simmer
Add artichokes to pan; cook 1 minute until sauce thickens
Stir in 2 tsp. juice and 2 tsp. parsley
Add chicken to pan, turning to coat; sprinkle with remaining parsley
