Title:
Keto Peanut Butter Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 tbsp softened butter
1 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp melted raw cocoa butter
1/8 cup almond flour
1/2 tbsp coconut flour
1/4 cup Trim Healthy Mama Gentle Sweet or my sweetener
2 T chopped raw cocoa butter
1/4 to 1/2 tsp salt (this was omitted in original try, need salt!!)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Put muffin liners in muffin pan
Combine first five ingredients until smooth with electric mixer.
Add in the flours, sweetener, and chopped cocoa butter.
Divide among 12 muffin tin/wrappers
Bake for 10-15 minutes until the center no longer jiggles and the edges are golden.
Remove and let cool completely.
