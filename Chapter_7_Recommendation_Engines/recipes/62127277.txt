Title:
Leftover Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
about 1 1/2 breasts left over roasted rosemary lemon chicken
1 cup chopped left over cooked green beans
6 baby bella mushrooms, sliced
1 12 oz can diced tomatoes w/celery and green peppers (Del Alpe is good)
2 12 oz cans chicken broth
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 small onion, sliced
1/4 cup canola oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
heat canola oil in a medium pot and sautee onions, garlic, and mushrooms until onions are clear and soft
chop chicken and green beans into bite sized pieces and add to the vegetables in the pot
mix in diced tomatoes with celery and green peppers, and then pour both cans of chicken broth in.
bring soup to a boil, then simmer on low heat for about 20 minutes
serve very hot with rice or bread.

Submitter: 
Kailey W.
Description: 
This is great for sick children, rainy days, or dads who work at home (like mine) who can't seem to find their way around the kitchen at lunch time. You can vary the amount of broth to your liking and the vegetables according to what you have left over !
