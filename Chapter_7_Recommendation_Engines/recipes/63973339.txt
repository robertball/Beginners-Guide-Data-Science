Title:
Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup vegetable oil, *
1/3 cup honey
5 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup raisins (add after cooked)
1/2 cup dry milk
3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
pinch salt
nuts, optional
wheat germ, optional
coconut, optional
sunflower seeds, optional
dates, optional

Directions:
Mix brown sugar, oil, and honey in saucepan.  Heat until sugar is dissolved.  Combine dry ingredients in large bowl. Pour sugar mixture over dry ingredients and mix well.  Spread out on parchment paper on 2 jelly roll pans. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes, stir and bake for another 8-10 min. removing when browned. Cool in pan.  Add raisins.  Store in air tight container.
*Can use 1/2 oil.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
My standard granola recipe.  Tastes like cookie crumbs.
