Chicken and Brussel Sprouts with Pasta Shells Recipe

Ingredients:

3 T butter
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 t salt
1/2 t black pepper
1/2 red onion, chopped
2 or 3 cloves garlic, chopped
3/4 # fresh Brussel sprouts (or 10 oz pkg. frozen), cut into quarters from top to stem end
1 C canned chicken broth ( I used whole 14 oz can)

Directions:
In a large nonstick frying pan, heat 1 T each of the oil and the butter over moderate heat. Season the chicken with 1/4 t each of the salt and pepper. Cook the breasts until browned and just done, 4-5 min. per side. Remove chicken from pan and let it rest for 5 min. Cut into small pieces.
In same pan, heat remaining 1 T oil ove moderately low heat, add red onion and cook, stirring occ.,  until starting to soften, about 3 min. Add garlic,sprouts, broth, and red pepper flakes. Bring to simmer and cook, covered, until the sprouts are just done, about 5 min. Add chicken, lemon juice, parsley, Parmesan, and remaining 2 T butter. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat.
In a large pot of boiling, salted water, cook pasta until just done, about 10 min. Drain and toss with sauce
