Title:
Chocolate Caramel Crunchies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/373552.jpg
Ingredients:
11 oz caramel bits
5 squares of chocolate bark
30 vanilla wafers

Directions:
Lay approximately 30 vanilla wafers out on wax paper spaced apart.
In a microwave safe bowl melt the caramel peices in 30 second increments, stirring in between until melted.  Scoop out with teaspoon over the vanilla wafers.
Next microwave the chocolate bark in a microwave safe bowl, stirring after each 30 second increment until melted.  Scoop by spoonful over the caramel.
Allow them to cool/set for approximately 30 minutes before moving.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mrs. CJR
Description: 
Sweet and chewy with a big crunch.  Family eats them faster than you can make them!
