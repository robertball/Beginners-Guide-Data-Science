Title:
Icebox Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling: 2# frozen strawberries
2 T lemon juice
2 T water
1 T unflavored gelatin
1 C sugar
Pinch salt
1# fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced thin
1 9-in. pie shell, baked and cooled
Topping: 4 oz. softened cream cheese
3 T sugar
1/2 t vanilla
1 C heavy cream

Directions:
Cooked frozen strawberries in large saucepan over med-low heat until berries begin to release juice, about 3min. Increase het to med-high and cook stirring freq. until thick and jamlike, about 25 min. (mixture should measure 2C)
Combine lemon juice, water, and gelatin in small bowl. Let stand until gelatin is softened and mixture hasthickened, about 5 mi. Stir gelatin mixture, sugar, and salt into cooked berries and return to simmer, about 2 min. Transfer to bowl and cool to room temp., about 30 min.
Fold fresh berries into filling. Spread evenly in pie shell and refrig. until set, about 4 hrs. (or 24 hrs).
With elictric mixer on med. speed, beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla until smooth, about 30 sec. Add cream and whip until stiff peaks form, about 2 min. Serve pie w/ cream

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
In step 1, imperative that cooked strawberry mixture measures 2 C, amy more and filling will be loose. If fresh berries aren't fully ripe, add extra sugar to taste in step 2.
