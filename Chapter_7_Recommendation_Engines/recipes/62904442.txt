Title:
Rocky Road Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 oz.) pkg. brownie mix
2 cups miniature marshmallows
1 - 6 oz. pkg semi-sweet choco. pieces, melted
1/4 cup milk
1 cup coarsely chopped nuts

Directions:
1.  Prepare and bake brownies according to pkg. and bake.
2.  Immediately sprinkle marshmallows over the brownies (continue baking until marshmallows begin to melt
3.  Melt chocolate w/milk over low heat, stirring until smooth
4.  Drizzle over marshmallows: sprinkle w/nuts
5.  Cool: cut into squares
