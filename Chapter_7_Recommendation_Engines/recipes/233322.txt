Title:
Grapefruit Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
7 fluid ounces lemonade
3 fluid ounces grapefruit-flavored vodka (such as Absolut Ruby Red®)
3 fluid ounces grapefruit juice
2 fluid ounces lemon-flavored vodka (such as Absolut Citron®)
1 fluid ounce cranberry juice

Directions:
Mix lemonade, grapefruit vodka, grapefruit juice, lemon vodka, and cranberry juice together in a pitcher. Pour mixture into 4 martini glasses.

Submitter: 
KEAL19
Description: 
Delicious, refreshing summer beverage! Garnish each glass with a grapefruit slice.
