Title:
Spicy "hot" Chocolate Chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup sugar
2 large eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (can add more if you like a lot of spice)
2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
set oven to 375.  Cream together the butter and sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla and combine well.  Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cayenne pepper. Fold in chocolate chips.  Drop by teaspoon onto a non-greased cookie sheet.  Bake for 10 min or until done.

Submitter: 
ALYSSIA71
Description: 
Surprisingly good. A hint of spicy with the sweetness with the chocolate goes very well together. Once eaten, they taste like your normal chocolate chip cookie, but a finish of hot & spicy.
