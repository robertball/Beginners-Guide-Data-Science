Title:
Copycat Cheddar Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. unbleached, all-purpose flour
1 T. baking powder
1/2 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. garlic salt
1/2 C. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 C. butter-flavored shortening, melted
1/2 C. milk
1 T. melted butter
1 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.
Combine flour, baking powder, cream of tartar, garlic salt and shredded cheddar in a large mixing bowl. Stir until thoroughly combined.
Pour in melted shortening and milk, and mix into dry ingredients with a pastry blender just until combined.
Drop dough by 1/4 C. onto a sprayed baking sheet.
In a small bowl, mix garlic powder into melted butter. Brush mixture over top of biscuits before baking.
Bake in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned on top.
Brush again with butter/garlic mixture as soon as they are out of the oven. Serve warm.

This recipe tastes just like the fluffy, tender biscuits you love at that certain seafood restaurant, without having to use a biscuit mix. Enjoy!
