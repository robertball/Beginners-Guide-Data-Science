Title:
Grasshopper Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BARS
1 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup + 2 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
1 tsp salt
FILLING
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups powdered sugar, sifted
2 Tbsp milk (or less)
1 tsp peppermint extract
A few drops green food coloring
GLAZE
6 Tbsp butter, softened
2/3 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
To make bars, mix 1 cup butter, cocoa and 2 cups sugar.  Add eggs one at a time.  Add 1 tsp vanilla, flour, and salt and mix well.
Put in greased jelly roll pan.  Bake for 15-20 minutes or until set (do not overbake).  Cool.
To make filling, mix together 1/2 cup butter, powdered sugar, milk, peppermint extract and food coloring.
Spread over cooled bars.
To make glaze, melt 6 Tbsp butter and chocolate chips in microwave.  Add 2 tsp vanilla.  Pour over green filling and chill.  Cut into small bars.
