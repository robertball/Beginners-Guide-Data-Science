Title:
Ground Beef Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750075.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground chuck
1 medium onion
1 stalk celery
1 potato
1 bag mixed vegetables
16 oz canned tomatoes
2 1/2 cups water
1-3 bay leaves
Worcestershire sauce (or A1)
black pepper

Directions:
In a large pot, brown ground chuck, onion, and celery; drain fat
Stir in remaining ingredients, cover and simmer until vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally
