Title:
Chicken Sausage Gnocchi Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2439490.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Links - Chicken Sausage - ( I use, Adelles Roasted Garlic & Gruyere Cheese)
2 Cups - Broccoli Flowerets - Steamed
1/2 lbs. Ricotta Gnocchi - Prepared
1 tbsp. - Garlic - Minced
1 1/4 stick - Butter
1/2 cp Diced Onion
2 tsp flour
Black Pepper
Salt

Directions:
Prep the gnocchi in advance. Set Aside.
Using a steamer, prepare two cups of Broccoli and set aside.  Keep warm.
Melt about a tsp of butter, add  1 tsp Garlic,  1 tbps Onion - Sautee
Add Sausage and brown.
In a separate pan - Melt 1 stick of butter, add 1 tbsp. Onion 2 tsp Garlic.  Add Flour and brown a bit.
Stir in Gnocchi
Add Broccoli to Sausage
Add Gnocchi mixture to Sausage mixture.
Salt and Pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Dave Niezabitowski Sr.
Description: 
A light tasting dish with mild flavors that is quick to prepare as long as you prep gnocchi in advance.
