Title:
Jalapeno Popper Spread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
2 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
6 ounces canned diced jalapeno peppers, drained
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese, sour cream,mayonnaise and 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in green chiles and jalapeno peppers. Pour mixture into a oven safe serving dish and refrigerate overnight. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until brown on top.
