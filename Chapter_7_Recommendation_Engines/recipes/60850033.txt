Title:
Healthy Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 serving of cooking spray
1 small potato
2 tsp of herbal seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.  Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray, set aside.
Scrub but do not peel potato. Slice in half, lengthwise, then slice each half into strips and place on baking sheet.
Spray potatoes lightly with cooking spray and sprinkle with herbal seasoning.
Bake for 20 minutes or until golden.
