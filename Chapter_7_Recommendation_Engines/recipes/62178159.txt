Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1can light red kidney beans
1 can butter beans
1 can lima beans
1 large can baked beans
1/2lb bacon fried(include grease)
1/2C vinegar
3/4C brown sugar
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2tsp garlic salt
1 Chopped onion

Directions:
Drain the kidney beans, butter beans and lima beans. In large pot fry the bacon, then add all the beans. Make a sauce with the remaing ingredients and pour over the beans. Bake in oven at 350 for 60-70min.
