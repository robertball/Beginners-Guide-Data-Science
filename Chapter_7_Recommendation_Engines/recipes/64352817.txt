Title:
low carb ricotta pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 egg
2 Tbsp. flaxseed meal (almond meal)

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together. Melt a bit of butter in a non stick pan, and ladle three pancakes. Cook on low to medium heat until edges dry and bubbles form on top of the batter, then flip and cook on the other side.
