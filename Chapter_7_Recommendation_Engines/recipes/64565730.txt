Title:
Cheesy Asparagus Caper Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cups of asparagus, tips and parts that are tender
1 egg
1 tbsp. melted butter
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper
3/4 tsp thyme
3/4 tsp marjoram
2 cups sharp cheddar
3 tbsp. of capers, drained
1/2 cup finely chopped green onion

Directions:
Trim and clean asparagus and steam in steamer or in microwave until tender and not mushy, approximately 5 minutes.
Mix flour, salt, pepper, baking powder, baking soda, thyme, marjoram in mixing bowl.
Beat egg in another bowl and add buttermilk,  melted butter, oil, capers, green onion, cheese and asparagus.
Slowly stir liquid mixture into dry mixture until just moist and do not over mix.
Spoon mixture into 12 prepared muffin cups, fill 2/3 full and bake @ 420 F (approximately 15 to 20 minutes) until a toothpick or wirepick inserted is clean.
Place on wire rack and allow to cool a few minutes prior to eating.
