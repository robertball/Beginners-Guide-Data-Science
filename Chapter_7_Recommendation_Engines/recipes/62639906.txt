Title:
spicy sweet potato and lentil stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. oil
1 yellow onion, diced
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 or 2 canned chipotle chiles, drained and chopped
4 slices fakin bacon, diced
1/4 c. dry white wine
4 c. veggie broth
1 c. red lentils
3 c. sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed (3 c.)
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
4 small corn tortillas, cut into thin strips
sour cream
green onions
cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in hot oil 5 to 7 minutes.  add chipotle chiles, bacon and wine, and saute one minute more.
Add broth, 3 c. water, lentils and sweet potatoes.  cook 20 minutes more or until lentils fall apart and potatoes are tender. Stir in cinnamon and salt and pepper to taste.
Cook tortilla strips in small skillet over high heat 3 to 5 minutes, or until crispy.
Ladle stew into bowls and top each serving with 1 T. sour cream, green onions, and tortilla chips.
