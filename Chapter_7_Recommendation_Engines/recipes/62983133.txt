Title:
Corn Souffle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can whole corn  (do not drain liquid)
1 can creamed corn
1 cup sour cream
1 box jiffy cornbread mix
1/2 cup butter melted

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl , pour into a 9x13 glass pan that has been sprayed with non stick cooking spray. Bake until crispy and golden.

Submitter: 
TabbyKat
Description: 
Sweet and fluffy side dish that goes perfect with Turkey and stuffing.  I always have to make two because my family devours the first one.
