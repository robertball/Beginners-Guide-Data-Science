Title:
Nuevo Mexico Calabacitas (Squash/Zucchini Casserole) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ears uncooked corn - cut corn off the cob
½ cup New Mexico Green Chile Peppers, chopped  (I use 2 - 4 oz cans Bueno Hot Chopped Green Chile you could use Bueno frozen Green Chile also) Temperature to taste.
1 med. onion, chopped
2 Poblano Peppers slice in thin strips
2 med Zucchini sliced
2 med Yellow Squash sliced
2 TBS chopped Garlic or to taste
Salt & pepper to taste
2 TBS Olive Oil
1 cups Cheddar Cheese, grated

Directions:
Cook poblano peppers, onions and garlic in olive oil until tender, add corn, zucchini/squash.
Drain if needed.
Add salt & pepper.
Place ½ of mixture in a casserole dish, top with grated cheese, add remaining zucchini/squash mixture and top with cheese again.
Bake 25 minutes or until cheese is melted at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Dan'ish
Description: 
This is a very good Mexican side dish.  It is great served with enchiladas, tamales, or any Mexican food.  I like it all by itself and it is the only way my husband likes squash or zucchini.
