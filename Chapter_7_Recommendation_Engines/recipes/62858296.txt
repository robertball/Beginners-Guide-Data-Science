Title:
Sandra's manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box manicotti shells (14)
2 c. cottage cheese
2 c. ricotta cheese
1 1/2 c. grated mozzarella cheese
2 eggs
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/4 t. nutmeg
1 T. parsley
spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Mix and stuff dry shells. Spread spaghetti sauce in pan then place shells on top, then cover with spag. sauce. Top with 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese & 1/4 cup parm. cheese. Refrigerate overnight. Bake 40 min. 350 degrees
