Title:
Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon-style prepared mustard
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 teaspoons chopped fresh dill (fresh as 'in season'.
2 tablespoons of Mayo.

Directions:
Whisk together sour cream, mustard, lemon juice, mayo and dill until well blended. Chill before serving.
If dill is not in season try dried dill as the dill you get in the grocery stores is too bland.

Submitter: 
Ron Lloyd
Description: 
This sour cream dill sauce is great over any fish, especially grilled salmon. You may add more lemon or mustard to suit your taste.Modified from: Dill Sauce
