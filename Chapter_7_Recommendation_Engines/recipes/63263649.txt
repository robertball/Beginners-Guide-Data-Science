Title:
slushy raspberry lemon aide Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 raspberries, muddled
1 cup sparkling water
1 cup ice
1 lemon
1/4 cup fine sugar

Directions:
muddle the raspberries until liquefied.
strain the seeds.
pour water, ice, and sugar into the blender.
juice the lemon and pour into blender.
blend until slushy and well mixed.
serve cold.
enjoy!

Submitter: 
Elle Mcgrath
Description: 
it is a hot summer day, and you need a cool drink. slushy raspberry lemon aide cools you of quickly.
