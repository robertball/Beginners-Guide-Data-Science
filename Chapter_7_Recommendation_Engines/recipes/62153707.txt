Title:
Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs pork tenderloin
2 cups onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 tablespoons lemon juice
4 tablespoons brown sugar
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons vegatable oil

Directions:
Marinate all ingredients overnight in a large ziplock bag.
Grill for about 20 minutes turning often until desired temperature.  170 degrees is recommended for pork.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Mitchell McGeown
Description: 
Definate crowd pleaser.  Great served with potato pancakes and a salad
