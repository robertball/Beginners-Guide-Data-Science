Title:
Old Bay Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts Halved, uncooked (6 half pieces total)
9 cups of cooked rice
6 Tablespoons of butter
18 Teaspoons of milk
Old Bay
Non-stick cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Tear off 6 pieces of aluminum foil about 8 inches wide.
Spray each piece of foil with the non-stick cooking spray.
Place 1.5 cups of the cooked rice on each piece of foil in a mound.
Add 3 teaspoons of milk on each mound of rice.
Sprinkle with Old Bay over the top of the rice enough to cover the rice thoroughly.
Place uncooked chicken halved chicken breast on top of each rice mound.
Place 1 tablespoon of butter on top of each chicken half.
Sprinkle Old Bay thoroughly over the chicken breasts.
Pull up corners of foil and tent each of the foil squares.
Place each of the 6 foil squares on to a cookie sheet and bake for 45 minutes.
Carfully remove from cookie sheet and serve.
