Title:
Mother's Down-Maine Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695532.jpg
Ingredients:
1 qt, wild Maine blueberries, divided
1 c. sugar
3/4 cup water
2 T cornstarch
1/4 t. salt
1/4 c. water
1/4 t. lemon juice
1 baked 9 inch pastry shell

Directions:
Pick over berries carefully, removing all stems, leaves, etc.  Set aside 3 cups.
Combine remaining cup of berries with sugar and 3/4 c. of water. Bring to a boil.
Combine cornstarch and salt with 1/4 cup of water. Add to boiling blueberries and cook, stirring constantly, until slightly thickened.
Add lemon juice.
Cool slightly, and then mix with raw berries.
Pour into pie shell and refrigerate.
Serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
This is a family favorite that's really only possible in July and August in Maine!  We.ve tried it with frozen berries, but it's not the same.  It works with high bush berries, but the flavor isn't quite the same.
