Title:
Garlic Spinach Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764769.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box stuffing mix
1 bag frozen spinach
2 Tbsp real butter (salted)
1 large onion
1 red bell pepper
4 stalks celery
3 cups cheddar cheese
1 Tbsp garlic powder
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Dice onion, bell pepper and celery and saute in 2 Tbsp real butter until soft/clear.
Prepare stuffing in pot with sauteed veggies.
Prepare spinach in microwave according to package instructions (REDUCE liquid requirements on package by half)
Combine spinach and stuffing together.
Add garlic powder and 2 cups cheddar.
Mix gently until combined and spread in casserole dish.
Top with remaining 1 cup cheese.
Bake at 350 for 15 mins.

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
A fresh update to bread only stuffing.  Quick and easy with boxed stuffing mix and frozen spinach.
