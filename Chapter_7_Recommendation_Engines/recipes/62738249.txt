Title:
Creamy Cauliflower (Fauxtato) Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads cauliflower trimmed and broken into florets
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/4 cup half and half
1/4 cup shreddded parmasan (1T reserved)
1/4 cup shredded mozzerella (1T reserved)
1/4 cup finely snipped chives
1 T Flour
2T butter
1/2 t salt (to taste)
1/4 t pepper (to taste)
Dash Nutmeg optional
Dash ground red pepper optional

Directions:
Steam cauliflower florets til tender soft (15 min)
Set cauliflower aside and empty pot of water.
add butter to pot and melt over low heat add flour cook 1 min add broth whisk til thickened
add cooked cauliflower back to pot and whip with hand mixers till smooth
add half and half, cheeses(not reserves) salt pepper and chives
pour mixture into shallow buttered baking dish and top with cheeses, nutmeg and red pepper
Broil in preheated oven for 12 minutes or until golden brown on top

Submitter: 
Katie
Description: 
Perfect replacement to mashed potatoes when carb cutting or just a delicious  alternative. Serve with main dishes that include gravy... roast chicken, pot roast or turkey...this side will make the gravy well you're looking for! Don't expect leftovers
