Title:
Simple and Spicy Beef Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb beef cubed
1 red Pepper
1 yellow Pepper
1 cup water
3 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp chilli powder
1/2 tbsp garlic powder
1/2 tbsp crushed red chilli peppers
1/2 tsp paprika
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp worchestershire sauce
4 tbsp Frank's Red Hot Sauce
2 cups rice or noodles, your choice.
You can also add any other vegetables you like, carrots and snow peas go very well with this recipe.

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cubed beef, olive oil, garlic powder,chilli powder, red chilli peppers, and paprika.
Stir until beef is well covered.
Pour beef onto hot pan. Continue to stir occasionally until beef is cooked to your likeness.
Once beef is cooked, covered with water and remaining ingredients, stir.
Cook on low for approsimately two minutes, keeping vegetables nice and crunchy. If you would like your vegetables abit softer, cook for abit longer.
Serve over rice or noodles.
Also, if you would like it to have a bit more spice, add more Frank's sauce and/or chilli peppers. Adjust to your own liking!!

Submitter: 
Jessica Radics
Description: 
Very easy to make, can use either rice or noodles. Has some great kick!
