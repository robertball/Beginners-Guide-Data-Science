Title:
Mary's Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1020392.jpg
Ingredients:
28	ounces tomato sauce
1	large garlic clove, minced or 1 ½ teaspoons garlic paste, or 2 teaspoons garlic juice
½	cup minced onion, (1 teaspoon) onion juice or onion paste (equal to 1 medium onion)
¼	cup firmly packed brown sugar
2	tablespoons molasses
¼	cup bourbon
½	cup ketchup
1	tablespoons hot sauce (up to 1/8 cup) (I used 4 teaspoons sriracha sauce)
¼	cup apple cider vinegar
1/8	cup red wine vinegar
¼	cup Worcestershire sauce
2	tablespoons Sister Mary’s BBQ Rub
1	tablespoon pureed chipotle in adobo sauce. from the can, puree the whole can in a food processor and use 1 tablespoon of that mixture.

Directions:
1.	In large saucepan over medium heat, add tomato sauce, and all other ingredients 1 at a time stirring after each addition.
2.	Simmer until flavors have blended and the sauce has thickened somewhat.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Tangy zesty sauce that compliments pork, and beef very well.
