Title:
Elaine's Classic New York Italian Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744791.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (6 oz) tomato paste and 1 can of water
I can (15 oz) tomato sauce with a little water to rinse out the can & add to recipe
1 large can (28 oz) plain tomatoes
1/3 C parmesan grated cheese
1 sm onion (diced)
1 can (4 oz) mushrooms
1 T garlic powder (or whatever your taste preference)
4 tsp of dried parsley
2 tsp dried basil
1 tsp salt
Pepper to taste
Couple of dashes of tobacco sauce

Directions:
In a sauce pan, saut√© tomato paste, sauce and water & let simmer. Break up the can of tomatoes by hand (preferred) or use a food processor and add to mixture. Add all remaining ingredients.  Add meatballs if desired. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, (add meatballs optional & separate recipe)and simmer partially covered for 2 hours or until desired thickness.  Serve over 1 lb of cooked pasta of your choice.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
My Mother-In-Law's classic sauce recipe served with her classic meatballs over pasta. This meal was served at every family gathering for generations. It's delicious.
