Title:
Lavender Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups water
1 1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons lavender
1/2 tablespoon hibiscus
1 1/2 cups lemon juice

Directions:
in a sauce pan, combine 3 cups water, lavender, hibiscus,sugar. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.  Remove from heat and allow to cool.  In a pitcher combine remaining water and lemon juice, refrigerate.  When lavender sugar mixture has cooled, strain into pitcher and refrigerate.  Serve very cold.
