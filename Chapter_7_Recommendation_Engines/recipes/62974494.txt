Title:
Smothered Potatoes with Ground Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/736648.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ground beef (if on a tight budget, use less)
4 potatoes, washed, peeled, snd cut
1 med onion, finely diced
1/2 cup oil (bacon grease is even better)
salt and pepper

Directions:
Wash, peel and cut potatoes into about 1/4 inch circles.
dice onion fine
Brown beef  in skillet. Do Not Drain .(Potatoes cook in meat grease)
Spoon meat out of pan into a bowl.
Add in a layer of potaoes,
add a layer of onions,
add a layer of meat,
lightly salt and pepper that layer,
repeat: layering unil ingredients are all seasoned and in skillet.
Cook covered, on med heat, until potatoes are bowned and tender. "potatoes on bottom will brown, to keep from burning, occationaly stir by flipping potatoes from bottom to top."
