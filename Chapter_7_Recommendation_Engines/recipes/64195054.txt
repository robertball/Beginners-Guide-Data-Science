Title:
Banana Bread (gluten free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1552132.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup (usually about 3) SUPER ripe bananas, mashed up
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 3/4 cups gluten-free flour
2/3 cup sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking soda
*can add chocolate chips, coconut flakes, nuts, etc

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Liberally butter up and flour a 9 x 5 bread pan.
In a medium bowl add: 1 cup (usually about 3) SUPER ripe bananas, mashed up, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, 2 eggs, 1 tsp. vanilla *if adding chocolate chips/nuts add it to this bowl and mix well
Beat together for a bit, then set aside.
In another bowl mix: 1 3/4 cups gluten-free flour, 2/3 cup sugar, 1 tsp. baking powder, 1/2 tsp. salt, 1/4 tsp. baking soda
Use a fork to mix these dry goods together.
Next add the dry mixture into the banana, oil and egg mixture a little bit at a time. Pour into the greased bread pan.
Bake at 350 for 55 â€“ 60 minutes.
Let the loaf cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes before removing it from the pan.
