Title:
Dad's Famous Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/679311.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 3 cups flour
2. 2 cups sugar
3. 1 1/4 cups oil
4. 3 cups grated carrrots
5. 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
6. 3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
7. 3/4 cup crushed pineapple with juice
8. 1 teaspoon baking soda
9. 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
10. 3 eggs
11. 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Cream Cheese Frosting/Filling
1. 8 oz cream cheese packages softned
2. 2 tablespoons sour cream
3. 1/2 cup confectionary sugar
4. 2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Grease 9x12 pan. Mix all dry ingredients. In seperate bowl beat eggs, oil, vanilla. Create crater in center of dry ingredients and pour wet mixture. Mix to combine. Then stir in carrots and pineapple. Once everything is combined pour mixture in pan and bake for 45 minutes or until toothpick comes out dry once it's inserted in the middle.  The batter will be thick. This will make 24 muffins.
Frosting: Mix softend cream cheese, sour cream, vanilla in mixer until well blended and smooth, then incorporate sugar until mixture gets the frosting conistency you desire.  This frosting will not be super sweet. You may add 1/2 cup of additional sugar to make it sweeter and stiffer. Store in refrigerator
