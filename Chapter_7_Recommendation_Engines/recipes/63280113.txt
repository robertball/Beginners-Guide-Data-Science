Title:
Double Chocolate Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 cup ground graham cracker
3/4 cup whole hazelnuts ground - no skin
1/4 cup cocoa powder
4 tbsp confectionery sugar
5 tbsp unsalted butter - melted
Filling:
1 1/4 cup heavy cream
250g or 9oz bittersweet  chocolate- no more than 65% I used 55%, cut in to chunks
2 large eggs
1 tbs vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt
Glaze:
2 tbsp heavy cream
55g or 2oz bittersweet chocolate - same one used above
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp warm water

Directions:
Toast your hazelnuts in a 400 degree oven for about 5 minutes or until lightly toasted and fragrant. Turn oven down to 350 degrees. Using a food processor, pulse until ground finely.
Sift cocoa and sugar in to a bowl. Add graham crackers and hazelnuts to bowl. Mix well. Add butter, mix well. Mixture should hold together when squeezed.
Press in to a tart pan. Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes. Set aside. Leave oven at 350 degrees.
In a pot, heat cream until just about boiling. Place chocolate in a medium-sized bowl. Pour cream over chocolate, and let sit for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes, whisk chocolate mixture until smooth. Whisk in eggs, one at a time, whisk in vanilla and salt.
Pour mixture in to tart pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes, or until middle is set.
Cool completely.
When ready to serve, heat cream until just about boiling. Place chocolate in a small bowl. Pour cream over chocolate, and let sit for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes, whisk chocolate mixture until smooth, add the rest of the ingredients and pour over cooled tart, turning the pan around to spread the glaze.
Let set for about 5 minutes. Serve with ice cream, whipped cream or fresh fruit.
