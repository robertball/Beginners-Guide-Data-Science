Title:
Tortilla Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
8 oz. sour cream
2 cups shredded cheese
1 small can chopped green chilies
1 small can chopped black olives
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 small jar diced pimentos
2 cloves garlic, minced (or a little garlic powder)
Dash ground black pepper
10 large flour tortillas

Directions:
Mix together everything but tortillas in a medium bowl; Spoon filling onto a tortilla and roll.  Place on a cookie sheet.  Repeat.  Chill filled tortillas overnight.  Slice before serving.  Serve with picante sauce.
