Title:
Alexander's Awesome Caramel & Crunch Filled Chocolate Candies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 Ounces Caramels
24 Ounces Rice Krispies
24 Ounces Chocolate Chips

Directions:
1. Open 1 bag of chocolate chips.
2. Pour 1 entire container of caramels into 1 of 2 microwave-safe bowls.
3. Pour 1 entire container of chocolate chips into the other microwave-safe bowl.
4.  Microwave the chocolate chips until they have become liquid.
5. Pour the melted chocolate that has the Rice Krispies mixed in on the bottom layer.
6. Pour the melted caramel as the next layer.
7. Use the rest of the chocolate mix as the final layer.
8. Freeze the chocolate filled ice tray for 2 hours.
9. When the chocolates have hardened, eat them up!

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe is one I recommend for those who just got onto the path of creating candies!
