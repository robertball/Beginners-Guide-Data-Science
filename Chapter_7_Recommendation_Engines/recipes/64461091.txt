Title:
Crescent Cristo Sandwich Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. can crescent dinner rolls
2 tbs butter, melted
2 tbs honey
6 oz. thinly sliced turkey breast
6 oz. sliced muenster cheese
6 oz. thinly sliced ham
1/2 cup raspberry preserves
2 tbs honey
1 tbs sesame seed

Directions:
heat oven to 375. Separate dough into 4 long rectangles. Place rectangles crosswise on a large cookie sheet, rectangles should not touch. Firmly press perforations to seal. In a small bowl, combine butter and 2 tbs honey. Brush over dough. Bake at 375 for 8-12 minutes, until golden brown. Cool 15 minutes. Grease a 15x10x1 inch baking dish. Carefully place 1 crust on pan. Top evenly with turkey. Place second crust over turkey, top with ham and cheese. Place third crust over ham, spread evenly with preserves. Top with fourth crust. Brush with 2 tbs honey. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake at 375 for 10-15 minutes, until crust is deep golden brown and loaf is hot. Let stand 5 minutes and cut into 8 slices.
