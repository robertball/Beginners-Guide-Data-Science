Title:
Sweet Mustard Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cauliflower, cut in florets
4 cups small cucmbers, whole or cut
4 cups large cucumbers, cut in small pieces
4 cups onions, sliced
2 lbs. small white pearl onions, peeled, whole
3 red peppers, seeded and cut in small pieces
2 green peppers, seeded and cut in small pieces
1 cup pickling salt
12 cups water
5 cups granulated sugar
water 1 1/2 cups
1 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup dry mustard
1 tbsp tumeric
t tbsp mustard seed
1 tbsp celery seed
4 1/2 cups white vinegar

Directions:
Put first 7 ingredients into large bowl.
Sprinkle with salt.  Pour first amount of water over salt.  Cover.  Let stand on counter overnight.
Drain vegetables.  Transfer to large pot.  Add sugar and second amount of water.  Heat on medium until boiling.  Stir often.
Put last 6 ingredients into medium bowl.  Mix into a paste.  Stir into boiling vegetables.  Continue to stir until it returns to boiling.  Boil slowly 30 minutes, uncovered, stirring occasionally.  Pout into hot sterilized jars to within 1/4 inch of top.  Seal.
