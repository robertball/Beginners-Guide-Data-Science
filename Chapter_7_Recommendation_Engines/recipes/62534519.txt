Title:
Cesky Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519670.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c. mashed potatoes
1 tsp. salt
Â¼ c. Cream of Wheat (regular)
2 egg yolks
2 c. flour

Directions:
Mix together the potatoes, salt, Cream of Wheat and egg yolks.  Add flour and mix together well. This will be a little stiff.  Roll into long roll on floured surface.  This roll will be approximately 40 inches long.  Cut roll in 1-inch pieces.  Roll each piece in flour to make 3-inch dumplings.  Drop into boiling water for 10-15 minutes.  They will float when almost done.  When done, place on cookie sheet and grease with butter.  Serve with kraut or gravy.  Make 30 dumplings.  Will freeze up to 6 months.
