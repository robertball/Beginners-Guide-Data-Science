Title:
Jackie's Killer Krab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2792865.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
8 imitation crab legs
1 tablespoon dried dill weed
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped
1/4 teaspoon vinegar-based hot pepper sauce

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl, stir together cream cheese, mayonnaise and sour cream. Stir in imitation crabmeat, dill weed, black olives, and hot pepper sauce.

Submitter: 
JKK5
Description: 
This is positively yummy!  I get asked to make it at least 4 or 5 times around the holidays. Serve with rye bread or crackers.
