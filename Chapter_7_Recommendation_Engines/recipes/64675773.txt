Title:
Apple and Pear Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ripe Bosc pears (4)
2 pound firm Maccourt apple (6)
1 teaspoon orange zest
1 teaspoon lemon zest
2 tablespoons orange juice
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
Topping:
1 1/2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1 cup old fashioned oatmeal
1/2 pound cold unsalted butter, diced

Directions:
Preheat over to 350
Peel, core and cut the pears and apples into large chunks
Add zest, juices, sugar, flour, cinnamon and nutmeg
Pour into 9x12 oval baking dish
Topping:
Combine the flour, sugars, salt, oatmeal, and butter into bowl of mixer with paddle attachment
Mix on low speed for 1 minute, until the mixture is in large crumbles
Sprinkle evenly over fruit, covering completely
Place on a sheet pan and bake for 50-60 minutes.
Serve warm.
