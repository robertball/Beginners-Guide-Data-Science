Title:
Cherry Nut Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982777.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
3 tablespoons tart cherry juice concentrate
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
2 tablespoons chopped toasted pecans
1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
Toasted bread or assorted crackers

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and tart cherry juice concentrate; mix until smooth.
Stir in sugar, pecans and thyme.
Refrigerate, covered, 2 to 3 hours to allow flavors to blend. Use as a spread on toasted breads or as a dip for assorted crackers.

Submitter: 
The Cherry Marketing Institute
Description: 
Serve this festive spread at a party or as a snack for kids.
