Title:
Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch fresh spinach
1 sm. can water chestnuts, sliced
5 slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
1 cup fresh bean sprouts
2 boiled eggs, diced
DRESSING:
3/4 c. sugar
1/3 c. ketchup
1/4 c. rice vinegar
Dash of salt
1 c. vegetable oil
2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 sm. onion, diced

Directions:
Toss spinach with water chestnuts, bacon, bean sprouts and eggs.
Mix dressing ingedients in blender.
Pour over salad just before serving.
This makes extra dressing which will keep well in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
cdagirl
Description: 
This salad has a lot of crunch and a tangy, sweet dressing.
