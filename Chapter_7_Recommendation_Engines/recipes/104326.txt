Ingredients:

4 carrots, peeled and grated
4 green onions, chopped
1/4 cup orange juice
2 teaspoons sesame oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop Broccoflower(R) into small bite size florets.  Add grated carrots and diced green onions to Broccoflower(R) florets in a large mixing bowl.  Toss the raw vegetables together with orange juice, sesame oil and salt and pepper. Refrigerate salad overnight to blend flavors and toss again before serving.
