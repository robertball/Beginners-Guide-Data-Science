Title:
Men Do Eat Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbl. olive oil
1 med. onion, diced
1 lg. clove of fresh garlic, minced
1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
2 C. chopped freshed or frozen broccoli
1 C. diced cooked ham
1 9 inch unbaked pie shell
1 1/2 C. grated Swiss cheese
4 lg. eggs, whisked very well
1 C. milk
1/2 C. heavey whipping cream
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. coarse black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Using frozed broccoli; thaw and drain all of the water from the thawed broccoli.
In a medium pan saute onions, garlic, bell pepper and broccoli in the 1 tbl. of olive oil, until tender.
Stir in ham and heat until warm.
Spoon ham and vegie mixture into pie shell and top with Swiss cheese.
Combine eggs, milk, cream, salt and pepper, pour over vegies, ham and cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for about 30 minutes, or until knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
Let cool 10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
This is a Quiche even a man will love, my husband does!  Great served with my Fruity Green Salad.
