Title:
EASY Ranch BBQ Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614330.jpg
Ingredients:
-4 skinless boneless chicken breasts
-red bell peppers
-yellow bell peppers
-white onion
- 1.5 cups of bottled hidden valley ranch dressing/ or homemade dressing

Directions:
1. slice onions, and peppers in rings
2. place chicken in a bakind dish.
place sliced onions and peppers on top
3. cover with ranch dressing.
4. place in fridge to marinate at least 30 minutes.
5. place chicken, onions and peppers on grill on low heat.
place some peppers and onions on top of chicken breasts while cooking.
6. Grill on bbq for 15 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink inside.

Submitter: 
mjhutch
Description: 
Chicken is marinated with ranch dressing, sliced onions, and peppers. All the ingredients are then slowly gilled on the BBQ for juicy chicken and creamy ranch sauce and topped with carmelized peppers and onions...yum! This dish is awesome topped on mash potatoes.
