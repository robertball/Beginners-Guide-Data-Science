Title:
Beans and Greens Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T Olive Oil
6 cloves Garlic
4 c. chicken broth
1 head of Escarole Chopped
1 Parmesan Rind
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 can White Beans
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in pot and saute garlic to soft but do not burn.
Add broth, escarole and rind.  Simmer about 15 mins.
Add pepper, beans, pepper and salt.
When serving add Parm cheese just before eating.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
A easy old Italian poorman's Soup.
