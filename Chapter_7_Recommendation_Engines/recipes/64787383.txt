Title:
Brew Burgers from Food Network Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ pounds ground beef
1 large Vidalia onion, cut in slices
4 slices of Swiss Cheese, or favorite cheese
4 slices of tomato
Lettuce
4 hamburger buns
Brew Sauce
¼ cup Heinz 57 steak sauce
¼ cup beer

Directions:
In a 1 cup glass measuring cup, combine sauce ingredients.
Microwave on high for 1 to 1 ½ minutes until bubbly
Set aside
Shape ground beef into patties (about ¾ inch thick)
Place onion slices on the grill, over medium coals.
Grill onions, uncovered, for about 4 minutes
Add patties, continuing to grill uncovered for 13-15 minutes or until onions are tender and burger centers are no longer pink, turning occasionally.
Season burgers with salt
Approximately 2 minutes before the burgers are done, brush generously with sauce mixture, but reserve a small amount
Top with cheese
Remove from grill
Place in buns and top burgers with some of the reserve sauce and other toppings.
