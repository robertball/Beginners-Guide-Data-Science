Title:
Air-Fried Greek Yogurt Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7641469.jpg
Ingredients:
1/8 cup minced onion
1/8 cup minced carrot
1/8 cup minced celery
1/8 cup minced Italian parsley
1/2 tsp light olive oil
4 large eggs
1/3 cup no fat plain greek yogurt
1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
50 grams fresh baby spinach leaves, roughly chopped
50 grams fresh baby spinach
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper flakes

Directions:
1. Heat an air fryer to 350Â° F with a non-stick 6x2- inch cake pan inside. After it reaches temperature, remove the cake pan with an oven mitt. Add oil, onion, carrot, celery, and parsley. Mix and return pan to the air fryer. Cook for 5 minutes or until the onions are translucent and tender.
2. Meanwhile, whisk the eggs, greek yogurt, black pepper and chopped spinach. Pour the mixture into the cake pan and lightly mix. Return the pan to the air fryer. Cook for 18 minutes or until browned on top and cooked through.
3. Remove the cake pan. Let cool for 5 minutes. Remove from pan. Sprinkle with cayenne pepper and serve over the remaining fresh spinach leaves

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Greek yogurt, eggs, and fresh spinach cook in your air fryer to make a healthy brunch meal. The yogurt gives a lovely texture and is healthier than fatty cheeses.
