Title:
Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 Cup of crushed corn flakes
1/2 Cup of parmesan cheese
3/4 Cup of mayo

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In medium bowl, combine parmesan and corn flakes.
In separate dish, cover chicken breasts with mayo.
Then roll chicken breasts in corn flake mixture.
Bake for 1 hour.

Submitter:

Description:
