Title:
Mexican Tortilla Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/343332.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pkg 8-10 inch tortillas
1 lb ground beef
1 can rotel
1 pkg taco seasoning
1 can refried beans
2 cups shredded cheese
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350, and grease a 8-10 inch round baking dish.  Brown beef, and drain off grease, put back in pan and add 1/2 cup water, taco seasoning, and rotel (undrained), and allow liquid to cook out, about 10 minutes.  Layer in baking dish, 1 flour tortilla, beans, beef, cheese, repeat 3 more times (4 layers total) with cheese on top.  Bake for 25-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Brooke129
Description: 
Flour tortillas, layered with seasoned beef, refried beans and cheese make for a simple and tasty meal that your kids will love!
