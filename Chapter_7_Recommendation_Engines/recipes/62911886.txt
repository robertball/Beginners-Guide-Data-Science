Title:
Oktoberfest Chicken and Red Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/708105.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices of bacon
Package of chicken thighs (skin off)
1/4 cup flour, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, and 1/2 tsp smoked paprika to dredge the chicken
1 red cabbage, sliced
1 red or sweet onion, sliced
1 large apple, sliced
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup dry red wine
(optional to add 1/2 tsp cinnamon)

Directions:
Fry bacon in a in a cast iron pot on med-med-high heat. While bacon is frying, dredge the chicken in flour, salt and smoked paprika mixture. Set bacon aside. Fry chicken in bacon fat until brown on both sides, transfer to a plate. Drain bacon fat, but leave a table spoon in the pot. Place pot on Med heat, add onion and apple and cook until translucent. Add the cabbage and a pinch or two of kosher salt, let cook until a little soft. Add red wine vinegar, brown sugar, and red wine, and use it to further de-glaze the pan.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place chicken on top of cabbage and place pot in the oven for 40 mins.
