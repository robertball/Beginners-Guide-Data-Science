Title:
Apple Strudel  / "Apfel Strudel" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz flour
4 oz Water
1 oz oil
pinch of Salt.
1 Egg for Make up.
18 oz Apple
4 oz Sugar
1 oz Starch
4 oz Raisins
3 oz Bread crumbs
ground:  Cinnamon, Nutmeg,  Cloves
3 oz Butter

Directions:
Mix a Dough of Flour, salt,  Water and Oil.  Let rest and then begin rolling it out on a board or Table on a  well floured Linen sheet. roll out as thinly as possible,  pull out the edges if thick.  / Cover.
Core and peel the Apples cut into eight and each slice into 6 pieces. mix with the Raisins, Sugar, Starch,cinnamon,Nutmeg and Clove.  Melt the Butter;  uncover the Drying Dough Sheet and Brush carefully with the Butter ( using your Hand).  Sprinkle the Bread crumbs evenly over the entire surface and carefully apply the Apple mix to two Thirds of the Length.  From the Apple side Begin lifting the Sheet under the Dough and roll it  carefully toward the  other end tucking the ends near the end.   carefully transfer to a sheeted Baking Tray;  seam down.  brush with egg yolk and Bake at 375 degree,  til Golden.  Cool before cutting. ( Rewarm by serving if Desired).   Garnish  with Powdered Sugar,  Whipped Cream.
