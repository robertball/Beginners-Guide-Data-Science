Title:
Mexican Mostaccioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 16oz package mostaccioli noodles
1 24 oz jar picante sauce
2 15oz can stewed tomatoes, undrained
1 1/2 C shredded mexican style cheese

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package.
In a large pot brown ground beef and drain.
Add picante sauce & stewed tomatoes to ground beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
Add cooked noodles to beef mixture. Stir until coated and then add shredded cheese and continue stirring until cheese is melted.

This is an easy quick dinner and stretches out hamburger for a budget meal
