Title:
Seafood Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick butter
1 clove garlic, minced
1 onion, diced
1-2T cornstarch OR flour
salt and pepper to taste
32 oz chicken broth
2 carrots - sliced in 1/4" rounds
2 stalks celery - sliced
1 box grape tomatoes - sliced in 1/2
1 green pepper - diced
8 oz okra - sliced in 1/2"rounds
2T paprika
2-3T minced parsley
2-3T minced cilantro
2-3T minced lemon balm (optional)
1 bay leaf
1 16 oz bag frozen cooked salad shrimp
1 12 oz bag frozen scallops
1 lb cat fish nuggets
1 lb andouille sausage
2C cooked rice
bottled hot sauce

Directions:
In a large stock pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour (or cornstarch), diced onion and minced garlic. Toss lightly until the onions begin to brown; season with salt and pepper.
Slowly add  the chicken broth while stirring to dissolve any remaining lumps of flour (or cornstarch) Simmer over medium heat ( small bubbles should be visible along the sides of the pot) Stir in vegetables, paprika and herbs.
While the vegetables are simmering, cut scallops, sausage and catfish into bite sized pieces. Add sausage, shrimp, scallops and catfish to the simmering broth. Cover and continue to simmer until flavors are blended and  vegetables and meats are tender (about 45 minutes total)
About 15 minutes before serving, remove the bay leaf and add the cooked rice to the simmering broth.
Serve in bowls; season to taste with hot sauce.
