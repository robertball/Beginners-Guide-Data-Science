Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pork chops
64 ounces sauerkraut with juice
1 onion, sliced
1 Granny Smith apple, diced
3 teaspoons Caraway seeds

Directions:
Season the chops with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.  Place 4 chops in the bottom the slow cooker and pour the half of the sauerkraut over the top.  Sprinkle with half of the Caraway seeds.  Place remaining four chops on top of kraut, then remaining kraut on top of chops.  Sprinkle with remaining Caraway seeds. Cook on high 2 hours then low for 5 hours.
