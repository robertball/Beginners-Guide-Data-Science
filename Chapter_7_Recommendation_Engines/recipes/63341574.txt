Title:
Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups sugar
8 cups water
1 1/2 cups lemon juice

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine sugar and 1 cup water.  Bring to boil and stir to dissolve sugar.
Allow to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate until chilled.
Remove seeds from lemon juice, but leave pulp.
In pitcher, stir together chilled syrup, lemon juice and remaining 7 cups water.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Lemonade is a very refreshing drink, and this is the best one ever!Modified from: Best Lemonade Ever
