Title:
Salmon Patties Recipe

Ingredients:
Salmon

chopped celery

egg

Directions:
saute onion and celery in butter till tender.
Flake salmon.
Throw all ingredients together.
Mashed potatoes are optional, but it does taste better with them in itMix all ingredients together, form into patties and fry
