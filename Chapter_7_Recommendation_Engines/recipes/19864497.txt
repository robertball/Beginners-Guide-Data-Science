Title:
Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb browned buffalo sausage with chili peppers
1 chopped onion
3 diced tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp cilantro
1 box veggie broth
1 bag frozen corn
1 can black beans

Directions:
Mix ingredients into slow cooker.
Cook all day.
