Title:
Fiesta Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds of ground beef
1 can black beans, drained
1 can pinto beans, drained
1 can yellow corn, drained
1 can Rotel Mexican Festival tomatoes
1 can Diced Fire Roasted tomatoes
1 packet dry Ranch seasoning
1 pack Taco seasoning
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped

Directions:
Brown beef. Add everything in crockpot with 1 can of water. Cover and cook on low 6-8 hours or on high 4-5 hours.

Submitter: 
WILKBROWN
Description: 
Simple, weeknight basic chili recipe with plenty of room to accommodate your special seasonings.
