Title:
Fried Green Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Green Tomatoes
Flour
Seasoned Salt
Pepper
Egg
Oil

Directions:
Put oil (or use olive oil and butter)to cover well the bottom of an electric skillet.
Have oil hot enough to sizzle when you put the tomatoes into it.
Slice tomatoes 1/4 thick
In a plastic bag put
Flour
Season Salt
Pepper
In a bowl, place well mixed eggs.
In a second bowl, place some flour.
Dip sliced tomato into flour in bowl
then into the beaten egg
and then place into mixture bag
When bag has enough tomatoes in it,fill bag with air, twist top, and shake well.
From this bag place tomatoes into fry pan and cook...not too long on each side or they will become ultra soft.
Drain on a paper toweled plate.
Keep warm in 200 oven...ENJOY!
