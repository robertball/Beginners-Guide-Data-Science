Title:
Easiest Baked Chicken Legs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254685.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 organic or air-chilled chicken legs
2 tablespoons olive oil or unsalted butter, melted
1 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 375 degrees.
Pat the chicken legs dry on a clean work surface.
Brush to coat with olive oil or butter.
Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika.
Place chicken in a shallow baking dish and bake until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees. (about 40-45 minutes)

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
These chicken legs are so easy and delicious. You can follow the recipes below or use any of your favorite herbs.
