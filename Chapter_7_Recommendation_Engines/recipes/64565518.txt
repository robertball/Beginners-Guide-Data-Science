Title:
Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups orange juice
1 large banana
1 cup pineapple
1 cup strawberries
1/2-3/4 cup blueberries
1-2 tablespoons agave syrup

Directions:
Put all in the blender and blend on high until VERY smooth (3 minutes?).
