Title:
Widow's Kiss Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
ice
1 fluid ounce Calvados (apple brandy)
1/2 fluid ounce yellow Chartreuse
1/2 ounce Benedictine® liqueur
1 dash aromatic bitters (such as Angostura®)
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and pour in Calvados, Chartreuse, and Benedictine(R). Shake in bitters and stir. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with a cherry.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
A great fall cocktail if there ever was one. Calvados mixes with Chartreuse, Benedictine®, and bitters.
