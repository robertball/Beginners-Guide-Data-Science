Title:
Berry Chocolate Candy Bar Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749479.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups frozen blueberries
1 cup frozen raspberries
2 cups dark leafy greens (red chard, purple kale, spinach...)
1 1/2 cups whole milk (cow, goat, almond, coconut...)
1 1/2 cups apple juice
2-4 TBS Cocoa Powder
4-5 TBS Maple Syrup (grade B)
1 tsp Vanilla
2-3 pinches Sea Salt

Directions:
Blend. Taste. Add extra maple syrup, cocoa powder, vanilla or salt if needed. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Shrazzi
Description: 
This is how I work some extra dark leafy greens into my kids' diet. Rich, chocolaty and packed with anti-oxidants and vitamins. The perfect way to detox in the midst of a decadent holiday season.
