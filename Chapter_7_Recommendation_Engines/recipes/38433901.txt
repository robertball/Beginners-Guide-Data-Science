Title:
Embers' Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade-
2 cups soy sauce
1 cup water
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tbl dark molasses
1 tsp salt
6 center-cut pork chop
Red Sauce-
1 tbl dry mustard
1/4 cup water
1 cup brown sugar
1 regular size bottle catsup
1 bottle chili sauce

Directions:
Mix and bring to boil soy sauce, water, brown sugar, molasse and salt.
Place pork chops, bone side up, in pan and pour cooled marinade over chops.
Chill overnight in refrigerator.
Next day, place chop in baking pan and cover tightly with foil.
Place in 375 degree oven and bake till tender, about 2 hours.
While chops are baking, combine mustard, water, brown sugar-leaving no lumps.
Combine with catsup and chili sauce.
Place in heavy pot or double broiler and bring to a slight boil.
when chop are tender, remove from oven.
Dip in red sauce and bake for 30 minute in an uncovered pan at 350 degree till slightly glazed.
Keep chop at room temp till ready to put on charcoal. (grill outside)
Put over bed of coals raised a high a possible.
Grill chops up to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sue
Description: 
Takes 2 days for this recipe to be complete as you have to marinade the pork chops overnight.  Final step is grilling chops for 15 minute.
