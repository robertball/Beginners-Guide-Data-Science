Title:
Mueller's Lasagna Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 lb. ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
3 cloves of garlic - minced
1/2 cup chopped Italian parsley
2 (28 oz.) cans of crushed tomatoes
1 (6 oz.) can of tomato paste
2 tsp. sugar
2 tsp. dried basil
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp. pepper
Filling:
1 (15 oz.) container of ricotta cheese
8 oz. frozen spinach, thawed and drained (optional)
1 egg - lightly beaten
1 cup grated Parmesan
12 oz. of shredded mozzarella
1 lb. cooked lasagna noodles

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot and add ground beef, onion, garlic and parsley.
Cook about 10 minutes or until beef is browned.
Add remaining sauce ingredients and simmer about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, 3/4 cup Parmesan and spinach if using.
In a 13x9 baking dish, spread about 1 1/2 cups of sauce in the bottom.
Layer 1/3 of lasagna noodles, 2 cups of sauce, 1/2 of the ricotta mixture and 1/3 of the mozzarella.
Repeat.
Bake at 350 for about 50 minutes or until bubbly.
Let stand at least 15 minutes before cutting.
