Title:
Adventure Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped dates
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup milk
1 stick butter
1 egg, well beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 cups crispy rice cereal (such as Rice Krispies®)
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 (14 ounce) package sweetened flaked coconut (such as Bakers® Angel Flake®)

Directions:
Combine dates, sugar, milk, and butter in a saucepan and cook over medium-low heat until thick, 5 to 10 minutes. Set aside to cool.
Add egg, vanilla extract, and salt to the date mixture; stir to combine. Add crispy rice cereal and walnuts and mix very well.
Pour coconut into a shallow bowl. Shape the date mixture into balls and roll in the coconut.

Submitter: 
Jacki_In_Simi
Description: 
This is another recipe that mom made for us frequently, especially in the summer, because they are a 'no-bake' cookie and freeze well. We'd eat them straight out of the freezer and, man, were they good!
