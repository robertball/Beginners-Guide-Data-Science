Title:
Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. Bottom Roasts
2 Tbsp Flour
½ tsp. Garlic
Salt & Pepper
2 Cans Stewed Tomatoes
2 tsp. Sugar
1 tsp. Dry Mustard
¼ tsp. pepper
Potatoes
Carrots
Onions
Celery

Directions:
Sprinkle meat with Flour, Garlic,Salt & Pepper
Beat to ¼”; Sear;
Mix Tomatoes, Sugar, Mustard, & pepper
Cut up veggies
In a Casserole Dish- Place steak in center, surround with vegetables; Cover with sauce. Bake @ 275 degrees 5-8 hours

Submitter: 
Lisa Harmon
Description: 
Mouthwatering Recipe. Plan on 8 hours for a fall apart steak.
