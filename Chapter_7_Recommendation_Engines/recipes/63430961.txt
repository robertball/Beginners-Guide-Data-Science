Title:
Spinach and Black Bean Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/929474.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup baby spinach, washed
1/2 tsp. olive oi
1 large flour tortillal
1/4 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 slice Monterey Jack cheese, ripped into smaller pieces
2 slices Mozzarella cheese, ripped into smaller pieces
Pinch of cilantro
Pinch of red pepper flakes
Pinch of cumin
Pinch of salt
Pinch of pepper
Spicy mayonnaise

Directions:
Heat olive oil in skillet over medium high heat.  Turn on panini press to heat.
Place spinach in skillet with olive oil.  Heat until slightly wilted and remove from heat.
Place large flour tortilla on plate.  Using half the cheese, place a layer on half of the tortilla.  Then add beans and spinach on top of cheese.  Add salt, pepper, cumin, cilantro, and red pepper flakes.  Add remaining cheese.  Fold tortilla in half to create the quesadilla.
Place quesadilla on heated panini press.  Heat until cheese melts and remove from panini press.  Serve with spicy mayonnaise.
