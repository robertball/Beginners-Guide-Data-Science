Title:
Great On The Lips, But Easy On The Hips- Spicy Chipotle Black Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 15oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
1- 7oz. can chipotle sauce
1- 16oz. jar salsa (mild, medium or hot)
Juice of one lime, or to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth.
Refrigerate several hours to allow flavors to blend.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This dip is great for any occasion. Serve it with light or fat free tortilla chips for a heathy snack or appetizer. Also great served on tacos or inside of wraps.
