Title:
Crunchy Taco Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup LAND O LAKESÂ® Butter
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
8 cups bite-size crispy corn cereal squares

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Melt butter in 13x9-inch baking pan in oven (3 to 4 minutes); stir in seasoning mix. Add cereal; stir to coat evenly. Bake, stirring after half the time, for 10 to 12 minutes or until cereal is crisp.

This snack mix is a crunchy lunchbox snack treat or a great party snack.
