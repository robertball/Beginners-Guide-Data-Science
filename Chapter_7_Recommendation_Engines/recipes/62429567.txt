Title:
The Best Honey Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
170g Honey
85g brown Sugar
140g Butter
1tbs milk
2beaten Eggs
200g Self Raising Flour

Directions:
Melt together Honey, Sugar, Butter, Milk (I do this in the microwave on med high for about 1 min). Let mix cool.
Add Eggs & SR Flour and stir well.
Pour into greased 1lb loaf tin
Bake at 180C for 30-40 min
Cool in tin, then turn out on rack

Submitter: 
love to cook
Description: 
Not your usual Honey cake tasting only of spices, but a recipe that lets you taste the honey! If you check the Foot note, you have the Gluten free version!
