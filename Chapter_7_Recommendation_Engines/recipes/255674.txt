Title:
Berry Apricot Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4061138.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Original Unsweetened Almond Breeze Almondmilk
1/2 banana
1 cup mixed frozen berries
2 fresh apricots, pitted
1 tablespoon chia seeds
1 scoop protein powder

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Banana, berries, fresh apricots, chia seeds, and protein powder blended with almondmilk make a quick and filling breakfast or snack.
