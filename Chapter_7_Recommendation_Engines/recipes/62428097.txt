Title:
White Bean and Roasted Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2C chopped coooked chicken
1 (19oz) can cannellini beans, rinsed
2 plum tomatoes, chopped
1/2C halved thin red onion slices
1/3C Ranch dressing
1 pkg. (10oz) torn mixed salad greens
1/3 C chopped fresh basil

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients
Cover platter with salad greens; top with chicken mixture
Sprinkle with basil

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Leftover cooked chicken, tomatoes, fresh basil, and cannellini beans served over salad greens, and its a quick summer dish
