Title:
Roast Salmon With Balsamic Vinegar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/w lbs. salmon fillets
2 Tbsp packed brown sugar
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 tsp dried rosemary, dill or tarragon (or 1 tsp chopped fresh)
1/4 tsp pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Pat salmon dry and place on foiled-lined baking dish.
In small bowl, combine sugar, vinegar, oil, herbs, pepper and garlic.
Pour over top of fish, cover and set aside to marinate for 30 minutes.
Sprinkle fish with salt and roast in 450F oven for 10 minutes. Spoon marinade over fish and broil for 1 minute or until glazed and fish flakes easily with a fork.

Submitter: 
cdagirl
Description: 
These simple and few ingredients make for quick but elegant entertaining. The final broiling carmelizes the sugar to give the salmon it's attractive, mildly sweet glaze.
