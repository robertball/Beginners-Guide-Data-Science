Title:
Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb bag of southern hashbrowns
1 stick of butter, melted
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 tsp of instant onions
1/4 tsp of salt
8 oz of sour cream
2 c of cheddar sheese

Directions:
Place frozen potatoes in a 9 x 13 dish. Mix all the other ingredents together and mix with the potatoes. Bake covered for 45 minutes and uncover for the last 15 minutes. (You can top with crushed cornflakes for last 15 minutes)
