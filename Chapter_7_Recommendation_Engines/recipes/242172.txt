Title:
Paleo Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup firmly packed fresh basil leaves
1/2 cup olive oil, or as needed
1/4 cup raw pumpkin seeds
1/4 cup raw cashews
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 cloves garlic
1/2 teaspoon sea salt (optional)
1/2 teaspoon coconut aminos (soy-free seasoning sauce)
1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
Process basil, olive oil, pumpkin seeds, cashews, lemon juice, garlic, sea salt, coconut aminos, and cayenne pepper in a food processor, adding more oil as necessary, until desired consistency is reached.
