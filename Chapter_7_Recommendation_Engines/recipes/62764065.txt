Title:
Dotty's Fruit Cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. lemon Jello
1 1/2 Cups boiling water
6 oz. frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
20 oz. pineapple chunks in heavy syrup (DO NOT drain)
11 oz. can mandarine oranges (DO NOT drain)
11 oz. can of mandarine oranges DRAINED
1 Cup red seedless grapes
16 oz. canned pears in heavy syrup, sliced and DRAINED
16 oz. canned peaches in heavy syrup, sliced and DRAINED
1 jar marichino cherries, RINSED and DRAINED
2 bananas, sliced

Directions:
Dissolve Jello in boiling water.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Chill over night and serve cold.  May be presented in individual cups, or spooned from a large, glass serving bowl.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
Make-ahead fruit salad with a surprisingly light and creamy texture.  Ideal for picnics.
