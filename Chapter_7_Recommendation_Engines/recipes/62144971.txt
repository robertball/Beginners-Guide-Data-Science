Title:
Rich Turkey Carcass Broth (crockpot) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 turkey carcass
1 carrot, coarsely chopped
1 onion, coarsely chopped
2 stalks celery, coarsely chopped
1 T whole peppercorns
2 Bay leaves
salt to taste
approximately 8 cups water, or enough to barely cover carcass

Directions:
Place turkey carcass, carrot, onion, celery, peppercorns, bay leaves and salt in crockpot. If carcass is too large for crockpot, break carcass into pieces to fit.
Pour water over carcass and vegetables until just covered with water. Cover with crockpot lid. Cook on low for twenty-four (24) hours.
Let cool for several hours. Strain well and discard carcass and vegetables.
Refrigerate for at least 6 hours. After completely chilled in refrigerator, remove any hardened fat from top of broth and discard.
Use broth as base for soup. Freezes well.

Submitter: 
BDEGER
Description: 
This recipe makes the most flavorful broth that is a perfect starter for turkey noodle / rice soup or any other recipe calling for turkey / chicken broth. An added bonus is your home will smell wonderful for the 24 hours it takes to cook.
