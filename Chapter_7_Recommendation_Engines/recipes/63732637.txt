Title:
Crock Pot Cream Cheese Chicken Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036469.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs boneless skinless chicken, cut into 1 inch pieces
4 tbsp butter, melted and divided
1 pack Italian salad dressing mix
1 small onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
10 1/2 oz cream of chicken soup
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 lbs fettuccine noodles

Directions:
Place the chicken in the crock pot and sprinkle 2 tbsp of melted butter over the chicken. Toss the chicken well to coat with butter. Sprinkle Italian seasoning over the chicken. Cook on low for 6 hours.
Melt 2 tbsp of butter in a saucepan then saut√© onion and garlic until tender. Add cream of chicken soup, cream cheese, and chicken broth. Stir until smooth. Add this mixture to the crock pot and cook on low for an additional hour.
During the last hour of cooking, prepare the fettuccine according to the package directions. When the noodles are done, add to the crock pot and stir well to completely coat the noodles in chicken mixture. Close crock pot and allow to finish cooking the remainder of the last hour.
