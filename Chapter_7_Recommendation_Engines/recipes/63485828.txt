Title:
Spicey Tomato Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of diced tomatoes
1 small onion chopped
1/2 half green pepper chopped
1 cup bread crumbs
Dash Worcestershire sauce
Dash hot pepper
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and bake @ 400 degrees until brown on top and sides are bubbling.

Melissa Vincent
