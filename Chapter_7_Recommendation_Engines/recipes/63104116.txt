Title:
Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787513.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 14 oz. can of Fat Free Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 - 12 oz. can of Fat Free Evaporated Milk
1/4 cup pumpkin puree
1 t. pumpkin pie spice (see my homemade recipe)
1 t. vanilla or more if you like it more vanilla*ey
I did use a bit of water to wash out both cans.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well in a bowl with a whisk.
Using a funnel, pour it into a mason jar.
Store in fridge

Submitter: 
Scotdog
Description: 
A mix of pumpkin and vanilla make this a delicious homemade coffee creamer.
