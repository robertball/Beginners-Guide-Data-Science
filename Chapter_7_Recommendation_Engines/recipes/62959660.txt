Title:
Kartoshka Russian  pastry I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.75 cups Bread crumbs
1/2 cup milk
1/4 to 1/2 cup sugar (can be powdered)
1/2 cup Milk
1/2 to 1 cup chopped walnuts
150g or 10.5 tbs butter or margarine

Directions:
1) combine milk, sugar, cocoa, heat on pan
2) once mixture boils stir in butter until it melts; leave to cool for a few minutes
2)combine bead crumbs, nuts, and the mixture
3) mesh together until thick consistency, shape into medium size balls
4) you can then roll them in: cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate shavings or glaze then in chocolate (after refrigeration!)
5) put into fridge for at least an 40 min. (the longer the better)
