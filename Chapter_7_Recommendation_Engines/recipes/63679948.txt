Title:
Homemade Turkey Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 pounds ground turkey
1 medium pear or apply, pealed, cored and coarsely grated
1.5 tbsp. chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
2 tbsp. finely chopped fresh sage (or 2 tsp ground sage)
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. ground allspice

Directions:
Stir together all ingredients in a large bowl until well combined.
Freeze for later or cook immediately in a little oil.
