Title:
Vegan Sweet Potato Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup smashed baked sweet potato - skins left on
1/2 cup almond, soy, or oat milk
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1-2 Tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
Stevia or sugar to taste

Directions:
Blend everything together until smooth. Add additional liquid if necessary. Chill til thick.
