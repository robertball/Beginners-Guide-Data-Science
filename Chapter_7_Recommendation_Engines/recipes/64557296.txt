Title:
Chewy Double Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup salted butter
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups flour
3/4 cups cocoa
1 tsp baking soda
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
1) Melt butter slightly, add sugar and eggs, beat well.
2) Add flour, baking soda, vanilla and cocoa and beat again.
3) Add chocolate chips.
4) Drop from teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet.
5) Bake at 300 for 9 minutes.
6) Remove cookies ASAP from pan as soon as they are cool enough to keep their shape. Do not let cool on pan.
