Title:
Sweet Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head of cabbage shredded
2 large carrots shredded
1/2 cup sugar
1 stalk of celery finely chopped (optional)
1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional)
1 1/2 cup mayo
3/4 cup sweet pickle relish
salt to taste or season salt

Directions:
In large mixing bowl mix cabbage, carrots, celery, sugar, salt and lemon juice until well blended. Add mayo and relish, mix well. Refrigerate for 2 hours or over night. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Simple and sweet. Great for family gathering. Barbeque Ribs or chicken, baked beans and cole slaw. What could be better than that?
