Title:
Collard Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/728054.jpg
Ingredients:
1 or 2 T olive oil
1 bunch collard greens, stemmed and cut in chiffonade
1 minced clove garlic (or more)
1 t red chili flakes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil over high heat in wok. Add chili flakes and garlic, cook for under one minute taking care not to let the garlic burn. Add the collards, toss to coat. sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cook until wilted, add a couple of tablespoons of water and cook/stir until the water evaporates and the collards are cooked through.
