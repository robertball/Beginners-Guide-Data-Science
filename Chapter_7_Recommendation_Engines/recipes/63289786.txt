Title:
Mildred P. Watermelon Pickles ( sweet ) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med watermelon- pulp removed- make sure leave some pulp on the rind
7 cups sugar
2 cups vinegar ( we used apple cider vinegar)
1/2 T oil of cloves
1/2 T oil of cinnamon

Directions:
cut up melon 1" pieces - make sure you leave pink on the rind
Day 1 in large pan combine sugar and spices - dissolve sugar - cook rind till fork goes through but not soft
let stand overnight
Day 2  drain liquid - reheat and pour over rind- let stand overnight
Day 3 drain liquid and reheat - pour int sterile jars and seal

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
oil of cinnamon flavor...worth the 3 day process
