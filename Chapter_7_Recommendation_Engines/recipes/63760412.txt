Title:
Italian Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup hot water
1/2 cup sundried tomatoes
1 lb ground beef
1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
3/4 cup finely chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped basil
1/2 cup ketchup
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 egg whites
2 ounces sharp provolone cheese

Directions:
Soak the sundried tomatoes in the hot water for 30 minutes.
Prepare loaf pan and preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine beef, bread crumbs, onion, basil, garlic, ketchup, provolone, egg whites and (drained) sundried tomatoes in a non-reactive bowl.
Place in loaf pan, and bake one hour, or until the meatloaf reaches an internal temperature of 160 degrees.
