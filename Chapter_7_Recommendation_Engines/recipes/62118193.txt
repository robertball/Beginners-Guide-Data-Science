Title:
Super tuna sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can solid albacore tuna, drained
1 large green apple, chopped (small pieces)
4 medium celery stalks, diced
1/2 cup red onion, minced
4 steinfelds dill pickles, chopped (medium pieces)
1/2 cup quartered red skin, seedless, grapes
2 tbsp low fat mayo (more if necessary)
fresh black pepper
your favorite bread (we use pitas)
green leaf lettuce

Directions:
Prepare all fruits/veggies as listed above.
In a large mixing bowl, place all ingredients.
mix well.
stuff your pita or spread on your bread of choice and enjoy!
tip: I always serve these with fresh fruit salad and carrot sticks. Its wonderful vitamin and protein packed lunch that adds a little extra flavor to the normal tuna and pickle sandwich.

Submitter: 
SerenaBloom
Description: 
This is a great way to get some extra fruits and veggies into your kids diet.. the best part of all, it only takes one can of tuna!
