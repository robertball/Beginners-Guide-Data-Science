Title:
Davenport Brunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp. paprika
8oz. sour cream
1 lb hot sausage (cooked and drained)
12 eggs (whipped)
butter or Pam

Directions:
In a 9x13 buttered baking dish, sprinkle half of the cheese on the bottom of the dish.  Mix paprika and sour cream.  Spread half of the sour cream mixture over cheese.  Spread crumbled sausage over sour cream mixture.  Pour eggs over sausage.  Spread remaining cheese over eggs.  Spread remaining sour cream over cheese.  Bake in oven at 350 for 35-45 minutes.
