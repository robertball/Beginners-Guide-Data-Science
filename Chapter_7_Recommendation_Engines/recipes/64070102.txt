Title:
Carrot Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
¼ cup chopped walnuts
½ cup milk
1 cup finely grated carrots

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.  Beat in eggs one at a time.  Mix in vanilla.  In a separate bowl mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, salt and walnuts.  Add 1/3 of the flour mixture into the butter mixture until combined (about 1 minute).  Then add half the milk and mix until combined.  Then add 1/2 the remaining flour mixture and mix until combined.  Then add the rest of the milk and mix until combined.  Then add the rest of the flour mixture until combined.   Fold in carrots.  Scoop into the cupcake liners and bake at 145 C for 15-17 minutes.  Cool and top with favorite cream cheese frosting.

Submitter: 
Bobbie Susan
Description: 
I've never liked carrot cake because it always being too dense.  So, I started experimenting with trying to make a lighter carrot cupcakes.  Viola!
