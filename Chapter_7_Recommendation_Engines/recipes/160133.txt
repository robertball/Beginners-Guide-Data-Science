Title:
Chicken and Mashed Potato Dumpling Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
Dumplings Ingredients:
1 cup Simply Potatoes® Mashed Potatoes
1 cup all-purpose baking mix
3 tablespoons milk
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
Soup Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup sliced carrots
2 stalks celery, chopped
1/2 cup chopped onions
3 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
2 cups chopped rotisserie or cooked chicken
1 bay leaf
1/2 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning

Directions:
In medium bowl stir together all dumpling ingredients; mix well. Set aside.
Heat oil in Dutch oven or 12-inch skillet*. Add carrots, celery and onion. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, 6 to 8 minutes or until vegetables are soft. Add chicken broth, chicken, bay leaf and Italian seasoning. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low. Cook about 10 minutes or until vegetables are soft.
Bring soup back to a boil. Divide dumpling mixture into 12 large balls. Drop dumplings on top of soup in single layer. Cook, uncovered, over low heat for 10 minutes. Cover; cook 10 minutes until dumplings are no longer dough underneath.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
A true comfort food, using Simply Potatoes® Mashed Potatoes makes a simple soup into a hearty meal.
