Title:
Luau Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs pork back ribs
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
1/4 cup packed brown or golden sugar
2 Tbsp. cornstarch
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 can (8 oz)crushed pineapple, undrained
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 Tbsp. soy sauce
1/8 tsp hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Line a large rimmed baking sheet with nonstick foil. Place ribs in a single layer in prepared pan. Sprinkle both sides of ribs with seasoned salt. Bake, uncovered at 350F for 30 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare sauce. Combine brown sugar, cornstarch, ginger, salt and  garlic powder in a mediuim saucepan. Stir in remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil over medium heat stirring constantlly. Cook, stirring constantly, until thickend about 1-2 minutes. Remove pan from over. Basste ries with hal of suace. Continue baking, basting occasionally with remaining sauce for 1 1/4 - 1 1/2 hours or until ribs are tender and glazed. Cut into serving pieces. Serves 6-8.
