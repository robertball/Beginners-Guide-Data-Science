Title:
Ginger Lime Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984052.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 teaspoon grated lime peel
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
Cantaloupe and honeydew slices

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the sour cream, mayonnaise, honey, lime juice, lime peel and ginger.  Cover and refrigerate until serving.  Serve with melon.

Submitter: 
Shirley  Glaab
Description: 
'This creamy snack is tangy and refreshing,' remarks field editor Shirley Glaab of Hattiesburg, Mississippi. 'It's a fun alternative to sweet fruit dips.'
