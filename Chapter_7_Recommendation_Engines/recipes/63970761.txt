Title:
Pareve Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
graham cracker crust
2 egg whites, save yolks
1 tub pareve cream cheese
1/2 cup splenda, divided
1/2 Tbl lemon juice

Directions:
Beat egg whites until stiff. slowly fold in 1/4 cup Splenda
In another bowl, beat lemon juice, 1/4 cup Splenda, egg yolks & cream cheese
Combine 2 mixtures. Pour over crust.
Bake at 350 for 30-35min
Topping: Mix little Splenda & some pareve sour cream. Coat the pie after it is cooled in ref rig.
