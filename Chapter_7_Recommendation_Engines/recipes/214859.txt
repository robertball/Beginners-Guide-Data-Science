Title:
Creamy PHILLY Rose Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982274.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups penne pasta, uncooked
1/3 cup PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese Spread
1 1/2 cups pasta sauce
1/4 cup fresh basil

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed on package, omitting salt.
Meanwhile, heat pasta sauce in nonstick skillet on medium-high heat. Stir in cream cheese spread; cook and stir constantly 2 to 3 minutes or until melted.
Drain pasta; toss with sauce until evenly coated. Top with basil.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia Cream Cheese
Description: 
Stir a bit of cream cheese into prepared tomato sauce to create a pasta topper that's creamy, rich, and a lovely rose color.
