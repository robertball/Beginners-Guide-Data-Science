Title:
Chicken Neptune Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
8 asparagus spears, cooked and chopped into 2 inch chunks
1 cup crab meat
1/2 cup cooked salad shrimp
Hollandaise sauce package, such as Mckormicks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Place chicken in a baking dish and cook in preheated oven 20-25 minutes, until no longer pink
Prepare hollandaise sauce according to package direction
When chicken is cooked remove from oven and top each breast with 2 asparagus spears, 1/4 cup crab meat, and 1/8 cup shrimp.
Pour hollandaise sauce on top, enough to cover all topping, approximately 1/4 cup per breast.
Return to oven until topping is heated through, approximately 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
ambibambi
Description: 
This is a popular dish in restaurants where I live.  The neptune topping goes just as well on steak too.
