Title:
Blood Orange Yogurt Olive Oil Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/775485.jpg
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 cups All Purpose Flour
2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
2/3 cups Agave Nectar
1 cup plain Greek Yogurt
3 Eggs
1/2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
Zest of 3 Blood Oranges
1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/4 cup  and 2 tbsp Blood Orange Juice
1 tbsp Turbanado (raw) Sugar
1 cup Confectioner’s Sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Line a loaf pan with parchment, then grease and flour the pan.
In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, orange zest, vanilla extract, agave nectar and yogurt.
Combine wet and dry ingredients in one bowl.
Fold in olive oil.
Pour batter into pan and cook for 50 minutes.
Pour blood orange juice into a pot, then add turbanado sugar.  Stir until sugar dissolves, then set aside.
To make the glaze, combine confectioner’s sugar and additional 2 tbsp blood orange juice.
Take the cake out of the oven, then immediately pour the blood sugar/turbanado sugar combo over the cake.  Let the cake suck in the juice.
Pour the glaze over the cake once it cools, then enjoy!

Submitter: 
Foodtorum
Description: 
Wonderful use for blood oranges in the winter.  Plus, the cake is moist and requires no butter and very little processed sugar.  It's delicious with or without the glaze.
