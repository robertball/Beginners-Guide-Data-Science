Title:
Cubed Venison & Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cubed venison
1 cup flour
1 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp black pepper
pinch of parsley
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp instant coffee
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup water if needed
1/4" oil in pan

Directions:
Cut steaks about 3/4" thick or use cubed.
Dredge the steaks in mixture of flour, garlic powder, pepper and parsley.
Heat oil in deep fryer.
Brown steaks on both sides.
Remove and drain on paper towel.
Measure drippings in pan, and adjust to make 3 tbsp.
Stir in 3 tbsp flour and brown slightly.
Turn to low.
Add milk and cook, stirring constantly until thick.
Add instant coffee.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Return steaks to gravy and heat through.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Southern country fried venison steak and gravy. A twist with the instant coffee, but a great addition, guarantee your family will love it!
