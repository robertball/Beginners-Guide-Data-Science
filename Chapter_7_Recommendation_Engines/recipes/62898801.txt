Title:
Hot Artichoke-Spinch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped canned artichoke hearts
1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach
8 oz.cream cheese
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp.salt
1/8 tsp.garlic powder
1/2 tsp.crushed red pepper flakes
dash pepper

Directions:
Boil spinach and artichoke hearts in a cup of water over medium heat for 10 min.untill tender.Drain well when done.
Heat cream cheese in a small bowl in microwave set on high for 1 min.OR use sauce pan and heat on medium till just hot.
Add the spinach and artichoke hearts to the cream cheese and stir well.Add remaining ingredients to cream cheese,stir well and serve.
