Title:
Spinach Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-10 oz. pkgs. frozen spinach
2 c herb dressing
1 onion, minced
5 eggs, beated
1 stick butter, softened
1/2 c parmesan cheese
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp thyme

Directions:
1.) Combine all the ingredients and mix well.
2.) Roll the mixture into balls and and put on greased pan or baking stone.
3.) Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
