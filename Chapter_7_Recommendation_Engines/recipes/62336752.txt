Title:
Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665422.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 apples peeled and chopped
1 20oz. can of crushed pineapple
1/2 cup of golden raisins
1 Tablespoon of roasted sunflower seed meats
1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a bowl.
Chill at least 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
andee
Description: 
This is a simple side dish that goes with just about anything. It can be made ahead because the juice from the pineapple keeps the apples from turning brown.
