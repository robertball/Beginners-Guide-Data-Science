Title:
Instant Pear and Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium pears, peeled and sliced 1/4" thick
1/2 cup softened butter
4 packets of apple cinnamon instant oatmeal
1/8 tsp. ground ginger
1/8 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Place sliced pears in an 8" casserole dish.
In a small bowl mix together ginger, cinnamon and salt.
Add vanilla and spice mixture to pears and toss until evenly mixed.
Smooth Pears out flat and even.
In another bowl, add softened butter and instant oatmeal packages. Mix with fork until butter is evenly mixed into oatmeal.
Pour on top of the pears in casserole dish and evenly spread crumble topping over. Lightly press topping down with fork.
Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes (until topping has browned and pears are tender).
Serve warm.
