Title:
Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. frozen orange juice concentrate
1 c. milk
1 c. water
6 T. sugar
1 t. vanilla
2 T. coffee mate
10 ice cubes

Directions:
Pour all of the ingredients in a blender and blend on high until smooth.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
This is just like the drink from the mall.  I used to love them but can't find them anymore so this is my take on it.
