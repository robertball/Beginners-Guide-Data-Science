Title:
Strawberrylicious Daiquiris Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/803700.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) strawberries, halved
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/3 cup unsweetened pineapple juice
3/4 cup spiced rum
ice

Directions:
Combine strawberries and sugar in blender; blend until smooth. Pour in pineapple juice, lime juice, and rum. Pulse until combined. Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
AZ
Description: 
Fresh strawberries paired with pineapple and lime make for a perfect drink on that first hot day of summer! Dark spiced rum adds a special flavor that stands up to the flavor burst you get from the fresh berries.
