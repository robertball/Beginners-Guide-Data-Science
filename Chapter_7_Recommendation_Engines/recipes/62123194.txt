Title:
Rice-crust chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 can milk
2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
2 cups of cooked, cooled rice
1 egg
onion powder, black pepper to taste
olive oil
bread crumbs optional

Directions:
Mix cooled rice with egg and seasoning to taste.  Place in lightly oiled pie plate (olive oil) and push into shape of dish.  Cook chicken in soup and milk until no longer pink.  Mix in vegetables.  Pour onto rice crust.  If desired, top with seasoned bread crumbs.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until egg in crust is set.
Serve immediately.

Description: 
This is a simple pot pie using a rice crust instead of a pie crust.
