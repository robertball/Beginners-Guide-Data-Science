Title:
Biscuit and Baking Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c all purpose flour
3 T Baking Powder
1 T Salt
3/4 c Shortening

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and pulse in food processor until mixture resembles corse cornmeal.  Store tightly covered in refrigerator.
