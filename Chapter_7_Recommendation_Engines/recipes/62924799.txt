Title:
Apple Bread Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TB honey
3/4 c apple cider
1 package yeast
3.5 C flour
1/2 C warm water
1 tsp salt
1/2 stick melted butter
1 TB Cinnamon
1 Medium apple peeled and diced

Directions:
Mix honey, cider, and yeast until frothy. Set for 5-10 minutes.
Mix the rest of the ingrediants, add the honey/cider/yeast mixture.
Cover with a warm towel for 90 minutes to rise.
Knead.
Form into 12 rolls of equal size. Place on baking sheet and allow to rise for 30 minutes.
Bake at 350 for about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chrissy Mc
Description: 
A tangy treat, these apple rolls will help you gear up for fall.
