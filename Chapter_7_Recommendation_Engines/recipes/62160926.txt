Title:
Asian Grilling Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/471789.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinade
1/2 C Soy Sauce
1/4 C Water
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
2 Tbsp Brown Sugar, packed
1 Tbsp Sesame Oil/Hot Sesame Oil
Juice of 1 Lemon
3 lbs Sirloin Tips

Directions:
Mix marinade ingredients together. Place beef in resealable plastic bag and add marinade. Seal well and gently knead bag around to distribute marinade. Place in refrigerator for 8 to 24 hours, rotating bag occasionally. Remove beef from bag and cook on grill or under broiler to desired doneness. Discard leftover marinade.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description:
