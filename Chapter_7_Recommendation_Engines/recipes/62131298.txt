Title:
Simple Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*FILLING*
2-3 cooked and chopped boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 cans condensed cream of chicken soup, as is
2 cans mixed vegetables, drained
few dashes hot sauce, (optional)
salt & pepper, (optional)
*CRUST*
1 cup baking mix (bisquick)
1 cup milk
1/2 chicken broth
1/2 stick of butter, melted

Directions:
-preheat oven to 350 degrees
-in casserole dish mix together filling
-in seperate bowl mix together crust
-pour crust mixture over the filling in casserole dish
-bake uncovered for 45mins-1 hour or until crust is cooked and slightly golden
-let sit for about 5 mins to thicken everything up
