Title:
Ms. Trish's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
1 Diced Onion
1 Can tomato sauce
1 Sleeve crackers
1 Â½ lb Hamburger meat
2 Eggs
1 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Pepper
1/2 TBLS Seasoning Salt
1/2 TBLS Paprika

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Mix all ingredients together in bowl. Shape into a loaf and pat until all air is out.
3. Cook for 1 hour. Put ketchup on top and bake for another 15 minutes.
