Title:
Indoor S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 graham cracker halves
3 large marshmallow, torn in half
1/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F. Melt chocolate chips in a double boiler, over medium heat.
Spread melted chocolate on half the crackers.
Place a marshmallow half on top of each chocolate-covered cracker. Place these crackers on a cookie sheet. Bake 3 minutes until marshmallows are puffed and browned. Remove from oven and top with remaining crackers.
Bake until top crackers are warm, about 1 minute.
