Title:
Easy and Tasty Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 strips of thick cut bacon, diced
1 egg
4 green onions, sliced into 1/4" pieces
3 cups cold cooked rice
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
3 Tbsp soy sauce

Directions:
Use large heavy pan and cook bacon until crispy brown.
Push bacon to edges of pan and crack an egg in the center.  Stir rapidly to scramble.
Move egg to edge of pan and add onions and garlic.  Saute 1-2 minutes.
Stir in rice and soy sauce and mix well.
Heat through and serve

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Bedsaul Johnson
Description: 
Simple basic fried rice that goes perfect with egg rolls
