Title:
Easy Summer Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Fusilli (corkscrew) pasta
1 8 oz. block cheddar cheese, cut into 1/2" cubes
2 cucumbers, peeled and cut into 1/2" cubes
2 tomatoes, cut into 1/2" cubes
1 bottle French dressing

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions and rinse with cold water. Transfer pasta to a large mixing bowl. Add remaining ingredients and stir to cover with dressing. Chill for at least one hour before serving.

Submitter: 
MOOSIE88
Description: 
A delicious and very easy to prepare pasta salad that is great paired with burgers or other sandwiches or as a meal by itself.
