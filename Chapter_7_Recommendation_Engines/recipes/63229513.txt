Title:
Banana Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sift together:
2 1/4 c flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp soda
1/2 tsp salt
Cream:
2/3 shortening (can substitute applesauce - remember that it won't have the preservatives so they won't keep as long)
2/3 sugar
Add:
2 eggs one at a time and beat after each
then add 2/3 c mashed bananas (2 small or 1/2 large bananas)
with lemon juice (enough to help with browning).
1/3 c evaporated milk
1 tsp vanilla
Stir in: Flour mixture
Add: 1 c chopped nuts, chocolate chips or raisens
