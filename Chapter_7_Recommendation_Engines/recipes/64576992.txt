Title:
12" x 2" Large Round Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cake mix (2 layer size), any flavor
PLUS:
another 1/2 cake mix = 8oz
4 eggs
3/4 cup oil
1 1/2 cups water

Directions:
Grease & flour pan.
Mix all ingredients as per cake mix instructions.
Bake @ 350 x 45 - 55 minutes, until tests done.
Cool x 20 min. before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This recipe is for a 12 x 2 large round cake pan, which it better than guessing how much batter you'll need! Classic for cake decorators.
