Title:
Sugar Glazed Walnuts Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 cups walnut halves
3/4 cup splenda
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Lightly grease a baking sheet. In a 1 quart glass measure, combine walnut halves, sugar, and water. Cook in microwave oven on high setting for 4 to 8 1/2 minutes, or until sugar has caramelized. Stir several times during cooking. Pour out onto prepared baking sheet, and separate into pieces. Let cool completely. store in an airtight container.
