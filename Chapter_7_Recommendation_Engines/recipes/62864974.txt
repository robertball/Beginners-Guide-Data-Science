Title:
Hamia's Red Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/686982.jpg
Ingredients:
2 green onions, chopped
3 tsp red chili
2 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp sesame seeds
1/2 tsp black pepper, freshly ground
1/2 cup soy sauce
*** 1/2 tsp MSG (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and chill for at least an hour.

Submitter: 
DadDuzTheCooking
Description: 
This is the sauce that made Hamia's seaweed soup sing.  Mild yet spicy, subtle yet flavorful.  This is the stuff that men kill for.
