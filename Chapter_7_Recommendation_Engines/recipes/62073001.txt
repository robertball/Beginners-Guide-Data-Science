Title:
Split Second Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c all purpose flour
2/3 c sugar
1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 c butter or margarine, softened
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1/3 c jelly or jam

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in medium bowl.  Blend in butter, egg, and vanilla. Divide dough into four parts, shape each part into a roll, about 1 inch diameter.  Arrange each roll about 4 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet.
With handle of wooden spoon, make an indentation a little less than 1/2 inch deep down center of each roll.  Fill indentation with jelly or jam.
Bake 350 for 20 minutes or until golden brown.  Lift each section carefully onto cutting board. Cut crosswise into bars approx 1 inch wide. Cool on wire racks.
Store leftovers, if any, in airtight container in cool, dry place.
