Title:
Elegant Dark Chocolate Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 ounce bag of Ghirardelli Bittersweet Chocolate Baking Chips
1 - 1/2 cups of La Choy Chow Mein Noodles
3/4 cup raw pecans and/or almonds
3/4 cup raisins

Directions:
Melt chips in double boiler and remove from heat. Mix noodles, nuts and raisins in a bowl, then stir into the chocolate in pan. Stir to distribute chocolate evenly onto nuts, noodles and raisins. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto wax paper on a cookie sheet. Place cookie sheet in the refrigerator for 15 minutes to solidify chocolate. Keep in a covered plastic dish with lid in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
ellie may
Description: 
No baking required. Great for holiday parties...a rich and sweet after-dinner treat with espresso. No one will guess the crunch comes from chow mein noodles! Hide these in the refrigerator in your Tupperware; keeps the chocolate firm and hubby and kids will think it's just leftovers...your secret will be safe!
