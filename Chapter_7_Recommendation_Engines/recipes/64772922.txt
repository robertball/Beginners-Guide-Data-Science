Title:
Ricotta Orange Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 sticks butter, at room temperature
1 1/2 cups cake flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups whole milk ricotta cheese
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 orange, zested
2 tablespoons amaretto
powdered sugar, for dusting

Directions:
1. Preheat to 350. Grease a loaf pan with butter. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.
2. Cream butter, ricotta, and sugar until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Add eggs one at a time. Then add the canilla, zest, and amaretto until combined. Add the dry ingredents, a small amount at a time, until just incorporated.
3. Pour the batter in the pan and bake until a toothpick comes out clean and edges pull away from the pan. About 45-50 minutes. Let cool completley. Dust with powdered sugar.
