Title:
Carrabba's House Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1/4 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon fresh parsley, minced
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Wisk together all ingredients in a small bowl.
Chill before serving.  Best is chilled overnight.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This recipe is a clone of the amazing house dressing at Carrabba's- a wonderful creamy parmesan dressing.  Is best served cold on chilled lettuce with cold plates.
