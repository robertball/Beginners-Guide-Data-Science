Title:
B.C.'s Olive Tapenade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pitted kalamata olives, finely minced
1/2 cup large pimento filled green olives, finely minced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tbsp shallots or onion, finely minced
1 tbsp flat leaf parsley, finely minced
1 tsp garlic, finely minced
3/4 tsp ground pepper

Directions:
Mix and enjoy!  Serve over crackers or sliced baguette.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
A delicious olive tapenade from my good friend B.C. Marks in Mazatlan Mexico.
