Title:
Tea Buscuits. No sugar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1425987.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
4 tsp baking powder
1tsp salt
1/2 cup margarine
1cup milk
1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions:
Sift dry ingredients. Cut in margarine.  Add milk,( and cheese)  Stir to make a soft dough.  Turn onto floured surface and knead 8 to 10 x's.  Shape into rectangle about 1/2 inch thick  and using a knife square off into equal portions.
Place onto ungreased baking sheet, 1 inch apart.
Bake 425 for 12 to 15 minutes
