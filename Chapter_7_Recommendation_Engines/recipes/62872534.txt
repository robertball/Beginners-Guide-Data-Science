Title:
Greek Seasoning Blend Spice Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp salt
2 tsp dried oregano
1 1/2 tsp onion powder
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp cornstarch
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 tsp beef flavored boullion granules
1 tsp dried parsley flakes
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
Combine salt, oregano, onion powder, garlic powder, cornstarch, pepper, beef bouillon, parsley, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl.  Transfer to an airtight container.  Store in a cool, dark place

Submitter: 
Kari
Description: 
Greek influenced spice blend featuring oregano, garlic, onion, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Great on grilled or oven-baked meats.
