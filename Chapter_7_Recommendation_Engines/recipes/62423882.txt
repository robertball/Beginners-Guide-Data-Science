Title:
Horseradish Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups crème fraîche or low fat sour cream
1/2 cup prepared white horseradish
6 tbso chopped fresh chives
4 tsp fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients to blend in small bowl.
Season with salt and pepper. Cover and chill.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
Perfect for beef tenderloin grilled with a good helping of mixed herbs! It can be made 2 days ahead.
