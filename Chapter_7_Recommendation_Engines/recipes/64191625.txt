Title:
Cream of Mushroom Duck Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1537674.jpg
Ingredients:
-1 can of Amy's Cream of Mushroom soup
-2-3 lbs of duck, in pieces no larger than 2" x 2"
-1/4 cup of butter
-3 tbs of parsley
-Pinch of fresh thyme leaves
-1 onion
-2 large carrots
-2 celery stalks
-pinch of nutmeg
-1/2 teaspoon of brown sugar
-salt and pepper to taste
-1 tbs half and half
-6 crimini mushrooms
-Wild rice, fully cooked

Directions:
Before cooking, soak duck in milk for 15-20 minutes.
Heat a small amount of oil in a pan and add chopped onion and duck. Add half and half and cook duck on medium/high heat until duck is browned.
Lower heat to a simmer and add butter, 1/4 can of cream of mushroom soup, brown sugar, nutmeg, thyme and 1 tbs of chopped parsley. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes to an hour.
Add the rest of the can of soup, mushrooms, carrots, and celery and simmer for 30 more minutes uncovered or until duck is fully cooked.
Serve with fresh parsley and over wild rice.

Submitter: 
Gina Newkirk
Description: 
This is a recipe my dad used to cook when I was a kid, and this is my rendition of it! Perfect for wild duck, no gamey flavor whatsoever,
