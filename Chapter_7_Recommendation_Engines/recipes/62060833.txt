Title:
Black Bean and Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5719185.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans corn, drained
1 can black beans, drained
1 red pepper, chopped
1 bunch green onions, chopped
finely chopped cilantro to taste
1 Tbsp canned chopped jalepenos
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp cumin

Directions:
Mix corn, beans, red pepper, onion, cilantro and jalepenos together in a large bowl.  Mix or shake the remaining ingredients together unitl well mixed, pour over salsa and combine.  Chill a few hours and serve with tortilla chips.
