Title:
Moist Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1 cup hot water
1/2 cup (or less) cocoa powder
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 egg
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Combine the first 6 ingredients.
Add egg and butter; mix well.
Pour into a greased 8" x 8" cake pan (or into 12 paper lined muffin tins for cupcakes).
Bake @ 350 x 30 - 35 minutes, or until tests done with a toothpick.
Bake approx. 15 min. for cupcakes.
Cool.
Frost or dust with icing sugar.
Freezes well.
* This recipe can be doubled for a 9" x 13" cake or 24 cupcakes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A quick, easy recipe for delicious chocolate cake or (wedding) cupcakes! A single batch makes an 8 square cake or 12 cupcakes, so double for 9 x 13 cake or for 24 cupcakes.
