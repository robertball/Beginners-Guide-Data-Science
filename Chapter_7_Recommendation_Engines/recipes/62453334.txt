Title:
Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 tbsp butter
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup peanuts dry roasted

Directions:
Combine sugar, corn syrup, and salt in 2 qt casserole dish.  Stir well.
Microwave on high for 8-10 minutes, the mixture will be light brown
Add butter, vanilla and blend thoroughly.  Return to microwave for additional 1.5 minutes.
Stir in baking soda QUICKLY, mix.
Pour onto a greased cookie sheet to cool- add peanuts and cool.

Submitter: 
charlotte henry
Description: 
My mother in law used to make this for Christmas and sometimes Thanksgiving.  I love it but must mind my teeth as this is very crunchy/chewey!
