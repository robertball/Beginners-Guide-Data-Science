Title:
Easy Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups of canned Tomato Juice
2 Beef Bouillon Cubes
1 large or 2 small Tomatoes - chopped
1/2 Cup Cucumber - chopped
4 Tbsp Green Pepper - chopped
6 Tbsp Onion - chopped
1 Tbsp Celery - finely chopped
4 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
2 Tbsp Salad Oil
1/2 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
Tabasco to Taste (Optional)

Directions:
Heat tomato juice to a low boil. Add the bouillon and stir until completely dissolved. Remove from heat and add the vegetables and Tabasco (if using). Chill 6-8 hours. This will store for up to 5 days if kept chilled.

Submitter:

Description: 
A light refreshing cold soup that's perfect for summer. I take this recipe to summer get togethers and it's always a hit!
