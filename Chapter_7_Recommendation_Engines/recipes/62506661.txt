Title:
Seven Layer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon
2 hearts Romaine lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
1 red onion, chopped
1 (10 ounce) package frozen green peas, thawed
10 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 1/4 cups mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon white vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
3 boiled eggs, chopped
2/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Crumble and set aside.
In a large flat bowl, place the chopped lettuce and top with a layer of onion, peas, shredded cheese, mushrooms and bacon.
Prepare the dressing by whisking together the mayonnaise, sour cream, vinegar, sugar, boiled eggs, and Parmesan cheese. Drizzle over salad and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Brent BeSaw
Description: 
A glass salad bowl is a must, otherwise your diner guests will miss all the pretty layers. Those layers begin with chopped Romaine lettuce and go on from there  - onion, peas, shredded cheese, bacon, mushrooms, and finally a creamy parmesan and mayonnaise dressing.Modified from: Seven Layer Salad
