Title:
Gluten Free Apple Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white rice flour
1/4 cup sweet rice flour
1/2 cup warm milk
1/3 cup tapioca flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon xanthan gum
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
2 eggs
2/3 cups white sugar
1/3 cup mayonnaise (not Miracle Whip)
1/2 cup finely chopped Granny Smith apples
1 teaspoon gluten-free vanilla extract
Caramel Frosting:
1/2 stick butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup evaporated milk
1/2 teaspoon gluten-free vanilla

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 350° F.
Grease and rice flour a 9X9 inch cake pan.
Mix the rice flours with the warm milk and set aside.
Mix the tapioca flour, salt, xanthan gum, baking soda, baking powder and spices and together and set aside.
Mix the eggs, sugar, and mayonnaise with mixer on medium until fluffy.
Add the dry flour/spice mixture, milk/rice flour, apples, and vanilla and mix on medium until well blended.
Spread batter into the prepared pan.
Bake at 350° F for 30 - 35 minutes. Cake is done when it springs back when lightly touched or when a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Let cool completely then frost with caramel frosting.
Frosting:
Melt butter in small saucepan and dissolve sugar in it.
Add evaporated milk and bring to a boil.
Boil for 1 minute.
Remove from heat and add vanilla.
Set the pot in a sink of ice water and mix with spoon until frosting thickens.
