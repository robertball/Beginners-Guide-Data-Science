Title:
Unstuffed Promenton Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066589.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Cups Rotini Pasta (boiled)
2 large Green Bell Peppers, sliced
1/2 Cup Celery, diced
4 Tbsp. Margarine
3 Tbsp. Flour
1 tsp Garlic Salt
1-3/4 Cup 2% Milk
3/4 Cup Swiss Cheese, shredded
1/4 Cup White Wine
2 Cans Whitefish Tuna

Directions:
1) In a large skillet over medium-high heat, stir and cook the celery and peppers in the margarine and add the garlic salt and flour.
2) Add milk while still stirring and keep stirring till it thickens. Add 1/4 cup of cheese and the wine; continue stirring the mixture till the cheese melts.
3)  Add the boiled pasta and the tuna, and mix the concoction again very well. Sprinkle remaining cheese over top. Remove skillet from heat and let sit 3 minutes to melt cheese on top and serve.

Submitter: 
@m
Description: 
This is a modified version of the Aruban recipe Promenton Yena, except with the peppers sliced and cooked into the skillet mix, rather than stuffed with the mix and baked. It also replaces the traditional baby shrimp with tuna.
