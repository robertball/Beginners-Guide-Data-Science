Title:
Zucchini Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 c. grated zucchini
2 T. grated onion
2 T. parsley
1/3 c. grated parmesean cheese
1 c. bread crumbs
1/4 t. pepper
3/4 t. salt (less if the crumbs are salted)
2 eggs, well beaten

Directions:
After grating zucchini, squeeze out as much liquid as possible. Combine zucchini with remaining ingredients minus the bread crumbs.
Shape into patties and cover with bread crumbs.
Put on greased baking sheet and brush with melted butter. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
CAN BE DONE IN A FRYING PAN ON THE STOVE.
