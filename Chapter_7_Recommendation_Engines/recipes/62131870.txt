Title:
Mie Goreng - Indonesian Fried Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles (any flavor because you're not going to use any of the packets)
1 pound of boneless skinless chicken breasts (cut into strips)
1 Teaspoon of garlic salt
1 Teaspoon of olive oil
2 Tablespoons of vegetable oil (halved)
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup sweet soy suace
1/4 cup oyster sauce
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup of shredded cabbage
1 cup of shredded carrots
1 cup of broccoli florets
1 cup of mushrooms
1/2 cup of shallots
5 cloves of garlic
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil ramen noodles for about three minutes and plunge into cold water to stop from cooking.  Drain, drizzle with 1 table spoon of vegetable oil, and set aside.
Toss chicken with olive oil, garlic salt, and pepper then sautee in wok until it is no longer pink.
Add shallots and garlic and sautee then add carrots, cabbage, broccoli, and mushrooms and sautee until veggies look done.
Add noodles, soy sauce, sweet soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
Make sure all the ingredients mix together well as you sautee.
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Fellicia R
Description: 
This tasty noodle dish is the same one my mom used to make for me when I was growing up.  It's definitely comfort food.  You can alter it with adding your favorite meats and veggies.
