Title:
Classic Banana Bread (yogurt) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 c sugar (could use brown sugar or mix)
1/4 c butter
2 large eggs
1 1/2 c mashed bananas (about 3 bananas)
1/3 c plain low-fat yogurt
1 tsp vanilla
cooking spray

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350
2. lightly spoon flour into dry measuring cups, level with a knife. combine flour, baking soda, and salt.  stir with a whisk.
3. place sugar and butter into a large bowl, bet with a hand mixer at medium speed until well blended (about a minute).
4. add eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each addition.
5. add banana, yogurt and vanilla. beat until well blended.
6. add flour mixture, beat at low speed just until moist.
7. spoon batter int an 8.5 x 4.5 loaf pan, coated with cooking spray.
8. bake at 350 for 1 hour or until clean knife insert.
9. cool 10 minutes in pan, then remove.

Submitter: 
Cyndi Martin McAlpine
Description: 
This is a classic banana bread using yogurt ... could use sour cream in place of the yogurt
