Title:
Fettuccine with Italian Sauage and Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810378.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. fettuccine pasta
1 lb. Italian-style sauages
2 Tbsp.olive oil
1 cup thinly sliced fresh mushrooms
2 cloves garlic,crushed
1/2 cup sliced black olives
1 tsp.grated lemon rind
1 Tbsp.lemon juice
2 Tbsp.chopped fresh parsley
2 cups cream

Directions:
Add pasta to large pot of boiling water,cook untill tender,drain well.
Cook sauages in dry pan over medium heat untill all browned and cooked through.Blot well with paper towel.Cut into 1"slices.
Heat oil in pan.Add mushrooms and garlic and cook till mushrooms are soft.
Stir in sauages,olives,lemon juice and rind,parsley and cream.
Simmer,stirring occasionally about 10 minutes or untill reduced to one-third.
Stir in pasta,cook stirring for 3 minutes.
