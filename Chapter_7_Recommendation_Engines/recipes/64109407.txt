Title:
Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bananas
1 1/2 cups frozen Strawberries
1/2 cup frozen Mango
1/2 cup Cranberry Juice
1/2 cup Coconut Milk
Vanilla to taste
Honey to taste
Chia Seeds (optional)

Directions:
1. Combine Bananas, Mango, and Strawberry in a blender.
2. Add Cranberry Juice, Coconut milk, Vanilla, Honey, and Chia Seeds. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Ellie Humphrys
Description: 
A smoothie created after sampling many delicious smoothies from a book. A favorite.
