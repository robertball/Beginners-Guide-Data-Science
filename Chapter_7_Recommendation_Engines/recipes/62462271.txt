Title:
Beckie's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
1-28oz. can diced tomatoes
1-28oz. can crushed tomatoes
2-3 Tbsp chili powder
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tsp salt
pepper to taste
1/8 to 1/4 cup light brown sugar, to taste
1 med. onion, chopped
1/2 green pepper, chopped (optional)
1-16oz. can light kidney beans, undrained
1-16oz. can dark kidney beans, undrained

Directions:
Brown ground beef; drain off any grease if necessary. Add rest of ingredients. Let simmer for about 30 min. Serve.

Submitter: 
Beckie Peach
Description: 
This recipe started out as a spaghetti sauce and turned into a really good chili. In fact it took 2nd place in our local chili cook-off. It's easy to throw together and ready to eat in less than an hour. Just add a hot plate of biscuits or cornbread, and a tall glass of cold milk.
