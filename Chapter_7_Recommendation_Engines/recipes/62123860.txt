Title:
Sugar Free Chai Tea Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsweetened instant tea
3/4 cup sugar free French vanilla powdered coffee creamer
3/4 cup sugar substitute (such as Splenda)
1/2 cup fat free non-dairy powdered coffee creamer
1/4 cup nonfat dry milk powder
2 tablespoons spoonable brown sugar substitute (such as Sweet N Low)
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon white pepper

Directions:
Combine tea, French vanilla creamer, Splenda, fat free coffee creamer, dry milk powder and brown sugar substitute in bowl of food processor or blender, and process 1 minute until mixture is a fine powder.
In a small bowl, combine ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, cloves, cardamom, salt and white pepper.  Transfer spices to bowl of a coffee grinder, and process grinder for 30 seconds until spices are ground to a fine powder.
Pour spices into tea mixture in food processor, and process an additional 30 seconds until well mixed.
Store in airtight container up to 6 months.
To serve:  Stir 3 tablespoons Chai tea mix into 6 ounces hot water or fat free milk.  Stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
LECHEFRENE
Description: 
I love Chai, but not the calories that come with the full sugar mixes or coffee stand Chais, so I've created this low fat, sugar free version.  For an even creamier Chai, mix this with fat free milk, heated, and pour all into a blender, then blend for 30 seconds.  To die for!
