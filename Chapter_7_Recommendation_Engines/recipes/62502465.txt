Title:
Baked Pumpkin French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf bread, diced (approx. 10 cups)
6 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup pumpkin
2t vanilla
1/2 cup brown sugar
2t cinnamon
1/2t nutmeg
1/2t ground cloves

Directions:
Grease 9x13inch baking dish. Add diced bread.
In a large bowl, beat together eggs, milk, pumpkin, vanilla, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves.
Pour over diced bread, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
Bake in 350 degree preheated oven, uncovered, for 45 minutes to one hour.
