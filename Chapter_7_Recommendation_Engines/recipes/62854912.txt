Title:
Eclair Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pkg instant french vanilla pudding
4 1/2 cups milk
16oz Cool Whip
1 box graham crackers
Frosting:
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
3 tbsp hot water
3 tbsp cocoa powder
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix pudding and milk together. Fold in Cool Whip.
In a 9x13 pan, place a single layer of graham crackers, then 1/2 of the pudding mixture. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of graham crackers. Set aside.
In a small bowl, mix together the butter, cocoa powder, hot water, and vanilla. Slowly whisk in the powdered sugar.
Pour frosting over top of the crackers and refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight.
