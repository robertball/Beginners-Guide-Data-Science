Title:
Strawberry Avocado Farro Salad with Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
vinaigrette
1/4 cup oil sub or water
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
Zest of 1 lime
2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
2 teaspoons sugar, or more to taste
Salad:
1 cup farro (preferably 2 cups cooked, chilled)
1 avocado, halved, seeded, peeled and diced
1/2 cup corn kernels
1/2 cup strawberries, quartered
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves
2 tablespoons pine nuts

Directions:
To make the vinaigrette, whisk together oil sub, apple cider vinegar, lime zest and juice, and sugar in a small bowl; set aside.
In a medium saucepan, cook farro according to package instructions. (Preferably early enough to chill)
In a large bowl, combine farro, avocado, corn, strawberries, cilantro and pine nuts. Stir in lime vinaigrette.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
With a refreshing lime vinaigrette and fresh, summer produce, this makes for a perfect summer salad meal
