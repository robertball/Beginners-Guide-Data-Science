Title:
Sweet Potato Peanut Butter Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3208264.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large sweet potato, pealed & sliced into 1/4 inch chunks
3 tbsp nut butter
1 15 oz can chickpeas, rinsed & drained
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/8 cup lime juice
1/4 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Place sweet potatoes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
Bake 40 minutes until softened
After the sweet potatoes have cooled, place all ingredients in a food processor or blender
Blend until smooth and creamy
Remove from blender & enjoy immediately

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Sweet Potato Hummus made with peanut butter and chickpeas and sprinkled with paprika for a smoky flavor. This dip is great for spreading, dipping or to eat out of the jar. A delightful dip that makes health snacking easy!
