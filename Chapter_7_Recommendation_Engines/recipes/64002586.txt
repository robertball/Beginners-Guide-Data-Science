Title:
Summer Heat Wave Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110871.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lbs Lean ground beef
2 tsp. Salt
2 Tbsp. Canola oil
1 Sweet red pepper, diced
2 Serrano peppers, chopped finely
1 Onion, chopped finely
1 14 oz can Kidney beans, drained
1 28 oz can Whole tomatoes
1 14 oz can Cut green beans, drained
2 tsp. Salt
2 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 tsp. Cayenne powder
1/4 C.  Oat bran

Directions:
On a large cutting board chop and dice sweet red pepper, carefully removing seeds and discard them. When cutting serrano peppers wear gloves while handling them and take the inside of the pepper out carefully and discard the seeds from cutting area.
Chop onion and dice finely with large chef's knife. Set peppers and onion aside in a small bowl
In a large Dutch oven brown ground beef in canola oil until meat turns a light brown. Add salt, chopped onion and peppers to beef mixture and cook over medium heat until peppers and onion are tender
Add canned tomatoes, kidney beans and green beans to beef mixture and mix all ingredients together well.
Bring mixture to a boil and then cook for a half hour on low heat and add ginger and cayenne, continue cooking and add oat bran to thicken mixture. Simmer slowly and season to suit preferences

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A spicy hot chilli that will heat you up on a cold evening with peppers and bran added to make a nice thick tomato sauce that you will enjoy with a thick slice of bread for dinner.
