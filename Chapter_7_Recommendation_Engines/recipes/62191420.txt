Title:
Orange Cranberry Whole Grain Breakfast Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/297957.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup canola oil
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 egg
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup chopped dates (optional)
zest of one medium orange
1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup rolled oats
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In mixing bowl, beat together canola oil, sugars, vanilla, egg and orange zest until well combined. Stir in cranberries and dates. Set aside.
In separate bowl, whisk together flour, oats, baking soda and salt. Add to wet ingredients and mix until combined. Drop by tablespoonful onto insulated cookie sheet.
*Bake 10 -12 minutes until just starting to brown.
