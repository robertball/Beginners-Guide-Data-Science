Title:
Rhubarb and Buckwheat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp coconut oil
1/3 cup raw buckwheat
1/2 cup rhubarb, chopped
big pinch salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
2/3 cup water
2 Tbsp golden flax seed, ground
1 tsp chia seeds
1 tsp honey (or pure maple syrup)

Directions:
In a small pot, heat coconut oil over medium heat and saute buckwheat and rhubarb with a pinch of salt for 1 minute.  Add cinnamon and saute for an additional 30 seconds or until fragrant.
Add water and bring to a boil over high heat.  Reduce heat and cover.
Simmer for 15-20 minutes or until most of the water is absorbed.
Remove from stove and add flax, chia seeds and honey.  Stir to combine.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
I love rhubarb and the tartness it adds to dishes.  I usually add it to oatmeal but had a nice surprise when I added it to my buckwheat.  This is also a dish that my kids really enjoy!
