Title:
Mahed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs potatoes
2 tsp salt
2 tubs onion and chive cream cheese
6 tbsp butter, divided
1 1/2 cups whipping cream
1/2 tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Cook potatoes with 1 tsp of salt. Drain and mash. Add cream cheese, 4 tbsp butter, 1 tsp salt and garlic salt.  Mix well. Gradually add whipping cream. Put in casserole dish, top with remaining butter. Bake at 350°for 30 minutes, increase temperature to 375°and bake additional 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Barb0607
Description: 
This rich mashed potato casserole is a requisite at Easter dinner.  It pairs wonderfully with ham.
