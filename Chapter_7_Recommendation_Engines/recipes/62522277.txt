Title:
Caribbean Sweet Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRESSING: 1 clove garlic, minced
1/4c. oil
3 T. rice or cider vinegar
1 T. sugar
1/4 tsp. prepared mustard
SALAD:
1 medium yam, peeled and cubed (2 c.)
1 medium white sweet potato, peeled and cubed (2 c.)
2 large purple sweet potatoes (2 c.)
1 c. corn kernels
1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 c. each, red and yellow bell peppers, sliced
1/4 c. sweet onion, thinly sliced
1/4 c. chopped cilantro

Directions:
Whisk together all dressing ingredients and set aside.
Cook all potatoes in boiling, salted water for 5-7 minutes or until tender but not soft.  Drain and rinse under cold water.  Transfer to large bowl.
Toss potatoes with corn, beans,bell peppers, onion, and cilantro.  Pour dressing over and toss to coat.
