Title:
Apple& Pear Crisp with Homemade Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Apple and Pear Crisp:
5 cups peeled& sliced apples(i use golden delicious)
1 cup Bartlett Pears peeled and sliced into Match stick slices
1/4 cup dried cranberries(optional)
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp.lemon juice
1 tsp.water
1 cup gluten free rolled oats
3/4 cup potatoe or rice flour
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup Earth Balance lactose, gluten, soy free spread

Directions:
For the Crisp:
Toss together the apples, pears, cranberries(if using) cinnamon,lemon juice and water. Place in a 8 inch square pan.
Combine the following in a medium size bowl:
1 cup rolled oats
3/4 cup potatoe or rice flour
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup Earth Balance spread.
Sprinkle on top of apples and pears
Bake at 375 degrees for 25-35 min.
Homemade Caramel Sauce
1 cup brown sugar
4 tbsp. Earth Balance lactose, gluten. soy free spread
1/2 cup Vanilla Silk Cream for coffee,lactose, gluten,soy free.
1 tbsp. Vanilla
Lactose free Vanilla yogurt
pinch of salt
In a medium sauce pan over medium - low heat add brown sugar and Earth Balance spread and salt stir with a wooden spoon till it starts to melt. With a whisk, whisk in the coffee cream and whisk till thick about 2-3 minutes. Serve this in small bowls over the Apple and Pear crisp, add a dollop of yogurt on top.* see notes*
