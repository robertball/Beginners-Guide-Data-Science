Title:
MINI CORN DOG BITES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix up an 8.5oz box of corn muffin mix, divide batter into the Mini-Muffin Pan, and add hot dog slices ( about 1-2 " long so they stick out of the batter}. Bake for 10-12 minutes at 375°. Enjoy this fun way to serve corn dogs with "flair!"

Directions:
