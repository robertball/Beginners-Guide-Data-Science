Title:
Feta Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 cup cream cheese
2 TBSP minced fresh parsley
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp minced fresh marjoram or 1/2 tsp dried
1/2 tsp minced fresh thyme or 1/4 tsp dried
Lots of fresh ground black pepper (to taste)
1 TBSP freshly squeezed lemon juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in food processor until mixed well.  Mixture will have a grainy look to it.
Serve with thinly sliced baguette, toasted.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is an amazing spread.  Great to serve as an appetizer for a party.
