Title:
Michelle's Grilled Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large zucchini
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon italian seaasoning
1 tablespoon grated cheese
dash of salt and pepper

Directions:
Cut zucchini into 1/2 inch slices
Combine all ingredients into a plastic bag
Add sliced zucchini to bag and shake to coat all pieces.
Place zucchini on grill for about 5 minutes each side or until zucchini is soft and has grill marks on each side.

Submitter: 
MICHMEIG
Description: 
This is a quick, thrown together mix to coat zucchini before grilling it.  I usually don't measure this recipe, but I have posted the recipe with actual measurements.
