Title:
Maple Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cups milk
1/2 cup real maple syrup
1 egg, beaten
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 cups bran flakes cereal
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 12 cup muffin pan.
In a medium bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, and salt.
In a separate medium bowl, mix milk, maple syrup, egg, and vegetable oil. Mix in the bran cereal. Thoroughly stir in the flour mixture. Transfer to the prepared muffin pan.
Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick in the center of a muffin comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Bruce Stone
Description: 
These moist bran muffins are sweetened with maple syrup and filled with raisins.Modified from: Molasses Bran Muffins
