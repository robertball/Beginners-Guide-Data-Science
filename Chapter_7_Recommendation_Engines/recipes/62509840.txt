Title:
Scallops wrapped in Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506056.jpg
Ingredients:
10 large scallops
10 slices bacon
1/4 cup horseradish
fresh ground black pepper
salt

Directions:
Cut scallops in half so you have 20 and generously sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cut bacon slices in half, so you have 20. Place 1/2 tsp horseradish on each bacon piece, top with seasoned scallop. Wrap bacon around and secure with toothpick. Preheat broiler to 500', broil for 10 minutes, turning once.

Submitter: 
Annmarie Akeret Reilly
Description: 
I love fresh scallops, just off the boat. The addition of Horseradish on these scallops makes all the difference.
