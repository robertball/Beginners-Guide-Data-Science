Title:
Whole Grilled Trout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  whole trout (cleaned)
½ lemon, thinly sliced
½ sweet onion, thinly sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 sprigs fresh rosemary
2 sprigs fresh thyme
Olive oil
Salt (coarse sea or Kosher) & pepper

Directions:
Rinse fish well with cold water, and pat dry.
Liberally rub outside of fish with olive oil and  salt.
Season inside cavity of fish with salt & pepper.
Stuff minced garlic, onion slices, rosemary sprigs, thyme sprigs, and lemon slices in cavity.
Preheat grill on high, turn down temp to low and place trout directly on grill.
Grill for 6-7 minutes on each side, carefully flipping once.

Submitter: 
Trina Cosgrave
Description: 
Whole trout stuffed with herbs and flavorings, then grilled directly on grates, producing a flavorful, flaky, tender fish, with tasty crispy skin.
