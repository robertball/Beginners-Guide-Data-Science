Title:
Turkey Sloppy Joe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tbsp oil
2 cups chopped onion
3 lbs. ground turkey
2 cups barbecue sauce
1 cup french classic yellow mustard
1/2 cup French clssic Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot
saute onion 3 min til tender
add turkey,cook til done and drain
stir in others and simmer 5 min.
