Title:
Nutella Ice Cream sandwhich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box puff pastry
Nutella
Instant chocolate pudding/pie filling
Vanilla Ice Cream

Directions:
Defrost pastry.
Heat oven to 400F.
Make pudding according to directions in box and refrigerate. Unfold pastry and cut across on bended lines. There should be 3 squares per strip. Total amount adds up to 18! bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
Cut across to make it hallow.
After cooled, Spread a thin layer of nutella on the bottom of the inside.
Spoon 3 tablespoons of pudding into each square.
Add a spoon of ice cream to each puff and freeze for 25-30 minutes!
