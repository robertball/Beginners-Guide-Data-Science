Title:
Honey Parmesan Chicken Dump Bag Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts
3/4 cup of Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup of honey
1/4 cup of soy sauce
2 T of dried Basil
2 T of minced garlic
2 T of olive oil

Directions:
Another simple one. In goes the seasonings, etc., in goes the chicken, marinate and freeze!
Cook on low 4-6 hours in the crockpot.
