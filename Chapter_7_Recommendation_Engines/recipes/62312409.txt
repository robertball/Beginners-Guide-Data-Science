Schneckennudeln Recipe

Yeast

Directions:
500 gm mehl
Yeast
2 small spoons sugar
small bowl of water to cover yeast
make pit in mehl, put water mixture in middle, let sit.
Warm 250 mL milk
Add 60 g sugar
80 g butter
One egg, everything at room temp
“preise” salt, maybe half a small spoon
Add extra flour as needed. Knead until nothing sticks to bowl, bread dough consistency
Let sit
Split into 2 batches. Roll into rectangle. Fill with mohn, raisins, and almond slivers, roll up. Cut. Place on cookie sheet. Let sit. Brush with egg yolk.
Bake at 200 deg C, 20-30 min.
