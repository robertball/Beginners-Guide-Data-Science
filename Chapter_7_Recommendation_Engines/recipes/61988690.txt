Title:
Crockpot Round Steak with Rich Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- to 2 1/2 pounds round steak
1(1 1/2 oz.)package onion soup mix
1/4 cup water
1(10 1/2 oz.)can cream of mushroom
soup

Directions:
Cut steak into 6 serving pieces; place in crockpot. Add onion soup mix, water, and mushroom soup. Cover and cook on Low for 6-8 hours.
