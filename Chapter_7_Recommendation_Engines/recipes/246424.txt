Title:
Spinach Power Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3397334.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large cucumber, chopped
2 apples, cored and chopped
3 ribs celery, chopped
1/2 cup cold water, or more as needed
2 cups fresh spinach
1 bunch fresh parsley
1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
1 lime, juiced
1/2 lemon, juiced

Directions:
Place cucumber, apples, celery, water, spinach, parsley, ginger, lime juice, and lemon juice in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Blue Cat
Description: 
The ginger, lime, and lemon add flavor. Apples add sweetness. Surprisingly delicious! Adjust the amount of water to the consistency you like. Make a thicker drink to replace breakfast.
