Title:
Greek Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 cups hot water
2 pounds fresh green beans, trimmed
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried mint
1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
1 pinch ground cinnamon
1 pinch white sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute onion and celery until golden. Stir in tomato sauce and water, green beans. Season with parsley, mint, dill, cinnamon, sugar, salt and pepper. Continue cooking until beans are tender, about 30 min.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This recipe is great made with either fresh or frozen green beans. If you use frozen use the French style beans. This is Greek stew that my mom always made while I was growing up and has now been handed down to my children. Enjoy!Modified from: Lamb Stew with Green Beans
