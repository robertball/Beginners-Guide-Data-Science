Title:
Aunt Clair's Party Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(1) 8 ounce cream cheese, softened
(1) can cream of celery soup
(3/4) stick pepperoni, diced into little triangles
(1) package party rye

Directions:
Blend ingredients, chill for at least one hour. Top each rye slice with a tablespoon or so of mixture, place on a cookie sheet in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 20 minutes or untill slightly golden and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Gweb8
Description: 
An incredible, easy hor d'ovre that gets rave reviews. Kind of tastes like a ruben sandwich, but the ingredients are a surprise.
