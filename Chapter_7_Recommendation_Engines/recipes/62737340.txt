Title:
Fried Banana Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/627554.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
4 very ripe bananas
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tbsp vanilla
powdered sugar for dusting
oil for frying

Directions:
Combine flour, salt and baking powder in bowl. In another bowl mash bananas with sugar, salt, baking powder and ground cinnamon. Add flour mix to bananas and mix.
Heat 2 inches of oil in pot. Drop by the tablespoon into oil, turn. Done when medium golden brown. Drain on paper towels. Dust with powdered sugar. Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
An easy delicious banana fritter recipe. Drop by them by the tablespoon to form the balls.
