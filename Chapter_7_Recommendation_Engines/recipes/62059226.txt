Title:
Nacho dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 package of cream cheese.
3/4 can of hormel chili (no beans)
1 lb ground beef
1 small can of sliced black olives
cheddar or taco shredded cheese (enough to cover plate)
Canned sliced jalapenos. (optional-to cover plate).
Sour cream on the side.
1 C. water
1 package of taco seasoning
1 bag of tortilla chips.

Directions:
1. Fry ground beef in frying pan until meat is browned. Drain oil and add taco seasoning.(follow directions on package.
2. On seperate plate, spread a layer of cream cheese on the plate. Spread a layer of chili over the cream cheese.
3. Pour seasoned meat on top of chili.
Pour shredded cheese over meat and microwave for 2-3 minutes until cheese is all melted.
4. Add your toppings: jalapenos, olives, and sour cream.
5. Plave your chips on the side and dig in!

Submitter: 
McGee4782
Description: 
This recipe can be used as a dinner or as a snack. This recipe fills up one dinner plate and can yield 3-4 servings for dinner.
