Title:
Troy's Favorite Southwestern Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 ts salt
1/2 ts pepper
1 1/2 ts cumin
1 ts chili powder
2 jalapenos, seeded and finely chopped
2 TB finely chopped onion
2 cloves on garlic, minced
2 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups shredded colby jack cheese
6 medium yukon gold potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
4 slices Bacon, cooked and crumbled (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a greased 9x13 casserole dish, layer potatoes.
In a sauce pan over medium heat, melt butter.  Add onion, garlic, and jalapenos.  Saute until slightly softened.
Add flour, and stir until smooth.
Season with salt, pepper, cumin, and chili powder.  Cook for 1 minute.
Add milk, stirring until thickened.
Turn off heat, and gradually add cheese.  Making figure 8 motions with spoon stir until cheese is melted.
Pour sauce over potatoes.  Cook, uncovered, for 1 hour to 1 1/2 hours.  Top with bacon if desired.

Submitter: 
I Love Troy
Description: 
Scalloped potatoes with a spicy twist.  Jalepenos, cumin, and chili powder make this dish unique.
