Title:
Skillet rosemary-lemon chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken thighs, skin on
1 lb red baby red potatoes, halved
1 onion chopped onion
2 tbsp fresh rosemary
1 tsp zest of lemon
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp parsley
4 tbsp olive oil
3 cloves of garlic

Directions:
Place the rosemary, lemon zest, chopped garlic, onion powder, parsley, salt, and pepper in a mini-chopper and pulse until well ground. Then add 2 tbsp olive oil and pulse again.
Place the chicken thighs and potatoes in a (non-reactive) bowl and rub all over with the rosemary lemon herb rub. Arrange the thighs skin-side down and sprinkle with the lemon juice. Let marinate for an hour at room temperature.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Heat 2 Tbsp olive oil in a cast iron (10 to 12-inch) on medium high to high heat. As soon as the oil is shimmery hot, take the chicken lay them skin-side down in the pan (set potatoes aside. Sear the thighs without moving them for 3-5 minutes or until nicely browned. Turn the thighs over in the pan so they are skin-side up. Remove the pan from heat.
Add potatoes, sliced onion and lemon slices evenly in pan.
Bake uncovered for about 45 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the chicken thighs reach 175Â°F.
Enjoy
