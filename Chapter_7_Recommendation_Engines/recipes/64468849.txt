Title:
Dinomite Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2456439.jpg
Ingredients:
1 qt. Vegetable Oil
1 egg
2 Tbs Vegetable Oil
1 Cup Milk
1 Cup Pancake Mix
1/2 Cup Marshmallow Cream (e.g. Fluff)
8 Graham Crackers, broken in half
1 Milk Chocolate Bar, broken in quarters
3-4 Kale Leaves, cleaned and dried.

Directions:
In a large pot or deep fryer, heat oil to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
Combine egg, oil and milk; whisk well. Add pancake mix and combine until smooth.
Spread about a tablespoon of marshmallow cream on one half of a graham cracker. Top with a piece of chocolate, add a little marshmallow cream on top of chocolate.
Spread another tablespoon of marshmallow cream on other half of graham cracker. top with a small amount of kale.
Press both halves of the graham crackers together.
Dip in batter mix until coated.
Carefully place in fryer and fry until golden brown (about 30 second). Remove from fryer and let cool. Repeat with rest of the ingredients.

Submitter: 
Team Meatfest
Description: 
A tasty concoction, perfect for feeding your Dinomite, Elopus, Wooster or what have you.  The kale adds a little zing!
