Title:
Kahlua Peppermint Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089849.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package brownie mix
1/4 cup Kahlua liqueur
1/3 cup vegetable oil
For the icing:
1/4 cup butter, softened
3 tablespoons Kahlua liqueur
1 tablespoon milk
2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup crushed peppermint candy

Directions:
Prepare brownies as directed on box mix, substituting Kahlua for the water.
Allow brownies to completely cool.
For the icing, stir together the butter, Kahlua, milk and Kahlua in a medium bowl until smooth. Spread evenly on the brownies and sprinkle with crushed peppermint.
