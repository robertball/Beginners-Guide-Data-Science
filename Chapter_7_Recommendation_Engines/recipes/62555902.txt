Title:
Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz.) whole grain pasta
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1-1/2 batch red sauce (1 lg. can tomato sauce, onion, garlic, basil, oregano and whatever leftover sauce there is in the house)
2 eggs
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
5 tablespoons butter, melted
16 oz. ricotta
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Stir in the sauce and seasoned salt; set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, Parmesan cheese and butter. Drain pasta; add to egg mixture and toss to coat.
Place half of the pasta mixture in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Top with half of the ricotta, meat sauce and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes. Uncover; bake 12-15 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
