Title:
Black and Blue Summer Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/439073.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, frozen
1 cup blueberries, fresh or frozen
1 cup blackberries, fresh or frozen
1 8 oz cup lite vanilla yogurt
1 cup skim milk
2 packets sugar-free sucralose sweetener (Splenda)

Directions:
Break banana or cut into chunks.
Place all ingredients into a blender. Blend until smooth. If using frozen berries, you may need to add a bit more milk.
Pour into glasses and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
I created this recipe after picking gallons of the blackberries that grow wild near my home. Enjoy the flavors of summer all year long by using frozen fruit in this delicious, refreshing smoothie!
