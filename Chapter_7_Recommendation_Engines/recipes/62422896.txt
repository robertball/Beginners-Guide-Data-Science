Title:
Jan's Old fashioned Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/562787.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground meatloaf mix - beef, pork, veal.
2 cups FRESH, soft bread crumbs.  You can use 4 or 5 slices of bread as well.
1 extra large egg.
1 1/2 cups whole milk.
1/4 cup minced onion
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon garlic powder or 2 cloves chopped
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon sage.
*1/2 cup ketchup for topping (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Place in a greased or foil lined 9x5x3 loaf pan.
Bake in a 350-degree oven for approximately 1 1/2 hours or until an internal temperature of 170 degrees is reached.
*optional topping of 1/2 cup ketchup may be added 10 minutes prior to finish.
Let cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
A treasured meatloaf recipe from my Great Aunt Laura!  All simple, basic ingredients resulting in a soft textured loaf.
