Title:
ERIKA’S -EASY-BUT-I’M-NOT BROWNIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks (12oz) butter
1 1/2 Cup Cocoa
4C sugar (white)
8 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
2C flour
Bag of Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Melt the butter in a large saucepan
Add cocoa and sugar
Add eggs, vanilla and salt.
Add flour and mix until smooth.
Line the 9x13inch pan with aluminum foil and spray with PAM or grease with margarine or butter - I just use PAM
Bake 350 for 40 min and then cover with foil and cook 20 more minutes or until knife inserted is clean.  When cutting them wait til cool then lift them out using the foil and cut them up outside the pan
