Title:
Fried Mushrooms With Feta Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Mushrooms:
1 package of sliced mushrooms
1 cup of vegetable oil
3 whisked eggs
1 cup of bread crumbs
For the Feta Sauce:
3 oz. of feta cheese
3 Tbs. lemon juice
Enough water to make it the consistency you like

Directions:
For the Mushrooms:
Heat a small pot and put in the oil.  While it is heating, make an assembly line of mushrooms, eggs, and breadcrumbs.
Once the oil is heated, drench the mushrooms in the eggs, then coat them in breadcrumbs and place them into the oil. Repeat until all of the mushrooms are done.
Once the mushrooms have turned golden, drain the oil off of them on paper towels.
Serve warm.
For the Feta Cheese Sauce:
Mix feta cheese and lemon juice in a blender.
Add water until it is the consistency you like.
Serve it cold with the fried mushrooms.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is the recipe I used in a cooking competition that had to use mushrooms and feta cheese. I hope you enjoy it as much as the judges of the competition did!!
