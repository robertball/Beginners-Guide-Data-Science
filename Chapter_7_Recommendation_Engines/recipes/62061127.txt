Title:
chili dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz carton of sour cream
1 8oz package of cream cheese (softened)
1 small jar taco sauce
1 can chili
2 cups shredded cheese

Directions:
1. mix together sour cream and softened
cream cheese. Spread half in bottom
of microwavable bowl.
2. pour half of the taco sauce over the
sour cream/cream cheese.
3. spread half chili over the taco
sauce.
4. Sprinkle half of the cheese over the
chili.
5. repeat layers and microwave for 3-5
minutes until cheese is melted.
Serve with your favorite dipping chip.
