Title:
Jess's Pasta Milano Recipe

Directions:
Chicken
•	4 chicken breasts
•	1 teaspoon garlic powder or garlic dried flakes
•	1 teaspoon salt
•	1 teaspoon pepper
Pasta
•	36 ounces bow tie pasta
•	4 tablespoons butter with olive oil to taste
•	1 1/2 cups sun-dried tomatoes
•	1 lb mushroom, sliced or sub 2-3 cans of mushrooms well drained & dried with paper towel
Cream Sauce
•	 12 oz box of cream cheese
•	 ¼-cup of milk (Process milk & cream cheese WELL in processor)
•	1 head garlic, roasted or you can use the dried garlic flakes from your spice section
•	1/2 teaspoon pepper
•	2 teaspoons salt
•	1 cup parmesan cheese
Garnish
•	fresh parsley
•	parmesan cheese
Directions:
1.	 Wash and dry chicken breasts.
2.	 Mix garlic powder, salt, and pepper and sprinkle on chicken.
3.	 Grill chicken on the stove top in a skillet or outside on the barbecue grill, until it is done all the way through.
4.	 Set chicken aside.
5.	Place pasta in salted boiling water and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente. Drain.
6.	Meanwhile sauté the mushrooms and tomatoes in butter until soft.
7.	 Place cream cheese, milk, roasted garlic, pepper, salt, and parmesan cheese in food processor and puree.
8.	 Place cream mixture in a sauce pan and bring to a boil. Turn down heat and simmer for 15 minutes or until sauce has thickened.
9.	Mix in mushrooms and tomatoes and add to the cooked pasta.
10.	 Slice the grilled chicken and place on top of the pasta.
11.	Garnish with fresh parsley and grated parmesan cheese.
12.	 Serve with salad and garlic bread.
