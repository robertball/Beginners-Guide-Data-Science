Title:
Apple pie yum yum sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup unsalted butter
1/2 Cup granulated sugar
3 Tablespoons of all-purpose flour
1/2 Cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan.
Stir in the flour to form paste.
Add water, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and bring to a boil, reduce temperature to low and let simmer until mixture thickens.Stirring ocassionaly.

Submitter: 
Sandy Lafleur
Description: 
This makes for a very good filling for apple pie.It has to be slightly thickened. The apples will make their own juice. you don't want it too juicy.
