Title:
Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
2 cups sugar
1 stick of butter or margarine
4 Tbsp. cocoa
2 Tbsp. peanut butter
1 Tbsp. vanilla extract
3 cups quick cooking oatmeal

Directions:
Over medium heat, bring the sugar,milk,cocoa and butter to a boil.
Boil exactly two minutes.
Remove from heat and add vanilla,peanut butter, and oatmeal.
Stir till peanut butter is melted.
Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper.
Let cool. Will usually set in about 2 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
These are the easiest and tastiest cookies to make. They go over well at church potlucks.
