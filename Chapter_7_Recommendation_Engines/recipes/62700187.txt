Title:
Sausage Brunch Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground pork sausage
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough
2 cups mozzarella cheese (or 1 C mozzarella & 1 C sharp cheddar
4 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 Sauteed onion.

Directions:
Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan. Lay crescent rolls flat in the bottom of the pan.
Pre-cook crescent rolls for 10 mins at 350 while cooking sausage.
Place sausage in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Combine cooked sausage, cheese, eggs, milk, salt and pepper; pour over crescent rolls.
Bake in preheated oven for 20-30 minutes, until bubbly and cooked through.
