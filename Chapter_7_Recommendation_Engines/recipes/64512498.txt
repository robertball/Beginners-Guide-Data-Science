Title:
Caramel Peanut Butter Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 caramels, unwrapped
1-2 tbsp. water
1/4 cup plus 2 tbsp. creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup finely crushed peanuts, optional
sliced apples, unpeeled

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl, microwave caramels and water for 1 minute on High. Stir.  Microwave 1 minute more, or until smooth.
Add peanut butter and mix well.  Microwave 30 seconds on High, or until smooth.
Stir in peanuts if you wish.
Transfer to a 1 qt. slow cooker.  Turn to warm while serving.
Serve with apple slices.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Red and green apples look very festive for the holiday season!
