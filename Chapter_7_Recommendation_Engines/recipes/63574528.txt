Title:
Sukiyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb beef, thinly sliced
1 tbsp oil
2 cups water
2 tbsps sugar
3 1/2 tbsps soy sauce, or to taste
2 green onions, thinly sliced
1 cup button mushrooms, sliced
1/2 lb asparagus, cut in 1" pieces
1/2 cup rice, uncooked

Directions:
Cook rice.
Saute meat in oil until done.
Add water, sugar and soy sauce, mixing well.  Add vegetables and simmer for 10 minutes.
Serve over rice.
