Title:
Lasagna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 lb. ground beef
- 1/2 C. chopped onion
- 15oz. can tomato sauce
- 1&1/2 tsp. oregano
- 4 oz. can sliced mushrooms
- 1 tsp. garlic powder
- 1/8 tsp. seasoned salt
- 1/8 tsp. black pepper
- 8oz. cooked rotini
- 2 Cups ricotta cheese
- 2/3 Cup milk
- 10oz. mozzerella cheese
- 3/4 C. parmesan cheese

Directions:
- Brown beef.  Drain; set aside.
- Cook onion, garlic powder, mushrooms, salt and pepper until onions are soft.
- Add beef back to pan with onion mixture with heat on medium-low.
- Add tomato sauce and oregano.  Heat thoroughly.
- Mix cooked rotini, ricotta, 1/2 Cup parmesan cheese and 4oz. mozzerella.
- Layer meat mixture and ricotta mixture.  End with remaining parmesan and mozzerella on top.
- Cook 350 degrees untill heated and browned on top.

Submitter: 
Singer6
Description: 
Lasagna without the fuss of lasagna noodles. It's tasty, comforting and cheap to make ans kids tend to love it.
