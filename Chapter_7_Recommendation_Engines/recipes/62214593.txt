Title:
Spinach Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/311419.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1 Tbsp chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
8 oz. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
1 cup chunky salsa
1 cup shredded Mexican mix cheese
3 large flour tortillas

Directions:
1.	Brown hamburger in nonstick skillet over medium heat until no longer pink (10-12 minutes), stirring occasionally. Pour off fat.
2.	Add chili powder, cumin, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Stir in spinach and salsa; heat through. Remove from heat and stir in cheese.
3.	Heat tortillas over the stove.  Spoon 1/3 of the mixture into center of ea. tortilla. Fold as a burrito.
