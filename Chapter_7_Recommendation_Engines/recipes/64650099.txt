Title:
Stars and Flowers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4537272.jpg
Ingredients:
Whole wheat tortillas
Ham, thinly sliced
Cheddar cheese slices
Baby carrots
Celery
Pimento cheese spread
Mixed nuts with craisins.
Black olives.

Directions:
Use a cookie  cutter to make the stars and flowers with the tortilla and cheese.
For the sandwiches place the ham between the tortilla cutouts and the cheese cutouts on top.
Spread the pimento cheese into the celery and cut into smaller pieces.
Serve sandwiches with the carrots, celery, mixed nuts with craisins and black olives.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Stars and flowers are the hit of this bento lunch with the added plush toy. What child didn't like putting black olive on the end of their fingers? This will make the little ones happy.
