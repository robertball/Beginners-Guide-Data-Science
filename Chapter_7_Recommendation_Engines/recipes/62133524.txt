Title:
Nichole's Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C flour
1 1/4 C Splenda
1/2 t salt
1/2 t baking soda
2 1/2 t baking powder
1/2 C butter
2 ripe bananas
1/2 C egg beaters
1/2 C skim milk
1 1/2 t lemon juice
1 T vanilla extract
1 T cocoa powder
1 t imitation rum
Topping:
1/2 C sour cream
1/2 C cream cheese
1/2 C coconut
1 T splenda

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and grease a 9 x 13 pan.
Add the lemon juice to the skim milk and set to the side.
Mix together the splenda, flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Next beat in the bananas, butter, vanilla, imitation rum, and cocoa powder until well combined.
Now beat in the lemon and milk mixture along with the egg beaters.
After the batter is smooth pour it into the 9 x 13 and bake for about 40 to 45 min.
While the cake is baking mix together in a small bowl the sour cream, cream cheese, coconut, and splenda.After the cake has finished baking and has had time to cool this will make a great frosting!

Submitter: 
Angel_1988
Description: 
The extra seasonings bring this basic banana cake over the edge and the coconut frosting is a perfect fit. I came up with the idea when a plainold chocolate cake just didn't seem creative enough for a friend's B-day, so I took a basic two-egg cake recipe and made it my own!
