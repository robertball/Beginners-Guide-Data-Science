Title:
Apple Pie Breakfast Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614926.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBSP butter
1 Granny smith apple, peeled, cored and cut into half-inch dice
1 TBSP brown sugar
1/3 Cup water
1/3 Cup 100% apple juice
2/3 Cup old fashioned oats
1/2 teaspoon apple pie spice
1/4 Cup chopped pecans
1/4 Cup raisins
2 TBSP milled flax seeds

Directions:
Melt butter in a saucepan.
Add diced apple. Stir to coat apples with melted butter; add brown sugar.  Stir to dissolve brown sugar.
Cook apples a few minutes to soften to your liking.
Add the water and apple juice and bring to a boil.
Stir in oats, pie spice, flax seed, raisins and  chopped pecans.
Simmer, stirring occasionally, 5 or so minutes or until liquid is absorbed and oats are cooked through.

Description: 
The comforts of apple pie for breakfast!
A small dice of tender apples melt in your mouth in this hearty oatmeal.
