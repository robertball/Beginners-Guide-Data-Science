Title:
pumpkin and a salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pumpkin
1/4c walnut, pecan, or almonds
1/2c baby spinach
1c feta cheese
Dressing
1/4c toasted pumpkin seeds
1T mustard
3T balsamic vinegar
1/4c olive oil

Directions:
Roast pumpkin chunks, tossed with oil for 30-40 min, cool.
Mix dressing together, toss all and top with feta cheese.
