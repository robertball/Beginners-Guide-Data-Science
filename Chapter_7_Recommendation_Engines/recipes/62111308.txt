Title:
Iced Vanilla Chai Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brewed Chai Tea (room temp or cool)
1/2 cup 1% or 2% milk (cold)
1 tsp honey
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup crushed ice (set aside)

Directions:
In a blender, pour in the cool brewed chai tea, milk, honey, and vanilla.  Blend for 15 seconds.  Place ice in tall glass and pour beverage over ice.  Enjoy.

Submitter: 
~Kelly~
Description: 
I discovered my own twist on Starbucks delicious chilled tea beverage.  A much healthier version, this drink is naturally sweetened and refreshing!....Just a few ingredients in a blender and a way you go! I recommend this drink after a nice workout or outside on a hot day! :o)
