Title:
Baba's Borscht Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566276.jpg
Ingredients:
14 cups of water
4 cups of diced tomatoes
1 cup tomato juice
1 onion, chopped
5 potatoes, peeled and halved
2 tbs butter
1 medium beet, quartered
1 cup carrots, chopped
1 cup celery, chopped
4 cloves garlic
1 green pepper, chopped
1 1/2 tbs salt
9 cups cabbage, shredded
1 cup whipping cream
1 cup potatoes, chopped
1/2 cup green onions, chopped
1/2 cup fresh dill, chopped

Directions:
Cook tomatoes and juice in pot for 1 hour. Sauté onions lightly with 1 tbs butter, then add 3 cups cabbage and sauté. Add water to tomatoes and bring to boil. Add beet and halved potatoes. Cook until done. Remove beet and discard. Remove potatoes and mash with 1 tbs butter, add 1/2 cup whipping cream. Add remaining cream to water and tomatoes in pot.
Add carrots, celery, garlic, and green pepper to water mix. Cook until done, then add mashed potatoes and diced potatoes. Bring to a hard boil. Add dill and green onions, bring to a hard boil. Add sautéed onion and cabbage, bring to a hard boil. Take off stove and let stand, uncovered until half cooled.

Submitter: 
oceanfriend
Description: 
This traditional Russian borscht is a classic. It makes for a hardy vegetarian meal, and extra portions can be frozen.
