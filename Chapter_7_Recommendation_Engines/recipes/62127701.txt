Title:
Tuna Melt Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Kraft Mac & Cheese
1 6oz can of albacore tuna, drained
3 Green onions, chopped (more or less to taste)
1tsp Black pepper

Directions:
Cook the Mac&Cheese according to the box instructions - Choose the milk type and margarine vs butter based on your personal preferences, however I always used 2% milk and sweetened/salted butter.
Add drained tuna, chopped green onions and black pepper. Stir thoroughly.

Submitter: 
Emily Bailey
Description: 
This easy recipe can be made in 15mins and is a great way to add flavor to a boxed favorite
