Title:
Gluten Free Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. sorghum flour
1 c. millet flour
1/2 c. tapioca starch
4 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. xanthan gum
1 stick of butter, cold
1 c. milk
1 Tbsp. cider vinegar*
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
2. Whisk together in a large mixing bowl the flours, starch, baking powder, salt, baking soda, and xanthan gum.
3. Cut the butter into the flour mixture until there are no large pieces left.
4. Mix the milk, vinegar and beaten egg in a separate small bowl.  Add the liquid mixture to the flour and stir until the dry and liquid ingredients are combined.
5. Roll out dough on a surface dusted with white rice flour and cut with a biscuit cutter.  Place biscuits on a greased cookie sheet.
6. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
