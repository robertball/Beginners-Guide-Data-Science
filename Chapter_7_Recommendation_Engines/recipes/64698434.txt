Title:
Gourmet Avocado Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados
6 thick slices of bread
1/2 red onion, thinly sliced in long strips
6 radishes, thinly sliced
3/4 c rice vinegar
6 peppercorns
1 t minced garlic (or fresh garlic)
1/4 t thyme
1 T sugar
1/4 t salt
1/4 c sliced lettuce
4 eggs
can crab meat
sliced tomatoes
2 c boiling water
2 T Caesar salad dressing

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine rice vinegar, peppercorns, minced garlic, thyme, sugar and salt. Stir until sugar dissolves.
Add red onions and radish to boiling water to para-boil them for 30 seconds. Then drain and discard water.
Add onions and radish to vinegar mixture. Stir well and let sit 30 minutes.
Scoop out and mash avocados into small bowl.
Toast thick slice bread fairly crispy so it is easy to cut through.
Fry eggs over medium in pan sprayed with non-stick spray.
Spread avocado mix on toast. Top with pickled red onions and radish, lettuce, crab meat, tomato slices.  Top with eggs. Garnish with Caesar salad dressing.
