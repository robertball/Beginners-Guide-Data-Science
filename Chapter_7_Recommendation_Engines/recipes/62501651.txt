Title:
Fresh Parsley Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves of garlic (to taste)
1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves
1 can of chick peas
1/4 cup of chick pea juice
1/4 cup of lemon juice
1/4 cup of tahini paste
salt to taste
pita bread for dipping

Directions:
Drain chick pea juice into a measuring cup and reserve 1/4 cup.  Put parsley and 1 clove of garlic in food processor.  Chop.  Add chick peas, chick pea juice, lemon juice, salt and tahini paste.  Blend to desired consistency.  Add second clove of garlic if desired after tasting. Blend again until clove is well chopped.  Cut pita bread into small pieces and serve with hummus for dipping.
