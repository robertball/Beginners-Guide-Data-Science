Title:
Scallops, shrimp, lobster with pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz. spaghetti
1 cup olive oil
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 cloves minced garlic
1 tsp. dill seeds
1 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 lb. small scallops
1/2 lb. large shrimp chopped
8 oz. imitation lobster or real
2 tsp. dry dill leaves
1 tsp. lemon juice
black pepper to taste (start with pinch)
parmesan cheese grated
dry dill leaves

Directions:
In large pot cook spaghetti per directions
In another part heat oil and butter on medium heat.
add garlic, dill seed and red pepper flakes.  Cook for about 1 minutes stirring occasionally.  Turn heat to low and simmer for another 3 minutes.
Stain oil through a sieve and return oil to pot and return heat to medium.  Toss the bits in the sieve.
Add scallops and cook for about 1 minute.
Add shrimp and lobster and cook for about 1 minute.
Add dill flakes and lemon juice and cook for about 3 minutes.
Measure 1/2 of starch water from pasta and set to one side.  Drain pasta and return to pot.
Add the scallop mixture and toss to coat.
Turn pot to simmer and let simmer for a few minutes.  If needed add pasta water.
Top with grated parmesan and dill if you wish.
