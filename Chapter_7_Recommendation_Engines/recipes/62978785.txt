Title:
Thanksgiving cranberry mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cranberry ground in cuisinart
2 cups sugar
2 small lemon jello dissolve in
1 cup boiling water
2 cups orange juice
1tbs orange rind or zest
2 small cans pineapple crushed drained out all juice
3 tsp gelatin  into 5 tsps cold water
1 cup celery chopped fine
11/2 cup pecans chopped

Directions:
mix cranberry and sugar. Let stand overnight in refridge. mix all other ingredients and pour into a greased mold or casserole dish if you do not want to unmold it. It is pretty served in the dish too!
