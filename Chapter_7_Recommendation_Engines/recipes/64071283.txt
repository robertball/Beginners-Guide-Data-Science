Title:
Ham Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat balls:
1 lb. ground fresh pork
1 1/2 lbs. ground ham
2 cups bread crumbs
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup canned milk
Coating:
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. ground mustard
1/2 cup cider vinegar, 4 1/2%
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together first 5 ingredients and form into balls.
Mix together rest of the ingredients and pour over meat balls.
Bake 30 minutes, stir and continue baking another 30 minutes
