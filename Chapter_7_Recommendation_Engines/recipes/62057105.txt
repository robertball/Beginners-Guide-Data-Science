Title:
Tim's Turkey Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey breast
2 (14 oz.) cans chicken broth
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon lime juice
20 oz. picante sauce (medium)
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilanto
Tortilla chips, broken into small pieces

Directions:
1. Brown turkey in a skillet and transfer to in 5-quart pot.
2. Stir in broth, picante sauce, lime juice, olive oil, and cilantro.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer for about 40 minutes.
3. Transfer to serving bowls, sprinkle tortilla chips over top, and serve.

Submitter: 
JUSTINB
Description: 
An easy and delicious spicy soup made with ground turkey breast.  Control the spice level by the picante sauce you use (we like medium).  Also works well with ground pork.
