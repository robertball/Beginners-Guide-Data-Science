Title:
Luscious Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup butter
1/2 cup powdered sugar
4 tsp. grated lemon peel, divided
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup granulated sugar
3 eggs
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
sifted powdered sugar

Directions:
Prehat oven to 350.  Grease 13 x 9 baking pan; set aside.  Place flour, butter, 1/2 cup powdered sugar, 1 tsp lemon peel and salt in food processor.  Process until mixture forms coarse crumbs.
Press mixture evenly into preped pan.  Bake 18-20 minutes until golden brown.
Beat remaining 3 tsp. lemon peel, sugar, eggs and lemon juice in medium bowl with electric mixer at medium speed until well blended.  Pour mixture evenly over warm crust.  Return to oven/  Bake 18-20 minutes until center is set and edges are golden brown.  Remove pan and cool on wire rack.
Dust with sifted powdered sugar.  Cut into 2 x 1-1/2" bars.
