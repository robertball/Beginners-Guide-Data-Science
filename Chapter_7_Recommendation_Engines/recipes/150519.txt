Title:
Chocolate Almond Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978056.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Pillsbury BEST速 All Purpose Flour
2/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 sticks cold butter or margarine
1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND速 Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 large egg, lightly beaten
2 cups almonds, toasted and chopped
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1 teaspoon CRISCO速 All-Vegetable Shortening

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine flour and sugar; cut in butter until crumbly. Press on bottom of 13x9-inch baking pan. Bake 20 minutes or until lightly browned.
Melt 1 cup chocolate chips with sweetened condensed milk; remove from heat. Cool slightly. Beat in egg. Stir in almonds and extract. Spread over prepared crust.
Bake 25 minutes or until set. Cool.
Melt remaining 1/2 cup chocolate chips with shortening. Drizzle over bars. Chill 10 minutes or until set. Cut into bars. Store leftovers covered at room temperature.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND速
Description: 
Topped with light drizzle of chocolate, these delicious bars are perfect for festive cookie trays or made as everyday lunchbox treats.
