Title:
Zucchini Stuffed With Ricotta, Herbs & Lemon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448408.jpg
Ingredients:
6 small zucchini
cooking spray
1 cup loosely packed fresh basil leaves, finely chopped
1 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 cup loosely packed flat-leaf parsley leaves, finely chopped
1/4 cup grated fresh parmigiano-reggiano cheese
2 tablespoons hot water
1-2 tablespoons fresh lemon (or lime) juice
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Slice each zucchini in half lengthwise & scoop out the pulp, leaving a 1/4" shell. Discard pulp or save for another use (in pasta etc.). Place stuffed zucchini shells in a single layer in a large baking dish coated with cooking spray.
Combine remaining ingredients, stirring well.. Divide mixture evenly among shells, pressing gently. Bake for 20 minutes or until zucchini is tender.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
Delish side dish with grilled chicken, etc; or serve as an appetizer. The cheese stuffing could also be used with bell peppers, mushroom caps, baby eggplant, or tomatoes
