Title:
Big Ray's Sweet Potato Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1174101.jpg
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup pure maple syrup
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
1 lb sweet potatoes, (2 medium)
1 pkg (6.4 oz) frozen sausage breakfast links, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 cup cottage cheese
1 cup spinach, chopped
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Spray 9x13-inch glass baking dish with cooking spray; set aside.
Microwave sweet potatoes, peel and cut into 1 inch cubes.
Combine eggs, milk, syrup, salt and pepper in large bowl. Add sweet potatoes, sausage, cottage cheese, spinach and 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese; stir to combine.
Pour mixture into prepared dish. Bake 35 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup cheese; bake 5 minutes more or until cheese melts.

Description: 
Just be sure to use real maple syrup (if you must use the sweetener at all). Read the label: most supermarket syrup is maple-flavored high fructose corn syrup which is the worst thing for people with insulin issues. (well, bad for anyone, really).
