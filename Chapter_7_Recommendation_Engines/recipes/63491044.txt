Title:
Quick Noodle Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed vegetable beef soup
one soup can of water
3 cups uncooked egg noodles

Directions:
Heat can of soup and water in skillet, bring to a boil; add uncooked noodles.  Reduce heat to low, simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring as necessary with lid on until noodls are tender.
Let set a few minutes, serve.

Description: 
Can't get any quicker than this simple one can of soup & noodle recipe similar to the skillet meals made with hamburger.
