Title:
Purely Pumpkin cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/483519.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pumpkin
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
2 cups flour
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Grease cookie sheets with non-stick cooking spray.
Cream together sugar and shortening.
Add pumpkin to sugar/shortening mixture.
Slowly mix dry ingredients into pumpkin mixture.
Add vanilla and chocolate chips to batter.
Drop cookies by heaping tablespoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets.
Bake until lightly browned; about 8-10 minutes.
