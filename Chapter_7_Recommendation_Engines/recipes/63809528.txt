Title:
Caramel Apple Minis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056424.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag (11 oz.) Kraft Caramel Bits
2 tablespoons water
Apples
Bag of Candy Sticks
Nuts of Choice (optional)

Directions:
Make apple rounds with melon baller (about 6 per apple)
Insert one candy stick into each apple round
Cover flat cookie sheet with wax paper.  Very lightly rub butter across paper.
Place caramel bits in medium saucepan with water.
Cook on medium-low heat until caramel bits are completely melted, stirring constantly.
Dip apples into melted caramel until evenly coated.  Allow excess caramel to drip off.
Optional:  Roll minis in finely chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans are best)
Place on cookie sheet. Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Remove from refrigerator 15 min. before serving.

Submitter: 
markfernandez
Description: 
Why spend the time and effort making huge caramel apples that no one will finish.  Try these minis instead!  Great for entertaining and parties and for kids!
