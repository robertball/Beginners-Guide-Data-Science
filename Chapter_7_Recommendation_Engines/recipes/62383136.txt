Title:
Big Daddy's Mango-Peach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 peach, sliced
1 mango, peeled and diced
1/2 cup milk
2 scoops of Mila
1/2 cup orange juice, or as needed

Directions:
Place the peach, mango, Mila, milk, and orange juice into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
