Title:
Broil Up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of french bread
2 portions of yellow cheddar cheese
3 slices of onion
2 slices of tomatoe
several thin slices of green pepper
3 slices of bacon

Directions:
Place bread on a small cooling rack with a cookie sheet under it. Cover each slice with cheese, then onion, peppers, tomatoe and green pepper.  Cut your bacon into 1/4" slices and distribute evenly over the top.  Broil on middle rack for about 6 minutes or until your bacon is cooked to preference.

Submitter: 
Carrie Elizabeth
Description: 
A fast and easy open-faced breakfast / lunch or dinner, the Broil Up can be your own creation by adding veggies, different cheeses.  If you don't have the French bread use sliced bread, a hamburger bun or what you have on hand.
