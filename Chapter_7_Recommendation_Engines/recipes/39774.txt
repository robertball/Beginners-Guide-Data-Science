Title:
Chocolate Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985015.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11.5 ounce) package NESTLE® TOLL HOUSE ® Milk Chocolate Morsels, divided
10 caramels
3/4 cup NESTLE® CARNATION® Evaporated Milk
ice cream

Directions:
COMBINE 1 1/2 cups morsels, caramels and evaporated milk in medium heavy-duty saucepan. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until morsels and caramels are melted and mixture is smooth. Serve warm over ice cream; sprinkle with remaining morsels.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Toll House®
Description: 
A bit of evaporated milk makes this ice cream topping creamy and rich. Served warm over a big bowl of French vanilla, it's a connoisseur's delight!
