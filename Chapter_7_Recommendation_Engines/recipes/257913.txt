Title:
Simple Stromboli from HORMEL® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4504784.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup pizza sauce
1 (13 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust dough
1 (7 ounce) package HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Uncured Hard Salami
1 (9 ounce) package HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Smoked Deli Ham, cut into strips
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
Parmesan cheese (optional)
1 egg, for egg wash (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven 400 degrees F. Spray baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray.
Unroll dough; place on sprayed baking sheet. Starting at center, press out dough with hands to form a 12x8-inch rectangle. Spread sauce over dough within 1 to 2 inches of the edge.
Evenly distribute salami and ham lengthwise down center, within 1/2 inch of short sides. Top with cheese, bell pepper, onion and Italian seasoning.
Fold long sides of dough over filling; press edges to seal. Spread beaten egg wash on top of sealed dough and sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of grated Parmesan cheese. Wrap in aluminum foil.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until crust is golden brown.

Submitter: 
HORMEL NATURAL CHOICE
Description: 
This rolled sandwich on pizza dough is filled with salami, ham, sauce, mozzarella cheese, and bell pepper then baked and sliced.
