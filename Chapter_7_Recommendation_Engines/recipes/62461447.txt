Title:
Brown Diner Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472024.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of seasoned flour
1 tsp dried onion
Paprika, to taste
1 egg
1.5 cups mi1k
1 fryer chicken, cut up
24 oz. peanut oil

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients, set aside.  Beat egg and milk together.  Rinse and pat the chicken dry.  Dip chicken into dry blend, shake off excess, dip into milk/egg and then roll in dry mixture.  Place chicken into hot peanut oil and maintain temp.  Cover and cook for 7 minutes, flip and cook for another 7 minutes.

Submitter: 
Angie Brown
Description: 
this is my own little recipe for fried chicken.  With it's double batter crunch and the blend of seasonings, this chicken is delish.  Fried in cast iron with peanut oil, this is sure to be a family dinner fav.
