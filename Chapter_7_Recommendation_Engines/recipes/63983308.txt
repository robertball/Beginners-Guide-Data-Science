Title:
Harvest Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (serve on the side)
3 bags baby spinach
1 cup dried cranberries
1 red onion, thinly sliced
1 cup celery diced
1/2 cup feta cheese (served on side)
1 jar or 8 tablespoons red raspberry jam
3/4 cup tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 cup canola oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 tsp. sugar
freshly ground black pepper to taste
salt to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together the spinach, cranberries, and red onion.
In a small bowl, whisk together jam, vinegar, walnut oil, pepper, and salt. Pour over the salad just before serving, and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Anne Soie
Description: 
This spinach salad is adorned with blue cheese, avocado, and cranberries, then drizzled with a raspberry walnut vinaigrette.Modified from: Harvest Salad
