Title:
DOLE Mandarin Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982228.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package DOLEÂ® Salad Blend, any variety
3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked, shredded or chopped
1 (11 ounce) can DOLE Mandarin Oranges, drained
1/2 cup pea pods or bean sprouts (optional)
1/4 cup chopped DOLE Green Onions
1/2 cup Oriental or Oriental chicken salad dressing
1/2 cup chow mein noodles or fried wonton strips

Directions:
Combine salad blend, chicken, mandarin oranges, pea pods and green onions in large bowl.
Pour dressing over salad; toss to evenly coat. Sprinkle noodles over salad; serve.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
Salad greens, diced chicken breast, mandarin oranges, and crispy chow mein noodles, all tossed with sweet oriental dressing, makes for a fresh, light main course alternative that is ready in an instant.
