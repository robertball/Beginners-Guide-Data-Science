Title:
Simple Smoked Beef Sausage Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fully-cooked smoked beef sausage, cut crosswise into 1/2-inch thick slices
8 ounces uncooked orecchiette or medium shell pasta
1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo Sauce
1 (15.5 ounce) can Great Northern or other white beans, rinsed and drained
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
Pepper
1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
Fresh basil sprigs (optional)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
Meanwhile place beef sausage in large skillet; add just enough water to cover bottom of skillet.  Cook, uncovered, over medium heat about 7 minutes or until sausage browns slightly, stirring occasionally.  Pour off drippings.  Remove and reserve 5 or 6 slices of sausage.
Add Alfredo sauce, beans and chopped basil to skillet; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer 5 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Combine pasta and sausage mixture in serving dish, tossing lightly.  Sprinkle with pepper, as desired, and pine nuts.  Top with reserved sausage slices.  Garnish with basil sprigs, if desired.

Submitter: 
Cattlemen's Beef Board and National Cattlemen's Be
Description: 
Beef sausage is served over pasta with an herbed Alfredo sauce. This delicious entree was a 1999 National Beef Cook-Off winner created by Carolyn Popwell of Washington.
