Title:
Bucci's Pomegranate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups top shelf Vodka (e.g. Skye, Three Olives, Absolut)
2 cups Ruby Red Grapefruit juice
1 cup 100% pomegranate juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a pitcher. Shake well and chill.  When ready to pour, give a good shake again and pour into ice filled glasses of your choice!

Submitter: 
Jackie Cree
Description: 
Jim, a good friend, brought this to a party recently and we all devoured it. Very yummy and deceptively easy to go down:) Enjoy!
