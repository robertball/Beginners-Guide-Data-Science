Title:
Never Rush Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. round steak
2 tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 (8 oz) can tomatoes
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon dry oregano
1 teaspoon dry parsley
1/2 cup water
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 stalk celery, sliced
i onion chopped, 1 inch pieces
1 clove fresh garlic

Directions:
1. Coat both sides of steak with flour and pound with a meat tenderizer (meat mallet) for a few minutes on each side.
2. Heat skillet on medium low and add oil, salt and pepper.
3. Add steak and cook both sides for about two minutes each, or until browned. Remove from heat.
4. In crock pot, combine the remaining ingredients and stir.
5. Slice the steak into serving size pieces and add steak to crock pot.
6. Cook on low for 8 hours. (Can cook on high for 4 hours, but this recipe is best when not rushed.)

Submitter: 
KATHARINENOLA
Description: 
My papa was from Switerzerland. He was Swiss and Welch and loved food. Whenever we would go to visit Papa and Grandma she would always make this delicious meal for us. Served with fresh baked bread and mashed potatoes, your family will scream for seconds.
