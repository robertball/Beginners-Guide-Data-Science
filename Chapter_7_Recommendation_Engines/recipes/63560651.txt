Title:
Carrot Cake With Cream Cheese Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4531841.jpg
Ingredients:
For the cake.
2 and 1/2cups white flour.
2 cups white granulated sugar.
1 and 1/4 cups vegetable oil
1/3 cup milk
2tsp.baking powder
2tsp.cinnimon.
1tsp.baking soda.
1/4tsp.salt.
4 eggs.
4 cups grated carrots.
For the Frosting.
1-8oz.cream cheese softened.
4tbsp.butter softened.
1tsp.vanilla.
1 and 1/2 cups icing sugar.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Put all except the eggs and carrots in a large bowl.Mix together with a wooden spoon,once mixed add the eggs,one at a time,then add the carrots and mix till it comes together.
Pour the batter into a greased bunt pan,and bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes. Let the cake cool for about 10-15 min. before taking it out of the pan.
Frosting.
Cream the butter and cream cheese together,add vanilla,then add the icing sugar a little at a time  and beat until fluffy.
When the cake is cold ice it with the frosting.
