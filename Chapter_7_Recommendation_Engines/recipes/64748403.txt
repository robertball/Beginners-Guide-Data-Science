Title:
Instant Pot Cabbage and Beef Soup by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6665038.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey or beef
1/4 c onion, diced
1/4 c green pepper, diced
1/4 c celery, diced
1/4 c carrot, diced
1tsp Lowry seasoning
1tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp garlic salt
2 tsp ground pepper
2.5-3 c cabbage, chopped (about half head)
Small can (14) petite diced tomatoes
Small can (8oz) tomato sauce
3.5-4 c beef broth (or chicken)
1 tbsp Worcestershire
2 bay leaves
1/3 c ancini di Pepe pasta

Directions:
Sauté ground turkey/beef, when about halfway done add root vegetables and sauté until meat is done. Add dry seasoning while cooking.
Add cabbage, tomatoes, sauce, broth, Worcestershire and bay leaves.
Instant pot:
Lock down the lis and set for manual high pressure for 16 minutes.  Slow, controlled pressure release.  Add  pasta, and replace lid. Manual high pressure for 5 minutes. Natural pressure release when done.
Stove top:
Bring to a boil and reduce heat to simmer for 30 minutes. Add pasta and cook another 10 minutes.
6 generous servings. With turkey: approximately 213 calories, 6g fat, 21g carbs, 4g fiber, 17g protein.
