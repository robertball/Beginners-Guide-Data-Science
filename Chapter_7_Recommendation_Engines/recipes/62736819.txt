Title:
Easy Spinach and Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 links sweet Italian sausage (good quality, preferably with fennel)
2 cans (15 oz. each) chicken broth
1 soup can water
2 cans (15 oz each) cannellini beans, undrained
1 pkg. (10 oz) frozen chopped spinach, unthawed

Directions:
Remove sausage from casings. Brown over medium-high heat in olive oil.  Add chicken broth, stirring and scraping bits off bottom of the pan.  Add cannellini beans with the liquid.  Add water.  Stir  in the frozen spinach, breaking it up with a spoon as it heats up.  Bring soup to a boil.  Turn heat to low and simmer about 30-45 minutes.  Serve!

Submitter: 
McBacon
Description: 
A tasty spinach and sausage soup with cannellini beans, that is sure to warm you up any time of the year
