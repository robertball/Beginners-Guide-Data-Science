Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of jumbo marshmallows
1 cup of uncooked & unsalted popcorn
Tablespoon of olive oil
Tablespoon of butter
Raspberry preserves
Crushed up pretzels

Directions:
Pour oil into a pot and add the popcorn kernels.
Cover with lid and cook over medium heat. Watching them pop up is super fun!
Lay out the cooked popcorn on a baking sheet to let it cool off.
In the mean time, take a medium pot and add the marshmallows and butter to it.
Slowly stir together until the marshmallows are melted.
Add in the raspberry preserves and mix quickly.
Place the popcorn in large bowl and pour the marshmallow & raspberry mix over it.
Stir in the crushed up pretzels for an extra bit of crunch.
Mix well and let cool.
Enjoy!
