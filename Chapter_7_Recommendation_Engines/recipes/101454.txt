Title:
Lawry's  Chicken  Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977008.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Bertolli® Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into serving pieces
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
3/4 cup water
1/4 cup dry white wine or water
1 (1.5 ounce) package Lawry's® Original Spaghetti Sauce Spices & Seasonings
Hot cooked pasta, rice or noodles

Directions:
In 12-inch skillet, heat Olive Oil over medium-high heat and brown chicken. Remove chicken and set aside; drain, if desired.
In same skillet, bring tomatoes, water, wine and Lawry's(R) Original Spaghetti Sauce Spices & Seasonings to a boil over high heat; stir well scraping up brown bits from bottom of pan. Return chicken to skillet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered 25 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Serve over pasta and garnish, if desired, with chopped fresh parsley.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Chicken is browned in the skillet, then cooked in a seasoned tomato sauce with your choice of pasta, rice or noodles.
