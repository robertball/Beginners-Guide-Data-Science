Title:
Corn in the Kitchen Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz. block of cream cheese
1 can whole kernel corn
1/2 cup jarred jalapeños (chopped)
A bit of juice from jarred jalapeños
* you can vary the amount of peppers and juice depending on the amount of heat you want in the dip

Directions:
Heat the cream cheese on the stove over medium heat.  Once the cheese is melted, mix in corn, peppers and juice.  Serve immediately with tortilla chips and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Heidi Dugent
Description: 
I got this recipe from a great friend and every time I bring it to a party people stand in front of it!  I'm always asked for the recipe and people are shocked at how simple it is.
