Title:
Linda’s Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup crushed pretzels
3 TBS sugar
3/4 cup melted butter
1 8 oz cool whip
1 cup sugar
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
1 lg pkg straw jello
2 cup boiling water
2 10oz. Packages frozen strawberries, sliced

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients together in sm. MB.
Press into 9x13 pan. Bake at 400° for 8 min. Cool.
Mix next three ingredients and spread CAREFULLY over pretzles.
Mix jell-o and water together. Cool.
Add sliced frozen strawberries.
Pour over cool whip mixture and let set for 2-4 hours. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A jello dessert with pretzels, strawberries, and whipped cream. This is the perfect blend of sweet and salty.
