Title:
Chicken Scallopini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (flattened)
1/2 cup seasoned flour (seasoned with salt and pepper)
1/3 cup cooking oil
2 T butter
1 tsp shallot, minced
1/3 tsp garlic, minced
4 oz dry white wine
1 cup cream
squeeze of fresh lemon juice
1 tsp capers
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp fresh parsley

Directions:
Cut chicken breasts in half.  Gently pound chicken breasts to achieve uniform thin slices, approximately 1/8" thick.
Preheat saute pan over meed-hi heat.
Lightly dredge chicken in seasoned flour.
Add oil to preheated saute pan.  Carefully add chicken and saute until golden brown on both sides and cooked through (approx. 3-4 minutes on each side).
Remove from pan and reserve, keeping warm.
Drain excess oil from pan and return to heat.
Add butter, shallot, and garlic.  Saute briefly, do not brown.  Deglaze pan with white wine and reduce liquid by 1/3.
Add cream and bring to a simmer.  Reduce heat and add capers.
Add squeeze of fresh lemon and simmer until sauce thickens.  Season with salt and pepper.
Stir in parsley.
Place sauteed chicken on warm platter and top with sauce.
Serve with rice, pasta, noodles, or polenta.
