Title:
Cheese and  Garlic Biscuits My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 cup chopped chives
1/2 cup cold butter
1 cup milk
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
For topping: 2 tbsp butter, 1 clove garlic, minced, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tbsp chopped chives, 1 tsp garlic powder.

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and chopped chives.
With your fingers, work in the cold butter until mixture is crumbly and butter is in pea-sized bits.
Make a well in mixture and add the milk.  Mix VERY gently, until milk is barely absorbed, do not overmix or your biscuits will be tough and flat. The mixture will look lumpy and rough, not formed into a batter or dough.
Add the cheeses, again being careful to barely stir.
Place in 1/2 cup amounts or so on a baking sheet sprayed with cooking-spray, and bake 15 minutes in a preheated 400 degree oven.
Add topping ingredients to a small bowl and microwave until melted and stir to combine.  Brush mixture over top of biscuits and serve.  Enjoy!
