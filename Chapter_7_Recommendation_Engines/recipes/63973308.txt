Title:
Coconut Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large chicken breasts, cut into pieces
1/2 cup chicken stock
1-2 tsp crushed garlic
2 tsp Seasonal All salt
2-3 Tbs curry powder
14 oz can coconut milk
1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup carrots, finely chopped
1/2 cup peas
1/2 tsp chili powder (or more to taste)

Directions:
Cook two large chicken breasts in chicken stock, Seasonal All and garlic. Add curry, chili and coconut milk. Simmer over medium heat for 5-10 minutes. While simmering, saut√© celery, carrots and onion in butter. Add all vegetables to curry and simmer another 10 minutes. Serve over rice, garnish with basil or cilantro.
