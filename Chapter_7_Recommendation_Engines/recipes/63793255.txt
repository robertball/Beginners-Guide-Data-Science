Title:
Easy Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of spice cake mix
1 15 oz. can of pumpkin
1 1/2 tsp of pumpkin pie seasoning
1/2 cup apple sauce
1/4 cup milk
confectioners sugar (just enough to sprinkle the tops)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350
Line a muffin tin with 12 large paper liners
Add the dry cake mix, the pumpkin, pumpkin pie seasoning, apple sauce and milk to a large mixing bowl and mix for at least 1 minute or until all the dry mix is incorporated.
Scoop the batter into the muffin liners and bake for 25-30 minutes or until the tops are set.
Remove, cool, the sprinkle with powdered sugar.  YUM!
