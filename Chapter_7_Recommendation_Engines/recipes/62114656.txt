Title:
Matcha Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon matcha powder (matcha green tea powder)
1 teaspoon sugar or 1 package artificial sweetener
1 tablespoons soy milk (or milk or cream)
1 cup (8 oz. hot water)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a cup.
Stir.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cynthia
Description: 
Enjoy this yummy Japanese drink, any time of day! Oishii!  (means delicious in Japanese)
