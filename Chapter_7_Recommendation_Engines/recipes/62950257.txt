Title:
Crock Pot Chicken Cacciatore (low sodium) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, sliced
6 bone-in chicken thighs (skin removed)
2 (8 oz.) can No Salt Added Tomato paste
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1/8 tsp red pepper fakes
1/4 cup dry white wine
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Place onion slices in bottom of crock pot.
Place chicken on top.
Combine rest of ingredients in small bowl, pour over chicken.
Cook on low for 5 hours

Submitter: 
limegreendog
Description: 
This makes a nice, thick sauce and does not require a lot of work.
