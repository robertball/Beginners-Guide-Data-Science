Title:
Mandarin Pumpkins (Healthy Halloween Snack) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4562483.jpg
Ingredients:
10 mandarin or clementine oranges, peeled, pith removed
2 stalks celery

Directions:
Cut celery into small pieces and stick a piece of celery into the middle of each peeled mandarin orange.

Submitter: 
muschel
Description: 
If you are looking for a fun, healthy Halloween snack for your kids, try these mandarin pumpkins. I've brought them to my kids' kindergarten Halloween party as a treat and everyone thought they were super cute.
