Title:
Quick Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Butter
1 Tableshpoon Sugar
4 Egg Yolks
2 Cups Milk
2 Cups Flour
4 Egg Whites
4 Teaspoons Baking Powder

Directions:
Melt butter; stir in the sugar and egg yolks. Beat well. Beat in milk and sifted flour; fold in egg whites and baking powder. Bake in a greased waffle iron.
