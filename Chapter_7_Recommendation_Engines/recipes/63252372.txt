Title:
Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic
1 cup Tahini
1 1/4 cup olive oil
3 lemons
salt
2 cans garbanzo beans

Directions:
Peel garlic and mash in a food processor.
Drain canned garbanzo beans.
Add beans, tahini, 1 cup olive oil and lemon juice to food processor. Blend for 2 minutes
Add a pinch of salt and blend until creamy and smooth.
Place hummus in a bowl and add 1/4 cup olive oil on top before serving
