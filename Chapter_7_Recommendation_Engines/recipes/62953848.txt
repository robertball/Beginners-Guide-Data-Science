Title:
Vegetable Wonton Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/727399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon sesame oil
2 cups cabbage
1/4 cup onion
1 clove garlic
1/4 cup carrot
1/4 cup spinach leaves
1 medium zuccini
1 bunch green onion
Vegetable oil for frying
1 (10oz) pagack wonton wrappers
SAUCE
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 table spoon red pepper flakes

Directions:
1. In food processor shred the cabbage, onion, garlic, carrot, spinach, green onion, and zuccini.
2. Heat sesame oil in large skillet over mediumhigh heat.  Add the shredded veggies and cook and stir until soft.  Remove from heat and dump into a fine holed collendar and squeese out any excess moisture from veggies.
3. In large pan heat vegetable oil on medium high heat.
4. Place approximately 1 tablespoon of veggie mixture in the center of each wrapper.  fold wrapper in half over filling and seal edge with moisened fingers.
5. In the preheated vegetable oil, fry the wontons a few at a time, for approximately 2-3 minutes on each side.  Drain on paper towels.  Serve warm with the below wonton sauce
SAUCE:
1. Mix the sauce ingredients in a small bowl with a fork.

Submitter: 
Kimi Sturgess
Description: 
These healthy wontons are easy and amazing.  They are best when eating hot, right after frying.  If you find egg free wonton wrappers they can also be part of a vegan meal!  Serve with white rice, miso soup and the tasty dipping sauce for a complete vegan meal!
