Title:
Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Monterey Jack
2 cups cheddar grated
2 small cans chopped chilles
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
3 Tbsp flour

Directions:
Chunk cheese in a grease 9 x13
Beat eggs, add flour, &milk
Add chilles, Pour over cheese and bake 350 fir 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
