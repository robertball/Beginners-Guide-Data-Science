Title:
Oven Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups brown rice
2.25 cups water
1 Tbsp butter, cut into small cubes
1 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray an 8 x 8 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.  Place butter cubes in the dish and sprinkle first with salt and then rice.  Bring water to a boil.  Pour water over  rice and stir.  Cover tightly with foil.  Bake 1 hour.  Remove foil and fluff the rice with a fork.
