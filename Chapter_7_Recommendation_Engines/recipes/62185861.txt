Title:
Liegnitzer Bomben II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz honey
1 1/4 c sugar
9 tbsp butter
5 eggs
1 pkg lebkuchengewurz
1 tsp fresh grated lemon zest
1/2 tsp cardamom
1/2 tsp mace
1/2 cloves
2 tsp cinnamon
4 c + 1 tbsp flour
1/2 c cocoa
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 c + 1 tbsp currant
3/4 c + 1 tbsp almonds (chopped)
3/4 c + 3 tbsp citron (diced)
4 tbsp rum
schokoladenglasur

Directions:
In a saucepan slowly and carefully heat the honey, sugar, and fat; Set aside to cool.
Sift the flour baking powder cocoa and spces; set aside.
Beat eggs until foamy Stirring eggs until foamy. Combine eggs with rum and honey mixture. Add currants, finely chopped candied citron, chopped almonds. Stir in sifted mixture untill it makes a stiff dough.
Use spray oil on the mini-muffin pans and fill 3/4 full.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 60 minutes.
Remove and let cool, cover in chocolate glasur, or white chocolate glasur.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
The Bombens are from a region in poland, and are a traditional german christmas treat with other cookies.
It is a mini-muffin shape soft cake, made with honey and covered in a chocolate glaze. For a variation i also will make a white chocolate glaze.
This recipe is a translation from my mothers cookbook she brought from germany 50 years ago.
