Title:
Creamsicle pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup All-purpose flour
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup orange juice
2 tbps sugar
2 tbsp melted coconut oil or butter
1 tsp orange zest
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Add milk and orange juice to medium-sized bowl and let sit while preparing the other ingredients.
Mix all dry ingredients together in a medium or large-sized bowl.
Add egg, melted butter or oil, and vanilla to the milk and orange juice mixture, and mix well.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
Cook on a skillet on medium heat in 1/4 cup portions. Flip pancake to other side when air bubbles appear on top.
