Title:
Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126619.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Light Soy Sauce
1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Tablespoon Cornstarch
1 inch Fresh Ginger, grated
1 Tablespoon Fresh Garlic, grated
1 pinch Red Pepper Flakes
3 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast halves, sliced into thin strips
1 Tablespoon Chili Oil
1 Red Bell Pepper, julienned
1 Cup Fresh Snow Peas
1 Head Broccoli, cut into florets
1 Cup of julienned Carrots
1 Large Spanish Onion, halved then sliced
1/4 Cup Chopped Cilantro
1/2 Cup Chopped Cashews
2 Tablespoons Sesame Oil, separated
Kosher Salt and Black Pepper

Directions:
Mix together soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, garlic, cornstarch, chili oil and red pepper into sauce; coat chicken with marinade and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes.
Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
Cook and toss all of the vegetables until just tender, about 5 minutes - salt/pepper lightly.
Remove vegetables and keep warm.
Remove chicken from marinade, reserving liquid. Heat 1 tablespoon sesame oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook and stir chicken until just done, about 3 minutes per side; remove to a separate dish. Return remaining marinade to the hot skillet and reduce until thick. Return Chicken and to skillet with reduced marinade. Cook/toss together for 2-3 minutes. Serve over hot rice and top with chopped cashews and cilantro.
