Title:
Holiday Sweet Tart Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1066365.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup seedless red grapes
1/2 cup water, more if needed
1/2 cup fresh cranberries
1/2 cup ice cubes
1 small red apple, chopped

Directions:
Blend grapes, water, cranberries, ice cubes, and apple in a blender until smooth. Adjust by adding additional water a little at a time to achieve the consistency desired.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Delicious, sweet, and pleasantly tart smoothie made with fresh cranberries. Ideal to make around the autumn and winter holidays when fresh cranberries are available.
