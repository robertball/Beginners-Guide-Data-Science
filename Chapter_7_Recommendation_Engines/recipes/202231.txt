Title:
Frozen Mango Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992125.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Especial Gold
3 ounces lime margarita mix
1 cup diced and peeled mangos
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
3/8 cup crushed ice
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Rub rim of a margarita glass with half a lime and dip into sugar.
Add Cuervo Especial Gold, lime margarita mix, diced and peeled mangos, and crushed ice.
Mix in blender until smooth. Pour into the glass.
Garnish with lime wedge.
