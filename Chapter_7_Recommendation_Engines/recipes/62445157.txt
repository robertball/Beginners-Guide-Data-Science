Title:
Vanilla Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup espresso
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup half and half
1/4 cup + 1 tbs vanilla syrup (can be found on my profile page)
1 cup ice cubes or 1/2 cup crushed ice

Directions:
combine first four ingredients in a 16 oz. glass, stir. than add ice until glass is full.

Submitter:

Description:
