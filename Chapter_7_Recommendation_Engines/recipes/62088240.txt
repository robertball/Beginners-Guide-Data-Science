Title:
Shrimp Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pasta your choice, rigati, ziti or linguine
1 med onion sliced
1 bell pepper sliced
1 sm zuchinni sliced
1 sm yellow squash sliced
3 or 4 cloves of crushed fresh garlic or to your taste
1 pkg. sliced mushrooms
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. dried basil
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 can 28oz. crushed tomatoes
2 lbs. fresh shrimp peeled and deveined

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions. Rinse and drain. Keep warm.
While pasta is cooking, in a large pot saute the vegtables and garlic in a small amount of oil. Adding the mushrooms last.
Add the crushed tomatoes and remaining spices. Bring to a simmer. Add fresh shrimp and cook until they are pink and cooked through.
Pour over the pasta and toss.
Serve with parmesan cheese if desired.
