Title:
Big Ray's Santa Fe Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119927.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups rice, cooked
1 can  black beans, rinsed (15 oz.)
1 can mexican corn, drained (14 oz.)
1 medium onion, chopped
1 yellow pepper, chopped
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup, (10-1/2 oz.)
1 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup  chopped fresh cilantro
4 small  boneless skinless chicken breasts (1 lb.)
1/2 cup salsa
1 can green chilles, (4 oz.)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400°F.
Combine rice, beans, corn, peppers and onions in 13x9-inch baking dish. Mix soup, broth and cilantro; pour over rice mixture.
Mix salsa and chilies and put on top of chicken.
Bake 45 min. or until chicken is done (165°F), topping with cheese for the last 10 min.
