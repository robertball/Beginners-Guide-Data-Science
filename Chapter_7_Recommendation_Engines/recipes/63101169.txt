Title:
Rice Croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked rice
salt/pepper
1 egg, beaten
1 cup grated parmesan
1 tea. onion powder
1/2 cup dry breadcrumbs
veg. oil for frying

Directions:
Mix everything except the oil and breadcrumbs. Working w/ moistened hands, scoop 2 Tab. of the mixture into your hands and shape into a fat, flat patty. Coat w/ the bread crumbs. Heat 1/4 inch of oil over med-high heat in a large skillet. cook the croquettes until browned on both sides about 2 minutes total. Drain and serve warm.
