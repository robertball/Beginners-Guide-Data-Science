Title:
Easy Jalapeno-Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (14 oz.) unmarinated artichoke hearts
1 cup light mayonnaise
1 cup shredded parmesean cheese
1 cup cream cheese
1/2 cup nacho-style jalapenos (with some juices)

Directions:
Chop artichoke hearts into bite-sized pieces.  In a microwave safe bowl, mix together mayonnaise, parmesean and cream cheese.  Add remaining ingredieants to cheese mix.  Microwave on high 3 to 5 mins, stirring at 2 mins.  Serve with tortilla chips, pita bread, or sourdough bread.

Submitter: 
Anne Van der Staay
Description: 
My mom made this recipie up for a party and everyone loved it.  After watching her make it, I figured out the recipe and now I make it for every party.  You can add more or less jalapenos depending on your tastes.  Enjoy!
