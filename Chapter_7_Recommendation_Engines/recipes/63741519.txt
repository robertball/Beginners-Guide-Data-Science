Title:
Graham's Of Subiaco BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 24 oz Bottle Hunt's Catsup ( DO NOT SUBSTITUTE)
1 Cup Sugar
1/4 Cup Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 Cup Liquid Smoke
2 Tbs Williams Chili Seasoning
1 Medium Onion Chopped
1 Chipolte Pepper in Adobo Sauce Chopped Fine.
2 Cups Water
1 ts. Black Pepper
1 Cup Red Wine

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large pot with lid on. Slowly cook until onions are almost dissolved, remove lid and cook low until sauce is rich and thick, about 5 hours total.
