Egg Replacers Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 tablespoons unsweetened applesauce (or other fruit puree) + 1 teaspoon baking powder OR
1 tablespoon flax meal or salba seed + 3 tablespoons hot water. (Let stand, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes or until thick. Use without straining.) OR
Egg Replacer, according to package directions OR
4 tablespoons pureed silken tofu + 1 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Replacing more than two eggs will change the integrity of a recipe.
For recipes that call for a lot of eggs, like a quiche, use pureed silken tofu.
Because egg substitutions add moisture, you may have to increase baking times slightly.
