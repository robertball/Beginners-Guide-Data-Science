Title:
Quick Lemon Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5151667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box lemon gelatin
16 oz. Cream Cheese
8 tbls sugar
lemon zest from 2 large lemon
2 packages graham crackers
1/4 lbs butter

Directions:
Prepare the gelatin with only half the water required. ( 1 cup hot water ) Do not chill. Set aside.
Crush the graham crackers into fine crumbs.
Melt the butter in a pyrex measuring cup ( microwave )
Add sugar to the melted butter and mix well.
Add crumbs to the melted butter and mix well.
Place crumbs into pie pan and shape crust.
Stir lemon zest into warm gelatin.
Add cream cheese to warm gelatin and mix till lumps are gone.
Add cream cheese mixture to pie pan.
Chill for at least 2 hours.
