Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 tsp salt
4 medium sized potatoes, peeled and cut into eigths
3 yellow onions chopped
3 fresh leeks chopped (or use one additional onion if leeks are unavailable).
1/2 tsp chervil
2 Tbsp fresh parsley minced
1/2 cup whipping cream
1 Tbsp butter

Directions:
Bring chicken broth to a simmer and add salt.
Add potatoes, onion and leeks to the broth.
Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Mash potatoes into the broth using a potato masher.
Add chervil, parsley and whipping cream.
Simmer 5 minutes.
Add butter and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This soup can be used as a base for many other soups.  As an example, left over cooked broccoli, asparagus or carrots with a cup of the broth and a nice creamy vegetable soup will be the result.
