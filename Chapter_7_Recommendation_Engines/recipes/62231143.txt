Title:
Cranberry-Orange Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz fresh cranberries
1 navel orange
2/3 cup granulated sugar

Directions:
Using the chopper blade in the food processor, process whole orange (including peel) until finely chopped. Add cranberries and continue processing until finely chopped. Add sugar (more or less to taste) and continue processing until all ingredients are well blended.

Submitter: 
DixieGrits
Description: 
Delicious with roast turkey, this recipe is super-simple using a food processor.
