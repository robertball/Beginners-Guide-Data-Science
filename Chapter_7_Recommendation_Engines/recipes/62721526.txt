Title:
greek yogurt with quinoa crunch and berries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Quinoa Crunch:
1 Cup quinoa
1/4 Cup flaxseed
1 Tbsp honey
1 Tbsp olive oil
For the greek yogurt:
1/2 cup plain non fat greek yogurt
2 tbsp quinoa crunch
1/2 cup fresh blueberries,raspberries or blackberries or a mixtures.
1/2 tsp honey
1/2 cup non fat plain greek yogurt

Directions:
Place the quinoa in a mesher and rinse until water is clear. Drain quinoa and shake off as much water as possible. Transfer quinoa to a mixing bowl. add flaxseed, honey and olive oil. Mix well. Spread the mixture in a thin layer on a baking sheet. Bake until lightly browned at 375, 10-15 minutes. Transfer the crunch to a large plate to cool.
Place yogurt in a small serving bowl. Top with quinoa crunch and the berries. Drizzle with honey.
