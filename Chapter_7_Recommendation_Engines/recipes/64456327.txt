Title:
Shish-Kebab with marinade and barbecue sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Beef cubes
Onions
Green or Red Peppers
Mushrooms
Tomatoes
Marinade:
3 tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup red wine or sherry
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp basil
1/4 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp salt
Sauce:
3 tbsp barbecue sauce
1/3 cup red wine or sherry
Marinade sauce

Directions:
Mix marinade ingredients and add beef cubes.   Marinate in the refrigerator for 12-14 hours.
Put on skewers alternating meat and vegetables keeping the marinade for the sauce.
Put the marinade in a sauce pan.  Add barbecue sauce and sherry.
Mix well and bring to a boil for approximately 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
cads550
Description: 
Shish-kebab can be cooked using barbecue foil trays instead of skewers.
