Title:
Vegan Breaded Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961233.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 Veganaise
2-3 Tablespoons water
1/4-1/2 cup Bread Crumbs
1 Box of Tofu
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F.
Slice the tofu into cutlets.
In a small bowl, stir water and Veganaise until fully blended.
Pour Bread Crumbs in a small bowl. (separate from the previous!)
Oil baking dish.
Dip tofu in Veganaise mixture, then bread crumbs, coat thoroughly.
Place in baking dish.
Bake for 45 minutes
