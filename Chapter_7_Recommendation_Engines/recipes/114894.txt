Title:
Italian Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982525.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package pita chips (or tortilla chips)
1 (7 ounce) package Borden® Shred Medleys™ Pesto Parmesan Shredded Cheese, seasoned according to package directions, divided
1/2 cup prepared Alfredo sauce
1/2 cup fully cooked crumbled Italian sausage
1/4 cup sliced black olives, drained
1/4 cup sliced pepperoncini peppers, drained
1/4 cup green onions, chopped

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 400 degrees F.
ARRANGE chips in bottom of shallow baking dish.
SPRINKLE 1 cup seasoned cheese evenly over chips. Top with sauce, sausage and remaining seasoned cheese.
BAKE uncovered for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
GARNISH with olives, peppers and onions.

Submitter: 
Borden® Shred Medleys™
Description: 
Pesto shredded cheese adds an Italian twist to traditional nachos.
