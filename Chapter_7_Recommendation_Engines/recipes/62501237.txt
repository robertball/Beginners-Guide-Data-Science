Title:
Matty's Tailgate Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (29 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 (28 ounce) cans peeled and diced tomatoes
1 diced onion
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1/2 pound bacon, diced
1 pounds spicy sausage
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1/2 (16 ounce) bottle hickory smoke barbeque sauce
1/4 cup chili powder
2 (15.25 ounce) cans kidney beans, undrained
1 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate, chopped
1/4 cup Maker's Mark
1 teaspoons crazy hot sauce
1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke

Directions:
In a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat, combine tomato sauce, tomatoes, onion and Italian seasoning.
In a large skillet over medium heat, cook bacon until slightly crisp.  Drain and stir into the pot.
In the same skillet over medium heat, cook sausage until brown.  Drain and stir into the pot.
In the same skillet over medium heat, cook the beef until brown.  Drain and stir into the pot.
Stir the barbeque sauce and chili powder into the pot; taste and adjust seasonings.  Stir in the kidney beans, chocolate, Maker's Mark, hot sauce, liquid smoke and simmer until flavors are well blended.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Emily Pollock Doyle
Description:
