Title:
Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces sharp white cheddar (Cougar Gold cheese)
1 Tbsp (heaping) cornstarch
1 clove garlic
1 cup white wine
1 Tbsp kirsch brandy
French bread cut into 1" cubes

Directions:
Grate cheese and mix with cornstarch. Cut garlic clove in half. Rub garlic clove half on the inside of fondue pot. Finely cut the other garlic clove half and add to pot. Add wine and heat slowly until it begins to bubble. Add cheese mixture slowly, stirring as it melts. When mixture is smooth, add kirsch. Serve with french bread.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
This is from the WSU website, where they make and sell Cougar Gold Cheese, but any aged sharp white cheddar will work nicely.  Don't leave out the kirch brandy, it is key.
