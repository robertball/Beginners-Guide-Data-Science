Title:
Hot Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz.canned white crab meat, rinsed and drained
1 8 oz. pkg cream cheese
2 tbs lemon juice
1 tsp milk
1 tsp horseradish
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. In large bowl, mix all ingredients. Spoon into casserole dish and bake 15 minutes, until bubbly.
