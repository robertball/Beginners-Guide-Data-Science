Title:
Lemon-Pepper Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/881694.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup lemon pepper (Penzey's is the best)
2 teaspoons minced garlic
4 (4 ounce) salmon pieces

Directions:
Mix olive oil, garlic and  lemon pepper into a paste. Spread on salmon. This can marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes or on the counter for 15 minutes. Bake or grill, lemon pepper side up, about 12 minutes, or until fish flakes with a fork.  Do not overcook.
