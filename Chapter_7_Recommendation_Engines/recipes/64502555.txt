Title:
Gourmet Bacon, Onion, and Mushroom Burger. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2617679.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds hamburger
8 oz pepper jack cheese
7 strips of thick cut bacon
1 package baby Bella mushrooms
1  yellow onion
3 tablespoons red wine
1 tbsp sea salt
1 tbsp black pepper
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 package bakery style  burger buns

Directions:
Cook bacon to desired crispness, reserve 1 tbsp of bacon grease in skillet.
Chop onion,  and slice mushrooms, add to skillet  and sauté on med high heat until onions begin to Carmelite. Add 3 tablespoons of red wine to skillet reduce heat to medium, stirring regularly until wine reduces.
Make burgers into patties. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Then grill.  Cook for about 6 min per side on medium heat. Cook to taste. Add pepper jack cheese to burgers grill buns. With slotted spoon place mushroom & onion on burger with bacon.   Happy Burger. !!
