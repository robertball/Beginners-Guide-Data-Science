Title:
French Toast to Die for Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Slices of White bread
1 Tablespoon of Brown Sugar
1 Teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 Teaspoon nutmeg
4 Med eggs
1/4 cup of Half and Half
1/2 Teaspoon Grand Marnier

Directions:
In a bowl combine eggs, half and half, brown sugar, Grand Marnier, Cinnamon, and Nutmeg whisk it all together.
Dip bread 1 slice at a time into the mixture, and Place onto a lightly greased hot griddle
Flip in the order placed on the Griddle after 1.5 minutes or when it is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Darin Terwilliger
Description: 
Quick easy French toast that is to Die for :)
