Title:
Dream-Inducing Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening
1/2 c. margarine
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
2 3/4 c. flour
2 t. cream of tarter
1 t. baking soda
1/4 t. salt
2 T. sugar
2 t. cinnamon

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar together. Add eggs and beat well.
Add all remaining ingredients except sugar and cinnamon and mix well.
Shape into 1" balls and roll in cinnamon and sugar mixture.
Place 2" apart on a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 400 degrees for 8-10 minutes, OR at 350 degrees for about 12 minutes.
