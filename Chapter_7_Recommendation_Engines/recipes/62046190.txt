Title:
scratch goulash meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground chuck
1/2 cup water
2potatoes peeled and cubed
2tablespoons diced onion
3/4 cup corn kernels
1 small can green beans drained
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1tsp worcestershire sauce
1 can tomato paste (1can water)
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp basil
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup macaroni
1/8 cup parmesean
1/2 to 2/3 cup water
dash of tobasco

Directions:
brown chuck. drain. put first 12 ingredients in elec skillet. set to simmer or 275-300 degrees for hour
add cheese, last of water, tobasco, and macaroni. cook another 30 minutes.
