Title:
Crispix Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. chocolate chips
1/4 c. peanut butter
1/2 c. peanuts (optional)
6 cups crispix cereal (you can use any kind of chex cereal as well)
1 c. powdered sugar

Directions:
Melt chocolate in microwave until smooth.  Stir in peanut butter and peanuts, then stir in cereal until well coated.
Place powdered sugar in 2 gallon storage bag.  Add coated cereal and toss to coat.  Store in fridge.

Traci Caha Myers

Great for kids during the holidays!
