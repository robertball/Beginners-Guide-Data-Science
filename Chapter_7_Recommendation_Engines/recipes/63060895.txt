Title:
Thick N Creamy Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/770505.jpg
Ingredients:
2 frozen and sliced very ripe bananas
2 1/2 tbsp natural peanut butter (not salted)
1/4 cup milk of choice (I use plain/vanilla rice milk)

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Eat immediately! The amazing creamy thickness of this smoothie dissipates as the time goes by.

Submitter: 
Beyker
Description: 
A deliciously thick and creamy smoothie made with 3 simple ingredients, ready in 3 quick minutes! What`s more, this recipe is vegan and contains no added sugars.
