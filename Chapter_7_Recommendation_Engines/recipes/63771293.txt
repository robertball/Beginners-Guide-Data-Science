Title:
Ramen Miso Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of instant ramen
1 T olive oil
Sauce
1 T miso paste (preferably red)
1 t fresh ginger, grated
1 T sake (or water)
1 t rice wine vinegar
1/2 t sesame oil
Topping ideas-
mushrooms (botton, shitake, wood-ear) sliced
green onions chopped
cilantro chopped
watercress chopped
tofu cut into little cubes
sesame seeds
seaweed torn into little pieces
cooked shrimp
edamame
grated parmesan cheese
fish roe
soft boiled egg (peeled and sliced, do not add until after removing pizza from the oven)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 425*, oil a 9"-12" oven proof skillet.(cast-iron would be best)
Boil ramen in salted water for 2 min, drain and press into skillet.
Mix miso, ginger, water, vinegar, and oil together and spread onto ramen crust
Top with desired toppings (except egg)
Bake in preheated oven for 20 min.
Remove and add egg, if desired
