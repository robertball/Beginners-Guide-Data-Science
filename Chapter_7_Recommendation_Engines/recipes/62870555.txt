Title:
Santa Fe Chicken Kiev Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C butter
1/4 C onion
1 clove garlic
2 aneheim chilis
2 oz goat cheese
2 oz cream cheese
4 chicken breast, pounded thin
1 C sour cream
1 (4 oz) can green chilis

Directions:
Chop onion, garlic, and chilis and saute in butter until soft. Mix in goat and cream cheese. Roll 2-3 T of cheese mixture into the chicken breasts. Secure with wooden picks. Pour remaining mixture over chicken. Chill for 6-12 hours. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
