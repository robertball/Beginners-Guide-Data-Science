Title:
Gooey Chocolate Raisin No-Bakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
4 Tbs. cocoa
1/2 cup milk
1 stick of butter
3 cups of oats
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Mix sugar, cocoa, and milk in a pot.
Add butter and boil for 1 minutes, stirring constantly.
Stir in oats, vanilla, peanut butter, and raisins.
Pour into a pan and let cool.

Submitter: 
Kaley
Description: 
This is one of my favorite recipes and adding the raisins only makes it better! We call them gooey no bakes because we couldn't ever wait for them to cool enough to solidify before we started to dig in.
