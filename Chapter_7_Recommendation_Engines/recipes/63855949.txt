Title:
Broccoli Cheese casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
24 oz frozen broccoli florets, thawed
1/4 cup butter
4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 t garlic powder
Â½ t salt and pepper
2 cups milk
1 lb velveeta
salt and pepper to taste
1 sleeve ritz crackers
4 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
drain thawed broccoli
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat and add flour, garlic powder, salt and pepper; cook until bubbly.  gradually add milk, stirring well.  Bring to a boil and cook until thickened.    Stir in cheese and mix well.  Combine mixture with broccoli  and transfer to a greased 9 x 13 inch casserole dish.
melt additional butter and mix with crushed crackers.  Sprinkle on top of casserole
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
