Title:
Italian Love Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. chocolate cake mix
3/4 c sugar
4 eggs
1 c milk
2 pt. part-skim Ricotta cheese
1 tsp pure vanilla extract
1 pkg. instant chocolate pudding mix
1 (12 oz)tub frozen cool-whip, thawed

Directions:
Prepare cake mix according to directions on box. Pour batter into 9x13" greased baking dish. Set aside.
Combine Ricotta, sugar, vanilla, and eggs. Mix until evenly blended. Spread mixture evenly over batter.
Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 75 minutes if using a glass baking dish (90 minutes for metal pan).
Blend pudding mix and milk until thickened. Blend in Cool-Whip topping. spread over cake after cake has cooled.

Submitter: 
FoodJunkie
Description: 
I got this recipe from a co-worker years ago, after she made one for an office party. It's easy to make and one of my all-time favorites.
