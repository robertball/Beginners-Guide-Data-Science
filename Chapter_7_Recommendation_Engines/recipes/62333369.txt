Title:
Roasted Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/445243.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ears corn
5 plum tomatoes, seeded, chopped
1/2 red onion, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
2 green onions, sliced
2 T chopped cilantro
Dressing
3 T olive oil
2 T lemon juice
1 t sugar
1/2 t crushed red pepper
1/2 t salt
1/4 t black pepper

Directions:
Heat grill.  Grill corn, covered, over medium heat or coals 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown in spots, turning occasionally.  Cool; cut kernels from cobs.  Place in large bowl, along with all remaining salad ingredients.
Whisk all dressing ingredients in small bowl.  Stir into vegetables until well-mixed.  Chill until ready to serve.

Incredibly light and refreshing salad, goes great with Mexican food.
