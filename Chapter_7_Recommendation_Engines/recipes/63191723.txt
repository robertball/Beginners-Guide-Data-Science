Title:
Slow Cook Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of zucchini
8 oz Small potatoes
8 oz of turnips
1 cup of baby carros
5 cloves garlic
2 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 salt
1/2 pepper
Fresh Parsley

Directions:
Combine vegetables in a bow, drizzle with olive oil. Salt pepper.
Place in slow cooker
Cook 3 - 4 hours
Sprinkle parsley
