Title:
Flank Steak Maninate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T sesame oil
3T toasted sesame seeds
2 inches ginger, microplaned
2 L cloves garlic, microplaned
1/2 cup Braggs soy sauce
Flank steak or other tough cut of steak

Directions:
Cut steak into portion size and place in a thick plastic bag. Pound steak until it is  about 3/4 inch thick. Score in a diamond shape 3/4 of the way through.
Place all ingredients in a dish and stir. Place meat in marinate and turn to coat.
Marinate for 8 hours only. Turn occasionally.
BBQ  on a hot grill, cut side down, flip and cook until med. rare.
This cooks very quickly

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
This fairly simple marinade produces a very tender, juicy grilled steak. I  always double the recipe and put one in the freezer for another day.
