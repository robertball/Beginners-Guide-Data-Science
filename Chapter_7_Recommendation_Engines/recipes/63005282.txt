Title:
CAPE COD  COCKTAIL MEATBALLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750199.jpg
Ingredients:
SAUCE
1 - CAN OCEAN SPRAY JELLIED CRANBERRY SAUCE
1 - 12 OZ KETCHUP
2 1\2 TBSP BROWN SUGAR, PACKED
1 TBSP LEMON JUICE
MEATBALLS
2 LB HAMBURGER
1 CUP BREADCRUMBS
1\4 CUP PARSLEY
2 EGGS
2 TBSP SOY SAUCE
1\4 tsp GARLIC POWDER
2 TBSP GRATED ONION (MAKE SURE THAT IT  IS GRATED,  NOT CHOPPED...WAY BETTER FLAVOR!)

Directions:
COMBINE ALL MEATBALL INGREDIENTS AND ROLL INTO COCKTAIL SIZE MEATBALLS. BAKE 375 DEGREES FOR 25 MINUTES.
WHILE MEATBALLS ARE COOKING, COMBINE SAUCE INGREDIENTS IN A SAUCE PAN OVER MEDIUM HEAT STIRRING CONSTANTLY UNTIL SMOOTH.
POUR SAUCE OVER MEATBALLS AFTER THE FIRST 25 MINUTES OF COOKING, THEN BAKE FOR ANOTHER 25 MINUTES..  BON APPETIT!
