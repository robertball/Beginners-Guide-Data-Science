Title:
Diet Friendly Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/601269.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package fat-free cream cheese
3/4 cup IdealÂ® brand brown sugar substitute
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 tbsp fat-free milk

Directions:
Cream the cream cheese, until smooth and whipped.
Add brown sugar, a little at a time until sugar is well blended.
Then add vanilla extract & milk until the sugar has dissolved, and the mixture is smooth and blended.
