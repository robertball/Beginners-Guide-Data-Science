Title:
Ham Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
HAM LOAF
2 1/2 lbs ground ham
1/2 lb ground pork
1/2 onion, chopped
2 1/2 tbslp chopped parsley
2 eggs slightly beaten
2 c rolled oats
1 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp pepper
1 1/2 c evaporated milk
1 1/2 brown sugar
1/4 c cider vinegar
HORSERADISH SAUCE
3/4 c mayonnaise
1/4 c sour cream
5 tblsp horseradish
1 tblsp lemon juice
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix first 9 ingredients together and shape into two loaves in a 9 x 13" pan.  Bake at 350 for 30 min. Mix brown sugar and vinegar together in a saucepan and boil for 1 min.  Pour over ham loaves. Bake 30 to 45 min longer, baste frequently with brown sugar - vinegar mixture.  Spoon sauce over slices of ham loaf and serve with Hoseradish Sauce.
Horseradish Sauce
3/4 c mayonnaise
1/4 c sour cream
5 tblsp horseradish
1 tblsp lemon juice
Salt and Pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Ham Loaf served with Horseradish Sauce.

A slightly sweet ham loaf that is great for holidays, everyday dinners, and sandwiches.
