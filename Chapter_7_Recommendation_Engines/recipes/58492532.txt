Title:
Ropa Vieja in a Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 poundspot roast
3 cloves garlic
1/2 a 6 ounce can tomato paste
1/4 red wine vinegar or dry red wine
2 red bell peppers, cut into 1/2 inch strips
2 green bell peppers, cut into 1/2 inch strips
2 sweet onions, cut in wedges
1 tablespoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste

Directions:
Place  meat into slow cooker with the garlic. Stir the tomato paste into the red wine vinegar or wine until dissolved; pour into slow cooker.
Cover, and cook on Low until the beef is tender, 7 to 8 hours  Add the red and green bell peppers, and onion wedges about 1/2 way through cooking time; season with garlic powder, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper. When done shred beef into long strands using two forks.
Serve with tortillas and other mexican condiments.  Sasa, guacamole, on fresh tortillas, corn or flour.

Submitter: 
Trish Gillogly
Description: 
This is a delicious pot roast with peppers and onions. Just like at the Cuban restaurants in the malls!Modified from: Ropa Vieja in a Slow Cooker
