Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs stewing beef
2 T olive oil
1 1/2c. water
2 tsp. beef boullion
1 tsp. diced dried onion
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 can diced mushrooms
1 lrg Tsp. corn starch
1/2 tsp. parsley
1 c. sour cream
dash of salt
1 16 oz. package of pasta (gluten free if needed)

Directions:
Brown stewing beef in olive oil. Drain fat. Add 1 1/2 c. water. Bring to a boil. Turn down to simmer for 1 hour.
Prepare pasta per directions on package.
Drain meat juices saving 1 cup. Mix together remaining ingredients and add to meat with 1/2 c. of saved juices. Mix thoroughly. Add remaing 1/2 c. of juices to meat and bring to a boil. Turn down and simmer for 10 min. Mix in sour cream and heat thoroughly.
Serve over pasta and enjoy!

Submitter: 
anja
Description: 
This is a wonderful variation of the traditional beef storganoff. I looked long and hard for a gluten free version of stroganoff and in turn had to come up with one myself, this is it! Enjoy.
