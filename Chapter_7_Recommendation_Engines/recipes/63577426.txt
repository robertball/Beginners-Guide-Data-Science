Title:
Mum's Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/994848.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBSP EVOO
1 - 4 LB beef roast
3 medium carrots
4 ribs of celery
1 medium onion
4 lg cloves garlic
2 beef bullion cubes
1/2 tsp peppercorn
1 bay leaf
1/8 tsp cinnamon
2 cups vegetable stock
2 cups water

Directions:
Heat EVOO over medium-high heat and add roast, rotating about every 5 minutes until all sides are brown.
While meat browns, chop carrots, celery and onion in 1/2" pieces and place in bottom of crock pot.
Peel and crush garlic and place in crock pot.
Add bullion cubes, bay leaf and cinnamon in crock pot.
Place beef roast on top of veggies and seasonings.
Add veggie stock and water to pan roast was browned in over medium-low heat scrapping up brown bits on bottom of pan.
Add veggie stock and water to crock pot.
Turn on crock pot to high for 4-hours or until beef is fall apart tender.
