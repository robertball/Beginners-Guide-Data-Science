Title:
Danielle's Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 honey
1 cup peanut butter
1 stick butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup whole wheat flour
4 1/2 cups old fashion oat meal
2 cups add-ins (chocolate chips, almonds, walnuts, dried fruit, ect...)

Directions:
1.) Preheat oven at 350 degress.
2.) Mix together honey, vanilla, sugar, eggs, peanut butter butter.
3.) Blend in baking soda, ww flour, and oats. Stir in add-ins.
4.) Place parchment paper on a cookie sheet.
5.) Dump dough onto sheet and roll out our press firmly in.
6.) Bake for 22 minutes.
7.) Let cool completely in pan.
8.) Cut into bars.
