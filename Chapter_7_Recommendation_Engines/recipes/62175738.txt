Title:
Bruce's Vegtable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 medium onion, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1 medium carrot, thinly sliced
1 medium zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1 medium red bell pepper, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
1 (14.5 ounce) stewed tomatoes with juice
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 dashes red hot pepper sauce
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 bay leaf
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Cook garlic and onion; cook until the onion tender (about 10 minutes). Stir in carrot, zucchini, and red bell pepper. Pour in tomatoes with juice and chicken broth. Season with red pepper flakes, red hot, basil, and bay leaf, and continue simmering for 60 minutes.  Stir in the butter until melted and add 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese and let it melt just before serving.

Submitter: 
Bruce Stone
Description: 
This is an easy and enjoyable vegetable soup hearty enough to be a meal in itself.Modified from: Quick Chicken Stew
