Title:
Summer Love chicken salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 1/2 tsp seasoned salt (just kinda sprinkle to taste)
1 1/2 cups red grapes
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup sliverd almonds, toasted

Directions:
boil chicken breasts on stove, until done.
let chicken cool in refrigerator or freezer.  Once cool enough to handle cut into cubes.
Place cubed chicken in salad bowl and toss with seasoned salt.
Take your 1 1/2 cups of grapes and slice them in half, then toss into the chicken.
In small bowl mix together mayo, and cream, and pour mixture over chicken and grapes. Mix well.  Toss in the toasted almonds when ready to serve, and mix those in as well.

Submitter:

Description: 
I got this recipe from a childhood friends mother.  They always made it for dinner, lunch, whenever.  I got the recipe from her when I graduated high school and have been making it ever since.  Its so good especially with the crunchy grapes and toasted almonds.  Hope you enjoy it as much as I have.
