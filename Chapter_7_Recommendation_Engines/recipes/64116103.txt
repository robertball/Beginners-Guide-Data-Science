Title:
French's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1286278.jpg
Ingredients:
1	tablespoon olive oil
4	to 6 skinless chicken thighs
1	medium onion, peeled and thinly sliced
2	to 3 ribs celery, sliced
1	to 2 chorizo sausages, sliced
1	can  cream of chicken soup
½	cup dry white wine
2	tablespoons fresh chopped thyme, or 2 teaspoons dried thyme
1	can cannellini beans, drained
½	cup light sour cream, optional

Directions:
Heat oil in a frying pan and brown the chicken thigh cutlets evenly, turning once. Set aside.
Gently cook the onion, garlic, celery and chorizo in residue oil until tender, but not brown. Stir in Condensed Creamy Chicken Soup with wine or water and thyme.
Return browned chicken to the sauce. Cover and simmer gently for 20 minutes or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
Stir in the can Cannellini Beans and light sour cream, season if wished and heat gently.
