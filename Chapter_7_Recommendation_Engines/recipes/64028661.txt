Title:
Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of garlic
1 tsp. olive oil
2 1/2 cups canned chick peas - rinsed and drained
4 tsp. tahini
1/3 cup water
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cumin
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Wrap garlic in foil and roast until dark brown and soft - about 1 hour.
Allow garlic to cool.
Squeeze cloves of garlic into the bowl of a food processor.
Add remaining ingredients and puree until smooth and creamy - at least 3 minutes.
Scrape sides of bowl and process another 3-5 minutes.
