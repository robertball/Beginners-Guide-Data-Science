Title:
Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 apples
3/4 cup sugar
1 - 2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
3-4 tbsp. flour
2-3 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. almond extract
butter

Directions:
Peel, core and slice apples.  Set in large bowl.
Mix in sugar, spices, flour and lemon juice.
Place in a pastry lined 9 inch pie plate
sprinkle with vanilla and almond flavorings.
Dot with butter
Top with crust or basic crumble topping.(3/4 c. flour,1/2 c sugar and 1/4 c. butter mixture)
Bake at 350 for 50 min.
You may need to cover with foil for half of baking time to keep the crust from burning

spicy pie with a hint of almond flavor.
