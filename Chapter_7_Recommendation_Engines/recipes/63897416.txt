Title:
Coffee cake by Rose Hasz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour-put around outside of bowl
1 1/2 cups milk (  dissolve yeast in 1/3 cup of it- lukewarm with pinch of sugar to proof)
2 teaspoons yeast
1 egg
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 stick butter- melted and cooled
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Mix liquids with proofed yeast
Mix flour and sugar
Stir together
Add enough flour do it does not stick to bowl
Place in greased bowl-cover-let rise 45 min
Punch down- turn over and let rise again. Divide into two balls.
Roll  each out about 1/2 inch thick and place in greased cake pans and let rise again poke holes with fingers
Sprinkle on melted butter then  generously sprinkle with
Sugar and cinnamon
Bake at 325 22 minutes

Submitter: 
Grammy M B H
Description: 
This was handed down by my mother in law Rose Hasz. My husband has now perfected the recipe and turns out luscious coffee cakes
