Title:
Oven Glorified Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. butter, melted
2 lb. chicken pieces
1 can(10 3/4 oz.)cream of mushroom
soup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a 13x9x2-inch baking pan, arrange chicken pieces skin-side down. Drizzle with butter.
Bake for 20 minutes. Turn skin-side up; bake for an additional 20 minutes. In a small bowl, stir soup; spoon over chicken. Bake for 20 minutes more or until chicken is fork-tender. Stir sauce before serving.
