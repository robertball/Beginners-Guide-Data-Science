Title:
Cheesy Sausage Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups baking mix
12 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, grated
12 ounces ground sausage, seasoned or hot

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.  Form into small balls and bake at 325 for appx. 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
RHONDA35
Description: 
Great recipe for parties or as a snack!  Sausage and cheese are combined with baking mix to form a wonderful baked blend to enjoy!
