Title:
Big Ray's Pour - a - Pan Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4045017.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/8 ground pepper
2/3 milk
2 eggs, beaten
1 lb bulk Italian sausage
1 package (3-1/2 oz) sliced pepperoni
3 Tbsp onion, chopped
3 Tbsp green pepper, chopped
1 cup pizza sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Brown sausage over medium heat until no-longer pink, breaking up large chunks, drain grease and set aside.
While meat is cooking, in a medium bowl combine flour, salt, garlic and pepper; stir in milk and eggs and mix well.  Pour into a greased 13" x 9' pan.  Sprinkle sausage over crust and top with pepperoni, onion and peppers.
Bake, uncovered , at 425 for 15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near center comes out clean.  Spread with pizza sauce and sprinkle cheese on top.  Bake 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
