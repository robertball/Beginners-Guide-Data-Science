Title:
Cauliflower Mac N Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups cauliflower florets (about 1 head)
2 Tab. olive oil
1/2 tea. kosher salt
1/2 tea. pepper (divided)
2 Tab. unsalted butter
2 Tab. all purpose flour
1 1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
3/8 tea. table salt
1 cup grated sharp cheddar

Directions:
400 degrees
Toss the florets w/ the oil, kosher salt and 1/4 tea. of the pepper. Roast until fork tender and lightly browned in spots, 25-30 minutes. Remove and turn oven down to 350.
Melt the butter in a skillet over med heat. Stir in flour and cook 1 min. Gradually whisk in the milk. simmer over med. heat, stirring frequently until thickened, 2-4 min. Whisk in the cream and cook 5 min. Reduce heat to low and stir in all but 2 Tab. of the cheese, the table salt, and remaining 1/4 tea. pepper. Stir until the cheese melts, then cook, stirring often, until thickened, 8-10 minutes.
Place florets in a 8X8 baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over top. Sprinkle w/ remaining cheese. Bake 20-25 minutes until cheese is bubbling.
