Title:
Mustard BBQ Sauce South Carolina Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups yellow  mustard
8 ounces beer
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar (or to taste may need more)
1/2 cup tomato puree (or ketchup if not on hand)
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 TBS cayenne peper or to taste
1 TBS black pepper
2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Heat all ingredients in medium saucepan and mix well.  Cook until sauce begins to thicken.  Poor or serve warm.  Keeps in fridge for many moons.
NOTE:  I'm still tweaking this recipe but this is the official version.
