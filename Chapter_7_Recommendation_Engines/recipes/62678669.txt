Title:
Creme Brulee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 Egg Yolks
3/4 Cup Sugar
1 Quart Heavy Whipping cream
1 Vanilla Bean

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. In a large bowl whisk egg yolks and sugar until it becomes a pale yellow and think. It should be a ribbon consistency (Dip a spoon and drizzle. There should be a ribbon left where you drizzled)
On the Stove heat heavy whipping cream. Split vanilla bean and scrape the sides of the vanilla bean. Put the scrapings inside the heavy cream on the stove along with the split vanilla bean. Simmer until frothy.
Temper the cream mixture into the egg yolk mixture.(pour a little of the hot cream into the egg yolks. Mix it together quickly, then add a little more cream and mix, until all the cream is incorporated.)
