Title:
no bake chocolate pb crunch bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch chips
1 cup peanut butter chips
4 cups crispy rice cereal

Directions:
mix together chocolate, pb chips, and butterscotch in saucepan, melt together on medium low, watching closely and stirring often.  When melted stir rice cereal in with mixture, (in seperate bowl if needed)stir until all cereal is coated.
Pour onto a cookie sheet with high edges, spread out with spatula
Chill until hardened about 15 minutes or more
When chilled completely break into pieces.

Submitter: 
NC baking girl
Description: 
These are like Nestle crunch bars, but with  peanut butter and butterscotch as well. 
They are soo good and VERY quick and easy to make.  I've been making them for more then 10 years, always with rave reviews.
