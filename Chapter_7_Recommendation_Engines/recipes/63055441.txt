Title:
Curry Chicken with Apples & Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp veg oil
8 bnls skinless chicken thighs (2 lbs)
2 medium onions
1c chicken stock or canned chicken broth
2 medium apples (preferably Rome beauty), cut into 1" cubes
2 medium bananas, cut into 1/2" slices
2 lg garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
11/2 tbsp curry powder
Salt & ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook:  heat oil in lg nonstick skillet.  Add chicken, sauté until lightly browned (about 4 minutes).  Remove chicken and set it aside.  Add onion and sauté until softened (about 5 minutes).
Add stock; bring to boil.  Return chicken to pan and add next 6 ingredients plus 1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper.  Bring to boil, cover, simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 15 minutes.
To serve:  spoon a portion of the curry onto each plate.  Serve over rice.  Garnish with onions, bananas, raisins, chutney, tomatoes, peanuts.

Submitter:

Description: 
Curry chicken flavored with delightful addition of apple sand bananas.  Perfect dish for chilly fall weather.
