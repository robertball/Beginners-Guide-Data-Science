Title:
Chinese Barbecued Pork Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 racks of pork back ribs
1/2 tsp red food coloring
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt
3 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp liquid honey
2 Tbsp white wine or cooking wine
1/4 tsp Chinese 5 spice
1/4 cup Hoi Sin sauce
1/4 cup jellied cranberry sauce

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together using a wisk and marinate the ribs for at least an hour in the refrigerator.
Preheat your oven to 350 degrees and coat the rib side of the ribs with the marinade place ribs on a roasting rack over a pan of water not allowing the ribs to touch the water and roast for 30 minutes.  Turn the ribs over to the meaty side up and coat with the remaining marinade and roast for an additional 30 minutes.
Serve cut into strips.  Do not use the uncooked marinade.

Submitter: 
Sneakyteaky
Description: 
This is a very popular dish for Chinese food lovers here in North America.  It's not too saucy and so easy to make!  Some of the ingredients can be found in a Chinese market store.
