Title:
Mom's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 potatoes, cubed
2 onions, quartered
4 carrots, sliced
1 1/2 lb cubed beef
1 can diced tomatoes
frozen or leftover peas, beans or corn
1/3 cup uncooked rice
1/3 cup uncooked macaroni
1/2 cup ketchup
3 bay leaves
salt and pepper to taste
water as needed.

Directions:
Sear beef over medium-high heat just until browned.
In crock pot, layer ingredients in order listed and cook on low all day.
