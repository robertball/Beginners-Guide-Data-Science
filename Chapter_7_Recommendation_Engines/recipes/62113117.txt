Title:
Chicken Milano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBLS Butter
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
1/2 TBLS Basil
2 Cloves Garlic, minced
2 Cups of sliced Baby Bella Mushrooms
Deli Sliced Smoked Ham
Sliced Provalone Cheese
1 cup flour

Directions:
Trim and butterfly the chicken breasts.
Pound chicken with meat tenderizer until about 1/2 inch thick.
Start with 2 TBLS butter in skillet, add basil, minced garlic cloves.
Then add sliced baby bella mushrooms and saute on med heat for about 5 min.
Pour mushrooms in a bowl and set aside.
Return skillet to stove and add the other 2 TBLS butter.
Dredge chicken breasts in flour and put in skillet.
Cook chicken 4-5 min on first side and 3-4 min on second side.
Line cookie sheet with foil.
Put done chicken breasts on cookie sheet.
Top chicken with mushrooms, 2-3 slices of deli ham, and 2-3 slices of provalone cheese.
Put chicken in oven under broiler until cheese is melted. (1-2 min).
Good served with fettucine alfredo.

Submitter: 
Valerie Tolin
Description: 
This is my version of the Chicken Milano that is served at Johnny Corino's!
