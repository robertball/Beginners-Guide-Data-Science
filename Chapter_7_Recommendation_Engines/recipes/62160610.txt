Title:
Pasta Fagioli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef browned well
1 lg onion
1 lg garlic clove
1 lg can stewed tomatoes - mex or italian style
1 can spagetti sauce
1 can favorite beans
Favorite cheese
salt
pepper
favorite pasta - cooked

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onions and garlic salt and pepper. Drain.
Add undrained beans,tomatoes, spaghetti sauce.
Toss with pasta or pour over pasta in baking dish
Cover with favorite cheese.
Bake 350 degrees until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
Want something more hearty than the soup,and a lot faster to make?  This is great!  All the flavor and so much more filling!  Kids love it.
