Title:
Poppy Seed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 c. cugar
2 c. vegetable oil
3 c. flour
1 T. baking powder
1 c. milk (1 small can of PET and the rest regular)
1 c. poppy seed
1 t. vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix in order given until well blended.
Pour into a well greased and floured bundt pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for an hour and 20 minutes (Bkae 40 minutes on bottom shelf and 40-45 mins on middle shelf)
