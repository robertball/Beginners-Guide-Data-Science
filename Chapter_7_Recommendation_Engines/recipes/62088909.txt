Title:
Summer White Chocolate Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 small boxes White Chocolate Pudding
2 cups whole milk
4 cups heavy cream
1 box vanilla wafer cookies
1/4-1/2 cup Bailey's Irish Creme Liqueur
2 teaspoons lemon juice
3-4 bananas, sliced thin or can mix bananas and fresh strawberries or raspberries

Directions:
Prepare white chocolate pudding using 2 cups whole milk and 4 cups heavy cream, using beater. Mixture will be thick and creamy. Then add 1/4 to 1/2 cup Bailey's and mix (depending on your taste). Next,in a large bowl, layer broken vanilla wafer cookies with a layer of the white chocolate mixture, then add a layer of fruit(tossed with the lemon juice to prevent browning) and repeat layers. You may not use the whole box of vanilla wafers. Garnish with fruit on top if desired and chill covered for at least 2 hours. Makes a great potluck picnic dessert!
