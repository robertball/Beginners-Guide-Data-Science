Title:
Yultetide kisses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg whites
1/8tsp salt
1/2tsp cream of tarter
2 1/4C powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2C chopped pecans

Directions:
Beat egg whites, salt and cream of tartar until peaks stand upright. Add sugar gradually, then add vanilla extract and fold in nuts. Drop on lightly greased cookie sheets. Bake at 275 for 15-20minutes.
