Title:
Garlic Lovers' Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-4.5 pounds Pork Shoulder, skin on
1 teaspoonful fennel seeds
10 cloves fat garlic, smashed
vegetable oil
coarse salt
kitchen string

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425F.
2. Wash and dry pork, place it skin side down. Remove skin of half the garlic and press them onto the pork, roll up and tie it with kitchen string.
3. Using a very sharp knife cut slits between each string. Grease and rub salt onto the skin and the openings. Insert the remaining garlic, skin on, into the slits. Sprinkle the fennel seeds.
4. Roast for 15 minutes, reduce heat  to 370F and cook for 25 minutes. Baste with the fat on the roast pan and continue cooking for another 20 minutes.

Description: 
Crispy crackling outside, tender and juicy meat inside that is drowned in the aroma of fennel seeds and garlic that mealts in your mouth. This is the most beautiful combination for a prok roast ever! Enjoy!
