Title:
Chinese Sticky Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Shirakiku Rice
1 1/2 cups cold water
sprinkle of salt (to taste)
To double recipe, use 2 cups rice and 2 1/2 cups cold water

Directions:
In a small sauce pan, bring rice, water, and a sprinkle of salt to a boil.
Turn heat down to low and cover with a lid.  Allow to simmer 20 minutes.
Take off the heat, but keep the lid on, and allow to sit for 20 minutes.
Remove lid and serve!
