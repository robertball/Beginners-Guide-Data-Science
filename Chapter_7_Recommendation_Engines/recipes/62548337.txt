Title:
Gramma's Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/528468.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. powdered sugar
1 c. softened (not melted) butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp almond extract
2 egg whites (whisked)
2 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cream of tartar

Directions:
In a large bowl combine : powdered sugar, butter, vanilla, almond and egg- mix or beat on medium speed.
Stir in flour, baking soda and tartar.
Cover and refridgerate 2.5 hours(at least). Half dough, roll out on floured pastry sheet to about 1/8".
Cut with medium or large cookie cutters.
Bake @ 375 for 6 minutes, or until lightly golden.
Cool for half an hour before icing.
I also have a recipie for a icing glaze that sets just lovely on these cookies.
