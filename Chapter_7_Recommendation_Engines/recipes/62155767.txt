Title:
Red cabbage slaw with a twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cups of thinly sliced red cabbage (1/2 medium cabbage)
3/4 cup diced red pepper
2 medium shallots diced
1 inch of fresh ginger peeled and minced
10 fresh basil leaves snipped
2 Tbs salted sunflower seeds
1/4 cup toasted pine nuts (cooled)
1/4 mayonnaise
2 Tb olive oil
juice of 1 medium lemon
1 1/2 tsp wasabi paste
freshly ground pepper mix (black, red and white)

Directions:
Thinly slice the cabbage, making sure to keep all pieces no longer than 2 inches. Transfer to a large mixing bowl.
Dice the red pepper and shallots. Peel and mince the ginger. Add all to cabbage. Snip the basil into the vegetables.
In a separate bowl, add dressing ingredients and whisk until mixed.
Add dressing to vegetables, mix well. Can be served immediately or refrigerate 2-3 hours to enhance flavor.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
Having discovered the wonderful qualities of fresh ginger, I tried to create a colorful winter salad with a suprising mix of flavor.
