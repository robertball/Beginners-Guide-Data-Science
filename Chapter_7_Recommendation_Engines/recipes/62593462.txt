Title:
Pork Chop & Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Pork Chops
2 Tbs. Vegetable Oil
1 C. Instant Rice
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 can Cream of Celery Soup
1 C. Milk
1 cube (4 oz.) Butter or Margarine

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet and brown chops on both sides.
Combine soups, milk & butter in saucepan & heat to a slow boil.
Stir in rice & pour over chops in a 9" X 13" baking dish.
Cover with foil & bake for 2 hours at 275 degrees.

Submitter: 
cdrewcia
Description: 
This simple recipe was handed down to me from my Grandmother.  She was a fabulous cook that could master elaborate dishes, but she also taught me how to make some easy & delicious meals.
Slow baking makes this a fall of the bone dish with even tougher cuts of pork!
