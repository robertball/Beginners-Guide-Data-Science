Title:
Cora's Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 stick butter
1 14.5 oz can chicken broth
2 (14.75 ounce) cans cream-style corn
4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
2 cups milk
2-3 slices crumbled cooked bacon
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3/4 teaspoon pepper
Minced fresh parsley

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, saute onion in butter until tender. Add the broth, corn and potatoes; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 16-20 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Reduce heat to low. Stir in the milk, bacon, salt and pepper. Cook for 5-10 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with parsley.
