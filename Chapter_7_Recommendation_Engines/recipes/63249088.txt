Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large, over-ripe roma tomatoes
1 small white onion
3 medium jalapenos, seeded
2 cups long grain rice
1/3 cup oil
4 cloves minced garlic
2 cups chicken broth
1 Tbl tomato or chili paste
1/4 cup fresh cilantro
1 lime (optional)

Directions:
Preheat over to 350. Process tomatoes and onion until smooth. Reserve only 2 cups. Mince jalapenos, reserve.
Rinse rice until water is clear. Drain well.
Heat oil in oven-safe pan. Drop a few peices of rice in to the pan, if they sizzle oil is ready. Add all rice and fry until brown (6-8 min). Add garlic, jalapenos, cook until fragrant (30 sec). Stir in broth, puree and paste. Bring to a boil.
Cover pan and transfer to oven, bake 15 minutes.
Stir well and continue to bake for another 15 minutes. Add cilantro, and freshly squeezed lime.

Submitter: 
BusyBeeTami
Description: 
A very fresh, flavourful rice, easily made vegetarian by using vegetable broth in place of chicken broth.
