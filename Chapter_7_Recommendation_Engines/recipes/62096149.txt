Title:
Crunchy Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Baking potaotes
1/2 cup melted butter
1 Clove garlic, minced
1 Cup Crushed Cornflakes
Paprika
Salt

Directions:
Peel potatoes and cut into quaters or big chunks
Combine butter and garlic, mix well
Dip potatoes into butter mixture, then into cornflake crumbs, coating evenly. If there is any butter mixture left, pour over potatoes after they are placed on a baking sheet
Srinkle with salt and place on lightly oiled (or nonstick) baking sheet
Bake at 375' for 45 minutes or until tender
Sprinkle with paprika before serving

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
This is a nice potato recipe but you can also use any herbs or seasonings you wish for added flavor.
