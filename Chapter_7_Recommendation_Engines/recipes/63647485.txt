Title:
Lekman cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz. lingonberry juice (syrup available at Ikea)
1 1/2 oz Absolut vodka
1/2 oz Blue Curacao
Sour key candy
Ice

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, add lingonberry juice, vodka, Blue Curacao and ice.
Shake.
Pour into low ball glass or cup.
Add sour key candy as garnish.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CHRIS GROUT
Description: 
This cocktail was invented in May 2013 in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Its name, ingredients and colour are inspired by the eponymous Ikea product. The recipe is the outcome of a rhetorical question posed while assembling Ikea furniture If the Lekman was a drink, what would be in it?.
