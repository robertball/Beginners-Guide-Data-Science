Title:
Cajun Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter
1 tbsp parsley flakes
1 tsp celery salt
1 tsp onion salt
2 tsp garli powder
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper [or to taste]
1 tsp cajun spice
6 cups chex cereal
3 cups gold fish
2 cups pretzel sticks [broken]
4 ozs shoe string potatoes
12 ozs mixed nuts
4 dropes hot pepper sauce [or to taste]

Directions:
Melt butter, stir in all seasonings and hot sauce.  Pour over cereal, pretzels, nuts and potatoes.  Mix until well coated.
Bake in 250 degree oven for 45 min stirring every 15 min.
