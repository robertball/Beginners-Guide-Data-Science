Title:
Mom's Yummy Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef (cooked)
1 (2 lb) jar spaghetti sauce
1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 1/2 cups water
2 TBSP sugar
dash garlic powder
1 TBSP italian seasoning
..................................
CHEESE FILLING:
16 oz. small curd cottage cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1/2 cup shredded parmesan (not kind in canister)
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/8 tsp pepper
1 TBSP dried parsley
...................................
lasagne noodles

Directions:
Bring water to boil with salt.  Add lasagne noodles slowly.  (A few at a time).  Cook until ALMOST done, usually about 9 minutes.  Drain and lay out on foil sheets.
While noodles are cooking, mix ground beef, spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, water, sugar, and spices.  Simmer for 10 to 20 minutes.
Combine ingredients for cheese filling.  Set aside until noodles are done and you are ready to assemble lasagne.
11x13 pan OR (2)8x8 pans
Spray pan(s) with cooking spray.  Put small amount of sauce to cover bottom of pan.
Layer in the folling order:
Pasta, sauce, cheese.  Pasta, sauce, cheese (again).
End with just a little pasta, then sauce.  Add a little cheese mix or extra mozzarella if you have any left over, but not really necessary.
Bake covered at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.
