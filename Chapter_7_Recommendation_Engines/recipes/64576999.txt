Title:
PB Butterscotch Marshmallow Slice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
12 oz pkg butterscotch chips
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 cup coconut (optional)
4 cups mini marshmallows

Directions:
Melt margarine. I prefer the microwave for this.
Add butterscotch chips, stirring and heating gently to melt.
Add peanut butter, stir to melt and mix well.
Add coconut, if using.
Stir in marshmallows.
Put into a greased 9" x 13" pan.
Chill.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This is a classic - try and eat just one piece! Creamy peanut butter & butterscotch surrounds mini marshmallows - colored ones are the most fun :)
