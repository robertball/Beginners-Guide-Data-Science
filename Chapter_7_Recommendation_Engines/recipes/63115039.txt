Title:
Simple Homemade Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/792219.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup floud
1 cup milk
1 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp oil OR applesauce
1 Tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat griddle to 375 F.
Beat the egg until fluffy. Beat in remaining ingredients until smooth.
Grease griddle. Drop by 1/4 cup full onto griddle. Cook until pancakes puff and are dry around the edges. When bubbles form, flip and cook other side until golden brown.
