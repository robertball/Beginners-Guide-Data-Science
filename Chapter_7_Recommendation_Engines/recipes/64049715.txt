Title:
Fresh pasta from the old country Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1182712.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
4 egg shell halves full of water
1 tbsp olive oil
pinch salt
palm full of semolina flour or fine-med ground corn- meal
3 cups flour or as needed
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Crack at least 2 of your eggs as evenly as possible in half and add eggs into a small bowl.
Fill those empty egg shells with water. (All 4 halves)
Add 2 more eggs into bowl.
Add olive oil and salt.
Add semolina to the wet ingredients.
Prepare your stand mixer with the paddle attachment.
Add flour to stand mixer bowl.
Turn mixer on to stir and add wet ingredients.
Keep on stir until the mixer is working hard and turn up until ingredients are just combined.
Switch out the paddle for the dough hook and turn to 4 or 5.
Watch dough carefully and add more flour a little at a time as needed. Do not exceed more than 1 cup.
Dough should be slightly sticky but not dry.
Knead with dough hook for 2 minutes or so.
Remove dough from bowl.
On a floured surface,knead dough by hand for an additional 2 minutes.
Shape dough into a ball.
Gently pour olive oil over ball and cover with plastic wrap.
Allow to rest for 20-30 minutes.
Prepare your pasta machine to make desired shape pasta or roll to desired thickness with a rolling pin and cut your noodle shape.
Add fresh pasta to boiling salted water for 3-5 minutes or until done depending on thickness.
Drain water and add your favourite sauce.
