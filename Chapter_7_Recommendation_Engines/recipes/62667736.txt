Title:
Indian Chicken Coconut Korma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/594993.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 C. cashew halves
1/4 C. boiling water
2 Tb vegetable oil (or ghee)
1 Tb. butter
2 bay leaves, crumbled
3 cloves garlic, peeled and roughly chopped
1 Tb fresh ginger, roughly chopped
1 onion, diced
1/4 C. golden raisins
1 Tb honey
1 Tb curry powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 C. chicken broth
1 tsp coconut extract
1 C. heavy cream
1/2 C. plain yogurt
1/3 C. tomato sauce
2 Tb fresh cilantro
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size chunks
fresh parsley (opt.)

Directions:
1. Pour boiling water over cashew halves and allow to sit for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan heat oil and butter, and cook bay leaves for 1 minute.
2. To oil, add garlic, ginger, onion, raisins, curry powder, cinnamon, salt, garam masala, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook until onions are translucent.
3. Add chicken broth, honey, coconut extract, cream, yogurt, tomato sauce, cilantro, and drained cashews to the mixture, then pour contents of saucepan into a blender.
4. Blend on high until mixture is pureed, then return to saucepan.
5. Add chicken and simmer for 30 minutes, or until chicken is tender.
6. Serve over rice with fresh chopped parsley (opt.)
