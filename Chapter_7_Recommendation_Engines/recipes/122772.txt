Title:
Chicken Salsa Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981102.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) package pre-baked Italian bread shell crust
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 (12 ounce) jar salsa
1 cup cubed cooked chicken

Directions:
Place bread shell on an ungreased 12-in. pizza pan. Sprinkle with 3/4 cup of cheese. Top with salsa, chicken and remaining cheese. Bake at 450 degrees F for 8-10 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Guy Turnbull
Description: 
'This zippy chicken pizza is sure to become the most-requested version in the house,' assures Mrs. Guy Turnbull of Arlington, Massachusetts. 'The cooked chicken and a prebaked crust make it quick, easy and oh-so-good.'
