Title:
Slow Cooker Ground Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777292.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 1/2 cups ketchup
1/4 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup prepared yellow mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
3/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
3/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place the ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook, stirring to crumble, until beef is browned. Drain.
Transfer the beef and onion to a slow cooker and stir in the ketchup, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, vinegar and mustard. Season with celery seed, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and salt.
Cover and simmer on Low setting for a few hours before serving.
