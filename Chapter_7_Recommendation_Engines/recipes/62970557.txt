Title:
Waffle Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 22 oz bag waffle fries
1 tomato, diced
1 onion, chopped (or sub in green onions)
1-2 cups colby jack cheese
1 3 oz package real bacon bits (or sub in 1 lb bacon cooked and crumbled)

Directions:
Cook waffle fries per directions on the package.
Put on a plate and top with onion, bacon bits and cheese.
Microwave until the cheese is melted.
Top with tomatoes.
Serve with sour cream or sour cream supreme (recipes).
