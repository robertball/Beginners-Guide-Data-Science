Title:
Big Ray's Pizza Beyond Compare Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1080451.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 3/4 ounce) can tomato puree
1/4 cup water
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp olive oil
1/4 tsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp oregano
1/8 tsp basil
1/8 tsp thyme
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a small saucepan over medium heat.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Make sure you double the batch and freeze it or use as dip for bread-sticks. It is truly as good as store-bought.
