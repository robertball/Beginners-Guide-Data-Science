Title:
Cranberry Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST
1 pkg. Pillsbury Plus Butter Recipe cake mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 egg
1/4 cup chopped pecans
FILLING
8 oz pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 egg
16 oz. can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/4 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  In large bowl combine cake mix, butter and 1 egg at low speed until crumbly.  Add pecans and mix well.  Press evenly into bottom of 13 x 9 inch pan.  Bake at 350 for 5-8 minutes until crust is set (crust will not be browned).
In small bowl combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla and 1 egg.  Beat until smooth.  Add nutmeg to cranberry sauce and blend well.  Carefully pour cream cheese mixture over crust.  Spoon cranberry sauce in 3 rows lengthwise in pan.  Pull knife through cranberry sauce and into cream cheese mixture to form swirls.  Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes or until cream cheese mixture is set.  Cool completely.
