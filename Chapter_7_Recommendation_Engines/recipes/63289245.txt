Title:
Summer Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 onions, chopped
2 large garlic cloves, chopped
2 tsp salt (or, to taste)
3 carrots, cleaned and sliced
2 medium red (or white rose) potatoes, cubed
2 cups fresh green beans, broken into bite sized pieces
2 quarts good broth (chicken or veggie)
2 cans (14.5 oz) petite diced tomatoes
1 16 oz bag of corn kernels, thawed
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
1/2 fresh lemon, juiced
16 oz fresh spinach, torn into bite sized pieces

Directions:
Heat a large pot over medium heat.  Add oil.  When shimmering add onions, garlic, and salt.  Sautee until vegetables are just starting to turn golden.  Add carrots, potatoes, green beans, and broth.  Simmer for 10 minutes.  Add tomatoes and corn.  Simmer 15 minutes.  Add parsley and lemon juice.  Add spinach.  Remove from heat and let sit 5 minutes.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
A light, fresh vegetable soup.  Perfect for using summer's bounty!
