Title:
Drunken Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken thighs
2 cans Cream of Chicken soup
1 can Cream of celery
1 can Cream of mushroom
2 1/2 cups of minute rice
1/4 cup melted butter

Directions:
Mix uncooked rice with all cans of soup
pour mixture into cooking dish 13x9
place uncooked chicken on top of mixture
pour melted butter on top of chicken
cook at 350 for 1 1/2 hours
