Title:
Pie Pastry (Jarvis Family Tourtiere) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084387.jpg
Ingredients:
5 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 lb crisco/lard (one box)
1 egg
1 Tbs white vinegar
cold water (ice)

Directions:
Combine flour and salt. Cut in lard with pastry blender
In a liquid measuring cut, beat egg and vinegar with a fork. Fill with cold water up to 1 cup. Mix well.
Slowly add liquid to the flour mixture 1 Tbs at a time.
Make into a ball handling as little as possible and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
AnneP
Description: 
The annual Jarvis Tourtiere Baking pie crust recipe. We usually make enough for 30 pies! Easy to make and flaky to eat!
