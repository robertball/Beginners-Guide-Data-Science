Title:
Egg Nog and Apple Bread pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Egg Nog 3 cups
4 eggs
Enough leftover bread to fill a 9X12 baking pan 1 1/2 inches deep
2 tablespoons of softened butter
Two apples peeled and chopped into 1cm square pieces.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Butter the pan
Line with bread
Add the peeled chopped apples to the bread.
Beat eggnog and eggs together
Pour eggnog mixture over the bread and apples.
Cover with aluminum foil
Bake at 375 for 30 minutes
Remove foil and bake 10 minutes more or until brown and sort of firm
