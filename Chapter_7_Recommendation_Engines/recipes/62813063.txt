Title:
HUMMUS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion,chopped
5 cloves garlic,minced
splash of olive oil
1 can chickpeas
3/4 cup tahini[sesame seed paste]
1 1/2 Tbsp.soy sauce
1/2 cup lemon juice or
1/4 cup water and 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup fresh parsley,chopped
1/4 cup jalapeno peppers,chopped[WEAR GLOVES]
1 tsp.cumin
1/4 tsp.cayenne pepper
1 tsp.salt

Directions:
In small sauce pan,saute onions and garlic in a splash of oil on medium heat untill onions are soft.
In blender add all ingredients and blend untill desired consistency.Makes 2 cups.
Serve with pita's,crackers or vegetables.

Submitter:

Description: 
Creamy smooth dip made from chickpeas,one of the most nutritious beans of all!
Also known for it's medicinal properties!
