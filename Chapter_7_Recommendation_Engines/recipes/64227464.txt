Title:
Dressing Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups yellow cornmeal
2teaspoons salt
1 1/2 teaspoon soda
2 2/3 cups buttermilk
4 eggs - separate yolks and whites
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Sift cornmeal, soda & salt together.
Beat egg yolks well, add buttermilk.
Add this wet mixture to the cornmeal mix and beat well.
Add hot melted butter and beat well.
Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites.
Turn immediately into hot, greased cast iron skillet.
Bake 450 for 20-30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Mom only uses this cornbread for dressing and never makes dressing with anything else.
