Title:
Cherry Cola Brownies (Frosting Recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
4 cups powdered sugar
1/3 cup cherry cola carbonated beverage
24 maraschino cherries, if desired

Directions:
In large bowl, beat frosting ingredients with electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
Spread evenly over brownies.
Cut into 6 rows by 4 rows.
Garnish each with cherry.
