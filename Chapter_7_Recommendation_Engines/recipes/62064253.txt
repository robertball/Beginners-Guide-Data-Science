Title:
Chocolate Pumpkin Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup canola oil
2 cups sugar
4 large eggs
2 cups canned pumpkin
2 cups flour
1/4 cup baking cocoa
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tb pumpkin pie spice
1 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Beat together the oil, sugar and eggs.  Add the pumpkin.  Sift the dry ingredients and add (do not overbeat).  Stir in the chocolate chips.  Pour in sprayed 10 x 15 inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees about 25-30 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.  Cool, then frost with your favorite chocolate buttercream frosting (or make two 9" layers).

Submitter: 
STARFLOWER
Description: 
My version of moist pumpkin cake with cocoa and chocolate chips added for a yummy chocolate, pumpkin, and spice cake taste.  Melts in your mouth.  You must try it! Ice with your favorite chocolate buttercream frosting.
