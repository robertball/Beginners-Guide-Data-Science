Title:
Lemon Jello Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 pkg. (4 serving size) lemon jello or instant pudding mix
3/4 cup oil
3/4 cup water
4 eggs
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix and beat cake mix, jello mix, oil, water, and eggs. Bake in 350 oven till done (40 minutes.) Combine lemon juice and powdered sugar. While still warm prick cake with fork and pour over warm cake.
