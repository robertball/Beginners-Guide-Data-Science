Title:
Fried Cinnomon Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
10 flour tortillas (8")
veggie oil

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg; set aside. Cut tortillas into 3 x 2 strips.  Heat 1" of oil in a skillet to 375.  Fry 4-5 strips at a time for 30 sec onds on each side or until golden brown.  Drain on paper towels.
While still warm, place strips in bag with sugar mixture; shake gently to coat.  Serve immediately or store in airtight container.
