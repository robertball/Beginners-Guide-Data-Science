Title:
bruschetta, 2 ways Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
for both : cut 1 baguette into diagonal 1/2 in slices
1. tomato basil version
2 large tomatoes, chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
1/2 c chopped fresh basil
1 tbsp olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
2. pepperonata version
1/2 c part skim ricotta
2 c pepperonata warmed (make a big batch and stuf in omletes or scatter across sandwiches and pizza)
for pepperonata:
1 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic
1/3 tsp red pepper flakes
3 bell peppers ideally ryo not g, cut into 1/2 in chunks
2 tbsp red wine vinegar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
to make pepperonata:
heat the olive oil in a skillet or saute pan over med- high heat. add the garlic cloves and pepper flakes and cook just long enough to infuse the oil, about 1 min. toss in the peppersand cook, stirring occasionally, until the peppers are soft and lightly blistered. add the vinegar and cook for a minute. season with salt and pepper. store tightly in the fridge for up to 1 week. makes 3 c
for bruschetta:
preheat oven to 450
place baguette slices on a baking sheet and bake until light brown but still soft in the middle.
while the bread slices bake, mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil. season to taste with salt and pepper.
remove bread from the oven and top half with tomato mixture and the other half with riscotta then top with a small scoop of peppers.
