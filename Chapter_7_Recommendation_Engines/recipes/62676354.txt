Title:
Home made baking powder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. corn starch-optional if your going to store for a couple of days.  Better fresh!

Directions:
Sift the ingredients together. Store in a air tight container in a cool place.

Submitter: 
Nancy Gibson
Description: 
Making this from scratch is much healthier. Commercial brands have aluminum in them, which is related to alzheimers.
