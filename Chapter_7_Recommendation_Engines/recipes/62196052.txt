Title:
Guacamole Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen peas
1 bunch green onions
2 fresh carrots
2 stalks celery
1/4 lb cheddar cheese cubed
1 can Rotel Jalepenos and Tomatoes
2-3 Avocados
2 Limes
1/4 cup Fresh cilantro
1/4 cup Fresh parsley
1/4 cup plain yogurt
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup Mayonaise
2 tablspoons chopped garlic - fresh

Directions:
Defrost peas in cold water for 10 min.
Chop and drop into bowl green onions, carrots, celery, cheese, 1/2 of 1 avocado, and add cold peas.
Drain Rotel and add.
Juice 1 lime over ingredients.
In Food Processor, place remaining avocados, parsley, cilantro, juice and zest of 2nd lime, yogurt, sour cream, mayonaise, garlic and pulse till well blended.
Salt and pepper to taste before pouring over chopped ingredients.
Stir well, check again for salt & pepper.
Ready to eat but gets better as it sits for an hour.
