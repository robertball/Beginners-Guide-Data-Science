Title:
Slow Cooked Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/342784.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Racks of baby back ribs or pork spare ribs
Grill seasoning blend or salt and pepper
Sauce:
1 cup Ketchup
1 Tbs Dijon mustard
2 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
3 Tbs Packed brown sugar
1 Clove of garlic, finely minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ÂºF
Line large baking pan with tinfoil and spray with non stick cooking spray.  Arrange ribs on baking pan and bake for 45 minutes flipping ribs after 30 minutes.
Cut ribs into serving size pieces and put in slow cooker.
Combine sauce ingredients and pour over ribs.  Stir together.
Cover and cook on LOW for 4-6 hours or until tender.
Stir frequently.
