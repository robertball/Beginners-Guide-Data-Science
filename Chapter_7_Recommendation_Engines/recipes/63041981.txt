Title:
Steamed brussel sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 to 10 heads of brussel sprouts per person
Boiling hot water

Directions:
Place steamer basket into large pot. Fill with water up to but not touching steamer basket. Place pot of water on high heat; Bring to boil. While waiting for water to heat, prepare fresh brussel sprouts: but off tough ends, remove any hard outer leaves. Cut an X in the bottom of large sprouts.  Place sprouts into steamer basket using tongs. Steam for 3 to 8 minutes - test for your preference of doneness by piercing sprout with fork.
