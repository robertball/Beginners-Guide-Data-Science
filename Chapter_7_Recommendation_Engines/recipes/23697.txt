Title:
Honeyed Ham Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4587906.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup honey
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup yellow mustard
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon dried minced onion
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Mix together in a bowl: honey, ketchup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, minced onion, cloves and ginger.  Pour over ham slices as a glaze.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
A very quick and sweet ham glaze that's easy to make.
