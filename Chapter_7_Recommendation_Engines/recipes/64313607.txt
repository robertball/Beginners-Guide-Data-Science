Title:
Chicken & Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1992352.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast
2 c. basmati rice
1 bag frozen peas
6 green onions, chopped
1 1/2 t. lemon zest
1/4 c. (heaping) golden raisins
1/3 c. fresh lemon juice
1/3 c. olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
Crumbled feta cheese to taste (optional)

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a large frying pan, bring 1/4 inch of water to a simmer over moderately high heat. Sprinkle the chicken with salt and pepper and add to the pan. Cover, reduce the heat, and simmer, turning once, until cooked through, about 12 minutes. Remove the chicken, let cool slightly, and then cut into approximately 1/2-inch chunks.
Cook peas according to package directions.
In a small glass jar, add the lemon juice, olive oil, and salt and pepper to taste. Put lid on jar and shake vigorously.
In a large bowl, mix the rice, chicken, peas, onions, raisins, and lemon zest. Add the dressing and toss gently.
If desired, sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese before serving.
Can be served either warm or cold.
