Title:
Country Style Fried Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup shortening
6 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 green pepper cut into small pieces
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
In a large cast iron skillet, heat shortening over medium-high heat. Add potatoes and green pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are golden brown. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
These fried potatoes are the perfect companion for bacon and eggs. You may use oil instead of shortening if you wish.Modified from: Country Style Fried Potatoes
