Title:
Fall Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches celery, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 baking potatoes, chopped
10 carrots, chopped
2 cups chicken stock
1 T olive oil
1 T flour
4 T butter
oregano
basil
thyme
garlic salt
onion salt
pepper

Directions:
Combine everything in crockpot.
Cook 5 hours.
Drain and reserve broth.
Puree vegetables.
Return to crockpot. Add broth as needed.
Cook 1 hour.
Serve with oyster crackers.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This is a great soup for a fall day. Use whatever veggies are available.
