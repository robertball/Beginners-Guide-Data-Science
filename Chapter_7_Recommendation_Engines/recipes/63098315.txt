Title:
Easiest Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. can Refried Beans
1 package Taco Seasoning
1 16oz. container Sour Cream
1 16oz. jar Salsa (such as Newman's Own Medium or Hot)
2-3 cups Taco Blend Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300°F.
In a medium bowl, combine refried beans and taco seasoning. Spread into an even layer in the bottom of an 8x8 glass baking dish.
Continue layering with the sour cream, then salsa. Sprinkle cheese evenly over top of the salsa layer.
Bake at 300° for 6-10 minutes, until cheese is melted but not bubbly.

Submitter: 
ambergetSHI
Description: 
I came up with this while on a limited budget, and craving a layer dip sans guacamole, lettuce, olives etc. Super easy and totally delicious!
