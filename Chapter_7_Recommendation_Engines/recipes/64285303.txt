Title:
Dila's Fresh Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp white onion, diced
8 plum tomatoes, diced
1/2 4oz can of chopped green chilies
2 Tbsp cilantro, finely chopped
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1 fresh lime, juiced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Fresh, easy salsa. This is one of my favorite apps for any season. My mother in law taught me this recipe and I make it for every event. Everyone loves it!
