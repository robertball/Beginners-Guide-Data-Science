Title:
Mom's Veggie Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Lasagna noodles (9 noodles)
1/2 lb. fresh sliced mushrooms
2 c. chopped carrots
1 c. chopped onion
2 cloves minced garlic
2 T. olive oil
1 pkg. fresh spinach - washed and torn into small pieces
2 c. (16 oz.) lowfat cottage cheese
2 eggs
15.5 oz. spag sauce (I used 32 oz.)
1/4 c. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook Lasagna noodles. Saute mushrooms, carrots, onion, and garlic in oil until tender but not browned. Add spinach and cook, stirring constantly until spinach is tender (2-3 minutes). Combine cottage cheese and eggs. Add to spinach mixture. Pour thin layer of spaghetti SAUCE in 13 x 9 pan. Spread 3 pieces lasagna noodles. Spread 1/2 spinach mixture evenly over lasagna. Sprinkle with 1 T. parmesan. Repeat lasa noodles, remaining spinach and cheese. Tope with remaining noodles and sauce. Sprinkle with 2T. parmesan. Cover with foil. Bake at 375 for 45 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Uncover. Bake an additional 5 minutes. Let stand for ten minutes before serving.
