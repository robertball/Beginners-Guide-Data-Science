Title:
Shrimp Scampi (Baked) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package pasta
1/2 cup butter
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 pounds shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 cup white wine (or chicken stock)
3/4 cup dry bread crumbs, Italian
3/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
dash Old Bay (Seafood Seasoning)
Lemon (optional)

Directions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add pasta and return to boil.  Cook till al dente.  Drain well.
2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
3. If shrimp is frozen, thaw and drain well.
4. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine all the ingredients except shrimp and breadcrumbs. When butter is melted remove from heat.
5. Arrange the shrimp in a shallow baking dish. Pour the butter mixture over the shrimp. Top with bread crumbs.
6. Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the shrimp are pink and opaque.
7. Mix the shrimp up to combine the bread crumbs and serve over pasta.
Squeeze lemon over each individual portion if desired.

Submitter: 
Lori L Dixon
Description: 
It is shrimp scampi in the oven and not on the stove top. Really good.
