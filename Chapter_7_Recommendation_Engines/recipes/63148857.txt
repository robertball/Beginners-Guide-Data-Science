Title:
Chicken Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/854716.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp.oil
2 Tbsp.lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp.salt
1 1/2 tsp.oregano
1 1/2 tsp.cumin
1 tsp.garlic powder
1/2 tsp.paprika
1/2 tsp.crushed red pepper flakes
1 1/2 lb.boneless chicken,cut into strips
1/2 medium red pepper,julienned
1/2 medium green pepper,julinned
4 green onions,chopped
1/2 cup onion,chopped
6 medium tortillas
shredded cheddar cheese
salsa
guacamole

Directions:
In a zip lock bag combine 2 Tbsp.oil,lemon juice and spices.Add chicken strips and coat well.Refridgerate for 4 hours.
Heat remaining 2 Tbsp.oil,saute peppers and onions untill tender but still crisp.Remove from skillet.Add chicken to skillet,cook untill no longer pink,about 6-7 minutes.Return peppers and onions to skillet,heat through.Spoon filling into warm tortillas.
Serve with cheese,salsa and guacamole.
