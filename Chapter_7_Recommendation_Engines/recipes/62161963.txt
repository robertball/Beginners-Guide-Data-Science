Title:
Snack Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds sourdough think pretzels
2/3 cups vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon lemon pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 package dry ranch dressing mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 F.
Mix all ingredients together.
Pour over pretzels and coat well.
Place pretzel pieces on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 20 minutes.

Description: 
A tasty and very simple finger food, lunch snack, or picnic treat.
