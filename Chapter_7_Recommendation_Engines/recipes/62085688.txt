Title:
Ham and Potato Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups frozen Ore-Ida Crispy Crowns
1 pkg. (16 oz.) frozen cut broccoli
1 cup chopped ham
8 eggs
1 1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Grease a 13 x 9-inch baking dish.  Arrange broccoli evenly over bottom of greased baking dish; sprinkle with ham.
Scatter frozen crispy crowns over the top.
In medium bowl, beat together eggs, milk and mustard until smooth.  Pour egg mixture evely over potato mixture in baking dish.
Sprinkle cheddar cheese.  Cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight.
Uncover and bake in a 350 degrees F oven for 40 to 50 minutes or until knife inserted near the center comes out clean.  Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Darci
Description: 
A great way to combine broccoli, ham and potatoes.
