Title:
Tortellini Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/331808.jpg
Ingredients:
2 9 oz. packages of three cheese tortellini, cooked according to package instructions, and cooled
1 lb. bacon, cooked to be slightly crunchy or to your preference according to package instructions, chopped (smaller than bite-size), and cooled
4 cups of raw broccoli, chopped into small pieces (smaller than bite-size)
1 package (approx. 10 oz.) of grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
2 green onions, minced
1 cup coleslaw dressing

Directions:
Prepare tortellini, bacon, broccoli, tomatoes, and onions. Toss the ingredients in a large bowl, including the coleslaw dressing, refrigerate until cold, then serve and eat!
