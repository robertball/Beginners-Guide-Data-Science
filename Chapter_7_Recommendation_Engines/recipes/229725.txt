Title:
Blended Mocha Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3854372.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brewed espresso
1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
1 cup whole milk
10 ice cubes, or as needed
3 tablespoons chocolate syrup, or more to taste

Directions:
Stir espresso and sweetened condensed milk together in a bowl. Add milk and stir; pour into a blender. Add ice and chocolate syrup to the espresso mixture. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jessie Crosby
Description: 
Easy delicious way to get that great pick-me-up without spending a ton of money!
