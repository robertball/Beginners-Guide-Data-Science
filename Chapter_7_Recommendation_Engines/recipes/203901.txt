Title:
Tequila Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993248.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Especial Gold
3 ounces pink grapefruit juice
1 splash club soda
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Fill shaker with ice. Add Cuervo Especial Gold and pink grapefruit juice.
Shake well.
Pour into a glass with ice. Top with club soda and garnish with lime.
