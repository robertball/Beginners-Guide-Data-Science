Title:
Chung Pao Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/469561.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 Chicken breast
1 TBspoon sugar
1 TBspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup cooking oil
SAUCE
3 TBspoon hoisin sauce
3 TBspoon plum sauce
2 TBspoon soy sauce
3 TBspoon rice wine vinegar
2 TBspoon sesame oil
2 TBspoon oyster sauce
Mix theses ingredients and set aside
DRY
6- 8 Dry chili pepper
3 TBspoon grated ginger
2 TBspoon minced garlic
1/4 cup chopped green onions
2/3 cup dry roasted peanuts
2 stalk dice celery
1 dice bell pepper
1 dice zucchini

Directions:
Cut chicken in 1 inch pieces, season with garlic powder, sugar, and set aside. When the oil is hot put dried chili in for 2 minutes, then remove and set aside. (For a more spicy dish you can cut chili and use the seeds in the oil) Add the ginger and garlic to the oil for a few seconds, then add chicken cooked for about 7 minutes or until the chicken is no longer pink. Add bell peppers, zucchini, celery, and peanuts. Stir in sauce mixture, and simmer for about five minutes, then add green onions and chili peppers you used in oil.If sauce is too light can be thicken with 1TBspoon corn starch mix in water.

Submitter: 
Maureen Hogan
Description: 
This is a chinese spicy chicken stir fry. If you never try this chicken before, it is a must try!! Can be serve with rice. All these sauces in this recipe can be found at any Asian market.
