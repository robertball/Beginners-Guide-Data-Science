Title:
Grandma Lulu's Sour Cream Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2 2/3 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp nutmeg
1 cup sour cream
¼ cup water

Directions:
Equilibrate shortening, sour cream and egg  to room temperature (2 hours).
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Cream shortening, sugar, eggs and vanilla in a medium sized bowl.
Blend dry ingredients, and add to creamed mixture alternately with sour cream.
Add up to ¼ cup water to batter – add only 1/8 cup for dryer cookies.
Drop a tablespoon of batter onto a greased cookie sheet (or use a nonstick baking mat) and bake cookies for 6-7 minutes. Cool on a wire rack completely before drizzling with Sugar Cookie Glaze.

Submitter: 
Guinevere Murphy
Description: 
These were my favorite cookies as a child. Grandma always had a fresh batch ready and waiting for our visits. Unfortunately, she didn't write the recipe down, but after many yummy iterations, this is as close as I can get...
