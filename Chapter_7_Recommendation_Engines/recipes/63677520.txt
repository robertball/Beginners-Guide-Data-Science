Title:
Miniature Sausage Crepe Quiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crepes. 3 eggs, 1/4 tsp salt, 2 cups sifted all purpose flour, 2 cups milk, 1/4 cup melted butter. Filling. 2 cups shredded zucchini, 2 tbsps. butter, 5 oz, sweet Italian sausage, 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese, 2 slightly beaten eggs, 1/2 cup milk, 1/4 cup half and half, 2 Tbsp grated parmesan cheese, 1/2 tsp salt, pepper to taste.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.For crepes. Beat eggs in small bowl till foamy. Gradually add flour alternately with milk; beat until smooth. Beat in melted butter, cover and put in fridge 1 hour.Crepes. In a lightly buttered 6 or 7" skillet using 1 TBSP batter for each crepe cook one side until golden brown, turn and lightly cook other side. Keep warm between sheets of waxed paper. Filling. sautee zucchini in butter about 5 minutes. Remove to a medium size bowl.Cook sausage in same pan till no pink remains breaking up with spoon while cooking. Drain on paper towels. Add sausage, Swiss cheese, eggs, milk, half and half, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper to zucchini. Mix well. Dress crepes brown side out into well greased mini muffin pans. Spoon filling into cups. Bake at 425 for 5 minutes. Lower heat to 350 and bake 15 minutes or until golden .Serve warm
