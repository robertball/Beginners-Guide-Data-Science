Title:
Reuben Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of cooked shredded corned beef
1 cup of sauerkraut
2 8 oz packages of cream cheese
1 package of sliced swiss cheese
1 cup thousand island dressing

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together, place in baking dish. Bake at 350 until bubbling. Serve with rye crackers or bread.
