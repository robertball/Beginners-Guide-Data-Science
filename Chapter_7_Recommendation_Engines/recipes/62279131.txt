Title:
Tropical Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/425427.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 a dragon fruit
1 horned melon
1 star fruit
1 passion fruit
6 strawberries
1 cup sugar
1 T white honey
splash lime juice

Directions:
Scoop the fruit out of the half a dragon fruit. Try for scoops about the size of an 1/8 of a cup.
Quarter the strawberries.
Cut off the ends of the star fruit and discard. Continue to cut the remainer of the star fruit in 1/4 inch slices.
Put all the fruit chunks into a blender.
Cut the horned melon & passion fruit in half. Scoop the seeds from these into the blender.
Add a cup of sugar and a tablespoon of white honey.
Pulse/blend the mix until smooth.
Pour the mix into dessert bowls and freeze for 3 hours.
