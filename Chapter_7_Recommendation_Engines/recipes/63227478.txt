Title:
Daddy's Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Deep Dish Pie Crusts
2 heaping teaspoons corn starch
1 can evaporated milk
14 eggs
1/3 cup ketchup
20 slices of bacon
2 teaspoons salt
1 tablespoon pepper
1/2 cup chopped, green onion (optional)
1 cup sliced, sauteed mushrooms (optional)
4-6 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Cook bacon until crisp and crumble.
3. Poke holes in thawed pie crusts and cook at 400 degrees making sure not to let it bubble.  Remove crusts just prior to browning (approximately 7-8 minutes.)
4. Reduce oven temp to 350 degrees.
5. Sprinkle 1/2 of crumbled bacon into each pie crust.  (Can also add green onion and/or mushrooms.)
6. Mix cornstarch and evaporated milk with wire whisk or mixer.  Add eggs, ketchup, salt, and pepper to milk/cornstarch.  Mix well.  Pour one half of mixture into each pie making sure not to overfill.
7. Sprinkle shredded cheese into each pie and gently push down with fork until covered with egg mixture.
8. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 40 minutes.  Check frequently.  Pie is done when domed at the top and golden brown.  Let set at room temperature for atleast 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tracy Crews
Description: 
Egg, Cheese, and Bacon Quiche.  Delicious for brunch or dinner.  Can be reheated or eaten cold as leftovers.
