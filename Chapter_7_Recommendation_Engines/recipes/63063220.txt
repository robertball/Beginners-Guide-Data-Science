Title:
Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound crab meat, coarsely chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
2 tablespoons creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip)
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning TM, or to taste
dash of cayenne
1 1/2 cups seafood breader (or to taste)
1 cup vegetable oil (for frying)

Directions:
Place crabmeat in a bowl. Add egg, cayenne, salt and pepper. Stir in the onion, mustard, salad dressing, Old Bay(TM), and seafood breader. Mix until evenly blended.
Heat oil in a large heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Form the mixture into patties, and fry in the hot oil. Drain on paper towels, and serve hot.
