Title:
Slow Cooker Ground Beef Throw Together Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/771726.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef, browned and drained
15 oz can tomato sauce
3 cans Campbell's beef gravy
1/4 Worcestershire
1 small onion, diced
1 bag (16 oz)frozen peas and carrots
1 large russet potato, cubed
1 clove garlic, crushed
2 tsp black pepper
2 tsp ginger
2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Mix everything and dump into crock pot.
High 3 hours.
Serve over rice, egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
