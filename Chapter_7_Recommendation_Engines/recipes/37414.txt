Title:
Rocky Mountain Grilled Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982295.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds unpeeled jumbo fresh shrimp
1/2 cup lime juice
1/3 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 clove garlic, minced
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
PEEL shrimp, leaving tails intact, and devein, if desired. Combine lime juice and remaining ingredients; pour over shrimp.
COVER and chill 2 hours. Remove shrimp, discarding marinade. Thread shrimp on skewers.
GRILL, without grill lid, over medium-hot coals (350 to 400 degrees) 3 minutes on each side or until done.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
To turn shrimp easily, use stainless steel, flat-bladed skewers. Round skewer often create large holes, causing the food to slip instead of turn.
