Title:
Sheet Pan Shrimp Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. raw large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 tsp. cumin
1 tsp dark chili powder
1 tsp onion salt
1 tsp paprika
1 large red pepper
1  large yellow pepper
1 large orange pepper
1 large red onion
2 T olive oil for shrimp
2 T olive oil for peppers and onions

Directions:
Preheat oven 450 degrees
Prepare shrimp
In a large bowl add shrimp, spices and oil.  Mix to coat
Slice onions, peppers in strips toss in oil
In two sheet pans lay out into a single layer the pepper and onions.
In the second pan lay out in a single layer the shrimp.
On top rack place veggies then the bottom layer the shrimp.
Bake about 13 min.
Turn broiler on and broil for approx. 2-3 minutes.
Warm tortillas in damp paper towel in microwave.  10 sec. per tortilla
Serve immediately in warm tortillas with any toppings you desire.
