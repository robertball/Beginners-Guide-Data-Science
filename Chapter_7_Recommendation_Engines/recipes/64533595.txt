Title:
Coconut Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c dry jasmine rice
1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tbsp. Coconut oil
2 cup water

Directions:
Put in 4 Qt pan and heat on low heat, when begins to simmer turn to lowest setting and place a lid.  Cook for 20 min until water is absorbed.
