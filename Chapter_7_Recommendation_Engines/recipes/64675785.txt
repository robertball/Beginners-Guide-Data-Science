Title:
Cauliflower and Cheddar Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium head of cauliflower, cut into small pieces
4 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
Coarse salt and pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a large pot, melt butter
Add onion, saute
Add cauliflower, browning slightly; 12 minutes
Add broth and 1 cup water, simmer until tender; 20 minutes
With immersion blender, blend soup off heat until smooth
Add cheese and spices
