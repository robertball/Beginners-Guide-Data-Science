Title:
Champane Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Oz Frozen Fruit Punch, slightly thawed
10 Oz. frozen strawberries
2/3 Cup Orange Juice
2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
24 Oz. Ginger Ale
1 Quart Champange
3 Cups Water

Directions:
Place frozen ice ring in punch bowl.
Add the fruit punch, strawberries, OJ, Lemon Juice, and water.
Stir to dissolve the punch.
Slowly pour in the ginger ale and Champange.

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
