Title:
Camembert Wine Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/712317.jpg
Ingredients:
125g Camembert cheese
dry white wine
2 tbsp. unsalted butter, softened
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Scrape off rind from cheese and place in a bowl.  Cover cheese completely with wine and refrigerate 12 hours.  Drain cheese.  Beat cheese with butter and 1 tbsp. wine.  Stir in walnuts and pack into small serving dish.  Cover and refrigerate until chilled  Remove from fridge 30 minutes before serving.
