Title:
Yummy Grilled Ham and Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Zero calorie butter spray
2 slices of bread
2 slices of cheese
2-4 slices of ham(lunch meat)
Mustard to taste

Directions:
Spray a skillet with butter spray
Spray one side of each piece of bread with butter spray
In the skillet over medium high heat, place one piece of bread, butter side down, in the skillet
Put mustard on bread
Lay one slice of cheese on bread
Lay ham on top of cheese
Lay other slice of cheese on ham
Place other slice of bread, butter side up, on top of cheese
With spatula, press on sandwich and let it cook for about a minute, or until golden brown
Flip sandwich with spatula and brown other side until golden brown

Submitter: 
Chelsie Cannon
Description: 
Easy twist on the normal grilled cheese.
