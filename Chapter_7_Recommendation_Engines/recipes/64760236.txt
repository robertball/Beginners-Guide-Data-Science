Title:
Dill Pickle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
6-8 med-lg potatoes
1/3 cup chopped onion
2 cups chopped carrots, smaller dice
2 cups chopped dill pickles, smaller dice
1/4 cup butter, or more, to taste
1/2 cup flour
1 cup+ sour cream
1/4 cup water
2 cups pickle juice
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Fry onion in butter in large pot.
Add broth, potatoes, & carrots. Bring to boil and simmer until potatoes and carrots are tender. Mash.
Add pickles and continue to boil for a few minutes.
Whisk together flour, sour cream, water, pickle juice, pepper & cayenne until smooth. Stir into soup and stir till thickened (~ 5 more min.). Remove from heat.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A thick, rich potato-based soup with LOTS of dill pickle flavor!!
