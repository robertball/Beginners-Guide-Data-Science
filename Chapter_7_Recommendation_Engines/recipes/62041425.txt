Title:
Fake Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1 scoop vanilla Slim-Fast
4-5 ice cubes

Directions:
Put orange juice, Slim-Fast, and ice cubes in blender.  Blend until smooth.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CLOUDSSUNRAIN
Description: 
Tastes a lot like an Orange Julius!  An easy way to stick to the Slim-Fast diet without being bored.  Makes a great nutritious breakfast drink.
