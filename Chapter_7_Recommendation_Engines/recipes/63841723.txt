Title:
Creamed Spinach W/Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boxes or 2 lg. bags of frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed of all liquid
1-2 cans of cream soups...mushroom, onion
1/2# of bacon or more, cooked and drained
1 onion, chopped, omit of using cream of onion soup
1 8oz pkg. cream cheese, softened
S&P to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cook onions in bacon drippings until translucent.
Combine all ingredients except bacon and put in dish sprayed w/cooking spray.
Top w/bacon
Bake for 20-30 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Simple but delicious recipe for creamed spinach with bacon, cream cheese and canned soup.
