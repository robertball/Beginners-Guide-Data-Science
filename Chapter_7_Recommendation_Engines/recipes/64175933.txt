Title:
Chap Chee Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 green onions, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon sesame seeds
1 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
8 oz sliced mushrooms
1 tsp coconut oil
1/2 cup thinly sliced carrots
1/2 cup sliced bamboo shoots, drained
1/4 pound napa cabbage, sliced
2 cups chopped fresh spinach
3 ounces cellophane noodles, soaked in warm water
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt (opt)
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine 1 tablespoon soy sauce, sesame oil, green onions, garlic, sesame seeds, 1 teaspoon sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Stir in sliced mushrooms, and marinate at room temperature for 15 minutes. Drain marinade, saving to add at end.
Heat wok or large skillet over medium-high heat, then drizzle with oil. Cook drained mushrooms, carrots, napa cabbage til crisp tender adding water if needed to keep from sticking. Add bamboo shoots, spinach, cellophane noodles, reserved marinade, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Reduce heat to medium, and cook until heated through.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Stir-fried mushrooms, Napa cabbage, spinach, and other veggies are tossed with bean thread noodles in this classic Korean dish.Modified from: Chap Chee Noodles
