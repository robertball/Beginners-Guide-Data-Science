Title:
Cherry Fudge Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/984224.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened or semi-sweet chocolate
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup water
2 egg yolks
1 teaspoon almond extract
1/2 pint whipping cream, whipped
2 packages prepared mini graham cracker or mini pie crusts
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling, chilled

Directions:
In heavy saucepan, over medium heat, melt chocolate, sweetened condensed milk and salt. Cook and stir rapidly until very thick and bubbly, 5 to 8 minutes.
Add water and egg yolks; cook and stir rapidly until mixture thickens and bubbles again. Remove from heat; stir in almond extract. Cool 15 minutes.
Chill thoroughly, about 30 minutes; stir. Fold in whipped cream. Spoon into crusts. Chill 1 hour or until set. Divide cherry filling evenly among tarts immediately before serving. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
These individual cherry topped chocolate tarts are perfect for entertaining.
