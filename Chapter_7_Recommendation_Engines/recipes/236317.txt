Title:
Way-Too-Easy Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1132328.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 large cloves garlic, crushed
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Whisk soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, honey, brown sugar, and onion powder together until the sugar is completely dissolved into the liquid.
