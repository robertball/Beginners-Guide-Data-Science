Title:
Moose Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6457749.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small carrot
1/2 small onion
1/4 red bell pepper
3 white mushrooms
3 tsp minced carlic
1 pound ground moose meat
1/2 pound lean ground beef
1/2 TBS Worchestershire sauce
1 egg, beaten
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 cup Panko bread crumbs
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1 tsp Melinda's XXX Hot Sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 325 degrees.
Coarsely chop carrot, onion, pepper and mushrooms.  Add veggies and garlic to a food processor and mix until finely grated.
Combine veggies, ground moose, ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, egg, salt, pepper, cayenne and bread crumbs and gently mix until all combined.
Form into a loaf in a sprayed baking dish and bake 20 minutes.
Mix ketchup, mustard and hot sauce.  Glaze meatloaf and return to oven for another 25 - 30 minutes.

Lean meatloaf full of minced veggies.  The kids will never know!
