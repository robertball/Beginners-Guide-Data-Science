Title:
Biker Size Mushroom Swiss Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/733667.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb - Extra Lean Ground Beef
2tbs - Spicey BBQ Sauce
2 - Portabllo Mushroom
4 - Slices of Swiss Cheese

Directions:
Take 1lb of ground beef and split it in 2 portions and make into patties
BBQ for Aprox. 10 minutes (or desired wellness)
Slice Portabllo into about 1/2 thick slices (aprox 5-6)
BBQ sliced Potabello Mushroom aprrox. 5-7 minutes, flipping a few times
Add Potallo's to burger, add Swiss Cheese until melted
Add condiments and enjoy this MAN size Burger with a side order of home made fries tossed with Sea Salt & Parsley!
