Title:
Quick Spicy Sausage Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bulk breakfast sausage (hot or mild depending on how spicy you want it)
1 c chopped onion
3/4 c chopped celery
2 c chicken broth
3 c red potatoes cut into 1/2" pieces
1 14 oz can whole kernel corn
1 14 oz can creamed corn
1 12 oz can evaporated milk
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (more or less  if desired )
1/2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
In dutch oven, brown sausage with onion and celery
Add chicken broth, salt, pepper and potatoes.  Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer 10 min or until potatoes are soft
Add canned corn, evaporated milk, paprika and cayenne pepper.
Cook until heated through and serve!

Submitter: 
Toni D
Description: 
A spicy, hearty easy to make soup with tender chunks of sausage, potatoes and corn...Can be made with out cayenne to cater to the kids..
