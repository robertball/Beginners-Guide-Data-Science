Title:
Beth's Perfect Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
One 15-oz can pumpkin puree
1 1/2 c sugar
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1 12-oz can evaporated milk
One box yellow cake mix (18-21 oz)
2 sticks margarine
1 1/2 c chopped nuts (pecans are best)

Directions:
Mix eggs and pumpkin in a medium bowl.  Add eggs one at a time.  Add sugar, salt, evaporated milk, and spices.  Pour into a greased 9 x 13 pan.  Sprinkle dry cake mix over mixture and drizzle with melted margarine.  Sprinkle top with nuts.  Bake at 350F for one hour.  Top with Cool Whip if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
Pumpkin bars with streusel topping-perfect for potlucks!
