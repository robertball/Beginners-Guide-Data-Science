Title:
Rosemary Roasted Chicken and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1.5 lbs potatoes cut in 1” cubes
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tsp. paprika
1 tsp. crushed rosemary
1/4 tsp. salt
¼ tsp. black pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425F
2. Mix oil and all of spices in a small bowl.
3. Add chicken and potatoes to ziploc bag, pour spice mixture on top, and toss to coat well.
4. Arrange chicken and potatoes in a single layer on a foil lined baking pan sprayed with no stick cooking spray.
5. Roast for 15 mins. Then take out of oven and stir.
6. Cook for an additional 15 mins. Until chicken is done. Enjoy. Pair with a veggie to get those greens!!!
