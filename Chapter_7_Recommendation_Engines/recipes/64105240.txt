Title:
Beef burger with feta cheese, tomato, and spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1246456.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef (I prefer 90% lean)
1 green onion, chopped
1/2C baby spinach, chopped
1/4C tomato slices
1/4C feta cheese, crumbled
1 1/2tsp dill weed (optional)
1/2tsp salt
1/2tsp pepper

Directions:
combine beef, onion, spinach, tomato, feta, and spices in a bowl.
form into 4 patties
grill or pan-fry over medium high heat to desired doneness.
