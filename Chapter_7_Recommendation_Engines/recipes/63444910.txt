Title:
Sweet Potato Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Sweet Potatoes
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 teaspoon Tony Chachiere's seasoning

Directions:
Peel skin off sweet potatoes and cut into fries/wedges.  Toss with oil and seasoning in bowl.  Bake at 425 for 25 minutes.
