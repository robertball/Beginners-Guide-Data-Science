Title:
Cold Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can white corn, drained
1 can black eyed peas, drained
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup chopped celery
1 red pepper, chopped
1/2 cup green onion, sliced
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup vegetable/canola oil
3/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine all veggies and set aside.
In saucepan, bring boil, sugar, oil, vinegar and pepper. Cool.
Pour cooled liquid over bean mixture. Refrigerate overnight.
Drain liquid off in the morning and serve!
