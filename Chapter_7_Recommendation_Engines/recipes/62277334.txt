Title:
Eclair Cake modified with Kahlua and Bailey's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3.5 ounce) packages instant cheesecake pudding mix
1 (3.5 ounce) packages instant chocolate pudding mix
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
3 cups whole milk
1 (16 ounce) package graham cracker squares
1 (16 ounce) package prepared dark chocolate frosting
Kahlua coffee liqueur
Bailey's Irish Cream

Directions:
In a medium bowl, thoroughly blend the cheesecake pudding mix , half of whipped topping, and half of milk. Add enough Kahlua to taste.
In a separate medium bowl, thoroughly blend the chocolate pudding mix, rest of whipped topping, and rest of milk. Add enough Bailey's Irish Cream to taste.
Arrange a single layer of graham cracker squares in the bottom of a 13x9 inch baking pan. Evenly spread cheesecake pudding mixture over the crackers. Top with another layer of crackers. Add chocolate pudding mixture to next layer of graham crackers. Top with a final layer of graham crackers.
Heat up frosting for 30-60 seconds. Pour the frosting and spread over the whole cake up to the edges of the pan. Cover, and chill overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Yuh-Mei Hutt
Description: 
This is a very quick and simple no bake dessert cake. It uses graham crackers and a pudding mixture. It tastes a little like tiramisu, but there is enough for a crowd!! There is no baking involved or any power kitchen tools. Just a bowl and a spoon!Modified from: Eclair Cake
