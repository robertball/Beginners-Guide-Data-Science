Title:
Raspberry Coconut Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/674417.jpg
Ingredients:
15 frozen tart shells
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup coconut
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup raspberry jam
Icing:
1 cup icing sugar
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1-2 teaspoons water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350?. Line baking sheet with parchment paper or tinfoil.
In a bowl, cream together butter and sugar; combined eggs. Stir in coconut and vanilla.
Place 1/2 teaspoon of jam into each tart shell. Spoon over about 1/8 cup coconut mixture over jam.
Bake 20-25 minutes until golden brown. Let cool completely before icing.
In a small bowl, mix icing sugar, extract and water. Place into small plastic bag and drizzle over tarts. Let set and enjoy!
