Title:
Panzanella (Bread Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large loaf  of a good quality, crusty white bread, stale
1 large red-skinned onion
2 large tomatoes
2 handfuls basil
olive oil
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Slice the bread thickly and put to soak in a basin of water.
Finely slice the onion and tomatoes.(Some versions of panzanella also have cucumber, which you can add if you like.)
Add 2 handfuls of chopped basil to the vegetables.
Squeeze the bread dry. This is the most important part. The bread must be well squeezed.
Add the bread to the vegetables, plus olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Stir to combine all ingredients thoroughly.

Submitter: 
TINATED
Description: 
This Italian bread salad is easy to make and is delicious eaten straight out of the refrigerator.
