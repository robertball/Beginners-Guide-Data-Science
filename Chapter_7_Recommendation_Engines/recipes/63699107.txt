Title:
Ceasar Salad (Traditional Style) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves, minced
Sea Salt
4 anchovies chopped (or 1Tbsp anchovie paste)
1 egg yolk (optional)
1 tsp dijon
1 Tbsp lemon jc
pepper
dash tabasco/dash worchestershire
1/4C good olive oil
1/3C flat leaf parsley

Directions:
1. Crack sea salt over minced garlic and mix together to make a paste.
2. To the garlic paste, add anchovies, egg yolk (if using), dijon, lemon juice, pepper, tabasco and worchestershire and mix.
3. Using wisk, slowly wisk olive oil into the mixture.
4. Break up one large (2 small) heads of romaine lettuce and mix with torn flat leaf parley
5. When ready to serve, pour dressing over lettuce and mix well.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
Delicious - just like an italian restaraunt.  The flat leaf parsley is definately a key ingredient.
