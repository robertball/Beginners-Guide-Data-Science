Title:
Pumpkin Pie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Yellow cake mix
1 (29oz) Can Pumpkin
3 eggs
1 (13oz) can evaporated milk
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350'
Beat all except cake mix and butter until blended.
Transfer to ungreased 9x13 pan.
Sprinkle dry cake mix over mixture.
Pour melted butter over cake mix.
Bake in 350' oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour. No Longer.
Top each slice with whipped Cream before serving.
