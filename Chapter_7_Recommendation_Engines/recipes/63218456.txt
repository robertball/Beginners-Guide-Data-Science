Title:
Simple White Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup water
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups white sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup milk
4 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a large saucepan, bring 1 cup butter or margarine and water to a boil.  Remove from heat, and stir in flour, sugar, eggs, sour cream, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, salt, baking powder, and baking soda until smooth.  Pour batter into a greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (190 degrees C) for about 30 minutes, or until cake is golden brown and tests done.  Cool for 20 minutes.
Combine 1/2 cup butter or margarine and milk in a saucepan; bring to a boil.  Remove from heat.  Mix in sugar and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.   Stir in pecans.  Spread frosting over warm cake.

Submitter: 
kirklandcook
Description: 
This cake is great as a base for poke cakes.  If you leave out the baking powder, you can bake it in a 10 x 15 x 1 inch pan at 375 degrees for 20 minutes and it will work great as a sheet cake.Modified from: White Texas Sheet Cake
