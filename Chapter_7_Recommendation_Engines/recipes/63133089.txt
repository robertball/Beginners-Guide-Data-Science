Title:
Apple Sausage Mac~N~Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons unsalted butter
plus some for dish
kosher salt
3/4 pound medium pasta shells
1/2 cup diced onions
1 lb butterball turkey kielbasi or 6oz chicken-apple sausage---sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 tablespoon mustard powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 cup all purpose flour
3 cups apple juice
1 pound monterey jack cheese, shredded to equal 4 cups
1/2 pound extra sharp cheddar cheese, shredded to equal 2 cups
1/2 cup sour cream
35 small butter crackers (1 sleeve ritz) crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare pasta as directed, al dente. Drain and set aside
Meanwhile, melt butter in a large saucepan over medium high heat. Add the onion and cook until soft, about 3 minutes. Add the sausage, mustard powder, cayenne and 1 tsp salt to cook, stirring until sausage is golden, about 3 minutes. Stir in the flour and cook, stirring until lightly toasted, about 2 minutes. Stir in the apple juice and cook, stirring until thickened slightly, about 8 more minutes. Slowly stir in about 3/4 each of the monterey jack and cheddar cheese into the sauce until smooth, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in the pasta and sour cream. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with crushed cracker and remaining cheese. Bake covered until bubbly, 20 minutes, uncovered 10 minuted. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
My kids love mac n cheese, and this recipe is their new favorite, it is a weekly request. This has a light fruity flavor, it's cheesy and with the meat gives it a dance of flavor to the palette. Fun to make too.
