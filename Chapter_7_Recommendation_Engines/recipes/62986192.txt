Title:
Deluxe Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/746456.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter softened
1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon soda
1 teaspoon cream of tarter
Granulated sugar

Directions:
Mix thourghly butter, confectioners sugar, egg, vanilla and almond extract. Blend in flour, soda and cream of tarter. Cover; chill 2-3 hours or overnight.
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Divide dough in half. Roll each half into 3/16 inch thick on lightly floured cloth covered board. Cut into desired shapes; sprinkle with granulated suger. Place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake 7-8 minutes or until light golden brown on egdes. About 5 dozen 2 inch cookies.
