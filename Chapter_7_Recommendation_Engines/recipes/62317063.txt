Title:
Zippy Rice Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb turkey kielbasa or sausage
1 tbsp oil
1 package Spanish Rice
1 1/2 - 2 1/4 cups of water
1 1/2 cups frozen corn

Directions:
Slice turkey kielbasa or sausage lengthwise, then slice thinly into half-medallions.
Heat oil in a skillet and cook kielbasa for about 5 minutes, giving it a little texture.
Depending on what type of Spanish Rice you have will determine how much water you need. Following instructions on your rice package, add the water, rice and seasonings to the skillet containing the kielbasa/sausage.
Add the frozen corn, stir well.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and cook according to time on package of rice.
Remove from heat and let sit for 5 minutes. Stir and enjoy!
