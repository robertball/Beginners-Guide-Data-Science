Title:
Ratatouille with Polenta Rounds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
1 large onion or 2 small chopped
4 cloves garlic chopped
1 teaspoon thyme
1 teaspoon oregano
2 tablespoons basil
1-2 bell peppers cubed
2 large zuchini sliced
1/8 cup kalamata olives(pitted)
Handfull or two of small  tomatoes
1/2 large eggplant
2 tablespoons burgundy wine
1 tube polenta sliced into rounds
1 pound fresh mozzarella sliced into rounds
salt
pepper

Directions:
1.Heat olive oil in large saute pan on med-high heat.
2.Place all chopped vegetables into pan, cook for 5-10 minutes(until they become floppy).
3.Add wine,salt,pepper and herbs cook another 3 minutes.
4.Place ratatouille in a greased baking pan and cover with polenta rounds and mozzarella rounds.
5.Place in oven for 20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
6.Enjoy!
