Title:
Passover Sweet Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups Matzah farfel
5 egss, beaten
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
4 ounces margarine, melted
2 large apples, peeled, cored and grated
1/2 cup ground nuts
2 bananas (over ripe works best) mashed

Directions:
Cover farfel with cold water and drain immediately
Add eggs, sugar, salt and margarine.
Add apple, bananas and 1/4 cup of nuts.
Pour into a 9x13 very well greased glass dish.
Sprinkle remaining nuts on top.
Cook in 350 degree oven for 1 hour

Submitter: 
SANDI
Description: 
I usually don't like Passover kugels and my family only likes sweet kugels...this one fit the bill! If you don't have farfel, it's really just tidbits size pieces of matzah. I leave out the nuts, but every Passover recipe seems to have them, it's your choice!
