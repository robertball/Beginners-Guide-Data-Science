Title:
Pork Chop Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3501091.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fennel seed
1 1/2 tablespoons coriander seed
1/2 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
1 pinch salt, or to taste

Directions:
Cook and stir fennel seed, coriander seed, and peppercorns in a skillet over medium heat until lightly browned and fragrant, 1 to 3 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool briefly.
Combine fennel seed mixture and salt in a food processor and blend to a fine powder.

Submitter: 
lrhauptman
Description: 
Great rub for grilled pork! Use on pork chops, pork tenderloin, pork skewers. Great for grilling!
