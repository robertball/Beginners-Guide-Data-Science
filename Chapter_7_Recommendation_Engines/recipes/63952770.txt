Title:
CINCINNATI CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp whole allspice
1/2 tsp whole cloves
2 lbs ground beef
2 cups chopped onions
1 15 oz can dark red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 1/2 cups water
3 tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
3/4 tsp ground cumin
3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 oz unsweetened chocolate, chopped
12 oz dried spaghetti, cooked and drained
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. For spice bag, cut a double thickness of 100 percent cheese cloth into a 4 inch square.  Place bay leaf, allspice, and cloves in center of cloth.  Bring corner together and tie with a clean string.
2. In a large skillet cook ground beef until meat is brown.  Drain off fat.  Transfer to a 3 1/2 of 4 qt slow cooker.
3. Stir onions, kidney beans, tomato sauce, water, chili powder, Worcestershire sauce, cumin, cinnamon, salt, cayenne pepper, and garlic into meat in cooker.  Stir in spice bag.
4. Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 8 to 10 hours or on high-heat for 4 to 5 hours, stirring in chocolate the last 30 minutes of cooking.  Remove spice bag.
5. Serve chili over hot cooked spaghetti.  Sprinkle each serving with cheddar cheese.
