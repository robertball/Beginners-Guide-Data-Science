Title:
Flourless Protein Pumpkin Pancakes (Gluten-Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7640201.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg, beaten
1 tbsp vanilla protein powder (I used PEScience Gourmet Vanilla)
2 tbsp pumpkin puree
Small pinch of baking powder
Cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Heat a non-stick skillet on low
Mix egg and protein powder well, breaking up lumps
Add pumpkin, baking powder, and cinnamon
Pour batter about 1/8 cup batter into pan (use olive oil spray if you want them a little browned).  Tilt pan to spread out into a circle (about 4-5" in diameter).  Cook for 60-90 seconds per side, or until browned and easy to flip.  Makes 2 pancakes.
