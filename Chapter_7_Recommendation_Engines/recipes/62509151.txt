Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup sweet baby ray's bbq sauce
1 teas. mustard (I usually leave this out)
2 T. brown sugar
salt and pepper

Directions:
1 pound cooked and drained ground beef.
Add 1/2 cup ketchup and 1/2 cup sweet baby ray's bbq sauce, 1 teas. mustard, and 2 T. brown sugar
salt and pepper to taste.
If this is too saucy for you , cut back the ketchup by 1/4 cup.

Justin Will
Description: 
Mary's sloppy joes. Simple, but amazing.
