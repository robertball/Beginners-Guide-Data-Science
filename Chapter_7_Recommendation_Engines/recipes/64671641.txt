Title:
Creamy Cinnamon Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-  8 ounce packages cream cheese, softened to room temperature
1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1 pound powdered sugar (no sifting needed)

Directions:
Allow cream cheese to come to room temperature in a medium size mixing bowl. About 1 hour. Or softened in the microwave.
Add vanilla, cinnamon and salt.
Whip with a hand mixer 2-3 minutes on med-high speed until light and creamy.
Add powdered sugar gradually until all incorporated.
Whip with hand mixer 2-3 more minutes until light and fluffy.
Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 weeks.

Submitter: 
MissB
Description: 
One spoonful of this will change your morning bowl of oatmeal from boring to over the top.  Even if your watching your waistline, it only takes a dollop to work magic on oats. Just cook your oatmeal regular, steel cut, whatever your fancy. Then place this into your cooked oats, stir and enjoy.   You can use regular, Neufchâtel, or fat free cream cheese depending on your person choice.   It will keep in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 3 weeks.  Go ahead and add nuts, fresh or dried fruit if that’s your thing.
