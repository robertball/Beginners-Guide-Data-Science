Title:
Sweet 'n' Sour Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 Tomatoes
1 Small onion
½ cup White sugar or SPLENDA
½ cup White vinegar
½ cup Vegetable oil
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut the tomatoes in half and thinly slice.
Cut the onion in half through the root end, then slice into half-circles.
In a LARGE bowl, toss together tomatoes, onion, sugar, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper.
Serve at room temperature or chilled.
