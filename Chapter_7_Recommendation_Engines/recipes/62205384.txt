Title:
Ham, green onion and Potato Frittata with Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
6 small red potatoes, sliced
1 cup diced cooked ham
1 cup chopped green onion
2 tablespoons sliced sweet onions
1 teaspoon crushed garlic or garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
6 eggs
1/3 cup milk
1 cup shredded gruyere (or cheddar) cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Place potatoes, sweet onions and 1/2 green onions and garlic in the skillet, cover, and cook about 10 minutes, until tender but firm. Season with salt and pepper. Continue cooking 1 to 2 minutes, until spinach is wilted.
In a medium bowl, beat together eggs, 1/2 of cheese, rest of green onions and milk. Pour into the skillet over the vegetables. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook 5 to 7 minutes, or until eggs are firm on bottom. Then, put into oven, at 375 f for about 15 to 20 minutes til center is firm and top is cooked.

Submitter: 
Frenchy
Description: 
A delicious frittata made with potatoes,ham, spinach, garlic, green onions, and cheese. Serve with a fresh green salad and french bread for a wonderful french meal. C'est ci bon!Modified from: Ham, Spinach and Potato Frittata with Cheese
