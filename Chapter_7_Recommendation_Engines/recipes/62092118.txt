Title:
Something Different Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz can of tuna, drained
1/4 tsp of curry powder
1/4 tsp of poppyseeds
1 tblsp of mayo
handful of dried cranberries
handful of your favorite chopped nuts
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.
Serve on bread or eat straight from the bowl.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Melissa Rapoza Fragoza
Description: 
If you're looking for a different way to make tuna salad, this is it.  Curry powder, dried cranberries and nuts  accompany tuna in this quick and easy recipe.
