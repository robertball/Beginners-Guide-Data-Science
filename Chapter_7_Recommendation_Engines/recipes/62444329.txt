Title:
Strawberry Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460102.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
9 strawberries, rinsed & stems cut
1/2 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup sugar
ice

Directions:
Combine banana, strawberries, orange juice, vanilla and sugar. Puree until smooth.
Add as much ice as you desire. Puree until smooth.

Submitter: 
JK81908
Description: 
This smoothie is very easy and a great summer drink for those hot days.
