Title:
Sweet n Hot Salsa/Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large jalapeño, quartered length-wise
1 sm jalapeño, split in half
(remove only the top portion of the ribs and seeds)
1/2 red onion, cut downward into about 6 pyramids
2 large tomatoes, seeded; cut into 2" "sheets"
5-10 cilantro leaves
2-4T white sugar (depending on spiciness of the peppers)

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 450deg.  Put peppers and onion in a chef skillet and spray with tops of veggies with Pam.
2. Cook 15 minutes (tenderized but not fully tender).
3. Meanwhile, fry tomato sheets in olive oil until color has slightly changed, signifying removal of internal acidity.
4. In a food processor, mix all cooked ingredients, cilantro, and sugar.  It should resemble a corn relish, and should not be very wet.
