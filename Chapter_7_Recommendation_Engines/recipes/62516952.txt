Title:
HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH PRETZELS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. vegetable oil
1 pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch salad dressing (buttermilk)
1 tsp. dill weed
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 (18 oz.) bag broken pretzels

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients together; pour over pretzels and toss. Heat in microwave for about 3 minutes, tossing after about 1 - 1 1/2 minutes. Or heat in warm conventional oven for about 20 minutes, tossing about halfway through warming.
