Title:
Overnight Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18-20 unbaked frozen rolls
2 3-oz pkg regular butterscotch pudding, not instant
1 c. chopped pecans
1 c. brown sugar
2 sticks butter

Directions:
Divide rolls between two well-greased 8" round cake pans or one bundt pan. Pprinkle dry pudding mix over rolls.  Sprinkle brown sugar and pecans over that. Pour melted butter over all.  To prevent the bottom from getting dry, cover with a damp towel or wrap tightly with plastic. Let rise at room temp overnight.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.  Allow to cool for 5 minutes.  Serve by turning flipping upside down onto serving platter.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
These are prepared the night before, allow to rise, and bake in the morning.
