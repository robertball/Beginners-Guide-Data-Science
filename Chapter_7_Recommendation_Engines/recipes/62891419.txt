Title:
NUTS 'N' BOLTS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/748891.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boxes cheese bites
1/2 box plain cherrios
1/2 box plain sheddies
3 bags original bugles
2 bags cheetos
3 boxes crispex
2 bags short pretzels
2 cups salted peanuts,or unsalted
2 cups salted mixed nuts,or unsalted
1 1/2 lb.margarine
8 Tbsp.worcestershire sauce
3 Tbsp.garlic salt
3 Tbsp.onion powder
2 tsp.celery salt

Directions:
Divide all ingredients in three LARGE roasters, except margarine and spices.
Melt margarine and spices in a sauce pan,pour over each roaster.Stir well.
Bake each roaster at 200 for 6 hours.

Description: 
Great combination of cereals,nuts,cheese bites.... and spices.Christmas favorite.
