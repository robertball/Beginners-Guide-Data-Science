Title:
World's Best Super-Healthy Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, finely minced
2 carrots, chopped
1 cup, fresh spinach
1 tsp chili powder
2 tbsp ground cumin
2 tbsp taco seasoning
3 cups vegetable stock
4 (15 oz) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
125 grams, frozen whole kernel corn
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
2 fresh tomatoes, chopped

Directions:
In large pot, heat oil over medium heat; cook onion and garlic stirring occasionally for 5 minutes or until onion is softened. Add chili powder and cumin; cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add vegetable stock, taco seasoning, 2 can of the beans, corn, and pepper;
Meanwhile, in blender, puree together tomatoes, carrots, spinach and remaining 2 cans of beans until mixture is a smooth and even texture; add to pot and bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
Serve with dollop of vegan sour cream or regular sour cream. Top with sprinkling of pani puri masala if desired.
