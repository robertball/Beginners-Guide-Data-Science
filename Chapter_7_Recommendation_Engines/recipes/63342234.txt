Title:
Cake Batter Rice Krispie Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887671.jpg
Ingredients:
Rice Krispies
6Tbs. Butter
3/4c. Yellow Cake Mix
1/2tsp. Vanilla Extract
3c. Rice Krispies
1/2 (10ounce) bag of marshmallows
1tbs. Sprinkles
Frosting
1/2c. Butter (Room Temperature)
2c. Confectioners' Sugar
2tsp. Vanilla Extract
1tsp. Milk

Directions:
Rice Krispies
Melt butter in a large microwave-safe bowl for about 45 seconds. Mix in marshmallows and continue microwaving for 30 second intervals (stirring inbetween) until melted. Stir in cake mix and vanilla until combined. Add sprinkles and 2 cups Rice Krispies and stir with a spatula. Add remaining cup of Rice Krispies.
Fill each well-greased muffin holder with the Rice Krispie mixture and press gently with the back of a spoon. Cool for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
Frosting
Cream butter (you can use a stand mixer with paddle attatchment). Slowly add confectioners' sugar until well combined. Mix in vanilla and milk (and food coloring if desired).
Remove Rice Krispie Cupcakes from tin with a knife. Frost and top with sprinkles!
