Title:
Roti Flat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  cups bread flour
1  cup self rising flour
1  tablespoon baking powder
1  pinch salt
vegetable oil

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together. Add enough warm water to form dough.
Cover and let rise for 10-15 minutes.
Roll out medium sized dough balls into flat circles.
Fry in hot pan with very little oil.
Should only take a few minutes per side.

Submitter: 
hobbyzu
Description: 
These can be filled and flattened before frying. Can use with sweet or savory things. (Nov.2007) I occasionally add a little sugar to the dough.
