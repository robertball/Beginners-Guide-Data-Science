Title:
Baked Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
1/3 cup butter, divided
3 tbsp. flour
2 1/4 cups milk
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (use your favorite flavor variety)
1/2 tsp. ground mustard
3/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 can artichokes, drained & chopped

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, saut√© the onion in 3 tbsp. butter.
Add flour; whisk until blended.
Gradually add milk, stir to make a smooth blend.
Bring to boil, stirring over medium high heat until thickened.
Remove from heat; stir in cheese, mustard, salt & pepper. Stir until smooth.
Drain pasta and add to sauce, along with artichokes, stirring until coated.
Put into a greased 9" x 13" baking dish.
Melt remaining butter and mix with bread crumbs.
Sprinkle over casserole.
Bake, uncovered, @ 350 x 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Comfort food, with a great flavor enhancement delivered by the artichokes.
