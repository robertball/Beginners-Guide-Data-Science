Title:
Banana Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1/2 cup water
2 cups Old Fashioned oats
1 cup low-fat buttermilk**
2 overripe mashed banana
2-6 Tbsp packed light-brown sugar*
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla
For topping:
Sprinkling of brown sugar
1 Tbsp butter or 2 Tbsp cream (optional)
2 Tbsp chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Bring water and milk just to a boil in a medium saucepan.
Reduce heat to medium low and stir in oats, buttermilk, mashed banana, brown sugar, cinnamon and salt. Cook 5 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove oatmeal from heat, stir in vanilla and optional butter or cream.
Cover with a lid and let rest 3 minutes.
Serve warm topped with optional chopped pecans.
**1 Tbsp of vinegar added to 1 cup of milk and let sit for 10 minutes to make
