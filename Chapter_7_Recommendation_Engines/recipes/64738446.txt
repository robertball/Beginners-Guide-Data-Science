Title:
Chocolate Spiked Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb mild sausage
1 large yellow onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 14.5 oz. peeled and diced tomatoes in tomato sauce, undrained
1 14 oz. can dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 14 oz. can light red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
2 oz. baking chocolate, unsweetened, 100% cocoa, shredded
1 cup beef or chicken broth
2 Tbsp chili powder
2 tsp ground cumin
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp cayenne (I used 1/4 tsp)
4 tsp kosher salt
2 tsp ground pepper (I used 1 tsp)

Directions:
Cook meat in dutch oven until browned, drain and return meat to pot (I didn't drain)
add onions, pepper and garlic and cook until soft
add tomatoes, chocolate, broth, beans and spices.  stir well and let simmer over medium-low heat for 45-60 minutes, stirring occasionally
serve with toppings such as shredded manchego, avocado, crushed tortilla chips, corn bread and raw onions.
