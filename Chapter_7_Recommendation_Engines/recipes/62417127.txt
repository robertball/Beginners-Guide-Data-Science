Title:
Dilly Onion Salt Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843546.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag salt potatoes
2 tablespoons dillweed (dried)
1 small - med sweet onion (vidalia)
1 stick butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
boil water and add salt according to directons for making salt potatoes. while cooking, cut up onion into smaller chunks.
When potatoes are done, drain, add butter and dill weed and onion. Let cook on low for about 5 minutes. Pour into a bowl and serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Chriss Pooler
Description: 
This is salt potatoes with a twist, the dill and onion are a perfect blend for a delicious flavor, and so easy with a handful of ingredients! This is a must try for raves! I've had a lot of compliments on this recipe! Perfect for Picnics, BBQ's and even pot lucks! It's easy and delicious!
