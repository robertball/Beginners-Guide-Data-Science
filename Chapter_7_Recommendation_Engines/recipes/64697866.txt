Title:
Lentils and pasta (Hlalem) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5305419.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion chopped
3 cloves of garlic crushed
1 large carrot grated
1 28oz  can of crushed tomatoes
4 cups of cold water
1 19oz can of lentils drained and rinsed
1 tsp of paprika
1 tsp of cumin
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp of pepper
16oz   spaghetti cut into 1.5 inches

Directions:
Saute onion, garlic and carrot until soft.  About 5 minutes.  Add tomatoes continue to cook on low until carrots and onions are mushy (10 minutes). Add lentils, spices  and water. bring to a boil.  Add pasta, once it reaches a boil, cover pot turn off and leave on burner.  Let sit 15 minutes, stir, cover, let sit another 15 minutes.   stir and serve
