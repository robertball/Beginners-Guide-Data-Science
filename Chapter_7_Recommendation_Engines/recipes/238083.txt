Title:
Party-In-A-Pitcher Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
1 orange, chopped
1 small apple, chopped
1/2 banana, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup drained canned pineapple chunks
1 (750 milliliter) bottle Pinot noir wine
1 cup cranberry juice
1 1/2 cups lemon-lime soda (such as SpriteÂ®)

Directions:
Combine strawberries, orange, apple, banana, and pineapple in a glass pitcher. Add wine and cranberry juice; stir well. Cover pitcher with a lid or plastic wrap and refrigerate, at least 5 hours to overnight.
Pour lemon-lime soda into wine-fruit mixture; stir.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Wynkoop
Description: 
A sangria everyone will love! Mix the fruit. Add your favorites and share what you love. Serve sangria over ice, dividing fruit evenly into each glass. Ready as soon as 5 hours, but if you can let it chill overnight, it comes out even better.
