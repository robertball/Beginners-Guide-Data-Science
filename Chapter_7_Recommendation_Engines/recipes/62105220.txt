Title:
Grilled Banana Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bananas
1/4 cup vanilla yogurt
Cinnamon

Directions:
Cut the bananas in half the long way with the peel still on.
Grill each half, fruit side down, for approximately ten minutes.
Remove the bananas from the grill and arrange them peel-side-down on a plate.
Spread a thin layer of yogurt over the bananas and sprinkle some cinnamon on top.
Scoop the banana out with a spoon and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
This is a deliciously simple way to enjoy bananas gourmet style.  A perfect cold night dessert that simply melts in your mouth.
