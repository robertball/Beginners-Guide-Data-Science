Title:
Spiralized Zucchini Crust Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
750g spiralized zucchini
2 Tbsp olive oil, divided
6 eggs, lightly beaten
3/4 cup parmigiano reggiano
1 green onion, chopped
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200 degrees C. Lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.
Lightly boil zucchini. Drain well. Press zucchini into lightly oiled pie plate. Brush with oil. Bake in preheated oven until lightly browned, about 15 minutes.
In a large bowl, stir together beaten eggs, milk, cheese, onion, and pepper. Pour egg mixture into baked crust.
Bake in preheated oven until center is set, about 20 minutes. The quiche will be browned on top and a knife inserted into the center will come out clean.
