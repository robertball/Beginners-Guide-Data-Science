Title:
Cranberry 'Surprise' Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base:
1 cup all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons icing sugar
1/2 cup slightly salted butter (cold)
Filling:
2 eggs
1 cup granulated white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup dried cranberries
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup flaked/grated coconut
Glaze:
4 tablespoons icing sugar
2 tablespoons milk
1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a 9x9 inch pan and keep aside.
In a bowl, sift flour and icing sugar together. Cut in cold butter until crumbly.
Tightly press this mixture into the prepared pan and bake for 12-13 minutes or until light brown. Once done, cool.
In a bowl, beat eggs, sugar and vanilla until well incorporated and fluffy.
In a separate bowl, sift together flour,baking powder and salt.
Add this into the egg mixture and mix well.
Stir in walnuts, dried cranberries, grated coconut, and semi-sweet chocolate chips by hand into the above mixture.
Spread over crust.
Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown and firm.
Cool completely (in pan).
Mix icing sugar,milk and vanilla extract well and ensure there are no lumps.
Drizzle over completely-cooled uncut-bars (whilst still in pan).
Once the drizzle has hardened, cut into bars.
