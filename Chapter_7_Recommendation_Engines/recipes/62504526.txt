Title:
Nancy's Pasta e Pollo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta
1/2 c pine nuts
1 teaspoon sundried-tomato oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
~1/4 c red onions, thinly sliced
~2 cups smoked chicken, diced
1/3 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips
1 cup pesto sauce*
fresh grated Parmesan

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Toast pine nuts.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic and onion until tender, then stir in chicken.
In a large bowl, combine pasta, chicken, sun-dried tomatoes and pesto.
Sprinkle with fresh Parmesan and toasted pine nuts.
