Title:
Coconut and Chili Poached Mahi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1`lb Skinless Mahi fillet
1 thai green chili, finely cut
1 can coconut milk
1 lime, zested and juiced
1 small head bok choy, or nappa cabbage (ripped/sliced into small bites)
2 green onion/scallions
cilantro leaves for garnish (optional)
1/2 t diced ginger/ or candied ginger (optional)
1 t Sea salt
1 T Sugar
2 T flower & 2T salted butter for Roux (blond) or 1T Cornstarch

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine the lime juice, coconut milk, green chile, and sugar. Bring to simmer.
Meanwhile, you could start your roux (cook flower with butter until mix is lightly golden)
Cut fish into several portions
rinse fish and pat dry with paper towels.
Rub salt on fish portions, then place in coconut milk mixture.
Cook fish, covered, 3-5 min.
Uncover; gently stir in napa/bok choy.
Cook for 3 to 5 minutes more or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
Ladle fish with cooking liquid into bowls.
Sprinkle lime zest, green chiles, ginger and cilantro.
