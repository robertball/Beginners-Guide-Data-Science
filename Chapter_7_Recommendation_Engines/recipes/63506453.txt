Title:
French Toast Bake with nuts and blueberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 slices day old French Bread (can use Italian   Bread) 1 inch thick.
5 eggs
2 1/2 cups milk
1 cup brown sugar, divided
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg (optional.  I don't use it)
1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
1/4 cup melted butter
2 cups fresh/frozen blueberries

Directions:
Arrange bread 9x13 or 10x12 dish
Combine eggs, milk, 3/4 cup br. sugar, vanilla, nutmeg.  Pour over bread.
Cover and refrigerate 8 hours or overnight.
Oven temp. 400 degrees 375 degrees for glass pan.
Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking.
Sprinkle with pecans or walnuts
Combine butter and remaining sugar (1/4 cup)
Drizzle over the top
Bake uncovered for 25 minutes
Sprinkle with blueberries
Bake 10 more minutes.
