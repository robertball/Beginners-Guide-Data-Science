Title:
T's Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757956.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. melted margarine
1 c. sour cream
1 egg
1 can whole corn, drained
1 can cream corn
1 8 1/2 oz. box cornbread mix (Jiffy - blue box)
1/2 c. chopped chilis
1 c. grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix melted margarine and sour cream.  Add other ingredients and stir.  Pour into greased 9 x 11 in. pan.  Bake 45 - 50  min. at 350 degrees.
