Title:
Avocado Dressing For Salads Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2234280.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
3/4 cup sour cream
1/3 cup milk
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
3 dashes hot pepper sauce, or to taste

Directions:
Whisk avocado, sour cream, milk, lemon juice, celery salt, and hot pepper sauce together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
Linda
Description: 
I hope you people like this recipe as much as I do. It was given to me by a friend a few years ago when I asked for a dressing recipe for green salad. 
This is very smooth and fantastic-tasting. Store, covered, in the refrigerator. Use within a few hours to prevent discoloration.
