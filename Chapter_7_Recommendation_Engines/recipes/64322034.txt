Title:
Spicy Jicama and Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup(s) jicama peeled and cut into sticks
1 cup(s) tomato(es), cherry halved
4 tablespoons lime juice
1 pinch salt
1 teaspoon chili powder or more to taste

Directions:
In a bowl, toss jicama and tomatoes with lime juice, salt, and chili powder. Refrigerate overnight to let flavor develop.
