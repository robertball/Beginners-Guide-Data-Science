Title:
Make it a Meal Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large Idaho baking potatoes
Approximately 1/8 cup Olive Oil
Approximately 8 teaspoons of Coarse Kosher Salt or Coarse Sea Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Wash and scrub potatoes.
Using a fork, prick each potato several times on all sides.
Place potatoes on a baking sheet covered with foil.
Pour olive oil on potatoes and rub to coat thoroughly.
Pour approximately 2 teaspoons of coarse salt on each potato and rub to coat.
Place baking sheet and potatoes in oven and bake for 1 to 1/2 hours, depending on size of potato.  When fully cooked, potato will be soft when pricked with a fork.
To serve, cut potato horizontally and vertically and squeeze inward.  Serve with butter and/or sour cream along with your favorite toppings.
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Linda B. II
Description: 
When I make these potatoes, we turn them into a meal by adding our favorite toppings.  The olive oil and coarse salt make these potatoes to die for....even picky eaters will enjoy the skins.
