Title:
Deluca Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1 8 oz. can tomato paste
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 tsp dried basil
1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried parsley
pinch rosemary
2 T red wine
1 23 oz can tomato puree
1 23 oz can petite diced tomatoes
1 23 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 tsp salt
2 T sugar
1 lb. sliced italian sausage links,  pan-fried and drained
1 12 oz package dry lasagna (cooked in boiling water 6-8
min)
1 T olive oil
3 hard boiled eggs, sliced
1 8 oz carton ricotta cheese
10 oz motzerella cheese, sliced into 1/4 inch slices
1 c frozen chopped spinach, thawed & drained
1 c grated parmeasan cheese
1 T chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat 1 T olive oil in large stock pot.  Add tomato paste, garlic, basil, oregano, parsely and fry in oil for 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
Stir in red wine, tomato puree, diced tomatoes, crushed tomatoes, salt, sugar & sliced sausage links.  Cook on low for 2 hours.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Oil a 9x13 casserole dish with the remaining olive oil.  Spread 1/4 cup of the sauce on the bottom of the dish.  Line bottom with noodles, then spread evenly over the noodles: 1 sliced egg, 1/3 of the motzerella, 1/2 of the ricotta, 1/2 of the spinach, 1 cup sauce.  Repeat, starting with the noodles.
On top of next layer of noodles, spread evenly 3/4 c sauce, the remaining motzerella, and the parmeasan cheese.  Sprinkle the parsely evenly atop lasagna.
Cover with aluminium foil and bake for 45 minutes or until heated through.  Remove foil and bake 10 minutes more.
Let lasagna sit for 10 minutes before cutting.
