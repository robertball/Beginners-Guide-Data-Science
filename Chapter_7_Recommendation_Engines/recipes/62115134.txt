Title:
sweet and spicy marinated pork tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 pound pork tenderloin
1 cup oil (any variety)
1/3 cup cider vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons ketchup
3 tablespoons maple syrup
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 small onion (any variety) diced
1 banana pepper, seeded and chopped
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 gallon size freezer bag (such as ziploc brand)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients except for the tenderloin, and blend together well.
Trim meat of excess fat and place in the freezer bag.
Pour marinade mixture over meat and close bag.
Turn bag briefly, to ensure that meat is covered well by the marinade.
Open bag and squeeze out excess air, and reseal bag.
Place bag in refrigerator overnight or up to 3 days (the longer it sits, the more tender it becomes)
When ready to cook, preheat oven to 350.
Place tenderloins in a greased 13x9x2 inch pan and spoon extra marinade over each piece of meat.
Place meat in oven and cook for 50-55 minutes or until a meat thermometer reads 160 degrees when inserted into the thickest part of the meat.

Submitter: 
T. Kent
Description: 
This marinade makes the tenderloin so juicy and tender, you can cut it with a fork. The marinade consists of maple syrup, vinegar, chopped onions and pepper, and a variety of other tastes when put together give your mouth an explosion of flavor.
