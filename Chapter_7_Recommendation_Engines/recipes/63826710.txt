Title:
Hoagie bites with Marinara sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 premade biscuits
30 precooked meatballs
1 8oz block mozzarella cheese
1 egg
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/4 cup melted butter
Garlic salt
Marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven per direction on biscuits.
Defrost the meatballs in microwave for 3 minutes
Slice the mozzarella cheese in thin pieces.
Flatten biscuits, place a piece of cheese and meatball on each one, Wrap biscuits up and around and pinch biscuit dough to seal. Place in greased 13x9 pan with seam side down.
Beat the egg with the parmesan cheese and Italian seasoning. Use a pastry brush to coat the tops of the biscuits and. bake till golden brown.
Immediately after removing from the oven brush with melted butter and lightly sprinkle with garlic salt.
