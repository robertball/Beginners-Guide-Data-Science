Title:
Jalapeno Jack Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butter flavored cooking spray
6 strips of bacon, fried and crumbled
1/3 cup diced green onions
3/4 cup jarred sliced jalapenos
8 ounces shredded chedder cheese
8 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese
5 eggs beaten

Directions:
In a skillet, fry bacon until crisp. Drain on paper towels, and crumble.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly spray a 8x11 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
In a bowl, gently toss together bacon, green onions, sliced jalapenos, chedder cheese and Monterey Jack cheese.
Pour into prepared dish.
Beat 5 eggs and pour the eggs over the mixture in the dish.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Remove from oven and let cool for 10 minutes and cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Janine
Description: 
I usually make these as appetizers, but my husband likes them for breakfast. Either way, they are delicious!
