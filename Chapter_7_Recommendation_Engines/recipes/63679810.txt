Title:
Cheesy Garlic Red Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium red potatoes, quartered
1/2 cup skim milk
1 cup low fat sour cream
4 ounces low fat cheddar cheese, shredded
3 tablespoons chopped garlic
3 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes for 20 to 25 minutes, or until fork tender, in a large pot of water, sufficient to cover potatoes by 2 inches. Drain and mash.
Add milk, sour cream, cheese, garlic, and parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Transfer to a microwave-safe dish and heat in the microwave until the potatoes are hot and the cheese has melted.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Everyone loves mashed potatoes with cheese or garlic, so why not combine them in a tasty and healthier version of red mashed potatoes.
