Title:
Corn and Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salsa:
1 large can white shoepeg corn, drained
1 large can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 red pepper, chopped small
1 bunch green onions chopped
1/2 cup red onion chopped small
1 to 2 teaspoons chopped garlic
2 cans Rotell (tomatoes and green chili's, drained
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped fine
Dressing:
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
1 8oz bottle of Italian dressing
3/4 teaspoon Tabasco
1/2 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
Mix all above ingredients for the salsa together in a large container.
Mix dressing as follows, pour over salsa ingredients, stir well and refrigerate at least one hour or overnight.

Submitter: 
Tiffiney Launhardt
Description: 
This salsa is delicious with tortilla chips.  Anytime I make it for potlucks and events, it is the first thing gone.
