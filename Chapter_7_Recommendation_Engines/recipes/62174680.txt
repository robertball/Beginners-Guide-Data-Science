Title:
Lori's Pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Red Bell Pepper, Chopped very fine (about 1 Medium)
1 Cup Green Bell Pepper, Chopped very fine (about 1 Medium)
3/4 Cup Jalapeno Pepper, Chopped very fine (about 6 Medium)
1 1/2 Cups Cider Vinegar
1 tsp Crushed Red Pepper (optional)
1 tsp Cayenne Pepper (optional)
6 1/2 Cups Sugar
1 pouch (3oz) Liquid Pectin (I use CERTO)

Directions:
Remove stems, veins and most of the seeds of all the peppers.  Mince peppers in a food processor, or chop by hand until finely diced.
In a 5 qt pot over med high heat, combine peppers, sugar, and vinegar.  Bring to a rolling boil; boil for 3 minutes.
Stirring constantly add pectin while still boiling and cook 1 more minute.
Pour into hot sterlized jars and top with hot sterilized lids and bands.  Process according to canner directions.  Hot water bath, cover by 1-2 inches and process for 10 min.  Cool on towels..

Submitter: 
Lori L Dixon
Description: 
Sweet and spicy jelly great on almost anything, especially with cream cheese and crackers.Modified from: Lori's Pepper Jelly
