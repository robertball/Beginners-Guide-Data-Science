Title:
Cucumber and Blue Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/855665.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cucumber, cubed
1 tomato, cubed
3 stalks celery, cubed
1 (3.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
1/2 cup red wine vinegar

Directions:
Place the cucumber, tomato, celery, black olives, and blue cheese in a mixing bowl. Pour in the red wine, mix, and you're done!

Submitter: 
mommyof4
Description: 
This salad is very fast and easy to make and has great flavor. This is my favorite salad to have on a hot summer day. It's refreshing and I know that I'm getting a good dose of veggies.
