Title:
Almond and Oat Fiber Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites (or 1 egg)
1/2 cup kefir (a cultured milk product similar to yogurt)
1/4 cup oil
1 cup almond meal
1/4 cup flax Meal (Golden)
1/4 cup oat fiber (not the same as oat bran)
1/2 cup Splenda (or sugar if you would rather)
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees and grease the bottoms of 12 muffin tins. (2 3/4 inches in diameter.)
Mix dry ingredients in one bowl, wet in another.
Mix both together just enough to moisten.  Do not over mix.  Batter should be lumpy.
Fill muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until they test done.
Immediately remove from pan.

Submitter: 
Ginn
Description: 
These are very healthful muffins with almond meal, flax meal, and oat fiber.  Good for your heart and your stomach!
