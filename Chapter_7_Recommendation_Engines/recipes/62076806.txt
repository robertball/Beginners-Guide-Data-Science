Title:
Chicken Livers in Ketchup Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500g chicken livers
2 tbs cornflour
2 tbs (olive or eastern spiced wok) oil
sauce:
1/4 cup (60ml) tomato ketchup
2 tbs white wine vinegar
2 tbs oyster sauce or ketjap manis (sweet soya sauce)
1 tsp sugar
1 chicken stock cube, crumbled
1/2 tbs cornflour
3/4 cup (180ml) water or dry white wine
dash of worchester sauce (optional)

Directions:
1. clean and half the chicken livers.  place them in a bowl or clear plastic bag and coat them with 2 tbs of cornflour.
2. heat the oil in a wok.  add the chicken livers and fry until browned.  don't over-cook them!  the insides should still be pink. reduce the heat slightly.
3. mix all the sauce ingredients together in a bowl, and add to the wok.  continue stirring until the sauce has heated through and begun to thicken.

Description: 
a delicious and simple recipe for chicken livers.  serve with either rice or mie and steamed vegetables.
