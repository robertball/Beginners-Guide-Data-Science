Title:
Witches Brew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/913662.jpg
Ingredients:
4 small packages grape jell-o
1 32 ounce bottle of Sunny-D
add enough water to make 1 gallon

Directions:
Boil 4 cups water and add to the 4 small packages of grape jello in a one gallon sized pitcher, stir until the geletin is dissolved, add the 32 ounce bottle of Sunny-D, fill the rest of the gallon with cold water and stir. Chill in the refridgerator for 4 hours, stirring about every hour to keep the orange pulp from settling to the bottom.

Submitter: 
Kirsten Mia
Description: 
Halloween Party Drink - I was looking to create a drink that was delightfully disgusting that tasted good to the children... this was it!
