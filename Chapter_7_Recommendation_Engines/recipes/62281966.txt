Title:
Rudy's Guacamole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351890.jpg
Ingredients:
3 avocados – newly ripened, peeled, pitted
1/2 oz fresh lime juice
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup finely diced onion
1/2 cup diced plum tomatoes
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
3 tablespoons finely diced jalapeños (optional)

Directions:
Mash avocados coarsely first.
Add remaining ingredients and mix with fork until consistency is what you like.
Serve immediately.

The best Guacamole you'll ever have in its purest form.
