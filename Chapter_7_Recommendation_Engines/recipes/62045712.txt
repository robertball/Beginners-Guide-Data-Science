Title:
Spicy  Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs. ground beef (salt and pepper to taste)
1 pkg of spicy taco seasoning
4 cans of Hormel(hot)chili with beans
1 block of mexican (hot) velveta cheese
16 oz of sour cream

Directions:
-in large cooker, brown and drain beef.
-over medium heat return beef to cooker and add taco seasoning
-add hormel chili
-add cubed velveta cheese and stir until melted
-add sour cream
-heat until all ingredients are mixed well
-pour into crock pot to keep hot
-serve hot with tortilla chips
yum yum!

Submitter: 
Amy Clum Nash
Description:
