Title:
Mom’s Meatballs and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6033803.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef (option to mix ground beef with ground pork 4:1 ratio)
Small onion, chopped
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 c cream of rice (or wheat)
Half & half (amount??)
1 can Golden Mushroom soup
1 can Beef Broth

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine cream of rice and enough half & half to make a paste. Let sit for five - 10 minutes to thicken.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine ground meat, onion, salt, and pepper thoroughly  and add the thickened cream of rice mixture.
Roll into meatballs, approximately 12 balls per pound of meat.
Brown in a frying pan with 2 tbsp canola oil. When browned, place in roaster.
Prepare gravy by mixing soup. May add a little allspice,  onion powder or other preferred seasonings. Pour gravy over browned meatballs. Bake/roast at 350° for at least 1 hour.
