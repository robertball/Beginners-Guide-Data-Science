Title:
Sweet and Sour Chicken I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained (juice reserved)
2 tbsp cornstarch
3/4 cups + 2 tbsp water, divided
1/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
4 drops red food color
1 tsp ginger
1 lb, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes
1 cups + 2 tbsp flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
1 egg
3/4 cups water
1 quart vegetable oil for frying
2 green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces *Optional

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine 3/4 cups water, sugar, vinegar, reserved pineapple juice, and food coloring.  Heat to boiling.  Turn off heat.  Combine 2 tbsp cup cornstarch and 2 tbsp water; slowly stir into saucepan.  Continue stirring until mixture thickens.
Combine flour, 1 tablespoon oil, 1 tablespoon cornstarch, salt, white pepper, and egg.  Add 3/4 cups water gradually to make a thick batter.  Stir to blend thoroughly.  Add chicken pieces, and stir until chicken is well coated.
Heat oil in skillet or wok to 360 degrees F (180 degrees C).  Fry chicken pieces in hot oil 10 minutes, or until golden.  Remove chicken, and drain on paper towels.
When ready to serve, layer green peppers, pineapple chunks, and cooked chicken pieces on a platter. Pour hot sweet and sour sauce over top.
