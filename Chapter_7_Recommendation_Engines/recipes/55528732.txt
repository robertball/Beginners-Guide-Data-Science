Title:
Cheesy Sausage Zucchini Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Italian sausage
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup uncooked long grain white rice
2 medium zucchini, cubed
2 medium yellow summer squash, cubed
1 cup diced fresh tomato
1 can (8 ounces) mushroom stems and pieces, drained
1 cup water
1/2 cup long grain rice
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
1/8 teaspoon dried oregano
salt and pepper to taste
Shredded taco flavored cheese or Cheddar Jack cheese with Jalapenos

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, cook sausage and onion over medium heat until lightly browned.  Drain.  Stir in zucchini and summer squash and tomato; saute for 5 minutes.
Add mushrooms, water, rice, cilantro, oregano, salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil.  Cover and reduce heat; simmer for 20-30 minutes or until vegetables and rice are tender.
Top each serving with desired amount of cheese.
