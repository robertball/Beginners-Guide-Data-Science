Title:
Microwave Cinnamon Apple Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium apple(s)
1 pinch cinnamon, ground
1/2 teaspoon vegetable oil spread, butter-flavored
sugar substitute, to taste
3/8 cup(s) apple cider, drink mix, sugar-free

Directions:
Combine all ingredients plus 1 tablespoon of water in a 2-quart casserole and toss well. Cover surface with wax paper and microwave on high for 5 minutes or until tender, stirring every minute or so. Let stand covered for 2 minutes to finish cooking. Serve hot or cold.
