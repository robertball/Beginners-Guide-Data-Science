Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4496879.jpg
Ingredients:
8 bone-in chicken thighs, skin removed
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
2 Tbsp rice vinegar
2 tsp fresh ginger, minced
1/4 tsp red chili flakes
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 small onion, finely diced
3 Tbsp water
3 Tbsp cornstarch

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, honey, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, fresh ginger, garlic, chopped onion and chili flakes.
Place chicken thighs in crock pot and pour sauce on top. Cook on low for 4-5 hours.
Remove chicken from slow cooker and shred, set aside.
In a small bowl, combine  water and cornstarch. Place liquid from slow cooker into a saucepan and bring to a boil. While stirring the liquid, add cornstarch mixture and stir until thickened. Let boil 1 minute.
Pour sauce over shredded chicken and stir to combine.
Serve over rice with desired toppings.
