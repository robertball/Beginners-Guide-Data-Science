Title:
Creamy Mushroom, Spinach, noodles and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag extra wide egg noodles
1 14oz can cream of mushroom soup
1 14oz can chedder cheese soup
1 can 2% milk
1/2 cup grated cheddar jack cheese
1 cup fresh spinach
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large pot cook egg noodles according to package, drain leave in strainer do not rinse . In same pot add soups, milk and garlic blend well. when mixture comes to a boil turn off heat and slowly stir in cheese then add spinach stir well. add noodles mix together and top with grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Val-Flowers
Description: 
Creamy cheese, earthy mushrooms  and tender pasta  warmed together making the tastes of pure heaven!
