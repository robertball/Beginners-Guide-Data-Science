Title:
P.M.S. (pineapple, mango smoothie) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667626.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup chopped pineapple
1 medium sized mango
juice of 1/2 a lime
1 1/2 C vanilla yogurt
ice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender and whiz until it's nice and smooth and the fruit is fully blended. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
