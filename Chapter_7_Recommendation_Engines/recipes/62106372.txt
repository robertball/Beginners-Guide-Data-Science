Title:
Angie's Baked Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz pckge frozen Spinach/Cheese Ravioli
Large Jar of Spaghetti Sauce
1 tsp Italian Seasonings
1/2 tsp Garlic Salt/Powder
1/2 tsp Sugar
1/8 tsp Cayenne
1 Cup Shredded Parmesean Cheese or Mozzerella Cheese(Fresh or bagged)

Directions:
Boil water and follow package directions for cooking the frozen Ravioli's. Do not overcook.
Preheat oven to 350.
Empty the jar of spaghetti sauce into a sauce pan and cook over medium heat.
Add all seasonings to sauce and stir occasionally.
After draining the cooked Ravioli's, add them to the sauce, and poor mixture into a 9x9 baking dish. Make sure all Ravioli are covered by sauce, and top with cheese. Place dish onto the low rack in heated oven until cheese has melted, and for the last couple of minutes, turn the broiler on until the cheese is lightly browned and bubbly.

Submitter: 
BRANGIE
Description: 
Easy peasy, quick and cheesy! Full of flavor, this is a family favorite all year long. Wonderful served with a green salad and garlic bread.
