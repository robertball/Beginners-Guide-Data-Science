Title:
Zippy Italian Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  pounds sweet Italian sausage
1/2 pound hot Italian sausage
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 large onions, chopped
2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1/4 cups dry red wine
5 cups beef broth
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
2 zucchini, sliced
1 green bell pepper, chopped
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup pot barley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot, cook sausage over medium heat until brown. Remove with a slotted spoon, and drain on paper towels. Drain fat from pan, reserving 3 tablespoons.
Cook garlic and onion in reserved fat for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, wine, broth, basil, and oregano. Transfer to a slow cooker, and stir in sausage, zucchini, bell pepper, parsley and pot barley.
Cover, and cook on Low for 4 to 6 hours.
Season with salt and pepper before serving.

Submitter: 
Phyllis Trinke Funk
Description: 
Italian sausage, zucchini and spinach fettucine are featured in this beef broth soup seasoned flavored with red wine, basil and oregano.Modified from: Best Italian Sausage Soup
