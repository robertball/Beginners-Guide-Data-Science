Title:
Angel Hair Carbonara Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (4.8 ounce) package PASTA RONI® Angel Hair with Herbs
1 2/3 cups water
2/3 cup milk
2 tablespoons margarine or butter
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup frozen peas
8 ounces ham, cut into thin strips
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
In large skillet, bring water, milk and margarine or butter to a boil; reduce heat to medium.
Slowly stir in pasta, seasonings, and onion. Separate pasta with a fork, if needed. Return to a boil. Boil uncovered 3 minutes. Add peas; continue boiling 1 to 2 minutes or until pasta is tender, stirring frequently.
Remove skillet from heat. (Sauce will be thin.) Stir in ham and sour cream. Let stand 3 to 5 minutes for sauce to thicken.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
This delicious pasta dish is a whole meal in one skillet. Strips of ham and peas are served with angel hair pasta in a creamy herb sauce.
