Title:
Allouette Cheese Spread Knock-off Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz Cream Cheese
3/4 tsp Onion powder
1/4 tsp Garlic powder
1/4 tsp White Pepper
1/4 tsp fresh grnd Black Pepper
1/4 tsp Thyme (rub in the palm until powdered)
1 tsp Chives - chopped
~4 Tbls Sour Cream

Directions:
Mix Sour Cream and Cream Cheese until smooth
Add all ingredients and mixt well.
Refrigerate.
Flavor builds with time - Best made 3-5 days in advance.
