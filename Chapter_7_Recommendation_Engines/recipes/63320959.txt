Title:
Green Bean Salad with Walnuts, Parmesan and Mint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb green beans (young), washed and trimmed
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp white balsamic vinegar
1/3 cup chopped fresh mint
1/3 cup shaved parmesan
salt and pepper

Directions:
Cut the beans into 4 inch long pieces. Steam in 1/2 cup water for 2-3 minutes.
Drain and ice.
Toast walnuts in skillet. Transfer to mixing bowl.
Whisk together oil, vinegar, and 1/4 tsp salt.
Dry beans and add to bowl.
Toss with dressing and mint.
Sprinkle with cheese.
