Title:
Very Easy Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/253919.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (4 ounce) can small shrimp, drained
1/2 (12 ounce) bottle cocktail sauce

Directions:
Spread the cream cheese on the bottom of a 8 inch square serving dish. Layer the shrimp over the cream cheese. Pour the cocktail sauce over the top.

Submitter: 
Dena
Description: 
A very quick and easy (and tasty!) shrimp dip. Serve with a nice assortment of crackers.
