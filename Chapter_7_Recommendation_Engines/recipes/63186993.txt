Title:
Banana Split Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups graham cracker crumbs
2 cups white sugar
2 cups butter
6 pounds cream cheese, softened
4 cups white sugar
1 tbls. vanilla extract
12 bananas, sliced
1 (#10) can crushed pineapple, well drained
3  bags frozen whipped topping, thawed
3  cups chopped walnuts or peanuts
1/4 jar maraschino cherries, halved

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Melt the butter or margarine over low heat.  Remove from heat and add sugar and graham cracker crumbs. Press into a long cafeteria pans.  Bake for 12 minutes; let cool.
Cream together the cream cheese, white sugar and vanilla by hand then whip with hand mixer for an additional minute.
Spread over graham cracker crust and chill for one hour.
Layer sliced bananas on top of cake.
Mix together drained pineapple and whipped cream dessert topping; spread on top of bananas.
Sprinkle with chopped nuts, then with cherries.  Cover and refrigerate 6 hours or overnight.
Garnish with chocolate & strawberry syrup
