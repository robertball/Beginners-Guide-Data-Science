Title:
Chocolate Chimichangas to Die For! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Burrito size flour tortillas
1 regular size bag of milk chocolate chips
1/4 cup of white confectioners sugar
3 tsp of cinnamon
1/4 cup of powdered sugar
2 cups of vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
on a flat plate dump white confectioners sugar and mix in cinnamon
In a frying pan add oil a quarter full to pan on medium/high heat.
place a tortilla on a flat surface, add 1/4 cup of chocolate chips at base of tortilla roll once folding in the sides then roll tightly the rest of the way like a burrito.
Add to Hot oil and cover with screen or lid slightly off.
Use tongs- rotate when all is golden brown. This cooks super fast so don't let it burn.
once browned pick up with tongs and place in plate of mixed sugar and roll until all sides are covered.
continue repeating these steps until you make your desired amount of servings. This recipe yields 5 servings.
After all Chimichangas are cooked sprinkle with powdered Sugar and serve.
