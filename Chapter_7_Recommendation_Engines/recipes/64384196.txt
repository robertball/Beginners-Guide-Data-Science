Title:
Indian Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
• Cauliflower florets, frozen - 1 lb
• Carrots and peas, frozen - 2 1/2 cups
• Green onions - 3 stalks, chopped
• 2 cups rice
• Curry powder - 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp
• Turmeric - 2 tsp
• Chili powder - 1/2 tsp
• Coriander - 1/2 tsp
• Cumin - 1/2 tsp
• Salt - 1/2 tsp
• Garbanzo beans - 1 can (drained & rinsed)
• Cooking oil - 1 Tbsp + 1 Tbsp
• Raisins, golden - 1/3 cup

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Stir cauliflower, peas, carrots, and whites of onions into skillet to coat with oil. Add curry powder, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, cumin, and salt; stir to coat vegetables.
Cover skillet and cook vegetables over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are softened, about 15 minutes.
Stir in chick peas and cook for 5 more minutes. Sprinkle with raisins and greens of onions.
Serve over rice.
