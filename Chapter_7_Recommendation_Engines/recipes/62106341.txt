Title:
Käsespätzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Spätzle Noodles:
2 C. Flour
4 Eggs
2 Tbsp. Salt
~1/3 C. Water
1 Onion finely sliced
6-8 Strips of bacon cubed
3 Stalks Green Onions Sliced
1/2 C. French Fried Onions
2C. Emmentaler or Swiss Cheese grated
2C. Farmers Cheese grated
Pepper to taste

Directions:
1) In large bowl, mix flour, eggs, salt and water.  Stir well, working into a relatively thick, elastic dough.
2) Bring large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3)Spray the bottom of a grater with non-stick cooking spray.  Pour dough through grater and into boiling water.
4) Cook spätzle noodles for 4-5 minutes in boiling water.
5) Remove spätzle noodles from water using slotted spoon.
Meanwhile:
5) Dice bacon and fry until crisp.
6) Remove bacon from skillet reserving bacon grease.  Fry thinly sliced onion and sliced green onion in bacon grease until browned.
7) Return bacon to skillet with onions. Add cooked spätzle noodles to skillet and fry until hot.
8) Stir in grated cheese 1 C. at a time and continue to add cheese and cook until cheese is melted and evenly distributed.
9) Sprinkle with french fried onions and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Eurocook
Description: 
This traditional German dish is like an adult macaroni and cheese.  Homemade noodles, sauteed onions, and a rich blend of cheeses made this recipe irresistible.
