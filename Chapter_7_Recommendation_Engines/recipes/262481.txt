Title:
Miso Gravy (Gluten Free and Vegan) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4628009.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups water
1 tablespoon miso paste
1 tablespoon liquid aminos
1 tablespoon vegetable bouillon base (such as Better Than BouillonÂ®)
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon onion powder, or to taste (optional)
1/4 teaspoon garlic granules, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Combine water, miso, aminos, and bouillon base in a saucepan over medium heat. Sift cornstarch, onion powder, and garlic into the mixture and whisk until no lumps remain. Bring to a simmer; cook until thickened, 6 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Bethany Mathews
Description: 
This simple vegan gravy, made with miso, is also gluten-free.
