Title:
BLAT Pizza with Basil Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6837911.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cauliflower pizza crust*
¼ cup mayo
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon chopped basil leaves
¼ teaspoon sea salt
¼ teaspoon pepper
¼ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
½ cup fresh arugula and spinach blend
1 avocado sliced (optional)

Directions:
For basil mayo:
Mix together mayonnaise, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, and basil leaves.
Drizzle pizza with ½ of mayo mixture
Top with tomatoes and bake according to packaging instructions, minus 5 minutes cooking time.
Top with greens and sprinkle with bacon and bake five additional minutes.
Top with sliced avocado if using, and drizzle with balsamic reduction and reserved mayo mixture.
*For a Paleo pizza, use Paleo cauliflower pizza crust, try traditional cauliflower pizza crust, or cauliflower crust three-cheese pizza for additional flavor.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
Bacon, lettuce, tomato, and avocado combine for a twist on a grain-free, gluten-free pizza explodes with bright, fresh flavors.
