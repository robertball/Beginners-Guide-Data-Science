Title:
Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups plain corn meal
2 tsp. baking powder
1 c. margarine (2) sticks margarine
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 cups very ripe bananas

Directions:
Cream margarine & sugar, add eggs 1 at a time and beat.
Add cornmeal & baking powder together and beat in with sugar mixture.
Stir in bananas
Bake in pre-heated prepared loaf pan at 375 degrees for 1 hr.
