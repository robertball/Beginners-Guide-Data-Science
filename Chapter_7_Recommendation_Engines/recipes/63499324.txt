Title:
Creamy Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955367.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
3 Cans of Milk (using the can from the soup)
3 C White Rice, uncooked (not instant)
1 lb Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast, trimmed into chicken tender sizes
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Use cooking spray to grease a 9 by 13-inch pan. Pour in the rice and spread evenly. Top with chicken pieces. Distribute the chicken as evenly as possible.
In seperate bowl mix together soup and milk. Pour over the rice and chicken in pan.
Cover pan with foil and bake for at least 1 hour. Check rice for tenderness after 1 hour. If still crunchy, continue to bake for and additional 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alicia
Description: 
This dish is a great fix-it and forget-it-for-a-while dish. Dump everything together, pop it in the oven and let the oven do all the work. Kids love this creamy meal.
