Title:
Quinoa Breakfast Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
4 large eggs
2 T olive oil
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 chopped avocado
6 oz smoked salmon
1 T fresh lemon juice
1 T sliced scallions

Directions:
Cook quinoa.
Cook eggs, covered, in oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat, 2-4 minutes for slightly runny yolks.
Season with salt and pepper.
Serve quinoa topped with eggs, avocado, and salmon.
Drizzle with lemon juice and top with scallions.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This breakfast bowl keeps things interesting by adding color to your morning.
