Title:
Amy's Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. Uncle Ben's converted rice
1 14 oz can petite diced tomatoes with jalapenos
1 14 oz can chicken broth
2 T. taco seasoning

Directions:
Bring liquids to boil.  Add rice.
Simmer covered for 20 -25 minutes.
Fluff and serve
