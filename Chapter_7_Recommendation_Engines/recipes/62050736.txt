Title:
Comfort Taco Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 refridgerated pie crust
1 pound ground beef
1 package taco seasoning
lettuce
shredded cheese
tomato
black olives
sour cream

Directions:
1. Cook ground beef; drain and follow directions on package of taco seasoning.
2. Meanwhile, bake pie crust in pie plate according to package directions.
3. Once crust is ready, add cooked, seaoned beef. Top with favorite toppings; lettuce, shredded cheese, tomato, black olives and sour cream.
4. Slice like a pie and serve.

Submitter: 
morgens_aunt
Description: 
This recipe is a great alternative to having regular tacos. I didn't have the regular ingrediants for tacos so I came up with this recipe!
