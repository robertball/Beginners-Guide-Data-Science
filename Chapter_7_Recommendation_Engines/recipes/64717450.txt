Title:
Thai Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. creamy peanut butter
3/4 C. Coconut Milk
2 T. Thai red curry paste
2 T. Apple Cider Vinegar
1 T. Sugar
2 T. Ground Peanuts
Salt to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a saucepan on low/med heat.  Serve over Chicken and Rice or Noodles.
