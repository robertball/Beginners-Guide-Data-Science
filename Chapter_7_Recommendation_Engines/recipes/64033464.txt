Title:
Pollo Mechado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120975.jpg
Ingredients:
1.2 lbs of chicken breast
1 small sweet red pepper
1/2 onion
4 cloves of garlic chopped
1/4 cup of cooking sherry
1 tablespoon of capers
salt
1 roma tomato
1/4 cup of fresh cilantro

Directions:
Boil a pot of water and when it starts to boil put chicken in and keep in until cooked through (about 20-25 minutes). When done take chicken out of water and let cool.
While chicken is cooking chop onions and put in pan with olive oil. After a few minutes chop red peppers and add. Let onions and peppers soften and then add chopped garlic.
Cover pan and let ingredients cook and slightly brown. While that is cooking shred chicken with hands and then when all shredded add to pan and mix with onions, pepper and garlic.
Add sherry and capers and let cook for another 10-15 minutes. Taste for salt. After add chopped tomato and chopped cilantro and let cook for another 5-10 minutes.
Serve hot.
