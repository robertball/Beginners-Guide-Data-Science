Title:
Steak Dinner Veggies the Easy Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/926959.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound New Potatoes halved (or if large cut into bite size pieces)
2 Medium Onions cut into sixths lengthwise
3/4 pound Fresh Mushrooms cut in half
4 tablespoon Olive Oil divided
1 teaspoon Rosemary
1 teaspoon Dill
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/8 teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Add Potatoes and Onions to a baking sheet pan sprayed cooking spray and sprinkle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
Bake for 35 Min.
Remove from oven and add Mushrooms.  Then drizzle with 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil, cover with the Rosemary, Dill, Salt and Pepper and Mix well.
Coninue baking for an additional 15 Min or until the potatoes are done

Submitter: 
Howard
Description: 
Potatoes, Mushrooms and Onions are a staple with every steak dinner in our house.  This easy way to get all of these favorites on the plate with my steak quickly with minimal hassle and cleanup.
