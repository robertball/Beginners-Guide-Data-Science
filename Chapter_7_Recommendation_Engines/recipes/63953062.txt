Title:
CHOCOLATE CHIP OATMEAL COCONUT COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4544099.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, unbeaten
2 cups of all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt.
2 cups rolled oats
1 16oz. package of chocolate chips
2 tablespoons unsweetened  coconut.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F. Grease or line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
cream together the butter white sugar, brown sugar and eggs and beat until glossy about 4-5 minutes.
add flour and the rolled oats and mix.
add coconut and chocolate chips, stir until all is mixed in.
Drop by tablespoon full, or a medium size ice cream scoop onto greased cookie sheet about 1 inch apart.
Bake on middle rack of oven for 9-12 minutes.
Cool on cookie sheet for a few minutes then transfer to wire rack to cool completely. **see notes**
