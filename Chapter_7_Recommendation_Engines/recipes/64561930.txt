Title:
Big Ray's Mediterranean Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3477014.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups olive oil
1/2 cup red wine vinegar, plus
2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1 tsp sugar
1 1/2 Tbsp oregano
1 1/2 Tbsp basil
2 tsp salt
1 Tbsp granulated onion
1 Tbsp fresh minced garlic clove (or to taste)
1 1/2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese (or to taste) (optional)
1/2-1 tsp fresh ground black pepper (or to taste)

Directions:
Pour all ingredients in a large glass jar, and whisk or mix with a spoon until well combined or you can also blend in a processor or a blender.
Adjust all seasonings to suit taste, including the sugar and salt.
Let sit in fridge for a minimum of 2 hours.
Shake very well before using.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This recipe makes a ton of dressing but may be scaled down to half, plan ahead the dressing needs to chill for a few hours before serving, it will keep well in the fridge up to 2 weeks --- all ingredients may be adjusted to taste. (Note there is no cook time but I can't enter 0 minutes).
