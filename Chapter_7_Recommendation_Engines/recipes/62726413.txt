Title:
Chocolate Mug Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. flour
1/2 c. sugar
2 Tbl. cocoa
1 egg
1/4 c. milk
1/4 c. oil
3 Tbl. chocolate chips (opt.)
Small splash vanilla
Pinch cinnamon
Chopped nuts (opt.)

Directions:
Spray 3 ramekins with cooking spray.
Mix dry ingredients.
Add egg, milk & oil; mix well.
Stir in vanilla, cinnamon, chocolate chips, & nuts.
Microwave on high for 2 1/2 min.
Allow to cool a little. Good served plain, or with whipped or ice cream.

Susan
