Title:
Mocha Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/808772.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup hot brewed coffee
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Pour hot coffee into a mug. Stir in cocoa, sugar and milk.

Submitter: 
Johanna Adams
Description: 
Mocha coffee is extremely easy to make in this recipe. Just add cocoa, sugar and milk to hot coffee and drink!
