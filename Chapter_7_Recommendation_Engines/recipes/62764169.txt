Title:
Raspberry-Cranberry Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Raspberry Vodka
1/2 Cup Cran-Raspberry Cocktail Juice
1/3 Cup Grapefruit Soda

Directions:
Place several ice cubes in a large on the rocks glass.  Pour the raspberry vodka, cran-raspberry cocktail juice and grapefruit soda over them.  Mix slightly.

Submitter: 
Randy
Description: 
A subtle mixture of raspberry vodka, cran-raspberry juice and grapefruit soda poured over ice makes this a refreshing summer cocktail.
