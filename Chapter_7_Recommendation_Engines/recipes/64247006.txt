Title:
Joe's Crockpot Loaded Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 bags of frozen southern style hashbrowns
1-2 bags frozen chopped onion
1-2 pouches of Real Bacon Bits
1-2 cans Cream of Chicken Soup
3 small cans of chicken broth
1 pkg cream cheese, softened
2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Put potatoes, onion, and bacon in crockpot.  Mix chicken soup, broth, and cream cheese together in a bowl.  Pour mixture over potatoes and stir.  Cook on high for 4-5 hours.  Stir in cheese 10-15 minutes before serving.
