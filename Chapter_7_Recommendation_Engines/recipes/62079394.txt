Title:
gourmet macaroni and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz dry elbow macaroni
5 slices of bacon
1 large onion thinly sliced
8oz shredded mozzarella cheese
4oz shredded parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon cracked pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Cook elbow macaroni until tender,then drain. While macaroni is cooking, pan fry bacon in a large skillet. When bacon is done, remove from pan and crumble. Saute onion in skillet in 3 tablespoons of the bacon grease until soft. Dump macaroni into a large bowl and mix in bacon, onion, 6 oz. mozzarella, parmesan, salt and pepper. Stir until mixed then pour into a 9x13 baking pan. Top with last 2oz of mozzarella. Bake for 20 minutes until cheese is melted and top is lightly browned.

Submitter: 
paigekowolewski
Description: 
Gourmet macaroni and cheese without the cheddar. The sauted onions and bacon add a great touch for this twist on mac and cheese.
