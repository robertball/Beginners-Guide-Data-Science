Title:
Perfect Rack of Lamb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rack of lamb, frenched
1 tablespoon oil
1 teaspoon dried dill
1 tablespoon crushed dried oregano
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Heat oil in a frying pan on high. Sear the rack of lamb, fat side down.
Place rack of lamb, rib side down, in an oven proof baking dish. Sprinkle with dill, oregano, salt and pepper. Insert a meat thermometer into the thickest part of the meat.
Bake for about 15 to 20 minutes or until the thermometer reads 150 degrees. The meat will be medium rare to medium.
Remove the baking dish and cover with aluminum. Let the meat rest for about 10 minutes. Serve.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
A simple way to bake a delicious rack of lamb to perfection. Searing the meat results in a tasty crisp crust, while the meat inside is tender and juicy.
