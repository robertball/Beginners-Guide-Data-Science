Title:
Crustoli (fried dough) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/2 stick of butter
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
6 tablespoons sugar
1 lemon, juice and zest
1 jigger whiskey
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
Flour to make a dough

Directions:
Add ingredients in order given, Stir well
Add sifted flour until right consistency to roll
Cut in strips and fry in hot grease (mixture of oil and shortening)
Dry on paper towels
Sprinkle with powdered sugar while warm
