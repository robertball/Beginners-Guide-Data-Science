Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/69878.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
4 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
1 pound cooked and peeled shrimp

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, ketchup and prepared horseradish. Gently fold in the shrimp. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
THEMCRAES
Description: 
A great and easy addition to a cracker or chip. Every shrimp lover will have a cow when they taste it.
