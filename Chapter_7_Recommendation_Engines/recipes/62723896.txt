Title:
Eggplant Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant
1 onion, chopped
Can mushrooms or 1/2 # fresh
1/4 stick of butter
Salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste
Fresh tomatoes
Basil
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Peel and dice eggplant. Cook in salted water for about 5 min. Drain. Saute onion and mushrooms in butter. Add to eggplant along w/seasonings. Put in a shallow baking dish. Slice tomatoes on top. Sprinkle with basil and Parmesan cheese. Bake at 425 degrees for 20-25 min

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Eggplant, fresh tomatoes, basil, fresh mushrooms, and Parmesan cheese.  Sounds good to me.
