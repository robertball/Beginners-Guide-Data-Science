Title:
Berry Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c strawberry puree (I blended about 9 large frozen-thawed strawberries)
Juice of 2 limes and the zest of one (about 1/4 cup)
2T maple syrup
1T poppy seeds
dash of white pepper

Directions:
Blend together well.
