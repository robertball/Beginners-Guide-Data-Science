Title:
Grammy and Deeder No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2 cups granulated sugar
5 tablespoons cocoa powder
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 cups oatmeal

Directions:
Combine butter, sugar, cocoa powder and milk in a pot. Bring to a boil at medium-high heat, and boil for 3 minutes. Remove from heat and add oatmeal and vanilla. Spoon onto wax paper and let cool. Enjoy!

Description: 
Chocolaty sweet goodness! The kids in my family grew up on these cookies- they bring back memories of childhood!
