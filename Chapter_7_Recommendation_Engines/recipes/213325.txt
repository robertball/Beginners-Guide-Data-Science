Title:
Spinach and Mushroom Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990005.jpg
Ingredients:
vegetable cooking spray
10 eggs
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1 (10 ounce) package frozen spinach, thawed and well drained
1 1/2 cups shredded Swiss cheese or Jarlsberg cheese
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat the oven to 375 degrees F. Spray a 2-quart shallow baking dish with the cooking spray.
Beat the eggs in a large bowl with a fork or whisk. Stir in the soup. Stir in the spinach, 1 cup cheese and black pepper. Pour the egg mixture into the baking dish.
Bake for 35 minutes or until set. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Make this frittata ahead of time or just before you are ready to eat for a pleasing breakfast or brunch.
