Title:
Rhubarb Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/650378.jpg
Ingredients:
2  CUPS ALL PURPOSE FLOUR
1 TSP BAKING SODA
¼ TSP SALT
1 ½ CUPS PACKED BROWN SUGAR
½  CUP MARGARINE
1 EGG
1  CUP MILK
1 TSP VANILLA
2 ½ CUPS FRESH RHUBARB CUT IN 1 INC PIECES

Directions:
IN A SMALL BOWL COMBINE FLOUR, SODA AND SALT TOGETHER..
IN A LARGE BOWL, CREAM SUGAR AND MARGARINE. BEAT IN EGG AND VANILLA.  AT LOW SPEED, BEAT IN FLOUR MIXTURE AND MILK ALTERNATELY UNTIL WELL BLENDED. STIR IN RHUBARB. (BATTER IS VERY SOFT)
TURN INTO A GREASED 9X13 INCH PAN. SPRINKLE TOP WITH ½ CUP WHITE SUGAR MIXED WITH 2 TSP CINNAMON. BAKE 40 TO 45-50 MIN. AT 350 F. COOL COMPLETELY BEFORE SERVING.            ENJOY!
