Title:
Big Ray's Balsamic-Maple Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1510077.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large acorn squash, about 1 1/2 lb
1/4 balsamic vinaigrette dressing
3 Tbsp maple syrup
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 ground allspice

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.
Cut squash in half; remove and discard the seeds.  Place squash on cutting board, cut side down, and cut crosswise into 6 slices.  Place in large bowl.
In small bowl, mix vinaigrette dressing, syrup and spices until blended.  Add to squash, tossing to coat evenly.  Place slices onto foil lined baking sheet and bake for 45 minutes, or until squash is tender; turning slices over after 25 minutes.

Description: 
One of the ways I got my kids to eat squash as well as making a hearty side dish to use up natures bounty of all those Autumn squash from my garden.
