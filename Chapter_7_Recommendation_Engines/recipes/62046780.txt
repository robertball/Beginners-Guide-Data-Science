Title:
Best Green Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz. apple vodka
1 oz. triple sec
1 oz. Apple Pucker

Directions:
Toss equal parts of all ingredients into a shaker full of ice, shake and serve.

Submitter: 
Melissa Pietrosanti
Description: 
This is THE BEST green apple martini, the only recipe you will ever need for them! You will be very popular if you make these for your guests. A very pretty and tasty drink!
