Title:
Vidalia Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986324.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Vidalia or sweet onions, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup sour cream
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
10 butter-flavored crackers, crushed

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, saute onions in butter until tender. Remove from the heat; stir in sour cream. Spoon half into a greased 1-qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Top with remaining onion mixture and crackers. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Libby  Bigger
Description: 
Georgia is famous for the sweet onions grown in the Vidalia area. My family looks forward to the time these onions are available to use in salads and casseroles.
