Title:
Green beans with gargonzola and French's onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound green beans - trimmed
1 cup French's French Fried Onions
3 oz Gargonzola cheese
2 tablespoons olive olive oil

Directions:
1.   Pour olive oil into heated skillet
2.  Saute green beans under tender
3.  Add onions and stir
4. Stir in cheese.
5.  SERVE
