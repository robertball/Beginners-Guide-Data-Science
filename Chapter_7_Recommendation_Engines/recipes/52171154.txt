Title:
Simple Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) cans baked beans
4 weiners
1/4 cup molasses
1/4 cup chopped onions
4 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
2 tablespoons ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix baked beans with weiners, molasses, onions, brown sugar and ketchup together and put in a  greased casserole dish.Cover and bake for 3 hours or until thick.

Submitter: 
Michele
Description: 
This baked bean recipe uses canned beans instead of the dry type so it is quick and easy to prepare.Modified from: Simple Baked Beans
