Title:
Pumpkin Bliss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(28 ounce)can solid-packed pumpkin (NOT PUMPKIN PIE FILLING)
1 (14 ounce) can evaporated milk
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/3 teaspoon mace
1 cup sugar
1 yellow cake mix
3/4 cup butter or margarine

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together, except for cake mix and butter.
Pour prepared pumpkin mixture into a greased 9x13 in cake pan.
Sprinkle dry cake mix over the top of pumpkin mixture, and drizzle melted butter over top of cake mix.
Bake 350 degrees for 1 hr 15 min.  Check after 1 hour because in certain altitudes it cooks faster.

Submitter: 
tigershoes
Description: 
My mom made this dessert every fall and now my sisters and I make it for our families.  It is so easy and one of my absolute favorite pumpkin treats.
