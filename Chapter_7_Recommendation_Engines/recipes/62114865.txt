Title:
Gingerbread Apple Cinnamon Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 oz) Pkg Gingerbread Mix, divided
3/4 cup Water
1/4 cup Butter, softened
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 can (21 oz.) Apple Pie Filling
3 Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored and sliced
1/4 c. Brown Sugar
1/4 c. Butter

Directions:
Mix two cups of gingerbread mix with 3/4 c. water, stirring till smooth.  Set aside.
Blend together gingerbread mix, 1/4 c. soft butter, and brown sugar, cutting in butter till crumbly.  Stir in pecans. Set aside.
In saucepan, heat remaining butter and pie filling and apples over medium heat til bubbly.
Pour hot apple mixture into a greased 11 X 7 pan.  Next, spread gingerbread mixture over apples.  The top layer will be the brown sugar, butter, and pecan mixture.
Bake for 30-35 minutes at 375 degrees.

Submitter: 
Nancy Towne McDonald
Description: 
Perfect for Fall, this spicy cobbler takes advantage of both fresh and canned apples.  The gingerbread mix makes this a quick and delicious dessert.
