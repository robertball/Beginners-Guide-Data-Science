Title:
Dark Rye Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365418.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup rye berries
1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup rye flour
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
4 tsp. baking powder
1 T instant coffee powder
1 tsp. caraway seeds
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup soy milk
1/4 cup molasses

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Place rye berries in shallow pan, roast for 20 minutes, until slightly browned.  Cool. Grind to a coarse grind.
Change oven temperature to 400.
Mix whole wheat flour, rye flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, coffee and caraway seeds in medium bowl.  Cut in butter until it is the size of small chunks. Add ground rye berries.
Add milk and molasses, stir until combined. Divide dough in half.
Place parchment paper on a cookie sheet.  Place each ball of dough on, flatten in a circle until 1/2" thick.
Bake 12- 15 minutes, remove from oven.  Cut each circle in 8ths.  Serve warm.
