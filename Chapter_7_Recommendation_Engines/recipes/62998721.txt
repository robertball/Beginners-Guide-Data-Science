Title:
The Best Oatmeal Cookie Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/748032.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. allspice
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract
3/4 cup shortening
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
1 1/2 cups old fashioned oatmeal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In a medium bowl sift flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and allspice. Set aside
In a large bowl, cream the shortening, brown sugar and white sugar until fluffy.
Add eggs and vanilla until well combined.
Add flour mixture to wet ingredients and stir until just combined.
Add oatmeal. Mix well.
Place a 2 rounded Tbsp. of the cookie mixture 4" apart onto (2) ungreased or parchment lined cookie sheets.  About 6 per sheet.
Bake 10 - 12 min. or until edges are golden brown.
Cool on cookie sheet for 1 min. then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
These are the best oatmeal cookies!  With this recipe you can also add many different ingredients to them like raisins, chocolate chips, walnuts, etc.
