Title:
Curried Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz elbow macaroni
1/4c cold milk
2T curry powder
1/4c peanut oil
2T mayonnaise
1/4c mango chutney
1/4c rice wine vinegar
1-10 oz bag broccoli slaw mix
1/2c chopped scallions
1/4c chopped cilantro

Directions:
Cook macaroni in salted water until al dente.
Drain pasta and toss with milk in a large bowl.
Toast curry powder in a small dry skillet until fragrant.
Whisk in peanut oil and cook to color oil; let cool.
Whisk mayonnaise, chutney, and curry oil in a large bowl, then whisk in vinegar until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Add broccoli slaw, scallions, and cilantro to dressing and toss.
Stir in pasta and stir to coat.
Refrigerate until serving.
