Title:
Hunan Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound thinly sliced beef fillet
3 red hot chili peppers, sliced lengthwise,seeds removed
1 slice minced ginger
6 cloves of minced fresh garlic
Marinade:
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 Tsp cornstarch
2 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp oil
4 Tsp water
1 tsp cornstarch and 2 Tsp water for thickening
Sauce:
2 tsp dark soy sauce
1 tsp sugar
2 tsp rice vinegar
1 tsp rice wine
3 cups of oil for frying

Directions:
Mix the marinade.
Add the beef. Marinade for one hour.
Heat the oil in a wok, deep fry the beef till it's almost done, with a little pink color left.
Remove the beef, and leave some oil in the wok, about 3 Tsp.
Add the chili peppers, ginger, and garlic.
Stir fry for 30 seconds, add the beef.
Then add the sauce.
When it is well blended together, add the cornstarch with water for thickening.
Remove beef from wok.
Sprinkle with some sesame oil and chili oil if desired.

Submitter: 
IrvineHousewife
Description: 
My mother in law used to make this before she became a vegetarian. Her version does require a lot of oil, but I use just 1/2 cup for a quick deep fry,and it's just as great!
