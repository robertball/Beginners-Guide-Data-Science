Title:
Heritage Pan Simmered Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5185911.jpg
Ingredients:
chicken pieces (with or without skin)
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/4 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1 1/2 cup water
Dash of salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder
2 tablespoons oil

Directions:
Brown chicken in oil.
Add ingredients and simmer (covered) until tender.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This was a recipe in a family cookbook from my mother-in-law. It's simple and a quick way to cook up some chicken. I thicken the sauce to make a gravy to serve with dinner.
