Title:
Slow Cooker Red Beans and Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz smoked sausage (Healthy Choice)
1 medium red onion, chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
1 can kidney beans, drained
2 chicken boullion cubes (Knorr)
4 1/2 cups water
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp thyme
1 tsp cajun seasoning
1/4 c long grain rice

Directions:
Quarter sausage lengthwise, then slice into 1/4 inch pieces.  Put all ingredients EXCEPT the rice into the slow cooker.  Cover and cook on high for 4 hours.  Stir in the rice and cook one more hour.

Submitter: 
Peach822
Description: 
If you love the flavors of red beans and rice, combine the ingredients to make a filling and healthy soup!
