Title:
Easy Broccoli and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bag frozen broccoli florettes
1/3 c chopped onion
2 stalks chopped celery
1 T butter
1 can cream of celery soup
8 oz jar cheese whiz
1 bag Uncle Ben's precooked original rice

Directions:
Put butter, onion and celery in a large covered bowl.  Microwave for 2 minutes.  Mix in bag of frozen broccoli and microwave covered 4 more minutes. Mix in remaining ingredients, pour into a casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes (325 degrees for convection). Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Peach822
Description: 
Very easy broccoli casserole.  Can be made ahead and baked later.
