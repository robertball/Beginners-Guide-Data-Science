Title:
Asparagus & Zucchini Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 asparagus spears (about 6 ounces)
Prebaked flat bread pizza crust
1 cup thinly sliced zucchini
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
2 tablespoons (1 ounce) shaved fresh Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Break off and discard ends of asparagus spears. Slice spears diagonally, crosswise into 1/2-inch pieces.
Place a prebaked flat bread pizza crust on a shallow baking pan; top with thinly sliced zucchini.
Sprinkle with asparagus pieces; drizzle with olive oil.
Bake 10-12 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Top with fresh lemon juice, fresh thyme leaves, and shaved fresh Parmesan cheese.
Cut into 8 slices & serve.

Submitter: 
Kelsee Jaro
Description: 
Bake your zucchini to perfection with this recipe for veggie-topped, crunchy pizza.
