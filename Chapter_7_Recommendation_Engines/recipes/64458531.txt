Title:
Watermelon, Blackberry, and Mint Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c cubed watermelon
1 1/2 c blackberries
2 tbsp finely chopped fresh mint leaves
Juice of 1 lime

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine fruit and mint leaves.
Squeeze fresh lime juice over salad, gently toss and serve.
