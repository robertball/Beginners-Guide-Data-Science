Title:
Slow Cooker Apple Butter with Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pounds apples - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup honey
2 to 3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon allspice
juice of 1/2 a lemon

Directions:
Stir apples, white sugar, brown sugar, honey, cinnamon, allspice and lemon juice together in a slow cooker.
Cook on High for 1 hour. Change heat to Low and continue cooking until apples are easily mashed with a fork, 9 to 11 hours.
add more sugar by taste depending on what kind of apples you use!!  Taste it a few hours through cooking add sugar then if needed.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
This simple apple butter recipe for the slow cooker uses both white and brown sugar, as well as a good dose of honey for a different type of sweetness.Modified from: Slow Cooker Apple Butter with Honey
