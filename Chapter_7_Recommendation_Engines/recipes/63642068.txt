Title:
Dale's Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2389802.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3-ounce package of cream cheese
2 cups grated sharp Cheddar cheese
1 cup grated Monterey Cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
3 tablespoons mashed pimentos
1 teaspoon grated onion (optional)

Directions:
With an electric mixer, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add remaining ingredients and beat until well blended.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This recipe was handed down to me and then I revised it a bit.  It isn't your same old bland pimento cheese!
Try a scoop on a grilled burger or substitute for the cheese on your next grilled cheese!
