Title:
Hearty Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz can tuna (drained)
2 large tart apples cored and chopped
1 16oz can peas and carrots (drained)
3 sweet pickles chopped
4 stalks celery chopped
1/4 cup mayonaise
salt and pepper to taste
2 hard boiled eggs

Directions:
Assemble all ingredients except eggs in a large bowl.
Mix well.
Slice eggs and arrange on top.

Submitter: 
Dan
Description: 
Throw this together quick for a refreshing and hearty meal on a hot day.
