Title:
Twinkie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix
1 cup milk
5 Tbsp flour
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Bake cake as directed in a 9x13 pan.
Cool
Dissolve flour in milk
Boil, stirring frequently until thick
Cool
Combine remaining ingredients and beat until smooth.
Mix two mixtures together.
Slice cake in half and spread filling between layers.
Refrigerate overnight.
