Title:
Chicken Macaroni Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni (5 oz)
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (4 oz)
1-1/2 cups diced cooked chicken
1 can mushroom pieces, drained (4 oz)
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup (10-1/2 oz)
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp curry powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Stir together all ingredients and pour into ungreased 1-1/2 qt casserole.  Cover and bake for 1 hour.
