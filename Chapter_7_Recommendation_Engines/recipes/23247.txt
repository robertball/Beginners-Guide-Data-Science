Title:
Strawberry Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/555931.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package strawberry-flavored cream cheese
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the strawberry-flavored cream cheese and marshmallow creme until well blended. Chill in the refrigerator approximately 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Jodie
Description: 
This is a great fruit dip recipe with a strawberry flavor.  Very easy and always goes fast! Best with apples, grapes, strawberries or pineapple.
