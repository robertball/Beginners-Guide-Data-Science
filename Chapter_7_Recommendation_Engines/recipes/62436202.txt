Title:
Rachel Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 (12 inch) flour tortillas
1 pkg coleslaw mix
2 TB cider vinegar
1/3 cup light mayo
2 TB Splenda
1 cup Light Thousand Island dressing
3/4 pound low salt deli turkey, thinly sliced
2 cups shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
Warm tortillas in microwave to soften; keep covered with a damp towel.
Toss together coleslaw, cider vinegar, Splenda and mayo.
Spread one side of each tortilla with 2 tablespoons thousand island dressing. Arrange equal amounts of the coleslaw in the center of each tortilla. Place equal amounts of turkey and cheese over the coleslaw. Tightly roll tortillas around the fillings. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
