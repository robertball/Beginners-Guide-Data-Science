Title:
Candy cane sugar scrub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750766.jpg
Ingredients:
2 mixing bowls
2 cups of white sugar
1 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 teaspoons of pure honey
8-10 drops of peppermint oil
Red food coloring
Jar with lid
Decorative ribbon and card with recipe listed

Directions:
Put 1 cup of sugar in each of the bowls, add 1teaspoon of honey and 1/2 a cup of olive oil to each of the bowls and mix well.  Stir in about 4 drops of peppermint oil to each bowl.  Add the food coloring to one of the bowls until desired color is reached.  Layer the sugar in the jar alternating between red and white until all of the sugar is used.
Put on the lid and if desired write the recipe on a card and tie with the ribbon.
This is for the body not the face, also once used it should be used or discarded after 3 months.
