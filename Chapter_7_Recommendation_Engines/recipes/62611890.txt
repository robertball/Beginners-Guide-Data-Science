Title:
Parsley and Parmesan Crusted Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (12 oz) pork tenderloins
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup fresh flat leaf parsley, chopped
1/3 freshly grated parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper
1/4 cup dijon mustard
2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Spray a baking pan with non-stick spray
Remove any silver skin from pork
In a dish, mix bread crumbs, salt, pepper, parsley and cheese.
In a dish, mix mustard and garlic.
Place pork on baking pan, spread mustard mixture over the pork, then cover in bread crumb mixture.
Bake until thickest part of the tenderloin is 155 - 160 degrees and transfer to a cutting board.
Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
An easy way to dress up pork for your family or for guests!
