Title:
Amazing and Quick Roasted Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower
"Pam" brand - Olive Oil spray
Sea Salt and Fresh Ground Pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees
2.  Lightly spray "Pam" olive oil spray on large cookie sheet
3.  Wash cauliflower and cut into florets
4. Place florets on cookie sheet
5. Spray with a little Pam olive oil and lightly sprinkle sea salt and pepper.
6.  Roast in 400 degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes, turning florets over half-way through.
(Feel free to experiement with other spices like garlic powder, cayenne, herbs de provence, onion powder, etc. )

Submitter: 
VailValleyCook
Description: 
Roasted Cauliflower tastes incredible and makes a quick and easy, healthy side-dish.  Everyone that has tasted this has asked for the recipe...even folks who thought they didn't like cauliflower.
