Title:
Whole Orange Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
1 cup butter
1 1/4 cup sugar
3 large eggs
2 oranges (about 1 lb. total), ends trimmed, then cut into chunks and seeded
2 1/2 cups flour
1/4 tsp. each salt and baking soda
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 cup powdered sugar
2 TBSP plus 1 tsp. orange juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Coat a 10 cup Bundt pan with cooking spray. In a large bowl with a mixer on medium speed, beat butter and sugar until fluffy. Beat in eggs.
Whirl orange chunks in a food  processor until mostly smooth but not pureed. Add 1 1/2 cups orange mixture to batter and beat until blended. Add flour, salt, baking soda, and baking powder to bowl and beat until smooth. Spread batten in prepared pan.
Bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with only a few crumbs clinging to it, about 55 minutes. Cool pan on a rack 10 minutes, then invert cake onto rack and let cool completely.
Whisk together powdered sugar and orange juice in a small bowl. Drizzle over cooled cake. Let glaze set, then slice cake.
