Title:
Traditional Anise Pizzelles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/549236.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons vanilla extract
2 teaspoons anise extract
3/4 cup I Can't Believe It's Not ButterÂ®, melted
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 cup anise seed

Directions:
Preheat the pizzelle iron.
In a medium bowl,  mix together the eggs and sugar. Slowly add melted butter, vanilla, and anise extract, while mixing. Sift flour and baking powder. Stir in the sifted flour and baking powder, and then the anise seeds. Drop about a tablespoon of batter on each side of the pizzelle iron and follow the directions given.
