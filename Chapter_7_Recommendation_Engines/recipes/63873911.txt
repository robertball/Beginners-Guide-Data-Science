Title:
Slow Cooker Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. whipping cream
1 (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
6 c. milk
1 tsp vanilla
2 c. chocolate chips (recommend semi-sweet)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crockpot, cover & cook on low for 2 hours, stirring occasionally, until mixture is hot & chocolate chips are melted.
