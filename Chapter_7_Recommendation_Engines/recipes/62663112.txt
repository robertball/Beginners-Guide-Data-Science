Title:
Black Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/592957.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
4 - 1oz. squares of semi-sweet chocolate
1 stick butter
1/2 cup of evaporated milk
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup peanut butter
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups rolled oats

Directions:
Combine sugar, chocolate, butter, milk and salt.
Bring to a boil over low heat, cook 1 minute, remove from heat.
Add peanut butter an vanilla, stir until smooth.
Add rolled oats.
Let set 2-3 minutes, then spoon out on wax paper.

Submitter: 
Lee Ann
Description: 
These cookies are smooth, chocolaty, and just down right yummy! This recipe was found in an old 1965 cookbook, and has become a family favorite. We believe these cookies to outshine the ordinary No Bake Cookie.
