Title:
Red & White Cherry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3004971.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. unflavored gelatin
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 85g pkg. cherry jello powder
1 cup boiling water
2 tsp. lemon juice
1 20oz can cherry pie filling
1 envelope dessert topping mix (prepped with milk, as per pkg. directions)
250g cream cheese, softened

Directions:
In a small saucepan, soften gelatin in 1 cup cold water. Heat on low till dissolved. Remove from heat.
Stir in 1 tsp. lemon juice. Cool to room temp.
Dissolve jello in 1 cup boiling water.
Stir in 1 tsp. lemon juice and pie filling.
Ladle 1/4 of cherry mix in bottom of glass bowl. Chill to set. Leave remainder @ room temp.
Prepare dessert topping with milk.
Beat cream cheese into dessert topping.
Mix cooled unflavored gelatin into cream cheese mixture.
Spoon/ladle over set cherry layer in bowl. Chill till set.
Layer remainder of both mixes, saving a small amount of cherry for topping. You should have a white layer on top.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A family tradition for special occasions, this salad looks great in a clear glass bowl to show off it's red & white layers. Plan ahead, as each layer needs a bit of time to set.
