Title:
Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9" pie crust
1 1-lb. bag frozen spinach, thawed, drained
1 onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
6 button mushrooms, chopped
3 eggs
1 8-oz. pkg. cheddar cheese, shredded
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
2 T sesame seeds

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Combine spinach, onion, garlic, mushrooms, eggs, cheese and Italian seasoning.  Pour filling into pie shell.  Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
Bake 1 hour, 15 minutes.  Remove from oven, let sit 10 minutes before cutting into serving portions.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Spinach quiche with onions, mushrooms and garlic.  This is a family favorite that we developed over time!  Just add a salad for an easy meal!
