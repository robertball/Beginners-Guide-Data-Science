Title:
Extra Virgin Olive Oil Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/498301.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Extra Virgin olive oil
1 tspn garlic, pureed
1/2 tspn red pepper flakes
1/2 tspn dried parsley
1/2 tspn dried oregano
1/4 tspn salt, kosher
1/4 tspn black pepper

Directions:
Mix all together and let sit for at least 30 minutes for flavors to marinade.
Keep at room temperature. Serve with some french baguette.
