Title:
Salsa Roja Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe Tomatoes - deseeded and chopped
1 Onion  chopped
3 Serrano Chillies  deseeded and chopped (or to taste for heat by retaining some seeds) Serrano Chillies are medium to hot in flavour
1 to 2tsps Salt (or to taste)
3tsps Lime Juice (or to taste)
2 tbsp fresh Coriander (Cilantro) - roughly chopped

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients together well in a bowl;
Cover with clingfilm and refrigerate for a minimum of 2 to 3 hours to allow all the flavours to mature.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A great, tasty addition for fajitas,tacos etc. Adds a lovely tangy spicy edge to the main dish.
