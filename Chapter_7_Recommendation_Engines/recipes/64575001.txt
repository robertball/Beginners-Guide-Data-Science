Title:
Big Ray's Pineapple Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594202.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 cup chopped nuts
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 can (20 ounces) crushed pineapple, undrained
1 package (8 ounces) Neufchâtel cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
3-3/4 cups confectioners' sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cake ingredients; beat until smooth. Pour into a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Bake at 350° for 35 minutes. Cool.
For icing, in a small bowl, combine the Neufchâtel cheese, butter, confectioners' sugar and vanilla until smooth. Spread over cake and sprinkle with nuts.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This cake is perfect for serving to a crowd. It keeps so well that you can easily prepare it a day ahead and it will stay moist.
