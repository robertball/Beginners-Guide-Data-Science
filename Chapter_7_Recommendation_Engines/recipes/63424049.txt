Title:
Eggs in Stuffing Nests Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/926287.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. packed leftover cooked stuffing
2 eggs

Directions:
Scoop 1/2 c. stuffing and squeeze/shape into a doughnut shaped patty.
Place patty in a lightly greased frying pan.
Repeat with remaining 1/2 c. stuffing.
Cover and heat on low to medium heat about 5 minutes so stuffing gets warm.
Crack an egg into the middle of each patty.
Cover and cook on low to medium heat till egg is set.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Leftover bread stuffing is shaped to hold an egg and pan-fried for an easy and delicious breakfast. My favorite is leftover homemade bread and sausage stuffing from a holiday dinner.
