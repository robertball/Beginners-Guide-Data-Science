Title:
Tamale Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced*
2 cups frozen corn, thawed
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 1/2 cups plant milk
1 cup cornmeal
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced ripe olives, drained
2 T chia seeds or other 2-egg sub & 6 T water
1 (1.25 ounce) home made taco seasoning
1/4 cup nutritional yeast (opt)
1 cup salsa

Directions:
In a bowl, mix jalapeno, corn, tomatoes, milk, cornmeal, chilies, olives, egg sub and taco seasoning until blended. Transfer to a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. non-stick baking dish or one lined with parchment.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes. Sprinkle with yeast. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting. Serve with salsa.
