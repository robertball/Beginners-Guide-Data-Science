Title:
Mom's Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup prepared Dijon-style mustard
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/3 cup buttermilk

Directions:
In a small bowl combine mayonnaise, mustard, dill and buttermilk.  Chill for at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
CAROL46
Description: 
This sauce is wonderful with salmon patties or with baked salmon.
