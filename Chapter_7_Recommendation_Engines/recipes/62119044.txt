Title:
'To Die For Cookies' - Double Chocolate Chip Variety Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C butter, softened or butter-flavored shortening
3/4 C brown sugar
3/4 C sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
1 tsp soda
1 (3 oz) package of chocolate pudding mix
2 1/2-3 C flour
1 20 oz bag chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cream all ingredients except flour and chocolate chips
Add flour until dough is desired consistency
Add chocolate chips
Ball dough and place on greased pan, or freeze for later
Bake for 10 minutes
Note: If frozen bake for 12-14 minutes
