Title:
Decadent Ugly Duckling Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
1 box yellow cake mix. 15.5 oz
1 box instant vanilla pudding mix, 3.4 oz
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 can fruit cocktail, 14.5 oz
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 c. canola oil
1/2 c. chopped pecans
1 c. coconut flakes
Frosting
1/2 c. butter
1 small can evaporated milk, 5 oz
1/2 c. granulated sugar
1 c. coconut flakes
1 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Prepare 9 X 13 pyrex baking dish with grease and flour.
Mix all ingredients in the order listed in a large bowl.  Spread in pan to bake.
Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
For frosting; melt butter in heavy pan then add milk and sugar.
Bring to boiling, boil 2 minutes.  Mixture will foam up at the end of the 2 minutes.
Add coconut and nuts then return to a boil for another minute.  Spread on warm cake, frosting will set up in a few minutes.
