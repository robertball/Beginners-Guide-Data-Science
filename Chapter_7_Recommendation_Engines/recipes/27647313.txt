Title:
Italian Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 tsp. salt
3 cups all-purpose flour
1-1/2 tsp. active dry yeast
1 TBS. cornmeal
Glaze:
1 egg beaten
1 TBS. water

Directions:
Place all ingredients except cornmeal and glaze ingredients in bread machine.  Program dough cycle setting, press start.  Grease large baking sheet and sprinkle lightly with cornmeal; set aside.
When cycle, remove dough to lightly floured surface.  If necessary, knead in additional all-purpose flour to make dough easy to handle.  Gently flatten dough to a 10" circle.  Starting with one side, roll up tightly, jelly roll style.  Pinch seams and ends to seal.  Taper ends gently by rolling back and forth.  (Loaf should be about 12" long)  Place loaf on prepared baking sheet, seam side down.  Loosely cover with lightly greased piece of plastic wrap.  Let rise in warm draft free place for 45 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400.  Combine egg and water in small bowl.  Cut 4 -5 diagonal slashes, each about 3" long, in top of dough using sharp knife.  Brush dough with glaze.  Bake 25-35 minuted.  Remove from pan and cool on wire rack.
