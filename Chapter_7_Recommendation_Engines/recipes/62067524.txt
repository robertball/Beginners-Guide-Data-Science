Title:
Coney Island Hot Dog Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Ground Round
3 Cups Water
1 - 12 oz. can of tomato paste
1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
1/2 Cup Chopped Onion
4 teaspoons chili powder (up to 6)
2 Tablespoons prepared mustard
2 Tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Cook meat in a large skillet over medium heat until brown, stirring to crumble.  Add water and remaining ingredients; stir well and bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer mixture, uncovered, 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
OLIVIAC15163
Description: 
This recipe is quick and easy.  Only have to simmer for 30 minutes!  It started as a very saucey coney island hot dog sauce.  I prefer a meatier chili sauce, but if you prefer it saucey with less meat, reduce ground round to 1/2 lb meat.
