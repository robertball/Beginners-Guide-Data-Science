Title:
Heath Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Saltine crackers, approximately 24
1c brown sugar
2 sticks butter
12 oz semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Line 9x13 pan with foil and lay single layer of saltines over foil.
Boil brown sugar and butter for 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
Pour over crackers and bake at 350 for 5 minutes.
Sprinkle chocolate chips over top and sit for 2 minutes.
Spread chocolate with knife.
Freeze for 1 hour and crack into pieces.
