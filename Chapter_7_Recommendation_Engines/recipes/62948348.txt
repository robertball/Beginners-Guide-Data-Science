Title:
Creamy Chicken Pasta Roni Clone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoons grated onion
1 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup half-and-half
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 pinch dried thyme
1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
6 to 8 oz dried fetuccini or linguine pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, melt butter and stir in the flour, salt and white pepper.
Add cold half-and-half and COLD chicken broth all at once. Stir well.
Cook, stirring frequently, at medium heat until thick. Remove from heat and stir in seasoning.
Transfer hot pasta to skillet and stir in enough sauce to nicely coat.  Mix thoroughly, garnish with dried parsley, and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
For a variation on a classic Sauce Bechamel, try chicken broth and half-and-half together with a roux of butter and flour flavored with grated onion. White pepper, thyme and cayenne give an earthy bite to the sauce.Modified from: Bechamel Sauce
