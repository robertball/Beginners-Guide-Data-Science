Title:
Tzatziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/774541.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces plain yogurt/sour cream
2 tsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
2 tbsp. chopped fresh dill
3 cloves pressed garlic
Just under 1/4 cup finely chopped cucumber

Directions:
Combine ingredients in bowl and enjoy with a souvlaki chicken pita or salad.
