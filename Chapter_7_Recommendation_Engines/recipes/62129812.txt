Title:
Roz Jerby (Tunisian Jerba Rice) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of frozen spinach
2 clumps of parsley, chopped
4 onions, chopped and cubed
4 cups of rice,
2 whole chicken breast, cubed
1 1/2 cans tomato paste
2 1/2 tblsp salt
1 tblsp. black pepper
1/2 tblsp. red cayenne pepper
1/4 tblsp. turmeric powder
2 tblsp. crushed dried mint
3/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
mix spinach, parsley, and onion together in large bowl.
Place rice in seperate bowl and cover with water for 10 minutes, then drain.
Add rice, chicken, tomato paste, and the remaining ingredients to the spinach mix.
Mix everything together well.
Place mixture in a steamer. Make sure to place the pieces of chicken at the bottom of the steamer, with the rest of the rice mixture covering it. Let steam for about two hours or until the rice, chicken, and onions are cooked.
