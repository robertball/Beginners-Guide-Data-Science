Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Burrito Bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1 1/2 pounds chicken breasts
14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
1 1/4 cup chicken stock
2 tsp chili powder
2 tsp salt
1 tsp cumin
1 cup brown rice
15 oz can black beans
1 cup frozen corn
Optional Toppings
shredded cheese
cilantro
sour cream
avocado
salsa
diced green onions

Directions:
Combine chicken, diced tomatoes and 1/2 of chicken stock, chili powder, salt, and cumin in bowl of slow cooker.  Make sure the chicken is covered.  Cook on low 3-4 hours.
Remove lid and add rice, beans and corn and remaining chicken stock.  Cook on low additional 3-4 hours.  Check rice periodically in last hour of cooking, stirring once or twice to make sure rice cooks evenly.  If the mixture seems dry, add more chicken stock.  Cooking is done when the rice is tender.
Shred the chicken using 2 forks.  Taste dish and season to taste.  Serve in bowls with desired toppings.
