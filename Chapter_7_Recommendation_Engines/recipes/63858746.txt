Title:
Honey Whole Wheat Pancake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups whole wheat flour
3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
1/2 cup granulated honey (available online)
2 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
4 teaspoons baking soda
4 teaspoons salt
3 cups powdered milk

Directions:
Whisk whole wheat flour, unbleached flour, honey granules, baking powder, baking soda, powdered milk, and salt together in a bowl; store in an airtight container.
To prepare: 1 1/4 cup mix, 1 cup water, 1 egg, two tablespoons oil or melted butter.

Submitter: 
PolyTheWicked
Description: 
Keep a container of this handy homemade baking mix in your pantry for quick pancakes or waffles anytime you get a craving.Modified from: Whole Wheat Pancake Mix
