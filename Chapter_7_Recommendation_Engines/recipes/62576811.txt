Title:
Spicy Sausage with Greenbeans & Kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/546015.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb bulk spicy sausage
1 onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 lb white mushrooms, trimmed and quartered
1 1/2 pounds green beans, trimmed and sliced into 1" pieces
sea salt & cracked pepper
1/4 cup white wine
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 bunch kale, rinsed, stemmed, and roughly chopped
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown sausage in a dutch oven or heavy bottom skillet.
Remove browned meat with a slotted spoon, set aside.
To oil left in pan, add onions & garlic, cook until translucent, about 4 minutes.
Add green beans, season lightly with salt & pepper, cook for 3 to 4 minutes, stirring.
Add mushrooms & pepper flakes, cook while stirring 2 minutes.
Add the wine and continue cooking until the green beans are almost tender, about 5 minutes.
Add the kale and top with the browned sausage, cover and cook another 3 to 5 minutes, or until kale is wilted. Add the cheese and toss until mixed well.  Serve alone or over pasta.
