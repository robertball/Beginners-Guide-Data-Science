Title:
A Mint Triple Chocolate Fudge Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510688.jpg
Ingredients:
cookie:
1 box triple chocolate fudge cake mix
2 eggs
1/3 c oil
2 T water
frosting:
2 cups powdered sugar
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup melted mint chips

Directions:
cookie: mix all ingredients in a mixer. Use a cookie scoop to make rounded mounds on cookie sheet, wet a rubber spatula and lightly compress. bake at 350 for 9 min or until top slightly cracks.
frosting:
cream butter and 1 cup powdered sugar, then add melted mint chips, mix, add remaining powdered sugar and incorporate. use high setting and beat for 4-5 mins until fluffy.
allow cookies to cool and frost
