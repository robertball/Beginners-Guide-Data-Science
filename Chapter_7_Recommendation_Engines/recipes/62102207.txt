Title:
Scrumptious Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 3oz. pkg. cream cheese, low fat if des.
1/2 cup Miracle Whip (not mayo)
1-4 oz. can med. shrimp, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped green onions-tops included
1-1/2 tsps. lemon juice
dash or two of worchestershire
a pinch of garlic powder
a little salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Take cream cheese out and let come to room temp.
Cream together cream cheese and Miracle Whip until smooth.
Stir in chopped celery, green onions and tops, lemon juice and worchestershire, salt and pepper to your taste - a pinch of each will do just fine. Mix well.
Chill at least 8 hrs. or overnight for best flavor.
Serve with an assortment of buttery type crackers.
NOTE:  recipe says ready in 20 min, but waiting really enhances all the flavor.

Submitter: 
SheasLounge
Description: 
A rich cream cheese based dip loaded with shrimp, celery, green onions and zested up with Worchestershire and lemon juice. 
Best served with rich buttery crackers.
