Title:
Winston Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970217.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger amaretto (almond flavored liqueur)
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger whiskey
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle chilled lemon-lime soda per serving

Directions:
Pour the amaretto and whiskey into a large chilled glass, or a glass of ice cubes. Top off with lemon-lime soda. Stir, and serve.

Submitter: 
KIRALEWIS
Description: 
My college roommate and I invented this drink in an attempt to avoid studying.  The drink is named in honor of our dorm.  Luckily we both graduated anyway, but we wound up with a darn good drink in the process.
