Title:
Camel Concoction Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ice
24 oz of citrus soda (mt. dew, moon mist etc)
6 oz vodka
grenadine

Directions:
1. Place ice in 4 high-ball glasses.
2. add 1 1/2 oz of vodka to each glass
3. fill glasses with citrus soda until almost full.
4. Add a splash of grenadine in each glass, stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jen Camilleri
Description: 
This is a fun fruity tasting drink which gives a nice energy boost as well!
