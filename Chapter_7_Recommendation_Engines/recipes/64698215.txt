Title:
New England Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 10 3/4 oz. cans  cream of potato soup
3 10 1/2 oz. cans  of new england clam chowder
3 cans minced clams
1 large onion
2 quarts half and half
2 Tsp unsalted  butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200 degrees
Melt 2 Tbsp in soup pot
Put chopped onion and  minced clams in pot and cook till softened, about 10 min. medium heat.
Add all soups and simmer for about 10 min.Take pot off heat and add half and half.
Bake in preheated for 2 hours.
ENJOY!!!!
