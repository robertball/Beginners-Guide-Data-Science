Title:
The Red-Headed Step Child Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/971333.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint beer
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger whiskey
1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger grenadine syrup

Directions:
Pour one pint glass of beer. Measure a shot of whiskey, and pour in. Top with 1/2 shot of grenadine.

Submitter: 
Amalia
Description: 
My friends and I came up with this drink about four years ago. We have had it at every party or BBQ ever since.
