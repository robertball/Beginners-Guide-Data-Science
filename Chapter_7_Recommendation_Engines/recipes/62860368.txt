Title:
Grilled chicken strawberry and avocado salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium boneless chicken breast
Marinade for chicken:
3T. of Teriyaki sauce
1/4 cup of Italian salad dressing
Salad Ingredients:
2 cups fresh strawberries hulled and sliced
2 cups baby spinach torn into bite size pieces
2 cups of torn Romaine pieces
1 ripe but firm avocado, pitted and sliced into bite sized pieces
Â½ cup of pecans toasted
Poppy seed dressing:
2 tablespoons of honey
2 teaspoons of sugar
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon grated onion
1/2 cup olive oil
1-1/2 teaspoons poppy seeds

Directions:
Make dressing in a blender or salad dressing shaker and keep refrigerated for at least an hour before serving.
Place chicken in zip-lock bag or sealed container with marinade for at least 30 min. to one hour before grilling.
Preheat the grill for medium/high heat.
Oil the grill grate. Grill chicken approx. 5-8 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear. Remove from heat let cool and cut into bite sized pieces.
Toss together chicken, spinach and Romaine.
Gently stir in strawberries and avocados slices.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Top salad with pecans and dressing when serving.

Submitter: 
*Sherri*
Description: 
Wonderful summer salad that is a meal all in one with grilled chicken, strawberries, avocado, spinach and Romaine topped with a sweet poppy seed dressing.
