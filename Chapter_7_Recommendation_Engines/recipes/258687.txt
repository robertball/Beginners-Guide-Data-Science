Title:
Vanilla Blueberry Blended Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4533200.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Gevalia® Cold Brew Concentrate - Vanilla
1/2 cup milk
1 cup frozen wild blueberries
1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste

Directions:
Place cold brew concentrate, milk, blueberries, and sugar in a blender and blend until smooth, about 20 seconds. Pour into a glass.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Baker
Description: 
Gevalia® Cold Brew Concentrate and blueberries blend together to form a delicious drink that's great anytime!
