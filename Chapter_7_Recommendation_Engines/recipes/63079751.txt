Title:
Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777876.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
3 tbsp chopped fresh basil
2 cloves of garlic, minced
7 plum or Roma tomatoes, chopped
1 baguette cut 1/2" thick, or a bag of crostini garlic toast
1 pkg Philadelphia Italian Cheese & Herb Creme or Aloulette garlic & herb cheese

Directions:
Mix together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil and garlic. Stir in chopped tomatoes. Salt & pepper to taste.
Cover & refrigerate for 20-30 minutes
Preheat oven to 350
Spread the bread slices in single layer on cookie sheet
Bake bread until golden brown, about 10 minutes
After bread has cooled, spread the cheese evenly over each slice
Spoon tomato mixture on top and enjoy!
