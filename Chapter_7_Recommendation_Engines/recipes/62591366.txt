Title:
Grandma's Old Fashioned Steak Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871690.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef
2 sticks margarine
1 cup flour
24 ounces water
1 large carrot - diced
1 medium onion - diced
1 stalk celery - diced
2 medium potatoes - cut into small pieces
1 package frozen mixed vegetables
1 (15 ounce) can tomatoes
4 Tbsp beef boullion granules
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Melt margarine, add flour and gradually add 2 cups of water. Stir until smooth. Add all other ingredients, except ground beef. In separate pan, saute meat in 2 Tbsp margarine. Drain, add meat to first mixture. Simmer about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
J Scholfield
Description: 
My Mother-in-law made this soup for my husband when he was a kid. The recipe is so old-fashioned, it calls for oleo instead of margarine and ground chuck instead of ground beef. Gotta love it!
