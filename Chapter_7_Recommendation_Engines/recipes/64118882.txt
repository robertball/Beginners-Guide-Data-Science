Title:
The Melting Pot's Green Goddess Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1294859.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, cut into slices
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
2 tablespoons finely sliced chives

Directions:
In a microwave-safe container, microwave cream cheese and milk for 2 to 4 minutes, whisking after each minute, until cream cheese melts and mixture is smooth.
Stir in sour cream, onion, parsley and chives.
Refrigerate until cold.

Submitter: 
Chris Mosley
Description: 
This is a beloved dip that comes from The Melting Pot.  It goes GREAT in grilled mushrooms or just as a simple dip for carrots, broccoli or any other veggie your heart desires.
