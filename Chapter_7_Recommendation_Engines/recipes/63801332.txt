Title:
Mother Teal's Crockpot Easy Camp Stew Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
2 cups chopped chicken or turkey or 2 cans 6 oz. chicken
3 - 1 lb. cans diced tomatoes
2 - 1 lb. cans cream corn
1 cup chopped onions (I saut√© mine when browning ground beef)
10 oz. ketchup
1 tbs. Worcestershire sauce
Dash Tabasco (or more to taste)
Salt/Pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown ground beef (with onions if preferred).  Mix with other ingredients in crockpot.  Cook on high for 3 hours.
