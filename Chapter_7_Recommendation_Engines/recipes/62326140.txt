Title:
Butterscotch Haystacks or Nests Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups butterscotch chips (2- 11oz bags)
1 12 oz. bag chow mein noodles
1/2 cup peanut butter
2 tsp. shortening or oil if needed to prevent seizing
Optional- 1 cup peanuts or 1 cup raisins

Directions:
In a double boiler, melt butterscotch chips over low heat, stirring constantly.  Stir in peanut butter til smooth. Fold in chow mein noodles and optional ingredients, if using.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto foil, parchment or Silpat. If making nests, use spoon to make small indentions in center of cluster and add jelly beans, 3 look nice.  Let stand until cool, peel from foil, place on serving plate. Keep leftovers in air tight container.
