Title:
Chahan (Japanese Fried Rice) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Cooked White Rice (Japanese Rice is preferred but you can use whatever white rice is available)
1 cup Minced Carrots
1 cup Thinly Sliced Green Beans
1 Medium Sized White Onion, Minced
3 Large Cloves of Garlic, Minced
250g Ground Beef
2 Tablespoons Butter
1 Large Egg
Salt and Pepper, To taste
3/4 cup Kikkoman or Soy Sauce, To taste

Directions:
In a large sautee pan, melt the butter over medium low heat, sautee the onion, garlic, carrots and green beans. Sautee till  onions are transparent.
Add the ground beef to the vegetables and sautee with a pinch or two of salt and pepper . Cook the ground beef and the carrots well.
Add the white rice and mix well. Crack the egg in a bowl and beat. Add to the rice and mix together till egg is cooked.
Add 3 Tablespoons of Kikoman/Soy Sauce to the rice. Taste before adding more soy sauce, salt and pepper. Season to taste. Continue to mix the rice well. When you're satisfied with the seasoning, turn off the heat and serve! Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Erica Aquino
Description: 
My boyfriend loved ordering this in our favorite japanese restaurant.. For dinner one time, i decided to surprise him by making my own.. he absolutely loved it. 
this is a quick and easy all-in-one meal.. has rice, meat and vegetables.. if you want a little more meat, you can match it up with some chicken teriyaki or grilled barbecue ribs.. YUM!
