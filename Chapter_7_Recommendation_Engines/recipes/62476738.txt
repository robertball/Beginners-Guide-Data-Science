Title:
Apple Pecan Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - Apples
1 - Large French Bread loaf
3 cups - Sugar, divided (2 and 1)
16 oz - Half and Half
5 - Eggs (beaten)
1 Cup - Lt Brown Sugar (packed)
3/4 Cup - Butter, divided (1/4 C and 1/2 C)
1-1/2 Cups - Pecans (chopped)
3 tsp - Vanilla, divided (2 tsp and 1 tsp)
1/3 Cup - Apple Cider
1 T - Cinnamon

Directions:
Cut loaf into 1" cubes and let dry.
Preheat oven 350.
Peel and thinly slice apples.
Cook in microwave on high for 5 minutes or until softened.
Place dried bread cubes in a 13" x 9" pan.
Sprinkle apples evenvly over bread.
Mix together beaten eggs, half and half, 2 tsp vanilla, and cinnamon. Mix together and pour evenly over bread. Let stand 20 minutes.
Combine topping - Mix together 1/4 cup softened butter and brown sugar, then stir in pecans.
Sprinkle evenly over the top.
Bake at 350 for 35-minutes (or until knife comes out of the center cleanly).
Glaze: In small sauce pan over medium heat whisk together 1/2 cup of softened butter and 1 cup of white sugar. Whisk constantly for 3 minutes. Take pan off of heat and let cool for 1 minute. Mix 1/3 cup apple cider with 1 tsp vanilla and 1 tsp lemon extract. Whisk into sugar mixture and serve warm over bread pudding.
