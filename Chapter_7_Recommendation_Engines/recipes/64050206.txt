Title:
Baked Coconut French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126086.jpg
Ingredients:
16 1-inch-thick slices, diagonally cut, French bread baguette
Cooking spray
1 1/4 cups light coconut milk
1 1/4 cups egg substitute
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup flaked sweetened coconut

Directions:
Arrange bread in a single layer in a 13 x 9-inch baking dish coated with cooking spray.
Combine light coconut milk, egg substitute, cup sugar, and tablespoon vanilla, stirring with a whisk, and pour evenly over bread.
Turn the bread over to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350°.
Remove bread mixture from refrigerator, and uncover.
Turn bread slices over, and sprinkle evenly with flaked coconut.
Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes.
Bake, uncovered, at 350º for 30 minutes or until coconut is golden.

Submitter: 
Andree Vaillancourt
Description: 
Such a nice recipe for Sunday mornings. Make it Saturday evening and Sunday morning just pop it in the oven!
