Title:
Bread and Muffin Crumb Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons melted butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a medium bowl until well mixed.
For muffins, crumble on top of batter before baking.
For breads, crumble on top of batter 20 minutes before bread is done.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
Sweet, buttery crumb topping perfect for breads and muffins.
