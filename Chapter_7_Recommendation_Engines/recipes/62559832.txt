Title:
Kale Salad with Pomegarnate. Sunflowerseeds and Sliced Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch kale (about 1/2 lb)ribs and stems removed
1 1/2 cups pomegranate (about 1 whole pomegranate)
2 tablespoons sunflower seeds
1/3 cup sliced or slivered almonds
5 tablespoons red pepper seasoned rice vinegar
5 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
salt to taste

Directions:
Wash and shake off extra water from kale. Remove the center ribs and stems. Chop the kale until its almost thin and fine but still a little leafy. Add pomegranate, sunflower seeds, and sliced almonds. Toss. Drizzle in all oils while tossing. Add salt to taste and enjoy.

Submitter: 
calabash0420
Description: 
A zesty combination of raw pomegranate & tender fresh kale make a surprisingly great winter salad! By adding sliced or slivered almonds and sunflower seeds you get a tasty crunch!
