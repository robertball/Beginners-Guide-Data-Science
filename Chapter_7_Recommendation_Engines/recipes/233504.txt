Title:
Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4877762.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup powdered milk
2/3 cup white sugar
1/3 cup boiling water
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2/3 cup milk, or more to taste

Directions:
Combine powdered milk, sugar, boiling water, and vanilla extract in a blender and blend until smooth; add milk. Transfer creamer to a container with a tight seal and keep refrigerated.
