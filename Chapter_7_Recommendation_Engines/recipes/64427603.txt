Title:
Honey Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2319869.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pure natural honey
1/2 cup kraft unsweetened unsalted peanut butter
1/2 cup ChefMaster 52% oil margarine (or ant low-fat margarine)
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce (to replace eggs)
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Cream together honey, peanut butter, and margarine, beating until fluffy, add the applesauce and beat some more
Sift together flour baking soda and salt
Combine dry ingredients into wet, mixing thoroughly
Drop by soup spoonfuls onto un-greased cookie sheet, pressing them down with fork dipped in water
Bake for 7-8 minutes cool 5 minutes and remove cookies aside, then lay next batch and bake
