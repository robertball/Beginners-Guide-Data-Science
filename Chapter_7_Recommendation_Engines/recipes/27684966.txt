Title:
Chicken and Strawberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
2 TBS. sesame seeds toasted
1 TBS. poppy seeds
6 cups washed spinach, torn into bite size pieces
2 cups diced chicken tenderloins thawed
2 cups strawberry slices
1/2 cup chopped pecans toasted

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine oil, vinegar, sugar, sesame seeds, and poppy seeds.  Mix well.
In a large bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  Add dressing; toss lightly to coat.  Serve immediately.
