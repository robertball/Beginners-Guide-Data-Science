Title:
Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup rice, uncooked
1/4 cup onion, diced
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 Tbsp oil (for frying)
2 cups tomato sauce
1 cup water

Directions:
In medium bowl, mix ground beef, rice, onion salt and black pepper.
Form into small balls.  Fry in hot oil, turning often, until lightly brown on all sides.  Drain fat.
In medium bowl, mix tomato sauce and water.  Pour over meatballs.  Cover and simmer for 45 minutes.
