Title:
Cherry Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (18 1/4 ounces) yellow cake mix, divided
1 cup all purpose flour
1 package (1/4 ounce) active dry yeast
2/3 cup warm water
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 can (21ounces) cherry pie filling
1/3 cup cold butter
Glaze:
1 cup powdered sugar
1 tbsp white corn syrup
1 to 2 tbsp water.

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups dry cake mix, flour, yeast and water. Blend until smooth. Stir in eggs until blended. Transfer to a greased 9 X 13 inch baking dish. Carefully spoon pie filling over top.   Place remaining cake mix in a bowl. Cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over filling. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 - 40 minutes, or until lightly browned. Cool on a wire rack. Combine glaze ingredients and drizzle over coffee cake.
