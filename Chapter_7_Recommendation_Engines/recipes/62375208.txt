Title:
Bacon-Wrapped Deviled Potato Bites Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
6 potato wedges from deli
9 slices bacon, cooked
1 T. dry mustard powder
1 T cider vinegar
1 t. sugar
1/8-1/4 t. cayenne powder
1/4 C finely shredded cheese (optional)
Sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°. Line CS with foil; spray with cooking spray
Cut potato wedges into 3 pieces. Cut bacon strips in half.
Blend mustard, vinegar, sugar, and cayenne pepper in small MB
Brush or dip eac h potato piece with mixture/
Wrap bacon piece around dipped potato piece and secure with
toothpick. Place on CS
Bake 8-10 min. or until hot.
Push pieces close together on CS and sprinkle with cheese.
Serve with sour cream, if desired.
