Title:
Cabbage Borscht Veggie Sausage Cream Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
2 C. butternut squash, chopped
1 yellow onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 beets, sliced
1/2 head green cabbage, chopped
4 red potatoes, diced
4 carrots, chopped
2 tsp fresh ginger, chopped
2 tsp. dry dill
2 tsp. dry sage
2 tsp. dry thyme
salt and pepper to taste
2 quarts chicken broth
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 pound elk breakfast sausage, browned
1/4 C. red wine vinegar
1-2 tsp. bullsnort or tabasco
sour cream to top

Directions:
pour olive oil in bottom of large soup pot; heat.
chop all veggies and place in pot.
Top veggies with spices and ginger;stir occasionally- 10 min.
Stir in broth, browned meat and cream of mushroom soup.
Boil covered until veggies are soft- stirring randomly.
Complete with red wine vinegar and tabasco sauce.
Taste and add any more herbs/spices to your liking.
Serve with a dollop of sour cream.

Submitter: 
Charissa
Description: 
Talk about great flavor!  This soup is unique and yummy- definitely put in the ingredients that sound bazaar like vinegar and tabasco. Tons of compliments await!
