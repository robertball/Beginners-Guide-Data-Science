Title:
Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F(220C)
Mix flour, soda, baking powder and salt. Cut in shortening with knife fork or pastry blender until the mixture looks like coarse meal.
Add milk and stir just enough to hold dough together.
Place dough on board or pastry cloth that has been dusted with flour.
Knead lightly several times. Roll out dough 1/2" thick. Cut with floured biscuit cutter.
Bake on ungreased baking sheet 10-15 minutes or until golden.

Submitter:

Description: 
These old-fashioned buttermilk biscuits are great with sausage gravy and eggs!
