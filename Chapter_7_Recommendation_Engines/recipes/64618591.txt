Title:
Leftover Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb shredded roast
10 oz Steam-in-a-bag mixed vegetables
1 cup gravy or au ju
4 - 5 cups mashed potatoes
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375
2. In a pie plate layer the beef, steamed vegetables, gravy, mashed potatoes and top with cheese.
3. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until thoroughly heated.

Submitter: 
Kate Leonard
Description:
