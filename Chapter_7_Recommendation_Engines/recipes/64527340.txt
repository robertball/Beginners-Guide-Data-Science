Title:
Asian Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3006877.jpg
Ingredients:
1L (4 cups) water
1 brown onion, halved
2 single chicken breast fillets
1 carrot, peeled, cut into matchsticks
1 red bell pepper, deseeded, thinly sliced
1/2 Napa cabbage,hard core removed, finely shredded
3 green shallots, ends trimmed, thinly sliced diagonally
1/2 cup fresh coriander/cilantro leaves
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon fish sauce
2 teaspoons brown sugar
1 fresh red chilli, deseeded, chopped
crushed peanuts

Directions:
Place water, onion and chicken in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low and cook, covered, for 10 minutes or until cooked. Drain chicken and discard onion.
Place carrot and shallots a bowl. Cover with boiling water. Set aside.
Shred or thinly slice chicken. Place chicken, carrot, red bell pepper, cabbage, shallot and coriander in a bowl. Combine lime juice, fish sauce, sugar and chilli in a jar. Pour over salad and combine. Divide among serving bowls to serve.
