Title:
Big Ray's Fast Fruit Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129885.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (21 ounces) peach, apple or cherry pie filling
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 cup cereal,Raisin Bran, Fruit & Fiber or similar.
1 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground allspice
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
In a medium skillet, heat pie filling and lemon juice over medium heat for 5 minutes or until bubbly, stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, place cereal in a resealable plastic bag; crush slightly with a rolling pin. In a skillet, melt butter. Stir in cereal, sugar, cinnamon, allspice and nutmeg; cook and stir for 2-3 minutes. Sprinkle over the fruit mixture. Serve warm with preferred topping (ice cream or heavy cream)
