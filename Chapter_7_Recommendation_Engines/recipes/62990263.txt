Title:
Turkey, Stuffing, And Cranberry Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/743512.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cooked turkey
2 oz cranberry sauce
4 oz of mayonnaise
2 Oz of stuffing
4 slices of bread

Directions:
Put chunks of turkey into a bowl and mix in mayonnaise to desired mayonnaise turkey mixture.
Take turkey mixture and spoon onto bread
take stuffing mixture and spoon it on top of the turkey
take cranberry sauce and spoon that on top of the stuffing mixture.
put last slice of bread on top to finish the sandwich, and slice bread in half

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
I believe this is a classic Turkey, Stuffing, And Cranberry Sandwich.
