Title:
Andrea's Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 T olive oil
2 c chopped onion
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 lb. sliced mushrooms
1 medium zucchini, diced
1 grated carrot
handful of chopped basil
2 t basil
1 t oregano
1 t thyme
1.5 t salt
1 lb 13 oz can tomatoes
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 T honey
lots of black pepper
4-6 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c minced parsley

Directions:
1) Heat the olive oil in a Dutch oven.  Add veggies, herbs and salt, and saute over medium heat until the onion is soft (8-10 mins).
2) Add tomatoes, tomato paste, honey, and black pepper.  Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer, partially covered, 20-30 minutes.
3) Add garlic, and cook 10 mins more. At this point, the sauce can sit for several hours, or be refrigerated for up to a week.  Add parsley at the last minute (optional).
