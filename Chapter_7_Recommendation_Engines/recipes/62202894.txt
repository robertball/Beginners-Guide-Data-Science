Title:
Holiday Pepper Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large green bell peppers
2 large red bell peppers
1 large white onion
1/2 cup (or to taste) oil-vinegar-type salad dressing

Directions:
Seed the peppers and cut them in rings.
Slice the onion and separate it into rings.
Toss with dressing and refrigerate for several hours.
Serve in a crystal bowl [opt.]

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This simple salad adds color and crunch to your holiday table or buffet.
