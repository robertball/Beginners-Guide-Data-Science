Title:
orangey salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 head of iceberg lettuce, washed and torn
3 roma tomatoes, chopped
1 firm pear, peeled and diced
1 carrot, peeled and chopped
3 Tbsp. chopped red onion
1/4 c. slivered almonds
Dressing:
1/4 c. olive oil
1/4 c. white vinegar
1/3 c. orange juice
1 Tbsp. honey
pepper to taste

Directions:
Toss salad ingredients together.  Mix dressing ingredients, and pour over salad when ready to serve.

Submitter: 
SPEARL20
Description: 
this is a quick salad to throw together with ingredients you already have.  If you want, make your own salad and just try the orange dressing!
