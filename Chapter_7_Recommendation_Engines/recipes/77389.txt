Title:
Fruit Ambrosia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
3/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup lime juice
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin orange slices, drained (reserve several for garnish, if desired)
1 1/2 cups grape halves
1 cup white miniature marshmallows
1 (3.5 ounce) can flaked coconut
1/2 cup maraschino cherry halves, well drained

Directions:
In large bowl, whisk sweetened condensed milk, sour cream, and lime juice. Stir in remaining ingredients.  Chill at least 3 hours to blend flavors. Refrigerate any leftovers.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
This delightful fruit salad can be made any time of year. Its tropical flavors of pineapple, orange, and coconut are accentuated with a marshmallow and sour cream dressing.
