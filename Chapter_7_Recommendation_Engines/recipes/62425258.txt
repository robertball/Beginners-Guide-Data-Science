Title:
Soba Noodles with Edamame Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/447259.jpg
Ingredients:
9 ounces of soba noodles
1 package of frozen edamame
4 teaspoons of black toasted sesame seeds
1 teaspoon of sesame oil
1 tablespoon apple juice
1 tablespoon rice vinegar
1 tablespoon soy sauce
4 green onions

Directions:
Bring 2 saucepans of water to boil (noodles, edamame)
Cook soba noodles
Cook edamame
Toast sesame seeds in a small, dry saucepan
Drain noodles and rinse under cold water
Put noodles in bowl and add apple juice, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and soy sauce.
Toss noodles so they are evenly coated with sauce
Stir in green onions and toasted sesame seeds
Drain soybeans and transfer to plate to be served with noodles
