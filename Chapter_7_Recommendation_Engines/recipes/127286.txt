Title:
Chocolate Plunge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979047.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup whipping cream
1 (8 ounce) package  BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Microwave corn syrup and whipping cream in large microwaveable bowl on HIGH 1-1/2 min. or until mixture comes to boil. Add chocolate; stir until completely melted.
Serve warm as a dip with cut-up fresh fruit, assorted NABISCO Cookies, pretzels and/or pound cake cubes.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
Three simple ingredients--semi-sweet chocolate, cream and corn syrup--are microwaved into a warm dessert dip for fruit, cookies, pretzels or cake.
