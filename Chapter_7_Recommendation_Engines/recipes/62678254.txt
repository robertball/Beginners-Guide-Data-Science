Title:
Blonde Puffed Wheat Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599940.jpg
Ingredients:
-1/2 Cup Butter
-1 Cup Brown Sugar
-1/2 Cup Corn Syrup
-1/4 Cup Flour
-Pinch Salt
-1 Tsp Vanilla
-Puffed Wheat Cereal

Directions:
Grease a 9x9 baking dish. Bring all ingredients, excluding the vanilla, to boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. When it starts to boil remove from heat and stir in the vanilla. Add puffed wheat gradually, making sure it is evenly coated. Keep adding until there the cereal can no longer be coated. Pour mixture into baking dish, pack down lightly. Leave to cool and firm up.

Description: 
A variation on a classic that our family has been making for years. There wasn't a lot of description in the recipe so I tried my best to explain how it's done.
