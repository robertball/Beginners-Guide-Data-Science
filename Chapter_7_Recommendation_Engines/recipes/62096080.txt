Title:
Zacks spicey shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T spoon unsalted butter
1 T olive oil
1 small red onion diced
2 garlic cloves diced
7 sun dried tomatos chopped
20 medium fresh peeled shrimp tails on
1/2 cup heavy cream
3 T crushed hot pepper sauce
1/4 cup fresh parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1 T spoon unsalted butter to finsh

Directions:
In frying pan heat butter and oil.
Add red onion and garlic cook until translucent.
Add shrimp,sun dried tomatos,heavy cream and hot pepper sauce.
Cook shrimp until done.(When they turn pink their done.)
Stir in cheese and butter.
Salt and pepper to taste.
