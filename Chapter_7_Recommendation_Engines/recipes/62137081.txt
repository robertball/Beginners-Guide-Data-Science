Title:
Basic Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Large Red Potatos
20 oz Evaporated Milk
1/4 c Hot Water
1 Tbsp oil or butter
1/2 c Butter
1 1/2 Tbsp Garlic salt
2-3 Chicken Boujon Cubes
8 slices of Turkey or Pork Bacon
1 Medium White Onion

Directions:
Place cubed potatoes in a very large saucepan with enough water to cover completely. Boil on high heat until tender. Meanwhile, Saute the onions and bacon in a medium frying pan till brown with a Tbsp of oil or butter.
Disolve the chicken boujon cubes in the 1/4c hot tap water breaking the cubes up with a fork or spoon stirring together. In Medium mixing bowl compine chicken boujon water with Evaporated milk and add Garlic salt to taste.
Empty boiling water from potatoes and set 1/8th of the potatoes asside in a bowl (This will be used to make soup chunkier similar to a chowder, if you like your soup not as chunky or not chunky at all do not set any potoatoes asside).
Add Evaporated milk and chicken boujon mixture to the potatoes along with Onions and Bacon. Mix together till creamy adding more evaporated milk for thinner soup. Once mixing is complete add the rest of the potatoes breaking them up to the chunky style of your taste.
Slow simmer the soup for 15-20 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Rutter Family
Description: 
My mother gave me this recipe for potato soup and it is extremely easy and tastes great.
