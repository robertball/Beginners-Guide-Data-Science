Title:
Kale Oat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups quick oats
1 cup oat flour
2 cups baby kale
1 Â½ cups silken tofu (mashed)
1 tsp curry powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven at 425 degree F and grab a nonstick muffin pan.
Combine all the ingredients in a food processor and process until smooth.
With an ice cream scoop spoon the batter into the muffin tray and bake in the middle rack for 15-17 minutes or until the tester comes out clean.
