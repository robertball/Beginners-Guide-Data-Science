Title:
Pecan Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz pk. cream cheese
1/3 C sour cream
1/2 C finely chopped toasted pecans
2 T picante sauce
1 serrano chili, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
4- 8 in flour tortillas

Directions:
Toast pecans AT 350 for 10 minutes. In a medium bowl with an electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sour cream until smooth and combined. Beat in the pecans, picante sauce, chili and garlic. Spread about 6 tablespoons of mixture on each tortilla and roll up. Wrap in plastic wrap. Refrigerate the rolls for at least four hours or overnight. When ready to serve, remove the plastic wrap and cut the rolls into 1/2 inch thick slices and serve immediatly.
