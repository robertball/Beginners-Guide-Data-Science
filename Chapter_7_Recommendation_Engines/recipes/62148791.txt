Title:
No Bake Date Nut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
3/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoon butter
1 8oz box Chopped Dates
5 cups rice crispies
3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Spray a 7x11 dish with cooking oil lightly.
In a medium sauce pan add butter, egg, sugar, and chopped dates.
Stir constantly over medium heat until dates are disolved.
Cook and stir over low heat for two more minutes.
Put rice crispies and nuts in very large bowl and add mixture.
Stir quickly with a large spoon to coat all rice crispies.
Dump in prepared dish, spread and lightly press.
Cool in refrigerator and cut into desired size bars.

Submitter: 
Shelby Snider
Description: 
Your family will love these light Rice Crispies Date Nut Bar. So quick and easy to make.
