Title:
Grandma Logan's Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1549937.jpg
Ingredients:
32oz southern style hashbrowns
1 cup Sour Cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup corn flakes
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. mix hashbrowns, sour cream, and cream of chicken in large bowl and then place into a well greased baking dish
3. mix melted butter and corn flakes and spread on top of potatoes
4. bake in oven for 45 minutes
