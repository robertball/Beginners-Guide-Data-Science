Title:
Tartar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Mayonnaise
1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
1 Tablespoon Pickle relish, sweet or dill to your liking
1 Tablespoon minced Onion
1 teaspoon Capers, drained and minced
1 teaspoon Chives, snipped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients well.  Adjust seasonings with salt and pepper to your liking; you may add a dash of pepper sauce. Chill.

Submitter: 
Mama Smith
Description: 
Southern Fried Catfish is a tradition of the south.  This easy tatar sauce recipe goes great with any seafood.
