Title:
Alexia's Lemony Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lemon (zest rind, juiced)
1/4 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon sugar
Kosher salt
Fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Remove zest from 1 lemon then slice in half and juice, discard seeds and rind
Add zest and juice to olive oil, mustard, sugar, salt and pepper. Whisk and serve.

Submitter: 
Alexia Patramanis
Description: 
Lemony dressing to serve over greens or as a finishing sauce. Especially great with fresh spinach leaves and freshly sliced avocado.
