Title:
Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup margarine
1-1/4 cups brown sugar
2 Tbsp milk
1 Tbsp vanilla
1 egg
1-3/4 cup flour
1 tsp salt
3/4 tsp baking soda
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup walnuts

Directions:
Cream margarine, brown sugar, milk and vanilla.  Blend in egg.
Add flour, salt and baking soda and mix well.
Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
Drop rounded Tbsp of dough 3 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees 8-10 minutes for chewy cookies (will appear light and moist), Do Not Overbake.,  11-13 minutes for crisp cookies
