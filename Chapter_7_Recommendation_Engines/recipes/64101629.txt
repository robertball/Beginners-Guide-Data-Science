Title:
Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. of canned diced tomatoes
1 10lb. can of marinara sauce
1 large onion, diced
2 Tbsp. chopped garlic
5 lbs. ground beef
1/2 C red wine
2 Tbsp. seasoning mix
1/4 C vegetable oil

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot on medium heat.  Add onions and cook 1-2 minutes.  Add garlic and cook until garlic becomes fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add ground beef and cook until browned.  Slowly add red wine.  Stir in diced tomatoes, marinara sauce, and seasoning mix.  Cook until heated through.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
A rich and chunky spaghetti sauce that is sure to please!
