Title:
Really Good Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Wheat Chex
2 cups Corn Chex
2 cups Rice Chex
2 cups corn chips
2 cups Cheez-its
1 1/2 cups salted cashew halves
6 tablespoons butter or stick margarine, melted
2 tablespoons olive oil
6 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoon seasoned salt
1 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the cereals and cashews. In a small bowl, combine the butter, oil, Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt and garlic powder. Pour over cereal mixture and toss to evenly coat.
Transfer to a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake at 250 degrees F for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes. Store in airtight containers.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
From Waldo, Wisconsin, Lori Genske writes, 'I grew tired of my family picking through a snack mix for their favorite items and leaving the rest. So I experimented using only their favorites and came up with this recipe. Now there's never any left!'Modified from: Really Good Snack Mix
