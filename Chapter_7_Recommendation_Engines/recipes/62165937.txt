Title:
Cheese Apple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup self-rising flour
1/2 lb velveeta
2 cans sliced apples, drained (not pie mix)

Directions:
Butter or grease 13x9 casserole dish; spread drained apples evenly.  Melt butter and velveeta in microwave. Add sugar and flour, mix then microwave 45 seconds to 1 minute.  Mixture will be a little fluffy. Spread over apples. Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
A warm comforting casserole that is good any time of the year!
