Title:
Tortellini Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cheese tortellini cooked & slightly cooled
2 heads of broccoli--cut up small
10-12 slices of bacon--fried crisp & crumbled
1/2 cup green onions
1/3 cup sunflower seeds
1 cup mayo.
1/4 cup sugar
2 Table. wine vinegar

Directions:
Put dressing on about 1 hour before serving.  I just cooked up some dry cheese filled tortellini ( I didn't measure)
