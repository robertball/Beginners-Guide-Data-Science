Title:
Croutons Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/692389.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 qts bread cubes (6 cups)
2 T Italian herb seasoning
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
In 12x8x2" dish, place cubes.  Microwave at High 6 minutes, stirring after every 2 minutes, until cubes begin to dry.
Sprinkle herb seasoning and garlic salt evenly over bread cubes.  Drizzle with butter, tossing to coat cubes.
Microwave at High 4 to 6 minutes, stirring every minute, until crisp and dry.

Submitter: 
Peggy
Description: 
This is a delicious snack food as well as a great way to use up dry bread.  Croutons may also be used as soup accompaniment or topping for tossed salad.
