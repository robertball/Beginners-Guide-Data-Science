Title:
Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter softened
1 cup confectioners’ sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon almond extract ( or lemon, orange, peppermint, etc.)
2 ¾ cups flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
Sliced almonds
Red or green frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Mix together sugar and butter. Add eggs and extracts.
Combine flour, baking powder and salt.  Slowly add to cream mixture.
Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Roll into one-inch balls. Form balls into finger shapes (about 3 inches by ½ inch)
Use the flat tip of a table knife to make the indention for the fingernail.  With knife, make 3 slashes in the middle of each finger for a knuckle.
Bake on a lightly greased baking sheet for 20-25 minutes.
Cool for 3 minutes and remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack.  Squeeze a small about of frosting to each nail bed, press a sliced almond over frosting.
