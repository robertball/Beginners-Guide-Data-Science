Title:
Speedy Smoked Pork Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground pork
2 tsp liquid smoke
2 tsp paprika
salt & pepper to taste
Provolone cheese (optional)
whole wheat English muffins/whole wheat burger buns, toasted
lettuce & tomatoes (for toppings)

Directions:
Heat grill or large skillet to medium high
Mix all ingredients in large bowl
Shape 4 equal size patties and cook about 5-7 minutes on each side until cooked through
Place cheese on last few minutes and turn off heat to allow to melt, not over cooking meat
Put burgers on toasted buns and top with lettuce and tomato

Submitter:

Description: 
My family really likes these juicy burgers with their smoked flavor.
