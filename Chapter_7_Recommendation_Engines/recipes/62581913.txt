Title:
Mimi's Wacky Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c flour
2 c sugar
6 Tbsp cocoa
2 tsp baking soda
2 Tbsp vinegar
2 tsp vanilla
10 Tbsp melted butter
2 c warm water
Frosting:
1/2 c coconut
3 Tbsp melted butter
5 Tbsp brown sugar
2 Tbsp milk
1/2 c pecans

Directions:
Sift all dry cake ingredients together with a fork in a 9" by 13" pan. Add vinegar to one corner & vanilla to the opposite corner, butter in the middle & pour the hot water over all of it. Stir well & bake at 325 for 30 min.
For the frosting, boil all ingredients except nuts for 1-2 min. Add nuts & pour over the cake. Put back into the oven for 2 min.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is my grandmother's recipe for Wacky Cake. There are many different variations but basically this cake was created during the depression & is made with out eggs or milk. You won't believe how moist & delicious it is!
