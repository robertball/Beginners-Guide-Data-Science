Title:
Dollie's Tomato Aspic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16-ounce can stewed tomatoes, do not drain
1 0.6-ounce package red raspberry gelatin
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
2 cups boiling water
1/2 cup cold water

Directions:
Heat the stewed tomatoes, along with the juice from the can.
Dissolve the red raspberry gelatin 2 cups boiling water.
Dissolve the unflavored gelatin in 1/2 cup cold water.
Add the dissolved unflavored gelatin to the red raspberry gelatin and stir.
Add the gelatin mixture to the heated stewed tomatoes, and stir.
Pour mixture into a gelatin mold and refrigerate until congealed.

Submitter: 
Clotho98
Description: 
This was my grandmother's (Dollie Cowan)recipe.  She often served with at luncheons.  Tomato aspic sounds a little strange to people nowadays, but it was very popular in the South in the 1930s-50s.  Dollie served it on butter lettuce, with a little mayonnaise on top.
