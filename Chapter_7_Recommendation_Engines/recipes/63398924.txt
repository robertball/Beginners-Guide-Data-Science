Title:
Corn Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. wheat flour or white
1 c cornmeal
3/4 t salt
2 T oil
2/3 c warm water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  Knead dough several minutes.  Divide dough into 12 balls.  Roll the tortillas out thin.  Heat oil in 14" skillet until very hot.  Fry one at a time for 35-40 seconds.
