Title:
Spinach and Grilled Chicken Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3733232.jpg
Ingredients:
1LB of boneless Chicken Breast
14.5ounce of Penne Noodles
5 to 6 cups  of Spinach
1 tablespoon of red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon of salt
1 tablespoon of pepper
1 tablespoon of butter
1 tablespoon of garlic powder
1 cup of parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut up Chicken breast into smaller pieces, grill with a tablespoon of butter, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Boil Penne Noodles in hot water until done/ Drain water from noodles
Combine all ingredients. Grilled Chicken, Penne Noodles,  5 to 6 bags of Spinach, red pepper, parmesan cheese. Cook about 7 minutes.
Season with salt, pepper and 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese.
