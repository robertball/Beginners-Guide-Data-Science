Title:
Soupier Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Cream Mushroom Soup
1/2 Cup Milk
1/2 tsp mustard
3 Cups Cooked Maccaroni
2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 dash pepper
1 cup French Fried Onions

Directions:
Blend Soup, Milk, Mustard, 1 1/2 cups cheese, and Cooked Maccaroni.  Cook at 400* for  25 minutes.  Stir and top with French fried onions and remaining cheese.  Bake for 5 more minutes.
