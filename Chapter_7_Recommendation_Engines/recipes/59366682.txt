Title:
Holuski Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles or cubed potatoes
1 medium head cabbage, chopped or sauerkraut
1/4 cup butter
1 onion, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
Sausage, either Keilbaska or browned pork sausage

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta or potaoes and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Heat butter in a deep skillet. Saute the onion and cabbage until tender. Stir in noodles. Season with salt and pepper. Add a little vinegar and sugar if using cabbage instead of sauerkraut.
