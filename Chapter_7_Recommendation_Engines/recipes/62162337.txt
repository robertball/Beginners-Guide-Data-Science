Title:
Curried Cottage Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. paprika
2 tsp. curry powder
2 T. oil
2 T. parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut potatoes in wedges
Place in a resealable ziploc bag with spices and oil.
Shake to coat.
Place on greased pan and bake 400 degrees for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
Melaknee
Description: 
Potato wedges with a kick! Great for those who love curry.
