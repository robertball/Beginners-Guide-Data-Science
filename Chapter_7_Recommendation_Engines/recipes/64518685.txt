Title:
Frog Eye Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2866807.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz.  package acini di pepe pasta
1 3/4 c. pineapple juice
2 T. flour
3 beaten eggs
1/2 t. salt
1 c. sugar
1 can pineapple tidbits (reserve juice)
2- 11 oz. cans mandarin oranges, drained
1/2  bag of mini marshmallows
8 oz. tub cool whip

Directions:
Cook pasta until tender  Drain, cool and set aside.
Combine 1 3/4 c. pineapple juice, flour, eggs, salt, sugar in a bowl.  Let thicken and cool.
Combine pasta, pineapple tidbits (drained), oranges, marshmallows and Cool Whip  with cooled sauce and refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
US92
Description: 
Nancy Moore shared this yummy sweet salad for our Thanksgiving dinner.   Don't worry, no frogs were harmed in the making of this salad.
