Title:
Tofu Whipped "Cream" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 0z of tofu
1/4 cup soy milk
1/4 cup maple syrup or sugar to taste
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp almond flavoring (opt)

Directions:
Put all in blender (Vitamix if you have one) and whip til light and fluffy.
