Title:
Connie's Christmas Meatballs (sweet & sour) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/929351.jpg
Ingredients:
Meatballs (use ready made or make as follows)
1 lb ground beef
breadcrumbs from 2 slices bread (or 6 - 8 crackers)
1 beaten egg, mixed with 1/4 cup water
spices to taste
SAUCE
1 C katchup
1 C beef bullion
1/2 C vinager
1/2 C brown sugar
2 Tbsp Cornstarch (mix into br. sugar)

Directions:
Meatballs - mix all ingredients together, season it as you would a hamburger.  form into bite sized meatballs and bake at 350f until cooked (25 - 30 mins) or panfry til cooked
Sauce - mix all ingredients in saucepan/dutchoven, heat until bubbling and hot (will change color).  add cooked meatballs and let simmer till ready to serve.
SERVE in a chaffing dish or serving dish.

Submitter: 
Catherine Parnell-Proulx
Description: 
My grandmother's meatballs - always a treat at Christmas and one of our family favorites!
