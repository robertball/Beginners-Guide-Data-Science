Title:
Beef Short Ribs with Dark Ale and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRY RUB:
1/4 cup chili powder
1 tsp.mustard powder
pinch of chilies[to your liking]
1 Tbsp.salt
1 Tbsp.brown sugar
1 Tbsp.minced garlic
RIBS:
12 beef short ribs
2 Tbsp.oil
2 large onions,diced
1 dark beer
1 Tbsp.tomato paste
2 cups beef stock

Directions:
Combine all spices and mix well.Toss ribs in spices,cover and refrigerate overnight.
Preheat oven to 275.
Heat oil in heavy skillet set on medium heat.Brown ribs on all sides.
Remove ribs and transfer to a baking dish.
Add diced onions to pan and saute till untill golden.
Add beer and cook untill reduced by half.Add tomato paste and broth to skillet,bring to a boil.
Pour over ribs,cover,bake for 3-4 hours,or untill the meat is very tender.
Remove from oven and let cool.
Place in refrigerator overnight.Remove any fat from the beef liquid.
Remove ribs from baking dish and pour liquid into pot set over med.-high heat bring to a boil and reduce untill thickens.
Add ribs to sauce and heat through.
Serve with noodles or mashed potatoes.
