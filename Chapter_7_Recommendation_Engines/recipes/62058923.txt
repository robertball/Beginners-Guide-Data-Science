Title:
No Peek Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
1 box original blend wild rice
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can water
1 packet dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Mix wild rice and seasoning packet, cream soups, and can of water in a 9 x 13 pan. Stir well. Place chicken on top of mixture. Shake dry onion soup mix packet well. Sprinkle over top of chicken. Seal the pan tightly with foil. Bake at 350 for 1 hour 15 minutes. No Peeking!

Submitter: 
Butterfly Flutterby
Description: 
I have been making this delicious dish for years and it is a snap to put together. Everything is mixed together in the pan it bakes in, easy cleanup!
