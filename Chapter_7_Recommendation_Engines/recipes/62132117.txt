Title:
Cucumber salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med size cucmbers
1 can evaporated milk
2 T of white vinegar
salt
pepper

Directions:
Peel cucumbers and thinly slice in a bowl, add salt to each cucumber.  Let stand at room temp for 3 to 4 hours to bleed out juice.  Drain juice and with a clean rag squeeze out as much juice as you can from all the cucumbers, set aside.
Mix evaporated milk slowly in a glass bowl with white vinegar, when milk is thick add to cucumbers and stir.  Add pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Kim Casey Nugent Roberds
Description: 
Sliced cucumbers in a creamy sauce, great on hot summer days.
