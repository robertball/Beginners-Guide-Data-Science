Title:
Ma's Frozen Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Quarts of cucumbers sliced and peeled
1 small onion sliced
2 Tablespoons Salt
1 1/2 Cups white sugar
1/2 Cup white vinegar

Directions:
Soak cucumbers onion and salt in the refrigerator for 2 hours and drain. Bring sugar and vinegar to a boil and allow to cool to room temperature. Pour liquid over cucumbers and freeze.

Submitter: 
Danielle Kroll
Description: 
This recipe was given to me from my Grandmother. She always served these at picnics. They are perfect on a hot summer day!
