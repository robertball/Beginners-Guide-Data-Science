Title:
Pork loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs pork loin *
1 c ginger ale *
1/2 c honey *
1/2 c dijon mustard *
1/4 c veg oil *
2 T onion powder *
1 1/2 tsp rosemary *
1 tsp salt *
1 tsp garlic powder *
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a  large plastic bag.  Refrigerate, marinate at least one hour.  Grill 20 min per pound.  Baste with marinade.  Simmer marinade in saucepan for 30 minutes or until reduced.  You can roast this in the oven if you aren't able to grill.
