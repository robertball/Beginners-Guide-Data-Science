Title:
Salmon With Peachy Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup uncooked quinoa rinsed and drained
1 TBS lime juice
1 TBS minced shallot
1/2 tsp honey
3/4 tsp grated ginger
1/4 tsp salt
2 TBS extra-virgin olive oil
1 peach, pitted and chopped (about 3/4  cup)
1 small green bell pepper, finely chopped (about 1/2 cup)
4 (4oz) fresh salmon fillets, about 1 inch thick

Directions:
1. In a saucepan, combine quinoa and 1 cup water.  Bring to boil, reduce heat.  Simmer, covered, about 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.  Place quinoa in medium bowl; let cool slightly.
2. Meanwhile, in as small bowl combine the lime juice, shallot, honey, ginger, and salt.  Whisk in olive oil.  Set aside 1 TBS of mixture in a shallow dish. Then stir peach, bell pepper, and remaining lime-shallot vinaigrette into quinoa.
3. Coat the salmon with vinaigrette mixture in dish.  Grill salmon on a greased grill rack over medium heat for 8-12 minutes or until fish flakes.  Be sure to turn once halfway through grilling.  Serve salmon with quinoa pilaf/
