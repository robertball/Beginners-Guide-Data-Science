Title:
(Chicken Thighs) Italian Chicken w/ Spinach Fettucine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg. Boneless Chicken Thighs
14.5 oz. Italian Style Stewed Tomatoes
1 tsp. Garlic Powder
1 Tbsp. Dried Basil
1 Tbsp. Dried Oregano
1 tsp. Dried Red Pepper Flakes
1 Tbsp. Olive Oil
1 pkg. spinach fettucine
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Season chicken thighs with garlic, salt & pepper. Saute in olive oil, turning until evenly golden brown. Top with Italian style stewed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano & dried red pepper flakes. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover & simmer about 30 minutes or until juices run cleasr. Serve over spinach fettucine.
