Title:
Chicken with artichokes and bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6723728.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Boneless chicken breasts, pounded thin
1 cup artichokes in oil, chopped
1 cup mushrooms, chopped
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
4 slices bacon, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
2 TB butter
1 tsp garlic
1 tsp dried oregano
2 tsp sugar
1/3 cup half and half
1-1/2 cups white wine, preferably a CA Viognier

Directions:
1.  Fry bacon in a large non stick skillet until crispy. Remove from skillet.
2.  Add chicken and olive oil and butter. Brown on both sides. Remove chicken to a plate.
3. Add mushrooms and green onions and sauté until mushrooms are soft.
4.   Add garlic, oregano and artichokes and sauté for 1 minute.
5.  Put bacon bits back in skillet. Add wine and sugar to taste.
6.  Reduce sauce for 3-5 minutes and place chicken breasts back in sauce.
7.  Add half and half and simmer for 1 minute.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Flavorful boneless chicken breasts with artichokes and bacon simmered in a white wine reduction with a splash of cream.  Serve this with orzo and a nice Greek salad.
