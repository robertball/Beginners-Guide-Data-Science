Title:
Chocolate Chip Coffee Bar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb brown sugar
1 cup oil
2 eggs
1 tsp valulla
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
3 cups flour
1 cup coffee
1 cup chopped and peeled nuts
12 oz bag chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Whip brown sugar, oil and eggs
Combine with vanilla, salt, baking soda, flour, and coffee
Fold in chocolate chips and nuts
Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes
