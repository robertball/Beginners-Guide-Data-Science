Title:
White Castle Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 3 lbs ground beef
2 envelopes Lipton Onion Soup Mix
16 slices cheese (American, cheddar or jalapeno pepper jack)
2 cans pillsbury crescent rolls
16 oz water
dill pickle chips
ketchup/mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Brown hamburger, drain excess grease
Mix both packets of onion mix into 16 oz water and stir into ground beef
Cook and stir on medium heat until most but not all of water is cooked off
Spray Pam on 9x13" baking dish
Line dish with one tube of crescent rolls
Lay 8 slices of cheese over crescent rolls
Spread ground beef evenly on top of cheese layer
Lay last 8 slices cheese evenly over ground beef mixture.
Top with remaining can of crescent rolls.  Spray pay lightly on top.
Bake at 350F for 20-25 minutes or until golden
Place hamburger dill chips on top of each square if desired.
