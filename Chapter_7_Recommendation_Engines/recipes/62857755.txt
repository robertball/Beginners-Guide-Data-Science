Title:
Puffed Millet Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup natural peanut butter, almond butter or Tahini
½ cup honey, brown rice syrup or pure maple syrup
1 tsp pure vanilla
½ tsp salt
3 ½  cup puffed millet
½ cups chopped dried fruit (dates, apricots, raisins, apples)

Directions:
Grease 8x8 pan
Combine nut butter, honey and salt in a saucepan and heat until boiling.  Add vanilla.
Stir in millet and dried fruit
Press into prepared pan and cool
