Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
3 1/2 cup flour
2 tsp. cream of Tartar
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Stir together butter and sugar until fluffy
Add eggs one at a time and add vanilla
Mix in dry ingredients 1/3 at a time.
Chill for a couple of hours
Roll to 1/4 inch thick and cut with cookie cutters
Bake at 375 for 6-8 min
When cool ice and decorate as desired
