Title:
3 minute Blueberry Mini Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cake dessert shells
1.5 cups of blueberries
3 tablespoons salted butter
6 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
Cut butter into tiny cubes and divide 1.5 tablespoons among the 6 dessert shells
Fill shells with 1/4th cup blueberries each
Place remaining butter cubes on top of the blueberries dividing equally among the 6 shells
Sprinkle 1 tablespoon sugar on each cake
Microwave all the "cakes" for 30 seconds each

Submitter: 
katiegrace1027
Description: 
Wonderfully easy blueberry cakes that you make in the microwave!  The look nice and taste great! Can be served with icecream or whipped cream
