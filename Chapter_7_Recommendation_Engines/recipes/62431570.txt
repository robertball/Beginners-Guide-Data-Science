Title:
Cheesy Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451451.jpg
Ingredients:
12 medium baking potatoes
1 package (8 oz.) cream cheese (softened)
1 container (8 oz.) sour cream
8 Tbs. butter (1 stick) (softened)
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Peel and slice potatoes into cubes.
Cook potatoes in water for 25 min. or until soft.
Drain potatoes and place potatoes in mixing bowl.
Add butter, cream cheese, sour cream and black pepper to potatoes.
Beat potato mixture on medium speed until fluffy.
Place potatoes in casserole dish and top with 1/2 cup shredded cheese.
Cover dish and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
Stir potatoes a couple of times while baking.
Add remaining 1/2 cup of shredded cheese to potatoes and bake uncovered for additional 15 minutes.
