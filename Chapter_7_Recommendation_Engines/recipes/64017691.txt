Title:
spinach stuffed chicken breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (6oz) boneless, skinless chicken breast.
10 oz package of spinach
1 cup sour cream
4 garlic cloves, diced
1 cup pepper jack cheese
8 slices bacon
tooth picks

Directions:
1. pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
2. place spinach in large bowl and microwave for 3 minutes, stirring every minute.
3. stir in sour cream, sliced cheese, and diced garlic in bowl.
4. place chicken between two pieces of plastic wrap and pound until about 1/4 in. thick.
5. place filling evenly on each chicken breast.
6. roll chicken up and secure with toothpicks.
7. wrap each chicken breast with 2 slices of bacon.
8. place in oven for 35 minutes.
9. increase oven heat to 500 degrees and cook for 5 more minutes to crisp up the bacon.
