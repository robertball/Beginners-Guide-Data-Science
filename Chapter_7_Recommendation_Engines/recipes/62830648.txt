Title:
My version of Olive Gardens Chicken con Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670746.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large BLSL chicken breasts
2 T olive oil
8 oz. dry orecchiette pasta (see footnotes)
1 1/2 C heavy whipping cream
2 T. butter
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 T. cornstarch dissolved in 1/3 C. cold water
1 1/2 C broccoli florets, lightly steamed, or quickly cooked in the microwave
salt to taste
ground black pepper
1/4 C grated Parmesan cheese
Spice Mixture:
1 tsp. dried basil
1 T. finely chopped fresh parsley
1 T finely chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 t. red pepper flakes (adjust to personal taste)

Directions:
Combine spice ingredients and rub onto the chicken breasts. Cook the chicken in olive oil til done and browned. Set aside.
Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until almost done. Drain and set aside reserving 1/3 C pasta water.
Stir butter and garlic into a saute pan. Cook until the garlic releases it's aroma and begins to brown, not burn. Add cream, and cornstarch mixture. Cook on medium high heat til it thickens to a sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed. Stir in the Parmesan and stir til it begins to melt into the sauce. Add the reserved chicken which has been shredded, broccoli, and pasta. Toss in the sauce and coat well. Serve with additional Parmesan. Enjoy!
