Title:
Easy-Creamy Sugar-Free Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487866.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups 10% fat cream
1 package (30 g/1oz) sugar-free/fat-free vanilla pudding mix
3 tbsp vanilla

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk all ingredients together until blended and thickened. Freeze in a 1 quart (1 litre) ice cream maker, according to directions. Transfer to a container with lid and freeze until firm.

Submitter: 
MaritimeGal
Description: 
In my search for a creamy vanilla ice cream that is also sugar-free for my diabetic husband, this is a winner! Three ingredients, creamy texture and a great taste!
