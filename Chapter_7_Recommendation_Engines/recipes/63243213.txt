Title:
Quick Corn Tortilla Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6" corn tortilla
1 egg
1 t milk
1 T shredded oaxaca cheese (can substitute mozzarella)
Dried dill to taste
Salt to taste

Directions:
Tear up the tortilla into 1 to 2 inch pieces.
Line the sides and bottom of a coffee cup with these pieces.
Whisk the egg, milk, and cheese together.  Pour into the coffee cup, careful not to move the tortilla pieces.
Sprinkle salt and dill to taste on top.
Microwave for 1 to 2 minutes, or until the egg is cooked.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
A quick and tasty egg dish inspired by leftover corn tortillas and a taste for something both bread-y and with egg.
