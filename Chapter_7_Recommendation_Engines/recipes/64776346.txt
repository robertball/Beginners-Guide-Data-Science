Title:
Raspberry Jello Pretzel Joy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C crushed pretzels
3/4 C melted butter
1/4 C sugar
9 oz Cool Whip
1 C sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
6 oz raspberry Jello
1 C Boiling water
16 oz frozen raspberries

Directions:
Combine pretzels, butter, and 1/4 C sugar. Press into 9x13 in baking dish and bake at 350 F for 10 minutes. Cool to room temperature.
Cream together cream cheese and cup of sugar. Fold in Cool Whip. Spread over cooled pretzel crust.
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Stir in frozen berries, chill until partially set (45m-1h depending on fridge). Spread over white layer and chill until fully set.

A delicious blend of tart, salty, sweet, and creamy that will be devoured by your guests
