Title:
Home made Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large potatoes
1/4 c mayo
1/4 c flour
1 onion
1/2 c shredded sharp cheddar
1/2 c diced bacon
1/4 package ranch powder

Directions:
Peal potatoes, cut in half
Cut onion in half, remove skin
Using a food processor, shred potatoes and onions.    Place in colander, rinse in cold water, then place in bowl of water. Allow to sit for about 1hr,strain, then allow to sit for at least 30min.
Place potatoes, onions, flour, mayo, bacon, ranch powder and cheddar in a bowl and mix well.
Spray an 8x8 pan, and press hash brown mix into pan.
Bake 300 for 1hr
If crisper hash browns are desired, remove desired
amount from pan, place in frying pan, flatten and
cook over medium heat till crispy.
