Title:
Baked Lemon Pepper Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Asparagus, trimmed
1 TBSP olive oil
1 tbsp Lemon Pepper Seasoning

Directions:
Toss Asparagus with oil and seasonings then bake in an oven proof dish for about 20 minutes at 350-400 until tender.
