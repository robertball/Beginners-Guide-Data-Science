Title:
Big Ray's Berry Pretzel Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2989724.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups crushed pretzels (about 8 ounces)
3/4 cup butter, melted
3 tablespoons sugar
FILLING:
2 cups whipped topping
1 package (8 ounces) neufatel cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
TOPPING:
2 packages (3 ounces each) berry gelatin
2 cups boiling water
2 packages (16 ounces each) frozen sweetened sliced berries
Additional whipped topping, optional

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the pretzels, butter and sugar. Press into an ungreased 13" x 9" baking dish. Bake at 350Â° for 10 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.
For filling, in a small bowl, beat whipped topping, neufatel cheese and sugar until smooth. Spread over pretzel crust. Refrigerate until chilled.
For topping, dissolve gelatin in boiling water in a large bowl. Stir in berries with syrup; chill until partially set. Carefully spoon over filling. Chill for 4-6 hours or until firm. Cut into squares; serve with whipped topping if desired. Yield: 12-16 servings.

Submitter:

Description: 
The sweetness of the cream cheese mixture and berries is a nice contrast to the saltiness and crunch of the pretzels. This recipe is great as a side dish, snack, and dessert and is perfect for potlucks.  Only problems is there never seems to be any leftovers!
