Title:
English Muffin Personal Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522274.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/3 cup chopped pepperoni
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup of pizza sauce
3 tablespoons chopped ripe olives (optional)
1 lb of hamburger meat (optional)
5 English muffins, split

Directions:
Toast english muffin (or broil).
Pile on all desired toppings.
Place on cookie sheet and cook until cheese is melted. (Microwave 15-25 sec)
Enjoy!
