Title:
Oriental Pork Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871687.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup plum jam
1 Tbsp cornstarch
1/2 tsp gingerroot
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp garlic powder
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 tsp red wine vinegar
4 flour tortillas
1 tsp oil
1 pound pork loin chops - cut into thin strips
6 cups broccoli slaw - purchased

Directions:
In small bowl, combine jam, cornstarch, ginger, dry mustard, garlic powder, soy sauce, and vinegar; mix well. Set aside. Heat tortillas as directed on package.
Meanwhile, heat oil in nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Add pork; cook and stir 4 to 5 minutes or until no longer pink.
Add jam mixture and coleslaw blend; cokk and stir until sauce in bubbly and thickened. Spoon mixture evenly down center of each warm tortilla; roll up and serve.
