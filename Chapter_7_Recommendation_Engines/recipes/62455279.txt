Title:
Clean Eating Hearty Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006293.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb lean ground meat (beef, bison or turkey)
3 - 4 fresh garlic cloves, minced or pressed
1 medium onion, chopped
2 small zucchinis, chopped (or fresh mushrooms to substitute)
28 oz. canned crushed tomatoes (if not crushed, whir in blender)
15 oz tomato sauce
2 - 2 1/2 tblsp Sucanat (unrefined dehydrated cane juice)
1 1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried marjoram
1 1/2 tbsp dried sweet basil
Whole grain spaghetti (use brown rice pasta for gluten-free diets)

Directions:
In large saucepan, brown ground meat, garlic, onion and zucchini (or mushrooms).
Pour in tomatoes, tomato sauce, Sucanat, herbs and seasonings.
Stir and simmer for 30 to 60 minutes (the longer the better) to blend flavors.
Serve over hot pasta.

This is a satisfyingly hearty sauce that you can feel good about eating!
