Title:
White Bean and Sausage Ragout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
8 oz. chicken sausage, sliced
2 cups broccoli florets
3 garlic cloves, minced
6 cups chopped, trimmed kale
1/2 cup water
2 16oz. cans white beans, rinsed and drained
1 14.5oz. can diced tomatoes
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium high heat.  Saute onion and sausage until sausage is browned.  Add broccoli and garlic and cook 2 min. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
