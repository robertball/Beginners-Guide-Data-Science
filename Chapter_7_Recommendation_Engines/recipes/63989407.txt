Title:
The Shack's BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Ketchup
1 C Vinegar
1 C Water
1 ½ T Chili Powder (Gebhardt, if you can find it)
1 ½ T Salt
1 ½ T Sugar
1 ½ T Black Pepper
1 small onion minced

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well. Stir over medium heat until boiling. Lower heat and simmer until thickened. You can store in a jar in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
The Shack is a Little Rock legend.  This is their famous BBQ Sauce.  Most people don't know that Little Rock has it's own style of sauce which is only slightly sweet, has a bit of tang and a bite of spice.
