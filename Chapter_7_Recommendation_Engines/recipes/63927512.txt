Title:
Gluten, Dairy and Grain Free Honey Sweetened Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup honey
1/2 cup coconut oil
1 Tbsp. vanilla extract
3 eggs
1 cup almond flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix honey, butter, vanilla and eggs until smooth. Add almond flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt. Stir to blend. Pour into greased 8x8x2 inch pan. Bake for 25 minutes or until center is no longer batter. Cool for 20 minutes before cutting.
