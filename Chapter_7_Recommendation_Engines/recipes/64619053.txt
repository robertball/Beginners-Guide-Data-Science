Title:
Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup flour
1/3 cup bacon grease
1 TBSP chili powder, or to taste
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp salt
1 can chicken broth
1 can water

Directions:
Melt bacon grease in heavy sauce pan, add flour, chili powder and salts and cook 2 -3 mins). Add broth and water and cook until boiling and slightly thick.
