Title:
Cheddar Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can mushroom soup
1/2 tsp. pepper
4 medium potatoes, thickly sliced
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Grease a 2 quart baking dish. Mix soup and pepper. Arrange potato slices in dish in over lapping rows and sprinkle with cheese. Spoon soup mixture over cheese. Cover and bake at 350 for 45-50 minutes. Uncover and bake until potatoes are tender and cheese has melted.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
This takes only 4 ingredients and is a fast and delicious recipe you can serve with any type of meat.
