Title:
Fabulous Cornbread Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese
1 lemon
5 cloves garlic
1 small can sliced jalepenos
olive oil

Directions:
*Preheat the grill to medium-high heat
*Peel garlic cloves and place in center of a square piece of aluminum foil.  Drizzle cloves with olive oil, seal foil and wrap package in an additional piece of foil.  Place directly on the hot coals of the grill.  Alternatively, place in a 425 degree oven.  Cook garlic for 10 minutes until the garlic is very soft and begins to carmelize.
*While garlic is cooking, place softened cream cheese in food processor.  Add juice of one lemon and pulse until smooth.
*Add garlic cloves to cream cheese mixture and blend until evenly incorporated.  Season to taste with jalepenos and liquid from the can, pulsing to incorporate evenly.

Submitter: 
ABROSEN
Description: 
A creamy, spicy dip for fresh cornbread cooked on the barbeque that also tastes great with corn chips or veggies.
