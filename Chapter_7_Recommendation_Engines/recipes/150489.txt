Title:
Chocolate Chip Toffee Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992913.jpg
Ingredients:
Crisco速 Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
2 1/2 cups Pillsbury BEST速 All Purpose Flour
2/3 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
3/4 cup butter or margarine
1 large egg, slightly beaten
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
1 cup coarsely chopped nuts
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND速 Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 (8 ounce) package toffee baking bits, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Coat 9x13-inch baking pan with no-stick cooking spray.
In large bowl, stir together flour and sugar. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add egg; mix well.
Stir in 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips and nuts. Reserve 1 1/2 cups mixture. Press remaining crumb mixture onto bottom of prepared pan. Bake 10 minutes.
Pour EAGLE BRAND(R) evenly over partially baked crust; reserve 1/4 cup toffee bits. Sprinkle remaining toffee bits over layer pan. Sprinkle reserved crumb mixture and remaining 1/2 cup chocolate chips over top.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup toffee bits. Cool completely. Cut into bars. Store covered.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND速
Description: 
These rich cookie bars are filled with nuts, chocolate chips, and crunchy toffee bits.
