Title:
Fish and Citrus Tahini Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
fish marinade
olive oil
salt
orange juice
garlic
12-16 oz fish
sauce
1/2 c orange juice
1 T soy sauce
2 T tahini
1/2 c water
juice of half lime
couple garlic cloves
handful cilantro
slice onion
salt

Directions:
marinate fish in olive oil, garlic, salt, orange juice
combine all ingredients for sauce in blender and season to taste
heat saute pan to medium/high heat
add little olive oil/butter
fry fish approx 2 min each side (or less!)
remove fish
add marinade and little sauce and reduce to taste

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Easy citrus marinade and sauce for fish; cooked on stovetop
