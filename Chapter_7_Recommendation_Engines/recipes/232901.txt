Title:
Lemon Fizz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1056274.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups vanilla ice cream, softened
1 1/2 cups lemon sherbet, softened
3 cups chilled lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Blend ice cream and sherbet together in a blender until smooth; pour into 2 large chilled glasses. Pour 1 1/2 cups soda into each glass and stir gently.

Submitter: 
Mr. Cookies
Description: 
Blend ice cream and sherbet together and stir with some lemon-lime flavored soda for a refreshing fizzy beverage.
