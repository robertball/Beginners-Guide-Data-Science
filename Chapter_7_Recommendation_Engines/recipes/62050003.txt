Title:
Crispy Onion Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 pkg. (1.8 oz. size) powdered onion soup mix
1/2 cup dried bread crumbs
1 egg, beatened
1 TB butter, melted

Directions:
Pound the chicken to 1/4 inch thickness.  In a shallow pan, mix soup with dried bread crumbs.  Dredge chicken in the egg and then into the soup mixture.  Place chicken in a greased, shallow baking dish.  Lightly drizzle the melted butter over the chicken.  Bake at 325 degrees for approximately 45 minutes or chicken is tender.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
I had some leftover packages of onion soup mix.  I coated the skinless, boneless chicken breasts with a mixture of soup and dried bread crumbs and then baked it.  It turned out crispy, tender and great tasting!!!  I served it with a sweet corn casserole and a nice garden salad.
