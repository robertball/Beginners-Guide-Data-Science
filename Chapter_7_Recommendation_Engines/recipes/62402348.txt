Title:
Easy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
breast meat from deli chicken (2 c.), chopped/shredded
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 c. sour cream
1 small can chopped chilis, undrained
2 T. taco seasoning
8 large tortillas
4 c. shredded Mexican cheese
chili powder

Directions:
Spray a 9x13" casserole with Pam; set aside.
In a large bowl, combine chicken, soup, sour cream, taco seasoning, chilis, and 2 c. cheese.  Mix well.
Take a flour tortilla and spread 1/3-1/2 c. filling mixture down middle and roll up.
Place in dish, seam side down.
Repeat until all tortillas are filled.
If there is any remaining filling, spread over top of enchiladas.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese and chili powder.
Bake 20-30 min; until hot throughout and cheese is melted.
