Title:
Starr's Meatball Subs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/553136.jpg
Ingredients:
4-5 Sub rolls
Vegan meatballs
Vegan parmesan cheese
A jar of your favorite red sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Heat up the sauce so itâ€™s nice and warm.
Slice rolls on one side and spread out onto a foil-lined baking sheet.
Spoon a ladle of sauce onto the rolls.
Place 3 or 4 meatballs on the roll.
Sprinkle generously with parmesan.
Bake for about 10 minutes or until the cheese becomes golden brown.

Description: 
I love meatball subs so much, but never could find them in restaurants that are just right, so I made my own!
