Title:
Red Devil's Food Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening
1 1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. cocoa
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. flour
1 c. boiling water
1 c. milk
2 tsp. baking soda
1 t. vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl cream shortening, sugar and cocoa.
Add eggs one at a time, blending well after each addition.
Sift flour, salt and baking soda into a separate bowl.
Add flour mixture and milk alternately, starting w flour and ending with flour.
Beat well after each addition.
Add boiling water and vanilla.
Scrape sides of bowl and mix well.
***Batter will be thin****
Grease and flour a 9X13 baking dish.
Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake at 350 for 30 min or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cool thouroughly before topping with your favorite frosting.
