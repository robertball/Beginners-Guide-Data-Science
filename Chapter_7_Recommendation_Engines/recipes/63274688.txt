Title:
The BEST Peanut butter frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter - softened
1 cup of creamy peanut butter
1 8 oz block of softened cream cheese
1 Tbs of pure vanilla
6 - 8 cups of confectionary sugar
1/3 cup of milk

Directions:
Cream the butter, peanut butter and cream cheese until smooth and creamy.  Add in the vanilla until well blended.  Add confectionary sugar one cup at a time until the frosting is the desired consistency.  If the milk is needed add it one tsp. at a time.
This frosting is thick and creamy.  Should be kept refridgetated.

Submitter: 
Brenda
Description: 
This is a frosting recipe that tastes great on any kind of cake or brownie.  It is devoured by all who I make it for.  The guys I work with ask me to make any kind of cupcake as long as this frosting is on them!
