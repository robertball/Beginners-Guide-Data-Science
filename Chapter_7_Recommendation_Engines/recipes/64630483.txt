Title:
Ratatouille with Bitter Melon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion
1 clove garlic
1-2 stems celery
1/3 pc red bell pepper
1/3 pc yellow bell pepper
3 pcs asparagus
1/3 pc bitter melon
1/2 pc eggplant
200 grams tomato sauce
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1 Tbsp olive oil
pepper

Directions:
Start by cutting and cubing the onion, celery, bell peppers, asparagus  and eggplant with widths of about 1-cm. Mince the garlic. For the bitter melon, cut it along its length then take the seeds using a spoon. Cut it into cubes then soak it in water with salt for about 5 minutes before using.
Put all the vegetables in the microwave pressure cooker  bowl.
Add the tomato sauce, basil, thyme and oregano. Mix well then cover.
Bring in to the microwave and cook for 7 minutes using 600W.
Wait for the pressure to go down before opening. Mix the ingredients and add salt to adjust taste. Add some ground peppers on top then serve as main dish or side.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This is a recipe for ratatouille cooked in a microwave pressure cooker.  This is a vegetarian dish and the delicious taste of this dish comes from the mixed vegetables. This is Okinawa-inspired as we've added bitter melon as one of the main ingredients. Bitter melon is one of the vegetables that make Okinawa prefecture famous in Japan. Try this recipe at home and you'll be surprised how delicious it is.
