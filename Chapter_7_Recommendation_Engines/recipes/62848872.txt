Title:
Rosemary Broccoli Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dry white wine
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
1 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 T applesauce
fresh broccoli (about 3 bunches)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Simmer, uncovered until broccoli is tender.  We usually spoon the sauce/broccoli over rice and serve with grilled pork chops.

Submitter: 
Tamara Gray
Description: 
It's surprisingly simple and quick.  Garlic, rosemary, and white wine flavor the broccoli.  The aroma is wonderful.Modified from: Modenese Pork Chops
