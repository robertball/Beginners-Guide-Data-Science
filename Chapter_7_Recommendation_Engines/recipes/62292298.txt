Title:
Kickin' Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb block Velveeta (cubed)
1 small tomato minced
1 small white onion minced
1-3 jalapenos minced (depending on the heat you want)
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Add all ingredients into a microwavable bowl. Microwave on high for 1 mins. Continue with this until cheese is melted and hot.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
My daughter wanted some pico de gallo from the store to go with our King Ranch Chicken dinner, so I got some for her. I took half and made some killer queso!
