Title:
The Hot Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. packages cream cheese, softened
4 T butter, softened
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
4 T macaroni & cheese seasoning powder (from the box)
1/2 t celery salt
1/2 t ground mustard
1/2 t cayenne pepper
1/2 t white pepper
3/4 t garlic powder
1/2 t red pepper flakes
4-8 T Frank's hot sauce (to taste)
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Stir together the cream cheese and butter until smooth.
Add the shredded cheese.  Add cheese seasoning powder, celery salt, ground mustard, cayenne pepper, white pepper, garlic powder, red pepper flakes and hot sauce.  Stir until smooth.  Continue adding hot sauce to taste.  Form into two balls, or one big log or ball (chill mix before rolling for 1 hour in refrigerator if necessary).  Roll in chopped nuts, coating evenly.  Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
