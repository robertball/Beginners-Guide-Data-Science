Title:
Big Ray's Creamy Pork Chops For the Crock Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131486.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs shortening
4 bonless pork chops
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
sprinkle seasoned salt
sprinkle ground pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 large onion, sliced
6-8 medium potatoes, diced
2 bell peppers, diced
1 can  4 oz)of sliced mushrooms
2 (10.75 ounce) cans cream of mushroom soup
2 cups milk

Directions:
Melt shortening in a large skillet over medium-high heat. mix flour with seasonings and dredge pork chops in flour mixture. Cook in hot skillet, turning once to brown both sides.
Layer pork chops and vegetables into a slow cooker. Mix soup and milk and pour over the meat.  Cover and cook on High for 4 to 5 hours, or on Low for 8 to 10 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
It's an easy way to get a homecooked meal into a busy schedule. Brown the pork chops the night before and the next morning throw it all into the slowcooker before you head out the door for work.  Come home and your meals' waiting.
