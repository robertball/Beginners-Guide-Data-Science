Title:
Ladoo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick unsalted butter
1/4 cup raisins
2 cups semolina
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups flaked coconut
1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
1/4 cup walnuts
pich of ground turmeric
1 can(8 ounce)evaporated milk

Directions:
Melt the butter in a wok.Add the raisins and saute them for 5 minutes.Remove the raisins from the pan.Add semolina.cook stirring occasionally till golden brown.Add sugar and coconut.Mix well then add cardamom,walnut,raisins and turmeric.cook another 5 minutes and remove from the heat.cool.
Dip your hands in cold water and shape small balls.refrigerate before serving
