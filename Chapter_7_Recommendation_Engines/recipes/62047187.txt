Title:
Herbed Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked long grain rice
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup butter
2 1/2 cups water
2.5 ounce package Lipton Chicken Noodle Soup mix
2 tsp. dried parsley
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/4 tsp. rubbed sage
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
In large skillet, saute rice, celery and onion in butter until rice is golden brown, about 5 minutes. Stir in the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand, covered, for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sue S.
Description: 
This is a very tasty herbed rice side dish. Easy to prepare and a nice change from the usual plain rice.
