Title:
Shrimp Nests Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. dry angel-hair pasta
1 clove garlic, minced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
3 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
1 cup cooked baby shrimp
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
¼ tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 Tbsp. fresh basil, chopped

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, sauté garlic and pepper with 1 Tbsp. olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat for 5 minutes. Stir in shrimp, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Cook for 1-2 minutes to heat through.
Drain pasta; toss with remaining oil and basil. Twirl into nests and spoon in shrimp.
