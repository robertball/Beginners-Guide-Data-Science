Title:
Creamed Tuna of Beef On Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup  tuna, drained (or chipped beef)
2 tbsp butter
3 tablespoons flour
2 cups milk
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
8 slices bread

Directions:
Melt butter and blend in flour, add milk and stir while cooking until mixture is thick and smooth.  Add salt and pepper and canned tuna.  Serve on hot squares of toast.
