Title:
White Castles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef (browned & drained)
1 pkg. Onion soup mix
1 can Cream of mushroom soup
1 block of Velveeta Cheese (not the small one!)
1 pkg. Dollar Rolls *optional*

Directions:
Throw it all together in a crockpot and let it melt!!!  Spread on the dollar rolls or use as a dip.

Description: 
A groundbeef mixture that tastes just like White Castle's cheeseburgers.
