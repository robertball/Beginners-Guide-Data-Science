Title:
Sweet & Savory Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup pecan halves
3 TBSP icing sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp allspice
1/8 tsp nutmeg
8 cups baby spinach leaves
1 cup radicchio
1 large ripe pear, peeled, cored and diced
2 oz. brie, diced
2 TBSP organge juice concentrate, thawed
2 TBSP olive oil
1 TBSP balsamic vinegar
1 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp liquid honey
1/2 tsp dijon mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Rinse pecans with cold water. Drain, but do not let dry. Combine icing sugar, cinnamon, allspice and nutmeg in a small bowl. Dip pecans in the sugar mixture, coating them well. Spread on an oiled baking sheet and bake 15 minutes. Remove, cool and chop coarsely. Combine the spinach, radicchio, pear and brie in a large serving bowl. Whisk the juice concentrate, oil, vinegar, garlic, honey and mustard together in a small bowl. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss. Garnish with pecans.
