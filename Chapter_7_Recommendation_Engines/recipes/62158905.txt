Title:
Tangy Buttermilk Scalloped Potatoes au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
6 Tblsp. Butter or margarine
6 Tblsp. Flour
3 cups Buttermilk
½ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. pepper
1½  cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
6 medium sized red skinned potatoes
1 medium onion, peeled

Directions:
1.	Melt butter or margarine in large saucepan over medium heat.
2.	Place buttermilk in microwave safe bowl or measuring cup and heat at high for 3 to 4 minutes. (Buttermilk may partially separate.  This is normal and won’t affect the flavour or texture of the finished product.)
3.	Whisk flour into melted butter and add salt and pepper to make roux.
4.	Whisk buttermilk into roux and continue cooking and stirring with whisk over medium heat until thick and creamy.
5.	Add shredded cheese and continue to whisk until cheese is melted and smooth.  Remove from heat.
6.      Preheat oven to 350°F.
7.	Wash potatoes and slice thinly leaving skins on.
8.	Julienne slice the peeled onion.
9.	Layer potatoes, onions and cheese sauce into a 13” x 9” baking dish, starting and finishing with cheese sauce. (Should be about three layers of potatoes, with onion in between.)
10.	Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
11.     Remove foil and continue baking for another 30 minutes to brown top.

Submitter:

Description: 
I was out of milk one day when I was half way through making scalloped potatoes, so I used buttermilk instead and the result was unbelievable.  I have made this dish a few times since and everyone who tries it really likes it, so I thought I would share it with my All Recipes family.
