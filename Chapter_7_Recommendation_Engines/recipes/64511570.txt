Title:
Cornflake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
3 cups cornflakes

Directions:
Mix margarine and sugars together till well blended.
Beat in egg and vanilla.
Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto baking sheet.
Bake @ 350 x 14 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A nice crunchy addition to spice cookies. This makes a very small batch of cookies, so I've doubled the ingredients. From my favorite cookbook series.
