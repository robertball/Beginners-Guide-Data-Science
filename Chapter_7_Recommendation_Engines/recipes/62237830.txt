Title:
Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 Carrots Coarsely Chopped
2 Stalks of Celery Coarsely Chopped
2 Onions Cut Into Wedges
2-3 Scallions Finely Chopped
1 Strip of Bacon
2-1/2 Tbs of Olive Oil
2 Large Potatoes Coarsely Cubed
1 Quart of Water
4 Tsp of BtB Chicken Base (or 4 Chicken Bouillon Cubes Crushed)
2 Tbs Butter
1/4 Cup of White Wine
1 Tbs of Sugar
1/2 of an Anaheim Pepper Coarsely Chopped
1/4 Tsp each, Nutmeg, Cumin, and Thyme
1/2 Tsp of Curry Powder
1 Tsp of Black Pepper
3-4 Cloves of Garlic Chopped
2 Bay Leaves
2 Cups of Pumpkin Puree (I use frozen pumpkin from the garden.)
4oz of Milk
4oz of Heavy Cream

Directions:
Saut√© carrots, onions, scallions, bacon, and olive oil for 20 minutes then remove the bacon and discard.
Add potatoes, water, bouillon, butter, wine, sugar, Anaheim pepper, nutmeg, cumin, thyme, curry powder, black pepper, garlic, and bay leaves.  Bring to a boil then reduce and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
Add the pumpkin puree and bring to a boil again, then reduce to a simmer for 10-20 minutes.
Remove the bay leaves and puree in a blender.  Add the puree back to the pot and then blend in the milk and cream.
