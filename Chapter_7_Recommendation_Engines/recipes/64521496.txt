Title:
Vegan Pumpkin Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 15 oz cans of solid pack pumpkin
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups water
3 tbsp lemon juice
3 tbsp ground cinnamon, or to taste
3/4 tsp salt
3/4 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
-In a large pot (standard sized large pots will be just slightly too small), combine all ingredients.
-Bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
-Reduce heat and simmer for 20 mins to allow flavours to blend
-Cool, spoon into jars, cover and store in fridge up to 3 weeks.

Submitter: 
Lexy N
Description: 
This is not as thick as butter, but is easily spreadable and tastes like pumpkin pie. I make a big batch up around Christmas and put into small jars to give it as part of a Christmas gift. Everyone looks forward to it.
