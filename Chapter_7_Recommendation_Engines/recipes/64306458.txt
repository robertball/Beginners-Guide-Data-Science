Title:
Gluten Free Cranberry Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 Oz (8 cups) fresh cranberries
3 cups gluten free flour (I used Bob's Red Mill pancake mix)
3 Cups organic sugar
1 Cup Hazel nut flour ( or one cup chopped hazelnuts)
1 Tablespoon Almond Extract
3 Cups (3/4 pound) melted butter
1 Teaspoon salt
8 eggs

Directions:
Butter a 9x13 baking pan (I used oval pyrex)
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix dry ingredients together in large bowl
Melt butter
Beat Eggs
Stir butter, eggs, and almond extract into dry ingredients
Stir in cranberries
Pour into prepared baking dish
Bake one hour or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Serve warm with Grace Harbor Farms vanilla yogurt, ice cream, or whipped chream
