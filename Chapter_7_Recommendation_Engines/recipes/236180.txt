Title:
Sriracha Cocktail Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1108642.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
3 tablespoons Sriracha chile sauce
2 tablespoons grated fresh horseradish
4 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons lime juice

Directions:
Mix ketchup, chile sauce, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, and lime juice together in a bowl; refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
