Title:
Smashed Turnips and Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small bag baby carrots
2 regular size turnips
Butter
Salt

Directions:
Peel the turnips. Chop the carrots and turnips into 1 inch cubes. Place in a pan and cover like you're making mashed potatoes. Boil until soft about 15-20 minutes. Drain and smash or use a mixer/immersion blender to blend them up. Add butter and salt to taste. Yum!
