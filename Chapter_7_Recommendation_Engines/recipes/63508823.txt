Title:
BBQ Pork tenderloin nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. pork tenderloin, Grill mates Pork rub or equivalent pork rub, apple juice, apple cider vinegar, worcestershire sauce,  hickory liquid smoke, KC Masterpiece Honey BBQ sauce or equivalent,  Utz Tortilla chips or equivalent, Monterey jack cheese, sharp cheddar cheese, iceberg lettuce, tomatoes, avocado, jalapenos, green onion, sour cream, salsa, cilantro.

Directions:
Place pork rub on tenderloin after it has been towel dried. Allow to marinate on the meat for 2 hours in the refrigerator. After 2 hours, bake in the oven covered at 325 F for 3 hours in 2 cups apple juice, 1 cup apple cider vinegar, 2 T worcestershire sauce, and 1 T hickory liquid smoke.  After pork had cooked, shred with a fork and knife and add to a sauce pan and cover with a whole bottle of honey BBQ sauce such as KC Masterpiece.  While the pork is cooking, prep the toppings by shredding the monterey jack cheese and cheddar cheese to the amount you desire.  Slice and prep lettuce, tomatoes, avocado, jalapeños, and green onion to taste.   desired.
Place tortilla chips (Utz restaurant style was great) in a pan and top with cheeses as desired, and jalapeños, and bake until the cheese is melted at 350 F. .  Once the cheese is melted, removed the pan and add toppings of the bbq pork tenderloin, lettuce, tomatoes, green onions, sour cream, avocado, and cilantro all to taste.
