Title:
Caviar Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard cooked egg
1/4 cup mayonaisse
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1/2 teaspoon dill weed
1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
1/8 teaspoon salt
Dash Pepper
1 Dash Paparika
6 teaspoons caviar

Directions:
Cut eggs in half lengthwise.
Remove yolks; set whites aside.
In a bowl, mash the yolks.
Add mayonnaise, horseradish, dill, mustard, salt and pepper; mix well.
Pipe or spoon into egg whites.
Sprinkle with paprika.
Refrigerate until serving.
Right before seving top with 1 teaspoon of caviar on each egg.
