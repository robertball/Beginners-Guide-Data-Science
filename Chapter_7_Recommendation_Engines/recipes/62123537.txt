Title:
Basic ice cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole milk, chilled
2 cups heavy cream, chilled
3/4 cup white granulated sugar
1-2 teaspoon vanilla extract, or to taste

Directions:
Pour whole milk and sugar in a small bowl and whisk until sugar is dissolved. Stir in heavy cream and vanilla. Turn on an ice cream maker and pour mixture into it. Let it mix for 25-30 minutes or until thickened. If you are going to add the semisweet chocolate chips, add it in the last 5 minutes of of mixing.

Submitter: 
xxDXxx
Description: 
It is a quick delicious ice cream. Also it is very important that you chill the heavy cream and the milk before you use it. Also if you want to make it sweeter you can add semisweet chocolate chips.
