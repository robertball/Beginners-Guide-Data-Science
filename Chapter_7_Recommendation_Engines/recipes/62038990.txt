Title:
Microwave Carmel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. brown sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/4 C. light corn syrup
1 stick butter
1/2 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
On high, microwave the first 4 ingredients for 4 1/2 minutes. Add 1/2 tsp. baking soda to the cooked mixture ans stir. Pour into a grocery bag containing 5 quarts of popped popcorn....and shake. Microwave on high for 90 seconds, 60 sec.,40 sec,and then 30 sec. In between each of these times you need to take bag out of the microwave and shake well. Ex:microwave 90 sec...remove from microwave then shake....60 sec..remove and shake..etc.
When i make this for Christmas, i substitute the brown sugar with white. I also add green food coloring or red:)
