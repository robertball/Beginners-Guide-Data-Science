Title:
Banket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup butter
1/2 cup water
1 1/2 cups almond paste
2 eggs
3/4 cup white sugar
1 pinch salt
1 egg white, beaten
Apricot Jam - optional

Directions:
In food processer, cut cold butter or margarine into flour until the mixture has a crumb-like texture.
Add cold water.
Mix together until the mixture forms a ball. Do not overmix.
Divide into 4 balls
Chill dough overnight.
Filling:
In a medium bowl, blend together almond paste, eggs, 3/4 cup sugar.
Refrigerate over night.
When it is time to prepare the banket:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (225 degrees C).
Take out one ball at a time to roll out on floured surface.
Squish the ball of dough briefly in your hands.
Then roll out into 15 inch by 6 inch strips.
Place filling along the center of each long strip of dough. Roll up, and pinch the ends to seal.
Seal with water.
Brush with egg white (and watered down apricot jam if desired)
Poke holes 1 inch apart
Parchment paper on cookie sheets
Bake at 450 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden.
If Freezing:
DO NOT BRUSH WITH EGG WHITES OR POKE HOLES, do this only when you are baking them.
Wrap in saran and freezer tinfoil
