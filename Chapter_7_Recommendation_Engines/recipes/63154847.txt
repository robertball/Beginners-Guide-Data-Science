Title:
Strawberry Bellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c strawberries, sliced
1/4 c powdered sugar
1 T brandy
1 1/2 c sparkling wine
3 large strawberries

Directions:
Place in a blender and let stand 10 mins. the sliced strawberries, powdered sugar and brandy.
Process until smooth.
Chill.
Place 1/4 c strawberry mixture in each of 3 champagne glasses.
Add 1/4 c sparkling wine to each glass.
Stir to blend.
Garnish each glass with a strawberry, if desired.

Submitter: 
Helen
Description: 
A nice drink for a ladies brunch or shower.
