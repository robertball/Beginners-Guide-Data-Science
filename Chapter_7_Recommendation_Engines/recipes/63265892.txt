Title:
Stan's Seasoned Ketchup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) bottle ketchup
2 teaspoons Tabasco sauce
1 tablespoon dried onion granules
2 teaspoons granulated garlic
1/4 cup prepared mustard (French's)
2 tablespoons smokehouse ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the ketchup, Tabasco sauce, dried onion, dried garlic, mustard, and black pepper. Return to the ketchup bottle or another squeeze bottle and store in the refrigerator. Use as you would ketchup.

Submitter: 
StanDiego
Description: 
a good base but give it a twang by adding 1/4 to 1/2 cup of prepared mustard. I prefer French's but dijon style will work as well. I also like to add a couple of tablespoons of ground black pepper. Smokehouse pepper is the best!Modified from: Bill's Seasoned Ketchup
