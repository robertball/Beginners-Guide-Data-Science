Title:
Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups uncooked long-grain brown rice
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a 2 quart casserole dish combine rice, broth, soup, butte r,  &Worcestershire sauce.
Bake covered for 1 hour, stirring once after 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
This robust rice is the perfect side dish to serve with any meat, or with vegetables.  Rice is baked with beef broth, French onion soup, butter, Worcestershire and basil.Modified from: Brown Rice
