Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764533.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground meat
4 Tablespoons oil (if meat is extra lean)
2 teaspoons salt
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 Tablespoons garlic, minced
1 ounce chili powder
3 cups water
1 can (12 ounce) tomato juice
2 Tablespoons ground cumin
1 can re-fried beans
2 cans New Orleans Style Kidney Beans

Directions:
Brown ground meat. Drain fat but reserve for later. Add salt to taste.
Add chopped onions and cook till tender.
Add garlic and cook until onions are clear.
Add chili powder and cumin. Mix well.
Add tomato juice and water. Simmer with top on for 2 1/2 hours.
Add re-fried beans, mix well.
(Optional) If a thicker chili is desired, mix 2 Tablespoons of cornstarch with 1 Tablespoon water and mix into chili. Note: when adding cornstarch/water into a hot liquid, make sure to drizzle the cornstarch/water mixture in while stirring.
Add beans and simmer for about 30 minutes.
