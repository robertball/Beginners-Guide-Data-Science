Title:
Easy and delicious squash soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups fresh butternut squash cubed (approximately 1 large squash or 3lbs frozen)
1 red bell pepper
1 yellow bell pepper
1 large yellow onion
2 teaspoons minced garlic (approximately 3 cloves fresh garlic)
6 cups water
1 tablespoon salt
2 tablespoons sugar
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1 tablespoon nutmeg
1 tablespoon cinnamon

Directions:
1. Chop both peppers and onion into big chunks
2. Add peppers, onion, garlic and squash to water in a large soup pot and bring to a boil
3. Cover pot and let simmer for approximately 30 minutes
4. Add salt and sugar
5. Using immersion blender, puree the soup until creamy and smooth
6. Using immersion blender, slowly add in the heavy whipping cream
7. Stir in nutmeg and cinnamon
8. Serve hot
*Before serving, taste and adjust seasoning.  You can add sugar if you prefer more sweetness.
* If you do not have an immersion blender, you can use a regular blender, just mix in portions then return to the soup pot to slowly whisk in the whipping cream, nutmeg and cinnamon
