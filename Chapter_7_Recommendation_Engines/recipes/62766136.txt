Title:
Creamed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1 1/2 cups half and half
4 (10 ounce) boxes boxes frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry; reserve 1/2 cup liquid
3 large garlic cloves, minced
1/2 c minced onion
3 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
Salt and ground black pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Sautee garlic and onion stirring constantly. Whisk in flour. Add milk mixture, then whisk until smooth. Stir in spinach; cook until sauce is thick and bubbly and spinach is tender but still green, about 5 minutes.  Stir in cheese, nutmeg, and a generous sprinkling of salt and pepper. Serve hot.
