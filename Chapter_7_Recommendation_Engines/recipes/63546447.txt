Title:
Crock Pot Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup cooked rice (or quinoa)
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 diced yellow onion
1 cup frozen vegetables (whichever ones you like)
3 cooked chicken breasts (or whatever meat you would like to use)
3 eggs
teryaki sauce (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crock pot, and top with teryaki sauce if desired. Cook on low for 2 hours and serve.

Submitter: 
MATTOON250
Description: 
A delicious, easy way to use leftovers! I seem to always have some kind of leftover rice or meat and I hate throwing away food, so this is a great way to make a meal out of leftovers.
