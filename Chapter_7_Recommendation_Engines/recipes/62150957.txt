Title:
Italian Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 small onion, minced
2 tablespoons chopped garlic
Italian herbs to taste
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup chopped carrots
1 can diced tomatoes
two handfuls fresh baby spinach
2 cans white beans, drained AND/OR 1 cup macaroni pasta
5 cans chicken broth

Directions:
1.Saute onion, garlic, chicken, and carrots in olive oil until chicken is cooked through.
2. Add can of tomatoes, Italian herbs to taste, and salt and pepper to taste. Reduce heat to low. Stir.
3. Place spinach on top of mixture. Cover and cook until spinach is wilted.
4. Add beans and chicken broth. Simmer.
5. If using macaroni, bring soup to a boil, add pasta, and cook al dente.

Submitter:

Description: 
This soup is so quick, easy and delicious. And it is packed with nutrition. I threw it together one rainy evening while searching through my pantry for something to make for dinner, and my family loved it.
