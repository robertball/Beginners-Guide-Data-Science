Title:
Whole Wheat Pineapple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106798.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup margarine
1 egg
1 cup crushed pineapple, undrained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Sift together: flours, baking powder, salt.
In small bowl, cream together until fluffy: sugar, margarine
Add egg
Beat well; stir in pineapple
Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture and stir just enough to moisten flour.
Fill greased muffin tins 2/3 full.
Bake 15-20 minutes.
Remove from tins at once and serve hot.
