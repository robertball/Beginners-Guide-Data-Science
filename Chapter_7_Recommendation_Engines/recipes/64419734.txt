Title:
Potato Salad with Bacon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Yukon gold potatoes
1 tsp salt
1/2 lb bacon, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
8 green onions, chopped
salt and pepper
For the dressing
1/2 cup mayo
1/4 cup dijon mustard
3 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 tbsp reserved bacon fat - optional but tasty
2 tbsp hot dog relish

Directions:
put potatoes in a 4 quart pot and cover with cold water.  add salt and bring to a simmer.  cook until potatoes are tender and easily pierced with a knife - cut the potatoes into finished size for boiling
meanwhile brown the bacon until it is golden and crispy.  drain the bacon fat reserving a few tablespoons for the dressing
place potatoes, celery and green onions into a large salad bowl.  add the dressing and season with salt and pepper - toss well
for the dressing
whisk together the mayo, dijon, vinegar, bacon fat and relish
