Title:
Romanian Style Boiled Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One onion (Chopped)
One Rib Celery (chopped)
One Bell Pepper (Chopped)
1 lb. Sausage (sliced)
3 Tomatoes (diced)
2 heads Cabbage (sliced/chopped)
One Tablespoon oil

Directions:
In a pot, add oil, and put fire on medium to medium low heat.
Add oinions, sausage, bell pepper, tomatoes.  Let saute till about halfway done.
Take the shredded cabbage, sprinkle with a little bit of salt, and "strangle" the cabbage with salt in your hands, then add to the pot.
Mix all ingredients well.
Let boil in it's own juices for about 30 minutes.
Check the flavor of the broth, and add seasoning if necessary.
Boil until cabbage is tender.

Submitter: 
Odessa Toma
Description: 
A mix of onions, tomatoes, celery bell pepper cabbage and seasoning.  Goes great with a load of French Bread
