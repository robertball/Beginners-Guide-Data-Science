Title:
Fig & Brie Crostini's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fig Spread:
1 cup dried figs, finely chopped
1/4 cups minced white onion
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 tsp ground ginger
pinch of garlic powder
pinch of cinnamon
pinch of cayenne pepper
pinch of ground cloves
Other ingredients:
1 loaf of french bread
1 circle of brie
1 granny smith apple
1 tbsp oregano
A few spritz's of olive oil spray

Directions:
Fig spread:
Simmer all ingredients in a saucepan for 30 minutes on a low heat, stirring occassionally.
Transfer to a bowl, cover and refrigerate overnight.
Assembly:
Turn your oven broiler on medium.
Cut the loaf of french bread into 1" slices.
Place each piece on a baking sheet and lightly spray each side with olive oil.
Toast under the broiler for 2 minutes each side, then set aside to cool slightly.
Use a blender or food processor to puree the refrigerated fig spread.
Cut the brie into bite-sized pieces.
Cut the apple into thin shoestrings.
Spread a teaspoon of the spread on each slice of toasted bread, top with a slice of brie and a few pieces of apple. Sprinkle a pinch of oregano on each slice, then broil till brie is starting to melt and brown.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
ShannonMcK
Description: 
Easy and impressive appetizer! Make the fig spread the night before to save yourself the prep time on the day of an event.
