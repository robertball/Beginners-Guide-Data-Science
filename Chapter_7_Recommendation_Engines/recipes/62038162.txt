Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 sliced of chopped bacon
1 medium onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 lb. ground beef
1 cup uncooked rice
1 large can whole tomatoes
1 12 oz. can tomato sauce
1 cup water
1 tsp. paprika
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute the bacon until crispy. Pour off most of the fat and brown the onion, green pepper and ground beef. Put this in a large slow-cooker along with the rice tomatoes and seasonings. Rinse the can with the 1 cup of water. Mix everything well, cover and cook high for 3-4 hours or low for 7-8 hours. Serve with grated cheese and crusty bread.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Judy Blum Ryan
Description: 
This has been in my family for many years and usually baked in a casserole.  I adapted it for the slow-cooker.
