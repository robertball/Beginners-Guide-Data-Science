Title:
Octoberfest Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb pot roast (beef)
2 Tbsp dijon mustard
1 1/2 Tbsp brown sugar
2 cloves garlic
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3 onions(sliced)
1 cup beer
1 cup beef stock
salt & pepper
1 Tbsp cornstarch blended with water

Directions:
Brown the roast on all sides in a dutch oven. Combine the next 4 ingredients in a small bowl, spread this mixture over the roast and than layer with onions.Pour beer and stock over top. Season with salt and pepper.
Cover with a lid or foil and place in a 325F oven for 3 1/2 hours.Remove the lid during the last 20 minutes.
Remove the roast from the pan and cover with foil for 10 minutes. In the meantime thicken the juices with cornstarch mixed in water and bring to a low boil until thickened. Serve on the side as gravy or pour over the sliced roast beef on a platter.
