Title:
Chocolate Glasur (Schokolodenglasur) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c powder sugar
2/3 c bakers chocolate (or semi sweet chocolate chips)
4 or 5 tbsp of milk
1 tbsp of cocoa butter (or butter)

Directions:
Cut chocolate into pieces,and combine all ingredients in a double bath. Melt all together until smooth, and use while still semi hot.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A german version of a chocolate glaze fro the cookies and cakes. This is translated from my mother's cookebook she brought over 50 years ago.
