Title:
Vinnie's Killer Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060868.jpg
Ingredients:
4 gallons red wine
1/2 gallon cherry brandy
1 (16 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 (16 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen fruit punch drink concentrate, thawed
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen grape juice concentrate, thawed

Directions:
Stir together the wine, brandy, orange juice concentrate, lemonade concentrate, fruit punch concentrate, and grape juice concentrate in a 5 gallon insulated beverage container or cooler.
