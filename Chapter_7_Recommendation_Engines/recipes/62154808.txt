Title:
Oatmeal Hazelnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (8oz) butter, softened
3/4 cup packed light-brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
2 large eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3 cups uncooked old-fashioned or quick oats
1 bag (12oz) semisweet chocolate chunks, chopped
1 cup hazelnuts, chopped

Directions:
Heat oven to 175 degrees C. Having baking sheet(s) ready.
In a large bowl with a mixer on high speed, beat first 8 ingredients (butter through salt) until well blended. Reduce speed to low, beat in flour just until blended. Stir in oats, chocolate and hazelnuts. Drop rounded tablespoons about 1 in. apart on ungreased baking sheet(s).
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown (depends on the type of oven used). Cool 1 minute on baking sheet, then remove to a wire rack to cool.
