Title:
Pumpkin Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can (27-29 oz.) pumpkin (with spices or add your own spice to a can of plain pumpkin)
1 cup sugar
3 beaten eggs
1 can evaporated milk
1 box dry spice cake mix
1/2 cup melted butter
1 tub whipped topping (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour 9x13 pan.
Mix pumpkin, sugar, eggs and evaporated milk together and pour in 9x13 pan.
Sprinkle the dry spice cake mix on top of the pumpkin mixture.
Drizzle with butter.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
When cooled, serve with whipped topping if desired.
