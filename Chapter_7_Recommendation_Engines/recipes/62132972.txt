Title:
White Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb package of lasagna noodles
1lb italian sausage
1lb of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 jar of your favorite alfredo sauce
pesto
1 8oz package of frozed, choped spincah, thawed
1 small can of sliced black olives
1 small can of mushrooms
4 oz of ricotta
8 oz of mozarella
Parmesan, Romano and/or Asiago

Directions:
Boil noodles until al dente, rinse and cool
Fry sausage, crumble, drain and reserve fat
Brown chicken breast in sausage fat, slice into strips
Press as much water out of the frozen spinach as possible
Begin assembly
Grease a large rectangular lasagna pan and place a layer of noodles on the bottom.
add alfredo sauce to cover and place sausage on this layer, top with mozarella cheese reserving anough for the top
Add another layer of noodles with sauce and cover with spinach reserving about three tablespoons for the top
Add mushrooms and black olives, top with ricotta
Add another layer of noodles with sauce and layer with chicken, pesto and your choice of parmesan, romano and/or asiago
Add the final layer of noodles top with the remaining sauce, the reserved spinach and the remaining mozarella.
Best if you let it sit overnight so the flavors can blend.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour or until the top is brown and the lasagna is bubbling.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Different from traditional pomodoro recipes. Spinach adds color and creamy alfredo makes this a great alternative to red!
