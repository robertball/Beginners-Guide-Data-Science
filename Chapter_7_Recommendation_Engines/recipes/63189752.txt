Title:
Prawns in a Red Pepper, Tomato and Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1kg/2lb Fresh Prawns - shelled, deveined, washed and patted dry with kitchen towel
6tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped
1 Onion - finely chopped
100g/3½ oz Fresh Button Mushrooms - sliced/chopped
1 medium sized Red Pepper - deseeded and finely chopped
150ml/5fl oz Dry White Wine
30ml/2tbsp Lemon Juice
1 x 14oz tin whole or chopped Plum Tomatoes
3tbsp Fresh Dill - finely chopped
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Optional - Touch Dried Chilli - crushed

Directions:
Heat half the oil in a heavy saucepan, add the onion and cook over a gentle heat, stirring occasionally, for some 5 minutes or so until softened a bit;
Add the mushrooms and peppers and continue cooking, still stirring occasionally, for a further 5 minutes;
Add the wine, lemon juice and tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, increase the heat to medium and bring to boil;
Reduce heat and simmer gently for some 20 to 30 minutes until the vegetables have softened and the sauce has thickened a bit;
Remove from heat, taste for flavour and seasoning, and “blitz” with a blender;
Heat the remaining oil in a separate saucepan, stir in the garlic, dill and chilli, if using, and, stirring continuously, cook on a moderate heat until the garlic starts to turn golden;
Add the tomato sauce, taste for seasoning and bring to boil;
Stir in the prawns, reduce heat and simmer and, stirring occasionally, for some 6 minutes or so or until the prawns turn pink;
Remove from heat and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
A Prawn dish worthy of fine dining status. Packed with delicious flavours,they complement the prawns at all levels. Served with pasta of choice, this is one of my favourite fish dishes.
