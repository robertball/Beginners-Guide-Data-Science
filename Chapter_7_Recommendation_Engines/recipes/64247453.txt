Title:
Lentil Barley Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp margarine
1/4 cup celery
1/4 cup onion
3 cups water
1/3 cup lentils
1 16 oz can chopped tomatoes, with juice
1/3 cup barley
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 cup carrots, shredded

Directions:
In large saucepan, saute celery and onion in margarine.  Add water and lentils and cook for 20 minutes.
Add tomatoes, barley, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning and garlic powder.  Cook for 45 minutes.
Add shredded carrots and cook for 5 minutes.
