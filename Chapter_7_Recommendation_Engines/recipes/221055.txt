Title:
Easy Green Monster Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/818336.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups baby spinach
2 apples, peeled, cored, and chopped
2 bananas, sliced
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup orange juice
1 cup fresh strawberries
1 cup ice

Directions:
Blend spinach, apples, bananas, carrots, orange juice, strawberries, and ice in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jessi Renee Bigney
Description: 
This is a smoothie packed full of veggies. Even your picky kids will love these 'fruity' smoothies.
