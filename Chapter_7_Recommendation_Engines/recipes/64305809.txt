Title:
CRANBERRY JELLO SALAD KEEPER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1968808.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large pkg. Cherry Jello
1 bag fresh whole cranberries
1 20oz. can crushed pineapple, and juice it will keep apples from turning dark
2 stalks chopped celery
1 or 2 apples cut up small
1 1/2 C. sugar
1 c. chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)

Directions:
Mix Jello with 1 1/2 cups boiling water. stir until dissolved stir in 1 1/2 cups cold water. put in fridge untill jello starts to set. Do not let it set all the way.
Grand cranberries, add sugar and let set until jello is thick.
Mix chopped apples, celery and pineapple to coat. The juice from the pineapple keeps the apples from turning dark.
Combine all ingredients in jello and Refrigerate
Let chill overnight until well set.

Submitter: 
Debbie Crawford
Description: 
This is my mothers recipe that I have carried on making. I make this every year and buy extra bags of cranberry and freeze them. That way I can make this recipe all year long. Enjoy and let me know what you think of this one.
