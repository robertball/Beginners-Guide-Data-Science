Title:
Gluten Free Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1053550.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Tbsp. Salted Butter
1 Cup Sugar
2 eggs
3 Over-ripe Frozen Whole Bananas
1/4 cup Cold Water
1 Cup Brown Rise Flour
1/2 Cup Millet Flour
1/4 Cup Almond Meal
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Xanthan Gum
1/4 tsp Baking Powder
Coconut Oil Cooking Spray

Directions:
1.Take Bananas out of freezer and let sit 30 minutes.  Preheat oven to 350F Degrees.
2.Spray 12 Muffin Pan with Coconut Cooking Spray and set aside.
3.In a small bowl Peel bananas (I use a butter knife) and mash up with a knife or fork.
4. Beat Butter and Sugar using an electric mixer until thoroughly mixed.
5. With mixer on low add eggs one a time and continue mixing until completely blended.
6. Add bananas and any juice at the bottom of the bowl
7. Add water.
8. Add Brown Rice Flour, Millet Flour and Almond Meal and continue to mix turning mixer to medium low, spraying down the sides.
9. Add rest of ingredients(baking soda, salt, xanthan and baking powder) and mix well.  Scoop into greased muffin tin.  Should make 12 muffins
10. Bake for 27 minutes
11. Remove from oven and let cool in pan 5 minutes
12. Remove from pan and enjoy!
13. Once cooled store in the frig for up to 4 days or freeze for up to 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
Lisa Ermgodts
Description: 
This is a great Gluten Free recipe that my kids love!!!  It does contain nuts so please make sure anyone eating it is not allergic.
