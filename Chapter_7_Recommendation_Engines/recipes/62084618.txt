Title:
Crockpot Tomato-y Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs ground beef
2 slices bread, soaked in 1/2 cup milk, then torn small pieces
1 egg, beaten lightly
1/4 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. dried Oregano
1/4 tsp. celery salt
1 tsp. Chili Powder
1 small red onion, peeled & chopped- divided in two portions
1 green pepper, chopped- divided in 2 portions
2 or 3 T. vegetable oil
1 15 oz. can chopped Tomatos
1 8oz can Tomato sauce
1 T. Worcestire sauce
1 T. soy sauce
2 T. brown sugar
1 T. Lemon juice
1 T. Vinegar - white or apple cider
1 tsp. Chili Powder
1/2 cup water
6 sandwich rolls/hamburger buns
Optional  Shredded lettuce
Small amount vegetable oil

Directions:
Soak 2 pieces of bread in 1/2 cup milk in bowl.  Put ground beef in bowl, and add one beaten egg, along with garlic salt, dried oregano, celery salt, chili powder, half of choped red onion and half of chopped green pepper. Mix well to blend.   Form mixture into 6 thick patties.
Heat oil in saute pan.  When pan is hot, add beef patties, and fry on one side until well browned, 4=5 minutes or so.  Turn over (carefully so as not to break up) and brown other side well for 3-4 minutes.   Remove carefully from pan and put into crock pot.  Add;  Can of chopped tomatoes, tomato sauce, Worcestire, soy sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice, Vinegar, chili powder, and water.   Stir lightly to blend.  Cook on low for 4 to 8 hours. To serve, place a burger over a nice thick bun or roll.. spoon some sauce over.  If using, put some shredded lettuce over.  Spoon additional sauce over rice or mashed potatoes on the side.

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
Burgers in a rich tomato sauce to serve over rolls/buns of your choice. The sauce is great over rice or mashed potatos.
