Title:
Baked Brie with Cheddar, Port and Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) wheel California Brie cheese
1/2 cup finely shredded California Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons port wine
2 ounces chopped toasted walnuts
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Heat oven to 500 degrees F. Cut Brie in half horizontally. Mix Cheddar and port; spread over bottom half of Brie. Cover with top half of Brie; place on baking sheet. Mix walnuts and honey; spread over Brie. Bake about 5 minutes or until Brie is warm and soft to the touch.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
A creamy wheel of brie is split and stuffed with port -laced Cheddar cheese and baked with a walnut and honey glaze.
