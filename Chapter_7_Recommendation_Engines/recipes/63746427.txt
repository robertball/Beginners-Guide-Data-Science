Title:
spaghetti and meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bread, soaked in milk for 5 mins
12 oz ground turkey breast
12 oz 85% lean ground beef
1 egg
1/2 c chopped parsley
2 tbs[ grated parmesan
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 minced onion
3 cloves minced garlic
2 tbsp olive oil
1 can (28 oz) whole peeled tomatoes

Directions:
for meatballs:
remove the bread from the milk, squeeze out most of the milk and tear into tiny pieces. mix the turkey, beef, egg, parsley. paremesan, salt, pepper, 1/2 the onion, and 1/2 the garlic. form into golf sized balls
heat 1/2 of the olive oil in a large skillet or saute pan and cook meatballs over med heat until well browned and set aside
for sauce:
heat the remaining tbsp of olive oil in a saucepan and cook the remaining onion and garlic over med heat until translucent. add the tomatoes and bring to a simmer. add meatballs and cook for 15-20 mins
cook pasta according to packaging divide among 6 and top with sauce and meatballs. garnish with parsley and parmesan
