Title:
Popeye's Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Bisquick
1/2 c. sour cream
1/2 c. 7-up
1/2 stick margarine or butter
flour

Directions:
melt butter in pre-heated oven.Mix Bisquick,sour cream,and 7-up.Roll out onto floured board.Cut out biscuits.Place in buttered pan and spoon remaining butter in top of biscuits.Bake at 400 for 20 minutes or until browned.
