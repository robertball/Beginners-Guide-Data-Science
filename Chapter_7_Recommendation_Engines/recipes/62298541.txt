Title:
Russian Meat Patties Kotelety Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground lean beef or turkey
1 slice bread cubed
1 egg
1 tablespoon dill
1 small onion diced
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/3 cup oil for frying
2 tablespoons ketchup
1 tablespoon mustard
1 teaspoon ground pepper
1 tablespoon seasoned salt
3/4 cup seasoned breadcrumbs

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in pad over medium heat. Add diced onion and saute until translucent.  In large bowl add, bread, egg, dill, pepper, seasoned salt, ketchup and mustard together.  Mix well.  Add ground meat to bowl and diced onions.  Make into small patties about the size of your palm.  Coat patties in bread crumbs.  Heat 1/3 cup oil over medium heat.  Add patties to the heated oil and cook approximately 5 minutes on each side until well browned.  Place on paper towel to soak up excess oil.

Submitter: 
Stasia
Description: 
Passed down from my russian Babushka (grandmother), these simple little meat patties are quick and delicious and make great leftovers.  Can be garnished with ketchup or mustard or even applesauce depending on taste.  Serve with a salad and baked potato for a quick and easy dinner.
