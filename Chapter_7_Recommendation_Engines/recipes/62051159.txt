Title:
Low Carb Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C peanut butter
1 C sugar substitute equivalent
1 egg, large
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  Drop on a silpat lined cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees for about 12 minutes or until set.

Submitter: 
Gin
Description: 
This simple low-carb cookie has approximately 3.2 grams of carbs with 1 gram fiber for 2.2 grams of net carbs!  Allrecipes has great recipes for low carb cookies - yes, many use sugar and tend to be small but those of us who practice controlled carb can still enjoy them.  Focus on meringues, macaroons or nut predominant recipes.
