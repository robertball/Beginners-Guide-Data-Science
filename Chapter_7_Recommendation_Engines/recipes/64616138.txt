Title:
Jenny's sauerkraut and pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork
small lima beans, bag
sauerkraut
stick of butter
heaping tablespoon of either flour or cornstarch

Directions:
Boil the pork in large deep pan of water for one hour
clean and then add the small bag of lima beans cook another hour
last hour add sauerkraut that has been rinsed
in another pan melt butter and add flour or cornstach to brown and add to the above sauerkraut and pork.
If you let it sit for a little, about 15 or 30 minutes it tastes better because it thickens up.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Ann
Description: 
This is what our family ate every new years day for good luck.
