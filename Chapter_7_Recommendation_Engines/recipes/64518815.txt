Title:
Pecan PIe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2879851.jpg
Ingredients:
1 , 9-in pie shell
1 cup packed light brown sugar
1 cup maple syrup
1/2 cup heavy cream
1Tbsp molasses
4 Tbsp butter, unsalted (cut into 1/2-inch pieces)
1/2 tsp salt
6 large egg yolks, lightly beaten
1 1/2 cups  *toasted and chopped pecans

Directions:
Chill pie crust in plate for 30 minutes
Adjust oven rack to lowest position and heat oven to 450 degrees.
Heat sugar, syrup, cream and molasses in saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally until sugar dissolves, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool 5 minutes.
Whisk butter and salt into syrup mixture until combined, then whisk in egg yolk until incorporated.
Scatter pecans in pie shell. Carefully pour filling over.
Place pie in hot oven and immediately reduce oven temperature to 325 degrees. Bake until filling is set and center jiggles slightly when  gently shaken, 45 to 60 minutes.
Cool pie on rack for 1 hour, then  refrigerate until set, about 3 hours and up to one day.
Bring to room temp before serving.
*to toast pecans,  put into microwave safe bowl and microwave for 1 minute at a time  for about three minutes.

Description: 
Andrea Comeau's/Americas test kitchen no corn syrup pecan pie. This is so super sweet, try cutting the sugar from 1 cup to 3/4 and maple syrup to 3/4.
