Title:
Zucchini Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large eggs, beaten
1/2 cup Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
1 tablespoon parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup zucchini, unpeeled and shredded
Oil for frying

Directions:
In large bowl, combine eggs, bread crumbs, parmesan, garlic powder, and salt. Add zucchini; mix well.
In large skillet, heat about 2 tablespoons of oil.
Working in batches, drop zucchini mixture into hot oil, using about 1/4 cup  per patty. Fry until golden brown on both sides, turning once, and adding oil as needed.
Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
Fran Weidman
Description: 
This is a great way to hide zucchini from the kids! These patties are not only tasty, but very simple to make. Seasonings can easily be adjusted according to taste. They can also be fried in bacon grease rather than oil.
