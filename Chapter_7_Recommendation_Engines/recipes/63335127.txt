Title:
Cajun Country Style Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Salt
2 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 Tablespoon vegetable oil
4 pounds country style ribs
1 onion chopped
1 stalk celery chopped
1 cup of chicken stock

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 500 degrees.
Coat the meat in oil and place in a cast iron skillet.
Combine the salt, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and pepper in a small bowl. Sprinkle seasoning mixture on all sides of the pork.
Put the skillet in the over and cook for 30 minutes, turning occasionally until fat is crispy.
While the for is cooking slice the onions into rings and chop the celery.
Remove the pork from the oven and reduce the temperature to 350 degrees.
Place the vegetables on top of the meat and pour the chicken stock over it.
Put skillet back in the oven and cook for 1 hour.

Submitter:

Description: 
Not really ribs, Country Style Ribs are cut from pork shoulder. The recipe works best in a large (12 or more) cast iron skillet or dutch oven.
