Title:
Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2/3 cups canned cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/2 lb of shredded cheese
1 medium onion chopped
2 lbs frozen hash brown potatoes
2 tablespoons butter, cut into peices

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Combine first 3 ingredients in cake pan.  Stir in onion and potatoes.  Dot  with butter.  Cover with foil and bake for about 45 minutes.  Remove foil and bake another 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
