Title:
Baked Tomatoes and Eggplant Parmasan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Eggplant
1 can of diced tomatoes (or if you'd like use fresh tomatoes with 1/4 cup of water)
1/2 cup of light parmasan cheese
1 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
cut eggplant into cubes and place into a flat casserole dish and pour can of diced tomatoes over the eggplant. Cover with parmasan cheese  and sprinkle garlic powder over top. and place in a 400 degree oven for 45-60 minutes (until the eggplant is fully cooked)

Submitter: 
SHANNON32
Description: 
This is a healthy alternative (even those who are not crazy about eggplant will like this recepie. The tomatoes and parmasan really give the eggplant a unique and mouth watering taste. And if this is also a great option for those who are on the south beach diet.
