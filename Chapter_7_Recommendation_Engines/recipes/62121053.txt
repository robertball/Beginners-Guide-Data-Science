Title:
Super Cheesy Garlic Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735689.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/4 cup Miracle Whip (or other tangy Mayo)
1/4 Sour Cream
2 cups shredded marble cheese. (or cheddar, mozzarella)
8 cloves garlic minced (I use more!)
1 tsp dried dill (optional)
1 tsp dried parsley (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400'F
Mix all ingredients all together in a bowl.
Spread mixture onto open faced buns or 1 loaf French bread halved.
Place buns or French bread on cookie sheet and bake for 5 min or until cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
nataliamomof3
Description: 
A melt in your mouth, buttery, cheesy garlic bread that is a great accompaniment to stews, chili's or summertime BBQ's!
