Title:
Rochester NY Style Hot Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon paprika
2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1 quart water

Directions:
Brown hamburger and drain well
Add seasonings and water
Bring to boil
Reduce heat to simmer and cook covered for 30 minutes, uncover and cook 30 minutes more or till 2/3 of the water had evaporated away

Submitter: 
Lori Krol Rothfuss
Description: 
This was printer in a Rochester NY newspaper some 20 years or more ago.  It is a classic Rochester NY Style Hot Sauce
