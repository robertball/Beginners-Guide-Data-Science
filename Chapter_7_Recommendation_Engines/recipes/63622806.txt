Title:
Sopapilla Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008428.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
16.5 ounce box of White Cake Mix
3 large egg whites
1 cup water
1/4 cup oil
2 T. cinnamon (divided)
½ teaspoon lemon extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 T. Sugar
Frosting:
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, room temperature
3-3 1/2 cups confectioners’ sugar
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2-3 tablespoons milk or cream, as needed
honey, for drizzling

Directions:
Cupcakes:
1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Place 18 cupcake liners into two muffin pans.
2. Mix cake mix, egg whites, water, oil, 1 tablespoon cinnamon, lemon extract and vanilla extract in a stand mixer for 30 seconds on low. Then mix for 2 minutes on medium. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full with batter.
3. In a separate bowl combine remaining 1 tablespoon of cinnamon and 1 tablespoon of sugar. Sprinkle the top of each cupcake with a bit of cinnamon and sugar mixture. Take a toothpick and swirl the mixture into the cupcake slightly.
4. Bake for 15 minutes in preheated oven or until cupcakes test done when a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean. Let cool and the frost.
Frosting:
Cream butter in electric mixer for 1-2 minutes until fluffy. Add powdered sugar, honey and cinnamon and mix until combined. Add 1 tablespoon of milk or cream at a time, and mix at medium-high speed until light and fluffy. Add more cream or sugar as necessary to achieve proper consistency. Pipe or spread onto cooled cupcakes. Drizzle with honey just before serving. You may want to serve these on a plate with a fork!
