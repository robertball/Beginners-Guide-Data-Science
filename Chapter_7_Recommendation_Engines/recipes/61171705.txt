Title:
Healthier Absolutely the Best Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/278545.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 kiwi, pureed
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons Mexican vanilla extract
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup toasted oat cereal like Cheerios, smashed
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, kiwi, brown sugar and white sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well with each addition, then stir in the vanilla .Combine the flour, baking soda and toasted oat cereal; gradually stir into the creamed mixture. Finally, fold in the chocolate chips. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto the prepared cookie sheets.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until light brown. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

Description:
