Title:
Sauerkraut Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. soft Camembert Cheese
3 oz. cream cheese
2 Tbl. Butter
1/4 cup Flour
1/2 Tsp Salt
Dash Cayenne pepper
1 Cup Sauerkraut
1 egg
3/4 cup milk
2 tsp water
1 package of bread crumbs

Directions:
Rub the cheeses through a sieve into a med. sauce pan and heat on  medium. Stir in butter, flour, salt, cayenne pepper, and milk.  Stir in melted and creamy.  Pour into a shallow pan and cool.  Then refrigerate until mixture can be handled easily.  Beat egg with water.  Add washed and chopped sauerkraut.  Mix in cheese mixture.  For inot small 1-2 inch size balls and roll in bread crumbs.
Fry 3 or 4 at a time in deep fat heated to 375 degrees.  Cook until golden and crisp,  about 50 sec.  Drain on paper towels,  cool and freezer wrap.
Un wrap frozen balls and place on a baking sheet.  Bake at 400 degrees for 10-12 minutes,  until Hot and Servce

REEDYGAL
Description: 
These addicting little appetizers should be made ahead and then baked before serving.
