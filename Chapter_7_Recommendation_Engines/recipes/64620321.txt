Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-½ pounds potatoes, 1-inch dice
2 medium onions, 1-inch dice
3 medium carrots, 1-inch chop
3 stalks celery, 1-inch chop
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 Packet of stew mix
1½ pounds beef stew meat, 2-inch cubes
Salt and pepper, to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup all-purpose flour, for dredging
1 cup red wine
3 cups beef broth
1  14.5-ounce can diced tomatoes (Drained)
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
1. Place the potatoes, onions, carrots, celery, and garlic  in the liner of a slow cooker.
2. Season stew meat with salt & pepper
3. Place flour in small bowl
4. Flour stew meat
5. Place in skillet and brown all sides
6. Remove meat from skillet and place in slow cooker
7. Place red wine in skillet and scrape bottom and simmer for 3 min. Place in slow cooker
8. Add diced tomatoes to slow cooker
9. Add Worcestershire sauce to slow cooker
10. Mix beef broth and stew meat packet until smooth
11. Place mixture in slow cooker
12. Cook on high for 6 hrs
13. Remove lid and cook for an additional 2 hrs
