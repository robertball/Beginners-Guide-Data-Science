Title:
Peanut Butter and Jelly Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup uncooked oatmeal
1 cup water
1-2 T. peanut butter
1 T. jelly or jam (flavor your choice)
Milk if desired

Directions:
Microwave: Combine oatmeal and water in a microwave-safe bowl and cook for 1+1/2 to 2 minutes. Stir in the peanut butter and dollop with jelly or jam. Add milk if desired.

Submitter: 
HorseCrazyBaker
Description: 
This tasty breakfast oatmeal is a great twist for pbj fans. It sounds gross, but it's delicious. If you pick a healthy pb and j, it's good for you too! If your like me, definitely a must with milk.
