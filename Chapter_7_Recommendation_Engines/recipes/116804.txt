Title:
Chunky Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978963.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup milk
3 cups mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
4 ounces crumbled blue cheese
2 teaspoons garlic salt

Directions:
Place the milk, mayonnaise, sour cream, blue cheese and garlic salt in a blender. Cover and process until smooth. Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Leona  Luecking
Description: 
This flavorful full-bodied dressing is better than any bottled dressing I've ever tasted...and it's easy to prepare, too! I found the recipe in a church cookbook. -Leona Luecking West Burlington, Iowa
