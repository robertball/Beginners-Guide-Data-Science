Title:
Dutch Oven Side by Side Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636215.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box white or yellow cake mix
1 can apple pie filling
1 can blueberry pie filling
1 stick butter, sliced into 1 Tbsp chunks
1/2 cup sprite

Directions:
Line Dutch Oven with foil or parchment paper insert. Spray with cooking spray.
Place dry cake mix in bottom of Dutch Oven, spread out evenly.
Pour apple pie filling on one side and blueberry pie filling on other.
Evenly distribute button on top.
Pour sprite over top, more to the apple side as it has less liquid.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes. Up to one hour if doubled.

Submitter:

Description: 
If you can't make up your mind on which you prefer, try this blueberry/apple dessert.
