Title:
Caramel Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Old Fashioned Caramel Apple Dip
8 oz Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
7 oz Kraft Jet-Puffed Marshmallow Creme

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and whip with mixer until smooth and no lumps.
Serve with fruit.

Submitter: 
Denise Millar Ramsey
Description: 
Caramel Apple Dip is magic for apples, strawberries, grapes, bananas and even for the center of a fruit plate.  It's very smooth and creamy with lots of great flavor!
