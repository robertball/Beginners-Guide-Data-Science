Title:
The Best BBQ'd Shredded Beef For Sandwiches....Seriously! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds Chuck Roast
Salt, Pepper And Garlic Powder, To Taste
1 12 oz. bottle Spicy BBQ Sauce
1 12 oz. can Beer
1 12-16 oz. bottle of your favorite BBQ Sauce

Directions:
Spray crockpot with non-stick cooking spray.
Sprinkle the chuck roast generously on both side with the salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Place roast in slow cooker.
Pour on the sweet and spicy chipotle sauce to cover completely.
Pour beer over top and around the sides of the roast.
Cover and cook on high for 7 hours.
Remove roast from pot and discard all liquids.
Shred beef and place back into slow cooker.

Set on warm 1-2 hours or until time to eat.
Serve on soft rolls.
