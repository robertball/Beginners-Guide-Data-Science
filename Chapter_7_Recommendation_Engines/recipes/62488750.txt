Title:
Peanut Butter Cup Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635172.jpg
Ingredients:
1 brownie mix (can be in a box or homemade)
16 peanut butter cups (like reese's)

Directions:
Prepare brownie mix batter according to package/recipe instructions. Pour half of the brownie batter into a 9x9 baking dish. Top with peanut butter cups. Spread the remaining half of the brownie batter evenly over cups. Bake according to package/recipe directions. Let cool completely and then cut into squares.
