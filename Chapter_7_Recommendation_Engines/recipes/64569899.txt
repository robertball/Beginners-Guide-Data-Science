Title:
Big Ray's Mediterranean Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3915051.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp cottage cheese
1 Tbsp mayonnaise
1 tsp  Italian Dressing
1 can (3 oz.) white tuna in water, well drained, flaked
4 pimento-stuffed green olives, sliced (about 1 Tbsp.)
1 cup cherry tomatoes, cut in half

Directions:
Combine cottage cheese, mayo and dressing in small bowl.
Add tuna, olives and tomatoes; mix lightly.
Serve tuna mixture with crackers or on pitas
