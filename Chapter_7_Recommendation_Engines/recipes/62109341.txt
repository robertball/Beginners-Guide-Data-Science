Title:
Cookies and Cream Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 chocolate sandwich cookies
1 1/2 cups milk
4 cups vanilla ice cream, softened
1/4 cup chocolate syrup (optional)

Directions:
Chop cookies coarsely or break each cookie into 3-4 peices.
Place milk, ice cream, and if desired, chocolate syrup into the blender. Add chopped cookies.
Blend on high speed until smooth.
Pour into 4 glasses. Serve immediately and enjoy!
