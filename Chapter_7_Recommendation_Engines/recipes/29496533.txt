Title:
Pancake/Waffle Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cup WW Pastry flour
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 1/2 cups buckwheat flour
1/2 cup stone ground cornmeal
1 1/2 cup oatmeal (blend in blender until powdered)
2 cups buttermilk powder
5 Tbsp baking powder
2 Tbsp baking soda
1 cup sucanat
2 Tbsp salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a very LARGE bowl and divvy upl into gallon sized freezer storage bags.  Can keep in the freezer or in pantry...freezer is recommended.
When ready to make pancakes:
1 cup mix
1 egg
2 Tbsp oil (I use applesauce)
1/2 to 2/3 cups water (I use buttermilk when I have it!!)
Mix and cook.
