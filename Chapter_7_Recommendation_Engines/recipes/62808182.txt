Title:
Cozy Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 - 8 oz Milk
Nutmeg
Cinnamon
Mexican Vanilla Blend (Regular Vanilla extract works too, but the Mexican blend has a more palatable rounded flavor.
Sugar Or Splenda

Directions:
Heat a mug of milk in the microwave or stovetop. Add just a tiny pinch of ground nutmeg, cinnamon, 1 splenda or teaspoon of sugar and 1/4 teaspoon of Mexican Vanilla blend (or 1/8 teaspoon of regular vanilla extract).Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
CSavaso
Description: 
Warm milk with a little something special. My kids love this milk as their night time snack.
