Title:
Peanut Butter Coco Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening  (or half butter & half shortening)
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar (packed)
1 egg
1 cup flour*
1/4 cup baking coco  (NOT from a drink mix)
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix thoroughly shortening, peanut butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and egg.
Blend in flour, coco, soda, baking powder and salt.  Mix well.
You may cover and chill for one hour or continue now.
Heat oven to 375o.
Shape dough into 1 inch balls.
Place 3 inches apart baking sheets.  You may lightly oil the sheets or use no oil, depending upon how oily the cookies are.
With a fork, flatten the cookies about halfway by making a criss-cross pattern on them.
Bake the cookies about 8-10 minutes, or until they are set but not hard.
Store in a covered container, will keep for a few days.
*if using self-rising flour, omit soda, baking powder and salt.
