Title:
Burek Middle Eastern Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3  4 cups olive oil
1.5 lbs lean ground meat
1 large onion chopped
½ teaspoon baharat
½ teaspoon black pepper
½ teaspoon salt
½ Cup parsley leafs chopped fine
1 package of egg roll wraps from store

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet
Brown ground beef
Add onions, baharat, black pepper, and salt
Brown until the onions are tender and clear
Turn off the heat
Add the parsley and stir
Drain remaining liquid from the meat mixture
Allow to cool
Place 3 tablespoons of meat mixture in each egg roll
Heat 3 cups of oil
Add egg rolls turn once when light brown, about 4 minutes
Remove and place on a paper towel to absorb oil
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Zachary Buckholz
Description: 
One of my favorites from local middle eastern restaurants.
I typically get the sampler platter that includes several items and this is the one I look forward to eating the most.
