Title:
Make you Skinny Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/389172.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
1 cup Whole Wheat Flour
½ tsp Fresh cracked Black Pepper
½ tsp Kosher Salt
½ tsp Garlic Powder
3 egg whites, beaten
1 cup Flax Meal
¼ cup Fresh grated Parmesan cheese
½ tsp Fresh cracked Black Pepper
½ tsp Kosher Salt
1 tsp Italian Seasoning
1 small can tomato sauce
½ cup Low-fat Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a Sil-pat.
Place chicken breasts between waxed paper or cling wrap and pound until thin.
In one bowl, mix Wheat flour, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp garlic powder, ½ tsp pepper and stir.
Place egg whites in a second bowl and beat lightly.
In a third bowl, mix Flax meal, fresh Parmesan, ½ tsp pepper, ½ tsp salt and Italian Seasoning.
Dip chicken in flour, then egg whites, then Flax meal. Make sure to coat both sides.
Place on the baking sheet and repeat with remaining chicken.
Put chicken in pre-heated oven for 25 minutes.
Remove from oven, top each with 2 TBS tomato sauce and 2 TBS Mozzarella.
Place back in the oven for 5 minutes to heat the cheese and warm the sauce.
Place under broiler for 1 minute if you wish to brown the cheese.

Submitter: 
Kathleen
Description: 
A tasty take on Chicken Parmesan, using Whole Wheat flour and Flax Meal, low-fat cheese and no oil!
