Title:
Oven  Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
5 3/4 cups cubed potatoes
5 3/4 cups carrots
3 cans cream of mushroom soup
3 onions or 1 large onion
6 cups water
3 cups frozen peas or corn
Onion soup mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees
in a 6 quart casserole dish, combine the stew meat, potatoes, carrots, mushroom soup, onions and water  Mix together well.
Bake uncovered 4 to 5 hours, stirring occasionally.
Mix in peas or corn before serving.
