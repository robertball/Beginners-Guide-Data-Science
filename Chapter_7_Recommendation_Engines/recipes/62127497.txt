Title:
Chewy Banana Strip Dog Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe banana
1 egg
1/3 C. milk
1 C. quick-cooking oats
1/8 C. cooked rice
1/2 C. all purpose flour

Directions:
Mash bananas then combine egg and milk.
Add quick-cooking oats, cooked rice, and all-purpose flour to the wet mixture.
Spread out batter over a plate (about 1/2" to 3/4" thick) and microwave on high for 5-6 minutes, or until fully cooked through with chewy texture.
Allow mixture to harden and cool for a few minutes. Cut into strips of desirable size.

Submitter: 
chriistina
Description: 
These chewy banana dog treats will have your dog howling for more!  Great way to use up ripe bananas.  This recipe is fast and uses a microwave, not oven, to cook.
