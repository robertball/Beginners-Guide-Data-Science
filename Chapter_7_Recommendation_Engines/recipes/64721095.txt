Title:
Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c peanut butter
2 T kerry gold unsalted butter
2 T unrefined coconut oil
1 tsp whey protein powder
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
melt peanut butter, butter, oil, add whey protein, add vanilla and salt
divide among cupcake papers
freeze until hard
