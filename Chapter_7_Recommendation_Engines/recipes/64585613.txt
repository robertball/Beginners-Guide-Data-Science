Title:
Little Sausage Smokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) packages Little sausage smokies
8 oz Yellow Mustard prepared
8 oz Red Currant Jelly

Directions:
Put equal parts mustard and red currant jelly in Crock Pot on low to melt the jelly using a liner in your slow cooker for easier cleanup.
Boil little sausages to get rid of some of the grease.  Rinse with hot water. Add to Crock Pot jelly/mustard mixture.
Cook on low for 1 hour until ready to serve.
Put toothpicks out to serve
