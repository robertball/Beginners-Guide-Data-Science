Title:
Peanut Shrimp Salad with Basil-Lime Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 green onions, white and light green parts only
20 unpeeled, uncooked, fresh or frozen jumbo shrimp
1 cup Japanese breadcrumbs (panko)
1/2 cup dry-roasted salted peanuts
1 TBS. cornstarch
1 tsp. red curry powder
1 large egg, lightly beaten
2 TBS. peanut oil
3 cups mixed baby salad greens
1 cup diced english cucumber
1 cup peeled, diced mango
1/2 cup mung bean sprouts
1/4 cup loosely packed fresh mint leaves
basil lime dressing
1/4 cup dry-roasted salted peanuts chopped
Basil Lime Dressing:
1/2 cup loosely packed fresh basil leaves
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 TBS. peanut oil
1 TBS. fish sauce
1 TBS. granulated sugar
1 tsp. Asian garlic chili sauce
1 tsp. grated fresh ginger
1 garlic clove, minced

Directions:
Cut green onions into 2" long thin strips.
If frozen, thaw shrimp according to package directions.  Peel shrimp; devein.
Pulse breadcrumbs and next 3 ingredients in a food processor 5 times or until mixture resembles fine crumbs.  Transfer mixture to a shallow dish or pie plate.
Dip shrimp in egg, and dredge in breadcrumb mixture.
Cook shrimp, in batches, in hot oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat 3 minutes on each side or until golden.
Gently toss green onions, salad greens, next 4 ingredients, and 2 TBS. Basil lime dressing in a large bowl.  Arrange salad on 4 individual plates; top each with 5 shrimp.  Sprinkle evenly with 1/4 cup peanuts, and drizzle with remaining dressing.  Serve immediately.
Basil Dressing: Process all ingredients in food processor 20 seconds or until blended.
