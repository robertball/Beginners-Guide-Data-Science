Title:
Mom's Baked Haddock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1&1/2 pounds of haddock
2 sticks of real butter
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1 can of whole milk
2 sleaves of Ritz crackers

Directions:
In a 13" X 9" (2" deep) glass pan mix the cream of mushroom soup, and the milk. In a med sized sauce pan melt butter on low heat. Place the haddock in the glass pan, then crush the Ritz crackers, and add them to the butter in the sauce pan. Spoon the cracker mixture onto the haddock. Bake at 300f for 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tiffanie DeMayo
Description: 
My mom would make this when i was a child, and has always been my one of my favorites. It is very good with a cheif salad.
