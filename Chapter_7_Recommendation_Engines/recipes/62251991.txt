Title:
Pierogi Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauerkraut Filling
2 Cans Sauerkraut
2 Tbsp. Butter
Dash Salt
Dash Pepper
Cheese/Potato Filling
3 Medium Potatoes
1 Lb. Farmers Cheese
1 Egg
2 Tbsp. Milk
2 Tbsp. Butter
Dash Salt
Dash Pepper

Directions:
Sauerkraut Filling
Saute all ingredients until slightly browned.
Cheese/Potatoe Filling
Boil pealed and chopped potatoes about 10 minutes
Mash potatoes and add milk, salt and pepper.
Mix egg and cheese together add mashed potatoes
