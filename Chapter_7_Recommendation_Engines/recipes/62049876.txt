Title:
Applesauce Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 egg whites
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 1/4 cups unsweetened applesauce
1/2 cup nonfat milk

Directions:
Whisk flour, salt, baking powder, and cinnamon in medium bowl.  Whisk together milk, egg, vanilla and applesauce, and stir in just until combined.  Make 4 inch pancakes on sprayed or greased griddle.  Be careful while turning as they are delicate!  Serve warm with maple syrup.

Submitter: 
STARFLOWER
Description: 
Eat without guilt these delicate, moist, lucious pancakes that melt in your mouth!  They contain no fat or sugar, but naturally sweet from the applesauce.
