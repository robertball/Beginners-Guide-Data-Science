Title:
Mark's Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6753272.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon paprika
1 1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in an empty, small spice container. If you do not have a spice container with a shaker lid on hand, a small ziplock bag works well, too!

Submitter: 
Christy Boston's Kitchen
Description: 
Need to turn up the taste factor in your grilled meats? How about your scrambled eggs, ground meat recipes, and salads? This easy and versatile  seasoning recipe does the trick, and it is made with ingredients that are easy to get at your local grocery store.
