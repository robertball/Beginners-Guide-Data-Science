Title:
KAHLUA CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 BOX DEVILS FOOD CAKE MIX
1/2 CUP VEGETABLE OIL
1 4 1/2 OZ PKG INSTANT CHOC PUDDING
4 LARGE EGGS AT ROOM TEMPATURE
3/4 CUP STRONG COFFEE
3/4 CUPS, PLUS 3 TBLS KAHLUA DIVIDED
1 CUP SEMISWEET CHOC CHIPS
3 TBLS BUTTER

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350 DEGRESS
GREASE A LARGE BUNDT PAN
COMBINE 1ST 6 INGREDIENTS (USING ONLY 3/4 CUP KAHLUA) IN A LARGE MIXING BOWL. BEAT AT MEDIUM SPEED UNTIL SMOOTH, ABOUT 4 MINUTES. POUR INTO BUNDT PAN AND BAKE FOR 45-50 MINUTES. COOL ON WIRE RACK. REMOVE FROM PAN WHEN COOL TO TOUCH
WHEN CAKE IS THOROUGHLY COOLED, MELT CHOCOLATE & BUTTER AND ADD REMAINING 3 TBLS OF KAHLUA AND STIR GENTLY.
DRIZZLE OVER CAKE
