Title:
BodyRocker's Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard boiled eggs
6 teaspoons of plain yogurt
2 teaspoons of dijon mustard
2 teaspoons of finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons of honey
2 tablespoons of paprika
chili powder

Directions:
Peel eggs and scoop yolks into a bowl.   Mix in all other ingredients.  Fill empty eggs whites with the yolk mixture.   Sprinkle with some extra paprika for show and enjoy!
