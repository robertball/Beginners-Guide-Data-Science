Title:
Chicken Wellington Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sheets of thawed puff pastry
8 small boneless skinless chicken breast
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
Season to taste with Salt and pepper
1 large onion finely chopped
1 tablespoon of minced/ crushed garlic
1/2 lb fresh button or baby bella mushrooms sliced (can use more if desired)
2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
1 (8oz)pkg of cream cheese softened
2 tablespoons of dijon mustard
1 egg, slightly beaten (if desired)

Directions:
1. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper on all sides.
2. Heat butter and oil in skillet.
3. Brown the breasts on all sides until almost completely cooked through; transfer to a plate.
4. To the same skillet add in onions, garlic and sliced mushrooms, saute until the mushrooms lose their moisture and the onions are tender.
5. Stir in parsley.
6. On a floured surface, roll out each puff pastry sheet to a 14 in. square.
7. Cut each pastry sheet into 4 even squares.
8. Place each breast in the center of each square.
9. Set oven to 375 degrees.
10. In a small bowl, combine the softened cream cheese with the mustard,  then spread over Each breast.
11. Top each breastwith 2 tablespoons of the mushroom/onion mixture.
12. Brush the edges of each pastry sheet with water.
13.  Wrap the pastry sheet around the chicken breast.
14. Place the sealed chicken breast seam side down on the baking sheet.
15. Brush the top of the pastry with egg wash (if desired).
16. Bake for about 20 minutes or until puffed and golden brown.
17. Slice into 1/2 for serving.

Submitter: 
What's Cookin' Mom?
Description: 
Here is a fancy way to serve chicken breast that will WOW your husband and family or special guest. You can scale the recipe to make it serve 4 to 20 people.
