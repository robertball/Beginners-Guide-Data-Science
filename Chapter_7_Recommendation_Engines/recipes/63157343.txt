Title:
JaMainecan Sunrise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 shots Bacardi Silver Rum
1 shot Appleton Estate Rum (or other fragrant dark)
1/4 shot Canton ginger liqueur
1/2 shot Grand Marnier or Cointreau
1 shot simple syrup
1/2 can sparkling cranberry
3-4 oz. Orange Juice
Splash of Grenadine

Directions:
Put ice into two tall cocktail glasses. Fill a shaker with ice.  Add the orange juice and all the alcohol to the shaker. Shake well and set aside. Pour sparkling cranberry into the bottom quarter of the glass. Add grenadine until the liquid hits the bottom third. Gently pour in the contents of the shaker through the strainer top. Repeat for the second glass.

This is a wonderful Caribbean-meets-Maine sort of recipe.
