Title:
Coconut Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. flaked coconut
1/4 c. powdered sugar
1 c. butter, softened
1/2 c. butter flavored shortening
3 c. sugar
5 eggs, room temp
3 c. flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 c. milk
2 tbsp. coconut flavoring
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 325.  Grease 10" tube pan.  Line bottom with parchment or foil.  Grease and flour pan.  Stir together coconut and powdered sugar until coconut is well covered.  Evenly sprinkle on the bottom of the pan.
In large bowl beat butter and shortening until fluffy.  Beat in sugar until light and fluffy.  Beat at low speed at and eggs one at a time.
In medium bowl, stir together flour and baking powder.  Alternately beat flour mixture and milk into butter mixture just until incorporated, beginning and ending with flour mixture.  Beat in coconut flavoring and vanilla.  Pour into pan.
Bake 1 hour and 35 minutes to 1 hour and 45 minutes until center comes out clean.  Cool for 20 minutes.  Invert onto wire rack, remove pan and foil.  Cool completely.
