Title:
Sloppy Joe Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion chopped
1 green pepper chopped
1 red pepper chopped
1 clove garlic chopped or pressed
1 teaspoon yellow mustard
1 cup ketchup
3 teaspoons brown sugar
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups frozen whole kernel corn
1 (14oz) can petite diced tomatoes
1 1/2 cups shredded colby/jack cheese
1 box penne pasta

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Brown ground beef with onion, peppers, and garlic. Drain any fat.
Add mustard, ketchup, brown sugar, salt, pepper, and tomatoes to the meat. Mix thoroughly. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
Cook the corn until tender and the pasta til al dente (or desired tenderness).
Mix pasta, corn, and cheese with the meat mixture.
Pour it into a 9x13 casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees for about 20 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Theresa Harnar
Description: 
This is a great comfort food recipe that I love to make on a cold winter day. It is also a hit at our church's pot luck dinners.
