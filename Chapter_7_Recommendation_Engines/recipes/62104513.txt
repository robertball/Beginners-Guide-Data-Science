Title:
Slow Soutwest Chicken Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 frozen skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can chicken broth
1 can (4 oz)green chilies
2-3 cups frozen mixed vegetables
1 box of southwestern flavored scalloped potatoes

Directions:
Spray cooker with nonstick spray.
Place chicken breasts in cooker.
Add mixed vegetables.
Add scalloped potatoes and flavor envelope.
Add green chilies, cream of celery and chicken broth.
(Broth should be last to moisten flavor envelope.)
Cook on HIGH for 3-4 hours.
Use a fillet knife to cut up chiken into small chunks before serving.

Submitter: 
B. Barnett
Description: 
This is a wonderful, creamy dish that involves few ingredients and just enough spice. Enjoy!
