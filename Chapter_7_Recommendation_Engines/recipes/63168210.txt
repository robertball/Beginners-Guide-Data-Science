Title:
Pot Roast with Carrots and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - 4 potatoes, thinly sliced
3 - 4 carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
1 onion, chopped
Tony Chachere, Garlic Power, Onion Powder, Black Pepper
3 - 4 lb beef rump roast
2 tbls vegetable oil
2 cans beef consomme

Directions:
Arrange potatoes, carrots and onions in slow cooker.
Sprinkle spices over meat. Cook in hot oil over medium high heat until brown on all sides
Place in slow cooker over vegetables. Pour consomme around meant.
Cover and cook on low 10 - 12 hours.
