Title:
Spinach and Artichoke Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pasta
5 Tablespoons butter
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 small onion
1 14 oz can artichoke hearts
10 ounces frozen spinach
2 cups milk
1/2 cup goat cheese
1 cup grated cheddar
1 cup grated swiss
1 cup grated parmesan
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package and preheat oven to 400.  Meanwhile, heat butter in a medium saucepan.  Add onion and garlic and cook until onion softens.
Add milk and heat through.  Then add goat cheese until melted.  Add 2 cups of shredded cheese 1/2 cup at a time until melted.  Reserve 1/2 cup for topping
Mix Artichokes and spinach into cheese sauce.  Transfer pasta to large casserole dish, and pour in sauce, mixing into pasta until even.  Top with remaining cheese and bake until heated through.  Broil for a minute or two to crisp the top.
