Title:
First Place Cinnamon Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 crumbled cake compressed yeast (or 2 1/4 tsp of active dry yeast)
2 teaspoons sugar
7 Tablespoons lard or butter
1 cup milk
7 Tablespoons sugar
3 beaten eggs
1 teaspoons salt
4 1/2-5 cups flour
Melted butter
2 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 Cup Dark brown sugar
Cream cheese icing:
½ Tablespoon butter, chilled
4 ounces cream cheese
½ pound powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Scald milk (180 degrees)  2. Combine milk and lard or butter until lard or butter is melted.  3. Pour milk mixture in a large bowl with yeast and 2 teaspoons sugar.  4. Let stand for 10 minutes. 5.  Combine 7 Tablespoons sugar, eggs and salt and add to milk mixture.  5.  Stir in flour.  6. Knead dough for about 5 minutes.  7.  Place in a bowl and allow to double in bulk.  8.  When the dough has doubled in bulk, roll it on a floured board to the thickness of ¼ inch.  9.  Spread generously with melted butter and sprinkle with cinnamon and dark brown sugar mixed together.  10.  Roll dough like a jelly roll and cut into 1 inch slices.  11.  Rub pan with butter.  12.  Place rolls in pan ½ inch apart.  13.  Cover and place in the refrigerator overnight.  14.  Take the rolls out of the oven 30 minutes before cooking.  15.  Place the rolls in a preheated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.  16.  Make cream cheese icing: Beat butter and cheese at high speed of a mixer until fluffy.  Gradually add sugar and vanilla; beat at low speed until blended.  Cover and chill.  17. When cool, spread with cream cheese icing.

Description: 
This recipe won 1st place at the local fair!  Make them the day before and bake them in the morning for fresh cinnamon buns.
