Title:
Crazy Cinnamon Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4002641.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
3 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
8 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine milk, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and ice in a blender; blend on high until smooth. Pour milkshake into a glass.

Submitter: 
Abby T.
