Title:
Carrot Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/452516.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup pure canned pumpkin
1/4 cup apple sauce
1 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
3 cups grated carrots

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Put muffin papers into tins.
In a large bowl, beat together eggs, pumpkin, apple sauce, white sugar and                 vanilla.
Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and pumpkin pie spice.
Stir in carrots.
Pour into prepared muffin tins
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the muffin comes out clean.
Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.

Submitter: 
miskyn
Description: 
With no oil or butter these are a very healthy and spiced up alternative to regular carrot cake/muffins. My family can't get enough so I make them once a week!
