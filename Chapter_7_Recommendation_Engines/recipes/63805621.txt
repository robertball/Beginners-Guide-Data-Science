Title:
White pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1055416.jpg
Ingredients:
1. Whole wheat pizza dough
2. Minced garlic
3. Olive oil
4. Salt
5. Pepper
6. Garlic powder
7. Onion powder
8. Sliced tomato
9. Italian 3 cheese blend
10. Ricotta cheese (optional)
11. Broccoli or spinach
12. Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450, add a pizza stone while the oven is preheating.
Rollout pizza dough to desired thickness.
When dough is ready brush with olive oil and spread minced garlic and the spices on the dough.
Add the 3 cheese blend, ricotta cheese, vegetables and swirl olive oil in a circular motion around the pizza.
Top with Parmesan cheese and bake for 20 minutes until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted.
