Title:
Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Spaghetti
1lb Bacon
4 Eggs
1/8t Garlic Powder
1c Grated Parmesan
1/2t Black Pepper
1/2c Hot Pasta Water

Directions:
Chop and fry bacon
Beat eggs, add cheese, pepper, and garlic mixing until smooth
Cook spaghetti as directed
Pull spaghetti from water, don't dump water
Toss bacon and spaghetti while hot in egg mixture and mix well
Add 1/2c pasta water to finish cooking eggs and thin out sauce

Submitter: 
John
Description: 
Simple and delicious. Don't be afraid, you are not eating raw eggs.
