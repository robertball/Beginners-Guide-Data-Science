Title:
sweet and spicy chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of cubed chicken breast
1 pkg. of taco seasoning
1 medium green pepper, diced
1 medium onion, diced
1 tbsp. minced garlic
1 jar salsa ( 18 oz.
1 cup peach preserves
2-3 tbsp oil
cooked rice -

Directions:
Place cubed chicken in bowl or plastic bag, sprinkle taco seasoning over chicken, toss to coat chicken.
Heat oil in large skillet, add chicken, green peppers and onion to skillet, cook until chicken is browned on all sides.
Add salsa, garlic and peach preserves to skillet, simmer for 10 to 15 minutes. Serve over hot cooked rice.
