Title:
My Italian Turkey Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1 egg
1/2 cup italian style bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp. kosher salt (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Gently form into meatballs with out handling or packing to hard.
Bake at 375 deg. for 20 mins or until internal temperature reaches 160 deg.
Pan fry on medium heat, with a drizzle of olive oil,turning to brown all around, for approximately 15 mins. Test for doneness
Drop raw meatballs into your favourite spaghetti sauce and gently simmer for 45 mins.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
These turkey meatballs are moist and flavourful. Pan fry, bake or cook from raw right into your favourite spaghetti sauce, as i do!
