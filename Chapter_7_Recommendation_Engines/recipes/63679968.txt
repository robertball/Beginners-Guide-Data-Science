Title:
Cracker Barrel Country Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slice bacon, trimmed and minced
15 ounces canned green beans, with liquid
1 tablespoon minced onion
1/3 tsp. sugar
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
In two quart saucepan on medium heat, cook bacon until lightly brown but not crisp.  When bacon has browned, add green beans.  Add salt, sugar, pepper and mix well.  Place onion on top of green beans, cover with a lid and bring to a light boil.  Turn heat very low and simmer for 30-45 minutes.
