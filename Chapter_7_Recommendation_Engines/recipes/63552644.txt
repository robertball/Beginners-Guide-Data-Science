Title:
Slow Cooker Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/970644.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb sausage
2 tablespoons of olive oil
2 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic chopped
1tablespoon basil
1tablespoon oregano
1teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (29 ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
1 (29 ounce ) can diced tomatoes
1 (16 ounce jar) jar roasted red peppers chopped
1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
1 cup Parmesan cheese
2 cups mozzarella cheese, divided
2 (16 ounce) box ziti pasta

Directions:
1. Mix ground beef, sausage and spices in bowl and then cook in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until no longer pink.
2. Dice and then saut√© onions in olive oil until slightly caramelized.
3. Mix together meat, tomato sauce, crushed tomato, and diced tomato and chopped roasted peppers. Set aside.
4. Mix 1 cup mozzarella, the ricotta and parmesan in a medium bowl.
5. Spoon 1/3 of meat sauce into the slow cooker; top with 16 oz. of pasta. Drop half of the cheese mixture, by rounded tablespoons, over ziti; carefully spread over ziti using back of spoon. Layer with 1/3 meat sauce, the remaining ziti and remaining cheese. Add remaining meat sauce, completely covering all the ziti and cheese. Place lid on slow cooker.
6. Cook on low for 6 to 7 hours or high for 3 to 5  hours or until noodles are tender.
7. Carefully remove lid to allow steam to escape. Sprinkle food with the remaining 1 cup mozzarella; let stand 10 minutes until melted. Serve directly from slow cooker.
