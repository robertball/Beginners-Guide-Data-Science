Title:
Cheese Ball - Easy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3343499.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup shredded cooked corned beef
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, corned beef and horseradish using your hands. Form into a ball, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for several hours or overnight for best flavor. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Andrea
Description: 
This is the easiest and best cheese ball.  Don't be scared by the horseradish!  I don't like it, but it tastes great in this recipe.
