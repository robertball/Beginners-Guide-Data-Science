Title:
Holiday Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh cranberries
1 whole orange, quartered and seeded
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup finely chopped celery
1/4 cup finely chopped pecans
1 small can crushed pineapple w/ juice
1 3 oz. pkg. raspberry Jello
1 3 oz. pkg. orange Jello
1 cup boiling water

Directions:
In a food processor, grind the cranberries and the  orange quarters.
Add the sugar and let sit for 1 hour.
Dissolve the gelatins in 1 cup boiling water.
Add the crushed pineapple, the celery and nuts.
Stir in the cranberry/orange mixture.
Refrigerate until salad sets up and you are ready to serve.

A tart and tasty salad to garnish your holiday table.
