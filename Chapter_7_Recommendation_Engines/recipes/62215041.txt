Title:
Rich Chocolate Chip Toffee Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/3 c. flour
2/3 c. brown sugar
3/4 c. butter, softened
1 egg, slightly beaten
2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
1 c. chopped pecans
1 14oz can Sweetened Condensed milk
1 3/4 c. Skor English Toffee Bits OR Heath Bits 'O Brickle Almond Toffee Bits, divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Line a 13 x 9 inch pan with parchment paper.  Spray with Pam.
Combine flour and brown sugar in large bowl.  Cut in butter until mixture resembles course crumbs.  Add egg; mix well.  Stir in 1 1/2 c. chocolate chips and nuts; set aside 1 1/2 c. mixture.
Pour remaining crumb mixture onto bottom of prepared pan.  Bake for 10 minutes.  Pour sweetened condensed milk evenly over hot crust; top with 1 1/2 c. toffee bits.  Sprinkle reserved crumb mixture and remaining 1/2 c. chips over top.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.  Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 c. tofee bits.  Cool completely in pan on wire rack.  Cut into bars.
