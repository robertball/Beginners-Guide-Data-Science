Title:
Special Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups shortening
1/2 c. butter
8 cups confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla
6 fluid ounces heavy cream

Directions:
Cream shortening and butter until fluffy. Add confectioner's sugar and continue creaming until well blended.
Add salt, vanilla, and whipping cream blend on low speed until moistened.  Add additional whipping cream if necessary (up to 2 ounces). Beat at high speed until frosting is fluffy, about 15 minutes.
