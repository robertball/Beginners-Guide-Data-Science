Title:
rocky road pudding pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (12 oz each) evaporated milk
1 pkg (5.9 oz each) chocolate instant pudding mix
1 can (7 oz each) Reddi-wipÂ® Whipped Topping
1/2 cup miniature marshmallows
1/4 cup coarsely chopped peanuts
1 graham cracker pie crust (9-inch)

Directions:
Pour milk into medium bowl. Add dry pudding mix; beat with wire whisk until well blended and mixture just begins to thicken.
Add contents of Reddi-wip can; stir gently but quickly until well blended. Then fold in peanuts and marshmallows.
Pour into crust, cover and refrigerate for 6 hours or until set.
