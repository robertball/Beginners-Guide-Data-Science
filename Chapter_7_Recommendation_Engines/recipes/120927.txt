Title:
Pizza Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/835214.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) jar pizza sauce
15 slices pepperoni, chopped
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
12 hot dog buns, split
12 hot dogs, cooked

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine pizza sauce and pepperoni; heat through. Stir in the cheese until melted. Spoon about 2 tablespoons into each bun; top with a hot dog.

Submitter: 
Rachel  Tollefson
Description: 
'I'm asked to make these special hot dogs whenever I come home from college,' shares Rachel Tollefson of Selby, South Dakota. 'Dressed up with pizza sauce, pepperoni and melted cheese, these sandwiches are sure to be a hit at your house, too.'
