Title:
Noodle Kugel (Dairy) Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
this is my favorite jewish recipe, from my mom, who got it from a jewish
cookbook.
KUGEL
1 lb. noodles (wide)***whole wheat
32 oz. Cottage cheese***low fat
2 eggs
15 1/2 oz. pineapple *** in own juice
8 oz. sour cream (use half)***reduced fat
1 c. raisins
2 tbsp. spoon butter
1 tbsp. sugar or Sweet & Low or Splenda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cook noodles until 3/4 done; drain and in same
pot mix butter. Add Cottage cheese; mix well sour cream, raisins, 2 eggs
(beaten), pineapples, sugar or Sweet & Low; mix well. Butter baking pan
thoroughly. Place kugel evenly and sprinkle cinnamon over top before entered
into oven, and bake until golden brown (approx 40 minutes).
