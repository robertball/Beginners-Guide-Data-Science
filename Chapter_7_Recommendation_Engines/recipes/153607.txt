Title:
Spicy Spaghetti Sauce Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976621.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons Spice Islands® Sweet Basil
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Marjoram
4 1/4 teaspoons Spice Islands® Onion Powder
4 teaspoons sugar
3 1/2 teaspoons salt
2 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Garlic Powder
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Rosemary
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Medium Grind Black Pepper
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Summer Savory
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® Ground Cloves

Directions:
Grind basil and marjoram in a small food processor or coffee grinder until fine. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Store in tightly capped container.
Use to season a favorite spaghetti sauce recipe or to liven up jarred sauces.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
This easy to make seasoning blend is the classic Spice Islands recipe loved by generations of cooks.
