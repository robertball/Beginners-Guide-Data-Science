Title:
Sausage and Ham Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb smoked sausage (cut into 1 inch pieces)
1 lb ham steak (cut into 1 inch pieces)
2 15 oz cans black beans (drained)
1 15 oz can pinto beans
1 15 oz can red beans
1 1/2 cups chopped onions
1 1/2 cups chopped green pepper
1 1/2 cups chopped celery
4 cloves garlic (chopped)
2 tsp leaf thyme
1 1/2 tsp leaf oregano
1 1/2 tsp white pepper
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 chicken bouillon cube
5 bay leaves
8 oz can tomato sauce
1 cup water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a crock pot.
Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.
Remove bay leaves.
Served over cooked rice

Submitter: 
MATTOON250
Description: 
This variation on chili provides a nice alternative to the traditional chili that has been all but perfected by this point.
