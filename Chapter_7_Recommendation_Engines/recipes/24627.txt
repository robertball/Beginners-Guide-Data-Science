Title:
Beer Cheese Dip I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/940746.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) package processed cheese food, diced
1/2 (12 fluid ounce) can beer, room temperature
1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whip together cream cheese, processed cheese food, beer and garlic. Continue whipping until smooth.

Submitter: 
Tracy Lyn
Description: 
Bring the Beer Cheese Dip, and go home with an empty bowl! This is very simple to prepare. Serve it with your favorite warm bread.
