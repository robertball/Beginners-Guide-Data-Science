Title:
Sheer Khurma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups milk
5 tablespoons white sugar
3 cardamom pods, or to taste
3/4 cup broken vermicelli pasta
6 dates, pitted and chopped, or to taste (optional)
6 pistachio nuts, chopped, or to taste (optional)
3 saffron strands, or to taste

Directions:
Combine milk and sugar in a saucepan over medium heat; bring to a rolling boil. Add cardamom pods. Stir in vermicelli; simmer until pudding thickens, 35 to 40 minutes.
Garnish pudding with dates, pistachios, and saffron threads. Serve warm or chilled.

Submitter: 
Rookie With A Cookie
Description: 
Sheer khurma, which literally means 'milk with dates', is a vermicelli pudding that is very popular in Muslim communities around the world. It is often served on religious holidays like Eid or Ramadan.
