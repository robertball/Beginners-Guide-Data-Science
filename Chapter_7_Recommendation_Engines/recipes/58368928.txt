Title:
Debra's Rotel Meatloaf! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers (such as RO*TELÂ®), drained
1 cup Peppridge Farm bread stuffing mix
1 sm can mushrooms (optional)
1 teaspoon onion flakes (optional)
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
1 1/2 teaspoons seasoned salt, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
1 sm can tomatoe sauce

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Beat the egg in a mixing bowl, then add the ground beef, tomatoes, and stuffing mix. Season with onion flakes, garlic powder, seasoned salt, and pepper. Mix until evenly combined. Pack into a 9x5 inch loaf pan. Pour tomatoe sauce on top.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
