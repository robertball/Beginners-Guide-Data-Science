Title:
Creamy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
2 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Clean chicken and rub wit salt and pepper. Put in Casserole dish and cover with cream of mushroom soup. Bake in oven at 350 for 25-30 minutes or until cooked thoroughly.
