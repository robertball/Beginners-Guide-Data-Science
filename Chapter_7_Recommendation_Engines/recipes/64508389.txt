Title:
Caramel Apple Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups quick cooking oats
1 1/4 cups flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup softened butter
1 1/4 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup milk
1 egg
2 apples peeled and finely diced (fuji or granny smith work well)
1 bag Kraft caramel bits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix together first six ingredients and set aside.
In a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, cream butter and brown sugar until fluffy.  Add egg, vanilla and milk and mix until combined. Add oats mixture and blend until just incorporated.  Finally Mix in apples and caramel bits.
Drop by the tablespoon onto parchment lined cookie sheet and bake for 11 minutes until lightly browned.  Let sit on cookie sheet until cooled.
