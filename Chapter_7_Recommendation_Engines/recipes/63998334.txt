Title:
Unbaked Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 stick margarine
* 2 cups white sugar
* 1/3 cup cocoa
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1/3 cup peanut butter
* 3 cups oats
* 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Mix the butter, sugar, cocoa, and milk in a saucepan and boil for 2 minutes, then add the peanut butter, oats and vanilla. Stir well and then drop on the aluminum foil and let cool.
