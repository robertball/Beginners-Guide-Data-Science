Title:
7UP Cherry Merry Mint Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985927.jpg
Ingredients:
1 quart pineapple sherbet
1 (2 liter) bottle 7UP® Cherry
6 ounces Rose's® Mojito
1 cup white rum (optional)

Directions:
Add the ingredients to 1 gallon punch bowl
Garnish the punch bowl with lemon and lime slices.

Submitter: 
7UP
Description: 
Pineapple, cherry, and mint flavor make up this quick and easy party punch.
