Title:
Cheesy Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Hash Browns:
1 pkg simply potatoes shredded hash browns
1 can (10 3/4 oz) cream of chicken soup
2 cups (8 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
3/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup butter, melted
Topping:
1 1/2 cups corn flakes, coarsely crushed
2 tbl butter, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Spray 2 quart glass baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. In large bowl, combine all hash brown ingredients; mix well. Spread into prepared baking dish.
In small bowl, stir together topping ingredients. Spread topping evenly over hash browns. Bake 45 minutes or until hash browns are tender.
