Title:
White Chocolate Pistachio Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332960.jpg
Ingredients:
1 18.25 box yellow cake mix
2 3.4 oz pistachio instant pudding mix
1 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup water
4 eggs
1 8 oz light sour cream
1 t almond extract
1 12oz bag white chocolate chips
1 t flour
Powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour bundt pan.
Combine cake mix and pudding mixes. Mix in oil, water, eggs, sour cream, and almond extract.
Toss white chocolate chips in 1 t flour. Stir into cake mix.
Batter will be thick. Pour into prepared pan.
Bake for 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
Cool in pan one hour before turning out onto a cooling rack. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

Description:
