Title:
Victor's Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salsa:
1 can tomato juice
Cilantro
1/4 can water (use tomato juice can)
7 tomatoes
1/2 stalk celery
large onion
{White pepper, salt, garlic powder, oregano- add to taste}
1 package crab meat
1 package chicken
1/2 block shrimp

Directions:
Slice Chicken, shrimp, crab meat - keep seperate
Cook chicken (with oil, cumin/black pepper)
When almost cooked, add shrimp
Cook until shrimp is done
Add salsa mix - boil 15 minutes (for flavor)
Add Crab, cook another 10 minutes.
Put in oven at 350' - topped with cheddar cheese
Cook until cheese is melted
