Title:
Carolyn's Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks margarine or butter
1/2 cup solid shortening
3 cups sugar
6 eggs
1 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp lemon extract
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
3 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
1. Grease and flour a bundt pan. Preheat oven to 300Â°.
2. Cream together margarine, crisco and sugar until fluffy. Add the 6 eggs one at a time, mixing well with each egg.
3. Add the milk, vanilla and lemon extracts. Mix well.
4. Add the salt, baking powder and flour (not all at once). Mix well.
5. Pour into prerared pan and bake for 2 hours or until toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean. Cool on stove for 10 minutes, turn onto a wire rack to complete cooling.
