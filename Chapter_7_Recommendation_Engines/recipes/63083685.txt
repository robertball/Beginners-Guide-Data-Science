Title:
Peanut Butter Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup oatmeal flour
1/3 cup skim milk
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup apricot seeds
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup walnuts

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. In a saucepan heat the skim milk over medium heat, then add the honey and peanut butter. Stir for 2 mins until texture is creamy. In a bowl add all the dry ingredients and pour the warm mixture it. Mix together. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and press the mixture into it evenly. Bake for 20-25 mins. Cool and then cut into squares and serve.
