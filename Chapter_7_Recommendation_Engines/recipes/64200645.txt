Title:
Ginger Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs beef top round steak,cut into 3/4 inch cubes
24 baby carrots
2 bell peppers, chopped
1 onion, chopped
8 oz mushrooms, cut in halves
1 cup beef broth
1/2 cup hoisin sauce
1/4 cup quick cooking tapioca
2 tbsp grated fresh ginger

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a slow cooker.  Cook on low 8 to 9 hours.  Serve over rice, if desired.
