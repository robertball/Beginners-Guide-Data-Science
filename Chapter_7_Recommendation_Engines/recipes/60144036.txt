Title:
Crispy Coconut Chicken Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade:
1 can coconut milk
2 tablespoon lime
Dry mix:
1 cup coconut flakes
1/2 cup breadcrumbs or panko
2 teaspoons flour or 1 teaspoon corn starch
1 teaspoon salt (org 1/2)
1/2 teaspoon black pepper (org 1/4)
1 teaspoon garlic powder (org 1/2)
1/2 teaspoon parika (org 1/4)
1/2 curry (optional) (org 1/4)
1/3 teaspoon Bankok mix (MDK)
1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast halves , cut into 1-inch strips
2 eggs, lightly beaten (org 1)
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted (org 1/3)
Capful coconut rum
Optional:
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon turmeric
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 cup apricot or strawberry preserves (optional) (2 parts)
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard (optional) (1 part)
2 tablespoons honey (1 part)
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Marinate chicken in coconut milk (optional TBLSP key lime juice) or cream of coconut
Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Consider frying instead.  If frying, use less butter
Mix coconut, salt, pepper, garlic powder, spices, breadcrumbs (a little flour?) in medium bowl.  Use ziploc to coat.
Alternative batter (from shrimp recipe) Mix together flour, cornstarch, salt and white pepper. Add 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil and the ice water. Stir to blend. Ingredients: 1/2 cup all-purpose flour , 1/2 cup cornstarch , 1 tablespoon salt , 1/2 tablespoon ground white pepper , 2 tablespoons vegetable oil , 1 cup ice water, 2 cups shredded coconut
Combine egg, butter, and rum in bowl
Double dip: Dip in dry mix.  Dip in egg. Dip in dry mix again.
Butter/ grease shallow baking pan.
Place in baking pan.  Add leftover coating.  Drizzle/ (throughly coat so flour cooks) with melted butter.
Bake 15-25 minutes or until chicken is browned and cooked through, turning once.  Broil 2-3 min for crispness.
Serve with Apricot Dipping Sauce, if desired.
Apricot Dipping Sauce: Mix 1 cup apricot preserves, orange, or strawberry and 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, honey, hot sauce until well blended.
