Title:
Mango Watermelon Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1160657.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups cubed seeded watermelon
1 mango - peeled, seeded, and diced
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon white sugar
ice cubes (optional)

Directions:
Blend watermelon, mango, water, and sugar together in a blender until smooth.
Place ice into glasses and pour smoothie over ice.
