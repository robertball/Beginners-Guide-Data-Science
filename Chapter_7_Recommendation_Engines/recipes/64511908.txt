Title:
Pasta in Squash Cheese Sauce Casserole with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
625 ml cooked acorn squash (or any squash)
1 medium onion, diced
250 g fresh mushrooms, chopped
900 g package penne pasta (rotini will work as well),cooked to al dente
200 g Libo Capri smoked ham, diced
150 g shredded light marble cheese
45 g bulk barn cheese sauce mix
750 ml water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cook penne or rotini according to package instructions,drain and set aside
meanwhile pasta cooks, in a blender beaker combine onions, squash, water and liquify until smooth, then empty contents into medium saucepan, put on medium low and slowly heat squash mixture
when mixture starts heating up add the cheeses and allow to melt and sauce to thicken, add diced ham and chopped mushrooms and keep stirring
Spray a 22 cm x 33 cm baking dish, with cooking spray to prevent sticking then add the cooked pasta to pan then pour the cheese sauce and fold pasta into sauce then cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes
Remove pan from oven and let stand about 10 minutes then serve
