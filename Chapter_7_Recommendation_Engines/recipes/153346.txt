Title:
Rocky Road Crunch Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981826.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup honey
2 tablespoons butter
4 cups JET-PUFFED Miniature Marshmallows
6 cups POST HONEY BUNCHES OF OATS Cereal
1 cup PLANTERS COCKTAIL Peanuts, chopped
4 squares BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, chopped

Directions:
Microwave honey and butter in large microwaveable bowl on HIGH 1 minute; stir until well blended. Add marshmallows; toss to coat. Microwave 1-1/2 minutes or until marshmallows are puffed; stir until well blended.
Add remaining ingredients; mix well.
Press cereal mixture firmly into greased foil-lined 13x9-inch pan. Cool. Cut into 24 bars.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
The classic rocky road combo of chocolate, nuts, and marshmallow comes together in these delightful cereal bars.
