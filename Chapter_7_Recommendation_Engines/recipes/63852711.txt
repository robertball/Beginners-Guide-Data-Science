Title:
Maple-Glazed Baby Carrots (KRAFT) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. baby carrots
1/4 cup KRAFT light Catalina dressing
1/4 cup maple-flavored or pancake syrup
1 tbsp. butter
1/2 cup chopped Planters Pecans, toasted

Directions:
Cook carrots in boiling water in large saucepan 12-14 minutes or until tender; drain. Set carrots aside.
In same empty saucepan, bring dressing and syrup to boil on medium heat, stirring frequently. Stir in carrots and cook until glaze is thickened, stirring frequently.
Add butter; stir until melted.
Stir in toasted nuts.
