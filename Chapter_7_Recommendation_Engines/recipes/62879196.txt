Title:
Black Bean Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15 oz. cans black beans, rinsed & drained
1/2 c extra virgin olive oil
1 c shredded carrot
1 c cornmeal
1 cup Panko all natural whole wheat breadcrumbs
2 Tbsp chili powder
2 Tsp sea salt
3/4 c chopped fresh cilantro
2+ Tbsp water, as needed

Directions:
In a large bowl, use your hands to mix beans, olive oil, carrot, cornmeal, breadcrumbs, cilantro and spices.  Mash together, adding oil and water as needed until the mixture is firm enough to form patties.
Form 6 to 8 bun-sized patties.  Saute in non-stick frying pan, turning once, until heated throughout.
Serve on whole wheat buns.  Garnish with guacamole & salsa.  Recommended side - spicy sweet potato fries.
