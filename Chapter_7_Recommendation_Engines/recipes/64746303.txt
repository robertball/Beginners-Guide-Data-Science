Title:
Bacon Wrapped Cream Cheese Stuffed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breasts (about 2)
6-8 slices bacon
6-8 oz cream cheese, softened
other cheese as desired (parm, cheddar, blue, swiss, gouda, etc)
1 tsp garlic powder
3/4 tsp onion powder
3/4 tsp parsley flakes
handful green onion, parsley, or other herbs
squeeze of lemon juice to taste
salt

Directions:
preheat oven to 375-400 F
cut chicken breast in half, then cut pouch into side of chicken
combine cream cheese filling ingredients
stuff cream cheese into chicken pockets
wrap chicken breast with 1-2 slices bacon
put on wire rack over foil lined baking sheet
bake 30 min, then broil each side for 2-3 min until bacon crispy
