Title:
Southwest White Chicken Chili (Campbell's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp veg oil;
4 chicken breast halves, cut into cubes;
4 tsp chili powder;
2 tsp ground cumin;
1 large onion, chopped;
1 medium green pepper, chopped;
1 can cream of chicken condensed soup;
3/4 cup water;
1 1/2 cups frozen corn;
2 (15 oz) cans white kidney beans, rinsed, drained;
2 tbsp shredded cheddar

Directions:
Heat oil in 4 qt saucepan over medium-high heat. Add chicken, chili powder, cumin, onion and pepper and cook until chicken is coked through and vegetables are tender, stirring often. Stir soup, water, corn and beans in saucepan and heat to a boil. Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook 5 min. stirring occassionally. Sprinkle with chese.
