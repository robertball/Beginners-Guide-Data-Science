Title:
Chicken Noodle Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. extra wide egg noodles
3 T. butter
1/2 c. celery, diced
1/4 c. onion, diced
1 can cream of chicken soup,
1 can cream of mushroom soup,
1 cup milk,
2 cups cheddar cheese, grated,
1 can sliced mushrooms, drained,
2 cups cooked chicken, diced,
1 tsp salt,
1/4 tsp pepper,
1/2 cup buttered bread crumbs,

Directions:
Prepare noodles according to package directions.  Drain.  In medium-sized pan, melt butter.  Cook celery and onion until tender.  Add soup, milk, and cheese.  Stir until cheese melts.  Combine noodles, cheese sauce, mushrooms, chicken, salt and pepper.  Pour into 3 qt. baking dish.  Top with crumbs.  Bake 350 for 30 minutes.
