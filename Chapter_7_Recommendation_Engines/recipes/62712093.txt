Title:
Quinoa & Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/616090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, minced (use amount to your taste)
1 c uncooked quinoa, rinsed*
2 c reduced sodium chicken broth
1/8 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes, opt.
4 c fresh baby spinach, packed, coarsely chopped
1/2 tsp Kosher salt

Directions:
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over med-high heat.  Add onion, garlic and red pepper flakes, stirring frequently, until onion is tender, about 3-5 min.
Add quinoa to skillet; cook stirring frequently, until quinoa starts to turn golden brown, about 2 minutes.
Add broth, black pepper; bring to a boil.  Cover skillet and reduce heat to low and simmer for 13 minutes; stir in spinach.  Cover skillet and cook until spinach and quinoa are tender and liquid is absorbed, about 3-5 min; season w/salt.  WW PointsPlus Value:  3 (per 1/2c).

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Weight Watcher's Side Dish that pairs well with grilled chicken or pan fried tillapia, or as a meal in itself.  Can vary it by adding various optional veggies, etc.  (See Footnotes).
