Title:
Turkey, Brie and Apple Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices 100% whole wheat  bread
3 ounces sliced turkey breast
1/2 Granny Smith apple, sliced thin
1/2 Gala apple, sliced thin
1 ounce Brie, sliced
1/4 cup Shredded Romaine lettuce*
2 tablespoons light mayonnaise, divided

Directions:
Spread one slice of bread with 1/2 of the mayonnaise. Layer turkey, apples, cheese, and lettuce (or coleslaw) on the bread.  Spread the second slice of bread with remaining mayonnaise and top sandwich. Slice in half.  Serve with extra apple slices if desired.

Submitter: 
Grain Foods Foundation
Description: 
Thinly sliced apples give this sandwich a bright crunch and lend a nice fruit sweetness to balance the richness of the brie.
