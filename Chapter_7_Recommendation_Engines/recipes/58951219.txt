Title:
Budget Sugar Cookie Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/278158.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb box of confectioners' sugar
3 egg whites

Directions:
In a kitchen aide mixing bowl fitted with the whip attachment place the 3 egg whites and whip on high speed until they become light and frothy, about 30 seconds.
On a low/medium speed add the  confectioners' sugar in 3 additions and whip just until frosting becomes smooth and glossy.  Add food coloring, if frosting is a little thick for your decorating needs, add a little water, one drop at a time, until desired consistency is reached.
Add food coloring, if frosting is a little thick for your decorating needs, add a little water, one drop at a time, until desired consistency is reached.

Description: 
This icing recipe cannot be any easier to make!!  With just 2 simple ingredients this batch of icing will cover at least 3 dozen cookies.  It dries hard and fast and the food color stays vibrant.Modified from: Sugar Cookie Icing
