Title:
squash on the grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 yellow squash
4-6 tblsp butter
salt
pepper
parmesean cheese

Directions:
wash, slice in half longwise, and take out seeds. then fill with butter sprinkle salt pepper & cheese. wrap individualy in foil gril medium 30 minutes to 1 hour till can squeeze with pot holder

easy way to add squash to your barbeque
