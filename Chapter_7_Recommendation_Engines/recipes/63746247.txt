Title:
apple streusel cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp baking soda
10 oz jar tart applesauce
2 oz/ 4 tbsp butter, softened
3 oz/ 1/2 c brown sugar
1 large egg
6 oz/ 1 1/4 c self rising flour
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
topping:
1 3/4 oz/ 1/3 c all purpose flour
1 3/4 oz/ 1/3 c brown sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp grated nutmeg
2 1/2 tbsp butter

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
put 14paper baking cases in a miffin pan or place 14double-layer cases on a cookie sheet
1st  make the topping. put the flour, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl. cut the butter into smaller pieces, then either rub it in by hand or blend unil the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs. set aside while making the cakes
to make the cupcakes, add the baking soda to the jar of apple sauce and stir until dissolved. put butter abd sugar in a bowl and beat together until light and fluffy. gradually beat in the egg. sift the flour,cinnamon, and nutmeg and, using a large metal spoon fold into the mixture, alternating with the applesauce.
spoon the batter into the paper cases. sprinkle the topping over each cupcake to cover the tops and pess down gently.
bake the cupcakes for 20 min or until well risen or golden brown. leave the cakes for 2-3 mins before serving warm or transferto a wire rack and let cool
