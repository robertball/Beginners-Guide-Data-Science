Title:
"Chocolately" Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana, peeled and broken into chunks
3/4 cup skim milk
1/3 cup fat-free plain yogurt
2 tsp honey
1 tbsp carob powder
2 ice cubes (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jenn G.
Description: 
This smoothie tastes decadent, but the use of non-fat ingredients and carob powder in place of chocolate makes it a healthy way to start your day!
Any flavour yogurt can be used in place of the plain, to add another flavour or additional sweetness.
