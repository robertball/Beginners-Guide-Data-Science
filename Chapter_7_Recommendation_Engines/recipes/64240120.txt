Title:
Sauteed Cauliflower with shallots and Currants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1767487.jpg
Ingredients:
1	head cauliflower, chopped (about 1 inch pieces or less)
¼	cup minced shallot
¼	cup currants, dates, or other type of fruit, diced
2	tablespoons honey or agave nectar
Salt and pepper
Olive oil and butter as needed

Directions:
in a large skillet over medium high heat add 2 tablespoons oil let it get hot, add about 2 tablespoons butter; add cauliflower let set DON’T touch it for a few minutes until it is nice and browned.  Stir in shallots and add more oil or butter if needed.  Add salt and pepper.  let caramelize a few more minutes; add fruit and agave nectar or honey.  Stir taste and adjust seasoning.
