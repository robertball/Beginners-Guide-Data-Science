Title:
Egg Salad with Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 avocado
3 hard boiled eggs, peeled
1T. sweet pickle relish
1T. chopped onion (optional)
1T. chopped celery (optional)
salt to taste

Directions:
In a medium size mixing bowl put in the avocado and mash until creamy. Chop up the eggs and add them to the avocado. Add the rest of the ingredients. Serve in a sandwich or on crackers.

Submitter: 
Karen Anderson Sorenson
Description: 
I wanted to make a healthier egg salad. This one uses avocado instead of mayonnaise.
