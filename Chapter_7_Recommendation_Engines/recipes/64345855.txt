Title:
Breakfast Baked Potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large russet baking potato, 2 eggs, sausage (any kind you like), 2 strips of bacon, 1/4 cup shredded cheese, salt, pepper, splash of milk, vegetable oil and 4 tbsps. of butter.

Directions:
Clean potato, rub potato with vegetable oil and salt. Wrap potato in foil and bake in a 425 degree oven until squeezable, yet firm. While potato is baking, cook breakfast meats. Slice done potato in the center to create a pocket. Add 2 tbsps. of butter, salt and pepper to potato. Then stuff with sliced or crumbled bacon and/or sausage. In a non-stick skillet, add rest of butter. In a bowl, stir the eggs with a splash of milk, salt and pepper. Pour into skillet and stir until are scrambled. Place scrambled eggs on top of breakfast meats. Then sprinkle cheese on warm eggs.

Submitter: 
Shandel L
Description: 
I came up with this idea one morning when my son wanted hash browns, but I didn't want to do all the chopping and such. So, I just baked a potato and stuffed all the other breakfast items inside. He loved it and is now making it on his own! And there are many options with this dish.
