Title:
Oat Energy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. raw honey
1/2 c. tahini butter
2 tblsp maple syrup
1 tblsp canola oil
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 c. light brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 c. toasted wheat germ
1/2 cu. dried apricots
1/2 c. dried figs or raisins
1/2 tsp. Kosher salt

Directions:
Spray 9 x 13 baking dish with non-stick spray. Set aside.  Combine honey, tahini butter, maple syrup, canola oil, 1/4 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 c. light brown sugar in pot.  Cook over medium heat 3 - 5 minutes, stirring.
Take off heat and stir in vanilla extract.
In another large bowl combine oats, rice cereal, wheat germ and dried fruit and salt.  Pour hot mixture into dry ingredients, mixing well.  Spoon out into prepared 9 x 13 pan.  Press down firmly with parchment paper.  Cool in refrigerator and cut into bars.
Makes 14 bars.  240 calories apiece.

Submitter: 
Keep it Tasty
Description: 
Make this nutless with Tahini Butter.  Otherwise you can use peanut butter and peanuts in the bar.
