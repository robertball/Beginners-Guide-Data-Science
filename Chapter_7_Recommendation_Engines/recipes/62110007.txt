Title:
Wild Bird Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups chunky peanut butter
2 Cups oats (uncooked)
2 Cups yellow corn meal
1 Cup whole wheat flour
2 Cups raisins
3 apples cored and chopped
1/2 cup bacon fat (optional)

Directions:
Core and chops apples and place in a bowl with all of the dry ingredients. Melt peanut butter (and bacon fat if using) either on top of stove or in the microwave. Add peanut butter to dry ingredients and stir until combined. Press mixture into a 9X9 pan and let chill in the refrigerator. Treats may then be cut into squares and individually frozen.

Submitter: 
LINDA MCLEAN
Description: 
On a cold winter's day, your feathered friends will appreciate and enjoy this nutritious and tasty treat.
