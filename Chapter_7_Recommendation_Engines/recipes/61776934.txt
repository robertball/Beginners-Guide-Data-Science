Title:
Elaine's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1 cup onion; chopped
1 cup green bell pepper; chopped
1 clove garlic; chopped
28 ounces canned whole tomatoes; chopped
8 ounces canned tomato sauce
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon oregano
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon sugar
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon cumin
15 1/2 ounces canned kidney beans

Directions:
Brown ground meat with onion, green pepper and garlic.
Add the rest and heat.
Serve with chopped onions, shredded cheddar, and/or sour cream.
