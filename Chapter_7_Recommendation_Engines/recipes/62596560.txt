Title:
easy gluten free peanut butter cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 egg
1 cup of sugar

Directions:
mix together
place balls on cookies sheet
make criss cross marks with some sugar on fork
bake at 350 for 10 to 12 minutes..
I like to add chocolate chips, m&m's or whatever you feel like
