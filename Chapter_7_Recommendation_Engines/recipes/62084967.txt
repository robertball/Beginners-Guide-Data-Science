Title:
Dave's Ginger Onion Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped fresh ginger
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 tablespoon sugar
2/3 cup sake or white wine
1/4 cup mirin
1 small diced carrot
1/4 cup diced apple

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 5 minutes.  Cool to a manageable temperature and blend the entire mix using a tabletop blender or hand blender.  Use immediately or refrigerate for up to two weeks.

Submitter: 
The Sushi Guy
Description: 
This dressing / sauce is versatile and is great used as an asian salad dressing, topping for sushi/sashimi (most white fish or fatty fishes), and also can bring a twist to some cooked dishes as well.  This sauce also works great as a steak marinade!
