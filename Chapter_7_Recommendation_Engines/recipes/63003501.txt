Title:
Beef Stew, Gluten Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb lean stew meat
4 cubed golden potatoes
1 cup baby carrots
4-5 stalks chopped celery
1 yellow onion, quartered
4 cups of water
Olive oil for browning meat
4 GF beef bouillon cubes
1 Tbl parsley
1 Tbl paprika
2 tsp Oregano
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp onion powder
2 cloves of minced garlic
1/4 tsp salt (there is salt in beef bouillon)
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Drizzle oil in pan, on high heat, quickly sear meat. Immediately remove from pan and into slow cooker.
Add 4 cups of water to pan with dripping. Add all spices from parsley down to pepper.  Bring to a boil while stiriing frequently.
Reserve approx 1 cup of liquid, add the rest of liquid to slow cooker. Add potatoes, onion, carrots and celery to slow cooker.
With remaining cup of hot liquid add a Gluten Free thickener such as rice flour or corn starch, whisk until lumps are gone and liquid is a gravy consistency.  Add to slow cooker and stir all ingredients together.
Cook on High for approx. 4 hours, or Low for 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
MARCIL
Description: 
Every Stew seasoning mix in the grocery store contains GLUTEN! i have never made stew before, so I was clueless on how to make it, especially GF!  I came up with this recipe by doing a little research, and my family loves this traditional, rich and hearty stew.
