Title:
Simple and Delicious Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans chicken chunks (drained) we use Sam's
1 T Lemon Pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
3 T sweet pickle relish
1/2 to 1 cup mayonnaise
salt to taste

Directions:
Put drained chicken in bowl. Smash with a fork to shred the chunks.  Add lemon pepper, garlic powder and pickle relish to the chicken. Stir well.
Add mayonnaise until the mixture is the consistency you like.  You may need more than 1/2 cup.

Submitter: 
Peggy
Description: 
A similar version of this salad was demonstrated at Sam's one morning.  We really like it for a quick meal.
