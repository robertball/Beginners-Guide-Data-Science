Title:
Rib BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp cider vinegar
2 tbsp Worcestershier sauce
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
pinch of red cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.   Marinate ribs in part of sauce.   Use the rest to baster and/or dip the finished ribs.

Submitter: 
jodygreiner
Description: 
My families favorite rib sauce.   Perfect with boneless country ribs.
