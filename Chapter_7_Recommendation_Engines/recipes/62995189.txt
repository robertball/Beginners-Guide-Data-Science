Title:
Buttery Sweet Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C frozen for fresh corn
1/4 C half-n-half cream
2 T butter or margarine
1 T sugar
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
In a sauce pan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Simmer uncovered for 6-8 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
Side dish that is easy and tasteful. Also easy to whip up on a moments notice.
