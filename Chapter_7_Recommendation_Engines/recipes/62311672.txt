Title:
Springtime Ranch Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups spiral pasta
1 medium zucchini, cubed
1 cup English cucumber, diced
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives
1/2 cup chopped sweet red pepper
1/4 cup chopped scallion
3/4 cup frozen peas, thawed
1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/4 cup sour cream
1 tbsp. dry ranch dressing mix
1 1/4 teaspoons dill weed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain and rinse with cold water. Place in a large bowl. Add zucchini, olives, red pepper, scallion, cucumber and peas. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over salad and toss to coat. Cover and chill for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
I added ranch dressing mix for flavor as well as peas and cucumbers.  I love the sour cream in this - I've done that with potato salads, but not with pasta salads.  A family favorite!Modified from: Springtime Pasta Salad
