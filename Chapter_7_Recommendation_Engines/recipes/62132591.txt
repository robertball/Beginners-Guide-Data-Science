Title:
Chocolate Hazelnut Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups whipping cream
1 jar (13 oz.) hazelnut spread (Nutella)

Directions:
Whip cream in a chilled bowl until soft peaks form.
Fold in hazelnut spread. Streaks of cream can be showing.
Spoon into 6 glasses.
May be garnished with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
This Nutella Mousse is rich and creamy!  It is very easy to make.
