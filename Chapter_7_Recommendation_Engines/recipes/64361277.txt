Title:
Zesty Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2147648.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup reduced fat mayonnaise
1/4 cup fat free sour cream
1 tbsp ketchup, organic
2 tbsp cream style horseradish sauce (I make my own or use Ingolhoffer)
1/3 tsp paprika
1/3 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp oregano
dash ground black pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well. Keep sauce covered in refrigerator for about 30 minutes to let the flavors blend.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
Restaurant style sauce for my Crispy Oven Fried Turkey Burgers. Also perfect for Onion Rings.
