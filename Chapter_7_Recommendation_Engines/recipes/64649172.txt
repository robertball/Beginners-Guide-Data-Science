Title:
Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123250.jpg
Ingredients:
9" or 10" pie crust, baked and cooled
1 Tbsp. grated lime zest
1 cup fresh lime juice (2-3 limes & bottled juice)
14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
3.5 oz. pkg.instant vanilla pudding mix
8 oz. container frozen whipped topping, thawed (more for garnish)
2 Tbsp. sour cream (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together the lime zest, lime juice , condensed milk and sour cream.
Whisk in the pudding mix and allow to set up 5 minutes.
Fold in the tub of whipped topping.
Pour mixture into the pie shell.
Chill at least 2 hours before serving.
Garnish with additional whipped topping and lime zest, if desired.
