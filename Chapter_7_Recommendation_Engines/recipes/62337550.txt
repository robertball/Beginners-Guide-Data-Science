Title:
spinach tomato artichoke pasta w/sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/389157.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cooked Italian chicken sausages, crumbled
1 can(~15oz) artichoke hearts, drained
1 can(~15oz) diced Italian tomatoes, drained
1/2 of a 9oz bag of spinach (may have been a 12oz bag)
8oz bowtie pasta
Parmesan cheese for topping.

Directions:
Prepare pasta per directions. Meanwhile, saute sausage over med heat until heated through. Stir in drained tomatoes and artichokes and let cook until pasta is nearly done. Add spinach and let wilt but not cook. Mix sausage mixture with drained pasta and serve.
