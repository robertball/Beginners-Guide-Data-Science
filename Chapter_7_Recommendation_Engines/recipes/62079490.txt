Title:
Chicken Honey Mustard Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs honey
1 tbs Corn Syrup
2 tbs Grained Mustard
1 tbs Mayonaise
1 tsp Dried Onion Flakes
1 Chicken Breast Fillet
2-3 large rashes of bacon cut into big pieces
1 cup of chopped mushrooms
1 cup of Mozzarella Cheese
3-4 tbs of Ranch Dressing
1 tbs chopped parsley
1 Pizza Base

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix mustard, honey, corn syrup, mayonaise and onion flakes.
Cut Chicken fillet into large strips.
Cook chicken in frypan and drizzle with half of the honey mustard sauce, reserve the rest of sauce.
Remove chicken, cool and then slice into smaller pieces.
On the pizza base, cover with Ranch Dressing. Then place on half of the cheese, all of chicken, mushrooms & bacon.  Drizzle remaining Honey Mustard sauce over pizza.  Top with remaining cheese.
Cook pizza in moderate to hot oven for 20-30 minute and then sprinkle with parsley.
