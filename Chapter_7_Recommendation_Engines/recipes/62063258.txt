Title:
Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T. butter
1 T. minced onion
1/2 cup flour
3 cups rich chicken broth
1/2 cup finely grated carrots
3 T. finely slivered almonds
2 cups cooked wild rice
1/3 cup finely minced ham
2 T. dry sherry
Salt to taste
1 cup half & half
Minced chives or parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Melt butter in a small soup pot; saute' the onion until tender.  Whisk in the flour; blend until smooth.  Gradually add the chicken broth; stir constantly for 1 minute.
Add the carrots, almonds, wild rice, minced ham and sherry.
Season with salt.
Slowly add the half & half.  Heat slowly.  Serve in soup bowls and garnish with chives or parsley.

Submitter:

Description: 
This soup has been a hit with friends and family for many years.  Yes, it is very rich but worth every decadent bite.  You can make it with whole milk rather than half and half but try it at least once as is - thick and creamy!
