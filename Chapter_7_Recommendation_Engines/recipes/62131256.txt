Title:
Armenian rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 cup very thin egg noodles (crushed)
1 1/2 cups of long grain whit rice
3 1/2 cups chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Rinse and soak rice in a bowl of water for 10 minutes
2. boil chicken broth in a large sauce pan
3. melt butter in frying pan and add noodles.
4. stir noodles until brown
5. Add drained rice to the noodles in frying pan
6. stir rice and noodles for 1 minute
7. Pour rice and noodle mixture into the saucepan with the boiling chicken broth
8.reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes until rice is tender.
9. do not lift lid until 20 minutes are up.
10. Add salt and peper to taste and fluff with a fork

Submitter: 
Michele
Description: 
This recipe is my grandmother's. She came from Armenia and it has been in our family for years. We all still make it and it is so easy.
