Title:
The best green beans...EVER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cut green beans, drained
6 slices of bacon
1 small onion chopped
1/2 c garlic flavored vinegar (or a flavored vinegar of your choice. Balsamic is nice but omit the sugar if you use this,as it it naturally sweet.)
1 tsp sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
brown bacon in skillet until crisp...drain on paper towel leving the bacon drippings in pan.
Add chopped onion to bacon drippings untill translucent.
add vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper to mixture and reduce.
add green beans, salt and pepper. sautee till warm.
before serving crumble bacon over mixture.

Submitter: 
superfoodie
Description: 
My family loves this dish and it is requested often.  Green beans in a slightly sweet,yet savory side dish.
You'll not want green beans any other way!
