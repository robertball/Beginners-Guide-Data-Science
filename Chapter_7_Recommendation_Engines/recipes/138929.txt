Title:
Vanilla-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812453.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bananas, broken into chunks
1 cup vanilla ice cream
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup fresh orange juice
1 cup milk

Directions:
Place banana and vanilla ice cream into a blender. Pour in vanilla extract, orange juice, and milk. Puree until thick and smooth.

Submitter: 
Emma J
Description: 
This is a very simple recipe (as are most smoothies) which is a great cool relief from a hot summer day.
