Title:
Pumpkin Layer Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6083793.jpg
Ingredients:
Layer #1
1 Box yellow Cake Mix
1 Stick butter
1 Egg, beaten
Layer #2
1 Large Can Pumpkin
4 Eggs
1/2 C Brown sugar
1/4 C White sugar
1 12 ounce Can canned milk
1 1/2 t Cinnamon
Layer #3
1 Cup Cake mix
1 C White flour
1/2 C Walnut pieces
1 Stick Butter
1/2 C White sugar

Directions:
Set aside 1 cup cake mix for third layer.
Cream butter with remaining cake mix
Add egg
Spread mix in a well greased 2 quart glass baking dish
Mix 2nd layer ingredients and pour over 1st layer
Cream butter with rest of layer 3 ingredients it should be crumbly
Sprinkle on top of cake
Bake covered in preheated oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 45 minutes
Uncover and bake 15 more minutes until cake is set and not jiggley and knife comes out clean
Cool
Top with whip cream and serve

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
This cake is like a combination of pumpkin custard and coffee cake and it is sooooooooo good!
