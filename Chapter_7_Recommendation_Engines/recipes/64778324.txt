Title:
rice a roni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup pasta of some kind in small pieces
1 1/2 cup of rice
1 onion diced
1 pepper diced
2 spicy sauages cut in small pieces
1 carton chicken broth(4 cups)
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
in wok, add butter, pasta, rice, onion, peppers, sausage and fry for maybe 10 minutes
add chicken broth and combined spices
bring to a boil, then simmer for until rice is absorbed
fluff and serve
