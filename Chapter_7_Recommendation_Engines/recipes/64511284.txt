Title:
Hawaiian Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
toasted slices of bread
margarine (or sub spaghetti/pizza sauce for a more authentic pizza taste)
1 ham slice (or more, if they're thin) for each serving
1 drained canned pineapple slice (ring) for each serving
1 cheese slice (cheddar, mozzarella, swiss, etc., to taste) for each serving

Directions:
Spread toast slices with either margarine or spaghetti/pizza sauce.
Place ham, then pineapple and top with cheese on each slice.
Broil for a few minutes until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A quick light snack or fun addition to a meal with a salad. Ham, pineapple & cheese make this taste a bit like Hawaiian pizza.
