Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans diced tomatoes
2 cans green chiles
2 cups brown rice
1/4 cup taco seasoning
3 cups water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F. Add rice and taco seasoning to baking dish and stir. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Remove from oven and pour tomatoes and green chilies over rice and stir. Serve and enjoy.
