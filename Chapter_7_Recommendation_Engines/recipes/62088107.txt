Title:
spicy deviled eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs, hardboiled
1/2 cup mayonnaises
3 teaspoons hot yellow pepper sauce
3 tablespoons Dijon mustard

Directions:
Cut eggs in half lengthwise & remove yolks to food processor.
Add all other ingredients and puree thoroughly in food processor.
Place filling in a decorating bag with
decorative tip and fill halves.
Decorate with a sprig of parsley or dill.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
I concocted this recipe for my longtime friend and collegue Colleen B. for her 60th birthday celebration.
