Title:
Ma's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion minced
1/3 cup celery minced
1/2 c green pepper minced
2 lb ground beef
2tsp vinegar
1 T lemon juice
1 C ketchup
1 C water
2 T worchestershire
2 T brown sugar (to taste)
and 1/2 tsp tobasco

Directions:
Saute onion, celery and bell pepper until brown. Brown ground meat. Add together and add seasonings. Cook 20 minutes. Add fresh parsley if desired
