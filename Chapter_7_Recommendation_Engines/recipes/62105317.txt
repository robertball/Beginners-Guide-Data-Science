Title:
Sourdough Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sourdough starter
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup veg. shortening
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg
2-3 overripe bananas (about 1 cup)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease and flour 2 loaf pans.
Cream together the shortening and sugar. Add egg, beat well. Add sourdough starter and vanilla, beat well. Add bananas, blend well.
Sift together the dry ingredients and add to banana mixture. Mix well.
Pour batter into prepared pans and bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted into middle of bread comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
Classic banana bread with the tang of sourdough! If you already have a sourdough starter going, this recipe is so easy and so good!
