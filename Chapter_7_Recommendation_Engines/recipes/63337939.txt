Title:
Herb Crusted Tilapia with Garlic Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tilapia fillets
6 Tbsp melted salted butter
2 tsp roasted garlic paste
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp Italian herb seasoning
1/2 tsp dried, crumbled parsley flakes
1/4 tsp salt
Lemon juice

Directions:
Brush a broiler pan with olive oil. Mix the garlic paste into the melted butter, and divide in two bowls. Reserve second bowl of butter for serving. Mix all dry herbs and salt. Place fillets on broiler pan. Brush fish with garlic butter. Sprinkle liberally with seasoning mix. Broil 2 minutes, brush with butter,Broil an additional 2 minutes, then turn fillets over. Brush with butter, and sprinkle with the rest of the seasonings. Discard the rest of the basting butter. Broil fish until tilapia flakes easily with a fork.

Submitter: 
ChefMom
Description: 
Broiled tilapia crusted in herb, and drizzled with garlic butter is simply divine in this gluten-free nightshade free dish.
