Title:
Smoked Fattie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3620292.jpg
Ingredients:
-1lb spicy pork breakfast sausage
-shredded sharp cheddar
-pork rub

Directions:
-Flatten out sausage and then put layer of cheese on meat
-Roll back up into original shape and add rub to outside
-Smoke at 225-250 for about 2hrs(internal temp. of 165-175)
