Title:
Creamy Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups uncooked medium egg noodles
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
3/4 cup lowfat milk
1/4 cup mayonnaise (light ok)
2 tsp lemon juice or mustard
1/8 tsp black pepper
1 1/2 cups frozen peas, peas/carrots, or mixed vegetables
1 (4 oz) can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (6 oz) can tuna, drained
2 slices white bread
3 Tb butter or margarine, melted
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
1) Prepare noodles.
2) Meanwhile, combine soup, milk, mayo, lemon juice and pepper in large bowl.  Add frozen vegies, mushrooms and tuna.  Toss with noodles.  Place in greased or sprayed 8 x 12" glass baking pan.
3) For topping, crumble bread in food processor with a few pulses.  Melt butter or margarine in small saucepan.  Saute garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.  Off heat, stir in oregano and bread crumbs.  Sprinkle on top of casserole.
4) Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes, uncovered.  Cool in pan 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
STARFLOWER
Description: 
After many years and attempts, I finally arrived at this perfect yummy recipe for tuna casserole.  It is different in that it has less tuna, more creamy sauce, frozen vegies, and a tasty buttered bread crumb topping.
