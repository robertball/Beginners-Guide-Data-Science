Title:
Baked Cranberry Jalapeno Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4228156.jpg
Ingredients:
10 slices LAND O LAKESÂ® Deli American, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons jarred jalapeno pepper, chopped
2 cups frozen cranberries
2 tablespoons sugar
Baguette slices, toasted (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine cheeses and jalapenos in bowl; mix well.
Place cranberries and sugar in another bowl; toss to coat. Gently stir cranberries into cheese mixture.
Spread mixture into 1-quart casserole dish. Bake 30-35 minutes or until bubbly around edges.
Serve with baguette slices, if desired.
