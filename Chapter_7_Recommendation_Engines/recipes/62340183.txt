Title:
Creamy Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. potatoes, peeled and boiled until soft
12 oz. cream cheese
1/2 c. butter
2 tbsp. garlic garlic
1/2 c. milk

Directions:
Mash potatoes thoroughly and add remaining ingredients while mixing.  Add the necessary milk until very creamy.  Place in large casserole.  Top with additional butter and paprika.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes until heated through.
