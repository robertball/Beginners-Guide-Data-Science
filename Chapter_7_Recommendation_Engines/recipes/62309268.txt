Title:
Jill's Orgasmic Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pam Spray
3 eggs
1 2/3 cup sugar
(3) 8oz. Crème cheese pkgs.
(melt in microwave 6 min on power 30)
3 tsp vanilla
Honeymaid graham cracker crumbs (comes in box)
(check the instructions for what other ingredients are needed…butter…)
Sour Cream Topping:
3 Tbsp sugar
1 1/2 (24 oz) sour cream
3 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Line the outside & underside of a 10" springform pan with aluminum foil
Spray the entire bottom & sides of the pan withy pam spray (butter flavor is best)
For graham cracker crust
Follow the instructions on the back of the Honeymaid cracker crumbs box and multiply it by 1.5 times.
Press in the bottom of the springform pan & bake @350 degrees for 10 minutes.
Then pour in cheesecake batter.
Cheesecake Batter:	Beat eggs & vanilla separately.
Add to melted crème cheese & then beat in sugar.
Blend thoroughly.  Pour in on top of graham cracker crust and bake @375 degrees for 45 mins or when top is turning beige.
Remove from oven
Add sour crème mixture on top
Sour Cream Topping:
Beat ingredients togeth
Pour over top of cheesecake
Bake in 400 degree oven 30 mins or until top turns beige
Cool @room temperature
Cover w/aluminum foil
Refrigerate for at least 3 hrs
(better if made the day before)
Keep chilled
