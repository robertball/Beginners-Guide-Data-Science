Title:
Jan's Fluffy and Hearty, Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2235959.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 Â½ Tablespoons baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 large egg beaten
2 cups buttermilk
2 Tablespoons melted butter
1 teaspoon maple extract
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, salt, baking powder and cinnamon together in a large mixing bowl. Whisk until well incorporated.
Add in egg, buttermilk, butter and extracts.
Let batter sit undisturbed for 10 to 15 minutes or until batter is fluffy and thick.
Heat your griddle to medium and lightly oil the pan.
Gently scoop batter by 1/3 measuring cup being careful not to disturb the batter and deflate it too much, and place on griddle.
flip pancakes when bubbles appear on the edges and edges look slightly dry.
Once flipped cook a minute or two or until pancake springs back when toughed in the center.
Serve hot.  cool leftovers and refrigerate or freeze.

Description: 
Sturdy yet light and fluffy pancakes.  Delicious on their own but also make a great breakfast sandwich complete with egg, sausage and cheese.  Leftovers freeze very well.
