Title:
Crock-Pot Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium onions, sliced thin
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 - 15oz can diced tomatoes
1 - 8oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp celery seed
1 bay leaf
1/4 cup white wine
6 boned chicken breasts OR equivalent weight of chicken pieces

Directions:
Mix together onions, garlic, tomatoes, tomato sauce, salt, pepper, herbs , and wine. Place chicken in mixture. Freeze in freezer bag.
Serving Day:
Place in crock-pot and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high 2 1/2 - 4 hours. Serve over pasta
