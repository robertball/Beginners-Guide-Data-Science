Title:
Holiday Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cubed, cooked chicken meat
1 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon paprika
1 1/2 cups dried cherries
1 cup chopped celery
2 green onions, chopped
1/2 cup green grapes cut in half
1 cup chopped pecans
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise with paprika and seasoned salt.  Blend in dried cherries, celery, grapes, onion, and nuts.  Add chopped chicken, and mix well.  Season with black pepper to taste.  Chill 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Dried cherries and green grapes add a sweet tartness to chicken and vegetable salad.  Make a delightful sandwich filling, too.Modified from: Holiday Chicken Salad
