Title:
White Bean Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
2 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. cumin
2 cans (15 oz. each) white kidney or cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
3 cups chicken broth, divided
4 carrots, sliced
2 stalks celery, sliced
white pepper to taste
6 oz cheddar cheese, shredded
cornstarch (optional)
sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.
Cook chicken in a large skillet over medium heat for 2 minutes.
Stir in the onion, garlic and jalapeno.  Cook 2 minutes longer.
Sprinkle with oregano and cumin.  Cook 1 minute longer or until chicken is browned and vegetables are tender.  Transfer to a 3 qt. slow cooker.
In a small bowl, mash 1 cup of beans.  Add 1/2 cup broth.  Stir until blended.
Add to the slow cooker with the remaining beans and broth.  Add carrots, celery and white pepper.
Cook on low for 3 to 3 1/2 hours or until chicken juices run clear.  Stir before serving, thicken with cornstarch if desired.
Sprinkle with cheese and garnish with sour cream (opt.)
