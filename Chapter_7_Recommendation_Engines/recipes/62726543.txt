Title:
Saltine Cracker Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut
4 cups 2% milk
2 eggs, separated
1/2 cup sugar
2 cups crushed saltine crackers (about 40)
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Beat sugar and egg yolks until fluffy. In large saucepan over medium heat, heat milk. Add sugar and egg yolks and boil for 1 minute. Add coconut and cracker crumbs. Boil until thick. Beat egg whites until stiff, fold into pudding; add vanilla. Serve warm or cold.

Comfort food at its best! Serve warm or cold, add raisins or dates, have fun with this easy and cozy recipe!
