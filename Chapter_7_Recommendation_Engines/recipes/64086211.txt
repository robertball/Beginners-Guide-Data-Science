Title:
Low Calorie & Low Fat Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Angel Food Cake Mix
½ Cup Splenda
½ cup Sugar
½ Cup Sugar Free Chocolate Syrup
1 pkg Fat Free cream cheese (room temp)
2 Tbsp Instant Coffee
½ tub Fat Free whipped cream
½ cup crushed chocolate graham crackers.

Directions:
1. Make Angel Food cake according to recipe on box, but bake in a 15 x 17 jelly roll pan.  Bake until golden brown on top.
2. Whip cream cheese with sugars.  Mix in choc syrup & Instant coffee.  Beat unitl smooth & light.   Fold in whipped cream.
3. Spread on top of cooled cake.   Sprinkle with crushed coc graham crackers.

Submitter: 
ArmyWO
Description: 
This is an easy & yummy twist on Tiramisu that won't leave you feeling guilty for serving up a second helping.
