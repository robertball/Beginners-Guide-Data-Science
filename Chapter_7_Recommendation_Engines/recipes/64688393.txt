Title:
Gnocchi with butter thyme sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 half cup unsalted butter
1 T fresh thyme leaves
1 (1 pound) russet potato
1/2 t salt
1/4 t freshly ground black pepper
1 large egg, beaten to blend
1/4 cup shaved Pecorino Cheese

Directions:
Cook the butter in a heavy medium skillet until it begins to brown, about 2 min. Remove from heaat. Add thyme leaves & set aside
Microwave large russet potato until tender, turnung once about 12 min. cut the potato in half & scoop the flesh into a large bowl; discard the skin.
using a fork, mash the potato well. Mash in the salt & pepper. Mix in 3 T of the egg; discard remaining egg.  Sift the flour over the potato mixturest till blended.
Divide the dough into 4 equal pieces. Roll each piece between your palms & the work  surface into a 1/2 " diameter rope ( about 20 " long) Cut the dough into 1" pieces. Roll each piece of dough over a wooden paddle with ridges or over the tines of a fork to form grooves in the dough.
Cook the gnocchi ina large pot of boiling salted water until it rise to the top, about 1 min. continue cooking about 4 minutes longer. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the gnocchi to the hot butter thyme in the skillet. Toss to coat.
Spoon the gnocchi and butter sauce into shallow bowls. Top  with Pecorino Chesse & serve
