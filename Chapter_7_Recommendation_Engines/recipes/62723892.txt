Title:
Bacon and Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large eggs
1.5 C heavy cream
1 C bacon bits
8 oz shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 C cooked chopped spinach

Directions:
Mix eggs and cream. Add bacon, cheese and spinach. Mix well
Pour into a 9-in pie pan that has been buttered.
Bake at 325 degrees for 45-60 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Easy to prepare and only 2.5 carbs because it is crustless. Keeps well and can be reheated in the microwave
