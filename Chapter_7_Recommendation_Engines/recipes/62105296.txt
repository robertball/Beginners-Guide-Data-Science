Title:
ham and pineapple fried sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS
4 pieces Sliced ham
4 pieces sourdough bread
cream cheese
1 can crushed pineapple
2 slices of cheese (either provalone or swiss)
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
2tbp of sugar

Directions:
heat a pan med heat with a mixture of oil and butter
take a slice of bread and spread cream cheese
layer on 2 slices of ham
Open the can of pineapple and drain out all juice
next put about 2 tblspoons of the pineapple.
put on top of that a piece of cheese.
put the top piece of bread on.
In a med bowl combine milk and egg and sugar
cut  the crusts off the sandwiches and dip both sides into the mixture
place in pan for about 2-3 minutes each side or until brown.
Place on a papertowl to get rid of extra oil.
let cool for 3 minutes

Submitter: 
brianad
Description: 
This sandwich is yummy and easy to make. Sorta in between grilled cheese and a monte cristo. Easy to make for lunch and snacks,Kid friendly.
