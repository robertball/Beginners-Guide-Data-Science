Title:
Juice Pulp Pumpkin Carrot Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup 100% whole wheat flour
2 tsp baking power
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp pumpkin spice
1 cup pumpkin puree
2 whole bananas
1 cup juice pulp (2 large carrots and 150g of apple)
1/2 cup maple syrup)
1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and spray a muffin tin with cooking spray or line with muffin cups. In a large bowl whisk all the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt & pumpkin spice).
In a separate bowl, mash the bananas and then mix in the juice pulp, pumpkin puree, maple syrup, and chocolate chips. Add wet ingredients to dry until just combined. Scoop ~1/4 cup of the batter into each muffin cup and bake for 18-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Description: 
Pulp from juicing give these muffins a vitamin and mineral punch worth waking up for.
