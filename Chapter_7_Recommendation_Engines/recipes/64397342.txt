Title:
Protein Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups protein
1/4 cup fiber
1 1/2 cups Brown Rice Syrup
3 cups oats (gluten free)
16 oz almond butter
Agave nectar honey

Directions:
Add agave nectar (honey) and almond butter in a bowl and microwave for 70-90 sec
Stir. Add protein and fiber, mix, and Stir in oats. Spread in cookie sheet or roll into balls and chill for at least 1 hr.

Submitter: 
Joseph Alvarado
Description: 
A great protein bar snack.
