Title:
Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/4 cup oil
1 tsp vanilla
2 Tbsp poppy seeds
1 fresh lemon
2 cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 Tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
In a medium size bowl combine egg, milk, oil, vanilla and poppy seeds.
Grate zest from lemon and squeeze out juice.  Add zest and lemon juice to milk mixture.  Add flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
Spoon into greased muffin tin.  Bake for 15-20 minutes.
