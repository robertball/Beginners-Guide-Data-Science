Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground chuck
1 large onion, chopped
2 pkg McCormick Chili Mix
1 regular can Ranch Style Beans
1 medium can Ranch Style Beans
1 can mild Rotel tomatoes
2 cans diced tomatoes
4 4-oz. cans tomato sauce
Chili powder to taste
Ground Cumin to taste
Garlic Powder to taste
Black Pepper to taste
1-2 cups beef broth or bullion

Directions:
In a 6-qt chili pot, brown ground chuck and onions together.  Drain.
Add remaining ingredients EXCEPT broth.  Stir thoroughly.
Add enough broth to obtain desired consistency.
Bring to a boil.  Lower to simmer.  Simmer 30 minutes to 1 hour.
