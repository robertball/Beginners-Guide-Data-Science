Title:
Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup half and half
1/2 cup Mayonaise
2 TBLS flour
1/3 cup onion (minced)
1/4 tsp Garlic powder
8 oz swiss or cheedar cheese (grated)
salt (to taste)
1 cup broccoli (optional)
1 cup diced ham (optional)
1 pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Hand whip eggs, half and half, mayonaise and flour in medium bowl.  Add remaining ingredients.  I make either a brocoli cheddar or ham and cheddar, or both!
Pour into a 9 inch pie crust and bake for 45 to 60 minutes until it sets up and is golden on top.
