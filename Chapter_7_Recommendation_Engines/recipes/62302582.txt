Title:
Homemade Toaster Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the crust:
3/4 C veg. shortening
1 Tbs. sugar
1 egg yolk
3 1/2 C. flour
1/2 C. preserves
Brown sugar Cinnamon filling: (Optional)
3/4 C. Powdered Sugar
1/4 C. Brown sugar
2 Tsp. Cinnamon
3 Tbls. Melted butter
For the frosting (optional)
1/2 C. Powdered sugar (Confectioner's)
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 tblsp. milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F
Cream shortening and sugar then beat in one egg at a time.
Sift together flour and baking powder, stir into shortening mix to make a soft dough.
Form a flattened ball. Wrap in plastic wrap. Chill for 2-3 hours.
Turn dough onto a floured surface and roll out 12 rectangles-each 8x12".
Spread about a tblsp. of preserves or cinnamon sugar mixture over 1/2 of each rectangle-staying well within the edges.
Fold dough over the preserves and trim the edges or crimp to close.
Place tarts on a greased cookie sheet and brush w/the egg yolk cream mix.
Bake for 15-20 minutes or until slightly underdone.
Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
Brown sugar Cinnamon filling: (Optional)
Stir together powdered sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon and butter until smooth.
For the optional frosting:
In a small bowl mix the 10x sugar, the vanilla and the milk until smooth.
Drizzle over cooled tarts.
