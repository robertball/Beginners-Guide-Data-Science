Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground meat
2 tbsp butter
1 cup finely chopped onions
½ cup finely chopped carrots
½ cup finely chopped celery
3 tbsp flour
2 tbsp tomato paste
½ cup dry white wine
1 1/2cups chicken broth
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
Pepper to taste
1 cup cooked corn kernels
2 or more cups mashed potatoes (instant mashed potatoes work well)
½ cup grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown the ground meat into fine (but not burned) pieces
Heat the butter and add the onions, carrots, & celery.  Cook, stirring, until the onions are wilted.
Sprinkle with flour and stir with a wire whisk.  Add the tomato paste, wine, broth, Worcestershire and pepper, stirring rapidly with whisk.  Let simmer 10 minutes and stir in the ground meat and corn.  Cook 5 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350.
Place meat and sauce in baking dish and spoon on mashed potatoes.  Sprinkle evenly with cheese and place in oven.  Bake 30 to 45 minutes or until dish is piping hot throughout and the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Charleen Don
Description: 
Any cooked meat could be used in this traditional British dish.  I prefer ground meat, but leftover roast lamb, beef, veal or pork.
