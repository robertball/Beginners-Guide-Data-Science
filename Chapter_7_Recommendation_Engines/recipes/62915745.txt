Title:
BBQ Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick unsalted butter
3/4 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup worchestershire
1 Tab. soy sauce
1 1/2 tea. fresh lemon juice
1 clove garlic, minced
3/8 tea. onion powder
1/4 tea. pepper
1/8 tea. paprika
pinch of cayenne
1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Combine all but the shrimp in cast iron skillet over med-high heat. Cook until reduced and slightly thickened, 4-6 minutes.
Add shrimp and cook until just barely opaque throughout, about 3 min. Serve hot w/ crusty bread to sop up juice.
