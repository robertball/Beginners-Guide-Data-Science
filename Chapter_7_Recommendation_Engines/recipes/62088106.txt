Title:
cabbage soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head of cabbage (meduim sized head) - use the top
4 large fronds of swiss chard
3 medium fronds of kale
4 scallions
1/2 red bell pepper
1/2 cup of thinly sliced carrots
1/2 cup of the celery tops w/greens
3 cubes of chicken bullion
3/4 c of slivered bacon strips
1 ts marojoram
1 tb parsley
1 ts sage

Directions:
put the coarsely chopped veggies in the food processor and bring them to a fine chop. Where everythings 1/4 of an inch to an 1/8.  Put the items in a slow cooker with the finely chopped bacon, make sure the water is to the brim and add your spices. Cook until done on hi.

Submitter: 
Victoria M.H.
Description: 
This is a different twist on cabbage soup, I think it normally is plain. You can do cabbage soup w/out the meat and have a nice vegetarian dish aswell.
