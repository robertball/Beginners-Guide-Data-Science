Title:
Southern Style Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2-lb pkg, southern style hash browns (cubed), partially thawed
2 10.5 cans reduced fat cream of chicken soup
16 ounces reduced fat sour cream
2 cups 2% sharp cheddar cheese, shredded (do not recommend fat-free cheese)
2 teaspoons onion powder
1 teaspoon pepper
2-3 Tablespoons louisiana-style hot sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray a 9x13 inch glass baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
Combine soups, sour cream, onion powder, pepper and hot sauce in large mixing bowl with  large spoon.
Add shredded cheese and mix.
Add potatoes and mix.
Pour into greased pan.
Cook, uncovered, in oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour until potatoes are cooked through.

Submitter: 
DRFISH
Description: 
A delicious, cheesy hash brown casserole with an extra special kick from your favorite hot sauce.  This recipe is at every family gathering we have!  My kids love this better than mashed potatoes.  I use reduced fat ingredients with no noticeable difference in taste.
