Title:
Dot Weiner's Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Graham Cracker Crumbs
1 - 16 oz. Small Curd Cottage Cheese
2 - 8 oz. Cream Cheese
1 1/2 cups Granulated Sugar
4 eggs
1/3 cup Corn Starch
2 TBLS. Lemon Juice
1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
1/2 cup Butter
1 pint Sour Cream

Directions:
1. Grease a 9" Springform Pan.
2. Dust pan with crushed Graham Cracker Crumbs.
3. Seive Cottage Cheese into a large bowl.
4. Add Cream Cheese and beat well on high speed until creamy.
5. Beat in Sugar, then Eggs at high speed.
6. Reduce speed to low and add Corn Starch, Lemon Juice and Vanilla.
7. Add Melted Butter and Sour Cream and blend on low speed.
8. Pour into prepared pan and Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour.
9. Leave in oven for 2 hours once baked.
10.  Remove cake and allow to totally cool on wire rack before refrigerating.
11. Wrap tightly being sure not to let plastic touch top of cake.  Chill,overnight.
12. Remove sides of pan and enjoy!
Can be topped with fruit.  Can be frozen.  May make 2 thinner cakes instead of pne really high one.

Submitter: 
Fantasy Gardener and Chef
Description: 
Dot Weiner had 6 boys to feed. She made a few things really well.  Cheesecake was one of them.  This cake has 4 dairy products in it and a nice, thin, hint or graham cracker crust. It is all about the cheese here.  

You could make mini cakes in a muffin tin instead.
