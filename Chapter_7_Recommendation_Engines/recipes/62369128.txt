Title:
World's Best Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sourdough bread, 2 slices, toasted or not
Mayonnaise (not Miracle Whip)
Avocado, 1/2 ripe
Lawry's Season Salt
2 - 3 slices crisp bacon
Roast turkey slices
Pepper, opt.
Tomato, sliced, opt.
1 or 2 lettuce leaves

Directions:
Spread both slices of sourdough with mayonnaise. On one slice of bread spread the avocado and sprinkle lightly with seasoning salt. Top with bacon slices. Layer turkey slices on top of bacon, add tomato slices and pepper if desired. Top with lettuce and second slice of bread. Cut sandwich in half and serve with beverage of choice.

Submitter: 
MAMA2EANDJ
Description: 
Roast turkey, avocado and bacon on sourdough bread. What could be better than this? Easy and scrumptious too. Enjoy!
