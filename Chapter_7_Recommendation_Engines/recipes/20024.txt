Title:
Six Things Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) can fruit punch
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
1 (6 fluid ounce) container frozen grape juice concentrate
6 cups cold water
3 cups ginger ale
1 blood orange, cut into rounds

Directions:
In a large punch bowl, combine fruit punch, lemonade concentrate, orange juice concentrate and grape juice concentrate. Stir in the water, then the ginger ale. Float orange slices on top.

Submitter: 
JANA M.
Description: 
A great fruity punch made with orange juice, lemon juice, grape juice and canned fruit punch. It's carbonated with ginger ale and has blood red oranges floating an top.
