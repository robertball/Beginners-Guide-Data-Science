Title:
Chiles Relleno Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked white rice
2 cups grated Monterey Jack cheese-divided use
2 cups grated cheddar or Longhorn cheese- divided use
1 - 7 oz. can diced green chilies
1 - 16 oz. container sour cream
1 tsp. Garlic powder
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. chili powder
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together in large bowl, reserving 1/2 cup of each type cheese.  Turn rice mixture into greased 3 quart casserole.  Sprinkle reserved cheese over top.  Bake at 350 for 30 min.

Submitter: 
SweetBasil
Description: 
A cheesy, creamy and spicy rice side dish. A fast and easy way to get your chile relleno fix, and use up leftover rice.  Makes a terrific potluck dish too!
