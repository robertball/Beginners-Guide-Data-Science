Title:
Pumpkin Banana Spice Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131555.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c oats
1.5 c rice puffs
5 T quinoa (red, uncooked)
1 c flaked cereal (gluten free)
1/2 c raw almonds (chopped)
2/3 c pumpkin puree
1 banana
5 medjool dates
1 egg white (beat until frothy)
1-2 T cinnamon
1 T pumpkin spice
1 tsp ginger
1 T flax seed
2 T coconut oil
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
mix oats, flakes, quinoa, rice puffs in large bowl
combine sliced banana and pitted dates in bowl, add water to cover, microwave until soft and almost caramelized, water absorbed
add pumpkin, banana and date mixture, coconut oil, spices, flax seed to blender and blend well
add egg white to blender and blend until smooth
mix wet and dry ingredients
bake 325 on parchment paper lined baking sheet for 45 min; stir every 15 min
