Title:
White Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup of whilte balsamic vinegar
1 tsp of dried Italian herb seasoning

Directions:
Mix all together in a jar.
Shaking vigorously.
The dressing can be use immediately or if time allows let it sit over night at room temperature for better infusion of flavor from the herbs.

Submitter: 
Sandy
Description: 
The white balsamic vineagar enhances the flavor of fresh garden vegetables in your salad.
