Title:
Spicy Rub Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123792.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, crumbled
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 pork chops, boneless
1 lime, cut into 8 wedges

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, combine olive oil, chili powder, paprika, garlic, salt, oregano, cumin and pepper. Rub in mixture evenly over the pork chops. Cover and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
2. Heat broiler. Broil pork chops for 5-6 minutes on each side, until browned and cooked through. Or you could also bake the chops at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until done. Serve immediately with 2 wedges of lime for each chop.
