Title:
Cocktail Sausages Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/758000.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup beer
2 lbs. (Little Smokies) cocktail sausages

Directions:
in a medium saucepan over low heat, mix the ketchup, dark brown sugar, and beer. Heat for approximately 10 minutes.
add cocktail sausages and keep warm in a crockpot.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Cocktail Sausages are easy to make and quick to disappear.
