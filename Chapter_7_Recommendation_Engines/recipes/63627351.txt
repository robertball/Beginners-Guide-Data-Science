Title:
Potato-Pea-Ginger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 2-inch piece ginger root, peeled and sliced
1 med onion, coarsely chopped
1 1-pound bag frozen baby peas, defrosted
1 med potato, peeled and chopped into 1/2 inch cubes(about 1.5cups)
1/3 cup slivered almonds, toasted
4 cups low-sodium veggie stock
3 drop pure almond extract
4 tsp creme fraiche (optional)

Directions:
1. Heat oil in large heavy-bottom pot over medium.  Add ginger and onion and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 mins or until soft and transparent.  Do not brown.
2.  Mix in peas, potatoes, almonds, and vegetable stock.  Bring to a boil and reduce heat to low; simmer for about 15 mins or until potatoes are easily crushed against the side of the pot with a spoon.
3.Transfer soup to a blender or food processor (in batches if necessary) and puree until smooth.  Pour through a fine mesh strainer and then return to pot and cook until warm.  Stir in almond extract.  Add a dollop of creme fraiche when ready to serve if desired.
