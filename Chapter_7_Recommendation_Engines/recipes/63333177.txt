Title:
Beef and Bean Taco Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 can seasoned black beans, drained (or about 2 cups, cooked)
1 medium onion, chopped
2 Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 cup tomato sauce
2 tsp apple cider vinegar
1 tsp brown sugar
1/2 cup beef broth
1 tsp kosher salt
1 Tbsp olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Saute onion in olive oil over medium heat until edges are translucent.  Stir in garlic, spices, salt and ground beef.  Cook until beef is no longer pink.  Stir in tomato sauce, broth, vinegar, and brown sugar.  Add beans and stir to coat.  Simmer until thickened, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
can be used for tacos, burritos, or nacho plates
