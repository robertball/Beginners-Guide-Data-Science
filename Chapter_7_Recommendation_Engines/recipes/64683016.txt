Title:
"Spears of Spring" - Simple & Flavorful Oven Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4948933.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh asparagus , trimmed
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic , thinly sliced with truffle slicer ( or minced just fine)
2 tbsp Parmesan , grated (extra for topping after roasted)
1 orange , thinly sliced
1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
2 tbsp freshly squeezed orange juice (approx. 1 orange)
1/2 tsp sea salt (for a little more kick, use Chipotle sea salt)
1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Line baking tray with parchment paper.
In large bowl mix and coat asparagus, olive oil, freshly squeezed orange juice, kosher salt, ground black pepper, sliced/minced garlic, and Parmesan cheese then line onto the baking sheet.
Top with orange slices an yellow bell pepper slices.
Sprinkle  with Parmesan cheese, Kosher salt, and garlic powder. (if want a little more kick use Chipotle sea salt blend)
Roast in preheated oven until fork tender on thickest part of stem, approximately 15 to 20 minutes.
Top with a little extra shredded Parmesan cheese and just enjoy.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Known as the spears of spring, their vivid color and savory flavor, are fantastic in meal itself.
Always look for pristine, firm buds that are not wilted and stalks of similar size.  Asparagus past its prime will get smelly fast....so use it or loose it... my motto!
