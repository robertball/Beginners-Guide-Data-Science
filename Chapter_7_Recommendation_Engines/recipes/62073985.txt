Title:
Cheesy Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Campbell's tomato soup
2 soup cans of milk
1/4 loaf Velveeta cheese
Saltine crackers
Butter or margarine

Directions:
Mix together soup and milk.  Cut the cheese into chunks and cook until cheese has melted.  Butter Saltine crackers and place in soup bowl.  Cover buttered crackers with soup.  Serve with grilled Velveeta cheese sandwiches.

Submitter: 
SDHARKER
Description: 
This cheesy tomato soup has been a family favorite for generations.  Serve this soup with grilled cheese sandwiches for a hearty meal on cold winter days.
