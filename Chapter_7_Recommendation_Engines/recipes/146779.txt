Title:
Sweet 'n' Sour Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/835213.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/4 cup cold water
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
8 cups shredded cabbage
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 tablespoon chopped pimento pepper

Directions:
In a jar with lid, combine SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, vinegar, water, salt, mustard seed and celery seed.  Cover tightly and shake vigorously until sugar is dissolved.  Chill dressing for several hours or overnight.
In a large bowl, toss together the cabbage, celery, green peppers, pimento and dressing until evenly coated.  Cover and chill until serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
This snappy cabbage slaw has a sweet, tangy dressing flavored with mustard and celery seeds.
