Title:
Keto Peanut Butter-Protein-Chocolate Chip Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1.5 scoops chocolate whey protein powder
1/2 scoop egg white protein powder
1 T peanut butter
1/4 c heavy cream (or nut milk of choice)
1 -2 T unsweetened vanilla almond milk
Lily's chocolate chips

Directions:
combine all with hand blender
cook on skillet over medium-low heat
add few chocolate chips to pancake in pan, flip and continue to cook until done
