Title:
Creamy Soda Freeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (14 ounce) cans EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1 (2 liter) bottle carbonated beverage, any flavor

Directions:
In electric ice cream freezer container, combine ingredients; mix well. Freeze according to manufacturer's instructions. Store leftovers in freezer.

Submitter: 
EAGLEBRAND®
Description: 
A simple recipe that makes enough shakes, frozen pops or ice cream to feed a crowd on a hot summer day.
