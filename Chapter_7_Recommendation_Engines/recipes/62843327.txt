Title:
Minty Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/676718.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 tsp. peppermint extract

Directions:
Over medium-low heat, melt butter in a medium saucepan and then stir in sugar, cocoa powder and milk. Stir and bring to a boil and boil for 30 seconds. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla and peppermint extract. Serve.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
I took my grandma's amazing hot fudge sauce recipe and gave it a peppermint twist! Best served over good-quality vanilla ice cream.
