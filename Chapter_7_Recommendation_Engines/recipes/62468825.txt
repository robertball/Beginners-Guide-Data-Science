Title:
Seasoned Tomato Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 # fully ripe tomatoes, coarsely chopped (8qts)
4 lg. red or gr. bell peppers, seeded and coarsely chopped
3 med. onions, coarsely chopped
4 med. carrots, coarsely chopped
2-3 cloves garlic, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Whirl in blender tomatoes, peppers, onions, carrots and garlic, small amount at a time, until smooth.
Press through a wire strainer and discard pulp.
In 12 qt. kettle, bring puree to boiling over med-high heat. Boil gently, uncovered, until thick enough to mound on a spoon (about 5 hrs). As mix thickens, stir often, reduce heat and partially cover.
Chill, pour into freezer containers, cover and freeze or can.
To can, prepare 5 pint jars. Fill jars to within 1/4 in. of rim, process for 30 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
When you have a lot of tomatoes that you need to get rid of, this needs about 5 hours of cooking time but worth every minute of it. Can freeze or can.
