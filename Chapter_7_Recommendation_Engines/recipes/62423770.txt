Title:
Lime-Chili Fried HotDogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/446540.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Hotdogs - Diced into small peices
2 Chipotle Chilies in Adobo Sauce & 2 Tablespoons Adobo Sauce
Juice of 1 lime
2 Tablespoon Oil for frying

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.  Once warm add hotdogs.  Fry hotdogs until golden brown and beginning to crisp, stirring frequently. Place chilies and sauce and blend until smooth.
Once hotdogs are brown drain oil. Add chilie sauce and juice of lime.  Cook about 2 more minutes and remove from heat.
Serve with rice and tortillas

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a recipe my hispanic mother in law taught me.  Its one of my husbands favorite and very quick to throw together.  Serve over mexican rice with tortillas.
