Title:
Potato and Salmon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/868136.jpg
Ingredients:
Salad:
3 lbs of Yellow Fingerling Potatoes
1 1/2 lbs Salmon Fillets
1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
8 Hard Boiled Eggs
Dressing:
1 cup Miracle Whip Light
1/2 cup Light Mayo
2 TBS Sugar
2 TBS Apple Cider Vinegar
1 tsp Prepared Honey Mustard

Directions:
1) Line a baking sheet with foil and spray with oil. Arrange the salmon fillets on the pan, seasoning with some salt and pepper. Bake at 375F for 10-12 minutes or until the fish is flaky. Remove from the oven and let cool.
2) While the fish is baking, wash the potatoes and cut them into 1 inch cubes. Place in a pot and cover with water. Add salt to flavor the water. Boil the potatoes until fork tender and remove from the heat. Drain the water and set the potatoes aside to cool.
3) Once the salmon and potatoes have cooled,transfer them to a large mixing bowl. Add the celery, onion and 6 of the hard boiled eggs, chopped, to the bowl.
4) Mix together the Miracle Whip, Mayo, Sugar, Vinegar and Mustard.
5) Gently mix the dressing into the potato and salmon salad until all of the pieces are covered.
6) Transfer the salad to a serving bowl. Cut the remaining two Hard Boiled Eggs into wedges and use to decorate the top of the potato salad.
7) Sprinkle with some paprika if desired.
