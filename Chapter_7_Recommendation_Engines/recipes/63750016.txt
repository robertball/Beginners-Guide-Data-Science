Title:
Crockpot Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb bacon
1/4 cup diced onion
4 cans pork and beans
1 can kidney beans
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon liquid smoke
3 tablespoons vinegar
3/4 cup ketchup

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onions until beef is brown.
Cut up bacon and fry until done.
Mix everything together in the crockpot and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Very easy and tasty baked beans. Made these for tailgating for football games all the time and were a big hit!!
