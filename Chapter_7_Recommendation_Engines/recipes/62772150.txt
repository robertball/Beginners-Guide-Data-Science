Title:
Grilled Bacon Jalapeno Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 fresh jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
1 (8 ounce) package fat free cream cheese
1 packet ranch dressing seasoning
12 slices turkey bacon

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
Spread cream cheese to fill jalapeno halves. Wrap with bacon. Secure with a toothpick.
Place on the grill, and cook until bacon is crispy.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
Jalapenos are stuffed with cream cheese, wrapped with bacon, and barbequed on the grill.Modified from: Grilled Bacon Jalapeno Wraps
