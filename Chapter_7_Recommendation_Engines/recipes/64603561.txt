Title:
Slow Cooker Thai Curried Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean stew beef
1/8 tsp salt
2 cups onion (finally chopped)
4 cloves minced garlic
3/4 cup beef broth
1 Tbs brown sugar
3 Tbs red curry paste
2 Tbs Peanut Oil
2 Tbs lime juice
1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
2 jalapeño pepper (seeded and minced)
2 Cups baby spinach
1/2 Cup fresh basil leaves (optional)
4 Cups Jasmine rice (prep before serving)

Directions:
Brown beef on all sides in a skillet over medium high heat
Drain and place beef in a 4-quart slow cooker and sprinkle with salt
Return skillet to medium high heat and sauté onion and garlic until tender (5 min)
Add onion/garlic mixture, brown sugar, red curry paste, peanut oil, lime juice, coconut milk, jalapeño pepper, and beef broth over the beef (refrigerate until ready to start the crockpot)
Cover and cook on LOW for six (6) to ten (10) hours
Prepare rice according to package directions
Stir in spinach and cook for fifteen (15) minutes or until spinach begins to wilt
Serve beef mixture over rice and sprinkle with basil leaves (optional)

Submitter: 
Sapper26
Description: 
Awesome and flexible crockpot Thai dish that is perfect for the busy family, couple or single person. Prep the night before or the morning of. Cook for 6, 8 or ten hours. This recipe is as tasty as it is forgiving.
