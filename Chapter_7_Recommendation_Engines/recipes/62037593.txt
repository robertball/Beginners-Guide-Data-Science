Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 ounces pork and beans
1 each large onion -- chopped fine
3/4 cup catsup
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 Tablespoons prepared mustard
1 teaspoon white vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 pound bacon -- unprepared

Directions:
-Combine all ingredients (except the bacon) in a 2 quart casserole dish.
-Top beans with strips of bacon in a crisscross pattern.
-Cover with aluminum foil.
-Bake in preheated 350º oven for 1½ hours. Remove foil for last 30 minutes of baking.
