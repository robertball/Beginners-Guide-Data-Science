Title:
Creole Shrimp and Hearts of Palm Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad
1 lb 40-50 count Shrimp
1 can hearts of palm
1/4 cup capers
Green onion tops
Romaine Lettuce (can be mixed with iceberg or other mix)
Dressing
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup creole mustard
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/tsp chopped parsley
1/2 tbsp lemon juice
Dash tabasco
salt to tase

Directions:
Mix Creole mayonnaise. Cook shrimp in salted and peppered (or shrimp boil) water until done. Cool completely. Cut hearts of palm in six pieces. Mix hearts of palm, shrimp, capers and Creole mayonnaise, add onion tops. Place mixture in refrigerator for at least one hour. Serve on bed of lettuce with lemon garnish.
