Title:
Moroccan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole rotisserie chicken or use your own
3/4-1 c olive oil
1/4 c white wine
1/4-1/2 c red wine
1/8-1/4 c balsamic
1 c olives
1/4 or more olive brine
5-10 whole garlic cloves, peeled, smashed
1-2 tsp cumin
1 tsp corriander
1-2 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp cardamom
1/2 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp turmeric
2 tsp salt
1 lemon, 1/2 juiced and then 1/2 sliced
zest of 1 lemon

Directions:
preheat oven 425
olive oil in baking dish
cut up chicken into pieces and put in baking dish with any juices
combine dry spices
rub 1/2 of dry spice onto chicken
zest the lemon, then cut in half; use 1/2 for juice and thinly slice rest
blend olive oil, wine, balsamic, lemon juice, olive brine and half of spices in immersion blender
pour mixture over chicken
put lemon slices in chicken
sprinkle lemon zest over chicken
bake 45 min at 425
serve with parsley
