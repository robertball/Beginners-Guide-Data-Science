Title:
Fake Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
Pinch of salt
Pinch of pepper
Pinch of dried basil
Pinch of chili powder
1 whole egg
1 tablespoon water
3 cups crushed Cornflakes
6 boneless, skinless chicken tenderloins

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Coat a cookie sheet with cooking spray.
2. Mix flour, chili powder,salt, pepper, and basil together in a bowl.
3. Mix egg and water together in another bowl.
4. Pour crushed cereal into a third bowl.
5. Dip each tenderloin in flour mixture first, shake off extra. Dip in egg and then in crushed cereal.
6. Place on cookie sheet.
7. Cook for 10-12 minutes on each side.

Submitter: 
Tess
Description: 
This chicken is dipped in crushed cereal, then baked. A delicious low-fat alternative to  fried chicken with a great crunch!
