Title:
Mushroom Barley Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 cups beef broth
1 lb beef stew meat, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
16 oz mushrooms, sliced
1 onion, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1/2 cup pearl barley
1/2 oz dried or 6 oz fresh porcini mushrooms
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in your slow cooker.  Cook on low 4-6 hours.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
My family describes this soup as having a more flavor - that is, they always ask for more!
