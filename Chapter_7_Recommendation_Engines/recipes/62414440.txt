Title:
Mom's Yorkshire Puddings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
125ml of milk
2 tbsp flour (heaped)
1 pinch of salt
2 eggs
2 tbsp water
9 tsp canola oil (for the pan, if you have fat drippings use that instead)

Directions:
Mix ingredients together.  A tupperware quick shake is recommended.
For better results, place quick shake in the fridge for a minimum of an hour.
Please roughly one tsp of canola oil into each cup in the pan.  Please pan into hot oven (525F or 275C) for 10-15 minutes to heat the oil (or fat)
Pour the mixture into the hot pan.
Low broil for approximately 5 minutes, observe through window as to not burn
Cook at 390F/200C for 15-20 minutes
