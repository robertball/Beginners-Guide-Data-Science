Title:
Italian Oven Cheese Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb zucchini, diced
2 med. onions, chopped
1 15-oz can garbanzo beans
1 15-oz can diced tomatoes
1/4 cup butter
2 tsp salt
1 tsp basil
2 tsp garlic powder
1 cup grated Parmesan or Romano cheese
1 cup grated Jack Cheese
1 cup milk or cream

Directions:
In a large oven proof pot or casserole (3 qt) combine all ingredients except cheeses and milk.  Cover and bake at 400F for one hour, stirring once halfway through.  Next, stir in cheeses and milk and bake an additional 10 minutes.  Cool slightly and serve.
