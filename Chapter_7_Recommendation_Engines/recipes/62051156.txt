Title:
Fabulous Fruit and Feta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 10 oz. pkg.  mixed salad greens
1 - 11 oz. can mandarin orange segments, drained
1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts, toasted
4 oz. crumbled Feta cheese or Blue Cheese
3/4 cup Raspberry Vinaigrette Dressing
Fresh cracked pepper

Directions:
Toss greens, orange segments, onion, walnuts and cheese in large bowl.  Add dressing: toss to coat. Leave fresh cracked pepper out on table for anyone who wants to add it on their salad.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Easy, great light salad that will go with any dish.
