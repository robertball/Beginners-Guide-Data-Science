Title:
Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix:
3 eggs
1 1/4 cups Wesson oil
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. nutmeg
Add:
3 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. soda
2 tsp. vanilla
1 cup nuts (optional)
3 cups chopped apples
Topping:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup milk
3/4 stick margarine (or butter)

Directions:
Mix and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Topping:
Mix and cook 2 1/2 minutes.  Pour over hot cake.

Submitter: 
Exhibits
Description: 
This is a recipe that was passed on to me through my son's mother-in-law, Glenda Stokes.  Had to add this one because it's best apple cake ever.
