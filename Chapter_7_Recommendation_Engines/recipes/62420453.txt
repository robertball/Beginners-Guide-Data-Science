Title:
Lemon Cream Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444255.jpg
Ingredients:
Cupcake Dough
3/4 cup butter
1 1/4 cups sugar
6 large eggs
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2 1/4 cups flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon fresh grated lemon zest
Cream Filling
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 cups cream cheese, softened
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon lemon zest

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream together butter and sugar in blender until fluffy.
Add eggs and lemon juice until well combined.
Add flour, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest.
Pipe into prepared cupcake pan.To about 1/2 full.
Bake for 15 min. Remove form oven
Cream together cream cheese, sugar, lemon juice and zest.
Pipe approx. 1 Tablespoon of mix into cupcakes.
Add more cupcake dough. Enough to just cover cream filling. (total fill should equal 3/4 of cupcake)
Place back into oven for 15 min or until light golden brown color on top of cupcakes.
