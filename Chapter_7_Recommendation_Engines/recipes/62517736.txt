Title:
Dinner in a Pumpkin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium sugar pumpkin
1  pound ground beef (80/20)
1 lb. italian sausage skin off(hot)
1 onion, chopped
3 clove garlic, minced
1tsp. cinnamon
4 slices colby cheese
4 slices montaray jack cheese
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
4C marinara sauce
1 small cabbage shredded
1 bag of instant brown rice uncooked(Uncle Ben's)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Wash pumpkin, cut off top, scrape out seeds and discard.
Place hamburgerand sausage in a large, deep skillet. Crumble and cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain fat, add onion and garlic; saute slightly.
Add salt, pepper, maranara sauce and rice to meat; mix thoroughly.
Layer inside of pumpkin with 1/3 of cabbage, sausage and beef and rice mixture. Repeat layers,then plac slices of both cheese on top and press down hard replace lid and bake for 4 hours.If pumpkin is large bake less if pumpkin is smaller.

Submitter: 
Josie
Description: 
Stuff and bake a whole pumpkin with layers of browned ground beef and italian sausage rice and cabbage and cheese in this  meal which serves up to 15 people or more. Its great for a buffet.Its always a big hit.Modified from: Dinner in a Pumpkin I
