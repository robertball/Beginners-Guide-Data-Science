Title:
Geeta Auntie's Potato (and Vegetable) Baji Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 4 Medium sized potatoes, peeled and diced
- 1 Medium sized red onion, minced
- 1 Large tomato, sliced and diced into small pieces
- 1 teaspoon of garam masala ( add more if needed)
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 /4 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon fresh chopped cilantro
- 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Directions:
1) Boil potatoes in salt water. Drain and set aside.
2) In a skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook for 2-3 minutes while stirring frequently. Add tomato once onion has softened. Once tomatoes are tender, add boiled potatoes along with spices. Mix together well and simmer for 5 minutes stirring frequently.
3) Add cilantro a minute before removing from heat, then serve.
Serve as a main dish with rice or nan.
