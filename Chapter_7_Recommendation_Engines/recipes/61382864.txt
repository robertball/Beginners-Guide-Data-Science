Title:
Brown Sugar Smokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (16 ounce) package little smokie sausages
3 pound bacon
3 cups brown sugar, or to taste
SAUCE:
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup red chili sauce
1 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Cut each piece of bacon in half and dredge in brown sugar. Wrap each strip around a little sausage. Place them in a large baking dish. Cover and let sit overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line roasting pan with foil. Spray grates with pam. Place smokies on grates and bake 45 minutes, turning once.
Combine sauce ingredients and let meld in frig overnight. Warm in microwave before serving.

Submitter: 
Kiersten Butler Stahl
Description: 
Bacon-wrapped yummies! You can make these little smokies on skewers or toothpick them to secure.Modified from: Brown Sugar Smokies
