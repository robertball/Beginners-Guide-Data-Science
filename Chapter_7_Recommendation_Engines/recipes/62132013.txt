Title:
Old-Time Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 small head of iceberg lettuce, torn into bite sized pieces
1/2 of a bunch of leaf lettuce, endive, red leaf, Romaine, etc., torn into bite sized pieces
6 hard cooked eggs, cooled, peeled and sliced
6 slices of bacon, fried and crumbled
1/2 onion, sliced thin and sperate into rings
1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
2 or 3 dashes of paprika
Vinegar-Oil dressing
1/4 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
2 Tablespoons Vinegar
1/2 or 1 Tablespoon of parsley
1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Directions:
In s medium sized bowl, put your pieces of lettuce in there.  Top that with your sliced eggs, bacon and onions.
Sprinkle with your sharp cheddar cheese.  Mix it with your clean hands and sprinkle with paprika.
Pour vinegar-oil dressing over this salad and toss again.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
This was in my Grandmother's handwriting so it must be very old and original.
