Title:
Caribbean Sweet Potato and Bean Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sweet potatoes (approx 1 lb), peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes
2 cups cut green beans
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
14 oz vegetable broth
1 onion, sliced
2 tsp Caribbean jerk seasoning
1/2 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/3 cup slivered almonds, toasted

Directions:
Combine all ingredients but almonds in a slow cooker.  Cook on low for 5 to 6 hours, or until vegetables are tender.  Serve garnished with almonds.
