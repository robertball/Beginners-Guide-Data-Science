Title:
Dill Pickle Party Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1192041.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups finely hand-chopped dill pickles (such as Claussen)
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup finely sliced green onions
2 tablespoons dill pickle juice
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Press chopped dill pickles into cream cheese using a spoon and stir to mix in. Add green onions and dill pickle juice; stir to combine. Add dried dill and black pepper and blend well with a spoon. Chill for at least 1 hour. Stir again just before serving.

Submitter: 
Jean K.
Description: 
If you love dill and dill pickles, you will love this dip! Easy to make and too easy to eat! Serve with pretzels, crinkle-cut potato chips, or fresh veggies. Enjoy!
