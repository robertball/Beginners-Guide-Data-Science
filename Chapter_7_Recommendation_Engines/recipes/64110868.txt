Title:
French Onion Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 med. onions
3 T. butter
2 T. all-purpose flour
dash of pepper
3/4 C. beef bouillon
1/4 C. dry sherry
1 1/2 C. plain croutons
2 T. butter
1/2 C. shredded Swiss cheese
3 T. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook onions in 3 T. of butter until tender. Blend flour & pepper. Add bouillon and sherry; cook and stir until thickened & bubbly. Turn into 1 quart casserole. Toss croutons with 2 tablespoons melted butter; spoon atop onion mixture. Sprinkle with Parmesan & Swiss cheeses. Place under broiler just until cheeses melt. Serve immediately.
