Title:
Smoked Gouda Beer Cheese v 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Smoked Gouda cheese, chopped
4 oz cream cheese
2 Tbsp. brown spicy mustard
1 Tbsp. finely chopped garlic
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup West Sixth Half-Bite beer

Directions:
Pulse all the ingredients except the beer in a food processor until well-mixed.  Scrape down the sides with a rubber spatula.  Turn the processor on and slowly add the beer until the mixture is a smooth puree.  Transfer mixture to a bowl and chill.  For a warm cheese dip, pour into shallow baking dish and bake 15-20 minutes in a 400 F oven.  Serve with pretzel chips, soft pretzels, or bread sticks.
