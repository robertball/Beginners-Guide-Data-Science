Title:
Hot Cheesy Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS. butter
!.4 Cup chopped onion
2 garlic cloves minced (1 tsp.)
1 TBS all-purpose flour
1-3/4 cups half and half
2 packages (9oz each) frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
1 can (8oz) sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 package (1oz.) dry vegetable soup mix
1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is crisp-tender and garlic is fragrant, 2-3 minutes.
Stir in flour; cook and stir an additional minute. Add half-and-half and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture boils and thickens, about 2 minutes.
Remove saucepan from heat.  Stir in chopped spinach, water chestnuts, cheddar and veggie soup mix.  Spoon dip into a 1-quart casserole or gratin dish.  Sprinkle with parmesan.
Bake dip until it is melted, 10-15 minutes.  Serve warm.
