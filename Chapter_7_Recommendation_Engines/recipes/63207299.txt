Title:
Best Peanut Sauce lp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon brown sugar
2 drops hot pepper sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup water

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together peanut butter, soy sauce, sugar, hot pepper sauce and garlic until well mixed. Gradually stir in water until texture is smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
Linda Purchase
Description: 
This spicy peanut sauce can be whipped up in an instant and makes a great addition to many Chinese and Thai recipes.Modified from: Best Peanut Sauce
