Title:
Michelle's Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Velveta Cheese, cubed
8 oz cheddar cheese, cubed or grated
8 oz Cream Cheese
8 oz Sour Cream
8 oz Cottage Cheese
8 oz Rotello diced tomatoes
1 lb Italian Sausage
1 lb Hamburger
1 med onion, diced
3 Cloves garlic, minced
11 oz can Diced Jalapeno's
salt and pepper

Directions:
Brown meat with onions, salt pepper and garlic and drain.  (Remove the Italian sausage from casing before cooking)
Put meat in large crock pot on high, add the rest of the ingredients.  Cook on high for 1 - 1 1/2 hours.  Stir occasionaly.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This is delicious and makes a lot of dip.  Fattening but yummy.
