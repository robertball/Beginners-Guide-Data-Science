Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs stew meat or boneless beef chuck into 1 inch pieces
1/4 cup A-1 Origianl Steak Sauce
1 Med sliced onion
14.5 oz undrained diced tomatoes
2 tbsp flour
1/4 cup of water

Directions:
Place into crock pot
meat
A-1 Sauce
onion
tomatoes
cook on Low 9-10 hours or High for 4-5 hours
mix flour with water stir into meat,cover and cook until sauce is thicken, stir occasionally
Serve with noodles or potatoes and a green veggie

Submitter: 
Lucille
Description: 
This is super for the working or busy day Mom. Simple,easy,kid and husband friendly. Great with noodles or mashed potatoes and green veggie
