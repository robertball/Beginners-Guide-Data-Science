Title:
Creamy Grilled Fish Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter
1/2 a large onion, finely chopped
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
4 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp Mrs. Dash Original seasoning
1 tsp black pepper
2 cups skim milk
1/2 cup grated Romano (or parmesan) cheese
*Choose 2 or more of the following:
- 1 cup frozen spinach thawed
- 1 cup frozen broccoli thawed and diced
- 1 cup frozen green peas, thawed and drained
- 1/2 cup canned mushrooms, drained
*Fish - choose 1 or both
- 10 ounces smoked salmon, chopped
- 12 ounces well seasoned grilled fish fillets, chopped
1 (16 ounce) package pasta (linguine, shells, rotini)
1 TBS Extra Virgin Olive Oil (EVOO)
1 to 2 tsp dried or fresh Basil

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain, drizzle with EVOO and Basil. Stir to distribute evenly.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onion in butter until tender. Add garlic. Whisk flour into the butter, garlic, and onions. continue whisking for two minutes. Gradually whisk in milk. Heat to just below boiling point stirring constantly, and then gradually stir in cheese until the sauce is smooth. Stir in vegetables, and cook over low heat until vegetables are heated through(about 5 minutes or so)
Toss in smoked salmon/fish, and cook an additional 2 minutes. Serve over pasta. Garnish with fresh grated Parmesan and fresh ground black pepper.
