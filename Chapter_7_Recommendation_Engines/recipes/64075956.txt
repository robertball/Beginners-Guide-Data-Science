Title:
Oma's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb bag red potatoes
6 hard boiled eggs
1 cup of mayo
1-2 TBSP prepared mustard
1 TBSP sweet relish
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook potatoes whole until fork tender.
Once cooled cut into bit size pieces.
Mince boiled eggs
Mix mayo, mustard, relish, salt and pepper to taste.
Stir in eggs and potatoes.
Chill until served.
