Title:
Easy Parmesan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986167.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hellmann'sÂ® Real Mayonnaise
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4 (5 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
4 teaspoons Italian-seasoned dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Combine Hellmann's(R) Real Mayonnaise with cheese in medium bowl. Arrange chicken on baking sheet. Evenly top with mayonnaise mixture, then sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake 20 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
