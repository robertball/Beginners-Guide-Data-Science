Title:
easy Green Tea cup cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/493303.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pack of white cake mix
1 1/3 cup water
1/3 cup banana cream pie youger( light )
3 eggs
3 tbs green tea powder (can get it in Asain store )
icying
1(8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 cup confectioners suger
1 tsp butter
1 tsp Almon ex
1/3 cup whip cream

Directions:
1.preheat over according the directions on package.
put cup cake on the side wait till it chill for the icying.
2. icying mix put all the icying mix together till smoth and keep it cold in refrigerate for 20 mins
then you can put the icying on the cold cup cake!
