Title:
Basic, Fluffy Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 pint cottage cheese
1 pound shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Mix all ingredients in large bowl
3. Pour into greased 9X13 pan
4. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes or cooked through.
*Variations- add any cooked breakfast meats, chopped veggies, green chilies, etc. to mixture before baking. Be creative.

Submitter: 
therapflu
Description: 
Simple egg casserole that can be made the night before, refrigerated, and popped into the oven in the morning.
