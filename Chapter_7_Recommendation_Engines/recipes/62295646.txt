Title:
Courgettes (Zucchini) in Yoghurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- Courgettes (Zucchini), as many as you want to make.
- Groundnut oil; 1 Tablespoon or more
- Brown or white Mustard Seeds
- Thick Yoghurt (plain)
- Paprika

Directions:
Coarsely grate the courgette(s).
Squeeze dry as much as possible.
In a wok, chef's pan or skillet, add the oil and heat.
Throw in the courgettes and a few mustard seeds.
Stir fry until tender, 4 or 5 minutes.
Stir in a few spoonsful of thick yoghurt and a sprinkling of Paprika.
Job done.
