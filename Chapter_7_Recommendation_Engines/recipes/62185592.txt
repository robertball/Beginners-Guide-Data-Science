Title:
Creamy Potato, Leek, and Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/294263.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBSP olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 leek, white part only, cleaned and finely chopped
2 medium russet potatoes, peeled and diced
3 cups water
2 chicken bullion cubes
1 cup chopped fresh spinach leaves, loosely packed
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
In a heavy 3 qt sauce pan over medium heat, cook garlic and leeks until softened, about 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Add potatoes and  continue cooking about 5 minutes more, continuing to stir frequently.
Add water and bullion cubes. Increase heat to medium high and bring to a boil. Cook 10 minutes. Add spinach and stir to blend. Cook 5 minutes longer or until potatoes are softened. Remove pan from heat and allow to cool 5 minutes.
Pour contents into blender and puree 30 second or until smooth.
Return puree to pan and add cream, stirring to blend. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Heat until hot over medium low heat. Do not allow soup to boil. Serve hot with crusty bread, if desired.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
This mildly flavored, creamy soup really hits the spot on a chilly day. Serve with crusty bread and a salad for a complete meal.
