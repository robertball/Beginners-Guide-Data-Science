Title:
Honey Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 salmon filets
2 tbsp honey
1 tbsp teriaki sauce
garlic salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2. Place salmon filets on a foil covered baking dish.
3. Drizzle honey over each filet equally, as well as teriaki sauce.
4. Season with garlic salt and pepper.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 15 mins or until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
