Title:
Spicy Italian SausageTortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb spicy Italian sausage
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves minced garlic
5 cups vegetable or chicken broth
3/4 cup red wine
1/2 cup water
4 tomatoes, peeled and seeded
8 oz can tomato sauce
1 cup thinly sliced carrots
1 tablespoon fresh basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 cup sliced zucchini
8 oz fresh cheese tortellini
2 tablespoons fresh parsley

Directions:
In a large stock pot, brown the sausage, remove and drain. Add olive oil to the pot and saute onion until translucent. Add garlic and saute for about one minute more being careful not to let it burn.
Add broth, wine, water, tomatoes, tomato sauce, carrots, basil, oregano, and sausage. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
Skim fat from soup. Stir in zucchini and parsley, cover and simmer for 20 minutes. Add tortellini and simmer for 8 - 10 more minutes.
Serve with warm french bread and fresh Parmesan cheese.
