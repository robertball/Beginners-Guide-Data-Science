Title:
Cucumber and onion salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 medium cucumbers,peeled and sliced thin
1/2 small red onion, sliced thin
2/3C splenda
2/3C white vinegar
1/8tsp black pepper

Directions:
Place cucumbers adn onions in glass bowl. combine remaining ingredients, stir well. Pour over cucumbers and onions. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally
