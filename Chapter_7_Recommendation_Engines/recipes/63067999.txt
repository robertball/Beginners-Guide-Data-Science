Title:
Creamy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 leeks, trimmed, washed,  halved and coarsely cut
1 stalk of celery,  coarsely cut
3 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley or 1 tbsp. dried
3 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. flour
8 peeled potatoes, coarsely diced
4 carrots, coarsely diced
3 cans chicken stock
1/2 cup dry white wine
4 cups water
1 bay leaf
pepper to taste
pinch of thyme, dried
a few cups of fresh veggies, chopped ( cauliflower, lima beans,peas,zucchini, whatever you have on hand.)
1 1/2 cups light cream

Directions:
In a stock pot. Saute leeks, celery and parsley in butter for 5 mins. Add flour and mix well. Add potatoes and carrots and coat with the flour. Add wine, stock, water, bay leaf, thyme and pepper.  Simmer partially covered for about 45 mins. When veggies are soft crush them with a fork for a chunky soup. Remove bay leaf. Add one or several veggies and simmer another 15 mins. Just before serving stir in cream a little at a time.
