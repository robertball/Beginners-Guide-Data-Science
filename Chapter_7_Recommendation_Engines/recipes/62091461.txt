Title:
Curry Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
1 tablespoon dried onions
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon curry powder
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Refrigerate for at least four hours so that onions can hydrate and flavors can meld.
Serve with any kind of sliced, raw vegetables you like.

Submitter: 
DAVISAM1
Description: 
This curry dip is awesome for veggies and is standard fare at all my parties.  Just make it the day before or early in the day so that the flavors have time to meld.
