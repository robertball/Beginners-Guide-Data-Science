Title:
Fresh Steamed Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup(s) beans, green

Directions:
Rinse beans and steam over boiling water until they've reached the desired texture. Alternatively, you may steam them by placing them in a covered, microwave-safe dish with about 1 tablespoon of water and heating to desired texture. Be careful of the steam when removing the cover.
