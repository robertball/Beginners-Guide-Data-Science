Title:
Reuben Quickies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  15 oz. can sauerkraut, rinsed and drained
1 can corned beef
1/2 c. thousand island dressing
2 c. shredded swiss cheese
12 rye or pumpernickle buns

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl,mix all the ingredients together (except the buns).  Fill each bun with the mixture and wrap indiviual buns in foil.  Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 20 to 30 minutes until cheese is completely melted.

Submitter: 
Amy Brolsma
Description: 
A fast conveinent way to have Reubens. I like to make up a batch or two and freeze for a quick lunch or snack.
