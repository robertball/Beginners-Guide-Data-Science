Title:
Sauerbraten Klopse (Sauerbraten Meatballs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS:
1/2 pound lean ground beef
1/2 pound lean ground pork
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1/8 teaspoon ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons vegetable oil (for frying)
GRAVY:
1 cup water
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1 bay leaf
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together ground meats, milk, and bread crumbs.
Season with cloves, allspice, salt, and pepper. Shape into 1 inch balls.
Heat oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Cook the meatballs until evenly brown; drain excess fat.  Place meatballs in crockpot.
MAKE THE GRAVY:
In a small bowl combine water, vinegar, brown sugar, ginger, and bay leaf.
Bring to a boil.
Mix 2 T. flour and 2T. water in a small jar w/ lid.  Shake to mix well.
Pour onto brown sugar and vinegar mixture, stirring constantly until desired thickness is reached.  Remove bay leaf and pour gravy over meatballs in crockpot.
Refrigerate and reheat thoroughly as party time approaches.
