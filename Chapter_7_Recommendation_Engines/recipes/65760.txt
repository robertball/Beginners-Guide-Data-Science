Title:
Warm Kalamata Goat Cheese Toasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 pita bread rounds, cut into wedges and toasted
1/2 cup roasted red bell pepper, chopped
1/3 cup Lindsay® Kalamata Pitted Olives, halved
1/3 cup goat cheese
3 tablespoons tarragon, chopped
2 tablespoons almonds, sliced

Directions:
Preheat broiler to medium-high.
Arrange pita wedges on a baking sheet. Top each with bell peppers, olives, goat cheese, tarragon and almonds. Place under a broiler for 30 to 90 seconds until nuts are lightly toasted and cheese is warm.

Submitter: 
Lindsay® Olives
Description: 
Nuts and cheese on pita toast points, perked up with sweet roasted red bell pepper and Lindsay® Kalamata Olives for a simple, yet delicious, quick snack.
