Title:
Tom's Turkey Injection Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Soy Sauce
1/2 cup Worcestershire Sauce
2 tablespoons Garlic Powder
1 tablespoon Ginger
1 tablespoon Chili Powder
3-4 splashes Tabasco

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a jar with a lid
Seal jar
Shake jar
Inject into turkey (see online instructions for proper injection, this is just the recipe)

Submitter: 
Tom
Description: 
Savory turkey injection with a great mixture of flavor and spice.
