Title:
Grilled Chicken w/ Peach Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups peach preserves
1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
Salt and fresh ground pepper
6-8 boneless chicken breasts
4 ripe peaches, halved and pitted

Directions:
Prepare Peach Sauce by combining the peach preserves, chopped garlic, olive oil, soy sauce and Dijon mustard in a medium bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste. Reserve 1/2 cup of sauce.
Preheat grill. Brush the chicken with olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste. Place chicken on grill and cook for 6 to 7 minutes, until golden brown. Turn over and continue cooking for 5 to 6 minutes. Brush both sides with the peach sauce and continue cooking an additional 4 to 5 minutes.
Place peach halves, cut side down on the grill and grill for 2 minutes. Turn over and brush with the reserved 1/2 cup of peach sauce, grill for 3 to 4 more minutes until peaches are soft.
