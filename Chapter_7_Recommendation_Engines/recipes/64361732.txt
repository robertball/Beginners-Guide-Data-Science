Title:
Cocoa Fudge Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 cup butter or margin = 2 sticks butter
* 2 cups sugar
* 1 1/4 cups white lily plain all-purpose flour (or self rising and omit the baking soda)
* 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
* 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 4 eggs
* 2 teaspoons vanilla
* 1/3 cup vegetable oil
* 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2) Grease a 15x9 in. pan (set aside).
3) In a saucepan melt butter or margarine. Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt. Add melted butter and oil and stir until smooth. Stir in 4 eggs and vanilla just until smooth. Stir in nuts. Pour batter into prepared dish. Bake in 350 degree oven for 35-40 minutes or until brownies start to pull away from edges of dish. Cool on wire rack
