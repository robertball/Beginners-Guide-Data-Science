Title:
Ground Flax Egg Substitute- Perfect for Baking Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon ground flax seed per egg replaced
3 Tablespoons very warm water per egg replaced

Directions:
Combine gound flax and warm water in a bowl and whisk about 30 seconds to combine.  Let this sit for 2 minutes.
Whisk again for about 30 seconds and let sit for another 2 minutes.
Add to recipe as it calls for eggs.
Store ground flax seed in a sealed container in the refrigerator or freezer

Submitter: 
Tricia Vaccaro-Coburn
Description: 
Wonderful substitute for eggs, especially in cookies, brownies, muffins and breads. It works every time.
