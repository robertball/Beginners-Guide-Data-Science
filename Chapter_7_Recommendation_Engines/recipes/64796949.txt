Title:
Simple Eggs and Soldiers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 fresh medium eggs, room temperature
1 slice white bread
1/2 tsp butter, room temperature
Salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover with one inch of cold water. Uncovered, bring to a boil over medium-high heat. This may take up to 5 minutes.
When the water begins to boil, turn off the heat, cover the saucepan, and let it stand for 2 minutes.
Note. Smaller or larger eggs will need time adjustments by reducing or increasing cooking time by 25%.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Simple yet comforting soft boiled eggs and buttered toast sticks. Use them to dip into the runny yolks to make this perfectly simple breakfast. Serve with a hot cup of tea.
