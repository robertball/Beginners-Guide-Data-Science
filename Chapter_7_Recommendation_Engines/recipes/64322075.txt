Title:
Raspberry French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 teaspoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
2 large egg(s)
1/4 cup(s) milk, fat-free
1 1/2 cup(s) raspberries
6 ounce(s) cottage cheese, low-fat, low sodium
4 slice(s) bread, whole-wheat

Directions:
In a large shallow bowl, beat together the egg and milk until blended.
Set aside half the raspberries. Mash the remaining berries with the cottage cheese.
Spread the raspberry-cheese mixture between the two slices of bread.
