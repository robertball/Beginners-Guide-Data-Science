Title:
Chicken and Ravioli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken and Ravioli Soup
Ingredients
1 ½ bone-in, skinless chicken breasts (3 half breasts)
1 lemon, juiced
salt
fresh cracked pepper
1 tbs. dried oregano
3 tbs. Olive oil
1 48 oz. can reduced-sodium, low-fat chicken broth
2 cups water
1 cup white wine
1 potherb, plus any additional fresh herbs
4 stalks celery, half cut into big chunks and half chopped into small pieces
4 carrots, half cut into big chunks and half chopped into small pieces
1 1/2 onions, one cut into big chunks and ½ cut chopped into small pieces
1 1 lb. Bag frozen spinach and cheese raviolis
Grated Locatelli Pecorino Romano cheese

Directions:
Place chicken breasts on a baking sheet.
Sprinkle with lemon juice, salt, pepper, oregano, and olive oil.
Bake in a 350-degree oven for 45 minutes.
Remove from oven and place chicken in a stockpot with broth, water, wine, potherb, and chunks of celery, carrots, and onions.
Cover and simmer for 2 hours.
Drain broth. Discard herbs and vegetables.
Shred chicken meat, discarding bones while allowing the broth to cool so you can fat will solidify for easy removal. You can let the chicken and broth sit overnight in the refrigerator.
After skimming the fat from the broth, add in the shredded chicken meat and chopped celery, carrots, and onions. Bring to a simmer.
Add frozen raviolis, and simmer as per package instructions (usually 12 minutes).
Sprinkle servings with grated cheese.
