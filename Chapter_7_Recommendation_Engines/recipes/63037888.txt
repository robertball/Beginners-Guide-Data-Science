Title:
Homemade Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 peck tomatoes(grocery bag full) 20 lbs
4 cups sugar
2 green peppers
4 cups cider vinegar
1/2 T. allspice
1 t. ginger
1 t. cloves
1 t. cinnamon
2 t. salt
1 t. pepper
4 large onions

Directions:
Mix & boil for 3 hours on low stirring often.  Put jars in boiling water.  Make sure lids are in hot water also to soften rubber for sealing.  Put chili sauce in jars and make sure you wipe rings of jars off and place lids on and don't disturb.  Will seal.

Submitter: 
Mary Guilds
Description: 
Wonderful chili sauce recipe that I received from a friend.  Easy to make and can.
