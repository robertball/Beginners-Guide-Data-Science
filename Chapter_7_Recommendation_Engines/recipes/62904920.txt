Title:
Pizza Style Portabella Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large Portabella Mushrooms
4 oz. Pepper Jack Cheese shredded
1/2 C Tomatoes diced
4 oz. mozzarella cheese shredded
Black olives sliced (to taste-optional)
2 cloves Garlic chopped
1 T Olive oil
2 T Balsamic Vinegar
1 T fresh Basil chopped
Italian breadcrumbs
Other spices, parsley, red pepper flakes to taste

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, add tomatoes, garlic, olive oil balsamic vinegar, basil, and other spices to taste.  Cook until all ingredients are mixed and all items are heated through.
Remove from stove, and in small bowl place the ingredients.  Top each mushroom with one half of the mixture.  Add cheeses ( 2 oz jack cheese, 2 oz mozzerella) and top with black olives and sprinkling of breadcrumbs.
Bake at 350 for 25 minutes or until top is golden brown and crispy.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Adina Ryan
Description: 
These are to die for if you are a vegetarian or just love great spicy pizza without all the calories.  I stumbled across these prepackaged and decided to make them part of my recipe box because they are so yummy and easy to prepare for family or guests.  Portabella mushroom topped with cheese, tomato, spices, black olives and balsamic vinegarette.
