Title:
Mississippi Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2 -3 lb chuck roast
1 packet Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing mix
1 packet McCormick Au Jus mix
5-7 pepperoncini peppers  (these are jarred)
1 stick butter

Directions:
Place roast in crock pot.
Mix together dry packets of mix, reserving a couple tbsp (for leftovers), and rub all over roast.
Place stick of butter on top.
Lay as many pepperoncinis on top to cover roast (@5-7)
Do not add any liquid.
Cook on low for about 8 hours. Every couple of hours, spoon juices over the top.
If there is leftover roast, add reserved packet mixture and vegetable broth or water. Serve as French dip sandwiches (in crusty buns with provolone cheese and the Au Jus for dipping).

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
Easy and tasty pot roast. Makes excellent French dip leftovers!
