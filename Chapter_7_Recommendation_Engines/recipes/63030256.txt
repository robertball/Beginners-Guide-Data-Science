Title:
Spinach, Cheese and prosciutto-stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts (4 oz ea)
Salt and Pepper
4 slices (1/2 oz) of prosciutto
4 slices (1/2 oz) of smoked provolone
1 cup spinach leaves, chopped
4 tbsp of all purpose flour, divided
1 tbsp of olive oil
1 tbsp of butter
1 cup of chicken broth
1 tbsp of heavy cream

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 F
2. To form pocket, cut each chicken breast horizontally almost to opposite edge. Fold back top half of breast, sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper.  Place 1 slice of prosciutto, 1 slice of provolone and 1/4 c of spinach on each chicken breast. Fold top half over filling.
3. spread 3 tbsp of flour on plate. Holding chicken breast closed, coat with flour, shake off excess. Lightly sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.
4. Heat oil and butter in large skillet over medium heat. Place chicken in skillet; cook about 4 minutes on each side or until browned.
5. Transfer chicken to shallow baking dish. Bake in oven 10 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink in center and juices run clear.
6. Whisk chicken broth and cream into remaining 1 tbsp of flour in small bowl.  Pour chicken broth mixture into same skillet, heat over medium heat, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens, about 3 minutes. Spoon sauce onto serving plates; top with chicken breasts.
Note: Swiss, Gruyere or mozzarella cheese may be substituted and thinly sliced deli ham may be substituted.
