Title:
Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Water
1 Lbs. or 4 Cups Cranberry
3 Cups Sugar
1 Tablespoon Ground Cinnamon
1 Teaspoon Ginger (optional)
1 Teaspoon Cloves (optional)

Directions:
Bring water, sugar, and Cranberries to boil. Let the cranberries pop (you'll hear them). After they stop add the spice(s), turn down the heat and let them simmer for about 15 min. Serve hot or cold.
