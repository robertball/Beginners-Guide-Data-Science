Title:
Cajun Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5890101.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large russet potatoes, peeled
3 tablespoons margarine
1 tablespoon Creole seasoning (such as Tony Chachere'sÂ®)
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
1/2 cup milk, or more as needed

Directions:
Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until easily pierced with a fork, about 20 minutes. Drain.
Add margarine, Creole seasoning, salt, and pepper to the pot. Beat together, gradually adding milk, until potatoes reach a whipped consistency.

Description: 
This is a Cajun-spiced version of the classic mashed potatoes.
