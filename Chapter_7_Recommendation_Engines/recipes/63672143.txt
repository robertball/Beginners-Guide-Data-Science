Title:
Cheddar Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1019952.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups biscuit baking mix
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2/3 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
2 tablespoons margarine, melted
2 teaspoons dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Grease a cookie sheet, or line with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, combine baking mix, Cheddar cheese, and garlic powder.
Stir in milk.
Drop batter by heaping tablespoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheet.
Bake at 400Â° for 10 minutes.
Brush biscuits with melted margarine, and sprinkle with parsley and garlic salt. Bake for 5 more minutes, or until lightly browned on the bottom.

Linda Ahrens
