Title:
Creamy shrimp and mushroom pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200g fettucini or liguini
160g frozen pre-cooked shrimp
5 or 6 medium size white button mushrooms, sliced
1 tbs butter or margarine
2 cloves garlic chopped
1/4 tsp fresh parsley
1/4 tsp herb salt
1/4 tsp lemon pepper
1 tbs lemon juice
1/2 cup sour cream
parmesan to serve

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook as directed on package.
Meanwhile, melt butter or margarine in a frying pan on medium heat. Add sliced mushrooms and cook untill soft, about 5 min. Add garlic, parsley, salt, lemon pepper and lemon juice. Stir together.
Add unfrozen shrimp, cook for about 5 min or untill heated thoroughly, stirring constantly.
Add sour cream and cook untill bubbly over low heat.
Drain pasta and top with the creamy shrimp mixture. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
I made this recipe with all my favorite ingredients, shrimp and mushroom with a nice sour cream sauce. Simply delicious!!
