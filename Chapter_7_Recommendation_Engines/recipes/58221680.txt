Title:
Chicken Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782090.jpg
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves -cooked, shredded
3 green onions, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
4 oz. cheddar, shredded
2 TBS. butter
Seasoned salt
garlic powder
pepper
3 (10 ounce) cans refrigerated crescent roll dough
stuffing mix, crushed
melted butter
garlic salt

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, slowly cook and stir onion and garlic. Cook until onions are tender. Add chicken, heating to blend.
In a medium bowl, blend chicken mixture, cream cheese, cheddar, and butter until creamy.  Add milk enough to stir.
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Unroll croissants and divide into squares. Press into muffin tin. Place approximately 1/3 cup of the chicken mixture on each rectangle. Pinch upward and seal edges.
Brush with melted butter, top with garlic salt, then crushed stuffing mix.  Bake in the preheated oven 12 minutes, or until golden. (Check time against crescent roll directions.)
