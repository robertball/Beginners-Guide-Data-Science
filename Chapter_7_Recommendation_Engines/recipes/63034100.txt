Title:
Beef Stick and Pineapple Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/761537.jpg
Ingredients:
3 - 8 oz. cream cheese - softened
2 bunches green onion - chopped fine
1 cup beef stick - ground fine
1 - 20 oz. can crushed pineapple - well drained
1 cup pecans - crushed
1/2 cup pecan meal ~or~ 1 cup pecan pieces

Directions:
Mix together cream cheese, green onions, beef stick, crushed pineapple and 1 cup of crushed pecans.  Mix well and form 1 large cheeseball or 3 smaller cheeseballs.  Roll in pecan meal or crushed pecans.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
A delicious cheeseball with the surprise ingredient of crushed pineapple.  It is one of our favorites and I like to make it for gifts.  You can change the taste depending on the type of meat used.
