Title:
KFC Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Butter
2 1/2 T. sugar
1 egg, beaten
3/4 buttermilk
1/4 C. club soda
1 t. salt
5 C. Bisquick biscuit mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°
Combine all ingredients.. Knead dough by hand until smooth.
Flour your hands. Pat dough flat to 3/4 inch thickness on waxed paper.
Punch out biscuits with round cutter
Bake on greased CS for 12 min. or until golden brown

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This recipe is from a book called Top Secret Recipes. These taste just like the real KFC biscuits.
