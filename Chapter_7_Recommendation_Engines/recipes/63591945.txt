Title:
Mango salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 mango, peeled and cut into small chunks
1/2 red onion, diced
1/2 jalapeno, diced
10 sprigs of cilantro, chopped into small pieces
juice of 2 limes
salt

Directions:
Peel and dice into small chunks mango and set aside
mix in chopped onion, jalapeno and cilantro
squeeze in juice of limes and thoroughly mix together with a wooden spoon
add salt as needed

Submitter: 
Casey McMahon
Description: 
Tastes delicious on its own with tortilla chips, but I think I goes best with any kind of fish or shrimp taco.  Try it out!  It also tastes better if you make it one day, then leave it overnight in the refrigerator so all the flavors have time to meld together.
