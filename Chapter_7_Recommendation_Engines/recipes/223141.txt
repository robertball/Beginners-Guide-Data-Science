Title:
Perfect Peach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1049450.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large peach, sliced and frozen
1 banana, cut into pieces and frozen
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup soy milk
1 tablespoon ground flax seed (optional)

Directions:
Blend peach, banana, orange juice, soy milk, and flax seed in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
AllisonB
Description: 
This delightfully light smoothie makes for the perfect start to a summer day.
