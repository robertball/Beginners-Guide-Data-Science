Title:
Peach Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large peaches
2 - 8oz. crescent rolls
1 cup butter or 2 stickes
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 - 12 oz, mountain dew

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
>Peal and pit peaches.Cut into eight wedges per peach.
>Roll each slice in a crescent roll.Place in greace 9 x 13 baking dish.
>Melt butter then stir in sugar and vanilla.Pour over dumplings.Pour half can of mt.dew around edge of bakong dish.Sprinkle cinnamon over all.
>Bake for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
