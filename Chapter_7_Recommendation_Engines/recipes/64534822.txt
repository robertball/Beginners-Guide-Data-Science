Title:
Pumpkin Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces linguine
4 c. veggie broth
1 c. pumpkin puree
1/2 c. white wine or water
2 T. olive oil
1/2 medium onion, chopped
4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. salt
freshly ground pepper and red pepper flakes to taste
4 ounces goat cheese

Directions:
Add all ingredients except goat cheese to a large, heavy pot. Bring to rolling boil over high heat. Cook for 9 minutes, stirring frequently. Check that pasta is tender, then add cheese and stir gently.
