Title:
Lazy Man's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cut up chickens, or equivalent pieces
1¼ pounds small white or yellow onions
1½ pounds new red potatoes
1 pound small or cut up large carrots
5-6 large cloves garlic, minced
1½ teaspoons salt [or to taste]
freshly ground pepper
1 teaspoon thyme leaves
1 Cup dry white wine [optional]
1-2 Cups chicken stock [more, if no wine]
3 Tablespoons butter

Directions:
Put chicken, vegetables, and spices in shallow pan.
Add wine and stock to half cover, and dot with butter.
Bake on upper rack at 500° for 40-60 minutes.
Add more stock throughout cooking if necessary. [Stock should be reduced by half at end of cooking.]

Submitter: 
Christine Foley Wanner
Description: 
Easy, one pan, delicious meal. My mother & aunt have been making this for years and now I do the same. I believe they originally got the recipe out of the NY Times.
