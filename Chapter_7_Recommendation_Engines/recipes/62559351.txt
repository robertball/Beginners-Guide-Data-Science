Title:
Turkey Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535312.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Lean ground turkey
1 packet onion soup mix
1 Cup Fat Free sour cream
1 can (10 3/4 ounce) low fat cream of mushroom soup
1 can (10 3/4 ounce) low fat cream of chicken soup
2 Cups sliced mushrooms
3/4 Can Water (use soup can)
Pepper/salt to taste

Directions:
Brown and drain turkey. Stir in soup mix, and mushrooms; cook until mushrooms get a little soft, about 5 minutes. Add in soups, sour cream, and water-mix well and heat over low heat. Serve mix over whole grain noodles or brown rice.

Submitter: 
SlimCookins
Description: 
A healthy twist on beef stroganoff, using ground turkey and mushrooms.
