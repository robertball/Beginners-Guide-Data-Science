Title:
CHICKEN ENCHILADA SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (2.82 oz.) Nissin Big Cup Noodles, roasted chicken
2 tsp. Knorr Chicken flavored Bouillon
2 (12.5 oz.) cans Kirkland Chicken Breast, in water
1 large onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, seeded & diced
4 ribs celery, thinly sliced
2 tsp. white pepper
1(1 lb. 10 oz.) can Campbell's Cream of Chicken soup, family size, undiluted
1 (1 lb. 12 oz.) can Las Palmas Green Chile Enchilada sauce, Picante Hot or Medium
1 (15.25 oz.) can sweet corn
1 (4 oz.) can diced green chile peppers

Directions:
Add 1 teaspoon of the chicken bouillon to the Big Cup Noodles and cook in the microwave according to directions. Cover an let steep for 10 minutes.
Drain the cans of chicken into a large stockpot and add the onion, bell pepper, celery and white pepper. Cover and steam, on low heat, the vegetables until tender. Add the broth from the cup of noodles and the can of cream of chicken soup. Stir until the soup becomes creamy and lump-free. Break up the canned chicken chunks and add to the pot.
Stir in the enchilada sauce, corn and green chiles. Heat to a simmer and serve. Or place on LOW in a slow cooker for later service.
Suggested toppings: shredded jack cheese, chopped chives, sour cream or tortilla chips.
