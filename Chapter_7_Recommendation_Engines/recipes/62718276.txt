Title:
Easy Simple chocolate Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick of butter - softened
3-4 cups of Confectioners (powdered) Sugar
1 level table spoon of cocoa powder
(1/2 teaspoon of Water or Vanilla extract)-Optional

Directions:
Soften the butter, *Variation* if you want a more liquid like frosting, melt it in the microwave.
Slowly add the Powdered sugar to the mix, you can go over or under the listed amount till it is at a consistency you like.
During this step, add the cocoa powder. continue to mix the ingredients and add the Vanilla sprinkle or water if the icing is too stiff.

Submitter: 
Cyn Determination
Description: 
This is a yummy icing that is made simple. You can use a mixer or a fork. I use this as dipping for fruit, cookie icing and home made cake icing.
