Title:
Spicy Pork Chops with Honey Apple Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Thick-Cut Pork chops
1 Fuji Apple, cored, chopped into 1/2 inch chunks
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup Apple cider
1/4 cup unsalted sweet butter
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 Tablespoon kosher salt, coarse ground
1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
1 teaspoon paprika
1 tablespoon white pepper
1 teaspoon garlic salt
Extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Place the Apples, cinnamon, and brown sugar in a small bowl, cover with a plate and shake to distribute the cinnamon/sugar evenly.
Place the butter, honey and apple cider in a small skillet over medium heat.
Add the coated apples and simmer/reduce until the sauce thickens, about 10-15 minutes.
Combine the kosher salt, garlic salt, white pepper, paprika, and red ppper flakes in a small bowl.
drizzle the pork chops with olive oil and rub in the spice mixture liberally on both sides.
Grill the pork chops to desired doneness, at least 160 degrees, about 5-7 minutes on each side, depending on thickness. Let rest for at least 5 minutes.
Cover with apple honey sauce and serve.
