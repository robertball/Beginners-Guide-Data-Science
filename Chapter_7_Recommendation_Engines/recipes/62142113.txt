Title:
JD Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 extra large eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup granulated sugar;
2 T. butter, melted
1 cup dark corn syrup
1 tsp. vanilla flavoring
1/4 cup Jack Daniel's whiskey
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup pecan halves
1 (10-inch) deep pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Combine eggs, sugar, butter, syrup, vanilla and whiskey; mix well.
Sprinkle chocolate chips over the bottom of the pie shell.
Add the pie mixture and pecans.
Bake at 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
