Title:
Orange Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup orange juice
2 tsp. orange peel
2-1/2 cups flour
4 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup shredded coconut

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add egg and beat well.  Blend in orange juice and peel.  In separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt and coconut.  Drop by teaspoonsful on greased cookie sheets.  Bake one cookie sheet at a time in 350 degree oven for 8-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
This is a soft, chewy, cake-like cookie.  The coconut makes it stand out from other orange cookies.
