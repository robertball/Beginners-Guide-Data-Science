Title:
Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of wild rice
4 ounces chicken broth
1/3 cup butter/margarine
2 tsp garlic powder
1 green pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 small cans of mushrooms
2 shredded carrots
1/4 cup flour
2 cups half and half

Directions:
Cook wild rice in chicken broth until rice is cooked.
Meanwhile, in skillet, cook vegetables in butter and garlic until tender.
Add flour to vegetables, then combine with cooked rice.  Stir well.
Add half and half and cook until heated through.

Submitter: 
Shawn Hoffman Zimmerman
Description: 
Great soup for a cold winter day...or summer day.  Great mixture of veggies and rice.
