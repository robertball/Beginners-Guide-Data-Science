Title:
zucchini and eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbls olive oil
2 med.zucchini
1 med. onion
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 parmesian cheese
4 eggs

Directions:
slice onion and zucchini
place 2 tbls olive oil in skillet
Add onion and zucchini to skillet.Salt and pepper to taste.
Saute onion and zucchini until tender. Add parmesian cheese and beaten eggs. Cook until eggs are done about 5 min.

Submitter: 
Patricia Terranova Bergstrom
Description: 
This recipe was one of my Italian grandmother's favorites. You can also subsitute green peppers or potatoes for the zucchini. A great meatless dish.
