Title:
First Class Bleu Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 hard boiled egg, seived or finely 'mashed'
8 Romaine Lettuce Leaves torn or chopped
1/2 cup of your favorite Bleu Cheese Dressing (I use Bob's Brand)
1/4 cup Toasted Almonds
Bleu Cheese or Gorgonzola Crumbles

Directions:
Hard Boil the egg, and toast the Almonds while the egg is boiling.
Peel and seive or mash the egg very fine
Tear or chop the Romaine Lettuce into a bowl.
Mix dressing with torn lettuce, toasted almonds, seived egg and crumbled cheese.
Some very finely chopped onion adds to this salad also, but not on an airplane.

Submitter: 
GRAYJETMAN
Description: 
We used to serve this salad in First Class (when service was still FIRST CLASS). Nearly everyone who had this salad commented on how good it was. This salad is best if served very cold and not made too far in advance of serving.
