Title:
Quick and Easy Snack Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 large flour tortillas
2-4 Tbsp soft butter
2 Tbsp granulated sugar
1-2 tsp ground cinnamon

Directions:
To prepare these, turn a gas burner on to low heat. Using metal tongs, place one tortilla at a time over the open flame and let cook on each side until golden and bubbly. Remove from heat and spread butter on one side of tortilla.
In a small bowl, combine the sugar and cinnamon, adjust to your taste preference. Sprinkle mixture over butter and roll tortilla up and serve. Quick, easy and delicious.

Submitter: 
Pinch of Sarcasm
Description: 
My mom used to make these for my brother and I when we were growing up. They are simple and delicious, I still make them for my kids. You can eat them as a quick breakfast or for a snack anytime of the day. You do have to have a gas stove to prepare the tortillas correctly.
