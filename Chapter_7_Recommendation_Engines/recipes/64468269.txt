Title:
One Pan Zucchini Mushroom Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound spaghetti
1 pound cremini mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 zucchini, thinly sliced and quartered
2/3 cup peas
2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
2 sprigs thyme
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
1/3 cup vegan grated Parmesan (opt)
1/4 cup heavy cashew cream

Directions:
In a large stockpot or Dutch oven over medium high heat, combine spaghetti, mushrooms, zucchini, peas, garlic, thyme and 4 1/2 cups water; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, until pasta is cooked through and liquid has reduced, about 8-10 minutes. Stir in Parmesan and cashew cream.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
An incredibly creamy, hearty vegan pasta dish that you can make in just 20 minutes. Even the pasta gets cooked right in the pot!
