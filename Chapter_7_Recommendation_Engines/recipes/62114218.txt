Title:
Winter Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup salted Butter
1 large onion (chopped fine)
4 med/large Garlic cloves, chopped
44 Fl.Ozs. Chicken stock
4 cups butternut squash(1 large squash), peeled & cubed
4 cups cooked acorn squash (2 small squashes)(recipe follows)
1 tsp. dry thyme
1 tsp. dry sage
1/4 cup heavy cream
2 tsp. brown sugar
cinnamon and brown sugar to sprinkle on acorn squash
1 small french loaf
grated parmesan cheese and seasoning of choice
olive oil

Directions:
ACORN SQUASH...preheat oven to 400F
halve and clean guts of 2 small acorn squashes
sprinkle cut side with cinnamon and brown sugar
place face down in baking dish with 1/4 cup water in it
bake about 60 minutes until squash is soft
scoop squash from skins and set aside
SOUP...
Melt butter in a large stock pot
add onions and garlic, sautee on low until onions are translucent.
Add chicken stock, Butternut Squash, and stir in herbs
Bring to a Boil.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer about 20 minutes until butternut squash is cooked.
Add in cooked Acorn Squash, stir to combine.
Remove from heat
With a submersible hand mixer (or in batches in blender)puree until smooth.
Add in cream and brown sugar, stir to combine
Bring to a simmer
Add salt and pepper to taste
GARNISH with Cauliflour & Croutons
CROUTONS...
cut loaf in cubes and toss in olive oil, parmesan cheese and seasonings of choice
Bake in a single layer on a baking sheet at 400F for about 15 minutes or place under broiler for 2-3 minutes.

Submitter: 
SMZCOZ
Description: 
A great Autumn creamy soup using Butternut and Acorn squashes and a garnish of Cauliflour & homemade croutons.
