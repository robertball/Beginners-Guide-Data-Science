Title:
Carol's Caramel Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 bags microwave popcorn (light butter type is best)
2 1/4 c brown sugar
1 c light corn syrup
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c butter

Directions:
Pop popcorn according to manufacturer's directions.  Transfer popcorn to two large bowls.
Heat brown sugar and corn syrup on low heat until sugar is dissolved.
Add condensed milk.
Stirring almost constantly, boil for 2 minutes.
Add butter and boil again for 2 to 3 minutes.
Pour evenly over popcorn and mix well.

Submitter: 
Camille Penner Morden
Description: 
I finally asked my mom for her caramel popcorn recipe after being disappointed with others I have found.  It is fast, easy, and does not need to bake. The consistency is perfectly gooey and delicious!  Hope you enjoy!
