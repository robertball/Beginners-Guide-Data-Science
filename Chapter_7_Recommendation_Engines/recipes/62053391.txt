Title:
Mushroom Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP olive oil,
8 pork chops,
1 large onion, chopped,
2 tsp chopped garlic,
1 small can mushrooms, drained,
1/2 pound your favorite mushrooms, sliced thinly,
1 can cream of mushroom soup,
1/2 cup water,
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown pork chopw with oil, on both sides.  Drain off fat.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, or until juices of pork chops run clear.

Rhonda Brock Fuller
