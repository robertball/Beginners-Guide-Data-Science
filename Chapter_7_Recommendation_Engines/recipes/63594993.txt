Title:
Fluffy Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20 oz can crushed pineapple, undrained
1 3 1/2 oz pkg instant lemon pudding
1 29 oz can peach slices, well drained and cut into pieces
1 16 oz can mandarin oranges, well drained
1 10 oz jar maraschino cherries, well drained and cut in half
1 8 oz carton frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Place pineapple in large mixing bowl.
Add pudding mix and stir to combine.
Add other fruit.
Fold in whipped topping and stir gently.
Refrigerate several hours.
