Title:
Ruth-Anne's Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1t salt
1/2t pepper
1t dried thyme
2T Dijon-style mustard
1C milk
1/2C chopped celery
1/2C chopped onion
1/4C + 2T ketchup
1 1/2# ground chuck
1 1/2C fresh bread crumbs
3 strips bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In lg. mixing bowl combine egg, seasonings, mustard, milk, celery, onion, 1/4C ketchup, ground chuck, and breadcrumbs. Mix well.
Place beef mixture in standard 8-in loaf pan. Spread surface with 2T ketchup. Layer bacon over top. Bake for 1 hr.
Drain any excess fat from pan. Remove meat loaf and let cool at room temp. for 5 min. Cut into 1/2-in. thick slices.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Celery, onion, ketchup,mustard, milk and breadcrumbs in the inside and topped with more ketchup and bacon make for a tasty meatloaf
