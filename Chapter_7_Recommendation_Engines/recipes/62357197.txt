Title:
Bacon Wrapped Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, sliced
8 slices bacon
1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Pound the chicken breasts until flat. Put a slice of cream cheese and 1 teaspoon chopped chives in the middle of each breast and roll up.   Place in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a medium bowl, combine condensed soup, mayonnaise, milk, lemon juice, pepper and salt. Mix until smooth, then pour over chicken.
Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until no longer pink and juices run clear.after 30 mins. of baking sprinkle crumbled bacon on top of chicken

Submitter: 
Sandy
Description: 
Topped with a rich cream sauce, these bacon-wrapped chicken breasts are rolled around a mild, cheesy filling.  Perfect with a side of fettuccini.Modified from: Bacon Wrapped Chicken
