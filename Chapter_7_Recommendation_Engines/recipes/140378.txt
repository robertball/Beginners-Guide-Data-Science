Title:
Knock Your Socks Off Sangria! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/618868.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle sangria wine
1/2 cup sour apple schnapps
1/2 cup rum (optional)
1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
1 cup halved seedless grapes
1 lime, sliced

Directions:
Stir together the wine, schnapps, rum, strawberries and grapes in a large serving pitcher.  Chill for at least one hour before serving.
Garnish with lime slices.

Submitter: 
Jen G
Description: 
This simple and delicious twist on sangria is sure to have your guests wanting more! The best part is you can add any fruit and it still works great! Stock up on the supplies as surely one pitcher will not be enough.
