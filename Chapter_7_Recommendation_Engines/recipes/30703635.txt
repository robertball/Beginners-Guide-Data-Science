Title:
Chocolate Crunch Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
1/4 cup cooking oil
1 Devil's food cake mix
3 chocolate covered buttery toffee bars (39 g each)

Directions:
Beat eggs and cooking oil in medium bowl until combined. Add cake mix. Beat until just moistened.
Add chocolate bar pieces. Mix until stiff dough forms. Roll into balls, using 2 tsp for each cookie. Arrange 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheets.
Bake in 350 F oven for about 10 minutes until tops are domed and cracked.
Let stand on cookie sheets for 5 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool.

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy to make, with outstanding results. Crispy outside, chewy inside - so good!
