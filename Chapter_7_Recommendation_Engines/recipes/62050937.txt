Title:
BlackBean Chorizo Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Chorizo sausage, skinless
8 oz Pepper Jack cheese, shredded
1 can black beans, drain and chop in food processor
1 - 3 chipotle peppers, finely minced
Tortilla Chips, of course.

Directions:
Cook the chorizo, then drain using a coffee filter and a strainer
Return the chorizo to the pan and add black beans and most of the cheese (save some for garnish). Heat till the cheese is melted. Add chipotles to taste.
Or substitute Jalapeno Tabasco, again to taste.

Submitter: 
wmat
Description: 
Try serving in a chafing dish or chocolate fondue pot to keep it warm.
