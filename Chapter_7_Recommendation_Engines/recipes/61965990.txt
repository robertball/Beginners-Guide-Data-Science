Title:
Spicy Tortilla Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces cream cheese, softened
4 ounces black olive, chopped
8 ounces green chile, diced
8 ounces pimientos, sliced, drained
4 green onion, minced
2 Tablespoons tobasco sauce
6 Tablespoons cilantro, chopped
8 tortillas

Directions:
In a medium mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, olives, chilies, pimentoes, green onions, hot sauce and cilantro.  Spread mixture onto tortillas.  Roll the tortillas up and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Slice up rolls and serve.
