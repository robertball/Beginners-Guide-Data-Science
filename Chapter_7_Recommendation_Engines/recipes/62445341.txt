Title:
Jim's Chicken Tomato Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460850.jpg
Ingredients:
4 - Boneless chicken breasts
4- 10.5 oz cans of stewed tomatos
1- pkg Lipton onion soup mix
2- tablespoons minced garlic
1- tablespoon Worsterchire

Directions:
Put everything in a slow cooker for 4 hours on high or 8 hours on low.

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
Boneless breasts of chicken simmer in a slow cooker for 4-8 hours for a delicious meal. Stand alone or  serve over rice or noodles.
