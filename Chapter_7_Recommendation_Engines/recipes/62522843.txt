Title:
Pineapple loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz can of crushed pineapple with juice
about 7-10 slices of white bread, cubed
4 eggs
1 cup of margarine
1 cup of sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350.
butter a loaf pan.
mix the pineapple, eggs, margarine and sugar together.
begin adding bread until mix is no longer runny and soupy. (add more bread if needed)
Pour into loaf pan.
Top with dabs of butter/margarine
Bake for 1 hour
