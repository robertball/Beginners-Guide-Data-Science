Title:
Stephen's favorite marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Italian Dressing
1/2 cup Barbeque Sauce
1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce
1 tsp Garlic (minced or powder)
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in bowl. Marinate choice of meat 1/2 hour to 12 hours depending on preference.

Submitter: 
hoglundks
Description: 
Very tasty and can be used on a variety of meat. We mostly use this for steak.
