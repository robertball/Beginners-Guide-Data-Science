Title:
Quinoa- tastes like buttered corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c Quinoa
2c Homemade chicken broth
2T butter
Salt
Black pepper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a pan and place on stove top.
Bring to a boil with lid on
Reduce heat to a simmer, simmer for 15 minutes
Remove lid, fluff with fork and continue simmering if there is too much liquid.

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
Description:
