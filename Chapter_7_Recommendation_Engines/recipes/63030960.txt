Title:
Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 cup cold butter or margarine
1 cup zest of 2 oranges
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
3 tbsp sugar
2 tsp ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
In a bowl combine flour, sugar, zest and baking powder.  Cut in the butter or margarine until mixture resembles fine crumbs.  Beat milk and eggs then add to the dry ingredients stirring lightly.
Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and roll to 1" thickness.  Cut with a 2-1/2" biscuit cutter.
In a small bowl stir together 3 tbsp of sugar and 2 tsp of ground cinnamon.  Sprinkle this on top of the Scones.
Place in oven and bake at 425 degrees F for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
