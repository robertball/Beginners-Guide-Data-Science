Title:
White House Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margerine softened
1 Cup Grantulated Sugar
1 Cup Brown Sugar Packed
1 Cup Oil
1 Tsp Cream of Tartar
1 Cup 1 Minute Oats
3 1/2 Cups Flour
1 Egg
1 Tsp Vanilla
1 Tsp Baking Soda
1 up Rice Crispies
1 Cup Coconut

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350
Beat together, margarine, sugars, oil.
Add Egg and vanilla and beat till fluffy.  Sift together Flour, Soda and Cream of Tartar
Add to mixture.
Stir in Rice Krispies, Oats, & Coconut
Drop by spoonful on baking sheet
Bake 10-12 min
