Title:
Chocolate Cream Cheese Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups butter
8 oz. cream cheese
3 cups brown sugar
vanilla to taste
8 oz. good quality semi-sweet or bittersweet chocolate (I prefer Valrhona manjari, guanaja or equitoriale)
6 eggs
2 1/2 cups flour
scant 1 cup cocoa

Directions:
cream butter, cream cheese, sugar and vanilla until light
melt chocolate in double boiler or (very carefully) in microwave; add to butter and combine well
add eggs 2 at a time, scraping bowl and combining well between additions
add flour in halves, mixing well, scraping bowl between additions
add cocoa and mix well
divide mixture into 3 loaf pans or 1 10" tube pan, which have been prepared with cooking spray
bake in preheated 325 oven for 45-50 mins for loaves or 65-70 mins for tube pan, until cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean
