Title:
Beanfree  Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 tsp. minced jar garlic
1/2 c. chopped onion
2 T Chili powder
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 T. Hot Pepper Flakes
28 oz. crushed tomatoes
1/4 red wine vinegar

Directions:
1.  Crumble ground beef and onion brown in dutch oven.  Drain excess fat.  Add the rest of the ingredients except vinegar.  Cover and simmer for 45 min.  Add vinegar, cover and simmer for 15 more min.  Serve over Elbow macaroni or rice.
