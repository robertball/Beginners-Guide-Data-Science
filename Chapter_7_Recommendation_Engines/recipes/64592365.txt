Title:
Unwrapped bacon Jalapino Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 eggs
2 cups shredded cheese
Pepper to taste
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp season salt
1 lb bacon cooked and chopped
1 brick cream cheese
2-4 Jalopenos

Directions:
Mix eggs, shredded cheese, pepper, milk, season salt, and bacon together and place into a 9 inch pie pan. Top with cream cheese and sliced jalopenios, bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes
