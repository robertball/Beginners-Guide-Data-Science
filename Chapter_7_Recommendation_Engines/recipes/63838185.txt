Title:
GARLIC BEEF ENCHILADAS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 med onion chpt
2 T flour
1 T chili pwdr
1 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic pwdr
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp rubbed sage
1 can (14 1/2oz) stewed tomatoes cut up
Sauce
1/3 c butter
4 to 6 garlic cloves minced
1/2 c flour
1 can (14 1/2oz) beef broth
1 can (15oz) tomato sauce
1 to 2 T chili pwdr
1 to 1 tsp cumin
1 to 2 tsp rubbed sage
1/2 tsp salt
.
.
10 6" flour or corn tortillas
2 C (8oz) shredded colby-monterey jack cheese

Directions:
In large skillet cook beef and onion over med heat 6 or 8 min till beef is no longer pink. Break into crumbles and drain grease.Stir in flour and seasonings.
Stir in tomatoes, bring to boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 15 min. Preheat oven 350*
In sauce pan heat butter over med hi heat, add garlic, cook and stir for 1 min or till tender. Stir in flour till blended; gradually whisk in broth. Bring to boil, cook and stir 2 min or until thickened. Stir in tomato sauce and seasonings. Heat through.
Pour 1 1/2 C sauce into ungreased 9x13 baking dish. Place about 1/4 C  beef mixture in ea tortilla, top with 1 or 2 T cheese. Roll up and place over sauce seam side down. Top with remaining sauce. Bake covered 30-35 min. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake uncovered 10-15 min or  till cheese is melted.
