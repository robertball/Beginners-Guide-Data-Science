Title:
Tangy Italian-Style Turkey Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/329866.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups elbow macaroni
1 lb ground turkey
1 cup green bell pepper, chopped
1 cup onion, chopped
1 (10.75 oz.) can tomato soup
1 (14.5 oz.) can diced tomatoes, drained
1 (6 oz.) can tomato paste
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Bring a saucepan of water to a boil; add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente and drain; set aside.
In the same saucepan, cook ground turkey, green pepper and onion; cook until turkey is no longer pink and vegetables are crisp-tender.
Add garlic powder, dried oregano,  black pepper, tomato soup, tomato paste and diced tomatoes; add macaroni to turkey mixture and mix until well combined.
Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes, or until heated through.

Submitter:

Description: 
I came up with this goulash recipe on the spur of the moment and off the top of my head.  It's goulash made with ground turkey and has a little twist of Italian flavor!
