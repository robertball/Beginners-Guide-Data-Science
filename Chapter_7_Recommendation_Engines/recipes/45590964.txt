Title:
No-Fry Chicken Parm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
* 1 egg beaten
* 3/4 cup Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
* 1 jar (1 lb. 10 oz.) marinara sauce (whatever variety you prefer)
* 1 cup shredded 2% milk mozz cheese (about 4 oz.)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 F
Dip chicken in egg and then bread crumbs
In 13x9 inch baking dish, arrange chicken
Bake 20 minutes
Pour pasta sauce over chicken; top with cheese
Bake an additional 10 min or until chicken is thoroughly cooked
If desired, serve with hot cooked pasta of your choice
