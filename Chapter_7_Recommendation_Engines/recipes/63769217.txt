Title:
Teriyaki steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 cup dry white or red wine
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
4 new york steaks

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour over steak in a ziplock bag or container.
Let steak marinate for at least an hour and up to 24 hours in refrigerator.
Grill steaks 4 to 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. When done, transfer to warmed plates.
