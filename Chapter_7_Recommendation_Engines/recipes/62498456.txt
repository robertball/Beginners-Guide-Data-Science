Title:
Kartoffel and Kloes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, peel and cut in small pieces
1 1/2 cubes butter
2 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup water
flour
bread crumbs
cream

Directions:
Make kloes by combining beaten eggs, salt, water and enough flou to make stiff dough. Cook potatoes in enough salted water to cover them well! When potatoes are nearly done, drop in kloes by teaspoonfuls. Boil 15 minutes or until done. Brown crumbs in 1/2 cube butter. When potatoes and kloes are done, drain. Garnish with a cube of melted butter and browned crumbs and cream.
