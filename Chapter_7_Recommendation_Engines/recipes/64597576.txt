Title:
Big Ray's Chickpea Salad With Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3890247.jpg
Ingredients:
10 oz box couscous
1 1/2 cup boiling water
1  (15 1/2 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
2 tsp orange zest, grated
2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
3/4 tsp kosher salt, divided
4 large tomato, cut into wedges
1/2 small sweet onion, thinly sliced
1/4 cup fresh mint, torn
1/4 cup (2 ounces) dry roasted almonds, roughly chopped

Directions:
Place the couscous in a medium bowl, pour 1 1/2 cups boiling water over the top, cover, and let sit for 10 minutes before fluffing with a fork.
Stir in the chickpeas, orange zest, 1 Tbsp of the oil, and 1/4 tsp each salt and pepper.
In a separate bowl, combine the tomatoes, onion, mint, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper, and the remaining oil.
Spoon the couscous and tomato mixture onto a platter and sprinkle with the almonds.
