Title:
Healthier Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277958.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour for coating
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded 1/4 inch thick
4 tablespoons butter
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1/8 cup Marsala wine
1/8 cup red wine
1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown.  Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms. Pour in wines and sherry. Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
MakeItHealthy
Description: 
This was a great recipe and I make it a little healthier by cutting down the wine and sherry and only frying in butter instead of butter and oil.Modified from: Chicken Marsala
