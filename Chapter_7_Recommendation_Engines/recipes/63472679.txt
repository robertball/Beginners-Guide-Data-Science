Title:
Grilled Cornish Game Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cornish Game Hens
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 tablespoon whole coarsley cracked black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
Spring fresh rosemary, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Directions:
Split hens lengthwise. Rinse hen halves, pat dry.
Place hens in a large sealable plastic bag. Add all ingredientsvand marinade for several hours or overnight.
Grill hens with rosemary over indirect heat for 30-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
momtoXandI
Description: 
Hens are juicy when grilled by the indirect method.
