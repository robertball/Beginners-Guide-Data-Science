Title:
Amy Vanderbilts Apple Pie done one better Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp powdered ginger
1 tsp powdered cinnamon
1/4 tsp powdered nutmeg
1/4 tsp salt
1 Tbsp lemon juice
2 Tbsp flour
3 Tbsp strong Earl Grey Tea
4 cups sliced and cored Granny Apple apples
2 Tbsp butter
2 pie crusts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Fit one pie shell in bottom of 9" pie pan
Boil water and place 2 Earl Grey Tea Bags in one cup of water and let soak
Combine sugar, spices, salt, flour, lemon juice and strong black Earl Grey Tea
Mix well
Alternate apples and sugar mixture until pie pan is full.
Dot with butter.
Top with second pie shell, trim, seal and poke holes in top with fork.
Bake 40-50 minutes until crust is brown.
Serve with sharp Cheddar cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is from an old Amy Vanderbilt cookbook I found at a church sale. My tweak is to use the Earl Grey tea. My friends rave about my apple pie but all I do is follow the recipe. Hope you enjoy!
