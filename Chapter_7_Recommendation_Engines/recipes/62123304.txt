Title:
Low fat Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3 bananas(fresh or frozen)
3/4 cup white sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup apple sauce( or individual portion lunch apple sauce)
chocolate chips(as much or as little as you like)

Directions:
mix dry ingredients and set aside:
1 1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp b. soda
1 tsp b. powder
Place peeled bananas in lge bowl and mash
add and stir between each addition:
sugar, egg and apple sauce.
Add dry ingredients and stir until mixed
Add choc. chips and combine.
Place in greased muffin tins 3/4 full
Bake @ 350 C x 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
ERIN
Description: 
Moist banana muffins with yummy choc. chips and next to no  fat. Next to no work either!
