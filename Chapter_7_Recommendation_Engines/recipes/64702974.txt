Title:
Traditional Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Large Eggs, hard cooked and cooled
1/4 Cup Soy-Free Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp Sweet Pickle Relish
1 tsp Yellow Mustard
Salt and Pepper
Paprika, for garnish

Directions:
Halve the eggs lengthwise.
Remove the yolks from the whites and transfer them to a seperate bowl. Mash the yolks with a fork until there are no large pieces left.
Stir the mayonnaise, relish and mustard with the yolks and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Scoop (or pipe if you would like to achieve a design) about a tablespoon of the mixture into each egg white half.
Sprinkle the tops with paprika for garnish.

Submitter: 
ToriGeib
Description: 
This is the traditional recipe we use in my family for picnics and holidays. Depending on the size of the group you are cooking for, you may want to adjust the quantities.  You can also switch the Sweet Pickle Relish for Dill if you'd like.
