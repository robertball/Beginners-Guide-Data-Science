Title:
Five Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can green beans
1 can wax beans
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
1 can garbanzo beans
1 can black beans
1 large onion, sliced into thin rings and separated.
1/2 cup white sugar
2/3 cup distilled white vinegar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon celery seed (optional)

Directions:
Drain all beans well, rinsing the kidney beans and the black beans.  Mix together sugar, vinegar, vegetable oil, salt, pepper, and celery seed. Pour over beans and onion rings.  Toss well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 12 hours.  If lid on container is tight, turn over every few hours to marinate beans evenly.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This is great for summer picnics and cookouts. It's easy to throw together, keeps well, and serves a lot of people.Modified from: Three Bean Salad
