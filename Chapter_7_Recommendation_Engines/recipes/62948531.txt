Title:
Caramel Apple Pork Chops by Deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs boneless pork Loin chops- 8-9 chops
2 tbsp butter
6 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tbsp  seasoning
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
4 tart apples - peeled, cored and sliced
6 Tbsp of cider vinegar
3 tablespoons pecans (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (80 degrees C).
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat melting butter.   Dredge chops in flour seasoning mix and fry till golden.  Transfer to the casserole dish.
In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Add butter to skillet, and stir in brown sugar mixture,cider vinegar and apples. Cover and cook until apples are just tender. Remove apples with a slotted spoon and arrange on top of chops.   Spoon sauce over apples and chops.
