Title:
Muz's Hot Dill Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. butter
1 T. flour
1 t. salt
1 T. fresh dill weed
1/8 t. pepper
1 c. milk
1/2 c. mayonnaise
2 T. chopped onion
4 c. cooked diced potatoes (@ 5 medium)

Directions:
Melt butter over low heat.  Stir in next four ingredients.  Add milk; cook & stir until thick & bubbly.  Blend in mayonnaise and onion.  Gently fold in potatoes.
Serve warm (also great cold).

Submitter: 
Cristi Zbella
Description: 
This wonderful recipe was handed down to me from my Grandma Alice Devine.  This is absolutely the BEST potato salad I've ever had.  It's so unique and it's just as wonderful cold, as it is hot.
