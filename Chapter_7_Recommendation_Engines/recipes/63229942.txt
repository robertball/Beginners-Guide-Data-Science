Title:
Yummy Polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
POLENTA:
1 tube (8 ounces) of polenta
1 tablespoon of red onion
1/8 cup of chopped fresh basil
3 oz sliced mozzarella
9 tablespoons diced mushrooms
1 oz vodka sauce
SAUCE:
1/2 lbs of ground italian sausage
12 oz of vodka sauce

Directions:
preheat oven to 360'
Cover a 9x9 inch pan in olive oil
slice polenta into 1/4 inch piece and lay in pan
microwave polenta for 30 seconds on high (this will make it softer)
lay sliced mozzarella, basil, mushroom, red onion and vodka sauce over polenta and place it in the oven.
for the sauce, brown sausage in medium frying pan
mix meat with vodka sauce and simmer until polenta is ready. about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ophelie
Description: 
This is a great way to cook polenta.  I made it after searching forever for a perfect polenta recipe.  It makes a pretty soft polenta so if you prefer firmer polenta this may not be the one for you.
