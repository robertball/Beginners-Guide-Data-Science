Title:
BBQ Chicken Cornbread Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030782.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
1 can creamed corn
1 cup plain Greek yogurt
1 box Glory or Jiffy cornbread mix
2 eggs
2 cups cooked chopped chicken
1 cup BBQ sauce
1 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease 9x13 pan.
Mix together corn, cream corn, yogurt, cornbread mix, and eggs.
Pour into pan.
Bake 30 minutes.
Toss chicken with BBQ sauce.
Remove cornbread casserole from oven.
Top with chicken mixture.
Top chicken with cheese.
Bake 10-15 minutes.
