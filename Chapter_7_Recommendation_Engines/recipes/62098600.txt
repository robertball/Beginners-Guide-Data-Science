Title:
The Great Grape Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pieces white or wheat bread
4 tsp butter
4 tbsp grape jelly
2 slices american cheese
1 oz. parmesean cheese

Directions:
To get started, spread butter on one side of each slice of bread and place butter side down on cutting board. Warm up pan to medium heat. Then place two  slices of bread into  pan (butter side down) and put cheese slices on top of bread, let melt. Meanwhile, spread grape jelly on the  remianing bread slices. Once your all jellied up, place the grape side down onto the cheese (Butter side out).
Sprinkle a little parmesean cheese on top of buttered bread top and wait 2-3 mins, flip and repeat to the other side and flip again for a minute to brown the parmesean and you are all done.

Submitter: 
ANGELA O.
Description: 
Grilled Cheese sandwich with a twist. Grape jelly melted along with cheese inside the sandwich, crusted with parmesean cheese. It may sound unappealing but is surprisingly delicious and fun. Go ahead and be daring...try it!
