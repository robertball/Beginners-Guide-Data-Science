Title:
Baked Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6667358.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb shell on shrimp
1 stick butter (1/2 c)
1/4 c parsley, basil, or other mixed herbs
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 lemon juiced

Directions:
preheat oven to 375 or 400
combine all ingredients except shrimp with hand mixer until paste formed
put shrimp on foil lined baking sheet
dollop paste over shrimp
bake 8-10 min, then broil briefly if desired
