Title:
Irish Spotted Dog (Soda Bread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/994828.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup packed light brown sugar
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup dried apricots, chopped
1/3 cup dried cranberries
1/3 cup golden raisins
5 tsp cold butter, cut into 1/2" cubes
1 cup buttermilk
1 egg
1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly grease baking sheet.
In large bowl combine flour, sugar, baking soda and salt.
Stir in fried fruits.
Using two knives, cut in butter.
In separate bowl, stir together buttermilk, egg and vanilla; stir into flour mixture until dough forms.
On lightly floured surface, knead 30 seconds; shape into 8: round loaf.
Place on baking sheet.
Bake 45-50 minutes until golden.
Cool.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description:
