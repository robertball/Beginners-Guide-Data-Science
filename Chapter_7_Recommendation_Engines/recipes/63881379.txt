Title:
Cream Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup flour
4 eggs
Quick filling
1 pkge instant pudding
1 pint heavy cream
1/4 cup milk
Whip all together till creamy- do not over whip.  Use to fill cream puffs.

Directions:
Bring oil, salt and  water to boil .  Add flour slowly and continue to stir over low heat till lumps are gone.  Set aside to cool.  Meanwhile beat eggs with mixer and add flour mixture to bowl a little at a time continuing to beat until smooth.  Spoon tablespoons onto greased cookie sheet and bake in preheated 375 degree oven for 35-40 minutes.
Fill once cooled with above filling or whipped cream
