Title:
Easy Instant Pot ® Cocktail Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5721023.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz apricot preserves
12 oz hickory flavored barbecue sauce
1/4 cup water
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
24 oz frozen precooked meatballs

Directions:
Turn on a electric pressure-cooker (such as Instant Pot(R)) and select the Saute function on low heat. Whisk together the apricot preserves, barbecue sauce, water and crushed red pepper. Add the meatballs and stir to coat.
Place lid on pot and turn venting knob to sealing. Select High pressure, and set timer for 5 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.  Release  pressure manually when timer is up.
Arrange on platter with toothpicks and sprinkle with additional crushed red pepper, if desired.

Submitter: 
France C
Description: 
A twist on the grape jelly cocktail meatballs that everybody loves.  This recipe uses apricot preserves, but feel free to use grape, orange marmalade, cranberry sauce or even pineapple jalapeño - they're all delicious! Try different flavors of barbecue sauce too!  These make the perfect party appetizer, especially when you're low on time!
