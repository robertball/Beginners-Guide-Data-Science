Title:
Grilled Avocados Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 avocados
Olive oil or Canola Oil
Chipotle powder
Chilli powder

Directions:
Cut the avocados in half an pit.  Then mix up the chipotle and chilli powder, or just paprika if you don't like spicy; in the oil and baste them facing up so the pit whole contains the mixture.  Let them sit while the grill is heating up or ahead of time.  Place them on the grill face down on medium heat.  Grill them till tasty char lines develope.  Grill them last and serve hot, YUM YUM.

Submitter: 
kcin.malo
Description: 
Had some ripe avocados left over. They were extras from guacamole the previous weekend.  So gave this quick and extremely easy recipe a shot on the grill, and the wife and kids loved it.  Fresh ones are good too.
