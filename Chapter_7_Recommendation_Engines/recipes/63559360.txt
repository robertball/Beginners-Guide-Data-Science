Title:
Beefy Baked Ravioli Baron's edit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972474.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion diced
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 (25 ounce) package frozen cheese ravioli
1 (25 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced fire roasted
tomatoes,undrained
Salt pepper, red pepper, oregano to taste
1 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 pint Ricotti chese
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Crumble the ground beef into a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until no longer pink. Add onion and continue cooking till beef browned and onion translucent. Add garlic and cook one minute more. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and tomatoes. Adjust seasonings  It should be fairly spicy.
Spread 1/3 of the sauce in the bottom of an 13x9 or similar inch baking dish. Arrange 1/2 of the ravioli over the sauce. Sprinkle 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese, 1/2 of the Monterey Jack cheese and 1/2 of ricotta over the ravioli. Repeat layers, ending with the last of the sauce on top. Cover with aluminum foil.
Bake for 40 minutes in the preheated oven. Uncover and sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top and bake another 15 min Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
