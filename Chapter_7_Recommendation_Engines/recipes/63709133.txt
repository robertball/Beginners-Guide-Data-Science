Title:
Cindi's Asian Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package cole slaw mix
3 tablespoons sunflower seeds
1 tablespoon sugar
1/3-1/2 cup oil
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1/2 cup diced onion (optional)
1 package ramen noodles (any flavor ok, but I use Oriental flavor)

Directions:
Saut√© sunflower seeds in a bit of oil until lightly browned (optional - I normally don't do this)
Mix oil, vinegar, sugar, and seasoning pack from ramen noodles.  Pour over coleslaw mix and stir in onions and sunflower seeds.  Mix well.
Break ramen noodles into bite-sized pieces and add 1-2 hours before serving.
