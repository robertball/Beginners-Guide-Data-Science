Title:
Spaghtti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. ground sirloin
2 medium onions, diced
2 medium green peppers, diced
2-4 garlic cloves, minced
2 large tomato sauce cans
2 small tomato paste cans
2 T Worcestershire sauce
1 t salt
1/4 cup chili powder
vegetable oil for browning vegetables
Whole grain thin spaghetti

Directions:
Brown onion, green peppers and garlic in 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil until translucent.  Add ground beef and continue browning.  Drain fat and add remaining ingredients.  Simmer covered with a cracked lid up to 4 hours.  Serve over hot spaghetti noodles.
