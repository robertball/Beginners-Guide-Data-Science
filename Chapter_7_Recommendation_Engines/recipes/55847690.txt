Title:
Tasty Baked Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef (or pork and lamb mix in)
5 oz frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1/2 c bread crumbs
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 t basil
1 1/2 t parsley
1 t garlic powder
1 t kosher salt
1/2 t red pepper flakes

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line two baking sheets with aluminum foil, or mini muffin pans.
Mix together everything
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description:
