Title:
Tomato 'n' Shrimp Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887236.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces uncooked spaghetti
1 1/2 pounds medium shrimp
1 teaspoon garlic, minced
3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup onion, chopped
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes, undrained
3 tablespoons tomato paste
2 tablespoons fresh basil, minced
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon oregano
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute shrimp and garlic in 1 tablespoon oil for about 1 minute.
Remove and set aside.
In the same skillet, saute mushrooms and onion in remaining oil until mushrooms are lightly browned.
Stir in the tomatoes and tomato paste. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low.
Add the shrimp, basil, sugar, oregano and red pepper flakes. Cook, uncovered, for 5-10 minutes or until heated through.
Drain spaghetti; top with shrimp mixture.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Diced tomatoes, a little tomato paste and fresh basil combine with onions and mushrooms to make a tasty sauce for sauteed shrimp.Modified from: Tomato 'n' Shrimp Pasta
