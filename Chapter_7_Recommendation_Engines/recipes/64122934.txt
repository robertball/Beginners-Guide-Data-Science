Title:
Chicken, Asparagus & Mushroom Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. butter
1 T. olive oil
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut in bite-size pieces
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 C. mushrooms, sliced
2 C. asparagus, sliced
black pepper
1 pkg ( 6 oz) cornbread stuffing mix
1/4 C. dry white wine
2 C. chicken broth
1 can cream of asparagus or chicken soup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Heat butter and oil in large skillet until butter is melted. Add chicken and garlic. Cook and stir 3 minutes over med-high heat until chicken is no longer pink. Add mushrooms, cook 2 minutes, add asparagus, cook about 5 minutes until asparagus is crisp tender. Season with pepper to taste. Transfer mixture to 2 1/2 quart casserole dish. Top with stuffing mix. Add wine to skillet, cook and stir about 1 minutes over med-high heat, scraping up any browned bits from bottom of skillet. Add broth and soup. Heat until well blended. Pour mixture in casserole; mix well. Bake uncovered about 35 minutes.
