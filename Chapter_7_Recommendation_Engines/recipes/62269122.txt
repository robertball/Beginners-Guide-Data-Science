Title:
Beans & Brown Rice Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown rice
2 tblspns chopped cilantro
2 tblspns olive oil
1 medium onion chopped
4 garlic cloves, chopped
1 jalapeno chile, chopped (for less heat, remove ribs                 & seeds)
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
salt & pepper to taste
3 tblspns tomato paste
1 15 ounce can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 15 ounce can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup water
1 10-ounce package frozen corn or 15 ounce can corn
4 green onions thinly sliced
8 10 inch flour tortillas
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
Salsa and sour cream

Directions:
1.  Cook rice according to package directions; stir in cilantro and set aside.
2.  Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Add the onion, garlic, jalapeno, chili powder and cumin.  Season onion mixture with salt and pepper to taste.  Cook and stir until onion is tender.  Stir in tomato paste and cook 1 minute longer.
3.  Add the beans and water; bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium and simmer until thickened, about 10 to 12 minutes, stirring occasionally, Add corn and continue cooking until corn is heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.  Remove from heat and stir in green onions.
4.  Heat tortillas according to package directions.  To assemble, mound about 1/4 cup rice, 1/2 cup bean mixture and 1/4 cup cheese on one side of tortilla.  Starting from filled end, holding sides in as you work, tightly roll into a bundle.  Place on a baking sheet, seam side down, and repeat with remaining burritos.
5.  Serve with salsa and sour cream.
