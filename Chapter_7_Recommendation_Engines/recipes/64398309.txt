Title:
fresh collards Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bunch of collards greens-remove all ribs
Get a big pot.
Add water (1/2c maybe more as you go), and very SMALL amount of liquid smoke and one beef (or chicken) bullion cube
cook collards on med-low, once they are bright green they are done. 15 min or so, Uncovered!(don't boil, simmer is ok)
1/4 c balsamic
1/4 c molasses

Directions:
Get a big skillet (or wok) and add liquid and everything else.
Once they are getting dark green take off heat-they are done. use the sauce on the side (or mix in if you think the company can take it)

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
This is the best way to do greens-not over cooked!!!!!
