Title:
Christmas Cape Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6203928.jpg
Ingredients:
Cinnamon Simple Syrup:
1 cup water
1 cup white sugar
2 cinnamon sticks
4 fluid ounces ginger ale
3 fluid ounces cranberry juice
1 fluid ounce vodka
ice as needed

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Stir in sugar until completely dissolved, 1 to 2 minutes. Add cinnamon sticks, reduce heat to low, and let simmer for 8 minutes. Remove from heat and let steep for 1 hour.
Remove cinnamon sticks and pour syrup into a squeeze bottle.
Mix ginger ale, cranberry juice, vodka, and 1 teaspoon cinnamon syrup mixture together in a 12-ounce highball glass. Stir or shake thoroughly. Fill with ice to the top of the glass. Serve.

Submitter: 
DFPChris
Description: 
A holiday spin on this classic cocktail, taking the refreshing taste of cranberry and marrying it with the Christmas flavors of ginger and cinnamon. Garnish with a maraschino cherry or cinnamon stick. Cinnamon syrup can be kept lidded and refrigerated for up to 2 weeks.
