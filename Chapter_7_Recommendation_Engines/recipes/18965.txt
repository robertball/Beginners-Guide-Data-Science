Title:
Adult Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts orange juice
1 (750 milliliter) bottle gin
6 (12 fluid ounce) cans or bottles caffeinated lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream

Directions:
In a large punch bowl combine orange juice and gin. Gently stir in the carbonated soda and slide in the ice cream.

Submitter: 
TONOFUN
Description: 
Adult punch spiked with gin.  Use the ice cream to make it a holiday punch or use crushed ice to make it a cool drink on hot summer days. Leave out the gin, and it is a good drink for kids at a birthday party because the ice cream cools the punch.
