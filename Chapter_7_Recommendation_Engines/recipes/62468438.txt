Title:
Mom's Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/948524.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) pkg dried lentils
2 (15 oz) cans stewed tomatoes
1 (15 oz) can low sodium beef broth
2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
2 cups celery, sliced
2 cups carrots, sliced
1 (16 oz) pkg all beef hot dogs
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Cook lentils according to package directions; then add tomatoes, beef broth and vinegar. Bring to a boil, then lower to a simmer.
Chop celery and carrots; add during last 45 minutes of cooking.
Cut hot dogs into 1/2" pieces; add during the last 15 minutes of cooking. Season to taste.

Submitter: 
Jessica Paige
Description: 
I like that my Mom uses hot dogs to flavor her lentil soup rather than ham, etc...
