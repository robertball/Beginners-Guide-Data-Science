Title:
Fresh Strawberry Lemon Oat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/676555.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup quick oats
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup liquid honey
1/3 cup canola oil
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup sour cream
1 egg
zest of one small lemon
1 cup diced fresh strawberries

Directions:
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan or line with paper liners. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the honey, oil, extract, sour cream, egg and lemon zest. Add in strawberries.
Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir just to combine. Divide mixture evenly among muffin cups.
Bake for 20 minutes or until edges are browning and tops are springy.
Allow to cool in pan 5 minutes, then remove to wire rack to cool completely.
