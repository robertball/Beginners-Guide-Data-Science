Title:
Crock Pot Italian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. chicken breasts
1 12 oz. jar banana pepper rings
1 packet dry Italian seasoning mix

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker.  Sprinkle with Italian seasoning mix.  Pour over banana pepper rings with juice.  Cook on low for 6 hours.
Remove chicken from juice and shred.  Return to juice in crock.  Serve warm on rolls with provolone cheese.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Simple, tangy Italian seasoned chicken prepared in the slow cooker.
