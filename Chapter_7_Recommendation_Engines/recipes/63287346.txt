Title:
Judy's Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound Hamburger
1/4 Cup Oatmeal
1 Egg
1 TBSP Worcestershire Sauce
1 TBSP A1 Sauce
2 TBSP Ketchup

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a medium sized bowl. Shape into patties of desired size. Cook on a griddle at 300 degrees for 5-8 minutes on each side or until done.

Submitter: 
Hope Clayton
Description: 
Every year my family takes an awesome beach vacation.  There are 9 of us squashed into a 2 bedroom condo and we are hungry!  We love burgers.  This is my grandma's recipe. I love vacations with my family, especially my grandma and her super awesome burgers!!
