Title:
Italian BBQ Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451550.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup balsamic vinegar
2/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
4 pork loin chops (about 1.5 lbs)
Salt and freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Combine all the BBQ ingredients (balsamic through black pepper) in a small saucepan. Stir until well incorporated and simmer on medium-low heat for 20 minutes. Remove from heat and allow to sit for 5 minutes.
Preheat grill (or grill pan) to medium heat.
Season both sides of pork chops with salt and pepper. Spread the pork chops lightly with the BBQ sauce. Reserve the rest.
Grill the pork for 5 minutes on each side. Remove from grill and allow to rest for 3 minutes.
Serve with the extra BBQ sauce alongside.
