Title:
Big Ray's Polish Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032566.jpg
Ingredients:
9  lasagna noodles, uncooked
4 cups  hot mashed potatoes
1/2 cup  (1/2 of 8-oz. pkg) Cream Cheese
1 large onion, diced
8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled,divided
2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese, divided
Sour Cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 375Â°F.
Cook noodles as directed on package, omitting salt.
Cook the bacon until crisp, remove and crumble saving grease.  Cook diced onions in bacon grease until limp and slightly brown.
Combine potatoes, cream cheese, 1/2 the bacon, 1/2 the onions and 1 cup cheddar.
Place 3 noodles in 13" x 9"-inch baking dish sprayed with cooking spray; cover with 1/3 of the potato mixture. Repeat layers twice. Top with remaining bacon, onions and Cheddar; cover.
Bake 30 min. or until heated through. Uncover; bake 3 to 5 min. or until cheese is lightly browned. Let stand 10 min. before cutting to serve.
Serve with sour cream.
