Title:
Pineapple Turnovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Cups Flour
2 Tbs Sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
2 Cups Shortening
2 Pkg yeast
1 Cup Milk
3 Eggs
Filling:
2 15 oz cans crushed pineapple
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 Tbs flour

Directions:
Dough:
Sift flour with salt, sugar then cut in shortening, add yeast.  Add eggs & milk little at a time.  Mix with dry ingredients until it forms a ball.  Cover & put in refrigerator overnight.
Filling:
Put pineapple in sauce pan & add flour, sugar & eggs & cook until thick stirring constantly.
Next morning put sugar and roll dough thin.  Cut in 4 inch squares.  Put teaspoon filling in corner of 4 inch dough & fold over & use fork on edges.  Bake 425 for 15 minutes.  Put a little flour on rolling pin to prevent sticking.
