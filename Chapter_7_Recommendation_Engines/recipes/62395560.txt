Title:
Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. shortening
1/3 c. plus 4 t. water
1 1/4 t. vanilla extract
5 c. powdered sugar
1 T. plus 3/4 t meringue powder

Directions:
Using KA stand mixer, combine all ingredients.
Beat all ingredients on High until frosting is fluffy.
Frost cupcakes.  Top with sprinkles.
