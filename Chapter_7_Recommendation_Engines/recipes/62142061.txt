Title:
Roasted Turkey Criolla Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade Ingredients:
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup olive oil
8 garlic cloves
1 grated orange rind
1 tablespoon salt
1 teaspoon black peppercorns
1 teaspoon chopped fresh oregano
1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup fresh orange juice
Turkey Cavity Ingredients:
1 cinnamon stick
1/2 an orange
1 bay leaf
1 small onion spiked with 2 whole cloves

Directions:
Mix Marinade Ingrients in food processor or blender until well mixed.
Place turkey cavity ingredients inside turkey.
Place turkey in an oven bag that has been prepared as instructed by manufacturer.
Loosen skin from turkey and pour marinade between the skin and turkey meat.
Seal oven bag and bake per oven bag instructions for the weight of the turkey
