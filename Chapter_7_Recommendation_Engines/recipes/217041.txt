Title:
Dannon Tropical Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990897.jpg
Ingredients:
1 kiwi, sliced
1 banana, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup blueberries
1 cup strawberries
1 cup ice cubes
1 cup orange juice
8 ounces Dannon® All Natural plain yogurt

Directions:
Place kiwi, banana, orange juice, ice, Dannon All Natural, blueberries, strawberries in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Dannon® All Natural
Description: 
Blend some super fruits--kiwi, banana, blueberries, strawberries--with orange juice and creamy yogurt for a great way to start the day.
