Title:
Green Onion Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese
1 pkg ranch dressing mix
8oz sour cream
1/2 c. mayo
6 bunches of green onions (diced)
to spice it up.. 1 fresh chopped jalapeno

Directions:
In mixer, add cream cheese, sour cream and mayo. Blend til smooth.
Add ranch mix while still blending then add green onions and the optional jalapeno.
For best flavor results, let sit overnight in fridge.
Serve with corn chips, potato chips or even butter crackers.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
Being my creative self, I made this for an office party years ago. It turned out so great that when I moved from Austin, one of my co-workers emailed me for the recipes so she could still make it for the group!
