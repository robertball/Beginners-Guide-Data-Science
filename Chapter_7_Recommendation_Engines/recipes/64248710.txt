Title:
Creamy berry smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1772666.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup heavy whipping cream
1 chopped banana
1 cup of crushed ice
1 cup strawberries diced
½ cup raspberries
½ cup blueberries
½ teaspoon of brown sugar
½ cup almond milk
1 tbsp.. Of chia seeds-optional

Directions:
pour all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.  Pour into glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Priscilla Akrong
Description: 
The heavy whipping cream and brown sugar made a big difference in my usual berry smoothie.  Try it out!
