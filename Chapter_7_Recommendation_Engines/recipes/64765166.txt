Title:
Pork Loin Roast with Peaches Carolyn Plummer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boneless pork loin roast (3 lbs tied)
1/4 t onion salt
1/4 t black pepper
1 can sliced peaches in heavy syrup   LAST HOUR
1/2 C red enchilada sauce
1/3  C brown sugar
3 T apple cider vinegar
1 t pumpkin pie spice
1 T corn starch mixed w 2 T water
Cooked egg noodles

Directions:
Coat a 6 Qt slow cooker w non stick spray, place roast in with seasonings
Drain peaches reserve the syrup. In a bowl whisk syrup, enchilada sauce, br sugar, vinegar, pumpkin pie spice and pour over meat. Scatter peaches over the roast.
Cook 3 hr on high or 6 on low.
Remove meat. Allow to rest 10 minutes. Spoon out peaches and reserve. Place liquid in a small sauce pan and  over high heat stir in corn starch. Cook 30 seconds until bubbly and thick.
To serve place meat on a serving platter, place egg noodles on the side
Place reserved peach slices on the roast like (((((((. Serve sauce and rest of noodles in another platter.
