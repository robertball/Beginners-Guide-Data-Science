Title:
Northern N.Y. Michigan Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068143.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
4 teaspoons chili powder
2 teaspoons cumin powder
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
2 teaspoons dried onion flakes
2 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 teaspoon hot sauce
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Mix tomato sauce, hot sauce and seasonings and green bell pepper in a large saucepan.
Add ground beef (pulled apart)
Cook on medium heat for 30 minutes until beef is browned, then simmer on low for 2-3 hours.
sauce should be thick,Serve Michican Sauce over steamed or boiled hot dog (Tobins,Glaziers,Nathans) on a steamed or toughened bun and top with chopped raw onions and optional strip of yellow mustard.
