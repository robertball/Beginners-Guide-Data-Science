Title:
Pasty(s) - Coal Miners Meat Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
1 tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoon salt
1 & 3/4 cups shortening, cold from refrigerator if possible
one large egg
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1/2 cup ice water
2 pounds uncooked ground beef
2 medium carrots
2 potatoes
1 onion
salt
pepper

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar and salt.  Place in food processor cut in 1 & 3/4 cups shortening, egg, vinegar and 1/2 cup ice water.  Place dough in refrigerator.
Meanwhile chop small or fine dice the carrots, potatoes and onion.
Combine uncooked ground beef with the carrots, potatoes,, onion, salt and pepper (to taste).
Roll out pastry and cut into large desired size circles.  Fill with meat mixture, fold over, close open edges with a fork, put slits with a knife on top.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Great to bake and store in refrigerator.  Take out to take for lunch & eat at room temperature.
