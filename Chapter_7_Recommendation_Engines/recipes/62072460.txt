Title:
Walla Walla Onion Asparagus and Roasted Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups firm yellow sweet corn (about 3 ears), roasted on the ear on the BBQ and cut from cobb.
1 1/2 cups grilled or sauted     asparagus, crisp tender, slice in 1/2 inch bite size pieces.
3/4  cup chopped spring walla walla sweet onion (or regular Walla Walla)
Dressing:
1/4 cup Olive Oil
juice of 1 lime
1 tsp. roasted garlic flavor seasoned rice vinegar
1 tsp lawry's seasoned salt
1 tsp ground cummin
1 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Grill and prepared corn, cut from ears and put in 2 quart serving bowl.
Add asparagus, either grilled or sauted as well as chopped onion.
Prepare dressing by combining all ingredients and add to vegetable mixture.  Chill salad thoroughly and serve.

Submitter: 
Fooddude
Description: 
A delicious late spring salad that can be prepared with leftover corn and asparagus from the barbeque the night before.
