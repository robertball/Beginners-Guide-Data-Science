Title:
Turkey Pepperoni Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbls coconut oil
1/2 cup each
diced carrots
diced sweet onions
sliced mushrooms
diced yellow pepper
2 cloves diced garlic
2 tbls chopped basil
4 diced turkey pepperettes
2 cups vegetable stock
1 cup gluten free rigatoni
2 cups diced canned tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a medium pot
Add all veg including garlic but not tomatoes
Simmer for 7 minutes on medium low heat
Add broth and noodles and turn heat up to high
Once boiling, turn heat to medium, simmer for 15 minutes or until noodles are tender stirring often (keep just covered in broth, adding more if necessary)
Add tomatoes and salt and pepper to taste. Cook another couple minutes until thoroughly heated.
