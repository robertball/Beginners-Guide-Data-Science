Title:
Reese's Peanut Butter Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/721300.jpg
Ingredients:
1 11.5 Ounce Bag Milk Chocolate Chips
1/2 Cup Creamy Peanut Butter
8 .55 Ounce Reese's Cups (full size)
2 Cups Chow Mein Noodles

Directions:
1.) In a glass bowl, melt milk chocolate chips at 30 second increments until melted.
2.) Stir in peanut butter until well mixed and smooth.
3.) Finely chop Reese's Cups and add into the chocolate mixture
4.) Add chow mein noodles and mix together until evenly coated.
5.) On wax paper, drop 2 Tablespoons at a time and refrigerate until set.

Submitter:

Description: 
A rich peanut buttery twist on the classic haystack.
