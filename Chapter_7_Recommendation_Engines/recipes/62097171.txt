Title:
Guinness Shepards pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 baking potatoes, boiled and mashed
butter
milk
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 2lb pack of ground sirloin
3 celery ribs, finely chopped
3 carrots finely chopped
1 large white onion, finely chopped
4 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tbsp basil, minced
1 tbsp oregano, dried or freshly minced
salt
pepper
1 pack beef stew mix
1 can sweet peas, drained
1 can diced tomatoes, drained
olive oil
1 can guinness

Directions:
Prepare vegetables as stated above.
When preparing potatoes, mix with milk and butter, salt and pepper to taste. Garlic can be added to potatoes when cooking for additional flavor.
Cook meat in deep, wide, pan with herbs, onion,carrot, celery and beef stew mix. Sautee until beef is browned. Add peas, tomatoes, salt and pepper to taste and continue to sautee until veggies are semi-soft. Add can of guinness to mix, cover and let cook for 10 minutes.
Transfer meat mixture to casserole dish. Mix cheese with mashed potatoes.
Cover meat mixture with mashed potatoes, set in preheated 425 degree oven uncovered, cook until potatoes begin to crisp and brown on top. Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream
