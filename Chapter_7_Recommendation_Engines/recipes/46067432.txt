Title:
Ivy's Fudge Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
3 T. cream or milk
2 T butter
1 cup sugar
2 oz. unsweetened chocolate

Directions:
Beat egg until light and fluffy.  Add it and all other ingredients to a small saucepan, and heat over medium-high heat until it comes to a good boil, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat, and beat with a wooden spoon until smooth and glossy.  Spread over sheet cake.

Submitter: 
Jill
Description: 
This is my grandmother's recipe for a thick, fudgy frosting that goes great on a yellow cake.  Makes enough to cover a 9x13 sheet cake.
