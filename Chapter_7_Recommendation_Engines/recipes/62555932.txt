Title:
Minnesota Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
1/2 tsp. salt
1 T. Italian seasoning
1 c. uncooked wild rice
1/2 tsp. pepper
4 ribs celery, sliced
2 large onions, sliced
1 c. water
3 drops Tabasco sauce
2 tsp. instant beef bouillon granules
3 cans cream of mushroom soup
2 soup cans water

Directions:
In a  heavy soup kettle, brown the beef with the salt and Italian seasoning, crumbling the meat as it cooks. Add the wild rice and water, Tabasco, bouillon granules, pepper, celery and onion and simmer, covered, about 30 min. Stir in the soup and water. Cover and simmer another 30 min.
