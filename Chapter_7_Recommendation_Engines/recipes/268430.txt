Title:
Biscoff® Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6306088.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon cookie butter (such as Biscoff®)
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup coffee

Directions:
Heat milk and brown sugar in a small saucepan until frothy, close to simmering. Reduce heat to low. Whisk in cookie butter and cinnamon, then mix in coffee.

Description: 
A latte made with Biscoff® cookie butter and cinnamon.
