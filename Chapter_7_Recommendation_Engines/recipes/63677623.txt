Title:
Parsley Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c cooked brown rice
1 bunch fresh parsley, rinsed and chopped
dried savory
salt
garlic mill
olive oil

Directions:
Microwave the parsley for 3 minutes.
Combine with rice.  Stir well.
Add savory, salt, garlic, and olive oil to taste.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
A fresh-tasting rice dish that uses parsley as more of a vegetable than an herb.
