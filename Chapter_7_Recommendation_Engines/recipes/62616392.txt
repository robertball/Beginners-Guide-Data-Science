Title:
Potato/Pinto Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med    onion
1 cup    mushrooms
2 teasp    olive oil
5 lrg    potatoes, peeled and sliced
2 cans    vegetable broth
15 oz    pinto beans, drained
½ cup    buttermilk
1 tbsp    cornstarch
¼ cup    sour cream

Directions:
Use food processor to shop mushrooms and onions.  Cook in oil until onion is tender.  Add potatoes and broth.  bring to a boil.  Reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Stir in beans.
In small bowl stir together buttermilk and cornstarch.  Stir into potato mixture.  Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.
Salt and pepper to taste.  Serve with dollop of sour cream.
