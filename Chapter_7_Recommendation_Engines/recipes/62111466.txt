Title:
Sweet Cornbread Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. cornmeal
3 C. flour
1 1/3 C. sugar
2 T. baking powder (make sure to use fresh)
1 t. salt
2/3 C. vegetable oil
6 T. melted butter
2 T. honey
4 eggs, beaten
2 1/2 C. milk

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
Then add the oil, butter, honey, eggs, and milk.
Mix well and pour into a greased 9x13 in. pan.
Bake at 350 degrees about 30 minutes or until the top of the cornbread starts to brown and show cracks.

Submitter: 
myfoursonsks
Description: 
The is a thick, sweet cornbread that tastes like you are eating cake.  This is a no-fail recipe.  You can't go wrong! If there are any leftovers, we have the cornbread the next day for breakfast served with honey and butter.
