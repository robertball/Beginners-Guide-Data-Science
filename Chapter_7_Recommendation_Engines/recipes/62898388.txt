Title:
Coconut Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702618.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
2 tbsn lime juice
5 cloves garlic
1 onion, quartered
1 inch ginger (remove skin)
4 Serrano peppers, remove stem (or to taste)
1 cup frozen shredded coconut, thawed
2 tsp unsalted, unsweetened peanut butter
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cumin powder
1/2 cup curry leaves
---
1 tbsn Peanut oil
1 tsp mustard seeds
3-4 curry leaves

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and puree. 2 or more minutes.
Heat oil and add mustard seeds, until mustard seeds pop.
Add 3-4 curry leaves to oil. 30-60 seconds. (they may sputter, especially if frozen)
Add puree to oil (it will sputter). Cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove the 3-4 curry leaves, and serve cooled.

Submitter: 
Sarah Garland
Description: 
This delicious chutney is made mostly in the food processor, and is a great side for Indian cuisine, or even chips.
