Title:
shrimp ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 tablespoons butter
2 tabelspoons all-purpose flour
1 can (14 1/2oz) Italian stewed tomatoes
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 bay leaf
1 whole clove
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 pkgs (9oz each) fresh or frozen cheese ravioli
1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
1/2 pound cooked med. shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 tablespoon parmesan cheese
1 tablespoons of minced chives

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute onion in butter.
Stir in flour till blended.
Bring mixture to a boil and stir till thickened.
Place tomatos in blender and pulse till pureed.
add to onion mixture.
Stir in the sugar, bay leaf, clove, basil, salt, and pepper.
bring to a boil.  Reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 15-20 minutes.
meanwhile cook ravioli according to package directions.
Remove and discard bay leaf from the sauce.  Reduce heat more, while gradually stiring in cream. Add shrimp and heat through.
Drain ravioli, and top with sauce cheese, and chives.
