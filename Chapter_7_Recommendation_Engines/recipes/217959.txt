Title:
Restaurant Style Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665845.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
1 (14.5 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes with chipotle
1/4 onion, coarsely chopped
1 clove garlic
1 lime, juiced
3 slices pickled jalapeno peppers, or to taste (optional)
1 bunch fresh cilantro leaves

Directions:
Place the canned tomatoes, onion, garlic, lime juice, and optional jalapenos into a blender. Cover, and blend until it reaches the desired consistency. Add cilantro and pulse until combined.

Submitter: 
Anna Doyle Lundy

This is a salsa like you would get at a Mexican food restaurant, and is perfect with thin tortilla chips...
