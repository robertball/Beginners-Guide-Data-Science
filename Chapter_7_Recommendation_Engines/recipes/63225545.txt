Title:
Family Style Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbl flour
1 large cooking bag
1 (14 1/2) cqn original style stewed tomatoes
10 1/2 oz condensed beef broth
1 med onion, sliced
1/2 tsp pepper
1-2 lb boneless round or chuck steak
carrots/potatoes

Directions:
Place flour in cooking bag. twist end & shake to coat inside. Place bag in 13 x 9 pan. add tomatoes, broth, onion & pepper. squeeze bag to blend ingredients.
Cut steak into serving size pieces & place in bag in single layer. Place carrots & potatoes in bag around steak. Close bag with tie. Cut 6 slits in top
Bake 1 hr 15 min until tender.
