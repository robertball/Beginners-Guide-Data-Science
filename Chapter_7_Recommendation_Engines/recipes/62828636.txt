Black bean soup Recipe

1 1/2 cup chicken broth
1tbsp. Cumin

10 pickled jalape√±o rings

Directions:
Drain and rinse beans. Reserve 1 cup beans. Blend remaining beans, cumin, pico de gallo, cilantro, chicken broth, jalapenos and garlic powder in blender until combined. Mix with remaining black beans over medium heat. Bring to simmer for 10 minutes. Top with pico de gallo and sour cream. Add Tabasco for additional heat. Enjoy!
