Title:
Broiled Lemon Pepper & Paprika Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinnless chicken breasts
1/2 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, mashed and chopped fine
2 Tbs. plus 1/2 Tbs. extra virgin olive oil
2 Tbs paprika
1 Tbs lemon pepper
1 Tbs salt

Directions:
Preheat oven on broil setting.
Use 1/2 Tbs. of oilve oil to grease broiler pan.
Rinse and pat dry chicken breasts.
Brush with olive oil, coating each piece.
Sprinkle each peice of chicken evenly on both sides with lemon pepper, paprika and salt.
Place chicken breasts on the broiler pan.
Sprinkle minced garlic over the top of the chicken.
Sprinkle onions around the base of the chicken, with several smaller peices on the breasts.
Broil for 10-15 minutes, checking often and flipping the breasts after 5 minutes. Check for doneness before serving.
Serve the chicken ontop of the broiled onions.
This recipe can also be carried out by pan-frying the chicken with the onions and garlic. Enjoy!
