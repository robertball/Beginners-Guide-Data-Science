Title:
Wrapping Skin for Egg Rolls and Won Tons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 cups flour
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1 egg
* 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Sift the flour and salt together. Beat the egg and water together and mix well. Blend thoroughly with the flour and salt mixture; knead dough until it is smooth.
Roll dough on floured board until paper thin. Be sure board is well dusted with flour.
For egg rolls, make about 18 6-inch square wrappings. If won ton wrappings are desired, cut each 6-inch square into four pieces.
