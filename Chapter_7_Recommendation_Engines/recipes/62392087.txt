Title:
Dill Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4469757.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lb. chicken
1 16-oz. carton sour cream
1 envelope dry onion soup mix
1 tsp. lemon juice
2 Tbsp. dill weed
2 10.75oz cans cream of mushroom soup
pepper to taste

Directions:
Place chicken in crock pot. Combine all other ingredients in a bowl and pour over chicken. That's it!
