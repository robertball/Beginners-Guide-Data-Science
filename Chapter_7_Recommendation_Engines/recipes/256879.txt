Title:
Fresh Mozzarella Stuffed Cherry Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4462956.jpg
Ingredients:
5 ounces BelGioioso Fresh Mozzarella Ciliegine cheese
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
Salt and pepper to taste
1 (16 ounce) jar pickled sweet cherry peppers or peppadew peppers

Directions:
Toss BelGioioso Fresh Mozzarella Ciliegine with olive oil, basil, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper.
Slice off pepper stem and stuff with a ball of Fresh Mozzarella.

Submitter: 
BelGioioso Cheese
Description: 
Sweet cherry peppers are stuffed with marinated fresh mozzarella bites for a quick and delicious appetizer.
