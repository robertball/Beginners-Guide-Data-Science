Title:
Elle's Grapefruit salad with raspberry dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 grapefruit
1/4head of lettuce
1/4 cup raisens and/or prunes
1 tsp brown sugar(optional)
(dressing)
15 raspberries
1 tsp honey
1/4 cup water

Directions:
(salad)
peel and segment grapefruit. Rinse and break up the lettuce into small pieces. mix together. sprinkle brown sugar over the top. Toss and enjoy!
(dressing)
muddle the raspberries in a muddler. add the water and honey.

Submitter: 
Elle Mcgrath
Description: 
This is a refreshing salad for a hot summer day. It is so simple, too.
