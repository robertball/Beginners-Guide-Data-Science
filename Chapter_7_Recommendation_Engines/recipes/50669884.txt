Title:
Creamy Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin
ground black pepper to taste
1 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of potato soup
garlic salt
carrots

Directions:
Season pork tenderloin with seasonings, and place in slow cooker. Pour undiluted  soups onto tenderloin, covering meat completely.
Cover, and cook on Low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Dawn Norby
Description: 
A very tasty and easy slow cooker pork tenderloin using cream of celery that goes well over rice.Modified from: Easy Creamy Pork Tenderloin
