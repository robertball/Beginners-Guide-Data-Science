Title:
Shrimp and Pasta Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. olive oil
1/4 cup chopped onion
3 cloves of fresh garlic, chopped
1 (28-oz) can of your favorite crushed tomatoes
1 tsp. dried basil
1/4 tsp. salt
Dash of black pepper
8 oz of your favorite pasta, (I prefer angel hair or penne)
1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined
Fresh Parsley to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add onion.
Cook until soft, abt 3 min.
Stir in garlic, cook abt 30 seconds.
Add tomatoes, basil, salt and pepper; bring to a boil
Reduce heat; simmer about 20 minutes.
Cook pasta according to directions on the box.
Gently stir shrimp into tomato sauce.
Cook for about 3 min or until shrimp turn pink.
Drain pasta; transfer to serving bowl.
Top pasta with the shrimp marinara and garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
Helene Rose-Carson
Description: 
A wonderful dish of tasty shrimp in marinara sauce over your favorite pasta. Serve with a big salad and some crusty bread on the side. What I like best about this dish is that it is Heart-Healthy.
