Title:
Turkey And Mushroom Croustades Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 slices white sandwich bread, crusts removed
1/4 cup butter
1 Tbsp melted butter
1 small onion, finely diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup finely chopped mushrooms
2 -  6 oz. can flaked turkey, drained and flaked with fork
3 Tbsp flour
1 cup milk
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp cayenne
freshly ground pepper
1 tsp lemon juice
chopped parsely

Directions:
CROUSTADES: Brush both sides of bread with 1/4 cup melted butter and press gently into muffin tins.
Bake in 350F oven for 15 - 17 minutes, until golden.
FILLING: Melt 1 Tbsp butter in a medium saucepan; saute onion, garlic and mushrooms until onion is transparent.
Stir in turkey.
Sprinkle mixture with flour and blend well.
Add milk gradually; continue heating and stirring until mixture thickens and comes to a boil.
Remove from heat and stir in salt, cayenne, pepper and lemon juice.
Fill each croustade with filling.
Place on a cookie sheet and heat in 350F oven for 10 minutes, until bubbly.
Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
Serve hot.
