Title:
Spinach and Strawberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
4 cups sliced strawberries
(use egg slicer!)
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 teaspoon paprika
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together the spinach and strawberries.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, sugar, paprika, sesame seeds, and poppy seeds. Pour over the spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
You can toss the spinach and straberries ahead of time and put in frige, but LEAVE the dressing on the counter and add immediately before serving (do not put dressing in frige)

Submitter: 
Diane Manzella
Description: 
This salad has lots of spinach, lots of fresh, sliced strawberries, and a delicious, sweetened oil and vinegar dressing spiked with poppy and sesame seeds.Modified from: Spinach and Strawberry Salad
