Title:
Roasted Red Pepper-Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (14.5-ounce) cans no salt added diced tomatoes, 3 cloves garlic, crushed, 3/4 teaspoon salt, 1 cup of carrots sliced, 1 stalk of celery sliced, 1 medium baking potato cubed, 1 roasted red pepper (from bottled peppers), 2 cups chicken broth, 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper, and 1/8 cup heavy whipping cream.

Directions:
1.  In large saucepan, combine all ingrediants except heavy whipping cream.  Bring to boiling, reduce heat.  Cover and cook for 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
2.  In food processer bowl or blender, carefully puree the tomato mixture a half at a time until smooth.  Return to saucepan, add heavy whipping cream.  Cook and stir until heated through.

A soup that is easy to make with good flavor and a good kick to it.
