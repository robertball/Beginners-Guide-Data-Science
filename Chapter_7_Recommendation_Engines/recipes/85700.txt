Title:
Chi-Chi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982722.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI® Mixers Pina Colada frozen mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
12 ounces crushed pineapple
5 cups ice

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, pineapple and BACARDI(R) Rum.
Add ice and blend for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish each guest's drink differently. Use stirrers, crazy straws, and umbrellas.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Go wild with your garnishes in this fabulous extra pineapple pina colada. Use stirrers, crazy straws, and umbrellas.
