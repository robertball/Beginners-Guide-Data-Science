Title:
Fruity Curry Chicken Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cooked and diced
1 stalk celery, diced
4 green onions, chopped
1 Golden Delicious apple - peeled, cored and diced
1/3 cup craisins
1/3 cup seedless green grapes, halved
1/2 cup chopped toasted pecans
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 teaspoons curry powder
1/2 cup light mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream or plain yogurt

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the chicken, celery, onion,  pecans,(lightly toast pecans if raw) pepper, curry powder, mayonnaise & sour cream. Mix all together.
Add grapes, craisins, & apple 2-4 hours before serving.
Serve!
