Title:
Chicken salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Cans of Chicken
1 Green pepper chopped
1 Bunch green onions chopped
1 Can water chestnuts chopped
8oz cream cheese softened
1 C. sourcream
1 package knorr veggie saesoning
1 C. Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 French bread loaf

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix first 7 ingredients in large mixing bowl. Set aside.
Cut french loaf into 12 pieces. And place on cookie sheet. Place chicken salad on the bread. Then top with Cheddar Cheese. Bake for 20 minutes.
