Title:
Pepperoni Pizza dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 pepperoni sausage, diced
1/2 cup green onions, diced
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/2 cup shredded mozzerella

Directions:
Prehead 350, beat cream cheese, sour cream, oregano, garlic & red pepper. Spread in 9 in pie pan, spread pizza sauce over top. sprinkle with pepperoni, onions and peppers. bake at 350 for 10 min. Add cheese, bake for five min more. serve with crackers.
