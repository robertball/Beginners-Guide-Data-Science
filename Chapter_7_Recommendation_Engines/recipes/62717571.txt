Title:
Mixed Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. bag frozen mixed vegetables
1 small onion, diced
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
3/4 cup light Hellman's mayonnaise
1 cup shredded white Cheddar cheese
15 buttery round crackers, crushed
2 tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl combine the mixed vegetables, onion, soup, mayonnaise and cheese.  Mix well and spread mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.  Top with the cracker crumbs. Pour melted butter on top of cracker crumbs.
Bake about 30 minutes, or until browned and bubbly.
