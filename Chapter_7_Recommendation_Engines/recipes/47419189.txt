Title:
Mexican Layered Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans refried beans
3 ripe avocados, chopped
2 Tbl. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 bunch green onions, chopped fine
3 tomatoes, chopped
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 package taco seasoning
Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Spread refried beans in bottom of serving dish
Combine avocados, lemon juice, salt and pepper.  Spread over refried beans.
Combine green onions and tomatoes.  Layer over avocado mixture.
Combine sour cream, mayonnaise and taco seasoning.  Spread over tomato mixture.
Top with cheddar cheese.
