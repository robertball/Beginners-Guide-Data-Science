Title:
Vegan Ricotta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7282624.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package tofu
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Pulse tofu, olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, and basil together in a food processor until crumbly and evenly mixed.

Submitter: 
chyena
Description: 
Tofu substitute for ricotta in lasagna dish.
