Title:
Swedish Vegetable Salad with Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 ounce package of frozen peas and carrots
1 15 ounce can white asparagus spears, drained
1 red apple, unpeeled
1/2 pound small cooked shrimp
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 t. Dijon mustard
1/2 t. dried dill weed
1/2 t. sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook one 10 oz package of mixed peas and carrots according to package directions- chill
Dice the apple into small pieces
Chop the asparagus into bite sized pieces
Add peas and carrots, apple, asparagus and shrimp to serving bowl
Combine mayonnaise, dijon mustard, dill weed, sugar, salt and pepper in a small bowl.
Add the mayonnaise mixture to the vegetable shrimp mixture.
Stir gently to combine.
Served chilled

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
My Mom's yummy side dish recipe. Please adjust the seasonings to your own taste. If you cannot find the white asparagus, the green works fine. She also advises NOT to use low fat or fat free mayo. I make this a day in advance to let flavors marry and keep cold.
