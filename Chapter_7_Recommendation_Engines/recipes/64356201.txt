Title:
slow cooker adobo chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2LB Bonless skinless chicken thighs
1 large yellow onion
2 cloves of garlic
1 can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
1 tsp cumin
1 12oz can sierra mist
1 15oz can diced tomatoes

Directions:
dice onion, add all other ingredients to slow cooker, cook for 6 to 8 hours, serve with rice
