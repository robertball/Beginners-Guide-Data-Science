Title:
Quick and Easy French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5934345.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Egg
1 Teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon, Ground
1/2 Teaspoon Nutmeg
1/4 Cup Milk
4 Slices Bread

Directions:
Whisk egg, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a shallow dish. Stir in Milk.
Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
Cook bread slices on lightly greased/butter nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with warmed syrup and whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Celestial
Description: 
Vanilla extract and cinnamon bring a richness of flavour to French Toast. Pick your favourite bread - white, Italian, French, or whole wheat. Serve with warmed syrup and whip cream.
