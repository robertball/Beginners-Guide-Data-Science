Title:
Easy Rib Eye Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Pound Rib Eye Roast Bone-In
1 Cup Softened Butter
6 Cloves Garlic Minced
Sea Salt
Cracked Pepper

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 500 Degrees.
Combine butter and Garlic.
Poke slits in rib roasts and rub butter/garlic mixture generously on all sides of the roast. Season with Cracked Pepper & Sea Salt.
Place fat side up in roasting pan.
Place in oven. Cook 20 minutes and then reduce to 325 Degrees until cooked to your likeness using a meat themometer.
