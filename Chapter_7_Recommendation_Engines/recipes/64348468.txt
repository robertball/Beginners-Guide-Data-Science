Title:
Meatball Sub Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 egg
1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp pepper
4-6 slices frozen garlic bread
4 oz cream cheese, softened
1 Tbsp mayo
1/4 tsp Italian seasoning
1 jar spaghetti sauce
1 cup mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400. Combine beef, egg, bread crumbs, onion, celery salt, garlic powder, Italian seasoning and pepper. Roll into 1 1/2 inch balls; bake for 20 minutes. Bake garlic bread 6-8 minutes, until nicely browned. Combine cream cheese, mayo and Italian seasoning. Place garlic bread in 2 qt casserole dish. Spread cream cheese mixture on top. Slice meatballs in half, arrange on top of cream cheese. Pour sauce over top and sprinkle with cheese. Bake 30 minutes or until cheese is hot and bubbly.
