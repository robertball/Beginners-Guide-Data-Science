Title:
Pumpkin Scones with a Ginger Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/916707.jpg
Ingredients:
Scones:
3 cups Flour
1/3 cup Packed Brown Sugar
3 tsp Baking Powder
1 tsp Baking Soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp Allspice
1 stick chilled Butter
1/4 cup Pumpkin- canned
3/4 cup buttermilk
Glaze:
3/4 cup Powdered Sugar
1-2 Tbsp milk
1/2 tsp Cinnamon (or less to taste)
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp Ground Ginger (more or less to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Lightly mix all dry ingredients and flour
Pastry blend butter, flour and all dry ingredients until the butter is about the size of a pea. (dough will be very crumbly and dry)
Add buttermilk and pumpkin and stir until pumpkin is evenly distributed
Form the dough into two rounds
On floured board, roll each round to 1/2 inch thick, fold a couple of times then roll again.  Repeat
Cut into serving wedges and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 425 for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned.
Move immediately to a cooling rack after baking

Description: 
These delicious pumpkin scones are a holiday favorite in our home.  They use pumpkin dough seasoned with Fall spices then drizzled with a ginger spice glaze. Enjoy!Modified from: Pumpkin Scones with Ginger Spice Glaze
