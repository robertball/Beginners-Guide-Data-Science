Title:
Rhonda's Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-LB pasta(I used corkscrew, shell and Penne mixed to equal a pound!
12-oz chunk micelli mozerella cut in 1/2 in squares
6-oz bridgeport italian salami sliced and cut into strips
1- 10oz can of drained sliced back olives
3-green onions sliced
2-ribs of celery chopped
1-Red bell pepper seeded and cut into bite size
pieces
1/2-cup shaved parmesan cheese
1-teas.each of dried oregano, basil & parsely
1-Tbsp lemon zest
1-cup dressing(ingredients: 1/4 cup lemon juice, 1 lg. garlic clove, salt and pepper, 2Tbsp. mayonaise, 2/3cup olive oil )

Directions:
Add 2 Tbsp salt to boiling water. Boil pasta till just tender. Drain, do not rinse, place on a lg lipped cookie sheet to cool and dry
Cut up cheese, pepper, onions, celery and salami. Place in a large bowl. Add herbs, parmesan cheese, black olives, lemon zest and mix.
Dressing: Put all dressing ingredients in food processor and process well.
Combine pasta and dressing  with cut up ingredients in the large bowl. Mix well and serve.
