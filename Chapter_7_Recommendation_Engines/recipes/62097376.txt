Title:
Olive Bar Marinated Veggie Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz dry spinach and riccota tortelini
2 bags prepared salad greens, such as romaine
2 roma tomato, sliced
marinated olives
marinated artichokes
marinated mushrooms
marinated roasted red pepper
Marinated or pickled green beans
marinated mozzarella balls, about 10, halved
4 oz shredded parmesean cheese
Purchased dressing, such as italian or balsamic

Directions:
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and add tortelini.  Cook until tender, about 8-10 minutes.  Rinse in cold water and place in the refrigerator while preparing the rest of the salad.
Place the greens in a large serving bowl.  Slice the red pepper into strips, and add all of the marinated vegetables, cheese and tomato.  Add the cold pasta.  Sprinkle with shredded parmesean, and toss with your favorite italian or balsamic dressing before serving.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
Busy cooks will love this recipe for a tasty salad with ingredients purchased right off your groceries olive bar, or found jarred in the italian foods section.  I love to take this to pot lucks or serve with homemade pizza.  Add pepperoni or salami for a heartier version.
