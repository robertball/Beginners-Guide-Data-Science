Title:
Chicken Almond Baguette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups cooked, chopped chicken breast meat
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 stalk celery, chopped
1/2 cup slivered almonds, toasted*
1/2 teaspoon onion salt
1 (8 ounce) loaf baguette
4 leaves lettuce leaves
2 tomatoes, thinly sliced
1 (6 ounce) container avocado dip
1/2 cup sliced green onions (optional)

Directions:
Mix chicken, mayonnaise, celery, almonds and onion salt. Cut baguette lengthwise in half.
Arrange lettuce on bottom half of baguette; top with tomato slices. Spread chicken mixture over tomatoes; top with avocado dip. Sprinkle with green onions. Top with remaining baguette half. Cut diagonally into 8 slices.
*To Toast Almonds: Bake uncovered in ungreased shallow pan in a 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden brown. Or cook in ungreased heavy skillet over medium low heat for 5 to 7 minutes, stirring frequently until browning begins, then stirring constantly until golden brown.
