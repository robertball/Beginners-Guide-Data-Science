Title:
Jungle Sherbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt mango nectar
1 small can of crushed pineapple
1/2 cup cream
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup peaches, pureed (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Pour into prepared ice cream maker and follow operating instructions.

Submitter: 
Ms. Munchie
Description: 
I miss Jungle Fruit Sherbet at Baskin Robbins, so this is my homemade version
