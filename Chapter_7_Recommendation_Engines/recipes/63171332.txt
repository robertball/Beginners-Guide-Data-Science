Title:
Feta Cheese and Shrimp Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, minced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 lbs. large shrimp, pealed and deveined
2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
1-28 oz. can chopped tomatoes
2 Tbsp. fresh oregano, chopped
2/3 cup reduced fat crumbled feta cheese
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°F. Heat olive oil in a large ovenproof skillet over medium heat. Add minced onion and cook for 2 mins., stirring often. Add garlic & cook an additional minute. Add tomatoes and their juice, bring to a boil. Cook until tomatoe juices thicken, about 5 minutes. Season w/pepper to taste.
Remove tomato sauce from heat and stir in shrimp. Sprinkle w/reduced fat feta cheese.
Bake until cheese melts and shrimp are cooked, about 10 minutes. Sprinkle chopped oregano & serve hot.
