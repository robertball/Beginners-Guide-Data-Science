Title:
Thanksgiving Leftover Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
refrigerator biscuit
leftover Stuffing
leftover mashed Potatoes
Leftover cubbed/ poeces of cooked Turkey
leftover turkey / chicken gravy ( to pour over baked muffins)
optional...teaspoon cranberry sauce

Directions:
take biscuit dough and flatten
in the center place all ingredients except gravy
fold over the dough and form into a ball
place in well greased muffin pan
Bake 350* 20-30 min
remove from oven and serve with hot gravy over

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Stuffing Potatoes Turkey in the center of refrigerator biscuit...folded up and baked in a muffin pan...gravy over the top ....video on FB 10-28-15 by Tym Bussanich
