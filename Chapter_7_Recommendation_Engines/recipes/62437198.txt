Title:
Sunny Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup  mayonnaise or reduced-fat mayonnaise dressing or salad dressing
1 small  onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup  raisins
1/4 to 1/3 cup  sugar (or agave nectar)
2 tablespoons  vinegar
7 cups  chopped fresh broccoli
1/2 cup  sunflower seeds
8 slices  bacon, crisp-cooked, drained and crumbled

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, onion, raisins, sugar, and vinegar.
Add broccoli and stir to coat.
Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or up to 24 hours.
Stir in sunflower seeds and bacon just before serving.
Makes 12 to 16 servings.
