Title:
Aunt Karen's Shredded Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. Chuck-eye Roast
3/4 cup water
1 envelope of onion soup mix

Directions:
1.  Place roast in slow cooker.
2.  Combine water and onion soup mix.
3.  Pour soup mixture over roast.
4.  Cook on high 4-6 hours.
5.  Pull apart with fork and serve on buns. Condiments optional.

Submitter:

Description: 
My aunt has made this for every family gathering for as long as I can remember.  I don't think she is allowed not to make it!  Yummy!
