Title:
Tracy's Penne Alla Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029570.jpg
Ingredients:
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 28oz can tomato puree
- 1 14oz can diced tomatoes, drained well
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup vodka
- 4 garlic cloves, sliced fine or minced
- 1/2 cup fresh parmesan cheese (shaved or grated)
- 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- sea salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 lb box penne pasta, cooked according to box directions

Directions:
In medium-sized sauce pot, melt butter over medium heat and then add in crushed red pepper and garlic, let simmer 1-2 minutes
Add vodka and cook 1 minute
Add can of tomato puree and drained can of diced tomatoes
Add Italian seasoning, salt and pepper, to taste, and stir for 1 minute
Stir in cream and cook for 1 minute while stirring
Reduce to low and simmer a few minutes longer, stirring occasionally
Serve over cooked penne pasta and top with fresh parmesan cheese! YUM!
