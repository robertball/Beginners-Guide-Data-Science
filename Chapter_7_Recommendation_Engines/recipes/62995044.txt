Title:
Creamy Cucumber Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg melba toast
2 small cucumbers, sliced
1 pkg cream cheese
1 pkg zesty italian dressing
Dill to garnish

Directions:
In a bowl, mix cream cheese and dressing together. Butter the melba toast with mixture. Add cucumber slice on top and sprinkle with dill to garnish.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
Refreshing and delicious appetizer. I've had friends make this with bottled zesty italian dressing and the dry packaged dressing. People love it either way.
