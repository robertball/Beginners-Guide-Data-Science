Title:
Chili Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz pks cream cheese softened
2 16 oz cans refried beans
2 16 oz cans chili with NO BEANS
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spread the cream cheese in the bottom of a 2 qt casserole, next spread the refried beans over the cream cheese, next spread the chili with no beans over the refried beans, then sprinkle the cheese over top. Bake in a 350 degree preheated oven for 30 minutes or until brown and bubbly. Serve with nacho chips.
