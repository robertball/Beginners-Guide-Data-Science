Title:
Mom's ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork back or side ribs
Garlic
1 can tomato soup
Garlic glove
4 drops Tabasco
2 tbp brown sugar
1/2 cup ketsup
1 tsp englsh mustard
1/2 tsp sage
2 tbsp vinegar

Directions:
Cut ribs and simmer in 1 cup of water, garlic and S&P for 45-60min. Drain well.
Mix all other ingredients.
Bake ribs on Cooke sheet coating with half sauce for 45min total. Flip ribs every 15 min and add more sauce.
