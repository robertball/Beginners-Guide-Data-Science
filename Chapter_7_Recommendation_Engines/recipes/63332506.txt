Title:
Lime peanut pork stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade:
8 oz. pork tenderloin, cubed
1 small onion
1 tsp. ginger
1 garlic clove, peeled
1 tsp. Thai chili garlic paste
1 lime, juice and zest
2 TB soy sauce
1 TB brown sugar
1 TB peanut oil
Sauce:
1/4 cup lime juice
1/8 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup peanut butter, smooth
1 TB rice vinegar
1 TB fresh cilantro or 2 TB dried cilantro
2 TB brown sugar
1/2 tsp. Thai garlic chili paste
2TB peanut oil
1 carrot, chopped
3 green onions, chopped
15 oz. can stir fried vegetables, drained

Directions:
Mix all marinade ingredients in a food processor and process until smooth.
Add pork tenderloin and marinate for approximately 4 hours.
Heat peanut oil in a large wok on medium high heat
Add carrots and green onions and stir fry for about 5 minutes
Add pork and stir fry until pork is no longer pink
Add stir fried vegetables and heat thoroughly
Mix sauce ingredients and add to pork and vegetables and stir fry until slightly thick about 5 minutes

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This recipe reminds me of a pork satay that normally is served on skewers.   Marinated pork tenderloin cubes and stir fry vegetables in a pork satay sauce is so good served with rice.
