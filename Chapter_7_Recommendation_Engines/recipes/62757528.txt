Title:
Chicken Spiedies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Preheat the grill, covered, for 5 min (15 if using gas)
6 twelve inch metal skewers
1/2 C olive oil
2 cloves garlic
2 Tbsp fresh basil, chopped
1/2 tsp oregano
2 Tbsp lemon zest
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
3 Tbsp mayo
1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
6 sub rolls, slit partially open lengthwise

Directions:
1)Make sauces- Combine oil, garlic, basil, oregano, lemon zest, and peppers in lg bowl. Transfer 2 Tbsp oil mixture to separate bowl and whisk in mayo, vinegar, and lemon juice; Refrigerate (up to 2 days)
2)Prep Chicken- Prick chicken breasts all over with fork, cut into 1-1/4 inch cubes, and transfer to bowl with remaining oil mixture. Refrigerate, covered 30 min or up to 3 hours
3)Grill- Remove chicken from marinade and thread onto 6 twelve inch skewers. Grill, covered, turning frequently, until lightly charred and cooked through, 10 to 15 min. Transfer to sub rolls, remove skewers and drizzle with mayo mix. Serve.

Submitter: 
Lisa Harmon
Description: 
These grilled sandwiches are upstate New York's best-kept secret. They were popular with Italian immigrants who came to the region in the early 20's. It originated in the Binghamton region in the 30's  at which time they marinated for a week and the meat choice was lamb.
