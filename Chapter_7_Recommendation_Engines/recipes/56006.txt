Title:
Black Forest Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985325.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) READY CRUST速 Graham Cracker Pie Crust
1 pkg. (4 serving size) vanilla instant pudding and pie filling mix
1 (8 ounce) container dairy sour cream
3/4 cup milk
1 (10 ounce) package REESE'S速 Peanut Butter Chips, divided
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 (12 ounce) jar Chocolate fudge topping
1 (12 ounce) can cherry pie filling for topping

Directions:
Whisk together pudding mix, sour cream and milk in medium bowl; set aside.
Place 1-1/3 cups peanut butter chips and oil in small microwave-safe bowl.  Microwave on HIGH (100%) 45 seconds; stir and microwave an additional 15 seconds; stir until smooth.  Using a hand mixer on high speed, gradually add to pudding mixture; spread evenly into crust.  Spread chocolate fudge topping over pie; cover and refrigerate 6 to 8 hours.
Prior to serving, top with remaining peanut butter chips and cherry pie filling.
