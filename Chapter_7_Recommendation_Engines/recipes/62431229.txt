Title:
Bayou Chicken & Sausage Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Success White Rice
1 tsp vegetable oil
1 small onion, copped
1 green bell pepper diced
1 stalk celery finely chopped
1 pkg (6 oz) grilled chicken breast strips, cubed
1/2 lb low-fat smoked turkey sausage, sliced
1 can (8oz) no salt added tomato sauce
1 can (14 1/2 oz) no salt added diced tomatoes, drained
1 tbsp salt-free Creole seasoning
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
1. Prepare rice according to package directions.
2. Heat oil in large skillet. Saute onions, bell pepper and celery for 5 minutes. Add chicken and sausage to skillet and saute an additional 3 minutes.
3. Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, Creole seasoning and Worcestershire sauce and heat through. Add rice to skillets stir to mix.
