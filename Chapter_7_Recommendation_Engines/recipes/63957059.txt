Title:
Puerto Rican Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097849.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 ½ C Rice
•	3 C Chicken or Vegetable Broth
•	3 T Olive oil
•	½  C minced Onion
•	1 small Jalapeno minced
•	2 cloves Garlic, minced
•	½ C chopped Tomato
•	1/4 Cilantro, chopped. Reserve 1 T for garnish
•	1 T Tomato Paste
•	¼ C roughly chopped Spanish Olives
•	1-2 bay leaves
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Over Medium heat Sautee Onions, Jalapeno, Garlic, tomato & Cilantro in olive oil till browned. 10-15 minuets.  Add Rice, stir constantly and cook till opaque, about 5 min. Add Tomato Paste and cook a few minutes more, stirring constantly.  Add Broth, Bay leaves, Olives, Salt & Pepper. Scrap fond (brown bits on bottom of pan)and stir to incorporate. Bring to boil and reduce heat to simmer for 5-10 minuets. Stir frequently. Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook 20 minuets. Remove from heat and fluff with fork. Remove bay leaves and garnish with chopped cilantro.

Submitter: 
CJ
Description: 
This rice dish is a great side with any meal. Serve it with some roast chicken, beans, plantains and avocado salad for a nice Puerto Rican meal. Don't forget the Pique sauce!
