Title:
Basil gnocchi with zucchini and chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
chicken
zucchini
garlic
spices
carrots
tomatoes

Directions:
cook ground or diced chicken breast and garlic when almost done add zucchini sliced lengthwise and cut at an angle on medium high heat. Add salt, fresh ground pepper, garlic powder, minced onion, turmeric, crushed pepper
Blend 1 carrot 2 roma tomatoes some water sugar and salt and garlic in vitamix then add to the chicken on high heat
Boil basil gnocchi and add cook for a few minutes then serve
