Title:
Cheese & Hamburger Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 to 1 lb. of lean hamburger
1/2 of small onion, minced
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. onion powder
S&P; to taste
2 c. water
2 c. pasta uncooked, any kind (I use large elbow)
1/4 brick 2% Velveeta, cut into small pieces
2 - 4 TBSP low fat sour cream

Directions:
Brown hamburger & onions over medium heat.  Season with spices while cooking.  Drain if desired.  Return to pan, turn heat up to high.  Add water, cover until boiling.  Add pasta.  Cover, turn down heat to medium.  Leave covered for 5 minutes without peeking!  Stir.  Cover and let cook for 5 more minutes or until pasta is tender.  Add cheese and stir gently until cheese is melted.  Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
Homemade version of Hamburger Helper - just as easy and tastes SOOOO much better!  You will need a large skillet with a tight fitting lid.
