Title:
Deluxe Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter and  1/2 packet of our pie crust mix
2T chopped nuts
3 eggs
1 cup sugar
1/2 t salt
2 T melted butter
1/2 cup dark corn syrup
1/2 cup whipping cream
1 t vanilla
1/4 cup brandy
1 cup pecan halves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Prepare pastry for 9 inch One - crust pie as directed on package - except stir in chopped nuts before adding water'
In a small mixer bowl beat eggs, sugar, salt, butter syrup and cream.
Stir in vanilla , brandy and pecans.
Pour into pastry lined pie pan
Bake for 40-50 minutes or until filling is set and pastry is golden brown.
Cool
