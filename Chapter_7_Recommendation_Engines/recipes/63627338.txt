Title:
Herdez Tomatillo Burger with Avocado Tomatillo Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008867.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup Herdez Tomatillo Sauce
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 avocado, diced
1 tablespoon sun dried tomato, chopped
1/2 tablespoon finely minced red onion
1 lime, juiced
2 tablespoons Herdez Tomatillo Sauce
4 burger buns, toasted

Directions:
Combine ground beef, 1/2 cup Herdez Tomatillo sauce and bread crumbs.  Shape into 4 equal patties, season, and grill until desired doneness.
Topping:  Mix together diced avocado, sundried tomato, onion, lime juice, and Herdez Tomatillo sauce.
Serve on buns with avocado topping.

Submitter: 
Grumpy's Honeybunch
Description: 
A burger with the subtle tang of spicy tomatillo salsa both in the burger and in the topping!
