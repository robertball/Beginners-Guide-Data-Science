Title:
"Broccaroni" and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1756638.jpg
Ingredients:
1 	16 oz. package pasta shells
1	8 oz. package shredded cheddar cheese
1 	15 oz. jar Alfredo sauce
4	ozs. Velveeta processed cheese (if you don't like Velveeta you can substitute with more cheddar)
Â½ 	cup milk
1	Tbsp pepper
1 	package frozen broccoli florets (Steamed)
4 	Strips fried and crushed bacon
1/2	cup Ritz cracker crumbs
1	Tsp oregano
1	Tbsp. minced garlic

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. In a Large pot cook pasta to package directions
3. In a large saucepan, steam broccoli as package directs
4. In a saucepan, combine Alfredo Sauce, all cheeses, spices, milk and bacon. Cook on low heat until smooth and creamy.
5. Place the pasta in a 13 X 9 baking dish, mix in steamed broccoli then pour cheese sauce over pasta and broccoli and mix together.
6. Sprinkle crushed Ritz crackers over top of mixture and bake for 20 minutes.
Hope you like it!
