Title:
Cold Beer Cheese Dip with Wonton Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2944008.jpg
Ingredients:
- 8 oz. Softened Cream Cheese
- 1/4 Cup of beer
- 1/2 Envelope of Ranch Dip Powder mix
- 1 Cup of finely Shredded Cheddar Cheese
- 12 Wonton Wraps (cut into triangles)
- Splash of Worcestershire Sauce
- Sea Salt
- Cooking Spray

Directions:
Mix Cheeses, Beer, Worcestershire, and Ranch Dip until well blended. Spray Wonton triangles lightly with cooking spray. Place on baking sheet and bake in Preheated 400' oven until crispy. About 4 minutes. Dip a chip and have a nice day:)
