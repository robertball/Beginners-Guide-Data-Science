Title:
Smoked Salmon Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Organic smoked salmon, 1/2 cup
2 eggs, cracked into a cup
1/2 lemon juiced
1 tablespoon of dill mustard
1 package of cream cheese
2 muffins cut in half

Directions:
Boil water with a drizzle of vinegar, add eggs to poach, about 3-4 minutes.
Place smoked salmon on one side of the muffin, place poached eggs on top.
Add lemon juice, dill mustard to cream cheese and mix well and add on top of the poached egg, add another layer of smoked salmon and eat!

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
A quicky and appetizing recipe for smoked salmon with poached eggs in a muffin.  This is quick, easy, and so delicious.  Try something new, this should be it.
