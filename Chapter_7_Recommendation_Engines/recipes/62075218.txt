Title:
Aussie Chow Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
600 grams Beef Mince
1 tbs oil
1 large Onion - diced
2 tbs curry powder
1 Pkt Chicken Noodle Soup
1 Cup sliced green beans
3 Cups of finely sliced cabbage
2 Sticks of celery sliced
3 Cups of water
3 Tbs of uncooked rice
Soy Sauce to serve

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large saucepan and fry onion and mince and curry powder until cooked.
Add remaining ingredients and bring to the boil, then simmer for 25 minutes.
Stir occassionally and serve with a splash of soy sauce on top.

Submitter: 
CZARDAS
Description: 
AUSTRALIAN MEASUREMENTS
This is a real crowd pleaser. Quick, easy and economical to make.  Great for a weeknight winters dinner. Recipe can be substituted with chicken or pork mince instead of beef.
