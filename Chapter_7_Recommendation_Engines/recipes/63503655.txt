Title:
Eggplant-Green pepper Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
non-stick spray
1 (2lb) eggplant
2 tsp olive oil
3 cups finely chop onion
1 1/2 cups finely chop bell pepper
3 Tbl pine nuts
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 eggs, lightly beaten
3/4 cup matzo meal
1 tsp paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Prepare baking dish w/light coat of nonstick spray. Cover a baking sheet with foil
Pierce eggplant several times w/fork & place on baking sheet. Roast in oven about 20min, or until tender. Set aside to cool. When cool, chop coarsely (&peel) & set aside in large bowl.
Oil in skillet over med-high heat. Add onion, bell pepper & nuts to pan. Cook about 5 min until onion is tender. Transfer onion mixture to bowl w/eggplant
Season eggplant-onion mixture w/salt & pepper. Add eggs & stir well to combine. Add matzo meal & toss gently to combine.
Spoon mixture into prepared dish. Sprinkle w/paprika. Bake for about 35min until golden brown.
