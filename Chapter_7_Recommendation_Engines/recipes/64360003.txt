Title:
Don's (on the) Sauce: sweet and spicy stir-fry sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chilli Sauce
2 tbsp tomato ketchup
1 tbsp honey
1 tsp dried chilli flakes
1 tsp white wine vinegar
splash water

Directions:
Place all the ingredients into a small saucepan and simmer gently for five minutes, adding enough water as necessary to make a pouring consistency.
