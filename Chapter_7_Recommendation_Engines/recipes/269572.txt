Title:
Almond Butter & Jam Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6106324.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
2 teaspoons chia seeds
2/3 cup Almond Breeze Vanilla almondmilk
2 tablespoons almond butter
1/2 small ripe banana
1 tablespoon strawberry preserves

Directions:
In wide-mouth pint jar or at least 12-ounce glass, mix oats, chia seeds, and almondmilk; let stand 3 minutes. Stir again.
In small bowl, mash almond butter and banana until almost smooth. Drop over oats mixture. Top with preserves. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight.
Uncover and stir slightly to serve.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Chia seeds add a little texture to standard overnight oats. They soften as the mixture stands overnight. Layer on a delicious banana and almond butter mixture and have each family member top with their favorite jam or jelly.
