Title:
Potatoes Casserole (Mrs. Springmann’s) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 pound bag frozen potato hash browns (squares), THAWED
1 can cream of chicken (or mushroom) soup (small, 10.5 oz can)
2 cups shredded mild cheddar cheese (8 oz)
1/2 cup margarine (1 stick)
3 Tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 pint sour cream (2 cups)
2 cups of corn flakes (crushed)

Directions:
Pre heat over to 350ºF
Mix and put in a 9” x 13” pan
Put 2 cups of corn flakes in a plastic bag and crush
Add 1/4 cup of melted margarine to corn flakes in bag and shake
Sprinkle mixture over the top of the pan of potatoes
Bake at 350ºF for 45 minutes
Serve while warm
