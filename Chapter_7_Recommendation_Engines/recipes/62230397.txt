Title:
Sausage Apple Breakfast Bundt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/320755.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 1 1/2 lbs. pork sausage
1 can apple pie filling
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 pkg. (36.5 oz.)frozen cinnamon rolls(12 count),thawed
Powdered sugar
Maple syrup

Directions:
Cook and drain sausage, mix with apple pie filling, cinn and vanilla. Flatten 8 cinn rolls and press into a well greased bundt pan. Unroll one and wrap around the center cone. Fill with sausage mixture, flatten remaining rolls and place over sausage mixture. Bake at 350 for 30-35 mins. Cool for 10 minutes before inverting onto a serving plate. Dust with powdwered sugar and serve with frosting packs or syrup.
