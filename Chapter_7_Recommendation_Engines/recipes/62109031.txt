Title:
- Orange and Raisin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cinnamon OR pumpkin pie spice
1 stick margarine (not butter), melted
1 1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 tsp. lemon extract
1/2 cup coarsely chopped mandarin oranges

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease a 12 cup muffin tin or 2 6 cup muffin tins or use paper liners.
In a large bowl, combine flour, spice, baking soda, salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, combine margarine and sugar and beat with an electric mixer until fluffy. Add eggs and extract.
Alternately beat in flour mixture and buttermilk into the margarine mixture.
Fold in raisins and oranges.
Bake 20 min. or until center is done.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
My older son does not like raisins, but my younger son does. This muffin recipe is the perfect solution---the raisins sink to the bottom when baked. My older son gets the tops of the muffins and my younger son gets the bottoms!
