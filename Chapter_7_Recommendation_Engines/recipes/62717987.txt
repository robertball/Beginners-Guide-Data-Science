Title:
Pumpkin Turkey Chili-Improved Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 large onion chopped
1 chopped green bell pepper
1 chopped yellow bell pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound ground turkey
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes w/ jalapenos
2 cups pumpkin puree
2 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 1/2-2 1/2 cups reduced sodium chicken stock
15 oz can black beans-drained
15 oz can red beans-drained
1 whole bag of frozen white sweet corn
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 dash salt
2 tablespoons Frank's Red Hot
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion, green bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and garlic until tender. Stir in the turkey, and cook until evenly brown. Mix in tomatoes, pumpkin, beans, corn and chicken stock. Season with all spices and Frank's. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 20 minutes. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
