Title:
Alexander's Super Amazing Bacon ' N ' Egg Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Flour tortillas
Scrambled eggs (filled with whatever kind of cheese is preferred)
12 Strips of smoked bacon (Broken into 3 pieces each)
Extra Cheese (Optional)

Directions:
1. Make scrambled eggs filled with cheese chosen.
2. Make bacon inside air fryer.
3. Prepare flour tortillas.
4. When bacon is done in air fryer break each strip into pieces each.
5. When scrambled eggs are done being made put an equal amount of it into each flour tortilla.
6. Then add the bacon pieces on top.
7. Wrap the tortilla up like a taco.
8. Eat it up.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This amazing recipe makes 4 wraps filled with sweet smoked bacon and cheesy, creamy scrambled eggs inside a nice warm tortilla.
