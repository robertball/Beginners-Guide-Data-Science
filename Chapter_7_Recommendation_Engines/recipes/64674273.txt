Title:
Bacon Cheeseburger Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4604657.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Ground Beef
3/4 pound bacon, cooked and diced
4 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 small onion, diced
3/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
2 eggs
2 tablespoons worchester sauce
2 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoons mustard
1 tablespoon brown sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine ground meat, bacon, cheese, onion, bread crumbs, eggs, and worchester sauce. Mix well.
Shape into a loaf, and place into a loaf pan.
In a small bowl mix ketchup, mustard, and brown sugar. Spread over top of meatloaf.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F oven for 1 hour or until done.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
If you love bacon then this is another way to use it has an ingredient in a meatloaf. It combines cheese and onion with other ingredients that are typical for meatloaf. Great leftover as a meatloaf sandwich.
