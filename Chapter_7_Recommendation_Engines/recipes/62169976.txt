Title:
Breakfast Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices of bread
8 slices of ham (deli sliced)
8 slices of american cheese
8 slices of muenster cheese
4 Eggs
2 C of milk
Dry Onion Flakes (to taste)
Corn Flakes (crushed)
1 stick of butter or margarine

Directions:
Layer the bread, ham, american cheese, muenster cheese, Repeat
Mix the eggs, milk and onion flakes and pour over the bread, ham and cheese, cover and refrigerate overnight
Crush the Corn Flakes and cover the top of the casserole
Drizzle melted butter (or margarine) over the top
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 40-45 minutes

Submitter: 
Becky
Description: 
This recipe is assembled the night before and it's always a hit when I make it.
