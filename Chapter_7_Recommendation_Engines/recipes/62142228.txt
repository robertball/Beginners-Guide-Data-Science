Title:
Chicken Sausage and Lima Bean Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon canola or olive oil
1/2 to 3/4 pound fresh hot Italian chicken sausage (about 2 large links)
3 medium carrots, diced
1 large red onion, diced
2 large celery stalks, diced
2 zuccini or summer squash, diced
1 1-pound package frozen baby lima beans
1 1/2 Tablespoons minced fresh thyme
1/4 Tsp crushed red pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 Tablespoon sherry vinegar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a 12-inch skillet
Squeeze sausage from its casings and crumble into hot oil.  Saute sausage until browned and no longer pink.
Add carrots, onions and celery to pan and cook until beginning to brown.  Stir in garlic, thyme and crushed red pepper and saute until garlic becomes fragrant.
Add frozen lima beans, squash, wine and broth and cook for 5 minutes, scraping up any brown bits.  Cover and cook over medium-low heat for 20 minutes.
Remove from the heat and stir in the sherry vinegar, salt and pepper.  Let stand, covered, for 10 minutes before serving.
