Title:
Pumpernickel Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975243.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups warm milk
1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 tablespoons molasses
2 1/2 cups bread flour
1 cup rye flour
1/3 cup cornmeal
1 teaspoon salt
1 3/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
Place ingredients into the bread machine in order suggested by the manufacturer.
Use the Light Crust or Whole Wheat settings.  Start the machine.

Submitter: 
Clem Zulinski
Description: 
This recipe makes a loaf that is not the very heavy textured bread made from the book that comes with the bread machine.
