Title:
Garlic, Oil, and Vinegar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt & pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1.5 tsp sugar
3 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients.

Submitter: 
Amy Cothran Gray
Description: 
A light salad dressing without the yuckiness of the store bought kind.
