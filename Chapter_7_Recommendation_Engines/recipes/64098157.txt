Title:
Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C white sugar
1 C unsalted butter
4 eggs
8 ripe bananas, mashed
4 C flour
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 C nuts (almonds,pecans)
1 TBSP vanilla extract

Directions:
Oven Temp: 375 degrees F
1. Spray and flour a 2" hotel pan
2. Cream together the butter or margarine and the sugar.
3. Add eggs, bananas, flour, soda, salt, nuts, and vanilla extract.  Mix thoroughly and pour batter into the prepared pan.
4. Bake at 375 degrees F. with no fan for 30-40 minutes.  Cool and frost cake.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
A very moist and perfectly sweet cake. Not crumbly!
