Title:
Honey Drizzled Curry Mustard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636054.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken leg quarters
1/2 cup of honey
4 tsps of mustard powder
1 tsp of curry powder
2 tsp of garlic powder
2 tbsp of soy sauce
2 tsp of apple sauce
3 tbsps of butter
2 tbsp of olive oil
1/3 cup of water

Directions:
In a bowl add chicken, mustard, curry, and garlic powders, soy and apple sauce together.  Mix well.
Heat butter and oil in a medium skillet over medium heat.
Add in chicken halves once butter has completely melted and starts to bubble.  Punture top of chicken and let bottom side cook till slightly brown.  Turn chicken and add water. Cover and cook for 15 - 20 minutes more.
Turn and slowly drizzle honey over chicken. Spoon drippings over chicken.
Set aside to cool.
Serve over rice.
