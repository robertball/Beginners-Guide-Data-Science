Title:
Chorizo Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/299766.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz. Pork Chorizo
4 oz. Mexican Cheese Mixture
6 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup green onions, chopped
3/4 cup dry Bisquick
1 1/2 milk
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
1.  Take Chorizo out of casing and cook meat over medium heat until done.
2.  Preheat oven to 350 dregrees.  Lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.
3.  Line bottom of pie pan with cheese and cooked Chorizo.  Combine eggs, onion, Bisquick, milk and black pepper; whisk together until smooth, although Bisquick may remain lumpy.; pour in to pie pan.
4.  Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes, until set.  Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
drbiffswife
Description: 
A tasty quiche with a Mexican flaire.  Most quiches are normally flat, although because of the Bisquick, it may rise a bit.
