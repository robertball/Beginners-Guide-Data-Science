Title:
coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage
1 carrot, shredded
2 cups mayonnaise
1 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
3 tbsp dried minced onion
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1> In a large bowl, combine shredded cabbage and carrot.
2> In a large empty mayonnaise jar, add the 2 cups mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, and dry onion flakes.  Shake well until blended.
3> Pour dressing mixture over cabbage and carrots and toss well.  Chill and serve.
