Title:
Chicken Curry Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 5.8 oz boxes of Chicken and Garlic Rice a Roni
1 6.5 oz jar of cara maria artichoke hearts
1/2 cup of mayonaise not miracle whip
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1 lb cubed cooked chicken
2 tbsp of yellow curry powder

Directions:
Cook and cube chicken
Prepare rice according to package directions
drain artichoke hearts and chop into small pieces
wash and chop up green onion
place all ingredients in a medium bowl and add mayonaise and yellow curry powder(adjust to your own taste)
Add salt and pepper if desired
Serve hot or let cool before placing in the refrigerator

Submitter: 
Danny Jaye
Description: 
A simple rice salad with yellow curry, marinated artichoke hearts, served hot or cold.
