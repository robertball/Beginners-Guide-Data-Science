Title:
Dry Ranch Style Seasoning for Dip or Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon dried parsley
3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
1-2 teaspoons dill weed

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir together the parsley, pepper, seasoned/reg. salt, garlic powder,dill, onion powder and thyme. Use as a substitute for Ranch Dressing Mix.

Submitter: 
Patty Cakes
Description: 
A dry ranch-style seasoning mix which can be combined with either mayonnaise and buttermilk (for a dressing) or mixed with sour cream, and served as a dip.Modified from: Dry Ranch Style Seasoning for Dip or Dressing
