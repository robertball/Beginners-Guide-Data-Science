Title:
Easy Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/389734.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mozzarella cheese sprinkled (add more cheese if wanted)
Pasta Sauce
Pepporoni
Dash of pepper for taste
Pizza Dough

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400, Put olive oil on a cookie sheet. Spread out dough to match size of cookie sheet. Spread sauce all over dough, leaving about a inch left for crust. Sprinkle cheese over sauce. Add toppings. Put in oven for 20-25 minutes.
