Title:
Easy Creamy Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/182182.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 tablespoons heavy cream
3 drops red food coloring

Directions:
Blend sugar, salt and flavoring. Add cream or water to make it easy to spread.  Makes enough icing for 3 to 5 dozen cookies.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
This icing is ideal for anise cookies.  Separate it into several little bowls for lots of colors. Tint with a few drops food coloring. Use little paintbrushes to color.  Kids will love it!
