Title:
Dutch Pie Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. unsifted flour
1/2 c. light brown sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
3 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Combine flour, light brown sugar and cinnamon.
Melt butter in microwave 10 seconds at a time so it doesn't separate.
Pour butter into flour mixture combine with fork until mixture is crumbly.
Sprinkle on pie and bake about 20 to 25 minutes at 350 degrees.
