Title:
Zucchini Jam by whodunitrdr Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups grated zucchini (no need to peel)
1/4 cup water
1 dash salt
1 package dry pectin
3 1/2 cups sugar
13 ounces crushed pineapple
6 ounces apricot Jello powder

Directions:
Heat zucchini, salt and water slowly in large kettle and cook until soft.
Add pectin and bring to a hard boil.
Add sugar and pineapple; bring back to boiling and boil 5 minutes.
Remove from heat; add Jello & stir until well mixed.
Ladle into sterilized jars and seal.
Makes about 8 cups.
NOTES : If you are using the giant zukes, remove the seeds before grating them.

Submitter: 
whodunitrdr
Description: 
This is an unusual way to use up some of that excess zucchini. Don't tell the kids there is zucchini in this, they will never know it.
