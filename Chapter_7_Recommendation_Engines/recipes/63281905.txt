Title:
Mexican Style Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked Quinoa
Water
1 can beans (black or pinto)
2 cups diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
1 cup chopped onion,
3 tablespoons taco seasoning,
2 cups frozen corn
1 bunch fresh cilantro.
Optional toppings:  Avocados, cheese, sour cream, lime juice, tortilla chips/strips

Directions:
Rinse and cook Quinoa according to package directions. While it simmers add in (any combination of): black or pinto beans, diced tomatoes, onion, taco seasoning, frozen corn, and cilantro. Top with (any combination of): Avocados, cheese, sour cream, lime juice, tortilla chips/strips
