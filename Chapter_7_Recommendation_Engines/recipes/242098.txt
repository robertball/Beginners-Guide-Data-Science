Title:
Tangy Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese (such as PhiladelphiaÂ®), softened
2 teaspoons Greek yogurt
1 1/4 cups confectioners' sugar, sifted, or more as needed

Directions:
Beat cream cheese in a bowl with an electric mixer until light and creamy; add yogurt and mix. Add confectioners' sugar 1/2 cup at a time, beating until fluffy after each addition.

Submitter: 
Vine
Description: 
Tangy cream cheese frosting that taste great on carrot cakes and apple cinnamon cakes. Use less sugar to use to fill cakes, and more sugar if you want to pipe swirls.
