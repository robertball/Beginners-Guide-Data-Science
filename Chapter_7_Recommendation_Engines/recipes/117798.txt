Title:
Mosaic Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980635.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium cucumber, sliced
2 yellow summer squash, thinly sliced
2 medium tomatoes, sliced
DRESSING:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 tablespoons minced fresh basil
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Arrange the cucumber, squash and tomatoes on a large serving platter. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the dressing ingredients; shake well. Drizzle over salad. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maria  Regakis
Description: 
'This pretty salad combines garden-fresh ingredients to create a colorful side dish,' says Maria Regakis from her home in Somerville, Massachusetts. It would be a great addition to a barbecue or picnic. TIP: 'I like to chill it a few hours to blend the flavors,' she notes.
