Title:
Bleu Cheese, Pear & Pecan Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp dijon or brown mustard
1/4 cup olive oil
2 Tbsp white-wine vinegar
1/2 Tbsp honey
1 bag mixed greens
1 pear (any type)
1/2 cup bleu cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup pecans, candied
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk together vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper. Add oil whisking coninuously so that the dressing infuses. Cut the pear into chunks and in a large bowl mix together lettuce, pear, bleu cheese and pecans.  Mix in the dressing and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
PSOCONNOR
Description: 
This dressing is excellent, and easy and can be used for any salad but it's great flavor enhances this salad the best
