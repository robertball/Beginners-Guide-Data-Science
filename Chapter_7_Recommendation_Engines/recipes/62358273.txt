Title:
Garlic & Onion Burger/Loosemeat Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/959894.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb lean ground beef (1137 calories)
1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce (8 calories)
2 small cloves garlic (8 calories)
1/4 cup onion, minced (7 calories)
1 teaspoon homemade italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon coarse pepper (1 calorie)
1 cup cheddar cheese, grated (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients together.
Form into patties 1/2 inch thick.
Broil 3 minutes per side on under high heat broiler (do NOT use stoneware.)
Remember to let rest for 5 minutes for juices to redistribute.
