Title:
Arroz con Pollo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts, Filleted
2 Tbs Olive Oil
4 Cloves Minced Garlic
1 Medium Yellow Onion
1 Medium Red Pepper, cut into slices
4 Cups Fresh Diced Tomatoes
2 Celery Stalks, sliced
2.5 oz Can of Sliced Black Olives
1 Jalapeno Pepper, sliced
1/2 Cup Chicken Stock
2 Tbs Diced Cilantro
1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1/2 tsp Ground Cumin
1/2 tsp Salt

Directions:
Heat 2 Tbs oil over medium high heat and brown the chicken fillets. Removed the chicken and tent with aluminum foil.
Add remaining oil to the pan and saute the minced garlic until fragrant. Add onion, and red peppers and continue to saute until tender.
Add tomatoes, celery, olives, jalapenos, chicken stock, cilantro, cayenne pepper, ground cumin and salt. Cover and simmer the sauce for 15 minutes.
Return the chicken to the pan and simmer for another 15 minutes or until the chicken is cooked.
Remove the chicken and slice into 1/2 inch strips. Place the chicken on a bed of rice and spoon the sauce over the chicken and serve.
