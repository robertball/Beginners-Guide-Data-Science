Title:
Celery Slaw Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/4c olive oil
3T apple cider vinegar
1/2c fresh parsley, finely chopped
6 celery ribs, thinly sliced on bias
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate until serving.
