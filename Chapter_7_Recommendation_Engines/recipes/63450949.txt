Title:
Seven Flavor Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter softened
1 cup vegetable oil
3 cups sugar
5 eggs beaten
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup milk
1 tsp rum
1 tsp butter flavoring
1 tsp coconut flavoring
1 tsp lemon flavoring
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp maple flavoring
1 tsp almond flavoring
Glaze Ingredients
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp each rum,coconut,butter,lemon,vanilla, maple and almond flavorings
pinch salt

Directions:
In large bowl, cream together butter, oil, sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs until combined. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt. in a measuring cup combine milk and all flavorings. Alternate additions of  flour and milk mixtures into creamed mixture, starting and ending with flour.  Spoon into greased and floured 10-inch bundt pan or 2 loaf pans. Bake at 350' oven for 1 hour until toothpicks comes out clean. Cool 10-15 mins and then remove from pan. Drizzle glaze over cake.
Glaze: In a small saucepan combine sugar water and flavorings until sugar is dissolved and mixture is warm. Pour over still warm cake.

Submitter:

Description: 
A friend at work gave me this recipe after years of begging. Don't skip the glaze, it really sets the cake apart.
