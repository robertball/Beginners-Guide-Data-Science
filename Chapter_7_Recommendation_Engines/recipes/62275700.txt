Title:
Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (8 ounce) can peas
1 cup diced carrots
1 (15.2 ounce) can corn
2 (6 ounce) cans chicken, drained or 1 lb chicken boiled and cubed
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1/4 tsp seasoning salt
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp paprika

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
In a large bowl, thoroughly mix noodles, onion, 1 cup cheese, peas, corn, carrots, chicken, soups, paprika, and salts. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish, and top with remaining 2 cups cheese.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheese is bubbly.
