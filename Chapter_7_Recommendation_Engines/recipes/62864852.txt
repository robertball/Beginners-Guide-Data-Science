Title:
Right as Ranch Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. butter
3 Tbsp. onion, sliced thin
3 Tbsp. flour
1 1/2 Tbsp. dry ranch dip or dressing mix
1 cup milk*
1 cup sour cream
5 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced thin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Melt the butter over medium heat.
Add onions and stir until softened.
Add flour and stir to coat all flour with butter and remove lumps. Continue to stir over heat for 1 minute.
Add ranch dressing and mix well.
Add sour cream, stirring constantly, then add milk. Continue to stir and heat just to boiling as it thickens.
Stir in potatoes to separate slices and coat each slice with the sauce.
Bake in a well greased casserole dish for 45-50 minutes until potatoes are soft and top is browned.
Remove from oven and let it sit for 10-15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
janet7th
Description: 
My granddaughters like ranch salad dressing and ranch dip so when I planned to make scalloped potatoes for dinner, I decided to try combining the flavors and we all loved them!
