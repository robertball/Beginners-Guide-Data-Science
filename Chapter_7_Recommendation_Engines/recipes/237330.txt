Title:
Carrot Cake Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1123828.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large carrot, peeled and diced
1/4 cup frozen mango chunks
1 large fresh peach, chopped
1/4 cup soy milk
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground allspice
1 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Pulse carrot and mango together in a blender until roughly chopped. Add peach, soy milk, cinnamon, allspice, and ginger; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
thegreatestthing
Description: 
This started out as a way to hide a carrot in a smoothie, but ended up being more about the carrot with fruit hidden inside! If you'd like to add ice, go ahead. I didn't and it came out fine.
