Title:
CHICKEN AMERICAN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 STRIPS OF THICK CUT BACON
4 BONELESS, SKINLESS CHICKEN BREASTS
1/4 - 1/2 CUP AP FLOUR
2 LG EGGS
1 TBL WATER
1 PKG CORN BREAD STUFFING MIX
1/2 - 3/4 CUP GRATED PARMESAN CHEESE
4 SLICES OF AMERICAN CHEESE
CANOLA OR CORN OIL
1/2 TSP EACH SALT AND PEPPER
LARGE PLASTIC BAG

Directions:
FRY THE BACON UNTIL IT IS ALMOST COOKED - LET DRAIN ON PAPER TOWELS.
WHILE BACON IS FRYING, RINSE CHICKEN UNDER COLD WATER AND TRIM ANY EXCESS FAT.
IN THE PLASTIC BAG, SHAKE UP THE FLOUR, SALT, AND PEPPER
PLACE APPROX 1/2 THE STUFFING MIX IN ANOTHER BAG AND CRUSH IT UNTIL COARSE.
ADD PARMESAN CHEESE TO THE STUFFING AND SHAKE
PLACE CRUMBS IN A MEDIUM-LARGE DISH (BIG ENOUGH TO HOLD A CHICKEN BREAST
ADD 1 CHICKEN BREAST TO THE FLOUR AND SHAKE.
REMOVE CHICKEN BREAST FROM BAG, SHAKING OFF EXCESS FLOUR
DREDGE CHICKEN IN EGGS BEATEN W/THE WATER
PLACE CHICKEN IN THE STUFFING MIXTURE, FIRMLY PRESSING DOWN.  TURN CHICKEN OVER AND FIRMLY PRESS DOWN, ASSURING THAT THE SIDES ARE ALSO COATED.
REPEAT STEPS 6-9 WITH THR REMAINING CHICKEN
HEAT OIL IN A FRY PAN
CAREFULLY PLACE CHICKEN IN THE HOT OIL AND FRY UNTIL COATING IS THAT GOLDEN BROWN COLOR.  TURN CHICKEN AND FRY UNTIL COATING IS GOLDEN BROWN
PLACE ON A BAKING RACK OVER A SHALLOW BAKING PAN
CUT BACON STRIPS IN HALF AND PLACE 4 HALVES ON EACH BREAST
PLACE 1 SLICE OF CHEESE ON EACH BREAST
BAKE IN A 350 DEGREE OVEN FOR 15-20 MINS OR UNTIL CHICKEN IS COMPLETELY COOKED

Submitter:

Description: 
I HAVE BEEN MAKING THIS DISH SINCE I WAS IN MY EARLY TEEN YEARS.  INSTEAD OF USING REGULAR BREAD CRUMBS, I USE A PACKAGED CORNBREAD STUFFING TO MAKE A MORE CRISPY/CRUNCHY COATING.
