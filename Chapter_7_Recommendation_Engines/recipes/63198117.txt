Title:
Lemon Blueberry Yogurt Parfait Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. lemon yogurt
1/2 c. fresh blueberries
2 honey graham crackers crushed

Directions:
spoon 1/2 c. of yogurt into a glass cup.
lay on topof yogurt 1 crushed graham cracker.
lay on topof graham cracker 1/4 c. of blueberries.
repeat.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Lemon yogurt, fresh blueberries and honey graham crackers make an easy and delicious breakfast parfait. Tastey enough too for a healthy and light dessert.
