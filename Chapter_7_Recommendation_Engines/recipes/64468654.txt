Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
1 package (10 oz., about 40) marshmallows OR
4 cups miniature marshmallows
6 cups Kellogs® Fruity Pebbles® cereal
1 1/2 cup shredded coconut
1 (3 oz) bar semisweet baking chocolate

Directions:
1. In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
2. Add Kellogs® Fruity Pebbles® cereal. Stir until well coated.
3. When cool enough to touch, form coated cereal into ½ inch diameter balls.  Place in wax lined cupcake pan to cool complete.
4. Melt chocolate coating in a glass bowl in the microwave, or in a metal bowl over a pan of simmering water, stirring occasionally until smooth.
5. Roll cereal balls in melted chocolate and immediately roll in shredded coconut after.
6. Place back in wax lined cupcake pan to cool.  Best if eaten in same day.

Submitter: 
Angelica Rose McAdam
Description: 
Delicious cereal treats rolled in chocolate and coconut.  Inspired by everyone's favorite mascot, Dinomite of G.I.s.h.W.h.E.S!
