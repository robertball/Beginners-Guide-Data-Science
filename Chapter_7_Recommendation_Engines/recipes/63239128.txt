Title:
Zesty Cold Chicken Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces of rotini pasta
1 cup no fat mayonnaise
1 teaspoon lemon and pepper seasoning
1/2 teaspoon dried mustard
2 5 ounce cans of chicken packed in water, drained
24 cherry or grape tomatoes, cut in half
1 15 ounce can of peas
paprika to sprinkle

Directions:
Boil pasta for 8 to 9 minutes, or al dente. Drain and rinse in cold water. Drain.
Together, in a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, lemon and pepper seasoning and dried mustard.
Add chicken, tomatoes and peas. Mix.
Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve at room temperature or cool for at least one hour. Sprinkle with paprika before serving.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
A quick, easy, tasty and colorful chicken pasta salad that looks as great as it tastes.
