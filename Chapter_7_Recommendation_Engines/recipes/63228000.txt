Title:
Spicy Oven Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Pounds of Potato Balls (Approx. 4 Medium/Large Potatoes Cubed)
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
6 Cloves Fresh Minced Garlic
1/4 Cup Minced Red Onion
1 Tablespoon Dried Pepper Flakes (Thai or Cayenne)
1 Tablespoon Cajun Seasoning
1 Teaspoon Black Pepper
Salt or Seasoning Salt to Taste

Directions:
Add olive oil and all ingredients except potatoes to pot and simmer on medium/high heat for 2 minutes.  Add potatoes and stir the mix in thoroughly.
Pre-heat oven to 375 then add full mixture to shallow baking pan.  Even out mixture throughout pan with potatoes.
Cook for approx. 50 minutes.
Add salt or seasoning salt as desired.

Submitter: 
Jim
Description: 
Easy and flavorful roasted potato recipe for those who like a bit of spice.  A great side dish.
