Title:
Paleo Omelet Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/772066.jpg
Ingredients:
8 muffin liners
8 eggs
1/8 cup water
8oz cooked ham or sausage, crumbled
1 cup diced onions
1 cup diced peppers
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350?.
Put muffin liners in muffin pan.
Beat eggs in medium bowl. Add meat, vegetables, salt, ground pepper, and any other ingredients you wish to add.
Pour mixture into the muffin cups.
Bake for 18-20 minutes.

Muffins in a cup! Easy, yummy, and best of all HEALTHY.
