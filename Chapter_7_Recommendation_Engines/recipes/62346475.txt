Title:
Olive Salad On A Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/394757.jpg
Ingredients:
12 spicy marinated green pitted olives (or any green pitted olive of choice)
12 whole grape or cherry tomatoes
12 leaves of fresh basil, washed and dried
12 mini fresh mozzarella balls
12 pitted kalamata olives
2-3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1-2 tablespoons basalmic vinegar, optional
Fresh ground black pepper, to taste
12 extra long toothpicks or 6" skewers

Directions:
Thread all ingredients onto toothpick in this order: green olive, tomato, basil, mozzarella, and kalamata olive.
Arrange onto serving plate.
Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and basalmic vinegar (if using).
Crack fresh ground black pepper over all, to taste.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
These make wonderful appetizers or sides to almost any meal. Just a little something different from the usual salad. Feel free to change up the olives to suit your taste. Enjoy!
