Title:
Al Fresco Buffalo Chicken Sausage Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/893188.jpg
Ingredients:
12oz package AlFresco Buffalo Chicken Sausage
1/2-3/4 cup chopped celery (to taste)
1/4 cup hopped onion
Frank's Hot Sauce (to taste)
6oz Laughing Cow Blue Cheese wedges
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
1 8ct package flour tortillas

Directions:
Spray a heavy skillet with cooking spray and heat to medium high.  Saute celery and onion for 2-3 minutes. Remove from pan and set aside.  Remove casings from sausages and brown in same pan 4- minutes.  Return celery and onion to pan, remove from heat, stir in cheese wedges. Stir to melt.   Preheat and spray another skillet on medium heat, lay one tortilla in skillet, spread 1/4 mixture on to tortilla, sprinkle with 1/4  shredded chesse, top with a 2nd tortilla and spray with cooking spray.  Brown, then flip quesadilla and brown the other side.  Repeat with the next 3 quesadillas.  Sprinkle tops of each quesadillas with 1/8 c. shredded cheese, serve with sour cream and extra Frank's Hot sauce.  Serve immediately.

Description: 
This recipe cmakes delicious, creamy quesadillas,but the filling is also very good used in lettuce wraps.  Delicious!
