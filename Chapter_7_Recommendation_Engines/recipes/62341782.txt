Title:
Lemon-Berry Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. frozen pineapple-orange-strawberry juice concentrate, thawed (or pineapple-orange)
3 Tablespoons orange liqueur or orange juice
1 cup light ricotta cheese
4 oz light cream cheese, softened
1 can (15.75 oz) lemon pie filling
2 loaf angel cakes
2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and cut into bite size pieces
1 cup fresh raspberries
1 cup fresh blackberries

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine juice concentrate and liqueur.  Blend well. Set aside.
With electric mixer, beat ricotta and cream cheese at medium speed until smooth.
Add pie filling.  Beat until well-blended and fluffy, scraping sides of bowl occasionally.
Line bottom of a 13x9 pan with one of the cakes, sliced into 1/2 inch (approx) thick slices.
Brush cake with 1/2 of juice concentrate mixture.
Spread 1/2 of lemon filling evenly over this.
Top with 1/2 of fruits.
Repeat layers.
Refrigerate until serving, at least 1-2 hours.  Store in refrigerator.
