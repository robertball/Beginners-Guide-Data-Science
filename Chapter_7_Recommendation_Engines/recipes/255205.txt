Title:
Summer Crunch Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4464412.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen mixed fruit
1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
1 cup lukewarm water
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup vanilla yogurt
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Combine frozen fruit and pomegranate seeds in a blender. Add water, orange juice, vanilla yogurt, and milk; blend until smooth, about 2 minutes.
