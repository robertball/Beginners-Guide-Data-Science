Title:
Avocado Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093866.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
3 1/4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
3/4 cup mashed avocado
1 1/2 cups white organic sugar
2 organic eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C )
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar and mashed avocado.
Add eggs and beat. Add dry ingredients and mix well.
Divide dough into walnut sized balls.
Place the cookies 2 inches apart onto a nonstick cook sheet, and flatten slightly.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until bottom is light brown. Do not over bake . Let them cool for 5 minutes  or cool completely. Then remove  cookies from the cookie sheet. Store in airtight container.
