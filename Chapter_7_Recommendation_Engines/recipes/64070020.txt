Title:
Big Ray's Southwest Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132318.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tsp dried minced onion
2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp cornstarch
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp dried minced garlic
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 cup barbecue sauce
2 Tbl lemon juice
6 bone-in pork loin chops (3/4" thick & 7 oz each)

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the first ten ingredients; mix well. Add pork chops & seal bag, turning to coat; refrigerate for 2-24 hours.
Discard marinade. Moisten a paper towel with cooking oil; using long-handled tongs, lightly coat the grill rack. Grill chops, covered, over medium heat or broil 4 in. from the heat for 4-5 minutes on each side or until a thermometer reads 145Â°. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Description: 
This simple combination of herbs and seasonings has no salt.  You will never miss it from the taste. We love the seasoning blend on pork, but you can also use it on chicken.
