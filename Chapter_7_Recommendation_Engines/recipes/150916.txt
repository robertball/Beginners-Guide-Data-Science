Title:
Royal Washington Apple (Royal Apple) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981087.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Crown Royal® blended Canadian whisky
1/4 ounce Smirnoff® Green Apple vodka

Directions:
Pour Crown Royal(R) blended Canadian whisky and Smirnoff(R) Green Apple Vodka into a shaker with ice.
Shake cocktail and pour into glass to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Classic cocktails need the authentic taste of Crown Royal® blended Canadian whisky, with a delicately smooth, creamy taste and long-lasting finish.
