Title:
Zucchini and Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 3 lbs. Zucchini, cubed
* 2 cups corn
* 1 onion, chopped
* 1 green bell pepper, chopped
* 2 Tablespoons butter
* salt and pepper to taste
* 4 eggs, lightly beaten
* 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
* Paprika to taste

Directions:
In a saucepan, cook zucchini in boiling water for 2 to 3 minutes; drain and set aside. In a skillet over medium heat, saut√© corn, onion and green pepper in butter until crisp-tender. Remove from heat and add zucchini to corn mixture; season with salt and pepper and let cool slightly. Stir in eggs and transfer to a greased 13inch by 9inch baking pan. Top with cheese and paprika. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees f for 40 minutes, or until lightly golden and bubbly.
