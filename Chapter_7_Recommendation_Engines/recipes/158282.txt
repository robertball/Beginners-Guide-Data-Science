Title:
Cherry Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984688.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups frozen unsweetened tart cherries
1/4 cup coarsely chopped dried tart cherries
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1 tablespoon chopped fresh or canned jalapeno peppers, or to taste
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
1 teaspoon cornstarch

Directions:
Coarsely chop frozen tart cherries. Let cherries thaw and drain, reserving 1 tablespoon cherry juice.
Once cherries are thawed, combine drained cherries, dried cherries, onion, jalapenos, garlic, and cilantro in a medium saucepan; mix well.
Combine reserved cherry juice and cornstarch in a small bowl; mix until smooth. Stir into cherry mixture.
Cook, stirring constantly, over a medium high-heat until mixture is thickened. Let cool. Serve with tortilla chips and/or cooked chicken or pork.

Submitter: 
The Cherry Marketing Institute
Description: 
Served with grilled poultry, roast pork or tortilla chips, this spicy mixture wows traditional salsa lovers.
