Title:
Toffee Chip Cookies I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 3/4 cup light brown sugar
1/2 tsp kosher salt
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cornstarch
2 1/2 cup white winter wheat flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 cup toffee bits (homemade or Heath)

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat butter with brown sugar and salt for about 3-4 minutes. Add eggs, one at a time then vanilla.
In a small bowl whisk cornstarch, flour, baking soda and baking powder then add flour mixture to dough.
Fold in toffee bits.
Chill dough for 30 minutes then shape dough into 1 oz (about 1 tbsp) balls and place on a cookie sheet lined with parchment or silpat. Bake in a 375 degree oven for 10-12 minutes. Remove cookies from oven but keep them on the cookie sheet for 2 minutes (if desired, sprinkle more toffee chips on top) After two minutes transfer cookies to wire rack to cool completely. Store cookies in air tight container for one week. ENJOY!
