Title:
Big Ray's Moroccan Spiced Vegetable Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7342718.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium red onion, chopped
1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
1 carrot, chopped into cubes
2 cloves garlic, minced
salt & pepper, to taste
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
cayenne pepper, optional, to taste
1 cup frozen peas
1 14 oz can chickpeas, drained
1 1/2 cups chicken stock
A good handful fresh parsley, chopped
1 cup couscous

Directions:
In a medium-size skillet heat the oil over medium heat.
Add red onion, yellow pepper, and carrot sauté 10-15 minutes or until all the vegetables are your desired tenderness
Add the garlic, sauté another minute.
Mix in the salt & pepper, paprika, ground coriander, turmeric, celery salt, cumin ground cinnamon, and cayenne pepper (if using). Stir-fry until fragrant (about a minute).
Add the frozen peas and cook briefly. Stir in the chickpeas, followed by the stock.
Add the chopped parsley and stir in the couscous.  Let simmer 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally or until liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes .  Fluff and serve.
