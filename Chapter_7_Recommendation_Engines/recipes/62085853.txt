Title:
Matzo Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/2 cup seltzer water
6 tbs canola oil
1 tsp parsley (optional)
1 cup matzo meal
kosher salt and pepper to taste
2 quarts of prepared chicken broth warm

Directions:
Mix all indgredients together and put into frig for atleast 1 hour. After a hour Boil 2 quarts of water in a big pot. Wet hands and start rolling matzo mix into big or small balls. Placing them into the hot water, Cook on a low fire for atleast 20 minutes. Strain the matzo balls and add into the prepared chicken broth.

Submitter: 
Love2c0ok
Description: 
I love matzo balls when I'm not feeling good. They are very delicious if made right. For an extra bit of flavor you can add carrots and celery. I sometimes use the lipton noodle soups instead of the prepared broth.
