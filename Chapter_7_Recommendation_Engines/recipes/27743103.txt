Title:
Vegetable Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS. olive oil divided
1 medium zucchini, cubed
1 medium yellow summer squash, cubed
1 cup shitake mushroom slices
1 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
6 plum tomatoes
1 tsp. dried oregano leaves
3 cups canned vegetable broth
3/4 cup arborio rice
1/4 cup freshly grated paremsan cheese
salt and pepper
1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
fresh oregano leaves

Directions:
Heat 1 TBS. oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add zucchini and squash; cook and stir 5 minutes or until crisp-tender.  Place in medium bowl, set aside
Add mushrooms, onion and garlic to saucepan; cook and stir 5 minutes ot unril tender.  Cut tomatoes crosswise into halves.  Squeeze to remove seeds.  Cut each tomato half into quarters.  Add tomatoes and dried oregano to mushroom mixture; cook and stir 2-3 minutes until tomatoes are soft.  Place in bowl with zucchini mixture.
Place broth in small saucepan; bring to a boil over medium heat.  Reduce heat to medium-low to keep broth hot, but not boiling.
Meanwhile, heat remaining 1 TBS. oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add rice; cook and stir 2 minutes.
Add 3/4 cup broth to rice.  Reduce heat to medium-low, maintaining a simmer throughout step.  Cook and stir until rice has absorbed broth.  Repeat, adding broth 3 more times, cooking and stirring until rice has absorbed broth. (cook total 20-25 minutes)
Stir in cheese.  Season with salt and pepper.  Srit in reserved vegetables and peas; cook until heated through.  Serve immediately.  Garnish with fresh oregano if desired.
