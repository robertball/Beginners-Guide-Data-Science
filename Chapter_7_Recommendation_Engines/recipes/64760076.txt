Title:
Slow Cooker Carnivore Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb boneless grassfed chuck or other roast
2 c beef bone broth
couple cloves garlic, smashed (optional)
sea salt (1 or more T; 2 was too much)
2-4 T wine (optional)

Directions:
Bring meat to room temp
Generously season with salt
Sear meat in bacon grease in cast iron skillet for few minutes each side on high heat
Deglaze pan with bone broth and/or wine
Put meat, drippings and meat in slow cooker on low
slow cook for 12-14 hours on Low
