Title:
Monica's Baptist Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970526.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
3 tablespoons lemon iced tea mix
2 oranges, sliced into rounds
1 lemon, sliced into rounds
3 cups ice

Directions:
Pour the lemon-lime soda into a punch bowl, and stir in the instant iced tea mix. Float the orange and lemon slices in the punch, and add ice. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
MULETEER
Description: 
This fresh, sweet nonalcoholic drink is beautiful in a punch bowl and will have your guests coming back for more and more!
