Title:
Ham and Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1095663.jpg
Ingredients:
9 cups of cubed coked ham
1 medium onion chopped
1 medium bell pepper (use your favorite color pepper), chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tablespoon of olive oil
1 can of diced tomatoes(28 oz), undrained
1 can of kidney bean(16-18 oz)s, rinsed and drained
1 can of black beans(16-18 oz), rinsed and drained
1 can of pinto beans (16-18 oz), rinsed and drained
1 jar (8 oz) of picante sauce
1 can (8 oz) of tomato sauce
1/2 cup of water, optional
1 can of sliced ripped olives, rinsed and drained
1 teaspoon of beef bouillon granules
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon of salt
1/4 teaspoon of pepper
Shredded cheddar cheese 2-2 1/2 cups

Directions:
In a large saucepan, cook the ham, onion, bell pepper, garlic and olive oil until tender. Stir in tomatoes, beans picante sauce, tomato sauce and water if desired. Bring to a boil. Stir in the olives, bouillon, thyme, salt and pepper. Reduce heat, simmer uncovered for 15-20 minutes. Garnish with cheese.
