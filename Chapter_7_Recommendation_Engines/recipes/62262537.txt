Title:
Fluffy Cookie Suprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Stick Butter
1- 8oz Pkg. Cream Cheese
1 Cup Powdered Sugar
3-1/2 Cups Milk
2 Pkgs Instant Vanilla Pudding
1- 12oz Tub Cool Whip
1- 20 oz Pkg Chocolate Cookies (ex. Oreos)- Crushed and divided into thirds.

Directions:
Whip/combine the butte, cream cheese, and powdered sugar.
Mix milk, pudding and cool whip in another bowl.
Combine the mixtures together.
Put 1/3 cookies on the bottom of a bowl. Alternate layers of cream mixture and cookies until gone.  Mix lightly together.
Enjoy!
