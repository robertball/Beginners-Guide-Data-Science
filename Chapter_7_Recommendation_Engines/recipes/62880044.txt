Title:
Frozen Banana Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1 cup coconut milk (refrigerate before using)
*3 frozen bananas
*2 Tbsp cocoa powder (or more to taste)

Directions:
Blend all ingredients until it is the consistency of frozen yogurt.
Top with any variety of nuts or other favorite ice cream topping.
