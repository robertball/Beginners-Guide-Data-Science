Title:
Red Wine Pork Roast with Carrots, Onions and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons bacon drippings
3 pounds bone-in pork roast
1/4 cup butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
5-6 carrots, diced
1 medium onion, sliced
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 1/2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 1/2 cups good red wine
1 1/2 lbs fresh mushrooms, cut in half or small whole mushrooms, and soaked in red wine for 1 hour before cooking.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Heat bacon drippings in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown roast on all sides. Remove roast to a casserole dish. Place butter, garlic, onion and carrots in the skillet. Reduce heat to medium. Stir in parsley, bay leaf, salt, pepper, and tomato paste. Pour in red wine, and scrape bottom of pan to pick up all the meat leavings, then stir to combine. Add mushrooms. Pour over pork roast. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Let sit for 10 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Frenchy
Description: 
Bone-in pork roast is browned in bacon fat then slowly roasted with carrots, onions, herbs, tomato paste, red wine, and wine-marinated mushrooms.Modified from: Red Wine Pork
