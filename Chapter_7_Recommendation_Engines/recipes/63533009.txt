Title:
Feta Cheese Stuffed Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. ground pork
1 tbsp. black pepper
1 tbsp. minced garlic
1 tbsp. Italian seasoning
1 egg
1/2 cup Feta cheese crumbled
1 package onion soup mix
1/4 cup Italian bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Mix all ingredients together except feta cheese.
Use feta cheese to stuff into meat and form into 2-3 inch meat balls.
Place in baking dish lightly sprayed with veg oil.
Bake for 20-30 minutes, Turning midway through cooking until internal temp is 150-155.
Remove from oven and place in separate pan, tent with foil for few minutes to rest before serving.
