Title:
Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Cream Cheese (Room Temp.)
12oz Imitation Crab Meat
1 Tb. Freeze Dried Chives
Garlic Salt and Pepper to taste.

Directions:
Chop crab meat medium to fine texture.
Mix crab using a fork with room temp. cream cheese
Once cheese and crab combined add garlic salt and pepper.
Allow to sit in fridge for at least 8 hrs.

Submitter: 
violet
Description: 
This is a super fast and yummy dip.  It's key to use imitation crab meat because of the stronger crab flavor.  This dip can also be made into a nice ball.
