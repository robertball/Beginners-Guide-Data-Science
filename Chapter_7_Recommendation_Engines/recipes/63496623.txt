Title:
Gelatin Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954359.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box any flavor gelatin
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with a whisk over medium heat until thick and bubbly.  Can also microwave 30 seconds at a time until thick and bubbly, if preferred.

Description: 
My mom made this for me when I was little.  Now, my kids love it.  So many flavor possibilities!
