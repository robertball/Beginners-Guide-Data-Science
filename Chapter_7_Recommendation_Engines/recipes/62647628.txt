Title:
Oatmeal Lace Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup applesauce or water
3 cups oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare baking sheet with silpat, parchment or non-stick spray.
Combine whole wheat flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
Cream butter and sugars together on medium speed until light in color and fluffy.
Add egg and vanilla to the creamed mixture.
Once egg and vanilla are well incorporated, add in applesauce or water.
Add flour mixture to the butter mixture. (DO NOT OVER MIX OR YOUR COOKIES WILL BE DRY AND DENSE!)
Add in oats by stirring in with a wooden spoon.
Roll cookies into 1 inch balls and place on a cookie sheet 2 inches apart. (THEY WILL SPREAD QUITE A BIT)
Bake cookies for 14-16 minutes. (COOKIES WILL NOT LOOK DONE).
Allow cookies to rest for 5 minutes before attempting to remove from the pan.

These cookies are chewy yet soft. They have a light buttery flavor with a burst of brown sugar. You'll fall in love with just one bite.
