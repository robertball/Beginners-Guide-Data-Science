Title:
Christmas Ribbon Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 			large pkg. lime and cherry jell-o
1 			small pkg. lemon jell-o
1c			miniature marshmallows
1 			8 oz. cream cheese softened
½ c			sour cream
1 c			whipped cream (or cool whip)
1			can drained crushed pineapple

Directions:
Dissolve Jello flavors separately using 2 cups of boiling water for the large packages of jello and 1 cup  for the small package of jello
Stir the marshmallows into lemon jello and set aside
Add ½ c. cold water to lime and cherry jello
Pour lime jello into large baking pan
Chill lime jello until set but not firm
Add ½ c. cold water to cherry and set aside at room temperature
Add cream cheese to lemon mixture beat on medium speed until blended
Chill lemon pixture until slightly thickened
Blend in sour cream, whipped cream and crushed pineapple
Chill until very thick, and spoon gently over lime jello
Chill cherry jello until thickened and pour over lemon jello mixture
Chill Jello until firm

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
A great addition to Christmas dinner. This is a favorite for our family. My grandmother makes it every year and it's so yummy!
