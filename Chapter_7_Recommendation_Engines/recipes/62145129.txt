Title:
Ranch Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. Shredded chicken
1/2 pkg of Powdered Hidden Valley Ranch
3/4 c. Mayo
2 tsp Mustard
Red Grapes, halved
Salt & Ground Black Pepper
1 tsp Lemon Juice

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients. This recipe is based on your preference, just taste as you go! Remember you can always add more!
Mix together and serve.

Submitter: 
LeighAnne
Description: 
A combination of shredded chicken, powered ranch, mayo and juicy grapes.  Delicious Summer Chicken Salad that everyone will love, even my husband who hates Mayo!
