Title:
Almond Butter and Berry Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two slices whole-grain bread
Almond butter
Fresh blueberries
Fresh strawberries, sliced

Directions:
Toast the bread slices.
Spread almond butter on each slice.
Top with blueberries and strawberries.
