Title:
Fresh Salsa with Cottage Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5055010.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container Shamrock FarmsÂ® Original Cottage Cheese
1 bunch green onions
3 hot yellow chile peppers
2 jalapeno peppers
1 bunch cilantro
8 firm, ripe tomatoes
3 cloves garlic, chopped
salt to taste

Directions:
Chop onions, peppers, cilantro, and tomatoes by hand. Place in a large bowl; stir in garlic and salt.
Mix in Shamrock Farms Cottage Cheese.

Submitter: 
Shamrock Farms
Description: 
Cottage cheese adds heft and creaminess to this zesty fresh salsa full of hand-chopped tomatoes and peppers.
