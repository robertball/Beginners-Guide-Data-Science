Title:
Pumpkin Frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4530166.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
1 cup pumpkin puree
1/4 cup stevia sweetener (such as Truvia®)
1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
3 cups cold coffee

Directions:
Whisk almond milk, pumpkin puree, stevia, and pumpkin spice together. Pour mixture into ice cube trays and freeze until firm, 8 hours to overnight.
Place pumpkin ice cubes and coffee in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Truvia® and unsweetened almond milk make this frappe drink low in calories. Top with fat-free whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®) if desired.
