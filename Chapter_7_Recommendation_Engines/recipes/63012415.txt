Title:
Nanas Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1/2 cup shortening
2. 1 cup butter brought to room temp
3. 1 1/2 Cup sugar
4. 5 eggs brought to room temp
5. 2 tea vanilla extract
6. 1 cup milk - room temp
7. 1/2 tea baking powder
8. 3 cups flour ( sifted - or may use cake flour)

Directions:
oven at 300* Lightly grease and flour 10" bundt pan
Cream with a mixer 1.2.3.till light and fluffy.
Add eggs one at a time beating after each one add 5.
combine 7. and 8 and add to mixture alternately with 6. start and end with flour
Pour into Bundt pan
Bake 1-1 1/2 hrs test with toothpick let cool in pan 10 min then flip out of pan

Gina Lee Lafreniere

Nana says best if eggs, butter,  milk are  at room temp
