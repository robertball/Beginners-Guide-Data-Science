Title:
Mushroom Ragu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Mushrooms (crimini, oyster, and shitake) sliced
1 onion, sliced
4 cloves of garlic, diced
1/2 cup of marsala wine
1 can of chicken stock
1/4 cup of Parmesan Romano cheese
2 tablespoons of fresh basil and parsley

Directions:
In a skillet, soften onion and garlic in olive oil and add salt and pepper to taste.
Add sliced mushrooms to the skillet.  (Shitake mushrooms have tough stems, discard them.)  Add salt and pepper and a drizzle of EVOO and toss together for 3 minutes.
Add Marsala wine and chicken stock.  Let simmer until reduced sauce about ten minutes.
When reduced, add herbs, basil and parsley add Parmesan Romano cheese.
Serve this over any pasta, chicken or seafood, and sprinkle some parmesan romano cheese.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
So simple and so good.  Serve this over any type of pasta or even over a sauteed chicken breast or shrimp. Mushrooms cooked in marsala wine, chicken stock, garlic, and basil.
