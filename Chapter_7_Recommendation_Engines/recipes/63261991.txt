Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
2 1/2c sugar
2c chopped zucchini
1t vanilla
1c chopped nuts
1c oil
3c flour
2t cinnamon
1t salt
1t soda
1t baking powder

Directions:
Beat eggs, add oil, sugar, zucchini, and vanilla. Mix in dry ingredients.
Bake 350* for 1 hour.
Add raisins if you feel like it.
