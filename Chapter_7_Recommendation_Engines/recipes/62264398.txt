Title:
Pasta Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 sweet onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes w / garlic & olive oil
5 1/2 cups water
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon salt
1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans
1 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 pound ditalini pasta

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, cook onion over medium heat in olive oil until translucent.  Stir in garlic and cook until tender - couple minutes.  Reduce heat, and stir in tomatoes, water, parsley, basil, oregano, salt, cannelini beans, kidney beans and Parmesan.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 1 hour.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.  Stir into soup.

Submitter: 
Shannon
Description: 
Tomatoes, cannelini beans, kidney beans and ditalini pasta are seasoned with parsley, basil and oregano in this chunky soup.Modified from: Andrea's Pasta Fagioli
