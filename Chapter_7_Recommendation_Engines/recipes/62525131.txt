Title:
Chicken Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6857263.jpg
Ingredients:
1(8oz.) pkg. med. egg noodles, uncooked
1/2 c. butter
1/2 c. flour
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1 t. dried basil
4 c. chicken broth
4 c. chopped cooked chicken
24 oz. cottage cheese
1 lg. egg
2 c. shredded mozzarella cheese
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook noodles according to pkg. directions; drain & set aside.
Melt butter in large saucepan over med. heat.
Stir in flour and next 3 ingredients.
Cook, stirring constantly, 1-2 minutes.
Add broth, stirring until smooth; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 5-8 minutes or until thickened and bubbly.
Stir in chicken; remove from heat.
Combine cottage cheese and egg, stirring well.
Spoon 1/3 of chicken mixture in bottom of a lightly greased 13x9x2 in. baking dish.
Top with half of noodles, half of cottage cheese mixture and 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
Repeat layers ending with chicken mixture.
Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.
