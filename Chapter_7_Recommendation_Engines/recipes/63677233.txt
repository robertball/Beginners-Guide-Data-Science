Title:
Shepherds Pie - clean Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1022936.jpg
Ingredients:
2.5 lb ground turkey or beef
1 large yellow onion, minced
2 tsp oregano, dried ( to taste)
2 tsp basis,dried (to taste)
1/2 tsp garlic powder (to taste)
Salt and pepper to taste
1 15oz can beef broth
12 oz frozen corn
4 medium zucchini, peeled and finely shredded
4 large sweet potatoes
2 tsp cinnamon
4 tsp- 2 tbsp maple syrup

Directions:
Peel the sweet potatos and cut into large uniform chunck and steam until soft.
While sweet potatoes are steaming bRown the meat with onion and spices in a large skillet. Drain off fat and add beef stock and simmer until liquid is reduced. Add additional spices if needed.
Mix in corn and shredded zucchini. Layer meet mixture at the bottom of a 9x13 glass dish.
Mash the swet potatoes with cinnamon and maple syrup then add the mashed sweet potatoes on top of the meat and carefully disburse evenly. For a pretty presentation you an use a large star decorating tip and pipe the sweet potatoes onto the meat layer.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes. The sweet potatoes will retain their heat so be sure to let it cool a bit before serving.
