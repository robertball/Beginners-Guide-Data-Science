Title:
Southwestern Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 Thawed Chicken breasts, cut into chunks
1 package or 1/4 cup Taco Seasoning
1 cup of water
1 green pepper, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 yellow pepper, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 pound Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Cook chicken until no longer pink inside, stirring frequently.  Add Taco Seasoning and water.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes or until water is reduced.
In a separate skillet, saute peppers and onions.
Sprinkle 1/4 cup of cheese on microwave safe plates, covering the entire bottom of the plate.  Microwave on high for 30-45 seconds until cheese is throughly melted.
Spoon peppers and chicken over the melted cheese and serve.

Submitter: 
ALETA1314
Description: 
This quick and easy chicken has a fabulous presentation for guests!  Make it even easier by buying preshredded cheese and bell pepper stirfry, and adding peppers to chicken while simmering.
