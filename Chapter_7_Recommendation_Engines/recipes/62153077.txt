Title:
momma's style dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
100g self raising flour
50g suet
1tsp mixed herbs
pinch seasoning
4-6tbsp cold water

Directions:
place all the dried ingredients into a bowl
1 tbsp at a time add and mix the water until a dough is formed.
roll into balls 3cm in diameter
place in simmering stew/soup/stock and cook for 15-20mins.
**note allow space between each dumpling for them to expand.

Submitter: 
barry
Description: 
jus the way momma makes them. best served in stew or good old broth soup.
