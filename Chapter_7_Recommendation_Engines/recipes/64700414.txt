Title:
Chocolate Chip Sandwich Cookies with Chocolate Buttercream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5342716.jpg
Ingredients:
COOKIES:
2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup soft butter
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1teaspoon vanilla extract
2 large eggs
chocolate chips
CHOCOLATE BUTTERCREAM ICING:
6 Tablespoons soft butter
2 1/3 cup Icing Sugar
3/4 cup cocoa
1/3 cup milk
2 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
CAKE:
Pre heat oven 350'
Gently mix all dry ingredients together in a bowl.
Make a well in center and add wet ingredients.
Mix well and drop by desired size (scoop or spoon) onto parchment lined cookie sheets.
Bake until golden brown, set aside in pairs once cooled
CHOCOLATE BUTTERCREAM ICING:
Whip butter until light and fluffy.
Add salt, vanilla, cocoa and icing sugar. Slowly add milk until incorporated well.
Place some icing into your piping bag fitted with a Wilton Tips Star 2110 and pipe icing onto the backside of one chocolate chip cookie, starting in the center and working your way out. Top with another chocolate chip cookie...........and enjoy!
