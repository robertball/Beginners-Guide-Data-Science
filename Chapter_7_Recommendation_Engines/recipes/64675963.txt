Title:
Rossi's Mandarin Orange/Lettuce Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Different varieties of lettuce : romaine, bib, boston
1/4 cup slivered almonds, carmelized
1 tablespoon parsley, minced
2 tablespoons green onions, chopped
1 can mandarin oranges, drained
Dressing:
4 tablespoons sugar
4 tablespoons red wine vinegar
4-5 shakes Tobasco sauce
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon kosher salt
black pepper

Directions:
Clean and  tear lettuces
Add parsley, onion, almonds and oranges
Add dressing, a little at a time until right consistency
For crowds:
Double recipe at least
