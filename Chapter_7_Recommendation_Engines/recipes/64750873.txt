Title:
Keto INSTANT POT CHICKEN THIGHS with Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Seasoning of your choice
1 cup chicken stock
3 tbsp olive oil divided
6 chicken thighs skin on
4 Cups of Green Beans

Directions:
Set oven at 400 for Green Beans
1. Put the chicken thighs in a bowl and coat the thighs in seasoning and olive oil
2. Select the saut√© setting of the Instant Pot, add in remaining oil and when hot brown the chicken thighs on both sides (about 3 mins on either side).
3. Remove the chicken thighs from the Instant Pot, and set aside on a plate.
4. Add the remaining chicken stock into the Instant Pot, and scrape any bits stuck to the bottom of the pot from frying with a silicon or wooden spoon.
5. Place the trivet inside before adding the chicken thighs on top of the trivet.
6. Cover your Instant Pot and set the valve to the SEALING position.
7. Select the manual or pressure cook button (dependent upon IP model), select high pressure and set the timer to 15 mins.
8. Cook till done then let the steam release naturally about 10-15 mins before opening.
Cook Green Beans in Oven at 400 for 25 minutes
