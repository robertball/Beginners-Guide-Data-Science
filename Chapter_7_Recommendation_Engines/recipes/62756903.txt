Title:
Stuffed Cabbage, Italian Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/637107.jpg
Ingredients:
·2/3 cup water
·1/3 cup uncooked white rice
·8 cabbage leaves
·1 pound lean ground beef
·1/4 cup chopped onion
·1 egg, slightly beaten
·1 teaspoon of garlic salt
·1 teaspoon of Italian seasonings
·1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
·2 jar of spaghetti sauce

Directions:
1.Bring a large pot of water to boil. Cut the center stem off the cabbage add to water.
2.Boil cabbage for about 6 to 8 mins. Leaves will start to fall off the core now  drain hot water and add cold to stop it from cooking anymore.
3.Cook rice as directed.
4.In a medium mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, 1 cup cooked rice, onion, egg, seasonings,  salt and pepper, along with 2 tablespoons of sauce. Mix thoroughly.
5.Divide the beef mixture evenly among the cabbage leaves. Roll and place seam side down in crock pot.
6.Can add extra cabbage between rolls plus sauce
Cover cook for 8 to 10 hours on low or 4 to 6 hours on high.  Add water if it starts to dry up.
