Title:
(Chicken) Armadillo Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package bacon (approx 24 pieces)
24 jalapeno slices
6 slices Pepperjack cheese (divided into fourths)
2 Chicken breast cut into 24 total inch size pieces
George Forman type Grill

Directions:
Preheat grill if necessary.
Assembly:
Take a piece of chicken top it with a jalapeno slice and piece of cheese.
Wrap tightly with piece of bacon.
Repeat until all chicken is wrapped.
Place wrapped chicken on grill and cook until crispy, turning often.
Serve as an appetizer with toothpicks.

Submitter: 
Tessa Miller-Schweder
Description: 
Not your typical armadillo egg! My wonderful friend makes these and everyone I know LOVES them. Make alot because they go super fast!
