Title:
Coconut/Flan Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Sugar, 3 Eggs, 1 can sweetened condensed milk, 1 can evaporated milk, 1 tsp vanilla, 2 cups flour, 1 cup unsweetened finely grated coconut flakes,1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp cinnamon,1/2 tsp ginger, 1 cup crisco shortening, 1 tsp salt.

Directions:
1. Mix sugar, eggs, vanilla, salt,sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk. mix well. 2. Add baking soda, cinnamon,ginger,crisco. mix well. 3. Add coconut & flour. mix well. Place in a well greased 9x13 cake pan. bake in a preheated 350* oven for 40 minutes.
