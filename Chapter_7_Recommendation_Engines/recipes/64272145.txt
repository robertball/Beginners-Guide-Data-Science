Title:
Roasted Squash & Coconut Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1922455.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 kg. butternut squash, halved lengthwise and seeded
3 Tbsp. unsalted butter
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
1 Tbsp. chopped garlic
1 Tbsp. chopped ginger
6 cups chicken stock
1- 400 mL can coconut milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 Tbsp. maple syrup, mixed with 1/4 teaspoon chili oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 F. Line a rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil. Place squash on baking sheet cut-side down and roast for 50 minutes or until cut side is browned and squash is tender when pierced with a fork.
Let cool slightly, then use a spoon to scoop out all the flesh. Set aside.
Heat butter in a large pot on med-high. Add onion and sauté, stirring for 5 minutes or until onion is lightly browned. Add garlic and ginger and sauté one minute longer.
Add squash and stir to combine. Add chicken stock and coconut milk and bring to a boil. Turn heat to med-low and simmer gently for 10 minutes. Purée soup with an immersion blender until completely smooth. Season with salt. Drizzle servings with maple chili oil.
