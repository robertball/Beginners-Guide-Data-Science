Title:
Family Favorite Sage & Onion Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714669.jpg
Ingredients:
14 cups bread cubes
3/4 cup margarine
2 1/2 cups onion,chopped
2 cups celery,chopped
4 tsp.crushed dried sage
2 tsp.seasoning salt
1 1/4 tsp.crushed dried savory
1 tsp.crushed dried marjoram
1/2 tsp.pepper
1/2 tsp.thyme
1/2 tsp.paprika
3 Tbsp.dried parsley OR
1 cup fresh chopped parsley
1 chicken bouillon
1 1/2-2 cups boiled water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
Dissolve bouillon in water.
Add little by little to bread cubes,till desired texture.
