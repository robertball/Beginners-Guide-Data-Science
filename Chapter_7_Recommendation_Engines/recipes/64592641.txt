Title:
Cranberry Crunchies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup flour
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 348ml can cranberry sauce

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients.
Cut in butter.
Place 1/2 in bottom of greased 9"x9" pan.
Cover with cranberry sauce.
Spread rest of crumbs over top.
Bake @ 350 x 45 min.
Can be served warm or cold as a dessert or squares.
Top with ice cream, milk or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
My aunt gave me this recipe for cranberry oat bars that are delicious.
