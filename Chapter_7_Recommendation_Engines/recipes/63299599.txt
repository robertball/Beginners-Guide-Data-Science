Title:
Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c canned crushed tomatoes
1 1/2 T tomato paste
1 T plus 1 tsp sugar
1 tsp dried oregano leaves
1/2 tsp garlic powder
Pinch of ground black pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste.  Whisk until smooth.  Add the sugar, oregano, garlic powder and black pepper.  Stir to blend well.  Store in airtight container for up to five days.  Makes a scant cup, approx enough for four personal pizzas or one large pizza.
