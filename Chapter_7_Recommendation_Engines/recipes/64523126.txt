Title:
Grandma Eleanor's Hot Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 cups cooked chicken (cut into medium pieces)
1 can cream of chicken soup
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 cup cut celery
1 can water chestnuts (sliced)
3 hard boiled eggs
1 cup cooked rice
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp onion (cut fine)
Salt and pepper to taste
Cornflakes
Almonds
Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix soup, mayonnaise, and seasonings.  Add to other ingredients and mix lightly.  Put in casserole dish.
Make buttered cornflakes and almonds: Use about 1 tbsp of butter per cup of cornflakes.   Melt 2 tbsp butter and sautee 2 cups of cornflakes until they get a little brown.  Use up to 1/2 cup of sliced almonds.
Cover with buttered cornflakes and almonds.
Bake 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
HDN
Description: 
A recipe from my husband's grandmother.  Shrimp may be used instead of chicken with cream of shrimp soup.  This recipe freezes well (before cooking).
