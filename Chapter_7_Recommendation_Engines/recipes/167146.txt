Title:
Lemon-Blueberry Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983619.jpg
Ingredients:
32 NILLA Wafers
1 (4 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups cold milk
2 pkg. (4 serving size) JELL-O Lemon Flavor Instant Pudding & Pie Filling
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIPÂ® Whipped Topping, thawed, divided
1 cup blueberries

Directions:
Line bottom and sides of 8-inch square dish with wafers, set aside.
Beat cream cheese in large bowl with electric mixer until creamy. Gradually add milk, mixing well after each addition. Add dry pudding mixes and lemon juice; mix well. Gently stir in 2 cups of the whipped topping and the blueberries. Spoon evenly into prepared dish.
Refrigerate 3 hours or until set. Cut into squares. Serve topped with the remaining whipped topping. Store leftovers in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Cool Whip Brand
Description: 
Blueberries in a creamy mix of lemon pudding and Cool Whip on a crust of Nilla Wafers make a cool, refreshing dessert.
