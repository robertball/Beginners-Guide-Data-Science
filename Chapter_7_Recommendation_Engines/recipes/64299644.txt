Title:
Blue-Ribbon Corn Dog Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 c. all-purpose flour
3/4 T. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1/2 c. yellow cornmeal
1/2 T. butter, melted
3/4 c. milk
16 oz. pkg. hot dogs, sliced into bite-size pieces

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together sugar and egg.
In a separate bowl, mix together flour, baking
powder and salt.
Add flour mixture to sugar mixture.
Add Cornmeal, butter and milk, stirring just  to combine.
Fold in hot dog pieces.
Pour into  well-greased 8" x 8" baking pan.
Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for about 15 mins, or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.   Serves 6

Submitter: 
Karalyn
Description: 
Easy, and tastes like a county fair corn dog.
