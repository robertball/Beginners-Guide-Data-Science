Title:
Cinnamon-Suger Bagel Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cinnamon
Sugar
Butter or plain cream cheese

Directions:
1. Toast bagel the way you like it.
2. melt butter and spread onto bagel or spread plain cream cheese onto bagel.
3. Mix cinnamon and sugar together.
4.Sprinkle cinnamon sugar mix on top of bagel
5. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
cmb2kool
Description: 
A very simple topping that is easy to make. Can either make a great Breakfast or a tasty desert!
