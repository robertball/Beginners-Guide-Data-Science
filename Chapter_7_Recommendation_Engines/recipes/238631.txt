Title:
Mom's Dill Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon dried parsley flakes
1 tablespoon finely chopped green onions (white portion only)
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
1 teaspoon seasoned salt (such as Spice IslandsÂ® Beau Monde Seasoning)

Directions:
Stir sour cream, mayonnaise, parsley, green onion, dill, and seasoned salt together in a bowl until smooth.

Description:
