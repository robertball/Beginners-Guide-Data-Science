Title:
Bhagarey Baingan (Eggplants) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup imli (tamarind) soaked in water
1 tbsp sesame seed
1 tbsp shredded coconut
1 tbsp peanuts
2 large onions, finely chopped
1 pinch cumin
1 pinch mustard seeds
1.5 cups vegetable oil
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp ground cumin
salt
eggplant

Directions:
Roast and grind sesame seed, coconut and peanuts separately.
Microwave chopped onion until dried, 10-20 min.
Grind onions and ground dry items into tamarind mixture to make paste.
Add cumin and mustard seeds in oil and put on heat. Add onion paste when they start crackling.
Add chili powder, turmeric, ground cumin, and pinch of salt. Stir until oil separates.
Leaving tendrils on, make light cross cuts at round ends of eggplant. Add to oil and cover and cook in steam until done, checking frequently and adding water as needed to prevent burning.
