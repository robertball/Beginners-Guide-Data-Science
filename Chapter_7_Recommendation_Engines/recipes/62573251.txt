Title:
Peppermint Cookie Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/543956.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 c butter
1 c sugar
1 egg
3 c flour
2 1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 c milk
1 tsp peppermint
3/4 c crushed peppermint candies

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375.
Cream the butter and sugar together until light in color.  Add the egg and mix well.
Slowly add the dry ingredients mixing in between each cup of flour.
Add the milk and the peppermint extract.
Stir in the crushed peppermint candies.
Form into golfball sized balls onto a cookie sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes until just golden brown to keep the soft inside.

Description: 
Soft and gooey on the inside and crunchy on the outside these will be gone before you can say Happy Holidays!
