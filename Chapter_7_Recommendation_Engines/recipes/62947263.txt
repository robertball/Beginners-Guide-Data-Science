Title:
Easy-for-One Jamaican Curried Ginger Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/724441.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cold chicken breast, pref. smoked
1/2 tsp Herbs de Provence
1-1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon light or fat free mayo or fat-free plain Greek yogurt
1/4 tsp. Jamaican curry powder (If indian cut to 1/8th)
2 tsps White Balsamic Vinegar flavored with ginger and honey*
1 tsp Meyer Lemon olive oil
1/2 tsp Harissa (spicy) olive oil
6-7 seedless black grapes, sliced
1 small handful arugula
Drizzle of French or Thousand Island dressing

Directions:
1. Wash greens and dry thoroughly. Set up on a small plate. Drizzle the dressing around the outer edges.
2. Get out a small high-sided work-bowl. Chop up the chicken and put in the bowl. Add all other ingredients and toss until well combined. Let stand 2-3 minutes to let the herbs moisturize.
3. Scoop on to greens and serve.

Submitter: 
The Jazz Chef
Description: 
An easy recipe for a quick lunch for one person. The spicy aragula balances nicely with the chicken and sweet hints of grape, ginger, herbs and lemon that finish with a whiff of curry.
