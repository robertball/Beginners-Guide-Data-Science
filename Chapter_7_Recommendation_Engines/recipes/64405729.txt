Title:
pasta secret Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pasta and water

Directions:
Take a skillet or large fry pan, place pasta and water to cover cook stirring and add water if needed... drain and reserve the water.... this is liquid gold.
back in pan make your sauce ( what ever you like ). melt butter, add garlic , tomatoe sauce , veggies , replace pasta and reserved water as needed to thicken sauce..
One pan quick pasta method....
