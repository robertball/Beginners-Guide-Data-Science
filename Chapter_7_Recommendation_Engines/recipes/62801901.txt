Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656689.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef or turkey meat
1 15oz can of corn or huminy
1 15oz can of ranch style beans
1 10oz can of diced tomatoes with green chiles(rotel)
1 14.5 oz can of stewed tomatoes
1 package of taco seasoning
1 1oz envelope of Ranch salad drsg and seasoning mix
optional : serve with tortilla chips or corn bread

Directions:
Cook meat until brown; drain off grease. Combine taco seasoning and ranch seasoning with meat.
While meat is cooking, combine in crockpot or in pot on stove top all canned ingredients; allow to simmer.
Combine meat with canned mixture. Cook until hot.
Serve with tortilla chips or cornbread.
