Title:
M&M; Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. margarine
2/3 c. shortening
1 c. white sugar
1 c. brown sugar
2 eggs
2 t. vanilla
3 c. flour
1 t. salt
1 t. baking soda
1 - 12oz pkg. M&M;'s

Directions:
Cream margarine, shortening, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
Add dry ingredients. Stir in M&M's.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10-12 minutes.
