Title:
Cheeseburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/613171.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 - 1 pound ground beef
3/4 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped celery
3/4 cup chopped carrot
2 tablespoons butter
1 tbsp. Worschestshire Sauce
1 tbsp. Better than Boullion Beef
Splash- 1/4 C Red wine
Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder to taste
1/4 cup flour
3 cups chicken broth
3-4 cups of peeled potatoes
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups milk

Directions:
Melt butter in Dutch oven. Add groung beef and vegetables except potatos.   Saute until beef is brown and vegetables soften.  Add Worschestshire, beef boullion, wine and seasonings, and cook together 1- 2 minutes.  Add flour and cook 1 minute. Add broth and potatos.  Cover and cook until potatos are tender.  Add cheese and milk.  Stir to combine, and keep warm on low until ready to serve.
Great crockpot meal after the first part, super easy, and soo yummy!
