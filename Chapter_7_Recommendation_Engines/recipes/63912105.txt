Title:
Seared Steaks with red wine, mushrooms and onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eight oz New York strip steaks trimmed
3/4 tsp kosher salt divided
3/4 tsp black pepper divided
4 tsp canola oil divided
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp chopped fresh rosemary
1 eight oz pkg sliced cremini mushrooms
2 tsp all purpose flour
3/4 cup red wine.
1 tbsp unsalted butter

Directions:
1. Salt and pepper steak, heat heavy pan, add 1 Tbsp oil. Cook steak 3-4 min per side depending on thickness to desired doneness. Remove steaks from pan and keep warm. Add remaining tsp of oil to pan, add onion, rosemary and mushrooms. Cook 5 min or until mushrooms begin to brown. Sprinkle mushrooms with flour and cook 30 seconds. Add wine and bring to boil cooking 1 minute. Scrape pan to loosen fond. Add remaining salt, pepper and butter to pan.
Cut steak across grain, serve with mushroom sauce, mashed potatoes and broccoli (prepared w/ginger, garlic and soy sauce)
