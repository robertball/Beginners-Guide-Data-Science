Title:
End of the Season Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped tomatoes
2 large onions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, sliced
4 cups chicken broth
3 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
4 tbsp butter
4 tbsp flour
1 cup heavy cream
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1/2 tsp sugar
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Warm the olive oil in a large stock pot over medium high heat.  Add garlic and onions and saute until slightly translucent.
Add the tomato and bring to a boil.  Add chicken stock, return to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 minutes.
Remove from heat.  Add basil.  Puree with a submersible blender.  Remove soup from pot and set aside.
In the now empty stock pot, add the butter and melt over medium heat.  Add flour and mix.  Cook until roux is medium brown.
Add several spoonfuls of the tomato mixture to the stock pot and mix thoroughly.  Add remainder of tomato mixture and combine.
Add cream, vinegar, sugar, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.  Heat through.
