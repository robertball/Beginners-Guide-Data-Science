Title:
costco poppy seed muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506397.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c. all purpose flour
2  cup sugar
2 tb poppy seeds
2 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. salt
3 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1 c. oil (can sub 1/2 applesauce)
3 tsp vanilla
3 tsp almond extract flavoring

Directions:
Directions:
1. Mix all dry ingredients in a mixing bowl.
2. add wet ingredients
3. Fill greased or lined muffin tins with batter.
4. Bake muffins at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes or until done.
5. Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing.
