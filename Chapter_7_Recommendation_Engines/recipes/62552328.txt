Title:
Walnut Date Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BARS:
1 cup sifted flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup shortening
34 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 Tbsp grated orange rind
2 Tbsp orange juice
1/2 cup chopped dates
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
FROSTING:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 tsp grated orange rind
1 egg yolk
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 cups powdered sugar
2 tsp orange juice
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Combine first five ingredients.  Cream shortening with sugar until fluffy.  Add egg, orange rind, and juice, and beat well.  Blend in dry mixture until smooth.  Mix in dates and nuts.
Spread evenly in a greased 9" square pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.  Remove from pan.  When cool, frost with orange frosting.
To make the frosting, cream butter until fluffy.  Blend in orange rind, egg yolk, and vanilla.  While beating, gradually add sugar and juice until smooth.  Spread over cake and sprinkle with walnuts.
Cut into 1-1/2" bars.
