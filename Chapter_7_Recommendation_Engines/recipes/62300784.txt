Title:
Chocolate cream dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364576.jpg
Ingredients:
100g milk chocolate
50g dark cooking chocolate
10 large white or pink marshmallows
I can condensed sweetened milk
cream to whip
caster sugar

Directions:
In a bain mari over hot water melt the milk and dark chocolate along with the marshmallows.
Once the chocolate is fully melted the marshmallows should start to become soft.
Add the condensed milk.
Stir until everything is blended and the marshmallows are completely melted.
Line a dish with greaseproof paper and add the mixture.
Cool over ice or set aside until ready to put into fridge.
Place in fridge for 3 hours.
Once set, prepare sweetened cream by whipping cream and caster sugar together.
In a small glass (verrine) add a teaspoon of the chocolate mixture followed by a teaspoon of cream and continue until the glass is full.
Top with cream and some chocolate flakes.
