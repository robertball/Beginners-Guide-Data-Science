Title:
Lamb Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of ground lamb
1 4in fresh rosemary sprig, chopped.
4 3in fresh thyme sprigs, chopped.
1 Tbls garlic power or 3 cloves chopped
pinch of salt
pinch of pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and shmoosh together with hands. Divide in 4ths and shape into patties. Place on grill on medium heat. Cook till done.
Mix 3 Tbls light mayo with 1 Tbls of Dijon mustard.
Serve burger on bun with dijon mayo, tomato and baby greens.

Submitter: 
KLemons
Description: 
I bought a selection of Lamb cuts from a CSA and got several one pound packages of ground lamb. I made the last one tonight and it was the best of all.
