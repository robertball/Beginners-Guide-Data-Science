Title:
Vidu's Fancy Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Whole Grain Bread, 8 slices
1 brick of Havarti Dill cheese
4 tsp Mayonnaise
4 tsp Spicy Brown Mustard
Butter or Margarine

Directions:
1. Slice bread to desired thickness, and butter one side of each slice.
2. Face slices butter side down, add a thin spread of mayonnaise to one slice and a thin spread of mustard to the other.
3. Slice Havarti cheese to 1/4" thickness and add to the sandwich with the buttered sides facing out.
4. Over medium heat, add sandwich to frying pan. Cook on both sides until lightly browned.
