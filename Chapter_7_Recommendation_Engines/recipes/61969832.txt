Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz sausage
1 T finely chopped green onion
1 T chopped fresh parsley
1/4 t pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
8 oz. cream cheese, softened and cut into large cubes
40 medium mushrooms
1/3 c margarine, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Brown sausage and drain. Add onions, parsley, pepper and garlic. Add cream cheese to sausage mixture. Remove mushroom stems and discard. Place mushrooms in shallow baking dish. Brush with melted butter. Stuff mushrooms with sausage mixture. Bake for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
