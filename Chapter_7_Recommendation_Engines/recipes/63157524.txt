Title:
Spicy Queso Blanco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/809262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole box of velveeta queso blanco
2 finely diced fresh jalapeno peppers
3/4 cup of Pace picante sauce (hot)or (medium)
1 tablespoon of cayenne pepper

Directions:
Cube velveeta and put into a microwaveable dish or large sauce pan.
Combine all other ingredients.
If microwaving microwave cheese dip until it is all combined well and stopping to stir dip every 2 minutes.
If using a sauce pan heat on low and stir frequently and then pour into a bowl when hot and mixed together well.
Serve with tortilla chips, or pita chips.  Also goes great on enchiladas or chimichangas.
