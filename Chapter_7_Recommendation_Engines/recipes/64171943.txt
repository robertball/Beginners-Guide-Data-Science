Title:
Quick Tikka Masala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups uncooked rice
1 package Saffron Road Tikka Masala Simmer Sauce
2 tablespoons butter
1 onion, sliced in thin strips
1 red bell pepper, julienned
1 carrot, thinly sliced
1.5 pounds chicken breast
Naan bread

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions.
Melt butter in a large frypan over medium heat.  Saute vegetables until they begin to soften.
Add chicken and saut√© until cooked through.
Pour in simmer sauce and heat until warmed through.
Serve with rice and naan.
