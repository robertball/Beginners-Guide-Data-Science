Title:
Ruth's Chris Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488610.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb lump crab meat, cleaned
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 cup mayo
2 T creole mustard
3 T cracker meal (crushed Ritz)
2 T parsley, minced
1 t. salt
1/2 t. cracked pepper
1 1/2 T evoo
2 T  red/green bell peppers, minced

Directions:
Combine egg, mayo, must, cracker meal, parsley, salt and pepper, mix well. Add crab, gently mix. Refrigerate till needed.
Heat sheet pan in oven @ 400.
Use 1/2 cup measure to shape crab into cakes, place on plate.  Remove heated sheet pan from oven,  drizzle with evoo,  transfer crab cakes to pan, return to oven.
Bake until golden and hot appx 5 mins.  Serve 2 per plate, garnish with minced bell peppers.  Optional drizzle with melted butter.  Serve immediately.

Description: 
More meat, less filler.  A light and flaky baked cake.
