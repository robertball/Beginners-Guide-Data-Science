Title:
Mexican Beef Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 lb. hamburger crumbled and cooked, fat drained
* 1 lb. Velveeta cheese
* 12 to 16 oz. thing of salsa
* chili seasoning to taste

Directions:
In a microwave bowl cube the Velveeta cheese and add the salsa.
Mix in hamburger and microwave at 2 minute intervals until all of the cheese melts. Add chili seasoning to taste and stir in.
