Title:
Instant Pot "Faux-tisserie" Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb whole chicken
2 tbsp. olive oil (divided)
sea salt and black pepper, to taste
1/2 med onion, cut into quarters
5 large cloves fresh garlic, peeled and left whole
Southwest seasoning (recipe below)
1 cup chicken stock/broth, or water
Southwest Seasoning:
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp basil

Directions:
Rub chicken with one tbsp. olive oil and sprinkle w/salt & pepper. Place the onion wedges and garlic cloves inside the chicken. Use butcher's twine to secure the legs.
Turn the Instant Pot on saute. Add the remaining olive oil to the pan. When hot, add the chicken and sear brown both sides, about 4 min per side. Remove the chicken and set aside.
Place the trivet at the bottom of the metal pan and add the chicken stock. Sprinkle seasoning mix over the entire chicken, rubbing it iin and spreading it around to cover the entire chicken.
Place the chicken, breast side up on top of the trivet and secure the lid. Make sure the lid is in the sealing position.
Set the Instant Pot to manual and set timer for 25 min. When the timer beeps, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 min. If the lid will not open, quick release the remaining pressure and remove the chicken.
Allow chicken to rest for 5-10 min before serving
