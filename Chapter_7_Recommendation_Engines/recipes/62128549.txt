Title:
chili cheesy joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chuck
1 small onion chopped
1 tbl chili powder
1 tsp salt
1/3 tsp garlic powder
2  tbl A1 steak sauce
1/4 lb velveeta cubed

Directions:
brown chuck with chili powder, A1, salt, and garlic powder
drain
add onion chopped & simmer 5 minutes (onion still crisp)
turn off heat add velveeta and cover
when cheese melts stir in well
serve on buns
