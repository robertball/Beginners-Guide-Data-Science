Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 c oil
2 c sugar
3 t vanilla
3 c flour
1 t salt
1 t soda
1/4 t baking powder
3 t cinnamon
2 c grated zucchini

Directions:
Mix and bake 1 hour or more (until tests done with toothpick) at 325 degrees. Makes 2 loaves.
