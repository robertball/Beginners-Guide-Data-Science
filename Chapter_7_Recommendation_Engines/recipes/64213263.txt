Title:
Whiskey Blush Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Ice
2 jiggers white whiskey like Jim Beam Ghost
5 oz. Raspberry Seltzer or mineral water
Splash of cranberry juice

Directions:
Fill a lowball with ice. Add white whiskey. Top with raspberry mineral water.  Add a splash of cranberry juice.
