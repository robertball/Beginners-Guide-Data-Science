Title:
Donna's Rhubarb Cake - The Ex's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1 egg
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 cup sour milk (1 cup milk with 1 T. vinegar mixed in)
1 1/2 cups rhubarb,cut up
For topping:
1/3 Cup brown sugar
1 T cinnamon

Directions:
Mix brown sugar, shortening and egg together.
Add vanilla, baking soda, flour and sour milk.  Mix.
Add diced rhubarb and stir to combine.
Put into greased 9 x 13 pan.
Mix 1/3 cup brown sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle on top of the batter.
Bake for 35 - 40 minutes at 350 degrees.
