Title:
Coconut Fricken Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 tablespoon tandoori paste
1/2 cup frozen peas
1 white onion, chopped
1 cup white potato and 1 cup sweet potato, cubed
4 cloves garlic, crushed
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - chopped
1 carrot, cut long/diagonal
1/2 red bell pepper, julienned
4 tablespoons yellow curry powder
squeeze of lemon or lime
2 tablespoons garam masala
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon Kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
a few scrapes fresh ginger
1 (14 ounce) can unsweetened coconut milk
1/3 cup chicken stock
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the onion, 3/4 of the garlic, and curry powder. Cook until tender.
Mix in the chicken, and cook 10 minutes, or until juices run clear.
Mix the carrot, red bell pepper, peas, potatoes, leftover garlic, scrapes of ginger, tandoori paste, brown sugar and kosher salt into the skillet. Pour in the coconut milk, garam masala, and chicken stock. Season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat to low. Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, 30 minutes.  Add Cilantro in the last 5 minutes before serving on basmati rice.

Submitter: 
Frosty
Description: 
Carrot, potato, red pepper, peas, and chicken are cooked in coconut milk and seasoned with curry in this rich, creamy dish.Modified from: Michelle's Coconut Chicken Curry
