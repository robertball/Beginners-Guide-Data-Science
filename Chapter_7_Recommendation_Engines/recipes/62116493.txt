Title:
Ultimate Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 15 oz. cans of green beans, drained, or frozen and defrosted green beans
Garlic salt to taste
4 oz. container of sour cream
1 10.75 oz. cream of mushroom soup
4 oz. processed cheese, sliced 1/8" thick
1 2.8 oz. can of fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place green beans in a 2 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with garlic salt.
In a bowl combine soup and sour cream and pour of green beans. Place sliced processed cheese on top and bake uncovered for 30 minutes. Remove casserole from oven, place fried onions on top and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
