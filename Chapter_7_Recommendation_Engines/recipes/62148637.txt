Title:
Healthy Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of oats
1/2 cup of wheat germ
1/2 cup of almond flour (or ground flax seeds)
2 cups of milk
2 eggs
1/4 cup oil
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
1 packet of stevia ( or 1 tablespoon of honey)
2 teaspoons of cinnamon
2 teaspoons of baking powder
1 teaspoon of baking soda
pinch of salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender. Blend tell smooth.
Heat pan with butter and place 1/4 or 1/2 cup of batter in the pan. Brown pancakes on both pancakes and serve hot with fresh fruit.
