Title:
Anna's pineapple lemonade cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package yellow cake mix
1 cup sour cream
6 oz. frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed (half of reg size can)
3 Tablespoons cream cheese
3 eggs
20 ounce can crushed pineapple, with juice
1 3oz pkg vanilla instant pudding
8 ounces whipped topping

Directions:
In a large bowl combine first 5 ingredients, and beat with mixer at low speed until well incorporated.
Spoon into a greased and floured 9x12 pan or a bunt pan.
Bake at 325 degrees for 40 minutes, or until done.  Cool in refrigerator.
For topping mix 20oz. pineapple with juice, vanilla instant pudding and whipped topping together and spread on chilled cake.  Refrigerate 1-2 hours before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
I thought this was different from the other lemonade cakes on this site because of the sour cream and cream cheese, and pineapple topping.  I think these ingredients together are just heaven on earth...yum!
