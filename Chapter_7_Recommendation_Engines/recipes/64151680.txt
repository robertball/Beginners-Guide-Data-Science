Title:
Beef Stroganoff III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked roast beef
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 Tbsp butter
4 green onions, sliced (white parts only)
2 garlic cloves, minced
3 tablespoons flour
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed beef broth
1/2 teaspoon mustard
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 package fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/3 cup sour cream
1/3 cup white wine
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, saute the onions and garlic and cook until soft. Stir the flour into the juices.
Pour in beef broth and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Lower the heat and stir in mustard and Worcestershire sauce,. Add beef, cover and simmer for 30 minutes or until the meat is tender.
Ten minutes before serving, stir in the mushrooms and cook until mushrooms are done.  Add the sour cream, and white wine. Heat briefly then salt and pepper to taste.
