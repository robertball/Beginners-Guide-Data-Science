Title:
Vegan ~ Healthy Cheesy Zucchini Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chia seeds
1 cup almond milk
2 eggs
2 zucchinis
1 onion
1 1/2 cup corn meal
1 cup flour (spelt or amaranth or quinoa)
2 teaspoons nutritional yeast
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven at 400 degrees.
Chia seeds in almond milk in blender. Let soak for 5-10 minutes
Add 2 eggs to blender.
Blend thoroughly.
Chop the onions and zucchini and pulse in a food processor, until in very small pieces.
Mix dry ingredients.
Add wet ingredients.
Grease baking pan.
Add mixture and bake for 30 minutes.
