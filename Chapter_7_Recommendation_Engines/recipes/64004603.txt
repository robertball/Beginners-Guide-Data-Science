Title:
Chocolate Refrigerator Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup flour
1/2 cup chopped pecans
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 cup powdered sugar
9 ounces cool whip
1 small package of chocolate pudding
1 small package of vanilla pudding
2 3/4 cup milk
1 Hershey bar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix butter, flour and nuts together, spread on 9x13 pan and bake 20 minutes. Cool
Mix together cream cheese, powdered sugar, 1 cup cool whip and spread on baked crust.
Mix puddings and milk on slow speed of mixer according to package directions. Spread on cream cheese layer. Spread remaining cool whip and top with nuts or grated chocolate bar.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
