Title:
Guavdew Smoothie with Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 guava, peeled and seeded
1 cup water
2 slices peeled honeydew
1 slice peeled papaya
1 slice peeled and seeded watermelon
6 ice cubes
1 cup plain yogurt
2 teaspoons palm sugar, melted
1 cup whipped cream

Directions:
Blend guava, water, honeydew, papaya, and watermelon in a blender until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add ice cubes and yogurt; blend until smooth, about 2 minutes.
Put 1 teaspoon palm sugar into each glass and top each with 1/2 of the smoothie and stir. Top each smoothie with 1/2 cup whipped cream.
