Title:
Deb's Amazing Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound beef sirloin tips
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can french onion soup
Dash or two of Worchestershire
1 can beef broth
2-3 tbs flour
1 can corn
Sour cream, to taste
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Mix the beef, soups,and Worchestershire in slow cooker.
Mix the flour with the beef broth in a separate bowl. When it is mixed well, pour in the slow cooker.
Depending on your slow cooker, cook for 4-5 hours on high, or 6-8 hours on low.
Add corn and cook another hour.
Stir in sour cream before serving.
Serve over hot mashed potatoes or egg noodles.

Submitter: 
groucho
Description: 
I needed to make something for dinner and made this with ingredients I had on hand.  I'm sure it's not a true stroganoff, but it is DELICIOUS.  Serve over mashed potatoes or egg noodles.  Add a salad and some french bread. Enjoy!
