Title:
Summer Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045642.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 Tbl butter
5 small yellow squash, trimmed and coarsely chopped
1 large zucchini, trimmed and coarsely chopped
3 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup half and half
2 Tbl tarragon
1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute onions in butter until tender, about 5 minutes.  Add garlic and let cook an additional 1 minute. Add squash, chicken broth,half and half, marjoram, black pepper, tarragon and cayenne pepper. Bring to boil; reduce to simmer and cook 20 minutes, or until squash is tender.
Puree squash and cream cheese in a blender or food processor in batches until smooth. Return to saucepan, and heat through. Do not allow to boil.
