Title:
Sicilian Mushroom Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
1/3 cup chopped onion
2 tbs finely chopped oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes
1 tbs minced fresh oregano, or 1 tsp dried
2-3 tsp minced fresh rosemary, or 1 tsp dried
1 tsp minced garlic
1 4.5 oz jar sliced mushrooms, drained
1 10oz can pizza crust
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 425. Grease 15x10x1 inch baking pan or large cookie sheet. Heat oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add onion, tomatoes, oregano, rosemary and garlic. Cook and stir 4 minutes or until onion is tender. Stir in mushrooms. Remove from heat. Unroll dough and place in greased pan, starting in center, press out with hands to a 12x9 inch rectangle. Spread onion mixture evenly over dough. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 425 for 12-14 minutes until edges of crust are golden brown. Cut into 16 rectangles.
