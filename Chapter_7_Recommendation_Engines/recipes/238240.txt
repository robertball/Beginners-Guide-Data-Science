Title:
Peanut Butter and Banana Frozen Yogurt with Chocolate Chunks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1207683.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups vanilla yogurt (such as Dannon速 Light and Fit速)
2 large bananas, broken into chunks
1/2 cup natural peanut butter (such as Skippy速 Natural), or more to taste
1 (4 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy bar (such as Hershey's速), chopped into chunks

Directions:
Blend yogurt and bananas together in a blender until smooth. Add peanut butter and blend until smooth.
Pour yogurt mixture into your ice cream maker and process according to manufacturers' instructions to solidify the mixture, 15 to 20 minutes. Add the chocolate chunks and process 2 to 3 minutes more. Transfer frozen yogurt to a container and freeze, at least 2 hours.
