Title:
Big Ray's Macaroni Salad Hawaiian Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6833391.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups elbow macaroni, uncooked
1 1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup HEINZ Apple Cider Vinegar
4 Tbsp sugar
1 tsp black pepper
3 stalk celery, chopped
3 small carrot, peeled, shredded
1 bunch green onions, chopped
2 pounds salad shrimp

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to direction, 9 minutes leaving al dente, drain.
Meanwhile, mix mayo, milk, vinegar, sugar,pepper and shrimp until blended.
Place macaroni in large bowl. Add vegetables and mayo mixture; mix lightly.
Refrigerate 4 hours or overnight. Garnish with green onions and serve.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A sweet and tangy macaroni salad is the perfect side dish to help fill out any outdoor BBQ or potluck. You can substitute chopped ham for the shrimp if your not a seafood lover.
