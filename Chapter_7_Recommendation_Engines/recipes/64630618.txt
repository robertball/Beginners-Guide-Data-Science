Title:
Japanese Simmered Bottle Gourd, Okra and Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200 grams bottle gourd
1/2 pc bitter melon
6-10 pcs okra
1 block assuage (deep fried tofu)
1/2 cup kombu dashi
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp Mirin
1 tsp ginger, grated

Directions:
Slice the bitter melon along its length h then take the seeds at the center using a spoon. Slice along its width thinly. Soak in water with salt and drain before using.
Peel and take the seeds of the bottle gourd then cut it into big bite sized pieces.
Cut the tofu into big bite-sized pieces.
Put the bitter melon, bottle gourd and tofu inside the microwave pressure cooker.
Add soy sauce and mirin to the dashi then mix. Pour it over the vegetables inside the microwave pressure cooker.
Cover then cook for 7 minutes using 600W.
Take the pressure cooker then add the okra inside the bowl. Cover and bring back to the microwave and cook for 1 more minute.
Place the vegetables in a serving plate then add the grated ginger to serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
A healthy Japanese side dish. It tastes mild. This recipe is simple and easy.
