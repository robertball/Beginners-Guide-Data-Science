Title:
Roasted Asparagus Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch of fresh asparagus
4 C lettuce or assorted greens
1 C grape tomatoes halved
1/2 C feta cheese
2 T olive oil
garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Cut asparagus into bite size pieces and put in large bowl.  Drizzle olive oil onto asparagus and season with garlic powder to taste. Toss well.  Place on baking sheet and put in oven.  About 20 mins.
Cut lettuce/assorted greens into bite size pieces and place in same bowl as you seasoned the asparagus.
Top with grape tomatoes, feta cheese and roasted asparagus.
Serve immediately.
