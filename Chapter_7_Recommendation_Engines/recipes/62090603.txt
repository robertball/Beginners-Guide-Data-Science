Title:
Amazing Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large garlic cloves
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup crushed corn flakes, corn chex, or rice chex cereal
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 - 1 packet of Ranch flavored Dressing
1 Tbsp. Spike Seasoning
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
1. In the morning put the olive oil and garlic cloves in the blender and blend up really good.  Put it in the fridge and let it sit all day.
2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
3. When you are ready to start cooking, mix the cereal, dressing mix (dry), parmesan cheese, and seasonings together.  4. Flatten the chicken beasts slightly.
5. Dip each breast in the oil mixture, then cover really well in the cereal mixture on both sides.
6. Place on a shallow baking dish
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
