Title:
Pinch of this Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For Sauce:
1/2 cup fresh parsley
6 cloves garlic
1/4 cup Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 white onion
1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
1 tsp oregano (dried)
1tbsp fresh ground pizza seasonings (mccormicks)
1/4 cup milk
For Pizza:
1 bag fresh Pizza dough from store
1/2 red onion, chopped
3 cups baby spinach, chopped
1 cup broccoli florettes, separated, ends cut off
2 roma tomatoes, chopped, seeds removed
1 yellow bell pepper, sliced thin and chopped
1/2 cup sliced green olives
1 cup fresh reduced fat mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2 cup fresh parmesan cheese
1/2 cup fresh goat cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
DO NOT Preheat oven!
Set racks in middle of oven.
Prepare pizza stone or use pizza pan, spray lightly with Pam.
For sauce:
Blend all ingredients in food processor or blender until thick and creamy.  Pour sauce into sautee pan and cook over med-low heat, stirring constantly for 5 minutes. Remove sauce from heat and pour over prepared pizza dough, leaving a 1/2 inch rim clear of sauce.
Prepare pizza toppings as directed. Sprinkle additional parmesan cheese directly over pizza sauce
Top with 1/4 cup of mozzarella cheese.
Top cheese with spinach.
Sprinkle broccoli over spinach.
Sprinkle onion over broccoli.
Top with more mozzarella cheese.
Sprinkle salt and pepper over pizza.
Top with tomatoes, green olives and bell pepper.
Top with additional mozzarella cheese and goat cheese.
Sprinkle more pepper over pizza.
Set pizza in middle rack of oven and heat oven to 525 degrees F.  Cook pizza for 17 minutes, checking at 12 minutes for doneness.
