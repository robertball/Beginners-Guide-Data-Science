Title:
Beef-less Beef Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819356.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups of water
2 beef bouillon cubes
1/2 cup pearl barley
2 chopped carrots
2 chopped celery stalks
1 chopped onion

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine water, beef cubes and barley and bring to a boil.  Add remaining ingredients and reduce heat and simmer for 1.5 hrs.  Add black pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Lucy Mirabella Nino
Description: 
Easy easy easy, creamy & delicious - without any chunks of beef but still beefy tasting!
