Title:
Mom's Onion Gravy Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops
6 thick slices onion
1 tsp. beef broth
1/2 c. hot water
1/2 c. evaporated milk
flour or cornstarch to thicken if desired

Directions:
Brown chops in large skillet on both sides.
Top each chop with a thick slice of onion.
Combine beef broth and hot water and pour over chops.
Sprinkle each onion with salt & pepper.
Reduce heat and cover.
Simmer 40 min. or until chops are cooked through and tender.
Remove chops and keep warm.
Heat drippings and slowly stir in evaporated milk.
Simmer until sauce reduces and thickens.  If needed, add cornstarch or flour slurry to reach desired consistency.

Mom's recipe for yummy skillet pork chops with sweet onions and creamy gravy.
