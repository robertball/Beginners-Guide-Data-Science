Title:
Grandmom's Pasta e Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box thin spaghetti, 1 can chick peas, 1 can tomato paste, 4 whole cloves of garlic (peeled), 2 tablespoons olive oil, grated parmesan cheese, salt & pepper to taste.

Directions:
Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in medium sauce pan coated with the olive oil, add the 4 cloves of garlic and saute until brown; remove and discard.
Add tomato paste to garlic oil and saute until smooth, add can of chick peas (drained) and water to this mixture.
When pasta is finished cooking, drain and bring back to pot.  Add the sauce mixture to the pasta and toss.  Serve warm sprinkled with parmesan cheese and fresh italian bread.
