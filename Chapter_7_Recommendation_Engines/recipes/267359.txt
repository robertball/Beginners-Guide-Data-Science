Title:
Tangy Peanut Butter Cup Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5728743.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® Rich Milk Chocolate Powdered Drink Mix
1 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/2 cup skim milk, or more as needed
5 ice cubes
2 tablespoons peanut butter

Directions:
Add chocolate drink powder, yogurt, milk, ice, and peanut butter to a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Angie Fuller
Description: 
Greek yogurt definitely gives this breakfast a bit of bite  much different from your standard breakfast smoothie. High in protein, this will get you through your morning with energy to spare!
