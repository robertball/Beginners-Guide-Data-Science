Title:
Chicken Sante Fe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 14.5 oz can chicken broth
1/4 teaspooon garlic powder
1/2 cup chunky salsa
1 cup long grain white rice
1/2 cup shredded cheddar or mexican blend cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown chicken on both sides (about 2-3 minutes each side, leaving center pink); set aside.
Pour chicken broth into the large skillet and bring to a boil.
Add garlic powder, salsa and rice to the chicken broth; stir.
Reduce heat to medium-low and place chicken in skillet.
Cover and cook for 20 mins.
Sprinkle with cheese and replace lid for 5 mins or until the cheese has melted.
