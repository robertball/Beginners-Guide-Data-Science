Title:
Cashew Broccoli Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/779448.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs boneless skinless chicken cut into bite size pieces
1 head of broccoli chopped
1 eggplant skinned and diced
3 (8 oz) cans water chestnut pieces
1 (15 oz) can cut baby corn
8 green onions chopped
6 cloves garlic chopped
6 oz cashews toasted
Marinade
2 tablespoons corn starch + 2 tablespoons water
2 tablespoons rice wine
2 tablespoons soy sauce
Brown Sauce
3 tablespoons corn starch + 2 tablespoons water
3 tablespoons rice wine
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons sesame oil

Directions:
Whisk together 2T cornstarch + 2T water, 2T rice wine, 2T soy sauce.
Add chicken to marinade and place in fridge for 30 min.
Prepare Brown Sauce in a separate bowl; 3T cornstarch + 3T water, 3T soy sauce, 2T brown sugar, 2T sesame oil.
Cook chicken thoroughly in a large skillet.
Add eggplant, broccoli, garlic, green onions, corn, water chestnuts and Brown Sauce, cook about 10 minutes.
Lastly stir in toasted cashews.

Submitter: 
meyer
Description: 
Easy and yummy! You can get creative with this recipe and make it your own, add in your favorite veggies, seafood, or tofu. Great served over rice.
