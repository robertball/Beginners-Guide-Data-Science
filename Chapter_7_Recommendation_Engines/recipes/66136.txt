Title:
Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Keebler® Golden Vanilla Wafer Cookies, finely crushed
2 cups peanut butter
2 cups powdered sugar
1 pound chocolate (milk or dark as preferred)

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients in a large bowl. Chill dough, then roll into balls. Chill again, until firm.
Melt chocolate, then dip balls to cover. Let stand until chocolate hardens, or refrigerate about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Keebler® Kitchens
Description: 
A family favorite made better with Keebler® Golden Vanilla Wafers, these peanut butter balls are a great afternoon snack as well as a wonderful addition to the holiday cookie tray.
