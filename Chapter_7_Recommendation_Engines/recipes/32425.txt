Title:
Mongolian Strawberry-Orange Juice Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/795115.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped fresh strawberries
1 cup orange juice
10 cubes ice
1 tablespoon sugar

Directions:
In a blender, combine strawberries, orange juice, ice cubes and sugar. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
