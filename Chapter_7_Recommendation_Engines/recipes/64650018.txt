Title:
Mediterranean Bento Box Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4537156.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cups chopped english cucumber
2 1/2 Cups chopped grape or cherry tomatoes
1/2 Cup chopped kalamata olives
1/2 Cup feta cheese
1 bottle greek dressing

Directions:
1. Put cucumber, tomatoes, olives, and feta in a bowl.
2. Add enough dressing to coat mixture.
3. Serve immediately.
Serving size is 1/2 Cup.
Serve with hummus, pita chips, and cooked couscous.
