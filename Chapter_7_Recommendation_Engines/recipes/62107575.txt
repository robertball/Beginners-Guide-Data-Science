Title:
Cheesy Steak Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the bake:
2 potatoes, peeled and diced.
1 teaspoon salt.
2 stalks broccoli, chopped.
420g beef steak, trimmed and cut into strips.
Salt and pepper, to season.
For the cheese sauce:
8 tbsp double cream.
8 tbsp crumbled cheddar cheese.
Salt and pepper, to season.

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.
Heat a pan of boiling water with salt and cook the diced potatoes for three minutes.
Add the broccoli and cook for a further five minutes.
Drain, place in a gratin dish and season.
Heat a griddle pan for two minutes and season the steak.
Cook the steak for two minutes and then place on top of the potatoes and broccoli.
To make the cheese sauce, place the double cream in a saucepan and bring to a simmer.
Let it reduce and stir in the crumbled cheese.
Season and pour the cheese sauce over the steak and potatoes.
Place in the oven until bubbling.

Submitter: 
Patches
Description: 
Tender steak pieces resting on potatoes and broccoli in a creamy cheese sauce.
