Title:
Turkey Ranch Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb gr. turkey
1 egg
3 Tbsp of Ranch mix, i use this one... http://allrecipes.com/recipe/214481/dry-buttermilk-ranch-mix/

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients thoroughly and form into four patties.
Grill or panfry 4-5 mins a side or until juices run clear and no longer pink inside.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
Since switching over from ground beef to ground turkey, i can't get enough of it and feel almost guilt free eating ground meat! LOL  these were a fav of mine back in the beef days, but now they are my MOST FAV burger! Moist and so very flavourful and EASY!
