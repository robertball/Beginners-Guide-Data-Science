Title:
Oriental Chicken Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz dried bowtie pasta
1/2 medium size red onion (sliced finely, top to root wise)
1 small can sliced black olives (drained)
1 can garbanzo beans (drained)
1 jar roasted red peppers (drained and sliced)
1 jar marinated arthicoke hearts (drained)
4 oz frozen soy beans (edamame) or lima beans
8 oz cubed or shredded chicken (rotisserie leftovers are great!)
1 sachet italian dressing prepared accordingly (or use prepared 8 oz bottle) with addition of:
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon sugar
1 oz grated ginger

Directions:
Boil pasta until al dente.  Add the frozen edamame or lima beans during mid boil.
Meanwhile, toss together all sliced and drained ingredients.
Strain pasta and soy or lima beans when done and add to other ingredients.  Add dressing and toss all ingredients together.  Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Misha
Description: 
Easy pasta salad dish for potluck party or that cookout!  Can be a side dish or a meal in itself!  Omit chicken and it makes a wonderful vegetarian dish.
