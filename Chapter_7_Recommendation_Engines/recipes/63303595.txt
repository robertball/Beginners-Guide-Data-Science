Title:
Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup uncooked penne pasta (use high fiber or whole wheat)
1 cup chopped onion
2 cups chopped brown mushrooms
1 Tbl chop garlic
4 cups fresh spinach
3/4 cup low fat ricotta cheese
1 1/2 cups crushed tomatoes (or tomato sauce)
1/2 cup part-skim mozzarella
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Spray 8x8 pan w/cooking spray. Cook pasta al dente according to pkg directions. Drain & transfer to large bowl.
Spray extra large skillet with cooking spray. Cook onion until soft, about 3 min. Add garlic & mushrooms. Cook until soft. Add spinach & cook until wilted & excess moisture has evaporated, about 3 min. Remove from heat. pat dry. Stir in ricotta.
Transfer contents of skillet to bowl of pasta. Add tomatoes & mozzarella. Toss to mix. Place in baking pan. Sprinkle with Parm & 2 Tbl mozzarella
