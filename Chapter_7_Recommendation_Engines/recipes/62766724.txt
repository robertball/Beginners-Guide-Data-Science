Title:
Orange Cannoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/641041.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tub (475 g) Tre StelleÂ® Extra Smooth Ricotta Cheese
1/4 cup (50 mL) confectioners sugar
1/4 cup (50 mL) mini dark chocolate chips
3 tbsp (45 mL) freshly grated orange rind
2 tbsp (30 mL) orange liquer (Grand Marnier, Triple Sec)
8 cannoli shells
confectioners sugar for dusting if desired

Directions:
Drain Ricotta Cheese to eliminate any excess liquid. In a small bowl combine all ingredients and stir with a wooden spoon until smooth.
No later than an hour before serving, place filling in a Ziploc bag, cut corner off of bag and carefully fill each cannoli with Ricotta Cheese filling.
Dust with confectioners sugar if desired and serve.
Makes 8 cannoli
