Title:
Pineapple Nut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3822437.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
2 cups sugar
2 t. baking soda
2 eggs
2 t. vanilla
1 20oz can crushed pineapple , undrained
ICING
1 8oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter, softened
2 cups powdered sugar
2 tsp. vanilla .
1 cup pecans, crushed
Mix together and spread over warm cake.
Refrigerate

Directions:
Put all ingredients  into a large bowl. Mix at medium speed for a minute or until all well blended
Bake at 350 for 30- 40 minutes. Let cool while you make icing. When cake is almost cool or cooled, frost cake. Can also put pecans on top of cake
ENJOY!!!!
