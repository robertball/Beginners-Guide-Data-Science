Title:
Low Fat Chocolate Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3276458.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small overripe bananas overripe bananas, pureed
1 1/2 cups  Gluten Free 1 to 1 baking flour
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 tsp pure almond extract
1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
1/2 cup reduced sugar maple syrup
1/4 cup coconut palm sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup  dairy free chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees F & spray a baking pan with nonstick cooking spray.
In a blender, combine apple cider vinegar, bananas, vanilla, salt, almond milk, maple syrup and coconut sugar. Blend until bananas are mashed well.
To the blender, add Gluten Free baking flour, baking powder, baking soda, unsweetened cocoa powder.
Place batter in a bowl and add dairy free chocolate chips and mix by hand.
Bake 30 minutes or until a toothpick can be inserted clean.
Allow the bread to sit in the pan one hour before removing then cooling and slicing into 12 pieces.

Megan Olson
Description: 
A low fat, gluten free banana bread made for chocolate lovers! A simple and healthy treat.
