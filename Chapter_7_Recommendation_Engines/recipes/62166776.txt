Title:
Everyday Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6435780.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup butter or margarine
1 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
3 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup rice cereal
1 cup quick-cooking oatmeal
1 cup coconut
Â½ cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Cream butter and sugars then add oil, eggs and vanilla and mix well.
In a separate bowl combined flour, cream of tartar, salt and baking soda then slowly add to wet mixture and mix well
Mix in rice cereal, oatmeal, coconut and pecans.
Spoon dough onto cookie sheets and press down slightly.
Cook for 10 minutes in a 350 degree oven.
