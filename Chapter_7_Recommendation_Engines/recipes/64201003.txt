Title:
Apple Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie Crust - pp 276 - 279 Complete step by step cookbook. (Single crust makes 2 dumplings)
1 T White Sugar
1 T Brown Sugar
1 T Flour
2 Pats or slices of butter
Cinnamon sprinkle on top

Directions:
Make crust recipe.
Put chunks of apple on crust.
Sprinkle Cinnamon on top
Seal Dumpling up.
In baking pan with dumplings already in it, put:  1/4 cup white sugar, 1/4 cup brown sugar, some cinammon, flour, butter and boiling water.
Bake 1 hour at 350.
