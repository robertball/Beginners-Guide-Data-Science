Title:
Cheezy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. margarine
6-7 medium potatoes, scrubbed
salt
pepper
garlic powder
grated cheese

Directions:
Melt margarine in a pyrex dish.
Peel and cube potatoes into a medium dice. Add to margarine.
Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
Bake at 375 degrees, uncovered for about 80 minutes, turning with a spatula several times.
When potatoes are cooked, add cheese and put into the oven until melted. Serve.
