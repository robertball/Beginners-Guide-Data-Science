Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white corn syrup
1 cup dark brown sugar
1/3 tsp. salt
1/3 cup melted butter
1 tsp. vanilla
3 whole eggs
1 heaping cup shelled pecans

Directions:
Mix syrup, sugar, salt, butter, and vanilla.  Add slightly beaten eggs.  Pour into a 9-inch unbaked pie shell.
Sprinkle pecans over filling.
Bake in 350 degree oven for approx. 45 minutes.
You can top with whipped cream or ice cream, but even plain, nothing tops this!
