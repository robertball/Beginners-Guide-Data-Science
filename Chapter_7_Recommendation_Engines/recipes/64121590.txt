Title:
Over That Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of butter, melted
1 cup of graham cracker crumbs
1 cup of chocolate chips
1 cup of coconut
1 cup of chopped nuts
1 14 ounce can of sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Layer ingredients in order listed in an 8X8 or 9X9 inch square pan. Bake at 300 degrees for 40 minutes. Cool and cut into small square.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
My great-grandmother was making Over That long before Magic Cookie Bars. It got it's name because you put the butter in the pan, then over that you sprinkle the graham cracker crumbs, then over that you sprinkle the chocolate chips, then over that, etc.  A family favorite!
