Title:
Slow Cooker Seafood Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018610.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package Velveeta cheese
2 tablespoons cream cheese
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1/2 cup small shrimp, cooked
1/2 cup crabmeat, cooked and flaked
1/2 cup lobster, cooked and flaked
2 teaspoons Old Bay seafood seasoning
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 loaf (1/2-inch-thick) slices French bread, lightly toasted

Directions:
Combine processed cheese food, cream cheese, sour cream, shrimp, crab, and lobster in a crockpot.
Cover and cook on Low heat until cheese is melted, about 1 hour, stirring occasionally to break up lumps.
Once the cheese is melted, stir in seafood seasoning and Worcestershire sauce. Serve with French bread.
