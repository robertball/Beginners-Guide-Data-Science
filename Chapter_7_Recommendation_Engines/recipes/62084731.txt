Title:
Easiest Slow Cooker French Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-5 pound sirloin tip roast
1 oz package dry Au Jus mix
1 cup water
8 slices Provolone cheese
1 package Hoagie rolls

Directions:
Place roast in slow cooker mix water and Au Jus together and pour over roast. Cook on low setting for 6-8 hours.
Remove roast from slow cooker and shred or slice.
Top Hoagie rolls with beef and Provolone serve with small bowls of Au Jus.

Submitter: 
Todi
Description: 
My Family loves French Dips so I created this super simple recipe I can throw together any time. The best part is the juice is created along with the roast.
