Title:
Bulgur with Basil and Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chicken broth
1 medium onion, chopped
2 tbsp butter
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dry or 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh basil
1-1/4 cups bulgur, uncooked
1/3 cup chopped walnuts
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Combine broth, onion, butter, garlic and basil. Heat to boiling.
Add remaining ingredients. Remove from heat. Cover; let stand 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
