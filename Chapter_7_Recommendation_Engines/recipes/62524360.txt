Title:
Tiff's Grandma's Sweet Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C warm water
2 pkgs dry yeast
1/3 C sugar
2 tsp salt
2 C flour
1/3 C shortening
2 eggs
4 C flour

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients. Stir until dissolved.
Add 2c flour, shortening, and eggs and beat 3 min.
Add 4c flour and mix until stiff.
Let sit 20 minutes.
Form rolls in greased pan
Let rise 45 minutes.
Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes - until golden brown.
