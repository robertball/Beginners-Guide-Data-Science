Title:
Broccoli Peanut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing
1 C. Miracle Whip or Mayo
½ C Sugar
2 Tbsp. Vinegar
Salad
3 large bunches of Broccoli  cut flower tops into small pieces
1 lb bacon, fried & crumbled (or one pkg. Oscar Mayer real bacon bits)
2 C shredded cheddar cheese
2 C shredded Swiss cheese or Mozzarella
1 can Honey Roasted Peanuts

Directions:
Cut broccoli and put in large bowl.  Mix dressing and pour over broccoli, stir, and cover.  Let sit overnight in refrigerator.  Before serving, mix in bacon, peanuts, cheeses, and stir well.
*May have to double dressing recipe depending on how much broccoli you use.

Submitter: 
LynnG
Description: 
The sweet of honey roasted peanuts and the salt of the bacon is what makes this salad unique.
