Title:
Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123355.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 oz. taco seasoning packet (half a 1 oz. packet)
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (1 pound)
1 cup salsa
1 cup shredded cheddar
2 T. sour cream, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Mist a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Place taco seasoning mix and chicken a a ziplock bag and shake to coat, then place chicken in baking dish.
Pour salsa over top.
Cover with foil and bake for 20 min., or until chicken is tender and cooked through,
Remove foil, sprinkle chicken with cheese and continue baking until cheese is melted, another 3-5  min.
Top with sour cream, if desired.
