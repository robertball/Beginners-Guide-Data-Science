Title:
Broccoli-Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886980.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup mayonnaise
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup finely onion, chopped
3 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped broccoli
8 ounces Sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
salt and pepper to taste
2 pinches paprika

Directions:
Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a medium bowl, whisk together condensed cream of mushroom soup, mayonnaise, egg and onion.
Place frozen broccoli into a very large mixing bowl.  Break up the frozen broccoli.
Using a rubber spatula, scrape soup-mayonnaise mixture on top of broccoli, and mix well.
Sprinkle on cheese and mix well.
Spread mixture into prepared baking dish, and smooth top of casserole.
Season to taste with salt, pepper and paprika.
Bake at 350ยบ for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
