Title:
Tea Party Sandwiches (puertorrican version) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/426558.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can of spam (12 oz.)
15 oz. of cheese whiz
1 can of pimiento (liquid included)
6 oz. of sandwich spread
2 bags of bread (wheat,and, white to make it colorful)

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender, until well blended.
Spread in to the bread.
Cut the edges of the bread, and, cut in half.
You should end up with 2 mini triangles.  Continue the process.
Sprinkle dry parsley over it or for a tea party a piece of cucumber on top for garnish.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Good
Description: 
This is an old recipe passed on from my mom, easy, tasty, and, kids love them!!
