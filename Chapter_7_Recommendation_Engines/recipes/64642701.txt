Title:
Easy fish bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 fish fillets, about 1 pound13 cup mayonnaise3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted13 cup onion, finely diced or 3/4 teaspoon onion powder14 cup parmesan cheese or 14 cup romano cheese, grated1 tablespoon lemon juice12 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning14 teaspoon black pepper12 cup Panko breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.Place fish, in a lightly greased 9x13 baking dish.In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, butter, onion (or onion powder), cheese, lemon juice, Old Bay, and pepper. Mix until a thick paste is formed.Spread mixture evenly over fish filets, then top with bread crumbs.Bake for about 10 minutes, or until fish is flaky.To finish cooking, place fish under the broiler, for another 2-3 minutes, or until fish is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
The mayo is the key to this recipe.  Easy and delicious and easy enough for a weeknight meal!
