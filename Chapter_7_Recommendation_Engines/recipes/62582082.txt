Title:
Whole Wheat Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C whole wheat flour
1/2 C cold butter
1 C buttermilk
1 t baking soda
1 t sea salt

Directions:
Sift flour before combining w/cold butter (use whisk to sift flour).
Mix together until resembles texture of cornmeal, then stir in buttermilk until it forms thick dough.
Cover bowl and let sit in warm spot overnight or at least 12 hrs.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Knead baking soda and salt into dough.
Flour hands and gently form biscuits either by rolling or dropping on baking sheet.
Bake 10-15 min or until puffed and golden brown.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
After making dough, let it sit overnight in warm spot, then knead, shape and bake.
