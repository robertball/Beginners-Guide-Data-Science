Title:
Randy's Slow Cooker Ravioli Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/996940.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef or turkey
1 25 ounce package frozen cheese ravioli
2 24 ounce jars prepared pasta sauce
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1 teaspoon italian seasoning
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon oregeno
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 cups shredded mozzarella or italian  blend cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef or turkey in a skillet while adding the chopped garlic, garlic powder, salt and pepper.  Drain.
Add both cans of pasta sauce to the drained ground beef along with the italian seasoning, basil and oregano.  Stir.
Ladle a generous layer of the meatsauce mixture on the bottom of the crock pot.
Add a layer of cheese ravioli to cover.
Add another layer of meatsauce and ravioli, alternating layers until the ravioli is all used up.
Ladle any remaining meatsauce on top.
Cook on low 4-6 hours, depending on your crock pot.
During the last 45 minutes, sprinkle the cheese on top and let it melt all the way through.

Submitter: 
RandyOSU
Description: 
A fantastic way to enjoy lasagna without all the fuss!  I thought using frozen ravioli already filled with cheese instead of layering layers of noodles and cheese would make a great dish so easy.  It all comes together, just scoop it out and serve with a salad and enjoy.
