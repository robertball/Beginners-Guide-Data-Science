Title:
Simple Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/819547.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1 pound) loaves frozen bread dough,* thawed, divided
ground cinnamon to taste
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 (4.6 ounce) package cook and serve vanilla pudding mix
2 tablespoons milk
1 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Cut each loaf of dough into 18 pieces.  Arrange half in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.  In a saucepan over low heat, melt butter.  Remove from the heat; stir in brown sugar, pudding mix and milk until smooth.  Pour over dough.  Sprinkle with pecans and raisins if desired.  Arrange remaining pieces of dough over top.  Cover and refrigerate overnight or let stand at room temperature for 3 hours.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 35 minutes or until center sounds hollow when tapped with fingers.  Invert onto a serving platter or baking sheet.

Submitter: 
Tyan  Cadwell
Description: 
'I prepare these nutty rolls every Christmas Eve,' reports Tyan Cadwell of St. John's, Michigan. 'Then I pop them in the oven while we're opening gifts on Christmas morning. They smell delicious while baking.'
