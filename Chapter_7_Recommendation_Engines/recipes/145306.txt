Title:
Archer Farms™ Southwestern Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.4 ounce) boneless pork tenderloin
1/2 cup Archer Farms™ raspberry chipotle grilling sauce
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Heat grill.
Put pork into a resealable plastic bag and add the cooking sauce and seal.
Rub bag to coat the pork with the sauce.
Cover and grill over medium-high heat 20 to 25 minutes, basting occasionally with additional sauce and turn until meat is just no longer pink.
Sprinkle with fresh cilantro and serve with additional sauce.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
The main course is in the bag. Simply rub a raspberry chipotle grilling sauce over a pork roast and you're good to grill.
