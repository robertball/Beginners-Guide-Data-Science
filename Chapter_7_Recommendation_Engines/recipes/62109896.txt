Title:
Herbed Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 hard boiled eggs, peeled and cut in half
4 tsp. chives (about 4 good sized stalks)
2 tsp fresh thyme (about 10 good sized stalks w/ leaves)
1/2 tsp fresh rosemary (about 5 small leaves)
1 tsp fresh oregano (about 6 leaves)
1 tsp fresh sage (about 1 large leaf)
1/8 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. garlic salt
1 T vinegar
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 T mustard
4 T mayonaise
2 T Sour Cream
2 tsp. Splenda

Directions:
Muddle all the fresh herbs except the chives with the salts with a mortar and pestle.
Pour the vinegar into the mortar bowl and swirl it around to mix.
Let sit while you mash up the yolks with a fork until very fine
Add the chives, cut very fine, and pepper to the egg yolks and mix well
Pour the vinegar/herb mix into the egg yolks and mix well
Add the rest of the ingredients and mix
Place the mixture into the egg white shells with a teaspoon

Submitter: 
Lisa Schmidt
Description: 
I use the fresh herbs from my garden for this recipe, so all measurements are in leaves or pieces.  I've approximated measures, where possible.  Feel free to play around with measurements and tweak where needed.  I rarely really measure, so these are approximate.
