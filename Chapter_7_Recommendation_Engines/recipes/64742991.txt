Title:
Clam Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6457906.jpg
Ingredients:
9 large white button mushrooms
6 ounces clams
1 green onion, minced
1/2 tsp minced garlic
pinch roasted garlic powder
1/4 cup clam juice
1 TBS melted butter
1 egg, beaten
1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
2 TBS grated Parmesan & Romano cheese
1 TBS shredded Gruyere cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
Spray a large baking dish with oil.
Gently clean and stem mushrooms.
Combine clams, onions, garlic powder, minced garlic, butter and clam juice in a bowl and mix well.
Add Italian bread crumbs and egg mixing well.
Add Parmesan/Romano and Gruyere cheeses to the clam stuffing and mix well.
Place approximately 1 1/2 teaspoons of the clam stuffing mixture inside mushroom cavity and slightly mound.
Place the stuffed mushrooms in sprayed baking dish, and spray tops lightly with olive oil mist.
Cover and place in oven for about 35- 40 minutes.
