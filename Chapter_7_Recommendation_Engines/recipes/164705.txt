Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Tranquil Mood Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984374.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
3 tablespoons instant banana pudding mix
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal

Directions:
In a large saucepan over low heat, melt margarine. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Stir in banana pudding mix. Add cereal and nuts, stirring until well coated.
Press into lightly-buttered 3.5l (13 x 9-inch) pan. Allow to cool. Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* squares with banana and walnuts make a perfect afternoon treat served with a pot of tea.
