Title:
Everyone's Favorite Pizza Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
.7 oz. packet dry Italian dressing
8 oz. pizza sauce
8 oz. pizza cheese (mozzarella/cheddar cheese blend)
Any pizza toppings (optional)

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and dry Italian Dressing.
Spread on the bottom of a 9" pie plate.
Top with half the shredded cheese, then all the sauce, then remaining shredded cheese.
Heat in microwave until cheese is melted or bake at 350 for 30 minutes until cheese is melted.
Serve with butter crackers.

Submitter: 
FoodFan
Description: 
When ever I serve this dip there are never any leftovers and am always asked for the recipe.
