Title:
Sunny Shell Beach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/373564.jpg
Ingredients:
For salad:
3 small pineapples
1 12 oz bag triple washed romaine lettuce
1 10oz can hearts of palm, sliced
1 carrot, shredded
1 sweet red pepper, diced
1 cup cooked bay scallops
1 cup cooked salad shrimp
3/4 cup shredded coconut
cherry tomatoes for garnish
For dressing:
1/2 cup honey
2 tsp grated onion
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp salt
1 cup vegetable oil (do not use olive oil)
1 1/2 tsp poppyseeds

Directions:
Make the dressing:
Place the first four ingredients in the container of a blender and begin blending on low speed. Slowly add the vegetable oil until all ingredients are blended.  Add poppyseeds and stir.  Place in the refridgerator several hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.
For the salad:
Stand each pineapple upright and cut in half, leaving the top attached.  Remove fruit, leaving a 1" shell.  Dice fruit, and place in a large bowl.  Add the romaine, hearts of palm, carrot. red pepper, shrimp, scallops, and 1/2 cup coconut.  Drizzle with enough dressing to coat well, about a cup.  Refridgerate mixture for several hours before serving.  To serve, place the salad mixture in the  pinapple shells, and top with reserved coconut.  Serve with dressing on the side.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
Shell Beach is located on the sunny French island of St. Barth.  This is my version of the salad served at one it's best beachside restaurants.  Bon appetit!
