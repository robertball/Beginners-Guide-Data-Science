Title:
Eggs from Hell Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 tablespoon veg oil for sauteing
4 medium to large tortillas warmed.(corn is my preference)
1/2 green red or yellow bell pepper (sweet capsicum) medium cubes
1 medium onion, medium cubes
3 cloves fresh garlic, chopped fine
1 500oz can peeled tomatoes, blended till smooth
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 c water
* optional fresh jalepeno,habanero or canned chipotle to your desired spice level
*cilantro chopped fine as garnish, sour cream, shredded cheese, avocado or guacamole on the side.

Directions:
in deep sautee pan on medium heat, sautee with the oil, peppers, onions, and garlic till they sweat, 1-2 min. Add cumin salt and pepper, chillies if using and tomoatoe puree. Taste. Add water until sauce is liquidy and bring to the simmer.
crack eggs individually into seperate cups, and slide gently into simmering sauce, trying not to get them too close together.
cover and cook to desired done-ness (well done, over easy..etc)
with a slotted spoon carefully extract eggs and place on warmed tortillas (on your plate) cover with remaining sauce and garnish.
Enjoy!

Description: 
Spicy mexican poached eggs, and amazing change from the regular ol poached eggs, or rancheros!
