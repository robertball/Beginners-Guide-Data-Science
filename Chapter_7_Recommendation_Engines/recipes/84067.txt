Title:
Jimmy Dean Maple Sausage Waffles with Cinnamon Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983925.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Maple Flavor Jimmy Dean Pork Sausage, cooked, crumbled and drained
2 cups pancake mix
1 1/3 cups milk
1 egg, lightly beaten
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 (20 ounce) can cinnamon apple fruit filling

Directions:
Preheat waffle iron; grease with vegetable oil or shortening. In a large mixing bowl, combine pancake mix, milk, egg, vegetable oil and sausage, stirring until well blended. For each waffle, pour 1/2 cup of mixture onto center of waffle iron and close lid. Bake 5 minutes. Top with fruit filling.

Submitter: 
Jimmy Dean
Description: 
Savory sausage waffles are topped with a cinnamon apple topping for an easy breakfast that will be sure to satisfy.
