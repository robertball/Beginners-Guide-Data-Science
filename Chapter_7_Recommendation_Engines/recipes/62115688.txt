Title:
Mae's Cinnamon Raisin Cornbread Pudding w/Vanilla Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Cornmeal
2 cups Buttermilk biscuit mix
2 Eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup Sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup Raisins
4 Eggs
2 cups Milk
1/2 cup Sugar
1 tsp. Cinnamon
*
Vanilla Sauce:
2 1/2 cups Whole milk
1/4 cup Sugar
1/4 tsp. Vanilla extract
2 Tbs. Cornstarch
1 Pinch salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
In large mixing bowl combine first six ingredients into a smooth batter.  Pour into greased 8x13 baking pan.  Place in oven for 30 to 35 min. until toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely.  Coarsely crumble into 9x14 greased ceramic baking dish, mixing in raisins evenly.
Preheat oven to 350.
In medium bowl whisk 4 eggs, 2 cups milk, 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon briskly until sugar is dissolved.  Pour over cornbread and place in oven for 20 to 25 min. until firm.
In 2qt. saucepan mix cornstarch, sugar and pinch of salt, slowly add milk stirring briskly with a wire whisk until blended.  Cook and stir over medium heat until thick and bubbly, remove from heat and add vanilla, cool 5 min. stirring once.
Pour sauce evenly over cornbread pudding.  Serve while warm.
*
NOTE: If making ahead, cool completely and refrigerate.  Reheat in microwave for 3 to 5 min. before serving.

Submitter: 
Mae Long
Description: 
Cornbread is the base for this unique bread pudding.  I've always gotten raves from family and friends alike when I serve this mouth watering desert.
