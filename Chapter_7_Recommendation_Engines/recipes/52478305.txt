Title:
Korean Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 t sugar or no-calorie equivalent
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
1 cucumber, thinly sliced
1/2 green onion, sliced
1/2 carrot, julienned

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together vinegar, black pepper, sesame seeds, sugar, and red pepper flakes.
. Mix in cucumber, green onions, and carrot. Cover, and refrigerate at least 8 hours.

Submitter: 
ItalianFoodie
Description: 
When I lived in Korea, I was shown this Korean Cucumber salad, made with cucumbers, green onions, and sesame seeds. Goes good with Bulgogi (Korean Barbecued Beef) from this site!!Modified from: Korean Cucumber Salad
