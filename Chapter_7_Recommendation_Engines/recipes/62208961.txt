Title:
cream cheese frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese
4oz butter
2cups powdered suger
2teaspoon vanilla
2tablespoon sour cream

Directions:
mix the cream cheese and butter together
add the powdered sugar small amounts at a time until incorporated
add the sour cream and vanilla mixing until blended.

Submitter: 
Brenda Cremaldi
Description: 
super creamy frosting, not too sweet, enough to frost a layer cake
