Title:
Mulberry Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664581.jpg
Ingredients:
Fruit Mixture:
3 cups mulberries, stems removed and washed
3/8 cup white sugar
1/8 cup flour
1/4 tsp cinnamon
Crumb Topping:
1/4 cup flour
1/8 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsps unsalted butter, softened
Pastry:
recipe for 1 9 inch crust, I use Butter Flaky Pie Crust

Directions:
Fruit Mixture:
Combine mulberries, sugar, flour and cinnamon in a bowl, toss lightly to coat.
Topping:
Combine flour, brown sugar, cinnamon and butter in a bowl, use a fork or pastry cutter to make topping crumbly.
Roll out pie crust thinly, using a 4 inch circle cut out dough, place in muffin cups.
Spoon fruit mixture into pastry cups and sprinkle topping over them.
Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 25 minutes.
Cool on a wire rack for a few minutes, then run a sharp knife around outside to loosen the tart.
Remove tarts from muffin cups and let finish cooling on rack.
