Title:
Hoe Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 to 2 cups boiling water
salt to taste
1 to 1 1/2 cups corn meal

Directions:
Boil water and add salt.  Pour in cornmeal slowly mixing contstantly until a dough is formed.  Let set to cool a bit.  Form into small cakes and fry on both sides about 2 minutes.  Serve with syrup.
