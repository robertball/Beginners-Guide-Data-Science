Title:
Sweet Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4483656.jpg
Ingredients:
2 frozen bananas, chopped
3/4 cup vanilla yogurt
3/4 cup almond milk
4 ice cubes, or as desired (optional)
1 tablespoon peanut butter, or more to taste
1 tablespoon manuka honey, or more to taste

Directions:
Combine bananas, yogurt, almond milk, ice cubes, peanut butter, and honey in a blender or high-speed blender (such as Vitamix(R)); blend until smooth.
