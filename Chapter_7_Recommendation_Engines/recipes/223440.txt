Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, well drained and chopped
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened*
1 cup light mayonnaise
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 cup chopped tomato
1 medium jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
Keebler速 Town House速 Original Crackers

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl combine all ingredients except Keebler Crackers. Place in a shallow baking dish
Bake at 325 degrees F for 35 to 40 minutes or until heated through and lightly browned.
Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Town House速
Description: 
Chopped artichoke hearts, tomatoes, jalapenos, and grated Parmesan cheese in a creamy base is baked until lightly browned for a perfect party dip.
