Title:
Healthiest Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812505.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers, peeled & sliced
2 large carrots, shredded
2 large bell peppers (1 Yellow & 1 Red), cored/seeded & sliced into strips
1/4 red onion, finely diced (optional)
1 large lemon
2 tsp lemon zest

Directions:
Use medium size bowl or serving dish. Chill all veggies prior to preparation.
Mix the following: sliced cucumbers, shredded carrots, sliced bell peppers, diced onion.  Next, add lemon zest and then squeeze the juice from lemon and toss with veggies. That's it!
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
jlo
Description: 
So fresh, crisp, inviting and full of flavor. Tastes best using fresh organic ingredients.  I always get rave reviews for this refreshing salad. Everyone loves the dressing and it's just fresh lemon.
