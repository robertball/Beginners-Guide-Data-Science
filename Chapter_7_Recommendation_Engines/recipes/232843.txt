Title:
Peach Sangria with Raspberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle dry white wine
3/4 cup peach schnapps
2 tablespoons frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 pound peaches, pitted and sliced
3/4 cup frozen raspberries

Directions:
Stir white wine, peach schnapps, and orange juice concentrate together in a large pitcher; add peach slices.
Refrigerate until completely chilled, at least 2 hours; add frozen raspberries just before serving.

Submitter: 
slsmitty
Description: 
A refreshing twist on the classic wine punch. White wine and peach schnapps are floating with white peaches and raspberries. A hit at summer parties! Serve over ice and use a slotted spoon to include sliced peaches and raspberries with each serving. May be topped with 7-UpÂ® or lemon-lime soda for a bubbly drink.
