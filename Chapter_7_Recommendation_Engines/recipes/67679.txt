Honeydew-Orange & Berry Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups honeydew, cantaloupe balls or chunks
1 (15 ounce) can DOLEÂ® Mandarin Oranges, drained
1 cup sliced strawberries, raspberries or blueberries
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
Toss together melon balls, mandarin oranges and strawberries, in medium bowl.
Stir together lime juice and honey; pour over fruit. Toss fruit gently to evenly coat with dressing.
Cover and refrigerate at least 15 minutes to allow flavors to blend.

Description: 
Delicious DOLE fruit stands on its own in this melon and berry salad, lightly sweetened with honey and lime.
