Title:
chicken delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/836408.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breast meat
2 Tbsp. Canola oil
2 onions
1 10 oz tin of sliced mushrooms (drained)
1 1/2 C. Milk
1 C. grated Monterey Jack cheese
1/4 C. butter
4 Tbsp. All purpose flour
Mozzarella cheese for topping chicken

Directions:
Saute chicken breasts in oil until lightly browned and then set aside to cool
Slice onions thinly and put in a dutch oven then cook until translucent.
Add sliced mushrooms to the onions and set to cool for a few minutes
Make cheese sauce by melting butter and flour together until well blended and butter is melted completely.
Add enough milk to make a nice thick sauce and then add to the onion and mushroom mixture
Slice chicken breast horizontally into thin slices and then lay on top of veggie mixture
Add the grated mozzarella over the slices of chicken and then heat through and serve with your favourite vegetable and pasta or rice

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
Slices of chicken breast  with a nice coating of cheese sauce with onions and mushrooms that you can serve over top of noodles or rice with green beans on the side.
