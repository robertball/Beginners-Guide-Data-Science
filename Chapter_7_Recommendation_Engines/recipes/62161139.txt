Title:
Asian Layered Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup finely chopped, cooked chicken
3 Tbsp. sliced green onions
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro, divided
2 Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2 tsp. finely chopped fresh ginger
1/2 tsp. minced garlic
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup unsalted peanuts, chopped
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/2 cup bottled sweet & sour sauce
endive lettuce or sesame crackers

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine chicken, green onions, 1 Tbsp. cilantro, ginger, garlic and soy sauce.
Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours to blend flavors.
To serve, spread cream cheese on a serving platter.
Spoon chicken mixture evenly over cream cheese.
Sprinkle with remaining cilantro, peanuts and carrots.
Drizzle with sweet and sour sauce.
Serve with endive lettuce leaves or sesame crackers.

Submitter: 
Anne Z.
Description: 
A fresh take on the traditional Mexican layer dip.
