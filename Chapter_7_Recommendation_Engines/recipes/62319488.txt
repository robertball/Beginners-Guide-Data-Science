Title:
Mark's Sweet Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Water
1/2 cup Milk
1 1/2 tsp Vanilla
1/4 cup Dark brown sugar
2 tbsp Sugar
1/8 tsp Cardamom
1/4 tsp Corriander
2 tbsp Oil
1 1/4 tsp Salt
3 cups High gluten flour
1 1/2 tsp yeast

Directions:
Combine all but the flour and the yeast in the bottom of the bread machine pan.
Add flour and top off with yeast.
Run the bread machine to the dough cycle.
After cycle completes, remove the dough to a bread pan and bake at 350 F for 35-45 min.
