Title:
Rotisserie Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123983.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 cups long grain rice
1 College Inn Bold Stock Rotisserie Chicken flavor + water to make 2 1/2 cups
1/4 cup cooking wine
Dried cilantro, sprinkle
Dried chives, sprinkle
1/4 tsp rubbed sage
Pinch of anise seeds
salt to taste
1 bag Tyson Grilled and Ready chicken strips
1/2 cup each of celery and onion, chopped
1 Red bell pepper, chopped
2-3 stalks of green onions, chopped
Optional - nuts and raisins

Directions:
Put stock and water in a measuring cup and warm up in the microwave until hot but not boiling.
Put olive oil in pot and turn on heat. Saute dry rice in the oil until off white color.
Add stock/water (warmed up already), and all the rest of the flavors. Let come to a boil, cover and lower the heat to low; cook until fluffy.
Once cooked and before you serve, add the chicken, celery, onions, red bell pepper and green onions and mix well. Let stand for a few minutes to heat through. Serve.

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
This is a 'deconstructed and then constructed' chicken with rice dish.
