Title:
Pasta with Kale, Blue Cheese and Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  pound linguine
1  pound greens of your choice, coarsely chopped
4  tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
2  garlic cloves, minced
2  ounces Roquefort, Gorgonzola or Stilton, mashed with a fork
1  cup creamy Greek yogurt or whole-milk yogurt
1/2  teaspoon coarse salt
Freshly ground black pepper
2/3 cup coarsely ground unskinned almonds

Directions:
Cook pasta in salted water according to package directions.
One minute before the end of cooking, drop greens into the pot.
Drain pasta and greens, reserving 1 cup of cooking liquid.
In the same pan, warm olive oil over medium heat.
Add garlic and saute 1 minute.
Add pasta and greens, cheese, yogurt and about 1/2 cup or more cooking liquid, enough to moisten pasta.
Add salt, pepper and almonds and toss well.
Serve in a warm bowl or platter.
