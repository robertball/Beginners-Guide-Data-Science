Title:
Quick Skillet Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 tbsp shortening
2 cups hot water
1 can diced tomatoes
1/4 cup Heinz 57 sauce
dash pepper
1 cup uncooked elbow macaroni

Directions:
Brown beef and onion in shortening. Drain off excess fat. Stir in water and next five ingredients. Add macaroni and mix well.
Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmmer, uncovered, for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until macaroni is tender.
