Title:
Big Ray's Kentucky Beer Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4146455.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled, at room temperature
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup stout beer, at room temperature
2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tsp white wine vinegar
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp Dijon  mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, or to taste
Crackers and rye bread, for serving

Directions:
In a food processor, combine both cheeses and the butter and pulse until thoroughly incorporated. Add the remaining ingredients and pulse until smooth.
Transfer to a container, then cover and chill for 2 to 3 hours to let the flavors meld. Serve with crackers and rye bread.

Submitter:

Description: 
Want an appetizer that will knock off the socks of your guests? Though it is, as advertised, a blend of beer and cheese, the addition of vinegar, garlic and Worcestershire sauce gives this thick spread a racehorse-strong kick that will knock you off your stool.
