Title:
No Sour Cream Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can mushrooms, drained
1 onion, chopped
1 clove minced garlic
1 T butter
1.5 lb ground beef
4 T flour
1/2 C white wine
1 can cream of mushroom soup
.5-1 can beef broth
worchestershire to taste

Directions:
1.  Brown ground beef with a little salt.  Add mushrooms, onion, and garlic.  Simmer for a few minutes.
2.  Mix flour into white wine in separate container.  Add to meat along with mushroom soup and beef broth.
3.  Simmmer for 10-15 minutes to allow thickening.
4.  Serve with salt, pepper, worchestershire (to add a little zing), and rice or buttered egg noodles.

Submitter: 
rachelguerrero1740
Description: 
I tried to find a recipe for beef straganoff without sour cream because i didn't have any.  The one I found didn't have white wine, which I like.  So I took two recipes and combined them, tweeked to our taste.  My husband and I really enjoyed what I came up with.
