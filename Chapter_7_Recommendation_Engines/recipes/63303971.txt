Title:
Decadent and Simple Mediterranean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/872652.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbsp honey mustard
2 tsp brown mustard
4 tbsp Greek dressing
1 can large black olives, sliced
1 can  sliced beats, sliced
1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes
5 cups fresh baby spinach
1 french baguette
olive oil spray
season salt
garlic powder

Directions:
Start by cutting your baguette into cubes.  Lay on a cookie sheet.  Spray olive oil over the cubes.  Season with season salt and garlic powder as desired.  Then place in oven at 325 for about 5 minutes.  Turn and cook for another 3 minutes or until golden on all  sides.
Add honey mustard, brown mustard, and greek dressing into small mixing bowl. With wire whisk stir together for about a minute. Place in refrigerator.
Slice black olives, beets, and sun dried tomatoes. Wash and dry fresh spinach. Place into large bowl, add black olives, beets, sun dried tomatoes, dressing and toss salad.
Take the home-made croutons off cookie sheet where they were cooling and toss in salad too. Serve and enjoy the decadent flavors of the Mediterranean!!!!!
