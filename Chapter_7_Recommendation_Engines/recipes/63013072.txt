Title:
Cookies & Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Sugar
3/4 Cup Butter
2/3 Cup Evaporated Milk
2 Cups White Morsels
7 oz Marshmallow Cream such as Fluff*
1/2 Cup Crushed Oreo* Chocolate Sandwich Cookies
1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1 Cup Crumbled Oreo* Chocolate Sandwich Cookies

Directions:
Line a 13x9 baking pan with foil or parchment paper
Combine sugar, butter and evaporated milk in a heavy duty saucepan. Over medium heat bring to a full rolling boil stirring constantly for 3-5  minutes. Remove from heat
Add the morsels and marshmallow cream; stir vigorously until the marshmallow cream and morsels are completely melted. Stir in vanilla. Gently fold in 1/2 cup crushed Chocolate Sandwich Cookies. (Do not over mix as this will cause the fudge to lose it's white color) Pour into prepared pan. Sprinkle remaining cup of crumbled Chocolate Sandwich Cookies evenly over fudge and press lightly. Refrigerate for 2 hours or until firm. Lift from pan, remove foil or parchment. Cut into 48 squares

Submitter: 
Lori
Description: 
This Chocolate Sandwich Cookie Fudge is sweet with loads of Oreo* Cookies. The comforting, gratifying  taste of the cookies we love from our childhood.
This lovely fudge is a special addition to any Holiday dessert platter.
