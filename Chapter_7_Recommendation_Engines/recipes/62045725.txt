Title:
Dried Fruit Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound raisins
1 pound figs
1 pound pitted dates
1 pound unsweetened coconut
1 pound pecans or walnuts
1 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. Vanilla

Directions:
Remove stems from the figs. Run all of the fruits and nuts through a meat grinder. Add salt and vanilla. Mix together with your hands and shape into logs. Wrap in plastic wrap. May be chilled but isn't necessary. Serve sliced. Freezes well.

This is a wonderfully sweet treat without any added sugar!
