Title:
Deviled Egg Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Pasta
2 cups mayo
4 tbsp yellow mustard
2 tbsp pickle brine
6 Large Eggs, hard boiled
3 ribs of celery, finely chopped
1/2 medium red onion, finely chopped
1/3 cup of pickle relish
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare pasta to directions on package.  Drain and shake dry.  Peel hard boiled eggs and dice into large chunks.  Combine all your other ingredients except the eggs.  Add pasta to the sauce and mix well, fold in eggs gently.
