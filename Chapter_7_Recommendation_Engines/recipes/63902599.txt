Title:
Beef Tips and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2½ - 3 lbs Stew Meat, cut into bite sized pieces
1 onion, chopped
1 can Rotel Tomatoes, diced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cans Beef Broth (preferably Campbells)
Garlic Powder, Salt, Pepper to taste
½ cup Vegetable Oil
½ cup Flour
Hot Rice

Directions:
In a big pot or DUtch oven:
Brown meat in its own juices, with onion.  Add garlic powder, salt, & pepper to taste.
When the meat is brown, add Rotel, cream of mushroom soup, & beef broth.  mixture should be stiff but not dry.
In a skillet:
Make a roux.  Add ½ cup Oil to skilleu.  Heat over Medium-Low heat.  Add ½ cup flour.  Cook over Medium Low to Medum heat until the roux is browned.
Add roux to meat mixture.  Add water or additional beef broth to make a grave.Stir until gravy thickens.
Serve over hot rice.
