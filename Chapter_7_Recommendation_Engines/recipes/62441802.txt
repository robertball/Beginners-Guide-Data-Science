Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. hamburger
1 ½ cups soft bread crumbs
¾ cup milk
2 tsp. seasoning salt
1 tsp. pepper
2 tsp. Italian seasonings
1 medium carrot, grated
1 small onion, chopped
2 eggs
Glaze:
¼ cup ketchup
3 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 Tbsp. mustard

Directions:
Mix meat loaf ingredients and pour into bread pan.
Mix glaze ingredients and pour over meatloaf.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
