Title:
Chocolate Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954879.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package chocolate cake mix
1/4 teaspoon soda
1/2 cup oil
1 small package chocolate chips
2 eggs
1 cup oatmeal

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Use small cookie scoop or shape into small balls with your hands.
Do not flatten cookies.
Bake 10 minutes in 350 Degree oven.
