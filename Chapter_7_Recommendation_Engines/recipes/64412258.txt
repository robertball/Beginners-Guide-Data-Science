Title:
Dip for the grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of good sausage, casings removed
1 T dried oregano
1 cup, shredded cheddar cheese
1 clove of garlic
1 - 15 oz can ready made chili
1 - 8 oz package cream cheese, broken up

Directions:
preheat grill to medium
in a large cast iron or heat resistant skillet, crumble and brown the sausage, drain, then ad remaining ingredients.  reduce heat to low, simmer mixture until it thickens and the cheese is melted, about 5 minutes,
immediately place pan on the edge of grill to keep warm and use right from the grill as a dip for pieces of crunchy bread or cut up pita bread or pita crisps

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
great dip for when the boys are bbq'g and the peanut crew can have some munchies while waiting for dinner
