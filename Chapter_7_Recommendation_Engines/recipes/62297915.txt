Title:
Corn Chip Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceburg lettuce
2 cups corn chips
2 avocados, pitted & peeled
2 cups grape tomatoes
1 cup mayonnaise
1 pkg dry ranch mix

Directions:
Wash lettuce and tomatoes and cut into bite sized pieces and put into large serving bowl.
Slice avocado and add to lettuce and tomatoes.
Mix mayonnaise with ranch mix and pour over salad.
Add corn chips and mix thoroughly. Serve immediately or chill for an hour then serve.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
This was made for an office party one year. Its light, its easy and tastes absolutely fabulous!
