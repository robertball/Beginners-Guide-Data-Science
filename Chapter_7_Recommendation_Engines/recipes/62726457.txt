Title:
Strawberry Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 C flour
2/3 C softened margarine
3 T sugar
Filling:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 C confectioners sugar
8-12 oz Cool Whip
Glaze:
1 1/2 C sugar
1 1/2 C boiling water
4 T cornstarch
1 box strawberry jello
1 qt. fresh strawberries

Directions:
Combine all crust ingred. and pat into a pizza pan. Bake 8 min. at 350 degrees. Cool
Combine cream cheese and sugar with a mixer. Fold in Cool Whip. Spread on cooled crust, then add strawberry glaze.
Combine sugar for glaze with boiling water and cornstarch and bring to a boil. Boil until thick. Add jello and allow to stand until cool. Add fresh strawberries. Place on pizza. Refrigerate for an hour or more.
