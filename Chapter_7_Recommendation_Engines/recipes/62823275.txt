Title:
Veggie Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. (uncooked) pasta spirals
1/2 med. sweet onion, diced (about 1/4 cup)
1 green pepper, chopped
1/2 small hot pepper of your choice, seeds and ribs removed, finely chopped
1 medium cucumber, seeded and chopped
2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
DRESSING
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup tomato sauce
juice of 1-2 limes (about 1/4 cup)
3 Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
fresh black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to manufacturer's directions, drain. While it cools, chop vegetables. Whisk dressing ingredients together. Stir veggies and dressing into pasta. Refrigerate in a non-reactive bowl, stirring after a few hours.

Submitter: 
janet7th
Description: 
This is a different and healthy pasta salad. Use whole grain or low carb pasta if you like and substitute veggies to suit your taste.
