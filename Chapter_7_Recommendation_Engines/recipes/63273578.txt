Title:
Cheesy Chicken and Wild Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Casserole:
3 Tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 medium onion, finely diced
8 oz mushrooms, chopped.
3 carrots, peeled and diced
2 Tablespoons fresh minced garlic
2 Cups shredded, cooked chicken breast
2 Cups steamed white rice
16 oz prepared wild rice (I get mine at Trader Joes, LOVE it)
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
______
Cheese Sauce
4 Tablespoons butter
1/4 Cup all purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 Cups chicken broth
2 Cups shredded cheddar cheese
Top with 1 1/2 Cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Make a foil packet and put in chicken, onion, garlic, carrots, and mushrooms. Brush with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake at 220 for 3 or 4 hours.  Shred chicken and combine with wild and white rice in casserole dish.
To prepare cheese sauce, melt butter in saucepan and slowly whisk in flour. Then whisk in chicken broth and salt and pepper. Bring just barely to a boil then remove from heat and whisk in cheese. Add cheese sauce to wild rice mixture, then top with cheddar cheese and bake at 350 for 25 min.
