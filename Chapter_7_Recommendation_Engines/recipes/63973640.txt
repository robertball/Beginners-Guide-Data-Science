Title:
Chicken and rice casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked rice (preferably wild rice)
1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup sliced red or green peppers
1/2 cup sliced onion
1 10.5oz can cream of chicken soup
1 lb skinless chicken (breast or thighs)
Salt and pepper to taste
water (fill cream of chicken soup can with water)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Spread uncooked rice across bottom of un-greased, 9x13in casserole dish.
Rinse chicken and place on top of rice. Make sure chicken is skinless. Bones are OK.
Slice mushrooms, peppers, and onions and place on top of chicken and rice in casserole dish.
In a separate bowl, mix cream of chicken soup with one can of water.
Pour soup mixture over chicken, rice, and vegetables.
Cover casserole dish tightly with foil.
Cook for about 1 hour or until chicken is cooked.
