Title:
Bob and Di's Chipotle Choco Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup wheat flour
1/2 cup Hershe's cocoa
2 tsp chipotle powder
1/8 tsp salt
1 tsp corriander
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/3 cup sugar
2 sticks butter
1/2 cup bittersweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Nuke the wet ingredients (butter and chips) in the microwave.
add dry ingredients and mix well
place 24 blobs onto a no stick cookie sheet (or 2)
bake at 350 F for 12 minutes

Submitter: 
KirbyZmom
Description: 
my SIL Diane got this recipe, with some prodding, from her friend Bob. The cookies have a slight kick, but are SOOOO good.
