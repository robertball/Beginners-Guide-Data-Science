Title:
Endless Paleo Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds shredded chicken
3 cups diced dill pickles
3 cups diced celery
3 cups craisins (or half cut seedless grapes)
3 cups walnuts
2 cups homemade garlic mayonnaise aioli

Directions:
Boil chicken in water with 2 oz salt for 30 minutes or until done.
Shred the chicken.
Combine cooled down chicken with all ingredients expect aioli in very large bowl.
Divide mixture into two separate storage containers.
With large spoon mix 1 cup of aioli with mixture in first container.
Save remaining cup of aioli and second container with the remaining mixture separately for later use.
Refrigerate prepared salad, aioli, and chicken mixture.
