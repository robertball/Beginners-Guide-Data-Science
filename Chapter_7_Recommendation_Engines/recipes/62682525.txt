Title:
Mussaman curry paste- Nam Prik Gaeng Mussaman Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T sliced green onions
1 T chopped garlic
1 t chopped ginger
1 T sliced lemongrass
2 cloves
1 T coriandar seeds
1 t cumin seeds
5 black peppercorns
3 dried chilies, sliced, dressed and soaked 15 min in hot water
1 t salt
1 t dried shrimp paste (dry-fry)

Directions:
Dry-fry onions, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, cloves, coriander, and cumin seeds for 5 min. Grind to a powder.
Add remaind except shrimp paste, grind. Add paste and grind again.
