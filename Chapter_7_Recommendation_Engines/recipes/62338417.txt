Title:
Miracle Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (26 ounce) jar PregoÂ® Traditional Italian Sauce
6 uncooked lasagna noodles
1 pkg frozen spinach, thawed & drained
1 large jar mushrooms
1 package shredded carrots
1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Divide out 1 cup sauce then mix remaining sauce with spinach, mushrooms and shredded carrots.  Spread 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch).  Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce.  Top with remaining 3 uncooked  noodles and remaining pasta sauce. Cover.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour  Uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese.  Let stand 5 minutes.
