Title:
Bear Paw Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
cup sugar (Sugar in the Raw)
1/3 cup cocoa
cup brown sugar
chocolate chips
cup almonds*
cup white chocolate*
1 teaspoon Vanilla
2/3 cup oil
3 eggs

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350
Combine day ingredients in to a bowl. Add to the dry ingredient the wet and beat until well mixed.
Pour into a greased 8 inch square pan.
Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, until a tooth pick come out clean.
* Variations:
Instead of the almonds try pecans or walnuts
Instead of the white chocolate try... Hersheys Carmel Chips or Butter Scotch chips

Submitter: 
Behr
Description: 
This is a great chewy brownie. Can be made with several variations. The dry ingredients can be put in a jar as a starter kit gift.
