Title:
Ground Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
3T flour
1-1/2 c water
2t beef boullion
1T parsley
1T ketchup
1t salt/pepper
4oz can mushrooms (optional)
1c sour cream
egg noodles

Directions:
Brown and drain ground beef.
Add onion and garlic and cook to soften onion.
Melt butter; add flour and cook 1 minute.
Stir in water and other ingredients except sour cream.
Boil, reduce, and simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in sour cream and stir to heat.
Serve over cooked egg noodles.
