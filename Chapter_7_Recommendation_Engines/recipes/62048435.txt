Title:
Pistachio Dessert Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 stick butter
1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup English walnuts
2nd layer
8 ounces cream cheese
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 large Cool Whip
3rd layer
2 boxes Pistachio instant pudding
4th layer
1/2 large Cool Whip

Directions:
Crust
1. Combine all ingredients
2. Press in buttered 9x13 pan
3. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 20 minutes
2nd layer
1. Mix together room temperature cream cheese with powdered sugar and Cool Whip.
2. Spread on cooled crust.
3rd layer
1. Make instant Pistachio pudding following instructions on box.
2. Layer.
4th layer
1. Layer rest of Cool Whip on top
2. refrigerate and serve cold

Submitter: 
Tracie
Description: 
Pistachio pudding layered between a crust bottom and whipped topping.  This is a family favorite.  You can also use different variations of the recipe (eg. sugar-free, fat-free pudding, or even different flavors of pudding).
