Title:
Seafood Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz Bottles of Clam Juice
1/4 cup of Red Cooking Wine
1/2 Container of Sofrito (spanish section of freezer isle)
1/2 Pound of Medium Shrimps
1/2 Pound of Scallops
10 Large Mussels
1 Bunch Crab Legs/Claws
1/2 Cups of Water (add as needed)
2 Filets of Whiting Fish
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
1) Clean and Wash all seafood thoroughly
2) In a large pot on medium-high heat, pour in the two bottles of clam juice. Next, add the red cooking wine, the sofrito, and the water.
3) Add all seafood and add the filet of fish into the pot and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover pot and lower heat to medium-low.
4) After about 25 minutes check the soup. If all the mussels have opened then the soup is fully cooked. Enjoy!
