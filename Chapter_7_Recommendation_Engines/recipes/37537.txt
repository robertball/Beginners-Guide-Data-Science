Title:
Open-Faced Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988431.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2 tablespoons margarine or butter
2 cups STOVE TOP Stuffing Mix for Chicken in the Canister
4 slices cooked turkey
1 cup whole berry cranberry sauce
1/2 cup hot turkey gravy

Directions:
Bring  water and margarine to boil in medium saucepan.  Stir in stuffing mix; cover.  Remove from heat.  Let stand 5 minutes.
Spread cranberry sauce evenly over turkey slices; top with stuffing.  Serve with gravy.
