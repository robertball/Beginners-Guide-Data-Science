Title:
Sausage & Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. fully cooked smoked turkey sausage sliced
1 medium onion, diced
1 T. minced garlic
6 cups chicken broth
3 medium potatoes, diced
3 carrots sliced or diced
8 cups chopped kale, stems removed
crushed red pepper or flakes

Directions:
Heat 5 quart pot, add sausage saute and saute 5 minutes or until brown.  Add onion & garlic, carrots and broth, bring to a boil.  Stir in kale and boil 10 minutes.  Add potatoes, cook until tender, put in red pepper flakes.

Submitter: 
Mary Guilds
Description: 
Great soup and so many vitamins!  It was surprising how filling this soup was.  I used ground pork in my recipe and was very good.
