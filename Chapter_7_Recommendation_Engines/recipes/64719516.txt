Title:
Yellow Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs yellow squash
2 onions, chopped
1 1/2 C grated carrot
1 1/2 C sour cream
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz herbed stuffing mix
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook yellow squash and onions until tender, drain and mash.  Combine sour cream, cream soups, carrots and squash.  In a 9X13 inch pan layer 1/2 stuffing on the bottom then vegetable mixture and 1/2 stuffing mixture on the top.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes until bubbly and starting to brown.
