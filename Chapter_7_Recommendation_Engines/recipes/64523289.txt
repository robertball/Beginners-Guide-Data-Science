Title:
Biscuit breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Biscuits baked and cooled
3/4 cup Sausage fried and crumbled
1/4 cup Bacon fried and crumbled (3 slices)
1.5 cups Shredded cheddar cheese
4 eggs
2 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
oven 350Â°
Spray 8x8 pan with pam. Cut cooled biscuits into quarters. (24 pcs) and place in sprayed pan.
Add sausage, bacon and 1 cup cheese, gently stir ingredients.
Beat eggs, milk and black pepper together and pour over biscuit mixture.
Top with remaining cheese.
Bake for 25 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
Let set for 10 minutes before serving.
