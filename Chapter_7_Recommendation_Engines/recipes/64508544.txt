Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
2 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
8 cups confectioners' sugar (about 2 lbs.)
1 tablespoon milk

Directions:
In large bowl, beat butter and cream cheese
Slowly add confectioners' sugar and milk. Beat on high until smooth
add milk 1 teaspoon at a time until icing reaches desired consistency

Description: 
The Frosting I use for any recipe that calls for a cream cheese frosting!
