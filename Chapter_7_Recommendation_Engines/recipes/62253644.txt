Title:
Marshmallow Buttercream Frostin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup room temp butter
1 7oz jar marshmallow fluff
1 1/2 cup powder sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Cream together butter and marshmallow.  Add vanilla and combine well.  Slowly add powdered sugar beat till fluffy.
