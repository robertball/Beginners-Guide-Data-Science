Title:
Slow-Cooked White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
1 medium onion, chopped
3 garlic clove, minced
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 medium bell peppers, one red and one green, chopped
2 small jalapenos, seeded and chopped
2 1/2 cups chicken stock
2 (15 ounce) can white kidney or cannelini beans, rinsed and drained
1 can small white beans
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained, and mashed
1 tablespoon ground cumin
Dash of Tabasco sauce

Directions:
In a slow cooker pan, saute chicken, onion and garlic in oil until onion is tender. Transfer to a slow cooker. Stir in the remaining ingredients. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours or until chicken juices run clear and flavors are blended.

Submitter: 
Melanie
Description: 
This satisfying slow-simmered chili features chicken, two kinds of beans and crunchy corn. 'It's quick, easy and tastes great,' says Lori Weber of Wentzville, Missouri. 'It's a family favorite that we enjoy with corn bread,' she adds.Modified from: Slow-Cooked White Chili
