Title:
Classic ROKA Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1087284.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1 (5 ounce) jar KRAFT ROKA Blue Spread
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 cup chopped PLANTERS Pecans
1/4 cup dried cranberries
RITZ Crackers

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients until well blended. Refrigerate 2 hours.
Shape into ball; coat with chopped pecans and dried cranberries.
Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia
Description: 
Take a trip down memory lane! This Classic ROKA Cheese Ball, studded with dried cranberries and chopped pecans, tastes as good as you remember.
