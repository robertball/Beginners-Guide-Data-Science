Title:
Chicken Glazed With Honey Mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980751.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Dijon mustard
1/4 cup honey
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon steak sauce
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
In a medium bowl, mix the mustard, honey, mayonnaise and steak sauce.  Add chicken and turn to coat well.
Place chicken on grill; cook, covered, until well seared and cooked through, or internal temperature reads 180 degrees F (80 degrees C), about 10 to 12 minutes. Serve.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
Mustard, honey, mayonnaise and steak sauce are the ingredients that make this flavorful coating for chicken.
