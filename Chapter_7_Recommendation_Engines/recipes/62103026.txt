Title:
Seafood Fettucine Alfredo with Mushrooms and peapods Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of fettuccini pasta
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
6-8 cloves of garlic
1 cup mushrooms
1 1/2 cups of shrimp, scallops, lobster,calamari-any seafood combination of your choice
2 tablespoons of Old Bay seafood seasoning
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 cup baby pea pods
2 cups half and half
12 tablespoons of parmesean cheese
2 tablespoons of parley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.  Add pasta and cook according to package instructions.
2. In a large skillet add evoo and butter and saute garlic for 1 minute.
3. Add mushrooms and saute until tender.
4. Add seafood, Old Bay seasoning, and red pepper flakes saute for one minute.
5. Add peapods.
6.  Add half and half.  Sprinkle parmesan cheese in one table spoon at a time stirring constantly.  After all the cheese has been added, mix in parsely and salt and pepper to taste.
7. Stir frequently making sure it does not boil.  Sauce will take a while to thicken. Cook until sauce reaches desired thickness.
8.  When sauce is thickened to desired consistency-combine with pasta and serve hot in a shallow bowl.

Submitter: 
Wendy Dixon
Description: 
This is a wonderful creamy dish that is very flavorful and great for any dinner party.
