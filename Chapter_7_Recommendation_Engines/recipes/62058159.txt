Title:
Mexican Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. boneless beef round or chuck, cut into 1" cubes
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup beef broth
1 cup salsa (any variety)
1 (15 oz.) can black beans, drained
2 zucchini, cut into cubes
1 cup corn (frozen or canned)
2 Tbsp. cornstarch, dissolved in
3 Tbsp. water

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Brown beef, then drain off the fat. Add salt, then stir in broth and salsa. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 1 hour, until beef is tender. Add beans, zucchini and corn. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and cook for 15 minutes, until veggies are tender. Add cornstarch/water mixture. Cook and stir 1 miunte or until thickened and bubbly.
Serve topped with sour cream and shredded cheddar.

Submitter: 
GINAGINA
Description: 
Salsa and black beans give a spicy twist to an old favorite. I tried this with venison, and it was FANtastic!
