Title:
Cranberry Chicken (or Turkey) Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c brown sugar
1/2 can  whole berry cranberry sauce
2 lbs ground chicken or turkey
3/4 c (6 oz) evaporated milk
2 tbsp bottled steak sauce
2 tbsp ketchup
1 tsp prepared mustard
3/4 c cracker crumbs
2 eggs
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp garlic powder ( or to taste)
2 tbsp diced onion

Directions:
Sprinkle brown sugar over bottom of greased 9x5 loaf pan.
Spread cranberry sauce over br sugar and set aside.
In large bowl, combine all remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Shape into loaf and set into loaf pan, patting down loaf until smooth and firm.
Bake at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes or until done.
Let set in pan for 5 minutes, then invert onto serving platter.
