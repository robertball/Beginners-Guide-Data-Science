Title:
Coconut Quinoa Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups quinoa
1 cup coconut milk
2 cups water (or broth)
1 bunch green onions, sliced
1 red bell pepper, seeds removed, diced small
3 - 4 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp crushed red chili flakes
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
Sea salt and ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients except the cilantro in a large sauce pan over medium-high heat.  Bring to a boil, then turn down the heat and allow to simmer for 15 - 20 minutes.
Remove from heat and allow to sit for about 5 minutes.
Add chopped cilantro and gently fluff with a fork.  Serve hot.
