Title:
Southern Salmon Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Salmon (deboned & de-skinned, if desired)
1 can Evoprated milk
1 stick of butter or margarine
6 medium potatoes, peeled & cut into 1 inch cubes
1 medium onion, chopped
salt, pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes & onion in just enough water to cover them. Add salt, pepper and liquid off the salmon. Boil until potatoes are just tender. Turn down heat to medium-low. Add butter or margarine, stir till melted, add can evaporated milk & 1 1/2 - 2 can's of water. Cook on low for about 10 - 15 minutes. Serve with Saltine or Oyster Crackers.
Other serving suggestion's: Add tobasco sauce if you like things a little spicey.

Submitter: 
Chris Braddy
Description: 
Great when its cold outside to warm you up inside.
