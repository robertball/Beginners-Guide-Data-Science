Title:
Creamy Greek Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 G Ranch Dressing
2 Cucumber (seeded and rough chopped)
1 Bell Pepper (rough chopped)
1 Red Onion (rough chopped)
1 C Kalamata Olives
1/4 C Dried Oregano
1/4 C Dried Basil (or 1 C Fresh)
2 T Black Pepper
4 C Crumbled Feta Cheese

Directions:
In a food processor, puree the cucumber, bell pepper, olives, oregano, basil, black pepper, and feta.
Mix in with Ranch dressing.
Keep chilled.
Lasts 2 weeks.
