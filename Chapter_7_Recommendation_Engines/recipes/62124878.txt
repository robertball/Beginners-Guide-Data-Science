Title:
Kansas City Steak Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup flour
1/3 cup butter
64 oz beef broth
1 cup frozen peas
1 cup frozen corn
1/2 cup matchstick carrots
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup diced onion
1 14oz can petite diced tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2lbs beef stew meat, diced into bite sized pieces
1 1/2 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp worcestershire
1/2 tsp dried basil
1 cup barley
3 medium potatoes, peeled & cubed

Directions:
Treat soup pan with non-stick spray and brown meat until cooked through; drain off fat and put meat aside.
Place butter in pan and melt.
Saut√© celery, onion, carrots and garlic until limp; whisk in flour to form roux.
Slowly add beef broth and cook, whisking until thickened.
Add the remaining ingredients except for frozen veggies.
Simmer until barley is cooked, approximately 1 hour.
Add frozen veggies.  Simmer for an additional 15 minutes or until they are heated through.

Submitter: 
BOILERMISH
Description: 
My husband loves this soup; says it tastes just like the soup he got out at his favorite steak house in Kansas City.  Serve with crusty bread and salad.
