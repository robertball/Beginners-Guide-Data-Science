Title:
Pan-Seared Chicken Breasts wit hCreamy lemon sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lemon
4 chicken breasts
1 tbsp Greek Rub
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
Orzo & Sauce
8 oz uncooked orzo
6 oz fresh baby spinach leaves
1 lemon
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 tbsp greek rub

Directions:
1. Cut lemon into four slices. Season with rub , place lemon slice on top of breast. Place chicken in Deep covered baker, microwave, high 10 min, REmove from baker. 2. Cook orzo according to pkg. directions. Place spinach in colander and drain orzo over spinach. Return orzo mixture to saucepan. For sauce, zest lemon to measure 1/2 tsp zest. Juice lemon, combine zest, juice and soup and rub in bowl set aside. 3. Add oil to pan. Med heat. Add chicken to skillet, cook 4-6 min or until golden brown turning once. Remove 2/3 cup chicken juices from deep covered baker, add to sauce mixture. Then add 1/4 cup sauce to orzo. And microwave remaining sauce for 2-3 min. Plate orzo, chicken and sauce.
