Title:
J C TACO SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 HEAD LETTUCE
3 TOMATOES-CUT IN BITE SIZE PIECES
1 CAN KIDNEY BEANS,RINSED AND DRAINED
1 1/2 LB.LEAN HAMBERGER
1 MEDIUM ONION,CHOPPED
1 CUP CHEDDAR CHEESE,GRATED
1 PKG.SPICY TACO SEASONING
CATALINA SALAD DRESSING
DORITO'S NACHO TACO CHIPS

Directions:
Cook hamberger,add taco seasoning,drain grease,chill.
Break up lettuce in pieces,add ALL ingredients.In individual bowls ,to avoid soggy salad,add desired dressing.Break tacos in pieces mix into salad.
I serve this salad in a baked big red wrap.
I put the wrap in a small metal oven safe bowl,making a bowl shape.Bake for 8 min.at 375.
