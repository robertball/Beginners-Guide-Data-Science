Title:
Healthier Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281250.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
2 cups natural cream of chicken soup
1 onion, finely diced
3 carrots, sliced
1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated reduced-fat biscuit dough, torn into pieces
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker. Cover and cook for 5 to 6 hours on High.
After about 5 hours in the slow cookier, stir in the carrots.  About 30 minutes before serving, place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center. Lift the edge of the biscuit dough and stir in the peas. Let stand 10 minutes longer before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an easy slow cooker recipe that cooks while you are at work! We added more veggies and used natural (not condensed) soup to make this recipe healthier.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings
