Title:
Beef Stroganoff with Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
whole grain egg noodles
6 tsp flour
6 tbsp water
22 tbsp sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 onion
8oz mushrooms
garlic powder to taste
pepper to taste
1 1/2 pound sirloin steak
green beans

Directions:
cook noodles
dissolve flour in water.  stir in sour cream and cream of chicken soup
warm a nonstick skillet over medium heat and add onions, mushroom, garlic powder and pepper to taste.  when the onions soften, add sliced sirloin and cook until just browned.  Pour the soup mixture over the steak and vegetables cook until hot a bubbly
