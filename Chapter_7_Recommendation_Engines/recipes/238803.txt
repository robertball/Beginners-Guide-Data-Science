Title:
Cucumber Herb Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1295568.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small cucumber, peeled, seeded and chopped
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon nonfat or low-fat plain yogurt
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Puree cucumber, oil, vinegar, chives, parsley, yogurt, mustard, horseradish, sugar and salt in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Breana Lai, M.P.H., R.D.
Description: 
As the base of this herb-spiked dressing, pureed cucumber provides a mellow grassy flavor and a luxurious texture.
