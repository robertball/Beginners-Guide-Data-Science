Title:
Green Chilis and Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can of fire roasted green chilis
1/2 pkg frozen white corn
1/2 C sour cream
salt and pepper
warm corn tortillas

Directions:
Slice chilis into thin strips
Simmer all ingredients in medium pan for 5 min.
Serve with warm corn tortillas

Submitter: 
jalexandropoulos
Description: 
Green chilis and white corn are simmered in a sour cream sauce and served with warm corn tortillas.  So simple and delicious!  I had this as a side dish while eating dinner at my Mexican friends house.
