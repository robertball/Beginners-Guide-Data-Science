Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871677.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup	margarine
1/3 cup	all purpose flour
1/3 cup	chopped onion
1/2 tsp.	salt
1/4 tsp.	pepper
1 3/4 cups	chicken broth
2/3 cup	milk
2 cups	cut up chicken
10 oz.	frozen peas and carrots
1 pkg	refrigerated pie crusts

Directions:
Heat oven to 425. Rinse frozen peas and carrots in cold water to separate; drain. Heat margarine over low heat until melted and blend in flour, onion, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat stirring until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Remove from heat and stir in broth and milk. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir a minute. Stir in chicken and vegetables. Place one pie crust into a pie pan. Pour mixture into the pan. Place other crust over the top, seal. Bake about 35 minutes or until golden brown.
