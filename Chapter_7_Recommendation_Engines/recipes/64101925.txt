Title:
Hot and Spicy Braised Peanut Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Onions
2 cups Carrots
2 cups Zucchini
1 Red Pepper
1/2 tsp grated lime peel
2 tbsp Lime Juice
2 tbsp Soy Sauce
2 tbsp flour
4 garlic cloves
2 lbs cubed chicken breasts
1 bag frozen peas
3/4 cup chicken broth
3 tbsp peanut butter
1 can coconut milk
1/2 tsp red curry paste

Directions:
Chop all ingredients and place in a slow cooker
Cover with chicken broth and peanut butter (wished together)
Cook on low for 5-6 hours
Add frozen peas and coconut milk for the last 5 minutes
to make it spicy add red curry paste before serving
garnish with chopped peanuts and cilantro
serve over rice
