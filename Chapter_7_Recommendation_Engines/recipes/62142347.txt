Ingredients:
1 pound dry great northern beans, soaked overnight
2-3 carrots, peeled and shredded (or diced)
2-3 stalks celery, sliced
1 medium onion, diced
1 cups cubed cooked ham
4-5 cups chicken broth
4 Tbsp fresh parsley
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 bay leaf
2 cloves fresh garlic
salt and pepper

Directions:
Add the vegetables to the slow cooker first, followed by the beans, the ham, and the seasonings.
Pour chicken broth over the top to cover.
Cook on High for 5-6 hours.

Submitter: 
Corena
Description: 
This is a wonderful crockpot soup I make for my one-year-old son; I puree it for him instead of regular baby food and he absolutely loves it!
