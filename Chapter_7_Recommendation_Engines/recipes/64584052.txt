Title:
Spiralized Cucumber and Jicama Tostada Salad with Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3687595.jpg
Ingredients:
1 English cucumber
1 med jicama
1/2 red onion
1 jalapeno, large and firm (Spiralized, sliced or diced)
2 mini sweet bell peppers, cut into match stick pieces, color of choice
2 limes (divided use)
1 tablespoon avocado oil or olive oil
2 avocados, seeded and removed from the skin
Garlic salt to taste
4 prepared tostada shells
10 grape tomatoes, halved
1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)

Directions:
Mash avocados and season with garlic salt to taste.
Spiralize cucumbers, jicama, red onion, jalapeno and place in a mixing bowl. Slice mini bell peppers into match sticks and add to the spiralized mixture. Add avocado oil and juice of one lime. Mix well and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
Mash avocados and add garlic salt to taste.
Spread avocado mixture evenly on the tostada shells.
Top evenly with spiralized salad mixture and grape tomatoes.
Garnish with cilantro. (optional)
Slice remaining lime into wedges and serve with tostadas.
Cook's note: Spiralizing jalapeno's can be a bit tricky. Make sure you have a large firm jalapeno and don't apply too much pressure when spiralizing.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Amaze your friends with this easy to make spiralized veggies atop a creamy avocado based tostada. Perfect for the summertime paired with your favorite margarita. Change up the veggies or the dressing to suit your taste.
