Title:
Tomato & Sausage Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
5 serrano or 2 jalapeno peppers, sliced
1/2 bell pepper, chopped
1/2 large onion, chopped
3 large cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp smoked sausage drippings (or 1 cup chopped smoked sausage)
Saute vegetables and sausage in oil until done/tender.  Add chicken:
4 chicken breasts, cubed, salted, and peppered
Stir fry for 2 minutes until half done.  Add:
1/2 can crushed tomatoes
1 can white Northern beans
1 can pinto beans-partially mashed
1 bay leaf
1 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp paprika
salt to taste
Cook on med heat for 30 minutes.  When ready to serve, stir in chopped fresh cilantro (about 2-3 tbsp) and 2 chopped green onion tops.  Serve with shredded cheese and tortilla chips.
