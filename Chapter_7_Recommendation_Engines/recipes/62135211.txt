Title:
Chicken Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
2 peppers (yellow and red)
1 pint whipping cream
1/2 cup butter
cajun seasoning to taste
salt and pepper to taste
12 oz. linguine noodels
8 oz. bag of shredded parmazaan cheese
3 tablespoons of olive oil

Directions:
Fry chicken with cajun seasoning in olive oil until not pink.  Add cut up peppers and cook a small amount.  Add butter and whipping cram to pan.  Summer while boiling noodels.  Put together and bake with cheese on 325 for 15 minutes.

The perfect chicken linguine that will lead you to believe you are at an Olive Garden!
