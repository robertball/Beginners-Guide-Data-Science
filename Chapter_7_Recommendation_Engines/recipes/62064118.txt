Title:
Rick's Caribbean Ropa Vieja Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb Flank Steak
4 tbsp butter
1 red onion
8 oz fresh mushrooms
1 1/2 cup favorite red wine
1 can (12-13.5 oz) coconut milk
8 oz coconut flakes (optional)
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tbsp ground allspice
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
1. Heat 4 tbsp butter in large deep skillet.  Saute onions and mushrooms until golden, add flank steak and brown on all sides.
2. Add 1 1/2 cup of favorite red wine.  Simmer until meat is tender and shreds easily, about 30 minutes.  Add can of coconut milk, coconut flakes, brown sugar, allspice, salt, pepper and sour cream.
3. Simmer 10 - 15 minutes.
Serve with rice.

Submitter:

Description: 
A touch of sweetness from the Caribbean gives a new flair to the standard Ropa Vieja (Cuban Meat Stew)
