Title:
S'mores Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993165.jpg
Ingredients:
4 colored wooden craft sticks
4 extra-large marshmallows
1 (7.25 ounce) bottle Smucker's® Magic Shell® Chocolate Fudge Flavor Topping
4 squares graham crackers, crushed

Directions:
Insert stick into each marshmallow. Freeze 15 minutes or until cold. Pour topping on small plate. Place cracker crumbs in shallow bowl.
Roll marshmallows in topping until sides and top are  covered. Sprinkle cracker crumbs over chocolate. Place on metal baking pan. Freeze 5 minutes. Serve immediately in paper baking cups, if desired.

Submitter: 
Smucker's Summer Desserts
Description: 
Chocolate-coated marshmallows on a stick are rolled in graham cracker crumbs then chilled for a delicious twist on the campfire classic.
