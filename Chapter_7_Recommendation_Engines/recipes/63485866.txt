Title:
Caramel Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 cups popped corn (~1 cup seeds)
1 cup marg
2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup light or dark corn syrup
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
melt butter and stir in brown sugar and corn syrup in a medium pop over medium heat.  bring to boil and boil without stirring for 5 mins.  add baking soda, salt and vanilla.  pour over popped corn in a large roasting pan and stir.
may add 1 cup peanuts or other nut after pouring the syrup over the corn
bake 1 hr, stiring every 15 mins at 325F
