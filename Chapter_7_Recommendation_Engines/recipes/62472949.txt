Title:
Good for You! Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups rolled oats
1 cup unsweetened coconut
1 cup chopped pecans (or nut of your choice)
1/4 cup nutritional yeast
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup honey
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup dried blueberries (or dried fruit of your choice)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, combine oats, cocnut, pecans and yeast. Combine honey, olive oil and vanilla and stir into oat mixture. Cook for approximately 11 minutes on a lightly greased baking sheet, stirring as needed. Continue to cook to desired browness. Remove and stir in blueberries. Let granola cool thoroughly and store in an air-tight container.

Submitter:

Description: 
With the unique addition of olive oil, this granola is easy to personalize. Add what you think tastes great!
