Title:
Pat's Healthy Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups rolled oats
1 1/2 cups unsweetened coconut flakes
1 cup sliced almonds
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup sunflower seeds
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 cup melted virgin coconut oil
1/2 cup real maple syrup
1 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250
In large bowl, mix together all dry ingredients. In a separate bowl or pyrex measuring cup, whisk together oil, syrup, and extract.
Pour wet mixture over dry and stir to evenly coat. Transfer mixture to baking dish and bake about 2 hours, until golden and dry.
Turn off heat but leave granola in oven to cool and set completely before using a spatula to release and break into chunks. Store in airtight container.
