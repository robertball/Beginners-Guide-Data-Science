Title:
Shredded Pork Pitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boneless Pork Roast
Large jar of BBQ sauce
Package of Pocket Pitas

Directions:
I usually cook the pork roast the night before in a crock pot and put it in the fridge to cool it over night.  Shred the pork roast in the crock pot and add the jar BBQ sauce.  Stir together.  Warm in crock pot for about 45 minutes on high.  Serve in pita pocket.

Submitter: 
Jaime Paugh
Description: 
My brother in law loves this dish!  It's get for parties and really easy!
