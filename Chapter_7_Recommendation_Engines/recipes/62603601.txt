Title:
Grammy's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs Meatloaf mix (from butcher)
1 c soft cubed bread
1/2 c celery diced small
1/2 c Onion diced small
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 Tbl Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp Allspice
4 Tbl Ketchup
1/4 c milk
1/2 tsp Dry Mustard or (Dijon mustard to taste)
1 Egg

Directions:
Mix all ingredients thoroughly reserving 1/2 of the Ketchup to spread on top.
Form into a loaf or use a loaf pan.  Brush remaining ketchup in the top and bake @ 375 for 1 hour or until no longer link in the middle. Let rest at least 10 minutes to keep juices inside

Submitter: 
Jim Cyberwolf Gaylord
Description: 
This is a Swedish style meatloaf from my Grandma.  It is the best recipe I have come across yet.  It uses a beef/pork/veal mixture that adds a nice texture and great flavor
