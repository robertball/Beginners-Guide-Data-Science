Title:
Strawberry Banana Smothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Half of a peeled banana, half teaspoon vanilla, 5 frozen strawberries, 3/4 of a cup to a cup vanilla icecream, half cup of orange juice, 4 regular sized ice cubes

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender. Blend until smooth (about 5 min.). Serve and enjoy :)!

Submitter: 
AANDJJOHNSTON
Description: 
Great to cool you off on a hot summer day! And healthy for you too!
