Title:
Linnie's Spanish Rice w/Hamburger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2# Hamburger
2 cup uncooked white rice
4 toe minced garlic
3 cups water
32 ounce jar salsa

Directions:
Combine the hamburger with the garlic in a large saucepan. Brown the garlic/hamburger combo. Pour water, rice and salsa into the meat mixture. Bring the water to a full boil and then reduce the temperature to simmer. Simmer for 20 minutes or until the rice is tender. Fluff the rice when finished.
