Title:
Broccoli Rice Casserole - Deb's Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 C cooked rice
1 10 oz pkg chopped broccoli
1 C chopped celery
1/2 C chopped onion
4 Tbl butter
2 10 oz tins cream of mushroom soup
1 C milk
1 10 oz jar cheeze whiz

Directions:
Cook broccoli. Saute celery and onion in butter. Comine soup, milk and cheese. Put all ingredients into a casserole. Bake at 350 for 40 to 50 minutes. Serves 12 to 16.
