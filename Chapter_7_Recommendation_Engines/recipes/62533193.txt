Title:
Mamaw Bea's Pineapple Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c shortening
1 c margerine
2-3/4 c sugar
6 eggs
3 c flour
1 t baking powder
1/4 c milk
1 t vanilla
3/4 c crushed pineapple in juice
(pineapple drizzle)
1 c confectioner's sugar
1 T margarine, softened
4 t pineapple juice (reserved from above)

Directions:
1. Combine shortening & margerine; gradually add sugar.  Cream until light & fluffy.  Add eggs, one at a time, beat well after each addition.
2. Combine flour & baking powder in a sm bowl; set aside.  Combine milk & vanilla in a cup; add to creamed mixture, alternately w/ flour, begin & end with flour. Gently fold pineapple into batter.
3. Pour mixture into a greased and floured 10" tube pan.  Place in cold oven and set temp @ 325.  Bake 1 hour1 15 min to 1 hour 45 min until a toothpick comes out clean.
4. Pierce top of cake with a toothpick.  Combine conf sugar & margarine.  Beat in pineapple juice until mixture in thin.  Drizzle over top & sides.
