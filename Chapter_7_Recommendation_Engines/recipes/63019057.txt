Title:
Shelly's Rolled Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks margarine, softened
1 1/2 c. powdered sugar
1 egg
1 t. vanilla
2 1/2 c. flour
1 t. cream of tarter
1 t. baking soda
1/4 salt

Directions:
Cream together margarine and powdered sugar; then add egg and vanilla and cream together.  Stir together all dry ingredients; add 1 c. at a time of dry ingredients, will make a firm dough.  Wrap in waxed paper and chill 2 hours or overnight.  Roll 1/4" thick and cut in to desired shapes.  Bake @ 400 degrees for 6 min. Cool and frost as desired.
