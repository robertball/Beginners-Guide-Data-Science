Title:
Pear Caramel Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Flour
2/3 cup White Sugar
1-1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon powder
1/4 tsp. Salt
"dash" of powdered Cloves
_____
1/2 cup Milk
1 ea. 16-oz. can of Pears, drained and chopped
1/2 cup chopped Nuts: Pecans, Walnuts, whatever you like
______________
1 cup Boiling Water
3/4 cup Brown Sugar
1/4 cup Butter
_______________
Whipped cream, Cool Whip, or vanilla Ice Cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 deg. F.
Stir together Flour, White sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, salt and cloves in a large bowl.
Add: Milk, chopped pears, Nuts. Mix well to blend.
Turn into an ungreased souffle dish, or oven casserole dish (8" or 9")
In separate bowl: combine boiling water, brown sugar and butter.
Pour the boiling water/sugar mix on top of the batter in the souffle dish/baking dish.
**Bake in oven for 45 min.
**Serve immediately with whipped topping, or ice cream!

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
A very quick Fall/Winter dessert easily made with ingredients from your pantry-- it's a cross between a cake, a souffle, and a pudding.  Very tasty and easy.
