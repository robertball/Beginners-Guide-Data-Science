Title:
Crock Pot Shredded Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/634527.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breast boneless & skinless (thin sliced or regular)
1 can of Rotel diced tomatoes and green chiles
3-4 spoonfools of jalepenos (for a spicy kick)
1 pkg taco seasoning (I used Ortega but doesn't make a difference)
*Extra Taco Stuffers*
Avocado or Guacamole
Chopped onion and cilantro mixed
Cheese
I use Ortega Stand and Stuff Shells

Directions:
Place thawed chicken breast in crock pot. Then add can of Rotel along with taco seasoning and jalapenos (Add pinch of cumin and chili powder for extra flavor). Mix into chicken. Cook on High for 3 1/2 hours or Low 7 hours. Once chicken is cooked thoroughly, shred with two forks.
