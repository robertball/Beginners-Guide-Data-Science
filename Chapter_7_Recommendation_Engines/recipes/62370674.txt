Title:
Tamarind Agua Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Cups Water
1/3 Cups Sugar
1/3 Cup Tamarind Concentrate (3oz)
Ice
Lime Slices
Orange Slices

Directions:
In pitcher combine water, sugar, and tamarind concentrate.  Stir until sugar is dissolved.  Serve immediately or chill until ready to serve.
In ice filled glass garnish with lime and orange slices.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is another favorite agua fresca drink of mine.  The tamarind has a sweet and sour kind of taste to it.
