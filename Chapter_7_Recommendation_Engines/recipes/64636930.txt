Title:
Tuna Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz albacore tuna- drained
1/4 cup finely dices onion
1/2 cup Best Foods
2 eggs
4 tsp lemon juice
Salt & Pepper to taste
2 1/2 cups breadcrumbs
2 T butter
2 T oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except butter, oil, and 1 cup of the bread crumbs. Mix well.
Divide into eight cakes. Coat each cake in remaining breadcrumbs.
Melt butter with oil in frying pan on medium heat.
Once butter has melted cook cakes 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Carrie
Description: 
A more budget friendly version of a crab cake.
