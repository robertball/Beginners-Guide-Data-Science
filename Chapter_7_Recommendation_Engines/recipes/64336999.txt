Title:
Victor's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions - diced
5 garlic cloves - minced
1-2 lb meat
1 29 oz can tomato sauce
1 29 oz can tomato puree
1 29 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 1/2 cups of water
1 tps each - basil, oregano and garlic powder

Directions:
In large pot – brown 2 diced onions and 5 fresh garlic (minced)
Remove from pot.
In same pot – don’t clean pot – brown meat. You can use meatballs, chicken, ground beef, sausage. (or all)
Add the tomato puree,tomato sauce,and crushed tomatoes. Add about 1 ½ cans of water. Toss in the onions and garlic. Add a teaspoon of each – basil, oregano and garlic powder.
Cook covered very slow for at least 2 hours.
