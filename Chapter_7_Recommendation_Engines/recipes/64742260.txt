Title:
Arlene's Caesar salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Hearts of Romaine-5 from Cosco
Marie's Caesar dressing
Sour Cream
Worcestershire Sauce
Lemon Juice
Salt
Pepper
Garlic Cloves
Parmesan cheese
Parmesan Crisps or Caesar croutons
Bacon
Anchovies

Directions:
As a base I buy some Marie’s Caesar dressing. It’s in the cooler section by the bagged lettuces.  In a blender I put about half of that, heaping tablespoon of sour cream, a shake of worsteshire sauce, 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice, salt & pepper, 2-3 cloves of garlic & a tablespoon or a little less of parmasean cheese. Blend all together.
Add bacon bits to the salad, parmasean crisps for the croutons & toss. Add fresh grated parmasean cheese on top of salad after tossing. Bon Appetite!!!
