Title:
Stovetop Stuffing Clone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups bread, cubed
1 tablespoon parsley flakes
3 teaspoons chicken bouillon
1/4 cup dried onion flakes
1/2 tsp celery salt
1 teaspoon thyme
1 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon sage

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake bread for 8 to 10 minutes or until dry.
In a large bowl, toss bread with remaining ingredients until evenly coated. Store in an airtight container.
It will keep for 1 to 4 months or 12 months frozen.
To prepare, combine 2 cups stuffing mix with 1/2 cup water, (1/2 cup celery and/or onion and/or mix) and 2 tablespoons melted butter.

Cloned recipe for stovetop stuffing.  I'd probably add in some dried cranberries (as we enjoy those in our stuffing mix)
