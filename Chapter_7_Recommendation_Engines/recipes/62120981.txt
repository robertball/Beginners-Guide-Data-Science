Title:
Egg White Omelet with cream cheese, smoked salmon and capers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
Salt & pepper, to taste
1 Â½ TB light chive and onion cream cheese
1 oz sliced smoked salmon
1 TB capers, drained

Directions:
Spray small omelet pan over medium heat.  Whisk together egg whites, salt and pepper.  Pour into small omelet pan.  Nudge in sides of egg with spatula going around in a circle, tipping pan slightly, until egg is almost set.
Dollop cream cheese on one half of omelet.  Fold omelet in half.  Turn heat to low, cook one minute.  Flip omelet over and cook additional minute.
Place omelet on plate.  Decoratively fold a piece of sliced smoked salmon on top of the omelet and sprinkle with capers.
