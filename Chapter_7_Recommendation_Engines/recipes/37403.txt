Title:
Tarragon Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984155.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons tarragon vinegar
1 clove garlic, crushed
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 large yellow squash
1 large zucchini
1 large green bell pepper
1 large yellow bell pepper
1 large red bell pepper
3/4 pound medium-size fresh mushrooms

Directions:
WHISK together first 6 ingredients in a large bowl.
CUT yellow squash and zucchini into 3/4 inch slices and bell peppers into 1-inch squares. Add squash, zucchini, bell peppers, and mushrooms to oil mixture. Let stand 1 hour, tossing occasionally.
DRAIN vegetables, discarding marinade, and thread alternately onto skewers.
GRILL, covered with grill lid, over medium-hot coals (350 to 400 degrees) 5 to 7 minutes on each side or until tender.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
Kabobs are so easy- - just cut, skewer, and grill. Though they are perfect for grilling, these kabobs also broil well on bad-weather days.
