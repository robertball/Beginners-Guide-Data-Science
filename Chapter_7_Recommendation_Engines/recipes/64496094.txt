Title:
Big Ray's Caramel Mock  Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2609542.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups old-fashioned oats
2 cups all-purpose flour
1-1/2 cups packed brown sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp allspice
1 cup cold butter, cubed
8 cups thinly sliced peeled zucchini
1 package (14 ounces) caramels, halved
1 cup apple cider, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large bowl, mix oats, flour, brown sugar and spices; cut in butter until crumbly. Press half of the mixture into a greased 13" x 9"-in. baking dish. Layer half of each of the following: zucchini, caramels and remaining oat mixture. Repeat layers. Drizzle 1/2 cup cider over top.
Bake, uncovered, 30 minutes. Drizzle with remaining cider; bake 15-20 minutes longer or until apples are tender. Yield: 12 servings.
