Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked egg noodles
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1 6-1/2 oz. can tuna, rinsed, drained and flaked
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 cup crushed potato chips

Directions:
Bring 4 cups water, 1 tablespoon cooking oil and 1 teaspoon salt to a full rolling boil in a 3 quart covered casserole dish for 6 to 10 minutes on High.  Stir in noodles and recover.  Microwave 8 to 10 minutes on Medium or until tender.  Drain, rinse with hot water and set aside.
Place butter, onion and celery in 2 quart glass casserole.  Microwave about 3 minutes on High or until partly cooked.
Stir in remaining ingredients except potato chips.  Sprinkle with crushed potato chips and microwave about 8 to 11 minutes on Medium High or until heated through (about 150F).
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Lynda McLean Falloon
Description: 
A Tuna Casserole recipe for the microwave - it has a creamy, tasty sauce and crunch from celery, sunflower seeds and potato chips.
