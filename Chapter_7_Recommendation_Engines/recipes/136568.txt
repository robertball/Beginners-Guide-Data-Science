Title:
Peanut Butter, Mayonnaise, and Lettuce Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F58545.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
2 tablespoons peanut butter
2  lettuce leaves

Directions:
Spread one slice of bread with mayonnaise. Spread the other slice with peanut butter. Place lettuce leaves on top of the peanut butter, then top with the mayonnaise-side of the other piece of bread to make a sandwich.

Description:
