Title:
Frozen Vanilla Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups vanilla or plain yogurt, or combination, suit to taste (use Greek Style)
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 to 1 1/2 t. quality vanilla, not imitation, suit to taste depending on the flavor(s)/combination of yogurt used.

Directions:
Combine ingredients, refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Follow directions for your individual ice cream maker!
