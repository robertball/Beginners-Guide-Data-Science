Title:
Tomatillo Soup with Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 skinless, boneless chicken breast
1 onion, cut in large pieces
2 cloves garlic
1 pound tomatillos, cut in quarters
2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chunked
3 cups chicken stock
1 tbsp. amano miso
398 ml can navy beans
2 tablespoons Gourmet Garden cilantro
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
Tajin, sour cream and tortilla chips

Directions:
Place chicken breast in slow cooker.
Add onions, garlic, tomatillos, jalapeno peppers, broth and miso.
Cook on high for approx 5 hrs.
Remove chicken and blend soup.
Shred the chicken and return to soup.
Add the beans and cilantro
Cook for 1/2 hour on high.
Taste the soup and season with salt and pepper.
Ladle into bowls.
Serve with sour cream, Tajin and tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
This is a Mexican-style chicken soup with tomatillos, chicken breast meat and beans in a stock seasoned with fresh jalapenos, onion and garlic.  Serve with a dollop of sour cream sprinkled with Tajin and topped with crushed tortilla chips.Modified from: Tomatillo Soup
