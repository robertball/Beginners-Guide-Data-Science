Title:
Slow Cooked Collard Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lrg bunch of collard greens
1/8 C. jallepeno pepper slices
3 slices of bacon
2 cups of chicken stock
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper

Directions:
prep greens by washing them and cut greens julian style
add to a pot of boiling salted water, let simmer for about 10 minutes then drain
while greens are boiling fry the bacon, dont discard the drippings
put greens into crock pot with the chicken stock, bacon and drippings, jallepeno peppers, salt, and pepper
slow cook on the lowest setting overnight or for about 8 hours

Description: 
Very tender greens with a zip.
