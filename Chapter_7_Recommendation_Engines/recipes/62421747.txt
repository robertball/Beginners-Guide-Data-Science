Title:
Turkey meatball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs lean ground turkey or chicken
1/2 cup finely chopped onions
1 egg beaten lightly
1 cup oat bran or bread crumbs
2 Tbsp fresh parsley finely chopped
2 Tbsp fresh oregano finely chopped
2 Tbsp fresh basil finely chopped
2 cloves garlic finely chopped
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 400 F/204 C
2. In a large bowl place egg and breadcrumbs or oat bran. Add spices and mix well. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Using and ice cream scooper make meat balls and place on prepared cookie sheets. Place in hot oven and bake for 20 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Micaella
Description: 
meatballs are great for dinner or sliced and put in a pita bread or on spaghetti.
