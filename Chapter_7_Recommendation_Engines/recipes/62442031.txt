Title:
Tex-Mex Black Bean Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 6" corn tortillas
4 teaspoons olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
1 jalapeno, seeded and finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup canned black beans, rinsed
1 cup seeded, chopped tomato
4 oz. (1 cup) reduced-fat Monterrey Jack, cheddar or mozzarella cheese, shredded
2 Tablespoons chopped cilantro

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees.
Lightly brush both sides of tortillas with 1 teaspoon of the olive oil and place on an ungreased baking sheet.
Bake about 3 minutes per side until crisp and lightly browned.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat 3 teaspoons oil over medium-high heat.
Add onion, jalapeno and garlic; cook until onion is tender.
Stir in black beans and tomato; heat through.
Sprinkle tortillas with half of the cheese and top with bean mixture.
Sprinkle evenly with the remaining cheese.
Bake 4 minutes or until cheese is melted and sprinkle with cilantro.
