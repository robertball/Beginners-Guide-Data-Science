Title:
Peanut Butter Stars by whodunitrdr Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/545403.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup butter; softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups flour; sifted

Directions:
Cream peanut butter, butter and sugar until fluffy.
Add egg and vanilla.
Mix in flour until blended.
Press through cookie press onto ungreased baking sheets.
Sprinkle with colored sugar, if desired.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes until firm and light golden at edges but not brown.
Transfer to wire racks to cool.
Makes 4 1/2 dozen.

Submitter: 
whodunitrdr
Description: 
I like to make these crispy peanut butter cookies for Christmas. I use a star shaped plate for my cookie press and sprinkle with colored sugars before baking. Recipe from Doubleday Cookbook.
