Title:
Kahlua Iced Latte Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 fluid ounces Kahlua
3 fluid ounces chilled coffee
Cream or milk

Directions:
Pour over ice in a highball glass. For a flavored iced latte, try Kahlua Hazelnut, French Vanilla, or Mocha.

The delicious flavor of an iced coffee latte made extra special with the addition of Kahlua.
