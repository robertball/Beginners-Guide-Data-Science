Title:
2-Step Chocolate Taco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4562065.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup milk chocolate chips
1 (8 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Melt chocolate in a microwave-safe glass or ceramic bowl in 15-second intervals, stirring after each melting, about 1 minute.
Pour melted chocolate over the tortilla. Fold tortilla over chocolate.
