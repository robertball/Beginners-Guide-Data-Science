Title:
Cherry Amish Friendship Bread Cupcakes with Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636699.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Amish Friendship Bread Starter
3 eggs
1 cup oil
1/2 cup milk
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
1-2 boxes cherry Jell-O
Buttercream Frosting ingredients
1/2 cup solid vegetable shortening
1 stick butter, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups sifted powdered sugar
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325° F (165° C).
In a large mixing bowl, add ingredients as listed.
Line cupcake tins with paper cups or grease wells and dust with sugar.
Pour the batter evenly into cupcake wells.
Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the loaf comes out clean.
Top cooled cupcakes with a fluffy mound of buttercream frosting.
ENJOY!
Buttercream Frosting directions: In large bowl, cream shortening and butter with electric mixer. Add vanilla. Gradually add sugar, one cup at a time, beating well on medium speed. Scrape sides and bottom of bowl often. Add milk and beat at medium speed until light and fluffy. Makes about 3 cups of frosting.

Submitter:

Description: 
These Amish Friendship Bread cupcakes are full of cherry flavor! Sprinkle a few white chocolate chips on the frosting and you will have the perfect Valentine’s treat for a classroom party.
