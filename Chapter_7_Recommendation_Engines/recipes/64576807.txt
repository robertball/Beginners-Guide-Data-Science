Title:
Pumpkin Cream Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1/3 cup butter or margarine
1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
Filling:
7g unflavored gelatin powder
3/4 cup cold water
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ginger
dash cloves
14 oz canned pumpkin
1 cup whipping cream
whipped topping

Directions:
Crust:
Melt butter. Stir in crumbs and sugar.
Press lightly into ungreased 9" square pan.
Bake @ 350 x 10 minutes.
Filling:
Sprinkle gelatin over water in med. saucepan. Let stand 5 minutes. Heat over med. heat to dissolve.
Mix sugar, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and cloves together well. Stir into gelatin. Remove from heat.
Add pumpkin and mix well.
Chill until it shows signs of thickening.
Whip cream until stiff. Fold into thickened mixture.
Spread over crumb crust. Chill until firm.
Serve with whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Chilled creamy pumpkin dessert on a graham crumb base.
