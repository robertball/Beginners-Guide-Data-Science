Title:
Lamb Meatballs Over Tandoori Naan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1046553.jpg
Ingredients:
Lamb Meat Balls:
1 lb Ground Lamb
1/4 tsp Dried Mint
1/4 tsp Dried Basil
1/4 tsp Dried Parsley
1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
1/4 tsp Paprika
2 Tbsp very finely chopped onions
2 Garlic Cloves, diced
1 Egg
1/2 tsp Olive Oil
1/4 Cup Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs
Salt + Pepper
Sauce:
1 Jar Tomato Sauce (any flavor)
1 Tbsp Capers
4 Leaves Fresh Basil, torn
1/4 tsp Dried Mint
Salt + Pepper
Tandoori Naan
Muenster Cheese

Directions:
Make the Meatballs:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Combine all the dry ingredients into a large bowl
Add the ground lamb, egg, and olive oil and hand mix until equally distributed
Form small balls (approx. 1 in) with the mixture
On a greased baking sheet, place the meatballs in a single layer, well-spaced
Cook for 15 min, then flip and cook an additional 10 min
Prepare the Sauce:
While the meatballs are cooking we will prepare the sauce
Add the jar of sauce to a medium pot and set on low heat
Add the capers, basil, mint, S+P; Stir occasionally.
Prepare Servings:
Combine the cooked meatballs to the prepared sauce; stir
Place up to 4 Naan pieces on a baking sheet
Scoop meatballs and sauce onto the Naan and cover with Muenster cheese slices
Bake in oven at 350 degrees F until the cheese is melted
Serve immediately, enjoy!

Description: 
I love making lamb meatballs, the combination of mint and lamb make for a very exciting flavor profile. These meatballs are combined with a hearty prepared tomato sauce and then served over Tandoori Naan with melted cheese to create a delicious dish.
