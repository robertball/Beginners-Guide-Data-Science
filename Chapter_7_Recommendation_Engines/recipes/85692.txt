Title:
Raspberry Rita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981944.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Margarita Mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
1/2 cup raspberries
5 cups ice
Raspberries for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Margarita mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and raspberries.
Add ice and blend for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with limes or raspberries.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Beautiful and delicious raspberry margaritas are a great addition to any festive occasion.
