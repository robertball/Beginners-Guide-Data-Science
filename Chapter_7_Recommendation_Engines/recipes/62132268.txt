Title:
Strawberry Snap Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb package sliced strawberries
1 lb sugar snap peas strings removed
Dressing:
1/4 cup olive oil
1-2 tablespoons good balsamic vinegar (to taste)
1-2 tablespoons raspberry jam (to taste)
1/2 tablespoon dijon mustard

Directions:
Combine the dressing ingredients in a bowl.  Whisk and set aside.
Steam the sugar snap peas for 30 seconds until bright green and still crunchy.  Cool in cold water and drain.
Combine strawberries, snap peas, and dressing. Toss to coat.
Serve immediately or chill no longer than one hour.

Submitter: 
CHRISPENTINA
Description: 
Fat lucious red strawberries pair with bright green sugar snap peas in a raspberry vinaigrette for a unexpected and delicious taste treat!
