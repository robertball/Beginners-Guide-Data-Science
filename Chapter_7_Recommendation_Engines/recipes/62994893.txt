Title:
Sioux Indian Pudding with Blue Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups fat free milk
1/8 cup yellow cornmeal
1/4 cup blue cornmeal
1 Tbs dark molasses
1/4 cup brown sugar (or stevia to taste)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 can fat free milk

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook milk until bubbles form at edges.  Slowly stir cornmeal and molasses into milk.  Continue to cook and stir until thickened, 10 minutes.  Remove from heat and stir in sugar, salt, ginger and cinnamon.  Pour into prepared dish.
Bake in preheated oven 2 hours, until set. Pour can of milk on top of pudding and heat another 15 minutes.  Serve hot or cold.
