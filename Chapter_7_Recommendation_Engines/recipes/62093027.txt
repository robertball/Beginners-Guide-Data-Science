Title:
Steak House Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup sour cream
1 Cup mayonnaise
1 Tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1/2 Tablespoon sugar
1/4 Cup finely chopped onion
1/2 Teaspoon salt
1/2 Teaspoon pepper
1/2 Teaspoon garlic powder
6-8 Ounces Blue Cheese

Directions:
Simply mix all the ingredients together. The salt, pepper and garlic powder are just starting measurements. Sample and adjust to your own personal taste.

Submitter: 
Gina
Description: 
This is an awesome blue cheese dressing. Very similar to the homemade dressings in nice steak houses.
