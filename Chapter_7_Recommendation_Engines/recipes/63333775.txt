Title:
Sunflower Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One quart water
1/4 c salt
Sunflower seeds (in the shell)

Directions:
Soak seeds in salted water for two hours or overnight.  Drain.  Spread on a cookie sheet at bake at 375F for 15 mminutes.
OR
Line seeds on cookie sheet (no soaking).  Sprinkle salt over seeds and bake 30-40 minutes at 375F.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
Sunflower seeds (not the sunflower nut) soaked in salted water and baked for a salty treat.
