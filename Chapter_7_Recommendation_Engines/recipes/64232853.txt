Title:
Gluten Free and Dairy Free Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white granulated sugar
1/2 cup packed dark brown sugar
1 heaping Tablespoon Cinnamon
1/2 cup (8 tablespoons) softened dairy free butter
2 large eggs
2 cups Gluten-Free All-Purpose BAKING MIX* (not flour)

Directions:
1) Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Line a couple of baking sheets with parchment, or simply take them out and set them somewhere handy; they don't need to be greased.
2) To make the dough: In a large bowl mix together the sugar and butter until blended.
3) Add the eggs, and stir until blended.
4) Add the baking mix and stir until evenly blended.
