Title:
Easy Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs. unsalted butter
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in  butter, salt, and pepper until butter melts. Cook garlic in mixture for a 1-2 minutes until soft but not browned. Add asparagus and toss with sauce.  Cook for 7-10 minutes while turning asparagus for even cooking.
