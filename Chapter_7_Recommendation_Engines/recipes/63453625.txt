Title:
Nutella Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Nutella
1/2 cup unsalted butter brought to room temp
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
pinch of salt
Optional: 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg
Mix butter, sugar and brown sugar until fluffy and light
Mix in the Nutella, vanilla and egg. Set aside.
In a different bowl, mix flour, salt, baking soda until there are no lumps.
Grease a cookie pan and set aside.
Combine the dry mixture with the set. Do not overwork the dough.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Roll a tablespoon worth of dough into a ball and place on parchment paper lined cookie sheet.
Bake for 10 minutes or until cracked
