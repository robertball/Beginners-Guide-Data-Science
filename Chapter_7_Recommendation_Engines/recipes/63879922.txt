Title:
Cheesy Creamy Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 medium onion, finely chopped
8 ounces cream cheese
1/2 cup fat free half and half
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 (10 ounce) boxes frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1/2 cup pine nuts
Sea salt and pepper to taste
Paprika

Directions:
Heat butter in a medium sauce pan over medium heat. Add onions and saut√© until onions are soft (about 5 minutes).
Stir in cream cheese, half and half, nutmeg, salt and pepper. Heat until cheese is melted.
Stir in spinach and pine nuts; heat until the mixture is warm. Sprinkle paprika over dish for color. Serve.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Pine nuts add a special flavor and crunch to this creamy dish, which can be served as an elegant side dish or a dip. It is excellent when served with lamb.
