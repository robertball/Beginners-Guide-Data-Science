Title:
Whipped Cream topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint heavy cream
1 1/2 tsp mexican vanilla
3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup cold water
2 teaspoon unflavored gelatin

Directions:
Whip cream with confectioners' sugar and vanilla until soft peaks form.  Dissolve gelatin in microwave for 2 min,stir after 1 min,let rest until you can put your finger in it and not burn yourelf. Whip into cream until stiff peaks form.

Submitter: 
He be GB
Description: 
Sweetened whipped cream topping  Use to garnish custard and cream pies. Gelatin must be warm when added to whipping cream.
