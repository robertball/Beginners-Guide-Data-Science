Title:
Pennsylvania Dutch Funnel Cake Mix 2/3/13 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large eggs
2 1/2 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup granulated sugar
3 1/2 cups sifted all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
1. Beat eggs, granulated sugar and milk.
2. Sift in flour, salt and baking powder.
3. Wisk until smooth. Batter should be thin.
4. To add flair, pour through funnel, drizzling into hot oil. Use your finger to control flow from funnel. Otherwise, drizzle in circular pattern from pyrex measuring cup.
5. Fry funnel cakes for 2-3 minutes until golden brown, turning over once.
6. Using slotted spoon, remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
7. Sift powdered sugar over top of cakes and serve immediately.
