Title:
Cavoli di Bruxelles con Pancetta Affumicata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1939276.jpg
Ingredients:
200 grams brussels sprouts
75 grams pancetta affumicata (in cubes)
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp white wine
Black pepper
1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar glaze (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 205Â°C.
Clean, trim and cut brussels sprouts into quarters.
Put pancetta, brussels sprouts, wine, il and pepper in a bowl and mix to coat.
Place in a single layer on a lined baking sheet and bake in the oven for 20 minutes until crispy but not crunchy.
Drizzle with balsamic vinegar glaze.
