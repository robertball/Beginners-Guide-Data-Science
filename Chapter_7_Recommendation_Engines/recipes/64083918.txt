Title:
Whole Wheat Applesauce Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1552392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup	Whole Wheat Flour
1 1/2 tsp	Baking Powder
1/2 tsp	Baking Soda
1 tsp	Cinnamon
1/2 tsp	Allspice
1/4 tsp	Nutmeg
1/4 tsp	Salt
2 large	Egg
2/3 cup unpacked	Brown Sugar
1 cup	Applesauce, unsweetened
5 tbsp	Butter, without salt
Topping:
1/2 cup, chopped	Walnuts
1/8 cup unpacked	Brown Sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
Whisk eggs and brown sugar together
Melt butter and whisk it into the wet mixture
Stir in applesauce
Fold in dry ingredients until just moist
Mix together walnuts and the 2 Tablespoons of brown sugar for the topping
Spoon muffin mixture into muffin tins and press topping into muffins
Bake for 13-15 minutes
