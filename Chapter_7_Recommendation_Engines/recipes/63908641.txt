Title:
French barbecued chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 garlic cloves
3/4 cup finely chopped parsley
salt and pepper
4 chicken breasts
3 tbsp butter
juice of 1 large lime

Directions:
Finely mince garlic and mix with parsley. Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.
Grill chicken until fully cooked.
Place butter in small saucepan. When butter starts to foam, add parsley and garlic mixture. Add lime juice. Pour sauce over chicken and serve.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Murray
Description: 
This is a family favorite and we always make extra for lunches.
