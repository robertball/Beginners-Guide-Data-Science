Title:
Strawberry Shooters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fresh Strawberries
Bottle of Amaretto
Can of Redi-Whip

Directions:
1.  Hull the Strawberries.
2.  Pour Amaretto into hulled strawberries.
3.  Top with Redi-Whip.
4.  Eat whole, all at once.  This will seem like quite   the mouthful, but will taste amazing!
5.  Repeat.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tried this at a country music festival and loved it!  Easy, a little messy, but oh so worth it!  Enjoy.
