Title:
Absolutely Fabulous Greek House Dressing for Five (from AR) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c olive oil
3/4 tsp garlic powder
3/4 tsp dried oregano
3/4 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp Dijon-style mustard
1/3 c red wine vinegar

Directions:
In a lidded container, mix together the olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, basil, pepper, salt, onion powder, and Dijon-style mustard. Pour in the vinegar, and mix vigorously until well blended. Store tightly covered at room temperature.
