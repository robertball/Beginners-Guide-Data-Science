Title:
Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pumpkin (any size will do), salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
Begin by cutting pumpkin open and separating flesh from seeds.  Discard flesh as necessary or put aside for use for another recipe.
Thoroughly wash seeds until all pumpkin flesh is gone.
Place seeds on a flat cookie sheet in one layer.  Add salt to the seeds as necessary and bake for 8-10 minutes or until seeds are dry.
Enjoy warm or store in an airtight container for another day!
