Title:
Chorizo Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556047.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound shredded hashbrowns
2 tb sp canola oil
1/3 stick chorizo sausage
cooking spray (I use olive oil)
5 eggs
1/2 cup mexican cheese dip (optional)
1/2 cup guacamole
1/2 cup sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Heat two skillets to medium-high heat, in the first one put two tb sp canola oil. Put the hashbrowns in the heated skillet and flatten into an even layer, cook 7-9 minutes on both sides
When the hashbrowns start to brown:In the other skillet lightly spray with olive oil. Remove the chorizo casing stir occasionally until cooked. Place on paper towels to soak up the grease.
In a bowl, whisk the 5 eggs.
Remove the hashbrowns from the skillet and set on paper towels
Scramble the eggs
When the eggs are cooked add the chorizo and hashbrowns, then mix well.
Once mixed, put in bowls and drizzle the mexican cheese dip on top and add the guacamole and sour cream.
Serve immediately.
