Title:
Chocolate mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. sugar
1/2 c. water
1 pint heavy cream
1 pkg (12 oz) choc. chips
1/4 c. dark rum
4 egg yolks
1/2 c. toasted almonds

Directions:
Combine sugar and water in a small saucepan and boil for 3 minutes.
Pour cream into food processor and process till a very thick whipped cream forms, about 1 minute.  Transfer to a 2 quart decorative mold.
Process choc. chips in processor, using pulse, for 15-20 seconds.  Continue processing and gradually pour in hot syrup, rum, and egg yolks.
Using a spatula, scrape chocolate mixture over whipped cream and fold together.  Place mold in freezer for 6 hours.  Unmold and garnish with toasted almonds if desired.
