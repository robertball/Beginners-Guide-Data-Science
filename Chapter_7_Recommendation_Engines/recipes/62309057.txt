Title:
Fancy Tuna Casserole-taken from boring to snazzy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/371152.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz can of tuna, drained
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup chopped celery (2 stalks + or -)
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
1/4 cup chopped onion (a little bit more accidently got in)
2 T lemon juice
1 to 2 T diced green jalapeno peppers
1/4 t salt
1/8 t pepper
2-8oz refridgerated crescent rolls
1 egg white slightly beaten (mayo as option)
2 T parmesan cheese (shredded or can)

Directions:
Combine the top 11 ingredients and mix well
Either place in a skillet or spoon into a large greased baking dish for table presentation
Separate crescent roll dough into triangles
Roll each triangle half way up
Arrange triangles over tuna mixture with tips towards the center.  DO NOT OVERLAP
Brush with mayo or beaten egg white
Sprinkle with paresan cheese
Bake at 350 for about 30 minutes or so until crescent rolls are a golden brown
