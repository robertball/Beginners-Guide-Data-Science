Title:
Dirt Cake I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter, softened
1 (8 ounce) container mascarpone, softened
2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
3 1/2 cups milk
2 (12 ounce) container Cool Whip, thawed
2 packages Oreo's

Directions:
Chop cookies very fine in food processor. The white cream will disappear.
With electric mixer cream mascarpone & butter.
In a large bowl, beat together milk & pudding for 4 minutes.
Fold in Cool Whip.
Combine pudding mixture and mascarpone mixture together.
Layer in serving container, starting with cookies then cream mixture.  Repeat layers to form 3 cream layers and 4 cookie layers (the 4th cookie layer should be on top)
Add Halloween decorations.
Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Shannon
Description: 
This is a great conversation piece at Halloween parties.  Adults love it as much as the children do.Modified from: Dirt Cake I
