Title:
French Cherry Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 3 oz. package of cream cheese
1/2 Cup powdered sugar
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 Cup whipping cream
1 Can (1 lb 5 oz) cherry (or blueberry) pie filling
1 Pillsbury prepared pie crust
1 tsp almond extract

Directions:
Following directions on box, bake pie crust.  Set aside and let cool.
Beat until smooth the cream cheese, powdered sugar and vanilla.
Whip the whipping cream to stiff peaks with mixers.  Carefully fold into cream cheese mixture.
Turn into prepared pie shell, spreading evenly.
Add almond extract into can of cherries and stir.  Spoon cherry filling evenly over top of pie shell.
Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Jeankat
Description: 
My girlfriend's mom gave me this recipe.  It has been in her family for generations.  This is a easy, delicious, no-bake cherry pie.  You can substitute the cherry with blueberry or anything you would like.
