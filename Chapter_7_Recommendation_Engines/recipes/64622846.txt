Title:
Roast Chicken with Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cut-up chicken ( 3 - 3.5 lbs.)
3 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
1.5 Tbsp. chopped fresh rosemary or 1.5 tsp.  dried rosemary, crushed
1 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1.25 tsp. salt, divided
3/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper, divided
3 bell peppers (preferably 1 red, 1 yellow,  and 1 green)
1 medium onion

Directions:
1.Heat oven to 375F. Rinse chicken in cold water; pat dry with paper towel. Place in shallow roasting pan.
2. Combine 2 Tbsp. of the oil, rosemary and lemon juice; brush over chicken. Sprinkle 1 tsp. salt and 1/2 tsp. pepper over chicken.  Roast 15 minutes.
3. Cut bell peppers lengthwise into 1/2 inch thick strips.  Slice onion into thin wedges. Toss vegetables with remaining 1 tablespoon oil, 1/4 tsp. salt, and 1/4 tsp. pepper.  Spoon vegetables around chicken; roast until vegetables are tender and chicken is cooked through, about 40 minutes.  Serve chicken with vegetables and pan juices.
