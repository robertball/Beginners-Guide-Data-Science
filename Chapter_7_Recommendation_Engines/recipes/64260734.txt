Title:
Hamburger Macaroni Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1/4 cup onion, chopped
2 cups tomato sauce
1/4 cup celery, chopped
1/4 cup green pepper, chopped
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 cups macaroni (penne)

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onion.  Add remaining incredients except macaroni.  Cover and simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
In the meantime, cook macaroni in boiling water according to directions.  Drain.
Add cooked macaroni to ground beef mixture and simmer for 10 more minutes.
