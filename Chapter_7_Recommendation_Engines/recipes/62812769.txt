Title:
Linda's Mango Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium mango
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/4 tsp ground cardamon
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground coriander
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup minced green onions
1/4 cup raisins

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in saucepan and simmer uncovered for 45 minutes.
Preserve in glass cans or refrigerate in an air tight container.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander

A great kick of flavor to any recipe!
