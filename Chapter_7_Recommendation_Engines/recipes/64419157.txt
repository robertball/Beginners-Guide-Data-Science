Title:
Big Ray's Grilled Greek Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4496661.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt
1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
2 Tbl fresh lemon juice
2 tsp Greek seasoning
8 bone-in chicken thighs
1 tsp minced garlic
1 large red onion, cut into wedges
1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Put all into a resealable bag, except the feta cheese, and marinate at least 1 hour (overnight is better). Over a medium heat,  grill the chicken 5 minutes per side until browned, serve over rice crumbling the feta cheese over dish.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
I love to make this because it's a plan ahead meal that when you want it, it's ready to prepare.
You can also add all ingredients (except feta) and toss it in the freezer.  Thaw and grill.
