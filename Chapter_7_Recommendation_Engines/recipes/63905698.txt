Title:
Roasted Tomatoes & Ricotta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cherry tomatoes
garlic
thyme
olive oil
balsamic vinegar
ricotta cheese
brown sugar
red pepper flakes
italian bread

Directions:
Drizzle cherry tomatoes, garlic, & thyme with good olive oil, balsamic vinegar & sprinkle of salt, brown sugar & red pepper flakes.  Roast in 325 oven until tender & caramelized.  Pile on top or toast slathered with fresh ricotta.
