Title:
Spinach Salad with Seeds and Cider Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup slivered almonds, chopped pistachios, pumpkin seeds,  or toasted walnuts
1/4 red onion, cut lengthwise into srips
4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
2 cups cherry tomatoes, quartered
1 orange bell pepper, sliced into rings, then rings cut in half
4 hard boiled eggs, diced
1 cup croutons
Dressing:
2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds (or raw sesame seeds + 1/8 tsp toasted sesame oil)
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1/2 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons dried minced onion
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
pinch salt
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Place all ingredients for dressing except oil in blender.  Stream in oil until fully incorporated.   Toss with salad ingredients just before serving.
