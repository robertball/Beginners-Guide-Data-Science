Title:
Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 lbs Idaho Russet Potatoes (about 5 medium), peeled and sliced 1/8" thick
2 Tbsp butter
1/4 c. minced yellow onion
1/2 tsp minced garlic
2 Tbsp flour
salt and pepper
1 c. evaporated milk
1/2 c. sour cream
6 slices American cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bring water to a boil in a medium saucepan. Boil sliced potatoes for 3-5 min. until tender yet firm. Drain.
In a medium saucepan, melt butter over med low heat and add onions and garlic. Stir frequently until tender, about 5 min. Add flour and stir until bubbly. Stir and additional minute. Slowly add evaporated milk and continue stirring until the mixture comes to a boil (1-2 mintues). Boil one additional minute and stir in sour cream, salt, pepper, and cheese slices. Stir until cheese has melted and remove from heat.
Grease a 1.5 quart baking dish. Place parboiled potatoes in an even layer in the baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over the potatoes. Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes or until heated through.
