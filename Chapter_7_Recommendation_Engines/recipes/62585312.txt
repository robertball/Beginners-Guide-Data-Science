Title:
Nate's Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/550840.jpg
Ingredients:
1-8 oz bar of cream cheese
1 package of the All Natural Jimmy Dean Pork Sausage
Montreal Steak Seasoning
Parmesan Cheese
24 mushrooms

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Clean mushroom and discard stems, set mushrooms on a cookie sheet and set aside
3. Cook sausage in a pan, drain fat and set aside to cool
4. Chop sausage up finely and mix with cream cheese and a Tablespoon or so of the seasoning (you can do this in a food processor or blender if you'd like)
5. Fill mushroom caps with filling
6. Bake about 10 minutes
7. Take out mushrooms and sprinkle with Parmesan Cheese
8. Return to oven and broil until tops of filling and cheese are golden brown

Submitter: 
Jen
Description: 
This is an easy appetizer to make. Everyone we've served it to has loved it!
