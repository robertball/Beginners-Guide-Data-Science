Title:
White Cheese dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 blocks of cream cheese
1/2 cup of chopped onions
1/2 cup of mayo
1 package of shredded Monterrey Jack cheese
1 package of shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Soften the cream cheese. Add in all ingredients and mix in medium sized bowl. Spoon mixture into baking dish or pie pan. Spread evenly. Bake at 425 for 20 minutes or until the top is browning and bubbly. Serve with tortilla chips.
