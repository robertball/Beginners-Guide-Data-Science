Title:
London Broil for the Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/490372.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds London broil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup water
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place the London broil in a slow cooker; add the garlic, mushroom soup, water, basil, oregano, and salt; cover; cook on low 6 to 7 hours.

Submitter:

Description:
