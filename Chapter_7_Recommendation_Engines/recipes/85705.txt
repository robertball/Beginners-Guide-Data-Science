Title:
The Perfect Pina Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI® Mixers Pina Colada frozen mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
5 cups ice
Pineapples and cherries for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with pineapples and cherries.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
For a classic Pina Colada, you can't go wrong with this easy recipe. Great for entertaining, or just relaxing on the weekend.
