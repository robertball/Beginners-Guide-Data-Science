Title:
Pita Peaunt Butter Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pita
2 tbsp peanut butter
1 tbsp honey
raisins

Directions:
Cut pita in half and open. Toast if desired. Spread peanut butter inside pita. Drizzle honey inside. Drop in a few raisins. Press pita each half of sandwich together so nothing leaks out the edge.

Submitter: 
dancer7889
Description: 
This simple sandwich is easy to make and easy to carry around without being messy. A good alternative to the normal peanut butter and jelly sandwich.
