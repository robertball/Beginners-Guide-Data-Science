Title:
Chunky Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/481768.jpg
Ingredients:
1 9x5 loaf pan
1 ripe Banana
¾ to 1 cup of coarsely chopped walnuts
1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
½ teaspoon of ground nutmeg
½ stick of salted butter
½ cup of light brown sugar
1 package of store bought refrigerated crescent roll dough
1 tablespoon of white sugar

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 375
Mash ripe banana in small bowl, add the cinnamon and nutmeg to mashed banana, mix well – set aside.
In a saucepan, melt the butter and brown sugar over low heat, stirring well - set aside.
Line bottom of loaf pan with chopped walnuts (enough to cover the bottom of the pan)
Open dough and tear apart the triangles
Add a tablespoon of the banana mixture to the wide end of the triangle then fold the edges of the triangle together and roll the wide end toward the small end sealing the dough around the banana mixture.
Add rolls to pan in one layer.
Dust with tablespoon of white sugar.
Pour butter and brown sugar mixture over rolls.
Bake for around 35 minutes or until the rolls are golden brown.

Description: 
My twist on the classic monkey bread. A lot less butter and sugar and have also added walnuts and banana's to make it extra yummy!
