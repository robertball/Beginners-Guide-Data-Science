Title:
Polenta with Herbed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 T olive oil, divided
1.75-2 lbs mushrooms (cremini, oyster, shiitake)
8 cloves garlic, crushed/chopped
1/2 c tarragon
1/2 c rosemary
1/2 c parsley
4 c vegetable stock
2 c water
1-2 c chicken stock
salt
1 T butter

Directions:
clean and chop large mushrroms
heat large saute pan on medium/high heat
heat 2 T olive oil
add half mushrooms, saute, try not to move so they brown; remove from pan
add 2T olive oil
saute remaining mushrooms until brown/soft
add garlic to soften
add other mushrooms back to pan
add herbs
add salt and turn heat off
make polenta
heat stock/water to boil
whisk in polenta
reduce to simmer and cook until done
add stock as needed to keep creamy
add salt and butter
serve mushrooms on top of polenta with Parmesan cheese if desired (can also broil with cheese on top)
makes leftover polenta -- use for baked polenta cakes/crust/fries
