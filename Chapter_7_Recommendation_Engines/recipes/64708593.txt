Title:
Air Fryer Asparagus Fries with Mustard Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5523514.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large egg
1 tsp. honey
1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated  Parmesan cheese
12 asparagus spears, trimmed
1/4 cup stone ground mustard
1/4 cup Greek yogurt
pinch cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat air fryer to 400
2. Beat  egg and honey in a long narrow dish
3. Combine Panko and Parmesan on separate plate
4. Coat asparagus in egg mixture,  roll in Panko mix to coat
5. Place 6 spears in the air fryer and cook 4-6 minutes or until desired brownness. Repeat with remaining asparagus spears.
6. Combine mustard, yogurt, and cayenne in a small bowl and serve as a dipping sauce
