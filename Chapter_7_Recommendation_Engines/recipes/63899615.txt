Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. (more or less) hamburger
1 can cream of mushroom soup
tater tots

Directions:
Brown & drain hamburger.  Mix in cream of mushroom soup.  Add cooked green beans, onions, or cheese (if desired).  Spray casserole dish with pam and pour in hamburger mixture.  Top with tater tots.  Bake at 325 - 350 until golden brown & bubbly.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Simple kid friendly casserole made with pantry ready ingredients.
