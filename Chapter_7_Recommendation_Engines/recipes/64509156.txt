Title:
Pork/Beef Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1#   80/20 Ground Beef
1#   Gianelli Brand Pork Sausage Roll
2 T. Lee & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
1-1/2 T. Dry Onion Flakes
1  t.    Granulated Garlic
2     Eggs

Directions:
Pre-heat grill with griddle.  Blend all ingredients and make into 6 (or more) patties. Brown each side in the griddle. Finish on the grill. Top with cheese if desired.  Add Red Pepper flakes for a hotter version. Serve with sliced potatoes cooked with sliced onions and butter seasoned with S&P and Old Bay.
