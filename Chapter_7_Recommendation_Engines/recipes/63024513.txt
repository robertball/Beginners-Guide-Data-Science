Title:
Broccoli Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 small onion, diced
2 cups uncooked Minute Rice
1/2 t. salt
2 Cups Water
1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz. cheez whiz
1 pkg. frozen, chopped broccoli

Directions:
Saute onion in butter.  Add rice and brown.  Add salt and walter, cover until water is absorbed.  Cook broccoli according to package directions.  Don't overcook.  Drain well.  Mix rice, broccoli, soup and cheez whiz together.  Bake in greased casserole dish for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
