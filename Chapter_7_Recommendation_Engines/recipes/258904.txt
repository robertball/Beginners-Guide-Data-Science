Title:
Nutty Kale Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 gallons kale salad blend
1/2 gallon coleslaw dressing
3 cups chopped walnuts
3 cups sweetened dried cranberries (such as Ocean Spray® Craisins®)

Directions:
Mix kale, dressing, walnuts, and cranberries in a large bowl. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Description:
