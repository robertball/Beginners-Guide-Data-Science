Title:
Apple Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
3 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 egg
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 apples, peeled and sliced
Topping:
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Measure flour, sugar, baking powder and salt into a large bowl.
Cut in butter until crumbly. Make a well in center.
In another bowl, beat egg until frothy.
Mix in milk and vanilla.
Pour into well. Stir just enough to moisten.
Pour into greases 8" x 8" cake pan.
Push apples into batter close together.
Topping:
Mix brown sugar and cinnamon together and sprinkle over top.
Bake @ 350 x 50-60 min. or until it begins to shrink from edge of pan.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A moist, delicious classic featuring apples and cake! Sure to please anyone.
