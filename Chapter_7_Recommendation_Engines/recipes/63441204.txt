Title:
Shannon's Milder Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 cup sliced white mushrooms
1 cup green bell pepper
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
3 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon of dried parsely
1/2 teaspoon of dried basil
1.5 teaspoons black pepper
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
1 Cook beef in skillet over medium heat until browned; drain.
2 Mix beef and remaining ingredients except beans in large pot or slow cooker
3 Cover and cook on low heat setting 4 to 5 hours, stirring every so often
4 Stir in beans. Cover and cook on high heat setting for 15 to 20 minutes or until slightly thickened.

Submitter: 
Shannon O'Keefe
Description: 
This chili is popular because it's not too hot, but it has a lot of flavour!
