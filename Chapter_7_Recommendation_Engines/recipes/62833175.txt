Title:
Blackened Seasoning Mix - Cajun Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons salt
1 tablespoons paprika
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon ground dried thyme
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon ground white pepper
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried thyme

Directions:
Combine everthing in a bowl until evenly mixed. Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.

Submitter: 
TexasJG
Description: 
Spicy Cajun mix for blackening.  Dip your protein in melted butter or oil, dip in the mix, put in a hot pan (no fat in the pan) until it is crisp but not burned.Modified from: Blackened Seasoning Mix
