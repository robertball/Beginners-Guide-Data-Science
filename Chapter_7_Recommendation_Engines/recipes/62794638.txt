Title:
Hamburg Bean Casearol Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lbs. hamburg
1 28oz can Original Bush's Baked Beans
1 green pepper
1 red onion
1 12oz bottle Heinz chili sauce
1/3 cup lightly packed brown sugar
1 Tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 Lb. bacon

Directions:
Dice/Mince Onion and pepper
Brown hamburg, onion, and peeper in large fry pan
add all remaining ingredients and mix well
put all ingredients in casserole dish with raw bacon strips lying flat along the top
bake at 375 for 30-40 min

Submitter: 
Joel Lacoss
Description: 
Hamburg, baked beans, and bacon mixed together with sweet and tangy spices, complimented perfectly with a side of cottage cheese.
