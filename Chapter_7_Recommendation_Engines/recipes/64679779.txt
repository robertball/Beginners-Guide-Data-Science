Title:
Instant Pot Baby Back Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4865845.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbs Kosher Salt
1 tbs brown sugar
1 tbs chili powder
1 tbs paprika
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 rack baby back ribs
1 cup beef broth
BBQ sauce

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, combine the salt, brown sugar, chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, cayenne, and black pepper.
2. Cut the rack of ribs into 4 equal parts. Rub all sides of each piece with the spice rub.
3. Add the broth to the Instant Pot. Add the ribs to the pot in a teepee formation and secure the lid.
4. Select Manual and cook on high pressure for 30 minutes
5. Preheat the oven to 425. Line a baking sheet with foil.
6. Once cooking is complete, use a quick release.
7. Use tongs to move ribs to the baking sheet. Brush the ribs on all sides with the BBQ sauce and bake for 7 minutes on each side.
