Title:
Chemical Gaarden Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6T. liquid blueing,6T. salt,6T. water. Add 1T. ammonia. old pie pan, small stones or rocks(3/4)

Directions:
Mix above mixture and pour over 3/4 small rocks in an old pie pan. Crystals start to grow soon and will continue as long as liquid remains. If you'd like more color, add few drops of food coloring. Set pan so you can watch it daily.
