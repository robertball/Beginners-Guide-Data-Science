Title:
Light Tuscan Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/305179.jpg
Ingredients:
4 3-oz chicken breasts (about half a breast), pounded thin
1 tbsp salt
2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped fine
1 tbsp fresh oregano, chopped fine
1 tbsp fresh thyme
2 tsp and a spray of olive oil
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 red pepper, julienne cut
1/2 cup white wine
2 cups low-fat buttermilk
1/2 lb fresh spinach
1/3 to 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
20 asparagus spears
6 to 8 oz (uncooked) whole wheat  linguine or fettuccine, prepared according to package directions

Directions:
Add asparagus to boiling water for about 3 minutes, dunk in cold water and set aside.
Mix salt, pepper, oregano, basil, and thyme. Spray chicken with a little olive oil and rub mixture into chicken. Heat 1 tsp oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken, about 3 minutes a side. Set aside.
Heat 1 tsp olive oil in sauce pan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and peppers, saute for about 1 minute. Stir in 1 tbsp flour, wine and buttermilk, heat until it thickens.  Add spinach. When spinach has wilted, finish with cheese.
Run hot water over asparagus to warm.
Evenly divide pasta, top with sauce, asparagus and chicken.
