Title:
SUMMER TIME SLUSH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 large can frozen orange juice
1 large can frozen lemonade
7 cups water
26 oz.bottle rye,or rum,or vodka or gin
large bottle sprite or 7up

Directions:
Boil sugar with orange juice and lemonade.Add 7 cups of water.Bring to a boil over medium heat.Turn off stove,let cool.Add the 26 ozs.of alcohol,mix well.  Keep in large container in freezer till slushy.To make a drink,fill glass with half slush and half sprite or 7up.
