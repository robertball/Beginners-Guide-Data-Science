Title:
Coconut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/821229.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 c. slivered almonds
1/2 c. coconut flakes
~10 dates
~1/4 c. cashews
1 tsp. coconut oil

Directions:
In a food processor add almonds and coconut.  Then dates.  Cashews and coconut oil until thick and bally.  Press on wax paper into a square folding the sides over the top and refrigerate.  Cut into bars and enjoy or wrap individually for on the go.

Submitter: 
Lacy Wilson
Description: 
-The on the go, travel light snack bar.  Derived from a famous raw food bar and the ultimate in foodie!
