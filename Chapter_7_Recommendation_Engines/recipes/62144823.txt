Title:
Swedish Meatballs with Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
2 pounds ground round
1 cup finely chopped onion
1 roasted garlic head (reserving 1/4 for gravy)
3 whole green onions, finely minced, white and green parts
1 cup italian breadcrumbs
2 large eggs
1 tsp sweet paprika
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp fresh grated nutmeg, or ground nutmeg
1/4 cup fresh parsley
salt and cracked pepper
1/4 cup light cream, or half and half
Gravy:
1 can low sodium chicken broth (about 16 oz)
1/2 pint whipping cream
1/2 tsp instant coffee
1/4 head roasted garlic head

Directions:
Meatballs: Mix together all meatball ingredients. Form into golfball sized meatballs. Saute beef over medium heat in skillet with 3-4 Tbs butter and 1 Tbs olive oil mixture until no longer pink. About 15 minutes. Drain. Reserve drippings. (Don't clean the pan yet!)
Gravy: Add reserved drippings into pan; gradually add chicken broth and instant coffee. Heat to a low simmer for about 1-2 minutes or until bubbly; add 1/4 roasted garlic head and whipping cream. Gradually add meatballs and simmer very low for 10-15 minutes or until heated thru.
**to roast garlic head: cut off bare top of garlic head, sprinkle with 2-3 tsp olive oil. Crimp a small piece of tin foil over garlic; Bake in prehead 350 oven for 25-35 minutes or until golden brown and soft to the touch. Cool. Garlic texture should be thick and creamy.
