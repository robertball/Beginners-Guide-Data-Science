Title:
Pub-style Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/562505.jpg
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts chopped into chunks
1/2 lemon
1 onion chopped
1 carrot chopped
2 stocks celery
1 tsp. curry paste
2 cups chicken stock
Sauce
3 Tbsps. butter
1/4 cup flour
2 Tbsps. curry paste
1 Tbsp. tomato paste
1 Tbsp. apricot jam
1/2 dark beer
1/2 cup milk
1 cup low-fat mayonnaise

Directions:
In a large pot on stovetop, boil chicken breast in water for 15 minutes until cooked through.  Drain and rinse well.
Return chicken to pot and add lemon, onion, carrot, celery, stock and curry paste. Bring to boil and cook on stovetop for 1 hour.
Remove lemon and add chicken mixture to crock pot.
For sauce, in a small sauce pan melt butter. Add flour and mix until smooth. Add curry, tomato, jam, beer and milk and cook for 5 minutes until thick.
Add sauce to crock-pot. Cook for 4 hours on High or 8 hours on Low.
Fold in mayo until smooth and heat through.
Serve over rice or with chips.

Submitter: 
redheadchef
Description: 
A flavourful chicken curry like those found in any corner pub.  It is creamy and rich, and should be served with rice or chips (fries).  Cooking in a crock pot makes prep easier!
