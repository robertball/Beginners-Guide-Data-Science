Title:
Carb-Free Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds 93% lean ground beef
1 medium onion, grated
1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
6 oz canned tomato sauce
1/2 cup crushed fried pork skins
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 large eggs
1 egg white
3 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon salt
1/2 tablespoon pepper
1 tablespoon garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large bowl, add all ingredients EXCEPT onion.
Neatly lay a tea towel or lint-free cloth into a strainer.  Gather up corners of cloth and twist, wringing excess liquid out of onions.  Add to bowl.
Mix until well blended. Form into a loaf and place on a greased rimmed cookie sheet.
Place in preheated oven and bake for 45 minutes to an hour or until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
Let stand for at least 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
Love meatloaf, but don't want the breadcrumbs?  This is for you.
