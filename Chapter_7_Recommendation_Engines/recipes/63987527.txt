Title:
Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Chuck
1 lb. Ground Italian Sausage
2 Eggs
3/4 c. Milk
2/3 c. Italian Breadcrumbs
1/3 c. Grated Parmesan
1 T. Onion Powder
1 T. Paprika
1/2 T. Ground Sage
1/2 t. Garlic Powder
1/2 t. Pepper
1/2 t. Salt
1/4 t. Ground Red Pepper

Directions:
Beat eggs, add milk, add all seasoning, and then add breadcrumbs plus cheese. It should now look like a paste, if too dry add more milk. Add meat, and make sure it is well mixed. Form into balls, brown in skillet, and add to sauce to finish cooking in sauce.

Submitter: 
John
Description: 
The perfect tender meatball without any chunks.
