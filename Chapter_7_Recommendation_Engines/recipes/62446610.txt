Title:
Tofu Hiyayakko Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/461577.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 pack  Tofu silken
1/2 Tbs  Ginger grated
a pinch of Scallion (cut thinly)
a pinch of Bonito freaks
a pinch of Sesame (Toasted is better)
1 Tbs  Soy sauce
1 Tea spoon  Sugar
1/2 Tea spoon  Dashi powder (Bonito Fish Soup Stock)
1/2 Tea spoon water

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the Soy sauce, Sugar, Dashi powder and Water. and mix.
Cut Scallion thinly, Grate ginger.
Place Tofu on the center of small plate, and top with the ginger, bonito flakes and scallion. Drizzle soy-dashi dressing and sprinkle the sesame seeds with Tofu.
Done! MESHIAGARE :-)

Submitter:

Description: 
This is one of a really traditional easy dishes in Japan. My dad likes it so much. We offen eat this dish with the rice, miso soup, grilled fish and Japanese pickles in the morning.
