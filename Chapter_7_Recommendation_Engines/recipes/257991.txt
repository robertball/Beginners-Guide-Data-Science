Title:
Pasta Side Salad Made Over Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4508756.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup MIRACLE WHIP Light Dressing
1/4 cup KRAFT Light Zesty Italian Dressing
1 cup multi-grain elbow macaroni, cooked
1 cup KRAFT 2% Milk Shredded Colby & Monterey Jack Cheeses
3/4 cup matchstick-cut carrots
1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
1/2 cup chopped red bell peppers

Directions:
Mix dressings in large bowl.
Add remaining ingredients; mix lightly.
Refrigerate 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Miracle Whip
Description: 
We cut the fat-but not the creamy, delicious flavor-from our favorite pasta side salad.
