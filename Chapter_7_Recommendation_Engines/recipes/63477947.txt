Title:
Christmas Breakfast Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground pork sausage
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/4 cup finely chopped onions
1 tsp butter
1 teaspoon mustard powder
5 eggs, beaten
1 cup milk
2 slices multigrain bread, toasted and cut into cubes
8 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Crumble sausage into a medium skillet. Drizzle with maple syrup. Cook over medium heat until evenly brown; drain. In same pan, saute onion in butter.
In a medium bowl, mix together mustard powder, eggs and milk. In a greased 1 1/2 qt casserole dish, layer bread cubes, sausage, and onions. Pour egg mixture over ingredients, and top with cheese. Cover, and chill in the refrigerator for 8 hours, or overnight (optional).
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cover, and bake 45 minutes. Uncover, and reduce temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until set.
