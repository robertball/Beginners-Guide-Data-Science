Title:
Firecracker Grilled Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 - 4oz. salmon filets
1/2 cup peanut oil
4 tbsp. soy sauce
4 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
4 tbsp. scallions - chopped
3 tsp. brown sugar
2 cloves garlic - minced
1 1/2 tsp. ground ginger
2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1 tsp. sesame oil
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients and pour over salmon.
Cover and marinate for 4-6 hours in the refrigerator.
Grill salmon for 10 minutes per inch of thickness, turning halfway through cooking, until fish just flakes with a fork.
