Title:
Homemade MarshMallow Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 cup water
2 cups light corn syrup
1 cup egg whites
1/2 cup light corn syrup
4 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Cook sugar, water and 2 cups corn syrup to 240 degrees.
In large bowl beat egg whites and 1/2 cup corn syrup until mixed.
Beat until light and fluffy then slowly add hot syrup while beating.
When well combined beat on high for 3 minutes then add vanilla extract.
