Title:
Fruit Skewers with Raspberry Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1/4 cup unsweetened frozen raspberries
2 teaspoons powdered sugar
1 pint strawberries
1/2 cantaloupe
1 10 3/4 ounce can pineapple
1 bunch grapes
1 banana
2 apples

Directions:
In Batter Bowl, combine cream cheese, yogurt, raspberries and powdered sugar; cream with Bamboo Spoon until smooth.  Chill.  Prepare fruit by washing, coring, stemming and peeling as necessary.  Cut larger fruits into chunks using 5 Inch Self-Sharpening Utility Knife.
Thread onto skewers, arrange around dip and serve.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
These pretty skewers are a great way to get fruit into the kids!
