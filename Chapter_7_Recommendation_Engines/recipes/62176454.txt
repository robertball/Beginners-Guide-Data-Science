Title:
Christmas cookies- Candy canes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C butter
1/2C powdered sugar
1tsp vanilla
1/4tsp salt
2c sifted flour
1/2tsp baking powder
Frosting
1/2C powdered sugar
1tsp vanilla
1/4tsp peppermint extract

Directions:
Cream the butter, then add the powdered sugar, vanilla and salt. Then add the flour and baking powder. Shape a level tsp of dough into a  pencil thick. Turn one end to resemble a candy cane. Bake at 350 for 10min.
