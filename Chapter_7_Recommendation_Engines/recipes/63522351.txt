Title:
Easy Caprese Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961785.jpg
Ingredients:
3 oz sliced prosciutto
2 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 Cups Sargento Off The Block Shredded Traditional Cut Mozzarella
1 Cup fresh basil, finely chopped
2 large plum tomatoes, seeds removed, diced small
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
15 Mini fillo shells, defrosted

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Line an edged baking sheet with foil.  Place prosciutto slices on foil and bake for 10-15 minutes or until crispy.  Allow prosciutto to cool and crumble into small pieces.
3.  In a medium mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, mozzarella, basil, tomatoes, prosciutto, italian seasoning, black pepper, and salt.  Work ingredients together until evenly mixed.
4.  Gently press filling into mini fillo shells, filling to the top of shell.
5. Place filled fillo shells on baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and filling is heated through, being careful not to allow fillo to over-brown.

An easy-to-make appetizer that captures classic caprese flavor in each bite!
