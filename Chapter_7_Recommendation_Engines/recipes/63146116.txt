Title:
Pineapple Upside-Down Cake in a Glass Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/805065.jpg
Ingredients:
1 part Cake-Flavored Vodka (such as UV Cake Vodka)
2 parts Pineapple Juice
1 Splash of Grenadine
Maraschino Cherry
Ice

Directions:
Fill glass with ice. Pour in Grenadine. Add vodka and pineapple juice. Garnish with maraschino cherry. Stir well before enjoying.

Submitter: 
CookinBug
Description: 
Cake-flavored vodka just may be the best thing in a bottle - it tastes great mixed with just about anything. This yummy cocktail combines the flavored vodka with pineapple juice for a sweet treat.
