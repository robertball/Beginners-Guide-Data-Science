Title:
dill Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart vinegar
3 quarts water
1 cup salt
7 quarts small cucumbers
sprays of fresh dill
cloves of garlic (optional)

Directions:
pack 7- 1 quart jars of cucumbers and a spray of dill in each jar.
Mix brine and bring to boil, pour this over cucumbers and seal.
