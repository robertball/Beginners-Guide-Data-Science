Title:
Boiled Mustard Potatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 potatos (whichever you prefer. I like Yukon Gold..they hold up more)
6 cups water
1/2-3/4 cup yellow mustard (depends on your taste)

Directions:
Peel, Halve or Quarter potatos.
Place potatos in cold water. Salt to taste.
Add mustard
Boil for 30 minutes or until fork-tender.
Use a slotted spoon for serving.
It's that easy!

Submitter: 
AbsintheMinded0
Description: 
This is a side dish that my husbands family in the south has been eating with Corned Beef and Cabbage for years.  Its a great addition to this traditional meal.  The potatos act like a sponge, taking in the mustard flavor. YUM!
