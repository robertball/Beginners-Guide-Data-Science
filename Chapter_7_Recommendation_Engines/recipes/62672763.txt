Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium  eggplant (peeled and cut into 1/2 inch slices)
1/2 C Italian bread crumbs
1 C Parmesan cheese (separate 1/4 cup out)
Sprinkle of red pepper flakes
1/2 C Olive oil or 1/4 cup mayonnaise
8 oz. fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced)
Sauce
1 6oz. can tomato paste
1 1/2 cans of water
1/4 t. basil
1/4 t. parsley
1/4 t. oregano
1 clove garlic crushed
1 tsp. brown sugar
Sea salt and fresh pepper ground to taste.

Directions:
Combine crumbs and red pepper flakes with 1/4 C Parmesan cheese.  Brush both sides of eggplant slices with olive oil or mayonaise and dip into bread crumb mixture.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet, bake 425 degrees for 15 minutes or until brown and fork tender.  Remove from oven and lower temperature to 375 degrees.
Meanwhile prepare sauce:  Blend tomato sauce, water, brown sugar, garlic and spices together in a small saucepan and cook gently for 10 minutes.
Grease 8x14 inch pan. Arrange eggplant slices, overlapping in dish. Spoon tomato sauce over eggplant. Top with mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese.  Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees for 15 minutes, or when cheese melts. Can broil for a minute to give cheese nice golden color. Let sit for 5 minutes, then serve.

Submitter: 
Brenda
Description: 
I wasn't an eggplant lover until a friend gave me this recipe which I have tweaked and updated.  The few ingredients in the sauce is far tastier than canned sauce and makes the dish. All you need is a side salad and crusty bread to make a meal!
