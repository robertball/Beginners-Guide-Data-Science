Title:
Grandma Rose's Sweet ‘n’ Sour Chili Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. lean pork spare ribs
¾ cup brown sugar
½ cup ketchup
½ cup white vinegar
2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. chili powder
¾ cup water
1 onion, diced

Directions:
Trim fat off ribs and cut in desired sizes. Spread in bottom of large, shallow baking dish or roaster.
Mix all other ingredients in medium-sized bowl and pour over ribs.
Bake, uncovered, at 250 for 3 hours.
