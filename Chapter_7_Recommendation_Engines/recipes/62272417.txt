Title:
Corn beef Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can corned beef
3 eggs
2 boiled potatoes
1 onion cut in small pieces
some parsley
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients but dont make it too soft.  Put some oil into a pan (deep fry).  Take mixture and make into patties and put them in the oil, flipping them till the brown.  Serve
