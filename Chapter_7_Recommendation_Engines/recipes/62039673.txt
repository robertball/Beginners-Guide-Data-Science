Title:
Easy Chocolate Chip Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30-40 chocolate chip cookies (your own or store bought)
2 cups whipping cream
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
Fruit or shaved chocolate for garnish.

Directions:
In the bottom of a 9 or 10 inch springform pan, place 10-12 cookies in one layer.  Crumble 3-5 cookies to fill in the spaces, using a total of 15 or 20.  In a bowl, at medium speed, beat cream, sugar, and vanilla until still peaks form.  Spoon half the whipped cream over cookies, spread evenly to edges.  Add a second layer of cookies using remaining cookies and cover with remaining whipped cream.  Cover and refrigerate.
Decorate with favorite fruit or shaved chocolate curls.
To serve, loosen edge with metal spatula and remove rim of pan.  Cut into wedges.

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
Do you have a bunch of chocolate chip cookies that you forgotten about and went stale? Don't break a tooth, grind them up and make this recipe!  The whipping cream will bring them back to life and make you new friends.  Delish!
