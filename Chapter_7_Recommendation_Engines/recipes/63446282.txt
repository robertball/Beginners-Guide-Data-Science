Title:
Egg Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 c milk
3 c flour
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together until batter follows spoon in bowl.
In large pot of boiling water or soup broth, drop spoonfuls of batter.
Serve in soups or as a side with butter.
