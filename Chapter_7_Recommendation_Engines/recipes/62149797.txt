Title:
Southern Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups (8 oz.) extra sharp cheddar
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
½ cup mayonnaise
¼ - ½ tsp garlic powder
¼ tsp cayenne powder (optional)
¼ tsp onion powder
1 jalapeno, seeded and diced fine (optional)
4 oz. jar diced pimento, drained
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large bowl of mixer. Beat at medium speed, with paddle if possible, until thoroughly combined. Makes 3 cups.

Submitter: 
QUEENREYNEY
Description: 
This wonderful version of pimento cheese can be used for grilled cheese sandwiches, as a spread for crackers, or served alongside your favorite fried green tomatoes.
