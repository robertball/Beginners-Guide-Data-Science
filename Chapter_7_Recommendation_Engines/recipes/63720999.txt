Title:
Crockpot Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp dried basi
1/2 tsp saltl
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tsp dried mince onion
4 garlic cloves minced
1 tblsp extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
8 boneless chicken breasts or thighs
sprinkle of fresh  chopped parsley

Directions:
Combine first 5 dry spices in a small bowland sread over chicken on both sides.  Set aside.  Pour olive oil and garlic on the bottom of the crock pot.  Place chicken on top.  Pour balsamic vinegar over the chicken.  Cover & cook on high for 4 hours.  Sprinkle with fresh parsley on top to serve.
