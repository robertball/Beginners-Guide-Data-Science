Title:
Yogurt Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped fresh spinach
1 cup plain yogurt (use Greek yogurt)
1 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoons seasoning salt
1/4 teaspoon dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon ground dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together spinach, plain yogurt, mayonnaise, seasoning salt, parsley, basil, oregano, dry mustard and garlic salt. Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Trish Gillogly
Description: 
Plain yogurt is a great flavor addition to a simple spinach dip.Modified from: Yogurt Spinach Dip
