Title:
Best Ever Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2365931.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Butter
1/2 plus 1/3 Cup Shortening
1 Cup White Sugar
1 Cup Brown Sugar
2 Large Eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
1 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Baking Soda
3 Cups Flour
12 oz. Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chip

Directions:
Cream together Butter, Shortening and Sugars until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
Add in the eggs and vanilla and mix well.
In a separate bowl, combine flour, soda and salt and stir together.   Slowly add dry ingredients to the dough, mixing just until combined.
Add the chocolate chips and stir by hand to combine.
Roll into 1 inch balls and place on a cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees for 12 minutes.
