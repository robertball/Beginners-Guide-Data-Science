Title:
Blueberry-Cinnamon Overnight Oats with Greek Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6241235.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup blueberries
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/4 cup soy milk
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon, or to taste
2 teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Combine rolled oats, blueberries, Greek yogurt, soy milk, cinnamon, and sugar in a lidded jar. Cover, shake to mix, and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.

Submitter: 
nikiki
Description: 
Blueberries, cinnamon, and Greek yogurt are great additions to overnight oats.
