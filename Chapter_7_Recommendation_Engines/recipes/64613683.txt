Title:
Cast Iron Skillet Sweet Honey Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. cornmeal
1 c. all purpose flour
1/3 c. sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
2 T. melted butter
3 T. vegetable oil
2 tsp. honey
1 extra large egg, beaten
2/3 c. milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Lightly grease an 8" cast iron skillet with shortening, put it the oven to preheat.
Combine dry ingredients in a medium sized bowl.
Combine wet ingredients in a second bowl, add all at once to dry ingredients. Stir until just combined.
Pour into hot cast iron skillet. Bake 25-30 minutes or until firm and beginning to lightly brown.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Our favorite homemade cornbread recipe. This is the perfect pairing for a hot bowl of chili in the winter.
