Title:
Slow Cooker Tapioca Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups milk
2/3 cup white sugar or Splenda White Sugar Blend
1/2 cup small pearl tapioca
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
pinch Kosher salt
2 large eggs, lightly beaten

Directions:
Stir together the milk, eggs, vanilla, Kosher salt, and sugar in a medium bowl.
Add the tapioca and the egg mixture into a slow cooker.
Cover, and cook on "HIGH" for 3 hours, or on "LOW" for 6 hours, stirring occasionally.
Serve warm.
