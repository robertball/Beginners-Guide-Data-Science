Title:
Egg White Oatmeal Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup liquid egg whites
1 whole egg
1/3 cup oats

Directions:
Combine eggs in a heated skillet.
Sprinkle the oats on top and cook until the bottom is browned.
Flip the pancake. Let cook until browned.
Top with fruit or almond butter, if desired.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
These can be rolled up and eaten like breakfast tacos if you're in a hurry.
