Title:
Zucchini Leek Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium zucchini, grated & well drained
4 leeks, finely sliced, white and pale green part of leek, washed well
1 large bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
8oz block extra sharp white cheddar cheese,  shredded
1 pkg (6oz) chicken flavor stove top stuffing, crushed to bread crumb size
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/4 tsp dried basil
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 large eggs
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Butter a 9x13 baking dish.
Combine the zucchini, leeks, bell pepper, parmesan, cheddar, and stuffing in a large bowl.  In a separate bowl beat the eggs, oil, garlic, salt and pepper, and basil.  Pour egg mixture over vegetables and mix together well.  Pour into buttered dish and bake for 45min to 1 hour, until top is golden and mixture is bubbling.
