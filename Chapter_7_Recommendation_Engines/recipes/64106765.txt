Title:
Salmon Filo Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz. can chopped tomatoes.
1 large green pepper.
Chopped garlic (amount to taste).
1-1 1/2 lbs of salmon (skinless)
1 can filo dough
1 tbsp butter (optional)
1 tsp garlic powder (optional)

Directions:
Sautee canned tomatoes, pepper, and garlic until mixture is dry.
Filet the skin off the salmon.
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.
Place 8 or 9 skeets of filo dough on counter.
Place salmon on dough and sauteed tomato mixture on top of salmon.
Wrap up.
Melt some butter in a bowl, stir in garlic powder.
Spread melted butter over the filo, salmon, tomato wrap.
Wrap another few 8 or 9 filo sheets around salmon & tomato.
Spread with melted butter again and repeat several times.
Place in wrapped salmon in casserole dish and put in 375 degree oven for 35 min or until salmon is cooked. Filo dough will look puffy.
