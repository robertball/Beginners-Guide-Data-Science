Title:
Cilla's Pineapple Glazed Ham Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons cornstarch
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1/3 cup vinegar
1 (6 ounce) can pineapple juice
1 (1.5 ounce) box raisins

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, mix together cornstarch and brown sugar. Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, pineapple juice and raisins. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 10 to 15 minutes or until sauce has thickened.

Submitter: 
KARLAWITHAK13
Description: 
This recipe is a glaze sauce to put over baked ham. It has pineapple, raisins, and brown sugar. My mom has made this recipe growing up and it is so good!
