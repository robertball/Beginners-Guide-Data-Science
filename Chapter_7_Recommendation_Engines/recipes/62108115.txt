Title:
Amazing Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12-18 Roma tomatos skinned
8 medium onions
8 apples peeled and grated
2 green peppers
8 garlic cloves crushed
2 1/2 cups vinegar
2/3 cup sugar
5 Tbsp coarse salt
1/2 Tbsp paprika
1/2 Tbsp dry mustard
2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 (13 oz) can tomato paste
1 jalapeno pepper (optional)

Directions:
Chop tomatoes, peppers,onions into chunks.
Put into large pot.
Add garlic, apples, vinegar and tomato paste.
Cook 1/2hr to 1hr until thick.
Add sugar, salt and spices.
Boil 3 minutes
Fill pints, seal and process for 15 min.
Ready to eat and stores wonderfully

Submitter: 
T.Margolis
Description: 
This recipe takes preparation time but the result is so worth it! And no one will suspect that apples are used to sweeten this salsa A staple in our house!
