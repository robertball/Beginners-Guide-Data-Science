Title:
Multigrain Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 cups boiling water
1/4 cup multigrain cereal
2 TBS. quick oats
2 TBS. honey
1 TBS. butter
1 tsp. salt
2-1/2 cups bread flour
1-1/2 tsp rapid rise yeast

Directions:
Place water in bread machine pan.  Add cereal and oats.  let stand 30 minutes or until cool.  Measuring carefully, place remaining ingredients in bread machine pan in order specified by owner's manual/
Program basic cycle and desired crust setting; press start.  Remove baked bread from pan; cool on wire rack.
