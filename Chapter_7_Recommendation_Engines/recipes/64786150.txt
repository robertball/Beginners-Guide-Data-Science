Title:
Summertime Mule Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the cocktails
5 mint leaves
1.5 inch piece of English cucumber , diced
1/4 cup fresh blueberries
2 fl oz premium vodka, such as Titoâ€™s
1/ 2 lime, cut into two wedges
4 fl oz premium brewed ginger beer, such as Fentimans
Ice
For the Garnish
3 blueberries
2 mint leaves
1 slice cucumber

Directions:
Fill a copper cup or mug with ice.
Meddle the mint leaves in a cocktail shaker until crushed. Add the diced cucumber, 1/8 cup blueberries, and two lime wedges. Meddle until the cucumber and blueberries are crushed and the limes have been juiced. Add ice and vodka. Cover the shaker and shake until frost has formed on the outside the shaker.
Strain the cocktail into the iced cups, top with ginger beer and garnish with 3 blueberries, 2 mint leaves, and a slice of cucumber.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Vodka and ginger beer mingle with summery flavors for a crisp and refreshing cocktail to enjoy on hot days. This pairs nicely with crunchy raw veggies for an aperitivo outdoors.
