Title:
Panera Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pasta
1/4 cup butter (1/2 stick)
1/3 cup flour
2 1/2 cups milk
4 oz process white American cheese
8 oz extra sharp white Cheddar, shredded
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp kosher salt
few dashes Tabasco
few sprinkles paprika (optional)

Directions:
Set water to boil and cook pasta to al dente.
Meanwhile, melt butter in large pot.  Whisk in flour and cook 1 minute.  Gradually whisk in milk and cook until thickened and bubbling.  Remove from heat and stir in cheeses, mustard, and Tabasco..  Stir in cooked pasta.  Sprinkle with paprika.
