Title:
Crunchy Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Adams Crunchy Peanut Butter*
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
3 cups crisp rice cereal
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips, divided
4 teaspoons butter

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the peanut butter and marshmallow creme; add cereal and 1/2 cup chocolate chips and stir until well coated.
In a small microwave-safe bowl, combine 1+1/2 cups chocolate chips and butter. Microwave, uncovered, for 1-2 minutes or until chips are melted; stir until smooth.
Roll cereal mixture into 1-in. balls; dip in chocolate.
Place on a waxed paper-lined pan.
Refrigerate until set.
