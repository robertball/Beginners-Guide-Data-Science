Title:
porcupine balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 cup instant rice uncooked
2 tablespoons minced onion
1/2 a teaspoon salt and pepper
two 10 1/2 ounce cans tomato soup
1 1/4 cups water

Directions:
mix first four ingredients together. In a large saucepan or slow cooker mix together tomato soup and water. roll ground beef mixture into balls and drop into soup.turn on low and slow cook.

Submitter: 
Nancy Edenfield
Description: 
my family just loves these. With my husband and father-in-law who have trouble eating a lot of different foods this is perfect meal for them.
