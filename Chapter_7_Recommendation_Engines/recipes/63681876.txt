Title:
Banana Pineapple Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-oz. container vanilla low fat yogurt
3/4 cup pineapple juice
1 large ripe banana

Directions:
Cut banana into 4 pieces. Place all ingredients into a blender. Blend on medium speed for about 1 minute or until smooth.
