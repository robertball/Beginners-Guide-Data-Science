Title:
Thai-Style Baked Coconut Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
375ml (1 1/2 cups) coconut milk
5 eggs, beaten
110g brown sugar
100g white sugar
2 tablespoons cream

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 175degrees Celsius.
Combine coconut milk, eggs, brown sugar, white sugar and cream. Put the bowl over a double boiler and cook until the custard coats the back of a spoon, about 10-20 minutes.
Pour the custard into a 22cm square pan and bake for about 30 minutes, or until the custard is done.
Place under a broiler and broil until the top is golden brown. Let cool and cut into squares for all to enjoy.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a basic coconut custard that is baked in an oven. It is baked in a square pan, then broiled to give it a caramelized top. It is then cooled and cut into squares.
