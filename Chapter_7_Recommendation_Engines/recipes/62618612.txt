Title:
Lemper Ayam (Sticky Rice with Chicken) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/570115.jpg
Ingredients:
Sticky Rice:
3 cups of sticky rice, soak for 2 hours or overnight with 2 cups of water (4 cups 3/4 coconut milk cook with rice cooker or 4 cups soak overnight, add 1 cups of coconut milk, add 3 cup of water - cover and microwave for 6 minutes, stir, another 6 minutes)
1 cup coconut milk
add 1/2 to 1 cup additional water
1 Tsp salt
Bay leave (Optional)
lemongrass, bruised (optional)
Stuffing:
2 Boneless Chicken Breast-boil and sredded
3 Gloves Garlic
5 Shallots
3 Candlenuts (Kemiri)
1 Tbsp of coriander (Ketumbar)
2 Tbsp of garlic chili
1/2 Tsp of Shcrimp paste (Terasi)
6 Kaffir Limes
3 Bay Leave
1/2 Tsp Salt
3 Tbsp Granulated Sugar.
3 pieces of Galanga (Langkuas)
2 pieces Lemongrass
1/2 tsp Cumin
1/2 tsp Kencur
1 Cup coconut milk

Directions:
1. Cook Sticky rice with rice cooker or steam.
2. Cook stuffing.
3. Spread half of the cooked sticky rice on 9x11 pyrex.
4 Layer with all the stuffing.
5.Cover with another half of the sticky rice.
6.Cut the rice to 1x2 inch pieces. Ready to serve or
7.For better presentation-wrap the lemper with banana leave and wax paper. (small 4/5-1/2m big 5x6)
