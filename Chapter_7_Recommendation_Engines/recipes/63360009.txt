Title:
Tortiglioni Melanzana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894823.jpg
Ingredients:
10oz tortiglioni
1 medium eggplant, thinly sliced
1 can whole tomatoes
1 medium carrot, finely chopped
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 tablespoons kalamata olives, sliced
2 tablespoons grated grana padano cheese
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon sugar
salt & pepper

Directions:
In a medium pan, saut√© the onion and carrots in olive oil until onion is transparent
Add the can of tomatoes, sugar, salt and pepper and simmer for 10 minutes
In a large frying pan, heat the vegetable oil and fry the eggplant slices 2 minutes on each side on high heat. Place the eggplant slices on a paper tower to absorb the oil
In a large pot, boil water and cook pasta until it is al dente, about 10 minutes
Mash the tomato mixture with a fork and transfer to a large bowl
Add the eggplant, kalamatata olives, pasta, mayonnaise and cheese and stir to incorporate

Description: 
An easy but very yummy pasta recipe with eggplant and kalamata olives.
