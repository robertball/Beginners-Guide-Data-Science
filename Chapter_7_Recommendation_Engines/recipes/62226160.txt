Title:
Tangy Slow Cooker Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chunked
4 red potatoes, quartered
3 carrots, chunked
2 1/2 pounds boneless pork loin roast
1 1/2 cup hot water
1 teaspoon chicken bouillon
1/2 cup white sugar
7 tablespoons red wine vinegar
5 tablespoons soy sauce
3 tablespoon ketchup
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon minced garlic

Directions:
Arrange onion slices, potatoes, and carrots evenly over the bottom of the slow cooker, and then place the roast on top of the onion.  In a bowl, mix together water, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, ketchup, black pepper, salt, and garlic; pour over roast.
Cover, and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours, or on High for 3 to 4 hours.
