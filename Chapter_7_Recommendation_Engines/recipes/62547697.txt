Title:
Dark Chocolate Mocha Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 cup sour cream
2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
*
Mocha Cream Cheese Frosting:
1 8 oz. package of cream cheese
1/2 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 tablespoon instant coffee
2 teaspoons hot water
2 1/2 - 3 cups powdered sugar
*
Chocolate Glaze:
1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1 tablespoon lite corn syrup
1/3 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees. Grease a bundt pan or 3 -9 inch round cake pans. In large bowl pour boiling water over cocoa, whisk until smooth. Add butter and sugar and let sit until melted and cooled stirring occasionally. After mixture has cooled whisk in eggs one at a time then mix in the vanilla and sour cream.
In another large bowl mix the rest of the dry ingredients together and then slowly add the cocoa mixture beating until well blended.
Optional: stir in  1 1/2 cups miniature semi-sweet chocolate chips before baking.
Bake in a bundt pan for 45-55 minutes. Or bake in 3 9 inch pans for 25 - 30 minutes.
Allow to cool 10 minutes and then remove from pans.
Mocha Cream Cheese Frosting:
In a cup mix coffee with hot water to dissolve. In large bowl beat cream cheese and butter; add vanilla and sifted powered sugar and beat with mixer. Add in dissolved coffee and mix well.
Frost cake with mocha frosting.
Chocolate Glaze:
In a medium bowl melt chocolate chips, butter and corn syrup in microwave for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Stir well and let sit for 5 minutes.
Pour glaze over frosted cake allowing the glaze to drizzle down the sides.
Chill cake for 15 minutes.
