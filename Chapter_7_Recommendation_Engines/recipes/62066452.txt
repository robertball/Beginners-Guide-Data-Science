Title:
Wendy's Indian Curry Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 onion
1 clove garlic
1/4 cp veg oil
4 chicken breast halves cut into bite size pieces
8 tomatoes (or 1 big can of stewed tomatotoes)
1 large green bell pepper
3 tsp salt
2 tsp Garam Masala
1/2 cp cream
1/4 cp butter
1 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Blend onion and garlic in blender with a couple Tb of water.  Sautee in frying pan with 1/4 cp oil.  Puree tomatoes and green pepper in blender and mix with onion and garlic in pan.  Cook until tender.  Add salt, garam masala, chili powder, cream, and butter.  Stir.  Add chicken and simmer 20 minutes.  Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
AJF180
Description: 
This recipe resembles a dish from an Indian restaurant in my town.  I love it.  It's got a great unique favor to it, sweet and curry, garam masala is the spice used.  Creamy - a favorite!
