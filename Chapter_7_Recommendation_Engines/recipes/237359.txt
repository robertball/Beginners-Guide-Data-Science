Title:
Spin-Off Puppy Chow  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
9 cups crispy rice cereal squares (such as Rice Chex Â®)
1 cup white chocolate chips
1/2 cup sunflower seed butter
1/4 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar, or more to taste

Directions:
Place cereal squares in a bowl.
Combine white chocolate chips, sunflower seed butter, and butter in a microwave-safe bowl; heat in microwave until melted and smooth, about 1 1/2 minutes. Stir chocolate mixture; add vanilla extract and stir. Pour chocolate mixture over cereal; stir to evenly coat. Add confectioners' sugar and stir until evenly coated.

Submitter: 
Allison Paige
Description: 
Like puppy chow, but with a few different ingredients. Great for people who are allergic to peanuts or cannot have milk chocolate.
