Title:
Beef Wing Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1/3 c Shredded Cheddar or Monterey Jack Cheese
1/3 c Softened Cream Cheese
1 Tbs Garlic Powder
1 Tbs Italian Seasoning
1 Tbs Siracha or Tobasco Sauce
2 Tbs Minced Celery
1/2 c Blue Cheese or Ranch Dressing
1/2 c Prepared Buffalo Wing Sauce
Lettuce Leaves for Garnish
4 Hard Rolls
Salt and Fresh Ground Pepper to taste

Directions:
Divide Ground Beef into 4 equal parts and press flat into patties. Set aside.
Mix shredded cheese, cream cheese, Garlic Powder, Italian Seasoning, and Hot Sauce in a small bowl. Refrigerate until firm enough to form into 4 equal balls.
Place cheese mixture into the center of each patty, form beef around mixture, sealing cheese with the beef patty making sure to seal and none of the mixture is exposed. Press down to form burger. Season with salt and pepper. Place on a platter in the freezer and freeze until patty is firm.
Mince celery and mix with dressing and set aside.
Pour Buffalo Sauce into a shallow dish.
Grill or pan fry burgers to desired doneness being careful not to press down on burger while cooking and only turning one time.
While burgers are cooking, toast or grill rolls.
After burgers have cooked, remove to plate and let rest for one minute. Dip burgers in wing sauce, place on rolls and top with dressing and lettuce.
