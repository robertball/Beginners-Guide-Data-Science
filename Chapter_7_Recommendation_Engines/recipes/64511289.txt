Title:
Rhubarb Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup margarine
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp. baking soda
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups rhubarb, cut fine
1/3 cup walnuts or pecans, chopped (optional, but very good!)

Directions:
Cream together margarine, sugar, sour cream and baking soda.
Blend in flour and salt.
Add rhubarb and nuts.
Put in greased muffin tin.
Bake @ 350 x 20 minutes. (add time if using frozen rhubarb).

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
The brown sugar & sour cream make these delicious! I've substituted apples for the rhubarb, which is also yummy. No eggs needed for this one.
