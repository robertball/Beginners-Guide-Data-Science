Title:
Red Wine Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds pork tenderloin (if cooking more, use several small tenderloins for better cooking)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 onion, minced
2 cloves garlic minced
1 cups good red wine
1 cup low salt beef broth
1 (.75 ounce) packet dry brown gravy mix
1/2 lb baby bella mushsrooms sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brown pork on all sides in oil
Saute mushrooms in meat leavings with butter - set aside
Place pork in a 9x13 inch baking dish, and sprinkle meat with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Top with onion and garlic, and pour wine and broth over all.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Use meat thermometer to asure medium but not overcooked.
When done baking, remove meat from baking dish, and place on a warm serving platter. Pour gravy mix into baking dish with wine and cooking juices, and stir until thickened. Add in mushrooms and heat thoroughly.Slice meat, and cover with the gravy.
