Title:
Brunch Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices white bread, coarsely torn
1 lb. bulk sausage, cooked and drained (or bacon)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
5 eggs
2 cups milk
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly grease 13x9 pan or oven-proof casserole.  Place bread in bottom of pan; sprinkle with sausage (or bacon) and cheese.
Beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper in medium bowl with wire whisk.  Pour over bread mixture in pan.
Bake 40 minutes or until mixture is set in center and lightly browned.
