Title:
BOB'S Red Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/366959.jpg
Ingredients:
8 medium red potatoes, cubed
3 teaspoons crushed garlic
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup half-and-half cream
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/4 teaspoon steak seasoning
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup of Sour Cream
1/8 cup Shreaded 4 Cheeses
3 Tbsp of ground Parsley

Directions:
Place the potatoes into a large pot, and fill with enough water to cover. Add 1 teaspoon of the crushed garlic to the water for flavor. Bring to a boil, and cook for about 10 minutes, or until easily pierced with a fork.
Drain the potatoes, and add the butter. Mash until the butter is melted. Mix in the half-and-half, sugar, steak seasoning, garlic powder and remaining garlic. Sour Cream,  Shreaded 4 Cheeses. Do Not whip unless desired. Defore serving add 1 Tbsp of butter, parsley, and salt to taste.
