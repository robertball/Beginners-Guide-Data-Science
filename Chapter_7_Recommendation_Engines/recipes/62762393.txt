Title:
Apple Cheddar Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639106.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup (60 mL) regular or low fat mayonnaise
1/4 cup (60 mL) apple sauce
1 tsp (5 mL) Dijon mustard
4 large flour tortillas or wraps
4 lettuce leaves
1 cup (250 mL) diced cooked chicken
1 apple, peeled, quartered and diced
2 green onions, sliced
1 cup (250 mL) Tre Stelle® Mild Shredded Cheddar
Cheese

Directions:
Combine mayonnaise, apple sauce and mustard.
Spread
about 2 tbsp on one side of each tortilla.
Top
with lettuce leaf, cup chicken, apple, 1 tbsp onion and cup Tre Stelle® Shredded
Cheddar.
Roll wrap tightly and cut in half on
a bias.

Submitter: 
TreStelle

Quick, easy and extremely versatile.
