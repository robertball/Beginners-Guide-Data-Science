Title:
Cottage Cheese Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6034437.jpg
Ingredients:
4 C Cottage Cheese
1/2 C Melted Butter
6 Eggs
1 C Walnuts finely chopped
1 package dry Lipton's onion soup mix
1 T Cumin
2 C Extra sharp cheddar cheese grated
3 C Herbed bread Stuffing, (dry)
3 C Special K Cereal
1 Large onion chopped and sautéed until translucent
1 Stalk Celery, chopped
2 heads garlic peeled and minced

Directions:
In the butter sauté onions until translucent add celery sauté another 2 minutes, mix in the onion soup mix.
Mix all ingredients together and place in greased 9 X 13 pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
* It is good if mixture feels quite dry and a little hard to work with
**If too dry to work with add small amount of milk
