Title:
Texas Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978600.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, or diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 (11 ounce) can Mexicorn, drained
2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chilies
4 green onions, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon dried minced garlic

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine all ingredients. Cover and cook on high for 4-5 hours or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Pamela  Scott
Description: 
This hearty stew made with convenient canned items is perfect for spicing up a family gathering on a cool day. 'It tastes great and requires so little time and attention,' notes Pamela Scott of Garland, Texas.
