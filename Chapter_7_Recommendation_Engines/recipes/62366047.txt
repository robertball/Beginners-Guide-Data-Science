Title:
Apple Shortbread Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (12 oz.) packs frozen apple chunks
6 Tab. butter or margarine
20 shortbread cookies (like Keeblers Sandies)crushed
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tea. cinnamon, divided
1/4 tea. nutmeg
2 Tab. brown sugar

Directions:
prick plastic wrap covering apples several times w/ a fork, nuke on medium ( 50% power) 7 minutes or until apples are thawed. let stand 2 min.
melt butter in skillet over med. add cookie crumbs and walnuts cook, stirring, for 2 min. Remove from heat and stir in 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1/2 tea. cinnamon, and the nutmeg. combine 2 Tab. brown sugar and the other half cinnamon, sprinkle in a lightly greased 1 quart baking dish. Sprinkle half of apples over top w/ half crumb mixture. repeat layers. bake at 375 for 25 min. or until cookie mixture is golden. Good w/ vanilla ice cream
