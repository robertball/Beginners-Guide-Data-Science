Title:
Mandarin Orange Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986831.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup frozen orange juice concentrate, pulp free
2 eggs
1 (15 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 (6 ounce) READY CRUST速 Shortbread Pie Crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Mix cream cheese, sugar and orange juice concentrate on medium speed until fluffy.
Add eggs, one at a time, and mix until blended; fold in mandarin oranges.
Pour into crust. Bake for 30 minutes or until center is just set. Cool and refrigerate 3 hours.
