Title:
Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  teaspoons  butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 teaspoons chopped garlic
4  cups  water
2 teaspoons chicken bouillon
3/4  cup  uncooked pearl barley
3/4  cup  brown rice blend or brown rice
1 Tbsp dried parsley
3 Bay leaves
1/4  teaspoon  freshly ground black pepper
2 Tbsp parmesean cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in medium saucepan, except parmesean cheese. Bring to boil, then turn to low and cover. Simmer for 35-40 minutes or until all water is absorbed. Turn off and let sit for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
