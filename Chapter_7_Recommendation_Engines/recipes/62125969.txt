Title:
Delicious Clam Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. packaged cream cheese
8 oz. can minced clams
1 tbl. Miracle Whip
garlic salt; to taste
1 tbl. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Drain clams and reserve juice.
Mix all ingredients together with an electric mixer.
Add clam juice little by little until a smooth consistency is formed.
Refrigerate before eating.

Submitter: 
Samantha Kristina
Description: 
This is my favorite recipe of all time.  My mother has to make my own private batch during Christmastime, so I don't eat the company's portion!
