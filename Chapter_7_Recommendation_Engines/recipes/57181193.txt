Title:
Grilled Cob Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ears corn, shucked
1/2 cup diced tomato
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 T. minced red onion
2 T. fresh lime juice
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Coat an outdoor grill or stovetop grillpan with cooking spray and preheat to medium high.
Place corn cobs directly on grill, tent with foil and cook 5 minutes, turning frequently, until browned all over.
Slice kernels from cob and place kernels in a medium bowl. Add next 6 ingredients and toss to combine.
Salt and pepper to taste.  Serve at room temperature or chilled.
