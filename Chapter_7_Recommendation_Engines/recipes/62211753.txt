Title:
EASY RITZ CRACKER SNACK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/309857.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup marshmallow cream
1/2 cup peanut butter
48 ritz crackers
1 1/4 cups semisweet chocolate chips,melted
1 cup flaked coconut or chopped nuts

Directions:
Spread about 1 teaspoon peanut butter on 24 Ritz crackers.Spread 1 teaspoon Marshmallow Creme pn remaining crackers. Sandwich crackers together,using one peanut butter and one marshmallow, then dip in melted chocolate or white chocolate [almond bark], and roll in coconut or finely chopped nuts to coat[optional]. Set crackers on waxed paper and let stand until set.
