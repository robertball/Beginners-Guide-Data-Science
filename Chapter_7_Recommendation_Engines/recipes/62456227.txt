Title:
Chipotle Crusted Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/468510.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon onion powder
1 Tablespoon garlic powder
2 Tablespoons chipotle chile powder
1 Tablespoons chile powder
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
4 tablespoons brown sugar
1 2 pound  or 2 (3/4 pound) pork tenderloins

Directions:
Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the onion powder, garlic powder, chipotle chile powder, salt, and brown sugar. Place tenderloins in bag and shake, coating meat evenly. Refrigerate for 10 to 15 minutes or up to 24 hours.
Lightly oil grill grate, and arrange meat on grate. or use heavy duty foil coated with cooking spray. Cook for 20-30 minutes, turning meat every 5 minutes, using the marinade left in the bag to baste it after every turn.. Remove from grill, let stand for 5 to 10 minutes before slicing.
ENJOY!!!

Submitter: 
Donna
Description: 
Sweet and spicy rub for pork tenderloins. Just coat, let stand for 20 minutes and grill! Goes great with garlic mashed potatoes and a beef bordeaux sauce.Modified from: Chipotle Crusted Pork Tenderloin
