Title:
Healthy Vegan Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 medium sugar pumpkin or 1 can of pumpkin.
4 tablespoons Chia seeds
1 cup hot water
1/4 cup agave
1 1/2 cups spelt flour
1  cup brown rice flour
1/3 cup sucanat
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2  teaspoon allspice
1 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
1/2  teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 cup walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Half pumpkin and clean out seeds.
Steam until fully cooked about 35-45 minutes.
Place chia seeds  in a blender add hot water and let sit for 5 minutes.
Scoop out pumpkin from baked pumpkin.
Add to blender.
Add agave to blender mixture.
Blend pumpkin mixture completely.
In a bowl, mix together flours,
Add chia seed mixture to dry ingredients.
Grease baking pans with olive oil.

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
Vegan Pumpkin Bread! This is a NO-fat, egg-free pumpkin bread. No oil, no butter, just pure flavor.  I love this bread, toasted with a little butter or ghee or margarine.
