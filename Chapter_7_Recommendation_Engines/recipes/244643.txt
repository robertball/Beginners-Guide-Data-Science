Title:
Super Easy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F7095398.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
2  eggs
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 (11 ounce) package semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Beat cake mix, eggs, and vegetable oil in a bowl using an electric mixer on medium until dough is well combined; stir in chocolate chips. Drop dough by the spoonful onto a baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes.
