Title:
Polish Meatballs with Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Make Mashed potatoes ahead of time.
Meatball Ingredients:
1/2 C. dry bread crumbs
1/2 C. milk
1 egg
2 pounds ground pork
1/2 C. chopped onions
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. dry marjoram leaves
1/2 tblsp. oregano
2 tblsp. flour
2 tblsp. butter
Sauce Ingredients:
2 Tblsp. flour
1 Beef Bouillon Cube
1 Cup Water
1 1/2 C. Sour Cream
1/4 C. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Meatball Directions:
Mix the meatball ingredients, except for flour and butter.  Shape into meduim sized meatballs. Roll meatballs lightly in the flour to coat.
In skillet, melt the butter and saute the meatballs until lightly browned on all sides.  Place meatballs in the center of a 2 quart baking dish, set aside.
Sauce Directions:
In the same skillte add the flour to the meatball drippings, blend in.  Add the water and bouillon cube, bring to a boil, stirring, add in the sour cream.
Spoon mashed potatoes around the meatballs.  Pour sauce mixture over meatballs and potatoes. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Bake uncovered, 1 hour at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
BASHERGIRL
Description: 
These pork meatballs are a nice break from the traditional beef ones.  Very tasty and always a hit when taking them to a party. My mom stole this recipe from me to use in her catering - people always request them! I often make them without the potatoes!
