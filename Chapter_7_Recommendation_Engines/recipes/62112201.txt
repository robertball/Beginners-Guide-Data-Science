Title:
Hot Onion Souffle` Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3  large pkg cream cheese
3/4 C mayonnaise ( Hellman's)
1 pkg. (10-12 oz.) Frozen chopped onion, rinsed and drained
2 C grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except 1/2 C. Parmesan Cheese.
Place in a baking dish and sprinkle with 1/2 C. Parmesan Cheese.
Bake 350 until the top browns.  About 15-20 minutes.
I prefer the large size corn chips but this dip would be good with pretzels or nachos

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Serve with corn chips for a delicious snack or appetizer for a cocktail buffet.
