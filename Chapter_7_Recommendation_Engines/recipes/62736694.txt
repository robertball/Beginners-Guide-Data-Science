Title:
Melon Bunny Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/627295.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe honeydew melon
1 ripe cantaloupe
fresh pineapple
Seedless grapes
1 watermelon or any other fruit that you like
2 raisins
1 grape
1 carrot

Directions:
Cut the honeydew into the shape of a bunny as shown in the photo.
Remove the fruit from the inside of the bunny shaped honeydew using a medium to large melon baller.
Make balls of cantaloupe and watermelon using a medium to large melon baller.
Cut the pineapple into chunks.
Mix the fruit together in a large bowl.
Using toothpicks to hold in place, add the raisins for eyes.
Cut carrots into a whisker shape and skewer into place with a toothpick. Leave the toothpick sticking out a little bit so that you can add a half of a grape for the nose.
Transfer the fruit salad into the honeydew melon bunny and serve.
It's guaranteed to get smiles and comments of delight.
