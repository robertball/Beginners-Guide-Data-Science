Title:
Meaty Thick Man Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds lean ground turkey
5 T. Worchestershire
5 t. powdered beef bouillion
1 (10 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (10 ounce) can organic fire roasted tomatoes
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
3/4 cup chili powder
1 cup low-fat, low-sodium beef broth

Directions:
Place the ground turkey in a large pot over medium-high heat with Worchestershire and powdered bouiliion. Cook, stirring to crumble, until evenly browned. Drain off grease. Place the pot over medium heat, and add the onion, garlic, green pepper, red pepper and kidney beans. Cook and stir for a few minutes. Stir in the tomato sauce and broth, and season with chili powder. Simmer over low heat for 40 minutes uncovered.
