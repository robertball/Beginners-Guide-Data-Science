Title:
Cucumber Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7032956.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers - peeled, seeded, and chopped
3/8 cup fresh lime juice
1/3 cup white sugar
2/3 cup water

Directions:
Combine the cucumber, lime juice, sugar, and water in a food processor; puree.

Submitter: 
Kelly Baker-Hefley
Description: 
I was desperate to come up with some cucumber recipes after my garden mass produced the veggie.  I find this is a refreshing treat, especially after mowing the yard. Serve over ice. Garnish with mint, as your taste dictates.
