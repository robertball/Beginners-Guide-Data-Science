Title:
London Fruit Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 containers candied fruit (8 oz)
2 c raisins, chopped coarsley
3/4 c port, sherry or madeira wine
1/12 c all purpose flour, sifted
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 1/2 tsp apple pie spice (or 3/4 tsp allspice, and 3/4 tsp of cinnamon, 3/4 ground ginger)
6 tbsp butter, softened
1 c light brown sugar, firmly packed
2 eggs

Directions:
Conbine fruits, raisins and wine; cover, let stand overnight.
Sift flour, soda and spice, set aside. Beat butter, eggs and sugar until fluffy. Stir in flour and fruit mixtures. Spread batter in a parchment lined 15"x10"x1" jelly roll pan.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 35 mins. Cool on rack; cut into bars. With green, red icing decorate with criss cross lines and top with a star sahpe in white, makeing it look like a present. Can use silver non parelles to add some sparkle.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Christmas fruit bars made with fruit soaked in wine, and decorated as presents.
