Title:
Serena's Guilty Mashers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 med potatoes, skinned, roughly cubed
1 large white onion, minced
4 scallions, finely sliced
4 cloves garlic, crushed
3 tbs fresh parsley, minced
2 tbs fresh basil, minced
1 package low fat cream cheese
4 tbs smart balance light spread
1 cup low fat sour cream
fresh black pepper
Salt
Garlic powder for xtra kick
onion powder for xtra flavor

Directions:
Prepare potatoes as directed.
Boil in a large pot until done through.
Soften cream cheese in a microwave safe bowl, until warm enough to stir.
Prepare vegetables and herbs as directed above
Place potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream and olive oil spread (smart balance light) in a large mixing bowl, whip on high speed with an electric hand mixer.
Add white onion, garlic, scallions, parsley,and basil to the potatoes, whip until just mixed. Season to taste with salt and pepper, you can add additional garlic powder and onion powder to the mix to increase the flavor. Enjoy!

My family loves my mashed potatoes! These are always such a big hit at family get togethers. Very flavorful.
