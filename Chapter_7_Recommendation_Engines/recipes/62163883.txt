Title:
Merrick's favorite Turkey n' Spinach Roll Up Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole wheat tortilla
3 slices turkey breast
1 slice cheese
1/2 cup baby spinach leaves
1 tb ranch dressing

Directions:
On the wheat tortilla, place the cheese, turkey, and baby spinach leaves.
Drizzle with ranch dressing
Roll - enjoy!
Helpful tip for packing it in your lunch:  Since it's too big to put in a sandwich baggie, we wrap ours in wax paper.

Submitter: 
VenturaMama77
Description: 
A super healthy roll up for your lunch - my son loves spinach and this is a great way to make his lunches tastey and nutritious!
