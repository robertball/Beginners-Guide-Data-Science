Title:
Salad with Asparagus and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz. baby greens
1/2 cucumber, sliced
1 1/2 cup broccoli florets
8 oz. asparagus, cut into 2 inch pieces
4 tablespoons flax oil
4 tablespoons apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Steam broccoli and asparagus until crisp tender.  Let cool for a few minutes.  Toss with greens, cucumber slices, oil, and vinegar.

Submitter: 
KIMMORAES
Description: 
Salad of baby greens, cucumber, steamed broccoli, and steamed asparagus, flavored with flax oil and cider vinegar
