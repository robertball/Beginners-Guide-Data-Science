Title:
Spicy Jalapeno Chicken Sausage Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz pkg al fresco Spicy Jalapeno Chicken Sausage, sliced into small pieces
6 10-inch flour tortillas
4 ounces Garden vegetable cream cheese
1 cup Frozen sweet corn and black bean salad or frozen corn, thawed
4 ounces Smoked gouda, grated
1 jar Prepared salsa

Directions:
Place tortilla on sheet of wax paper or clean work surface
Divide cream cheese equally and spread on entire surface of each tortilla
On one side of tortilla, place chicken sausage pieces and sprinkle corn in equal amounts
Sprinkle with gouda
Fold the quesadillas over to cover filling completely
In large frypan or griddle over medium-high heat, place tortillas (one or two at a time) and cook until brown spots form, about 3 minutes
Turn and cook the other side
Remove to cutting board and cut into wedges
Serve with salsa and sour cream
