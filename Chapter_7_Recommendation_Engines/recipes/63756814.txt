Title:
Mexican Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 (15 ounce) can ranch-style beans
1 (10 ounce) can Rotel diced tomatoes with chilies
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons chili powder
1/2 cups finely chopped onions
1 Tsp Cumin
1/4 tsp Basil
1 cup frozen Corn
1 small can Green Chilies
1 smal can sliced black olives
1/2 cup salsa
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brown the ground beef with chopped onions, in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add taco seasoning following package directions. In a seperate bowl add chili powder, beans, tomatoes, sour cream, corn, cumin, basil and soup; mix well; pour into hamburger mixture and heat thoroughly.
Line a 13x9 inch dish with tortillas. Then make a layer with the meat mixture. Make another row of tortillas, then finish off with rest of meat mixture.  Top with salsa, grated cheese, and black olives.
Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes.
