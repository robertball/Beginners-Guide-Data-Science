Title:
Pucks Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/898343.jpg
Ingredients:
Puck’s Pumpkin Bread
Ingredients
•	1 ¼ cup whole wheat flour
•	2 cups all-purpose flour
•	¼ cup ground flax seed (optional)
•	2 teaspoons baking soda
•	1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
•	1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
•	2 cups solid packed pumpkin puree
•	1 cup vegetable oil blend
•	1  ½ teaspoons salt
•	2 cups white sugar
•	2/3 cup water
•	4 eggs
•	 ½ cup chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Directions:
Directions
1.	Grease and flour three 7 x 3 inch pans. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2.	Measure flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and spices into a large bowl. Stir to blend. Add pumpkin, water, salad oil, eggs, and nuts. Beat until well combined. Pour batter into prepared pans.
3.	Bake for approximately 1 hour.

Description: 
A slightly healthier version of my fabulous pumpkin bread. I have to make 15-20 loaved every fall for people who love it!
