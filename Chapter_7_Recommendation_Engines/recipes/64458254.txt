Title:
Squash, zucchini, and tomato casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Zucchini, sliced thin
3 Yellow Squash, sliced thin
3 small tomatoes, sliced thin
2 T McCormick Montreal Chicken seasoning, to taste
2 T Butter
6 oz Feta cheese
4 oz Blue cheese
1 c Sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Layer vegetables in 9 x 13 baking dish.  Sprinkle each layer with Montreal chicken seasoning and a few pats of butter. Top with the cheeses.  Bake covered dish at 375 for 35-40 min.

Submitter: 
Kisha McDaniel Logsdon
Description: 
This is a perfect recipe to use up all of the fresh veggies out of the garden.
