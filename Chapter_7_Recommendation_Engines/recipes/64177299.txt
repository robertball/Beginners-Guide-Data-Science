Title:
vintage Restaurant Spaetzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1 lb. ground pork sausage
1 cup fresh mushrooms, chopped
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup diced red pepper
salt/pepper
2 cups heavy cream
1 cup crumbled blue cheese
spaetzle:
4 cups all purpose flour
1 1/2 tea. salt
1 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ground white pepper
8 eggs
1 cup milk

Directions:
sauce: brown sausage, remove reserving 1 1/2 tsp. drippings.  Saute mushrooms and next 4 ingredients about 7 min. or until vegtables are tender. stir in sausage and cream, bring to a boil. reduce heat and simmer stirring often, about 6 min. or until thickened. remove from heat and stir in cheese. keep warm.
Spaetzle: whisk together flour and next 3 ingrediants in a large bowl. make well in the center. in another bowl whisk the eggs with the milk. add to dry mixture and stir til moistnend. bring a large stockpot half full of salted water to a boil, place a large-holed metal colander on top of stockpot..colander should not touch water. press half of the dough through the holes of the colander into the boiling water using the back of a spoon. cook 1 min. or until they float.
