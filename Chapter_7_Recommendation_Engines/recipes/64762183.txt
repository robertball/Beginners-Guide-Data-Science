Title:
Traditional German Marble Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb butter softened
7 oz white sugar
1 tsp vanilla
5 eggs
pinch of salt
3 tbsp rum
2 1/2cup flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup milk for all batter
2 tbsp of cocoa
3 tables spoons milk for cocoa batter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 365
In a large bowl, beat, butter, sugar, vanilla. Beat eggs in batter, 1 at a time
and add rum.
In a separate bowl, blend flour and baking powder
Add to sugar mix and blend.
Remove about 1/3 of batter and put in bowl. Add cocoa and milk and blend
In bundt pan that has been sprayed, pour 1/2 of white batter and smooth. Add the chocolate batter and smooth.
Take a spoon and swirl. Add the remaining white batter and swirl again.
Bake in oven ffor 50 minutes. remove and test with toothpick.
Let cake cool for 10 minutes, then place on cake rack
Either sprinkle with icing sugar or make sugar glaze
