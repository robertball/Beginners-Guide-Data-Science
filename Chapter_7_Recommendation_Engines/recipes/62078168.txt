Title:
Marmalade Glazed Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-Pork loin roast (2.5 to 3 lbs.)
1/2 cup orange marmalade
1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 lb. parsnips, peeled and cut into 1 inch wedges
2 mediun red onions, peeled and cut into 1 inch wedges
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat oven to 375 degrees. Place pork roast in center of large roasting pan and season liberally with salt and pepper.
2. Spread half of marmalade on pork. Combine remaining marmalade and rosemary in large bowl. Add orange juice and olive oil to mixture. Toss parsnips and onions with 2 tablespoons of mixture and season with salt and pepper. Arrange vegetables around pork. Roast until thermometer reads 120 degrees in the thickest part of roast (30-45 minutes). Pour remaining mixture over pork. Increase oven temperature to 450 degrees and roast until temperature reads 140 degrees (15-20 minutes).
3. Transfer roast to cutting board and tent with foil. Toss vegetables and juices and redistribute evenly over pan bottom. Roast until juices thicken and vegetables carmelize (about 10 minutes). Slice pork roast and serve with vegetables covering both with pan juice.

Submitter: 
Ryan Morgan
Description: 
A simple two ingredient glaze creates a browned crust on the pork and flavours the vegetables too.
