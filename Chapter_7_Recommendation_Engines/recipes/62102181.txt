Title:
Grandpa Schmidts Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250 ml  margerine
187 ml  white sugar
125 ml  brown sugar
1     egg
7.5 ml  vanilla
500 ml  flour
7.5 ml  baking powder
5 ml  baking soda
125 ml  chocolate chips
62  ml  walnuts or coconut (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Cream margarine and sugar and add beaten egg and vanilla, mix well.
Add dry ingredients and chocolate chips, mix well.
Drop by teaspoonful on baking sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes.
