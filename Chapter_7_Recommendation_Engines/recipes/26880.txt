Title:
Artichoke-Chile Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986643.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided
3/4 cup sour cream
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 (4 ounce) can ORTEGA® Diced Green Chiles
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
bread cubes

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350 degrees F.
COMBINE artichokes, 3/4 cup cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise, chiles and garlic in medium bowl; stir well.  Spoon into 1-quart casserole dish.  Sprinkle with remaining  cheese.
BAKE for 25 to 30 minutes or until bubbly.  Serve with bread cubes, pita wedges  or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
This creamy baked dip is packed with spicy, rich flavors. It's easy to make, and your guests won't stop dipping until the dish is empty!
