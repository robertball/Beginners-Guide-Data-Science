Title:
Alexander's Peppermint Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Ounces Unsweetened Chocolate
1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter
2 Eggs
1 Cup Sugar
1/2 Cup All-Purpose Flour
1 1/2 Cups Confectioners' Sugar
3 Tablespoons Unsalted Butter
1/4 Cup Heavy Cream
1/2-1 Teaspoon Peppermint Extract Depending On Taste.
1/4 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1/3 Cup Heavy Cream
12 Ounces Chocolate Chips
Crushed Peppermint Candies To Garnish

Directions:
1. Preheat Oven To 350 Degrees. Grease & Flour 9' Square Baking Dish.
2. Melt 2 Ounces Of Chocolate With 1/2 Butter. Stir Until Smooth. Cool.
3. Beat Eggs & Sugar. Blend In Cooled Chocolate & Flour. Spread In Pan; Bake 20-25 Minutes, Or Until A Tester Comes Out Clean. Cool Completely.
4. Combine Confectioners' Sugar, 3 Tablespoons Of Butter, 1/4 Cup Of Heavy Cream, And Extracts; Beat Until Smooth. Spread On Cooled Brownies. Chill Until Firm.
5. Bring 1/3 Cup Of Heavy Cream To A Simmer. Remove From Heat. Stir In Chocolate Chips; Stir Until Melted. Spread Quickly Over Peppermint Layer. Sprinkle With Crushed Peppermints. Chill.
