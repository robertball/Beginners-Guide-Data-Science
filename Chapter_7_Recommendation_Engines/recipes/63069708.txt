Title:
Easy, Grilled Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773714.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 Zucchinis
3-4 Tablespoons Butter, Melted
Desired Seasonings (Salt, Pepper, Garlic Salt, Etc.)

Directions:
Slice 1-2 zucchini squash into 1/2 inch rounds.
Melt 3-4 tablespoons butter in a heat safe bowl with a lid.
Add desired seasonings such as garlic salt, and pepper to your melted butter.
Add sliced zucchini to the melted butter, cover with lid, and toss in seasoned butter until coated.
Pre-heat large skillet on medium-high heat. Once warmed, add zucchini one-by-one to the hot skillet. (Leave remaining butter)
Sear for 4-5 minutes, until golden brown.
Baste the tops of the squash with the remaining butter.
Flip the zucchini and sear on medium-high heat for another 4-5 minutes, until golden brown.
Let cool for at least 5 minutes to allow the inside to complete cooking.

Submitter: 
Lisa Lou
Description: 
A very simple, skillet seared zucchini squash; just as delicious as fresh off of the hot charcoal grill!
