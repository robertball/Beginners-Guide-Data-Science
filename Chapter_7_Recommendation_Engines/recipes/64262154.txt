Title:
Cannoli Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1826979.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz Whole Milk Ricotta Cheese
1 ½ C Confectioners’ Sugar
1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
¼ tsp Nutmeg
¼ Mini Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, whip at medium speed until cheese is smooth and blended. Last, fold in, with a
spatula, mini chocolate chips.
Either pipe into cannoli shells or serve as a dip with cannoli chips.
