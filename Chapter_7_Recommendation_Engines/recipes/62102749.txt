Title:
Chocolate Butterscotch Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Butter
1/4 C. Crisco
1 & 1/4 C. White Sugar
2 T. Milk
1 T. Vanilla
1 egg
1 & 3/4 C. Flour
1 t. salt
3/4 t. baking soda
1 C. Coconut
1 bag of chocolate chips
1/2 bag of butterscotch chips

Directions:
In a mixer cream butter, shortening, and sugar.
Add vanilla and egg, mix well.
Add flour, salt, and baking soda, mix well.
Add chips and coconut and stir.
Drop by the spoonful onto a parchment lined baking sheet. Bake @ 375 for 10-12 minutes. Cookies may look underdone but should be just slightly browned on the edges.
Remove and let sit for a minute or two. Place on racks to cool. Enjoy.

Description: 
Slightly crispy on the outside, chewy goodness on the inside!
