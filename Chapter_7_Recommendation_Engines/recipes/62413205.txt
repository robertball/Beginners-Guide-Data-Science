Title:
Strawberry whipped cream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint fresh strawberries
2 cups heavy whipping cream
2/3 cup sugar
1 tea vanilla extract
*optional 1 tsp merangue powder

Directions:
Put strawberries in a food processor until they are a thick liquid. Put strawberries in the fridge.  With a chilled bowl and mixer attachments beat 2 cups of whipped cream with the sugar, vanilla if you want, the merangue powder  until stiff peaks form.  Fold strawberries in the whipped cream.  Enjoy!
