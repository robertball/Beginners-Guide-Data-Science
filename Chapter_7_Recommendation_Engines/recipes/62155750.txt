Title:
Orange Cream jello mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 6oz. pkgs of orange jello
2 cups boiling water
2 cups orange soda
2 pkgs dream whip, whipped and prepared as box states
2- 11oz cans of mandarin oranges, drained
1- 20oz can crushed pineapple, undrained

Directions:
Mix together jello, boiling water and soda
stick in fridge to let cool for 20-30 min.
prepare dream whip as box indicates
once jello is cooled off(but not firm yet) wisk in the whipped topping, mandarin oranges, and crushed pineapple.
Pour into a bunt pan and refridgerate until firm.

Submitter: 
tigershoes
Description: 
This is an awesome gelatin mold that truely tastes like a creamsicle.  This is a family favorite that we always have at holidays, Delicious!!
