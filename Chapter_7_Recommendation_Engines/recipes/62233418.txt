Title:
Ham Balls w/ Pineapple Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (20 oz.) sliced pineapple
3 cups ground ham (about 1 lb.)
1/2 cup ground pork (not sausage)
1/3 cup dry bread crumbs (unseasoned)
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 - 3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 &#0176;
Drain pineapple, reserving juices.
Arrange pineapple slices in 9 X 13 baking dish.
Combine ham, pork, bread crumbs and eggs, mixing thoroughly.
Form into 8 balls for meal size servings, or smaller for appetizer size servings.
Place on pineapple slices.
Mix together the juice from the pineapple, brown sugar, vinegar and mustard in a small, heavy saucepan.
Bring to a boil and pour over the ham balls.
Bake, uncovered for 1 hour for larger balls or 30 minutes for smaller balls, basting ocassionally if desired.
Serve on the baked pineapple slices as an entree.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
These cute balls are basically ham loaf and can be baked in a loaf pan if you don't want to form them into balls.
