Title:
Turkey Meatballs in Tangy Cranberry Sauce  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987800.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 small onion, chopped
1 egg
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (8 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix turkey, bread crumbs, milk, salt, 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, the pepper, onion and egg.  Shape mixture into twenty 1 1/2-inch meatballs.
Heat oil in 10-inch skillet over medium heat.  Cook meatballs in oil about 10 minutes, turning occasionally, until brown.
Stir remaining ingredients into skillet with meatballs.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Simmer uncovered about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until meatballs are no longer pink in center.
