Title:
Sarah's Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can pumpkin puree
4 eggs
1 cup vegetable oil
1 snack container cinnamon applesauce
2/3 cup water
3 cups white sugar
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
3 snack packs raisins
2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together pumpkin puree, eggs, oil, water, applesauce and sugar until well blended.
In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves and ginger. Stir the dry ingredients into the pumpkin mixture until just blended.
Add raisins and walnuts.
Bake for about 60 minutes at 350 - turn half way.
