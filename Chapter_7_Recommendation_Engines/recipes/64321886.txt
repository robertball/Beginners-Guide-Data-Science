Title:
Apple Walnut Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
6 ounce(s) chicken, breast, boneless, skinless, roasted, cut into 1/2"â€“3/4" chunks
1/2 cup(s) celery, chopped
3/4 cup(s) apple(s), chopped
2 ounce(s) nuts, walnuts chopped
1 tablespoon raisins
1/3 cup(s) dressing, Italian, low-sugar
1 head(s) lettuce, bibb, chopped

Directions:
In a medium bowl, gently stir together the chicken, celery, apple, walnuts, and raisins. Pour the dressing over the mixture and toss gently to coat.
Serve on a bed of Bibb lettuce.
