Title:
Strawberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Head of Romaine Lettuce or Bag of Spinach
1 pint fresh Strawberries
1/2 c almonds (opt)
1/4 c sugar
Dressing:
1/2 c mayo (low fat is fine)
1/3 c sugar
1/2 T. Sour Cream (low fat is fine)
1 1/2 T vinegar
1 T poppy seeds
1/4 c milk

Directions:
Wash & dry lettuce/spinach.  Slice strawberries and to spinach.
Heat almonds & 1/4 c sugar in non-stick fry pan.  When sugar melts, shake to coat almonds.  Remove from heat immediately.  Spread out to cool on cookie sheet.  When cool, add to spinach and strawberries.
Dressing:  Mix all and refrigerate.  Serve on side with salad.
