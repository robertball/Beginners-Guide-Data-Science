Title:
Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup oil
4 eggs
1 15 oz can pumpkin
2 cups sugar
Frosting:
4 1/2 oz cream cheese
3 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Combine all ingredients for bars in a kitchen mixer for 2 minutes.
Grease a 15x20 baking pan with 1" sides.
Bake for 20 minutes; let cool.
Prepare frosting in mixer, and once bars are cooled, frost the bars.

Submitter:

Description: 
These pumpkin bars will melt in your mouth and the sweet cream cheese frosting is gauranteed to satisfy your sugar cravings and get you in the fall spirit.
