Title:
Bill's Stewed Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
2 onions, chopped
2 stalk celery, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1 medium head cabbage, cut into squares
2 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
1/4 cup chicken bullion
1 tsp dried thyme
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
Add onions, celery, carrots, and garlic and saute for 3 to 5 minutes, or until translucent.
Stir in cabbage, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 15 minutes.
Pour in tomatoes and bullion.
Season with thyme, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper to taste.
Cover pan and cook over medium heat for 30 to 40 minutes, or until cabbage is tender.
