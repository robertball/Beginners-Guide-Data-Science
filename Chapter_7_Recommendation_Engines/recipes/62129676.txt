Title:
Ginger Beer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Ginger Roots
4 TBS Mascarpone Sugar
2 Large Lemons
Fresh Mint
Ice Cubes
1 LTR Sparkling Mineral Water

Directions:
Skin the ginger roots and shred them finely in a bowl.
Grate the skin of the lemon and add to the shredded ginger.
Add sugar and lemon juice to the ginger mixture and mix with a spoon.
In a large jug, add ice and mint leaves.
Strain the ginger mixture through a sieve, directly into the jug.

Submitter: 
Celestial
Description: 
A refreshing instant summer drink! You can enjoy the drink as soon as you make it.
