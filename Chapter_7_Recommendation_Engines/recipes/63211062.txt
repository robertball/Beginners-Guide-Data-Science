Title:
Blackened Salmon Taco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp brown sugar
2 tsp ancho chile powder
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cayenne
1 lb Salmon
Vegetable Oil
Corn tortillas
1 mango
1 avocado
sour cream
cilantro

Directions:
Mix brown sugar, ancho chile powder, paprika, salt and cayenne and rub on salmon fillet. Cook in an oiled skillet. Chop and serve in tortillas with chopped mango, avocado, sour cream, and cilantro
