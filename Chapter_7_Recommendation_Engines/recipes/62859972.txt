Title:
Mmmm Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup canola oil
1/4 cup almonds
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1/4 cup nutritional yeast
2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried cilantro

Directions:
Easily double it and keep in fridge for 7-10 days
In a high speed blender, combine oil, almonds, water and chickpeas.  Blend until relatively smooth.
Add remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.
Transfer to a bowl/container until serving

Submitter: 
Mary Rantanen
Description: 
Cheesy creamy vegan sauce for anything and everything
