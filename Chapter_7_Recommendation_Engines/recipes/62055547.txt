Title:
Cheesecake in a cake pan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CHEESECAKE
1 1/2 pkgs. graham crackers, crushed
1 stick melted butter
2 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs
TOPPING:
1 pt. sour cream
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 c. sugar

Directions:
Mix together graham crackers and butter and press in 13 x 9 pan. Beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add 1/2 cup sugar and vanilla, then blend in one egg at a time. Pour over crust and bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes. Stir together sour cream, 1/4 cup sugar and vanilla and spread over top. Return to oven for 10 more minutes.
Cool in the refrigerator for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Eric Burton
Description: 
This cheesecake is made in the way of others, but this time its made in a 13x9 pan instead of the spring form which sometimes gets messy.
