Title:
Frozen Pineapple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/704308.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lemon
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice
1 (16 ounce) package frozen whipped topping, thawed
2 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crusts

Directions:
Mix condensed milk and non-dairy whipped  topping together.
Add the juice of one lemon and the pineapple to this creamy blend.
Pour pineapple blend into 2 graham cracker crusts.  Freeze overnight.  Take pies out of freezer 30 to 45 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Suzanne Stull
Description: 
This is a good recipe to make ahead for company on hot, summer days. You may use lowfat or fat-free ingredients if you like.
