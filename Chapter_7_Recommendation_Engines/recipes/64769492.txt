Title:
Uncle Bob's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar medium salsa
1 jar grape jelly
1 can cranberry sauce
small meatballs

Directions:
Mix salsa, jelly, and cranberry sauce together.
Add meatballs into mix.
Cook in crockpot for 3 hours or until meatballs are tender.
