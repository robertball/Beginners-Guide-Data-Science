Title:
Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raw oats
3 egg whites
1/4 cup water
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend for 30 seconds.
Pour onto a hot grill and cook like a normal pancake.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
An additional option is to add some frozen fruit after the batter is blended.
