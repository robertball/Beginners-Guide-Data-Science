Title:
Cool Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979831.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium cucumber, quartered and sliced
1 medium tomato, chopped
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1/3 cup chopped sweet onion
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar or cider vinegar
3/4 teaspoon dill weed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, tomato, green pepper and onion. in a small bowl, combine lime juice, vinegar, dill, salt and pepper. Pour over cucumber mixture; toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 15 minutes. Serve with a slotted spoon.

Submitter: 
Joy  Sauers
Description: 
Whenever I pack a picnic, this salad comes along. It's great with grilled entrees or sandwiches. In summer and fall, it's among my most requested side dishes. It keeps well in the refrigerator, so you can make it ahead of time.
