Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2463851.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups milk
1/4 cups white vinegar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
1 tablespoon lemon emulsion or extract

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine milk and vinegar. Set aside.
Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Whisk eggs and oil into the "soured" milk mixture.
Slowly pour the "soured" mixture in the dry ingredients while stirring. Keep stirring until smooth. Add lemon emulsion.
On an electric griddle or non-stick frying pan over med-high heat, scoop spoonfuls of batter onto the hot surface. Flip when golden brown, same on other side.
While cooling, dust with powdered sugar.
