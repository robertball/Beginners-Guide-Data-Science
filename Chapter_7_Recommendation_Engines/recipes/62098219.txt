Title:
Fake-out Seafood Fettucine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fettucine
1 (8 oz) package imitation flake-style crab
1 (8 oz) package imitation flake-style lobster
3 (1.25 oz) envelopes creamy garlic alfredo sauce mix
1 stick butter
3 cups milk

Directions:
Boil pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Wisk in sauce mix. Stir in milk. Add crab and lobster.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low. Stirring occasionally, cook until thickened.
Drain pasta. Return to pot. Pour sauce over pasta and toss together.

Submitter: 
sweetaddiction
Description: 
This is a super easy recipe that is an all-time family favorite. Sometimes, if I have broccoli on hand, I will add it to the pasta pot as well. This is such a quick fix that I always keep the ingredients on hand so I can throw it together in a pinch.
