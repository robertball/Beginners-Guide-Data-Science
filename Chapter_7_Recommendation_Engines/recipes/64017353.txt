Title:
Chicken Gnocchi Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T (1/2 stick) butter
1 T extra virgin olive oil
1T onion powder
1/2 c finely diced celery
1/4 t garlic powder
1/4 c all-purpose flour
1 quart half-and-half
2 (14-ounce) cans chicken broth (if you enjoy a thicker soup, use 1 can)
1/2 t Salt
1/2 t dried thyme
1/2 t dried parsley flakes
1/4 t ground nutmeg
1 c finely shredded carrots
1 c coarsely chopped fresh spinach leaves
1 c diced or shredded cooked chicken breast
1 (16-ounce/500g) package gnocchi

Directions:
Melt the butter into the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion powder, celery, and garlic powder and whisk in the flour and cook until a nice rue is formed. Whisk in the half-and-half. Stir in 1/2 teaspoon salt, the thyme, parsley, nutmeg, and simmer until well blended and heated through. Whisk in the chicken broth, carrots, and chicken. Bring to a boil, add gnocchi, and cook per box instructions. Add spinach, heat until reduced, and serve.
