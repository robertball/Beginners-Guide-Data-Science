Title:
Mama's Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Sugar
2 c. Buttermilk
2 c. Self Rising Flour
1 lg Can of Peaches with Heavy Syrup
2 T. Flour
3/4 c. Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Put 13x9 baking dish with butter into oven to melt butter.
Mix sugar, buttermilk and flour. Pour into baking dish over melted butter. DO NOT MIX.
Pour peaches in medium saucepan and add 2 T. Flour.  Stir well and heat on medium heat until thickened.
Pour Peaches over dough reserving enough syrup to pour over baked cobbler. Sprinkle with a bit of sugar if you like.
Bake for 35 - 40 minutes until brown.
Remove from oven and pour syrup over cobbler.
Serve immediately if desired.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by my mother.  It makes the best peach cobbler I have ever had with a soft light texture.  Tastes great with a big scoop of vanilla ice cream on top!
