Title:
Crock Pot Ratatouile Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4581722.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Italian Turkey Sausage
2 pkgs. 8 oz. sliced mushrooms
2 sm. eggplants, diced
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. dried oregano, divided
1 tsp. salt, divided
1 tsp. black pepper, divided
4 tomatoes, diced
2 tbs. tomato paste
2 tbs. fresh chopped basil
2 tsp. fresh lemon juice
fresh grated parmesan

Directions:
1. Shape sausage in 1-inch meatballs. Brown in large skillet over medium heat.
2. Place half of the meatballs in a 5-quart slow-cooker. Add half of the mushrooms, eggplant, and zucchini. Top with onion and garlic, and half of the oregano, salt, and pepper.
3. Layer the remaining meatballs, mushrooms, eggplant, zucchini, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cover; cook on LOW for 6-7 hours.
4. Stir in diced tomatoes and tomato paste. Cover; cook on LOW 15 minutes.
Stir in basil and lemon juice.
5. Top with fresh grated parmesan and serve with your choice of carb.

Submitter: 
Amber LeAnn Lee
Description: 
Traditional French vegetable soup, but with turkey meatballs for added protein. Vegetarians nix the meat.

Great on pasta, beside white rice, or my favorite,  buttered and toasted baguette slices!
