Title:
All Day Vegetarian Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium sized onion minced
1 rib of celery chopped
1 large carrot chopped
2 minced garlic cloves
4 oz. green beans
1 15.5 oz chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
1 medium zucchini or yellow squash diced
1 14.5 oz can of tomatoes undrained
6 cups of vegetable stock
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup raw or 1 cup cooked ditalini or other small pasta
1/4 cup pesto.

Directions:
Pour oil in the bottom of a 4 or 6 quart slow cooker and cook on high while you assemble the rest of the ingredients.
Add the green beans, chickpeas, tomatoes, zucchini, and stock to the slow cooker. Cook on low for 7-8 hours.
If using raw pasta add to the slow cooker 1 hour before ready to serve. If using dried pasta cook seperately and add with pesto right before serving.
