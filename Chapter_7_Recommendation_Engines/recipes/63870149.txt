Title:
Buttermilk Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks of margarine
1 cup of sugar
2 eggs
1 cup of buttermilk
1 teaspoon of vanilla
4 cups of flour
2 teaspoons of baking powder
1 teaspoon of baking soda

Directions:
Cream together margarine, sugar and eggs
Add buttermilk, and vanilla
Blend flour, baking powder and baking soda and add to above
Drop on greased cookie sheet, by teaspoon
Mix powder sugar, milk and vanilla to desired thickness, dip cookies in icing while still warm and drain on rack over a bowl
Bake in 350 degree oven
