Title:
Fiesta Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Water
1/3 cups Teriyaki Sauce
2 tablespoons Lime Juice
2 teaspoons Minced Garlic
1 teaspoon Liquid Barbeque Smoke
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/4 teaspoon Ground Ginger
3-4 Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup Mayo
1/4 cup Sour Cream
1 tablespoon Milk
2 teaspoon Minced Tomato
1 1/2 teaspoon Minced Jalapeno Peppers canned
1 teaspoon Minced Onion
1/4 teaspoon Parsley
1/4 teaspoon Hot Sauce
1/8 teaspoon Paprika
1/8 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
1/8 teaspoon Cumin
1/8 teaspoon Chili Powder
1/8 teaspoon  Salt
1 dash Black Pepper
1 cup Mexican Cheese

Directions:
Combine first 8 ingredients in medium tuberware and marinade for 2-6 hours.
Combine next 14 ingredients in medium bowl, cover, and chill until needed (mexi-ranch dressing)
Grill the marinated chicken breasts for 5-8 minutes per side until cooked through.
Arrange chicken in baking pan and brush with mexi-ranch dressing and sprinkle with cheese.
Broil chicken for 2-3 minutes or just until the cheese has melted.
Serve over crushed tortilla chips with spanish rice and refried beans.

Submitter: 
richgator
Description: 
A stab at the popular Applebee's Fiesta Lime Chicken.  Great with Mexican Rice and Refried Beans.
