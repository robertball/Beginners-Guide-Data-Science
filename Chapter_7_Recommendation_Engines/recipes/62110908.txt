Title:
supersonic salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 cherry tomatoes
4 roma tomatoes
4 jalapeno peppers
3 banana peppers
1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 tsp lemon zest
1/3 cup cilantro
1 tsp onion
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp cayenne pepper
8oz tomato sauce

Directions:
in large blender combine tomatoes, jalapenos and banana peppers pulsate till smooth
add bell pepper, lemon, cilantro, salt, black and cayenne pepper and tomato sauce blend till smooth
refrigerate till serving
