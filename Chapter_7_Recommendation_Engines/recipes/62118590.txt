Title:
Fun Size Frittatas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butter (for greasing tin)
1 tablespoon oil
1 tomato, diced
1/2 cup diced cooked ham (thick sliced deli works fine)
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon dried parsely
9 eggs
3/4 cup chedder cheese
salt
pepper
2 tablespoons water, milk, or cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Coat each cup of a 6 cup large muffin tin with butter. (6-8 ounce ramekins also work).
In a skillet heat oil over medium heat. Add tomato and ham with onion powder and parsely and saute until tomato is warmed through and the juice has evaporated.
In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, and cheese with salt and pepper to taste.
Divide ham mixture into each of the 6 cups. Cover with egg mixture until about 3/4 of the way full.
Place muffin tin in a larger pan filled with hot water. Bake for 25 minutes or until set. Cover with foil if necessary.

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
These are perfect for lunch boxes! Kids love the size, and parents love the nutrition. Pack these little frittatas with potato sticks, bread and jam, and fruit. There you have a fun spin on breakfast for lunch! If your kids don't like veggies, just have the meat and cheese. Everything, including spices and type of cheese, is customizable.
