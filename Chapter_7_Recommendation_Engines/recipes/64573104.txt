Title:
Frozen Derby Mint Juleps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3564586.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz bourbon
20 mint leaves
2 oz simple syrup
2 1/2 oz lemon juice
4 C ice
Mint for garnish

Directions:
Place bourbon, mint leaves, simple syrup, and lemon juice in blender and blend on high until combined.  Add ice to blender and blend until slushy.  Serve with mint garnish if desired.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
Perfect for Derby Day or any day spent lounging in the sun, these cool refreshing drinks are dangerously delicious!
