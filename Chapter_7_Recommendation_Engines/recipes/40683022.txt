Title:
Icebox Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter
3 eggs
4 t. baking powder
1/2 T. vanilla
2 c. sugar
1/3 c. milk
1/2 T. lemon extract
Flour

Directions:
Cream butter.  Add 1 1/2 c. sugar.  Beat eggs, add remaining sugar, combine.  Add milk, flour and baking powder, enough flour to make a stiff dough.  Pack in cans or round plastic containiers, let stand overnight in refrigerator.  Cut into thin slices and bake. 350 until light brown.

Submitter: 
PacNWCook
Description: 
A dough you make and refrigerate overnight, slice and bake.
