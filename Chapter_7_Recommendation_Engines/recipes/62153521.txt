Title:
Turkey and Couscous stuffed peppers with Feta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 peppers, assorted colors
2 T olive oil
1 lb. ground turkey
1 box roasted garlic and olive oil couscous
3 tomatoes, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped or put in garlic press
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
1 T cumin or to taste
Salt and pepper
1/4 cup of water
reduced fat or regular crumbled feta cheese
sauce
1 small can tomato sauce
few shakes of worstershire sauce
few shakes of hot sauce
5 T ketchup
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Heat oil in a nonstick pan and brown turkey.
Add chopped tomatoes, onion and garlic.
Season with cumin and salt and pepper and couscous flavor packet.
Add coucous, stir and cover.
Reduce heat and cook for 5-10 minutes.
Meanwhile, mix all the sauce ingredients in a bowl
Take tops off peppers and clean out membrane and seeds.
Blanche in boiling water for 3-4 minutes and cool under cold water. Shake to dry and sprink salt and pepper inside peppers.
Take half of the sauce and add to the couscous mixture.
Sprinkle the inside of the peppers with a little bit of olive oil and then salt and pepper.
Put a small amount of feta in the bottom of each pepper, fill halfway with the couscous mixture, add more feta and then fill to the top with the turkey couscous mixture.
Cover the top with crumbled feta and spoon remaining sauce on top of the peppers.
Place peppers in a overproof dish and cook on 350 for approx 20 minutes.
