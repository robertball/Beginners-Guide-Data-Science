Title:
Sundried Tomato, Garlic and Basil Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
8 oz goat cheese, softened
2-3 Sundried tomatoes in oil
2 cloves garlic, roasted
6-8 basil leaves

Directions:
Add all the ingredients to a food processor.
Pulse until cheeses are blended and the tomatoes, garlic and basil are finely minced.
Place in a bowl or container, cover and refrigerate at least one hour to allow the flavors to marinate.
Serve with crackers, bread or vegetables.

Submitter: 
Miss B
Description: 
This spread is a delicious combination of creamy cheese and savory tomatoes and garlic. It was a favorite at a recent potluck lunch!
