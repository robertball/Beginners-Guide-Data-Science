Title:
Cranberry Jezebel Sauce Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2/3 cup sugar
2/3  cup dark brown sugar
1 (10 ounces) package fresh cranberries
2 tablespoons bottled horseradish, drained
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Directions:
Bring water a boil, stir in sugars until dissolved.
Add the cranberries. Return to boil and cook for 10 minutes.
Add the horseradish and mustard.
Chill.

Submitter: 
Behr
Description: 
Spread over cream cheese as an appetizer.
