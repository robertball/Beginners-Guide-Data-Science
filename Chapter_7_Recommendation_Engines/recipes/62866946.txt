Title:
Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19x14x20 inch) reynolds oven bag
2 tbsp. flour
1/4 c. packed brown sugar
1/4 c. vinegar
2 tbsp. soy sauce
1 (20 oz.) can pineapple chunks in juice, drained
1 medium green pepper, cut in rings
4 skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Put flour in oven bag. Add sugar, vinegar, and soy sauce to bag; blend well. Add pineapple and green pepper. Add chicken. Turn bag to coat chicken. Arrange evenly. Close bag. Cut 6 (1/2 inch) slits in bag. Bake until tender, 30 to 35 mins. serve with white rice
