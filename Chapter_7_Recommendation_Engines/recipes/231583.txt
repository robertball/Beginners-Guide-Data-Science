Title:
Family Style Italian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/973545.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 green pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 (15.5 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt'sÂ® Diced Tomatoes, undrained
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese, divided

Directions:
Brown meat in large skillet; drain. Add pepper and onions; cook 5 min., stirring frequently.
Add beans, tomatoes, seasonings and 1/4 cup cheese; stir. Simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
Served topped with remaining cheese.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
Description: 
Slowly simmered to blend the flavors, this beef chili with bell pepper, kidney beans, tomatoes, and spices is a hearty dish for a cool night.
