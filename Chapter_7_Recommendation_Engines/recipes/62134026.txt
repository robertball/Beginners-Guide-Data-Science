Title:
Chicken With Black Olive Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken, cut up or shredded
1 cup frozen corn, thawed and drained
1/4 or 1/2 cup onion, chopped
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 can (4 ounce) chopped green chilies
1 can (2.25 ounce) sliced black olives, undrained
5 ounces cheddar or jack cheese, grated
1 1/4 cup milk
3/4 cup Bisquick
3 eggs
sour cream, dollop (optional)

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 400 degrees.
Grease an 11x7 baking dish.
In a medium bowl mix the chicken, corn, onions, taco seasoning mix, chopped green chilies and undrained sliced black olives.  Sprinkle with your cheese.
In another medium bowl mix the remaining ingredients (milk, bisquick and eggs) until smooth, about 1 minute with mixer.
Pour over the chicken mix in your baking dish .
Bake for about 25 to 30 minutes or until a knife inserted comes out clean.  Cool for 5 minutes.
Dollop with sour cream.
