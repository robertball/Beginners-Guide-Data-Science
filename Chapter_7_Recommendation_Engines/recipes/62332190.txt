Title:
Bread Maker Cinnamon Raisin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
3 tablespoons butter, softened
1 egg
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3 cups flour
1 package bread maker yeast
3/4 cup raisins

Directions:
Add all ingredients, except raisins, to the bread maker pan in the order listed (or as directed in your bread maker instructions; mine is wet ingredients, dry ingredients, yeast).
Set bread maker to the regular setting (I use fruit & nut setting and 1-lb. loaf) and start.
Add raisins when the beeper sounds to add additional ingredients.
