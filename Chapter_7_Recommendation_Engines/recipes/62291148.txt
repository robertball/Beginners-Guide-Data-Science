Title:
Ritz Cracker Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 Ritz Crackers
1/2 c chopped walnuts
3 egg whites
1 c sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 c red raspberry perserves
1 small container cool whip

Directions:
Rolls crackers and nuts in ziplock bag to make crumbs.
Beat egg whites till foamy.
Add sugar continue to beat until peaks form.
Add salt and vanilla
Add baking powder to crumbs and fold into whites.
Spread into 8 inch pie plate (bottom buttered)
Bake 350 for 30 minutes
When cool...
Mix 1/2 cup red raspberry jelly with a small cool whip spread over torte.
Chill 3 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Lynda Wright
Description: 
This was a recipe that my mother gave me years ago.  It is easy and very different.  It sounds a bit odd but really is delicious.
