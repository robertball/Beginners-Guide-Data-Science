Title:
Grilled Marinated Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 split chicken breasts
1/8 - 1/4 C. garlic powder
2 C. cider vinegar
1 stick butter, melted
2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp Worchestershire sauce

Directions:
Wash and towel dry the chicken breasts.
Coat each piece generously & completely with garlic powder
Place chicken in either a zip lock bag or a marinate container
Cover the chicken with the cider vinegar & tightly close.
Refrigerate overnight, turning occasionally
Melt butter; add garlic powder and Worchestershire sauce
Put chicken on a preheated grill (medium heat), bone side down
When the chicken is turned, baste the bone side several times
Turn the skin-side up in the last 5 min. & baste with butter
Turn breast skin-side down for a couple of min. to crisp
Remove from grill and serve.
