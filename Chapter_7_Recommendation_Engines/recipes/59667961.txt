Title:
Velveeta Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups very hot water
1/2 cup instant milk, plus
1 tablespoon instant milk
1/2 (1/4 ounce) envelope unflavored gelatin (1 1/2 teaspoons)
1 1/2 lbs grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Directions
1Line a 8x4x2.
25" loaf pan with plastic.
3In a blender, put 1/2 cup of the water , 3 tablespoons of the milk, 1/2 teaspoon of the gelatin.
4Whip until gelatin is desolved.
5Quickly add 1/2 lb.
6of the cheddar to the hot mixture.
7Whip until blended.
8Pour into the prepared loaf pan.
9Repeat this twice, until all ingredients are used.
10Cover the pan with more plastic wrap and chill overnight before unmolding.
11Keep cold and slice as needed.
