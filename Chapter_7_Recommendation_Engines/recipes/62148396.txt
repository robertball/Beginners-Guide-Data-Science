Title:
Ethiopian Spicy Braised Vegetarian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz tomato sauce
¼ Cup paprika
¼ Cup dry red wine
1 Tablespoon fresh grated gingerroot
½teaspoon red pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1/8 teaspoon ground allspice
2 Large onions, chopped
4 Garlic cloves, minced
2 Tablespoons cooking oil
½ teaspoon ground tumeric
4 Small Cans or 1 large Can Frichick
¼ Cup dry red wine

Directions:
Make the red pepper sauce first
Combine tomato sauce, paprika, ¼ cup dry red wine, grated ginger root, red pepper, cardamom, nutmeg, cloves, cinnamon and allspice.
Set red-pepper sauce aside.
In a large skillet cook onion and garlic in hot oil until it is translucent.
Stir in red pepper sauce, turmeric, and 1-teaspoon salt.
Add frichick pieces to skillet.
Spoon onion mixture over frichick pieces.
Bring mixture to boiling; reduce head.
Cover; simmer about 3 minutes.
Stir in ¼ cup dry red wine.
Cook uncovered about 15 minutes.
Turn frichick pieces often.
Serves 4
