Title:
Ellie's Hot Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese (250 g pkg) softened
1 tbsp milk
2 tbsp chopped red onion
1/4 tsp salt
8 drops of Tabasco (or more to taste)
1 tsp horseradish (or more to taste)
1 can crab meat, drained
dash of pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and bake in oven at 325 for 15 mins or until warm throughout.  Serve right away or chill and reheat later.

Submitter: 
ellie
Description: 
Crab Meat Dip served warm with crackers or try with pretzels and breadsticks.  Surprise kick from the horseradish keeps your company asking.....what's in this?.
