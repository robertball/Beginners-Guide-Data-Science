Title:
Sausage & Dirty Rice Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bell peppers, tops cut off and seeded
Tops of Bell Peppers chopped and cooked with sausage
1 pound sage sausage
1/2 a chopped onion
1 Can Tex-Mex Tomatoes cooked with Rice
1 Box Zatarains Black Bean Rice (Prepared)
1 Box Zatarains Original Dirty Rice (Prepared)

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to boil. Place peppers in boiling water, and cook 5 minutes. Remove, and set aside to cool.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large skillet, cook sausage until almost brown. Drain fat. Stir in tops of peppers, 1/2 an onion.  Cook until soft, about 5 minutes. Season with garlic powder and pepper. Stir sausage mixture into cooking rice mixture. Cover, and simmer until rice is cooked, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat. Put Italian Cheese bland and Cheddar mixture into bottom of peppers, add meat, more cheese, meat and top with cheese. Cover with foil.
Bake in preheated oven about 30 minutes.
