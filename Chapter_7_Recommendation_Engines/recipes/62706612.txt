Title:
Sugar Free Chocolate Dip / Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2324395.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz Almond Milk
4 TBSP Butter
2 TBSP Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)
1.75 Cups Erythritol
11 scoops Kal Stevia (equivalent to 11 TBSP sugar)
8 oz Baking Chocolate

Directions:
Easy way is to just combine everything and microwave it. 1st on defrost then low temps less than or equal to 40% or the stove top method is below. Avoid boiling!
Heat on defrost in microwave: almond milk, butter, vanilla, and sweeteners in glass Pyrex pitcher until butter is melted and mixture is very smooth
Melt chocolate in a doubler boiler to a temperature of 115째F on simmer (2 of 10)
Remove chocolate from heat
Make sure almond milk, butter, vanilla extract, & sweetners mixture is 115째F or mixture won't blend right with chocolate
Mix in almond milk, butter, vanilla extract, & sweetners mixture
Cool chocolate to 83째F (May need water bath, but don't cool too fast)
Heat chocolate up to 89째F
Remove entire boiler from heat to keep the chocolate melted
