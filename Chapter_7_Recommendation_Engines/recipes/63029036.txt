Title:
Great Grandma's Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759639.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup self-rising flour
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 stick butter
1 30oz can of sliced peaches

Directions:
Discard half the juice from the can and pour the peaches and remaining juice into a 9" round casserole dish. Combine the dry ingredients and egg the pour on top of the peaches. Melt the stick of butter and drizzle on top. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
st1100pilot
Description: 
This was my great grandma's recipe from the 40's and earlier. Its so easy to make. I remember this being my favorite dessert from when I was a child. I love eating it cold from the fridge too.
