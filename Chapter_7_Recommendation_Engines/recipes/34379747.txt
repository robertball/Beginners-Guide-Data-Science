Title:
"cueco de gallo" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cucumber, diced
3 lemons, sliced in half
1 tomato, diced
4 sprigs cilantro, snipped
1 sm. onion, diced
Tabasco, to taste
12 dry fried corn tortillas (like tacos)

Directions:
Squeeze lemon juice over cucumbers and sprinkle Tabasco on top.  Mix and chill 1 & 1/2 hr.  Half way through chilling add onions. When ready, drain and add tomato and cilantro. Eat with tortillas.

Submitter: 
Hot Coco
Description: 
This is my DH mother's recipe.  Very refreshing on a hot summer day.
