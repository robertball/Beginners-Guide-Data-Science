Title:
Fruited Oatmeal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985777.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups Pillsbury BEST速 All Purpose Flour
1 1/2 cups quick-cooking rolled oats
1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup cold butter or margarine
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 (9 ounce) package NONE SUCH速 Condensed Mincemeat
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND速 Sweetened Condensed Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In large bowl, combine flour, oats, brown sugar and baking soda. Cut in butter until crumbly. Remove 3/4 cup of crumb mixture; stir in nuts and set aside. Firmly press remaining crumb mixture into a 13x9-inch baking pan.
Crumble mincemeat into medium saucepan; add sweetened condensed milk. Cook and stir over medium-low heat until thickened, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
Drop by small spoonfuls atop crust in pan; spread carefully to cover crust. Sprinkle with reserved crumb mixture; press down lightly.
Bake 40 minutes or until top is golden. Cool on wire rack. Cut into bars. Store loosely covered at room temperature.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND速
Description: 
These nicely spiced chewy oat bars are a great fall or winter treat.
