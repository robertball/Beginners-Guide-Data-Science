Title:
Easy White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Chicken. Boiled and cut into cubes
1 Med Onion
1 1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1 Tbs. Veg. oil
2 4oz cans Chopped chilies
2 15 1/2 oz cans Great Northern beans
1 14 1/2 oz can Chicken broth
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Oregano
1/2 tsp Pepper
1 cup Sour Cream
1/2 cup Whipping Cream

Directions:
In a large sauce pan saute Chicken, Onion, and Garlic Powder in oil till onions are soft and chicken is lightly browned.
Mix in Beans, Broth, Chilies, seasonings, Sour cream and Whipping cream.
Bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for thirty minutes to one hour. Stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and serve.
