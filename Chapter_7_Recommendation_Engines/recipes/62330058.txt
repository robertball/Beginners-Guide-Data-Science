Title:
Irish Cream Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
2 cups milk chocolate chips (divided)
2 cups white chocolate chips
2 (14 ounce) cans sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup butter (divided)
1 shot irish cream

Directions:
Line 9 x 13 pan with foil or parchment. Spray the foil or parchment with nonstick spray.
Place 2 cups semisweet chips, 1 cup milk chocolate chips, 1 can of condensed milk and 1/4 cup butter into a microwaveable bowl. Microwave on med-low until chips are melted (~3 minutes). Stir occasionally during cook time.
In a 2nd bowl, do the same with 2 cups white chips, 1 cup milk choc chips, 1 can condensed milk, 1/4 cup butter, AND the irish cream.
Pour contents of both bowls into pan. Swirl together with butter knife (swirls should remain visible. Refrigerate until set. Cut into 1 inch squares.
