Title:
Thyme-Garlic Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 garlic cloves - minced
1 lb. asparagus, trimmed
1 tsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Spread garlic in greased 11x7 baking dish.
Arrange asparagus evenly over garlic.
Drizzle with olive oil.
Combine thyme and salt, and sprinkle evenly over asparagus.
Bake for 15 min. or until asparagus is crisp tender, turning once.
