Title:
Big Ray's Florentine Chicken-Penne Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1067362.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box 16 oz. penne pasta, uncooked
8 oz. frozen chicken nuggets
1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
1 Tbsp Orgeneo
8 Tbsp Sun Dried Tomato Vinaigrette Dressing
1 cup chicken broth
1 8-oz pkg. neufchatel cheese, cubed
1 pkg  (10 oz.) frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
1 12 oz jar portabello mushrooms with roasted red peppers in olive oil, undrained
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
2 Tbsp grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375ÂºF. Wipe 11" x 13" baking dish with olive oil.
Cook pasta as directed on package.
Cut chicken nuggets in half.
Heat dressing in large skillet on medium heat. Add broth and neufchatel; cook 3 min. or until neufchatel is melted, stirring frequently. Stir in spices, spinach and mushrooms. Add chicken nuggets.
Drain pasta. Add to chicken mixture; mix lightly. Spoon half into casserole dish; top with 1/2 cup mozzarella. Repeat layers; sprinkle with Parmesan.
Bake 16 to 18 min. or until mozzarella is melted and casserole is heated through.
