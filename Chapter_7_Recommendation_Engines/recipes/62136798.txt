Title:
Vegetable Ribbons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large carrots, peeled into long ribbons
2 medium zucchini, peeled into long ribbons
1 red bell pepper, sliced into long, thin strips
1 yellow bell pepper, sliced into long thin strips
3 cups purple cabbage, shredded into large strips
1/4 cup green onions, thinly sliced
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
pepper to taste
olive oil or cooking spray

Directions:
In a large saucepan, heat oil or cooking spray over medium-high heat. Add vegetable ribbons. Saute the vegetables for 8-10 minutes or until limp and slightly translucent. Add green onions and cilantro and saute for another minute. Season with black pepper.

Submitter: 
RHOV
Description: 
This versatile vegetable saute has a mild flavor and a fancy, appealing presentation. All the colors of the rainbow are represented! The tastes of the different vegetables really shine through because there is no extra seasoning. It's easy to substitute your favorite vegetables and satisfy picky eaters.
