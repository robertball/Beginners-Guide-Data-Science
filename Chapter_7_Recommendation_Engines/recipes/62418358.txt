Title:
Capatini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ounce cold strong coffee
1 ounce vodka *
1/2 ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1/2 ounce Irish cream liqueur
a couple of coffee beans to garnish

Directions:
fill cocktail shaker with ice
pour the first four ingredients into the shaker
cover and shake vigorously to create foam
strain into chilled martini glass
Garnish with coffee beans
serve and repeat

Submitter: 
laughingmagpie
Description: 
Smooth and delicious, this is the perfect after dinner cocktail. I created this drink while working in a small neighborhood pub, I bet they wish I had left the recipe but I didn't. Shhhh!
