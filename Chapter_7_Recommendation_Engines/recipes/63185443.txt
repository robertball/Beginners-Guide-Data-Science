Title:
Italian Stewed Tomato Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/820089.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C.  All Purpose flour (unbleached)
1/4 tsp. Salt
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1 tsp. Ginger
1/2 tsp. Cloves
1/2 tsp. Nutmeg
1 C. Italian stewed tomatoes (juice included)
1 C. Grated carrots
2 eggs
1 C. Granulated sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
Grease and flour a 9" by 13" by 2" cake pan
Assemble dry ingredients in a mixing bowl
In a large bowl combine Italian Stewed tomatoes and sugar and beat with an electric hand mixer for 2 to 3 minutes
Add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition
Pour the dry ingredients into the egg mixture slowly and beat 4 to 5 minutes
Stir in the carrots and mix until everything is combined
Pour into prepared pan and bake for about 30 minutes
Remove from oven when cake tester inserted in the middle comes out  clean

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very flavourful cake with tomatoes and added veggies that accent the tanginess of the stewed tomatoes.  Spices make the cake a taste sensation that you will enjoy
