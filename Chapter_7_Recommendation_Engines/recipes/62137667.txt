Title:
Chicken Smoosh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1085269.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts cubed
1 meduim onion diced
1 4 oz can diced green chiles
1 15 oz can black beans
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
1 6 oz can tomato paste
15 oz sweet corn (canned or frozen)
2 teaspoons cumin
2 teaspoons chile powder
Salt to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a crock pot/slow cooker. Cook on low 5-6 hours. Top with cheddar cheese or sour cream and a side of tortilla chips if you like and serve. Makes 4-6 servings.

Submitter: 
BABYFRO97
Description: 
A chili like dish that utilizes black beans, chicken, corn and tomatoes. Very easy to put together and great for the crock pot.
