Title:
Creamy Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup white sugar
1 pinch salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp chilli powder
1/3 cup boiling water
3 cups milk
1/2 cup half-and-half cream
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine the cocoa, sugar, cinnamon, chili powder
and pinch of salt in a saucepan.  Blend in the boiling water.  Bring this mixture to an easy boil while you stir.  Simmer and stir for about 2 minutes.  Watch that it doesn't scorch.  Stir in 3 cups of milk and 1/2 cup half & half heat until very hot, but do not boil!  Remove from heat and add vanilla.  Divide between 4 mugs.
