Title:
Non-Dairy Snow Ice Cream  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6175791.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups soy milk (such as SilkÂ®)
2 2/3 tablespoons white sugar
16 cups clean fresh snow

Directions:
Mix the soy milk with the sugar in a bowl until the sugar has dissolved.
Place 2 cups of snow per serving into bowls, and spoon 1/2 cup of the soy milk mixture over each portion of snow to serve.

Easy, quick, and fun recipe for the whole family. Use soy milk, almond milk, or dairy milk, and your preferred sweetener.
