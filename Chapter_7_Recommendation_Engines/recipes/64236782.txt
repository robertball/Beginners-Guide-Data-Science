Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds beef, sirloin or round, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
1 medium onion, chopped
1 T olive oil
2 15 oz cans pinto beans, rinsed and drained
2 14 1/2 oz cans diced tomatoes with green chilies, undrained
2 cups water
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 14 3/4 oz can cream corn
1 envelope ranch dressing mix
1 envelope taco seasoning

Directions:
In a large pot, brown beef and onion in oil.
Add remaining ingredients; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes or until the meat is tender.
Serve with chopped cilantro, shredded cheese and/or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
Great, quick soup!  Very flavorful and filling!
