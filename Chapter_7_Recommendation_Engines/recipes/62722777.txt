Title:
Southern-Style Oven Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C all-purpose flour
1/4 t salt
1/2 t cayenne pepper (or to taste)
3 oz buttermilk
3/4 C cornflake crumbs (leave some big pieces, DO NOT crush flakes into fine powder)
1 # boneless skinless chicken breasts (four 4 oz pieces)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 365 F
Lightly coat 8 x 8 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray; set aside.
Combine flour, salt and cayenne pepper together in med-sized bowl.
Place buttermilk and corn flake crumbs in 2 separate shallow bowls.
Roll chicken breast halves in flour mixture and evenly coat each side.
Next dip chicken into buttermilk and then corn flake crumbs.
Place coated chicken in prepared baking dish.
Bake until chicken is tender and no longer pink, about 20 min. (there is no need to flip chicken during baking)
If chicken breasts are larger than 4 oz, may take 45-50 min to cook.
