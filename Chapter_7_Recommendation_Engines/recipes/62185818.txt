Title:
Belgian Endives with black forest ham and smoked gouda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 belgian endives all about the same size outter leaves removed
6 thin slices smoked gouda or swiss cheese
6 thin slices black forest ham

Directions:
Par boil endives for about 10 minutes
drain well
place 1 slice ham on cutting board and top with 1 slice cheese,roll endive up and place in a buttered casserole dish repeat with remaining endives.  Bake at 350  for about 20 minutes until cheese begins to melt

Submitter: 
Glenda Morrison de Kruif
Description: 
This is a simple yet elegant dish that is a real party pleaser
