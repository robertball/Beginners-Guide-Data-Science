Title:
Tantalizing Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz flat rice noodles
1/2 c fish sauce
2/3 c fresh lime juice
2 tsp white sugar
1/3 c oyster sauce
1/4 c asian chili sauce
2/3 c chicken stock
2/3 c vegetable oil
6 cloves garlic, minced
2 chicken breasts, skinned and cubed
1 small can sliced water chestnuts
3/4 lb Edamame (in shell)
1 c finely diced eggplant
6 oz diced mushrooms
12 sliced green onions
1/2 c shredded carrots
1/4 c fresh cilantro
2 limes
1 lb fresh bean sprouts

Directions:
Bring water to boil in a saucepan. Add rice noodles, soak for 20 minutes.
Mix fish sauce, 2/3 c lime juice, sugar, oyster sauce, chili sauce, 2 minced cloves of garlic, and chicken stock in separate saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and keep warm.
Brown chicken breast cubes in oil. Remove.
Shell edamame.
Mix sauce made previously in large saucepan with chicken breast, edamame, green onions, eggplant, sliced green onions, mushrooms, water chestnuts, bean sprouts, cilantro, and carrots.  Simmer for 15 minutes.
Serve over rice noodles

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe takes some time and makes a large amount, but it freezes perfectly and is absolutely delicious. It's like bringing the restaurant home to you!
