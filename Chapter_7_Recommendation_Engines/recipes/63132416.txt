Title:
Bomb  Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799544.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Large Potatoes Peeled
3 Cup shredded cheese
1 Box cream cheese
3 Tablespoons butter
¼ Cup condensed milk
½ Tablespoon garlic powder
½ Tablespoon onion powder
½ Tablespoon black powder
1 Tablespoon salt

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 425.
2.  Cut potatos into chunks and boil them
3.  While boiling chunks, microwave cream cheese until slightly soft
4.  After draining chunks and returning to pot, add all the the rest ingredients except 1 cup of shredded cheese. Mash it up good
5.  Pour  it into a baking dish and spread it around
6.  Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top and bake for about 30-40 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden
