Title:
Alexander's ultimate Brown Sugar Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups packed light brown sugar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 cup butter, softened
4 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3/4 cup milk
1 cup butter
1/2 cup light brown sugar
2 tablespoons milk
1 tablespoon light corn syrup
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Grease 12-cup fluted tube cake pan with shortening; lightly flour.
3. In medium bowl, mix flour, baking powder and salt; set aside.
4. In large bowl, beat 1 1/2 cups brown sugar, the granulated sugar and butter with electric mixer on low speed 30 seconds, scraping bowl constantly.
5. Beat on medium speed for about 5 minutes, until light and fluffy.
6. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each.
7. Add 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.
8. On low speed, alternately beat in flour mixture and 3/4 cup of milk.
9. Pour into pan.
10. Bake 55 to 65 minutes or until toothpick or knife comes out clean.
11. Cool 10 minutes.
12. Remove cake from cooling rack.
13. Cool completely, for about 2 hours.
14. Meanwhile, in a 2 quart saucepan, heat all the sauce ingredients except vanilla extract to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.
15. Boil and stir for 2 minutes.
16. Cool to room temperature.
17. Stir in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
18. Serve warm sauce with the cake or on top of cinnamon rolls.
