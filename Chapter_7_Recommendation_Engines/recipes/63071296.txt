Title:
Pork Chops and Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887215.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 (3/4 inch) thick pork chops
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, thinly sliced
16 ounces sauerkraut
1 teaspoon caraway seed
2 tablespoons apple juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Brown chops on all sides and remove from pan.
Add the garlic, onion, sauerkraut, caraway seed, apple juice, and salt and pepper to taste to the hot skillet. Saute for 3 to 4 minutes.
Lay browned pork chops on top of sauerkraut mixture, cover, reduce heat to low and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description:
