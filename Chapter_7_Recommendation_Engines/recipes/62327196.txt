Title:
Bamia- Meat and Okra stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cubed lamb or beef
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 package frozen chopped okra (or green beans)
12 oz can of diced tomatoes
8 oz can of tomato paste
apprx. 3 c. water
basmati rice
olive oil

Directions:
Mix tomato paste with diced tomatoes in a pot. Add enough water to make a soup-like consistency. Saute okra (or green beans) in a little bit of olive oil and add to tomato mixture (do not drain oil). Saute meat and garlic in a little olive oil (about 1 tbsp.) until meat is browned. Add to pot. Simmer for at least three hours. Serve over rice. Add toasted pine nuts as a garnish if you would like.

Submitter: 
MrsB
Description: 
Really easy and delicious tomato based stew that I got from a friend on another networking sight. The recipe calls for okra, but I substituted green beans because I just don't like okra! Even my picky 5 yr old loved it!
