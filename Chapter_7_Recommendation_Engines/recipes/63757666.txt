Title:
Kansas City Rib Rub for two Racks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 baby back rib racks
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 c paprika
3 tsp black pepper
1 Tbsp unrefined salt
1 Tbsp chili powder
1 Tbsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp onion powder
2 tsp cayenne pepper
Mustard
BBQ Sauce

Directions:
Remove membrane from back of rib racks.
Combine all ingredients together and rub on ribs front and back. Allow to sit 5 minutes.
Brush mustard on both sides of the ribs.  They are now ready to grill.
