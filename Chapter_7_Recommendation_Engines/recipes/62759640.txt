Title:
People Magazine- Beer Butt chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 lb.chicken
Dry Rub:
1 tsp.paprika
1 tsp.dry mustard
1 Tbsp. sea salt
1 tsp. brown sugar
1/4 tsp.pepper
1 tsp.garlic powder
1 tsp.onion powder
Basting Spray:
1 can beer
2 cups cider
2 Tbsp.olive oil
2 Tbsp.balsmamic vinegar

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together,rub all over chicken,inside and out.Open can of beer,pour half into spray bottle,add cider oil and vinegar.Insert can into cavity of chicken.Put chicken on BBQ,have one side on high heat and the side with the chicken on med.low.Grill should be at 350.Cook for 2 hours.
Spray chicken often with basting spray.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
Crispy on the outside--Juicy on the inside!!
Sprinkle the dry rub inside and out,spray often with beer and cider basting spray.
