Title:
Voodoo Cajun shrimp & grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539513.jpg
Ingredients:
Fish:
2 fish Fillets cut in half (makes 4)
pinch of Montreal steak seasoning
Grits:
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup water
1 tsp. butter
1/2 cup grits
1 oz. grated parmesan cheese
salt & pepper to taste
Green beans:
2 cups cut green beans
2 tbs. butter
Pinch of salt
Cajun cream shrimp:
1 cup heavy cream
2 tsp butter
18- 20 shrimp (add more or less as desired
Blackened seasoning:
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 tsp oregano

Directions:
1. In a soup/sauce pan bring cream, water, butter to a boil and boil for 1 min.
2. Remove from 4
3. Stir in grated parmesan cheese
4. Add salt & pepper to taste
5. Set aside for use later
6. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
7. In a bowl, mix all blackened seasoning ingredients and stir to even combine
8. heat skillet to medium high
9. Spray skillet with non stick cooking spray
10. place fish fillets side by side In skillet
11. Sprinkle 1 tsp blackened seasoning mix on each side of fish fillets
12. pan sear fish on both sides.
13. Place in oven and  bake fish at 400 degrees for 10 mins.
14.  Cut & clean green beans (or add frozen or canned pre cut green beans) to a new sauce pan
15. Add 2 tbs butter amd a pinch of salt to pan 16. Boil 5 mins
17. In same skillet used to sear fish fillets melt butter
18. Add shrimp and cook shrimp on medium-high in skillet
19. sprinkle pinch of all seasonings over shrimp
20. Cook shrimp in skillet on medium- high until opaque
21. Nove shrimp atound edges of skillet
22. De-glaze pan by adding heavy cream, and reduce by half while scraping shrimp remnants from pan
23. Add shrimp from esges of pan into sauce and stir, coating shrimp evenly in sauce mix
24. On each plate 1st add large scoop of grits, top with fish fillet from oven, and pour desired amount of shrimp mixture over fish, then arrange green beans in a circle around fish/grits/shrimp combo.
25. Serve while all ingredients hot.

Submitter: 
Heather Bryant
Description: 
Creamy parmesan grits topped with a grouper, Cod, or Mahi-Mahi fillet, then topped with blacke ed seasoned and sauteed Cajun shrimp.
