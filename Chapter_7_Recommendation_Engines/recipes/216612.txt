Title:
Uptown Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986033.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 pound smoked turkey sausage, sliced
1 (8 ounce) package ZATARAIN'SÂ® Red Beans and Rice Mix
3 1/4 cups water
Chopped green onions (optional)

Directions:
Heat oil in medium saucepan on medium-high heat. Add sausage; brown on both sides.
Stir in Rice Mix and water. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 20 to 25 minutes or until rice is tender, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat. Stir in green onions. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Zatarain's
Description: 
Filling, flavorful and easy to make, this New Orleans-style dish is a Monday night tradition in Louisiana. And the addition of smoked turkey sausage makes it perfect as a delicious meal all by itself, or as a robust side.
