Title:
Alley's Sweet Ham Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/774473.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 pound) canned ham
2 cups orange juice
1/2 cup water
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple
3 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Place ham in the slow cooker. Pour orange juice, water, and pineapple over the top of the ham. Sprinkle brown sugar along the top and sides. Cover, and cook on Low for 6 1/2 hours.
