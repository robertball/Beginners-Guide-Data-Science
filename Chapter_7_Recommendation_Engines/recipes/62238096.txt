Title:
Granpa's Apple cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/325366.jpg
Ingredients:
1 quart "canned" apples
1&1/2 cups sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 tps. nutmeg
1/4 tps ground cinnamon
1 tbls lemon juice
2 cups all purpose flour
1 tbls baking powder
1/2 tps salt
1/4 cup vegetable shortening
"cooking spray"
1 tbls melted butter
apx 4 tbls water

Directions:
preheat oven 425 degrees
In a large sausepan, combine apples,sugar,lemon juice,nutmeg,cinnamon;and brown sugar.
Cook on medium heat until sugars are well desolved.Reduce heat to simmer.
Prepare cobbler dough. Mix flour,salt,baking powder,and vegetable shortening.Cut shortening in with fork until well distributed. Add water until desired dough density is achieved. Roll out on floured surface apx 1/4" thick.
Apply nonstick spray to 2 qrt. ovenware dish.
Cover bottom with layer of prepared dough.
Add cooked apple mixture. Lattice top with remaining dough. Brush top with melted butter. Sprinkle with sugar and bake for 30 to 40 minutes,until crust turns brown. Remove from oven, let cool; and serve.
