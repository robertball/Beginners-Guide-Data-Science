Title:
HOT PEPPER JELLY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/884309.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups apple cider vinegar
1 cup chopped bell pepper                      (green-red-yellow-orange)
1 T. crushed hot pepper
1 drop of food coloring
1 package pectin
5 cups sugar

Directions:
Combine first five ingredients and bring to a full rolling boil over high heat while constantly stirring
Remove from heat.
Stir in sugar and again bring to a full rolling boil over high heat while continuing to stir cook for 1 min.
Pour into sterilized jars 1/8" from lip and seal.
Hot water bath for 5 min.

Submitter: 
Diana Karolak
Description: 
Easy Yet So Yummy!!! Try it as a snack with cream cheese and ritz crackers.  Also a Great dipping sauce for chicken and egg rolls, may also try as a grilling sauce.Modified from: HOT PEPPER JELLY (easy)
