Title:
Oriental Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs. Ramen noodles
2 pkgs. slaw mix
2 bunches scallions
chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1/4 c sesame seeds
1 pkg. almonds
6T sugar
6T rice vinegar
1 C oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix sugar, oil, seasoning pks. and vinegar. Mix with chicken, slaw, crushed ramen noodles,and scallions, sesame seeds,almonds, salt and pepper.  Chill and serve
