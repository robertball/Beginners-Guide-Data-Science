Title:
Banana Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking powder
2 eggs, well-beaten
1 cup mashed ripe banana (2 large)
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup chopped pecans, optional
Frosting:
3 Tbsp. Butter
2 Tbsp. cocoa
1-1/2 cups confectioners sugar
2 or 3 Tbsp. milk

Directions:
Microwave chocolate chips on low power to melt; cool slightly, set aside.  Combine flour, sugar, salt and baking powder in a mixing bowl.  Add chocolate, eggs, bananas and vanilla.  Beat with mixer on medium speed until well mixed.  Stir in pecans, if desired.
Spread batter in a well-greased 9" square glass baking dish.  Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes.  Cool. Frost with chocolate frosting.  Cut into squares.
To make frosting:
Combine butter and cocoa in medium saucepan.  Place over low heat, stirring constantly until smooth. Add confectioners sugar and 2 Tbsp. milk.  Add more milk to make spreading consistency.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
These dense, fudgy brownies get their flavor and texture from ripe bananas.  An easy favorite with our family.
