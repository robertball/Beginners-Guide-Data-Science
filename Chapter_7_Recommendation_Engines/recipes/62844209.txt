Title:
Yogurt Cheese Healthy Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. nonfat yogurt cheese
1/2 c. light brown sugar, packed
1/2 c. unsweetened apple sauce
3 med. size ripe bananas, mashed
4 egg whites
1 c. flour
3/4 c. oat bran
1/2 c. whole wheat flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 c. raisins or chopped dates
1/2 c. chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a 9 x 5 inch loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine yogurt cheese, sugar and apple sauce; whisk until smooth.
Beat in bananas and egg whites.
Combine dry ingredients, walnuts and raisins in a large bowl.
Add cheese mixture and blend until moistened. Transfer to pan and bake 60 to 70 minutes. Makes 1 loaf (16 slices).

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson

This is a very healthy spin on traditional Banana Bread
