Title:
Pesto Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/310000.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pieces of Food For Life's Ezekiel bread
Mayonnaise
Slices of red onion
1 3/4 slice of cheddarella cheese
2 Tbsp Kirkland/Costco pesto sauce
2 slices of tomato

Directions:
Preheat your pan.  Spread one side of each slice of bread with mayonnaise and spread the pesto on the cheese.  Layer the sandwich on the pan: bread (mayonnaise side down), cheese, pesto, tomato, onion, and bread (mayonnaise side up).  Place the lid on the pan until the cheese is just about melting and the mayonnaise has browned (about 4 mins.).  Flip the sandwich and cook until the mayonnaise has browned.  Remove and enjoy!
