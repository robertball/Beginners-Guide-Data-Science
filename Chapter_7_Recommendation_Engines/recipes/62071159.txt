Title:
Soonye's Korean Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube of Jimmy Dean "regular" Sausage, or equivalent.
5 cups of cooked white rice
1 carrot, Julienne
1 head Napa Cabbage or Chinese Cabbage
1 Onion, Chopped
2 Green Onions (opt)
1 Celery, Chopped
3 Tblspns. Soy Sauce (or to taste)
2 cloves of minced garlic
1 tsp. Hot Pepper Flakes
2 Tblspns Sesame Oil

Directions:
In a large Frying Pan, brown the sausage, chopping it into very small pieces with your spatula, as you would taco meat.  When completely browned, drain and set meat in a different bowl.  Cut the cabbage into bite sized pieces.  Julienne carrots.  Chop Celery and onions and green onions into small pieces.  Add this to frying pan with minced garlic over medium heat, and fry approximately 4 minutes, or until cooked through but still crispy.  Add the cooked sausage and cook an additional 2 minutes.  Add the soy sauce, Sesame Oil, and Red Papper flakes.  In a very large bowl, add this mixture with the rice and stir until the rice is evenly mixed with the other ingredients.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Snapper
Description: 
This is a great recipe of an Americanized version of fried rice that will be a great meal in itself.  Some basic ingredients include Jimmy Dean Sausage and Napa cabbage.  Serve with a cup of green tea for full enjoyment.
