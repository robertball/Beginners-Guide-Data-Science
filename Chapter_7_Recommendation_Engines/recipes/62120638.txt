Title:
Rich & Delicious Cream Cheese Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/893674.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Powdered Sugar
1 soft stick Butter
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
1/4 tsp Salt
Milk, or Evaporated Milk
1 8oz pkg Cream Cheese (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except milk.
After mixed well, adding milk to consistency desired.
Beat with mixer starting with low speed, then to high speed for a minute or two.
If you want Cream Cheese Icing, just add a pkg of cream cheese then beat with mixer.
