(ENDLESS variations)
Fill the slow cooker with your favorite cereal, snacks, etc... (I used Chex, Pretzels, and Peanuts) In a saucepan melt Butter, then add Worcestershire, Garlic and Onion Powders, Salt and Sugar.
Pour that mix over the Cereal, cover and cook on low for 2-3 hours, stir regularly to get the mix on everything
