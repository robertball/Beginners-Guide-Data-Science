Title:
'Dark Night' Vegan Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990396.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package silken tofu, drained
3/4 cup Stevia Extract In The Raw® Cup For Cup
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 tablespoon light agave syrup
1/4 cup soy milk
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tablespoons carob powder
Mint leaves

Directions:
Place tofu, Stevia Extract In The Raw and vanilla in a food processor or blender. Process until well blended. Add remaining ingredients and process until mixture is fully blended.
Pour into small dessert cups or espresso cups. Chill for at least 2 hours. Garnish with fresh mint leaves just before serving.

Submitter: 
Stevia Extract In The Raw®
Description: 
A rich tasting healthy dessert that is sure to satisfy - thanks to the perfect combination of sweetness, texture and great looks!
