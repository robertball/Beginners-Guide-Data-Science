Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package uncooked pasta shells
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained
2(10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 1/4 cups milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon crushed garlic
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups crushed potato chips

Directions:
Boil pasta in lightly salted water for 10 minutes, or until aldente; drain well.  Mix tuna with cream of mushroom soup, milk, salt, black pepper, and garlic in the empty pot, then return the pasta to the pot, and mix tuna mixture with pasta.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 2 1/2 quart casserole dish with cooking spray.
Spread 1/2 of the noodles into the prepared dish.  Arrange the cheese slices over the noodles, then spread the remaining noodle mixture over the cheese. Top with crushed potato chips.
Bake, uncovered, for 20 to 30 minutes; cook until the casserole is hot, and the chips begin to brown. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
