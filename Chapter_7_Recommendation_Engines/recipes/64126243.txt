Title:
Blueberry Crumb Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crumble topping...
1/2 c brown sugar
1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 c butter, softened
Cake...
2 c flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
3/4 c sugar
1/4 c butter
1 large egg
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c buttermilk
3 c fresh Blueberries

Directions:
Mix together crumble topping and set aside.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees and grease 9 inch square pan.
For cake...in medium bowl, stir together dry ingredients.
In large bowl, cream butter and sugar, then blend in egg and vanilla until smooth.
To cream mixture, alternate adding some of the flour mixture and some of the buttermilk and stir well until all is blended in and creamy.
Gently fold in berries, Dough will be stiff so be careful not to mash berries.
Spread in own, sprinkle with crumb mixture and bake for 50-60 minutes.
