Title:
Mommy's Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz. pkg frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained (squeeze in towel)
1 16 oz. carton sour cream
3 eggs, beaten
1/4 c. butter, melted
1 c. grated sharp cheddar cheese
3 T. flour
1/4 t. salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Pour into a greased 9" pie dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes
