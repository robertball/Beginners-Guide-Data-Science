Title:
Frugal Summer Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4556045.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups skim milk
2 cups coffee, at room temperature
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
ice cubes

Directions:
Mix skim milk, coffee, sugar, vanilla extract, and almond extract together in a 1-quart container. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Pour over ice.

Submitter: 
LeslieFSU
Description: 
Tired of paying so much money for iced coffees, I invented my own version that I keep on hand in the refrigerator for summer mornings when I want coffee but not HOT coffee. Store the the refrigerator.
