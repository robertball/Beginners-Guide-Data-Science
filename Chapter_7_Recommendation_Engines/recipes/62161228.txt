Title:
tukish rice pilaf with chicken breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chiken breast , boiled
1 cup rice
2 cups chicken broth
4 Table spoons orzo
1 teaspoon salt
4 Tablespoons butter

Directions:
one cup rice put it in a bowl and wash it .
By washing the rice, we are getting rid off the white dust that makes the rice stick.
We doont like sticky rice .
Drain the water.
Melt the butter in a non-stick pan. Add the orzo and sautee at medium heat until light brown.
Add the rice and continue sauteeing until the excess water evaporates and the rice looks shiny .
this is important , if you wait too long, the rice will stick to the pan and it will be ruined.
Pour two cups of boiling hot broth  into the pan. Reduce the heat to low Close the lid and leave the rice untouched for 10 minutes.a chicken breast is boiled, the broth is used to make the pilav. Hand picked chicken pieces are typically added to the the rice during cooking.  At the end of the 10 minutes, turn off the heat, place a paper towel under the lid to absorb the excess moisture in the pan and let the rice rest for at least 25 minutes before serving.
