Title:
Mom's Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. macaroni
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed Cheddar cheese soup
1/4 cup sour cream
3/4 cups milk
1 cups shredded cheese
Bread Crumbs
Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Stir in cheese soup, sour cream, cheese and milk until well combined. Transfer to casserole dish. Sprinkle with pepper and paprika.
Top with bread crumbs and melted butter
Bake 40 minutes, or until cheese is brown and bubbly.
