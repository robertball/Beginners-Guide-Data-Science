Title:
Low Carb Mexican Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037456.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups water
4 Cups Swanson chicken stock
1 head cauliflower ( or 1 bag dozen cauliflower florets 5 c)
1/4 Vidalia onion
1 can black soy beans (or black beans)
1 cup baby white corn fresh or frozen
1/4 cup roasted red peppers cut in strips
1/4 c lime juice
3 tsp cayenne pepper
2 tbsp Cumin
2 bay leaves
4 stalks celery
Two chicken breasts about 12 oz
1 cup sliced button mushrooms raw
2 tbsp garlic powder
3 tbsp seasoning salt
1 tbsp black pepper
4 cloves garlic
Serve with Shredded Cabot extra sharp cheddar and lime wedges

Directions:
In large stock pot add water, stock, chicken, spices, onion, garlic, 1/2 of the cauliflower & 1/2 of the celery.  Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, 20 min. Remove chicken and shred. Remove stock from heat and pur√©e with emulsion blender. Add all remaining ingredients and shredded chicken (except cheese and lime wedges) Return to boil, reduce to simmer. Serve with shredded cheddar and lime wedges.  Add franks red hot sauce for spicier version to taste.

Description: 
Great low carb  twist on an old favorite. Cauliflower replaces the potatoes and roasted red peppers replace the carrots. Black soy beans subbed for black beans.  Rich and filling this soup is a health and  satisfying low carb meal. Freezes well.
