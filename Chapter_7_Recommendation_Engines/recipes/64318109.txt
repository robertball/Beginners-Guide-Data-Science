Title:
Yellow Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices bacon
2 medium yellow squash, thinly sliced
1/2 medium onion, thinly sliced
3 cloves, garlic, minced
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 dash soy sauce
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. onion powder
lemon pepper to taste
Mexican blend cheese

Directions:
Cut bacon cross ways and fry over medium-high heat until crisp. Remove bacon from pan, and set aside. Add the onion, and fry till caramelized. Add squash, garlic and bell pepper to the skillet. Cover for about 15 minutes. Season with soy sauce,  garlic salt, onion powder and lemon pepper. Stir, to blend in seasonings, sprinkle with cheese and serve.
