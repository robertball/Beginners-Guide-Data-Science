Title:
Western Egg White Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 spray(s) cooking spray to coat skillet
2 tablespoon pepper(s), green, bell chopped
2 tablespoon pepper(s), red, bell chopped
2 tablespoon scallion(s) (green onions) chopped
1 cup(s) egg white(s) use 4 separated large eggs
6 tablespoon cheese, reduced-fat shredded

Directions:
Lightly coat a medium skillet with cooking spray. Saut√© the peppers and the scallions until they are tender-crisp. Pour the egg substitute over the vegetables. When partially set, spread the cheese over half of the egg substitute and fold the omelet in half over the filling. Continue cooking until cooked through. Serve immediately.
