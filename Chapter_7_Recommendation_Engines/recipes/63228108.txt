Title:
Whole Wheat Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp sugar
1 egg, room temp
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup blueberries

Directions:
Sift together flour and baking powder. Add cinnamon, salt, and sugar. Set aside. In a separate bowl,whisk together the egg with milks and add vanilla extract. Combine the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients. Add blue berries and stir.
Preheat non stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Pour about 1/4 cup batter into pan for each pancake. Cook for about 1 1/2 minutes, flip and cook other side for about 1 minute or until golden brown.
