Title:
Chicken Milanese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/389705.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breast cut thinly
2 Egg
1 Cup Italian Breadcrumbs
3/4 Cup Flour
1/4-1/2 Cup Oil for Fyring
Ground Black Pepper
Kosher Salt
Lemon wedges for garnish

Directions:
In dish beat eggs with salt and pepper.  In a seperate dish place flour.  In another dish place bread crumbs. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.  Dip each peice of chicken (1 at a time) into flour, shake excess off. Next place chicken into  egg mixture coating well.  Then place chicken into bread crumb mixture and press crumbs onto chicken.  Repeat for each peice of chicken.
In a skillet heat oil until warm.  Place 2-3 peices if chicken into pan and fry each side about 2-4 on each side or until golden brown.  Place chicken onto baking sheet and place into warm oven to keep until all chicken is ready.
Garnish with lemon wedges.  Serve with your favorite pasta dish.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
I made this recipe up after not finding very many recipes for chicken milanese to go with my pasta.  It is quick and delicious.
