Title:
Mommy's Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. flour
2 t. baking soda
1/4 t. salt
1 c. shortening
1 c. granulated sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. peanut butter
1 t. vanilla
2 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugars.  Add egg and mix.  Add peanut butter, vanilla, flour, soda, and salt.
Roll into 1 inch balls and press with fork.
Bake at 375 degrees for 15-18 minutes.

Amy Hague Miller
