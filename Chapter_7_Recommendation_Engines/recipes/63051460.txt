Title:
No-Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c Sugar
1 cube Butter
1/2 c Milk
1/4 c Cocoa
1/2 c Peanut Butter
3 c Oatmeal
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Combine the first four ingredients in a pan and bring to a boil for 1 1/2 minutes, then remove from heat.  Immediately add and stir in the peanut butter, oatmeal and vanilla.  Drop on waxed paper for cooling, then serve.
