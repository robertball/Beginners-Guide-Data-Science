Title:
Christmas morning French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf crusty bread cut into 1 inch cubes
2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup melted margarine
3 tbsp light corn syrup
5 eggs
5 cups milk
3 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat over to 350. Grease 9x13 pan or casserole dish
Mix 1 cup brown sugar, butter, and corn syrup in the pan
Mix eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla, and remaining brown sugar in a bowl
Dip each bread cube into egg mixture
Place dipped bread on top of syrup mixture, evenly
Pour excess egg mixture on bread
Bake 1 hour
Serve sprinkled with powdered sugar

My mom's Christmas morning breakfast. Perfect to bake while everyone opens gifts!
