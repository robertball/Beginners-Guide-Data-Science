Title:
Chuck Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1082092.jpg
Ingredients:
6 lb. church roast
Â½ tsp. salt and pepper
2 TBSP Olive Oil
1 TBSP Seasoning Mix
1 large onion, sliced
2 cups Beef Stock
2 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
4 TBSP Steak Sauce

Directions:
Pat roast dry and season on both sides with salt and pepper.
In a skillet add olive oil and medium-high heat, once the skillet is hot, place roast in and sear on both sides about 4 minutes per side.
Once browned remove from skillet and season on both sides with seasoning mix and place in roasting dish laying the sliced onion on top of the roast.
Combine beef stock, Worcestershire sauce and steak sauce then pour into roasting dish with roast.
Cook at 350 degrees for 3-4 hours until the desired donness.
