Title:
Unstuffed cabbage roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042624.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1 large onion chopped
1 small cabbage chopped
2 14.5 oz cans of diced tomatoes
1 8 oz can of tomato sauce
2 cloves minced garlic
1/2 c water
2 tsp salt
1tsp pepper

Directions:
Brown beef and onion in Dutch oven or large electric skillet. Drain grease and add garlic, tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, & cabbage.   Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat.  Let simmer for about 30 minutes or till cabbage is tender.

Submitter: 
tlc_adams
Description: 
This is a quick and easy main dish.  My kids don't even like cabbage, but they love this. I usually try to serve it with green beans w/ new potatoes, corn and a pan of corn bread.  They eat over half the pan every time. Also, the longer it stands the better it tastes.
