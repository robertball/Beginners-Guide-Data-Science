Title:
Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups butter
1 cup sugar (icing or brown)
4 cups all purpose flour
1/4 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream butter, add sugar and blend thoroughly. Add vanilla. Add flour in four additions and mix well after each addition. Turn onto mixing board and knead until all cracks have disappeared. Spread in ungreased shortbread pan. Prick deeply with a fork. Bake in a moderate oven (350) for 20-30 minutes until very lightly brown. Watch it!
