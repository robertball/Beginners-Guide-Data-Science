Title:
Pigs in a blanket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Whole Wheat (or White All Purpose) Flour
1/4  Cup Vegetable Oil
3/4 to 1 cup Cup Hot (just boiled) Water
½ tsp Baking Powder
For the Hot Dogs (The Pigs):
12 Hot Dogs cut in half

Directions:
prepare the dough. roll into a rectangular sheet then cut narrow triangles (like croissant). put half a dog in it and roll. Bake @180° for 25 minutes.
