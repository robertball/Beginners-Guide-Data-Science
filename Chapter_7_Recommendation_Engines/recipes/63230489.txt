Title:
Mango Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840523.jpg
Ingredients:
6 med. mangos
3 c. water
3 c. sugar
1 cinnamon stick
2 cloves
1 tsp. vanilla
3 tbsp Powder Pectin

Directions:
Peel and cut mangos in chunks. Combine mangos, cinnamon, cloves, and water and bring to hard boil. Remove cinnamon stick and disgard. Cook at low heat to medium for 10 minutes.
Add sugar and Pectin; mix well.
Continue cooking in medium heat until mixture registers 250 degrees on candy thermometer.
Remove from heat and skim off foam if necessary. Add vanilla.
Add some of the mixture to a blender to blend together and not be so chunky. Repeat for less chucks of mango.
Boil jars in water, then fill with jam mixture and place back in canner for 10 mins.

Submitter: 
Alicia C.
Description: 
This is a great, yummy and easy recipe for a mango jam that will sure put smiles on your kids faces.
