Title:
Arizona Carnitas With Green Chiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds boneless pork (tenderloin, shoulder, or butt - whichever is available)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 large yellow onion, cut into thin slivers
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles, undrained
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can rotell
1 T taco seasoning
Flour tortillas OR corn tortillas
Shredded Cheddar cheese
Chopped tomato
Sour cream

Directions:
Add all to crockpot and cook on high 4 to 5 hours.
stirring occasionally. Add additional liquid if needed.
Remove pork and shred.
Serve pork in tortillas topped with Cheddar cheese, avocado, tomato, onion and sour cream if desired.
