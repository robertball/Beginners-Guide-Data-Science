Title:
Pears in chocolate sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pears, peeled
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon vainilla
4 ounces of dark chocolate, grated
2 tablespoons heavy cream
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon Kahlua or creme de cacao
Maraschino cherries

Directions:
Leave stems on peeled pears. Sprinkle thorougly with lemon juice and set aside. Heat water and sugar in pan large enough to hold pears upright. When sugar is dissolved, add vainilla and pears, simmering over low heat about 15 minutes. Let cool in sugar water, then drain.
In a double boiler, melt chocolate; add cream, butter and liqueur. To serve, pour chocolate sauce over pears. Garnish with maraschino cherries.
