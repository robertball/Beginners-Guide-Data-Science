Title:
Pasta with Smoked Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843319.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. pasta
1/4-1/2 cup crumbled feta
fresh cilantro or parsley
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
4.5 oz. smoked salmon
cayenne pepper
black pepper
salt
*may need 1 1/2 Tbsp. flour and 2 Tbsp. butter to make a rue
*add a sprinkle of lemon juice

Directions:
Cook pasta.
Make a rue with flour and butter.
Pour cream into saucepan and bring to a simmering point. Do not let boil.
Cut smoked salmon into thin strips and add to cream mixture. Season with cayenne, salt and black pepper.
Add feta and cilantro just before serving.
If necessary, thin with milk.
Garnish with Parmesan, parsley and lemon juice.
