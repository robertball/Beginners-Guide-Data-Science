Title:
A+ Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-14.5 oz can of diced peeled tomatoes
1/3 c Lime juice
1/2 c chopped onion
3 T fresh chopped cilantro
2 T red wine vinegar
2-3 T chopped jalapenos
1-2 T jalapeno juice (optional)

Directions:
put all ingredients in the blender.
puree for 2 min.
pour into a jar or bowl and chill for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Laura Moody
Description: 
This is a great, spicy salsa- not for mild people.
