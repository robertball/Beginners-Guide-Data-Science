Title:
Stir-Fry Pork with Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065807.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 inch piece fresh ginger root, thinly sliced
1/4 pound pork, thinly sliced
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon dark soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1 green onion, chopped
1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
Fry ginger in hot oil until fragrant, then add pork, soy sauce, dark soy sauce, salt, and sugar.
Cook, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes.
Stir in the sesame oil, green onion, and rice wine.
Simmer until the pork is tender.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
A simple Chinese dish. The wine and ginger gives the dish its fragrant smell. Best served with warm rice.Modified from: Stir-Fry Pork with Ginger
