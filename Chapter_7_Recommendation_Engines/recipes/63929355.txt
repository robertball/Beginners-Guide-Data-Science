Title:
Toasted Black and White Rice and Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
sesame oil
coconut oil
garlic
1.5 c black rice
1.5 c white rice
1-2 c red cabbage
1 c kale
1-2 c cilantro
1/2 c parsley
2 avocado
4-5 baby carrots
4-5 gr onion
Greens handful
1/2-3/4 c cashew
Sauce
2T soy sauce
2T sesame oil
2T apple cider vinegar
Lime juice
Lemon juice
Salt
Ginger powder
Garlic raw chopped
Little agave

Directions:
Heat coconut oil and sesame oil in pan
saute garlic, add rice and toast until fragrant
add edamame to rice if using frozen (to thaw/heat)
remove from heat
mix all vegetables
add rice to vegetables
prepare sauce and add to rice/vegetables
season with additional lime/lemon, salt/pepper to taste
