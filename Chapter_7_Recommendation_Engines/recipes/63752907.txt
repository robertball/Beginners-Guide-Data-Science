Title:
4 INGREDIENT COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Ripe bananas
1 cup quick oats
1/2 c choc chips
1 heaping spoonful of natural peanut butter

Directions:
Mix well.
Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheet.
Bake at 350 for 15 mins.
