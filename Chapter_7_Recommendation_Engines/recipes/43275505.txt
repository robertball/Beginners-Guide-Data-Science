Title:
Chicken Doodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small chicken
3 stalks celery
3 carrot sticks, peeled
1 sweet onion
1 package high quality egg noodles
chicken base
poultry seasoning
salt and pepper

Directions:
In a food processor, liquefy onion, celery, and carrots
Put in a stock pot filled with water along with whole chicken
Add 1 Tablespoon chicken base, 1 Tablespoon poultry seasoning and salt and pepper to taste
Slow boil for about an hour, adding water when needed.
Take chicken out and cook noodles in stock
While noodles are cooking, debone chicken and add meat back to stock.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
I don't know if this is chicken and dumplins or chicken noodle soup, so I call it both!
