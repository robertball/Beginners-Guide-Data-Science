Title:
Tito's Summer Heat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/8225635.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Tito's Handmade Vodka
2 ounces sparkling water
1/2 ounce lime juice
1/2 ounce agave nectar
3 slices cucumber
3 slices jalapeno
1 slice lime

Directions:
Muddle cucumber and 2 slices jalapeno in a shaker tin. Add Tito's Handmade Vodka, lime juice, agave, and ice. Shake vigorously and strain into a glass over ice.
Top with sparkling water. Garnish with a slice of lime and jalapeno.

Submitter: 
Tito's Handmade Vodka
Description: 
It's gettin' hot in here, and we're not just talking about the summer weather. With sweet agave, a squeeze of citrus, and a helping handful of heat, this spicy cocktail is perfect for poolside sipping.
