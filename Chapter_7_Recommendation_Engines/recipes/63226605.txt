Title:
Orange sweet rolls to die for, made in bread machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/838832.jpg
Ingredients:
for the dough:
1 (25 ounce) pkg dry active yeast
1/4 cup warm water (110-115 degrees F)
1 cup warm milk (110-115 degrees F)
1/4 cup frozen orange juice concentrate, defrosted
1 tsp orange extract
1/4 cup butter,melted
1/4 c instant vanilla pudding mix
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
2 cups all purpose flour
2 cups bread flour
Filling:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp orange extract
2 Tbls grated orange peel
Glaze:
8 oz cream cheese softened
2 cups confectioners sugar
4 tsp butter softened
1 tsp orange extract
4 tsp milk

Directions:
Place ingredients for dough in bread machine according to manufacturers instructions. set to sweet and select dough cycle. when cycle is finished, punch dough down, divide in half and roll into 15X10 in rectangle, spread 1/2 the filling on each rectangle. roll up jelly roll style starting with long end.cut each into 12 rolls, place in 2 well buttered 9x13 pans. let rise 45 min or until doubled. bake 375 for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned. mix glaze ingredients and spread over warm rolls.

Kristy Guillotte
Description: 
the texture of these rolls is so light and fluffy with just a hint of orange.
