Title:
Slow Cooker Mini Italian Sausage Sub's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds hot Italian sausage
3 pounds sweet Italian sausage
1 29 ounce can crushed tomatoes
1 16 ounce can tomato paste
2 green bell peppers, thinly sliced
2 red bell peppers, thinly sliced
3 large onions, cut in wedges
3 garlic cloves, pressed
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried thyme
24 club rolls or mini sub rolls
4 cups shredded Italian cheese blend

Directions:
Cut sausages to fit rolls.
Place in a large baking pan and pierce with a fork.
Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes, set aside.
Combine next 9 ingredients in the slow cooker, mix well.
Add sausages and 3 tablespoons of the pan drippings from the baking pan.
Cook on high for 3 hours, then reduce the heat to low and cook 3 hours longer, stirring occasionally.
Add a few teaspoons of water if needed to acheive desired consistency.
Serve on rolls and top with the shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This is a tasty recipe for sausage & peppers we have been using for quite some time. It's mostly done using the slow cooker, which makes these little sub's perfect for parties and Holiday buffets. Topping these with the shredded cheese blend put's these over the top!
