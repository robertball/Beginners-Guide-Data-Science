Title:
Easy Personal Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/873998.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
5 tbsp sugar
2 tbsp cocoa powder
1 egg
3 tbsp milk
3 tbsp veggie oil
dash salt
dash vanilla or almond extract

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a microwave safe bowl, or coffee mug. Microwave for two minutes.
Poke holes in the top to help cool, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kristin Nicole Patton
Description: 
Got a late night craving for chocolate? Want dessert for one? With a drizzle of chocolate syrup on top, some strawberries tossed in, or a dollop of your favorite ice cream, this microwave cake is a delicious single person dessert option!
