Title:
Chicken Pork Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups liquid (vegetable/chicken broth, dry white wine or water)
1 clove minced garlic
1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
1 cup long grain rice
6 center cut loin pork chops about 2 pounds
1 can cream of mushroom & roasted garlic soup
1/4 cup dry white wine/milk
1 clove minced garlic
1 teaspoon dried italian herbs
6 slices swiss or provolone cheese
1 cup herbed stuffing mix
Butter flavor spray

Directions:
In saucepan bring vegetable broth, wine, garlic, and herbs to a boil.  Stir in rice, reduce to simmer and cover.  Allow to simmer for 20 minutes.  Remove from heat, allow to sit, covered 30 minutes.  While rice is cooking/resting, mix soup, wine, garlic & herbs in a small bowl until blended.  Put stuffing mix in a plastic bag, and crush to remove large chunks. Preheat oven to 350F.
Remove cover from rice & fluff with fork.  Spray 9 x 13 baking dish with butter flavor spray, spread rice across bottom.  Lay pork chops on top of rice.  Pour soup mixture over pork, lay cheese over soup.  Sprinkle top with stuffing mix.  Spray stuffing with butter-flavor spray.  Bake 40-50 minutes, uncovered.  Remove from oven and serve.

Submitter: 
LARANEFF
Description: 
This is named Chicken Pork because my family always comments on how good the chicken is, when it is really pork chops.  Of course you can substitute boneless skinless chicken.Modified from: Chicken Pork Dinner
