Title:
Duncan Hines® Original Marbled Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Duncan Hines Family Size Brownie Mix
2 (3-oz.) pkgs. cream cheese, softened
5 Tbs. butter, softened
1/3 cup granulated sugar
4 large eggs
2 Tbs. all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Cream Cheese Mixture:  Beat together cream cheese & butter.  Add sugar, 2 eggs, flour and vanilla extract; beat until smooth.  Set aside.
Brownie Batter:  Preheat oven to 350º F.  Grease 13”x9” pan.  Empty brownie mix into medium bowl.  Add water, oil & 2 eggs.  Mix by hand about 50 strokes until smooth.  Pour half of brownie batter into prepared pan. Evenly pour all of cream cheese mixture over brownie layer.  Spoon the remaining brownie batter here & there over the cream cheese layer.  Swirl through layers with knife.  Bake at 350º F for 35-45 minutes or until done.  Cool.  Store refrigerated.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mom
Description: 
This recipe is from the '70s.  It makes a rich, addictive bar cookie.
