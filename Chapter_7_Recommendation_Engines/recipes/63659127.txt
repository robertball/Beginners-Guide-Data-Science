Title:
Bacon and Green Olive Cheddar Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1016677.jpg
Ingredients:
4 oz. cream cheese - softened
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese + 3 Tbls. for topping
1/4 cup minced fresh onions
3 Tbls. packaged bacon bits
1/4 cup Spanish green olives with pimentos-minced(about 15 olives)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix all ingredients together very well.
Spread into a small(2 cup)greased baking dish.
Top with 3 Tbls. of shredded cheddar.
Bake for 25-30 minutes or until bubbling.
Serve warm on plain crackers or on sliced white bread.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
A creamy cheese spread, bursting with green olive and bacon flavors! Perfect on crackers or as we love it, on sliced white bread!
