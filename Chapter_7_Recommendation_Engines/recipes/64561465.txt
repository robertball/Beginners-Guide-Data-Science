Title:
Barbecue Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3469642.jpg
Ingredients:
One pack of chicken or drumsticks
Cup of barbecue sauce
Season all
Onion powder
Garlic powder
Pepper

Directions:
1. Rinse your chicken and put it in a pan. Set your oven on 375 degrees.
2. Put your desired amount of seasoning on your chicken
3. Put your chicken in the oven and cook for 45 minutes or until it's done
4. When your chicken is done take it out and apply the barbecue sauce evenely
5. Put the chicken back in for another 5-6 minutes
6. Take chicken out and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jada Evans
Description: 
This barbecue chicken is great when you want an easy, quick, delicious dinner.
