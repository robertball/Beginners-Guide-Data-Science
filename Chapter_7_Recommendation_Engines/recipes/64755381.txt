Title:
Greek Yogurt Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups nonfat greek yogurt
3 eggs
1 box sugar free pudding (any flavor)
5 packets stevia

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix all ingredients together
Pour into a spring form pan
Bake for 40 minutes
Let it cool for 30 minutes
Refrigerate over night

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
DELICIOUS dessert, SUPER EASY and ZERO Weight Watchers Points! Very customizable. Add any topping you like but that could change the WW point value.
