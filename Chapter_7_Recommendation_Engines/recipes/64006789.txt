Title:
Easy Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1112152.jpg
Ingredients:
Eggs 2
Milk 1 cup (warmed in micowave for 30 seconds)
All purpose flour 1 cup
Salt 1 tsp
Melted butter 3 Tbsp

Directions:
Brush 10 muffin cups with 1 Tbsp of melted butter.
Preheat the oven with the muffin tin to 400 degrees F.
Mix the eggs in a blender until light yellow. Add the warmed milk and flour,salt and remaining melted butter and blend until smooth.
Pour the batter into the warmed tin and return it to the oven.
Bake for about 30 to 35 mins.
