Title:
In-a-Hurry Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup butter or margarine
½ cup milk
2 cups sugar
¼ cup unsweetened cocoa
¼ tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla or almond extract
½ cup peanut butter
3 cups quick-cooking oatmeal

Directions:
Place butter and milk in a large microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high for one minute. Stir.
Add sugar, cocoa, and salt. Microwave for 4 minutes on high. Stir well. Microwave 1 more minute on high.
Add extract and peanut butter. Stir until well mixed. Add the oatmeal. Stir well and drop by teaspoons on waxed paper or cookie sheet. Refrigerate 1 to 2 hours if you can wait for them to set.

Submitter: 
Shawna Hambly
Description: 
These ridiculously good cookies require no baking! Just make in the microwave, spoon onto a baking sheet and let harden!
