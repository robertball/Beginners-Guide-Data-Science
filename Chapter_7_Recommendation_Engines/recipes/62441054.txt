Title:
Good Boy Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 ¼ cup milk
¾ cup white flour
¾ cup brown flour
1 cup quick oats
1 tsp. baking soda
¼ tsp. salt
2/3 cup craisins

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and bake at 375 for 15-17 minutes.
