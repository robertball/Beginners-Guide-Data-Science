Title:
Lemon Snow Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large lemons
2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
3/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup milk
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup sugar

Directions:
Cut lemons in half crosswise. Juice lemons reserving juice and remove pulp with a spoon.Remove ends of lemons to stabilize. Cover with plastic wrap and freeze. In large bowl, combine zest, juice, milk and cream. Add sugar and stir until dissolved. Cover and freeze 8 hours. Remove and beat with electric mixer until smooth but still very firm. Spoon into lemon shells, cover and freezefor 2 hours.

Submitter: 
PIPPYMOE
Description: 
Creamy texture,almost like shorbet yet a little bit richer. Great fun for kids also.
