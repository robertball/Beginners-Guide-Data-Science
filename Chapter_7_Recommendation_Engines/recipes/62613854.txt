Title:
Shrimp & Veggie Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/567999.jpg
Ingredients:
4 corn tortillas
1 cup diced tomatoes, juice and all
1 cup diced celery
2 tsp Raspberry Walnut Vinaigrette Salad Dressing
1/2 tsp minced garlic
18 (40-50 ct sized) shrimp

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
Stack tortillas and cut with kitchen shears into pie shaped wedeges.
Spread out evenly in a single layer on cookie sheet, spinkle with salt and bake for 8-12 minutes.
Remove and let cool 5-10 minutes. They'll crisp up while cooling.
In a bowl, add diced tomatoes, celery, minced garlic, and salad dressing.
Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Cook shrimp, cool and refrigerate
Just before serving, dice 6 of the shrimp and stir into the tomato mixture.
Spoon tomato/shrimp mixture into 4 Martini glasses. Garnish with a stalk of celery and 3 shrimps on side of each Martini glass. Serve with baked tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
A delightfully light seafood appetizer with fresh tomatoes, crunchy celery and topped with cooked shrimp. Simple homemade tortilla chips for scooping out every juicy drop of this sure to please starter.
