Title:
Cold Blueberry Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt. frozen or fresh blueberries
1C.  apple juice
2T. white sugar
1 wedge of lemon
1 cinnamon stick
1/2t. vanilla extract
1  8 oz. container vanilla yogurt

Directions:
1. Place all the ingredients except the yogurt in a heavy saucepan. Bring to boil over high heat, stirring to dissolve sugar. Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
2. Remove and discard lemon and cinnamon stick.
3. Let soup cool to room temperature. Puree in a food processor or blender until smooth.
4. Chill the soup. Serve it cold. Swirl each serving with some vanilla yogurt.

Submitter: 
Deb
Description: 
Quick and easy to put together, this chilled soup has almost no fat and is low-cal.
