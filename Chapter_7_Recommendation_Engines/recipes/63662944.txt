Title:
Never Fail Chocolate cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Flour
2 Eggs
1/2 C Cocoa
1/4 tsp salt
2 c sugar
2/3 c milk
1 cube butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in bowl thoroughly.  Now add 1 cup of hot water with 1 tsp of baking soda dissolved in it.  Pour into greased and floured 9x13 pan and bake 25 to 30 minutes at 350.  Frost with fav frosting .

Submitter: 
Troy Meyer
Description: 
This is our absolute favorite chocolate cake recipe.  It is very moist.
