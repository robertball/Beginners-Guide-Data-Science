Title:
Sour Cream Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp curry
1/8 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Blend all in a bowl and refrigerate for at least an hour prior to serving.

Submitter: 
duboo
Description: 
A jazzed up sour cream that goes well with waffle nachos.
