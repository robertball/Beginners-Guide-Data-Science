Title:
Pumpkin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
3 cups brown sugar
1 can pumpkin mix (approx. 3 cups)
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 TB baking powder
1 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ginger
1 tsp. salt
5 1/2 cups flour
1 bag of chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix together and bake at 400 degree oven for about 12 min.
