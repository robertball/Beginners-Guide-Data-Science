Title:
Tuna Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cans of Tuna in Water (Drained by about half of water)
3 Tbsp Olive Oil
3 Cloves Minced Garlic
1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
1/2 C Chopped Fresh Italian Parsley
1 28oz Can Crushed Tomatoes
1Tbsp Concentrated Tomato Paste or 1 small Can of Regular Tomato Paste
28oz of Water (From the Can of Crushed Tomatoes
Chopped Fresh Basil
1tsp Salt
1tsp Pepper
1/2 tsp Sugar

Directions:
In a Medium Sauce Pan heat Olive Oil on Medium High Heat and Sautee Garlic, Red Pepper, and Parsley until fragrant.
Add Tuna and continue to cook for about 2 minutes. Add the Tomatoes, Water, Paste, Basil, Salt Pepper, and Sugar.
Simmer over Medium Low Heat for at least 30 minutes until it is reduced into a consistency to serve over Pasta.
This Sauce works well with Angel Hair Pasta, Small Pasta Shells, or Bowtie Pasta.

Submitter: 
Anissa
Description: 
A favorite for me as a child. Quick and easy. Great idea for a meatless dish.Even served at room temperature, it is still delicious.
