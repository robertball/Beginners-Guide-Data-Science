Title:
Cheesy Chicken Spaghetti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked, chopped chicken  (I like a whole rotisserie chicken)
12oz spaghetti, cooked and drained
5 slices of bacon, cooked crispy and chopped
2 cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
1/4 cup chicken broth
16oz sour cream
1 stick of unsalted butter, melted
1/4 cup dried parsley
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground pepper
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, if you like a little spice)
2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 cup of Italian breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a large mixing bowl, combine chicken, spaghetti, bacon, soup, broth, sour cream, butter, parsley, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, Parmesan cheese, cayenne pepper
Mix well
Spread into a lightly greased 9x13 in dish.  Top with the cheddar cheese and breadcrumbs.
Bake uncovered for 45 mins, until bubbly and brown on top.

Submitter: 
Brent Berkeypile
Description: 
A wonderful alternative to plain spaghetti or chicken.  I guarantee to be a big hit with the family.  My kids loved it!!!!
