Title:
Mixed Berry and Banana Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups hulled, roughly chopped strawberries
A handful of raspberries
1 cup blueberries
1 apple, peeled, cored, roughly chopped
2 bananas, peeled and thickly sliced
1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
Wash the berries before the bananas and apples, and add them to a large bowl. Sprinkle lemon juice over them to keep them from turning brown. Add zest and honey.  Toss gently.
Can be served immediately or refrigerated for up to 2 hours.
