Title:
Crabmeat & Sherry Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. fettuccine
1 tbs olive oil
1 cup chopped red bell pepper
1 10 oz container Alfredo sauce
2 tbs dry sherry
cayenne pepper
2 6 oz cans lump crab meat, drained and picked over
2 tbs fresh parsley
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to a boil. Add fettuccine. Cook al dente, 12 minutes. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet, add bell pepper. Cook until pepper softens, about 2 minutes. Stir in pasta sauce and sherry and season with cayenne to taste. Bring mixture to a boil, stirring. Remove from heat and fold in crab meat. Drain fettuccine, remove all water. Spoon crab meat sauce over noodles and stir to coat well. Serve at once garnishing with parsley and Parmesan.
