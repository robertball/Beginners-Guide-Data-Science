Title:
Chili Chicken Pasta Topper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978889.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Chicken with Herbs Soup
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
4 cups hot cooked corkscrew-shaped pasta

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned, stirring often.
Add soup, milk, cheese, chili powder and garlic and heat through. Serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Let the pasta cook while you stir together this creamy, spicy chicken sauce seasoned with chili powder and garlic. Serve with breadsticks and a sliced cucumber salad.
