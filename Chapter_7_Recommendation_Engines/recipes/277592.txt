Title:
Spicy Sparkling Cucumber Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7422007.jpg
Ingredients:
6 thin slices cucumber
2 ounces vodka
1 ounce lime juice
1 ounce simple syrup
3 dashes hot pepper sauce
1 cup ice, or as needed
1 cup club soda, or as needed
2 thin slices cucumber (optional)

Directions:
Muddle 6 cucumber slices in a cocktail shaker. Add vodka, lime juice, simple syrup, and hot sauce. Fill shaker with ice and shake for 30 seconds.
Fill 2 glasses with fresh ice. Strain vodka mixture into the glasses and top off with club soda. Garnish with remaining cucumber slices.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
This is a spicy yet cool and refreshing cocktail that is perfect for a hot summer day.
