Title:
Carrot-Nut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/745683.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups whole-wheat flour
1/2 cup all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup canola oil
1/3 cup honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
zest of one orange
2 tablespoons orange juice
1 1/2 cups shredded carrot
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 cup muffin pan or line with paper muffin liners.
Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt in a bowl. Whisk together egg, yogurt, oil, honey, vanilla extract, zest, orange juice and carrot in a separate bowl. Stir wet ingredients into the flour mixture until just combined. Divide batter among muffin cups.
Bake in preheated oven 20 minutes. Cool in the pans 5 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Alberta Rose
Description: 
Light textured and moist, these flavorful muffins are full of good-for-you ingredients.
