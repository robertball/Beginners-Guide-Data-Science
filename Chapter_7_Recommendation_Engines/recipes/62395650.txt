Title:
Hawaiian Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/434744.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pkgs of Hawaiian Dinner rolls (24 rolls in all)
1 to 1 1/2 pounds of shaved ham
1 lb of swiss cheese shredded fine
Sauce:
1 1/2  sticks of melted butter
1 1/2  Tablespoon  dijon mustard
1 1/2  Tablespoon  Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 Tablespoon  minced onion - chop very fine

Directions:
Cut the rolls in half and place on a cookie sheet that has sides.
Fill the rolls with ham and cheese.
Mix the ingredients for the sauce mixture.
Drizzle this mixture over the tops of the rolls.
***Cover the tops of the rolls with foil and refrigerate over night. (this is important)
The next day heat the oven to 350 degrees and bake coverd for 20 minutes.
Uncover the last 2-3 minutes till the cheese melts.

Submitter: 
Vanessa Megan
Description: 
This is an addicting sandwich recipe. It's perfect to make for picnics or potlucks. I think they taste good warm or cold.
