Title:
Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Potatoes
1/4 C. Olive Oil
1/2 t. Seasoning Salt
1/4 t. Pepper
1/4 t. Celery Salt
1/4 t. Onion Powder

Directions:
Cut potatoes in half lengthwise and then slice 1" thick. Add oil and seasonings to bowl and combine; then potatoes and coat. Arrange potatoes around meatloaf in a shallow casserole dish.
Bake at 375F.
