Title:
Festive Corn Hushpuppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833714.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ( 8.5) package cornbread mix
1 cup corn (canned or frozen and thawed)
1/3 minced red bell pepper
1/2 cup minced onion
1/2 jalapeno, seeded and minced
1 1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh herbs (your choice)
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
Oil - frying

Directions:
Combine cornbread mix, corn, peppers, onions,jalapeno, herbs,salt, and eggs until mixed well.
Heat 2 inches of oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Test the oil by dropping a small piece to see if it sizzles. It should slowly start to turn golden.
Drop batter by tablespoonfuls and fry, turning once, until golden brown.
