Title:
Grilled Turkey and Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pepper, black ground
4 slice(s) turkey, deli-style, reduced sodium
1 medium tomato(es), plum, thinly sliced (optional)
2 slice(s) cheese, cheddar, reduced-fat, each slice divided in half
2 cup(s) salad greens, baby prewashed
salt to taste
1/8 teaspoon peppercorns, black, freshly ground

Directions:
Lay turkey slices topped with cheese and tomato on broiler pan of toaster oven. Toast until cheese melts. Fold turkey over to enclose cheese and tomato, and let set.
Meanwhile, divide salad greens between two plates and sprinkle each with salt and pepper. Cut turkey and cheese into strips and serve on top or beside salad.
