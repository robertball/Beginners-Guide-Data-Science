Title:
Snickers Ice Cream Bar Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 can (1 cup) hot fudge sauce
1/2 cup peanut butter
10 ice cream sandwiches
1/2 container (12 ounces) cool whip
1/2 cup salted peanuts
1/2 cup caramel sauce

Directions:
Pour the chocolate syrup into a medium microwave safe bowl and microwave 2 minutes on high.  Do not allow to boil.  Stir peanut butter into hot chocolate until smooth.  Allow to cool to room temperature.
Line the bottom of serving dish with a layer of ice cream sandwiches.  Spread half the whipped topping over the sandwiches.  Spoon half the chocolate mixture over that.  Top with half the peanuts. Spoon half the caramel over that. Repeat layers.  Freeze until firm, 1 hour.  Cut into squares to serve.

Submitter: 
Deb Blagg
Description: 
I modified for 10 servings and added caramel topping so it became a Snickers dessert.Modified from: Hot Fudge Ice Cream Bar Dessert
