Title:
Cranberry Crunchies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup flour
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 can cranberry sauce (or 1 1/2 cups)
whipped topping, milk or ice cream

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients. Cut in butter.
Place 1/2 in bottom of greased 9"x9" pan.
Cover with cranberry sauce.
Spread rest of crumbs over top.
Bake @ 350 x 45 minutes.
Serve warm or cold, with whipped topping, milk or ice cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Cranberry tang between layers of oatmeal. A great way to use up cranberries left over from holiday meals.
