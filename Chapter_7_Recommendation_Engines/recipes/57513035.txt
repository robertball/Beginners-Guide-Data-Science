Title:
Overnight Caramel Pecan Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups milk
1/4 cup warm water
2 1/2 Tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 egg
2 1/2 Tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1teaspoon salt
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 1/4 t dry yeast
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 tablespoons light corn syrup
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Put ingredients into bread machine in the order recommeded.
Heat brown sugar and 1/2 cup butter until melted. Remove from heat; stir in corn syrup. Pour into greased 13x9 pan.  Sprinkle pecans over caramel.
Mix 1/2 cup butter, brown sugar and cinnamon.  Roll dough out into a large rectangle.  Sprinkle sugar mixture over dough.  Roll dough up and cut into 12 pieces with a serrated knife.  Place rolls in pan.  Refrigerate at least 12 hours but no longer than 48 hours.
Remove rolls from refrigerator and let stand in a warm place for at least 20 minutes.  Meanwhile, preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bake uncovered in preheated oven until golden 30 minutes. Immediately invert pan on heatproof serving plate. Wait for 1 or 2 minutes before removing pan, so that caramel drizzles over rolls.
