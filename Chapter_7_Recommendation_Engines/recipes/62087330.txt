Title:
Feta stuffed Chicken with Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
3/4 cup feta
3 tbsp fresh parsley
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1 tbsp oil
3 large tomatoes diced
1 clove garlic minced
1 hot pepper chopped
salt

Directions:
Mix together feta, herbs and oil in a boil.
Slice thick end of breasts to form a pocket. Stuff the feta mixture inside. Place chicken in a baking dish.
In another bowl, mix together tomatoes, garlic, hot pepper and salt to taste. Pour mixture on top of chicken.
Cover and bake in preheated 350F oven approximately 1 hour until chicken is cooked.  Check occasionally and add water if needed.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
Chicken is stuffed with feta and herbs, covered with tomatoes and baked. Good served with pasta or rice.  Taste great as leftovers the next day and freezes really well too.
