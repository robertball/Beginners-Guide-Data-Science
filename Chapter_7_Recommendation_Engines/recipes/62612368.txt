Title:
Beef Teriyaki and Broccoli over Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/567152.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups minute rice (cooked-either white or brown)
2 tbsp flour
2 2/3 cups hot water
1 pound boneless center chuckeye beef steak, cut into chunks
2 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp red pepper flakes
2 beef bouillon cubes
2 tbsp soy sauce
3 tbsp packed brown sugar
2 tbsp garlic, minced
1 tbsp onion, minced
1 bag of broccoli florets (steamed, ready to eat)

Directions:
Cut beef into chunks, place in bowl and toss with flour. Meanwhile, heat vegetable oil in skillet over medium heat.
In a mixing bowl combine water, ginger, red pepper flakes, beef bouillon cubes, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic and onion. Mix well and set aside.
Add beef chunks to heated skillet, cook until browned, tossing frequently with tongs (drain any excess oil before adding liquid mixture, if desired).
Add liquid mixture and cornstarch, stir well. Cover and boil for 10-12 minutes, stirring occassionally. Stir in steamed broccoli and set aside, uncovered.
Serve over the cooked rice.
