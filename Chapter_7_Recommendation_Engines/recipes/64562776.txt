Title:
Chatelaine's Sticky Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp sesame oil
1/2 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup prepared teriyaki sauce
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp minced fresh ginger
1 tbsp lime juice
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 green onions, chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander or basil (optional)
1 tbsp oil

Directions:
In a small bowl mix chili sauce, sesame oil, teriyaki sauce, garlic, ginger and lime juice. Lightly brush chicken with sauce, reserving the extra.
Heat tablespoon on oil in a large nonstick pan over medium-high heat. Fry chicken 10 minutes or until cooked through, flipping once.
Pour sauce in pan. Bring to a boil. Add sugar and herbs. Cook until slightly thickened and sticky! Stir in green onions just before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
This unique combo of ingredients produces a lip smacking sauce that's great with boiled potatoes and a cold beer.
