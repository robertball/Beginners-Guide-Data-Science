Title:
Roasted Salsa Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/640812.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cloves garlic, peeled and left whole
2 large onions, peeled and left whole
12 medium to large tomatillos, peeled and whole
2 jalapeno peppers
3 anaheim peppers
2 Tbsp lemon juice
sprinkling of salt, optional
1 bunch of cilantro

Directions:
Set oven to broil.
Lay all vegetables on large cookie sheet and broil until tomatillos are oozing
Remove tomatillos and garlic from cookie sheet, turn peppers and onions and return to over to continue broiling.
After peppers are blackened, remove all from oven
Once cool enough to touch, peels away blackened parts from vegetables as much as you can without taking off to much flesh.
Cut off the bottom stemmy parts of cilantro
Add all ingredients to the food processor.
Pulse until well combined and chopped.
Refrigerate until cold and serve.
