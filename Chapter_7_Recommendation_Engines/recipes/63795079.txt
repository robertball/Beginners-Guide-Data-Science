Title:
Creamy Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Russet Potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters
2 cans of Chicken Broth
1/4 stick of butter
3-4 oz of cream cheese
1/4 Cup of Milk
Reserve broth to taste

Directions:
Put potatoes in pan and add broth to pan. Bring to a gentle rolling boil and cook for 20-25 mins. Better to slightly overcook than undercook

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
Very east creamy mashed potato recipe that takes from the best recipes on the web to accommodate my families taste. Please adjust it to suit your own tastes.
