Title:
Beef/Cabbage Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2  pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
2 carrots sliced
2 cloves garlic or garlic salt
chili powder to taste
2 squirts of ketchup
1/2 head cabbage, shredded
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
2 cans of water
salt to taste
try star anise, clove, espelette, peppe, oregano
as well as a handfull of mushrooms or rice

Directions:
In a large pot over medium high heat, combine ground beef, garlic and onion. Saute for 10 minutes, until beef is browned and onion is tender.
Add the rest. Mix together well and reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, until cabbage is at desired tenderness.

Submitter: 
tommy boy
Description: 
This meal is fast and inexpensive, yet it's very filling. It combines ground beef with cabbage in a tomato base. I like to serve it with corn and bread.Modified from: Thirty Minute Meal
