Title:
Cranberry, Feta and Walnut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) bag mixed salad greens
1/4 cup Good Seasons Salad Dressing Italian Mix made with Balsamic Vinegar and Olive Oil
1/4 cup walnut pieces
2 tablespoons dried cranberries
4 ounces crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Toss the salad greens, vinaigrette, walnut pieces, and dried cranberries together in a large bowl. Divide the salad into individual salad bowls or plates. Garnish each salad with crumbles of the feta cheese and serve!
