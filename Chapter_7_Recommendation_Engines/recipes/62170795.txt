Title:
Fall Harvest Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box pound cake mix
3 eggs
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons milk
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1 (15 ounce) can of pumpkin
3/4 cup sugar
1 can sweetened condensed milk
4 tablespoons pumpkin pie spice
1 cups milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup crushed nut topping
1/2 cup caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Butter a 9x13 baking dish.
Combine cake mix, 1 egg, milk, and butter until well mixed. Spoon into baking dish and press evenly into pan. Bake for 10 minutes and remove.
Beat cream cheese, pumpkin, sugar, condensed milk, and pumpkin pie spice until smooth. Add 2 eggs and mix well. Pour over crust. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until center is set. Remove and cool in refrigerator for 4-6 hours.
Top with chocolate chips and nuts. Drizzle with caramel and serve.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
Pumpkin cheesecake is topped with chocolate chips, nuts, and caramel to make beautiful, tasty bars that are great for the holidays!
