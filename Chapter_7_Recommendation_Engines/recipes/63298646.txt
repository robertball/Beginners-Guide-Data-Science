Title:
Shot Glass Almond Joy Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/870454.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup miniature chocolate chips
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese-fat free
8 oz. part skim ricotta cheese
1/2 tsp. pure almond
1/2 tsp. coconut extract
3/4 cup white sugar
Shredded sweetened coconut - garnish

Directions:
Line the bottom of 12 shot glasses with miniature chocolate chips.
Mix cream cheese, ricotta, extracts and sugar together in a bowl until well blended.
Layer cheese mixture into the shot glasses, alternating with layers of mini chocolate chips. Garnish with more mini chocolate chips and shredded coconut.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
Cream cheese and ricotta whipped with sugar and extracts is layered with chocolate chips and topped with shredded coconut in this recipe for small sweet bites served in shot glasses.Modified from: Shot Glass Cream Cheese Dessert
