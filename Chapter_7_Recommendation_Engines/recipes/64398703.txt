Title:
Curried Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups (370 gm) Quinoa
3 Cups (750 ml) Vegtable broth, low sodium
1 Cup (150 gm) Cucumber, sliced
1 Cup (150 gm) Mango
1/4 Cup (40 gm) Cranberries,dried
2 tbsp (30 ml) Mint, fresh chopped
2 tbsp (30 gm) Almonds, sliced & toasted
1 tbsp (15 ml) Curry powder
1/2 Cup (125 ml) Yogourt, Plain
2 tbsp (30 ml) Canola Oil
2 tbsp (30 ml) Rice Wine Vinegar
2 tbsp (30 ml) Honey
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
To make dressing, whisk together the curry powder, yogourt, canola oil, rice wine vinegar, honey and season with salt & pepper.
Meanwhile, bring vegetable broth to a boil.
Add quinoa and 1/2 tsp salt and simmer covered for 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
Turn off heat and let quinoa sit covered for 10 minutes then fluff with a fork.
While quinoa cooks, prepare the remaining ingredients.
Let quinoa cool slightly, toss with dressing.
Transfer quinoa to a serving bowl and top with cucumber, cranberries, mint, and almonds.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Submitter:

Description: 
Mango, cucumber and mint are just a few of the ingredients that make up this summery quinoa salad.  Topped with a curried yogourt dressing, curry powder and lime zest, this is a side dish that tastes great with just about everything.
