Title:
Jo's Punch Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 liters pineapple juice
3 liters orange juice
3 liters fruit punch
2 liters ginger ale
2 (12 fluid ounce) cans frozen pink lemonade concentrate, thawed

Directions:
Stir pineapple juice, orange juice, fruit punch, ginger ale, and lemonade concentrate together in a large punch bowl.
