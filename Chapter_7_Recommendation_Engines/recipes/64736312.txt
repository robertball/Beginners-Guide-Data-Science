Title:
Piña Colada Wild Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6308801.jpg
Ingredients:
To one 64 ounce blender container
13.5 ounce can of coconut milk
13.5 ounces of water
12 dried dates, pitted
3 T chia seeds
Greens*, packed in blender until 3/4 full
Frozen Pineapple, to top edge of blender
2 ripe bananas

Directions:
Place ingredients in blender in order from top to bottom with holding the bananas
Place lid firmly on blender and start at lowest speed and increasing to full speed
Once smoothie is being blended well open feeding hole on lid of blender and add both bananas
Blend for 1-2 minuets until smooth
*Greens I use kale, spinach, wood sorrel, dandelions, plantain, sheep sorrel, lambs quarters, dock, cats ear, stinging nettles or other non-peppery or mustard greens (you can use one type of greens or combine them as you like)
