Title:
Hot Pita Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One red bell pepper, chopped
One cup of chopped broccoli
1/8 cup real bacon bits
One cup mozzerella cheese, shredded
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, shredded
One garlic clove, minced
2 tsp ranch seasoning
1/2 cup mayonnaise
pita pockets
butter (optional)

Directions:
Chop the red bell pepper and broccoli into small pieces, set aside.
Combine the mayonnaise, garlic and rance in a medium bowl.
Add cheeses to the mayonnaise mixture.
Stir in the vegetables and bacon bits.
Spoon mixture into the pita pockets and spread a small amount of butter onto the outside of the pockets.
Place the pockets on a skillet or griddle at medium-high heat and allow to lightly brown and then turn and let the other side lightly brown.
Cheese should be melted.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
Red bell peppers, broccoli, fresh garlic combined with ranch seasoning, parmesan cheese and mozzerella cheese grilled in a pita pocket will become an instant favorite! Perfect for a quick lunch.
