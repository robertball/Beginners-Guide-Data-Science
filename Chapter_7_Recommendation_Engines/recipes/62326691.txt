Title:
Donna Hay's Lemongrass Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stalks lemongrass, chopped into 1-inch pieces
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1/4 cup dark soy sauce
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 teaspoons fish sauce
8 boneless chicken thighs (about 2 pounds total)
Donna Hay's Hoisin Peanut Dipping Sauce

Directions:
Place lemongrass and garlic in bowl of small food processor and process until finely chopped. Add soy sauce, vegetable oil and fish sauce. Pulse to combine. Pour over chicken; cover and refrigerate 1 hour.
Remove chicken from refrigerator and allow to come to room temperature. Grill over high heat to desired doneness (about 2 to 3 minutes a side).

Submitter: 
Gregg Hollander

A light Thai-style marinade makes tasty chicken in a snap
