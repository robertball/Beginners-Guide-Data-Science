Title:
5-Minute Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/375107.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2/3 cup vegetable oil (divided)
1/2 cup boiling water (measure accurately)
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 1/3 cup flour
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to  350ยบ. Stir cocoa and baking soda together in a mixing bowl. Blend in 1/3 cup vegetable oil. Add boiling water; stir until mixture thickens. Stir in sugar, eggs and remaining 1/3 cup vegetable oil; stir until smooth. Add flour, vanilla, salt and walnuts; blend completely.
Pour into a 13x9-inch baking pan which is lined with parchment paper. Bake at 350ยบ for 25-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely and frost or sprinkle with confectioners' sugar, if desired.

Description: 
This scratch recipe is almost as quick and easy to make as a box mix, but the brownies taste 100 times better; incredibly moist and chocolatey.
