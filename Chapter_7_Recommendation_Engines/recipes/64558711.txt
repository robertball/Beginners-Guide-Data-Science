Title:
Low-Carb Parmesan Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef or turkey Italian sausage
1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
6 tablespoons crushed pork rinds
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried minced onion
1/8 teaspoon dried oregano
6 tablespoons water or milk

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well.
Divide mixture into four equal portions, then divide each part into four equal portions.  there should be 16 fairly equal portions.
Roll each portion into a ball and bake on a parchment-lined cookie sheet at 350 degrees until cooked through, approximately 20 minutes.
Serve with your favorite pasta and sauce.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
Who says you can't have meatballs when you're on a low-carb diet?
