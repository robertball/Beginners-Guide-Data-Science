Title:
Grilled Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/691919.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
5 sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices (5 lb.)
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
Salt and pepper
2 tablespoons fresh flat-leaf parsley or cilantro, finely chopped

Directions:
Preheat a gas grill to high. Place butter in a large serving bowl.
Place sweet potatoes on another large bowl and toss with oil, salt and pepper. Lower grill heat to medium, place potatoes around edges of grill and cook, turning once, until soft when pierced with a skewer, 8 to 10 minutes total.
Transfer potatoes to serving bowl with butter, sprinkle with parsley and gently toss to coat. Season with more salt if desired; serve warm.
