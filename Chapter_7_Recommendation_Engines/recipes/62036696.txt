Title:
Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg of rice, corn or wheat chex cereal (2 different types of the three)
1 pkg of pretzel sticks
1 can of potato sticks
1 can of cashews
1 t. celery salt
1 t. garlic salt
1 t. onion salt
2 sticks of melted butter or margarine

Directions:
Mix cereals into a large bowl (huge).  Mix salts together.  Sprinkle melted butter and mixed salts alternatingly.  Stiring after each addition.  Cook at 300 degrees for about 30 minutes stirring several times.  Cool.  Store in airtight tin or large plastic bag.

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
I grew up with this all through the holidays.  Make a batch and it will last for all the holidays, open houses, drop in's, etc.  But you will find that you can't keep your hand out of it.
