Title:
Chicken Cacciatore Italian Family Favorite Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken whole legs ( thigh and all)...thighs have the best flavor after wings
4 gloves of garlic minced /sliced
1 large onion- sliced
1 pepper - cut up
mushrooms - fresh or a can of mushrooms
salt and pepper
Basil
2 large cans tomatoes - diced or whole - or  can make with a jar of Ragu sauce
olive oil - 1/4 cup
1 can tomato paste ( save to thicken the sauce in the end)
serve over pasta

Directions:
in med size roaster or 9 x 13 pan place chicken...drizzle olive oil over...add onions, peppers mushrooms and rest of ingredients ( do not add can of paste)...cover with tinfoil or roaster lid
Bake 350* 1 1/2 - 2 hrs...till chicken tender
remove the chicken and place pan on stove top...add the tomato paste and cook med heat till all paste is mixed in and thickens the sauce
serve over pasta...grated parm cheese

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
the family Sunday favorite...one dish pop in the oven and is ready when you come home from church
