Title:
Froggie Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon lime sherbert
6 ounces of frozen lime-ade concentrate
2 liters lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Put frozen sherbert and frozen lime-ade in a large punch bowl. Poiur lemon-lime soda over sherbert and concentrate, leave for 15 minutes. Stir gently. Serves 16 2 ounce serving.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
The perfect green punch for a frog/lizard theme party. I plan to serve this at my daughter's Princess and the Frog party. From Paula Deen's cookbook for children.
