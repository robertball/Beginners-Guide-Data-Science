Title:
Chicken Salad( basic) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken boiled and boned
celery
salt
white pepper
Mayonnaise
Lemon juice

Directions:
Boil the chicken and bone,while the bird is cooking chop the celery,if you have for example, 4 pound of boned chicken, then you need 2 pound of celery.
after the chicken is cooked chop the meat and mix with the celery enough mayo and season with white pepper and salt then add lemon juice ( the lemon juice acts as a preservative)
