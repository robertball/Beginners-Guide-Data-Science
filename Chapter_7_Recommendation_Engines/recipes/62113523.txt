Title:
Stuffed Green Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large green peppers
1 medium onion chopped
1 clove garlic chopped
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. spicy ground sausage (e.g. Jimmy Dean)
3-4 10 1/2 oz size tomato soup
1 cup rice
2 cups water

Directions:
Prepare peppers by cutting off the tops and removing the ribs and seeds but keeping the pepper whole.  Cut up tops may be added with the onion and garlic when browning the meat.
Chop onion and garlic and saute with the ground beef and sausage until meat is no longer pink.  Drain.
Meanwhile heat rice and water, covered, until boiling reduce heat and simmer 15-18 minutes.  Add to drained meat mixture. Add one can tomato soup to the meat mixture and stuff into prepared peppers.
Place peppers in a large pot in a single layer.  Mix 2-3 cans of tomato soup with 1-2 cans of water to make a broth for the peppers to cook in, pour into pot over peppers.  Heat to boiling simmer until peppers are tender about 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kelly Nagy Cramer
Description: 
Fresh pepppers stuffed with a flavorful filling of ground beef, sausage, and rice, bathed in a rich tomato sauce.
