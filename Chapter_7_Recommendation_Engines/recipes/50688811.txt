Title:
Strawberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup olive oil
1/2 pint fresh strawberries, halved
6 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried tarragon
1/4 teaspoon white sugar
1 tablespoon dijon mustard

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, mix olive oil, strawberries, balsamic vinegar, salt, pepper, tarragon and sugar. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Soncea Braden-McCann
Description: 
A perfect dressing to pour over a spinach salad with fresh strawberries, toasted almonds and crumbled blue cheese! Mmm!Modified from: Strawberry Vinaigrette
