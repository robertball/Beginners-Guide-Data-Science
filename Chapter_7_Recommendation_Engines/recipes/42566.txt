Title:
Vegetable Rice Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985823.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked long grain rice
2 1/4 cups water
2 tablespoons onion or vegetable soup mix
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups frozen corn, peas or mixed vegetables

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the rice, water, soup mix and salt; bring to a boil. Add the vegetables; return to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Cook until the rice and vegetables are tender.

Submitter:

Description: 
Vegetable Rice Medley combines rice with convenient frozen vegetables.
