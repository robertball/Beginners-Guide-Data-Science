Title:
Spinach, Feta and Sundried Tomato Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/463019.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
1 package fresh spinach
1 Tbs canola or olive oil
1 clove garlic, crushed
4 oz red. fat feta cheese
1 oz sundried tomatoes, chopped (not in oil)
1/2 tsp dried basil
pinch each salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat oil over medium flame
Add spinach and garlic and sautee until spinach has wilted
Turn off heat and allow spinach to cool; place about 1/3 spinach in a mixing bowl
Add feta, tomatoes and spices and mix
Divide spinach mixture between the chicken breasts and roll pounded breasts over filling
Place in baking dish sprayed with cooking spray and sprinkle with salt and pepper
Bake in 400 degree over for 30 minutes or until chicken has cooked through and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
kashmirkat
Description: 
I put this together because I wanted to use up some sun dried tomatoes we had left over from something else. It was even better the next day on a salad :) You'll only use 1/3 of the sauteed spinach in the chicken, I have the rest as a side dish.
