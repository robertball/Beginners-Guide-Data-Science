Title:
Cabbage Kielbasa Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large red onion, sliced
1 large green pepper, julienned
1 large red pepper, julienned
2 T butter
1 pound smoked kielbasa or Polish sausage, cut into 1" slices
1 16oz package coleslaw mix
1 t garlic powder
1/2 t pepper

Directions:
In large skillet saute onion and peppers in butter until tender.
Add remaining ingredients.
Cook and stir for 6-8 mins until heated through.
