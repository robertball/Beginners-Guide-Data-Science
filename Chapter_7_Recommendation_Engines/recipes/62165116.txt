Title:
Creamy Dijon Stuffed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
8 oz. package cream cheese, softened
1 Tablespoon Dijon Mustard
Egg
Italian Bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Pound out chicken breasts flat.
Mix cream cheese and dijon mustard.
Spoon cream cheese mixture on chicken breast.
Roll and secure with a wooden toothpick.
Dip chicken in beaten egg and roll in bread crumbs.
Bake 30 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Junebug's Mom
Description: 
Breaded chicken breasts stuffed with a mixture of cream cheese and dijon mustard.
