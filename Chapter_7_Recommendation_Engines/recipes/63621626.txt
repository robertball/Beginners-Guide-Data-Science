Title:
Skinny Vegetable Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1007348.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked brown rice
2 egg whites, scrambled
1 whole egg, scrambled
1 tbsp Smart Balance oil
1/2 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, diced
5 scallions, chopped
1/2 cup carrots, chopped
1/2 cup broccoli, chopped
1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
cooking spray
2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce (or more to taste)
1 tbsp sesame oil
salt and fresh pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk eggs and egg whites, season with salt and pepper. In a hot wok, spray a little oil and cook the eggs. When cooked, remove from pan and set aside.
Let the wok get really hot. Add oil and cook onions, scallions, carrots, broccoli, peas and garlic for about 30-60 seconds, careful not to burn. Add brown rice and stir well a few minutes to heat through. Add cooked egg along with soy sauce and sesame oil, mixing well for about 3-4 minutes.
