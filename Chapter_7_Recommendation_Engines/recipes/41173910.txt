Title:
CHICKEN - CILANTRO WRAPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BBQ chicken, left over is best
Wraps, multi-grain or Whole Wheat
Bag of shredded cabbage
Fresh Cilantro
Shreded Mexi-blend cheese
Parmasaen or Queso Fresco
Advacado
Limes
Herdez tomatillo salsa

Directions:
Preperare filling:
Chop leftover chicken
Slice advacado, squeeze on lime
Chop bunch of cilantro
Grill tortillas on stove
Add approx. 1 tbsp. each:
chicken
cheese
coleslaw
herdez
Add slice advacado, sprinkle of parmasaen and fresh cilantro

Quick and easy spicey wrap that uses left over grilled chicken
