Title:
Krista's Chocolate Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 sticks of butter
1-1/2 cups self-rising flour
1-1/2 cups sugar
1-1/2 tsp vanilla
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup sugar
5 Tbs cocoa
1-1/2 cups cold water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter in a 9 x 13 pan in the oven.
Mix together the flour, 1-1/2 cups sugar, vanilla, and milk. Pour over melted butter.
Mix 3/4 cup sugar and cocoa.  Sprinkle on top of flour mixture.
Slowly pour cold water on top.
Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.
Remove from oven, let stand for about 15 minutes, and then serve.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
A yummy dessert that satisfies any chocoholic's sweet tooth.  Serve with ice cream  or whipped cream.
