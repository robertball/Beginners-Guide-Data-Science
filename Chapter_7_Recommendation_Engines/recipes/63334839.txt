Title:
Corn Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie crust
1 1/3 cups half and half
3 eggs
3 Tbsp butter
1/2 small (or 1/4 medium) onion, minced
1 Tbsp flour
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp white pepper
2 cups corn kernels
6 oz swiss cheese, shredded

Directions:
Place pie crust in pie pan and line with double layer of foil.  Bake for 5 minutes at 400.  Remove foil and bake another 5 minutes.  Saute onion in butter until very soft.  Stir in corn and set aside.  In small bowl, toss cheese with flour.  In large bowl, whisk eggs with half and half, brown sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon, salt and pepper.  Spread corn mixture onto pie crust.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Pour in egg mixture.  Bake at 325 for 45-50 minutes.  Cool on wire rack at least 15 minutes before cutting.
