Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb stew meat
1 medium onion
1-2 cloves garlic
1 can tomatoes
1 can kidney bean
1 can brown bean
1 can tomato sauce
2 Tbsp Soya sauce
1 Tbsp Worchershire
1 1/2 Tbsp Chili powder
1/2 tsp each cumin, thyme

Directions:
Brown Stew meat and remove
Cook the sliced or diced onion and garlic
Put into a crock pot and add all other ingredients.
Mix well and cook on high 6 hours
Let cool and refrigerate overnight to blend flavors better
