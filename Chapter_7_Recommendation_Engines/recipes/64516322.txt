Title:
KFC 'Copycat' Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2822561.jpg
Ingredients:
***11 SPICES***
1 teaspoon ground Oregano
1 tablespoon ground Basil
1 teaspoon ground Tarragon
1 tablespoon ground Marjoram
1 tablespoon ground Sage
1 tablespoon ground Black pepper
1 tablespoon ground White pepper
1 teaspoon ground Chili
1 tablespoon ground Paprika
3 tablespoon ground Salt
1 tablespoon Onion Powder
3 tablespoon granulated garlic
3 Cups All-purpose Flour
***BATTER***
2 eggs
1 Cup of buttermilk

Directions:
Mix the 11 spices and flour into a bowl and stir around to make sure all the spices are mixed through the flour evenly
Dip and coat the chicken into egg mixture
Dip and coat the chicken into the spice mixture
Repeat steps 2 and 3
Deep fry the chicken for 17 minutes at around 160 degrees Celsius (or 320 degrees Farenheight)
