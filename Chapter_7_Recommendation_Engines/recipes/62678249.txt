Title:
Dilly Bread - Reservation Inn Approved Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599949.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chives or chopped Onion
2 Tablespoons butter or margarine
1 Pkg of dry yeast
1 Cup of cottage cheese
2 Tablespoons of sugar
2 Tablespoons of dill weed (fresh if possible)
1 Teaspoon of salt
1/4 Teaspoon of baking soda
1 egg
2 1/2 cups of unbleached flour

Directions:
Add 1 package of yeast to 1/4 cup of warm water and set aside to froth up
In a saucepan melt butter and cook chives or onions until tender.
Add cottage cheese, sugar, dill, salt &amp; baking soda to saucepan and mix well
Put 1/2 of flour and prepared yeast into Kitchenaid bowl and mix.  Add cottage cheese mixture and egg.  Mix well.  Add more flour to make a stiff dough.  Cover and let rise until double in bulk.
Stir down and turn out onto well floured counter and knead. Shape and put into greased pan.  Let rise until double in bulk.
Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees) 40-50 minutes.  Cover with foil for the last 15 minutes if it is getting too brown.  Remove from oven.  Brush with melted butter &amp; sprinkle with course salt.  Remove from pan.  Let cool.

Submitter: 
Tiki Sam
Description: 
Delicious bread made even better with fresh dill...made famous by my Grandma Fanelli at East Bay High School's Reservation Inn Restaurant.
