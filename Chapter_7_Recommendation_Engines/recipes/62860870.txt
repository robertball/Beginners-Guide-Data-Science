Title:
Parmesan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
1 1/2 Cup grated parmesan cheese
2 Eggs beaten with 1 tablspoon water
Oil for frying

Directions:
In a large fry pan, hald an inch of oil over medium heat.
Dip each chicken strip into the egg mixture, then Coat with parmesan cheese.
Fry the strips in oil for about 4 minute on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.

Description: 
Low carb chicken strips coated in a crispy parmesan cheese crust.
