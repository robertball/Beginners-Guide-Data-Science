Title:
Arlene's Chicken Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10oz chopped frozen spinach, cooked and drained
2 T butter
3 T flour
1 tsp Accent
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
2 cups whole mile
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
2 cups cooked white meat chicken chunks
2 slices bread, torn and crumbled

Directions:
Place cooked and drained spinach to the side.
Bubble butter and flour for one minute in saucepan.  Add Accent, salt, cayenne pepper, and milk, stirring until mixture thickens.  Add parmesan cheese and chicken chunks.
Butter and 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.  Place prepared spinach on bottom. Pour chicken mixture over spinach.  Top with torn bread and dot with butter.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.

Wonderful texture and delicious.  Great with rice, crisp salad and a jello salad.
