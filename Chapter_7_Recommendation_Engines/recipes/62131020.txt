Title:
Easy Warm Chick Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14oz. cans, drained chick peas
1/2 cup diced red onion
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp fresh chopped parsley
1 tsp fresh chopped fresh mint
1 tsp red pepper flakes
salt and pepper taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil over medium heat with add red onion.  When onions have softened add chick peas and garlic.  Let heat thourghly, but do not allow garlic to brown about 5 minutes.Add red peppers, lemon juice, salt and pepper.  Cook three more minutes.  Toss in mint and parsley and serve hot.

SHUNPR
Description: 
A yummy warm salad perfect with grilled chicken or fish.  So easy, so good!!
