Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lb. boneless chuck roast cubed
3 tsp. olive oil
4 cups boiling water
2 tsp Better than Bullion Beef Base
1 1/2 Tbsp Better than Bullion Low Sodium Chicken Base
1 bay leaf
2 cloves garlic, smashed
2 small onions quartered
3 carrots cut into 1 inch pieces
4 medium red potatoes peeled and quartered
1/2 tsp black pepper and salt to taste
3 tsp corn starch
3 tsp cold water

Directions:
Heat olive oil in heavy pot or Dutch oven and brown the meat over moderate heat. Remove meat as necessary to brown remaining meat.
Return all meat and juices to the pot. Add a little boiling water and scrape up any brown bits on bottom of pan, add remaining water, beef and chicken base and mix well.
Add bay leaf and garlic, bring to a boil. Lower heat, cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
After 1 1/2 hours. Mix the 3 tsp. corn starch to 3 tsp. cold water add to simmering stew, mix well. Add carrots, onion, potatoes and pepper mix well, cover and simmer additional 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
Delicious beef stew! Do make this stew in the morning or the day before, so it can get thick and flavorful.
