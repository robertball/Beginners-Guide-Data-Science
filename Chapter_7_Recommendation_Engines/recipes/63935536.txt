Title:
Honey Rice Cider Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tablespoons Rice Cider Dressing (no seasoning)
3 Tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup honey

Directions:
Measure ingredients into blender and blend until well combined. Pour it into a shaker/blender cup. This allows you to shake it up if the dressing separates before you've used it all.
Serve over freshly chopped veggies or any salad of your choice.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
A light dressing to splash over veggies to give a little flavor boost.
