Title:
Hungarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1099644.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef, 1/3 lb. of ground pork, 5 pre-fried Italian sausages diced afterwards, 1 medium organic onion, 1 796 ml. can of organic tomatoes, 1 398 ml. can of tomato sauce, 19 fl oz. of mixed beans or bean medley, 2 or 3 table spoons of extra virgin olive oil, 1/2 cup of organic ketchup,
Spice Mix: 2 tablespoons of dried onion flakes, 2 teaspoons of onion powder, 2 teaspoons of Californian garlic powder, teaspoon of black pepper, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika, 1 tablespoon regular paprika, 1 tablespoon TEJA brand Hungarian paprika, 1 tablespoon SZEGED brand Hungarian paprika, 1/2 TEASPOON of cmin or chili powder, 1 teaspoon of salt.

Directions:
Add olive oil to pot and add ground beef and pork to the heat and brown with onion. drizzle some of the spice mix on top of the browned meat and let sweat into meat. After meat is browned nicely, add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, ketchup, diced Italian sausage,  and bean medley, mix well. Then add remainder of dried spice mix. mix well and bring to a boil. Then, let simmer for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Jasen Richie
Description: 
The lack of traditional chili powder and or cumnin in large amounts is what makes this dish more Hungarian. Being Hungarian, I put paprikas in almost everything!
