Directions:
Mix all ingredients except the pork steaks, salt and pepper, add to gallon size bag, put in the pork steaks, seal and rub the marinade around. Marinate 2-3 hours in refrigerator. Remove from refrigerator, light gas grill, salt and pepper steaks, grill to desired doneness. Rest 10 minutes and plate.
