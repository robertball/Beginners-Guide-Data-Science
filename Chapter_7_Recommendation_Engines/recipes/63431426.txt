Title:
Dumpling Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbl Light Soy Sauce
3 Tbl Dark Soy Sauce
3 Tbl Red Wine or Red Rice Vinegar
1 Tsp Hot Chili Oil
1/2 Tsp Granulated Sugar
1 Clove Garlic, Chopped
1 Tsp Minced Ginger
Pinch of Chopped Scallions

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Store in sealed container in refrigerator for 1 hour to give flavors chance to blend.

Submitter: 
Rick Pisciotta
Description: 
Great for steamed or fried dumplings, egg rolls, spring rolls!
