Title:
Broccoli with Buttery Crumbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 ounces frozen broccoli, flowerettes
1 cup butter, divided
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
2 cup croûtons

Directions:
Prepare broccoli according to package directions.  Drain well.
Melt butter in microwave.  Pour half of melted butter into broccoli and season with seasoned salt.
Place in oven safe casserole and top with crushed croûtons, mixed with remaining butter.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until hot and croûtons are lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
This quick and easy dish even gets picky kids to try broccoli!
