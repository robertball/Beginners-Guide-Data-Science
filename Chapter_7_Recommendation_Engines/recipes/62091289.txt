Title:
Hot Italian Turkey Sausage Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package hot italian turkey sausage
5 flour tortillas (cut into quarters)
1/2 of a red onion (chopped)
5 slices of american cheese

Directions:
Slice open each sausage, remove casing and set aside.
Use cooking spray on a large skillet and fry tortilla wedges until they are crisp and light brown.
Set aside tortillas after they are cooked.
Place all of your sausage into a medium-high heat frying pan.
After three minutes add red onion.
Once sausage is cooked (approximately 12 minutes)add cheese to the mixture.
Stir until cheese is completely melted.
Spoon into center of a dish and surround with your homemade tortilla chips.
ENJOY!!
