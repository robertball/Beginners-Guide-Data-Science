Title:
HCG Crab Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769129.jpg
Ingredients:
100 grams crab meat (~1 can)
1 cup tomatoes chopped
2 cups vegetable broth (or substitute 1 cup water for 1 cup broth, or less for a thicker soup)
1 tablespoon onion minced
1 clove garlic crushed and minced
1 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
1 bay leaf
1 tablespoon milk (optional)
Cayenne pepper to taste
Salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Puree tomatoes and broth in a food processor or blender.
Heat in small saucepan
Add the crab and spices and simmer for 20-30 minutes stirring frequently.
