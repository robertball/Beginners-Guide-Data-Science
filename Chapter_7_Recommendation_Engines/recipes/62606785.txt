Title:
Tagliatelle with Salmon and Zucchini Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563189.jpg
Ingredients:
90 grams smoked salmon cut up in small squares
300grams tagliatelle pasta
1 small onion
1 small bouquet of chopped Marjoram
250ml of creme fraiche for cooking (not sweet)
50grams of butter
100grams cubed zucchini
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large non stick wock add butter and fry onion, salmon and cubed zucchini for 5 minutes stirring often, add creme fraiche and cook for 2/3 minutes more.
In the meantime add salt to water and when it boils put in the pasta (follow directions for cooking time on the package), strain pasta when it is "Al Dente", add Salmon and Zucchini Sauce
Add chopped Marjoram and serve while piping hot.
