Title:
Carrot Ginger Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 scallions very thinly sliced
3 TBS. white wine vinegar
6 TBS. veggie oil
salt and pepper
1 - 10 oz package of shredded carrots
1 TBS. grated fresh ginger

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients in a small bowl to make the dressing.
Pour over slaw and toss to blend. This dish can be made an hour ahead.
