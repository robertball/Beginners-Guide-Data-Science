Title:
Crockpot Deep-Dish Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 medium carrot, chopped
1 medium celery stalk, chopped
1 jar (4.5 oz) sliced mushrooms, drained
1 tsp. poutry seasoning
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 Tbsp. water
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 soup can of water
1 1/2 cups frozen mixed vegetables, thawed and
drained
1 refrigerated pie crust (from 15 oz box), softened
as directed on box
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Spray 4-quart slow cooker with nonstick spray. In cooker, mix chicken, onion, carrot, celery, mushrooms, poultry seasoning, salt, pepper and 2 Tbsp. water. Cover and cook on Low for 5-6 hours.
2.Stir in soup, 1 soup can of water and mixed vegetables. Increase heat to High. Cover and cook about 20 minutes, or until heated through.
3. Meanwhile, heat oven to 450 degrees. Remove pie crust from pouch; unroll on work surface. With cookie cutter, cut 12 rounds from crust, rerollong once; discard scraps. Place pie rounds on ungreased cookie sheet. Sprinkle evenly with Parmesan cheese. Bake about 8 minutes or until rounds are golden brown and crisp. Immediately remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack.
4. Top each serving of pot pie with 2 baked crusts.
