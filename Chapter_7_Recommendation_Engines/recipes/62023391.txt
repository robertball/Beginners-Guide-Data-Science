Title:
Easy Italian Chicken Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chicken
2/3 cups dry bread crumbs
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon  garlic pepper
1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese
1 egg slightly beaten
1 jar favorite spaghetti sauce
hot cooked pasta

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.Combine chicken, breadcrumbs, onion, basil, garlic pepper,  cheese and egg in a bowl and mix well. Form into 8 balls . Place on baking sheet  and bake 10 minutes turn and bake 10 more minutes. Remove from oven place meatballs in sauce and simmer for 20 minutes. Serve over hot cooked pasta. Top with more grated cheese. Makes 8 meatballs
