Title:
Butterscotch Toffee Bits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1117871.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb (2 cubes or 1 cup) unsalted butter
1/2 lb (1 cup) cup raw sugar or evaporated cane juice
7 grams fine sea salt
1 tablespoon molasse, unsulfered (NOT blackstrap)

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium sized pot. Once butter is melted ann the sugar, salt, and molasses. Increase heat to medium high and stir constantly until candy reaches 305 - 310 degrees. Pour onto a greesed cookie sheet and let set until cool.
