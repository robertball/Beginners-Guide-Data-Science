Title:
Mexican Spaghetti Soup Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/405320.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Lb un-cooked spaghetti noodles
1- 16oz can tomato sauce
1 Pinch Mexican Oregano
1 Pinch Cumin
1 Pinch Marjoram
1 Pinch Ground Cayenne Pepper
1 Tablespoon Chicken Bouillon Powder
2 Cups Water
6 Tablespoons Sour Cream
3 Tablespoons Cojita Cheese

Directions:
Follow directions on package for noodles.  Once noodles are done drain water and place them back into pan.
Add 2 cups water, tomato sauce, mexican oregano, marjoram, cumin, cayenne pepper, chicken powder to noodles. Stir to combine. Heat on medium heat until warm.  About 5-8 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Dish noodles and some sauce into each bowl.  Top with 1 tablespoon sour cream and 1/2 tablespoon cojita cheese.  Stir together and enjoy with tortillas.
