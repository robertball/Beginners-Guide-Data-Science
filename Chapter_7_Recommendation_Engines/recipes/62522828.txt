Title:
Liquored up yams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cans of yams
1 cup of sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup of butter, melted
1/2 cup of milk
1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
1 cup of chopped pecans
1/4 cup of rum or butter shots liquor
topping
1 cup of brown sugar
1 cup of rolled oats
1 cup of chopped pecans
1/2 cup of butter

Directions:
pre heat oven to 350. Drain yams and place in baking dish. Mix sugar, eggs, butter, milk, vanilla, pecans, and alcohol together until smooth and 'frothy'. Pour mixture over yams. Mix topping ingredients together and sprinkle over yams. Bake for about 45 minutes until topping is brown and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Christine Douglas- Kaiser
Description: 
Looking for a candied yam recipe that didn't have marshmallows in it was hard. So I took a sweet potato casserole recipe and tweeked it.
