Title:
Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe nectarines
3 handfulls or more Mixed green salad with baby spinach (can get alreaddy made)
1 small package of herbed unripened goat cheese
1/2 cup of pecans
Balsamic Vinegar and olive oil dressing to taste

Directions:
Place the green salad and baby spinach in a boal. Placed diced nectarines( skin on) on top with peacans and flake goat cheese over top. Then drizzel the entire mixture with balsamic vinegar and olive oil dressing. It's soo easy and sooo good!!!
