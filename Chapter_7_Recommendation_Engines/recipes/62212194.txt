Title:
Old Fashioned Soft Pumpkin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ½ C flour
1 ½ C sugar
1 large egg
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
½ tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp salt
½ C butter, softened
1 C pumpkin
1 tsp vanilla extract
Glaze (recipe below)

Directions:
Preheat over @ 350. Grease baking sheets.
COMBINE dry ingredients in a medium
bowl. Beat sugar & butter, in a large bowl,
till well blended. Beat in pumpkin, egg, &
vanilla till smooth. Gradually beat in flour
mixture.  Drop by rounded tsps onto
baking sheets.
BAKE 15-18 min. or till edges are firm.
Cool on baking sheets 2 min, then remove
to wire racks.  Cool completely & glaze.
GLAZE: combine 2 C sifted powdered
sugar, 3 Tbsp milk, 1 Tbsp melted butter,
& 1 tsp vanilla, till smooth.

Submitter: 
Amy King
Description: 
I got this recipe from my Aunt who made these for thanksgiving.  They were really good.
