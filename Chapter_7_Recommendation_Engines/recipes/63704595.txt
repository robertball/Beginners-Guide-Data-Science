Title:
Fiesta Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030795.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked chicken, chopped
1 cup instant rice (uncooked)
1 can cream of chicken (98% fat free)
1 can Rotel, undrained
1 tbsp taco seasoning
2 tbsp milk
2 cups Cheddar cheese, divided
1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips or doritos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease 9x9 pan.
Combine chicken, rice, soup, Rotel, seasoning, milk, and 1 1/2 cups of cheese.
Pour into pan.
Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
Top with crushed chips and remaining cheese.
Bake uncovered for 5-10 minutes.
