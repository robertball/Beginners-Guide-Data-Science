Title:
Candy Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz Crown Royal whiskey
4 oz Apple Pucker
Cranberry juice to taste (2-4 oz)
2 Paper thin apple slices (Optional)
Ice

Directions:
Rinse two martini glasses in cold water
Place in freezer
Fill a shaker halfway with ice
Pour whiskey, pucker, then juice into shaker
Shake vigorously to form a foam on top of liquid
pour into well chilled martini glasses
Garnish by dropping one paper thin apple slice into glass (optional)
Drink!

Submitter:

Description: 
A delicious, tart martini that you will only need one of - but you'll want more!
