Title:
Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb all purpose potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1-1/4c grated cheddar cheese
1/4c milk
2T olive oil

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 and place rack at top position.
Layer potatoes and cheese in well-oiled 8x8 dish, ending with cheese on top.
Season each layer with salt and pepper.
Pour in milk.
Drizzle oil over potatoes and cheese.
Bake on top rack until bubbly and golden, about 1 hour and 15 minutes.
