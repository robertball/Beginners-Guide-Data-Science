Title:
Creamy Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F948866.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup whipping cream
½ cup smoked almonds
4  grilled skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
½ cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Whip cream to soft peaks. Chop almonds in food processor. Shred chicken or chop finely.
In a large bowl combine the cream, almonds, chicken, mayonnaise, tarragon, salt and pepper. Mix well and serve.

Chicken salad made with whipping cream and smoked almonds.
