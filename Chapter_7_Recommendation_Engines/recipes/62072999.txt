Title:
Puppy Chow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box Rice Chex, or preferred cereal
1 12oz package milk chocolate chips (substitute with semi-sweet if desired)
1 pkg powdered sugar
1 large paper sack

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips in large double boiler or in large glass bowl in the microwave.  Add cereal, a cup or so at a time, folding to coat evenly.
Using a large paper bag, pour powdered sugar into bag, then add cereal/choc mixture, a little at a time, shaking bag gently to coat.
Serve in the bag if desired or place in covered container.  Can be refrigerated if in warm climate.

Submitter:

Description: 
Don't be fooled by the name.  This recipe came from a Veterinarian I used to work for.  It is a great, quick, people snack!
