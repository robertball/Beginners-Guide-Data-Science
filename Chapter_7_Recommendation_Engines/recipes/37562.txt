Title:
Sausage and Sweet Potato Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound bulk pork sausage
1 (16 ounce) can whole sweet potatoes in syrup, undrained
1 cup water
3 cups STOVE TOP Cornbread Stuffing Mix in the Canister

Directions:
Brown sausage in large skillet on medium-high heat. Meanwhile, drain sweet potatoes, reserving syrup. Slice sweet potatoes. Add water and reserved syrup to skillet; bring to boil. Stir in stuffing mix and sweet potatoes just to moisten.
Spoon into 1-1/2-quart casserole; cover.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
