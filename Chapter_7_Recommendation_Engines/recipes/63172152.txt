Title:
Herbed Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plus 2 Tablespoons lukewarm water
2 tablespoons powdered milk
1/3 cup honey
3 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
3 cups bread flour or all purpose flour(In Canada)
1 cup cornmeal
1/2 cup frozen corn kernels

Directions:
Place ingredients in the breadmaker according to the manufactueres instructions. Press Basic or white bread setting.Allow to cool before slicing.
