Title:
Old Fashion Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One large Pie crust
two sticks of butter
ts of ciniammon
ts of allspice
ts of cloves
one cup of brown sugar
one and half cups of granulated sugar
one ts of vainilla
two ts of leamon juice
six large winesap apples

Directions:
Roll one large pie crust
cut apples into small slices
pour into large bowl add all other ingrediants
then add leamon juice, vinilla pour contents into crust and and add butter and cover with other portion of crust sprinkel with sugar all over the top and bake on 320 degrees for one hour and and 45 minutes until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Great Chef
Description: 
Flakey pie with juicey apples inside and the taste of real butter and fresh spices.
