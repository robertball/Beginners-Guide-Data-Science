Title:
Beef Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups grated peeled potatoes
2 1/4 cups frozen mixed vegetables
1/3 cup diced onion
2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 lb lean ground beef
1/2 cup tomato juice
2 double crust pie shells

Directions:
Mix pie ingredients and divide into two pastry lined pie shells.
Cover with pastry for top crust.
Pie(s) can be frozen at this point or baked fresh.
Bake @ 375 x 15 minutes.
Reduce heat to 350 and bake another hour.
** To use frozen pie: bake @ 375 x 70 min. right from the freezer. Add time if pie doesn't appear to be done (ie. crust browned and meat cooked).

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A hearty main course pie made with ground beef, potatoes and veggies. ** The recipe makes 2 pies; 1 (or both!) of which can be frozen and baked later.
