Title:
Vegetarian Caviar (Pindzur--Poor Man's Caviar) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 kilo peppers roasted and peeled
1 eggplant roasted and peeled
2-3 tomatoes [roasted or raw]
1-2 cloves of garlic ground with salt
2-3 walnuts [finely ground]

Directions:
In a large bowl, start with the finely ground nuts and garlic. Chop the roasted peppers. Put them through a food mill or processor and add them to the garlic and walnuts. Follow the same procedure with the eggplant and the tomatoes [chop and put through food mill].  In a large, deep skillet over medium-low heat, add 1/3 c oil, preferrably sunflower or olive.  Add vegetable mixture to oil and allow to stew, stirring often, for approximately 30 minutes.  Add salt to taste.
Serve as a side dish or with bread and feta cheese.

Submitter: 
SAMOVILA
Description: 
Pindzur (pronounced pin-joor) is a flavorful and traditional Macedonian side dish mainly consisting of roasted red peppers and tomatoes.  It is often used as an appetizer as well, spread on bread or crackers, hence poor man's caviar!
