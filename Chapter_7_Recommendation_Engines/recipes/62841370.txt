Baked Pork Chops Recipe

Ingredients:
pork chops
seasonings

Directions:
line baking pan with foil. Spray with nonstick cooking spray.
lay pork chops in single layer
season with Tony Chacherie's, onion powder, pepper
Cover with foil. Bake 35 mins. Uncover & back another 10 mins until brown
