Title:
Gone In 60 Seconds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup finely chopped pecans
1/2 cup chopped onion (red or white)
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/4 +cup of Miracle Whip

Directions:
Mix everything together adding enough Miracle Whip to hold it all together.  In a small/shallow bowl lined with plastic wrap, press the mixture in fimly.  Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours (over night is better).
Turn out onto plate and spoon desired sauce over top.  Serve with crackers

Submitter: 
ttkc
Description: 
Quick to put together...top with chilli sauce, sweet & sour sauce or Salsa according to you taste and serve with your favorite cracker.  People love it!
