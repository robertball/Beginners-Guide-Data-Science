Title:
Strawberry Shortcake Hot Milk Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/181382.jpg
Ingredients:
For the beverage:
8 fluid ounces low-fat (1%) or reduced-fat (2%) milk
1 tablespoon strawberry syrup
2 tablespoons almond syrup
1 tablespoon strawberry sauce (ice cream topping)
For the garnish:
Whipped cream
1 fresh strawberry, sliced

Directions:
Beverage: In a small saucepan, combine milk, syrups and sauce over medium-low heat, stirring well to incorporate the strawberry sauce. Be careful not to let it boil. Pour into glass mug.
Garnish: Top with whipped cream and sliced strawberry.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
Fresh milk, a double dose of strawberry flavor and a hint of almonds give this warm drink its summer-scented charm. The whipped cream topping and a fresh strawberry are the icing on the cake.
