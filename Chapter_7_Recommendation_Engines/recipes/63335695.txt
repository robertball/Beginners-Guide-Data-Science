Title:
Heart Attack Breakfast Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs thick-sliced bacon
1/2 cup light or dark brown sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Lay parchment paper on a jelly roll pan.  Lay out bacon on parchment paper side by side (edges may overlap slightly). Sprinkle all over with brown sugar.  Lightly sprinkle all over with cinnamon.  Bake 20-30 minutes, or until desired crispness.  Remove from parchment paper, drain BRIEFLY on paper town (or bacon will stick to paper towel) and transfer to serving platter.
