Title:
Banana Nut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup brown sugar (light or dark)
2 eggs
1 1/4 cups mashed banana (about 3 large)
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup nuts (walnuts, macadamia nuts)
1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degree F
Cream shortening for 3 minutes
Add sugar, cream for 3 more minutes
Mix in eggs
Mix in bananas
Mix in dry ingredients
Mix in nuts and chocolate chips (optional)
Pour mixture into greased muffin pan (or regular loaf pan).
Bake 30 minutes for muffins, 60 minutes for bread

Submitter: 
Carole
Description: 
This recipe can be used to make either muffins or banana cake/loaf.  It's my husband and son's favorite breakfast.  Sometimes I add mini chocolate chips for a treat.
