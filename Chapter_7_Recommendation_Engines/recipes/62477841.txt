Title:
Basic BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable oil
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp. Onion powder
1 can tomato soup
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 tsp. Salt (optional)
1 tsp. Thyme
1/3 cup honey or brown sugar
1 tsp. Dry mustard
1/3 cup worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Coat bottom of saucepan lightly with oil.
Combine the rest of the ingredients in the saucepan and turn heat to medium.
Bring sauce to simmering; simmer 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
This sauce can be stored and used over many barbecued meats. We like to serve it with brisket.
