Title:
Egg and Potato Breakfast Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755925.jpg
Ingredients:
5 eggs
1/4 cup milk
3 cups shredded potato
6 sliced green onions
1/2 tsp salt
5 bacon slices, crumbled
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Coat 9 inch pie plate with butter or nonstick spray
Mix eggs, milk, potato, green onions, salt, about half the bacon crumbles, and one cup of the shredded cheese into a bowl.
Pour into pan and bake for 1 hour, or until middle is set.

Submitter: 
Renee
Description: 
A breakfast pie made with shredded potato, eggs, cheese, and bacon.
