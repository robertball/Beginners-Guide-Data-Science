Title:
Sweet and Spicy Grilled Corn sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ears corn on cob
8 oz cream cheese
1/8 c sugar
jalepeno 6-7 peppers or to taste
1/2 c butter
salt and pepper to taste (1/4 t)
papricka 1/4 t
garlic 1t
onion powder 1/2 t
milk 1/2c

Directions:
low to med heat add butter,milk, salt, pepper, garlic, sugar, paprika and onion to pan
Warm this a few minutes.
Add cream cheese and jalepenos. stir to combine
Add pepper juice to taste.(Alot of juice will spice your corn up)
Cook until everything is melted and combined. Stir often and cook slowly.
(u can sub the jalepeno with chile peppers or chipotle)
Grill corn on cob: med heat grill, turning often for approx 10 - 12 min, baste for last 1 min. remove and baste with sauce on plate.

Submitter: 
WendyWendy
Description: 
Sweet and Spicy Corn sauce for grilled sweet corn on cob... you wont ever go back to plain butter.
