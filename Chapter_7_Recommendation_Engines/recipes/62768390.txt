Title:
Cake Mix Extender Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake Mix Extender
Add to any cake mix to make it equal 1.5x the batter amount.
1 cup flour ( I use cake flour, but all purpose will work too)
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp baking powder
1 egg
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Add to a regular box mix for 9 inch cakes...Follow directions for your cake mix...may take a little longer as the cake(s) will be thicker, do a toothpick test for doneness.

Submitter: 
Cindy Capps Lepp
Description: 
will lift your cakes...doesn't change the flavor---great for 9inch rounds or a thicker 9x13 sheet cake
