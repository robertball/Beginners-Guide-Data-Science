Title:
Glazed Corned Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Bonne Maman apricot preserves
1/2 cup coarse, prepared mustard
2 T brown sugar
3 lb boiled corned beef brisket

Directions:
Mix preserves, mustard, and sugar. In baking dish, coat brisket with glaze.
Bake at 350F for 15 minutes or until bubbly and beginning to crust.
Remove from oven and rest for 10-15 minutes. Slice against the grain.

Submitter: 
meghanmacrae
Description: 
After boiling, corned beef can be dressed with this sweet, piquant glaze. Good for Shabbat.
