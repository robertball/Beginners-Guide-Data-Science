Title:
Banana Hemp Seed Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2554827.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bananas
1 cup frozen peach slices
2 tablespoons almond butter
1 tablespoon hemp seeds
2 cups water

Directions:
Layer bananas, peach slices, almond butter, and hemp seeds in a blender; pour in water. Cover and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
This banana-based smoothie with hemp seed and almond butter is a hearty way to start the day and is paleo-friendly!
