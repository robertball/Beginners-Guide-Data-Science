Title:
DUTCH APPLE CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6744862.jpg
Ingredients:
½ C butter
1 pkg yellow cake mix
½ C flaked coconut
1 can apple slices
½ C sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 C sour cream
2 egg yolks

Directions:
Mix butter, cake mix and coconut flakes well.
Pat into bottom of ungreased 13 x 9 bake pan. Bake 10 minutes.
Arrange apple slices on top of cake mix
Mix sugar and cinnamon & sprinkle over apples & cake mix.
Mix sour cream with egg yolks & drizzle over apples & cake mix
Bake 25 minutes at 350 til light brown.
Serve slightly warm.this a great brunch/breakfast/anytime treat
