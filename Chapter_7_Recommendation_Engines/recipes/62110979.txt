Title:
Greek Pasta Salad with Dijon Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pasta
2 16 oz packages spiral pasta
1 bunch green onions
1 red bell pepper
1 yellow bell pepper
1 english cucumber
2 containers cherry tomatoes, halved
8 oz thin sliced salami
12 oz jar pitted kalamata olives
6 oz feta cheese
1/4 C packed fresh basil
Dijon Vinaigrette
1 1/2 C Olive Oil
1/2 C Vinegar
1/4 C Water
6 T dijon mustard
1/2 yellow onion
4 cloves garlic
1 T sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil pasta according to directions al dente.
Drain and run under cold water.
In a food processor with slicer attachment, thinly slice green onions, bell peppers and cucumber.
In food processor, chop kalamata olives.
Slice salami into thin strips
Combine all salad ingredients except basil and toss with vinaigrette.  Gently slice basil and sprinkle on top of finished salad.
For Dijon Vinaigrette:
Add all ingredients into food processor and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
jalexandropoulos
Description: 
Colorful, flavorful, pasta salad with fresh vegetables, kalamata olives, fresh basil and a delicious dijon vinaigrette.  Not your boring pasta salad.
