Title:
Crock Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (32 oz) whole tomatoes
1 can chicken broth
2 c. water
2 med. onions - chopped
4 carrots - sliced
2 celery stalks - sliced thin
1 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp paprika
2 tsp dried parsley
2 c. ham - diced
1 lb. dried beans - mixed

Directions:
Soak beans in cold water overnight.
Drain soaked beans, rinse and add them along with the rest of the ingredients to the crock pot.  Cook on low all day or on high for 3-4 hrs.

Submitter: 
Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
Great way to use up extra ham without spending all day in the kitchen.  Soup freezes well.
