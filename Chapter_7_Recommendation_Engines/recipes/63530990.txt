Title:
Oven Roasted Cabbage Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One head cabbage
salt
pepper
seasoned salt
garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. On a greased pan, place 1" thick round slices of cabbage in a single layer. Season as you wish (salt, pepper, garlic, hot pepper, etc). Roast until cabbage is tender and edges are golden, 25-35 min.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is my favorite way to cook cabbage. Very tasty...and not fried in oil!!!!
