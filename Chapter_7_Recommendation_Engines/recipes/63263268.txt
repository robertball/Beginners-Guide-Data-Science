Title:
Turkey Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 LBS angel hair pasta
3 cups butter
8 cups sliced onion
3 cups all-purpose flour
25 cups milk
1 tablespoon pepper
1 tablespoon ground mustard
12 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese, divided
1 can chopped pimento peppers (optional)
1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
1/2 of a cooked turkey, sliced or 12 Lbs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Bring a large pot of  water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook for 4 minutes, or until almost tender. Drain.
Melt the butter in  a saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion; cook and stir until tender. Stir in the flour until blended, then gradually stir in the milk so that no lumps form. Season with seasonings. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. Remove from the heat and add 2/3 cup cheese and pimento, stirring until cheese melts.  Add undrained mushrooms to cheese sauce.
Place a layer of pasta in the bottom of a hotel pan.  Cover with a layer of turkey, and then a layer of cheese sauce.  Repeat the layers.  Sprinkle remaining 1/3 cup cheese over top. DO NOT SPREAD SAUCE OVER ALL PASTA KEEP TO SIDE TO BATCH COOK IF NEEDED!!
Bake for about 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until sauce is bubbly and cheese on top is toasted.

Description: 
This is a great recipe for leftover turkey.  Even those who say they don't like turkey will eat this.  May be assembled in advance and frozen: heat covered dish at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 1/2 hours.Modified from: Turkey Tetrazzini
