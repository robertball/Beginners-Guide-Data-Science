Title:
Rob's Healthy Blueberry Pancakes - 15 MM, LC, LF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Whole Ground Flaxseed Meal (Bob's Red Mill)
1/4 Cup All Natural Unprocessed Wheat Bran (Hodgson Mill Miller's Bran)
1 Tbsp Super High Protein Powder (Mlo Sports Nutrition)
1 Large Egg
3 Tbsp 100% Liquid Egg Whites (All Whites)
1 Tbsp Pumpkin Pie Spice (Mccormick)
1/4 Cup Tap Water
1 Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract (Badia)
1/8 Tsp Double Acting Baking Powder (Great Value)
1/8 Tsp Baking Soda (Great Value)
1/2 Cup Frozen Blueberries
4 1-Second Shots of Buttery Cooking Spray
1/4 Cup Calorie Free Pancake Syrup (Walden Farms)

Directions:
1 - mix all ingredients in a bowl (except syrup)
2 - heat griddle to medium
3 - spray griddle with pam for 1 second
4 - pour 1/4 cup of mix on griddle per pancake
5 - serve with calorie free syrup

Submitter: 
Robert
Description: 
Pancakes of flaxseed, bran, & soy are lower calorie and lower carb than traditional hotcakes. Yet these  tasty cakes are healthy for you. Great for a 15 minutes meal.
