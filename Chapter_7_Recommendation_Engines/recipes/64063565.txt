Title:
Gluten Free Chicken Fajita marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup gluten free soy sauce
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbs lime juice
2 tbs white vinegar
3 cloves garlic
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Mix all ingredients for the marinade together in a large bowl. Marinate for 1 hour to 12 hours.
