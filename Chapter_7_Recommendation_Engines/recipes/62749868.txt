Title:
Gingerbread Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup all purpose flour
3/4 Cup whole wheat flour
1 T baking powder
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground cloves
2 eggs slightly beaten
1 1/3 Cup milk
1/2 Cup Cannola oil
1/3 Cup molasses

Directions:
Combine the first six ingredients in a mixing bowl.
Whisk together the last 4 ingredients separately and then add all at once to the flour mixture. Leave the batter slightly lumpy.
Depending on the size of your waffle maker and how it works, pour 1/2 cup to 1 1/2 cups of the batter on the waffle maker and cook 4-5 minutes or until done.

Submitter: 
Cat Hill
Description: 
A flavorful waffle that uses wholewheat flour. Top with banana ice cream for dessert or your favorite jam or syrup for breakfast.
