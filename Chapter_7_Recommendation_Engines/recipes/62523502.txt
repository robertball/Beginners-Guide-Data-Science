Title:
Pat's Easy Guacamole Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and diced
1 lime, juiced (about 2 TBSP)
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup diced onion
3 TBSP chopped fresh cilantro
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1 TBSP minced garlic (to taste)
1 small jalapeno, minced (optional)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in jalapeno if using. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
