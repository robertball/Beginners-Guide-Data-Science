Title:
Cinnamon Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C brown sugar
3 (8oz) cream cheese softened
2 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 TLBSP vanilla extract
3 eggs
1/3 C sour cream
9 in. graham cracker crust

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and brown sugar until very smooth. Add cinnamon and vanilla. Mix in eggs one at a time.
Stir in sour cream.  Pour into crust. Bake 325o for 50 min. Chill completely. (This will take a long time.)

Submitter: 
ELIZRA
Description: 
My aunt makes this around Christmas time.    The cinnamon adds a very unique flavor that is different than the typical cheesecake.
