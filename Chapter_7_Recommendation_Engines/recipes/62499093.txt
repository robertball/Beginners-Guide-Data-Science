Title:
Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 3 lbs stew meat, cut into bit-sized pieces
2-3 medium onions, sliced into thin rings (halve rings if they seem too large)
6 carrots, sliced
2 ribs celery, sliced
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
1-2 potatoes, cut into small cubes
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 14.5-oz cans diced tomatoes
3 T minute tapioca
1/2 - 3/4 c bread crumbs
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put all ingredients in slow cooker and mix well.
Cover and cook on medium-low 10 hours.
Note:  slow cookers vary so cooking time may need to be adjusted.

Submitter: 
fullerla
Description: 
Comfort food at its best and easiest.  Serve with a crusty sour dough loaf and enjoy!
