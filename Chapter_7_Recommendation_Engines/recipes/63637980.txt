Title:
Chaos Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 oreos cookies
1/2 cup honey roasted peanuts
1/2 cup,melted
1 1/3 cups lightly packed brown sugar,divided
1(8 ounce)package cream cheese,softened
1 3/4 cups creamy peanut butter
1 cup whipped topping,divided
1 teaspoon coconut extract
1 cup chopped granola bars
3/4 cup milk chocolate chips
10 peanut butter cups,unwrapped,coarsely chopped
1/2 cup caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Process first 3 ingredients and 1/3 cup brown sugar in a food processor until crumbly.Press mixture into bottom and up sides of a 10-inch deep-dish pie plate.Bake at 350 degrees for 5 minutes.Set pie crust aside.
Beat remaining 1 cup brown sugar,cream cheese,peanut butter and 1 tablespoon whipped topping at medium speed with a electric mixer until mixture is light and fluffy.Beat remaining whipped topping and coconut extract until stiff peaks form.Fold one-third whipped
topping into peanut butter mixture;fold peanut butter mixture into remaining whipped topping.
Place granola bar pieces,chocolate chips and chopped peanut butter cups on the crust.Spread peanut butter mixture evenly over granola mixture.Drizzle with caramel ice cream topping,if desired,chill for 2 hours.Serve with a glass of milk and enjoy.
