Title:
Flaky pie crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2/3 cups lard
1 egg, beatened
1 TB vinegar
ice cold water
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine salt with flour
Add lard and with a pastry blender stir until resembles large peas
Add egg and vinegar.
With a fork stirring gently and adding just enough water that the flour is away from the sides of the bowl and you can form into a large ball.
Wrap ball in plastic wrap and refrigerate for several hours.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
I got this flaky pie crust recipe from my mother-in-law who makes great pies.  It's extremely flaky!  She's from the old school where she still uses lard.
