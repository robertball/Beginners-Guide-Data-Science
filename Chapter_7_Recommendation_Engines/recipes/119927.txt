Title:
Tater Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989350.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning
2/3 cup water
1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed nacho cheese soup, undiluted
1 (32 ounce) package tater tots

Directions:
In a skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes. Add corn and soup; mix well.
Transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Arrange Tater Tots in a single layer over the top. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until potatoes are crispy and golden brown.

Submitter: 
Ronna  Lewis
Description: 
'Our family lives and works on a ranch,' remarks Ronna Lewis from Plains, Kansas. 'I like to fix this nicely seasoned casserole ahead of time and freeze it for later use. It's good with a tossed salad or nacho chips and dip.'
