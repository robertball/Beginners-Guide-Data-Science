Title:
Corned Beef and Cabbage I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/807468.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds corned beef brisket with spice packet
10 small red potatoes - cut into quarters
5 carrots, peeled and cut into 3-inch pieces
1 large head cabbage, cut into small wedges
3 large cloves garlic - chopped
SAUCE INGREDIENTS;
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tbls. butter
2 tbls. prepared yellow mustard
1/3 cup ketchup
3 tbls. apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Place corned beef in large pot or Dutch oven and cover with water. Add the spice packet that came with the corned beef.  Cover pot and bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.  Simmer approximately 50 minutes per pound or until tender.
Add potatoes and carrots and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are almost tender.  Add cabbage and cook for 15 more minutes. Remove meat and place meat in a baking dish. Then make the sauce to put on top of the corned beef by mixing all the sauce ingredients together in a small saucepan over medium heat. cook and stir til smooth. Pour over corned beef and bake uncovered at 350 for 30 minutes.
Place vegetables in a bowl and cover. Add as much broth (cooking liquid reserved in the Dutch oven or large pot) as you want. Slice meat across the grain. Serve with the veggies.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
This traditional Irish dish is the centerpiece for many a St. Patrick's Day table. Corned beef takes a long, lovely simmer with potatoes, carrots and cabbage for a hearty and satisfying dish.Modified from: Corned Beef and Cabbage I
