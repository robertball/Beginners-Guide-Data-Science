Title:
Glorified Rice aka Heavenly Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked white rice, cooled
1 20 oz. can pineapple chunks, well drained with juice reserved
1 .30 ounce package sugar-free lemon gelatin
1 cup boiling water
1 cup Craisins
1 cup chopped pecans
2 cups Cool Whip, thawed

Directions:
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water.
Stir in 1 cup pineapple juice from drained pineapple.
Place in fridge until the consistency is like whipped cream.
Place cooked rice in large bowl.
Stir in well-drained pineapple chunks.
Add Craisins and chopped nuts.
When gelatin has partially set up, stir in the Cool Whip.
Then combine gelatin mixture and rice mixture and chill.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
When my grandmother made glorified rice, there was no such thing as Craisins or Cool Whip.  This is my updated version of an old family favorite.
