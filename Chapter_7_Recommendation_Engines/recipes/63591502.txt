Title:
Shirley J's 3 Cheese Cauliflower Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup Butter
1 cup Green Onion
1-2 heads Cauliflower (or Broccoli)
1½ TBSP Garlic
2¾ cup Cheddar Cheese
3 slices (1/3 cup) Pepper Jack Cheese
2 1/3 cup Shirley J's White Cheddar Base
2 qts Water (hot)

Directions:
In a pot, place butter, chopped green onions and cauliflower( just break apart. Leave in fairly large pieces).
Cook on medium heat with the lid on stirring occasionally until vegetables are well cooked. About 25 min. The cauliflower will become very soft and you will then have bite size pieces in the soup.
Roast garlic in a pan until slightly brown. Add to vegetables and stir well.
In a separate pot, boil hot water and then add Shirley J's white cheddar base mix. Using a whisk, stir until the Shirley J's in completely incorporated. Heat to 140 degrees.
Slowly add cheddar cheese and crumbled pepper jack into the pot. Stirring while adding. Then add cheese soup base to vegetable mixture and simmer soup until it reaches 160 degrees (10 minutes). Enjoy!
