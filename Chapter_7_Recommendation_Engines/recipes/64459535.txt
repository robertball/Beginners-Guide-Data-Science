Title:
Penne with Sausage & Prosciutto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups penne
2 1/2 cups broccoli florets
1/2 lb Italian turkey sausage
4 tsp olive oil
1 red bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips
1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips
1 red onion. thinly sliced
1 cup thinly sliced mushrooms
2 oz. prosciutto, cut into thin strips
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1 tbs grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large pot of boiling water, cook penne 6 minutes, add broccoli and cook another 4 minutes. Drain and transfer to a serving bowl. Meanwhile spray a large skillet with cooking spray, heat. Saute sausage until cooked through, 6-8 minutes. Add to penne. In skillet add 2 tsp oil. Saute pepper and onion until softened, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms, prosciutto, garlic and pepper flakes, saute until tender. Add to penne, add remaining oil, basil and Parmesan cheese, toss to combine. Serve
