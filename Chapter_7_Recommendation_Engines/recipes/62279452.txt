Title:
Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/425430.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag fresh spinach
1 tomato
6 strips of bacon
1 small onion
1/4 cup sun dried tomato strips
1/4 cup calamata olives
2 marinated artichokes
2 slices of dark wheat bread
1T butter
2 dashes herbs de provence
1/8 cup Sun Dried Tomato Vinaigrette
1/8 cup goat cheese

Directions:
Cook & crumble the bacon.
Toast the two slices of bread.
Butter both pieces  of toast and sprinkle with herbs de provence.
Cut the slices of toast into 1cm squares and set aside.
Dice the onion, olives, and marinated artichoke.
Place the spinach in a large bowl.
Add the toast squares and other ingredients.
Drizzle on the vinaigrette & toss to coat.
Crumble the goat cheese on top & serve.
