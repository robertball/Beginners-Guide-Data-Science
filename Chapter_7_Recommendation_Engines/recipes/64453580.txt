Title:
Cocoa Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2423136.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup solid honey
250g cocoa butter
170 g coconut oil (virgin)
250 coconut butter
2 tsp vanilla extract
100 grams dark chocolate

Directions:
Melt together cocoa butter, oil and coconut butter.
Add honey and mix well. Heat over medium temperature until all the ingredients are well combined. Add the vanilla and mix well.
Pour into a lined 9x13 dish and pound on the counter to knock out any bubbles.
Leave to cool until it reaches room temp. Add the  this will depend on the ambient temperature.
Melt the dark chocolate.
Give it one last mix and then drizzle in the dark chocolate swirling with a skewer.
Leave in the fridge until cooled then cut into pieces.
Refrigerate or freeze.
