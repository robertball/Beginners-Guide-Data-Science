Title:
Ginger Tea (Salabat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 1" slice ginger root, peeled and crushed
4 cups water
4 Tbsp brown sugar

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a 2 qt saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Lower heat and simmer 5-10 minutes.  (The longer it cooks, the stronger the ginger flavor).  Remove ginger slice before serving.  Serve hot.
