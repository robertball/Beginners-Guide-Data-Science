Title:
Chocolate Mayonnaise Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2027600.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake Ingredients
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 c. mayonnaise
1 1/3 c. water
1 2/3 c. sugar
1/4 tsp. baking powder
1 1/4 tsp. baking soda
2/3 c. unsweetened cocoa powder
2 c. all-purpose flour
Peanut Butter Frosting Ingredients
3 c. powdered sugar
1/3 c. peanut butter
1 2/ tsp. vanilla
1/4 c. milk

Directions:
Cake:
Mix all wet items in a mixing bowl until well blended.  Add all dry items except flour and cocoa.  Once well blended, add the flour and cocoa a little at a time.  Once it is well blended pour batter into a greased baking pan and bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes or until tooth pick comes out clean.  Let cake completely cool before adding frosting.
Peanut Butter Frosting
Mix powdered sugar and peanut butter in medium bowl with spoon or electric mixer on low speed. Stir in vanilla and milk.  Beat until smooth and spreadable.  If necessary, stir in additional milk, 1/2 teaspoon at a time.
