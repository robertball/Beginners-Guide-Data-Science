Title:
Grandmama Fuller's biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising flour
1/2 TBSP baking powder
1/2 TBSP sugar
1/3 cup CRISCO shortening
1 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well but not too long, and roll out  On a well floured board.  You can hand roll or cut with a biscuit cutter.  Brush with melted butter.  Bake at 400 degrees until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My husbands grandmother taught me to make these when I was first married.  Everyon who eats them loes them.  And so do I.
