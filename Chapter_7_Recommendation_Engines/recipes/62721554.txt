Title:
Chicken salad with red grapes and toasted pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken breast (shredded)
1 Cup halved red grapes
1/3 cup chopped pecans (toasted)
1/3 cup low fat plain greek yogurt
1/4 cup chopped celery
1 tsp honey
1 tsp cider vinegar
1/4 tsp salt
pinch of black pepper

Directions:
shred chicken. Add grapes and pecan. In a small bowl stir together the yogurt, celery, honey, vinegar, salt and pepper. Pour the dressing over the chicken and toss to coat.
