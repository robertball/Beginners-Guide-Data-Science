Title:
RJ's Polish Sausage Stew by Mom & R.J. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798583.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 cans condensed cream of celery soup
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup (generous) chopped onion
4 lbs skinless polish sausage cut in 2 inch pieces
40 oz sauerkraut
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
6 Yukon Gold potatoes scrubbed and cubed

Directions:
In slow cooker combine soup, sugar and sauerkraut.  Stir in sausage, potatoes and onions.  Cook for low heat for 8 hours (or 4 hours high heat).  Spoon off excess fat.  Stir in cheese.  Spoon into serving bowls.  Serve with additional shredded cheese to sprinkle over the top, if desired.

Submitter: 
RJ Ellingsen
Description: 
Polish Sausage with a sweetened sauerkrout and veggies.  Easy to prepare ahead of time, a great hit for the holidays.  Mom's recipie with a little extra.
