Title:
Cheese and Chili Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of chili
1 brick of creamcheese (1 brick per can)
Shredded cheese - mexican or four cheese (1 bag)

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375
Mix together the can of chili, brick of creamcheese and a generous amount of shredded cheese
Bake the mixutre for 15 mins
