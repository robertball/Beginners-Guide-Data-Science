Title:
Chocolate Power Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2124625.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup rolled oats
3/4 cup natural almond butter
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup chopped milk chocolate chips
2 tablespoons flax seeds
2 tablespoons chia seeds
2 tablespoons chocolate protein powder

Directions:
Stir ingredients together in a bowl until evenly mixed.
Squeeze/roll mixture into balls (dishwashing gloves are handy for this) and chill. Keep cold until serving.
