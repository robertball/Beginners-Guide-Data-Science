Title:
Fruity And Sour Duck Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4529301.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Apple Vinegar
2 tablespoon ketchup
3 tablespoon brown sugar light
3 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon sesame oil
8 ounce Apple Sauce
15 ounce can Mandarin Orange
6 Strawberries (Optional)

Directions:
In a bowl mix vinegar, ketchup, brown sugar, white sugar, sesame oil, and Apple Sauce.
Strain juice from Mandarin Orange.
Add strawberries and oranges to food processor. Then pulse until consistency like apple sauce. Add to bowl and mix and refrigerate at overnight.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
Condiment dip for deep-fried Asian foods with a fruity and sour flavor and a translucent orange appearance similar to a thin jelly.
