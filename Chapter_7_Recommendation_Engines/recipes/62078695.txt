Title:
Grilled Teriyaki Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. Soy Sauce
1/4 c. Rice Vinegar
3 Tbs. medium dry Sherry
1 Tsp. Chinese 5-spice powder
1 Large garlic clove minced
1- 1 inch piece of ginger root peeled and grated
1- 1.5 lb pork tenderloin

Directions:
In a large ziplock bag (gallon size) mix together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sherry, chinese 5 spice powder, garlic, and ginger root. Shake in bag until well mixed. Put pork tenderloin in bag and place in refigerator for 1-3 hours to marinate depending on how strong you want the taste to be. Remove from refrigerator and grill about 20-30 min until pork is no longer pink. Slice in 1/2 inch slices.
