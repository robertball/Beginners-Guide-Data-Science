Title:
Oven Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1361803.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs sirloin, cut in 2" strips
3 medium potatoes, cubed
4 carrots, sliced
4 stalks celery, sliced
1/2 large onion, chopped
1 can 4 oz mushrooms, drained (I skip this)
1/2 green pepper, chopped (I skip this, too)
2 cans golden mushroom Campbells soup
1/4 cup red wine (optional)

Directions:
In a 9 x 13" pan, layer meat, potatoes, and vegetables.
Pour the soup (do not dilute) over the layers. Cover with foil
Bake without opening oven or foil for 3 hours at 325 degrees.

Submitter: 
Taylor Luiso
Description: 
Perfect for warming and filling you up on a cold winters day.
