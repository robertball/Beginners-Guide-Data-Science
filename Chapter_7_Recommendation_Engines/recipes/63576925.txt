Title:
Chocolate Pecan Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c sifted flour
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 c butter
1/2 c brown sugar
1 egg, separated
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
8 oz pecan halves
1/2 c semi-sweet chocolate bits

Directions:
Sift flour and salt together.
Cream butter, gradually adding brown sugar.
Blend in unbeaten egg yolk and vanilla.
Add dry ingredients; mix thoroughly.
If desired, chill 1 hour for easier handling.
Shape dough into 1" balls.
Dip in slightly beaten egg white and press each ball onto three pecan halves.
Place on ungreased baking sheet.
Bake at 375* for 12-15 minutes.
Meanwhile, melt chocolate bits over boiling water.
Blend until smooth.
Place about 1/2 teaspoonful of chocolate over top of each baked cooke.
Let stand until chocolate hardens.
Store in flat container.
