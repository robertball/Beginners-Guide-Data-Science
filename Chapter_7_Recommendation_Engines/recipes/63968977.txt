Title:
Chewy Oatmeal Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101584.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups rolled oats
1 cup flaked coconut
1-2 cups chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a small bowl combine flour, soda, and salt and set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth. Blend in instant pudding, then beat in eggs and vanilla until the batter is light and fluffy. Add flour mixture and mix well. Stir in oats, coconut and chips if desired. Dough will be stiff.
Drop dough by rounded spoonfuls onto the baking sheets.
Bake for 8 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven until very lightly browned. Cool on wire racks.
