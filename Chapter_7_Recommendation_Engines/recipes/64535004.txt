Title:
No Butter Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3091564.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Cups Flour
3 Tbsp baking powder
2 Cups Sugar
1 tsp salt
1 Cup Milk
4 eggs
1 Tablespoon Lemon extract
2 tsp vanilla extract
Glaze (Optional)
3 Cups powdered sugar
3 Tbsp Milk
2 teaspoons lemon extract
1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Coat tube pan with margarine and dust with flour
In a large bowl cream margarine and sugar.
Add eggs one at a time.
Add one cup of flour until well incorporated
Add 1/2 cup of milk
Continue alternating flour and milk until both are incorporated
Add salt, baking powder, vanilla extract and lemon extract and mix until well blended.
Taste batter and add more flavoring if needed. Batter should be very flavorful.
Bake at 350 for 50 minutes to an hour, until cake is completely done.
Tip: If cake is brown enough but still not done, drop oven temperature to 325 to complete the baking process.

Submitter: 
Danielle Lockley
Description: 
I wanted pound cake but didn't have butter so I adapted my normal recipe to use margarine instead. The result was a light, fluffy and flavorful pound cake that tasted just as good as the butter version. Must try!
