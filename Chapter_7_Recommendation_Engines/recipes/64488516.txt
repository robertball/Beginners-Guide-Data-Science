Title:
Raw Snickers Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base
1 1/2 c raw cashews
1/2 cup shredded coconut
8 medjool dates, pitted
3 Tbl honey
Peanut Butter Caramel
8 medjool dates, pitted
1/4 cup natural peanut butter
2 Tbl almond milk
1 Tbl honey
pinch of Himalayan sea salt
Topping
1/4 cup unsalted, roasted peanuts
2 Tbl cocoa powder
2 Tbl coconut oil
2 Tbl honey

Directions:
For the base:
In a food processor, process cashews into small pieces.
Add in coconut, dates, and honey and process until a sticky dough forms.
Press into a loaf pan lined with parchment paper and put in the freezer.
For the caramel:
Process all ingredients, adding almond milk a little bit at a time until a smooth caramel forms.
Spread over the base.
For the assembly:
Scatter peanuts on top of the caramel and press down lightly. Mix cocoa powder, coconut oil and honey. Microwave for 10 seconds if necessary.
Spread chocolate evenly over top of the peanuts. Place back in freezer until hardened. Remove from the freezer and cut into squares. Store in freezer until ready to eat.
