Title:
Sweet Burgundy Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812696.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bunches of beet greens (Approximately 2 packed cups)
2 cups pre-washed spinach
7 dried Turkish figs
1 garlic clove
salt to taste
1/2 cup Riesling or Gewurztraminer wine
2 teaspoons butter
Optional: 1/2 oz shaved Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Wash beet greens.  Trim stems off leaves, leaving approximately 1 1/2 inch of the red stem.
Mince garlic clove and set aside.
Trim hard stem off top of figs and quarter.
Place wine, greens and figs in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
Cook over medium heat for approximately seven minutes, stirring often until nearly softened.
Add spinach, garlic and butter. Continue cooking on medium-low heat until spinach is wilted.
Salt to taste and serve with optional shavings of Parmesan cheese

Submitter: 
JoyFullGrl
Description: 
This healthy dish presents beautifully with an intense burgundy-green color.  Dried Figs and greens combine in this simple, quick side dish.
