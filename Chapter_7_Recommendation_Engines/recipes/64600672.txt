Title:
Vidalia onion risotto with feta cheese and shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
2 cups chopped Vidalia or other sweet onion
2 large garlic cloves, minced
1-1/2 cups uncooked Arborio or other short-grain rice
40 oz vegetable broth
1/2 cup (2 ounces) crumbled feta cheese, divided
1/3 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
1/4 cup (1 ounce) grated fresh Parmesan cheese
Freshly ground black pepper
Flat-leaf parsley sprigs (optional)
1 lb small shrimp, thawed and shelled

Directions:
Heat vegetable broth in saucepan.
Heat oil in a large sauce pan over medium heat.
Add onion and garlic; saute 1 minute. Stir in rice.
Add 1/2 cup broth; cook until liquid is nearly absorbed,
stirring constantly. Add remaining broth, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring
constantly until each portion of broth is nearly absorbed before adding the
next (about 20 minutes total).
Add shrimp a few minutes before the end of cooking.
Remove from heat; stir in 1/4 cup feta, parsley, and Parmesan. Spoon into a large serving bowl; top with 1/4 cup feta and pepper. Garnish with parsley sprigs, if desired. (serving size: 1 cup).

Based on a recipe in Cooking Light.  We added shrimp to make it a main dish. You could also add chicken.
