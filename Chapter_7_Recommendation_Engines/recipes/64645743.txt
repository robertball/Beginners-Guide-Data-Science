Title:
Blueberry Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4568367.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups blueberries
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp. lemon juice
1/4 cup water
1 tbsp. corn starch

Directions:
Add blueberries, sugar, 1/2 cup water, and lemon juice to a sauce pan, and simmer for 12 minutes.
Put 1/4 of mixture into a food processor and puree.
Add back to pan, mix corn starch with 1/4 cup water and add to the blueberry mix.
Simmer for another 5 minutes or until thickened.
Serve immediately or freeze for later.

Submitter: 
judy2304
Description: 
This recipe makes a thick blueberry syrup with whole blueberries that is great for pancakes or Belgian waffles. It is so much better and healthier than anything you can buy in the store.  It's easy to make and stores well in the refrigerator or in the freezer for longer term storage.
