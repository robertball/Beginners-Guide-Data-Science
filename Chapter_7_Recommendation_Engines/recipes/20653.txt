Title:
Valerie's Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/21616.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 cup whole milk

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the vanilla pudding mix, frozen whipped topping, cream cheese, confectioner's sugar and milk.  Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Valerie Cain Cuff
Description: 
Spear your favorite fruit on a toothpick and dip it! It will taste fabulous with this creamy vanilla dip!
