Title:
Pine Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Saltine Crackers
1 c butter
1 c sugar
chocolate chips

Directions:
Line the bottom of a 9x13 pan with saltine crackers
Boil butter and sugar until light brown, making a caramel
Pour over crackers
Top with chocolate chips
Bake at 350 for 8-10 minutes, or until chips have melted
