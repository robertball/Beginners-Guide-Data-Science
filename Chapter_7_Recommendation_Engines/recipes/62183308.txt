Title:
Glazed Cornish Game Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cornish game hens
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 stalk celery, chopped
1/4 green bell pepper, chopped
1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained and chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup melted butter
chopped zuchinni & summer squash,1 each
1 small jar Pineapple/Apricot jelly
2 cans Chicken broth

Directions:
Brine overnight in refrig (1/2 cup salt & 1/4 sugar- add to heated 1 3/4 cans chicken broth & 1 cup water ;cool and place hens in large zip-lock bag with COOLED brine liquid; refrigerate).Next day rinse hens and prepare stuffing. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a small bowl combine the 1/2 cup melted butter, onion, celery, bell pepper,chopped squashes, mushrooms, garlic,and herbs.
Season hens inside and out with salt, pepper and poultry seasoning to taste, then stuff with equal amounts butter/vegetable mixture. Place stuffed birds in a 9x13 inch baking dish, breast side up. Drizzle with olive oil.
Cover dish and bake in the preheated oven for 1 hr 10 mins . Remove cover and brown at 450 degrees F to crisp skin for 15 mins. Simmer jelly in 1/4 cp. chicken broth on stovetop to reduce,approx. 20 mins while hens cook ;makes glaze)Glaze with reduced jelly mixture and continue baking another 15 mins. Great served with roasted red potatoes that are sprinkled with grated parmesean cheese hot out of oven !
