Title:
Christmas Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bag of precooked meatballs
Bottle of Hunt's Ketchup Large
Jar of Smuckers Grape Jelly Large

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a large crock pot. Leave alone and let them cook on High until all meatballs are heated through and through. Then enjoy. One of our favorite Holiday treats.

Submitter: 
K.E.M.
Description: 
The perfect Meatball fingerfood for any party or a comfort food snack for any day.
