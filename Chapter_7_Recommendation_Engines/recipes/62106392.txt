Title:
Easy Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Louis de Kamp crab meat
1/2 c. yellow onion, finely chopped
1 clove of minced garlic
1-8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
1 stalk celery finely chopped
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl, mix well.  Spoon into Saran Wrap lined mold and chill for several hours.  Turn out onto plate and serve with crackers.

Submitter:

Description: 
Super easy crab dip that tastes great.  Always a crowd pleaser.
