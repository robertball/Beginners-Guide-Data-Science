Title:
Deb's Awesome Baked Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh cranberries
2 cups sugar
1/3 cup brandy (we use blackberry brandy)

Directions:
Mix the cranberries and sugar in your baking pan. Bake for 1 hour at 300 degrees, stirring every once in a while.  Remove from oven. Let cool 5 minutes.  Add the 1/3 cup brandy - or a little more or less - depends on personal taste.

Submitter: 
Mother Ann
Description: 
These cranberries are so good you will never open a can of cranberries again!  Goes great over cheesecake, as well.
