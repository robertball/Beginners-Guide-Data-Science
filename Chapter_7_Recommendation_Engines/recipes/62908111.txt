Title:
Spinach-Ricotta Pinwheel Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ripple style oven-ready lasagna noodles
8 garlic cloves, minced
3 Tbsp. olive oil
56 oz. crushed tomatoes or marinara sauce
2 Tbsp. dried basil leaves
salt/pepper to taste
1 16 oz. bag frozen chopped spinach thawed and squeezed dry
2 c. ricotta cheese
1 3/4 c grated parmesan

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400`. Add lasagna noodles to piping hot salted water, covering noodles well. Soak until soft. Drain and cut in half to make 24 strips.
Put basil and 3 of the garlic in a skillet with 1 Tbsp. of the oil. When grli sizzles add spinach. Season with salt/pepper and add ricotta. If you want ground meat, have it brownded and drained well and add now. Remove to a bowl.
Put 5 of the garlic and 2 Tbsp. oil in emptied skillet. When garlic sizzles add tomatoes or Marinara.Ground meat can be added here instead of spinach, or both if desired. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 min. to blend flavors.
Assembly: Spread 2 c. of the marinara in the bottom of a 13 x 9 baking dish.
Working with 6 noodles at a time, spread spinach mixture on top. Roll into spiral and set in pan. When done, Spread the rest of the sauce on top and top with parmesan.
Cover and bake 30-40 min.  Remove top baking til cheese melts in and slightly browns.
