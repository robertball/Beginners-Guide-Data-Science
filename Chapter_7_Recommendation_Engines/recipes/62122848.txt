Title:
mushroom pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion chopped, or 3 green onions
1/4 cup butter
1 1/3 cups uncooked ring pasta
1 can beef broth or omit onion and use beef onion broth
1 cup water
6 mushrooms sliced and cooked
1 1/4 tbls worcestershire sauce
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp soy sauce
pepper to taste

Directions:
saute onions and mushrooms in butter about 3-4 minutes. add all other ingredients, bring to a boil.
reduce heat to simmer.
cook 20 minutes or until noodles are done.

Submitter: 
Sally Campbell Taylor
Description: 
wonderful side dish. My family loves it with steak, burgers or kababos.
