Title:
Lemon Sugar Wafers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 cup butter (no substitutes)
2 eggs
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1 Tbsp. lemon rind
1 tsp. vanilla
3-1/2 cups cake and pastry flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cream of tartar
extra sugar for rolling

Directions:
In large bowl, combine flour, soda, salt and cream of tartar. Set aside.  In mixing bowl, cream sugar, butter, eggs, lemon juice and rind until smooth. Stir in flour mixture.  Chill dough 1 hour. Shape dough into 1-inch balls, roll in sugar.  Place on greased cookie sheets, bake at 325 for about 15 minutes, until light golden brown.  Cookies will be soft in center.   Cool 2 minutes before removing to counter.  Cool completely before storing.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
These are so yummy--our son's very favorite.  Using cake and pastry flour is what makes these cookies soft and chewy.  They are a nice change from chocolate chip cookies.
