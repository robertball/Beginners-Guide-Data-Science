Title:
Creamy Broccoli Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c fresh broccoli florets
2 Tbsp olive oil
4 (6 oz each) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 3 pieces each
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 c heavy cream
3 large cloves garlic, minced
1/3 c grated parmesan or romano cheese

Directions:
In saucepan, cook broccoli in boiling water until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes.  Drain & transfer to a large bowl.
Heat oil in nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Add chicken and saute until done, about 5 minutes per side.  Transfer chicken to the bowl with broccoli. Add broth, cream, and garlic to the skillet, and boil until sauce slightly thickens, about 5 minutes.   Add chicken, broccoli and cheese to the sauce and cook while stirring until heated through and well coated with the sauce.
