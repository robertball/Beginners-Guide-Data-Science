Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken wings
1 (8 ounce) jar ORTEGA速 Thick & Smooth Taco Sauce
Ranch-style salad dressing

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 375 degrees F.
COMBINE chicken wings and 1/4 cup taco sauce in medium bowl.  Arrange on a foil-lined baking sheet.
BAKE, brushing frequently with remaining taco sauce, for 35 to 40 minutes or until no longer pink near bone.  Serve with salad dressing.
