Title:
Boneless Turkey Crock Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boneless turkey breast
1/2 medium onion, sliced
1/2 cup water
Garlic powder
Seasoned salt
Black pepper

Directions:
Place onions on bottom of crock pot.  Season turkey breast with seasonings (be careful not to use too much salt).  Place turkey on top of onions and add water.  Cook on low for 6-7 hours.
