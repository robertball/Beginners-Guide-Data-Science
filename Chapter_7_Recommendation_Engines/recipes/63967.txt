Title:
Holiday Egg Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992940.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
3 cups milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Dash salt
Nutmeg

Directions:
In large mixer bowl, beat eggs.  Gradually beat in remaining ingredients.  Chill.  Garnish with nutmeg.  Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
EAGLEBRAND®
Description: 
Egg nog is a wonderful drink for celebrating the winter holidays. Try your hand at this delicious recipe, and impress your guests!
