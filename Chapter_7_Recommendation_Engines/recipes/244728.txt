Title:
Baby Arugula with Radishes & Hazelnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497409.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5 ounce) package prewashed baby arugula
4 medium radishes, trimmed and sliced
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Avocado Dressing
1/4 cup chopped toasted hazelnuts

Directions:
Compose salad of arugula and radishes on each of 4 salad plates. Drizzle 2 tablespoons of dressing decoratively over each salad; sprinkle with nuts.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Creamy Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Avocado Dressing is perfect tossed with peppery arugula and radishes with a sprinkle of toasted hazelnuts.
