Title:
Yaya's Moist Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1892665.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups sifted flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon allspice
3 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 can libbys pumpkin(15 oz.)
1 cup oil (we use corn)
3/4 cup water

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients in separate bowl and mix well.
Combine eggs, sugar, pumpkin, oil and water, in separate bowl then blend well.
Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and beat well.
Pour mixture into 2 greased and floured loaf pans and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until inserted tooth pick comes out slightly clean.
Let cool in pan to finish cooking. Store in pan with foil covering or transfer to foil after cooled.

Submitter: 
Lauren Hillin
Description: 
A family recipe that everyone is in love with. Its moist and yummy. Its devoured quickly so don't adjust down!
