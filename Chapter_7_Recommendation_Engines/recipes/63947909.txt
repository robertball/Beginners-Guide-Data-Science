Title:
Big Ray's Sweet Potato and Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1094928.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 medium red onion, chopped
4 Tbsp olive oil, divided
2 medium jalapenos, seeded and roughly chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
Juice of 2 limes
1 15 oz. can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 medium bell pepper, finely chopped
1 tsp salt
freshly ground pepper
1/3 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Place sweet potato, onions and 2 Tbsp olive oil on a baking sheet; toss well.  Bake, turning occasionally, until edges brown, about 30-40 minutes
Place jalapenos, garlic, lime juice and 2 Tbsp olive oil in a blender or food processor and process until blended
Place warm sweet potatoes ans onions, beans and bell pepper in a large bowl.  Add dressing, salt and pepper.  Toss well.  Top with cilantro.  Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This sweet potato salad features the Southwest flavors of jalapeno, garlic and lime and is a great side to any Mexican meal.
