Title:
West Tennessee Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/4 cup fat free plain yogurt
1/4 cup buttermilk
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup yellow cornmeal
1/2 cup fresh or frozen corn kernels
3 tbsp honey
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat the egg, yogurt, buttermilk, honey and oil until smooth. Combine the cornmeal, baking powder and salt; add to egg mixture and beat just until combined, fold in whole corn kernels. Grease an ovenproof 6-in. skillet or round baking dish; dust with cornmeal. Add batter. Bake at 425 degrees F for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
