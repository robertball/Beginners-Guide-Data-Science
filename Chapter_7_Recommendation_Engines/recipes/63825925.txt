Title:
Simple Baked Cinnamon Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apples to fill 2.2 qt pan
8 tbsp brown sugar
cinnamon
3 tbsp butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Peel, cut, and slice apples.   Put in pan.  Put brown sugar on top.   Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Cut butter into pats and put on top of apples.  Place in oven and bake for 30 to 40 minutes until tender.

Submitter: 
jodygreiner
Description: 
This is a simple version of cinnamon apples I created trying to mock my daughter's school recipe.   Although it wasn't like the school recipe it was delicious.
