Title:
Onion and Beef with Stuffing topping crock pot meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. roast (top round, sirloin, bottom round)
1 can onion soup
1 onion sliced
mushrooms, sliced
1 box stuffing mix, herb (Stouffers)
1/4 c. butter
1 cup mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cut beef into chunks & place in crock pot
put sliced onion and mushrooms on top of beef
pour can of soup on top of onions, mushrooms and beef
Cook on low for 8-10 hrs.
Mix 1/4 cup melted butter with 1 cup of meat broth from crock pot.  Combine mixture with box of stuffing mix.  Put stuffing on top of beef mixture in crock pot and cover.  Cook until stuffing is fluffy.  Put mozzarella cheese on top of stuffing & cook until melted.
