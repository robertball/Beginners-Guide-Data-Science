Title:
Easy Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs butter or margarin
2 Tbs flour
2 tsp (or to taste) garlic powder
2 Cup Milk
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup parmesian cheese
mozarella cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in sauce pan.  Add flour, salt, pepper and garlic.  Whisk together. (you can substitute fresh minced garlic for a more garlicy flavor, just add to the butter and sautee before you add the flour)  Slowly whisk in milk.  Bring to a boil.  Add parmesian cheese.  Simmer till sauce has thickened.  If sauce isn't thickening add a handful of mozarella.
Serve over you favorite kind of pasta, sprinkle on mozarella cheese.
Serve w/your favorite veggie (we love brocolli), and garlic bread.  Top Pasta w/grilled chicken for a complete meal.

Submitter: 
Alisha Bowers
Description: 
A quick and easy alfredo recipe that is sure to please the whole family.
