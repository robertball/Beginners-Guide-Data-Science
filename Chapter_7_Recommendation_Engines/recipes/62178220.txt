Title:
Baked Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb cranberrie, washed and drained
1 3/4C sugar
2Tbsp water
3-4Tbsp lemon juice
1 8oz jar of orange marmalade
grated lemon rind
1/2C brandy(optional)

Directions:
Spread cranberries in a 9x13 in pan. Sprinkle water and sugar evenly over the top of the berries. Cover with foil and place in 325 degree oven 30-40min or until berries pop. Shake pan once or twice during baking. Remove from oven and stir in lemon juice, marmalade and rind and brandy. Chill well.
