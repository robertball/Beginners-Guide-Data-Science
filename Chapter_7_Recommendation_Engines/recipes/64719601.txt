Title:
Healthy Butter Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T Olive Oil
1 C Onion, Chopped
3-4 Large Garlic Cloves, minced
1 1/2 Tbs Yellow Curry Powder
1 t ground cumin
1/2 t sea salt
1/4 t cayenne
1/2 C Vegetable Stock
1 15 oz can Plain Tomato sauce
1 19 oz can Chick Peas
1/4 T Lime Juice
1/4 Peanut Butter
1/2 C Coconut Milk
1 t sugar
Extra salt and lime juice to taste
Rice to serve

Directions:
Saute onions in oil until transparent
Ass garlic and all spices
Deglaze pot with vegetable stock
Add tomato sauce, chick peas, lime juice
Bring to a simmer and cook 20-25 minutes
Remove from heat and add peanut butter, coconut milk and sugar
Serve on rice
