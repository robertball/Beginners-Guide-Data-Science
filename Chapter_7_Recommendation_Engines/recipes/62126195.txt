Title:
Peanut Butter Snickers Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cookie pie crust
1 8 ounce tub whipped cream
4 ounces cream cheese
3/4 cup chunky peanut butter
3/4 cup powdered sugar
3 Snickers bars, chopped

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and powdered sugar. Add whipped cream and peanut butter and whip. Stir in candy bar pieces. Pour into pie crust and refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
Cream cheese, whipped cream, peanut butter, and big chunks of candy bar are all combined to create a fluffy but rich pie that everyone loves!
