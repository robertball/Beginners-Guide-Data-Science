Title:
Vegan Mexican Quinoa Bowl with Green Chile Cilantro Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cup quinoa, uncooked
1/2 cup chopped red onion
3 cups chopped red bell pepper
2 romaine hearts
2 15-ounce cans black beans, rinsed
1/2 cup chopped cilantro + a little more for garnish
2 avocados
1-1/2 cups Vegan Green Chile Cilantro Sauce [Hyperlink to sauce recipe]

Directions:
Prepare quinoa according to package's directions.
Chop onion, bell pepper, and romaine.
Make Vegan Green Chile Cilantro Sauce.
To serve, divide romaine into four bowls. Top with quinoa, red bell pepper, black beans, and red onion. Pour cilantro sauce over the top, then garnish with chopped avocado and cilantro.
