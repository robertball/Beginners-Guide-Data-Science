Title:
Colorful Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385024.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, peeled quartered and sliced thinly (about 3 cups)
1 cup thinly sliced red cabbage
1 small can of red beans, rinsed and drained
1 cup of freshly shucked green peas, blanched and cooled
1 cup of cooked crawfish sliced lengthwise in half
Dressing:
the juice of 2 small limes
3 heaping tablespoons of plain yoghurt
1 t wasabi paste
1/2 fresh ginger peeled + minced or grated (about 1 t)
1 T snipped chives
about 20 large basil leaves, snipped (about 2 T)
1 T fresh cilantro, snipped
1/2 t salt
1/2 t pepper/spice blend
Garnish
fresh baby basil leaves
sunflower seeds

Directions:
Shuck the green peas while boiling the water to blanch them in. Blanch peas for 2 minutes, drain and chill with ice water.
Begin preparing the cucumber, cabbage, beans and crawfish, adding each to the same large mixing bowl. When cool, add the green peas.
Prepare the dressing by whisking all ingredients in a small bowl.
Pour over salad, mixing well.
Garnish with sunflower seeds and fresh baby basil leaves before serving on a bed of red leaf lettuce.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
A quick and crunchy salad to serve on a nice bed of red leaf lettuce with fresh homemade bread.
