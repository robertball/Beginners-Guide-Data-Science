Title:
Mussels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Net of Mussels (about 3lbs)
1 bunch of scallion chopped
1/2 cup of butter
1 Cup of white wine (Pinot Grigio) you can add or decrease the wine to your preference
3 finely chopped cloves of garlic

Directions:
Wash mussels and set aside.
Melt the butter in a large skillet and add garlic till slightly cooked. Add wine and scallions and simmer for 5 minutes.
Put mussels in the large skillet and put heat on high boil for 5 minutes covered. After 5 minutes shake the pot (or stir) and let boil for another 2 minutes.
Turn heat off and let mussels steam for 10 minutes.
