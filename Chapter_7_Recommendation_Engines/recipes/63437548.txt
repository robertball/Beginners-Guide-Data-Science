Title:
Orange Coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb large uncooked shrimp, peeled and deveined, seasoned with salt & pepper
2 T canola oil
2 T minced scallion whites
1 T minced garlic
1 c. fresh orange juice
1 T grated fresh ginger
1 T minced orange zest
1 c. coconut milk
1/2 t cornstarch
1/2 t red pepper flakes
1/4 c. thinly sliced scallion greens

Directions:
Saute shrimp in 1 T canola oil over medium high heat until pink and beginning to curl, 2-3 minutes. Remove from pan.
Heat remaining 1 T oil  in pan and add scallion whites and garlic.
Cook 1 minute.
Stir in orange juice, ginger and zest.
Cook to reduce mixture until it measures 1/2 c when strained, 3-5 minutes.
Discard solids and return juice to pan.
Whisk together coconut milk and cornstarch; stir into juice in pan, add pepper flakes, and bring to a boil over meduim high heat.
Cook sauce until slightly thickened. Season sauce with salt.
Toss shrimp in sauce, stir in scallion greens and cook 1-2 minutes more.
Serve shrimp with cooked rice or rice nooodles
