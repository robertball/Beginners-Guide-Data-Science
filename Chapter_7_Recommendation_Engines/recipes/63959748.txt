Title:
Sweet and Sour Warthog (pork) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1098711.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs pork steak, cubed;
1 cup flower;
1 tsp salt;
1 tsp pepper;
6 T olive oil;
1 clove garlic, minced;
1 (10oz) canned pineapple chunks, save juice;
1 medium onion, halfed and sliced;
1 green pepper, sliced;
1T cornstarch;
2T sugar;
1 tsp soy sauce;
1/2cup water;
3T vinegar;
1 chicken bouillon cube

Directions:
1.Mix flour, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl.
2.Roll cubed pork in flour mix.
3.Heat oil and brown pork on medium.
4.Lower heat and add garlic, drained pineapple, onion and green pepper.
5.Cook ten minutes or until tender.
6.While waiting prepare sauce. Mix cornstarch, sugar, soy sauce, water, vinegar, chicken bouillon and saved pineapple juice.
7.Add sauce to pork, and simmer 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Donovan Jones
Description: 
This recipe was perfected by my Grandmother while living in East Africa hence the warthog.  I hope your family likes it as much as ours has.
