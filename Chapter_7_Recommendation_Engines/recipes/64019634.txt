Title:
Ali's Amazing Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large tomatoes, coarsely chopped
1/2 sweet onion, chopped
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
1 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
2 lb. chopped cooked chicken breast
angel hair pasta cooked Eldente

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine tomatoes, onion, olive oil, oregano, basil and parsley. Set aside. Fill two quart pasta pan with water plus one to two teaspoons salt, and heat on medium/high heat until boiling. At boiling add angel hair pasta, stir immediately and every few minutes until pasta is freely moving in water. Cook until eldente or your preference. In the meantime, in nonstick skillet place one tablespoon of olive oil, chopped chicken breast that has been seasoned with basil, cilantro, Italian parsley and salt /pepper to taste. Over medium/low heat cook chicken until done and is a golden brown.
After draining and rinsing pasta place half on each of the two plates and divide the chicken basil mixture into two. Place one half over pasta on each plate and stir to mix pasta and chicken basil together well and serve with garlic toast or bread.
