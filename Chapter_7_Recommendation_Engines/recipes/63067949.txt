Title:
Zucchini Beef Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium zucchini's, sliced 1/4" thick
2 tbsp. oil
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 med. chopped onion
1/2 cup diced celery
1 can tomato sauce
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup red wine
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. oregano or basil
1/4 tsp. pepper
8 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*F. Lightly oil a 9x13 pan. Spread zucchini on bottom of pan. In a skillet heat oil and brown beef, onion, celery. Stir in tomato sauce, mushrooms, wine and spices. Spoon over zucchini. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake for 25 minutes or  until heated through and zucchini is tender.
