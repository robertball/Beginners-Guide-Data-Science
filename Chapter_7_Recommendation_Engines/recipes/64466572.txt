Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites -SupernaturalUnited Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup (half a stick) of butter
15 oz (1.5 packages) of mini marshmallows
6 cups Rice Krispies

Directions:
1. In a large saucepan, melt butter over low heat.
2. Add marshmallows to melted butter.
3. Stir frequently until marshmallows are completely melted.
4. Turn heat off and stir in Rice Krispies until coated.
6. Spray a 13 x 9 inch pan, or even slightly larger.
5. Using a greased or buttered spatula, press mixture into Greased pan.
6. Let cool
7. Cut into 1.5  inch "bite sized" squares
