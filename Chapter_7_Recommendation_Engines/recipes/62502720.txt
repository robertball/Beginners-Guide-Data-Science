Title:
Crisco Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Crisco butter flavor shortening
1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
2 Tbsp. milk
1 Tbsp. vanilla extract
1 large egg
1 3/4 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 F.
Beat shortening and brown sugar in large bowl with an electric mixer at medium speed until light and fluffy. Beat in milk, vanilla and egg. Stir in flour, salt and baking soda.
Blend in chocolate chips. Drop by rounded measuring tablespoonfuls 2-inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 8-10 minutes for chewy cookies. Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet. Ransfer cookies to cooling rack.
