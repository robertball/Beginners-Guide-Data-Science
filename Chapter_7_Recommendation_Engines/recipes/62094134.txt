Title:
Jackie's Awesome Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless chicken breasts
1 .7 oz. packet dry Italian dressing mix
2/3 cup olive oil
1/2 cup warm water
Juice of 1 medium lemon
2 cloves minced garlic (optional)

Directions:
Mix warm water with packet of Italian dressing mix.  Blend until most of the salt has melted.
Whisk in olive oil, lemon juice and garlic.  Whisk until marinade turns a little thick and opaque, about 2-3 min.
Place chicken parts in a large ziploc bag and pour dressing over chicken.  Marinate overnight or at least 8 hours.  Can also use thighs and legs with this recipe.  This recipe can marinate about 1 lb. of chicken.

Submitter: 
MommyFromSeattle
Description: 
A super-quick and flavorful chicken marinade that leaves chicken tender and moist.  Friends and family always asks me for the recipe.  Goes great with 'Awesome and Easy Creamy Corn Casserole' and good enough to serve to company.
