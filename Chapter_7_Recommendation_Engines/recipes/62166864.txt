Title:
Baked Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups milk
3 tablespoons flour
3 tablespoons butter
salt
pepper
onion, chopped
potatoes, thinly sliced

Directions:
1. In a bowl mix milk and flour until cloudy. heat with butter until thick and heavy over medium heat. stir constantly. add a couple sprinkles salt and pepper while cooking.
2. Butter a baking dish. Layer potatoes on bottom, sprinkle onions on top and pour milk mixture on top. repeat twice.
3. Bake at 350 for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
heatherkuehnel
Description: 
Extra creamy thinly sliced potatoes make an excellent side with meat is a personal favorite.I like making these for an after church lunch. I put them in the oven before I leave and they are finished by the time my guest arrive.
