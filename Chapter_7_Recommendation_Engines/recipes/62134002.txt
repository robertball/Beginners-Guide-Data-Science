Title:
Chicken Stuffed Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large zucchini
1 medium banana pepper, chopped
1 10.5 oz can cream of chicken soup
1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
1 cup shredded Colby cheese

Directions:
Slice zucchini in half and scoop out the inside of the zucchini (reserve the inside)
place zucchini shells, hallow side up, on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Cube the reserved zucchini and mix it with remaining ingredients (except the cheese) and fill zucchini shells with it.
Bake at 375 degrees for about 15 minutes, or until mixture is bubbly.
Sprinkle cheese over mixture and bake for 5-10 minutes longer, or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Cathy Zeltner
Description: 
I made up this recipe years ago. Its an easy way to use up extra zucchini from your garden. Its unique because instead of a traditional sausage filling, it is filled with chicken and banana peppers.
