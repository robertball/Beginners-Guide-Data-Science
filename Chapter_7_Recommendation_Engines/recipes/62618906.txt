Title:
West Indian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/570328.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, pressed
2 tsp ginger root, grated
4 tsp lime juice, freshly squeezed
salt and pepper to taste
2 chicken legs, backs attached, skinless
1 onion, finely chopped
1 1/2 green peppers, cut into strips
1 1/2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
1 1/2 tsp olive oil
2 tbsp curry powder (adjust to taste)
2/3 cups unsweetened coconut milk
1/2 cup water, approximately
3/4 tsp brown sugar

Directions:
Place garlic and ginger in a resealable plastic bag. Pour in lime juice, add salt and pepper. Separate chicken drumstick from thigh and put all pieces in the bag. Seal bag and toss to coat. Let stand in refrigerator overnight
Heat oil in sautepan or wok over med-high heat. Add chicken and saute throughly on each side until golden brown, about 8 minutes.
Take chicken out of pan, place on plate and keep warm in oven.
Lower heat to medium, add onion and peppers, saute 2-3 minutes.
Mis curry powder a little bit of coconut milk. Add to pan. Cook 2 minutes over low heat, stirring constantly.
Return chicken to pan, add remaining coconut milk and enough water to make mixture moist. Cook over medium heat, uncovered for about 15 minutes.
Add potatoes and brown sugar. Cover and cook an additional 45-50 minutes, until potatoes are soft.
Garnish with cilantro leaves (optional) and serve
