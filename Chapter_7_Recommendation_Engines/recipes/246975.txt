Title:
Energy Elixir Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4488543.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup spring salad greens, or to taste
1 cup frozen red grapes
1 chopped frozen banana
1 cored and chopped frozen pear
2 tablespoons walnuts
water as needed

Directions:
Layer salad greens, red grapes, banana, pear, and walnuts in a high-powered blender; add enough water to cover. Blend mixture until smooth, adding more water to reach desired consistency.
