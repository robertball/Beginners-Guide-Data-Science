Title:
Avocado Corn Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head, cleaned & chopped romaine lettuce
1/2 cup bottled Caesar salad dressing
2 ears corn, grilled and cooled then cut off of the cob
2 avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
18 cherry tomatoes, halved

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the corn, avocado, and tomatoes. Drizzle with dressing. Toss and serve over chopped romaine.
