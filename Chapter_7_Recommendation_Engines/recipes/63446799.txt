Title:
Spicy Kielbasa Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/935968.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ounces cream cheese, reduced fat or regular
1 cup mayonnaise, light or regular
1/2 cup Sargento Chef Blends Shredded 6 Cheese Italian
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
7 ounces kielbasa sausage (fully cooked), diced
1 jalapeno, diced (seeds and ribs removed as desired)
4 green onions, chopped
2 sourdough baguettes, sliced

Directions:
Place cream cheese in a medium sized microwavable bowl. Microwave for 25 seconds or till softened. Add remaining ingredients through the green onions and mix well to combine.
Transfer dip to a small baking dish. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes or till hot and bubbly.
Serve with baguette slices.

Submitter: 
Valerie Brunmeier
Description: 
Put this in front of the guys during the next game and watch it disappear! It's easy to control the heat to result in a dip that is mild, medium, or hot and spicy, to suit your taste.
