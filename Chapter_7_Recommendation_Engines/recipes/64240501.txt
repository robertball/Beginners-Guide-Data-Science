Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz can of all white chicken meat, 1/2 cup ranch dressing, 1/2 bottle franks red hot sauce, 1 bar cream cheese, 1 bag of shredded cheddar cheese.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in pan on stove top heat through and mix thoroughly.  Move to dish and sprinkle with cheddar cheese.

Andrew Lieser
