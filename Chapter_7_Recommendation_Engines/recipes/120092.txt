Title:
Dilly Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound whole fresh mushrooms
3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon snipped fresh dill
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon onion salt

Directions:
In a 1-qt. microwave-safe dish, combine all ingredients. Cover and cook on high for 3 minutes; stir. Cook 3 minutes longer or until mushrooms are tender. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
Kathy  Rairigh
Description: 
Kathy Rairigh dresses up fresh mushrooms with mild seasonings that let their flavor shine through. 'I've prepared this side dish often-mushroom lovers always come back for more,' reports the Milford, Indiana cook.
