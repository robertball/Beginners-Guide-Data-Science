Title:
Chicken Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fresh or frozen chopped onion
1/2 cup fresh or frozen chopped green pepper
1/3 cup slivered almonds
1/4 cup butter or margarine
2 1/2 cups cubed chicken
3/4 teaspoon salt
dash pepper
*********************************
6 slightly beaten eggs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In medium skillet cook onion, green pepper, and almonds in butter till veggies are tender but not brown.
Add chicken, salt and pepper; mix well.
Cover and cook till chicken is heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine eggs with cheese; pour over chicken.
Cook and stir gently over low heat till done, 7 to 10 minutes.
