Title:
Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
8 oz cool whip
Oreo cookie pie crust

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar and peanut butter well with mixer.  Stir in cool whip, beat until fluffy.  Pour into oreo pie crust.  Chill till set.
