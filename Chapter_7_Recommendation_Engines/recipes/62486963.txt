Title:
Chili Rellenos Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large Poblano chili peppers
16 oz Mexican cheese blend, shredded
4 eggs, beaten
1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
4 tomatoes, small (peeled)
2 garlic cloves
1 onion, small
1 Serrano chili peppers, small
1 T canola oil
1/2 c chicken broth (1/2 c water + 1/2 t bouillon)
1/2 t salt
1/8t ground cloves
1/8 t cinnamon
4 oz Mexican cheese blend, shredded

Directions:
Roast Poblano chilies under broiler till browned, turning once.
Remove roasted chilies from oven when done.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Slice open roasted chiles (in half)and remove seed bundles and stems.
Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Lay half of the roasted chilies evenly in bottom of baking dish.
Sprinkle with half of the Mexican cheese blend.
Cover with remaining chilies.
In a bowl, mix together the eggs, milk, and flour.
Pour over the top of the chilies.
Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine tomatoes, onion, garlic and Serrano chiles in blender and puree.
Heat oil in medium saucepan.
Add tomato mixture and cook 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add broth, salt, cloves and cinnamon.
Simmer gently for 15 minutes.
Remove baking dish from oven and pour tomato sauce evenly over the top.
Continue baking another 15 minutes.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese and serve.
