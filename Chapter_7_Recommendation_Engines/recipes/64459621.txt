Title:
French Onion Appetizer Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbs butter
4 cups onions, cut into 1/4 inch slices and halved
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tbs beef flavor bouillon
3 tbs Worcestershire sauce
1 10 oz. refrigerated pizza crust
1 tbs butter, melted
1 cup Parmesan cheese
2 tbs chopped fresh parsley or 2 tsp dried
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 425. Lightly grease cookie sheet. Melt 3 tbs butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add onions, bouillon, garlic and 2 tbs Worcestershire sauce, cook and stir 4-5 minutes or until onions are crisp-tender and sauce is absorbed. Unroll dough and place on greased cookie sheet,  starting in center, press into a 12x8 inch rectangle, pinching edges to form a rim. In a small bowl combine 1 tbs butter and remaining Worcestershire sauce, blend well. Brush evenly over dough, including rim. Spread onion mixture over entire crust. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, parsley and pepper. Bake at 425 for 15-18 minutes until golden brown. Cut into 12 pieces.
