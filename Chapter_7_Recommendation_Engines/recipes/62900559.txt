Title:
Andrea's Empanadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702979.jpg
Ingredients:
2 box's refrigerated pie dough
2 chicken breasts cooked and chopped
1 small bag of frozen peas
1 cup frozen potatoes
8 oz cream cheese at room temp
cheddar cheese grated
1 egg beaten

Directions:
preheat oven to 375
Mix all ingredients except egg in large bowl. Unroll pie dough onto greased cookie sheet, cover half of pie dough with mixture. Beat egg in small bowl and brush around edges of pie dough before closing. Once folded on half press edges with fork to seal in ingredients and brush dough with remaining egg wash. Cut two slits onto top of pie and bake for 20-30 until golden brown.
