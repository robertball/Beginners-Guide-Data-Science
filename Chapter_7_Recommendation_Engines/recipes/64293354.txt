Title:
Vegan "Cheesy" Poppyseed Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cup / 200g all-purpose flour or spelt
2 1/2 tablespoons / 30g poppy seeds
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons Nutritional Yeast Flakes
3/4 tablespoon ground flax mixed with
2 tablespoons water (let thicken)
1/2 cup / 120ml water

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 392°F/200°C.
In a bowl add in the dry ingredients, mix and then add in the wet ingredients. Mix and knead with your hand until it’s a smooth dough.
On a floured baking sheet roll out the dough until thin and even.
Cut it with a pizza cutter length- and width-wise.
Bake them in the oven for about 10 minutes or until they start to get a light brown color.
