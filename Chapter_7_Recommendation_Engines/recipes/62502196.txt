Title:
Square Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500469.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb lean Ground Beef
5 Potatoes, cubed
1 Onion, diced
1 l Beef Broth
1 l water
1 package Onion Soup Mix
Salt and Pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a large soup pot, over medium heat, brown the ground beef, drain off any excess fat. Add the onions and cook until translucent. Add potatoes, broth, water, and onion soup mix and bring to a boil, cook until potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Darren
Description: 
Pretty simple, but warms you up on a cold day. My mom used to make this recipe for us all the time growing up.
