Title:
Jambalaya That Pleases Everybody! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - diced
6 cups chicken broth
3 cups long grain white rice
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 green,yellow, red and orange bell peppers, seeded and chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
4 carrots, thinly sliced
2 stalks celery, thinly sliced
1/2 pond mushroom pieces,cooked and drained
pepper to taste
1 tsp chopped sage
1 tsp chopped parsley
1 tsp chopped basil (green and purple)

Directions:
Heat oil in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add onion and cook, stirring frequently until soft. Add the chicken and continue cooking and stirring so that it does not stick. When the chicken is browned, add the carrots, celery, mushrooms,and bell peppers
Pour in the broth, and bring to a boil. Add the rice, and season with pepper and herbs. Cover and cook over low heat for 20 minutes, until rice is tender and broth has been absorbed.
