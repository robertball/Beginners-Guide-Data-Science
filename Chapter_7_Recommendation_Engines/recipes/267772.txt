Title:
Air Fryer Roasted Okra Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5768211.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound okra, ends trimmed and pods sliced
1 teaspoon olive oil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine okra, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl and stir gently. Place in a single layer in the basket of the air fryer.
Cook in the air fryer for 5 minutes. Toss and cook 5 minutes more. Toss again and cook 2 more minutes. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Love the flavor of okra, but dislike the slimy texture? Air-fry the slime out of it!
