Title:
Yellow Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c.butter
2 c.white sugar
3 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 3/4 c. milk
2 3/4 c. flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease and flour one 9 x 13 inch pan.
Cream together shortening, sugar, eggs, vanilla salt and baking powder, and milk
Combine with flour and mix well. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for 45 minutes, or until inserted tooythpick comesout clean.  Allow to Cool.
