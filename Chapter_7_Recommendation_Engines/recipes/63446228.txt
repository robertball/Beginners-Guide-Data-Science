Title:
Great Garden Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5537647.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion, cut into wedges
1 zucchini, sliced
1 yellow squash, sliced
2 tbsp butter
1/4 tsp garlic salt
2 tomatoes, seeded and cut into wedges
2-3 tbsp Parmesan cheese (or other shredded cheese)

Directions:
Melt butter and sauté onions until translucent.
Add squashes and sauté another 5 minutes.
Add seasoning, tomatoes, and cheese, and sauté another minute.
