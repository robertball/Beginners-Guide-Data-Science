Title:
thekruser's World Class Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1 lb pork breakfast sausage
12 oz beer
1 45-oz jar Ragu Traditional spaghetti sauce
1 1-lb box spaghetti noodles
1/2 of a medium to large sized white onion
3-4 bay leaves
1 T Oregano
1 t Paprika
1 t garlic powder
1 t Thyme
1 t Cajun seasoning
1/2 t Black pepper
1/2 t Seasoned salt
2 T Basil
1 T Sugar

Directions:
Put hamburger and sausage in a large skillet.
Mix beer and all dry ingredients except basil and sugar in bowl. Pour over meat cook over high heat until almost brown.
While meat is browning, start water boiling for noodles. Put bay leaves in the water. Chop onion.
Once meat is almost brown, add onion. Onion needs to cook for appx 4 min.
When meat is completely browned, strain and put back in skillet. Reduce heat to med-low and add Ragu, basil, and sugar. Simmer uncovered.
Add noodles to water, which (hopefully) should be boiling by now. Cook per manufacturer's instructions.
When noodles are done, put in bowls and top with sauce.
Enjoy.

One of my signature dishes designed to be a notch above your standard spaghetti sauce and noodles quickie, but still relatively easy to prepare for those holy , your parents are coming to dinner moments.
