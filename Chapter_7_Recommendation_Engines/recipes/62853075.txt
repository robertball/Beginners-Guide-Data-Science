Title:
Pappy's Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 bottle Liquid Smoke
1/3 cup Kosher salt
2 TBL garlic powder
3 or 4 dashes of soy sauce
5 - 6 pound pork shoulder

Directions:
Wash and pat dry pork.
In small bowl combine the rest of ingredients. Rub on pork shoulder all over. With heavy duty foil, wrap pork well crimping all edges TIGHTLY. Wrap one more time sealing tightly. Place in sprayed baking dish fatty side up. Bake at 300 for 6 hours.
