Title:
Chimichurri Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/743886.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb flank steak
1 bunch of cilantro
3 cloves of garlic, chopped
3 T lemon juice
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/4 t cayenne pepper
1 t paprika

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a food processor and blend until combined. Save a few tablespoons of the sauce to serve with the steak and pour the rest into a ziploc with the steak. Refrigerate for an hour. Grill or cook in a grill pan for 5 minutes on the first side, flip, and cook another 2 or 3 minutes. Serve with the extra sauce

Submitter: 
tjmallick
Description: 
This sauce is so incredibly flavorful and the steak cooks so fast that no oil is even necessary.
