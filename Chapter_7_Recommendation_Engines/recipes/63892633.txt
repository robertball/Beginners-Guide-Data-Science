Title:
Shredded Potato Casserole Better Than Cracker Barrel's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 potatoes
1/4 c. butter, melted plus 1 T. solid
1 t. salt
1 c. sour cream
1/2 t. ground black pepper
1 t. garlic powder
1 small onion, chopped or 1 t. onion powder
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of cheddar cheese soup
1 c. shredded Cheddar cheese
1 c. shredded Colby cheese

Directions:
Peel potatoes and shred in food processor.
To remove starch from the potatoes, cover shredded potatoes with water and allow to soak for 10 minutes.
Drain and cover with water again. Place potatoes in a salad spinner and spin until dry.
Place potatoes in a bowl and microwave for 5-8 minutes. Drain potatoes and spin dry.
Grease a 9" x 13" casserole dish with 1 T. of butter.
In a bowl, place potatoes, and remaining ingredients. Combine gently.
Spoon into casserole dish.
Cover and refrigerate and chill for at least one hour or overnight.
Remove from refrigerator and allow to come to room temperature.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Bake uncovered for 35 minutes. Remove from oven and sprinkle cheese over the top.

Submitter: 
Shearone
Description: 
This is my version of Cracker Barrel's Hash Brown Casserole.  Be sure and use Colby and Cheddar Cheese!
