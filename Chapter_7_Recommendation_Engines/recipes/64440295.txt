Title:
Scrumptious Salisbury Steak in Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 egg
3 tablespoons crushed buttery round cracker crumbs
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon poultry seasoning
2 to 3 cups gravy of your choice

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, cracker crumbs, onion, salt, pepper and poultry seasoning using your hands. Shape into 6 patties about 1 inch thick.
Fry the patties in a large skillet over medium-high heat for 3 to 4 minutes per side, or until browned. Drain off grease, and remove patties to a platter; keep warm.
Poor gravy into skillet. Add patties to gravy and heat for about 10 min.
Put over top bow tie noodles.

Description: 
A classic Salisbury steak in gravy of your choice. This simple, hearty dish will remind you of Sunday dinners at grandma's house.  This is my husband's all-time favorite meal. The recipe makes plenty of gravy, so serve with mashed potatoes or buttered noodles.Modified from: Scrumptious Salisbury Steak in Mushroom Gravy
