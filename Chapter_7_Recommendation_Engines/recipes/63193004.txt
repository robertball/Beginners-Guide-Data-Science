Title:
Vegan Quick & Simple Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups of frozen strawberries
1.5 cups of vanilla or regular soy milk
1/4 cup sugar
(optional) 1/2 banana

Directions:
combine all ingredients into blender
blend on low until smoothie is creamy
may want to add more soy milk depending on how thick your smoothie turns out.

Submitter: 
ishallbevegan
Description: 
A quick and easy snack for any occasion. Triple recipe for large quantities and cut recipe in half for a simple yet satisfying tasty treat. 
Goes great with sandwiches on a hot summer day!
