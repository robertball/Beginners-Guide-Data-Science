Title:
Becky's Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BAKED OATMEAL
1/2 cup oil  (I use ¼ cup flaxseed oil and ¼ cup applesauce)
1 cup sugar.  (I use ¼ cup brown sugar)
2 eggs
3 cups oatmeal
¾ Tbs. baking powder
1 cup milk
2 ripe bananas, mashed
Can add raisins, cranberries, nuts, cinnamon, chopped apples, flaxseed, wheat germ, etc.

Directions:
Mix together with a spoon and pour into ungreased square pan.
Double recipe for 9x13 pan and after baking, wrap and freeze for individual servings
Bake 350 degrees for 40 minutes
Serve with or without milk.

Submitter: 
Mara
Description: 
I had this recipe for Baked Oatmeal while in the Dominican Republic on a mission trip.  Everyone loved it and asked for it often. With less sugar it is a healthy breakfast.
