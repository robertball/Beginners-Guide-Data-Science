Title:
Honey Garlic Chicken - crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
1 lb red potatoes, cut in half (if using baby red potatoes, do not cut in half - leave whole)
1lb bag baby carrots
1lb green beans, fresh & trimmed
3/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup honey
2 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried thyme
6 garlic cloves, minced
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients for the sauce - ketchup, brown sugar, soy sauce, honey, basil, thyme, garlic, pepper.
Place chicken in the center of the crockpot (pile up it up and leave room around perimeter of crockpot.
Add potatoes to the perimeter of crockpot
Add carrots on top of potatoes
Pour the sauce over the chicken
Cover and cook for 4  hours on high, or 6 hours on low
After cooking time is up - add green beans on top of the food.
Continue to cook for 1 more hour or until green beans & other veggies are soft.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
sweet & savory chicken cooked in crockpot with carrots, green beans & potatoes. You can use any type of chicken but I recommend bone in chicken thighs for the most flavor. If your crockpot is big enough you can also add a few drumsticks.
