Title:
Hot Carmel Apple cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of Apple cider
1 can of whipped cream (Reddi wip) does better
1 jar of Smuckers carmel topping (the kind you would use for ice cream)

Directions:
Heat a cup of apple cider in the microwave.
Add 2 teaspoons of carmel toping( or to your taste. )
Top generously with the whipped cream. Slowly stir and sip your way into heaven.

Submitter: 
Levixen
Description: 
This is soooo good. Just like you would buy from the Coffee shops. My son asks for this every year in the fall. I've tried to subsitute the ingredients with the no name brands, but they just don't have that flavor like the name brands do. So for that really nice treat for yourself and your family, I spend the extra pennies for the ingredients.
