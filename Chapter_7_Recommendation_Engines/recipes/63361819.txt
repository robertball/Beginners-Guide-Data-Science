Title:
Diet/Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon plus one teaspoon canola oil
1 clove garlic
1/2 cup sliced celery
2 carrots*, shredded or chopped fine
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
2 cups chopped fresh mushrooms (or 1 large can)
2 large cans of tomatoes (I use whole tomatoes breaking them up)
2 cups of tomato sauce
1 can red kidney beans
1 can chick peas, drain
1 1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 t. salt, optional
1 fresh jalapeno, diced, optional
2 ounces Monterey Jack or Pepper Jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
In a large soup pot saute the garlic, celery, carrots, onion and bell pepper until tender.  Do not burn the garlic.
Add; mushrooms, cans of tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, chick peas, cumin, black pepper, chili powder.  Add salt and jalapeno if desired.  Bring to a boil and simmer for at least 30 minutes.
Add 2 ounces of cheese.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is a vegetarian chili, it's called 'diet chili' because it has no meat.
