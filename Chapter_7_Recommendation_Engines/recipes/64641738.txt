Title:
Bagel Egg in a Hole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bagel
1 egg
2 tablespoons cream cheese
4 slices lox
1 teaspoon capers
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Slice bagel in half. Melt butter in skillet and add the bagel, sliced side down. After 3-4 minutes over medium heat, crack the egg in the hole of one half of the bagel. Season with salt and pepper.
Remove the second half and spread the cream cheese, add the capers and top with the lox. Once the egg is cooked to your degree of doneness, place the cream cheese and lox side to create a sandwich.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Leslie Kelly
Description: 
These beloved bread products make for a perfect platform for the classic preparation of frying an egg in a bread product.
