Title:
Pulled Chicken Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/889519.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) pouch Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ fire roasted tomato cooking sauce
2 tablespoons honey Dijon mustard
5 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
3 cups shredded deli rotisserie chicken
4 soft burger buns, split
1 (14 ounce) container prepared coleslaw (optional)

Directions:
Mix cooking sauce, mustard and Worcestershire sauce in 3-quart saucepan.
Heat to simmering over medium-high heat. Stir in chicken. Reduce heat to medium; simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve chicken mixture on buns. Top each with coleslaw.
