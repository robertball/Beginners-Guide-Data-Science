Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Tenderloin/Pork Loin Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) pork tenderloin or pork loin roast
1 onion, cut into rings
1 cup water
3/4 cup red wine
3 tablespoons minced garlic
3 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
freshly ground black pepper to taste
Mrs Dash Garlic & Herb Seasoning to taste
2 tablespoons cornstarch
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Sprinkle Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb Seasoning all over the pork.
In a large skillet sear pork, about 3 minutes per side.
Place pork in a slow cooker.
Pour 1 cup water water, wine, and soy sauce over the top, turning the pork to coat.
Carefully spread garlic and onion rings over the pork, leaving as much on top of the roast during cooking as possible.
Sprinkle with ground black pepper, cover, and cook on "HIGH" setting for 4 hours; remove from crockpot, cover with foil and set aside.
Remove onion rings and discard.
Mix 2 tablespoons cornstarch and 2 tablespoons water; add to the crockpot and cook an additional 10 minutes to thicken gravy.
Slice pork when gravy is finished cooking.
Serve with mashed potatoes and green beans, broccoli or asparagus.
