Title:
Garlic Egg Drop Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
1-2 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
1/4 teaspoon vinegar
1/2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp cornstarch
1 tbsp warm water
2 eggs - lightly beaten
1 green onion, chopped thinly

Directions:
Bring chicken broth and garlic to boil then simmer 5 minutes.
Add vinegar and soy sauce.
Beat eggs until mixed but not frothy and set aside.
Mix cornstarch with water and slowly add to broth.
Slowly pour eggs into broth while stirring quickly with a spoon.
Take off heat and serve. Garnish with green onion.

Submitter: 
Tamaralynn
Description: 
Want a nice twist to your resturaunt style egg drop soup?  This will warm your tummy on a cold winter day with just a few minutes prep time.
