Title:
peanut butter rice krispies bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup corn syrup
1/2 cup white sugar
1 1/2 cups peanut butter
6 cups crisp rice cereal
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
(or other flavor chips)

Directions:
Butter a 9x13 inch pan.
melt sugar, syrup, and peanut butter over low heat.Take off heat and stir in cereal.Pour into pan and top with chocolate chips. Bake at 350 for 60 seconds to melt the frosting.(or melt in the microwave)
