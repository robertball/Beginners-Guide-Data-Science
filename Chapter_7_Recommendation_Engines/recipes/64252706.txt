Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can chicken breast (fresh chicken breast probably tastes better, but the canned is easier!)
1 package (8oz) low fat cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1/2 cup Frank's red hot
Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Add chicken and red hot to a saucepan or skillet.  Heat through.
Add cream cheese and ranch dressing to chicken mixture.  Heat and stir until well-blended.
Transfer to serving dish to to mini crock pot to keep warm.
Serve with tortilla chips.
