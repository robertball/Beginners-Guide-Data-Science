Title:
Chipotle Chicken and Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons ground cumin
4 (15 1/2 ounce) can navy beans , rinsed and drained
4 (14 1/2 ounce) can no-salt-added stewed tomatoes
4 (14 ounce) can reduced-sodium fat-free chicken broth
4 canned chipotle chile in adobo , finely chopped
4 cups chopped cooked chicken breasts (about 1/2 pound)
1/2 cup reduced-fat sour cream
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large saucepan; bring to a boil.
Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes.
Partially mash tomatoes and beans with a potato masher.
Stir in chicken; cook 2 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
Place 1 1/4 cups soup in each of four bowls. Top each serving with 2 tablespoons sour cream and 1 tablespoon cilantro.
