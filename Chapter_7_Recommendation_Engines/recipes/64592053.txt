Title:
Apple Pie Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 tbsp. hot water
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. salt
2 1/2 cups peeled and sliced/diced apples
1/2 cup chopped walnuts, optional
1/2 cup raisins, optional

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar well.
Beat in egg, vanilla and hot water.
Stir in next 5 ingredients.
Add apples, nuts and raisins. Stir.
Bake in greased 9" pie plate @ 350 x 40 minutes, or until tests done with toothpick.
Serve warm or cold. Delicious with ice cream or whipped cream topping. Could also drizzle with caramel dessert topping!

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Apple cake baked in a pie plate. A quick, easy recipe to assemble and a delicious, moist, spiced apple dessert.
