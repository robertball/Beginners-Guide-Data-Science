Title:
Ooey Gooey Peanut Butter Cup Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4548426.jpg
Ingredients:
1.  Crust:
One box chocolate cake mix
½ cup melted butter
1 egg
2.  Filling:
One 8 oz package of softened cream cheese
2 eggs
1 teaspoon of vanilla
½ cup melted butter
1 lb of powdered sugar
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup crushed miniature peanut butter cups
3. Topping:
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1/3 cup crushed miniature peanut butter cups

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Crust:  In medium bowl, combine crust ingredients. Mix well. Pat into a lightly greased 13 x 9-inch baking pan.
Filling: In medium bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Mix in eggs and vanilla.  Add butter, powdered sugar, peanut butter and crushed peanut butter cups. Mix well.  Spread mixture over crust and bake for 40 to 50 minutes. Expect the center to be gooey.  (Don’t over-bake).
Topping: In a small bowl, mix chips and crushed peanut butter cups.  After taking the bars out of the oven, wait a few minutes and then spread chip mixture over the top, so as to melt.
