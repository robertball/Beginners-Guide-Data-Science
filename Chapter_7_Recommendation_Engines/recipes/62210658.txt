Title:
Martha's Coconut Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
125 grams Butter
100 grams Sugar
1 packet Vanilla Sugar
1 Egg
200 grams Flour
1 teaspoon Baking Soda
100 grams grated Coconut
Juice and Zest from 1/2 a Lemon

Directions:
Mix ingredients together.
Roll into balls and line on baking sheet.
Bake at 150ºC for about 20 minutes.
