Title:
Chocolate Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782713.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour (self-rising)
1 stick margarine
1/2 cup chopped nuts (I like pecans)
8 oz package of Cream Cheese
1 cup sugar
8  oz container of Cool Whip
5.9 oz package of Chocolate Pudding
3 cups Milk

Directions:
Make crust of flour, margarine and nuts.  Mix and press into bottom of casserole dish.  Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
Make filling of cream cheese, sugar & 1/2 of Cool Whip.  Mix and layer over crust.
Mix pudding mix with milk and beat until thick.  Spread over cream cheese.  Spread remaining Cool Whip over top.  May sprinkle with nuts.  Chill.
NOTE: This torte can be made in different flavors, such as butterscotch instead of chocolate pudding.

Submitter: 
Christina Sealey Czysz
Description: 
This a simple recipe that is my family's favorite!!  My mom got the recipe from a 1979 PFW Baptist Ladies Auxiliary Cookbook.
