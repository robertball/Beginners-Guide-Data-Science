Title:
Chocolate Crackle Top Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter - softened
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 egg
1/2 cup light molasses
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/2 cups flour
Sugar to roll cookies in

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream together butter and sugar.
Add molasses, salt, baking soda, egg, vanilla and cinnamon and mix well.
Add flour and stir until just blended.
Form into 1" balls and roll in sugar.
Bake for 8-9 minutes or until they puff and start to crack.
