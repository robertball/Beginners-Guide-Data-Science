Title:
Chocolate-Lava Cakes Made with Bisquick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1/4 c each spreadable butter and semisweet chocolate chips, melted
1/2 c skim milk
2 eggs
1 box 1.4oz sugar free fat-free instant chocolate pudding mix, like jell-o
2/3 c bisqu

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 425 degree F. In bowl, combine first 2 ingredients. Whisk in milk and eggs. Stir in remaining ingredients. Divide batter evenly among four 4oz. greased and wax paper-lined ramekins. Bake 10 min. or until edges are firm and center is slightly soft. Run knife around edges of cakes to loosen; invert onto plates. Remove paper. Garnish as desired. Serve immediately.
