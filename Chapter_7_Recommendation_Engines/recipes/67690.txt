Title:
Honey Glazed Chicken Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976166.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) can DOLEÂ® Pineapple Chunks or Pineapple Slices
3 tablespoons honey
1 1/2 teaspoons soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
5 chicken breasts

Directions:
Drain pineapple; reserve 1 tablespoon juice.
Combine reserved juice, honey, soy sauce and garlic powder in small bowl; set aside.
Place chicken in single layer in foil-lined 13 x 9-inch baking pan. Pour pineapple chunks over chicken. Pour reserved sauce over chicken and pineapple.
Bake at 400 degrees F., 20 to 25 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink in center. Spoon sauce over chicken and pineapple.  Serve with green beans and roasted potatoes, if desired.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
Can you say easy? Simply combine reserved pineapple juice with ginger, honey, and soy sauce for an unforgettable teriyaki chicken marinade.
