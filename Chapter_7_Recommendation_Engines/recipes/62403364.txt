Title:
Pecan Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C butter
1/4C powdered sugar
1/4tsp salt
1tsp vanilla
1Tbsp water
2C flour
2C grated pecans

Directions:
Cream butter, blend in sugar, salt, sugar,vanill. Once well blended add the water, flour and pecans. Mix well. Chill dough 2 hours. Shape dough into finger like ovals. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet for 8 minutes at 350 degree's.
