Title:
Nutella Swirl Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 tablespoon butter, softened
3/4 cup white sugar
3 eggs
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour, sifted
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon baking powder
Nutella, about 1/3 cup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325F.
Line 12 muffin tins with paper liners.
Cream butter and sugar til fluffy.
Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla, flour, salt and baking powder.
Fill each muffin liner 3/4 full with batter.
Top each muffin with 1 heaping teaspoon Nutella.
Swirl Nutella in with a toothpick, folding some batter over the nutella.
Bake 20 minutes.
May appear moist when you do toothpick check, but they are done after 20 minutes.
Cool on wire rack.

Submitter: 
CosmicMojo
Description: 
Rich buttery cupcakes with a swirl of Nutella that bakes the frosting in the top. A woman at work made these and I love the rich buttery Nutella taste.
