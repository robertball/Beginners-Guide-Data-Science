Title:
Raspberry Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix and put in 9X12 pan:
16 graham crackers
1 cube melted butter
1/2 c. sugar
Beat together with mixer:
2 eggs
1 8oz. pkg cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp vanilla
4 c. raspberries

Directions:
Pour cream cheese mixture over cracker mix and bake 20 minutes at 375 degrees.
Cook 1 qt berries and thicken with corn starch. Add sugar to taste. When cool, spread over baked mixture. Serve with whipped cream.
