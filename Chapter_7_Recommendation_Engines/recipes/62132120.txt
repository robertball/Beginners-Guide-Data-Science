Title:
Banana Honey Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups baking mix
1/4 cup whole wheat flour
2 very ripe mashed bananas
1 tablespoon canola oil
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup milk
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
and a dash of nutmeg

Directions:
So simple... Mix all ingredients just until blended.
Drop by heaping tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 450 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
Mix 1 tablespoon of melted butter with 1 teaspoon of honey.
Brush over warm biscuits as soon as they come out of the oven.

Submitter: 
Carol S. Margerum
Description: 
These are great hearty biscuits with a touch of sweetness... wonderful for breakfast on a special day or to serve to your guests at brunch.
