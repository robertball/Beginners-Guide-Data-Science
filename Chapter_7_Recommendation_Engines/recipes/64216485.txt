Title:
Nutty Lentil Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large carrots, finely chopped
1 yellow onion, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
4 celery stalks, finely chopped
1 tomato, finely chopped
2 cups lentils, cooked
½ cup oats, dried
2 slices sprouted bread, finely diced
1 cup walnuts, crushed
¼ cup goji or cranberries
3 tablespoons flaxseed + ½ cup water
1 tablespoon rosemary
1 teaspoon thyme
2 teaspoons sage
sea salt
pepper
black pepper as desire

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a non stick skillet, saute chopped carrots, onions, garlic cloves, celery, and tomatoes in water. Saute for about 10 minutes or until tender.  Transfer to large bowl when done.
Thoroughly combine in the bowl the cooked lentils, oats, diced sprouted bread, walnuts, berries, flaxseed + water or eggs, spices, salt and pepper.
Place mixture into parchment lined loaf pan and firmly press down.
Bake at 350 degrees. Allow to cook for about 45 minutes - 1 hour or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
No oil added, vegan loaf good for main dish and sandwiches.
