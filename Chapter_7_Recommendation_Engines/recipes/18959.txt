Title:
Peanut Butter and Jelly Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/829361.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
2 tablespoons blackberry jelly
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 bananas, frozen and chunked
2 tablespoons honey
2 teaspoons wheat germ

Directions:
In a blender combine milk, jelly, peanut butter, banana, honey and wheat germ. Blend until smooth.
