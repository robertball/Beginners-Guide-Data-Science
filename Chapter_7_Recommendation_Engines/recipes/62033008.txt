Title:
Moms Frozen Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces cream cheese
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup heavy cream
3 1/2 cups canned fruit cocktail
1/2 cup chopped nuts (walnuts)
few grains salt

Directions:
Mash cheese, add mayonnaise.  Whip cream, fold into cheese mixture.  Drain fruit cocktail.  Fold fruit, nuts, and salt into first mixture.  pack into deep pan.  Freeze firm.  Serve on lettuce if desired.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This is a very delicious salad.  My mom makes it for all the special holidays.  The whole family loves it.
