Title:
Pulled Pork in a Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 pound) tenderloin of pork
1 1/2 tablespoons sea salt
1 tablespoon liquid smoke flavoring
Spoon of minced garlic
1/2 cup broth

Directions:
Pierce pork all over with a carving fork. Rub salt and garlic then liquid smoke over meat.  Place roast in a slow cooker.
Cover, and cook on Low for 16 to 20 hours, turning once during cooking time.
Remove meat from slow cooker, and shred, adding drippings as needed to moisten.  Put back in the slow cooker to heat.
Serve with bbq sauce on the side.
Reheat the next day with more broth as needed.  Freezes well!
