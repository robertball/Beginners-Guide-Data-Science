Title:
Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup skim milk
2 TBSP all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/2 cup chopped onion
Nonstick cooking spray
4 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
For sauce, in a small saucepan, stir together milk, flour, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.  Cook and stir over medium heat til thickened and bubbly.  Stir in onion.
Spray a 1 1/2 quart casserole with nonstick cooking spray.  Place half of the potatoes in the casserole.  Top with half of the sauce.  Repeat layers with remaining potatoes and sauce.  Bake, covered, in a 350 degree oven for about 65 minutes or until potatoes are tender, stirring once.  Remove from oven and sprinkle with cheese.  Cover and let stand 1 to 2 minutes or until cheese is melted.
