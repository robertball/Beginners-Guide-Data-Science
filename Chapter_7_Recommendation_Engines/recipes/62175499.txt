Title:
Cactus Salad (Ensalada de Nopales) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/439431.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Jar bottled Nopales (Cactus)
1 Medium white Onion-Diced
4 Roma Tomatoes-Diced
1/2 Bunch Cilantro-diced thin
1 Jalapeno Pepper diced (do not remove seeds)(optional)
1/2 Teaspoon Mexican Oregano
1/2 teaspoon cumin
Juice of 2 Limes
1/2 cup Cojita cheese (if desired)

Directions:
1.Drain and rinse the nopales in colander. Set them aside in a dish.
2.Add the diced onion, tomatoes, cilantro,jalapeno, cumin and oregano to the dish with nopales. Mix it well.
3.Take the juice of 2 limes and pour over the top of the mixture of nopales. Sprinkle cheese on top(if desired).
4.Refridgerate about 1 hour before serving. Serve on top of carnitas!

Description: 
This is a salad that is great served on top of carnitas or any tacos!
