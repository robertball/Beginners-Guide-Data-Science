Title:
Sweet Chili Thai Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/784268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup rice vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
2 teaspoons fresh ginger root, minced
1 teaspoon garlic, minced
2 teaspoons hot chile oil or to taste
2 teaspoons ketchup
4 teaspoons cornstarch

Directions:
Pour water and vinegar into a saucepan, and bring to a boil over high heat. Stir in sugar, ginger, garlic, chile pepper, and ketchup; simmer for 5 minutes. Stir in cornstarch. Remove saucepan from stove to cool. Then transfer to a bowl, cover, and refrigerate until needed.

Submitter: 
Sanderson32
Description: 
A savory sauce to use as a dip with shrimp or spring rolls, in stir-fries and noodle dishes, even on whole wheat pizza crust instead of typical pizza sauce with feta, sun-dried tomatoes, chicken, and vegetables.Modified from: Sweet Chili Thai Sauce
