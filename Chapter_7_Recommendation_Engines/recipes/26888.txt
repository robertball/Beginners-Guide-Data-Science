Title:
Salsa Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can ORTEGA® Refried Beans
1/2 cup ORTEGA® Thick & Chunky Salsa
1/2 cup SARGENTO® ChefStyle Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
tortilla chips

Directions:
HEAT beans, salsa and cheese in medium saucepan over medium-high heat for 3 to 4 minutes or until bubbly and cheese is melted.  Serve with chips.
VARIATIONS:  Add 1/4 cup ORTEGA (R) Diced Green Chiles or 2 teaspoons ORTEGA (R) Diced Jalapenos.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Whether you make it for half-time or make it all the time, this dip is a tasty Ortega ® snack you can whip up in minutes. There will be cheering all the way around whether there's a game or not.
