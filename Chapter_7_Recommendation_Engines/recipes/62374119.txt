Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413223.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
2 eggs (well beaten)
1 3/4 cups All Purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda dissolved in 3 tsp. warm water
2 Tbsp. ground flaxseed (optional)
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
3 mashed bananas
Chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Beat together shortning and sugar with electric mixer until creamy, about 2 to 3 min.
Scrape down sides of bowl.
Add eggs, flour, flaxseed(optional), baking soda/water mixture, salt, and vanilla to the sugar and shortning mixture. Mix until well incorporated, about 1 min.
Add mashed bananas.  Mix until just blended.
Fold in nuts if desired.
Pour batter into ungreased  11 3/4" X 5 1/2" X 3 1/4" loaf pan.
Bake for 60 min. on center rack.
Cool in pan before removing, about 1 hour.
When bread is completely cooled cut into desired slices.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
This banana bread recipe has been in my family for 4 generations.  It is very quick and easy to make. I have found this bread to be very moist and delicious.
