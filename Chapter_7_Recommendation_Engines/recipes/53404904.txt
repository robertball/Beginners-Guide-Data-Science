Title:
Monte Cristo Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
2 slices cooked ham
2 slices cooked turkey meat
1 slice Swiss cheese
1 egg
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Spread bread with mayonnaise and mustard.   Alternate ham, Swiss and turkey slices on bread.
Beat egg and milk in a small bowl. Dip then flip the sandwich quickly into the egg and milk mixture.  Heat a greased skillet over MEDIUM heat, brown the sandwich on both sides.   Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
A quick and easy sandwich with ham, turkey and swiss slices, dipped in an egg/milk mixture and fried to a golden brown.  A hot alternative to the usual lunch or snack.  Try it with berry jam on the side.Modified from: Monte Cristo Sandwich
