Title:
Irish Car Bomb Cocktail  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/994831.jpg
Ingredients:
8 fluid ounces stout beer (such as Guinness®)
1 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur (such as Bailey's®)
1/2 fluid ounce Irish whiskey (such as Jameson®)

Directions:
Pour beer into a pint glass. Pour Irish cream and Irish whiskey into a shot glass. Gently drop the shot glass into the glass of beer.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
This classic St. Patrick's Day drink is simply a shot glass filled with Irish cream and Irish whiskey, dropped into a glass of stout beer. The combination of the three will knock you off your feet!
