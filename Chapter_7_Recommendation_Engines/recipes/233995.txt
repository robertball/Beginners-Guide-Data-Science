Slow Cooker Beef Stew by McCormick速 Recipe

Ingredients:
3 cups water
1 package McCormick速 Beef Stew Seasoning Mix
2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/4 cup flour
1 1/4 cups cut-up fresh potatoes
1 1/4 cups cut-up fresh carrots
1 1/4 cups cut-up fresh celery
1 1/4 cups sliced onions

Directions:
Mix vegetables, water and Seasoning Mix in slow cooker.
Coat beef with flour. Stir into ingredients in slow cooker. Cover.
Cook 8 hours on LOW or 5 hours on HIGH until beef is tender.
