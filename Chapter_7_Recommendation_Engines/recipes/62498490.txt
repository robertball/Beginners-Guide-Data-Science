Title:
Squash & Ginger Hot and Sour Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 T vegetable oil
1 T minced garlic
1-inch peeled ginger root, julienned fine
1/2 T toasted sesame oil
2 cups butternut squash, peeled, seeded, julienned small
1/2 cup small button mushrooms, sliced very thin (optional)
4 cups vegetable broth
1/4 c natural rice wine vinegar
1/8 cup soy sauce (more or less, depending on your broth)
1/4 t cayenne pepper or 1 tsp red chili flakes
cilantro sprigs

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil to medium in small saute pan.  Add garlic and ginger, saute two minutes, being careful not to let it burn.  Add squash and mushrooms, cook five minutes, stirring constantly. Turn off heat and toss with sesame oil
Meanwhile, heat broth to high simmer. Add vinegar, soy sauce and pepper.
Add vegetables to broth.  Cook for about five minutes until squash is tender.
Ladle into cups, top with cilantro leaves, and serve.
