Title:
French Toast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1 cup brown sugar
French or crusty bread, enough to cover a 9 x 12 inch pan
6 eggs
2 cups milk
2 tsp. vanilla extract
Cinnamon

Directions:
In a sauce pan, combine butter and brown sugar.
Cook until blended.
Pour into greased 9 x 12 dish. Layer bread, cut or ripped into bite sized pieces. Needs to be at least 1 1/2 to 2 inches thick.
Mix eggs, milk and vanilla. Pour mixture over bread. Make sure all bread had absorbed liquid.
Top with sprinkled cinnamon.
Cover with saran wrap and soak overnight.
Sprinkle brown sugar on top and place in preheated oven at 450 degrees for 30 minutes until it browns and bubbles.

Submitter: 
Heather Bogle
Description: 
Delicious and decadent, simple spin on French toast. Got the idea from a bed and breakfast in the south.
