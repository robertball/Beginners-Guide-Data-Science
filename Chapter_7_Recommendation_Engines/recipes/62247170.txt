Title:
Curry Tomato & Green Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
300 g, One can of Condense Tomato Soup
400 g, One can of Green Beans (any style)
15 mL lemon juice
15 mL curry powder
5 mL garlic power
5 mL onion power
5 mL black pepper

Directions:
Combine soup, one half can of water, beans with liquid, and lemon juice to pot and add rest of ingredients to taste.
Heat to a light boil.

Spice up your winter day with this easy tomato soup.
