Title:
Mint Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bars:
1 sq. unsweetened chocolate
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. butter
1/4 c. flour
1 egg
Filling:
2 T. margarine
1 T. milk
1 c. powdered sugar
1/4 t. mint flavor (not peppermint)
Glaze:
1 T. butter
1 sq. unsweetened chocolate

Directions:
Bars: Melt chocolate and butter. Let cool.
Beat egg for 2 minutes.
Stir in chocolate mixture and sugar. Add flour.
Bake 20 minutes at 350 in greased 8x8 in. pan. Cool
Filling: Blend all and spread over cooled bar layer.
Chill at least 10 minutes.
Glaze: Melt ingredients together and pour over filling layer, tilting pan for glaze to flow evenly.
Chill for 15 minutes.
