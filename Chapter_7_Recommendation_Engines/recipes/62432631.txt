Title:
Indian Fry Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups warm water
2 Tbsp. sugar
2 tsp. salt
2 Tbsp. margarine/butter/oil
6 cups flour
1 Tbsp. yeast

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients in one bowl (water, oil, sugar, salt).
Pour flour on top of wet mix, then put yeast over top.
Lightly mix yeast in with flour, and then mix it all together.
Fry round flat bread in frying pan and serve with taco fillings.
