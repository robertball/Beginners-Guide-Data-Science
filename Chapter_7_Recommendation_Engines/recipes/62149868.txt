Title:
Winter Endive Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads endive
1 bunch watercress, rinsed and dried
1 small shallot
1 pomegranate, seeded
Juice and zest of 1 lemon
6 Tbsp. Olive oil (or to taste)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
On a mandoline, or with a knife, finely slice the endive and shallot into thin pieces.
Lay the watercress on a platter.  Arrange the endive and shallot over the top and sprinkle with the pomegranate seeds.
Combine the lemon juice, zest, olive oil, salt and pepper. Taste and adjust the lemon juice/olive oil to your taste. Drizzle over the salad right before serving.

Submitter: 
Mama_B
Description: 
This very simple salad makes great use of heartier greens like endive and cress, and balances their bite with the sweetness of pomegranate.
