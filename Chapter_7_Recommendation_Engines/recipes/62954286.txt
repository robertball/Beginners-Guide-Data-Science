Title:
My Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 granny smith apples
juice of 1 lemon
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup golden raisins
1/4 cup seedless grapes, halved
1/4 cup half and half
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sugar or Splenda

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together the apples and lemon juice. Stir in the celery, nuts, raisins and grapes.
Fold together the cream, sugar and mayonnaise; add to salad and mix well.
Chill before serving.

This is my version of the classic salad with apples, grapes, celery, golden raisins and nuts.Modified from: Waldorf Salad I
