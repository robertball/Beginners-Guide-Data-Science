Title:
Chicken with Rosemary and Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. boneless chicken cutlets
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. fresh ground pepper
1/4 cup chopped pancetta
2 red bell peppers, cut into strips (about 2 cups)
1/2 cup thinly sliced shallots
2 tsp. lemon zest
2 tsp. garlic powder
2 tsp. crushed rosemary
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken stock
4 tsp. flour
1/4 cup pitted green olives, quartered

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper.
Heat oil in skillet, add chicken and cook 5 minutes on each side or until browned on both sides.
Remove chicken from skillet and add pancetta, cook about 2 minutes or until crisp.
Remove pancetta from skillet.
Add peppers, shallots, zest, garlic powder and rosemary to skillet.
Cook and stir 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Add wine, reduce heat to low and simmer about 2 minutes or until reduced by half.
Mix broth and flour and add to skillet.
Bring to a boil then add chicken.
Cover and simmer about 8 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and sauce is slightly thickened.
Sprinkle with olives and pancetta.
Serve over orzo or rice if desired.
