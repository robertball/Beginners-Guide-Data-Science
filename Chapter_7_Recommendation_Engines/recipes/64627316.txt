Title:
Chicken Parmesan Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken thighs, diced
½ teaspoon kosher salt
¼ teaspoon black pepper
½ teaspoon garlic powder
1  24-ounce jar marinara sauce
½ cup shredded Parmesan
1­½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup panko, toasted
2 tablespoons olive oil
¼ cup fresh herbs (parsley, basil, oregano, etc.), chopped

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
2. Spray an 11­½-inch-by-7­½-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
3. Place the chopped chicken into the pan.
4. Season  with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
5. Pour the marinara sauce over the top and stir to mix.
6. Sprinkle the two cheeses over the top of the chicken and sauce.
7. In a small bowl, combine the panko, herbs and olive oil.
8.Spread the seasoned panko evenly over the top of the cheese layer.
9. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
10. Remove foil and continue to bake for an additional 15 minutes, until the chicken has cooked through and the casserole has begun to brown and is bubbling.
11. Serve over spaghetti.
12. You can also make this recipe ahead of time and freeze it it! Just let it thaw in you refrigerator overnight, then bake.
