Title:
Summer Salmon Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/669601.jpg
Ingredients:
4 small corn tortillas
1 large salmon fillet
1 avocado
1/2 cup green onions chopped
1/2 cup radishes shredded
1 cup cherry tomatoes quartered
1 15 oz can black beans
1/4 cup low-fat sour cream
1/4 cup prepared salsa
1/2 tsp cilantro
1 tsp hot sauce
1/2 tsp chilli powder
1/2 tsp cumin
garlic salt
pepper
canola oil spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. Coat each side of corn tortillas with canola oil and place on baking sheet. Place salmon in a baking dish and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and cilantro.
Coarsely purée black beans, hot sauce, cilantro, chili powder, and cumin to form a thick, chunky paste. Set aside.
place tortillas on top rack and salmon on bottom. Turn tortillas periodically and back until crispy (approximately 15 minutes). Remove salmon when it reaches 145° (approximately 20 minutes).
Spread 1/4 of the bean paste onto each tortilla. Top with diced tomatoes and avocado. After salmon has cooled, lightly break apart and spread onto each tostada. Top with green onion, radish, salsa, and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kays
Description: 
These tostadas are quite the catch! Pile them high with black beans, avocado, tomato, radish, and green onions. Finish off with a dollop of sour cream.
