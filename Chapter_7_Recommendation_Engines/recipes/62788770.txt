Title:
Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sm. Yellow Squash, cut into 1-in. squares
4 Zucchini, cut into 1-in. slices
2 lg. Purple Onions, sliced
2 T Olive Oil
2 tsp. Dried Oregano
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Pepper
2 lg. Yellow Peppers, cut into 1-in. strips
2 lg. Red Peppers, cut into 1-in. strips
2 T Balsamic Vinegar

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 500 degrees
Toss squash, zucchini, and onions with olive oil and spread in a large roasting pan.
Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper, bake for 10 minutes.
Add pepper strips and toss gently. Bake 10 additional minutes or until tender.
Place in serving bowl and cool slightly. Add vinegar and toss gently.
Makes 6-8 servings
