Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans black eyed peas (rinsed & drained)
4 oz can green chilis chopped
2 oz jar pimentos
1/2 sliced red onion
1 c chopped celery
1 chopped red bell pepper
2/3 c apple cider vinegar
2/3 c sugar
1/2 c oil
1 tsp seasoning salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 T minced garlic

Directions:
Combine salad ingredients & dressing ingredients seperately. Pour dressing over salad & chill 24 hrs.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
A nice appetizer for New Year's Eve parties, plan ahead so it can chill.
