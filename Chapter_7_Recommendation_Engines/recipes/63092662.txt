Title:
Creamy Pasta Caprese with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 pound Penna pasta
-1 cup Vodka Sauce
-1/3 cup heavy cream
-1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
-8 ounces mozzarella cheese, cut into cubes
-1 pint of grape tomatoes
-1 bunch of fresh basil leaves
-3 boneless chicken breast cut up into cubes
-3 Garlic cloves minced
-1 Tbsp butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Boil water and cook pasta according to directions on box
Heat Vodka sauce over low heat in a large saucepan.  Once warm stir in heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese.
In separate sauce pan melt butter, once melted add garlic. Simmer 2-3 minutes add chicken.  Cook until chicken is done.
Once pasta and chicken is done, combine them with the sauce mix.
Once combined spray a baking dish with non-stick spray and add pasta mixture, tomatoes, basil, and 4 ounces of cubed mozzarella into dish.  Top with remaining fresh mozzarella cheese.
Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until cheese is golden and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Nicole Hermann Coovert
Description: 
Goes great with a Cesar Salad, and some garlic bread. Can be used with no meat, meatballs, or shrimp
