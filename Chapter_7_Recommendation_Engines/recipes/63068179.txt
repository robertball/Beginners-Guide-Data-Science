Title:
Pumpkin Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
1 pound ground turkey
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 cups pumpkin puree
1 packet chili seasoning
1 can mushrooms
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion, green bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and garlic until tender. Stir in the turkey, and cook until evenly brown. Drain, and mix in tomatoes with natural juice, add chili seasoning.  Mix thoroughly then add the pumpkin. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 20 minutes. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.

Submitter:

A chili for autumn! Turkey, pumpkin, and traditional chili ingredients go together well in this spicy concoction.Modified from: Pumpkin Turkey Chili
