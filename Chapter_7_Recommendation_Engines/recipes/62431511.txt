Title:
Lemon-Mustard Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451417.jpg
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
3 tsp. dijon mustard (can use honey-dijon)
1/4 cup butter
3 tsp. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. tarragon seasoning (optional)
1 tsp. rosemary seasoning (can use Thyme or Marjoram for substitute)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Place chicken breasts in shallow baking pan.
Melt butter in saucepan on medium low.
Stir in mustard, lemon juice, salt and seasonings.
Pour sauce over chicken breasts and bake for 45 minutes.
Optional:
For extra flavoring, make another batch of lemon-mustard sauce and pour over chicken breasts 5 minutes before baking time is done.
