Title:
Sweet treat's Country Style Gravy Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c all-purpose flour
2 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp paparika
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 tsp poultry seasoning
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
# 1 cup all-purpose flour # 2 teaspoons garlic salt # 1 teaspoon paprika # 1 teaspoon ground black pepper # 1/4 teaspoon poultry seasoning # 1/2 teaspoon salt # 1 Mix all ingredients well and store in a tightly sealed container. # 2 To make Gravy: Discard all but 2 tablespoons of the oil used to fry the chicken (or melt 2 tablespoons unsalted butter) in a skillet. # 3 Over low heat, mix in 2 tablespoons of the Country Gravy Mix. # 4 Stirring constantly, cook about 2 minutes. Whisk in 1 cup chicken broth, scraping browned bits off bottom of skillet. # 5 Stir in 1 cup milk and bring all to a boil over high heat, stirring constantly. # 6 Reduce heat to low and simmer about 5 minutes. # 7 Serve immediately with chicken, mashed potatoes or biscuits
