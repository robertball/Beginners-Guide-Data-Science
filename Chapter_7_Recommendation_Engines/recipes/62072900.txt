Title:
Scrumptious Fat Free Maple Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c maple syrup
1/4 honey
1 t almond extract
1 T vanilla
2 cups rolled oats
2 cups rice crispy cereal
1 t cinnamon

Directions:
Heat your oven to 350 degrees.
Boil liquids in pot until mix foams and rises, this takes about 3 min. Quickly stir everything in. Spread it out onto a baking sheet. Bake it for 13 minutes and store in an air tight container.

Submitter: 
LOOLAY
Description: 
I came up with this years ago when I was bored on a cold winter day.It doesnt have many ingredients, but it still is bold in flavor! Whenever there is a birthday or holiday where I need a quick gift, I just whip up a warm batch of this, and it is an instant hit!
