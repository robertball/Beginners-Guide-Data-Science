Title:
Fried Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One large zucchini sliced thin
4 Slices of bacon crumbled
1/2 cup of mozzerella
1/4 cup chopped onion

Directions:
Fry bacon in skillet.  Drain some of the grease, but leave enough to fry the zucchini in.  Add sliced zucchini and onion and fry at medium heat for about 15-20 minutes stirring from time to time.  Drain remainder of grease from the skillet and add crumbled bacon and mozzerella.  Heat on low til mozzerella is melted.  Then serve!

Submitter: 
HAULBUR
Description: 
Fried zucchini with crumbled bacon and melted mozzerella cheese.  This is the only way my husband will eat zucchini and loves it!
