Title:
Best of the Best Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998733.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup milk
2 tsp lemon juice
1/2 cup apple sauce
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cloves
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 cups shredded carrots
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
1. Grease and flour an 8x12 inch pan.
2. In a medium bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, salt and nutmeg, cloves, and cinnamon. Set aside.
3.In a large bowl, combine eggs, buttermilk, apple sauce, oil, both sugars and vanilla. Mix well.
4. Add flour mixture and mix well.
5. In a saucepan, add the Raisins and the orange juice together and boil until soft and plump, about 2 minutes, then drain the juice.
6. In a medium bowl, combine shredded carrots, coconut, walnuts, pineapple and raisins.
7. Using a large wooden spoon or a very heavy whisk, add carrot mixture to batter and fold in well.
8. Pour into prepared 8x12 inch pan, and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour. Check with toothpick.
9. Allow to cool for at least 20 minutes before serving.
