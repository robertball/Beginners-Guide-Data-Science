Title:
Reese's Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/574184.jpg
Ingredients:
8  .55 ounce Full Size Reese's Cups
1/2 Cup Hershey's Milk Chocolate Chips
1 1/4 Cups Chow Mein Noodles

Directions:
1.) Unwrap Reese's Cups and break them apart and place in a microwave safe bowl. Add Hershey's Milk Chocolate Chips and begin microwaving in 30 second increments until melted. Remove from microwave and mix well.
2.) Add chow mein noodles to chocolate and mix until incorporated. Using a tablespoon, scoop mixture and drop onto wax paper until dry.

A simple twist on an old favorite.
