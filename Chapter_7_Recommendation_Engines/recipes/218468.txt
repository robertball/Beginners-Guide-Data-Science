Title:
Grapefruit Soda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5357926.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen concentrated grapefruit juice, thawed
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen white grape juice concentrate, thawed
9 cups chilled club soda

Directions:
Mix the grapefruit juice and white grape juice concentrates in a 1-quart pitcher until thoroughly combined. Store mixture in refrigerator. To serve, pour 1/3 cup of concentrate mix into a glass, and mix with 1 cup of cold club soda or sparkling water.
