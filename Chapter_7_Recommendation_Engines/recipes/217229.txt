Title:
Bijou Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
ice
1 fluid ounce gin
3/4 fluid ounce sweet vermouth
3/4 fluid ounce green Chartreuse
2 dashes orange bitters
1 twist lemon

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add gin, sweet vermouth, Chartreuse, and orange bitters. Stir well. Strain into a chilled martini glass; garnish with lemon twist.

Submitter:

Description: 
Another jewel of a gin drink. Adjust the ratios to your liking. Try it with equal parts gin, vermouth, and Chartreuse.
