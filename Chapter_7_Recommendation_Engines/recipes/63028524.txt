Title:
Benihana Japanese Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886771.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups rice, cooked
1 cup peas, thawed
2 tablespoons carrots, grated
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup onions, diced
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons soy sauce
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Pour cooked rice into a large bowl and let cool in refrigerator.
Scramble eggs, separate into pea-sized bits while cooking.
Add peas, carrots, scrambled eggs, onions to rice and toss.
Melt butter in large frying pan over medium/high heat.
Dump rice mixture into pan and add soy sauce, salt and pepper.
Cook for 6-8 minutes, stirring often.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
You can prepare it on an open hibachi grill
