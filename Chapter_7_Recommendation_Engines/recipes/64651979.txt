Title:
Portland Bar Cookies - Seattle Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, melted
2 cups crushed graham crackers
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
1 1/2 cups unsweetened shredded coconut
1 1/4 cups milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup crushed peanuts

Directions:
Melt 1 stick of butter and crush graham crackers until finely ground. Combine the butter and crushed graham crackers until combined. Press into the bottom of the parchment-lined 8x8 baking sheet.
Sprinkle white chocolate chips over the graham cracker layer. Then drizzle half of the the sweetened condensed milk over the top.  Sprinkle coconut next, then drizzle 2nd half of condensed milk over coconut.  Last, sprinkle chocolate chips, pecans, and peanuts over the top. Press down gently to pack.
Bake in a 350ÂºF oven for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown.  Remove from oven, lift parchment lining out of pan and cool on wire rack completely before slicing.

Submitter: 
Amy Randy Smith
Description: 
Small modification from the original Portland recipe for us Seattle-ites... we're not big on walnuts so I substitute crushed peanuts and I swap out white chocolate chips for butterscotch chips.
