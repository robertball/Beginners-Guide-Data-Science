Title:
BEETS IN A JAR Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036978.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
red beets
white wine vinegar
Salt

Directions:
Preparation:
Clean the beets, removing leaves and using only the roots; Cook in water for an hour. Then, let them cool in the water and then drain, Peel the beets. Cut into pieces and store beets in jars are not too large (300 ml pots): in order to use all of the content. Put each jar a spoonful of vinegar and a teaspoon of salt.Close the jars tightly and boil in water-filled pot x 30 minutes.Preserved and kept in the pantry ... seasoned with olive oil and herbs when serving.
