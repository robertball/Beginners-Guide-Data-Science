Title:
Sourdough Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cubes butter
1 Tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 Tablespoon poppy seeds
1 Tablespoon Beaumonde Seasoning
1 Tablespoon minced onion
1 sourdough round
1 pound swiss cheese, slice thickly

Directions:
Cut a checkerboard pattern in the sourdough round, being sure to leave the bottom in tact by slicing no more than 1/2 inch from the bottom.
In a small saucepan over medium low heat, melt butter and mix in mustard, lemon juice, poppy seeds, Beaumonde seasoning, and onion.
Pour butter mixture over bread and into slices.
Stuff sliced swiss into cracks of bread.
Line a cookie sheet with aluminum foil and place bread round on it.  Bake 1t 350 for 30 minutes uncovered.
