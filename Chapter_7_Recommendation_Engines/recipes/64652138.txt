Title:
Buffalo Chicken Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound elbow macaroni, cooked al dente and drained
4 tablespoons butter, divided
2 tablespoons flour
2 teaspoons dry mustard
2-1/2 cups milk (I use 2%)
3/4 cup hot sauce, such as Franks®, divided
1 pound processed cheese (I used light Velveeta®)
4-8 ounces shredded pepper jack cheese (use this to taste)
2/3 cup sour cream (I used light)
1 small onion, chopped finely
2 stalks celery, chopped finely
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
1 cup panko breadcrumbs or Italian seasoned
1/2 cup blue cheese, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and spray a 13x9 dish with non-stick cooking spray.
FOR THE CHEESE SAUCE:
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in saucepan over medium heat.
Stir in flour and dry mustard.
Whisk in milk and 1/4 cup hot sauce; stir until thickened.
Stir in cheeses, and remove pot from heat when melted.
Whisk in sour cream and set aside.
FOR THE CHICKEN:
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a medium skillet.
Add the onion and celery and cook until soft.
Stir in the garlic and chicken and cook a minute or two.
Add in remaining 1/2 cup of hot sauce, and stir until slightly thickened.
In the pot you boiled the pasta in, combine the pasta and chicken mixture; stir.
Add in cheese sauce and stir to combine well.
Pour into prepared baking dish,
Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and blue cheese, if using.
Bake for about 30 minutes, until browned and bubbly.
Allow to set 10 minutes before serving.
Enjoy!

Description: 
This recipe is the combination of two of my family's 2 favorite things...buffalo chicken wings and macaroni and cheese. This is a match made in heaven!
