Title:
Lorna and Jim's Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg McCormick slow cooker chili mix
1# ground beef (coarse ground chili style if available)
1# ground turkey (or you can use 2# ground beef.
1 can Ranch beans undrained
1 Can Joan of Arc medium chili beans undrained
1 Can Black beans (drained)
1 14.5 oz can petite diced tomatoes undrained
Tomato juice or V8 for thinning

Directions:
Brown and drain the meat(s)
Add all other ingredients
Cook on low 8-10 hours
Thin to desired consistency with tomato/V8 juice.
