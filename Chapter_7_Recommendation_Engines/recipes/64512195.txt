Title:
CAULIFLOWER PIZZA CRUST Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower riced to equal 1 c
1 1/2 c mozzarella
1 t oregano
1 t minced garlic
1 t sea salt
1 egg

Directions:
nuke cauliflower 8 minutes
stir all ingredients
Pam a cookie sheet
flatten mix to whatever shape
brush w/evoo
bake 450 for 15 minutes
top with mozzarella and toppings of choice
bake another 5 minutes @ 350
