Title:
Lofthouse Sugar Cookies Knockoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(18.9 oz)Pbury Fr Van cake mix.
1/3 C vegetable oil.
2 eggs.
Buttercream Frosting.
1 C(2 stks)unsaltedbutter,soft.
6-8 C powdered sugar.
1/2 C milk.
2 tsp vanilla extract.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Blend cake mix, eggs, and oil until moistened.
Roll into 1" balls.
Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten with a glass.
Bake at 375F for 6-8 minutes. Allow to cool 1 minute.
remove to cooling rack.
While the cookies still warm spread some buttercream frosting. Allow the frosting to set up before storing.
Place butter in a large bowl.
Add 4 C sugar milk and vanilla. Beat on medium speed until smooth and creamy, approx 3-5 minutes. Gradually add remaining sugar 1 C at a time beating well after each time, about 2 minutes, until the icing is thick enough to spread. Add food coloring if you'd like.
