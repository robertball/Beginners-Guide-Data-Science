Title:
Southwestern Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/882135.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds chuck roast, trimmed and cut into cubes
1 large onions, chopped
1 medium green or red pepper, diced
5 garlic cloves, minced
28 oz crushed tomatoes
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 cup beef broth or consomme
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 tablespoons chili 3000
1 Tbl. brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 can pinto beans, rinsed
1 can butter beans, rinsed
1 can kidney beans, rinsed
1 Tbl. unsweetened chocolate

Directions:
In a soup cook the beef in batches over medium high heat browned drain. Saute onions and garlic in remaining grease; cook for 8-10 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Stir in the remaining ingredients up until beans. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered for 1-1/2 hours. Before serving add beans and chocolate.  After 15 min adjust seasonings.
