Title:
Droolin' Dog Birthday Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix together:
2 lbs- ground turkey
1 1/2 cups cooked brown rice
1/2 cup grated carrots
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup fresh minced parsley
Mix together and add to the ingredients above:
1 beaten egg
1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/8 teaspoon vanilla
Add to the above and mix thoroughly:
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup chick pea or spelt flour
For decorating:
2 cups prepared mashed potatoes
*Any type of dog bone treat or other eatable you want to decorate with. Some ideas...cranberries, carrot coins, or grated carrot to look like confetti, frozen blueberries, sweet potato, apple slices, liver bits, bee pollen, anything healthy that your Pups' heart desires.
* You can color the mashed potatoes using the juice from raspberries, strawberries or other fruits.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly grease 2-8" round cake pans.
Divide mix and lightly press half into each pan.
Bake for 1 hour.
Let cool completely and then frost with mashed potatoes.
* You may freeze leftovers.

Submitter: 
KitchenHazard
Description: 
This birthday cake is sure to make your dog drool with anticipation, so keep a towel on hand! It's easy to swap ingredients if your dog has allergy issues, and decorating it allows for much creativity.
