Title:
Orange Sunrise Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package orange cake mix (can sub yellow)
1 (3 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
3/4 cup fresh orange juice
1/2 cup apple sauce
Eggbeaters, equaling 4 eggs
1 teaspoon lemon extract
1 teaspoon orange extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 Tablespoon of orange zest
1/3 cup orange juice
1/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Grease a 10 inch Bundt pan. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a large bowl, stir together cake mix and pudding mix. Make a well in the center and pour in 3/4 cup orange juice, applesauce, eggbeaters, lemon, orange and vanilla extracts, and orange zest. Beat on low speed until blended. Scrape bowl, and beat 4 minutes on medium speed. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Immediately, bake in preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
In a saucepan over medium heat, cook 1/3 cup orange juice, sugar and butter for five minutes. Brush over cake.
