Title:
Smoked Salmon Lox Bento Box Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540794.jpg
Ingredients:
-2 mini everything bagels
-2oz smoked salmon lox
-1oz chive and onion cream cheese
-1/4 cup red onion
-1/2 tbsp capers
-8 to 10 baby carrots
1/4 English cucumber thinly sliced into coins
4 to 6 cherry tomatoes halved

Directions:
Thinly slice red onion and English cucumber.   Cut cherry tomatoes in half.  Mix capers into cream cheese and place into a small container able to fit into bento box.   Place all items into bento box.
