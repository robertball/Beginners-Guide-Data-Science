Title:
Jja Jang Myun (Korean black bean noodles) vegetarian version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Noodles (special "myun" for Jaa jang myun (you can find in refrigerated section in Korean/asian markets. use 2 bunch from the package of 4.
4TBS black bean paste (chunjang) (from Korean market)
1/2 cup onion chopped
1/2 cup carrots cubed .5 in.
1/2 cup potato pealed & cubed .5 in.
1/2 cup zucchini pealed & cubed .5 in.
salt
pepper
1/2 TBS canola oil
1-2 cloves of garlic minced
1 cup water
1-1 1/2 TBS sugar (depends on your taste)
1 TBS potato or corn starch
1 TBS water

Directions:
In a wok heat oil and add minced garlic, chopped carrot and potato.
Stir together then add a few dashes of pepper and salt.
when carrot and potato have softened add chopped zucchini & onion, stir.
While veggies are cooking mix 1TBS starch with 1 TBS water until fully mixed.
Add black bean paste and 1 cup water to wok & stir.
Add starch mixture to wok slowly while stirring, keep cooking until sauce thickens.
Noodles: add noodles to boiling pot of water and cook for about 3 minute then drain.
Serve noodles in large pasta bowl then add black bean mixture on top, mix well and enjoy!
