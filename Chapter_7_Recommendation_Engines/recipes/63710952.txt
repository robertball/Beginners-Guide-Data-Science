Title:
Emily's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 avocados
1 lime, juiced
1/2tsp salt
1 tsp cayenne
1 tsp cumin
1/2 jalepeno
1/4 Cup chopped onion
2 roma tomatoes

Directions:
With a potato masher, mash together avocado, lime juice and salt.
Gently stir in rest of ingredients and enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description:
