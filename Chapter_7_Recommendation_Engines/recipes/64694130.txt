Title:
Chopped Liver "a la my Mum's" style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5210282.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs calves liver sliced (I use store bought sliced for ease)
2 large white onions sliced
1 teaspoon salt
dash of ground pepper
1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil (for cooking onions/liver and processor)
4 hard boiled eggs (1 for garnish/serving)
Parsley for garnish

Directions:
Cook eggs to hard boiled and set aside
Saute sliced onion till semi translucent in pan with tablespoon of olive oil on high heat
When onions semi translucent add liver and cook approximately 1/ 1/2 - 2 minutes per side and set aside.
Place  cooked liver, onion and hard boiled eggs (3) in  food processor and blend till smooth.
Slowly add oil in processor from cooked liver to make a smooth mixture.
Season with Salt and Pepper
Spread Liver on serving platter and garnish with remaining chopped egg & sprigs of parsley.
Serve with thin sliced grilled garlic baguettes or crackers.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Always has been a tradition with our family.  I remember as a child going to  Luby's and loved the liver and onions. I guess will say it is a taste that is acquired if you love, you do love.
