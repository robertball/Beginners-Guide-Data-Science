Title:
Root Beer Paralyzer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970796.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce root beer schnapps
4 fluid ounces milk
3 1/2 fluid ounces cola-flavored carbonated beverage
ice

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice. Pour in coffee liqueur, root beer schnapps and milk. Top with cola.

Submitter: 
Helena Blodgett-Goddu
Description: 
A paralyzer made with coffee liqueur, root beer schnapps, milk and cola.
