Title:
Stir-Fried Snap Peas and Mushrooms with Baked Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. Veggie broth, plus 1 T. for later
1 T. miring rice cooking wine or dry sherry
2 tsp. soy sauce
3 T. oil, divided (high heat oil)
1 T. minced ginger
8 medium fresh shiitake mushrooms, stems removed and caps quartered (4 oz)
8 ounces sugar snap peas, strings removed
1 red pepper, thinly sliced
1 6-oz pkg. baked tofu (savory) or follow recipe for baked tofu (Moosewood)
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups brown rice for serving

Directions:
Mix together the broth, mirin, and soy sauce. Set aside.
Heat a skillet over high heat till a bead of water vaporizes.  Swirl in 2 T. of oil, add peppers, and stir-fry for 2 minutes.  Add ginger and stir-fry for 10 seconds. Add the mushrooms and stir-fry for 30 seconds. Swirl the broth mixture into the pan, cover, and cook for a minute or until about 1 T. of broth remains.
Swirl in the remaining 1 T. oil, add the peas and tofu, then sprinkle on the salt. Stir-fry 1 minute or until the peas are bright green. Add the remaining 1 T. broth and cook 30 seconds longer, till peas are just crisp-tender.
