Easy Mediterranean Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups salad greens
2 TBL miracle whip
1 tomato, cut into wedges
10 pitted medium black olives
Ritz Crackers

Directions:
Put salad greens on 2 plates. Drain tuna; mix with dressing. Put on greens. Add tomato and olives. Serve with crackers.
