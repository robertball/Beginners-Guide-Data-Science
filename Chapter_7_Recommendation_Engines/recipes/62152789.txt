Title:
Jenny's Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken skinned-cut into pieces
1.5 cups plain yogurt
4 tbs curry paste
1 can tomatoe sauce 398mls
1/3 cup butter
2 tbs olive oil
1 medium onion
1 tbs dried cumin
3-5 garic buds
3-4 tbs fresh ginger
1 tbs paprika
1/2 cup cream
fresh cilantro

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in bag with yogurt and curry paste.marinate overnight (at least three hours).
line bakng sheet with foil-put chicken in preheated oven (400) bake until done (dark bits are fine- this adds to the tandoori flavour)about 30 minutes- test for doneness
to prepare sauce: melt butter, add olive oil and onions. saute until golden yellow.
add cumin
add garlic
add ginger
add paprika
continue to cook for one minute
add tomatoe sauce
continue to cook for 5 minutes
transfer to blender and puree until smooth
return to pan
add cream and simmer gently.
garnish with lots of cilantro

Submitter: 
Jenny
Description: 
Melt in your mouth goodness! Served with couscous or coconut rice and fresh cilantro it is a huge hit with adults and children alike.
