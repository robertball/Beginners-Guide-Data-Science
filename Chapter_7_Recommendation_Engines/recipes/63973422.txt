Title:
Yummy Yummy Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package yellow cake mix
3 ounces vanilla instant pudding mix
1 3/4 cups water
4 eggs
1/4 cup oil
2 cups coconut
1 cup walnuts, chopped
Coconut Cream Cheese Frosting:
4 tablespoons butter
2 cups coconut
8 ounces cream cheese
2 teaspoons milk
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Combine first five ingredients and beat for 4 minutes.  Stir in coconut and nuts.  Pour into 3 greased and floured 9 inch pans or a sheet cake pan.  Bake for about 35 minutes at 350 degrees (longer for sheet cake).  Cool in pans, remove, and frost with Coconut Cream Cheese Frosting.
For Frosting:
To make frosting, melt 2 tablespoons butter in skillet.  Add 2 cups coconut.  Stir over low heat until brown and toasted.  In bowl, cream softened cheese and 2 tablespoons butter.  Add milk, powdered sugar and vanilla.  Mix until smooth.  Stir in 1 3/4 cup of the toasted coconut.  Frost cake.  Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup toasted coconut on sides and top.
