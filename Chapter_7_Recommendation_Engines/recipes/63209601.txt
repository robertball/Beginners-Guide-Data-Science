Title:
Asparagus Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
5 slices bacon
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 sweet onion, diced
1 (8 inch) unbaked pie shells
1 egg white, lightly beaten
4 eggs
3/4 cup half-and-half cream
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground nutmeg
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Place asparagus in a steamer over Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until beginning to brown.  Add onion and garlic and saut√© until onion is tender.  Season with Salt and pepper at this point.  Add asparagus, cover and cook for 6 minutes, or until tender.  Set aside.
Brush pie shells with beaten egg white. Sprinkle crumbled bacon, onion, garlic & chopped asparagus into pie shells.
In a bowl, beat together eggs, cream, nutmeg, salt and pepper. Sprinkle Swiss cheese over bacon and asparagus. Pour egg mixture on top of cheese.
Bake uncovered in preheated oven until firm, about 35. Serve warm.
