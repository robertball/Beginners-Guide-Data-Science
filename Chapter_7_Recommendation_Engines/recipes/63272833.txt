Title:
Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 c. vegetable oil
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 t. vanilla
2 c. flour
1 t. salt
1 t. baking soda
1 t. cinnamon
4 med. apples, peeled and chopped
1 c. nuts, chopped

Directions:
Beat eggs and oil until lemon colored.
Add sugar and vanilla. Beat well.
Sift flour, salt, soda and cinnamon, and add to mixture.
Stir in apples.
Bake in a 9x13 pan at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
