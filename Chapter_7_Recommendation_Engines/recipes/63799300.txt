Title:
Molasses Spice Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. oats
1 c. milk
1 small frozen banana
2 T. unsulphured molasses
1/2 t. ground ginger
1/2 t. ground cinnamon
dash of ground cloves
1/4 t. vanilla extract
1/2 c. ice

Directions:
place all ingredients in blender and blend till smooth.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
All the flavor of a Molasses Spice cookie in a thick, rich, wholesome smoothie. An ideal and filling start to those crisp Autumn mornings or an afternoon snack (without the guilt) to satisfy a craving.
