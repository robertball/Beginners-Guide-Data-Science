Title:
Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757962.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks melted butter
2 1/2 cups confectioners sugar
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 12 ounce bag semi sweet or milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup smooth peanut butter

Directions:
Melt butter then mix with sugar, graham cracker crumbs and crunchy peanut butter.
Mix well and pat into a slightly greased 9 X 13 cake pan.
Melt chocolate chips and smooth peanut butter in microwave for 1 to 1 1/2 munutes.
Mix until smooth.
Pour over bottom mixture and spread until covered.
Chill for 2 hours.
