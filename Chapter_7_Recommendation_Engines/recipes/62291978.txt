Title:
Garlic Barbeque Chicken for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2-cups water
1 1/2-cups cider vinegar
1-Tbsp sugar
3-Tbsp salt
1-Tbsp pepper
1/2-lb butter
1-tsp garlic powder
2-Tbsp worcestershire sauce
1/2-cup chopped onion
3-cut up chickens

Directions:
Sauce:
Combine all ingredients, except for the chicken, in a medium sauce pan and bring to a boil. Once you bring sauce to a boil it is ready to baste the chicken and needs only to be stirred during the grilling!
Place chicken on a hot grill and baste with sauce. Turn and baste often for about 1 hour or until tender and golden.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill

This is a tasty change from the typicle red barbeque sauces.
