Title:
3-Ingredient Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677958.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey (could also use beef, chicken or buffalo)
1 (6 oz.) box of dry, uncooked chicken flavor  stuffing mix
1/3 cup balsamic vinegarette salad dressing

Directions:
1.  Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
2.  Combine dry stuffing mix, meat and salad dressing in a large bowl.
3.  Mix with your hands and form into balls.  (To make them uniform size, put the meatballs into mini-muffin tins.)
4.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
TOVARK77
Description: 
Making delicious meatballs does not get any easier than this!  In our family, we always have some of these handy in the freezer because there are so many options for meatballs.  This is the fastest, easiest and most consistent recipe I have created for making meatballs for my family.  Enjoy!  :-)
