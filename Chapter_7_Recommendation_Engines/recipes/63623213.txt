Title:
Tekrouri's Tasty Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 3 Fillets of Fish (Hallibut, Mahi-Mahi or Tilapia)
1/2 cup of water
2-3 potatotes
1 bell pepper
15 baby carrots
1 cup olive oil
1 large bunch of cilantro
2-3 tomatoes
2 lemon
1 tspn diced garlic
1 tspn salt
1/2 tspn black pepper
1 tspn paprika
2 jalapenos (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Dice cilantro.  In large bowl, mix water, 2 squeezed lemons, cilantro, diced garlic, salt, pepper and paprika in olive oil.  Whisk until smooth. Cut potatoes, bell pepper and tomotoes into 1/2 inch slices.  In casserole dish, add a teaspoon of olive oil and spread to coat bottom.
Dip baby carrots in sauce. Layer on bottom of casserole dish. Layer fish, potatoes,tomato slices, bell peppers.
Pour sauce over dish and cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes.  Check potatoes to make sure they are soft and cooked.

Gina M. Ford
Description: 
Tasty Morroccan fish dish in olive oil sauce!
