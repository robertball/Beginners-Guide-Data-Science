Title:
Orange Chocolate Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 tablespoon orange zest
1 tablespoon fresh orange juice
1 1/2 cups buttermilk baking mix
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup HERSHEYÂ®'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 F.
Beat butter in medium bowl; gradually add brown sugar, beating until fluffy.  Add egg, orange peel and juice; beat until smooth.  Add biscuit mix, mixing well; stir in walnuts and chocolate chips.  Drop by teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes.  Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.  Cool completely.

Submitter: 
HersheysKitchens.com
Description: 
Better than regular chocolate chip cookies, these cookies have fresh orange juice and orange zest in the dough.
