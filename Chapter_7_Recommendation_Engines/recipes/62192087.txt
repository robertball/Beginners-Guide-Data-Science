Title:
Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box chocolater cake mix
1/2 cup oil
2 eggs
1 bag chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients, mix throughly then add chocolate chips to mixture and mix again.  Place about 1 tablespoon full on cookie sheet until it is filled, bake about 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
We make these every year to add to our cookie baskets for the holidays.  Very easy and fast.
