Title:
Crockpot Jalapeño cheese dip by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese
1/2 c sour cream
1 1/2 c shredded cheese (I used 1/2 c each: Mexican blend, Parmesan and Colby jack)
1 can copped green chilis
3 medium jalapeños, chopped
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin

Directions:
Put all in crockpot, should only take 2-3 hours to melt together. Stir occasionally. Serve with pita chips or tortilla strips.
Could top with green onions, bacon bits, bread crumbs or more cheese.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
Easy to throw together and only needs a couple hours to melt together.
