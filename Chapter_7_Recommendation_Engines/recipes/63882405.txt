Title:
S'more Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 Honey Graham Crackers
2 3/4 cups milk
1 pkg 5 1/4 oz vanilla pudding and pie filling
3 1.65 oz plain milk chocolate candy bars
2 1/2 cups mini marshmallows

Directions:
Line bottom of 8x6x2 inch glass or metal baking dish with three graham crackers.
In a medium saucepan, combine milk and pudding mix. Cook over low heat until mix comes to a full boil. Cool 5 minutes.
Spread half of pudding over crackers, top with second layer of crackers. Place candy bars on crackers and spread remaining pudding over the candy bars, top with third layer of crackers and any left-over chocolate.
Sprinkle with marshmallows, broil until golden brown, about 2-4 minutes.
Serve warm or chill in refrigerator.
