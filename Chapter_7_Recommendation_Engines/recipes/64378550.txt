Title:
Honey Mustard Soy Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Salmon fillet
2 tbsp honey mustard
2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp dried dill weed
1/4 tsp rosemary
1/4 tsp thyme

Directions:
Preheat BBQ
Mix ingredients in a bowl
Coat a large piece of foil in vegetable oil and place salmon on it
Use a basting brush to coat the salmon
Wrap salmon tightly in foil.
Optional: let marinate for 20-30 minutes.
Cook foil wrapped salmon on BBQ grill for 12-15 minutes
Open foil for last 2 minutes of cooking time.

Submitter:

Description: 
Fantastic salmon recipe, very easy to make, good for a large crowd too! Short prep and cooking time.
