Title:
Amaroidal Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup light olive oil
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1 clove crushed garlic
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1 pinch salt
ground black pepper to taste
Juice of 1/2 fresh lime
Juice of 1/2 fresh lemon
1 small bag of spinach
2 heads of romaine lettuce
1 roma tomato, sliced in wedges
Several handfuls croutons
1/2 cup Broccoli sprouts
Lemon and lime wedges, garnish

Directions:
For the dressing, combine olive oil, balsamic vinegar, crushed garlic, ground mustard, salt, and pepper. Chill.
Combine spinach, lettuce, tomato, croutons, and sprouts in large bowl.
Toss salad together with dressing. Squeeze fresh lemon and lime sparingly over top of salad.
Garnish with lemon and lime slices.

Submitter: 
SouthernSon
Description: 
Invented this for a light dinner, although it probably works best as an accompaniment to a larger meal. I love my mom's dressing but wanted something more zappy - thus the lemon!
