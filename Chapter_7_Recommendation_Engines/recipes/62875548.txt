Title:
Caesar Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Romaine lettuce
8 tbs. Caesar salad dressing
1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
garlic croutons

Directions:
Clean, dry and tear lettuce. Toss with dressing. Divide evenly and top each serving with 1/4 of the cheese and a several croutons.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
Serve as a side dish with pasta, or as a main dish with chicken or fish.
