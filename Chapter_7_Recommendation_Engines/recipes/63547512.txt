Title:
Not Your Ordinary Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 pieces of chicken
1/2 C mayonnaise
1 jar apricot preserves
1/2 C Russian dressing
1 pkg onion soup mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Wash and pat the chicken dry.  Mix together all other ingredients. Dip the chicken in the sauce.  Place chicken in a greased pan.  Pour thew remaining sauce over the chicken.  Cover with foil. Baked for 40 - 45 minutes.
