Title:
Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless,skinless chicken breast
3/4 cup melted butter
1 1/2 cup Herbed Peppridge Farm stuffing (in blue bag)
1/2 cup Parmesans cheese
1 1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Dip chicken breast in melted butter
Coat the chicken in the stuffing and seasoning
Pour the remaining butter and stuffing mixture and over the top
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour, uncovered
