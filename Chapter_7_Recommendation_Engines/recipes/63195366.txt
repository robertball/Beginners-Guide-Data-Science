Title:
Garlic & Herb Rice with Chicken (Gluten Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup(s) Long Grain Rice
1 tablespoon(s) Chopped Onion
3 cube(s) Vegetable Boullion
2 teaspoon(s) Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb Blend
1 bag(s) Frozen Broccoli, Cauliflower & Carrots
2  Chicken Breasts, cubed
1 teaspoon(s) Olive Oil

Directions:
1.Make rice according to package directions for desired quantity, adding the chopped onion and boullion cubes to the water when bringing to a boil.
2.Cook bag of frozen vegetables and set asside.
3.Add diced chicken to large non-stick skillet with olive oil heated. Add Mrs. Dash when tossing the chicken to cover in oil and seasonings. Cook on Medium heat with the lid on, stirring often until cooked through.
4.Add cooked rice and vegetables to the chicken and stir.

Submitter: 
Jessica Schultz
Description: 
Light easy rice dish that my family loves! This recipe serves our family of 2 adults and 3 children.
