Title:
Foil Baked Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448261.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. cod
2 Tbsp butter
juice from 1/2 lemon
1/3-1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. dried parsley
1/4 tsp. dried dill weed
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
paparika

Directions:
Melt butter, lemon juice, salt, parsley, dill weed,ground black pepper,and garlic powder in pan.
Place cod fillets on a greased piece of heavy duty aluminum foil.
Pour the sauce over the top. Then sprinkled with paparika.
Wrap the fish tightly in the foil so it didn't leak.
Bake at 400 for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Joan Gerland Crabtree
Description: 
The cod has a very good blend of flavors and is easy and fast to prepare. I created this recipe from my favorite parts of other recipes.
