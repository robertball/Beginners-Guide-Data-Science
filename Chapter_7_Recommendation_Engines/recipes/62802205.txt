Title:
Rice pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/953047.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cooked rice(bag of success)
1 1/2 c milk
1/3 c sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1/2 c milk
T butter
1/2 tsp vanilla
tsp cinnamon
dash nutmeg

Directions:
Cook first four ingredients stovetop 15-20 minutes on medium heat. Mix egg and 1/2 c milk, temper by adding a bit of the hot mix into it first, then add it back into the pot and cook 2 more minutes. Remove from heat and add butter, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla. Sprinkle more cinnamon on top.
