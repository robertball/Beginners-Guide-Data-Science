Title:
Little Bit of Roasted Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Garlic cloves, unpeeled
1/8 tsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
5" by 9" piece of aluminum foil

Directions:
Place garlic cloves at one end of foil. Drizzle with olive oil. Fold over foil and seal edges, well, to form a loose packet, garlic should be able to move in packet when gently shaken. Place packet in a small dry skillet over medium low heat. Cook for 12 minutes, turning packet over every 3 minutes. Remove packet from pan and allow to sit for 10 minutes without opening. Open packet and peel garlic.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description: 
For those times when you just need a few cloves of roasted garlic, this is quick and easy.
