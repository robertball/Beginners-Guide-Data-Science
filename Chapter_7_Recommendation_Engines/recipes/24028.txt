Title:
Easy Hugs Chocolate Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981811.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups slivered almonds
2 (1 ounce) squares HERSHEY®'S Unsweetened Baking Chocolate
2/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
20 HERSHEY®'S HUGS® Chocolates

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Spread almonds in thin layer in large shallow baking pan.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until light golden brown; cool.  Finely chop almonds.  Do not turn oven off.  Spray cookie sheet with vegetable cooking spray; set aside.
Melt chocolate in medium microwave-safe bowl as directed on package; stir in sweetened condensed milk, almonds, almond extract and vanilla.  Drop by rounded tablespoons onto prepared cookie sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or just until set; cool 10 minutes.  Meanwhile, remove wrappers from chocolate pieces; gently press chocolate piece in center of each cookie.  Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.  Cool completely.
