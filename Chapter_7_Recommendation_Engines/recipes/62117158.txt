Title:
Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c self rising flour
3 tb sugar
1 can (12oz) beer
1 tb melted butter

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350
Mix flour, sugar and beer together
Pour into a greased loaf pan
Bake for 45 min
Brush butter on top of bread
Bake 15 more min, or until toothpick come out clean

Submitter: 
violet
Description: 
This is a quick and easy bread.  It's a good way to use up and beer you don't like to drink very much.
