Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of Duncan Hines Moist Deluxe Swiss Chocolate Cake Mix
1 container of Duncan Hines creamy Homestyle Vanilla Frosting
1/2 cup JIF Creamy Peanut Butter
15 mini peanut butter cup candies, wrappers removed and cut in half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Place 20 (2-1/2") paper lines in muffin cups.
Prepare, bake and cool cupcakes following package directions.
Combine vanilla frosting and peanut butter in medium bowl.  Stir until smooth.  Frost one cupcake.  Decorate with peanut butter cup candy, cut side down.  Repeat with remaining cupcakes.
