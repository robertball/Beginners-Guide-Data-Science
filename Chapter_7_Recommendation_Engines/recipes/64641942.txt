Title:
Lime-Cilantro Crunchy Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup red cabbage chopped in 1/2 inch pieces
1 cup savoy cabbage chopped in 1/2 inch pieces
1 cup jicama chopped in 1/2 inch pieces
1 cup carrot chopped in 1/4 inch pieces
1 cup celery chopped in 1/4 inch pieces
1 cup sweet red pepper chopped in 1/2 inch pieces
3/4 cup red onion chopped in 1/4 inch pieces
1 jalapeno seeded and minced
1 cup walnuts chopped
1 cup feta cheese crumbled
1 recipe of Lime-Cilantro Dressing (You can find one on this site. I used a wonderful recipe submitted by lin.)
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients in a large bowl. Taste for salt and pepper. Let this sit in the refrigerator for about an hour.

Submitter: 
peppini
Description: 
This is a great take along side dish for a summer picnic.  It's deliciously crunchy and it holds up well.
