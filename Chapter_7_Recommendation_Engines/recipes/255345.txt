Title:
4-Ingredient Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3973755.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds boneless center-cut pork loin
1/2 cup salsa
2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
water to cover

Directions:
Place pork loin, salsa, and chipotle peppers in a slow cooker. Pour in enough water to cover the pork halfway.
Cook on High until pork is very tender, 4 to 5 hours.
Transfer to a cutting board; shred with a fork and knife.

Submitter: 
elisa
Description: 
This is a super easy, delicious way to cook up the most moist and flavorful pulled pork. Adjust the amount of chipotles to your body temperature! Serve with any fixings you like, including corn or flour tortillas, Cheddar cheese, lettuce, salsa, and sour cream.
