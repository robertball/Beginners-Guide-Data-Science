Title:
Fajita Marinade (Marie C's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice of 2 limes
¼	cup tequila
¼	cup olive oil
¼	cup chopped cilantro
2	tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
1	teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
2	cloves garlic, minced
½	teaspoon oregano
1	teaspoon cumin

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well, cut chicken into strips, pour marinade over chicken and let marinate in the refrigerator overnight.

Very Very Tasty marinade.  Just like the restaurants!
