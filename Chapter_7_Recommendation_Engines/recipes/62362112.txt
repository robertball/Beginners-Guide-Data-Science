Title:
Island Kielbasa in a Slow Cooker II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds kielbasa sausage, sliced into 1/2 inch pieces
1 cup BBQ Sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, undrained
1 can (15-oz.) diced tomatoes, undrained
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped bell pepper (any color)
1 tsp. minced garlic

Directions:
Place the sausage, onion, garlic and  bell pepper in a skillet over medium heat and saute until sausage is browned and veggies are softened. Pour into slow cooker, adding BBQ sauce, diced tomatoes, sugar and pineap-ple in the slow cooker and mix together.
Cook on high setting for 2 to 4 hours, until sausage is cooked through.
Serve over favorite Pasta, Rice or baked Potato

Submitter: 
Plain ole Bob
Description:
