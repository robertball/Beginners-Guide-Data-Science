Title:
Texas Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6868458.jpg
Ingredients:
cake:
2 sticks margarine
4 T. cocoa
1 cup water
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1/2 t. salt
1t. soda
2 eggs
1 cup sour milk
Frosting:
1 stick margarine
4 T. cocoa
6 T. milk
1 cup chopped nuts
1# box powdered sugar
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Cake:
Bring to boil margarine, cocoa and water.
Remove from heat and add: flour, sugar, salt and soda. Mix well.
Beat in eggs and sour milk.
Pour onto greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees for 20-22 minutes.
Frosting:
Bring to boil margarine, cocoa and milk.
Remove from heat and add chopped nuts and powdered sugar.  Mix well then add vanilla.
Spread over warm cake.
