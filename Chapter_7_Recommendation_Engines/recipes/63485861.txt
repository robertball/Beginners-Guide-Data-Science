Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 sticks margarine
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
2 tbs water/milk
1 tsp vanilla
2/3 cup flour
1 tsp cinammon
1/2 tsp baking soda
3 cups quick cooking oats
1 cup raisins

Directions:
cream margarine and sugars.  add egg, water and vanilla.  sift together flour, cinnamon, and baking soda.  add to creamed mixture
stir in oats and raisins
using icecream scoop splat cookies onto greased cookie sheets (6 per)
bake 350F for 15 mins do NOT over cook
