Title:
Cocoa Sleigh Ride Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750582.jpg
Ingredients:
1 instant cocoa
3 tablespoons whipped cream
1 tablespoon red and green candy sprinkles
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
In a large mug pour 6 to 8 ounces of hot water over instant cocoa; mix well until dissolved.  Spoon whipped cream onto cocoa; top with candy sprinkles and a cherry.
