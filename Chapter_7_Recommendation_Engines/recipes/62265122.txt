Title:
Ham and Sausage Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 (20-ounce) package kielbasa or other smoked sausage, cut into 1/2-inch slices
1 med onion, chopped
1 medium green pepper, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 cups chicken broth
1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
1/2 pound cooked ham, cut into cubes
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon Tabasco sauce
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
1/8 teaspoon ground allspice
1 1/2 cups uncooked rice

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add sausage, onions, green pepper, celery and garlic and cook 8 to 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Add chicken broth, tomatoes, ham, bay leaf, Tabasco sauce, thyme and allspice; simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
Stir in rice; cover and simmer 15 minutes or until rice is tender, adding more broth or water if needed. Remove bay leaf before serving.
