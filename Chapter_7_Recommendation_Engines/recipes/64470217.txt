Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2464220.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Instant mashed potatoes
Cheese (Cheddar, Gouda, Havarti)
Bacon bits
Bread crumbs
Chives

Directions:
Make mashed potatoes as described on box, but omit 1/3 of the required water.
Sprinkle bread crumbs and chives on a serving platter for garnish.
When potatoes are ready and slightly cooled, shape the sheep bodies and place on serving platter.
Cut cheese slices and place on front of body for each sheep.
Shape more potatoes to form the head and place on top of the cheese slices so they stick out a bit.
Cut smaller cheese slices to place four legs on sides of sheep bodies.
Place two bacon bits at edge of cheese muzzle and head potato head to serve as eyes.
Sprinkle bread crumbs on potato sheep for texture.
Eat fluffy sheep bites. Om nom nom.
