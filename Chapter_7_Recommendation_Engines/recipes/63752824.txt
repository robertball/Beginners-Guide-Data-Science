Title:
Buttery Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
5 tablespoons butter
salt

Directions:
Steam Brussels sprouts in a pot of lightly salted boiling water for 15 minutes, or until fork-tender; strain through a colander, removing as much water as possible. Set aside.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat.  Toss Brussels sprouts with butter. salt to taste. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Miriam
Description: 
My children love Brussells Sprouts because I cook them this way.
