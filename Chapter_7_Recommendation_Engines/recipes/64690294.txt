Title:
Amy’s Broccoli Ditalini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5118210.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stalk broccoli
1/2 pound of Ditalini
1/2 Cup Parmesan cheese - grated
1/2 Cup Pecorino cheese - grated
1/4 Cup of olive oil (extra virgin)
1T salt
Red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
Wash and roughly chop broccoli
Add broccoli to a large sauce pot and fill with water. Add salt  and bring to a boil
Let broccoli cook for 1-2 minutes
Add Ditalini.
Cook until Ditalini is al dente and broccoli is soft .
With a ladle, reserve about 4-6 Cups of cooking liquid.
Drain broccoli and Ditalini.
Add olive oil to the now empty pot. Heat oil.
Lightly sauté garlic and add red pepper flakes if desired.
Sauté the broccoli and some of the Ditalini until well coated with garlic oil.
While sautéing, break up large broccoli pieces.
Some of the Ditalini will stick as you sauté . To avoid this add a ladle of reserves cooking liquid.
Add the rest of the Ditalini to the sautéed broccoli and combine well.
Add the cheese and stir to combine.
Add most of the reserved coooking liquid.
Cook on medium low heat until the cheese and cooking liquid create a creamy sauce.
Serve and enjoy!
