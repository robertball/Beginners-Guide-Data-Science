Title:
Caesar Salad Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
4-5 skinless boneless  cubed chicken breasts
1Tbsp oil

Directions:
Blend all ingredients except chicken and oil.
Fry chicken in pan with oil until browned.
Add blended ingredients to browned chicken.
Serve over Caesar salad, rice, or noodles
