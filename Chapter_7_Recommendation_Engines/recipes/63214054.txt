Title:
Kim's Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot Buffalo sauce or similar
1 cup mild cheddar cheese shredded
2 large cans shredded chicken, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together cream cheese, ranch dressing, 1/2 cup shredded cheese & hot sauce.
When mixed well add in chicken.
Pour mixture into 9x9 glass baking dish and cover with remaining shredded cheese.
Bake for 20 minuted or until mixture is bubbling.
