Title:
Brown Sugar & Honey Glazed Salmon with Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695771.jpg
Ingredients:
2 5-7oz pieces of salmon, skin removed
1/2 cup honey
2 TBL dark brown sugar
1 TBL fresh parsley, chopped fine
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
1/2 yellow bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup chicken broth
1 box Isreali couscous (Trader Joe's brand is best)
salt
pepper
cayenne pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350. Cook couscous according to package instructions, but substitute 1/4 cup water with 1/4 cup chicken broth. Once couscous is ready, add diced peppers and parsley, mix, then set aside.
Mix the honey and brown sugar. Place salmon in a baking dish and season with salt, pepper and cayenne pepper. Pour 1/2 the honey/sugar mix on top of salmon and bake for 15 min. Remove from oven and pour remaining honey/sugar mix on top and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
Plate the couscous and place a piece of salmon on top and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
The perfect combo of flavors and textures are sure to please everyone with sweet and spicy. Couscous makes this dish ordinary to extraordinary.
