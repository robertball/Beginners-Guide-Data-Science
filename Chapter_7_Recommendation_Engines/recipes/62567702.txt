Title:
Slow-Cooked Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound flank steak, cut in half
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 onion, sliced
1 can rotel
1 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Season meat and quickly sear in hot pan.
Transfer to a slow cooker.
In the same skillet, saute onion for 1 minute.  Deglaze pan with a little wine or water, then canned tomatoes stirring to loosen browned bits from pan.
Transfer all to crockpot and pour over the flank steak.
Cover and cook on low for 2 to 2 1/2 hours or until the meat is tender.
Slice the meat and serve with onion and juices.
