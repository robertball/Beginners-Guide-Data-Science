Title:
Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Parmesan Cheese
1 Teaspoon Pepper
1 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Granulated Garlic
1 1/2 Teaspoon Pizza Yeast
1 1/4 Cups of All Purpose Flour
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
1/2 Cup Warm Beer/Water

Directions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, mix in Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Parmesan cheese and yeast with 1/2 Cups of warm water or beer. Let sit until the yeast starts to expand (10-20 minutes)
2. Add Olive oil to the mixture
3. Mix in flour until the dough ball is smooth (add more water/beer or flour in tiny amounts to even it out) and let rise for 1 hour
4. Roll out the dough on some flour to keep from sticking and bake pizza in 350 degree oven for 20-40 minutes depending on size until bottom is golden brown. You can bake it on a pizza stone, Screen or a pan of any size.
