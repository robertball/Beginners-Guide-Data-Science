Title:
Four Cheese Shrimp Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 block cream cheese
1 c. cottage cheese
1/2 c. cheddar cheese
1 can small salad shrimp or 1/3 lb. pre-cooked small shrimp
1/3 c. green onions
1/2 c. cocktail sauce
2 Tbsp. shredded parmeasan cheese

Directions:
Allow cream cheese to sit a room temperature until softened.  Combine cream cheese, cheddar cheese, and cottage cheese.  Stir until combined.  Spread mixture onto serving plate.  Spread cocktail sauce on mixture.  Add shrimp and green onions on top of cocktail sauce.  Sprinkle with shredded parmeasan cheese.  Chill for 1 hour. Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.

Description: 
I love the basic shrimp spread, but became tired of the same ingredients.  Here is a new delicious twist on the classic shrimp spread.
