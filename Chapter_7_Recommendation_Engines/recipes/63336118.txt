Title:
Caesar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
2 large cloves garlic, chopped
10 anchovy fillets
1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (3 lemons)
1-2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 cup good olive oil
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place everything but the parmesan and oil in food processor bowl.  Process until blended.  Stream in oil slowly as the machine is running.  Add in parmesan and pulse a few times.  Taste and adjust with more salt as needed.
