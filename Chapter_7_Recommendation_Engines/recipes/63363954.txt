Title:
Carmel Apple Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (18 1/4 oz) spiced cake mix or carrot cake mix
2 cups chopped peeled tart apples
20 caramels
3 tbsp 2% milk
1 cup fine chopped pecans, toasted
12 popsicle sticks

Directions:
Prepare cake batter according to package directions; fold in apples.
Fill 12 greased or paper-lined jumbo muffin cups three-fourths full. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks for cooling.
In a small saucepan, cook the caramels and milk over low heat until smooth. Spread over cupcakes. Sprinkle with pecans. Insert a woodn stick into the center of each cupcake.
