Title:
Adas Polow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351427.jpg
Ingredients:
lentils, 400 grams
rice, 500 grams
dates (pitted), 100 grams
raisins, 120 grams
saffron, 1/2 teaspoon
onions, 2 large
cooking oil
salt

Directions:
Soak rice in water for 3-4 hours, then cook in salted water for 10-15 minutes using a non-stick pot until it just softens. Drain water and empty the pot.Bring 2-3 cups of water to boil. Wash lentil and add to water with a bit of salt. Cook over medium heat for about 15-20 minutes until tender.Peel and thinly slice onions. Fry in oil until slightly golden.Pour
some oil in the non-stick pot.Pour in half of the rice.Follow lentil. Then add in remainder of rice. Cook over low heat for about 20 minutes. Dissolve saffron in 1/3 cup of hot water and pour over the rice.After cooking decorate it by raisins, dates and Fried onions
