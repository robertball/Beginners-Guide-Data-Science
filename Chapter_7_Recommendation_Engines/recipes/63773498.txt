Title:
Granny Eden's Old-Fashioned Chocolate Cake Brownie with Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045776.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
1 cup margarine
1 cup water
1/4 cup cocoa
1/2 cup buttermilk
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
Icing ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
6 tsp milk
1/4 cup cocoa
1 tsp vanilla
1 pound confectioner's sugar
Nuts (if desired)

Directions:
Sift together flour and sugar.  Bring to a rapid boil the margarine, water, cocoa and buttermilk.  Pour over flour and sugar mixture, mix well.  After slight cool, add the eggs, soda and vanilla.  Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes in a 9x13 pan.
For icing:
Bring all icing ingredients, except the confectioner's sugar, to a rapid boil.  Then mix the confectioner's sugar thoroughly.  Add nuts, if desired.  Spread over cooled cake.  Slice cake, pour large glass of milk, enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alicia Eden Stidam
Description: 
This was a favorite of mine growing up in an old-fashioned Kentucky kitchen.  My Granny ran a hometown restaurant for many years, and this is one of many delicious dishes she cooked for the community.  Hope you all get as much enjoyment from this cake as I have over the years!!
