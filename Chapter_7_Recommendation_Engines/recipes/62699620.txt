Title:
Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/610259.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices white bread
2 tablespoons butter, divided
2 slices Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat skillet over medium heat
Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread
Place one slice, butter side down, in a skillet;
Top with 1 slice of the cheese
Top with remaining bread, butter side up.
Cook over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
