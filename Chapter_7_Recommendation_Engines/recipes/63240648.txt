Title:
Spicy Asian Peanut Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/845094.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz. of your favorite pasta (angel hair, linguini, spaghetti)
1 1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
1/3 cup red pepper seasoned rice vinegar
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon Sriracha chili sauce

Directions:
Boil pasta according to package directions.  Meanwhile whisk together peanut butter, rice vinegar, soy sauce and chili sauce until smooth.  Drain pasta and toss with sauce.  Serve immediately or chilled.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
Easy and delicious Asian inspired pasta dish.  Your favorite pasta with a smooth and spicy peanut sauce.  Serve warm or cold.  Also excellent tossed with grilled chicken or beef.
