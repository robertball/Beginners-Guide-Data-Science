Title:
Lemon fruit curd ( pudding) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 Talb grated lemon zest
1/2 cup lemon juice ( from 2-3 lemons)
Pinch of salt
3 large eggs plus 3 egg yolks
4talb cold unsalted butter cut into small pieces

Directions:
Whisk sugar lemon zest juice salt in med sauce pan ... in separate bowl will eggs then will into the Lemon mix
Cook med heat wisk constantly 6-8 min
Remove from heat and wisk in the butter a few Pieces  at a time
Strain the mix thru sieve into small bowl press thru with spatula
Press plastic wrap directly on surface so no skim forms
Refrigerate 4 hrs for up to 5 days
For lime curd follow above 4 tea grated lime zes 1/2 c lime juice ( (3-4limes) or use bottle juice

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Make and place in graham cracker crust for pie or serve on top of cake
