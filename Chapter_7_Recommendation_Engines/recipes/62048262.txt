Title:
Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups 1/2" cubed zucchini/squash (I like to mix them)
4 carrots
2 small onions
2 cans of cream of chicken soup
1 pint of sour cream
1 (8 oz. pkg.) of Pepperidge Farm Seasoned Stuffing
6 Tbsp. margerine
Buttered cracker crumbs

Directions:
1. Bring pan of water to a boil, cook cubed Zucchini for 3 minutes, no longer, drain.  Set aside.
2. Preheat oven to 350, grease 9x13 pan
3. Grate Carrots course, dice Onion fine, add soup, melted margerine, sour cream.
4. Add stuffing, fold in Zucchini (careful not to mash Zucchini)
5. Sprinkle with buttered cracker crumbs
6. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes

Submitter: 
Tracie
Description: 
Yummy Zucchini dish!  My Nanny has made this for us for many years and it is always a terrific surprise.  Enjoy!
