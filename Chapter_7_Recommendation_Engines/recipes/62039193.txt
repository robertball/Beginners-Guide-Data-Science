Title:
O'Henry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup corn syrup
12 ounces chunky peanut butter
6 cups Special-K cereal
1 (12 ounce) package milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Bring one cup sugar and one cup corn syrup to boil. Add chunky peanut butter and mix well. Make a well in the cereal which has been placed in a large bowl. Add peanut butter mixture and mix well. Drop onto a greased cookie sheet and pat down. Melt 12 ounces chips and spread over top. Chill in refrigerator until chocolate is set. Cut into squares or bars.

Submitter: 
SUZZANNA
Description: 
Very quick and easy to make.  Tastes like an O'Henry candy bar!
