Title:
Pepperoni Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871451.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4c warm water
2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
1 1/2T white sugar
3 1/2c bread flour
1/4c Parmesan cheese
1T dried oregano
2t garlic powder
1t red pepper flakes
1t salt
1t black pepper
2T butter, melted
1c. shredded mozzarella cheese
8oz sliced pepperoni

Directions:
Combine yeast, warm water and sugar in a mixer bowl and let stand until frothy.
Add bread flour, Parmesan cheese, oregano, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper.  Knead well.
Cover dough and allow to rise in a warm location for one hour or until doubled in size.
Punch down dough and roll out into an 18in by 9in rectangle.  Spread melted butter on dough and sprinkle evenly with mozzarella cheese.  Layer pepperoni slices on top of cheese.
Starting at one end, roll the dough into a tight log and place in a lightly greased 9x5 loaf pan.  Cover and allow to rise in a warm location for 30 minutes.
Bake loaf at 350 until lightly browned on top, about 35 minutes.
