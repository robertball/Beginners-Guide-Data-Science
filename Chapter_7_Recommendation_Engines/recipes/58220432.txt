Title:
Excellent Crab Imperial (HB) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup dry sherry
2 tablespoon finely chopped shallots
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning
1 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 egg yolk
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 pound crab meat
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup Gruyere cheese, shredded
1 tablespoon. chopped parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F  Lightly grease 8&#34; pie dish.
Bring sherry to boil in a medium saucepan, and stir in shallots,  Over medium heat, cook, stirring occasionally, until only a SMALL amount of liquid remains.
In a small saucepan over low heat, whisk together cream, Old Bay, lemon juice, Worcestershire, cayenne, and egg yolk for about 3 minutes. Stir into the shallot mixture. Mix in dry mustard.
Remove the mixture from heat, and stir in the crab. Cool, stir in the mayonnaise, then scoop into ie dish. Top with Gruyere cheese and parsley.
Bake 10-15 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned.

Hank Blakely
