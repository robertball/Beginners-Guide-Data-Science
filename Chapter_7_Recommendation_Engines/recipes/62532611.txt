Title:
Personal Banana Bread Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour (may need to add up to an additional 1/2 cup depending on size of bananas)
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 medium sized mashed bananas (about a cup and a half)
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
