Title:
Cinnamon Sugar Butter Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c Bisquick biscuit mix
3/4 c whole milk
1/2 c butter, melted
6 Tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees, lightly grease a baking sheet.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine biscuit mix and milk until combined.
In a separate bowl mix together cinnamon and sugar, set aside.
Drop heaping tablespoons of biscuit dough on greased baking sheet, and bake for 8 minutes.
When cool enough to handle, but still warm, dip entire biscuit in the melted butter, and then roll in the cinnamon sugar mixture.
Set on cooling racks until cooled.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
I decided to make these when trying to use up biscuit mix. If you love cinnamon as I do, you will love these biscuits. They are a nice sweet addition to any breakfast or brunch. Enjoy!
