Title:
Southwest Stuffed Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976692.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds extra lean ground beef or lean ground turkey
1/4 cup KRAFT Mayo Light Mayonnaise, divided
1 package (1 1/4 oz) TACO BELL速 HOME ORIGINALS速 Taco Seasoning Mix
1/2 cup KRAFT Natural 2% Milk Reduced Fat Colby & Monterey Jack Cheese Crumbles
1/4 cup TACO BELL速 HOME ORIGINALS速 Thick 'N Chunky Salsa, divided
lettuce leaves
6 slices tomato
6 whole wheat hamburger buns, split

Directions:
Preheat greased grill to medium-high heat. Mix meat, 3 tablespoons of the mayo and the seasoning mix; shape into 12 thin patties. Combine cheese and 2 tablespoons of the salsa; spoon over 6 of the patties. Cover each with one of the remaining patties; pinch edges together to seal.
Grill 7 minutes on each side or until cooked through (160 degrees F). Meanwhile, combine remaining 2 tablespoons salsa and remaining 1 tablespoon mayo.
Place lettuce, tomatoes and burgers on bottom halves of buns; top with salsa mixture. Cover with tops of buns.

Submitter: 
Kraft
Description: 
A mixture of cheese and salsa is at the center of these zesty burgers.
