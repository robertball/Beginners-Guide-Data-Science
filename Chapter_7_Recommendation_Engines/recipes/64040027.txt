Title:
Big Ray's White Bean Crostini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123834.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can cannallini beans, drained and rinsed (15 oz.)
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling on top
white pepper
1 loaf crusty bread, cut into 12 slices or wedges (1 lb.)

Directions:
Combine beans, garlic, salt in blender or food processor.  Slowly add oil and blend until smooth.  Add pepper and spread on bread.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Have friends just drop in?  Here is a fast and tasty treat that goes great with wine.
