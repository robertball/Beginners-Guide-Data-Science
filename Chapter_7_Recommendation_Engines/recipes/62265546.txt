Title:
Mom's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. hamburger
1 c. + 1 T. ketchup
3 T. dried onions
2 T. yellow mustard
1 T. brown sugar
1 t. salt
1/4 t. black pepper
1/4 c. water

Directions:
Mix everything except hamburger in sauce pan over medium heat until hot all the way through.
Simmer hamburger in 1/4 c. of water over medium-high heat (makes hamburger crumble easily).
Drain hamburger and add to the sauce.
Combine thoroughly.
Serve on hamburger buns with slices of American cheese and dill pickle chips.

Submitter: 
Bover
Description: 
I'm fairly certain my mother borrowed this recipe from a well known ice cream royalty chain that carried them on their menu as BBQ Burgers.  Simple, yet very tasty!
