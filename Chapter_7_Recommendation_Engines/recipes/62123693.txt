Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked, finely diced chicken breast
8 oz cream cheese
8 oz bottled bleu cheese salad dressing
12 oz buffalo wing sauce
2 tablespoons tomato paste
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour

Directions:
In 10 inch skillet,over medium low heat, melt butter, mix in flour to make a roux, simmer slowly for 10 minutes.
Add wing sauce and tomato paste, whisk together until smooth.
Add in chicken, mix together to combine chicken and sauce. Remove from heat.
Soften cream cheese in microwave for 20 seconds. Beat well.
Mix in bleu cheese dressing.
Spread cream cheese mixture in bottom of an 8x8 glass baking pan.
Top with chicken mixture.
Bake in a 350* oven 20 minutes.
Serve with celery stick 'dippers' and buttery crackers.

Submitter: 
Billie Miller
Description: 
Tastes like a chicken wing in a dip! Use celery sticks as 'dippers'
