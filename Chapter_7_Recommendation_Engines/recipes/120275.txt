Title:
Cream-Topped Grapes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977647.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup sour cream
3 cups seedless green grapes
3 cups red seedless grapes

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar and vanilla. Add the sour cream; mix well. Divide grapes among individual serving bowls; dollop with topping.

Submitter: 
Vioda  Geyer
Description: 
Viola Geyer, Uhrichsville, Ohio, dresses up brunches with a decadent dressing that she makes in a dash. She dollops the heavenly four-ingredient sauce over refreshing red and green grapes.
