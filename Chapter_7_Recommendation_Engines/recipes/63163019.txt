Title:
Tangy Spinach and Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz Frozen or par-boiled chopped adult spinach
10 oz Frozen diced butternut squash
Old Bay Seasoning
Salt

Directions:
Place a pan with the spinach and squash in it on high heat.
When they defrost, season to taste.
Everything is done when it is hot.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
Spicy, but not in a hot sense.  Note: if you are not using frozen spinach, you might have to add some water.
