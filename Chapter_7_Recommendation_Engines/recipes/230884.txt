Ingredients:
2 cups ice cubes
1/3 cup triple sec
1/3 cup tequila (such as Jose CuervoÂ®)
1/3 cup freshly squeezed lime juice

Directions:
Fill 4 margarita glasses each with 1/2 cup ice.
Blend triple sec, tequila, and lime juice together in a blender; pour into the prepared margarita glasses.

Submitter: 
Beverly Fischer-Steel
Description: 
A different margarita, so smooth you can't do two. Drink slowly and responsibly. Dip the rim of the glass in equal parts white sugar and salt if desired.
