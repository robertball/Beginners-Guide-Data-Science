Title:
Easy Tuna Mushroom Casserole with Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked macaroni
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3/4 cup frozen peas
1 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. Spanish Smoked Paprika
1/2 tsp. White Pepper
1 cup Tempura flakes (or French Fried Onions)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a 9x13-inch baking dish, combine the macaroni, tuna, peas, onion powder, Smoked paprika, white pepper, and soup. Mix well, and then top with cheese and tempura flakes (NOTE: Tempura flakes are added before you put casserole in the ove, but if you are using French Fried Onions, add them in the final 5 minutes of baking).
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 25 minutes, or until bubbly. Serve hot.
