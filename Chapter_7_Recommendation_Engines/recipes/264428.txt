Keto Detox Green Smoothie for 2 Recipe

1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and quartered
1 cup fresh spinach
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon low-calorie natural sweetener (such as SwerveÂ®)

ice cubes

Directions:
