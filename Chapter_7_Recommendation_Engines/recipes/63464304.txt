Title:
Festive Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lbs. Chopped chuck (good grade)
1 package Lipton's onion soup mix
3/4 C. ketchup or chili sauce
3/4 C. hot water
2 eggs
1 1/2 C. bread crumbs or oatmeal (a little light on the measure if you use oatmeal)
4 strips bacon, cut for the topping

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients except the bacon. Place in a 9"x4" bread loaf pan. Cover with bacon. Bake at 350 degrees at least 1 hour.
