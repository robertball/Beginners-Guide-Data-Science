Title:
Pumpkin Butterscotch Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1499540.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Crisco butter flavor baking stick (1 stick)
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup pumpkin puree
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp ground cinnamon
2 1/2 cups old fashioned oatmeal
1 cup butterscotch chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Beat Crisco stick, sugars, and pumpkin until creamy.
Add eggs and vanilla, mixing well.
In separate bowl combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon.
Add flour mixture to dough gradually mixing well.
Add oatmeal gradually and mix well.
Lastly add the butterscotch chips and make sure they are evenly mixed.
Drop by rough tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake for 8-10 minutes until light golden brown.

Submitter: 
Laura Goodman
Description: 
I was in the mood for something sweet, with pumpkin and butterscotch and I came up with these cookies. My husband and kids love them!
