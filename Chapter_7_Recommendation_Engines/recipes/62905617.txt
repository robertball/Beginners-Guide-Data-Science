Title:
Judi's Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups chicken broth
1 1/4 cups water
1 cup uncooked white rice
1 large head cabbage
2 pounds lean ground beef
1 med. onion, chopped fine
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 t. cinnamon, divided
3 (8 oz.) cans tomato sauce, divided

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, bring water, chicken broth, and rice to a boil and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Steam cabbage leaves in little water for approximately 20 min,; drain. Carfully separate cabbage leaves and pile on a plate.
Pour 1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce in bottom of a large crock pot.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, 2 cups cooked rice, onion, egg, salt and pepper, 1 t. cinnamon, 1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce. Mix thoroughly.
Scoop a generous portion of the beef mixture (approx. 1/4 to 1/3 cup) onto a cabbage leaf and roll. Place cabbage roll in crock pot. Repeat until all beef mixture is used.
Pour 1 (8 oz.) can tomato sauce on top of cabbage rolls.  Sprinkle with additional salt and pepper and remaining 1 t. cinnamon.  Cover rolls with any remaining cabbage.  Cover and cook on low setting for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Judi M.
Description: 
Cabbage leaves are stuffed with ground beef, rice, onion, egg, and cinnamon. Works best in a slow cooker.Modified from: Stuffed Cabbage Rolls
