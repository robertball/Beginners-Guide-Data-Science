Title:
Restaurant style mexican salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can whole tomatos
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1 tbl chopped onion (optional)
2-3 tbls fresh cilantro (to taste)
2-3 tbls pickled jalepenos (to taste)

Directions:
Pour entire can of tomatos into a food processor.  Add all other ingredients and blend until smooth.  It is ready to eat immediately, however the flavors blend better overnight.

Submitter: 
Capepointfisherman
Description: 
This salsa tastes exactly like the salsa seved at the local Mexican restaurants in town.
