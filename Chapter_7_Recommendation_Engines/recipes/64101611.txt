Title:
Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 gallons water
1 gallon split peas
8 C celery, small diced
8 C yellow onion, small diced
8 C carrots, small diced
3 C soy sauce
1 lb. bacon, raw, chopped
6-7 C ham, small diced
1/2 C chicken base
ham base, to taste
1/3 C granulated garlic
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a large soup pot and bring to a slow, rolling boil.  Cook about 2-2 1/2 hours, or until split peas are fully cooked.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Thick and hearty!  Perfect to warm you up on a cold day!
