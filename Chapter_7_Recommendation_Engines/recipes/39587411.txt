Title:
Coco Loco Banana Daiquiri Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C raw sugar
1/4 C melted butter
1/2 C milk
2 eggs, beaten
1 T cointreau liqueur
3 T rum
2 C mashed overripe bananas(about4)
2 1/4 C flour
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t baking soda
1/4 t cinnamon
1/4 t ground allspice
1/2 t salt
1/2 C shredded coconut

Directions:
1. Preheat oven 350
2. Grease and flour loaf pan
3. In lg. bowl, thoroughly combine raw sugar and melted butter
4. Add milk,eggs, liquors and bananas
5. In separate large bowl, combine dry ingred.
6. Add this to butter-banana mixture
7. Combine until JUST mixed-do not over mix
8. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan and bake at least 1 hr (more like 1hr, 15-30min) or until pick inserted in center comes out clean
9. Cool 25-30 min in pan
