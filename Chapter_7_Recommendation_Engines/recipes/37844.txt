Title:
Fun Taco Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983652.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Taco Seasoning Mix
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1/2 cup KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Brown meat; drain.  Add Seasoning Mix; prepare as directed on package.
Press biscuits onto bottoms and up sides of medium muffin pan.  Fill biscuits with seasoned meat.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake an additional 2 to 3 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Serve with Taco Bell Home Originals Thick 'N Chunky Salsa, if desired.
