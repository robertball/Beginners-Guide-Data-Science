Title:
Camp Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/684151.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups honey-oat cereal
2 cups peanuts
1 cup dried cranberries
1 cup carob chips
1/2 cup sunflower seeds

Directions:
Combine the cereal, peanuts, cranberries, carob chips, and sunflower seeds in a mixing bowl. Store at room temperature in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Erin
Description: 
This is a great take anywhere snack. Good for camping and long car trips.
