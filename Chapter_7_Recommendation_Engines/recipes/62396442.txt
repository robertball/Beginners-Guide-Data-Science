Title:
Roast Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pound chuck roast
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 can chicken gumbo soup

Directions:
Place roast in pan.  Sprinkle onion soup mix over the top. Then pour can of gumbo soup over the top. Cover and bake at 250 for 2-1/2 hours.  Take out of oven and pull apart with fork.  Serve on bakery buns.

Submitter: 
Shawn Hoffman Zimmerman
Description: 
Quick and easy recipe for roast beef sandwiches.  Always a crowd pleaser.
