Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2457173.jpg
Ingredients:
-1 and 1/4th cup white sugar
-1 cup shortening
-1 egg
-1 tsp vanilla
-1 tsp baking powder
-1 tsp baking soda
-a pinch salt
-3 cups pastry flour
-a pinch salt
-marshmallows

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
2. Cream together the shortening and sugar in a bowl an add in vanilla and eggs. In a seperate bowl mix together baking powder, baking soda, salt, and the flour.
3. Using your mite arms mix together the dry ingredients with the wet.
4. Roll out the dough using a bit of extra flour to help. Cut out the shapes you want, preferably circles to go with the marshmallows. Cut marshmallows into 3's or desired amount to put on top of cookies after.
5. Bake on cookie sheets for 7-8 minutes.
6. Put marshmallow pieces on top of cookies and under broil for a a minute.
7. Take out your cookies and enjoy the fluffy sweet  goodness!
