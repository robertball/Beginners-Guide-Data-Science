Title:
Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15 ounce) cans whole kernel corn, drained
8 green onions, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and diced
1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos
3 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1/4 cup lime juice
1/2 cup Italian salad dressing
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
2 bags corn chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the black beans, corn, green onions, bell pepper, avocado, pimentos, tomatoes, lime juice, and Italian dressing. Season with garlic salt. Toss, and chill until serving.
Serve with corn chips for dipping.

Description: 
This salad is a kaleidoscope of color and taste - black beans, yellow corn, green peppers, and red, red tomatoes. Lime juice, garlic and jalapeno give it some punch.Modified from: Black Bean Salad
