Title:
Chicken Paprika Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken, cut up
2 qt can tomatoes
2 med onions
2-3 green peppers
2-3 Tb paprika
salt to taste
1 pint sour cream (2 cups)
Wondra to thicken

Directions:
1. Saute onion and pepper until onions are clear.
2. Add tomatoes, stir, add paprika and salt.  Add chicken.
3. Leave till cooking, turn to simmer, cover (slanted).  Cook one hour, stirring occasionally.
4. Thicken sauce, add sour cream.  Serve over egg noodles.
