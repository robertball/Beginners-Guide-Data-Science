Title:
Montana Mulligatawny Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 onion chopped
3 chopped celery stalks
3 cloves minced garlic
2 teasp minced fresh ginger
2 chopped bell peppers one red one green
1/4 teasp ground cinnamon
1/4 teasp ground cloves
1 teasp ground coriander seed
1 1/2 teasp ground turmeric
1 teasp ground cardamom
1 tbsp ground curry
4 chopped carrots
1 chopped apple
6 cups chicken broth (can be vegetarian)
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 can coconut milk
1 cup apple juice
1 can pureed pumpkin
1 can rotel (canned tomatos)original
1 can chicken (optional)
1 cup quinoa
2 cup lentils
1 chopped sweet potato (optional)
1 chopped butternut squash ( optional)
3 Chopped green onions
1 cup chopped cilantro
1/2 cup chopped peanuts

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large soup pot, add celery, onion, carrots, garlic, ginger, and apple. Cover and cook for 15 minutes or until tender. Add apple juice, chicken broth, lemon juice, coconut milk, chicken, quinoa, and lentils. Add spices and cook for 2 hours. Serve each bowl with a topping of chopped green onion, chopped cilantro and chopped peanuts.
