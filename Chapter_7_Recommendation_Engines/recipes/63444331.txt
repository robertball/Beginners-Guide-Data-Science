Title:
Lorraine's Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. dry red beans rinsed and soaked over night
3 lbs. sausage, sliced
3 onions, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup oil
20-30 shakes Tony Chacere's seasoning
3 or 4 bay leaves

Directions:
Place all ingredients in crock pot and cook until done. High a couple of hours or low all day.
(Depends on your crockpot.)

Description: 
I serve this over white rice.
