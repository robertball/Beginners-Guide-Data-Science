Title:
pickle rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar KOSHER WHOLE pickles
2 packages Budding sliced corned beef
1 8 oz package cream cheese softened

Directions:
place 2 slices of corned beef flat overlapping 1/2 of each with eachother.spread softeneed cream cheese layer on top. Roll pickle up in cream cheese and corned beef. Chill for 1 hour. Slice to make circles.

Submitter: 
MISTIDAWN
Description: 
This recipe came from my mother in law. I finally got the recipe and how she makes them! They are pretty, tempting, and absolutely delicious! How can you go wrong with something so simple and different to take to a party or pot luck!!??I'm sure you'll be asked for the recipe so make copies!!
