Title:
Fried Perch Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093068.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound perch fillets
1/2 cup corn meal
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
ground pepper, to taste
2 tablespoons oil, for frying

Directions:
Heat oil in a large frying pan, over medium high heat.
In a re-sealable plastic bag combine corn meal, Parmesan cheese, oregano, salt, garlic powder and pepper.
Rinse fillets, shaking off excessive water, and place in the plastic bag. Shake until fillets are coated.
Fry coated fillets over medium high heat, turning over once, until fillets flake easily, about 3 minutes per side. Serve.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Fish fillets coated in a seasoned corn meal mixture are fried quickly. The result is a recipe that is tasty, quick and healthy.
