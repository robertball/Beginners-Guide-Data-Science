Title:
Chocolate Bar Brownies and Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownies:
1 cup chocolate chips
2 70% cocoa bars, regular size (Lindt)
1/2 cup butter or margarine
3 eggs
1 and 1/4 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup finely ground nuts
Icing:
1/2 can sweetened condensed milk
1/4 cup chocolate chips
2 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup icing sugar

Directions:
Brownies:
In saucepan on low heat melt butter and add chocolate chips and chunked up chocolate bars. Stir continuously until chocolate is melted. Take saucepan off the burner and stir in eggs, sugar, ground nuts and vanilla. Add flour and baking powder stir until smooth. Finally add the sweetened condensed milk. Put in preheated 350 degree oven in a 9x13 greased pan for 20 minutes. Test and take out when knife or toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. For thicker brownies cook in 8x8 greased pan at 325 for 30 minutes.
Icing:
While brownies are baking all the rest of the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla, butter and chocolate to a pan and stir on low heat until the chocolate is melted. Take the saucepan off the heat and add the icing sugar. Let cool in pan on counter or stove. Do not refrigerate. Add on top of brownies once they have slightly cooled.
