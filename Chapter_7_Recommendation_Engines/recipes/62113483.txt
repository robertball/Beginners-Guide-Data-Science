Title:
Potluck Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
4 cups milk
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp onion salt
10 slices white or sourdough bread
2 tsp prepared mustard
1 cup favorite cooked meat

Directions:
First, toast the 10 slices of bread and cube into 1" squares.
Then beat the eggs together with the onion salt and mustard.
Pour evenly over bread in a casserole dish.
Add the meat and top off with milk and cheese.
Prepare the night before serving, cover with plastic and refridgerate.
Morning of, bake at 325 degrees for 35 minutes or until it isn't soupy anymore.

Submitter:

Description: 
Prepare the night before, cook in the morning, easily a favorite!
