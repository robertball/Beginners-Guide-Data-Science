Title:
Simple Maple Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup boiling water
1/2 teaspoon maple flavored extract
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine sugars and water. Cook and stir until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat, and stir in maple flavoring and vanilla. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
We never had store bought syrup in the house, and when you taste this you'll know why.  It's so simple and so good, anything else seems pointless.Modified from: Simple Maple Syrup
