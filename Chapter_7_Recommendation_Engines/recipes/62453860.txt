Title:
Daddy's Barbecue Sauce - doubled Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1 1/2 c. chopped onion
1 1/2 c. ketchup
1 1/2 c. water
2/3 c. lemon juice
6 T. sugar
6 T. Worcestershire sauce
4 T. prepared mustard
4 t. salt
1 t. pepper

Directions:
Brown onions in oil until soft.
Add remaining ingredients and cook slow until thick.
Store in 1 qt jar in refrigerator

Submitter: 
Amy Hague Miller
Description: 
This is the best barbecue sauce.  I always double or triple and keep in the refrigerator for grilling season.Modified from: Daddy's Barbecue Sauce
