Title:
Cucumber Raita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033536.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Greek Yogurt – well beaten
1 medium Cucumber
1/2 medium Tomato, finely chopped
Green Chili – to taste, finely chopped
1 Clove garlic - minced
1/4 tsp Cumin, roasted and coarsely ground
1/4 tsp Red Chili Powder
1/4 tsp Chat Masala
Salt – to taste
1/4 tsp Mint Powder, optional
5 springsCilantro, chopped
1/2 Lemon, juiced

Directions:
Seed cucumber. Grate 1/2 and chop 1/2 cucumber with or without skin.
Squeeze out the water from grated Cucumber.
Add all the ingredients into the beaten yogurt and mix well.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This is a great yogurt dish, very soothing and cooling….especially for the times when you are finding your meal too spicy and you need something to counter it!!!!
