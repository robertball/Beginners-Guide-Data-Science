Title:
Puerto Rican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 medium onion diced
1/2 of a bell pepper, you can choose one color or use several different colors
6 oz. can tomatoe sauce
1 Cup rice
2 cups water
1 can of diced tomatoes, juice seperated
14 oz can of corn
15.5 oz can of pinto beans
1/2 tablespoon salt
1/2 tablespoon pepper
2 packages Sazo"n Goya
2 Tbls Goya Adobo

Directions:
In a large stock pot oil the bottom of pan with olive oil. Add you garlic, onions and green peppers till you smell the garlic pop. Once the garlic pops add the tomatoe sauce, rice, water, canned tomatoes, corn, beans, salt and pepper and Stir well. Add 2 packages Sazon Goya, Adobo (You will find this around the Mexican isle). Bring contents of the pan to a boil then simmer till Rice is done and juice starts to reduce. At this time you are welcome to add chicken, hamburger, fish or pork and remain cooking till all the juice has evaporated. Rice should have a stiff consistancy.

Submitter: 
Kathi Richards Smith
Description: 
I was honored when we had a guest stay with us for about 6 months who was visiting from Puerto Rico. The only thing I mastered when he lived with us was his rice. It is unbeliveable!
