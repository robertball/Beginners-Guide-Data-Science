Title:
Pineapple Delight Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4564970.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
2 bananas, frozen and chunked
6 pineapple rings
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
In a blender combine milk, frozen bananas, pineapple and honey. Blend until smooth.

Description: 
A refreshing healthy smoothie with bananas, pineapple, milk and honey.
