Title:
Halloween Cat Eyes JIGGLERS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045142.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups boiling tonic water (do not add cold water)
2 (3 ounce) packages JELL-O Brand Lemon Flavor Gelatin
4 red grapes, cut into sixths

Directions:
Spray 24 mini muffin pan cups with cooking spray; set aside. Add boiling tonic water carefully to gelatin mixes in large bowl (gelatin will foam); stir 3 min. until completely dissolved. Skim off any residual foam.
Spoon 1 tsp. of gelatin into each mini muffin cup. Place grape piece in the center. Chill 5 min. Spoon remaining gelatin over grapes. Refrigerate 3 hours or until firm.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
These spooky cat eyes glow under a black light if you use tonic water instead of water!
