Title:
Wilted Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bagged baby spinach
10 oz. thick bacon, cut into 1/2 in. cubes
1/2 of red onion
3 boiled eggs, quartered
3 T cider vinegar
1/2 t. sugar
1/4 t. pepper
pinch salt
1 clove garlic, finely chopped

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp, about 10 min. Remove bacon bits from skillet and drain on paper towel. Pour off grease and return 3T to skillet. Add onion and cook until tender, then add garlic and cook about 15 sec, just until garlic releases odor. Mix vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper until sugar melts. Pour into skillet, stir and pour over spinach. Add eggs and bacon and serve immed.
