Title:
Tracy Ann's Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599666.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons CriscoÂ®
2 (14.75 oz.) cans red salmon, drained & spines removed
1 cup plain bread crumbs
4 large eggs, beaten
2 tablespoons flour
2 teaspoons dill weed
2 teaspoons basil
1 small onion, chopped fine (optional)
1 teaspoon garlic salt (optiona)
1 teaspoon onion powder (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together all ingredients, except Crisco(R), and form into 5 large patties. Melt Crisco(R) in a large frying pan, and fry patties until golden brown. Move patties to an uncovered baking dish and bake in the oven for another 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
This is a clone of the large salmon patties served at my favorite restaurants.  I always ask them to put cheese sauce on mine.
