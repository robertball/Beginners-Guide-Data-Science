Title:
Arzate Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/687773.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 large can kidney beans
2- 15oz cans of hot chili beans
1 large can of chili beans
2-15oz cans stewed tomatoes
1-15oz can tomato sauce
1/2 white onion
1-2 green bell peppers
1-2 large tomatoes
1 1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
3 table. chili powder
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Cooke meat drain off grease add remaining ingredients and chopped onion, bell pepper, and tomato,season and let simmer stirring often.
