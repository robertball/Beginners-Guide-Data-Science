Title:
Apple Butter-Cinnamon Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons apple butter
4 slices SARA LEE® Soft & Smooth™ Whole Grain White Bread
2 tablespoons cinnamon sugar

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Spread a thin layer of apple butter onto both sides of each slice of bread, and cut into cubes. Place these in a bag or bowl, and pour the cinnamon sugar over them. Shake or stir to coat. Arrange the bread cubes in a single layer on a baking sheet.
Bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven, then stir and continue baking for 10 more minutes, until toasted.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee Soft & Smooth™
Description: 
Apple butter and cinnamon sugar flavor these croutons. Use them atop winter squash soups, or green salads.
