Title:
Low Sodium Teriyaki Sauce and Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2304443.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce:
¼ 	cup Trader Joes low sodium soy sauce
½ 	cup water
2 	tablespoons sweet rice wine (mirin)
2      teaspoons brown sugar or more to taste (or 2 teaspoons Splenda brown sugar blend)
¼	cup sugar (or 3-4 teaspoons splenda to taste)
1 ½	teaspoons minced garlic
1 ½	teaspoons minced ginger
Glaze:
½	tablespoon cornstarch
1 	tablespoon water

Directions:
1.	Combine ingredients in a saucepan on medium heat until the sugar is dissolved.
2.	To make glaze: Mix 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch with 1 tablespoon water. Heat sauce on medium high heat and add cornstarch water mixture. Simmer until thickened.

Asian sauce quick easy and much lower sodium than store bought.
