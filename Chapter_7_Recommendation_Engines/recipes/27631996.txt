Title:
Minty Hot Mocha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 cup water
2 tablespoons instant coffee granules
4 3/4 cups skim milk
1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract

Directions:
Combine sugar and cocoa in a medium saucepan; stir well. Add water; bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Stir in coffee granules. Gradually add milk, stirring well. Cook over medium heat 5 minutes or until mixture is thoroughly heated, stirring frequently. Remove from heat; stir in peppermint. Beat with a whisk until foamy; serve warm.
