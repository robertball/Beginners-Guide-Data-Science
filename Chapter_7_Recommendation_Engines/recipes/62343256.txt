Title:
Spicy Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of canola oil
Crushed Red Pepper (to taste)
One package of Ranch Dip Mix
5-6 packs of unsalted crackers

Directions:
Mix together canola oil, crushed red pepper and Ranch Dip Mix. Put in microwave for 30 seconds. Place crackers in a covered container and pour microwave mixture over them and shake.

Submitter: 
Tasha Graves Cleveland
Description: 
My Aunt always makes these at get togethers and they are always the first thing gone. Very simple recipe.
