Title:
Gyoza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oackage Gyoza wrappers
1/2 lb. ground pork (fry and drained)
1/2 lb. ground beef
12 medium mushrooms (chopped very fine)
1/4 cup green onions (chopped very fine)
1 clove garlic (chopped fine)
1/4 head of cabbage (boil and chop fine)
3 tsp. sesame oil
1 tsp. sake
1/4 tsp. Aji-Mirin
1/5 cup water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except wrappers.
Fill wrapper with mixture and seal.
Pan fry with small amount of oil for about 5 minutes until bottom becomes golden brown.
Pour 1/5 cup hot water into frying pan.
Cover and reduce heat to low and cook for about 7 minutes.
