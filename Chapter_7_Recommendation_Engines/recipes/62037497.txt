Title:
dirty rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream mushroom soup
2 cans consumme (not substitutions!)
1 cup uncooked white rice (long grain preferred)
1/2 stick butter
vegetable cooking spray

Directions:
preheat oven to 350, spray a 2 quart baking dish with cooking spray to coat the bottom and the sides.  Pour all soups and rice and stir till cream soup is no longer in lumps - dot with butter and bake, uncovered for 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Upon removing from the oven, stir b/c the soup will float to the top & let sit for 5 minutes.
