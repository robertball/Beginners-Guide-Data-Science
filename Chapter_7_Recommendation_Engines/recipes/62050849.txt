Title:
Tex-Mex Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of lettuce
5 tomatoes diced
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 pound ground beef, browned
1 bottle Catalina dressing
1 bag of Nacho Cheese Doritos, crushed
1 package of taco seasoning

Directions:
Brown and cook beef and taco seasoning accoring to directions on package. Pour off fat and refrigerate until meat is cold. In a large bowl, layer lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and meat. Make 3 layers. Just before serving, add crushed doritos and toss with catalina dressing.

Submitter: 
chefmommyof4
Description: 
An original taco salad with a great flavor. Great for parties, large get togethers, barbeque's etc since you can only serve immediately and can't save leftovers, which there probably won't be any of anyway
