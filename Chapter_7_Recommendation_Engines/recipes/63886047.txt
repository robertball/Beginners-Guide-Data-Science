Title:
Double Dark Chocolate Hazelnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078105.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, room temperature
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 to 3/4 cup granulated sugar, depending on taste
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 oz 70% dark chocolate, melted
1 tablespoon cocoa
1/4 teaspoon espresso powder (optional)
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup roasted hazelnuts, finely ground
1/2 cup roasted hazelnuts, chopped
1/2 cup semisweet or bittersweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325Â°
Cream butter until light and fluffy.
Beat in salt and sugar.
Beat in egg and vanilla.
Blend in melted chocolate and cocoa powder.
Gradually mix in flour till well-blended.
Finally mix in hazelnuts and chocolate chips.
Drop heaping tablespoonfuls of dough onto cookie sheets covered in parchment paper. Use the back of a wet fork to squash the cookies a bit, in a criss-cross pattern.
Bake for about 15 minutes, until done. Let cool a few minutes before enjoying.
