Title:
Stacey's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. Oil
2 lbs stew beef
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 t. salt
1/2t pepper
1 large onion chopped into large pieces
14.5 oz petite diced tomatoes NOT drained
21 oz water
3 oz ketchup
2 t salt
2 large potatoes, cut into large chunks
1 lb carrots cut peeled and cut into large chunks
2 large onions, chopped into large chunks
3 stalks celery sliced
1 c. canned sweet green peas

Directions:
In electric pressure cooker set to brown setting and add oil until heated.  Mix flour with 1 t. salt and 1/2 t pepper.   Pour into plastic bag and add beef shaking to coat. Pour beef mixture into pressure cooker and cook on all sides until dark brown about 30 minutes.  Add 1 chopped onion, tomatoes, ketchup, water and remaining 2 t salt.   Mix well to combine.  Cook on high pressure for 45 minutes.
Release pressure and add vegetables except peas.  Cook on high pressure an additional 10 minutes.  Allow to drop pressure naturally.  Stir in canned peas.
Serve.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
I love beef stew from Cracker Barrel.  This is my own interpretation of the recipe.  The secret is the ketchup!
