Title:
Broiled Lamb Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons fresh parsley
3 tablespoons fresh thyme
3 teaspoons olive oil
12 (5.5 ounce) lamb chops
dash of salt
dash of white pepper

Directions:
Rinse and pat dry lamb chops. Season with salt and pepper. In small bowl make a paste with parsley, thyme and olive oil. Rub gently into both sides of the lamb chops. Turn on the broiler. Place lamb chops into oven on shallow baking sheet. Broil both sides (3) to (4) minutes each. Serve immediately!
