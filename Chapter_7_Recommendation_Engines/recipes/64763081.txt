Title:
Slow Cooker Barbecue chicken breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 boneless chicken breasts
1-2 18 oz bottle(s) of your favorite barbecue sauce
3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions:
Step 1) Coat the chicken breasts in salt and pepper and place in slow cooker.
Step 2) Add vinegar and 1/4 cup barbecue sauce to slow cooker and cook on low for 3 hours.
Step 3) In a small mixing bowl combine brown sugar, onion powder, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes.
Step 4) After 3 hours empty liquid from slow cooker.
Step 5) Add remaining barbecue sauce and add sugar spice mixture.
Step 6) Cook on low for 3 more hours stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Jayson Pittman
Description: 
A super easy recipe for barbecue chicken. you can also serve 4 with this recipe, just add 2 more breasts.
