Title:
Jamie's Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pork roast (with or without bones)
2 medium onions
2 Granny Smith apples
1 large carrot
1 stalk of celery
3 cloves of garlic - smashed
3 tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 bay leaves
1/4 tsp. of dried thyme
1/4 tsp. poultry seasoning
Pinch of nutmeg
2 tbsp. honey
2 tbsp. apple cider vinegar
1 cup of chicken stock
3 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Cut onion, carrot, apples and celery into 1/2 inch chunks.
In a large bowl, toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, bay leaves, thyme, nutmeg, honey and vinegar.
Season pork with salt and pepper and place in large roasting pan.
Place vegetables around roast.
Roast until golden brown and meat is cooked through.
Cooking time will depend to size of roast.
Remove roast from pan and cover with foil to keep warm.
Remove vegetables and turn through food mill until pureed.
Add more salt and pepper to taste.
Add 1 cup of chicken stock to mixture and mix well.
Place roasting pan on top of stove over medium heat and melt 3 tbsp. of butter.
Add 3 tbsp. of flour to melted butter and stir until light brown and smooth.
Add apple/vegetable mixture and stir until thickened.
Slice pork and pour sauce over pork before serving.
