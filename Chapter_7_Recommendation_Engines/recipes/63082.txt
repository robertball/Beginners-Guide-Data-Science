Title:
Coconut Raisin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup Equal® Spoonful*
6 tablespoons unsalted stick butter or margarine, melted
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup toasted flaked coconut
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup seedless raisins

Directions:
Beat eggs and Equal(r) in medium bowl. Mix in butter and vanilla. Combine flour, baking powder and salt; stir into egg mixture. Mix in coconut, nuts and raisins. Spread batter in lightly sprayed 8-inch square pan.
Bake in preheated 350 degrees F oven 18 to 20 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean and top is golden. Cool completely on wire rack.

Submitter: 
EQUAL®
Description: 
Easy one bowl preparation for these bar cookies that combine flaked coconut, walnuts and raisins in a buttery bar cookie dough that have 41% less calories than the traditional recipe will make them a favorite.
