Title:
Sweet Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2132152.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pork Tenderloin (2 pieces) 2 1/2- 3lb.
1 tsp.Fresh Rosemary, chopped fine
1 tsp Sage
1 tsp Fennel Seeds, chopped fine
2-3 large Cloves Garlic,(to taste),chopped fine
1/4 tsp Black Pepper
1/2 tsp Salt
1 tsp Olive Oil
1 large Sweet Onion cut into wedges
Apricot Preserves 6oz.
1 Cup Blueberry Wine, or any red wine of your choice
1 T. Balsamic Vinegar
Additional Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Mix Spices together in small bowl. Use wooden spoon to meld spices and Garlic together, add Olive Oil. Divide spice mixture between the 2 Tenderloins and rub evenly over all surfaces. Place in a roasting pan or Dutch Oven. Heat in oven for 15 minutes.
Add Onion to Tenderloins, and continue cooking for Approximately 15 more minutes.
Meanwhile heat Apricot Preserves in Microwavable 2 Cup measure, for 1 minute. Add Balsamic Vinegar and Blueberry Wine, mix well. Add to Tenderloins and continue to cook for 20 minutes or until internal temperature is 140 degrees. Remove from oven and let rest on cutting board covered with aluminum foil.
Heat onion and wine mixture in Dutch oven on burner until reduced by half, add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Vanessa C
Description: 
I wanted a different way to cook tenderloins and had a bottle of Maine Blueberry Wine. You can substitute cider or fruit juice, a red or a sweet white wine. Make it your own.
