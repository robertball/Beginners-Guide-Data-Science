Title:
Ham and Broccoli Cheddar Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614175.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cubed fully cooked ham
1 1/2 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2  cup chopped onion
1 1/2 cups of thawed Ore Ida Potatoes
1 Box of thawed Broccoli/Cheddar Green Giant vegetables
1 T. Garlic Powder
Salt and pepper to taste
4 eggs
2 cups milk
1 cup Bisquick

Directions:
Place  mixed ham, cheese, potatoes, broccoli, garlic powder and onion into greased 10 x 10 in. casserole dish. In a bowl, combine eggs, milk, bisquick. Mix until well blended; pour over ham mixture. Bake 400 degrees F for 40-45 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let stand for 5 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Laura
Description: 
A variation of a ham and cheddar pie.  This has broccoli and potatoes for a substantial meal. Great with fresh fruit or grits and a slice of ham.Modified from: Ham 'N' Cheese Pie
