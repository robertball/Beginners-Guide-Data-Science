Title:
Vanilla Chip Maple Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c unsalted butter
2 c brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp maple flavoring
3 c flour
2 tsp baking soda
2 c white chocolate chips
1/2 c chopped pecans
Frosting:
1/4 c softened butter
4 c powdered sugar
1 tsp maple flavoring
4-6 Tbsp milk

Directions:
Cream butter & brown sugar. Add eggs 1 at a time beating well after each addition. Beat in vanilla & maple flavoring. Combine the flour & baking soda & gradually add to creamed mixture. Stir in vanilla chips & pecans. Drop tsp of dough 2 in apart on ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 350 for 8-10 min. Cool 2 min before removing to wire cooling racks.
In a mixing bowl cream butter & powdered sugar together for frosting. Beat in maple flavoring & enough milk to achieve a spreading consistancy. Frost cooled cookies.
