Title:
Harvested Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped onion
2 cups cubed, cooked boneless chicken
2 cups diced tomatoes, with liquid
2 cups sliced carrots
5 cups chicken broth
1 cup sweet corn
1 cup peas
1 can cream of celery

Directions:
In a large soup pot combine the onion, chicken, celery soup, tomatoes with liquid, carrots, broth, corn, peas. Stir together and simmer over medium low heat for 1/2 hour, or until vegetables are cooked and tender.
