Title:
Chocolate Tarts with Peppermint Crème Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages (15 shells) frozen mini phyllo shells
1/3 C plus 3/4 C heavy cream, divided
4 oz. bittersweet or semi-sweet baking chocolate, cut into chunks
2 oz. cream cheese, softened
4 T sugar, divided
1/2 t pure peppermint extract

Directions:
Prepare phyllo shells as directed on package for crisp unfilled tarts. Cool completely.
Microwave 1/3 C of the cream and chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl on high 1 min, stirring half way through.
Stir until chocolate is completely melted. Cool5 min. or until chocolate ganache starts to thicken.
Using a pastry bag, fill phyllo shells with ganache. Cool completely
Beat cream cheese and 2 T sugar in large bowl until smooth.
Beat remaining 3/4 C cream, remaining 4 T sugar, and extract in medium bowl until stiff peaks form.
Add 1/2 of the whipped cream to cream cheese mixture; stir until well blended.
Gently stir in remaining whipped cream.
Using a pastry bag, top each tart with about 1 T Peppermint crème mixture.
Garnish with chocolate shavings. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These are a great holiday treat to bring to a party. They receive rave reviews and there is a lot you can do with them to make them unique every time.
