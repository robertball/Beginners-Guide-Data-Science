Title:
Crockpot Chicken and Sausage Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2179027.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup of all-purpose flour
1/3 cup of vegetable or canola oil
1 package of mild Italian sausage cut in bite-size pieces
4 chicken thighs cut in bite-size pieces
1 yellow, red or green pepper diced
4 cloves of garlic cut in small pieces
1 onion diced
2 celery stalks diced
1 can of diced tomatoes (14oz)
1 jalape√±o cut into small pieces
2 cups of chicken broth
1 teaspoon of oregano
1 teaspoon of pepper
1 teaspoon of paprika
1 teaspoon of garlic powder
1 teaspoon of onion powder

Directions:
Make a roux by mixing together the flour and oil in a sauce pan over medium heat for about 10 minutes stirring regularly until mixture becomes golden brown
Cook sausage in frying pan until crispy
Cook chicken in frying pan until no longer pink inside
Add all of the vegetables, cooked sausage and chicken into the crockpot
Cover the mixture with the roux and spices
Add chicken stock and stir all ingredients together
Cook on low for 6-8 hours
Serve over rice
