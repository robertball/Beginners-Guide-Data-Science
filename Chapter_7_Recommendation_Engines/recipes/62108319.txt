Title:
Carne Con chile Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound of chopped boneless pork chops
3 tbs of veg oil
1/4 cup of rough chopped red onion
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 cup of water
flour slurry-2 tbs of flour +3 tbs of water
for chile-
1 large tomato
3-4 jalapeno chile peppers

Directions:
In a small pot add chile and tomato and cook covered until tender, remove from heat, and add tomato and chiles(stems removed) to blender.  add a couple tbs of cooking water, a pinch of salt.  Blend until slightly chunky.  Set aside.  In a large saucepan, add rinsed chopped pork to pan cover add enough water to cover slightly.  Bring to a boil and 1 tsp of salt.  Let simmer until water is all the way evaporated.
Once water is gone, Add oil and seasonings and red onion and sautee until pork is nicely browned.  add about 1/2 cup of chile or more to taste depending on how hot you want it.  stir together then add the cup of water, bring to a boil.  Make the slurry with the flour and water, making sure there is no lumps, slowly drizzle the slurry into the pork/chile mixture stiring constantly(only add as much slurry as needed to get it thick)discard remaining slurry if any, it will thicken, but should not be runny.  let continue to cook for another 5 mins or so.  check seasonings and add more if needed.  serve with rice beans and tortillas.
