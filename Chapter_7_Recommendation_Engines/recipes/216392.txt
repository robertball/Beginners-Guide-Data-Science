Title:
Sausage and Apple Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976012.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large Golden Delicious apples, peeled, chopped
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
8 eggs
3 cups milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
8 cups French bread cubes (3/4-inch pieces)
1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy DeanÂ® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.  Toss apples with sugar and cinnamon; set aside.
Beat eggs, milk and vanilla in large bowl with wire whisk until well blended.  Add bread cubes, sausage and 1 1/2 cups cheese and apple mixture; stir gently until evenly coated.
Spoon into lightly greased 13x9-inch baking dish; top with remaining 1/2 cup cheese.
Bake 40-45 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.   Let stand 10 minutes before cutting into 12 squares to serve.
Serve with warm maple syrup.

Submitter: 
JimmyDean
Description: 
Sweet, spicy apple chunks, sausage, and Cheddar cheese add loads of flavor to this egg and bread cube casserole that's perfect for brunch.
