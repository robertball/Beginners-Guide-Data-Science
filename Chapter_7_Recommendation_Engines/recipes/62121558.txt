Title:
Fried Chicken Chunks (Chicharrones De Pollo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast.
1/2 cup Bacardi Puerto Rican Dark Rum.
1/2 cup Lemon Juice.
1/2 cup Minced Garlic.
1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce.
3 Tablespoons of Garlic Powder.
1 Teaspoon Goya Adobo.
1 Teaspoon Ground Cumin.
1 Teaspoon Onion Powder.
1 Teaspoon Pepper.
1 Teaspoon Ground Coriander.
2 packets of Goya Sazon with Cilantro & Achiote.
3 Cups of All Purpose Flour.
3-4 Cups of Corn Oil.

Directions:
Prepare at least 4 hours prior to frying to obtain the best flavor.
Cut chicken breast into small chunks and then place in mixing bowl.
Mix all the ingredients into the mixing bowl except for the flour & corn oil.
Let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
Heat the corn oil in a pot that will be deep enough to deep fry.
Coat each chicken chunk in the flour.
Begin frying.
Remove chicken chunks when golden brown.

Submitter: 
Tracie Borrero
Description: 
This is my Puerto Rican version of fried chicken with added flavors.  You'll fall in love with this recipe. You can serve it with Spanish white rice or you can serve it fried plantains (tostones).
