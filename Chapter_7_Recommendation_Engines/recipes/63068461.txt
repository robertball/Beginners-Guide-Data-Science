Title:
Shredded Beef Barbecue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6857252.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 -3 lb chuck roast
1 onion
1 c. celery
3 T. worcestershire sauce
2 T. brown sugar (may add more)
4 T. lemon juice
1 tsp. chili powder
1 c. ketchup
1 1/2 c. water
2T. vinegar
salt & pepper

Directions:
Place roast in roaster.
Pour all of the rest of the ingredients over the top.
Cover with lid.
Bake in 350 degree oven until tender or in crockpot for 8 hours.
Remove and take out fat and shred.
