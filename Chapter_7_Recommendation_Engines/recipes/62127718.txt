Title:
No Guilt Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Roma Tomatoes
2 Avocados
2 Onions
1 Bunch Cilantro
1 Green Pepper
2 Jalapeno Peppers (with or without seeds)
1 Lime

Directions:
Chop first 6 ingredients
Juice the Lime and add salt and pepper to taste.
Let set for at least 2 hours and serve.

Submitter: 
Cheesecakemama
Description: 
This is a dieters friend.  You can eat this with no guilt.  Enjoy with baked chips or celery.
