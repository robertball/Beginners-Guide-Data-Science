Title:
Omelet Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
1 1/2 c frozen shredded hash browns
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/4 lb mushrooms, thinly sliced
1 small green bell pepper, finely chopped
1/2 c sharp shredded Cheddar cheese
4 eggs
2 tablespoons milk
Salt
Ground black pepper
1/4 c grated Parmesan or Romano cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Unroll dough. Press into the bottom and 1/2" up the sides of a greased 13x9 baking dish.
Sprinkle potatoes, onions, mushrooms, peppers, and cheddar cheese evenly over dough.
Beat eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. Pour over veggies. Sprinkle with Parmesan. Bake, uncovered, at 375F for 25-30 minutes or until eggs are completely set.
Let set 5 minutes before cutting.
