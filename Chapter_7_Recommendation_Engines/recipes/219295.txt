Title:
Slow Cooker Tropical Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/769967.jpg
Ingredients:
4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces
4  sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
1  onion, chopped
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks with juice
Â½ cup raisins
2 tablespoons Jamaican jerk seasoning
1 tablespoon grated lime zest
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon dried thyme

Directions:
Place chicken, sweet potatoes, and onion into a slow cooker.
In a bowl, stir together the pineapple chunks with juice, raisins, jerk seasoning, lime zest, Worcestershire sauce, ginger, and cumin until well combined; pour the mixture over the chicken mixture in the cooker. Sprinkle with thyme. Cover the cooker, and set to Low setting.
Cook until the chicken and vegetables are tender, about 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Janet
Description: 
This spicy slow cooker meal will bring you a taste of Jamaica on a chilly winter's night! Serve with rice or buttered crusty rolls.
