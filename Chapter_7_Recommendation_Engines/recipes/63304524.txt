Title:
Hello Dollie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, unsalted
1 cup graham cracker crumbs
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch chips
1 cup coconut flakes
1 cup pecans
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Melt butter and pour into 9x13 pan.  Layer ingredients in order given, pouring condensed milk over all.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  Cool.  Cut into squares.

These are one of my husband's favorites.  They are common but good.
