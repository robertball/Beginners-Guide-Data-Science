Title:
Summer Strawberry Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/772462.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package of cream cheese softened at room temp.
1/2 cup or more of powdered sugar (depending on your taste of desired sweetness.)
strawberries sliced in half ( around a couple cups if desired)

Directions:
whip cream cheese and powdered sugar in bowl.
Dollop the spread on strawberries. (or for a more prettier look, place spread in zip-bloc bag cut a slanted hole and squeeze spread on a half of strawberry. Then add another half on top of that strawberry. Place on dish facing upward :)
