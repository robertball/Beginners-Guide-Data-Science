Title:
Chicken Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Prepared Flour tortillas ( size is up to preference)
1 lb. Queso Chihuahua Cheese
1/4 cup corn oil (divided)
3 large Chicken breasts
1 large Green or Red pepper (sweet)
1 medium onion
1/2 tsp cilantro
1 tbsp paprika
1 tbsp chili powder
1/4 cup of sprite
1/2 tsp mesa flour
Sour cream for garnishing *optional*

Directions:
Prepare chicken by boiling it until just cooked.
Meanwhile, densely chop the onions and peppers.
Using a large heavy frying pan heat 1/8 cup of oil on medium heat.
Add spices and vegetables and simmer for approximately 4 minutes or until level of crispness (or uncrispness is achieved).
Remove from heat and allow to cool.
Shred cooked chicken.
Using pan that has the pepper onion mixture add 1/4 cup of sprite and mesa flour heating on medium until a light sauce forms.
Add shredded chicken and saute until all sprite has cooked away.
Heat remaining 1/8 cup of oil in pan.
Add 1 tortilla shell.
Sprinkle cheese to approximately 1 inch away from edges.
Add chicken.
Fold and fry until tortilla has golden brown bubbles.
Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly on paper towel.
Following the above steps continue until all shells and mixture are used.
Cut with a pizza wheel and serve sour cream on the side.
