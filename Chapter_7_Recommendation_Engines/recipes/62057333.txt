Title:
C Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole red peppers
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 box of stuffing mix
1 cup breadcrumbs
2 tbsp butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup water

Directions:
Prepare stuffing according to directions on box, set aside. Melt butter and add breadcrumbs,mix well.Cut top off washed peppers,cut in half. Clean inside of all seeds.Stuff peppers with stuffing mix and place in cassarole dish. Top with chicken breast.Place breadcrumb mixture on breast and lightly pat down.Mix  soup and water together and pour over peppers and chicken. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes taking cover off last 15 minutes of cooking time to brown crumb mixture.Serve with white rice or mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Party Chef
Description: 
Red peppers filled with a boxed stuffing mix topped with a boneless chicken breast and breadcrumb topping.Cream of chicken soup is poured into cassarole dish and baked.
