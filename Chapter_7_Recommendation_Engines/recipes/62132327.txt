Title:
Gumbo with 3 Meats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, boiled and deboned (keep the broth)
1/2 lb Polish Sausage, cut and quartered
1 can chopped oysters with liquid
1 lb frozen okra
3 Tablespoons oil
2 green peppers chopped
1 large onion chopped
1 1lb can tomatoes, chopped undrained
1 teaspoon thyme
2 Bay leaves
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 c Chicken broth
3 c  Water
Steamed Rice

Directions:
In Dutch oven brown sausage and set aside. Add oil to sausage fat, and saute garlic, onions and peppers until soft. Add tomatoes,okra and spices. Simmer 15 minutes. Add broth, and water. Simmer 1 hour. Stir in sausage, undrained oysters and chicken. Simmer 5-8 minutes longer. Remove Bay leaves and serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Julia
Description: 
Cooking the chicken ahead is key to making this dish. After that it's just putting it togeather and letting it simmer awhile.It makes a lot, but doesn't last long around our house!
