Title:
Lemon-Ginger Frozen Yogurt with Blueberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups plain yogurt
1 1/4 cups simple syrup (bring 1 cup water and 1 cup sugar to a boil; then let it cool before using.)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/8 Cup Crystallized Ginger, sliced
1 pound frozen blueberries
3 strips lemon peel
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest

Directions:
For Yogurt: In a bowl, whisk together the yogurt, simple syrup, lemon juice and vanilla. Chill 30 minutes
Freeze in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions.
Fold in ginger
Place in container and chill for at least 4 hours
For Sauce: Combine blueberries, lemon peel, sugar, lemon juice, and water in saucepan.
Bring mixture to boil, then let simmer for approximately 4 minutes.
Strain mixture, and then return sauce to pan.
In a seperate container, add together water and cornstartch.
Add mixture to sauce, stirring till mixture is boiling
Boil for 1 minute
Remove from heat, add lemon zest
Cool till room temperature, then serve over yogurt.

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
This meal is refreshing with the fresh lemon in the yogurt and sauce. The ginger adds a sweet kick, and the blueberry brings it all together. Fantastic for a summer desert!
