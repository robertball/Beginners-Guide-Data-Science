Title:
Cinco de Mayo Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1112751.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can refried beans
2 cans evaporated milk
6 eggs
1 can corn
8oz blue cheese, crumbled
2 avocados, chopped
1lb ground beef
1c lentils
1c shredded cheddar
1c rice
8 - 1/2c dollops of mayonnaise
8 slices bacon, cut into strips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350ยบ.  Grease a 9 x 13 casserole dish.
Spread the refried beans in the bottom of the casserole dish.  Layer with corn, blue cheese, avocados, ground beef and lentils.
In a large bowl, mix the evaporated milk and eggs.  Pour over the layered ingredients.
Layer on the cheddar and rice.
Bake for 30 minutes, then dollop the mayo on top and add the bacon strips and bake for 10 min.
