Title:
Blueberry Oat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup all purpose flour
1 cup instant rolled oats
11/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 teaspoon all spice
1 cup sugar
1 tbsp Orange peel
1/2 cup applesauce
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup butter melted
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
11/2 cups fresh/frozen blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Spray 2 9X5 loaf pans with cooking spray / liners.
Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl.
Use mixer with flat beater.
Stir applesauce, yogurt, egg, orange peel and vanilla extract together in a separate bowl.
Stir flour mixture into applesauce mixture until just combined.
Fold blueberries into batter.
Fill prepared pans 2/3 full with batter.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of bread comes out clean, about 55 to 65 minutes.
Cool in pan 10 minutes.
Remove from pan and cool on wire rack.
