Title:
Curried Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cubed beef
1 Tbl olive oil
1/2 C chopped onion
1-3 Tbl curry powder (per your taste)
2 bay leaves
2 cloves minced garlic
1/2 tsp each black pepper and  cayenne pepper
1 1/2 C low sodium beef broth
2 Tbl corn starch mixed in 1/4 C water
1 1/2 cups bagged coleslaw vegetables
1/2 C fresh broccoli florets
1/4 C sour cream
1/2 C chopped green onion
1 1/2 C cooked basmati rice

Directions:
In a large pan, heat your oil and brown meat (About 7 minutes).
Add all ingredients except broccoli, sour cream and green onion. Stir until well mixed. Cook covered, over medium-low heat for 1hr 45 min (add water when needed).
Add broccoli, simmer for an additional 15-20 min.
Remove bay leaves. Add sour cream and onion. Stir to combine. Remove bay leaves and serve over rice.
