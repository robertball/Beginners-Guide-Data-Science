Title:
Big Ray's Honey Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594182.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup honey
3 Tbsp spicy mustard
1 Tbsp lemon juice
3 small zucchini, cut into strips
3 small yellow squash, cut into strips
2 Tbsp olive oil
3 bell peppers, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4  tsp red pepper flakes
salt & pepper to taste
1 1/2 lbs shrimp, peeled, deveined and tails removed
chpped parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Combine broth, honey,  mustard and lemon juice.   Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.  Add peppers and cook until edges are slightly blackened.  Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cook about 30 seconds.
Add shrimp, salt and pepper.  Cook about 1 minute.
Add honey mixture and squash.   Cook 2-3 minutes until veggies have cooked down and shrimp is opaque.  Top with parsley and serve.
