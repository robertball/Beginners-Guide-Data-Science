Title:
Pasta Primavera with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cut into strips and cooked
2 Tbls extra virgin olive oil
4 Cloves of fresh garlic, crushed
4 Tbls butter
4 Tbls flour
2 cups milk
4 oz. cream cheese
1 cup grated parmesean cheese
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. parsley
salt and pepper to taste
16 oz. bag frozen california blend vegetables
1 small zucchini, chunked
8 oz. pkg bow-tie pasta

Directions:
1)cut 2 chicken breasts into strips and stir fry in 2 Tbls. EVOO until no longer pink; set aside.
2) melt butter in large skillet on medium heat, stir in crushed garlic, then add flour; mix well. Slowly add milk and continue stirring until creamy and sauce begins to thicken. Add cream cheese and stir until melted.  Stir in parmesean cheese. Turn heat to low. Add seasonings and cooked chicken, stirring occasionally.
3)heat enough water to boil pasta. After water boils, add pasta and stir. Cook pasta for 8 minutes or until done.
4) while waiting for water to boil, cut the zucchini into bite sized chunks and add to white sauce. Then defrost vegetable blend in microwave and add to white sauce.
5) drain water from pasta and serve with sauce. Top with more parmesean cheese if desired.
Garlic bread is a terrific side for this dish.

Submitter: 
Jenny Brannon Elder
Description: 
I started with a simple white sauce and ended up with this. My family and I loved it! Hope yours will too!
