Title:
Slow-Cooker Brat and Sauerkraut Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. uncooked brats, cut into 1/2 inch thich slices
1 lb. tiny new red potatoes, quartered
2 small oniions, coarsly chopped
2 cloves garlic
3 Bay leaves
1 14.5 oz. can sauerkraut
2 14.5 oz cans beef broth
1 TBSP spicy brown mustard
1 TBSP cider vinegar
1 tsp paprika
Sour Cream (optional)

Directions:
Cook brats, until browned. In a slow cooker combine, potatoes, onions, garlic and bay leaves. Top with browned brats and sauerdraut. In a large bowl, whisk together broth, mustard, vinegar, paprika. Pour into cooker. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours or on high for 3 to 3.5 hours. Discard the bay leaves. If desired top each serving with sour cream.
