Title:
Penne with Lemon, Asparagus and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices thick cut bacon or pancetta
3 cloves garlic, sliced
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2 tsp. dried thyme
zest of 1 lemon
3/4 lb. penne pasta
12 oz. asparagus cut into 1" pieces
1/2 cup part skim ricotta cheese
2 oz. feta cheese crumbled

Directions:
Put bacon and garlic in small pan and cook until garlic is golden.  Remove from heat and add lemon juice, thyme and zest.  Cook pasta and four minutes before it's done, add asparagus.  Remove 3/4 cup of pasta water, then drain pasta.  Add bacon mixture along with ricotta and feta.  Toss until thoroughly coated.
