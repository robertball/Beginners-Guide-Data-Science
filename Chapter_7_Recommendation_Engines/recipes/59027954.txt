Title:
Traditional Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
3/4 cup warm milk
1/4 cup butter or margarine
3/4 teaspoon salt
Dash pepper
clove garlic
galic powder

Directions:
Place potatoes in a saucepan and cover with water, 1/4 cup of the milk, and uncrushed garlic clove. Cover and bring to a boil; cook for 20-25 minutes or until very tender. Drain well. Add milk, butter,garlic powder, salt and pepper; mash until light and fluffy.

Submitter: 
Tamara Allison
Description: 
Mashed potatoes make a wonderful accompaniment to most any meal, so keep this recipe handy.Modified from: Traditional Mashed Potatoes
