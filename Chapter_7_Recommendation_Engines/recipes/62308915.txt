Title:
Old Fashioned Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, peeled and quartered
2 large eggs
1 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon celery salt
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
ground black pepper to taste
1 cup mayonnaise, plus more if needed
CHILL IN REFRIGERATOR OVERNIGHT

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Rinse under cold water, drain, cool, and chop.
Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
Bring water to a boil; cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, relish, garlic salt, celery salt, mustard, pepper and mayonnaise.
Mix together well and chill in refrigerator overnight.
