Title:
Jen's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. grounds beef
1 onion, diced
1 TBSP minced garlic
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 sleeve saltine crackers, crushed
1 bell pepper, chopped
3 TBSP Worcheshire sauce
1 cup ketchup
Salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Mix all of above ingredients until incorporated.
2. Pat down into a 9 by 13 inch pan.
3. Bake at 350 in a preheated oven for 45 min or until there is no more pink in the meat.

Submitter: 
jenmannaz
Description: 
I grew up with this easy and untraditional meatloaf in my home and now its my husband's favorite.  I haven't tasted any that tops it.
