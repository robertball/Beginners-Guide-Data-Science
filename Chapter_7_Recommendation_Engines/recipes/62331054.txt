Title:
Texas Two Step Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of coleslaw mix
1 can Mexicorn, drained
1/2 of medium chopped red onion
minced fresh cilantro (1/4 to 1/2 cup)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped (optional)
Dressing:
Ranch salad dressing
1to 2 teaspoons lime juice
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
Combine all ingredients then pour dressing over the coleslaw mix and toss to coat just before serving.  Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
SoccerMom
Description: 
This is a yummy twist on traditional cole slaw.  I crave it all the time! You can also add other fresh veggies to it to, depending on what you have on hand. Be careful and use gloves when handling the  jalapeno pepper!
