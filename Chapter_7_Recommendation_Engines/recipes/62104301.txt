Title:
Italian Calamari Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C olive oil
1=2 garlic cloves, minced
1 hot dried red chili pepper,chopped
1 medium onion, diced
2 tsp. oregano, crushed between fingers
Salt and pepper
1 tbsp.  dry Sherry wine
1 can (28 oz.)California Roma Tomatoes in Puree, diced or chopped
1 lb. Calamari, cleaned and sliced in 1/2 inch ringlets
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Saute the onion and garlic in the oil for 5-10 minutes. Add the hot chili pepper, chili pepper and crushed the oregano between fingers into the pot. Stir in the dry Sherry and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes over very low heat. Add salt and fresh ground pepper.
Chop or dice tomatoes, if necessary, and add with the puree. Half can of water can be added if necessary during cooking. Simmer for 30 minutes, uncovered. Stir frequently and add water if sauce becomes too thick.
Drop the Calamari into the simmering sauce and continue to simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the Calamari is opaque.
Serve over cooked linguini or spaghitti with Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A tradition on Christmas Eve for Italian families, this recipe is good all year long.
