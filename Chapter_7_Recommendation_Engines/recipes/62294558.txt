Title:
Blackened Chicken with Avocado Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/360362.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (1 1/4 to 1 1/2 lb. toat)
2 tsp. blackened steak seasoning
1 Tbsp. olive oil
2 Tbsp. rice vinegar
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/4  tsp. ground cumin
1/8 tsp. salt
Dash ground black pepper
1 avocado, halved, seeded, peeled & chopped
2/3 cup chopped fresh or refrigerated papaya
1/3 cup chopped red sweet pepper
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
Fresh cilantro sprigs

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 375 F. Lightly sprinkle both sides of chicken with blackened seasoning. In a large ovenproof skillet heat the 1 Tbsp. of oil over medium heat. Add chicken, cook until browned, turning once. Bake about 15 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
2. Meanwhile for salsa, in a large bowl whisk rice vinegar, the 2 Tbsp. oil, cumin, salt and black pepper. Stir in avocado, papaya, sweet pepper and chopped cilantro. Serve with chicken, and garnish with cilantro. (optional)
