Title:
Bone Broth - Dr. Axe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2550133.jpg
Ingredients:
chicken bones (crack the leg bones to help release marrow)
chicken feet, backs, necks (they are higher in collagen)
1 large onion
a few carrots
a little celery
garlic
3 T. apple cider vinegar
2 T. sea salt
a few peppercorns
enough water to cover the bones well

Directions:
Bring to a boil.
Cover and reduce heat.
Simmer about 24 hours.
Strain.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
You can make this broth in a crock pot or on the stove.
