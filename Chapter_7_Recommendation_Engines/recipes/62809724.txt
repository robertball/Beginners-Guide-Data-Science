Title:
Cucumber Sandwiches I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/660596.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package garlic cream cheese
Shrimp in water
1 package cocktail rye bread
1 cucumber, thinly sliced
Fresh Dill

Directions:
Spread a thick layer of cream cheese on each slice of the cocktail rye bread. Top each with a slice of cucumber. Then put one shrimp on top and take tiny dill pieces & lay them over the shrimp.

Submitter:
