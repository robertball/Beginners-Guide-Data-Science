Title:
Swedish Pickled Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers
1/2 cup white vinegar
2 T water
1/4 tsp salt
3 T sugar
2 T minced parsley (optional)

Directions:
Wash and dry cucumbers. Slice very thinly and place in a bowl.
Mix together vinegar, water, salt, and sugar. Pour over cucumbers.
Sprinkle with parsley, if using. Let stand refrigerated overnight or at least 3 hours.
