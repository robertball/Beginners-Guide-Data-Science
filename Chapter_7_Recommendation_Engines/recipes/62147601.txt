Title:
Sunny Gingered Honey Lemon Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon water
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 tsp. lemon extract
2 oz. fresh gingerroot, peeled and thinly sliced
1 cinnamon stick
1 tsp. whole cloves
4 herbal tea bags
1/2 cup honey

Directions:
In a large Dutch oven, combine water, lemon juice, lemon extract, gingerroot, cinnamon stick, and cloves. Bring to a boil. Remove from heat. Add tea bags; let steep for 30 minutes. Remove tea bags. Strain; add honey. Chill until cold; serve over ice cubes and garnish with mint leaves if desired.

Submitter: 
larkspur
Description: 
This yummy beverage gets its bright, sunny flavor from ginger, honey, cinnamon, and lemon. It is a wonderful thirst-quencher on hot summer days!
