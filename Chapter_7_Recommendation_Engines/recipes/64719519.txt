Title:
Potage Jaqueline Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5874671.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T Butter
2 C Chopped Onions
2 Chopped Celery Stalks
1 t Grated Ginger Root
4 Large Sweet Potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
4 Cups Water
1 Bay Leaf
1/2 t Salt
1 1/2 C Half and Half
Black Pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt the butter in a heavy sauce pan and saut√© the onions until they are translucent stirring occasionally.  Add celery and ginger and continue to cook until onions start to brown.  Add the sweet potatoes, water, bay leaf, salt and pepper and simmer 15-20 minutes until the sweet potatoes are tender.
Remove and discard the bay leaf.  Using a immersion blender puree the soup mixture with half and half.  Taste to adjust for salt and pepper and reheat gently.
