Title:
Healthy Beet Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1116189.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium-sized beets (3 small)
1 19 oz can of chickpeas
Juice of 1 1/2 lemons
5 cloves of garlic
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup water

Directions:
In a medium pot, cover beets with water and boil until soft (approx 40 minutes).
Once cooked, run beets under cold water and peel off skins. Cut into quarters and let cool.
In a food processor add all ingredients and blend until smooth. Taste and add more salt if needed.
Serve with vegetable crudities, rice crackers or fresh bread.

Description: 
Super healthy lemony hummus.  Also very good for the liver.
