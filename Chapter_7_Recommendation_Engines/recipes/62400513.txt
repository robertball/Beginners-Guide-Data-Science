Title:
California Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 10 oz. packages frozen Normandy mix vegetables (carrots, cauliflower, broccoli); cooked & drained
1/4 pound Velveeta cheese
1/2 cup butter
1 tube (1 1/2 cup) crushed Ritz Crackers

Directions:
Stir cheese into Normandy mix vegetables, cooked & drained.  While hot, put mixture into casserole dish.
Melt butter in pan and stir in crushed Ritz crackers.
Put cracker mixture on top of vegetables.
Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
