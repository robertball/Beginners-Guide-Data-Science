Title:
Flavourful Creamy Chicken Bale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7-8 chicken drumsticks (or breasts, whichever you prefer)
1 can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 cup water
1 cube chicken bouillon
3 sprigs of green onion (chopped)
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 parsnip (diced)
1 tsp. freeze dried chives
1 tsp. parsley
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. rosemary leaves (optional)
1/2 tsp. basil leaves

Directions:
In a saucepan mix 1 cube chicken bouillon with 1 cup water.
Add the cream of mushroom soup into the mixture along with the green onions.
In a small bowl mix all herbs and spices, then add to saucepan.
Add diced parsnip and mushrooms.
Pour over chicken drumsticks (or breasts), and bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes. Or until desired.

Submitter: 
Maisha Dewan
Description: 
A VERY flavorful creamy chicken bake, with parsnips, mushrooms and green onions. Great over rice or linguine.
