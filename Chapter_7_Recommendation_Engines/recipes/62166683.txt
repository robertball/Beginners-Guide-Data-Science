Title:
Ground Cherry Freezer Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups ground cherries
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
zest of one lemon (or to taste)
1 3oz. pkg of lemon jello

Directions:
Place ground cherries in food processor and pulse till coarsely chopped then put in 2 qt. saucepan.  Stir in sugar, lemon juice and lemon zest.  Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally and boil for 15 minutes.  Add jello stirring till dissolved.  Skim off any foam and pour into two 8 ounce freezer containers.  This recipe is easily doubled.
