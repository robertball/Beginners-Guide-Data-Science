Title:
Best Sauteed Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024541.jpg
Ingredients:
5 slices bacon (not microwave type)
1/4 cup butter (1/2 stick)
1 pound fresh or frozen whole green beans
1/4 tsp salt
sesame seeds to taste

Directions:
Place bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook until browned on one side, turn and cook on the other side.
Remove bacon to paper-towel lined plate, leaving the bacon drippings in skillet. Add butter to drippings. When butter is melted, add green beans, salt, sesame seeds to skillet then (still at medium-high), cover and cook until tender, about 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Crumble bacon, and return to skillet. Stir, just to blend and remove with slotted draining spoon to serving dish.
