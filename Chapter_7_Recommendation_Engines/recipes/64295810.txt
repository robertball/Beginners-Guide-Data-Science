Title:
Rose's Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Elbow Macaroni, cooked, drained, cold
1 large can Albacore Tuna, drained
1 c Miracle Whip
1 c Lettuce, shredded
1 c Green Olives, sliced

Directions:
1. Cook macaroni per container directions.  Drain and place in fridge to chill.
2. Combine miracle whip, shredded lettuce, tuna and olives to desired consistency.  May use more or less of any ingredient based on personal taste.
3. Add chilled macaroni to the mixture.  Place back in fridge for 1-2 hours.
4. Add any additional miracle whip needed due to pasta drying out.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
My mother-in-law's tuna salad.  This is the only way to get my husband to eat fish.
