Title:
Ranch Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
1 egg
1 onion, chopped

Directions:
Preheat frying pan. Blend all ingredients in a bowl, but do not over work the meat or it could end up tougher that desired. Shape in to four large meat patties. Cook a minimum of five minutes per side

Submitter: 
Cory Price
Description: 
These burgers have a very unique taste and stay very juicy on the stove top.Modified from: Ranch Burgers
