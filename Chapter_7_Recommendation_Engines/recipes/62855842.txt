Title:
Split Pea Soup with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of split peas
1/2 lb of 1 inch cubed ham
1/2 of a red onion minced
2 cloves of garlic minced
4 cups of reduced sodium chicken broth
2 cups of water
1 tablespoon of brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
Salt to taste

Directions:
Soak peas in 6 cups of water over night then rinse and drain.
Add 4 cups of chicken broth and 2 cups of water to a large pot over high heat.
Then add ham, red onion, garlic, brown sugar, pepper, salt and peas.
Bring to a boil then reduce to simmer until peas are soft(about 2 hrs).

Submitter: 
spinn710
Description: 
A thick and creamy pea soup that is quick and easy to put together. Peas soak overnight and ham can be purchased pre-cubed at the store.
