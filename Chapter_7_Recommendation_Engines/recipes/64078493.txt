Title:
Real Food Orange Cream Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1145674.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice (about 3 medium oranges)
1 cup canned coconut milk
3 Tbsp raw honey
1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a large mixing cup or a bowl with a spout.
Whisk together until well combined.
Immediately pour into ungreased popsicle molds or small paper cups. Then, add sticks.
Freeze overnight.
To remove from mold, run under warm water for about 15 seconds. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
Grocery store popsicles are full of sugar, corn syrup, and artificial colors and flavors. Treat your kiddos to a delicious homemade version without all the junk.
