Title:
Old Fashioned Tea Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bread, crusts removed
1/4 c. butter
1/4 c. ground nuts, your choice
1/4 c. brown sugar
1 tbsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Melt butter, add sugar, nuts and cinnamon, spread on bread, cut in thin strips and put under broiler, toast until browned.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
These lovely tea cookies are still as tasty as they were 50 years ago. Make them your own.
