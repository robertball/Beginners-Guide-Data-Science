Title:
Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
1 lime, juiced
1 teaspoon salt
1 tsp onion powder
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1 garlic paste
1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion powder, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor.

Submitter: 
Toni
Description: 
Cilantro and cayenne give this tasty guacamole a kick. Makes four servings.Modified from: Guacamole
