Title:
Brussel Sprout Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. Brussel sprouts, quartered
1 c. sliced carrots
1 c. sliced celery
1 c. sliced chicken sausage
1/2 c. sliced rotisserie chicken
1 medium onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 1/2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 bay leaf
2 T crushed red pepper
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the vegetables, chicken sausage, chicken and bay leaf into the basin of the crock pot.
Pour broth over the mixture and add crushed red pepper, salt and pepper.
Set crock pot on medium and cook for 3 1/2 hours, stirring every hour.
Reduce heat to low and cook an additional hour, or until all vegetables are soft.
