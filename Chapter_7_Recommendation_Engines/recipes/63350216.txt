Title:
Bearnaise Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. white wine
2 tsp. chopped shallot
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. tarragon vinegar
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 tsp. dried tarragon
3 egg yolks
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup butter
1/4 tsp. Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Combine tarragon, vinegar and wine in a pan and cook until almost all liquid is evaporated.
Heat butter until hot.
Put egg yolks, lemon juice, Tabasco and salt in a blender.
Add tarragon mixture, then SLOWLY stream in butter while blender is on low.
Add a few drops of hot water if mixture is too thick.
Serve immediately.
