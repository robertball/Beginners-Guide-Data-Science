Title:
Ranch dressing for wedge salad with blue cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce, cut into 8 wedges
4 slices bacon, thick cut
4 eggs
8 T crumbled blue cheese
Dressing:
1 c buttermilk
1/2 c mayonnaise
1/2 t Worcestershire sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
2 T fresh chives, minced
2 T fress dill, minced
2 T shallot, finely chopped
1/2 t salt
1 t pepper

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp. Drain.
Put eggs and 6 c water into a medium saucepan. Bring to a bare simmer (180-190F) over high heat. Reduce to lowest heat, cover, and cook for 10 min. Drain eggs into colander and run cold water over to cool. Peel. Dry. Slice in half lengthwise.
Whisk together all dressing ingredients. Adjust salt.
Place 2 wedges, 2 egg halves, and 1 slice bacon on each of 4 plates. Drizzle with about 1/4 c dressing and 2 T blue cheese.
