Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/4 tsp salt
1 dash ground black pepper
1 cup plain bread crumbs
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
3 tablespoons yellow mustard, divided
1/2 cup ketchup, divided
3 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, thoroughly mix ground beef, onion, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Mix in bread crumbs, milk, egg, 2 tablespoons mustard and 1/4 cup ketchup.
Press the meat mixture into a 9x5 inch loaf pan. In a small bowl, stir together remaining mustard, ketchup and brown sugar; pour on top of the meat loaf.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kemberli Paes
Description: 
This easy meatloaf is flavored with garlic powder, mustard, ketchup and onion, then topped with a brown sugar, ketchup and mustard mixture.Modified from: Mary's Meatloaf
