Title:
Asian Rice Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
2 tablespoons chili paste with garlic
1 teaspoon dried mint
1/4 cup lime juice
1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
1/3 cup seasoned rice wine vinegar
1 tablespoon sesame oil
8 ounces rice noodles
1 pound lean ground pork
1 large red onion, cut in quarters then very thinly sliced
1 cup scallions, thinly sliced
1 cup snow peas, thinly sliced
1 cup chopped cilantro
1/4 cup roasted peanuts

Directions:
In a jar with a tight fitting lid, combine ginger, chili paste, mint, lime juice, soy sauce, rice wine vinegar and sesame oil.
Shake to blend and let stand.
Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
Add noodles and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
Drain, cool and place in a serving bowl.
With kitchen shears, cut noodles up a bit in bowl.
Cook ground pork in a non-stick skillet until just done.
Drain and cool on paper towels and add to bowl.
Add onion, scallion, snow peas and cilantro.
Place peanuts in a heavy zipper bag and lightly crush with the bottom of the jar or a large glass before adding to bowl.
Pour sauce over everything and toss well.
Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Lally
Description: 
A refreshing Asian-style cold salad with rice noodles, ground pork, cilantro and veggies. Add crushed red pepper flakes to the dressing for extra heat.
