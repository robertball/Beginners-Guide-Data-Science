Title:
Dad's Home With the Kids Slow-cooker Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lb beef roast
1 cup baby carrots
1 cup favorite dried pasta
1/2 cup Soya Sauce
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup lemon juice
5 tblsp of Wocestershire sauce
2 tsp minced garlic
2 tblsp garlic powder (roasted garlic powder preferred)
2 tblsp dried basil flakes
2 tblsp dried parsley flakes
1 tblsp fine ground black pepper
1 can beef broth

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except carrots, roast and pasta in a blender. Blend for 20 seconds until fine.
Brown all sides of the roast for a few seconds in a large pan, and place in slow cooker.
Pour blended mixture over roast.
Cook on medium for 5-6 hours.
Add carrots cook for 10 minutes more.
Add pasta.
Allow to simmer for 20 minutes, or until pastya has reached desired firmness, slice beef and serve.

Submitter: 
Kookie
Description: 
Garlicky-saucy slow cooker recipe that has 3 of 4 food groups, just add a glass of milk, and mom will be happy!
