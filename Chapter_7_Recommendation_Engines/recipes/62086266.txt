Title:
spicy string-beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups string beans
2 purple onions
1/2 cup soy sauce
olive oil
garlic powder
ginger powder
onion powder
red pepper flakes
salt
black pepper

Directions:
Cut the onions into small peices. Saute' them in the oil in a medium sized pot for 3-5 minutes. Add the string beans  and saute' for about 2 minutes. Sprinkle on the ginger, garlic, onion powder, salt,black pepper and hot pepper flakes to taste. Saute' for about 15 minutes covered. Searve hot.

Submitter: 
Ro Oranim
Description: 
this recipe is a great way to turn greenbeans into something interesting.
