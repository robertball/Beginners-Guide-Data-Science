Title:
Italian Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head garlic, peeled and minced
3 T olive oil
1 tsp salt
italian seasoning blend, 1 tsp or to taste

Directions:
Mince garlic, then toss in a bowl with olive oil, salt, and seasoning (if desired).  Spread on cookie sheet and broil  at 400 degrees 5-10 min, or until darkened and crunchy, shaking/turning often to prevent burning.  Allow to cool and store at room temp in a tightly closed container.

Description: 
This is a reasonable fascimile of the crunchies found in italian olive oil for bread...place these in the cruette dish before drizzling with oil.
