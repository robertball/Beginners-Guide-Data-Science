Title:
Curry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cooked chicken breasts cooked and cubed
1/4 cup raisins (optional)
1/4 cup green or red peppers diced
1 cup miracle whip
3 tablespoons curry powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Just combine all the ingredients together until all is covered. You may have to add more miracle whip or seasoning of course!

Submitter: 
usmcwifey
Description: 
This salad tastes better the next day, as does everything else. Amazing on a croissant or crackers. Might want to add more mayo depending on how dry/moist you like your salad!
