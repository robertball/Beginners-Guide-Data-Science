Title:
homemade pumpkin pie with fresh pumpkin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pie (sugar) pumpkin 6-8 inch diameter will use 3C of goop
1C sugar or substitute
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground cloves
1 tsp ground allspice
1/2 tsp ground ginger
or substitute cinnamon, cloves, allspice and ginger with 3tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/2 tsp salt (optional)
4 large eggs
1 1/2  12oz can evaporated milk
1/2tsp vanilla extract  (optional)
2 pie crusts your choice

Directions:
Pumpkin goop
Preheat oven to 350°
Clean outside of the pumpkin
cut pumpkin in half and clean out seeds.
Place pumpkin cut side down in baking dish with cover.
bake 45-90 minutes or till soft
Scoop out pumpkin and put into blender and Puree
once smooth measure out 3 cups you'll need for this recipe. The rest can be frozen for later.
for pie
preheat oven to 425°
mix 3 cups of pumpkin goop with rest of ingredients.
pour mixture into 2 pie crust till about 1/2in from top
place in oven and bake for 15 minutes
turn oven down to 350° and cook 45-60 minutes till fork comes out clean.
