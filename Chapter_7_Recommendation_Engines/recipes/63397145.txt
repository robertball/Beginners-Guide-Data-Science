Title:
Herb Skillet Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/913595.jpg
Ingredients:
7 frozen dinner rolls
1/4 t. basil
1/4 t. pepper
1/4 t. oregano
1/4 t. rosemary
1/4 t. garlic powder
3 T. butter, melted

Directions:
Place the dinner rolls in a greased 9" cake pan or pie tin.
Set in a warm place to rise. When doubled in size, brush with melted butter and sprinkle on the seasonings.
Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes, or until golden brown

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
While I left the original recipe intact, I usually prepare a white bread dough and use this in place of the frozen rolls.
