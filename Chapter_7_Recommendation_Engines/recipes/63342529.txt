Title:
Linguine and Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cans chopped or minced clams
4 chopped onions
15 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup olive oil
4 tbsp. butter
2 tsp. oregano
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. onion salt
3 tbsp. fresh flat leaf parsley
4 tbsp. lemon juice
4 tsp. lemon zest
1/2 cup Parmesan
1 lb. cooked linguine

Directions:
Drain clams but save juice.
In a large pan, add olive oil and butter, then saute onions and garlic until tender.
Add clam juice, oregano, salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion salt, then bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add clams, parsley, lemon juice and zest.
Continue heating on low, stirring occasionally.
Add cooked linguine to sauce and add Parmesan.
Mix well and serve.
