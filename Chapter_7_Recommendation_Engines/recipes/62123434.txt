Title:
rice cooker oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  cup quick oats
1  cup water
1  cup milk
1  pinch salt
1  tablespoon sugar
2  tablespoons honey
1  teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients in the rice cooker.
Stir the mixture.
Turn the rice cooker on.
Wait for around 10-15 minutes.
Serve warm :).
