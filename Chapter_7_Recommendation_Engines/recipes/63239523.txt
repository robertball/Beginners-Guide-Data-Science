Title:
FAT GRANNYS CLASSIC SALISBURY STEAK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb ground chuck
3/4 lb ground sirloin
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 small onion minced
1/2 tbl minced garlic
1 egg
1/2 tsp seasoning salt
1/2 tsp coarse ground pepper
1/2 tsp celery seed
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1lb fresh mushrooms,sliced
1 large onion, sliced
2 tbl olive oil
1 stick of butter
1/2 tbl course ground pepper
2 tbl au jus gravy mix
3(15oz) jars beef gravy(heinz)
1/4 cup water

Directions:
In a large bowl,combine the ground meat,egg, minced garlic and onions,worcestershire sauce,seasonings, salt and pepper.
mix well and form into 6 oval shaped patties.
In a large skillet,on medium high heat.add 1/2 of the oil.when oil is hot add 3 of the steaks,cook for 3-5 min on each side.remove and put on a platter.add the remaining oil.when the oil is hot add the last 3 steaks,cook for 3-5 min on each side and  put with other steaks, cover with foil tent and put in a 200 degree oven to keep warm.
while the steaks are cooking,in a medium sauce pan,on medium heat add the 3 jars of beef gravy, au jus gravy mix,water and 1/2 tbl coarse ground pepper. .stir with a small wire wisk for 3-4 min.lower heat to low.
If gravy is to thick thin with a little bit of water.
In a small saute pan,on medium high heat.add the 2 tbls olive oil and the butter until hot,add the mushrooms and onions, saute for 3-4 min.stirring constantly,until all of the liquid is absorbed into the mushrooms. remove from heat.
to serve: place steaks on a serving platter,pour gravy over steaks(save 2 cups of gravy to serve in a gravy boat).top with the sauted onions and mushrooms.
