Title:
Shredded Brussels Sprouts with Dried Apricots and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups of cleaned, cored and thinly sliced Brussels Sprouts
1/4 olive oil
3/4 cups crisp, diced cooked bacon
3/4 cups diced dried apricots

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet and add brussels sprouts and simmer, covered for 5 minutes. Add apricots and simmer until sprouts are cooked but still firm and bright green. Just before serving add bacon bits.
