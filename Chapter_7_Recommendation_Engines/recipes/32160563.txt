Title:
Citrus Cilantro Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
4 cups instant rice
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup Smucker's® Orange Sugar Free Marmalade
OR Smucker's® Sweet Orange Marmalade
•	2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro or parsley

Directions:
BRING water to boil in a 3-quart saucepan.
Add rice, cumin and salt.
Let stand 5 minutes or until water is absorbed.
Add marmalade and cilantro.
Combine ingredients with fork.
