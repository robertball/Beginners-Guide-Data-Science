Title:
Kellogg's Rice Krispies Treats Peter Cottontail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986188.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package miniature marshmallows
6 cups KELLOGG'S速 RICE KRISPIES速 Cereal
Coconut
Miniature marshmallows

Directions:
In a large microwave-safe bowl, heat margarine and marshmallows at HIGH for 3 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes.
Stir in KELLOGG S RICE KRISPIES cereal until well coated. Using spatula sprayed with cooking spray or waxed paper, press mixture into a 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1-inch pan coated with cooking spray.
To make 7-inch high bunny, shape 3 1/2 cups of mixture for body and 2 cups of mixture for head. Join head and body with toothpicks to create a bunny. Shape remaining mixture into a ball for the  cottontail;  roll in coconut and attach to the body with a toothpick. Make ears with colored paper. Cut two slits in head to attach ears. Create eyes, nose and buttons with miniature marshmallows and gumdrop halves. Use paper for whiskers.

Description: 
Try this fun project that you can eat! Kellogg's Rice Krispies and marshmallows are formed into a large bunny with a fluffy coconut cotton tail. It makes a lovely centerpiece for a children's Easter party!
