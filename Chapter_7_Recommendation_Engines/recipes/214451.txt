Title:
Skillet White Fish with Feta, Green Onion and Basil Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989135.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large Roma or beefsteak tomatoes
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
1 tablespoon bread crumbs or panko
1 tablespoon Bertolli® Classico™ Olive Oil, plus
1/4 cup Bertolli® Classico™ Olive Oil
2 cups arugula or baby spinach leaves
20 large fresh basil leaves, halved
4 green onions, sliced (white part only)
6 ounces feta cheese, cubed
4 (4 ounce) pieces of cod
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 tablespoon Bertolli® Extra Light™ Tasting Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475 degrees F.
Cut tomatoes in half horizontally and remove seeds. Season with 1/4 tsp. salt, then evenly sprinkle with bread crumbs. Drizzle with 1 Tbsp. Bertolli(R) Classico(TM) Olive Oil. Bake 20 minutes or until tomatoes are tender.
Toss arugula, basil, green onions, cheese and remaining 1/4 cup Olive Oil; set aside.
Season cod with remaining 1/4 tsp. salt and pepper. Heat 1 Tbsp. Bertolli(R) Extra Light(TM) Tasting Olive Oil in 12-inch nonstick skillet and cook cod, turning once, 6 minutes or until cod flakes with a fork. Arrange tomatoes on serving plates, then top with cod and salad. Drizzle, if desired, with additional Olive Oil.

Submitter: 
Bertolli Olive Oil
Description: 
Delicate white fish is lightly seasoned and served with a simple salad of roasted tomatoes and arugula.
