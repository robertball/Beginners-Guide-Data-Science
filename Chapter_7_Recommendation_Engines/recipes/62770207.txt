Title:
Yam, Jicama, and Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs yams, peeled, 1/2-inch dice (about 4 cups)
5 1/2 Tbsp Wegmans Pure Olive Oil, divided
Salt and pepper to taste
1 jicama (about 1 lb), trimmed, cut in thin strips (about 3 cups)
2 pkgs (9 oz each) Food You Feel Good About Mango Chunks, 1/2-inch dice
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
Juice and zest of 1 orange
Juice and zest of 1 lemon
Juice of 2 limes (about 5 Tbsp)
2 Tbsp Wegmans Apple Cider Vinegar
1/2 tsp ground allspice

Directions:
Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Toss yams in 2 Tbsp olive oil; season with salt and pepper. Place on baking sheet. Bake about 20 min or until tender. Remove from oven; let cool.
Blanch jicama 2 min in large pot of boiling, salted water until cooked but still firm. Drain; transfer to bowl of ice water. Drain.
Toss yams, jicama, mango, cilantro, all zest, three juices, vinegar, allspice, and remaining 3 1/2 Tbsp olive oil in mixing bowl; season to taste with salt and pepper.
Chill before serving.
Chef Tip(s):
Prepare 1 day ahead to allow flavors to meld.
