Title:
Citrus Sunset Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976515.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Smirnoff® Citrus Vodka
1/2 ounce Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ Coconut
2 ounces cranberry juice
2 ounces pineapple juice
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Fill glass with ice.
Add Smirnoff(R) Citrus Vodka, Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) Coconut, cranberry juice, and pineapple juice.
Stir well.
Garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Add that bit of extra flavor to this or other cocktail recipes easily with the natural fruit flavors of Smirnoff® Flavored Vodkas.
