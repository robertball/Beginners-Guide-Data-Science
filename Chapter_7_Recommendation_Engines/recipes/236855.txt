Title:
Matcha Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2005545.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes, or as desired
1 cup orange juice
1 ripe banana
1/2 teaspoon matcha green tea powder, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, orange juice, banana, and matcha powder together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
JIMZGRL
Description: 
This is a fabulous smoothie. Mango, orange, and peach juice blend can be used in place of orange juice.
