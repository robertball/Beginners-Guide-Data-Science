Title:
Easiest Butter Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 frozen, unbaked tart shells
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
3 tbsp softened butter
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350. Place frozen shells on baking sheet of muffin tray.
2. Mix brown sugar, egg, butter, and raisins in a small bowl until well mixed. Spoon evenly into tart shells.
3. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until shells turn golden brown, and filling has puffed up.
4. Cool and serve. These freeze extremely well in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
MONICA DEREGT
Description: 
A gooey and decadent butter and raisin tart recipe that takes minutes to prepare, and impresses people every time!
