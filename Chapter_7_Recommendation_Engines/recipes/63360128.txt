Title:
Grandma Moss's "Never Fail" pie crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C Shortening
4 C flour
1 Tblspn Sugar
Optional - 1 Tsp salt
Mix 1/2 C cold water, 1 egg and 1 tblspn vinegar together.

Directions:
Cut shortening, flour, sugar and salt into size of peas.  Beat egg, water and vinegar. gradually add in other ingredients and mix until you can handle the dough well. Use for your favorite pies, pot pies, pasties... whatever you like!!
