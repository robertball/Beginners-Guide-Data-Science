Title:
Cinnamon Apple and Havarti Tea Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/869734.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
8 slices cinnamon raisin bread
4 slices Havarti cheese
1 large Granny Smith apple, cored and thinly sliced

Directions:
Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on 4 slices of bread. Top the cream cheese with a slice of Havarti cheese, then a thin layer of apple slices. Place the remaining slices of bread on top to form 4 sandwiches.
Using 2 diagonal cuts, divide each sandwich into 4 triangles.

Submitter: 
CookieEater
Description: 
These tasty tea sandwiches are simple but unique and perfect for entertaining. The combination of the tart apples, creamy cheese, and sweet cinnamon raisin bread is surprisingly delicious.
