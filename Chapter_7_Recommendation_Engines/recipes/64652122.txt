Title:
Rice Balls alla Christina Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked long-grain white rice, cooked and cooled
2 eggs
1 onion, chopped small
1- 8oz. can tomato sauce
1 Tbsp. dried parsley
2 tsps. garlic powder
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs, or as needed, plus extra for rolling balls into Salt and pepper, to taste
1-8oz. block mozzarella cheese, cut into 24 cubes
Marinara sauce or ranch dressing for dipping, optional

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except cheese cubes, in a large bowl; mix well.
Spoon about 1 to 1-1/2 Tbsps. rice mixture into the palm of your hand and flatten out slightly.
Place 1 mozzarella cheese cube into the center and wrap the rice mixture around it to enclose it completely.
Roll into a ball and into seasoned breadcrumbs to coat.
Continue until all rice mixture/cheese cubes are used.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour to overnight.
Heat deep fryer or large pot filled w/ oil to 350 degree.
Fry rice balls, a few at a time, until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.
Place on rack or paper towels to drain.
Serve immediately w/ a side of marinara sauce or ranch dressing for dipping.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
These rice balls are not your 'normal'. Tucked inside is a special, yummy surprise...a gooey, melty chunk of mozzarella cheese! Mmmm...Prep time does not include chilling time.
