Title:
Mexican cornbread casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 small yellow onion, chopped
1 can corn, drained
1 4 oz can chopped green chilies
1/2 TBS chili powder
dash each onion powder, garlic salt, and pepper
1 can ranch beans
1 can sliced black olives (optional)
8 oz shredded monterrey jack cheese
1 8 oz box cornbread mix
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
1 TBS mince jalapeno (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.Brown ground beef and onion. While beef is browning, mix together cornbread mix, egg, milk, and jalapenos in a small bowl. Set aside. When beef is done, drain fat from skillet. Add chili powder, onion powder, garlic salt, and pepper. Stir well. Mix in corn, green chilies, ranch beans, and olives. Spray a 13x9 casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray. Put beef mixture in to dish. Top with 8 oz of shredded cheese. Top cheese with cornbread mix covering entire casserole. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until cornbread is done.
