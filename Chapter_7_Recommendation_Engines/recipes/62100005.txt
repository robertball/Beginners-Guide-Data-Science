Title:
Amy's Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp nutmeg
2 cups pumpkin
1 cup milk
2 eggs beaten
1 unbaked 9" pie shell

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients.
Add pumpkin.
Combine milk & eggs.
Mix with dry ingredients & pumpkin.
Set pie shell on baking sheet.
Pour pie filling into pie shell.
Bake at 450 degrees F for 10 minutes.
Reduce temp to 350 degrees F for 35-45 minutes.
Pie is done when knife inserted into center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Jen
Description: 
Very easy.  No evaporated milk necessary.
