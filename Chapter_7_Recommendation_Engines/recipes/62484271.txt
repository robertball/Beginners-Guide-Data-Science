Title:
Oat Molasses Horse Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C dry oatmeal
1/2 C. of grated carrots
3 T. molasses
1/2 C brown sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together in bowl, mix together.
Add a little water, 1T at a time, to make dough-like and soft.
Form cookies on greased cookie sheet.
Bake 350 degrees for 10-12 min. or until brown.
