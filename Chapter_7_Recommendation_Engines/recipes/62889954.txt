Title:
Stromboli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/698005.jpg
Ingredients:
7 1/2 Oz (3/4 cup + 3 tablespoons) water
2 tablespoons of olive oil
2 1/2 cups of flour
1/2 teaspoon of sugar
1/2 teaspoon of salt
2 teaspoons of yeast (7 grams)
3 cups of marinara sauce (Cubed Tomatoes, Chopped Onions, Chopped garlic)
1 egg yoke

Directions:
In a measuring cup add warm water, yeast and sugar, mix till dissolved, allow to set for 5 Min's. you should be able to see the yeast growing.
In a Bowl, add flour, salt, olive oil, add yeast and water mixture, and whip with a fork dough into a ball. turn dough out and kneed for 5 Min's.
NOTE: You can also do this in a mixing bowl with a mixer and a dough mixing attachment.
Form into a ball coat with oil put back into bowl and cover with a wet towel for 15 to 20 Min's.
Turn Dough Out Onto A Floured board, and roll out into a rectangle about 3/8 inch high.
Top with Meats First, Genoa Salami, Peperoni, Ham, Then Cheeses, Mozzarella, Provolone, Asiago, Parmesan, Romano, Fontina, And Ricotta Cheese
Now fold over dough, Crimp On Edges By Just Pressing With Your Fingers, and tuck ends under
brush the dough with an egg yoke. this will allow the dough to take on a golden brown color.
500F for 15 to 20 Min's (you may need to rotate your pan in the oven to cook evenly)
Top With A Home Made Marinara Sauce (Cubed Tomatoes, Chopped Onions, Chopped garlic) cooked in a sauce pan for about 30 Min's
