Title:
Hot Chicken Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 50 oz. can chunk chicken, drained
1/4 cup Miracle Whip
1/2 can cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 chopped onion, if desired
black pepper, to taste
1 Tbsp. olive oil

Directions:
Heat olive oil in pan over medium heat. Add onion and cook until transulcent. Combine all ingredients in crockpot. Mix well. Shred the chicken with two forks. Cook for 2-3 hours or until hot. Serve with hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
HARTMR
Description: 
Creamy, cheesy chicken sandwiches that are easy to mix up and throw in the crockpot. Perfect for entertaining!
