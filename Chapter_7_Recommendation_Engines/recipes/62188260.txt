Title:
Boat Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 shot silver tequila (patron or 1800)
1/2 shot melon liqueur (Midori)
1/4 shot triple sec
1/2 shot sour mix
Splash each of grenadine and lime juice
Orange juice
Maraschino cherry, garnish
Orange wedge, garnish

Directions:
Fill a margarita glass with crushed ice. Add each ingredient in the order listed and garnish with a maraschino cherry and a wedge of orange.

Submitter: 
witchywoman
Description: 
I had something similar at a local Mexican restaurant and recreated it to the best of my knowledge at home. I figured if I had to recreate it, I could name it whatever I want, that's how I got the name!
