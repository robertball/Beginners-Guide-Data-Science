Title:
Parmesan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C Mayo
1/4 C Parm cheese
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast
4 tsp italian bread crumbs

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425
2. Combine mayo with cheese in medium bowl.  Arrange chicken on baking cheese.  Evenly top with mayo mixture and sprinkle with bread crumbs
3. Bake 20 minutes or until chicken cooked through
