Title:
Cabin Style Fried Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Canola oil
6 large potatoes, peeled and sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
In a large cast iron skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add potatoes & onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are golden brown. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika. Serve hot.
