Title:
Hawaiian Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 blocks of unsalted butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
2 boxes Honey Comb cereal
1 box Rice Chex
1 box Corn chex
1 bag buttered pretzels (waffle cut)

Directions:
Divide half of each boxed cereal between 2 large roasting pans
Add half the pretzels to each pan
Melt butter and add all other ingredients/spices.  Stir for 20 minutes on low heat or until the mixture become fluffy and bubbly.
Pour mixture evenly over each roaster pan, taking care to cover every cereal piece.
Once the cereal mixture is ready, bake at 200 degrees, stir the mixture every 20 min, and bake a total of 2 hours.
Serve immediately or pour into little baggies for gifts.
