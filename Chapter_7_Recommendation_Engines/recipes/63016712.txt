Title:
Marge's Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755735.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups of sugar
1 1/2 cubes of Parkay margarine (3/4 cup)
2/3 cup evaporated milk
Dash of salt
2 tsp. of vanilla
12 oz. bag semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 large jar (13 oz.) marshmallow creme
3/4 cup of chopped nuts

Directions:
Bring sugar, margarine, salt, and milk to a full rolling boil at medium heat in a medium sized saucepan and boil for 5 minutes stirring constantly. (234 degrees on candy thermometer)
Remove from heat. Add vanilla if desired, a bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips, marshmallow creme, and chopped nuts; mixing well until blended.
Pour into a buttered  9x13 pan and refrigerate.
Cut into (78) 1 1/2 inch squares.

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
A delicious creamy fudge recipe came from my friend Karen's mom-Marge. It uses less sugar than most recipes and is easy to make.
