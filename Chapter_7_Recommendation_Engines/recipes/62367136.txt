Title:
Sweet Italian Sausage Bolognese w/Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. carrots, peeled chopped
2 stalks of celery, chopped
1 medium to large onion, chopped
1 T. olive oil
1/2 c. milk or cream
1 pkg (20 oz) Sweet Italian sausage, remove casing
1 box (13.5) linguine or fettucine
3/4 c. dry white wine
1 can (28 0z) whole tomatoes

Directions:
In a food proccesor pulse carrots, celery and onion until they are finely chopped.  Heal oil in large skillet over med-high heat.  Add vegetables and saute, stirring constantly about 5-6 minutes.  Rinse processor, set aside.  Crumble the sausage and add to vegetables in skillet, cook 3 minutes.  Add wine and simmer over medium-low heat 10 minutes.  Drain tomatoes.  Chop tomatoes in processor and add to skillet.  Reduce heat to low and simmer 30 minutes or until most of the liquid is absorbed.  Stir in milk or cream and cook 5 minutes.  Cook pasta in salted water per package directions.  Serve pasta w/sauce and grated parmesan.

Submitter:

Description: 
This bolognese sauce differs from the typical vodka sauce by using wine instead.  The taste is similiar to the vodka and is the cost is less.
