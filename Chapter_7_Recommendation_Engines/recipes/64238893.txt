Title:
Microwaved Egg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
salt to taste
pepper to taste
Any other dried herb or seasoning to taste (dried basil, dried onion powder, etc)
Cooking spray

Directions:
Spray a microwavable plate with cooking spray.  A paper plate works great for this.  Crack egg onto plate.  Add salt, pepper and seasonings to egg.  Invert another plate on top of the first plate.  Microwave 30-40 seconds.  Wait 1 minute to let egg finish cooking.  Check for doneness.  Microwave in 10 second intervals until done.
