Title:
Crab Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. rotini noodles (shells, bowties, etc
1 cup low fat mayonnaise
1 small bag frozen peas, thawed
1-2 8 oz. pkgs. imitation crab (chunk style)
2 T. chives
1 T. dill
1 can water chestnuts, chopped

Directions:
Cook pasta according to the package directions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
Add all ingredients to a large bowl and mix carefully.
Add more mayo, crab, and/or chives to taste.

Submitter: 
fishinwidow
Description: 
This creamy pasta salad is always a must at family gatherings.  It is simple to make because there are only a few ingredients.
