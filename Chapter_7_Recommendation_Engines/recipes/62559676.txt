Title:
Vegetable Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535535.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8 sheets whole wheat lasagna noodles, cooked according to package directions
1 Portobello mushroom, chopped
1 yellow squash, chopped
1 zucchini, chopped
1 small eggplant, chopped
1 (28 ounce) can low fat marinara sauce
1 (15 ounce) container fat free ricotta cheese
2 tablespoons parmesan cheese
1 egg
3 garlic cloves, crushed
2 teaspoons dried oregano
salt and peper to taste
4 ounces reduced fat mozzarella cheese

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, combine mushroom, squash, zucchini, eggplant, and marinara sauce. Cook until vegetables are tender.
In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, mozarella cheese, garlic, oregano, salt and pepper.
In an 8x8 baking dish, pour 1/3 of the sauce mixture into the bottom and spread evenly. Top with 2 sheets of lasagna noodles. Spread 1/2 the ricotta cheese on top, and cover with two more noodles. Then another 3rd of sauce, 2 noddles, remaining 1/2 cheese, 2 noodles, and the remaining sauce.
Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 40  minutes

Submitter: 
SlimCookins
Description: 
Rich and hearty, eggplant and portabello mushrooms give this rather meatless lasagna a meaty texture and taste.
