Title:
Texas Hotdog Sauce- Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
8 ounces ground beef
8 ounces ground pork
8 beef frankfurters
1/2 cup diced sweet onion
1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
1 teaspoon browning sauce
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1  teaspoon salt
1 (10.75 ounce) can tomato soup
1/2 cup ketchup
1 tsp. French's mustard
4 cups water
1 and 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon chili powder
To thicken:
make a roux with flour

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil in a large, deep skillet over medium high heat. Place ground beef, ground pork, and sweet onion in the skillet. Cook until meat is evenly brown and onion is soft. Drain and lower heat.
Mix in garlic, browning sauce, ground black pepper, salt, tomato soup, water, paprika, chili powder. Slowly simmer until thick, about 25  minutes.
