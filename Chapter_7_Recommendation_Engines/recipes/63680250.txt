Title:
Passion Crush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cantaloupe, peeled, seeded, & divided into 2 equal portions
8oz. (250g) strawberries, hulled
Pulp of 2 passion fruit
1c. crushed ice

Directions:
Process cantaloupe & strawberries through Juice Fountain™ using speed 1.
Stir in passion fruit pulp.
Scoop ice into 4 glasses, pour juice over ice, mix well to combine. Serve Immediately.
