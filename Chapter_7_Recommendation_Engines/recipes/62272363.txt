Title:
Cresent Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk
3 cups cooked,cubed chicken
16 oz bag of frozen vegetables-broc,cauliflower,carrot mix
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 can durkee fried onions (i used big can)
1 pkg crescent rolls
1 cup cubed potatoes (i used left over baked potatoe)
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
combine soup,milk,chicken,vegetables, 1 cup cheese, 1/2 can of onions,potatoes.  Place in 8 x 12 baking dish.  Cover and bake 45 mins.
Open crescent rolls press together to make 1 sheet.  Place on top of casserole, top with remaining cheese and more onions.  Place back in oven for 10-15 minutes uncovered.
