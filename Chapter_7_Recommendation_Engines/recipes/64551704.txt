Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1¾ cups flour
2 tsp. baking powder
¼ tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. salt
1/3 cup shortening
2/3 cup granulated sugar
1 cup mashed bananas
½ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
1. Cream shortening.
2. Add sugar and beat until light and fluffy.
3. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt alternately with mashed bananas.
4. Stir in walnuts.
Bake @ 350° for 60 minutes. Time may vary. Check with toothpick. Done when the toothpick comes out clean.
