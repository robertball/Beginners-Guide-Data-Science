Basic Dumplings Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups biscuit baking mix
2/3 cup milk

Directions:
Stir until soft dough forms.  Using cookie scoop, drop onto boiling stew and reduce heat.  Cook uncovered for 10 minutes and covered for an additional 10 minutes.
