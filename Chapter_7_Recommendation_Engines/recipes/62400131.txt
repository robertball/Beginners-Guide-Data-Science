Title:
White Mac n' Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb - Macaroni
1/2stick - Butter
3T - All-Purpose Flour
2c - Milk
2c - Monterey Jack Cheese
2c - Motzerella Cheese
2c - White Cheddar Cheese
1 1/2c - Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Boil noodles as directed. Melt butter and stir in flour until forms a paste, add milk blend and simmer, and then stir in cheeses until fully blended. Then combine cheese and noodles.
