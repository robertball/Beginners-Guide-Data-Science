Title:
Secret Ingredient Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground sirloin beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 (15 ounce) cans dark red kidney beans, drained, rinsed
2 (14.5 ounce) cans Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes in Juice, undrained
1 (15 ounce) can Hunt's® Tomato Sauce
1 cup water
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon granulated sugar
1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
Sliced green onions (optional)
Sour cream (optional)
Shredded Cheddar cheese (optional)
Chopped avocado (optional)
Crushed corn chips (optional)

Directions:
Cook and stir ground beef and onion in large skillet over medium-high heat about 7 minutes, or until beef crumbles and is no longer pink; drain.
Add beans, undrained tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, chili powder, sugar, cocoa and salt. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low. Simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve chili topped with onions, sour cream, cheese, avocado and/or corn chips if desired.

Classic meat and bean chili with tomatoes and cocoa powder for the rich ‘secret' flavor.
