Title:
My Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pork loin
2 tsp dijon mustard
cajon seasoning
salt and pepper
white pepper
balsamic vinegar
bacon grease

Directions:
pre heat the oven to 350  rub the entire loin with bacon grease.  Then top with salt, pepper then white pepper.  then generously go over the entire loin with essence cajon seasoning.  Then drizzle with balsamic and mustard and rub the entire loin until evenly covered.  Bake until liked doneness.
