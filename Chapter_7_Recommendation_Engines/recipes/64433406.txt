Title:
Spicy Sauteed asaparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2338269.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound of asparagus, ends snapped off
1 can of button mushrooms
1 tbsp of butter
1 tbsp of olive oil
1/2 tsp of cayenne
1 tsp of garlic powder
1 cup of torn up basil leaves
1 can of condensed cream soup (your favorite)

Directions:
I a large skillet saute mushrooms and asparagus in olive oil and butter.
Season with cayenne and garlic powder (at this poing add any seasoning you like.
Add the can of cream soup.
Stir and heated through and simmering.
Add the basil leaves and stir until wilted.

Ron Lloyd

Very tasty and somewhat spicy side dish.
