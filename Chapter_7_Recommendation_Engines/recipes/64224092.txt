Title:
Moms Oatmeal Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pie shell
3/4 C Sugar
3/4 C Oatmeal
3/4 C Corn Syrup ( light or dark)
1/2 C shredded coconut
1 stick butter/ margarine melted
2 eggs well beaten

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and pour into unbaked pie shell...bake 350* for 45 min
