Title:
Energy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C oats
1/2 C honey
1 C olive oil
6 eggs beaten
1 C chopped walnuts
4 tsp vanilla
1 C chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Press firmly into a 9x13 greased pan. Bake in a 350 degree NON PREHEATED oven for 25 min. Allow to cool before cutting into squares and wrapping in wax paper or plastic wrap and store and serve from refrigerator or freezer.

Submitter: 
CHEFROMANO
Description: 
This recipe is an easy/basic bar great for breakfasts, snacks on the go and easily changed depending on what's in your cupboards!
