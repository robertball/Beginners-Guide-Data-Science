Title:
Jalapeno Popper Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
4 ounces canned diced jalapeno peppers, drained
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or mexican blend)

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese, sour cream, and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in green chiles, jalapeno peppers, and cheese. Pour mixture into a oven safe bowl and top w/bread crumbs or panko.  Spray w/PAM.  Bake at 350 until browned and bubble (about 30-40 minutes).  Serve w/fresh bread slices and crackers.
