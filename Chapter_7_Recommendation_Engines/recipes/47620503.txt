Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
2 cloves garlic
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
1/2 pound chopped chicken breast
1 1/2 cups egg noodles
1 cup sliced carrots
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
1/4 tsp dried thyme

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, melt butter.  Cook onion, celery and carrots in butter until just tender, 5 minutes.  Pour in chicken and vegetable broths and stir in chicken, carrots, spices, salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.  Boil egg noodles accordint to package directions.  Drain and add to soup.  simmer 20 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
When you don't have time to make your soup totally from scratch, this is a very easy, very good substitute.Modified from: Quick and Easy Chicken Noodle Soup
