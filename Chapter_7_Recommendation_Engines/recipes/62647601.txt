Title:
Phad Siewe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large Dry Rice Noodles (soaked)
Meat of your choice
Vegetable Oil - 1 TBS
Garlic - 1 teaspoon
Sugar - 1 TBS
Light Soy Sauce - 2TBS
Dark Soy Sauce - 2-3 drops
Ground Pepper - 2-3 pinches
Chinese Kale - 1/2 handful
Savoy cabbage - 1 handful
Carrots - 4-5 slices

Directions:
1. Soak Noodles in cold water about 20-25 minutes. Bring 1 liter of water to a boil, soak noodles in boiling water 3-4 seconds and remove them and place them on a plate.
2. In the same pot of boiling water quickly soak the veggies (cabbage and kale) for 3-4 seconds and then drain. Put them together with the noodles and add the garlic, light soy, dark soy, and sugar on the noodles and veggies.
3. Heat the oil in a wok and then add the meat, stir until meat is cooked, then add step 2. Sitr to combine then serve with a pinch of pepper.
