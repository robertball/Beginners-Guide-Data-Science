Title:
Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C flour
2tsp baking powder
1/4tsp salt
small can evaporated milk about 1/2C

Directions:
Sift together the dry ingredients then add the milk. Drop dumplings on boiled liquid or soup. keep covered about 12minutes.
