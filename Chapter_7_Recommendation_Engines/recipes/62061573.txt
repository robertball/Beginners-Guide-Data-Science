Title:
Egg Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 jar of roasted red peppers
2 Portuguese rolls
2 slices of cheddar cheese
1 tbs of wet crushed red pepper found in tall jars in the international section of the grocery store (not peppercorns in brine)

Directions:
~Fry the eggs over easy making sure that egg yolks are fully cooked.
~Place the fried egg in the sandwich bread along with cheddar cheese, crushed pepper and roasted pepper.
~Enjoy

Submitter: 
Melissa Rapoza Fragoza
Description: 
This is one of my husband's favorites.  This is so quick and easy even he can make it!
