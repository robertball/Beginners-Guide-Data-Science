Title:
Saucy Meatloaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1/4 cup oatmeal
2 Tbsp onion, chopped
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup milk
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 Tbsp catsup
1 tsp mustard
1 tsp brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In medium bowl, combine ground beef, oatmeal, onion, egg, milk, salt and pepper.
Shape into 3 individual loaves and place in a greased shallow baking pan.
Mix catsup, mustard and brown sugar and spoon onto meat loaves.  Bake for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Mini meatloaves- leftovers make great sandwiches.
