Title:
Cheesy Cheddar Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium potato, peeled and diced
2 ribs celery, thinly sliced
2 carrots, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
3 TBSP. butter or margarine
1 can (14 oz) chicken broth
3 TBSP. flour
2 C. milk (divided)
8 oz. (2 C.) finely shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In large heavy saucepan, cook potato, celery, carrots, and onion in butter over med-high heat for 5 minutes or until onion is translucent. Add broth, bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 12 minutes. In small bowl, stir flour into 1/4 cup of the milk to make thin paste. Gradually stir into vegetables. Stir in remaining milk. Increase heat to med-high; cook, stirring frequently until chowder beings to thicken. Remove from heat; stir in cheese until melted.
