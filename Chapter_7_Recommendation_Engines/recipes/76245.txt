Title:
Sweet Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983843.jpg
Ingredients:
8 packets SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener
3 tablespoons cocoa (preferably Dutch processed)
2 cups 2% reduced-fat milk

Directions:
Mix together SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and cocoa in a small saucepan. Gradually add milk, whisking until blended.
Cook over medium-low heat until thoroughly heated. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Treat yourself to this rich hot chocolate, and don't even worry about the calories. A great way to warm up your day.
