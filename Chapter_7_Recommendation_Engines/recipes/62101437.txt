Title:
Ham and Egg Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large 1/4 in thick ham slices (2 pkgs store bought or use leftover baked ham)
4 hard boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
1 1/2 c light mayo
1/2 c dill pickle relish
1/4 c finely chopped onion
3 stalks celery, chopped or finely sliced
black pepper
Optional:  2 tbsp Dijon mustard

Directions:
Using the shredding blade on your food processor, shred the ham.
In a large bowl, mix ham and all remaining ingredients.
Chill for 2 hours before seriving to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
JennyB
Description: 
This is a twist on traditional ham salad recipes for those of you who prefer dill pickles over sweet.  Serve on pumpernickel bread with fresh alfalfa sprouts!
