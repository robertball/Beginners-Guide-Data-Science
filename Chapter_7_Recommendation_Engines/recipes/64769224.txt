Title:
Mom's Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup butter
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Sift flour, baking powder, and salt.
Melt butter and add to flour mixture.
Slowly stir in milk until dough is soft, not sticky.
On a floured board knead and press to approx 3/4" thickness.
Cut into 2x2 inch squares and place on a sheetpan.
Bake for 15 minutes.
