Title:
Kid's Turkey and Cream Cheese Spread Bento Box Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540741.jpg
Ingredients:
1 /2 large carrot, sliced in circles and cut into penguins and cats using veggie cutters*
8 slices nitrate free lunchmeat (4 for each lunch)
round crackers
1 red apple, sliced
1 green apple, sliced
grapes
6 slices of cucumbers sliced vertically on mandoline
3 sliced tomatoes, cut in 1/2
1/2 of the recipe Cream Cheese Penguins  http://allrecipes.com/recipe/19687/cream-cheese-penguins/
1/2 recipe Party Vegetable Sandwich Spread http://allrecipes.com/recipe/223094/party-vegetable-sandwich-spread/
2 T. of pimento subbed for red pepper
omitted cucumbers

Directions:
1. Prepare Cream Cheese Penguins according to directions-1/2 of the recipe. Refrigerate.
2. Prepare Party Vegetable Sandwich Spread according to directions-1/2 of the recipe. Refrigerate.
3. Slice red and green apples. Dip in lemon juice. Wash grapes.
4. Slice carrots in round circles. Use penguin and cat vegetable cutters to make penguin carrots. Wrap in a wet paper towel, place in a plastic bag and refrigerate.
5. Slice cucumber in long slices using a mandoline. Pat the cucumbers dry. Slice a tomato in a round disc and cut in 1/2.  Roll tomato and cucumber  together and use a toothpick to keep together.
Assemble Bento box and refrigerate overnight. This recipe makes 2 lunches.
Place carrot cats and penguins in the box.  Use a silicone baking cup for the Party Vegetable Sandwich Spread and a cup for the crackers.  Place sliced apples and grapes in bento box. Add the Cream Cheese Penguins, turkey slices and cucumber tomato rolls.
To serve:  Add the cream cheese spread and turkey to a cracker.
NOTES:
Recipes Used: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/19687/cream-cheese-penguins/
http://allrecipes.com/recipe/223094/party-vegetable-sandwich-spread/
*Use vegetable animal cutters to cut carrot slices.  If you don't have animal vegetable cutters just cut carrots in round circles.  Use silicone cups for cream cheese spread and crackers.

A  whimsical bento box that is sure to delight any kid.  Inside the box are turkey slices, crackers, cream cheese spread, carrot cat and penguin cut-outs, cucumber tomato rolls, apple slices and grapes.
