Title:
Olive Cheese Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/251775.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup black olives, chopped
1/3 cup green onions, chopped
1 1/2 cups shredded 6 Cheese Italian cheese
1/2 cup Miracle Whip or mayo
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 plain bagels, split in half
8 slices of fresh tomato, split in half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine olives, green onions, cheese, miracle whip, and salt. Mix well.
Cut the bagels into fourths. Spread mixture on bagels. Note: If you want to freeze these, now is the time! Arrange the bagel pieces on a cookie sheet.
Add sliced tomatoes to the tops decoratively.
Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 10 minutes.
