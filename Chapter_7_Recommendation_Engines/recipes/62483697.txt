Title:
Loaded Pumpkin-Carrot Cake Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of flour (all purpose or whole wheat)
2 tsp ground cinnamon
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
2 tsp baking soda
Pinch of salt
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 eggs
1 can pumpkin puree (not pumpkin pie mix)
1 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 (8-ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained well
1 cup sweetened flaked coconut
1 cup grated carrots
1/2 cup chopped pecans (other types of nuts can be substituted)
1/2 cup raisins (or craisins)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and place 24 cupcakes liners into cupcakes pans.
Combine flour, cinnamon, pumpkin pie spice, baking soda and salt in small bowl and set aside.
Combine milk, lemon juice and vanilla extract in measuring cup and set aside.
Beat eggs, pumpkin, granulated sugar, brown sugar, oil and milk mixture in large bowl; mix well. Gradually add flour mixture; beat until combined. Stir in pineapple, coconut, carrots, nuts and raisins. Let mixture sit for 5 minutes.
Give batter another stir and then spoon into cupcake liners; fill 2/3 full.
Bake on middle rack of oven for 20 to 25 minutes or until cooked through.  (Test doneness with toothpick).
Cool in cupcake pans for 5 minutes. Remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Pamela Souza LeBlanc
Description: 
I love Pumpkin cupcakes and my husband loves Carrot Cake (with all the add-ins)...so I came up with a recipe that combines the two!  I made a Chai Spice Cream Cheese frosting to go on them...YUM!!!
