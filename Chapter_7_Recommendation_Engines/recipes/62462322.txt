Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (12oz. approx) skinless & boneless chicken breast halves - cubed or stripped(can be frozen)
2 tsp olive oil
1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1 can or mushroom slices
1 Cup water
1 Chicken Boullion Cube
1 (8 ounce) package Fat Free cream cheese
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed  Reduced Fat Cream of Mushroom Soup

Directions:
Put chicken, olive oil, water, mushrooms, boulion cute and dressing mix in slow cooker; mix together and cook on low for 5 to 6 hours.
Add cream cheese and soup, mix together and cook on high for another 15 minutes or until heated through and warm.

Submitter: 
Tracy Taras-Soika
Description: 
Creamy chicken lightened up with fat free cream cheese and reduced fat cream soup. This crock pot dinner comes together quickly.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff
