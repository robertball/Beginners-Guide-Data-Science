Title:
Small Batch Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985849.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
In a bowl, combine flour and salt.  Whisk together eggs and milk; stir into dry ingredients just until blended.  Pour into four greased and floured 8-oz. custard cups.  Place on a baking sheet.  Bake, uncovered, at 425 degrees F for 20 minutes or until puffed and edges are golden brown (do not open the oven door during baking).

Submitter: 
Pauline  Sniezek
Description: 
'These golden popovers are a great way to round out any meal,' notes Pauline Sniezek of Adams, Massachuesetts. 'We have enjoyed these popovers often during 50 years together.'
