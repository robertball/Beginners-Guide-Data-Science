Title:
Great White Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993014.jpg
Ingredients:
Dough and Cheese:
1 (12 inch) pizza crust
1 1/2 cups California Mozzarella, shredded
1 1/2 cups California Monterey Jack, shredded
Pizza Pantry Ingredients:
1/2 cup Ranch dressing
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Spread Ranch dressing on partially baked crust.  Sprinkle Mozzarella and Monterey Jack evenly over dressing.  Sprinkle herbs evenly over cheese.
Bake about 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is crisp and golden.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
If you like Ranch dressing, this is your kind of pizza. Great White Pizza combines everyone’s favorite cheeses  California Mozzarella and California Monterey Jack  and replaces the traditional red sauce with Ranch dressing for a smooth tasting pizza that is sure to attract attention.
