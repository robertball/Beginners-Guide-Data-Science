Title:
Homemade Italian Turkey Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
2 tsp garlic powder
1 1/2 tsp fennel seed, crushed well
1 1/2 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.
Cover and refrigerate for at least hours or overnight to allow flavors to combine.
Use in recipes calling for Italian sausage or form into patties and pan-fry.
