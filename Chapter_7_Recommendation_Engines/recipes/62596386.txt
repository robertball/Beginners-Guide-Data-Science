Title:
Spicy Garlic Red Pepper Shrimp with Penne Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For shrimp:
1/3 cup extra virgen olive oil
4 cloves garlic, riced in garlic press
1 tsp red pepper flakes
2 tsp paprika
2 lbs shelled, deveined jumbo shrimp
Juice and zest of 1 lemon
2 tbsp chopped fresh basil
salt & pepper to taste
For pasta:
4 cloves, garlic riced in garlic press
1/4 cup parsley, finely minced
1/2 pound mushrooms, sliced
3 tbsp extra virgen olive oil
3 tbsp butter
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 lb penne pasta
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
For Shrimp:
Saute garlic in olive oil until translucent.
Sprinkle red pepper flakes and paprika into the garlic oil mixture, mix well.
Add shrimp and coat well with oil mixture.
Once shrimp begin to turn pink, add lemon juice.
Cook for another 2 minutes.
Reduce heat and add basil, toss lightly.
Salt and pepper taste.
Set aside
For Pasta:
Boil penne pasta for 10 -12 minutes, or until al dente.
While pasta is boiling, saute garlic in olive oil and butter until translucent.
Add parsley and Italian seasoning.
Add mushrooms and cook until tender, approx 5 minutes.
Drain pasta and add to garlic/mushroom mixture, mix until pasta is well coated.
Add shrimp mixture and continue to mix with pasta until pasta is well coated.
Salt & pepper to taste.
Serve with grated parmesan cheese.
