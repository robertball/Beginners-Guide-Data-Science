Title:
Southwestern Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4558260.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Roma Tomatoes
3 Tomatillos
2 Poblano Peppers
1 White Onion
2 Jalapeno Peppers
2 Cloves Garlic
1/2 Cup Fresh Chives
1 Lime
1 Bunch Cilantro
Olive Oil
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Prepare ingredients adding to a large mixing bowl as you go:
Medium chop tomatoes, tomatillos, poblano peppers (seeds removed), onion & jalapenos (seeds if you want heat).
Small chop garlic
Mince chives
Grab a good portion of fresh cilantro and roughly chop (leaves only, no stems)
Add juice from one lime
Drizzle with olive oil (this will act as a binder)
Add salt as desired (this will pull the juices from the ingredients for the proper consistency.)
Add freshly ground pepper to taste.
Stir together and allow to marinate for at least a few hours before serving or (better overnight).
Serve with chips or however you enjoy salsa.
