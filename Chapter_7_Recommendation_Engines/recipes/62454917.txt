Title:
Ray's Sasuteed Mushrooms II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 1/2 ounce) cans beef broth
1/2 cup onion, diced
2 (16 ounce) cans small whole mushrooms, like button mushrooms (plus the juice of one can or jar)
1/3 cup Burgundy wine

Directions:
1.Place beef broth in a saucepan and simmer onion for 15 minutes.
2.Add mushrooms, along with juice from 1 can or jar and wine.
3.Simmer another 15 minutes.
4.Serve with steak.

Submitter: 
RAYRIGGS
Description: 
Delicious! This recipe combines the essential flavors for a savory dish of sauteed mushrooms: beef broth, and wine.
