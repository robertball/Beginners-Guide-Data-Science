Title:
Simmered Chipotle Chicken for Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 boneless skinless chicken thighs
water
2 T vegetable oil
sauce:
1 7-oz can chipotle chili's in adobo sauce
half small onion, sliced
handful fresh cilantro
2  cloves garlic
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper or red pepper flakes can be subbed
salt and pepper

Directions:
Remove one chipotle chili from the can, slice it open and remove the seeds.  Add the pepper and all the adobo sauce from can, onion, cilantro, garlic, to a blender / chopper and blend until smooth.  Save the rest of the chili's for another time.
Heat oil in a Dutch oven, add chicken and cook for five minutes.  Add chili sauce and mix in.  Add water to cover chicken, cumin, cayenne and salt and pepper.
Cover and simmer on low until chicken is easy to shred, about 2 hours.
