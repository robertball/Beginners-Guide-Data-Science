Title:
Spence's Pesto Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636133.jpg
Ingredients:
1-8oz chicken breast, cut in small pieces
salt and pepper, to taste
3T olive oil
6 whole cloves of garlic
4oz fresh mushrooms, halved
6oz roasted red peppers
7.5oz artichoke hearts, quartered and marinated
3oz fresh spinach
1/2 lb linguini (or pasta of choice)
4-5T pesto
fresh parmesan romano cheese, grated (optional)

Directions:
Season the chicken with salt and pepper to taste. Brown the chicken on medium heat in 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet.
Add the garlic, mushrooms, roasted red peppers, and artichoke hearts to the skillet and simmer for 5 minutes. Then, add the spinach.
Meanwhile, prepare the pasta as directed. Drain the pasta. Mix in 2 tablespoons of pesto and then distribute it evenly on two plates.
Mix the remaining pesto in the chicken and vegetables before topping the pasta with equal amounts.
Grate some cheese on top.
