Title:
Tiffany’s Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483113.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. salt
3 mashed bananas (1 cup)
¾ cup sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup butter
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients. Cream sugar, butter and remaining wet ingredients, then add dry ingredients.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
