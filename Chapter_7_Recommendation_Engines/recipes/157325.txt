Title:
Most Delish Quick Dessert In the Universe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/559440.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sweet potato, cooked and mashed
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste
1/4 cup chopped pecans (optional)
1 tablespoon honey, or more to taste

Directions:
Place the mashed sweet potato in a microwave-proof dish. Mix in the cinnamon and pecans, if desired. Drizzle with honey.
Place in microwave and heat until warm. Serve immediately.
