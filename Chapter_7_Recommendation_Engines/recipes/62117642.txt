Title:
A spanish sentence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sheet puff pastry
1 egg
lard/olive oil
1kg of beef fillet
mustard
salt and pepper to your liking
For the sauce:
20ml white wine
2 onions, chopped
20ml boullion
1 tablespoon flour
1 truffel
2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
Rub the fillet with lard/olive oil and mustard.
Fry both sides for 5 minutes each.Pre-heat over to 220 degrees C.
Leave the meat in a bowl (so the juice will come out) and salt,pepper it and leave it to cool.
Roll the puff pastry out and place the cooled fillet on it and wrap it up.
Separare the egg yolk with the egg white and brush the meat roll with the egg yolk.
Cut out different decorations and shapes from the remaining pastry and place them on the roll.
Place in the oven for 30 minutes. Serve with sauce.
For Sauce:
Fry the onions in the vegetable oil and add the flour,white wine and the boullion.
Mix thoroughly until the mixture becomes less like a liquid.
Then add the leftover juice(from the meat) and the cut truffel. Leave it on the stove till it boils.
