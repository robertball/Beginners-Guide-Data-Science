Title:
Classic Italian Minestrone - My way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3112813.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 celery stalks - diced
1/2 onion - diced
1 leek - sliced
4 oz green beans cut into 4" pieces
2 carrots - diced
3-4 Garlic cloves  -minced
1/2 green cabbage - chopped
1 cup spinach - chopped
1 zucchini - diced
1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes - not drained
1 (15 oz) can cannelloni beans
1/2 cup ditalini
salt - to taste
Fresh ground black pepper - to taste
3 tbsp Italian seasoning
48 oz vegetable or chicken stock
1/4 cup basil - chopped
Parmigiana-Reggiano cheese = grated

Directions:
In a  large pot over medium heat, heat the olive oil.
Add celery, onion, leek, carrots, and green beans
Season to taste with salt and pepper
Sweat the vegetables until they are tender (10-15 minutes), stirring frequently
Add the stock and Italian seasoning then bring to a boil
Add cabbage, tomatoes, and zucchini
Bring back up to a boil then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 10 minutes
Add pasta and beans then cover
Simmer until the pasta is al dente, ~ 8-10 minutes
When pasta is cooked, add spinach and simmer for 5 more minutes
Turn off heat, add the basil, and season to taste with salt and pepper
Garnish with grated cheese
If using an Instant Pot, sweat the vegetables, except for the zucchini, cabbage, and spinach.  After the vegetables are soft, turn off the Instant pot and stir in all of the remaining ingredients, except the spinach..  Set the pressure on high for 5 minutes.  When  finished cooking, do a quick release of the pressure, stir in the spinach. Keep stirring until the spinach is wilted. Serve in  individual bowls topped with grated Parmesan-Reggiano.
