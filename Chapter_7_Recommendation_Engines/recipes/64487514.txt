Title:
Sunflower Seed Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Sunflower Seeds
1/2 cup Plant Milk I used almond milk
1 tbsp Lemon Juice
2 tbsp Scallions/Shallots sliced
1 large clove Garlic
2 tbsp Fresh Parsley
1 tbsp Dill
1/2 tsp Paprika
1/4 tsp Black Pepper freshly ground
1/2 tsp Celtic Sea Salt Coarse
1 tsp Dijon Mustard

Directions:
Place all the ingredients into the blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This super tasty vegan sunflower seed ranch dressing will get your taste buds dancing! The fresh flavors of this dressing are fantastic on a salad, pasta or potatoes. This is one of my favorite vegan dressings ever! Best of all it is a whole food plant based recipe that does not use any added oil
