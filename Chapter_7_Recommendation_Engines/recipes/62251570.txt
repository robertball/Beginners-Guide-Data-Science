Title:
Decadent Chocolate Mouse Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 regular sized Hershey Bars with Almonds
1 (8 oz.) tub of Cool Whip + more to top pie or mousse with
Chocolate Cookie Crust (if making pie)
chocolate shavings (optional)
chocolate covered almonds (optional)

Directions:
Melt the candy bars in the microwave
Add Cool Whip to melted candy bars
Pour mixture into chocolate crust (could use individual chocolate crusts) or into individual serving dishes if making mousse
Top with more Cool Whip
Garnish with chocolate shavings and chocolate covered almonds
Refrigerate until ready to serve

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
I got this easy-to-make decadent pie or mousse from my Pampered Chef consultant.  It tastes like something you would order at a fine restaurant.
