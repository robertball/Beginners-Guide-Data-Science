Title:
Cream-Filled Pumpkin Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
3/4 cup veggie oil
1 can (15 oz) solid-pack pumpkin
2 cups all purpose flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
FILLING:
1 tsp. cornstarch
1 cup milk
1/2 cup shortening
1/4 cup butter, softened
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine eggs, sugar, oil and pumpkin.  In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, and cinnamon; add to pumpkin mixture and beat until well mixed.
Fill paper lined muffin cups 2/3 full.  Bake at 350 for 18-22 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.  Cool 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.
For filling, combine cornstarch and milk in a small saucepan until smooth.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat; cool to room temp.
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening, butter and confectioners' sugar.  Beat in vanilla.  Gradualt add the cornstarch mixture, beating until light and fluffy.
Using a sharp knife, sut a 1" circle 1" deep in the top of each cupcake. Carefull remove tops and set aside. Spoon or pipe filling into cupcakes. Replace tops.
