Title:
Slow Cooker Pot Roast with veg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of potato soup
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
1 1/4 cups beef stock
5 1/2 pounds pot roast (bottom round?)
3-4 carrots, trimmed & sliced into long ovals
1/2 bag frozen peas
8 oz. fingerling potatoes or Yukon gold potatoes, cut into 1.5-inch chunks

Directions:
Flour the roast with flour, granulated garlic, salt and pepper. Brown roast on all sides in a little olive oil in a hot skillet.
Mix cream of potato soup and dry onion soup mix in the slow cooker. Place pot roast in slow cooker and roll to coat.  Pour in beef stock to cover.
Cook on High setting for 4 hours.
Add carrots and potatoes and reduce heat to Low.  Cook for an additional 3 to 4 hours.  Add frozen peas and cook for another 1 hour before serving.
Serve with buttered egg noodles or cheese risotto.
