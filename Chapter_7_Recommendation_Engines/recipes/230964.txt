Title:
Smore's Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1177742.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup light corn syrup
1 tablespoon butter
1 (6 ounce) package miniature chocolate chips
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups honey graham cereal (such as Golden GrahamsÂ®)
1 1/2 cups miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Combine corn syrup and butter in a large pot and bring to a boil. Remove pot from heat and immediately add chocolate chips; stir until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth. Fold cereal and marshmallows into the chocolate mixture to coat.
Grease a 13x9-inch baking dish. Press cereal mixture into the dish; refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
