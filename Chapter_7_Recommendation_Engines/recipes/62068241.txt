Title:
Creamy Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of milk
1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
4 oz. cream cheese
1 1/4 to 1 3/4 cup cooked rice
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg beaten
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tbsp. butter
raisins (optional)

Directions:
Combine milk, 1/2 cup of cream, cream cheese and sugar in a non stick pot.  Stirring with a wire whisk, cook over medium heat until well combined, and simmering.  Add rice, and cook over medium heat until thickened, about 15-20 minutes, stirring frequently.  Combine remaining cream and beaten egg and add to rice mixture.  Add vanilla and nutmeg, and 1 cup raisins, if desired.  Continue to cook until thickened again, about 3-5 minutes.  Remove from heat, add butter, and stir when melted.  Serve warm or cold.

Description: 
*Very* rich and creamy rice pudding.  Not exactly low fat.  Great hot or cold.
