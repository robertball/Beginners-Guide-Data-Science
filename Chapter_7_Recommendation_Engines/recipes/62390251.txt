Title:
Saboob (Egyptian flatbread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp olive oil
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Combine flour and salt in a bowl and make a well in the center.
Add the oil and milk
Stir to combine
Turn the dough out to a lightly floured surface.
Knead until smooth.
Let rest 15 minutes.
Divide the dough into four parts.
Roll out each part thinly.
Place on a parchment lined sheet tray.
Bake 8-10 minutes until lightly browned.
