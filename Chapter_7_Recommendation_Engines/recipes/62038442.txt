Title:
microwave rice pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion chopped
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 green pepper chopped
1 carrot peeled and chopped
4 garlic cloves chopped
4 tablespoons butter
6oz. can sliced mushrooms drained
1 cup uncooked long grain rice
2 chicken boullion cubes
2 cups water
(you can substitute 2 cups chicken broth for the boullion and water if you want)

Directions:
In very large microwave safe bowl combine first 6 ingredients. Cover microwave on high for 5 minutes. Stir in remaining ingredients, cover with vented plastic wrap. microwave on high full power for 5 minutes. Reduce power to half (or medium) and continue to microwave 15 minutes. let stand 5 minutes fluff with fork.
