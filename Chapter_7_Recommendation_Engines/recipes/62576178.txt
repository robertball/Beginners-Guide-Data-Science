Title:
Pickle Roll Up Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package cream cheese, room temperature
2 tbsp sour cream
1/2 pound corned beef, chopped
1/2 cup dill pickles, chopped
Serve with crackers

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and sour cream until well blended. Add in remaining ingredients, chill for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Nia
Description: 
Why go through the hassle of rolling all those roll ups when you can just put it in a dip. So much easier. I am always asked for this recipe.
