Title:
Easy Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large russet potatoes peeled, halved then cut into large chunks
6 cloves peeled garlic smashed
1/2 stick butter cut into slices
1/2 cup half and half
1/4 cup sour cream
1 tespoon white pepper
1-2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/4 cup grated parmesan or romano cheese

Directions:
Cover potatoes and garlic with water
Gently boil till tender, about 15 -20 mins.
Drain and return to pan
Cook over medium heat for about 3 -4 mins to evaporate moisture.
Remove from heat and add butter.
Mash thoroughly
Add half and half along with sour cream and mash again
Stir in remaining ingredients
Adjust salt to taste

Submitter: 
Ken from CA
Description: 
Very easy to make and very indulging with mellow garlic flavor
