Title:
Chicken Enchilada with red sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6524312.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp chipotle salsa (or Chipotle in adobo)
1.5 c tomato sauce
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
3/4 c chicken broth or water
Salt & pepper, garlic powder, dried cilantro
Chicken
1.5 c chicken, cooked & shredded
1 tbsp olive oil
1 c onion, diced
2 cloves garlic
1tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/3 c broth or water
1/2 c tomato sauce
1 tbsp chipotle salsa
1/4 c cilantro
6 low flour tortilla
1 c shredded Mexican cheese
Top with lettuce, scallions, cilantro, sour cream

Directions:
Sauce
In medium sauce pan spray a little oil and sauté garlic.
Add 2tbsp chipotle sauce, seasoning, 3/4c  broth,  1.5 c tomato sauce.
Bring to boil, reduce heat to low and simmer 10 minutes.
Chicken
Heat oil over  medium high heat, sauté garlic and onions.
Add chicken, seasoning,  1tbsp chipotle sauce, .5c tomato sauce  and broth.
Cook 4-5 minutes.
Spray baking dish, put a little sauce on the bottom.
Put a small amount of the cheese on a tortilla,  add chicken mixture and roll up. Place seam side down in dish.
Top with sauce and half of the remaining cheese.
Cover with foil, bake at 400 for 20 minutes.
Remove from oven, switch oven to high broil, remove foil and add remaining cheese.
Broil 5-7 minutes.
Per burrito: 263 cals, 17g fat, 35g carbs, 20g protein
