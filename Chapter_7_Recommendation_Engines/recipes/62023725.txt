Title:
Honey Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3/4 cup orange juice
2 T. lemon juice
1/4 cup oil
1/2 cup honey
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1 t. curry or dried mustard
1/2 t. paprika

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Marinate chicken breasts for a few hours or overnight.  Bake at 350 until done (about 20-25 minutes).

Submitter: 
Sarah Ripley
Description: 
This is a great marinade for chicken breasts. For a more low-fat recipe, try reducing the amount of oil.  As far as I can tell, it's just used to glue the marinade to the chicken.
