Title:
Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, diced in cubes
1 onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
olive oil
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 carrot, diced
1 cup pureed sweet potato
1 generous tbsp curry powder
4 generous tsp Bisto Chip Shop Curry Sauce, prepared as directed but with broth instead of water.
6-7 mushrooms, quartered
salt & pepper
2 tbsp butter

Directions:
Heat olive oil in saucepan.
Season chicken with salt and pepper.
Add chicken to saucepan and cook until brown.
Add onion and garlic.
Once onion is cooked add chicken broth, carrot, sweet potato, curry powder and Bisto Sauce.
Simmer until carrots are soft, about 15 minutes.
Add mushrooms and simmer for 5 more minutes.
Season with salt and pepper if required.
Melt in butter just before serving.
Serve with rice or mash.

Submitter: 
Shantelle
Description: 
Adding the sweet potato to the sauce makes all the difference in the world. My husband says it's the best curry that he's ever had!
