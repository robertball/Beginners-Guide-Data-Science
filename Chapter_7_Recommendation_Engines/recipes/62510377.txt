Title:
SCD Mashed Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506386.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag cauliflower florets, or 1 medium head cauliflower
1/4 cup SCD yogurt
1/4 cup grated cheese (almost any kind works well)
1 TB butter
1 tsp finely chopped garlic
dried dill (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Steam cauliflower until soft. Drain. Mash with yogurt, cheese, butter, and spices until all well combined.

Submitter: 
lovetoeat
Description: 
Substitute for mashed potatoes for those on a carb free diet. Nothing can replace good old mashed potatoes but if your body tells you to stop, then stop you must. Cauliflower makes a comeback in my recipe box.
