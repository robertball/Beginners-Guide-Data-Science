Title:
Sunny Citrus Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/445542.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T Butter
4 boned and skinned chicken breast halves
1/4 C chicken stock or water
1/4 C Honey
1/2 C orange juice
1 T lemon juice
1 T cornstarch
1 t curry powder
1/8 t ginger
1/8 paprika
salt, and pepper

Directions:
In skillet, melt butter; brown chicken lightly on both sides; remove and set aside.
Mix together chicken stock, honey, orange juice and lemon juice; blend in cornstarch, curry powder, ginger, paprika, salt and pepper.
Pour into skillet; bring to boil, stirring as it thickens.
Return chicken to skillet; simmer until cooked through, about 5 minutes (do not overcook). Arrange chicken on platter; spoon sauce over.
Garnish: If desired, garnish with halves orange slices and sprinkle with toasted almonds.
