Title:
Enchilada's Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Dozen Corn Tortillas
1/2 C Canola Oil
3/4 C Chopped Onions
Olives, (chopped)
SAUCE:
2 C Sour Cream
1 4 Ounce Can Green Chili's, diced
1/4 C Butter
2 C Water
2 pkg George Washington's Golden Season
2 t Chicken McKay's Seasoning

Directions:
In skillet, cooked tortillas one at a time in hot oil for approximately 15 seconds on each side.
Place 2 Tablespoons grated cheese, 1-2 teaspoon each, diced onion and olives (optional) in each tortilla.
Roll up and place seam side down in baking dish.
In a sauce pan melt butter and blend in flour.
Add broth and water.
Cook till heated thru but do not boil.
Pour over tortillas in baking dish.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 20 minutes.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese on top and return to oven for 5 minutes longer or until cheese melts.
*Serve with refried beans and a garden salad
