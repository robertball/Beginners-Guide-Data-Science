Title:
Delicious Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Ounce cranberries
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup water
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat combine white sugar, brown sugar, orange juice, water and cinnamon. Stir till  sugar is dissolved. Stir in cranberries until they start to pop about 10 min. Mash cranberries with spoon to make less chunky if desired. Leave on stove an extra 10 min for thicker sauce. Remove from heat put in bowl to chill. Will thicken as it cools.

Submitter: 
Liz
Description: 
Just the right amount of sweet and tart. So perfect for Thanksgiving or Christmas.
