Title:
Smartie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/8 cup flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
3/4-1 cup Smarties/M & M's

Directions:
Mix everything together.
Drop from teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet.
Keep balls 2 inch apart.
Bake at 350 for 7 minutes.
