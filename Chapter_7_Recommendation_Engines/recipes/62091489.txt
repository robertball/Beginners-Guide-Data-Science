Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Maple Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 fresh pork tenderloin
¼ tsp. dried minced garlic
1 tsp. herb seasoning
1 Tbsp. butter
7 Tbsp. maple syrup
6 Tbsp. apple cider vinegar
1 tsp. mustard flour

Directions:
Sprinkle tenderloin with garlic and herb seasoning. Melt butter in skillet and cook tenderloin, turning to brown all sides, for 6-8 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook for 15 minutes.
Mix 6 Tbsp. maple syrup, ¼ cup vinegar, and mustard in a small bowl.
Remove tenderloin from skillet and keep warm. Raise heat; add remaining 2 Tbsp. vinegar and deglaze skillet, bringing it to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low again; return tenderloin to skillet and add maple-vinegar mixture. Turn tenderloin to coat it well about 2 minutes. Remove tenderloin and slice. Add remaining tablespoon of maple syrup to the glaze, and pour over the sliced tenderloin.

Submitter: 
Akatsukami
Description: 
A tasty, piquant yet sweet, way to treat fresh pork tenderloin.
