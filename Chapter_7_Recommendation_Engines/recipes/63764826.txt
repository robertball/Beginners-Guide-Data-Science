Title:
Ranch House Crock Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 BLSL Chicken breasts
2 (10 oz.) cans cream chicken soup, undiluted
1/4 cup sour cream (I added extra)
1 packet Hidden Vallley Ranch Dressing Mix (dry)

Directions:
Put chicken in crock pot. Mix sour cream, soup, and Ranch dry dressing mix together. Pour over chicken. Cover and cook on low 5-6 hours or high for 4 hours.
Serve gravy over potatoes or rice.
