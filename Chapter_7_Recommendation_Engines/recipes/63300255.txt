Title:
Deluxe Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large eggs
1/2 tsp baking soda
1.5 Tbsp miracle whip
1.5 Tbsp mayonnaise
3/4 tsp spicy brown mustard
1 Tbsp sweet pickle relish
salt and pepper
6 slices bread
3 slices American cheese
3 large slices tomato
6 lettuce leaves

Directions:
Place eggs in medium saucepan and fill with cold water to an inch above eggs. Add baking soda to water (this will help the eggs peel easier) Bring to a boil. Boil for 2 minutes. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand for 18 minutes. Drain. Cool eggs in ice water for 15 minutes.
Peel eggs (helps to do this under running cool water). Slice eggs vertically and horizontally in an egg slicer.
Place sliced eggs in a meduim bowl. Add miracle whip, mayonnaise, mustard, pickle relish, salt, pepper and stir until just combined.
Spread approx 2 Tbsp of egg salad on each slice of bread. Top three slices with cheese, tomato, salt and pepper, and lettuce. Place remaining slices on top.
