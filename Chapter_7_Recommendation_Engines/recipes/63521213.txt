Title:
Squash Harvest Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large butternut squash - halved and seeded
1/2 cup evaporated milk
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. allspice
1/8 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup dark brown sugar
2 tbsp. chilled butter, cut into small pieces
3 tbsp. chopped pecans
Store bought pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Place squash on foil lined cookie sheet coated with cooking spary.
Bake for about 30 minutes or until squash is tender.
Cool slightly and mash enough for 2 1/2 cups.
Combine squash and evaporated milk and mix until smooth. (Best in food processor).
Add sugar, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, allspice and cloves and mix until smooth.
Increase oven to 425.
Combine flour and brown sugar then cut in butter using 2 knives or pastry blender.
Add pecans to butter mixture and toss to coat.
Place crust in 9" deep dish pie plate, fold edges under and flute crust.
Pour squash into crust.
Bake on lowest oven rack for 15 minutes and remove from oven.
Reduce heat to 350.
Sprinkle pecan mixture on pie and shield crust.
Return to oven and bake about 40 more minutes or until center is just set.
Cool and serve with whipped cream.
