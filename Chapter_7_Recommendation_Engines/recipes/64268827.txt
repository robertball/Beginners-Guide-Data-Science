Title:
Slowcooker Cherry Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1860156.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans cherry pie filling
1 (18 1/4 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
Ice cream or whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Place the pie filling in the bottom of your slow cooker.
Mix together cake mix and melted butter.  Spread over the top of the pie filling.
Cook on LOW for 3 to 4 hours or on HIGH for 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
Serve with ice cream or whipped cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Chris Mosley
Description: 
A rich slow cooker recipe that can be modified to your taste.  You can use strawberry filling with chocolate cake, whatever you like, just keep the ratio the same and you will have a quick and easy, set and forget dessert.
