Title:
Neiman-Marcus Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C butter
12 oz chocolate chips
2 C flour
1 C brown sugar
1 C white sugar
1/2 t salt
1 t soda
1 t baking powder
4 oz Hershey bar (grated)
2 1/2 C blended oatmeal
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
1 1/2 C chopped nuts (your choice)

Directions:
In blender, grind oatmeal to a fine powder.
Cream butter and both sugars, add eggs and vanilla
Mix together flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder and soda. Add to butter mixture
Add chips, Hershey bar and nuts.
Roll into balls and place 2" apart on cookie sheet
Bake 10 min. at 375 degrees
