Title:
Smirnoff Watermelon Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981088.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Smirnoff® Watermelon Vodka
1 tablespoon cranberry juice
1 tablespoon sour mix
1 sliver watermelon

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
Add Smirnoff(R) Watermelon Vodka, cranberry juice, and sour mix.
Shake and strain into chilled martini glass.
Garnish with watermelon sliver.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add that bit of extra flavor to this or other cocktail recipes easily with the natural fruit flavors of Smirnoff® Flavored Vodkas.
