Title:
Easy Box Sour Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg white, chocolate or yellow cake mix (2 layer size)
3 eggs
1 c sour cream
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 c water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and beat with electric mixer 2 minutes. Pour into a 10-in greased tube pan and bake 45 minutes at 350F. Frost with ready to eat lemon frosting.
