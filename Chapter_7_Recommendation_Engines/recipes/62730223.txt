Title:
BABA'S CHEESE CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782940.jpg
Ingredients:
1 BOX LEMON JELLO
1 PKG.CREAM CHEESE
1CAN PACFIC MILK
1 CUP SUGAR
1 1/2 CUP GRAHAM WAFER CRUMBS
1/4 CUP MELTED MARG.

Directions:
Dissolve jello in 1 cup boiling water,let completely cool.Meanwhile whip pacfic milk till it resembles whip cream.Cream together the cream cheese and sugar,add the jello.Fold mixture into whipped milk.Pour graham crumbs into 9x13 baking dish,add the melted marg.Stir well,then spread out and press onto bottom of pan.Top with cream cheese mixture.Sprinkle with graham wafer crumbs.Let chill in fridge.
