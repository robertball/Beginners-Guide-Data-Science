Title:
Mamma's Chiken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked chicken diced
1 bay leaf
salt & pepper to taste
4-5 potatos cubed large
1 large onion cubed large
1 16oz pkg frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 package egg noodles
1 tbs Oregano
1/2 tbs Tyme
Salt & pepper to taste
64 oz chicken broth or broth from boiled chicken plus enough canned or boxed broth to make it soupy.

Directions:
Boil chicken with salt & pepper & bay leaf unitl done, discard bay leaf. Debone if necessary, reserve broth. To the broth add the diced potatos and boil until fork soft. Add package of frozen vegetables, (I use the carrot,pea,green bean mix) and add diced onion. bring to a simmer and add seasonings. Add in chicken pieces. let simmer 5 minutes unitl heated through. Add 1/2 package egg noodles and simmer until al dente.
Serve iwth your favorite corn bread or crackers.

Submitter: 
LREYNAL
Description: 
True Southern comfort food. My Mom makes chicken stew like nobody's business. Served with either a good ole pan of warm corn bread or crackers, it just feels like home.
