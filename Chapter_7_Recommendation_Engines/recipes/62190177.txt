Title:
Peach Melba Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 vanilla wafers
2 Tbsp. melted butter
1 egg white
1 1/2 cups sliced peaches
4 Tbsp. sugar
2-3 tsp. lemon juice
1 quart vanilla ice cream
1/2 cup seedless raspberry jam
1 Tbsp lemon juice
2 1/2 cups fresh raspberries
*(Optional amaretti crust instead of vanilla cookie crust))
1/4 cup toasted, chopped almonds
2 Tbsp. sugar
3/4 cups crushed amaretti cookies
2 Tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Vanilla crust:  Pulse cookies, butter and egg white in food processor 7-8 times. Press into 10-inch pie plate.  Bake 8-10 minutes at 350 degrees.  Cool, then freeze at least 30 minutes.
Wipe out processor and add peaches, 4 Tbsp. sugar and 2-3 tsp.  lemon juice and process until smooth.  Add ice cream and fold together;  freeze until set but not solid (30 minutes).  Spoon into pie shell and freeze 6 hours or more.
Melt jam over low heat; stir in 1 Tbsp. lemon juice.  Combine with raspberries in bowl and serve warm.  Drizzle some over pie slices as you serve them.
(Let pie stand 30 minutes at room temperature before serving.)
*Optional Amaretti Crust:  Process almonds and sugar until fine  Add cookies and cookies and butter and pulse 5-6 times.  Press into pie plate and bake 350 degrees for 12 minutes.  Cool, then freeze.

Submitter: 
food-dancer
Description: 
A luscious, light pie with a frozen peach filling, topped with a fresh raspberry sauce.  The crust can be vanilla cookie or *amaretti cookie crust. Both are listed below.
