Title:
Alexander's Super Candy Bar Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine softened
1 cup peanut butter
1/2 box powdered sugar
1 1/2packages graham crackers
1 (12 ounces) package chocolate chips
2 tablespoons margarine or 1/4 cup milk

Directions:
1. Combine margarine, peanut butter, powdered sugar and graham crackers.
2. Press mixture in (8"x8") pan.
3. Melt chips and 2 tablespoons of margarine or milk.
4. Spread over graham cracker crust.
5. Refrigerate.
6. Cut when cool.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes amazing irresistible chocolate candy bars!
