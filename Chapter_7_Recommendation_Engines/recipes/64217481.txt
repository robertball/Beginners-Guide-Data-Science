Title:
Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen hasbrowns
8 oz sour cream
1 can crm chicken soup
1 bag 8 oz cheddar cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and bake at 350 for 45 minutes in baking dish sprayed with Pam.
