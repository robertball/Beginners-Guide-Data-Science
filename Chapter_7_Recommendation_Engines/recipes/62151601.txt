Title:
Popcorn seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs garlic salt
2 tbs celery salt
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp powdered cumin
1 tsp curry powder
3 tbs Brewer's yeast
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Sprinkle on hot popcorn.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Diamondgirl
Description: 
This spice mix gives popcorn a garlicky and slightly smoky flavor.
