Title:
Fish with Pico Garlic Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981928.jpg
Ingredients:
Rub:
1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
2 tablespoons Spice Islands® Garlic Powder
2 tablespoons Spice Islands® Parsley Flakes
1 tablespoon Spice Islands® Cilantro
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Paprika
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Chipotle Chili
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® Sea Salt
Fish:
1 1/2 pounds tilapia fillets
1/2 cup flour
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 lime wedge

Directions:
Mix rub ingredients in a medium bowl. Set aside.
Coat fish with flour. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet on medium heat.  Place fish in skillet. Cook on one side for 3 to 4 minutes. Turn and brush on the garlic rub. Cook 3 to 4 minutes.
Remove fish from skillet and squeeze lime juice over all.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
A garlic, parsley, cilantro, paprika and chipotle chili rub transforms these tilapia fillets into a dynamite dinner option.
