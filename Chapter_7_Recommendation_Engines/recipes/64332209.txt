Title:
Chili Beans Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 pieces celery, chopped
1 can (27 oz.) Bush's Chili Beans, undrained
2 cups water
1 teaspoon chili powder (or to taste)
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper or hot pepper sauce

Directions:
In a large non-stick skillet, cook beef until lightly browned.  Push to one side of pan.  Add onion, garlic and celery and cook until soft.  Drain excess fat.  Mix in remaining ingredients and simmer 30 to 45 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
