Title:
Open-Face Sausage and Peppers Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. olive oil, divided
1 lb. of sausage links, slice into 1/2 in. pieces
1 c. Roasted red peppers from jar, drained, cut into 1/2 in. strips
3 T. balsalmic vinegar
1 t. honey
1/4 t. cayenne pepper
1 c. torn fresh basil leaves
Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper
3 Dutch rolls, split in half, toasted

Directions:
Heat 1 T. oil in skillet over medium-high heat.  Add sausages.  Cook until browned and cooked through.  (8-10 mins).  Add roasted peppers, vinegar, honey, cayenne, and remaining tablespoon of oil.  Cook & stir occasionally, until peppers are warmed through & sauce is thickened (about 4 mins).  Stir in basil and season with salt & pepper.Serve over toasted rolls
