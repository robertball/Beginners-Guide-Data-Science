Title:
Chicken Taquitos (Baked or Fried) philly cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups cooked shredded chicken
salt and pepper to taste
2 teaspoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
12 small corn tortillas
oil if frying
cooking spray if baking

Directions:
1. Place the chicken, salt, pepper, chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, cheddar cheese and Monterey Jack
cheese in a bowl. Stir until combined.
2. Wrap the tortillas in a damp paper towel and place on a plate. Microwave for 1 minute or until tortillas are
warm and pliable.
3. Lay out the tortillas on a flat surface and divide the chicken and cheese mixture among the 12 tortillas.
Roll up each tortilla tightly and secure with a toothpick.
4. To fry the taquitos: Heat 3/4 inch of oil in a deep pan to 350 degrees F. Add 3-4 taquitos and fry until crisp
and golden brown, 3-5 minutes. Repeat with remaining taquitos.
5. To bake the taquitos: Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Place the taquitos in a single layer on a sheet parchment or tin foil
pan coated with cooking spray. Spray the tops of the taquitos with the cooking spray. Bake for 11
minutes or until crispy and golden brown.
6. Remove toothpicks, then serve
