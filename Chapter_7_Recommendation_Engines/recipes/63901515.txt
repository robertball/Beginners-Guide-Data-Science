Title:
KIELBASA CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. smoked kielbasa, halved & sliced 1/2" thick
1 Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
3 (10 oz.) cans Rotel diced tomatoes with habaneros
2 (15 oz.) can chili with beans
1 (1 lb.) can kidney beans, drained
1 (8.75 oz.) can whole kernel corn, drained

Directions:
In a large Dutch oven, over medium heat, saute the kielbasa in the oil until browned, about 7-10 minutes. Add the onion and stir and cook until softened, about 3-5 minutes.
Stir in the tomatoes, chili with beans, kidney beans and corn; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer 10-15 minutes. May be kept warm, on LOW, in a slow cooker, Serve with your favorite chili toppers.
