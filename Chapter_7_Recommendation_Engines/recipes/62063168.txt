Title:
No Cook Pretzels with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup of vegetable oil
1/2 to 3/4 tsp of cayenne pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp lemon pepper
1 pkg Hidden Valley Ranch dry seasoning (the kind you mix with milk or mayonaise)
15 oz bag of pretzels (sticks or twists not the big thick braided pretzels)

Directions:
In a gallon ziploc bag mix all ingredients excepts pretzels. Gently shake back and forth to mix oil with dry ingredients. Once dry ingredients and oil are mixed together, add entire bag of pretzels. Shake to coat thoroughly. Let pretzels marinate for 30 minutes. Shake bag again and let marinate another 30 minutes. Let pretzels dry on cookie sheets lined with paper towels.

Submitter:

Description: 
A spicy snack of pretzels with no baking required. Great for parties or poker night!
