Title:
Nana Bebe's Chestnut Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c chopped, roasted chestnuts
3/4 c butter or margarine
1 c diced celery
1 c diced onion
8 c (1 loaf) slightly dry bread cubes
2 eggs
3 T milk
1 1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1 t poultry seasoning

Directions:
With a sharp knife, cut an X into each chestnut, trying to pierce through skin without breaking flesh.
Bake chestnuts for 15 minutes at 475
Cool chestnuts, shell and peel.  Break into small pieces.
Saut 1 c diced celery and 1 c onion in 3/4 c butter or margarine
When onions are soft and transparent, add chestnuts and seasonings.
Pour this mix over the bread crumbs (in a large bowl).
Beat the eggs and milk together and drizzle over the stuffing mix.  Toss.
Stuff turkey and place extra in aluminum foil in oven to cook.

Submitter: 
mrmonkmonks
Description: 
This is a family favorite...my Nana Bebe made it and now all of the girls in my family do, too.  The chestnuts give a slightly sweet, piquant flavor to a traditional stuffing.  Yum!
