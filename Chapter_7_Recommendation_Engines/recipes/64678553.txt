Title:
BAKED GARLIC PARSLEY POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4802504.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken drumsticks
1 garlic clove
1/2 tbsp. mustard
2 tsp. brown sugar
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tbsp. olive oil
pepper and salt

Directions:
1.Preheat Air Fryer to 392°F.
2.In a bowl, crush the garlic and mix with sugar.
3.Season with salt and pepper.
4.Rub the marinade over the chicken and leave for 20 minutes.
5.Place the drumsticks in the Air Fryer and cook for 10 minutes.
6.Adjust heat to 300°F and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
7.Serve and enjoy.
