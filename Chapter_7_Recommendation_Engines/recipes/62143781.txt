Title:
The Cuban Burger "FRITA" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Ground Beef
1/4 lb Ground Pork
1/2 Cup Bread Crum
1 tbsp of very fine chop onions.
1 egg
1/4 cup of milk
1 tbsp of Ketchup
2 tsp of salt
1/2 tsp of Pimenton (red Spanish style pimientos peppers)
1/2 Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 tsp of black pepper
1/2 tsp of vegetebale oil.

Directions:
Add to the ground meats the bread crumbs, the milk the egg and all other ingredients and mix very well so that all ingredients are well blended.
Once you have combined all the ingredients well, make about 12 little balls.
Let them rest in the refrigerator for about 3 hours.
Fry them on a hot frying pan with the vegetable. Until medium well or well done, flatten with spatula as you fry them.
Serve on Hamburger Bread with Ketchup and Honey Mustard and Julian fries
