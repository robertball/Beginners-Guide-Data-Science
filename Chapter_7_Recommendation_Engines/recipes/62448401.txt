Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup margarine
½ cup white sugar
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup peanut butter
1 cup flour
1 egg
½ tsp. baking powder
¼ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. baking soda
½ pkg. chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream margarine and peanut butter together. Then add white and brown sugar and eggs. Mix well.
Add the dry ingredients to the creamed mixture. Mix until smooth, and then add in chocolate chips.
Drop by spoonful onto cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 F for 8-10 minutes.
