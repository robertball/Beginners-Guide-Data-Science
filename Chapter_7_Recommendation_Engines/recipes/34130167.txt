Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. or 2 cups dry navy beans
½ tsp. soda
4 Tbsp. molasses
½ tsp. dry mustard
1 small onion, chopped
½ cup brown sugar
1/3 lb salt pork
¼ cup ketchup
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Wash beans well and cover with cold water. Boil until skin breaks (about 30 minutes). Add soda; stir well and drain.
Add clean water; bring to a boil and boil for 5 minutes. Drain and place in bean pot.
Add the rest of the ingredients and cover with hot water. Cook at 350 until beans are soft (several hours required).

Submitter: 
Jill Fisher
Description: 
Great Grandma's homemade baked beans, sweet and savory all at once.
