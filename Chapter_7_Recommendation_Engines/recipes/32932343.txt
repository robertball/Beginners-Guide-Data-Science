Title:
Sand Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 boxes Graham Cracker Crumbs
2 small boxes Vanilla Instant Pudding
8oz. Cream Cheese
8oz. Cool Whip Free
1 stick of Butter (softened)
1 cup Sugar
8 cups Milk
1 tsp. Vanilla Extract

Directions:
In a large bowl combine pudding and milk (following the instructions on the box)
Set in the refrigerator
In a separate bowl, combine cream cheese, butter and sugar until smooth.
Fold in Cool Whip into the pudding and add vanilla.
Combine both mixtures until smooth.  Layer starting with pudding and graham cracker crumbs ending with crumbs on top.  Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Allison
Description: 
Vanilla pudding mixture with graham cracker crumb layers.  Similar to a cheese cake kind of taste with a smooth pudding texture.  Always a hit!!
