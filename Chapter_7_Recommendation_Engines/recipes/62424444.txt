Title:
Daiq-Attack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C light rum
2 T frozen limeade concentrate
1 medium banana
1/2 C pineapple chunks
1 1/2 C crushed ice
sugar (optional)
lime slices

Directions:
In blender container combine rum, limeade, banana, pineapple and ice.
Blend until slushy.
Sweeten to taste if necessary.
Garnish with lime.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar

Refreshing summer drink- just what is needed for Innsbrook
