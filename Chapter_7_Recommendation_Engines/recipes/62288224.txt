Title:
Garlic Braised Chicken with Olives and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small chicken cut in pieces
salt and pepper to taste
1 and 1/2 tblspn olive oil
2 heads garlic smashed and peeled
1 10 oz package mushrooms, halved
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup green olives
1/2 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Heat skillet over emdeium high het. Add 1 tablespoon oil. Add chicken to pan, season with salt and pepper. Brown 5-6 minutes. Remove from pan and set aside.
Add 1 teaspoon oil to pan. Add garlic and mushrooms. Brown 5-6 minutes.
Add wine to mushrooms and garlic, bring to a boil, cook 1 minutes. Return Chicken to pan.
Add olives and chicken stock. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through, 15-20 minutes.
