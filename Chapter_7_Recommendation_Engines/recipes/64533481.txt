Title:
Crowning Glory Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cream Puff
1 cup water
1/2 cup butter
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. sugar
1 cup flour
4 large eggs
Chocolate Filling
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
4 oz. unsweetened chocolate
2 tsp. vanilla
6 large eggs
Chocolate Glaze
2 oz. semi-sweet chocolate
2 Tbs. butter

Directions:
Cream Puff
Bring first four ingredients to a boil then take off heat. Add flour. Beat in 4 eggs one at a time. Spread into a 9" circle. Bake at 400* for 40 minute on a buttered pizza pan. When cooled slice in half to create a top and bottom. Fill with filling after it chills.
Chocolate filling
Cream butter and sugar. Beat in chocolate and vanilla. Beat in eggs one at a time for 2 minutes each. Chill.
Chocolate Glaze
Melt together choc and butter. Spread on top of cream filled puff. Top with Whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Wendy Wright Martin
Description: 
Heavenly cream puff and chocolate yumminess. We would only make this once a year for my mom's birthday.
