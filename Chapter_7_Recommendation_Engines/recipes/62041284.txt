Title:
Kahlua Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups sugar
4 cups water
1/4 cup instant coffee granules
2 cups water
1/5 of vodka
1/2 cup vanilla extract

Directions:
-Combine the sugar and 4 cups water.
-Boil for 10 minutes.
-Set aside.
-Combine coffee and 2 cups water.
-Boil for 8 minutes.
-Add to sugar water.
-Let sit until room temperature.
-Add vodka and vanilla to coffee mixture.
-Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Robbie Rice
Description: 
Save money making this liquer at home. It also makes great holiday gifts.
