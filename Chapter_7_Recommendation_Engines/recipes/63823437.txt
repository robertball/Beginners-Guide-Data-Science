Title:
Iced Cinnamon Raisin Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Biscuits:
2 1/2 c. Bisquick mix
1/2 c. raisins
2/3 c. milk
2 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
Icing:
2/3 c. powdered sugar
1 tsp. warm water
1/4 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix Bisquick, sugar, and cinnamon with milk, just until combined.  Add raisins.  Knead dough about 10 times.  Roll out to 1/2 inch thickness.  Cut with round cutter.  Bake at 450 for 8-10 min.  Mix icing ingredients until a glaze forms.  Drizzle glaze over warm biscuits.

Submitter: 
Karen Zastrow
Description: 
A quick and easy breakfast item...reminiscent of a famous fast-food version.
