Title:
Cranberries In A Cloud Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. fresh cranberries
2 c. mini marshmallows
3/4 c. sugar
2 c. cubed green apple
1/2 c. green seedless grapes
1 c. whipping cream (whipped)
1/2 c. chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
In food processor, chop cranberries til coarsely ground.
Combine cranberries, marshmallows and sugar in a large bowl and stir gently.
Cover and chill for 1 1/2 hours.
Add apples and grapes and stir.
Fold in whipped cream
Cover and chill 1 hour.

Submitter: 
thebakedbaker
Description: 
Fluffy and flavorful and crunchy, this is a nice change of pace. I like to think of it as a Thanksgiving ambrosia.
