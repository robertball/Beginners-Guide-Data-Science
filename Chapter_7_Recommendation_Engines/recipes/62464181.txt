Title:
Smokey Creamy Tuscan White Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/473934.jpg
Ingredients:
2	slices bacon, chopped
1	medium stalk celery, quartered lengthwise
1	medium carrot, quartered lengthwise
2	cloves garlic, lightly crushed
2 ½	to 3 cans cannellini beans
1	dried bay leaf
¼	cup dry white wine
4	cups chicken broth
1	tablespoon chicken base
Fresh ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
1.	In 4-quart saucepan or Dutch oven, cook bacon, onion, celery, carrot and 2 cloves garlic over medium-high heat 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to medium. Add beans, bay leaf, wine and broth; cover and cook 20 to 25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender. Remove from heat; cool about 15 minutes.
2.	Remove bay leaf from bean mixture. Pour mixture into food processor; cover and puree. Return to saucepan; stir in salt and pepper. Simmer over medium heat 5 to 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until thoroughly heated.
3.	Ladle soup into individual soup bowls.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
thick pureed white bean soup, with smokey bacon, garlic, carrot, and celery.  and freezes wonderfully.
