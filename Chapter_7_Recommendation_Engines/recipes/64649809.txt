Title:
Cheesy Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz pkg shredded hash browns
1 (10.75 oz) can cream of chicken soup
2 c shredded cheddar cheese
3/4 c sour cream
1/4 c chopped onion
1/4 c butter, melted
1.5 c cornflakes, crushed
2 T butter, melted

Directions:
1.  Spray a square baking dish.  In a large bowl combine all ingredients EXCEPT cornflakes and 2T butter; mix well.
2.  Spread into prepared dish.
3.  In a small bowl, stir together topping ingredients (cornflakes and 2T butter.)  Spread evenly over the hash browns.
4.  Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes or until hash browns are tender.
