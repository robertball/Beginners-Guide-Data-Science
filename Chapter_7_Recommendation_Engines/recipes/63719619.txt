Title:
Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies- No flour or Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Peanut Butter
3/4 Cup Brown sugar
1/4 Agave
1 egg
1 tsp baking soda
a little salt
1 1/4 Cups quick cooking oats

Directions:
Preheat oven 350, line pans with parchment paper. Beat peanut butter and sugar. Beat in egg, baking soda, salt. Stir in oats. Bake for 8 minutes. Cool.
