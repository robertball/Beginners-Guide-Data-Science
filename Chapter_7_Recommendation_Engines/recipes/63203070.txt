Title:
Spicy Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/829226.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 c diced onion
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
2 Tbsp sriracha
2 tsp red pepper flakes
4 Tbsp creamy peanut butter
3 Tbsp ketchup
1/2 c water
2 tsp sesame oil
3 tsp white sugar

Directions:
Heat olive oil over med heat.  Add onion, garlic, sriracha, and red pepper.  Cook 5 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients.  Stir until smooth.  Cook over low heat for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
Slightly sweet, very spicy, this surprising sauce is nutty and delicious!  Great for everything from chicken and seafood to dipping spring rolls.  A favorite in our house!
