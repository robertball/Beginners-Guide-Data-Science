Title:
Beef Brisket Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2   Tbsp Lucini Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2   cloves garlic, peeled, finely chopped
1   lb Idaho potatoes, peeled, very finely chopped (3 cups)
1/4   lb carrots, peeled, finely chopped (1/2 cup)
1/4   lb celery, finely chopped (1/2 cup)
1/4   lb Spanish onion, peeled, finely chopped (1/2 cup)
3   cups finely chopped, cooked Beef Brisket
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Heat large ovenproof skillet on MEDIUM HIGH. Add oil; swirl to coat. Add garlic and potatoes, cook 3 min, turning occasionally, until lightly browned. Add carrots, celery, and onion, cook 3 min. Add brisket; cook, without turning, 5 min. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Place skillet in oven and bake 10 min, until lightly browned.
