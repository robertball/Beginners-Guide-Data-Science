Title:
Fran's Butternut Squash Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056230.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. butternut squash steamed and mashed
3 eggs
1/2 cup butter softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup whole milk
1 1/2 cup self rising flour
1tsp baking soda
Ground nutmeg to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, (170 degrees C).
Peel and cube squash and then steam it until soft. Pur√©e it with a potato masher and let cool until room temperature.
In a bowl cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time (eggs should be at room temperature) until batter looks foamy. Add milk slowly and always beating. Fold in baking soda, nutmeg and the squash.
Add the flour while folding batter gently and from the bottom of the bowl up.
Pour batter into muffin cups until 3/4 full. Bake about 18/20 min or until toothpick inserted comes out clean
