Title:
Italian Sausage with Rotini and Italian Herbed Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 links turkey italian sausage, casings removed and crumbled
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 (15 oz) can Contadina tomato sauce with italian herbs
1 cup roasted broccoli
1/2 onion, chopped
1 tsp minced garlic
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 (12 oz) box whole wheat rotini pasta

Directions:
Prepare pasta per package directions.
In a large skillet sauté italian sausage in olive oil.  Brown until no longer pink.  Add onion and garlic and sauté.  Add crushed red pepper.  Once the onions are translucent add broccoli and heat through.  Add tomato sauce and heat through.
Top with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a delicious slightly spicy quick and easy sausage and pasta dish that is great on a busy weeknight.
