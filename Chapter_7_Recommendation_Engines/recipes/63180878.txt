Title:
Broccoli, Tomato and Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large or 2 medium broccoli heads
3 medium tomatoes
1 medium onion, any color,finely chopped
4 hard-boiled free-range eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. mustard
2-3 Tbs. apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Hard boil the eggs about 15 minutes and remove shells and chop fine.
Steam the broccoli until just tender. Do not overcook. Break into bite size pieces.
Cut tomatoes into bite size pieces.
Chop onion finely.
In a separate bowl combine mayonnaise, salt, mustard and apple cider vinegar. Whisk until smooth. Combine all vegetables in a big bowl, pour on the dressings and mix together gently.

Submitter:

Description: 
Most everyone loves Broccoli prepared this way with hard-boiled eggs, onions,and tomatoes with a mayonnaise dressing
