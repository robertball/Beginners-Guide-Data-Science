Title:
Carrot Juice Float Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2891291.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large carrots
1/4 cup vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Wash the carrots and trim the tops off, then juice using a juice machine.  Pour the carrot juice over the ice cream to serve.

Submitter: 
Autumn Leaves
Description: 
This is so simple and you won't believe how delicious the combination is. It makes a healthier alternative to that old fashioned root beer float.
