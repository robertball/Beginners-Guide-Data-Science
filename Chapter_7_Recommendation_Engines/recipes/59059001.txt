Title:
Budget Mom's Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/265962.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
2 eggs
1/2 cup margarine
3/4 cups white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups grated zucchini

Directions:
Grease one  8 x 4 inch pan.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Sift flour, salt, baking powder, soda, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Cream together margarine and sugar together in a large bowl.  Add sifted ingredients to the creamed mixture, and beat well.  Stir in zucchini until well combined.  Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
This recipe is a great way to use up the abundant zucchini that seems to end up on my doorstep at the end of the summer.  I cut the cost of this bread by using margarine instead of the vegetable oil and left out the costly walnuts.Modified from: Mom's Zucchini Bread
