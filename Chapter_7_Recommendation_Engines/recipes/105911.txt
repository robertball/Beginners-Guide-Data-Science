Title:
Grilled Thai Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons canola oil
1/4 cup light soy sauce
2 teaspoons rice vinegar
1 tablespoon fresh minced ginger
1 tablespoon sesame oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon pepper
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Combine canola oil, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, sesame oil, garlic and pepper in a sealable plastic bag.
Add chicken to bag and squeeze bag to coat chicken; seal bag.
Let stand 20-30 minutes in the refrigerator.
Remove chicken from bag; discard marinade.
Grill over medium heat for 12-15 minutes or until cooked through.

Submitter: 
The Canola Info Virtual Kitchen
Description: 
Canola oil's high smoke point ensures that foods cook up light and crispy and its clear taste allows the flavor of other foods to shine through.
