Title:
Beefy Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound sausage
1 pkg medium taco seasoning
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
2 pounds processed cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a large skillet, season with seasoning salt and brown beef and sausage. Drain excess fat.
In another large skillet, mix soup, milk, salsa and processed cheese over a low heat. Continue stirring until cheese melts.
Add meat to the cheese mixture and simmer uncovered until ready to serve.
