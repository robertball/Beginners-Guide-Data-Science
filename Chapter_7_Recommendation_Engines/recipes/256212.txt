Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Tot Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4148644.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Green Giant™ Broccoli Veggie Tots
1 (12 ounce) package refrigerated chicken nuggets
20 grape tomatoes
7 sticks string cheese, each cut into 3 pieces
10 (10 inch) bamboo skewers
1 cup pasta sauce, heated

Directions:
Cook Green Giant® Broccoli Veggie Tots and chicken nuggets according package directions.
Alternately thread Green Giant® Broccoli Veggie Tots™, chicken nuggets, grape tomatoes, and string cheese onto skewers.
Serve with hot pasta sauce for dipping.

Submitter: 
Green Giant
Description: 
Put a little fun into dinnertime with these Chicken and Tot Kabobs. It's a great way to get the kids involved. They can assemble their own kabobs and even add some of their own favorite ingredients. And they're not just great for dinner.  Make these with mini skewers for snacks or a simple appetizer to liven up your next party!
