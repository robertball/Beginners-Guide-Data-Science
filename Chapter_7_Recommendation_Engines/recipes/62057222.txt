Title:
GREEK POTATO SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP REGULAR OR LIGHT MAYONNAISE
1 CLOVE GARLIC MINCED
1 TBSP. FINELY CHOPPED OREGANO OR 1 TSP DRIED.
PINCH OF BLACK PEPPER AND HOT PEPPER FLAKES
16 SMALL NEW POTATOES - COOKED AND PEELED
8 CHERRY TOMATOES - HALVED
1 GREEN PEPPER DICED
4 OZ. FETA CHEESE COARSLEY CHOPPED
CUCUMBER SLICES
BLACK OLIVES

Directions:
IN SMALL BOWL COMBINE MAYO, GARLIC, OREGANO, SALT, PEPPER AND HOT PEPPER FLAKES.
IN LARGE BOWL, CUT POATOES IN CHUNKS (ABOUT 3 CUPS).  MIX IN TOMATOES, GREEN PEPPERS AND CHEESE.
ADD DRESSING, TOSS GENTLY TO COAT.
GARNISH WITH CUCUMBERS AND OLIVES.
