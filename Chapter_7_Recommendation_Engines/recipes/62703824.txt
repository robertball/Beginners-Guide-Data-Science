Title:
Frosty Strawberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
* 1 3 oz. package strawberry flavored gelatin
* 2 C. sliced fresh strawberries
* 2 C. vanilla ice cream
* 1 1/4 C. boiling water
* 1 9-inch prepared graham cracker crust

Directions:
Directions
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water and gradually add ice cream, stirring until melted. Note, if pie is to be chilled 3-4 hours before serving, increase to 1 1/2 C. water. Chill until thick but not set (15-25 minutes) and then fold in strawberries and pour into pie crust. Chill until firm; garnish with whipped cream and walnut halves.
Variation: You can substitute sugar-free gelatin. Be sure to keep it refrigerated!
Yield: 8 servings
