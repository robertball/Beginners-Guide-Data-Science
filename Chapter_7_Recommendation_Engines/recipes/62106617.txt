Title:
Chicken Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of crescent rolls
2 large, skinless, boneless, chicken breast
1 pkg. cream cheese
1/4 - 1/2 cup green onion tops chopped
1

Directions:
In a skillet, cook chicken in small amount of oil.  Remove and shred.  Place back in skillet and add cream cheese and green onions.  Mix thoroughly.  Roll out crescent rolls and pinch center to form a rectangle with two pieces.  Equally divide mixture among the four rectangles and fold over to form square pocket.  Pinch ends together well and pinch any other seams shut.  Bake according to the crescent roll directions.  Eat hot.

Submitter: 
cmccreight
Description: 
Super easy and delicious chicken pies.  Please note, these do not save very well.  They must be eaten immediately.
