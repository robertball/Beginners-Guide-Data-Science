Title:
Genevieve's Mexican Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T EVOO
8 oz. onion, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 clove garlic, diced
3 lbs skinless chicken thighs
15 oz chicken broth
14 1/2 oz Mexican style stewed tomatoes
10 oz. red enchilada sauce
1 C. flour
1/2 C. yellow cornmeal
1 T. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
2 T. pickled jalapeno, chopped
3 T. butter, melted
3/4 C. milk

Directions:
Heat oil in a 5-6 qt pan or dutch oven.
When hot add onion, bell pepper and garlic.
Cook 1-2 minutes and add chicken thighs.
Brown thighs, about 2 minutes each side to get a nice, brown crust on both side.
Add broth, tomatoes and  enchilada sauce to pan and deglaze.
Bring to boil then reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally until meat is no longer pink at the bone (about 30-40 minutes)
Meanwhile in a bowl, mix flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt and jalapenos.
Ina small bowl whisk butter into milk and stir into flour mixture until blended.
Drop batter in tablespoon portions into simmering chicken mixture.
Cover and simmer gently until dumplings are cooked all the way through, 10-12 minutes.
Ladle chicken, dumplings and sauce equally into wide shallow bowls and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This twist on the traditional chicken and dumplings is sure to add a zesty twist to your table!
