Title:
Garden Herb Crusted Salmon and Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/816301.jpg
Ingredients:
HERB CRUSTED SALMON
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (have done it without)
2 tablespoons fresh rosemary
2 tablespoons of fresh tarragon
2 tablespoons of fresh oregano
1 tablespoon of fresh thyme
1.5 pound of salmon cut in to four fillets
2 garlic cloves minced
2 tablespoons of olive oil
DILL SAUCE
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons honey mustard
1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon chopped tarragon
1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
1/4 minced garlic
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Herb Crusted Salmon
1-Combine first five ingredients (parsley, rosemary, tarragon, oregano, and thyme in shallow dish. Dredge fish in mixture. Do not coat skin side of fish.
2-Cook garlic in ovenproof skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds. Add fish coated side down and cook five minutes until browned on coated side.
3-Turn salmon, skin side down in skillet and place uncovered in oven. Bake at 350* degrees for 7 minutes until fish flakes with fork.

Description: 
For anyone that is does not like fish they will love this dish. I have cooked this a number of times and it is one of my favorite salmon recipes. This is relatively easy to make and quick.
