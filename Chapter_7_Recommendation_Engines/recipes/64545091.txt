Title:
Vegetarian Greek Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz veggie pasta (such as Wacky Mac)
7 oz jar grilled, marinated artichoke hearts, chopped
1/2 small red onion, sliced
1/2 large cucumber, diced
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup kalamata olives, halved
1/3 cup feta cheese, crumbled
1/4 Tsp fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Serve immediately or cover and chill. Garnish with Greek salad dressing.

Submitter: 
Nathan Hendrix
Description: 
So much goodness packed into a single dish! With so many flavor profiles, there really is no need for sauce or seasoning.
