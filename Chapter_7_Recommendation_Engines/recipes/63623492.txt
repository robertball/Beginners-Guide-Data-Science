Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tomato, cubed
1 cup shredded lettuce
1/2 teaspoon cilantro
1 mango, cubed
1/2 red onion, chopped
1 avocado, cubed
1/3 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
1/8 cup lime juice
1 tablespoon olive oil
7 tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix the first 7 ingredients together in a large bowl.
In another smaller bowl, mix together the lime juice and olive oil.
Drizzle the lime/olive oil mixture into the larger bowl.
Crumble up the tortilla chips, and sprinkle on top.

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
If you're craving a healthy Mexican meal, then this salad is for you. The combination of the light crisp lettuce, the creamy avocado, the sweet juicy mango, the nutty beans, the tangy lime  juice, and the tortilla chip crunch, makes this salad to die for!
