Title:
Gluten Free Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz gluten free penne or elbow macaroni
4 tbsp butter
1 1/4 tsp salt
3/4 tsp dry mustard
4 cups milk
1/4 cup cornstarch
4 cups shredded cheese
if you desire a crunchy topping:
2 slices gluten free bread, toasted
1 tsp butter, softened
1/2 tsp paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Grease 13 x 9 casserole dish.
Cook macaroni according to package directions, however cook macaroni a few minutes less than package directions, leaving pasta al dente
Drain pasta, set aside.
Melt butter in medium saucepan.
Stir in salt and dry mustard; remove from heat and set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, whisk corn starch into milk until smooth.
Stir milk into butter mixture and whisk until well-blended.
Stirring constantly, cook over medium heat until thickened (about 5 minutes or so) and remove from heat.
Stir three cups of shredded cheese into sauce until melted; reserve one cup of cheese for topping.
Combine cheese mixture with macaroni and spoon into prepared pan.
If you do not desire a crunchy topping, place reserved shredded cheese on top and bake uncovered for 30 min
If you do desire a crunchy topping:
break up toast into crumbs or use food processor.
Combine butter and paprika and 1 cup cheese.
Sprinkle on top and bake for 30 min, or until crunchy
