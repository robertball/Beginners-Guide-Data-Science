Title:
Kelly's Super Simple Shower Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970524.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint raspberry sorbet
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Spoon the sorbet into the bottom of a punch bowl. Slowly pour the soda over the sorbet. Use the back of a spoon to soften the sorbet until combined.

Submitter: 
Wendi Leitzell
Description: 
This is a fabulously simple and delicious punch. It has only 2 ingredients!
