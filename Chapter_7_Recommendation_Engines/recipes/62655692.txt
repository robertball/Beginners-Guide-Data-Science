Title:
Mayo-Free Deli-Style Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/589259.jpg
Ingredients:
4 hard boiled eggs
1/4 sweet onion or large shallot, finely chopped
2 ribs celery, no leaves, chopped very fine
1 tbsp dijon mustard
Kosher salt to taste
Fresh ground pepper to taste
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp fresh dill, chopped

Directions:
Slice the eggs...do not chop super fine. Add onion, celery, mustard, olive oil, and dill. Mix thoroughly. Add salt and pepper to taste. Chill before serving on bread.

Submitter: 
Army Wife
Description: 
I had the best egg salad sandwich of my life at a Kosher deli in Portland, OR! It was mayoless and insanely delicious. I *think* I have finally duplicated the recipe. Enjoy!!
