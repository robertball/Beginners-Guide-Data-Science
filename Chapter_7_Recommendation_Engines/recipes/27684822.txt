Title:
Layered Tortilla Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 8" flour tortillas
1 cup vegetarian refied beans
1-1/2 cups salsa
1-1/2 cups fajita chicken strips thawed
1 cup corn, thawed and drained
1 cup shredded low-fat Mexican cheese
1/2 cup chopped green onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  In a lightly greased 9" pie plate, place one flour tortilla.
Spread tortilla with 1/3 cup refried beans amd 1/3 cup salsa.
Sprinkle evenly wirh 1/2 cup chicken, 1/3 cup corn, 1/4 cup shredded cheese and 2 TBS. green onions.  repeat layering twice, ending with tortilla.
Spread remaining 1/2 cup salsa over tortilla.  Top with remaining cheese and green onion.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until hot.  Serve with light sour cream if desired.
