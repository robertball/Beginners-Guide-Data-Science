Title:
Sid's Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955749.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 cup shortening
3/4 cup milk
2 eggs
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
Preheat to 375 degrees.
Cream together shortening and sugar. Then Mix in eggs and beat well.
Sift together flour, powder, soda, and salt. Add to shortening in thirds. Add 1/4 cup of milk in between.
Bake for 10 minutes on a cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
Sidnie Anderson
Description: 
Best sugar cookies I've ever had. Really pretty simple recipe and good for if you are going to use cookie cutters.
