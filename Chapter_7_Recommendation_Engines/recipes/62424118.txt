Title:
Batter Fried Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Bisquick
1 pkg itallian bread crumbs
1 dash hot sauce
2 eggs
1 can beer
1.5  pound whole mushrooms
Deep fryer filled with oil

Directions:
Wash the mushrooms, set aside
Beat the egg and add hot sauce, set aside.
Roll the bread crumbs and bisquick together
Add the beer and egg to the flour to make a batter
Heat oil in the deep fryer to 285 degrees (F)
Take each mushroom, roll in the batter.
Roll the mushroome through the batter again
Deep fry until golden brown and crispy
