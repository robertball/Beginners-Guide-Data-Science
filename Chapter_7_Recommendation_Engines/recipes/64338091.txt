Title:
Vegetable Rescue and Kielbasa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2073946.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1 lb. kielbasa, cut into slices
2 yellow skin potatoes, cut into small chunks
2 medium carrots, peeled and cut into slices
1 small head cabbage, chopped
1 sweet onion, sliced
1/3 cup frozen petit green peas
1/3 cup frozen sweet white corn
2 tablespoons unsalted butter or margarine
Seasoning salt or Steak seaoning

Directions:
Prepare potatoes and carrots. Bring a pot of water to boil and add potatoes. When potatoes are just starting to soften, add the sliced carrots and finish cooking until vegetables are tender but not too soft. Drain.
Heat a large nonstick skillet, add butter. Saute onion and cabbage until tender. Add kielbasa slices and frozen vegetables. Stir to combine. Add seasoning salt as desired.

Submitter: 
Kathy Young
Description: 
I created this when I was trying to use up odds and ends of vegetables- hence the rescue in the name. I love that it is fast to get to the table, my family loves it, and we are getting in great variety and portions of vegetables.
