Title:
Kluay Buad Chee (Bananas in Cocount milk) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 bananas
1 cup coconut milk
5 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Separate the coconut milk,cut the bananas into 4 parts.
Boil the coconut milk.
upon boiling add sugar and salt and add thick coconut milk add bananas and bring it to boil.
remove from heat
serve it cold or hot
if you using canned coconut milk add 1 part water to thin it,
choose bananas that are not too ripe.use any bananas but thai bananas are recommended.there are many varities of bananas grown in Thailand such as Kluay Hom,Kluay Nam wa,Kluay Hak mook,and Kluay thai etc.
