Title:
Hobo Campfire Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Use left over Hobo's Delight OR
2 pounds turkey Kielbasa
1 onion, sliced
1 (16 ounce) package baby carrots
4 potatoes, peeled and sliced
16 Tablespoons butter
seasoned salt to taste
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat up a skillet with some fat (butter, Pam spray, etc.)
Warm up Hobo's Delight in a skillet
OR
Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
Slice Kielbasa into medallions and toss into foil large enough to hold all of the ingredients. Season with pats of butter, seasoned salt, salt, and pepper to taste. Wrap foil around food, and seal each packet tightly.
Grill 30 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender. Carefully open each packet (the steam that escapes will be very hot), and serve.
Warm up in a skillet.
