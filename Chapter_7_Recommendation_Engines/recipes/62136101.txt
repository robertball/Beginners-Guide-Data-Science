Title:
Memphis Dry Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Sugar
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
1/4 cup Paprika
3 tbsp Salt
1 tbsp Pepper
1 tbsp Garlic Powder
1tbsp Onion Powder
1 tbsp Chili Powder
1 tbsp Cayenne

Directions:
Mix all ingreedients together in a bowl.  Rub into raw meat and prepare.

Submitter: 
Nikki Anderson
Description: 
This is a wonderful and spicy rub that goes great on beef or pork ribs! Put it on ribs lightly to get a nice kick and put it on heavy to get a bold hot dish!
