Title:
Orange Chicken II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1 cup water
1/3 cup + 1 tablespoon packed brown sugar
5 tablespoons soy sauce
5 tablespoons ketchup
5 tablespoons mandarin orange juice
1 tablespoon mandarin orange peel
2 1/2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
8 cloves garlic (or 1 tbs garlic powder)
3/4 teaspoon crushed chili flakes
1/16 teaspoon clove
1/16 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon lemon pepper
1/4 medium red pepper (approx 3-4 tablespoons)
Chicken:
2 1/4 pounds chicken breast
1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 Tablespoon cornstarch
2 tablespoon cold water

Directions:
Cut chicken breast into cubes. Heat olive oil at medium heat in a skillet and add chicken breast. Cook for 10 minutes. Turn to high and brown chicken, then turn heat back down to medium. (Chicken will cook completely when sauce is added).
Start cooking rice at this point. (takes approx 20 minutes).
Combine all sauce ingredients and heat in a pot until brown sugar is dissolved. Add to chicken and turn heat to medium-high. Cook uncovered for 15 minutes to boil off excess water until sauce starts to thicken. Mix cornstarch in 2 tablespoons of sauce, then whisk into sauce in skillet. Cook for 2 minutes and serve.
