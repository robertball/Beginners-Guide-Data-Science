Title:
Rich Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887302.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups butter (no substitutes), softened
9 cups confectioners' sugar
2 1/2 cups baking cocoa
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter.
Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar, cocoa and vanilla.
Add enough milk until frosting reaches spreading consistency.
