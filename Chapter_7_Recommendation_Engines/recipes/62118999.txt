Title:
Kelly's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 lb beef stew meat
4 medium potatos, peeled and chopped into 2" pieces
2 cups baby carrots
1 large onion
2 T. sugar
2 T. oil
1/2 cup flour
salt
pepper
Base:
2 cups water
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 beef boullion cubes
1 bay leaf
1 T. dried parsley
1 T. dried basil

Directions:
Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
Put flour in a ziplock bag, drop in meat in batches and shake to coat.  Shake off excess before adding to pan.  Add onions to pan.  Season with salt and pepper
Lightly brown meat (it will cook thouroughly in the pressure cooker) for about 5 minutes and add sugar
Remove pressure cooker rack (if it has one) and heat all ingredients of the base.  bouillion is dissolved, replace rack.
Layer potatos, carrots and meat mixture in the pressure cooker, seasoning with salt and pepper
Seal with lid and turn stove to high heat.  Once the pressure cooker gets going, reduce to medium heat and cook for 8 minutes.
Let cool according to your pressure cooker's directions.
Dump all of the contents into a large bowl, so the ingredients are lathered in the gravy.

Submitter: 
Kel Kel
Description: 
My mother used to make beef stew in a pressure cooker at least once a week.  I crave it all the time, and have doctored up the flavor so it's no longer just meat and potatos tasting
