Title:
Low carb coffee frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of cold brewed coffee. (strength is up to you but the stronger the better)
3-4 large scoops of sugar-free vanilla (or chocolate) icecream.
1/2 cup heavy cream.

Directions:
blend all of the ingrediants in a blender until combined and smooth. Enjoy!
