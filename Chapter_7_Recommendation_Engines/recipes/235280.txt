Title:
Coffee and Cream Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1091436.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ice cubes, or as desired
1 1/2 fluid ounces coffee-flavored liqueur
1 1/2 fluid ounces Irish cream liqueur

Directions:
Fill a small glass with ice cubes; pour in coffee-flavored liqueur and Irish cream liqueur. Stir well.

Submitter: 
kellieann
Description: 
Delicious blend of coffee and cream liqueurs. If you don't stir the two liqueurs together they will layer for a different presentation.
