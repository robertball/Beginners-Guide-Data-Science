Title:
Breakfast Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C Milk
1/3C Non-fat instant milk
2Tbs Frozen orange juice concentrate
1 Peeled and frozen banana (broken in pieces)
1tsp vanilla
1 Raw egg (optional)

Directions:
Add all ingredients except banana into blender.  Blend on medium speed and add banana chunks one at a time until mixture is smoothe.  Increase speed as needed to blend well.  Pour into chilled glasses and enjoy!
Variation:You can also substitute 1 package of carnation instant breakfast drink for the powdered milk AND orange juice concentrate

Submitter: 
A Baker's Dozen
Description: 
Great anytime!  This shake is packed with calcium and great for summer mornings and pregnant women (which is when I first started drinking them). You can modify to suit your own taste.
