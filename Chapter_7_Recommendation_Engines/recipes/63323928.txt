Title:
Bacon Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/880414.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon
2 stalks of celery
20 oz. package fresh green peas
1/2 cup cubed Cheddar cheese
1/3 cup chopped onions
For the dressing:
3/4 cup mayo dressing
1 T. honey
1 T. bacon grease
1 T. sugar
S & P to taste

Directions:
Place bacon in a skillet over medium-high heat, and cook until evenly brown. Save grease. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
Rinse peas under cold running water. Assemble dressing.
In a bowl, toss together bacon, peas, onion, celery, dressing, and Cheddar cheese. Refrigerate 30 minutes or until chilled before serving.

Submitter: 
CarolTyson
Description: 
Peas, bacon, cheese, onions, celery and mayo dressing are tossed together and chilled in this great summer salad.Modified from: Bacon Ranch Pea Salad
