Title:
Peppery Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves, boneless & skinless
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp honey
1 tsp olive oil
1 Tbsp cider vinegar
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 cup mushrooms, sliced (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Place chicken in a small bowl. Mix together all ingredients except chicken and pour over chicken.  Marinate for at least 1 hour.
Transfer chicken to a greased casserole.  Pour sauce over chicken and bake for 30-45 minutes or until done.  If desired, add sliced mushrooms during the last 5 minutes of baking.
