Oven-Baked Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:
2 tablespoon salted butter
1 cup Original BisquickÂ® mix
1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1 1/4 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 tbsp garlic pwdr
1 tbsp onion pwdr
1 tbsp dried sage
1 (3 to 3 1/2 pound) cut-up whole chicken

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Melt butter in rectangular baking dish, 13x9x2 inches, in oven.
Stir together Bisquick and1/2 the spices.Season chicken with other halve. Coat  with seasoned bisquick. Place skin sides down in dish (dish and butter should be hot).
Bake 35 minutes; turn chicken. Bake about 15 minutes longer or until juice is no longer pink when centers of thickest pieces are cut.
