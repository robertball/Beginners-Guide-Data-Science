Title:
Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
7 slices bacon
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
½ cup onion, chopped
½ cup celery, chopped
½ cup red bell pepper, chopped
1 can cream-style corn
1 can whole kernel corn
1 can evaporated milk
1 cup chicken broth
½ cup Pepper Jack cheese, shredded
¼ cup dry sherry
1 tsp. ground cayenne pepper
1 tsp. dried parsley
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Directions
In a large soup pot, fry bacon until crispy. Remove and set aside, leaving the bacon grease.
Add garlic, onions, celery, bell pepper, and flour.  Cook on high, stirring occasionally, for 5-10 minutes.
Now add the corn, milk, chicken broth, sherry, cayenne, parsley, salt, and pepper.
Mix thoroughly and let simmer on low for 15 minutes.
Finally, add the shredded cheese, and mix well. When the cheese is melted, remove from the heat and serve immediately.
Garnish with chopped bacon bits.

Sandra Holding

Thick and flavorful comfort food for those cold winter nights.
