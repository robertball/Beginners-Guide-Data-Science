Title:
Pork Adobo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pork tenderloin, cut in chunks
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 onion, sliced
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp cider vinegar
1 tsp brown sugar
1 bay leaf
1/8 tsp salt
Ground black pepper to taste
Water

Directions:
Place pork in saucepan.  Add garlic, onions, soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, bay leaf, salt, pepper, and mix well.  Add water, just enough to cover meat. Bring to a boil, then simmer 45 minutes or until done.  (You can use chicken in place of pork.)
