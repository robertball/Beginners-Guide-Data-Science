Title:
Broccoli with Panko Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small bunch broccoli
3 Tbl extra-virgin olive oil
3 garlic cloves
1/2 cup Panko bread crumbs
Kosher salt
fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Separate broccoli into florets. Steam until crisp to bite
Heat oil in wok or pan. Add garlic & saute over low heat for a few seconds. Add panko & stir until golden. Add broccoli & combine well. Season to taste with s & P
