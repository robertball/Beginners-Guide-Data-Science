Title:
spring salad of asparagus and caroots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T roasted hazelnut oil
2 T veg oil
1 T chopped tarragon
2 T chopped chives
2 t white wine vinegar
1/2 t salt
8 oz asparagus (half a bunch) washed and trimmed
4 - 6 carrots peeled
1 oz shaved parmesan or gruyere

Directions:
in a small bowl whisk together oils, tarragon, chives,vinegar and salt - set aside
using vegatable peeler, shave both the asparagus and carrots into long strips, place in a large bowl, dress with vinaigrette and gently toss to combine
