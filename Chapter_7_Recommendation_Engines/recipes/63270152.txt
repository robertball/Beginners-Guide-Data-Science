Title:
Sour Cream Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, peeled, cubed
2 cups sour cream
4 tablespoons margarine, melted
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup chopped green onion
2 tablespoons margarine
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes until almost tender; drain. Slightly mash potatoes.
Combine all ingredients, except 2 tablespoons margarine, in a large bowl and mix. Pour into a greased baking dish; dot with margarine.
Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.
