Title:
Papa's Pico Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup tomatoes (fresh), seeded and diced
1 1/2 T. red onion, diced
1 fresh Jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
1½ t. garlic, minced
1 T. fresh lime juice
1 T. fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Chill or not per your own preference.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
I am not much of a tomato or pico/salsa person, but I love this pico recipe! The lime and fresh cilantro help balance the tomato flavor. Seeding the the tomato keeps the texture nice, too.
