Title:
Cabbage Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium or medium large head of fresh, green CABBAGE
1 can Cream of Potato Soup
1/4 cup milk
3/4 pound of mild Cheddar cheese, cut
into small chunks
Optional   2 T. caraway seeds

Directions:
Core cabbage.   Cut head into small chunks.  Put into a shallow baking dish.  Mix Cream of Potato soup and milk.  Pour over Cabbage in pan. Sprinkle Cheddar chunks over all.  Bake at 350 deg. F. for 55 or 60 min.   Stir once midway through baking. If using, sprinkle caraway seeds over top 10 minutes before it is done.  Serve with any roasted meats or poultry.  Tastes wonderful!

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
This tastes wonderful... so much better than the recipe 'sounds'.  It is an easy, quick side dish which goes with roasted meats and poultry.  Ideal for Sunday dinner for the family.  My kids won't eat cabbage normally, but LOVE this dish!
