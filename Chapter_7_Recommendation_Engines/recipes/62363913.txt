Title:
Cabbage and Noodles with Apple and Carrot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 head of cabbage, shredded
1 small apple, such as macintosh, peeled, cored and shredded
1 large or 2 small carrots, shaved.
1 16 ounce bag wide egg noodles
salt and pepper

Directions:
Saute cabbage, apple and carrots in the butter until tender.
Cook egg noodles as directed, drain and stir into cabbage mixture.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
madninnie
Description: 
A tasty variation of cabbage and noodles. The apple and carrots add a subtle sweetness that enhances of the flavor of the cabbage.
