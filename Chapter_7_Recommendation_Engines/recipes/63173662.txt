Title:
The Co-Pilot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/815099.jpg
Ingredients:
10 fresh cinnamon basil leaves
2 oz. Bluecoat gin
1 oz. simple syrup
1 oz. fresh lime juice

Directions:
1. In a pint glass, muddle fresh basil, simple syrup, and lime juice.
2. Add ice and gin.
3. Shake vigorously and double strain into stemmed glass.
4. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Submitter: 
Rudy Torrijos III
Description: 
You will have 5 of them on your patio before the grill is even hot. I'm just saying, its like flying with Jesus.
