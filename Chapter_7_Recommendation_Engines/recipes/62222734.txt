Title:
Crock Pot Chicken & Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
2 Tablespoons butter
2 (10.75 oz) cans of condensed cream of chicken soup
1 small onion or 1/2 medium - finely diced
1 stalk celery
14-20 oz chicken broth
1 (10oz) can refrigerated biscuits
(Pillsbury Grands Homestyle Buttermilk are best)

Directions:
Place chicken, butter, soup, onion, celery, broth in the crock pot - fill with enough water to cover.
Cover and cook 8 hours on low or 5 hours on high.
Approx. 60-90 minutes before serving, uncover, remove celery stalk, shred chicken with a fork.
Cut biscuits strips and cover chicken mixture.  Gently push biscuit strips into chicken mixture to coat them.  Cook on high for 60-90 minutes.
