Title:
Easy Shepherds Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 can corn
1 can peas
envelope onion soup mix
envelope brown gravy mix or 1 can gravy
1 cup boiling water
5-6 cups mashed potatoes (you can use homemade, but that will increase prep time)
3 tbl butter
1 cup shredded cheddar (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Meanwhile, brown and drain ground beef and prepare mashed potatoes.In a ungreased 9x13( liquid does increase during baking) add ground beef. Next, layer the drained corn and peas. Finally top with mashed potatoes and slice butter into tabs and top potatoes. If desired, sprinkle cheese on top. ( for my family, i add cheese to half) Cover with foil and bake for 30-35 minutes. Remove foil and bake for additional 5 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Melissa Edwards
Description: 
This is a quick and easy variation for the family favorite. All the ingredients are common pantry staples and is complete in less than an hour.
