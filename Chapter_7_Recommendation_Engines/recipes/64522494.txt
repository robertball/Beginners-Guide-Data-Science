Title:
Caramel dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown sugar
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup milk, more as needed

Directions:
Stir sugar and condensed milk together.
Heat over low to medium heat, stirring constantly until sugar disolves.
Remove from heat and add butter and vanilla.  Stir well.
Add milk, stir well.
Store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Kisha McDaniel Logsdon
Description: 
Gave it a try because I had the stuff on hand.  I didn't add this much milk, but I should have.  It hardens up as it cools.
