Title:
Angel Fruit Trifle delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One prepared angle food cake cut or torn into chunks
2 12 ounce tubs of light cool whip thawed
2 lb.fresh strawberries chopped
do not use frozen strawberries
1 12 ounce bag of frozen blueberries
1 12 ounce bag frozen peaches chopped
1 package fresh raspberries
5-6 fresh peaches chopped can be used
fresh blueberries can be used as well

Directions:
if using frozen blueberries rinse in strainer and allow to dry while you prepare the following
if using fresh blueberries wash & set aside
wash & chop fresh strawberries set aside
peel fresh peaches cut up in chunks set aside
cut or tear up angel food cake into chunks
in deep clear bowl start first layer with angel food cake chunks, covering the bottom of the bowl they do not have to be level or neat just toss in enough to cover the bottom
next add some chopped strawberries
next add some blueberries
next fresh peach chunks
top with some coolwhip
start layers again starting with the cake
until all ingredients are used
last layer will be cool whip
note you may have some cool whip left over
cover & refrigerate about an hour before serving
NOTE: for an alternate to plain coolwhip put both tubs of cool whip in large bowl and fold in one small package of strawberry jello powder, do not whip or over stir

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
A light fresh and cool summer dessert.  Angel food cake, fresh berries, & cool whip, layered in a trifle bowl makes for a pretty presentation, then you can see the layers of the dessert, This is a great 4th of July red, white, and blue dessert. This is my families favorite summer dessert.Modified from: Angel Fruit Trifle
