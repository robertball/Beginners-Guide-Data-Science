Title:
Chocolate Fudge Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can (14 oz.) Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 Cups Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips or Chunks
2 Pounds Butter Softened
1 Pound Powdered Sugar Sifted

Directions:
Heat sweetened condensed milk and chocolate together on low heat until chocolate is melted. (Stir to avoid burning)
Beat butter with a mixer until smooth.
Let chocolate cool while beating the butter.
Add cooled chocolate to butter.
Mix until well combined (scrape the bowl)
Add powdered sugar 1 cup at a time.
Frost cake, cupcakes, or cookies.
Let them set up in the fridge.
