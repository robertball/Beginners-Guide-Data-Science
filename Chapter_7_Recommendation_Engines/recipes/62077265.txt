Title:
Olive Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T minced garlic
1 C diced colorful bell peppers
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 C chopped red onion
1 C sliced mushrooms
2 10 oz. jars pimento olives, drained
2 cans, black olives, drained
1 C olive oil
1 T garlic powder
2 T sugar
1 C white vinegar
1 t ground black pepper
1 T lemon juice
1 T onion powder

Directions:
Combine the peppers, celery, minced garlic, mushrooms and olives in a large, sealable bowl and chill while cooking the seasoning. In a 2 qt. saicepan, combine oil, vinegar,pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and sugar. Bring mixture to a boil over medium high heat. Add chopped onions and remove from heat. Let stand until cool. Combine the dressing and vegetables together until well mixed. Cover and refrigerate over night. Keepe refrigerated between servings.

Submitter: 
Chrissy DeCosmo Fessler
Description: 
This is a delicious and unique start to any meal. You can also use this as an appetizer with sliced bread, cheese and Lambrusco wine.
