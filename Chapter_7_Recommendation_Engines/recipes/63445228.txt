Title:
Bruschetta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
2 medium tomato , cut into 1-inch pieces (about 2 cups)
1/2 cup thinly sliced cucumber
1 medium red onion , thinly sliced (about 1/2 cup)
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil leaves
1 tablespoon drained capers
2 cup Pepperidge FarmÂ® Whole Grain Seasoned Croutons

Directions:
Beat the oil and vinegar in a large bowl with a fork or whisk.
Add the tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, basil and capers and toss to coat.
Add the croutons just before serving and toss to coat. Serve immediately.
