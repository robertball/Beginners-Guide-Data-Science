Title:
Ursula's Turkey Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground turkey
1 egg
3/4 cup bread crumbs
1 tbs garlic powder
1 tbs salt
1 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp worchestershire sauce
10-12 grape or cherry tomatoes (or 1/2 cup cheese)
dash of milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray.
Beat egg in bowl with a little milk.  Add all ingredients except tomatoes (cheese) and mix together.  Take a tomato (or cheese) and cover it with meat mixture shaping into a ball.  Place on baking sheet.  Place meatballs in oven and bake for 30-40 minutes or until evenly browned and cooked through.

Submitter: 
Ursula Blakely
Description: 
My unique twist on meatballs.  You can stuff them with grape or cherry tomatoes, or even cheese.  Yummy!!!
