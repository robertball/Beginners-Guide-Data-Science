Title:
Herb Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. salted butter, softened
4 t. lemon juice
1/2 t.garlic powder
1 t. dried oregano
1 t. dried chives
1 t. dried thyme
1 t. dried rosemary
1 t. dried tarragon

Directions:
Whip butter with lemon juice and garlic powder.  Crush herbs and  add to butter mixture.  Mix well.
May be divided into crocks or rolled into logs using plastic wrap.  Chill well.  Best if chilled overnight before using so flavors blend well.  Freezes well.
Set it out to soften for about an hour before serving so it spreads easier.

Submitter: 
springermom
Description: 
This butter will take your hot bread or rolls to another level.  It's great as a steak topping too!
