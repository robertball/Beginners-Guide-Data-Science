Title:
Fried Wild Turkey Breast Recipe

Ingredients:
-turkey breasts cut against the grain into nuggets
-poultry seasoning
-mustard
-flour
-season salt
-black pepper
-Franks Red Hot Wing Sauce

Directions:
-get fryer up to 350
-coat turkey nuggets with mustard then cover with poultry seasoning
-dump nuggets into flour, salt, and pepper
-fry for 2 minutes
-douse with Frank's
