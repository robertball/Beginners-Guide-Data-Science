Title:
Slow Cooker Veggie Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cup Green Pepper
1 10 oz. package chopped spinach thawed and drained
1 1/2 onions, diced
12 oz sliced mushrooms
32 oz jarred spaghetti sauce
1/2 cup sliced black olives
1 1/2 tsp Italian herbs
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1/2 cup red wine
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Spray slow cooker liner with non-stick spray.  Place everything, except cheese, in slow cooker.  Stir gently.  Cook on LOW for 6 to 7 hours.  Add cheese, stir gently, and cook on HIGH for 15 - 20 minutes.
