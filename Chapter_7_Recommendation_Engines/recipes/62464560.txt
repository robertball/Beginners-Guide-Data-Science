Title:
Scrumptious Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs (well beaten)
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups zucchini (grated)
1 cup pineapple tidbits (drained)
3 cups flour
2 tsp. soda
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 cup raisins
1 cup walnuts

Directions:
Beat eggs in a large bowl. Add vegetable oil, sugar and vanilla. Mix well. Gradually add grated zucchini and pineapple.
In a separate bowl sift flour, soda, b. powder, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg. Add these to the first bowl and mix well. Then mix in raisins and walnuts.
Pour batter into 2 lightly greased bread loaf pans and bake at 350ยบ for 1 hour. Enjoy!
