Title:
Carter Syle Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. spaghetti, vermicelli or angel hair pasta
8 oz. fresh or canned mushrooms, chopped
3 Tbsp. olive oil
2 cloves garlic
1 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 tsp. basil
4 oz. bacon
14 oz. canned Italian plum tomatoes
1 small can tomato paste
3 oz. tuna
parsley to garnish

Directions:
Heat oil with garlic and chili till garlic starts to change color. Add basil and bacon. When bacon fat melts, throw in mushrooms. Cook 5 minutes, then add drained tomatoes and paste. Cook 15 minutes on low heat, then add drained tuna. Cook sauce an additional 15 minutes. Prepare pasta while sauce simmers. Toss together and add parsley.
