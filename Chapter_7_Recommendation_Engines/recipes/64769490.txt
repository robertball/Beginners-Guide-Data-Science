Title:
Pepperoni Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pepperoni
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 cup shredded mozzerella
2 packages (10 each) biscuits
1 tablespoon milk
1/4 cup Parmesean cheese
garlic salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a bowl, combine cheese, pepperoni, and pizza sauce.
Separate biscuits and flatten each into a 3-inch circle.
Place approx 1 rounded tablespoon of filling in each biscuit circle.
Pinch edges of dough into crescent shape and place on baking sheet.
Brush the rolls with milk and sprinkle with Parmesean and garlic salt.
Bake at 350 for 12-15 minutes.
