Title:
Tangy Tomato Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1122607.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup corn syrup
1/4 cup tomato and clam juice cocktail
2 tablespoons tomato sauce
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Thoroughly blend ketchup, corn syrup, tomato and clam juice cocktail, tomato sauce, lemon juice, prepared horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, and cayenne pepper. Cover,  and chill until serving.

Description: 
Tangy is an understatement for this dressing, and it will certainly add some ZIP to your salad!
