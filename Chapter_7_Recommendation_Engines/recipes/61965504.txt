Title:
Mom's Wild Rice Casserol Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups wild rice
3 cups beef bouillon
1 cup water
1/2 cup  onion, chopped
1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
1 cup mushrooms, chopped
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup cream

Directions:
Soak wild rice for 6 hours and drain well.  Boil rice in bouillon and water until liquid is absorbed.  Saute onion, pepper and mushrooms in all but 1 tablespoon butter.  Mix rice with sauteed ingredients and stir in cream and remaining butter.  Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.  Cover and bake at 325 for 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
