Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound small cubed beef stew meat
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1 small chopped onion
8 oz package crimini/baby bella mushrooms sliced
1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup water
4 ounces cream cheese
Salt & pepper to taste
1 tbs flour
1 tablespoon oil
2 tablespoon butter
8 oz. wide egg noodles

Directions:
Lightly coat stew meat in flour, salt and pepper.
Heat a pan over medium-high heat.
Add oil, 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce and meat and brown all sides.
In a slow cooker, combine the meat, soup, 1 tbs Worcestershire sauce and water.
Turn stove top down to medium low.
Add 1 tablespoon butter and onions. Salt onions and sauté until soft.
Add onions to the slow cooker.
Cover and cook on Low setting for 8 hours or high for 5 hours.
1 hour before its done, sauté mushrooms in 1 tbs butter, salt and pepper to taste.
Add to slow cooker.
Cook egg noodles according to package. Drain and set aside
Stir in cream cheese just before serving and mix until completely melted.
Add egg noodles to slow cooker and mix until evenly coated.
Plate beef stroganoff, sprinkle fresh parsley on top and serve

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
Easy and delicious beef stroganoff! Let your slow cooker do the work and enjoy a tender beef with an extra creamy sauce. Pair with egg noodles, rice, or gluten free pasta.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I
