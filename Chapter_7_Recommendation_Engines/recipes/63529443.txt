Title:
Award-Winning Slow Cooker Chile con carne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/963807.jpg
Ingredients:
1.4 lbs. lean ground beef
.75 lbs. ground sausage
1 cup diced onion
3/4 cup diced green pepper
1/2 cup diced celery
3 diced cloves of garlic
1 small diced jalape√±o pepper
1 large can peeled tomatoes
1 large can tomato puree
2 cans kidney beans (1 not drained)
2 cans white beans (1 not drained
2 TB. to 1/4 cup of chile powder (to taste)
1 TB. cumin
1 TB. Italian seasonings
1 TB. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 TB. unsweetened powdered baking chocolate

Directions:
saute meat in a skillet in olive oil till browned
put diced veggies into slow cooker
add beans (if you are using pre-spoaked, dried beans, best to cook beans before adding to the recipe)
add browned meat
add herbs, salt and sugar
add tomatoes and tomato puree
add powered chocolate after about 4 hours in slow cooker, then continue cooking for at least another 4-5 hours in slow cooker.

Submitter: 
Marjorie Stradinger
Description: 
This was a Superbowl Sunday Chile Cook-Off entry and tied for 1st place with one that was a 3-day recipe using beef brisket and shredded pork--and was much meatier.
