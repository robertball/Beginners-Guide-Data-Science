Title:
Euna's Fudge Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 Tablespoon cocoa
1 Cup sugar
1/3 Cup butter, melted
Dash of salt
2 eggs
1 small can condensed milk
1 shortbread pie crust

Directions:
Mix together melted butter, cocoa, and sugar.
Add eggs, vanilla, milk, and salt.
Bake at 325 for 50 minutes.  Should be barely jiggly in the middle.
