Title:
Metal Mouth Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1Lb. package of Creamette Large Ring Macaroni
1 chopped yellow onion
1 chopped green pepper
3 grated carrots
1 cup sugar
1 cup vinegar
2 cups mayo
1 14 oz. can Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk.

Directions:
Cook macaroni until tender and drain.  In a large bowl, combine macaroni, onion, green pepper, and carrots.  In a separate bowl, mix condensed milk, sugar, vinegar, and mayo.  Pour dressing over macaroni and stir.  Refrigerate overnight and serve the next day for best flavor.

Submitter: 
Junebug's Mom
Description: 
My aunt has a large family and usually has a crowd to feed.  She makes this macaroni salad with ring macaroni noodles.  This is my favorite pasta salad.  When I was younger, I thought it looked like rubber bands for braces.
