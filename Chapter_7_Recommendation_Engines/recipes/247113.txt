Title:
Cornbread Chili Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7273980.jpg
Ingredients:
cooking spray
3 (15 ounce) cans chili with beans
1/4 (1 pound) loaf processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®), thinly sliced
1 (16 ounce) package cornbread mix (such as Marie Callender's®)
1 1/2 cups water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Spread chili evenly over the bottom of the dish. Arrange processed cheese in a single layer over the chili.
Mix cornbread mix with water in a large bowl until smooth. Spread evenly over processed cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven until chili is heated through and cornbread topping is golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
