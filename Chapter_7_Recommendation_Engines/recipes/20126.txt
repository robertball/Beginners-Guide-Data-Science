Title:
Bananerberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/433563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh strawberries
1 banana, sliced
1 cup fresh peaches
1 cup apples
1 1/2 cups vanilla ice cream
1 1/2 cups ice cubes
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
In a blender combine strawberries, banana, peaches, apples, and ice cream. Blend until smooth. Add ice, pour in milk and blend again until smooth. Serve immediately.
