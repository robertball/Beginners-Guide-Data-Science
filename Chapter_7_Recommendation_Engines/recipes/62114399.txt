Title:
Southern Pineapple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (20 oz) Crushed Pineapple
1 can (20 oz) Chunked Pineapple
2 cups Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 cup Sugar
6 tbl Flour
40 ritz crackers, crushed (at least 1 tube)
1 Cup (2 sticks) butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Drain pineapple from can BUT reserve Â¼ cup of pineapple juice
Mix Flour and Sugar
Mix cheese and pineapple
Mix the flour/sugar with the pineapple/cheese
Pour into 1 1/2 quart casserole dish
Combine cracker crumbs and butter so that all the cracker crumbs are covered in butter, cover pineapple mixture
Bake 30min. @ 350

Submitter: 
bex
Description: 
My grandmother used to make this for us on Easter. She recently revived the dish and I begged her for the recipe. It is now a staple to all my cookouts and is always a favorite.
