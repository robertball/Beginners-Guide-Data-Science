Title:
Cookie Dough Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1 1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. cocoa
1/2 t. salt
1 c. vegetable oil
4 eggs
2 t. vanilla extract
1/2 c. chopped walnuts, opt.
1/2 c. butter or margarine, softened
1/2 c. packed brown sugar
1/4 c. sugar
2 T. milk
1 t. vanilla extract
1 c. flour
1 c. chocolate chips
1 T. shortening
3/4 c. chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour, cocoa, and salt. Add oil, eggs and vanilla. Beat for 3 minutes. Stir in walnuts if desired. Bake in a 9x13 at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Cool completely.
Cream butter and sugars in a bow. Add milk and vanilla and beat well. Beat in flour. Spread over brownies. Chill until firm.
Melt chocolate chips and shortening, stirring until smooth. Spread over brownies and filling. Speinkle with nuts.
