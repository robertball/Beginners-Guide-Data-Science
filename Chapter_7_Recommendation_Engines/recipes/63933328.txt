Title:
shrimp creaole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp of oil
1 cup of chopped onion
1/2 cup of chopped celery
1/2 cup of chopped bell pepper
6 cloves chopped garlic
1 can of diced tomatoes and chilies
1 small can of tomato sauce
2 bay leaves
1 tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 lb of medium raw shrimp (peeled)
1/2 cup of flour seasoned with salt and pepper.
1/2 cup of tomato juice (or water).

Directions:
Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion, celery, bell pepper and garlic and saute until soft. Add canned tomatoes and chilies, tomato sauce, bay leaves, sugar, salt and pepper. reduce heat to medium low and cook about 30 minutes until vegetables are soft.
Put flour mixture in a plastic zip top bag and add shrimp. Shake to coat.
Add juice (or water) and reduce heat to low. Cook for 5 minutes to blend flavors.
Add shrimp and cook until the start to turn pink. Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes to finish cooking.

Submitter: 
Ron Lloyd
Description: 
We made this last night and it was delicious. If you like it spicy add some more heat when cooking the vegetables.
