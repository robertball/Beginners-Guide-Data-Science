Title:
Chicken Diane Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, halved
2 Tbsp. oil
1 can cream of brocolli soup (Campbells half fat)
1 cup milk
1 cup brocolli flourets
1 cup cauliflower flourets
3/4 cup shredded cheddar or mozerrela chesse

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F.  In a non stick skillet brown chicken with oil.  Mix soup, milk, brocolli, cauliflower, and 1/2 cup of chesse.  In a casserole dish layer chicken then pour mixture over top and place in oven for an hour until chicken is tender.  Sprinkle remaining chesse over top in last 10 minutes of cooking time.

Submitter: 
Angie Achter
Description: 
An easy recipe which can also be low fat if you use low fat soup, milke, and chese.
