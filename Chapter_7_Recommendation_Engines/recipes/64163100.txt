Title:
Christmas Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4566695.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large baking potatoes, peeled and cut in thick slices.
1 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp salt
pinch of pepper or more to taste
1/4 cup turkey drippings

Directions:
Cook potatoes in boiling, salted water until just tender, 10-12 mins.
In a 13x9 inch baking dish combine paprika, salt, pepper and turkey drippings.
Add potatoes and toss to coat.
Bake for 40 mins at 325, turning occasionally.
Increase oven temperature to 425 and cook until golden, 10-15 mins, turning occasionally.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
This my go to recipe whenever I roast a turkey. Wonderfully crispy with just a hint of chicken flavour.
