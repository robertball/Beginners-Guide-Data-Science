Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) box of elbow macaroni
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups whole milk
2 tbls melted butter
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp salt
2 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
2 tbls flour

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions, being careful not to overcook. Drain and rinse with cold water. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Pour 1/2 of macaroni noodles into a buttered 2 quart casserole dish. Top with half of grated cheese. Repeat layer. Combine eggs, milk, butter, cayenne peeper, flour, and salt and pour on top of noodles and cheese. Bake up brown and bubbly 35 to 45 minutes.
