Title:
Pecan Crusted Chicken with Dijon Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless boneless chicken breast pounded to 1/2 inch
2 T Melted Butter
6 T dijon mustard
3/4 cup pecans-chopped very fine
8 oz sour cream

Directions:
Prepare baking sheet with foil and cooking spray
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix 2T of melted butter with 3T Dijon Mustard in a shallow dish
Dip chicken in mustard, then dredge in finely chopped pecans
Placed on prepared baking sheet
Bake until cooked thouroughly approx 25 minutes.
Just prior to removing chicken, prepare sauce.
Place sour cream and leftover dijon mustard in a small sauce pot
Heat until warm. Careful to stir so it does not burn.
Serve sauce over chicken

Submitter: 
HOPHOP1125
Description: 
Chicken that is tasty and fancy enough for company but easy enough for a regular meal. Add more butter, cream, and mustard if your chicken breasts are very large or you like lots of sauce.
