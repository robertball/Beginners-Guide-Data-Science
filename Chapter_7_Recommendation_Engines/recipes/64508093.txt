Title:
Southwestern Turkey Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground turkey
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp dijon mustard
1 tsp adobo sauce
1 1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp salt
4 whole hamburger buns
1 whole tomato, sliced
AVOCADO MOUSSE:
1 whole avocado, peeled & pitted
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/3 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon tabasco sauce
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
Make the Avocado Mousee:  Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and creamy.  Adjust the seasoning to taste with more lemon, salt or tabasco sauce, as desired.
Combine turkey, garlic, chili powder, oregano, cumin, mustard, adobo, worcestershire and salt in a large mixing bowl.   Mix well.  Shape the seasoned meat into 4 patties.
Grill the burgers until cooked through, about 6 minutes on each side.   Place each burger on a bun and top with Avocado Mousse and sliced tomato.
