Title:
Soft Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/93803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 cup quick cooking oats
1 tablespoon unsweetened applesauce
egg substitute equivalent to 1 egg
2 tablespoons margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon apple pie spice
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the first six ingredients; beat until blended. Stir in raisins. Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto baking sheets coated with nonstick cooking spray.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly browned. Cool for 5 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter:

Description: 
With mild spice flavor and touch of sweetness from raisins, these cookies are better than store-bought.
