Title:
Homemade Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/428663.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup Sugar
2 Cups Milk
1/3 Cup Flour
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
4 Whole Eggs, Separated
1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
3/4 Box Vanilla Wafer Cookies
4 Whole Bananas

Directions:
Combine sugar, flour, salt, separated eggs, and milk and cook over medium heat on the stove-top.
Continually stir until creamy and thick.
Remove from the stove-top and allow to cool to room temperature.
Layer a bowl with the pudding bananas and vanilla wafers to taste. Enjoy!
