Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. cucumbers
1 1/2 T sliced green onions
3 T white vinegar
1 1/2 t sugar
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
Peel and thinly slice cucumbers. Place in serving dish. Add gr. onions, vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper; toss gently to coat. Cover and freeze few min.
