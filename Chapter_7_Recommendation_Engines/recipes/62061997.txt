Title:
Vermont Pumpkin Walnut CheeseCake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 (6 oz) pkg. Zwieback crackers, crushed (about 1.5 cups)
- 1/4 c. sugar
- 6 Tbsp. butter, melted
- 3 (8 oz) pkg. cream cheese, softened
- 3/4 c. sugar
- 3/4 c. firmly packed brown sugar
- 5 eggs
- 1 (16 oz) can pumpkin
- 1 3/4 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
- 1/4 c. heavy cream
WALNUT TOPPING:
- 6 Tbsp. softened butter
- 1 c. firmly packed brown sugar
- 1 c. coarsely chopped walnuts

Directions:
1. Blend Zwieback crumbs, sugar, and butter in a medium-sized bowl.  Then, press firmly over bottom and up side of a lightly buttered 9-inch spring-form pan.  Chill.
2. Beat cream cheese in a large bowl with electric mixer until smooth.
3. Add sugars gradually, beating until well-mixed.
4. Beat in eggs one at a time until mixture is light and fluffy.
5. Beat in pumpkin pie spice and heavy cream at low speed.
6. Pour mixture into prepared pan and bake at 325 for 1 hour and 35 minutes.
7. While pie is baking, combine walnut topping ingredients in a small bowl until crumbly.
8. Remove cake from oven and sprinkle walnut topping on, then bake an additional 10 minutes.
9. Cool cake on wire rack, then refrigerate for several hours, or overnight.
10. Garnish with whipped cream and walnuts if desired!
