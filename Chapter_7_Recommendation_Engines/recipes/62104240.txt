Title:
Amy's Perfect Pineapple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 18 oz box yellow cake mix
1 cup sweetened, flaked coconut
1 15 oz can crushed, undrained pineapple
2 large eggs
Topping:
1 cup granulated sugar
1 stick butter
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1 tsp coconut extract
Frosting:
1 3 oz package vanilla instant pudding
12 oz frozen whipped topping
1 15 oz can of crushed, undrained pineapple

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Coat with cooking spray or grease and flour 9x13 inch baking pan.
In large bowl, combine cake mix, coconut, crushed, undrained pineapple and two eggs.
Beat at high speed for two minutes.  Pour into 9x13 inch pan.
Bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes.
When cake is out of oven, prepare topping as follows:
Combine 1 cup sugar, 1 stick butter, and 1 cup heavy whipping cream in saucepan.  Cook over medium heat until butter is melted.  Add in coconut extract.  Pour hot topping over hot cake.
Allow cake to cool completely.
Prepare frosting as follows:
Combine vanilla pudding with whipped topping and crushed, undrained pineapple.  Mix on medium speed for one minute or until fluffy.
Spread over completely cooled cake.
This cake should be stored in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description: 
A moist, delicious, tropical pineapple-coconut cake with fluffy frosting.  This refrigerated cake is a great summertime dessert -- extremely rich and satisfying.
