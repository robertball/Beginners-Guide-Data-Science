Title:
Figgy Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2385667.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped fresh figs (approximately 10 figs)
1 package of ramen noodles

Directions:
Cook the noodles as instructed on the packaging.
Once the pasta is fully cooked and cooled, place chopped figs in.
Stir thoroughly, mixing the figs in the noodles evenly.

Submitter: 
Chef Lizzie
Description: 
Our fig tree gives us more fruit than we can eat. I discovered this unique recipe by adding fig to every dish I ate! Who knew pasta and fig would go perfectly together?
