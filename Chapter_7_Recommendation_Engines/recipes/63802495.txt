Title:
Grandma's Cocktail Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2lb ground beef (chunk or round)
- 1 package of dry onion soup mix
- 1/4 cup of cracker crumbles
- 1/2 cup of red wine
- 1 egg
- 13 oz bottle of ketchup
- 14 oz jar of apple or currant jelly
- 2 TBSP of veg oil

Directions:
- Mix together beef, cracker crumbs, onion soup mixture, and eggs. Form into 1inch round balls: should make about fifty.
- Warm oil in a non-stick skillet. Brown balls on each side. About 2 min each side.
- In a large pot, melt ketchup, and jelly on medium-high heat. Once melted, add wine and then combine with meatballs. Simmer for 25 min on stovetop.
-To keep warm, maintain on low heat or transfer to crockpot under the "warm" setting.
