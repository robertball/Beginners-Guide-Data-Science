Title:
Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
1/2 cup unsalted butter
3 tablespoons flour
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced
sugar/cinnamon mix

Directions:
Melt the butter in a saucepan.
Stir in flour to form a paste.
Add water, sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
Place the bottom crust in your pan.
Fill with apples, mounded slightly.
Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over apples.
Place the top crust over apples and crepe edges together.
Spread a little soft butter over the top crust and sprinkle sugar/cinnamon all over the top crust.
Bake at 425° for 15 minutes.
Reduce the temperature to 350°.
Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
