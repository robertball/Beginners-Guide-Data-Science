Title:
Roasted Pasilla & Chorizo Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chorizo
1/2 pound ground beef
8 ounces HERDEZ® Roasted Pasilla Chile Mexican Cooking Sauce
8 ounces spaghetti sauce
15 ounces ricotta cheese
4 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
4 ounces shredded pepper jack cheese, divided
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, divided
1 egg
2 tablespoons dried parsley
6 lasagna noodles, uncooked

Directions:
Brown meats in large skillet; drain. Stir in HERDEZ® Roasted Pasilla Chile Mexican Cooking Sauce and spaghetti sauce; simmer.
Mix ricotta, 3 oz. mozzarella, 3 oz. pepper jack cheese, 2 tbsp. parmesan, egg and parsley in a medium bowl.
Spoon 1 cup meat sauce into slow cooker. Top with layers of half each of the noodles (broken to fit) and cheese mixture. Cover with 2 cups meat sauce. Top with remaining noodles (broken to fit), cheese mixture and meat sauce. Cover with lid.
Cook on low 4 to 5 hours or until liquid is absorbed. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella,  pepper jack and parmesan.  Let stand, covered until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Faith N
Description: 
This is my Mexican inspired version of lasagna that is easy to make and packed with flavor. HERDEZ® Roasted Pasilla Chile Mexican Cooking Sauce gives this dish a mild, flavor-able kick that paired with pepper jack cheese and chorizo is a new spicy twist on a family favorite.
