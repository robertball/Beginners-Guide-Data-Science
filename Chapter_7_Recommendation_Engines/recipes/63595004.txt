Title:
Perfect Picnic Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs fryer parts, cut up
3 Tbl flour
1/4 cup lemon
2 1/2 cups dry pancake mix
1/2 tsp sage
1 1/2 cup milk
2 pkg Italian salad dressing mix
1 tsp salt
2 Tbl butter, melted
1 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp pepper
2 cups salad oil

Directions:
Make a paste by combining salad dressing mix, flour, salt, lemon juice and butter.
Brush paste on chicken parts.
Stack chicken in bowl; refrigerate for 20 minutes.
Mix pancake mix, paprika, sage and pepper.
Pour milk in a bowl.
Wet chicken pieces in milk and dust in pancake mixture.
Let pieces dry 5 minutes on waxed paper.
Heat oil in frying pan.
Fry 3 pieces of chicken at a time for 5 to 6 minutes.
Place on baking sheet; bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
