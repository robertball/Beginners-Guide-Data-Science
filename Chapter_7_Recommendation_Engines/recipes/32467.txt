Title:
Green Gecko Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937719.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger green Chartreuse
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger 151 proof rum

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer full of ice, combine Chartreuse and rum. Shake vigorously and strain into 2 shot glasses.
