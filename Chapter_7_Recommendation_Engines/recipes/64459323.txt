Title:
Chicken Cutlets with Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs lemon juice
2 tbs oil
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 tbs parsley
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
10 oz spinach, cooked and drained
4 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/2 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Mix lemon juice, oil, garlic and parsley together. Coat chicken with mixture and lay in a baking dish. Cover with spinach and mozzarella. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top. Cover and bake for 25-30 minutes at 350.
