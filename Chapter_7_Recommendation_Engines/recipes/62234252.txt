Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bananas, mashed
2 eggs
1 1/4 c sugar
1 t vanilla (or banana flavored rum)
1 t salt
2 1/2 c all purpose flour
1 t baking soda
6 T butter, room temp
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (more or less to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 - lightly grease muffin tins (xlarge ones) or loaf pan
1) Cream butter and sugar.
2) Stir in eggs and vanilla
3) Follow with well mashed bananas
4) In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt and baking powder
5) Add dry to wet, stirring as little as possible to avoid toughening muffins
Bake at 350 for 35 minutes
May add chopped walnuts if desired (John won't eat lol)
