Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 stick unsalted butter, softened
3 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
2-3 tbsp milk

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy. Mix in the vanilla, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar and milk (as needed). Store in the refrigerator after use.
