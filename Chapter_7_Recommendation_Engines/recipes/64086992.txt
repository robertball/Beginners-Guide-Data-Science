Title:
Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups rolled oats
1-1/2 cups wheat germ
3/4 cup bran, unprocessed
1 cup unsalted sunflower seeds
3/4 cup raw peanuts
3/4 cup unsalted cashews
1/3 cup coconut
1/8 cup sesame seeds
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup oil
1/4 cup water
1 Tbsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F.
In large bowl mix rolled oats, wheat germ, bran, sunflower seeds, peanuts, cashews, coconut, and sesame seeds.  Set aside.
In a smaller bowl, mix brown sugar, oil, water and cinnamon.  Microwave for 1 minute or until sugar is dissolved.
Add sugar mixture to large bowl and mix well. Pour                                                                                                     into two greased cookie sheets.  Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
