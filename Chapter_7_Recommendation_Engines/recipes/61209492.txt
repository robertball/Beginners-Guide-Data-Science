Title:
Bacon 'n' Egg Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 bacon strips
6 eggs
Cooking spray
Milk
Salt and pepper
Grated cheese
Fresh parsley sprigs

Directions:
Lightly spray muffin tin cups.. In a large skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until cooked but not crisp. Drain on paper towels.
Mix eggs, a little milk, salt, and pepper.  Pour mixture evenly into lined muffin cups.
Cut six bacon strips on half width-wise; line the bottom of each muffin cup with two bacon pieces. Line the sides of each muffin cup with one or two bacon strips. Break an egg into each cup. Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until eggs are cooked to desired consistency.  Garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
Kathleen Redman
Description: 
This is a fun way to serve bacon and eggs all in one bite! The recipe can easily be doubled for a larger group.Modified from: Bacon 'n' Egg Bundles
