Title:
Low Carb Vegetarian Lasagna in a Crock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 Medium Zucchini
1 Medium Yellow Onion
1 Large Portobello Mushroom
1 16 oz Can Stewed Tomatoes
1 Tbsp Italian Seasoning
2 C. Lowfat Cottage Cheese
1 C. Garlic Croutons
1 C. Sliced or Shredded Cheddar, Swiss, or Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Saute zucchini, onion, and mushrooms in olive oil over medium-high heat until onions are translucent. Add tomatoes and Italian Seasoning and cook on medium-low for 5-10 minutes. Add cottage cheese and cook another minute, just until warmed through. Place 1/4 c. croutons in bottom of each crock, fill with vegetable mixture, and top with sliced or shredded cheese of choice. Put under broiler until cheese is melted.
