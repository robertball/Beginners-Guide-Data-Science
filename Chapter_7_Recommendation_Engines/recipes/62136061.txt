Title:
Super Easy Halloween Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
1 can pure pumpkin puree (not pumpkin pie filling)
2 eggs
1 cup semi-sweet or bittersweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients together with electric mixer for two minutes. Stir in chocolate chips. Bake according to directions on back of cake mix box, but check a few minutes early for doneness. Decorate spookily!

Submitter: 
ELEVIN
Description: 
More nutritious than an ordinary cake, and very simple to make. This is an orange and black treat!
