Title:
DinoMite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2454073.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Chicken, cut into bite sized chunks
1 egg, beaten
1 tsp salt
1 tsp red pepper
1/2 cup mango juice
3 Tbsp cornstarch
1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
1 cup panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Beat together egg, salt, red pepper and mango juice.  Add cornstarch slowly, stirring to prevent lumps.  Mix panko and coconut together in a separate bowl. Coat chicken- first into the wet ingredients and then into the dry.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
