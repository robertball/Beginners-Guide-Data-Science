Title:
Low Fat Corn and Potato Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white onion, chopped
1 large clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon olive oil
1/2 tsp coriander
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper (or more to taste)
2 tsp brown sugar
1/8 cup white flour
2 cups fat free half and half
2 cups water
1 vegetable bouillon cube
1 medium baking potato, diced
1 can no salt added corn

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot.
Add onion, coriander, black pepper, salt, and red pepper. Cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add garlic, cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
Add water, half and half, flour, sugar, and the bouillon cube.  Stir well and bring it to a soft boil.
Add potato, keep at a low boil about 5 minutes or until potato is soft.
Add corn, reduce heat and simmer additional 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
ArianeK
Description: 
This vegetarian chowder is low in fat with a mix of savory and sweet flavors.
