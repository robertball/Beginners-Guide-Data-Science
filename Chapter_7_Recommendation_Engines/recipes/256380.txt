Title:
Hot Green Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4236610.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups water
6 1/4-inch thick cucumber slices
1 large jalapeno pepper, halved and seeded
2 sprigs fresh mint

Directions:
Combine water, cucumber, jalapeno pepper, and mint in a pitcher and refrigerate until chilled, about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
This water has a cool, fresh taste thanks to the mint and cucumber then there is a bit of spark aftertaste from the jalapeno. It's fantastic!
