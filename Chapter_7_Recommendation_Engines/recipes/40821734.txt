Title:
Cajun shrimp pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 tbsp evoo
1/2 onion, chopped fine
1/2 red pepper, chopped fine (can be optional
1 cup cremini mshrms, thin sliced
1-2 garlic cloves minced
chik broth for saute
2 tsp. Emeril essence
1 cup diced, canned tomato with liquid reserved
1 tsp. Louisiana Hot Sauce
1 Tbsp. Red wine
1 tsp. worchetshire sauce
Sea salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
1 lb. Med. shrimp
1/2 lb. spagetti, whole wheat
shredded parmasaen
fresh parsely for garnish

Directions:
Saute onion, pepper, mshrooms and garilc in olive oil until mshrooms are golden.  Add broth during saute to prevent burning.
During this saute add half of Emerils. Add tomato and continue saute, lowering heat when it thickens.  Add reserve tomato liquid as needed and remaining spices.  Add shrimp and cook until just pink, making sure not to over cook.
Serve over spaghetti with parmasaen and parsely on top, in pre-warmed pasta dish.
Meanwhile cook spaghetti
