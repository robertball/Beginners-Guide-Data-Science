Title:
Beef and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060358.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2	pounds leftover Pot Roast, cut into bite size pieces
3-4	cups Home Made Noodles
4-6	cups water
2	tablespoons kitchen bouquet
1	tablespoon garlic powder (to taste)
1	tablespoon  onion powder (to taste)
2	teaspoons black pepper (to taste)
Plus any leftover gravy from your pot roast
3-4	teaspoons beef base if needed (to taste after you add gravy)

Directions:
1.	In stock pot or Dutch oven add water, seasonings (all but beef base and meat), gravy, and kitchen bouquet.
2.	bring to a boil stirring occasionally, check taste to make sure it has enough flavor.  Add seasoning if needed.
3.	After it comes to a boil add leftover pot roast now.  Let return to boil.  Stirring constantly add noodles one handful at a time.  Stirring between additions to keep noodles from sticking to each other. After all noodles have been added and are cooked enough not to stick together.  Let simmer stirring occasionally for about 5 to 10 minutes or until the noodles are done.
4.  To serve: in a bowl add mashed potatoes, and pour beef and noodles over them mix and eat or have a dinner roll.  They are very filling and comforting.  Enjoy

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Creamy filling comfort food.  inexpensive and stretches out the meat. Freezes well too.  Great use for leftover pot roast.
