Title:
Green monster smothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped carrots
2 cups baby spinach
2 cored and peeled apples
2 bananas
1 cup orange juice
1 cup strawberries (you can use any kind of fruits. I love putting pineapples in my smoothies)
1 cup ice

Directions:
core and peel apples. this is very important, you dont want nasty peel chunk or seeds in your shake
put all the ingredents in the blender on smoothie setting for a few minutes
pour into glass and enjoy. its just that simple to eat healthy :)

Submitter: 
Jessi Renee Bigney
Description: 
This is a healthy smoothie pack full of veggies. Even your picky kids will love these fruity smoothies.
