Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz packages frozen broccoli
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 beaten egg
1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
5 Ritz crackers
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Cook broccoli. Place in a flat, greased casserole dish. Mix mayonnaise, egg, soup, cheese, onion, salt and pepper. Pour over broccoli. Sprinkle crushed crackers on top. Bake 30 minutes at 350.
