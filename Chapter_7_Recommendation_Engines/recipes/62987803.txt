Title:
Quick Cantaloupe Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cantaloupe
3/4 Cup milk
1 Tbsp. sugar (optional)

Directions:
Cut cantaloupe in half and take the seeds out. Make sure there are no seeds.
Put in blender or juicer. Add milk and sugar.
Blend till you cant see the cantaloupe.
Taste: You might need more sugar according to how sweet you want it to be.
Sit back and enjoy!
