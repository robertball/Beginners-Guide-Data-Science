Title:
Easy Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat
1 1/2 lb. ground beef
1 box package pork stuffing
1 medium onion
2 medium eggs
2 tb worcestershire sauce
Sauce
1 16 oz Sour Cream
1 jar beef gravy mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Mix together meat ingredients.  Roll into 1"-1 1/2" balls place on greased baking sheet.  Bake for 25 minutes.  Heat sauce on stove mix together and place over Egg Noodles.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
This recipe is a perfect appetizer!  It's simple and delicious and you are always wanting more.  Sour cream base with pork stuffing.  Great over buttered egg noodles with natures seasoning.
