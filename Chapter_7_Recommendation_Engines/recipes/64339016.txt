Title:
Buckwheat Millet Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. buckwheat flour
1/2 c. millet flour
3 T. chia in 1/4 c. water
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 c milk (soy or other plant)

Directions:
Start by soaking the chia seeds while putting the dry  ingredients in a bowl. Mix chia mixture with milk and quickly stir into mixture. Bake as usual for pancakes on non stick pan.
