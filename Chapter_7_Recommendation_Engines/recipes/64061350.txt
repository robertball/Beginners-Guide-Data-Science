Title:
Ice Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 tea bags
2 cups boiling water
10 cups ice
3 gallons water
2 lemons sliced

Directions:
steep tea in boiling water for five minutes
add enough ice to cool the tea mixture
add the rest of the ingredients to the beverage server

Rhonda Holtz Hill
