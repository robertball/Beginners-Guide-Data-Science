Title:
Garry's Cajun Dirty Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3981439.jpg
Ingredients:
-2 cups rice (Uncle Ben's long grain works best)
-1-1/2lbs. ground beef
-1 can cream of chicken soup
-1 can cream of mushroom soup
-1 can French onion soup
-1-1/2 soup cans of water
-1 onion chopped
-1/2 of a bell pepper chopped
-1 tsp. garlic powder
-1 tblsp. salt
-1 tsp. red pepper
-1 tsp. paprika
-1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
-preheat oven to 350
-brown meat with onions and then drain
-stir all ingredients together and put in an 11x13 baking pan
-cover with foil and place into oven for 1 hour and 10-15 minutes (whenever rice is done)

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy recipe. Just dump, stir, and cook. Goes great with Club Crackers and a cold beer or all by itself
