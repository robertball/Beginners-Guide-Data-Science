Title:
Ice Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6906039.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. light Karo
2 T. brown sugar
3 T. margarine
2 1/2 c. Rice Krispies
1/4 c. peanut butter
1/4 c. fudge sauce
3 T. light Karo
1 qt. softened vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Bring to boil 1/4 c. Karo, brown sugar & margarine.
Take off heat and add rice krispies.
Press into 9 inch pie pan sprayed with Pam.
Mix peanut butter, fudge sauce & light Karo.
Take 1/2 and spread over crust.
Freeze until firm.
Spread ice cream over crust.
Top with remaining sauce.
May add peanuts on top if wish.
Keep frozen.
