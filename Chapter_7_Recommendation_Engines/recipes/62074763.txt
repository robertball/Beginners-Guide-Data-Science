Title:
Newlywed Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-14 oz can Balsmic vinegar, basil & olive oil flavored diced tomatoes- with liquid
1 1/2 cup water
1 pkg. ramen noodle soup - oriental flavor
1/2 pound smoked suasage, sliced
1 14oz can corn - drained (or 1 cup frozen corn)
chopped brocolli - fresh or frozen

Directions:
1. place water and can of tomatoes in medium sauce pan, bring to boil
2. add ramen noodles, seasoning packet, corn, sausage & brocolli.
3. Bring back to a boil, reduce heat and simmer about 10 minutes. Add water, if neccesary. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
HEIFERYUNG
Description: 
As a newlywed, we often ate Ramen soup. I learned by adding some inexpensive ingredients it made more of a meal. Now my family asks for it by name! Easy to double for family size.
