Title:
Carrot souffle recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb carrots-- boiled until soft
1/2 stick butter
3 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
3 T flour
3 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Mash carrots while they are still hot.
Add butter and beat until they reach the consistency of chunky applesauce. Add the rest of the ingredients.
Bake in an 8x8 pan at 350 for about 40 minutes. Center should be set and edges light brown.
