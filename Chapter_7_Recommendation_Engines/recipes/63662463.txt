Title:
Boiling Water Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups shortening
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. milk
1/2 cup boiling water
4 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine shortening, salt, milk and boiling water. Whip with fork until smooth and creamy.
Add 2 cups flour and stir with round-the-bowl strokes until all flour is incorporated. Do not overwork or crust will be tough.

Submitter: 
lgmarge
Description: 
This recipe uses boiling water instead of ice water and the pastry turns out flaky, manageable, and delicious every time.Modified from: Hot Water Pie Crust I
