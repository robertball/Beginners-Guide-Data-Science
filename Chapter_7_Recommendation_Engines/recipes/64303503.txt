Title:
Abbey's Best Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole fryer chicken, giblets removed
3 stalks celery, cut in half
olive oil
3 T. himalayan pink sea salt
1 T. kosher sea salt
1 1/2 T. black pepper, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Wash chicken with cold water and pick off any pin feathers.  Pat dry with a paper towel all over.  Place in baking dish.
Pour pink salt and 1 T. pepper into the cavity of the chicken, making sure it spreads evenly inside the cavity.  Stuff cavity with celery.
Brush olive oil liberally on outside of chicken to coat all the skin. Sprinkle with remaining sea salt and pepper.
Place chicken in preheated oven and bake for 1 1/2 hours or until juices run clear when the thigh is pierced.  Cover chicken loosely with aluminum foil for 10 minutes before serving.
