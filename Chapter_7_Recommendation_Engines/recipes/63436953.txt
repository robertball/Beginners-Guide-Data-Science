Title:
Agilo E Olia Spaghetti with Shrimp (Spaghetti With Garlic And Oil) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4531612.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz.spaghetti
salt
4 tbsp.olive oil
4 garlic cloves
1 sprig parsley or fresh basil.
freshly ground pepper.

Directions:
Cook spaghetti in plenty of salted water,put the oil in a frying pan,mince the garlic and fry until golden brown,then discard.Keep the oil warm.Chop the parsley finely.Drain the pasta when al dente,season with the freshly ground pepper,and cover with the garlic flavored oil and chopped parsley.Mix well and serve hot.
Butter can be added at the end if you wish for a glossy finish.
If i add butter it is Natrel lactose free butter.

Submitter:

Description: 
Adding shrimp to this makes for a tasty meal..
Can be made Gluten free by using Gluten free spaghetti. Such as Catelli.
