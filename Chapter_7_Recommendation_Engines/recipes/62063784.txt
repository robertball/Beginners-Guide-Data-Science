Title:
Rice Balls ala Tim Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white long grain rice.
1/2 pd lean ground beef
2 Tsp Olive Oil
3 Cloves Garlic finely chopped
2 Cups of Red Sauce ( Jar or Fresh)reserve 1/2 cup
2 Eggs Beaten
1 Cup Breadcrumbs
1/2 Grated Parmesean Cheese
1/2 Shredded Mozzerella Cheese
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees. Cook rice according to package directions.
Coat non stick skillet with olive oil and saute garlic. Add ground beef and saute until brown. Drain off fat. Remove from heat.
In a large mixing bowl combine cooked rice, ground beef, salt and pepper, add 1/2 cup of sauce and stir. When combined add cheeses and stir until cheese is melted. Carefully roll softball sized balls and place on cookie sheet. Pop cookie sheet into refigerator until cool (About 20 minutes) When balls have stiffened up roll balls in beaten egg and then in breadcrumbs and place back on cookie sheet. Bake 25 minutes until outside shell is crispy. Cover with remaining sauce and serve.

Submitter: 
QUEENBEE28
Description: 
My father LOVES these- so I name them after him. I make these softball size as a main course for a quick filling dinner. Serve with a side of sauce and garlic bread.
