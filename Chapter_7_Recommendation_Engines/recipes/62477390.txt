Title:
Pumpkin Pudding Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cups crushed graham cracker crumbs
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/4 tsp pumpkin pie spice
6 tbsp melted butter
Pie Filling:
1 pkg (5.1 oz) instant vanilla pudding mix
1 can (12 oz) 2% evaporated milk
1 can (15 0z) pumpkin
3/4 tsp pumpkin pie spice
whipped cream for garnish, if desired

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Stir together graham cracker crumbs, sugar, pumpkin pie spice, and butter.  Press into bottom and sides of 8 1/2 or 9 inch pie plate.  Bake for 7 minutes, or until lightly browned.  Cool completely before filling.
For pie filling:
Whisk vanilla pudding mix and evaporated milk together for two minutes.  Chill for five minutes.
Stir pumpkin and pumpkin pie spice into pudding mixture until well combined.
Spread filling evenly in cooled pie crust and chill for 30 minutes or until ready to serve.
Garnish with whipped cream if desired.

Submitter: 
A.S.G.
Description: 
This quick, easy dessert is a flavorful alternative to traditional pumpkin pie.  It's short on effort, but big on flavor!
