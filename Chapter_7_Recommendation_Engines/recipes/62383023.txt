Title:
Big Daddy's Fig Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 frozen bananas, peeled and chopped
6 fresh figs, halved
1 scoop of Mila
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup orange juice

Directions:
Place the bananas, Mila, figs, milk, and orange juice into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
