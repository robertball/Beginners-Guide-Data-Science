Title:
Black Bean Soup with a side of Garlic, Kale, and Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Black Bean Soup
1 tbs EVOO
1 tbs garlic
1 small onion, diced
1 tbs cumin
2 15 oz cans black beans
2 C water or vegetable stock
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 tbs wheat free tamari
1 tbs lemon juice
chopped fresh cilantro for garnish
Garlic, Kale and Cauliflower
1 tbs EVOO
1 tbs garlic, minced
2 C cauliflower, small florets
6 C tuscan kale or lacinato, chopped
1/2 C water

Directions:
Heat the olive oil over medium heat in asoup pot.
Add the garlic and onions and cook until onion is translucent.
Add the cumin and saute a few more minutes.
Add the beans with liquid, water or stock, and bayleaf. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Add the tamari and lemon juice and simmer a minute more.
Top with cilantro.
Serve with Garlic, Kale and Cauliflower
Heat the oil in a saute pan over medium heat.  Add the garlic and cauliflower and saute until the cauliflower starts to soften.
Add the kale and water.  Cover and steam until the kale is tender and the water evaporates, about 3-4 minutes.
