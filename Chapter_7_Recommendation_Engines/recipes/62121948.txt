Title:
Dijon Mustard Baked Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of catfish
2 tablespoons of olive oil
2 tablespoons of season all(or any seasing you like)
1/2 cup of dijon mustard
1 teaspoon of soy sauce(low sodium)
1 teaspoon of worcestershire
1 lemon (juiced)
1 orange (juiced)
couple dashes of hot sauce
1 green bellpepper(sliced)
1 onion (sliced)
2 cloves of garlic(chopped)

Directions:
Season catfish and heat oil in a skillet on medium high.  Allow to cook for 1-2 minutes on both sides.  Then mix next 6 ingredients to make the sauce.  Baste or Rub sauce on both sides of each filet until well coated.  Pour any remaining sauce on top of fish.  Then add the sliced vegetables on top and the garlic.  Place in oven at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.  Take out and enjoy!!!!
