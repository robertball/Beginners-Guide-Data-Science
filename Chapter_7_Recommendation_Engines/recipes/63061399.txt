Title:
Havana Slow Cooker Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. pork tenderloin
1/2 cup orange juice
1 lemon or lime and the zest of the lemon/lime
1 onion, sliced
3 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp cumin
1 tbsp grilled season blend (like Montreal style)
1 tsp ground red pepper
1 tsp salt
3 tbsp red wine vinegar
3 bay leaves

Directions:
Place sliced onions in the bottom of slow cooker. Place pork tenderloin on top of onions. Sprinkle garlic evenly over both pork and onions. Pour orange juice and vinegar over the pork and squeeze the lemon or lime over the pork.  Add the cumin, season blend, salt, bay leaves,and red pepper evenly on top of the tenderloin. Last, add the lemon or lime zest on top of the pork.  Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours. Serve by slicing the tenderloin into 1 1/2 inch slices and drizzle some of the juices on top of each slice. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
This dish has bright flavors and becomes incredibly easy too with the help of a slow cooker! I came up with this recipe with the flavors and style of cooking meat slowly, inspired by my friend from Cuba.
