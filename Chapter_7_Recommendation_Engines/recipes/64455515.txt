Title:
Seafood Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Kosher salt
1/2 lb jumbo sea scollops(6-8)
1/3 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 c clamato juice(clam-tomato juice)
1/4 c ketchup
1/4 c fresh lime juice
3 tbs cilantro chopped
1 tsp seeded & finely chopped serrano
chile pepper
1 tsp hot sauce
fresh ground pepper
1/2 pound cooked small shrimp, peeled and deveined
saltined or tortilla chips for serving

Directions:
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the scallops, reduce the heat and poach gently for about 2 mins. Drain well and chill at least 30 mins or   overnight.
Stir together the onion, clam-tomato juice, ketchup, lime juice, cilantro, serrano chile pepper and hot sauce in a large bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
Quarter the scallops. Add the scallops and shrimp to the sauce and stir gently to combine. Divide among 4 glasses or bowls. Serve with saltines or chips
