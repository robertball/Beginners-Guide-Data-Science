Title:
Orzo With Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1019023.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups orzo(rice shaped pasta)
1 bunch fresh broccoli; in flowerets
1/4 cup olive oil
3 tablespoons pine nuts
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
3 1/2 ounces feta cheese; crumbled
3/4 cup black olive; sliced
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup fresh basil; chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook orzo, al dente about 8 minutes, add broccoli and cook until tender-crisp (about 2 minutes). Drain and transfer to a large bowl.
Heat oil in skillet, add pine nuts, cook 3 minutes until brown. Add red peppers and stir until aromatic (about 30 seconds). Remove from heat.
Pour over pasta, toss to coat. Add feta, olives, Parmesan and basil. Toss again. Season to taste.

Submitter: 
HOWEWASS
Description: 
Light tasting salad with orzo, broccoli, olives, pine nuts, Parmesan, and feta. Perfect accompaniment to grilled meats. Can be served warm or cold. Leftovers are delicious the next day. Recipe was discovered on scuba vacation on Little Cayman Island.
