Title:
Greens and Beans with Chicken Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can cannellini beans
10 oz fresh spinach
1 cup chicken broth
1 lb mild italian chicken sausage
1 tsp minced garlic
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
1/4 c. white wine
1/4 c. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
brown sausage and garlic
combine all other ingredients and simmer for 15 minutes
