Title:
Honey Beet Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Large Beets
3 Tablespoons Balsamic Glaze
1 Tablespoon Honey
½ Tablespoon Red Wine Vinegar
¼ Teaspoon Black Pepper
¼ Cup Chopped Sweet Onion
¼ Cup Chopped Walnuts
1/3 Cup High Quality Crumbled Blue Cheese
2 Tablespoons Fresh Chopped Parsley
Salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Chop the greens and roots off the beets.  Place in a baking dish with ¼ cup of water and cover with foil.  Bake for one hour or until soft.
Allow beets to cool overnight and remove outer skin when cooled.
Chop beats into bite sized pieces.
In a medium size bowl combine beets, balsamic vinegar, honey, red wine vinegar, and pepper.  Mix well.
Fold in onion and walnuts
Top with blue cheese, parsley, and salt to taste
Serve Chilled
