Title:
Cherry Coconut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup cold butter (no substitutes)
FILLING:
4 eggs
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup quartered maraschino cherries
3/4 cup flaked coconut

Directions:
In a bowl, combine flour and confectioners' sugar; cut in butter until crumbly. Press into a lightly greased 10-in. x 15-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on wire rack.
For filling, combine the eggs, sugar and vanilla in a bowl. Combine flour, baking powder and salt; add to the egg mixture. Stir in walnuts, cherries and coconut. Spread over crust. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until firm. Cool on a wire rack. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Monica
