Title:
Simple Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2322751.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fresh mangos or peaches; peeled and diced
1/2 cup diced onion; sautéed
1/2 tsp. olive oil
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp. curry
salt / pepper for seasoning

Directions:
Sauté the onions in 1/2 tsp. olive oil until translucent; add the rest of the ingredients.  Combine  over medium heat until the mixture simmers.  Simmer until the fruit breaks down and thickens, about 10-12 minutes.  Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
I received this recipe from my SIL Laura, when I needed to make a small amount of mango chutney.
