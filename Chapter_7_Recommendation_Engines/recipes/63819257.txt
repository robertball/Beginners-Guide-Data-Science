Title:
Susu's Texas Cavair Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans black-eyed peas
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 cup onion,minced
1 red and 1 green pepper, diced
2 green onions, minced
1 tomato chopped
1 can shoepeg corn
1 package good season garlic and herb salad dressing- make up recipe as directed

Directions:
Rinse and drain black-eyed peas and corn. Combine remaining ingredients with salad dressing and refrigerate 24 hrs.Toss before serving with Fritos or Tostitos.
I also put one small dark green chili pepper, chopped finely for a little zip.
