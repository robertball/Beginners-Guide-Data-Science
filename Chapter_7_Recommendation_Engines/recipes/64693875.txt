Title:
KC's Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked macaroni
2 (5 ounce) cans of tuna, drained (white albacore preferred)
1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of chicken
1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom
1 cup frozen peas
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese  - divided 1/2 cup and 1 1/2 cups
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups (2.8 oz package) crispy French fried onions
* Alternate topping - mix 1 cup panko bread crumbs with 2 tablespoons of melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Boil water, and liberally salt, and cook macaroni as directed
Drain macaroni, return to pot.
Combine macaroni, the two soups, two cans of tuna, peas, Dijon, onion powder, black pepper, and 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese.
Mix well, and then transfer into a casserole dish - 10 inch square /  or a 9x13-inch
Top with the remaining 1 1/2 cups of cheddar cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 25 minutes. Sprinkle with French fried onions and bake for another 5 minutes.
* If using panko topping sprinkle topping at 15 minutes, and bake for another 15 minutes until golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
Classic tuna casserole recipe that's not too dry, and full of flavor!
