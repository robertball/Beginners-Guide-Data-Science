Title:
Broccoli and Sausage with Penna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Italian Sausage links (hot or sweet)
1/4c. Oilve oil
2 small Onions Diced
1 head Broccoli
1 38oz Tomato Sauce
1 16oz Diced Tomatos
1 1# package penna Noodles
Italian Seasoning
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Bring 6qt water to a boil. In the meantime, dice the onion and cut the flowerlets off the broccoli head. put 2T. olive oil in a pan and bring to medium high heat. Cut the sausage links into chunks and brown in the olive oil. Take the broccoli stalks and chop into chunks. Now add another 2T. olive oil if desired and add the onion and broccoli stalks. Cook over medium heat till onion is soft. Add the penna to the boiling water and cook till almost el dante. Now add the broccoli flowerlets to the boiling water and cook about two minutes. Drain the broccoli and the penna and add to the sausage mix. Add the tomatos and sprinkle lightly with Italian seasoning. Heat through and serve. Top with parmesan cheese if desired.
