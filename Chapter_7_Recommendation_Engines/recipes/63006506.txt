Title:
Dine In with Take Out Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 folder of take-out menus
1 telephone book (if you have no folder of menus)
Paper
Pencil
Timer
Cash OR credit card

Directions:
Peruse the take out menus OR check out the restaurant listing in the Yellow Pages.
Decide which restaurant to call.
Make a list of the item each person chooses.
Call in the order, noting time for pick-up.
Set timer to remember when to leave.
Hop in car and go get dinner.
Optional:  Order pizza and have it delivered.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This recipe is for those times when you are just to busy to cook, when you are packing for vacation or just plain need a break from the kitchen!
