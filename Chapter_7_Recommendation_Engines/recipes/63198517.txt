Title:
Turkey-Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large red peppers, tops and seeds removed, then cut lengthwise
1 pound ground turkey
2 tbls olive oil
2 small onion, chopped
6 garlic clove, minced
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 cup milk
splash of half and half
1 cup chopped tomato (fresh or sun-dried)
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup Panko crumbs

Directions:
In a large saucepan, cook peppers in boiling water for 3 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water; set aside.
Heat oil in a skillet on med heat, cook the turkey, onion, mushrooms and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain and set aside. In the same skillet, melt butter. Stir in flour, salt and pepper until smooth. Gradually add milk. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 1-2 minutes or until thickened. Return turkey mixture to skillet. Stir in tomato and 2 tablespoons cheese; heat through. Spoon into peppers; sprinkle with the remaining cheese. Place in a greased 1-qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with Panko crumbs.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until peppers are tender and filling is hot.  Remove cover and broil for 5 mins.
