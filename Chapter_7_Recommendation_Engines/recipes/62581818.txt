Title:
Cathy D's Home Made Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c sugar
2 T flour
5 eggs
1 can Eagle milk ( I use fat free)
1 can evaporated milk ( I use fat free)
2 Tbsp pure vanilla
Milk or whipping cream to fill

Directions:
Mix all ingredients up to vanilla & pour into your ice cream canister. Fill to the top with milk or whipping cream ( we use the cream & it's the BEST!) You can also use Silk & sometimes we use chocolate. Add any additional items like fruit or candy & place in your ice cream maker.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is an amazing ice cream recipe that can be made to your tastes.
