Title:
New England Crab Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Phillips Crab Meat, lump or backfin
4 oz Olive oil
1/4 stick butter
1 lb Smoked bacon, crisped and crumbled
1 Large onion, chopped
1/3 each red, orange, yellow peppers, diced
48 oz Seafood broth
2 Lg potatoes
1/2 quart Milk
4 oz Half and Half
3 Tbsp Dry Sherry
2 Tbsp Old Bay Seasoning
1 Tbsp Parsley
1 Tbsp Thyme
1/4 C. Corn Starch

Directions:
Heat oil and butter. Melt together in a  large kettle.
Add half of the bacon and cook off for 15-20 minutes.
Add onion and peppers. Cook over medium heat for 20 minutes. Stir every 4 minutes.
In a separate medium pot, bring water and broth to a boil
Add potatoes and cook for 12 minutes.  Remove potatoes from medium pot and reserve liquids.
Add cooked potatoes to the large kettle, with the bacon/onion mixture.
To the large kettle, add milk, half and half, crab meat, corn starch and reserved cooking broth.
Add sherry, Old Bay, parsley, thyme and corn starch to kettle.
Simmer for 1 hour, stirring frequently.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
Crumble leftover bacon and extra crab on top. 
Serve while hot.
Makes 8 HEARTY servings
