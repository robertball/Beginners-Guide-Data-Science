Title:
Classic Campbelled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992680.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Celery Soup
8 eggs, beaten
1 dash ground black pepper
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
chopped fresh parsley or chives

Directions:
Stir soup until smooth.  Beat in eggs and pepper.
Heat butter in skillet.  Add egg mixture.  Cook until set but still very moist, stirring lightly.  Sprinkle with parsley.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
The secret to these extra-creamy scrambled eggs is the addition of Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup beaten into the eggs before cooking.
