Title:
Spicy Slow Cooker Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Potatoes medium, peeled and cubed
1 large onion
1 garlic clove, minced
2 green chile peppers, thinly sliced (optional for added kick!)
2 tbs olive oil or coconut oil
1 tbs mustard seed
1 tbs cumin seed
2 tbs hot red pepper flakes
1 tsp sugar
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Slice onions into very thin, long slices and set aside.
Add olive oil to bottom of slow cooker and layer sliced onions along bottom of slow cooker.
Add minced garlic, green chiles, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, sugar, salt and pepper.
Layer cubed potatoes and sprinkle salt and hot red pepper flakes on each layer.
Cover and set slow cooker on High for 3.5 to 4 hours.  Stir carefully before serving.

Submitter: 
Sriyani
Description: 
Easy spicy potatoes using unique spices all thrown into a slow cooker!  No pre-cooking anything!  This recipe is a modified version of an old Sri Lankan spicy potatoes recipe, but is for all tastes!
