Title:
Cobb Salad Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981578.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package TysonÂ® Frozen, Fully Cooked, Breaded Chicken Breast Tenders
2 cups chopped iceberg lettuce
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
2 cups bacon bits
1 ounce blue cheese, crumbled
1/4 cup reduced fat ranch dressing
1/2 cup prepared guacamole
4 (10 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
PREP: Prepare chicken according to package directions. Combine lettuce, tomatoes, bacon, cheese and dressing. For each roll-up, heat one tortilla in microwave 20 seconds. Spread a line of guacamole down center of tortilla; place 2 cooked chicken tenders on tortilla; top with one-fourth of salad mix. Fold in sides of tortilla and roll up into cylinder shape.
SERVE: Serve with tortilla chips and salsa, if desired.
CHILL: Refrigerate leftovers immediately.

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods
Description: 
A handheld version of this classic chicken salad served in a flour tortilla.
