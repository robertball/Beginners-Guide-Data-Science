Title:
White Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/564496.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup vanilla soymilk
4 cups milk
1/4 cup marshmallow creme

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine white chocolate chips and vanilla soymilk. Stir continuously until white chocolate chips have completely melted. Add 4 cups of milk and 1/4 cup marshmallow creme.
