Title:
Sausage and Peppers Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large bell peppers
4 Italian sausages
1 lb orecchiette pasta
4 tbsp olive oil
4 tbsp chopped garlic
3 jalapenos
2 cups gruyere cheese (grated)

Directions:
Cook Italian sausages according to package instructions.
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Drain; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
Slice peppers (bell and jalapeno) into strips.
Slice Italian sausages into bite-size pieces.
Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add garlic and all peppers; saute for 2-4 minutes.
Add cooked pasta, reserve water, sausages, 2 tbsp olive oil, and cheese.
Turn heat to high and stir constantly to combine and melt cheese.
Transfer to serving dish when all liquid is evaporated.
