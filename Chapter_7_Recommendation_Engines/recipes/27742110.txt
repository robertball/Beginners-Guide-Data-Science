Title:
Three Cheese Vegetable Lasanga Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (28 oz) no salt added tomato puree
1 can (14-1/2 oz) no salt added tomatoes, undrained and chopped
2 cups (6oz) sliced fresh mushrooms
1 zucchini, finely chopped
1 large green bell pepper, chopped
2 tsp. dried basil
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp. dried oregano leave crushed
2 cups (15oz) Sargento Light Ricotta Cheese
1 pack (10oz) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
2 egg whites
2 TBS. Sargento Fancy Shredded Parmesan cheese
8 oz. lasanga noodles, cooked according to package directions
3/4 cup Sargento Light Fancy Supreme Shredded Mozarella
3/4 cup Sargento mild Cheddar Cheese divided.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Spray large skillet with nonstick vegetable spray.  Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat; add onion and garlic.  Cook until tender, stirring occassionally.  Add tomato puree, tomatoes and liquid, mushrooms, zucchini, bell pepper, basil, salt, sugar, pepper flakes and oregano.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender.
Combine ricotta cheese, spinach, eff whites and parmesan cheese in medium bowl; mix well.  Spread 1 cup sauce in bottom of 13 x 9 baking dish.  Layer 3 lasanga noodles over sauce.  Top with half ricotta mixture and 2 cups of remaining sauce.  Combine mozarella and cheddar cheeses.  Sprinkle 3/4 cup cheese mixture over sauce.  Top with remaining noodles amd sauce.  Cover with foil; bake 30 minutes.  uncover; bake 15 minutes more.  Sprinkle remaining 3/4 cup cheese mixture.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
