Title:
summer bean salad/salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of black beans
1 can of chopped tomatoes
1 can of yellow kernel corn
1 chopped red onion
1/2 a lime, squeezed
1-2 tsps of Olive Oil
Cilantro leaves
chopped mango(optional)

Directions:
Drain cans into strainer.
Mix canned items first.
Add lime juice, red onion and olive oil.
Garnish with cilantro leaves.
enjoy!
I've found that having black corn chips make it really tasty. you can also add salt/pepper for taste.
