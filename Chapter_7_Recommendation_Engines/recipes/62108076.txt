Title:
Paradise Frozen Margaritas! Heaven in a sip! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup gold Tequila (I prefer Jose Quervo Especial Gold)
1/4 Orange Liquor (I use triple sec)
6 oz of frozen limeade concentrate (1/2 of a 12 oz can)
Ice
Lime slices and Kosher salt for rims

Directions:
Place first 3 ingredients in the blender. Add ice cubes to fill blender up to a scant 2/3 full. Blend until smooth but still frozen.
Run lime slice around rim. Dip rim in Kosher salt.
Place frozen margaritas into the glass.
Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
These are the best tasting and easiest margaritas. I have tried so long to find rhe perfect balance between yummy taste and easy prep, and here it is! With this recipe you will always have the ingredients on hand to create an instant party (even if it is just for you!). No more hours in the kitchen squeezing limes or gross artifical flavors that you get with a mix. Have it quick AND tasty with this recipe!
