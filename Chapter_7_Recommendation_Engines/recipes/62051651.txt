Title:
Peanut Butter Banana Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margerine
1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1 cup crushed banana chips
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 pinch of ground nutmeg

Directions:
-preheat oven to 350 F
-cream first five ingredients
-combine dry ingredients, blend into batter
-dust hands with flour, and roll into 3/4 inch balls and press gently with fork onto an ungreased cookie sheet.
-bake for 10 minutes untill lightly browned  and cool on wire rack
-ENJOY

Submitter: 
EBSEPKE
Description: 
Every kid loves peanut butter cookies, every kid loves banana chips!  So put them together to make a very tasty, much requested cookie.
