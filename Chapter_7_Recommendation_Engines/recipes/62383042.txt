Title:
Big Daddy's mixed fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana
1/4 cup frozen blueberries
3/4 cup frozen peach slices
1/4 cup yogurt
2 scoops of Mila
1/8 cup milk

Directions:
In a blender, combine banana, Mila, frozen blueberries, frozen peach slices. Blend until smooth. Add milk and blend to desired consistency. Pour into glasses and serve.
