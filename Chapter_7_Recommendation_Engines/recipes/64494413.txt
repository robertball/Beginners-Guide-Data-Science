Title:
Lemon-Butter Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (1 1/2-2 lbs total)
1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp lemon-pepper seasoning
1/3 c butter
2 lemons, sliced
2 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Place each breast between wax paper and pound to 1/4-1/8 inch thick
In shallow dish stir together flour and salt.
Dip chicken into mixture to coat.
Sprinkle chicken with lemon-pepper.
In large skillet, cook chicken In hot butter over med-high heat about six minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, turning once. Remove chicken to separate plate.
Add lemon slices to skillet, cook for 2-3 minutes or until slightly browned, turning once.
Return chicken to skillet, overlapping chicken and arranging lemon slices. Drizzle with lemon juice.
Cook for 2-3 minutes or until pan juices are reduced slightly.
Transfer to serving platter and pour pan juices over chicken.
Serve with rice.
