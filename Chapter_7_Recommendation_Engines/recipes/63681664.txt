Title:
Three Philosophers Chili: Yields 3.5 gallons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. Hot Italian Sausage (sliced)
5 lbs. Sweet Italian Sausage (sliced)
10 lbs. Beef (Top Round, cubed)
5 Large Green Peppers (diced)
5 Large Red Peppers (diced)
5 Large Yellow Onions (diced)
5 (15oz) cans black beanswash and rinse
5 (15oz) cans Red Kidney Beanswash and rinse
15 cloves garlic (chopped fine)
4 cans crushed tomatoes
5 tablespoons Cumin
7 tablespoons Dark Chili Powder
6 tablespoons Kosher Salt
6 tablespoons black pepper
4 Bay Leaves
1-tablespoon cayenne pepper
3 habanera peppers chopped fine
1 bottle Three Philosophers

Directions:
Brown beef and sausage.
Add Onion, Peppers, Garlic, Habanera Peppers and spices.
Add Bottle of Three Philosophers when onions become glassy. Allow to boil.
Add Tomatoes and beans. Bring to a slow boil, reduce to low heat and allow to cook for about 2 hours stirring occasionally
