Title:
Glazed Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken wings
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Cut chicken wings into three sections; discard wing tip section.  Season chicken with garlic and onion powder. Place in a greased broiler pan.  Combine barbecue sauce, honey and soy sauce.  Bake, uncovered, at 400 for 20 minutes, turning halfway. Add sauce to meat and then bake at 350 degrees F for 30-40 minutes or until chicken juices run clear. Broil for few minutes on each side in the end to crisp glaze.
