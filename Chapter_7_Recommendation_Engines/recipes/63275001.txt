Title:
Bacon Stuffed Jalapenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-14 Jalapenos
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 Cup Pepper Jack cheese, shredded
4-6 slices bacon, diced

Directions:
Heat grill on high.
Cook bacon until done.  Drain on paper towels.
Slice jalapenos in half and scoop out seeds.
Mix the cream cheese, pepper jack cheese and bacon together.
Spread the cream cheese mixture in the jalapeno halves.
Place the jalapenos on the grill.  Grill for 5-8 minutes.  Keep an eye on them so they don't burn.
