Title:
Smothered Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Chicken Breast
4 oz Cream Cheese
1 bunch fresh Spinach
6 oz sliced mushrooms
2 cloves Garlic minced
1/2 onion diced
8 Slices of Mozarella cheese
Seasoning salt
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup Milk

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 375
2.Season chicken breasts with seasoning salt
and cook in the oven for 20 minutes
3.While chicken is cooking
Saute chopped onion for 2-3 minutes till transparent 4.add minced garlic and cook for 3 more minutes
5.And spinach and stir ( will reduce greatly)
6.add cream cheese and stir until cream cheese has melted,
7.Add 1/4 cup milk to mixture and stir till thickens and milk reduces.Remove from heat.
8.Saute mushrooms in tablespoon butter until browned   ( do not salt as this prevents browning)
9.Once Mushrooms have browned add 1 cup water or (Chicken stock optional) and cook till liqued has reduced.
10.Mix with Spinach/Cream cheese mixture including broth from pan
11.Remove chicken from oven and flip over each breast, adding salt and pepper,
12.spread Spinach/ Mushroom mixture over Chicken then place slices of Mozarella cheese over each.
13.Return to oven and cook for 20 more minutes till chicken is cooked through.

Description: 
I tried a dish similar to this once and never found it again. So this is my version using ingrediants that my family and I love so it's a win win all around. I usually serve this with a salad and crusty bread because it is a heavy meal.
