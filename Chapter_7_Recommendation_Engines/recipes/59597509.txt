Title:
Sauerkraut Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can sauerkraut,drained and juice squeezed out
1 20 oz. can diced tomatoes w/ juice
3/4 C. brown sugar
1/4 cup butter
1/2 lb. bacon, cooked and diced

Directions:
Cook bacon and dice.
Butter an 8 x 8 baking dish.
Preheat oven to 350.
Mixed together the kraut and the tomatoes.
Pour into the dish.
Cut together the brown sugar and
butter.
Crumble over the mixture in dish.
Sprinkle the diced bacon over the top.
Bake for 30 minutes.
