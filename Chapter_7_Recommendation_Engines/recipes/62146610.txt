Title:
Grilled Peanut Butter and Apple Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices white bread
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 small apple
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Butter one side of each slice of bread.
Spread peanut butter on the other sides of each slice of bread.
Peel and core apple.
Cut apple into circles.
Place one slice of bread, butter side down, in frying pan.
Place apple circles on top of the peanut butter side.
Sprinkle cinnamon over apples.
Place other slice of bread, peanut butter side down, on top of apples and other slice of bread.
Cook until golden brown.
