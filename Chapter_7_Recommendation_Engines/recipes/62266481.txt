Title:
Raspberry Vinaigrette Hybrid Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/341734.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. olive oil
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. pureed raspberries
1/4 c. balsamic vinegar
1/4 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. poppy seeds
2 tsp. dijon mustard
2 tsp. lemon juice

Directions:
Pour it all together and shake, shake, shake!

Submitter: 
Jennifer DeFosse
Description: 
I borrowed heavily from several other recipes to make this sweet and tangy vinaigrette.  I had it for lunch with fresh spinach, craisins, walnuts and blue cheese crumbles.
