Title:
Four Cheese Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536156.jpg
Ingredients:
9 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
1 cup skim milk
3 ounces reduced-fat sharp cheddar cheese, grated
3 ounces reduced-fat Monterey Jack cheese, grated
1 1/2 ounces American cheese, grated
1 1/2 ounces reduced-fat mozzarella cheese, grated
1/2 teaspoon white pepper

Directions:
In large pot of boiling water, cook pasta wheels 8-10 minutes, until tender. Drain and
return to pot.
Add milk, cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, American cheese, mozzarella cheese
and white pepper. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, about 5 minutes, until cheeses are
melted.

Submitter: 
SlimCookins
Description: 
Macaroni with sharp cheddar, monterey jack, american, and mozzarella cheeses. Add cooked diced ham and turn it into a meal of its own with a side salad or steamed veggies.
