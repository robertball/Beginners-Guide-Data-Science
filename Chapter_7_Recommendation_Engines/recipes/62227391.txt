Title:
Savory Baked Panko encrusted Catfish for two. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/339412.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finally grated Parmesan cheese (i.e. Kraft's 100% grated Parmesan cheese)
3 tablespoons Kikkoman panko bread crumbs
1 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 egg white
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil (evoo)
2 (8 ounce) fillets catfish
Fresh lemon slices to squeeze over catfish at table.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a shallow dish, combine the Parmesan cheese, panko bread crumbs, paprika, kosher salt and pepper; set aside. In another shallow dish, whisk egg white and evoo together. Dip fillets in evoo mixture, then in Parmesan cheese/panko mixture. Place in a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
