Title:
Shepherd's Pie a la Mexicana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of ground beef
5 medium potatoes
1 bag frozen peas
1 can of corn (or frozen)
2 small onions
3 cloves of garlic
salt
Mrs. Dash spices- Spicy, Table blend
garlic powder
black pepper
taco spices
1/4 cup milk
olive oil

Directions:
Boil potatoes until soft. While potatoes are boiling, choponions and garlic into small pieces. Pour small amount of olive oil in a pan, fry onion and garlic until golden brown. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Add garlic powder, black pepper, salt, Mrs. Dash spices, taco blend spices to taste. Add green peas and corn to ground beef, cook for 10 minutes. After potatoes are boiled, put in blender or mash by hand. Melt butter, add to potatoes. Add milk, garlic powder, salt, and and any other spices to taste. Put ground beef and vegetables in a casserole dish, pour potatoes on top. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
