Title:
Blueberry Streusel Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
In a large bowl combine:
2 cups all purpose flour
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
1/4 cup sugar
* In another bowl combine:
6 tbsp melted butter or margarine
1 egg
1 cup low-fat yogurt or buttermilk
1 tbsp vanilla
1 cup fresh or frozen(not defrosted) blueberries
Streusel Topping:
* In a small bowl combine:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp almond flavoring
1/4 cup chopped almonds
Cut the butter into the flour , almond flavoring and sugar ,till mixture makes fine crumbs and then add almonds

Directions:
*Preheat oven to 375*
Grease muffin tin or use paper liners
Add liquid ingredients into the dry ingredients
Stir the batter just till combined
Divide batter into muffin tin ( makes 12)
Sprinkle the streusel topping onto each muffin
Bake for 20 minutes, and let cool for 5 minutes
