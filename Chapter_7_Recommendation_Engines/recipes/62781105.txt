Title:
Hush Puppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup self-rising cornmeal mix
2 heaping Tablespoons sugar
1/2 can creamed corn
1/2 small onion, chopped very fine
1/3 cup jarred jalapenos, chop fine

Directions:
In a medium bowl mix all of the ingredients.
Using a small bowl of water, wet a tablespoon to scoop out enough of the mixture to make a hush puppy.  Also wet the palm of one of your hands and place the mixture in hand to roll slightly into shape.
Fry in hot oil. Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is easy to make and makes the best hush puppies ever.  I got this recipe from a dear lady friend, Carolyn in Lousiana.
