Title:
Julie's Cheesy Chicken Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 lasagna noodles
3-4 cups cooked diced chicken
2 12 oz. cans evaporated milk
1 pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch dressing mix
pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Boil lasagna noodles according to directions, set aside.
In a skillet, simmer chicken, dry ranch dressing mix, evaporated milk and pepper for 20 minutes.
Layer greased 9x13 baking pan with 4 lasagna noodles to cover the bottom of the pan.
Spread the chicken mixture over the noodles.
Sprinkle 1/2 of both the cheddar and mozzarella cheeses over the chicken mixture.
Then layer again in same order.
Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and slightly browned on top.  Let rest 10 minutes prior to cutting.

Submitter: 
Joan Gerland Crabtree
Description: 
My sister Julie has made this recipe for years and it has turned into a family favorite.  Kids and adults love it and I love that it is easy to prepare and tastes wonderful.  It is truly a comfort food.  Goes great with a lettuce and fruit type salad along with garlic bread.
