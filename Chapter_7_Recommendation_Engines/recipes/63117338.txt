Title:
Blue Ribbon Apple Crumb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 Cups flour
2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup vegetable or canola oil
3 Tbsp milk
Crumb Topping:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup dark brown sugar, packed
1 cup flour
Additional:
6 Golden, Red Delicious, Gala, or other sweet apple
1/4 cup sugar
Cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Mix crust ingredients together with a fork and pat with fingers into 9-10 inch pie plate.  Pare and slice apples.  Add to apples 1/4 cup sugar and the cinnamon.   Mix together to coat apples.  Place apples in pie crust.  Mix crumb topping ingredients together with a fork or pastry cutter.  Spread crumbs over pie.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until golden and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Danielle W.
Description: 
Delicious and easy!  No rolling out the pie crust!
