Title:
Very Cherry Treats™ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989123.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
2 teaspoons cherry flavoring
1 teaspoon red food coloring (optional)
6 cups KELLOGG'S® RICE KRISPIES® cereal
1/2 cup dried tart cherries

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat. Stir in cherry flavoring and food coloring (if desired).
Add KELLOGG'S(R) RICE KRISPIES(R) cereal and dried cherries. Stir until well coated.
Using buttered spatula or wax paper evenly press mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Cool. Cut into 2-inch squares. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
These light red treats have real dried cherries mixed right in for a tart and sweet Valentine's Day snack.
