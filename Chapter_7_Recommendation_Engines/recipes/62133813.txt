Title:
Coconut Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese
1 stick of butter, softened
14 ounces coconut
1 tsp. vanilla
1 (2lb. bag) confectioners sugar
1 tsp. coconut extract
2 lb. bag of chocolate (Michael's or other Craft store)

Directions:
Mix together cream cheese, butter, coconut, vanilla, sugar and coconut extract in large mixing bowl.
Roll into small balls and place on wax paper.
Melt chocolate in microwave or in a double boiler.
Dip coconut balls into chocolate and return to wax paper to cool. For quicker cooling, place in freezer for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
This delicious and sweet dessert will disappear is seconds at your next party. More like a homemade chocolate candy than a cookie! Sprinkle with powdered sugar in the winter for a snowball effect.
