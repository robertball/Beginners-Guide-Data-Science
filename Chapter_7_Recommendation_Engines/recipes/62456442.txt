Title:
Sneaky Healthy Carrot Oatmeal Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups wheat flour
4 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
2 cups yogurt
2 Tablespoons canola oil
3 eggs, beaten
3 Tablespoons flax seed
1 1/2 cup vegetable or tomato juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup uncooked rolled oats
1 1/2 cup raisins
4 cups shredded carrots

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease muffin tins.
In a large bowl, mix the all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and pumpkin pie spice. Make a well in the center of the mixture, and add white sugar, brown sugar, canola oil, eggs, tomato juice, molasses, and vanilla. Mix just until evenly moist. Fold in the oats, raisins, and carrots.
Approximate bake times (350 degrees)
Mini Muffin Tins, cook 10-12 minutes
Large (regular sized tins, full) 15-17 minutes
