Title:
Chicken Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656422.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken cutlets
Tone's Citus Grill Seasoning
12 oz. of small broccoli florets (rinsed and drained)
2 Tbsp. of sun dried tomatoes, diced
1/2 regular sized fresh mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
1 cup of grape tomatoes cut in half
1 16 oz. bag of Alessi Casarecci pasta
1/4 cup basil pesto
2 Tbsp. of butter
salt and pepper to taste
grated paresan or pecorino romano cheese

Directions:
Sprinkle Tone's Citrus Grill seasoning on both side of chicken cutlest. Grill or cook in a pan. Cut into bite size pieces when done and set aside.
Steam broccoli for 2 minutes, drain and set aside
saute mushrooms in the butter
Cook pasta according to directions (15 minutes)and drain well. In a large skillet put your chicken, broccoli, mushrooms, sundried tomatoes and cooked pasta. Add pesto and sliced grape tomatoes. Stir well and heat til tomatoes are just slightly cooked. Salt and pepper to taste. At this time if you want to add more basil pesto you can. Serve on a plate and top with grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Nancy Gibson
Description: 
A lovely combination of chicken, vegetables and pasta dressed with pesto basil.
