Title:
Grandma's Beefy Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1/4 tsp. Italian seasoning
3 cups water
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 celery ribs, chopped
1/2 cup uncooked wild rice or white rice
1 tsp. beef bouillon granules
1/4 tsp. pepper
1-2 cans condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
2 oz chopped mushrooms

Directions:
In a saucepan, cook beef and Italian seasoning over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
Add 1 cup water, onion, celery, rice, bouillon, pepper; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 45 minutes.
Stir in soup, mushrooms, and remaining water.
Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
