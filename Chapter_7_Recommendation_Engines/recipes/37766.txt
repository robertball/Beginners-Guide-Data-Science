Title:
Creamed Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985260.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon lemon pepper
1/3 cup crushed buttery crackers or seasoned croutons

Directions:
Mix spinach, cream cheese, milk and seasoning salt until well blended.
Spoon mixture into 1-quart casserole. Sprinkle with crushed croutons.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 25 to 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
