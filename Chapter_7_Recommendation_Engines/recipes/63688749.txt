Title:
Marinated Grilled Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025636.jpg
Ingredients:
1 to 2 medium pork tenderloins, trimmed of most or all fat. (such as the ones you can buy at Sam's and Costco, that are not seasoned. 1 of the packages would equate to 2 medium tenderloins)
1 bottle (17oz.) Yoshida's Original Gourmet marinade and cooking sauce.

Directions:
1.    In a 1 gallon baggie add 1 or both tenderloins.  cover with Marinade seal and place in the refrigerator overnight, turning once in a while.
2.    when ready to grill; remove the meat from the refrigerator, take of of marinade discard marinade. place meat in a microwave safe dish and microwave for 3 minutes.  then put straight onto a preheated grill at medium heat.  cook 15 to 20 minutes or until at least 145 degrees internal temp.
3.    remove from grill cover and let rest for 5 to 10 minutes.  Then slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
This quick and easy 2 ingredient dish looks and tastes like you have spent hours making it.  We Rarely have leftovers.
