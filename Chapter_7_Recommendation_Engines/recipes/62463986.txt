Title:
Basic Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup water
1/2 t. vanilla
1 T. sugar (if making sweet crepes)
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
In a blender, place together the liquid ingredients. blend.  Gradually add in the butter, sugar, flour, & salt, blending until smooth. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
No need to be intimidated -crepes are surprisingly easy.  Mix simple, everyday ingredients -milk, egg, flour and the like -swirl the mix in a lightly oiled pan and fill with sweet or savory delights.Modified from: Basic Crepes
