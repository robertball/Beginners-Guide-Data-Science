Title:
Shari's Brushetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15 oz) cans petite cut tomatoes, drained
3 Tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/3 cup (or less) olive oil
1 package fresh basil, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup shredded parmesan
garlic and herb Alouette cheese spread
garlic bread toasts

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except cheese.
Lightly spread each piece of toast with the Alouette.
Top with a spoonful of tomato mixture.
Serve right away.  If it sits too long it goes soggy.

Submitter: 
Dutchgirl
Description: 
The cheese spread makes the difference in this twist on a traditional bruschetta.
