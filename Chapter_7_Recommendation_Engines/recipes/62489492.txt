Title:
Hobo Dinners Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/491364.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
4 large carrots, chopped
4 medium size potatoes, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook 450 F for 30 to 45 minutes
Fully cook ground beef, boiling carrots and potatoes until they are fully cooked. Then pour meat, carrots, potatoes, and onions into foil. Wrap the foil very tight so that it stays closed while cooking in oven.

Submitter:

Description: 
Throw anything you desire into some foil and bake and walla yummy hobo dinner.
