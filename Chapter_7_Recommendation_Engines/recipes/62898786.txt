Title:
SPINACH DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782961.jpg
Ingredients:
10 oz.pkg.frozen spinach
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1 cup hellmans mayo.
1 pkg.knorrs veg.soup mix
1 can chopped water chestnuts
3 chopped green onions

Directions:
Squeese all water from thawed spinach.In plastic container combine all ingredients together.Chill in fridge for several hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Creamy dip served with pumpernikel bread,mixed cubed bread or vegetables.
