Title:
Mamie's Mexician Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon
1 lb hamburger
1 cup drained whole corn
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 cup cornmeal
1/4 tsp black pepper
8oz. tomato sauce
2 ready made pie shells
Topping
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1 egg beaten
1/4 cup milk
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.Fry bacon and set aside. Leave small amount dripping in pan, brown hamburger in pan. Mix everything except for the topping ingredients in with hamburger. Pour into two 9 inch pie shells. Cover edge of crust with foil for first 20 minutes of baking time to prevent overbrowning, then remove. Bake in preheated 400 degree oven for 30 minutes.
Mix together topping ingredients. Remove pies from oven. Top with cheese mixture, spreading evenly.  Bake another 10 minutes until brown. Remove from oven. Top with cooked bacon and olives.

Submitter: 
Jeannie Johnson Lanier
Description: 
One dish main course.  Hamburger, corn, onions, green pepper, chili seasoning, topped with egg, milk and cheese mixture and put in a pie crust.
