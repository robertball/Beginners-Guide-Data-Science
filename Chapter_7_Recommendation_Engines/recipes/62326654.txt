Title:
Pounded Lime Pound Cake, Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 C. AP Flour
1 t. ground allspice
1/2 t. salt
1 t. Baking Powder
2 C. Unsalted Butter (room temp.)
2 3.4 C. Sugar
8 Large Eggs (Room temp.)
1/4 C. Soured Cream
2 T. Vanilla Extract (or the seed from 2 vanilla pods)
3 T. Finely grated Lime Zest
For the Glaze:
2 C. Powdered Sugar
1/4 cup freshly squeezed Lime Juice

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 325, placing your rack firmly in the centre of the oven.
In one bowl, mix together the flour, allspice, salt and baking powder.
In a separate bowl, cream together the butter and sugar.  When fully incororated, add the eggs one at a  time, and finally the soured cream and vanilla, creaming well.
Fold the dry into the wet, until just mixed.
Place in non-stick bundt pan, and place in oven.  Cook for 90 minutes, or until toothpick placed in the cake comes out clean. Removed from the pan, and allow to cool.
While the cake is cooking, mix together the powdered sugar and lime juice.  While cake is warm,but not hot, drizzle over the top.

Description: 
Yes, I came up with the title all by myself.  This is why I stick to cooking, and not creative writing!  This is a traditional pound cake, with the addition of lime and allspice.  It is wonderful with a lime-infused whipped cream, ice cream, or even toasted with a bit of lemon curd!
