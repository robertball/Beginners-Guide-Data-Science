Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup peanut butter
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg
2 Tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 3/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream together butter, peanut butter, and sugars.
Beat in egg, milk, and vanilla.
Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; add to creamed mixture.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet.  No need to press with fork.  Bake 11-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
NEgirl
Description: 
This is the only chocolate chip cookie recipe that I make, and the most requested by my family.  This cookie is moist and not too heavy on the peanut butter.  Goes great with a big glass of milk!
