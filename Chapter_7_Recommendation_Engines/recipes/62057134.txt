Title:
Roasted Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces Mushrooms, fresh, quartered large or whole button mushrooms
1 1/2 Tablespoons Onions, minced
1/2 teaspoon Garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon Thyme
1 1/2 Tablespoon White wine
1 Tablespoon Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 375.
Toss together all ingredients.
Place in a casserole dish, cover and cook for 35 minutes (stirring occasionally).
