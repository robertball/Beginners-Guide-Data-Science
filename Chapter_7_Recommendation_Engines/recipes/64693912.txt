Title:
Smoked Sausage and Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	1 Can of Cream of Chicken Soup or Mushroom Soup
•	1 Cup of Sour Cream
•	1 32-oz. bag of hash browns, defrosted
•	14 oz. smoked sausage, diced
•	1 onion, diced
•	1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese and/or Velveeta
•	1 Stick of Butter melted

Directions:
Place hash browns, smoked sausage, onion, sour cream and cheese together in a lightly greased crockpot. Pour over condensed soup and melted butter and stir. Cover and cook on high for 3 hours or until potatoes are soft and sausages are hot. Serve.
