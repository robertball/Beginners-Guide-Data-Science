Title:
Savory Garden Fresh Tomato Soup with Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 medium sized fresh tomatoes
1 large yellow onion chopped
4TBSP butter
2 tbsp flour
2tbsp white sugar or according to taste.
6 whole cloves
2 tsp chicken base
3 sprigs fresh thyme
3 sprigs fresh sweet basil
salt to taste

Directions:
Immerse tomatoes in a stock pot of boiling water to loosen skins.  Discard water.
Remove skins and cores and chop peeled tomatoes returning them to the empty stock pot.  Add cloves, thyme,  basil and chicken base. Cook tomatoes thouroughly, approximately 20 minutes. Remove thyme and cloves.
Puree remaining ingredients with immersion blender. Strain through a fine sieve to remove tomato seeds. Reserve cooked tomatoes in a large bowl.
Add 2 tbsp butter to empty stock pot over low/medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until opaque, approx 5 minutes.
Remove onions to bowl of cooked tomatoes reserving butter in stock pot. Add remaining 2 tbsp butter. When butter has melted stir in 2 tbsp flour making a reux. Add a small amount of the pureed tomatoes  into the stock pot stirring thouroughly to prevent lumps. Add remaining tomato puree and onions.
Add sugar and salt to taste. Blend briefly with immersion blender to reduce onion to desired size.
Let simmer additional 10 minutes to blend flavors. Serve hot with your favorite bread or as a side to any meal.

Description: 
A hearty healthy soup perfect for end of season tomato harvest when there is a chill in the air.
