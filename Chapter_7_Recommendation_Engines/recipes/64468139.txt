Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites-Team Charlie'sAngelsLoves#yoloswag Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2451555.jpg
Ingredients:
4 waffles (square)
1 bottle chocolate syrup
1 small bag powdered sugar
1 small package of strawberries
1 bag chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Cut the waffles int 4 squares.
2. Fill each small square with chocolate syrup.
3. Sprinkle powdered sugar over each quarter of the waffle.
4. Cut a thin slice of strawberry and cut a small slit in the bottom of it.
5. Use the tip of the knife to cut out jagged teeth on either side of the slit.
6. Place the strawberry on top of the waffle and place a small chocolate chip  on top, forming the eye.
