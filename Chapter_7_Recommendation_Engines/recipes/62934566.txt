Title:
Red Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Head of Red Cabbage
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/2 C. Water
1/2 C. Vinegar
2/3 C. Sugar
2 Apples, peeled and diced

Directions:
Bring cabbage, salt, and water to a rolling boil, then brain.
Add vinegar, sugar, and apples.
Bring to boil again, then turn down to simmer.
Simmer until the apples are camouflaged.
