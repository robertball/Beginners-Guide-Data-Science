Title:
Taco Salad With Lots of Stuff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head of Iceberg lettuce
6 leaves of green leaf lettuce
1 can of medium olives, drained and halved
2 scallions, thinly sliced
12 small cherry tomatoes, halved
1/4 large cucumber, cubed
3 radishes, sliced thin
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 lb. ground beef, browned, drained and seasoned with salt and pepper
1/2 bag warmed tortilla chips
Dressing:
1/2 C green tomatillo salsa
2T fresh lime juice
2T olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Rinse and chop very finely the lettuce leaves.  Toss in olives, tomato, cucumber and radishes.  In a separate bowl, combine dressing ingredients and whisk until blended.  Combine salad and dressing and toss.
Serve over warm chips with cheddar and ground beef.  Top with sour cream and chiles, if desired.

Submitter: 
Bellwether Nesingwary
Description: 
A taco salad with tons of vegetables for flavor and crunch, and lean ground beef for delicious flavor and protien.  Gluten free.
