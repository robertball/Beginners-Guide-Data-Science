Title:
Pecan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken pieces(breast or thigh)
2 eggs, whisked
3/4 cup finely chopped pecans
1/2 t dried oregano
1/2 t dried thyme
1/2 t paprika
1 T Olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Coat the bottom and sides of a 10x13 inch baking dish lightly with olive oil.
Pound the serving-sized chicken pieces to an even thickness.
Whisk the eggs in a shallow bowl or pie plate with salt and pepper.
Combine the pecans, herbs and spices in a shallow bowl or pie plate
Dip the chicken in the egg mixture, then in the nut coating (taking care to cover completely.
Place each piece into the baking dish as you complete the coating.
Bake until done, approximately 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
squawk93
Description: 
When I gave up all grains I needed to find an alternative breading to panko. Finely chopped pecans work well, and the chicken can be baked in the oven rather than sauteed on the stove.
