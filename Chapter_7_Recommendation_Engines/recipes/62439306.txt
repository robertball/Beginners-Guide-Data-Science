Title:
Saffron Mash Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/456601.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into chunks
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 tsp of saffron threads, crushed
5 cups chicken or vegetable stock
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup milk
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put the potatoes, garlic, and saffron in a large pot. Add the stock and bring to boil.
Cover and let simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Drain potatoes and return contents to pan. Add olive oil, milk, salt, and pepper. Mash until smooth.
Divide among 4 plates and serve.
