Title:
Milk Shakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup homemade yogurt
1/2 cup fresh or frozen fruit such as strawberries, peaches, raspberries, bananas, blueberries (if fresh fruit is used, add a few ice cubes)

Directions:
Place yogurt in blender and add fruit.
Sweeten to taste with a little honey or saccharin.
Blend until thick and creamy.
