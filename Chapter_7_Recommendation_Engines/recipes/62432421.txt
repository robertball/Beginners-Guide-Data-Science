Title:
Jackie Jo's Oven Chipped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451902.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium russet potatoes, scrubbed clean, unpeeled and thinly sliced
1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
1 Tbsp. grated onion
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsely
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Grease a 13"x9" baking pan.  Arrange potato slices in bottom of pan.  Combine melted butter, onion, salt and pepper and pour over potatoes.
Bake uncovered for 35- 40 minutes, or until potatoes are tender, stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle parsley, thyme and cheese over potatoes and continue to bake for another 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
This is one of my favorite potato recipes and comes from my Aunt Jackie Jo.  It's a wonderful blend of flavors with lots of cheese!
