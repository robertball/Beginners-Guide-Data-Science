Title:
Buttermilk Pound Cake II* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/459782.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder*
1/2 teaspoon Morton Lite® salt*
1 cup Benecol® margerine*
3 cups white sugar
7 eggs*
1 teaspoon almond extract*
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup low-fat buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 330 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease(or spray with Pam) one 9 or 10 inch tube pan.  Mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, beat margerine with sugar. Mix in the eggs, one at time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the almond and the vanilla extracts.  Gently mix in flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 60 minutes. Do not open oven door until after one hour. Reduce oven temp. to 325 degrees and cook another 30 minutes. When cake begins to pull away from the side of the pan it is done. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
