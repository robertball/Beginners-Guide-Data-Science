Title:
One-Skillet Pork Dish with Rice and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/701670.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Oz Olive Oil, To Fry Mushrooms
1 Oz Olive Oil, To Fry Pork
1 Oz Olive Oil, To Fry Rice
8 Oz mushrooms, sliced
8 Lbs pork, trimmed, 3/4 inch to 1 inch cubes
1/3 cup uncooked white rice
1 1/4 cups chicken broth
6 Oz brandy (Optional)
1 Onion, chopped
1 teaspoons oregano
1 teaspoons Basel
1 teaspoon rosemary
3 garlic cloves
1/2 cup of carrots
1/2 cup of peas
1/2 cup of corn
black pepper To Taste
Salt To Taste

Directions:
Put olive oil in fry pay
Add Mushrooms, And Fry Till Brown On High, And All Moisture Is Removed.
Remove Mushrooms From Pan
Put olive oil in fry pay
Add, Pork And Brown
Remove Pork From Pan
There should still be some oil in the pan, if there is more than 1 Tablespoons Of olive oil and pork fat, remove some.
Add Rice To Pan, Add Chicken Stock
Add oregano, Rosemary, And Basel
Cook Rice Covered For 15 Mins
Uncover Allow Any Excess stock to boil off.
add onions, And Garlic
Optional: Add 4 Oz Of Brandy, ignite Brandy, Allow Brandy to burn Off.
Add Pork, Carrots, Peas And Corn Cook For Another 5 Min's
all liquid should be absorbed or evaporated
Add mushrooms 1 Min's before
Add Salt And Pepper to taste
