Title:
Dump and Shake Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	14.5 oz. can of Diced Tomatoes
¼ C.    Frozen Diced Onion
½ tsp.  Minced Garlic
1 Tbsp.   Lime Juice
1 small can Green Chiles
2 tsp.   Cilantro Paste (in tube)
½ tsp.   Salt
½ Tbsp.   Tomato Paste (tube so you don’t have to open a can)
32 ounce Glass Jar

Directions:
“Dump” all the ingredients into the jar and “Shake” until well mixed.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Carol Castellucci Miller
Description: 
This recipe is great for throwing together a salsa with ingredients you can keep on hand.
