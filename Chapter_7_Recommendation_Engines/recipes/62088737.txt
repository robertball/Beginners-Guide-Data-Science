Title:
Summer Avocado & Mandarin Orange Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 avocados cut in 1/2 inch segments
2-15 oz cans manadarin oranges
1/2 small red onion sliced thin or diced
French or Catalina style salad dressing

Directions:
In a medium bowl or serving dish add  avocados, orange segments and red onion. Drizzle with salad dressing and toss lightly. Cover with plastic wrap and chill for 30 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Evan
Description: 
A quick and easy summer salad that my Mom has made for many years. If you have access to really good oranges - you can use those as well. You can also lay these in an arrangement and alternate between the orange, avacado and red onion. JUst slice them evenly.
