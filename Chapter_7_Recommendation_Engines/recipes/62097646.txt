Title:
Poppyseed Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. sugar
2eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 C. oil (Mazola)
3 C. flour
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 C. nuts
1 large evaporated milk
1/2 ground poppyseed or 1/2 can Solo poppyseed filling.

Directions:
Beat the sugar,eggs, vanilla and oil together. Sift and add the flour, salt and baking soda.
Blend in the nuts, evaporated milk and poppyseeds.
Pour into a tube pan and bake 1 hour 10 minutes at 350 degrees
When the cake is done, turn upside down and cool 1 hour.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Good when you want a different cake to bring to a bake sale or community gathering
