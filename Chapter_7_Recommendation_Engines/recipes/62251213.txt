Title:
Creamy Avacado-Black Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped avacados
1/2 cup reduced fat sour cream
3TBS fresh lime juice
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp salt
19oz can of black beans, rinsed
1 10oz pack of frozen corn, thawed
1 tomato chopped
2TBS chopped cilantro
Serve on wheat thins toasted multigrain chips.

Directions:
Mash avacados in large bowl; stir in sour cream, lime juice, cumin and salt.
Add all remaining ingredients except crackers; mix well.
Refrigerate 15 minutes.  Serve with crackers.
