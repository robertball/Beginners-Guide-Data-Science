Title:
Honey Mustard Herbal Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup light honey mustard salad dressing
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup skim milk
1 cup whole wheat cracker crumbs
1 tbsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp parsley
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dill weed
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp thyme
4 chicken breast halves

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine honey mustard, lemon juice, milk, cracker crumbs, and herbs.
Slice chicken breasts into 1 inch squares, and drench with marinade. Let sit in refrigerator for 20-30 minutes.
Spoon chicken pieces into skillet or wok and cook until done.
Serve over pasta and enjoy!

Submitter: 
erinelz
Description: 
A delicious, low-fat chicken dish to spread over any type of pasta for an original, flavorful meal.
