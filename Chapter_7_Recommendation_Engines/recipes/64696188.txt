Title:
Baked Olive Oil Lemon Rosemary Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rotisserie chicken, cut up
1/2 c olives (optional)
1/4 c olive juice/brine
1 c olive oil
1/4 c white wine
juice of 1/2 lemon
4-5 lemon wedges (can use these for lemon juice)
5-8 garlic cloves
5-6 sprigs fresh rosemary
salt
pepper

Directions:
heat oven to 400 degrees
cut rotisserie chicken into pieces and place in pan
combine remaining ingredients and pour over chicken
bake 30 min
serve with chimichurri, herbs, or alone
