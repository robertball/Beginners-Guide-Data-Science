Title:
Spanish Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans sweet corn 6 oz
1 can black beans 6 oz
1 larg tomato (2 smaller)(diced)
1 bunch green onion (thinly sliced)
1 can chopped black olive 2.5 oz.
1 bunch cilantro (chopped)
1 large red onion (diced)
1/3 cup olive oil
1/3 cup lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste
2 table spoons cumin powder

Directions:
combine all ingrediants in large bowl. serve with tortill chips cold.

Submitter: 
shahrzad
Description: 
huge hit at parties! Serve as an appetizer with tortilla chips.
