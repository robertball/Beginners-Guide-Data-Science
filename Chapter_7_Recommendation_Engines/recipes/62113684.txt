Title:
Very easy mushroom barley soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken broth
4 tbps. olive oil
¾ cup carrots
1 med onion chopped (1 cup)
½ cup celery chopped
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 lb. fresh mushrooms sliced (6 cups)
¾ cup barley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large soup pot over medium heat.  Stir in carrots, onions, celery and garlic.  Cook until onions are transparent.  About 5 minutes.
Add mushrooms.  Cook 2 minutes
Add chick broth and barley.  Bring to a boil over high heat.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover, simmer 50 minutes or until barley is tender.

Submitter:

Description: 
I love mushroom barley soup and I was originally making one that took about 3 hours to cook.  This one is so simple and easy and takes 1/3 of the time.  If you like mushroom barley soup, you will really like this.  It's perfect for a cold winter night.
