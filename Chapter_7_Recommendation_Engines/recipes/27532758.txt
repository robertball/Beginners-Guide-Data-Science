Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu en Crot with White Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3310376.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken:
1 pkg Frozen Pastry Sheets Thawed
2 Boneless Chicken Breast
1/2 lb Ham
1/2 lb Swiss
Sauce:
1/4 cup dry white wine, or chicken broth
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme

Directions:
Slice each chicken breast into three fillets. Using a meat tenderizer pound the chicken flat and alternately layer  2 slices of ham and 2 slices of swiss on top and roll.  Place the chicken-Ham roll on a pastry sheet fold the end up and roll from one side.  Dip a finger in water and blend any visible seams.
Repeat with the other five chicken fillets and bake in a preheated oven  425 for 28 min.
Ten minutes before removing the chicken, Pour wine into hot skillet; cook, stirring, until reduced by half, about 1 minute. Whisk in cream, mustard, and thyme. Cook, whisking, until thickened, about 2-4 minutes.
