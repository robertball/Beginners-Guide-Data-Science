Title:
Honey Berry Milk Shakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995356.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt
2 1/2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup honey

Directions:
In a blender, combine the ice cream, berries, milk and honey; cover and process until smooth. Pour into glasses.

Submitter: 
Mary Humann
Description: 
For sipping with breakfast or as a snack, this thick, smooth and creamy milk shake is a 'honey' of a treat that requires only four ingredients.
