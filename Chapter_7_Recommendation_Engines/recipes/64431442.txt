Title:
Best Hamburger Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 egg
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon beef bouillon
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
3 tablespoons bread crumbs
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat a grill for high heat.
In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef and egg.
Mix bouillon, Worcestershire sauce, garlic salt,  salt, and pepper.
Heat 15 seconds in microwave, and add to hamburger mix, mixing well.
Add crumbs and mix thoroughly.
Form into 2 patties.
Grill patties for 5 minutes per side on the hot grill, or until well done.
Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
These burgers are the best on the grill in the summertime. The addition of the egg and crumbs make this a burger that will hold its shape when turning. Serve on fresh hamburger buns with lots of toppings.Modified from: Best Hamburger Ever
