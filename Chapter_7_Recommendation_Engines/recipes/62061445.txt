Title:
Chicken Alfredo Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 small can mushrooms, chopped (optional)
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tbsp olive oil
4-6 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 tsp salt & ¼ tsp pepper (more or less to taste)
2 tbsp white cooking wine
2 packages Knorr Alfredo sauce mix, prepared as directed (or substitute 4 cups of your favorite Alfredo sauce)
8 oz spaghetti, cooked and drained
Shredded Parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese
Dried parsley

Directions:
Sauté onion, garlic and mushrooms in olive oil until onion is soft, add chicken, salt & pepper and white wine, cook until chicken is white throughout but do not overcook. Set aside.
Combine spaghetti, chicken mixture and sauce in 2 ½ quart baking dish, sprinkle with cheeses, and then lightly sprinkle with parsley.
Bake 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly at 350°

Submitter: 
Nicole
Description: 
Creamy Alfredo sauce, sauteed onion, garlic and mushroom with white wine makes this chicken dish a favorite with my family.
