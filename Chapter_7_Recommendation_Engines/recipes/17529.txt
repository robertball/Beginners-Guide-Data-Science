Title:
Grilled Chicken with Orange Cilantro Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975767.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mandarin oranges, drained, 1/4 cup juice reserved
1 cup fresh chopped cilantro
1/2 cup sliced red onion
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon ORTEGA® Diced Jalapenos
1 teaspoon salt
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
COMBINE oranges, cilantro, onion, olive oil, vinegar, jalapenos, reserved juice and salt in medium bowl. Cover; refrigerate salsa for at least 2 hours.
GRILL or broil chicken until golden on outside and no longer pink in center. Top each breast with salsa.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Salsas do not have to be tomato based. Any number of ingredients can be combined to make a Mexican salsa. Here's one with oranges, cilantro and jalapeno chile peppers that makes an excellent sauce to complement chicken.
