Title:
Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts -- OR -- 1 Fryer, cooked and chopped into bite size pieces
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Tomato Soup
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
Chili Peppers to taste, seeded and chopped
1 cup Chicken Broth
1 dozen Corn Tortillas, cut into bite size pieces
Brick or American Cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°.  Butter or spray casserole dish.
Combine all ingredients except for cheese.  Mix and place half in prepared casserole dish. Cover with cheese.  Repeat layers.
Bake for 1 hour at 350°.  This freezes well and is enough for 2 casseroles.
