Title:
Conference Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cinnamon Rolls:
6 cups flour
2  packages yeast
1/2 cups sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 cup milk
1 cup water
1/4 cup butter
2 eggs
1/3 cup butter melted
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
Glaze:
3 tablespoon water
2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 tsp maple extract or vanilla

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 375 degrees
In large bowl combine 2 cups flour, salt, yeast, 1/2 cup sugar. Mix well.
In saucepan, heat milk, water, & butter till warm. Add to flour mixture.
Add Eggs.
Mix till moistened, beat for 3 minutes at medium speed.
Add remaining flour. Kneed on floured surface.
Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
Punch down and divide into two.
Roll out flat and brush with butter. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar mixture and nuts.
Roll, cut, and place on baking sheet. Let rise for 30 minutes.
Bake for 20 minutes.
Combine glaze ingredients. Drizzle over hot rolls.
