Title:
Family Favorite Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp sweet relish
1 tsp minced onion
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1/4 grated parmesan cheese
1/2 (12-oz) bottle chili sauce
1 cup ketchup

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet, drain.  Add remaining ingredients & simmer 20-25 minutes.  Serve on hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
ACPHIFER
Description: 
This is a simple recipe that was given to me by my mother.  She served it when I was a child, and my family of 6 loves it too.
