Title:
Buttermilk Garlic Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 C. Sour Cream
1 C. Mayonnaise
1 1/2 tsp. Garlic powder
1/2 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Paprika
1/2 tsp. Freshly Ground Pepper
1 tsp. Mustard
2 Tbsp. Sugar
3/4 C. Buttermilk (Regular milk is OK too, but will taste different)

Directions:
Add ingredients to medium sized bowl and beat with wire whisk until smooth. Allow to sit in fridge for at least 30 minutes for optimum flavor. Serve over before once iceberg/romaine lettuce, diced tomatoes, cucumber, chopped bacon, and croutons

Submitter: 
ANGELSTAR
Description: 
This is a lot like the dressing from that local steakhouses house salad
