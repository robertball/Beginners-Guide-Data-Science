Title:
Wenny's White Cake Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1489904.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
2 fluid ounces vanilla-flavored vodka
2 fluid ounces hazelnut liqueur

Directions:
Fill a shaker with ice. Pour vanilla-flavored vodka and hazelnut liqueur into shaker; cover the shaker and shake until chilled. Strain into 4 shot glasses.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
These festive shots taste just like a piece of white cake! Follow shots by biting into sugar-coated lemon slices.
