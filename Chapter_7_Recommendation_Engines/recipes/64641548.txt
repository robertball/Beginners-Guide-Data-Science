Title:
Mish's Homemade Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9x13 glass baking dish
parchment paper
cooling rack
flour sifter
3 Large eggs
1 ½ cups white sugar
2 Tbs. brown sugar
2 Tsp. vanilla extract
¼ cup of brewed coffee( can use 1 tsp. instant coffee with ¼ cup water)
1 Tbs. sour cream
1 stick slightly melted butter
1 ½ cups sifted all purpose flour
¼ cup Herseys cocoa powder
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking powder

Directions:
Misty's Homemade Brownies
preheat oven to 350 degrees..meanwhile.
Mix well together in a large bowl:
3 Large eggs
1 ½ cups white sugar
2 Tbs. brown sugar
2 Tsp. vanilla extract
¼ cup of brewed coffee( can use 1 tsp. instant coffee with ¼ cup water)
1 Tbs. sour cream
1 stick slightly melted butter
In separate bowl mix dry ingredients:
1 ½ cups sifted all purpose flour
¼ cup Herseys cocoa powder
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking powder
Add the dry ingredients into the large bowl of premixed moist ingredients until smooth.
Line a 9x13 pan with parchment paper and pour the ingredients into the pan. Smooth it out evenly and then trim off any excess parchment paper from edges.
Bake at 350 degree 20-25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean when tested.
Remove from oven and place on flat surface. Using edges remaining carefully lift brownies out of pan and onto a cutting board. Using a sharp knife cut into preferred serving sizes.
Enjoy!
