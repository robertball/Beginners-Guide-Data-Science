Title:
Quinoa Vege Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rinsed quinoa, cooked in 3/4 cup lightly salted water (or you can use bulgar)
1 carrot
6 small green onions, cut up
1 clove minced fresh garlic
1 can rinsed garbanzo beans
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/4 c plain, whole wheat bread crumbs
1 t cumin, or to taste
1/4 c peas, can be frozen
1/4 c corn, can be frozen, fresh cut is best
Tahini sauce: 1/2 c plain yogurt, 3 T tahini, juice of 1 lemon, 1 t hot madras curry powder, fresh chopped cilantro

Directions:
Cook quinoa.  Process carrot lightly in food processor, add onion, garlic, beans, egg, bread crumbs, cumin, and quinoa.  Process lightly (pulsing) until mixed.  Transfer to medium sized bowl. Stir in corn and peas,season to taste, chill for 15 minutes.  Form into patties and pan fry in 2T olive oil over medium heat until golden brown.
Mix together ingredients for Tahini sauce.
Serve Burgers with sauce.
