Title:
Vanessa's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Skinless boneless chicken breasts
4 Tbs Olive oil
4 C Red Peppers, thinly sliced (or more!)
1 Onion, sliced
4 Large garlic cloves
1/4 C Fresh Basil
5 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
s + p

Directions:
Fry chicken till brown in 2 TBS oil. Remove.
Add 2 TBS oil and let warm 30 sec.
Cook peppers, onion, and garlic till wilting.
Stir in 1/2 basil and all vinegar.  Add chicken and simmer on low.
Add rest of basil and serve over brown rice.

Submitter: 
venymae
Description: 
Delicious chicken, marinated in balsamic, with lots of tender red peppers atop rice.
