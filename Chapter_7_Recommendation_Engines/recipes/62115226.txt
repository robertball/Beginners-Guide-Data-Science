Title:
Windmill Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter or margerine
3/4 c. white sugar
3/4 c. brown sugar
1 egg
2 c. flour
1 t. soda
1/2 t.salt
2 t. cinnamon
1 t. nutmeg
1 t. cloves
1 c. oatmeal

Directions:
Cream together butter, sugars and egg.
Add all of the dry ingredients and mix well.
Roll into small balls and place on cookie sheet.
Flatten each ball with a wet glass dipped in sugar.
Bake at 325 degrees for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
dutchkidzx2
Description: 
These spice cookies are also called Santa Claus cookies in our region that has a lot of Dutch influence. They are awesome dipped in milk and are great any time of year!
