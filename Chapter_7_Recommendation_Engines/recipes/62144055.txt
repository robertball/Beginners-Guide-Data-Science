Title:
Garlic Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 8 wedges (1 package) of Original Laughing Cow Wedges
- 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
- 5 cloves of garlic, minced (or to taste)
- 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mash wedges of Laughing Cow cheese in a bowl
Stir in mayonnaise, garlic and cheddar
Add additional mayonnaise to make a creamier spread
Serve!
(Can be refridgerated for a few days. Tastes amazing on crackers, bread, or almost anything!)

Submitter: 
Lin Snow
Description: 
Tingly, tasty garlic flavour makes this spread a hit!
