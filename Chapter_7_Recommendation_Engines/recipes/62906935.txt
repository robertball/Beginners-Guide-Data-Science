Title:
Caramel Crispy Rice Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
11 oz bag Kraft Premium Caramel Bits
16 oz bag of marshmallows
9 oz box of crisp rice cereal

Directions:
Spray a large mixing bowl with non-stick cooking spray.  Pour box of cereal into the prepared mixing bowl and set aside.
In a large sauce pan over low heat, melt the butter and Caramel Bits.  Add the bag of marshmallows.  Melt marshmallows in caramel mixture over low heat until smooth.
Immediately pour marshmallow mixture into center of of cereal in mixing bowl.  Stir until all of the cereal is coated with the marshmallow mixture.
Pour mixture into a baking pan prepared with non-stick cooking spray (I used a lasagna pan).  Spread evenly into baking dish with the back of a spoon sprayed with non-stick cooking spray or coated with butter.
Allow to cool completely.  Cut into squares of desired serving size.
