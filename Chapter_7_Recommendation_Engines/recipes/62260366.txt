Title:
Cheater Pancake Syrup 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup water
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a microwave-safe container, combine the white sugar, brown sugar and water. Microwave on high for 2 minutes, stir, and microwave for an additional minute.  Let cool slightly before stirring in the vanilla. Cool completely before transferring into the serving container. Store in a sealed container in the refrigerator. Be aware that it may crystallize a little if the container is not completely airtight.
