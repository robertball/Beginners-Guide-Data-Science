Title:
Stacie's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs ground beef
1 onion
1 pack of crakers
1 egg
1 pck of lipton onion soup mix
1 cup of Zesty Italian salad dressing
1/2 cup of lea & Perrins
garlic salt
pepper
1/2 cup of brown sugar
1/2 cup of ketchup/or tomatoe paste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375"
Mix the first 9 ingredients in larg bowl. Leave about 10 crackers out of mix.
Making sure to get it mixed together well.
Use seasonings to your taste.
Crumble remaining crackers and cover the bottom of your meatloaf pan or casserole dish.
Now add your meat mixture to pan making sure to press out any air.
Place in Oven.
Now place your brown sugar and ketchup into a bowl and microwave for a few seconds until soft.
Stir.
After about 30 mins. cover your loaf with this mixture and cook another 15 mins.
Enjoy
