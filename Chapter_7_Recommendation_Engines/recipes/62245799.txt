Title:
Mommy's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sliced apples
1 t. cinnamon
1/2 c. water
3/4 c. flour
1/2 c. butter
1 c. sugar (or a bit less)

Directions:
Wash, peel, and slice apples.  Place in greased baking dish.
Pour water over apples and sprinkle on cinnamon.
Work together flour, butter, and sugar until crumbly.  Sprinkle over top of apples.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
Serve hot.
