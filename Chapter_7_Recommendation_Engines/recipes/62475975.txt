Title:
Pumpkin Cheesecake Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
1/3 c. sugar
4 eggs
1 T. milk
3/4 c. all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1 c. sugar
2/3 c. canned pumpkin
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Grease a 15x10 baking pan; line with waxed paper; grease paper. Combine cream cheese and 1/3 c sugar; mix until blended. Add 1 egg and milk, beat to smooth. Spread in prepared pan, set aside. Stir together flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. In large mixer bowl beat remaining eggs at high speed with mixer for 5 minutes. Gradually add 1 cup sugar, beating until sugar dissolves. Stir in pumpkin. Fold in dry ingredients. Spread over cheese mixture. Bake in 375 oven for 15 minutes. Cool in pan 5 min. Loosen cake from sides of pan. Turn out onto towel sprinkled liberally with sifted powdered sugar. Carefully peel off paper. Starting with narrow end, roll cake up fairly tightly (without towel). Cool. You can sprinkle more powdered sugar over it, or make a cream cheese frosting.
