Title:
Rava Dosa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1c rice flour
2. 1/2c fine rava(indian cream of wheat)
3. 1/4c wheat flour
4. 1/2c yogurt
5. 1tbsp curry leaves finely chopped
6. 2tbsp hot green chilli peppers finely chopped
7. 1/8c thin cut cashew nuts.
8. 1tbsp oil
9. 1/8tsp mustard seeds.
10.1tsp salt to taste.
11.2cups water.

Directions:
In a mixing bowl add yogurt. Add salt.
Heat oil in a saucepan, add mustard seeds, let pop, turn off heat & add chilli pepper + curry leaves. Let cool for 5 mins then add to yogurt.
Mix all 3 flours separately in a bowl.
Add 1cup water to yogurt, mix well.
Add flour mixture gradually to yougert water removing all lumps and adding rest of the water gradually.
consistancy of the liquid should be thinner than pancake mix or same as fat free italian salad dressing.
Pour batter to make dosas on a saucepan-non stick-by circular motion holding laydle high. You will not be able to spread this dosa like traditional dosa.
The saucepan should be sprayed with cokking oil and heated mod warm.
First dosa may not come out as good, but as the tawa heats up doas will brown evenly & will separate from tawa easily.
Pour 1/2 tsp oil on edges of dosa, cover to cook. When edges are brown flip it by slowly saparating dosa from edges. let cook 1/2 the time, remove.
