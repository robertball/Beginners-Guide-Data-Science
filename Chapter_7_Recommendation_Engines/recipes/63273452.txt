Title:
Spicy Taco Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz . Sour Cream and Chives
Juice of 1 lime
1 jalapeno, seeds removed and sliced
1 serrano pepper, seeds removed and sliced
1/2 bunch cilantro leaves, finely chopped
salt to taste

Directions:
In blender add lime juice, sour cream and chives, jalapeno, serrano pepper and cilantro.  Blend on slow for several minutes (3-4) using a utenil if needed to keep blending until smooth. Season with salt if desired.  Pour into bowl and serve with tacos.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This adds a burst of flavor to any taco.  It is creamy and spicy, goes great on top of shredded chicken or fish tacos.
