Title:
Pinto Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic
3T lime juice (about 1-1/2 limes)
2t kosher salt
1/4t chili powder
1/4c EVOO
1 can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1-1/3c fresh corn kernels
1/2 small red onion, chopped
1 yellow or orange bell pepper, seeded and diced
kosher salt and fresh pepper
1c cherry tomatoes, halved
1 small avocado, seeded and diced
1/4c fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Smash garlic clove, sprinkle with salt, and mash into a paste.
Whisk with lime juice, chili powder and salt.
Whisk in olive oil.
Toss beans, corn, pepper, and onion.
Add dressing and coat evenly.
Fold in tomatoes, avocado, and cilantro.
