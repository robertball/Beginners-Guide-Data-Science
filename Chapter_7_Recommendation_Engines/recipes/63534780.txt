Title:
Healthy Pesto Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole wheat pita
1 14 oz(or whatever you have) can drained tuna
Your preferred amount of diced red or white onion
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1 Tablespoon pesto
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon minced garlic(optional)
1 teaspoon olive oil mayonnaise(optional)

Directions:
1. Drain canned tuna, combine in a bowl with onion, olive oil, pesto, lemon juice, garlic, and mayo.
2. Toast your pita bread and put the goodness inside.
ENJOY

Submitter: 
Kiowa Pendleton
Description: 
My 1 year old daughter loves this meal! I love making it as a quick, healthy lunch for me and her to enjoy on the run.
