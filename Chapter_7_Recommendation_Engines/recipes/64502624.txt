Title:
Apple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oil
2 cups sugar
3 eggs
2-21/2 cups chopped tart apples
3 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Beat together eggs, oil and sugar.
Stir in apples and vanilla.
Add dry ingredients.
Bake @350 degrees for 50 minutes in 2 ungreased  loaf pans.
