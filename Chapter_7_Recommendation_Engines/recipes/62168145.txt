Title:
Peanut Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Reese's Pieces
1 cup White Chocolate Chips
2 cups Rice Krispies
2 cups salted peanuts

Directions:
In a medium size bowl melt the chocolate chips and Reese's Pieces together on 30 second intervals.
Mix in the Rice Krispies and peanuts.
Spread onto wax paper or cookie sheet and cool.
Break into pieces and enjoy

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
I was experimenting last Halloween, trying to find a sweet treat. I got this kind of weird combination, but for me it worked.
