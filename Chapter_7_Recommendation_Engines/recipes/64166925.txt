Title:
Lentil Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown lentils
1/2 cup red lentils
4 cups (2 L) stock.
2 large potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 stalk celery, finely chopped
1 large carrot, finely chopped
I large yellow onion, diced.
1 tablespoons chicken or veggie bouillon powder.
3 chopped garlic cloves
1 TBSP curry
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Rinse lentils in cold water several times before adding to cooking pot.
In a large-size cooking pot, add lentils and stock and bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium.
After 20 mins add potatoes.
After another 10 mins, add everything else. Bring back to boil, then reduce to medium.
Cook until potatoes are tender.
Using a hand blender partially or fully puree the soup.
Adjust seasonings to taste.
