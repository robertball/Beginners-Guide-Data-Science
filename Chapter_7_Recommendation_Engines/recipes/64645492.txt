Title:
Big Ray's Tahini Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4526241.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Tahini
1/4 cup honey
2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 whole chicken (3 1/2 lb)

Directions:
Preheat ove to 350.
Combine first 4 ingredients.  Rub 1/2 under the shin of the chicken and remaining over the skin.  Roast for about 1 hour, or until thermometer inserted into thickest portion of thigh registers 160.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Easy to make and a tasty treat for the family.  serve with roasted sweet potatoes and a salad.
