Title:
Kathy's Award Winning Barbeque Sauce 2.0 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 cup molasses
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon onion power
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 tablespoons liquid smoke  flavoring
1 tablespoon chipotle pepper powder

Directions:
In a small pot, combine the ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, molasses, brown sugar, onion powder, garlic powder, ground black pepper, cayenne pepper, lemon juice, tomato sauce,chipotle pepper powder and liquid smoke flavoring.
Place saucepan on the stove over medium heat. Bring mixture to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer for about 1 hour, or to desired thickness.
