Title:
Mom's Salmon Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 potatoes
1 can salmon drained) or 1# fresh salmon, or left overcooked salmon, cut in pieces
1 tsp. salt (or less if using canned salmon)
½ tsp. pepper
1 medium onion
4+ T flour
1 c. milk
2 pats butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. Peel and slice potatoes and onions. Butter casserole dish. Place one layer of potatoes and onion in csserole dish. Add salmon. Sprinkle a few T of flour until salmon layer is covered. Sprinkle with salt & pepper. Cover with second layer of onion and potato slices, sprinkle with salt & pepper. Add milk and top with butter. Cover and bake at 350° for an hour and fifteen minutes or until golden brown.
Note: this recipe can be made omitting flour and using water instead of milk for great results.

Submitter: 
Andytofu
Description: 
During World War II when meat was scarce, my Mother used to feed her large family casseroles using whatever meat or fish was available. My favorite is a type of salmon casserole which not only tasted great but one which satisfied her family of 7 kids.
