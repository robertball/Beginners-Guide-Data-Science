Title:
Kielbasa Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. polska kielbasa.
18 oz. beer(I use Miller Lite).
18 oz. BBQ sauce(your preference).
1/2 cup brown sugar.
1/4 cup Dijon mustard.

Directions:
Cut polska kielbasa into 1/2 inch slices. Set aside.
Combine rest of ingredients in large pan.
Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
Turn down to a simmer and add kielbasa.
Simmer for 1 hour until tender.
Serve with toothpicks and enjoy!
