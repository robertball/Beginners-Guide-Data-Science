Title:
Beef Short Ribs Rice Bowl (Gyu kalbi don) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200 grams beef
1/3 pc bitter melon
1/4 pc onion
60 g shredded konnyaku
5 pcs garlic scapes
1 tsp ginger
1/4 cup kombu dashi
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp sake
1 Tbsp mirin
1 Tbsp sugar
1 tsp cornstarch
2 tsp water
steamed rice (for 2)
myoga (garnish)

Directions:
Slice the bitter melon along its length then take the seeds at the center using a spoon. Slice thinly crosswise then soak in water with salt for at least 5 minutes. Drain before using.
In a small bowl, combine the dashi, soy sauce, sake, mirin and sugar. Mix well then pour over beef short ribs. Marinade for about 30 minutes.
Add the bitter melon, onion, konnyaku, ginger and garlic scapes to the beef. Cover then microwave for 4 minutes using 600W.
Take the bowl from the microwave. Dissolve cornstarch in 2 tsp water then pour over the beef. Mix well then return to the microwave and cook for another minute.
Pour the beef over steamed rice then add some myoga for garnish then you can serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
Japanese beef rice bowl using short ribs with bitter melon.
