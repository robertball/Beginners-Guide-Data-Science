Title:
Joy Notes Cherry Salad Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. raspberry flavored gelatin
3 oz. lemon flavored gelatin
1/3 C. mayonnaise
21 oz. can cherry pie filling
3 oz. cream cheese, softened
8 3/4 oz. can CRUSHED pineapple, UNDRAINED (1 Cup)
1/2 C. heavy whipping cream
1 C. tiny marshmallows

Directions:
Dissolve raspberry gelatin in 1 CUP boiling water, gently stir in pie filling.  Turn into 9 x 9 x 2" baking dish and chill until partially set. Next, dissolve the lemon gelatin in 1 CUP boiling water and set aside.  Beat together the cream cheese and mayonnaise and carefully fold into the dissolved lemon gelatin.  Stir in UNDRAINED crushed pineapple.  Next, whip 1/2 C. heavy whipping cream and fold the whipped cream into the lemon/pineapple mixutre.  Stir in marshmallows.  Spread on top of the cherry layer.  Chill several hours or over night, until set.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
A refreshing, make-ahead fuit salad, that's perfect for a picnic or served on a holiday buffet. This recipe requires some time to prepare.  But, it is really worth the effort.
