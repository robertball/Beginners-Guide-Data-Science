Title:
Gluten Free Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup Bisquick Gluten Free Mix
1/3 Cup Milk
2 Tablespoons Butter, Melted
1 Egg
1 Tablespoon Chopped Parsley
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together.  Let sit 5-10 minutes before cooking. Drop by spoons full into simmering broth.  Cook uncovered over LOW heat 10 minutes.  Cover and cook 15 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
battgirl6
Description: 
Perfect for Chicken and Dumplings. Quick, easy and gluten-free.  They have a light, fluffy texture, a bit different than the heavier traditional dumpling, but still good!
