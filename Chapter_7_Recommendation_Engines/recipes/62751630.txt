Title:
Scalloped Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz. frozen corn
1/2 cup cream
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
2 eggs
1/2 cup butter
1 (8.5 ounce) package corn muffin mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
Mix together corn, sour cream, eggs, and melted butter.  Mix in muffin mix.  Pour into prepared baking dish.
Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until a knife inserted midway into pan comes out clean. Watch carefully and don't overbake.
