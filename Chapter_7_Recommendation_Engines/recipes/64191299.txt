Title:
Red Cabbage Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
2 Tablespoons Asian fish sauce
2 Tablespoons sugar
1 3/4 pounds red cabbage, cored and very thinly sliced
1 cup finely chopped parsley
Salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, whish the lime juice with the vinegar, fish sauce and sugar.  Add the cabbage, parsley and a generous pinch of salt and toss well.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.  Season with salt and serve

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
This is a Burmese-Thai slaw from San Francisco.
