Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter (smooth or chunky)
1 egg, beaten
1 cup sugar
Chocolate kiss candies

Directions:
Mix peanut butter and sugar, add beaten egg and mix well. Roll dough into small balls and place on greased cookie sheet. Press chocolate kiss candy into middle of each ball. bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pamela Euless-Barker
Description: 
This is a flour free cookie recipe. It's fast and easy.
