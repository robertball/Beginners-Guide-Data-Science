Title:
Garlic Soy Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Soy Sauce
Garlic Powder
Pepper
1/4 cup sugar
chicken - any kind

Directions:
Cover chicken with soy sauce.
Sprinkle w/garlic powder and pepper to taste.
Stir in 1/4 cup sugar.
Marinade overnight for best results
Grill and serve with rice
