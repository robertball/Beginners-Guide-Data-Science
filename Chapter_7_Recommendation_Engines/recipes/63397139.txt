Title:
Mini Corn Dog Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/913453.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. melted butter
1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 c. buttermilk
1/2 t. baking soda
1 c. corneal
1 c. all purpose flour
1/2 t. salt
8-10 (all beed) hot dogs, cut into 1" bites

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Combine butter and sugar in a bowl and whisk to combine. Add eggs and whisk again. Add buttermilk.
In a seperate bowl, combine baking soda, cornmeal, flour and salt and stir. Whisk into wet ingredients.
Spray a mini muffin tin and spoon 1 Tablespoon of batter into each. Place a hot dog into the middle of each cup.
Bake 8-12 minutes or until golden brown and cornbread is set. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pan.
