Title:
From-Scratch Venison Stroganoff - Revised Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed lean venison
1/4 cup almond flour seasoned with salt and pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
2 (8 ounce) package sliced mushrooms
1/2 - 1 cup good red wine
2 onions, thick sliced
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Toss venison cubes with flour to coat. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil and butter to in a large skillet. Add venison, and cook until well browned, then remove and set aside. Add mushrooms and wine and cook until softened and beginning to reduce. Add tomato sauce, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and Worcestershire sauce.
Place onion slices in bottom of slow cooker.  Add venison, the pour sauces and mushrooms over the top.
Cook on low for 8 hours - meat will be fork tender.
Stir in sour cream to warm it and serve with a fresh salad.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
This venison stroganoff is baked all day in the slow cooker. It's wonderful as a lower-carb dinner alternative - serve with a salad and roasted brussels sprouts!Modified from: From-Scratch Venison Stroganoff
