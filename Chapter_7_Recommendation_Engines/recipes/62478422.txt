Title:
Sausage & Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (1 lb pkg) Sauerkraut, rinsed
1 TBSP caraway seeds (optional)
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 apple, diced
1/2 lb bacon cut into 1" x 1" pieces
1 large onion, chopped
1.5 lb Polska Kielbasa (Polish sausage) sliced in 1" rounds

Directions:
Simmer sauerkraut, caraway seeds (optional), brown sugar, and apple for at least 2 hours.
Fry bacon and add to already simmered ingredients.
Cook Polish sausage and add to above mixture.  Add onions and bake in casserole dish for about 1 hour in 325 degree oven.
