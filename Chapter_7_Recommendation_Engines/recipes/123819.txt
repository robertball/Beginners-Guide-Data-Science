Title:
Favorite Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979525.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chili sauce
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup prepared mustard
2 teaspoons chili powder
12 hamburger buns, split
12 slices Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook beef and onion until beef is browned; drain. Add chili sauce, water, mustard and chili powder. Simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Spoon 1/2 cup onto each bun; top with a cheese slice.

Submitter: 
Eleanor  Mielke
Description: 
I've prepared these sandwiches for years. I've tried many sloppy joe recipes, but this one is the best by far. It also travels well for picnics or potlucks.--Eleanor Mielke, Snohomish, Washington
