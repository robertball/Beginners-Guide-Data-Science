Title:
Ham Loaf with red currant sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one pound ground ham
3/4 pound ground pork
one cup soft bread crumbs
1/3 cup chopped onion
one large beaten egg
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, mix thoroughly. Shape into loaf in shallow baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees for 1.5 hrs or until done.  To make red currant sauce:  Combine 1/2 cup currant jelly (dark), one and 1/2 tsp. grated orange peel, 4 Tbsp. orange juice, and one large Tbsp. horseradish in the jar. Heat and stir sauce until smooth.  I double this and serve in a bowl to pour over the loaf.
