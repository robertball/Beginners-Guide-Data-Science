Title:
gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
can of cream of mushroom soup
dripping of turkey meat or other
baking soda for thickener

Directions:
When finished baking the meat use the drippings to make a delicious gravy.  take couple heaping tablespoons of the baking soda and add to very warm water.  mix this together before adding it to the drippings and thicken up.   May add cream of mushroom soup

Submitter: 
Cheryl Ann
Description: 
turkey gravy or whatever meat you want to use as long as there are drippings left
