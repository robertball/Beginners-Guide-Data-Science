Title:
Remoulade Sauce for Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c mayo
1 1/2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
2 tsp dijon mustard
1/8 tsp ground pepper
1 tsp prepared white horseradish
1 T finely diced onion ( optional)

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together, Refrigerat for 1hr to allow for flavors to blend Serve Cold.

Submitter: 
Joanne Dembinski
Description: 
A little switch from tartar sauce to serve w/ crab cakes
