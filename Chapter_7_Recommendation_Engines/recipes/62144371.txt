Title:
Big Perm's Bloody Mary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of Ice
1-2 Shots of Vodka
Worcestershire Sauce (Enough to discolor liquid)
1 Tsp. of Horseradish Sauce
2 Dashes of Hot Sauce ( Optional )
1 Tsp. of Celery Salt
Splash of Olive or Pickle Juice (Lemon works too)
Fresh Ground Black Pepper
Clamato Juice
Your favorite premixed Bloody Mix
1 Beef Stick or Celery stalk ( Optional )
2 Green Olives

Directions:
Fill 1 glass of your choice with ice
Pour in Vodka ( In Wisconsin we use 2:)
Splash in Worcestershire Sauce until vodka is discolored
Add Horse Radish, Hot Sauce, Celery Salt, and Pepper into the mix
Fill Remainder of glass with 1/2 premix and 1/2 Clamato Juice to the top
Take another glass of same size and pour the mix back and forth about 4 times. ( It's known as rolling. )
Add Olives, Beef Sticks, Celery and/or any other garnishes you may enjoy.
Drink Up!

Submitter: 
Big Perm
Description: 
A tasty Breakfast beverage after a good long night.
