Title:
Italian Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb. Italian Sausage (casings removed)
3 cups thinly sliced mushrooms
1 cup chopped onion
2 garlic cloves(minced)
28 oz. can chopped tomatoes
5 1/4 cups water & 5 beef bullion cubes
1 1/2 cups hearty red wine
1 tsp. dried basil leaves
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1 8 oz pkg. Ronzoni Ricotta & Spinach tortellini (uncooked)
2 cups chopped zucchini

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook sausage until crumbly and drain.
Add mushrooms, garlic; cook until onion and mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
Add tomatoes with juice, water bullion, wine and seasonings; heat to boiling.
Reduce heat and simmer, covered for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Return to boiling; stir in pasta. Â Cook 12 minutes.
Stir in zucchini; simmer 5 minutes or until tender.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
