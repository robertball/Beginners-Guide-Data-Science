Title:
Trailside Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 C. shortening or margarine
1 C. sugar
1 C. brown sugar
1/2 C. crunchy peanut butter
2 eggs
1/4 C. milk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 C. uncooked oatmeal
1/2 C. chocolate chips
1/2 C. raisins

Directions:
In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking soda and salt together.  Set aside.  Beat shortening or margarine, sugars, and peanut butter until creamy.  Beat in eggs, milk, and vanilla.  Stir in flour mixture. Add oats, chips and raisins.  Drop on cookie sheets. (Sprayed with non-stick cooking spray)
*Using shortening will make the cookies crunchier while using margarine will make the cookies softer.
Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
jeanneferg
Description: 
Combines crunchy peanut butter, chocolate chips and raisins for a jazzed up oatmeal cookie.
