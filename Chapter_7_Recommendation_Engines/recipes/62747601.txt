Title:
Lemon Caper Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsps Finely Chopped Shallots
2 tsps Lemon Zest
1 Tbsp Capers, Drained
2 Tbsps Flat Leaf Parlsey, Chopped
3 Tbsps Extra Virgeb Olive Oil
Salt & Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients into a food processor.  Blend until smooth.  Serve over grilled or steamed vegetables.
