Title:
Grilled steaks with onion sauce and onion relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp cracked black pepper
2 dry bay leaves,crumbled
1 tbsp asian fish sauce
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
six - 12-14oz ribeye steaks, 3/4 inch thick
4 tbsps unsalted butter
1 lb red onion, thinly sliced
2 pickled jalapenos, seeded
1 tbsp dry red wine
2 tbsp red wine vinegar
salt and pepper
1/2 cup drained cocktail onions,coarsley chopped
1/4 cup oil cured moroccan olives,pitted and chopped
1/4 cup torn mint leaves

Directions:
Combine cracked pepper with bay leaves, fish sauce and 2 tbsps olive oil . Add the steaks and rub all over with mixture.Let stand at room temp for 2 hours or refridgerate for 4 hours.
Melt butter in saucepan. Add onions and jalapenos and cook over moderate heat until the onions are just softened, 5 minutes. Add wine and 1 tbsp of the vinegar and season with salt and pepper. Add 2 cups of water and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook over low heat until the onions are very tender, 40 minutes.
Uncover the onions and cook over moderate heat, stirring frequently until the liquid is evaporated, about 10 minutes. Transfer the onoins to a blender. Add the remaining 1 tbsp vinegar and puree until very smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
In medium bowl, toss cocktail onions, olives, and mint leaves with the remaining 2 tbsps olive oil.
Preheat grill pan or light a grill. Grill the steaks over moderate heat, turning once or twice until lightly charred, about 7 minutes for med rare meat. Let the steaks rest for 5 minutes, then serve with onion sauce and picked onion relish
