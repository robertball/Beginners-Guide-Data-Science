Title:
Kathy's Eclectic Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup whisky
- 1/4 cup honey
- 2 tbsp. worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp. ground horseradish
- 1 tbsp. minced garlic
- 1 tsp. black pepper
- 1 tsp. hot sauce
- 2.5 pounds sirloin steak

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and pour over steak in a resealable freezer bag.  Marinate overnight or at least 6 hours.  Grill steak as desired.
