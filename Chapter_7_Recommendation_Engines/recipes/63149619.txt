Title:
Grilled chicken with tropical mango sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1 mango
2 limes (3 T)
1 jalapeno
.5 C cilantro leaves
1 clove garlic (minced)
1/4 t. cumin
salt to taste

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and garlic powder, and grill
FOR THE SAUCE:
Add lime juice and seeded mango and puree.
Transfer to bowl and stir in finely chopped jalapeno and cilantro, minced garlic, and 1/4 tsp cumin. Serve with grilled chicken breast and lime wedges. Serves 4.
