Title:
Not-So-Chunky-Monkey Shakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups non-fat chocolate frozen yogurt
1 banana
4 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Place banana in the freezer for thirty minutes, then cut into chunks
In blender, place milk, banana, and yogurt. Blend for 30 seconds or until smooth and mixed. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
This sinfully delicious, but remarkably healthy shake can be made for breakfast, a snack, or desert. I use Edy's non-fat chocolate frozen yogurt. It has a great texture and flavor! The frozen banana adds great thickness, and lots of vitamins. Yummy!
