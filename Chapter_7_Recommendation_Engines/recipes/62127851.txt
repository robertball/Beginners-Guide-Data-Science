Title:
Easiest Crock Pot Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Chili
1 Can Ranch Style Beans
1 Can Black Beans
1 Can Hominy (yellow or white)
1 Can Italian Diced Tomatoes
1 Package Taco Seasoning Mix
1 Package Ranch Dressing Mix
Water as needed

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients together in a crock pot. Add water to dilute if needed. Cook on high for about 1 hour. Serve over tortilla chips with sour cream and cheddar cheese on top.
