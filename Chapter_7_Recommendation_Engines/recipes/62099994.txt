Title:
Perfect Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Frozen 9" Pie Crust
1.5 c. Brown sugar
0.5 c. Butter, softened
2 Eggs
2 T Whiskey
2 T Whipping cream
1 T Vanilla
1 T Flour
1.5 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Combine butter, sugar, eggs, whiskey, cream, vanilla and flour. Mix until smooth.
Spread chopped pecans over frozen pie crust. Pour sugar mixture over pecans and poke with a fork to release any air bubbles.
Bake for 45-60 minutes or until filling is set.

Submitter: 
Stefanie N
Description: 
A not-too-sweet, but melt-in-your-mouth version of the holiday classic.
