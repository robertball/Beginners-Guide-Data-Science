Title:
Homemade Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3204224.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups of juice or herb tea, sweetened as desired, divided:
1 cup room temp juice
1 cup boiling juice
2 cups cold juice
2 packets gelatin

Directions:
Bring 1 cup of juice to room temp.
Sprinkle gelatin over 1 cup of cool or room temp juice.
Bring 1 cup of juice just to the boiling point.
Pour over gelatin mixture and stir well to dissolve.
Stir in last 2 cups of cold juice.
Refrigerate until set.
