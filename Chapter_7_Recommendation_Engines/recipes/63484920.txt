Title:
Breakfast Burritoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pks hash browns (2 lbs each) 1.18$
8 dozen eggs- 2.15 each
8 pkgs shredded cheese, mexican four cheese finely shredded 1 lb each--2.50$
8 pkgs ground sausage, 4 hot and 4 regular (1 lb each) 1.79$
16 pkgs tortillas burrito size (8 per pkg) 1.43$
aluminum foil 200ft--$6.35
1 large jug salsa mild- 4.43$
1 jar salsa hot--2.17$

Directions:
Tear squares of foil about 15 in long. Brown the sausage.  Lightly toast the hash browns on a cookie sheet in a 350F over for 15 mins.  Scramble and cook the eggs.  Mix the sausage, hash browns, eggs, and cheese (in batches)
Wrap the tortillas in foil and heat at 250F oven for 15 mins.  Or in the package put in microwave and heat for 1 min until warm and pliable
Place a tortilla on a square of foil.  Scoop about 1 cup of mixture (2 med spoonfuls) onto a tortilla.  Roll as a burrito.  Place in a large cooler that has been warmed with boiling water.
Pour salsas into two labeled bowls and add a spoon each.  Serve with salsa.
3 people can make this in 3 hours
total cost=92.07$ cost per burrito is .72$
