Title:
One Single Falafel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup chickpeas (from can, drained)
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp smoked paprika
1/4 medium onion, peeled and chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tbsp roughly chopped flat-leaf parsley
1/2 tbsp lemon juice
Vegetable or canola oil, for frying
Pita bread, lettuce and hummus (for serving)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients (chickpeas through lemon juice) in the container of a blender or food processor. Add the 1 tbsp flour.
Blend until smooth.
Form patty with hands.
Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the patty to pan; cook 3 minutes on each side or until browned.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
If you love falafel as much as I do, you're willing to make a single patty just for yourself. The Middle Eastern vegetarian sandwich is delicious and nutritious. Go ahead make one for yourself and add any fixings you like! I like to use garlic or roasted red pepper hummus on mine.
