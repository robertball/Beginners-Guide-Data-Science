Title:
Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Ground Beef
1 Lg. Onion (chopped)
1 t. Garlic Powder
2/3 c. All-Purpose Flour
3 T. Chili Powder
1 1/2 t. Seasoned Salt
2 t. Beef Bouillon
1/8 t. Pepper
5 c. Hot Water
15 Corn Tortillas (7 inches)
3 c. Sharp Cheddar Cheese (Shredded)
Salsa
Sour Cream
Green Onions

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 375Â°.
COOK ground beef and onions, in a dutch oven, over medium heat; drain.
REMOVE from heat.
SPRINKLE with flour, garlic powder, chili powder, seasoned salt, beef bouillon and pepper.
ADD hot water, return to burner and bring to a boil.
REDUCE heat; simmer, uncovered, for 10-15 minutes or until reduced.
PLACE a layer of tortillas in a greased 9" x 13" baking dish.
SPOON on some meat mixture.
ADD a cup of the cheese.
REPEAT two additional times.
BAKE uncovered for 30-35 minutes.
SERVE with salsa, sour cream and green onions.
