Title:
Slow Cooker Beef "Kabobs" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. sirloin steak cut into 1 inch cubes
1 medium to large zucchini sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 medium to large yellow squash sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 each yellow, red, green peppers sliced 1/4 inch thick
1-2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1 packet dry onion soup mix
2/3 cup water
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place meat in bottom of slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper.
Combine onion soup mix and 2/3 cup water together and pour over meat.
Layer zucchini, squash, peppers, and mushrooms. drizzle with Worcestershire sauce.
Cover and Cook on low for 6-8 hours. Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Julie Scheer
Description: 
My family loves beef kabobs on the grill but sometimes I just don't have the time to cook them! This is a fantastic alternative that even my 2 year old devours!
