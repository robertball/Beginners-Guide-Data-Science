Title:
Indian Kurli Papad (Stuffed Poppadom Rolls) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tblsp. olive oil
1 small yellow onion, diced
1/2 cup cleaned crabmeat
1/2 cup shrimp; peeled, devained and cleaned
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp. turmeric
2 tbsps. cilantro, chopped
1/2 tsp. ginger paste
1/2 tsp. garlic, minced
1 tbsp. tomato ketchup
1 tsp. white wine vinegar
Salt to taste
2 Eggs
Oil for frying
4 Papads (Poppadom) *

Directions:
Heat 2 tblsp. olive oil in a saute pan, saut√©  until light brown. Add crab and shrimp meat, combine. Add next 8 ingredients stir fry for 3 minutes. Take a papad, coat one side with half a beaten egg, put a thin layer of meat mixture and roll up like a spring roll. Repeat the same for the other Papads. Deep fry rolls in about 2 inches of oil until golden brown.  Serve warm with prepared sweet chutney.

Description: 
These spicy seafood Indian-style rolls are a great beginning to any ethnic dinner party.  *Papads are a thin wafer like discs about 4 to 8 inches in diameter made from a variety of lentils, potato, shrimp, rice etc. The discs are deep fried or dry roasted on an open flame and served as a crispy savory appetizer. Served in many Indian restaurants complimentary before a meal.  Can be purchased in most gourmet grocery stores or Indian grocery stores. Enjoy!!
