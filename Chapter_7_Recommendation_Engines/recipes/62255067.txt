Title:
Best Ever Southern Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/684583.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising white cornmeal
1 egg
1/4 cup oil
1-1/4 cups milk (or 1-1/2 cups buttermilk)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  Pour into heated skillet or muffin tins that have been sprayed with Pam.   Bake at 450 for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
My southern family and friends prefer this light crusty cornbread without flour.
