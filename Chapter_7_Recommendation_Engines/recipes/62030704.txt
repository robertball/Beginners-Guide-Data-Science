Title:
Low-Carb Zuppa Toscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz 50% Less Fat Ground Sausage
1 large onion (approx. 3/4 cup)
6 cups chicken broth
2 cups Kale - washed, dried, & shredded
6 slices of cooked bacon crumbled
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 head or frozen bag of califlower
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Cook sausage and onions together until sausage is well done, drain.  Add chicken broth, bacon, kale, garlic, and califlower.  Simmer for about 15 minutes until califlower is tender.  Add heavy cream and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.

Low-carb version of a delicious creamy sausage soup.
