Title:
Diana's Seafood Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 Lasagna noodle (depending on numbers of layers)
1 pound crab meat
1 pound shrimp (1/2 large and 1/2 small)
1 cup chopped onion
2 Tablespoons butter
1 1/2 cups small curd, cottage cheese
1 egg, beaten
2 teaspoon dry or fresh basil
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded sharp cheese (a little more for baking at the finish of cooking time)

Directions:
Cook noodles in boiling water until al dente. Don't let the noodles get soft.  Drain.
Spray 9X13-inch glass pan with Pam. Place 1/2 of the noodles in bottom of pan.
Cook chopped onions in frying pan in the butter until glazed.
In a bowl, mix soften cream cheese, cottage cheese, egg, basil, the 2 cans of mushroom soup, wine, milk and onions.  Add a little salt if desired
Layer this mixture into a two or three layers in the glass pan. Add crab and shrimp and sprinkle each layer with parmesan cheese and sharp cheese.
Cook uncovered for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
Top with extra shredded sharp cheese and put in oven to bake for 5 minutes.
Let set at least 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
