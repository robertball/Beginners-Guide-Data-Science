Title:
MeMe's Pasta Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 carrot, diced
1 stalk celery, diced
1 thin slice onion, diced
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 32 ounce V8 vegetable juice
1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon dried basil leaves
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 lb of lean ground beef, browned
1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1 1/2 cups ditalini pasta

Directions:
Brown hamburger meat, drain well and set aside.  Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Saute carrot, celery and onion until soft. Add garlic and saute briefly. Stir in V8 juice, chicken broth, pepper, parsley, oregano and basil; simmer for 20 minutes.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ditalini pasta and cook for 8 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Add beans and browned hamburger to the broth and vegetable mixture and simmer for ten minutes.  When pasta is done, add 1/3 cup to each bowl and keep separate when putting up the left overs.

Submitter: 
Karyn Osborne
Description: 
White cannellini beans, ditalini pasta with vegetables, lean hamburger and herbs in a Vegetable juice and chicken broth/stock. Like an Italian Chili ... even better the second dayModified from: Easy Pasta Fagioli
