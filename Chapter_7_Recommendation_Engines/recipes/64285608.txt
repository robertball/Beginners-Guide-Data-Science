Title:
Esquites (Mexican Corn Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tbls Butter
3C corn (about 4 ears) cut from the cob
1/2 jalapeno, seeded and finely diced
3Tbls Mayo
1 clove garlic, grated
2 green onions, sliced
1 handful cilantro, chopped
1 lime, juiced
2Tbls cotija (or feta) crumbled
chili powder to taste

Directions:
1. Melt butter in a heavy skillet over medium/high heat
2. Add corn, toss and let sit cooking until charred, mix it up and let it char again, about 6-10minutes
3. Add the jalapeno, saute for a minute and remove from heat.
4. Mix everything and serve warm or at room temp.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
A tasty Mexican streen corn salad with an amazing blend of sweet corn, salty cheese, sour lime juice and a hint of spicy chili powder; pure summmer in a bowl.
