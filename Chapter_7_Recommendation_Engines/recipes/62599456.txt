Title:
Nutty Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter flavored shortning
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 half cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 large egg
1 cup all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/3 cups of honey roasted peanuts, corsely chopped
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F
Lightly spray cookie sheets with cooking spray or use parchment paper.
In large mixing bowl, combine shortning, peanut butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar.  Beat on medium speed until well blended.
Beat in egg.
In a medium size bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.  Mix into creamed mixture at low speed until just blended.
Form dough into 1 inch balls.  Roll in chopped nuts.
Place onto cookie sheets 2 inches apart.  Flatten slightly.
Bake for 6 - 7 minutes, or until set.  Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes then put on cooling racks to cool completely.
