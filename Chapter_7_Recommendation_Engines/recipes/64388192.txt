Title:
Tammy's Breakfast Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c peanut butter
1/4 c butter
1/4 c brown sugar
1/2 c honey
1 tsp vanilla
.
2c oats
1/2 c ground flax
1 c Rice Krispies
1 c dried cranberries
1/2 c mini chocolate chips
1/2 c slivered/chopped almonds

Directions:
Melt and blend first section of syrupy ingredients in a large bowl in the microwave or on stove.
Mix together next section of dry ingredients. Add to melted syrupy mixture. Mix well.
Stir in final add-ins.
Press into 9x11 pan. (Glass pan wont need to be greased or lined). Chill 1 hour, cut into snack sized bars. Wrap individually in plastic wrap.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
I wanted a healthier snack or breakfast bar that I could make myself. I combined a few different recipes to come up with this one.
