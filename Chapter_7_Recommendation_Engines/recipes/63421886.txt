Title:
CRAZY CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/924850.jpg
Ingredients:
3 CUPS FLOUR
2 CUPS SUGAR
1/2 CUP COCOA
2 TSP BAKING SODA
1 TSP SALT
3/4 CUP VEG OIL
2 TSP VANILLA
2 CUPS WATER
2 TBSP VINEGAR

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350 DEGREES.
SIFT FLOUR, COCOA, SUGAR, BAKING SODA AND SALT THREE TIMES TO MIX WELL.
MAKE A HOLE IN THE CENTER OF THE DRY INGREDIENTS AND ADD THE OIL, VINEGAR, VANILLA AND WATER.
MIX UNTIL BLENDED.
SEE NOTES FOR BAKING TIMES.
OPTIONAL: Dust with powdered sugar once cooled.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
A NO EGG CAKE THAT COMES OUT MOIST AND RICH. DON'T EVEN NEED ICING.
