Title:
Coconut cream pie martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz Malibu rum
1 oz Vanilla Schnapps
3 oz half and half

Directions:
combine ingredients with ice in a martini shaker or large glass, strain ice and pour into a small cocktail glass

Submitter: 
lindsaymarie
Description: 
This amazing cocktail is an at home easy adaptation of a drink I love! It tastes like sweet coconut and is a real crowd pleaser for those who don't like the taste of traditional alcoholic drinks.
