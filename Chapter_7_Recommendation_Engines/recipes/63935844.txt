Title:
Chicken Noodle Soup (revised) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups wide egg noodles
12 cups chicken broth
1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup sliced carrots
1 cup frozen peas
4 cups fresh spinach
4 cups diced rotisserie chicken
1/2 can cream of chicken soup

Directions:
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, combine broth,  and poultry seasoning. Bring to a boil. Stir in celery, carrots, onion and noodles. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10 minutes. Add peas and fresh spinach, and chicken.  return to simmer and cook for 5 more minutes. Stir in 1/2 can cream of chicken soup and heat through.
To serve, may top with parsley and green onion.

Submitter: 
Ruth Rae
Description: 
Quick and easy comfort food.  Chicken soup with noodles and vegetables.  Use homemade chicken stock for a great touch.Modified from: Grandma's Chicken Noodle Soup
