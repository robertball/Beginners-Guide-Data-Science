Title:
Caramelized Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup soy sauce
3/4 C ketchup
3/4  cup honey
4 clove garlic, minced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Mix together the oil, soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, salt and pepper. Pour over the chicken. Can freeze at this point.
Bake in preheated oven for one hour, basting a few times, or until sauce is caramelized.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
Soy and ketchup, laced with honey and garlic, make for a potent sauce.  This chicken is ideal for appetizers or a main course.Modified from: Caramelized Baked Chicken
