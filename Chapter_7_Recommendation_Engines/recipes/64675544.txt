Title:
Coconut Macarooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4830652.jpg
Ingredients:
14 ounces sweetened flaked coconut
14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
2 extra large egg whites, at room temperature
1/4 teaspoon Kosher salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Combine the coconut, condensed milk, and vanilla in a large bowl
Whip the egg whites and salt on high speed until the they form medium firm peaks
Carefully fold the egg whites into the coconut mixture
Drop the batter onto sheet pans with parchment paper using a 1 3/4 inch scoop
Bake for 25-30 minutes, until golden brown
