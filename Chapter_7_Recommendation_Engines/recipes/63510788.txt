Title:
Lentil Soup with Chickpeas and Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/958980.jpg
Ingredients:
- Splash olive oil
- 1 Red Onion
- ½ bag Baby Carrots, chopped
- ½ small bag Peas
- 3 cloves garlic, mashed in garlic press or chopped
- 1 tsp ground ginger
- 2 tsp each: Ground cumin, Coriander, Turmeric, Cayenne Pepper
- A little less than 1 tsp each: Ground Cinnamon, Curry powder (optional), Garam Masala (optional), Paprika
- 2 cups red lentils
- 7-8 tsp Better Than Bouillon (part veggie part chicken) and 7 cups warm water (or  7 cups veggie or chicken stock)
- 2 cans drained & rinsed garbanzo beans
- 2 cups cooked quinoa
- Splash of lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in thick bottomed pot, fry onions and carrots on medium heat until onions are soft or almost soft
Add cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne, cinnamon; stir and heat until aromatic
Add garlic and ginger, stir for a couple minutes
Add red lentils and stir for one minute
Add Better than Bouillon and water, cook on medium-high heat until it comes to a rapid boil
Lower heat & simmer 10-15 minutes or until carrots are almost to preferred doneness
Add chickpeas and peas, simmer 5 more minutes
Take off heat, stir in quinoa, curry powder, garam masala, paprika, and lemon juice. Salt & pepper to taste.

Description: 
This delicious, stick-to-your-ribs meal is easily thrown together with stuff probably lying around in your spice cabinet! Perfect for rainy days, the old-world flavors of this soup are perfect for the whole family!
