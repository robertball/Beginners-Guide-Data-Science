Title:
Kathy's Banana Poppy Seed Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Muffins:
2 ripe, medium bananas, peeled
1 egg
3/4 c. sugar
1/4 c. vegetable oil
1/4 - 1/3 c. orange juice
2 t. grated orange peel
2 c. flour
1 1/2 t. poppy seeds
2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
Citrus Glaze:
1 1/4 c. powdered sugar
1/4 c. orange juice
1 t. grated orange peel
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Puree bananas.
In medium bowl mix bananas, egg, sugar, oil, orange juice, and orange peel until well blended.
In separate bowl, combine flour, poppy seeds, baking powder and salt.
Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until evenly moistened.
Spoon batter into prepared (papers) muffin cups.
Bake @ 375 degrees for 20 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.
Remove from pan; cool on wire rack.
Prepare glaze by mixing all ingredients.  Top muffins with glaze while still warm.
