Title:
Cranberry Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of fresh cranberries
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 t. ground cinnamon
1 cup water
1 1/2 t. ground ginger
1/2 t. ground cloves
1/4 t. ground alspice
1 tart peeled and chopped apple

Directions:
Place cranberries, sugars, water and spices into sauce pan.  Bring to a boil and simmer 20 minutes.  Add apple and simmer another 5 minutes or until thick.
You may place this into a jar or dish and refrigerate until cold.
Sauce will keep for 2 weeks.
Serve with turkey or pork.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description: 
The best cranberry sauce you will ever taste.  Many spices make this delightful sauce the hit of any occasion.
