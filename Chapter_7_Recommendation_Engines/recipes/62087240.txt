Title:
Pistachio Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 stacks Ritz crackers
1 tablespoon sugar
1 stick butter or margarine, melted
2 pkgs pistachio instant pudding mix
1 1/2 C. milk
1 quart vanilla ice cream, softened
8 oz. tub Cool Whip

Directions:
Crush crackers and mix with melted butter and sugar. Line bottom of 9x13 baking dish.
Mix pistachio pudding, milk and ice cream together and spread on top of crackers.
Frost with Cool whip and top with 3 crushed Heath Bars. Set overnight in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
MSABBYK
Description: 
This is a quick easy recipe that requires no cooking. The main ingredients are: pistachio pudding, cool whip, vanilla ice cream and Ritz crackers.
