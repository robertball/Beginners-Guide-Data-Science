Title:
Vegan Whipped Cream with Cashews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cashews
1 cup water, or as needed
1 teaspoon maple syrup, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine cashews and 2 cups of water in a bowl; cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
Discard soaking water, rinse cashews, and transfer to a blender. Add just enough fresh water to cover cashews. Blend, adding water as needed to achieve a smooth consistency. Add maple syrup and vanilla; blend to combine.

Submitter: 
DANII777
Description: 
This vegan whipped cream recipe can be used on anything you would put whipped cream on!
