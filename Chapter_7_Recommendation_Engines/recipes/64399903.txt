Title:
Baked Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2247257.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb package bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Line a jelly roll pan with foil.  Lay bacon strips out on foil-lined pan.
Bake for 15-20 minutes for crisp bacon, adjust time according to preference.  Transfer cooked bacon to a paper towel-lined plate.

Description: 
The easiest way to make perfect bacon every time- plus no greasy mess to clean up!
