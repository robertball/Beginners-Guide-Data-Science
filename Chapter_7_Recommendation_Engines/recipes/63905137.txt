Title:
Lime Cilantro Cauliflower "Rice" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 head cauliflower, cut into florets
1-1/2 tablespoon water
1-1/2 lime, juiced and zested
3/4 cup chopped cilantro
3 tablespoons butter (optional)
-------------------------------------
grated peccorino cheese
garlic clove(s)

Directions:
Grate cauliflower florets or pulse in a food processor until it resembles rice.
Place grated cauliflower and water in a microwave-safe covered dish.
Cook cauliflower in microwave on high until tender, about 7 minutes.
Stir lime zest, lime juice, cilantro, and butter into cooked cauliflower until well blended.

Submitter: 
Gerry L.
Description: 
Grated cauliflower stands in for rice in this flavorful, grain-free side dish.Modified from: Lime Cilantro Cauliflower Rice
