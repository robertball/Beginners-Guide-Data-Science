Title:
ANDY'S SAUCY ITALIAN STYLE ZUCCHINI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 LARGE ZUCCHINI - SLICED THIN
1 MEDIUM ONION - CHOPPED
2 CLOVES GARLIC - MINCED
5 LARGE TOMATOES CHOPPED
2TB FRESH BASIL CHOPPED
2TB FRESH PARSLEY CHOPPED
2TB FRESH OREGANO CHOPPED
1TB FRESH THYME
1/4 CUP FRESH GRATED PARMESAN CHEESE
2TB EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE

Directions:
IN A LARGE SKILLET HEAT OLIVE OIL
ADD ZUCCHINI AND SAUTE' 5MINS
ADD ALL THE OTHER INGREDIENTS EXCEPT CHEESE, SALT AND PEPPER
COVER AND SIMMER ON LOW 30 MINS
ADD PARMESAN CHEESE AND SALT AND PEPPER JUST BEFORE SERVING
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
THE USE OF ALL FRESH INGREDIENTS IN THIS RECIPE IS SURE TO IMPRESS! EVEN BETTER IF PICKED RIGHT FROM YOUR GARDEN TO THE STOVE. THIS MAKES A GREAT SIDE DISH ON THOSE HOT SUMMER NIGHTS!
