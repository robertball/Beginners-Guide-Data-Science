Title:
Lemon Honey Glazed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/4 C. honey (warmed)
2 Lemons (juiced)
6 cloves of garlic, chopped finely
1 tsp. Paprika
1 Tbsp. Thyme
1 tsp. Salt
Olive Oil
1/4 tsp. freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 475.
In a bowl mix all but the olive oil.
Pound chicken with a meat mallet until about 1/4" - 1/2" thick.  Coat both sides of chicken with mixture.
Drizzle a large glass baking dish with olive oil.  Place chicken in baking dish and cook for 20-25 minutes or until chicken registers 160 on a meat thermometer.

Submitter: 
Alisha Bowers
Description: 
A delicious blend of honey, lemon, garlic and herbs.
