Title:
Toothache Tartlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Light Brown Sugar
4 T Sweetened Condensed Milk
6 T Butter
2 Eggs
1 1/2 T Flour
1 C Toasted Pecans Crushed
12 Individual Servings of Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Press one serving of dough into each of a muffin pan.
Bake cookies 6-8 minutes until puffy but not yet browned. Distribute pecans evenly among tins.  Set aside.
Preheat oven to 400.
Combine sugar, milk, butter in a sauce pan over medium-low heat until simmering.
Allow sugar mix to cool for a few minutes.  Lightly beat egg and flour in a small bowl.
Combine sugar mix and egg mix stirring quickly to make sure egg incorporates.
Spoon sugar evenly over pecans in tins.
Bake 5 minutes at 400.  Reduce temp to 350 and continue baking 8-10 minutes or until just set.
Allow to cool.  Loosen edges with a knife if needed.

Submitter: 
Lindsay Deaver
Description: 
Basically mini-pecan pies in chocolate chip cookie crusts.  A Christmas-time favorite for me.
