Title:
Pumpkin Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups softened real butter
2 cups packed brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 15 ounce can of pumpkin
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
4 cups flour
2 cups quick cooking oats
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream softened butter and sugars. Beat in pumpkin, egg and vanilla until smooth.
Add all dry ingredients in a separate bowl. Once all dry ingredients (except the mini chips)have been mixed well, add dry ingredients to the creamed mixture. Once dry and creamed mixtures are combined, stir in the mini chips.
Drop spoon sized batter on cookie sheets.Bake at 375 degrees approx. 10 to 12 minutes. Cookies should brown slightly around the edges Test for doneness.

Submitter: 
Ken Sherer
Description: 
Excellent moist chocolate chip cookie with oatmeal and mini chocolate chips.
