Title:
Jello Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounce Jello
1 cup boiling water
1 cup cold water
4 ounces vodka

Directions:
Dump Jello package into mixing bowl.  Add boiling water to dissolve.  Add cold water and vodka.  Pour into shot cups and refrigerate.
