Title:
Improved Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6189303.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Frozen carrots
1/4 cup Frozen broccoli
1/4 cup Frozen cauliflower
1 teaspoon Lowery's Seasoning Salt
2 tablespoons Olive oil

Directions:
Place oil, seasoning salt, and frozen vegetables in skillet
Cook 10-15 minutes on medium to semi high temperature until vegetables are hot
Can be served alone, with cooked rice, precooked chicken or turkey meat, and/or with parmesan cheese as a topping.
