Title:
German Chocolate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992444.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce Godiva® Original Liqueur
1/2 ounce Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ Coconut
1/2 ounce Smirnoff® Black Cherry vodka
1/4 ounce chocolate shavings

Directions:
Add Godiva(R) Original Liqueur, Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) Coconut, and Smirnoff(R) Black Cherry Vodka to a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
Shake with ice and strain into martini glass.
Garnish with chocolate shavings.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add that bit of extra flavor to this or other cocktail recipes easily with the natural fruit flavors of Smirnoff® Flavored Vodkas.
