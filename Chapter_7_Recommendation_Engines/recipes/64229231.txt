Title:
Indian Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp oil
1 onion, diced
1 piece ginger root, 1"
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp masala (I used 1/2 tsp nutmeg, 1/2 tsp cumin, cracked black pepper, 1 Tbsp curry, 2 cardamon pod & 1 tsp sesame seeds- crushed)
1 chicken, cut up and skinned
1 cup yoghurt
1-1/3 cups water

Directions:
In Dutch oven, saute onion in oil until soft.  Add ginger root, garlic and masala.  Stir. Add chicken and yoghurt.  Cover and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add water and cook for 45 minutes or until tender.
