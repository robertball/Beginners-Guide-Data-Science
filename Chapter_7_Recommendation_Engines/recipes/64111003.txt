Title:
Impossibly Easy Vegetable Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. shredded zucchini
1 C. chopped tomato
1 C. shredded Swiss cheese
1/3 C. chopped onion
2 C. milk
4 eggs
1 C. Bisquick
1/3 C. Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Grease pie plate. Sprinkle zucchini, tomato, onion, and Swiss cheese in plate. Beat remaining ingredients until smooth. Pour into plate. Bake 35-40 minutes until knife comes out clean.Cool 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
This recipe is so versatile! Substitute any veggies or cheese, just keep the measurements the same. Broccoli, eggplant, etc.
