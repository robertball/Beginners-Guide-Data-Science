Title:
Chongos Zamoranos Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Serves: 12
21 grams of unflavored gelatin
3 cups of water
1 cup of sweetened condensed milk
1 cinnamon stick
2 cups of drained chongos zamoranos.

Directions:
Hydrate unflavored Gelatin for at least 5 minutes in a half cup of water.
Heat the rest of the water with condensed milk and cinnamon until it boils.
Remove from heat.
Double boil the gelatin until soft.
Add the softened gelatin to the water, condensed milk and cinnamon mixture and mix well. Let it cool down, remove the cinnamon stick and add the chongos zamoranos.
Spray a gelatin mold with nonstick spray, pour in the mixture and refrigerate. Will be ready and delicious in  approximately 4 hours.
