Title:
Banana-Nut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
2 ripe bananas
2 heaping tbsp. smooth peanut butter
2 tsp. vanilla
6-7 ice cubes
nutmeg, to suit your tastes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients exept nutmeg in blender, and puree.  Pour into 4 glasses and sprinkle with nutmeg.  Enjoy!
