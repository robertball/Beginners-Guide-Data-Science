Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese (softened)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 package italian dressing seasoning (dried kind in the packet)
2 cups rice

Directions:
Stir the cream cheese, cream of chicken, and seasoning together in the crockpot.
Start crockpot on low. Let them sit there for about 30 minutes until cream cheese is soft enough to stir.
Put in chicken and cover with mix.
Cook on high 4-6 hours or until the chicken is cooked and fall-apart-tender.
Cook the rice according to package directions. Serve with chicken mixture on top.
