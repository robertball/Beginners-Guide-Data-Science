Title:
Italian salad dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup white vinegar
1 tsp. vegetable oil
2 TB corn syrup
2 TB Parmesan cheese
2 TB romano cheese
1/4 tsp. garlic salt or one clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp. parsley flakes
1 TB lemon juice

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a blender until well mixed.  If this is a little too tart, add a little extra sugar.

Description: 
This is pretty close to Olive Garden's salad dressing.
