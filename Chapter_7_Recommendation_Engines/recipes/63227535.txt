Title:
Blackberry Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ice
1 1/2 jiggers Gold Tequila (such as a Reposado)
2 jiggers Sweet and Sour
juice from half a lime
one sweetener packet (such as Splenda)
5 or 6 fresh blackberries

Directions:
Fill cocktail shaker with ice. Add rest of ingredients. Put lid on shaker and shake. Pour over ice into margarita glasses or rocks glasses.

Submitter: 
Jamie Lowe
Description: 
My husband and I had a similar drink at a restaurant on evening and decided to try to recreate it. We like our version even better.
