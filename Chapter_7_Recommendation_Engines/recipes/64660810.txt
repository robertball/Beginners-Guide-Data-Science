Title:
Reese's peanut butter cup lush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg of PB oreos
1/2 c butter, melted
1 3.4 oz box instant chocolate pudding
1/4 c creamy PB
1 16 oz tub cool whip
1 c mini reeses, cut in half
chocolate syrup

Directions:
Grease a square pan.
Mix oreo crumbs and butter pressing into the pan for a crust.
Mix pudding, milk, and PB stir in 1/2 cool whip until combined. Spread on crust. Top with remaining whip cream and PB cups.
Refrigerate for 6 hours+ drizzle with chocolate syrup before serving.
