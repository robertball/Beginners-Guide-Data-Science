Title:
Easy Mock Twice Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989077.jpg
Ingredients:
2 strips bacon
1 (24 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Country Style Mashed Potatoes
1/2 cup chive and onion cream cheese
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup Crystal Farms® Shredded Cheddar Cheese
Paprika
Chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. In 10-inch skillet cook bacon on medium-high heat until crisp. Drain grease; crumble bacon. In medium bowl combine Simply Potatoes(R), cream cheese, bacon and salt; stir to mix well. Spoon about 2/3 cup Simply Potatoes(R) mixture into 6 (1 cup) glass custard cups or ramekins. Top each with 1 tablespoon cheese. Sprinkle lightly with paprika.
Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until heated through. Garnish each with chopped fresh chives.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
If you love twice baked potatoes but hate the hassle, you will want to try this quick and easy recipe.
