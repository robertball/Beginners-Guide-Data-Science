Title:
Cheddar Sour Cream Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
4 oz. sour cream
1/8 cup of milk
2 tsps. baking powder
1/3 stick of maragarine or butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Cut butter in flour, salt, and baking powder with a pastry blender or fork.
Add sour cream and enough milk to make a soft dough.
Knead dough, add cheese and work cheese into dough by kneading.
Roll dough out and use a biscuit cutter to cut biscuits. Place on an ungreased baking sheet and bake for 10-12 min. or until golden brown.
