Title:
CHICKEN TORTELLINI PASTA SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SALAD:
2Cs. Cooked, Smoked or Rotisserie Chicken, shredded
1 {8oz. pkg.} Tortellini Pasta, cooked as pkg. directs. or al-denta-ta. Drain well.
1Med. Cucumber, peeled and diced
1Med. Tomato, diced
1/4C. Onion, diced
1/2C. fresh Parmesan Cheese, grated
DRESSING:
1/3C. Pure Olive Oil
1/4C. Red Wine Vinegar
1Tsp. Dried Oregano Leaves, crushed
1Tsp. Dried Parsley Leaves, flaked

Directions:
SALAD:
In a medium-size bowl, combine first 6 ingredients, mix well.
DRESSING:
Add all dressing ingredients and mix well in a medium-size bowl; with a lid. Cover and place in frig. and chill over night or at least 12 hours, mixing occasionally!!
To Serve:Place salad on serving plates and pour dressing over top!
This salad is really great, its healthy and flavorfull.
Serve with Texas Herb or Garlic Toast!!

Submitter: 
Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
This is a very savory and tasty salad! Easy to make and very yummy, great for family get togethers and for when friends drop on by!!
