Title:
Incredible Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FILLING
3c cooked chicken
3 ¼c mix of cooked vegetables and potatoes
GRAVY
1 ½ cans cream of chicken soup
3 c hot chicken broth
2/3 c flour
¾ c milk
ENGLISH PASTRY CRUST
3 c flour
3t baking powder
1 ½ t salt
1 c butter
¾ c hot water
1 ½  T lemon juice
2 egg yolks

Directions:
Combine chicken and vegetables in a 9x13 pan.
Whisk together ingredients for gravy and pour over chicken mixture.
For tender pastry topping -
Sift together dry ingredients.
Whisk wet ingredients in a large bowl.
Combine wet and dry. Roll out dough to cover filling, or pat small pieces of pastry over filling for a rustic pie.
Bake at 400 for 30-40 minutes or until browned.
