Title:
Pomegranate Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups pomegranate juice
1- 1 1/2 cups cranberry juice cocktail
1/2 cup orange juice
3" long cinnamon stick
1 tsp. grated ginger

Directions:
Put all ingredients in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on low 1-2 hours or until heated through.
