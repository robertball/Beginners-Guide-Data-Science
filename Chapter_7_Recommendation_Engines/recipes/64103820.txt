Title:
Toffee Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1 egg yolk
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease baking pan, 13x9x2 inches.
Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg yolk and vanilla and mix thoroughly.  Blend in flour and salt.  Press evenly in bottom of pan.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until very light brown (crust will be soft).  Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle chocolate chips over crust.  Return briefly to oven to melt chocolate.  As soon as chocolate is soft, spread evenly.  Sprinkle with nuts.  While warm, cut into bars.
