Title:
Crystal's Super Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/344083.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 bags tortilla chips
Pico de Gallo Salsa:
4 vine ripe tomatoes, seeded & chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded & finely chopped
1 small white onion, chopped
1/4 cup cilantro leaves, finely chopped - substitute parsley if cilantro is not to your liking
Beef & Bean Topping:
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 pound ground chuck
4-5 cloves garlic, chopped
1 small onion chopped
1-2 jalapeno peppers, seeded & chopped
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 1/2 tablespoons cumin
1 tablespoon Franks red hot sauce
1 can black beans, 15 oz drained
Cheese Sauce:
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons all purpose flour
2 cups milk
2 1/2 cups shredded monteray jack cheese
Toppings:
Chopped Scallions
Sour Cream
Chopped black olives
Sliced advocado, dressed with lemon juice

Directions:
Combine pico de gallo salsa ingredients together and set aside for flavors to marry.
Heat a medium nonstick skillet over medium high heat.  Add oil, garlic, onion, & peppers to the pan and saute 2 minutes, then add meat and crumble.  Season meat with salf, chili powder, cumin, and cayenne pepper sauce.  Cook meat 5 minutes, then stir in beans & reduce to low.  In a medium sauce pot, melt butter and add flour to it.  Cook flour and butter 1-2 minutes over moderate heat, then whisk in milk.  When milk comes to bubble, stir in cheese and whisk.  Remove cheese sauce from heat.
Pour cheese sauce over the chips and top evenly with the beef & beans and the pico de gallo.  Serve immediatly as is or garnish with your choice of toppings.
