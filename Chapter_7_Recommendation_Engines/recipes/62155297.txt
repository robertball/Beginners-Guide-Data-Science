Title:
Super Simple Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 Cup onion finely chopped
2 garlic cloves finely chopped
4 chicken breasts, diced
2 to 3 cups frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, peas and corn)
1/2 cup flour
1 1/2 cup of cream
salt
pepper
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (optional)
1 package Pillsbury Grand Biscuits (cut into squares)

Directions:
In a large pot, melt butter and add onion and garlic and cook until tender.
Add diced chicken until cooked but not browned
Add frozen vegetables and heat thoroughly
Mix in flour gradually
Pour cream slowly while mixing into the vegetables evenly
Stir constantly until desired thickness is reached
Pour into four individual oven-safe bowls, or 9X9 baking dish
Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese
Place biscuit squares on top, covering evenly
Place in oven until biscuits are golden
Let cool for 10 minutes

Description: 
One pot meal, ready in minutes... and your whole family will be full and happy!
