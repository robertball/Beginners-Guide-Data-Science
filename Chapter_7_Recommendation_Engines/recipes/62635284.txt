Title:
Chocolate Velvet Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/579253.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
8 oz. chocolate wafer cookie crumbs
1/3 cup melted butter
1/2 tsp cinnamon
Filling:
12 oz. semisweet chocolate
2 Tbls butter
3-8oz. packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
3 eggs, room temperature
2 cups heavy cream
1 tsp. vanilla
dash of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Start large pot of water boiling for water bath. Grease 10 inch springform, set aside.
Mix cookie crumbs, melted butter and cinnamon in small bowl. Press on to the bottom of prepared springform evenly. Cover with 2 layers of foil, chill.
Heat chocolate and 2 Tbls butter in microwave. Time will vary depending on microwave. Put in for small incraments (20 sec) and stir each time. After melted so it is smooth, set aside. Beat cream cheese until light, gradually adding sugar. Add eggs, one at a time, scraping bowl after each addition. At low speed beat in chocolate just until incorporated. Mix in cream, vanilla and salt just until smooth. Pour into prepared crust. Place cheesecake in roasting pan and fill with hot water until 1/2 way up springform. Be careful not to get water into the cheesecake. Bake about 1 1/2 hours until center is not quite set. Pull out of oven, but leave in water until water is room temperature. Pull out of water, remove foil and cool on wire rack until it is room temperature. Chill overnight.
