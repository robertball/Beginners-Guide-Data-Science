Title:
Grilled Salmon with Raspberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint of raspberries
1 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
2 tbsp. olive oil
1/2 lemon (juiced)
12 oz salmon
salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Slice the salmon into 6oz portions.
2. Season the salmon with salt and pepper
3. In a medium size bowl, crush raspberries with a fork.  Combine vinegar, oil, and lemon juice.
4. Place salmon portions into bowl and mix into marinade.  Refrigerate for at least one hour
5. Grill the salmon (turning once).
6. While grilling, pour marinade into a small frying pan and bring to boil stirring constantly.
Serve the salmon hot, topped with sauce.
The dish goes well when served over rice (dirty rice or whole grain)
