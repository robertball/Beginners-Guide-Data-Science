Title:
Chicken Zucchini Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken boullion cubes
2 cups boiling water
1 cup uncooked rice
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup diced onion
1 cup thinly sliced zucchini(about 1 or 2)
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1 cup diced cooked chicken
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can roasted garlic cream of mushroomsoup
1/2 cup mayonaise
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 can sliced water chestnuts drained
1 can french fried onions

Directions:
In large saucepan, dissolve 2 chicken boullion cubes in 2 cups boiling water.
Cook rice in chicken boullion water until all water is absorbed, about 15-20 minutes.
While rice is cooking, saute onions, celery, and zucchini in olive oil.
Mix together cooked rice, vegetables, and remaining ingredients (except french fried onions).
Pour into greased casserole dish and top with french fried onions.
Bake uncovered 30-35 minutes at 350 degrees.
