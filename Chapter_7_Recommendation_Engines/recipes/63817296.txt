Title:
Tracy Ann's Awesome Taco Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1059240.jpg
Ingredients:
MEATLOAF:
2 lbs. ground beef
2 eggs
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup (uncooked) oatmeal
1 cup (uncooked) rice
1 packet McCormick's Taco Seasoning
1/2 cup catsup
1 Tbs. spicy mustard
TOPPING:
2 Tbs. catsup
(1/4 cup of chopped onion)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x5 loaf pan. Reserve 1/4 of chopped onion and put the rest of the ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Squish everything together with your hands until well blended. Form the mixture into a loaf and put in the loaf pan. Smooth the mixture into the pan, then top with 2 Tbs. of catsup. Sprinkle the reserved 1/4 cup of chopped onion over the top. Bake for 1 hour. Cut into 6 slices and serve. Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
A side of your favorite veggies will turn this into a super healthy meal. This is an easy first recipe for a Beginner Cook!
