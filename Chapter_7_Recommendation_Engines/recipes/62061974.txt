Title:
Extra Hot Curry Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 jar of Hot Concentrated Curry Paste (I use Patak's)
bag of red chili peppers (about 5)
1 onion, cubed
olive oil
butter

Directions:
Sautee cubed onion in a few tablespoons of olive oil and butter.  Add 1 lb of thawed ground beef to the pan.  Break up beef, then add half jar of hot concentrated curry paste and about 5 or 6 red chili peppers.  Simmer about 50 minutes or until meat is soft.

Submitter:

Description: 
Extra hot and spicy!  Concentrated curry paste gives this recipe a real kick!
