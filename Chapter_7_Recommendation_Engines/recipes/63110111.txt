Title:
Cauliflower Mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 head cauliflower
2. 2 T olive oil
3. 2 T butter
4. 4 cloves of minced garlic
5. 1/4 C cream cheese
6. 1/3 C half and half
7. Salt and Pepper
8. 2T grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Steam cauliflower florets for 10-12 minutes.
While cauliflower is steaming, combine and heat over a low flame olive oil and garlic, approx 5 minutes.
Add cream cheese and half and half to the oil and garlic mixture until just warmed through, 1-2 minutes.
Combine in a food processor all ingredients and season mixture with salt and pepper.
Sprinkle parmesan cheese over top as garnish.

Description: 
A creamy, yummy alternative to mashed potatoes.  It goes well with steak.
