Title:
Deli Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices deli-style baked cooked ham, each cut into 4 strips
4 oz. (1/2 of 8-oz. pkg.) Colby and Monterey Jack cheese, cut into 32 cubes
4 slices hard salami, each cut into 4 strips
16 grape tomatoes
16 small Spanish olives
RITZ Crackers

Directions:
Wrap  1 ham strip around each of 16 cheese cubes; wrap 1 salami strip around each remaining cheese cube.
Thread  1 ham roll-up, 1 tomato, 1 salami roll-up and 1 olive onto each of 16 frilled long toothpicks or 5-inch skewers. Arrange on serving plate.
Serve  with crackers
