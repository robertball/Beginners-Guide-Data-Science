Title:
Creamy Avocado Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small ripe avocado
¼ cup cilantro, stems and leaves
1 clove garlic
2 tablespoons plain nonfat Greek yogurt
2 tbsp lime juice
¼ cup water (or as needed)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients for the dressing in a blender or food processor.
Pulse for 1 minute or until the dressing is creamy.
Add more water as needed.
Dressing will keep fresh covered in the fridge for up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
A lighter, healthier version of creamy avocado dressing that can be used on salads, sandwiches or even as a dip.
