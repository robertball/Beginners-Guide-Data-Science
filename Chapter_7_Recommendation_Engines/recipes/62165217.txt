Title:
Sundried Tomato Pasta with Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Shrimp, peeled, deveined, and without tails
3 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 cup Sundried tomatoes, packed in oil (sliced)
1/2 onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
salt
pepper
1 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
16 oz angel hair pasta

Directions:
Heat 2 Tbsp olive oil in large skillet.  Add shrimp and cook until done.  Remove from pan and set aside.  Add remaining oil, and cook onion, garlic and tomatoes, until tender.  Add cream and combine.  Return shrimp to skillet and let simmer until sauce thickens, adding parmesan cheese at the very end.  Serve with angel hair pasta.

Shrimp pasta, with sundried tomatoes, and a creamy sauce!  Sure to be a crowd pleaser!
