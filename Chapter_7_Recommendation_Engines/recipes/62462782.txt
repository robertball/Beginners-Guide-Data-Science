Title:
Granny's Fluffy Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 egg white
1/4 t. cream of tartar
pinch of salt
1/4 c. confectioner's sugar

Directions:
Mix sugar, egg white, cream of tartar and salt, heat in double boiler. Whip for 4 minutes. Add 1/4 c. confectioner's sugar.
