Title:
Green Chili Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
start ingredients:
3 pound trimmed pork roast (tenderloin)
1 can green enchilada sauce
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can green chiles
1 diced onion
end ingredients
1 can diced potaotes or 1 cup frozen hash browns
1 can drained diced tomatoes

Directions:
mix start ingredients and pour over pork roast in crockpot
cook 4-6 hours on high or 6-8 hours on low
cook until fork tender
shred and return to sauce
add end ingredients
cook additional hour
serve
garnish with cheese and sour cream
serve as a soup, over chips, with tortillas
