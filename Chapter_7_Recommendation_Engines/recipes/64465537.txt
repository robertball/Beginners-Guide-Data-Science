Title:
Caribbean Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 2inch jalapeno pepper
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoon cocoa
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice

Directions:
Coarsely chop onion and add to food processor with chopping blade. Add all other ingredients and process for 15-60 seconds. Marinade meat or chicken for 30 minutes and BBQ until meat is cooked. Remaining marinade can be cooked and used as a sauce. Unused portions keeps for a few weeks in the fridge.

Submitter: 
Debi
Description: 
Quick marinade with exceptional flavour of the Caribbean Islands. For your next dinner party throw it together and then get ready for your guests while it marinades. So easy and the delightful dark brown colour pairs well with fresh summer veggies.
