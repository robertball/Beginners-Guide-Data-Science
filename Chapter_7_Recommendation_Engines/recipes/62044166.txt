Title:
Grandpa Jack's Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb beef roast (any cut will do)
3 stalks celery chopped
1 onion chopped
1 or 2 beef bouillon cubes
1 46oz can tomato or vegetable juice
1 large can whole peeled tomatoes
2 lb potatos peeled and chopped
2 lb carrots peeled and sliced
1lb pkg frozen peas
1lb pkg frozen corn
1lb pkg frozen cut green beans
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place roast, celery, onion, and bouillon in large pot and enough water to cover. Cover and cook approx 2 hours or until meat is falling apart. Or can be easily pulled apart with a fork. Add all the remaining ingredients. Let simmer approx 3 hours or until the potatos and carrots are tender and the aroma overcomes you and you can't wait any longer.

Submitter: 
RICHARDSON7
Description: 
An easy to make homemade vegetable soup. The longer it simmers, the better it tastes.  Freeze leftover and the taste quality only improves. All the ingredient measurements should be adjusted to your taste.
