Title:
The BEST Margarita Mix !!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1036443.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup tequila
1/2 cup triple sec
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
2 tablespoons honey
4 cups ice

Directions:
Blend tequila, triple sec, lime juice, honey, and ice in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
weybourne
Description: 
Similar recipe to the margaritas we drank in Montreal in the old port. So delicious!
