Title:
Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Pints whipping cream
1 12oz package semi sweet chocolate chips
1 frozen Sara Lee Poundcake (or make yourself and freeze, I'm too lazy)
1/2 cup rum or Frangelica or whatever you like
2 oz chopped hazlenuts
powdered sugar
1 small jar of Nutella

Directions:
1. Melt chocolate in double boiler, let cool bit, but not hardened.
2. Line a medium bowl with plastic wrap
3. Cut thin slices of frozen pound cake and line bowl with cake, including bottom of bowl. Cut shapes as needed to fit.
4. Brush with rum or other liqueur.
5. Whip cream until stiff, divide whipped cream in half into two bowls. Add melted chocolate into one bowl and whip.
6. Add chopped hazelnuts into other bowl, and mix in 1 cup of Nutella..
7. Next line bowl with the hazelnut cream layer, leaving an empty place in the center.
8. Fill that center space with the chocolate cream.
9. Cover all with another layer of rum-brushed poundcake, and another layered of spreaded Nutella.
10. Cover tightly with plastic wrap, and refrigerate at least overnight.
11. To unmold, remove the plastic wrap from the top, put a plate over the top, and flip over.
12. Remove the plastic wrap and sift with powdered sugar. (cocoa would be good too)
