Title:
Apple-Cinnamon Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
175 ml unsweetened cinnamon flavored apple sauce
75 ml 1% milk
2 30 g packages instant oatmeal (Plain or apples and & cinnamon)
1 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients blender beaker in order and blend until smooth
Pour onto heated griddle and cook until bubbles form and edges appear dry, turn and cook couple more minutes
