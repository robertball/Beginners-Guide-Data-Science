Title:
Pasta salad dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup	canola oil
1/2 cup	vinegar
1/4 cup + 2 tbs. corn syrup
1 clove garlic
2 tbs. fresh minced onion
2 tbs. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. parsley
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 tbs. Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender for 30 sec.

Submitter: 
Caleb1993
Description: 
A simple to make pasta salad dressing, based on the ingredient list found on a box of pasta salad mix, my recipe is almost identical to the Italian style pasta salad that comes from a box.
