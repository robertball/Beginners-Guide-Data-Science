Title:
Banana Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup apple juice
3 bananas, sliced
1 tablespoon honey
2 cups ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine apple juice, bananas, honey and ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
ARMax
Description: 
This is a great way to cool down. Bananas and pineapple juice, sweetened to taste and then blended with ice until smooth.Modified from: Banana Fruit Smoothie
