Title:
Green Bean and Bacon Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb green beans
1/4c almonds
3 slices bacon
1 red onion
1/2t salt
1/4t pepper

Directions:
Cook beans in salted boiling water, 8 minutes and remove.
Roast almonds 5 minutes and remove.
Cook 3 slices of bacon 8 minutes and remove.
Cook onion 8 minutes in grease.
Add beans, salt and pepper, bacon, and almonds until heated.
