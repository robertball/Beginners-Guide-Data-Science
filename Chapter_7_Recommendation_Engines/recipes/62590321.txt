Title:
Pork Egg Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pork, cooked and shredded
carrots, shredded
cabbage, shredded
red pepper, shredded
onion, shredded
garlic clove, minced
ginger, minced
water chestnuts, chopped
mushrooms, chopped
sugar
pepper
soy sauce
red wine vinegar

Directions:
Combine ingredients and cook in olive oil.
Roll 2 T in egg rolls. Use cornstarch/water to close edges of egg rolls
Fry in olive oil in fondue.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
We invented this recipe to test out our fondue. They turned out great!
