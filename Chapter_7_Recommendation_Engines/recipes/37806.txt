Title:
Zesty Three Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992787.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15.25 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can great Northern beans, rinsed, drained
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed, drained
1/2 cup diced green pepper
1/2 cup diced red pepper
1 cup SEVEN SEAS FREE Italian Fat Free Dressing

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl. Refrigerate several hours or until chilled.
