Title:
Taco Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 pkg taco mix
1/2 cup butter
1 cut milk
taco chips
chicken thighs or breasts

Directions:
Mix flour and taco mix in a plastic bag.  Melt the butter.  Crush the taco chips.  Dip chicken in butter than in bag with flour mix than in milk than in taco chips.  Put on greased cookie sheet and bake 30-45 min at 350.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
Taco seasoning and chips make the chicken crispy
