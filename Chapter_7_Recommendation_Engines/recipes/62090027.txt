Title:
low fat, low sodium chicken noodle soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless chicken breasts, boiled & fat removed
4 celery stalks, leaves and all
2 leeks, 1/2 inch cut from top, cleaned and sliced into medallions
1 large white onion, roughly chopped
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
1 large carrot, minced
2 cloves garlic, smashed & minced
5 cans reduced sodium, fat free chicken broth/stock
1 bag no yolk, or low carb pasta cooked (ribbon or small noodle pasta is best)
1 tsp spanish paprika
2 tsp madras curry spice
fresh ground pepper
1 can low sodium diced stewed tomatos (optional)

Directions:
Wash and clean all vegetables, to remove dirt/grime/pesticide residues.
Chop celery (fine or rough depending on desired consistency) including leaves, prepare onion, leeks and carrot as stated above. Smash and mince garlic, chop parsley, sautee all vegetables/herbs in 4 tbsp of chicken broth in a large soup pot, until garlic becomes fragrant and vegetables are hard/soft. Add remaining chicken broth, cover and simmer over low heat.  Add spices to pot.
Cook chicken and dice, add to soup pot.
Cook pasta according to directions on
box, add to soup pot.
Let soup simmer for about 4 more minutes, adjust flavoring to taste.
