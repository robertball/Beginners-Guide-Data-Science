Title:
Avocado Awesomeness on Rye Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/396114.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado, mashed (skin removed)
4 slices of Rye bread
1 large tomato, sliced
4 slices of chicken or turkey deli meat
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350F
Divide avocado evenly, spread onto each piece of bread and place on a cooking sheet.
Place 1 slice of deli meat on each piece of bread.
Next, place 2 slices of tomato, side by side, over top of deli meat.
Sprinkle shredded cheese over the tops of the tomatoes.
Place in oven on middle rack and bake for 5 - 10 minutes, let it toast and enjoy!

Submitter: 
SuperMom
Description: 
Easy, quick and delicious! This lunch can be altered in many ways to create new tastes. Keeps your guests asking for more!
