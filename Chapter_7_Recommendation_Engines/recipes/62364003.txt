Title:
Broiled Summer Squash With Radish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/406927.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup chopped red or green onion
1   tsp. fresh dill weed  (dry works too)
1/2 tsp. freshly cracked black pepper
1/4 tsp. table or sea salt
1   large zucchini squash (1/4" half wheel slices)
1   large yellow (crook neck) squash (1/2" cubes)
4-5 large red radishes (sliced 1/4" thick)

Directions:
Preheat oven to broil.
Combine oil, vinegar, dill, salt and pepper in mixing bowl, stir until mixed well.
Stir in onion, zucchini squash, yellow squash and radishes until all vegetables are coated well.
Transfer the mixture into a baking dish large enough for the mixture to be approximately 2" deep.
Cover with foil and broil for 10 minutes.
Remove foil and stir mixture.  Broil uncovered for 10 more minutes.

Description: 
This recipe is delicious served either hot or cold, and makes for a flavorful side dish.  I personally serve this recipe hot, over a bed of *quinoa.  Remember to use organic and locally grown produce whenever possible!
