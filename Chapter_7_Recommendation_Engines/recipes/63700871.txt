Title:
Rice & Beans (Haitian Style) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons olive oil
1 bulb shallot, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
16 ounces canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 pound cooked, shredded chicken
2 cup uncooked long grain white rice
2 bay leaves
1 teaspoon adobo seasoning (optional)
1 tablespoon kosher salt
ground black pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 tbsp. dried parsley
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1 scotch bonnet chile pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in a soup pot over medium heat. Saute shallot and garlic until fragrant. Stir in canned beans and shredded chicken, and cook for 2 minutes. Add 5 cups water and stir in the uncooked rice. Season with bay leaves, adobo seasoning, salt, pepper, cloves, parsley and thyme. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 18 to 20 minutes.
