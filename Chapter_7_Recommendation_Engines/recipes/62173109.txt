Title:
Tuna noodle casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10oz medium cooked noodles
1 can tuna
1/2C mayo
1C chopped celery
1/3C chopped onion
1/4C chopped green peppers
1/4C chopped pimentos
1/2tsp salt
1Can cond. cream of celery soup
1/2C milk
4oz sharp cheddar cheese (shredded)

Directions:
Combine all ingreidnets except soup, milk and cheese.  Then combine the soup, milk and cheese in a small sauce pan. Heat and stir until cheese melts. Add to the noodle mixture. Turn into a 2quart casserole cover and bake at 425 for 20min.
