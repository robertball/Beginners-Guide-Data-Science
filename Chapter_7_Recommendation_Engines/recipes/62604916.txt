Title:
Deli Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two cans Great Northern beans with liquid
5 strips bacon-cooked and diced
1 C. Light brown sugar
1/3 C. Dark Molasses
1/2 white onion chopped
mustard to taste.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a 2 qt casserole dish or baking pan, cover with foil and bake in a 350 degree oven for 25-30 minutes or until bubbling.

Submitter: 
Jeanni Kistler
Description: 
Great Northern beans, molasses, brown sugar, bacon, mustard and onions make this easy, to die for baked bean recipe top of the list.
