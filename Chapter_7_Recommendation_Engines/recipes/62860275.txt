Title:
Goulash Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/684736.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups macaroni
1 quart stewed tomatoes
2 teaspoons paprika
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
6 ounces tomato paste (optional)

Directions:
In large saucepan brown ground chuck, drain.
Add tomatoes, onions, garlic, paprika, chili powder, macaroni and tomato paste, if desired. Add water, a tablespoon at a time, if mixture seems too dry.  Simmer until macaroni is tender.

Submitter: 
messyjessi, RD
Description: 
Macaroni is cooked in a spicy tomato sauce flavored with onions, garlic, paprika and chili powder. Add cooked ground beef to make a hearty, satisfying meal.Modified from: Goulash Supreme
