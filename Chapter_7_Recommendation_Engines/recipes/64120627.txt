Title:
Fizzy orange creamsicle shot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1301272.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 oz of pinnacle whipped cream vodka
1/2 oz of svedka orange cream pop liquor
1/2 oz sunkist orange soda
1/2 a teaspoon of vanilla ice cream
Whip cream to top off

Directions:
In a shot glass pour whip cream vodka , svedka and orange soda, add a dollop of vanilla ice cream top off with whip cream shoot back and enjoy they are delicious.

Submitter: 
Vidalina Tolerson
Description: 
Creamy orange shooter great for summer time parties that make you remember the yummy Ice cream pops and floats.
