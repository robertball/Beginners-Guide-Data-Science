Title:
Freezer Berry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups blueberries
5 cups sugar
2 tbsp water mixed with 2 tsp Fresh Fruit
3/4 cup water
1 (1.75 ounce) package powdered Sure Jell

Directions:
In a large bowl, crush the blueberries.  Stir in sugar and Fresh Fruit mixture.  Let stand for 10 minutes.  In a small saucepan, bring water and Sure Jell mixture to a boil.  Boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Add to fruit mixture; stir for 3 minutes.  Pour into jars or freezer containers; cool to room temperature, about 30 minutes.  Cover and let stand overnight or until set, but not longer than 24 hours.  Refrigerate and use within 2 weeks or freeze.
