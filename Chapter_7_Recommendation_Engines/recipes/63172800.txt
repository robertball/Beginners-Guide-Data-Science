Title:
Amazing Coconut Almond Crusted Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup chopped toasted sliced almonds
1 cup flaked coconut
1 tablespoon sesame seeds
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 pinch nutmeg
1 pinch salt
olive oil
4 tilapia fillets

Directions:
Toast sliced almonds by baking 350* oven flat baking pan for  3 min till brown....place in bowl...set pan aside to cook fish on
Place the beaten eggs in a dish.... Mix the almonds, coconut, sesame seeds, brown sugar,nutmeg,salt together in a mixing bowl.....  Dip each tilapia fillet into the beaten egg and then press into the almond coconut mixture..... make sure both sides coated well...Place the coated tilapia onto flat baking pan that has been sprinkled with olive oil....
Bake in the preheated oven 350* for 20 min until the fish flakes easily with a fork...
