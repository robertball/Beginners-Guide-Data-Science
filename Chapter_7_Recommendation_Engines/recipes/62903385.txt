Title:
** Skillet Apple Dutch Baby Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl. melted butter - divided
1 lg. sweet apple; peeled, cored & cut into 1/4" wedges (opt: substitute pears or peaches if desired.)
1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. AP flour
4 eggs
3 Tbl. sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven 450 degrees with rack in middle.
Measure 1 Tbl. melted butter into med. skillet. Add apple wedges & cook until softened (3-5 min.).
Pour remaining melted butter into a blender. Mix butter, milk, flour, eggs, sugar, vanilla, & salt in blender until smooth.
Pour batter over apples in skillet & transfer skillet into oven.
Bake 15 min. or until pancake is puffed & golden.
Serve immediately with desired toppings.
