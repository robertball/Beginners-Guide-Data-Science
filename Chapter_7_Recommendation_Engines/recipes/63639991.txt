Title:
Homemade Crystalized Ginger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peeled & sliced ginger root
3/4 cup water
3/4 cup sugar
Additional sugar for dusting

Directions:
In small saucepan, bring water & sugar to a rolling boil.
Add sliced ginger, reduce heat, and continue to cook with a soft boil for 20 minutes.
Remove ginger to rack (place foil underneath) and let dry.
Once dry, roll slices in additional sugar.
Store in airtight container for up to 3 months.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Don't pay $10 for a tiny bottle of crystalized (candied) ginger when you can make it yourself in a half hour.  My total cost was apx $1.00;  0.82 for ginger (1/4#) and perhaps an extra .18 for sugar.
