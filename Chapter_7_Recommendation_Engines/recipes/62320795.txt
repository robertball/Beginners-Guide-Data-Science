Title:
Jan's Cinnamon Bun Sticky Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter butter
1/4 cup molasses
1/4 cup dark corn syrup
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place all ingredients in sauce pan and bring to a simmer for 3 minutes and sauce begins to thicken.
Cool mixture for 5 minutes.
Pour over cinnamon buns or use a pastry brush for even coverage.
Decorate with nuts, raisins if desired.
You can also place sauce in the bottom of your pan that is lined with parchment paper or non-stick foil, add toppings if desired, and place dough on top.  After baking place a foil lined cookie sheet over top of pan and turn upside down.  Remove foil and serve.
