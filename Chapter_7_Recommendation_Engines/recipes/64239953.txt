Title:
Paleo Chicken and Wild Rice Stuffed Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Acorn Squash, halved and seeded
1/2 c. water
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp olive oil
1 c uncooked cubed chicken breast
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp chopped celery
3/4 c chopped yellow onion
1/4 c chopped orange bell pepper
1/4 c chopped fresh mushrooms
1 1/4 c reduced sodium chicken broth
1 c water
1 (6 oz) LONG GRAIN and wild rice (seasoning mix included)
1/2 c Panko bread crumbs
1 tbsp butter melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Pace acorn squash cut side down on a baking pan that has one inch deep sides. Pour 1/2 c water into pan. Bake squash halves for 30 minutes or until fork-tender.
Meanwhile, for filling heat 1 tbsp butter and the oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add chicken, salt and black pepper. Brown chicken. Stir in celery, onion, bell pepper and mushrooms. Saute 5 minutes stir in broth, water, rice and rice seasoning packet. Cook stirring frequently 10-15 minutes until liquid is absorbed
In a small bowl combine breadcrumbs and 1 tbsp butter. Fill each squash half with about 1/2 cup filling. Top with one heaping tbsp bread crumb mixture. Bake for 25 minutes more.
