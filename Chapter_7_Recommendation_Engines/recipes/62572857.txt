Title:
Perfect Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tea bag each of the following (Orange Pekoe tea, Green tea, Mint tea)
6 cups boiling water
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup Lemon juice
6 cups cold water

Directions:
Put tea bags in a large container and add boiling water, let steep 5 minutes.
Add sugar and lemon juice and stir untill disolved.
Add cold water, stir and refrigerate.
Enjoy!!!

Veronique Jong

A mix of Orange Peko, Green and Mint Tea
