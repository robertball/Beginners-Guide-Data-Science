Title:
Grilled Vegetables with Balsamic Vinegar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 yellow squash, cut into 1/2-inch slices
1 zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch slices
1 red bell pepper, cut into 1/2-inch slices
1 red onion
8 ounces crimini or baby bella mushrooms

Directions:
Whisk olive oil, soy sauce, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Toss vegetables in soy sauce marinade. Marinate for about 45 minutes.
Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Remove vegetables from marinade, shaking off excess.
Grill vegetables on preheated grill until tender, 10 to 15 minutes, brushing vegetables with marinade. Transfer cooked vegetables to a platter and serve with any remaining marinade.
Can also roast in the oven at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
