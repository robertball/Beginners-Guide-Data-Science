Title:
Oven Fried Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups (or less) long grain rice
3 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup canola oil
1 envelope dry onion soup mix
1 can mushroom pieces, drained
OR 1 can miniature corn (my personal favorite)
1 green pepper, diced (I've substituted peas here)
1 stalk celery, chopped
3 cups leftover meat (beef, pork or chicken)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Put into a small roaster or large casserole dish.
Cook, covered, @ 350 x 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A great way to use leftover chicken, beef or pork. Makes quite a lot. Tastes great reheated too.
