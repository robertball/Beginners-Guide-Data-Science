Title:
Hard Boiled Egg Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/473443.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices white bread (or bread of choice)
1 tablespoon butter, softened
2 tablespoons whipped cream cheese
1 hard boiled egg, peeled and sliced
5-6 dashes hot pepper sauce (such as FranksÂ®), or to taste
Salt and pepper, optional, to taste

Directions:
Toast bread and butter each slice.
Spread 1 tablespoon of cream cheese onto each piece of toast.
Layer hard boiled egg slices evenly onto one slice of toast.
Add hot sauce, and salt and pepper, optional.
Top with other slice of toast.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
These are so good! I have been making these for years and we absolutely LOVE them. This is a great on-the-go breakfast or just a different way to use up leftover hard boiled eggs. Enjoy!
