Title:
Spicy Cheddar Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups frozen hash brown potatoes
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese, divided
2 cheddar brats, sliced into coins
2 Tbsps real bacon bits
1 Tbsp minced chives
1/4 c. minced yellow onion
1 jalapeno pepper seeded and minced
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/8 tsp pepper
4 eggs
12 ounce can evaporated milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degress. Grease 2 quart baking dish.
Pour hash browns evenly on bottom of greased dish.  Sprinkle 3/4 c. cheddar, onion, chives, jalapeno, bacon bits, garlic salt, and pepper over potatoes.  Arrange sliced brats on top.
Beat eggs and evaporated milk in medium sized bowl until combined.  Salt and pepper to taste.
Pour egg mixture over potato mixture in the dish.  (At this point, the dish can be covered and refrigerated overnight).
Bake, uncovered for 45 - 55 mintues (or 55 - 65 mintues if refrigerated overnight) until knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Sprinkle with 1/4 c. remaining cheddar cheese. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
shackbart
Description: 
This delicious breakfast casserole uses many ingredients you probably have on hand.  Diced ham or additional bacon can be substituted for brats.  Shredded pepper jack cheese can be substituted for the cheddar and jalapeno pepper.
