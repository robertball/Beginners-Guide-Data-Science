Title:
Buttercup Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large buttercup squash, yielding 6 cups
1 8 oz. package cream cheese, softened(I use light cream cheese)
4 cups chicken broth
1 medium yellow onion, diced
3 tablespoons of butter
1-2 teaspoons freshly ground pepper
1/3 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon dried (or fresh, finely shredded)basil
1 teaspoon either marjoram, summer savory or thyme

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
2. Halve the squash and cook it in oven until soft (about 45 mins) facedown in roasting pan with 1/4 inch of water.
3. Dice and cook onion with the butter in a saucepan.
4. Prepare the 4 cups of chicken broth and add the dried spices, cooking over medium heat.
5. Puree onion, cream cheese and squash in blender, or food processor (can be done in batches) then add it to the chicken broth, stirring frequently and cooking through (but do not boil).

Submitter: 
leahmarie5
Description: 
A creamy soup with a ton of flavour. You can substitue any winter squash.
