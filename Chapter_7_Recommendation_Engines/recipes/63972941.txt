Title:
Mom's Egg Fu Yung Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Patties:
3 eggs, beaten
3/4 c. bean sprouts, drained (1/2 can)
1/2 c. sausage or hamburger, cooked and drained.
2 T. chopped onion or scallions
1 T. soy sauce
Sauce:
1 tsp. cornstarch
1 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. vinegar
2 1/2 tsp. soy sauce
1/2 c. water

Directions:
For Patties:  Combine beaten eggs, cooked and drained meat, drained bean sprouts, onion, and soy sauce.  Mix until well combined.
Heat large skillet to med-high and spray with cooking spray.
Spoon egg mixture into hot pan using a measuring scoop.
Fry in batches until patties are browned and cooked through, keeping coked patties warm on a platter until mixture is gone.
For Sauce:  Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan.
Bring to a boil and simmer until thickened.
Serve over cooked patties.
