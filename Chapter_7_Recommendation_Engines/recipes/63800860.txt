Title:
Slow Roasted Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•1 pound cherry tomatoes, halved
•1 tablespoon brown sugar
•3/4 tablespoon salt
•1 tablespoon capers
•1 tablespoon chopped oregano
•2 tablespoons olive oil
•Basil leaves, for garnish
•1 loaf French baguette, toasted and sliced (optional)

Directions:
Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 250 degrees F.
2.Soak capers in water for about 10 minutes. Drain, then squeeze the capers lightly to remove excess liquid.
3.In a small bowl, mix sugar, salt and basil. Pour sugar mixture on the halved tomatoes. Add capers and olive oil. Mix well. Transfer to a baking sheet.
4.Bake for 2-1/2 to 3 hours. If tomatoes are drying out, drizzle with additional olive oil.
5.To serve, top with basil leaves over slices of French baguette, if desired.
