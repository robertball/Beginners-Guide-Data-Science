Title:
Rick Gallop GI Grilled Tilapia with Black Bean Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tilapia fillets (4 oz each)
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp each salt and ground black pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 TB olive oil
Black Bean Mango Salsa
1 c cooked black beans
1 large diced ripe mango
1 finely chopped red pepper
Zest and juice of 1 lime
3 TB each chopped red onion and fresh coriander
1/4 c chopped fresh mint
2 tsp chopped fresh chili pepper or 2 tsp hot chili sauce
1 TB extra virgin olive oil
pinch each salt and ground black pepper

Directions:
fish-in a small bowl, mix together all seasonings. rub mixture on pat dried fish fillets. place fillets on greased grill over medium-high heat. close lid and grill for about 10 minutes or until fish is opaque and flakes easily with fork.
salsa-stir together black beans, mango, red pepper, lime zest and juice, red onion, mint, coriander, chili pepper/hot chili sauce, olive oil, and salt and pepper.
