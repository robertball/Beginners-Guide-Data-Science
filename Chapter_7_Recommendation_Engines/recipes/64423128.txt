Title:
Healthy Cocoa, Banana, PB Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2306526.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk (you may substitute unsweetened almond milk)
2 teaspoons cocoa powder (raw, unsweetened)
2 tablespoons nut butter
1/2-1 frozen banana (may be replaced with 1/2 avocado)
1 teaspoon honey
optional: a pinch of cinnamon or cayenne pepper to make the cocoa "pop"

Directions:
Blend all ingredients until smooth. Protein: 16.75g (based on 1 cup cow milk, 2 Tbsp nut butter and 1/2 banana) or 10g when almond milk is substituted.

Submitter: 
Dr. Cora Rivard
Description: 
This recipe is ideal for a post-workout protein drink, or afternoon pick me up. It is high in protein, potassium, antioxidants, and L-arginine, which supports healthy blood vessel function. No supplemental protein mix needed!
