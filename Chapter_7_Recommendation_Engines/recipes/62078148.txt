Title:
Red Sauce Meatless Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 (4 oz) cans mushrooms, drained
1 pt ricotta cheese (healthier option: cottage cheese)
½ cup parmesan cheese, divided
1 (10 oz) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
8 oz. grated mozzarella
1 teaspoon garlic powder
8 lasagna noodles, dry
1 (26 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
½ teaspoon salt
1 can artichokes, drained and cut into smaller pieces(you won’t use all of them)

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350º.  Lightly grease a 13x9 baking dish.
2) In a medium bowl: beat eggs and mix in the ricotta cheese, salt, spinach, ¼ cup parmesan cheese, and garlic powder.
3) Spread ½ cup spaghetti sauce into dish, put 4 noodles over sauce to cover the bottom of the dish.
4) Spread ½ of the spinach mixture, then ½ of the mozzarella cheese, 1 can of the mushrooms, pieces of 3 artichokes, and half of the jar of spaghetti sauce over the noodles.
4) Add another layer of lasagna noodles.
5) Repeat layers and top with the remaining parmesan cheese.
6) Cover securely with aluminum foil and bake 1 hour.
