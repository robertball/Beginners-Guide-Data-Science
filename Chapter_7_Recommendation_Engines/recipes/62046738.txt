Title:
Corn Flake Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup corn syrup
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
6 cups corn flakes

Directions:
Combine corn syrup and sugar.  Boil for one minute.  Remove from heat and add peanut butter.  Mix thoroughly.  Mix in corn flakes.  Drop by spoonfuls on to wax paper.  Cool.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
My mother in law is allergic to wheat so I am always looking for recipes she can eat.  Not only does this candy taste great, but it is also quick and easy to make.
