Title:
Candied Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup honey
1/2 cup white sugar & 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
2 Tbsp. butter
2 cups whole almonds

Directions:
Roast on baking sheet for 12-20 min. stir up occasionally.
Boil honey, butter over medium.
Reduce to med/low & simmer for 2 min stirring occasionally.
Add almonds to pot and simmer for 2 min.  Take off heat.
Transfer to cookie sheet sprayed with nonstick spray or wax paper.
Spread in a single layer and cool for a couple minutes...
Then mix your cinnamon and sugar (try adding in 1 T. vanilla), then toss almonds in it.
Place on baking sheet and broil a few seconds, till sugar carmelizes.  Store in airtight container.
