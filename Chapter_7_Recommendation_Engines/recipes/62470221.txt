Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/846761.jpg
Ingredients:
Pastry:                  ½ Cup Powdered Sugar
8 oz. (2 sticks) butter
2 Cups Flour
¼ tsp salt
Filling:                   4 eggs beaten
2 Cups sugar
¼ Cup fresh lemon juice
¼ Cup  plus  3/4 Tbs Flour
1 tsp grated lemon rind (or a little more)
½ tsp baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Pastry:
Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Stir in flour and salt.  Mix well.  Press mixture into a 13 x 9 baking dish line with aluminium foil & grease.
Bake for 15 – 20 minutes or until light golden brown.
Remove from oven and cool slightly.
Filling:
Blend all filling ingredients and pour over the baked crust.  Bake for 25 minutes.  Let cool. Sift additional powdered sugar over the bars.
Cut in squares.
