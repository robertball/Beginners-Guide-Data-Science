Title:
Basic Bread  Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices bread, toasted and cubed
1/2 stick butter, melted
1/2 c EA. onions and celery [optional]
* 2 T parsley
* 1 t. EA sage, thyme, salt, pepper
* 1/4 t. EA ground nutmeg and cloves
***the seasonings may be replaced with 3T poultry seasoning***
1/2 c chicken broth
1 lrg. egg, beaten

Directions:
Sautee veggies in butter
Remove from heat
Add seasoning and bread
Toss lightly to coat.
Slowly add broth, you need it moist but not packed
Finally incoperate beaten egg.
Cook in a cassarole at 350 for 25 min
Cook in a bird as per bird size.

Submitter: 
MRSREAMESNOODLE
Description: 
Great for a small bird, 4 cornish hens, or an 8x8 cassarole. It's easy and you'll already have all the ingreadiants! If you wanna stuff a 12-14lb turkey, double the recipe.
