Title:
Hawaiian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
whole chicken
Apricot Jam
canned pineapples
maraschino cherries

Directions:
Rinse and dry chicken.
Place in Deep Dish Covered Baker
Brush on a thick coat of Apricot jam
Pour pineapple over chicken, add some cherries.
Cover with lid and bake 1 1/2 hours at 375'.
use chicken juice as gravy.
