Title:
Impossible Quesadilla Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 4oz. cans chopped green chiles, drained
4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups milk
1 cup Bisquick mix
4 eggs

Directions:
Heat over to 425 degrees. Grease 10" pie plate.
Sprinkle chiles and cheese in plate.
Beat milk, baking mix and eggs 15 seconds in blender.
Pour into pie plate.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
Serve with sour cream, salsa, and/ or guacamole.
