Title:
Oven Roasted Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh Brussells Sprouts
1/2 cup olive oil
2 Tbsp butter, melted
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 Tbsp garlic powder
1/2 Tbsp onion powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 Tbsp Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven at 350 degress.
Combine olive oil, melted butter, and dry ingredients in a large zip-lock bag and mix with your hands. Toss in Brussels sprouts and coat thoroughly. Let sprouts marinate in the bag for 15 minutes.
Place Brussels sprouts on a non-stick cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degress for 15 minutes.  After 15 minutes, stir the Brussels sprouts and turn, cook for another 15 minutes.
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kays
Description: 
This is a simple, but tasty preparation for Brussels sprouts that everyone will love!
