Title:
Amish Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 potatoes, large
4 oz. cream cheese
milk, till right consistency
2 teaspoons butter
Salt to taste

Directions:
Peel potatoes.  Cut into 1" pieces.
Boil potatoes in water until softened.
Pour off water and mash.
Add salt, butter, cream cheese, and milk.
Mash together.
