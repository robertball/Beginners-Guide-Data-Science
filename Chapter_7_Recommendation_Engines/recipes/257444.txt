Title:
Watermelon Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4487369.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed seeded watermelon
2 cups crushed ice
1/3 cup tequila
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup lime juice
1 tablespoon vodka
1 tablespoon orange liqueur

Directions:
Combine watermelon, ice, tequila, sugar, lime juice, vodka, and orange liqueur in a blender; blend well. Pour margaritas into glasses.
