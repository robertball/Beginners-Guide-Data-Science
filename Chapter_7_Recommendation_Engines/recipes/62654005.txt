Title:
Andi's Chicken, Broccoli and Wild Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588418.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb chopped, cooked, boneless chicken breast
6 oz box Uncle Ben's Long Grain and Wild Rice (Original Recipe), uncooked
3 cups of water
2 tsp chicken bouillon granules
10 3/4-oz can cream of chicken soup
16-oz bag frozen broccoli
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 package Lipton Onion Soup Mix

Directions:
Chop chicken and cook until done
Combine all ingredients and stir in slow cooker
Cook on high for 3-4 hours

Submitter: 
sammyc14
Description: 
Got this recipe from a crock pot cook book and put our own twist on it.  This will be great as a main dish or a side dish for family cooking.  This is one of our favorite slow cooking recipes!
