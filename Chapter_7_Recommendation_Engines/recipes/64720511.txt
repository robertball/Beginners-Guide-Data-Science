Title:
Grammy's Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package  graham cracker crumb mix
5 8 oz. packages cream cheese
1 1/2 cup sugar
3 T flour
1/4 t. vanilla
5 eggs plus 2 yolks
1/4 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.  Put a pan with water on the lowest rack
Prepare crust according to package instructions.
Pat crust firmly in 10 inch springform pan.
In mixer bowl, blend cream cheese, sugar, flour and vanilla at high speed.
Beat in eggs and yolks one at a time until smooth.
Beat in cream.
Pour into prepared pan .
Bake 10 minutes.
Reduce temperature to 250 and bake 1 hour more.
Turn off oven and let cheesecake sit for 1 hour.
Cool almost completely and then refrigerate for 4 hours or more.  Loosen cake from sides of pan before removing sides.
If using a 9 inch pan, increase cooking time to 1 1/2 hours.
