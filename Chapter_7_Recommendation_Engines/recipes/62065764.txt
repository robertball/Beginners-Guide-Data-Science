Title:
Cherry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 21 oz can cherry pie filling
1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1 can diced peaches, drained
1 can mandarin oranges, drained
1 12 oz container whipped topping
2 C miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Combine cherry pie filling, sweetened condensed milk, peaches, oranges, and whipped topping until no streaks remain.  Fold in marshmallows.

Submitter: 
Rochelle Brooks Thompson
Description: 
This rich and fluffy dish makes a wonderful addition to any summer barbeque or picnic.  Also goes great with a traditional holiday dinner.
