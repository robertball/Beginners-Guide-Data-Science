Title:
The Punky Monkey Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 cardamom pods
1 ounce aged rum
1 ounce bourbon, preferably overproof
1/2 ounce agave nectar
1/2 ounce pineapple juice
1/2 ounce fresh lemon juice
Dash of Angostura bitters
Dash of Peychaudâ€™s bitters
1 cardamom shoot, for garnish (optional)

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, lightly muddle the cardamom pods. Add ice and all of the remaining ingredients except the cardamom shoot and shake well. Strain into a chilled coupe and garnish.
