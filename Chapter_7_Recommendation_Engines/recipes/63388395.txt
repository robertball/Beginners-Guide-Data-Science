Title:
Green Chile Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 # lean pork sirloin or loin, cut into 1/2" cubes
1 jar (16 oz) Gemetti's Hot roasted green chiles with lime and cilantro
1 onion, diced
1 tsp garlic and herb seasoning (mrs. dash)
salt & pepper to taste
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp olive oil
1 qts chicken broth
1 can ro-tel tomatoes with chiles
2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
2 cans shoepeg corn, or 3 cups frozen
if desired, hot pepper flakes

Directions:
in stockpot, heat the oil over medium heat and add onions. Stir until onions are translucent then add pork, garlic, seasonings and green chiles.add 1 cup[ water,reduce heat and cook covered for  90 minutes.  Add stock, potatoes, corn and Rotel and simmer covered for 45 minutes. Add water to give soup consistancy desired.
