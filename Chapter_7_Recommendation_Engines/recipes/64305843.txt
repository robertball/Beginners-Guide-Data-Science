Title:
Real Food Sweet Onion and Pineapple Chicken Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1968980.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
2 large skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 1/2 C. frozen organic bell pepper slices
1/2 sweet onion, cut into 1/2-inch squares
1 cup teriyaki sauce, home-made
1/2 sweet onion, chopped
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup cashew pieces
3 dashes Asian-style hot sauce, or to taste (optional)
1 cup fresh pineapple chunks

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir chicken, salt, and pepper in hot oil until chicken is almost cooked through, 5 to 7 minutes.  Add additional T. of sesame oil and bell peppers and onions.  Saute slightly for 4 minutes.
Add teriyaki sauce and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until liquid is reduced by half and chicken is cooked through, 15 to 20 minutes.
Stir in cashew pieces and hot sauce into chicken mixture and simmer until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove skillet from heat. Stir pineapple into chicken mixture.
