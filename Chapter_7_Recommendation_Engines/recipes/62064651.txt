Title:
classic cocktail franks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-pkg little smokies or 2 pkg cut up hot dogs
1 lg jar grape jelly
1 lg bottle bar-b-q sauce
1/2 C white wine

Directions:
brown franks in dry skillet
put browned franks in dry crock pot
add jelly, bbq sauce and wine
cook on low until bubble and yummy
serve with tooth picks

Submitter: 
Pam Rosterman
Description: 
This is a classic party dish that goes fast -- with a simple addition
