Title:
unstuffed cabbage dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pd. ground beef
3/4 teas. garlic powder
1 teas. black pepper
1 15 oz. can beef broth low sodium
1 envelope Lipton Onion Soup Mix or Beefy Onion Soup Mix
1/2 small chopped onion
1 cup of uncooked minute rice
2 cups of uncooked diced cabbage
1/2 large can of Vegetable Juice (such as V-8)

Directions:
Brown hamburger with onion, garlic powder and pepper in a skillet.  Drain off fat.  Add one can of beef broth along with the onion soup mix.  Stir until blended.  Bring to boil.  Add minute rice, cabbage and vegetable juice.  Mix well. Cover and reduce heat to low.  Cook ontop of stove for 30 mins. until liquid absorbs.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Michele DArcy
Description: 
This recipes cuts the time of baking cabbage rolls and it tastes just as good!
