Title:
Guinness Beer Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32-oz package Kraft Velveeta cheese product
1 Cup Guinness beer
1/2 Cup salsa
2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 Tsp chili powder
1/2 Tsp onion powder
1/4 Tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Cut Velveeta block into 1/2-inch cubes. Cook in slow cooker on high until melted, approx. 20 minutes.
Add beer, salsa, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper. Stir frequently until smooth and heated through, about 10 minutes.
