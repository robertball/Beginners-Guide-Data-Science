Title:
Mexican Chili Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25# Ground Chuck
1 15oz can Dark Kidney Beans, Drained
1 15oz can elRio Enchilada Sauce
1 15oz can Tomato Sauce
8 oz Shredded Mexican Cheese
1 Bag Fritos

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain.
Combine ground beef, beans, enchilada sauce, tomato sauce, 1/2 bag of shredded cheese, and 1/2 bag of Fritos in a 9"x13" glass pan and cook on 350 for 30 minutes.
During the last 5-10 minutes add additional cheese and Fritos on the top.
This meal is very good with sour cream.

Great and easy mexican dish with Fritos.  A hit for the whole family.
