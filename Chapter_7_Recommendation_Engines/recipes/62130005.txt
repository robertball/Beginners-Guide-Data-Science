Title:
High-Protein, Low-Fat, Low-Sugar Granola with Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups quick rolled oats
1 cup  almonds-no salt
1 cup  sunflower seeds-no salt
1 cup  raisins
1 cup  coconut flakes
1/2 tsp salt (optional)
1 tsp  cinnamon
3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa
2 tsp  vanilla extract
3      egg whites
1 cup  granulated sweetener-such as Altern *liquidified
water*

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray with non-stick cooking spay flavored with butter 10x13 pan and set aside.
In a large bowl, mix oatmeal, almonds, sunflower seeds, raisins, coconut, cinnamon, salt*(opt) and cocoa together by hand throughly; set aside.
In a small bowl, measure 1 cup of sweetener and put enough water in it to make it syrupy-estimate-best use a fork to blend it.
Add egg whites, vanilla to liquidified sweetener and blend well.
Add egg white mixture to dry granola, mix throughly until it is saturated and sticky.
Pour mixture into pan and press down into pan completely.
Bake for 20-25 minutes.
Cool.
Break into pieces or cut into bars.
