Title:
Sweet Restaurant Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/686088.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head grated green cabbage
3 grated carrots
1/2 medium sized onion, diced
2/3 cup Mayo, fatfree
2/3 cup Butermilk
1/2 cup white sugar
1 Tbsp Seasoned rice wine vingegar
1 teaspoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Combine the coleslaw cabbage, carrots and onion in a large bowl.
Whisk together the mayo, buttermilk, sugar, vinegar, and poppy seeds in a large bowl; blend thoroughly. Pour liquid mixture over coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
charlotte henry
Description: 
Easy to make with convenient, packaged coleslaw, in this version the creamy dressing with vinegar, sugar and vegetable oil is close to what's served at popular fried chicken, burger and fish restaurants.Modified from: Sweet Restaurant Slaw
