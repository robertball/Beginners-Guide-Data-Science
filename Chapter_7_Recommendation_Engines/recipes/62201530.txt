Title:
Easy Cinnamon Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BREAD
3 cans of biscuits (cut into quarters)
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup nuts
SAUCE
1 1/2 stick butter or margarine
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
1.  Mix bread dry ingredients in a bowl or bag.
2.  Shake biscuits pieces - coating each piece.
3.  Place 1/2 of the biscuit quarters in a bundt pan.
4.  Mix sauce ingredients together.
5.  Pour half of the sauce over the biscuits in the bunt pan.
6.  Sprinkle with half of the nuts.
7.  Repeat above for the 2nd layer.
8.  Bake at 350 for 45-50 min.
