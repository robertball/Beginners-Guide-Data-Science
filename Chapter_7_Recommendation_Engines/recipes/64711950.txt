Title:
Roasted Tomato Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5615048.jpg
Ingredients:
•	500 grams ripe roma tomatoes
•	2 cloves garlic
•	1 tbsp olive oil
•	Salt and pepper, to taste
•	10 leaves fresh basil

Directions:
1.	Heat the oven to 190°C Prepare a baking sheet with tin foil or parchment paper.
2.	Cut the tomatoes in half lengthwise. Seed the the tomatoes.
3.	Place the tomato halves skin side down on the prepared baking sheet. Add the two cloves of garlic. Drizzle the oil evenly over the tomatoes and garlic. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Cook in the oven until the tomatoes are bubbling, about twenty-five minutes.
4.	Turn up the heat to 215°. Cook another ten minutes or until the tomatoes just begin to caramelize. Turn off the heat. Let the tomatoes sit in the hot oven with the door closed until caramelized, about 5 minutes.
5.	Transfer the tomatoes and garlic to a bowl. Tear the basil into small pieces and add them to the tomatoes. Using a wooden spoon, crush the mixture to break apart the tomatoes. Remove the peels if desired. Leave the tomatoes a bit chunky.
6.	Serve over your favorite cooked pasta.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Roma tomatoes and garlic are oven roasted and caramelized to give your pasta a deep, rich flavor. Roasting the tomatoes really draws out the sweet flavor, which mixes so well with the roasted garlic.
