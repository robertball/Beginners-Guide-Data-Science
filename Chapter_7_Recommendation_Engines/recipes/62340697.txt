Title:
Broccoli Beef stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/391050.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinade- whisk together:
1 egg white
1 tbs chinese rice wine or dry sherry (I used white wine)
1 tbs cornstarch (Toss all with meat and refrigerate for 1 hour)
OYSTER SAUCE
3/4 cup chicken broth
1 tbs cornstarch
1 tbs cornstarch
1 tbs chinese rice wine or dry white wine
1 tbs soy sauce
3 tbs oyster sauce
2 tsp sesame oil
1 lb beef, thinly sliced
1 pinch each of salt, sugar, powdered ginger
vegetable oil

Directions:
1. drain excess marinade from beef. heat vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over medium heat.  Add beef, slowly stir until browned on both sides.
2. Wipe pan clean. Heat the pan over high heat, add 2 tbs oil, then minced garlic, broccoli, a pinch of salt and a pinch of sugar, and a dash of powdered ginger.  Stir fry until crisp-tender.
3. Add the beef and sauce to broccoli, and stir until the sauce is thick and the vegetables and beef are cooked through, about 3 minutes.
