Title:
Butter Almond Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289118.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sliced almonds
1 tablespoon butter
3/4 cup light brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/4 cups heavy cream
1/4 cup 2% milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a small skillet over medium heat, saute almonds in butter until lightly browned, stirring frequently.  Set aside.
In a medium saucepan over low heat, stir together brown sugar, eggs, 1 1/4 cups heavy cream and 1/4 cup 2% milk until smooth.  Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally, and cook 2 minutes more.  Remove from heat and stir in 1/2 cup heavy cream, vanilla and almonds.
Pour into ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturers' directions.
