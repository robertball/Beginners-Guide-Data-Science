Title:
Flank Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C lemon juice
1/4 C soy sauce
1/2 C oil
1/4 C teryaki sauce
garlic salt to taste
onion salt to taste
1 t ginger
2 t brown sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. pour into 9x12 pyrex dish. Marinate all day, turing often. BBQ and enjoy!
