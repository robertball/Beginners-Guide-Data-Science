Title:
Worcestershire Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/974931.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tsp - Balsamic vinegar
1½ cups - Vegetable  broth (beef or chicken brothwil wor as well)
2 tsp - Molasses, (unsulphured variety)
¼ tsp - Ginger, (ground)
Pinch - Garlic powder
Pinch - White pepper
Salt, to taste (optional)

Directions:
You need to brew all the ingredients together, barring the salt, for about 15 minutes on medium heat.
Once the liquid begins to boil, allow it to simmer steadily until the entire thing reduces by half.
Once you have about a cup of the liquid left, stir in the salt (if you want it at all).
Store it in the fridge for up to seven days.
