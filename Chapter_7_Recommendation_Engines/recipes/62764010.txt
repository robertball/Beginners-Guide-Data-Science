Title:
raspberry cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening (non-hydrogenated)
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 eggs
2 1/4 cups cake flour
1 teaspoon salt
3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1 red raspberry Jell-O packet, regular size (3 oz.)
1/2 10-oz. pkg. frozen red raspberries, thawed (about a cup)

Directions:
preheat the oven to 325Â°F. grease and flour two 9" round cake pans.
mix the ingredients together in the order they're listed - creaming the softened shortening and sugar first, then adding the eggs, flour, salt, baking powder, and finally the milk, sour cream, gelatin, and raspberries. using a stand mixer, beat everything together on low for 30 seconds, then high for 1 1/2 minutes.
pour into the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the tops spring back slightly when pressed. begin to test for done-ness around 30 minutes, to be sure.
let cool on wire racks for at least 15 minutes, loosen around the edges of cake with knife and turn out onto cooling rack.
