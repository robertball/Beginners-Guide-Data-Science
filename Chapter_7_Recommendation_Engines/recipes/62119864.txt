Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cooked and shredded chicken thighs
1 tsp poultry seasoning
2 cans cream of chicken
1 tbsp chicken base
1 medium size can whole potato's
2 tbsp butter
1 tbsp pepper
1 can chicken broth
2 cans refrigerated biscuit

Directions:
Add cream of chicken, shredded chicken, potato's,chicken broth, pepper, poultry seasoning, chicken base and butter to the slow cooker.
Cook on high for about three hours.
Lightly flour a surface and roll out the biscuit dough to 1/4 inch circles.
Using a pizza cutter, cut the dough four times.
Place the dough into the slow cooker mixture.
Repeat until all of the dough has been used.
Cover and cook for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Granny's version with a few twist from me. These are truly southern, with a few shortcuts added by me. Whole potatoes, shredded chicken, and refrigerated biscuit have never tasted so good. My family raves over them. Once you cook these the others won't be able to compare.
