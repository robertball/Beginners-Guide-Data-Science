Title:
Redneck Bhelpuri  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup diced onion
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 lime, juiced
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste
1 cup crispy rice cereal (such as Rice KrispiesÂ®)

Directions:
Combine onion, tomato, olive oil, lime juice, salt, pepper, and cayenne in a bowl. Let sit 3 to 5 minutes. Add crispy rice cereal and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Darren Richmond
Description: 
This is my version of the great Indian snack, but with easy to find (and cheap) ingredients. Try it before you knock it.
