Title:
Strawberry Yogurt Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2387090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 container of Yo plait light strawberry yogurt
2 tablespoons milk
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/2 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup  Truvia (or any natural/stevia based sweetener that pours like sugar)
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
pinch of salt
Chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Grease a square baking dish
Combine all ingredients into a blender and mix until smooth.
Pour into pan and sprinkle on top chocolate chips.
Bake for about 10-15 min, or until a toothpick comes out clean when poked through.
Allow to cool before cutting it into pieces.
