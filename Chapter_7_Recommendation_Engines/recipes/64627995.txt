Title:
Becca's Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chopped broccoli florets (about 2 heads)
3 Tbsp cherry Craisins
3 Tbsp dry-roasted salted sunflower seeds
1/2 cup chopped fresh-cooked bacon (or real bacon bits, not pre-cooked bacon)
1/2 cup plain yogurt (not vanilla)
4 Tbsp orange juice
2 Tbsp mayo
1 1/2 tsp sugar

Directions:
Combine the broccoli, Craisins, sunflower seeds and bacon in a large bowl.
In a smaller bowl, combine the yogurt, orange juice, mayo and sugar.
Pour sauce over the broccoli mixture until well coated. Serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
A delicious broccoli salad with a tangy and sweet sauce. Make ahead of time to let the flavors meld.
