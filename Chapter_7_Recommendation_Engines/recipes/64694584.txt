Title:
Crab Salad Mozzarella Melt Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5223419.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. imitation crab meat, chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onion
1/4 up finely diced  celery
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded mozzarella cheese
1/8 tsp salt (I prefer Kosher salt, but course sea salt gives a bit more bite)
1/8 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 loaf (approx 1 lb) french bread not sliced and halved lengthwise

Directions:
Pre-heat oven 400 degrees
In large mixing bowl, combine  crab, mayonnaise, green onion and celery, shredded mozzarella cheese and salt and pepper.
Spread mixture over bottom half of french bread loaf the top with other half of loaf.
Wrap completely in aluminum foil ( heavy duty).
Place on a baking sheet and bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or until heated through.
Cut into slices and enjoy.
