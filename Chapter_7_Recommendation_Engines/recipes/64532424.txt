Title:
Black Lentil and Vegetable Tagine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3065217.jpg
Ingredients:
250g black lentils (Black lentils make everything black, possibly this is better with brown or green lentils. Soaking/Cooking times will change.)
1/2 zucchine
1 small carrot
1/2 red bell pepper
1 hot pepper (or more to taste) - I used a jalapeño the first time
1 shallot
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/4 smoked paprika
Fresh parsley to taste, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 190°C
Soak lentils in fresh water.
Sauté shallot then add carrot, zucchine, and potato.
Add jalapeño and drained lentils. Sauté some more.
Transfer to  tagine, sprinkle turmeric and paprika over everything. Add 1.5 cups of water.
Cook 45 minutes. Turn off oven and leave until needed.
