Title:
Chicken Pozole Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Chicken Breast
2 tbls. olive oil
1 lg. onions, chopped
1 lg. red peppers, chopped
1 sm. jalapeno pepper, chopped
2 tbls. chili powder
1 tbls. ground cumin
1 tbls. garlic, chopped
1 cup crushed tomatoes,canned works
1 qt. chicken broth
1 28oz cn. hominey
Cilantro, chopped
Scallions, sliced thin
Sour cream

Directions:
Poach chicken in water with a little salt and pepper for about 20 min. and cool. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces and set aside. Heat oil in dutch oven and brown onion, red pepper and jalapeno. When onion start to brown add garlic and cook for about 1 min. then add chicken to pot, sprinkle with cumin and chili powder,stir in tomatoes. Add chicken broth and 1 cup water or you can reserve some poaching liquid and use it. Bring to boil, cover and then simmer for about 1 hour. Add hominy and continue to cook for another 15 min. Serve with the cilantro, green onion and sour cream.
