Title:
Copycat Olive Garden Zuppa Touscana Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773719.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Italian Sausage
4 Cups Water
16oz Chicken Broth
2-3 Large Russet Baking Potatoes (Sliced into 1/4 inch slices)
1 Large Onion, Roughly Chopped
2 Garlic Cloves, Minced
1/4 Cup Bacon Bits
1 Cup Heavy Cream
2 Cups Kale, Chopped

Directions:
Brown 1 lb of Italian sausage on medium-high heat in a large stockpot.
Add 4 cups water, and 16oz chicken broth. Stir to combine.
Stir in sliced potatoes, chopped onion and minced garlic.
Lower the heat to medium, and simmer for about 20-30 minutes, until the potatoes are fully cooked, and the onions have become transparent.
Add 1/4 cup bacon bits, and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 10 more minutes.
Stir in 1 cup heavy cream, and 2 cups chopped kale.
Reduce heat to low and simmer again for 10 minutes, and your soup is warmed throughout.
Serve immediately and freeze/refrigerate leftovers.

Lisa Lou
Description: 
A delicious copy of Olive Garden's famous Zuppa Tousaca creamy, Italian soup!
