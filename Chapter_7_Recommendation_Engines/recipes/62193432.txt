Title:
Peanut Butter Creme Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dark brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 Tbsp butter or margarine
2/3 cup fat free evaporated milk
7 oz jar marshmallow creme
1 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter

Directions:
Line a 9 by 13 baking dish or cake pan with foil and spray lightly with a cooking spray.  Set prepared dish aside.
Combine sugar, milk and butter in a heavy pot and bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally, then allow to boil for 3 minutes.  Working quickly, stir in marshmallow creme till smooth and then add peanut butter and stir till well combined.
Pour into prepared pan and spread out evenly.  Allow to cool completely before cutting.  Lift foil from pan and cut into 10 strips and then 8 pieces per strip.
Store in fridge to keep firm.
