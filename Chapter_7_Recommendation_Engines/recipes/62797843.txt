Title:
LATKES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/709586.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes[yukon gold or russet]
1 small onion
2 eggs
1/3 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp.salt
canola oil

Directions:
Peel potatoes and place in ice water.Grate onion and potatoes.Using a clean tea towel,squeeze all water from the potatoes and onion.Add to bowl with potatoe mixture the eggs,flour,baking powder and salt,mix well.
In heavy frying pan heat oil.Add mixture by 1 heaping Tbsp.Flatten as frying.Cook untill golden[3 min.per side]
