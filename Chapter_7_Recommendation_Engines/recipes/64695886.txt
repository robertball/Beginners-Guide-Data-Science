Title:
Raspberry Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup of stevia
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup apple sauce
3/4 cup cocoa
1 1/4 flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
6oz fresh rasperries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix sugars, eggs, vanilla, melted butter and apple sauce.
Then mix flour cocoa and salt into wet ingredients.
Fold in raspberries.
Pour batter in sprayed 9x13 pan.
Bake for 40-45 minutes
