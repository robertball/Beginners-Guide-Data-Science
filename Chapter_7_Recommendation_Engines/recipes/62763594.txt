Title:
Quick Couscous with Raisins and pine nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup chicken stock
3/4 cup couscous
1 TBS olive oil or butter
3/4 of an onion or small onion chopped
1 or 2 minced garlic cloves
1/4 cup chopped raisins or dried cranberries
1 tsp curry (or so)
1 tsp cumin (or to taste)
3/4 cup toasted pine nuts

Directions:
toast the pine nuts in a little oil and remove from pan.  Add to the pan the onions and garlic and saute.
Cook the couscous in the chicken stock (can add white wine too).
Add to the couscous the other ingredients and some salt or garlic salt to taste.
