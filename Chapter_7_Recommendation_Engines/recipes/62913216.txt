Title:
Grilled Bread Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 t. honey
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. ground black pepper
2 ears of fresh sweet corn, husks and silks removed
3 1/4-inch-thick slices red onion
3 1/2-inch-thick slices Italian bread
4 cups baby arugula
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

Directions:
For dressing, in a screw-top jar combine 1/4 cup of the oil, the vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper.  Cover and shake well; set aside.  Generously brush all sides of corn, onion slices, and bread slices with the remaining 1/4 cup oil.
Place the corn on the rack of medium heated grill.  Grill for 15-20 minutes or until corn is tender, turning occasionally to brown evenly.  Add onion slices tot he grill rack the last 10 minutes of grilling or until browned and tender, turning once.  Add bread slices to the grill rack the last 3 to 4 minutes of grilling or until toasted, turning once.
In a large bowl toss together the arugula and tomatoes.  Cut corn from cobs and add to arugula mixture.  Shake dressing and add to vegetable mixture; toss to combine.
