Title:
Gluten Free Lime Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946586.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups Gluten Free Vanilla Cake Mix (Nutsonline.com)
1 cup Sugar
5 Egg Whites
1 cup butter (softened)
1 tsp Vanilla
2 Lime(s) Juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Whisk egg whites on high speed until fluffy like Meringue.  Slowly add sugar as the egg whites are mixing.  Slow the mixer to medium and add the butter until well incorporated into the egg white mixture.  Add vanilla.  Add Cake mix and lime juice.  Mix until well combined on med/high speed.  Pour mix into cupcake lines using 1/4 cup for measurement. Bake at 350 degrees for 18-20 minutes.  Frost with your favorite cream cheese frosting!

Submitter: 
Kristi Francis
Description: 
Gluten Free cupcakes with simple ingredients that will actually please EVERYONE!  A light lime flavor hides the typical starchy or corn meal flavor associated with most gluten cakes.  The egg whites make them lighter too.
