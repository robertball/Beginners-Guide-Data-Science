Title:
Curry Chickpea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2761182.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can of chick peas
1 medium red pepper chopped into bite sizes
1 medium orange pepper chopped into bite sizes
1 medium yellow pepper chopped into bite sizes
1/4 cup raisins
6 tbsp water
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp (or to your taste) curry powder
1 tsp (or to your taste) honey
Dash salt
Dash pepper

Directions:
Drain and rinse a can of chick peas in a strainer.
In a medium bowl mix water, olive oil honey, curry powder, salt and pepper.
Add chick peas, raisins and chopped peppers to the bowl.
Mix to coat all the ingredients.
Enjoy now or refrigerate and enjoy it later!

Submitter: 
jenniejenjen
Description: 
Quick and easy salad - makes a great lunch option and gets better as it sits and marinates over a couple of days.
