Title:
Greyer & Emmentaller Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Greyer
8 oz. Emmentaler
2 1/2 T. Flour
1 1/2 C. DRY White Wine
1 LG. Glove Garlic
Fresh Ground Black Pepper
2 T. Kirsch

Directions:
Grate Cheeses, and Toss with flour.
Rub pot with Garlic and add the bulb.
Heat pot and Add Wine.
Add Cheeses to warm/hot Wine.
Add Blk. Pepper & Kirsch.
