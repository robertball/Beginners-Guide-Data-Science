Title:
Meat Samosas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large potatoes
1 C peas
2-3 T oil
1 pinch cumin seeds
1-2 bayleaves
2-3 largh onions
500g minced beef
4-5 cloves of garlic crushed
1 T ginger pureed
1/2 t black pepper
1 1/2 t salt
1 t cumin powder
1 t corriander powder
1 t tumeric powder
1 t chilli powder
1/2 t cinnamon powder
1/2 t cardomon powder
2 T chopped fresh corriander
2 T chopped fresh green chillies
2 hard boiled eggs chopped.(optional)

Directions:
1/ Boil potatoes and peas till cook well, mash together and set aside.
2/ Heat oil in pan, add cumin seeds and bayleaves and fry till browned taking care not to burn them. Add the diced onions and and fry till soft and translucent. Add the minced beef and and brown taking care to remove all lumps in the mince. Finally add the spices ( excluding the chopped fresh corriander and chilli).
3/ add the potatoe mixture to the mince mixture and mix well, cool. When cold, add the chopped fresh chilli and chopped fresh corriander and the diced hard boiled egg.
4/ place heaped tablespoonsfull onto the corner of a springroll pastry sheet (or filo, or any kind really) and fold into triangles.
5/ Pan fry in hot oil till golden brown on both sides.

These Samosa's use minced beef. You can also use chicken livers instead of minced beef if you choose. My Bangaladesh friend gave me the recipe, and it's wonderfull and spicy, you just can't buy better in the shops.
