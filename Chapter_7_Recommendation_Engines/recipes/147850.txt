Title:
Kellogg's速 Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983935.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup All-Bran速 Original
1 cup mashed ripe bananas
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup margarine, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Stir together flour, baking powder, soda and salt. Set aside.
In separate mixing bowl, mix together Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal, bananas, eggs and vanilla. Let stand about 5 minutes or until cereal softens.
In large electric mixer bowl, beat together margarine and sugar on medium  speed, until thoroughly combined. Add flour alternately with cereal mixture. Mix well after each addition. Stir in walnuts. Spread evenly in 9x5x3-inch loaf pan coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 350 degrees F about 45 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Remove from pan and cool on wire rack. Serve warm or cold. Store in airtight container.
