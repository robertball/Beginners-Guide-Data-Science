Title:
Lime Raspberry Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	Juice of one Lime
•	3 egg yolks
•	¼ cup sugar
•	½ cup flour
•	1 ¼ tsp. vanilla
•	½ tsp. raspberry extract (optional)
•	1 tsp. soda
•	1 tsp. powder
•	½ cup butter
•	¼ tsp. salt
Icing directions and ingredients:
•	¼ tsp. vanilla or lime
•	3 egg whites, at room temperature
•	4 cups powdered sugar
•	½ tsp. cream of tartar
•	food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Cookie Directions
Mix egg, flour, vanilla, and sugar. Add in lime, soda, powder, and salt. Beat for 2-3 minutes. Stir in butter and raspberry extract, if desired. Mix well. Chill for 1-2 hours. Roll into 1-inch balls and place on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 for 4 minutes. Let cool for 2 minutes. Then ENJOY!
Icing Directions:
Place the egg whites, powdered sugar, and cream of tartar.
•	Mix until very thick, shiny, stiff and white. This should take about 7-10 minutes.
•	Add a little more powdered sugar for stiffer icing
•	If you want to dye your icing just one color, you can add a few drops of food coloring into the bowl and mix it for a few seconds until the coloring is evenly distributed.
