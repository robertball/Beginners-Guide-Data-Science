Title:
Coconut Oil Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074797.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup coconut oil
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 1/4 cup white sugar
3 egg yolks
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Lightly grease baking sheet or parchment lined baking sheet.
Cream together sugar, butter, and coconut oil.
Mix in egg yolks and vanilla.
Gradually add flour, baking soda and baking powder and mix until blended.
Mix dough together with hands at the end to incorporate ingredients (no mixer).
Roll dough into 1 1/2" balls and place on baking sheet 2" apart.
Bake 10-12 minutes until cookie tops are cracked.

Submitter: 
Kristonia
Description: 
This is a variation on the sugar cookie using coconut oil. It tastes similar to a shortbread cookie, but they are very moist and delicious!
