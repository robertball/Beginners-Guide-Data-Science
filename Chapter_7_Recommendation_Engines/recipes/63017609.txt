Title:
Pumpkin Pie Spice Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese (softened)
1 cup confectioners' sugar
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
8 oz whipped topping, thawed
16 oz gingerbread man cookies

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese, confectioners' sugar and pumpkin pie spice. Beat in whipped topping until blended. Refrigerate until serving.
