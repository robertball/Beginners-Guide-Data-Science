Title:
Jude's farmer's hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cook sweet potatoes,baked  potatoes,1 red oion,1 red pepper,spinach,fresh leafy..cinnamon,salt,pepper to taste. use approx.4 large sweetpotatoes,and 4 large bake potatoes!!

Directions:
put olive oil in bottom of a wok size pan,or deep skillet.add diced oion,diced red pepper,add leafy spinach.stir put a lid on it. when sinach is cooked add potatoes chopped.you can leave the skin on the baked pototoes. stir it all up,add little hint of cinnamon and salt an pepper to taste! put lid on and let simmer until hot.

Description: 
this is so easy to make,and it's and alternative to sweet potatoes. I like to make it when, I have left over bake potatoes an sweet potatoes!I first tasted it at a very popular deli,zingerman's. so,because it's a hash,you add the ingedients in whatever amount,suits your taste!
