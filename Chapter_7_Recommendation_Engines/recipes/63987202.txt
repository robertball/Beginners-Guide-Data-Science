Title:
Tomato-Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106371.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
2 small  cucumbers, thinly sliced
1 small  red onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup  Italian dressing (bottled or homemade)
4 oz. feta cheese
1/2 cup chopped black olives

Directions:
Mix vegetables on bowl.
Drizzle with dressing.
Add crumbled feta cheese.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A perfect way to use the bounty of the garden, it super fast and a great side salad.  The tastes improve if allowed to sit overnight.
