Title:
Yummy Honey Lemon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookies:
1 stick margarine (room temp)
1 stick butter (room temp)
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup honey
1 tbs lemon zest
3 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon cloves
2 tablespoons milk
Icing:
juice of 1 lemon
1-1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 egg white, beat til foamy

Directions:
Cookies:
Pre-heat oven to 350
Cream margarine, butter, sugars, milk, eggs, lemon zest and honey.
Sift together flour, baking soda and spices.
Add flour mixture to the other mixture using a wooden spoon.
Chill dough overnight.
Make 1 inch balls and bake at 350 degrees for 11 to 12 minutes.
*Icing:
Whisk together lemon juice and egg white until foamy. Add powdered sugar. Chill until cookies are cooled then spoon on as desired.

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
My mom used to make these cookies for us every winter. 

*Instead of using the icing you can just drizzle some honey on the tops once they are cooled.
