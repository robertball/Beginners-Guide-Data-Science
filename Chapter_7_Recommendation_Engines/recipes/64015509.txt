Title:
Goin' fishing chex mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. butter
9 c Corn Chex, Rice Chex
2 c. pretzel gold fish
2 c. cheese gold fish
1 pks ranch dressing mix
1/2 c. grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. In large bowl microwave butter until melted, stir in cereal and crackers until evenly coated, stir in dressing mix and cheese until evenly coated.
2.  Microwave on high 3 min. stir after every mi. Spread on wax paper to cool.
