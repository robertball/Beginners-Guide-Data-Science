Title:
Mom's Christmas Cut-Outs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C butter (or 1/2 C butter & 1/2 C shortening)
2 C sugar
4 eggs
1 t vanilla
6 C flour
1 t baking soda

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs one at a time, mixing after each egg.
Sift dry ingredients in a separate bowl.  Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture, mix until blended.
Roll dough 1/8" thick & cut.
Bake in a 375 degree oven for 8-10 minutes or until edges turn light brown.
Makes 5 dozen.

Submitter: 
munchmn
Description: 
Easy to work with dough, tastes great too!  A family favorite for years!!
