Title:
Chrissy's Easy Make Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 cup cooked brown rice
1 egg white
1 tb chopped parsley
1/4 ts garlic powder
1/4 ts salt
Filling:
1 cup sliced red onion
10 oz chopped spinach
1 ts margarine
5 egg whites
1/3 cup skim milk
2 tb flour
1/2 cup parmesan cheese or cheddar
1 tb dried italian seasoning

Directions:
Crust: In a medium mixing bowl, combine the crust ingredients. Mix Well. Press the mixture over the bottom and up the sides of a 9 inch pie plate. Microwave at high power until the cust is set, about 1 to 2 minutes, rotationg plate twice. Set aside.
Filling: In a 2 quart casserole dish, combine the vegetables and margarine. cover. microwave at high power until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 4 to 6 minutes, stirring once or twice. Set aside.
In a small mixing bowl: combine the remaining ingredients. mix together the egg whites mixture and the vegetable mixture. Microwave the egge whites and the vegetable mixture at medium power, uncovered, until the mixture just begins to set around the edges, stirring every 2 minutes, for a total of 4 to 6 minutes.
Pour the egg whites and veg mixture into the prepared crust. Microwave at medium power until set in the center, about 10 to 12 minutes, rotationg the pie plate 3 or 4 times during cooking. Let sit on your counter 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Chrissy Mc
Description: 
This is a quick recipe that I love to make in the summer. It gets us away from the ordinary grill foods and salads. This is all done in the microwave, so it won't heat up the house.
