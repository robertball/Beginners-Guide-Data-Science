Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436141.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg Cream Cheese
1 can Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 (3.4) pkg Instant Vanilla Pudding
3 c Milk
1 t Vanilla
1 cont Whipped Cream
4 Bananas
3/4 (12 oz) pkg Vanilla Wafer Cookies

Directions:
SOFTEN cream cheese in microwave or just leave out for ahead of time.
WHIP cream cheese until fluffy.
BEAT in condensed milk, pudding mix, milk, vanilla and 1 banana until smooth.
FOLD in half of the whipped cream.
LINE the bottom of a 9"x13" glass dish with cookies.
ARRANGE remaining 3 sliced bananas evenly over cookies.
SPREAD pudding mixture.
TOP with remaining half of whipped cream.
CRUNCH up 5 or 6 cookies and sprinkle over the top.
CHILL & serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is way creamier and tastier than your run-of-the-millBanana Pudding that is simply made with just bananas, vanilla pudding and vanilla wafers.  You must try.  You'll never go back!
