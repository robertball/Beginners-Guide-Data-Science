Title:
Chicken and Spinach Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  12oz box of jumbo shells cooked per directions
1  10oz frozen chopped spinach drained well
2c chopped cooked rotisserie chicken
1 med onion chopped fine
2 cloves of garlic chopped fine
1  15oz whole milk ricotta
5oz of cream cheese
4oz parmesan cheese
1 egg
1/4 cup cleaned packed frozen parsley from the garden
1tsp of Italian seasoning
1/2tsp of salt
1tsp of black pepper
1c mozzarella cheese shredded
1qt of marinara sauce
1/4c of mozzarella cheese shredded

Directions:
Wait to cook the pasta until the filling is made.
Chop and mix onion, garlic, spinach, egg, spices and herbs, cream cheese, parmesan cheese, and salt in a food processor.
Put chopped mixture in a bowl and mix in mozzarella and ricotta.
Cook shells and cool with running water.  Remove from the pot by hand.
In a large 13x16 baking dish put half of the sauce on the bottom.
Put filling in a bag and pipe into shells one at a time.
Place in baking dish filling side up.
Put the remainder of the sauce on top.
Bake 50 to 60 minutes at 350F.
Sprinkle the 1/4c of cheese on top and let rest 20 minutes before serving.
