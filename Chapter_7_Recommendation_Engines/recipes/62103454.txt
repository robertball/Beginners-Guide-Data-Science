Title:
Salmon Loaf Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonaisse
1 can cream of celery soup
1 egg beaten
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped grean pepper
1 T. Lemon juice
1 can salmon flaked
1 cup fine bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350f. Mix all ingredients together and bake in a greased loaf pan for one hour.
