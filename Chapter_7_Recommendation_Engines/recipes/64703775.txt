Title:
Corn and Black Bean Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5430713.jpg
Ingredients:
15 oz can Busch’s Black Beans, rinsed and drained
19 oz bag Bird’s Eye Super Sweet Corn
3-10 oz cans Rotel Diced Tomatoes with Green Chilies, one drained
1 cup red onion, chopped
1 cup green bell pepper, chopped
1 envelope Great Seasonings Garlic and Herb dressing mix
1/4 cup Apple cider vinegar with the Mother

Directions:
Chop the red onion and bell pepper, set aside.
Thaw the corn, set aside.
In a bowl add the drained and rinsed Black Beans, two whole cans of the Rotel and the one can that has been drained.
Add in the Good Seasonings dressing mix, the corn, the chopped peppers and onions, and the quarter cup of vinegar. Mix thoroughly and taste.
If desired, salt and pepper or more vinegar can be added to taste.
Refrigerate overnight for best marriage of flavors but can be served instantly.

Submitter: 
Miss B
Description: 
A quick and delicious homemade salsa that goes well with chips, on sandwiches, or over your favorite protein!
