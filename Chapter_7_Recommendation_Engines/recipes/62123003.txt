Title:
Greek Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
5 cloves of garlic, chopped
5 stalks of celery, chopped
5 carrots, chopped
2 bay leaves
olive oil to taste
salt and pepper to taste
1 package of dried lentils
vinegar to taste

Directions:
Cover all the vegetables with water in a stock pot and bring to a boil.
Add oil, bay leaves, and salt and pepper.
Reduce heat and cook the vegetables for 15 minutes.
Add lentils and cook until tender.
Add vinegar.
Cool slightly and serve.

Description: 
Greek style Lentil soup, popular during fasting times.
