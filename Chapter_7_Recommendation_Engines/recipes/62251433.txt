Title:
Divinity Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DIVINITY FUDGE
2 1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. white Karo corn syrup
1/2 c. water
Whites of 2 eggs
1 c. cut pecans
1 tsp. almond extract

Directions:
Mix sugar, syrup and water and boil until it forms a firm ball. Beat egg whites until stiff. Gradually pour the boiling syrup into the stiffly beaten egg whites. Add almond extract (1 teaspoon).
When candy begins to get dull, add finely chopped pecans or candied cherries. Beat until dull. Pour on buttered cookie sheet or drop by spoonful on buttered cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
Anne Olson
Description: 
this light, white candy is not a true fudge but just as sweet and popular a treat
