Title:
Clean Out the Pantry Hamburger Soup!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/728885.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 lb ground beef or turkey
1 14.5 oz can green beans
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 16 oz can butter beans
1 15 oz can sliced potatoes
1 14.5 oz can sliced carrots
1 15.25 oz can whole kernel corn
1 bay leaf
3 cloves garlic, minced
1.5 tsp garlic powder
1.5 tsp onion powder
3 cubes beef bullion
3/4 cup red wine
pinch of sugar
water
black pepper to taste

Directions:
in large pan over medium high heat, cook beef or turkey until no longer pink--drain off fat.  Combine potatoes, carrots, corn, butter beans and green beans in colander.  Drain and rinse well.  Place green bean mixture in crock pot; add cooked meat, onion, celery, bay leaf, and garlic--stir to combine.  Add pepper, garlic powder, sugar, bullion, onion powder, red wine and tomato sauce.  Fill tomato sauce can with water twice and add to crock pot.  Cover and let cook on low for 8-10 hours.  Remove bay leaf, adjust seasonings if necessary and serve.

Submitter: 
Arizona Desert Flower
Description: 
I came up with this recipe when I was unemployed and we didn't have a lot of money to spend on groceries.  Adjust to what you have on hand, it's easy, tasty, and fills the belly!  I had to guess-timate on the seasonings, I always throw things in to taste!!!
