Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon fresh oregano,chopped
1 teaspoon fresh basil,chopped
1 teaspoon onion salt
2-3 cups (500 -750 ml) beef broth
1 teaspoon chopped parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 bay leaf
2/3 oz (20 g) dried Italian salad dressing mix
5 pounds  beef chuck roast
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan combine all ingredients, except meat. Stir well and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Remove from the heat and set aside.
Place beef chuck roast in slow cooker. Pour mixture over meat. Cover slow cooker and cook on low-heat setting for 4-5 hours or until beef is cooked through.
Discard the bay leaf. Using a tongs,remove cooked meat from slow cooker into a large bowl or cutting board and shred using a two forks.
Return shredded meat to slow cooker and cook for a further 1 hour. Remove from slow cooker and serve on Italian bread with pickled cucumbers.

Submitter:
