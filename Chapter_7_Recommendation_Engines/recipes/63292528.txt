Title:
Honey Ginger Plum Sundae Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red or purple plums, pitted and chopped
2 tbsp orange juice
1 tbsp honey
1 tsp fresh ginger, minced
1 tsp ground cardamom
vanilla frozen yogurt, ice cream, or greek yogurt
2 Tbsp chopped almonds or pistachios

Directions:
Combine plums, orange juice, honey, ginger, and cardamom until mixed but chunky and serve over yogurt or ice cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
Plum/pstachio or almond topping for Greek yogurt or ice cream!
