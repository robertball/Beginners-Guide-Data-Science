Title:
Snicker's Santa Surprise Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. tube of refrigerated peanut butter cookie dough
18 Snickers Brand Minis, unwrapped
1 16 oz. container vanilla frosting
Red and green food color

Directions:
1. Divide the dough into 1 Tbsp. pieces and flatten.
2. Place Snickers Brand Minis in the center of each piece of dough.
3. Form the dough into a ball around each Snickers Brand Mini.
4. Place on non-stick cookie sheet and bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 10-12 minutes, until golden brown.
5. Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 3 minutes and then move them to a baking rack or waxed paper to cool completely.
6. Divide the frosting into two bowls. Tint one bowl green and the other bowl red. Place the frosting into separate re-sealable bags. Snip off a corner from each bag and drizzle the cookies with frosting.
