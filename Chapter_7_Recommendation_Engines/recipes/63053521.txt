Title:
Green Chile Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb to 1-1/2 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
1/2 onion, rough chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, rough chopped
1 bay leaf
1 clove garlic, rough chopped
1/2 tsp parsley flakes
1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1 chicken bouillon cube
pepper to taste
8 corn tortillas
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 can diced green chiles
1/4 cup mayo
12 oz sour cream
1/4 onion, finely chopped
1/4 green bell pepper, finely chopped
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp chili powder
dash or two of tabasco sauce or siracha
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese, divided into 4 parts
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Poach the chicken with the 1/2 onion and green bell pepper, garlic, parsley, cumin, chili powder, chicken bouillon and pepper. Cover with water. Simmer for 1 hr.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix the soup, green chiles, mayo, sour cream, onion, green bell pepper, garlic powder, cumin, chili powder and tabasco sauce. Ladle in about 1 1/2 cups of cooking liquid from poaching chicken. Mix well.
Spray 7x11 casserole dish with cooking spray. Spread some of the soup on the bottom of the pan. Place two tortillas in the dish.
Shred chicken and mix into soup mix. Start layering chicken mixture on tortillas and top with 2 oz of cheese. Continue layering.
Place on sheet tray to catch any overflow. Bake for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
