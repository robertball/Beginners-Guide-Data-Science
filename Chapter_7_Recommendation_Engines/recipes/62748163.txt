Title:
Onion Flat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small onions, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
garlic powder to taste
1 c. warm water
1 tbsp. cooking oil, your choice
3 c. whole wheat flour

Directions:
Saute onions, celery and garlic powder until tender. Add warm water and oil to mixture and stir in flour and let stand 10 mins. Divide dough into 12 balls and roll those into 14" circles. Cook in a non-stick griddle until browned on both sides.
