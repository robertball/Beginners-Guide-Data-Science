Title:
4 B's Cream of Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz. Diced Tomatoes, canned
1 oz. Butter
1 TB Onion, chopped
9 oz. Chicken Broth (undiluted)
2 TB Sugar
Pinch of Baking Soda
2 c. Cream (Heavy)

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingredients and simmer for 1 hour. Heat cream in double broiler and add to hot tomato mixture. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
The Native Cowgirl
Description: 
*This soup is served at the 4 B's Restaurant chain located mostly in Montana. My grandmother obtained a copy of this recipe (at one time, they handed them out). I decided to share it because it is simply the best cream of tomato soup I have ever eaten!
