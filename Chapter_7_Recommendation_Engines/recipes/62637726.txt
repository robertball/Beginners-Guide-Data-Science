Title:
Midnight Madness chocolate cake for one Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580610.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons unsweetened dutch cocoa (Hershey's)
2 tbsp tapicoa flour
6 tablespoons Ideal Brown sugar substitute
1 egg white
1 tbsp plain nonfat yogurt
2 tbsp non-fat dry milk
2 tbsp hot brewed coffee
3 tablespoons sugar-free chocolate syrup
2 mini bars of sugar-free hershey's chocolate (or 1 tbsp of sugar free chocolate chips, if using bars, break into pieces)
1/4 cup torani/da'vinci sugar-free chocolate flavor syrup

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in mug throughly.
Add wet ingredients and blend throughly.
Add chocolate pieces, gently fold in.
Top (swirl-like motion) with syrup-Be generous!.
Microwave on high for 5 minutes.
Cool.
Enjoy a fudgy pudding like dessert! Yummy!
