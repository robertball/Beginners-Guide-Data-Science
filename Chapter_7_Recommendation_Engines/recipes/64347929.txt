Title:
Sweet & Sour Sauce #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup pineapple juice
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp cooking wine
2 Tbsp cornstarch

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a small saucepan and bring to a boil until thickened.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description:
