Title:
Chocolate Cream Cheese  Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 square (1oz) unsweetened chocolate, melted
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 cup chooped pecans
Second Layer:
6 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
2 tbsp. all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 cup chopped pecans
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
3 cups miniature marshmallows
Top Layer:
1/4 cup butter
2 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 square (1oz.) unsweetened chocolate
2 tbsp. milk
3 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
Add eggs, chocolate and vanilla adn mix well
Combine flour and baking powder then stir into chocolate mixture.
Fold in chopped pecans then pour all into greased 9x13 pan.
2nd layer
In another bowl combine cream cheese and butter
Beat in sugar, egg, vanilla and flour and mix well
Fold in pecans and spread over 1st layer, then sprinkle with chocolate chips
Bake at 350' for about 20-25 minutes or until edges pull away from pan
Then remove from oven and sprinkle with marshmallows and bake 1-2 minutes longer or until they puff.
Spread marshmallows evenly over all if they don't spread themselves nicely
Cool on wire rack.
Top layer:
In a heavy sauce pan combine butter, 2 oz. of cream cheese, chocolate square and milk
Cook over low heat stirring constantly until smooth
Transfer to a mixing bowl and add powdered sugar and vanilla, beat until smooth
Spread over bars (when cooled)
Store in refrigerator
