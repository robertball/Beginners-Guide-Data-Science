Title:
Vegetarian Borsht Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter/olive oil
1 lb onion, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves
1 carrot, thinly sliced
6 cups cold water
3 med beets (3/4 lb) peeled & finely chopped
½ lb white cabbage, shredded
1 lg potato, peeled & finely chopped
1 small tart apple, peeled & finely chopped
1 14 oz can of tomatoes, inc. liquid
1 tbsp tomato paste
5 black peppercorns
2 allspice berries
1 bay leaf
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp minced parsley
1 tbsp fresh snipped dill
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp sugar
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat butter or olive oil in large stockpot.  Stir in onions, garlic & carrots & cook over low heat until soft, about 12 – 15 min.  Add cold water, stir in beets, potato, cabbage, apple, tomatoes, tomato paste, peppercorns, allspice, bay leaf & salt.  Bring to a boil.  Simmer, covered for 2 hrs, until veggies are tender.
Stir in parsley & dill. Season with lemon juice, sugar, & black pepper to taste.  Serve with dollop of sour cream.

Description: 
Hearty, complex and delicious, especially good for freezing and serving later.  Modified from Darra Goldstein's recipie in 'Winter Vegetarian.
