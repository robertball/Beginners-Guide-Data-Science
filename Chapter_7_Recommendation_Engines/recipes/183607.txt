Title:
Salmon Teriyaki Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/197806.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds salmon fillet
2 cups Newman's Own® Teriyaki Marinade

Directions:
Rinse and cut fillet into four pieces.
Soak in Newman's Own Teriyaki Marinade for about an hour (in a covered pan in fridge).
Cook on grill or place in preheated oven at 400 degrees. Depending on size of fillets, should cook in about 7 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Newman's Own®
Description: 
Prepared sweet and tangy Teriyaki marinade makes this delicious main dish an easy recipe any night of the week. Enjoy with steamed rice and vegetables for a complete Asian-inspired meal.
