Title:
Wilton Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup shortening
1/2 Cup butter
1 tsp. clear vanilla
4 cups powdered sugar
2 TBsp milk

Directions:
Mix together and add food coloring. You can freeze it and it will last up to 6 weeks.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Put this frosting on the cake before the fondant icing.
