Title:
Stewed Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh Shrimp
1 slice bacon (for bacon fat)
salt (to taste)
pepper (to taste)
1 - 2 Tbsp Flour
Kitchen Bouquet (to darken)

Directions:
Peal and de-vein Shrimp.
In skillet fry Bacon till crisp.
Set aside.
Add in shrimp and cook till done.
Add in flour, salt & pepper.
Stir till combined.
Add in Kitchen Bouquet.
Simmer till gravy is slightly thick.

Submitter: 
Dawn Raegene Mendenhall Scott
Description: 
Shrimp in a gravy that you put over steaming hot rice.
