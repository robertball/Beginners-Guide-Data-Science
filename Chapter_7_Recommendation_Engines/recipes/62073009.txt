Title:
Crunchy Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable oil, for frying
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper
1 12 oz. beer (Tecate or Corona)
1 cup buttermilk
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken thighs
1 lb. skinless chicken breast tenderloins

Directions:
Soak chicken in beer for 2 hours, then pat dry.
Mix flour with paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper.Set out 3 pie tins divide the seasoned flour in 2 of the tins and the buttermilk in the other one. Arrange tins in a line, flour, buttermilk and then flour.
Heat 1 1/2 inches vegetable oil in a deep skillet over medium high heat. Oil should be wavy but not smoking, when it is ready. Make sure the oil is very HOT or you will have soggy chicken.
Coat chicken pieces in flour then in buttermilk, then a second coating in flour.
Cook chicken appx. 6 min. on each side, until a deep golden brown and firm. Drain on paper bags, serve immediatly or cool before packing for picnic.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
Soaked in beer, dredged in flour dipped in buttermilk and dredged in flour again, makes this the most moist, crunchy fried chicken ever.
