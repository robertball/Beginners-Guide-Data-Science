Title:
Teriyaki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8	chicken drumsticks or thighs, skinless, bone in (about 1 ¾ pounds)
1	can (8 oz.) sliced water chestnuts, drained
¼	cup teriyaki sauce
¼	cup orange marmalade
1	teaspoon ground ginger
2 	cloves garlic, finely chopped
1	cup snow (Chinese) pea pods
¼	cup low sodium teriyaki sauce or bottled teriyaki sauce
3	tablespoons cornstarch
4	cups hot cooked rice
Sliced green onions, for garnish
Low Sodium Teriyaki Sauce
1	tablespoon low sodium soy sauce
1/8	cup water
2 	teaspoons Mirin
1 ½	teaspoons brown sugar
2	teaspoons granulated sugar
1/3	teaspoon minced garlic
1/3	teaspoon minced ginger

Directions:
Place chicken and water chestnuts in 2 ½ to 3 ½ quart slow cooker.  Mix ¼ cup teriyaki sauce, the marmalade, ginger and garlic in small bowl; pour over chicken.
Cover and cook on low heat setting 7 to 8 hours.
Remove chicken from cooker; keep warm.  Stir pea pods into cooker.  Mix ¼ cup teriyaki sauce and the cornstarch; stir into cooker.  Increase heat setting to high.  Cover and cook 20 to 30 minutes or until pea pods are crisp tender.  Serve chicken and pea pods over rice; sprinkle with onions.
If making low sodium Teriyaki sauce, combine all Teriyaki ingredients in a small saucepan and heat over medium low heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. At this point you can put it in the refrigerator for later or use immediately.
