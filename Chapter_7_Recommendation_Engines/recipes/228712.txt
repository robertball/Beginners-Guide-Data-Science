Title:
Loaded Bacon Potato Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1101728.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (13.8 ounce) package Pillsbury® refrigerated classic pizza crust
1 (11.8 ounce) bag Green Giant® Steamers™ frozen backyard grilled potatoes
1 (9 ounce) pouch Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy three cheese cooking sauce
1 (2.5 ounce) package cooked real bacon pieces or bits
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
Garnishes, if Desired:
Diced seeded tomatoes (optional)
Sliced green onions (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Spray large cookie sheet with cooking spray. Unroll pizza dough on cookie sheet; press into 15x12-inch rectangle. Bake 6 to 8 minutes or until light golden brown.
Meanwhile, microwave frozen potatoes 3 to 4 minutes to thaw. In medium bowl, using fork, mash potatoes, leaving some small pieces.  Stir in 1/2 cup cooking sauce. Spread remaining cooking sauce evenly over partially baked crust. Spoon potato mixture over sauce. Sprinkle with bacon and cheese. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
Sprinkle with tomatoes and green onions.

Baked Pillsbury® pizza crust topped with potatoes and bacon gives you a cheesy dinner that's ready in 20 minutes--made using Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy three cheese cooking sauce.
