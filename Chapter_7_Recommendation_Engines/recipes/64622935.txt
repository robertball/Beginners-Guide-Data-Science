Title:
Scallop Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Sea Scallops
1 onion chopped
3 T butter
2 cups heavy cream
2 cups whole milk
1 lb. fingerling creamer potatoes cut into bite sized pieces (or equal volume potato to scallop ratio)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Rinse scallops and cut them into 1 inch pieces. (cutting them in half is fine)
Saute chopped onion in butter.  Saute the scallops in the butter and onion mixture.  Add salt and pepper.  Use medium low heat to not overcook the scallops.  In a sauce pan,  warm the heavy cream and milk mixture.  Add the onion, butter, scallops mixture to the warmed cream and milk mixture.  Bring to a boil.  Add the cut fingerling potatoes and cover.  Low simmer until the potatoes are just cooked.  Salt and pepper to taste!
