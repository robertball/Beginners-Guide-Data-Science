Title:
Sweet Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups mashed sweet potatoes
1/2 c butter (softened)
3 eggs
1 t vanilla
1 T lemon juice
1 c sugar
1 t cinnamon
1/2 t salt
1/2 c pecans, chopped
1/2 cup evaporated milk
mini marshmallows

Directions:
Combine sweet potatoes & butter.  Stir well.  Add eggs, vanilla, lemon juice, evaporated milk, cinnamon & salt.  Stir well.  Stir in pecans.
Bake 20 minutes @ 350F or until heated through.
Top with mini marshmallows, bake until brown & puffed
