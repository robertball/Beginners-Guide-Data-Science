Title:
Shorba Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken or 1-2 pounds of lamb or beef cubed
1 cup of tomato puree
1 large onion
2 bay leaves
2 cardamon pods
2 pieces of mistika(found in any arabic halaal store)they are very small, look like clear or yellowish rocks.
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. wash your meat or chicken
2. In a large pot, fill with water and allow to boil.
3.  Add chicken or meat and allow to cook for at least 10-15 minutes as you skim the scum off the surface of the water.
4. add onion whole, tomato puree, and all the spices.
5.  allow soup to cook until meat is tender.
if water gets low, add more, but remember to spice it up again with more salt and pepper.  no need to add more cardamon or mistika.

Submitter: 
Felicia Manocchio
Description: 
This is the basic egyptian soup.  A traditional egyptian meal consists of meat or chicken, soup, rice, and a vegetable.  And this is the base soup for almost every egyptian recipe.  A RAMADAN winner.
