Title:
Italian Spaghetti Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1 pound lean ground beef
1/2 pound ground pork
1/2 cup crushed crackers
1 tablespoon dried parsley
3 Tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 egg, beaten
a little milk
SAUCE
3/4 cup chopped onion
5 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
2 (28 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
1 teaspoons salt
1 bay leaf
3/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine ground beef,pork,  crackers, parsley, Parmesan, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, garlic powder and beaten egg and a splash of milk.  Mix well and form into 18 balls.  Store, covered, in refrigerator until needed.
In a medium skillet saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent. Add to a large crockpot  Stir in tomatoes, salt, and bay leaf, 1/2 teaspoon pepper. cook on low for 6-8 hours. (I didn't need to add any water or tomato paste)
Cook meatballs on a rimmed cookie sheet on 375 for 20-30 min untill cooked through. Drain grease and add meatballs to crockpot for the last hour or so.
