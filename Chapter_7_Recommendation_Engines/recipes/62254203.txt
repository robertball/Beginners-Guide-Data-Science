Title:
Slow Cooker Wedding Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/334731.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 large carrots, chopped (or 1 can, sliced carrots, halved or quartered)
2 32oz. boxes chicken broth
1 14oz. can chicken broth
1/2-3/4 pound escarole, washed and cut into bite-size pieces
1 pound lean ground beef or ground turkey
1 small onion, minced
2 large eggs
1/2 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In slow cooker, combine the carrots, chicken broth and escarole.
Cover and cook on LOW 4 hours.
In a bowl, combine the ground beef or turkey, onion, eggs, breadcrumbs, cheese, salt, pepper and garlic powder; Mix well.
Shape into 1 inch meatballs and place on waxed paper lined cookie sheet.
Refrigerate at leat 1 hour to firm up. (I usually do this the night before).
After 4 hours on low, remove lid and carefully place meatballs into the soup.
Cover and cook on HIGH for an additional 4 hours, until all is tender and cooked through,
Serve with additional parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This soup freezes beautifully! My dad grows swiss chard in the summertime and we cook it and freeze it for later use.  If your greens are precooked, add them to the soup when you add the meatballs. This would be great with any greens you like. Enjoy!
