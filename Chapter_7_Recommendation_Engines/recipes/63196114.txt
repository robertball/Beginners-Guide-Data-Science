Title:
Bubblegum Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 scoops sugar free, low fat vanilla ice cream
1 cup milk
6 ice cubes
2 T Sugar Free Vanilla Instant Pudding Mix
2 Sweetener packets
1/16 t duncan hines frosting creations bubblegum flavoring powder

Directions:
Combine all in blender and mix until smooth and creamy.  Yum!!
