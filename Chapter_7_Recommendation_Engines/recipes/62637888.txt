Title:
High Fibre Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plain youghurt
1/2 cup wheat bran
1 banana, broken into chunks
1 cup frozen blueberries
1 cup frozen raspberries
1 orange, peeled cut into cubes
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp honey

Directions:
In a blender, combine yoghurt, wheat bran, banana and fruit. Add honey if desired. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
