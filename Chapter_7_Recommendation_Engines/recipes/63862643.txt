Title:
chicken satay Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
chicken breast tenders.
skewers
Brine
1/2 gallon of water
1/2 cup kosher salt
1/2 cup light brown sugar (dark will work if it's all you have)
Rub
1/4 cup of Hellman's REAL mayonnaise
'to die for' Smoking bone dust rub
Dip
Thai chili sauce

Directions:
Brine for 2 hours
Remove from brine and wash
Soak skewers
Mix mayo and rub together
apply to tenders  about 2 hours before smoker
Heat up smoker to 235 using hickory chips
Skewer tenders and Rack
Put in smoker when smoke starts
Takes maybe 30 minutes to reach 160f

Submitter: 
George. P. Cooper
Description: 
favourite appetizer or main course. You can slice chicken breast to give you more chicken, but the tender is best
