Title:
Fruit Crisp Breakfast Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1046387.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp coconut oil or butter
4 cups sliced fruit (apples, peaches, plums, or nectarines, peel fruit if desired)
1/2 cup honey
1/2 tsp cinnamon
3 cups oats
1 cup sliced almonds
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup coconut oil or butter
Apple juice (optional)

Directions:
Melt coconut oil in a large skillet.  Add sliced fruit and saut√© on medium high heat until crisp tender, stirring frequently. Add honey and cinnamon and stir until incorporated.
While fruit is cooking; stir together oats, almonds, cinnamon and salt.  Mix in coconut oil until mixture is crumbly.
Drop oat mixture over fruit mixture and gently stir together.  Lower heat to medium and cook covered until oats are tender, stirring occasionally.  (Add apple juice if oats are not yet complete and mixture begins to dry out.)
Remove from heat and serve.

Submitter: 
KESSIANNE
Description: 
A skillet version of classic fruit crisp, healthy enough for breakfast!  We love to eat this for breakfast in the summer and fall when fruit is abundant.  It is a nice alternative to oatmeal if you want to avoid milk.
