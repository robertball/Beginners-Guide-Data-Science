Title:
Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Whole herb-roasted chicken-thinly shredded or the equivalent
2 - 16oz pkg. low fat cream cheese
1 C Ranch dressing (can use low fat version)
1/2 C shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 C hot sauce (use less)

Directions:
Heat until all ingredients are melted and blended together.
Serve with any type of scoop chips-Doritos are best.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Barb got this recipe from a co-worker.  She used canned mexican spiced chicken and it was very good. She said someone brings it to work whenever the need occurs.  All love it and Barb's dish was quickly devoured.
