Title:
Chocolate Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
3/4 cup icing sugar
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
few grains salt
1/3 cup Fry's cocoa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300
Cream butter in mixing bowl
Stir in flour, cocoa , salt and icing sugar.
Chill if necessary
Shape into 1 inch balls
Place on an un-greased  cookie sheet about 2 inches apart
Flatten with a fork

Submitter:
