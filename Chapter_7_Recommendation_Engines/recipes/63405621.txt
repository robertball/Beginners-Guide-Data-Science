Title:
Alfredo Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of par boiled broccoli florets
1 Tablespoon butter
2 Tablespoons cream cheese
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt (or to taste)
1/3 parmesan cheese

Directions:
Put cream cheese and butter in a  medium skillet and heat until melting. Put the rest of the ingredients in the butter mixture and stir until hot and blended. Do not over cook.
