Title:
Gypsy Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs olive oil
2 cups chopped onion
2 cloves crushed garlic
2 cups chopped, peeled sweet potatoes
1/2 cup celery
1 cup chopped tomatoes
1 can chickpeas
3/4 cup chopped sweet peppers
3 cups water
2 tsp paprika
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp basil
1 tsp salt
dash of cinnamon
dash of cayenne

Directions:
In a large saucepan saute onions, garlic, celery and sweet potatoes in olive oil for about 5 minutes. Add seasonings and water. Simmer covered for 15 minutes. Add remaining vegetables and chickpeas. Simmer another 10 minutes until vegetables are tender.
