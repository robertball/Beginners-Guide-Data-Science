Title:
Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 large carrots
4 stalks celery
1 large red pepper
1 large onion
5 cloves garlic
1/3 cup chili powder
2 T cumin
1 T paprika
1/2 cup Frank's hot sauce
2 15 oz cans tomato sauce
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can kidney beans, drained
1 15 oz can chili beans in sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Season chicken with olive oil, salt, and pepper and bake in the oven at 375 for 30 minutes. Once cooked, shred into small bite size pieces and add to crock pot. Chop up carrots, onion, celery, and garlic and add to slow cooker. Add all other ingredients and mix together. Cook on low for at least 4 hours until veggies are at desired tenderness.

A delicious twist on traditional chili. Great on a cold fall day or for football Sunday.
