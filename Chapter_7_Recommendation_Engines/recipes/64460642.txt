Title:
Duane's Famous Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup tequila
1/3 cup Cointreau or Triple Sec
1/3 cup lime juice
1/3 cup simple syrup
1 cup ice cubes
lime wedge
Kosher salt (optional)
Grand Marnier (float)

Directions:
Use lime wedge to moisten edge of glasses, then dip in salt (optional).
Combine other ingredients, except Grand Marnier, either in blender with ice or just stir in a pitcher.  If not blended with ice, pour over ice cubes in glasses.
Pour a small amount of Grand Marnier over top in each glass.

Submitter: 
Jo Bice
Description: 
Best Margarita I have ever had, and worth the extra steps and ingredients.
