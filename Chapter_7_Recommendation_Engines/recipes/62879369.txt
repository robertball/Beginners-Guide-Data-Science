Title:
Layered Taco Dip with Meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Sour Cream
8 oz cream cheese
2 pkgs McCormick Mild Taco Seasoning mix
1 lb ground beef
1 bag shredded lettuce
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1 jar salsa

Directions:
Brown Ground beef and prepare according to taco seasoning mix.  Let cool and set aside.
Soften cream cheese and mix with sour cream and  one taco seasoning mix.
Spread cream cheese/sour cream/seasoning mixture on medium sized cookie sheet.  Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese to the top of the mixture.  Spread the the ground beef evenly.  Sprinkle lettuce evenly.  Sprinkle remaining shredded cheese.  Add salsa to taste.
Chill at least 1 hour in refrigerator prior to serving.
