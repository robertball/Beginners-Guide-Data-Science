Title:
Homemade Chocolate cookie crumbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Flour, 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate coco powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 cup softened butter, 1/3 cup white sugar, 1/3 cup brown sugar, 1 egg, 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
combine flour, coco powder & baking soda.  Set aside.  In a larger bowl blend butter, sugars, egg & vanilla.  slowly mix in dry ingrediants.  smooth out on greased baking sheet or baking stone.  bake at 350 for 14 min.
