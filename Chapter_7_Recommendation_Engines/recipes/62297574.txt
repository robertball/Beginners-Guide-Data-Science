Title:
Sweet and Sour Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans green beans, drained, juices reserved
1 cup chopped onion
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Cook onion in a medium skillet over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until translucent. Stir in flour and cook 2 minutes more.
Pour vinegar and 3/4 cup of the reserved green bean liquid into the pan. Add sugar, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, and stir in the green beans. Continue cooking at a low simmer until beans are hot.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
A slightly tart green bean side dish flavored with onion and bacon. Goes great with ham or pork.Modified from: Sweet and Sour Green Beans
