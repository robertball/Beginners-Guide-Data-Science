Title:
Perfect Wrap Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1413071.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C mayo
1 Tbsp mustard
1 Tbsp sriracha

Directions:
Mix all ingredients thoroughly to combine, spread on inside of wrap before adding fillings.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
This quick and easy sauce is the perfect condiment for any wrap- creamy, tangy, and a little spicy it never fails to please!
