Title:
Portabella and Beef Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb bacon
2 Lbs patio steaks
3/4C flour
1 1/2 tsp season salt
1 med onion, diced
1 large (4-5") portabella mushroom
1C Cabernet or other red wine
1/2 C chopped pars;ley
2 large tomatoes or 4-5 romas diced
1C beef broth OR reserved pasta water
8oz package of small shell pasta
Freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. In a 10" heavy bottom pan, cook bacon until crisp, drain and crumble.  Boil pasta according to package instuctions.
2. While bacon is cooking prepare other ingredients.  Dice meat into 1/2" cubes.  Place meat into plastic bag with flour and season salt.  Pound meat until tenderized (I use the back of my cleaver).
3. When bacon is done, remove from pan and drain fat, reserving 2-3Tbs.  Add meat to pan and cook on med heat until cooked to medium.
4. Add diced onion and cook until onion is tender, about 3-4 min.
5. Add diced mushrooms and cook for 2-3 min.
6. Add 1C red wine to de-glaze the pan.  Sauce will begin to thincken.
7. Add diced tomato, parsley and 1C beef broth OR reserved pasta water.  Stir over med. heat until sauce is desired consistency (add more broth or water if necessary).
8. Serve over pasta shells and top with fresh parmesan and crumbled bacon bits.  Season to taste.

Submitter: 
A Baker's Dozen
Description: 
Tender bits of Beef and portabella mushrooms pair together with a rich red wine sauce and is served over a bed of shell pasta.
