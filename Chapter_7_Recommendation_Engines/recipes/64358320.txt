Title:
Sauteed Spiced Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large Granny Smith Apples
1 Tbsp butter
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
1/8 tsp ginger
1/8 tsp cinnamon
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Peel, core and chop apples.
Melt butter in skillet over medium heat and add apples, sugar, lemon juice, ginger, cinnamon and salt.  Cook until the apples have softened and browned, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Great topping for hot oatmeal or pancakes.
