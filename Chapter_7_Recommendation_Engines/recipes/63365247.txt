Title:
3 Cheese pizza with Kale & Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sweet Italian sausage
1 bunch of Kale
1 1/2 cup ricotta
1 1/4 cup parmigiano
1 loaf ciabatta bread
2 cup mozzarella
2 tbsp finely chopped thyme

Directions:
1. In a large pot bring a few inches of salted water to a boil. Add kale and boil for 3 min. Drain kale and plunge into an ice bath to stop cooking.
2. Preheat oven to 400. In a large skillet cook sausage until brown.
3. In a bowl combine ricotta, parmigiano 1 tbsp olive oil and thyme; season with salt and pepper. Slather the cheese mixture onto bread then top with sausage, kale and mozzarella. Sprinkle with parmigiano and bake for 10 min.
