Title:
sesame chicken original Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8ckn thighs cut into pieces.
1/8c. flour
1/8c. part cornstarch
garlic powder
salt
pepper
1/8c. cooking sherry wine
1/4c. water
sesame seeds (brown in low heat then set aside)
Sauce:
2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
4 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup water
1/4 cup vinegar
2 tablespoons cornstarch w/ 1/4c. cold water

Directions:
Season chicken with garlic powder, salt and pepper. Marinate in sherry wine and water, flour and cornstarch.
Dredge marinated chicken in flour. Deep fry in wok or frier until golden brown and crispy.
Heat sauce ingredients until simmering, add cornstarch mixed w/ 1/2c. cold water to thicken. Pour sauce over chicken and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
