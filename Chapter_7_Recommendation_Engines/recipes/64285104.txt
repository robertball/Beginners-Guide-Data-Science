Title:
Bloody Mary Base Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1914090.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. A1 Steak Sauce
1 oz. Frank's Red Hot
2 oz. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
4 oz. Pickle Juice
1 oz. Peperoncini Juice
1.5 oz. Fresh Lemon Juice
2 T. Prepared Horseradish
2 T. Minced Garlic
1 T. Cracked Pepper
2 T. Old Bay Seasoning
1 T. Brown Sugar
1 t. Chili Powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a quart glass jar or other resealable container.  Store in the refrigerator.
In a glass with ice, combine one ounce of the base with 1.5 - 2 ounces of vodka or tequila.  Top off with tomato juice or Clamato.  Serve with a pickle spear, slice of bacon, olives, and/or peperoncini.  Mix and serve.

Submitter: 
Long Arm
Description: 
A good Bloody Mary base that you can mix up prior to entertaining for a quick and consistent Bloody Mary.
