Title:
Dipping Toasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices white or whole wheat bread
1 tablespoon butter, softened
Dipping Sauces:
1/4 cup light cream cheese mixed with
1 tablespoon dried cherries or cranberries
1/4 cup store bought hummus
1/4 cup store bought tabouli
1/4 cup peanut butter with a swirl of jelly in it

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Trim crusts from bread and brush each slice on both sides with some of the butter. Cut each slice lengthwise into 4 pieces.
Arrange the bread on a baking sheet and bake 5 minutes; turn and bake 4 to 5 minutes longer or until golden.

Submitter: 
Grain Foods Foundation
Description: 
A quick and easy appetizer treat! Pieces of bread are cut into slices, then brushed with butter, and baked until golden brown. Serve with a variety of dipping sauces, like hummus, tabouli, peanut butter with jelly, and cream cheese mixed with dried cherries.
