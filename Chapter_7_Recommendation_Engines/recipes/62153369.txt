Title:
Kathryn's Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head green leaf lettuce, washed and chopped into bite sized pieces
1 apple, diced
1 ripe avocado, diced
1 cup grape tomatoes
1/4 cup purple onion, diced finely
1/2 cup toasted sliced almonds
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
4 chicken breasts, cooked and diced (optional)
Dressing:
2 tsp. Dijon mustard
2 tsp. honey
2 tbsp. Balsamic vinegar
1/3 cup olive oil
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place salad ingredients in large salad bowl.
Pour dressing ingredients into small mixing bowl and whisk until combined.
Toss together and serve.

Submitter: 
Hunter's Mom 2008
Description: 
This salad is delicious as a side dish or add chicken for a healthy and tasty meal sized salad.
