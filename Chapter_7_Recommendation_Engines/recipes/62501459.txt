Title:
Migas Especial Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510542.jpg
Ingredients:
4 corn tortillas, cut into 8 triangles
2 Tablespoon oil
2Tablespooons butter
1/4 c. chopped onion
1/4 c. chopped tomato
2 Tablespoons chopped green pepper
4 large eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 c. grated cheddar or monterrey jack
1/2 teaspoon salt
2-3 dashes hot sauce

Directions:
Cook tortilla triangles in hot oil, stir occasionally and cook until crisp. remove from skillet.
In a skillet melt butter and add onions, tomatoes, green pepper. Cook until soft. Add hot sauce, salt, and cheese to beaten eggs. Add eggs to skillet with the veggies. Cook without stirring until eggs begin to set on the bottom of the pan. Draw a spatula across the bottom of the pan to form large curds. Add tortilla triangles to the eggs; cook until eggs are firm but still moist. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Glenda Beall
Description: 
Eggs,cheese, veggies, and corn torillas combine for a heavenly, crunchy, treat. Not only for breakfast, but they are awesome for a simple supper.
