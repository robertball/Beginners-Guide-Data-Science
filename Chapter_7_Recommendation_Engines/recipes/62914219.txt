Title:
Raid-the-Pantry Sloppy Joe Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. mini Ziti pasta
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 15.5oz can Hunt's Manwich Sloppy Joe sauce
1 12oz jar Heinz Home Style Savory Beef gravy
1 Tbsp. Heinz Ketchup
grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Boil pasta 'til tender. Drain. Set aside.
Cook ground beef thoroughly. Drain oil.
In large bowl, mix pasta, beef, sloppy joe sauce, gravy, and ketchup.
In appropriately sized pan, spread out mix evenly.
Shake on a layer of Parmesan cheese. (optional)
Bake on middle rack for 1 hour.
Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Megan Leigh
Description: 
For when you're in the mood for Sloppy Joe's but don't have any bread in the pantry.
