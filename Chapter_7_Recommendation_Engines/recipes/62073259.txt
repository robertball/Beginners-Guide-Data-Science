Title:
Punch for hundreds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon fruit punch concentrate (at Costco or BJ's warehouses) Mix according to bottle directions
2 large cans of Pineapple drink 32 ounce size
2 large cans any other fruit drink your choice such as grapefruit etc.
2 bottles of each;
orange soda pop
7-up or sprite
grapefreuit
After the water is added and all of the fruity punches and sodas are added this will create a huge punchbowl fountain type aproximatly 2-3 of them and the concentrate is aproximatly 9 dollars and soda can be bought at Wal-mart for 2 for a dollar. You can afford to make a lot.

Directions:
Mix it all well and if you like add a gallon of your favorite fruity ice cream or sorbet. You can also make a real fruit ring frozen in a special shape or ring and plae it in the bowl at the last minute.
