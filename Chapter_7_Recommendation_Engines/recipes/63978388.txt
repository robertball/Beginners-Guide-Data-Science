Title:
Crunchy Cauliflower and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1/3 cup good EVOO
3 Tablespoons white wine vinegar
a splash of fresh lemon juice
S&P; to taste
1 large head of cauliflower chopped in pieces about the size of a thumbnail.
1 cup cherry tomatoes halved or more if you like
1/4 cup fresh minced parsley
1/4 cup red onion, minced

Directions:
In a small glass bowl whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice and salt & pepper. Set aside
Chop cauliflower into small pieces and add to a large glass bowl.  Cut cherry tomatoes in half and set aside.  Mince parsley and red onion then add to cauliflower.  Pour dressing over cauliflower and toss completely.  Add the tomatoes and give a quick but gentle stir.  Chill salad up to 3 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Mary P.
Description: 
A crisp and crunchy salad that complements any summer picnic or barbeque.
