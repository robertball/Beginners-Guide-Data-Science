Title:
Homemade Biscuit Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2933393.jpg
Ingredients:
10 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup baking powder
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons salt
1 1/4 cups vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a mixing bowl. Add oil and mix with a fork or pastry blender; you should have small lumps throughout the mixture.
Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place for up to three months.

Description: 
Add a few simple ingredients, bake, and viola! You'll have hot, tasty homemade biscuits fast!
