Title:
SIZZLER RESTAURANT BLEU CHEESE DRESSING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP COTTAGE CHEESE
1/4 CUP SOUR CREAM
3-4 DROPS TABASCO SAUCE (OPTIONAL)
3/4 TO 1 CUP BLEU CHEESE
2 TABLESPOONS BUTTERMILK

Directions:
USE BLENDER FOR 1ST STEP
BLEND COTTAGE CHEESE/SOUR CREAM
ADD TABASCO,CHEESE, BUTTERMILK
LET SIT 30 MIN BEFORE TASTING
MORE BUTTERMILK IF NEEDED TO THIN
