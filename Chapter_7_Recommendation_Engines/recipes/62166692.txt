Title:
mexican chocolate chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground round
1 C prechopped onion
2 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes with garlic, undrained
1 15 oz can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 14.5 oz can corn, drained
1 C hot water
2 t chili powder
1/4 cup (heaping) semisweet chocolate chips
1 T ground cumin
1 t salt
1/2 t dried oregano

Directions:
Cook beef and onion in a large saucepan over medium-high heat 5 minutes or until beef is browned, stiring to crumble. Drain if necessary.  Add beef mixture to crock-pot/slowcooker. Add tomatoes and remaining ingredients; cover  medium heat or high heat, and bring to a bubble. Reduce to low heat.
Cook longer for thicker chili, cook less time for more liquid.
Serve with shredded cheese, frito chips & sour cream (optional).
