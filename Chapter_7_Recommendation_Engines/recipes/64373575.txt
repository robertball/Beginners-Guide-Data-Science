Title:
Lemon Caper Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. white wine vinegar
2 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. lemon zest
3 tbsp. chopped fresh dill
1 tsp. honey
1/8 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/3 cup olive oil
2 tbsp. capers - drained and roughly chopped

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth
With blender running, slowly add olive oil and blend until emulsified.
Pour into a small bowl and sir in capers.
If using for fish, pour over while still hot so fish can absorb flavors.
