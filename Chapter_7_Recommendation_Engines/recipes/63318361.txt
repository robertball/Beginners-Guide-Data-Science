Title:
Best Marinara Sauce Yet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1-3 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon of dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon of brown sugar
2-6 tablespoons olive oil
1/3 cup finely diced onion
1/2 cup white wine

Directions:
In a food processor place Italian tomatoes, tomato paste, chopped parsley, minced garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
In a large skillet over medium heat saute the finely chopped onion in olive oil for 2 minutes. Add the blended tomato sauce white wine, and brown sugar.
Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Michelle B.
Description: 
Tomatoes, parsley, garlic, basil, and oregano go for a spin in your food processor before simmering with sauteed onion and a goodly dash of white wine. The consistency is smooth and the taste is pleasing indeed.Modified from: Best Marinara Sauce Yet
