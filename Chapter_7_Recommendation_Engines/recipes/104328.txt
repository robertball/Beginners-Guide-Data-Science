Title:
Broccoflower速 and Roasted Red Pepper Salad with White Balsamic Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976878.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts water
1 tablespoon salt
1 head Broccoflower速, cut into 1 inch florets
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup diced red onion
1 cup roasted red pepper sliced
1/4 cup white balsamic vinegar
1/8 teaspoon dried tarragon
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring water and salt to a boil in a 4-quart pot.  Add Broccoflower(R) florets and cook for three minutes.  Remove from heat, drain, and put in ice water to stop cooking.  Drain well and place in serving dish.
In a small saucepan saute onion and peppers in 1-tablespoon olive oil until onions are transparent.  Add balsamic vinegar and tarragon.  Bring to a boil and remove from heat.  Whisk in remaining olive oil.  Pour over Broccoflower(R), toss to combine and refrigerate.
