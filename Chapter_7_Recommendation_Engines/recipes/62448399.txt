Title:
Lemon-lime Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Prepare 1 pkg. (500 g) lemon-lime cake mix
1 pkg. (4 servings) lemon-lime jello
¾ cup boiling water
1 envelope whipped topping (mix with 1 ½ cups cold milk)

Directions:
Prepare cake as to directions. Put in 9 x 13 pan. Cool 20 minutes.
Dissolve 1 pkg. lemon-lime jello in ¾ cup boiling water. Set aside.
When cake has cooled, poke deep holes about 1” apart with a meat fork. Pour jello over cake into holes.
Refrigerate. In a chilled bowl, blend 1 envelope whipped topping, mix with cold milk.
Beat until set (about 5 minutes). Frost. Refrigerate.
