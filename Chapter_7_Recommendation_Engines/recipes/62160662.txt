Title:
Codfish fritters (Stamp n go friters) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb.salted codfish
1/4 cup water
4 tsp flour
1 small onion minced
1 tomato chopped
1 stalk escallion finely chopped
1/2 tsp. diced hot pepper

Directions:
cook fish and flake finely.(soak at least an hour before use to get rid of excess salt).mix flour and seasoning add fish and water.fry by tablespoonfuls in very hot oil until bron and crisp.
