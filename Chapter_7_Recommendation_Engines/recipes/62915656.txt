Title:
Asian Inspired Grilled Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/710027.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Bunch of Asparagus
1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
1 Tablespoon Sesame Oil

Directions:
Grill the asparagus on an indoor grill till they turn bright green and have grill marks. Whisk the soy sauce and sesame oil together and pour over asparagus. Transfer the pan to the refrigerator and chill for at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
John Mark
Description: 
I like to cook this asparagus side-dish on an indoor iron grill to get grill marks but feel free cook the asparagus any way you like. The key to this dish is the simple marinade but tasty soy sauce and sesame oil marinade..
