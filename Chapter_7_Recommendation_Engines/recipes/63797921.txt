Title:
Griddled Marinated Courgettes with Feta, Lemon and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1053728.jpg
Ingredients:
2 courgettes
olive oil
small bunch or parsley
1 garlic glove
1/2 lemon, zest and juice
40g Greek Feta cheese

Directions:
- Slice courgettes lengthways into long strips about 1/2 cm thick;
- Fry courgettes on a hot pan with a little olive oil and season until they are soft enough for you;
- chop parsley, add lemon zest and juice, crushed garlic and 4 tbsp of olive oil;
- Arrange the courgettes on a plate, drizzle with the dressing and crumble over the feta cheese.

Submitter: 
sevasta
Description: 
A wonderful evocative taste of the Mediterranean, this quick starter takes no time to prepare. Just leave it for 5 min before serving to allow the flavours to develop.
