Title:
Deep dark healthy chocolate pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups unsweetened vanilla almond, soy, or coconut non dairy product
1 large egg yolk
8 tablespoons organic fair trade unsweetened cocoa
6 tablespoons corn starch
2/3 cup coconut sugar
dash sea salt
2 teaspoons bourbon vanilla
1-2 tablespoons coconut oil
optional toppings: unsweetened shredded coconut, sliced almonds, walnuts, mini dark chocolate chips and non dairy whipped topping

Directions:
Place non dairy liquid, corn starch, coconut sugar, egg yolk and salt in medium/large sauce pan and bring to slow boil, stirring constantly until thickened.  About 10 minutes.  Remove from heat, add the vanilla and coconut oil and stir in.  Pour into 6-8 bowls depending on size and amount wanted in each.   Top with sliced almonds and unsweetened shredded coconut if desired.   Let cool to room temp, and refrigerate until ready to eat.  NOTE: this is great warm!
Top with non dairy whipped topping before serving, may dust with dry cocoa and walnuts if desired.  And a sprig of  mint leaf.

Description: 
Depth beyond normal, dark, rich, sinful and elegant.  A fix for daily chocolate quota.  A must  in our house.  Easy and quick and almost healthy!
