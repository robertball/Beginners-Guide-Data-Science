Title:
Harvest Sweet Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 sweet potaoes, scrubbed, peeled and halved
8 cups of chicken stock or broth
2 tsp tsp of boulion
1 tbs olive oil
2 garlic cloves
1 Tbs dried basil
2 tsp nutmeg
generous pinch salt and pepper
sour cream, optional, for serving
a few leaves of fresh basil, sliced, optional, for serving

Directions:
boil potatoes until tender, drain
meanwhile, heat oil in large sauce pan
add garlic, saute till translucent
add stock and boulion
bring to vigorous boil
add potatoes and seasoning
return to boil
in small batches, add to blender or food processor
blend until smooth
Return to a soup pot, bring to a low boil
season with more salt & pepper to taste
ladle into bowls, top with sour cream, fresh basil, serve

Submitter: 
KMOMMYZ
Description: 
This is a deliciously fast soup, that requires few ingredients and just a few minutes to prepare.  I make this soup year round, but it really seems to bring out the fabulous flavors (and color) of fall!
