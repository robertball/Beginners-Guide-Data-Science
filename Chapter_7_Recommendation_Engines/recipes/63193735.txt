Title:
One Bowl Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups white sugar
7 cups all-purpose flour
3 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tbls. baking powder
2 tbls. baking soda
2 teaspoons salt
2 cups liquid eggs
4 cups milk
2 cups vegetable oil
3 tbls. vanilla extract
4 cups boiling water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Grease two long cafeteria pans & one half pan.
In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
