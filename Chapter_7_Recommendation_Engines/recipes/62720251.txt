Title:
Egg, Bacon, Bagel Breakfast Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bagel Thin
1/4 Cup Egg White Mixture
1 slice Tillamook cheese
2 slices Bacon

Directions:
Cook egg and bacon as you like it for a sandwich. Place cheese on egg to melt. Place all on a thin bagel.
