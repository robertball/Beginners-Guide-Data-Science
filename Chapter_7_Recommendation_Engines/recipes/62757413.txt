Title:
Chicken Baseballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 tsp margarine, melted
¼ tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper
2 Tbsp chopped onion
2 Tbsp milk
2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
8 oz. can cresant rolls
Bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Combine cream cheese, margarine, salt, pepper, milk, onion. Pour mix over chicken and stir.
Separate cresant rolls into 4 rectangles and seal perforations. Spoon ½ cup of chicken mix into each center and seal. Sprinkle each with bread crumbs. Place on baking sheet.
Bake for 20 minutes.
*Can add ½-1 packet of dry ranch dressing mix.
