Title:
My Family's Classic Pickled Eggs & Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 dozen hard boiled eggs, peeled
2 15 ounce cans sliced beets
1 15 ounce can pickled whole beets
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
5 cups white vinegar

Directions:
In a 1 gallon glass jar, add 3 cans of beets with liquid, vinegar and sliced onion.
Stir just until combined.
Gently add eggs to liquid mixture, and stir carefully just until mixed.
Top jar with lid and store in the refigerator for two weeks.
These have to sit for five days before eating, for best flavor.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This pickled egg recipe has been in my family for many years. The liquid is a no cook version, and is very flavorful and delivers a perfect pickled egg, with bright color. We make these every year a week before Easter. Enjoy!
