Title:
Healthy Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup flaxseed (flax meal)
1 1/2 cups instant oatmeal
3 TBLS brown sugar
2 TBLS baking powder
1/2 TSP salt
3 large eggs (beaten)
2 cups of milk
1/4 cup of olive oil
1 tsp of vanilla
3/4 cup of frozen blueberries

Directions:
In a large bowl combine all the dry ingredients (whole wheat flour, flour, flaxseed, oatmeal, brown sugar, baking poweder, and salt).
In a smaller bowl mix the beaten eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla with a whisk.
Pour the wet ingredients from the smaller bowl into the dry ingredients all at once and stir to combine. Gently fold in the blueberries.
Ladle 1/4 of cup of pancake mix into a pan over medium heat that has been sprayed with a cooking spray. Flip once the top starts to bubble.
