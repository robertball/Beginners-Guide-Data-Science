Title:
Cool-Down Grapefruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6821809.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups grapefruit juice
1 fresh peach - peeled, pitted, and diced
1 tablespoon milk
4 pitted cherries
4 ice cubes

Directions:
Place grapefruit juice, peach, milk, cherries, and ice cubes in a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Mini Chef
Description: 
Great grapefruit smoothie for the Summer. All you need are some simple fruits.
