Title:
Mexicale Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL速 Tamales
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL速 Chili No Beans
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar
1 (8.5 ounce) package corn muffin mix

Directions:
In small skillet, saute celery and green pepper in butter until tender. Remove papers from tamales and slice into bite-size pieces. Combine tamales, chili, celery and green pepper. Pour half of mixture into 8 or 9-inch baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese and top with remaining mixture. Prepare corn muffin mix according to package directions; drop batter by spoonfuls over top of mixture. Bake in 375 degrees F oven 25 minutes.
