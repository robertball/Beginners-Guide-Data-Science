Title:
Cocktail 707 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798890.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup citron vodka
1/4 cup vodka
1/4 cup pear vodka
1/2 cup cranberry-raspberry juice blend
1/2 lime, juiced
2 wedges lime, for garnish

Directions:
Pour the citron vodka, plain vodka, pear vodka, cranberry-raspberry juice, and lime juice into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover, and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into chilled glasses; garnish each glass with a lime wedge to serve.
