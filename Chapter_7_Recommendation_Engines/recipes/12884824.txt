Title:
Country Fried Steak With White Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488177.jpg
Ingredients:
3 T Oil
3½ t Salt (divided)
2½ t Pepper (divided)
6 Cube Steaks
3 Eggs
5 c Milk (divided)
60 Saltine Crackers (crushed)
2½ c Flour (divided)
1 t Baking Powder
1 t Beef Bouillon

Directions:
SPRINKLE salt and pepper evenly over steaks, set aside.
COMBINE cracker crumbs, 2 c flour, baking powder, 2 t salt, 1 t black pepper.
WHISK together 1 c milk and eggs.
DREDGE steaks in cracker crumb mixture; dip in milk mixture and dredge in cracker mixture again.
POUR oil into a skillet; heat to 360.
FRY steaks 10 minutes on first side.
TURN over and fry 4-5 more minutes or until golden brown.
REMOVE to a wire rack on a foiled pan.
KEEP steaks warm in a 225 degree oven.
DRAIN oil, reserving cooked bits and 1 T drippings in skillet.
WHISK together remaining ½ c flour, 1 t salt, 1 t black pepper, 1 t beef bouillon and 4 cups milk.
WHISK mixture into skillet over medium-high heat for 10-12 minutes or until thickened.
SERVE gravy with steaks and mashed potatoes.

Thickly breaded cube steaks served with a peppery white gravy.
