Title:
Strawberrylicious Daiquiris Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of strawberries
1/2 C sugar
1/3 C unsweetened pineapple juice
1 Tbsp lime juice
3/4 C dark spiced rum
Ice

Directions:
Wash berries, remove green tops and slice in half.
Add berries and sugar to blender, puree.
Add pineapple juice, lime and rum.
Pulse blender until well incorporated.
Pour over ice and serve immediately!

Submitter: 
AZ
Description: 
Fresh strawberries paired with pineapple and lime make for a perfect drink on that first hot day of summer! Dark spiced rum adds a special flavor that stands up to flavor burst you get from the fresh berries.
