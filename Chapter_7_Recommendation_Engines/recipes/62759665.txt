Title:
Ensalada de Pulpo (Octopus Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked octopus, in chunks
1/2 cup red onions, diced
1 cup fresh tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup green bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup roasted red bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 teaspoons white vinegar
2 teaspoons lime juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredents in a bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Let rest in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
Serve on a bed of lettuce, as a stuffing for halved ripe avocados, tostones rellenos or mofongo.

Submitter: 
LatinaCook
Description: 
This octopus salad can be served on top of lettuce or as a filling to tostones rellenos or mofongo. Adjust the ingredients to your liking, but try to keep it colorful and fresh. You can play with this recipe just as you would any salad or salsa.
