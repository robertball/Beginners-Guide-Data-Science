Title:
Johnsonville® Chipotle and Monterey Jack Cheese Chicken Sausage Potato Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988510.jpg
Ingredients:
3 medium russet potatoes, washed and cubed
2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green or red bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 (12 ounce) package Johnsonville® Chipotle Monterey Jack Cheese Chicken Sausage, cut into 2-inch pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, onion and bell pepper. Add the oil, salt and pepper; toss to coat. Transfer to a greased shallow baking pan. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until potatoes are tender and lightly browned, stirring occasionally. Arrange sausage over vegetables. Bake 5-10 minutes longer or until sausage is heated through.

Submitter: 
The Kitchen at Johnsonville Sausage
Description: 
Potatoes, bell pepper, and onions are oven roasted, then roasted some more with slices of chipotle chicken sausage.
