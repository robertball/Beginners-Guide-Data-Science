Title:
Jana's Italian Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/398433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package whole wheat, cheese filled, tortellini
1 package turkey Italian Sausage
1 cup sliced celery
1 cup diced onion
1 cup diced bell pepper
1 can diced Italian Style tomatoes
3 cans, or 1 large box, Organic vegetable or chicken broth
1 1/2 tsp Italian Seasonings
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tsp salt
1-2 tp olive oil

Directions:
Remove sausage from casing and brown in stock pot in 1-2 tsp olive oil.
Remove sausage and add onions, celery and garlic to drippings in pan.  Cook 5 min.
Add back in sausage, broth, green peppers, tomatoes and seasonings. Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, cook tortellini in separate pan according to directions, until just cooked (al dente). Drain and add to soup mixture.
