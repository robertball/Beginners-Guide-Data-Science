Title:
Super Blueberry Lemon Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483018.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
3 tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
Rind of 1 lemon
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
Topping:
1/2 cup melted butter
1 tsp. lemon juice
1/2 cup white sugar

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and lelmon rind in bowl.
Beat egg in medium-sized bow, add milk and butter.
Add egg mixture to dry ingredients. Stir until just mixed (batter will be lumpy).
Stir in blueberries and fill tins 2/3 full.
Bake at 425 F for 20 minutes.
