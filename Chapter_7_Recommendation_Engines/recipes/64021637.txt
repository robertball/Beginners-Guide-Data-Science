Title:
Texas Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 sticks butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup water
1 tsp. vinegar
2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
4 tbsp. cocoa powder - unsweetened
1/2 cup milk
Frosting:
1 1/2 sticks butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1 to 1 1/2 boxes powdered sugar
6 tbsp. cocoa powder - unsweetened
8 tbsp. milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
For cake - Add vinegar to milk to sour it.
Mix sugar, flour milk, eggs, cinnamon and vanilla.
In a saucepan, melt butter, cocoa powder and water.
When butter is melted and mixture is smooth, add to flour mixture.
Mix until smooth.
Pour batter into greased 15x10 cookie sheet.
Bake for 15-20 minutes.
For Frosting - In a saucepan, bring butter, cocoa powder and milk to a boil.
Remove from heat and add vanilla and powdered sugar until desired consistency is reached.
Frost cooled cake and garnish with chopped nuts of desired.
