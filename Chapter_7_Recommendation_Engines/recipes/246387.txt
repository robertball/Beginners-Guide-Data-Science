Title:
Western Style Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3308061.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
2 1/2 cups water
1 (15 ounce) can no salt-added kidney beans
1 (14.5 ounce) can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
1 (5.5 ounce) package Knorr® Rice Sides™ - Rice Pilaf

Directions:
Season ground beef with salt and pepper if desired. Heat large deep nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and cook ground beef, onion and green pepper until beef is browned, about 5 minutes.
Stir in chili powder and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 30 seconds, Add water, beans and tomatoes and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
Stir in Knorr(R) Rice Sides(TM) - Rice Pilaf; reduce heat and simmer covered, stirring occasionally, until rice is tender, about 7 minutes. Serve, if desired, with shredded Monterey Jack, Pepper Jack or Mexican blend cheese and corn bread.

Submitter: 
Knorr
Description: 
This flavorful one pot recipe for Western-Style Chili includes creamy beans, fresh peppers, and hearty meat.
