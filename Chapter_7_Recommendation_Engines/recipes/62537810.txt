Title:
Pecan Pie Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour (use a little less)
1/4 cup + 2 Tblsp white sugar
3/4 cup margarine (softened)
3/4 teaspoon salt
----------------- Filling ------------------
4 eggs, slightly beaten
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups light corn syrup
3 tablespoons margarine, melted
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease the sides and bottom of one 15x10 inch pan.
Beat crust ingredients on medium speed until crumbly (mixture will be dry)
Press firmly into the pan.
Bake until light golden brown about 20 minutes.
In a large bowl mix filling ingredients except pecans until blended.
Stir in the pecans.
Spread the filling evenly over the hot crust.
Bake until filling is set, approximately 25 minutes. Let cool before slicing.
