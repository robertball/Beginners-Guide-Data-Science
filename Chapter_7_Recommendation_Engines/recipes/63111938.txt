Title:
Creamy Pasta w/Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Coarse Salt & Ground Pepper
Farfalle Pasta (12 oz)
Package of frozen peas (10oz)
3/4 c heavy cream
1/3 c reduced-sodium chicken broth
1/2 c grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to cooking directions.
Add peas 1 min before end of cooking time.
Return pasta and peas to pot.
In large skillet combine chicken broth and cream, simmer 7 mins until slightly thick.  Stir in parmesan until melted.
Add sauce to pasta and peas, toss to combine.  Season with salt and pepper.
