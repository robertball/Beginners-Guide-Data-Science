Title:
Unstuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887769.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of ground beef
1 24 oz jar of your favorite spaghetti sauce
2 bell peppers (one green one red)
1 1/4 cup water
1 cup white rice

Directions:
Brown the ground beef in a large skillet.  Drain beef, chop bell peppers, combine all ingredients into skillet.  Bring to boil and simmer until peppers are tender.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jennifer Waxham
Description: 
This recipe is a staple in our kitchen...a must have.  It's a one skillet recipe and so easy to make.
