Title:
Reeses Peanut Butter Cup Stuffed Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/471200.jpg
Ingredients:
One Bowl Chocolate Cake I (from this site)
Fluffy Peanut Butter Frosting (from this site)
24 mini Reeses Peanut Butter Cups

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Prepare One Bowl Chocolate Cake batter as directed. Fill 12 cupcake liners halfway with batter. Drop in a mini Reeses Peanut Butter Cup. Continue filling cupcake liners.
Bake cupcakes for 20-25 minutes.
While cupcakes are cooling prepare Fluffy Peanut Butter Frosting.
After frosting cupcakes add one miniture Reeses Peanut Butter Cup for decoration.
