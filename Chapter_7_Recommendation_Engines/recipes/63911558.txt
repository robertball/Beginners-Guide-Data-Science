Title:
Shredded Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large, frozen chicken breasts
1 chicken bouillon cube
hot water
2 tbl oil or butter
3 tbl chili powder
1 tbl onion powder
1 tbl garlic powder
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Add chicken to small pot. Turn heat to high and add enough hot water to cover chicken and leave an inch of water above.
Add remaining ingredients.
Once boiling, turn heat to low and simmer for 45 minutes, adding water if necessary.
Reserve 1/4 cup of juices and set aside.
Remove chicken from pot and shred. Toss with reserved liquid.
