Title:
Maryland Crab Cakes - Lowfat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. fat free plain Greek yogurt
1 T. Dijon mustard
1 T. lemon juice
2tsp. reduced-fat mayonnaise
1 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
1/4 tsp. ground red pepper
1 T. finely chopped parsley
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
3/4 bread crumbs
1 lb. lump crab meat, broken into bite-size pieces
2 egg whites

Directions:
Coat a baking dish with no-stick spray and set aside.
In Large bowl, stir together yogurt, mustard, lemon juice, mayonnaise, Old Bay, pepper, parsley and Worcestershire sauce. Add the bread crumbs and stir until well combines.
Add the crab. Gently stir until combined, being careful to leave the lumps of crab intact.
In a small bowl, use a whisk and beat eggs until they form soft peaks. Fold the egg whites into the crab mixture.
Form into 6 patties and place the m on the prepared baking sheet. Bake at 375 for 12-15 minutes, or until lightly browned.
