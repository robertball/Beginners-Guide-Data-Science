Title:
Corn Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 regualr size can whole kernal corn, (drained)
1 regular sized can cream style corn
1 8 ounce package corn muffin mix, (Jiffy preferred)
1 cup sour cream
1/2 stick salted butter, (melted)
1- 1 &1/2 cups shredded cheddar

Directions:
Combine corn(s), mix, sour cream and butter, mix and pour into 13x9 greased casserole.
Bake 45 minutes until golden brown.
Remove from oven and top with Cheddar cheese, return to oven for 5-10 minutes.
Serve warm

Submitter: 
Ed Price
Description: 
This is a decadent holiday favorite.  Very rich and filling.  Not an everyday bread or side dish, rather a seasonal indulgence.
