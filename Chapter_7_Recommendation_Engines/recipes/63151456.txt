Title:
Nagasari Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/807328.jpg
Ingredients:
5 very ripped bananas
250 gr Rice Flour
2 Tbsp Tapioca Starch
175 gr Sugar
1/2 Tsp Salt
2 Pandan Leaves
1 Can (400 ml) Coconut Milk
350 ml water

Directions:
Peel and halve each small banana or if using large bananas, slice each diagonally  into six Â½ -in (1cm) thick slices, set aside.
Combine flour and 1/2 of coconut milk and water in a bowl and mix into a smooth batter, free of any lumps. Set aside.
Combine the remaining coconut milk, sugar and salt in a saucepan, and heat over over medium heat, mixing well until the sugar is dissolved. Bring into a boil, then pour in the batter and continue to cook, stirring constanly until  the mixture thickens, 5 to 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool
To wrap the cakes, spread 2 tablespoons of the flour mixture across the center of a piece of banana on top and then spread another 2 tablespoon of the flour mixture across the center of a piece of banana leaf. Lay a slice of the banana leaf on top and then spread another 2 tablespoons of the flour mixture over it, covering the banana. Wrap by folding one side of the banana leaf over the filling, then the other,
Steam the parcels in a wok on a rack over  boiling water or in a stearner for about 20 minutes. Remove from the heat and allow to cool before serving.

Submitter: 
JFKR
Description: 
Creamy on the outside with a white puddingy texture and sweet in the inside (depending on banana sweetness), it makes a wonderful dessert.
