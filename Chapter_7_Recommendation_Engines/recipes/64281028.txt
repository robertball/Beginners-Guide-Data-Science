Title:
Tropical Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
63 oz Sun Warrior chocolate protein powder (3 scoops)
3 cups spinach
1 banana
1 cup mango
1 cup pineapple
22 ice cubes (10.55 oz worth - 1 cup water)

Directions:
Put water, protein powder, and spinach into a blender and blend until smooth.
Next all all remaining ingredients and blend. Ice can be substituted for water. Total you want 2 cups liquid from H2O in your smoothie.

Description: 
A tropic twist on the green smoothie for your veggie protein needs. 300 calories, 27,5g protein
