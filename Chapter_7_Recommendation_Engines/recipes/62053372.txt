Title:
Cherry Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cherry pie filling,
1 large can crushed pineapple,
1 box yellow cake mix,
1 stick butter.

Directions:
Pour pie filling into baking dish, top with crushed pineapple.   Mix cake mix and butter together.  Spread on top of cherry mixture.  Bake one hour at 350 degrees.
