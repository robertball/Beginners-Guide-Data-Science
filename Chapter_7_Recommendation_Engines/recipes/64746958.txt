Title:
Big Ray's Asian Hot Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6607792.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp Olive oil
1 medium onion, minced
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 lb ground pork
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp hot sauce
2 Tbsp toasted sesame seed oil
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 medium head cabbage cut into 3/4 inch strips
1 cup julienne carrot
cooked ramen noodles or rice
sliced almonds

Directions:
Heat a large and deep cast iron skillet (or dutch oven) over medium heat and add the olive oil.
When hot, add onions and garlic and saute' until soft, about 4 minutes.
Add the ground prk, ginger and hot sauce, cooking the pork and breaking  it up into small pieces until it loses its pink color.
Add the sesame seed oil, soy sauce, sugar and sea salt, mixing well.
Add the cabbage, a handful at a time, allowing it to cook down until soft before adding the next, about 15 minutes.
Add carrots and cook for 4 minutes more.
Cover and remove from heat, let sit for 5 minutes.
Serve with noodle or rice and top with almonds.
