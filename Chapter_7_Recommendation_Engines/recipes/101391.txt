Title:
Barbecue Pork Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982882.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pork chops, about 3/4-inch thick
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
1/4 cup Italian-style salad dressing
1/4 cup barbeque sauce

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat and brown chops about 1 minute on each side. Add remaining ingredients to pan, stirring to blend. Cover and simmer for 5-8 minutes, until chops are cooked through.

Submitter: 
National Pork Board
Description: 
This is a quick way to serve up tangy barbecue flavor in a hurry from your stovetop. Serve these saucy chops with warm dinner rolls and deli cole slaw.
