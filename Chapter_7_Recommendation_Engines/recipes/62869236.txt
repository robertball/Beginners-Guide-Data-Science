Title:
Sand Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. shortening or butter
1 c. sugar
2 well beaten eggs
2 1/2 c. flour
1/2 t. salt

Directions:
Cream together shortening (or buuter) with the sugar.  Stir in eggs.  Add vanilla.  Sift together flour, salt and add to first mixture.  Drop by teaspoon onto lightly greased cookie sheets.  Press with glass.  Sprinkle with sugar.  Bake 8 minutes at 400 degrees.

Submitter: 
Mary Guilds
Description: 
This was my grandmas recipe that we always looked forward to having when we visited her.  A light cookie that is so easy to make.
