Title:
caramel check mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups box Golden Graham's,
2 cups rice chexs
2 cups corn chex
2 cups wheat chex
2 cups toasted coconut
1 cup light corn syrup
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
In a saucepan bring corn syrup, sugar, and butter to a boil for 2 an half minutes then pour over cereal and serve.
