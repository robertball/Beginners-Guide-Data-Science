Title:
Corey's Brown Snapper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/885233.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
3 fluid ounces Canadian whiskey
4 ice cubes
3/4 cup chilled root beer

Directions:
Pour the amaretto and whiskey into a glass with ice. Top with root beer. Stir and enjoy!
