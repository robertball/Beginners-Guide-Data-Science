Title:
Sausage stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf of bread(chopped in chunks)
1 1/2C celery(chopped)
1/2C onions(chopped)
1tsp sage
1tsp salt
pepper to taste
1ibs pork sausage
1 egg
gizzards and liver from turkey(or chicken)

Directions:
Cook pork sausage until done; then add the celery, onion, and spices. Meanwhile cook the gizzards and liver in a small saucepan and cover with water. cook until tender, then chop up and add to the rest of the ingredients.
