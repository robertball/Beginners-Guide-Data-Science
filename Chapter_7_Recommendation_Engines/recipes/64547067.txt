Title:
California Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3243683.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 white onion
2 stalks celery
2 small potatoes
3 cans chopped clams (with juice)
8oz clam juice
2 tblsp butter
2 tblsp flour
3/4 cup heavy cream
1/2 tsp dried tarragon
1/2 tsp fresh Italian parsley

Directions:
1. Chop onions and celery, peel and chop potatoes.
2. In a deep pan, heat a small amount of butter and cook onions and celery until onions are translucent and fragrant.  Add potatoes and toss to coat, cook 3-5 minutes.  Add clam juice and drain canned clams.  If necessary, add water to cover potatoes.  Bring to a boil and simmer uncovered.
3. In a separate pan, heat butter and whisk in flour to make a roux.  Add dried tarragon and parsley to cream and heat, whisk into roux and mix until smooth.  Add to potato mixture and mix until smooth.
4. Add drained clams to chowder and heat until warmed through.  Salt and pepper to taste, add parsley as a garnish.  Serve immediately.
