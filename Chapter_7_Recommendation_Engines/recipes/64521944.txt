Title:
Gluten Free Banana Bread made easy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4077078.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large ripe smashed banana's
1 Tablespoon Bourbon Vanilla extract
1 egg
1-1/3 cup of Gluten Free all purpose flour
1/4 cup fine ground flax seed
1/4 cup vegetable oil of choice
1/4 cup raw honey
dash salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon xanthan gum
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, line with parchment paper a regular medium sized loaf pan.
In large mixing bowl, first smash bananas, add egg and beat by hand together, adding vanilla, oil, honey, dash salt, flour, ground flax seed, nutmeg, xanthan gum, baking powder and soda mixing well, add nuts if desired and/or chocolate chips.  Pour into loaf pan and bake about 50 minutes to 1 hour until done.

Description: 
Perfect touch of sweet honey, nutmeg and pecans lend the flavor combination I go back to over and over for a light healthy snack of little guilt.  Fast and easy mix in one bowl.  Healthy too!
