Title:
Halibut Olympia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb halibut filets seasoned with salt & pepper
2 bunches of chopped green onions
1 tablespoon butter
½ cup white wine
½ Cup Sour Cream (or plain greek yogurt)
½ Cup Mayonnaise
2 cloves diced garlic
fresh dill
1/2 tbs Old Bay seasoning
panko bread crumbs
capers
Garnish with fresh dill and lemon

Directions:
1.Season halibut filets with salt & pepper
2.In skillet, sauté chopped onions in 1 tablespoon butter until translucent. Add ½ cup wine, simmer until slightly reduced, but still juicy. Add this mixture to the bottom of the baking dish. Place halibut on top. Meanwhile, mix sour cream topping: ½ Cup Sour Cream
½ Cup Mayonnaise
3.Add to taste; diced garlic, fresh dill, salt, white pepper, paprika
4.Pour on top of halibut. Add panko, green onion and capers
5.Bake 15 minutes at 350
6.Garnish with fresh dill and lemon
