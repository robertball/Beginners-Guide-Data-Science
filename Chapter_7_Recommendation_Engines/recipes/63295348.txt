Title:
Linda's Tropical Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. each baking soda and cinnamon
1/2 tsp. each salt, nutmeg and allspice
(mix dry ingredients)
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 1/4 cups Crisco oil
(mix this together)
2 cups finely shredded raw carrot (about 6 medium)
1 can (8 oz) crushed pineapple, drained (about 3/4 cup)
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
1/2 cup flaked coconut (optional)
Cream Cheese Frosting
1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese
1 lb icing sugar
2 tsp. orange or lemon juice
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. greated orange or lemon peel

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients.
Mix wet ingredients
(mix all together and spread in 9 x 13 pan, lightly greased, or three layer pans.
Bake in 350 oven 40 minutes for 3 layers, or 55 to 60 minutes for sheet cake.
