Title:
Mango Lassi I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/770743.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup plain yogurt
1 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
1 tablespoon white sugar
3 cups cold water
1 pinch salt
4 sprigs fresh mint, garnish

Directions:
In a blender, combine yogurt, mango, sugar, water and salt. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve garnished with a sprig of mint.

Submitter: 
Cathy
Description: 
A cooling drink made with mango and yogurt, Eastern Indian Lassi.
