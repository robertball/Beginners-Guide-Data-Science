Title:
Mommy's Orange Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening
2 1/2 c. flour
1 1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1 T. fresh grated orange rind
1/2 c. orange juice
1 c. sugar
2 eggs

Directions:
Blend shortening, sugar, salt, orange rind, and juice.  Stir in dry ingredients.  Mix well.  Drop onto parchment paper lined baking sheets.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.  Ice with orange icing while still warm.
