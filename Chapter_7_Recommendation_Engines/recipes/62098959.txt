Title:
Strawberry Supreme Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz package frozen strawberries
1 3oz package strawberry jello.
1 box of sponge cake mix
vanilla flavoring
powdered sugar
1 pt heavy cream
water
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Defrost frozen strawberries.
Pour strawberries and juice in blender.
Blend for 15 seconds.
Combine strawberry puree with 1 1/2 cups water.
Put in sauce pan.
Cook over medium heat.
When mixture comes to a boil.
Add the package of jello.
Stir to dissolve.
remove pan from heat.
Let mixture cool.
Bake cake following box directions.
Add 1 1/2 tbsp vanilla to batter.
Bake in 9 x 13 pan.
Bake according to box directions.
When the strawberry mixture has cooled to room tempeture.
Divide in half into two bowls.
Beat the heavy cream until it forms peaks.
Add 1 tsp vanilla
Add 1 tbsp powdered sugar.
Fold the cream into one of the bowls.
Mix until well combined.
Cover and put in refrigerater.
This is your strawberry mousse.
Add 1/4 cup water to the other bowl.
Mix, cover and refrigerate.
This is your strawberry syrup.
Let mousse and syrup stay in refrigerater at least 3 hours.
After cake is done
Let cool in refrigerater.
When the cake has cooled and the mousse has firmed up.
You are ready to assemble your trifle.
First cut cake into small squares.
Lay cake pieces on bottom of bowl.
Add a layer of strawberry mousse.
Add another layer of cake squares.
Using a teaspoon add a swirl of strawberry syrup.
Add another layer of strawberry mousse..
Add another layer of cake squares.
Swirl on more strawberry syrup.
Add more stawberry mousse.
Crumble cake squares on top.
Add another swirl of strawberry syrup.
Top with lots of cool whip.
Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
Serve with vanilla ice cream on side with strawbeery syrup.
This is a favorite at our house in the winter.
When you can't get a good strawberry shortcake.

Submitter: 
Melissa Carmela
Description: 
This is a delicious  strawberry mousse accompanied by light sponge cake, strawberry syrup, and cool whip.  A great dessert for parties.
