Title:
Banana Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/187792.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pineapple juice
3 bananas, sliced
1 tablespoon honey
2 cups ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine pineapple juice, bananas, honey and ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Margo
Description: 
This is a great way to cool down. Adjust the honey to your personal taste.
