Title:
Southern Squash Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium yellow squash, cubed
1 egg, beaten
1/3 c flour
1/3 c corn meal
1 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1 medium onion, grated
vegetable oil

Directions:
Place cubed squash in saucepan and cover with salted water and bring to a boil.  Boil 10-15 minutes or until tender.  Drain and mash.  Combine with egg and blend well.  Add flour, corn meal, baking powder and salt.  Mix well.  Add onions and stir til blended.  Drop squash into hot oil in frying pan with large spoon.  Cook until golden brown, turning once.

Submitter:

Description: 
This was one of my great-grandmother's favorites!  A real southern recipe with real southern taste.  Enjoy!
