Title:
Really Good Rib Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup vinegar (1/3 cup if you like it tangy)
1/3 cup honey
2 healthy tbsp steak sauce
tsp cayenne pepper
tsp paprika
3 cloves garlic
tbsp liquid hickory flavored liquid smoke

Directions:
Finely chop the onion and garlic, and sweat them in a large skillet (cast iron if you have it)
add ketchup, vinegar, and steak sauce, and slowly bring them to a bubble, turn heat to medium low, and add honey, cayenne pepper, paprika, and liquid smoke.  Bring back up to a bubble, and then turn on low for about 20-25 minutes.
Brush sauce onto ribs the last 15 minutes of grill time to caramelize.
