Title:
Salami Cream Cheese Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Genoa Deli salami, cut on a 1
3 oz pkg. cream cheese
1 jar sweet Bavarian mustard

Directions:
Spread each slice of salami with a thin layer of cream cheese. Spread a small amount of mustard on the cream cheese. Roll slice of salami up and repeat procress until all the salami is gone. Place roll-ups on a plate or tray and in frig until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
This a very easy recipe that tastes great...perfect for a light snack or to take somewhere. Perfect picnic food! Buy the best Bavarian sweet mustard you can afford for these roll-ups, it really makes them delish!
