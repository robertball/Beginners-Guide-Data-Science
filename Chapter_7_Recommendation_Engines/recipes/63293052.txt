Title:
Ramen Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb coleslaw
2 pkg ramen noodles - chicken (break up noodles, save flavor packet)
2 oz silvered almonds
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
chopped green onion
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup salad oil

Directions:
Mix coleslaw, noodles, almonds, sunflower seeds, and green onion in large bowl.
Mix sugar,flavor packet from noodles, cider vinegar, and oil well.
Pour over coleslaw mixture and stir together.
