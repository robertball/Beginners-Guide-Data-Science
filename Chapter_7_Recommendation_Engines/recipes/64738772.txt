Title:
Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, at room temperature
1 cup olive oil
1/4 cup grated Parmigano Reggiano cheese
1 clove garlic, smashed
4 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 1/2 tsp. anchovy paste

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a large Mason jar, all at room temperature
Put an immersion blender down into the jar and turn on
Blend, pulling up as you go, until dressing is emulsified
Keep refrigerated and eat within a few weeks.
