Title:
Penne a la Vodka II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
1 cup chopped fresh basil
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup vodka
1 pint heavy cream or
condensed milk
1 box penne

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, cook garlic in olive oil until tender, 1 to 2 minutes.  Stir in tomatoes, breaking up by hand held mixer.  Stir in basil, salt and pepper and simmer 15 minutes.  Stir in vodka and cream.
Add pasta, uncooked, to mixture. Stir and cover to cook for 10-20 minutes, or until pasta is cooked. Stir occasionally during cooking.

Submitter: 
Dawn Norby
Description: 
This is an easy recipe.  The sauce can be made while you cook the pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.Modified from: Penne a la Vodka II
