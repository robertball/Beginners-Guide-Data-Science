Title:
Shabu-Shaby Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lemon grass
handful of cilantro
1 red chili
1" ginger thick sliced
2lbs Lg shrimp, cooked shell on-head and shell remonved for soup
10 c water
3 chicken breasts-thinly sliced
1lb pork tenderloin-thin
1lb beef tenderloin-thin
mushrooms
1/2 nappa cabbage, leaves chopped
broccoli small florets
carrots diagonal tin slices
SAUCE
1/4c soy
3 1/2 T mirin
3 1/2 T rice vineger
2 green onions
2 t ginger
1 egg yolk
cooked egg noodles
ponzu
soy

Directions:
Cook first 6, not shrimp, just shells for 1 hour-strain-put this in fondue pot.
Bring fondue pot to simmer, cook food for 10-60 sec.
Mix all sauce ingredients to dip in.
Serve remaining stuff with noodles.
