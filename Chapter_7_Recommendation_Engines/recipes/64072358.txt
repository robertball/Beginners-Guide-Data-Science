Title:
Eggs Poached in Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1133024.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 a med. onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
Olive oil for cooking onion/garlic. About 2T
8 eggs
1T dry parsley
1T dry basil
1T dry oregano
pinch hot pepper flakes
1/4-1/2 freshly ground pepper or to taste
1/2 t salt
2 Litres tomato sauce
1-2 bay leaves
1/4-1/2 cup dry red wine

Directions:
In a large skillet cook onion and garlic. Do not brown.
Add tomato sauce, herbs, salt, pepper and wine.
Simmer until sauce has thickened, approx 45 mins
Add eggs, 1 at a time, and spoon sauce over eggs.
Put a lid on the skillet and simmer until eggs are cooked through, approx 30  mins.
