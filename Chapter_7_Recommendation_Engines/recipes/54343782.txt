Title:
Sugar Free Homemade Sweetened Condensed Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup instant nonfat dry milk powder
2/3 cup Splenda
1/3 cup boiling water
3 Tbsp. melted Land O Lakes Light Butter(with canola oil) spread

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender.  Blend until smooth.  Can be refrigerated for up to 1 week.  Makes 1 1/4 cups which is equal to a 14 ounce can.  ww points equal 9 (as opposed to 22 in a 14 ounce can of fat free sweetened condensed milk.

Submitter: 
suetoyou
Description: 
I was making a recipe which called for condensed milk; I had none on had and decided to make my own with what ingredients I had on hand.Modified from: Homemade Sweetened Condensed Milk
