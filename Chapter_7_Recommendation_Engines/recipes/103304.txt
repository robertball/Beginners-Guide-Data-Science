Title:
Grilled Sherry Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/997977.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Holland HouseÂ® Sherry Cooking Wine
1/4 cup molasses
2 tablespoons soy sauce
4 pork chops, 1-inch thick

Directions:
In plastic bowl, combine sherry, molasses and soy sauce; pour over pork chops. Cover; refrigerate 30 minutes.
Prepare grill. Drain pork chops; save marinade.
Grill pork chops over medium-high heat 20 to 30 minutes or until pork is no longer pink in center, turning once and brushing frequently with marinade. Do not baste during last 5 minutes of grilling. Discard any remaining marinade.

Submitter: 
Holland House
Description: 
Pork chops are soaked in a sherry marinade then quickly grilled for an easy dinner.
