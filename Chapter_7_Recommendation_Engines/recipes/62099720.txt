Title:
Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Mini Bundt Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Sugar
1 1/2 Cups Oil
4 Eggs
1 16oz Can Pumpkin
3 Cups Flour
2 tsp Baking Powder
2 tsp Baking Soda
3 tsp Salt
1 tsp Ginger Powder
2 tsp Vanilla
1 Cup of Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325*
Beat sugar, oil, and vanilla until well blended then add pumpkin and beat until well blended into mixture. Stir dry ingredients together in seperate bowl. Add dry ingredients into pumpkin mixture blending well. Add chocolate chips. Pour batter into well oiled mini bundt pan. Bake 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mrs. T
Description: 
This family favorite dessert is the perfect conclusion to one todays favorite holidays. Add this dessert to your family dinner anytime of the year or enjoy with a favorite beverage. The combination of pumpkin and chocolate adds a delicate yet tantalizing sweet twist. Top off with ice cream or whip cream.
