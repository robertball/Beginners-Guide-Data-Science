Title:
Sweet Pumpkin Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese (room temperature, lightly beatened)
1 cup brown sugar
2 cups pumpkin puree
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ginger
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
1. Cream the cream cheese.
2. Mix in the sugar followed by the spices and then the pumpkin puree.
3. Cover and chill overnight in the fridge.

Submitter: 
The Viking Bunny
Description: 
Tastes very much like a no-cook pumpkin cheesecake!
