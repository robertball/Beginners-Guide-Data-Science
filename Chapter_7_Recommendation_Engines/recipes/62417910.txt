Title:
Rice Cooker Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442782.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Macaroni of your choice
1 can Evaporated Milk (12 oz)
1 1/2 Cups Chicken Broth
3/4 Cup Shreaded Cheddar Cheese
1/2 Cup Shreaded Mozzarella Cheese
1/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
1/2 tsp Kosher Salt or to taste

Directions:
Combine noodles. broth and evaporated milk in the rice cooker.  Turn to cook.  When machine switches to warm open lid and stir.
Add the cheeses and stir thoroughly.  Close lid and switch to cook.
When the cooker switches back to warm the mac & cheese is done.
The bottom will be brown and crispy.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This is a great way to have baked Mac & Cheese without turning on the oven.  Use what ever type of pasta you like.  If you are using a 10 Cups rice cooker this recipe can easily be doubled or tripled.
