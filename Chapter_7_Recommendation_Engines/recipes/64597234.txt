Title:
Sriracha Honey Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3885039.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Brussel Sprouts
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
1 Teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
1 Tablespoon Honey
Â½ Tablespoon Sriracha
1 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
1 Teaspoon Fresh Cracked Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Wash Brussel sprouts and remove any discolored outer leaves.
Cut stem off brussel sprouts, then cut in half.
In a medium bowl, toss brussel sprouts with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
Spray baking sheeting with non-fat cooking spray and place brussel sprouts on pan in a single layer.
Roast brussel sprouts in the oven for 25 minutes until they are golden brown and fork tender.
While the brussel sprouts are roasting, in a small bowl, mix honey and Sriracha until well combined.
Once brussel sprouts are done roasting, transfer to a medium bowl, drizzle Sriracha Honey sauce and toss to coat.
Top with a drizzle of sriracha if desired.

Healthy and full of flavor Sriracha Honey Brussel Sprouts are crispy and coated with a delicious sweet and spicy sauce.  Perfect side dish for any meal!
