Title:
Cran Oat Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319104.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, softened
2 snack-size tubs applesauce
1 cup packed dark brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1-3/4 cup wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt (optional)
3 cups oats
1 cup dried cranberries
3/4 cup white chocolate chips

Directions:
OVEN 350
Beat butter, apple sauce, eggs, vanilla, and brown sugar until smooth.
In separate bowl, combine wheat flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.  Add to egg mixture (a little at a time), mix well.
Stir in oats, dried cranberries, and white chocolate chips.
Drop oversized spoonfuls onto greased (lightly sprayed with healthy oil is perfect) cookie sheet.
Bake for 15 minutes - Remove to wire rack.  Cool.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
VenturaMama77
Description: 
A healthier version of oatmeal cookies with tasty dried cranberries and white chocolate chips.  Great for picnics and camping - kids love 'em too.
