Title:
Shana's Candied Yam Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter-softened
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup pecans-chopped
2 lg. cans yams or sweet potatoes-drained and mashed
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 jar marshmallow cream

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except marshmallow cream. Spread mixture into a casserole or baking dish.
Top with marshmallow cream and back on 325-350 until top is golden brown.

Submitter: 
shana dominguez
Description: 
This is a great addition to your Thanksgiving or Christmas dinner. Or you can eat it anytime you have a craving for something delicious!
