Title:
Spinach & Tortellini Layered Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package cheese tortellini, refrigerated
2 cups red cabbage, shredded
1 (6 oz.) bag baby spinach leaves, divided
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved (or reg. tomato)
1/2 cup green onion, sliced
1 (8 ounce) bottle ranch dressing
8 slices bacon, cooked & crumpled

Directions:
Cook tortellini, drain and rinse with cold water.
Layer in dish: half spinach, tortellini, cabbage, tomatoes, green onions, spinach.
3Pour dressing over top, Cover, refrigerate at least 3 hrs.  top with bacon toss before serving
