Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound ground hamburger
1 small onion-diced
1 six-ounce can tomato paste
1 can tomato soup
1 can chicken gumbo soup
1 1/4 Cup water
2 Tablespoons brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 teaspoon mustar
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Brown hamburger and onion. Drain and return to pan. Add all other ingredients. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 30-40 mins. or put in crock-pot.
