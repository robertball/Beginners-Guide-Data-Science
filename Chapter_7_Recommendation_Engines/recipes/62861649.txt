Title:
Ginger Snaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 egg
4 Tbsp. molasses
1 tsp. ginger
1 tsp. cloves
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 tsp. baking soda
2 cups flour

Directions:
Mix. Cool in refrigerator. Roll into balls – do not flatten. Roll in sugar.
Bake at 350F for 10-12 minutes.
