Title:
Warm Crab dip in a Bread Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Round Crusted Breads
2 cups Sour Cream
6-8 Green Onions chopped (optional)
4 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
16 oz. Cream Cheese (softened)
2 packages Imitation Crab
1 can Water Chestnuts (diced)

Directions:
Oven 350 for aprox. 1 1/2 hours minimum
Place softened cream cheese in a bowl and set on counter from very beginning.
Prepare breads by cutting top off in a circle. Save all bread pieces for dipping later. Press down inside of bread to make a "well". Do this to both and set a side. In a strainer open packages of crab and wash and break apart pieces. Smaller the better. In second bowl combine sour cream, chopped onion (suggestion: make 1 with onion 1 with out- helpful with large company) water chestnuts, and slowly crab to mixture. Place cream cheese and cheddar in and blend well. If you would like you can also add Garlic salt to taste. Fill bowls with dip and place top of bread back on and wrap in foil and  place on cookie sheet in middle oven you might have to cook one at a time if they don't fit together or save one for later and freeze it.

Submitter: 
Giamarie Saponaro
Description: 
This is the best Crab dip ever! It is a Hit at all parties and makes a good amount but, don't worry you won't be stuck with left-overs I promise! Made with Imitation Crab, Cream cheese & Cheddar... Enjoy!!
