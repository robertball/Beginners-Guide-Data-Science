Title:
funeral potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz hashbrowns
16 oz sour cream
1 can cream of chicken
green onions or regular
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 1/2 cups of cheese
1/2 c melted butter
1/4 c melted butter
2 c crushed corn flakes

Directions:
combine everything except 1/4 C butter and corn flakes. spread into 9 x 13 dish. Mix remaining butter and corn flakes and sprinkle on top. Bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes.
