Title:
Cucumber Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large ripe avocado
2 Medium cucumber, seeded
1 Medium Tomato
1 tsp cayenne
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
2 fresh lime

Directions:
Cut the avocado, cucumber and tomato into bite size pieces and put in a large bowl.
In a smaller bowl combine the cayenne, garlic powder and onion powder with the juice from the limes.  Stir to mix well.
Pour the seasoned lime juice over the avocado, cucumber mixture and toss lightly to coat each piece.
Cover and refrigerate at least an hour for flavors to meld.
