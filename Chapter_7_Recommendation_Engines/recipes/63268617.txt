Title:
Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. cracker crumbs
1 c. milk
1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
2 eggs
1/4 c. grated onion
1 t. salt
1/8 t. pepper
1/2 t. sage

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.
Shape into a loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
