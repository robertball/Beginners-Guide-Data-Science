Title:
Vegan Savory Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegan Savory Sauce
1/2  lb. firm or extra firm tofu (not silken)
3/4  cup vegetable broth or water
1/4  cup canola oil
2    Tablspoons soy sauce
2    Tablespoons nutritional yeast flakes
2    Tablespoons fresh chopped basil or  1 tsp. dried basil
2    Tablespoons lemon juice
1    teaspoon Dijon mustard
2    cloves of garlic minced

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a blender or food processor and blend.

Submitter: 
DragonShoes
Description: 
This sauce is good as a dip or warm it up and use as a sauce for rice, baked potatoes or even a pasta toss.
