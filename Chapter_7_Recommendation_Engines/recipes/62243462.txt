Title:
Brown Sugar Soy Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 cup soy sauce
3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger OR 1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/4 cup canola oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except canola oil in a blender. Place lid on blender and pulse to combine. With blender running, pour thin steady stream of oil into blender. store in airtight container in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
To use, brush on grilled seafood, poultry or meat during last 4 or 5 minutes of cook time, then brush again just before removing food from grill.

Submitter: 
Gregg Hollander
Description: 
Great tasting, simple-to-make glaze perfect to brush on grilled food. Based on a recipe from the Norwegian Seafood Export Council (seafoodfromnorway.com). We've  brushed this on grilled salmon, chicken and pork and loved the results.
