Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/817326.jpg
Ingredients:
16 slices of bread
I large slice of ham or 1 lb sausage cooked
6 eggs
1/2 tsp each salt , pepper, dry mustard
1/4 C chopped onion
1/2 C chopped green pepper
1-2 tsp Worchester sauce
3 C milk
1/2 C butter (oleo)
1 C crushed potato chips

Directions:
Remove crusts from bread and place in greased 9 by 13 pan, cut up ham slice put on top of bread
top with cheese
Beat eggs add seasonings
pour over above layers
top with 8 more slices of bread
pour milk over all
let stand over night in fridge,
melt butter pour over and sprinkle with crushed potato
chips
Bake at 350 for at least 1 hour
