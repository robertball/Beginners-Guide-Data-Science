Title:
Trail Mix for Kids Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups small pretzel twists
2 cups unsalted nuts or sunflower seeds
2 cups dried fruit (such as raisins or dried cranberries)
4 cups whole grain cereals (such as Cheerios or Chex)
Small bag of M&M;'s

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a large bowl.
Mix together with clean hands.
Store in plastic container or bag.
