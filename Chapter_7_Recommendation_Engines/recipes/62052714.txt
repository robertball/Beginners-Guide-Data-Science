Title:
Southern Fried Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 medium onion, peeled, and chopped
1 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
1/3 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Place oil in a skillet on stove til hot, but not smoking.  Add potatoes, onions, and salt.  Cook until tender yet golden, stirring occasionally as not to burn or stick..YUM!

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
This is the way my Grandmother and my mother used to fry potatoes.  Everyone that has them loves them.  Excellent served with butter beans, and corn bread.
