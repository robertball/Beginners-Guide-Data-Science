Title:
MDK Smokey Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Black beans
1 strip bacon
shallot
dash of cinnamon
half teaspoon brown sugar
salt

Directions:
1) Cook the beans
2) dice the raw bacon
3) In a frying pan, cook the bacon until almost done.
4) add a full shallot (diced)
5) cook shallot halfway
6) add beans
7) add brown sugar and cinnamon
8) cook for 2-5 minutes on med-low.
