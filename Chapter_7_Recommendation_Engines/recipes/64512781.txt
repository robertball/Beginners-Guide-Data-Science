Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2765618.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Oreo cookie crumbs (about 24 cookies)
1/3 cup butter, melted
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
3 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
1 teaspoon all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350° F.
Mix Oreo cookie crumbs and melted butter.  Press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
Beat softened cream cheese until smooth.
Gradually add sweetened condensed milk, mix well.
Add eggs, one at a time, and beat until smooth.
Stir in vanilla extract.
Toss 1/2 cup of the mini chocolate chips with the flour and toss to coat.  Stir into the cream cheese mixture.
Pour ibatter nto prepared crust.
Sprinkle top with remaining chocolate chips
Bake at 350° F for 55 minutes until top is golden brown, and center of cheesecake batter is set.
Remove and cool on a cooling rack until cool enough that cheesecake pulls away from sides of springform pan.  Remove sides of springform pan.
Continue cooling on rack until cooled.
Refrigerate for 4 hours.  Slice and serve!  Refrigerate leftovers
