Title:
Zucchini Chocolate Oatmeal Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3586490.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup zucchini, shredded
1 1/4 cups Gluten Free oats
3 tbsp honey
1/2 cup almond butter
3 tbsp flax meal
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup dairy free mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Using a food processor or blender, roughly shred zucchini.
To a medium bowl, add zucchini with the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
Divide batter and roll into 18 bites and place in a storage container.
Place the storage container with the bites in the refrigerator an hour to set before indulging.

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Zucchini Chocolate Oatmeal Bites make a stunning homemade snack. Made with shredded zucchini, oats and nut butter, these no-bake gluten free, low calorie bites will soon be your new easy go-to summer snack!
