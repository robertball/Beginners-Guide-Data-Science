Title:
vegetarian mexican soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz can black beans
1 8 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 large onion chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 bay leaves
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 cup frozen vegetables( fresh if you like)
salt to taste
8 cups water
cilantro, lime wedges, sour cream and shredded cheese for garnish.

Directions:
Place all the ingrediets in the crock pot and slow cook for 6 hours.  Garnish.
