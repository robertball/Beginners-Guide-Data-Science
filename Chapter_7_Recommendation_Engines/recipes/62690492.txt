Title:
Baked Doughnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Bisquick
2 Tablespoons sugar
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
1/3 cup milk
1 egg
For coating:
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Mix thoroughly Bisquick, sugar, cinnamon, milk and egg.  Beat vigorously 20 strokes.
Knead dough 5 times on floured board.  Roll 1/2 inch thick and cut with floured doughnut cutter.
Bake at 400 degrees for 7-9 minutes.  Immediately after baking, dip each doughnut into the melted butter, then into sugar.

Submitter: 
JESSICALKING
Description: 
These easy baked doughnuts are great when you need a quick sweet treat or a breakfast for kids.
