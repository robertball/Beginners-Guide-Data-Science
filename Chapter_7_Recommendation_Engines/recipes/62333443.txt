Title:
KayLove's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup milk
2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cracked pepper
2 tsp garlic powder
1 pack of french onion soup mix
1/4 tsp ginger
3/4 cup crispy french onions
3/4 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 and spray 9X5 loaf pan with cooking spray
put ketchup and brown sugar in bottom of pan
mix all other ingredients in a large bowl then place on top of ketchup/brown sugar
Bake in oven for an hour, then enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kay
Description: 
A family favorite! The french onions really make this super yummy! excellent paired with mashed potatoes and green beans with bacon.
