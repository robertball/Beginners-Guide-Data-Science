Title:
Alexander's Sweet Surprise Cubes Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups Rice Krispies Cereal
4 Cups any type of chocolate chips

Directions:
1. In a large bowl melt chocolate in the microwave at 50% power, stirring every 30 seconds until melted.
2. Once the chocolate is melted, stir in the Rice Krispies. Stir until mixed together well.
3. Pour mixture into each slot inside ice cube tray.
4. Freeze the chocolate cube.
5. Every 32 minutes check if the chocolate cubes are solid.
6. When the chocolate cubes are solid, eat up while they are cold.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe is as simple as getting a drink of water from a cup!
