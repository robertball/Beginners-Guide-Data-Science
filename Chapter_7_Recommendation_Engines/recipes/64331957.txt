Title:
Single Serving Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 Cup Half and Half
1/4 Cup Chocolate Chips
6oz Milk
Sugar, to taste
Vanilla, to taste

Directions:
1. Heat chocolate chips and half and half in the microwave for 30 seconds at a time. Heat and stir the mixture until it is thick and there are no small bits of chocolate left.
2. In a separate cup heat the milk in the microwave, about 2 minutes.
3. Slowly pour the milk into the chocolate mixture while stirring.

Submitter:

Description: 
I like to use semisweet chocolate chips and top with whipped cream.
