Title:
Garbage Burritos aka Camping Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 flour tortillas
6 eggs
1 (14 oz) can diced potatoes, drained
2-3 T. butter
1/2 to 1 cup shredded cheese (mild cheddar, colby/jack blend, jack/cheddar blend, etc.)
1/2-1 cup Crumbled sausage or 6-8 pieces bacon leftovers
Salsa, to taste
1/4 cup Diced onion and/or bell pepper (optional)

Directions:
Place cast iron skillet on grill and heat.
Add butter and diced potatoes.
Cook for about 5-10 minutes, stirring every so often.
Stir in crumbled sausage or bacon.
Cook until meat starts to warm.
Add eggs, scrambling them into the potato and meat mixture.
Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until eggs are cooked thoroughly.
Warm tortillas on grill and add approximately 1/2 cup of egg mixture to each one.
Sprinkle with shredded cheese and add salsa if desired prior to folding.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
These are great for the last morning of a camping trip.  It's a perfect way to use up leftovers so you don't have to cart a ton of stuff home!
