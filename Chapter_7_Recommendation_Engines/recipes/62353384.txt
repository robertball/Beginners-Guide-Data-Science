Title:
Hawaian Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/458509.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 3/4 cups sugar
2 cups All Purpose flour
2 eggs
2 tsp. baking soda
1 20oz. can crushed pineapple with juice
1/2 cup coconut
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
Topping:
1 stick butter (softened)
1 8oz. pkg. cream cheese (softened)
1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
For cake:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease and flour 9 X 13 pan.
Mix all ingredients by hand with a wooden spoon until well blended.
Pour into prepared pan and bake for 45 min. or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool cake completely.
For topping:
Beat all ingredients with electric mixer until smooth.
Spread on cake.
Store cake in refridgerator until ready to serve.
