Title:
Montezuma's Revenge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 small flour tortillas
2 1/2 cups sour cream
1 large bag of chocolate chips
1 small bag chocolate chips
maraschino cherries
1 small square dark chocolate for grating

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips in saucepan.  Stir in 1 1/2 cups sour cream.  Place 1 tortilla on plate and spread thin layer of chocolate mixture, being sure to cover completely.  Alternate tortillas with chocolate, ending with chocolate.  Top with remaining sour cream.  Refrigerate at least 24 hours before serving.  Garnish with grated chocolate and maraschino cheries with stems, cut into small pieces to serve.

Submitter: 
Nurse Ellen so not a
Description: 
This is a fun dessert to make with your kids that includes tortillas, sour cream and yummy chocolate!! Enjoy!
