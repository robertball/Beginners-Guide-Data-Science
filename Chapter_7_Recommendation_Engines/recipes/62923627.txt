Title:
Jelly Doughnut Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713740.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup plus 3 tbsp warm(95-105 F) milk, divided
1 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1 tbsp butter, softened
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup raspberry jam
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine 3 tbsp warm milk and yeast in large bowl of electric mixer. Stir; let stand 5 minutes. Add granulated sugar, butter and remaining 1/2 cup milk; mix well. Add flour, egg and salt; beat with dough hook at medium speed until dough starts to climb up dough hook. If dough is too sticky, add additional flour 1 tbsp at a time.
Transfer dough to greased medium bowl; turn dough over to grease top. Cover bowl; let stand in warm place 1 hour.
Grease 48 mini muffin cups. Punch down dough. Pull of pieces of dough. Shape into 1-inch balls; place in prepared muffin cups. Cover; let stand 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 375 F. Uncover, bake 10-12 minutes or until light golden brown. Remove to wire rack; cool completely.
Place jam in piping bag fitted with round tip. Insert tip into side of doughnut; squeeze about 1 tsp jam into center. Dust filled doughnuts with powdered sugar.
