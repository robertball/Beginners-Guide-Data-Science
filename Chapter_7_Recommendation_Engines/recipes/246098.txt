Title:
Orange Juice Goji Berries Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3315176.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
1 tablespoon goji berries
2 slices fresh ginger

Directions:
Blend orange juice, goji berries, and ginger together in a high-powered blender until smooth and goji berries are pureed.
