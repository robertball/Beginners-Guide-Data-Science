Title:
Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans diced tomatoes
1 or 2 tomatoes, or more
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 clove garlic
5 or 6 stems of cilantro leaves
1/4 red onion
1 jalapeno
2 T vinegar
1/4 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Use food processor to gently chop canned tomatoes and fresh tomatoes.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe comes from my daughter's high school Culinary Arts class.  Add more jalapeno, onion, cilantro, garlic to taste.
