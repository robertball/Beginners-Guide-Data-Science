Title:
Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074453.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups quinoa
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp oregano
1 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
8-10 grinds fresh ground pepper
1/2 English cucumber de-seeded and chopped
1/2 green pepper chopped
1 med tomato de-seeded and chopped
1/2 cup sliced kalamata olives drained
3-4 oz crumbled feta cheese
Optional avocado sprinkled with sea salt and cayenne pepper

Directions:
Rinse quinoa and drain as much as possible. Bring quinoa and chicken broth to a boil than reduce heat and simmer 10-12 minutes till cooked.
Dressing: mix olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, garlic powder, onion powder, crushed red pepper and ground black pepper. Set aside.
Once quinoa has cooled completely add all the chopped vegetables and olives. Pour on dressing and stir. Gently fold in feta cheese. When serving top with a couple pieces of the prepared avocado.
