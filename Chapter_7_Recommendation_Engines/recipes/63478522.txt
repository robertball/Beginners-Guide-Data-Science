Title:
Skinny Peppermint Mocha Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/948435.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. coffee
2 drops peppermint extract
1/3 cup non-fat milk
2 tsp dark chocolate syrup
2 tsp Sugar free chocolate syrup

Directions:
Brew coffee of your choice and pour into a mug.Stir in syrup,peppermint and milk to your liking.

Submitter: 
HeLeJe
Description: 
Slash calories by replacing sugary syrup with peppermint extract and skip the whipped cream for a hot tasty peppermint mocha that is diet friendly!
