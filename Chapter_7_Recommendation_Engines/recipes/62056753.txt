Title:
Zucchini-Vegetable Sautee with Cheddar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 medium zucchini, sliced into 1/2 inch rounds
-1 medium tomato, large dice
-1 small onion, sliced into strips
-1 glove garlic, minced
-1 cup frozen corn, thawed
-1 tsp chicken bullion granules
-generous dash black pepper
-about 1/2-3/4 cup grated cheddar

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat, sautee onion until slightly tender, about 5 min. Add in remaining ingredients, except cheese, and sautee over med-low heat until zucchini is crisp-tender. Add cheese and stir until melted.

Submitter: 
DARLA
Description: 
A simple and quick side dish of sauteed veggies topped with melted cheese.  A good way to sneak in the zucchini for picky eaters. This is one of those throw together recipes that just asks for additions. Use whatever's on hand.
