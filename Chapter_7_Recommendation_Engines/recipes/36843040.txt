Title:
Sour Cream Noodle Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2# broad egg noodles
1# ground beef
1T butter
1C cottage cheese
1C sour cream
1T salt, 1/4t. garlic salt, 1/8t pepper
8 oz can tomato sauce
6 green onions, chopped
3/4C shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook noodles as directed on pkg.;rinse and drain
Brown beef in butter. Add salt, garlic salt, pepper, and tomato sauce.
Simmer 5 minutes.
Mix noodles, cottage cheese, sour cream, and onion together.
Alternate layer of noodle mixture and meat in greased 2qt. casserole, beginning with noodles and ending with meat.
Top with cheese
Bake 350 for 20 min. or until cheese melts and browns
