Title:
Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1/2 c. dry bread crumbs
1 large egg, lightly beaten
2 tbls Worcestershire sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 8oz package sliced baby bella mushrooms
2 tbls all purpose flour
1 101/2 oz can condensed french onion soup
1 10 1/2 oz can beef consomme'

Directions:
combine beef, bread crumbs, egg, Worcestershire sauce,garlic, pepper and salt. Formmixture into 6 (4-inch) patties. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add patties anc ook 7-8 mins per side until browned. Set aside patties. Reserve 2 tablespoons drippings in skillet. Add mushrooms. and saute for 1 - 2 mins. until tender. Add flour and onion soup. Cook, stiring constatly 2 mins. Stir in consomme'. Return patties to skillet and bring to a boil. Simmer uncovered 30 - 40 mins until patties are cooked through & sauce is thickened.
