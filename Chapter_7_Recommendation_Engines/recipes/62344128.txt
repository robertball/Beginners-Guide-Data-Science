Title:
Soft & Chewy Banana Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C Butter
1 C Brown Sugar OR Honey
1/2 C Applesauce
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
1/2 tsp Almond Extract (lemon can also be used)
4 Bananas, Mashed
2 Tbs Cornstarch (potato can also be used)
2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 C Whole Wheat Flour

Directions:
In first bowl cream together butter and sugar/honey.  Add applesauce, bananas, and extracts.
In second bowl mix together, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, and flour.
Slowly add dry mix to wet and blend thoroughly.
Drop rounded tablespoons onto GREASED cookie sheet (a light spritz with cooking spray works great!) Bake at 350 degrees for 14-16 minutes.
Let cool slightly, and then remove and enjoy with a cold glass of milk!

Submitter: 
venymae
Description: 
Cake like banana cookie with a chewy side.  Easy, fun, & pretty healthy!  Best of all, this recipe requires no eggs. Stores pretty well and not messy to eat. Also I guess it could qualify as vegan (if you use margarine instead of butter).
