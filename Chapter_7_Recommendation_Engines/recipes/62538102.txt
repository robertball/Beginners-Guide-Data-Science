Title:
Nana's Fudge Pie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522137.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Stick Butter
2 Squares Chocolate
3 Eggs
1 1/4 cups sugar
1/3 cup flour
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Melt butter and chocolate together in double boiler (or microwave).
Add sugar.
Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.
Add pre-sifted flour and vanilla.
Bake in a well greased 8-9 inch pie pan in 325 oven about 30 minutes.
Test as any cake (with toothpick that comes clean)
Cut in pie shaped pieces and serve with ice cream.

Submitter: 
MSLIB5
Description: 
This really isn't a pie or a cake. It is more like a super rich most brownie that is sliced like a pie. Handed down from my grandmother. Serve with vanilla ice cream.
