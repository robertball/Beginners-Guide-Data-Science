Title:
Blue Cheese Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
1/3 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 cup parsley, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1/2 cup red onion (or green), chopped
1 dash black pepper

Directions:
Sprinkle cheese into a shallow dish.  Mix together garlic and olive oil and drizzle over cheese.  Combine remaining ingredients and pour over the cheese.  Refrigerate for 1 hour.  Sprinkle with pepper.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
Great on tart apples or crackers!  Or try as a topper on steak!
