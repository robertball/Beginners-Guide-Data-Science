Title:
Chicken ala can can Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1Can cream of chicken soup
1Can cream of celery soup
1Can water
1Can 12oz deboned chicken
1Can minute rice
1Can 3 1/2 refired onions

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except onions. Put into 13x9 pan and top with onions, Then Bake at 350 for 30 to 45 min.
