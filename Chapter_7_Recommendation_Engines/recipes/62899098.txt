Title:
Chunky Guacamole with Serrano Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe avocados, cut into 1/2" dice
3 medium ripe tomatoes, cut into 1/2" dice
1 serrano pepper, finely minced (optional)
3/4 cup red onion, chopped
1/2 bunch of fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
2 tsp minced garlic
Juice from 1/2 lime
1/2 tsp chili powder
2 tsp kosher salt; or to taste

Directions:
Place all the ingredients except salt in a bowl. Stir gently until well combined. Add salt to taste
Chill and Serve.
