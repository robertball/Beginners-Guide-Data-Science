Title:
Healing Cabbage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 1/2 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 C chopped carrotes
1 stalk celery diced
2 quarts water
1 beef bouillon cube
1 envelope onion soup mix
1/2 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
1 small head cabbage, cored and coarsely chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

Directions:
In a large stockpot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Stir in onion, carrots, celery and garlic; cook until onion is transparent, about 5 minutes.
Stir in water, bouillon, onion soup mix and one bay leaf. Bring to a boil, then stir in cabbage. Simmer until cabbage wilts, about 10 minutes.
Stir in tomatoes. Return to a boil, then simmer 15 to 30 minutes, stirring often.
