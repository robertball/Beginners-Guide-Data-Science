Title:
Italian Wedding Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 c, seasoned bread crumbs
1 lb ground beef
3/4 lb bulk sausage (sweet)
3 medium carrots slice
3 celery ribs diced
3 cloves garlic minced
4&1/2 Tsp of olive oil
4 cans (14.5oz) low sodium chicken broth
2 cans(14.5oz) low sodium beef broth
1 pkg(10oz) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry. (I use about 1/2 pkg)
1/4 cup minced fresh basil
1 envelope onion soup mix
4&1/2 tsp of ketchup
1/2 tspdried thyme
3 bay leaves
1& 1/2 cup cooked penne pasta

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the eggs and bread crumbs, crumble the beef & sausage over mixture, mix well. Shape into 1/2 inch meatballs.  Place meatballs on a greased rack in a foil lined baking pain.  Bake @ 350 degrees for 15-18" or until no longer pink.
Meanwhile in a stock-pot saute the carrots, celery & garlic, in olive oil until tender.
Stir in the broth, spinach, basil, thyme, onion soup  mix & ketchup, and bay leaves.
Drain meatballs on paper towels.
Bring soup to a boil, add meatballs, reduce the heat and simmer for 30" uncovered.
Cook the pasta, and add before serving.
Discard the bay leaves before serving.

Submitter: 
Joanne Dembinski
Description: 
This is one of my favorite soup recipes. Takes a bit of time to make, but worth every minute of it. Enjoy!
