Title:
Jae's Almost Too Tender Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 pounds of stewing beef cut into chunks, 3 large potatoes cut into wedges, 1 large white onion sliced thin, 4 stalks of celery chopped, 1 can onion flavored beef broth, 1/2 cup white wine, 1/4 cup A-1 steak sauce, 1/8 cup Worcestershire sauce, 1 1/4 cups water, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, oregano and parsley

Directions:
In a crockpot (slow cooker) combine wine, steak sauce, worcestershire sauce, and water.  Add beef and cut vegetables.  Top with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, parsley and oregano to taste.
Cook on low for 7-8 hours or until tender.
