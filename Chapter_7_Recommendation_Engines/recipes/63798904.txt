Title:
Classic Swiss Fonddue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1053913.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 pound of grated Gruyere
3/4 pound of grated Swiss
1  tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 cup dry white wine
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
1 Tablespoon kirsch liqueur
1 pinch Freshly grated nutmeg
1 pinch ground pepper

Directions:
Mix cheeses together in mixing bowl with flour. Rub a clove of garlic into the pan. Place wine, kirsch liqueur, and lemon juice to saucepan and bring to a simmer, being careful not to let it come to a boil. Gradually add cheese to wine, adding a handful at a time and stirring constantly until melted. After the last handful has melted, add freshly grated nutmeg, pepper to taste slowly stirring to incorporate until you have a creamy cheese sauce. Transfer to fondue pot. Serve with crusty french bread cut into 1 inch pieces.
