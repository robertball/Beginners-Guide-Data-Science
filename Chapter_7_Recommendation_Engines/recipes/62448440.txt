Title:
Meldrum’s Chocolate Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2-3 Tbsp. cocoa
¼ cup melted butter
¾ cup milk
¼ cup corn syrup
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Boil above ingredients for 2 minutes. Add 1 tsp. of vanilla after boiled.
