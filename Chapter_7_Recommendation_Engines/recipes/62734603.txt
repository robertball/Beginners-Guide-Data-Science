Title:
PEPPER ROLLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp.salt
3 eggs
2 cups warm water
1 tsp.baking powder
5-6 cups flour
1/4 cup marg.
4-6 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Start with 5 cups flour,add baking powder,make a well in the center,add salt,eggs and water.Add more flour if needed.Roll out on floured counter,spread marg.over dough.Sprinkle salt and pepper over dough.At one end roll dough just like making cinnamon buns.Once rolled up cut into sections and place in roaster.pour broth over pepper rolls till just covered.Bake at 350 for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
A Pasta type dough rolled cinnamon bun style,baked in chicken broth.
