Title:
Coconut-Lime Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 8oz bricks of cream cheese
4 large eggs
1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
zest of 1 lime
1 cup of shredded coconut
1 cup of sugar, or to taste
splash of Bacardí Limón rum
splash of Bacardí Coco rum
1 pack of honey grahams
6 tbsp melted butter for crust
1/3 c shredded coconut for crust
1/4 sugar for crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Prepare crust: Crush crackers, mix with melted butter, coconut and sugar, press against sides of springform pan.
Mix all of the other ingredients, pour in pan and bake until done.
