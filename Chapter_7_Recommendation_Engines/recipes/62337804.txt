Title:
Chocolate Chip Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	1 and 3/4 cup boiling water
•	1 cup uncooked oatmeal
•	1 cup brown sugar
•	1 cup white sugar
•	1 stick of butter
•	1 bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips (12 oz)
•	2 eggs
•	1 and 3/4 cup flour
•	1 tsp soda
•	1/2 tsp baking soda
•	1/2 tsp salt
•	1 tbsp cocoa

Directions:
•	Mix oatmeal and water
•	Let stand for 10 minutes
•	Add sugars and butter stirring till butter melts
•	Add rest of ingredients and 1/2 of the chocolate chips
•	Mix
•	Put in prepared 9x13 pan
•	Sprinkle rest of chips on top
•	Bake at 350 for 40 minutes
