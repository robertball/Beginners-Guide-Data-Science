Title:
Marie's Healthy Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2225094.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup spinach, or more to taste
1 frozen banana, cut into chunks
1/2 small avocado, peeled and pitted
1/4 cup almond milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Blend spinach, banana, avocado, almond milk, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
The avocado makes this smoothie almost more rich in texture than a milkshake. What a healthy and satisfying breakfast!
