Title:
Thai Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbs or 8 bags Thai spiced tea
2 quarts water
sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Combine the tea and water in a container and store in the refridgerator for at least 3 hours.
Once the tea is steeped, pour into glasses, straining if necissary. For every 8 oz of tea stir in 1 tablespoon of sweetened condensed milk.

Submitter: 
GODGIFU
Description: 
Thai iced tea, like you get in restaurants. The thai tea is a particular blend you can find in most oriental markets or well stocked tea sections in a grocery store.
