Title:
Chicken and Broccoli, With Pasta, Olives, White Wine, And Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695603.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Oz Chicken, 3/4 to 1 inch cubed
3 Cups of broccoli
1 Cup of penne pasta
4 Oz white wine
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 Oz of Parmesan cheese
2.25 oz of sliced or whole black olives

Directions:
Cook pasta in large pot with boiling salted water until al dente, about 11 mins. Drain.
While the water is coming up to a boil, Pull off all chicken fat, and skin, cut out any bones And cube the chicken. You can use breast meat or dark meat.
In a medium skillet over medium High heat brown chicken fat, any bones, And skin allow the chicken oil to render out. When browned, and oil rendered out You Should Have About 2 Tablespoons Of Oil In The Pan (Remove Excess Oil), With Some Browned Bits In The Pan. About 5-10 mins. remove all bones, and solid chicken fat, and skin.
In a medium skillet over medium High heat, brown chicken cubes, about 5 mins
Stir broccoli into chicken, and add white wine, salt, pepper, cover, 5 Mins
Stir in pasta, olives, into chicken mixture, Cover, 5 mins
Using a slotted spoon plate
sprinkle on freshly grated cheese.
