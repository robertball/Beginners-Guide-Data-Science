Title:
Mini Apple Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 granny smith apples
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
6 Tablespoons butter
1 can Grands Original jumbo biscuits

Directions:
Peel and dice apples.  In saucepan, add apples, 4 Tablespoons butter, sugar and cinnamon.  Bring to boil then cover and simmer on low heat.  Uncover after 30 minutes and simmer until thickens and apples are soft.  Mash with fork to form a thick applesauce.
On nonstick surface or floured board, roll biscuits as flat as possible in a circle.  On each round, place 1 1/2 heaping tablespoons of mixture.  Fold over and press edges with fork.
In a cast iron skillet or stoneware pan, melt a pad of butter in oven at 400 degrees.  When melted, Coat the pie on both sides with melted butter and bake 5 minutes.  Turn pie over once and continue to brown an additional 5 minutes. Let cool on paper towels.

Submitter: 
Bonnie Lang Turnage-Mortgage O
Description: 
This is the non fried version of fried apple pies.  It's an easy but great recipe passed on to me by my husband's family. Very good with less guilt than the deep fried version!
