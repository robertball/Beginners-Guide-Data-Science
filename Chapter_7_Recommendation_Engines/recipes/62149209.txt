Title:
Mom's Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
No-Roll Bottom Crust:
1-1/2c. Flour
1tsp Sugar
1/2tsp. Salt
2tbs. Milk
1/2c. Vegetable Oil
Filling:
1/2c. Sugar
1/4c. Flour
1tsp. Cinnamon
4-1/2c. Peaches peeled and sliced (or drained canned peaches)
Top Crumb Crust
1/2c. Softened Butter
1/2c. Brown Sugar
1c. Flour

Directions:
Prepare crust by mixing all ingredients directly in the 9" pie pan. Pat dough to fit pan.
Heat oven to 425F
Mix sugar, flour, and cinnamon. Then gently mix in to peaches. Pour into pan.
Mix all ingredients for top crust in a medium bowl until crumbly and crumble over the top of the filling.
Cover edge of crust with foil to prevent burning.
Bake 35-40 min.
Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.

Submitter: 
Lindsay Thorpe
Description: 
This was my favorite thing my mom used to make when I was a kid. I could sit and eat the whole thing by myself if left to it!
