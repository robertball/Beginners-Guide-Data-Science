Title:
yummy fried rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/440693.jpg
Ingredients:
boiled rice
*1 cup white rice
*2/3 cup water
*1/4 tsp butter
*1/2 tsp salt
for frying
*1-1/2 tbsp cooking oil
*1 large egg
*2 tbsp soy sauce

Directions:
1. In a medium sized pot put all ingredients from the boiled rice section into it.
2. turn on high and wait for boil
3. once boiled place lid on pot and turn to low. let sit 20 minutes
(If the rice is not mooshy looking its done if not wait another 5 minutes and check again)
4. get a large frying pan and turn on medium and cover the bottom with half the oil
5.put the cooked rice and egg into the pan
6.stir occasionally and let cook for about 7 minutes or until egg starts to turn yellow.
7.pour in soy sauce and cook another 5 minutes
8. turn off heat and enjoy
*if rice isn't salty enough mix in a little more soy sauce per serving instead of in the whole thing of rice.
