Title:
Rolo Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
3/4 cup cocoa
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup sugar
1 cup margarine
2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 eggs
75 chocolate covered caramel candies(such as Rolos"
4oz. white chocolate

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350(F).
Combine flour, cocoa, and baking soda well.  Cream together the brown sugar, white sugar, and margarine.  Add vanilla and eggs.  Add flour mixture, and mix until blended.
Surround each Rolo candy with 1 1/2-2 tsp. of dough.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake for 7-8 minutes.  DO NOT OVERBAKE! Cool completley.
Melt white chocolate in microwave or small saucepan.  Using the tip of a spoon, drizzle some over each cookie.  Make sure the chocolate has completley hardened before placing in a tin or container.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chocolatey dough surrounds a chocolate covered caramel candy in these soft, chewy cookies.  They are drizzled with white chocolate to make them a bit more interesting.  Everyone will love these, especially kids!
