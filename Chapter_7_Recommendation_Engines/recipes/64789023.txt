Title:
The Skinny Deep Sea Diver's Mermaid Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8030055.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz overproof rum
1 oz dark rum
3/4 oz coconut rum
3/4 oz triple sec OR 2-3 drops wild orange essential oil (food grade)
1/4 oz sugar free lime syrup
1 oz lime juice
1/8 tsp powdered stevia sweetener
club soda to taste
sliced lime for garnish

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with ice in cocktail shaker. Pour into glass, add club soda as desired. Garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Angie Fuller
Description: 
A fun, lighter twist on a cocktail first made popular at a Fort Lauderdale landmark
