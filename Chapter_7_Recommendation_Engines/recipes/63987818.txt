Title:
Sesame Crusted Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
salt
3/4 cup sesame seeds
4 (6-8 oz) salmon steaks
2 scallions sliced
1 Tbsp grated lemon zest
2 tsp lemon juice
4 tsp tahini
2 tsp grated fresh ginger
1/8 tsp cayenne
1 tsp oil

Directions:
Oven 325.
Dissolve 5 Tbsp salt in 2 quarts water. Transfer 1 cup brine to bowl, stir in sesame seeds and let stand at room temp for 5 minutes. Submerge salmon in remaining brine & let sit for 15 minutes.
Drain seeds and place in a 12 in skillet. Cook seeds over med heat stirring constantly for 2-4 min until golden brown. Transfer seeds to pie plate and wipe out skillet. Remove salmon from brine & pat dry.
Place scallion white & lemon zest on cutting board & chop until whites & zest are finely minced & combined. Mix with lemon juice, tahini, ginger, cayenne, & 1/8 tsp salt.
Evenly distribute half of paste over skinned sides of filet. Press coated sides in seeds and transfer, seed side down to plate. Evenly distribute remaining paste & seeds over tops of fillets.
Heat oil in skillet over med heat. Place fillets in skillet, skinned side up, and reduce heat to medium low. Cook until seeds begin to brown. 1-2 min. Remove skilled from heat and using 2 spatulas, flip filets over. Transfer skillet to ovem. Bake until center of fish is transluent (125 degrees) 10-15 minutes.
