Title:
Amish White Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups water
1 cup milk
1/2 cup white sugar
1 1/2 tablespoons Rapid Rise (Instant) dry yeast
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 cup vegetable oil
5.5 to 6 cups bread flour

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl, warm water and oil to between 120 and 130 degrees.
Meanwhile, mix 2 cups flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in mixing bowl of stand mixer and using the paddle attachment, stir to combine.
Stir in warmed liquids until combined.
Add 3 cups flour, 1 cup at a time stirring well after each addition.
Remove paddle attachment and replace with dough hook.
Add 1/2 to 1 cup more flour a 1/3 cup at a time until dough forms a ball and does not stick to bottom of bowl.
Knead dough on 2nd to lowest speed 2 minutes. Dough should be smooth and elastic. Dough should spring back when poked with a thumb or finger.
Remove dough from bowl and knead a few times then shape into ball.
Oil shaped dough and cover with plastic wrap or inverted bowl. Allow to rest 10 minutes.
Knead for a few minutes to remove air bubbles, and divide in half. Roll into rectangles 8.5 inches wide. Roll up dough to form a loaf and place into two well oiled 9x5 inch loaf pans. Allow to rise for 30 to 45 minutes, or until dough has risen 1 inch above pans.
Bake at 360 degrees F for 22 to 25 minutes.
