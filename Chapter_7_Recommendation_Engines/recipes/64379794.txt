Title:
No Wheat Meatloaf with Beef and Pork Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
6 oz pork sausage
1 cup long grain white rice, cooked
1 tsp granulated onion
1 dash Worcester sauce
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Combine all ingredients. Press into a loaf pan sprayed with Pam and prepped with aluminum foil strips. Bake for 1 hour. Drain grease from loaf pan periodically during baking. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
morcar
Description: 
This is a wheat-free meatloaf I can serve to my mom, who can't eat wheat. My family likes it too.  It uses an egg and cooked rice as binder/filler. Pork sausage gives it good flavor.
