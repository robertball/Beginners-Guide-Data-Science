Title:
No Workout Needed Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2369390.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake
1 12 oz Package Soft Tofu
1 15.25 oz Dark Chocolate Cake Mix
1/2 Cup Brewed Coffee
Chocolate Topping
3 Tablespoons Unsweetened Almond or Cashew Milk
1/4 Cup Dark Chocolate Chips
2 Tablespoons PB2 Powdered Peanut Butter

Directions:
Preheat Oven to temperature indicated on your box of cake mix. Prep a 9 inch spring form cake pan by rubbing a light coating of coconut oil on bottom and sides.
Combine 1 package soft tofu with contents of 1 box dark chocolate cake mix in a food processor and blend for about 1-2 minutes or until well mixed. Add the brewed coffee. Blend an additional minute until well mixed. Scrape side with spatula as needed. Pour mixture into prepared spring form pan. Bake according to the directions on the cake mix box.
Remove cake from pan and let cool.
Chocolate Topping
Combine almond or cashew milk, dark chocolate chips, and PB2 into a microwave safe bowl. Microwave for 30 seconds. Mix. Microwave an additional 30 seconds. Do not over heat as the chocolate will burn.
Spoon warm chocolate topping over cake and serve.
