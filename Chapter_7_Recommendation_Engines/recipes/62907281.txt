Title:
Healthy Pita Pizza's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 wheat pita's
2% Kraft mexican cheese (1 bag)
Turkey pepperoni
red onion
bell pepper
black olives

Directions:
Take the pita and spoon the pizza sauce on top. Place light layer of cheese then the pepperoni. Sprinkle cut up vegetables over. Sprinkle more cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 12 minutes or until browned.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Falvo
Description: 
Individual pizza's that help keep your weight loss on track and no leftovers to tempt you the next day!If you have a Trader Joe's get their fat free pizza sauce. Yum! If not get what works for you.
