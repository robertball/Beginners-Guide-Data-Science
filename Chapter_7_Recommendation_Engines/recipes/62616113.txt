Title:
Skillet Ziti with Chicken and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into 1-in pieces
table salt and black pepper
3 T unsalted butter
1 onion, minced
1/4 t red pepper flakes
1/4 t dried oregano
3 cloves garlic, minced
8 oz ziti (2 1/2 C)
2 C chicken broth
1 C milk
1 bunch broccoli (1 1/2#), stems discarded and florets cut into 1-in pieces
1 C jarred roasted red peppers (12 oz), rinced and sliced 1/4 in. thick
1/2 C grated Parmesan cheese
2 T fresh lemon juice ((opt)

Directions:
Pat chicken dry, then season with salt and pepper. Melt 1T butter in 12-in skillet over med. heat. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned but not fully cooked, about 4 min. Transfer chicken to bowl.
Add 1 more T of butter, onion, red pepper flakes, oregano, 1/2 t salt to skillet. Cook until onion is softened, about 5 min. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant, about 15 sec.
Sprinkle ziti evenly into skillet. Pour broth and milk over ziti. Cover and bring to a simmer, about 2 min.
Reduce heat to med-low and stir in broccoli and roasted peppers. Cover and continue to simmer, stirring occas. until pasta begins to soften and broccoli turns bright green, about 8 min.
Stir in chicken and any accumulated juice. Cover and continue to simmer until chicken is cooked through; about 3 min longer.
Stir in remaining 1 T butter, cheese and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
