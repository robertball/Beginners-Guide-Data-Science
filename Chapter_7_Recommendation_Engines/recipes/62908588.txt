Title:
WW Chinese Pineapple Chicken with Black Bean Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 apray cooking spray
4 medium scallions, chopped (green & white parts)
1 Tbsp ginger root, fresh chopped
2 cloves, garlic, minced
1 Lb. chicken breast, raw, without skin & bone, cut into 1 ince cubes.
20 oz canned pineapple, packed in juice, use tidbits
1/4 cup black bean sauce
2 cups cooked brown rice

Directions:
Coat a large non-stick skillet with cooking spray and set pan over medium high heat.  Add scallions, ginger and garlic; cook until soft, about 3 minutes.  Add chicken and cook until lightly browned on all sides, stirring often, about 5 minutes.
Add pineapple (with its juice) and black bean sauce to skillet; bring to a simmer.  Simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes more.
Divide rice amoung 4 shallow bowls and spoon chicken mixture over top.
