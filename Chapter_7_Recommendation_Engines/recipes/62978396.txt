Title:
B.L.T. Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
25 to 30 Cherry Tomatoes
1 lb Bacon
1/2 cup Miracle Whip or Mayo
3 tbls grated Parmesean Cheese
1/3 cup Green Onions (chopped)
2 tbls Parsley

Directions:
Fry bacon until crisp, drain on paper towel until completely cool
Mix Miracle Whip, cheese, onions and parsley
Crush bacon and add to mixture
For best flavor let sit in fridge for at least 2 hours
Core tomatoes and dispose of insides
Fill tomatoes with mixture
Hint: Leftover mixture is great on toast for a qhick sandwich.

Submitter: 
rmosher10
Description: 
This is a recipe that takes alot of prep time but worth every minute. You wont be able to stop popping these little guys in your mouth.
