Title:
Cranberry Walnut Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Wet ingredients:
1 cup oil
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 tbsp vanilla flavoring
2 tsp lemon juice
2 cups grated zucchini
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup dried cranberries
Dry ingredients:
3 cups plain flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2-1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cardomom
Topping
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp flour
1 tbsp oatmeal
2 tbsp cold butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix dry ingredients in bowl and set aside.  Mix wet ingredients and gradually add in dry ingredients.  Pour into three greased & floured loaf pans.  For topping, mix brown sugar, flour  & oatmeal, and cut in butter with a fork.  Sprinkle on top of batter.  Bake for 50 minutes or until an inserted knife comes out clean.  Turn out immediately onto tin foil squares.  Let cool a few minutes, then wrap.

Submitter: 
Susan Bray Garrison
Description: 
This has a fresh, sweet but slightly tart taste from the cardomom and cranberries.  Different than typical zucchini bread recipes.
