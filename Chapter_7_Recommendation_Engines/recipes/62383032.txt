Title:
Big Daddy's Avocado Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
1 cup milk
1 scoop Mila
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
3 tablespoons honey
8 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine the avocado, Mila, milk, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
Try this avocado, Mila, vanilla yogurt, and honey smoothie in the summer for a refreshing treat.Modified from: Avocado Smoothie
