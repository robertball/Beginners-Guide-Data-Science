Title:
Grilled Pork Chops with Tangy Peach BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS olive oil
2 TBS brown sugar
2 tsp. chili powder
2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
6 10-ounce pork chops (1 inch thick)
1 cup bbq sauce
1 ripe peach, peeled and diced
2 TBS apple-cider vinegar
1 TBS water

Directions:
1) In a bowl, mix olive oil, brown sugar, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Rub onto pork and put chops in 2 resealable plastic bags. Refrigerate at least 1 hour and up to over night.
2) In a sauce pan over med. heat, combine bbq sauce, diced peach, apple-cider vinegar, and water, stirring until it reaches a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer, cover and cook 5 minutes. Transfer to a blender and puree.
3) Light grill to med. heat. Cook chops about 12 minutes per side. Brush each with barbecue mixture. Cover and grill 4 more minutes until a nice glaze is created. Serve with extra sauce on the side
