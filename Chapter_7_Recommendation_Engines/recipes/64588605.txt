Title:
Veda's Freezer Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts corn, cut off the cob
1 1/2 TB sugar
2 tsp salt
3/4 cup water
1 stick butter

Directions:
Bring ingredients corn-butter to a boil, then simmer for 5 minuets, stirring to prevent sticking.
Remove from stove.
Add 1/2 pt cream, stir until incorporated.
Serve, or cool and package in freezer bags and freeze.
