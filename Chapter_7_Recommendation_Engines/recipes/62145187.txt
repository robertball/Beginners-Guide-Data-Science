Title:
Cherry Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 c. graham cracker crumbe
1/3 c. suger
1/4 c. melted butter
Filling:
2 Pkgs. Softened cream cheese
1/3 c. Sugar
1 Tub cool whip
Topping:
1 can Cherry pie filling/topping

Directions:
For Crust:
Mix graham cracker crumbs and 1/3 c. sugar together. Slowly mix in melted butter while stirring, it should be crumbly, and not drenched in butter. You might not have to use all the butter. Press into bottom of a 9 X 9" dish.
For the Filling:
Beat together the cream cheese, 1/3 c. sugar and cool whip. Pour on top of crust.
Then pour the can of cherry topping on top of cheese cake and refrigerate until ready to serve!
