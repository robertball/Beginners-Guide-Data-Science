Title:
Tofu Broccoli Cashew Peanut Madness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbl butter or oil
1 large onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 lb herbed tofu, cubed
2 Tbl tamari or soy sauce
1/2 to 3/4 cup peanut butter, crunchy
2-3 tsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp cumin, or more
Pinch cayenne, or to taste
1 bunch broccoli, peeled and chopped
Brown rice, cooked
Handful roasted cashews, chopped

Directions:
Heat butter or oil in skillet.  Add onion and garlic.  Saute until soft.
Add tofu and 1 Tbl tamari, saute until brown.  Remove from pan.
In same pan, mix peanut butter, lemon juice, remaining tamari, cumin, and cayenne.  Thin with up to 1 cup water to obtain gravy like texture.
Stir in tofu mixture.
Steam broccoli.
Serve sauce over broccoli and brown rice, topped with cashews.
