Title:
Brine - All Purpose for Pork & Poultry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup kosher salt
2 tablespoons sugar
1 quart cold water
1 sprig rosemary
2 sprigs thyme

Directions:
Mix cold water, salt and sugar. Stir to dissolve
Add rosemary & thyme
In non-reactive container, immerse thawed food in brine, seal and refrigerate.
Use 1 quart brine per pound of meat
Brine for 1 hour per pound (when brining multiple items, time is based on weight of a single item), but not less than 30 minutes or more than 8 hours.
