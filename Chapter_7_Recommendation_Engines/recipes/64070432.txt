Title:
Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 package lemon Jello, large
1 yellow cake mix
4 eggs
3/4 cup oil
3/4 cup water
Icing:
3 cups powdered sugar
4 TBSP butter
3-4 TBSP lemon juice

Directions:
Cake:
Combine all cake ingredients, blend well. Bake in a 9x13 cake pan.
Icing:
Combine lemon juice, sugar and water to make thick glaze. Spread over warm cake.
