Title:
Mom's Fruit Whip Cream Wafer Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large container of whipping cream, whipped
Chocolate wafers - 2 packages or graham wafers
Raspberries or blueberries, enough to color - mashed

Directions:
Mix the mashed fruit and whipped cream and spread a bit  on to wafer, add a wafer, repeat and build into a log. Cover with with either plain whipping cream or fruited whipping cream. Refrigerate a few hours until wafers are softened. Sprinkle with leftover fruit

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
This is a very easy variation on the chocolate wafer dessert Mom used to make. Can be made with any fruit or wafers.
