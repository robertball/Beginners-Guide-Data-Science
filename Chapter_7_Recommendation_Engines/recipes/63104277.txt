Title:
Felicia's Slow Cooker Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 1 pound bag dry black beans
One onion finely chopped
Four cloves of garlic finely chopped
Half green bell pepper finely chopped
Dash of cumin
3 tablespoons of tomato sauce
1 tablespoon of olive oil
One bay leaf
1 tablespoon of salt divided

Directions:
Soak beans overnight in 6 to 8 cups of water
Drain water
Boil beans and 2 teaspoons of salt for 40 minutes on high heat with fresh water (6 to 8 cups)
Place onion, garlic, green pepper, cumin, tomato sauce, olive oil, 1 tsp salt, and bay leaf in slow cooker.
Transfer black beans and water into slow cooker, thourouhjlu mixing all the ingredients
Cook on low for 4-6 hours
Serve with white rice

Submitter: 
Vivienne Borchers
Description: 
My best friend gave me her recipe for Cuban-style black beans. I made minor tweaks to make them in the slow cooker. Serve with white rice for an authentic Cuban meal.
