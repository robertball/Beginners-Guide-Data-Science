Title:
Chocolate Star Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 egg
1 1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. baking powder
2 dozen chocolate stars or hershey kisses/hugs

Directions:
Mix butter, peanut butter and sugars.  Add vanilla and egg.  Blend the dry ingredients and add to sugar mixture.
Grease cookie sheet.  Roll cookies in white sugar, flatten slightly on cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 for 10-12 mins.  When cookies are still hot place one chocolate star in center of cookie.  Allow cookies to cool completely before eating, or the chocolate will be melted!

Submitter: 
BEARNESTA
Description: 
If you like peanut butter and chocolate this is for you!  This is a holiday tradition in my family.  Very good as frozen treats as well.
