Title:
Super Easy Chicken and Yellow Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126954.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 pounds of boneless skinless chicken breast
16 ounce package of yellow rice (I use Vigo brand)
3 quarts water
4 tablespoons of olive oil

Directions:
1. Bring water to boil in large saucepan and cook chicken till cooked through (about 30 minutes).
2. Remove chicken from boiling water, saving 4 cups of water from boil. Cut chicken into bite size pieces.
3. Mix water, chicken, and rice package in saucepan and bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
5. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Renee Coley
Description: 
This is a quick, easy, and affordable one pot meal. My mom used to make this growing up and now my family loves it. My son and I like to mix our favorite hot sauce into our dish for a little extra spice.
