Title:
Fiber One Kid Approved Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793347.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups FiberOne Honey Squares Cereal
½ Cup FiberOne Original Bran Cereal
½  Cup Yogurt Chips
½ Cup M&M;’s
½ Cup Roasted Peanut Halves

Directions:
Mix together cereals, yogurt chips, M&M's, and Peanuts. Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Recently while creating trail mixes for my daughter's class, I decided to incorporate a healthier cereal such as FiberOne. The cereal is super tasty and this recipe can entice any little one to eat it up. This recipe is easily adjustable.
