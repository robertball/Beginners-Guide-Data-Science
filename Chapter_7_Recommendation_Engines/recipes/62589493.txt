Title:
Lentil Soup with Sausage and Escarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cups lentils (11 oz.), rinsed well
5 cups water
3 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 bay leaf
4 garlic cloves. minced
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 lb. sausage, cut into 1" pieces
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 medium carrots, finely chopped
2 celery ribs, finely chopped
2 tbsp. tomato paste
1/2 lb. escarole, chopped (4 cups packed)
1-2 tbsp. red wine vinegar

Directions:
Simmer lentils, water, broth, bay leaf and half of garlic in an uncovered pot.  Meanwhile, heat oil in large pot, add sausage and cook until brown, about 7 minutes. Remove sausage, reduce heat, and add onion, carrots, celery, rmaining garlic, 1 tsp. salt and 1/2 tsp. pepper.  Cook until softened, about 5 min.  Add sausage and lentils with liquid, and simmer, uncovered until lentils are tender, 3-5 min. Stir in escarole and cook about 3 min. or until tender.  Add vinegar to taste and season with salt and pepper.
