Title:
Smoky Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1947214.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, divided
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
Cooking spray
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon adobo sauce
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 (15-ounce) can no-salt-added black beans, rinsed and drained
Chopped green onions
Chopped red pepper
Grilled corn (optional)
Cherry tomatoes
1 (9-ounce) package baby arugula
1/2 cup (2 ounces) crumbled goat cheese

Directions:
1. Toast walnuts in oven or in pan
2. Dressing: Combine olive oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, and adobo sauce in a bowl; stir with a whisk.
3. Mix together greens, veggies, nuts and cheese with dressing and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
You'll find chipotle chiles canned in adobo sauce on the Latin food aisle. Use just the sauce here; reserve chiles for another use.

Yield:
 Serves 4 (serving size: 2 cups arugula, 3/4 cup bean mixture, 2 tablespoons nuts, and 2 tablespoons cheese)
