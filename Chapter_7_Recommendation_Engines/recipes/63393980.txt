Title:
Apple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BREAD
1c sugar
¼ C apple sauce
2 eggs
2c sifted flour
1t baking powder
1 t soda
1/2 t salt
1 1/2 cup diced apples
1/2 cup chopped nuts
1t vanilla
½ tsp cinnamon
1 tbsp sugar
CARAMEL GLAZE
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/8 cup milk
½ tsp. vanilla
Boil 2 1/2 minutes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F Cream sugar and apple sauce, then add eggs and vanilla. Sift flour, baking powder, soda and salt.  Add to creamed mixture. Mixture will be very thick, add apples and nuts then pour into greased loaf pan. Sprinkle top with sugar and cinnamon.  Bake for 1 hour.
Place loaf pan on cooling rack and poke holes in the top with a toothpick.  Pour half of the caramel glaze on top and let soak in for at least 30 minutes.
