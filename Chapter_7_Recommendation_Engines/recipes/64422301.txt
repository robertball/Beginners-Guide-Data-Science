Title:
Big Ray's Simple Shrimp Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2304383.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbl olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1 green bell pepper, diced
2 cups  long-grain rice
1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
3 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 tsp  Old Bay seasoning
1 pound  medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
In a large ovenproof skillet with lid, heat olive oil and cook onion, garlic and bell pepper until softened, about 3 minutes. Add rice and cook, stirring, 1 minute. Stir in tomatoes, broth, Old Bay seasoning; bring mixture to a boil.
Cover and transfer pan to oven. Cook until rice is tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Uncover, lay shrimp on rice, re-cover, and continue to bake until shrimp are opaque, another 5 to 7 minutes.
