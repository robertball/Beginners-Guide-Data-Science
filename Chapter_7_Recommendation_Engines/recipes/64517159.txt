Title:
Raising Cane's Sauce Copycat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Mayonnaise (I used Kraft)
1/4 cup Ketchup
1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
Ground Black Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl mix the mayo, ketchup, garlic powder, and worcestershire  cover the top with pepper.
Mix well and cover again with pepper.
Refrigerate for several hours to let the flavors marry.
