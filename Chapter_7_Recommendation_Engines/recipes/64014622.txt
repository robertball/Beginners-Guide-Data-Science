Title:
Gluten Free Donut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1115181.jpg
Ingredients:
100g white sugar
55g butter, melted
3/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon baking powder
125 gluten free flour mixture
Nutella, powdered sugar icing for glazing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 190Â°C.
Mix 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup margarine, and nutmeg in a large bowl.
Stir in the milk, then mix in the baking powder and flour until just combined.
Fill the prepared mini muffin cups about half full.
Bake in the preheated oven until the tops are lightly golden, 15 to 20 minutes.
While warm glaze with Nutella or powdered sugar icing.
