Title:
Green Bean and Blue Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981852.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh green beans, cut into 2 inch pieces
1/3 cup prepared GOOD SEASONS Italian Salad Dressing Mix
1 small red onion, coarsely chopped
1 (4 ounce) can pitted ripe olives, drained, sliced
1/2 cup KRAFT Blue Cheese Crumbles

Directions:
Cook beans until tender-crisp; drain well.
Place dressing in large microwavable bowl. Microwave on HIGH 30 seconds or until warm.
Add beans, onion, olives and cheese; toss until well chilled.
