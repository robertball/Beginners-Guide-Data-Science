Title:
Healthy Vegan Pumpkin Oat Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126400.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 Cups Whole Wheat Flour
- 1 Cup Oats
- 1 tsp. Baking Powder
- 1 tsp. Baking Soda
- 1/2 tsp. Salt
- 1/2 tsp. Nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
- 1 Cup Raw Cane Sugar
- 1/2 Cup Coconut Sugar or Maple Syrup (will make dough sticker but tastes just as good!)
- 1/2 Cup Coconut Oil (or any vegan Oil/Butter replacement)
- 1 Cup Pumpkin Puree
- 1 Flaxegg (1 Tbsp. ground flaxseed and 3 Tbsp. water)
- 1 tsp. Vanilla
- 1 Cup Vegan Chocolate Chips (optional)
- 1 Cup Chopped Pecans (optional)

Directions:
-Preheat oven to 350 degrees
-Start by making your flaxegg. Either use pre-ground flaxseed or whole flaxseed thats been put in a blender and mix with water. Set aside until mixture thickens.
-In a large bowl, combine the flour, oats, salt, the baking powder and soda, and your spices and set aside.
-In a smaller bowl, cream your  oil with your sugars until smooth. Then add in your pumpkin puree, your flaxegg, and your vanilla.
-Pour your wet ingredients into dry and stir until just combined.
- Add in your Chocolate Chips and Pecans (optional)
- Scoop onto prepared cookie sheet with a small ice cream scoop.
-Bake for approx. 10 minutes, then once out of the oven, let them cool on sheet for about 5 minutes and place them on a cooling rack to cool completely.
