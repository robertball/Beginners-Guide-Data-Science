Title:
Chicken and Mushrooms in Sherry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/329996.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breast halves
6 tablespoons olive oil
3 large shallots (thinly sliced)
2 cloves garlic (minced)
12 ounces baby bella mushrooms (sliced)
1/2 cup sherry cooking wine
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 chicken boullion cubes dissolved in 1 cup warm water
3 tablespoons lemon juice
3 teaspoons sugar
2 tablespoons italian seasoning
black pepper/garlic powder to taste
6 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Brown chicken in olive oil then remove from pan and cut into strips.
In remaining olive oil saute shallots and garlic over medium/low heat for 3 minutes.
Add mushrooms, sherry, chicken broth, boullion, lemon juice, sugar and seasoning.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
*If sauce seems too thin just mix 2 teaspoons
cornstarch to 5 tablespoons water and add to pan.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
SlowcookerSeduction
Description: 
This recipe evolved from many attempts at creating a last minute (edible) sauce using ingredients I had handy. Everytime I made it there was a variation from the last - then FINALLY I got it right! (Works over rice or pasta)
