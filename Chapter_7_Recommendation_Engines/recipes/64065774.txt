Title:
Big Ray's French Toast in the Crock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130801.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 cans (12.4 oz each) refrigerated cinnamon rolls with icing
6   eggs
1/2 cup whipping cream
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup maple syrup

Directions:
Spray 5-quart oval slow cooker with cooking spray. Pour melted butter into slow cooker. Separate each can of dough into 8 rolls; set icing aside. Cut each roll into 8 pieces; place pieces over butter in slow cooker.
In medium bowl, beat eggs. Add whipping cream, cinnamon and vanilla; beat until well blended. Gently pour mixture over roll pieces. Drizzle with 1/2 cup of the syrup.
Cover; cook on Low heat setting 2 hours 30 minutes or until set and temperature in center is 160Â°F. Drizzle with reserved icing and remaining 1/2 cup syrup.

Description: 
Take your French toast up a notch by using cinnamon rolls in place of bread. It's an easy prep you can sit back and enjoy your coffee while it cooks.
