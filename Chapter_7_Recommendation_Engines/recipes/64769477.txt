Title:
Creamy and Spicy  Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10-12 oz bag frozen corn kernels
1 8 oz block Cream cheese, softened
4 Tbsp Butter, softened
1 can diced green chilies or
3-4 diced fresh jalapenos, diced or
1/4 c dried chives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream together the cream cheese and butter.
Mix in green chilies, jalapenos or chives.
Mix in corn.
Place in baking dish and bake for 30 minutes.

Kisha McDaniel Logsdon
Description: 
Frozen corn, cream cheese and jalapenos.
