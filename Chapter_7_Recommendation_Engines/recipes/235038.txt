Title:
Kahlua® and Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060423.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces Kahlua
4 fluid ounces hot chocolate
Whipped cream

Directions:
Pour prepared hot cocoa and Kahlua into a heated mug. Top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Kahlua®
Description: 
Stir some Kahlua into your favorite hot chocolate and top with a dollop of whipped cream for a perfect winter warm-up.
