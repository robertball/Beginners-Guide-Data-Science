Title:
Cheeseburger pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup milk
1/2 cup Bisquick baking mix
2 eggs

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onion; drain. Stir in salt. Spread in 9" greased pie plate; sprinkle with cheese.
Stir remaining ingredients with fork until blended. Pour into plate.
Bake 25 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
