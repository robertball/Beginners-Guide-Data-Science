Title:
1600 Beer Haus Veggie Burger Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tbsp olive oil
1 red onion finely chopped
2 red peppers finely diced
2 green peppers finely diced
1 cup mushrooms chopped
1 cup spinach roughly chopped
19oz can black bean rinsed
19oz can chick peas rinsed
1 cup toasted almonds
1 cup toasted sunflower seeds
1 cup sunflower seeds roughly chopped
2Tbsp cumin
3Tbsp Chili powder
4Tbsp sesame oil
1 Cup soy sauce
10 Eggs
4 Cups carrots grated
6 cups panko bread crumbs
4 cups oats

Directions:
Sweat onions, garlic and mushrooms in olive oil.  let cool.  Combine all ingredients and let stand for 6 hours.  Portion to 6 oz patties.  Fry in olive oil.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
These are hands down the tastiest veg burgers in any calgary eatery that I've had!
