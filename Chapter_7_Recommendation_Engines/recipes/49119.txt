Title:
Sunset Patio Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976761.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces penne pasta
4 cups assorted vegetable pieces (such as zucchini, yellow squash, bell pepper, mushrooms and onions)
4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
3 teaspoons McCormick® Grill Mates® Montreal Chicken Seasoning, divided
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Toss vegetables with 1 tablespoon oil and 1 teaspoon Grill Mates(R) Montreal Chicken Seasoning.  Place vegetables in a grill basket or on skewers; grill until tender, about 8-10 minutes, turning frequently.
Combine balsamic vinegar, remaining oil and remaining Grill Mates(R) Montreal Chicken Seasoning in a large bowl. Add cooked pasta and vegetables and toss well.  Serve warm or chilled.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Sunset Patio Pasta combines the great taste of grilled vegetables with seasoned pasta for the perfect summertime side dish.
