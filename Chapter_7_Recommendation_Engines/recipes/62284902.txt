Title:
Ladybug's Lowcountry Cinnamon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup raisins
2 med apples, peeled, cored, and chopped fine
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1 can water chestnuts, chopped fine
1/2 - 3/4 brown sugar
1 stick melted butter
1 tsp cinnamon or more to taste
2 tsp curry powder or more to taste
8 chicken breasts, skinned and boned

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix all ingredients, except chicken in small mixing bowl
Place full tablespoon of apple mixture in each chicken breast
Bring up corners and secure with a toothpick
Turn over and place in a medium sized baking dish
Place remaining apple mixture on top of each breast
Cover tightly with foil and bake for 35-40 minutes basting at least twice with juices
Serve chicken on platter of Cinnamon-Curried Rice (see Ladybug's Cinnamon-Curried Rice recipe).

Submitter: 
Lisa Schwartz-Gunnison
Description: 
No where is the living easier than at Sullivan's Island in the summer.  Here's another low country favorite served up with a side of Southern charm and hospitality.
