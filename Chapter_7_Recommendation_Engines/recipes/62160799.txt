Title:
True Cheeseburger Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz) package pre-baked Italian bread shell crust
3/4 lb Ground Chuck
1/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
2/3 cup Ketchup
2 tbsp Yellow Mustard
1/2 cup chopped Onions
1 1/2 cups shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1 cup shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup Dill Pickle slices
1 cup shredded Lettuce (optional)
1 cup diced Tomatoes (optional)

Directions:
Brown the ground chuck until just browned and drain well.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix together the ketchup and mustard.
Spread ketchup mixture onto pizza crust.
Top pizza with cheeses, meat, and then onion.
Bake for approximately 20 minutes or until cheese has melted.
Once removed from oven, top with pickle slices.
Let pizza cool for 5 minutes.
Add lettuce and tomato if desired.
Slice Pizza and enjoy!

Submitter: 
JILLENA
Description: 
This is a unique twist on two favorites - pizza and cheeseburgers!  It is super fast to make and you can easily had your favorite burger toppings such as lettuce and tomato.  This is a family favorite and disappears fast!
