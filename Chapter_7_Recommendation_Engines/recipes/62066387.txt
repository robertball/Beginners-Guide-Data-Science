Title:
fiesta beef dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6.4oz.) pkg. RICE-A-RONI Mexican Style Flavor
1 pound ground beef
2 cups water
1 15oz. can corn with peppers
1 15.25oz. can black beans drained
1 tsp. cumin
1 cup of your favorite salsa
shredded cheddar cheese
sour cream
Doritos

Directions:
In a large skillet brown ground beef and drain. Add pasta mix and cook for a few minutes to brown pasta slightly.  Stir in water, salsa, seasoning packet, corn, beans and cumin.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover and simmer 15-20 minutes or until rice is tender. Use cheese, sour cream, additional salsa and crushed Doritos to top it off.
