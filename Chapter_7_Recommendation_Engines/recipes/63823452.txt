Title:
Baked Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 tsp butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp parsley
1/2 tsp paprika
4-6 tilapia fillets

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt the butter and add the garlic, salt and pepper, parsley and paprika.  Brush some of this mixture on the bottom of a shallow baking dish.  Place tilapia fillets in dish, brushing the remaining butter mixture over the top.  Bake at 350 for 12-15 minutes or until fish flakes easily.
