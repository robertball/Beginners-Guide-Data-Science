Title:
Cherry Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5068201.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. Canola or Grape Seed Oil
1 pint of cherry tomatoes
1 C of chopped Leeks
2  Cloves minced garlic
2 Cups Low Sodium Chicken broth - Divided
1 tsp Sea Salt
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1/4 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1/2 tsp. Dried Thyme
1/4 C Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 C Heavy Cream or Half and Half  - Optional
1 Tbsp. Unsalted Butter - Optional

Directions:
Heat a medium frying pan on medium. When hot add the oil.
Add the cherry tomatoes. leeks and garlic and saute until the tomatoes and leeks are softened.
Add 1 cup of the chicken broth and let simmer for 5 minutes.
Carefully pour the hot tomato mixture into a blender and puree and return to pan.
Add the remaining cup of chicken broth to the blender to "rinse" it out and then add it to the tomato mixture.
Add salt, black pepper, Cayenne pepper, dried thyme, Parmesan cheese and, if using, the cream and butter to the tomato mixture and simmer until heated through.
