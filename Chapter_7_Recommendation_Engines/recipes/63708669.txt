Title:
Corny Corn Bread (Vegan, Wheat Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031077.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Chia Seeds
1 1/2 cup warm water
1/2 cup sucanat
1/4 cup agave
1 1/2 cup corn meal
1 cup spelt flour
1 1/2 corn kernels (frozen)
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
Soak Chia seeds in warm water for 5 minutes.
Blend Chia seeds
Add sucanat, agave, blend.
Sift corn meal, spelt flour and salt.
Add frozen corn.
Combine wet ingredients and dry.
Pour into greased pan.
Bake for 25-30 minutes
