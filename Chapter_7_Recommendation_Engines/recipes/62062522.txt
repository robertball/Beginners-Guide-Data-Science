Title:
Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
2 cups polish sausage
*any kind works, we like Eckrich
1 can tomato soup
1.5 cans water
1 lb pork & beans

Directions:
Put all ingredients into crock pot.  Stir well.  Cook Low 6-8 hours.
