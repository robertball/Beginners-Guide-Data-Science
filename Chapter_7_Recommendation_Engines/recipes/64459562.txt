Title:
Turkey Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, peeled
2 garlic cloves
2 tbs oil
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp ground cardamom
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 1/2 tsp coriander
16 oz. can crushed tomatoes
salt
1 1/4 lb cooked turkey
1 tsp garam masala
2/3 cup plain yogurt
chopped cilantro to garnish

Directions:
Chop onion, mince garlic. Heat oil in a saucepan, add onion and garlic and fry until golden. Add turmeric, mustard seeds, chili powder, cardamom, cumin and coriander. Cook, stirring 1 minute. Add tomatoes and season with salt. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer 20 minutes. Meanwhile, chop turkey into large chunks. Stir in garam masala and 1/4 cup yogurt. Add turkey, stir well, cover and cook gently 10 minutes. Stir in remaining yogurt and sprinkle with cilantro beore serving. Accompany with rice.
