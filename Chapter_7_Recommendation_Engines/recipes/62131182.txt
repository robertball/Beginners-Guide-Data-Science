Title:
Clam Dip Number One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz packages cream cheese
2 6.5 oz cans one each, chopped,and minced,clams
clam juice reserved from one can
2 cups mayonaise
1.5 - 2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce (to taste)
2 tablespoons minced yellow onion
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce(to taste)

Directions:
Note:Best results will require an electric mixer
place opened cream cheese in large mixing bowl to soften
in same bowl,add mayo,salt,worcestershire,hot sauce
blend at medium speed while preceeding to next step
add clam juice slowly to mix to create creamy consistency
(it should take roughly 3/4 of reserved juice of one can)
add clams,onions,and continue mixing for 1 minute
place in serving bowl
allow to ripen,covered, in refrigerator overnight
serve with potato chips and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Chuck Dagel
Description: 
This is a very smooth ,and you will find ,very addictive tasting dip!.If you can,it's best to ripen overnight to absorb flavors,but can be enjoyed right away.It goes best with big ruffled potato chips.
