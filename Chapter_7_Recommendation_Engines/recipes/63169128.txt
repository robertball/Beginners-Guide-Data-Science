Title:
Lemon Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sugar
1 tbsp. cornstarch
1 c water
1 tbsp fresh lemon zest
1 c lemon juice

Directions:
Combine sugar and cornstarch in large pot.  Add water, zest and juice.  Bring to a boil and simmer just until thickened.   Remove from heat.  Pour into hot jars through a sieve, leaving 1/4 inch head space. Adjust caps. Process 10 minutes in a boiling water bath.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
Refreshing and sweet like lemonade.  Delicious on gingerbread with a little whipped cream.
