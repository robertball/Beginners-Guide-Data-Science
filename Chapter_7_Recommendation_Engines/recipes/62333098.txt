Title:
Poppyseed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/386276.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Dukes mayonnaise
1/2 cup whole milk
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
2/3 cup white sugar
2 Tbsp poppyseeds

Directions:
Combine the mayonnaise, milk, and vinegar and whisk until well-blended. Then add the sugar and poppyseeds. Drizzle over salad and enjoy!

Submitter: 
paralee
Description: 
This is the dressing everyone begs for when I make salads. My mom's best friend always served it over a bed of romaine with strawberries and vidalia onions. It is kind of thin, but I like it that way since you really only need a little bit.
