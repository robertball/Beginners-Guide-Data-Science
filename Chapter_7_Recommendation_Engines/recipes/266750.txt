Title:
Fruitable Fruity Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5638311.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) containers cherry yogurt
3 teaspoons honey
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon ground cloves
1 apple, sliced
1 banana, sliced

Directions:
Combine yogurt, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves in a bowl. Stir dip until mixed. Serve with fruit.

Submitter: 
Island Freerunning
Description: 
This fuity dip recipe is a great and fun little party snack you can serve with your favorite fruit.
