Title:
chicken taco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless chicken breast
1 can cream of mushroom or cream chicken (your preference)
1 cup salsa
1 package taco seasoning
1 cup sour cream
Shredded cheese

Directions:
Put chicken, soup, salsa in slow cooker.
sprinkle taco seasoning over everything.
cook on low for 6 hrs
shred chicken breast with 2 forks after the 6 hrs. (it will fall apart easily)
stir in sour cream and keep covered until everything is combined and melted.
sprinkle with shredded cheddar-jack cheese if you like.
serve over rice or scoops!!!!  enjoy

Submitter: 
Denise Maggio Medina
Description: 
easy and taste great... You can use it as a dip or for tacos
