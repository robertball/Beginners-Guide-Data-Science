Title:
Tataki Salad with Ahi Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/707420.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 cups mixed salad greens
2 avocados, sliced
2 tomatatoes, sliced
1 cucumber, cut into sticks (optional)
1 lb. Ahi tuna (also called yellowfin tuna)
WASABI VINAIGRETTE:
2/3 cup pure olive oil
2 tsp wasabi powder
1 tsp ginger, minced
2 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
1 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
Kosher salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
1 small handful fresh cilantro, chopped
PLACE INGREDIENTS FOR VINAIGRETTE IN BLENDER. BLEND ON HIGH UNTIL EMULSIFIED.

Directions:
Cook Tuna rare.  Serve.
