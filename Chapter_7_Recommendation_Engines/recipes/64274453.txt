Title:
Toasted Hazelnut Coffee Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Fresh brewed coffee
1 oz Halzenut liqueur (like Frangelico)
Half and half to taste
Sugar to taste
Whipped cream- optional

Directions:
Pour hot  coffee in a mug. Mix in remaining ingredients. Top with whipped cream.
