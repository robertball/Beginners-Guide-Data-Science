Title:
Homemade Nutella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of hazelnuts
2 teaspoons of canola oil
1/4 cup of cocoa powder
1/3 cup of honey

Directions:
roast the hazelnuts for about 10 to 15 minutes at 400 until the skins break open and the nuts are pale golden.
Cool the nuts and use a dish cloth to remove the skins
Pulse the nuts until ground.  Let machine run and add one teaspoon of canola
Allow to turn into a thick paste and then add cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon of canola oil and honey. mix until spreadable.
Store in fridge for up to 2 weeks

Submitter: 
Christine Douglas- Kaiser
Description: 
Found this on a blog site... I thought that it sounded easy and cheap.. Nutella spread is normally a staple in our house.
