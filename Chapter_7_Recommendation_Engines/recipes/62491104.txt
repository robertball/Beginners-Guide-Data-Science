Title:
cheddar and roast beef melt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 rolls of refrigerated pizza crust
1/2 lb. roast beef from the deli
1/2 lb. cheddar cheese from the deli
1 tsp oregano
1 egg white
1 diced green pepper
1 diced onion
1 Tbsp oil

Directions:
preheat oven for 450
heat oil in skillet and add green pepper and onion, sautee for a couple of minutes unitl soft.
roll out one tube of pizza dough on floured surface and make a rectangle.
lay out half roast beef, half cheddar, half green pepper and onion mix, and sprinkle some oregano.
roll up long ways
Repeat these steps for the second tube of pizza dough
Brush top with egg white and sprinkle with rest of oregano
join the tubes together to make a circle ( or just make two separtate) and bake 20-25 min or until golden brown
Enjoy!
