Title:
Rabbit Food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cabbage 1 pound of bacon 1 small bag of eggnoodles

Directions:
lay strips of bacon on cutting board and chop into 1 inch peices then fry till crispy, set aside cook bacon, keep 2-3 tablespoons of bacon grease and chop up whole head of cabbage into small to med peices add to bacon grease and cook on med stiring until soft. boil eggnoodles till soft and drain. in large bowl add bacon eggnooles and cabbage together and mix.
