Title:
Garlic Jalapeno Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Jalapenos, stems removed
3/4 cup water
1/8 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 white onion, chopped
1 head garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon mexican oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt, may add more to taste

Directions:
In small pot add water and jalapenos, bring to boil.  Continue boiling for about 8-10 minutes until jalapenos are tender.
Pour jalapenos and water into blender, add apple cider vinegar, onion, garlic, cumin, mexican oregano and salt.  Blend 2-3 minutes until smooth.  Pour into a container and allow to cool.  Can be stored in fridge for up to a week.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a versatile sauce that can be used for dipping quesadillas, tacos or any other mexican favorite that needs a little spice.
