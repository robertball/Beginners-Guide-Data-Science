Title:
Easy Chicken and Cheese Chilaquiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (18 oz) Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy three cheese cooking sauce
1 can (4.5 oz) Old El Paso® chopped green chiles, undrained
1 box (4.6 oz) Old El Paso® taco shells (12 shells)
2 cups shredded deli rotisserie chicken (from 2-lb chicken)
2 cups shredded Cheddar or pepper Jack cheese (8 oz)

Directions:
Heat oven to 400ºF. Spray 2-quart casserole with cooking spray. In medium bowl, mix cooking sauce and chiles.
Step 2
Break up taco shells into bite-size pieces. Place half of pieces in casserole. Top with shredded chicken, half of the cooking sauce mixture and half of the cheese. Repeat layers with remaining ingredients.
Step 3
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until cheese is melted and top is golden brown.
