Title:
Jamie's Yucca Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon glass jar with lid (ie pickle jar)
3 large lemons
1 cup sugar
ice
a fifth of vodka

Directions:
Squeeze the lemons into the jar and drop them in. Pour the sugar over top of the lemons. Fill the jar all the way to the top with ice. Pour in the fifth of vodka. Shake the jar for 20 minutes (instill the help of your groom for this). Drink and enjoy!
