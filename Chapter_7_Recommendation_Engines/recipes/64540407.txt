Title:
Coco Tea Mix Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 or 2 tea bags of any tea of your choice.( 2 bags depending how strong you want it)
3 Tbsp of Milk
1 1/2 Tbsp hot coco mix
2 Tsp sugar (any sugar you have)
Hot Water

Directions:
1) Boil water till hot
2) Put tea bag in cup
3) Pour hot water into cup
4) Brew for 2 minutes
5) Take out tea bag
6) Add in Coco
7) Add in Milk
8) Add in Sugar
9) Stir for 20 seconds

Submitter: 
Morgan13
Description: 
Not everyone will be a fan of this earthy, sweet drink. Depends what tea you use really, I use Earl Grey Black Tea, but that's me. You can use any tea you love. Try out this unique drink.
