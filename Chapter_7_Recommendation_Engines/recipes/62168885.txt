Title:
Cold Veggie Ranch Party Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg. crescent roll dough
16 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 c. mayonnaise
1 pkg. dry ranch salad dressing mix
1 small head of broccoli
1 small head of cauliflower
1 carrot
1 red pepper
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Unroll crescent dough and press flat into the bottom of a large rectangular cookie sheet to create the pizza crust.
Bake for 12-15 minutes until lightly browned.
Cool crust completely.
Mix the cream cheese, mayonnaise and ranch dressing mix in a large bowl until well combined.
Spread ranch mixture evenly over the cooled crust.
Finely chop the broccoli, cauliflower and red pepper.
Peel and shred the carrot.
Scatter all the veggies evenly over the ranch spread.
Scatter the cheese evenly over the veggies and press lightly to set.
Put into the fridge for one hour, then cut into squares.

Description: 
A tasty appetizer to bring to your next shindig. Crescent roll crust with ranch/cream cheese spread and your favorite fresh veggie toppings. It's like a salad on a roll!
