Ingredients:
1/2 cup Milk or Cream
1/4 cup Whipped Vodka
1 1/2 teaspoons Oreo Instant Pudding Mix
Chocolate Syrup
Crushed Oreo Crumbs
Whipped Cream (optional)
1 mini Oreo (optional)

Directions:
Mix milk/cream, whipped vodka, and pudding mix in shaker with ice. Shake for 30 seconds.
Dip rim of glass in syrup, then dip rim in Oreo crumbs. Pour drink into glass.
Top with whipped cream and a mini Oreo if desired
