Title:
Breakfast Burrito Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4483428.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package Johnsonville® Original, Brown Sugar & Honey, or Vermont Maple Syrup Breakfast Sausage links
4 (6 inch) Mission® Super Soft Flour Tortillas
1/4 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons red pepper jelly
2 tablespoons sliced green onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Arrange sausage in an 8-in. square nonstick baking pan.  Bake sausage according to package directions.
Remove sausage from baking pan; set aside.
Cut tortillas into four equal quarters; cover with plastic wrap.
Place one tortilla wedge on a work surface; place one sausage link on wide end of wedge.   Brush pointed end of tortilla with water; roll up and press to seal. Place in pan, seam side down.
Repeat with remaining sausage links.
Bake for 5 minutes.
Turn seam side up and sprinkle with cheese. Bake 3 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Cool slightly.
Top with red pepper jelly and green onions.

Submitter: 
From the Kitchen at Johnsonville Sausage
Description: 
Breakfast sausages are rolled up in tortilla triangles, baked with shredded cheese, and served with red pepper jelly and green onions.
