Title:
Chocolate-Covered Marshmallow Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups  flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup milk
18 large marshmallows
FROSTING:
1 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
2 1/2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
pinch teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
2 tablespoons light cream

Directions:
Sift together flour, salt, soda and cocoa; set aside. In a mixing bowl, cream shortening and sugar; add egg, vanilla and milk. Add dry ingredients and mix well. Drop large cookie scoop about 2 in. apart on greased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F for 8 minutes. Do not overbake. Remove cookies from the oven and top each with a marshmallow. Return to oven for 2 minutes. Remove cookies to wire racks to cool.
Meanwhile, beat all frosting ingredients together. Spread frosting on each cookie
