Title:
Slow Cooker Pulled Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Chicken Breasts
5-6 cloves garlic minced
1 C spaghetti sauce
2 Tbsp Basil
1/4 C grated parmesan
*water (only if needed)
To Make Sandwiches:
Buns/Rolls
Provolone Cheese Slices
Grated Parmesan

Directions:
All of the ingredients minus water go into your freezer bag.
Cook on Low for 4-5 hours
Take out Chicken and Shred
Place chicken back in slow cooker - add a bit more sauce if needed and cook on low for another 30 minutes.
To Make Sandwiches
Heat oven to 350
Place chicken on one side of bun and provolone on the other
Top chicken with parmesan
Bake for about 10 minutes and serve
