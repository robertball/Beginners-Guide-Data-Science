Title:
Classic Pub Style Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (230 g) bag of tortilla chips
2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup of onion (red or white, either will do)
4-6 ripe tomatoes, seeds removed, finely diced
1/2 cup of chopped parsley
1/4 cup olive oil
juice of 1 lime
pinch of salt and pepper (optional)
1/2 cup sour cream (optional)
1/2 cup guacamole (optional)
1/2 cup extra salsa (optional)

Directions:
For Salsa: combine the onion, tomato, parsley, olive oil and lime juice in a medium bowl.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
Preheat the oven to 350 Degrees F.  Spread one layer of tortilla chips out on a pizza/cookie tray. Layer with spoonfuls of the salsa and sprinkle a layer of shredded cheese. Layer once more with chips, salsa and cheese.
Heat the nachos for 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbling.
Serve with your choice of sour cream, guacamole, and salsa on the side for dipping.

Submitter: 
sweets
Description: 
I got this recipe from summer camp and when I made them at home, my whole family was impressed. Layers of tortilla chips and salsa with the works on the side - tastes just like the nachos they serve at restaurants.  Perfect for a party!
