Title:
Candied Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Carrots, peeled and sliced diagonally
Brown sugar, to taste
Butter, to taste

Directions:
Steam carrots till tender/crisp (but mostly tender!), or to your liking
Stir in butter till melted and coating carrots to your liking
Sprinkle brown sugar over top, stirring gently to distribute evenly and as much as you'd like for desired taste

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Easy addition to basic carrots that takes them up a notch in the sweet department
