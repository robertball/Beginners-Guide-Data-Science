Title:
Basic Wheat Bread for Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup warm water (110 degrees F)
2 T. white sugar
1 package bread machine yeast
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 cup whole wheat flour
2 cups bread flour
1/4 cup dry milk powder
2 T. butter, softened
1 1/2 t. salt

Directions:
Place water, sugar and yeast in machine, let proof for 10 minutes. When 10 minutes is up and yeast is foamy, add the remaining ingredients. Select basic bread setting and press start.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Lynn Gunderson
Description: 
A soft wheat bread that's a good all purpose loaf. Originally adapted from diggetydog best bread machine bread.
