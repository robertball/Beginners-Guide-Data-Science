Title:
Easy Delectible Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 med to lrg potatos
10 eggs
3/4 cup real mayo
1/2 tsp mustard
1/2 cup real bacon bits
1 tbs dill weed
1/4 cup diced green onion
1 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Put potatoes in lrg pot of water and boil till fork goes through them with ease
Fill Medium sauce pan 3/4 way with water bring to boil
Carefully put eggs into boiling water and return to rapid boil approx.20 min
While they boil in large mixing bowl add the bacon, mayo, dill weed, mustard, green onion, and ground pepper.
Stir until well blended.  Set bowl of mix in fridge to cool while you finish potaoes and eggs
When eggs are hard boiled set entire pan water and all under cold running water and allow it to run approx 7 minutes.  Drain and peel eggs, dice them and add them to the wet mixure.  Drain the potaoes and let cool before peeling off the skin.  Cut into either squares or dice up if you prefer small chunks add to the remaining mixure and mix well.
Cool in fridge till ready to serve.
