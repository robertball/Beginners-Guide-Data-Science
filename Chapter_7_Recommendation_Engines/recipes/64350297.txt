Title:
Potato and Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cup chicken broth
4 cups cubed potatoes
2 cups sliced carrots
1/2 cup sliced celery
1 leek, sliced (use only the white part)
1/8 tsp white pepper
1/2 tsp dill weed
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup half & half
1 tbsp flour

Directions:
In stock pot, add broth, vegetables, and spices and cook until vegetables are tender.
Mix together half & half and flour and add to soup at the end to thicken.
Serve.
