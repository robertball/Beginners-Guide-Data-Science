Title:
Cranberry Chicken (or pork chops) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken breasts or pork chops
1 16 oz can whole berry cranberry sauce
1 cup (or 8 oz bottle) French or Russian Dressing
1 envelope onion soup mix

Directions:
Mix and cook in crockpot on low 6 hours or high 2-3 hours. Serve with potatoes (boiled or mashed) or rice.
