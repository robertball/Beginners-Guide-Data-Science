Title:
No Guilt Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (approx. 1 pound) Ground turkey prefer 93/7 or higher
1 package of favorite taco seasoning
2 hearts of Chopped romaine lettuce
low fat Sour cream (optional)
salsa:
1 can Black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can diced tomatoes with green chilies and lime juice
1 half small onion diced
1 cup frozen corn thawed
Sauce:
4oz of reduced fat cream cheese
1/4 cup of store bought salsa

Directions:
Drain and rinse black beans, place in medium bowl
Drain tomatoes and add to beans
Add thawed corn and diced onion to bean mixture.
Stir to combine and set aside.
In large skillet brown turkey over medium high heat. Drain any excess water and or fat out of pan.  Follow directions on seasoning packet.
In microwave safe dish combine store bought salsa and cream cheese.  Microwave on High for 1 minute or until smooth stirring every 20 seconds.  (you want this to be slightly runny so it coats your salad.)
Set aside.
Chop romaine lettuce and place into serving bowl or smaller individual serving size bowls.
Top with browned seasoned meat.
Add black bean and corn salsa on top.
Drizzle cream cheese mixture over entire salad. (a little goes a long way after you mix it all together)
Garnish with sour cream, chives, and/or cilantro leaves.

Submitter: 
A.Davala
Description: 
This addicting salad will have you craving more without the guilt and better yet it is easy on the wallet.  It's made with lean ground turkey and low fat sauce but you'd never know the difference. It has become a weekly staple in our house.
