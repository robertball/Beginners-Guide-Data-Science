Title:
Turkey Eggplant Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lb ground turkey
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 large eggplant, cubed
1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
3/4 cup seasoned dried bread crumbs
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Spray and 9x13 baking dish and a Dutch oven with nonstick cooking spray.  In the Dutch oven, add onions and garlic and saute until browned.  Add the turkey and cook til browned.  Add the eggplant and tomatoes, bell pepper, bread crumbs and basil.  Bring the mixure to a boil, stirring as often as needed.  Transfer the mixture to the baking dish.  Bake, covered with foil, 45 min.  Uncover and sprinkle with cheese and bake an additional 15 min.

Submitter: 
JUNEBUFF1
Description: 
In one pan, ground turkey, eggplant and tomatoes can be quickly combined to make a warm, filling casserole!
