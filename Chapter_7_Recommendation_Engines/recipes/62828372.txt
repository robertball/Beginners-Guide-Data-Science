Title:
Simple Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Boneless Chicken Breasts
1 small Onion, chopped
1 (1 oz. ) pkg. Chicken Taco Seasoning
1 (16 oz.) can Fat-Free Refried Beans
8 Whole Wheat Soft Tortilla Shells
16 oz. Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
Salt & Pepper, to taste
Optional Pico Topping:
2-3 Tomatoes, diced
1 small Onion, diced
1/4 C chopped Cilantro
Salt, to taste

Directions:
Place chicken breasts in large pot with salt & pepper.  Cover with water and boil until done, about 45 minutes.  Remove chicken and shred.  Reserve 1/2 C of broth to use in place of water for the seasoning mix.  Heat large skillet over med-high.  Add chopped onion, chicken, 1/2 C chicken broth, and package of seasoning.  Cook ingredients together, about 5-10 minutes, then add can of refried beans.  Stir all ingredients together.  Heat tortillas in microwave according to package directions.  Spoon 1/8 of mixture in center of a tortilla, sprinkle with a little bit of cheese, fold and place in greased 9x13.  Repeat process for remaining 7 tortillas.  Cover top of burritos with remaining cheese.  Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 20-30 minutes.  Serve with homemade Pico de Gallo (optional).

Submitter: 
Sara B.
Description: 
These chicken burritos are inexpensive and easy to make, especially if you cook the chicken ahead of time.
