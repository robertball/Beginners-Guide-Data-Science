Title:
Peking Chicken Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c uncooked white rice
1 can golden mushroom soup, undiluted
2-1/2 c water
1 lb chicken, chopped
1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
½ c onion, chopped
½ c peanuts, chopped
Sauce:
¼ c soy sauce
¼ c hoison sauce
1 T corn starch
2 T rice vinegar
½ T red pepper flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix rice, soup and water in a 9x13 baking dish.
Top with peppers, onions and chicken.
Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
Whisk together corn starch and soy sauce.
Mix in other sauce ingredients and pour over hot dish.
Top with peanuts.
Bake uncovered for an additional 20 minutes.
