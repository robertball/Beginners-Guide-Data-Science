Title:
Jim's Old Fashioned Oyster Dressing (Stuffing) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/742589.jpg
Ingredients:
4- pints oysters
2-loaves bread
2- sticks butter
1 1/2-onions minced
4-garlic cloves minced
2- stalks celery diced
1/2 bunch- parsley fresh

Directions:
Pull the bread in cubes and leave out for 24-48 hours.
Sautee the onions, garlic and celery in butter until the onions become translucent.
Add the Parsley and oysters ( including the juice) Mix everything in a large bowl.
Stuff that bird!

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This is a family recipe to stuff your Turkey with. my dad used to ask me to make a Thanksgiving dinner in June so he could have the dressing!! If you like oysters this will be your new tradition!
