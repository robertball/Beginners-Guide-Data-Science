Title:
Cranberry Pecan Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup steel cut oat flour
1/4 cup almond flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 t salt
1/4 t cinnamon
1 1/4 cup Coach's Oats
2 ounces light whipped butter
2 ounces applesauce
1/4 cup Splenda Brown
1/4 cup Splenda Measure
1 egg
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/2 cup Reduced Sugar Craisins
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix dry ingredients and oats.  Cream butter, applesauce and sugars until mixed.  Add egg and vanilla.  Mix well.  Add flour mixture until blended.  Stir in craisins and pecans.  Bake for 12 - 15 minutes.
