Title:
Mint Chocolate Leprecone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6408743.jpg
Ingredients:
3 parts (4.5 oz) Godiva Chocolate Liquor
1 part (1.5 oz) Peppermint Schnapps
Green Whip (Optional)
1/2 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
1 Tablespoon Confection Sugar
1/4 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
3-4 Drops Green Food Coloring
Chocolate Shavings (Optional)

Directions:
In a shaker filled with ice mix the Godiva and Peppermint Schnapps together. Shake vigorously for 2-3 minutes. Pour into glasses. Top with green whip and shaved chocolate.
To Make Green Whip.
Add first three ingredients into a small bowl, using the whisk on a hand mixer whisk the cream until thick. Add in food coloring until desired color.
Take a chocolate bar, using a cheese grater, grate chocolate for shaved chocolate.
To make non-alcoholic version, take chocolate milk, add 1/4 teaspoon mint extract top with whip and chocolate.
