Title:
Delish Meatball Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR MEATBALLS:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup finely grated Parmesan
1 egg
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
1 1/2 teaspoons dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 cup bread crumbs
FOR STEW:
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup sliced celery
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon sea salt (or to taste)
1/2 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
1 teaspoon
3 tablespoons flour
1 6 ounce can tomato paste
4 - 6 cups beef broth (as needed to barely cover meat and vegetables)
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1 bay leaf
2 cups potatoes, peeled and cut into 1" cubes
1 cup cubed carrots
1 10 ounce package frozen green peas

Directions:
FOR MEATBALLS:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, cheese, egg, basil, parsley, garlic powder, salt, and bread crumbs.
Using your hands, mix all ingredients until well blended.
Portion the meatballs using a small 1 to 1 1/2 ounce ice cream scoop.
Using your hands again, shape the meat into balls and place on a cookie sheet that has been sprayed with vegetable spray.
Bake for 20 minutes.
Remove from oven and set aside.
FOR STEW:
Place onion, garlic, celery and olive oil into a dutch oven and cook over medium heat for 3 minutes, stiring often.
Stir in salt and pepper.
Sprinkle flour over mixture and stir constantly for 2 minutes then stir in beef broth and tomato paste, until gravy is smooth.
Add remaining ingredients along with meatballs.
Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 90 minutes.
