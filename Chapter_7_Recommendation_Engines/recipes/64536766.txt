Title:
Slow Cooker Thai Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small jar green curry paste (or about 2-3 tbsp red curry paste)
2 12-oz cans of coconut milk
2 cups low sodium chicken stock
2 tbsp fish sauce
2 tbsp brown sugar
2-3 tbsp creamy peanut butter
1.5-2lbs chicken breasts, cut into cubes or small strips (to save time, ask the butcher to cut chicken in advance)
3 red bell peppers, seeded and cut into small slices (to save time, buy the precut strips at the grocery store)
1 onion, thinly sliced (or 1/2 bag frozen chopped onions)
1 heaping tbsp fresh ginger (or 1-2 heaping tbsp ginger paste)
1 heaping tbsp fresh lemongrass paste (OPTIONAL)
salt (optional)
cayenne pepper (optional)
1 bag frozen peas, thawed
juice of 1 lime
cilantro for garnish
cooked jasmine or white rice

Directions:
In slow cooker insert, mix the curry paste, coconut milk, chicken stock, fish sauce, brown sugar, peanut butter, onion, ginger, and lemongrass (optional).  Add the chicken and peppers.  Cook on high for 2.5 hours (check and make sure chicken is no longer pink inside).
Taste the broth - add salt and cayenne pepper, if needed.
Add the peas and cook on low for another 1/2 hour.  Stir in lime juice and serve with cilantro and rice.

An easy curry soup - lots of peas and pepper to make sure you get your vegetables!
