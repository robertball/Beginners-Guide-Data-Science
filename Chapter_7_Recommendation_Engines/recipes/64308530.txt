Title:
Fish Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter
1 cup chopped artichoke hearts
1 cup minced crabmeat
1 tablespoon dried parsley
2 cloves garlic
salt and pepper to taste
1 sleeve HEB triple onion crackers
4 (6 ounce) fillets swai
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly coat a 9x13 inch baking pan with cooking spray.
2. Melt butter in frying pan over medium heat. Add garlic and artichokes, saute for 1 minute.
3. Stir in crabmeat, parsley, salt, and pepper and cook 1 minute more.  Remove from heat and stir in crushed crackers.
4. Spread mixture over filets; Roll up filets, sprinkle with lemon juice and Parmesan cheese. , seal with toothpicks and place in prepared baking dish, and cover pan tightly with foil.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 21 minutes until fish flakes.
