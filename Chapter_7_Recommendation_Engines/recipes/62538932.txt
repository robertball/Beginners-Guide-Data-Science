Title:
Peanut Butter Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup margarine
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
2 cups icing sugar
1 cup chocolate chips
2 tbsp margarine

Directions:
Melt peanut butter and margarine together; add graham cracker crumbs and icing sugar.
Mix well and press into 9 X 13 inch pan.
Refrigerate while making topping.
Melt chocolate chips and margarine together; mix well.
Spread evenly over layer in baking pan. Cool completely before cutting into squares.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
These squares taste similar to peanut butter cups.
