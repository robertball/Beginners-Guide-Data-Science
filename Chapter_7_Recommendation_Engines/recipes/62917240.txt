Title:
Sweet and Sour Beef and Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1 chopped onion
1 chopped green bell pepper
1/2 cup tablespoons quick cooking oats
1 teaspoon garlic powder
3 cups chopped cabbage
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Place a large skillet over medium heat; cook the ground beef, onion, and green bell pepper in the hot skillet until the beef is completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes. Sprinkle the oats, salt, pepper and garlic powder, tomato sauce, cider vinegar, and brown sugar  into the beef mixture. Cook 5-10min. Stir chopped cabbage into the beef mixture. Place a cover on the skillet and simmer until the cabbage is tender, about 20-30 minutes.
Server over rice or as a stuffing in cooked rolls(for this place a few tablespoons of cabbage mixture into your favorite dough for rolls fold the dough over the filling and bake until cooked.)
