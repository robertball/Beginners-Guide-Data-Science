Title:
B and L's Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/416503.jpg
Ingredients:
8 strawberries, hulled
1/2 cup skim milk
1/2 cup plain yogurt
3 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
6 cubes ice, crushed

Directions:
In a blender combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar and vanilla. Toss in the ice. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Lisa and Brittany
Description: 
You'll love this icy cold strawberry smoothie.
