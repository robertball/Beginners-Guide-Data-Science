Title:
Mom's Famous Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Jiffy Muffin Mix
1 egg
1 tablespoon melted butter
3/4 cup milk
2/3 cup vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Pre~heat your waffle iron and grease it.
Then, mix muffin mix, egg, melted butter, milk,and vanilla yogurt.
Pour the mix onto your waffle iron and cook until slightly brown.
Then, Enjoy!

Submitter: 
birdluv4ever
Description: 
This is my mom's famous waffle recipe! The vanilla yogurt makes all of the difference!
