Title:
Harvest Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. cloves
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
3/4 cup pumpkin
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate morsels

Directions:
Grease 9x5 inch loaf pan.  Combine flour with soda, salt and spices.  Cream butter.  Gradually add sugar; cream well.  Blend in eggs; beat well.  At low speed, add dry ingredients alternately with pumpkin. beginning and ending with dry ingredients.  Stir in chocolate morsels and 1/2 cup walnuts.  Pour into pan and sprinkle with remaining nuts.  Bake at 350 degrees for 65-75 minutes.
