Title:
Spicy Bloody Mary Mix Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) bottle tomato-vegetable juice cocktail
1 1/2 lemons, juiced
1/2 teaspoon SPLENDAÂ® Brown Sugar Blend
1 tablespoon steak sauce
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/2 teaspoon celery salt

Directions:
In a large pitcher, combine juice cocktail, lemon juice and SPLENDA(R) Brown Sugar Blend. Season with Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, hot sauce and celery salt. Cover, and refrigerate 8 to 12 hours to allow flavors to meld.
