Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies with Cinnamon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2/3  cup shortening
1 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 1/2 cup white sugar
4 eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1  teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons cinnamon
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 teaspoon salt
4 cups rolled oats (quick)
2 packages chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
In a large bowl cream shortening, brown sugar and granulated sugar.  Add eggs and mix thoroughly.
Combine the baking soda, salt and flour and stir into creamed mixture
Add oatmeal and chocolate chips and stir until well blended.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet.  Bake for 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Julie Enyeart
Description: 
I've modified a traditional chocolate chip cookie recipe to include adding cinnamon, vanilla and a combination of shortening and butter.  These cookies are a big hit in my family.Modified from: Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies I
