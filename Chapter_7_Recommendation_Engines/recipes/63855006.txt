Title:
Honey Chai Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups liquid honey
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups unsalted butter
3 eggs well beaten
1 1/2 cups whipping cream
1 tbsp baking soda
1 tbsp cinnamon
1 tsp cloves
1 tsp salt
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cardamom
7-8 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
Cream together honey, sugar and butter.  Add well-beaten eggs.  Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Add 7-8 1/2 cups flour and let stand for 4-5 hours.
Spoon into walnut-size balls and bake at 350 for 14 minutes.
Glaze with quick icing (Icing sugar, cream and lemon juice - icing is a bit runny but will firm up).

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
I make this every year around Christmas. My friend brought it over one year and I've been making it ever since.
