Title:
Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3419334.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Avocado (diced)
1/2 medium red onion, sliced
2 tomatoes (chopped)
1/2 cucumber (chopped)
1/2 carrot (grated)
1/4 cup cilantro(coriander), chopped
1 teaspoon cumin seeds powder
2 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place diced avocados, chopped red onion, tomatoes, cucumber and carrot and chopped cilantro in one large bowl.
Add teaspoon of lemon juice, black pepper, cumin seeds powder and salt and mix it well.
Put salad aside with lid on the bowl for 10-15 minutes.
