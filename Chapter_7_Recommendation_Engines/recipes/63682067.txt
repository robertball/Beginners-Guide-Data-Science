Title:
Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs Chuck Roast
1 packet dry Hidden Valley ranch dressing mix
1 packet dry McCormick Au Jus mix
1 stick of Butter
.

Directions:
Put chuck roast in crock pot, Sprinkle with dry Hidden Valley ranch dressing mix, add packet of dry McCormick Au Jus mix, Put the stick of butter on top of the roast and cook on low for 7-8 hrs.
DO NOT ADD WATER
