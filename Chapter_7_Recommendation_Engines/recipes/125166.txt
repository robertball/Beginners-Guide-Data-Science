Title:
Quick Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991639.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups buttermilk baking mix
2/3 cup milk
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a bowl, stir in the biscuit mix, milk and cheese just until moistened. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto an ungreased baking sheet. Mix butter and garlic powder; brush over biscuits. Bake at 475 degrees F for 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Donna Engel
Description: 
Cheddar cheese adds a burst of sunny flavor to the flaky biscuits Donna Engel bakes in her Portsmouth, Rhode Island kitchen.
