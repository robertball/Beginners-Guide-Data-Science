Title:
Butternut Squash, Chicken and Quinoa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/965606.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs skinless, boneless chicken breasts or thighs
Salt & pepper to taste
1-1/2 lbs butternut squash
3-1/2 cups chicken broth
2 tsp olive oil
1 med onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp salt
4 cloves garlic, crushed
1-1/2 tsp oregano or Italian seasoning
1 - 14 oz can diced tomatoes
2/3 cup uncooked quinoa
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper.  Wrap in foil and place on baking sheet.
Cut butternut squash lengthwise.  Remove seeds and discard.  Cut squash in half again and place cut side down in 9" x 13" baking pan.  Add about a half inch of water to the pan and cover with foil.
Bake chicken and squash in 400 degree oven for 30 minutes.  Allow to cool until comfortable to handle.
Heat oil in skillet and saut√© onion and garlic until softened - about 5 minutes.
Remove squash from skin into a large stock pot.  Add chicken broth and mash or combine with a hand blender.
Cube cooked chicken and add to pot with remaining ingredients.
Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce to simmer and cook for about one hour.
Serve with crusty rolls or bread.
