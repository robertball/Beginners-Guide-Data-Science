Title:
PORCUPINE MEATBALLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, peeled
1 bell pepper, seeded
3 ribs celery
2 cloves garlic, peeled
olive oil
1 (28 oz.) can crushed tomatoes
1 (14.5 oz.) vegetable stock
salt and pepper to taste
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups cooked white rice
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp. celery salt
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1 beaten egg

Directions:
In a food processor, pulse the onion, bell pepper, celery and garlic to a very fine chop. Saute in olive oil until soft. Add crushed tomatoes, stock, salt and pepper and simmer while making meatballs.
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Spray a covered casserole dish with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, rice, onion, celery salt, garlic salt and pepper.  Add the beaten egg. Mix well. Shape into 1 1/2" balls. Place the formed meatballs into the casserole dish, pour the tomato mixture over all.
Cover and bake in a preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover, and cook for an additional 15 minutes.
Serve with a loaf of crusty bread and a green salad.

Description: 
Seasoned beef meatballs with rice are braised in a homemade tomato sauce. Great reheated the next day for a meatball sub with cheese.Modified from: Porcupine Meat Balls
