Title:
Cherie's Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans of beans - Garbanzo, red Kidney, White Kidney, Pinto, Black any combo
1 can green beans - optional
1 sm. red onion -diced
1 sm. bell pepper, any color -diced
1 C. Corn - frozen or fresh/cooked
1-2 stalks celery diced
1/2 C Olive Oil
1/2 C Seasoned Rice Vinegar
1/8 C Sherry -optional
1 Minced garlic clove or garlic powder
1/2  tsp Cumin, Chili Pwdr, salt
Cayenne to taste
Cilantro - fresh to taste- minced with stems removed

Directions:
1. Rinse and drain all beans, if using green beans cut to bite size
2.  Combine all beans, corn, celery, onion and bell pepper in a large mixing bowl
Dressing:
1. Combine all ingredients -taste and adjust as needed
Add dressing to bean mixture. May be done a day or two in advance.
Serve cold as is or add to rice or quinoa, add goat or feta cheese, toss over spinach or salad greens.

One of our family favorites. So handy to have on hand for snacks or lunch.  This was given to me from my BF Cherie who is a fabulous cook!
