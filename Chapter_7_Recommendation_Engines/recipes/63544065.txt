Title:
Amaratto Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Heavy Cream
1 Cup Milk
3/4 Cup Sugar
2 Tsp Amaretto Extract
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
1/4 Tsp Cinnamon Extract

Directions:
In a small pot combine heavy cream, milk and sugar. Heat on low until steam appears (about 10 minutes). Turn off heat and add extracts. Stir. Allow to cool and transport to sealed container. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
may2mac
Description: 
A sweet and delicious coffee creamer that mimics the chemical filled one that can be purchased at the grocery store.
