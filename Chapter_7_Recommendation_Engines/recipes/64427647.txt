Title:
Lake Erie Yellow Perch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
corn oil for frying
1 Tablespoon of butter
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 tbsp of table cream (18%)
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1 tsp paprika
1 tbsp of garlic powder.
3/4 pound of yellow perch fillets

Directions:
Heat 1/8 inch of oil and butter in a large heavy pan over medium-high heat.
Arrange three separate plates or shallow bowls for breading the fish. Combine flour, salt and pepper in one bowl, cream and eggs in another, and bread crumbs mixture in the third bowl.
Coat each fillet in the flour mixture, then dredge them in egg wash, then coat them well with bread crumbs.
Place fillets in frying pan. Cook until golden brown, about 5 to 7 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels and serve immediately with cole slaw and fries.

Submitter: 
Ron Lloyd
Description: 
This recipe is for pan fried yellow perch. it is an adaptation of the Canadian Walleye recipe on this site.  They are a delicious mild fish that are hard to find unless you live close to the great lakes and know someone who goes out fishing.
