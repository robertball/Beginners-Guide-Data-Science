Title:
Summer Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocado
1/4 cup pico de gallo
12 shakes green tabasco
red tabasco to taste
3 shakes Worcestershire
lime juice to taste
cilantro to taste
2 cloves roasted garlic

Directions:
Mash avocado with a fork.
Add remaining ingredients and mix.
Serve with chips or use on BLTs.
