Title:
Fudge-Nut Oatmeal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 cups packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspon almond extract
3 cups quick-cooking oats
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup of sliced almonds
FUDGE FILLING
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 tsp of almond extract

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and brown sugar. Add eggs and vanilla and almond extract; mix well. Combine oats, flour, almonds, baking soda and salt and ; add to the creamed mixture. Spread two-thirds in the bottom of an ungreased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan; set aside. For filling, heat milk, chocolate chips, butter and salt in a saucepan or microwave-safe bowl until melted. Remove from the heat; stir in vanilla and almond extract. Spread over oat mixture in pan. Drop remaining oat mixture by tablespoonfuls over chocolate. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 to 25 minutes.
