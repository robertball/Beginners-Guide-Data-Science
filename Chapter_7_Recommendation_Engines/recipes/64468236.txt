Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 spoon fulls of fun
1 cast member of the Hillywood show
3 Kale heads
Infinite rainbows
4 spoonfuls of pure goodness
So many marshmallows

Directions:
1. Chop the kale heads into perfect cubes
2. Mix the perfect cube shaped kale bites with the 5 spoonfuls of fun
3. Sprinkle the infinite rainbows and 4 spoonfuls of pure goodness while singing the "Shake it Off" parody by the Hillywood show with one of the cast members
4. Add so many marshmallow liberally

Submitter: 
Emma Balkoski
Description: 
Your fluffy dinosaur will love it and it can make any devil turn into any angel!
