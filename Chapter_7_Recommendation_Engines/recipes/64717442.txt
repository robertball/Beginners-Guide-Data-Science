Title:
Salsa Verde Pork Roast in Slow Cooker served over Cilantro Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5796989.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3-4 lb pork roast
1 16 oz jar of salsa verde
1 can of Rotel tomatoes with green chilis, liquid drained
1/2 cup dice onion
1/4 cup diced cilantro
3 cloves freshly minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste
Cilantro-Lime Rice
3 Cups Basmati Rice
1/4 cup Chopped Cilantro
Juice of one lime
Salt to taste

Directions:
Place pork roast in slow cooker.
Top with garlic, cilantro, onion, diced tomatoes and salsa verde.
Cook on high for 5 hours.
Shred pork and stir all ingredients together.
Let cook for another hour, so all of the flavors can come together.
Rice Directions-
Cook rice according to instructions.
Fluff rice with a fork.
Stir in cilantro, lime juice and salt.
