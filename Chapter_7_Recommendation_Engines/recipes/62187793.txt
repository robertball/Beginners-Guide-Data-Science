Title:
Eggplant and Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
4 tablespoons butter
8 ounces seasoned bread crumbs
1 large eggplant,peeled and diced
2 large zucchini, diced
2 yellow squash,diced
1 onion, chopped
1 tomato, chopped
1 teaspoon dried thyme
2 cups shredded Colby cheese(fiesta cheese)
1 cup parmesean cheese
garlic powder to taste
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Place eggplant, zucchini,yellow squash, tomato, onion into a large skillet.  Season with thyme, salt, and pepper.  Cook and stir over medium low heat for 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from heat.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F ( 175 degrees C).  Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
Layer vegetables, cheese, and bread crumbs in the dish until all ingredients have been used, ending with cheese.
Bake for 30 to 40 minutes.
