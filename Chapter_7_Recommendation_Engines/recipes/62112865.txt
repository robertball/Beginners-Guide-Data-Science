Title:
Hoppin' John in the Summertime Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups cooked long-grain white and wild rice mix
1 can black-eyed peas, well drained
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1/2 cup finely chopped bell pepper
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
1 tbsp E.V.O.O.
pinch or two of salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 lb. lump crabmeat, shell pieces removed
dash or two of your favorite hot sauce
1 large tomato, sliced

Directions:
Combine all but the tomato and toss in a large salad bowl.  Serve with the tomato slices.

Submitter: 
Kris Swiggum
Description: 
A hearty Low Country stew adapts itself to a southern summer salad.  I got this from my Low Country mother-in-law, who tweaked an idea from her mother's hoppin' john soup one year when it was just too hot.
