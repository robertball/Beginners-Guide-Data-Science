Title:
Chicken in Roasted Poblano Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988961.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup KRAFT Zesty Italian Dressing, divided
8 (4 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
4 large poblano chiles, roasted, peeled and deveined
1 cup BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream
1 chicken bouillon cube

Directions:
Heat 2 Tablespoon of the dressing in large skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook 5 minute on each side or until browned on both sides. Add remaining 2 Tablespoon dressing.
Meanwhile, place chiles, sour cream and bouillon cube in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.
Pour chile mixture over chicken in skillet; bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover. Cook 10 minute or until chicken is cooked through (165 degrees F).

Submitter: 
BREAKSTONE'S
Description: 
A creamy sauce with roasted poblano chilies and BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream adds a zesty dimension to simmered chicken breasts for a quick weeknight meal.
