Title:
Coffee Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1977826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
1/2 cup water
1 1/2 tsp. instant coffee granules
2 T sugar
1 cup miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Spray 8"x8" pan, wipe with paper towel to remove most of oil.
Combine gelatin, water, instant coffee and sugar in small saucepan.  Let sit for 1 minute.
Heat over low until gelatine dissolves, about 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Add marshmallows, stir constantly until they start to melt. Remove from heat, continue stirring until they no longer dissolve.
Pour into pan.  Refrigerate for 1 hour, until firm.
Start rolling up from one side, moving back and forth across pan until tightly rolled.  With seam side down. cut into slices between 1/2" to 1" thick, (depending on amount you wish to have).

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Coffee flavor gelatine spirals with light fluffy layer and darker gelatin layer.  Great snack!
