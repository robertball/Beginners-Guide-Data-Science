Title:
Pink Stuff  (Stawberry Jello Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/944249.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) cans crushed pineapple (undrained)
1 (6 ounce) envelope strawberry gelatin
1 pint cottage cheese
1 cup chopped pecans
2 cups whipped cream
4 tablespoons white or powdered sugar

Directions:
In a small pot, bring pineapple (with juice) and gelatin to a boil.  Boil for 3 minutes.  Remove from heat, and allow to cool completely.
Add cottage cheese and pecans to gelatin mixture.
Whip whipped cream with sugar until firm.  Fold into gelatin mixture.
Spread in any dish (9X13 or casserole dish).  Refrigerate until firm.  2-3 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Brian
Description: 
This is a wonderful, light gelatin salad.  The pineapple give it a little zip while the whipped cream makes it fluffy sweet.  My family actually eats this instead of cranberry sauce during the holidays!  Simple and yummy!
