Title:
Staci's Shrimp & Tortellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688626.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Cheese Filled Tortellini
1/2 lb. Medium Shrimp, peeled
2T Olive Oil or Butter
1 bunch Green Onions, chopped
2 cloves Minced Garlic
1/4c White Wine
2T Dried Basil
1/2c Shredded Parmesan

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package directions. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, heat oil or butter over medium heat. Add garlic and green onions. Cook 3-4 minutes. Add shrimp, wine and basil. Continue cooking until shrimp are no longer pink. Stir in tortellini and parmesan. Heat until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Staci
Description: 
One of my all-time favorites. I usually double the recipe because the leftovers are so yummy!
