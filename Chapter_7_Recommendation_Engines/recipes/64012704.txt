Title:
Celery Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 green onion - sliced
1 tbsp. whole-grain mustard
Juice of 1 lemon
1/3 cup olive oil
1/2 tsp. coarse salt
1 bunch of celery, sliced
1 cup toasted, coarsely chopped pecans
Parmesan cheese shavings

Directions:
Combine green onion, mustard, lemon juice, olive oil and salt.
Whisk well.
Toss with celery and pecans then top with Parmesan if using.
