Title:
Spicy Steak Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large potatoes, unpeeled
2 tbsp of Mrs. Dash and Spicy seasoning
2 tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Wash and cut potatoes into wedges. Place potato wedges in paper towels and pat dry. In a large, bowl, toss the potatoes with seasoning blend, olive oil and garlic.
Coat a baking sheet with cooking spray. Arrange potatoes in a single layer on baking sheet. Bake at 425 for 20 min. Turn potatoes and bake for an additional 15 min until browned and tender.
