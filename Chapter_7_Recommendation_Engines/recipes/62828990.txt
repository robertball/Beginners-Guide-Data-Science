Title:
Hint of Mint Double Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. white sugar
1/2 c. butter, softened
1 egg
1 t. vanilla
3 drops mint extract
2 c. flour
1/4 c. cocoa powder
1/2 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°F.  Cream butter and sugar; add eggs and flavorings and beat well.  Add flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.  Stir in chocolate chips.  Drop by teaspoon onto paper-lined cookie sheets and bake for 8-10 minutes or until slightly underbaked.  Cool approximately 1 minute before transferring to a plate to cool.  While cookies are warm, enjoy one--or two--with a glass of milk.

Submitter: 
Anne
Description: 
Soft chocolate cookies with a hint of mint flavor--a midsummernight's dream to be sure.
