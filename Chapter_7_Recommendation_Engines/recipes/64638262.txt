Title:
Pork egg roll in a bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground pork
14 oz coleslaw mix
5 green onions , chopped
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 garlic cloves, chopped
3 T sesame oil
2 T garlic chile paste
3 T Coconut aminos acids
2 T sesame seeds, toasted
1 T Rice Wine Vinegar

Directions:
Heat  Sesame oil.
Add white part sliced green onions, onions and garlic and cook until translucent.
Add pork, garlic chile paste, salt, pepper and cook until pork is done
Add coleslaw mix, coconut aminos and rice wine vinegar.  Cook coleslaw to desired level of done.
In another pan toast sesame seeds to golden.  When pork and coleslaw mix is ready, sprinkle sesame seeds and green onion on top and serve.
