Title:
Champagne Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/824481.jpg
Ingredients:
1 quart vanilla ice cream
1 quart prepared lemonade
1 (750 milliliter) bottle champagne

Directions:
Place the softened ice cream into a punch bowl. Pour in lemonade and champagne. Stir briefly then serve.

Submitter: 
aussiemum
Description: 
A delicious and refreshing drink. The combination of the ice cream and the champagne is very addictive.
