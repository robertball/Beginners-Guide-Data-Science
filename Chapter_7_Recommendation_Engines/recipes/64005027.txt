Title:
Mom's Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 small container of sour cream
* 1 small container of cottage cheese
* 1 large jar of mushroom spagetti sauce
* 2 pounds or more of hamburger, fried and drained
* Lasagna noodle strips
* Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Mix sour cream, cottage cheese, spaghetti sauce and hamburger together, set aside.
Boil lasagna noodles until tender.
Layer hamburger mixture and noodles in a baking dish then top with mozzarella cheese. Bake at 400 degrees F until cheese is bubbly and tan on top.
