Title:
Curried Vegetables with Coconut Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C red onion, julienned
2 tbs poblano peppers, diced
1/3 C celery, diced
1/3 C carrots, 1/4 " slices
1 garlic clove, minced
2 tsp fresh ginger, minced
4 C cauliflower, in 1 1/2 " pieces
1 tbs EVOO
1/2 C organic vegetable broth
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 C light coconut milk
1 tbs lemon juice
1 tsp fresh cilantro
1 tbs fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Directions:
In a saute pan heat the oil on medium heat.
Add the peppers, onions, garlic, ginger, carrots, and celery, sauteeing till tender, 8-10 min.
Add the cauliflower, broth, and all herbs and spices and bring to a simmer (do not boil).
Cover, cook an additional 10 - 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Once the cauliflower is tender, stir in the coconut milk, lemon juice, and cilantro and bring back to a simmer for 3-5 minutes and allow the liquid to thicken slightly.
Serve with a bit of parsley sprinkled over the top.
