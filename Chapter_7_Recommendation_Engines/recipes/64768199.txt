Title:
Camembert Baked  /  "Gebakener Camembert" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Egg
2 tbsp. Bread Crumbs
2 Tbsp. Flour
1 x 3 oz Camembert Cheese, Wheel.
2 oz. Cranberry Sauce  ( With Cranberry Pieces)
1 oz Sprigs of Parsley
Salt &  Pepper
(Deep Fat Fryer)

Directions:
Unwrap the Camembert;  Dredge in Flour, Beaten Egg, and Breadcrumbs.
(Fry for a Minute or Two in a Deep Fat Fryer). Drain on Paper. Center on  a Serving Plate.  Take Large sprigs of Parsley;  rub dry and Fry for 5 Seconds.   serve over the Camembert and Garnish with the Cranberry Sauce.
Serve with Buttered Toast.
