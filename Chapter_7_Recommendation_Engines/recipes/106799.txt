Title:
Banana Colada Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983494.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
1 small ripe banana, sliced
3 tablespoons SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 cup reduced-fat coconut milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender in the order listed; process mixture until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This smoothie is a refreshing tropical treat.
