Title:
Focaccia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Plain flour-315g
Dried instant yeast-7g
Warm water-175ml
Olive oil-6 tablespoons
Fresh rosemary-3 tablespoons, chopped
Garlic-2 cloves, chopped

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 230 degrees C(450 degrees F). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
Place flour and yeast in a bowl, mix in warm water and 3 tablespoons of olive oil. Knead for 10-15mins.
Place in a greased bowl, cover and allow to rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
Briefly knead the dough again, pat into a round shape at 1.5cm(1/2 inch) thick and place on the baking sheet. Cover and allow to rise in a warm place for a further 30-40 minutes.
Make dimples on the top. Brush with 2 tablespoons olive oil, sprinkle on rosemary. Bake for 12mins. Remove from oven, sprinkle with garlic and drizzle with remaining oil. Bake for 5 mins more.
