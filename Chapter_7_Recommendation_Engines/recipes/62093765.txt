Title:
my version of homemade so cheezy baked macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
400g macaroni
1 tspn olive oil
1 tbspn garlic
1 tbspn onions
1/2 c mushroom
500 ml spaghetti sauce (sweet style)
1 c stripped chicken breast
1 tbspn sugar
1/2 c grated cheese
salt and pepper to taste
**1 tspn red bell pepper (optional)
1 c all purpose cream
1/2 c cheese spread

Directions:
1. Cook the macaroni pasta according to package instruction
2. In a large pan saut√© garlic and onion in olive oil
3. Mix in the chicken strips then the mushroom and red bell pepper (optional)
4. Mix in the spaghetti sauce, add the sugar, salt and pepper
5. Add the grated cheese. let it simmer for a while
6. Stir in the cooked macaroni. mix well. Set aside.
8. Pre heat oven to 350 deg F (175 deg C)
7. In a small pan stir all purpose cream and cheese spread (low heat), stir until cheese spread melted or when the mixture starts to boil. Set aside.
9. Arrange the macaroni in the baking dish. Top it with the all purpose cream and cheese spread mixture. Bake 15 mins or until golden brown.
**you can top the macaroni with grated cheese before baking it.**
Enjoy.
