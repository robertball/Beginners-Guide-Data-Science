Title:
All Natural Pink Frosting! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg confectioners sugar
1/2 cup Butter
1 tsp vanilla
6-7 pureed strawberries

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl  and mix with stand-up or hand held mixer. Mix gradually increasing your speed until on high and beat until smooth.

Submitter: 
AlyPurdue
Description: 
This is a great way to make your valentines day baking more colourful without the added harsh ingredients! AND adds an extra brilliant flavour to compliment a chocolate based cake or vanilla!
