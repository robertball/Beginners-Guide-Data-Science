Title:
Venison Shish Ka Bobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SHISH KA BOB
5 lb Venison, cut in large chunks
6 Whole onion, quartered
10 Whole jalapeno chile pepper, cut in half
4 Whole green bell pepper, sliced large
MARINADE
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Cooking oil
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Dry mustard
1 tb Garlic powder
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper, ground

Directions:
Marinate meat for at least 4 hours.
Alternately place vegtables and meat on Ka-Bob skewers. Marinate in sauce above. Cook on open grill over hot coals turning often until done.

Description: 
Grilled marinated venison skewered with vegetables with a kick.
