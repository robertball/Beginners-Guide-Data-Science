Title:
Lemon Cheese Cake Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 cup flour
1/2 cup chopped nuts
1 TBSP lemon juice
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup sugar
8 oz. cream cheese
1 egg
2 TBSP milk
1 lemon peel, grated
1/2 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Blend flour, sugar and butter until crumbly. Mix in nuts. Reserve 1/2 cup for topping. Press remainder into an oiled 8 inch square pan and bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
Soften cream cheese, blend in sugar and rest of ingredients. Spread over baked crust. Sprinkle with reserved crust. Bake an additional 25 minutes.
