Title:
Corned Beef and Cabbage I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds corned beef brisket with spice packet
3 cloves garlic
black pepper
10 small red potatoes
5 carrots, peeled and cut into 3-inch pieces
1 large head cabbage, cut into small wedges

Directions:
Place corned beef in large pot or Dutch oven and cover with 1 beer and water. Add the spice packet that came with the corned beef.  Cover pot and bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.  Simmer approximately 50 minutes per pound or until tender.
Add whole potatoes and carrots and cabbage, and cook until the vegetables are almost tender. About 15 more minutes. Remove meat and let rest 15 minutes.
Place vegetables in a bowl and cover. Add a little broth. Slice meat across the grain.
