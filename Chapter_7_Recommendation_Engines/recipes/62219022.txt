Title:
Chicken Adobo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 kilograms chicken legs and thighs
175 ml water
175 ml white vinegar
60 ml soya sauce
1 tsp sugar
1 medium onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, crushed
1 tsp whole peppercorn, crushed
2 bay leaves
salt to taste

Directions:
Rinse chicken and pat dry with paper towel. Place in a 6 litre pot.
Pour water, vinegar, soya sauce, and sugar into the same pot. Add the onion, garlic, peppercorns and bay leaves.
Bring to a boil for at least 2 minutes, then cook over low heat for 30 minutes. Remove chicken from pot when cooked, leaving the liquid to simmer until it thickens. Add salt to taste.
Return chicken to the pot and mix to coat the chicken with the sauce. Continue cooking for 3 minutes. Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
lola
Description: 
This is a family favourite and is served over rice. Easy to prepare especially when you are in a hurry.
