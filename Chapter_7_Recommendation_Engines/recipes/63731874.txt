Title:
Best Steak Marinade in existence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup oilive oil
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tbl garlic powder
3 tbl dryed basil
1 11/2 tsp ground white pepper
1 1/2 tbl dried parsley flakes
1/4 hot pepper sauce
1 tsp dried minced garlic

Directions:
Put all ingredients in blender and blend.  Pour over meat and marinate for 8 hours.
