Title:
Easy Layered Taco Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (7.25 oz) macaroni & cheese
1 lb ground beef
1 pkg (1.25 oz) taco seasoning mix
1 cup mild salsa

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F. Make Macaroni & Cheese. Cook meat with taco seasoning. Layer seasoned meat, Mac & Cheese and salsa in a 2 qt casserole. Bake for 20 minutes or until heated through.
