Title:
Hacienda Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984794.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (16 ounce) jar ORTEGA® Thick & Chunky Salsa
tortilla chips

Directions:
BEAT cream cheese in small mixer bowl until smooth.  Gradually beat in salsa.  Cover; refrigerate for at least 2 hours.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
What could be more simple than an appetizer that only requires two ingredients? Just add tortilla chips, family and friends, and the enjoyment begins!
