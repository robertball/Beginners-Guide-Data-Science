Title:
7 Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Hot Sausage
1 1/2 cup Chopped Onions
1 can Tomato Soup
1 6 oz. can Tomato Paste
3/4 cup Brown Sugar
2 cans Pork & Beans
2 cans Green Beans
1 can Butter Beans
1 can Chili Beans
1 can Kidney Beans
1 can Black Beans
1 can Black Eyed Peas

Directions:
Cook hot sausage and onions until browned & tender. Drain all beans except for pork & beans and chili beans. Add tomato soup, tomato paste, and sugar to sausage and onions. Pour into a crock pot. Add beans. Cook in crock pot for 4 to 5 hours or in a oven for an hour at 350.

Submitter: 
shayalanna
Description: 
Simple 7 Bean Salad. You can substitute any & all of the beans - best when many varieties are used.
