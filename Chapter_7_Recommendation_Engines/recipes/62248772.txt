Title:
Figs w/ rosemary and goat cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz soft goat cheese
1 t honey
1 t choppen fresh rosemary/ whole for garnish
18 plump figs
4-6 branches soaked in water for skewers
1 T olive oil

Directions:
Combine cheese, honey and chopped rosemary, put in a pastry bag.
Squeeze about 1 t in each fig.
Thread a skewer through to make a hole then remove and thread rosemary through each fig. Brush with oil.
Grill 5-8 min.
