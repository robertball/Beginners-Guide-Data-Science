Title:
Basil Infused Fried Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 leaves basil
Extra-virgin olive oil
1 large egg
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1/2 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1 large red tomato, thinly sliced (about 6 slices)

Directions:
Take 4 leaves of basil and add to a heated pan over a medium temperature.  Add in about 2 tablespoons of olive oil.  Let it get very hot then remove basil leaves.
Take a plate and add in bread crumbs.  Whisk egg in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
Dip each slice of tomato in the egg then coat both sides lightly with bread crumbs.  Place in the pan for 1-2 minutes per side.  Place on a plate and do in batches.  Take remaining basil leaves and break apart on the fried tomatoes and enjoy immediately.
Eating with your fingers in acceptable!
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
These were inspired by a fresh basil planter given to me from my girlfriend.
