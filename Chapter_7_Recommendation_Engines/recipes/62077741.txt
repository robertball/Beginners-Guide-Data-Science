Title:
Ashourah Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cup seeded raisins, chopped fine
1 1/2 pounds granulated sugar
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup chopped toasted almonds
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup chopped hazelnuts
1 pound borgoul or brown rice, cooked until tender
Salt
1 teaspoon rose water or almond extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon or nutmeg

Directions:
1. Bring sugar and raisins in the water to a boil; Remove from heat and add nuts.
2. Stir bogoul or brown rice into the raisins and nut mixture; cook about 5 minutes. Fold in the rose water or almond extract.
3. Serve a heaping spoonful of this mixture over more nuts and top with an almond. Add a sprinkling of cinnamon or nutmeg if desired.
