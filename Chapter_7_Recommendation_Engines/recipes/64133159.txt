Title:
Ground Beef and Chopped Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon olive oil;
1 large onion, chopped;
1 1/2 pounds, ground beef;
1 teaspoon garlic powder;
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes;
1 Tablespoon Italian seasoning;
1 small cabbage, chopped or 1 14 ounce package of the cut cabbage that is used for coleslaw;
2 14.5 ounce cans of diced tomatoes;
1 14.5 ounce can of tomato sauce;
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil over medium heat in a large heavy pot, such as a 6 quart dutch oven.
When oil is just hot, add onion. Saut√© onion until translucent.
Add meat and brown, breaking up the meat while it cooks until the meat is cooked through and there is no pink.
Mix in the garlic powder, red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning, and a dash of salt.
Mix in cabbage, diced tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer covered until the cabbage is fork tender (about 25 minutes).
Drain using slotted spoon. Serve with rice.

Description: 
This is a hearty meal that's easy to make and keeps well. I serve it with rice and sweet cornbread.
