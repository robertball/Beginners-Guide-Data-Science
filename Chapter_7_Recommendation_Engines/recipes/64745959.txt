Title:
Apple Texas Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup water
1 cup applesauce (unsweetened)
2 eggs
2/3 c buttermilk
2 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
FROSTING:
1/2 cup  butter
1 cup light brown sugar (packed)
1/4 milk
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350-degrees. Grease a jelly-roll pan and set aside.
CAKE:
Add the flour and sugar to a large, nonreactive (glass) bowl. Mix it up and set aside.
In a small saucepan add, oil, butter, water, and applesauce, turn the heat to medium-high and bring to a boil.
Pour the boiling oil mixture over the flour and sugar and mix until well blended.
Add the eggs, cinnamon, baking soda, vanilla, and buttermilk and mix with a beater until well blended.
Pour into the jelly-roll pan and bake for 20-25 minutes until case tester comes out clean.
FROSTING
Add the butter and brown sugar to a saucepan and cook on low until butter is melted and sugar is dissolved.
Whisk in milk and stir until combined.
Remove from heat and allow to cool for 1 minute. Add in the vanilla and combine
Add the powdered sugar and mix with a beater until smooth
Pour over the cake and let it cool.
