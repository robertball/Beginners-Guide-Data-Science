Title:
Summer Snow Cone Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 cup water
1 (0.13 ounce) package unsweetened, fruit-flavored soft drink mix

Directions:
In a saucepan, stir together the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and water. Bring to a boil, and boil for about 1 minute. Remove from heat, and stir in the drink mix. Allow to cool, and store in a container for pouring. Pour over shaved ice to flavor.

Description: 
Here's the secret to snow cone syrup made at home.  It's perfect over shaved ice on a hot summer day.
