Title:
Siple's CrotchPot Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2233031.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Stick of Butter
1 Normal Size Chuck Roast (enough for 2 people)
1 Packet of Dry Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing Mix
1 Packet of Dry Auju Seasoning Mix
1 Jar of Peppercinis
Long Wheat Sub-Style Buns
Provolone Cheese Slices
Mozzerella Cheese Slices
Garlic & Butter for Buns
Optional:
Jalopenos
Olives
An Additional Auju packet to make Auju sauce for dipping

Directions:
Cover meat with the 2 packets of dry seasoning
Put the meat in the crotchpot
Dump the remainder of the seasoning ontop of the roast
Put the stick of butter on top of the roast
Dump the jar of pepperchinis on top
DO NOT ADD WATER
Cook in the crotchpot 8 hours on LOW
Take your wheat buns and spread crushed garlic and butter on them
Broil in oven under buns get crispy
Then place meat from crotchpot on the buns, add provolone and mozzerella cheese on top
Place sandwiches back in oven and broil until cheese is melted
Optional:
Jalopenos
Olives
An Additional Auju packet to make Auju sauce for dipping
VOILA!
