Title:
Creamy Brie baked in Puff Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Brie Cheese Wheel
1/4 chopped dried Cranberries
1/4 chopped Glazed Walnuts or Pecans
3 tbls of Honey
1 egg white
1 Puff Pastry Sheet

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 & lightly spray foil lined baking sheet.
Slice one top side off, just enough to get the 'skin' off of the Brie
Place Puff Pastry on your work surface; mound nuts, dried cranberries and drizzle with honey.
-spread out only to the match the size of your cheese wheel.
Place your cheese 'skinless' side down and fold over your puff pastry edges to completely seal.
Turn upside down or smooth side up and place on a lightly sprayed baking sheet.
Brush with egg white
Bake for 25 or until golden brown

Great holiday or quick and easy appetizer for any occasion. I like to serve with sliced Baquette
