Title:
Moist and Delicious Strawberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 C sugar
1 C vegetable oil
1 t vanilla
1 C quick oats
2 C flour
1 T cinnamon
1 t baking soda
1 t salt
1/2 t baking powder
2 C strawberries, crushed

Directions:
In a large bowl beat eggs and sugar.  Add oil and vanilla and beat.  Mix in flour, oats, cinnamon, baking soda, salt, and baking powder.  Add strawberries and mix by hand.
Pour into 2 greased loaf pans (9x5x3).  Bake at 350 F for 1 hour, unto toothpick comes out clean.

A wonderful breakfast bread with oats and strawberries.
