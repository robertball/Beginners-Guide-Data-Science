Title:
Hotdog Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 uncooked crescent rolls (pillsbury etc)
1 package hot dogs
1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
10 slices cooked bacon (wiggly NOT CRISPY)
toothpicks

Directions:
Coat a cookie sheet with non-stick spray.
Cut a slit lengthwise in each hotdog.
Fill each hotdog with a little cheddar cheese.
Wrap 1 slice of bacon around each hotdog.
Wrap a crescent roll around each hotdog.
Use toothpick to hold together.
Bake according to crescent roll package instructions.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Toast
Description: 
Kids and adults both will love these simple little treats. Use as an appetizer
