Title:
Pomegranate Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448517.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pomegranate juice (I use POM Wonderful)
1/3 cup orange juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 tsp  fresh ginger, minced
1 tsp salt
1 tsp fresh black pepper
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 lbs raw shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Combine first seven ingredients in a small bowl. Mix until well combined.
Place shrimp into a ziploc bag or deep glass bowl. Pour the juice mixture over the shrimp. Stir well and make sure all of the shrimp are coated.
Marinate in refrigerator for 45 minutes.
Preheat grill.
Drain off the shrimp from the marinade and thread on skewers. Discard marinade.
Grill for 3 minutes on each side, until pink and slightly curled.
Remove from grill, serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
The fruitiness of the pomegranate juice combined with the orange juice is nice change without being overpowering.
