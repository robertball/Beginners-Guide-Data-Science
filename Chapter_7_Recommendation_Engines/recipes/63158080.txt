Title:
Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp Peanut, Sesame, or Vegetable Oil
1 clove of garlic, chopped
1 inch piece of fresh ginger peeled and diced
1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 lb boneless skinless chicken breast, evenly and thinly sliced
1/2 large onion cut into segments
3 carrots, peeled and julienned
1/2 red bell pepper, julienned
1/2 yellow bell pepper, julienned
1/2 orange bell pepper, julienned
1 head of broccoli, cut into pieces
2 cups of sugar snap peas
1 can whole baby corn, rinsed, drained & halved (found in Asian section of supermarket)
1 can bean sprouts, rinsed and drained (found in Asian section of supermarket)
1/4 cup Hoisin Sauce
1/4 cup Low Sodium Soy Sauce
1 tsp cornstarch
2 tsp cold water
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
Heat oil in a wok or a deep large frying pan over medium-medium high heat. Add the garlic, ginger, and crushed red pepper flakes and cook for about 1 minute. Add the chicken stirring until cooked. Remove chicken and set aside. Next add the onion, carrots, and bell peppers stirring constantly for 2 minutes. Next add the broccoli, sugar snap peas, baby corn, bean sprouts, soy sauce and hoisin sauce stirring constantly for 4 more minutes. In a separate dish mix the cold water and cornstarch. Add this mixture to the hot pan. Stir and then add the cooked chicken back in. Stir sauce evenly all over for about a minute. Serve over hot rice.
