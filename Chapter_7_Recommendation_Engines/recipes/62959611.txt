Title:
Rice Pudding-stove top Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts whole milk
1/2 lb. rice
1 c. sugar
2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 eggs
raisins to taste at least 1/2 c.
cinnamon

Directions:
Over low flame heat milk, stir in sugar, rice and vanilla.  Simmer on low boil until rice absorbs most of the milk.  (There should be some liquid left).  Remove from heat, mix in 2 well beaten eggs and raisins.  pour into shallow pan.  Cover with a dusting of cinnamon and cool until thick.
