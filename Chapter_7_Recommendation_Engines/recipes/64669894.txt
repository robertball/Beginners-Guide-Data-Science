Title:
Big Ray's Crack Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4581193.jpg
Ingredients:
5 regular cans of green beans, drained
12 slices bacon, cooked until crisp
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter, melted
7 tsp soy sauce
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic powder

Directions:
Put the drained beans in a 9" x 13" pan.  Crumble the cooked bacon pieces on top. Mix the remaining ingredients ( the crack sauce). Pour over the beans and bake 40 minutes at 350. Toss and serve
