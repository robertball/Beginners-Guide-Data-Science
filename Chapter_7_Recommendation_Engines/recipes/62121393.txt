Title:
Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion (chopped)
4 cloves of garlic (minced)
1 small green peeper (diced)
28 ounce can diced tomatoes
16 ounce can tomato sauce
6 ounce can tomato paste
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon oregano
2 teaspoon basil

Directions:
Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in large saucepan.
Cook until meat is brown and vegetables are tender.
Drain grease and return to pan.
Add the rest of the ingredients.
Stir and combine well.
Simmer for 1 hour; stirring occasionly.
