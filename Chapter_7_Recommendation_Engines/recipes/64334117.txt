Title:
Blue Cheese Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
4 green onions, finely chopped
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 clove garlic, minced
6 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
dash of worcestershire
dash of tabasco
dash of lemon juice
garlic salt to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise, sour cream, green onions, dried parsley, blue cheese, worcestershire, tabasco, lemon juice, minced garlic and garlic salt. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Blue cheese crumbles kindly lend their distinctive flavor to this simple, smooth mixture. Serve with corn chips. This dip tastes better the longer it chills before serving.Modified from: Blue Cheese Dip II
