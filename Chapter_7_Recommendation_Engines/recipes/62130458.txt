Title:
Chicken Salad Sandwiches With Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12.5 ounce can Premium Chunk White Chicken in water
1 medium Red Delicious apple, diced
3 medium stalks celery, diced
2 Tablespoons finely minced yellow onion
2 Tablespoons fresh chopped parsley
3/4 C mayonnaise
3/4 C sour cream
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon dijon style mustard
1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Drain the chicken thoroughly.
In a small bowl, combine the diced apple and the lemon juice. Mix to coat.
In a separate bowl, blend the mayonnaise, sour cream, mustards, honey, parsley, black pepper, and salt.
Drain any excess lemon juice from apples. In a medium bowl, combine the chicken, celery, onion, and apple. Stir in dressing mixture until all ingredients are incorporated evenly.
Cover and refrigerate 2 hours or overnight.
Stir and serve as a Chicken Salad Sandwich filler with lettuce, or on a bed of greens as a salad!

Submitter: 
LITTLE OREGON GIRL
Description: 
This is a delicious apple infused chicken salad. Can be used on top of romaine lettuce as a salad topper, or DELICIOUS as chicken salad sandwiches (our favorite!). The blend of apples, chicken, onions, and the slight crunch of celery make this to die for!
