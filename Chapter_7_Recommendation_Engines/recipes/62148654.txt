Title:
Pati's Green Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	1 ½ lbs hamburger meat
•	½- 1 bell pepper, diced
•	½ yellow onion, diced
•	8 (fajita sized) flour tortillas
•	1 28 oz can medium green enchilada sauce
•	12 oz can mild green chilis- optional
•	12 oz cheese (I used ½ med cheddar & ½ pepper jack)
•	1 cup  onion dip or sour cream (I used French onion dip)
•	½ -1 can olives
•	1-3 cups crumbled corn chips

Directions:
1.	Partially cook and crumble hamburger meat.  Add bell pepper and onion.  Cook till slightly soft.
2.	Add a bit of sauce from the can to the cooked meat mixture.
3.	Spray a  9”X13” Pyrex pan  and the 8 tortillas with oil.
4.	Layer in this order into the pan: ½ the tortillas, ½ the meat mixture, the diced green chilis if using, ½ the cheese and a little less than ½ the sauce.  Then layer in this order: the remaining tortillas, meat mixture, olives and cheese.
5.	Bake in a 400° oven for about 20 minutes until bubbly.  Do not over bake.
6.	Top with crumbled corn chips and serve.
Makes 4 hearty servings

Description:
