Title:
Pineapple Splash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ounce Peach Schnapps
1 cup ineapple juice
1/4 cup orange juice
dash cherry grenadine syrup
crushed ice

Directions:
Mix Schnapps, juices, and syrup.  Pour over crushed ice.

Description: 
I had a Bahama Mama at Apple Bees and loved it.  I wanted to make one, but didn't have everything it needed.  I created this with what I had on hand, and it tastes even better.  We drink them often.
