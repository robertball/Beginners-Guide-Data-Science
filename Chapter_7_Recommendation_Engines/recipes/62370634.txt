Title:
EASY Garlic Bread Croissants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/410930.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package refrigerated crescent rolls
3 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 teaspoon oregano
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â° F. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
In a small bowl mix butter, crushed garlic, oregano and salt. Roll out crescents flat on baking sheet and spread butter mixture evenly over entire surface.
Tear dough apart at seams and roll into crescents. Bake for 11-13 minutes.

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
This is a super easy way to jazz up your ordinary crescent rolls, by turning them into garlic bread!
