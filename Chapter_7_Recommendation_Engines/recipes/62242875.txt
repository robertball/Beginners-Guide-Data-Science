Title:
French Cream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353628.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbs. flour
3/4 cup milk
1 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine flour and milk. Cook over low heat until it is thick enough to form a paste. Set aside to cool. Cream together butter and sugar. Add cooled flour and milk to cream mixture; beat with electric mixer until smooth. Add vanilla; beat well to incorporate.

Submitter: 
larkspur
Description: 
This luscious frosting recipe has been in my family for several generations. It is rich and creamy, yet light and fluffy at the same time.
