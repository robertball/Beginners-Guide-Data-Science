Title:
Peppered Shrimp Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces penne pasta
1/4 cup unsalted butter
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 onion, diced
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 red bell pepper, diced
10 ounces fresh cremini mushrooms, diced
1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 batch "Quick And Easy Alfredo Sauce" (see recipe)
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
Kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and place in a large serving bowl; set aside.
Meanwhile, melt butter together with the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
Stir in onion, and cook until softened and translucent, about 3 minutes.
Stir in garlic, red pepper, and mushroom; cook over medium-high heat until soft, about 2 minutes more.
Stir in the shrimp, and cook until firm and pink, then pour in Alfredo sauce, bring to a simmer stirring constantly until thickened, about 5 minutes.
Season with cayenne, salt, and ground black pepper to taste.
Pour the sauce over the paste and mix well.
Serve with crusty Italian bread.
