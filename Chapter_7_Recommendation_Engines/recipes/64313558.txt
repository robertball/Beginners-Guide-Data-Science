Title:
Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (8 ounce) package elbow macaroni
4 hard-cooked eggs, diced
1/3 cup diced onion
1/3 cup diced celery
1/3 cup diced green bell pepper
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain and rinse macaroni under cold water until chilled. Transfer macaroni to a large salad bowl.
Toss pasta with hard-cooked eggs, onion, celery, and green bell pepper. Whisk mayonnaise, milk, and pickle relish, in a bowl and pour dressing over the salad; mix well and season with sea salt and ground black pepper.
