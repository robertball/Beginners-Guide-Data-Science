Title:
Sugar Spanish Peanuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups  Raw Spanish Peanuts with  skin
2 Cups White Sugar
1 1/2  Cups of Water

Directions:
1. use large pan & wooden spoon
2.Place water sugar and peanuts in large pan Over medium to low heat Stirring constantly with wooden spoon
3.Once all liquid evaporates and peanuts are coated remove from pan and place over a plain paper bag on counter to cool.
*Peanuts will be very hot do not handle with bare hands!!!!*
* for added kick you can add cayenne pepper to peanuts while cooking.

Submitter: 
Rosa Letty Gonzalez
Description: 
Spanish Peanuts coated in a hard sugar great for snacking!
