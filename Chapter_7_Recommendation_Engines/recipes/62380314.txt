Title:
Sausage and Cabbage Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb smoked sausage or kielbasa 1/2 inch slices
1 14 oz bag original coleslaw mix (can use cabbage shredded)
1 large baking potato (sliced thin)
1 medium onion (sliced thin)
1/2 cup carrots sliced thin (optional)
3/4 cup apple juice
1 Tb caraway seeds
2 tsp brown mustard
2 chicken boullion cubes (or 2 tsp chicken granules)

Directions:
In a large skilled over med-high heat combine coleslaw mix, carrots, onion, and potato and top with sausage.
In a small bowl combine apple juice, mustard, caraway seeds, and boulion.  Pour over mixutre in skillet.  bring to a boil and reduce heat to low.
Cover and simmer for about 30 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
We serve with potato rolls or bread to sop up the extra juice.
