Title:
Armenian lula kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground round
1 large yellow onion chopped
1/4 cup chopped parsley
1 T finely chopped mint
1 large egg
2 tsp cumin
1 T paprika
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
2 T tomato paste
juice of 1/2 lemon

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and let chill for 30 minutes or longer.  When ready to make preheat grill 450 degrees to 550 degrees.  Wrap meat in log shapes about 8 inches long around metal skewers and grill for about 8-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cat Hill
Description: 
Found this in a magazine and thought it would be something different than regular hamburger to try.  Original recipe calls for ground lamb.
