Title:
Harvest Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/747560.jpg
Ingredients:
15 oz. can pumpkin purée, OR 1 1/2 cups sweet potato (cooked, peeled, and mashed)
1/4 cup butter, cut into chunks
1 cup sugar
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1/8 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 cup milk
Topping:
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1/2 cup quick oats
1tsp. Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease bottoms of muffin tin.
Mix puréed pumpkin or sweet potato with sugar, eggs, and butter.
Add flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and nutmeg.  Do not over mix.
Carefully add milk.
Scoop batter evenly into muffin tin.
Mix brown sugar, oats and cinnamon.  Top each muffin with 1Tbsp. of mixture.
Bake for 15-18 minutes.
