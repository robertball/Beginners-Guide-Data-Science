Title:
Loaded Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033924.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz bacon, cooked and crumbled
4 stalks celery, minced
1/2 C mayonnaise
1/4 C yellow onion, minced
1 1/2 Tbsp sweet pickle relish
1 1/2 Tbsp yellow mustard
2 tsp chile garlic sauce (such as sriracha)
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tsp dried dill
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp salt
12 hard boiled eggs, shells removed

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except eggs, stir to combine.
Add eggs to bowl, break eggs and yolks with a potato masher.  Stir to combine.  Refrigerate 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
I created this recipe using my favorite flavors from a loaded potato salad.  Delicious, flavorful, and filling this salad is one of my favorites!
