Title:
Sweet n Sour Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/376684.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz pork cut into thin strips
1 egg lightly beaten
¼ onion sliced
¼ carrot sliced
1 cucumber sliced
1-cup cornstarch
1-cup water
¼ tsp salt
1/16 tsp pepper
1 tsp of vegetable oil
Vegetable oil for deep-frying.
Sauce
1½ cup of water
2 tbsp rice vinegar
5 tbsp sugar
¼ tsp salt

Directions:
Mix cornstarch and water.
Stir and let sit for 1-2 hrs
After the corn starch and water separated carefully drain water
Heat oil in a large pot for deep-frying.
Mix salt, pepper, and egg into the mixture.
Add pork and coat well.
Once coated deep fry until golden
fry twice for extra crispiness
Sauce
Add vegetable oil to hot pan.
Sauté vegetables for 1-2 min.
Add sauce ingredients.
Stir to dissolve sugar, and bring to a boil.
In a separate bowl mix 2 tbsp cornstarch 2 tbsp water.
Add to BOILING sauce.
Boil for 4-5 min.
Pour over meat and serve hot.
