Title:
Mammy's Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1076829.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 Cup sugar
3/4 Cup shortening
1/4 Cup molasses
1 teaspoon ginger
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 Cups flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

Directions:
Mix egg, sugar, shortening & molasses.
Combine dry ingredients and add to wet.
Form balls and roll in sugar.
Bake at 350* for 8 minutes.

Submitter: 
Clover
Description: 
As my grandmother notes, these cookies crack on top.  She made these cookies for years.
Sometimes her recipes call for shortening, sometimes they call for fat, but they are always good.
