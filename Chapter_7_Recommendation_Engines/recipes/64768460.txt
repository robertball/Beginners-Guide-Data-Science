Title:
Yeast Bread Braid  "Hefe Zopf" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz Flour
1 pack of Dry Yeast
4 oz Butter
3 oz Sugar
2 Eggs
1/2 tsp.Salt
5 oz of Milk
1 egg for make up.

Directions:
Sift the Four into a Mixing Bowl, make a well in the Four and add the Salt, Sugar,  Yeast and the Milk which has been warmed to about 80 Degrees. Pour into Bowl and Stir a bit to make a thick Cream in the Well.  After several Minutes begin Mixing and gradually add the Melted Butter( *at this time one may add Raisins if one Chooses ); and continue Mixing and  (adjust with Flour or warm Water)  Knead until it is elastic , Form a Ball with Buttered Hands and Covered with a Kitchen Towel.   Put in a Warm Place 80 degree oven for to rise.  When Doubled;  Weigh out into 4 equal parts and roll into Ropes about 16 inch.  Braid from the middle;  to one end. then Turn over and braid from the middle, to the other end.  pinch in the ends.  using a wash of Yolk and a tablespoon of Water, Brush the entire Braid, place on a Baking Tray and Parchment;  Bake at 400 degrees for 45 min, or until Golden Brown.
