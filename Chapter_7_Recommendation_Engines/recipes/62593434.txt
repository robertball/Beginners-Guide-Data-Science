Title:
Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8-oz. cans Tomato Sauce
1/2 C. Water
1 C. Instant Rice
1 lb. Ground Beef
1 Egg, beaten
2 Tbs. grated Onion
2 tsp. Salt
Pepper to taste
1/2 tsp. sugar

Directions:
Combine tomato sauce with water & set aside.
In a separate bowl, comine rice, beef, egg, onion, salt, pepper 7 1/2 c. tomato mixture.
Shape into 18 meatballs & place in skillet.
Blend sugar with remaining tomato mixture and pour over meatballs.
Bring to a boil; cover & simmer for 30 minutes.

A quick & tasty meal.  Meatballs come out light & moist.
