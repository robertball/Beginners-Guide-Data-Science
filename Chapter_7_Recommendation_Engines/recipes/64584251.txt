Title:
Big Ray's Pork Roast with Orange and Rhubarb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3867797.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb boneless top pork roast
1 cup Dijon mustard
2 Tbsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
3 cups fresh rhubarb cut into 1/2 pieces (diagonally)
1/3 orange juice
1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
6 Tbsp sugar
1/4 salt

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees.
Score top of roast with sharp knife in a diamond pattern.  In a small bowl mix mustard, garlic, salt and pepper.  Spread mixture on all sides of roast.  Place in roasting rack and bake for 90 minutes or until meat is done.  Allow to cool about 10 minutes before slicing.
While meat is cooking, put remaining ingredients in medium sauce pan and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minute, stirring often.  Spoon sauce over sliced roast.

Description:
