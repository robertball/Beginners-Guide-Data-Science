Title:
Roasted Pepper Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2069851.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium yellow onions, chopped
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 jar (7oz) roasted red peppers, undrained and chopped
1 can (4oz) chopped green chilies, drained
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
3 cups diced potatoes
3 cups low sodium chicken broth
2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 cup reduced fat cream cheese

Directions:
In a soup pot, saute onions in oil until tender. Stir in the roasted peppers, chilies, cumin and salt. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Stir in potatoes and broth, bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and cover. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until potatoes are tender. Stir in parsley and lemon juice. Let cool.
Pour half the soup into a blender and puree with cream cheese until smooth. Pour back into the soup pot with the other half of the soup and heat through on medium.

Description: 
Rich and creamy without the added fat or calories. If you love roasted red peppers, you will love this soup.
