Title:
BUSH'S® Sweet and Spicy Bean-Macaroni Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977034.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can BUSH'S® Baked Beans
1 pound lean ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
2 (15 ounce) cans tomatoes, chopped
2/3 cup elbow macaroni, uncooked
1 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
In large skillet, cook ground beef and onion until meat is browned; drain. Add remaining ingredients; stir well. Bring mixture to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 minutes or until macaroni is tender, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
We spiced up traditional macaroni by adding baked beans, ground beef, onions, tomatoes and a hint of chili powder.
