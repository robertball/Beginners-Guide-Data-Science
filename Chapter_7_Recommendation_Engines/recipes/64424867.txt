Title:
Vegan Spinach Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches of fresh spinach
1 lb idaho potato
1 medium onion
3 garlic cloves
1 flax seed egg (1 tbsp flaxmeal/3 tbsp water)
4 tbsp all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
cook potato and allow to fully cool
saute onion and garlic until fragrent, then set aside to cool
Rinse and dry spinach
chop spinach in food prosessor
(I also juice a little to add to potato for green color)
mash potatoes until smooth, add spinach juice to help smooth them
combine all ingredients in a bowl
form into patties (it will be sticky, cover generously with flour
cook in oil canola ooil and place on paper towel
once all patties fried, place on cookie sheet and cook on 425 for 10 mins.

Submitter: 
visionatevms
Description: 
Spinach bites that are allergen friendly for my little guys.
