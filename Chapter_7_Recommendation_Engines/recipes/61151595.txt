Title:
Apple Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen bananas, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup orange juice
1 Gala apple, peeled, cored and chopped
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
In a blender combine frozen banana, orange juice, apple and milk. Blend until smooth. pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Fidencio Soto
Description: 
Thick and healthy fruit drink with apple, banana and orange juice. The frozen banana takes the place of shaved ice and results in a smooth, creamy texture. Serve with dollop of whipped cream for added effect.Modified from: Apple Banana Smoothie
