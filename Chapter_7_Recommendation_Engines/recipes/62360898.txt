Title:
Crock Pot Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 pound ground turkey or pork
1 cup chopped onion
1 clove minced garlic
1 6 oz can V-8
1/2 cup catsup
1/2 cup water
2 Tablespoons brown sugar
1 Tablespoon mustard
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Cook meat, onion, and garlic in skillet until meat is brown and onion is tender.  Drain off fat.
Combine remaining ingredients in slow cooker.
Stir in meat mixture.
Cover and cook on low 10-12 hours.

This is a nice recipe.  Not too tomatoey with a nice hint of chili.
