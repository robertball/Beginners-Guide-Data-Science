Title:
CUCUMBER RAITA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup 		plain yogurt
2 cups 		diced cucumber
2 Tbsp. 	cilantro, chopped
1 Tbsp. 	chopped mint
1/2  tsp. 	Cumin powder
Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Stir yogurt in a bowl until smooth and creamy.  Stir in cucumber, cilantro, mint and cumin.  Season with salt and pepper.  Cover and chill well before serving.  (Can be refrigerated up to 24 hours.)
