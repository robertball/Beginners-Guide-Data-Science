Title:
Freezer friendly Split pea soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb dry split peas
2 cups diced ham
1 large yellow onion, diced
1 cup celery, chopped
2 cups carrots, chopped
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon thyme
2 bay leaves
32 oz of chicken broth (homemade is best)
2 cups water

Directions:
Sautee onion,celery, carrots and garlic in a hot skillet for 1 minute.
Set aside to cool.
Place all other ingredients (minus the water) in a large (gallon size), freezer safe, sealable bag.
After the veggies cool, place them in the same bad with all the other goodies.
Seal the bag with the least amount of air possible.
Freeze.
When ready to prepare, remove from freezer the night before, dump contents in your slow cooker and add the water.
Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 4-6 hours.

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
Description: 
This can be prepped and plopped into the freezer for a easy weeknight meal
