Title:
Tater Tot Casserole - Ground Turkey & Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/2 cup sour cream, to taste
1 tablespoon ketchup
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
16 ounces frozen mixed vegetables
4 cups frozen Tater Tots

Directions:
Cook turkey and onion in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat until turkey is lightly browned, stirring to break up meat.
Stir soup, sour cream, ketchup and Worcestershire into skillet.
Gently mix in the vegetables.
Spoon turkey mixture into 13x9-inch shallow baking dish. Arrange potatoes around edge of casserole.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 30 minutes or until the edges bubble and potatoes are crispy.
