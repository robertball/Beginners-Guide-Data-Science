Title:
Beef Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 jar (17.25 oz) Pace Enchilada Sauce
2 cups shredded cheese
10-12 flour or corn tortillas (6")

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook beef in skillet until browned.  Pour off fat.  Stir in 1/2 cup sauce in 13x9x2" baking dish.
Spoon about 2 Tbsp. meat mixture down center of each tortilla.  Roll up and place seam-side down in prepared dish.
Top with remaining sauce and cheese.
Bake 20 minutes or until cheese melts.
