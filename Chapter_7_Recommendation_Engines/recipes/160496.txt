Title:
Pub Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/108581.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Red Potato Wedges

Directions:
In small bowl stir together salt, thyme, paprika and garlic powder. Set aside.
Heat oil in deep fat fryer or large deep saucepan* to 370 degrees F. Carefully add 1/2 of Simply Potatoes(R) to hot oil. Cook, turning potatoes occasionally, 4 to 8 minutes, or until potatoes are light golden brown. Carefully remove potatoes; drain on paper towel. Quickly sprinkle Simply Potatoes(R) with half of seasoning mixture.
Repeat with remaining Simply Potatoes(R). Serve with malt vinegar.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
When in Ireland, call them 'chips.' Lightly seasoned potato wedges are deep fried or oven-baked for crisp, delicious flavor. Perfect for snacks or a quick and easy side dish. Traditional Pub Fries are often served with malt vinegar.
