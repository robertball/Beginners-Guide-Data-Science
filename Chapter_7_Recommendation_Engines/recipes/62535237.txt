Title:
Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/582048.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup confectioners sugar
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
2 Tbl. lemon juice (1 lemon)
zest of 1 lemon - finely minced (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Mix throughly the flour, butter and powdered sugar.  Press into an ungreased 8 x 8 pan building up a 1/2 inch edge.  Bake for 15 - 20 minutes.
Beat remaining ingredients for about 3 minutes or until light and fluffy.  Pour over hot crust.  Bake about 25 minutes or just until no imprint remains when touched lightly in the center.
Cool and cut into squares.
