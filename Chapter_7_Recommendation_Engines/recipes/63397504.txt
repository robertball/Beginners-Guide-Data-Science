Title:
Paleo Chorizo Sweet Potato and Kale Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/913571.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
1 large onion, diced
8 ounces Spanish chorizo, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
3 celery stalks, diced
3 carrots, diced
2 t ground cumin
1 T paprika (good quality)
1/2 t turmeric
2 t kosher salt
1 t freshly ground black pepper
Pinch saffron threads
5 garlic cloves, minced
2 sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces
8 cups chicken broth
4 cups lacinato kale, washed, stemmed and torn into pieces.
1 lemon, juiced
Salt and Pepper to taste
Optional: store-bought harissa and/or chopped Italian parsley for garnish.

Directions:
Saute onion in olive oil over medium-high heat for 5 minutes. Add chorizo and continue cooking (stirring frequently) for another 3 minutes.
Add celery and carrots, continue stirring and cooking for 3 minutes. Add spices and garlic, cook for 3 more minutes, stirring constantly.
Add sweet potatoes and broth. Bring to a boil, and cook for 20 minutes. Add handfuls of kale, and cook for 10 minutes, or until vegetables are soft and the kale is wilted and cooked through.
Finish with the lemon juice, and adjust the seasoning to your liking with salt and pepper.
Serve with a dollop of harissa and/or a sprinkling of chopped Italian parsley.
