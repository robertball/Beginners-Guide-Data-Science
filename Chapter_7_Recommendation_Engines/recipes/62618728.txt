Title:
The Ultimate Steak, Creamy Blue Cheese and Red Onion Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Steak
225g/8oz Sirloin Steak
2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
2 slices Bread
Onion Rings
1 small Red Onion - sliced into rings
4 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Stilton Cheese Sauce
75ml/5 tbsp White Wine
100g Stilton Cheese
30ml/2 tbsp Double Cream (Heavy Whipping Cream)

Directions:
The Steak Sandwich
Rub the steak with half the oil and season with salt and pepper;
Grease a griddle pan with a touch of oil, heat to sizzle and cook the steak for 3 - 4 -5 (to taste) minutes, turning once;
Remove, rest for a couple of minutes and cut in half; keep warm;
Spread the bread, both sides, with the remaining oil and toast on the griddle pan.
The Onion Rings
In a bowl, mix the onion with the oil and salt and pepper (use your hands);
Add to the griddle pan and cook for about 5 minutes or until soft and golden
The Stilton Cheese Sauce
In a small saucepan, pour in the wine and, on high heat, reduce by half;
Reduce to low heat, add the cheese, stirring until melted then add the cream;
Continue stirring on low heat but not to boil until sauce thickens to your taste.
To serve, place the steak onto the toasted bread, heap over with onions, drizzle over with sauce then top with the other slice and drizzle sauce all around.
Mop up with whatever.
