Title:
Chicken Bacon Ranch Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5oz boneless chicken breast cooked and shredded. (canned chicken breast works well)
3 slices bacon cooked and crumbled
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup finely chopped bell pepper
1 package dry ranch dressing
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
salt to taste
pepper to taste
1 package cream cheese (softened)
5 10 inch flour tortillas

Directions:
Fry bacon and lightly sautee onions and bell peppers in left over bacon grease. Drain onions and bell peppers well. Combine chicken with dry ranch dressing, add cheese, salt and pepper. Stir until well blended. Mix in cream cheese and crumbled bacon. Spread thinly on flour tortilla and roll up tightly. Wrap each roll up in saran wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours or over night. Cut into 1/2 inch pieces and arrange on large plate or platter. Serve with ranch dressing for dippng.
The colored and flavored tortillas can make these very festive for any occasion.

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Cream cheese, chicken, bacon and ranch dressing rolled in a flour tortilla. These make great snacks or appetizers.
