Title:
Green and Red Bean  Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can red beans
1can green beans (no frozen)
1 cup chopped sweet pickles
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3 oz. goat cheese
4 chopped scallions or 1/2 diced onion
1/2 tsp. Thyme
1/2 tsp. Paprika
salt to tasts
black peper to taste
1/2 Tsp. Old Bay seasoning
1/2 Tsp. Apple Cider Vinegar

Directions:
Drain red beans and green beans. Place in medium size bowl.
Add Mayonnaise, pickles, onions, Goat cheese, Thyme, Paprika, vinegar and old Bay with salt and pepper. Mix well and refrigerate 1 hour.
Serve cold
