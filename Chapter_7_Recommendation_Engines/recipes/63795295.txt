Title:
Chicken Veggie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 carrots
5 celery ribs
2 large onions
3 garlic cloves
1.5 oz ginger, chopped
10 peppercorns
1 tsp dried rosemary
roasted chicken (already cooked)
salt to taste

Directions:
simmer all ingredients for 2 hours

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
homestyle chicken veggies soup. Add noodles if you want a chicken noodle classic.
