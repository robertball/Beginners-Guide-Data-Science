Title:
Avocado Cottage Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C 1% Cottage Cheese
1C Avocado
1/2C Canned Chopped Green Chilis (opt)
1Tbsp Lemon Juice
1/2tsp salt
1/4tsp pepper
1tsp Crushed Garlic
dash of hot sauce or cayenne pepper
1-2Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
2-4Tbsp chopped ripe or kalamata olives
3/4C chopped seeded tomatoes (if not, add more cottage cheese)

Directions:
1) Process or hand crush cottage cheese and avocado.
2) Move to serving dish and stir in next 7 ingredients.
3) Sprinkle olives & tomatoes on top or mix them in.
4) Serve with rice crackers or tortilla chips

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This dip is a taste explosion!  Has all the taste of a high fat, super yummy dip without the calories and fat.
