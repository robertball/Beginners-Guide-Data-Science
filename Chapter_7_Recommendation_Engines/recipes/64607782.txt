Title:
Piri-Piri Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp grated lemon rind
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 head garlic minced
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 Tbsp hot pepper flakes
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp salt
6-8 pieces of chicken

Directions:
put all ingredients in a bag and let sit in refrigerator overnight.
Arrange chicken skin side up on foil rimmed baking sheet. Roast in 425 degree oven 45 mins or utill done
