Title:
Steverino's Nacho Slop Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 to 2 pounds ground hamburger; 1 can corn, drained; 1 small jar salsa; 1 small jar Miracle Whip; 1 bag tortilla chips, crushed; 2 cups shredded cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Brown and drain hamburger. Mix meat, corn, salsa and Miracle Whip. In a large casserole dish, layer half the meat, half the chips and half the cheese. Repeat. Bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
charlotte221
Description: 
This sounds awful but tastes awesome! My husband Steve's mom made it for him and his friends as kids and it didn't have a name so they - being boys - dubbed it Nacho Slop. She would make it for him and me in college and it quickly became our favorite treat! It's good hot or cold.
