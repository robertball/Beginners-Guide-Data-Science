Title:
Oriental Beef & Noodle Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981262.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound thin beef strips for stir fry
1 (.87 ounce) package McCormick® Brown Gravy Mix
1 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 cup shredded carrots
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
4 cups water
2 ounces angel hair or thin spaghetti, broken into small pieces
1/4 cup chopped green onion

Directions:
Coat 3-quart saucepan with cooking spray. Add beef and cook over medium heat 3-4 minutes.
Mix in remaining ingredients except pasta and onion. Bring to a boil, simmer, uncovered for 5 minutes.
Add pasta. Cook 5 minutes longer. Stir in onion just before serving.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
McCormick® Brown Gravy Mix makes a quick and easy soup base. Get an Oriental flair from the soy sauce.
