Title:
Easy Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Spice Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Spice Cake Mix
1 15 oz can 100% Natural Pumpkin
1 cup Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together cake mix and pumpkin until well blended.
Stir in Chocolate chips.
Drop by rounded tablespoons onto parchment lined cookie sheet, about 2 inches apart.
Bake 10-13 min.
Cool on wire rack.
