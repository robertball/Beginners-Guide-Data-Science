Title:
Strawberry Spinich salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4C orange juice
1/4C honey
2Tbsp veg. oil
2tsp dijon style mustard
12C bite size spinich
1 pint diced strawberries
2/3C sliced green onoins
2Cans (11oz) madarin oranges, drained
1/4C pine nuts

Directions:
Prepare dressing:
Combine the juice, honey,oil, mustard in tightly covered container. Shake until well mixed.
Prepare salad:
combine spinich,strawberries, onions and oranges, toss with dressing.
Sprinkle the pine nuts on top.
