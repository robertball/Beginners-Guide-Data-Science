Title:
Turkey, Gouda and Caramelized Onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989471.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices whole wheat bread, toasted
1 slice smoked Gouda cheese
2 slices cooked skinless turkey breast
1/4 cup caramelized onions
1 tablespoon Hellmann's® 1/2 the Fat mayonnaise-type dressing
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Spread Hellmann's(R) 1/2 the Fat mayonnaise-type dressing on one slice of bread and caramelized onion on the other. Layer turkey and cheese between slices of bread and season with pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Hellmann's®
Description: 
Caramelized onions and a slice of smoked Gouda cheese make turn an ordinary turkey sandwich into a lunchtime feast.
