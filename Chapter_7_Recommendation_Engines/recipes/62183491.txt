Title:
Cream Soup Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c instant nonfat dry milk powder
5/8 c cornstarch
1/2 c potato flakes
1/4 c vegetable bouillon granules
2 T dried vegetable flakes
1 t onion powder
1/2 t garlic powder
5-6 grinds of pepper

Directions:
Process all ingredients in blender of food processor until uniform. Store in airtight container. Keeps for 1 year.
To Use:  1 1/3 c water: 3/8 c mix = 1 can soup. Heat in saucepan, stirring, until thickened.
