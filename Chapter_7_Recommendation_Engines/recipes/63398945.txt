Title:
Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c brown sugar
2 eggs
2 c sour milk
1 t baking soda
salt
1 c raisins
1 c nuts
3 1/4 c flour

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and bake for 1 hour at 350'.
