Title:
Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup(s) oatmeal
2 cup(s) milk, fat-free
cinnamon, ground to taste
2 tablespoon nuts, walnuts chopped

Directions:
Over low heat, cook old-fashioned oatmeal and nonfat milk in a small saucepan. When cooked to desired consistency, remove from heat and sprinkle with cinnamon and chopped walnuts.
