Title:
German Chocolate Cake Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup evaporated milk ADD 1 tbs cornstarch
1 cup white sugar
3 egg yolk, beaten with 1 teaspoon water
1/2 cup margarine
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup flaked coconut

Directions:
In a large saucepan combine evaporated milk, sugar, egg yolks, margarine and vanilla. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until thick. Remove from heat and stir in pecans and coconut.  Spread on cake while still warm.

Submitter: 
cookless cook
Description: 
German chocolate cake frosting that is cooked on top of the stove and put on warm cake while frosting is warm.Modified from: German Chocolate Cake Frosting II
