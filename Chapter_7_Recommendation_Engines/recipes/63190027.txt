Title:
Snow Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/822275.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups freshly fallen snow
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 tbs vanilla extract
toppings of choice

Directions:
Measure 8 cups fresh snow into a medium-large mixing bowl.
Add can of sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract. Mix until ingredients are thoroughly blended.
Add toppings or mix-ins and serve!

Submitter: 
sparrow
Description: 
Fresh snow turned ice cream in 5 minutes or less! Fun to do with kids, the toppings/mix-in options are endless.
