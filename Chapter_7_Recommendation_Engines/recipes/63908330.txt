Title:
Mock Rum and Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084084.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1 C. Quick oats
1 C. Thompson seedless raisins
1/2 C. Molasses
4 Tbsp. Pineapple juice
1 tsp. Almond extract
1/2 C. Butter
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
In a small bowl combine molasses, pineapple juice and almond extract together stirring them until mixture is thinned out.
Blend flour, b. powder and b. soda together in a bowl and set aside
Mix butter, sugar and molasses mixture together and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until butter is dissolved and mixture is smooth
Add quick oats, raisins and  continue to beat on medium speed until oats are combined well
Stir in dry ingredients and add enough flour to make dough less sticky when making the cookies
Roll dough into a ball and place 2 inches apart on parchment paper
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes and remove from oven
Place on wire rack to cool completely before storing in container.

Description: 
A very soft cookie with a sweet rum like flavour for your snack time.
