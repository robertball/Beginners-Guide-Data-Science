Title:
Yam Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c fresh yams, cooked & mashed
1 refrigerated 9" pie crust
2 large eggs, divided
1/4 c light brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3 large egg whites
2/3 c dark corn syrup
1/2 c sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
2/3 c pecans, chopped

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°.
2. Lay pie crust in a 9 " pie dish.
3. In mixing bowl, blend together yams, 1 egg, brown sugar, cinnamon, & nutmeg. Spread evenly on bottom of pie crust.
4. In another mixing bowl, beat together remaining egg, egg whites, corn syrup, sugar, & vanilla until mixture is frothy. Stir in pecans. Carefully spoon over yam layer.
5. Bake for 50 - 60 min or until filling is set around edges and knife inserted halfway between center & edge comes out clean. Cool & serve.
