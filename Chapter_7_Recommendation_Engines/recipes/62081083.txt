Title:
Quick Ham and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups hot water
2 tsp. chicken bouillon
2 15 oz. cans of Great Northern Beans - one drained and one with liquid
1/3 cup frozen or fresh cut/diced carrots
1/3 cup diced onions
1 small celery stalk diced
1/2 tsp. minced garlic
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp. ground pepper
Half of a packaged ham steak diced

Directions:
1. Boil water and add chicken boullion
2. Lower heat and add one can of beans undrained and one can drained.
3. Chop all vegetables as needed and add (frozen veggies make this step *fast*)
4. Add seasonings and bring to a boil.
5. Lower temparature to a simmer while you dice ham steak. Add and simmer for 30 minutes.
6. Salt as desired, but usually none or very little needed.

Submitter: 
HARRYMARTINII
Description: 
I was in the mood for soup on a cold night and using a basic ham and bean soup, I created one that is ready in less than an hour, with items found in the pantry/fridge. Easy and delicious!
