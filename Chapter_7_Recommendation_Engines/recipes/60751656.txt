Title:
LIMA BEAN CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg frozen chopped broccoli
1 pkg frosen limas
1 8 ounce sour cream
1 pkg onion soup mix
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can sliced water chesnuts
3-4 cups rice krispies
1 stick butter

Directions:
1. Cook lima beans and broccoli aac'ding to pkg directions. Cool 3-4 minutes.
2. Mix soup, sour cream, and onion soup mix until well blended, and the add limas and broccoli.
3. Drain water chesnuts and add to mixture.
4. Melt butter in skillet and brown rice krispies.
5. Place mixture in greased casserole dish and top with rice krispies.
6. Bake covered w/ foil for 25-30 minutes.
