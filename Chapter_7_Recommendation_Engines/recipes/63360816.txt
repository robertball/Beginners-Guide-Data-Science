Title:
Minnesota Wild Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/895157.jpg
Ingredients:
1 onions, finely chopped
3 celery, diced
2 (6 ounce) packages dry instant long grain and wild rice mix
2 1/2 cups water
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 chicken breast cooked and diced up or meat off a rotisserie chicken.
1 can of water chestnuts chopped
1/2 cup or more sliced fresh mushrooms
2 Tablespoons of soy sauce

Directions:
In a slow cooker, place onions, celery, rice mix, water, condensed cream of mushroom soup, butter, American cheese and mushrooms. Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 10 hours or on High 2 to 4 hours.
