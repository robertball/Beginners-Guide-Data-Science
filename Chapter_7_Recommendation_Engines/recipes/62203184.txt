Title:
MEXICAN CASSEROLE WITH LEFTOVER TURKEY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium Onion, chopped
1 tbsp oil
1/2 tsp cumin
salt and pepper to taste
3 c shredded cooked turkey
1 (1.25 oz.) package taco seasoning mix
2 (15 oz.) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15 oz.) can sweet corn, drained
2 c salsa
1 c water
2 c cooked rice
2 c shredded Mexican-style cheese
3 c crushed tortilla chips

Directions:
1.	In a large skillet over medium high heat, saut√© onion in oil, cumin, salt and pepper until tender. Add shredded turkey, corn, black beans, 1 cup salsa, 1 cup water, and taco seasoning. Mix well. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.
2.	Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
3.	Mix cooked rice with remaining cup of salsa and spread evenly into the bottom of an ungreased 10x15 pan. Pour turkey mixture over rice. Layer 1 cup of  shredded cheese over turkey mixture. Top with crushed tortilla chips. Cover loosely with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove foil and top chips with remaining cup of shredded cheese. Return to oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serve and enjoy!
