Title:
My Favorite Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound broccoli
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 to 1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
2 tsp. water
1 clove garlic minced

Directions:
Trim broccoli into florets, rinse well.
Place olive oil, salt, and red pepper flakes into a heavy skillet and place over medium heat.
Add broccoli, cover and cook for 2 minutes
Stir in water and garlic, cover and cook for 3 to 4 minutes or until just tender.
Serve hot or cold.
