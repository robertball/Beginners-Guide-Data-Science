Title:
Apple Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-#2 can crushed pineapple
2-#2 cans apple pie filling (can also use cherry pie filling)
1 box yellow cake mix
1 stick margarine

Directions:
In a greased 9x13 baking pan,layer as follows:   pineapple
apple pie filling
cake mix (dry)
margarine (cut in slices)
Bake @ 350* for 1 hour.  Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top.
