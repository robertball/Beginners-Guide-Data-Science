Title:
Terri's Favorite Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1174217.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. beef or other meat and bones OR 1 lb. ground beef, chicken or turkey
4 cups water, to start
1 T. salt
1/2 medium cabbage, chopped
2 onions, chopped
2-3 stalks of celery, sliced
1 large can of crushed tomatoes
1 small can of diced tomatoes
2 potatoes, peeled and chopped OR 1 bag diced Simply Potatoes
1 bag of frozen vegetables for soup
1 small bag of frozen lima beans and/or other frozen beans or peas.
1/2-1 cup catsup OR 2-3 T. steak sauce
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t. black pepper

Directions:
In a LARGE soup pot, add the meat and bones, water and salt. Bring to a boil. Skim. If you're using ground meat or boneless meat, at this point you want to brown it in a little oil first and then add water or broth.
Add cabbage, onions, celery and tomatoes.
Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer covered, 30 minutes.
Add the rest of the ingredients.
Add more water if necessary.
Simmer, covered, at least 3 hours.
Remove bones and large chunks of meat.
Cut up meat and return to the pot.
Taste for seasoning.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
This is my favorite vegetable soup recipe and it makes a lot of soup. I have been making it for over 40 years. You can change the meat, vegetables and seasonings to suit whatever you have on hand. Make it thin or thick; cut it in half or even double it, it always tastes great.
