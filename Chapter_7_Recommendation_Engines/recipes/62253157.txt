Title:
Corned Beef Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/333856.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 large potatoes, peeled and diced
1 (12 ounce) can corned beef, cut into chunks or 3/4 pounds thick cut deli corned beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 bell pepper
3/4 cup beef or chicken broth
2 tablespoons of butter

Directions:
In a large deep skillet, over medium heat, combine the potatoes, corned beef, onion, bell pepper and beef broth. Cover and simmer until potatoes are of mashing consistency, and the liquid is almost gone. Add butter or oil to pan to brown and crisp hash
