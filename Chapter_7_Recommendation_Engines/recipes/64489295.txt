Title:
Banana Almond Butter Chewy Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rolled oats
1 cup flour
1/3 cup light brown sugar
¼ tsp salt
½ cup almond butter
3 small ripe bananas
½ cup applesauce
½ tsp baking powder
1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
½ c slivered almonds
¼ c flaxseeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl. Mix wet ingredients together in a smaller bowl.
Slowly stir the wet ingredients into the dry and mix until fully combined.
Line a 9x13” baking sheet with parchment paper and pat the mixture on top of it evenly, leaving about an inch of space around the border of the pan.
Bake for 15-18 minutes.
Let it cool for 5 minutes and then cut into small squares.
