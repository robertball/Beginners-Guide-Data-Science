Title:
Mustard Potato Salad-egg free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/638604.jpg
Ingredients:
8 small russet potatoes, boiled, peeled and chopped
2 ribs celery, sliced
1/2 c chopped onion
1/2 c dill relish
1/2 c plain fat free yogurt
1/2 c prepared mustard
salt and pepper, to taste
3 chopped radishes(optional)
paprika garnish(optional)

Directions:
Place prepared potatoes in a large bowl.
In another bowl, mix mustard, yogurt, salt and pepper.
Stir celery, onions,and relish into sauce and pour over the potatoes.
Combine well and garnish with radishes and paprika if desired.
