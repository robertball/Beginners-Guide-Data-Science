Title:
mocha shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream
1 cup warm coffee
chocolate syrup

Directions:
put half of the ice cream into blender add 1/2 cup of warm coffee and start blending on low speed, drizzle in chocolate syrup slowly about 3 or 4 table spoons worth,
stop blending sample to taste and add more ice cream depending on your blender size and coffee and chocolate to get the right flavor for you that's it
