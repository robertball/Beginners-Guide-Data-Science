Title:
Zesty Pepperoni Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986215.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces stick pepperoni
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened*
Cherry tomatoes, halved
Keebler® Town House® Original Crackers

Directions:
Remove casing from pepperoni. Cut two slices for garnish. Set aside. Slice remaining pepperoni into 1-inch pieces. In blender or food processor container process pepperoni pieces, a few pieces at a time, until crumbly.
In small bowl stir together pepperoni and cream cheese. Refrigerate at least 1 hour to blend flavors.
Let stand at room temperature for 1 hour. Garnish with pepperoni slices and cherry tomatoes. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Town House®
Description: 
Cream cheese mixed with chopped pepperoni is garnished with halved cherry tomatoes and served with crackers for an easy and delicious appetizer!
