Title:
Apricot Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (13oz) bottle vodka
1 (13oz) bottle apricot brandy
1 can frozen orange juice
1 can frozen lemonade
1 1/2 litres of pinapple juice
1 litre of apricot nectar
2 litres of 7-up

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except 7-up) well in one or two ice cream pails. Freeze,stirring periodically. Serve slush in a large glass topped up with 7-up. Enjoy!
