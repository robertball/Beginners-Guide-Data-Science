Title:
Angel hair pasta with summer vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package of angel hair pasta
3T Olive Oil
1/2 Onion, diced
3 Garlic Cloves, chopped
12 Cherry tomatoes, quartered
1 Zucchini, diced
4T Parmesan Cheese
Salt, to taste
Basil (garnish, optional)

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed
In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat
Add onion to skillet, cook 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally
Add zucchini, tomato and garlic to skillet, cook an additional 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally
Season with salt to taste
Add drained pasta and toss until evenly distributed
Plate pasta and top with Parmesan cheese and basil

Submitter: 
jmn
Description: 
A light pasta dish filled with seasonal vegetables and a light olive oil & garlic sauce, the flavor is amped up with parmesan cheese & fresh basil.  

This is a great dish to try for meatless Mondays, or any day you're craving a light, but filling, meal on a warm summer night. 

Or to accommodate meat lovers in the family, you can simply add any protein to this versatile dish.
