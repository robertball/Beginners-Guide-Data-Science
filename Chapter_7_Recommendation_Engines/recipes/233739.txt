Title:
Almond Delights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups almond meal
1 1/2 tablespoons warm honey
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
3 egg whites, beaten stiff

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Stir almond meal, honey, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a bowl. Mix beaten egg whites into the almond meal mixture; scoop by teaspoonful onto prepared baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven until browned, 10 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chris Denzer

Baked Paleo-treats made with almond meal and egg whites.
