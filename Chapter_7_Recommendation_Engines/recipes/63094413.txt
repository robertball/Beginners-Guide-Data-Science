Title:
Greek Style Skillet Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 tsp oregano leaves
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 can (14 1/2 oz) reduced sodium beef broth
1 can (14 1/2 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
2 TBL tomato paste
1 1/2 cups uncooked penne pasta
1 1/2 cups frozen leaf spinach or cut green beans, thawed
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese, divided

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onion in large skillet on medium-high heat until beeg is no longer pink, stirring occasionally. Drain fat. Add oregano, cinnamon and garlic powder; mix well.
Stir in broth, tomatoes and tomato paste. Bring to boil. Stir in pasta. Reduce heat to medium; cover and cook 10 minutes or until pasta is nearly tender. Stir in spinach and 1/2 cup of the feta cheese. Cook 5 minutes longer or until pasta is tender.
Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup feta cheese. Cover. Let stand 5 minutes.
