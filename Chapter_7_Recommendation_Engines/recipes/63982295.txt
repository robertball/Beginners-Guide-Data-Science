Title:
Coconutty Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package yellow cake mix, with pudding
3 eggs
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 21 oz can coconut cream pie filling
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup chopped pecans
2 cups toasted coconut, divided
1 3.5 oz package instant coconut pudding mix
1 cup milk
1 8 oz container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Grease 9x13 pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together: cake mix, eggs, 1 tbsp vanilla extract, coconut pie filling, white chocolate chips, 1 cup toasted coconut, and nuts. Stir until just combined. Pour batter into greased 9x13 pan.
Bake in 350 degree oven 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Remove from oven and let cool.
To make frosting: in a medium bowl, combine pudding mix with milk and 1/2 c toasted coconut. Mix until smooth. Gently fold in whipped topping until no streaks remain. Spread over cooled cake.
Sprinkle cake with remaining 1/2 c toasted coconut.
Store cake in refrigerator.
