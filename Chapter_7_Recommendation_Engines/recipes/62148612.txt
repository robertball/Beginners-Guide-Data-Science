Title:
Soy Sauce Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 pound package of chicken thighs (bone-in with skin)
1 cup sugar
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup water
1/4 cup minced garlic
1/4 cup grated fresh ginger
1/4 cup chopped green onions for garnish

Directions:
In a large stock pot, combine sugar, soy sauce, water, garlic and ginger, and stir until sugar is disolved.
Add chicken to stock pot.
Turn on stovetop to high and let mixture begin to boil. Using tongs,turn and rotate chicken in the pot every 5 minutes.
Continue to boil for 35 minutes or until chicken is completely done, and mixture has turned into a rich, brown glaze.
Remove pot from heat, and let cool 10 minutes.
Remove and place on platter and sprinkle with chopped green onions for decoration.

Submitter: 
Too Hot Tamale
Description: 
A delicious, fast and easy recipe for any week night dinner. The chicken comes out flavorful with a sweet and tangy glaze.  Serve with rice, and steamed snow peas for a complet meal.
