Title:
Lemon Blueberry Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup oat bran
1/2 cup blueberries
5 egg whites
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp grated lemon peel

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients. Mix together and whisk until smooth.
Cook batches in pre-sprayed skillet on medium-high until bubbles form on the surface, then turn over and cook until dark golden brown.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Serve with sugar-free syrup or Greek yogurt.
