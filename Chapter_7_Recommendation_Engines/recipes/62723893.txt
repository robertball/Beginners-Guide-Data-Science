Title:
Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # lean ground beef
1 C chopped sweet onion
10 oz pkg. taco kit, divided
3/4 C water
15 oz can whole kernel corn, drained
4 oz can chopped green chiles
8 oz shredded Colby Jack cheese
1 med. tomato, seeded, chopped
3 green onions, sliced
2.25 oz can sliced black olives, drained
Sour cream, opt.
White corn and black bean salsa, opt.

Directions:
In lg. skillet, cook beef and onions until beef is browned; drain. Stir in taco seasoning from taco kit and water. Cook over low heat for 2-3 min. Add taco sauce from taco kit, corn and green chiles. Break taco shells from taco kit into chop size pieces.
Layer 1/3 broken taco shells in a 9 x 13-in. baking dish that has been sprayed with non stick cooking spray.
Layer half the beef mixture and half the cheese over shells. Continue layering w/another 1/3 of taco shells, remaining beef and cheese.
Bake in preheated 350 degree v=oven for 20 min. Layer with remaining shells, tomatoes, green onions and black olives; continue baking for 5 min.
Serve w/sour cream and salsa, if desired.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can add or subtract ingredients according to taste or what you have on hand.  Add rinsed and drained black beans in with the corn, if desired or add salsa and/or sour cream. Very versatile.
