Title:
Curried Tofu with Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991701.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound firm tofu
1 tablespoon cooking oil
1/2 cup red onion slices
1/2 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 1/2 cups Kikkoman Thai Yellow Curry Sauce
1/2 cup canned coconut milk
2 green onions, thin sliced
1 pound cooked spaghetti

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet, saute onion and pepper until soft, about 3 minutes, remove from pan. Add tofu to the pan and brown on all sides. Pour in Thai Yellow Curry sauce and coconut milk. Add onion and pepper mixture, stir gently to combine. Saute over medium heat for 5 minutes. Serve with pasta; garnish with green onion.

Submitter: 
Kikkoman
Description: 
Bite size chunks of tofu are simmered in a tempting yellow curry with a colorful mix of red onions and green bell peppers, then served over a bed of spaghetti noodles in this quick and easy Thai-inspired dish.
