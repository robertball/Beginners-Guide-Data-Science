Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie crust
3 eggs
1 cup dark corn syrup
1 cup sugar
1/8 t salt
1 t vanilla
1 cup pecans

Directions:
Partially bake the pie crust. While the crust is still warm brush it with melted butter.
Mix ingredients except pecans.
Layer the pecans in the pie shell. Pour mix over the pecans. (They will start floating to the top.)
Bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
Knife will come out clean when the pie is done.
