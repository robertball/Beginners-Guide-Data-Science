Title:
Berry Eggnog Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977908.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) can frozen cranberry-raspberry juice concentrate, thawed
1 quart dairy eggnog, chilled
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage, chilled
1/2 cup whipping cream, whipped
nutmeg
fresh raspberries (optional)

Directions:
Combine cranberry-raspberry juice and eggnog in a pitcher. Carefully add lemon-lime beverage. Dollop each serving with some of the whipped cream. Sprinkle with nutmeg. Top with a fresh raspberry, if desired.

Submitter: 
Better Homes and Gardens
Description: 
Eggnog takes a fruity spin in this blush-colored punch.
