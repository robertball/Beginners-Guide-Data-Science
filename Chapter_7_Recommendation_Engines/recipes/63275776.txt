Title:
Texas Easy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Ketchup
2 tbsp white vinegar
1 tbsp prepared mustard
2 tbsp honey
2 tsp kitchen bouquet
Dash of tobasco sauce
1 tbsp cornstarch
1/2 large green diced pepper
1/2 cup fresh mushroom diced

Directions:
Cook peppers and mushrooms in oil in skillet.
Drain off oil.
Mix the ketchup and cornstarch well.
Add to mushrooms and peppers.
Add rest of ingredients.
Mix and heat to thicken sauce about 1 min. on low heat
