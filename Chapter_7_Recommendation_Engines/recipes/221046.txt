Title:
Charoses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
6 large apples - peeled, cored, and shredded
1/2 pound pecans, very finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste
1 tablespoon red wine, or as needed

Directions:
Stir shredded apples, pecans, cinnamon, and sugar together in a bowl until thoroughly combined. Mix in red wine as needed to help the mixture hold together.
