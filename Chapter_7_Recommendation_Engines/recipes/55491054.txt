Title:
Chewy Oatmeal Caramel Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/320228.jpg
Ingredients:
CRUST:
2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
2 1/2 sticks butter
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups oats
FILLING:
1 bag chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 jar caramel sauce
3 tbs. flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
melt butter then mix together with first 5 ingredients to form crust, reserving half of the mixture for topping.
Press half of mixture into the bottom of a 9 X 13 inch greased pan and bake for 10 minutes.
Mix the caramel with the 3 tbs. flour.  Sprinkle the warm crust with the chocolate chips and chopped walnuts then drizzle with caramel/flour mixture.  Top with the reserved crust mixture, put back into the oven for 18 - 22 minutes.
