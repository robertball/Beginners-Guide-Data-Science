Title:
Cheesy Chicken over Puff Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, dices
4 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup butter
3 chicken breast halves, cubed
3/4 cup white wine
1/2 tsp dried tarragon, crushed
1/2 tsp pepper
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup sour cream
6 oz swiss cheese
4 oz muenster cheese
Puff Pastry Shells

Directions:
Bake puff pastry per package directions.
Cook and stir onion and mushrooms in melted butter in large skilled until tender.  Add chicken, wine, tarragon, salt and pepper.  Bring to boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
Add cream cheese, heavy cream, sour cream, and cheeses.  Cook and stir until cheese is melted.  Serve over puff pastry.
