Title:
Kim's Hearty Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean stew beef, cut into bite size pieces
2 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tbsp cooking oil
3 cups V8 juice
1 cup water
1 medium onion, chopped
1 tsp beef bouillion
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp marjoram, crushed
1/2 tsp basil, crushed
1 bay leaf
3 medium potatoes, diced
2 medium carrots, sliced
1 zucchini, sliced
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Place flour, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a lg ziplock bag. Add meat, shaking to coat. Heat oil in large pot. Brown beef. Stir in vegetable juice, water, onion, bouillion granules,worcestershire sauce, marjoram, basil, and bay leaf. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for an hour to hour and fifteen minutes or until meat is nearly tender. Stir in veggies. Bring to a boil, reduce heat. Cover and simmer about 30 minutes more until meat and veggies are tender. Discard bay leaf.

Description: 
Old fashioned, filling, beef stew. Great served with fresh bread and a salad.
