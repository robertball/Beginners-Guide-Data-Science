Title:
Grilled Pastrami and Swiss sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/881641.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups shredded cabbage (or 12oz prepared bag coleslaw mix)
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoons celery seed
1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
1/2 teaspoon marjoram spice (or oregano)
salt and pepper to taste
1 loaf of focaccia bread sliced in half and cut into four sandwiches. (or hard sub rolls)
1 pound sliced pastrami (divided)
8 slices cooked bacon
8 slices Swiss cheese
Dijon mustard

Directions:
Mix cabbage, oil, vinegar, sugar, celery seed, garlic powder, marjoram, onion powder, and salt and pepper in a bowl and set aside.
Cook bacon until crispy over med/low heat in skillet, set aside on paper towels.
Spread Dijon mustard on bottom slice of bread, top with 2 cooked bacon slices, 1/4lb. of the pastrami slices, 2 Swiss cheese slices, then top with a cup of the cabbage mixture. Wrap sandwich in foil and grill at 350Â° on grill for approx. 20 minutes, until the cheese is really nice and melted.
Serve with a kosher dill pickle and fries or chips.

*Sherri*
Description: 
A nice easy to make tasty summer sandwich, with pastrami, crispy bacon,Swiss cheese and coleslaw.
