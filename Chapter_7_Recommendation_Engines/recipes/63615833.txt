Title:
Buffalo Veggie Quinoa Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup raw quinoa
1 small yellow or sweet onion OR ½ of a large yellow or sweet onion
1-2 cups of kale
½-1 red pepper
1 sweet potato
Seasonings (oregano, basil, thyme, etc.)  use at your discretion re: amount
1-2 tablespoons of minced garlic
1lb of buffalo
1-2 tablespoon ketchup or tomato paste
1-2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
BBQ sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Cook ¼ of raw quinoa and set aside (bring ½ cup of water and ¼ cup of quinoa to a boil, reduce heat and simmer with cover for approx. 10-15 min)
Sauté vegetables and potato in olive oil until soft and set aside
Combine and mix seasoning, garlic, buffalo, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, eggs, salt, and pepper
Add veggies/potato and quinoa into buffalo mixture
Place mixture into greased banana bread pan or any other greased cooking dish that will work
Add a very thin layer of BBQ sauce
Cook for approx. 1 hour

Submitter: 
catherinem
Description: 
Well it's hard to make a healthy meatloaf with 'traditional' ingredients. So this recipe is hearty, healthy, and guilt free.
