Title:
BLT Iceberg Wedge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980806.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head Tanimura & Antle® Iceberg Lettuce
3/4 cup ranch dressing
1 cup chopped tomato
6 strips bacon, cooked, crumbled
Croutons or Crostini (optional)

Directions:
Core lettuce; rinse and drain well. Cut into 6 wedges.
Spoon dressing over wedges, as desired. Top with tomato, bacon and croutons.

Submitter: 
Tanimura & Antle®
Description: 
Bacon, croutons, and chopped tomatoes dress up this flavorful lettuce wedge salad.
