Title:
My Butter Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds cubed beef stew meat
1 Tbsp olive oil
garlic salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp worchestershire sauce
1/2 cup butter
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium high heat. Add the beef and sprinkle with garlic salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle Worchestershire sauce and onion soup mix over beef as well, and brown.  Place the beef and butter into a slow cooker. Cover, and cook on Low for 8 hours, or High for 4 to 5 hours. Stir once or twice.  Serve over egg noodles
