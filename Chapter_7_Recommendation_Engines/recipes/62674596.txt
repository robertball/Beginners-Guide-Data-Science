Title:
Crazy Coconutty Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/598105.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs
1 C. unsalted butter (soft)
1-1/4 C dark brown sugar
1 Tbs. honey
1 tsp. vanilla
3/4 C. sweetened coconut flakes
1/2 C. golden raisins
1/2 C. dried currents
2 C. quick oats
1/2 C. pecans

Directions:
In a medium bowl sift flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt together. Set aside
Inlarge bowl/mixer, cream together butter and sugar. mix in honey until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, until fully mixed in. Mix in dried fruits, oats and cocnut. Slowly add in flour mixture. form into balls, approx 2 Tbs. Place 2 inches apart on parchment paper lined cookie sheet. Bake to golden brown 13-15 min. Cookies will be very soft. Move to cooling rack after sitting 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tina Comerdinger
Description: 
Oatmeal cookie with a twist. Much better than a boring old Oatmeal cookie.
