Title:
Easy Apple Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 apples, cored and peeled
1/4 C chopped nuts
1/2 C butter
1/2 C sugar
1/2 C Flour
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 C rolled oats

Directions:
preheat oven to 350degF
grease 9x9" baking tray with butter (I use pyrex glass)
Slice apples with wide food processor slicer
dump into baking tray and pack down
sprinkle nuts on top
use food processor blade to chop butter (pulse 6-8 times for 1 sec each)
add sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg
pulse 2-4 times
add rolled oats mix
pack in even layer on top of apples
bake 350degF for 40 minutes or until top starts browning slightly
cool slightly and serve with vanilla ice cream

Submitter: 
PlaygroundHunt
Description: 
Easy to make, tastes excellent. It can be made with any kind of flour to make it gluten free
