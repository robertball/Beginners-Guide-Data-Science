Title:
Coconut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1811224.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded sweetened coconut
2 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup oil
1/4 unsweetened applesauce
1 1/2 teaspoon coconut extract
1 1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 buttermilk (or sour milk with 1 teaspoon of vinegar)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Blend eggs till fluffy and then add sugar, oil, applesauce, coconut extract, and blend until combined.
In another bowl mix together flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
Fold dry ingredients into wet mixture
Add coconut and milk and fold everything all together.
Pour into greased loaf pan and bake for 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
A nice fluffy bread! The smell reminds me of summer. Its a great bread for anyone who really enjoys coconut, and makes a nice gift or freezes well.
