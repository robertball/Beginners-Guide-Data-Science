Title:
Helyn's Raspberry Cream Cheese Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1747477.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large (6oz) box raspberry jello
2 (10 oz) pkgs of frozen raspberries, drained reserving juice
1 (7oz) can crushed pineapple, well drained , reserving juice
6 oz cream cheese, at room temperature
1/4 c confectioner's sugar
1 cup soft minature marshmallows
1 small package sliced almonds
1 (10 oz) container Cool Whip (slightly thawed)
Water (see instructions for amount)

Directions:
Drain raspberries.  Drain pineapple separately.  Arrange raspberries evenly on bottom of serving dish.  Combine drained juices and add enough water to equal 3 cups.  Bring liquid to boil.  Pour over jello, stir to dissolve.  Refrigerate until it JUST STARTS to gel. Add mixture to raspberries.  Refrigerate until set  (at least 2 hours).
Topping:
With electric hand mixer whip cream cheese until light and fluffy.  Add drained crushed pineapple mixing well.  Gently fold in mostly thawed Cool Whip.  Fold in sliced almonds reserving some for garnish on top.  Layer topping onto firmly set raspberry jello.  Refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.

Description: 
Refreshing side salad that is great with holiday meals.   Almost tastes like dessert!
