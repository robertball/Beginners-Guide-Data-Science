Title:
Hound Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hot dogs
16 ounces tomato sauce
4 servings prepared mashed potatoes,instant or homemade
10 strips American Cheese

Directions:
Slit hot dogs lengthwise, but not all the way through. Fill with 1 tsp tomato sauce and mashed potatoes.  Top with cheese strips.  Place in 9x10 baking pan lightly sprayed with non stick spray.  Pour remaining sauce over hot dogs.  Bake at 375 degrees for about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
This is quick and easy, also tasty.  Kids usually love this.
