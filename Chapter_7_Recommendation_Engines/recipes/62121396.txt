Title:
Cheesy Sausage and Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces of ground italian sausage
1/2 Cup chopped onion
1/2 Cup chopped red pepper
2 cloves minced garlic
2- 8 ounce packages cream cheese
1 1/2 Cup parnesan cheese
1/4 Cup milk
1/4 Cup mayo
1/4 Cup sour cream
2 Cups chopped fresh spinach
1/3 Cup drain and chopped banana peppers rings

Directions:
In large skillet, cook sausage, onion, red pepper, and garlic until sausage is brown, stirring often.
Drain fat.
Set sausage mixture aside to cool.
In large bowl, stir together cream cheese, parmesan, milk, mayo, and sour cream.
Add sausage mixture, spinach, and banana peppers.
Stir well until combined.
Spread mixture into 9 inch baking dish.
Bake in 350 degree oven for about 30 minutes or until bubbly.
Serve as desired.
