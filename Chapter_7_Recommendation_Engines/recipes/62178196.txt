Title:
Sweet potaotes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-9oz can pinapple tidbits and juice
5C mashed sweet potatoes or yams
1/2tsp salt
2Tbsp butter
1 small pkg mini marshmallows
1/3C pecan halves

Directions:
Combine pinapple with juice, potatoes, salt and butter. Place 1/2 of the mix in a buttered casserole. top with 1/2 the marshmallows. Add the remaining half of the potato mixture. Arrange pecans on top. Cover and bake at 350 for 30min. Remove cover about 10min before serving, add remaining marshmallows.
