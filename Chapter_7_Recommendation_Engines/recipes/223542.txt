Title:
Peach-Basil White Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5466528.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle Pinot Grigio
1 fresh peach, chopped
1/2 cup peach schnapps
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
2 tablespoons agave nectar
ice cubes
1 fresh peach, sliced

Directions:
Stir Pinot Grigio, 1 chopped peach, peach schnapps, basil, and agave nectar together in a pitcher; add ice. Pour into glasses and garnish with peach slices.

Submitter: 
Liza
Description: 
My inventive twist on a delicious white sangria - perfect for summer! Use any un-oaked white wine, such as Pinot Grigio.
