Title:
Grilled Cheese Stuffed Jalapenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668784.jpg
Ingredients:
8 jalapenos
1 cup shredded jack cheese
4 tablespoons Hidden Valley® Spicy Ranch Salad Dressing
4 teaspoons KC Masterpiece® Spicy Original Barbeque Sauce

Directions:
Mix cheese and Hidden Valley(r) Spicy Ranch Dressing together in a small bowl; set aside.
Place chilies on heated grill; roast about 4 minutes per side or until peppers begin to blister.  Remove from heat and allow to cool about 10 minutes.
Reserving stem end, cut chilies in half lengthwise and remove seeded core.   Spoon cheese mixture into chilies.  Arrange stuffed chilies in a small heatproof skillet or pan.  Return to grill and heat 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.   Drizzle with KC Masterpiece(r) Spicy Original Barbeque Sauce.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
In this appetizer jalapenos are stuffed with cheese and spicy Ranch dressing, grilled, and drizzled with barbeque sauce.
