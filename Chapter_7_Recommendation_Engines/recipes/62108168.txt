Title:
Simple Fruit Cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 3 c melon, cut into cubes (canteloupe, honeydew, watermelon -- alone or in combination, it's up to you)
1 pint fresh blackberries
1 quart fresh strawberries, halved or quartered depending on size
Sprigs of fresh mint
1/3 - 1/2 c. hibiscus flower vinegar, or to taste

Directions:
Put fruit in bowl.
Tear leaves (and stems) of mint into pieces, reserving a few sprigs for decoration.
Mix fruit, herbs and vinegar in bowl; decorate with reserved sprigs.
Put in refrigerator and allow fruits to meld with herbs for an hour or two.
Serve in a bowl of ice.

Submitter: 
The Magpie
Description: 
Simple and easy, goes together in minutes -- and can make the best of whatever's on sale this season.  At breakfast or a barbecue, can be a great part of an attractive presentation.
