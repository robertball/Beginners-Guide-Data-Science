Title:
Mexican Crock Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/733569.jpg
Ingredients:
1 2-3 lb. roast
1 can whole tomatoes, cut in half
1 sm can green chilies
1 pkg taco seasoning

Directions:
Place roast in crock pot.
Pour canned tomatoes over roast, cut tomatoes in half.
Dump in green chilies.
Sprinkle taco seasoning on top.
Cook on low for 8-9 hrs. (or on high for 3-4 hrs).
Serve over rice

Submitter: 
Nauntie
Description: 
Easy to make, and good change of pace. Recipe source: Susan Bolster, Splendora Postal Carrier.
