Title:
Confetti Peanut Butter Snack Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3536014.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup KRAFT Smooth Peanut Butter
1/4 cup honey
1 cup large flake rolled oats
3/4 cup crushed crisp rice cereal
1/2 cup JET-PUFFED Fruit Flavoured Mini Marshmallows, cut in half

Directions:
Mix peanut butter and honey in medium bowl until blended. Stir in remaining ingredients. Refrigerate for 20 to 30 min.
Roll into 20 (1-inch) balls, using about 2 Tbsp. for each.

Submitter: 
Kraft Peanut Butter
Description: 
Try our Confetti Peanut Butter Snack Bites with the extra crunch of crushed rice cereal and fruit flavoured mini marshmallows. Our easy-to-make recipe is sure to be a favourite with your family.
