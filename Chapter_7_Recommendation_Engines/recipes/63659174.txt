Title:
pepper jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c	finely chopped hot pepper
1/4 c	finely chopped bell pepper
1 1/2 c	vinegar
61/2 c	sugar
1 tsp	salt
1-6 oz	liquid fruit pectin
1-6 oz	red-green food coloring

Directions:
Put pepper in blender with vinegar,blend well.Mix pepper mixture,sugar and salt in a large pan.Bring to a boil,boil for 2-3 minutes.Remove from heat and add fruit pectin and food coloring,stirring constantly.Let stand 2-minutes,then skim off foam.Pack in clean hot jars and seal
