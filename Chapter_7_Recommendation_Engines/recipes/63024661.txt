Title:
Rotel Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757969.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Velveeta Cheese
1 can Rotel Tomatoes

Directions:
cut Velveeta Cheese into small blocks with a knife and place them into a crock pot.
open the can of Rotel Tomatoes and pour them in as well.
mix it all up as it heats up and the cheese melts.
get out your favorite corn chips and enjoy.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Cheese dip & chips is a good snack or a good side to go with hamburgers or sandwiches. It's also good to have around when people are coming over, or for the Holidays. And the best thing... it only takes a few minutes to make.
