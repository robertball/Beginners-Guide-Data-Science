Title:
Strawberry Cream Freeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/37394.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (10 ounce) package frozen sweetened strawberries, thawed
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup whipping cream, whipped

Directions:
Place cream cheese in a bowl; mash with a fork. Add berries and sugar; mix well. Fold in whipped cream. Spoon into foil-lined muffin tins. Freeze until firm, about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Ruth Ann Stelfox
Description: 
Youngsters need to gather just four ingredients to fix this fruity frozen dessert. 'It's delicious, fast and easy to throw together,' promises Ruth Ann Stelfox of Raymond, Alberta.
