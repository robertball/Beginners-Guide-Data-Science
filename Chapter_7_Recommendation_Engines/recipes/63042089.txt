Title:
Simple Tuna Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
1/2 tsp sweet pickle relish (adjust to taste)
1 tsp light mayo (or just enough to hold it together)
dill to taste
pepper to taste
2 English muffins, split
4 slices Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, combine tuna, mayo, relish and spices. Divide mixture onto 4 halves of English Muffins. Top each with a slice of Cheddar cheese. Place on baking sheet.
Bake in oven for 10 minutes, or until cheese begins to bubble.
