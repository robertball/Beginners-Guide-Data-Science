Title:
Mama's  Bloody Mary Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Makes One Gallon
2 (46 fluid ounce) cans tomato juice
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed beef consomme (such as Campbell's ®)
1/3 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup  pickle juice
1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce (I use Bar Louis)
2 tablespoons celery salt
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon Seven Pepper Blend
1 tablespoon Horseradish

Directions:
Combine 1 can tomato juice, beef consomme, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, pepper, pickle juice, and celery salt in a blender and mix. Pour contents from blender in a glass gallon jar and add remaining tomato juice, stir well. Refrigerate for 24 hours before drinking.Use as mix with vodka. Garnish with a pickle spear or celery stalk.
