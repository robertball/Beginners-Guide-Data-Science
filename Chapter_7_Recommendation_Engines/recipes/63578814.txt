Title:
Green Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP Ground Flax Seed
4 Cups Baby Spinach
1 Banana (medium)
1 Cup Frozen Fruit Mix

Directions:
Put all ingredients in blender.
Blend until smooth.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Carrie Reed
Description: 
Awesome green smoothies. Even my one and three year old love this!
