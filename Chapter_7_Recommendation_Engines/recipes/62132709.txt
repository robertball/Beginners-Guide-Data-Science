Title:
MA's Too Easy Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese - light version okay to use
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder - more or less to taste
1/4 lemon - juice of
1 can Campbells Cream of Shrimp soup
8 - 10 oz pkg precooked salad shrimp - roughly chopped
*optional - 1 Tablespoon fresh chopped cilantro
* optional - 1/4 teaspoon Old Bay seafood seasoning

Directions:
Allow the cream cheese to come to room temperature.  Add garlic powder, Old Bay, and lemon juice, beat until  smooth.  Fold in salad shrimp,  cream of shrimp soup and cilantro.
Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
MA McBridges
Description: 
Light and delicious,  I love this simple cold Shrimp dip best served with buttery Ritz crackers.  But I promise you, any dipper will do!   Warning: it's hard to resist and not so easy to stop once you get started so,  watch out!
