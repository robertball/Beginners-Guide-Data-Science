Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
4 cups frozen mixed veggies (carrots, corn, peas, etc.)
1 cup beef gravy
6 oz Velveeta (or shredded cheese)
garlic salt and pepper to taste
2 cups mashed potatoes
1 stick butter or margarine (if making mashed potatoes)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Brown beef in large skillet.
Stir in mixed veggies and gravy and season with spices.
(If cooking mashed potatoes: bring chopped potatoes to boil, drain, mash, add butter and seasonings.)
Spoon beef mixture into 9x13 pan.
Top with mashed potatoes and shredded cheese.
Bake 20 mins.
