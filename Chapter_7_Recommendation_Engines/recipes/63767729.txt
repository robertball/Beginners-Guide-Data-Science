Title:
Mom's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger (or Italian sausage or meatballs)
1 medium onion, chopped
28 ounce can stewed diced tomatoes
8 ounce can tomato paste
15 ounce can tomato sauce
8 ounces canned sliced mushrooms
1 tbsp. sugar
2-3 bay leaves
1 tbsp. oregano (or to taste)
2 tbsp. basil (or to taste)
1-2 tbsp. garlic powder (or to taste)
1 lb. spaghetti noodles

Directions:
Brown meat and onions in an electric frypan.
Add tomato products, mushrooms and seasonings.  If sauce is too thick, add water 2-3 tablespoons at a time.
Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to simmer and simmer for at least 30 minutes.
While the sauce is simmering, cook the spaghetti noodles according to package directions.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
A wonderful base recipe for spaghetti sauce that can be endlessly tweaked to your own personal taste.  My dad loved it with a ton of oregano, but I like it with just a little oregano and heavy on the basil and garlic.  I've also added sliced black olives before and it was delightful.
