Title:
Yummy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. bag frozen hash browns, 1 pt. sour cream, 1 c. grated cheddar cheese, 1 sm. chopped onion, 2 c. crushed corn flakes, 1 can cream of celery soup, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1 stick melted butter

Directions:
Pour 3/4 of the melted butter into a 9*13 pan. Mix sour cream, soup, onion and seasonings. Add hash browns and cheese; mix and pour into pan. Mix cornflakes and remaining butter; sprinkle on top of hash brown mixture. Bake 350 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shannon Lindell
Description: 
Easy potato side dish. everyone seems to like it and it goes well with ham.
