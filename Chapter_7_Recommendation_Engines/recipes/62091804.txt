Title:
My Most Requested Dinner Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759526.jpg
Ingredients:
For the dressing:
1 tablespoon prepared minced garlic
2 tablespoons ground mustard
6 tablespoons white vinegar
6 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon salt (or more to taste)
1/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes
2 cups vegetable oil (do not use olive oil)
For the salad:
2 bags triple washed greens (such as iceburg, radichio, leaf lettuces)
8 slices pepper bacon, cooked and torn into bite-sized pieces
6 oz crumbled blue cheese
1/2 small, red onion, thinly sliced into rings
2 medium sized tomatoes, cut into wedges

Directions:
For dressing:
Place the first 7 ingredients into the blender and mix on high until smooth.  Drizzle in the vegetable oil on low speed over several minutes until dressing is thick.  Season with salt and pepper, if desired, and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
For the salad:
Place salad greens in large party sized bowl.  Add onion, bacon, blue cheese, and arrange tomatoes on top.
Drizzle desired amount of dressing over salad and toss gently to coat.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This is my most requested salad recipe ever.  It's a staple at family gatherings, potlucks and dinner parties.  I think the blue cheese and pepper flavors make this a great accompanyment to beef, but my friends and family tell me it tastes great with anything!
