Title:
Non Toxic Dishwasher Rinse Agent Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3619482.jpg
Ingredients:
Hydrogen Peroxide 3% strength
Pure lemon essential oil

Directions:
Put 2 drops of lemon oil in the rinse aid compartment. Add enough peroxide to fill the dispenser.

Submitter: 
CarolynCooks
Description: 
This rinse aid is anti bacterial. It will not break down the rubber parts in your dishwasher, unlike vinegar. It is also super cheap!
