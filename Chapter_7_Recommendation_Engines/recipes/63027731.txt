Title:
Chicken and Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759160.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 2 pound of chicken breast
2. 1 green pepper
3. 1 large onion
4. 1 can of corn
5. 2 tablespoon of chili power
6. 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
7. 2 cans of Swanson chicken broth
8. 1 can of black beans with liquid
9. 1 can of diced tomato
10. Half can of tomatoe sauce
11. 1 tablespoon of sofrito (just a little Puerto Rican taste opt)
Salt to taste
1-3 Jalapeno's (depending on how hot you want your Chili, I usually just put it on the side and everyone can add their own jalapeno's)
Sour Cream
Cilantro
Sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cut chicken into cubes and cook until no more pink, cut green pepper and onion add to chicken cook until tender.  Add the rest to the ingredients from 4 to 11 bring to boil lower heat and cook for about one hour.
Garnish with cilantro, cheese and sour cream
