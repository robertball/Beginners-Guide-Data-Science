Title:
Garlic Butter Pan fried Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 steak of your choice 12-16 oz
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tbsp olive oil
garlic butter topping
4oz butter softened to room temp
1tbsp parmesan cheese shredded
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
1.lightly coat your entire steak with oil. Sprinkle the salt and pepper over the entire steak.
If you are cooking a thicker steak and will be finishing it off in the oven, make sure to
preheat over to 400 degrees
2. heat your skillet to med/med high heat. Once heated add steak and cook for 5 mins
on each side. I you have thicker steak, you can also cook on each long sides for 1 min.
3. To make garlic butter, combine the butter, parmesan cheese, garlic in a small
bowl. Place in refrigerator to harden.
4. Finish cooking thick large steaks in a 400 degree F oven for 2-5 minutes in a cast iron skillet.
5. remove from heat and top with garlic butter. Let rest for a few minutes, while loosely tented with foil.
Notes: remove steak from oven 5 degrees before your desired doneness
