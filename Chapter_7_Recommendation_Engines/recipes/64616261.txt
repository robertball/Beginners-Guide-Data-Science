Title:
Peanut Butter Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Creamy Peanut Butter
2 cups (5 oz each) Vanilla Greek Yogurt
1/4 tsp Ground Cinnamon
1/8 tsp Ground Nutmeg
1/8 tsp Chili Powder
1/8 tsp Salt
1-2 tblsp Milk

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a small bowl. Whip with hand mixer. Refrigerate. It's ready to eat right away!

Submitter: 
Mandi
Description: 
I put this together when my apples & peanut butter addiction was at its peak. It's light, fluffy, and has a great peanut butter taste.
