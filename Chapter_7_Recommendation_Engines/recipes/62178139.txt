Title:
Ham and chesse pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Block of Chedder cheese
Smoked cooked ham
Whipped cream cheese with chives
Sliced cucumbers

Directions:
Cut cheese into 1/4 x 1/4x 4in sticks. spread each ham slice with a thin layer of cream cheese. Place cheese at one edge of the ham; roll up. chill for 1 hour. Cut into 1/2in slices just before serving. Serve on cucumber slices.
