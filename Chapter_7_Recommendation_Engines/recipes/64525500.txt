Title:
5 Cup Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup canned tidbits pineapple, drained
1 cup canned orange sections, drained
1 cup coconut
1 cup mini white marshmallows
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.
Let sit a few hours or overnight.
Stores well in fridge for a few days.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This is an old stand-by from many generations. It's fast, tasty, stores well and with it's sweet ingredients, can substitute as a dessert.
