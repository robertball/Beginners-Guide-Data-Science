Title:
Squash Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups yellow squash pieces
1 onion, chopped
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 or 2 grated carrots
1 cup boiling milk
1 stick butter, melted
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
2 eggs, beaten
8 oz sour cream
1 12 oz pkg herb seasoned stuffing crumbs

Directions:
Saute squash and onion with a tablespoon of butter until tender (about 10-12 min on medium heat).
Mix butter with stuffing crumbs.
Mix 1/2 stuffing mixture and all other ingredients in casserole dish and top with remaining crumbs.
Bake at 350 for 35 min.
