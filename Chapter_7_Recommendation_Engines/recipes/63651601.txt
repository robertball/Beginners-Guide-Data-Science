Title:
Sloppy Joe Cornbread Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can manwich
1 pkg cornbread mix
1/3C milk
2 T melted butter
1/4 cup egg beater

Directions:
Spray 8" square dish with PAM.  Cook and stir beef in skillet 7 min or until done.  Drain.  Add Manwich, heat until hot.  Spoon into square dish.
Stir together muffin mix, milk, butter, and egg beaters in bowl.  Spoon over beef mixture
Bake at 400 15 min or until set
