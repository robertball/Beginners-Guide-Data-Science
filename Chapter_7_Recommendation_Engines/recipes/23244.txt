Title:
Chicken and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/65243.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons LAND O LAKESÂ® Butter
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 3x1/2-inch strips
2 1/2 teaspoons fajita seasoning
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 (16 ounce) package frozen pepper stir-fry vegetable combination
1 cup frozen whole kernel corn
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Melt butter in 12-inch skillet until sizzling; add chicken, fajita seasoning and salt.  Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until chicken is no longer pink (5 to 6 minutes).
Stir in vegetables.  Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until chicken is fork tender and peppers are crisply tender (5 to 6 minutes).
Stir in cilantro.  Serve with warm tortillas and sour cream, if desired.
