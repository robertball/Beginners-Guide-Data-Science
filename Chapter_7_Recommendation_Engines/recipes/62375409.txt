Title:
Pad Thai Delish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SAUCE:
1/2 cup white sugar
2 TBSP fresh lime juice
1/4 cup fish sauce
2 tablespoons tamarind concentrate mixed w/1/4 cup water (or use tamarind pulp alone without water)
NOODLES:
1/2 cup peanut oil
1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, cut into 1/2 inch cubes.
1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
4 eggs
1 (12 ounce) pkg. Pad Thai noodles (get at asian mkt)
FINAL TOUCHES:
1 1/2 TSP salt
1 1/2 cups finely chopped/unsalted/dry roast peanuts
1 1/2 TSP ground, dried oriental radish (optional)
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1 TSP chili powder
2 cups fresh bean sprouts
1 lime, cut into wedges
cilantro

Directions:
To prepare Pad Thai sauce: In a small saucepan over medium heat, blend sugar, fish sauce, and tamarind, set aside.
To make Pad Thai: Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft; drain.
In a large skillet or wok over medium heat, add tofu and fry until lightly golden, drain from oil, keeping oil in the pan and set aside. Scramble and fry egg and garlic. Add the tofu back to the pan with noodles and stir until noodles are cooked.
Stir in Pad Thai sauce, and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt (or sub soy sauce). Stir in peanuts and ground radish. Remove from heat. Garnish with green onion, peanut and cilantro.
Serve with lime and bean sprouts on the side.
