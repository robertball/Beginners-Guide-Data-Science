Title:
Cranberry Orange Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup milk
1 cup orange juice
2 1/4 cups flour
4 tsp. finely grated orange peel
1 tbsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup thawed, drained cranberries

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 F.
Blend butter & brown sugar.
Mix in eggs, vanilla, milk & orange juice.
Combine flour, peel, baking powder, cinnamon and salt in another bowl.
Add wet ingredients to dry, mix just until blended.
Stir in cranberries.
Bake in greased muffin tin x 22 - 25 minutes, or until tests done with toothpick.
