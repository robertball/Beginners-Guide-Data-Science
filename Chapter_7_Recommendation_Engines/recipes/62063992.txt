Title:
Post Holiday Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced turkey
1 cup pineapple chunks, well drained
1 cup diced celery
1/2 cup sliced green onion
1/4 c. dry roasted peanuts
1 cup seedless green or red grapes, halved
1/4 t. salt
1/2 t. curry powder
1 T. lime juice
2 T. chopped chutney
2/3 cup mayonnaise
Lettuce leaves

Directions:
Combine turkey, pineapple, celery, green onion, grapes and peanuts in med. bowl.
In a small bowl, mix together the salt, curry powder, lime juice, chutney and mayonnaise.
Mix the mayonnaise mixture gently with the turkey mixture.
Chill about 20 minutes or cold to your liking.
Serve on lettuce lined plates.

Submitter: 
BAJATHECAT
Description: 
I always look forward to post turkey-day so I can use up the left-overs in this tasty salad.  Easy and delicious.  If you are concerned with the calorie content, use low or non-fat mayonnaise.
