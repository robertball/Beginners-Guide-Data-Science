Title:
Deliciously Simple Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982783.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds apples - peeled, cored and chopped
3 cups water
1/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Place apples in a large saucepan and just barely cover with water. Simmer over medium-low heat until apples are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
Run cooked apples through a food mill or blender. Stir in the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, lemon juice, cinnamon. Cook over medium heat for about 3 to 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Just a plain and simple applesauce to serve with latkes.
