Title:
Dessert Sushi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 crispy rice  treat, unwrapped
1 fruit roll-up, green looks realistic but any color works.
Gummies, I prefer Swedish fish but whatever you prefer.

Directions:
Flatten the crispy rice treat.
Lay your gummies length-wise from end to end.
Roll the Rice Krispie treat around the gummies then roll the Fruit roll up around the crispy treat.
Slice your sushi roll and enjoy.

A yummy sweet version of a sushi roll.
