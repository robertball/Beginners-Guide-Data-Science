Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves or 1 whole chicken, skinned, cut in serving pieces and remove bones
2 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon thyme
1/4 teaspoon sage
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 onion, finely diced
1 large box chicken broth
water if necessary to cover
2 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces
fresh parsley

Directions:
Place the chicken (remove wing tips), butter, spices and onion in a slow cooker, and fill with enough chicken broth (and water if necessary) to cover.
Cover, and cook for 5 to 6 hours on High. About 1 hour before serving, de-bone chicken if using whole chicken, stir in both cans condensed chicken soup in the pot; replace de-boned chicken pieces, place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center (approx 2 hours).
Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley and serve

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
Easy, creamy chicken and dumplings made from refrigerator biscuits, slow cooked to comfort food perfection.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings
