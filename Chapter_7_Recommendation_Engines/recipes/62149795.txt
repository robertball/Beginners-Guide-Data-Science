Title:
Gourmet Garden Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP kalamata olive oil
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
1 8 oz can tomato paste
1 lb small assorted tomatoes (yellow, green, red)all halved
1/2 lb cherry tomatoes
2 green bell peppers, diced
2 red bell peppers, diced
1/4 lb sliced button mushrooms
1/2 white onion, diced
1 head garlic, chopped or coarsely minced
two dozen fresh basil leaves, torn
2-3 TBSP fresh thyme
2-3 TBSP fresh oregano, torn
1 TBSP dark brown sugar
4 spicy Italian chicken sausages, browned and diced
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp ground white pepper
1 TBSP ground black pepper
dash of Worcester sauce
1/4 cup each of freshly grated Parmesan-reggiano, aged Gouda, and sharp cheddar
dash red wine or balsamic vinegar

Directions:
In a large pot or dutch oven heat olive oil. Gently saute onions and garlic.
Add vegetables and spices
Add cheeses
Keep on very low heat and simmer at least 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
Add tomato juice to thin or tomato paste to thicken as needed.

Submitter: 
ally-gator
Description: 
A chunky, full-bodied sauce, this sauce holds its own when combined with breads and pastas.
