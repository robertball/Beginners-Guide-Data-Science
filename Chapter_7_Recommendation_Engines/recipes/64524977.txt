Title:
Old Fashion Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf white bread torn into 1 inch pieces
1 loaf wheat bread torn into 1 inch pieces
2 Tbls parsley
1 Tbls poultry seasoning
1 Tbls garlic powder
1 Tbls Italian seasoning
1/2 Tbls paprika
1 1/2 pound chopped bacon
1 cup chopped onions
1 cup chopped carrots
2 cups chopped celery
Chopped Turkey Gizzards from one turkey
6 eggs

Directions:
3 days before cooking put bread and all spices into a large bowl to dry
Day of cooking, place bacon in large pot, cook until grease is rendered, add onion, carrot and celery, cook for about 5 minutes, add gizzards, cook 5 minutes and let cool mostly.
Mix bacon mixture well with bread mixture, add eggs and mix well.
Bake in 325 degree oven for 2 hours or until done, or stuff turkey tightly and cook as instructed.
