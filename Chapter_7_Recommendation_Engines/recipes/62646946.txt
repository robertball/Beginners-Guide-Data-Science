Title:
Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fresh asparagus, 2 clusters
1 medium yellow onion, diced
1 TBSP minced garlic
3 TBSP Olive oil
1 TBSP Sea Salt

Directions:
1.  Heat oil in skillet.  Add onion and garlic and cook until carmelized.
2. Drizzle olive oil over cookie sheet lightly.  Place trimmed asparagus onto the sheet.
3.  Spoon garlic and onion mixture over asparagus and sprinkle with salt.
4.  Roast at 350 until asparagus is cooked, about 30 min.

Description: 
A simple and healthy roasted asparagus with minced garlic, carmelized onion, and sea salt
