Title:
Easier Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/747772.jpg
Ingredients:
1 dozen eggs, boiled and peeled.
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard
1/2 teaspoon white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Halve eggs, scooping yolks into the small bowl of a food processor.. Pulse yolks until crumbly, about 10-15 pulses. Arrange whites on serving platter.
Add remaining ingredients, process until well-combined, about 20 seconds.
Pipe or spoon yolk mixture into egg halves, sprinkle with more paprika, chill and serve.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
After many years of making deviled eggs with almost a dozen ingredients, necessity forced a change, resulting in all the flavor with a lot less work.
