Title:
Chocolate-Covered Figs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. semisweet chocolate chips
12 fresh or dried figs
1/4 tsp. coarse salt

Directions:
Microwave chocolate chips on HIGH for 30 sec. Stir.
Microwave on HIGH for 30 seconds more and stir until completely melted.
Dig figs by stem in melted chocolate and set on wax paper-lined tray.
Sprinkle with salt.
