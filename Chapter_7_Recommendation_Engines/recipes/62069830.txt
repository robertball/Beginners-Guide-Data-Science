Title:
Stuffed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of bacon (fried crisp and crumbled)
2 cups of mayo
2 packages of shredded colby jack cheese (4 cups)
4 cans of sliced potatoes
1 medium size onion

Directions:
Dice onion & drain potatoes
Mix mayo, onion, potaoes and 2 cups of shredded cheese in a  medium size bowl.
Layer a 9x11 pan with potato mixture.
top with layer of cheese and crumbled bacon.
Bake for 35 minutes at 350

Submitter: 
Jen Camilleri
Description: 
Kids & adults alike will go crazy for this delicious side dish.  It has the taste of a loaded baked potato in a casserole!
