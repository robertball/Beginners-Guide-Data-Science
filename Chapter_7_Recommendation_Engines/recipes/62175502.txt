Title:
Grilled Cajun Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c salad oil
1/4 c vinegar
1 Tbl sugar
1 Tbl fresh thyme or 1/2 tsp dried, crushed
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1 TBL salad oil
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 ground red pepper
4 med SLBL chicken breasts
4 cups salad greens
1 green onion
1/4 c sliced radishes
1 med carrot, shredded

Directions:
Dressing:
Combine 1/4 oil, vinegar, sugar, thyme and  mustard in screw top jar, mix and refrigerate.
Sauce:
Combine 1 TBL oil, onion powder, black pepper,salt, and red papper in small bowl.
Rinse the chicken, pat dry. Brush with 1/2 the sauce. Grill on uncovered grill over med coals for 12-15 min. or til chicken is done, turning once. Brush with remaining sauce after first 4 minutes and again after 4 more minutes.
