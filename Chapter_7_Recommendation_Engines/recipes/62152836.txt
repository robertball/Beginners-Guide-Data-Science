Title:
Cari's Breakfast Power Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Light Vanilla Soy Milk
½ Banana
¼ C Blueberries
¼ C Rolled Oats
½ tsp Flax Seed Oil
1 tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
Add all ingredients into blender.
Blend until smooth

Submitter: 
tristin
Description: 
This power shake will help jump start the say.  Flax seed oil contains high levels of lignans and Omega-3 fatty acids which have numerous health benefits.
Cinnamon is known to reduce cravings throughout the day.
