Title:
SOUVLAKIA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700158.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs.tender meat[lamb,pork or chicken] cut into 1 1/2inch cubes
MARINADE:
juice of 2 lemons
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tsp.each of seasoning salt and pepper
1 1/2 tsp.oregano
1 clove mashed garlic

Directions:
Mix marinade ingredients in bowl.
Add meat and let marinate in fridge overnight.Turn now and again.
Soak skewers in water.Thread meat on bamboo skewers.
Broil or BBQ. turning often untill meat is done.
