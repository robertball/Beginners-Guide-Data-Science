Title:
Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 1/2 tbsp. sugar
1 1/4 cups milk
1 tbsp. sour cream
1 tbsp. vinegar
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tbsp. baking powder
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix in order given.
Pour onto medium hot griddle.
Cook till bubbles are forming on top.
Flip and cook another minute or so.
