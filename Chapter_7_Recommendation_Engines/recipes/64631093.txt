Title:
Garlic Teriyaki Beef & Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4477307.jpg
Ingredients:
olive oil
minced garlic
brussel sprouts
beef strips
teriyaki sauce
arugula
sesame seeds

Directions:
olive oil & minced garlic in a skillet, add your brussel spouts, stir up well ... then add your beef strips, drizzle with teriyaki sauce ... let simmer for a bit ... while you put a bed of arugula & sesame seeds on your plate ... then remove your pan from the heat ... and add your meat and sprouts on top of your greens ... drizzle the pan droppings over the top ... walla!! there's your dinner

Submitter: 
Colleen Marie
Description: 
easy supper to make in minutes.  if you can find the prepared sprouts at the market ... along with the beef strips ... then there is no prep time.
