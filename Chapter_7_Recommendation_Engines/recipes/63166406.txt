Title:
Curried Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- Whole head of cauliflower
1/4c.-light mayo
1/4c.-plain Greek yogurt
2-3 tbs.- curry powder

Directions:
core cauliflower, leaving enough core that it remains whole.
mix mayo, yogurt and curry powder.
cover outside of cauliflower evenly with curry mixture.
place in deep bowl and cover with a microwave safe cover (plate is good).
microwave on high 9 to 15 minutes,and let stand covered for 2 min.
quarter and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tasty, Quick, Flavorful, Easy. Use your favorite curry mix.
