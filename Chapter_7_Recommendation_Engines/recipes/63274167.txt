Title:
Herb Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/860065.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium sized potatoes (peeled or unpeeled)
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 tsp Italian seasoning
4 tsp dried Oregano
4 tsp Seasoning Salt
3 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp minced garlic cloves

Directions:
1. Pre-heat oven to 400F
2. Clean and cube potatoes, similar to home-style fries. Place them in a casserole or similar glass baking dish.
3. Add oil and herbs, and mix to fully coat potatoes.
4. Place uncovered dish into oven.
5. Stir potatoes every 10 minutes.
