Title:
Parmesan-puffed Tilapia Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332732.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1/3 cup mayonnaise
2 tbsp thinly sliced green onion
1/4 tsp hot sauce
1 lb tilapia fillets, thawed if frozen
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Blend Parmesan, mayonnaise, green onion and hot sauce in a small bowl; set aside.
Pat fish dry with paper towels. Coast a shallow baking dish with non-stick cooking spray; place fillets in baking dish. Spread Parmesan mixture evenly over fillets and sprinkle with paprika.
Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork and topping is puffed and light golden brown.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
This fish dish is a great with melted cheese with a hot kick to it. Light and lively, high in protein low-carbs.....wonderful.
