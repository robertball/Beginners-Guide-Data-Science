Title:
Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk, or more

Directions:
Stir dry ingredients together in medium size bowl.
Cut in butter till crumbly.
Stir in milk to make a soft dough.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto boiling stew or thick soup, with heat turned to med-low.
Cover and simmer x 15 min without lifting lid.
Serve with stew or soup.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These squishy cushions of carbs add fun to thick soups and stews and cook right on top of them right before serving.
