Title:
Roasted Garlic Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/873554.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans garbanzo beans, drained
2 tablespoons tahini
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon salt
1 garlic bulb, roasted (cut top off bulb then sprinkle w/ s&p and olive oil then bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees)
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 pinch paprika

Directions:
Place the garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice, salt, cumin and garlic in a blender or food processor. Blend pouring olive oil in until smooth. Transfer mixture to a serving bowl.
Drizzle with olive oil . Sprinkle with paprika.

Submitter: 
Laura Lu
Description: 
Traditional hummus with a milder garlic flavor. Great for mediterranean wraps, stuffed in chicken or as a dip with flatbread, pita, and /or fresh veggies!Modified from: Hummus III
