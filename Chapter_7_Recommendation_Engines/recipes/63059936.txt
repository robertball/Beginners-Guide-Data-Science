Title:
Italian Mashed Potato Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs potatoes peeled and sliced
3 Tbsp butter
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup grated parmesian cheese (or  pecorino Romano, if desired)
2 lg eggs beaten
Salt and pepper to taste
Fine dry bread crumbs (Italian seasoned preferred)
8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded or thinly sliced
Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
In large pan boil potatoes until tender.
Mash potatoes, add 3 Tbsp butter, milk, grated parmesian(or Romano) cheese, beaten eggs and salt & pepper to taste.  Beat until creamy and light; add more butter and milk, if needed and to taste.
Butter 11" pie plate (can use deep dish 9") and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Arrange half the mashed potato mixture in the plate.  Cover completely with all the mozzarella cheese.
Top with remaining potatoes.
Sprinkle top with bread crumbs and dot with butter.
Bake in oven 40-45 minutes or until top is crusty.
