Title:
Alexander's Skinny Cookies & Cream Popsicle Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Chocolate Cookie Sandwiches, Crushed
4 Oz. Fat-Free Cream Cheese, Softened
4 Oz. Fat-Free Plain Greek Yogurt
1/4 C. Fat-Free Milk
2 Tbsp. Powdered Sugar

Directions:
1. Combine the cream cheese, yogurt, milk and powdered sugar together in a medium bowl until smooth and creamy.
2. Stir in the crushed cookies.
3. Pour the mixture into the popsicle mold of your choice.
4. Place in the freezer until set and frozen.
5. Remove and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes 12 fat-free popsicles that taste like heaven on popsicle sticks!
