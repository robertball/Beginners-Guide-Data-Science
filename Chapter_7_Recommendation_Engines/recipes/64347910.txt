Title:
Garlic Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp fresh basil, minced (or 1 tsp dry)
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Melt butter.  Add garlic, basil and salt and mix.
Use pastry brush to brush both sides of French bread.  Place bread slices on baking sheet or baking stone.  Bake for 13-15 minutes or until bread is hot and crispy around edges.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Makes crispy garlic toast.
