Title:
Challah Bread French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf Challah bread, sliced
8 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup Wondra (or flour)
1 1/2 cups milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 tablespoons cinnamon sugar
1/4 cup oil (for frying)
butter (optional)
whipped cream (optional)
syrup (optional)
cinnamon sugar (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl whisk together the eggs, Wondra, milk, vanilla and cinnamon sugar.
Dip the slices of bread into the egg mixture.
Fry the dipped bread slices, in batches, on a lightly greased griddle or large frying pan on medium to medium/high heat until golden brown on each side (approximately 2 minutes per side).
Serve immediately.
Butter, whipped cream, syrup or cinnamon sugan can be used to top the French toast.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
Cinnamon sugar and vanilla added to the batter make for French Toast that is excellent to serve to guests or for a special family brunch. Twisted Challah bread is a delicious, attractive and festive alternative to regular bread.
