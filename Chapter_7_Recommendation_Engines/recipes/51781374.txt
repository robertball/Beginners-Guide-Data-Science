Title:
French Savory Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 2/3 C white flour
1/3 C olive oil
2 1/2 t. baking powder
2 T butter, melted
1/2 C dry white wine
1/4 C port wine
1 1/2 C pitted green or black olives
2 C chopped ham
2 C gruyere or emmenthaler cheese
salt
pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees
In bowl, beat eggs until pale yellow and frothy
Stir in flour, baking powder, olive oil, melted butter, wine and port
Chop ham, cube cheese and slice olives
Stir in ham, cheese, olives, salt and pepper to taste
Pour into greased bread tin, loaf tin or cake pan (11 x 4 in. would be a good size)
Bake for about 50 min. or until well risen and golden brown
Serve cold in slices or as sandwich bread
Can be frozen in slices or as one whole loaf

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Serve as alternative sandwich bread or as a light snack spread with cream cheese or butter. It will be slightly holey with a robust and slightly chewy texture, almost like sourdough.
