Title:
Taiwanese Style Beef Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For beef stew--
1 medium onion
2 beef tomatoes
4 carrots, sliced
several slices of ginger (peeled)
1 cup of ketchup
1 cup of beef broth
1 cup of Chinese rice wine (or red/white wine)
1 cup of soy sauce
3 cup of water
1500g of beef heel muscle, cut into 2cm cubes
1 package of spices for beef (about 8g of ground spice mixes of Anise, Cumin, Clove, Black pepper, dried Chinese red pepper in a sachet)
For Noodles --
800g raw Ramen
2000g of water
1/2 cup of diced garlic
1/2 cup of diced scallions
Boiled Chinese white cabbage

Directions:
Place beef cubes in cold water and bring to boil. (This will "force out" the blood.) Wash the beef cubes clean and pat dry.
Arrange the onion, ginger slices, tomatoes, carrots in the bottom of the Crocpot; add beef cubes & the spices for beef sachet.
Mix ketchup, soy sauce, rice wine beef broth & water and pour it over the beef. Cook at High for 2-3 hours & then Low for 4 hours until beef turns tender.
For noodles -- Bring 2000g of water to boil. Place the raw Ramen and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add 250 ml of cold water when boil and bring to another boil. Transder the Ramen to a big bowl and use the water to cook Chinese white cabbage.
Add beef, carrots and soups to the Ramen. (onions and tomatoes will have alreayd melted into the soup) Sprinkle with diced garlic and scallions. Serve

Submitter: 
Anita Chen
Description: 
Beef stew cooked with onions, carrots and tomates in a slower cooker, paired with Ramen and veggies -- This is my Mom's recipe of the famous Taiwanese beef noodle soup. I don't eat beef noodles anywhere else because I can't find anything like this anywhere!
