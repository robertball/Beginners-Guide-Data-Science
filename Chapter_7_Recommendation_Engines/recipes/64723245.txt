Ingredients:
2 pounds frozen beef bones
1 Medium onion, quartered
2 carrots, roughly chopped
2 Celery stalks, roughly chopped
5 cloves of garlic, whole
Cooking spray
6 cups boiling water
2 Bay leaves
! Teaspoon sea salt
1 Tablespoon Apple Cider Vinegar
10 Peppercorns

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Line a cookie sheet with foil and spray with cooking spray
Place the bones, carrots, celery, onion and garlic on the sheet and roast for 45 minutes.
Scrape the contents of the cookie sheet into the Instant Pot and add the bay leaves, salt, peppercorns, vinegar and water.
Place the lid on the pot and close the valve.
Press Manual and adjust the time to 120 minutes.
Remove and discard the bones and veggies.
Strain through a cheese cloth lined strainer.
Cool, remove fat layer and discard.
See cooks notes.
Note: I use bones from roasts and grilled steak,  do not use bones that have BBQ sauce.
