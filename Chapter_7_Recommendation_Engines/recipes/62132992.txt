Title:
Potato Salad With Class Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 Tablespoons prepared mustard
2 Tablespoons cider vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt (or to your taste)
1 teaspoon celery seed
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon pepper (or to your taste)
4 cups potatoes (5 or 6 medium) cooked, peeled and cubed
1 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup onion, diced
4 or 5 Gherkin sweet pickles, chopped
2 or 3 Tablespoons of Gherkin sweet pickle juice
2 hard cooked eggs
paprika (optional)

Directions:
Put the potatoes in boiling salted water until they are just a very little hard (about 20-25 min.).  When you think they are done, take them out and let them cool off.  When they are cool, peel them and cut into cubes.
In a small saucepan hard boil your eggs in salted water that covers the eggs.  Let them come to a boil for 12 min.
Set them aside and let them cool.
In a large bowl combine the mayonnaise, mustard, cider vingar, salt, celery seed, sugar and pepper.
Stir in the remaining ingredients with 1 egg chopped.  Slice the other eggs on top of the salad, sprinkle with paprika, cover the salad and put in your refrigerator for three hours.
Makes 5 cups

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
After my family comes over and eats this recipe, there is more left.  I just cover it, put it in the refrigerator and serve the rest for my husband and myself the next day.
