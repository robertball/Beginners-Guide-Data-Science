Title:
Poor Man's Tasso Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tablespoons butter
3 Tablespoons corn starch
1 clove garlic
2 Tablespoons green onions, chopped
6-8 thin slices of pre-packaged ham
2 cups milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter over low heat in saucepan.
Add garlic, onions, and ham to food processor and grind until the ham is minced.
Add mixture to melted butter, cooking over low heat for 2-3 minutes.
Add corn starch and whisk until blended.
Add milk and whisk together until smooth.
Simmer over low heat for 10-15 minutes or until sauce has thickened.
Serve as desired.

Submitter: 
KMSOUTHERN
Description: 
A simplified version of the rich, ham-flavored cream sauce.  It is very easy to make and can be used in a variety of dishes.  This sauce is excellent when served over breaded chicken and mashed potatoes.
