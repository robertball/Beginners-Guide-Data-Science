Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. sirloin steak, cut into 1x2 inch strips.
1 C onion, chopped
1 4 oz. can mushrooms, drained
1/4 C butter, divided
3 beef bouillon cubes
1 C boiling water
2 T tomato paste
1 T dry mustard
1/2 t salt
2 T flour
1/2 C water
1 C sour cream
Hot cooked egg noodles

Directions:
In a large skillet saute onions and mushrooms in 3 tablespoons of butter; remove and set aside.
Add remaining butter to skillet; brown meat on all sides.
Dissolve bouillon cubes in boiling water and pour over meat.
To the meat add tomato paste, dry mustard and salt.
Cover and simmer 45 minutes.
Blend flour and water, slowly stir into skillet contents.
Cook over medium heat stirring often until mixture comes to a boil.
Add mushrooms and onions, and stir in sour cream.
Heat through without boiling.
Serve over noodles.
