Title:
Chili Philly Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. package Philadelphia Cream Cheese (block)
1 bottle of Chili Sauce (NOT cocktail sauce)

Directions:
Unwrap cream cheese, put on serving plate and let come to room temperature.
Pour entire contents of bottle of chili sauce (similar to cocktail sauce, but NOT the same) over the cream cheese.
Use a knife to spread cream cheese, with sauce, on Ritz crackers.
Yummy! Amazingly simple and amazingly good.
