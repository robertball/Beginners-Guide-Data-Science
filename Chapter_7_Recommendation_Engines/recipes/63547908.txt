Title:
Melissa's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs ground beef
1/2 onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 1/2 cups tomato sauce
8 oz. jar mild salsa
4 Tbsp chili powder
15 oz can black beans
15 oz can red kidney beans

Directions:
Brown the ground beef and drain. Combine the onion (cut up) and salsa in a blender. Blend until smooth. Add to the cooked beef, and add the rest of the ingredients to the pot. Simmer for one hour.
