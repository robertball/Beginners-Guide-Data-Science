Title:
Nutella,Coffee,Almond Ice Cream = YUM! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. french bean vanilla Ice cream (any vanilla ice cream is usable)
3 Tbl. Hazelnut chocolate (Nutella)
1 Tbl. Coffee of your choice
Desired amount of crushed Almonds

Directions:
Scoop Ice cream and Nutella into large glass and mix throughly or until creamy with a hand mixer. Add the coffee slowly. Serve cold in a shallow bowl and Sprinkle crushed Almonds on top!

Description: 
Nutella, a touch of coffee and Nuts make Ice cream to die for! PLEEZ ENJOY ~Cozycook~
