Title:
Simple Tater Tot HotDish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 Lbs Hamburger
1 Yellow Onion -Diced (optional)
3 - 12oz Cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
Large Bag of Tater Tots
Seasoned Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Place hamburger and the diced onion into a pan.  Shake some seasoned salt and pepper on top of mixute and fry until brown.  Drain grease.  Transfer hamburger into 9 x 13 baking pan stir in cans of cream of mushroom soup.  Then top with the tater tots, add seasoned salt and pepper on top.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 50-65 minutes or until tatertots turn golden brown on top.

Description: 
This is very quick and easy to throw together.  The kids always gobble it right up!
