Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can tomato paste
2 lbs. bacon, chopped small
5 lbs. ground beef
4 medium yellow onions, small diced
2 Tbsp. beef base
1/2 C red wine
2 Tbsp. chili powder
2 Tbsp. cumin
2 Tbsp. poultry seasoning
2 Tbsp. cajun seasoning
2 12oz. cans beer (or water)
2 10lb. cans pinto beans, most liquid drained off

Directions:
In a large pot, saute bacon until medium. Add onions and saute 2-3 minutes on medium heat.  Add ground beef and red wine.  Cook until ground beef is browned.  Add chili powder, cumin, poultry seasoning, cajun seasoning, beer, and pinto beans.  Simmer on medium heat for 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Rich and hearty, yet not too spicy.
