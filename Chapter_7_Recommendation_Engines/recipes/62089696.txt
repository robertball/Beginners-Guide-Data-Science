Title:
Craig's Chili Con Carne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. vegetable oil
2 cups chopped onions
2 cloves garlic
2 pounds ground round
1/2 cup chili powder or to taste
6 oz. tomato paste
8 oz. tomato sauce
14.5 oz. beef broth
1/2 t. celery salt
1 t. cumin
oregano to taste
cayenne pepper to taste
salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan and cook onions until wilted.  Add the ground round and brown, then add chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and oregano.  Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and half of the broth.  Stir in celery salt, cumin, and cayenne pepper, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer on low for 1 to 2 hours.  Add remaining broth if desired. Serve over rice.

Stephanie Zartman
Description: 
One of my husbands easy creations.  Freezes well.
