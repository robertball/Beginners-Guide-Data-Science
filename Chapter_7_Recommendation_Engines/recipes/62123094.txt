Title:
Easy Blender Yorkshire Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. milk
4 eggs
2 c. flour
¼ c. oil (preferably vegetable or canola)

Directions:
In a blender, combine eggs and milk. Blend well.
Add flour ½ c. at a time, blending thoroughly in between each.
Let stand for 1 hour at room temperature. Every 15-20 minutes, give the blender a whirl to mix the batter.
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
Distribute the oil equally among 12 muffin cups, a little over a teaspoon per cup.
Place in the oven for 5 to 10 minutes, until smoking.
Remove from the oven and quickly ladle about 1/4 cup of batter into each cup.
Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.
Smother with gravy and enjoy!!!
