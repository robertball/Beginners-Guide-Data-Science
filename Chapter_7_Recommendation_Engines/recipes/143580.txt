Title:
Blue Cheese Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937836.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package shredded coleslaw mix
2 cups seedless red grapes, halved
1/2 cup shredded carrot
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup prepared Dijon-style mustard
1/3 cup crumbled blue cheese
2 tablespoons SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 tablespoons cider vinegar

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, mustard, cheese, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and vinegar.  Add the coleslaw mix, grapes, and carrots and stir until evenly coated.  Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Colorful and crunchy, this slaw bursts with flavor.  Convenient shredded coleslaw mix sparkles with red grapes and a tangy, blue cheese dressing.
