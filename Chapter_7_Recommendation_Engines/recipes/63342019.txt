Title:
Andalucian Style Sea Bass with Potatoes in a Creamy White Wine and Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 fillets Sea Bass/Tilapia/ Other Firm White Fish - skinned & deboned - cut into serving pieces
1 to 1½ Potatoes - peeled and cut into small cubes
180ml/6fl oz White Wine
100ml/3½ fl oz Chicken Stock
2 large Tomatoes - peeled, de-seeded and chopped
150ml/5fl oz Crème Fraiche (or Double Cream)
1 to 2tbsp - to taste fresh Dill - finely chopped
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
To Serve - 1 Lemon - cut into 2 halves - to taste
To Serve - Crusty Bread

Directions:
Heat the oil in a pan and, on low medium heat, fry the potato cubes for 10 - 12 minutes or until golden-brown;
Add the wine to the pan and cook until reduced by half;
Pour in the stock, tomato and crème fraiche and bring to simmering point;
Gently place the fish pieces into the pan and, reducing heat to low, poach for some 2 to 3 minutes or so or until fully cooked through;
Season to taste with salt and pepper;
Gently stir in the dill, taking care not to break up the fish;
Dish into serving bowls;
Squeeze lemon juice over fish and into sauce - to taste.

Serve with a dry white wine and masses of fresh crusty bread.
