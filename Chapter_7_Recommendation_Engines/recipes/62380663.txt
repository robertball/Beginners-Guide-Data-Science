Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pt. of Heavy Cream
1 stick of butter
2 T. Cream Cheese
1/2 - 3/4 C. Parmesan cheese
1 t. Garlic powder

Directions:
In a saucepan melt butter. When butter is melted add cream cheese.
When the cream cheese is softened add heavy cream.
Add parmesan cheese and stir until melted.
Simmer this for 15 - 20 minutes on low.
You may wish to season with a little salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is one of my favorite sauces. Rich and creamy, it goes with everything!
