Title:
Vegan Tropical Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4876538.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mixed frozen berries
1 banana
1/2 cup frozen strawberries
1/4 cup shredded coconut
1 tablespoon chia seeds
3/4 cup orange juice

Directions:
Place frozen berries, banana, strawberries, coconut, and chia seeds in a blender; pulse briefly to combine. Pour in orange juice; blend until smooth.
