Title:
Turkey Dressing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 slices of bread, toasted and broken into pieces
1 C. finely diced celery
1 C. finely diced onion
1/4 C. butter
1/4 t. rubbed sage
1/4 t. freshly ground pepper
1/4 C. dried parsley
4 packets G. Washington Golden Seasoning
6 C. hot broth
2 C. hot water
2 C. hot milk
4 large eggs, beaten
5-6 C. shredded turkey meat (or less depending on what you have leftover).

Directions:
Prepeat over to 325&#0176;
Saute the onions and celery in the butter.
Add all the seasonings.
Heat all the liquids and add the saute mix.
Pour the beaten eggs over the bread and gradually add the hot liquids w/ seasonings.
Bake for 1 - 1 1/2 hours or until golden brown and bubbly throughout.
