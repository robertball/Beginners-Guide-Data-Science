Title:
Larb - Laotian Chicken Mince Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup long grain rice
2 lbs chicken thigh meat
2 Tablespoons peanut oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tablespoons fresh galangal - minced
2 small red chilies, finely chopped
4 green onions, finely chopped
1/4 cup fish sauce
1 Tablespoon shrimp paste
1 Tablespoon sugar
3 Tablespoons chopped fresh mint
2 Tablespoons chopped fresh basil
4 Tablespoons lime juice

Directions:
Spread raw rice on baking sheet and roast it in the oven at 350 degrees for about 15 minutes or until golden.  Cool slightly and process in a food processor until finely ground. Set aside.
Process the chicken in the food processor until finely minced.
Heat oil in a wok or large frying pan, add the garlic, galangal, chilies and green onions and cook over medium heat for about 3 minutes or until golden.
Add the chicken to the wok and stir for 5 minutes.  Break up any lumps.
Stir sugar, fish sauce and shrimp paste together and add to the meat, bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.
Remove from heat, stir in the herbs, rice and lime juice and mix until well combined.
Serve with sticky rice or in lettuce cups.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
A Vietnamese friend taught me how to make this dish that originates from Laos and Cambodia.  If you can't find galangal substitute fresh ginger and a squirt of lime juice.
