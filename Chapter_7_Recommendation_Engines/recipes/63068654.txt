Title:
Mom's Pie Crust Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups flour
1 Teaspoon salt
1/2 Cup + 2 Tablespoons Crisco
4 - 8 Tablespoons Ice Water

Directions:
Step 1:  Gently mix flour and salt with fork.
Step 2:  Add Crisco to flour.  Mix with fork until mixture is crumbly (the size of peas).
Step 3:  Start by adding 4 Tablespoons water.  Mom normally uses 6 - 7 Tablespoons.
Step 4:  Mix.  Once you have mixed the ingredients, press mixture down HARD onto the bottom of the bowl.
Step 5:  Divide dough in half.  Form 2 balls(handle the dough as little as possible).
Step 6:  Use a linen towel to roll out dough.  Lay towel flat and sprinkle with flour.  Sprinkle rolling pin with flour.  Roll out ball into pie shape.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
Mom makes the very BEST pies!  It all starts with her pie crust.  Although this recipe is similar to others, pay attention to the details outlined in this recipe.  These are the secrets she uses to make her fabulous flaky and tasty crusts!
