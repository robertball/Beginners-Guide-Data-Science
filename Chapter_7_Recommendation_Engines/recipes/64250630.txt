Title:
Sunday Morning Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. sifted flour
3 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 c. sugar
2/3 c. milk
2 beaten eggs
1/2 c. melted shortening
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 c. melted butter.

Directions:
Combine flour, baking powder, 1/4 tsp. salt, 1/2 c. sugar, milk, eggs, and shortening.
Blend and roll to 1/4' thick.
Mix 1/2 c. sugar, 1 tsp. cinnamon, melted butter, and spread on.
Bake at 375 for 25 - 30 min.
