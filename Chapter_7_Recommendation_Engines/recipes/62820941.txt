Title:
Chicken Tettrazini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Boiled Chicken, de-boned (see footnote)
8 - 12 oz. Grated Mild Cheddar Cheese
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 large Onion, chopped
12 or 16 oz. Boiled Spaghetti (see footnote)
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300°.
Boil onions in a few tablespoons of chicken broth until done - not real soft, but cooked.
Butter 9½ x 11" pan or large casserole dish.
In prepared dish, layer half of boiled spaghetti, half of chicken, half of onion, cream of chicken soup, salt and pepper.
Layer remaining spaghetti, chicken, onions, cream of mushroom soup, butter, salt and pepper.  Top with as much cheese as desired.
Bake at 300° for 20 to 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
