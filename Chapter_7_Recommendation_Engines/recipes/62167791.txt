Title:
PEI Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Becel margarine
1 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
2 1/2 cups flour
2 cups extras (chocolate/peanut butter/butterscotch chips, M & Ms, nuts, milk chocolate, etc)

Directions:
Beat margarine and sugar until fluffy.
Add eggs and beat well.
Mix vanilla, soda and salt into sugar mixture.
Add flour and stir to combine.
Add extras.
Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Cook for 10 minutes at 350F or until slightly brown at edges and soft in the middle.
