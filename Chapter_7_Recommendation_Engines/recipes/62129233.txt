Title:
Chile Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken, cubed
1/2 large onion, diced
5 cloves garlic, diced
1 large green bell pepper, diced
1 jalapeño pepper, diced (canned jalapeños may be substituted)
2 12-oz cans diced green chiles
1 tablespoon green Tabasco sauce (more or less to taste)
1/4 cup mild green salsa (optional)
5 cups chicken broth
salty and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute chicken, onions, peppers, and garlic in a small amount of water or broth in the bottom of a large pot over medium heat until chicken is cooked through. Use a paper towel to swab up the juice and fat released by the chicken.
Add chicken broth and stir. Raise heat to high.
Bring to a boil and add the canned chiles, salsa, and Tabasco.
Boil for 5-7 more minutes, then turn off heat.
Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.

Submitter: 
RHOV
Description: 
A low-fat, healthy soup with a spicy kick! Get creative with the vegetables: beans, broccoli, cauliflower, and tomatoes would make a great addition.
