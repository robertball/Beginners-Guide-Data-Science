Title:
Big Ray's Shrimp Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2993752.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb spaghetti, uncooked
1 cup milk
8 oz. Neufchatel cheese, cubed
1/3 cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
1 tsp. cracked black pepper
1 Tbl Italian seasoning
1 Tbl Oregano
1clove garlic, minced
1 lb. frozen cooked cleaned medium shrimp, thawed
2 cups grape tomatoes, halved

Directions:
Cook spaghetti as directed on package, omitting salt.
Meanwhile, bring milk just to simmer in large nonstick skillet, stirring constantly. Add Neufchatel  cheese, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, oregano, garlic and pepper; cook and stir 3 to 5 min. or until cream cheese is melted and sauce is well blended. Add shrimp and tomatoes; cook and stir 2 min. or until heated through.
Drain spaghetti. Add to sauce; toss to evenly coat.
