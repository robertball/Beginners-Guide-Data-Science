Title:
Cilantro Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 bunch cilantro chopped
2 tbs lime juice
1 tbs olive oil
1 clove garlic minced
Water or Chicken Broth

Directions:
Cook rice according to directions on the bag using the recommended amount of water or chicken broth for more flavor; but, add the salt, cilantro, lime juice, olive oil and garlic when cooking.

Description: 
A lightly flavored rice that pairs well with most Mexican type dinners.
