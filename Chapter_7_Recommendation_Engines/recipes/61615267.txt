Title:
Healthier Grandmother's Buttermilk Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281835.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter
2 eggs
1 cup low-fat buttermilk
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup white whole wheat flour
1 cup frozen corn
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Heat 9-inch iron skillet in oven for 10 minutes. Add butter and melt. Brush butter over sides of skillet. Pour excess butter into buttermilk. Whisk in eggs.
Mix together sugar, baking soda, cornmeal and whole wheat flour. Then, whisk cornmeal mixture and frozen corn into the buttermilk mixture and scrapeit all into the skillet.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
