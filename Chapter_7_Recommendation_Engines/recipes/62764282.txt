Title:
Baileys West Indies Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion chopped fine
1 lb fresh lump crabmeat (cleaned)
4 oz light oil
4 oz cider vinegar
3 oz ice water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place 1/2 onions on bottom layer of shallow  bowl
Layer crabmeat over onions
Layer remaining onion
salt and pepper
Mix oil, vinegar and water and pour over salad
Cover and marinate in refrigerator 2-12 hours (the longer the better)
toss lightly
