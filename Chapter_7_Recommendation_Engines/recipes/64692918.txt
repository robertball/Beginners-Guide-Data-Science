Title:
Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups bugles
5 cups nacho cheese bugles
4 cups mini cheese crackers
1 pkg goldfish (6 oz)
3 cups mini pretzels
2 cups crispix
2 cups cashews
3/4 cups butter flavored popcorn oil
2 (1 oz. each) ranch mix

Directions:
combine dry ingredients. Combine oil and ranch. pour over crackers and toss to coat. Transfer to cookie pans. Bake at 250 for 40-45 miniutes or until crisp, stirring occasionally. Cool on wire racks. Store in airtight container.
