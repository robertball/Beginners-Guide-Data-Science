Title:
Classic Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985552.jpg
Ingredients:
1 vanilla bean
2 cups half-and-half
2 cups whipping cream, unwhipped
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)

Directions:
Split vanilla bean lengthwise; scrape out seeds or use vanilla extract.  In large bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well.
Pour into ice cream freezer container.  Freeze according to manufacturers instructions.  Freeze leftovers.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Made with a vanilla bean, this easy vanilla ice cream recipe is delicious alone or as the basis for tasty frozen treats.
