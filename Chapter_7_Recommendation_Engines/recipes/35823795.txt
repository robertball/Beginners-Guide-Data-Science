Title:
The No BS Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs of lean ground beef
1 lrg egg
1/2 tsp 18% cream
2 cloves garlic (minced)
5 dashes worcestershire sauce
4 dashes hot sauce
1 Tblsp steak spice (any favorite)
1/2 cup bread crumbs
cheese (blue is best, chedder is excellent too)
buns (

Directions:
In a large bowl, add ground beef, garlic, steak spices, bread crumbs and sauces, mix slightly with hands
beat egg and cream until mixed, and add to ground beef mixture.
mix all ingredients with hands until well mixed.
make into personal sized patties (recipe makes 8 mid size patties, or 6 VERY large patties)
Grill 2.5 minutes per side, and then move to upper grill
Add cheese and let sit for final minute
Serve on your favorite bun, either toasted or not... that's your call!
