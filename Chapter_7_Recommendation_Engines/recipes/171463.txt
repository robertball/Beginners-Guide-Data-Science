Title:
Strawberry Daiquiri II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664307.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen strawberries
4 ice cubes
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger rum

Directions:
Place the strawberries, ice cubes, salt, sugar, and rum into a blender. Cover and puree until smooth.
