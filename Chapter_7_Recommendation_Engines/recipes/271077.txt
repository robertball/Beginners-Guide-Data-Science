Title:
Ranch Dressing  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6443957.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoons buttermilk
1 1/2 tablespoons sour cream
1 1/2 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon fine salt
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh dill
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
1 tablespoon minced celery

Directions:
Whisk together buttermilk, sour cream, mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, Dijon mustard, onion powder, dill, parsley, and celery in a bowl until smooth and well blended.
