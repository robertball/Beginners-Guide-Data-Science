Title:
Wilted Collards Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large bunches collard greens
1/2 lb. bacon, diced
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 TB. sugar
Hot pepper vinegar, if desired

Directions:
Wash collards, throughly, removing tough center stems
then chop
In large, deep pot, saute bacon until limp
add onion, garlic & sugar.
Stir in collards.
Cover pot & steam collards about 3-4 minutes or until limp.
Uncover, raise heat to high & reduce pan juices.
Collards may be cooked ahead to this point & re-heated.
Serve with hot pepper vinegar.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
This is a very good recipe for people who do not like collard greens.
