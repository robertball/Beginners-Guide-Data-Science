Title:
Alisa's Asian Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken breast, cut in to 1x1 inch pieces
2 bags Birdseye Asian Medley mixed vegetables (steam fresh)
1 cup sliced mushrooms
4-5 Tbs. honey (or to taste)
4-5 Tbs. soy sauce or stir-fry sauce*
7 sliced cloves garlic (or to taste)
salt and pepper to taste
2 Tbs. olive oil
3 cups water
3 cups minute rice

Directions:
Heat wok over medium heat with garlic, olive oil, and chicken
Cook until chicken browns- add 4 Tbs. honey + 1 Tbs. soy sauce
Remove chicken from heat when nearly finished- set aside
Leave juices in pan
In same wok add mushrooms, veggies, and rest of soy sauce
Cook until veggies are crisp outside, tender inside (7 min.)
Boil water for rice-- follow package instructions for cooking
Add chicken back to the wok, cooking for another 5-10 minutes
Serve hot over a bed of rice
