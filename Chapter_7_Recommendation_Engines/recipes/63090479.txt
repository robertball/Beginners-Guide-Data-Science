Title:
Perfect Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp red cheyene
2 eggs
2 T hot sauce (like Frank's Original)
1 cup Panko
2 large sweet onions (thinly sliced into rings)

Directions:
Slice onions thinly into rings.
In three seperate bowls
Mix flour, salt and red cheyene pepper into a bowl
Mix Eggs and hot sauce into another bowl
put Panko crumbs into another bowl
Working from left to right
Flour onion rings, then egg wash, then panko
Single layer on cookie sheet until ready to fry.  Can prepare hrs in advance of frying.
Deep fry at 350-375 until golden brown 2-3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
These come out beautiful, very crispy with lots of flavor.
