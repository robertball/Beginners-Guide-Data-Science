Title:
Vanilla IceCream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 1/4 C. Sugar
2 Tbls Vanilla
1/2 tsp Salt
1 can Milnot
1/2 Gal plus 1 C Whole Milk or 1/2 & 1/2

Directions:
Beat Eggs, Sugar, Vanilla, Salt & Milnot.  Mix well. Pour into freezer, add milk, stir.
Crank at a quick pace until firm (about 20-30 mins). Pack-off freezer and let set for 30-40 mins.
