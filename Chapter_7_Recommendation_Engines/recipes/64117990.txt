Title:
Baked Chicken Chimichangas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups cooked chicken
2/3 cup salsa
1/3 cup sliced green onions
2 tsp taco seasoning
8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
1/4 cup melted butter
1 can green chiles
1 cup colby jack cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 475. Combine all ingredients except butter in a bowl. Spoon 1/3 cup mixture into center of each tortilla. Roll up and place seam-side down in baking dish. Brush with butter. Bake 13 minutes or until crispy and golden. Top with guacamole and sour cream.
