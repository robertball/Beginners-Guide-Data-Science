Title:
Unbelievable Fish Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup water
1 tbs. baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 tbs. Old Bay Seasoning

Directions:
Whisk together the flour, milk, water, baking powder, Old Bay Seasoning, and salt in a bowl until smooth.

Submitter: 
Dan Rasmussen
Description: 
All you need for this fish batter is flour, salt, baking powder, milk, and water. Your result will be thinly-coated, crispy, and delicious.Modified from: Unbelievable Fish Batter
