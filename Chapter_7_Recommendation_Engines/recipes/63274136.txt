Title:
Carrot Pineapple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 8.25oz. can crushed pineapple
Milk
2 c. flour
1/3 c. packed brown sugar
1 T. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1 beaten egg
3/4 c. finely shredded carrot
1/3 c. vegetable oil
1/2 t. vanilla
2 T. sugar
1/2 t. ground cinnamon

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving syrup. Add milk to syrup to equal 3/4 cup.
Stir together the flour, sugar, and baking powder and salt.
Combine egg, carrot, milk/syrup mixture,, dreained pineapple, oil, and vanilla; add all at once to dry ingredients, stirring just until moistened.
Fill greased muffin pan OR pan with cupcake liners 2/3 full. Sprinkle tops with a mixture of remaining ingredients.
Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
