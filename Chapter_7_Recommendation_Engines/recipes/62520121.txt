Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
6 tbsp butter
1 cups confectioners' sugar
1 (8 inch) prepared graham cracker crusts
1 1/2 cups milk
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
About 4 oz. frozen whipped topping, thawed
Shaved chocolate for garnish

Directions:
In a medium, microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and peanut butter. Heat in the microwave until soft; mix well. Gradually stir in confectioners' sugar until the mixture is combined and fluffy. Spread mixture into pie crust.
In a small bowl, mix the milk with the instant pudding. Pour over the peanut butter mixture in each crust. Chill until firm.
Top pies with whipped topping when ready to serve.
Garnish with shaved chocolate

Description: 
Graham Cracker Crust with a creamy peanut butter layer, a chocolate pudding layer, and a whipped topping layer. Very good but also very rich!Modified from: Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie
