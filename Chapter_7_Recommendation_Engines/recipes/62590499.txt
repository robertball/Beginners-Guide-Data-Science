Title:
Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cubed
3T butter
1/2 onion, diced
1/4 t cinnamon
2t garlic
2T ginger
3/4 t turmeric
1t chili powder
1T coriander seed
1c canned tomatoes
1/4c tomato paste
1/2c plain yogurt

Directions:
Melt butter.
Add onion and cinnamon.
Stir in garlic and add rest of spices.
Add chicken and cook until done.
Add tomatoes and cook 20 minutes covered.
Add yogurt and heat through.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Serve over rice or noodles
