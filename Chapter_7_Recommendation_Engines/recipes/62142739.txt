Title:
Incredibly Yummy Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Thick Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
2 cups Dry Bread Crumbs
1 1/2 Cup Frozen Broccoli Florets
4oz Block Medium Cheddar Cheese (cut into thick strips)
5 Slices of Bacon (Cut into 1 inch pieces)
1 Egg
1 Tbsp. Water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees ferenheit.
Cut a pocket into the chicken breast lengthwise.
Combine egg and water and whisk together.
Dip chicken breast into egg wash then into bread crumbs coating all sides evenly.
Line bottom of pocket with several (8-12) pieces or bacon.
Stuff 1/4 the amount of cheese into chicken pocket.
Stuff 1/4 the amount of broccoli into chicken pocket.
Place in baking dish
Repeat steps 4-8 for each breast
Place in oven and bake 40 minutes or until cooked thoroughly.
