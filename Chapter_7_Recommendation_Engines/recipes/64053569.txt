Title:
Sherill's Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet French onion soup
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
sautéed onions and mushrooms
2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup instant oatmeal
2 eggs
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 T Bacon Hot pepper sauce  (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together 2/3 packet French onion soup with ground beef, oatmeal, 2 eggs, salt and black pepper. Shape into 6 oval patties.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown both sides of patties. Pour off excess fat.
In a small bowl, blend soup, water, remaining  packet of onion soup mix, sautéed onions and mushrooms, and Bacon hot sauce.
Take patties and put them in pyrex. Cover with sauce and put in the oven at 350 for 30 minutes.
