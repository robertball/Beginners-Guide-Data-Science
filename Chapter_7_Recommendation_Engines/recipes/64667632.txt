Title:
Hamburger Stroganoff by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
Seasoning (main: Lowarys, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Secondary: oregano, cayenne, thyme, paprika)
1/2 chopped onion
2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 lb small elbow macaroni or wide egg noodles, cooked
1 cup beef broth
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Start cooking noodles per package directions.
Brown meat, drain.
Saut√© onions (add 1c mushrooms if desired) until tender, add garlic when nearly done.
Add meat, cooked noodles, broth and sour cream. Bring to boil, reduce heat and cook on medium until mixture starts to thicken, 5-6 minutes.
Let stand for couple minutes, can add cheese and parsley.
(Go easy on the second set of seasons)
