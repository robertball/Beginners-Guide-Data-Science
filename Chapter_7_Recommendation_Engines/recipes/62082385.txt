Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large cabbage- parboiled, leaves seperated and trimmed
1 pound chicken breasts, chopped
3 eggs, yolks seperated from whites
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
2 oz. prosciutto, thinly sliced
1 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Mix chicken with lemon juice. Bind with egg yolks. Whip egg whites until stiff and fold in. Stuff cabbage leaves with mixture and roll up. Line a casserole dish with prosciutto. Then place cabbage rolls seam side down on top of procuitto, packing them tightly. Pour in stock. Cook, covered for 2 hours. Serve with juices.
