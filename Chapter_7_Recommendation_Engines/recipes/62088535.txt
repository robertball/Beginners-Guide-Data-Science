Title:
KISS Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 6-ounce salmon fillets
1/4 cup barbecue sauce
1/4 cup seedless raspberry jam

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Grease an 11x7 pan.  Place salmon in pan, skin side down.
In a small bowl, combine barbecue sauce and jam.  Generously brush this mixture over the salmon.
Bake for 35-40 minutes, until fish flakes easily with a fork.  Brush with remaining sauce again mid-way through cooking time.
