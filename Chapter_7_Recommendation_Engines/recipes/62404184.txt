Title:
Al's Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz rainbow rotini
8oz chedder cheese
6oz olives cut up
1 large tomato cut up
8oz italian dressing
8oz summer sausage cut up

Directions:
Boil noodles and drain. Add cheese, olives and tomato's and summer sausage. Toss together. Pour dressing over and mix well. Cover and chill overnight.
