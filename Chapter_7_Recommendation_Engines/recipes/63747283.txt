Title:
Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1039904.jpg
Ingredients:
64 oz. container of vegetable juice or tomato juice
48 oz. container of beef broth
Vegetables: I use potatoes, celery, carrots, corn, green beans, peas, and beans ( you can use fresh or frozen)
Pasta- I used alphabet pasta
3 pounds beef- I used London broil
Onion soup mix packet
Worcestershire sauce
Beef base
Water
Seasonings- I used onion powder, garlic powder, oregano, basil, parsley, bay leaves sea salt, and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.
Place London broil into a baking dish.
Top with onion soup packet, Worcestershire sauce, and seasonings.
Add about 2 cups of water to pan.
Cover with aluminum foil, and then bake in oven for about 3 hours. Turn it over about ½ way through cooking.
When beef is finished cooking, let it rest for a little while.
Mix vegetable juice and beef broth in a 12 quart pot.
Add enough water to pot so that it is almost ½ full.
Add about 2 tablespoons beef base and seasonings
Turn heat on medium to medium high
Cube potatoes and add into pot.
Cut up carrots and add into pot.
Bring to a boil, then let simmer for about 20 minutes.
Add corn, peas, and green beans, and then continue to simmer.
Add alphabet pasta into soup.
Pour liquid from London broil pan into pot.
Cut up beef and then add into soup.
Simmer for about 10 minutes.
Taste and add any more needed seasoning.
Serve
Enjoy!
