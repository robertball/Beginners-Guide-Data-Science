Title:
Best of All: Crockpot Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
•	2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
•	1/3 cup all-purpose flour
•	1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
•	1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
•	2 tablespoons olive oil
•	1 onion, chopped
•	1/2 cup red wine
•	1 1/2 cups beef broth
•	1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
•	2 bay leaves
•	2 cloves garlic, minced
•	1 teaspoon paprika
•	1 packet McCormick's Beef Stew Seasoning
•	3 potatoes, diced
•	4 carrots, sliced
•	1 stalk celery, chopped

Directions:
In a small bowl mix together the flour, salt, and pepper. Put into gallon-sized, zippered bag. Shake beef in bag to coat.
Sauté coated beef in 2 tablespoons olive oil until browned.
Add chopped onion to beef to sauté and soften.
Transfer all to crockpot.
Mix beef broth, Worcestershire, and wine.
Pour into hot skillet to deglaze, and then pour over beef and onions in cooker.
Add remaining ingredients.
Cover and cook on high setting for 1 hour.  Then low setting for 6 or 7 hours.
