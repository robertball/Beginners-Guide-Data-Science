Title:
Wasabi Soy Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 teaspoons prepared wasabi
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons rice vinegar
1 teaspoon honey
1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
3 tablespoons salad oil, grapeseed, olive, or any nut oil

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a mason jar, close lid tightly and shake a few times.

Submitter: 
Nathan Hendrix
Description: 
Beware the addictive properties of this dressing.
