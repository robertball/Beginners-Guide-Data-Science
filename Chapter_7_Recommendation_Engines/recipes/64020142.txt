Title:
Gluten Free Pesto Chicken Cakes with Spicy Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dipping Sauce:
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream or full fat plain Greek yogurt
1/2 teas. hot sauce or to taste
1 level Tbls pesto
S&P taste
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup low fat mayonnaise
2 Tbls prepared pesto
1 clove garlic minced
1 cup gluten free panko bread crumbs
1/3 cup finely minced red onion
3 cups cooked finely shredded chicken breast
1 Tbls olive oil

Directions:
Dipping sauce:  Combine all ingredients in a smallish bowl and set in the refrigerator to chill.
Chicken Cakes:  Combine egg, 1/4 cup mayo, pesto and garlic in large bowl.  Mix well.  Add panko crumbs and red onion.  Stir to combine.  Add chicken and thoroughly combine.  Form mixture in balls (about 3 Tbls mixture each) and compress to about 1/2 inch cake.
Heat oil in non stick skillet over medium heat.  Add cakes and fry on each side for about 3 min until brown.  Remove to a paper towel lined platter.  Serve warm with a dollop of dipping sauce.

Submitter: 
Mary P.
Description: 
A hardy appetizer packed with great flavor. These cakes have always been a crowd pleaser.
