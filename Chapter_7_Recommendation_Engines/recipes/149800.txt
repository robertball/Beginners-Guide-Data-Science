Title:
'Warm Tiramisu' Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981956.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet SPLENDA® Flavors for Coffee, French Vanilla
1 packet SPLENDA® Flavors for Coffee, Cinnamon Spice
1/4 cup espresso
1/2 cup 1% milk
2 teaspoons mascarpone cheese
1/2 teaspoon cocoa powder
1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix SPLENDA(R) Flavors for Coffee, espresso and milk together in a mug. Heat in a microwave 30 to 40 seconds or to desired temperature. Pour into jar of blender.  Add remaining ingredients.  Blend 15 to 20 seconds or until frothy. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This Italian inspired latte tastes just like everyone's favorite layered  dessert. Serve this to friends for an afternoon coffee break or as part of dessert at your next dinner party and take everyone on an Italian adventure!
