Title:
Linguine with Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. brown rice pasta
4 cans clams (DO NOT DRAIN)
1 Tbs. minced garlic (from a jar)
1 cup white wine
1/2 cup olive oil
2 Tbs. butter
1 Tbs. lemon juice
Splash of half and half
2 tsp. Italian seasoning (basil, oregano, etc.)
2 tsp. Kosher salt
3 handfuls Shredded Italian cheese blend (Parmesan, Romano, Asiago, etc.)

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed.
In a saucepan, heat the wine, olive oil, garlic and butter.
Add clams WITH liquid.
Add the other liquid ingredients and seasonings and stir.
Add cheese a handful at a time, stirring as it melts, to blend.
Simmer while pasta finishes cooking.

Submitter: 
KimmerG40
Description: 
I made this up trying to replicate the linguine and clam sauce they served at Abrusci's in Wheatridge, CO.  Their version had a lot of garlic that made it a little too spicy for the girls.  This is my version and they said Don't change ANYTHING!
