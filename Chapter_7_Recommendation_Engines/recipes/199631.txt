Title:
Peanut Butter and Honey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/341172.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 teaspoon honey
1 teaspoon butter, softened
2 slices bread

Directions:
Stir the peanut butter, honey, and butter together in a bowl until smooth; spread on 1 of the bread slices and sandwich with the other slice.
