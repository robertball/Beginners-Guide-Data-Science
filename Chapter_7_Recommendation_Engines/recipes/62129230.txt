Title:
Vegan Mocha Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable oil
3 cups soymilk
1/4 cup intsant coffee powder
2 cups cocoa powder
2 cups granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons vanilla extract
1 tablespoon Kahlua coffee liquer
4 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:
Grase and flour a 9 x 13 inch baking pan and preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Sift flour, cocoa powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.
Add the sugars and salt and mix well.
Stir in oil, Kahlua, and vanilla.
Dissolve the coffee powder in the soymilk and add to the bowl.
Mix all ingredients well.
Pour batter into a 9 x 13 inch baking pan.
Bake for an hour or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Frost as desired.
