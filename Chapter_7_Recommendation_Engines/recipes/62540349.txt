Title:
Memere's Holiday Pork Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground pork
1 onion, finely chopped
1 32 oz box chicken broth
1 box Bell's Stuffing Mix

Directions:
1. In a large pot, place ground pork, onion & chicken broth and cook for approx. 1/2 hour. Stir occasionally - mixture should not have large chunks of meat.
2. Remove from heat. Add stuffing mix - mixture should be moist (if mixture seems dry, add more chicken broth or water).
3. Place in baking dish, cover and bake in 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
WorkingMom
Description: 
My grandmother made this savory, Canadian-based stuffing every Thanksgiving and Christmas. It's the only stuffing I know and I can't have the holidays without it!
