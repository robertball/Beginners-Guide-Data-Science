Title:
"Birthday" Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 cup softened butter
2 cup sugar
3 cup plain flour
1 tsp. salt
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 1/4 cup milk (part evaporated milk)
4 eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
Icing:
8 oz. softened cream cheese
2 boxes confectioners sugar
Evaporated milk (enough to get a good spreading consistency)
1 tsp. vanilla
Dash of salt

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 3 9" cake pans. Cream butter sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, to creamed butter and sugar mixture, mixing well after each. Mix dry ing. together in a seperate bowl. Begin adding dry ing. to sugar mixture alternately with milk (beginning and ending with flour mixture).
Bake 30-40 min. Test for doneness with a toothpick. Cool 10 min. in pans, then turn onto cake racks. Ice with cream cheese icing when cool.
Icing:
Mix all ing. together until you have a sgood spreading consistency.

Description: 
A sweet, smooth, and rich cake with a flavorable cream cheese icing. Perfect for any occasion, not just birthdays.
