Title:
Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700793.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans tomatoes, Chunk
1 tablespoons dried basil
1 tablespoon dried oregano
3 to 5 cloves Of garlic, minced
1 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup finely diced onion

Directions:
Place In a Pot, over medium heat Add tomatoes, basil, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
Simmer for 1hr, stirring occasionally.
Take An Immersion Blender Or A Blender, And Puree Till The Sauce Is Smooth.
Note: There Is No Need For Tomato Paste Since Your Going To Be Cooking This Sauce Down.
