Title:
Quick Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb of ground beef
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp shortening
1 C chopped onion
1 beef bouillon cube
1 Can mushroom soup
1 Small Can button mushrooms
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 Tbsp chili sauce
1/2 C sour cream, or use 1 Tbsp vinegar to sour regular cream
3 C hot rice or egg noodles

Directions:
Season beef and shape into balls.
Brown in hot oil over medium heat.
Add onion and simmer 3 minutes.
Stir in next five ingredients.
Mix well and cover.
Simmer 10 minutes.
Blend in sour cream and heat.
Serve over cooked rice or cooked egg noodles.
