Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 cup Hellmans Mayonnaise
1 cup Miracle Whip
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
2 Tablespoons Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
2 dashes Tabasco
2 Tablespoons horseradish
salt & pepper, to taste
2 pounds of shrimp (or use canned shrimp)
1 bag shrimp boil seasoning
garlic toast rounds from the bakery, to serve opt.

Directions:
Cook shrimp in shrimp boil according to directions. Peel and let cool.
Chop cooked shrimp.  (or use canned shrimp)
Mix all ingredients well.
As with any dip like this, it needs to sit for a while or overnight to let the flavors blend.
Serve with hard rounds of garlic toast, sold in bags in the bakery department of grocery stores.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
I use canned shrimp in this recipe when I can't find good fresh gulf shrimp to boil.
