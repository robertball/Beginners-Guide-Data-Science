Title:
Garlic and tamari glazed chicken with vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large bone-in chicken thighs
1-1/2 lb vegetables (carrots, turnips, sweet potatoes--what ever suits you)
MARINADE:
1 T Dijon mustard
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/3 c. olive oil
1/3c. Tamari or Soy sauce
1/3 c. V8 or tomato juice
3 large cloves of garlic, minced

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients for marinade together and place 2/3rds of it in a large plastic bag. Reserve 1/3 for later.
Add chicken and marinate for at least 3 hours.
When ready to cook, preheat oven to 400F.
Toss vegetables in reserved marinade.
Put chicken in center of 9x13 sheet pan. Surround with vegetables.
Cook for about 30 minutes, or until veggies are soft with brown edges and internal temperature of chick is 160F.
Serve with tossed salad.
