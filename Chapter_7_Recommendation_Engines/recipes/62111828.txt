Title:
Stuffed Mashed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 potatoes, cooked and mashed
1 pkg. frozen spinach, cooked
1 C. sour cream
1 stick butter (1/2 C.)
1 tsp dill, dried or 1 tbsp. fresh
Cheddar Cheese for topping

Directions:
Cook the potatoes and mash as usual.
Cook the frozen spinach according to direction on package and drain liquid put back into the pan and add butter stirring until melted.
Combine all the ingredients and pour into a casserole dish and top with cheddar cheese.
Bake in preheated 350 oven for 20-25 min.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Tired of the same old stuff? This will add new flavors to those mashed potatoes.
