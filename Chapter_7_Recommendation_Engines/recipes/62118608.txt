Title:
A fruitcake's fruitcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup and 1 tablespoon butter
1 1/3 cup powdered sugar
3 eggs, separated
16 oz container of Extra Fancy Fruit cake Candy
1 heaping cup golden raisins
1 cup finely chopped pecans
1 cup and 1 tablespoon flour
3 tablespoons OJ

Directions:
Soak raisins and fancy fruit cake candy over night in the 3 tablespoons OJ.
Preheat oven to 275 degrees with a glass dish full of water on bottom shelf.  Line loaf pan with wax paper and then coat with non stick butter spray.
Cream butter and powdered sugar.  Stir in beaten egg yolks, saving the whites for later.  Stir in fruit mixture, chopped pecans and then mix in the sifted flour.
In a clean bowl beat egg whites at high speed until stiff peaks form.  Fold egg whites into batter.
Bake for 2 to 2.5 hours or until golden brown and toothpick comes out clean.
After the fruitcake has completely cooled, wrap it tightly in cling wrap and let it sit a minimum of 2 days.
