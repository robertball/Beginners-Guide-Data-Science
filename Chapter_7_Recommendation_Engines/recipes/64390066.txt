Title:
New Zealand Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of ground beef
3 carrots, peeled and diced
2 medium onions, peeled and diced
3 sticks of celery, peeled and diced
1 cup of frozen peas
1 cup of gravy or beef stock
1 Tbsp Corn Starch
Reconstituted Instant Mashed Potatoes

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 350F
In a big fry pan melt 2 tablespoons of butter.
Add the diced vegetables to the pan and soften these.
Add the ground beef, turn up the heat and toss the mixture together until the meat is browned.
Add the peas. Season well with salt and pepper.
Dust this with 1 tablespoons of corn starch. Mix and cook for another 5 minutes.
Add the gravy or stock. Stir over the heat until you have a thick stew-like consistency. Place this mix into a  9" x 9" dish
Top with 2 cups reconstituted mashed potatoes and a couple of dollops of butter
Bake in oven until mashed potatoes are slightly crusted and a bit brown
