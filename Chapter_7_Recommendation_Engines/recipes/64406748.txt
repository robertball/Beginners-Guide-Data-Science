Title:
Momma's Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. shredded cooked shredded chicken
3 lbs. shredded Monterey jack cheese
1 large onion, chopped
1 pckg. corn tortillas
2 cans Cream of Mushroom soup
I can of chopped green chili's

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large mixing bowl mix togethers chicken, cheese and onions until even incorporated.
In a baking dish 9x11 or larger, spread one can of soup on the bottom.
Scoop several tablespoons of mix onto the tortilla, roll and place seam down in the baking dish.
Continue the previous step until all the mix has been used (you can use multiple backing dishes, just remember to spread soup on the bottom).
Cover rolled enchiladas with second can of soup...and sprinkle with green chili's if using them.

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
Description: 
My Momma's FAMOUS chicken enchilada recipe! This recipe is great for freezing or feeding an army! My mother cooked for our church summer reunions for 15 years...this was a demanded meal every year :)
