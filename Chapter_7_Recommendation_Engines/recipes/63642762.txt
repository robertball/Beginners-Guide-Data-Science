Title:
Lemon blue ice pops Recipe

Ingredients:
Lemon blue ice pops
1cup sugar
1 cup water
1 cup lemon juice
3-4 cups cold water
3 cups blueberrys

Directions:
1. heat sugar and water until the sugar dezolved.
2. mix lemon, blueberrys, and cold water in a tall cup.
3. mix all lemonade and sugar together.
4. pour into molds or cups and freeze over-night
