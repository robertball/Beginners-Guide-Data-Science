Title:
Cheesy Bacon Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs bacon
1 pkg cubed mozzarella cheese
8 pack can of biscuits

Directions:
Quarter biscuits, place cheese cubes inside each quartered biscuit and close tightly. Wrap bacon tightly around biscuits and use large skewer to hold together. Heat pan of oil to 350 and place them in only a few at a time. Once a golden color and bacon is cooked, take out and drain on paper towel. Serve warm
