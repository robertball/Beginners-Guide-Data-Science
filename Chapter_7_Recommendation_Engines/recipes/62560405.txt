Title:
EASY HOMEMADE CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
chopped green pepper
2 cloves minced garlic
1 (14.5 oz) stew tomatoes
1 (15 oz) tomato sauce
1 (15oz) kidney beans (opt)
1 cup water
1 Tbl chili powder

Directions:
In large skillet combine beef, onion, pepper & garlic. Saute until meat is browned & onion is tender. Add stewed tomatoes with juice, tomato sauce, beans & water.
Season with chili powder, s & p. Bring to boil, reduce heat to low, cover & simmer until right thickness
