Title:
Rebecca's Chocolate Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
1-1/2 cups flour
1-1/2 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
3/4 cup milk
1 cup sugar
6 T. cocoa
1-3/4 cups warm water

Directions:
Melt the butter in a 9 x 13 pan.
Mix together the flour, 1-1/2 cups sugar, vanilla, and milk. Pour over the melted butter but do not stir.
Mix together the 1 cup sugar and cocoa. Sprinkle over the flour mixture.
Pour warm water over all, covering completely.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 30-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This is my son's favorite dessert.  My daughter-in-law was sweet enough to let me have the recipe.  It is really good with vanilla ice cream on top.
