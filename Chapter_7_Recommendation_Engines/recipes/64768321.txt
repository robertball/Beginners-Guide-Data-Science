Title:
Air Fried Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head fresh broccoli, cut into florets
1/4 cup olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a ziploc bag and allow the broccoli to soak up the oil.
Air fry on 375 degrees until broccoli is tender and starts to get crispy.
