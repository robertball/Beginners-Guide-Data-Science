Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags Pepperidge Farm Chessemen Cookies
6 to 8 bananas - sliced
2 cups milk
1 (5 oz) box instant french vanilla pudding
1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (12 oz) frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Line the bottom of a 13 x 9 x 2 inch dish with 1 bag of cookies and layer bananas on top.
In a bowl, combine the milk and the pudding mix, blending well using the mixer.  In another bowl, combine the cream cheese and condensed sweetened milk together and mix till smooth.  Fold  the whipped topping into the cream cheese mixture.  Add the cream cheese mixture to the pudding mixture and stir until well blended.  Pour the entire mixture over the cookies and bananas and cover with the remaining cookies.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
