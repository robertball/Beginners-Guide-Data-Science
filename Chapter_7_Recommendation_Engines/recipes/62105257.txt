Title:
taco chili salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can tomato paste
1 can kidney beans
1 pkg taco seasoning
1 bag of nacho chips
1 8oz pkg shredded cheese
1 large bag of shredded lettuce
container of sour cream
diced onions (optional)
diced tomatoes (optional)

Directions:
brown ground beef and drain fat
in a 2 qt pan, add ground beef
add tomato paste and 2-3 cans of water
add drained kidney beans
add taco seasoning
simmer for about 30 minutes stirring frequently
crush desired amount of nacho chips in a bowl or plate, top with shredded lettuce
pour taco chili over lettuce
top with cheese, sour cream and optional veggies if desired

A warm hearty meal you can cook in one pot!
