Title:
Garlic Chile Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/484367.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoon sesame oil
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
15-20 dried chile de arbol peppers
10 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 cup water

Directions:
In pan heat vegetable and sesame oil add chiles and garlic. Saute a few minutes. (carful not to burn) Set aside.
Slowly add vinegar and water stirring to combine. Bring back to boil. Remove from heat and pour mixture into blender. Let cool about 10 minutes and blend until smooth.

Description: 
Got sick of buying the jarred sriracha sauce that my husband loves; so I came up with my own version. Be warned this is garlicky and very spicy!
