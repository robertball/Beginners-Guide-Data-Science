Title:
Stuffed yellow Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Med. Yellow squash
8 Tbls. Butter
1 cup Onions, chopped
1 Tbls. Fresh Parsley,chopped
1 teas. Garlic,chopped
3/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
1 Cup Bread Crumbs
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/4 Water

Directions:
Hollow out 2 squash to within 1/8" of the skin. Chop flesh and 3rd squash into small pieces, then set aside. Cook onions in butter for about 3 min. or until tender. Add chopped garlic and chopped squash. Cook until tender. Add bread crumbs,Parmesan cheese,parsley salt and pepper; stir and set aside to cool. Stuff each hollowed squash with veg. mixture and place them into a casserloe dish stuffed side side up. add 1/4 cup of water and cover with lid or foil. bake at 350F. for 20 min.

Submitter: 
Mad Butcher
Description: 
This stuffed squash can be topped with stewed tomatoes or tomato sauce to give it an additional layer.
