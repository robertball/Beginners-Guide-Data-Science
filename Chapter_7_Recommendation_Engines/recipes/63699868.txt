Title:
Lyndsey's Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box vanilla instant pudding
1 can Eagle Brand Condensed milk
8 oz cool whip
4 bananas
Graham crackers or vanilla wafers

Directions:
Make pudding according to instructions on the box.  Add in 1 can condensed milk and cool whip.
Layer graham crackers, pudding, banana.  Repeat layering
Refrigerate to firm up.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
Given to me by my friend Lyndsey.  Although I am not a banana pudding fan, I LOVE this!  Still good a day or two later.
