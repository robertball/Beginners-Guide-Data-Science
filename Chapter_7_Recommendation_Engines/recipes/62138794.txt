Title:
Pumpkin Cookie Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Flour
1 Cup Uncooked Oats (Oatmeal)
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1 Cup Crisco (Butter can be substituted)
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Cup Granulated Sugar
1 Egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 Cup Canned pumpkin
1-2 Cups Chocolate Chips
*nuts and raisins optional

Directions:
1. Beat sugars, crisco and vanilla.  Add egg and beat well. Add pumpkin and mix well.
2. Add flour, oats, bakin soda, cinnamon, salt.  MIx until well blended.
3. Stir in chocolate chips.
4. Place spoonfuls on ungreased cookie sheets.
5. Bake at 350 for 18-20 min.
*Cookies should appear undercooked.  Let them cool on cookie sheet, then move to cooling racks to cool completely.  The cookies are best when they are moist.

Submitter: 
Tammy M Curry
Description: 
Yummy, Moist, Pumpkin/Oatmeal cookies with a hint of chocolate.
I received this recipe from my son's teacher.  She brought some from the class during a field trip and they were fantastic.  Enjoy!
