Title:
Grandmothers Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
6 tbsp cocoa
3 cups milk
1 cup flour
4 egg yolks
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 lump of butter
2 baked pie shells
Meringue:
4 egg whites
8 tbsp sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Blend together all ingredients except butter and vanilla. Cook in double boiler until mixture thickens.
Add butter and vanilla.
Beat until smooth.
Poor into pie shell.
Beat egg whites, sugar, and vanilla until stiff.
Spread over pie.
Bake at 275* just until meringue is done.

Submitter: 
Stephanie Holt
Description: 
A very rich and fudgey chocolate pie, passed down from my grandmother from her grandmother! We have it every thanksgiving and there is nothing left, even the dieters can't resist. Best served with a tall glass of milk!
