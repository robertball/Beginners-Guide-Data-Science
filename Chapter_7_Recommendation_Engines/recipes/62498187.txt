Title:
Quick Enchilada Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1- 21oz can Bush's Texas Ranchero Grillin' Beans
2 cups Mexican blend cheese, divided
1- 10oz can enchilada sauce
16 corn tortillas
Garnish:  chopped onions or green onions, sour cream, black olives or whatever else you like!

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees.  Grease a 13 x 9 baking dish.
Brown and drain ground beef.  Add Ranchero beans and 1 cup of the cheese and mix well.
Pour half of the enchilada sauce into the baking dish and spread evenly.  Layer 8 totillas over the bottom of the dish overlapping them.  Spoon meat mixture over tortillas and then cover with the remaining 8 tortillas.  Spread the rest of the enchilada sauce over top and sprinkle with 1 cup cheese.
Bake in pre-heated oven for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
