Title:
Delicious Ham and Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/763371.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups peeled and diced potatoes, mixed red, golden, and russet.
1/3 cup diced celery
1 medium finely chopped onion
1 heaping tsp. garlic, minced (optional)
3/4 cup diced cooked ham
1 cup corn
1 cup shredded carrots
3 1/4 cups chicken broth, white wine and herb, college inn
1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
1 teaspoon ground white or black pepper, or to taste
5 tablespoons butter
5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup soy milk

Directions:
Saute onions, celery and garlic in butter or olive oil until soft.
Combine the potatoes, celery/onion mixture, ham and broth in a stockpot. Bring to a boil, then cook over medium heat until potatoes are tender, about 10 to 15 minutes. Add corn and carrots, salt and pepper to taste.
In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat. Whisk in flour with a fork, and cook, stirring constantly until thick, about 1 minute. Slowly stir in soy and cream as not to allow lumps to form until all of the milk mixture has been added. Continue stirring over medium-low heat until thick, 4 to 5 minutes.
Stir the soy mixture into the stockpot, and cook soup until heated through. Add hand full of cheddar cheese Serve immediately.
