Title:
Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup flour
1 tbsp baking powder
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
5 tbsp shortening (1/3 cup)
1 cup buttermilk

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 425 F
2. In large bowl sift dry ingredients
3. Cut shortening into flour mixture with 2 knives until course crumbs form
4. Add buttermilk tossing with a fork until a dough forms
5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface
6. Gather into a disk and pat dough to 3/4 inch thick
7. Using glass dipped in flour, cut out biscuits
8. Place biscuits 2 inches apart on an non-greased baking sheet
9. Bake 12-15 minutes at 425 F
