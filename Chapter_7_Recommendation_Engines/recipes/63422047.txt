Title:
Cranberry chicken Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/924945.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2-1/2  to 3 pounds
chicken thighs and/or drumsticks, skinned
or 2 chicken breast
1  16-ounce can whole cranberry sauce
2  tablespoons  dry onion soup mix
2  tablespoons quick-cooking tapioca
1/8 t garlic salt
3  cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in a 3-1/2- or 4-quart slow cooker. In a small bowl, stir together cranberry sauce, dry soup mix, garlic salt and tapioca. Pour over chicken pieces.
Cover; cook on low-heat setting for 5 to 6 hours or on high-heat setting for 2-1/2 to 3 hours. Serve chicken and sauce over hot cooked rice or mashed potatoes. . Makes 6 servings.

Submitter:

Description: 
A beautiful,  delicious chicken and sauce.  Serve over rice, mashed potatoes or with green beans.
