Title:
Brocolli Raab Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Broccoli raab
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 cloves garlic
1 teaspoon red pepper
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Boil broccoli raab and salt in large pot of water for 5 minutes and drain.
Flatten garlic cloves with spoon or side of knife, remove peel, and mince cloves.
Add oil, minced garlic, and red pepper to pot and cook over medium heat for 30 seconds.
Add drained broccoli raab to pot and toss.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
A popular side dish in Italy.
