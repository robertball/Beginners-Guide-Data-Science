Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2474754.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box of chocolate instant pudding.
8 oz Whipped topping
3 (9 in) Crepes

Directions:
Mix instant pudding and whipped topping.
Spread a thin layer of mixture on half of a crepe.
Spoon 5 to 6 heaping spoonfuls of mixture along the edge of the mixture that has been spread on.
Roll crepe.
Cut into one inch pieces.

Submitter: 
Betsy Havel
Description: 
When you are in the mood for something quick and full of chocolatey goodness. Dinomite's Fluffy bites are perfect. These little mouthfuls deliver a burst of sweet chocolate and are very easy to make.
