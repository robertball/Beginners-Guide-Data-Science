Title:
Sue's Stuffed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pork chops
1 box Stove Top Stuffing
Olive oil
Lawry seasoning salt
fresh garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Make stuffing.
Rub one side of each pork chop with olive oil, season with Lawry salt and fresh garlic.
Put 4 chops in a pan, olive oil side down.
With a large spoon make an indention in each chop in the pan.
Mound some stuffing in the indention and top with other 4 pork chops, olive oil side up.
Bake uncovered for 3/4-1 hour until pork chops are cooked through.
