Title:
Hot Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 hot chocolate pouch
1 cup powdered sugar
2 tbsp. water

Directions:
Put the powdered sugar and hot chocolate mix into a bowl and mix thoroughly.
Add the water and mix.
Enjoy!
