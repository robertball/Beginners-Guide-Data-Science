Title:
Momma's Brunch Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (2 sheets)Frozen Puff Pastry, thawed
6 Eggs
1 cup Ricotta Cheese
Dash  Hot Pepper Sauce
1 Roll Sausage, cooked
1 ½ cups Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
½ cup Red Pepper finely chopped
½ cup Green Pepper finely chopped
½ cup Onion finely chopped
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 40O*F.  Unfold pastry sheet and roll out one sheet to about 11-inch square and one to 12-inch square.  Line bottom and side of greased  glass casserole dish with 12-inch pastry sheet.
BEAT eggs in medium bowl with wire whisk.  Reserve 1 Tbsp.  of the eggs; set aside.  Add ricotta cheese, pepper sauce, sausage, cheese, red peppers, green peppers, and onion  to remaining eggs; mix until well blended.  Pour onto pastry in casserole dish.
PLACE remaining pastry sheet over mixture; fold and tuck pastry edges in pan.  Pinch edges to seal.  Brush top with remaining Tbsp. egg.  Cut 5 or 6 slits in top crust to allow steam to escape.
BAKE 45-55 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool for 10 minutes.
