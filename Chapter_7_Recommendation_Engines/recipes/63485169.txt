Title:
Cheeseburger Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion chopped
1 clove garlic minced
1/4 cup flour
1/3 cup dill pickle liquid
1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup dill pickle chopped
1 cup cheddar cheese shredded (4oz)
2 cups bisquick
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
In skillet brown beef, onion, and garlic.  Drain fat. sprinkle on flour.  stir in pickle liquid, milk, pickles and cheese
mix bisquick and milk.  spoon onto beef mixture. bake 450F for 10 mins
