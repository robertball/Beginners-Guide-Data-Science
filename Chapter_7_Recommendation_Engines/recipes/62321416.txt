Title:
Drunk Peaches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 500 gms (1 1/4 pounds) Peaches, washed and cut into 8 slices.
- 40 gms (1 1/2 ounces) caster sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons grond cinnamon
- 1/2 bottle (plus a splash if you like) red wine, such as Chianti or Dolcetto (the better the wine, the better this dish)

Directions:
Place the peach slices in a bowl.
Mix all the rest of the ingredients thoroughly, then pour over the peaches.
Chill well for 1 hour at least.
Serve.
