Title:
Nut Crust For Light Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stick melted oleo-or butter
1 cup VERY finely chopped nuts
2 cups plain flour

Directions:
mix together oleo(butter) with nuts-
incorporate flour by quarters-
bake on bottom rack at 300 degrees for 10 minutes-
NOTE:- crust will not be cooked thru- rather fluffy and lightly browned

Submitter: 
nanny129
Description: 
Quick and light crust for any no-bake, light cream pie
