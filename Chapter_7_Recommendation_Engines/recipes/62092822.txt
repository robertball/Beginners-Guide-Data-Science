Title:
Balsamic vinegar and white wine Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown rice (dry)
4.5 cups water
1 T chicken bouillon
6 boneless/skinless chicken breasts
1 large red bell pepper
1 large green bell pepper
1 medium red onion
1 T minced garlic
1/2 cup white wine
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
olive oil
salt/peper to taste

Directions:
Cook the rice according to product instruction, addind the bouillon to the water
Slice the bell peppers and onion, set aside
Salt and pepper the chicken breasts
Heat the olive oil in a heavy skillet
Add the garlic and sautee over high heat until garlic becomes aeromatic
Add the chicken breasts to the pan and brown on one side for 3-5 minutes, until golden.
Repeat on other side
Remove chicken and set aside
Add the wine and vinegar, reduce heat to medium
Sautee the peppers and onions in the liquid for 2-3 minutes
Add chicken, covering the chicken with the peppers and onion
cover and reduce heat to low, simmering just until the juices run clear, about 5-7 minutes.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
White Raven
Description: 
A lovely blend of balsamic vinegar and white wine used to enhance and flavor chicken breasts, bell peppers, and red onion that come out moist and yummy!
