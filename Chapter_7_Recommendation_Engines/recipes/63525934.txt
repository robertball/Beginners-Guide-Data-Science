Title:
Lean and Green Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz can tuna, drained
1/2 cup total: diced dill pickle
celery
bell peppers
2 Tbs low fat mayonnaise
2 cups mixed salad and sprouts

Directions:
Drain the tuna. Dice the dill pickle, celery and bell peppers. Mix in a bowl with 2 Tbs low fat mayonnaise.  Serve with salad greens.
