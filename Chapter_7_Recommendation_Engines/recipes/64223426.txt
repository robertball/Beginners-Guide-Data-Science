Title:
Pumpkin Seed Yeast Bread - Breadmaker (Gluten Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbls Melted Butter
1/4 - 1/3 cup Vegetable Oil
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 eggs
1 can Pumpkin Puree
3 1/2 cups Pamela's Gluten Free Bread Mix
1/2 cup Brown Sugar
1 tsp Pumpkin All Spice
1 tsp Cinnamon
1 Yeast Packet
1/2 cup Pumpkin Spiced Pumpkin Seeds (Trader Joe's)

Directions:
Layer ingredients in order into the breadmaker.  Cook on basic loaf.  Add pumpkin seeds to the top of the loaf during the final rise stage.

Submitter: 
Adam Glenn Thomas
Description: 
A gluten free pumpkin bread I devised for the fall holidays.
