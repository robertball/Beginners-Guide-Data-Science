Title:
Pie Crust - Gluten & Sugar Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819005.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tbsp Butter
1 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)
1/4 cup Xylitol (equivalent to 1/4 cup Sugar)
2 cups Almond Flour or Brown Rice Flour
sprinkling of Xylitol & Cinnamon

Directions:
Melt butter
Mix in the rest of the ingredients
Press evenly into a 10" pie pan
Lightly coat crust with a sprinkling of Xylitol & Cinnamon
Bake at 350Â° for 13-15 minutes
Allow to cool
Add filling

Description: 
An alternative pie crust that goes great with cheesecake.
