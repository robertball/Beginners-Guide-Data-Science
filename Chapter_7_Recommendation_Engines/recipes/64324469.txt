Title:
Crockpot Fresh Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped onion
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 small carrots chopped or run in food processor
1/4 cup celery w leaves chopped or run in food processor
4 peeled fresh tomatoes
2 cups vegetable broth
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme (opt)
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 drops hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Remove peels from tomatoes by cutting an x on the bottom and dropping in boiling water for a few seconds until the skins can be easily removed. Add all ingredients to crock pot and cook on low about 8 hours. Blend until smooth. When ready to eat, heat and add soymilk or other non-dairy milk to taste.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Yummy tomato soup without oil or additives that requires little work. Good if you have tomatoes you need to use up in a hurry.
