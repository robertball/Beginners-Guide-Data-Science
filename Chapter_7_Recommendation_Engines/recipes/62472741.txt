Title:
Baked (sweet) rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked white rice
1 cup sugar
1 cup cream
2 beaten eggs
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. cinnamon + extra for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease a large casserole dish.
Whisk together eggs, sugar, cinnamon, cream, vanilla and raisins.
Add eggs custard to cooked rice and mix well.
Pour into casserole dish.
Sprinkle the top with cinnamon.
Bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
This dish is great with ham!  Although it is similar to rice pudding, it is a staple for any holiday meal with my husband's family.
