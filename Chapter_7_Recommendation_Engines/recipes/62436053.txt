Title:
Strawberry Cake with Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/454439.jpg
Ingredients:
oven temp. 350 degrees F
1 package white cake mix
1 package (3 ounces) strawberry-flavored gelatin powder
1 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup water
3/4 cup mashed strawberries
Topping:
1 banana (sliced)

Directions:
Combine cake mix and gelatin powder in a large bowl. Beat in oil, eggs, water, and strawberries. Pour into greased and floured cake pan. before placing cake into oven place the sliced banana ontop in what ever way you want.
