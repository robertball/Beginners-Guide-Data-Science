Title:
Sausage Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pork sausage
1 8 ounce package cream cheese
2 cans crescent rolls

Directions:
Brown sausage and drain; add cream cheese and heat until cheese is melted.  Spread one can of crescent rolls out on a cookie sheet pressing ends together to seal.  Spread sausage mixture over dough.  Place the other can of crescent rolls over sausage mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes

Description: 
This is always enjoyed by all who taste it.  It's also very easy to make.
