Title:
Easy Cheesy Chicken Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chicken broth
2 cups diced potatoes
1 cup diced carrots
1 cup diced celery
1/2 cup diced onion
1-1 1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 cup butter or margerine
2 cups milk
2 cups shredded cheddar
2 cups diced cooked chicken

Directions:
In 4 qt saucepan boil broth, reduce heat add, potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, salt and pepper
cover and simmer 15 minutes or till veggies are tender
*** to can, stop and can at this point. Process quarts for
meanwhile, melt butter in smaller saucepan, add flour and mix well. gradually stir in milk, cook over low heat till slightly thickened. Stir in cheese and cook til melted., add to broth along with chicken.
