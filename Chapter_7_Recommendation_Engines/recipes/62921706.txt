Title:
Raw Marinated Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Large Portobello Mushrooms, Sliced 1/3? thick
1/3 cup Extra virgin olive oil
1/3 cup Nama Shoyu or Braggs Aminos
1 Tbs Raw Honey

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a shallow dish.
Marinate for at least 4 hours.
Serve with some raw cauliflower mash!

Submitter:

Description: 
No need to cook these mushrooms, marinate them instead!
