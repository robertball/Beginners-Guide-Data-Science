Title:
Ham Cheesy Corn Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954739.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Butter or Margarine
4 Teaspoons Flour
1/8 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
3/4 Cup Milk
1 1/2 Cup Shredded Sharp American Cheese
3 oz. Cream Cheese, cubed
3 10 oz. packages frozen whole kernel corn, thawed
3 oz. diced ham

Directions:
Melt butter.  Stir in flour and garlic powder.  Add milk; cook and stir over medium heat.  Heat until thick and bubbly; stir in cheeses.  Cook/stir over low heat until cheeses melt.  Stir in corn and ham.  Bake in 2 quart casserole for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
I've also had success putting this in the slow cooker and taking to potlucks, etc.
