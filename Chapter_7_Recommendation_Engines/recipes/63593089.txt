Title:
Ham and Cream Cheese Roll-ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 slices ham (lunch meat style)
8 oz cream cheese, softened
12 dill pickle spears

Directions:
Place 1 slice ham on paper towel and blot both sides dry. Spread 1/12th of cream cheese over ham. Place 1 pickle spear on short end of one piece of ham and roll up. Place four toothpicks into roll and slice between toothpicks. Repeat.
