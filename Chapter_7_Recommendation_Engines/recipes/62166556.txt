Title:
Smashed Cheesy Red Potato's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/337429.jpg
Ingredients:
3lbs of Small Red Potato's
8 oz of Shredded Extra Sharp Cheese (or your favorite cheese)
Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper (to taste)
Paprika
4oz of Real Bacon Bits (or cook 6 strips and crumble)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Wash off your potato's and pat dry.
Place potato's in a baking dish.
Drizzle Olive Oil over each Potato (enough to coat lightly)
Season with Salt and Pepper approximately 1/2 teaspoon of each.
Bake in oven approximately 25-30 minutes, use a fork to check they're fully cooked.
Remove from oven and smash each potato with a potato masher.
Spread cheese evenly over potato's.
Sprinkle a little Paprika for color.
Return to oven for approximately 5 minutes or enough for the cheese to melt.
Remove from oven top potato's/cheese with Bacon Bits and serve.
