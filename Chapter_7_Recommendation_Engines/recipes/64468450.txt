Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2453887.jpg
Ingredients:
2. C graham cracker crumbs
2 T. sugar
1 (8 oz) pkg of coconut
1 (15oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1 large package of chocolate chips
1 package of marshmellows
1 large kale leaf

Directions:
Mix crumbs, sugar and melted butter. Pat into 9 x 13 inch pan. Lick the bowl you used with sugar and butter. It is delicious. Combined coconut and milk; pour over the graham cracker crust. Bake for 15 minutes or until the edge gets brown. Melt chocolate chips and put on top. Add marshmallows and then put it under the broiler for them to brown. Once they are 'tanned' let cool for a bit. Cut into little bites. Serve on kale leaf.
