Title:
Oriental Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. onion powder
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 bell pepper, cut in long strips
1 small onion, chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 8oz. can water chestnuts
1 jalapeno, finely chopped
salt and pepper

Directions:
Cut the chicken breasts into 1/2 inch strips.
Mix the onion powder, garlic powder and some salt and pepper.
Dredge the chicken strips in the mixture.
Combine the soy sauce and brown sugar in a bowl or plastic bag and place the chicken strips inside.
Let it marinate for 30-45 minutes.
Saute the bell pepper, onion, jalapeno water chestnuts and garlic in a pan, coated with non stick cooking spray, until soft. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to them.
Remove from heat and set aside.
Once the chicken has marinated, saute the chicken with the remaining sauce until no longer pink in the middle.
Add the cooked veggies and simmer for 5-8 minutes.

Submitter: 
Misty Darnell
Description: 
This is a very yummy asian style dish and it is super easy to make! Its great with rice because it soaks up all the delicious sauce! You can't beat an easy, delicious and healthy meal!
