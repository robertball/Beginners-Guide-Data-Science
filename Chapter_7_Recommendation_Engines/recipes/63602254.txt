Title:
Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 c. minced onion
1 clove garlic, minced
salt and pepper
1 package fresh mushrooms
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 c. sour cream
1/2 package egg noodles

Directions:
Brown ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat. Add salt and pepper and flour. Cook 5 min, add mushrooms, then cook 5 more minutes. Add soup. reduce heat and simmer about 15 minutes. Prepare egg noodles (or rice if preferred) according to package directions. Just before serving, mix in sour cream and heat through. Serve over noodles or rice.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This was always a favorite dinner of mine in childhood. Secretly I think it was one of my mom's too, since it's so easy to make!
