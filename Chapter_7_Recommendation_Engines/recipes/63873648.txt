Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting for cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3oz cream cheese softened
1 Tablespoon butter softened
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups confectioners sugar

Directions:
In small mixing bowl combine the cream cheese, butter and vanilla and beat at low speed with an electric mixer till light. Gradually add sugar beating till fluffy.
(Optional: Add milk one tablespoon at a time to make it spreading consistency)

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
I have used this for many years to frost my cut out cookies!
