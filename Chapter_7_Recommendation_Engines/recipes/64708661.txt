Title:
Instant Pot® Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T avocado oil
1 cup finely diced onion
1 cup finely diced red pepper
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 1/2 t micro planed or finely diced garlic
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 T worcestershire sauce
3 T dark brown sugar
1 1/2 cups strained and crushed tomatoes, such as Aurora®
1 t coarse sea salt
1/2 t freshly ground pepper
1 t chili powder
large pinch red pepper flakes
1/4 cup water
1 T tomato paste, optional
8 good quality hamburger buns, toasted and buttered

Directions:
Press the saute button to heat the pot, high
Add the oil and tip the pot so the oil covers the entire bottom of the pot
Add the onions, pepper and ground beef, season with salt and pepper
Stir frequently until veggies are soft and the beef is no longer pink.
Drain and return to the pot.
Add the garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
Add the apple cider vinegar
Stir in mustard, brown sugar, worcestershire sauce, chilli, red pepper flakes and water, stir well.
Add the crushed tomatoes and stir.
Lock the lid into place, close the vent and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes.
Allow the pressure to release naturally for 10 minutes then manually release pressure, if necessary
Serve on the toasted buns.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
A combination of lean ground beef and apple cider vinegar make this slightly sweet and slightly tart. Enjoy!
