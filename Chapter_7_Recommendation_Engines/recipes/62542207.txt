Title:
Spice Island's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/524540.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups apples, peeled and sliced
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon Spice Islands Lemon Peel
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands Cinnamon, Ground Saigon
Topping:
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands Cinnamon, Ground Saigon
1/4 teaspoon Spice Islands Nutmeg, Ground
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Mix apples, lemon juice, lemon peel, brown sugar and cinnamon together.  Spoon into 8 x 8-inch greased, shallow baking dish.  Set aside.
Mix butter, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg in medium bowl until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Stir in walnuts.  Sprinkle evenly over apples.
Bake at 375Â°F 35 to 40 minutes.  Serve warm
