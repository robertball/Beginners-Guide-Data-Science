Title:
Thick Fruit Whip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985007.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups sliced unsweetened strawberries
1 medium firm banana
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 cups ice cubes

Directions:
In a food processor or blender, combine all of the ingredients. Cover and process until smooth. Pour into glasses; serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Nancy  Zimmerman
Description: 
This is a refreshing drink that combines the great tastes of bananas and strawberries. I've experimented with other fruit combinations, so this recipe can be adapted.
