Title:
5-Minute Teriyaki Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 green pepper
1 can pineapple rings
4 Tbsp barbecue sauce
4 medium squares aluminum foil

Directions:
Gut green pepper.  Slice green pepper into 4 rings.  Place chicken breast on 1 square aluminum foil.  Top with 1 green pepper ring and 1 pineapple ring.  Drizzle 1 Tbsp barbecue sauce over all.  Fold aluminum foil around chicken breast.  Bake at 375 degrees for 40-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Michel Brewer
Description: 
I have about 10 seconds from the time I walk in the door until I hear, What's for dinner?  This is by far the easiest chicken dish I have ever made. My kids like to prepare their own making smiley faces out of all the ingredients!
