Title:
Tropical Pineapple Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
½ cup dark coconut rum
½ cup bbq sauce
2 tbsp Caribbean hot sauce (such as Pickapeppa sauce)
1 can pineapple rings
1 small red onion
½ red bell pepper
Juice of two limes

Directions:
Combine rum, bbq sauce, hot sauce and lime juice
Add chicken to marinade
Put in refrigerator for 1-2 hours
Grill chicken on high for approx. ten minutes each side   Put remaining marinade in sauce pan/bring to a boil
Chop up onion, red bell pepper and one pineapple ring
mix together
Grill remaining pineapple with chicken
Place grilled pineapple on each piece of chicken
Top with relish
