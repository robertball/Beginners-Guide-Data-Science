Title:
Hot Pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 1/2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup hot peppers, shredded
1 package certo (both envelopes)
3/4 cup bell peppers, chopped

Directions:
Chop/shred all peppers.
Add sugar and vinegar to a large pot with peppers.
Boil for approximately 10 minutes.
Remove from heat and add Certo.
Add appropriate food coloring if desired.
Ladel into sterile canning jelly jars.
Turn upside down to cool and seal.

Submitter: 
BOILERMISH
Description: 
Mom used to make this to serve with havarti cheese or cream cheese and crackers.  Can adjust the hotness by adjusting the types of peppers used.  We always use food coloring to denote hot (red) vs mild (green).
