Title:
Summer Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package shaped pasta
1 pint cherry or grape tomatoes halved
1 pound boccacini mozzarella halved
1/3 cup chopped fresh basil torn
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot with boiling salted water cook pasta until al dente. Drain.
Meanwhile, in large bowl combine the halved tomatoes, halved mozzarella balls, fresh basil, garlic, olive oil,  salt, and ground black pepper.
Toss pasta with tomato mixture. Serve at room temperature. Refridgerate any leftovers.

Description: 
This is a great salad for Cookouts and Picnics. So easy to pack and carry. You don't have to worry about the sun because there is no mayo.Modified from: Tony's Summer Pasta
