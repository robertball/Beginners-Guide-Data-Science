Title:
Fluffy Oven Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 cup almond milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup brown rice flour
1/4 cup tapioca flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
1. Preheat over to 400 degrees.
2. Melt butter and pour evenly into 9x13 casserole dish
3. In a large bowl, beat eggs, milk, and vanilla.
4. Add rice flour, tapioca flour, and salt. Mix with hand mixer until well combined.
5. Pour batter over melted butter.  Bake for 22 to 24 minutes.  Serve with powdered sugar and syrup.
