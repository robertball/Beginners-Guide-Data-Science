Title:
Salmon eggs avocado and cheese  lunch bowl   SCD legal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Avocado, cut into one inch chunks
2 ounces cheddar cheese (or havarti or gouda), cut into 1/2 inch cubes
2 eggs, hard boiled,peeled and chunked
1  6 ounce can boneless skinless salmon packed in water,  drained and flaked
1 Tablespoon bottled lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt, or to taste
Dash of pepper (I used finely ground)

Directions:
Combine  avocado, egg, salmon and cheese  in a large bowl or 1 pint container. Drizzle with lemon juice. Sprinkle on garlic salt and pepper.  Toss to combine.

Submitter: 
DEBBITZEN
Description: 
Salmon,eggs,avocado and cheese come together quickly and easily for a delicious meal that's easy to pack and take along . SCD legal.
