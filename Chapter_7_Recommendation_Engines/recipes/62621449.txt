Title:
Tasty Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
2tsp Sesame Oil
½ tsp Salt
2tbsp Vegetable or Sunflower Oil
400g long grain Rice (Basmati is good) - cooked and let go cold
100g frozen Peas - defrosted
225gr Onion - chopped
100g Sweetcorn - canned or frozen - defrosted
½tsp freshly ground Black Pepper
175gr Cucumber - peeled, seeded and cut into cubes
Optional: 1tbsp Soy Sauce and/or 1 tsp Chilli Oil

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat eggs, sesame oil and salt together and set aside;
Heat a wok until very hot, add a little oil, heat until smoking and add onions and pepper and stir fry for 2 minutes
Add rest of oil, egg mixture and stir fry for 1 minute;
Add the rice and stir fry for 3 - 4 minutes until heated through;
Add peas, sweet corn and cucumber and stir fry continuously for 5 minutes;
Add Soy sauce and/or Chilli oil if using;
Stir well and serve

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A simple but tasty, full of flavour chinese dish, fit for all occasions.
Will convert into a full meal if you add extras like chopped ham, cooked shrimp or prawns, bean sprouts or more vegetables.
Served cold, it becomes a delicious rice salad
