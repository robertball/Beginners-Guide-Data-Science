Title:
Deluxe Green Chile Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 12 oz jar green chile/verde salsa
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3-4 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup white mexican cheese, shredded
Sauce:
1  12 oz jar green chile/verde salsa
1 pint sour cream
1/2 cup white mexican cheese, shredded
Enchiladas:
Filling
Sauce
24 corn tortillas
oil for frying
2 cups white mexican cheese, shredded

Directions:
Make filling - In a slowcooker, add chicken breasts, garlic and salsa. Let cook until breasts are cooked through. Remove chicken from cooker, shred, and return to sauce. Stir together.
Remove chicken from crock pot and let cool until you can handle it.
To make sauce, warm 2nd jar of salsa and remove from heat. Stir in sour cream.
To build enchiladas:
Heat oil until it sizzles when you put a pinch of flour in
Add 1 corn tortilla, and fry, 30 seconds per side. Remove tortilla to a paper towel and pat away grease. Add a scoop of filling and about a tablespoon of white cheese. Roll and place seam side down in a 9x13 pan.
Repeat until filling is gone.
Cover with prepared sauce, top with more white cheese.
Bake at 350 for 1 hr
