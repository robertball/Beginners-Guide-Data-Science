Title:
Cinnamon chocolate cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup butter
1/2 Cup sugar
1/2 Cup brown sugar
1/3 Cups cocoa powder
1/3 Cup melted semisweet chocolate
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
3 3/4 Cups flour
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
3/4 cups white chocolate
1/2 semisweet chocolate

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Beat together the butter, sugars, melted chocolate and cocoa powder stir in eggs and vanilla
Sift together the flour, baking soda and cinnamon
mix together the dry ingredients with the chocolate mixture. fold in chocolates. Drop into heaping spoonfuls (i use a mini ice-cream scoop) and bake for 8-12 min
