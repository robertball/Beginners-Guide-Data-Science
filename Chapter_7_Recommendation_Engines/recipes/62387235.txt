Title:
Honey Orange Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound baby carrots
1/4 cup orange juice
3 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons butter
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Place carrots in a shallow saucepan, and cover with orange juice. Boil until fork tender (I leave them a little crunchy).   Stir in honey , butter, and salt. Simmer for about 3 minutes.
