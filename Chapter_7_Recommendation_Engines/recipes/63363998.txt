Title:
Catalan Style Baked Fish with Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
60ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1kg Rosada /Turbot/Halibut/Tilapia/Cod steaks (or other firm flesh white fish) - 2.5cm thick
600g potatoes - cut into 1/8" slices
1 clove Garlic - finely chopped
1 medium onion - finely sliced
150g fresh ripe Tomatoes - sliced in very thin wedges
2tsp fresh Thyme - leaves only (or ½tsp dried thyme)
1½ tsp fresh Rosemary Leaves - finely chopped (or ¼tsp dried rosemary)
2tsp fresh Oregano Leaves - finely chopped (or ½tsp dried Oregano)
2tbsp fresh Parsley - finely chopped
1 lemon - Juice only - to taste
150ml dry White Wine
1 or 2tbsp Dried Bread Crumbs
Sea Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper - to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 180c/350f/gas 4
Arrange the potatoes and onion in layers in a greased baking pan, sprinkling each layer with oil (a total of 3 tablespoons),  garlic, salt, and pepper;
Scatter the tomato wedges over the potatoes, cover tightly with foil, and bake for some 45 minutes to an hour or so, or until the potatoes are almost tender;
Meanwhile, sprinkle the fish steaks on both sides with a little salt and let sit;
Place the fish over the potatoes, brush the fish with about 1 tablespoon oil, sprinkle thyme, rosemary, oregano, parsley, and lemon juice over the fish and potatoes;
Pour in the wine;
Dust the fish with bread crumbs, return to the oven, and cook until the fish is just done, about 15 minutes, basting several times.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A delightful dish in the  North West Spanish style, from around Barcelona. Packed with flavour, the herbs provide a wonderful foil to the fish and potatoes.
