Title:
Jazzy Chicken Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pcs Chicken (leg/thigh with bones)
2 T Olive Oil
4 T Butter
1 onion, chopped
1 can sliced mushrooms
1 can viena sausage, sliced
2 Cups Chicken Broth
2 carrots, sliced
1 potato, cubed
3/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1 small can sliced olives
2 refrigerated pie crusts (defrosted)
1 egg yolk
1 link of liguica, sliced
1 tablespoon flour
3 tablespoon Water

Directions:
1. In a large heavy skillet, brown the chicken, drain. Remove from pan and let rest. The chicken does not have to be fully cooked, just nice and lightly golden.
2. Add butter into the pan and saute the onions until soft and fragrant. Add the mushrooms, vienna sausage, linguica, potatoes and chicken.
3. Pour in broth and simmer for 30 minutes.Add carrots and grated cheese. Mix flour and water in a separate bowl then add into the pot to thicken the sauce.
4. Pour into a 9x13 casserole pan. Cover the pan with the pie crusts. Slit the pie crust to enable steam to escape. Brush top of crust with the egg yolk.
5. Bake for 20 minutes until the crust is golden.
