Title:
Tomato Bon Bons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 grape tomatoes, cored
1 ounce Asiago cheese, cut in 24 small cubes
3 eggs
1 pound ground sausage
1 cup flour
Breading, recipe follows
4 cups vegetable oil
1 cup fresh salsa
1 cup ranch dressing
1/2 cups seedless cucumber, minced
Breading:
1 cup shredded Asiago cheese
1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1 tsp. kosher salt
1 tsp. granulated garlic
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 tsp. black pepper
dash cayenne pepper
Mix all the breading ingredients.

Directions:
Stuff each tomato with cube of cheese.  Beat 1 egg in med. bowl and mix in sausage meat.  Make small pancake in palm of your hand with sausage meat and put stuffed tomato in the middle.  Now wrap sausage meat around and create a meatball covering entire tomato.
Beat remaining 2 eggs in small bowl.  Put flour and breading in separate bowls.  Place bowls in order of flour, egg and breading mixture.  Place bon bon in flour first, followed by egg wash and then roll in breading mixture.  Set all aside.  Heat the oil to 350 degrees and fry in batches of 6 for about minutes.  Drain on paper towel.  Process the salsa with ranch dressing and then stir in the cucumber.  Serve with bon bons.
