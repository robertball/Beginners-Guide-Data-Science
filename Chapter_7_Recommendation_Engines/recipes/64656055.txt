Title:
Chinese Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. ketchup
1/4 c. hoisin sauce
2 T.  rice vinegar
2 T.   soy sauce
1 T.  curry powder
1 T.  sesame oil
1/4 t.  Tabasco Sauce

Directions:
Simmer 5 minutes to release the oils.  Let set overnight in frig for best flavor

Submitter: 
Slowturtle
Description: 
This is a good marinade if you like spicy foods.
