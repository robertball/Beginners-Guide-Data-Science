Title:
Campbell's® Turkey and Broccoli Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977590.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (16 ounce) package linguine*
1 cup fresh or frozen broccoli flowerets
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups cubed cooked turkey

Directions:
Cook the linguine according to the package directions. Add the broccoli for the last 4 minutes of the cooking time. Drain the linguine and broccoli well in a colander.
Stir the soup, milk, cheese and black pepper in a 12-inch skillet over medium heat.  Add the turkey and linguine mixture and cook until it's hot and bubbling. Serve with additional Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Turkey, broccoli and linguine team up with a creamy sauce made with Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup and Parmesan cheese, for a family-pleasing dinner.
