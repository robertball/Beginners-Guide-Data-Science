Title:
Great Scot Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - 28 oz cans Tomato Sauce
2 - 28 oz cans Diced Tomatoes
1 - 28 oz can Tomato Puree
1 - 28 oz can Whole Tomatoes
1 - 14.5 oz can Tomato Paste
1/4 cup Chopped fresh basil
Spaghetti Seasoning (or oregano/bay leave/thyme)
Garlic - to taste
Salt & Pepper to taste
30-40 thin slices pepperoni (optional - usually leave out)
3lb Ground Beef, scrambled, browned (or use mix with ground pork)
3 stalks finely chopped celery
3-4 large carrots, chopped
1 large onion, chopped
1 large green pepper, chopped
3 white mushrooms, chopped
1 jar parm cheese

Directions:
Brown Ground Beef.
While Beef is browning, add all tomatoes to large pot, cutting up whole tomatoes into smaller pieces.
Chop and add vegetables to tomato mixture.
Add ground beef and pepperoni.
Cook on med-high until bubbly, then turn down to low for 2-3 hours, or until vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally.
Add Parm cheese during last 20-30 minutes, being sure to stir semi-often after adding this
