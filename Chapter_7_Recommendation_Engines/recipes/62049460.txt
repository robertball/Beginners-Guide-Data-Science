Title:
Strawhats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
corn chips or crumbled tortilla chips
mozzarella cheese, shredded
14 oz. chili beans, drained
1 tomato, diced
lettuce, shredded

Directions:
Crumble ground beef in a skillet and brown.  Drain well.  Place corn chips on two plates and top with ground beef.  Add cheese.  Heat beans and place on top of cheese.  Add lettuce and tomato.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
A quick and easy all in one Mexican dish that kids and grownups will love. Any kind of cheese will work.  To kick it up a little, add some jalapeno peppers or pour a little salsa on top!
