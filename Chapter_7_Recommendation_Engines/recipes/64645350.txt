Title:
Garlic Ginger Miso Black Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 inch ginger, divided
5-8 garlic cloves, divided
1/2 tsp sesame oil
2 T coconut aminos
1 T miso
2-3 T rice wine vinegar
2-4 T avocado oil
1 T ghee
1 lb black cod

Directions:
preheat oven 400-425
combine garlic and ginger (reserve some for baking), sesame oil, aminos, miso, rice wine vinegar, avocado oil in blender and blend until smooth
pour over fish, let marinate
smash whole garlic cloves, add garlic and slices of ginger to fish
dab ghee on top
bake 425 for 10-15 min, broil last few minutes to desired caramelization and doneness
