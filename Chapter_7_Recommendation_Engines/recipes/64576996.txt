Title:
Marshmallow Krispie Brownie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
batter for 1 batch 9" x 13" brownies
4 cups mini marshmallows
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1 tbsp. butter or margarine
1 1/2 cups rice krispies

Directions:
Bake brownies, removing from oven about 3 minutes early.
Sprinkle marshmallows over hot brownies.
Return to oven for 3 minutes longer.
Melt choc. chips, peanut butter and butter together.
Mix in rice krispies.
Spread this mixture over marshmallow layer.
Refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These brownie based bars are smore-like, without the graham crackers.
