Title:
Eggo Breakfast Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1960288.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Eggo home style waffles
1 slice of cheese
1 egg
1 slice of ham

Directions:
Cook the egg into a circle-like shape; flat enough to fit between the waffles. While waiting, place the waffles in the toaster. Next, put on the cheese and ham. Place the egg between once finished cooking.
