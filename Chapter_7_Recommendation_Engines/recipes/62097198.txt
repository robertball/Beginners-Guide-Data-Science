Title:
Sweet Potato Dessert Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 40oz can Sweet Potatoes with liquid
1/2 cup White Sugar
1/2 cup light Brown Sugar
¼ tsp. Cinnamon
1 15 oz can Crushed Pineapple
½ cup Raisins
½ tsp. Rum flavoring or extract
½ Cup chopped Pecans
Marshmallows to cover dish

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with liquids into a 9x12 baking dish
Bake for 30-40 minutes
Top with layer of Marshmallows
Put back into oven and brown

Submitter: 
AVALONFAAYRE
Description: 
A different twist on Sweet Potatoes! Really good, and almost a dessert!
