Title:
Agave mustard daikon radish salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound daikon radish, peeled and julienned
1 tsp. fresh grated ginger
1 garlic glove finely diced
2-3 tsp. whole grain mustard
2 tsp. agave nectar
Juice of 1/2 lemon

Directions:
Julienne daikon radish and place in serving bowl.  Combine remaining ingredients and pour over radish. Lightly toss and let marinade 1 hour in refrigerator.  Serve chilled as a BBQ side dish or salad.

Submitter: 
Too Hot Tamale
Description: 
A fresh, light and healthy side dish that would be perfect to take to any BBQ or to go with grilled meat.  The whole grain mustard, lemon juice and agave give it a lively taste!
