Title:
Crabby Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/330326.jpg
Ingredients:
1-8oz. block cream cheese
1-6oz. can crabmeat, drained well
1-12oz. jar chili sauce

Directions:
Unwrap cream cheese and place on a plate.
Top with crabmeat, mounding up and pressing to adhere to the top of the cream cheese.
Pour chili sauce over all to cover completely.
Cover and refrigerate until well chilled.
Serve with crackers or toasted bread rounds.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This is one of the easiest recipes you will ever make. I remember my mom making this for get togethers when we were younger and I make it for all of our get togethers now. With only three ingredients this recipe is a definate winner.
