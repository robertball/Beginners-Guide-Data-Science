Title:
Kickin' Collard Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon bacon grease
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1tablespoon apple cider vinegar
3 slices bacon
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
3 cups chicken broth
1 pinch red pepper flakes
1 pound fresh collard greens, cut into 2-inch pieces

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add bacon, and cook until crisp. Remove bacon from pan, crumble and return to the pan.  Add onion, and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.  Add garlic, and cook until just fragrant. Add collard greens, and fry until they start to wilt.
Pour in chicken broth, and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes, or until greens are tender.

Submitter:

Description: 
If you like greens you will love this recipe.  The bacon and onions give them a wonderful flavor.  Add more red pepper for a little more spice.Modified from: Kickin' Collard Greens
