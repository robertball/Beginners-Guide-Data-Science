Title:
Deep Dish Pea and Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. frozen peas
1 cup of chopped cauliflower
1 cup of sliced carrot
1/2 cup of chopped green bell pepper
1 cup green beans
1 chopped onion
1 Tbs. chopped Garlic
2 cups of shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 Tbs. Flour
2 Tbs. Butter
1 tsp. sugar
1 Cup Milk
1 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Steam Cauliflower, carrots until just tender drain and set aside.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large skillet cook onion and green peppers over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth, and cook for one minute. Stir in the salt,pepepr, sugar, and milk. Cook until sauce is thickened Add Peas and other vegetables Add 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese and then stir to coat. Transfer the mixture to a 2 1/2 quart casserole dish. Spread remaining shredded cheese over the top.
Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven or until the top is golden and cheese is bubbly.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
A creamy deep dish casserole featuring every ones favorite PEAS!  Don't be afraid of these little green morsels of goodness... And consider this one for a change up this Thanksgiving instead of the same old same old green bean and soggy deep fried onion casserole.
