Title:
Glazed Carrots (Karen's recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sliced peeled carrots
4 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons firmly packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon crushed dried parsley

Directions:
In a saucepan, boil carrots in lightly salted water about 8 to 10 minutes or until tender.
Drain well and place in a serving dish.
Melt butter in a small saucepan, then add honey and brown sugar.
Stir in parsley if desired.
Heat thoroughly, stirring until blended.
Pour over carrots.
Serve hot.
