Title:
Salmon with Dill and Caper Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
4 - 6 oz. skinless salmon fillets
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp. chopped fresh dill
1 tbsp. chopped capers

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Sprinkle salmon with half of the salt and pepper.
Place salmon in  skillet and cook, turning once, for about 10 minutes or until it is no longer opaque.
In a small bowl, whisk together yogurt, lemon juice, dill, capers and remaining salt and pepper.
Top salmon with a dollop of sauce, sprinkle with some dill and serve.
