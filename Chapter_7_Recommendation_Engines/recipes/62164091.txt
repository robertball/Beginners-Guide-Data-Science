Title:
Kristin’s Shrimp Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	4 cloves garlic, chopped
•	1 large shallot, chopped
•	2 TBS olive oil
•	2 TBS butter
•	1 lb shrimp
•	½ lemon, divided
•	2 tomatoes chopped
•	1 small bunch fresh basil, chopped
•	1 cup+ half and half
•	3 ounces cream cheese (1/3 less fat kind ok)
•	3 ounces grated parmesan cheese
•	4 slices bacon, chopped and fried
•	1-9 ounce package of fresh fettuccine, cooked
•	¼ cup toasted pine nuts

Directions:
Cook fettuccine to package directions.
Melt the butter in the olive oil in a large saucepan.
Add the shallots and garlic and sauté until soft.
Stir in the prawns and 1/4 juice of lemon and sauté till prawns are pink.
Remove prawns leaving as much oil, butter, garlic and shallots behind as possible.
Add tomato, basil, and 1/4 juice of lemon and sauté until soft.
Add half and half, cream cheese Parmesan and bacon. Heat until cheese is melted.
Add prawns and fettuccine and heat through.
Sprinkle with toasted pine nuts and serve.

Submitter: 
3BoysMomma
Description: 
This is a creamy shrimp fettuccine which uses fresh ingredients from the garden or grocery store. An easy, fancy meal! Get out the garlic bread and enjoy!
