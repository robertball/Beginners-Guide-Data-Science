Title:
Zucchini Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/93795.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 (1 ounce) package Ranch-style dressing mix
3 cups shredded zucchini
1 cup shredded cucumber
1/2 green bell pepper, coarsely shredded

Directions:
Stir mayonnaise and Ranch dressing mix together in a large bowl.  Stir in the zucchini, cucumber, and bell pepper.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Vickie Spencer
Description: 
This is a refreshing summer salad and a tasty way to use those garden cucumbers and zucchini.
