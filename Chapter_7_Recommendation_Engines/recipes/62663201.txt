Title:
Chocolate Chip Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1 1/4 cups quick cooking oats or old-fashioned
1 cup splenda
2 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
2 eggs
2 cups buttermilk
4 1/2 cups bran flakes cereal
1 cup mini semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the flour, oats, splenda, baking soda and salt; set aside. In a large mixing bowl, beat the peanut butter and applesauce until combined. Beat in the eggs and buttermilk, Stir into the dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in the cereal and chocolate chips.
Fill greased muffin cups two-thirds full. Bake at 400 degrees F for 14-17 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks. Serve warm.
