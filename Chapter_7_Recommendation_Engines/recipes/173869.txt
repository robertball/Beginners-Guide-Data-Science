Title:
Peanut Butter, Raisin and Honey on Whole Wheat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices soft whole wheat bread
2 tablespoons peanut butter
3 tablespoons raisins
1 tablespoon honey from a honey bear

Directions:
Spread the peanut butter onto one slice of bread. Press in the raisins. Drizzle the other slice of bread with honey from the honey bear and close together with the raisin/peanut butter side.

Submitter: 
Grain Foods Foundation
Description: 
Have fun with your kids, or play a kid yourself, making and eating these ooey-gooey, peanut butter and honey sandwiches.
