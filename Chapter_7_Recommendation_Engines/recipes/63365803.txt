Title:
Black Forest Cake Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/897234.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup hot chocolate powder
1 oz cherry brandy(use maraschino cherry juice for a non-alcoholic version)
1 cup ice
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Put the all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
Serve garnished with maraschino cherries if desired or with some whipped cream and grated chocolate.
