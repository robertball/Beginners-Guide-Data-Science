Title:
BACARDI®-Gras Smash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985741.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Pina Colada mix
1/4 cup BACARDI® Rum
1/4 cup BACARDI® Gold Rum
6 fluid ounces Minute Maid® Orange Juice
4 ounces crushed pineapple
1 banana
5 cups ice
Oranges for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, Minute Maid Orange Juice, pineapple, banana, BACARDI(R) Rum and BACARDI(R) Gold.
Add ice and blend for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with orange slices.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Take a trip to the Big Easy with this fancy tropical blend of Pina Colada mix, rum, orange juice and banana in a refreshing icy blended cocktail.
