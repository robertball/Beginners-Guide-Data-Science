Title:
Mary's Cheesy Corn Fiesta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-4 oz can diced green chilies
1-20 oz bag frozen corn
1- small jar Cheez Whiz
1- 8 oz container sour cream and chive dip

Directions:
Spray crockpot with non-stick cooking spray or use a crockpot liner for easy clean up.  Combine all ingredients in crockpot and cook on low for 3-4 hour, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
You can't go wrong with spicy, cheesy corn!  This recipe originally came from a co-worker a few years ago.  It has been a hit in my family ever since!  It's always requested for every family gathering.
