Title:
Loaded Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs red potatoes, cut
1/2 stick butter
3-4 cups 2% milk (amount depends on how thick/thin you want the soup)
Garlic powder, 2-3 tablespoons to taste
Salt, to taste
Black Pepper, to taste
Toppings (optional)
Real Bacon Bits
Sharp Cheddar, shredded
Sour cream
Green Onions, diced

Directions:
Cut potatoes into 1-2 inch chunks, skin on, and add to a medium sized pot of warm (not boiling) water.
Turn stove on medium-high heat. Bring potatoes to a boil and cook 5-10 minutes until potatoes fall apart when tested with a fork.
Drain water from pot.
Add butter, 3 cups milk, 2 tablespoons garlic powder. Stir until potatoes are smooth.
Add more milk for a thinner soup.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Stir in toppings or allow people to add their own toppings.

Submitter: 
Amanda W
Description: 
This recipe is for thick and hearty loaded baked potato soup. Toppings can be added or left for diners to add individually to accommodate differing preferences.
