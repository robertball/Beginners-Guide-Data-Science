Title:
Sausage Reuben Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen BOCA Meatless Smoked Sausage
1/4 cup sauerkraut, drained
1 slice Swiss cheese
1 slice rye bread, toasted

Directions:
COOK sausage as directed on package.
CUT sausage in half lengthwise and brown in skillet on medium heat. Add sauerkraut and cheese; cover. Heat 2 minutes or until cheese melts.
SERVE sausage on toast slice with mustard or reduced fat thousand island salad dressing.
