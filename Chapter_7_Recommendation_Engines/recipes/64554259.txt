Title:
Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3348887.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Lbs Grass Fed Chuck Roast
1 Bundle Organic Carrots
2 Cups Chopped Celery
2 lbs New Potatoes
1 Large Onion Cut In Half and Quartered
4-6 Sprigs Rosemary
2-3 Sprigs Thyme
6-8 Cloves of Garlic
1 Quart Beef Broth (Low Sodium)
2 Beef Bullion Cubes
2 Tbsp Corn Starch
1 Cup Red Wine
Pepper
Cheese Cloth

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 275
Heat a skillet on very high for 5+ minutes.
Using a paring knife, create evenly spaced slits into meat and insert one clove of garlic into each slit, about 1/2 way into meat.
Brown the roast in the skillet on all sides to develop a nice caramelized outer layer.
Cut carrots into 2 inch lengths
Cut celery into 1/4 inch lengths
Place carrots and celery into bottom of dutch oven pan
Place rosemary and time into a cheesecloth bag and tie closed.  Place in middle of pan.
Place roast on top of carrots, celery, and herb bag.
Liberally grind fresh pepper over top of roast
Place onion around edges of roast
Mix corn starch with 1 cup of broth
Pour wine and all broth and corn starch into pan
Add Beef Bullion Cubes to broth next to roast
Place covered with tight lid in oven for 2 hours
Remove from over, and add potatoes
Return to oven for 1.5 to 2 hours
Let sit on counter for 10-15 minutes before serving
