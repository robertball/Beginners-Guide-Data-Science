Title:
Hula's Sweet Potato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Sweet Potato
1/2 cup Butter softened
1 cup Sugar
1/2 cup Milk
2 Eggs
1/2 tsp Ground Nutmeg
1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
1/4 tsp Ginger
1/4 tsp Cloves
1 Tblspn Lemon Juice
1 Tblspn Flour
1 tsp Vanilla
9" Pie Crust - unbaked

Directions:
Boil sweet potato whole in skin for 40-50 minutes, or until soft.
Preheat oven to 350
Run under cold water and remove skin.
Break apart potato in bowl.  Add Butter and mix well.
Stir in sugar, milk, eggs, spices, lemon juice, flour and vanilla.  Beat until smooth.
Pour into unbaked pie crust.
Bake 50-60 minutes until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
Pie will puff up like a baddass souffle but will shrink down disappointingly as it cools.
