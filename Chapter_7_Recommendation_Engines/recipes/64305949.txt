Title:
Dr. Fuhrman's Vegetable Pea/Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups carrot juice
2 cups celery
4 cups water
4 cups kale, chopped
8 carrots, chopped
6 onions, finely chopped
8 tomatoes, finely chopped, retain all juice
1/2 cup dried spit peas
1/2 cup mixed dried soup beans
5 tbsp. Powdered Vegetable Soup Mix or VegeBase
1 tbsp. Mrs. Dash Table Blend
2 bay leaves
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried oregano

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and simmer on low heat for 90 minutes.
