Title:
Pita Crisps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, room temperature
2 cloves garlic
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, freshly grated
2 green onion, finely chopped
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice, freshly squeezed
salt, to taste
16 ounces pita pocket bread, thicker the better
1 red pepper, seeded, deveined and sliced

Directions:
1.	In a small bowl combine butter, garlic, parmesan cheese, green onions, lemon juice, salt and lemon pepper to taste.  Split pita bread in half around seams (looks like a tortilla).
2.	Spread mixture on inside surface of pita halves and slice pita into strips or triangles.
3.	Place on baking sheet and toast in a 400 degree oven until surface turns crispy and brown.  Watch carefully.  Remove crisps from oven as they begin to brown and allow to cool and harden.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
Good to eat as an appetizer or with soup and salad!
