Title:
Chocolate Carrot Banana Eggless Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - Bananas
1cup - Carrots, chopped
1 3/4 cup - all purpose flour
3/4 cup - White sugar
1/2 tsp - Baking powder
1 tsp - Baking soda
3/4 cup - Cocoa Powder
1/2 cup - Butter
1 cup - Milk
1tsp - Vanilla Extract
a pinch - salt
optional:
1/2 cup - chopped walnuts
1/4 cup - semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 tsp - cinnamon powder
1/4 tsp - nutmeg powder

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 350 degree F or 175 degree C.
Grease and Preapare the cake pan.
Puree the chopped carrots and bananas in a blender.
Whisk together flour, salt, Baking powder, baking soda and cocoa powder.
Beat butter and sugar with a electric mixer.
Stir in the pureed carrots and banana mixture.
Add Vanilla extract and milk.
Stir in chopped walnuts, chocolate chips, cinnamon powder and flour mixture until just incorporated.
Pour the batter into the prepared pan. tap on the counter to release any air pockets.
Bake in a preheated oven for 35 - 40 minutes, or until an inserted wooden skewer comes clean.
Let the cake rest in the pan for 15 - 20 minutes.
Remove form pan, slice and serve.
