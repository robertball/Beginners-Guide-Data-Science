Title:
The Classic Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986869.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Margarita Mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
5 cups ice
Limes for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Margarita mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with limes.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
A blender makes light work of this famous cocktail, especially if you are entertaining a crowd.
