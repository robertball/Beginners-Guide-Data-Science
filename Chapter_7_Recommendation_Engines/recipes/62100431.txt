Title:
Mary Kay's Marvelous Chocolate Square Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. sifted flour
1/2 t. soda
1/2 t. salt
1/2 C. sugar
1/2 C. firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 C. soft shortening
1 egg
1/2 t. vanilla
1 1/2 C. quick or old fashioned oatmeal
1/4 C. chopped pecans
1 pkg of Hershey Milk Chocolate Squares
Pecans halves, about 3 doz.

Directions:
Sift together flour, soda, & salt
Add sugars, shortening, egg, & vanilla, beat until smooth, about 2 mins.
Stir in oats & Chopped pecans. Shape dough into one 12 inch roll.
Wrap and chill thoroughly.
Once chilled, slice and place on cookie sheets. If slicing becomes difficult you can easily reshape the dough to a round or oval shape.
Bake at 375 about 10-12 minutes. Remove from oven and immediately place small individual Hershey's square on each cookie.
Remove cookies from sheets, press a pecan half into the chocolate Let set.

Submitter:

Description: 
After receiving this recipe a few years ago from a co-worker it has since become a staple on our christmas cookie platter. These cookies are delicious and attractive.
