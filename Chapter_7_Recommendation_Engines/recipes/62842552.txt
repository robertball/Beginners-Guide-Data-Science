Title:
roasted corn chowder with potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/676251.jpg
Ingredients:
5-8 cobs of corn (all ready roasted)
1 32 oz. box of chicken stock (or veggie stock)
3 cups of milk (2% or whole)
1 yellow onion, quartered
3 garlic cloves, smashed
6 -8 small red or yukon gold potatoes, cut into small cubes
salt to taste
italian parsley for garnish

Directions:
With a knife, cut off all the corn kernels catching them into a large bowl. Set aside the kernels. Through the cobs in a large pot on the stove, add the stock, milk, onions and garlic. Bring to a boil, add the potatoes then simmer for at least 30 min.
Add discard the cobbs, and add the corn kernels. Simmer for another 30 mins. Add salt/pepper to taste, garnish with parsley.
