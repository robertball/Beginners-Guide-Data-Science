Title:
Grandma's Swiss Cheese and Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz pkg noodles
2 - 10 oz packages frozen chopped spinach
16 oz sour cream
2 c white sauce
16 oz Swiss Cheese, grated
1 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/2 t. garlic powder

Directions:
Cook noodles, 5-6 mins and drain.
While noodles cook, drain spinach well and combine with sour cream and white sauce.  Add salt, pepper and garlic powder, adjust to taste.
Add cooked noodles and swiss cheese, then mix.
Place in a well-greased casserole dish, then cover with foil, bake at 350 degrees for 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Dave Hirschler
Description: 
I always requested this dish when I stayed at Grandma's.  I changed it up a bit and made it simpler.  Grandma layered it.  I can't be bothered with all that extra work!  Still tastes just the same.
