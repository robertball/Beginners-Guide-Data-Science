Title:
COCONUT CUPCAKES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 CUPS CAKE FLOUR (NOT SELF RISING)
2 HEAPING TSP BAKING POWDER
3 STICKS UNSALTED BUTTER
2 1/4 CUPS SUGAR
1/2 TSP VANILLA
1 CUP COCONUT MILK
8 LARGE EGG WHITES

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350. LINE CUPCAKE PANS
IN A BOWL MIX TOGETHER FLOUR & BAKING POWDER.
IN ANOTHER BOWL BEAT BUTTER AND 2 CUPS SUGAR UNTIL LIGHT & FLUFFLY. ABOUT 3-4 MINUTES.
ADD VANILLA. CAREFULLY ADD 1/3 OF THE FLOUR MIXTURE WHILE BEATING. THEN ADD HALF OF THE COCONUT MILK. THEN ADD THE 2ND THIRD OF THE FLOUR MIXTURE AND THEN THE REST OF THE COCONUT MILK. FINISH BY ADDING THE REST OF THE FLOUR MIXTURE. WHEN IT'S COMBINED SET ASIDE.
IN A CLEAN BOWL FOLD THE EGG MIXTURE INTO THE BUTTER & FLOUR MIXTURE UNTIL COMBINED. THEN CAREFULLY STIR IN THE SHREDDED COCONUT. DO NOT OVERMIX
DIVIDE THE BATTER INTO THE CUPCAKE LINERS
BAKE AT 20-25 MINUTES.
*FOR TOASTED COCONUT-USE FRESH COCONUT. USE 2 COCONUTS-GRATE AND SPREAD ON A BAKING SHEET AND PUT IN A 350 OVEN-IT'S DONE WITH IT TURNS GOLDEN BROWN
*FOR THE FROSTING A BUTTERCREAM IS FINE
