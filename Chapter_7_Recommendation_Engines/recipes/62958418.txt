Title:
Super Smoothies Forever Young Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729406.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup low-fat cherry yogurt
1/4 cup cranberry juice
1 cup frozen pitted cherries
3/4 frozen unsweetened blueberries

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into the blender.
Blend until smooth.
Pour into a tall glass. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
jennifer_russell
Description: 
Cherries, blueberries, and cranberries are all fantastic sources of vitamin C and antioxidants that may protect against some types of cancer. Enjoyed on a regular basis, this smoothie just may be one of the secrets to staying young.
