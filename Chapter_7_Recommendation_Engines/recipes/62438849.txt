Title:
Mushroom Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 onion, chopped
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup white wine
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Season pork chops with salt, pepper to taste.
In a large skillet, add oil and brown the chops over medium-high heat. Remove chops from pan and add the onion and mushrooms, and saute for one minute. Whisk cream of mushroom soup, milk & wine together.  Return chops to pan and pour mushroom soup mixture over chops. Cover skillet, and reduce temperature to medium-low. Simmer 20 to 30 minutes, or until chops are cooked through.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Transform cream of mushroom soup into a delicious simmering sauce by tossing in some chopped onion and sliced fresh mushrooms. Add to seasoned and sauteed pork chops and you have a rich and creamy one-pan meal.Modified from: Mushroom Pork Chops
