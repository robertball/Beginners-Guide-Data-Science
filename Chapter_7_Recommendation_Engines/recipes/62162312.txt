Title:
Fideo (mexican Vermicilli) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Vegetable oil
2 tablespoons fine chopped onion
7 ounces vermicelli pasta
4 cups water or chicken stock
1 tablespoon of Knorr Chicken bouillon powder (3/4 tablespoon if using chicken broth)
1/4 cup of tomato sauce
1/8 teaspoon of pepper
1/4 teaspoon of ground cumin

Directions:
1. Heat the oil in a large sauce pan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the onion in the hot oil for about 60 seconds. Stir in the vermicelli and cook until golden brown, stirring frequently. Add the water/stock, tomato sauce, pepper, and ground cumin; cover. Simmer until the vermicelli is tender, 10 minutes remove from direct heat and let it sit for 5 minutes (covered).

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a staple in my Meican Culture, and it is such a versatile dish. Serve as the main course or as a side!
