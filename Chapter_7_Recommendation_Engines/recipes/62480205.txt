Title:
Chicken Verde Enchaladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/485113.jpg
Ingredients:
2lbs boneless skinless chicken breats
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 small onion, diced
3 small cans of diced green chilis
3 8 ounce cups of plain yogurt
20 medium flour tortillas
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
paprika
oregano

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Boil chicken with onions and garlic.
Shred chicken and put in large bowl.
Strain water to save onion and garlic, add to chicken.
Mix green chili's and yogurt into chicken.
Stir in 1/2 cup of cheese.
Spoon some of the yogurt mixture (Avoid chicken peices) into 13x9 inch baking dish, covering bottom of pan.
Put about a one inch line of chicken mixture down center of tortilla. Roll and place in pan.
Continue until pan is full, squeezing them in where you can.
When done, use left over mixture to completely cover the top of the enchaladas.
Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
Take out, remove foil and cover with remaining cup of cheese. Sprinkle with paprika and oregano for color and bake an additional 15 minutes.
Let cool 5 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Chel_C
Description: 
Quick and easy to make! Only needs a few ingredients and yes, the whole idea of yogurt in enchaladas sounds strange, but it is actually quite good!
