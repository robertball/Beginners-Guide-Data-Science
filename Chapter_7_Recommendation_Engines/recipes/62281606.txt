Title:
Cowboy Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black-eyed peas
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
juice of 1 lime
1 packet Good Seasons salad dressing, mixed according to directions
1 teaspoon taco seasoning
salt to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine black-eyed peas, black beans, corn, onion, green bell pepper, jalapeno peppers, tomatoes, and cilantro. Season with lime juice, Italian-style salad dressing, taco seasoning, and salt; mix well. Cover, and refrigerate to blend flavors.
