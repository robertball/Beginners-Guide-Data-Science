Title:
Tomato Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cp water
20 oz. Tomato Puree**
3/4 cp brown sugar
dried basil to taste(I almost 1 Tbs.)
salt to taste
10 slices cubed bread
1/4 cp melted butter
**Substitutions for puree:
(1) OMITT WATER and use three 8 oz cans of tomato sauce.
OR
(2) 1 6 oz can tomato paste, original 1/2 cp water, additional6 oz. water, and one 8 oz can tomato sauce.

Directions:
In saucepan:  boil 1/2 cp water
Add: 20 oz. Tomato Puree**
3/4 cp brown sugar
dried basil (abt. 1 slight Tbs.)
salt to taste
Simmer until all well mixed
In PAM coated casserol dish put:
10 slices cubed bread
Pour over 1/4 cp melted butter
Add contents from sauce pan
Do NOT stir.
Cover and bake 1 hour at 375. Remove lid for last 10 minutes.
Let sit at room Temp for a few minutes before serving (until it 'falls')
ENJOY!

Randi
