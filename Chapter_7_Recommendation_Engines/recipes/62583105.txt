Title:
Triple Threat Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/549662.jpg
Ingredients:
---Layer #1:
1/2 bag semi-sweet chocolate chips (or milk or dark if you prefer)
1/4 can sweetened condensed milk
2 T Gran Marnier (or vanilla)
---Layer #2:
1 8oz bag individually wrapped caramels - unwrapped
1 T cream
1 T butter
---Layer #3:
1/4 pkg cream cheese
1/4 can sweetened condensed milk
2 T powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 bag white chocolate chips
1 tsp course salt

Directions:
Grease an 8x8 pan and set aside
Layer #1: in a microwave safe bowl, heat semi-sweet chips with the 1/4 can sweetened condensed milk in the microwave (approx 2 minutes), stirring often after 1 minute, until nearly melted. Remove from microwave and stir in Gran Marnier (or vanilla), stir until smooth. Spread evenly into prepared pan and chill 30 minutes.
Layer #2: Melt the caramel, cream and butter together in the microwave (approx 3 minutes), stirring often. Spread evenly over the chocolate layer and chill 30 minutes.
Layer #3: In a small bowl, blend the cream cheese, half of the sweetened condensed milk, powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth. In a separate bowl heat the white chocolate chips and remaining sweetened condensed milk together the microwave (approx 2 minutes), stirring often after one minute. Remove from microwave when chips are nearly melted and continue to stir until smooth. Fold into the cream cheese mixture. Spread evenly on top of the caramel layer.
Sprinkle salt evenly on top, careful not to sprinkle too much in any one place. Chill at least one (1) hour.

Description: 
Layers of chocolate fudge, caramel and soft white chocolate/cream cheese fudge sprinkled with coarse salt. Need I say more?
