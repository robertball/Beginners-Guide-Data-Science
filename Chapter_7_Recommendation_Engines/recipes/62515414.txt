Title:
Peter's ultimate mac and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of mac and cheese
4 strips of bacon
4oz  of Velveeta
1/4 cup of brown sugar

Directions:
*cook box of mac and cheese according to the box
*cook bacon, degrease, and set aside. when cool, crumble
*add all ingreidents and mix thoroughly
*if needed put in oven at 350 until all cheese is melted
