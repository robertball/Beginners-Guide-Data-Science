Title:
Noodle Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz of medium noodles (egg noodles are good)
1 stick melted butter
4 eggs
16 oz Cottage Cheese
8 oz Sour Cream
8 oz Cream Cheese (room temp)
1/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon Cinnamon
1 & 1/2 cups raisins
Topping:
1 & 1/2 cups Crushed Vanilla Wafers
1/4 cup Packed Brown Sugar
1 teaspoon Pumpkin Pie (or Apple Pie) Spice
1/4 Cup Cold Butter

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees (325 degrees for Pyrex)
Cook noodles according to the package directions; rinse & drain. Mix the noodles and melted butter together in a large baking pan (9x13 is usually a good size).
In a large bowl; mix cottage cheese, sour cream, cream cheese, eggs, sugar, & cinnamon.
Combine in the pan with the noodles. Stir in Raisins. Bake for 30 minutes.
In small bowl; mix together the crushed vanilla wafers, brown sugar, pie spice. Then cut in the butter until mixture is crumblely.
Sprinkle topping over top of kugel, and bake for another 30 minutes. Take out and let cool for about 15 minutes.
Can serve warm or cold.
Notes: You can change the raisins to different dried fruit to change the taste; Apples, Cranberries, Cherries, Blueberries, etc...

Submitter: 
Elizabeth E. Aiello
Description: 
This makes a great addition to a holiday Breakfast, brunch, or a wonderful dessert.
