Title:
Oriental Salad with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
4 1/2 T. Honey
2 1/4 T Rice Wine Vinegar
6 T. Mayonnaise
1 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp. Sesame oil
Salad:
3 cups Romaine lettuce, chopped
1 cup Red Cabbage, chopped
1 cup Napa Cabbage, chopped
1/2 Carrot, julienned
1 Green Onion, sliced
1 T. Sliced almonds
1/3 cup crispy chow mein noodles
1 grilled chicken breast (or 2-3 cooked chicken strips)

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients for dressing.  Chill in refrigerator.
Prepare salad by tossing the romaine lettuce, red and Napa cabbages and carrots.  Put on plate and sprinkle with sliced green onion, sliced almonds and chow mein noodles.
Cut your choice of chicken into bite size chunks and place onto top of salad.
Pour dressing over top, or serve on side.

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
If you're a fan of Applebee's oriental chicken salad, this recipe is for you.  I prefer grilled chicken, but you can use your favorite chicken strips too.
