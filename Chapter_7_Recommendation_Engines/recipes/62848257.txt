Title:
Sweet and Spicy Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 lb. ground beef
1 pkg. dry onion soup mix
salt and pepper
1 bottle chili sauce
1 (6oz.) jar grape jelly

Directions:
Directions
Combine ground beef with onion soup mix and salt and pepper to taste. Form mixture into small meatballs; brown and drain. In a saucepan, thoroughly heat together chili sauce and grape jelly. Add meatballs, mixing well. Keep warm and serve
