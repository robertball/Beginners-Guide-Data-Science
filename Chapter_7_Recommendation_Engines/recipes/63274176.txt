Title:
HERSHEY's Peanut Butter Marble Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter or margarine
1/3 c. cocoa
3 eggs
1 1/4 c. sugar, DIVIDED
1 t. vanilla extract
1/2 c. flour
1/2 t. baking powder
1/4 t. salt
1 c. peanut butter chips, DIVIDED
3 oz. cream cheese, softened

Directions:
Melt butter and stir in cocoa; set aside.
Beat 2 eggs in a mixing bowl until foamy; gradually add 1 c. sugar and vanilla, blending well.
In a separate bowl, stir together flour, baking powder and salt; blend into egg mixture.
Add cocoa mixture and 1/2 c. peanut butter chips, blend well.
Remove 1/2 c. batter and set aside.
Spread remaining batter in a greased 9" square baking pan.
Microwave remaining peanut butter chips until melted. In a small mixing bowl, stir melted chips together with cream cheese, and remaining sugar; beat until smooth. Add remaining egg and blend well.
Spread this mixture over the batter in the pan. Drop the remaining batter into the pan, and swirl.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes.
