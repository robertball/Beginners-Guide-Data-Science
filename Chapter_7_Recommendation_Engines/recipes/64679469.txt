Title:
Mixed Bean Soup or Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4856278.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. mixed dried beans* (about 2 cups), soaked overnight
Ham, beef or chicken bones (optional)
3 qts. fresh water
1/2-1# cooked ham or bacon
4 beef, chicken or vegetable bouillon cubes
2 lg. onions, chopped
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 C. shredded carrots
1 lg. can crushed, diced, pureed tomatoes, or tomato sauce
1 T. liquid smoke
sea salt (if needed) and pepper to taste
For minestrone:
Any desired vegetables: carrots, green beans, cabbage, potatoes, peas, corn, spinach, zucchini, etc.
1/2 C. brown rice
1/4 C. barley (eliminate for gluten free)
4 oz. spinach noodles or other pasta (optional (eliminate for gluten free)

Directions:
Rinse beans thoroughly and cover with water, 2-3" above beans, and soak overnight.
If you forgot to soak your beans, you can bring them to a boil and drain them several times (reduces gas) and then bring them to a boil again and soak for at least 1 hour.
Pour off water and put beans into a large soup pot with bones, if using.
Add 3 qts. water
Add brown rice and barley, if making Minestrone.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 3-4 hours.
Remove bones and add meat, onions, garlic, tomatoes, liquid smoke and vegetables and continue simmering for at least 1 hour.
Add pasta and cook for 15 more minutes.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
*Dried mixed beans: pintos, black beans, great northern, navy, lima, kidney, black eyed peas, green and yellow split peas, lentils, field peas, etc.
