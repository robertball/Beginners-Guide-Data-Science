Title:
Pizza Bread w/chorizo sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf french bread cut length wise like a giant hogie..then each 1/2 in 1/2 so we have 4 pizza bread surfases
1 lb chorizo sausage
1 C shredded cheddar cheese
1 small onion finely chopped
1 sm can tomatoe paste

Directions:
brown the sausage and onion and drain -- chorizo is tasty but often grease.  Don't rinse, just remove as much grease as can easily be done.
Fold tomatoe paste into sausage
toast bread
spread 1/4 meat mixture onto each slice of bread
top wtih 1/4 cheese on each slice
broil on middle rack for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbley

Submitter: 
Pam Rosterman
Description: 
This is easy and tasty -- great for a brunch or movie night
