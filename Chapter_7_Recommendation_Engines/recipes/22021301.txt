Title:
Stuffed Green Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972952.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large green sweet peppers
Dash of salt optional
1 lb pound ground beef
1 lb pound ground sausage
2/3 cup chopped onion
1 14-ounce can tomatoes, cut up
1 cup water
1 cup long grain rice
2 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon dried basil or oregano
1/2 cup shredded American cheese

Directions:
Halve sweet peppers lengthwise, removing stem ends, seeds and membranes. Immerse sweet peppers into boiling water for 3 minutes. If desired sprinkle insides with a dash salt. Invert on paper towels to drain well.
In a large skillet cook meat and onion till meat is brown and onion is tender. Drain off fat. Stir in undrained tomatoes, water, uncooked rice, Worcestershire sauce, basil or regano, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Bring to boiling. Reduce heat. Simmer, covered for 15 to 18 minutes or unil rice is tender (longer if brown rice). Stir in 1/4 cup of cheese.
Fill peppers with meat mixture. Place in a 2-quart swuare baking dish along with any remaining meat mixture. Bake in a 375 degree oven about 15 mintues or till heated through. Sprinkle with the remaining 1/4 cup cheese. Let stand about 2 minutes or till cheese is melted.
