Title:
Chicken and Snow Pea Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
2 cups fresh snow pea pods, cleaned
4 green onions, sliced thinly
8 ounces bowtie pasta
1/2 cup black bean and garlic paste
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 Tbsp. vegetable or peanut oil

Directions:
Cook pasta according to pasta directions.  Drain and set aside.
Heat oil in large skillet.
Add chicken and saut√© until no longer pink.  Remove from pan and set aside.
Add pea pods and green onions to skillet and saute for 3-4 minutes, until pea pods are tender crisp.
Lower heat and add black bean and garlic paste and peanut butter.  Stir all over low heat until blended well, then add chicken and pasta.  Toss together and allow to heat through, approximately 2-3 minutes.  Serve with sesame seed garnish if you like.
