Title:
Budget No Bake Cookies I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/275207.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
5 tablespoons butter
16 large marshmallows
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups quick cooking oats
1 cup coconut or raisins (if you do not want to use this add 1 cup more oats)

Directions:
I a large sauce pan melt together the butter, chocolate chips, and marshmallows. Stir in vanilla extract, oats, and raisins or coconut.  Stir until just combined and drop by rounded spoonfuls onto wax paper.  Let cool.  You can cool this cookies in the refrigerator if you want to eat them quicker.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
This revised budget version of no bake cookies are perfect for the little ones who have peanut allergies.  I took out the step of boiling as well so these cookies turn out perfect every time!Modified from: No Bake Cookies I
