Title:
Oh So Easy Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick butter.  (No substitues please.)
1 Cup packed brown sugar
1/4 Cup milk.
1 teaspoon vanilla (Optional)

Directions:
Mix butter, brown sugar, and milk together in saucepan.  Bring to gentle rolling boil, and boil no more than 2 minutes.  Remove from heat and add vanilla if desired.
Can be used immediately.  Store leftover sauce in refrigerator and use warm or room temperature.

Submitter: 
BARB MAXWELL
Description: 
This is a rich, sweet caramel sauce that can be made at the last minute that is great on bread pudding, gingerbread, apple pie, chopped apples, ice cream, and anything else you want to put it on.
