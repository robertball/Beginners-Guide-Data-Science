Title:
Fudge Almond Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 CUPS GINGER SNAP COOKIES crumbs 1/4 cup melted butter
3  8 oz. packages creame cheese, cut into chunks
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
2 t almond extract
2 t vanilla
1 6 oz. semisweet chocolate
3 T butter

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350*. Place cookies into a food processor and crumble finely, add melted butter and process a few seconds, press mixture into a 9 inch springform pan. Process the cream cheese until smooth, add sugar, eggs, and extracts, process until well blended. Pour into pan and bake 45 minutes. Turn off the oven.  Allow to cool in the oven with the door open.  When cool remove from the pan. Melt the chocolate and butter together. Swirl over the top of the cake.
