Title:
Rolled Pickles- Dip Style! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/548210.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz package of cream cheese (room temp)
2-3 TBS of dill pickle relish
1 (2 oz) pkg dried beef or Buddig Beef

Directions:
Chop beef into small pieces.
Mix with cream cheese and relish (add relish to consistency you like).
Serve with crostini or crackers.

Description:
