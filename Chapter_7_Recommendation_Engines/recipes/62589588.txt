Title:
Mini Italian Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c Bisquick
1/2 c shredded cheddar
1/2 t garlic powder
1/2 t oregano
1/2 t basil
2/3 c milk

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Drop by tsp on sprayed sheet pan.
Bake at 450 for 7-8 minutes.
