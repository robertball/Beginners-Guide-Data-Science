Title:
Tabbouleh with Edamame Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1026390.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked wheat bulgur
1 cup boiling water
1 cup cooked shelled edamame
1 cucumber seeded and chopped
1 bunch green onions, sliced into rings
2 cups cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
2 cups fresh parsley, chopped
1/4-1/2 cup olive oil
1/8 cup apple cider vinegar
The juice of 2 large lemons
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Add bulgur to boiling water, cover, and let sit for 1 hour.
In a large bowl, toss together the cooked bulgur, edamame, tomatoes, cucumber, onions, and parsley.
In a separate bowl, whisk together, olive oil, lemon juice, and vinegar.
Pour dressing over bulgur mixture and toss well.
Season with salt and pepper as desired.
Cover and Chill at least 6 hours, or up to 3 days.

Submitter: 
bakeaholic
Description: 
This is my own version of traditional tabbouleh. The addition of soybeans makes it a meal in itself. It gets better after chilling for a day or two.
