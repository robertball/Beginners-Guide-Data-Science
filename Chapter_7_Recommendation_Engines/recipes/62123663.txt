Title:
Dirt 'n Worms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/292027.jpg
Ingredients:
20-25 chocolate cookies
1 large box instant chocolate pudding
10 gummy worms

Directions:
In a food processor or blender grate chocolate cookies until crumbly.
Make pudding as directed on box. In 5 dessert dishes, layer pudding and grated cookies as much as you'd like. Insert 2 gummy worms per dish, so that they stick out just a bit. Chill for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
heather88
Description: 
A fun and chocolaty dessert that pleases both kids and parents alike!
