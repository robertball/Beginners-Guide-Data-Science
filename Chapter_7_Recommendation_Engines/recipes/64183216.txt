Title:
Stove Top Chicken and Stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.5 ounce) cans cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
3 cups cubed cooked chicken
2 (6 ounce) package stuffing mix
2 cups water
3/4 cup butter

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Mix cream of chicken soup and sour cream together in a bowl; stir in chicken. Spoon mixture into the prepared baking dish.
3. Combine water and butter in a saucepan over medium heat until butter is melted; pour over stuffing layer in a side bowl.  Wait 5 minutes until stuffing is softened.  Spoon over chicken mixture.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbling and stuffing is browned, about 30 minutes.
