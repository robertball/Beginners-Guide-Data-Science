Title:
Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
3/4 cups oil
1-1/2 cups sugar
2 cups pumpkin
2 cups flour
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix eggs and oil.  Add remaining ingredients.
Bake in a greased cookie sheet for 25 minutes. Cool and frost if desired.
