Title:
Garlic Scrambled Eggs II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, beaten
5 cloves garlic, slivered
1 tablespoon butter
salt
pepper

Directions:
Heat butter and garlic in skillet over medium high heat until garlic is just starting to brown.
Add eggs to butter and garlic. Cook to desired consistency.
Season eggs with salt, and pepper.
Eat them.
