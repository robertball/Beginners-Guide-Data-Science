Title:
Pork Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 bunch green onions Or 1/2 cup reg onions minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
6 pork butt steaks
1/4 cup flour
2 tbsp oil

Directions:
Dust steaks with flour and brown in skillet with oil.  Remove, melt butter in same skillet, and mix in the soy sauce. Saute the onions and garlic until lightly browned.
Place the pork steaks in the skillet, cover, and cook 8 to 10 minutes on each side, Remove cover, and continue cooking 10 minutes, or to an internal temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
