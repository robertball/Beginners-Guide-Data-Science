Title:
Sri Wasano's Infamous Indonesian Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup peanut oil; 3 tbsp sesame oil; 1/2 cup orange juice; 2 medium cloves of garlic; 1tsp salt; 2tbsp soy sauce; 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper; 2tbs cider vinegar; 1 cup chopped pineapple; 3 scallions finely minced; 1 stalk celery finely minced; 1 medium red or green pepper thinly sliced; 1 8oz can water chestnuts, drained and thinly sliced; 1/2 cup raisins; 1/2 lb bean sprouts; 1 cup coarsely chopped cashews, 2 tbsp sesame seeds; 2 cups brown rice

Directions:
Cook rice as directed on package. While rice is cooking combine the first 9 ingredients in a large bowl. Mix hot rice with the dressing, let it cool to room temperature. Stir in the rest of the ingredients and refrigerate covered until cold. Serve

Ursula

It's one of my favorite salads! Makes a great lunch.
