Title:
Meatball Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  pounds  ground beef
1   package onion soup mix
2  cups flour
1/2  cup vegetable oil
4  tablespoons flour, for roux
1   onion, chopped
1/2  bell pepper, chopped
1  tablespoon garlic, minced
creole seasoning to taste
4  cups  water

Directions:
Mix ground beef and onion soup mix, form into 2 inch meat balls. Roll meatballs in flour, and brown in oil.  Drain meatballs, reserving 1/4 cups of the oil.  Sprinkle 4 heaping tablespoons flour in the oil. Stir constantly.
After the roux is browned, add onions, garlic, bell pepper, stirring constantly for about 2 minutes.  Add 4 cups of water and creole seasoning to taste.  Simmer about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Brenda Laughlin Sumner
Description: 
This stew is true southern comfort food when served over rice.
