Title:
Sour Cream Meatloaf with Parmesan Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
3/4 pound ground pork
1/2 pound ground turkey
2 eggs
1 cup Ritz cracker crumbs
1 cup sour cream
1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
3 Tablespoons chopped chives
1/2 teaspoon thyme leaves
1/2 teaspoon chervil
1/2 teaspoon basil leaves
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup Parmesan sauce
Sauce
3 Tablespoons butter
3 Tablespoons flour
1 1/4 cups chicken broth
1 1/4 cups half and half
1/2 cup freshly shredded Parmesan

Directions:
In large bowl, combine all ingredients except sauce.
Line a large loaf pan with foil.  Pour mixture into pan.
Bake for 1 1/2 hours.
Turn loaf out.  Remove foil.  Slice and serve with Parmesan sauce.
To make Parmesan Sauce:
Heat butter in sauce pan.  Add flour and cook 2 minutes over low heat.
Stir in broth and cream.  Reduce heat and simmer until thickened.
Stir in cheese and simmer 2 more minutes.  Serve with meatloaf.
