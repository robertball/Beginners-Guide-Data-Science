Title:
Ranch Dressing II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup milk
3 tsp lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon dried chives
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together lemon juice and milk. Set aside for 5 minutes to thicken. Whisk in the mayonnaise, sour cream, chives, parsley, dill, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
ChefMom
Description: 
This is for those who prefer herbs rather than spice. Creamy and delicious - it is the perfect accompaniment to chicken, ham or crisp salad greens.Modified from: Ranch Dressing II
