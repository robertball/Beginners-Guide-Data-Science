Title:
Tomato Basil Parmesan Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups tomatoes with juice
1 Tbsp fresh oregano or 1 tsp dried
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup flour
1 cup parmesan cheese, freshly grated
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup roughly chopped onions
1/2 C. fresh basil or 1 Tbsp dried
1/2 bay leaf
1/2 cup butter
2 cups half and half (if you want you can use whole milk, evaporated milk insteadâ€¦or even skim if you want to skinny it up)
1 tsp salt
Sugar to taste (will depend on sweetness of tomatoes)

Directions:
1.  Heat oil in 4 quart soup pot.  Add onions.  Saute 5 minutes.  Add basil, oregano, bay leaf, tomatoes, and chicken broth.  Bring it to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for about 10 min
2.  While soup simmers, melt butter over low heat, add flour whisk constantly over low heat. Slowly stir in milk and seasonings and cook for 5 min. Add this mixture into soup pot.
3.  Simmer until soup begins to thicken.  Blend in batches in a blender until smooth. Put pack in pot and dd Parmesan cheese then whisk to blend. Add sugar to taste (usually about 1.5 Tb).  Simmer over low heat 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
