Title:
Easy Taco Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg uncooked crescent rolls
1 lb ground beef, browned
1 pkg taco seasoning
1 can refried beans
1 cup sour cream
1 cup salsa drained slightly
1-1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
shredded lettuce, extra cheese and sour cream for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Press refrigerated crescent rolls into the bottom and sides of a large deep pie pan. Pan may be lightly sprayed with cooking spray if necessary.
Spread refried beans onto crescent rolls.
Spread sour cream on top of beans.
Season browned ground beef with package of taco seasoning and spread on top of sour cream.
Spread drained salsa on top of meat and cover with cheddar cheese.
Bake for 25-30 min. or until cheese is melted and bubbly and crust has browned.
Serve with shredded lettuce, salsa, cheese, and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Michelle Blum
Description: 
Tacos meat and fillings meet packaged crescent rolls for an easy dinner that your whole family will love.
