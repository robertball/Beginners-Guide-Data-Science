Title:
Best Watermelon Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/957648.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups ice
2 cups watermelon chunks
1/2 cup cantaloupe chunks
1/4 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon honey
4 sprigs mint, for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Blend the ice, watermelon, cantaloupe, orange juice, and honey together in a blender until no chunks remain and the mixture is a thick slush. Garnish with the mint if desired.

Submitter: 
xxBubblie13xx
Description: 
This is a healthy, delicious, refreshing treat for a hot day. Replace the honey with blue agave sweetener if you wish. Enjoy!
