Title:
Turbot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the marinade:
Olive oil
Rosemary
Thyme
Parmesan cheese (over the top)
2 Turbot Filets

Directions:
Rinse the the turbot filets with cold water and pat dry with paper towels.
Create a simple marinade using olive oil and the seasonings
Coat the filets with the marinade and let sit for 30 minutes.
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
Brush the filets with the marinade just before cooking
Bake for approximately 20 minutes or until  the fish begins to flake.
Remove from the oven and serve with lemon wedges, if desired.

Description: 
Turbot is a mild, firm whitefish.  It is native to Europe.  When baked  in the  in the oven, it produces a moist, flaky, and flavorful delicacy.
