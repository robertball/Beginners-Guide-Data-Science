Title:
Fideo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons dried onion
2 tablespoon ground cumin
8 oz.can tomato sauce
1 small beef boullion
3 to 4 thin slices of green bell pepper
2 tablespoon garlic powder
2 tablespoon onion powder
1 lb. ground beef, browned
1 box fideo noodles, browned in canola oil
3 to 4 cups hot water give or take
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pan brown 1 lb. ground beef with 1 tablespoon of dried onion, 1 tablespoon of garlic powder, 1 tablespoon of onion powder and salt and pepper to taste. After ground beef is well browned remove from pan and set aside. In the same pan heat about 4 tablespoons of canola oil and empty 1 box of fideo noodles and brown. Add ground beef and then carefully add water. Enough to cover all of the noodles and ground beef. Add 8 oz of tomato sauce, remaining spices, beef boullion,and green bell pepper along with salt and pepper to taste. Cook until noodle are tender. Enjoy!
