Title:
Chocolate Chip Pudding Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992820.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package cook and serve vanilla pudding mix
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup flaked coconut, toasted

Directions:
Prepare pudding according to package directions. In two parfait glasses, layer 2 tablespoons chocolate chips, a fourth of the warm pudding and 2 tablespoons coconut. Repeat layers. Serve or refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Pauline  Piper
Description: 
This was one of our children's favorite desserts. It's simple to put together, and I liked to surprise them by varying the ingredients in the parfait layers. -Pauline Piper, Egg Harbor Township, New Jersey
