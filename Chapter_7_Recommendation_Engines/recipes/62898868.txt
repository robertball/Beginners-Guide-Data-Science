Title:
English Style BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp.salt
1/4 cup H.P steak sauce
1/4 cup vinegar
1/2 tsp.worstershire
3 cloves crushed garlic

Directions:
Combine all ingredients till well blended,for a spicier sauce add hot sauce to taste.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
I got this recipe from my friend in England.
I've had this recipe for over 30 years,it belonged to her mother.Easy when you've run out of BBQ sauce,
and you need something fast.
