Title:
peanut butter sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. light corn syrup
1/2 c. peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
1/4 c. butter
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Put syrup, peanut butter and butter in a heavy saucepan. Cook over low heat until the peanut butter and butter are melted, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and add vanilla.
