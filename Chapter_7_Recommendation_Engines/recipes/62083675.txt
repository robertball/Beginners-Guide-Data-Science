Title:
Rebecca's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground sirloin meat
1/3 cup diced red onion
1/3 cup of a green pepper diced
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2c of ketchup
1 tlbs of brown sugar
1/4 tsp of mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup of cooked rice
2 eggs
a dash of woshier sauce
15 crackers crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix in microwavable bowl ketchup,mustard,brown sugar ,pepper and woshier sauce. Heat for about 20 seconds or until the brown sugar is disolved set aside. Mix in large bowl meat,garlic green pepper,onion, salt,eggs and rice. Mix well. Add a couple of table spoon of the ketchup mixture to the meat.. Place in meat loaf pan.  Now spead the remaining ketchup mixture on top of loaf cook for 60min meat is done. Enjoy

The best meatloaf ever you can leave out the veggies for picky eaters.
