Title:
Chinese Glass Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz. thread noodles
3 14 1/2 cans chicken broth
2 tbsp thin strips of ginger root
4 2" pieces of lemon grass
1 large clove of garlic
2 skinless boneless chicken breast cut into 1/2" pieces
6 large shrimp
2 tbsp lime juice
2 tbsp fish sauce
8 thin jalepeno chili slices
1/4 c chopped cilantro

Directions:
Soak noodles and cut.
Combine broth, garlic, ginger and lemon grass in saucepan.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat. Simmer 15 min.
Add chicken and shrimp. Simmer for about 3 minutes.
Stir in lime juice and fish sauce.
Divide noodles into bowls. Ladle soup into bowls. Top with cilantro and jalepenos.
