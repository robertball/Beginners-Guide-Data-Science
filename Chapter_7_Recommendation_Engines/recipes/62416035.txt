Title:
Blueberry Oatmeal Cream Brulee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/441396.jpg
Ingredients:
Cream:
•	½ cup heavy whipping cream
•	1 small cinnamon stick
•	¼ tsp. ground cardamom
•	3 tbs. pure maple syrup
Oatmeal:
•	4 cups water
•	2 cups old-fashioned oats
•	1 tsp. salt
•	 2 tbs. brown sugar
•	2 tbs. pure maple syrup
•	2 tbs. butter
•	1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries, thawed
•	¼ cup sugar

Directions:
Pour cream into a small saucepan, add the cinnamon stick. Bring to a boil over medium-low heat, cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove pan from heat; discard cinnamon stick. Add cardamom and syrup to cream mixture, set aside.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, bring water to a boil. Add the oats and salt; cook and stir for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat; stir in brown sugar, syrup, and cream mixture. Cover and let stand for 2 minutes.
Grease four 10-oz. ramekins or seven 6-oz. ramekins with the butter; sprinkle blueberries on the bottom of each ramekin. Spoon oatmeal over the top; sprinkle with sugar. Place on a baking sheet. Broil 4-6 in. from the heat for 7-9 minutes or until sugar is caramelized.
