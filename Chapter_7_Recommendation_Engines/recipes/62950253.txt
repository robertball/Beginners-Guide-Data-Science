Title:
Wacky Cake WWII Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
3/4 cups sugar
1/4 cup coco powder
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
5 TBLS vegetable oil
1 TBLS vinegar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and have oven rack in middle position; coat 8" square pan with cooking spray.
Put all dry ingredients in pan and whisk to blend.
Make 1 large crater and 2 small craters in the dry mix.
Pour oil into large crater; pour vinegar in one small crater and vanilla into the last crater.
Pour water into the pan.
Using wooden spoon, gently mix everything together until you see only a few streaks of flour in mixture.
Place pan immediately in oven and bake for 30 minutes or until toothpick poked in center comes out clean.
Cool cake, dust with powdered sugar.

Submitter:

Description: 
Very easy recipe used during WWII that uses no butter or eggs and very little sugar and chocolate. Make sure to mix as instructed for it to come out right.
