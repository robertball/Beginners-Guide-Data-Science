Title:
Baked Potato Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium sized baking potatoes
1/2 small onion, diced
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream (optional)
3 tablespoons cooked bacon, crumbled
2 tablespoons dried chives
4 tablespoons cold butter, cubed
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Chop potatoes into about 1 inch cubes. Dice the onion, but not too finely. Stir the two ingredients together.
3.Grease a 9 by 9 inch oven proof dish. Assemble potatoes and onions into the dish until they lay evenly across. Pour milk evenly across the potatoes. Sprinke the cheese, chives, and bacon bits across the mixture. Sprinke salt over top and then evenly distribute the chopped butter all over the potatoes.
4. Cook for 45 minutes or until top is slightly brown and bubbly.
5. Serve with a dallop of sour cream(optional).
