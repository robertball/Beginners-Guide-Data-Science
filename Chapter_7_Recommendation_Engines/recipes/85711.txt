Title:
Simple Strawberry Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986867.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
5 cups ice
Strawberries, for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with strawberries.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Classic Strawberry Daiquiris are fun and easy to make. Serve in fancy glasses with a strawberry garnish.
