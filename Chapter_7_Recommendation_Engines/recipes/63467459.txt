Title:
Reuben Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz can crescent dinner rolls
3 oz deli corned beef
4 slices swiss cheese
1/2 cup sauerkraut, drained and squeezed dry
1/2 tsp caraway seeds
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Unroll dough onto a baking sheet coated with cooking spray; separate dough into 4 rectangles; gently pressing perforations down center of rectangle to seal. Layer corned beef, 1 slice cheese, and 2 tbsp sauerkraut on each rectangle. Sprinkle with caraway seeds. Roll up rectangles starting at short edge pressing firmly to eliminate air pockets. Pinch the seams to seal. Cut each roll into 4 slices. Bake at 375 for 14 minutes or golden brown. Serve warm.
