Title:
Quick Creamy Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1012653.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon steak sauce (such as A1Â®)
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, mustard, lemon juice, steak sauce, and dill in a bowl; season with salt and pepper. Cover and chill for 4 hours before serving.
