Title:
5 ingredient Slow cooker "Spanish Rabbit" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 fryer rabbits, quartered out into 8 total pieces.
2 tbsp of vegetable oil
1 Cup red wine vinegar
2/3 cup of soy sauce
1 teaspoon garlic powder
4 6 inch cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Brown rabbit quarters in hot oil in a large skillet over medium high heat until browned on all sides.
Place in greased slow cooker
Combine wine vinegar, soy sauce and garlic powder in a bowl. Pour over rabbit.
Break cinnamon sticks into several pieces and distribute among rabbit quarters/chicken thighs.
Cover and cook on low in your crock pot 5-6 hours.

Submitter: 
Frugal Dusky
Description: 
Very simple meat dish with great flavor, best served on a bed of spanish style rice.
Also works well with skinless chicken thighs.
