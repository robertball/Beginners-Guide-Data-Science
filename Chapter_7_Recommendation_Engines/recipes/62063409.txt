Title:
chocolate chip cheesecake bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18.25 oz. yellow or your preference cake mix,  2 eggs,  1/2 cup margarine,  16 oz. cream cheese,  2 eggs,  2/3 sugar,  1 cup chocolate chips.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  line 9x13 inch pan with foil and spray.  Beat together the cake mix, 2 eggs, and margarine.  spread into pan.  Bake 15 minutes.  reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees.  beat together cream cheese, 2 eggs, sugar and then add chips.  Pour over the crust and bake 25 more minutes until set. cool.  Remove bars by lifting foil. cut into bars and keep in fridge. Mmmm
