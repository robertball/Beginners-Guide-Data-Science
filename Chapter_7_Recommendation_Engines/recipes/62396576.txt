Title:
South Beach Grilled Salmon with Rosemary Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. salmon
2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
pinch fresh ground pepper
2 cloves minced garlic
2 teaspoons fresh chopped rosemary (or 1 teaspoon dried)
Optional: Rosemary sprigs and capers

Directions:
Cut fish into 4 equal parts.  Combine olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, garlic & rosemary in bowl.  Brush mixture onto the fish.
To grill: arrange fish on a grill rack or use a grill basket sprayed with olive oil cooking spray, grill over medium-hot coals until fish flakes easily (allow 4 - 6 minutes per 1/2 inch of thickness).  If the fish is more than 1 inch thick, gently turn halfway through grilling.
To broil: Spray rack of broiler pan with olive oil cooking spray and arrange fish on it.  Broil 4 inches from heat 4 - 6 minutes per 1/2 inch thickness.  If fish is more than 1 inch thick, gently turn halfway through broiling.
To serve: top fish with rosemary sprigs and capers if desired.
