Title:
Spinach Ricotta Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777152.jpg
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts,bone in,skin on
1 Tbsp.butter,melted
1 small onion,finely chopped
1 small clove minced garlic
1 1/2 cups chopped fresh spinach
2 Tbsp.chopped fresh parsley
1 Tbsp.chopped fresh basil or
1/2 tsp.dried basil
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
2 Tbsp.freshly grated parmesan cheese
1 egg yolk
1/4 tsp.salt
pinch of nutmeg and pepper

Directions:
Prepare stuffing:
In skillet on med.heat melt marg.cook onion and garlic till soft.Add spinach,parsley and basil,cook for 2 min.till spinach wilts,and water evaporates.Let cool.
In bowl,combine spinach mixture,all cheeses,egg yolk,salt,nutmeg and pepper,mix well.
Gently loosen skin from chicken.Stuff about 1/4 cup of stuffing under the skin of chicken breasts.
Place on greased rack,brush with butter.Bake at 375 for 45-60 min.or till golden brown and no longer pink inside.
