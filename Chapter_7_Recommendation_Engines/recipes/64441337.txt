Title:
Sausage Pasta Casserole ~"Six Ingredients or Less" by Carlean Johnson Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces penne pasta
1 pound sausage
1 cup chopped onion
1 jar (28-ounces) spaghetti sauce with mushrooms
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives
4 ounces mozzarella (shredded)

Directions:
Cook pasta following package directions, cooking a minute or two less than you normally would; drain. Meanwhile, brown sausage and onion until cooked through; drain. Place in a sprayed slow cooker along with the pasta, sauce, and olives.  Cover and cook on LOW 2 to 2 1/2 hours or until very hot. Sprinkle with cheese on top, cover and continue to cook 20 to 30 minutes or until cheese has melted. Makes 6 Servings.

Submitter: 
Michelle Haid Eudy
Description: 
This is a great comfort food selection from the Six Ingredients or Less Cookbook by Carlean Johnson. She recommends pairing this with a green salad and some French bread.
