Title:
MexiMac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of KD Macaroni (or your choice of macaroni)
Some milk and butter for the cheese sauce (as recommended on the box of KD)
1 package of extra lean ground beef
1 package of Taco Seasoning
1 red pepper (diced)
Optional: you can add green peppers, onions or whatever your heart desires***

Directions:
Noodles:
1. Boil Macaroni noodles
2. add cheese sauce
3. mix in 1/2 pack of taco seasoning into the cheese sauce
4. Mix cheese sauce to the cooked macaroni
(basically make macaroni by following the box instructions but add taco seasoning to the cheese sauce)
Meat:
1. Brown meat
2. slice peppers and add to the meat
3. optional ... add green peppers, and onions
4. add 1/2 pack of taco seasoning
5. Mix meat, peppers, Taco seasoning together
6. mix meat and macaroni together
7. serve

Submitter: 
Ann
Description: 
Macaroni with an added spicy taste. 

When my son was little I would make KD, but he wouldn't eat it, he didn't like the cheese sauce on it. So,  I decided to change it up a bit and add meat, peppers and taco seasoning, what a difference! Its our most loved recipe!
