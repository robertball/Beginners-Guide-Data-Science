Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2884186.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 cup mashed overripe bananas (about 3)
1/3 cup buttermilk or sour milk (sour with a bit of vinegar & let sit a few minutes)
3 tbsp. canola oil
1 tsp. vanilla
1 2/3 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1/3 cup chopped nuts (pecans or walnuts or whatever you like)
1/3 cup chocolate chips (I like dark choc. best)

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingredients till well blended.
Add dry ingredients. Mix well.
Pour into greased loaf pan.
Bake @ 350 x 50 - 55 minutes, until tests done with a toothpick.
Let cool in pan x 5 min or more.
Turn out onto rack to cool.
Freezes well.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
I classic moist loaf. Tastes good with either chocolate chips or nuts (or both!) added.
