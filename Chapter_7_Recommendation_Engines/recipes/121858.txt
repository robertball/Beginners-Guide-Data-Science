Title:
Saucy Apple Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/166884.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups applesauce
1/3 cup sugar
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
6 (1/2 inch thick) boneless pork loin chops
2 tablespoons butter or margarine

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the applesauce, sugar, soy sauce, garlic and ginger; mix well. Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. In a skillet, brown pork chops in butter; place over applesauce mixture. Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees F for 30 minutes or until meat is tender.

Submitter: 
Amy  Church
Description: 
'My mother gave me the recipe for these juicy pork chops oven-baked in a slightly sweet sauce,' writes Amy Church of Camby, Indiana. This effortless entree is a snap to prepare and a tempting way to use up leftover applesauce.
