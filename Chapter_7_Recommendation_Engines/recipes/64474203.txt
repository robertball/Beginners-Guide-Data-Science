Title:
Root Beer Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless skinless Chicken breast halves
2 Tbsp. Canola oil
1 cup Root Beer
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup ketcup
4 tsp. Dijon mustard
2 tsp. grated lemon peel

Directions:
Flatten chicken breasts slightly, cook chicken in oil in large skillet for 6-8 min on each side until 170 degrees. Remove and keep warm
Stir root beer, brown sugar, ketchup, mustard and lemon peel into skillet. Bring to a boil. Cook for 6-8 min. or until thickened. Return chicken to skillet, heat through.
