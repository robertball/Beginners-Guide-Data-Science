Title:
The best Shish Kabob Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PACKAGE DRY ONION SOUP MIX
4 T. SUGAR
1 CUP CATSUP
½ C. VEGETABLE OIL
2 T. PREPARED MUSTARD (not dry)
½ t. SALT
2 DASHES TOBASCO SAUCE
1 CUP WATER

Directions:
Bring to boil.  Simmer 20 minutes. COOL.  Marinate meat only, overnight and then skewer with your favorite vegetables.  Baste with remaining marinade while grilling.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
This recipe has been in our family for over 30 years.  It is tried and true.  We never even look for another Shish Kabob Marinade because there just none better.
