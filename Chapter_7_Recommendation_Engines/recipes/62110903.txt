Title:
Mary's Southern Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can tuna packed in water
2 tbsp light mayo
2 tbsp sweet pickle relish
1/4 tsp salt and pepper
4 crushed saltine crakers
Slices of tomato (optional)

Directions:
Drain tuna.
Mix all ingredients (except for tomato slices)
Enjoy with tomato on bread or crackers
