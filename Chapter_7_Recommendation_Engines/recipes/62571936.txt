Title:
Creamy Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/543060.jpg
Ingredients:
2 8 oz packages of cream cheese (regular or light), softened
1 cup sour cream (regular or light)
1 cup salad dressing or mayonnaise (regular or light)
1 10 oz package of frozen, chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
4 stalks green onion, chopped
2 tsp dill weed
1 tsp garlic powder or 1 clove fresh, crushed garlic
1 tsp seasoning salt
1 tsp lemon juice (from concentrate or fresh-squeezed)

Directions:
Cream cream cheese and add in sour cream and salad dressing, blend till well combined and smooth.  Stir in remaining ingredients till well blended.
Transfer mix to either hollowed-out round loaf of bread (save bread to cube for dipping) or generously buttered four cup baking dish. Wrap bread loaf in foil or cover dish. Bake at 325Â°F for 2 hours. Remove foil/lid.  Stir top to blend in browned edges. Serve with bread, crackers or bagettes.
