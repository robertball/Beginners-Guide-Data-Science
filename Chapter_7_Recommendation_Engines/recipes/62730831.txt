Title:
Spicy Slow Cooker Black Bean Soup II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dry black beans, soaked overnight
2 slices bacon, chopped
1 large onion, chopped
5 cloves garlic, chopped
2 jalapeno peppers, chopped (seeds included)
6 cups chicken broth
1 can diced tomatoes (15 ounce can)
1 tablespoon chili powder
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
1.  Soak beans overnight.  The next day drain and then rinse the black beans.
2.  In a small skillet saute the diced bacon.  Set aside, but do not discard the drippings.
3.  Combine beans, onion, garlic, jalapenos, bacon, bacon drippings, tomatoes, and chicken broth in a slow cooker. Season with chili powder, cumin, cayenne, salt, and pepper.
4.  Cook on High for 3 hours. Reduce heat to Low, and continue cooking for 3-4 hours.
