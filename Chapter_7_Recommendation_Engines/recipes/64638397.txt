Title:
Loaded Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads of cauliflower
4 tbsp heavy cream
2 - 8oz blocks cream cheese, softened
8 oz shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 tbsp chives
1/4 c real bacon bits
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Steam cauliflower florets until tender, about 10 minutes in micro steamer.  Mash well with a potato masher.  Mix cauliflower and heavy cream with hand mixer until pieces are very fine.  Mix in cream cheese.  Mix in sharp cheddar, chives and bacon bits.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Taste for seasoning, and add as necessary.  Pour into a casserole dish and sprinkle with cheddar and chives.  Bake for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kisha McDaniel Logsdon
Description: 
Loaded Cauliflower Casserole is unbelievably tasty, full of fantastic flavor, and tastes like loaded potatoes, but without all the carbs! Cauliflower, bacon, cheese, and chives make a wonderful combination!
