Title:
tracy's sausage balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sausage, cooked and drained
1 pound cheddar cheese, shredded
3 cups biscuit mix

Directions:
Combine sausage, cheese, and biscuit mix.  Roll into balls.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes, until golden brown.  You can freeze these unbaked and remove from the freezer the amount you wish to bake.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
I cook my sausage before making into sausage balls.  They don't turn out as greasy, but still taste great.
