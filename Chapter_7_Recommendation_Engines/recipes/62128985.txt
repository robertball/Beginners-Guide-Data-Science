Title:
Roses & Cream Cinnamon Swirl Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 - 8oz pkg cream cheese, softened
3 Tbsp powdered sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp vanilla
Cake:
2 c flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 c butter, margarine or shortening
1 c sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 c sour cream OR 6 oz cream cheese, soft and 3 T milk
Topping:
2 T sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
Rose Petal Jam
1/2 lb. rose petals
2 c sugar
3 c water
juice of 2 lemons
1 box sure jel or similar
Trim white part from each petal place petals in bowl
cover with enough of the sugar to cover leave overnight.
Place remainder of suger and the water in a medium pan
heat until sugar dissolves, add petal mixture and simmer
20 min. Bring to a boil and boil for 5 minutes, add the
sure jel and boil an additional minute. Pour into
clean warm jars, seal, store in a cool place.

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
Spray a bundt pan with pan coating(PAM)
Prepare filling. Set aside.
Prepare cake as follows
Mix butter, margarine or shortening with sugar till light and fluffy
add eggs i at a time beat well after each
add vanilla
Mix together dry ingredients and add alternately with the sour cream till well blended.
Pour half of the batter into the prepared pan
Spoon the filling mixture to within 1/2" of the edge of batter
top filling mix with rose petal jam
pour remaining batter over
Sprinkle cinnamon sugar mix over top
Bake for 40-45 min.
Cool 10 minutes in pan Turn out on rack cool completely
Dust with powdered sugar

Submitter: 
Crystal McChesney
Description: 
Traditional coffee cake with a cream cheese and rose filling with a slight cinnamon swirl. Amazing!!
