Title:
Eggless Peanut Butter Chocolate Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 1/3 cups oatmeal
1 1/2 cups sugar
2/3 cup cocoa
2 tbsp baking powder
1 cup peanut butter
2 cups milk
Chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Prepare muffin pan with liners. Mix together flour, oatmeal, sugar, cocoa and baking powder.  In a saucepan, melt peanut butter with milk stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.  Allow peanut butter mixture to cool for 5 min.  Once cool, add the peanut butter/milk mixture to the dry ingredients and mix just until combined.  Spoon batter into cups, 2/3 full.  Bake for 13 min or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
