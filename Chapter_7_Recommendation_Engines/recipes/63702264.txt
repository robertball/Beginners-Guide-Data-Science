Title:
Quick & Easy Tuna & Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c. egg noodles
1 can cream of mushroom soup-reduced fat is fine
1/2 can milk-skim is fine
1/4c. butter or Smart Balance buttery spread
1c. frozen peas
1 can tuna, drained
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook noodles per pkg. directions-add peas last 5 min.
Drain & add remaining. Stir & heat till warm.
Opt: use cut broccoli or asparagus instead of peas. Add more or less vegies &/or milk & butter.

Submitter: 
mamaG
Description: 
It's been a family favorite, since  I started making it when I was  12 & learning to cook. This is the only way my Dad would eat fish.
