Title:
Bad Day Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger amaretto liqueur
4 fluid ounces champagne

Directions:
Pour the amaretto into a champagne flute. Top with the champagne. Serve with a lemon twist.
