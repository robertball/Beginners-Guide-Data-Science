Title:
Instant Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5025757.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 cup low sodium chicken broth
2 celery stalks, cut in half
Small white potatoes
Baby carrots
Seasoned salt

Directions:
Pour the chicken broth into the Instant Pot. Add the steamer rack. Sprinkle seasoned salt on both sides of the chicken; place on top of steamer rack. Add potatoes, carrots and celery. Close the lid and set to "pressure" and "chicken".

Submitter: 
Melaniemg2000
Description: 
Boneless skinless chicken breasts come out tender and juicy in just 25 minutes.
