Title:
peanut crumbles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups sugar
2/3 cup milk
1/2 cup peanut butter
3 cups crushed crackers (i.e saltines, graham crackers)

Directions:
Combine sugar and milk in saucepan.  Bring to a boil and boil for one minute.  Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter until smooth.  Stir in crackers of your choice.  Pour onto a slightly greased cookie sheet or cake pan and spread out.  Allow to cool, stirring occasionally to break up.  Store in an airtight container.  Yum!!!
