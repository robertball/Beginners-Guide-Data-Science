Title:
Parmesan Garlic Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 chicken wings, cut and tips discarded
1 1/2 cups parmesan cheese grated
1 tsp dried parsley
3 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp white pepper
1 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease broiler pan
In a wide shallow bowl, combine cheese, parsley, garlic powder, and white pepper. Mix Well.
Dip wings into melted butter, then coat in cheese/spice mixture.
Arrange on broiler pan and bake uncovered for 1 hour.
Serve hot

Submitter: 
Nia
Description: 
These wings are a staple in my household. You can adjust the seasoning however you like and after baked hold up in a crockpot on low if needed.
