Title:
Whipped Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy whipping cream
3 T. powdered sugar

Directions:
Whip cream with whisk attachment in KitchenAid mixer until foamy.
Add sugar, continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
