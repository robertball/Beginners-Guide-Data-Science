Title:
Shish Kebab with Ginger Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Beef cubes
Onions
Green or Red Peppers
Mushrooms
Tomatoes
Marinade:
1/4 cup soya sauce
1/4 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup sherry or red wine
1/8 cup olive oil
1 tbsp grated ginger root or 1 tsp dry ginger root
Or substitute wine for 1/8 cup olive oil and 1/8 cup balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Marinate meat for 12-14 hours.
Put meat and vegetables on skewer and cook for approximately 10 minutes on each side.
Sauce: Heat marinade to a boil for approximately 10 minutes.
