Title:
Poppyseed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 Chicken Breasts, cooked and diced
1 8 ounce container of sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 T. poppy seeds
1/4 c. butter, melted
1 roll of ritz crackers

Directions:
Mix soup, sour cream and poppy seeds til blended.  Add chopped chicken and stir until chicken is well coated.  Put in a casserole dish.  Smash crackers in plastic it came in and combine in a small bowl with the melted butter.  Stir until crackers are coated.  Sprinkle over chicken and soup mixture and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

Chicken in a mild cream sauce guaranteed to please picky eaters.
