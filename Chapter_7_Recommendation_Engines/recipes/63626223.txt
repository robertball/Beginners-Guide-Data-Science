Title:
Cookies and Cream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 regular containers of whipped topping, thawed
3 regular packages of instant vanilla pudding
3/4 package of Oreo (or similar) cookies, chopped in food processor to roughly a ground coffee consistency.

Directions:
Blend topping and pudding until well mixed.
Add chopped cookies and blend well.

Submitter: 
JonG
Description: 
Super easy and not too heavy. This was an experiment to go on a devil's food cake for my daughter. She does not like most frostings but loved this one.
