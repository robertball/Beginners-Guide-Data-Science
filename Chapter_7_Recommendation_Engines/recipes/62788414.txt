Title:
Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. water
1 c. white wine
3 T. cornstarch
3 T. water
1 lb. swiss cheese, grated
1/4 t. baking soda

Directions:
Put water and wine in fondue pot and heat on HI until bubbles start to rise. Add cheese by thirds, stirring constantly until all cheese is melted. When mixture starts to bubble, quickly add mixture of cornstarch and water, stirring constantly until mixture  is heated and bubbly. Add baking soda, mix well.
