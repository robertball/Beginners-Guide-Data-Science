Title:
Veggie Rose Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 tbsp vegetable oil
2 Portabello Mushrooms (sliced)
1 Sweet Red Pepper (strips)
2 Cups Chopped Brocolli and/or Cauliflower
1 Cup Heavy Cream
2 Cups Spaghetti Sauce
1 tsp each dried oregano & parsley
Fresh ground pepper to taste
Red pepper flakes (optional)
4-5 cups of your favorite pasta (bowties, spirals, etc)

Directions:
1.  Saute vegetables in oil until tender-crisp.
2.  Add cream and dry seasonings to vegetables and let boil until thickened, stirring frequently.
3.  Meanwhile, cook pasta until al dente.
4.  Add spaghetti sauce to cream/veggie mixture and stir well.  Cook for additional 3-5 minutes or until sauce is hot.
5.  Stir cooked pasta into the sauce mixture and Enjoy!!!
***try adding a variety of seasonal vegetables including asparagus, white mushrooms, green peppers, etc.

Submitter: 
Andrea Longo Policella
Description: 
Pasta with a wide variety of veggies in and smooth mushroom rose sauce. The portabello mushrooms really add a great flavor to the sauce!  My family requests this one all the time!
