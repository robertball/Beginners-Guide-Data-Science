Title:
Corn and Broccoli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz creamed corn
10 oz frozen chopped broccoli
1/2 cup saltines,  crushed
1 egg, beaten
1 tbsp dried minced onion
Dash pepper
2 tbsp melted butter
Shredded cheddar

Directions:
In bowl,  combine corn and broccoli, 1/4 cup saltines,  egg, onion, pepper and cheese. Place in greased baking dish. Combine butter and remaining saltines,  sprinkle on top. Cover and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
