Title:
Tummy yummy muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups blueberrys
1 cup strawberry and/or blueberry yogurt
1 egg
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup strawberry-apple sauce
1 tbsp vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 cup bran
1 cup whole wheat flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In Mixer mix all dry ingredients and then wet. Drop mixture into muffin tin . Cook 25-30 minutes at 350F
