Title:
Bulgarian Moussaka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3201492.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb ground beef (85% lean)
3/4 lb ground pork
6 russet potatoes peeled and chopped small
1/2 yellow onion chopped small
1 large carrot chopped small
1 red bell pepper chopped small
2 celery sticks chopped small
1 cans of diced tomatoes
1/4 bunch of parsley stems and leaves chopped separately
1/2 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons paprika
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 bay leaves
Ingredients for topping:
2 eggs
2 cups plain yogurt
1/4 cup of flour
1 teaspoons baking soda

Directions:
In a large frying pan, brown meat and drain fat
Add 1/4 cup olive oil, onions, carrots, celery, parsley stems (save leaves for later) and tomatoes
Add bell pepper, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, paprika and bay leaves
Cook on medium-high heat for about 10 minutes or until the veggies soften
Preheat to 400 degrees
Take out mixture and place in large baking pan (don't bake yet)
Add remaining 1/4 cup olive oil to bottom of the pan and cook potatoes in the frying pan until lightly browned
Move potatoes to the baking pan and mix well with the rest of the ingredients
Bake at 400 degrees for 45 minutes
Take out baking pan, mix the remaining parsley into it
Mix eggs, yogurt, flour and baking soda into a spreadable mixture then pour over the pan with the moussaka to completely cover the dish
Put back in the oven and cook for another 15 minutes. When the cover is golden brown, its ready to come put.
