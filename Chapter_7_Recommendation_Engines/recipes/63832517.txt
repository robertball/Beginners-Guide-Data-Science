Title:
Pumpkin Crunch Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz. can pumpkin
1 12oz. can evaporated milk
1 1/2 C. sugar
3 eggs
1 1/2 t. cinnamon
1 Yellow Cake Mix
1 C. melted butter

Directions:
Set cake mix and melted butter aside.....Combine rest of ingredients and pour into greased 9Ã—13 pan. Sprinkle cake mix on top of pumpkin mixture and pour melted butter over top. Bake at 350 for 1 hour and 10 minutes. This is what it will look like fresh out of the oven.
