Title:
Shrim po' boy with crunchy slaw and lemon aloili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
marinate 1-1/2 pounds of thawed shrimp in 1 lemon, 1/4 vodka and 1/4 t cayenne pepper
slaw
2 cups store-bought coleslaw
2 T vegetable oil
1 t lemon juice
1/4 t salt and pepper
about 8 cups of vegetable oil for frying
1 egg
4 t each of oregano and thyme
1-1/2 t garlic powder nd cayenne
1 t salt
1/4 c flour
1 cup panko crumbs
2 t lemon juice
1/3 c mayo
1 baguette
1 tomato

Directions:
place shrimp in bowl and marinate for two hours (optional)
meanwhile, place coleslaw in a medium bowl toss with oik, lemon juice, salt and pepper - cover and refrigerate until ready to use
drain dhrimp, placing marinate in a medium bowl.  whisk egg into marinade.  in a small bowl, place stir spices and salt.  in a large bowl place flour and 1 T of spice mixture, balance for panko crumbs.  Toss shrimp into flour to coat, shaking off excess, then into egg mixture, then panko - place shrimp on baking sheet until all shrimp are complete
Once oil is hot, it will bubble when shrimp are added - add one-third to one-half, don't overcrowds, cook until golden brown, about three minutes, repeat with remaining shrimp
stir garlic and lemon juice into mayo
split the baguette in half lengthwise, keeping one side attached, spread both sides with aioli, top base with tomato slices, followed by shrimp, then slaw
serve with hot sauce
