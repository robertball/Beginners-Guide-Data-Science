Title:
Balsamic chicken sausage and potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken pieces
1 lb. sausage (browned and cut into bite size pieces
6 potatoes THINLY sliced
2 Cups. Parm. cheese
2 onions sliced
balsamic vinegar
fresh garlic, onion powder, garlic powder and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour enough vinegar into a roasting pan to make about a quarter inch puddle.  Place meat, potatoes, onions and seasonings in pan.  Add half the cheese. Stir until meat and potatoes are well coated with vinegar and cheese. Bake at 400, covered, for 20 mins. Lower to 350 and cook for another 20 mins. Uncover, sprinkle remaining cheese on top of mixture and cook for an additional 20 mins.

Description: 
A delicious one pot crowd pleaser with chicken, sausage, potatoes, balsamic vinegar and parmesan cheese
