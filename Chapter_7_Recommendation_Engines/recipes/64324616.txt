Title:
Sesame Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon soy sauce, less sodium, plus 1 teaspoons
3/8 teaspoon oil, dark sesame
1/2 clove(s) garlic, small, minced
salt, to salt water
1/2 pounds beans, green, trimmed
6 medium tomato(es), cherry, halved

Directions:
Whisk together soy sauce, oil, and garlic in a large serving bowl.
Bring a saucepan of salted water to a boil. Add beans and cook until tender, about 2 minutes. Drain and run under cold water for 30 seconds. Pat dry. Add beans and tomatoes to soy sauce mixture and toss to combine. Serve at room temperature or refrigerate until ready to serve.
