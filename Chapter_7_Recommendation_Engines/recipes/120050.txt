Title:
Honey-Mustard Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978559.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free plain yogurt
1/3 cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
4 1/2 tablespoons white wine vinegar or cider vinegar

Directions:
In a bowl, whisk together all ingredients. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Terri  Webber
Description: 
Terri Webber of Miami, Florida pairs sweet honey with zippy Dijon mustard in this quick-to-fix recipe. The creamy yellow blend nicely coats mixed salad greens with a mildly tangy flavor. 'No one would ever guess this is low in fat,' Terri confides.
