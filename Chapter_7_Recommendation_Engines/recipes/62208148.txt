Title:
Cucumber dill salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 English hothouse cucumbers
1 Tbsp kosher salt
1/2 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 cup fresh dill finely chopped
3 Tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Slice the cucumber very thinly with a mandolin.
Put in a colander, sprinkle with salt and toss to coat. Put the colander in a bowl to catch the excess liquid.
Let sit 15 minutes, stir occasionally.
Mix the other ingredients and stir to disolve sugar. I mix them in a casserole/serving dish.
Drain the cucumbers and pat dry.
Add cucumbers to the dressing and stir.
Refrigerate 1-2 hours, stir occasionally) Serve Cold
