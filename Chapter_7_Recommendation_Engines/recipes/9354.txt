Title:
Frozen Christmas Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup sultana raisins
1/2 cup dried currants
1/4 cup candied cherries, chopped
1/4 cup candied mixed fruit peel
1/4 cup fruit juice
1/4 cup almonds
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
4 1/4 cups chocolate ice cream, softened

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine fruit with fruit juice and spices. Cover, and allow to stand overnight.
The next day mix together soaked fruits, almonds, cream, and ice cream.  Pour mixture into a large mold, and cover with foil.  Freeze for at least one week to allow flavour to develop.
Unmold by quickly dipping into hot water, and inverting onto serving plate.
