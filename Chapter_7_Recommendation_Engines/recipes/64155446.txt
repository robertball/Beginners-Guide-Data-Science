Title:
One Pan Chicken Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1413115.jpg
Ingredients:
3 boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 cans of green beans
6 red skinned potatoes
1 packet of zesty italian dressing mix
1 stick of butter

Directions:
In a 9x13 pan, cut the chicken breasts in half and lay down the middle
On one side of the chicken, add 2 cans of drained green beans
Cut up the red skinned potatoes and add them to the other side of the chicken
Sprinkle the packet of zesty italian dressing mix over the top of everything
Melt the stick of butter and drizzle it over everything
Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350 degrees for an hour
