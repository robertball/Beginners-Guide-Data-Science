Title:
Citrus Frost Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983505.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, undrained
1 cup orange juice
1/3 cup sugar
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/4 cups vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Place the first five ingredients in a blender; cover and process on high speed until smooth. Add ice cream and process until blended. Pour into chilled glasses. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
John  Eberhardt
Description: 
This creamy drink is refreshing any time of the year, but especially on hot and humid days. It's easy to prepare and very flavorful. -John Eberhardt, Jamestown, North Dakota
