Title:
Tomato and Cucumber Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium tomato(es) chopped
1 medium cucumber(s) chopped
salt to taste
peppercorns, black freshly ground, to taste

Directions:
Combine chopped cucumber and tomato in a bowl. Toss to mix. Season with salt, if using and pepper and divide onto 2 serving plates.
