Title:
Maple Asparagus Mini Frittatas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large eggs
1/4 cup milk
salt & pepper to taste
1/4 cup grated swiss cheese
3 spears asparagus, lightly steamed
4 tsp real maple syrup
1/4 onion, chopped small
pam cooking oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*F and grease a 6 cup muffin tin with pam cooking spray.
In a medium bowl, mix the eggs, milk, maple syrup, and salt & pepper.  Chop the asparaus into small pieces.  Grate the swiss cheese finely.
Put 1/2 of an asparagus spear in each muffin cup and approx 1 tsp onion.  Fill the cups with egg, approx 1/4 cup each.  Sprinkle each with the cheese.  Bake for 25 minutes, or until the frittatas are golden and puffed.
Remove promptly and let cool only a few mintues, as they will deflate, like a souffle.
This recipe freezes exceoptionally well.  Place in a ziploc bag and freeze.  To reheat, remove from freezer and micorwave on high for 1 minute adding time as needed to heat through.
