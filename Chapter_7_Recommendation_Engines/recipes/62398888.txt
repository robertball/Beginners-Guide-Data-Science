Title:
World's Best Blueberry Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c softened butter
1 3/4 c  plus 2 T sugar
6 eggs
1 T vanilla
5 1/4 c all purpose flour
2 T baking powder
2 t salt
1 1/2 c milk
3 c blueberries
2 T flour
Topping:
2 t flour
1/3 c flour
2 T softened butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Beat softened butter on low until creamy. Gradually add in sugar, beating on medium. beat in eggs, one at a time and vanilla. Combine flour, baking powder and salt. Alternately beat in flour and milk. Toss blueberries with 2 T flour, fold into batter. Divide batter among 24 greased muffin cups.
Combine flour, sugar butter to form crumbs for topping. Sprinkle over muffins. Bake 35 to 45 minutes, until cooked through.
