Title:
enchiritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
2 pkgs taco seasoning
1 onion
1 large can refried beans
flour tortillas
2 cans el paso enchilada sauce
shredded cheese to cover

Directions:
*brown 2 lbs ground beef with onion
*mix in taco seasoning with water as per instructions on the package (I use a little less water then what it calls for to keep from being soupy)
*mix in refried beans
*put mixture in flour tortillas and roll into burritos
*place in glass baking dish
*cover with enchilada sauce
*bake at 350 for 25 min
*cover with cheese and bake for another 5 min
