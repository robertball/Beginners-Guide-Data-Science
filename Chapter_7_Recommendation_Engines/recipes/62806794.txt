Title:
Quinoa Salad with Black Beans, Corn and Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa, cooked with broth
2 cups chicken broth
1/15 oz can black beans drained & rinsed
1/15 oz can of corn kernals, drained
1 large avocado, cut into 1/2" pieces
1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup red onion, finely diced
3/4 cup cilantro salad dressing
1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
Zest of 1 lime
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook quinoa with broth according to package directions.
While quinoa is cooking, combine beans, corn, avocado, tomatoes & onion.
Top with salad dressing and gently toss.
Add salt & pepper and lime zest to taste.
Add 1/2 of cilantro and gently toss again and set aside.
When quinoa is cooked, toss with olive oil then salt & pepper to taste.  Set aside to cool.
When ready to serve, spread quinoa on a large serving platter and top with corn/bean/avocado mixture.
Garnish with remaining cilantro.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Avocado, corn, black beans, cilantro dressing and quinoa create a flavorful medley with a nice texture.
