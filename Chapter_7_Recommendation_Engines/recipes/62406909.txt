Title:
Caper Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T red wine vinegar
2 T olive iol
1/2 t Dijon mustard
1/4 t fresh ground pepper
1/4 t salt
1 garlic clove
2 t capers, coarsly chopped

Directions:
Combine salt, vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper. Add oil to the vinegar mixture, stirring constantly witha  wisk. Stir in capers.
