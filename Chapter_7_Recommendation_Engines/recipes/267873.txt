Title:
Three-Ingredient Keto Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5689622.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
5 fluid ounces heavy cream
2 cups finely grated Parmesan cheese
1 pinch freshly grated nutmeg, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir with a wooden spoon and add heavy cream. Stir until combined.
Add Parmesan cheese to the cream mixture in the saucepan and stir until blended. Season with nutmeg.
