Title:
Race Day Buffalo Chicken Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breast halves, cooked
3 TBL Butter, melted
1/2 cup Hot Sauce
1/2 tsp Dry Ranch mix
1 sheet Puff Pastry, thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Coat a baking sheet with cooking spray.
In a medium bowl, shred chicken.  Add butter, hot sauce, and ranch mix; stir well.
Unfold puff pastry onto a lightly floured surface.  Use a little flour to gently cover any wet spots on top surface.  Spread chicken mixture evenly over sheet leaving a 1/4 inch space at edges.
Roll like a jelly-roll.  Pinch seam to seal.
Cut into 1/2 inch slices and place onto prepared pan.  Bake 15 minutes, until pastry is golden brown.

Description: 
It's a family tradition to have appitizers on raceday. My husband was hungry for buffalo wings one day but I didn't want to deal with the mess.  I had puff pastry thawed that needed to be used so I came up with this recipe.
