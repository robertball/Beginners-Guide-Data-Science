Title:
Asian Beef Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4 cup catsup
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 teaspoon hot sauce (such as Tobasco)
1 can (10 oz.)beef broth

Directions:
Mix ingredients and place in a sealable bag with beef. Refrigerate, turning occasionally to ensure all meat is in marinade, for at least an hour or overnight. You can also use the marinade to baste the beef while cooking. Discard leftover marinade.

Submitter:
