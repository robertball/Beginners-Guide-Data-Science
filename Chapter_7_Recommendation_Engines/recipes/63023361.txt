Title:
Greek Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Mayonnaise
3-4 tablespoon Olive Juice
2 tablespoon Minced Garlic
6oz Feta Cheese divided
8oz Baby Spinach
1 tablespoon Olive Oil
1/4 cup Red Onion Sliced Thin
1 Tomatoe sliced thin
2oz Klamata Olives
1 1/2 cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese.

Directions:
Mix the Mayo, olive juice, minced garlic, 1/3 of the feta cheese together in a small bowl to make your sauce. Spread that onto your pizza crust then mix together in another bowl the spinach, and olive oil then layer that onto the pizza then add the onion, tomato, olives, then the rest of the feta and Mozzarella.
