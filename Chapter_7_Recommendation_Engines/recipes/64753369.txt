Title:
White country Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons Fat (grease, could be bacon or sausage  grease or  just any type of  cooking oil, it's up to you)
1/3 cup of non-dairy creamer power
1 cup of water
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Take  1/3 cup of non-dairy creamer powder and whip with 1 cup of water, Set aside.
In a small skillet on medium heat,  add 3 tablespoons Fat, when heated, add your flour and mix until lightly brown in skillet,
Take the 1 cup that you set aside and slowly pour while stiring mixture at all times, cook until thickened.
Add in Salt and Pepper to Taste
If it is too thick, then add a little more water with creamer until it is how you like it smooth again.

Submitter: 
Chris
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by a cook who worked in cafes all of her life, My son loved her gravy, and this is how it was made. ( granted I had to reduce the serving size) ! It's good on anything you use county gravy with.
