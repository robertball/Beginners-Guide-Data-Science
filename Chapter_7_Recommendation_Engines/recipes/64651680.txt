Title:
Sweet Potato Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4540487.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large sweet potatoes, cooked ( 2 cups)
2 large eggs
1/4 cup goat cheese, crumbled
1 tablespoon rosemary, minced
1 tablespoon coconut milk
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 teaspoon sea salt
3/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
2-3 tablespoons pecans, crushed
Coconut oil, melted for coating tins

Directions:
Heat the oven to 400Â°F and lightly brush the cups of a regular muffin tin, or a mini-muffin tin with coconut oil.
In a large mixing bowl add cooked sweet potatoes and if they are not mash then do that now with a fork.
Add eggs, goat cheese, rosemary, coconut milk, butter, salt and pepper. Combine all ingredients together.
Put a spoonful of the mixture in each muffin cup. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the sweet potato cups are set, browned on top, and hot through and through.
Remove  and enjoy immediately.
Note: If this is not a leftover recipe then here are 2 ways to prepare  your sweet potato:  Clean them first!

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
This is a great recipe to use up some of your Thanksgiving left overs! You can make this recipe by using up all the leftover sweet potatoes and combine it with simple ingredients, which are found in many refrigerators. Enjoy!
