Title:
Baked Turkey Wings, And Cauliflower With Poached Potatoes And Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/699543.jpg
Ingredients:
2 turkey wings
3 potatoes
1/4 cup chicken stock
1 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon pepper
4 oz mushrooms (optional)

Directions:
Slice Potatoes
Lay potatoes at the bottom of a casserole pan
add mushrooms (optional)
add chicken stock
Season: Salt And Pepper
Season turkey wings: Salt And Pepper
place turky wings on top of the potatoes
Cook At 400F For 60 Min's Uncovered
