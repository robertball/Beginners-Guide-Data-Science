Title:
Healthy Vegan Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package dry active yeast
1 cup warm water
2 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
4 heads fresh garlic (peeled, but not crushed)
2 cups olive oil
1 teaspoon marjoram
1 teaspoon sage
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon basil
1 cup nutritional yeast (flakes or powder)
3 cups smooth tomato or pasta sauce
1/2 small red onion
1 large avocado
other toppings

Directions:
Set oven to 350
A few hours before meal-time, let yeast, warm water and sugar set for a few minutes.
Incorporate flour, using more or less as needed until doughy consistency is achieved
Knead and cover with towel and set aside.
Place peeled garlic cloves in oven-safe container
Bathe in olive oil and add spices
Place in oven until garlic cloves are soft, but not crisp
Remove garlic cloves from oven and let cool
Use the cooled olive oil and spices to rub a large cookie sheet
Spread out dough to desired thickness (will not rise very much)
Brush more of the oil oil and spice mix over the dough
Ina separate bowl, use a garlic press or fork to mash the garlic cloves
Add the nutritional yeast to the garlic puree, if dry add some of the olive oil and spice mix
Mix tomato sauce into the nutritional yeast and garlic mix
Spread out sauce mix over dough
Top off with thin slices of red onion
Bake for 10 -20 minutes or until dough is crunchy
Remove from the oven and add pieces of avocado
