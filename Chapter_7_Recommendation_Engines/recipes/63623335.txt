Title:
THE BEST MACARONI AND CHEESE EVER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of elbow macaroni
4 slices of American cheese

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to directions on box.  Once cooked, drain the water and put the pasta back on the stove.  Add the cheese, stir it, and once it is melted, take it off the heat. Serve and enjoy!
