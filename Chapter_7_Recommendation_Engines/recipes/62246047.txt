Title:
Baked Butter Herb Perch Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb yellow perch
1/2 cup butter
2/3 cup crushed saltine crackers
1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 teaspoon basil leaves
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt the butter.
Combine cracker crumbs,cheese, basil, oregano, salt, and garlic powder in a bowl.
Dip the perch in to butter, then coat in crumb mixture.
Arrange on a cooking sheet.
Bake for 25-30 minutes until fish flakes easily with a fork.
