Title:
Fruit Smoothies from EAGLE BRAND速 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983154.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND速 Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 (8 ounce) container strawberry banana yogurt
1 small banana, cut up
1 cup frozen or fresh whole strawberries
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple packed with juice, chilled
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 cup ice cubes
Halved fresh strawberries (optional)

Directions:
Chill can of sweetened condensed milk. In blender container, combine sweetened condensed milk, yogurt, banana, whole strawberries, pineapple with its juice and lemon juice; cover and blend until smooth. With blender running, gradually add ice cubes, blending until smooth. Garnish with halved strawberries if desired. Serve immediately.
