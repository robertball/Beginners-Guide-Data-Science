Title:
Lemon Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c flour
3/4 sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 c butter, softened
2 large eggs
2 tbsp lemon zest
2 tbsp lemon juice
Glaze...
4 tbsp lemon juice
8 tsp lemon zest
1 c powdered sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat flour, sugar, salt and butter until combined.
In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, lemon zest and lemon juice until continued.
Pour lemon mixture into batter and beat until smooth and creamy, approximately two minutes.
Pour into greased 8x8 inch baking dish and bake 23-25 minutes at 350 degrees. Do not overbake. Bars should turn golden around the edges. Allow to cool.
Mix glaze and spread over brownies. Allow to set before cutting.
