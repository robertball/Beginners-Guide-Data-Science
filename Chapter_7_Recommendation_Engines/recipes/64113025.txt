Title:
Peachy Almond Panini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices whole-grain bread
4 tsp almond butter
1/4 peach, sliced
1/2 tsp oil

Directions:
Spread each slice of bread with 2 tsp of almond butter.
Place peach slices on top of the almond butter of one slice of bread. Top with the other slice.
Spritz sandwich with the oil.
Grill in a preheated panini press on medium-high until toasted or in a skillet over medium-high heat.
