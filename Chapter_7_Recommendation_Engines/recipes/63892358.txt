Title:
Microwave Toasted Pine Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pine nuts
1 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions:
Place the pine nuts in a small, fine mesh strainer and rinse under cold water.  Toss with the salt.  Transfer the nuts to a small brown paper bag, fold over the bag and microwave on high for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes.  Remove the bag from the microwave and set aside to cool for 1 minute.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
Easier and very quick compared to oven roasting
