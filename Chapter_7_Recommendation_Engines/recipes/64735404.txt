Title:
Rustic Tart - Apple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds of apples,  peeled, cored, sliced
1/4 teaspoon orange zest
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon allspice
4 Tablespoons butter, cold

Directions:
Preheat at 450 degrees
Pare apples and layer on 1 pie crust
Combine all other ingredients in food processor
Sprinkle over apples
Fold over edges
