Title:
Bill's Cabbage with Portobello Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 medium head cabbage - cut into thick, short strips
1 large red onion thickly sliced, coarsely chopped
5 cloves of garlic, pressed
4 Portobello mushroom caps, thickly sliced
kosher salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
red pepper flakes to taste
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt the butter with the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Stir in the cabbage, onion and garlic in the butter and saute until soft, about 10 minutes
Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper.
Add the mushrooms; continue cooking, stirring frequently, until mushrooms are tender.
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Portobello mushrooms lend an earthy and rich flavor to this simple cabbage dish.Modified from: Cabbage with Portobello Mushrooms
