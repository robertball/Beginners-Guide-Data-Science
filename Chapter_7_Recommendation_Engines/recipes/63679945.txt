Title:
Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 large fresh whole baby bella or cremini mushrooms
1/4 cup sliced green onions
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2/3 cup fine dry breadcrumbs
1 egg, beaten
1/2 teaspoon dried dill
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
3/4 pound Homemade Turkey Sausage

Directions:
Crumble and brown sausage.  Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
Remove stems from mushrooms and chop.
In same pan that sausage was cooked in, cook stems and onions in butter until tender but not brown.
Add bread crumbs, egg, dill, salt and Worcestershire.
Fill mushroom crowns with onion mixture; place on baking sheet.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 5-10 minutes (depending on size of mushrooms).
