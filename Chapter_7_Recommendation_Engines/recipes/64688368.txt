Title:
Keto Carnitas in Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs bone in pork shoulder or butt
1 medium yellow onion, sliced
7-8 garlic cloves
1.5 T cumin
1.5 T oregano
1/2 T cinnamon
1 tsp or more smoked paprika
1/2 T sea salt
1/2 T smoked salt
dash or more of chili powder
dash of chipotle
few grinds of black pepper
1/3 c lime juice (1.5 limes)
1/2 orange, juiced (to make 1/2 c lime and orange juice)
little orange zest (maybe 1/2 tsp)
2 T apple cider vinegar
1/2 c beef stock (if desired or to make more sauce)

Directions:
put sliced onion and chopped garlic in bottom of crock pot
combine all spices
rub spices over all sides of pork shoulder
put pork shoulder in crock pot
pour lime and orange juice, apple cider vinegar and beef stock into bottom of crock pot (don't rinse off spices)
cover and let marinade overnight in refrigerator
cook on low for 12-14 hours
after cooking, shred (and remove fat if desired) some of the meat and broil for 3-5 min if desired; add more sauce and broil 3 min more until crispy -- watch carefully
and/or shred and put back into crockpot
keep the fat
tastes even better next day heated in oven at 400
serve with sauce, cilantro, avocado/guacamole, cheese, red cabbage, lime, sour cream, or other favorite toppings
