Title:
Zuchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 c oil
2 c sugar
2 c chopped or shredded zuchini (not too fine or it gets too wet)
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp ginger
2c flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
3 tsp vanilla
1/2 c chopped nuts, roasted for extra flavor

Directions:
Beat the eggs.  Add sugar, oil, and zuchini.
Mix the dry ingredients and combine the wet ingredients with the dry.
Add the vanilla and the nuts. Mix by hand and then pour into two greased and floured loaf pans.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
Great moist bread. It never lasts very long in our house!
