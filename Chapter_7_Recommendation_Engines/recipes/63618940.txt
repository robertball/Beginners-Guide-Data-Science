Title:
Roast Chicken with Curry Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 tbs Patak's Curry Paste
3 tsp olive oil
1  4 lb chicken (Whole)
1 small onion cut into quarters

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degress
In a small bowl mix curry paste (I used Mild Douce Curry Paste it is what I had) with the oil and set aside.
Wash and dry chicken.
With fingers carefully lift but do not removed the skin from the breast and legs of the chicken.   Rub the inside of the chicken then under the skin with the paste mixture then add the onion.  Then rub the paste under the skin. With the balance of the paste rub it all over the chicken.
Place on trivet in roasting pan with some water and roast for about 2 hours or until chicken is cooked.

Submitter: 
Nadine
Description: 
This is a very simple change for a Roast Chicken Dinner.   You can even add the vegetables (carrots, Potatoes etc) and the whole meal is ready.
