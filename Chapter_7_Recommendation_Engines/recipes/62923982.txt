Title:
Chops with Curried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713943.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
1 chicken bouillon
1 cup boiled water
3 Tbsp.butter
3 Tbsp.flour
2 tsp.curry powder
1 1/2 cups milk
2 tsp.ketchup
salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp.butter
1 tsp.curry powder
1 cup long grain rice
2 1/2 cups. boiling water
1 tsp. salt
1 cup frozen peas OR 1/2 cup lentils

Directions:
Brown chops in large skillet.Add bouillon cube dissolved in 1 cup boiling water.
Lower heat and simmer 30-45 minutes untill chops are tender.Turn once.
Meanwhile,melt 1 Tbsp.butter in sauce pan.Add 1 tsp.curry powder and rice,stir well till coated.Add 2 1/2 cups of boiling water and 1 tsp.salt.
Bring rice to a boil,if using lentils add now,cover,simmer over low heat for 15 minutes.Add frozen peas,cover,cook untill rice and peas are done and liquid is absorbed,about 10 minutes.
Remove chops from broth keep warm,drain liquid and save.
Melt 3 Tbsp.butter in same pan.Stir in flour and curry powder.Stir for 1 minute.
Gradually whisk in milk and the saved broth.Stir constantly till thickened.Stir in ketchup,salt and pepper to taste.
Strain and serve over rice and chops.
