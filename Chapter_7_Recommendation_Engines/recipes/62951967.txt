Title:
Pulled Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frank's red hot sauce
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup worcestershire sauce
1 cup chili sauce I use my own homemade version
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Place desired meat in crockpot, cover with sauce and let cook all day until meat is tender and shreds easily.

Submitter: 
Tina Pendergraph
Description: 
Use on chicken, pork or beef for shredded barbecue.  Equally delicious!
