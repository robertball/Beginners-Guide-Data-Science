Title:
cheesy sausage dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Smaller Pkg. of Velveeta Cheese
1/2 roll of frozen sausage thawed.
Jar of Salsa

Directions:
Cook sausage and drain. Add cheese and salsa and cook until cheese is completely melted. Serve with tortilla chips.
