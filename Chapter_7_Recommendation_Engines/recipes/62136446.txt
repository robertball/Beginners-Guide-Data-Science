Title:
Veggie Bulgur Salad (KISIR) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fine bulgur
1 cup boiling water
2 finely chopped big tomatoes
1 finely chopped cucumber
1 finely chopped onion
2 finely chopped green peppers
1 finely chopped red pepper
6-8 finely chopped spring onions
1/2 cup minced fresh parsley
1/2 cup minced fresh mint
1 lemon's juice
2 tablespoons olive oil
1-2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (to taste)
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons sour pomegranate sauce (you can substitute balsamic vinegar)

Directions:
Place bulgur in a bowl; stir in the boiling water. Cover and let stand for 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a pan, fry the finely chopped onion until they get tender and pinkish.
Drain bulgur and combine all the ingredients in a large bowl with the bulgur. Toss gently until they are well mixed. Serve or refrigerate.
