Title:
Lemony Lentil Soup with Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/791739.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp canola oil
1 small onion, chopped
1 large carrot, peeled and chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
16 oz green or brown lentils
12 cups low-sodium chicken broth, plus more as needed
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp salt
8 cups chopped kale leaves (about 6 oz)
3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp finely grated lemon zest

Directions:
Heat oil in a pot over medium high and cook onion until translucent.  Add carrot, celery and garlic and cook, covered, stirring for 5 minutes or until carrots are soft.  Add 12 cups of broth, lentils, and seasoning.  Bring to a boi, reduce to low and add kale.  Cook 30 to 35 minutes or until lentils are tender adding broth if necessary.  Stirin lemon juice and top with grated peel before serving.
