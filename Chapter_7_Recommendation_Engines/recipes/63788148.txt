Title:
Brunzie's Best Egg Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
9 Hard Boiled Eggs; 1/2 Cup Mayo; 1 Teaspoon Mustard; 2 Tablespoons Dill Relish; 1/3 Cup Chopped Green Onion; 1/3 Cup Chopped Celery; 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Salt; 1/4 Teaspoon Paprika; 1/4 Teaspoon Sea Salt; 1/4 Teaspoon Coarse Black Pepper

Directions:
Hard boil eggs using your preferred method.
Chop Eggs
Add remaining ingredient and stir together well.
