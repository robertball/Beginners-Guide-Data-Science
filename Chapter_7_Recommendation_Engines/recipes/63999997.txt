Title:
Not My Mom's Seafood Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1113723.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup finely chopped chelery
1 small grated carrot, for colour
1/2 can carnation milk, 0 % fat
1 1/2 cups cubed potatoes(small)
1 1/2 T flour
1 1/2 cups combined - sm bottle clam juice 3/4 cup homemade chicken stock and 1/4 dry white wine. More or less will not effect the recipe.
pinch of dried parsley
salt and pepper to taste
1 envelope chicken bouillion
Sea food:
2 cups clams
1 cup bay scallops
1 cup shrimp, small or roughly chopped
2 cups steel headed trout or haddock, cut into small pieces
1 T olive oil, or more

Directions:
Saut√© onions and celery in 1 tbsp. oil 5 min.
Add potatoes, liquid, chicken bouillon, grated carrot and salt.
Heat to boiling. Cover and simmer 10-15 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Add seafood and cook for 5 minutes.
Mix flour with small amount of evaporated milk to make a paste. Gradually stir in remaining milk. Add to potato mixture. Cook over medium heat until mixture thickens.
Sprinkle with paprika and serve.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
I usually serve this soup on a special occasion. It's great with crusty bread to sop the very last drop. I sometimes change the seafood depending on what I have but at Christmas I add a can of frozen , minced lobster.
