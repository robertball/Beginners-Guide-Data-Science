Title:
Moist Double Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter, melted
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
3 eggs
3/4 cup flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease an 8-inch square pan.
Stir together butter, sugar, and vanilla. Add eggs, beating well. Stir together dry ingredients. Gradually add to egg mixture, beating until well blended. Stir in chocolate chips.
Spread batter evenly into pan. Bake 40-45 minutes. Cool completely before cutting.
