Title:
Hot Tuna Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can tuna or 2 small cans, drained well
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
3 hard boiled eggs, chopped
2 T. pickle relish
1/2 c. Miracle Whip
6 hamburger buns

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and put on buns. Place on baking sheet and cover with foil. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Bake for 20 min.
