Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped broccoli
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken & mushroom soup
2 cups Uncle Ben's Wild Rice
3/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup butter
16 ounces Velveeta cheese, shredded
1 sleeve of Ritz crackers
1/4 cup butter, melted
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook rice as directed on box except use chicken broth for water.
Saute onions in butter until done.
Cook broccoli as directed on package and drain.
Mix together broccoli, soups, rice, onion, butter, and cheese.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.  Place in a 9 by 13 inch oven proof dish.
Crush Ritz crackers and mix with 1/4 c. melted butter. Put on top of dish.
Bake for 30 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees F.
