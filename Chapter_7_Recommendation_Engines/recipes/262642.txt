Title:
Vegan Cashew Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5196285.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup raw cashews
1 cup coconut milk
3 tablespoons agave nectar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/2 tablespoon rose water

Directions:
Place cashews in a bowl; cover with cold water and let soak for 2 to 3 hours. Drain.
Combine cashews, coconut milk, agave, vanilla extract, and rose water in a blender; blend until smooth and creamy, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Fioa
Description: 
Try this silky homemade vegan cashew cream, made with 5 simple ingredients. It is a perfect vegan and dairy-free cream to serve with any dessert.
