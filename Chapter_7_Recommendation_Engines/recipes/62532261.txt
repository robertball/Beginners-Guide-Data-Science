Title:
Swedish Papparkakor Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Flour
2 Cups Sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 Egg
1 cup Milk

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together.  Mix egg and milk.  Mix liquid into dry ingredients.  Bake for 45-55 minutes at 375' in a bread pan or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Submitter: 
claudia in ny
Description: 
Traditional Swedish Spice Cake
Perfect to have on hand for the Christmas holiday season. This is an incredibly easy recipe and is perfect for your children to help make and bake.
