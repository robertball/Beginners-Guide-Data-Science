Title:
flax seed muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. whole wheat flour
1 1/2 c. ground flax
1 T. baking powder
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ginger
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/2 c. whole milk or add oil to 2%
1/2 c. honey
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 c. raisins
1 c. grated carrots
1 c. chopped fruit

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Whisk dry ingredients together and beat wet ingredients.  Mix them together then fold in remaining items.  cook till muffins have risen and are lightly browned.  Do not overfill as they rise a lot.
