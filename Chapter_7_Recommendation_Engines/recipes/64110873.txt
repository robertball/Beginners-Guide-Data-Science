Title:
Spicy Mulled Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Q. apple cider
6 slices fresh ginger; cut about the size of a quarter
3 strips orange rind
8 whole cloves
2 cinnamon sticks
2 T. honey
2 T. lemon juice

Directions:
In large saucepan over medium heat, mix all ingredients. Bring mixture to a simmer then reduce heat to low, cook partially covered for 25 minutes. Strain through a sieve before serving
