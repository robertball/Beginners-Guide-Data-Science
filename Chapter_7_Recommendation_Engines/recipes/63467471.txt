Title:
Dark Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package devils food cake mix
1/4 strong brewed coffee
3 tbsp butter
1 tbsp water
1 egg lightly beaten
1/2 cup milk chocolate mini chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Combine first 5 ingredients in a large bowl. Beat with mixer medium speed for 2 minutes. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop a tbsp 2 inches apart on baking sheets coated with cooking spray. Bake at 350 for 11 minutes or until just set.
