Title:
Valentine's Day Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1/3 cup of vinegar
- 1/3 cup of olive iiil
-1 tablespoon brown sugar
-3 tablespoons of soy sauce
- two med onions- sliced
-1/2 pound of sliced mushrooms
-2 cloves of garlic; crushed
- 1/2 teaspoon course ground pepper
- two rib-eye or good quality steak

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients in a medium size bowl, except for the steal. Place steaks in a large, gallon size zip lock bag. Pour mixed ingredients over steaks and marinate overnight, or for at least 8 hours.
Remove steaks and broil each side for at least 8 minutes- depending on the size of each one.
While steaks broil, transfer onions and mushrooms, along with the remaining marinade to a stove-top pan. Cook on medium low heat until steaks are done.
