Title:
Rhonda's Apple Cider Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chilled ginger ale
2 cups chilled apple cider
2 cups chilled pear martini mix
1 cup chilled peach schnapps
1 cup chilled white zinfandel
1 pear sliced in slivers
1 apple sliced in slivers
1 peach sliced in slivers
Grapes that have been pulled off the stems, washed and frozen. To be used in place of ice cubes!

Directions:
Place sliced fruit in a 2-qt pitcher. Pour all the liquids into the pitcher with the fruit and stir.
When serving put a handful of frozen grapes in the bottom of the glass. Pour the sangria over the grapes.
Garnish each drink with some of the sliced fruit!
