Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 or 4 ripe bananas, mashed
1/3 c melted butter
1 c Splenda
1 egg, beaten
1 tbs Southern Comfort (optional)
1 tsp Kaluha or vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
Pinch of salt
1 1/2 c all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
In a mixing bowl, mix bananas, butter and mashed bananas.
Add sugar, egg, Southern Comfort and Kaluha and mix well.
Add baking soda and salt.
Sift in flour, mix til incorporated.
Pour mixture into a buttered 4x8 inch loaf pan.
Bake for 1 hour.
Cool on a rack.
Remove from pan and slice to serve.
