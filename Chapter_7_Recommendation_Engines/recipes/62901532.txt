Title:
Chicken Saagwala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 teaspoons curry powder
1 teaspoon coriander, ground
1/2 teaspoon cumin, ground
2 tablespoons ginger root, grated
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound chicken breast, uncooked boneless/skinless
1 cup diced tomatoes, canned
10 oz baby spinach
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons cilantro, fresh chopped

Directions:
Place oil, curry powder, coriander, cumin, ginger and garlic in a large nonstick skillet; cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until toasted and fragrant, about 2 to 3 minutes.
Add chicken to skillet; stir to coat with spiced oil.
Add tomatoes to skillet; cover skillet and cook for 10 minutes.  Uncover skillet and stir to combine.
Add spinach to skillet; cover and cook for 5 minutes more.  Uncover skillet and stir to combine.
Add salt, water and cilantro to skillet; simmer for 1 minute.  Yields about 1 1/2 cups per serving.
