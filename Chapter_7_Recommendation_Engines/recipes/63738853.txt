Title:
Stuffed Fillet Of Sole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Fillet of sole thawed or
2 cups shrimp= salad shrimp thawed
4 talb butter
1/4 cup olive oil
1 small onion chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2 stalks celery chopped (1/2 cup)
1 talb herbs de Provencal
1/3 cup white wine
salt n pepper
1/3 cup panko bread crumbs

Directions:
preheat oven 400*
in frying pan sauté with olive oil celery, onions, garlic and herbs salt n pepper, cover and heat  till onions clear...add the wine and heat for 2 min... then toss in the panko crumbs
8 x 8 pan melt the butter
place two  pieces of sole side by side- place 1 cup shrimp per side salt n pepper the shrimp- add half of the sautéed onions and crumb mixture to one sole and the other half to the other sole...top with the other filet of sole...cover with tinfoil...bake 400 * 30 min...place on plate to serve and drizzle juice from pan over them
makes two stuffed sole
