Title:
Blueberry and Raisin Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Pints fresh Blueberries
12oz box of Raisins
4 cups of sugar
1/2 cup orange juice
1 box low or no sugar pectin

Directions:
1. Sterilize jars in a hot bath for 10 min.
2.Add raisins and orange juice to a blender or food processor until well blended. Move raisins to a pot, next blend the blueberries and add them to the raisins with pectin. Stir together on high heat until boil, add 4 cups of sugar return to boil stir often.
3. Remove from heat and transfer to hot sterile jars, leaving 1/4 to 1/2 inch headspace, and seal. Process in a water bath for 10 min to seal, set on counter to cool.
