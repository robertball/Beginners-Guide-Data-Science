Title:
Italian Herb Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/985826.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 tablespoon oil
1/3 cup McCormick® Italian Herb & Cheese Crusting Blend
1/2 cup spaghetti sauce
1/2 cup shredded Italian cheese blend or mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Brush chicken with oil.  Coat evenly on both sides with Crusting Blend.  Place on foil-lined shallow baking pan.  Discard any remaining Crusting Blend.
Bake 15 minutes.  Remove from oven.  Top each chicken breast with 2 tablespoons each of the sauce and the cheese.  Bake 5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted and chicken is cooked through.  Serve with spaghetti and additional sauce, if desired.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Crusting Blends
Description: 
Preparing Chicken Parmesan will now be a snap with Italian Herb and Cheese Crusting Blend.  Use convenient jarred spaghetti sauce.  Or, prepare an extra batch of Chunky Tomato Sauce to use in this recipe.
