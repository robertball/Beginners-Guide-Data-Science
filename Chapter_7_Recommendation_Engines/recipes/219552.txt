Title:
Grecian-Style Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986273.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Pepperidge Farm® Deli Flats® Soft Honey Wheat Thin Rolls, split
1/4 cup hummus
1/4 seedless cucumber, cut into 28 thin slices
1/4 cup crumbled reduced fat feta cheese
1/4 cup finely diced red pepper
4 leaves lettuce

Directions:
Spread 1 tablespoon hummus on each bottom roll half. Divide the cucumber, cheese and red pepper among the bottom roll halves. Top with the lettuce, if desired, and the top roll halves.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Here's a sandwich you can really feel good about...it features a thin wheat roll filled with hummus, cucumber, feta and red pepper. It's got lots of crunchy good flavor!
