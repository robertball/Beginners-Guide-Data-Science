Title:
Meme's Meatloaf (On the recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs ground beef, 80/20
1 small yellow onion
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 cup graded parmigiano region cheese
2 eggs
4 tbl tomato ketchup
1 to 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup bread crumbs
2 tbl chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tbl fresh thyme
1 tsp soy sauce
salt and pepper to taster

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350.  In a large mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients and 4 tbl of the ketchup.  Form the mixture into a large loaf and place on a sheet pan lined with parchment paper.  Rub some extra ketchup all over the top and sides of the loaf in a thin layer.  Season with salt and pepper.
Bake for 45 min to an hour, until the internal temp is 140.  Remove the meatloaf from the oven and let rest for 15 to 20 minuets before slicing and serving.
