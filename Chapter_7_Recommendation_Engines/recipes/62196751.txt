Title:
Mom's Amazing Candied Yams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 yams
1 cup butter
3/4 cup maple syrup
3/4 cup brown sugar
zest of one orange
salt & pepper

Directions:
Boil yams until fork tender. Cool, peel and slice short ways about 1/4 to 1/2" thick, and place in large casserole dish. Grate orange zest over top and lightly salt & pepper.
In small saucepan, melt butter, add brown sugar and maple syrup and simmer til thickened, stirring occasionally. Pour over top of yams.
Bake @ 350 for 30 min.

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
A family tradition for generations, this dish is loved by kids and adults alike.
