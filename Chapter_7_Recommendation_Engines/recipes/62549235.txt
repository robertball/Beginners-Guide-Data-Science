Title:
Potato, Corn and Cheddar Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/529056.jpg
Ingredients:
4 strips bacon
3 tablespoons butter
1 med yellow onion (minced)
1 clove garlic (minced)
1 tablespoon flour
32 oz vegetable broth
3 cups potato (peeled & cubed)
2 cups white corn
1 cup milk
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar
Salt & pepper to taste
1/4 cup green onion (chopped)

Directions:
Cook bacon, drain and crumble.
In medium pot melt butter and brown onion over medium heat. About 5 minutes.
Add garlic and flour then stir.
Pour in broth and bring to a boil over high heat.
Add potato and reduce heat. Simmer for 20 minutes.
Stir in corn, milk, cheddar and seasoning. Cook until cheese is melted.
Garnish with bacon and green onion.
