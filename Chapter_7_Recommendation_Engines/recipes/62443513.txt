Title:
Susan's Salsa Ranchero Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup cooking oil
1 onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 medium green bell peppers, chopped
4 tomatoes, diced
1 (14.5 oz) can, fire-roasted chopped tomatoes
3/4 t ground cumin
1-1/4 to 1-1/2 t ground cinnamon, or to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet until hot.  Cook onion, garlic, peppers and fresh tomatoes for two minutes, or until transparent.  Stir in the canned tomatoes and spices, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until sauce is lumpy.

Submitter: 
Susan Stone
Description: 
This is my take on a sauce my husband and I had at a Mexican restaurant, that I wanted to duplicate. I use it for chiles rellenos, but I'm sure it would work well with other things too.  It's great for people who like Mexican flavors but want a sauce that is not spicy hot.
