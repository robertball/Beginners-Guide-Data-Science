Title:
MY Peach Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Peach Ingredients:
2 c. finely sliced peaches
1/4 c. flour
1/4 c. sugar
1 T cinnamon
pinch nutmeg
1/4 c. rum
Bread Pudding Ingredients:
1 c. milk
1 c. heavy cream
1/4 c. butter
1 c. sugar
1/4 tsp salt
2/3 c. brown sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tsp vanilla
3 c. stale bread cubes, remove crusts

Directions:
Mix the Peach Ingredients together in an oven-safe bowl and bake at 400 deg for about 30 min.
In med. saucepan over med. heat combine milk & heavy cream just until film forms over top. Combine butter, stirring until melted. Cool to lukewarm.  Combine remaining ingredients except bread, millk and peach mixture. Beat with electric mixer at med. speed for 1 minute.  Slowly add milk to mixture.  Add bread cubes and stir gently. Fold in peaches.
Place in lightly greased 1-1/2 qt. casserole dish. Bake at 350 deg. for 45-50 min. or until set. Serve warm.  Top with melted vanilla ice cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Mother Ann
Description: 
My husband reviewed numerous bread pudding recipes, and came up with this version.
