Title:
Easy Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup corn meal
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
1/3 vegetable oil
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat to 400 degrees
Grease 8x8, 9x9 or 9' iron skillet
Combine corn meal, flour, sugar, salt and baking powder in one bowl.
Mix milk, oil and egg in separate bowl then add to dry mix and stir until blended.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
