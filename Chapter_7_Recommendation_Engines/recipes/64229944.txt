Title:
Creamy Baked Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 onions, cut in half horizontally
1 cup chicken broth
1-1/2 T olive oil
1 tsp fresh rosemary
salt
pepper
1/4 milk
1 T flour

Directions:
preheat oven to 435
place onions cut side up in baking dish, pour chicken broth over onions and drizzle with olive oil - sprinkle with rosemary, salt and papper
bake for 50-60 minutes, depending on size of onions, basting often
In a small boil, mix milk and flour, mix until smooth, pour over onion mixture
bake for 20 minutes longer or until the pan juices are thickened and onion tops are golde
