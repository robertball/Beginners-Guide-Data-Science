Title:
The Perfect Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3659527.jpg
Ingredients:
3 medium avocados ( 4 if small)
1  White onion large
1  tomato large
1  tablespoon of fresh chopped cilantro
1  lime
10  cranks (on a hand crank) of both salt and pepper *Approximately 1/4 tsp.

Directions:
1.  Squeeze avocados until soft
2.  Chop tomato and onion into large pieces then place in an electric blender or chopper and tell it looks like the consistency of pepper jelly
3.  Strain tomato and onion mixture
4.  Cut open avocados and scoop out into the electric blender or chopper and blend fully until smooth
5.  Chop cilantro and add to the avocado
6.  Juice the lime completely into the avocado and cilantro mixture
7.  Add the tomato and onion mixture to the avocado mixture
8.  Add 10 cranks of both salt and pepper
9.  Chill for about 30 minutes then enjoy!
