Title:
Frozen Berry Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982017.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans raspberry or strawberry pie filling
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, undrained (optional)
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
Fresh berries and mint (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine pie filling, milk and pineapple if desired. Fold in whipped topping. Spread into an ungreased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. pan. Cover and freeze for 8 hours or overnight. Remove from the freezer 10-15 minutes before serving. Cut into squares. Garnish each with berries and mint if desired.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
Your family will love this cool, refreshing dessert no matter what flavor pie filling you use.
