Title:
Before-You-Know-It Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz.) can black beans, drained
1/4 cup onion
1-2 cloves garlic
1/4 cup roasted red peppers
1/4 cup (fat-free) chicken or vegetable broth
2 Tbs. balsamic vinegar
dollop of sour cream (optional)
cayenne pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Drain most of the liquid out of the canned beans.
Load all ingredients, in order, into Tall Cup.
Twist on Cross Blade and quickly Pulse until it reaches desired consistency.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This dip is great both warm and cold, chunky or smooth. The warm version goes perfectly with nachos or crusty Italian bread. You can add broth to make this a black bean sauce or soup.
