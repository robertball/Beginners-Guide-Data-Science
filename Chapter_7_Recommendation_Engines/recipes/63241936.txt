Title:
Homemade Strawberry Poptarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie crust, refrigerated or homemade
Jam or preserves
1 cup powdered sugar
milk

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
Unroll one of the pie crusts and square the edges.  Cut into rectangles, about 3" wide and 4" long.  Place 1-2 tsp. jam in the center of half of the rectangles, top with the other half.  Crimp edges with a fork.
Bake about 7-8 minutes or until slightly brown.  Allow to cool, pour glaze over and decoratge with sprinkles as desired.
to Make glaze, place powdered sugar in a bowl, thin with milk 1 tlbsp at a time until desired consistency.
