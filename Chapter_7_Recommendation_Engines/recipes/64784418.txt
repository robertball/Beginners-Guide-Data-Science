Title:
Arroz Mamposteao Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Cooked Rice
1 Can Goya Seasoned Red Beans (red can)
1 Green or Red Bell Pepper, diced
1 Small Onion, diced
2-3 Garlic Cloves, minced
3-5 Slices of Bacon, cut into small pieces
2 TBL Soy Sauce
3-4 TBL Fresh Cilantro, chopped fine

Directions:
1. In skillet over medium heat, saute the bacon until golden brown.
2.  Add peppers and onion and saute until onions appear clear.  Add garlic and saute one minute.  Add soy sauce and stir.
3.  Add red beams, mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
4.  Add rice and stir until no white grains are seen.
5.  Add cilantro and mix.  Cook for 10 minutes.   Done.
