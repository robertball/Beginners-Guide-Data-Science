Title:
Apple & Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1977643.jpg
Ingredients:
10 cups chicken stock
1 can lentils, drained & rinced or 1 cup of dehydrated Lentils
4 medium apples, diced
1 medium onion chopped
1 teaspoon celery seed (optional)
4 tablespoons flour (sifted)
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
In a large pot, put olive oil,and when hot add onions, apples and
saute 4 min then add flour, mix and continue cooking for 2 min.
Add chicken stock and bring to a boil
Then add lentils, celery seeds and bring to a boil again.
Reduce heat and simmer on low 45 min ( or more )

Submitter: 
Mikey's Kitchen
Description: 
I know it sounds weird.... apples & lentils ?? But it's a delicious , light and healthy soup ! Serve this with some homemade bread a salad and it's a meal ! This is a regular at our family table , I hope you like it !
