Title:
Creamy Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbs. butter
25 Nutter Butter Cookies
8 Peanut Butter Cups
3/4 Cup Creamy Peanut Butter
1 1/4 Cup Cold Milk
2 packages vanilla pudding mix (try other flavors to mix it up!)
8oz. Cool Whip
Reese's Peanut Butter ice cream topping

Directions:
Crush the cookies as small as possible, leaving some larger chunks.  Melt 4 tbs butter and stir into cookie crumbles.  Press into pie dish.
Either cut or crush 4 of the PB cups and press into the bottom of the pie mix.
Mix together milk and peanut butter with whisk or metal spoon.
Add the dry pudding mixes and beat until well blended and creamy.
Fold in half the tub of Cool whip and spread peanut butter mixture over pie crust.  Add more cool whip depending on the consistency desired.
Spread the remaining cool whip over the mixture.  Cut peanut butter cups into 1/8 pieces.  Place on top of pie and drizzle peanut putter topping.
Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Mary Montero
Description: 
This is a decadent combination of several different peanut butter pie recipes.  It is the ultimate for peanut butter taste!!
