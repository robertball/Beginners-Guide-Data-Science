Title:
Angel Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F6410392.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1 (10.75 ounce) can reduced-sodium condensed cream of mushroom soup
¼ cup KRAFT Tuscan House Italian Dressing
¼ cup dry white wine
1 ½ pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
½ pound angel hair pasta, uncooked
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Whisk first 4 ingredients until blended; pour over chicken in slow cooker. Cook on LOW 4 to 5 hours (or on HIGH 2 to 3 hours).
COOK pasta as directed on package, omitting salt; drain. Serve topped with chicken mixture; sprinkle with parsley.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia
Description: 
Prep in the morning and come home to slow-cooked chicken in a creamy mushroom sauce. If only every dinnertime were this easy.
