Title:
Confetti bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz pkg refrigerator crescent rolls
2 8ozpkg cream cheese
3Tbsp mayonaise
1/2tsp basil leaves
1/4tsp garlic powder
Assorted chopped vegetables
salad  supreme

Directions:
Press crescent rolls into a cookie sheet. Bake in oven at 350 for 12-15min. Combine cream cheese, mayo and spices. Spread over cooled crust. Top with chopped vegetables: broccoli,mushrooms, peppers. Then sprinke with Salad supreme
