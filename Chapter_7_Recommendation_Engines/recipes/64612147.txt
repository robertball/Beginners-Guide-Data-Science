Title:
Beer-can Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6-lb. chicken
2 Tbsp smoked paprika
2 tsp.  salt
2 tsp. coarse pepper
2 tsp. dried oregano leaves
2 tsp. dried thyme leaves
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. onion powder
2 Tbsp. butter, softened
1 can (16 oz.) dark beer
foil, bunched into 4 balls

Directions:
1. place bunched rolls of foil in bottom of 6 qt. slow cooker and pour in beer
2. wash chicken and pat dry.  pull skin gently away from bird
3. mis all dry seasonings together
4. mix 1/2 tsp. of seasoning blend into butter.
5. spread butter mixture under skin of chicken
6. with clean dry hands, rub remaining spice blend onto chicken
7. place chicken on top of foil balls in slow cooker
8. cover and cook on high for 4-6 hours, or low on 7-8 hours.  check the temperature - chicken is done when the temperature is 160-165 in the thickest part of the thigh.  continue to cook if needed.
