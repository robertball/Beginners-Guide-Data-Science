Title:
Green Chili Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, 5 egg whites beaten
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 pinch salt
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chili peppers, drained
1 (16 ounce) container low-fat cottage cheese
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a large bowl, mix the eggs and whites, flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir in the green chili peppers, cottage cheese, Cheddar cheese, and melted butter. Pour into the prepared baking dish.
Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce heat to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C), and continue baking for 35 to 40 minutes. Cool slightly, and cut into small squares.

Submitter:

Description: 
This rich recipe is great for either a brunch or breakfast. You can make this a day ahead if you want to serve it for an early morning breakfast. Just reheat in the microwave. You may use any combination of cheese in this recipe, even non-fat.Modified from: Green Chile Frittata
