Title:
Curried Sweet Potato & Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet potato
2 large carrots
1 cup red lentils
1 tbsp chicken soup powder
6 cups water
1 dried red chilli
2 tsp curry powder
1/2 - 1 cup chopped cilantro
2 cups milk / 1 cup   cream

Directions:
Peel and roughly chop the sweet potato and carrots.
Sort and wash the lentils.
Add the chopped vegetables, lentils, chicken stock powder, chilli and water into a large saucepan.
Bring to boil and simmer for 25 minutes or until the vegetables are completely soft.
Take off heat and liquidize soup with a kitchen or hand blender until smooth. Return to pan.
Add milk, curry powder and cilantro and simmer for a further 10-15 minutes.
Check seasoning. Serve sprinkled with a few fresh cilantro leaves.

Submitter: 
Shelley
Description: 
Rich, spicy and delicious, this soup makes a great starter or increase the serving size and add whole bread for a hearty meal.
