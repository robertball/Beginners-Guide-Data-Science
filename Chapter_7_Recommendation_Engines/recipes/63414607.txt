Title:
Lemon Twang Cake, the best! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lemon cake mix
3 eggs
¾ cup oil
1 small box lemon jello
1 cup powdered sugar
½ cup lemon juice

Directions:
Mix cake mix using the oil and eggs.
Add 1 cup hot water to the jello; slowly mix jello mixture into the cake mix.
Bake in a 9x13 pan at 350 for 30-40 minutes.
Combine the powdered sugar and lemon juice; mix well.  Using a toothpick, poke holes in cake top and drizzle lemon mixture over cake.
