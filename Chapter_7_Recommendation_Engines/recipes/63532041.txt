Title:
Carrol's Cocoa Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks (1 cup) room temperature butter
1 cup (8 oz) Crisco
6 oz heavy whipping cream
5 cups (20 oz) powdered sugar
1/2 cup cocoa powder
2 tsp vanilla extract
dash of salt

Directions:
In an extra large bowl, using a hand mixer, cream butter & shortening together till completely blended, smooth & shiny.
In another bowl, combine the powdered sugar, cocoa powder & salt; whisk to blend well.
Add half the sugar to the butter bowl & beat well, starting on low & increasing speed once the cloud settles.
Add half the cream & all the vanilla.  Blend well. Scrape down the sides of the bowl as you work.
Add remaining sugar & cream, beat well for several minutes on medium-high speed till all combined & smooth.
If the frosting is a bit too loose, add another 1/2 cup to 1 cup powdered sugar & another Tb of cocoa powder.
