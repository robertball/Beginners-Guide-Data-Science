Title:
Super Avacado Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889625.jpg
Ingredients:
1 hass avacado
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp grated garlic
4 tbsp minced onion
1 tsp salt
2 servings angel hair pasta (6-8 oz)
1/4 cup pine nuts or sunflower seeds
1/4 cup crumbled bacon
bean sprouts, for topping

Directions:
In large bowl, mix the avacado, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, onion, qnd salt until well combined. Place in refrigerator until noodles are ready.
Cook angel hair pasta according to package directions, making sure to heavily salt water. Once pasta is done, place in collander or strainer to remove water, run briefly under cold water to cool the noodles.
Remove avacado mixture from fridge, mix in noodles, pine nuts and bacon.
Place in serving bowls, top with a pinch of sprouts, if desired.

Submitter: 
Kristin Nicole Patton
Description: 
I fell in love with avacados this summer, and became determined to find as many recipes as possible to use it in. This is my favorite. A super easy,  yet super delicious pasta dish that takes no time at all. Add any of your favorite complimentary avacado flavors!
