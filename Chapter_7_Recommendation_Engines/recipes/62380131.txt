Title:
Butter balls (aka Mexican Wedding Cakes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C butter (1lb.)
1 C powder sugar (1 additional C. separated for butter balls)
1 t. vanilla
4 C flour
1-1 1/2 C pecans, finely chopped (for butter balls.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°
Whip butter, sugar, vanilla.
Add pecans and stir
Add flour, mix well
Make small balls.
Bake 8-10 mins. There should be a tinge of golden brown on the bottom edge.
Roll in powder sugar while still hot.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A perfect balance of crunchy and moist. These also come with a warning: one is never enough, or two, or three...
