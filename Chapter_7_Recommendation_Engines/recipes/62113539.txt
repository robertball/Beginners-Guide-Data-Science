Title:
Plum Peach Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 plums
2 peaches
1/4 cup fruit and veggie juice - we use harvest surprise
3 chicken breasts
black pepper
salt
red pepper
paprika
cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Take 3 plums, 1 peach and the 1/4 cup juice and put it in the food processor. Add in black pepper, salt, red pepper, paprika and cinnamon to taste. Blend until smooth.
Take chicken and place in pan. Poor mixture on top but save a little.
Cook chicken for 50 minutes.
When finished, put on plate and drizzle leftover mixture on top. Cut up remaining plum and peach and put on top of chicken. Serve with rice or noodles.

Submitter: 
Jessica R
Description: 
My son is in the stage where he is eating adult food, but only chicken and starches. So I came up with this delicious recipe to help get some more fruit in him!
