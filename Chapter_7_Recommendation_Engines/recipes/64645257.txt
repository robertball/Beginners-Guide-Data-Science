Title:
Jollar's spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4525826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb spaghetti noodles
1 lb of ground beef
1 medium onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp onion powder
1 tbs canola oil
1 tbs chopped garlic
2 tbs dried oregano
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 jar Ragu - Sundried Tomato and Sweet Basil sauce (Garden Combination sauce works too)
1 tbs butter
Optional:  4 cooked hot dogs, sliced

Directions:
In a large pot, heat oil.   Add onion and garlic.  Add 1 lb of ground beef and cook on medium-high heat for 5 mins.  Drain excess fat.  Add salt, pepper, onion powder, and oregano to ground beef.  Mix well and continue cooking on medium-high heat for 2 mins.
Add 1 jar of Ragu Sundried Tomato & Sweet Basil sauce to ground beef.  Add 1/2 cup water and 1/4 cup of sugar to sauce.   (Optional:  add 4 sliced hotdogs to the sauce).  Stir occassionally until it begins to boil.  Lower to medium heat, cover,  and let cook for another 7-10 mins.  Stir occassionally.
In a large pot, boil water.  Add spaghetti noodles and cook for 7-10 minutes.  Drain and rinse with warm water.   Add 1 tablespoon of butter to noodles.  Mix well and set aside.
Serves up to 6.

Submitter: 
Juler Hernandez
Description: 
When you need to make dinner in less than 30 mins, this is the perfect dish!  This Filipino style inspired spaghetti dish is rich and full of  flavor!
