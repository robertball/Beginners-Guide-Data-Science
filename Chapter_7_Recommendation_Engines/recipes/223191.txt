Title:
Insalata Caprese with Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/850388.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
4 large tomatoes, cubed
1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, cubed
1/3 cup basil leaves, thinly sliced
3 1/2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 pinch fine sea salt to taste

Directions:
Combine avocados and lemon juice in a large bowl; toss to coat. Add tomato, mozzarella, and basil; toss again. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt.
