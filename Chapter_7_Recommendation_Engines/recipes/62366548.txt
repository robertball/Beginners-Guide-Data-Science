Title:
Loaded Veggie Crustless Quiche Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/410430.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Milk
6 Eggs
1 1/2 Cups Bisquick biscuit mix
1 Cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 Cup shredded mozzerella cheese
1/2 Cup shredded cheddar cheese
10 oz. frozen chopped broccoli, thawed & squeezed
10 oz. frozen shopped spinach, thawed & squeezed
1 Cup zucchini or yellow squash, diced
1/2 yellow onion,  diced
salt, pepper, spices to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Spray muffin pans with non-stick spray, or grease with butter
Whisk together all of the ingredients in a large mixing bowl
Scoop into muffin pans (about 1/4 C. of mixture per muffin)
Bake for 35-40 minutes

Submitter: 
emi
Description: 
This is the easiest and most versatile one-bowl meal. You can add meat or substitute your fave veggies, and make these cute little muffins for breakfast, dinner, or party appetizers.
