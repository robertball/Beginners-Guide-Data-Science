Title:
chocolate chip banana bread with walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (softened)
8 oz  cream cheese (softened)
2 C brown sugar
4 ripe bananas
2 eggs
1/2 whole milk
1 1/2 t vanilla extract
1/2t salt
2t baking soda
3C flour
optional 1/2t cinnamon
optional dash cloves
optional dash nutmeg
1C walnuts
1C chocolate chips

Directions:
1 Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
2 Spray three loaf pans.  Dust with coc0a or cinnamon sugar if desired.
3 Sift flour baking soda and salt and (spices if desired) Set aside.
4 Cream together the butter, cream cheese and brown sugar.
5 Add eggs, mashed bananas then milk and vanilla.
6 Slowly and dry ingredient mixture to the banana batter.
7 fold in walnuts and chocolate chips
8 pour into pans
9. Bake, Cool, Eat up.
