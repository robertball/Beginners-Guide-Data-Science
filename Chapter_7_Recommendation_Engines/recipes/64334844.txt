Title:
Roast Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Roast (3-3.5 pound)
salt
pepper
margarine
flour
water
soya sauce

Directions:
preheat oven to 300 - 325
put roast in the roaster, salt and pepper the roast add a blob of butter on top
Bake uncovered for 2 1/2 hours uncovered (for 3 - 3.5 pound roast)
If pan gets dry towards the end add 1/4 cup water
Gravy
Heat drippings and add a little flour and potato water
Stir until smooth and thickish
add salt and pepper to taste
add soya sauce for color if needed
