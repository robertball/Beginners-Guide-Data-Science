Title:
Dutch Baby Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs melted butter
2 eggs
1 TBS sugar
1 c milk
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 c flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.  Whisk together melted butter, eggs, sugar, and milk.  Add flour and salt.  Mix together well.  Pour into well greased ramekins (4-5 inch in diameter).  Sprinkle with cinnamon/sugar.  Bake at 450 degrees for 20 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake for an additional 10 minutes.  Remove from ramekin, fill with fruit, sprinkle with powdered sugar and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
CorinneMike Huseby
Description: 
Fill with strawberries, blueberries, and banana.  Or add thinly sliced apple or pear to top of batter just before baking.
