Title:
Stuffed Aburaage Pouches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pieces aburaage
100 mL kombu dashi
1 tablespoon mirin
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 egg
1/8 piece onion
10 grams crab sticks
5 g mochi
1 tablespoon kimchi
30 grams steamed rice
1/2 teaspoon vinegar
1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
1/4 teaspoon sugar
pinch of salt
1/8 teaspoon sesame seeds

Directions:
Cut out the aburaage to open.
Put the egg in a small bowl then pierce the egg yolk with the sharp end of a toothpick. Carefully pour the egg inside the tofu pouch then secure the open part with a toothpick.
Put inside the onion and crab sticks inside the second tofu pouch then secure the open part with a toothpick.
Cut the mochi so that it will fit inside the tofu pouch. Put it inside then add the kimchi. Secure the open end with a toothpick.
Add vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, salt and sesame seeds to the rice then mix. Wet your hands then form it into a ball then insert inside the tofu pouch. Secure the open end with a toothpick.
Place the four pouches on a microwave-safe bowl. Mix the kombu dashi, mirin and soy sauce then pour it over the tofu pouches. Cover then cook in the microwave for 4 minutes using 600W. Slice the tofu pouches into half then serve.
