Title:
Mom's Banana Split Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c flour
1 c sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 c semi-sweet mini chocolate chips
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c maynaise
3 bananas, ripe and mashed
sundae nut topping
Maraschino cherries

Directions:
Whisk flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and chocolate. In a bowl beat egg; stir in mayonaise and vanilla. Combine with mashed bananas. Add to flour and combine until moist.
In paper cup lined muffin tins, fill 3/4 full. Sprinkle iwth nuts.
Bake in a 375 degree oven for 20 mins until lightly browned. Remove from oven and press i cherry half into each muffin.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
The mayonaise makes these muffins so tasty and moist. The whole gang will love 'em.
