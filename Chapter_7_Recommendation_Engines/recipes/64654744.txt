Title:
Homemade Honey Candy Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups honey ( melted, I always use Cox's Honey )
1 cup cream
1 cup Sugar

Directions:
1. Combine the first 3 ingredients and cook slowly on medium heat until it forms a hard ball when you drop it into ice water.

This recipe is as sweet as honey & as healthy as a piece of a loaf of bread that was just baked!
