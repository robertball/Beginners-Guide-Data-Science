Title:
Favourite Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2404734.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Roma (plum) tomatoes, quartered (or 2 cups canned tomatoes)
1 large cucumber, peeled and chunked (can leave peel on if using English cuke)
1 onion, peeled and quartered
2 cloves garlic
1.36 l. (48 ounce) can tomato juice
1/4 cup olive oil - or to taste
1/8 cup red wine vinegar- or to taste
1/8 cup apple cider vinegar - or to taste
dash hot pepper sauce
1 1/2 - 3 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a blender combine the tomatoes, cucumber, onion, garlic and at least 1/2 of the tomato juice. Blend at high speed for 30 seconds to puree the vegetables.
In a soup tourine mix the pureed vegetables with remaining tomato juice, olive oil, vinegars, hot pepper sauce, salt and ground black pepper. Stir. Cover mixture and refrigerate until it is well chilled (about 2 hours).
Serve soup in chilled bowls with chopped avocado, tomatoes and cucumber as accompaniments.
