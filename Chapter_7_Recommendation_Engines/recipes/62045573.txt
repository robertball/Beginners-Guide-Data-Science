Title:
Apple Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 ½ cup oatmeal (uncooked)
1 ½ cup sugar
1 cup margarine (melted)
1 ½ cup apple butter

Directions:
Combine flour, soda & salt in a large bowl; add oats and sugar.  Stir
in margarine and mix well.
Press ½ of mixture into a greased 9 x 13" baking pan; top with apple
butter; sprinkle remainig crumb mixture and press gently with a spoon.
Bake at 350 for 55 minutes.
