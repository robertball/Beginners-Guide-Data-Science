Title:
Rich Pesto Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2343436.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups packed fresh basil leaves
1/4 cups pine nuts
1 garlic clove
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Toast the pine nuts on low heat until they start turning golden.  Add toasted pine nuts and the remainder of the ingredients to a food processor and blend

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
I've tried many pesto recipes and I've found this one gives the richest basil flavour
