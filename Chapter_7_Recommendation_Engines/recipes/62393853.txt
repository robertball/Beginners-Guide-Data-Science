Title:
Chocolate Mint Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457222.jpg
Ingredients:
Shot Of Smirnoff Vodka
Shot Of White Chocolate Godiva
Shot And A Half Of Baileys Mint Chocolate
Squart A  Little Hersheys Sundae Syrup In Alcohol
Then Shake, Place In Freezer Let Get A Little Cold.
Line Chilled Martini Glass With Chocolate Syrup.
Very Tasty!!

Directions:
all ingredients into shaker
