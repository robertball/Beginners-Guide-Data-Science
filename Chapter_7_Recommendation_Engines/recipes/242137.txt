Title:
Mint and Melon Freeze Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups cubed cantaloupe
1 1/2 cups ice cubes
1 cup red grapes
1 cup rice milk (such as Rice DreamÂ®)
6 large mint leaves
4 small cantaloupe wedges

Directions:
Blend cantaloupe, ice cubes, grapes, rice milk, and mint leaves together in a blender until smooth; pour into martini glasses and garnish each with a cantaloupe wedge.
