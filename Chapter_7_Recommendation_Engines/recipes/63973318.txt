Title:
Debbie's Best Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. frozen southern style hash browns
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 onion, chopped
1/2 stick butter, softened
1/4 tsp. salt
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
8 oz. sour cream
8 oz. sharp white cheddar cheese, shredded
2 oz. sharp yellow cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
In large bowl, combine soup, butter, salt, cream cheese, and sour cream; mix well.
Add pepper to taste.
Stir in onions and shredded white cheddar.
Add potatoes and stir gently to coat.
Pour into casserole dish.
Bake at 350 for 40 min.
Remove from oven, sprinkle with shredded yellow cheddar, and return to oven for an additional 20 min.
Garnish with chives, crumbled potato chips, or crumbled butter crackers.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
This is my favorite version of the ever popular potato casserole.
