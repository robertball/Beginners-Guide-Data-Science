Title:
Rootin' Tootin' Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb of bacon cooked and then cut or crumbled
1/2 lb or ground round or chuck cooked and fat drained
1 cup of chopped onion
28 oz can of pork-n-beans
15-16 oz can of dark red kidney beans rinsed and drained
15-16 oz can of lima beans rinsed and drained
1/2 cup of BBQ sauce
1/2 cup of ketchup
1/2 cup of sugar
1/2 cup of packed brown sugar
2 TBS of prepared yellow mustard
2 TBS of molasses
1 tsp of salt
1/2 tsp of chili powder

Directions:
Cook beef and drain.  Lightly saute chopped onion.  Place beef and onion in a large pot.  Add rinsed and drained beans, then continue to add the rest of the ingredients.  Crumble the bacon last on top of of the ingredients.  Mix well.  Cover and cook on stove top for at least an hour.
