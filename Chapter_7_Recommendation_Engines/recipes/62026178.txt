Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup shortening
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup water
2 cups bisquick
1-2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix peanut butter, shortening and sugars.  Add water.  Stir in bisquick and then chocolate chips.  Shape into balls and place on cookie sheet.  Flatten with fork to form criss-cross pattern.  Bake 8-10 minutes at 400 degrees.  These are best when slightly underbaked.

Submitter: 
cicada77
Description: 
Delicious peanut butter cookies with chocolate chips.  These contain no eggs and are very easy to make.
