Title:
Marlene's Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/595878.jpg
Ingredients:
10 slices of white bread (decrusted and broken in pieces)
1 small can of diced green chiles
1 lb of sharp grated cheddar cheese
3 1/2 cups of milk
4 eggs
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In one large mixing bowl, add the bread pieces, cheese and chiles, mix. In another bowl, whip up eggs and milk together, then combine in large mixing bowl with bread.Mix well. Salt and pepper to taste
Put mixture in a well-greased 13 x 9 pan. Cover and put in refrigerator for overnight.
Next day, take out of frig so casserole can come to room temperature (about 30 minutes). Bake on 350 degrees for about 45 minutes or until center is not jiggly.

Submitter: 
Marlene
Description: 
This casserole is a welcomed addition to breakfast potlucks.  My family would make this on our Thanksgiving visits to my parents cabin in Running Springs. You assemble it the day before.  The morning of, pop it in the oven.
