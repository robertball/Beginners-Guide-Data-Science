Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup cocoa
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
3 cups quick cook oats
1 cup unsweetened flaked coconut

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, milk, butter, cocoa and salt.
Heat to boiling, stirring frequently.
When it has reached a rolling boil, cook for 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove pan from heat and mix in vanilla, oats and coconut and mini marshmallows
Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper.
Work quickly!
Let cool.
