Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 rolls chocolate chip cookie dough
16 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 eggs
1 box powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, eggs, and powdered sugar.  Slice one roll of cookie dough and place in the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan.  Spread cream cheese mixture over cookie dough.  Slice remaining dough and place over cream cheese mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
UCMOM
Description: 
This is quick, easy, and good.  Who could ask for more?  It's really rich though, so cut small pieces.
