Title:
Johnsonville Italian All Natural Ground Sausage Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993020.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small red pepper, cut into thin strips
1 small green pepper, cut into thin strips
1 small yellow pepper, cut into thin strips
1 small onion, cut into thin strips
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup White Zinfandel wine
1 (16 ounce) package JohnsonvilleÂ® Italian All Natural Ground Sausage
1 loaf focaccia bread
4 slices smoked provolone cheese

Directions:
In a skillet, saute the peppers, onion and garlic in olive oil for 2 minutes. Add wine; cook and stir until vegetables are tender and wine is reduced by half. Keep warm.
Shape sausage into four hamburger shaped patties. Grill burgers over direct heat for 5-7 minutes on each side or until a meat thermometer reads 180 degrees F. Cut focaccia in half horizontally. Cut into quarters. Layer the sausage burger, peppers and cheese on bread bottoms; replace bread tops.
