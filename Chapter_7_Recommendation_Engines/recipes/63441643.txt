Title:
Taco pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package refrigerated crescent roll dough
1 cup tortilla chips, crumbled
1 lb. ground meat (beef, turkey, venison)
1 packet taco seasoning
1/2 c. sour cream
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
shredded lettuce
chopped tomato
taco sauce
avocado slices

Directions:
Spray pie plate with non-stick spray. Unroll crescent roll dough, press in to pie plate to make crust.
Put 1/2 c. of the chip crumbs in the bottom of the crust.
Prepare ground meat with taco seasoning packet according to package directions. Put in crust.
Cover with the sour cream and top with cheese. Top with remaining chip crumbs.
Bake 25 minutes at 325 or until crust is golden and cheese is melted and bubbly.
Slice and serve with your favorite taco toppings.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Easy, kid-friendly weeknight dinner. Top however you like to eat tacos.
