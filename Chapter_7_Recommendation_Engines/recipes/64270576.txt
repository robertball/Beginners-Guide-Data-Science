Title:
Porcupine Meatballs (slow cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb     ground beef
1/4 Cup  uncooked long-grain rice
1/2 tsp  salt
10.5 oz can  tomato soup, divided
2 T      butter or shortening
1 Cup    Water

Directions:
Mix beef, rice, salt, and 1/4 cup of tomato soup. Shape into 1.5" balls. Brown the balls in shortening or butter in a large non-stick skillet, being careful not to crowd them. Place the browned meatballs in your slow cooker. In the bowl, mix together 1 cup of water and the remaining tomato soup. Pour over meatballs. Cover and cook on High 2-4 hours, or until the rice is fully cooked.
