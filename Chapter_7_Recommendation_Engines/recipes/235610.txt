Title:
Ranch with Bacon Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075344.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (16 ounce) package penne pasta, cooked and drained
1 (14.5 ounce) jar PregoÂ® Alfredo Sauce Flavored with Bacon
1/2 (1 ounce) envelope ranch salad and seasoning mix
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Heat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Stir the penne, sauce, dressing mix and half the cheese in a large bowl.  Spoon the penne mixture into an 8x8x2-inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with the remaining cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes or until the penne mixture is hot and bubbling.
