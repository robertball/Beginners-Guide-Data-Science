Title:
Misty's Avocado Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained
1 avocado, mashed
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
paprika to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the tuna, avocado, celery, onion powder, mayonnaise, lemon juice, parsley, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Mix well and refrigerate until chilled. Sprinkle with paprika if desired.

Submitter: 
Misty
Description: 
The mixture of tuna and avocado provides a perfect balance to a hot weather snack or lunch. Good on crackers or toast.Modified from: Tuna Fish Salad
