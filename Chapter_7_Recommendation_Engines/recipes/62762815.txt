Title:
Deviled Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup green onions or chives, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, mustard, salt, garlic powder, paprika, pepper and green onions (or chives).
Coarsely chop the eggs and mix gently with the dressing.
