Title:
Enchiladas with Red Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
8 garlic cloves
3-4 T cumin
1-2T chili powder
2-4T oregano
1-2 tsp Paprika
Pinch cinnamon
2 cans tomato sauce
Splash orange juice
1-2 tsp sugar
Water
8 corn tortillas
1 c or more meat
1 c beans
1 c rice
1-2 c shredded cheese

Directions:
heat olive oil
saute garlic
add spices and stir until fragrant
add tomato sauce
add water
add orange juice, sugar as needed
adjust spices
simmer 20 min
Enchiladas
preheat oven to 400 degrees
combine 1/3 c or more enchilada sauce with meat
mash beans slightly and mix with rice and cheese and 1/3 c or more enchilada sauce
spread bottom of 9x13 inch baking pan with enchilada sauce
put 1/4 to 1/3 c meat mixture into 4 tortillas, roll, and place in pan
put 1/4 to 1/3 c bean/rice/cheese mixture into each tortilla, roll and place in pan
cover in enchilada sauce and sprinkle cheese on top
bake 400 for 20 min
reserve extra sauce for serving
