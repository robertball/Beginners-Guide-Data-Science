Title:
Vegan No Fat Banana Bread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large ripe bananas
1 cup rolled oats
½ teaspoon vanilla flavoring
¼ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Mash the bananas in a bowl. Add vanilla, oats & walnuts, if using. Drop tablespoons of batter on a baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Vegan, no added oil cookies. Lots of room for experimenting.
