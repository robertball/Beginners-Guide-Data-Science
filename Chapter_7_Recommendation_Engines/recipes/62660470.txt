Title:
Friday Night Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box elbow macaroni
1 box velveeta, cut into cubes
1 can evaporated milk
1 bag shredded cheddar cheese
1 stick of butter
1 tsp dry mustard
2 tbsp white flour
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place cooked macaroni in a baking dish, large enough to mix ingredients.  Add all cheese.  In a saucepan melt butter and then add flour until thick.  Add 1/2 can evaporated milk and mustard; salt and pepper.  Pour over and mix well.
Bake 350 for 15 minutes, add remaining milk and bake until all cheese is melted and is bubbly and brown on edges, about another 10 minutes.
