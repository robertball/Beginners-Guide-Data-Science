Title:
Asparagus Guacamole Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 10 ounce pkgs. frozen asparagus (cuts)
1 garlic clove
2 teaspoons lime juice
1/4 cup canned green chilies, chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
1/2 cup seeded & chopped tomato

Directions:
Cook asparagus in small amount of water until tender; drain well.
In a food processor: process asparagus, garlic, lime juice, chilies, salt and cumin for 30 seconds or until mixture is smooth.  Scrape bowl frequently.
Remove from processor bowl, place mixture in storage bowl/container; stir in onion and tomato.  Chill in refrigerator for 2 hours before serving.  Serve with tortilla chips.
