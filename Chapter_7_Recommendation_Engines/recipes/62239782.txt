Title:
Pumpkin Pie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c canned pumpkin puree
4 c chicken stock
3 tbsp brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
2 c half and half or light cream
sour cream

Directions:
Mix first 7 ingredients together in a large saucepan and bring to simmer.  Simmer 10 minutes.  Turn off heat and add half and half.  Let sit 5 minutes then reheat gently.  Serves 4-6.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
Serve in mugs with a rosette of sour cream piped on top.
