Title:
Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/933332.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups All-Purpose Flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
5 tablespoons butter
1 cup Buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
In a deep bowl, sift dry ingredients; cut in shortening. Add buttermilk all at once and stir into a ball of dough.  Knead lightly. Roll out onto a floured board into 3/4 to 1-inch thickness. Cut with a buscuit cutter. Place onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or until browned.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
These are my family's favorite biscuits.  They are easy to make as they only use things I generally have in the kitchen.  We like to serve them with sliced ham and scrambled eggs!
