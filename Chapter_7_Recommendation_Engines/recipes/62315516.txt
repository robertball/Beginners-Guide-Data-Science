Title:
Jim's Shrimp and Scallop Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups risotto, precooked
2 cans chicken broth
6 cups of heavy cream
4 finely chopped garlic cloves
4 finely chopped shallot bulbs
10 oz. baby shrimp
10 oz. dry bay scallops
6 oz. grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped parsley
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
. Precook measured risotto, and let cool.
2. Heat sauté pan with Extra Virgin Olive oil on medium heat.
3. Sauté garlic, shallots, shrimp and scallops for 2-3 minutes.
4. Fold in precooked risotto.
5. Add chicken broth and cream.
6. Reduce heat to medium-low until mixture is creamy.
7. Fold in parmesan cheese.
8. Serve immediately and sprinkle chopped parsley over top.
