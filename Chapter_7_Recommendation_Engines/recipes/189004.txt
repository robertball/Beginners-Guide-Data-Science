Title:
Eyes of Ox (Ojos de Buey) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/605017.jpg
Ingredients:
24 pitted prunes
12 bacon strips, cut in half
8 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 20 minutes

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Wrap each prune with one of the pieces of bacon. Thread three of the wrapped prunes onto each skewer. Arrange the skewers on a baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until the bacon is crisp, about 12 minutes. Allow to cool before serving.

Submitter: 
Cecy
Description: 
This is a favorite at any Venezuelan get-together from Maracaibo. My mom used to make these Ojos de Buey for almost every party at our home. It is so easy to make that your kids can help.
