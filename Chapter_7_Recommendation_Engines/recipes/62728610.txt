Title:
Fresh Herb Vinaigrette from AR TV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of white wine vinegar
2 tbs of fresh basil leaves
1 tbs of fresh oregano leaves
1/2 tsp of fresh rosemary leaves
1 small clove of garlic
1/2 tsp of sugar
1/4 tsp of salt
1/8 tsp of pepper
extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
1. In a blender, combine the white wine vinegar, basil, oregano, rosemary, garlic, sugar, salt and pepper.
2. Blend 10-15 seconds until all the herbs and garlic are finely minced.
3. Gradually add extra virgin olive oil and continue blending for 10-15 seconds or until everything is mixed well.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mom
Description: 
Fresh Herb Vinaigrette is a salad dressing we can make all year long. The fragrance alone is worth the two minutes it takes to whip together.
