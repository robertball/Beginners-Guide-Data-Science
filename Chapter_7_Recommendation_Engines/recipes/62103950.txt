Title:
Blueberry Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups blueberries, fresh or frozen (no need to thaw)
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk, whole or 2%
1 1/2 cups heavy
cream
1/4 teaspoon vanilla or almond extract

Directions:
Pick through blueberries and remove  any stems or dried out berries. Bring berries, sugar and salt to a boil over medium heat. Stir a few times and simmer on low for about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool for about 15 minutes. Puree berry mixture and milk in blender until smooth. Strain through medium fine strainer into a glass or metal bowl. Add cream and extract, stir to incorporate, then chill for at least 2 hours or up to 24 hours. Freeze according to ice cream maker instructions.

Submitter: 
Gail A. Guild
Description: 
My husband's favorite fruit is blueberries, so I'm always trying ways to bring them to the table. This is quite a different flavor for ice cream and such a vibrant color!
