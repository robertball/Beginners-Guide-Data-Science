Title:
Easy Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/569891.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Ground Turkey
1/2 Yellow Onion/Chopped
2 Cups of Chicken Broth
1 (28 ounce) can of Crushed Tomatoes
1 (16 ounce) can of Black Beans-drained
1 (16 ounce) can of Kidney Beans-drained
1 (16 ounce) can of Re-fried Beans
1 Tablespoon Garlic/Minced
2 1/2 Tablespoons of Chili Powder
1 Teaspoon Paprika
1 Teaspoon Dried Oregano
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Cumin
Salt and Pepper to taste
Shredded Cheese-Optional

Directions:
Heat Large Pot over Medium Heat, add and brown the ground turkey.
Stir in the chopped onion, and cook until the onion is tender.
Pour Chicken Broth into the pot, stir.
Mix in the tomatoes, black beans, re-fried beans, kidney beans, and garlic. Stir well.
Mix in the Chili Powder, Paprika, Oregano, Cumin, salt and pepper.  Mix well.
Bring to boil.
Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
Garnish with Cheese.
