Title:
Mexican Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
180g Mexican Chocolate (2 Disks 6.2oz)
1 oz Unsweetened Dark Chocolate
1 Stick of Butter (1/2 Cup)
1/2 teaspoon ground Cinnamon
1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
3/4 Cup packed Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup All-Purpose Flour
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Melt down chocolate and butter in medium saucepan over medium heat, when thoroughly  combined remove from flame and let cool for a few minutes
Mix in cinnamon, vanilla extract, brown sugar, flour, and eggs in that order to easily combine dry ingredients and cool before mixing eggs
Pour into a greased 8x8 Pyrex pan or 9 inch Pyrex pie dish
Bake for 25-30 minutes until fork/toothpick comes out clean
