Title:
Whole Wheat Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
Â½  cup unsweetened applesauce
1  pint fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease muffin cups or line with muffin liners.
Combine flour, sugar, salt and baking powder in a medium mixing bowl. In a second bowl, mix together the oil, egg, milk, and applesauce. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry ingredients and stir together until all of the ingredients are incorporated. Fold in blueberries. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full.
Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until done.
