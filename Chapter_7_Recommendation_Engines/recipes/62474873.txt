Title:
Marscapone  Strawberry Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box supermoist white cake mix
1 cup water
8 oz marscapone cheese
2 egg whites
1/4 cup veggie oil
1/2 cup thawed frozen strawberries
2 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Blend cake mix, water, cheese, egg whites and veggie oil. Put in lined cupcake tins and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
Puree strawberries. In a separate bowl, pour pureed strawberries on top of powdered sugar and mix. Spoon this glaze on top of finished cupcakes.

Submitter: 
laureng
Description: 
These are the most moist cupcakes I have ever had. More of an adult cupcake, as it is not piled with frosting. The glaze on top gives just enough sweetness and flavor!
