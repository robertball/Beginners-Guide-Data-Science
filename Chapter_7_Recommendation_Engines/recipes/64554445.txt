Title:
Lemony Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3351359.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cup water
1 cup sugar
2 cups fresh lemon juice
Water

Directions:
Put the 2 cup water and 1 cup sugar in a medium sized pan and turn heat to high, stir the mixture until sugar is dissolved completely.
Let the sugar and water mixture cool completely.
Put the lemon juice into a gallon sized container,  dump in sugar and water mixture, and fill the rest of the way with water.
Stir and let sit in fridge until cooled, and then enjoy!
