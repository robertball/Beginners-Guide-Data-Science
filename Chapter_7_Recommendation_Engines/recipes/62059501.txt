Title:
Easy Low Fat Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded cooked chicken breasts
3/4 cup Fat Free sour cream
3 minced scallions
1 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 cup diced celery
1/4 cup slivered almonds (optional)
1/4 cup Fat Free Crumbled Feta Cheese (optional)
1 tsp. dried parsley
dash of lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine the sour cream, scallions, garlic, celery, almonds and feta cheese (if using), parsley and lemon juice.  Mix well.
Salt and pepper the cooked chicken, then combine with the dressing, mixing gently.
Refrigerate overnight for best flavor.

Submitter: 
NURSE24HR
Description: 
You won't feel deprived of your traditional mayonnaise-based dressing here!  This light-but-yummy treat is perfect for a cool summer lunch on a bed of lettuce.
