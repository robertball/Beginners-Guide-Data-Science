Title:
Magic Cookie Bars II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
1 (14 oz) can Eagle Brand®
1 (12 oz) pkg chocolate chips
1 1/3 cups flaked coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup Heath® toffee chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350&#0176;
Melt butter in the 9x13 baking dish.
Sprinkle the rolled oats over the butter.
Layer the remaining ingredients over the rolled oats in the order given.
Baked for 25 minutes or until lightly browned.
Cool and cut into bars.
Store loosely covered at room temp or wrap well and freeze.
