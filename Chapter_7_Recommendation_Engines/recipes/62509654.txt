Title:
Hash & Ham Eggs Benedict Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium white potatoes shredded
2 green onion chopped fine
1 small apple diced small
1 egg beaten
2 tbsp. flour
salt & Pepper to taste
oil for frying
4 slices of ham
4 eggs poached
hollandaise sauce
finely chopped parsley
fresh ground nutmeg

Directions:
Combine shredded potatoe, green onion, apple, flour and salt & pepper together and stir to mix.  Add beaten egg and form into patties.  Heat oil in skillet and drop patties into it, frying approximately 5 minutes on each side or until they are a nice golden brown.  As they are finishing, add sliced ham to the pan just to heat thru.  Meanwhile, poach 4 eggs.  Quickly drain potatoe patties on papertowel and put two on each plate, top with a slice of ham, then a poached egg and pour hollandaise sauce over the top.  Sprinkle with fresh ground nutmeg and chopped parsley.
