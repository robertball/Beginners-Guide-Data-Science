Title:
Marvelous Mini Mexican Quiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017762.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp cream cheese
4 Tbsp butter
2/3 C flour
3 eggs
1/2 C milk or cream
1 C monterey jack cheese, shredded
1/2 C ground chorizo sausage, browned
1 4oz can diced green chilies
1/2 C red onion, diced
2 Tbsp fresh cilantro, minced
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground cayenne

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In mixing bowl, combine cream cheese and butter.  Mix in flour until dough has formed.  Shape dough into 24 balls, refrigerate while making egg mixture.
Whisk eggs and cream together.  Mix in cheese, sausage, green chilies, red onion, cilantro, garlic powder, black pepper, cumin, salt, and cayenne.
Grease mini muffin pan.  Place a dough ball in each well of pan and gently press to create shells.
Spoon egg mixture into each shell to fill.  Bake for 25 minutes or until center is set. Can be served with sour cream and diced tomato on top.
