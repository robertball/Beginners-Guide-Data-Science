Title:
Melissa's Greens!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg thick sliced bacon
2 pkg diced or julienne ham
2 bunches green onion, chopped
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoon salt
2 tablespoon pepper
4 large cans chicken broth (enough to cover greens) Red pepper flakes to taste
2 bags of ready washed/chopped greens

Directions:
Heat pot to medium/medium high. Add bacon, and cook until crisp. Crumble bacon (leave bacon grease in the pan).  Add ham and fry until golden brown. Add onion, and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.  Add garlic, and cook until just fragrant. Add collard greens, and fry until they start to wilt.
Pour in chicken broth, and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes, or until greens are tender.
