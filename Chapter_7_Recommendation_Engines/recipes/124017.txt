Title:
Whipped Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/729824.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3 tablespoons milk

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese. Gradually beat in milk until light and fluffy. Serve on toast, English muffins or bagels.

Submitter: 
Rita  Addicks
Description: 
When I want a break from butters or jams, I like to whip up this simple spread. All of the variations are wonderful and taste great on toasted breads.
