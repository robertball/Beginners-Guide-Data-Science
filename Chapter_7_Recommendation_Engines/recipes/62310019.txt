Title:
Cathy's  Irish Soda Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all purpose flour
1 Tablespoon baking powder
2/3 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 Tablespoon melted butter
2 cups buttermilk
2 eggs beaten
2 cups baking raisins
1 Tablespoon caraway seeds(optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease one 9"x 5" loaf pan.
Mix all dry ingredients in large bowl.
Add raisins and mix.
Add buttermilk,eggs and melted butter mix well
Add to greased pan
Bake on middle rack of oven
Check after 60 minutes
If cake tester comes out dry bread is done
If not cook 5-10 minutes longer and test again
After 5 minutes remove from pan, cool on wire rack.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe was given to me by my dear friend who was from the Emerald Isle. It is a favorite of family and friends. A moist not a dry soda bread.
