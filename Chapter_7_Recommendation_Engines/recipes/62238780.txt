Title:
Eclair-Like Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 pkgs instant vanilla pudding
2 cups milk
1 tub Cool Whip
1 box Honey Graham Crackers (about 3 packages)
Chocolate Icing:
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup cocoa
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Mix pudding powder with milk. Fold in cool whip. Line a 13x9 pan with graham crackers. Pour half of pudding mixture over crackers. Place another layer of graham crackers, then another layer of pudding, followed by a final layer of graham crackers. Glaze with chocolate icing and refrigerate for 5 hours.
For chocolate icing, boil milk, sugar and cocoa 1 minute. Mix in butter and vanilla. Let stand 50 seconds.
