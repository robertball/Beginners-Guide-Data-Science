Title:
Lemon Garlic Chicken Kababs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tbsp. Lemon juice
2 Tbsp. oil
5-6 cloves garlic(or more), minced
1 tsp. lemon and pepper seasoning
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/8 tsp. cayenne
4 chicken breasts, cubed

Directions:
If you are using wooden skewers, soak in water while marinating chicken.  Combine lemon juice, oil, garlic, and seasonings.  Add to cubed chicken and marinate for at least 30 minutes.  Thread chicken on skewers and place on a medium-high heat grill.  Turn a couple of times to grill evenly for about 15 minutes or until done.  Serve with grilled veggies and rice.

Submitter: 
Shannon LePage
Description: 
All the flavors blend wonderfully.  It is one of our favorites for the BBQ.
