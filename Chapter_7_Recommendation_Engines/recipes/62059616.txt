Title:
Fruity Cottage Cheese "Soup" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. package of Cool Whip Free or Lite
32oz Low fat or fat free cottage cheese
1 package of sugar free orange jello.
1 large can of crushed pinapple, drained
2 small or 1 large can of manderin oranges, drained.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together, chill, and enjoy

Submitter: 
PattiMcQ
Description: 
Cottage cheese mixed with pineapple, maderin oranges, Cool Whip and jello mix. Can be used as a snack or as a desert. Healty and Yummy!
