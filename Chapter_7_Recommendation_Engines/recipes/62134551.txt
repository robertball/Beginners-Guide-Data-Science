Title:
Country Apple Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
1/3 c butter, softened
1 egg
1 (20 oz.) can apple pie filling
1/2 c. brown sugar, packed
1/2 c. walnuts, chopped
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 c. sour cream
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a bowl, combine cake mix, butter and egg
Mix at low speed with electric mixer to form a crumbly dough.  Press mixture into bottom of an ungreased 9 x 13 inch pan.  Spread apple pie filling over top.
Combine brown sugar, nuts and cinnamon in small bowl and sprinkle over pie filling.
Combine sour cream, egg and vanilla, pour over top.
Bake 40 to 55 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
LMLSCA
Description: 
A bit like cake and a bit like pudding, this sweet dessert is real comfort food. It tastes great with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
