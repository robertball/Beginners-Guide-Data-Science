Title:
Lentil Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/8 cups green lentils or 2 cups cooked, drained
2 1/4 cups water for cooking lentils
1 tablespoon olive or sesame oil
1/2 cup onion, minced
1/4 cup carrot, grated
1/4 cup celery, diced
1/4 cup bell pepper, diced (opt.)
4 slices GF bread, torn into small pieces
1/2 oats, dry
2 egg or sub (3 T. chia seed & 1/2 c. water)
1 cup vegetable broth
2 tablespoons tomato paste or 1 diced tomato
1 tablespoon miso (opt.)
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 T garlic minced
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried parsley

Directions:
Combine lentils and water in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer until tender, about 40 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Saute onion, carrot and bell in oil until tender.
In a large bowl, mix together 2 cups cooked lentils, bread, eggs, broth, tomato paste, basil, garlic, black pepper, parsley, sauteed vegetables.
Spread into prepared pan.
Bake for 40 minutes.
Cover top with foil or sauce if using and continue baking another 10 minutes.
Let sit for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
A perfect dinner loaf, but would also would make great sandwiches for lunch. This recipe is easy and after you've cooked the lentils, everything else is stirred into the pot, and transferred to a loaf pan to bake.Modified from: Lentil Loaf
