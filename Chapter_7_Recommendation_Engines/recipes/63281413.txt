Title:
Best Healthy Pizza Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pizza Crust (store bought or homeade)
olive oil
fresh garlic minced
italian seasoning
yellow squash
fresh mushrooms
fresh spinach
tomatoes
artichoke hearts jared and sliced
black olives sliced
parm cheese
motz cheese

Directions:
pre cook bread dough as directed (400 degrees 5 min)
coat the pre cooked crust with olive oil and sprinkle the fresh garlic and italian seasons
layer the rest of the veggies ending with the motz and then parm cheese.
bake 400 for 6 mintues or until lightly browed

Submitter: 
Love2cook32
Description: 
I came up with this fantastic pizza with fresh veggies I had on hand one night.  It is not only healthy but so versatile as you can use whatever you have on hand.  This will give you the fix on the more tratidional high fat pizza.
