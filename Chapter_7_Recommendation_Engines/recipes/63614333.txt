Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup blue cheese or ranch salad dressing
1/2 cup FRANK'S REDHOT Sauce
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese or shredded mozzarella cheese
2 cans (12.5 oz. each)  White Premium Chunk Chicken Breast in Water, drained

Directions:
HEAT oven to 350Â°F. Place cream cheese into deep baking dish. Stir until smooth.
MIX in salad dressing, Frank's RedHot Sauce and cheese. Stir in chicken.
BAKE 20 min. or until mixture is heated through; stir. Garnish as desired. Serve Crackers or Tortilla Chips.

Submitter: 
NessTheMess
Description: 
This is one of my favorite dishes to take to parties and gatherings. I've always served it with Chips and a side of celery.
