Tri-Tip Rub Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 tablespoons garlic powder
1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon black pepper
1 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
Mix seasonings together.
Rub over tri tip and refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours
