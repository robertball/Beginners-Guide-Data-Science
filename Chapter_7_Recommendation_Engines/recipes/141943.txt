Title:
Pink Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413440.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 cup mustard powder
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup water

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and mustard powder. Whisk in the oil, mayonnaise and ketchup until well blended. Gradually whisk in water. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours before serving. Use within 5 days.
