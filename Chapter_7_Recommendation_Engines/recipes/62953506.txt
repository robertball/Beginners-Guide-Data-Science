Title:
Traditional Mashed Potatoes by Deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 cup warm milk
1/2 cup butter or margarine
3/4 teaspoon salt
Dash pepper

Directions:
Place potatoes in a saucepan and cover with water. Cover and bring to a boil; cook for 20-25 minutes or until very tender. Drain well. Warm the milk, butter, salt and pepper and add.  mash until light and fluffy.
