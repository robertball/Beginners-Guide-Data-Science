Title:
Tuna/Salmon Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/2 cup Bisquick
1/2 cup melted butter
1 1/2 cups of milk
1/4 t. salt
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 can salmon or 3 5oz. cans of tuna
1/4 chopped onion (opt.)

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients.
Pour in buttered pie pan.
Mix cheese & salmon together.
Press mixture in egg mixture to submerge.
Bake at 350o for 45 minutes.
