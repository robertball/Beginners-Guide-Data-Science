Title:
Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz frozen California Blend Vegetables
1 large jar Cheez Whiz(or mix spicy with regular)
1 sleeve Ritz crushed
1 stick butter

Directions:
Cook vegs, melt cheese and mix with vegs, crush ritz and mix with melted butter. Crumble over top. 350F 30 minutes or until brown and bubbly.
