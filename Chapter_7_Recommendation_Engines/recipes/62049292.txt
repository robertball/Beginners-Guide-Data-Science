Title:
Chicken with vegetables mustard and dill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14oz can chicken broth
1 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
2 teaspoons dill weed
2 teaspoons dijon mustard
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into pieces
1 cup thinly sliced celery
1 cup carrots thinly sliced
1 onion halved and sliced
3 garlic cloves chopped
1 cup sliced mushrooms
hot cooked rice

Directions:
Measure 1/4 cup of broth and set aside. In bowl whisk together remaining broth, cornstarch,dill, mustard and pepper set aside.Spray nonstick skillet with cooking spray season chicken with salt and pepper and saute 5 minutes. Add the reserved 1/4 cup of broth and simmer covered for 8 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet with slotted spoon set aside .In same skillet add celery, carrot, onion and garlic cook 3 minutes.Stir in mushrooms cook 3 minutes until all vegetables are crisp tender. Add reserved broth mixture bring to a boil. Stir and cook 1 minute. Reduce heat return chicken to pan and heat through. Serve over rice.
