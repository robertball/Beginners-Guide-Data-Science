Title:
Super Simple Kielbasa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7oz. elbow macaroni
1 tablespoon oil
3 garlic cloves minced
1 to 1 1/2 pounds kielbasa sliced and quartered
1 1/2 cups chopped cabbage
1/2 cup chopped carrot
4 scallions sliced including tops
7 cups chicken broth
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
2-3 drops hot sauce

Directions:
Prepare  macaroni and drain. Set aside.
In pot heat oil cook garlic,kielbasa,cabbage,carrot and onion till tender. Add broth and all seasonings. Simmer for 5 minutes. Add pasta and heat through.
