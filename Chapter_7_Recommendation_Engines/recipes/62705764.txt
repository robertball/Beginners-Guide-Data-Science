Title:
Raspberry Poppyseed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T sugar & 1 T Splenda
3/4 C  vinegar (Mix Raspberry & White Wine vinegars)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground dry mustard
1 teaspoon grated onion
3/4 C vegetable oil (Mix Canola and Olive)
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, combine sugar, vinegar, salt, mustard and onion and process for 20 seconds.  With blender or food processor on high, gradually add oil in a slow, steady stream.  Stir in poppy seeds.

Submitter: 
schuylcountians
Description: 
This slightly thick, sweet and pungent raspberry poppy seed dressing takes only minutes and is a wonderful complement to almost any salad!Modified from: Poppyseed Dressing
