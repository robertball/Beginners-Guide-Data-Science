Title:
Mexican Venison Meatlof Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb ground venison
1/2 can corn
1 egg
3/4-1 cup crushed tortilla chips
1.5 cup chunky salsa
1 packet taco seasoning
1/2 can black beans (optional)
Topping
salsa
shredded Mexican cheese

Directions:
Mix venison, egg, chips, corn, salsa and taco seasoning (and beans if you are using). Pat into 2 loaf pans (or 4 mini loaf pans). Bake for 50 minutes at 350. Top loaves with additional taco chips, salsa and shredded cheese, bake for another 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Angie Fuller
Description: 
I came up with this recipe after I couldn't find a Mexican meatloaf anywhere. Definitely a keeper!
