Title:
Chipotle Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. chicken breast, with bone (3-4 large pieces)
Oil
2 Bay leaves
3 small Spanish onions
2 large, ripe tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can chipotle peppers w/ adobo sauce
Salt
Pepper
1 pkg. corn tostadas
Optional toppings: Queso fresco, lettuce, pico de gallo, sour cream

Directions:
Place chicken breasts, 1 quartered onion, and 2 bay leaves in a large pot.  Season with salt and pepper.  Cover with water and boil until chicken breasts are fork tender (approx. 30 minutes).  Remove chicken and let cool.  Debone and shred the chicken.
Meanwhile, blend 2 tomatoes, 1 onion, 2 cloves of garlic, and the can of chipotle peppers in the blender, adding a little water as needed. Set aside.
In a medium pot, saut√© 1 thinly sliced onion in 2-3 Tb. oil until translucent.  Add shredded chicken and tomato mixture.  Salt and pepper to taste.
Let chicken simmer, stirring occasionally, for approx. 30 minutes or until the liquid evaporates.
Serve on tostadas with queso fresco, chopped lettuce, pico de gallo and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Laura
Description: 
This recipe is simple, yet very authentic, and SO flavorful!  Be warned that it is VERY spicy.  Adding an extra tomato or using only half the can of chipotle peppers will tone down the heat.  Sour cream topping is a must!  Serve with yellow rice.
