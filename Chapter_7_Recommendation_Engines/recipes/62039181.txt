Title:
Watermelon Bombe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lime sherbet
Pineapple or lemon sherbet or sorbet
2 Raspberry sorbet
12 oz. bag Chocolate chips

Directions:
In a round metal bowl, line with plastic wrap.  Using slightly softened lime sherbet, line the bowl with your green layer.  Next, use the pineapple or lemon sherbet for the next layer.  Place softened  raspberry sorbet in a separate bowl and mix in the chocolate chips.  Pack the raspberry sorbet with chips onto the last layer filling the bowl.  Take another layer of plastic to cover.  Freeze 24 hours.  Slice to look like watermelon slices.

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
Perfect summer dessert.  Not for those hot days but warm nights.  Very cute.  Easy to layer.  Will get tons of compliments
