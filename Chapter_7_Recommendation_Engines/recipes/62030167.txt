Title:
Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken pieces
2 quarts water
1 large onion -- chopped
2 stalks celery with leaves -- chopped
1/2 cup fresh parsley -- chopped
5 whole peppercorns
6 each chicken bouillon cubes
1 each bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1 dash dried thyme
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup sliced carrot
1/2 cup sliced celery
1/4 cup minced onion
1 Tablespoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 cup rice -- un-cooked OR
1 package egg noodles

Directions:
-Combine chicken, water, onion, celery, parsley, peppercorns, boullion cubes, bay leaf, celery seed, thyme, and salt in large pot.
-Cook over low heat for 4 hours.
-Strain vegetables and spices from broth.
-Return broth to pot.
-set aside chicken.
-Discard vegetables and spices.
-Refrigerate broth for 30 minutes.
-Remove skin and bones from chicken and cut meat into bite-size pieces.
-Skim fat from top of cooled broth.
-Bring broth to boil.
-Add remaining ingredients, including chicken meat.
-Simmer for 30 minutes.
