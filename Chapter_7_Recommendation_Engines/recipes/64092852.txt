Title:
Fresh Quick Pickled Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1394370.jpg
Ingredients:
About 1 Cup each.
1 small to medium cucumber, thinly sliced
1 medium to large tomato, thinly sliced
1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
1/3 cup vegetable or canola oil
1/3 cup white distilled vinegar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Add sliced vegetables to a large bowl.
Add vinegar, oil, salt, and pepper.  Stir to coat; cover and let set in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Try this Quick Easy Cooling side dish next time you are pressed for time or just want a fresh cool quick Salad!
