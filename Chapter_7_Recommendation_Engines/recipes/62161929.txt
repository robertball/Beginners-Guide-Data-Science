Title:
Non-fat Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Low fat salad dressing
1/2 cup non-fat yogurt
1/4 cup non-fat cottage cheese
1 garlic clove, very finely minced or run through a garlic press
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
2 tsps. vinegar
2 tsps. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. dry dill weed
1 TBS fresh parsley, finely minced
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Mix everything together.  Taste and adjust seasonings.  If too tart, crush a nuitrasweet tablet and add half.  Mix and taste again.

Submitter: 
Scott Simmons
Description: 
I'm tired of store-bought non-fat salad dressing.  What the heck is guar gum?!  This recipe uses refrigerator-pantry ingredients for a good, clean dressing that is non-fat but packed with flavor.  Especially great for seafood salads.
