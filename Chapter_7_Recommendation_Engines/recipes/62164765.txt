Title:
Roasted Potatoes with Bacon-FAST & EASY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 Medium to Large Potatoes - washed and cut to bite size
1/2 lb sliced smoked bacon
1/3 cup olive oil
1 tblspn garlic powder
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
So easy!  Preheat oven on ROAST to 400 degrees.
Wash and cut potatoes. Put in tossing bowl.
Add olive oil, garlic powder, salt & pepper.
Toss and place on baking sheet.
Place bacon slices on top of potatoes.
Roast 30-40 minutes until potatoes are browned.
