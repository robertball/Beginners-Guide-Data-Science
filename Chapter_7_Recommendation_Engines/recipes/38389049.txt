Title:
Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
24 flour tortillas
1 package shredded mexi blend cheese
1 can medium black olives, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can mild enchilada sauce
1 can medium or hot enchilada sauce
1 cup vegetable oil
1 container sour cream
sliced jalepenos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In large skillet, brown ground beef, with diced onion until cooked, through.
Remove from heat, stir in black olives and half can of medium or hot enchilada sauce, and handful of cheese.
Pour vegetable oil into a shallow dish.
Take tortilla and run through oil, covering entirely, let excess run off.
Place 2 tablespoons of beef mixture in center of tortilla.
Fold bottom of tortilla up to middle of tortilla, while holding that, wrap left and right sides toward middle, overlapping one over the other.
Place seam side down in 9x13 dish.
Place enchiladas tightly together in dish.
When done, pour sauce over all enchiladas, sprinke with remaining cheese and jalepenos, if you like.
Bake covered for 30-35 minutes.
Remove foil and bake additional 10 minutes.
