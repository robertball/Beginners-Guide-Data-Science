Title:
Creamy Bacon and Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986762.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (270 g) tub PHILADELPHIA Savoury Garlic Cooking Creme
3 tablespoons white vinegar
8 cups small broccoli florets
1/2 cup Oscar Mayer Real Bacon Recipe Pieces
1/2 cup shredded Cracker Barrel Old Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup chopped red onions
1/4 cup sunflower seeds

Directions:
Mix first 2 ingredients in large bowl.
Add remaining ingredients; toss to coat.
Refrigerate 1 hour.

Submitter: 
PHILADELPHIA Cooking Creme
Description: 
Broccoli florets, bacon pieces, shredded cheese, chopped onions, and sunflower seeds are tossed together in a creamy garlic sauce.
