Title:
Marinated Grilled Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp red wine vinegar (or apple cider vinegar)
4-5 tbsp dijon mustard
1/2 cup olive oil
Dried oregano to taste
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
3-4 peppers (one of each color is nice), sliced into strips
1 large onion, sliced into strips
1 package of sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Mix vinegar, mustard, oil, oregano, salt, and pepper in bowl and whisk until oil is blended into mixture.
Pour liquid mixture over sliced vegetables.  Marinate in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, preferably 2 hours.
Preheat grill.  Place vegetables on grill pan and cook until tender and slightly charred.
