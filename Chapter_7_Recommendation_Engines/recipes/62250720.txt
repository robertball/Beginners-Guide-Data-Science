Title:
Bj's Power Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups quick-cooking rolled oats
3/4 cup chocolate protein powder
1 cup wheat and barley nugget cereal (e.g. Grape-Nutsâ„¢)
1 teaspoon to 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
4 beaten egg whites, or equal egg substitute
1/2 cup applesauce
3/4 cup honey
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 cup canola oil
1/2 cup salted sunflower seeds
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup sliced almonds
1 (7 ounce) bag chopped dried mixed fruit, apricots, cranberries etc.
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/3 cup flax seed, ground
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).  Line a 9 x 13 inch baking pan with wax or parchment paper.
In a large bowl, stir together the oats, protein powder, cereal, and cinnamon.  Add the eggs, applesauce, honey, brown sugar, oil and vanilla.  Mix well.  Stir in the sunflower seeds, flax seed, walnuts, almonds and dried fruit.  Spread mixture evenly in the prepared pan.
Bake 30 minutes, or until firm and lightly browned around the edges.  Let cool.  Use the foil to lift from the pan. Cut into bars or squares, and store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Brad J Hall

Full of dried fruit and nuts these power bars are delicious and much more filling than most granola bars.Modified from: Jo-Ann's Power Bars
