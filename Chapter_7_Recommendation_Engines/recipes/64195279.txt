Title:
Spinach Stuffed Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small acorn squash
2 T. olive oil
1 10 oz package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 8 oz. container ricotta cheese
1 T. grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Cut squash crosswise in half. Scoop out seeds and fibers. Brush insides and outsides of squash with olive oil. Place in large shallow roasting pan. Bake, uncovered, 35-40 minutes or until tender.
In medium bowl, combine spinach, ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and spices. Spoon equal amounts of spinach mixture in squash halves. Bake uncovered for 15 minutes.
