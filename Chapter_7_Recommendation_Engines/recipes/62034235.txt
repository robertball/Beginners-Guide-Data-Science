Title:
The Ultimate “CONEY ISLAND” Hot Dog Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chuck
1 onion (processed fine in food processor)
1-cup beer
1 6oz can Tomato paste
2 teaspoons cumin
2 Tablespoons Chili powder
2 Tablespoons  paprika
1 tablespoon Oregano
1 ½ teaspoons Onion Powder
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
½ teaspoon celery salt
1 teaspoon Caraway Seed
½ teaspoon Cayenne pepper (more if your bold)
1teaspoon Worchester sauce
1 teaspoon white vinegar
1 ½ Tablespoons Brown sugar
1-Tablespoon  Brown Mustard

Directions:
Brown ground chuck, onion and vinegar in a saucepan. Making sure the meat mixture is crumbles up fine as it cooks. Cook till it is almost completey brown; drain meat; return to stove and add the remaining ingredients. On  Med-high heat; bring mixture to a boil then reduce heat and simmer uncovered to desired consistency (Approximately 1 hour).

Submitter: 
DIXYCHIK
Description: 
This addictive recipe is for the seriously discriminating Coney Dog lover. Just like Coney Island, each ingredient blends into a perfect marriage with the classic old taste sure to please a crowd! Tastes even better the next day and freezes well.
