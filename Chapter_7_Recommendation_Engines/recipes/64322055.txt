Title:
Edamame Snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray to coat pan
1 cup(s) edamame, shelled, frozen
2 pinch salt, Kosher

Directions:
Heat a medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Coat pan with cooking spray and add edamame.
Saute edamame until heated through, about 5 minutes. Season with Kosher salt, if desired. Serve immediately.
