Title:
Sweet and Easy Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter, melted
1 (8.5 ounce) package corn bread mix
1 (14oz) can of cream corn
1 (14oz) can of whole corn, drained
1 egg, lightly beaten
3 tbsp sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
Mix corn bread mix with melted butter, sour cream, and egg
Incorporate corn into the mixture
Pour mixture into 9x5 glass dish
Bake for 35 min

Submitter:

Description: 
My aunt and I have been making this for years. Its simply the best and I don't just have it at Thanksgiving. It pairs very nicly with poultry.
