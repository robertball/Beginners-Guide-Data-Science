Title:
Wonton Soup With A Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/832676.jpg
Ingredients:
8 c chicken broth
3 Tbsp soy sauce
2 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp rice wine vinegar
2 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp minced garlic
1 1/2 tsp sriracha
salt to taste
8 c water
20 wontons

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except water and wontons in a large pot over medium heat.  Allow to simmer at least 10 minutes.
While broth is simmering, bring water to a boil in another medium pot.  When water is boiling, add the wontons and allow to boil for 5 minutes.
Remove wontons from water with a slotted spoon and add to broth.
