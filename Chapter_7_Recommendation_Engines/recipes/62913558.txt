Title:
Fresh Salsa I (Customized Smaller) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/710240.jpg
Ingredients:
3 jalapeno chile peppers (depending on hotness)
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped or minced
1 onion,chopped and divided in half
1 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 (14 ounce) cans diced  tomatoes
1/2 of a 4 1/2 oz can green chile's
Juice of one lime
1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro (more or less to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place jalapeno chile peppers on a medium baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 17 - 20 minutes, or until roasted. Remove from heat and chop off stems and coarsely slice.
Place jalapeno chile peppers, half the chopped onion, garlic, sugar, salt, cumin, cilantro, lime juice, green chile's, and 1 can diced tomatoes in a blender or food processor. Chop using the pulse setting for a few seconds.
Remove to a medium bowl and add the 2nd can of petite diced tomatoes and stir.  Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Kilyena
Description: 
This all-purpose salsa is great on tortilla chips, tacos and other Mexican-style favorites. Roasted jalapeno chile peppers give the salsa an excellent flavor.Modified from: Fresh Salsa I
