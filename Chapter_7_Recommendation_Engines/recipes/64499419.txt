Title:
Big Ray's Crumb-Topped Cheesy Zucchini Casserole Recipe

Ingredients:
4 zucchini (1-1/2 lb.), cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices
6 green onions, chopped
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup  Ranch Dressing
1-1/2 cups Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
16 round buttery crackers, crushed

Directions:
Heat oven to 350ÂºF.
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients except cracker crumbs . Spread into a greased 11" x 7"  dish.
Top with crumbs and bake 30 min. or until heated through.
