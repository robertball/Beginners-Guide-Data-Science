Title:
Arugula Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups (or 1 can, drained) chickpeas
2 cloves garlic
1/4 cup tahini
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup fresh arugula, packed
2 tablespoons olive oil
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a food processor, blend chickpeas, garlic, tahini, lemon juice, and arugula.
While blending, drizzle in olive oil as mixture comes together, until smooth.  Add extra lemon juice or water, as needed, to achieve desired consistency.
Taste. Add salt and pepper, to taste. Blend once more.
Serve, or chill for an hour to allow flavors to blend.  Goes great with carrot sticks and celery as a dip, or as a spread on bread or sandwiches.

Submitter: 
Poto
Description: 
Spicy arugula is a delightful parsley alternative in this hummus. Excellent as a vegetable dip or a sandwich spread.
