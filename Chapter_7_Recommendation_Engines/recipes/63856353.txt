Title:
wild rice orzo and mushroom casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orzo
1 cup wild rice
2 1/2 cups beef broth
8 cups mushrooms sliced
1T oil
2T butter
1/4 cup parsley chopped

Directions:
step1- cook orzo according to package directions
cook wild rice in the broth according to package directions
step 2 - saute mushrooms in oil and butter and cook until liquid has almost evaporated.
step 3 - add mushrooms and parsley to rice and orzo mix well
step 4 bake at 350 degrees 30 min or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Shelley Dahl
Description: 
A Holiday favorite from Minnesota. Serve with Turkey. The leftovers are a great addition to cream of chicken or turkey soup.
