Title:
Chocolate Chip Zucchini Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup margarine or butter, softened
1/4 cup oil
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
2-1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup buttermilk (or sour milk)
2 cups shredded zucchini
1/2 to 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°.  Grease and flour 13X9-inch pan.  In large bowl, combine sugar, margarine, oil, vanilla and eggs; beat well.  Add flour, cocoa, baking soda and buttermilk; blend well.  Fold in zucchini, chocolate chips and nuts.  Spread in greased and floured pan.
Bake at 350° for 35-45 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool completely.  Frost if desired.
