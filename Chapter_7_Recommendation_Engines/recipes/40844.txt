Title:
Robert's Rosemary Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/819149.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 teaspoon dried minced garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix rosemary, garlic, salt, and pepper. Rub onto both sides of steaks before grilling as desired.

Submitter: 
RMUTH
Description: 
Quick, easy, and (most importantly) delicious rub gives steaks a refined and flavorful taste.  Particularly good on a grilled rib-eye. Not your run-of-the-mill rub!
