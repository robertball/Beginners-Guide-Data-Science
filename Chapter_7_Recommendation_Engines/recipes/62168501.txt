Title:
butternut squash- stuffed shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 package of large pasta shells
-2 medium butternut squash
-3 or 4 cloves garlic
-1 medium, red onion
-1 quart cream
1 sprig rosemary
3/4 stick butter
2 tbls fresh goat cheese.
2 tbls brown sugar
3 tbls flour
parsley for garnish
1 package spinach

Directions:
-In a large pot, boil the butternut squash. Cook it till its soft to a fork- poke.
-Peel the skin and put in a food processor.
-Depending how much garlic you can take, blend 2 to 3 cloves in with a slash of cream a forth stick butter and a few rosemary pines. Add salt to taste
-Cut the onion in 1/4 inch rings and put it in a pan with enough oil to cover the pan. Add the brown sugar and still until the onions are completely cooked and a little sweet,on low heat.
-While waiting for the onion, on low heat melt the butter with the flour completely. Add the rest of the cream. Slowly add the goat cheese till completly melted. Add a sprig of rosemary.
(If you have paprika and nutmeg add a dash)
Cook the sauce untill it becomes thick then add the onions.
-In a large pot, cook the noodles, very aldante because the cook more in the oven.
-In glass, medium baking pan, cover the bottom with the whole package of spinach. Always grease pans first.
- stuff the shells with the mashed squash so that the are a little over-filled. Line then on top of the bed of spinach.
-Bake it in the oven at 375 for 10 minutes.
-serve the spinah and shell then drizzle a healthy amount of the sauce on top, garnish with parsley.
