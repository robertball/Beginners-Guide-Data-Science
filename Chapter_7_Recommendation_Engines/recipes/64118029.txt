Title:
Chicken Lazone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp chili powder
1 1/2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper, or to taste
2 lbs chicken breasts, sliced
1 cup cream
1/4 cup butter, divided

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients and sprinkle on chicken. In large saute pan, melt half the butter and saute chicken until cooked through, about 8 minutes. Remove chicken. Add remaining butter and cream to skillet. Lower heat and simmer until sauce thickens, about 6 minutes. Serve over chicken.
