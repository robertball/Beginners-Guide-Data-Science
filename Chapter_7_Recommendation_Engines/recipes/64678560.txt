Title:
POPCORN CHICKEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4802940.jpg
Ingredients:
chicken legs,oil,salt, pepper,breadcrumbs,sesame oil,soy sauce,chili oil

Directions:
1.Wash the chicken leg thoroughly and cut it into smaller pieces.
2.Add all seasonings and stir for about 30 minutes.
3.Preheat the Air Fryer to 392? for 5 minutes.
4.Put the chicken pieces into fryer and bake for 10-15 minutes.
5.Serve and enjoy
