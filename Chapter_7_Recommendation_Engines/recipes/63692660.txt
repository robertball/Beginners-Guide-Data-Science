Title:
Quick Chicken & Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (18.6 oz each) Progresso Rich & Hearty chicken pot pie style soup
1 can (7.5 oz) refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
Chopped parsley, if desired

Directions:
In 10 inch non-stick skillet, heat soup over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until bubbly.
Separate dough into 10 biscuits; cut each into quarters.  Place biscuit quarters on top of hot soup.  Simmer uncovered 10 min.
Cover; simmer an additional 8-10 min or until biscuits are no longer doughy.
Sprinkle with parsley.
