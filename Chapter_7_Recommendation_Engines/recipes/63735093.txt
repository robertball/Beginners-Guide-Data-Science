Title:
Homemade Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037119.jpg
Ingredients:
red ripe tomatoes (all that will fill a roasting pan), quartered
2 onions, peeled and quartered (separate layers)
8 cloves garlic, whole
1 cup of herbs (ie. rosemary, thyme, basil, oregano), coarsely chopped
olive oil, enough to drizzle
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
preheat oven to 450 (this will be lower depending on your particular oven)
put all ingredients into large roasting pan, drizzle with olive oil, add salt and pepper
mix together
heat in oven (without lid) for one hour
After one hour, stir. Return to oven.
Stir approx every 20 mins over the next hr. You will roast these ingredients for a total of 2 hours, start to finish.
Remove from oven and let stand to cool.
After cooling, ladle into blender and pulse until desired consistency is reached.
pour approx 2-3 cups of tomato mixture into quart-sized freezer bags. Label and freeze.

Submitter: 
Dede
Description: 
This is a wonderful homemade sauce for pasta, spaghetti, stuffed zucchini, or whatever else you desire that needs an Italian lift
