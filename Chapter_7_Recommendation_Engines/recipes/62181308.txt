Title:
Oma's Rum or Bourbon Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c vanilla wafers, crushed very fine
1 c pecans, chopped find
1 c powdered sugar
2 tbsp Dutch Cocoa
1 1/2 tsp light corn syrup
1/4 c rum (or bourbon, the better quality the smoother)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Form 1" balls with hands. Roll in powdered sugar, and store in airtight container.
DIFFERENT VARIATION:
Substitute ginger snaps or chocolate wafers for the vanilla wafers. Omit the cocoa if using chocolate wafers.
Also can roll the balls in cocoa, or chopped nuts, or colored sugar crystals, or sprinkles instead of the powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
My mother passed this recipe for the christmas nugget.
It packs a kick, but is very delicious in a decadent way. Be careful how many are consumed before driving.
