Title:
Zu's Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/579308.jpg
Ingredients:
3 c. All purpose flour , sifted
5 tsp. Baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. Salt
6 tbsp. Sugar
2 c. Milk
6 tbsp. Vegetable oil
4 eggs

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly. Do not overmix or they won't rise. A few lumps is fine.
Heat a skillet with a little oil to a med-low heat.
Using a 1/4 c.measure, scoop out the batter and pour into pan.
Turn pancakes over once bubbles start to form and fry until brown..
Serve warm with butter and syrup.

Submitter: 
hobbyzu
Description: 
This has been the pancake recipe for my family since I was able to eat them. I don't know where my mother got this recipe but it's the best I've ever had. They rise up nice and high. I sometimes add vanilla and spices like cinnamon and nutmeg. Very tasty!!
