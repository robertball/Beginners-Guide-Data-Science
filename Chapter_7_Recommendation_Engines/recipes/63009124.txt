Title:
Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs OreIda Southern Style Hash Browns
8oz (2cups) Sharp Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 can Cream of Potato Soup
2-3 Tbl minced onion (to taste)
1 stick melted butter or margarine
8oz sour cream
salt & pepper to taste
Sprinkle with bread crumbs if desired

Directions:
Mix everything in a large bowl.  Spread in 9 by 13 greased baking pan.  Bake covered at 350 for 1 hour 15 minutes. Uncover for last 15 minutes if using bread crumbs.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Allumi
Description: 
Great side dish for potlucks or family dinners.
