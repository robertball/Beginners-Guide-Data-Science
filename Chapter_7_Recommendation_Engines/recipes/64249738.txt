Title:
Sesame Cheese Dip Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
4 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
1 teaspoon celery salt
1/4 cup garlic powder
2 cups sour cream

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and let it chill for 1 hour in the refrigerator.  Serve with chips, veggies, pita crackers.
