Title:
Sweet Cream Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4558017.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups whole milk
2 cups cream
4 egg yolks
5 T sugar
2 T cornstarch
2 t vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Combine milk and cream in a pot and heat over medium-low heat.
2. In a medium size bowl, whisk together egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch.
3. Pour 1/2 cup hot milk mixture into egg mixture; mix thoroughly. Repeat with remaining milk mixture. Pour mixture back into the pot.
4. Cook and stir custard over medium heat until thickened, about 5 minutes. Custard can be enjoyed warm or chilled.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
This is a smooth, rich, creamy vanilla custard in the true British style.  We love to enjoy it with fresh strawberries!
