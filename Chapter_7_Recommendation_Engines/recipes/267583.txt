Title:
White Peach-Lavender Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5650672.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds white peaches, pitted and shopped
1/2 tablespoon lavender honey
5 sprigs lavender flowers

Directions:
Combine peaches, honey, and lavender flowers in a microwave-safe bowl and cook for 5 minutes on high power. Discard lavender flowers.
Transfer peach mixture to a blender; blend until smooth. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour. Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
eloline
Description: 
White peaches and lavender are a lovely combination as long as you use the lavender sparingly to avoid an overwhelming lavender flavor. You can use yellow peaches if you can't find white ones.
