Title:
Tomatoey cheese ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 1/2 oz. can chopped tomatoes, well drained
1 8 oz. soft cream cheese
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup onions, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, pressed
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Stir together all the ingredients.  Shape into a ball or desired shape.  Cover and chill for at least 8 hours.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This is so easy and quick to make.  The chopped tomatoes in it gives it that extra pizzazz!!! And it's quite colorful too.  It's great on crackers.  I like to spread it on a piece of celery.
