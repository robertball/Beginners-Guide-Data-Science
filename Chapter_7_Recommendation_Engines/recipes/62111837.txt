Title:
Best Ever Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C. apples, coarse diced
2 eggs
1/2 C corn oil
2 C. sugar
1 C walnuts, large chop
1 tsp. vanilla
2 C. flour
2 tsp. cinnamon
3/4 tsp. salt
2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Put diced apples in a mixing bowl. Break 2 eggs over apples, mix well.  Add oil, sugar, nuts and vanilla. Mix well with wooden spoon.
Add flour, salt, cinnamon, and baking soda. Mix well and pour into greased 9x13 oblong pan. Bake 1 hour at 350.  Cool and sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Fall's coming. Get out the apples and bake this easy to make treat. Delicious and simple. You'll be very pleased with this cake and everyone will want you to share the recipe.
