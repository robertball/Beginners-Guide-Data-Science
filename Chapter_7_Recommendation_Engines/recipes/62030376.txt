Title:
Bookbinder Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp butter
1 large onion, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 large green pepper, diced
1 lb red snapper or grouper
1 cup beef bouillon
1 bay leaf
1 tsp Maggi
1 garlic clove, pressed
1 tsp pickling spice(placed in cheese cloth bag)
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups canned, diced, tomatoes
1 can baby clams
1 tsp MSG
sherry

Directions:
saute onion, celery, and green pepper in butter. Place red snapper in 1 quart of water; add beef bouillon, bay leaf.Maggi, garlic, pickling spice, salt and pepper. Cook for 15 minutes. Add vegetables, tomatoes, and clams to fish mixture. Cook to reduce to three fourths the original amount. Flake fish into bite size pieces. Add MSG. At serving time add 1 ounce of sherry to each 6 ounce serving of soup.
