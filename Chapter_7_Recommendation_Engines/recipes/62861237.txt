Title:
Stuffed Banana Pepper Poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small tomato chopped fine
1 small onion chopped fine
2 cups grated monteray jack cheese
1 small jalepeneo chopped fine
1 egg well beaten
1/2 cup fine cornmeal
1 lb thin sliced bacon
1 dozen banana peppers, seeds removed

Directions:
Combine everything up to the bacon
stuff into peppers
wrap in bacon
bake at 375

Submitter: 
Pam Morin
Description: 
great as a snack or with rice and beans for a meal
