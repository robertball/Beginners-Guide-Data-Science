Title:
Potato Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. butter or margarine
4 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced
1 10-oz. pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
Seasoned salt to taste
4 C. chicken broth
1 C. half-and-half
2 Tbsp. flour
2 C. cheddar cheese, shredded
2 Tbsp/ Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Melt margarine in large soup pot. Add peeled and sliced potatoes, frozen broccoli, and seasoned salt.
Saute 5 minutes until potatoes are fork tender. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil. Boil 2-3 minutes until potatoes are cooked completely through.
Mix half-and-half with flour; stir half-and-half mixture into boiling soup.
Bring soup to a boil again, stir in cheese. Reduce heat to medium. Continue stirring until cheese is fully melted.
Add Worcestershire sauce. Reduce heat to low and stir occasionally until ready to serve.
