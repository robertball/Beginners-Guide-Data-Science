Title:
Saucy Baked Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535247.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Marinara sauce (jar or home made!)
2 eggs
parmesan cheese-to sprinkle on after it bakes

Directions:
Place sauce in corning ware- add two eggs, cover and bake for 20 minutes at 425 degrees or until eggs are cooked to your liking. We enjoyed ours with some crusty Italian bread.
