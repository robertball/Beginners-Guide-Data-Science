Title:
Infused Olive Oils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/339161.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Extra Virgin Olive Oil (per mixture)
5 or 6 Thyme sprigs, hung & dried
3 or 4 Rosemary sprigs, hung & dried
2 or 3 Fresh garlic cloves, skin and ends removed
2 Tablespoons Dried Red Pepper flakes

Directions:
In a trivet or size appropriate glass spouted container, add 2T of pepper flakes or thyme and garlic cloves or roasemary and garlic cloves.
With a funnel, pour in olive oil.
Cover, swish around, and let sit for at least a week before using.
Add infused oils in place of plain oils for cooking or drizzle over salad.

Krystal
Description: 
These oil infusions make delicious cooking a snap!
