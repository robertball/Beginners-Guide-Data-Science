Title:
Pumkin Buckle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2		Cup		Margarine; melted
1		Cup		Flour
1		Cup		Sugar
4		tsp		Baking powder
1/2		tsp		Salt
1		Cup		Milk
1		tsp		Vanilla
FILLING:
1		Cup 		Evaporated milk
2				Eggs
1		Cup		Sugar
1		Tbs		Flour
3		Cups 		Pumpkin; cooked or canned
1/2		Cup		Brown sugar
1		tsp		Cinnamon
1/4		tsp		Ginger
1/4		tsp		Cloves(ground)
1/4		tsp		Nutmeg

Directions:
Bottom (crust) ;  Place margarine in  13x 9 in" pan (with 2-3" sides) place in oven to melt. Take out and cool slightly before adding rest of ingredients. Mix rest of ingredients and pour over margarine. DO NOT STIR
FILLING:
Beat filling ingredients together and pour over crust; DO NOT STIR!! Dot with butter (just a tablespoon or so in 8-10 dabs) and sprinkle with sugar (as much as you want. Depends on how many cavities you want.) Bake @ 350F for 55-60 min. until knife comes out clean.
.
