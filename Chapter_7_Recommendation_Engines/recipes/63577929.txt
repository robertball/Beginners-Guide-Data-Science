Title:
Hawaiian Salad, the best Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple tidbits
6 cups torn head lettuce; washed, dried well, put back in fridge to chill well so that it's very crisp
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise, Hellman's mayo.
1 Tablespoon sugar

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving 1 tablespoon juice. In a large bowl, combine greens, pineapple and cheese. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sugar and reserved pineapple juice; mix well. Pour over salad; toss to coat. Serve immediately.
