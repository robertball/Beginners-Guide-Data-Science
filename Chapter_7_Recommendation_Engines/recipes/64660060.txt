Ingredients:
1/4 cup Apple Cide Vinegar
1/3 cup olive oil
1 small shallot (or one lobe of a large), peeled, cored and quartered
2 teaspooons sugar free Dijon mustard
½ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Puree shallot, oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, salt and pepper in a blender until smooth(~30 seconds)
Store in the refrigerator up to 1 week.
Bring to room temperature before serving if it becomes solid.
