Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 lb. kidney beans
1/2 c. Catalina dressing
1/2 c. water
1 T. Chili powder
4 c. shredded lettuce
1/2 c. shredded cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef; add chopped onion and cook till tender.
Stir in kidney beans, dressing, water, and chili powder. Simmer 15 minutes.
Combine lettuce and cheese. Add meat sauce and toss. Sprinkle with cheese.
Serve with Fritos, Tortillas, or Taco Salad Shells.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description: 
This was always one of my kid's favorite meals, and one of my favorites because it is quick and easy, as well as good.
