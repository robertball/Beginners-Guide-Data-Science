Title:
Savory Seven Grain Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979975.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Archer Farms™ seven grain blend rice, from 15-oz. pkg.
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
2 tablespoons chopped green onions
2 tablespoons Archer Farms™ mango curry sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Cook rice blend as directed on package. Pour cooked rice into large bowl.  Refrigerate 20 minutes to cool.
When rice has cooled, stir in remaining ingredients.
Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.  Garnish with fresh cilantro, if you have some.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
This salad is a kaleidoscope of colorful vegetables and tasty grains. Mango curry sauce gives it an extra kick.
