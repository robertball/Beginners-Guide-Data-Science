Title:
Lemon Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1122730.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. butter flavored shortening
1 c. granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 TB water
1 1/4 tsp. lemon extract
2 1/4 c. flour
1/2 t. baking soda
1 t. salt
1 1/2 c. lemon flavored baking chips
2 TB lemon zest (optional)
6-8 TB granulated sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°
Cream shortening and sugar until well blended.
Add eggs, one at a time.
Add lemon extract and water.  Blend until creamy.
Sift together, flour, baking soda and salt.  Gradually add to creamed mixture, mixing just until blended.
Stir in lemon baking chips and lemon zest, if using.
Roll in 1" balls and roll in to granulated sugar.
Drop on a parchment lined baking sheet and bake 8-10 minutes.
Cool for 1 minute before moving to a cooling rack.
Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
Having 4+ pounds of lemon baking chips, I had to get creative in using them up.  This recipe was created and enjoyed by my family.
