Title:
Easy Baked Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tomatoes, diced
1 onion, diced
1/2 a bunch of cilantro, chopped
1 cup of ground beef, spiced with taco seasonings.
1/2 cup of refried beans
1 cup of spicy habernero cheese
1 cup of sharp cheddar cheese
1 cup of sour cream
2 large avocados
1/2 lemon
1/2 bag of tortilla chips
1/2 cup of pickled jalepenos

Directions:
Ground Beef:
sautee onion in olive oil and add the taco seasoning. salt and pepper as desired.
guacamole:
pit the avocado and scoop out the and
place in a bowl.  mix with a handful of tomatoes, onions, and cilantro. add the juice of 1/2 lemon, salt.
nachos:
lay the chips in a tray.
sprinkle both cheeses over the nachos.
add the ground beef.
add the tomatoes, onions, jalepenos, refried beans, and cilantro
if you have left over cheese, sprinkle some on top and place in the oven for 15 min. or until cooked.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
The most classic and flavorful nachos you can ever make. Fast, simple, and a great snack for parties and an evening with some friends.

Add sour cream and guacomole after the nachos are baked.
