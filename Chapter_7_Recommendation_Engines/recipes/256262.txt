Title:
Easy Homemade Whiskey Sour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4429994.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces simple syrup
1 fluid ounce whiskey
1 fluid ounce fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup crushed ice cubes
1 maraschino cherry (optional)

Directions:
Stir simple syrup, whiskey, and lemon juice together in a glass. Stir in ice. Garnish with cherry.
