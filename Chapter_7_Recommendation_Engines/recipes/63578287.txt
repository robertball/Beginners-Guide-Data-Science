Title:
Seared Mahi Mahi with Basil Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 large garlic clove, minced
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil leaves
3 tablespoons olive oil
4 mahi mahi fillets

Directions:
Combine butter, lemon juice, garlic, salt and pepper in medium sauce pan, stir until butter melts.  Add basil.  Cover and keep warm over low heat.
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.  Season fish with salt and pepper to taste.  Cook 3-4 minutes each side.
Spoon with basil butter and serve.
