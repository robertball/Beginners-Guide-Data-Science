Title:
SPINACH TART Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 INCH TART LINED W/PIE DOUGH
9 OZ BABY SPINACH
4 TBSP BUTTER
1 ONION FINELY CHOPPED
2 HEAPING CUPS OF GRATED CHEDDAR CHEESE
2 WHOLE EGGS, PLUS 2 YOLKS
2/3 CUP HEAVY CREAM
1 TSP DIJON MUSTARD

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 400
PLACE LINED TART PAN IN OVEN FOR 10-15 MINUTES
GENTLY COOK SPINACH WITH HALF OF THE BUTTER UNTIL WILTED.
DRAIN & CHOP FINELY
HEAT REST OF BUTER & ONIONS UNTIL SOFT
SPREAD SPINACH & ONION OVER BOTTOM OF THE PIE CRUST
IN FOOD PROCESSOR PULSE CHEESE, EGGS, YOLKS, CREAM & MUSTARD & SEASONINGS.
POUR OVER SPINACH & ONION AND RETURN TO OVEN FOR 20-25 MINUTES UNTIL TOP IS GOLDEN BROWN
