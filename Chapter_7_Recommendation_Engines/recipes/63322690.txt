Title:
Red Curry Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds stew meat
Olive  oil
1 onion
2 cloves garlic
1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
1 T Red Curry Paste
1 Can coconut milk
2 Cups chicken stock
2 Cups chopped purple cabbage or other greens
3 Yams, peeled and cut into chunks

Directions:
Brown the stew meat, place in slow cooker.
Use the same pan to saute onion, garlic, and curry paste (add a little water to make a sauce towards the end of cooking -- about 10 minutes)
Add the onion mixture to the slow cooker.
Add the yam and cabbage.
Add the coconut milk and chicken broth.
Season with salt and pepper.
Stir it all together.
Cook for 9 hours in slow cooker on high.
