Title:
Bourbon and Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983463.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
3 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup white karo syrup
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 tablespoons bourbon
1/2 cup pecans
1 cup chocolate chips
1 pre-made pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Cream butter by hand, then gradually add in sugar followed by eggs.
Next stir in syrup, salt, vanilla and bourbon.
Add pecans and chocolate chips, stir well and pour into crust.
Bake 40 - 50 minutes or until top of pie is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Kentucy Darling
Description: 
The smooth, rich flavors of traditional pecan pie are further enhanced with the addition of bourbon.  Using pre-made pie crusts, you can effortlessly whip this up for a special dessert.
