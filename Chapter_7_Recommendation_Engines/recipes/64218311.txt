Title:
Wild Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sausage
1 sm can sliced mushrooms
1 box Uncle Ben's Long Grain & Wild Rice
1 tsp. salt
1/4 c. flour
2 1/2 c. condensed chicken broth
1/2 c. cream
1/2 tsp. pepper
Pinch of oregano, thyme, and marjoram
chopped onion to taste

Directions:
Saute sausage meat; drain on paper towel.
Saute chopped onions in fat left in pan, then add mushrooms and cooked sausage.
Cook rice according to package directions.
Mix flour with cream until smooth.  Add chicken broth and cook until thickened.
Add seasonings. Combine with other ingredients.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes at 350ÂºF.
