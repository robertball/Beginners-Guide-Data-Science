Title:
Real Thai Peanut Eggroll Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129542.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Sauce Pan
4 Cups of Water
1 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
3 Cups of Sugar
1 Garlic Clove
3 Small Red Peppers
1/2 Cup Vinegar
2 Tablespoon Chopped Peanuts

Directions:
In Sauce Pan, add Brown Sugar and Regular Sugar to water.
Turn on high and down to medium high once it starts to really boil.
Cook until Sauce thickens which takes 45-55 mins.
Chop garlic clove into fine pieces. Cut Stems off Red Peppers.
Take the 3 small Red Peppers and 1 Garlic clove and mash together until everything is smashed evenly and finely.
In a serving Bowl, add the peppers, garlic, and salt along with the chopped peanuts. Stir.
(You may add more as necessary. The idea is to have an appropriate amount of chopped peanuts in each serving.)
After the sugar and water thickens, add in Vinegar.
Pour the sauce into the serving bowl with the rest of the ingredients.
Best served at cool temperature.
Place into  refrigerator to cool down before serving.

Submitter: 
Josh Charlton Cornelius
Description: 
This recipe belongs to my mother. It has been a family favorite and friends often enjoyed this for many years now. Theres nothing like it and is perfect for Egg rolls.
