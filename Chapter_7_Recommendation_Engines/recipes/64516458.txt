Title:
Gift of the Magi Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
pinch salt
1 can (approx. 1 cup) mandarin oranges, drained
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
1 cup coconut
2/3 cup sliced almonds (or less)
1//2 cup maraschino cherries, drained
1/2 cup figs
1 banana, mashed

Directions:
Mix butter and sugar together well.
Add eggs and vanilla.
Stir in remaining ingredients until mixed well.
Divide into 2 greased loaf pans.
Bake @ 350 x 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This is a moist Christmas fruitcake my aunt used to make. It has some unique ingredients, like mandarin oranges and chocolate chips, not usually found in traditional fruitcake.
