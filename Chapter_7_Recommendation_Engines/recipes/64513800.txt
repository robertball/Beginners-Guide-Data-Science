Title:
Spinach and Artichoke Ravioli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz spinach
1 can Artichoke hearts, diced
2 T of Pesto
2 Cups Alfredo Sauce
1/4 C vegetable or chicken broth
25 oz of frozen Ravioli
1 C Italian cheese

Directions:
1. Combine spinach, artichoke and pesto an mix well
2. In a separate bowl, combine alfredo sauce and broth
3. Spread 1/3 of Alfredo sauce on bottom of 9/13 baking dish.
4. Top with Spinach mixture.
5. Lay half of the ravioli in a single layer over spinach mixture.
6. Repeat layers once more.
7. Finish up with remaining sauce.
8. Bake uncovered 375 for 30 mins
9. Sprinkle with cheese and broil  450 for 3-5 mins
Note:
2 cups of Alfredo
8T of butter
1 C of heavy cream
Salt and pepper to taste
2 Cups Parmesan cheese freshly grated
Over medium heat melt butter and pour in heavy cream.  Stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add parmesan in portions and stir to mix.
