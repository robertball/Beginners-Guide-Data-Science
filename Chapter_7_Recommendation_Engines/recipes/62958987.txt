Title:
Tomato and Zucchini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb Ripe tomatoes, blanched, peeled and chopped
6 medium zucchini, trimmed, blanched and chopped
7 1/2 cups chicken stock
1/4 cup butter
1/4 flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 tsp nutmeg
2 tsp sugar
1 tbl chopped parsley
1 tbl chopped dill
1/2 sour cream

Directions:
Place tomatoes, zucchini and 1/2 the chicken stock in a large saucepan. Bring liquid to a boil over high heat, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 to 20 mintues until vegetables are soft.
1n a small saucepan, melt the butter. Remove the pan from the heat and, wtir in the flour to amke a smooth paste.  Stir in 4 tbl of the soup liquid and stir until the mixture is well blanded.
Pour the mixture into the soup. Cook, stirring constantly, for 2 - 3 minutes or until the soup is smooth and thickened.
Gradually add the remaining stock, and bring mixture to a boil.
Add the salt, pepper, nutmeg, sugar, parsley and dill, stirring often. Remove the pan from the heat, and serve with sour cream garnish.
