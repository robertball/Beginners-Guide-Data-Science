Title:
Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup quiona
1 cup water

Directions:
in a 3QT,
add 1 cup quinoa (rinsed, drained),
1 cup water
Mix well
set IP to 5 min in Steam More mode (press Steam Twice) and pressure valve set to Sealing.
you can do Quick release after its done or Just let the steam settle.
Fluffy Quiona is ready
