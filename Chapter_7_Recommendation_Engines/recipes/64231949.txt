Title:
Orignial Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (slightly toasted)
1/2 cup celery -chopped
1/2 cup red seedless grapes, sliced
1 red delicious apple chopped
3 tsp plain yogurt (or mayonnaise)
1 tsp fresh lemon juice
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4  tsp Pepper

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl, whisk together the yogurt and lemon juice,  Add salt & pepper.  Mix in apples. grapes, and walnuts.
