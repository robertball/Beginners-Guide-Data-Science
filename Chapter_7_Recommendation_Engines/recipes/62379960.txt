Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb of lean ground beef
1/2 large white onion, diced
1 family sized can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
8 oz sour cream
paprika and nutmeg to taste
red pepper to taste (optional)
1 lb cooked pasta

Directions:
Fry ground beef in skillet.  Drain and discard oil except for 1 tbsp.  Fry onions in skillet until clear.
Combine beef, onions, cream of mushroom soup, and only half the water the soup can suggests (usually half a can of water).
Slow cook on low for eight hours.
Stir in sour cream.  Add nutmeg and paprika to taste.  For a spicier taste, also add red pepper.
Serve over pasta.
