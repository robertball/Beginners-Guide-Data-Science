Title:
Zesty Sausage Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044096.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Mexican Style Rice-a-Roni
1 smoked sausage (any flavor; preferably skinless)
2 tbsp butter or margarine
2 1/4 cup water
2 cans diced tomatoes
1 can black beans
1 can chili beans
1 can dark red kidney beans

Directions:
Cut sausage into slices or dice it, whichever you prefer.
Stir together sausage, butter or margarine, water, and contents of Rice-a-Roni box (including flavor packet) in medium stock pot.
Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 50-20 mins or until rice is tender.
Stir in the tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, and chili beans.
Cook on medium heat for 5 minutes and then remove from heat.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lainee Richmond
Description: 
A delicious dish that combines chili, rice, and sausage to make something new and wonderful!
