Title:
Cowboy Salad (aka. Southwestern Pasta Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound SMALL shell macaroni
2/3 c cider vinegar
1/4 c vegetable oil
1 c celery (apx 2 stalks) minced
1/2 c green pepper (apx 1/2 large pepper) - minced
6 green onions minced
1 2oz jar chopped pimento drained (just dump in after drain)
3 generous dashes worcestershire sauce
3 dashes hot pepper sauce (chilulah best for this)
1 Tbs green chili pepper minced
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 15oz can black eyed peas, drained
1 12oz can corn drained
1/2 c pited black olives, drained, minced
1 2oz jar pimento stuffed green olives, minced
1/3 c mayo apx - to taste

Directions:
Macaroni(must be small shells) cook aldente, drain well.
Place macaroni in large bowl.
Pour vinegar on and let sit while prepping everything else.
Add all other ingred. mix well.
Cover and refrigerate  at least 1 & up to 2-3 days ahead.
Taste & reseason w/ more salt & splash vinegar bef. serving.
Add more hot sauce too if you like it spicy.
The longer it sits the better it gets!

Submitter: 
Jennifer Oliver ElBassuni
Description: 
A south western inspired pasta salad with everything but the kitchen sink.  Tips: Best if made day before serving, and mince all items for best flavor!
