Title:
Ultimate Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) bag frozen shredded hash browns(thawed)
1 stick butter melted (reserve 3Tbs of the melted butter for topping)
8 oz sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cheddar soup
2 cups crushed corn flakes

Directions:
Pre heat oven 350*
Grease a 9x12 dish
In a large bowl mix all ingredients except the corn flakes and 3 Tbs of melted butter.
Bake 45 minutes.
Remove dish from oven.Sprinkle corn flakes on top of potatoes and drizzle butter on top of corn flakes.
Return dish to oven and bake for 15 minutes or until the corn flakes to brown.

Submitter: 
LISA 1212
Description: 
This is a really creamy and rich side dish.
You can make it in a crock pot and use the cornflakes as a garnish at serving time.You can add cooked meat or vegtables if you like.
