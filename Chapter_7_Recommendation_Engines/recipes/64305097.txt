Title:
Wilted Lettuce Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon bacon drippings
1 Tablespoon flour
1/4 Cup sugar
1/4 Cup vinegar
1/4 Cup water
salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat bacon drippings.
Add flour and stir to mix
Add remainder of ingredients and boil until it thickens.
