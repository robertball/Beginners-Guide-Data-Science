Title:
Deviled Eggs "Chesapeake" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, hard boiled
4 oz blue fin crab meat
1-1/2 tsp Old Bay
1-1/2 tsp dry mustard
2 tsp cooking oil
water (approx. 1/2 cup)

Directions:
Peel and halve eggs lengthwise. Place yolks in mixer bowl and place whites in serving tray.
Mix crab meat, oil and seasonings in a cereal bowl and add to mixer. Mix on low adding water until smooth, gradually increasing mixing speed. Spoon into egg whites, then sprinkle a little additional Old Bay on top.

Submitter: 
Bob Miller
Description: 
Eggs deviled with crab meat, seasoned with Old Bay and dry mustard.  If you like Maryland Style crab cakes, you'll love these deviled eggs.
