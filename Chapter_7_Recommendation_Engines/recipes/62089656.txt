Title:
Warm Summer Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow bell pepper, seeded, chopped small
1 orange bell pepper, seeded, chopped small
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 cup pitted kalamata olives, whole
1/2 white onion, minced
2 cloves garlic, smashed, minced
1 cup fresh cilantro, minced
1 cup fresh parsley, minced
2 tbsp olive oil
sea salt
fresh ground pepper
package crumbled feta cheese
1 package dreamfields Linguini, prepared according to package

Directions:
Cook linguini according to package with sea salt and olive oil added to water to give some flavor and to keep noodles separated.
Prepare vegetables and herbs as directed, place all in large mixing bowl (any juices as well), cover and let sit at room temp until noodles are ready. Place unrinsed noodles in mixing bowl with other ingredients, toss to coat.  Serve warm with feta cheese on the side.
