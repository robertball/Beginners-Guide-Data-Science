Title:
Hawiian Spareribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 to 6 lbs lean spareribs
1/4 cup plain flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup soy sauce
4 tablespoons vegetable oil
3/4 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup water
1 15 ounce can pineapple slices
1 clove garlic pressed
1 tsp fresh gingeroot grated or 1/2 tsp powdered ginger

Directions:
Cut spareribs into two inch strips.  Mix flour, salt and soy to a smooth paste, brush on ribs.  Allow to stand for 10 minutes.  Heat oil in a large skillet and brown ribs on all sides.  Transfer ribs to a large roasting pan.  Drain remaining oil from skillet.  Add sugar, vinegar, water, pineapple juice, garlic and ginger.  Heat and stir to loosend any browned bits.  Place ribs in a roasting pan.  Pour mixture over ribs.  Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 1 & 1/2 hours, turning ribs and basting every 20 minutes.  Add pineapple slices during last 10 minutes of baking time.

Submitter: 
Sydney Sadler Walker
Description: 
Succulent spareribs baked in a brown sugar, soy, ginger and pineapple sauce until fork tender.
