Title:
Cranberry Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/741375.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cup Cranberries
Zest of 1 Orange
1 Peeled Orange
1 Apple
Zest of 1/2 Lemon
Juice of 1/2 Lemon
3/4 Cup Sugar

Directions:
Grind all ingredients, or finely chop all the ingredients in a food processor.  Mix in the sugar.  Additional sugar can be added if you prefer it a little sweeter.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
An easy tart cranberry relish that my family has  made every Thanksgiving and Christmas.
