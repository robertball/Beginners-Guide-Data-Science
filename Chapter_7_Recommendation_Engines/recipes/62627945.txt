Title:
Broiled Tuna Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/577685.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz tuna,
1/2 c. miracle whip,
8 oz velveta cheese, or shredded cheddar cheese
dill pickle to taste
8 slices bread, butter on one side.

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients, lay 4 slices of bread butter side down, on a cookie sheet, top each slice with 1/4th of the mixture, and another slice of bread.  Broil till golden on both sides.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Root
Description: 
A friend gave me this recipe, she said her family begs her to make them!
