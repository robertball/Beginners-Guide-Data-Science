Title:
Beef Stroganoff I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3134124.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 1/2 pounds sirloin tip, cut into thin slices
1 onion, chopped
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.  Add beef and the onions; cook until the meat is browned and the onion tender.
Stir in sour cream, and heat through.  Do not boil.

Submitter: 
Sue Kerr
Description: 
Stroganoff is a wonderful dish for guests; it is rich and creamy, but is simply and quickly prepared. Traditionally it is served with a rice pilaf, but is equally good over plain rice or egg noodles.
