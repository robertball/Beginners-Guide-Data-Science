Title:
Company French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
1/2 c. toasted pecan pieces (optional)
1 1/2 c. brown sugar
1 t. ground cinnamon
8-12 slices of white bread, crusts removed
8 eggs
2 c. milk
pinch of salt

Directions:
Melt butter and pour in a 9" x 13' pan. Sprinkle brown sugar, pecans (if desired), and cinnamon over butter.
Cover the bottom of the pan with bread slices, number of slices needed will vary depending on the size of the bread.
Beat eggs and salt, add milk. Pour over bread. Garnish top with a dusting of cinnamon and sugar.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
In the morning, bake at 350 for 45 minutes. Serve immediately if possible, this puffs up like a souffle and is very pretty!

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This is a great recipe to make the night before and quickly pop in the oven when company is in town, or when you need something easy like on Christmas morning!
