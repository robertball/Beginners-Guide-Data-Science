Title:
American Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium yellow onion, finely chopped
1 green pepper, seeded + finely chopped
4 stalks Celery
-----
1 lb 80/20 ground beef
3 teaspoons garlic powder
3 teaspoons onion powder
2 teaspoons paprika
1/8 tsp (reduced from 1/4) crushed red pepper flakes
-----
1 28-ounce can diced tomatoes
45 oz can tomato sauce
2 tablespoons tomato paste (or ketchup)
scant 1/8 tsp (reduced from 1/4 teaspoon) ground cinnamon
-----
8 oz large elbow macaroni
salt + pepper, to taste
-----
optional: grated parmesan cheese + freshly-chopped parsley, to garnish

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat (ht setting 5 for Dutch Oven). Add onion, celery, green pepper, + cook until softened, about 8 minutes.
2. Add ground beef, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, + red pepper flakes. cook, stirring occasionally, until meat is no longer pink, about 8 minutes.
3. Add in diced tomatoes (and juice), tomato sauce, tomato paste, cinnamon, + salt/pepper to taste â€“ stir well. bring to a boil, then reduce heat, + simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until sauce thickens, about an hour.
4. While sauce is in its final minutes of simmering, cook pasta according to package directions, reducing the cooking time by two minutes. drain pasta well + transfer to pot of sauce, stirring to combine well. add more salt + pepper to taste (if needed), + cook until flavors combine, about 10 more minutes.
serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese + garnish of parsley, + hot, buttered bread.
