Title:
Potato and Chorizo Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
450g/1lb Chorizo - sliced
220g/8oz New Potatoes - cooked and sliced
2 to 3 Spring Onions - chopped
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Eggs - poached - to serve

Directions:
Heat a frying pan and fry the chorizo for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently;
Add sliced cooked potatoes and spring onion and cook gently, stirring occasionally, for about five minutes or so or until spring onions are softened and potatoes heated through;
Slightly crush potatoes with a fork, season to taste, with salt and pepper and mix everything together;
Dish the hash onto a serving plate and top with a poached egg.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A quick and easy, packed with flavour, comfort joy. Fit for all occasions. Without the egg,can also be used as a side dish accompanying meat dishes.
