Title:
Eileen Hilleren's Dried Beef or Venison Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg dried beef/venison (8 ozs)cut up
1 cup milk
1 can cream mushroom or chicken soup
1 cup diced cheddar cheese
1 cup uncooked macaroni
3 Tbsp diced onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients and place in refrigerator overnight.
Bake for 1 hour.
