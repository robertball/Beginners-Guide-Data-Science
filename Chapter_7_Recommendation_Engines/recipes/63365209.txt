Title:
Thai Chicken with Minted Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Jasmine Rice
2 tbsp Olive Oil
1 1/2 lbs Chicken Breasts cut in 1" chunks
2 Carrots sliced
1 Bell pepper chopped
5 tbsp Pesto
1 can Coconut Milk
5 Mint Leaves

Directions:
1. In a large skillet heat 1tbsp Olive Oil over Medium heat. Add chicken season with salt and pepper, cook until browned. Transfer to a plate.
2. Add remaining olive oil carrots and bell peppers season. Stir in pesto and cook stirring continuously for 30 seconds. Stir in coconut milk and 3/4 of mint leaves. Lower heat and simmer until carrots are tender.
3. Return chicken to pan and cook for 5 min. Serve over minted rice

Submitter: 
fire1676
Description: 
Minted rice can be substituted with minted cauliflower rice.
