Title:
Strawberry Banana Protein Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Large Ice Cubes
1 Scoop Vanilla Protein Powder
1- 1 1/4 Cup Vanilla Almond Milk (Sweetened or Unsweetened)
About 10 Strawberries
1-1 1/2 Medium Bananas

Directions:
Place ice, powder and almond milk in blender and blend until ice is completely pulverized. Add fruit and blend again until smooth. Pour into a large glass and Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Danielle Lockley
Description: 
The perfect healthy meal replacement smoothie. Everything you need to start your day off right!
