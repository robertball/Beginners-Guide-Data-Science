Title:
Chicken Waldorf Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared pizza crust of your choice
1/2 - 1 cup chopped tart apple (Braeburn or Pink Lady are good choices)
1 cup chopped cooked chicken, seasoned to taste
1/2 cup slivered almonds, toasted
1/3 cup mayo
1/3 cup sour cream
1 tsp. sugar or to taste
1 - 2 cups shredded Swiss or Mozzarella or mix
1 bunch chopped green onion
a handful of bacon, cooked crispy and crumbled

Directions:
Mix the mayo and sour cream together until smooth.  Add sugar to taste.  Mix chicken, apple, and almonds.  Stir into the mayo mixture until evenly coated.  Spread evenly over the pizza crust.  Sprinkle with cheese.
Bake 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and beginning to brown.  Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle with the green onion and bacon.  Cut and serve.
