Title:
Three Cheese Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9-inch unbaked pie shell
4 eggs
1/2 cup milk
2 tsp. sugar
6 oz. cheddar cheese, grated
6 oz. monterey jack cheese, grated
8 oz. small curd cottage cheese
3 TB butter, room temperature
3/4 tsp. baking powder
1/3 cup flour
1/2 cup ham, sausage or bacon, chopped

Directions:
Beat together the eggs, milk and sugar.
Add the cheeses, butter and meat, vegetables or herbs of your choice.
Stir well and then add the flour and baking powder and mix thoroughly.
Pour into 9-inch unbaked pie shell or quiche pan.
Bake in preheated 400 degree oven for approximately 60 minutes.  If the crust starts getting too brown, place foil on the crust.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This moist and custardy quiche is very versatile.  Any kind of meat, herb or vegetable is great in this quiche. This is great with spinach or asparagus. With three different kinds of cheese in this, it is a very rich quiche.
