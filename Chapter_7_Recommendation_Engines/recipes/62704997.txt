Title:
Beef Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. egg noodles, cook half the bag.
1 t. butter
1 lb. ground beef, cooked and drained
1 8-oz. can tomato sauce
4 oz. cottage cheese
1 4-oz. pkg. cream cheese, room temperature
1/8 c. sour cream
1/8 c. green onions, chopped
salt pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook hamburger, drain completely. Mix beef and tomato sauce together, set aside. Cook noodles per package instructions. Drain well. In medium bowl mix cottage cheese, cream cheese, sour cream and green onions. Butter the bottom of a casserole dish and spread 1/2 of the cooked noodles. Add cottage cheese mixture then top with the remaining noodles.  Top with beef mixture.  Let stand in refrigerator all day or overnight. Bake in 350 oven for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
PacNWCook
Description: 
A layered casserole with a delicious tomato beef topping, cheese and cream middle layered between egg noodles.  Full of flavor.
