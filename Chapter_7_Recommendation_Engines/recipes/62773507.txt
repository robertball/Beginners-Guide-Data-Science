Title:
Guajillo Spice Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/644085.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons ground guajillo pepper
1-1/2 teaspoons ground garlic powder
1-1/2 teaspoons mexican oregano
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1 teaspoon smoked spanish paprika pepper
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper

Directions:
Mix spices together well and store in a conainer.  To use start with 1 teaspoon per pound of meat.  Adjust to your taste.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This spice mix works well on all meats, beef, chicken or fish.
