Title:
Moo Shu Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3454246.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb pork  tenderloin
1 teas. minced garlic
1 Tablespoon grated or chopped fresh ginger
2 Tablespoons  soy sauce
1 Tablespoon sesame oil
2 Tablespoons veg. oil
2 Cups shredded napa cabbage
1 Grated carrot
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Remove any visible fat from pork
Cut pork into 1/4" strips
Combine minced garlic, minced ginger, soy and sesame oil for marinade
Add port to marinade - for 1 hour or overnight
Heat veg oil in wok or large skillet
Add cabbage & carrots and toss 1-2 minutes
Push cabbage aside and add pork with sauce to middle of pan and cook 3-4 min
Draw cabbage into center with meat and cook additional 1-2 min
Season with salt and pepper to taste
Serve over coconut rice
