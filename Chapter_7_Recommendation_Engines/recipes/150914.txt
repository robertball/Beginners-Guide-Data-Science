Title:
Rob Roy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992445.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Johnnie Walker® Red Label® blended Scotch whisky
1/2 ounce sweet vermouth
1 dash bitters
1 cherry

Directions:
Pour Johnnie Walker(R) Red Label(R) blended Scotch whisky, sweet vermouth, and bitters over ice.
Stir the cocktail, strain into a cocktail glass, and garnish with cherry.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
One of the blends, Johnnie Walker® blended Scotch whisky is still the first and only choice for blending your own premium cocktail.
