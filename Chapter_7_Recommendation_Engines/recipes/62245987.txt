Title:
Sour Cream Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup sour cream
1 t vanilla
1 3/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1 t baking powder
1/2 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips (6 oz)
1/2 cup vanilla chips (3 oz)

Directions:
Using KitchenAid:
Sift dry and set aside.
In bowl, add sugars, egg, butter, and vanilla
Beat at speed 2 for 30 sec;
Increase to speed 4 for 30 sec
Turn to stir and gradually add 1/2 of dry, then sour cream, then remaining dry
Continue for 2 minutes
Increase to speed 2 and mix 30 sec;
Continue as directed
REGULAR INSTRUCTIONS:
Cream butter and sugars.  Beat in egg, sour cream and vanilla.
Sift dry and gradually add to creamed mix.  Stir in chips.
Drop by tablespoonfuls 2" apart on gresed sheet.  Bake at 350 for 12-15".

Description: 
Chocolate chocolate/vanilla chip cookies.  These were good - not as sweet as a regular chocolate cookie.  Next time I make, may try sprinkling a little granulated sugar on top.
