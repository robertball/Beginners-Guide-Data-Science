Title:
Individual Molten chocolate cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick unsalted butter
5 ounces good quality dark chocolate
a pinch of coarse sea salt
2 eggs
2 egg yolks
1/4 cup sugar
1 tablespoon flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Melt butter and chocolate in microwave or double boiler.  sea salt. Meanwhile beat together the eggs, egg yolks, and sugar with a whisk or electric beater til light and slightly foamy. Add egg mixture to warm chocolate; whisk quickly to combine. Add flour and stir just to combine. The batter will be thick.
Use individual molds (several foil cupcake holders work well.)
Divide batter evenly. Chill until ready to bake, but bring to room temp before baking. (You can also bake straight from freezer - take out 10 mins in advance and bake at 420 for 15-17 mins.)
Bake 7 - 8 minutes.
