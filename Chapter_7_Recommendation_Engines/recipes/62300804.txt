Title:
Creamy Mac-N-Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb medium shell pasta
1/4 c butter or margarine
1/4 c flour
12 oz sharp cheddar cheese
2 c cold milk
1/8 c parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Boil pasta according to package specifications. drain and rinse.
2. In medium sauce pan melt 1/4 c butter or margarine on low heat. Add flour and stir until dough like.
3. Add cold milk and wisk until no clumps remain. Cook on medium heat for about 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Take off heat and stir in about 11 oz of sharp cheddar cheese until smooth.
5. Add cooked pasta to the cheese sauce and stir. Pour mac-n-cheese into 8x8 inch pan. Sprinkle with remaining 1 oz cheddat cheese and 1/8 c parmesan cheese.
6. Bake at 375 degrees for approx. 35 minutes.
