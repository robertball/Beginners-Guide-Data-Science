Title:
Pork with Apricot Preserve Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pork
3 TBS. apricot preserves
5 TBS. honey mustard

Directions:
Mix two ingredients together, warm in microwave to create a smooth consistancy for a glaze marinade on the pork.
Cook pork with marinade, use extra marinade for dipping if desired.
