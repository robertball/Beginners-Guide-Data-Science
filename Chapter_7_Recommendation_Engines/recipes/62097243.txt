Title:
Homemade Breakfast Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground pork
1/2 tbsp salt
1/2 tsp rubbed sage
1/2 tsp rubbed savory
2/3 tsp ground majoram
1/3 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well using hands and shape into 2 oz round patties the size of a sm burger bun.  Fry for 3 min on each side and serve.

Submitter: 
Sneakyteaky
Description: 
This recipe came from the Burrows side of our family and nothing gives you better satisfaction than knowing you made your own breakfast rounds.  It's quick and easy, the mixture can be made in advace and the cooked patties thrown in the freezer for a quick minute in the microwave.  This recipe easily doubles.
