Title:
Big Ray's Shredded Chili Sauce  Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4531822.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz chili sauce
1/4 cup white wine
3 Tbsp cup olive oil
10 to 12 garlic cloves, minced
3 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
8 boneless skinless chicken thighs

Directions:
In a crock pot, whisk the first seven ingredients until blended. . Add chicken; cook on low for 8 hours (or high for 4 hours).
Remove  chicken and shred with 2 forks, add chicken back to slow cooker.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This  chili and basil sauce will add flavor to these moist chicken thighs. You can enjoy the shredded chicken in salads, tacos or burritos.
