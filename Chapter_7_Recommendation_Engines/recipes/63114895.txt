Title:
Irresistable Chicken Enchaladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3255818.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs chicken breasts or tenders
2 limes
1 large yellow onion
1 package shredded mexican cheese blend
2 cans old elpaso red enchalada sauce one mild one medium
1 small can tomatilla sauce
1 15 oz can chicken broth
1 package mexican rice
1 package refried beans
1 package tortillias
Fresh cilantro

Directions:
Place chicken, onion, chicken broth, 1/2 can medium enchalada sauce and 1/2 can mild enchalada sauce, tomatillia sauce and sliced and squeezed limes in crock pot, cook on low for 6 hours, add cilantro 1/2 hour prior to end of cooking time.
Remove chicken from crock pot and shred using a two forks.
Make the filling:
Cook rice and refried beans according to package directions mix together rice, 1/2 of the refried beans, shredded chicken, and 3/4 cup of sauce left over in the crock pot.
stuff tortillias with filling and roll up, place in a casserol dish and top with remaing sauce from the cans of enchalada sauce. Sprinkle with cheese, cover with foil, bake for 40 minutes at 350 degrees, remove foil and bake for 5-10 minutes longer.
serve with a side of lime wedge, left over refried beans with cheese mixed in and a vegtable of your choice.

Submitter: 
Robyn Dye
Description: 
With the assistance of a slow cooker the chicken is teneder and irresistable in these enchaladas.
