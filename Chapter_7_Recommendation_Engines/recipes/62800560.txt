Title:
Rich Chocolate Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656206.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter(or I Can't Believe It's Not ButterÂ®), softened
4 1/4 cups confectioners' sugar
1 1/4 cups baking cocoa
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, cream the sticks of butter. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar, cocoa and almond extract. Add enough milk until frosting reaches spreading consistency.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
Makes enough frosting for one 2 layer cake and more than enough for a bundt cake.Modified from: Rich Chocolate Frosting
