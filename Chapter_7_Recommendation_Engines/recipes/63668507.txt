Title:
Tomato Basil Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	32 ounce can whole tomatoes, drained you can also use diced tomatoes
1	cup fresh basil leaves, washed and spun dry
4	tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
6	cloves garlic, peeled
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
2	large baguettes, sliced 1 inch thick (about 36 slices)
1 ½	pounds fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced ¼ inch thick

Directions:
1.	Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2.	In the bowl of a food processor, add drained tomatoes, basil leaves, olive oil and 2 cloves garlic.  Pulse until smooth, but somewhat chunky.  Season with Salt and pepper.
3.	On a baking sheet, line up baguette slices.  Toast in oven about 3 minutes or until golden brown.  Working quickly, rub remaining garlic on toasted side of each slice, then lay a piece of mozzarella cheese on top.  Place bread back in oven and melt cheese slightly, about 45 seconds.  Remove from oven and spread 1 tablespoon of the tomato mixture on each piece.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
This is great for intimate dinner parties or large parties.  there is also a way to make this very quickly using fresh tomatoes in place of canned, also shredded mozzarella (not melting the cheese over the bread), and just leaving it in big bowl for people to scoop what they like.
