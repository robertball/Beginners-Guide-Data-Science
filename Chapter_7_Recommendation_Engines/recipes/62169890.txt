Title:
Chilean potato pie (Pastel de Papas) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of mashed potatoes (add butter and salt to taste)
2 eggs (Beaten then added to the mashed potatoes)
1/2 teaspoon of chopped parsley
2 TBSP of grated cheese
1 onion, diced
2 tomatoes, diced
1 TBSP tomato paste
1 minced clove of fresh garlic
1/2 cup of oil
1 lb. ground beef
Dash of cayenne pepper (opt.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
1. Heat the oil. saute onions and garlic.
2. Stir in tomatoes and tomato sauce when onions turn light brown.
3. When tomatoes are cooked, add ground beef.
4. Cook until beef is brown (remove from heat and season to taste)
5. Continue stirring to blend well all ingredients. Add the cheese and parsley
6. Grease a baking dish and then spread 1/2 the potatoes on bottom.
7. Spread the ground beef above the potatoes.
8. Cover the ground beef with the remaining mashed potatoes.
9. Bake in oven approx 40 minutes till the top is lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Cucina di papa
Description: 
This is a lot like a Shepard's Pie with a little Latin flair. Good comfort food
