Title:
Orange Dream Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon orange sherbert
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream
2-2 liter bottles of lemon lime soda

Directions:
Place sherbert and ice cream in a punch bowl, slowly add lemon lime soda, stir to dissolve into a creamy mixture.

Submitter: 
Livie's Mommy
Description: 
Creamy sweet and delicious with a balance or orange and vanilla reminiscint of an orangesicle.
