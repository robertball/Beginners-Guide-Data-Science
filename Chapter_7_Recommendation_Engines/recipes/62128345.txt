Title:
Ferah's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons tomato paste
2 sweet green chillis
2 teaspoons pepper
2 teaspoons cumin powder
2 teaspoons sweet paprika
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon sugar
2 cloves garlic (peeled)
1 large  onions (peeled)
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 bunch parsley
200 ml chicken stock
4 large tomatoes (you can substitute with 400g can of diced tomato)

Directions:
Dice garlic on a chopping board.  Then sprinkle salt over garlic and use a fork to crush garlic.  Set aside.
Dice onion, then add to garlic.
Thinly slice peppers (careful to remove seeds) then set aside.
Chop tomatoes, then set aside.
Heat olive oil in a medium size pan.  Add diced onion and crushed garlic.  Sauté till soft. And transparent (careful not to let onions go brown).
Add sliced peppers, chop tomatoes, pepper, sugar, salt, cumin and paprika.  Sauté for a further three minutes.
Add chicken stock and tomato paste, stir well and then reduce heat simmering for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Ferah
Description: 
Simple Salsa with a Mediterranean twist, this acts as a quick and easy dip that is versatile and suitable as an accompaniment to main meals and snacks.  It may be served hot or cold and can also be used as a pasta sauce. When served alongside Garlic yoghurt they compliment each other well with grilled meat dishes and grilled vegetables.
