Title:
Peanut Butter Cup Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup unsweetened almond milk
*1/2 cup oats
2.5T peanut butter
1/2T cocoa powder
1/2t vanilla
1/4 cup vanilla yogurt
1/2t Stevia
Pinch of salt

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, combine peanut butter, vanilla, and salt. Microwave for 10 seconds, stir, then microwave for 10 more seconds.
2. Add in the rest of the ingredients.
3. Stir well making sure the peanut butter is mashed in thoroughly.
4. If desired, add in dark chocolate chips.
5. Transfer to a sealed container and refrigerate for at least three hours or overnight. Time depends on type of oats.
***Can be stored in the refrigerator for 3 days.
