Title:
DeAnna's Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white shortening
2 Tbsp. butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup powder sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
2 cups sifted flour
1/2 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Cream shortening, butter, sugar, and vanilla. Beat in egg, then add sifted dry ingredients. Chill one hour in refrigerator. (More time depending on if you scale the recipe.) Roll the dough out about 1/4-1/2" thick and cut out with fun shapes, or x-mas shapes.
Icing recipe:
2 cups powder sugar
2 Tbsp. plus 1 tsp. whole milk
drops of food coloring
Just mix up and use!

Submitter: 
DeAnna L.
Description: 
Soft and melt in your mouth sugar cookies. All my friends and family love these sugar cookies! Even my boyfriend's little nieces and nephews are fans! The icing recipe is enclosed too! I use a pastry brush and gently brush it on!
