Title:
Blueberry and Banana Steel Cut Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2012689.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
2 cups almond milk
2 cups blueberries
2 ripe bananas, mashed
1 cup steel-cut oats
2 tablespoons honey
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Stir water, almond milk, blueberries, bananas, oats, honey, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt together in the crock of a slow cooker.
Cook on Low for 5 to 8 hours.
