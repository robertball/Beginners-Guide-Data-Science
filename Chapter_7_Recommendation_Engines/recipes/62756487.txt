Title:
Masala Dosa Filling (goes with Pattu Dosa) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb potatoes, boiled, peeled and cubed
1/2 C peas (fresh or frozen, canned won't work)
1/4 c oil
1/2 T black mustard seed
1 tsp channa dal
1/2 T urad dal
4 - 6 hot green chili peppers, chopped
4 med onion, chopped (about 1 c)
1 T ginger paste
3/4 tsp curry powder
8 curry leaves, fresh
1/4 c water
1 tsp salt
2 T lemon juice
2 T chopped cilantro

Directions:
set veggies aside in a bowl.
measure out and set aside all remaining ingredients so they'll be ready when you need them
Heat oil in a frying pan over med-high heat for 3 mins.  Add dals; just when they begin to brown, add mustard seeds.  When they begin to pop, add the curry leaves.
When you being to smell the aroma of the curry leaves, add the peas.  Fry til they begin to brown.
Add the green chilies, onions, and ginger, and cook, stirring constantly, until the onions begin to brown (about 5 to 7 mins)  Add the curry powder and the veggies and stir fry another 5 mins.  Add the water, cover, and let cook over low or low-med heat for about 5 mins, til the water appears to be largely absorbed.  Remove the lid towards the end if necessary to evaporate any excess water.
Sprinkle on the salt, lemon juice, and cilantro.
Put about 1/2 c of this filling in the center of a dosa (see recipe for Pattu dosa) and roll it up - serve warm
You may serve with a sweet or savory chutney if desired.
