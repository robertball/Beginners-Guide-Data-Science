Title:
Breakfast casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag hash brown potatoes, thawed
3 Tbsp butter, melted
1 cup (4 oz) cooked ham, diced
1 cup (4 oz) cheddar cheese or American cheese
1 cup (4 oz) Monterey Jack cheese
12 drops Tabasco sauce
6 eggs
1.5 cups milk
3/4 tsp season salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 F
Blot hash browns using paper towel
Press hash browns in 9x13 pan to form a crust
Drizzle melted butter over crust
Bake for 20-25 min until lightly browned
Reduce temp to 350F
Toss ham, cheese together.  Place in crust
Beat eggs, milk, hot sauce and salt.  Pour in pan.
Bake uncovered in 350F oven for 25-30 min or until knife inserted comes out clean
Let stand 10 min before serving.
