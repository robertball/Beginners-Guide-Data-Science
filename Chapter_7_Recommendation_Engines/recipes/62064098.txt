Title:
Mom's Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sirloin, cut into strips
1 T flour
1/2 t salt
2 T butter/margarine
1 small onion, diced
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
2 T butter/margarine
3 T flour
1 T tomato paste
1 (10 oz) can beef broth
1 cup sour cream
2 T white wine

Directions:
1. In plastic bag, toss together beef, flour, and salt until meat is coated.
2. Melt 2 T butter over medium heat. Brown meat in melted butter.
3. Add onion and garlic, cook 3-4 minutes until tender. Remove meat from pan.
4. To drippings, add (in order, stirring constantly) 2 T butter, 3 T flour, tomato paste, and beef broth. Cook over medium-high heat until thick and bubbly.
5. Return meat to sauce, add in sour cream and wine. Cook until heated through. Serve over hot buttered egg noodles.

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
A dinner-time favorite that's easy to make. If you're watching your diet, substitue lower fat ingredients (light sour cream, margarine, low fat broth) and it will still taste great!
