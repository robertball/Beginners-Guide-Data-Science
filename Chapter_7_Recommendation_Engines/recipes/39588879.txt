Title:
Chocolate Truffle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4(1 oz)squares unsweetened chocolate, chopped
1 C semisweet choc. chips
6 T butter
3 eggs
1 C white sugar
1 1/2 t vanilla
1/2 C flour
2 T unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 t salt
1 C semisweet choc. chips

Directions:
1. In microwave or metal bowl over pan of simmering water, melt unsweetened chocolate, 1 C choc. chips,and butter stirring occ. until smooth. Remove from heat and set aside to cool. In lg. bowl, whip eggs and sugar until thick and pale, about 2 min. Stir in vanilla and choc. mixture until well mixed. Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt; gradually stir into choc mixture. Fold in remaining 1 cup choc. chips. Cover dough and chill at least an hour or overnight.
2. Preheat oven 350. Roll chilled dough into 1 inch balls. Place on ungreased cookie sheets so they are 2 inches apart.
3. Bake for 9-11 min. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 min. before removing to wire rack.
