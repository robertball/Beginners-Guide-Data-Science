Title:
Chocolate Coconut Burfi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plus 2 tablespoons unsweetened coconut
One 8 ounce package cream cheese
6 squares white chocolate, melted
1 teaspoon cardamom
2 squares semi sweet chocolate, melted

Directions:
Cook 2 tablespoons of coconut in non stick skillet on medium for two minutes or until criisp and lightly browned, stirring frequently. Beat cream cheese until creamy. Add remaining coconut white chocolate and cardamom; mix well. Pat to 1/2 inch thickness on bottom of an 8 inch square baking dish that has been greased. Drizzle with semi sweet chocolate and top with toasted coconut. Score, refrigerate and cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
This is an addictive square that is easy to prepare. Sliced almonds can be added to the top of the drizzled chocolate if desired.
