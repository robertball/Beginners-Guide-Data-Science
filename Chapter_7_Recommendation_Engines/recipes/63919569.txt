Title:
Creamy Tomato Sauce with Greek Yogurt (Un-Vodka Sauce) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz pasta (used gluten free spaghetti)
olive oil
6-8 cloves garlic, chopped
basil, chopped
dried oregano (1-3 tsp)
salt
28 oz tomatoes (Marzano)
1/2 c. Greek yogurt
fresh chopped basil to serve
parm cheese, grated, to serve

Directions:
heat oil in large sauce pan, add garlic, add some chopped basil and oregano and salt; cook on low to soften;
add tomatoes, mash if whole;
continue simmer 20-30 min
puree 1/2 to 3/4 tomatoes in blender
add yogurt to blender
add salt/basil to taste
add agave if too tart
return to pan and heat through
meanwhile cook pasta
add pasta to sauce
add fresh basil and parm to serve
