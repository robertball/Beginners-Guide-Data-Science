Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. Sugar
1/4 lb. Butter
1/2 C. Peanut Butter
1/2 C. Milk
1/3 C. Cocoa
3-1/2 C. Quick Cook Oatmeal
1 t. Vanilla
1/2 C. Salted Peanuts or Walnuts (or use chunky peanut butter)

Directions:
Bring first five ingredients to a boil, stirring constantly. Stir in remaining ingredients, drop on wax paper and cool.
