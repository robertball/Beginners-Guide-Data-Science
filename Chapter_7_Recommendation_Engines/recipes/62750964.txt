Title:
Easy Bagel Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Cheese Bagels
10 slices baby swiss cheese
Sliced honey turkey deli meat
2 tomatoes, sliced thin
Fresh or dried basil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Slice bagels in half lengthwise and place them on a baking sheet.  Cover each half with deli meat, a slice of baby swiss cheese, and a slice of tomato.  Top tomato with basil and salt and pepper to taste.  Place under the broiler, watching closely, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.  Enjoy!
