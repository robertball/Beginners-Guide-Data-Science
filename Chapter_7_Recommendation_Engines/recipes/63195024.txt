Title:
Campfire S'Mores Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hot dog buns, cut in 1-inch pieces
4 eggs
1 14-oz. can sweetened condensed milk
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 cup tiny marshmallows
3/4 cup semisweet chocolate pieces (mini chips work as well)
5 graham cracker squares, coarsely crushed (about 1/2 cup)
2 Tbsp. milk

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325. Grease a 2-quart square baking dish; set aside. Place bun pieces on shallow baking sheet. Leave out 2 hrs to dry them out or bake 7-8 min or until dry and crisp; cool.
2. In med bowl lightly beat eggs. Stir in sweetened condensed milk, 3/4 c milk, vanilla, and nutmeg; set aside.
3. Place bun pieces in prepared baking dish. Evenly pour milk mixture over buns. Sprinkle chocolate pieces, marshmallows evenly over top. Let stand 5 min. Sprinkle evenly with graham crackers. Bake uncovered, for 35 min or until a knife inserted near center comes out clean. (May use double boiler method in oven if desired to keep from possible burning or sticking).
4. For drizzle, in small saucepan heat and whisk handful of marshmallows, handful chocolate pieces, and 2 tbsp milk over low heat until melted and smooth. Drizzle over bread pudding. Cool 20-30 min before serving.
