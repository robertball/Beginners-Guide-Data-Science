Title:
Mary Schmidt's Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) bag of fresh cranberries, washed
1 cup pure maple syrup (not pancake syrup)
1 cup cranberry-raspberry juice
grated zest of 1 orange
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Wash berries, remove crinkled and old berries. Let drain. Measure and prepare all other ingredients and zest orange. Set all ingredients aside.
In heavy sauce pan, combine cranberries, maple syrup, juice and zest, all at once. Bring to boil, skim off any foam. Lower heat and cover; cook on low until cranberries pop, about 10 minutes. In the meantime, chop walnuts. After 10 minutes of cooking add walnuts and turn off fire on cranberries. Let sit for 1/2 hour then pour into bowl and place in refrigerator. Ready in the morning for Thanksgiving!
