Title:
Banana Oat Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 mashed ripe bananas
1/3 c. unsweetened applesauce
2 cups rolled oats (not quick oats)
1/4 c. almond milk
1/2 c. raisins or dried cranberries (optional)
1 tsp. vanilla
baking powder opt
baking soda opt
unsweetened coconut opt
dried sweet pineapple opt
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Put into an 8X8 pan that has been lined with parchment paper.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
