Title:
Fruit  Cookies/Quickbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups all-purpose flour
(You can use a blend of different flours and if you use self rising then omit the baking powder and salt)
2 TBSP baking powder
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup coconut oil
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
4 eggs, beaten
1 and 1/2 teaspoon extract (your choice, I use vanilla)
1 cup almond milk
3-4 cups fresh fruit (chopoed if larger than a marble)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease cookie sheet.
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
In a large bowl,  mix coconut oil with sugars. Beat in eggs and extract flavoring.  Mix in milk and flour mixture alternately in three parts, starting with the milk.  Gently fold in fruit.
Drop batter by tablespoons onto prepared sheets 1 1/2 inches apart.
Bake 12 to 15 minutes.
