Title:
Cheesy Hasbrown Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of cream of chicken
1 16 oz. container of sour cream
2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
1 tsp of salt
1 bag of frozen hash brown potatoes
1 cup melted butter
2 cups of crushed corn flakes

Directions:
Defrost hash browns in refrigerator. In a bowl mix sour cream, can of soup, cheese and 1/2 a cup of melted butter. Put in a casserole dish evenly. Layer crushed corn flakes on top. Pour the other half of melted butter on top. Bake at 350 for 1 hour
