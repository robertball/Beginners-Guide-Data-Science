Title:
Grandma Kohl's Whole Wheat Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c milk
3 TBSP molasses
2 TBSP sugar
2 tsp salt
1/4 c shortening

Directions:
Add 1 pkg yeast in 1/2 c warm water to dissolve. Add to cool mixture and add flour about 2 1/4 c white flour and 2 1/2 c whole wheat flour. Let rise til doubled in bulk. Make into rolls and let rise again. Bake @ 375 til brown.
