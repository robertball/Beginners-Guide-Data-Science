Title:
Venezuelan Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs of Extra lean ground beef or Ground Turkey
1 teaspoon of white pepper
1 small onion (chopped)
1 small green pepper (chopped)
1 small red pepper (chopped)
1 tablespoon of chopped garlic
3 tablespoons of chili powder
2 tablespoons of cumin
1.5 tablespoons of dried oregano
1 large bay leaf
1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
1/2 tablespoon of salt
1/4 cup of olive oil
15oz. can of Red Kidney Beans
2 28oz. cans of crushed tomatoes
(2 packets of beef bone marrow if using ground turkey)

Directions:
Turn stove on and heat oil in a pot and glaze onions. Add garlic and then remove onions and garlic from pot.
Brown ground beef/turkey and return onions and garlic to the pot. Add chopped peppers, salt and oregano. Wait about 1-2 minutes and add the 2 cans of crushed tomatoes. Stir well and taste for salt.
Add chili powder, cumin, cayenne and white pepper.
Cook for about 3 hours on very low heat. Stir and check about every hour. Add the beans in the pot for the last 45 minutes of cooking. Remove bay leaf after 3rd hour.
