Title:
Simple Soup Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2642336.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb dried pinto beans
1 smoked ham hock
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cayenne
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp oregano
Water to cover

Directions:
Add all ingredients to crock pot, cover with enough water to cover by 3"; stir to combine.  Cook covered and on high for 8 hours.  Check occasionally first two hours or so to make sure that the beans remain covered with water; add additional water to cover beans if necessary.
Turn heat to low, vent or remove lid and cook for 4 hours or until liquid has thickened.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
Pinto beans are a simple, inexpensive, and delicious dish.  Try them with a big slice of cornbread for a wonderfully satisfying meal!
