Title:
Chinese Brown Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup beef broth
1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon oyster sauce
1 teaspoon dark soy sauce (I use low sodium soy sauce the dark soy sauce will give it a deeper brown color)
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions:
Combine the above ingredients in order and bring to a boil, stirring.

Submitter: 
rainbowsandy
Description: 
This is the type of sauce used in a lot of Chinese dishes. Ex.; chicken and broccoli
