Title:
Slow Cooker Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982130.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Ground Turkey, any variety
1 (19.5 ounce) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Hot Italian Sausage, casings removed
1 (1 pound) package processed cheese, cubed
1 (11 ounce) can sliced jalapeno peppers, drained
1 medium onion, diced
1/2 pound Cheddar cheese, cubed
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, cubed
1 (8 ounce) container cottage cheese
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 (8 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
3 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and black pepper
Crackers or tortilla chips

Directions:
Slow Cooker Directions: Cook and stir ground turkey and sausage in a medium skillet over medium-high heat; drain. Transfer to slow cooker. Add processed cheese, jalapeno peppers, onion, Cheddar cheese, cream cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream, tomatoes and garlic to slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper. Cover; cook on HIGH 1 to 2 hours or until cheeses are melted. Serve with crackers or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
Prepared in the slow cooker and packed with seasoned turkey, jalapenos, onion, and plenty of smooth, cheesy goodness, this party hit is sure to disappear in minutes!
