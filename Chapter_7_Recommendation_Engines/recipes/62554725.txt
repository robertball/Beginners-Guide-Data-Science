Title:
Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken breasts
1 (28 ounce) can whole crushed tomatoes
1 t cumin
1 medium onion, chopped
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups water
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 cup white wine
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 bay leaf
1 can corn
1 can kidney beans
1 can northern beans
1 can navy beans
1 can black beans
1/2 c chopped cilantro
Corn Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Place chicken, tomatoes, onion, green chiles, and garlic into a slow cooker. Pour in water and chicken broth, and season with cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and bay leaf. Stir in beans, corn and cilantro. Cover, and cook on Low setting for 6 to 8 hours or on High setting for 3 to 4 hours. Shred chicken before serving.
To serve, sprinkle tortilla chips over soup.

Submitter: 
Divemistress
Description: 
A quick, no-fuss version of chicken tortilla soup! All you do is put everything into the slow cooker, and turn it on. Then garnish with baked corn tortilla strips!Modified from: Slow-Cooker Chicken Tortilla Soup
