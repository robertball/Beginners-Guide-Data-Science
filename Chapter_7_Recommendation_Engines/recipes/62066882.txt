Title:
White Trash candy/snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Chex cereal
2 cups Cheerios toasted oat cereal
2 cups peanuts
2 cups M&M's
2 cups of mini pretzles
2-3 8 oz bags white chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix all of the dry ingredients in a large bowl. Melt the white chocolate chips in the microwave and then pour into the bowl with the dry ingredients until covered.  Lay the mixture flat on cookie sheets w/wax paper and let dry until hardened (you can refrigerate if in a hurry). Remove from wax paper and break the mixture apart. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
MRS. SEALS
Description: 
The best darn snack on the planet!  My friend makes this at Christmas and once you start eating it you cannot stop.  I have seen different varieties (w/or w/o peanuts; w/or w/o m&ms); you can make it howwever you want.  Makes a ton and is great as a holiday gift.
