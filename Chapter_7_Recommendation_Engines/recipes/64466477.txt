Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2441822.jpg
Ingredients:
Marshmallows, Hershey's Chocolate bar, DOTS (by Tootsie)

Directions:
Melt Hershey's chocolate bar till liquid. (Don't over melt, it turns to powder).
Roll the marshmallow in the chocolate like the Dinomite rolls around in mud.
Once smothered, add a green Dot to the top. GREEN ONLY. The Dinomite only likes Green Dots.
Let cool
offer as a sacrifice to the Dinomite
Then it's ready to eat.
