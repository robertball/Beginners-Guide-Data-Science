Title:
Foolproof Flat Iron Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2, 1 pound each flat iron steaks
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
4 cloves mined garlic
3 tablespoons finely chopped green onions
1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
In an extra large zip lock freezer bag - place all ingredients and seal bag pressing most of the air out of it.
Knead the ingredients with your hands making sure to mix everything well covering the steaks thoroughly.
Place bag in refrigerator to marinate for 1 hour.
After marinating, let steak reach room temperature before cooking. About 10 minutes.
Meanwhile preheat grill to medium high heat.
Remove meat from bag (discard marinade) and grill steaks for 6-7 minutes per side or until steak reaches desired doneness.
Remove the steak from grill and let stand for a couple minutes to rest before slicing.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
My special recipe combines balsamic vinegar, olive oil and spice creating a simple delicious marinade; while my technique yeilds the most tender and juicy steak.
