Title:
BOCA® Sausage, Barley and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen BOCA Meatless Smoked Sausage
4 cups water
1 (15.5 ounce) can great Northern beans, drained, rinsed
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1 medium onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, sliced
2 medium carrots, sliced
1/2 cup quick-cooking barley
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
Hot pepper sauce (optional)

Directions:
CUT sausage into 1/4-inch-thick slices.
PLACE sausage and all remaining ingredients except hot pepper sauce in Dutch oven or large saucepan. Bring to boil; cover.
REDUCE heat to medium-low; simmer 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.  Season to taste with hot pepper sauce.

Submitter: 
BOCA®
Description: 
Making and sitting down to a hearty bowl of low-fat, cholesterol-free bean soup has never been this easy, or this delicious.
