Oven "Fried" Fish Recipe

Directions:
Salt and pepper the fish filets. Beat egg and Milk. In separate dish, combine corn flakes, shredded cheese and thyme. Dip fish filets in egg mixture and then dredge in corn flakes mixture. Place on baking sheet sprayed with Pam or other non-stick cooking spray. Drizzle with olive oil. Bake at 500 for 20 - 30 mins until fish is flaky.
