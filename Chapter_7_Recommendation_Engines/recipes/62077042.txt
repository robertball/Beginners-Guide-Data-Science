Title:
Hearty Sausage Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-	2 lbs. sweet Italian sausage (bulk)
-	1 onion, chopped
-	4 cloves garlic, minced
-	2 - 49 oz. cans chicken broth
-	2 small zucchini, chopped
-	2 carrots, chopped
-	3  14.5 oz. cans Cannelini (white kidney beans)
-	1 - 29 oz. can diced tomatoes
-	1  10 oz. package frozen, chopped spinach
-	2 tsp. Italian seasoning
-	1  12 oz. package shell pasta

Directions:
In large stockpot (8 quart or larger) brown sausage with onion and garlic.  Drain off fat.  Add all remaining ingredients except pasta.  Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 hour.  Add pasta and simmer for an additional 20 minutes.  Serve with grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
KATHYP100
Description: 
This makes a big kettle of soup and is perfect for freezing.  The addition of sausage to a traditional minestrone recipe makes this soup a main dish.  Serve with a salad and a crusty french bread and you have a complete meal.
