Title:
Quick and Yummy Sauerbraten Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 pound chuck roast, salt and pepper, 2 onions- sliced, 2 beef bouillon cubes dissolved in boiling water, 1/3 cup brown sugar, 1/3 cup cider vinegar, 8 ginger snap cookies (crumbled), 2 Tablespoons flour, optional - noodles

Directions:
Sprinkle roast on all sides with salt and pepper.
Place roast in crock pot, add onion, bouillon, brown sugar and vinegar.
Cover tightly and cook on high 5-6 hours, turning occasionally.
Remove roast and set aside (leave crock pot on high).
Put 1/2 cup sauce into a cup or bowl, Add 2 Tablespoons flour.  Stir until smooth.
Add crumbled gingersnaps and sauce/flour mixture back into the crock pot (still on high).  Stir until sauce thickens.
Slice meat and spoon sauce over the slices.
Optionally serve over noodles.

Submitter:

Description: 
A rich and flavorful sauce, this sauerbraten recipe is sure to please! It couldn't be easier to make and will taste like you've slaved over your stove all day.
