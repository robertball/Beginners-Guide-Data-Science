Title:
Meatball Potato Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/290940.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
6 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potatoe soup, undiluted
1 cup milk
Paprika
Celery Salt
Season Salt

Directions:
1.In a bowl, combine the eggs, bread crumbs and soup mix. Crumble beef over mixture and mix well. Cover cookie sheet w/ foil. Shape meat mixture into 1-in. balls and place on cookie sheet. Bake meatballs in oven at 425 degrees for 10-15 minutes until brown. Sprinkle with flour; gently roll to coat.
2.Place half of the potatoes in a greased 2-1/2-qt. baking dish. Top with meatballs and remaining potatoes. In a bowl, combine cream of potatoe soup and milk, sprinkle with celery salt, paprika, and season salt. Stir until blended; pour over potatoes. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 60-65 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
