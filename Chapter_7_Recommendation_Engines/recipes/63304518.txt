Title:
Creamy Chicken and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP margarine
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 small onion, sliced
1/8 tsp garlic powder (or 1 clove, minced)
1 (10 3/4 oz) can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Brown chicken in butter on both sides for approx. 15 minutes total.
Remove chicken and add mushrooms, onion, and garlic.  Saute.
Add soup, milk, and pepper.  Heat to boiling.
Add chicken.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is one of mine and my husband's favorite early recipes.  We used to make it all the time.  It's great to make for company since it can be finished in the oven if you are having a crowd.
