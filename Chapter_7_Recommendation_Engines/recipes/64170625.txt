Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 T. Butter, Room Temp
3/4 C. Sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Flour
1 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 tsp. Salt
1 C. Whole Wheat Flour
3 Large Bananas, Mashed
1 tsp. Vanilla
1 C. Chocolate Chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350.  Grease 9 X 5 X 3" bread pan.
2. Cream butter & sugar till light & fluffy.  Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
3. Sift all purpose flour, baking soda & salt together, stir in whole wheat flour and add to the creamed mixture, mixing well.
4. Fold in bananas, vanilla & chocolate chips.
5. Pour mixture into pan, bake 50 to 60 min. or till cake tester comes out clean-cool in pan.
