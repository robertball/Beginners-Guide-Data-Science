Title:
Air Fryer Breakfast Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5633006.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 pound breakfast sausage, fully cooked and crumbled
4 large eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup shredded cheese blend of Monterrey Jack and cheddar
2 tablespoons red bell pepper, diced
1 green onion, chopped
pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)
non stick cooking spray

Directions:
In a bowl mix sausage, eggs, cheese, bell pepper, onions and cayenne until well combined.
Preheat air fryer to 360 degrees Fahrenheit according to manufactures instructions.
Spray a non-stick 6"x 2" cake pan with cooking spray. Place mixture in the cake pan and cook for 18-20 minutes or until the egg mixture is set.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Frittatas are easy to make using a cake pan that fits in the fryer basket. Switch up the ingredients to suit your taste. If you are worried about the frittata sticking to the cake pan line the bottom of the pan with parchment paper.
