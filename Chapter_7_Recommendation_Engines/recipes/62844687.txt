Title:
Slimers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 flour tortillas
Refried beans
Cheese
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1- 1 1/2 cup milk
4oz can diced green chili

Directions:
Make 8-10 bean and cheese burritos, place in cake pan.
Mix cream of chicken soup, milk and green chili, pour over burritos.
Bake at 350 for 45 mins.
