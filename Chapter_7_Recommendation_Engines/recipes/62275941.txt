Title:
Troy's Favorite Pimento Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/347674.jpg
Ingredients:
5 slices of bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
1/2lb extra sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2lb extra sharp white cheddar cheese, shredded
1 7oz jar of pimentos, drained and diced
1/2ts black pepper
1/2ts chili powder
1/4ts seasoned salt
1/4ts garlic powder
1/3 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together the bacon, cheese, peppers, and seasoning until thoroughly incorporated.  Add mayo and combine.  Refrigerate for an hour.

Submitter: 
I Love Troy
Description: 
I generally don't like Pimento Cheese because there is too much mayonnaise.  After playing around one day, I came up with this recipe.  A pimento cheese where you can taste the ingredients, and not the mayonnaise.
