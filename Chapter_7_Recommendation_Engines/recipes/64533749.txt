Title:
Peanut Butter Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3076475.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or use milk of preference)
1 tbsp cacao powder (or 1/3 of dark chocolate bar)
1/2 tbsp almond butter, peanut butter or any nut butter you love
2 packets stevia or sweetener of preference
1-2 shakes of cinnamon
Optional toppings: GF marshmallows, Reddiwhip

Directions:
In a small pot, add all ingredients & bring to medium heat
Whisk together the ingredients until fully incorporated and steaming hot
Serve, add optional toppings & enjoy

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Steamy, hot, chocolate, peanut butter and marshmallows melt in your mouth in this Peanut Butter Hot Chocolate. A decadent and healthy drink!
