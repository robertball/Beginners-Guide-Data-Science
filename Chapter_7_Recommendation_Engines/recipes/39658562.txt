Title:
Genevieve's Bayou Seafood Casserol Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2669223.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese
1 stick plus 2 T butter
1 pound shrimp
1 lg onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
2 ribs celery, sliced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can mushrooms, drained
1 T garlic salt
1/2 t tobasco
1/2 t red pepper
1 pound crab meat
1 cup cooked rice
2 C sharp cheddar, grated
2 C cracker crumbs

Directions:
Melt cream cheese and butter.  Set aside.  Saute shrimp. onion, bell pepeer and celery in 2 T butter.  Add to cream cheese mixture.  Add soup, mushrooms, seasonings, crabmeat, and rice.  Mix well.  Put in two quart casserole. Top with cheese and cracker crumbs and bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
A delicious casserole for those cold winter days.
