Title:
Gluten Free Busy Day Meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. chuck roast
1 package HerbOx beef bouillon
1 can Amy's Organic Cream of Tomato Soup
3 lb. red potatoes (peeled and cut in quarters)
1 lb. bag carrots, peeled and cut in big chunks
1 small onion, diced
1 10 oz. package frozen peas
3 teaspoons brown sugar
1 cup water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat some olive oil in a large saute pan.
Sprinkle the roast with the powdered bouillon and brown on both sides.
Place roast in large roasting pan.
Place vegetables around the roast.
Pour the soup over the roast.
Combine the brown sugar and water. Then pour it over the roast.
Seal with heavy duty foil.
Bake at 375 for 3 hours.
