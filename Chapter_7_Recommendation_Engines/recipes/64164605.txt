Title:
Dad's Sunday Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef, 1lb ground sausage, 2 cans kidney beans, 1 can chili beans, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 can tomato paste, 1/2 sweet onion (chopped), cumin, garlic salt and chili powder. Add peppers of your choice to spice it up!

Directions:
Add kidney beans, chili beans, diced tomatoes, chopped onion and peppers (optional) to a large stock pot. Heat on low. Brown ground beef and sausage, then add to pot. Add garlic salt, cumin and chili powder to taste. Cook on low - med heat, covered, for 30 minutes. Enjoy with crackers, corn bread and ice cold beer.
