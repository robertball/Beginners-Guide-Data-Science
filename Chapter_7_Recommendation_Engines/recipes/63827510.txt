Title:
Chicken Lemon with Capers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
2 tbsp capers - drained and finely chopped
1 cup dry white wine or chicken stock
2 lemons - juice and zest
3 cloves garlic
1/3 cup butter
1/3 cup olive oil
1/2 cup Italian parsley - finely chopped
salt & pepper to taste
1/4 cup flour

Directions:
Cut the chicken breasts in 1/2 lengthwise and lightly pound to a uniform thickness. Flour lightly.  Heat the oil and butter in a skillet until very hot, add the garlic and saute for only a few seconds.  Add the chicken and cook for about 1 minute.  turn the meat, season with salt & pepper, add the wine, lemon juice, lemon zest, capers and parsley.  Allow the liquid to evaporate, turning once or twice to coat the meat.  Transfer to a serving platter and serve hot.
