Title:
Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
* 2 Tablespoons chopped green onions
* 1 cup chopped pecans
* 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
* 2-3 slices chopped ham

Directions:
Mix cheese, onion, Worcestershire sauce, and small pieces of ham together and form into a ball. Roll cheese ball into chopped nuts. Refrigerate and serve with crackers.

Submitter:

Description: 
this cheese ball is versatile, you can basically add different meats or flavors that you want to it.
