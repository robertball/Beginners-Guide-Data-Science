Title:
mashed sweet potatoes with mini-marshmellow's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 sweet potatoes
shortning
1 stick butter(melted)
1/2 bag or less mini-marshmellow's
brown sugar (to taste)

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 375*.  Coat the sweet potatoes with the shorting. Place the sweet potatoes in the pre-heated oven and cook for about 1 1/2 hours. The potatoes will be done when you can easily pierce the sweet potato with the fork. When the potatoes are cool enough to handle, peel the skins.
Mash the sweet potatoes in a 9x13 baking dish. Mix in melted butter and brown sygar.
Cook in pre-heated oven for 30 min. Top with mini-marshmellow's and bake for an additional 15 - 20 min. Enjoy!!!!
