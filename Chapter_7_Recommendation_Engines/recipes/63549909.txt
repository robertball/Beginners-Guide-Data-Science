Title:
Chicken Tortilla Salsa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969775.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds chicken tenders (cut into bite size pieces... do not precook)
1 medium chopped onion
1 tsp minced garlic
2 tsp chili powder
2 tsp cumin
1 (28oz)can diced tomatoes
1 (32oz)chicken broth
1 (16oz) chunky medium/hot salsa
1 (12oz) can yellow corn
1 (12oz) can of hominy
1 (15oz) black beans rinsed & drained
Tb Olive Oil
Fritos (optional)
Sour Cream (optional)

Directions:
Saute onion/garlic in olive oil until onion is soft.  Add cumin and chili powder, tomatoes, chicken broth, salsa, black beans.  Simmer for 45 minutes.  Add hominy and corn and simmer for an additional 15 minutes.
Serve in bowl with topping of fritos and sour cream

Submitter: 
Lynn Mauk
Description: 
Great dish for a cold winter's night.  Husband went back for seconds.  Great dish for low carb diets.  Make a double batch and freeze
