Title:
Mini Jalapeno Corn Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442556.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/4 cup cornmeal
2 t baking powder
1 t salt
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
1/3 cup jalapenos, pickled
2 T honey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Grease a 24-cup mini muffin pan with a nonstick spray.
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cornmeal, baking powder and salt.
In a separate bowl, beat the eggs with the jalapenos, milk and honey.
Add the dry mixture slowly to the wet mixture.
If desired, add a little of the jalape?o liquid to make it nice and spicy.
Spoon the batter into the sprayed muffin tin. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Bite sized corn muffins with a nice kick.  I've substituted buttermilk for milk and diced green chilies for jalapenos if milder taste is preferred.
