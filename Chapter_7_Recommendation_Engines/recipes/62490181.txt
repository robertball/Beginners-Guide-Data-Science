Title:
Steamed white cod / garoupa with crispy salted radish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 piece white cod fish fillet
50 g preserved chopped radish (chai por), washed and drained
1 tbsp chopped garlic
2 stalks spring onions
3 thin slices Ginger
Seasoning :
2 tbsp light soy sauce
1 tbsp fish sauce
1 tbsp sugar
1 tsp Chinese cooking wine (Shao Hsing Hua Tiao cooking wine)
2 tbsp water

Directions:
Clean fish fillet and pat dry with kitchen paper towels.  Place stalks of spring onion and Ginger on the fish and steam over rapid boiling water for 5-7 minutes.  Remove fish from steamer and discard spring onion and Ginger.
Heat oil in a wok and deep fry the preserved radish and garlic until golden and crisp.  Remove and set aside.
Combine seasoning ingredients in a small saucepan and bring to just a boil.  Pour sauce over the cooked fish and sprinkle with the crispy radish and garlic and add garnishing before serving.
