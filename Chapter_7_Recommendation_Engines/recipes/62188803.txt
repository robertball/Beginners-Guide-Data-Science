Title:
CJ's Vanilla Spice Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/300454.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups water or milk
2 cups old-fashioned oats
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
3 - 4 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 - 1 cup raisins (optional)
1/2 - 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Bring water/milk and salt to boil.
Add oats, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
When oats are cooked - approximately 5 minutes - swirl in remaining ingredients.
Serve with milk.

Submitter: 
Cynde Contreras
Description: 
I couldn't seem to find a homemade oatmeal recipe that was tasty enough for me...so after trying a whole lot I came across on the web, this is what I make on a regular basis. I hope you like it as much as my son does!
