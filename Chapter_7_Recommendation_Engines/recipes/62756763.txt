Title:
Blueberry Cream Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups - graham crackers crumbs
1/4 cups - sugar
6 tbsps - butter (melted)
1/2 cups -  sugar
1 envelope - unflavored gelitin
3/4 cups-  cold water
1 cup-  sour cream
8oz - blueberry yogurt
1/2 tsp-  vanilla
1/2 cup - whipping cream
1 cup-  fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
1. Crust. In bowl combine graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Take 1/4 of crust mixture and set aside. Press remaining crumb mixture into bottom of 10x6x2 pan.
2. In saucepan mix the 1/2 cup sugar and gelatin, add cold water. Heat and stir mixture until gelatin and sugar is dissolved. In a bowl, combine your sour cream and yougurt, gradually blend in gelatin mixture. Add vanilla. Set in fridge to chill for 30 min or until partially set.
3. Whip cream to soft peaks, and then fold in partially set mixture of sour cream and yogurt. Stir in blueberries.
4. Pour completed blueberry mixture over crust. Even out in pan and sprinkle remaining graham cracker mixture on top.
5. Chill overnight.
