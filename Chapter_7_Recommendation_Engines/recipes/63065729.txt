Title:
Microwave Herb Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
Shredded cheddar cheese
Dried dill weed
1 t milk
Buttery spread or softened butter

Directions:
Whisk egg, milk, and a pinch of cheese until smooth.  Pour into a coffee cup.
Microwave for 30 seconds, then whisk again.  Repeat until the egg is almost but not quite done.
Then, spread a little spread or butter on top.  Layer cheese and dill until there is a 1/2 inch layer of cheese.
Put in the microwave until the cheese is completely melted.
Remove from the coffee cup and serve cheese side up.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
A no-hassle solution to breakfast or lunch.  If you don't like dill weed, you can use nearly any other dried herb instead.
