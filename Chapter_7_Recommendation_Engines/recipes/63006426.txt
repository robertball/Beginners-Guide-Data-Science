Title:
Spicy Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container light sour cream
1 cup light mayonnaise
2 (11 ounce) cans Mexican-style corn, drained
2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
1 (10 oz) can Rotel diced tomatoes & chilies, drained
1 tsp cajun seasoning
1 dash tabasco (adjust to taste)
1 1/4 cups reduced fat shredded cheddar or pepper jack cheese

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together sour cream, mayonnaise, corn, jalapeno chile peppers, RoTel, cajun seasoning, tabasco and cheese.  Ready to serve!

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
A can of Mexican-style corn adds texture, taste and color to this standard Southwestern dip.Modified from: Spicy Corn Dip
