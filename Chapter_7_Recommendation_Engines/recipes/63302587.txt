Title:
Spicy Watermelon Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups watermelon - cubed
1 large yellow tomato - cubed
1 large red tomato - cubed
1/8 cup Sriracha Hot Sauce
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup olive oil
2 teaspoons honey - generous
Fresh Lemon Basil - chopped
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut up watermelon & tomatoes and place on paper towels to drain some of the mositure
Mix Sriracha, rice wine vinegar, olive oil & honey in a bowl and whisk until oil & vinegar have combined
Put watermelon & tomatoes into a bowl & pour mixture over the top. Sprinkle lemon basil, salt & pepper over the top and lightly toss until coated and lemon basil is distributed equally.
Serve

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
A quick, colorful, spicy & refreshing salad that is perfect for any cookout.
