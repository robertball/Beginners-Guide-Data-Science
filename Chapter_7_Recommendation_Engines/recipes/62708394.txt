Title:
Shoestring Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614301.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large russet potatoes
1 tablespoon olive oil or corn oil
¼ tsp salt
¼ tsp freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475 F
Peel potatoes.
Cut into lenghtwise strips. Place in a large bowl. Add the oil, salt and pepper toss to coat.
Place potaote strips in a single layer on non stick baking sheet ( leave a little space between each strip)
Bake until the undersides are golden brown about 10 minutes.
Turn potatoes over and bake 10 minutes more.
Place in a serving dish or basket
