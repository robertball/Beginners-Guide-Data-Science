Title:
SF FF egg nog by CAJUNCHEF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon FAT FREE or SKIM milk
1 tsp. Nutmeg,
2 tsps Vanilla,
3/4 tsp Rum Flavoring
one 1.34 oz. package Sugar Free Instant French Vanilla Pudding mix.

Directions:
Furst, yous pour 1/2 gallon FAT FREE or SKIM milk into a large mixing bowl, add 1 tsp. Nutmeg, 2 tsps Vanilla, 3/4 tsp Rum Flavoring and one 1.34 oz. package Sugar Free Instant French Vanilla Pudding mix. Dat yous can get at Wal-Mart.
Using your electric mixer, mix all together fur 2 to 3 minutes. Pour mixture back into milk container, shake well and refrigerate 4 to 6 hours, that way the flavors get to meet each other and get acquainted.
When ready to serve, shake well again, pour into glasses, add Rum or Bourbon as you like, FAT FREE whipped topping, lil more nutmeg or cinnamon and/or crushed Pepper Mint Candy on top and serve, Hot or Cold. Upon sitting mixture may thicken somewhat, ifn it becomes too thick add lil more FAT FREE milk and shake well until desired consistency.
