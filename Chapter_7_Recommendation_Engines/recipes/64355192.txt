Title:
Chipotle Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Xanthan Gum________________________1/4 teaspoon
Salt, Kosher, Coarse_______________1 1/4 teaspoons
Black Pepper_______________________1/4 teaspoon
Dark Brown Sugar___________________1/2 Cup
Tomato Paste_______________________1/2 Cup
Chipotle Peppers, Canned in adobe__1/4 Cup
Water______________________1/4 Cup
White Vinegar, Distilled___1/4 Cup+2 1/2 Tablespoons
Worshershire Sauce_________1/2 Tablespoon

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Blend the Xanthan Gum, salt, black pepper, and Brown sugar together in a medium sauce pan.
Puree the tomato paste, chipotle peppers, water, vinegar, and worshersire sauce together in a blender; add the dry ingredients in the sauce pan.
Heat the mixture on high heat and continue to cook until all of the sugar has dissolved; simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
