Title:
Crockpot Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg chuck roast
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 packet dry french onion
1/2 cup wine
onions
carrots
potatoes
celery

Directions:
Chop all vegetables into large chunks
Place roast, soups, and wine in crockpot
Put veggies on top
Cook on low 6-8 hours
