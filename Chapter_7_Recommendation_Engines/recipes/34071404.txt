Title:
Favorite Strawberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups halved or quartered firm strawberries
2 cups granulated sugar
1/4 cup lemon juice
*add 1 cup finely chopped rhubarb to strawberries in step one

Directions:
1.  Mix berries and sugar and let stand for 8 hours, stirring occasionally.
2.  Place berry mixture in medium stainless stell.  Bring to a boil over medium heat.  Add lemon juice, return to a boila nd boil rapidly for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat, cover and let stand for 24 hours.
3.  Bring berries to a full boil over high heat and boil rapidly for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.
4.  Ladle into hot jar and process for 10 minutes.
