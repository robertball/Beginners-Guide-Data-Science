Title:
Slow Cooker Creamy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions, finely chopped (use cuisine art, small)
20 ounce thick chicken broth
5 - 8 large potatoes, diced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups cream
1 cup water
1 cup milk

Directions:
Place onion in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until onions are soft.
Transfer the onion to a slow cooker, and stir in chicken broth, potatoes, and salt. Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 7 hours, stirring occasionally.
In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and cream/half-and-half. Stir into the soup along with the milk. Cover, and cook another 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Laurie Sherman
Description: 
This is a very rich and creamy soup.  It may be cooked on the stove or in a slow cooker. Garnish with chives or other herbs, pref. fresh, if desired.Modified from: Slow Cooker Creamy Potato Soup
