Title:
Monster Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1 T. vanilla
1 T. syrup
8 t. baking soda
18 c. oatmeal
1 lb. chocolate chips
1 lb. M&M;'s
2 lb. brown sugar
4 c. white sugar
1 lb. margarine
3 lb. crunchy peanut butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Bake at 350 degree for 12-15 minutes.

Description: 
My favorite cookie since I was a child; still a favorite today. WARNING: Makes an enormous batch!
