Title:
Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c milk
1 c crushed ice
1 1/2 t vanilla
1/2 package sugar free/ fat free pudding mix
1 frozen banana cut into chunks before freezing or 5 - 6 oz frozen fruit

Directions:
Blend it all together until thick rich and creamy.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
Just fruit and a little milk mixed with pudding makes this low fat and creamy treat!
