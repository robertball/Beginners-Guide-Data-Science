Title:
Dirt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 - 2 bags of Oreo Cookies
1 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
1 cup powdered sugar
1/4 cup butter  (softened)
1 large box of instant vanilla pudding
2 1/2 cup of milk
1 12 oz cool whip

Directions:
Crush cookies, a few at a time, in a blender.  You want the result to look like potting soil; set aside.
Cream together butter, powdered sugar and cream cheese; set aside.
Combine pudding and milk; beat to pudding consistency.  Add to cream cheese mixture.  Fold in Cool Whip.
Layer cookie and filling mixture alternately ending with cookie.
Chill until ready to serve.
