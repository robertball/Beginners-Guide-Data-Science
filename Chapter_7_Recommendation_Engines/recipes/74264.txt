Title:
California Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
1 tablespoon ketchup
1/2 teaspoon ginger, ground
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound chicken breasts, boneless, skinless, cut into strips
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 cups Mann's California Stir Fry
8 ounces pineapple, chunks, unsweetened, drained
rice, cooked

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine soy, sugar, cider vinegar, ketchup, ginger; set aside.
In a large skillet or wok, stir fry chicken and garlic in oil for 6-7 minutes until chicken is no longer pink. Add Mann's California Stir Fry and stir fry for 3-4 minutes or until crisp tender. Stir in pineapple and soy sauce mixture; heat through. Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
Mann's Fresh Vegetables
Description: 
Chicken, vegetables, and pineapple chunks are stir-fried, and served with a tangy sauce. California Stir Fry Mix makes this popular cooking method quick and easy!
