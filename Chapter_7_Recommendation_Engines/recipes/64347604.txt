Title:
Jeni's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 yellow onion, diced
1 bell pepper (red, yellow, or green), seeded & diced
garlic powder, onion powder, seasoning salt, black pepper, italian seasoning to taste
1 can chopped tomatoes
2 cans tomato sauce
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, opt
3 cups spaghetti, cooked al dente

Directions:
Dice onion and bell pepper. Cook with ground beef in a large skillet on med-high heat until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear, stirring occasionally to break up meat. Season as desired while cooking. When beef is throughly cooked, drain and blot gently with paper toweling (depending on fat percentage). Return to skillet. Add tomatoes and tomato sauce, heat on medium until bubbling. Stir in parmesan cheese, if desired.
Serve over cooked spaghetti noodles, or stir cooked noddles into sauce and serve.

Submitter: 
Jeni Reeves Smart
Description: 
Quick and easy homemade spaghetti sauce. I don't like store-bought sauce, so this recipe is perfect with lots of flavor and less sodium and sugar than from the jar.
