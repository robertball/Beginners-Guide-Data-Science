Title:
Easy Keto Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5681786.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, unsalted
2 cloves garlic, crushed
2 oz. cream cheese
2 cups heavy whipping cream
1 1/2 cups shredded parmesan cheese
dash white pepper, or to taste
dash nutmeg, or to  taste
dash salt, or to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat melt the butter.  Once the butter has melted saute the garlic in the hot butter.
Add the cream cheese and cream to the butter. Slowly add the parmesan cheese to the mixture, stirring constantly until the sauce is well incorporated and thickened.
Cooks note: The sauce will thicken as it cools and will become very thick if stored in the refrigerator. Simply heat the sauce up for it to be pourable.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Treat yourself and guest to creamy homemade Alfredo sauce with this easy recipe. This sauce pairs well with pasta or zoodles (spiralized zucchini). You can even add chicken or shrimp.
