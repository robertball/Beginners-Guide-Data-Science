Title:
Easy Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles, cooked and drained
6 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 onion, chopped
1/4 cup butter
2 pounds lean ground beef
4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups beef broth
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 can cream of mushroom
salt and black pepper to taste
wine

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in boiling water until done, about 8 minutes. Drain.
Meanwhile, prepare the sauce. In a large skillet, cook mushrooms and onions in 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat until soft; remove from pan.
Using the same pan, melt remaining butter. Cook ground beef in melted butter until browned. Mix in flour. Stir in beef broth adn a little wine, and cook until slightly thickened. Add mushroom and onion mixture; stir in sour cream and cream of mushroom. Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Continue cooking until sauce is hot, but not boiling. Serve sauce over egg noodles.

Description: 
A lusciously tart sauce of sauteed mushrooms, onions and sour cream is beefed up and served over tender egg noodles. Hint: do not bring the sour cream sauce to a boil.Modified from: Easy Beef Stroganoff
