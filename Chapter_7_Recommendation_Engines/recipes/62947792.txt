Title:
Taco Casserole (low sodium) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 TBLS chili powder
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp cumin
1 cup fresh cilantro leaves (washed, stems removed)
1 14 oz. can No Salt Added diced tomatoes
1 8 oz. can No Salt Added tomato sauce
1 14 oz. can No Salt Added Kidney beans
12 small size corn tortillas
3/4 cup Swiss cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Saute ground beef, onion, pepper & garlic in skillet over medium heat. Drain off excess fat.
Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce and spices. Simmer for 2 minutes.
Layer 4 tortillas on bottom of 9x13 pan prepared with nonstick spray. Place layer of beef mixture, 4 more tortillas, beans, 4 more tortillas, then rest of beef mixture.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes, lightly covered with aluminum foil.
Sprinkle with cheese and return to oven until cheese has been melted.

Submitter:

Description: 
Mexican lasagna with corn tortillas substituted for the noodles, layered with beef and beans.
