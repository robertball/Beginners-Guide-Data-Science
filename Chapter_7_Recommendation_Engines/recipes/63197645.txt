Title:
Mexican-Style Chorizo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb extra-lean ground pork
2Tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 1/2 Tbsp water
1 Tbsp red chili powder
2 Tbsp ancho chile powder
1 Tbsp red pepper flakes, crushed
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp dried oregano
1 tsp cumin seeds, crushed
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp black pepper
Corn tortillas, warmed

Directions:
Place all ingredients except tortillas in food processor and pulse to combine. Transfer to skillet coated with cooking spray and cook over medium heat until no longer pink, 12 to 15 minutes. Serve with tortillas.
