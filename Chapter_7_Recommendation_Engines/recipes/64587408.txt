Title:
Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 extra large egg yolk, at room temperature
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
2 teaspoons chopped garlic (2 cloves)
1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (3 lemons)
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups good olive oil
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place the egg yolk, mustard, garlic, lemon juice, 2 teaspoons salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper in the bowl of a food processor fitted with the steel blade.
Process until smooth.
With the food processor running, slowly pour the olive oil through the feed tube and process until thick.
Add the Parmesan and pulse 3 times to combine.
