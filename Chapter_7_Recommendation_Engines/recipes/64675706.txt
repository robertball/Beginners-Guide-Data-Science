Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup vegetable oil
2 bananas, mashed
3 cups flour
3 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups sugar
1 cup boiling water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Place ingredients as listed in large bowl without mixing
After pouring in boiling water, stir with spoon until well mixed
Do not use mixer or blender
Pour into 2 greased 5x9 loaf pans
Bake 35-40 minutes
