Title:
ribbon zucchini frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil 1 1/2 tbsp
large zucchini (with peel) (cut lengthwise in 6mm slices ) 1
Large eggs 8
Julienned deli ham 1 cup
Grated cheddar 1/2 cup
Chopped parsley 3 tbsp
Milk 1 tbsp
salt 1/8 tsp
pepper 1/4 tsp
olive oil 1 1/2 tsp
grated cheddar cheese 1/2 cup
ground nutmeg 1/4 tsp

Directions:
heat oil in pan medium high.
Add zucchini (2 to 3 minutes per side)until browned transfer to large plate
beat eggs in large bowl until frothy
Add ham, cheddar cheese, parsley, milk, salt, pepper
Heat second amount of oil, add egg mixture, reduce heat to medium low cook covered for about 10 minutes until bottom is golden remove from heat
Add zucchini on top sprinkle with second amount of cheese and nutmeg. Broil on center rack for about 2 to 3 minutes until cheese is melted and golden
