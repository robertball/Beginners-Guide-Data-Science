Title:
Bubble Pizza Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Can of biscuits
Pizza toppings of choice
Pizza Sauce
Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cut biscuits in 4th
Spray 8x8 pan.
Toss biscuits in the pan
Toss in the toppings
Pour sauce over the toppings and biscuits
Top with Shredded Cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes.
