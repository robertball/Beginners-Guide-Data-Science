Title:
Irish Breakfast Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans of sliced white potatoes or sliced white new potatoes.
1 large onion.
salt and pepper
1/4 cup of vegetable oil

Directions:
Drain potatoes on pat dry on a paper towel.
Dice onion to desired thickness I like to finely dice mine.
Combine potatoes and onions and vegetable oil in a very large skillet.
Cook on medium/high heat...Cook until potatoes are tender and stir/flip frequently, allow onions to caramelize and turn brownish. You can also cover this dish while cooking too.
Season to taste with pepper and salt.

Submitter: 
kristinamarie
Description: 
These potatoes will have your house smelling so good for breakfast people will crawl out of the wood work to come eat these!  I grew up with these potatoes being made every Sunday.
