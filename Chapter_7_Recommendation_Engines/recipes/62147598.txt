Title:
Garlic Spiniach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 oz.) bags of fresh spiniach
6 cloves of garlic thinly sliced
1 tbs unsalted butter
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 lemon juiced

Directions:
Melt the butter in a pot at medium heat. Add garlic, stir and cook about two minutes.  Add a few handfuls of spiniach and stir until wilted down.  repeat until all the spiniach is in the pot.  Add the lemon juice and garlic salt and stir until evenly distributed.

All the green flavor you love with tastey garlic-y goodness.
