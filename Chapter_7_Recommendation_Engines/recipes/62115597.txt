Title:
Apple Core and Peeling Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cores and peelings from 20 to 24 apples
7 cups water
1 (1.75 oz) box dry fruit pectin
9 cups sugar
1 - 2 drops red food coloring (optional)
Apple juice concentrate, thawed

Directions:
Cook peelings and cores in 7 cups water for 25 minutes.
Strain through a prepared cheesecloth or jelly bag.
Add enough apple juice concentrate to equal 7 cups liquid.
Add pectin and bring to a rapid boil.
Add sugar and boil hard for 1 minute.
Remove from heat and add food coloring if desired.
Pour into sterile jars, leaving 1/8" headspace; wipe jar rims, adjust lids and rings.  Process in water bath for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Graden
Description: 
Don't throw away the cores and peelings from your apples after you bake that pie, but use them to make a very delicious apple jelly.
