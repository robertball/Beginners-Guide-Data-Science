Title:
Savory Crock Pot Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2 lb. pork tenderloin
1 onion, chopped
1 can mushroom soup
1/2 cup dry white wine
4 cloves crushed garlic
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 tbsp worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Place all sauce ingredients into a crock pot and stir. Add pork tenderloin and spoon sauce over the roast. Cook on low heat for about 6 hours, basting a few times with the sauce.

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
This is a simple yet succelent pork roast in a white wine mushroom sauce.
