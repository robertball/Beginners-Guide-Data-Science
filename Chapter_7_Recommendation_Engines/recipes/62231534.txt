Title:
No-knead Cinnamon Yeast Twists Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough:
2-1/2 C flour
3/4 t salt
1/3 C sugar
2 packages (4-1/2 t) yeast
1/2 C butter
1/2 C milk
1 t vanilla
3 eggs
Coating:
1/2 C sugar
1-1/2 t cinnamon

Directions:
Stir together flour, salt, 1/3 C sugar and yeast.  Combine melted butter, milk and eggs; whisk thoroughly.
Pour liquid into dry ingredients and beat vigorously with a wodden spoon - about 30 seconds - to make a smooth, wet, sticky dough.  Cover tightly with plastic wrap and let stand at room temperature until dough has doubled in bulk, 30 to 45 minutes.
Preheat the oven to 325.  Coat baking sheets with cooking spray.  Stir together the 1/2 C sugar and cinnamon.
Scoop up generous tablespooons of dough, pushing off into sugar mixture.  Toss to coat well, then stretch the dough to a 7- to 8-inch length and twist into any shape you like.  Set twists on baking sheets.
Bake at 15 minutes until twists are golden brown.  Transfer to wire racks with a wide metal spatula.
