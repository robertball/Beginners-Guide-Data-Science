Title:
Cheesy  chili    Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cresent rolls
1 sm can diced green chilis
1 small can chopped black olives
1 tablespoon diced pimento
2 green onions finely dices
1 cup grated chedder cheese
1 8oz package cream cheese
2 tablespoons mayo

Directions:
Open cresent rolls and press together onto a cutting board creating 2 rectangles. In a small bowl cream together cream cheese,and mayo . Mix in remaining ingredients until well blended.
spread evenly onto cresent that have been pressed together into  a rectangle. Starting on the edge closest to you roll dough with mixture into a log. With a sharp knife cut into 1 in pieces and lay with the pinwheel facing up. Bake at 350 for about 10 minuetes or until slightly brown. Cool slightly before serving

Description: 
An appetizer that can be whipped up for a last minute gathering. Cheesey with a little bit of heat thay taste as good at room tempeture as they do straight from the oven.
