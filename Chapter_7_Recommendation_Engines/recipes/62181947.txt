Title:
Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups macaroni cooked al dente
1 quart stewed tomatoes
2 teaspoons paprika
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
6 ounces tomato paste = 1 can water

Directions:
In large saucepan brown ground chuck, drain.
Add tomatoes, onions, garlic, paprika, chili powder, cooked macaroni and tomato paste. Add water, a little at a time.  Simmer until heated through.
