Title:
Tangy Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
80g/2 Â¾ oz Crumbly Blue Cheese - Stilton or Roquefort etc
90ml/6tbsp White Wine Vinegar
160ml/10tbsp light Olive Oil
60g/4tbsp fresh flatleaf Parsley - finely chopped
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
In a small glass bowl, crumble the cheese and, with a fork, mash it into a smooth paste;
In a separate bowl for the vinaigrette, whisk together the vinegar and salt and pepper to taste;
Gradually add the oil slowly whisking continuously until the mixture is thick and smooth i.e emulsified;
Pour the vinaigrette over the cheese and add the parsley;
Combine well together and taste for seasoning.
