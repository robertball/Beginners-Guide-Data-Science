Title:
Marvel's Egg in a Nest Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/572434.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice of bread (white, whole wheat, homemade, etc)
1 egg
Butter or "buttery spread"
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Butter bread on both sides
Cut a circle or other shape with a cookie cutter or small tumbler
Put bread in frying pan. Place cutout circle next to bread. Turn on heat to medium
Crack egg into hole in bread and season with salt and/or pepper
When bread is toasted brown (check underside of cut-out to see) Flip bread and cut-out and finish cooking until egg yolk is soft, medium or hard as you want

Submitter: 
Marvel's Kitchen
Description: 
Kids (of all ages) will love this!  One pan, two ingredients, no mess. A classic meal for breakfast, but also great as a midnight or midday snack. Even first-time chefs will shine with this recipe.
