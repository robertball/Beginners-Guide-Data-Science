Title:
Infamous Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
.
3/4 cup crushed buttery round cracker crumbs
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 tsp italian seasoning
2 tsp grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
.
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
.
1/4 cup butter, cut into pieces
1/4 cup white wine
6--8 whole cloves of garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375ยบ and line a 9 x 12 inch baking dish with non-stick foil.
Place eggs and cracker crumbs in two separate shallow bowls.
Mix cracker crumbs with flour, Italian seasoning, Parm, garlic powder and pepper.
Dip chicken in the eggs, then dredge in the crumb mixture to coat.
Arrange coated chicken in baking dish.
Place pieces of butter around the chicken and pour in the wine and sprinkle garlic cloves around the chicken.
Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear,  turning over after 15 minutes.
Serve with buttered parsley noodles and green peas.
