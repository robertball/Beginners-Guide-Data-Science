Title:
Cream Cheese Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 large potatoes
1 package cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup green onions, finely minced
2 tbs butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook Potatoes then drain and mash them.
Use a blender to mix the mashed potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream, green onions, butter and salt & pepper together.
Grease casserole Dish.
Bake at 350F for 1 1/2 hrs.

Submitter: 
Trish McQuhae
Description: 
A wonderful combination of potatoes, cream cheese and sour cream that makes a delicious, tangy side dish to any meal.
