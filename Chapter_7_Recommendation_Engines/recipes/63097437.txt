Title:
Jul's Vegetable Tofu Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/784650.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 large onion, diced
3 stalks of celery, sliced
3 cloves garlic, chopped
3 carrots, sliced
2 tablespoons olive oil
12 cups chicken broth
28 oz can of diced tomatoes
15 oz can of crushed tomatoes
2 zucchini sliced
2 handfuls of sliced mushrooms
4 large handfuls of kale
2 cups frozen corn
1 block tofu, crumbled
sea salt
cracked ground pepper
1 tbsp dried basil
crushed red pepper flakes
bay leaf
fresh grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large stock pot. Add carrots and saute for few minutes then add the celery and onion and 1/2 tbsp basil and 3 shakes of red pepper flakes, salt and pepper.  Saute until just about soft then add the garlic.  Saute another 2 minutes. Pour in the chicken broth, diced tomatoes and crushed tomatoes, 1/2 tbsp dried basil and bay leaf.  Bring to a boil.  Then add the zucchini, mushrooms, corn and tofu.  Add a little more salt and pepper to taste.  Bring back to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 1 hour.  Add the kale, cover and simmer additional 15 minutes. Ready to serve! Sprinkle with some fresh grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
jul
Description: 
Tasty vegetable soup I threw together one cold afternoon.  You could, of course, add as many or as little veggies as you like.  Topping it with some shredded parmesan cheese really makes it yummy.
