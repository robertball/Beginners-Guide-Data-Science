Title:
Fish Sticks in a Blanket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985339.jpg
Ingredients:
16 Gorton's® Crunchy Golden Fish Sticks
1 (11.5 ounce) can refrigerated bread dough or corn bread twists
Ketchup
Mustard

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Unroll dough; separate at perforations into 16 strips. Wrap one strip of dough, stretching slightly, around each Fish Stick. Place wrapped frozen Fish Sticks with ends of dough underneath Fish Sticks in ungreased jelly roll pan, 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1.
Bake about 20 minutes or until bread is golden brown. Serve with ketchup and mustard.

Submitter: 
Gorton's®
Description: 
Get creative with the kids!  Fish Sticks in a Blanket is a great change from pigs in a blanket.
