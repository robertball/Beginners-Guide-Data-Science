Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. flour
2 tsp. salt
½ tsp. pepper
4 to 4 ½ lb. boneless beef short ribs
¼ to 1/3 c. butter
1 large onion, chopped
1 ½ c. beef broth
¾ c. cider or red wine vinegar
¾ c. packed brown sugar
½ c. chili sauce
1/3 c. ketchup
1/3 c. Worcestershire sauce
5 garlic cloves, minced
1 ½ tsp. chili powder

Directions:
Combine flour, salt and pepper and dredge ribs.  In a large skillet brown ribs in butter.
Transfer to a 5 qt. slow-cooker.  In same skillet, sauté onion till soft.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cook and stir until mixture comes to a boil.  Pour over ribs.  Cover and cook on low for 9-10 hours or until meat is tender.
