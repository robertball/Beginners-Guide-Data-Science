Title:
Cajun Penne Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Whole wheat Penne pasta
3 Chicken breast, cut into strips
1- 29 oz. can tomato sauce
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 red pepper
1 green pepper
1 yellow or orange pepper
1 onion
1 1/2 cups mushrooms
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper or less
salt & pepper

Directions:
Start large pot of water to boil pasta.
In large skillet or wok, add olive oil over medium high heat.
When hot, add chicken to skillet, cook until middle no longer pink.
When chicken is done, add a splash of water to deglaze the skillet and add minced garlic.
Reduce heat to medium low.
Add onion, green pepper, red pepper, yellow pepper & mushrooms.
Cook until tender, about 9 minutes.
Boil penne pasta for 9-10 minutes in other pot.
Add tomato sauce, cayenne pepper & whipping cream to skillet.
Let simmer on low.
Drain pasta, do not rinse.
Add pasta to chicken & veggies in skillet & mix well.
