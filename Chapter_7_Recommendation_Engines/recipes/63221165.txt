Title:
Rhubarb Pie - Single Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/836601.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups diced rhubarb
1 tblsp melted butter
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
3 tblsp flour
Frozen pie crust (or homemade)

Directions:
In a 4 cup measuring cup melt butter in microwave
Add eggs and beat with fork
Add sugar and flour
Mix well and pour over rhubarb in an unbaked pie shell
Bake in a 350 degree oven
Mixture will cover the fruit and be slightly browned

Submitter: 
Su
Description: 
Mother In Law's Recipe.
Can easily be doubled for two pies.

This recipe can also be used for other fruit
i.e.  rhaspberries, peaches
