Title:
Quick Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp AP flour
2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ancho chili powder
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
2 tsp granulated garlic
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp sugar
1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
1 1/2 cup (12 oz) water

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, whisk flour into oil and cook for 2 minutes. Whisk in cumin, chili powder, coriander, and paprika; cook 1 more minute.
Add remaining seasonings, sugar, tomato sauce and water.
Simmer 10-15 minutes.

Description: 
A savory sauce for enchiladas or wet burritos.
