Title:
Sweetened condensed milk sugar-free fake-out Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup Nonfat dry milk powder
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup splenda

Directions:
In glass measuring cup stir together milk powder & water till it's a paste. Cover and microwave on high for 45 seconds or until hot but not boiling.
Stir in Splenda.
Cover and store in fridge. Chill at least 2 hrs before using.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott Provost
Description: 
I can find sweetened condensed milk in every level of fat content, but not sugar free? Why the heck not?! Going to try this fake-out for yummy stuff like icebox pie, and see if I can't make those treats just a teeny bit healthier.
