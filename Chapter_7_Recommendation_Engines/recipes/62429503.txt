Title:
Peanut Butter Trail Mix Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 1/2 cups chunky peanut butter
1/2 cup margarine or butter, softened
2 tsp vanilla
3 eggs
4 1/2 cups rolled oats
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups trail mix

Directions:
In large bowl, beat sugar, brown sugar, peanut butter and margarine until light and fluffy.
Add vanilla and eggs; blend well.
Stir in rolled oats, baking soda and salt; mix well. Stir in trail mix.
Drop by tablespoons 2 inches apart on greased cookies sheet.
Bake 350 for 13-16 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 1 minute; remove from cookie sheets.
