Title:
Lisa's Great Dorito Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz pkg frozen corn
1 16 oz can black beans
1 diced tomato
1/3 c green onion
2tsp garlic powder
1/4 c celentro
1 bag doritos
2 lbs round steak ( or stew meat)
steak seasoning salt/ pepper
1 head of lettuce
1 16 oz can black olives
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 Dorito Salad Dressing Recipe
Dorito Salad Dressing Recipe:
1 pkg Hidden Valley Fiesta Ranch Dip
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup mayonaise
Do not follow pkg instructions.  In small bowl combine all ingredients and refrigerate.

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients in bowl.  Refrigerate at least 1 hr.  Make up Dorito Salad dressing.  30 min before actual dinner time, if using round steak, cube meat.  Heat 3 Tbls oil in large skillet.  Add meat.  Use steak seasoning salt and pepper to taste.  In large bowl layer lettuce,doritos, corn & bean mixture,meat,cheese and black olives.  Repeat until bowl is full.  Refrigerate any leftover ingredients.  Serve with Dorito Salad dressing

Submitter: 
knlneal
Description: 
This is a great anytime salad, especially for those Dorito lovers in your family.  Served with steak strips over a bed of lettuce and southwestern style vegetables.
