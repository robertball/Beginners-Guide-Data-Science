Title:
Stuffed Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 cup uncooked white rice
3 large red bell peppers, halved and seeded
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 onion, diced
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbls Oregano
1 can diced tomatoes
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 cups finely shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Half peppers, clean, and aside in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, brown the ground beef; drain. Return to heat and mix in onion, cooked rice, garlic, oregano, salt and pepper. Pour in tomato sauce and diced tomatoes and mix thoroughly. Let simmer for about 10 minutes. Remove from heat.
Spoon the meat mixture onto each half of the green peppers. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes covered and and 15 uncovered or until mixture begins to turn golden brown.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top of each stuffed pepper. Return to the oven and bake until cheese is lightly browned, about 5 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deanna Latrice Payne-Schmidt
Description: 
Red peppers stuffed with a mixture of ground beef, rice and onion, seasoned with garlic and held together with tomato sauce are topped with shredded mozzarella cheese and baked. Excellent with corn and mashed potatoes.Modified from: Kelsey's Favorite Stuffed Green Peppers
