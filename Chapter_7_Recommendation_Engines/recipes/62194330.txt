Title:
Almond Poppy Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. flour
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
3 eggs
2 c. sugar
3/4 c. oil
1 1/2 c. milk
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 tsp almond extract
1 1/2 tsp butter flavoring
1 1/2 Tbs poppy seeds

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mix until blended.  Poor into 2 greased & floured loaf pans.  Bake at 350° F for one hour (or until toothpick comes out clean).

Submitter: 
Aimée Weissman
Description: 
Great quick bread.  Perfect for fast breakfast or snack.
