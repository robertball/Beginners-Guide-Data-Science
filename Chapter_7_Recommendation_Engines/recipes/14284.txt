Title:
Grandma's Cherry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4496751.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 (15.25 ounce) can fruit cocktail, drained
3 bananas, sliced

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the cherry pie filling, fruit cocktail and sliced bananas. Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Mary J
Description: 
This was always a favorite when we went to my Grandma's house, it is easy and kids love it! We like lots of bananas in ours, so you may want to adjust this according to your own family's tastes.
