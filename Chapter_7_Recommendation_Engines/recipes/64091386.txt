Title:
Bill's Butter Flaky Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1.2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
7 oz. or 190 grams butter, chilled and diced
1/2 cup ice water

Directions:
In a large bowl or food processor, combine flour and salt. Cut in well chilled butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Do not over process.
Stir in water, a tablespoon at a time, until mixture forms a ball. Wrap in wax paper or plastic and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
Roll dough out to fit a 9 inch pie plate. Place crust in pie plate. Press the dough evenly into the bottom and sides of the pie plate.
Fill pie plate with your prepared pie filling, top with second crust and bake as instructed for pie recipe.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This delicious, flaky pie crust made with butter makes two pie crusts, but can be scaled to meet your pie baking needs.Modified from: Butter Flaky Pie Crust
