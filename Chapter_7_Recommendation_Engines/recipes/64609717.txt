Title:
Christmas Crackle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz packages of dark chocolate chips
1 14.3 oz package of mint chocolate sandwich cookies broken into chunks
1/4 white baking chips
1/4 tsp peppermint extract
2 candy canes crushed

Directions:
Line a 15x10 in rimmed baking pan with parchment paper.  Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave stirring every 15 seconds.  Stir in cookie pieces.  Spread onto prepared pan.  In a separate bowl melt the white baking chops in microwave stirring every 15 seconds.  Stir the peppermint extract into the melted white chocolate and drizzle over the dark chocolate mixture.  Sprinkle the crushed candy canes on top and refrigerate for at least an hour before breaking into pieces.  Store in an airtight container.
