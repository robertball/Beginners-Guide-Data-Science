Title:
Quick Rhubarb Chia Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4567212.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups rhubarb, chopped
1/4 cup water
5 tbsp pure maple syrup
3/4 tsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp chia seeds

Directions:
Place rhubarb in medium pot with 1/4 cup water and maple syrup. Bring to a simmer over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, for about 10-12 minutes or until rhubarb is soft and water is absorbed.
Remove from heat and stir in chia seed. Let cool and pour into 2 small jars or plastic container. Store in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
France C
Description: 
Making jam can be tedious, especially the canning process.  Whip up this quick, no fuss rhubarb jam in 15 minutes from start to finish! The chia seed is used in place of pectin to thicken the jam.
