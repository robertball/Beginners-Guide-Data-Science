Title:
Simple Caprese Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. container mini mozzerella balls, or sliced sections of mozzerella, or marinated mozzerella
1 container small cherry or other tomatoes
1 large bunch basil
3 T extra virgin olive oil
sea salt
12 small or large wooden skewers

Directions:
Drain water from mozzerella, add to large bowl
Wash and add cherry tomatoes to cheese
Pour olive oil over all; toss until lightly coated
Wash basil and pat dry
Begin threading skewers with mozzerella, a few basil leaves (fold in half before skewering), and a cherry tomato
Display skewers on festive platter and sprinkle with coarse sea salt. Use any left over basil as decoration on platter.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Cherry tomatoes, mini mozzerella balls, fresh basil leaves served on skewers. This recipe is great for all seasons. Use in summer for an easy, refreshing picnic or use during Christmas holiday for a quick, red-white-green appetizer.
