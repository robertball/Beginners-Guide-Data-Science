Title:
Quick Pull Apart "Monkey" Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough
3 tablespoons margarine
1/3 cup sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Cut each biscuit in quarters
Melt butter, sugar and cinnamon in small skillet.
Toss in biscuit quarters and turn/coat each piece with melted mixture.
Cook over skillet about 5 minutes or until biscuits are cooked through.  Serve warm.
Optional:  This recipe can also be used by changing the ingredients (butter, garlic, rosemary) and you can enjoy it for dinner with spaghetti.

Submitter: 
LiveToBless
Description: 
My Mother's classic monkey bread, but prepared in more than half the time and without having to heat the kitchen from the oven.
