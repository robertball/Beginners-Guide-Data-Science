Title:
Fingerpaint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T sugar
1/2 c cornstarch
2 c water
Liquid dish soap
Muffin tin
Freezer paper
Food coloring

Directions:
Mix sugar, cornstarch and water in a medium saucepan and cook over low heat until mixture thickens.  Do not overcook.  Pour mixture into muffin tins.  Add 1-2 drops food coloring and a drop of dish soap into each tin.  Stir and let cool.  Paint on the shiny side of the freezer paper.  Store in an airtight container.
