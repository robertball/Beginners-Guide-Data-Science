Title:
Red Wine Flavored Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/497681.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup red wine
1 cup water
4 pounds beef roast
1/2 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix

Directions:
Place the red wine, water, and beef roast in a slow cooker. Season with dry onion soup mix.
Cover, and cook 5 hours on Low.
