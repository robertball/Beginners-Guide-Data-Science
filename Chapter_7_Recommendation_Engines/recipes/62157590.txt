Title:
Bodacious 4 Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can green string beans (drained well)
1 Can yellow wax beans (drained well)
1 Can red kidney beans (drained well)
1 Can garbanzo beans (draned well)
1 green pepper, sliced
1 large sweet or Bermuda onion, sliced and ringed
Marinate:
1/2 cup oil
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup vinegar
1/4 tsp coarse grind pepper
1/4 cup sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and marinate at least 8 hours or overnight in refrigerator.
Mix with wooden spoon to prevent breaking beans.

Submitter: 
Muffin Mom N Garlic Girl
Description: 
This recipes was passed down to me from my mother in law. Its the only bean salad recipe I've ever tried and actually liked and makes the perfect side to any back yard barbecue.
