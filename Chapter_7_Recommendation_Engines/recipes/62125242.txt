Title:
One Pot Hamburger Deluxe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean hamburger
1/4 onion, chopped
2 tsp prepared minced garlic
2-10 oz cans Campbell's Vegetable Soup
1-14 oz can Heinz beans in tomato sauce
1-10 oz can mushroom pieces & stems
1/2 10 oz soup can of water
1 cup Old El Paso Thick'n'Chunky Medium salsa
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tsp oregano
1/2-1 14 oz can Green Giant Sweetlet Peas

Directions:
Fry hamburger, drain.
Add onion & garlic & fry until onion clear.
Then add remaining ingrediants except peas.
Simmer 15-20 mins.
Stir in peas & heat through.
Serve plain or over rice, noodles or mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Kerri
Description: 
This is an original meal by my husband and I.  Time crunched, thawed hamburger, and this is what we got!  - a delicious, super fast, meal to serve as is, or over rice, noodles or mashed potatoes.  Our kids (3&6) love it!  Great as leftovers!
