Title:
Italian Chickpea Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2220382.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chickpea flour
1 cup water
2 T oil
1 1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1 1/2 tsp. chopped dried rosemary
ground pepper
salt (opt.)

Directions:
Combine chickpea flour and water, stir with whisk until smooth.  Let sit at room temperature 2-6 hours.
Heat oven to 450. Spray metal 8" pie pan.
Skim foam from top of chickpea-water mixture. Add oil, Italian Seasoning and rosemary, stir until combined. Pour into sprayed pie pan.
Sprinkle liberally with ground pepper.  If desired, sprinkle salt on lightly.
Bake at 450 for 15 minutes.  This is done when edges have begun to brown. Remove from pan, cut into wedges.
Best when served warm!

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
A bread like polenta, but without the corn!  This is gluten free bread with herb flavors, based on my take of Lituria, Italy recipes.
