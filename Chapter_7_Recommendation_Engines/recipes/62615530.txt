Title:
Honey Mustard Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
3/4 cup honey
1/4 cup spicy mustard
1/4 cup yellow mustard
4 pieces of deli ham
3/4 cup reduced fat shredded mozzerella cheese

Directions:
1. Cut a long slit in each breast and open up flat
2. Place one piece ham in each and sprinkle each with cheese
3. Close and hold together with toothpicks
4. Mix together honey and mustard
5. Place breasts in Pam sprayed small casserole dish
6. Pour honey mustard over breasts and cover dish with foil.
7. Bake in preheated 375 degree oven for 40 min or until juices run clear and no pink.

Submitter: 
Gillian Ross
Description: 
Delicious and super easy! Great with a baked or sweet potato and broccoli! 250 calories per serving!
