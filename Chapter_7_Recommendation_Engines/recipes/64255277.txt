Title:
Gingerbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups Flour
2 tbsp ground ginger
1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1 tsp Ground Cloves
1/2 tsp Salt
1 cup Granulated Sugar
1 cup Shortening
1 Egg
1 cup Molasses
6 tbsp Butter
4 1/2 cups Confectionery Sugar
1/4 cup Whipping Cream
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
DOUGH
In mixing bowl add 1 cup GRANULATED SUGAR & 1 cup SHORTNING. Mix on med. until light and fluffy. Beat in 1 EGG, 1 cup MOLLASSIS & 2 tbsp VINEGAR. Reduce the speed to stir & add the flour mixture in 3 additions. Divide the dough into 3 & wrap in plastic wrap. Refrigerate the dough for at least one hour.
FROSTING
Place 6 tbsp of BUTTER in the mixing bowl. Beat until smooth & creamy on low/med. Reduce speed and mix in 4 Â½ cups CONFECTIONARY SUGAR in 2 additions. You can add WHIPPING CREAM to make a smooth frosting. Add 1 tsp of VANILLA. Set aside.
FLOUR the work surface & rolling pin. Roll out to 1/8" thickness. Use cookie cutter to make gingerbread boys and girls. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
