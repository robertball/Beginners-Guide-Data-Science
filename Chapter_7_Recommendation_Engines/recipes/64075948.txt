Title:
De's Macaroni Salad - My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Macaroni
2 cups Ranch Dressing
8 oz jar of dill pickles
8 oz jar of sweet pickles
1 lg cucumber
1 cup purple onion
1 cup green pepper
1 16 oz jumbo black olives
1 6 oz jar of green olives (Spanish)
8 oz Mozzarella
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to the box directions.
Cut pickles, cucumber, onion, green pepper, olives and cheese into bite size pieces.
Combine cooled macaroni and all other ingredients in large bowl and pour in the ranch dressing to coat.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Chill until served.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
A summer salad my sister-in-law would make every year.
