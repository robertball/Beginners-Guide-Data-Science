Title:
Chicken Parmigiana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast pound to 1-inch thick
1 egg white, lightly beaten
1 tsp dried breadcrumbs
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp olive oil
Â½ cup canned tomato sauce
2 Tbsp part skim shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350F.
Coat a small oven proof dish with cooking spray. Brush chicken on both sides with egg white. Mix breadcrumbs with the seasoning on plate; dip chicken in breadcrumbs.
Turn to coat. Heat oil in a large skillet to oven proof dish. Place chicken on top. Pour remaining sauce over chicken. Sprinkle with cheese.
Bake until bubbling about 15 minutes.
