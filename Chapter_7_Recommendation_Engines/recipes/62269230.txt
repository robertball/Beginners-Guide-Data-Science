Title:
Southwestern Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions, chopped
3 pounds ground turkey
4 cups tomato sauce
8 Tablespoons Taco Seasoning
3 cups salsa
2 cans black beans
1 cup frozen corn
Lasagna noodles
Mexican blended cheese

Directions:
On medium heat, brown onion and ground turkey until cooked thoroughly. Drain liquid. Add tomato sauce, seasoning, and salsa. Return to boil, simmer for 15-30 minutes. Add beans and corn.
In an 8x8 pan, pour a thin layer of meat and sauce mixture on the bottom of the pan. Break noodles as needed to layer, side by side, on top of sauce mixture. Add another generous layer of sauce on top of the noodles. Follow with 1/2 of the cheese, noodles, remaining sauce. End with cheese as the top layer. Cover tightly with aluminum foil. If still warm when baking, bake for 45 minutes. If refrigerated prior to baking, bake for 60-70 minutes. Remove aluminum foil for last 10 minutes of cooking. Allow to sit for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
This is a nice twist on lasagna. This has become a favorite in our family, and one that freezes well. No need to boil the noodles!
