Title:
Peanut Stir Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066745.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1/4 cup soy sauce
4 tsp. granulated sugar (less if not using natural peanut butter)
4 tsp. natural peanut butter
2 Tbsp. water
2 teaspoons Asian chili garlic paste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and heat in microwave for 20-30 seconds.

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
Its simple and is the best peanut sauce I have come across so far.  My family loves it.
