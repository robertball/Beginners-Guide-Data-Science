Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sweet potatoes (boiled and mashed)
2 eggs
1/3 cup margarine
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup Karo Syrup
1 T vanilla
1/2 cup sweet milk
TOPPING
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup plain flour
1/3 cup unmelted margarine
1 cup finely chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix first 7 ingredients thoroughly and pour into casserole dish that has been sprayed with Pam.  Mix topping ingredients with a fork or hands to form a crumble.  Sprinkle over potatoes and bake 30 minutes in 350 degree oven.
