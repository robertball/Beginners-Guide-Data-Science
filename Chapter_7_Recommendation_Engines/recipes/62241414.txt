Title:
Goat cheese quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. goat cheese
4 oz Jack cheese
1/4 c. chopped cilantro
1 T. chili powder
1 can (15 oz) red beans
chopped veggies
8 flour tortillas
Green chile Salsa:
1 1/3 c. diced seeded tomato
1/4 c. Sweet onion
2 tsp. fresh lime juice
1 tsp. minced cilantro
1/4 tsp. pepper
5 oz. chopped green chiles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine first 5 ingredients; spread about 3/4 c. cheese mixture onto each of 4 tortillas, leaving a 1/2 inch border.  Add green peppers, zucchini, whatever. Top each with a tortilla, pressing gently.  Place on baking sheet coated with cooking spray; cover with foil.
Bake for 25 minutes; Serve with salsa.
