Title:
Rice Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dipping Sauce
3/4 cup water
1/3 cup seasoned rice vinegar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tbsp grated ginger root
2 tsp corn starch
1 tsp minced garlic
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
Rice rolls
2 oz (57 gm) rice vermicelli noodles, uncooked
1 cup grated carrots
1 cup chopped bean sprouts
1/2 cup peeled, seeded and finely chopped cucumber
1/2 cup chopped green onions
2 tsp sesame oil
14 6 inch rice wraps
14 whole fresh mint leafs

Directions:
To make dipping sauce, whisk together all sauce ingredients a small sauce pander medium high heat until it comes to a boil and thickens, remove from heat and let cool to room temperature.
Boil vermicelli in a large pot water for 4 minutes or until tender.
Drain and rinse with cold water and drain again, blot dry with paper towels.
Coarsely chop noodles and place in a medium bowl.
Add carrots, bean sprouts, cucumber and green onions, mix well.
Add sesame oil and mix again.
Fill a pie plate with very hot water.
working one at a time, soak rice papers in hot water for about 30 seconds or until soft and pliable.
Transfer to a clean towel, lay wrapper flat and blot dry.
Place a mint leaf in the centre of the wrapper and top with 1/4 cup of filling.
With fingers, place filling in a 3" horizontal cylinder.
Fold bottom edge over filling and roll once to enclose, fold in sides and wrap tightly.
