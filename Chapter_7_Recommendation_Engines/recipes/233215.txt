Title:
KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS Verde Chicken Enchiladas  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025668.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.2 ounce) package KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS Verde Chicken Enchiladas
4 cups shredded cooked chicken
12 (6 inch) corn tortillas
1 cup KRAFT Mexican Style Shredded Four Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 13 x 9-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Combine chicken and Filling Sauce.
Stack tortillas; wrap in damp paper towel.  Microwave on HIGH 30 sec. to 1 min. or until warmed.  Pour half the Finishing Sauce into prepared baking dish.
Spoon 1/4 cup chicken mixture down center of each tortilla; roll up. Place, seam-sides down, in prepared baking dish.
Cover with remaining Finishing Sauce and cheese. Bake 15 to 20 min. or until heated through.

Submitter: 
KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS
Description: 
These enchiladas are filled with shredded chicken in a Creamy Ranchero Filling Sauce and baked with Tomatillo Finishing Sauce and lots of shredded cheese for a quick and tasty weeknight meal.
