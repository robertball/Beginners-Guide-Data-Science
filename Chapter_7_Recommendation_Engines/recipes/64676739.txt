Title:
Corn-beef White Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4703090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup shredded cornbeef
1 1/2 quarts of cornbeef cooking liquid strained
3/4 cup leftover white potatoes sliced
1/2 cup leftover carrots sliced
1/4 cup leftover celery diced
15.5 ounce can white beans
1 tablespoon celery seed
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon yellow mustard

Directions:
Mix everything together and reheat before serving.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
Whenever I cook a corn-beef and cabbage simmered with potatoes, carrots, and celery...I like to make a soup with leftovers. Instead of cabbage in soup I use white beans. The cooking liquid from the leftovers is ideal for the soup broth.
