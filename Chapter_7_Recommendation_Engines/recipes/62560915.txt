Title:
Easy Pumpkin Spice Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536324.jpg
Ingredients:
1 18oz box spice cake mix
15 oz can pumpkin
1 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to directions on the box
Mix all ingredients together in a medium sized bowl
Line 24 mini muffin tins with cupcake wrappers or spray the pan with nonstick cooking spray
Divide the mix evenly to make 24 muffins
Bake 20-25 minutes (or longer depending on your oven) until complete
Let cool and Enjoy!

Submitter: 
SlimCookins
Description: 
Spiced cake mix and pumpkin puree make these delicious and healthy mini muffins.
