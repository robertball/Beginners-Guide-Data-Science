Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
1 egg beaten
1 onion, small diced, sauteed until soft and translucent
1 small green pepper diced and sauteed with onion until soft
1 tbsp. worschestshire sauce
3/4 C breadcrumb
1/4 C Ketchup
1 tbsp. Mustard
salt and pepper
1 can hunts tomato sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare onion and green pepper.  Combine all ingredients except tomato sauce.  Form into loaf on foil lined cookie sheet.  Top with tomato sauce.  Bake at 350 for approximately 40-45 minutes.
