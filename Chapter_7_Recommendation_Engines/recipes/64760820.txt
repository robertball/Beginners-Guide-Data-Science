Title:
Beet Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 - 6 cups beets with 1" tops intact (~ 3 lbs)
water to cover
Brine:
1 cup vinegar
1 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Cook beets in water in large pot until tender. Cool in cooking water until you can put your hands in comfortably. Slide off beet skins. Leave tiny beets whole. Cut larger beets into chunks. Pack into hot sterile jars to within 1" of top.
Brine: while beets are cooling, mix brine ingred. Heat on med. & stir often until it reaches boil. Pour over beets in jars to within 1/4" of top. Seal
Makes 3 pints

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
No spices added. Great flavor. Won a local fair with these!
