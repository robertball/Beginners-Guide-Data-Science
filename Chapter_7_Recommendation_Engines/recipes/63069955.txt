Title:
Chicken Pot Pie My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen peas & carrots (mixed veggies)
1 cup frozen green beans
1/2 cup celery
1/3 cup butter
1/3 cup onion
1/3 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp celery seed
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp italian seasoning
3/4 cup  chicken broth
2/3 cup non-dairy milk
2 cups chicken--cooked & diced
3/4 sheet Puff Pastry

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Place peas/carrots/green beans & celery into a saucepan; cover w/water, bring to boil, & simmer over med-low heat until celery is tender, about 8 min. Drain & set aside
Melt butter in saucepan over med heat, cook onion until translucent. Stir in flour, salt, pepper, celery seed, onion powder & italian seasoning. slowly whisk in chick broth & milk until mixture comes to a simmer & thickens. Remove from heat; stir in veggies & chicken until well mixed.
Place mixture in 9" sq pan. roll out pastry dough & cut to fit pan. Brush with egg wash (egg + 1 Tbl water). cut several slits on top
Bake 25 min until golden brown
