Title:
Endless Energy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1122539.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1 cup fresh spinach
1 kiwi, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup chopped pineapple
1/2 cup ice

Directions:
Blend almond milk, kiwi, spinach, pineapple, and ice in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
biabia00
Description: 
This is a great recipe to wake you up in the morning. The protein, iron, and fiber will keep you alert until lunch time.
