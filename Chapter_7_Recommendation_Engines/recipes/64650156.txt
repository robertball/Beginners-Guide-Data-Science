Ingredients:
1 (3 to 4 pound) Roast Beef
1/2 cup Water
1/2 cup firmly packed Brown Sugar
1 envelope Onion Soup Mix
1 Onion, chopped
1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder, or to taste
Salt and Pepper to taste
8 Onion Hamburger Buns (or regular Hamburger Buns if you prefer)

Directions:
1. Place roast in crock pot.
2. Stir remaining ingredients (except the buns, of course) together and pour over roast.
3. Cook for 8-10 hours stirring occasionally.
4. Serve on buns.
