Title:
Candied Yams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lb 12oz sweet potatoes
1 cup butter
3 cups brown sugar
1/8 cup water

Directions:
Peel sweet potatoes, cut lengthwise then slice into 1/8" half moon pieces.
In a square electric skillet (12x12") melt 1/4 butter on the bottom of skillet at 250 degrees.
Layer 1lb 4oz of cut sweet potatoes on top of the yams. Cut 1/4 of butter over the yams and sprinkle with 1 cup brown sugar.
Repeat process up to three layers.
Add 1/8 cup of water, put the lid on and cook at 250 for 45 minutes, turning yams every 15 minutes to coat with sauce and evenly cook.
If sauce is not thick after 45 minutes, turn heat up to 325 to caramelize. Should take 7-15 minutes.
