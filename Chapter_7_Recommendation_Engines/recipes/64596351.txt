Title:
Corn cake muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3872213.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Jiffy corn muffin mix
2 eggs
1/4 cup heavy cream
1 can creamed corn
1 stick of salted butter melted
1/2 tsp onion salt
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Preheat convection oven to 350
Lightly mix all ingredients in a large bowl
Fill muffin tins 3/4 full
Optional sprinkle sugar on top prior to baking
Bake 22 min or until done
Try mixing 2 boxes Jiffy for lighter, less dense muffins.

Submitter: 
Angie Brindley
Description: 
Corn casserole in muffin form.  Soft, dense and delicious!
