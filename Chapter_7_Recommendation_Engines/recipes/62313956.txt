Title:
Pull-Apart Caramel Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tubes (12 ounces each) refrigerated flaky buttermilk biscuits
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Cut each biscuit into four pieces; arrange evenly in a 10-inch fluted tube pan coated with cooking spray.  Combine the brown sugar, cream and cinnamon; pour over the biscuits.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool for 5 minutes before inverting onto a serving platteer.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
Refrigerated bread dough and a caramel sauce makes a great breakfast or brunch addition
