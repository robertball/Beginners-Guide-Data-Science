Title:
Cream Cheese Chocolate Hazelnut Square Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/935455.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Philadelphia Cream Cheese
1 cup white sugar
1 cup old fashion brown sugar (packed)
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
½ tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
½ cup Hazelnut (chopped and toasted)
½ cup dark chocolate chips

Directions:
Pre-heat over at 350f.
Cream cream cheese, sugars, add eggs and vanilla whip until fluffy.
Mix dry ingredients, add to cream cheese mix well. (Reserve Hazelnuts and chocolate for centre).
Line 8x11 pan with parchment paper, pour dough I inch thick add Hazelnuts and chocolates, pour balance of dough.
Bake for 30 minutes.
