Title:
Big Ray's Easy Cake Mix Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4628121.jpg
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix (regular size)
1 large egg
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup canola oil
1 cup white baking chips
1/3 cup sprinkles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large bowl, combine cake mix, egg, milk and oil (mixture will be thick). Stir in baking chips and jimmies. Spread into a greased 15x10x1-in. baking pan.
Bake 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely in pan on a wire rack. Cut into bars.
Freeze option: Freeze bars in freezer containers. To use, thaw in covered containers before serving.
