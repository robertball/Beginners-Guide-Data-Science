Title:
Grilled chicken with herbed corn salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 lb. skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
salt
pepper
3 large ears corn, shucked
1 c. packed fresh basil leaves
1/2 c. packed fresh mint leaves
1/4 c. grated Parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium.
While grill is preheating, in food processor, pulse basil, mint, Parmesan, garlic, and 1/4 teaspoon salt until finely chopped. Add lemon juice and olive oil; pulse until just combined. Put in medium bowl and set aside.
Season chicken thighs with 1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper.
Grill corn on medium 10 minutes, turning occasionally. When corn is done, take it off the grill, grease grill and add chicken, skin side down.
While chicken is cooking, cut kernels off cobs; stir into herb sauce.
Grill chicken on medium, covered, 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is cooked (165 degrees F), turning over once.
Serve chicken with corn mixture.

A yummy hot night supper.  Keep the kitchen cool and serve with thick slices of fresh-from-the-garden tomatoes.
