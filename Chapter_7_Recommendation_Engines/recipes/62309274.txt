Title:
Moms Chilled Melon Buttermilk Soup Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups orange juice
1/3 cup Saco buttermilk blend
1 medium cantaloupe, peeled, seeded, and cubed
2 T. honey
¼ t. salt
¼ t nutmeg
½ t. vanilla extract
1/8 t. cayenne pepper (optional)
6 mint sprigs for garnish

Directions:
Pour orange juice into blender.
Add buttermilk blend and blend untill powder is dissolved.
Add cantaloupe, cover and process until pureed.
Add the honey, salt, nutmeg, vanilla, and cayenne pepper.
Cover and process till smooth.
Chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
Pour into six bowls and garnish each with a mint sprig.

Submitter:

Description: 
This delicious soup won my mom a blue ribbon (and $150) at the WI state fair, for a SACO buttermilk powder contest!
