Title:
Layered Mousse Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988257.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups boiling water
2 (3 ounce) packages JELL-O Strawberry Flavor Gelatin
2 cups thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping

Directions:
Stir boiling water into gelatin mixes with whisk 2 min. until completely dissolved. Add COOL WHIP; stir until blended.
Pour into 8-inch square pan.
Refrigerate 3 hours or until firm. Cut into 36 pieces.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
These light, fun-to-eat fruity treats make any get-together festive.
