Title:
Apple and cinnamon pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 smashed apples (or four tablespoons of your favourite apple jam or marmalade).
2 tablespoons of cinnamon powder.
1 pint (1/2 litre) of yoghurt without flavour.
1/4 gill (30 ml) of gelatine dissolved in hot water.

Directions:
Put the smashed apples and the yoghurt in a bowl to beat them.
Mix in one tablespoon of cinnamon and the gelatine.
Blend until it takes a creamy look.
Pour into the Graham crust (or make your own crust if you like).
Sprinkle the rest of the cinnamon to decorate and leave it in the fridge for at least an hour and a half.

Description: 
A refreshing pay with a really delicious taste. Although apple with cinnamon it`s quite simple, my family loves it.
