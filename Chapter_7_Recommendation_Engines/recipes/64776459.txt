Title:
Green Detox Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7641093.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
5 oz celery, cleaned and chopped
4 oz leek, cleaned and sliced into rounds
5 oz fennel, washed and diced
2 garlic cloves, smashed
6 oz fresh broccoli florets
10 oz zucchini, unpeeled and cubed
6 leaves of tuscan kale, ribs removed and cut into small pieces
10 ounces raw spinach
1/2 oz fresh chopped Italian parsley, leaves only
4 cups low sodium vegetable broth
2 cups hot water, divided
1 avocado, diced
1 tsp spirulina powder
10 tbsp gomasio (toasted, crushed sesame seeds)

Directions:
Heat the oil in a Dutch oven. Saut√© the celery, leek, fennel, and garlic cloves until soft and fragrant. Add the broccoli, zucchini, kale, spinach, and parsley. Mix to combine until kale and spinach have wilted slightly . Add 4 cups low sodium vegetable broth and stir. Bring to a boil then simmer, covered, until vegetables are soft. About 15 minutes.
Turn off heat. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth. Add hot water if needed to achieve the desired consistency. Add avocado and spirulina. Blend again.
Serve with one tbsp gomasio per serving.

Description: 
Full of iron, potassium, vitamin K, folic acid, and many other nutrients, this cleansing soup full of green veggies is as delicious as it is healthy.
