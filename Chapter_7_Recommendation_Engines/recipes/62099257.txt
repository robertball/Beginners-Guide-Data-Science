Title:
Pork Chop Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 center cut pork chops
6 med. yukon gold potatoes, peeled and quartered
2 med. onions peeled and quartered
1lb bag of baby carrots
1 can beef broth
1 pkg dry onion soup mix
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place chops in bottom of roasting pan and add onions, carrots and potatoes around them. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and soup mix. Pour broth over all. Cover and bake 1 hour at 350 deg. Put under broiler last 5 min. for crunchier chops.

Submitter: 
lisascooking
Description: 
My mom always made this in the fall months when the weather turned colder, my family loves it. Very easy Sunday dinner.
