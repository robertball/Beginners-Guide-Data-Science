Title:
Walnut Pie Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup light corn syrup
3 Tbsp. Butter
1 large egg
3/4 cups chopped walnuts
1 tsp. vanilla
Pinch of salt
1- 9 inch ready made pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425*.
In medium saucepan, melt butter over low heat.
Mix in eggs, vanilla, sugar and corn syrup.
Add in salt and walnuts.
Bring to boil, careful not to cook the eggs.
Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened.
On lightly floured surface, roll out pie crust.
Cut out 3-inch circles with circle cutter.
Fill each circle with approximately 1 tsp. of filling.
Fold the crust over and crimp edges with a fork.
Arrange on cookie sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
Cool on wire rack.
