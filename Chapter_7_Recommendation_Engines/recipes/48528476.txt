Title:
APPLESAUCE CINNAMON SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup RED HOTS candy
1 cup water
1 large pkg. cherry jello
2 cups thick applesauce

Directions:
Combine the candies and water in a sauce pan and bring to a boil over medium heat until candies are dissolved. Remove from heat; gradually add the Jello; mix well. Cool slightly. Blend in the applesauce. Pour into a mold or a bowl and chill overnight

Submitter: 
FatManWhoCooks
Description: 
This is a holiday tradition at our house.  We have tried it with other flavors of Jello and it just isn't right. Use the Cherry!
