Title:
Mackerel Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound smoked mackerel
4 ounces cream cheese
1/2 cup softened butter
1 lemon, juiced

Directions:
Remove any skin and bones from the mackerel, and place into the bowl of a food processor. Pulse until no large pieces remain.  Add the cream cheese, butter, and lemon juice; process until smooth.  Place into a small serving bowl, and refrigerate until firmed, about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Catrin
Description: 
Northern Germany is big on fish! This is a delicious spread that goes well on toasted bread as a starter, and it's so easy!
