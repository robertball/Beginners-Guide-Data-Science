Title:
Antipasto Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Archer Farms® balsamic vinaigrette dressing
16 (1 inch) cubes Archer Farms Asiago cheese
16 (1 inch) cubes Archer Farms hard salami cubes
16 grape tomatoes
16 green olives
4 whole large pepperoncini, halved

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in medium bowl.  Cover and refrigerate 15 minutes to marinate.
Alternately thread antipasti onto eight 8-inch wooden skewers.  Discard remaining salad dressing.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Here’s antipasto on a stick, appropriate for a fair or casual fare at your next cocktail party. The piquant marinated skewers are easy to assemble and fun to eat - must have plenty of napkins on hand.
