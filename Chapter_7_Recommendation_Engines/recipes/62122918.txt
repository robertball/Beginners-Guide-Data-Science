Title:
Erik’s Cheesy Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, divided into small florets
8 strips turkey bacon
1 pkg (8oz) fat free cream cheese
1/4 cup lowfat cottage cheese
½ cup light sour cream
1 ½ cups 2% mild cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
1.	Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2.	On stove, boil cauliflower florets until soft, about 10-12 minutes
3.	Microwave bacon until crisp, following cooking directions on pkg. After bacon has cooled, crumble into small pieces.
4.	Drain cauliflower, transfer to mixing bowl. Combine cauliflower, bacon pieces, cream cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream and ½ cup cheddar cheese.
5.	Using hand mixer, mix until mixture is similar consistency to mashed potatoes.
6.	Transfer to 9x13 baking dish, add remainder of cheddar cheese on top. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes (until cheese is melted on top).

Submitter: 
Leah
Description: 
A delicious low-carb comfort food! Mashed cauliflower combined with low-fat ingredients make you feel as though you are splurging on this mouth-watering side dish.
