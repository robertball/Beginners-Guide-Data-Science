Title:
Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb spaghetti
1 lb ground beef
1 med onion, minced
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
2 large cans stewed tomatoes
10 oz Velveeta

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Boil spaghetti according to pkg directions
Brown ground beef
Add minced onion, pepper, salt and pepper flakes
Puree tomatoes and Velveeta in blender
Mix ground beef, tomato/cheese mixture and spaghetti
Pour into 9 x 13 baking dish
Bake 30 minutes

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
This great spaghetti dinner freezes well for enjoying later.
