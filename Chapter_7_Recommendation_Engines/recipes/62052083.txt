Title:
Bekkala Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. quick oatmeal
1 T dried minced onion
1 T parsley
1½ t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/4 t garlic powder
dash of nutmeg
2 eggs
½ cup milk
2# ground beef

Directions:
Mix first 9 ingredients together,then add to 2# lean ground beef.
Scoop out with smallest scoop (scant 1 T). Roll into balls. Place on greased baking pan (may be touching each other), bake for 15-20 min. or until cooked through, at 375°. Remove from pan and put in roaster with gravy*. Cover and bake until gravy is bubbly (about 40 min. at 350°.
*Gravy:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 T. beef base
1 can water
Bring to boil, pour over meatballs.
Note: 10# beef = 21 doz. meatballs

Submitter: 
Andytofu
Description: 
Our favorite family recipe created by my sister Bea Bekkala for small meatballs in wonderful gravy. Ingredients can be adjusted to serve large number (used traditionally for many years in our church for wedding reception meals and/or other church functions).
