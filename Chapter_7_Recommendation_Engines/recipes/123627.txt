Title:
Potatoes Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987463.jpg
Ingredients:
8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 cup sour cream
3 green onions, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place potatoes in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil; cover and cook until almost tender. Drain and cool.
In a large bowl, combine soup, 1-1/2 cups cheese, sour cream, onions, salt and pepper; stir in potatoes.
Place in a greased 13-in. x 9-in.x 2-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Mrs.  Afton Johnson
Description: 
Hailing from the state known for its potatoes, I thought I'd send in this recipe. Every time my grandson comes home from college, he asks me to make it. In fact, it's the whole family's favorite potato dish.
