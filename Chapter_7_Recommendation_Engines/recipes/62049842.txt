Title:
Bubba Gump's Shrimoin Dippin Broth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. shrim in the shell
1/2 c butter
4 T finely chopped onions, green pepper and celery
1/2 t cayenne
1/2 t oregano and thyme
1/2 c clam juice
crusty bread for dipping

Directions:
In a large pot melt butter and saute onions, pepper and celery about 5 minutes, add cayenne, oregano and thyme and saute 20 seconds, add shrimp and clam juice and simmer until shrimp turn pink, serve in the pot with the bread. Seasoning maybe ajusted for your taste.
