Title:
Salad Dressing Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons honey (adjust for sweetness)
1&1/2 tablespoons white vinegar (rice wine can be used in a pinch)
4 teaspoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon Grey Poupon Dijon mustard
1/8 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions:
1. Pour ingredients into bowl.
2. Beat with electric mixer.
3. Chill in refrigerator until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
N. Adams
Description: 
This tastes great on salad.... the honey sweetens it beyond belief. I received it from a cook who used to work at Applebee's - it is near to the same thing they use on their Oriental Chicken Salaed
