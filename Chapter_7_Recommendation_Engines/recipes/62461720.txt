Title:
French's Crunchy Onion Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (6 oz) French's Original French Fried Onions (or cheddar flavored)
2 Tbsp. flour
5 chicken breasts
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Crush onions with flour in a plastic bag.
Dip chicken in egg. Coat in onion crumbs. Press firmly to adhere.
Bake at 400 F for 20 min (or until cooked).
