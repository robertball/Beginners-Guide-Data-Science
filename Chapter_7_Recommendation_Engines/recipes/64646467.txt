Title:
Farfalla Salad with Arugula and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4528730.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound box, farfalla pasta
2 small zucchini, sliced
1 bag arugula
1/4 cup olive oil, divided
1 1/2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
2 clove garlic, minced
1/2 shallot, minced
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
4 ounces cherry tomatoes, halved
2 breasts chicken, cooked and chopped (great use for leftovers or use a rotisserie)

Directions:
Take a large pot of water and bring to a boil.  Add a tablespoon or salt then the pasta.  Cook based on directions.  Take out 1 cup of the water prior to draining. Put pasta in a bowl
While you are cooking the pasta.  Add 1 tablespoon to a large saut√© pan over medium heat and cook zucchini for about 8-10 minutes.  Until it is fork tender and starts to brown. Add to the pasta bowl. Add arugula, tomatoes, and chicken.
In a small bowl add the remaining olive oil, white wine vinegar, garlic, shallot, salt, and pepper then whisk together. Pour over pasta salad and toss together. Enjoy!
