Title:
Low calorie chocolate frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup chocolate pudding mix
1/4 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
combine vanilla, pudding mix, and  whipping cream. Mix with an electric mixeruntil mixture forms soft peaks. serve room tempurature.
