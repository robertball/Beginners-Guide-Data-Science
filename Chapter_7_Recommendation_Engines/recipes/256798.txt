Title:
Arkansas Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4462894.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package processed cheese product (such as Velveeta®), cubed
1 cup sour cream
1 (4.5 ounce) can chopped green chiles, undrained
1 (1.12 ounce) package McCormick® Cheesy Taco Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large microwaveable bowl.
Microwave on HIGH 2 minutes. Stir. Microwave 1 minute longer or until heated through. Stir until smooth.
Serve hot with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
McCormick Spice
Description: 
Four ingredients and eight minutes are all you need to whip this dip together. Mix sour cream, green chiles and cubed cheese with McCormick® Cheesy Taco Seasoning Mix. Bake until ooey-gooey and get to dipping with your favorite tortilla chips.
