Title:
Pizza Wheel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5094453.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (13.5 ounce) package Johnsonville® Fully Cooked Three-Cheese Italian Style Sausage Rope
1 (11 ounce) can Pillsbury™ French bread dough
3 slices provolone cheese, or more to taste
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly grease a sheet pan.
Remove dough from can, cut in half, and join pieces so long sides are together. Roll the dough out into a square.
Place provolone on top of dough. Dice sausage and sprinkle over dough. Top with shredded mozzarella. Roll up and pinch seam to seal. Slice into 8 pieces.
Bake until golden brown, about 10 to 15 minutes. Serve with marinara dipping sauce.

Submitter: 
From the Kitchen at Johnsonville Sausage
Description: 
When the wheel was invented, humanity rejoiced. Today, Johnsonville is making food lovers rejoice with the pizza wheel - a delicious combination of mozzarella and provolone cheese, French bread and sausage. Serve with marinara sauce.
