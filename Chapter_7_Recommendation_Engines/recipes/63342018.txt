Title:
CHOCOLATE CAKE WITH CREAM FILLING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BOIL 1 CUP WATER, ADD 2 TSPS BAKING SODA & SET ASIDE
2/3 CUPS CRISCO
2 CUPS SUGAR
2 EGGS
2 TSPS VANILLA
2 1/2 CUPS FLOUR
1/2 CUP COCOA
DASH OF SALT
1 CUP OF BUTTERMILK OR SOURMILK
PUT FLOUR & COCOA IN BOWL TOGETHER AND SET ASIDE
CREAM CRISCO, SUGAR & EGGS 1 AT A TIME, ADD VANILLA
ALTERNATE FLOUR MIXTURE & MILK, BEGINNING AND ENDING WITH FLOUR
THEN ADD WATER W/BAKING SODA
GREASE 2 ROUND CAKES PAINS AND BAKE AT 350 FOR 1 HOUR
CREAM FILLING
1/2 CUP CRISCO
1 STICK OLEO (ROOM TEMPATURE)
1 CUP SUGAR
1 TSP VANILLA
BOIL 1 CUP OF MILK & MEASURE 3/4 CUP & COOL
MIX ALL ABOVE TOGETHER THEN ADD COOLED MILK A LITTLE AT A TIME
