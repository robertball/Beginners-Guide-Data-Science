Title:
Sour Cream Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Italian salad dressing
7 med. red potatos
3/4 cup sliced celery
1/3 cup sliced green onion
4 hard boiled eggs
1 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
1 1/2 tsp. prepared horseradish mustard

Directions:
Cook potatos in jackets, peel if (desired) and cube. Pour Italian dressing over warm potatoes; chill 2 hours. Add celery and onion. Chop egg whites; add. Sieve yolks mix with mayo, sour cream and mustard. Fold into salad. Add salt and celery seed to taste. Chill 2 hours

Submitter: 
Victor Chilton
Description: 
This is Moms recipe, when ever I have made this people raved that it is the best they have had! and I agree!
