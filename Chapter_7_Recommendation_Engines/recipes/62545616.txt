Title:
Pasta Con Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. box large shell pasta
2 sticks butter
3 10.5 oz. cans cream of mushroom soup
2 14 oz. bags frozen broccoli florets
6 cloves of garlic, pressed
1 c. milk
16 oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced

Directions:
Fill a large dutch oven with water, bring to a boil.  Add pasta.
While pasta is cooking, melt butter in a large (12") skillet.  Add garlic and saute 1 min.  Add mushrooms and saute 10 min.  Add cream of mushroom soup and milk, whisk until combined.  Keep warm over very low heat.
When pasta is al dente, add both bags of broccoli.  Return to a simmer.  Pour pasta & broccoli through a collander to strain.  Return pasta & broccoli to dutch oven.  Add mushroom mixture and mix well.  Serve immediately.
