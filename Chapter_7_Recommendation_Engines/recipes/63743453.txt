Title:
SWEET MARIE BALLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1//2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter  (crunchy)
1/2 cup corn syrup
2 1/1 cups rice kris pies
1 cup melted chocolate

Directions:
Melt peanut butter, syrup, brown sugar together
Add rice kris pies
Mix well to be sure whole mixture is well coated
Form 1 inch balls and set on waxed paper
Melt chocolate and coat balls in chocolate
