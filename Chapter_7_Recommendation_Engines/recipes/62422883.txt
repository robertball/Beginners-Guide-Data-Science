Title:
Spring Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Spring Roll Wrappers
Mung Bean Noodle Thread
1 lb Ground Pork
1 head cabbage
1 lg carrot
1 onion
1 1/2 T Salt
4 T Sugar
2 squirts Light Soy Sauce
2 eggs
1 egg white, save yolk to put on edges to seal rolls

Directions:
Soak 1/4 pkg of mung bean noodles in water
Shred cabbage finely, chop and dice onion, grate carrot. Mix these together in a bowl. Drain mung bean noodles and chop small. Add to mixture, along with salt, sugar, soy sauce and eggs. Mix together well with hands. Fry a little of the mixture to make sure it's seasoned correctly. Put 1 T mixture in middle of Spring roll wrapper and follow directions to fold. Put a little of the yolk on the last edge and seal. Fry in vegetable oil until golden brown. Serve with sweet chili sauce,
