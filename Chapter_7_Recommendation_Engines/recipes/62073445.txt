Title:
Any Fruit Shortbread bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
1 1/2 cup butter
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp. vanilla or almond extract
1 2/3 cup brown sugar
18 oz. fruit preserves (I like raspberry the best!)
3/4 cup powdered sugar
1 Tbsp. milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a medium bowl, combine 2 1/2 cups flour, 2/3 cups brown sugar, and 1 cup butter.  Pressinto  ungreased 9x13 pan.  Bake for 15 minutes, or until slightly golden.  Cool slightly, spread with preserves.  In same bowl, combine remaining 1 1/2 cups flour, 1/2 cup butter, and 1 cup brown sugar, salt, and extract.  Mix until crumbly.  Srinkle over jam.  Bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.  Combine powdered sugar and milk, drizzle over completely cooled bars.

Submitter: 
Ali
Description: 
You can make these bars with any fruit filling, preserves, or jam.  I put different extracts in the dough, depending on the kind or fruit I use.  I like to use almond for raspeberry filling, vanilla for strawberry filling, etc.
