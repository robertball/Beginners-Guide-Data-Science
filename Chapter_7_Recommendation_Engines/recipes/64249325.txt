Title:
Sandwich Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 packs of flavored flour tortillas or sandwich wraps
3 8oz carton soft flavored cream cheeze
3 heads Leaf Lettuce
1 lb deli turkey
1 lb deli ham
1 lb deli roast beaf

Directions:
spread tortilla with cream cheese
layer with left lettuce
layer with deli meat
roll up tightly and wrap in plastic wrap.  Refrigerate 8 or overnight.

Submitter: 
Randy Warner
Description: 
Easy to prepare can be made 1 day in advance. Each wrap makes approx. 6-8 appetizers.
