Title:
thick irish stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 forequarter lamb chops (trimmed)
4 carrots (preferrably organic)
4 onions (preferrably organic)
6 potatoes (preferably organic)
1 litre vegetable stock
500 g split yellow peas
3 bay leaves
pinch of salt
4 pinches of cracked black pepper
water

Directions:
roughly chop onions, potatoes and carrots and place in a large pot along with chops trimmed of excess fat.  season with salt and pepper and add bayleaves.  cover with stock and water.  simmer for 1 hour then add split peas and bring to boil.  reduce to simmer for further 90mins  until peas have dissolved and meat is tender.

Submitter: 
karyn
Description: 
a hearty stew that tastes great and will stick to your ribs!
