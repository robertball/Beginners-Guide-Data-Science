Title:
Salmon with Sriracha Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097122.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz salmon
2 Tbsp olive oil
For Marinade:
1/4 C olive oil
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp sriracha
1 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp ginger
For Sauce:
2 Tbsp mayo
2 tsp sriracha
1 tsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp lime juice
1/2 tsp rice vinegar
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp sesame oil

Directions:
Mix together all marinade ingredients.  Place salmon in dish or zip-lock bag and cover with marinade.  Refrigerate for 2 hours.
Mix together all sauce ingredients and set aside.  Heat skillet over medium heat.  Add salmon filets, skin side down, and salt and pepper to taste.  Allow to cook for 4 minutes, then flip.  Cook for another 4 minutes or until desired doneness is reached.  Drizzle with sauce.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
Most of the time required for this tasty dish is in the marinating- the fish itself is ready in minutes!  Full of flavor and easy to cook, this one is sure to please!
