Title:
Boona's Butterscotch Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) pkg butterscotch chips
1/3 c. butter
2 c. graham cracker crumbs
1 (8 oz) pkg cream cheese
1 (14 oz) can condensed milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt butterscotch chips and butter in microwave, stirring often. Add graham cracker crumbs. Press half of the mixture into the bottom of greased 9 x 13 pan. In large bowl, beat cheese, milk, vanilla, and egg until fluffy. Pour over crust. Top with remaining crumb mixture. Bake 25-30 minutes. Chill before cutting and serving. Best when served VERY chilled.
