Title:
Chutney Cracker Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons sour cream
1 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 cup sliced green onions with tops
1/2 cup chopped peanuts
1 (9 ounce) jar chutney

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat the cream cheese, sour cream and curry powder until smooth. Fold in the onions and peanuts. Spread about 1/2 in. thick on a serving plate. Chill. Just before serving, pour chutney over all. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Carolyn  Eastham
Description: 
'This savory spread on crackers is a delicious alternative to the usual cheese spread,' reports Carolyn Eastham, a South Bend, Washington field editor.
