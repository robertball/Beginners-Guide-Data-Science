Title:
Jana's Crunchy Garlic Protein Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chickpeas
2 tablespoons olive oil
garlic powder
onion powder
Italian seasoning
black pepper
1 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
Drain chickpeas and pat dry with a paper towel.
Preheat oven to 375.
Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
Put chickpeas in a bowl and add olive oil. Mix well.
Spread chickpeas on baking sheet and top with spices.
Bake for 20-45 minutes until chickpeas are golden or reach desired crunchiness.
