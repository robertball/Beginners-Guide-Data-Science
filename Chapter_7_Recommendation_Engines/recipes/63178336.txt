Title:
Dijon-Tarragon Cream Chicken ala Elaine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
dijon mustard thinned with a little chicken broth
seasoned flour (salt, pepper and dried tarragon)
3 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
chopped shallots
sliced mushrooms
finely diced garlic
1/2 dry white wine
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
2 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon

Directions:
Thin dijon mustard with a little chicken broth and slather on chicken breasts, let set while assembling ingredients.
Melt the butter and heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Season floured chicken with salt and pepper, and place in the skillet. Brown on both sides. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and continue cooking 15 minutes, or until chicken juices run clear. Set aside and keep warm.
Add shallots and mushrooms, a tab of butter and cooked until mushrooms have given up liquid. Add garlic and stir for about 1 minute.
Add dry white to deglaze pan, scraping up brown bits.. Stir cream into the pan. Mix in mustard and tarragon. Cook and stir 5 minutes, or until thickened. Return chicken to skillet to coat with sauce. Drizzle chicken with remaining sauce to serve.
