Title:
- Marinated Venison Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 pounds venison (deer)
Marinade, 1 bottle divided (teryaki, or your favorite flavor)
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Tony Checker's seasoning to taste
8-10 strips of bacon
2 large onions, sliced
2 bell peppers, sliced
Ranch dressing

Directions:
Marinade the venison in your favorite marinade, reserving about 1/4 cup of the marinade for later use.
On a large piece of aluminum foil, sprinkle salt, pepper and Tony Checker's seasoning on the foil.
Place venison on top of seasonings.
Lay bacon strips on top of the meat.
Place the sliced onions and bell peppers over the venison and bacon.
Pour Ranch dressing over the top.
Place remaining marinade around the bottom, then wrap it up in the alluminum foil.
Place on a cooking sheet or roasting pan.
Bake at 350 for two hours.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
My brother developed this recipe after years of perfecting it.  It is so tender and delicious! Definately a family favorite in the fall!
