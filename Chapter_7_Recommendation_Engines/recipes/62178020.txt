Title:
Coconut Walnut Shortbread bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups butter, room temperature
1 3/4 c powdered sugar
4 c plus 2 T all purpose flour
4 t cornstarch
7 eggs
3 c firmly packed light brown sugar
1 T vanilla
1 T light corn syrup
5 1/2 c chopped walnuts (19 oz)
3/4 c plus 2 T flaked coconut
1 egg beaten with 1 t water(glaze)

Directions:
Position rack in lower third of the oven and preheat to 275.
Cream butter with powdered sugar until light and fluffy.
Blend in flour and cornstarch.
Press dough into bottom of 12 X17 inch baking pan.
Pierce surface with fork.
Bake until golden brown, about 70 minutes.
Remove shortbread from oven and cool 10-15 minutes.  Increase oven temperature to 350.
Meanwhile, blend eggs, brown sugar, vanilla and corn syrup in large bowl.  Mix in coconuts and walnuts.
Brush shortbread with glaze.  Spread walnut mixture over shortbread evenly.  Bake until top is golden and set.
Cut into 2 x 1 inch bars.
