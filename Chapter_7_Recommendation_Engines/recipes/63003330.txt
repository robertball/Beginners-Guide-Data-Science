Title:
No-Bake Chocolate Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749313.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups quaker oats
1 cup dark chocolate chips (I had the jumbo ones on hand)
2 tbsp margarine
1 tbsp sugar
1 cup shredded coconut
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Melt the chocolate chips in a double boiler
Melt margarine and stir until combined.
Remove bowl from heat and stir in oats, sugar, coconut, almonds, milk, vanilla. Scoop into little saucers and chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
