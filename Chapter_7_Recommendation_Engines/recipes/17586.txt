Title:
Waldorf Astoria Cake Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/193975.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package confectioners' sugar
1 cup shortening
2/3 cup milk
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon butter flavored extract
1 teaspoon clear imitation vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine confectioners sugar, shortening, milk, salt, butter flavored extract and vanilla. Beat until smooth and fluffy.

Submitter: 
Tina Ashbridge
Description: 
This easy white frosting is perfect for wedding cakes.
