Title:
Cucumber Crunch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
1 large tomato, thinly sliced
1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 teaspoon seasoning salt

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onion in a salad bowl.
In a small bowl, stir together rice wine vinegar, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and seasoned salt. Pour over vegetables.  Chill for 1 hour.  Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
StanDiego
Description: 
This cucumbers and red onion salad has the flavor of a fresh sweet pickle. Serve as is, spoon over grilled hamburgers, or layer between two slices of cheese in a grilled cheese sandwich.Modified from: Cucumber Crunch Salad
