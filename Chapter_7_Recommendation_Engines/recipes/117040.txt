Title:
O'Brien Kielbasa Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769304.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fully cooked kielbasa or Polish sausage, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1 (24 ounce) package frozen O'Brien hash brown potatoes
3 tablespoons canola oil
pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown sausage; remove with a slotted spoon and set aside. Add potatoes and oil to drippings. Cover and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with pepper. Return sausage to the pan; cover and cook until heated through.

Submitter: 
Sher Koczko
Description: 
'When serving this, be prepared to dish out second helpings-and even thirds!' says Sher Koczko of Winchester, California. Her combination of hash browns and sausage complements many menus.
