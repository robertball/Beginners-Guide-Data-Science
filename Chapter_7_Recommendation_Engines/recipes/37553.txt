Title:
Pumpkin Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981566.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix
1/4 cup KOOL-AID Orange Flavor Sugar-Sweetened Soft Drink Mix
1 (16 ounce) container ready-to-spread vanilla frosting

Directions:
Prepare and bake cake mix as directed on package for cupcakes, adding soft drink mix before beating.
Frost cooled cupcakes with frosting. Sprinkle with additional soft drink mix to resemble pumpkins. Create a "face" with Halloween candies, decorating gel and decorating icing.
