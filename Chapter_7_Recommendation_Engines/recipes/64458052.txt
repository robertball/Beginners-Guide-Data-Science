Title:
Blackened Salmon tacos with Chunky Mango Avacodo Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Fresh wild caught salmon filet
6 Corn tortillas
3 Large limes
Sweet and spicy seafood rub
Chili powder
Olive oil
Salt and Pepper
-Salsa-
3 Ripe mangos
1 Orange or red bell pepper
1 Jalapeno
1 Avacado
2 Tbsp chopped cilantro
Pinch of salt
Juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Dice Mangos, Jalapeno, Bell pepper, Avacado and add to a large bowl. Add chopped cilantro. Mix in lime juice and salt and mix. Refrigerate.
Slice salmon in half to create 2 steaks.
Sprinkle desired amount of rub over salmon adding a small amount of chili powder, salt and pepper.
Rub spices into fish.
Add enough olive oil in pan to coat bottom and heat oil to med- high.
Add fish, skin side down and cook 4-5 min. Flip fish and carefully peel skin off. Then season top with rub and cook 4-5 min. Then remove and slice each peice long ways to create 6 peices of fish.
Add a small amount of oilve oil to a second pan and cook tortillas just until soft.
Place peice of fish with mango salsa on tortilla, squeeze wedge of lime on top and enjoy.
