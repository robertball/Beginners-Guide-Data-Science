Title:
Homemade Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 pint heavy whipping cream
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium nonstick saucepan over medium heat. Stir in cream cheese and garlic using a wire whisk until smooth, about 5 minutes. Add heavy cream a little at a time and whisk until smooth, cooking slow over medium-low heat, 7 to 10 minutes. Stir in Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper until blended. Adjust seasonings to taste. Remove from heat.

Submitter: 
zandcjones
Description: 
This recipe is the best and most delicious homemade Alfredo sauce. It's really creamy, and I promise that you will not make it any other way. When I cook this at the firehouse this will feed 5 of us and we'll have leftovers. Serve over pasta.
