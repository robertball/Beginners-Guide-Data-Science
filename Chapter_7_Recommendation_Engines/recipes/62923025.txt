Title:
apple cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723429.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
3 eggs plus 2 egg whites
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 1/2 cups cake flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 TBS cinnamon
1 cup applesauce
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
preheat oven to 350. line cupcake pan with paper or foil liners. in a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. beat in eggs, one at a time. add vanilla; mix well. combine dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately with applesauce and sour cream (batter will be thick). mix for about 1 minute with stand mixer. DO NOT OVER-MIX or you will get large air pockets in your cake.
pour into lined cupcake pans and bake for 16-18 minutes.
allow to cool for 2 minutes, and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
