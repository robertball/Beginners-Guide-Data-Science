Title:
Taco "Dump" Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/963912.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger, browned and drained
1 can corn, undrained
1 can kidney beans, undrained
1 can pinto beans, undrained
1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1 package taco seasoning mix
1 package ranch seasoning mix
Toppings after serving (optional):
Shredded Cheese
Corn chips
Sour Cream

Directions:
Combine everything except the "topping" ingredients in a large pot and heat up.  Can use crock pot if not serving immediately.

Submitter: 
havit-please
Description: 
Made this for dinner with a bunch of girlfriends at a local Bed & Breakfast.  Was super simple to make and tasted great sitting around the campfire that night!
