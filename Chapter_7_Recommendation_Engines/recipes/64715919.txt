Title:
Keto Mexican Street No-Corn Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, chopped into small florets
1 T olive oil
1 T cumin
1 tsp chipotle powder
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
1-2 tsp sea salt
1/2 c mayonnaise
1/4 to 1/c c sour cream
juice 1/2 lime (or to taste)
1/4 to 1/3 cup finely crumbled Cotija cheese
1/2 c or more cilantro
1/4 c or more green onion

Directions:
preheat oven to 450
combine cumin, chipotle, paprika, garlic, salt
toss cauliflower in olive oil and about 1 T of spices (reserve remaining spices)
roast cauliflower at 450 for 20-30 min until tender and starting to caramelize/brown
meanwhile, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, lime and few pinches of spices
remove cauliflower from oven, toss with sauce, cilantro, onion, and sprinkle cotija cheese on top
