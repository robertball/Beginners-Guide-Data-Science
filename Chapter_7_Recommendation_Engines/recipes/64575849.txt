Title:
Big Ray's BBQ Glazed Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3608295.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup apricot jam
1/4 cup barbecue sauce
1/2 tsp granulated garlic
1 small onion, finely chopped
8  boneless skinless chicken thighs

Directions:
Heat oven to 375ºF.
Mix first 3 ingredients in large bowl until blended. Stir in onions. Add chicken; turn to evenly coat.
Place chicken in single layer in 7x11 inch  baking dish; cover with sauce.
Bake 25 to 30 min. or until chicken is done (165ºF).

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Tender, juicy chicken thighs get a sweet and sticky glaze made with apricot jam and BBQ sauce in this easy weeknight recipe.
