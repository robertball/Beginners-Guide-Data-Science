Title:
Asian Vegetable Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- Onion
- Garlic
- Wok Oil
- Red pepper paste (Sambal)
- 1 tsp. Katoembar
- 1 tsp. Laos
- 1 tsp. Marjoraan (Marjorie)
- 1 tbsp. Klapper (or 1/2 cup Coconut milk)
3 oz. snow peas, cleaned.
1 head of Chineese cabbage, sliced.

Directions:
Sautee the onion and garlic in the oil.
Add the sambal
Add the snow peas
After a couple of minutes add the Cabbage.
Add the spices.
Add the Klapper.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
This dish can be done with different types of vegetables. I like the snow peas because they stay crunchy and taste so good!
Tip: Don't overcook !
