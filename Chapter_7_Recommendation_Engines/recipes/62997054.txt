Title:
Baked Flounder with Panko & Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5069159.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 flounder or tilapia filets
1 cup soft  fresh bread crumbs
4 tablespoons butter, divided
2/3 cup grated parmesan
1/2 teaspoon salt
Pinch dried thyme
dash black pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400.  Lightly greese a large, shallow baking dish.  Arrange thefish fillets on the prepared baking pan.  Brush with 1 tablespoon of the melted butter.  Combine the remaining 3 tablespoons of butter with the bread crumbs, cheese, and seasonings.  Sprinkle the crumbs over the filets.
Bake the fish fillets for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through   Fish will flake easily when fish is done.
Carefully remove the fillets to plates with a spatula.
Serves 4
