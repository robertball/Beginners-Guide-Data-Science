Title:
French-Style Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843004.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium chicken breasts
1/2 cup of dry white wine or vermouth
1 can cream of mushroom soup
4 oz. mushrooms, sliced
1 cup sour cream, mixed with 1/4 cup flour
Salt and pepper
Paprika

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken breasts lightly with salt, pepper, and paprika.
Place chicken in crock-pot.
Mix white wine, soup, and mushrooms until well combined. Pour over chicken.
Sprinkle with a little more paprika. Cover and cook on low for 7 to 9 hours or on high 3 to 4 hours.
Remove chicken and stir in sour cream mixture during last 30 minutes.
Serve sauce over chicken

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
Give your chicken dishes a French kiss. The combination of wine, creamy mushrooms and sour cream make this exotic poultry pleaser absolutely divine.
