Title:
Garlic Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725284.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Bisquick baking mix
2/3 cup milk
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup butter
1/4 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Mix the bisquick,milk and cheese together untill soft dough forms.Beat vigorously for 30 seconds.
Drop dough by spoonfuls onto lightly greased cookie sheet.
Bake for 8-10 mins.at 450F untill golden brown.
Mix butter and garlic powder together,brush over warm biscuits before removing from cookie sheet.
