Title:
Almond Mocha Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283910.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup very strong, hot, coffee
1 Tbls. sugar
1 Tbls. semisweet chocolate chips - *see note
1/2 cup milk
3 Tbls. half n' half
1/2 tsp. pure almond extract
6 ice cubes

Directions:
Stir chocolate chips and sugar into hot coffee til melted.
Add all remaining ingredients.
Pour into a tall glass.
Sip and enjoy!
* I love the tiny droplets of chocolate in my iced coffee and at the bottom of the glass! But if your not into that kind of thing...use 2 Tbls. of chocolate syrup instead of the chocolate chips.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
You don't have to go to an exspensive cafe to get a great iced coffee! This is so quick and easy and oh, so satisfying!
