Title:
Chicken Tortilla Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 Cans of cream of chicken soup
1 Can of diced tomatoes and green chili's
12 6in Corn tortilla's
1 cup shredded mexican cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Boil Chicken.
Combine soups and tomatoes in a mixing bowl and set aside.
Chop tortilla's into bite sized pieces.
Layer 1/3 of tortilla's on bottom of pan. Place 1/2 of the chicken on top of the tortilla's, and spoon 1/2 of the soup mixture on top of the chicken. Repeat Layer
Cover and bake for 40 minutes. Then top with cheese and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
