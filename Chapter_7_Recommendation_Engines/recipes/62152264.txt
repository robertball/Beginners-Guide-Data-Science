Title:
Jelly Donut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 egg lightly beaten
1/3 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup sugar
Strawberry Jelly
2 Tablespoons melted butter
1/3 cup of sugar mixed w/ 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients
Mix liquid ingredients
Add liquid to dry ingredients and stir just until combined
Spray muffin pan or use muffin cups.                    Fill 10 muffin cups half full.
Put 1/2 to 1 tsp Strawberry Jelly on each muffin, and top with more batter.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes
When muffins are cool enough to touch, dip muffin tops in butter, then sugar/cinnamon mixture.
ENJOY!!!!

Submitter: 
Cindy Peterson Kilday
Description: 
These are an absolute favorite of my kids, and a hit wherever I take them.  They taste just like a jelly donut, maybe even better!
