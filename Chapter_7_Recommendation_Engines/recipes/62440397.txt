Title:
Crab Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Dungeness Crab (about 2 lbs)
1 stick unsalted butter/8 tablespoons
3-4 cloves garlic (chopped coarsely)
2 teaspoons chicken bouillon powder
2 teaspoons black pepper (cracked)
1/4 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
Clean and chop the crab into small pieces. Heat up the butter in a wok or pan (between medium to low heat) and saute the garlic until aromatic, but not brown. Add in the black pepper, chicken bouillon powder, and then add in the crab and stir well. Add in the sugar and cook the crab until half done.
Dish out and bake it in the oven at 350 degree F for 25-30 minutes. Serve hot.
