Title:
Pineapple Black Bean Protein Topper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 20-oz. can pineapple chunks packed in juice, drained
One 15-oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 1/2 cups pico de gallo or fresh chunky salsa

Directions:
Roughly chop pineapple and transfer to a large airtight container.
Add black beans and pico de gallo or salsa.
Stir thoroughly. Seal container and refrigerate until chilled. Enjoy!
