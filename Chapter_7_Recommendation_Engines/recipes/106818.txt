Title:
Vanilla-Orange KOOL-AID速 Yogurt Float Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3/4 cup SPLENDA速 No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 (0.13 ounce) package KOOL-AID速 Orange Flavor Unsweetened Soft Drink Mix
1 liter seltzer water, chilled
2 cups reduced-fat vanilla frozen yogurt, divided

Directions:
Combine SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, KOOL-AID(R) Soft Drink Mix, and 1 cup of the seltzer in a pitcher, stirring until SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener dissolves. Stir in remaining seltzer.
Pour 1 cup of the mixture into each of 4 tall glasses; top each serving with 1/2 cup of the frozen yogurt. Serve immediately.
