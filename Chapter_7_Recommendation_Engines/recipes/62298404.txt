Title:
Slow Cooker Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/362863.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef sirloin, cut into 2 inch strips
garlic powder to taste
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cube beef bouillon
1/4 cup hot water
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 cup chopped onion
3-4 large bell peppers (red, yellow, orange or green), sliced.
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
3 cloves garlic, pressed
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Sprinkle strips of sirloin with garlic powder to taste. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the vegetable oil and brown the seasoned beef strips. Transfer to a slow cooker.
Mix bouillon cube with hot water until dissolved, add corn starch. Pour into the slow cooker with meat. Stir in onion, green peppers, stewed tomatoes, garlic, soy sauce, sugar, and salt.
Cover, and cook on High for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low for 6 to 8 hours.
