Title:
Pasta Salvatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pre-made chicken tortellini
2 large garlic cloves, finely diced
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp flour
2 cups chicken stock
1 Tbsp sun-dried tomatoes, finely diced
2 tsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped
1/3 cup mascarpone cheese
1 packed cup chopped kale (optional)

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to boil and add tortellini. Cook according to package (7-9 minutes) and keep warm as you make the sauce.
Melt the butter and slowly saut√© the garlic over low heat for 5 minutes.
Add the flour and stir until light golden.
Add the chicken stock slowly while stirring, then add the sun-dried tomatoes and rosemary (and kale if using). Simmer on low until it has the consistency of a thick sauce.
Add the mascarpone cheese, stirring until smooth.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Toss warm tortellini in sauce, top lightly with parmesan cheese, and serve with warm garlic bread.

Submitter: 
paralee
Description: 
Chicken tortellini with a sun-dried tomato, rosemary, and mascarpone cream sauce. Had this in a fantastic Italian restaurant in San Francisco and had to recreate it.
