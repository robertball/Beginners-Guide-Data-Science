Title:
Braised Red Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T EVOO
1 med. onion, thinly sliced
2 T. light-brown sugar
1/2 C cider vinegar
2 C fresh cider
1 C low-sodium chicken stock
1 cinnamon stick
1 dried bay leaf
coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
1 small head red cabbage (about 2 1/2 #), cut into 8 wedges, core intact

Directions:
Preheat oven 350. Heat oil in lg heavy ovenproof saucepan over med. heat. Cook onion until edges start to caramelize, 10-12 min. Add sugar, and cook 1 min more. Add vinegar. Scrape up brown bits from bottom of pan with wooden spoon. Stir in cider, stock, cinnamon, bay leaf, 1 1/2 t. salt and 1/4 t. pepper. Add cabbage, arranging in single layer. Bring to a boil. Cover, transfer to oven. Braise until cabbage is tender, 40-45 min.
Transfer cabbage to a serving platter using a slotted spoon; discard cinnamon stick, and bay leaf. Bring remaining liquid in saucepan to a simmer over med-high heat. Cook until reduced by half, about 10 min(you will have about 1 C sauce). Spoon sauce over cabbage.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Red cabbage is braised in cider sauce until tender. Started on stove and finished in oven.
