Title:
Arroz con Camaron or Shrimp with yellow rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
1.5 C frozen green peas, thawed
1 8 ounce package seasoned yellow rice
2 1/2 C water
3 T butter
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1 lb large shrimp, shelled and deveined
salt and pepper to taste
lemon wedges for serving

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large deep skillet over medium high.
Sautee onion until soft.
Add green peas and sautee until just warmed.
Pour in water and bring to a boil.
Add yellow rice package and stir for one minute before covering and reducing heat to a simmer.  Let simmer for 20-25 minutes.
When rice has about 10 minutes left, melt butter in a large non stick skillet over low heat.
Sautee garlic until soft being careful not to let it brown.
Increase heat to medium and sautee shrimp until just cooked through, about 5 minutes.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Add cooked shrimp, garlic and butter to the rice mixture and stir to combine.
Allow to sit for a couple of minutes.
Serve with lemon wedges.
