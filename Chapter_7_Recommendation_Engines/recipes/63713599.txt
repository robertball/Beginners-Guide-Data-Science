Title:
Creamy Hazelnut Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 oz hazelnut liquor
1 1/2 oz vanilla vodka
1 oz simple syrup
splash of half and half
ice cubes

Directions:
put all ingredients into a cocktail shaker and shake until cold. strain into a martini glass.
