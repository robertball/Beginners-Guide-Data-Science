Title:
Odd Bagel Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5264360.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 bagel, split and toasted
4 slices sliced pepperoni

Directions:
Spread the peanut butter on the bagel while still warm. Arrange the pepperoni on the peanut butter and sandwich between the 2 bagel halves.

Submitter: 
dani
Description: 
My dad introduced me to the odd combination of peanut butter and pepperoni. It doesn't sound appetizing but is totally amazing!
