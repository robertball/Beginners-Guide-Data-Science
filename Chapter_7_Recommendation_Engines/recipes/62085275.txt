Title:
Hot Chicken Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c minute rice, cooked
2c cooked chicken, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
2 8 oz cans mushrooms, drained
1/2 lrg onion (3/4 c) chopped
2 T garlic, chopped
1 can french style green beans, drained
1 can cream of chicken soup
3/4 c sour cream
3/4 c mayonnaise
1/2 t accent (msg)(optional)
2 t salt-free Mrs. Dash
1/4 t crushed red pepper
salt and pepper to taste (I used 1t salt and 3/4 t pepper)
1 c mozzerella cheese
1 1/2 c crushed potato chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. In alarge mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except cheese and potato chips.
3. Spread in a 13x9 cake pan.
4. Top first with cheese, then with crushed potato chips. Sprinkle with pepper if desired.
5. Bake at 350 degrees for 25-35 minutes, or until chips are golden brown and cheese is melted.
6. Serve hot, with a green salad and biscuits or bread

Submitter: 
RANCHGIRL_59714
Description: 
This is a compilation of hot chicken salad and chicken rice casserole recipes I've collected over the years, and a concoction that my fiance requests frequently!  A great use for leftover chicken! Feel free to ad-lib ingredients to taste, as it is very versatile.
