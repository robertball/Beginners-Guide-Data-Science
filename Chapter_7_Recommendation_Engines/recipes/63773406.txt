Title:
Zucchini Tomato Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045747.jpg
Ingredients:
6 T. Arrowroot Powder
12 T. Coconut Oil
6 C. Almond Milk
3 tsp. Sea salt
1 10oz package goat chevre
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. italian seasoning
3 lb. cubed no nitrate honey ham
10 C. cubed zucchini
1 large onion
5-6 cups tomatoes, chopped
2lbs. pasta (farafelle bow tie)
1 c. goat cheddar cheese shredded (reserved for topping off)

Directions:
1. Saute onions with oil.
2. In a LARGE mixing bowl add the sauteed onion, dry pasta, chopped tomatoes & cubed zucchini pieces & salt & pepper. Mix with hands to incorporate all items.
3. Divide among the three 2 quart containers leaving about 1 inch of head space.
4. Make the white alfredo sauce with the top 7 ingredients above and then divide evenly among the 3 containers. (approximately 2 1/3 cups each)
5. Mix in the white sauce.
6. Top with the shredded goat cheddar cheese & foil cover.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes, remove foil last 5 minutes of bake time.
**If frozen...bake for 90 minutes...with foil & remove foil last few minutes of bake time

Submitter: 
angela
Description: 
Modification of Zucchini Sausage Casserole that we make every summer with our garden fresh produce.  Tastes great in the middle of the winter!
