Title:
Aunt Mary's Monster cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1     Cup Oleo
3/4   Cup White sugar
3/4   Cup brown sugar
2     Eggs
1     tsp Vanilla
1/2   Cup Peanut Butter
1 1/2 Cup Flour
1     tsp Baking Soda
1/2   tsp Baking Powder
1     tsp Salt
2     Cups Oat Meal
1     Cup  Corn Flakes (crushed) or Rice Crispies
12    Oz Chocolate Chips
1/2   Cup Pecans or Walnuts chunks

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, Refrigerate 3 hrs. Preheat oven to 375 Drop on cookie sheet w parchment paper,
lower heat to 350 cook 10 to 12 min, cool makes 2 dozen cookies
