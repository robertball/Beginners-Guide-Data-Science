Title:
SEA FOOD NACHOS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PKG. OF FROZEN IMATATION CRAB, 8 COOKED AND PEELED SHRIMP, NO TAILS. 8OZ OF MONTERAY JACK CHEESE, 1 BAG OF WHITE TORTILLIA CHIPS, COCKTAIL SAUCE FOR TOPPING

Directions:
PLACE SHRIMP AND DEFROSTED CRAB IN FOOD PROCESSER, CHOP UNTIL ALL BLENDS TOGETHER. REMOVE AND PLACE IN A BOWL, ADD SHREDDED CHEESE MIX ALL TOGETHER. TAKE BY TSP. AND TOP EACH CHIP WITH MIXTURE, PLACE IN 350 DEGREE OVEN AND KEEP CLOSE EYE ON IT AS ALL OVENS VARY, WHEN CHEESE MELTS THEY ARE DONE!! HOPE YOU WILL ENJOY.
