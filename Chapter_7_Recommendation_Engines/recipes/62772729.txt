Title:
Tuna Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/643754.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can tuna
1 egg
mayonnaise
carrot, grated
onion, chopped
bread crumbs
2 crusty buns
cheese
lettuce
cucumbers
mustard
mayonnaise
ketchup

Directions:
Mush together tuna, egg, mayonnaise, carrot, onion, and bread crumbs. Form them into patties by hand.
Cook in a frying pan until done.
Put patties on buns along with whatever fixings you want.

Submitter: 
rchurchley
Description: 
They're hamburgers... but with tuna instead of beef! Not much else to say about them.
