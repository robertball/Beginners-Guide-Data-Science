Title:
Four Star Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/338933.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese (softened)
1 1/2 c. sour cream
1 tsp. diced onions
1 tsp. diced green pepper
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix sour cream, cream cheese, onions, green pepper, and black pepper.  Spread in a small baking dish.  Sprinkle pecans on top and bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  Serve with Ritz Crackers.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
My grandfather used to make this every Christmas and me and my mother are carrying on the tradition.  It's a hit everywhere we take it.  Enjoy!!
