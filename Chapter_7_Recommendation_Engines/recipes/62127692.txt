Title:
World's Greatest Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. boneless, skinless, chicken breasts (cooked, cooled, and cut into cubes
1 small box Uncle Ben's Original Wild Rice (not fast cook)
1 pkg Italian Salad Dressing (prepared as directed)
1 Large can atichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 cup celery, chopped
1/2 cup mayonaise
2oz chopped pimento
1 can sliced water chesnuts-chopped
1 8oz pkg mushroom-sliced and sauted in butter

Directions:
Boil chicken in water with onion and celery.  Drain, and chop chicken.
Prepare Wild Rice and Italian Dressing.
Saute' mushrooms in butter.
Combine all ingredients and marinate overnight in fridge.
Serves 6-8 Luncheon size servings.

Submitter: 
Kari
Description: 
This is an easy but exquisite chicken salad.  Great for everyday and suitable for showers, teas, etc.!!
