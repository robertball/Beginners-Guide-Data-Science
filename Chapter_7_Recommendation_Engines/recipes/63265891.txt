Title:
Salmon and Tomato tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856346.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Wheat germ
1/2 C. Bread crumbs
1 tsp. Ginger
1 tsp. Dried mustard
1/2 tsp. Cumin
1 tsp. thyme
1 tin Italian stewed tomatoes
1/2 C. canned salmon (drained)
Parmesan cheese
1 C. All purpose flour
1/2 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 C. Lard (chilled)
10 Tbsp. cold water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F (190 C)
In a medium size bowl combine flours, salt and chilled lard together, cut into small pieces with a pastry blender or butter knife.
Add cold water slowly to dry ingredients and stir with a fork until mixture comes together to form a smooth ball of dough
Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface and cut out rounds with a large lid or cutter and place in a muffin pan then set aside
In another bowl combine wheat germ, bread crumbs and spices.
Stir mixture together well and then add tomatoes and salmon mixing everything together well until thoroughly combined
Spoon mixture into ready made tarts and top with some parmesan cheese
Place in oven for 20  minutes and then remove and set aside on counter until cool enough to remove from muffin tin.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very spicy tart that holds together well with wheat germ and bread crumbs along with canned salmon and a tin of stewed tomatoes that make it very flavourful for a nice lunch meal
