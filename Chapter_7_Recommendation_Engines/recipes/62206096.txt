Title:
Apple Crisp Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
3/4 cup unsweetened chunky applesauce
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
1 cup raisins
1 3/4 cups whole wheat flour
1/4 cup wheat germ (optional)
3 cups old fashioned oats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, add applesauce.  Add eggs, 1 at a time until well blended, then stir in the vanilla, baking soda, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon and raisins until well distributed.  Add the whole wheat flour, wheat germ and oats and combine.
Drop by well rounded tablespoons 2" apart on parchment covered cookie sheets.
Bake 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned.
Allow to rest for 2 minutes on cookie sheet then transfer to a cooling rack for at least 30 minutes.
Store in an airtight container and cookies will remain moist, soft and chewy.

These soft, chewey cookies taste just like apple crisp and smell heavenly while they bake.
