Title:
Spicy Chicken Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup lowfat plain Greek yogurt
1 tsp white wine vinegar
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 small red onion, divided
1 ripe avocado, cut into 1-inch chunks
1 cup thinly sliced cucumber
1 portobello cap
1 cup frozen shelled edamame, thawed
2 gloves garlic
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (6oz each), quartered
2 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
4 whole wheatEnglish Muffins (or whole grain buns), split
lettuce leaves (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 400
In a bowl, whisk together yogurt, vinegar and sugar
Dice half the onion; add diced onion, avocado and cucumber to yogurt mixture, toss to coat
In a food processor, process portobello, edamame, garlic and remaining half of onion until finely chopped
Add chicken, soy sauce and cayenne.
Pulse until chicken is chopped and ingredients and combined, about 12 times
Form mixture into 4 patties.
Heat oil in a large over-safe skillet over high heat.
Add patties to cook, turning once, until both sides are browned (2-3 mins per side)
Place skillet in over and bake until patties are cooked through (12-14mins)
Place lettuce on desired muffin or bun, top lettuce with 1/2 cup avocado mixture and put the remaining end on bread on top of the sandwich
