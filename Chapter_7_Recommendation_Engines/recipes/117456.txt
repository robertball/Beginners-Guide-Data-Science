Title:
Fluffy Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992184.jpg
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
2 tablespoons butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a bowl, whisk the eggs and milk until combined. In a skillet, heat butter until hot. Add egg mixture; cook and stir over medium-low heat until eggs are completely set. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Marjorie  Carey
Description: 
'I started fixing Fluffy Scrambled Eggs years ago when we raised chickens and had fresh ingredients every morning,' she recalls. 'They're hard to beat when it comes to taste and texture.'
