Title:
Turkey Meatball Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/891275.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T olive or coconut oil
2 large carrots, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 tsp fennel seed
1, 15 ounce, can diced tomatoes
5 C chicken stock
1 to 1 1/4 lb ground turkey breast
1 egg
Â¼ C shredded Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 pinch nutmeg
4 or so handfuls fresh spinach, kale, or collard greens, roughly chopped
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat a large soup pot over medium heat and add the oil.  Add carrots, onion, and fennel seed.  Cook until onion starts to become translucent, stirring frequently.  Add canned tomatoes and chicken stock.  Bring to a simmer.
Meanwhile, combine  the turkey breast, egg, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and nutmeg.  Roll into about 24 meatballs.  Drop meatballs into simmering soup and cook through, 15 minutes.
When the meatballs are done, stir the greens into the soup until wilted.  Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Nancy Anne Morris Martin
Description: 
This healthy recipe is kind of fun, because you drop the raw meatballs into the soup and let them cook while the soup simmers.  It's a delicious and nourishing soup that my whole family enjoys.
