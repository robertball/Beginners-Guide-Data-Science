Title:
Crock Pot Round Steak With Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. round steak
1 (1.5 oz.) package of dry onion soup mix
1/4 Cup water
2 (10 3/4 oz.) cans of cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Cut fat off of meat.  Tenderize Round Steak, if needed. Cut into serving pieces and place in Crock Pot.
In a medium bowl mix the dry onion soup mix, water, and cream of mushroom soups.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
Serve over mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
A very delicious and easy main dish that is excellent when this is served over mashed potatoes.
