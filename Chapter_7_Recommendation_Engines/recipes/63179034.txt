Title:
Chipotle Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs Unsalted butter
4 cloves garlic; minced
1/4 cup White wine
1/2 can Chipotle chile; pureed
2 1/2 cups Heavy cream
2 tbs Chicken stock

Directions:
To make the cream sauce saute the garlic in butter briefly, add wine and reduce heat.
Add pureed chipotle and heavy cream, reduce heat and season with the chicken base.
Let simmer for about 10 mins

Submitter: 
Nesto
Description: 
For pasta, risotto, or sauteed pork medallions, or whatever else needs rich and spicy.
