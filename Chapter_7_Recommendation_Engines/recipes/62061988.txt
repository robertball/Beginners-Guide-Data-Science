Title:
Pasta Ragout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) package of ribbon pasta
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 or 3 garlic cloved, chopped
1 tsp. dried rosemary
1 green or yellow pepper, chopped
1/2 lb. mushroom, sliced
2 medium zucchini, chopped
1 Tbsp. dried basil
1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 (15 oz) can crushed tomatoes
1/2 c. Mirin (in the soy sauce section of the grocery store)
salt to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta, drain, and set aside.  Meanwhile, heat oil and saute onion, garlic, and rosemary for about 3 minutes.  Add basil, diced tomatoes, crushed tomatoes, mirin, and simmer for about 5 minutes.  Salt to taste and serve over pasta.

Submitter:

Description: 
A great vegetarian dish!  An array of veggies, spices, and mirin really bring this pasta dish together... and it's so simple and fast!
