Title:
Whole Wheat Flax Bread (Bread Maker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886594.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups of water
2 Tbsp skim milk powder
3 Tbsp honey
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
1/4 cup oatmeal (oat flakes)
1/4 cup flax meal
1 3/4 tsp yeast

Directions:
Put ingredients into the bread maker in the order given.  Use the whole wheat setting.  Cool slightly before devouring!

Submitter: 
Cathryn Aune
Description: 
A moist, whole wheat loaf that uses healthy ingredients.  One loaf is never enough!
