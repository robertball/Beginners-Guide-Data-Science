Title:
Real Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A pot of fresh, strong-brewed coffee, as little or as much as you like.
Enough ice cubes fill a glass 3/4 of the way, or to fill a pitcher 1/2 of the way.
Milk to taste.
Sugar to taste, optional.

Directions:
1) Brew a strong pot of coffee (as little or as much as you'd like), transfer it to a pitcher, and refrigerate it for at least 5 hours.
2) Fill a glass or pitcher 3/4 of the way to the rim with ice cubes, add coffee, leaving space at the top for your milk.
3) Add milk and sugar (sugar is optional) to taste, mix thoroughly.  Drink immediately.

Submitter: 
THREDDIES
Description: 
Sick of sugary coffee drinks?  This traditional iced coffee recipe involves only three ingredients, coffee, ice and milk, and is perfect served on a hot summer day.
