Title:
Peachy Chicken Picante Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, chopped
1/2 cup bell peppers, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 teaspoon cilantro
4 Roma tomatoes, chopped
3 peaches, peeled, pitted, chopped

Directions:
Saute chicken 8-10 minutes turning once.
Add other ingredients. Cook 15-20 minutes at medium heat.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Great with fall fresh peaches.
