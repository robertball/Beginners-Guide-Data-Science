Title:
Jalapeno Pesto Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pasta - 16 oz (Penne or your preference *cook to package directions
baby bella mushrooms - 12 oz, sliced
onion - small, sliced
red bell pepper- 1/2 sliced
olive oil - 4 tbsp
all-purpose flour 2 tbsp
milk - 1 1/2 cups
jalepeno basil pesto - 2 tbsp (or any pesto)
salt - to taste
pepper - to taste

Directions:
1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in pan.
2. Add mushrooms, onions, and a little salt.  Saute until mushrooms and onions are slightly brown.
3. Add bell pepper, toss quickly and keep aside.
4. In same pan, heat remaining 2 tbsp olive oil.
5. Add all-purpose flour and cook until flour cahnges color to slightly pink (approx 1 1/2 mins). Stir continously.
6. Add milk * whisk continously to avoid lumps.  Cook unti sauce thickens slightly.
7. Add jalapeno basil pesto, salt and pepper to taste.
8. Add back in the sauteed vegetables and mix well.
9. Add cook pasta, mix and heat through.
10. Serve with garlic bread.
A light sweet fruity moscato accompanies this dish perfectly! :)
