Title:
Spinach Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf french bread
1 (10 oz.) package frozen chopped spinach
1/2 stick of butter or margarine
1/4 t. hot sauce or to taste
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese
1 bunch green onions, chopped
16 oz. shreeded Colby-Jack cheese (or your choice)

Directions:
Cut french bread in half lengthwise.
Preheat oven to 350.
Melt butter in medium saucepan over medium heat, then add green onions.  Once green onions are just tender, add cream cheese.
Meanwhile, cook spinach as per label instructions.  Drain well.
Add spinach and Tabasco to melted cream cheese mixture.  Stir thoroughly.
Smooth spinach mixture over bread and sprinkle with cheese.
Bake in oven for 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Jodi Hanlon
Description: 
Great appetizer or side dish-I don't like spinach, but I eat this all the time!!!
