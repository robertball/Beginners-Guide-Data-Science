Title:
Instant Pot Haluski with Kielbasa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7663315.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp butter
1 small yellow onion, diced
1 medium head of cabbage, shredded
1 pound kielbasa, sliced
2 cups chicken broth
12 oz bag egg noodles
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Using the saute feature, melt the butter in the Instant Pot. Add the onion. Cook and stir for 1-2 minutes, until lightly browned. Add in cabbage and kielbasa. Cook and stir for about 10 minutes, until cabbage has cooked down.
Add the chicken broth and egg noodles. Mix well.
Place lid on Instant Pot and set to manual high pressure for 3 minutes.
Quick release pressure, remove lid and season with salt and pepper.
Serve immediately.
