Title:
Cheesy Chicken-and-Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Italian blend shredded cheese, divided
2 cups cooked rotini
2 cups coarsely chopped cooked chicken
1/2 cup diced celery
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
garnish: celery leaves

Directions:
STIR together 1/2 cup cheese and next 6 ingredients; spoon into a lightly greased 11-x 7-inch baking dish.
BAKE at 375 degrees for 25 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese; bake 5 more minutes. Garnish, if desired.
