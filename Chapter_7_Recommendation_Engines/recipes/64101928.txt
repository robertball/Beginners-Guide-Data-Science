Title:
Coconut Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs chicken breasts, cubed
2 lbs  butternut squash, chopped
2 medium onions, chopped
8 garlic cloves, minced
1 oz ginger, fresh minced
2 tbsp curry powder
1 tsp coriander, ground
1 tsp cumin, ground
coarse salt
2 cans coconut milk
1 bag frozen peas
chopped cashews
cilantro

Directions:
Chop all vegetables and add to the slow cooker
Add cubed Chicken to the slow cooker
Add all the spices to the slow cooker
Pour 1 can of coconut milk over the contents of the slow cooker
Cook for 9 hours on low
Add 1 can of coconut milk and frozen peas for the last 25 minutes of cooking time
Garnish with chopped cashews and cilantro
Serve over rice
