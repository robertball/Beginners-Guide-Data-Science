Title:
Cinnamon Muffin in a Mug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/809964.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 TBS flour
1 TBS ground flax meal
1 TBS quick oats
2 TBS chopped walnuts
½ tsp baking powder
2 tsp cinnamon
1 egg
1 TBS honey
2 TBS vanilla yogurt
Optional: add fruit, such as raisins or a few pieces of chopped apples or dried apricots.

Directions:
Whisk the egg in the cup.
Add the dry ingredients and the yogurt and stir well until blended.
Put in microwave for about 90-100 seconds. Don’t overcook.
Tip out onto a plate and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Nana Clares Kitchen
Description: 
Make a healthy breakfast in a coffee mug in under two minutes in the microwave.
