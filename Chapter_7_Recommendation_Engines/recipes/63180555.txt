Title:
Homemade Nutella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/817944.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup hazelnuts – toasted
220 grams dark chocolate – melted
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
3 tablespoons honey
4 tablespoons coconut oil – melted
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup coconut milk

Directions:
Process hazelnuts in the food processor for about 5 minutes until smooth, nut butter in texture. After desired consistency is met add melted chocolate, unsweetened cocoa powder, honey, melted coconut oil and vanilla extract. Pulse to combine and add in coconut milk.  Pulse again until combined. Add to storage container and enjoy!
