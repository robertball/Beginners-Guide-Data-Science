Title:
CucinaPro Heartshaped Waffles Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/616829.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 Tablespoon sugar
1 stick (1/2 cup butter, melted and cooled
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Whipe eggs and sugar until light and foamy. Add cooled melted butter, milk and vanilla extract, and mix. Sift flour and baking powder, then add to egg mixture. Add salt and beat well. Bake in waffle iron.

Submitter: 
ALASKANSNOBUNNI
Description: 
Fragrant and tender waffles, shaped like hearts. This recipe is the one that came with the waffle iron.
