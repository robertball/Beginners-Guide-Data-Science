Title:
Chicken Schnitzel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves
½ cup flour
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup crushed Cheerio cereal, crushed into a powder
1 tsp garlic powder
½ tsp salt
½ tsp ground black pepper
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 lemons, cut into quarters

Directions:
Cut out the jugular veins and cut off the excess fat from the breasts. Place between sheets of wax paper and pound to ¼” thick. Put the flour on a plate and put the beaten eggs onto another plate. Put the crushed Cheerios, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a large sealable bag. Wash and dry the breasts, dredge in the flour and the eggs then put in the bag of crushed Cheerios and shake well. Place the breasts on a plate and refrigerate for 1 hour.
Heat the oil in a 12” skillet over medium high heat till hot. Fry the breasts till browned on both sides. Squeeze the lemons over the breasts for excellent flavor.
