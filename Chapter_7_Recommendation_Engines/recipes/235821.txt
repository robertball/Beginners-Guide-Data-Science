Title:
Allie's Espresso Kahlua® Liqueur Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4486096.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups dark brown sugar
2 cups hot brewed espresso
2 cups dark rum (such as Bacardi®)
1 vanilla bean

Directions:
Stir brown sugar into espresso in a bowl or container with a spout until dissolved; stir in rum. Pour mixture into a clean, dark bottle; add vanilla bean. Seal bottle tightly with a lid and let sit for 1 month before drinking.

Submitter:

Description: 
Like Kahlua® liqueur but with an Italian kick from espresso.
