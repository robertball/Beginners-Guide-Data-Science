Title:
Christmas Breakfast Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Jimmy Dean Maple flavoredground pork sausage
1 teaspoon mustard powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
6 eggs, combo eggbeaters and eggs, beaten
2 cups milk
1 bag garlic and cheese flavored croutons
8 ounces mild Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Crumble sausage into a medium skillet. Cook over medium heat until evenly brown; drain.
In a medium bowl, mix together mustard powder, salt, eggs and milk. Add the sausage, croutons, and cheese, and stir to coat evenly.  Pour into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover, and chill in the refrigerator for 8 hours, or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cover, and bake 45 to 60 minutes. Uncover, and reduce temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Bake for an additional 30 minutes, or until set.
