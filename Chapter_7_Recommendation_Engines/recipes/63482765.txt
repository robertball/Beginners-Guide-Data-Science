Title:
Asian Stir-Fried Chicken Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chicken stock
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1 lb, boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
3 Tbsp sesame oil, divided
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
1 Tbsp fish sauce
2 tsp lime juice
2 tsp sugar
1 (16)oz bag cole slaw
1/4 cup basil, sliced
1/4 cup red onion, sliced
2 Tbsp green onions, sliced
1/4 cup celery, sliced
1/4 cup slivered almonds, toasted
1 lime, cut into wedges

Directions:
In a covered bowl, combine stock and red pepper flakes.  Stir, add chicken and toss to coat.  Cover and place in refrigerator.  Marinate 3 hrs or overnight.
In a large skillet, heat 1 Tbsp sesame oil over medium-high heat.  Drain chicken from marinade and cook thoroughly, 5-7 min, stirring.  Discard marinade.
For the dressing, in a small bowl, combine soy sauce, garlic, vinegar, fish sauce, lime juice and sugar.  Whisk in remaining 2 Tbsp sesame oil.
In a large serving bowl, combine slaw, basil, onions and celery.  Add chicken and toss with dressing.  Garnish with nuts and lime wedges.
