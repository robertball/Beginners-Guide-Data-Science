Title:
Chili-Lime Shrimp Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp chili powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tbsp lime juice
1 lb uncooked, thawed shrimp (26-30 count OR 31-40 count) peeled, deveined and cleaned
1 large green pepper, sliced length wise
1 med-large (depending how much onion you like), sliced into "rings" then separated
1-2 Serrano peppers (1 will add mild-med spice, 2 will be spicy), sliced
4 large cloves of fresh garlic, minced
Fajita sized flour tortillas
Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
Sour cream
Lettuce, diced
Tomatoes, diced

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together chili powder, garlic, salt, olive oil, 1/4 (not the whole 1/2 cup recipe calls for) cilantro, and lime juice. Add shrimp and toss to coat. Set aside to marinate in fridge for 20 minutes.
Heat a large skillet over med-high heat. Spray with non-stick cooking spray. Add green bell pepper, onion and serrano peppers. Saut√© until tender (about 10-12 minutes). Once tender, remove from pan and set aside in a bowl. Keep warm by covering with foil.
In the same skillet, add shrimp with marinade. Cook shrimp about 2 minutes each side.
Add back the mixture of green bell peppers, onions and Serrano peppers and toss in pan for about 2-3 minutes. Serve with flour tortillas and top with cheese, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes and remaining cilantro.
