Title:
Green Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6207607.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup packed fresh spinach
3/4 cup oats
1/2 cup cottage cheese
1/3 cup applesauce
1 tablespoon chia seeds
1 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
Combine eggs, spinach, oats, cottage cheese, applesauce, chia seeds, and baking powder together in a smoothie blender (such as a NutriBullet(R)); blend until smooth, about 5 minutes.
Pour mixture into the preheated waffle iron in batches if needed, and cook until each waffle is golden brown and the iron stops steaming, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Luke Hubbard
Description: 
These are tasty, healthy waffles with the addition of superfoods spinach and chia. Top with peanut butter, whipped cream and honey, sugar and cinnamon, or choose your favorite toppings.
