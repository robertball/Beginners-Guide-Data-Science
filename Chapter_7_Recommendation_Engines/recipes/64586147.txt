Title:
Mary's Old Southern  Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 deboned chicken
6 fresh eggs
1 can of cream of mushroom (or cream of chicken) soup; (1-10 1/2 oz. can)
1 cup finely chopped Celery
1 cup finely chopped Green bell pepper (other colors of bell pepper changes the taste.)
2 medium sized onions finely chopped
1 Tbsps. dried parsley
2Tbsp of poultry seasoning
2Tbsp of sage
6-7 Saltine crackers
1 cup of whole milk
2 sticks of butter (16 tbsp.)
3-4 Slices of white or wheat Bread
1 lg. pan of cooked cornbread or 2 bags of Pepperidge farm Stuffing mix (original or cornbread flavor)
2-16oz cans of Campbellâ€™s Chicken Broth (if you're cooking a chicken for the dressing use that broth instead)

Directions:
1. Combine soup, celery, bell pepper, onions, parsley, milk, sage, chicken broth, crackers, crumbled
bread slices, and Deboned Chicken in a large bowl and mix all ingredients well.
2. melt butter in the pan the you are going to cook the dressing in leaving enough to keep the
dressing from sticking to the pan, pour the rest into your dressing mixture. (Remember the
butter in the dressing mixture will render into the pan, so adjust butter to fit your pan and
taste)
3.  pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees, put the dressing mixture in the pan and bake until golden
brown. let cool and serve
