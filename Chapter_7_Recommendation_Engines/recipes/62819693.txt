Title:
Tweakable Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c water, divided
1/2 c kosher salt
1/2 c brown sugar
2 bay leaves
1 tsp whole peppercorns
2 tsp granulated garlic
2 Tbsp minced dried onions

Directions:
Bring 2 c water to a boil with salt, sugar, and spices. Remove from heat and let steep for 5 minutes.
Add 2 c of ice water, including ice. When the ice is melted and brine is cold, pour into a large container.
Add boneless skinless chicken breasts or pork chops. Brine for 30 minutes to an hour.
Rinse and pat dry before cooking.

Submitter: 
zeebee
Description: 
Brining makes a difference? Moist and flavorful lean white meat? Yes, it really works! I use a less concentrated solution when i need to leave it in the brine for a longer period of time. Add any dried herbs or spices you wish. Enjoy the moist and flavorful results!
