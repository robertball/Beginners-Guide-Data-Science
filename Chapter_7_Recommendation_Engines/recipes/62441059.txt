Title:
Grandma Jean's Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ½ cups flour
5 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
4 eggs yolks (beaten)
2/3 cup vegetable oil
2 ½ cups milk

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder and salt. In a separate bowl, combine egg yolks, oil and milk and blend into dry ingredients. Fold in 4 beaten egg whites and ENJOY!!
