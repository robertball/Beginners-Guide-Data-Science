Title:
Grilled Peanut Butter, Banana and Chocolate Cones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3686970.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup miniature semi-sweet chocolate morsels, divided
4 sugar cones
1/4 cup Peter PanÂ® Creamy Peanut Butter, divided
1 banana, cut in half lengthwise, sliced
Reddi-wip Original Whipped Light Cream

Directions:
Prepare outdoor grill for medium heat.
Place 1 tablespoon morsels in each cone; top each with 1 tablespoon peanut butter. Divide banana slices among cones.
Loosely wrap each filled cone with aluminum foil. Place cones on side on grill and heat 5 minutes or until morsels melt, turning on other side after 3 minutes.
Remove wrapped cones from grill; let aluminum foil cool. Remove foil and top each with a serving of Reddi-wip.

Submitter: 
Reddi-wip
Description: 
A dessert recipe with chocolate morsels, peanut butter and bananas stuffed in a sugar cone and grilled before topping with Reddi-wip. Recipe developed by George Duran.
