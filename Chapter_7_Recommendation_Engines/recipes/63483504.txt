Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
2 cups dry noodles--cooked
1 cup peas--defrosted
1 can (7oz) tuna--drained and flaked
1 tbs marg
1/3 cup dry bread crumbs

Directions:
combine soup and milk in casserole.  Stir in noodles, tuna, and peas.  Bake 400 degrees F for 25 mins covered.
Brown crumbs in marg.  Top casserole.  Bake 5 mins more uncovered.  May season with curry powder
700 calories total without bread crumbs
