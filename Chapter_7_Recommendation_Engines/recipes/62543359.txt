Title:
Easy Polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
(Can also use 2 cups milk and/or 2 cups chicken broth)
dash of salt
2 cups of cornmeal
2 cups cold water

Directions:
Bring 4 cups of water to a boil.  Whisk together 2 cups cornmeal and 2 cups cold water.
Add this mixture to the boiling water (chicken broth/milk)
Simmer for 20 - 30 minutes until thickened, stirring constantly.
Butter a dish and pour polenta mixture into dish.
The polenta will form to the dish.  Turn upside down and cut into slices.
Can be served with most anything!! Can be topped with cheese, salsa, bacon bits, etc!
