Title:
Venison Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Venison roast
1 can cream of mushroom soup
salt
pepper
pinch rosemary
Worcestershire sauce
1 medium onion, chopped
3 carrots, peeled and chopped
3 potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
2 cups water

Directions:
In slow cooker add Worcestershire sauce, pinch rosemary, salt, pepper on high heat.  Add roast and brown on both sides.  Add water, mushroom soup and cover, cooking on high for 5 to 6 hours.  Add onion, carrots, and potatoes to crock pot and continue to cook for another two hours or until veggies are tender.

Submitter: 
Jeri Wofford
Description: 
Roast done in a slow cooker makes a wonderful dinner for any night of the week or a special dinner for the week-end.
