Title:
Mama's Sunday Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 vidalia onion, chopped
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 bay leaf
2 6 oz. cans tomato paste
1 cup of water
1 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
1 28 oz can whole tomatoes
2 tbs dried basil
2 tbs dried oregano
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
dash of crushed red pepper

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in a sauce pot over medium heat until soft with bay leaf. Be careful not to burn garlic. About 2-3 minutes.
Add tomato paste to onions and slowly add water until the paste is slightly thinned.  (hint: You may not need the entire cup of water.
Add both cans of tomatoes, break the tomatoes up with a spoon and stir.
Add spices to the sauce and bring to a slow boil 5. Lower heat and simmer for at least an hour.
Remove bay leaf
Serve over pasta for an easy delicious tomato sauce.

Submitter: 
Erinn Danna
Description: 
I like to keep this stove top simmering all day for a more intense flavor. This is a pretty basic tomato sauce.  It was the first thing I learned to cooked and it is my family's favorite. Often times I make it with meatballs and sausage.
