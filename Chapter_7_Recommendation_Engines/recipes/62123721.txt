Title:
Perfect Meat and Veggie Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. ground turkey
2 15-oz cans spicy chili beans, drained
2 15-oz cans light red kidney beans, drained
1 can corn, drained
2 15-oz cans diced tomatoes, with juice
1 6-oz can tomato paste
2 c water
1 large onion, chopped
1 zuchini, chopped
1 pkg. mushrooms, chopped
1 c. chopped carrots
3 garlic cloves, minced
4 cubes beef boullion
1/4 c chili powder
1 T sugar
1 T worstechire
1 T hot pepper sauce (I use sriracha)
1/2 T oregano
1/2 T cumin
1/2 T basil
1/2 T cayenne
1 t salt
1 t pepper

Directions:
Brown meat and drain
Put all ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil
Reduce heat and simmer for 2+ hours, stirring occasionally
Serve with crackers, cheddar cheese and sour cream

Submitter: 
Kel Kel
Description: 
EVERYONE loves this chili!  Its loaded with meat, beans and veggies and has the perfect kick to it!
