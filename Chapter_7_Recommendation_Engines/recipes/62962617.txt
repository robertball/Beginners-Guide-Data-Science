Title:
Carrot - Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups quick oats
1 cup flour
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon all spice
1/2 cup canola oil or substitute for applesauce
1/2 cup water
2 egg whites

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Mix: flour, sugars, cinnamon, powder, soda
In measuring cup mix: oil, water & egg whites (add to dry ingredients)
stir in oats & carrots
bake on oiled cookie sheet, 1 inch apart, 10 minutes or until edges are golden brown
