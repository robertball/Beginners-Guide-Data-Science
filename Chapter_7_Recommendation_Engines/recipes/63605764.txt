Ingredients:

1/4 C freshly squeezed lime juice
1/4 C fresh pineapple or orange juice
1/4 C Sriracha
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 T fish sauce
1 tablesppon ginger paste
2 T sugar
Slaw
1.5 # napa cabbage, shredded
1/2 # red cabbage, shredded
2 carrots, peeled and julienned
2 red bell peppers, julienned
1 jalepeno, seeded and minced
6 green onions, white part only, thinly sliced on the diagonal
1/4 C chopped fresdh cilantro

Directions:
Dressing:
In a medium bowl, conbine the peanut butter, lime juice, pineapple juice, Sriracha, garlic, fish sauce, ginger paste, and sugar.  Cover and store in teh refrigerator until you are ready to use it.
Slaw:
In a large bowl, mix together the cabbages, carrots, peppers, green onion, cilantro and mind. Addd the dressing and toss to mix. Season with salt and pepper.
