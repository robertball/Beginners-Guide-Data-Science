Title:
Marinated Broccoli Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups broccoli florets, broken into small pieces
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup Colby/Montery Jack cheese, grated
1/4 cup red onion, sliced
1 T sunflower seeds
1/2 T poppy seeds
1/4 cup vinaigrette, prepared
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp sugar

Directions:
Combine broccoli, tomatoes, cheese, onions, sunflower and poppy seeds in bowl.
Stir together dressing, mustard, and sugar until well blended.
Pour over veggies, toss, and refrigerate at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
This looks like a lovely simple salad.  Requires a good 4 hours of resting time (even better if overnight).
