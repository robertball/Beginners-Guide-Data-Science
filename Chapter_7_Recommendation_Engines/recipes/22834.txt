Title:
Almond Sour Cream Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472309.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup white sugar
1 pinch salt
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1/2 cup heavy cream, whipped

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the sour cream, sugar and salt. Fold in the almonds and whipped cream. Chill in the refrigerator 1 hour or longer before serving.

Submitter: 
GWYNN
Description: 
I got this recipe from a former co-worker.  It is the best dip for fresh fruit I have ever tasted. Slivered almonds are blended into a sweet, thick mixture of pure, creamy goodness.
