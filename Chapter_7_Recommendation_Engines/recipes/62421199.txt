Title:
Mixed Fruit Cobbler with Crunchy Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 peaches
2 nectarines
4 plums
1/2 c packed light brown sugar
4 tsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp nutmeg
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup water
1 Tbs. lemon juice
1 Tbs. butter
For topping:
1 cup flour
1 cup old fashioned oats
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
8 T butter (1 stick), softened
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
In medium saucepan, add 1/2 c brown sugar, 4 tsp cornstarch, nutmeg, 2 tsp cinnamon, and water.  Heat over medium heat, stirring until cornstarch is dissolved.
Wash fruit.  Cut open fruit, remove pits, do NOT peel, just dice fruit. Place in saucepan with sugar mixture and toss to coat.  Cook stirring often, about 10 min until thick and bubbly,and fruit is semi-soft.  Remove from heat, stir in lemon juice and 1 Tbs of butter. Pour into pie plate.
In large bowl, mix flour, oats, 3/4 c brown sugar, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp cinnamon.  Add 8 Tbs. of softened butter and stir until combined.  Mound topping on top of fruit.
Place pie pan on top of cookie sheet and place in middle of oven.  Bake at 325 for about 20 min or until topping is nicely browned.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
oscar'smom
Description: 
I had purchased too many peaches, nectarines and plums. So I thought I would make a cobbler, but I wanted a crunchy top.  This recipe was born and it's been a hit at home and at work ever since.  Use very ripe fruit.
