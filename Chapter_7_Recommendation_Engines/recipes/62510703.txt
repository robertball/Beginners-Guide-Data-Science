Title:
Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
2 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup corn starch
canola oil
Sauce:
3/4 cup sugar
4 Tbsp. ketchup
1/2 cup vinegar
1 Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2 tsp. garlic salt

Directions:
Place beaten eggs in one bowl and cornstarch in another bowl. Dip whole chicken breasts in eggs and then cornstarch. Fry in oil until brown (chicken doesn't have to be cooked).
Mix sweet and sour sauce together in a bowl. Place chicken on 9 x 13 pan and pour sauce over top.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes, turning chicken every 10 minutes.
