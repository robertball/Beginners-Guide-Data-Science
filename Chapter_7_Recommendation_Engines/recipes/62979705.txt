Title:
Nana's Molasses Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups soft shortening
2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup molasses
4.5 cups flour
4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cloves
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp ground ginger
Additional sugar

Directions:
Mix together shortening, brown sugar, eggs, and molasses.
Sift together the flour, baking soda, salt, ground cloves, cinnamon, and ginger.
Stir/mix sifted ingredients to wet ingredients.
Once ingredients are mixed, wrap dough in plastic wrap and allow to chill in refrigerator for approximately one hour.
Roll dough into walnut sized balls and roll in additional sugar.
Place balls 3" apart on lightly greased baking sheet and place 2-3 drops of water on each ball to produce crackled look.
Bake in peheated 350 degree oven for 10-12 minutes.
