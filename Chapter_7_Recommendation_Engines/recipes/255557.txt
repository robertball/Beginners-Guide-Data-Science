Title:
Pink Champagne Mocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6833973.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts ginger ale
1 (46 fluid ounce) bottle cranberry juice
1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
ice cubes

Directions:
Combine ginger ale, cranberry juice, pineapple juice, and orange juice concentrate in a large punch bowl. Stir in ice.
