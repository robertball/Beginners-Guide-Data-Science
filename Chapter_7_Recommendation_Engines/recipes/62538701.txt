Title:
Mom's tomato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons butter/margarine
1 cup chopped celery
1 large onion, chopped
1 28 ounce can plum tomatoes, chopped
2 Tablespoons chopped fresh or dried basil
1 Tablespoon fresh or dried oregano
1 Tabelspoon fresh or dried thyme
2 Tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 bay leaves
4 cups fresh or canned beef broth
Sugar
Salt
Fresh ground black pepper
1 Cup shaved, not grated, parmesan

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat, add celery and onion and cook until the celery is tender, the onion transparent, about 7--10 minutes
Add tomatoes, basil, oregano, thyme, parsley, bay leaves, and stock. Bring to boil. Simmer about 30 minutes. Remove bay leaves. If you like, you can puree the soup, but it's awesome with the lumps. Season to taste with sugar, salt and pepper. Garnish with Parmesan/Romano.

Description: 
Best served hot, when it's cold outside and you're ready to just curl up on the couch for a day of movies.
