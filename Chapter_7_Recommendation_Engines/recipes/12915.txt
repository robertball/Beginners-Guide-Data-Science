Title:
Spicy Potato Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/712487.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes, cubed
2 red bell peppers, seeded and diced
1 1/2 serrano chiles, finely chopped
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1 dash paprika
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
8 cups water

Directions:
In a saucepan, fry potatoes in oil until golden brown.
Stir in peppers, water, and seasonings.  Cover, and simmer until potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Felicia Martinez
Description: 
This savory soup will warm you on the coldest day, and can help you chase away head colds.
