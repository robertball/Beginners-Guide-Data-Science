Title:
Stuffed Chicken (cordon blu style) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Skinless boneless chicken breast
2 slices pre-cooked ham
8 oz cream cheese
2 oz mozzarella cheese
Garlic powder (to taste)
1/8 cup Bread crumbs (for topping)

Directions:
Cut pocket in chicken (straight cut from the top, down the center, being careful not to cut through, then cut to the left and right, creating a baked potato style pocket) Mix cream cheese, mozzarella cheese, and garlic into a paste.  Line the bottom of chicken pockent with ham, fill pocket with cream cheese mixture.  Top with bread crumbs and back at 350 for approximately 45-60 minutes until done.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
Great way to use that left over ham.
