Title:
Vegan Pillows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1067975.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of sifted all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon of baking powder
A small pinch of salt
2 tablespoons of Vegan Butter
3/4 cups of water
Canola oil (for frying)
Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Directions:
Put dry ingredients in a bowl.
Fork (smash) the butter into the dry ingredients.
Add water into dry ingredients.
Stir with a wooden spoon (Result will be a dough).
Flour a flat surface (to avoid sticking).
Take out your dough, and roll it (no more than a 1/4 inch thick).
Cut the rolled dough into 1 inch rectangles (or really, any desired shape)
Fry in oil until golden.
Dust with powdered sugar and enjoy!
