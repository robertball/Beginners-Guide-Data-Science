Title:
Simple Sweet and Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup white vinegar
1 cup ketchup
1 cup water
red pepper flakes to taste (optional)

Directions:
Mix together sugar, vinegar, ketchup and water in a sauce pan.
Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until thick and bubbly (about 1 hour - thickens more when cooled).
Add red pepper flakes to taste (optional)

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
This Sweet and Sour Sauce is easy to make with ingredients normally on hand. Add a little crushed red pepper flakes for some hot, sweet and sour. Serve as a side for dipping or on ham or pork.
