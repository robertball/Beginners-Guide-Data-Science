Title:
Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 package taco seasoning
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chili peppers
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
9 (6 inch) corn tortillas
1 (19 ounce) can enchilada sauce
1 (6 ounce) can sliced black olives
16 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly oil a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain.
Add taco seasoning to ground beef.
In a medium bowl, combine the ground beef, garlic, onion, half of the cheese, chili peppers and jalapeno pepper.
Pour enchilada sauce into a shallow bowl.
Dip three tortillas in the enchilada sauce, and place them in the prepared baking dish. Be sure to cover the bottom of the dish as completely as possible.
Place 1/2 of the beef mixture on top of the tortillas
Dip more three tortillas in the enchilada sauce, and place them on top of the mixture.
Put the second half of the mixture on top of the tortillas.
Dip more three tortillas in the enchilada sauce, and place them on top of the mixture.
Drizzle the remaining sauce over the casserole, and sprinkle with olives and shredded cheese.
Cover, and bake for 30 minutes. Uncover, and continue baking for an additional 15 minutes or until the casserole is bubbling and the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Walter Masten
Description: 
No wrapping or burning of fingers necessary.  This easy dish may take some time, but is well worth it.
