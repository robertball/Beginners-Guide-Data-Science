Title:
Apple Sauce with Red Hot Candies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5938226.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs. wine sap apples, peeled, cored and cut up.
1/2 c. white sugar or less if you like tart
1 package red hot candies (Valentine's Candy)
1/4 c. water
1/2 t. cinnamon
1/4 t. nutmeg
1/4 t. ground cloves
1/4 t. allspice

Directions:
Mix sugar, water and red hots over med to low heat until red hots are melted.
Add apples and spices
Cook on med heat to reduce liquid about 20 - 30 minutes
Mash with potato masher leaving chunks.
Pack in prepared canning jars if you want to save for later use as you would any canning project.
