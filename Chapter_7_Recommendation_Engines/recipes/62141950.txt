Title:
Karen's Best Seafood Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs. Butter
Scallion Greens -- small bunch, chopped
One (1) can whole baby clams
Two (2) cans Creamed Corn
Cream Cheese -- 8 oz. block
1/2 tsp. Old Bay Seasoning
Two (2) cans Cream of Mushroom soup
Two (2) cups Half-and-Half
1/2 lb. baby bay scallops
1/2 lb. crab meat (real or imitation)
1/2 lb. mock Lobster meat
salt and pepper
Fresh Italian parsley sprigs (optional)

Directions:
Saute scallion greens in butter until soft.
Add juice from one can baby clams -- reserve clams for later.
Allow mixture to boil, then simmer for 5 minutes or until liquid is reduced by half.
Add both cans Creamed Corn and cream cheese. Heat until mixture is smooth.
Add Old Bay seasoning and soups. Mix well and continue to simmer over medium-low heat. Gradually mix in Half-and-Half (increase or decrease amount according to your own tastes), and bring to a second low boil.
Add left over baby clams, scallops, crab and mock lobster. Simmer until scallops are cooked through (about 5 minutes).
Salt and pepper to taste.
Garnish with fresh snipped Italian-style parsly sprigs if desired.

Submitter: 
KINCATT
Description: 
This is quick and easy. Very thick and seafood choices can be as varied as the individuals cooking it! A great dish to experiment with!
