Title:
Yummy choc chip cookie cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet of choc chip golden cookies
1 can of small orange or clementine segments
whipped cream
chocolate flakes

Directions:
Pour the fruit juice from the can into a bowel.
Put a cookie into the juice for a few secs until soft.
Then put some cream on the soft cookie and one piece of fruit and then sandwich with another soft cookie.
Repeat this over for all cookies and place in a log shape on a plate.
When finished cover with cream and grated chocolate or chocolate flakes.
Chill in fridge
