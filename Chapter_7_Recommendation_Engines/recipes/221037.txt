Title:
Ham Pate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/998045.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces cubed cooked ham
2 tablespoons ranch salad dressing (such as Hidden Valley® Original Ranch®)
1 tablespoon capers with juice
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
2 teaspoons finely chopped onion
1 pinch minced garlic

Directions:
Place ham, ranch dressing, capers, mustard, onion, and garlic in a food processor; process mixture to your desired consistency.
