Title:
Velvet Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 cup extra virgin coconut oil, room temperature (no substitutions*see footnotes)
1/2 cup butter, softened
3 cups white sugar
5 eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup milk
1/4 tsp salt
3 tablespoons vanilla extract
1 tablespoon almond extract
1 tablespoon lemon zest
Glaze (Optional):
1 cup powdered sugar
3-4 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tsp lemon zest

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.  Grease and flour 10" bundt pan.
In a small bowl mix flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.  Place sour cream and 1/2 cup milk into mixing cup, add vanilla, stir well to incorporate, set aside.
In large mixing bowl, cream coconut oil, butter and sugar until light and fluffy by mixing on medium speed.  Add eggs,one at a time mixing well after each addition. Add lemon zest, stirring well.  Beat in flour mixture alternately with sour cream mixture, beginning and ending with flour mixture. Spoon mixture into prepared pan.
Bake for 1 1/2 hours or until cake tests done.
Prepare glaze.  Mix together glaze ingredients, stirring well.
Cool cake on wire rack for 10 minutes.  Turn out onto plate. Spoon 1/2 of the glaze onto hot cake.  Let cool and drizzle remaining glaze onto cooled cake before serving.

Submitter: 
Shannon Clayton Flanagan
Description: 
Extra virgin coconut oil is the key to the velvety smooth texture of this cake. It's moist but with a fine crumb, and every bit as rich as a pound cake should be. Don't expect a coconut taste, it gently disappears into the background.
