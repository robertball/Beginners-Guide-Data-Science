Title:
Cinnamon Roll (Bisquick type) Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1142509.jpg
Ingredients:
PANCAKES:
1 cup milk
2 eggs
2 cups Bisquick
2 T lemon juice
4 t sugar
2 t baking powder
CINNAMON FILlING:
4 T butter, melted
1/4 + 2 T brown sugar
1/2 T cinnamon
CREAM CHEESE TOPPING:
2 oz cream cheese, room temp
3/4 cup powdered sugar
4 T butter
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Remove milk early, add lemon juice, and bring to room temp to curdle
Make cinnamon filling by mixing all together and tossing into baggie.
Make cream cheese topping by melting butter in small pan, then whisking all other ingredients in.  Remove from heat and set aside until pancakes are ready.
Mix pancake ingredients together with whisk stirring only until ingredients incorporated (lumps are okay)
Pour onto hot griddle and wait until bubbles begin to form.
Snip end of cinnamon filling baggy and (starting in middle) create a circular pattern moving from middle out = don't get too close to edges of pancake tho.
Flip pancakes over and continue to cook.  Once done, serve topped with cream cheese topping.
