Title:
Air Fryer Lemon Pepper Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5619976.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon lemon pepper seasoning
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
2 lemons, divided use
12 oz. large (16 to 22 count) shrimp

Directions:
In a bowl mix olive oil, lemon pepper seasoning, paprika, garlic powder and juice of one lemon. Add shrimp to the bowl and toss until coated.
Place shrimp in a 400 degree air fryer for 6 to 8 minutes, just until shrimp has turned pink and is firm.
Slice the remaining lemon for drizzling and serve with the shrimp.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
These easy to make shrimp can be served with a salad, on top of rice or pasta. Also great as a cold appetizer.
