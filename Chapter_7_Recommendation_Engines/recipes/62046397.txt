Title:
Grandma Ruby's Buttermilk Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup shortening
3 cups sugar
1 cup buttermilk
6 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. butter extract
2 tbsp. lemon extract

Directions:
Heat oven to 325.  Grease and flour a bundt pan.
Sift the flour, soda and salt 3 times.  Cream the shortening and sugar together until smooth.  Add the flour, buttermilk, eggs and extracts and mix until well blended.  Do not overmix.
Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.  Let cool in pan for 15 minutes and invert onto cooling rack.

Submitter: 
James Buddy Clower
Description: 
This lemon, butter poundcake is super moist and dense.  It's not for those watching calories!  It's a great comfort food and goes especially well with vanilla ice cream.
