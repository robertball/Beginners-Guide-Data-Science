Title:
Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (30 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 onion, chopped
1 green and/or red pepper chopped
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the cheese, sour cream and soup. Add the hash browns.
In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the onion and peppers with 1 stick butter and saute for 5 minutes. Add this to the soup and potato mixture. Spread into a 9 X 13 pan. Bake at 425 for about an hour or until browned.
