Title:
Nuts 'n' Berries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 oz. almond liquor
1/2 oz. raspberry liquor
1/2 oz. irish cream liquor
1.5 oz. half & half
Ice Cubes

Directions:
Pour all ingredients into a chilled shaker filled with ice.
Shake until completely cold.
Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice cubes or into chilled shot glasses.

Submitter: 
AZ
Description: 
This drink was a favorite ladies cocktail when I was bartending, but even more than that it also makes a great party shot.
