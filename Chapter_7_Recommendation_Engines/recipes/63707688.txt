Title:
African Peanut Crockpot Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, diced
1 green pepper, diced
1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed
1 1/2 tsp curry powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp garam masala
1 tsp cumin
1 14oz can diced tomatoes
1 can coconut milk
2 cups vegetable stock
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup red lentils

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients into a slow cooker and stir. Cook on high for 4-5 hours or low for 8-10 hours.

Submitter: 
Daniel den Hoed
Description: 
This African stew is a hit among peanut lovers in my family! It has a complex flavor but is quick and easy to prepare.
