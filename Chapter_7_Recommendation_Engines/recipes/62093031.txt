Title:
Pudding Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pudding Shots
1 small pkg. INSTANT choc. pudding
3/4 C. skim milk
1/4 C. Vodka
1/2 C. Irish Cream
8 oz. Extra Creamy Cool Whip - thawed

Directions:
Mix pudding and milk for a couple of minutes with an electric mixer, then add alcohol, mix well. Mix in Cool Whip.
Put into individual serving cups (Dixie Cups). Keep in the freezer.  Batch can be doubled.

Submitter: 
Tina McKellar Musial
Description: 
This is a great cool treat on a hot day - for adults!  Make a separate virgin batch for the kids!  Plan ahead - needs to freeze.
