Title:
Steak N Shake Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 can (10.5 oz) Campbell's French onion soup
2 cans (15 oz) chili beans in mild sauce
1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
1 cup Dr Pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 tablespoon chili powder
2 teaspoons cocoa

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet with salt. Drain.
Blend onion soup in a blender for a couple of minutes.
Put all ingredients in a crock pot, and simmer on low for 6 hours.
