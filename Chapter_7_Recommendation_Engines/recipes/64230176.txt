Title:
Shorecook's Cranberry Enhanced Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1686395.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can sliced apples (27.16 ounces)
1/4 cup cranberries
1/2 cup butter (cold)
3/4 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 gal. vanilla ice cream (optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Butter a square glass baking dish (8 in) and set aside.
Drain apple slices and place in baking dish.   Randomly place cranberries amongst the apples.
In a food processor place butter, sugar, and flour.  Process until pea sized crumbs are formed stopping just short of it being cookie dough.
Cover the apples with the crumb mixture.
Bake in preheated oven until golden brown  (approximately 45 minutes).
Once crumb mixture is browned, remove from oven and sprinkle with cinnamon.
Serve warm topped with a scoop of ice cream.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
This is an easy way to enjoy a yummy dessert that looks like you spent hours in the kitchen.   Using canned sliced apples,  makes this a breeze to put together.  Topped with vanilla ice cream makes it a winner!
