Title:
Bacon Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/841157.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Russet potatoes
4 eggs
4 Slices of bacon cooked and crumbled
1/4 cup pickles (finely chopped)
2 tbsp chopped parsley
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1/3 cup fat free ranch dressing
2 tbsp light mayonnaise
1 tbsp mustard
1/4 cup finely shredded cheddar cheese
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
Peel and cube the potatoes, boil until tender, but not falling apart.
Boil eggs until hard boiled. (About 10 minutes after water has reached a boiling point)
Drain potatoes and place in a large bowl.
Remove shells from the eggs and chop the eggs.
Add eggs to potatoes.
Add bacon, pickles, green onion,cheese, salt and pepper.
In a medium bowl combine ranch dressing, mayonnaise, and mustard.
Add to potato mixture and mix well.
Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
