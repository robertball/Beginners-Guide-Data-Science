Title:
Classic Chocolate Whoopie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie:
1 2/3 cups of All Purpose Flour
2/3 cups of Un-Sweetened Cocoa powder
1 ½ tsp. of Baking Soda
½ tsp. of Salt
4 tbsp. of Unsalted Butter (Room Temperature)
4 tbsp. of Vegetable Shortening
1 cup of dark brown sugar (packed)
1 Large Egg
1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
1 ¼ cup of milk
Marshmallow:
1 ½ cups of Marshmallow Fluff
1 ¼ cups of Vegetable Shortening
1 cup of Confectioners’ Sugar
1 tbsp. of vanilla extract

Directions:
Pies:
1.	Put rack in the center of the oven and preheat to 375 F.
2.	Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
3.	Sift together flour, cocoa powder, and baking soda on a sheet of waxed paper.
4.	In work bowl beat butter, shortening, and brown sugar until just combined. Beat on medium for 3 minutes. Add egg and vanilla and beat for 2 more minutes.
5.	Add half of flour mixture and half of milk to batter. Beat briefly on low, and then beat in remaining flour and milk.
6.	Using a spoon, drop 1 tbsp. at a time onto 1 of the baking sheets. Space at least 2 inches apart.
7.	Bake 1 sheet at a time for about 10 minutes or until pies spring back when pressed gently. Remove and let cool for 5 minutes, then move to rack to cool.
Filling:
Beat fluff and shortening for 3 minutes on medium. Reduce speed to low, add sugar and vanilla. Beat until incorporated. Increase speed to medium; beat until fluffy. Put filling in between pies.
