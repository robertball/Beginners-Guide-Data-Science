Title:
KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS Southwest Chicken and Bell Pepper Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034329.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16.7 ounce) package KRAFT Recipe Makers Hickory BBQ Beef
2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
3 green peppers, cut into thin strips
8 (6 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker; top with peppers.  Add Simmer Sauce; cover.
Cook on LOW 6 to 8 hours (or on HIGH 3 to 4 hours).
15 min. before serving, remove chicken from slow cooker; place on cutting board.  Cool 10 min.; shred with 2 forks. Return to slow cooker. Stir in Finishing Sauce; cook, covered, on HIGH 15 min.
Serve in warmed tortillas.

Submitter: 
KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS
Description: 
Chicken thighs and strips of bell pepper are slow-cooked in a hickory barbeque sauce, blended with prepared finishing sauce, and served on warm tortillas.
