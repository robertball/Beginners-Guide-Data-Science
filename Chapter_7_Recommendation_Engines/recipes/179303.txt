Title:
Kings Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987128.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Crown Royal
2 ounces heated cider
1/4 ounce honey
1/4 ounce lemon juice
1 dash sprinkle cinnamon
1 piece cinnamon stick

Directions:
Serve warm in a coffee mug.
Sprinkle cinnamon on top and garnish with cinnamon stick.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Crown Royal whisky and cinnamon spice up this warm cider drink.
