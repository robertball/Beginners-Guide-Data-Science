Title:
Avocado Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2233834.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 medium avocado
1/2 lime of juice
1-2 teaspoons course brown mustard
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 cup cooked chicken breast, chopped
1/2 cup chopped pre-mixed vegetable bag
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl, mash the avocado with the lime juice and mix in the brown mustard, garlic powder, and salt and pepper. Mix in the chopped chicken breast and chopped veggies.
