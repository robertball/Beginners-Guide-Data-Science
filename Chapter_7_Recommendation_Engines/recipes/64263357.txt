Title:
Mini Pecan Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1832229.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 tablespoon almond meal
1 tablespoon milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 3500 degrees F.
In a large bowl, beat eggs until foamy, and stir in melted butter.  Stir in the brown sugar, white sugar and the flour; mix well. Last add the milk, vanilla and nuts.
Divide the pie crust into 12-24 small balls. Press them into the cups of two 6 cup cupcake pans or two 12 cup mini cupcake pans to make tartlet shells. Evenly pour filling into mini formed unbaked pie crusts. Bake at 350 degrees and bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until done.

Submitter: 
Amethyst Douglas
Description: 
A pecan pie without the corn syrup. Pecans, white sugar, and brown sugar are combined to create a satisfying dessert.Modified from: Pecan Pie V
