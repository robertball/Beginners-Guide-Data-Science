Title:
Caramel Ice Cream Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup white corn syrup
1 cube margarine/butter
1 Tbsp. water
2 cans sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Put brown sugar, corn syrup, butter and water in pan and bring to a full boil. Add both cans of sweetened condensed milk. Stir. Pour over ice cream and serve.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
For 1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream.
