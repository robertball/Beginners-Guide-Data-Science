Title:
Chili Con Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2 pound brick of Velveeta
2 cans chilli with no beans

Directions:
Put chili in food processor and blend until no large pieces remain
Melt cheese in crockpot or on stove over med. heat stirring constantly
Add chilli heat through
Tip: if mixture is to thick add a little milk and stir

Submitter: 
Kristy
Description: 
Great dip for tortilla chips, like dips served at national food chains 2 ingredients make this the easist to prepare
