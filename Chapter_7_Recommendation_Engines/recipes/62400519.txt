Title:
Buca's Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes; unpeeled (about 8 medium potatoes)
1/4 cup garlic; chopped (can use less)
1 tablespoon oregano
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup Romano cheese
1 cup butter; softened (two sticks)

Directions:
Wash potatoes and place in a large saucepan, cover with cold water.
Over medium heat, bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and continue cooking until potatoes are very soft.
Transfer potatoes to a large mixing bowl and add garlic, oregano, salt, cheese and butter.
With potato masher, mash potatoes until all ingredients are incorporated.
