Title:
Grapes-Orange-Apple-Avocado & Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/974448.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Grapes
2 Oranges
2 Apples (pealed)
1 Avocado
2 Cups Baby Spinach
1 Cup Red Cabbage
2 Dates
1/2 Cup Water
12 Ice Cubes

Directions:
Put Water, Ice, Soft Fruits, Hard Fruits, Vegetables, Dates in a blender in the order written. Start to blend slow increasing power to high. Blend for 1 minute.
