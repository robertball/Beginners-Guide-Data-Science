Title:
Chicago Style Deep Dish Pepperoni Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/974796.jpg
Ingredients:
For the dough:
1½ cups plus 2 tbsp. (8 1/8 oz.) all-purpose flour
¼ cup (1 3/8 oz.) yellow cornmeal
¾ tsp. salt
1 tsp. sugar
1 1/8 tsp. instant yeast
½ cup plus 2 tbsp. (5 oz.) water, at room temperature
1½ tbsp. unsalted butter, melted
1 tsp. olive oil
2 tbsp. unsalted butter, softened
For the sauce:
1 tbsp. unsalted butter
2 tbsp. grated onion
Pinch of dried oregano
¼ tsp. salt
1 clove garlic, minced or pressed
1 (14.5 oz) can crushed tomatoes
Pinch of sugar
2 tbsp. fresh basil leaves, coarsely chopped
1½ tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
Ground black pepper
For assembling and topping:
2 tbsp. olive oil
8 oz. mozzarella, shredded (about 2 cups)
¼ oz. Parmesan cheese, grated (about 2 tbsp.)

Directions:
Dough: combine flour, cornmeal, salt, sugar & yeast in bowl of stand mixer w/  dough hook on low speed.  Mix until blended, about 1 min. Add water & melted butter & continue on low speed until fully incorporated, 1-2 min, scraping the sides & bottom of bowl.   Increase speed to med-low & knead until dough is glossy & smooth, & pulls away from sides of the bowl, 4-5 min.  Coat med bowl with 1 tsp of olive oil.  Transfer dough to bowl, turning once to coat. Cover tightly w/ plastic wrap.  Let rise at room temp until doubled, 45-60 min.
While dough is rising, prepare sauce.  Melt butter in saucepan over med heat.  Add onion, oregano & salt.  Cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid is evaporated & onion is golden brown.  Add garlic & cook just until fragrant, about 30 sec. Stir in tom & sugar & increase heat to med-high.  Bring mixture to simmer, then lower the heat to med-low & simmer until reduced to about 1¼ cups, about 25 min. Stir in basil & oil.  Season with salt & pepper to taste.
To laminate dough, turn onto dry surface & roll into 8x6" rectangle. Spread butter over dough, leaving ½" border around edges.  Starting at short end, roll dough into tight cylinder.  W/ seam side down, roll  into 9x2" rectangle.  Fold into thirds like a business letter, pinch seams to form ball.  Return to bowl.  Cover tightly w/ plastic wrap & let rise in refrig until doubled, 40-50 min.
Preheat oven to 425°.  Coat 9" round cake pan with 2 TBL oil. Transfer dough to dry work surface & roll into 13" disk abt ¼" thick.  Press dough into pan, working into corners & 1" up sides.  If dough resists stretching, let rest 5 min.
Sprinkle mozzarella over dough.  Spread sauce over cheese & top with Parmesan.  Bake until crust is golden brown, 20-30 min.  Let rest 10 min before slicing.
