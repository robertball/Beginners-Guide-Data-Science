Title:
Orange Roughy with lemon, Dijon Mustard and butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBS Dijon mustard
3 TBS lemon juice
2 TBS butter or margarine
1/8 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp garlic powder
4-6 fish fillets (orange rought, flounder, etc.)
PAM

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, stir in the first six ingredients
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Place the fish fillets in a shallow baking pan coated with PAM
Brush the filllets with lemon juice mixture
Bake in oven for 25 minutes
Sppon any remaining pan juices over fish and serve
