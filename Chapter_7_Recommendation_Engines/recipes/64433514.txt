Title:
Rockin' Apricot and Coconut Breakfast Smoothie - Wheat and Oat Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2338563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
2 fresh apricots, pitted
1 banana cut into chunks
1/2 cup plain yogurt
2 teaspoons ground flax seed
2 teaspoons rice bran
2 walnuts, shelled
2 large pitted dates

Directions:
Put all the ingredients in a blender and blend until creamy and smooth.
