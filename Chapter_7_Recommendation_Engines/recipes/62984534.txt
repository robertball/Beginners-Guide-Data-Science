Title:
Belmont sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp butter
1/3 cup ketchup
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 drops of liquid hot peper
1 tsp Dijon-style mustard
Â¼ tsp sat
Dash of pepper
Dash of nutmeg
2 Tbsp dry sherry

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan.
Blend in ketchup, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, liquid hot pepper, mustard, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.  Blend well and heat to a simmer.
Add dry sherry and heat to boiling.  Stir for a minute, then serve hot.

Submitter:

Description: 
This tangy sauce is a perfect companion with steaks and other beef dishes.
