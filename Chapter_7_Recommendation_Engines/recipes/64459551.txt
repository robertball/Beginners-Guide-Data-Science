Title:
Cheddar Herb Pull-Apart Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can large flaky biscuit dough
3 tbs melted butter
1 tbs fresh parsley
1 tsp fresh thyme
1 tsp fresh garlic, chopped
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup cheddar, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Mix butter, herbs, garlic, salt & pepper. Open biscuits and separate so they are half as thick as normal. Dip both side of each piece of the biscuit into the butter mixture and set aside. Spray a small bundt pan or cake pan.Layer the biscuit pieces in the bottom of the pan with the grated cheese between each layer. Bake for 15 minutes. Turn bread out onto a serving platter. Serve warm.
