Title:
Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2537585.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Elbow Macaroni
1 Green Pepper - Chopped
1 onion - chopped
4 carrots - chopped
1 14oz. can Sweetened Condensed Milk (I use the green can)
1 c. Vinegar
1/4 c. Sugar
15oz. Jar Mayo
1 t. Salt
1/4 t. Pepper

Directions:
Cook, cool and drain macaroni. Add, green pepper, onion, and carrots.
Combine: Milk, sugar, mayo, vinegar, salt & pepper. Whisk together, pour over macaroni. It will be soupy. Mix well. Chill at least 4 hours.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jannie Anderson Gomoll
Description: 
My sister lived in Kansas for a while and she got this recipe from a restaurant. It makes the best macaroni salad ever! Give it a try, you won't be disappointed.
