Title:
Sweet Potato, Corn & Andouille Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (14oz) cans vegetable broth
1 mid-sized sweet potato, peeled & cubed
3 links of andouille sausage, cubed
1 tsp Cajun seasoning
1 tsp peanut/vegetable oil
1 can corn or 1 small pkg. frozen corn

Directions:
Heat broth and add potato. Bring to boil and cook approximately 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, sprinkle sausage with Cajun seasoning and brown in peanut/vegetable oil in heavy skillet over medium heat. When slightly browned, drain and set aside.
Once potato is tender, but not mushy, add sausage and corn to broth. Bring to boil and serve.

Submitter: 
Cheryl W.
Description: 
My husband had a soup similar to this in a restaurant and loved it. Since they would not share the recipe, this is my interpretation.
