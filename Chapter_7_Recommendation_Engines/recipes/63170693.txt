Title:
Garlic Expressions Dressing and Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ c. + 1 T. Cider Vinegar
3 Tablespoons sugar
1 t. salt
3 cloves minced garlic
1 t. dried basil
½ t. Italian seasoning
3 Tablespoons water
¾ c. canola oil
5-6 whole cloves garlic

Directions:
Blend (for a creamier dressing) or whisk first 7 ingredients.  Then, slowly blend or whisk in the canola oil.
Store in bottle with whole garlic cloves in refrigerator.
Shake well before serving.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
My favorite salad dressing is Garlic Expressions, but it is so expensive.  This is my best effort in duplicating.  You can either blend it for a creamier consistancy or whisk for a more liquid dressing.  Great to use as a marinade for chicken too.  Hope you enjoy it.
