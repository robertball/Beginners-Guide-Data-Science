Title:
Gluten-Free Drop Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4557932.jpg
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 cups gluten free all-purpose flour blend
1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 Tbsp powdered skim milk
1/4 cup butter (57g), cold and cut into cubes
1 egg, cold
6 Tbsp buttermilk, cold

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 230°C. Prepare a baking tray with parchment paper.
Whisk together the egg and buttermilk, set aside in the refrigerator.
Mix together all the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Using your fingers, "cut in" the butter leaving pieces of butter about the size of peanuts.
Make a well in the flour mixture and add the egg/buttermilk mixture. Mix on low speed in a mixer until just combined. The mixture should be more of a batter, if it isn't add more buttermilk.
Using a wet spoon, drop scoops of batter (about 2 inches in diameter) on the prepared baking sheet.
Bake at 230°C for 5 minutes and turn down the heat to 215°C. Continue baking for another 7 minutes or until the biscuits are golden brown. Remove from the oven and serve warm. Store extra biscuits in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Gluten free version of country biscuits with no need to roll out and cut them.
