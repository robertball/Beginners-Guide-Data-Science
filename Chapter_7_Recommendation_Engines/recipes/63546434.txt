Title:
Croque Monseuir Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 inch thick slices bread
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 pound thinly sliced ham
1/2 cup grated swiss (or gruyere)
2 eggs
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Place the bread slices on a baking sheet and spread with the ricotta cheese. Top each slice with ham, sprinkle with swiss cheese. Place sheet on middle rack in the oven, and bake until the cheese is bubbling and golden and the bread is crispy, about 10 minutes.
While the sandwiches are toasting, fry the 2 eggs to desired done-ness.
Remove the bread from the oven, and top each piece with an egg. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and serve immediatly.
