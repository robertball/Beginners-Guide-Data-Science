Title:
Hershey's "Perfectly Chocolate" Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter or margarine
2/3 c. Hershey’s cocoa
3 c. powdered sugar
1/3 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Melt butter. Stir in cocoa. Alternately add the powdered sugar and milk, beating on medium speed to spreading consistency. Add more milk, if needed. Stir in vanilla.
Frost cake.

Submitter: 
hobbyzu
Description: 
Delicious frosting to go with the cake of the same name. From the Hershey’s cocoa container.
