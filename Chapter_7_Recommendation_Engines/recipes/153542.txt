Title:
COLLEGE INN® One-Pot Noodles with Turkey and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978071.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups COLLEGE INN® Turkey Broth (99% Fat Free)
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
3 3/4 cups wide egg noodles, uncooked
2 cups frozen or fresh tiny broccoli florets
1 1/2 cups cooked turkey, diced
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In 6-quart saucepan, combine broth, milk and butter; heat to boiling.
Stir in uncooked noodles and broccoli; return to a boil. Reduce heat to medium; cover. Cook 7 to 8 minutes, stirring frequently, or until noodles are tender and most liquid is absorbed.
Stir in turkey; remove from heat.
Stir in cheese.

Submitter: 
COLLEGE INN® Broth
Description: 
This quick noodle dish uses leftover turkey and frozen broccoli for a delicious, filling weeknight meal.
