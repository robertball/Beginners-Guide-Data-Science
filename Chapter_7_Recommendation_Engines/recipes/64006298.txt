Title:
Sweet n' Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. Ground Beef                                1 med. Onion, finely chopped                         Salt & Pepper, seasonings to taste                   2 Eggs                                               4 Tbls. Flour                                        1/2 tsp. salt                                        3 Green peppers,diced                                       1 1/2 C. Beef broth or Boullion                      1/2 C Vinegar                                        1/2 C.Sugar                                          1 Tbls. Soy Sauce                                    1 1/2 C. Pineapple Chunks                            3 Tbls. Cornstarch                                   Oil for frying

Directions:
Combine Ground Beef, onions, and seasonings. Make into small balls about 1 inch through. Set aside on wax paper. In separate bowl: mix eggs, flour,and 1/2 tsp. salt. Dip meatballs in batter and brown in oil. Remove from pan to warm  ovenware dish. Pour off oil leaving 2 Tbls. in pan. Into this dice 3 Green peppers, adding beef broth, vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, and pineapple chunks. Cook together 10 minutes. Mix Cornstarch and add to mixture, sauce will be thin. Add meatballs and cook together 10 minutes over low heat with lid on pan. Remember: meatballs must be small. Delicious served with rice, veg, and rolls. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Janet Shum
Description: 
My Mom used to make this Sweet n' Sour dish, it was always a favorite back in the day. I hope you enjoy this yummy dish!
