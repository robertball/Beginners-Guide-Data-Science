Title:
Baked Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lime
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
6 tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
1. Mix lime juice, olive oil and salt in a bowl
2. Brush mixture on 6 tortillas
3. Bake for 4 minutes
4. Flip over and bake for another 4 minutes
