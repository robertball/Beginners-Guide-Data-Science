Title:
Lemon Broccoli Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
3/4 c cream (or half & half)
3/4 c mayonnaise
2 tsp cornstarch
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp soy sauce
1/8 tsp salt
2 tsp lemon juice
Crushed red pepper (to taste)
Pepper to taste
1 1/2 c grated cheese (swiss or your choice)
8 oz can sliced water chestnuts
Fresh broccoli

Directions:
Combine ingredients except broccoli.
Line unbaked 9-inch pie shell with cut broccoli (bite size pieces).
Pour egg/cheese mixture over all and make sure the broccoli is covered.
Sprinkle the top lightly with paprika (I like to used smoked, but plain is fine).
Bake 350 degrees 35-40 minutes.
Let stand 15-20 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Hearty broccoli quiche the whole family loves.  Freezes well.  Reheats well (gently in the microwave).
