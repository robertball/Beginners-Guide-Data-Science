Title:
Fast, Easy, Elegant - Summer Zucchini Side Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1038560.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes (any type), skins left on, scrubbed and diced
1 large zucchini (approximately 4 cups, skin on, diced)
1 small onion, diced
1/4 c. extra-virgin olive oil
1 Tbsp yellow curry powder
1 tsp. sea salt (or table salt)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Spray a 9 x 13 pan with cooking spray or line with parchment paper.
Place all of the above ingredients into a large mixing bowl and stir to blend well.
Pour into prepared baking pan.
Bake for one hour, or until vegetables are to desired doneness, stirring every 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Whether I'm in a hurry, having company, or just overjoyed that it's zucchini season, this is our all-time favorite side dish.  Toss some meat on the grill and you have minimal dishes and a complete meal!  It only takes 15 minutes to prepare and the exotic aroma of the curry will have your mouth watering before the dish ever hits the table!
