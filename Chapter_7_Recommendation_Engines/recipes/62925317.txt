Title:
Grandma's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Potatoes
5 Eggs
1 cup Mayo
1/2 cup Mustard
3 tbsp vinegar
3 tbsp white sugar

Directions:
Boil potatoes.
Hard boil eggs.
Dice up potatoes and eggs, mix with all other ingredients.
Chill in fridge for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Bryn Johnson
Description: 
A great, simple mustard potato salad.
