Title:
Mexican Pin-Wheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Cream Cheese
8 oz Sour Cream
4oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 Sm Can Green Chiles
1 Sm Can Blck Olives
.25 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1 Bunch Green Onion
8-10 Flour Tortillas

Directions:
Combine first 8 items.  Spread evenly over tortillas and roll tightly and wrap in foil.  Chill overnight.  Slice into 1/2" slices and serve with salsa.
