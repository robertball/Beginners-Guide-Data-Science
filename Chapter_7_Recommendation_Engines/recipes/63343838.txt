Title:
Nacho Plate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups refried beans
1/2 lb beef taco filling
6 oz cheddar cheese
black olives
lettuce
taco sauce
salsa
green onions
sour cream
guacamole
corn chips

Directions:
Spray a pie pan with nonstick spray.  Spread beans in pan evenly.  Top with beef filling, then cheese.  Bake at 350 until hot and cheese is melted.  Top with desired toppings.  Serve with corn chips.
