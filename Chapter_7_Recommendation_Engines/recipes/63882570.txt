Title:
Quick n Easy Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3oz package softened cream cheese
1 tbsp milk
1 tbsp vanilla
dash of salt(optional)
2 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
For chocolate:
Add either 2 ounces melted unsweetened chocolate, cooled, or 3 tbsp cocoa powder.

Directions:
Blend cheese, milk, vanilla and salt. Slowly add sugar, beating until frosting is smooth and of desired spreading consistency; you may add additional milk 1/2 tsp at a time to adjust consistency.

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
This cream cheese frosting is delicious on a red velvet cake and so simple to make!
