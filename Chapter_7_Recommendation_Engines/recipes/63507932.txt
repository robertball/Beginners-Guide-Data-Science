Title:
Bok Choy with Carrot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ounces of peeled carrots
31/2 ounces of peeled diakon
2 premier bella mushrooms
31/2 ounces of bok choy
1 small peeled beet
1 tbsp of butter
1/4 tsp cardamom

Directions:
Cut carrots, diakon, mushrooms, and beets into thin slices
Melt butter in pan and saute the carrots, diakon, mushrooms and beet for about 5 minuets.Cover. Then cut off bottom half of the bok choy and cook until tender.Add cardamom
Cut bok choy leaves into ribbons and saute for another minuet.
