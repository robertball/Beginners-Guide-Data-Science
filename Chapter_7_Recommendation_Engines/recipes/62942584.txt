Title:
Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bottle of Sangria Wine
1/4 cup sugar
Sliced citrus fruit like Orange, lemon, and lime.
A splash of orange juice or frozen orange concentrate.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a big pitcher 1-3 hours before serving. Do not store leftovers with the fruit. It may become to bitter to drink the next day.

Bottled Sangria with sugar, fruit and orange juice.
