Title:
Big Daddy's Gamecock Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen peach slices
1/2 cup frozen strawberries
2 scoops of Mila
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup ice cubes

Directions:
In a blender, blend the peaches, strawberries, Mila, milk, and ice cubes until smooth.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
It's a great football drink with peaches, milk, strawberries, and ice. It is a great drink to cool you off.Modified from: Yummy Smoothies
