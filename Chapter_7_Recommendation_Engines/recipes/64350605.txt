Title:
Vegan “Meatloaf” Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp coconut oil
1 small red onion, diced
1 can kidney beans, drained well
1 can black beans, drained well
1 can diced tomatoes mild or spicy
1 cup whole kernel corn, unsweetened
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp black pepper
Sea or kosher salt to taste
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1-1/4 cups bread crumbs
1/2 cups vegan shredded cheddar style "cheese"
barbecue sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In large skillet, saute onions until tender.  While onions are sauteing combine all spices into a small bowl. Add beans, diced tomatoes, corn and cook until beans are tender working in the spices as you cook.
In a large mixing bowl combine bean mixture, bread crumbs and "cheese". Lightly spray a loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray, add bean mixture, shape into a loaf or put into a loaf pan.  Cover and cook for 30 minutes.  Remove from oven, spread barbeque sauce over the top and return to oven uncovered for 5 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Adam Venn
Description: 
A delicious vegetarian alternative to traditional meatloaf that actually tastes like traditional meatloaf. Even my girlfriend, who is not a vegetarian enjoyed this dish.
