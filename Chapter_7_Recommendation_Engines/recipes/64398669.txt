Title:
Healthy Peanut butter mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp peanut buyer (or more depending on preference)
1/4 cup Greek yogurt (plain or vanilla)
1/2 tsp brown sugar blend stevia
1/8 tsp vanilla (optional)
Chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Put the peanut butter in a microwave safe bowl and microwave for 20 seconds or until smooth and liquid-y
2. Add the Greek yogurt, stirring until both are combined (you will have to scrape off the spoon to ensure it mixes evenly throughout)
3. Stir in stevia until completely mixed in.
4. Add optional vanilla
5. Stir in chocolate chips and serve immediately.
