Title:
REFRESHING SALAD WITH GRILLED OYSTER MUSHROOMS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4518692.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large avocado1 medium onion 2 medium tomatoes1 cucumber2 medium carrots1 pack of fresh oyster mushrooms1 green peppersalt, lemon and olive oil

Directions:
Start by preparing the oyster mushroom. Place them in a  shallow pan. Add a pinch of salt and two tablespoons of olive oil and grill them for 10-15 minutes or until they are a bit crispy.In a bowl combine all the rest ingredients. Shred the carrot ,cut the onion in round slices, the green pepper, the cucumber and the avocado. Cut the tomatoes into 4 slices and then cut them again in half and add them to the bowl.Add the olive oil,salt and lemon and toss. Place on the top the grilled oyster mushrooms and toss again. Your salad is ready!!! Enjoy!
