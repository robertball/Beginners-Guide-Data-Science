Title:
fruitcake Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbsp butter
4 c vanilla wafers (crushed)
3/4 c halved green candied cherries
3/4 c halved red candied cherries
1/2 c chopped candied pineapple
3/4 c chpped dates
1 c whole pecans
1 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
1/4 c bourbon
1/4 c grand marnier

Directions:
In saucepan melt butter; pour into 15"x10"x1" baking pan. Sprinkle vanilla wafer crumbs evenly on top. Arrange fruits and nuts evenly over crumb mixture; press down gently. Combine sweetened condensed milk and bourbon; pour evenly over top. Bake in 350 degree oven for 20n - 25 mins. Remove from oven; cool completley. Cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A Yummy version of the fruitcake with a cookie base, and rum (or bourbon) to add a wonderful kick.
