Title:
Cream Cheese-Spice Streusel Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
1 cup all-purpose flour, divided
2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup sugar
1/8 teaspoon allspice
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon fresh grated nutmeg
1 teaspoon instant coffee crystals
1 egg, beaten
1 cup milk
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
Streusel topping:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup margarine1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease a 12-muffin cup pan with a nonstick cooking spray.
In a small bowl, combine pecans and 2 tbsp of the flour. In a large bowl, combine remaining flour, the baking powder and salt; stir in the sugar, allspice, cinnamon, nutmeg and coffee crystals.
In a medium bowl, combine egg, milk, oil and cream cheese. Stir until mixed together. Stir this mixture into flour mixture, along with pecans, until just moistened. Evenly spoon mixture into each muffin cup.
In a medium bowl, combine streusel ingredients. Sprinkle on top of each muffin. Bake about 18-22 minutes or until light brown and a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Immediately remove for muffin pan and cool on wire rack for 5 minutes. Great served warm with cinnamon butter.
