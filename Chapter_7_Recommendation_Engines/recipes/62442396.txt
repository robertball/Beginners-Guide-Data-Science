Title:
Mexican dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. block cream cheese
1 jar salsa
2-4 squirts of kethup
1 bag of fritos scoops

Directions:
Let your cream cheese set out to room temperature.  Then in a bowl mix all ingredients together ( I use an electric mixer)Refrigerate to thicken and enjoy with frito scoops.

Submitter: 
Aimee Leigh Flynn
Description: 
My mother-in-law always makes this dip for occasions and it is so simple and delicious!!
