Title:
Dried Beef Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) pkg cream cheese, softened
1 (16 oz) pkg sour cream
1 (4 oz) can green chiles, diced
1/2 medium onion, minced fine
1 (10 oz) jar Armor dried beef, chopped
8 oz Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
In a medium mixing bowl combine the cream cheese, sour cream, green chiles, onion and dried beef.  Spoon the mix into a shallow serving bowl and top with the shredded cheese.  Place in oven and bake for 20 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
