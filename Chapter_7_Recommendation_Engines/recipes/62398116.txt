Title:
Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/429187.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 small onion (finely diced)
garlic powder
Italian seasoning
1/2 c white rice
1 c water
1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
1/2 c shredded cheese
3 large bell peppers
3 large banana peppers

Directions:
1. Preheat oven on 350.
2. Brown & drain ground beef and onion.
3. Cook rice according to package directions.
4. Cut tops off of bell peppers & clean out the inside with a spoon. Cut tops off of banana peppers and split down one side. Open banana pepper and remove membrane and seeds.
5. Add 1/2 can of tomato sauce, garlic powder and Italian seasoning to the ground beef mixture and mix well.
6. Stuff all peppers and cover with the remaining tomato sauce.
7. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour.
8. Remove foil and cover peppers with shredded cheese.
9. Broil for 5 minutes or until cheese bubbles.

Submitter: 
Veronica Sawyer
Description: 
This is a very simple recipe for Stuffed Peppers. I like to stuff both Banana Peppers and Bell Peppers.
