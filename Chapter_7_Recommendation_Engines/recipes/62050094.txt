Title:
coconut almond brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownie mix (14x9 size)
Small pkg. of slivered almonds
1 pkg. of fun size Mounds candy bars
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Prepare brownie mix as directed on box, except decrease oil to 2 TBS. Spread batter in 14 x 9 pan. Break up mounds candy bars evenly over batter and then sprinkle on the almonds. Pour the sweetened condensed milk over the top and bake for about 30 minutes at 350F.
