Title:
Acini De Pepe Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735510.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg.- Acini de pepe pasta(1 lb.)
1 3/4 cups- Pineapple juice
1 cup- Sugar
2 egg yolks
1 (13.5oz) can crushed pineapple
1 (13.5oz) can pineapple tidbits
2 (13.5oz) cans mandarin oranges(or as much as you want)
1 small package of mini colored marshmallows
1 Large Cool whip (folded in)

Directions:
~Cook acini de pepe pasta as directed on the package.
~Drain, rinse with cold water and put into a large bowl
~In a medium saucepan, cook together pineapple juice, sugar, and egg yolks on med/low heat. (about 6-10mins)
~When partially cool, add to the acini de pepe.
~ Refrigerate overnight.
~In the morning, add pineapple tidbits, crushed pineapple, oranges, marshmallows, and cool whip. Stir it well until all is mixed together.
~ Refrigerate another hour or two and serve.
~This is a VERY large salad.

Submitter: 
Odessa Hettinga
Description: 
This is my family's traditional dish at Thanksgiving... Make half the night before, then finish the rest in the morning! It is very addicting and hard to stop eating. I guarantee everyone will love this recipe!
