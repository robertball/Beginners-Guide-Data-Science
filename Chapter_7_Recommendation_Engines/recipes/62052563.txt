Title:
Linda's Fried Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 center-cut pork chops about 1/2 inch thick
1/2 cup flour
3/4 cup buttermilk
salt and pepper to taste
oil for deep frying

Directions:
marinate pork chops in buttermilk about 30 minutes. Combine flour, salt and pepper and mix with a fork mixing thoroughly.  Coat buttermilk covered chops in flour and add to hot oil.  Fry about 30 minutes til center is no longer pink and they are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
This is cooked at a little cafe owned by a friend of mine they are easy and delicious.
