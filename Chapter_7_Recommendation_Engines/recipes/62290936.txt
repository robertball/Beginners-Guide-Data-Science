Title:
Muffuletta Panini's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/706754.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pimento-stuffed olives
1/2 cup Kalamata olive pieces
1/2 tsp. roasted garlic, minced
2 tsp. capers
2 marinated artichoke hearts
1/4 cup diced red onions
1 Tbsp. fresh basil, chopped
juice from 1/4 lemon
2 tsp. olive oil
crusty loaf italian bread
thinly sliced deli ham
Genoa salami
provolone

Directions:
Prepare the Olive Salad ahead of time. Drain all ingredients. Place the olives, roasted garlic, capers, marinated artichoke hearts, red onions, basil, lemon juice and olive oil in a food processor and pulse until coarsely chopped. Refrigerate overnight.
Spray a panini grill with Pam Olive Oil spray and preheat the grill. Cut the bread horizonally into slices.
Layer each slice as follows: provolone, olive salad, 2 slices ham, 2 slices Geona Salami, Olive Salad then provolone.
Cover sandwich with top piece of bread and grill until the bread is toasted, about 3-5 minutes. Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately.
