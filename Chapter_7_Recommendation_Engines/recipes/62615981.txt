Title:
Diet Friendly Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 18 oz yellow cake mix
1 12 oz can diet lemon/lime soda
1 c nonfat milk
1 small pkg sugar free instant lemon pudding
1 8 oz container frozen non dairy whipped topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease bottom of 9x12 pan.
Mix cake mix and soda together 2 minutes. Pour into prepared pan.
Bake in 350 degree oven, 32-36 minutes, until toothpick inserted in middle of cake comes out clean. Remove from oven. Cool completely.
Combine milk and pudding. Mix until smooth.
Gently fold in whipped topping until no streaks remain.
Spread over cake.
