Title:
Baked Ham & Ziti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819934.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. penne pasta
12 oz. fresh or frozen broccoli florets
8 oz. ham cubed
3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
White Sauce:
4 TBS butter
4 TBS flour
2 cups milk
1/4 cup sherry
1/4 tsp sage or 3 leaves of fresh, chopped
salt & pepper top taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Add pasta to large pot of rapidly boiling, salted water.  Cook 4 minutes.
Add broccoli florets to pot with pasta.  Cook until vegetables are tender and pasta is al dente, about 6 minutes.
Drain immediately and transfer to large serving bowl.  Add ham and toss to mix.
Prepare white sauce:
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
Whisk in flour until smooth.
Slowly add milk and stir until sauce thickens.
Add shredded Parm. cheese.  Mix in until melted.
Add sherry, sage, salt, & pepper.
Immediately pour sauce over ham-pasta-vegetable mixture.  Toss lightly to mix and bake 30 minutes or until browned and bubbly.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Lisa Proctor
Description:
