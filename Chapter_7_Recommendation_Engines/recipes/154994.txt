Title:
Bangkok Broccoli Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992558.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound chicken or beef strips
1 tablespoon oil
1 (1 pound) package Birds Eye® Frozen Broccoli Stir-Fry
1 tablespoon water
1/2 cup Thai style stir-fry cooking sauce

Directions:
Brown chicken or beef strips in oil for 2 to 3 minutes. Add Broccoli Stir-Fry with water and cook uncovered, stirring occasionally for 5 to 7 minutes or until meat is thoroughly cooked.
Stir in cooking sauce and heat 1 to 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Birds Eye®
Description: 
No chopping and dicing vegetables for this stir-fry.  If you buy pre-cubed chicken or beef you won't even need your cutting board!
