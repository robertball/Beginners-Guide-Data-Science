Title:
The Best Chewy Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter
1 1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. granulated sugar
2 t vanilla extract
1 egg + 1 egg yolk
2 c. flour
1 t salt
1 t baking soda
1 1/2 c. quick oatmeal (not instant)
1/2 c. finely shredded coconut (optional)
1/2 c. finely chopped almonds (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Melt butter in large bowl
Add sugars and vanilla, mix until combined
Add egg and egg yolk, mix until combined
Stir flour, salt and baking soda together, add to mix with oatmeal and optional coconut and almonds.  Mix just until combined and no flour traces remain.
Portion dough by 1/4 cupfuls on ungreased cookie sheet, bake in preheated oven about 15 min or until golden.

Submitter: 
M. McCarthy
Description: 
A perfect oatmeal cookie-buttery and chewy without being cakey.
