Title:
Campbell's Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup sour milk
1 Tbsp. mayo
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
1/8 tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2 cups grated sharp cheddar, divided
3 1/2 cups hot cooked corkscrew-shaped pasta or shell-shaped pasta
2 tablespoon dry bread crumbs
2 teaspoons butter or margarine

Directions:
Mix soup, milk, sour cream, mayo, 1 cup cheese, spices and pasta in 1-qt. baking dish.
Mix bread crumbs and butter and sprinkle over pasta with 1/2 cup cheese.
Bake at 400 degrees F. for 20 min. or until hot.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
This extra-easy version of a classic favorite, this casserole features a special cheesy sauce for cloaking pasta and a crunchy bread crumb topping.Modified from: Campbell's Baked Macaroni and Cheese
