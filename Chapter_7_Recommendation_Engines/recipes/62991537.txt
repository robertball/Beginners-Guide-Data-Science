Title:
Glass Onion Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups grated sharp cheddar
1/2 cup canned or jarred pimento peppers, drained and choppped
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp freshly ground pepper
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)

Directions:
combine cheddar, pimentos and green oinions in medium bowl, set aside...
combime mayo, peper, salt, cayeen in med bowl and whisk together.
add mayo to cheese mixure, gently stirring together until thoroughly combined
