Title:
Carnivore Baconaise/Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 garlic clove (optional)
2-3 large egg yolks (4 small plus 1 large)
1/2 or more bacon grease, melted, strained
1/2 lemon, juiced (or 1 T or so ACV)
salt
water

Directions:
mix garlic and egg yolks with immersion blender
slowly add bacon grease through strainer while blending
add salt and lemon juice to taste
add water to  get desired consistency
