Title:
caesar potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon
24 baby potatoes
4 green onions, diced
1/3c Caesar dressing
Cracked black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400c. Fry bacon and break into little pieces. Parboil baby potatoes for approximately 8 minutes. Put potatoes in oven proof dish and drizzle with olive oil. Add cracked black pepper and cook for 10 minutes. Mix potatoes, bacon bits, green onions, dressing in a salad bowl and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Murray
Description: 
We love this recipe on a summer's day, but it works at any time of year.
