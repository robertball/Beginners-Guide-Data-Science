Title:
Chicken Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast - boned and skinned
1 eff
3 Tbs. Lemon juice concentrate
1/4 cup unsifted flour
1/8 tsp. each garlic powder and paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tsp. Chicken bouillon
1/2 cup boiling water

Directions:
Beat egg w/ 1 Tbls lemon juice.  Combine; flour, garlic powder and paprika.  Dip chicken breat into egg then in flour mixture.  In skillet turn on medium heat.  Add olive oil and then brown chicken.  Dissolve bouillon in boiling water and add remaining lemon juice.  Pour over chicken.  Cover and let simmer for 20 minutes until tender
