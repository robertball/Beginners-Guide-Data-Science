Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. butter or margarine
1/2 C. firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 C. granulated sugar
1 egg
1 C. peanut butter
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 to 11/2 C. flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Beat butter until soft, then gradually add the brown and granulated sugar and blend until creamy.  Beat in the egg, peanut butter, salt, baking soda and vanilla.  Sift before measuring and add flour.  Roll the dough into small balls. Place them on a greased cookie sheet and press flat with a fork.  Bake about 10 to 12 min.
