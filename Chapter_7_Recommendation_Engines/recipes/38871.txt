Title:
Cheesy Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989080.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA FREE Fat Free Cream Cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) package KRAFT FREE Shredded Non-Fat Cheddar Cheese, divided
1/4 cup sliced green onions
6 (6 inch) flour tortilla (6 inch)
1 (16 ounce) jar TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Thick 'N Chunky Salsa

Directions:
Beat cream cheese with electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.  Add 1 cup of the cheddar cheese and onions; mix until blended.
Spread 1/4 cup cheese mixture down center of each tortilla; roll up.  Place, seam-side down, in 11x7-inch baking dish.  Pour salsa over tortillas.  Sprinkle with remaining cheddar cheese; cover. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 to 25 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
