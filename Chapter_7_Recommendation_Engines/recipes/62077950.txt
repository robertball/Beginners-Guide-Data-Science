Title:
Mom's Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	Chopped Onion to taste
•	1 can tuna
•	Sweet Relish to taste
•	1 tsp. mayonnaise
•	1/8 tsp. celery seed OR celery salt

Directions:
1.	 Drain tuna and move to another bowl
2.	Add onion, relish, mayo, and celery seed (or celery salt)
3.	Mix thoroughly and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Megan Conrad
Description: 
A favorite my mum always made. It's a hand-me-down tradition in our family
