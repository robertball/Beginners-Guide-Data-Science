Title:
Spinach and Leek White Bean Soup Redux Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons olive oil
4 leeks, bulb only, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 (16 ounce) cans fat-free chicken broth
2 (16 ounce) cans cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
2 bay leaves
2 teaspoons ground cumin or substitute either thyme and rosemary or sage and rosemary
2 cups packed fresh spinach
salt and pepper to taste
1 4 0z can chopped green chiles
1 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
grated parmesan

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large saucepan or soup pot over medium heat. Add the leeks and garlic; saute until very tender, about 5 minutes or more. Stir in the chicken broth, cannellini beans, chiles, tomatoes, bay leaves and spices. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover, and simmer for 5 minutes. Stir in spinach and season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately, with grated parmesan if desired.
