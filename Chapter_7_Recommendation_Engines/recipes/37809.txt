Title:
Tortelloni Wreath with Pesto Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980181.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package DI GIORNO Mozzarella Garlic Tortelloni
1 (9 ounce) package DI GIORNO Portabello Mushroom Tortelloni
1 (8 ounce) container PHILADELPHIA Soft Cream Cheese
1 (7 ounce) package DI GIORNO Basil Pesto Sauce
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Add both packages of pasta to 4 quarts boiling water. Boil gently, uncovered, 6 minutes, stirring frequently. Drain; rinse with cold water.
Mix cream cheese, sauce and juice until well blended. Place in small round bowl.
Place bowl in middle of platter; arrange pasta in wreath shape around bowl.  Garnish with green and red pepper slices and pitted ripe olives.
