Title:
Lemon Yogurt Quick Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup melted butter
1 cup honey
2 cups plain yogurt
1 egg
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tsp vanilla
4 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup flaxseed
1 tbsp baking soda
2 tsp nutmeg
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup rolled oats
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Combine melted butter, honey, 1/4 cup lemon juice, and vanilla in large bowl. Whisk in egg and yogurt.
In a separate bowl, combine flour, 1 cup oats, baking soda, flaxseed, nutmeg, and 2 tsp cinnamon. Fold slowly into yogurt mixture until blended. Pour mixture into 4 lightly-greased small loaf tins.
In small bowl, combine remaining oats, lemon juice, and cinnamon. Lightly spread over each loaf.
Bake in preheated oven for about 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of each loaf comes out clean.
