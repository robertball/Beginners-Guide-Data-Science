Title:
Maple Syrup Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup Maple Syrup
1 bag Fresh cranberries
1tbsp brown sugar
Dash of lemon
1 tbsp brandy

Directions:
Mix water and maple syrup and bring to a boil.  Add cranberries and boil until cranberries pop.  Add lemon and brown sugar and mix well.  Mix in brandy and serve.
