Title:
Kimchi Pork - Microwave Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200 grams thinly sliced pork
80 grams kimchi
1/2 onion
1/4 pc green onion
1 Tbsp sake
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
2 pcs scallions

Directions:
Marinate the pork with salt, pepper and sesame oil.
Slice the onion and green onions. Chop the scallions.
Combine the pork, soy sauce, sake, onion and green onions in a microwaveable bowl.
Mix well then put on the lid or cover with a kitchen wrap. Microwave for 4 minutes using 600W.
Take out from the microwave then mix quickly. Put back the lid or wrap then microwave again for 3 minutes.
Mix then serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This is a very easy and simple dish to prepare. Great way to consume kimchi that has been in the fridge for some time.
