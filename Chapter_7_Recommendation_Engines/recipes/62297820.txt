Title:
Cheeseburger Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium bell pepper, chopped
1 cup grated Cheddar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup crushed crackers (recommended: Ritz)
* Sauce, recipe follows
1 (10 3/4-ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup milk
1 1/2 cups grated Cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix all ingredients together. Shape into a loaf. Bake loaf for 35-40 minutes. Heat the soup and milk over medium heat; add the cheese. Pour Sauce over the meatloaf or pass at the table.
