Title:
Vegan Sugar cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. veg. oil.
1/3 c applesauce, smooth.
1/2 tsp van extract.
1/4 c white sugar.
1/4 c brown sugar, packed.
1 3/4 c flour.
1/4 tsp baking powder,
1/8 tsp salt.

Directions:
Mix wet ingredients well.
Mix dry ingredients in a separate bowl
Combine Dry ingredients in the wet ingredients slowly mixing well.
If baking roll into 2 inch balls and coat with choice of sugar or spices.
Bake 350 degrees until edges are golden brown. Do not over bake
To cut cookies: roll out and chill for at least 1 hour. Use cookie cutters and follow directions above.

Submitter: 
Tab Norris
Description: 
I found this when had no eggs or milk to make cookies..I added cinnamon and they were great! Good for people with allergies.
