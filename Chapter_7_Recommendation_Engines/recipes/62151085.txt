Title:
Cranberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12-oz. bag fresh or frozen cranberries
1 cup orange juice
4 medium eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup honey
3 cups whole wheat flour
1 T. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Spray 9x13" pan.
Wash, sort and pick through cranberries. Add orange juice and eggs; slightly chop berries with hand blender.
Combine all ingredients, just until mixed. Pour into pan.
Bake 45 minutes, or until lightly browned.
Cool, cut in squares.  Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Hearty, dense cranberry bread squares made with whole wheat flour and sweetened with honey.
