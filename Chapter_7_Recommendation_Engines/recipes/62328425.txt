Title:
Joanne's Chocolate Eclair Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages French Vanilla instant pudding
1 box graham crackers
3 cups milk
1 tub (8 oz) of Cool Whip
2 tsp light Karo corn syrup
1 tsp vanilla
5 tbsp butter
1Â½ cups powdered sugar
3 tbsp milk
6 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
Butter 9x13 pan
Make pudding with 3 cups of milk
Mix in Cool Whip
Put a layer of graham crackers on the bottom of the pan
Pour half of the pudding mix over graham cracker layer
Add another layer of graham crackers on top
Pour the other half of the pudding mix over the crackers
Add another layer of crackers
Prepare the icing: Mix cocoa powder, butter, corn syrup, vanilla, powdered sugar, and milk in a small pot over low heat until melted and smooth
Pour melted chocolate mixture over top layer of graham crackers.
Chill for several hours before serving

Submitter: 
Melissa Watson
Description: 
This is the best version of the chocolate eclair cake! My mom makes it for me every year for my birthday and every time I make it for a party it's a huge hit.
