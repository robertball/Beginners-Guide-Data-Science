Title:
Sauerkraut Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups water
4 medium potatoes, peeled, and cubed
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 pound smoked sausage
1 TBSP flour
16 ounces sauerkraut
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
8 ounces sour cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In dutch oven bring water to boil; add potatoes and salt and cook until tender. Add sausage cut into pieces, undrained sauerkraut, onion, and garlic.  Bring to a boil.  In a small bowl combine sour cream and flour.  Gradually stir 1 cup hot soup into sour cream mixture.  return all to pot of soup; blend well.  Heat thoroughly, but do not boil.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
