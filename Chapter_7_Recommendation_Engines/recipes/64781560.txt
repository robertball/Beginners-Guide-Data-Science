Title:
Pan seared turkey burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pound ground turkey
2 TBS mayonnaise
1 shallot
2 TBS Steak sauce like A-1
2 TBS dries parsley
Garlic Salt or seasoned Salt
Pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl grate shallot with the medium size plane of the grater. Be sure to collect the juice. Add the remaining ingredients.
Mix gently with your hands until fully incorporated.
Divide into 4 equal portions and gently form into patties- season the top with salt and pepper.
Set aside.
Spray a Place a large pan with cooking spray and place over medium high heat.  When pan is hot place burgers top side down into pan.
Season other side of burgers.
Cook in pan for 4-5 minutes. Flip making sure the burger is nice and brown.
Cook another 4-5 minutes until cooked through.
If adding cheese, add slices, turn off the heat and cover the pan until melted.
Serve on your choice of bun or lettuce wrap and enjoy.

Description: 
Too cold out to grill? No grill? No problem. These are tasty turkey burgers made on the stove top. The secret is to only flip them once. And don’t go for the super lean turkey. You’ll want a little bit of fat to make them juicy.  Top with your favorite cheese and garnishes.
