Title:
Peanut Butter Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
3/4 C granulated sugar
1/4 C creamy peanut butter
3/4 C milk
Topping:
1/4 C creamy peanut butter
1/3 C light brown sugar, packed
1/3 C all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In a medium bowl combine flour, baking powder, sugar, peanut butter, and milk.  Fill lined mini muffin pan 1/2 to 3/4 full.                                                 Topping:                                                                                                                                                                                               With pastry blender cut peanut butter, sugar, and flour together until mixture is course and crumbly.  Sprinkle mixture on top of muffin batter.  Bake for 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.  Cool muffins in pan for 10 minutes and then finish cooling on wire rack.

Submitter: 
Rochelle Brooks Thompson
Description: 
My 4 year old son loves peanut butter and so I had him in mind when I created these muffins for playgroup one week.  They were a hit and I am asked to bring them quite often now!
