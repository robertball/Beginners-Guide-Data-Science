Title:
Better Than Your Mom's Tom & Jerry Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, separated
1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar
1/4 teaspoon ground clove
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 cups powdered sugar
2 envelopes of Dream Whip mix

Directions:
Add cream of tartar to egg whites and beat until very stiff, set aside.
In a large bowl, mix egg yolks with the spices until yolks are beaten well.  Add powdered sugar to the egg yolk and spice mixture, and beat until the mixture is lemon colored and thick.
Add egg whites and Dream Whip envelope contents to yolk mixture, and beat for 5-10 minutes, or until really thick.
To Serve:  Fill large mug 1/3 full of batter, add equal parts of brandy and light rum to taste, and fill with boiling hot water.  Stir well, and sprinkle a little nutmeg on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an incredibly delicious holiday cocktail, which can be made with or without booze.  These go down pretty easily, which can work for or against you.
