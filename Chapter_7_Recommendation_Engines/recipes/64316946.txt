Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2004191.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust
1.5 c almond flour plus more
1/2 c tapioca flour
11 T salted butter
1 tsp salt (too much)
1/2 tsp baking powder
ice cold water
Cauliflower Cream Sauce
Olive oil
5-6 cloves garlic
1/2 large yellow onion
1/2 large head cauliflower
3 c chicken stock
Salt
Filling
Olive oil
Garlic
1-2 c carrots
1/2 c peas
1-2 c mushrooms
1-2 c chicken
Thyme
Rosemary
Salt
1 c approx Chicken stock
Cauliflower cream sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven 400
Crust:
Combine dry ingredients
cut in or shred cold butter
combine all and add water as needed to make dough
refridgerate until not sticky and cold
half dough, press or roll into 9-inch pie pan (used pyrex)
bake 1-15 min at 400
keep other half refrigerated
Roll out second half between parchment paper Needs to be very cold to unroll.
(NOTE: Crust: Use less butter and more tapioca/almond flour and less salt and maybe no or less water (less butter more water) next time)
Cauliflower Cream Sauce
heat olive oil, saute garlic, onion, cauliflower, add salt, add stock and simmer/boil until very soft
Filling
heat olive oil, saute garlic, add vegetables and little stock, herbs, salt until softened, add chicken and simmer, add cauliflower cream sauce
Assembly
roll out second half of crust between parchment paper (add almond flour as needed); refrigerate as assemble rest
add filling mixture to baked pie crust
top with second pie crust
Bake 20 min at 400, then 10-15 min at 375
