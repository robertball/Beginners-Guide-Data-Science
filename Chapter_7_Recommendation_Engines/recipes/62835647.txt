Title:
Zucchini Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 med. zucchini, cut into 1/4" slices
1/2 C seasoned dry bread crumbs
1/8 t black pepper
garlic and onion powder, to taste
4 T paresan cheese, shredded
2 egg whites, or egg substitute, or whole egg

Directions:
Preheat over to 475 degrees.
Spray cookie sheet w/cooking spray
Mix dry ingred. until well combined in med. bowl or plate.
Put egg whites in a bowl and dip zucchini in to coat, then dip in breadcrumb mixture. Put on cookie sheet. Spray tops with cooking spray.
Bake in oven for 7 min. Flip zucchini over, spray tops again and bake another 10 min, until crispy and browned

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Baked in oven until crispy.  Seasoned w/breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese, these are healthy as well as tasting good
