Title:
Instant Pot Spinach Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5719300.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons ghee, divided
1 red onion, minced
1 tablespoon minced garlic
? teaspoon red pepper flakes
½ teaspoon sea salt
? cup homemade mayo (or store bought; check ingredients)
1 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
juice of 1 lemon
3 6-ounce cans crab meat, pressed to fully remove liquid
¼ cup full-fat canned coconut milk
4 ounces frozen spinach, thawed and pressed to fully remove liquid
¼ cup almond flour (divided)
¼ cup nutritional yeast (divided)
2 green onions, chopped
For dipping
Cucumber, red and yellow peppers, plantain chips and radishes

Directions:
Set instant pot to saute setting.
Saute onion, garlic, red pepper flakes and sea salt in 1 tablespoon ghee.
Stir in mayo, crab meat, coconut milk, spinach, and 2 tablespoons each of almond flour and nutritional yeast.
Seal Instant Pot and cook at high pressure for 10 minutes, then do a quick release.
Meanwhile, combine remaining almond flour and nutritional yeast with 1 tablespoon ghee.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees (optional).
Transfer to a serving dish and top with almond flour mixture and green onions.
Bake at 450 degrees for five minutes to brown top.
Serve with homemade plantain chips and/or sliced cucumber, tri-color peppers and radishes.

Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
Using an Instant Pot speeds up this easy dip that is DELICIOUS.  I may or may not have eaten the leftovers (which I squirreled away from my kiddos) for breakfast.  This dip is also Paleo and Whole 30 friendly!
