Title:
floyd eudy's pork chops & dirty rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large pork chops
1 cup rice
1 can beef consomme
3/4 cup water
1/4 cup butter
1/4 tsp garlic poder
1/4 tsp salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees.
brown 6 large boneless pork chops in skillet.
while pork chops brown- add other ingredients to large casserole dish.
place chops  on top of the rice & liquid mixture.
cover and bake for 1 hour.
