Title:
Golden tuna casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Campbell's Golden Mushroom soup
1-2 cans (based on your preference) White Albacore tuna
1 box Kraft Macaroni & cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Prepare macaroni & cheese according to box directions on stovetop
3. Add macaroni, tuna & golden mushroom soup to cassarole dish, mix well
4. Bake for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
This was one of my favorite meals as a child, it is my mom's recipe.  Very tasty!
