Title:
Grandma Sally's Honey Pecan Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 fryers (2-3lbs each) cut up
4 cups buttermilk
1 cup self rising flour
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp cayenne
1 cup butter
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup pecans

Directions:
Refrigerate chicken in buttermilk for an hour and a half; drain. Combine flour, cayenne, salt and garlic powder. Place chicken in flour mixture a piece at a time to coat. Let the chicken stand for 20 minutes while melting butter on stove. Add honey to melted butter, bring to a boil; add pecans and let simmer on low for 15 minutes. Fry the chicken in 1/2 inch of oil until brown, then drain. Heat oven to 300 degrees. Put the chicken in a baking pan, poor on honey pecan glaze. Bake for 30 minutes.
