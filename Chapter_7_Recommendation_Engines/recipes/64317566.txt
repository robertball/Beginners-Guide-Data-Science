Title:
Sugar-Free Black Bean Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups black beans (Eliminate salt if they are canned.)
3 eggs
1/2 c. maple syrup (or honey)
1/3 c. cocoa powder
1/3 c. coconut oil or butter
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder (aluminum free)
1/4 cup chocolate chips or sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease 9x9 glass pan. Warm beans and coconut oil until liquid.  Add all remaining ingredients.  Blend all ingredients until smooth.  Pour into pan.  Sprinkle with chocolate chips, nuts or sprinkles (or all 3!)  Bake for 30 minutes or until batter pulls away from the side of the pan. Cool & enjoy! :)
