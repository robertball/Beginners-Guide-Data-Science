Title:
Marvelous Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1682105.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained and rinsed
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt'sÂ® Diced Tomatoes, undrained
2 cups frozen Italian-style vegetable blend
1/2 cup elbow macaroni, uncooked
1/4 cup KRAFT Zesty Italian Dressing
1/4 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except cheese in large saucepan.
Bring to a boil on medium-high heat. Reduce to medium; simmer 6 to 8 min. or until macaroni is tender.
Sprinkle each serving with cheese.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
Description: 
Ready to serve in 25 minutes, this easy minestrone with garbanzo beans, macaroni, and veggies is topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
