Title:
Chuck Valencea Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-4 1/2 lb. chuck /beef blade roast
1 tbsp oil
1 can 1/2 fat cream mushroom or onion soup
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp mustard seed
3 tbsp instant onion flakes
3 tbsp instant vegetable flakes

Directions:
Brown meat in dutch oven, cover and simmer 30 minutes.   Mix remaining ingredients and pour over meat.  Cover tightly and cook at 325 for 2 1/2 hours or longer till tender.  Baste occasionally.  Remove meat, add 1 cup water to wine-cream mixture.  Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.  Thicken with 3 tsp flour and 1/4 cup water.   Simmer till thickened.

Submitter: 
Lynn Mott
Description: 
Excellent flavor and such tender meat.  So good my daughter has added it to her recipe box.  Can be served over rice, potatoes or noodles.
