Title:
Simple Beer Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - 12oz beers (your favorite kind)
1 plastic popsicle mold

Directions:
Allow beer to sit out at least 24 hours before freezing.
Pour beer into popsicle mold and insert sticks
Place mold into freezer for 8-10 hours until frozen
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
Everyone loves popsicles including adults. These are made from your favorite beer and sure to cool you down on a hot summer day
