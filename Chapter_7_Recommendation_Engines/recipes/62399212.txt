Title:
Charlie Brown Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/441461.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkgs Graham Crackers
3/4 Cups Creamy Peanut Butter
1 Pkgs Instant Chocolate Pudding
1 Tub Whip Cream

Directions:
1. Roll the graham cracker with rolling pin to make them into crumbs.
2. Pour the crumbs into a bowl and add the peanut butter. You may need to add more peanut butter to get a good consistency to make a crust.
3. Place the mixed crumbs and peanut butter in a 8x8 cake pan and press along bottom to form a crust.
4. Prepare the instant pudding, and then pour it on top of the crust.  Let set in fridge for about 15 minutes until set.
5. Then take the tub of whip cream and spread over the top.
6. Refrigerate for about an hour and serve!

Description: 
This is a nice cool refreshing dessert for summer!  No baking required which makes it even better.
