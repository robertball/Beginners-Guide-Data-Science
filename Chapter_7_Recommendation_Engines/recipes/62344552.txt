Title:
STAGE PLANKS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
2 cups cane syrup
1/2 cup boiling water
1 tbsp cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp cloves
1/2 tsp salt
6 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
In a large bowl cream butter, add sugar gradually, beat until light and fluffy.
Add cane syrup, cinnamon, ground ginger, cloves, salt and boiling water, beat well.
Work in flour until dough is well mixed and stiff.
Roll or press dough into one large flat piece.
Cut into 6x2 inch rectangles and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake in preheated 300 degree oven for 15-20 minutes. Cool and frost with pink frosting.

Submitter: 
PET
Description: 
It's a cookie! When I was a kid we loved going into town with Papa, my grandpa, because we each got a store bought cinnamon flavored 'Stage Plank' cookie and a RC cola. This recipe is about as close as I've come to my childhood memory of what the cookie tasted like.
