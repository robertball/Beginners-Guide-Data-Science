Title:
Granola Bars, honey based. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/305426.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups oats
3/4 cup wheat germ, or whole wheat flour
3/4 cup ground flax seed
11/2 cups sunflower seeds
1 cup roasted peanuts, crushed
1 cup roasted almonds, whole or halved
11/3 cup brown sugar
1 cup honey
8 T butter
1 T vanilla extract
1 t salt
2 cups dried fruit, ie. dried cranberries, raisins, dried cherries etc.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Spread oats on a cookie sheet and toast in oven 12 minutes, stirring every few minutes.
Mix wheat germ, flax seed, sunflower seeds, peanuts, and almonds in a large bowl.
Bring brown sugar, honey, butter, and salt just to a rolling simmer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
Add vanilla.
Add oats and dried fruit to the contents of large bowl and stir in candy mixture.
Liberally grease 9/13 pan and pour granola into it and press down with wax paper.
Refrigerate for 2 hours.
Unmold onto a large cutting board. Cut in half lenthwise, then cut into 1" bars, widthwise.
Store in snack size plastic bags in the freezer or refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Claire Edwards Grover
Description: 
Chock full of grains, fruit and nuts, with a delicious, chewy coating. You and your kids will love these for a quick breakfast, snack, or to grab on the go.
