Title:
Pink Pina Colada Slush  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
1 (750 milliliter) bottle light rum
1 (15 ounce) can cream of coconut
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen pink lemonade concentrate
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime soda (such as 7-UpÂ®)

Directions:
Mix pineapple juice, light rum, cream of coconut, and pink lemonade concentrate together in a large freezer-safe container.
Freeze pineapple mixture until icy and slushy, 4 to 6 hours.
Fill a glass halfway with slushy mixture and top off with lemon-lime soda to serve.

An alcoholic beverage sure to cool you down on a hot summer day.
