Title:
Easiest Tomato Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/469494.jpg
Ingredients:
Tomatoes
Salt (optional)
Hot peppers (optional)
Garlic (optional)
Italian seasoning (optional)

Directions:
Halve or quarter fresh tomatoes and place in crock pot or soup pot.
Simmer in covered pot until stirring creates a stew-like appearance (2-3 hours)
Pour through sieve leaving behind peels and cores
Pour back into pot, leave off lid and allow to reduce to the desired consistency. (2+ hours)
Pour into lidded, plastic baby food cubes or small plastic bowls in desired portions and freeze.
If bowls were used, take out when frozen, pop out of bowl and place the newly molded brick into a freezer storage bag. Stack in freezer and enjoy tomato-based dishes all winter long!

Submitter: 
BirgitJ
Description: 
This recipe takes all of the hassle out of putting up tomatoes for later use. Takes time but very little effort!
