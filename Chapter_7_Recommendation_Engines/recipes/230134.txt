Title:
Crisp Marinated Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/931170.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
2 cucumbers, sliced
1/4 cup sliced sweet onion

Directions:
Whisk vinegar, sugar, salt, and celery seed together in a large bowl; stir in cucumbers and onion. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Serve cold.
