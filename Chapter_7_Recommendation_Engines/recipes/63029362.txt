Title:
Pureed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup baby carrots
splash of water
ginger (optional)
nutmeg (optional)
basil (optional)
garlic (optional)
oregano (optional)

Directions:
Toss baby carrots and water to the Short Cup, twist on Shaker/Steamer Top and steam carrots until tender.
Strain off most of extra water.
Twist on Cross Blade and blend until you've achieved desired consistency.
Serve warm or room temperature.
