Title:
Rio Ranchero Layered Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped/torn romaine
8 large hard-cooked eggs, sliced
3 cups halved cherry tomatoes
19 oz. can black beans, rinsed and drained
4 green onions, sliced
1/2 cup thinly sliced celery
1 medium sweet pepper, diced
2 cans of kernel corn (7 oz. each), drained
2 ripe large avocados, cubed
1 tbsp. lime juice
Sassy Salsa Dressing:
1 cup mayo
1 cup sour cream
2/3 cup chunky salsa
1/2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
1/3 cup lime juice
1/2 tsp. grated lime zest
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. granulated sugar
8 cherry tomatoes
3/4 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese
3/4 cup grated Monterey Jack with Jalapeño cheese
8 bacon slices, cooked crisp and crumbled
2 tbsp. sliced ripe olives
2 tbsp. diced jalapeño pepper

Directions:
Put lettuce into extra-large glass bowl. Pack down slightly.
Press some egg slices against inside of bowl to decorate. Arrange remaining egg slices in single layer on top of lettuce.
Arrange some tomato halves, cut-side out, against inside of bowl to decorate. Layer remaining tomato halves on top of egg slices and lettuce.
Layer next 5 ingredients, in order given, on top of tomato halves.
Put avocado into small bowl. Drizzle with lime juice. Toss gently until coated. Scatter on top of corn.
Dressing:
Combine first 9 ingredients in medium bowl. Makes about 3 cups dressing. Spoon on top of avocado. Carefully spread dressing to edge of bowl to seal. Cover with plastic wrap. Chill.
Just before serving, scatter remaining 6 ingredients, in order given, over dressing.
