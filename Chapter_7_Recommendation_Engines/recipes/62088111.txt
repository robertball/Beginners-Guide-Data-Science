Title:
cauliflower with shrimp sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower, leaves removed but left whole
1 T butter
5 cloves garlic, mashed
3 T butter	3 T flour
half and half, whole milk
2 cups precooked shrimp
1/2 t cayenne	1 T dill and T parsley, chopped

Directions:
Steam head of cauliflower, about 20 minutes, until fairly soft.
Meanwhile melt 1 T butter in small pan and add garlic.  Cook over low heat until cooked, about 5 minutes. In another pan
make a roux with the butter and flour and add cream and milk until sauce is quite thick.  Add garlic, shrimp, cayenne, dill and parsley and cook 3 more minutes.  Add salt to taste. Remove and drain cauliflower and place on platter.  Pour sauce over the cauliflower and serve immediately. You may wish to serve this with saffron rice.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
Cauliflower and shrimp are a surprisingly great combination. If you are a cauliflower enthusiast, give this a try. Easy and tasty.
