Title:
Apple Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
22 oz filtered or sparkling water
8 Granny Smith apple slices
5 fresh ginger slices
2 1-inch lemongrass sticks

Directions:
Place ingredients in 32-oz mason jar or pitcher.
Stir gently, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (longer for more intense flavor).
Refrigerate for up to 3 days.
