Title:
Creamy Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup; mayonnaise
1/4 cup; sugar
1/4 cup; whole mile or 1/2 & 1/2
1/4 cup; buttermilk
3 TBL; juice of fresh lemon
2 TBL; white vinegar
1/2 tsp; Kosher salt
1/4 tsp; fresh ground black pepper
1 head (8-10 cups); cabbage ... finely chopped
1/4 cup; carrots ... shredded
3 TBL; onions ... minced

Directions:
Be sure cabbage, carrots, and onion are finely prepared (about the size of a grain of rice).
Combine mayonnaise, sugar, milk, buttermilk, lemon juice, vinegar, salt & pepper in a large bowl.  Beat until smooth.
Add cabbage, carrots, and onions.  Mix well.
Cover and refrigerate ... minimum three hours; better if overnight.
