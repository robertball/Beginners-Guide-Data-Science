Title:
Peanut Butter Brownies by Mandy's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownie Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter
½ cup butter, softened
2 cups brown sugar, packed
3 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
½ tsp. salt
Peanut Butter Frosting: (optional)
2 cups creamy peanut butter
1 cup butter, softened
1 tsp. vanilla extract
? tsp. salt
3 to 4 Tbsp. heavy cream or milk
2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl or the bowl of your stand mixer, mix together the peanut butter and butter.
Add in the brown sugar, eggs and vanilla; mix until light and fluffy, about a minute or two.
Add in the flour and salt; mix well.
Pour the batter into a 9x13 pan that's been greased or lined with foil. Spread out evenly.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
Cool in pan; frost with Peanut Butter Frosting.
Peanut Butter Frosting:
Beat peanut butter and butter together until light and fluffy, about one or two minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients and mix until smooth.
Frost brownies.
