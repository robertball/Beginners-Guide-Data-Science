Title:
Chili to Share With Friends Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
2 large cans crushed tomatoes (*fire roasted version)
1 can chopped tomotoes (*fire roasted version)
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
1 large stalk celery chopped
1/2 large green pepper chopped
1/8 cup chili powder
1/8 cup cajun blend powder
2 tsp ground cumin
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 T salt
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
1/2 tsp oregano
2 T white sugar
2-4 T olive oil
1 cup red beans
1 cup pinto beans

Directions:
In a dutch oven saute onions, celery and green pepper in olive oil until just soft.  Remove from pot and set aside.
Add more olive oil to pot and brown beef.
Add all remaining ingredients plus onion mixture.  Stir well.
Simmer for at least an hour.  Make in advance because it tastes much better the next day!
