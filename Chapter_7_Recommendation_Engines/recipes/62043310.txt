Title:
Pineapple,  Banana, Coconut ,Baby Food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh or canned pineapple, diced(if canned, remove the juice)
1/2 banana, sliced
1/2 tsp sugar
2tbsps coconut milk

Directions:
In a blender, combine the pineapple, and banana.  When pureed, add the sugar.
Remove from blender, place mixture in a bowl and add the coconut milk.  Serve.

Submitter: 
DRAPOSO
Description: 
When my daughter was an infant, all her food was homemade.  Some of the store bought baby food was so bitter that she wouldn't eat it so I made everything at home.  My daughter is 5 now and eats this instead of applesauce!!!
