Title:
Roast Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 10" flour tortillas
1/2 cup horseradish sauce
8 thin slices deli roast beef
8 slices muenster cheese
1/2 cup green onion, diced
1 cup tomato, chopped
2 cups mixed salad greens

Directions:
Easy Steps:
1) Spread each tortilla with 2 tablespoons of horseradish sauce. Place 2 slices of roast beef and one slice of cheese on each tortilla.
2) Divide onion, tomato and greens among tortillas. Roll up and serve.

Description: 
Wraps create an easy, fun way to enjoy a sandwich alternative without the bread. Horseradish, roast beef and fresh vegetables roll up into one terrific meal.
