Title:
Ellie May's Iron Skillet Herman Dinner Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677473.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
1/2 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
1 cup Herman Sourdough Starter
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon of salt
4 Tablespoons of Olive Oil

Directions:
In a large bowl combine yeast and warm water. Stir until yeast is dissolved. Add Herman Sweet Sourdough Starter, flour, salt, and oil. Mix well.
Shape into 8-10 rolls. Place in a lightly greased iron skillet. Let stand for 30 to 45 minutes.
Bake in a 350 degree F (175 degree C) oven for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
ellie may
Description: 
These are tender white sourdough rolls, just make 8 - 10 and put them in a greased iron skillet for baking. Feeling lazy? Just fashion a whole loaf from it, and bake in the skillet.Modified from: Iron Skillet Herman Dinner Rolls
