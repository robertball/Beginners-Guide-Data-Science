Title:
Cappuccino Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/195486.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cold coffee
1 1/2 cups chocolate ice cream
1/4 cup chocolate syrup
crushed ice
1 cup whipped cream

Directions:
In a blender, combine coffee, ice cream and chocolate syrup. Blend until smooth. Pour over crushed ice. Garnish with a dollop of  whipped cream and serve.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
The combination of coffee, chocolate ice cream, chocolate syrup and whipped cream makes this a perfect pick-me-up drink!
