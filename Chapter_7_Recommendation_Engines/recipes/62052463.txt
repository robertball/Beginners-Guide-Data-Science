Title:
Green Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen green beans, 2 cans cream of mushroom soup, 2 teaspoons salt, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1 medium onion (diced), 1 teaspoon butter, french fried onions.

Directions:
In a non-stick pan, saute the onion in butter and set aside.
In a medium pot, bring 4 quarts of water to a boil,  add cream of mushroom soup, beans, onion, pepper, and salt.
Simmer for 35 minutes.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
Top with french fried onions.

Description: 
A yummy green bean soup for the winter chill.
