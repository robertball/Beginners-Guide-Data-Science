Title:
Maple Dijon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dijon mustard
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
1-1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken
Salt
Black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°F.
Whisk together mustard, syrup, and vinegar.
Toss chicken in sauce.
Place in greased baking dish and place in oven.
Roast 20 minutes; turn and baste with sauce.
Bakes 20 minutes more or until done. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
