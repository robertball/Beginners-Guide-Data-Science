Title:
Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Egg Beaters
2 1/4 C Sugar
2 T Vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
5 C. Milk
4 C. Cream

Directions:
Whip Egg Beaters until light, add in sugar and beat until well mixed. Add Milk, Cream, Salt, and Vanilla. Pour into ice cream maker and follow directions.
