Title:
Easy Baked Mixed Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dark green zucchini
1 yellow summer squash
1/2 (your favorite) onion
1 1/2 tblsp real bacon bits
1/2 tblsp Country Crock
Mrs. Dash Original Seasoning

Directions:
Slice zucchini, squash and onion.
Transfer to baking dish.
Add bacon bits and Country Crock.
Sprinkle with Mrs Dash to taste.
Bake for 20 minutes or desired doneness.
