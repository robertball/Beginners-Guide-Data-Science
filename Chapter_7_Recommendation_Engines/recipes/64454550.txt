Title:
Layered Nacho Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can tomato paste
1 can bean dip
1 small can diced green chiles
1 ripe tomato, fine dice
1 ripe bell pepper, fine dice
1 sweet onion, fine dice
1 tsp. lemon juice
16 oz. sour cream
1 pkg. taco seasoning
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 bundle green onions, diced

Directions:
You will layer the ingredients in a 9 x 13 baking dish, preferably glass. Combine the bean dip, tomato paste, and diced green chiles. Spread evenly on the bottom of the dish and place in refrigerator. Finely dice tomato, green bell pepper, and onion and toss in lemon juice. Sprinkle diced vegetables over the bean dip layer and return to refrigerator. Stir taco seasoning powder into the sour cream making sure to break up and lumps. Spread this mixture carefully over the diced vegetables. Sprinkle the sour cream with shredded cheddar cheese and top with diced green onions. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Serve with Fritos or Doritos Scoops.
