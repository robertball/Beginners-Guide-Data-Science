Title:
Summer Hummer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/643274.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cubes  ice
2 fluid ounces lime vodka
2 fluid ounces prepared lemonade
4 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1 lemon wedge

Directions:
Place the ice into a tall glass. Pour in the vodka, lemonade, and lemon-lime beverage. Garnish with a lemon wedge to serve.
