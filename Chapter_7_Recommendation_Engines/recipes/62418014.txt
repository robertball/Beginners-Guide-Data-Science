Title:
Gingered Adzuki Beans with Carrots and Leeks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442839.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can Adzuki beans
4” piece kombu
½ cup water
1 large Carrot, diced
1 Tbsp Ginger, grated
3-4 Leeks, chopped (the bottom half – where white and light green)
1 tsp Olive oil
Salt, black pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain and rinse adzuki beans. Place adzuki beans, kombu and water in pot over low-medium heat, leave the beans over heat for 12-15 minutes, add water as necessary (bottom of pot should be lined with approximately 1/2 inch of water). Remove kombu from beans before eating.
Heat oil and 1-2 Tbsp of water, sauté leeks and carrots for 8 minutes. Add to beans. Add ginger to beans and keep over low heat for a couple more minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!!

Adzuki beans flavored with ginger and sauteed carrots and leeks.
