Title:
3 bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of kidney beans - have used dark red - try light red
1 can of green beans
1 can of wax beans
3/4 cup of vinegar
5 teaspoons of sugar - not sure about this one - to taste
celery seed
onion green or white to taste
vegetable oil

Directions:
try to put the cans in the frig before hand.  Then they are nice and cold for the salad.

refreshing sweet/ sour bean salad
