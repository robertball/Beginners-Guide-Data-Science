Title:
Low Fat Chocolate Ice Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp. cocoa powder
1 cup milk
5 packets splenda

Directions:
Whisk together cocoa powder, milk, splenda until smooth.
Pour mixture into a large ice tray and chill for at least 2 hours.
