Title:
Taco Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 bunch leaf lettuce -
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 avocado, diced
1 can chili beans with sauce
3 cups shredded Cheddar-Monterey Jack cheese blend
2 tomatoes, chopped
4 ounces crushed Doritos TACO FLAVORED chips
1/4 cup Western dressing

Directions:
Place hamburger in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown and drain. Add taco seasoning mix, onion and chili beans. Set aside to cool.
Layer lettuce, bell pepper, avocado,& tomatoes. Sprinkle with & mix in dressing.  Add beef mixture, cheese, and tortilla chips to top of salad. Add more dressing as desired.

Submitter: 
Razorback
Description: 
A delicious crispy taco salad brimming with chopped tomatoes, ground beef, avocado and shredded cheese and topped with tangy and sweet western dressing.Modified from: Taco Salad II
