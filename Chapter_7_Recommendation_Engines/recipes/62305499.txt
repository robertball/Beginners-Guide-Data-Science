Title:
FOUR INGREDIENT ONION & GARLIC ROASTED CHICKEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Roasting chicken (7lb) or cut up chicken equiv
Kosher salt & pepper
4 c chopped onions
20 garlic cloves, peeled & roughly chopped
1/4 c olive oil

Directions:
Position rack in bottom 1/3 of oven. Preheat to 400. Season chicken inside & out with salt & pepper. Arrange chicken in roasting pan (with or w/o rack)
Combine onions, garlic & olive oil & toss well. Arrange around chicken
Roast chicken 30min(less if using pieces). Reduce heat to 350 & spoon colected juices & onion mixture over the chicken. Onions may not stick. Roast chicken 40min (less if using pieces) more until golden (180degrees). Transfer to platter; surround with onions & garlic & drizzle with pan juices. Tent with foil until ready to serve.
