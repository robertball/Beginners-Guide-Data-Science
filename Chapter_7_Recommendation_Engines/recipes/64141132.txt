Title:
Chicken Helene Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Chicken Breasts
2 mushroom soups
1 pint /sour cream
1 lb bacon
3 packs chipped Beef

Directions:
Wrap bacon - Chipped beef on bottom - put chicken in - mix soup and sour cream - pour over - bake low 300 - 325 for 2 1/2 hours.
