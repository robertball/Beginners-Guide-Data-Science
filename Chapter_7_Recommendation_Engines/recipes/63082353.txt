Title:
Marinated Artichoke Hearts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T Lemon juice
1/3 c salad oil
3 T vinegar
3 T olive oil
2 tsp salt
pepper
1/2 tsp sugar
1 clove garlic
1 can artichoke hearts drained

Directions:
Combine all, but hearts. Shake well. Pour over hearts and marinate at least 3 hours. Drain and serve on greens.
