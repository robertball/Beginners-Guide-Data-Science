Title:
Slow Cooker Spicy Black-Eyed Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken broth
1 pound dried black-eyed peas, sorted and rinsed
1 packet Lipton's onion soup mix
3 cloves garlic, diced
1 red bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, and diced
1 jalapeno chile, seeded and minced
8 ounces diced ham or ham bone with plenty of meat on it
1 ham hock
4 slices bacon, chopped
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons cumin
salt, to taste
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Pour the broth into the slow cooker, add the black-eyed peas, onion mix, garlic, bell pepper, jalapeno pepper, ham, bacon, cayenne pepper, cumin, salt, and pepper; stir to blend. Cover the slow cooker and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours until the beans are tender.

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
Black-eyed peas grab some heat and spice from jalapeno peppers and cumin in this flavorful slow cooker preparation that also includes diced ham, bacon, bell peppers, onion, and garlic.Modified from: Slow Cooker Spicy Black-Eyed Peas
