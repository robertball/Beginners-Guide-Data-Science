Title:
Poblano Enchiladas Suizas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs skinless, boneless chicken breast
garlic salt to taste
16 oz low-fat sour cream
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
3-4 poblanos
1 28 oz can green chile enchilada sauce
1 jalapeno
half bunch cilantro
1-2 tsp lemon juice
18 corn tortillas, cut in quarters
16 oz shredded cheese - Colby/Monterey Jack is good.

Directions:
Roast Poblanos on foil-covered cookie sheet under broiler. 6-7 min, turning once, till they blister.  Cool and peel.
Preheat over to 350. Grease large casserole.
Chop chicken into chunks. Brown with a little olive oil and a healthy sprinkle of garlic powder.
Rough-chop cilantro and jalapeno.  Add to blender with lemon juice and can of enchilada sauce. Blend till smooth-ish.
Chop browned chicken into tiny pieces.  Add to bowl with 8-12 oz sour cream, cayenne, and a little more garlic powder and salt to taste. Mix well.
Dice cooked poblanos.
Cut tortillas in quarters.
Dip tortilla pieces in sauce and line bottom of pan. Add 1/2 chicken, 1/2 poblanos, 1/3 cheese, and 1/3 sauce.
Add another layer tortillas (don't need to dip), other half chicken, other half poblanos, 1/3 cheese, 1/3 sauce.
Finish with layer of tortillas dipped in sauce.  Cover with remaining cheese.
Cover with foil and cook 45 min at 350.  Remove foil to brown for last 10 min.
Top with dab of remaining sour cream.

Description: 
Break-your-heart-good green chile and chicken enchiladas, the easy way.  Serve with beans and a chilled white wine or cerveza.
