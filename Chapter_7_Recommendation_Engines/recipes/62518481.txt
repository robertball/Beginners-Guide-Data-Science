Title:
Holiday Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511209.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 package cranberries (fresh)
3/4 cup  water
1/8 teaspoon cloves (ground)
1/8 teaspoon allspice  (ground)
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon  (ground)
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup pecans (pieces)

Directions:
Place fresh cranberries and water in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the cloves, allspice, and cinnamon.
Cook until cranberries begin to burst, about 10 minutes.
Stir in sugar and reduce heat to low. Continue cooking 5 minutes, or until sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat and allow to cool. Once cool add in the pecans. Chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight, before serving.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Fresh cranberries are boiled with spices and cooked with sugar to create a delicious sauce. Great for either Thanksgiving, Christmas or just have it for both!
