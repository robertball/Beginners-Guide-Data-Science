Title:
Green Chile Beef Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lb boneless chuck roast
1 packet of taco seasoning mix
16oz jar of green chile salsa
2 cups of beef broth

Directions:
Layer the ingredients in the above order in a crockpot or dutch oven with a tight fitting lid. Cook low and slow for 4hrs. Check and add liquid if needed. Remove meat from pot and shred. Return the shredded beef to the pot and mix with the pan juices. Serve with all your favorite taco sides. Yummy!!

Submitter: 
Mary McCarthy Maslowski
Description: 
This recipe can be done in the crockpot or a slow oven (325). It is pulled beef taco meat. The leftovers makes superb enchiladas. Spicy and full bodied flavor without overdone heat.
