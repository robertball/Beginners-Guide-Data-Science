Title:
Bratwurst Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032028.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. Cauliflower florets, steamed
1 Onion, chopped
2 Tbsp. Canola oil
2 C. Rotini
3  Cooked Bratwurst Sausages, sliced
1 14 oz can stewed tomatoes, (drained)
1 C. Cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Break cauliflower into small florets and steam until tender. Set aside
Cook rotini according to package directions, rinse and drain
Saute chopped onion  in 2 Tablespoons Canola oil until translucent
Chop cauliflower into smaller pieces and spread on the bottom of casserole dish
Spread onion over top of cauliflower
Layer rotini and stewed tomatoes over cauliflower and onion
Place sliced sausage evenly over top of rotini noodles spreading grated cheese on top then bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A pasta casserole with veggies, sausages and melted cheddar cheese for a satisfying meal.
