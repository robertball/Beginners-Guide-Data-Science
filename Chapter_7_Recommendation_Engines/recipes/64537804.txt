Title:
Hot & Sour Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Chicken Broth
1/2 Cup Water
1 Can "Fancy" Chinese Vegetables (Stir Fry Assortment, etc.)
3 Cloves Garlic (Crushed)
2 Slices fresh ginger
1 tbls Soy Sauce
1 tbls coarse ground black pepper
1 Can Chunk Chicken
2 tsp Hot Pepper Oil
2 tsp Sesame Oil
4 tbls Red Wine Vinegar
4 tbls Corn Starch
1 Egg beaten

Directions:
Combine broth, water, vegetables, garlic, ginger, pepper and soy sauce in a pot and bring to boil. Cover and let simmer.
Mix Chicken & oils in a bowl.
Mix Vinegar & Corn Starch in another bowl.
Bring to mid heat, add chicken
Bring to roiling boil and string in egg
Stir in Vingar/Corn Starch mix
Simmer about 3 minutes until it thickens.
