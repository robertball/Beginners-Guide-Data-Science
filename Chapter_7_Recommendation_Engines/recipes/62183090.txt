Title:
Buko Breakfast Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/295110.jpg
Ingredients:
5-6 eggs
1/8 cup milk for eggs (if desired)
4 slices bread
2 tbl sp Butter/Margarine
4 cooked Sasuage patties or
6-8 slices cooked Bacon or
4 servings Ham
4 slices cheese
1/2 Tsp parsley
Salt/Pepper
Cooking spray

Directions:
Choose a baking dish that will fit 4 slices of bread laid out flat and spray with cooking spray.
Melt 1 tbl spoon butter in dish, add bread slices, top with breakfeast meat(s) and remainder of butter.
Add slice of cheeses to each.
In seperate bowl scramble eggs, add salt/pepper/parsley and milk (if desired) pour over bread/meat layer.
Cover loosely with foil and bake for 33 min @350*F, remove foil near end to brown slightly.
