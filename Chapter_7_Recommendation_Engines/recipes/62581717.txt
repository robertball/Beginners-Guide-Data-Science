Title:
Chocolate Beet Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/548856.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (divided)
1/2 cup EVOO
1 cup organic sugar cane
3/4 cup agave nectar (divided)
3 eggs
4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened bakers chocolate squares
1 TSP unprocessed stevia
1 TBS cacao powder
2 cups pureed cooked beets
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup unbleached white flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
In a mixing bowl, cream 1/4 cup butter, EVOO, sugar cane and 1/2 cup agave nectar.
Add eggs; mix well.
Melt chocolate with 1/4 cup butter, 1/4 cup agave nectar, stevia, and cacao powder; stir until smooth. Cool slightly.
Blend chocolate mixture, beets and vanilla into the creamed mixture (mixture will appear separated).
Combine flour, baking soda and salt; add to the creamed mixture and mix well.
Pour into a greased and floured 10-in. fluted tube pan.
Bake for 45-55 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack. Cool completely.
