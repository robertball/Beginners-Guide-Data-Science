Title:
Avocado Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2908623.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups milk
1 avocado, peeled and pitted
3 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:
Blend milk, avocado, and sugar together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
LA Lady
Description: 
This is a yummy milkshake. You can add some ice if you like it really cold.
