Title:
Fudgy Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie crust
1/3 cup butter
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1 cup chopped pecans
3 eggs
1 cup light corn syrup
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Place crust into pie plate
Melt butter, sugar and cocoa powder in a saucepan
Beat together eggs, corn syrup and salt, then add to cocoa mixture.
Add nuts then pour into crust.
Bake for 45-50 minutes - do not over bake.
Cool before serving
