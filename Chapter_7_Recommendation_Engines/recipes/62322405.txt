Title:
Yummy in Your Tummy Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/432721.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup apple juice
5 strawberries (frozen or fresh)
1 banana, best if cut in half

Directions:
1. Add cup of apple juice
2. Then put in strawberries and banana.
3. Mix all together on medium for 1 minute, then on high for 1 minute.

Submitter:

Description: 
As apple juice as a base instead of milk, this makes this smoothie different than others, but definetly better
