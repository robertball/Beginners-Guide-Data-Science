Title:
Chicken with zucchini and tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2446859.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp butter
1 package chicken thigh fillets
Flour
2 cloves garlic chopped
4 plum tomatoes chopped
1 zucchini peeled and chopped
1 chicken bouillon cube
1 cup water

Directions:
Cut chicken into strips, season with salt/pepper, and shake in a ziploc bag with flour. Brown in melted butter. Add garlic, zucchini and tomatoes; cover and cook for 5 minutes. Add bouillon dissolved in water. Cook until slightly thickened.
