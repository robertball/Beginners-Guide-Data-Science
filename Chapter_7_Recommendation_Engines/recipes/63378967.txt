Title:
Gourmet Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs cooked chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
1 4 oz can water chestnuts, chopped
3 fresh jalapenos, chopped
6 stalks celery, chopped
1 red onion, chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup coarse ground dijon mustard
2 tbsp pickle relish
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
MATTOON250
Description: 
This version of chicken salad is crunchy and has a kick to it. Mix up your lunch routine by serving this on a pita or croissant.
