Title:
Crazy Pineapple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 20oz. can pineapple chunks
2 eggs
1/4 t. salt
2 c. sugar
2 c. flour
1 t. baking soda
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. margarine
2/3 c. canned milk
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Beat eggs, salt, and first sugar together.
Add soda to flour and sift into egg mixture.
Mix pineapple in, juice and all. Pour into a greased 9x13 pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
FROSTING: Cook remaining (last 4) ingredients in a saucepan for 8 minutes. Pour over cake while both are still hot.
