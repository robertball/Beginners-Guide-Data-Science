Title:
Creamy Monkey Bread Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1 8oz. package cream cheese, cubed
1 stick butter, melted
1 cup sugar
2 tablespoons cinnamon
1 can of cream cheese frosting, warmed
1/2 cup pecans, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cut biscuits in half, set aside.
Cut cream cheese in 1/2 inch cubes, set aside.
Combine sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
Roll cream cheese cubes in the sugar mixture.
Wrap the cream cheese cubes in a biscuit half and seal.
Dip the stuffed biscuit in butter, then in the sugar mixture, place it in a 9x13 cake pan.
Repeat until all the biscuits are stuffed, dipped and placed in the pan.
Bake until bubbly and the biscuit dough is fully cooked. (15-20 minutes)
While the cake is still warm, drizzle warm cream cheese frosting over it.
Sprinkle pecans if desired.
Eat it like a monkey with your fingers while it is still warm!
