Title:
Grandma Carol's Sloppy Toms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground turkey
2 onions, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
2 tbsp sugar (optional - I prefer it without the sugar)
2 tbsp prepared mustard
2 tbsp vinegar
2 tbsp worcestshire sauce
1 tsp Tabasco sauce (or to taste)
1.5 cups ketchup
8 hamburger buns

Directions:
Brown turkey in skillet.
Drain liquid from browned turkey.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
HDN
Description: 
Grandma Carol's healthier version of sloppy joes, using ground turkey instead.
