Title:
Figgy Bourbon Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Bourbon
3/4 cup fig jam
1-1/2 cup buffalo wing sauce (or you can add 1 cup of your favorite hot sauce and 1/2 cup melted margarine)
1 teaspoon soy sauce
1 clove garlic, pressed

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a sauce pan and bring to a boil, stir often.
Reduce heat and allow to simmer to desired thickness.
Allow sauce to cool.

Submitter: 
Jose Napoleon Cuasay Artiaga I
Description: 
A sticky sweet and spicy sauce, this goes great on any wing - fried, grilled, baked, breaded, battered or naked. Fantastic with your favorite bleu cheese dip.
