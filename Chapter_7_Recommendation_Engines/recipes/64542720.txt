Title:
Stuffed Cauliflower Rice Peppers with Chicken and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium cauliflower, riced (see below)
4 large or 5 medium peppers
1 medium onion, minced
1 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 cups shredded, cooked chicken (rotisserie works fine)
1 - 2 tbsp. minced garlic
1 tsp. poultry seasoning or ground sage
2 tbsp. minced fresh parsley
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup whole milk or half and half
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
salt and pepper to taste
cayenne pepper (if desired) for garnish

Directions:
Prepare cauliflower rice by cutting out the core and putting in food processor or shred using large hand grater.
Slice tops off of peppers and discard seeds, etc.  Put in cooking dish where they can easily stand up without falling over and be covered.
Melt butter and oil in large fry pan, add minced onion and cook for two minutes.  Add mushrooms and cauliflower rice and cook for another 5 minutes stirring frequently.
Add garlic, poultry seasoning and fresh parsley, stir well and cook another minute.
Remove  fry pan from heat and stir in soup, milk, cheese. chicken and salt and pepper.
Stuff peppers full of fry pan mixture and push down, sprinkle with cayenne pepper.
Put covered, into 400 degree oven and cook for 30 minutes.  Uncover and cook for another 15 minutes.

Description: 
Different than a traditional stuffed pepper that usually utilizes regular rice and a tomato based sauce.
