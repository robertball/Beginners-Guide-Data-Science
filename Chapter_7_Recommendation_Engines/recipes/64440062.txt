Title:
Creamy Chicken Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken breasts, cooked & shredded
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 bunch baby spinach leaves (4-5 cups after itâ€™s chopped)
4 medium sized russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
4 large carrots, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 clove of garlic
6 cups chicken broth
1 tsp each: garlic powder, minced onion, salt, parsley flakes
1/2 tsp each: dill, salt, pepper
2 Tbsp arrowroot powder
2 Tbsp cold water
15 oz can evaporated milk

Directions:
In a large stockpot, saute vegetables in olive oil 5 minutes.
Add chicken and dried seasonings.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer, covered for 20 minutes.
In a small bowl, stir arrowroot and water until arrowroot is dissolved.  Stir in evaporated milk.  Pour mixture into soup and simmer 10 more minutes.
