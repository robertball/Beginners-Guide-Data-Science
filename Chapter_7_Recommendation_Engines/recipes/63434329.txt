Title:
Hot crabby-artie dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7-8 oz wedge parmesan freshly shredded
2 5 oz cans of crab meat drained(or 12 oz imitation crab)
1 THINLY sliced medium yellow onion
3/4 of 15 oz can of NON-marinated artichoke hearts drained
2 cups BEST FOODS mayo (has best fat content)
2 baguettes sliced across thin area at 3/8 inch 30 degree angle(for presentation)

Directions:
preheat oven to 450 degrees
mix first five ingredients in large bowl and place in 9x11 in. baking dish or single serve oval ramekins
place in oven and cook for 30-50 min depending
on dish size(s) until medium brown on top
remove and serve hot with baguette slices

Submitter: 
Chuck Dagel
Description: 
A wonderful gourmet appetizer with a beautiful caramel looking crust made with basic ingredients and having awesome results.
 Served in single portions it can also be an elegant little treat as appetizer course for dinner.
 Try it,I am sure it will become a favorite of your family for years to come,(and you can show off a little bit too!!).
