Title:
Jim's Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce, chopped into bite size pieces
1 can (14.5 oz) kidney beans, drained, rinsed and chilled
1 can (14.5 oz) medium black olives, chilled and drained
8 oz sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 Roma tomato, chopped
1 avocado, chopped
1 lb ground beef
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 bottle Italian salad dressing
1 bag nacho cheese Doritos

Directions:
Make taco meat using directions on the back of the seasoning packet.  Drain fat and set aside to cool slightly.
Toss together lettuce, beans, olives, cheese, tomato, avocado, meat and dressing.
Serve with a coarse crumble of Doritos over the top.
