Title:
Anise Wine Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 inches ginger root
2 cloves garlic
1 small onion
2 star anise
1/2 cup white wine
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tbsp cooking oil
1 tsp rice vinegar
2 breasts chicken
1 tbsp cooking oil
20 new potatoes
1 cup cherry tomatoes
4 tbsp thai basil, chopped
1 tbsp water + cold water to dissolve

Directions:
Marinate chicken in first 9 ingredients for 4 - 6 hours.  Meanwhile, boil potatoes until soft and cut in half.
Heat pan over high heat.  Add oil and heat.  Add chicken, reserving marinade.  Fry, stirring, until chicken is cooked on all sides.
Add potatoes, tomatoes, and rest of the marinade.  Cook for another 1 - 2 minutes.  Stir in basil.
Stir cornstarch into cold water and stir to dissolve.  Add slurry into pan, stirring right away.  Sauce will thicken.

Submitter: 
Victoria
Description: 
A delicious Chinese style stir-fry.  Normally, I would use Chinese cooking wine, but I was out of it.  The white wine adds a wonderful sweetness that pairs nicely with the star anise.
