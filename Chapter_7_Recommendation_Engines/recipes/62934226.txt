Title:
Bacon Feta carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Water 12 cups
Salt 1 1/2 tsp
Spaghettini 12 Oz
Whipping cream 1/2 cup
Egg yolks(large) 2
Olive oil 1 tsp
Bacon(sliced and diced) 4
Garlic cloves 1/8 tsp
ground nutmeg 1/8 tsp
Pepper 1/8 tsp
crumbled feta 3/4 cups
Parmesan 1/4 cup

Directions:
Boil pasta with salt for about 9 to 11 minutes then drain (*save 1/2 cup of water aside*)
Meanwhile, whisk cream and egg yolk in small bowl until combined set aside
Heat olive oil in pan on medium, cook bacon until crisp
Add Garlic, Nutmeg, and Pepper heat and stir for about 1 minute reduce heat to medium low. Add pasta, toss to coat. Add reserve water to cream mixture. stir add to frying pan toss to coat.
Add feta and Parmesan toss and serve immediately
