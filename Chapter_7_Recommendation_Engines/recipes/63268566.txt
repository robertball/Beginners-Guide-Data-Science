Title:
Grandmother's Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup cocoa powder (see note below)
3 eggs
3/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon salt (if using unsalted butter)
3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except the chocolate chips JUST UNTIL there are no clumps of dry.
Stir in the chocolate chips
Bake in a 9x13 pan, greased, at 325 degrees for 20-30 minutes.

Description:
