Title:
Liz's Strawberry and Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/968352.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cucumber - peeled, seeded, and sliced
2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
1 cup plain yogurt
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 tablespoons minced fresh mint
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Place cucumber and strawberries in a bowl. Mix yogurt, garlic, mint, and parsley together in a separate bowl; pour over cucumber and strawberries. Stir to combine. Chill for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
CarrieLou65
Description: 
With its seasonal red and green, this salad is perfect for a summertime Christmas in Australia.
