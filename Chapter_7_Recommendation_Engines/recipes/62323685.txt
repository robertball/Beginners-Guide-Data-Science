Title:
Layered Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
1 large tomato, chopped
1 bunch chopped green onions
1/2 small head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 (6 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a medium bowl, blend the taco seasoning mix and refried beans. Spread the mixture onto bottom of a 9 x13 baker. Mix the sour cream and cream cheese in a medium bowl. Spread over the refried beans.
Top the layers with salsa. Place a layer lettuce.  Top with cheddar cheese; sprinkle with tomato, a few green onions, and black olives. Chill until ready to serve.
