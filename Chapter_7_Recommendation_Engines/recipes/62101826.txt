Title:
Sour Cream Ham Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 full-sized smoked ham slice 1/2" thick,
fully-cooked...approx. 1 lb.(cut into 1/2" x 3" strips)
1 large or 2 regular-sized onions(sliced 1/4" thick)
1 Pkg fresh sliced Mushrooms(OR 1 lg. can or 2 small cans, drained, reserving liquid)
1 Pint Sour Cream
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
2 Tbsp. flour
4 Tbsp. butter
1 Tbsp. fresh chopped parsley
3 c. cooked white "minute" rice

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet on low heat.  Add ham strips, onion and pepper.  Saute until onions are tender. Sprinkle flour over mixture and stir briefly.  Then add sour cream and mushrooms, stirring well and constantly while mixture thickens and heats through.
Serve over cooked white "minute" rice and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
NOTE*** If you use canned mushroom slices, you may use reserved liquid to thin sauce if need be.  Fresh mushrooms will supply their own liquid to thin sauce or you may add a little milk if it needs further thinning)

Submitter: 
KELT
Description: 
My Mom always used this recipe with leftover smoked ham after New Year's and Easter, but You can whip it up with a thick ham slice from the market.  It's different and delicious, yet oh-so-easy!
