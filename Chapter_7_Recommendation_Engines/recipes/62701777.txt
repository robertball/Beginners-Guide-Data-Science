Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/611191.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 eggplants, sliced 1/2" thick
plain breadcrumbs
2 eggs
milk
1 pkg. mozzarella cheese packed in water
handful fresh basil
salt
pepper
1 jar Ragu garden pasta sauce

Directions:
Spray 9x13: pan with PAM.
Peel, slice, lay on paper towels.  Salt the eggplant on both side.  Let sweat for 30 minutes.  Rinse in colander.
Mix eggs and milk together.
Mix crumbs, basil, salt & pepper,
Dip eggplant in egg mixtures; dip in crumb mixture.
Place in pan with a 3-2-3; repeat.
Grate cheese.  Sprinkle a little on eggplant.
Pour sauce over.
Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
Sprinkle with more basil.
Bake at 375 degrees for 45-90 minutes or until tender.
