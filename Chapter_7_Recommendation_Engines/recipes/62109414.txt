Title:
Vegetarian Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons canola oil
2 teaspoons diced garlic
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes with juice
1 large onion, chopped
1 large green bell pepper, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup mock chicken broth
2 teaspoons dried oregano
2 teaspoons dried parsley
2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1 can Big Franks, sliced
2-3 cans of Fri Chik, chopped
1 box sliced mushrooms
1 cup brown rice

Directions:
Heat the oil on medium heat in a large pan and then saut√© the garlic, onion, mushrooms, bell pepper, & celery until the onion is clear. Then add the tomatoes with juice, sliced Fri Chik and Big Franks, rice, and spices. Stir thoroughly for about 10 minutes. Then put in a large corningware dish and bake in the oven at 350 degrees for about 60 minutes.
