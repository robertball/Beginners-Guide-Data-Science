Title:
Slow Cooker Mexican Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb beef stew meat
1/2 cup flour
4 Tbsp oil
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 can (28 oz)diced tomatoes, undrained
1 red onion, chopped or sliced
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 package (1 oz)taco seasoning mix
1 can (15 oz)black beans, drained, rinsed
1 cup frozen corn

Directions:
Stir the flour, garlic, salt and pepper together.
Dredge the beef in the flour mixture, then brown the beef in heated oil in a large saucepan.
Remove the meat to drain.
Mix beef, tomatoes, onions, chili powder, taco seasoning mix. beans and corn in 3 1/2- to 4-quart slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low heat setting 6 - 7 hours or until beef is tender.
