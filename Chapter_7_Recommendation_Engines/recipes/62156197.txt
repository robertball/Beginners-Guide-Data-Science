Title:
Southern Buttermilk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Eggs, lightly beaten
1 C Granular sugar
2 TB All-purpose flour
1/2 C Butter, melted
1 C Buttermilk
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 9-inch pie shell, unbaked

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine eggs, sugar, flour, and butter and mix well.
Add buttermilk, vanill, and lemon and mix well.
Pour into pie shell and bake for 45-50 minutes until set and golden brown.

Submitter: 
Donna Bertheola LeMaster
Description: 
A deeply rich, easy to make, smooth custard pie to die for. This is NOT a run-of-the-mill pie, this one's a keeper!
