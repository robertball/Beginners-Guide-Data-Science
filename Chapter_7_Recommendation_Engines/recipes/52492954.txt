Title:
Swirled Cheesecake Nibbles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
36 2-inch foil baking cups
36 vanilla wafers
1 2/3 C (10oz pkg.) Nestles toll house swirled semi-sweet and premier white morsels, divided
2 pkgs. (8oz ea.) cream cheese,room temperature
1/2 C sugar
2 T flour
2 eggs
1 t vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees. Place 36 baking cups on baking sheet w/sides. Put 1 vanilla wafer, flat side down on bottom of ea. cup. Place 5-6 morsels on top of ea. wafer.
Beat cream cheese, sugar and flour in lg. mixing bowl until creamy. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well. Spoon heaping tablespoon of cream cheese mix into ea. cup.
Bake for 15-17 min. or until just set and not browned. Remove from oven to wire rack. While still warm, top cheesecakes w/remaining morsels. Morsels will soften but will retain shape. Cool completely. Cover and refrigerate.
