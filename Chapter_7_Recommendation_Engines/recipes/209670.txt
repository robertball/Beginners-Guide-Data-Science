Title:
Chicken Satay Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988777.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 3/4 cups water
2 tablespoons Skippy® Creamy or Super Chunk® Peanut Butter
1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar or white vinegar
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger (optional)
1 (4.6 ounce) package Knorr® Sides Plus™ Veggies - Vegetable Fried Rice
1/4 cup sliced green onions

Directions:
Season chicken, if desired, with salt and pepper. Heat oil in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and cook chicken, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Remove chicken and set aside.
Stir water, peanut butter, vinegar and ginger into same skillet. Bring to a boil over high heat. Stir in Knorr(R) Sides Plus(TM) Veggies - Vegetable Fried Rice. Cover and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, 8 minutes or until rice is tender.
Stir in chicken and green onions; heat through. Serve, if desired, with finely chopped fresh cilantro, fresh lime wedges and chopped peanuts.

Submitter: 
Knorr
Description: 
Golden brown chicken strips are stirred into a savory rice mix flavored with peanut butter, ginger, and green onions, then served with optional garnishes of cilantro, lime wedges and chopped peanuts.
