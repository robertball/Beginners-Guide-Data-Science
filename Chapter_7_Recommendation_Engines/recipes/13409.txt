Title:
Strawberry Soup IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/420026.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup dry white wine
1/3 cup white sugar
2 cups chopped strawberries
1 cup orange juice

Directions:
In a small saucepan mix wine and sugar, and bring to a boil.  Let boil for 5 minutes and then remove from heat.  Let cool.
Puree strawberries and wine mixture in a blender or food processor.  Blend until very smooth and then stir in orange juice. Chill for at least 3 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
GODGIFU
Description: 
This is a very nice soup to eat during hot summer days. The flavor of the wine in this soup is important, so be sure to use a good one.
