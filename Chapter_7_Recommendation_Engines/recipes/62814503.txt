Title:
Honey-Lime Fruit Salad (Crowd Size) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2	 cup honey
1/2	 cup frozen limeade concentrate, (thawed)
1	 tablespoon poppy seed, if desired
12	 cups cut-up fresh fruit, (cantaloupe, kiwifruit, pink grapefruit)
1/2	 cup slivered almonds, toasted

Directions:
Mix honey, limeade concentrate and poppy seed in medium bowl.
Carefully toss fruit with honey mixture. Sprinkle with almonds.
