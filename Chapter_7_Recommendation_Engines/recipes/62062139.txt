Title:
Totally Awesome Guacamole Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 c. sour cream
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp Tapatio hot sauce (more if desired)
Juice from 1/2 fresh lime
Salt & pepper to taste
6 slices bacon, fried crisp, crumbled

Directions:
Chop avocado in small pieces and place in a mixing bowl. Add remaining ingredients except bacon. With an electric mixer or blender mix the ingredients till smooth. Stir in crumbled bacon. If not serving immediately, place a plastic type wrap directly over the dip and refrigerate. By doing this the guacamole will not turn brown. Serve with your favorite chips.
