Title:
Not Quite Hellmann's Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065210.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg (at room temp)
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cup oil (corn, vegetable, or canola)
1 dash cayenne pepper
3 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar

Directions:
Place egg, mustard, salt, cayenne pepper and 1/4 c. oil in blender and blend on low.
While the machine is blending, SLOWLY pour in another 1/2 c. oil. You may have to stop and scrape down the mayo. Add the lemon juice or vinegar and the remaining 1/2 cup oil. Blend until well combined.
Store in refrigerator, tightly covered.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Not Quite because there is no preservatives in this one so it will never taste Exact.  But Wow!  It's Mayo baby! I like it. so close to Hellmann's I will not be paying 4 dollars for a 30 ounce jar again.
