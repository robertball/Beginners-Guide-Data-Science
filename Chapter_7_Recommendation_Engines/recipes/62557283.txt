Title:
Mary's Baked Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Cream Cheese Softened
1     Cup Cottage Cheese
1/4   Cup Sour Cream
1     26 oz Jar of Spaghetti Sauce
1 1/2 lbs Hamburger
1     lb Angel Hair Spaghetti
1/2   Cup Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Spray a casserole dish with a non-stick spray.
Cook the angel hair pasta and put one half of the pasta in the casserole dish.
Mix the cream cheese, sour cream and cottage cheese in a small bowl and spread over the noodles.
Put the other half of the noodles on top of this.
Mix the hamburger and spaghetti sauce and pour over the noodles.
Sprinkle the parmesan cheese on the top.
Bake at 350 for 35-45 minutes.
Enjoy!
