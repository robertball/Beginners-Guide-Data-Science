Title:
Stef's Sizzling Spinach & Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz. Artichoke Hearts, drained, coursely chopped
1 cup of frozen spinach, thawed & drained
1/4 cup of mayonnaise
8 oz. Cream Cheese, room temperature
3/4 cup of Parmesan Cheese
3/4 cup of Mozzarella Cheese, shredded, divided
1 Garlic Clove
1/2 tsp. Garlic Salt
1/2 tsp. Basil

Directions:
Mix together mayonnaise, cream cheese, parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, garlic clove, garlic salt, & basil.  Add in artichoke hearts & spinach.  Pour mixture into 8x8 baking dish.  Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.  Bake on 350 for 25 minutes or until cheese is browned.

Submitter: 
Stefany Grider Baldwin
Description: 
A delicious blend of spinach & artichoke topped with mozzarella cheese.  Serve with toasty bread.
