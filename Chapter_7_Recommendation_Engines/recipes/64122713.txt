Title:
Mustard Glazed Corned Beef & Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bay leaves
1 3-3 1/2 lb corned beef brisket
8 C. water
1 small onion, halved
2 medium carrots, quartered crosswise
3 small potatoes
3 stalks celery, quartered crosswise
1/2 small head of cabbage, cut in 6 wedges
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup prepared mustard
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Mustard Glaze: In medium saucepan combine brown sugar, vinegar, mustard and garlic powder. Bring to boil, stirring until sugar dissolves. Cook uncovered 5 minutes.
Corned Beef & Cabbage: Place corned beef in a 4-6 quart dutch oven. Add spice packet and water to coned beef. Bring to boiling;reduce heat. Cover and simmer 2 1/2 hours.
Add onions, carrots, potatoes and celery to corned beef. Cover and simmer 10 minutes. Add cabbage wedges; simmer covered about 10 minutes more or until vegetables are tender.
Drain meat and vegetables and arrange on a serving platter. Spoon mustard glaze over meat and vegetables. Slice corned beef across the grain.
