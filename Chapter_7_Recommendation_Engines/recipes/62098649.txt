Title:
Tangy BBQ sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 litre ketchup
1 lg onion finely chopped
2 tbsp butter
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/2 c brown sugar
3 tbsp soya sauce
3 tbsp worchestire sauce
3 tbsp vinegar
3 tbsp lemon juice
3 tsp ginger
3 tsp chili powder
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Fry onions in butter til translucent. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer on low for 1 hr or longer of desired.

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
A girlfriend gave me this recipe years ago and I have had many requests since.I prepare meatballs (cooked)then pour the sauce over top then place in a slow cooker on low and let simmer all afternoon.
