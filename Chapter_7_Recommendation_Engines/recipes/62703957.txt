Title:
Fish Fillets Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian style diced tomatoes
1/2 cup black olives, pitted and sliced
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 pound cod fillets

Directions:
In a large frying pan, heat oil over medium heat.  Saute onions and garlic in olive oil until softened.
Stir in tomatoes, olives, parsley, and wine.  Simmer for 5 minutes.
Place fillets in sauce.  Simmer for about 5 more minutes, or until fish turns white.

Submitter: 
Salvador
Description: 
Cod cut thick and haddock fillets work well for this braised dish.  An extra easy and quick way to fix a superb tasting fish dish!Can add 1lb of raw shelled shrimp the same time as fish. Substitute Talapia, Orange Roughy. Serve over Rice,pasta or wilted Spinach.Modified from: Fish Fillets Italiano
