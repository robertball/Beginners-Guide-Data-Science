Title:
Hawaiian Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/753994.jpg
Ingredients:
- Whole Ham
- Round Pineapple slices (2 Cans)
- Maraschino Cherries
- 1cup Brown Sugar
- Cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees!
In a small saucepan add a cup of brown sugar with 1/2 cup of water, once brown sugar is melted add cloves (1 Tbsp) bring to a light simmer, set aside.
Garnish the ham with the slices of round pineapple slices, add a cherry in the middle & toothpick it into the ham. 3) Pour the brown sugar sauce over the ham spread evenly & place the ham in the oven for about 20 minutes! Serve & Enjoy!
