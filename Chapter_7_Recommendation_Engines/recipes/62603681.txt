Title:
Guevara's Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/561381.jpg
Ingredients:
1# black beans
1 clove garlic, smashed
1 T. salt
2 med. sweet onions, chopped
¼ t. ground cloves
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 large carrot, chopped
½ cup chopped celery
1 red pepper, chopped
A pinch of black pepper
2 cups canned diced tomatoes
1 bay leaf
2 t. salt
1 T. chili powder
Finely chopped sweet onion for garnish
Mexican-style grated cheese for garnish

Directions:
Clean, rinse and soak 1# of black beans in cold water overnight. Drain, cover with 2 quarts fresh water, add one clove of garlic (smashed) and simmer for 3-4 hours, or until beans are soft. When fully cooked, add 1 t. salt and cook ½ hour more. Remove garlic. Do not drain. Sauté ingredients 4-10 in olive oil. Add sautéed ingredients to beans in cooking water. Add tomatoes, bay leaf, salt and chili powder and Simmer 1 hour. Use hand blender to blend until uniformly smooth. This can now be served immediately, or refrigerated for several days.  It only gets better!
For serving, add good dark rum, about ½ cup, and heat. Ladle into bowls, garnishing with grated Mexican cheese and finely chopped onion.

Submitter: 
Fred Guevara
Description: 
The most delicious soup I have tasted! A rich and hearty delight that is a favorite with our holiday party guests, this festive and flavorful soup will warm any chilly night!
