Title:
Meatball Parmesan Hot Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4531695.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) package frozen cooked Italian-style meatballs, thawed
1 (26 ounce) jar tomato pasta sauce
1 (14.5 ounce) can Del Monte® Diced Tomatoes, undrained
1 (14.5 ounce) can Del Monte® Sliced Carrots, drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can Del Monte® Cut Green Beans, drained
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 (32 ounce) package frozen Ore-Ida® Tater Tots®
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Chopped fresh basil (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
Combine meatballs, pasta sauce, tomatoes, carrots and green beans in a 13x9-inch baking dish; sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and top with Tater Tots(R).
Bake until sauce is bubbly and Tater Tots(R) are golden brown, about 30 to 35 minutes.  Remove from oven; sprinkle Tater Tots(R) with Parmesan cheese and garnish with basil, if desired.
