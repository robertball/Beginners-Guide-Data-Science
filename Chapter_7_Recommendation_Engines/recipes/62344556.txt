Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/393514.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Minute Rice
3 Small Tomatoes
1 Cup of Marinara Sauce
1/2 a Onion
2 Stalks of Celery
One Red Bell Pepper
One or Two Jalenpeno Peppers
One Can of Pinto or Black Beans
1 teaspoon Chili Powder
Salt/ Pepper
4 teaspoons of Taco Bell Hot Sauce
1 Tablespoon minced garlic
Garlic Powder
Onion Powder
1/2 Cup Salsa
Paprika
2 Cups Water

Directions:
Saute the onion, celery, peppers, and tomatoes. Add Spices, beans, rice, sauces and water. Cover on Med High for 5 Min. or till rice is cooked.
