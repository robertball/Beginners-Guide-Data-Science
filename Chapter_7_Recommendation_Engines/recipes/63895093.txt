Title:
Super Easy Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Torn romaine lettuce
2 medium cucumbers, quartered and thinly sliced
Chopped scallions
Grape Tomatoes
Cilantro
Ranch dressing
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a bowl, toss together the lettuce, cucumbers, scallions, grape tomatoes and cilantro. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with Ranch Dressing or other dressing of your choice.
