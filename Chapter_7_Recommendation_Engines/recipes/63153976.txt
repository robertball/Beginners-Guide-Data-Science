Title:
Antipasto Pasta Salad (NIGHT BEFORE) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR THE VINAIGRETTE:
1 1/2 T capers, drained
2 t anchovy paste
2 t garlic, minced
1/4 c red wine vinegar
1 t dried oregano
black pepper to taste
FOR SALAD:
8 oz tri-color rotini pasta, cooked
2 c cucumbers, seeded and sliced
1 c grape/cherry tomatoes, halved
1 c smoked mozzarella, diced
1 c Italian salami, diced
1 c red onion, thinly sliced
3/4 c kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
1/2 c pepperoncini, rinsed and stemmed
1/2 c roasted red peppers, sliced
1/2 c marinated artichokes, quartered
2 T capers, drained

Directions:
Cook pasta, drain and rinse with cold water. Set aside.
Puree capers, anchovy paste, garlic.
Add vinegar and oregano. Pulse.
Drizzle in oil and blend.
Season with pepper.
Mix together cooked pasta, cucumbers, tomatoes, mozzarella, salami, onion, olives, pepperoncini, roasted red peppers, artichoke hearts, capers in large bowl.
Drizzle and toss salad with vinaigrette.
Refrigerate overnight.
