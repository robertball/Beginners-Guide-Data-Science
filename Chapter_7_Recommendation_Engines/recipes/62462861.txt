Title:
Delmar's Shrimp Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 cups of shrimp, shelled and deveined
1 small box Uncle Ben's Rice (white or wild rice)
1 small jar pimentos, drained and chopped
1 can sliced water chestnuts
1 can cream of celery soup
1 c. mayo
1 small onion, chopped (1/2 cup)
Grated chedder cheese

Directions:
Cooke rice according to package directions.
Saute shrimp in butter and Old Bay until no longer pink.
Mix all ingredients (except cheese) and put in a 13x9 dish. Top with grated cheddar cheese. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
