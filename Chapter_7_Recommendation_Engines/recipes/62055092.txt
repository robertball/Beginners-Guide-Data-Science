Title:
creamy monty cristos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices good quality sandwich bread,6 slices very thin,preferably shaved deli meat such as ham or turkey or a combination of both.2 very thin slices swiss cheese.1 egg,2 T. milk.2 T.sour cream,1 t.powdered sugar,strawberry preserves

Directions:
mix egg and milk together with fork.dip bread slices in egg mixture and lightly brown on medium heat skillet in a bit of melted butter. assemble sandwich with meat and cheese and heat until bread is golden and cheese is melted.remove from heat and top with sour cream and a sprinkle of powdered sugar.serve with preserves on the side.

Submitter: 
LINDA
Description: 












a simple and rich hot sandwich.I was raised thinking these were how all monty cristos were made. they are special,easy and guest-worthy!
