Title:
Sugar Substitute Cranberry Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C peeled, cored, sliced apples
2 C fresh or frozen thawed cranberries
1 C sugar substitute
Topping: 1/3 C flour
1/4 C chopped pecans
1/4 C butter or margarine, melted
1/2 C sugar substitute

Directions:
Combine apples, cranberries and 1 C sugar in ungreased 10-in pie plate.
Combine flour, butter and 1/2 C sugar. Mix until well blended. Sprinkle flour mixture over apples. Bake 350 degrees for 55-60 min. or until fruit is tender.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Make with sugar substitute and fresh or frozen cranberries
