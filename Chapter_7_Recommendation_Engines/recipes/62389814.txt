Title:
Lemon Mint Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups water
6 sprigs mint
6 lemon balm leaves
1 cup white wine (moscato wine is a good choice)
2 tablespoons lemon juice

Directions:
Place sugar and water in saucepan with herbs. Bring to boil. Remove to heat and add wine. Cover and cool. Chill for serveral hours, then add lemon juice.
Freeze in a freezer safe container. As soon as it begins to freeze stir briskly and return to freezer. repeat every 15 mins for at least 3 hours, or until ready to serve.
Alternately, use an icecream machine

Submitter: 
Michael Bach
Description: 
Try making some mint and lemon balm sorbet. Its good and refreshing, especially on a hot summer day.
