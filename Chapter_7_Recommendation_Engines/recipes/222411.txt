Title:
Sea Breeze Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1043977.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
4 fluid ounces cranberry juice
1 fluid ounce grapefruit juice
1 1/2 cups ice cubes
1 lime wedge

Directions:
Combine vodka, cranberry juice, and grapefruit juice in a highball glass and stir to combine. Add ice.
Garnish with a wedge of lime.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
The cocktail is as refreshing as its name. The sea breeze cocktail is a refreshing blend of vodka, cranberry juice, and grapefruit juice. Plant your beach umbrella and enjoy this simple drink.
