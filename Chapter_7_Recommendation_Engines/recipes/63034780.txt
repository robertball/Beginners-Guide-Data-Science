Title:
Italian Love Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package chocolate cake mix
2 lbs ricotta cheese
3/4 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
FROSTING
1 small box vanilla instant pudding
1 cup milk
1 8oz container of cool whip

Directions:
Mix cake according to directions on box..  Pour into 13X9 inch pan.  Mix cheese, sugar, eggs and vanilla until smooth.  Pour on top of cake mix.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Let cool before frosting.
For frosting, mix all ingredients until well mixed and frost cake when cool.
Keep cake refrigerated!

Submitter:

Description: 
This is kind of a mix between a cake and a cheesecake.  It was a favorite of mine as a child.
