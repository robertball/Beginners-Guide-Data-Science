Title:
Coffee Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977661.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (no substitutes), softened
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons instant coffee granules
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips, melted
1/2 cup vanilla or white chips, melted

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter, sugars and coffee granules.  Gradually beat in flour and salt.
On a lightly floured surface, roll out to 1/4-in. thickness. Cut with floured 2-in. to 3-in. cookie cutters. Place 2 in. apart on ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 300 degrees F for 20-22 minutes or until set. Remove to wire racks to cool. Drizzle with melted chips.

Submitter: 
Dixie  Terry
Description: 
'You'll be remembered for these cookies when you serve them for a morning coffee or at a gathering,' predicts Dixie Terry, Marion, Illinois. 'Melted chips drizzled on top make them look fancy, but they're so easy to make.'
