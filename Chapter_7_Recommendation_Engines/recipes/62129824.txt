Title:
Alta's Mexican Style Tuna Salad and Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/322287.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6oz) cans white albacore tuna in water, well drained and flaked.
1 small green bell pepper, finely chopped
1 small red bell pepper, finely chopped
1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 scallions, finely chopped
1/3 cup Pace Salsa Verde (green salsa)
6 - 8 large pimento stuffed green olives, chopped
4 tbsp reduced fat or fat free mayonnaise or salad dressing
Juice from 1 small lime
1 tsp finely chopped garlic
2 level tsp ground cumin
Ground black pepper (to taste)
1 tbsp honey (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients mixing with a fork - and serve atop a bed of lettuce or with savory whole wheat crackers for a healthy snack.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
This is a healthier, lower calorie version of tuna salad. You just can't stop eating it! It makes a great dip or spread with crackers.
Sooo delicious! Only 198 calories per serving.
