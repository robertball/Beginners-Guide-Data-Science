Title:
Tiny Limoncello Popcycles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c sugar
2 1/4 c freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 9 lemons)
1 1/2 c limoncello liqueur.

Directions:
Prepare 50 1 oz popcycle molds and holders (or ice cube trays with wooden sticks).
Prepare a large bowl filled with ice cubes, and 1/2 c water.
In a sauce pan bring 1 1/2 c water and sugar to a boil over medium heat. Stirring frquently, cook until sugar has dissolved, about 10 mins. Transfer to a bowl, and place in the bowl with ice. Stir until mixture is chilled.
In a large bowl, combine sugar misture, lemon juice, and liqueur with 3/4 c water. Pour into molds, freeze 1-2 hrs. Add sticks. Freeze completley.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A delightfully refreshing treat with a little kick. Can't go wrong with limoncello.
