Title:
Aunt Jo's Pumpkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/335163.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons baking soda
2 tablespoons cinnamon
4 eggs beaten
2 cups granulated sugar
1 cup oil
2 cups pumpkin
1/2 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
chopped walnuts
3 oz. cream cheese
2 cups confectioners sugar

Directions:
CAKE: Combine flour, salt, soda and cinnamon in a large bowl. Mix eggs and granulated sugar in a small bowl. Add the small bowl to the large bowl and mix well. Fold in the oil and pumpkin. Place in greased and floured tube pan (or bundt pan) Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
ICING: Mix together butter, vanilla, nuts (optional), cream cheese, and confectioners sugar.
Spread icing on cooled cake.

Submitter: 
Paul Nenni
Description: 
My Aunt Josephine brought this cake to her family reunion for a large Italian family to enjoy every year. She was my Godmother and died at a very young age in 1967 while I was in Marine Corps boot camp. She owned a restaurant in Huntington, WV and was a great cook.
