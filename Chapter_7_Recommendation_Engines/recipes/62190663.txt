Title:
Best Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C. baked yams (smashed)
1/2 C. Sugar
1/4 c. Milk
1/2 C. butter or margarine
1 tsp. Vanilla
1/2 c. coconut
2 eggs
1 C. chopped pecans
1 C. Brown Sugar
1/2 C. Butter or margarine
1/2 C. flour

Directions:
Mix first 7 ingredients and place in a 9 x 13 baking dish.  Mix the next 4 ingredients until the mixture is nice and crumbly.
Place dish in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes or until the topping is slightly brown

Submitter: 
Sandy Scott
Description: 
This casserole is easy to make but absolutely delicious.  Even guest who hate sweet potatoes love this dish!
