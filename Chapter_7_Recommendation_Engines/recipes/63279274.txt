Title:
Party Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 Medium potatoes, cooked whole, peeled & diced
1 Large green pepper, diced
1 Small jar diced pimento
1 Large Spanish onion, diced
3 Slices white bread, cubed
1 bunch parsley, chopped
1/2 Lb. processed cheese, (Velveeta) cubed
3/4- 1 C. Butter or Margarine, melted
1/4-1/2 C. Milk ( lowfat may be substituted)
Corn flake crumbs (about 1 cup)

Directions:
Cook potatoes, cool, peel and dice
Mix the potatoes, green epper, pimento, onion, bread, parsley, and cheese together in a large bowl.
Salt and pepper taste.
Pour melted butter or margarine over and mix well.
Place in a 9x13 baking dish (glass)
Pour enough milk over to moisten the ingredients.
Crush corn flakes over top.
Bake 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
