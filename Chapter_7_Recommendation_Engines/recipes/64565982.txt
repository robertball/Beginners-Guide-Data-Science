Title:
fish in garlic sauce~~~filete de piscado mojo de ajo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tilapia 12 filets /6 persons
2 large garlic bulps  ~6 Tablespoons   minced
1 1/4 bars butter
3 small lines

Directions:
1. Mince garlic
2. melt one bar of butter in medium frying pan and cook garlic for 5 minutes
3.add lime juice to ajo and let cool
4. fry filets in cast iron frying pan in butter. Keep cooked fish warm in oven on a plate.
5.when all the filetes are fried put garlic and butter & lime in cast iron pot and mix up all the filetes in it. Ready to serve
