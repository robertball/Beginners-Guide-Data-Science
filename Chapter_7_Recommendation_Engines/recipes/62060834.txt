Title:
Layered Meal Deal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean hamburger
1 can tomato sauce
bread crumbs / or crushed crackers
onion powder
garlic powder
salt / pepper
1/2 large onion sliced in rings
4 - 5 large potatoes peeled and sliced in thin circles
1 pkg shredded cabbage
1 can low fat cream of mushroom soup
several slices velveeta cheese
optional: 1/2 can fried onion rings

Directions:
Prepare meat with tomato sauce, crumbs, and seasonings. Pat in the bottom of 9x13 pyrex dish.
Add the next layer of onions, potatoes, and finally cabbage.  Top with mushroom soup and cook at 400 degrees for about an hour.  Top with velveeta and onion rings the last 5 to 10 mins of cooking.

Description: 
Layered casserole with meatloaf type base, potatoe layer, and cabbage.
