Title:
'Shortcut' Cranberry Orange Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups of baking mix
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup of orange juice
2 Tblsp Heavy Cream
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
rind of one orange
6 oz dried cranberries
1 egg beaten with 1 Tblsp heavy cream

Directions:
Combine baking mix and sugar in a large mixing bowl.  In a separate bowl, mix orange juice, heavy cream, vanilla, egg and orange rind.  Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until incorporated.  (batter will be crumbly.)  Fold in cranberries.
Roll out dough into a 9" circle on a floured surface and divide into 12 pieces.
Place scones on a parchment covered baking sheet, and brush with egg/cream mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 18 minutes.
(Optional-drizzle with glaze made from confectioners sugar/orange juice)

Submitter: 
Cindy Peterson Kilday
Description: 
These scones use a baking mix instead of cutting butter into a flour mix and making scones the old fashioned way.  They're a perfect scone consistency.
