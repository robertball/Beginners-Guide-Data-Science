Title:
Cheddar Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 Tablespoon baking powder
1 Teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/3 cup butter
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2/3 cup milk
1 large egg
2 Tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease or line the cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Combine the baking powder, salt, and garlic powder.  Combine milk and egg in a seperate bowl.  Mix milk mixture and dry ingredients until a chunky texture is formed, careful not to over mix the biscuits.
Drop batter by tablespoonfuls or larger if desired onto cookie sheet.
Bake in a preheated oven for about 10 minutes.  Brush melted butter on top and bake about 5 mintues more or until golden brown.
