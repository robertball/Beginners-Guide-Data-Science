Title:
Dirty, Bloody Maria with Herdez® Guajillo Chile Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006566.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz tomato or vegetable juice
1/4-1/2 C Herdez® Guajillo Chile Cooking Sauce
1tsp Worchestershire sauce
1 Tbsp olive juice
1/8 tsp. celery salt
1/4 fresh lemon, juiced
1/2 fresh lime, juiced
black pepper to taste
1/2-1 tsp prepared horseradish, according to taste
2 oz Vodka
lemon and lime slices
optional garnishes:
olives
celery stalk with leaves
Sprig of parsley or cilantro
cucumber
dill pickles
cherry tomatoes
pickled okra
artichoke hearts
slices of lemon and lime
*use your imagination*

Directions:
Combine the first 10 ingredients into a large shaker filled with ice.  Shake vigorously. Strain into 2 chilled pint glasses.  Garnish with desired   vegetables, lemon or lime slices

Submitter: 
Tracey Ferrari Posner
Description: 
The perfect way to start off a Sunday morning over a lovely brunch, sitting outside on a sunny day. Just the thing to ward off a hangover from the night before had you over indulged :-)
Bonus :  there is no limit to how mild or spicy you want it.  Simply adjust the Guajillo or horseradish sauce until you achieve YOUR level of heat.
