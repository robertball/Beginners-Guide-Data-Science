Title:
Stuffed Chicken Breast with Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125877.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Fresh Chicken Breast,1/2 CUP Pesto, 1/2 LARGE Chopped onions, 2 crushed garlic, Monterey Jack Cheese. Bread crumbs, olive oil

Directions:
preheat oven to 350, spoon out some pesto and spread on the inside then layer on top of pesto sauted onions & garlic also a slice of Monterey Jack. Close flap over and gently roll in bread crumbs. Add olive oil to frying pan heat to med high and sear chicken to brown for about 5 min a side. Get a baking dish and add a bit of  olive oil spread sauted chicken and bake until the juices run clear. Dont over cook. It will be tender, juicy and so delicious! ENJOY...

Description:
