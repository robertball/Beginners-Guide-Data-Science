Title:
Homemade Thin Crust Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1646019.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 Cups of Lukewarm Water
1 Teaspoon of Instant Yeast
2 Cups of Flour
1/4 Teaspoon of Salt

Directions:
Combine water and yeast; stir to dissolve the yeast.
Add flour and salt; kneed for 5 minutes.
Cover the dough and let dough rise for 1 hour.
Set oven to 450F.
Work dough until it covers the area of a pizza crisper.
Brush with olive oil and add toppings.
Cook until edges are browning and crispy.
