Title:
Deluxe Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 env. Italian salad dressing mix
2-10oz pkgs chopped frozen spinach, uncooked, thawed & drained
1/4 cup sauted sliced almonds (opt)
1 can (8 1/2oz) water chestnuts
3 oz french fried onion rings

Directions:
Mix sour cream & dressing mix. Add to spinach. Add almonds & water chestnuts. Mix together & place in 2 qt greased casserole or oblong pan. Top with crushed onion rings.
Bake at 350 for 15-20min.
