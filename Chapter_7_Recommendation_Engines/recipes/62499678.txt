Title:
Insanely Amazing Jalapeno Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers, drained
1 cup shredded Mexican Blend shredded cheese
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
1 can Rotel tomatoes and chilies, drained (any variety you prefer)
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 clove garlic minced (or 1/2 tsp garlic powder)

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well
Put into a baking dish that you can serve out of.
Bake in the preheated oven for 50-55 minutes.
Length of time in over may vary depending how melted you want the dip- warm and still firm is about 45 minutes - gooey is an hour.
