Title:
Honey and Feta Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3712896.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 ounce) package feta cheese
1 cup honey
3 tablespoons cracked black peppercorns

Directions:
Place the block of feta cheese in the center of a large dinner plate. Drizzle honey over the top. Sprinkle with cracked black pepper. Serve with crackers.

Description: 
Though it sounds odd, this delicious appetizer is quick and easy.  It always receives rave reviews at parties.
