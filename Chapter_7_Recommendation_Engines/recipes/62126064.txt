Title:
Creamed Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c small pearl onions, peeled
3/4 c water
4 T butter
1 1/2 c whole milk
4 T Wondra flour
salt to taste

Directions:
In a small saucepan, cook onions in water and salt on low until just tender.
Drain, reserving 1/2 c water in pan.  Add butter to onions in pan.
Mix Wondra into cold milk and add to saucepan.  Cook on med-low until thickened.  If too thin, add a bit more Wondra in cold milk.

Submitter: 
Erin Noel
Description: 
My great-grandma's recipe.  Always a favorite at Thanksgiving dinner.  Worth using fresh onions instead of frozen.
