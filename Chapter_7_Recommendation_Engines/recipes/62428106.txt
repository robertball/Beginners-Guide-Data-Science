Title:
Pasta With Eggplant-Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med. eggplant
1 med. onion
28 oz. tomatoes, Italian-style
1 can tomato paste, Italian-style
4 oz. canned mushrooms
1/4 C dry red wine or broth
1/4 C water
2 cloves minced garlic
1 1/2 t. dried oregano
1/2 C Kalamata olives, pitted
2 T parsley
salt and pepper
4 C penne pasta
1/3 C Parmesan cheese
2 T pine nuts, toasted

Directions:
Peel eggplant; cut into 1-in cubes. In a slow cooker combine eggplant, onion, undrained tomatoes, tomato paste, mushrooms, wine or broth, water, garlic, and oregano.
Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 7-8 hrs. or on high-heat setting for 3 1/2 to 4 hrs.
Stir in olives and parsley. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve over pasta with Parmesan cheese, and if desired, toasted pine nuts.
