Title:
Mom's Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 small onion - diced
1/4 cup taco seasoning
1 cup shredded cheese
1 tomato - diced
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup sour cream
taco sauce
corn salsa
8 taco shells

Directions:
Brown meat with onions.  When meat almost done, stir in taco seasoning.  Drain browned meat and keep warm.
Warm taco shells according to package instructions.
Divide meat by 8 and place into taco shells, place cheese on top of meat.  Add other toppings as desired.

Basic Tacos - easy to make on a busy night!
