Title:
Apple-Mango Madness Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4461293.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen mango cubes
1/2 cup fat free milk
1/2 cup apple juice
1/2 banana
1 packet Classic French Vanilla Flavor CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® LIGHT START™

Directions:
Place mango, milk, apple juice, banana, Carnation Breakfast Essentials Drink and ice in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation Breakfast Essentials
Description: 
Taste the tropics in minutes with the flavors of banana, apple, mango and creamy French Vanilla in this delicious Apple-Mango Madness smoothie!
