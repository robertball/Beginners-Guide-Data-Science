Title:
Healthier "Ricotta" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
1 tablespoon of egg whites
1 tablespoon almond milk
garlic powder, to taste
onion powder, to taste
pepper

Directions:
Place skillet over medium heat. Add all ingredients and stir until well combined. Reduce heat to low to medium-low and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Use to replace ricotta in a recipe.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This was a happy mistake that I found yields  a wonderfully ricotta-esque product that is lighter and healthier than actual ricotta.
