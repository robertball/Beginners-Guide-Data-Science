Title:
peach cini cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
3 eggs
6 Tbl sugar
2 tsp cinnimon
1 can 21 oz peach pie filling

Directions:
350 oven
mix cake , eggs and 1/2 pie filling. sir in remaining filling
mix sugar and cinnimon, spread half cake on bottom 9x13 greased pan, 1/2 sugar mix, 1/2 cake top with sugar.bake 30-35 min
