Title:
Cherry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972728.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16 ounce can cherry pie filling
8 1/4 ounce dry white cake mix
1/2 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup melted butter (NOT margarine!)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Pour Cherry Pie Filling in a 9"x9" baking dish. Smooth if needed. Sprinkle dry cake mix over the top of the cherry pie filling, smoothing evenly and covering the top.
Sprinkle the rolled oats and chopped pecans over the top.
Pour the melted butter over the top, covering evenly.
Bake for 40-50 minutes or until brown on top and bubbly.
Let cool 30 minutes. Serve warm or cooled.

Description: 
My great-great aunt Joy shared this recipe when my mum was a young bride.  It has been a family favourite ever since, especially for my father.  A scoop of vanilla ice cream pairs wonderfully when it's fresh out of the oven
