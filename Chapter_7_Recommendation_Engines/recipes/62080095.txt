Title:
BBQ beans with mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of beans
one bunch of cilantro, chopped
1 cup of mushrooms, diced
salt and pepper as desired
2 jalepenos, diced
1 teaspoon of paprika
1 teaspoon of cajun seasoning

Directions:
open can of beans and pour into pot.
add the mushrooms and jalepenos and stir and add the seasonings.
salt and pepper as desired.
when the mushrooms and jalepenos are cooked, turn off heat and stir in cilantro.
serve

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This is a great simple way to quickly make beans for a bbq.

Adding cilantro is vital to the success of this recipe.
