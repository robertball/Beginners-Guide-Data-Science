Title:
Agua de Pina (Pineapple Water) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/399421.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole pineapple (2 Cups)
3 Cups Cold Water
1/2 Cup White Sugar (add more or less as needed, to taste)
3 Tablespoons honey
3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice

Directions:
Core pineapple.  Dice into small peices and place into blender.
Add about 1 cup of water to blender and blend until smooth.
Strain the liquid into 1 gallon pitcher a little at a time.  Discard pulp.  Stir in the white sugar until dissolved, add honey and lime juice also.  Add remaining water.  Serve immediately or let stand overnight for best taste.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a mexican beverage served in mexico.  It is very simple and tasty.
