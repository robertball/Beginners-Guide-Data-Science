Title:
Astro Pop Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 parts Absolut Vodka
1 part Midori Melon
1 part Chambord
Ice cubes
Sugar for rim of glass

Directions:
Put Vodka & Midori into a shaker with ice cubes and mix until frothy. Strain sugar rimmed martini glass. Drizzle the Chambord down the side of the glass and let it settle on the bottom.

Submitter: 
PSOCONNOR
Description: 
When this one is done it should have 3 distinct layers of color. It's very pretty and yummy too!
