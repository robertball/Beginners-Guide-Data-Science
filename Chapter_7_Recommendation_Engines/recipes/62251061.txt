Title:
Tuna Mmmmmelts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332726.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna in water, drained and flaked
1-2 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste
1 pinch garlic powder
1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
4 slices sourdough bread
margarine or butter

Directions:
Combine tuna, mayo and garlic powder in a small bowl.
Heat skillet over medium heat.  Butter one side of each of the 4 slices of bread.  Place 2 slices butter-side-down on skillet.  Sprinkle 1/2 of the cheese on the 2 slices.  Divide the tuna mixture between the 2 slices and spread evenly.  Top with remaining cheese and other slices of bread, butter-side-up.  Grill until golden brown, flip, then grill other side until golden brown.

Description: 
A crispy warm sandwich with a hint of garlic.  Mmmmmm!
