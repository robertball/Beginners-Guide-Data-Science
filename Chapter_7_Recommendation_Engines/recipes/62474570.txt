Title:
Curried Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. - Stew Meat
4 - Celery stalks sliced
3 - Carrot sticks sliced
1 - Onion chopped
4 - Garlic cloves minced
1 can - Chicken Broth
1-2T - Curry Powder
1T - Cooking Oil
3/4 C - Flour
Salt to taste
2 C - Rice (cook according to directions)

Directions:
Slice and chop all vegetables.
Cut stew meat into 1" pieces.
Put flour and stew meat in large ziplock bag and shake to coat each piece of meat in flour.
Heat large skillet on med-high. Add oil and let heat until sprinkled flour sizzles.
Add meat to skillet and brown all sides.
Push meat to outside and add vegetables in the center. Stir and turn until onions are clear.
Add chicken broth and stir.  Add water or more chicken broth to cover ingredients.
Bring to boil and then cover and turn heat down to simmer.
Stir every 15-minutes to keep from sticking.
After 45-minutes of simmering add 1T curry powder and then salt to taste.  Cook an additional 15-minutes.  Stir and taste to see if you'd like to add additional curry powder and/or salt.  Stir in and cook an additional 5 minutes.
Serve over hot rice.
