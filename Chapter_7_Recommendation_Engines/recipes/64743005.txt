Title:
Meat Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mushrooms (sliced and sauted' with butter, oil and salt & pepper until browned and dry) (use up to 2 cups cooked)
Sausage 1 -2 c cooked crumbled (any variety altho maple flavor might be weird)
Cooked diced onions (how much? Do you love onions? I used 1 c cooked)
Cooked greens drained (I used 1 cup sauted' kale)
1 can mushroom soup or 1 cup medium white sauce
1 1/2 t herb de provance or 1/2 t each thyme, tarragon and oregano...sub out basil, rosemary or marjoram for any of them)
Pie crust

Directions:
Cook off raw ingredients
Cool
Mix all but pie crust
Make a two crust pie, a one crust pie or pasties (as I did) .Brush with milk and make steam holes.
Bake at 350 F for 40-55 minutes depending on the form your pie takes. My pasties took 45 minutes. I made two out of the ingredients above.
