Title:
Chocolate Krunch (No Bake Cookies) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750064.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups quick oats
4 TBS cocoa
1/4 tsp salt
1 stick of butter
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup milk
2/3 peanut butter

Directions:
Combine oats, cocoa, and salt; set aside
In a small pot, mix together butter, sugar, and milk; bring to a boil, stir in peanut butter
Combine wet and dry ingredient and spoon out in balls onto waxed paper
