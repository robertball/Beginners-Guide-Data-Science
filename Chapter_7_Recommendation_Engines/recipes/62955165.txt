Title:
Simply Divine Italian Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1½ tsp Garlic - finely minced
1½ tsp English Mustard
½ tsp fresh Oregano (or ¾ tsp dried)
½ tsp Red Pepper Flakes or Dried Chillies - crushed
A drop or two (to taste) Tabasco Sauce
2 tbsp Sugar
30ml/2tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
75ml/5tbsp White Vinegar
200ml/7fl oz Extra Virgin Olive Oil
120g/4oz Parmesan Cheese - grated
Salt - to taste
½ tsp freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Mix together the vinegars, mustard, tabasco, sugar,  garlic and salt in a blender;
Slowly drizzle in the oil then the remaining ingredients, blending continuously until all combined together.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
This piquant and delicious salsa will embellish and enhance any salad.
