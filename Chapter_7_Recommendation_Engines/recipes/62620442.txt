Title:
Julie's Chocolate Ladyfinger Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs Ladyfinger, split
1/2 C. med. dry sherry or orange juice
1 lb cream cheese, softened
3/4 C. Sugar
1/2 t. salt
2 t. vanilla extract
3 eggs, separated
3 pkgs (4 oz.) sweet cooking chocolate, melted and cooled
2 C heavy cream, whipped
1/2 C semisweet chocolate pieces, melted and cooled.
Chocolate Coconut
Confectioners sugar

Directions:
Arrange ladyfinger on baking sheet.  Sprinkle with sherry (or orange juice) using spoon.  Arrange around inside rim of 9" spring form pan 3" high.  Put remaining ladyfingers on bottom of pan.
Combine next 4 ingredients.  Beat until well blended and smooth.  Add egg yolks and blend until smooth.  Blend in sweet chocolate.
Beat egg whites until stiff and fold in with whipped cream.
Pour into pan and chill until firm.
Remove rim from pan.
With back of spoon, make a slight indentation on top.
Add 3 to 4 tablespoons warm water to semi-sweet chocolate and stir until smooth.  Pour on top of cake and sprinkle with coconut.
Brush ladyfingers with confectioners sugar.  Make 14-16 servings.
