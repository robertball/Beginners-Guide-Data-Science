Title:
Low Carb Beef and Cheese Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/766233.jpg
Ingredients:
Tortilla
4 eggs
1 Tlbs. cornstarch
1 Cup riccota
1 teas. salt
Filling
1 lb. ground beef
1 small chopped onion
3/4 cup taco or enchilada sause
Hot sause or Hot pepper to taste
Salt to taste
2 Tbls. oregano
2 Tbls. chilli powder
1 Tbls. paprika
1/2 teas. gound cumin
1/2 teas. pepper
1/2 teas. garlic powder
1/2 teas. onion powder
4 oz. of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Blend riccota, eggs, salt, cornstarch, in mixing bowl. In a medium slippery frying pan, medium heat, use cooking spray, make tortillas toasting each side well before turning. Stack and let completely cool before handling,Very fragile.
Set aside tortillas.
Brown ground beef with onion, spices, and 3 Tablespoons of taco sause.
Let meat cool.
Carefully fold tortilla filled with meat and shredded cheese and line into baking dish side by side. Top with remaining sause and cheese.
Bake covered in 375F oven for 45 min.
Allow to set 5 min before cutting.
11gm of Carbohydrates based on 4 servings.
