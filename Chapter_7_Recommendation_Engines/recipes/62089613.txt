Title:
Olive Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. packages cream cheese
1 can green olives with pimento, drained and chopped
2 slices cooked, crumbled bacon
1 green onion, chopped
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in bowl.  Refrigerate at least 30 minutes.  Serve with crackers or vegetables.  Black olives can be substituted for green olives.
