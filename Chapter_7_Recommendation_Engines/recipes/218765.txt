Title:
California Crescent Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989313.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can Pillsbury速 refrigerated crescent dinner rolls or Pillsbury速 Crescent Recipe Creations速 refrigerated seamless dough sheet
1/2 cup guacamole
8 lettuce leaves
4 ounces sliced smoked turkey
8 slices cooked bacon

Directions:
Separate or cut dough into 8 squares (if using crescent rolls, press perforations to seal). Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees F 8 to 10 minutes.
To serve, spread guacamole on 4 of the rectangles. Top with lettuce, turkey and bacon. Top with remaining rectangles.

Description: 
Build a better sandwich when you bake flaky Crescent dough and stack it with the irresistible combo of turkey, bacon and guacamole.
