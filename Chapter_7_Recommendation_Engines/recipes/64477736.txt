Title:
Chick-fil-A Sauce Clone 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Marzetti coleslaw dressing
1/4 cup Honey BBQ sauce
1 tbs Yellow mustard
1 1/2 tsp Honey

Directions:
Measure ingredients into bowl and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
I got this from a friend, whose high school friend worked at the Chic-fil-A in Fredericksburg, VA, where the Chic-fil-A Sauce was invented. It is supposed to be the original recipe. (Even it isn't, it's still really good and very close to what you get in the  restaurants today.)
