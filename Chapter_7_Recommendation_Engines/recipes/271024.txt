Title:
Sous Vide Dulce de Leche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6055633.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 pint-sized Mason jar

Directions:
Set a sous vide precision cooker to 185 degrees F (85 degrees C) according to manufacturer's directions.
Pour sweetened condensed milk into the jar and seal. Immerse in the water bath for 14 hours.
Cool in the refrigerator for 1 hour before serving.
