Title:
Chicken Chow Mein *FAMILY FAVOURITE* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Egg Noodles
500 g Prawns
500 g Chicken, sliced thinly
2 brown onions
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 Tbsp grated ginger
1 medium yellow or red capsicum, sliced thinly
2 cups finely shredded cabbage
Sauce:
1/8 cup light soya sauce + 1 Tbsp Oyster Sauce + 1 tsp sesame oil
Slurry:
1/2 Cup Chicken Stock, mixed with 2 Tbsp cornflour

Directions:
1. Marinate chicken in soya sauce, sugar & sesame oil
2. Boil egg noodles till it separates
3. Drain and set aside
4. Stir fry chicken and prawns separately, in batches
5. Stir fry onion, garlic, ginger
6. Add capsicum & celery - stir fry till tender
7. Add cabbage, 1 Tbsp Oyster Sauce + 1 tsp sesame oil + 1/8 cup light soya sauce
8. Add Noodles, stir fry 1-2 minutes
9. Add Chicken and prawns and Slurry. Stir fry till sauce thickens
