Title:
Nectar of the Gods - A Champagne Beverage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/807706.jpg
Ingredients:
1 shot Chambord
1 shot Agave Nectar
4 oz or so of Champagne

Directions:
In an ale glass (tall, thin sided) combine the agave nectar and chambord by stirring vigorously with a spoon. Tilt the glass at an angle and slide in the champagne. Serve.

Submitter: 
The Jazz Chef
Description: 
A nice easy champagne variation with a Southwestern twist.
