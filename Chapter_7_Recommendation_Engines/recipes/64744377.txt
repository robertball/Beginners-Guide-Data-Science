Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked
1 cup White Onion, Chopped
1/2 bag Spinach
1 1/2 cup Whole Milk Greek Yogurt
1 small can sliced Black Olives
1 10oz can Diced Tomatoes
2 4 oz can Diced Green Chili
8 Flour Tortillas

Directions:
1- Shred cooked chicken
2- combine green chillies, onion, diced tomatoes, olives, and greek yogurt in a bowl. Mix well.
3 - Mix in cooked chicken and spinach to mixture.
4 - in a 9x13 baking dish, spread a small layer of mixture on the bottom.
5- Spoon some of the mixture into a tortilla and roll. Place rolled tortilla in baking dish. Repeat.
6 - Sprinkle Colby Jack cheese on the top of the enchiladas.
7 - Cover with foil and cook for 20 minutes at 350 degrees. Then remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
