Title:
Reuben Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/594295.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup light Mayonnaise
1-2 tablespoon Dijon-style Mustard
1/2 teaspoon Caraway Seeds
1 cup (4 ounce) cooked Corned Beef, finely chopped
4 slices of cooked Bacon diced
1/4 cup Onion diced
1 cup shredded Swiss Cheese
1 cup Sauerkraut, rinsed, drained and patted dry
with paper towl
1 package (10 ounce) refrigerated Pizza Crust dough

Directions:
- Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
- In a medium bowl mix mayonnaise, mustard and
caraway seeds.
- Add finely chopped Corned Beef, shredded Cheese,
drained Sauerkraut, Bacon and Onion. Blend well!!
- Unroll dough onto a large ungreased cookie sheet
(rectangle)
- Spoon the Sauerkraut/Corned Beef mixture onto the
dough, spread within 1/2 inch. of edges.
- From long side roll and pinch to seal edges.
- Bake for 10-12 minutes on 425 degrees F or until
to is golden
- Let stand 5 minutes
