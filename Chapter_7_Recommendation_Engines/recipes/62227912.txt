Title:
Spicy Candied Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/319174.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups of sugar
1/2 cup water
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cloves
2 tsp cinnamon
1/8 - 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (to taste)
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp corn syrup
4 cups of walnut halves

Directions:
1) Heat walnuts for 5 minutes in an oven set at 375Â°F, then set aside in a large mixing bowl.
2) Mix sugar, water, spices, salt & corn syrup in a sauce pan.  Heat mixture over a medium heat until reaches a soft boil stage (8-10min) or 230 degrees with a candy thermometer.  Remove from heat, quickly stir in vanilla.
3) Pour mixture over nuts and gently stir until the nuts are evenly coated (work quickly).  Pour nuts onto a cookie sheet covered with wax paper.  Take two forks and gently separate the nuts.  When fully cooled store in a sealed container

Stan
Description: 
These are easy to make, tasty to eat and wonderful on salads.
