Title:
Tuna Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces pasta (rings, shells, bowtie etc.)
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained
1 pkg frozen peas, cooked
1 (1 ounce) package dry Ranch-style dressing mix
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup whole milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
In a large bowl, combine pasta, tuna and peas.
Whisk together the salad dressing mix, mayonnaise and milk. Stir into pasta mixture. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Jim
Description: 
The dressing makes the difference in this salad. Mayonnaise, a bit of milk, and a package of dry ranch salad dressing mix combine to make a delicious dressing for the tuna, peas, carrots, and cooked macaroni. Chill and serve to six.Modified from: No-Egg Tuna Macaroni Salad
