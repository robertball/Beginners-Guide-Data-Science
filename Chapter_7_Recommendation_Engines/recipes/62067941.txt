Title:
Sweet Potato Pudding II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups grated raw sweet potatoes
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup melted butter
3 eggs, beaten
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup coconut (optional)
1/2 cup chopped nuts or raisins (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mixing well
Spoon mixtured into a lightly greased 2 Qt casserole
Bake at 325 degrees for 1 1/2 hrs or until browning.

Submitter: 
KR
Description: 
A real southern tradition, serve it for Thanksgiving! We always have it for the first game of the World Series.
