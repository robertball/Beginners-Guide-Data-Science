Title:
Bread Machine Honey-Oat-Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
2 Tablespoons sugar
2 1/2 teaspoons yeast (quick-acting or regular)
1/4 cup honey
3 Tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 cups flour, all-purpose or bread
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup oats, quick-cooking or regular
3 Tablespoons dry milk
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Attach kneading blade to bread pan.
2. Place yeast and sugar in pan, then slowly add water.  Let sit for 10 minutes.
3. In mixing bowl, combine all dry ingredients together, mixing until thoroughly combined.
4. After yeast has sat for 10 minutes, add honey, oil, and butter, then flour mixture to pan.
5. Place pan in bread machine and select "dough only" cycle.
6. When machine is finished with cycle (approximately an hour and a half), transfer dough to a traditional loaf pan coated with cooking spray.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes at 375 degrees F.

Submitter: 
notajenny
Description: 
Although dense, this bread is very flavorful and has a nice old-fashioned-home-made taste.  This recipe yeilds a 2 1/2 pound loaf.
