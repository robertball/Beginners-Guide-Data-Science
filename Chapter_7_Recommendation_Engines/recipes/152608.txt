Title:
Cinnamon-Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976304.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold fat free milk
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Chocolate Flavor Fat Free Sugar Free Instant Reduced Calorie Pudding & Pie Filling
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup thawed COOL WHIP Sugar Free Whipped Topping

Directions:
Pour milk into medium bowl. Add dry pudding mix and cinnamon. Beat with wire whisk 2 min. or until well blended. Gently stir in whipped topping.
Spoon evenly into four dessert dishes.
Serve immediately. Or, cover and refrigerate until ready to serve. Store leftover pudding in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
This creamy chocolate pudding has a hint of cinnamon.
