Title:
Broccoli, Rice, Cheese, and Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rice
2 (10 ounce) cooked chicken breast halves
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup butter
1 cup milk
1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
1 small white onion, chopped
1 pound shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Make rice according to package. Let stand 5 minutes.
Cook broccoli according to package.
In a 9x13 inch baking dish, mix the prepared rice, chicken, cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, butter, milk, broccoli, onion, and cheee.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until cheese is melted. Stir halfway through cooking to help cheese melt evenly.

Description: 
A meal-in-one casserole with chicken, rice, broccoli, cheese, onion, and creamy soups all baked into one dish. May be made ahead and frozen.Modified from: Broccoli, Rice, Cheese, and Chicken Casserole
