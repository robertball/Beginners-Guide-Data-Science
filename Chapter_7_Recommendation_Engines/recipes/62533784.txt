Title:
Slow Cooker Thanksgiving Turkey Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (5 1/2 pound) bone-in turkey breast with skin
2 tsp olive oil
1/2 teaspoon garlic pepper
1 tsp rosemary, crushed
1 tsp fresh thyme
2 teaspoon dried sage
salt and pepper to taste
1 onion, chopped
2 stalks celery chopped
2 carrots, chopped

Directions:
Spray a slow cooker with cooking spray. Place onion, carrot and celery in bottom of cooker.  Place turkey in the slow cooker. Rub oil over turkey and then season with all the spices and herbs.
Cover slow cooker, and cook turkey 1 hour on high and 7 hours on Low.
Thicken juices for gravy.

Submitter: 
Karen B
Description: 
Easy way to prepare turkey and leave your oven open for the side dishes.Modified from: Slow Cooker Thanksgiving Turkey
