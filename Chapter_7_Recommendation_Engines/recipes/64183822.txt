Title:
Big Ray's White Bean, Kale & Kielbasa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2015296.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch kale or collard greens
1 Tbsp olive oil
8 oz kielbasa, thinly sliced
1 bunch green onions, sliced
1 can white beans, drained & rinsed (15 oz)
3 cups chicken broth
1/2 tsp salt
freshly ground pepper, to taste
freshly grated Parmesan cheese, optional

Directions:
Rinse kale, shake dry and cut out tough center stems.  Roll leaves into tight tubes and cut crosswise into 1/4 strips.
Heat oil in large heavy saucepan over medium heat.  Add kielbasa and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
Add onions and cook additional 3 minutes.  Add kale and heat until kale is wilted,about 3 minutes.
Stir in beans and chicken broth.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until kale is tender, about 15 minutes.  Add salt and pepper. Serve and add grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Description: 
This soulful soup, made with a Polish twist, uses kielbasa for a hearty meal to enjoy on those cool Autumn evenings.
