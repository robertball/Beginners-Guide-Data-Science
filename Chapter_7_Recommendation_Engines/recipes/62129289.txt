Title:
Baby Argula Salad with Balsamic Vinegar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags baby arugla or mixed green salad
2 heads of endive lettuce chopped
1 cup grape tomatoes
1/2 cup thinly sliced radishes
3/4 cup sliced calimyrna figs
1 sliced avocado
1/4 cup chopped red onions
1 c glazed walnuts
2/3 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/3 cup aged balsamic vinegar
1/8 to 1/4 tsp adobo seasoning

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the first 8 ingredients and toss.
In a separate container, mix EVOO, balsamic vinegar and seasoning.
Toss on salad just before serving.

Submitter: 
prell2k4
Description: 
This is a tasty salad that is great as a side or just as a light meal.  The combination of flavors make this salad.  Great year round.
