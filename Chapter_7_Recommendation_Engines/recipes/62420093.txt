Title:
Summer succotach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tbs butter
1/2 olive oil
1/2 cup of red onion
1 garlic clove minced
1 1/4 cup of baby lima beans frozen or fresh
1/3 cup frozen or fresh peas
1/2 cup frozen or fresh corn kernels
about 7 ripe cherry tomatoes halved
2 tsp fresh basil chopped or minced
3/4 tbs white balsamic vinegar. if no white you can use red.

Directions:
In med skillet, heat butter and oil over medium heat. Toss in onions, stirring occasionally for about 4 minutes, or until they begin to turn translucent.Stir in garlic and cook about 1 min. Add lima beans and sautee 5 minutes. Add corn and peas and cook for 3 minutes. Add tomatoes and cook about 1 min. They will turn to mush if you leave them much longer. Remove from heat. Put veggies in a serving bowl and stir basil and vinegar. You can serve it warm or cold. Both are great.

Description: 
Yummy side dish that you can store in the fridge for a week.
