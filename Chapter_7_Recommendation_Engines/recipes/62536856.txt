Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521115.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Shortening
1 cup Sugar
2 each Eggs
4 each Bananas
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon Soda, dissolved in water
1/4 teaspoon Salt
2 cups All-Purpose Flour
2 teaspoons  Baking Powder
1 cup Pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cream together shortening and sugar. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in bananas, and vanilla. Mix in flour and baking powder. Stir in nuts if desired. Pour batter into two greased 9x5 inch pans.
Bake for 50 to 60 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.

Submitter:

Description: 
Go bananas! Nothing beats the taste or aroma of baked banana bread. This recipe makes a moist and delicious banana nut bread that everyone will enjoy.
