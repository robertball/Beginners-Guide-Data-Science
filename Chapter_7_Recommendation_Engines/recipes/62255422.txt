Title:
Liege Belgian Waffles with Pearl Sugar - Lars Own Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet yeast
3/4 cup lukewarm milk
1 1/2 TBS sugar
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
3 eggs
1 cup melted butter
2 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cup (1-8oz box Lars own) Pearl Sugar
Topping (Whip cream, berries, chocolate)

Directions:
1. Combine yeast, sugar, and lukewarm milk.  For best result, let yeast activate for 15 minutes.
2. Mix flour and salt. Make a well for the wet ingredients.
3. Add yeast mixture, eggs, melted butter and vanilla to flour and mix well.
4. Let dough rise until it doubles (about 30 minutes)
5. Gently mix in Pearl Sugar.
6. Butter heated waffle iron.
7. Bake baseball size dough balls about 2 minutes, pressing the dough flat in the waffle iron.
8. Let waffles cool for a few minutes as Pearl Sugar will be VERY hot.
9. Serve waffles warm or cooled with whip cream and berries or dizzled with warm, melted chocolate.

Submitter:

Description: 
These are sweeter than traditional Brussel Belgian waffles sweeter wafle that can be served for breakfast or dessert. I prefer them with whip cream and berries.
