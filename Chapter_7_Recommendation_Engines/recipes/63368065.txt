Title:
Mary's Tater-Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb	ground beef or bison
Â½ 	large yellow onion, chopped
2	cloves of garlic, minced
1 	packet of taco seasoning
1	12-oz can black beans
1	10.5-oz can cream of mushroom condensed soup
8 oz	sour cream
2 cups	shredded Monterey-Jack cheese
1	32-oz package frozen potato rounds
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a mixing bowl, combine taco seasoning, salt & pepper, sour cream and mushroom soup.  Set aside.
In a large skillet, brown meat with onion and garlic.
Drain off fat and add the taco seasoning mixture and bring just to a simmer.
Pour the meat mixture into the prepared baking dish.
Layer the cheese and beans over the meat and top with an even layer of potatoes.
Bake for 40 minutes and top with cheese before serving, if desired.

Submitter:

This is my version of a tater-tot casserole with a Mexican twist!
