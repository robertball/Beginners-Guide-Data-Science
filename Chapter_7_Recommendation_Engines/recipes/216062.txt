Title:
California Creamy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991230.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potato soup
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 1/2 pounds red potatoes, very thinly sliced
1 cup shredded sharp white Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix first 3 ingredients in large bowl. Add potatoes; toss to coat. Spoon into 13x9-inch baking dish sprayed with cooking spray; top with cheese.
Bake 55 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Sprinkle with chives.

Submitter: 
KNUDSEN
Description: 
A richly-flavored, creamy potato side dish made with sharp white Cheddar cheese and sour cream is flecked with green chives.
