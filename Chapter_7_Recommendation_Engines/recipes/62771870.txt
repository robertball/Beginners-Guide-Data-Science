Title:
Greek Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/643432.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cucumber
4 small hothouse or roma tomatoes
1 small yellow or red onion
1 green bell pepper
1 package feta cheese
1 jar kalamata olives
Dressing:
5 tbsp malt vinegar
1 1/2 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black ground pepper
1/2 tsp garlic

Directions:
Chop the cucumber, tomatoes, bell pepper and onion and put them in a bowl.
Mix together the vinegar, sugar, olive oil, salt, pepper and garlic. Pour the dressing over the vegetables. Add as many olives as to your liking.
Serve and add a sprinkle of feta cheese to each salad.
Makes about 4 small salads or 2 larger salads.
