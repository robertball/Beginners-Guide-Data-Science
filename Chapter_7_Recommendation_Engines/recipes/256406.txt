Title:
Slow Cooker Cabbage Almost Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7098712.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
1 small head cabbage, diced
1 pound ground beef
2 cups water
1/2 cup rice
1 small green bell pepper, diced
1 small onion, diced
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine spaghetti sauce, cabbage, ground beef, water, rice, green bell pepper, onion, salt, and pepper in a slow cooker, stirring to ensure all ingredients are covered with enough liquid.
Cook on Low until cabbage is tender, 6 to 8 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy cabbage roll dinner without the hassle of actually rolling the meat in leaves. Perfect for the slow cooker!
