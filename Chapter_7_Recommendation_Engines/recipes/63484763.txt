Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
2 large slices bread torn into pieces (3 reg)
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
1 small onion, chopped
1 tbs worchestershire sauce
1/2 tsp sage and thyme
1 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp pepper
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 cup ketchup
parmesan  cheese

Directions:
mix all ingredients except ketchup and parmesan cheese. put into a 9x5" loaf pan.  spread ketchup on top and cheese.  bake 350 degrees for 1 hr.  drain off fat.
try baking, drain off fat, then topping with ketchup and cheese. return to oven to finish baking about 15 mins
