Title:
Bourbon Hot Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs hot dogs, cut into 1" pieces
32 oz BBQ sauce
1/2 cup bourbon

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a large sauce pan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
Transfer to a chafing dish or slow cooker (set on warm) and serve.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
My mother used to bring these bite-size treats to pot-lucks. There were always lots of requests for her secret recipe.
