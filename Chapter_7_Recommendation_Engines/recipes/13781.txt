Title:
Candied Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1098077.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can baked beans
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 (16 ounce) package large marshmallows

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a 9x13 inch baking dish, combine the beans and brown sugar. Mix together well and cover with a single  layer of marshmallows.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until marshmallows are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Lanette Allison
Description: 
For this baked bean dish, use your favorite style of beans, however I suggest using those with brown sugar and bacon.
