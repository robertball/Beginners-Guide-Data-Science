Title:
Chicken and Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 frozen boneless chicken breast
2 cups of yellow rice
2 large cans of cut pineapple
1 tsp of soy sause
dash of salt and pepper
dash of McCormicks Chicken seasoning
1 1/2 cups of water

Directions:
season chicken with salt and pepper and chicken seasoning
combine water, soy sause, and uncooked rice in the baking dish
place chicken on top of rice
cover chicken and rice with the pineapple
bake at 350 for 45 mins or until done
then enjoy
