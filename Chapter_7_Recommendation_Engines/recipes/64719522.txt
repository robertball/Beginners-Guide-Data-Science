Title:
Armenian Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T Butter
2 Cups Chopped Onions
3 Cups Fresh Green Beans, whole
4 Medium Fresh Tomatoes, chopped or 1 1 /2 Cups Canned Crushed Tomatoes
6 T Fresh Lemon Juice
1 t Fresh Lemon Zest
1/8 t Dried Thyme
1/4 t Dried Marjoram
1/4 t Dried Basil
Salt and Black Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Sauce the onions in butter until translucent.  Add the beans and cook about 5 minutes, stirring frequently until they turn a bright shiny green.
Stir in tomatoes, lemon juice, lemon zest, herbs and spices.  Lower the heat and simmer covered 10-15 minutes until the beans are tender and the flavors have merged.
Stir occasionally to prevent sticking
