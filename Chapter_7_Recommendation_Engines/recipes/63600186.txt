Title:
Caramelized Cherry Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dried tart cherries
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup orange juice
juice from 1 lime
1 tbsp. vanilla
1 cinnamon stick
1 loaf challah bread, shredded or cubed
1/2 stick butter, melted
1 - 20 oz. can crushed pineapple in juice
1 1/2 cup light brown sugar
1 cup light cream OR half and half
1 tbsp. allspice
6 eggs

Directions:
1. Combine the cherries, honey, orange juice, lime juice, vanilla, and cinnamon stick in a glass bowl. Cover tightly and let sit overnight.
2. Shred or cube the challah bread. Spread the pieces on a cookie sheet, cover with a clean towel, let sit overnight.
3. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.
4. Melt the butter in a large glass bowl.
5. Add pineapple, sugar, cream, and allspice to the butter and combine.
6. Remove the cinnamon stick from the cherry mixture and stir the fruit mixtures together.
7. Beat the eggs in a separate bowl and stir them into the fruit/sugar mixture.
8. Stir in the challah pieces a few handfuls at a time till all the ingredients are thoroughly combined.
9. Spread the bread/fruit mixture into a deep glass 9 x 12 ungreased dish.
10. Bake for 30 to 45 minutes until a knife comes out clean.

Description: 
A staple at our regular brunches. The fruit juices and sugar create a caramelized texture/flavor that everybody likes-- even kids!
