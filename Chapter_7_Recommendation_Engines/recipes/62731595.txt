Title:
Mom's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1.5 lbs ground beef
2 medium onions shopped
salt and pepper
1 TBPS minced garlic
2 28 oz cans tomatoes chopped
~1 water
2 6 oz cans tomatoe paste
2 TBPS sugar
2-3 tsp whole oregano leaves
parsley flakes or basil (optional, but reccomended)
1 or 2 bay leaves

Directions:
1. Brown beef, onion and garlic in large pan.
2. Drain
3. Add the rest of ingrdients.
4. Cook on med until warm.
5. Simmer for about an hour.

Submitter: 
Lisa Ann Critchfield
Description: 
My mom had been making this since she was a teenager- it came from my grandma!
