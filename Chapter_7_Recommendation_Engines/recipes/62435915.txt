Title:
Montego Bay Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Montego Bay Chicken Breast or 2 chicken tenders cooked and chilled
1 rib of celery chopped
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 black pepper
3 Tbs Mayonnaise
1/4 C Raisins (optional)

Directions:
Chop Chicken into small chunky bits, place in a medium mixing bowl.
To the chicken add the remaining ingredients; mix well.
Serve as a filling between your favorite bread or over a bed of greens.
