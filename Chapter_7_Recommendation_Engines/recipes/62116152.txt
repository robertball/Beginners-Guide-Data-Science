Title:
Flat Iron Steak Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soy sauce
1 1/2 cups pineapple juice
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1/2 tablespoon garlic, minced
3 1/2 tablespoons lime juice, divided
2 pounds flat iron  steak
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, sliced
1 red pepper, sliced
1 teaspoon Mrs. Dash Southwest Chipotle Seasoning Blend
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
1 green onion, chopped
1 cup salsa
1 cup guacamole
12 flour tortillas
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients plus three tablespoons lime juice.  Add steak, cover and marinate in the fridge for at least four hours.
Combine sour cream, cilantro, green onion and 1/2 tablespoons lime juice.  Keep cold in fridge.
Turn broiler on high.
Lightly grease broiler pan and add steak.  Cook steak in preheated oven until medium rare (about 14-16 minutes, flipping once). Slice steak into strips.
While steak is cooking, heat olive oil in a large pan over high heat. Add onions and cook 1 minute.  Add peppers and saut√© for an additional minute. Season with Chipotle Seasoning Blend. Remove from heat.
Place vegetables on a serving plate. Place sliced Flat Iron Steaks on top of the vegetables.
Serve with tortillas, cheese, guacamole, salsa and the cilantro-sour cream sauce.

Submitter: 
THENEWTRICIA
Description: 
I tried many times to copy the flavors of the best fajitas I have ever had (from a little restaurant outside Hollywood).  I believe I have figured out the recipe finally - the big differences being the  marinade, the Chipotle seasoning and the cilantro-sour cream sauce.
