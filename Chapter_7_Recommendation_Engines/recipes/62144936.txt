Title:
Squat Corn or Skillet Fritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/476095.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 can corn
1/2 loaf velveeta cheese
3 grab bag of corn chips
salt and pepper

Directions:
1) Brown hamburger, drain, return to skillet
2) Add corn and velveeta.
3) When velveeta begins to soften add chips.  Stir well.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Rico
Description: 
This is an old campfire recipee that can also be made on the stove.  It sounds strange but my kids can't get enough of it.  They ask me to make it all the time. If you like the ingredients seperately you will love the final product.
