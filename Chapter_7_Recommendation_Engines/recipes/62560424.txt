Title:
Greenbeans with Scrambled Eggs (Ejotes con Juevos) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup frozen green beans, diced
1/2 white onion, diced
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 roma tomatoes, diced
1 jalapeno, diced (optional,remove seeds for less heat)
6 Eggs
1/4 oil
1/3 cup water
1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
In skillet heat about 4 tablespoons oil. Add onions, green beans, jalapeno and garlic. Saute until tender, and almost translucent.
Add remaining oil, once hot add eggs.  With spatula, stir and break apart eggs until eggs are cooked.  Add tomatoes, mix together.  Add chicken bouillon to water and disolve.  Pour into pan and mix together. Salt and pepper to taste and remove from heat.  Top with cheese and serve with tortillas.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
Dont let the green beans scare you away from trying this!  This is one of my husbands favorite breakfast dishes and its quick and easy to throw together.  Serve with tortillas, hot sauce and sour cream.
