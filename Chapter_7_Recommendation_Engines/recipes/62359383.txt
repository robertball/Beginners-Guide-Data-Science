Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
3/4 cup whole-grain wheat flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup packed  brown sugar
1 large egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup raisins, choped

Directions:
Preheat oven: 350 degrees
In a med. bowl, combine oats, flour,  baking powder, salt, and cinnamon: set aside
Using an electric mixer,  cream butter and both sugars until incorporated
Add egg and vanilla; mix thoroughly
Add oat mixtue and mix until just combined; fold in raisins
Drop rounded teaspoons of batter about 1 ince apart onto 2 ungreased cookie sheets.  Bake for 9-10 min. for chewy cookies or 11-12 min. for crispy ones.
Remove from oven and let cookies rest for about 2 min.; remove cookies to wire rack and cool completely.
