Title:
Ham-Prei salade (Ham-Leek salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6744857.jpg
Ingredients:
flinke prei -  1 Large Leek
2 ons schouderham - 2 oz. sliced ham
2 el  mayonaise - 2 tbsp mayonnaise
1 el  zure room - 1 tbsp sour cream or yogurt
peper, zout - pepper and salt

Directions:
Cut the leek into 1/4 " sliced half circles.
Wash thoroughly and let drain or dry with a paper towel
mix in all ingredients
put in the fridge for 30 min - 1 hr.
