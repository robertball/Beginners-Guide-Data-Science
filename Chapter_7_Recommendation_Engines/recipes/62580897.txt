Title:
Chicken Apple Dinner Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rotisserie chicken
1 bag romaine hearts
1/3 red onion
2 Fuji apples
6 strips of cooked bacon
Dressing
1 c mayo
1/2 c sugar
1/4 c red wine vinegar
pepper

Directions:
Dice & combine all salad ingredients. Mix dressing ingredients & drizzle over salad.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
A fresh & light salad perfect for a lunch w/ friends.
