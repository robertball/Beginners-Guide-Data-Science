Title:
Cranberry Sea Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992443.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Smirnoff® Cranberry Vodka
2 ounces grapefruit juice
2 ounces cranberry juice
1 twist lemon

Directions:
Fill glass with ice.
Add Smirnoff(R) Cranberry Vodka, grapefruit juice, and cranberry juice.
Stir well.
Garnish with lemon twist.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add that bit of extra flavor to this or other cocktail recipes easily with the natural fruit flavors of Smirnoff® Flavored Vodkas.
