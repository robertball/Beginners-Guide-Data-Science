Title:
Easiest Meatloaf EVER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground chuck
1 lb. ground hot pork sausage
Your favorite BBQ or Steak Sauce (optional)

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Combine the hamburger meat and sausage in a large bowl, eventually shaping the mixture into a small loaf.  Put mixture in a loaf pan.  Cover loaf with your favorite BBQ or Steak Sauce (optional).
Bake (uncovered) for an hour, or until internal temp is 160 degrees Fahrenheit.  Allow a 10 minute rest period before cutting.

Submitter: 
jeyeiter
Description: 
How simple can it get? The hot pork sausage brings enough herbs and spices to make this delicious without it being too spicy hot when combined with the ground chuck.  The mixture also makes for great spaghetti or appetizer meatballs.  Easy AND Versatile!!
