Title:
Neiman Marcus Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
8 oz. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 can Oscar Mayer bacon pieces
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
1/4 cup chopped green onions
pepper, to taste
1 1/2 - 2 cups REAL mayonnaise

Directions:
Mix together and serve with any type of crackers.
