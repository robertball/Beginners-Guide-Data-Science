Title:
Chicken "Noodle" Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036870.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 t. olive oil
Finely chopped vegetables, such as mushrooms, carrots, celery and onion
1/2 - 1 cup chopped or shredded cooked chicken
1 whole egg + 1 T. egg white, beaten
fresh chives or green onions

Directions:
Bring the chicken broth and olive oil to a boil.
Add the vegatables and cook until tender, 15-20 minutes.
Stir in chicken and return soup to a medium boil.
Slowly stream in egg mixture (noodles), stirring constantly.
Adjust seasoning if necessary.
Serve with fresh chives or green onions.

Submitter: 
Terri Myers
Description: 
Simple, delicious soup w/o traditional egg noodles.
