Title:
Swedish Cabbage Roll Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 # ground beef (or 1 # gr. beef and 1 # Italian sausage)
1 C chopped onion
1 green or red pepper, diced, (opt.)
1 can (29 oz) tomato sauce
3 1/2 # chopped cabbage ( med/large head)
1 t salt
1/2 t black pepper or more
4-6 cloves minced garlic
1 t oregano
1 t basil
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 can diced tomatoes or salsa
red pepper flakes to your liking or chili powder
2-14 oz cans beef broth
1-1 1/2 C instant brown rice

Directions:
Preheat oven 350.
In skillet brown beef with onions and garlic (and pepper) in oil over med-high heat until redness is gone. Drain off fat.
Add tomato sauce and 1/2 the broth to meat mixture and stir in the rice. Turn off heat and let rest for 10-15 min.
Mix all the ingredients together and put in large baking dish or 2 9x13 pans.
Cover and bake for 50 min and test for doneness. May take up to 90 min to complete.
Can put in crock pot and cook on low for 6-8 hrs.
