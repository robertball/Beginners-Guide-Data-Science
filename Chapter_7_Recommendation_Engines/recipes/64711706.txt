Title:
Greek Mason Jar Steak Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5608472.jpg
Ingredients:
5 wide-mouth quart size Mason jars
10 ounces Greek dressing
1 pint of grape tomatoes cut in half
1 1/4 cups of pitted Kalamata olives
2 red onions diced
15 ounces cooked steak, cut into bite-size pieces
10 cups Romaine lettuce chopped
1 1/4 cups crumbled feta cheese
3/4 cups croutons, optional

Directions:
Divide the dressing evenly between the 5 mason jars, forming the first layer.
Divide the halved tomatoes evenly between the jars, forming the next layer. Top the tomatoes with the Kalamata olives and
diced red onions.
Place 3 ounces of steak over top the tomatoes and onions.
Top the steak with 2 cups of chopped romaine in each jar.
Place 1/4 cup of feta cheese over top of the chopped Romaine. Seal and refrigerate each jar until ready to use.
If using croutons, divide these evenly into 5 small resealable plastic bags.
When ready to eat, dump jar contents and optional croutons into a bowl and toss.
