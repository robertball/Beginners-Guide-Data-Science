Title:
Spicy Chicken and Red Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Cups low sodium chicken broth
3 Cups cooked chicken, cut into bite sized pieces
1 10-oz can diced tomatoes & green chillies
2 Cups red lentils, rinsed
1 sweet onion, diced
4 ribs celery, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 tsp dried rosemary, chopped
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Add lentils to chicken stock. Add tomatoes, onion, celery, garlic, pepper, rosemary, and red pepper. Bring to a boil and then simmer about 45 minutes or until lentals are tender. Add cooked chicken and simmer another 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Debbie
Description: 
This is an easy and healthy recipe everyone will enjoy. You can spice it up or down depending on what you like. It is also a great way to use up any left over chicken you may have.
