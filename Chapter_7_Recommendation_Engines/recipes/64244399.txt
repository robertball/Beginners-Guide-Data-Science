Title:
Hamburger Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground Beef Top Round
1/2 cup onions, chopped
1 15 oz. can green beans
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon oregano
1 10 3/4 ounce can tomato soup
2 frozen puff pastry, or pie shells

Directions:
Cook ground beef, onion, salt, pepper & oregano until meat is browned.  Stir in drained beans & soup.  Pour filling into pastry lined pie plate.  Place top crust over pie filling.  Bake in preheated 400F for 25 minutes.

A quick and easy dinner with old-fashioned good taste.
