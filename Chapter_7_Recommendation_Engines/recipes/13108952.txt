Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670506.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c Margarine (softened)
2 c Sugar
2 lg Eggs
2 t Vanilla
1 t Baking Powder
1 t Baking Soda
3 c Flour
Cinnamon & Sugar to Coat

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350Â°.
BEAT margarine until smooth.
MIX in sugar.
ADD vanilla, eggs, baking powder and mix for 30 seconds.
MIX in the flour until well combined.
STIR the cinnamon and sugar together, in a separate bowl.
MAKE 1-2" balls.
COAT them in the cinnamon & sugar mixture.
PLACE on a cookie sheet about 2 inches apart.
BAKE for about 10-12 minutes.  They may appear not quite done, but they will be good.

Jody
Description: 
Simple cookie in which you can always make because I bet you always have the ingredients on hand.
