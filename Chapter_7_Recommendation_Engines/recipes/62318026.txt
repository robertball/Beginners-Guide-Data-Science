Title:
Grandma Fiala's Rice Krispie Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups Rice Krispies
8 cups of mini-marshmallows (heaping!!)
1/2 cup of butter
1 cups Reese's Pieces

Directions:
Melt butter.
Mix Butter with marshmallows.
Put marshmallow mixture in microwave for 1 minute.
Stir.
Put marshmallow mixture back in microwave for another minute.
Add Rice Krispies and Reese's Pieces to marshmallow mixture and stir until well mixed.
Pat into a greased cookie sheet and chill.
Enjoy!
