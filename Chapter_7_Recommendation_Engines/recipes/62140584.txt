Title:
Spicy Sausage and Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb hot & spicy sausage
1 package Velveeta
2 cans rotel tomato's
3/4 jar of Hot Salsa(more or less depending on how spicy you want it)
1 regular can refried beans

Directions:
Brown sausage, drain 1 can of rotel, add all ingredients in crock pot and turn on low.
Serve with tortilla chips.
