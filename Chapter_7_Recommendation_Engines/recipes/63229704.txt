Title:
French Veggie Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 shallot, chopped
1 clove of garlic, minced
1/2 green bell pepper, diced
1/2 eggplant, cubed
1 small zucchini, diced
1 med. tomato, diced, seeds removed
1 1/4 cups self-rising flour
1/3 cup, plus 2 Tbsp. olive oil
3 eggs
1/3 cup milk
150 grams Swiss or Gruyere cheese, shredded
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Saut√© the veggies in two tablespoons of the oil until soft, but not mushy, about ten to fifteen minutes. Salt and pepper as desired.
2. Mix the flour in a large bowl with the milk and eggs, until well blended. Add the remaining olive oil, and stir until blended. Mix in the veggie mixture, and stir. Add the cheese, and stir. Pour into a greased and floured loaf pan, and put in oven that has been Pre-heated to 375 degrees. Bake for 45 minutes, or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
3. Let the bread cool about 10 minutes in the pan before removing loaf to a wire rack. Then let the loaf cool completely before slicing.

Submitter: 
spanishdona
Description: 
I found this savory loaf recipe in a cookbook I picked up in the Alsace region of France. It is wonderfully moist, and because of the densiity of the veggie mixture, it doesn't rise too much. So be sure to pour all of the batter into the pan. And enjoy!
