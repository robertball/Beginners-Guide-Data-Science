Title:
Chinese Chicken (or Pork) Fried Rice II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 tablespoon water
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 onion, chopped
1 green onion, chopped
2 cups cooked white rice, cold
1 - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
3/4 to 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat or pork
1/4 cup finely chopped parsley

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat egg with water. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium low heat. Add egg and leave flat for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from skillet and cut into shreds.
Heat oil in same skillet; add onions and saute until soft. Then add rice, soy sauce, salt, pepper and chicken/pork. Stir fry together for about 5 minutes, then stir in egg and parsley. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Lucky Noodles
Description: 
Cooked egg is shredded and mixed with a stir fry of chicken or pork, rice and onion in soy sauce in this fundamental Chinese dish.Modified from: Chinese Chicken Fried Rice II
