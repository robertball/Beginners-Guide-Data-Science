Title:
Braised Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 strips bacon
1/2 head of shreded cabbage
1 apple, peeled and thinly sliced
1 clove garlic
1 Tbsp bacon drippings
1 Tbsp mustard
1/4 cup water
pinch salt
splash of white wine

Directions:
Fry bacon in a large skillet until brown. Remove cooked bacon to paper towel lined plate to drain. Remove all but 1 Tbsp of bacon drippings; while still hot, add shredded cabbage, garlic and apple. Saut√© until cabbage begins to soften. Stir in mustard to coat, and then add water and salt. Once water evaporates, add wine and simmer until wine is cooked off. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Ann Mason Blake
Description: 
This is a warm cabbage salad that brings me back to my grandma's dinner table, during a cold Minnesota winter. This is fantastic with pork chops!
