Title:
spinaci con ceci italiano (italian spinach with chickpeas) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796744.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup onion, diced
1 (10 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained (not well)
1 (12 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
1  roasted red pepper, diced (or 8oz jar diced)
8 ounces vegetable broth
1 tablespoon Italian blend herbs
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
Pepper to taste
2 Tablespoons balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat. Cook the garlic and onion in the oil until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the spinach, garbanzo beans, roasted red pepper,  vegetable broth, Italian seasoning , garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Simmer 5 minutes or until most of broth evaporates.   Sprinkle balsamic vinegar over the mixture and stir.  Remove from heat.  Serve.

Italian version of espinacas con garbanzos.  A quick tasty side dish or main served over rice.
