Title:
No rice cooker Basmati rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Basmati rice
1 large chicken boullion cube or 2 small
1 TB Olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
Water

Directions:
Rinse and soak your rice in Hot water for about 20 minutes to soften it.
Heat about 1TB of oil in a pan.  Drain your rice and add to the hot oil.  Add about 1/2 a teaspoon of salt or to taste and stir to get rid of extra water.
Add the chicken cubes and a little bit of water to dissolve.  Once dissolved add water to pot and cover rice about 1/3 of an inch.  Cover rice and keep on high heat until it starts to boil.  Then reduce to medium heat.   Keep covered and cook for about 25 minutes.  Uncover and serve.
For best results you must use Basmati rice!
