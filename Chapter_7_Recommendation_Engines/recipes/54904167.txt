Title:
Agedashi-esque Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (12 ounce) package extra firm tofu
1/2 cup + 1 tablespoon cornstarch
oil for frying
6 green onions chopped
1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons of hoisin sauce

Directions:
Cut tofu into 12 cubes. Place cornstarch in a plate or in a shallow bowl. Dregde tofu in it, coating thoroughly.
Heat enough oil so that tofu will be half-way submerged. Fry tofu in hot oil for 3 to 5 mins on each side, or until crispy. Drain on paper towels.
sprinkle green onions, and drizzle hoisin sauce. serv immediatly

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Tofu is coated with cornstarch and then deep fried. Serve it with green onions and any dipping sauce desired.Modified from: Taiwanese Fried Tofu
