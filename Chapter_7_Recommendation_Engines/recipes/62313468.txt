Title:
Kimchi Fried Rice (Kimchi Bokkeumbap) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of store bought Kimchi
3 cups of cooked short grain rice (cold or day old)
1/4 cup any type of protein (ground beef, diced chicken, ham, pork, or spam)
1 TB Gochujang, sweet Korean hot pepper paste (optional, but really adds a another level of flavor, depending on your palate you may want to add a teaspoon at a time)
1 tsp sesame oil
1 fried egg, sunnyside up
1 Chopped green onion
1 TB canola oil or butter

Directions:
Slice or chiffonade the green onion (separate the white from the green part).
Chopped the kimchi in small bite size pieces (1x1 inch squares).
Heat the oil on high in a pan.
Add green onions (white part only, save the green part for garnish) and your protein and turn down the heat to medium.
Cook the meat until it's almost cooked and the green onions are fragrant about a minute or two.
Mix in the kimchi and gochujang and cook for 2-4 minutes.
Add cold day old rice and continue to cook until the rice is heated, soft and covered with gochujang, approximately another 3-5 minutes add sesame oil, mix thoroughly.
Garnished with the sliced green onions and top with a fried sunny side up egg.
Open mouth wide and eat like hog -- nom!
