Title:
Dried beef gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
2 cups milk
1/4 cup flour
1.5 oz dried beef, chopped into thin strips
1/4 teaspoon pepper (more or less to taste)
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
1. Melt butter in medium sauce pan over medium heat.
2. Wisk in floor and milk
3. Add the chopped dried beef, pepper, and cayenne pepper (if desired)
4. Reduce heat to medium/low and thicken for 6 to 8 minutes. Stir about once a minute.
5. Serve over biscuits or toast.

Submitter: 
Kevin Watts
Description: 
A very fast, very cheap meal. My mom always made this for us when we needed something quick for supper.
