Title:
Mushkazone Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Ground Walnuts
2 cups ground graham crackers
4 egg yolks
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp whisky
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup sugar
1 Tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
1. Preheat oven 350 degrees.
2. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl & then dump on counter.
3. Dip mushkazone mold or teaspoon into a cup of sugar and "smoosh" into the pile. Be sure to compact it tightly.
4. Tap the cookie mold on parchment lined cookiesheet until it falls out. If the cookie breaks, put it back into the pile of "dough".
5. Bake for 8-12 minutes.
