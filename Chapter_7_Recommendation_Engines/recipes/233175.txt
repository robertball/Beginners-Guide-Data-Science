Title:
Fabulous Fruit and Feta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5357356.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package mixed salad greens
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts, toasted
1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
1 (4 ounce) package feta cheese, crumbled
3/4 cup raspberry vinaigrette dressing
freshly cracked black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine salad greens, mandarin oranges, walnuts, onion, and feta cheese in a large bowl. Drizzle raspberry vinaigrette over salad; toss to combine. Season with black pepper.
