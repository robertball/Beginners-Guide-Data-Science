Title:
America's Favorite Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981120.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices white bread
2 KRAFT Singles
1 teaspoon butter or margarine, softened

Directions:
Top 1 of the bread slices with Singles; cover with remaining bread slice.
Spread outside of sandwich with butter.
Cook in skillet on medium heat 3 min. on each side or until golden brown on both sides.

Submitter: 
Kraft Kitchens
Description: 
The classic grilled cheese sandwich is fast, easy to prepare, and loved by the entire family.
