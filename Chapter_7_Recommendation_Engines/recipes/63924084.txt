Title:
Chocolate-Covered Cherry Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1088126.jpg
Ingredients:
Vanilla Vodka
Chocolate Liquor
Irish Cream
Maraschino Cherries
Chocolate Sauce

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker 1/2 way with ice, add 2 parts vanilla vodka, 2 parts chocolate liquor, 1 part Irish cream liquor, and a splash of maraschino cherry juice. Shake vigorously. Drop 1 maraschino cherry in the bottom of a martini glass drizzled with chocolate sauce and pour shaker contents on top. Delicious!
