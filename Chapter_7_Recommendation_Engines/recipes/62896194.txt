Title:
Homemade Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package active dry yeast
1/2 cup warm water
1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon oil
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon butter, melted
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
2 jars pizza sauce
2 pkg. 2% shredded mixed cheese (2 cups)
1 lb. hot sausage, cooked and drained on towels
1 lb. pepperoni, baked and drained on towels

Directions:
Combine yeast, water, sugar, and oil in mixing bowl.  Blend in flour and oregano.  Stir to mix well.  Knead dough on lightly floured surface 1 minute.  Cover and allow to rise 15 minutes.
Pat dough evenly across griddle.  Brush top of crust, (including edges) with melted butter.  Sprinkle evenly with 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese.  Set griddle to 300 degrees.  Cover and cook 5 minutes.  Top with sauce, sausage or pepperoni and cheese.
Reduce temperature to 250 degrees.  Cover and cook 10 minutes.
