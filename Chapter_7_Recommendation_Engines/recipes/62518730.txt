Title:
Graham Cracker Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511335.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/4 cup finely crushed graham crackers (about 18)

Directions:
Melt butter, stir in sugar.
Add crushed crackers and toss to mix well. Press into 9-inch pie plate.
Chill for 1 hour. (Or, bake at 375 for 4-5 minutes until edges are lightly browned. Cool on wire rack.)

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
This pie is a yummy blend of butter and graham crackers, perfect for all kinds of pies. Delicious!
