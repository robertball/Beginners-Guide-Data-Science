Title:
Peachy Appletini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354274.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 oz. vodka
1 oz. sour apple schnapps
1/2 oz. peach schnapps
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Combine vodka and both schnapps in a shaker with ice.  Shake vigorously and strain into a martini glass.  Garnish with the cherry.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
This drink has just the right amount fruitiness and kick!
