Title:
Quick Chicken Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978244.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices OSCAR MAYER Bacon, chopped (optional)
1 cup chopped green pepper
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 (12 ounce) package OSCAR MAYER Beef Smokies, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1 1/2 cups chopped cooked chicken
1 1/2 cups water
1 cup MINUTE Premium White Rice, uncooked
1/2 cup KRAFT Original Barbecue Sauce
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper (optional)
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

Directions:
Cook bacon and vegetables in 3-quart saucepan or large skillet until vegetables are tender and bacon is crisp.
Add Smokies, chicken, water, rice, barbecue sauce, salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil; cover.  Reduce heat to low; simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Stir in tomatoes.  Cook until thoroughly heated.
