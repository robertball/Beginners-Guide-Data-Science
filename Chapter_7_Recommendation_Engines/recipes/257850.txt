Title:
RITZ PB and Banana Bumblebees Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4517147.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut butter
12 RITZ Crackers
1 banana, cut into 24 slices
1 1/2 teaspoons chocolate spread, room temperature
3 seedless red grapes, quartered

Directions:
Spread peanut butter onto crackers; top each with 1 banana slice.
Spoon chocolate spread into resealable plastic bag; cut small piece off one bottom corner of bag. Use to pipe horizontal lines onto banana slices to resemble bumble bee bodies.
Cut remaining banana slices in half; place next to banana slices on crackers for the bees' wings. Add grapes for the bees' heads.

Submitter: 
RITZ Crackers
Description: 
Ritz crackers spread with peanut butter are topped with banana slices, stripes of chocolate, and red grape sections to make 'bumblebee' snacks.
