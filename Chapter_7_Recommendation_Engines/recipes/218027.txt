Title:
TJ's Creamy Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/779573.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup sour cream
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced, or to taste
3 tablespoons salsa verde (green salsa)
3 tablespoons red salsa
3 tablespoons minced fresh onion
2 teaspoons fresh lime juice
1/4 teaspoon chili-lime seasoning (such as TajinÂ®)

Directions:
Stir the cream cheese, sour cream, jalapeno pepper, salsa verde, red salsa, onion, lime juice, and chili-lime seasoning together in a bowl until well blended. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
I made this cream cheese-based dip to go with tortilla chips at a lakeside BBQ.  When I checked to see if it was a success, I found the bowl nearly empty and people wiping it with their fingers - I guess you could say finger licking good.
