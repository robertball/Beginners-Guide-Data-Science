Title:
Tropical Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029446.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C.  All purpose flour
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 tsp. Dried ginger
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. Salt
1 C. Medium shredded coconut
1 C. Banana, mashed
1 C. Crushed pineapple
1 C. Milk
2 Eggs
1/4 C. Canola oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan and set aside
In a medium size bowl combine flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, spices, salt  and sugar together and blend well with a spoon. Set aside
In a large bowl combine milk, eggs and oil, stir with a wire whisk until well mixed
Add mashed banana, coconut and pineapple mixing together until smooth and creamy
Mix the flour, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, spices and sugar with the wet ingredients and stir until dry ingredients are mixed in to make a lumpy batter
Pour batter into greased muffin cups filling 3/4 full and bake in oven for 20 minutes.
Allow to cool for 5 minutes before removing from muffin pan to a dinner plate

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A delicious and chewy muffin with banana, coconut and pineapple to make a very delectable muffin for breakfast or dessert.
