Title:
Couldn't be Simpler Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (no substitutes), melted
1 cup graham cracker crumbs
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch chips
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Pour butter into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan.  Sprinkle with crumbs and coconut.  Top with chips.  Pour milk over all.  Sprinkle with walnuts.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 23-28 minutes or until browned and bubbly.  Cool completely before cutting.

Submitter: 
Kerry  Bouchard
Description: 
'Every time I take these sweet, chewy bars to a gathering or serve them to guests, I get lots of compliments,' reports Kerry Bouchard of Augusta, Montana. 'They're easy to make, too - just sprinkle a few ingredients in a pan and bake.'
