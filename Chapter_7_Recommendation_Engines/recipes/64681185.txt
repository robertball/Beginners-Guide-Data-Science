Title:
Best Instant Pot Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5124049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb skinless boneless chicken thighs, cut in half
2 small skinless boneless chicken breast cut in half and again if larger
3 Tbsp EVOO
3 stalks celery chopped
1 med to small onion chopped
1 green pepper sliced and cut in half
1 red pepper sliced and cut in half
1 reg 8 ounce package of sliced mushrooms
4 cloves minced garlic
3 Tbsp of tomato paste
2 large fire roasted peppers (do not substitute) from the jar, chopped
1-1/2 tsp of crushed red pepper (or to taste)
1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
2 Tbsp Better Than Bouillon Organic Chicken Base
1 Tbsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp  onion powder
1 tsp sea salt (or to taste, can add more later)
1 tsp of fresh ground black pepper (or to taste, can add more later)
2 Tbsp of  flour
3/4 cup of dry red wine (do not use cooking wine, I like cab)

Directions:
Use all organic/free range ingredients if you can.
Season chicken with flour or cornstarch & salt and pepper.
Set instant pot to sauté, add ½ of the EVOO, use tongs and brown chicken on both sides, remove from pot and set aside.
Add rest of EVOO, add onion and celery, sauté until clear, about 3 minutes, add garlic and the rest of veggies, sauté another minute or so.
Add seasoning, wine, tomato paste, and chicken back in and stir to mix.
Set Instant Pot to Pressure Cook on High for 11 minutes.  Release pressure using the quick release, takes about 5 minutes.
Serve by itself or over pasta or rice.   Also can add a little freshly ground Parmesan on served dish.
