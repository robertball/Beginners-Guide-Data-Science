Title:
Grilled Sweet Italian Chicken Burger with Peppers & Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-14 Oz al frescoÂ® Fresh Sweet Italian Chicken Burger
Cooking Spray, olive oil
2 cups Vidalia Onion, thinly sliced
1 Red bell pepper, thinly sliced
3 tbsp Vinegar, balsamic
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
4 Rolls, deli style

Directions:
Spray a small saute pan with the cooking spray, and saute the onions and peppers over medium high heat for 8 - 10 minutes or until the onions are caramelized. Add the balsamic vinegar and season with the black pepper.
Meanwile, heat a skillet over medium high heat and coat with cooking spray. Pan grill Sweet Italian chicken patties for 5 minutes on both sides, to an internal temperature of 165 f.
To assemble: slice the deli rolls in half, top with the grilled patties, and top with the onion and peppers. Serve immediately. Serve immediately.
