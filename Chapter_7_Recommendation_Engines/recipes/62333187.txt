Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup butter
¼ cup all-purpose flour
½  teaspoon garlic salt
2 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
1 Tbsp parsley flakes
2 cups half and half
1/3 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
Whisk in the flour and salt until smooth.
Gradually whisk in the ½ and ½.
Add in garlic, parsley and parmesan cheese and continue to whisk.
Bring to a simmer on medium heat; cook and stir for 4 - 5  minutes or until thickened.
Use immediately or refrigerate.

Submitter: 
distasi
Description: 
This alfredo sauce is lower in fat than most as it is made with 1/2 and 1/2. This is delicious over pasta and is easy to make. You can also make it the day before and refrigerate it until ready to use.
