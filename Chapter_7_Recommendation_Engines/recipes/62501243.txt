Title:
Autumn Apple Pepita Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/501942.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 Tablespoons apple cider vinegar
2 Tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon dijon mustard
1 teaspoon mayonnaise
1 teaspoon finely diced shallot
ground black pepper to taste
Salad:
3 cups torn romaine heart leaves
3 cups torn baby spinach
1 cup dried apple chips
1/4 cup hulled, roasted pepitas
1/4 cup crumbled bleu cheese
1/4 cup sweetened dried cranberries

Directions:
To make the dressing, in a jar or container with a tight fitting lid, combine the olive oil, vinegar, sugar, mustard, mayonnaise, shallot and pepper.  Tightly screw on lid and shake vigorously.
To make the salad, in a large bowl, toss together the romaine, spinach, dried apples, pepitas, bleu cheese, cranberries, and dressing. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
I work in natural foods and some of the items we sell at the store, such as pepitas, dried apple chips and apple cider vinegar, inspired me to create this recipe.  Pepitas are Mexican pumpkin seeds and a perfect crunchy topping for this fall-influenced salad.
