Title:
Chicken Fajitas from Mazola® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1470488.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Mazola® Corn Oil
1 pound chicken breast tenders
1 medium onion, cut into julienne strips
2 green or red bell peppers, cut into julienne strips
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Oregano
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Garlic Salt
3/4 teaspoon Spice Islands® Ground Cumin
8 (6- to 8-inch) tortillas, warmed

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet on medium heat. Add chicken, onion, and pepper; stir fry until chicken is no longer pink and vegetables are crisp and tender, about 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in oregano, garlic salt, cumin, salt and pepper. Fill warm tortillas with chicken mixture. If desired, garnish with sour cream, avocado, cilantro, and lime wedges.
