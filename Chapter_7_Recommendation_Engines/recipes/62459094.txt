Title:
Sushi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472097.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 1 Package of Seaweed (10 Sheets)
2. 6 Cups of Japanese sushi rice
3. 6.5 Cups of water
4. 2/3 Rice Wine Vinegar
5. 4 Tbsp of Sugar
6  2 Tsp of Salt
7. 3 Package of Smoke Eel

Directions:
1. Cook rice in the rice cooker
3. When done, put in Vingar, Salt and Sugar, mix well
4. Eel can be cut into 3 pieces, and each piece can be cut into 4 strips.
5. Each roll of sushi needs 4 strips of Eel
