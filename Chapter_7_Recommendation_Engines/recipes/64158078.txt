Title:
Dad's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Ripe Bananas
1 egg
2 tablespoons oil
1/4 cup milk
1/4 - 1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1.5 cup flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mash bananas, add egg, oil, milk, sugar, salt, baking soda, and baking powder.
Mix well.
Slowly add flour while stirring.
Put mixture in loaf dish (spray dish or line with wax paper).
Bake 45 min or until toothpick comes back clean.
