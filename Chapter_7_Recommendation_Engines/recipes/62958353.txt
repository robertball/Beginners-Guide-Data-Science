Title:
Bourbon Chicken with note about sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. raw chicken breast
Corn starch and egg whites as needed for dipping chicken
1 teaspoon minced garlic
2 teaspoon minced shallots
1/2 cup Bourbon
1/2 cup low sodium chicken stock
1 T+ 1 tsp low sodium soy sauce
2/3  tablespoon brown sugar
I teaspoon molasses
pinch red pepper flakes
I teaspoon Dijon mustard
2 teaspoon cornstarch
1 T + 1 tsp cold water

Directions:
Dip chicken breasts in egg white, then coat lightly with cornstarch. Brown
on both sides in a pan with cooking spray on medium heat. Finish cooking
in a 350 F oven for about l5 minutes or until no longer pink inside. Set
chicken aside and keep warn.
Meanwhile, saut√© garlic and shallots over medium heat with cooking
spray. Pour bourbon into the pan and ignite (*be careful if you are using a gas
stove you may simply tilt the pan to ignite; if not, use a long match or lighter).
Cook off the alcohol; add chicken stock, soy sauce, brown sugar,
molasses, red pepper flakes, and Dijon mustard and continue heating.
Meanwhile, mix the cornstarch and cold water to make a
slurry; add to sauce as needed to thicken. Continue heating and stirring
sauce until it reaches desired consistency. Toss chicken breasts in
sauce to coat and serve.
