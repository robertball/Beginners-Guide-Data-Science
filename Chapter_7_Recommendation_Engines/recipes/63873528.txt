Title:
Coconut Chicken and Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1074670.jpg
Ingredients:
* 1 Medium Butternut Squash
* 2 Zucchini quartered and sliced
* 2 Celery Stalks thinly sliced
* 3 Tbsp. Green Onions Chopped
* 1 Chicken Breast
* 1 Can Chicken Broth
* 2 Cans Light Coconut Milk
* 2 Tbsp grated Ginger
* Dash Cayenne Pepper (to taste)
* 2 Tbsp Sesame Oil
* 2 Tbsp Soy sauce
* 4 Tbsp Lime Juice

Directions:
1. Place chicken breast and chicken broth in crockpot and cook for until breast begins to shred (about 4 hours). Using a fork pull apart chicken breast to shred it completely.
2. Add  coconut milk to crockpot
3. Cut Butternut squash in half and remove seeds.  Place face down in pan with about ¼ inch of water. Cook at 450 for about 30 min.
4. Heat sesame oil in sauce pan over medium heat. Add Zucchini, celery, and green onions. Cook for about 2 minutes then add lime juice, soy sauce and ginger. Cook, stirring frequently, about another 2 minutes. The zucchini and celery should be soft, but not mushy.
5. Add sauce pan mix to the crockpot and cayenne pepper to your liking.
6. When squash is done slice halves into 1 inch strips. Cut the meat into 1 inch cubes (don’t cut through skin) and remove cubes with a spoon.
7. Add Squash to crockpot and allow to cook through at least 1 hour

Description: 
I love Thai food, but have trouble finding some of the unique ingredients at my small town store. This soup has a similar flavor to traditional thai soup with easy to find ingredients. My husband was very impressed with the recipe and is eager to eat it again.
