Title:
Girganza & pear Filled Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Package frozen Ravioli filled with Gorgonzola cheese and pear ( Aldi beans )
1/4 c olive oil
1 teaspoon chopped garlic
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
Salt and fresh ground pepper
8 dates ( dried) chopped
14 grapes
Fresh Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Boil water to cook Ravioli as per direction on pkg
In frying pan cook olive oil . Garlic, cook 2 min add dates seasonings cook for 20 min med heat ... add grapes ... cook 2 min ... take potato masher and mash together the dates and grapes ... cook 2 more min ... pour over drained Ravioli ... toss with cheese

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Frozen Ravioli filled with pear and cheese .. olive oil and Dates and grapes sauce
