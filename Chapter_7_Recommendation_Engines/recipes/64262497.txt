Title:
Bread Pudding with crescent rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tubes of crescent rolls
2 8oz packages of cream cheese
1 Â½ cups of sugar
1 tsp of vanilla
1 tsp of cinnamon
1 stick of butter

Directions:
Spread 1 tube of crescent rolls on bottom of greased 9x13
Mix together 1 cup of sugar, both cream cheese packages, and vanilla
Spread mixture over crescent rolls
Spread 2nd layer of crescent rolls
Combine remaining sugar and 1 tsp of cinnamon and sprinkle over top
Melt the stick of butter and pour over top
Bake at 350 for 30 min. and enjoy!!
