Title:
Graham Cracker Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 Graham crackers, rolled
1/2 c scant melted butter
1 1/2 c milk
1/2 c sugar
3 egg yolks, 3 egg whites
2 scant T cornstarch
1/2 t vanilla

Directions:
Mix the graham crackers and the butter, and press into a pie pan, saving out a few crumbs for the top.  Bake 10 min. at 350.
Stir all other ingredients together and cook until thick in a double boiler.
Make meringue out of egg whites and 6 T sugar. (I add 1/4 t baking powder.)
Add custard to pie shell; topw ithe meringue and sprinkle with remaining crumbs over top and brown in 300 degree oven for 15 minutes.  Chill thoroughly before serving.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
This recipe is from my first husband's mother.  It was very popular at family get-togethers.
