Title:
Tuna and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978059.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups medium egg noodles, uncooked
3/4 pound (12 oz.) VELVEETA Pasteurized Prepared Cheese Product, cut up or 1 (8 ounce) package KRAFT Cheddar CLASSIC MELTS
1 (16 ounce) package frozen vegetable blend, thawed, drained
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained, flaked
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Bring 2 cups water to boil in saucepan. Stir in noodles. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover. Simmer 8 minutes or until noodles are tender. Do not drain.
Add VELVEETA, vegetables, tuna and pepper; stir until VELVEETA is melted.
Try serving with whole wheat rolls and a quick bagged salad tossed with your favorite KRAFT Dressing such as Ranch.
