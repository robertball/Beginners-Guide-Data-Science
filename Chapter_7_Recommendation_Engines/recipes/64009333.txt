Title:
Garlic Burnt Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Small bunch of broccoli cut into 2" florets w/some stem
3 - 5 cloves of garlic minced (depending on your taste)
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
1 tsp. bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Wash and then dry broccoli well. In a large bowl, whisk all ingredients well adding the broccoli last. Coat it evenly. Sprinkle in the bread crumbs and lightly mix. Toss it in a prepared pan spread out flat and bake it for 15-20 minutes uncovered. Stir it once or twice while baking. When you see the florets browning and/or burning, it is ready. Remember all ovens vary in temp.

Submitter: 
joannemerc
Description: 
Great healthy vegetable side dish. If you love crisp broccoli and love garlic, this simple recipe is for you. When the florets start to burn, its ready to serve.
