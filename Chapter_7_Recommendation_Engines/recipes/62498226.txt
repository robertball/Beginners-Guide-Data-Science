Title:
Banana Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6906019.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 T. flour
1 c. milk
1 c. brown sugar
3 or 4 T. butter, softened
bananas

Directions:
Beat eggs.
Beat flour into eggs.
Add milk, sugar and butter and mix well.
Cook on stove at medium heat until desired thickness.
Add sliced bananas when cooled.
