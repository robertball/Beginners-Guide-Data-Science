Title:
Drunk Monkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/617454.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce banana liqueur
1 fluid ounce melon liqueur
1 fluid ounce orange liqueur
2 fluid ounces pineapple juice
2 fluid ounces cranberry juice

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice. Pour in the banana, melon and orange liqueurs, and stir. Top off with pineapple juice and cranberry juice, and enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
This drink is tropical, fruity, and soooo refreshing! A great summertime drink.
