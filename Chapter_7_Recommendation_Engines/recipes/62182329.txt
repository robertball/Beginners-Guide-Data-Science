Title:
Yummy Pizza Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (8 pack) cresent rolls (uncooked)
1 bag of pepperoni (8oz)
1 bag of string mozzerella cheese (4 pack)
1 jar pizza sauce (14 oz)

Directions:
Preheat oven to heat required for cresent rolls. Roll out cresents on lightly greased baking sheet. overlap pepperoni, try to keep it inside the cresent roll. Slice a string cheese in half, and add one half to the top of the cresent roll. (the bigger top) Roll the cresent from the biggest part, to the littlest. Fold in the edges, and repeat with the rest.
While they are in the oven, take the pizza sauce, and microwave it until warm. Makes a great dipping sauce!
