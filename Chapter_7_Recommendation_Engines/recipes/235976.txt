Title:
Islander Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice, or as desired
2 2/3 cups champagne
1 1/3 cups pineapple juice
1/2 cup half-and-half
1 tablespoon simple syrup

Directions:
Put ice in a blender. Pour champagne, pineapple juice, half-and-half, and simple syrup over the ice; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
CJVanderhoef
Description: 
Cool refreshing drink. Excellent for Christmas morning or New Year's Eve! Orange juice is a suitable substitute for pineapple.
