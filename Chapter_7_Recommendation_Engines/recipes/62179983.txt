Title:
Fruitcake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz can frozen concentrated juice, thawed
1/2 c light brown sugar, firmly packed
2/3 c light brown sugar, firmly packed
1 c raisins
1 1/2 lb mixed candied fruit, finely chopped
1/2 butter, room temp
4 eggs
1 c flour
1/8 tsp bakins soda
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp all spice
1/4 tsp cloves
1 cup chopped pecans
ICING:
1 1/2 c powde sugar
1/4 c orange juice
1 tbsp butter

Directions:
In a sauce pan combine undiluted orange juice and 1/2 c light brown sugar. Stir over low heat until mixture comes to a boil. Add raisins, bring to a boil agian.
Remove from heat, stir in candied fruit and set aside.
Cream butter and 2/3 c light brown sugar. Beat in eggs, one at a time. Blen in flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice and cloves. Stir in nuts and fruit mixture.
Turn into 2 parchment lined baking sheets (15"x10"x1") with cooking sparyed on.
Bake in 300 degree oven 35 to 40 mins or until cake tester inserted comes out clean. Cool on wire racks.
ICING:
In small bowl mix all ingredients until smooth.
Cover both pans of bars. Cut into 3"x1" bars. Garnish with halved candied cherries each all.
Let icing dry before storing in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A different take on fruitcake, with an orange base to the cake, with wonderful taste of the nuts and fruit.
Been in the family for a long time, and is a annual tradiotn at christmas time.
