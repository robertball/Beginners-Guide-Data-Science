Title:
Arroz Rojo (Mexican Red Rice) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Roma tomatoes, cored
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cup of minced onion
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 cup of long grain rice
1 3/4 cups low sodium chicken broth
1/4 cup canned tomato sauce
1 jalapeno chile
2 sprigs fresh cilantro
salt to taste

Directions:
Use a box grater to grate tomatoes into a bowl, discard the tomato skins.
Heat the oil in a medium heavy skillet over medium-high heat.  Add the onions and saute until translucent. About 5 minutes.
Add garlic, cook for 1 minute.  Stir in rice, and cook until slightly toasted. About 3 minutes.
Stir in tomato sauce, grated tomato, and broth.  Bring to a boil.
Add chile, cilantro, & salt to taste.  Reduce the heat to low, cover pan with lid, simmer for about 15 minutes.  DO NOT REMOVE LID
Turn off heat, let rice stand for 8 minutes with lid on.  Fluff rice with fork before transferring to serving dish.

Submitter: 
Paulette
Description: 
Authentic mexican red rice.  Easy to make!  Use a different chile if you want milder or hotter rice.
