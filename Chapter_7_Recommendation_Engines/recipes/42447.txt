Title:
Sweet Pretzel Nuggets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984706.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package sourdough pretzel nuggets
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions:
Place pretzels in a microwave-safe bowl.
In a small bowl, combine oil, sugar and cinnamon; pour over pretzels; toss to coat.
Microwave, uncovered, on high for 2 minutes; stir. Microwave 3-4 minutes longer, stirring after each minute or until oil is absorbed. Cool to room temperature.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
This crowd-pleasing snack is a hit both at home and at work. The fun crunchy bites have a sweet cinnamon-toast taste and just a hint of saltiness that make them very munchable.
