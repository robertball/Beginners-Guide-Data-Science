Ingredients:
1 whole garlic bulb
Olive oil, as needed
pinch of salt
1 cup water

Directions:
Cut 1/4 inch off the top of the bulb
Place the steamer basket on the bottom of the pot and add the water
Place the garlic on the steamer basket and drizzle with oil.
Sprinkle the salt on the bulb.
Put the lid on the pot and press Poultry, closed vent.
Adjust the time to 10 minutes, if necessary.
It will take about 10 minutes for the pressure to build.
Allow natural release, about 10 minutes.
Squeeze the pulp into a glass jar and refrigerate when cool enough to handle.
Note: Double, triple or steam as many as will fit into the basket.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
The colour won't be the same as those roasted in the oven but the flavour is wonderful! Use the garlic to make garlic bread, aioli ...the possibilities are endless, enjoy!
