Title:
Must Have Ham Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 lbs thicker sliced ham (not honey baked)
1/2 red onion, chopped
2 dashes Worcestershire

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix softened cream cheese, onion and Worcestershire.  Pat ham slice with paper towel to dry (makes spreading easier), spread cream cheese mixture evenly on entire slice.  Roughly a 1/4" thick.  Roll ham slice long way up.  Put in fridge and let chill at least 1 hour.  Cut ham rolls in bite size pieces.  Can serve with toothpicks if wanted.
