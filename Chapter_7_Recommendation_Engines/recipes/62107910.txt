Title:
Easy Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup half/half
1 cup shredded/cubed cheese of choice
single pie crust
opt: 1 cup shredded zucchini, bacon, ham or pkg thawed spinach

Directions:
preheat oven 425
place pie crust in pie plate & set aside
combine all ingredients & mix well
pour mixture in pie crust.
place pie plate on cookie sheet in case of overflow
bake for 15 minutes
Decrease oven temp to 325 and bake for additional 30-35 minutes, until set.
let stand 15-20 minutes before serving.
