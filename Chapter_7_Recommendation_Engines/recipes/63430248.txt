Title:
Easy Apple Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crescent rolls
2 Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored and cut into quarters
1 c sugar
1/2 c butter, melted
Cinnamon
1 c Mountain Dew

Directions:
Separate dough into triangles; wrap each around an apple wedge.
Place in 9x13 baking dish.
Cream butter and sugar together and spread over crescents.
Sprinkle with cinnamon.
Pour Mountain Dew around, not over, dumplings.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
