Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2471382.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Egg Whites, at room temperature
1/2 teaspoon Cream of Tartar
1 cup Sugar
1 large box of Nerd candies

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 225 F
Beat egg whites in a large bowl using a handmixer at medium speed until frothy. Add Cream of Tartar; beat until soft peaks form. Increase speed to medium-high. Add Sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time (16 tablespoons total) beating until sugar is dissolved and stiff peaks form. Gently stir in Nerds
Drop rounded spoonfools about 1 inch apart onto 2 large foil-lined baking sheets sprayed with non-stick spray
Bake both sheets at the same time for 45 min. Turn oven off, and leave cookies to cool in oven for 1 hour.
Remove and enjoy
