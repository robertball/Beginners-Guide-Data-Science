Title:
Fruit glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Glaze
2 cups strawberries
3/4 cup sugar
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Puree the 2 cups strawberries, sugar and cornstarch.  Now add this to a pan and simmer until thickened. Cool.
Stir in the lemon then fold in  2 lbs strawberries.

Submitter: 
Slowturtle
Description: 
Use this as a glaze, syrup or over ice cream.
