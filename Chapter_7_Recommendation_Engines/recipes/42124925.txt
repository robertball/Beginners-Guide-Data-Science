Title:
Best Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil
2 T finely chopped red pepper
1 T dijon mustard
2 large garlic cloves
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
Combine lemon juice, oil, red pepper, mustard, garlic, salt and pepper, reserve 1/4 cup.   Pour remaining marinade over chicken
Use remaining marinade to baste as grilled.
