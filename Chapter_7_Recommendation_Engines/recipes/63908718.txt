Title:
Funky tomato sauce with hot italian sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb spicy italian sausage
1 medium diced onion
4 cloves minced garlic
1 fresh chili, chopped
1/2 green pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine
3 anchovies, minced
1/2 tsp oregano
1 bay leaf
1 tsp basil
28oz whole tomatoes, mashed up
basil and mayonnaise, to desired taste

Directions:
Boil sausages and drain fat. Skin and cut in chunks. Saut√© onion, garlic, chili, green pepper in the oil over low heat for about half an hour. Add wine, sausage, anchovies, herbs and tomatoes. Simmer for 2hrs. Spoon mayo over pasta and add sauce. Add parmesan.
