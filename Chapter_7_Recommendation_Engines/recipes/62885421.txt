Title:
Chocolate Chip Snack Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (18 ounce) rolls chocolate chip cookie dough
1 (8 ounce) cream cheese, softened
1 egg
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Slice 1 whole and approximately 1/3 of the other roll of cookie dough and line a 9x13 pan. Press to cover entire pan. Beat cream cheese until soft, add sugar and beat very well. Add egg and vanilla, again mixing well. Spread mixture over cookie dough and drop by teaspoons over cream cheese layer giving it a marbled effect. Bake for 25 - 30 minutes. Cool completely before cutting into squares. Refrigerate.
