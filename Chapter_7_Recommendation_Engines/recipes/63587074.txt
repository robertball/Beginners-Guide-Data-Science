Title:
Spicy vegetable stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bean sprouts, water chestnuts, onion, bok choy, bell peppers, carrots, celery, soy sauce coconut oil, baby corn, snow peas, garlic.

Directions:
Slice one yellow onion
4 sliced carrots - peeled
4 celery stalks sliced
3 bell rainbow bell peppers sliced
3 stalks of bok choy sliced
1 can of bean sprouts drained
1 can water chestnuts drained
1 can baby corn drained
1 tbsp minced garlic
6 tablespoon soy sauce
3 tablespoon water
1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
Mix together and set aside
2 tablespoons coconut oil
Heat oil in skillet over medium flame
Onions, carrots, celery to the oil heat until soft 7-10 mins.
1/2 tablespoon cayenne powder
1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper.
1 tablespoon minced garlic added to the vegetables.
Stir occasionally
Step 2
Add peppers
Stir peppers into mix let cook for 5 minutes.
Add the soy sauce/ cornstarch mixture to the mix.
Now add the bean sprouts, watercheastnuts and baby corn to the stir fry. Optional snow peas cook for 5-7 minutes.
Now add the steamed broccoli and bok choy and cook for 5 minutes.
