Title:
Nutty Chicken Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbs peanut butter
2 tbs honey
4 tbs Orange juice
1 tbs olive oil
1 tsp grated ginger
2 tbs Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 lbs skinless boneless chicken breast, cut into thin strips

Directions:
1. Make marinade by combining the peanut butter and honey in mixing bowl and whisking well.  Add all other ingredients, except the chicken, and mix until well combined.
2. Put marinade and chicken in large zip-top bag. Use hands to massage sauce into meat.  Let chicken marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
3. Rub grill with cooking oil and heat up to medium. Thread chicken pieces onto bamboo skewers and discard marinade.
4. Grill chicken 8-10 minutes per side, or until no longer pink and juices run clear.
