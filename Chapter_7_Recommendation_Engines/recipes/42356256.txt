Title:
Country Cornbread Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups buttermilk cornbread
4 slices of bread (or 2 cups bread crumbs)
3 cups chicken broth
2 eggs (optional)
2 celery stalks, finely chopped
1 cup onion, finely chopped
1 tsp sage
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together until smooth and place in a lightly greased 13 x 9 inch baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Cover until time to serve.

Submitter: 
Karen Tinsley Medlin
Description: 
Some may call it dressing, some may call it stuffing, but I just call it good. This recipe is a basic cornbread dressing and it wouldn't be Thanksgiving at my house without it. Serve this side dish with giblet gravy and you are good to go.
