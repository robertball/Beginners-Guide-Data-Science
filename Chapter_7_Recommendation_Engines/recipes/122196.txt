Title:
Baked Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983113.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup chopped sweet onion
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
Minced fresh parsley
Assorted crackers

Directions:
In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, onion, Parmesan cheese and garlic salt; stir in Swiss cheese.  Spoon into a 1-qt. baking dish.  Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees F for 40 minutes.  Sprinkle with parsley if desired.  Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Mona  Zignego
Description: 
'Some people like this cheesy dip so much that they can't tear themselves away from the appetizer table to eat their dinner,' writes Mona Zignego of Hartford, Wisconsin.
