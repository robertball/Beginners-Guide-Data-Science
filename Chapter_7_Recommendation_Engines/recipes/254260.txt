Title:
3-Ingredient Frozen Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4553269.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups ice cubes
3 cups milk
3 cups hot cocoa mix

Directions:
Combine ice cubes, milk, and hot cocoa mix in a blender; blend to milkshake consistency.

Submitter: 
KimberTower
Description: 
Great for a hot chocolate lover year-round! Toss some whipped cream on top if you want to.
