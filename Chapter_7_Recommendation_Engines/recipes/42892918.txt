Title:
1921 Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922106.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp dry mustard
Pinch paprika
Pinch red pepper
1 Tbsp water
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 egg
2 cups vegetable oil

Directions:
Add first 6 ingredients together and stir until sugar dissolves.
Add lemon juice
Beat egg into mixture until the dressing starts to thicken
Add the 2 cups of oil in larger amounts beat well after each addition.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
Interesting old school recipe for turn of the century mayo... or would we call this a special Aoli now days? I found this in an old book on the shelf in my family's cabin on Lake Champlain. The cabin has been in the family since the 1920's.
