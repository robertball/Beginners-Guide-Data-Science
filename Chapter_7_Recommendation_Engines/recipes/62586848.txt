Title:
Cutout Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lg egg yolks
1/2 c almond paste (4oz) broken into pieces
2 c unsalted butter cut up & softened
1 3/4 c sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3 3/4 c flour

Directions:
In a lg bowl with a heavy duty mixer at med speed beat egg yolks & almond paste 2 min or until crumbly. Add butter, sugar & salt, beat 3 min till creamy. Reduce speed to low & gradually add in flour till just blended. On a lightly floured surface divide dough into 4 equal pieces & flatten into a disk. Wrap each w/ plastic wrap & chill 2 hrs. Preheat to 375. On a lightly floured surface w/ a floured rolling pin roll 1 pc of dough 1/4 in thick. With floured 3-4 in cookie cutters cut into cookies & chill trimmings. Place cookies 1 in apart on an ungreased large cookie sheet. Bake 10-12 min or till lightly browned. Transfer to cooling rack. Repeat with remaining dough. When cookies are cool you can ice & decorate. Store in a tightly sealed container w/ waxed paper between layers at room temp for up to 2 weeks or freeze for up to 3 months.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
My mother in law uses this recipe every holiday season to bake cookies with the kids. I'm not much of a sugar cookie person but these taste wonderful & hold up to shaping very well. They don't get dry & hard like so many other recipes.
