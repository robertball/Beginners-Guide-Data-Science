Title:
Strawberry Yogurt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
3/4 C water
1/3 C veg. oil
3 egg whites
1 (6 oz.) container strawberry yogurt
2 C whipped frosting (or use 2 cups fruit dip)
1 quart (4 C) strawberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (325 for dark or nonstick pans.)
Generously grease and lightly flour, or spray with baking spray with flour, two 8 or 9-inch round pans.
In a large bowl, beat dry cake mix, water, oil, egg whites, and yogurt on low speed for 30 seconds; beat on medium speed for 2 min. Pour into pans
Bake 8-inch rounds 26-30 min., 9-inch rounds 22-26 min. or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cool 10 minutes.
Run knife around sides of pans to loosen cakes; remove from pans to cooling rack.
Cool completely, about 1 hour.
Place 1 cake layer, rounded side down, on serving plate.
Spread 1/3 cup frosting over layer to within 1/4 inch of the edge.
Cut about 10 strawberries into 1/4-inch slices; arrange on frosted layer.
Top with second layer, rounded side up.
Frost side and top of cake with remaining frosting.
Cut remaining strawberries in half; arrange on top of cake.
Store loosely covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a Betty Crocker recipe. It is super rich and sweet, so a very little goes a long way. It's nice and cool, which makes it a great summer treat.
