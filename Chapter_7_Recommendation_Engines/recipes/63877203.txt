Title:
Connie's Greek Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
In a glass screw top jar combine the following:
2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
6 tablespoons red wine vinegar
4 tablespoons lemon juice
3 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 cloves peeled minced garlic

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in glass screw to jar. Shake to mix and refrigerate overnight to combine flavours.
