Title:
Easy Peas-y Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056362.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of uncooked brown rice
1 1/4 cups of water or chicken broth
1 tablespoon of curry powder
1 teaspoon of salt
1/2 tablespoon of canola or olive oil (optional)
1 can or 1 cup of cooked, drained peas

Directions:
In a medium-large sized pot, bring the water to a boil
Stir in rice and return to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer covered for 40 minutes, or until most of the water is absorbed
Mix in peas and cover for another 5-10 minutes, until the rice is fully cooked and no water remains visible.
Remove from heat. Sprinkle the curry and salt over the rice and mix in. Add oil, if desired.

Submitter: 
karkar
Description: 
Quick and easy side dish to whip up while the chicken is in the oven. A great, healthy alternative to pasta or white rice and an easy way to get the kids to eat their veggies.
