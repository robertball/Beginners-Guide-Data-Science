Title:
Couscous chickpeas artichoke and black olive salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup instant couscous
2 tbsp finely chopped dry-packed sun dried tomatoes
1 cup boiling water
3 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp dried mint
1 tbsp white wine vinegar
1 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp pepper
1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts , drained and quartered
1 can of garbanzo beans
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (about 5 oz/140 g)
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley or spinach
1/3 cup sliced black olives, rinsed and drained
optional chopped green onions

Directions:
Preparation:
Place couscous and sun-dried tomatoes in heatproof bowl. Add boiling water; stir once, then cover and set aside for 10 minutes. Fluff with fork.
In measuring cup, whisk together oil, lemon juice, mint, vinegar, oregano, and pepper; stir into warm couscous.
Top with artichoke hearts, garbanzoes, feta cheese, parsley and olives; stir gently to combine.

Submitter:

Description: 
I used whole wheat couscous.  This is very versatile recipe, you can add chopped chicken, red onion, or whatever you like.  Try it in a pita with fresh tomatoes.
