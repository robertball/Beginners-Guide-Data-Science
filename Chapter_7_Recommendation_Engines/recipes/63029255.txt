Title:
Annabel'sPrune Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup butter or shortening
3 eggs
3 TBL sour cream
1/8 tsp salt
1 tsp soda
1 tsp allspice
2 cups flour
1 cup cooked chopped prunes

Directions:
Cream sugar, shortening and eggs. Add sour cream. Sift together flour, salt soda and allspice. Add to mixture. Add prunes and mix thoroughly. Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees). Ice with Sour Cream Prune Frosting
This can be made as a double layer or sheet cake (Grans always made it as a double layer)

Submitter: 
baileyt0216
Description: 
I have many fond memories of my Grandmother making this cake at Thanksgiving or Christmas. It is the most heavenly cake!
