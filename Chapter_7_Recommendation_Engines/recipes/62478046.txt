Title:
Beef Barley Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) beef chuck roast
1 cup barley (long-cooking, not instant)
1 bay leaf
2 tablespoons oil
3 carrots, trimmed & sliced into coins
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 onion, chopped
4 cups beef stock
2 tsp - 1 Tb. worcestershire sauce
white sugar?
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
Vege-Sal or salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients EXCEPT BARLEY AND SALT in the slow cooker and cook on Low for 9 hours.
Increase heat to High.  Add barley and salt; stir well.  Cook for 45 additional minutes.
Remove bay leaf and correct seasoning (may require a small amount of sugar) and serve with good bread.
