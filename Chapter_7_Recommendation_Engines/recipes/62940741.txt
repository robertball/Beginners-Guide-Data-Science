Title:
Cheese and Carrot Bread Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 C Water
1/2 C Milk
1 pkg. Yeast
1 T Sugar
1 tsp. Salt
1/2 C Shredded Carrot
2 T Oil
1/2 C (4 oz) Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 tsp. Grated Orange Peel
1 Egg
3 C Bread Flour

Directions:
Put all ingredients in bread maker and bake.
