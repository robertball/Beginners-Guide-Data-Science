Title:
Chicken Asparagus Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 1/2 lb cooked chicken or 6 half breasts of chicken
4 tbsp butter
3 eggs hard boiled
2 15 oz cans of asparagus or fresh steamed
1/2 c. mayo
1 10 3/4 oz can cream of mushroom soup
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese
25 Ritz crackers crumbled
3 tbsp butter melted

Directions:
Boil and cool chicken, bone; keeping pieces large
Salt and pepper chicken to taste and saute for 2-3 min in butter
Slice eggs thinly
Drain asparagus and spread on bottom of casserole
Top with layer of eggs, then chicken
Mix mayo, soup, lemon juice, and cheese and pour over chicken
Saute crackers in melted butter and sprinkle on top
Bake 350 degrees for 25 minutes; Serves 6

Submitter: 
Lisa Schwartz-Gunnison
