Title:
Grandma's Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 BONE IN pork chops
1 Tbsp pepper
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp (or to taste) garlic salt
1 cup flour (or 1/2)
1 med. onion (chopped)
2 cloves garlic (minced)
dried or fresh parsley (for garnish)

Directions:
Wash & paper towel dry chops.
Pre-heat oven to 350F.
Chop onion & mince garlic....set aside.
Heat 1-2 Tbsp oil in heavy cast-iron skillet.
Season chops with pepper, onion powder & garlic salt.
Dredge chops in flour & place in hot skillet (Dragging chops to position in pan keeps sticking to a minimum).
Brown chops on both sides (5-7 min per side depending on thickness).
Scatter onions & garlic atop chops.
Pour 1/2 cup water into skillet.
Place in oven & bake till internal temperature reaches .............
Garnish with parsley (if desired) and serve.
