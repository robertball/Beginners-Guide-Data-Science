Title:
Quick Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
2 cups baby carrots
1 onion, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
greens or spinach, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

Directions:
Bring the chicken broth to a boil in a medium saucepan over medium high heat. Add the carrots,celery, onion, garlic and celery salt.
Reduce heat to low and add the chicken breast. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Add spinach and cook another 10 minutes.
Carefully remove the chicken breast, cut it into chunks and return it to the pot. Stir in the cilantro OR dill to taste.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe, yet it makes a tasty, mostly homemade chicken soup.Modified from: Quick Chicken Soup
