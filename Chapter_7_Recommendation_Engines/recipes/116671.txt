Title:
Spicy Roast Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/815329.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons butter, softened
2 Kaiser rolls, split
1/2 pound thinly sliced deli roast beef
2 slices Monterey Jack cheese
1 teaspoon canned diced jalapeno pepper

Directions:
Spread butter over the cut sides of rolls. Layer beef, cheese and peppers on the bottom halves; replace tops. Wrap each sandwich in foil; place on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees F for 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Shirley Wayne
Description: 
Jalapenos add zip to this quick-to-assemble combo of roast beef and cheese from Shirley Wayne. 'It's a good hot sandwich to eat in the car or take to sporting events,' writes the Laguna Woods, California cook.
