Title:
Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/871616.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons chopped sweet pickles
1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, drained and chopped

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, sweet pickles and black olives. Chill in the refrigerator until ready to use.

Submitter: 
CRAGHOLM
Description: 
Flavored cream cheese spread on lunch meat of choice, rolled up, and sliced into pinwheels secured with toothpicks.
