Title:
Italian Country Style Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Garlic powder
Salt
Pepper
3 pounds country style pork ribs
2 tablespoons olive oil
Onion
2 cloves minced Garlic
Mushrooms
Green Pepper
Fresh tomato
1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
Paprika
Oregano
Basil

Directions:
Season ribs with garlic powder, salt and pepper. Heat the oil in a extra-large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ribs to skillet and brown extra well on all sides. Put ribs in crock pot, add onion, garlic. mushrooms, green pepper and tomato. Pour spaghetti sauce over ribs. Season with Paprika, Oregano and Basil. Turn crock pot on low for 8 hours. Turn ribs after 4 hours of cooking. If the sauce gets too thick, thin it with a little water.
