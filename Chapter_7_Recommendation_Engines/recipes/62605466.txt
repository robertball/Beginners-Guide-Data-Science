Title:
Clone of King's Hawiian Bread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups bread flour
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
4 teaspoons vital wheat gluten
3/4 cup pineapple juice, warmed
1/4 cup water, warmed
2 teaspoons yeast
2 eggs
1/2 stick I Can't Believe It's Not ButterÂ®, melted
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon ginger

Directions:
Mix bread flour, whole wheat flour & vital wheat gluten set to the side.
Heat water & pineapple juice and put in large bowl. Sprinkle with yeast and let rest for about 5 minutes. Add egg, I Can't Believe It's Not Butter, sugar, vanilla & ginger and mix well. Stir in flour mixture, 1/2 cup at a time until fully incorporated. Cover and let rise, about 1 hour. Punch down, put in a greased pie pan and cover. Set aside for a second rise, about 1 hour.
Bake in an oven preheated to 350 degrees, for 25 to 30 minutes.
