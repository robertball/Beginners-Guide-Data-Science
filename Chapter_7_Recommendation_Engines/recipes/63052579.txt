Title:
Columbia Spanish Resturant Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Black Beans
3 Med Onion - Diced
4 Cloves Garlic - Mashed
1/4 tsp ground Cumin
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1 Qts H2O
1 Qts CHIX Stock
2 Green Peppers - Med Dice
2 Bay Leaf
1 tsp Oregano
1 TBLS Salt

Directions:
Wash Beans, remove broken beans.  Soak overnite in 2 Qts H2O.
Pour beans & water into a 4 Qt kettle. Cover and simmer over medium heat.
Saute' Onions and Green Pepper in Olive Oil.  Add Oregano, Bay Leaf, Cumin, Garlic.
Add to beans and stir well.  Add Pepper and Salt to taste.
Serve over White Rice and top with chopped Onion or Green Onion.
