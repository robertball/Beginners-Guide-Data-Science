Title:
Ramen Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg Ramen noodles (chicken)- with flavor packets
1/4c sugar
1/2c sesame oil
2T rice vinegar
Dash soy sauce
1/4c toasted sesame seeds
1/2c toasted almonds
Greens of choice

Directions:
Break noodles up and fry in a bit of canola oil.
Mix flavor packs, sugar, oil, vinegar, and soy sauce; simmer to dissolve sugar.
Let dressing cool and mix with greens.
Top with noodles and almonds.
