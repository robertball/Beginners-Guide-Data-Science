Title:
Amaretto Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7152156.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 cup powdered sugar
4 tablespoons amaretto liqueur
2 tablespoons vanilla extract
1 1/4 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, brown sugar, powdered sugar, amaretto, vanilla extract, and milk in a food processor. Process until creamy.

Submitter: 
kristinsuzanne
Description: 
Great flavorful amaretto dip. Perfect for fall apples.
