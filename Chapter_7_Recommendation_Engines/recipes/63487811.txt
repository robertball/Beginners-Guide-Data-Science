Title:
Black Bean and Corn Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123097.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups black beans (1#)
1 10-oz. pkg. frozen whole kernel corn
1 cup chopped onion (1 lg.)
4 cloves garlic, minced (1/2 tsp. garlic powder)
1 Tbsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. gr. coriander
1/4-1/2 tsp. bottled hot pepper sauce
4c. boiling water
2 14 oz. cans Mexican-style stewed tomatoes (undrained)

Directions:
Rinse beans, place in lg. saucepan.  Add enough water to cover beans by 2 inches.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Simmer, uncovered for 20 minutes.  Remove from heat. Cover; let stand for 1 hour.  Drain and rinse beans.
In a 3 1/2 or 4 qt. slow cooker combine beans, corn, onion, garlic, cumin, salt, coriander and hot pepper sauce.  Pour boiling water over all.
Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 8-10 hours or on high 4-5 hours.  To serve, mash beans slightly to thicken.  Stir in undrained tomatoes.
