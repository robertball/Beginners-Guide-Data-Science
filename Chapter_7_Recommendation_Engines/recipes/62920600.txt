Title:
Best Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup frozen green peas
3-4 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained and de-chunked
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon pepper
2 (4.5 ounce) cans sliced mushrooms or 1 pkg. fresh
1 1/2 cup crushed potato chips (OR crushed buttery crackers with some melted butter)

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
In a large bowl, thoroughly mix noodles, onion, cheese, peas, tuna, soup and mushrooms. Transfer to a 2 quart casserole dish, and top with potato chip or cracker crumbs.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheese is bubbly.

Kilyena

This is a tuna casserole that even my picky family loves! The potato chips give the casserole a crunchy crust.Modified from: Best Tuna Casserole
