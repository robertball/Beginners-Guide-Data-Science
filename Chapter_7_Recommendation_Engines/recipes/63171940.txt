Title:
Summertime Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tea bags
4 cups boiled water
1.5 cups sugar
1 6 oz. frozen orange juice
1 6 oz. frozen lemonade
10 cups water

Directions:
Steep tea bags in boiled water for 5 minutes. Mix in remaining ingredients. Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
foodaholic
Description: 
My mom made this by the jugful for us during the hottest time of the summer. Makes a gallon of refreshing citrus iced tea punch. It's a great tea to serve from those popular spigot containers.
