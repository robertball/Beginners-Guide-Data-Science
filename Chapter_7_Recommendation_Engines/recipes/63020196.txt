Title:
Holiday Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 sweet potatoes (unpeeled)
1 Cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 eggs
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Boil sweet potatoes until tender
Peel sweet potatoes
Mash with sugar, butter, vanilla, eggs, and milk
Spread in a 11"x13" baking dish
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
Top with marshmallows return to oven and bake until marshmallows are brown.

Submitter: 
Dara Nichole Caffey
Description: 
This is a wonderful sweet potato recipe perfect for holidays!
