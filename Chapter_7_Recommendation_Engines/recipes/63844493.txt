Title:
King Authur Pumpkin Cake Donuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup + 2T all purpose flour
3 eggs
1/2 cup oil
1 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine all but flour in mixing bowl.
Add flour and stir until just moistened.
Fill lightly greased donut wells (or muffin tins) 3/4th full.
Bake @ 350 for 15-18 minutes (muffins 23-25")
Remove from pan and roll in cinnamon sugar mixture (try to do soon when they're still hot)
