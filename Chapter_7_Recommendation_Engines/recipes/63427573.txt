Title:
Danish Puff Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927841.jpg
Ingredients:
*Bottom*
1/2c Butter
1/4t Salt
1c Flour
Water
*Top*
1/2c Butter
1c Water
1t Almond extract
1c Flour
3 Eggs
For the frosting (after baking): Powdered sugar with a dash of milk and almond extract.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Add all of the bottom ingredients together and add enough water to make like pie dough. Pat thin on to ungreased cookie sheet.
Top: In sauce pan combine butter and water, bring to a boil, add almond extract and flour (all at once), beat till smooth and then add your eggs one at a time and mix until smooth, remove from heat. Top the bottom crust with this mixture and bake! Enjoy this delicious treat and be sure to share with friends ;)

Description: 
My absolute favorite for the holiday season. Nothing compares to the light, fluffy, flaky sweetness of this treat. Always a hit, your friends will think you slaved all day for this but it's very simple to make!
