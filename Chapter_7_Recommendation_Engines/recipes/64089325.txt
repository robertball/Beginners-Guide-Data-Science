Title:
Belgian Chicken Booyah Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1183836.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
1 lb beef, cubed
1/2 lb pork, cubed
8 cloves of minced garlic
1 lb onion, chopped
10 sliced carrots
1/2 head of cabbage, shredded
12 medium potatoes, cubed
1/2 bunch celery, chopped
2 14.5 oz cans peas, strained
2 14.5 oz cans corn, strained
2 14.5 oz cans green beans, strained
2 14.5 oz cans peeled whole tomatoes, chopped
2 32 oz carton of chicken broth
1 qt tomato juice
1/4 jar chicken soup base
1 T pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Cut up chicken.  Place chicken, beef, and pork in a kettle.  Add garlic.  Cover with water until just covered.  Bring to a boil and simmer for 1.5 hours.  Remove from heat.  Remove meat and cool.  Strain broth, return to kettle.  Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil.  Simmer until vegetables are tender.  Meanwhile, remove fat and bones from chicken and return meat to soup.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
