Title:
Cilantro and Parsley Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb med/large size uncooked shrimp, shells removed, deveined
1/2 bunch fresh parsley
1/2 bunch fresh cilantro
10 sun dried tomatoes, cut into strips
1 medium onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, mashed
olive oil
2 cups spaghetti or angel hair, cooked (optional)

Directions:
Heat olive oil in skillet over medium high heat.  Add shrimp and onion, cook until shrimp are just turning pink.  Add garlic and sun dried tomatoes.  Cook until shrimp is cooked through and onions are soft.
Serve over pasta if desired.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
Sauteed shrimp and sun dried tomatoes in a light garlic and olive oil sauce.  Great over pasta.
