Title:
"Adios, Turkey" Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 cups cooked turkey, diced
1 medium onion, diced
1 bell pepper, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (10 3/4 oz.) can cream of chicken soup
1 (10 oz. can) Rotel tomatoes with chiles
1 (6 oz.) jar of sliced mushrooms, well-drained
OR
1 lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2-3 tortillas, cut into strips

Directions:
Preheat over to 350.
Mix together the first 8 ingredients and 1 cup of shredded cheese.
Lightly grease a 3 quart casserole dish.
Cover bottom of dish with half the tortilla strips.
Pour turkey mixture into dish and top with remaining tortilla strips.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour, covered.
Remove from oven and top with remaining cheese.
Return to oven and bake, uncovered, an additional 15-20 minutes until cheese is melted and casserole is bubbling.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Need a dish to use the last of that Thanksgiving turkey?  This spicy southwest dish will fill the bill, and it's quick and easy as well.  Your family won't even know it's leftovers.
