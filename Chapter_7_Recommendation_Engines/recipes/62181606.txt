Title:
Butternut squash kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked butternut squash
1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup oil
3 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
Mash butternut squash in bowl and fold in all ingredients except cinnamon.
Pour mixture into round greased 9x1.5 inch pan, and bake for about 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted into middle comes out clean.
Sprinkle cinnamon on top for extra flavor.
