Title:
Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz- 1/3 cup frozen oj
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
5-6 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in blender and belnd untill smooth- about 30 seconds.  Pour into a glass and sip with a straw.
