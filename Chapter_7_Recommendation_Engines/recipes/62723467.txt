Title:
Mom's Rhubarb Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Flour, separated
1/4 Cup brown sugar
1/2 Cup Margarine
1 Tablespoon baking powder
Salt
2/3 cup evaporated milk
1 egg, beaten
2 pounds Rhubarb, cut into 1/4 inch pieces
1 small Package jello (strawberry or raspberry)
1 Cup sugar or so, separated
1/2 Cup butter, softened

Directions:
With a pastry blender, mix 2 cups flour and then all ingredients through salt. Add the milk and the egg. Press dough into a 9X13 inch pan, sprinkling with flour if necessary. Can do it on the bottom only or push it up the sides.
Mix rhubarb, jello and 1/2 cup or so of sugar and spread over the dough.
Mix the remaining 1/2 cup sugar, the remaining 1 cup flour and the butter and spread over rhubarb mixture.
Bake for 45 minutes at 350.
