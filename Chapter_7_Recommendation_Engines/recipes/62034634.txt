Title:
Delicious Roast Beef and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Lb Boneless Chuck Roast
2 Pks Onion Soup Mix
1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
12 oz Coca Cola

Directions:
Line a roasting pan with foil making sure it overlaps the sides of the pan.  Place roast inside the pan.  In a bowl combine soups and coca cola.  Pour the mixture over the roast.  Seal the foil around the roast tightly but make sure it is loose around the roast.  Cook in a 250 degree oven for 4 hours.  If you  like vegetables add them the last hour of cooking.  You now have super tender roast and gravy!

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
This easy and delicious Roast Beef bakes up delious and super tender with its own gravy!  The secret to this roast is using Coca Cola.  You can bake this roast while at church and it will be ready when you get home!
