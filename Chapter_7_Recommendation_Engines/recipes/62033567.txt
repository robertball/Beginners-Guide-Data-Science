Title:
Avocado Cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Hamburgers
1/2 cup cold water
1 Tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon corn starch
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 avocado or 1 medium tomato
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Prepare Hamburgers.  Cook patties in skillet until they are brown.  Remove patties:drain fat from skillet.
Mix water, chili powder,cornstarch,cumin,salt and garlic in same skillet.  Heat to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat.  Return patties to skillet and turn to coat with sauce.
Cut avocado into 4 rings.  Top each patty with avocado ring.  Cover and simmer for 10 minutes; sprinkle with cheese.  Cover and simmer until cheese is melted, about 2 minutes longer.  SPoon sauce onto patties.
