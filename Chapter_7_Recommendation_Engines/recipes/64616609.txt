Title:
Pasole Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# Fresh Pork Butt
1/3 Cup Salt
1# fresh Poblano Peppers (Sliced Julian)
1# Yellow Onion (Slice Jullian)
3 Tbsp mince garlic
2 by leafs
2 cups of tomatillo Salsa
2 cups of Chicken Broth
2 Cans of (12oz each) White Hominy (One drained and one undrained)
Shredded Cabbage
Mexican Oregano
Diced Onions
Sliced Radishes

Directions:
Cut Pork into half inch cubes and season with salt,
Saute Pork until brown with slight brown crust.
Add Peppers, Onions and garlic Saute until onions are translucent
In large pot, add bay leaf, salsa and chicken broth and add pork, peppers and onions to the pot
Cook on low for 2hr
Add hominy at the 1hr mark
Shred Cabbage, Dice Extra Onions and slice lettuce to garnish and serve
