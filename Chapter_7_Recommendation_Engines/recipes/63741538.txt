Title:
Sauerkraut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Can Sauerkraut (drained well)  (No. 303)
2/3 Cups Chopped or shredded Carrot
2/3 Cups Chopped Onion
2/3 Cups Chopped Celery
2/3 Cups Chopped Green Pepper (I mix mine with red and yellow also)
1 Can Whole Kernel Corn-Drained Well
2/3 Cups Vinegar (White or Apple Cider)
2 Cups Sugar
1 Tsp. Black Pepper

Directions:
Combine Sugar, Vinegar, and Pepper in a medium sauce pan and heat until sugar is dissolved. Combine all veggies in a large bowl and mix well. Pour dressing over veggies and mix until all coated, place in a covered dish and refrigerate 4-8 Hours stirring occasionally--a shock for family and friends!
