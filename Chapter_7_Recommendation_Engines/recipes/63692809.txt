Title:
White Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C. milk
1 (6 ounce) pkg. white chocolate baking squares
1/8 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine the milk, baking squares, and nutmeg over low heat; stir until baking squares are melted and the liquid is hot but not boiling.
Remove from heat, stir in vanilla and pour into mugs.  Serve warm.
