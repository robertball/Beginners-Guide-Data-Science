Title:
Mexican Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-boiled eggs
1/4 cup sour cream
2 tsp. taco seasoning (or to taste)
1-2 tsp. spicy brown mustard
1/4 tsp. pepper
Paprika (optional)

Directions:
Cool and peel 6 hard-boiled eggs.
Cut in half lengthwise; carefully remove yolks and remove to a small separate bowl.  Mash with fork.
Add sour cream, taco seasoning, mustard and pepper.  Blend with fork until smooth.
Scoop or pipe into egg halves; garnish with paprika, if desired.
Refridgerate until ready to serve.

Description: 
Deviled eggs with a little south-of-the-border touch!
