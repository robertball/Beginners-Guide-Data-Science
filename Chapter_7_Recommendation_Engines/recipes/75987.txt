Title:
Pomegin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060861.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ice cubes
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger gin
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger pomegranate juice
4 fluid ounces tonic water
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Place ice cubes in a small glass. Pour in gin, pomegranate juice, and tonic water. Stir, and garnish with lime.

Submitter:

Description:
