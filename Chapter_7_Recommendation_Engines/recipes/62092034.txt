Title:
Rhubarb Meringue Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
single pie crust
3 cups rhubarb, chopped
1 cup white sugar
2 Tbsp flour
dash of salt
3 eggs yolks
1 cup 10% cream
3 egg whites
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 tsp cream of tartar

Directions:
Place chopped rhubarb in bottom of pie shell.
Whisk first amount of sugar, flour, salt, egg yolks and cream.
Pour over rhubarb.
Bake at 350 degrees for approx 50-60 minutes, or until set and crust is browned.
Beat egg whites until stiff.
Gradually add second amount of sugar and cream of tartar.
Pour meringue on top of hot pie, making sure to go right to the edges of the pie crust.
Return to oven to brown meringue.
This is great served just a little bit warm.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Marsha Mitchell Hiebert
Description: 
A sweet and creamy rhubarb custard filling topped with meringue.
