Title:
Zesty Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 # whole mushrooms
1/2 # baby carrots
1 med. onion cut into 1/2 inch wedges
1 lg. pepper, any color, cut into 8 strips
1/3 C zesty Italian dressing
1/3 C grated Parmesan cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Line 15 x 10 inch baking sheet w/foil, spray w/cooking spray
Toss veg. w/dressing and 1/4 cup cheese, spread onto prepared pan.
Bake 28-30 min or until veg. are tender, stirring after 15 min. Sprinkle w/remaining cheese
