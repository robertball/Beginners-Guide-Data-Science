Title:
Chili Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef or venison
1 onion, diced
1/4 cp diced green or red bell pepper
1 pkg spicy taco seasoning
1 tbsp ground cumin
salt and pepper to taste
1 16-oz can diced tomatoes or tomatoes with chiles (ro-tel), juice included
1 1/2 cp. frozen corn
1 16-oz can dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 1/2 cp shredded mexican blend cheese, or cheddar
2 boxes jiffy corn muffin mix,prepared per directions
cayenne or jalepeno pepper, to taste, if desired

Directions:
Brown the ground meat in a large skillet, drain off the fat and add onion, pepper, seasoning mix, cumin and salt and pepper. Stir well, cook until onion is transparent,then add the diced tomatoes, cook 1 min and add the corn and beans. Allow to simmer for about 5 minutes then turn into a 9x13 pan. Sprinkle evenly with cheese, then spread the corn bread batter over and distribute evenly. Bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes, allow to sit for 5 minutes before serving. ( good with a dollop of sour cream)

Description: 
This is a delicious casserole of taco flavored ground beef with beans, corn, tomatoes, and cheese topped with corn bread. Filling and delicious, this is a remake of a childhood school-lunch favorite!
