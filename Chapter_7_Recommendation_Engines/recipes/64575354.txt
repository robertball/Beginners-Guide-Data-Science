Title:
Big Ray's Cherry Tomatoes with Buttermilk Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594174.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup minced fresh tarragon
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
3 Tbsp canola oil
1-1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp ground mustard
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp pepper
4 pounds cherry tomatoes, halved
1/3 cup minced fresh chives

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk the first eight ingredients until blended..
Mix with tomatoes and refrigerate, covered.  Refrigerate at least 25 minutes.
Sprinkle with chives before serving.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Even if you have just one cherry tomato plant in your garden, you get LOTS of cherry tomatoes at one time. I like to make this with an old-fashioned homemade dressing with a summery taste.
