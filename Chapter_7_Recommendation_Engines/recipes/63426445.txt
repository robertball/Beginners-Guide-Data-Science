Title:
Ham and Cheese Crescent Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (8oz) refrigerated crescent dinner rolls.
8 thin slices cooked ham (8oz).
4 thin slices cheddar cheese (4oz) each cut into 4 strips.

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350F. Separate dough into 8 triangles. Place 1 piece of ham on each triangle; place 2 strips of cheese down center of ham. Fold in edges of ham to match shape of dough triangle.
2. Roll up each Crescent, ending at tip of triangle. Place with tips down on ungreased cookie sheet.
3. Bake 15 to 18 minutes or until golden brown. Immediately remove from cookie sheet. Serve warm.
