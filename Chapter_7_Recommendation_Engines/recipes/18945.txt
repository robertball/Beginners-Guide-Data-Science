Title:
Larbo's Mango Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/653471.jpg
Ingredients:
4 fluid ounces tequila
1 fluid ounce triple sec liqueur
1 mango, peeled and seeded
2 fluid ounces water
2 cups crushed ice

Directions:
In a blender combine tequila, triple sec, mango, water and ice. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Goldie
Description: 
This delicious fresh mango margarita is very refreshing. For an extra special twist, pour a thin layer of Grand Marnier over the top of the margarita.
