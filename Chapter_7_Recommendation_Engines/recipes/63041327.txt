Title:
Grilled Vegetables with Basil Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBL butter, melted
1 1/2 tsp sweet basil
3/4 tsp onion salt
2 fresh corn, cut crosswise into 2" pieces
2 patty pan squash, cut into 2" thick pieces
2 zucchini, cut crosswise into 2" pieces
1 medium red onion, quartered

Directions:
In small bowl, combine butter, basil and onion salt; mix well. Set aside.
Place vegetables on grid over medium coals; brush generously with butter mixture. Grill 10 minutes. Turn vegetables; brush with remaining butter. Grill 5 minutes longer or until crisp-tender.
