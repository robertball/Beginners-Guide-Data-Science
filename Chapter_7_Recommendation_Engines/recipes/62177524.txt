Title:
Dill pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cucumbers
dill
salt
vinegar
peppers
Brine:
2 1/2 quarts water
1 quart vinegar
1C salt

Directions:
Soak pickles overnight. Scrub and poke. Wash jars, seals and covers. Make brine and boil. Pack jars. 1 piece dill ontop and bottom & 1 piece of pepper. Pour boiling brine over pickles and seal.  1 pail of pickles makes 10quarts, 1 bushel= 32 quarts
Seal jars by processing in hot water canning method.
