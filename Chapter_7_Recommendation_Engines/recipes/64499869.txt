Title:
Chanterelle Mushroom Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 strips bacon- cooked and crumbled
4 cups chanterelle mushrooms- chopped
1 shallot- minced
2 cloves garlic- minced
1/2 c. grated swiss cheese
4 medium eggs
1 cup half and half
1 pie crust

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Saute mushrooms, garlic and shallot in a lightly oiled pan until all the water has been removed and the mushrooms are tender
3. Beat together the eggs and half and half
4. Add the mushroom mixture and bacon to the pie crust and sprinkle with swiss cheese. Pour the egg mixture over the top
5. Cook 30-45 min until the quiche is set. Cool 10 min before eating

Submitter: 
Emily Hunt
Description: 
Delicious quiche perfect for breakfast, lunch or dinner! I use fresh picked chanterelle mushrooms but you could use another kind easily.
