Title:
Chocolate Banana Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954650.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large ripe bananas
8-12 wooden ice cream sticks or skewers
2 cups semi-sweet choc, cherry or white chips
2 tablespoons shortening
toppings (sprinkles, graham cracker crumbs, etc.)

Directions:
Cut bananas in half or thirds.  Insert wooden stick into each one.  Put on wax paper-covered tray.  Cover and freeze until firm.  Melt chocolate chips and shortening.  Remove bananas from freezer just before dipping.  Cover and return to freezer.

Submitter: 
havit-please
Description: 
Great treat at the lake in the Summer.  Gets a fruit serving in the kids and makes them think they are eating dessert.
