Title:
Frozen Peanut Butter Pudding Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/877985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3.9 oz) small package instant chocolate pudding mix
1 1/2 c. milk
1 c. peanutbutter
2 sleeves of graham crackers

Directions:
split graham crackers in half.
add milk to pudding. stir to mix.
add peanutbutter. stir to mix. let sit about 3 minutes to thicken.
spread about 1 heaping tablespoon pudding mix onto one half of graham cracker.
place remaining half on top to make a square.
place on cookie sheet and cover lightly with plastic wrap and freeze.
Once frozen store in a zip-lock bag.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
My sister-in-law taught me how to make these. Some people know them as Puddingwiches.  Chocolatey peanut butter goodness layered between graham crackers and frozen. Make a batch and keep in the freezer for quick summertime snack or dessert.
