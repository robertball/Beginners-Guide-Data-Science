Title:
Oreo Shakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. cold milk
4 Oreo cookies
3 or 4 scoops of vanilla ice cream (or frozen yogurt)

Directions:
Blend the milk and ice cream (or frozen yogurt) in blender unil desired thickness.  Crumble in Oreos and beat until smooth.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
A chilly cookies & cream delight!  Use frozen yogurt instead of the ice cream for a healthier shake.
