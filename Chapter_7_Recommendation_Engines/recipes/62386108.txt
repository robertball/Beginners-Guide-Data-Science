Title:
Crispy Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour
1 t salt
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 cup club soda
1 cup panko breadcrumbs
8 oz cod or other firm white fish (or shrimp)

Directions:
Whisk flour, salt and baking powder, Add club soda.  Whisk together until combined.  (It could still be a little lumpy).  Put panko onto a plate or shallow dish.  Dip pieces of fish into  batter and then into panko.  Fry the fish in batches until brown.

Submitter: 
pelicangal
Description: 
Crispy fish in Panko breadcrumbs for fish and Chips or Fish Tacos.
