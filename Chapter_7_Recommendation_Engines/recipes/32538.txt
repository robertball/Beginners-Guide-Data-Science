Title:
Mocha Cola Recipe

Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces coffee flavored liqueur
2 fluid ounces cola-flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Fill a highball glass to 3/4 with ice. Pour in coffee liqueur and cola. Stir.
