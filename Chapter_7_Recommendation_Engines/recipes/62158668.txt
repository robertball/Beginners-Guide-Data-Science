Title:
Roasted Eggplant and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 japanese (purple) eggplant
1 small yellow onion
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp minced garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Spray rectangular baking sheet with cooking spray.  Slice eggplant into 1-inch thick slices, and then quarter the slices.  Chop onion in half, and then quarter the halves.  Place onions and eggplant on baking sheet.  Sprinkle them with salt, pepper, garlic, and olive oil, and toss with your hands to make sure the vegetables are well coated.
Put in oven and bake for 40 minutes, tossing ingredients halfway through cooking. Serve warm or let cool.
