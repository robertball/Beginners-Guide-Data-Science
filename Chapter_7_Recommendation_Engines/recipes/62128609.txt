Title:
Ps'ghetti Corn! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.75oz)can whole kernel corn
1 (14.75oz)can cream-style corn
1 cup broken raw spaghetti noodles
1 cup diced Velveeta processed cheese
onion (optional)
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in 2 Qt casserole.
Bake uncovered for 1 hour at 350 degrees
Stir after the first 1/2 hour.

Submitter: 
NEgirl
Description: 
Cheesy corn casserole made with creamed corn spaghetti noodles and velveeta.  Very simple, just throw your ingredients in a dish and bake!
