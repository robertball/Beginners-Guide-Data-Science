Title:
rosemary roasted potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Diced red potatoes
2 tsp rosemary
1 tsp Olive Oil
1 tsp Garlic Pepper

Directions:
Place ingredients in a bag to mix
transfer the coated potatoes to a baking dish
bake at 350 until potatoes are soft
