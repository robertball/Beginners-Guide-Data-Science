Title:
Sandy's Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon Minced garlic
1 teaspoon Marjoram leaves
1 teaspoon Minced onion
1/2 teaspoon Coarse Ground black pepper
1 teaspoon Basil leaves
3/4 teaspoon Parsley flakes
1/3 Cup all purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2  pounds thinly sliced boneless skinless chicken breast
3 tablespoons butter, divided
2 Tablespoons olive
2-4 ounce cans mushrooms stems and pieces,drained
1/2 Cup chicken broth
3/4 cup Marsala wine

Directions:
Mix flour, minced garlic,marjoram, minced onions, salt and pepper a shallow dish, Reserve 1 tablespoon of the flour mixture. Coat chicken with remaining flour mixture.
Heat 2 tablespoons of the butter and oil in a large skillet on medium high heat. Cook a few pieces of the chicken 3 minutes per side or until golden brown. Remove from skillet, Keep warm. Repeat until all chicken is cooked.
Add broth and reserved flour mixture and mushroom to skillet along with wine. Bring to a boil, stirring to release browned sits at the bottom of the  skillet. stir in the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter and basil. Cook for about 2 minutes or until slightly thickened. Add chicken to pan and simmer for 5 minutes or until heated through. Just before serving sprinkle wit the parsley flakes.
