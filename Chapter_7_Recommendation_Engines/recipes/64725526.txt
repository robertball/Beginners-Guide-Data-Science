Title:
Wood Sorrel Savory Onion Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6598519.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs butter
1 large onion thinly sliced
2 shallots thinly sliced
3 Cloves garlic minced
1/4 cup of dry white wine
1 tsp salt
3 Tbs flour
1 C fresh wood sorrel leaves, flowers and pods (avoid tough stems)
4 eggs
2 cups heavy cream
2 tsp  freshly ground black pepper
1 cup grated gruyere cheese
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 pre-baked pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Sauté onions and shallots in butter slowly on medium-low heat until they are translucent, add add garlic and sautéed for 1-2 minutes longer, add salt.
Raise the heat to medium and add the wine allowing it to evaporate.
Add flour and stir well.
Combine sorrel and stir constantly for one minute.
Remove from heat allowing to cool.
In a large bowl whisk together the cream, eggs, black pepper.
Combine the sorrel mixture with the egg mixture, onions and half of the cheese.
Pour mixture into the prefaced pie crust.
Place the other half of the cheese evenly on top the dish.
Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 1 hour and until a knife comes out clean and tart is firm.
