Title:
Milk Potato's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potato's
1 cup milk
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Peel and slice potato's crossways, so they form circles.
Place them in a large skillet.
Cover them with water.
Cover skillet and cook over medium high heat till tender.
Most of the water will cook away.
Turn temperature down to low for simmering.
Pour in the milk.
Add salt and pepper.
Stir and cover.
Let simmer till mixture thickens.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
A creamy,simple recipe for potato's the whole family will love.
