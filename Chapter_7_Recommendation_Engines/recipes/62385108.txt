Title:
Easy baked coconut shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs raw peeled and deveigned shrimp
1 cup milk
1/2 bag shredded coconut
2 cups crushed white rice chex cereal
Asian sweet chili sauce for dipping

Directions:
Mix shredded coconut and crushed chex in gallon size ziploc baggie. Dip shrimp first in milk and then place in baggie and shake to coat.  Transfer to greased baking sheet and top shrimp with remaining chex/coconut mixture until coated with desired amount. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until done.  Serve with Asian sweet chili sauce for dipping.

Submitter: 
STOLZS
Description: 
Simple and delicious, this is a family favorite even the kids enjoy. This is also easy to present in a gourmet fashion on a white serving dish with the sauce swirled around the shrimp.
