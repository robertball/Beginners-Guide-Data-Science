Title:
NANCY'S MEAT LOAF WITH TASTY SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEAT LOAF
2 lbs. very lean ground beef
3 large raw eggs
1 pkt of saltines (about 38), crushed
2 rounded tsps Kosher salt (or to taste)
1 scant tsp. freshly ground pepper
2 heaping tsp onion powder
1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
TASTY SAUCE
1 c. brown sugar
1/2. catsup
1 tbsp. cider vinegar
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 tsp. dry mustard

Directions:
For meat loaf:  Mix all meat loaf ingredients untill well incorporated.
Place in lightly oiled 9-inch loaf pan.
Bake at 350F for 50-60 minutes or until cooked through and lightly browned.
Optional:  may drizzle Tasty Sauce on top for last 10 minutes of baking.
For Tasty Sauce:  combine all ingrdients in saucepan.
Bring to a boil over medium heat stirring constantly x 3 minutes.
Remove from heat.
Serve over meat loaf.
