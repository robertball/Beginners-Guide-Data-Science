Title:
Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 sticks of butter (softened)
2 c. sugar
3 eggs
3 c. plain flour
1/2 cup + 2 tblsp. cocoa
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. buttermilk
1/2 c. water
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
Icing:
1 lb. box of confectioners sugar
1 stick butter (softened)
3/4 c. cocoa
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. evap. milk
Dash of salt

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 3, 9" cake pans. Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix dry ing. together in a seperate bowl. Begin adding dry ing. to sugar mixture alternately with water and buttermilk. Begin and end with dry ing. Devide batter as equeally as possible innto prepared cake pans. Bake for 30 min. Cake is done when a toothpick comes out clean. Cool 10 min. pans then turn out onto cake racks. Ice when cool.
Icing:
Blend some confectioners sugar with butter. Then blend the rest of ing., sort of alternating, until you get a good spreading consistency. May take a little more milk.
