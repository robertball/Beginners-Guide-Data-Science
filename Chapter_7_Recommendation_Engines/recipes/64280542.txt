Title:
Kale and Feta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch kale, large stems discarded, leaves finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1 apple, diced
1/3 cup feta cheese
1/4 cup dried cranberries or dried cherries
1/4 cup toasted pine nuts

Directions:
Massage kale with salt in a large mixing bowl for 2 minutes. Pour vinegar over the kale and toss to coat. Fold apple, feta cheese, dried cherries, and pine nuts into the kale.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Chopped kale tossed with apple cider vinegar, diced apple, currants, pine nuts, and feta cheese makes a refreshing salad.Modified from: Kale and Feta Salad
