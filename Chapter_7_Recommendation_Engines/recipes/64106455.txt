Title:
Ratatouille with Sweet Italian Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1250741.jpg
Ingredients:
3 TB olive oil
2 TB crushed garlic
2 tea parsley
1 small eggplant peeled and diced
1 c Parmesan cheese
2 zucchini peeled and sliced
2 yellow squash peeled and sliced
1 onion sliced
2 c sliced mushrooms
1 tea basil
1 tea oregano
2 lg tomatoes chopped
1 pound sweet Italian sausage
1 c mozzarella
Salt

Directions:
Reheat oven 350 degrees.  Coat bottom of casserole dish with olive oil.
Coat bottom of Dutch oven with olive oil, add garlic and eggplant. Cook til eggplant softens..about 10 min.  Add basil, oregano, parsley and salt
In skillet, brown sausage.  Blend sausage with eggplant mixture, add zucchini, squash, tomatoes, mushrooms, onion
In casserole dish, layer mixture and Parmesan cheese.  Top with mozzarella and bake 60 minutes
Serve with rice

Submitter: 
Lynn Mauk
Description: 
Great way to use summer vegetables.  Prep work is a bit much but the results are worth it
