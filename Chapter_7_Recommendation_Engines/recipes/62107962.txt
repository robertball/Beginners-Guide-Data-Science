Title:
Carribean Mango Rice & Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups (15. oz. can) beans (pink, kidney or black) drained and rinsed
2 cups quick-cooking brown rice, cooked according to package directions
1/2 cup red onion, chopped
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
2 mangos, peeled and chopped into 1/2" cubes
1/2 cup olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. minced or grated fresh ginger root
1 tbsp. chopped cilantro
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
3 tbsp. fresh squeezed lime juice
Salt and pepper
2 avocados, cut into 1/2" cubes

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions.
Combine cooked rice, beans, onions, red bell pepper and mango in large bowl.
Whisk together oil, garlic, ginger, cilantro, red pepper flakes and lime juice.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Pour over rice mixture and stir to blend.
If desired, salad can be covered and refrigerated overnight at this point.
Add avocado just before serving.
Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Sweet mango, creamy avocado, zesty spices and cool lime make this bean  and rice salad a great summertime dish that beautifully compliments grilled chicken or fish.
