Title:
Simple Tuna Pasta Salad with Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups bow tie pasta, cooked to your liking
1 7oz. Can albacore tuna
1 cup frozen green peas
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup onion, finely minced OR 1 teaspoon onion powder
A few grinds of black pepper

Directions:
Combine drained tuna, frozen peas, and hot pasta in a medium bowl. Stir to allow peas to defrost using the heat from the pasta. Add mayo, minced onion or onion powder, and pepper. Stir well to combine and chill for at least an hour before consuming.

Submitter: 
PolyTheWicked
Description: 
I'm not sure when I invented this dish, but it's quick and easy to prepare. It's great for lunches at work, but make sure to keep it refrigerated! Also makes a great salad in Summertime. Can also be made with light mayo, but I don't recommend non-fat mayo.
