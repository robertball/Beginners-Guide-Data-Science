Title:
Snapper Veracruz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988006.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (6 ounce) red snapper or halibut fillets
1 tablespoon fresh lime or lemon juice
1 (8 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, undrained
1/4 cup sliced pimento-stuffed green olives
2 tablespoons hot salsa
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 1/2 cups SargentoÂ® Fancy Shredded 4 Cheese Mexican Cheese
Lime wedges (optional)

Directions:
Place fish in single layer in shallow baking dish. Sprinkle lime juice over fish.
Combine tomatoes, olives, salsa, coriander and oregano in small bowl; spoon over fish.
Bake in preheated 350 degrees F oven 20 minutes or until fish is opaque. Sprinkle cheese evenly over fish; let stand 3 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serve with lime wedges, if desired.

Submitter: 
Sargento Shreds
Description: 
Red snapper fillets are baked with a spicy tomato and olive sauce, then topped with shredded cheese.
