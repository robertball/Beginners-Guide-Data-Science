Title:
Trail Mix Cookies II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/464396.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup wheat germ
1/4 cup ground flax seeds
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup coconut
1 cup dried cranberries
3/4 cup walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter, sugar, vanilla and eggs together until smooth. In a separate bowl combine flours, wheat germ, flax seeds, salt and baking soda until well blended. Mix in oats, coconut, dried cranberries and nuts until just incorporated.
Drop onto cookie sheet and bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.
