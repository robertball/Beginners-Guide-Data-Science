Title:
Cheesy Chicken Monterey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Knorr Chicken Rice Sides
1 can corn, drained
1 can black beans,rinsed and drained
1 can chopped green chilies, undrained
1 lb bonless skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
1 C shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
1. Prepare Rice sides according to pkg directions.  Stir in corn, beans, and chilies
2. Meanwhile, heat 1 Tbs oil in lg skillet over medium high heat and cook chicken, stirring frequently, 4 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked
3. Arrange rice mixture on serving platter, then top with chicken and cheese
