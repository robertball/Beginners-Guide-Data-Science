Title:
Yummy Steel Cut Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/558752.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups water
1 cup steel cut oats
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon dried cranberries
2 tablespoon ground flax seed (optional)

Directions:
Bring 3 cups of water to a boil and add 1 cup of steel cut oats, reduce heat. Simmer for 15-20 minutes depending on how chewy you like your oatmeal. Remove from heat, add brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, milk, dried cranberries, and flax seed.
Dried cranberries may be replaced by other fruits or raisins. Serve warm and enjoy.
