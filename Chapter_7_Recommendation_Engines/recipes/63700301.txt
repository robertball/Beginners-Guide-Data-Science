Title:
15 Minute Apple Dumpling Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Apples
1 Can Biscuits (regular, not Grands)
Light Corn Syrup
Cinnamon
Sugar
Butter

Directions:
1) Preheat oven according to biscuit directions.
2) Separate biscuits in half and press out to make thin rounds
3) Slice apple in 1/4" slices (about 10 per apple)
4) Place one apple slice per biscuit round
5) Squeeze a thin line of corn syrup per slice.
6) Sprinkle with Cinnamon
7) Pinch closed
8) Line pan with foil and spray with cooking oil
9) Place each dumpling in pan with 1" between
10) Squeeze a line of corn syrup on top of each dumpling
11) Sprinke with cinnamon
12) Spoon on sugar covering generously
13) Place 1/2 slice pat of butter on top of each
Bake 15 minutes

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
Super easy, quick and delicious apple dumpling. Made with items you already have on hand. Avoids the biscuit taste when served hot.
