Title:
Strawberry, Pear, Pineapple, and Mint Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7132146.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups frozen pineapple chunks, or to taste
2 cups apple juice, or as needed
2 pears - peeled, cored, and cut into wedges
1 cup strawberries
20 fresh mint leaves

Directions:
Blend pineapple chunks, apple juice, pears, strawberries, and mint leaves together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
sixsnowflakes
Description: 
For those who like icy all-fruit smoothies, this one offers a refreshing, complex blend of fresh flavors.
