Title:
Grandma Mara Keen's Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apples- 3 varieties usually used
1/2 c water
Sugar
Cinnamon

Directions:
Wash jars in dishwasher or in hot sudsy water.  Keep warm in dishwasher or in over.  Needs to be warm to add very hot applesauce.  In oven- put them upside down on a cookie sheet.
Wash the apples
Cut them apples into quarters and remove the seeds.
Fill a pot with apples & add water.  Cover the pot.
Bring to boil & then lower the temperature to simmer. Stir now & then to prevent sticking.
When soft, put the apples through a Foley mill.  Return the sauce to the pot.
Add sugar & cinnamon to taste.
Bring to boil again & fill jars.
Make sure top of jar is very clean & add the lids & rings.  Do not screw tight.
Place the filled jars in a canning pot filled with water.
Make sure water is 1 or 2 inches above lids.  Bring to boil & boil 10 minutes.
Remove jars & let cool.  Next day remove screw bands tight & wipe jars.
