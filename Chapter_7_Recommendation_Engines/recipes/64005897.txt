Title:
Watergate Salad featuring The Hulk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Blue Raspberry Jello
1 package Peach Jello
2 packages Pistachio Pudding
1 pint of Whipping Cream
1/2 Cup sugar
1 tbs. Pure Vanilla Extract
(The Following measurements are not precise. They can be adjusted up and down to your needs and yield. If you don't like nuts leave them out. If you want Pecans instead use them. This is your salad)
1/2 cup pistachios peeled and chopped (or more if you like)
1-2 cups mini marshmallows
1 cup Marachino Cherries

Directions:
Step 1: Take Blue Raspberry & Peach powders dump them in a bowl and mix them together. This makes The Hulk portion of the Watergate Salad. Follow package directions to make the jello. Let set.
Step 2: Make the Pistachio pudding according to directions.
Step 3: Take whipping cream, vanilla, and sugar whip with mixer till soft peaks form.
Step 4: Once jello is set break up into pieces with fork, gently fold in jello, whipped cream, and all remaining ingredients. Let chill for half an hour or over night.
