Title:
Healthy Rhubarb Cake in a Mug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3649939.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons milk
1 teaspoon chopped rhubarb
1/3 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Mix egg, flour, sugar, milk, rhubarb, and cinnamon together in a large microwave-safe mug.
Cook on high until cake rises above the top of the mug, about 3 minutes. Let cool for 1 minute before serving.

Submitter: 
Melissa Brandt
Description: 
This is a great way to use rhubarb.
