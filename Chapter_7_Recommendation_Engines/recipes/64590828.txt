Title:
Tropical Fruit with Chocolate Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 cups assorted fresh tropical fruit:  banana, pineapple, strawberries,     carambola, papaya, mango
2 to 3 tablespoons rum
6 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 teaspoons butter
1 tablespoon rum

Directions:
Cut fruits into bite-size pieces.  Toss fruit and rum.  Cover and refrigerate, stirring once or twice, at least 1 hour.
Chocolate Sauce: Heat chocolate, milk and sugar to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly; remove from heat. Stir in butter and rum.  Let stand 1 hour.
Spoon fruit into dessert dishes; drizzle with Chocolate Sauce.
