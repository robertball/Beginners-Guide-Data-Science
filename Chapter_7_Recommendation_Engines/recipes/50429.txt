Title:
Apricot Honey Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Apricot KERNS速 from LIBBY'S速 All Nectar
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
1 pound baby carrots

Directions:
COMBINE nectar and cornstarch in small bowl; mix until smooth.
COOK carrots in boiling water for 10 minutes or until crisp-tender; drain. Return to pan and add honey, butter and salt. Add nectar mixture and cook over low heat for 5 minutes or until apricot glaze is thickened.
