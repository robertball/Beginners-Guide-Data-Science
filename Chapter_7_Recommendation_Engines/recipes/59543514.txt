Title:
Chrissy's Sweet 'n' Sour Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 tomatoes in season or Roma if not home grown
1 small red onion
sliced black olives (optional)
1/2 cup Splenda sugar
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Thinly slice the tomatoes.  Cut the onion in half through root end,  then thinly slice into half-circles.  In a large bowl, toss together tomatoes, onion, sugar, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper.  You may adjust the amounts of vinegar and sugar according to your taste. Add olives if using. Serve at room temperature. May also add low fat blue cheese (crumbled) after plating salads

Submitter: 
Hana
Description: 
An easy to prepare, delicious sweet 'n' sour tomato salad just right to compliment any meal - or eat alone!Modified from: Chrissy's Sweet 'n' Sour Tomato Salad
