Title:
SLOW COOKER CARAMEL APPLES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large tart apples, cored
1/2 cup apple juice
8 tablespoons brown sugar
4 tablespoons butter or margarine
12 caramels
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
vanilla ice cream, optional

Directions:
Peel about 3/4 inch off the top of each apple, place in a slow cooker. Pour juice over apples. Fill the center of each apple with 2 Tablespoons of sugar,  1 Tablespoon of butter and 3 caramels. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or until apples are tender. Serve immediately with vanilla ice cream.
