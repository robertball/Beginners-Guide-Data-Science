Title:
Linda's yogurt sandwich thins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2105174.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C greek yogurt (fat free Chobani),room temp
1/2 T oil
1/2 T honey
2/3 tsp salt
1 C/120g white whole wheat flour (KA)
1 C/85-120g unbleached bread flour (KA)
2 tsp bread machine yeast
2 T luke warm water

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients except bread flour together in mixing bowl.
Add yogurt, water and oil to bowl and mix til combined.
Gradually add bread flour until dough forms.
Knead for about 5 min, lightly oil ball and return to bowl. Cover and let rise until double, about 1 hour.
Knead dough back into a log shape and cut into 8 equal pieces.
Form pieces into discs about 4 inches in diameter, dock all over with a fork, and lightly brush with water.
Let rest while oven preheats to 350 and allow the thins to puff up a bit.
Bake for about 10-12 min.
