Title:
Tandoori Chicken Nirvana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CHICKEN
900g/2lb Chicken Pieces (Thighs/Drumsticks) - skinned/Wings - unskinned
1tsp Chilli Powder
1tsp Salt
Â½tsp freshly ground Black Pepper
2tbsp Lemon Juice
2oz Butter - melted
MARINADE
3tbsp Greek/Greek Style Yoghurt
4 cloves Garlic - crushed
2in piece fresh Ginger - grated
1tsp ground Cumin
1tbsp ground Coriander
2 dried Red Chillies - crumbled
1tsp Red Food Colouring

Directions:
CHICKEN
In a saucer, mix chilli powder, salt, pepper and lemon juice;
Make gashes in chicken pieces, rub mixture all over them all and set aside;
MARINADE
Put all ingredients except food colouring into a bowl and blend together well;
Add food colouring and combine with mixture;
Rub this mixture into and coat the chicken pieces;
Chill and marinate for up to 24 Hours.
COOKING
Preheat oven to 200c/400f/Gas 6
Melt butter in a small bowl
Place chicken pieces on a rack over a large baking dish;
Pour half melted butter over chicken and bake for some 15 to 20 minutes or so until beginning to crisp up;
Turn chicken over and pour over remaining butter and bake for a further 15 to 20 minutes or so until again or until beginning to crisp up;
Ensure both sides crispy then serve with salad and crusty bread;
Use juices remaining in oven tray as gravy into which to dip the bread.
