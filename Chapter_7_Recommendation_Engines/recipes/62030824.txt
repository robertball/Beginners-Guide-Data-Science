Title:
Tabbouleh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. bulgher (cracked wheat)
1/2 onion, finely chopped
1/2 cucumber, finely chopped
3 tomatoes, finely chopped
4 tbsp. parsley, chopped
1 tbsp. dried mint or handful of fresh
4 tbsp. lemon juice or 1 1/2 lemons, squeezed
4 tbsp. olive oil
salt
pepper
1/2 to 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
2 C. boiled water

Directions:
1.  Place bulgher in boiled water in a small bowl.
2.  Soak for 1 hour. Drain.
3.  Mix with other ingredients.
4.  Let stand for 30 mins before serving.

Submitter: 
MICHELLE_F
Description: 
This is one of the national dishes of Syria and Lebanon.  Tastes great served with falafals, pita bread or lamb kebabs.
