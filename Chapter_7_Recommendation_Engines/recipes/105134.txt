Title:
Teriyaki Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces dried penne pasta
1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
3 cups packaged shredded broccoli (broccoli slaw mix)
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Return pasta to saucepan.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet cook ginger and garlic in hot oil for 15 seconds. Stir in the shredded broccoli, mushrooms, and teriyaki sauce. Cook and stir about 5 minutes or until broccoli is crisp-tender.
To serve, toss broccoli mixture with hot pasta; add green onions.

The Sonoma Diet Online
Description: 
Wave 2 is wonderfully easy with this quick broccoli and mushroom pasta toss.
