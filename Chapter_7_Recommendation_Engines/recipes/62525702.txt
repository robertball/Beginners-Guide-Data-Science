Title:
Easy Cranberry Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag (12 oz) fresh or frozen cranberries (if frozen, do not thaw)
1 can (8 oz) crushed pineapple
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup orange juice
1 Tbsp. minced crystallized ginger

Directions:
Combine cranberries, pineapple, sugar and orange juice in a non-reactive saucepan.  Bring to a boil over medium heat; reduce heat and simmer, just until berries burst.  Remove from heat.  Stir in crystallized ginger.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
