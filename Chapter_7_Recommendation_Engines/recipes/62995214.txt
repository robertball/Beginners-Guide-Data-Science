Title:
Stuffed New Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 small new red pototoes, boiled in their skins
1/4 C reduced fat sour cream
1 T butter or margarine, melted
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp dried thyme leaves
1/4 C finely chopped green onions
1/4 C finely shredded ceddar cheese
Paprika (if desired)

Directions:
Cut potatoes in half. Scoop out insides of potatoes leaving 1/8 inch shells. Place the insides of the potatoes in the mixer. Attach the flat beater. Turn to speed 2 and mix for 1 minute. Add sour cream, butter, garlic salt and thyme and mix for 30 seconds. Stop and scrape bowl and continue for 30 more seconds.
Change to stir speed and adding onions until blended.
Spoon or pipe potato mixture into the shells. Place filled shells in a shallow baking dish.
Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 20-25 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
Sprinkle with cheese and paprika. Bake an additional 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
