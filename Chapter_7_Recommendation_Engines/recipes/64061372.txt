Title:
Roast beef sliders for a crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 packages of Hawaiian rolls
2 pounds thin sliced Roast beef
1 pound thin sliced provolone
mayonnaise
mustard
horse radish

Directions:
Slice the entire package of rolls before separating the rolls. Slice all four packs!
Layer the meat on the bottom layer. Approximately 0 .5 pound per package.
Cover the meat with slices of provolone.
Place the roll tops on and cut them into 12 sandwiches each.
Serve with the mayonnaise and mustard.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
These sliders are quick and easy to make and taste great!
