Title:
Mitzi's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*2 cups all-purpose flour1 teaspoon baking soda1/4 teaspoon salt**1/2 cup butter3/4 cup brown sugar2 eggs, beaten2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas1 tsp cinnamonvanilla*1 1/2 c. flour + 1/2 c. whole wheat pastry flour;**1/4 c. butter + 1/4 c. applesauce; 1/2 c. brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda & salt.In a separate bowl, cream together butter & brown sugar. Stir in eggs & mashed bananas until well blended.Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten.Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.***Takes about 70 min if making it healthy
