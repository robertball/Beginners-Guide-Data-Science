Title:
Easy, Tasty Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/325405.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef or ground turkey
1 to 1-1/2 cups chopped onion
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
1 medium green pepper, seeded and chopped
1-15.5oz. can dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1-15.5oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
1-15oz. can whole kernal corn, drained
1-14.5oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
1-15oz. can tomato sauce
1-8oz. can tomato sauce
1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons chili powder
tabasco sauce, to taste, optional
1 teaspoon salt
reduced fat shredded cheddar cheese
light sour cream

Directions:
In a large pot, cook ground turkey and onion over medium-high heat, 8-10 minutes; drain well.
Add garlic and green pepper, and cook 1-2 minutes.
Stir in all beans, corn, diced tomatoes (with liquid), tomato sauce, water, chili powder, tabasco sauce (if using), and salt.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low.
Cover and simmer 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve topped with cheese and sour cream.
