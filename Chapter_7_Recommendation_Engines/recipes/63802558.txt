Title:
Healthy From Home Hungarian Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg sliced portabella mushrooms
1 med onion
2 med size russet potatoes
2 T olive oil
3 T flour
1 clove garlic minced
2 tsp dried parsley
2 T paprika
1 32 oz box no salt vegetable broth
1 cup skim milk; I used a brand that is a copy of 2% w/o the fat and is  thicker than regular skim

Directions:
Heat pan with 2T olive oil on med heat. Dice onion and combine with mushrooms and cook until tender.   Add flour and mix/cook until all flour is blended.  Add garlic, paprika and parsley.  Mix.  Add container of broth and stir until all flour dissolved.  Simmer for 45 minutes on med heat and let cool.  Add milk and reheat on low; stir on occasion.
