Title:
Spicy Chicken and Black Bean Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
1 T chili powder
1 large onion, diced
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 - 2 T chipolte peppers in adobo sauce, minced
2 cans low sodium chicken broth (can add more)
1 14 oz can diced tomatoes with green chilis
3 cans black beans(or can sub 1 can with pinto beans)
3 - 4 cups cooked, shredded chicken

Directions:
Heat oil and add onion. Saute until soft. Add garlic, salt, chili powder and chipolte peppers. Saute and additional two minutes.
Add chicken broth, tomatoes, and beans. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 5 minutes. Add chicken and heat until chickent is hot.
Serve with a dollop of sour cream, sliced avocado and or tortilla strips or chips.

Submitter: 
Debbie
Description: 
This is a very healthy and hearty meal everyone will love. You can spice it up or down which makes it really great. You can top with an unlimited number of toppings. A family favorite.
