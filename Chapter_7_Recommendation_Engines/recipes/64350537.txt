Title:
Marie's Super Simple Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Water
1/2 cup Nonfat Greek Yogurt
1/4 cup Nonfat Cottage Cheese
1/2 Banana, ripe
1 Tbsp Peanut Butter
pinch Cinnamon
1 cup Ice

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in your blender. Serve immediately.
