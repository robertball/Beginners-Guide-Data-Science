Title:
Tomato, Basil Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup Plain Couscous
1 1/4cup Chicken Broth
1Tbs Olive Oil
1/4tsp Salt
1 Clove Minced Garlic
1 Large Diced Plum Tomato
1/4c Chopped Fresh Basil
1/2c Fresh Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
In medium sauce pan bring water, olive oil, salt and garlic to a boil over high heat. Turn off heat and quickly stir in couscous, tomato and basil. Cover and let stand for 10 minutes. Uncover and add cheese. Fluff gently but thoroughly with fork.

Description: 
This recipe makes a delicious, quick and healthy side dish with an Italian and Moroccan flair.
