Title:
Banana Chocolate Chip Cake Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1254693.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe bananas, mashed
2 tablespoons almond butter
1 large egg
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
3  teaspoons cacoa powder, unsweetened
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
1 cup almond flour
1/4 cup tapioca flour
2 tablespoons coconut sugar
1/4 cup chocolate chips/chunks
Melted coconut oil or melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line your cupcake tin with paper.
In a large bowl, mash bananas using a fork the us a hand mixer to combine almond butter, egg, vanilla, cacoa powder,  baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
Add almond flour and tapioca flour to  the bowl and incorporate well.
Stir in chocolate chips/chunks into batter using a spatula.
Using an ice cream scoop to add batter to cupcake tin evenly.
Bake for 20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center.
