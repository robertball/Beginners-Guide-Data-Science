Title:
Italian Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1/2 cup fine cracker crumbs
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
3/4 tsp salt
dash pepper
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 3 oz can chopped mushrooms
1 12 oz carton cottage cheese
1 tbsp parsley

Directions:
1.COMBINE FIRST EGGS, TOMATO PASTE,CRACKER CRUMBS, ONION, PEPPERS, SALT PEPPER AND GROUND BEEF IN LARGE BOWL. MIX WELL.
2.PAT HALF OF THE MIXTURE INTO 8 " SQUARE PAN.
3.COMBINE MUSHROOMS,COTTAGE CHEESE, AND PARSLEY. MIX WELL. SPREAD OVER MEAT MIXTURE. TOP WITH REMAINING MEAT MIXTURE.
4. BAKE AT 350 FOR 50 MINUTES. LET STAND 10 MINUTES BEFORE SERVING.

Submitter: 
Lori C
Description: 
I don't very often make this recipe, but when I do, my family raves. I tend to forget about it and make regular meatloaf, or stuffed peppers. It is a wonderful change. It is a combination of meatloaf and lasagna, without the noodles.
