Title:
Chocolaty Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package Neufchatel cheese, softened
2/3 cup confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup HERSHEYÂ®'S Dutch Processed Cocoa
1/4 cup skim milk
1/4 teaspoon orange extract

Directions:
Beat Neufchatel cheese in small bowl until fluffy.  Add powdered sugar, cocoa, milk and extract; beat on medium speed of mixer until smooth.
Cover; refrigerate several hours or overnight for flavors to blend.  Just before serving, wash and dry fruit; cut into serving size pieces, if necessary. (Brush fruit with lemon juice to prevent browning).  Serve dip well chilled; refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
HersheysKitchens.com
Description: 
Use assorted fresh fruit for dipping (bananas, kiwifruit, papaya, strawberries).
