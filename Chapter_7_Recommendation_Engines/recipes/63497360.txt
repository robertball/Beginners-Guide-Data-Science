Title:
Caramel apple salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954638.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. tub whipped topping
1 box butterscotch pudding
1 20 oz. can crushed pineapple in juice
2 cups mini marshmallows
3 large apples cut into bite-size pieces

Directions:
Mix pineapple with juice and butterscotch pudding together.  Cut up apples and add along with whipped topping and marshmallows.
