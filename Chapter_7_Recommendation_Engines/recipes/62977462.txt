Title:
Cheesy Chicken Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/737507.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Chicken Breasts, cubed (about 1-1/2 pounds)
1-1/2 cups uncooked instant rice
1-3/4 cups milk
1- 10oz package frozen broccoli
3/4 pound processed cheese (ex:Velveeta), cubed
1/2 cup light miracle whip
1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Ground pepper, to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375.
Combine all ingredients except shredded cheese in 12 x 8 baking dish and mix well.
Cook 30-45 minutes or until chicken is cooked through. Top with shredded cheese, place in warm oven and let melt.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a simple casserole that can thrown together in minutes and left to bake.  Turns out so creamy and delicious!
