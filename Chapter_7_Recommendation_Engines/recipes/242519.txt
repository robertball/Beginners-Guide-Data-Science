Title:
Good Morning Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4418234.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
1/2 cucumber, diced
1 avocado, peeled and pitted
2 stalks celery
2 ounces baby spinach leaves
lemon, juiced
2 cups ice
1 apple, cored
1 banana

Directions:
Blend water, romaine lettuce, cucumber, avocado, celery, spinach, and lemon juice together in a blender on high until smooth, about 30 seconds. Add ice, apple, and banana to blender and blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.

Submitter: 
Heather Barbian
Description: 
A green smoothie to start your day.
