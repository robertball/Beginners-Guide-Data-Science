Title:
Creamy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 fully ripe avacados
1/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cup black bean, corn salsa
1 jalapeno diced and seeded
1 tomato chopped
2 teaspoons lime juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel and smash avacados into a bowl
add 1/4 cup of sour cream, 1/2 cup of salsa mix well
add jalapeno, tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper stir all
together.
Serve with chips.

Submitter: 
everydaycook
Description: 
A creamy guacamole great with chips, or served on top of a burger.
