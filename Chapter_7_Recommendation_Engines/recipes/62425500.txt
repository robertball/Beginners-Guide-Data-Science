Title:
Whole Wheat Apple muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. Granulated Sugar
1/4 c. Butter, unsalted
2 small eggs
1 c. Milk, nonfat
2 c. King Arthur white whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. Allspice
1/2 tsp. Nutmeg
1 1/5 c. Apples, fresh, quartered or chopped
Topping:
1/4 c. Brown Sugar
1 tsp. Cinnamon, ground

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar. Add egg and beat well. Stir in milk. In another bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder, and spices. Add butter mixture to flour mixture and blend until just moistened.( Batter will be lumpy.) Add apples and blend carefully. Fill well greased mini muffins tins. Sprinkle with sugar cinnamon blend. Bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
These are low in fat, and really light textured.
