Title:
Fruited Tzimmes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  lb. carrots
2  large sweet potatoes
3/4 cup pitted prunes
1/2 cup dried apricots
1/2 cup raisins
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 teaspoon lemon zest
1/2 teaspoon grated ginger root
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup orange juice (plus water to cover)
2 T honey
dried parsley (optional)

Directions:
Peel and cut sweet potatoes and carrots into 1" chunks.
Combine all ingredients in a large pot.
Add enough water to just barely cover.
Bring to a boil, cook for 10 minutes, then lower heat and simmer for about 1 hour until liquid has reduced and carrots are tender.
Sprinkle with dried parsley for serving.

Submitter: 
JOYCE LITOFF
Description: 
I developed this traditional recipe for a Passover Seder when we were having Vegan guests.
