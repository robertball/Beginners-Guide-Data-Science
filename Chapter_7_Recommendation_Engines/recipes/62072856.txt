Title:
EZ Spiked Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. Captain Morgan spiced rum
12 oz. nonalcoholic eggnog
ground nutmeg to taste

Directions:
Pour rum and eggnog into a blender.  Blend for 15 seconds.  Pour into glasses and sprinkle with ground nutmeg if desired.

Submitter: 
CLOUDSSUNRAIN
Description: 
Spiked eggnog without a lot of hassle.  All you need to do is pick up some Captain Morgan and some eggnog from the dairy case.  This recipe can be easily doubled, tripled, etc. for all the adults celebrating the holidays.
