Title:
Creamy poblano chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 poblano chile peppers
2 cups of chicken stock/broth
salt and pepper to taste
1 chicken breast, cooked and shredded
1 14 oz can of corn
1-1/2 cups of cream, half and half of evaporated milk or any type of milk
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 Tablespoons butter
1 T flour
Cilantro for topping
Sour cream for topping

Directions:
Set your oven at broil.  Roast the peppers until charred.  Put into paper bag and close and let stand for 10-15 minutes.  Remove them and peel the skin off the peppers, rinse under cold water and remove seeds.
Place in blender along with garlic powder, salt, pepper and chicken stock.  Blend until smooth.  Let stand.
Melt butter in a pan and add flour.  Add milk to the mixture.  Combine milk mixture and chile mixture in a large sauce pan.  Add in shredded chicken and corn.  Simmer for 30-45 minutes until cooked through.
Top with cilantro and sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
naedwards
Description: 
This is a very fragrant soup combining poblano chiles, chicken, corn and cream to make this delicious soup, perfect meal for those chilly fall days!  Goes great with warm crusty bread and a salad for the perfect meal!
