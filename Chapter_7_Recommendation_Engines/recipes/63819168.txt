Title:
Roasted Pumpkin Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1061443.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup 1/4" diced pumpkin
1/2 cup 1/4" diced onion
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 T oil
1/2 cup diced tomatoes with juice
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1 T finely diced fresh ginger
1 T grapefruit juice
1 jalepeno pepper, finely diced

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
In a plastic bag, combine pumpkin, onion, cinnamon and oil.  Combine until coated, then spread on cookie sheet.  Bake about 25 minutes, until lightly browned.
In medium bowl, combine tomatoes, cilantro, ginger, grapefrit juice and jalepeno pepper.  Add roasted vegetables.  Chill for an hour to combine flavors.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Roasted pumpkin is the base for this holiday salsa.  Great on crackers or  raw vegetables.  This goes well with pinto bean tacos!
