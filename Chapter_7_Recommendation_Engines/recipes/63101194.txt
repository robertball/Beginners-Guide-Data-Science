Title:
Southern Style Green Beans w/ Bacon and Tomato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 small onion, chopped finely
1 lb. green beans
2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or 28 oz. can diced tomatoes, drained)
salt/pepper

Directions:
Add the onion to the left over bacon drippings and cook till very soft, 7-10 min. Add everything else. Reduce heat and cover. cook until beans are tender and tomatoes are falling apart, about 30 minutes. top w/ the bacon.
