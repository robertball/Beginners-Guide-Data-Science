Title:
Hearty Caesar Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(4-5) chunks of garlic
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 egg
3/4 cup real mayo
1 Tsp. pepper
1 Tsp. seasonong salt
1 head of Romaine Lettuce
1/2 red onion
6 fresh mushrooms
1 lb. bacon
1 box of croutons

Directions:
Mix first seven ingredients in a blender till it is whipped together smooth.  Set aside in refrigerator.
Fry up bacon and crumble when done.
Tear up clean lettuce in a large salad bowl.
Add sliced onion and mushrooms.
Add bacon and croutons.
Toss in the dressing and mix together.
*Can add cooked chicken breast if desired to make it more of a meal in one.

Submitter: 
Sheri Y.
Description: 
This Caesar Salad does not mess around.  It has everything in great amounts to make it an extremely flavourful and sustaining side dish (or meal in itself).
