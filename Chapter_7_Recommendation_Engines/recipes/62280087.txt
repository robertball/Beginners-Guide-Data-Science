Title:
My Mango Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/361953.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ripe avocados, peeled and seeded
4 limes, juiced and separated
2 serrano peppers (or more)
4 tablespoons of cilantro, chopped
2 tablespoons of white onion, finely chopped
1 mango, diced
Sea salt to taste

Directions:
Mix chopped onion and 1/2 of the lime juice in a small bowl. Let the onion soak for no less than 1 hour. When ready, discard the lime juice.
Grind the peppers with the remaining lime juice and salt to taste in a food processor. Add the avocado and mix well until creamy and smooth.
Transfer mixture into a serving bowl and mix in soaked onion, cilantro and mango.
Serve at room temperature with plain tortilla chips. Irresistible!
