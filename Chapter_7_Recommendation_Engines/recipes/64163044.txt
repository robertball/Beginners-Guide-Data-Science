Title:
Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion
1 clove garlic
1 lb. ground beef
1 - 16 oz. can tomato sauce
1 - 4 oz can tomato paste
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. basil

Directions:
Brown meat, onions and garlic.  Add tomato sauces and spices.  (Can be done in same skillet.)  Cook for approximately one hour.
Serve over spaghetti.
