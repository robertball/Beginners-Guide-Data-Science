Title:
cheese filling fat free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages Fat Free cream cheese, room temperature
sugar sub to taste
1 egg yolk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together cream cheese and sugar.  Beat in egg and vanilla.  Refrigerate before using to fill pastries prior to baking them.

Submitter: 
respire8
Description: 
This is the cheese filling I use for several different sweet breads and it tastes like cheesecake.  Everyone loves it.  Can be used to fill kolachkes, braids or even dropped in the center of chocolate cupcakes -- yummy but loaded with calories.Modified from: Cheese Filling
