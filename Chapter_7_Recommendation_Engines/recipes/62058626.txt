Title:
Chocolate Frosted Toffee Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup flour
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup softened butter or margarine
14 oz tin of sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup corn syrup
2 tsp. vanilla
4 cups toasted coconut
Frosting:
1 cup milk chocolate chips
3-4 Tbsp. milk or cream
1/2 cup icing sugar
2 Tbsp. butter
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Stir in coconut and spread in greased 9x13 pan. Bake at 350 for 25-35 minutes until set and slightly brown. Let cool completely. In a small pot, melt the chocolate chips with the milk stirring constantly. Stir in icing sugar, butter and vanilla and mix well. Spread over bars and refrigerate for 30 minutes before cutting.

Submitter:

Description: 
Decadent and delicious.......for coconut lovers only! Watch carefully while toasting the coconut as it can easily scorch!
