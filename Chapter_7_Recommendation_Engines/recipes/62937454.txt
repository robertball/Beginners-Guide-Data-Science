Title:
Winter Spiced Plum Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/731695.jpg
Ingredients:
2lb red plums, halved and pitted
100g/4oz sugar
Finely grated zest and juice of 1 small orange.
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground Nutmeg
225g/8oz wholemeal plain flour
pinch of salt
100g/4oz chilled butter cut into chunks.
single cream or custard to serve

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F
Place the plums in a baking dish and add half the sugar, the orange zest and juice, cinnamon and nutmeg and toss to mix.  bake in the oven for 5 minutes.
meanwhile, make the topping.  Put the flour and salt into a mixing bowl, add the chilled butter and rub it in using the fingertips, until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.  Stir in the remaining sugar.
Scatter evenly over the plums then return to the oven for 25-30 minutes, until golden brown.  Serve hot with cream or custard.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
This traditional crumble recipe comes with a winter twist.  It uses plums spiced with cinnamon, nutmeg and orange.
