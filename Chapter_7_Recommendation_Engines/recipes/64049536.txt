Title:
Caramel Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1125880.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Pie Crusts (Homemade is best)
Pie Filling:
8 Cups Granny Smith Apples, Peeled & Sliced
3/4 Cups Sugar
1/3 Cups Flour
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/4 tsp Salt
2 Tablespoon Butter
Topping:
1/4 Cup Butter
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
2 Tablespoons Milk (or half and half)
1/2 Cup Chopped Nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
Prepare Pie Crusts - Place one in the bottom of the pie pan, save the other for the top of the pie.
Combine seasoning into large bowl: Sugar, Flour Cinnamon, & Salt
Add apples into seasoning and mix
Put seasoned apples in pie pan
Top with slices of butter
Top pie with 2nd pie crust, flute, and slit top
Bake in 350 Degree oven for 55 minutes
Topping: While pie is cooking, in a small/medium sauce pan, add butter until melted. Then add brown sugar and milk.
Bring to boil - let it boil for 10-15 minutes until caramel sauce starts to thicken, stirring the entire time.
When done, pour over the top of the pie and sprinkle with chopped nuts.

A very good apple pie and a favorite for the family.
