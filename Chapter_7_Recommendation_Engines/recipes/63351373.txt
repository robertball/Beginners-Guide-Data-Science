Title:
Red Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken thighs; cut up
2 14.5 oz. cans diced roasted tomatoes with garlic
1 14.5 oz can kidney beans (drained)
2 cups chicken stock
1 jalapeno, chopped and seeded
1 small onion, chopped
2 tbl chili powder
4 tbl vegetable oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
In a heavy or cast iron skillet heat the oil on medium, high heat.
Cut up the chicken into chunks and season with salt and pepper.
Add chicken to the skillet and brown; about 8 minutes per side.
After chicken is fully cooked and no longer pink drain off the oil.
Add all ingredients to the crock pot and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 4 hours.
