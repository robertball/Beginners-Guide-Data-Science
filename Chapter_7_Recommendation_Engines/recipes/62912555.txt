Title:
Double layer no bake strawberry cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/708542.jpg
Ingredients:
One 8 ounce package of softened cream cheese.
One 16 ounce package semi-frozen whipped topping.
1/3 cup granulated sugar.
1/2 cup strawberry preserves.
One 9 inch gram cracker crust.
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.

Directions:
Place softened cream cheese, sugar and vanilla extract into a large mixing bowl. Whip on high speed using an electric hand mixer until mixture is smooth and well mixed.
Add whipped topping and mix on medium speed until mixture is smooth and no lumps remain.
Scoop one half of the mixture into the bottom of the gram cracker crust, spreading it around evenly.
Spread the strawberry preserves on top of the first layer, spreading it around evenly.
Take the rest of the cream cheese mixture and spread around on top of strawberry layer.
Cover pie with plastic wrap and refridgerate for up to one hour to ensure each layer has set properly.
Cut pie into 8 to 12 pieces and serve on chilled plates. Be sure to garnish!

Description: 
This recipe is super simple and easy to make. If you want to impress friends and family, and do not like to bake, this recipe is for you. With a few easy to find ingredients you can create a fancy dessert in no-time! This recipe is also easy for kids to make as well, be sure to keep an eye on your kids while still making the experience fun and exciting!
