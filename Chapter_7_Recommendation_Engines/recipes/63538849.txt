Title:
Amish Poor Man's Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 c milk
1 med onion, chopped fine
1 c crushed soda crackers
1 t salt
1 t pepper
1 14.5 oz can cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Add all ingredients to large bowl and mix well. Once all ingredients are incorporated, shape into a narrow loaf and place on baking sheet. Cover and place in refrigerator at least 8 hours to overnight. Preheat oven to 350. Slice loaf to desired thickness (1" thickness is typical) and sear pieces in hot skillet. Put slices in a roaster pan in layers, spread cream of mushroom soup on top. Bake in oven for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
ashli113
Description: 
First had this dish at an Amish church in rural Ohio. I will never forget the delicious smell of that church kitchen! After a little questioning a kind woman gave me the recipe. Great for a plan-ahead meal.
