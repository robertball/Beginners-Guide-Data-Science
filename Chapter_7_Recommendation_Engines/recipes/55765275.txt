Title:
Almond Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup slivered almonds
2 1/2 tablespoon butter or margarine
2 1/4 cups chicken broth
1 cup rice
1/4 c celery
1/4 c onion
1 t parsley

Directions:
In a saucepan, saute onion, celery and almonds in 1/2 t butter until the onion is tender and the almonds are lightly browned.  Place 2 T butter in sauce pan, add rice cook till brown; add broth, bring to a boil.  Coverand simmer for 20 min. Remove from the heat, add almond mixture. Let stand for 5-8 minutes or until the liquid is absorbed.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
Sharon Adamczyk of Wind Lake, Wisconsin writes, 'With quick-cooking rice, this is a speedy side dish...but the almonds make it special enough to serve to company. It goes well with all kinds of meats.'Modified from: Almond Rice Pilaf
