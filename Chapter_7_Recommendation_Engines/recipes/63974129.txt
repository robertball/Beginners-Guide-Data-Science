Title:
Mrs. Perina's Sugar Cokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. sugar
4 c. flour
1 c. margarine or butter
2 eggs
4 T. cream or milk
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
optional - 1 tsp. lemon flavoring

Directions:
Rub together sugar, flour, and butter until well combined like when making pie crust.
In a separate bowl, beat eggs, milk, baking soda, and vanilla.
Mix egg mixture into flour mixture with a fork.
Wrap in plastic and chill thoroughly.
Roll out dough on cloth or board sprinkled with powdered sugar.
Cut out and bake at 350 until edges turn brown.
Optional Icing:
Beat 1 egg white and salt. Add powdered sugar to desired consistency.
