Title:
Black Forest Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-1 oz.squares semi-sweet chocolate
or 3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
3/4 c. butter
1 1/2 c. sugar
3 eggs beaten
1 tsp. vanilla or cherry extract
1 c. flour packed
1/2 c. pecans or walnuts
1/2 c. dried cherries or cranberries
1/2 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
9x13 pan with foil larger than pan, spray with Pam.
Melt chocolate in bowl in microwave oven with butter.
Stir sugar into mixture. When bowl cools add beaten eggs
stirring thoroughly. Mix in vanilla.
Mix in flour until just moistened. Add pecans and dried fruit that have been chopped in processor.(if fruit sticks add 1 Tablespoon of flour to processor)Add chocolate chips stir and spread in pan.
