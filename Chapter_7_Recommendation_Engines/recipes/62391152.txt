Title:
Nutty Crust Strawberry Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 C. Butter
2 C. Flour
1/2 C Brown Sugar
1 C. Chopped Pecans
Filling
1-8 oz. Pkg Cream Cheese
2 C. Cream Whipped
1 C Powdered Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
Topping
1 container strawberries sliced
1 container strawberry glaze

Directions:
Crust:
Cut butter into flour, sugar and nuts just as you do for a pie crust. As soon as mixture holds together, lay it out in the bottom of a cake pan, and bake @ 350 deg. for 18 minutes. Take out and let cool. When cool crumble with a fork. Divide it in half, use the first half for the bottom and save the second half for the top.
Filling:
Combine Cream cheese, and powdered sugar and beat until smooth. Whip cream and add vanilla, then blend together with cream cheese mixture. until fluffy. Put on top of crust.
Topping:
Mix strawberries and glaze together and spread on top of filling.
