Title:
Grilled Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs tilapia fillets
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 teaspoon tsps cajun seasoning
1 teaspoon tsps Old Bay Seasoning
For the Aioli:
1 cup low fat mayonnaise
6 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
3/4 teaspoon dried tarragon

Directions:
Preheat grill, spread oil on both sides of filets.  Sprinkle with seasoning.  Place on hot grill and cook for about 4 to 5 minutes over a medium high heat. Turn once during grilling.  Serve with Aioli.
To prepare aioli, combine all ingredients in a bowl, mix and refrigerate until you are ready for use.
Good served it with boiled new potatoes and green beans to dip in the Aioli.
