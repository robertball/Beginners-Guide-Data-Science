Title:
Beef and Rice Skillet Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Ground Beef
1 Medium Onion, Chopped
1 10 oz. Can tomatoes with green chilies
2 C. Brown Rice
4 C. Water or Beef Broth
1 can (15 ounces) Pinto Beans, drained
1 â€“ 2 t. Cumin
1 T. Onion Powder
1 T Worcestershire Sauce
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
1 Cup shredded cheese
1/2 C. Sour Cream (optional)

Directions:
In a large pot, fry Ground Beef and onion until no longer pink.
Drain and return to pot.
Stir in tomatoes with green chilies, rice, water or broth, beans, cumin, onion powder, Worcestershire Sauce, salt, and pepper.
Simmer on stove top for 35 - 40 minutes until rice is tender.
Add cheese and sour cream (if desired) and heat until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Heather Santos
Description: 
This recipe is an easy for weeknights. Pair it with cornbread to complete the meal.
