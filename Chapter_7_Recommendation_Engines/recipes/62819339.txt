Title:
Tortellini Salad with Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665228.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 oz.) pkg.  fresh spinach tortellini
1 (9 oz.) pkg.  three-cheese tortellini
3 to 4          green onions, chopped
2 cloves        garlic, finely chopped
1/4 cup         olive oil
1 1/2 to 2 lbs. peeled/deveined shrimp
1/4 tsp.        garlic powder
4 to 6          fresh mushrooms, sliced
10 to 12 stalks fresh asparagus, cut into bite-sized
pieces, blanched until tender (about
3 minutes)
1 (3.8) oz. can sliced black olives
10 to 12        cherry tomatoes, cut in half
1/2 cup         vinaigrette dressing (recipe follows)
1/2 cup         feta cheese (optional)
salt & pepper   to taste
.
.
Vinaigrette - Whisk together:
1/2 cup         extra-virgin olive oil
1 Tbs.          red wine vinegar
1 Tbs.          fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp.        dried tarragon
1/4 tsp.        dried basil
dash            sugar

Directions:
1.    Cook tortellini according to package directions. (They can be cooked together in the same pot.) Set aside to cool.
2.    Saute' green onions and garlic in olive oil for 1-2 minutes.  Add shrimp.  Sprinkle with garlic powder and saute' until shrimp are cooked.
3.    Add sliced mushrooms and saute' another 2-3 minutes.  Pour shrimp mixture over cooked tortellini.
4.    Add blanched asparagus, black olives and cherry tomatoes.  Pour 1/2 cup vinaigrette over the tortellini mixture. Add feta cheese, if desired, and salt and pepper to taste.
Enjoy immediately or refrigerate until later.

Submitter: 
Cookie Lover
Description: 
A wonderful meal with cheese and spinach tortellini and shrimp with a quick and simple vinaigrette.
