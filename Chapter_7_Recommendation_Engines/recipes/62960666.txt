Title:
Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/730351.jpg
Ingredients:
For the Alfredo sauce
1/4 cup water or chicken broth
3 T. flour
1 tsp. butter
1/2 cup cream cheese
1/4 tsp. or to taste black pepper
1/4 tsp. nutmeg-optional
Salt-optional or to taste
Generous amount of Parmesan cheese
Fettuccine
8 oz. of fettuccine
Water- according to the package
2 T frozen sweet green peas-optional

Directions:
Boil the fettuccine according to the instructions on the package.
When par-boiled add the sweet peas
In a bowl, mix the flour with water or chicken broth. Use a wire whisk to break the lumps.
In another saucepan, melt the butter.
As soon as it melts, add the flour mixture and keep stirring on LOW heat.
Add the cream cheese, using the wire whisk, mix the cream cheese in with the mixture.
Add the Parmesan cheese, nutmeg,pepper, and salt.
Keep stirring to avoid scum on top of the sauce.
Drain the pasta.
Add the sauce to the pasta. Keep the stove on med-high heat and if too much sauce-keep stirring and let the sauce thickens up.
Serve hot with Parmesan cheese on top.
