Title:
Easy Apple Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
¼ teaspoon salt
¾ teaspoon cinnamon
1/3 cup milk
1 egg, well beaten
1 can (20-21 ounces) apple pie filling
vegetable cooking oil or peanut oil for frying
Cinnamon Sugar- Combine ½ cup sugar and 1 ½ teaspoons cinnamon.

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.  Mix egg, milk and apple pie filling (cut up large pieces of apple in the filling) together well.  Combine wet and dry ingredients with a wooden spoon, mixing well.  Heat cooking oil to 375 degrees.  Fry by dropping rounded tablespoonfuls into oil.  Cook, turning once or twice until golden brown all over and cooked through to the middle.  Drain well and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Stephany Brown
Description: 
Easy apple fritters made the shortcut way with canned apple pie filling and common ingredients you already have in your pantry.
