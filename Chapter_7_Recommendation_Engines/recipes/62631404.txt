Title:
Smoky Corn Chowder with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Slices of Bacon, chopped
1 leek finely chopped (white part only)
1 russet potato, finely chopped
2 cups of frozen or canned corn kernels
1 1/2 cup chicken broth
1 1/2 cup milk
398 ml can of creamed corn
1 sprig of thyme or 1/2 tsp of dried thyme
1 bay leaf

Directions:
HEAT a large pot over medium. ADD bacon and cook just until crisp (approx 4 mins.) Discard all but 1 Tbsp of bacon fat. Add leek and saute until tender (about 1 min) Then add potato, corn, broth, milk, creamed corn, thyme and bay leaf. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are tender (20 mins). Remove thyme sprig and bay leaf before serving. Season with fresh pepper. Soup will keep well in refrigerator up to 2 days or in a freezer for up to 1 month.
