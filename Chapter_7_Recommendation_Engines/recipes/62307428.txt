Title:
Frijoles con Chorizo (Re-fry Beans with Chorizo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/369145.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 Cups Re-fried Beans
1/2 Lb Mild Mexican Chorizo
4 T. Cojita Cheese
4 T. Sour Cream
tortillas

Directions:
Fry chorizo in pan until crispy texture.  Add beans and mix together.  Cook until beans are warm about 2-3 minutes.  Top with cojita cheese and sour cream. Serve with tortillas

This is a quick and easy mexican breakfast.
