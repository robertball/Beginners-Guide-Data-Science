Title:
Enchillada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp oil
1/2 small onion, chopped
1 1/2 T chili powder
1/2 T cumin
1 tsp sugar
8 oz tomato sauce
1/4 cup water
pepper to taste

Directions:
Sautee onion in oil over med/low heat until soft and brown (apx 4 to 5 minutes)
Add spices to pan to release oils and lightly toast (apx 30 sec to a minute)
Add water and tomato sauce and simmer 5 minutes or so or until slightly thickened.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Insanely quick and simple, yet flavorful enchilada sauce.  Should make enough to cover 12 corn shell enchiladas .
