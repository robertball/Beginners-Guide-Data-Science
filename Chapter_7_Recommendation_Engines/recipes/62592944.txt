Title:
Roasted Parmesan Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871602.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound asparagus
1 tsp olive oil
1/4 tsp ground pepper
2 Tbsp grated parmesan cheese
1 lemon

Directions:
Steam asparagus. Place oven rack in the top third of the oven and preheat to 475. Place the asparagus on a rimmed baking sheet. Drizzle on the oil. Roll the asparagus in the pan until they glisten with oil. Dust on the pepper and distribute the cheese over the spears. Bake for 5 minutes. Don't turn the asparagus or shake the pan. Serve with lemon wedges.
