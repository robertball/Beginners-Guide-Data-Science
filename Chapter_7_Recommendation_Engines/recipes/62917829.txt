Title:
BAKER'S Simply Sensational Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Â½ pkg. (20 squares) BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate, divided
1 pkg. (8 oz) PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
Decorations: chopped peanuts, multi-colored sprinkles

Directions:
Melt 8 chocolate squares.  Beat cream cheese with mixer until creamy.  Blend in melted chocolate.  Refrigerate until firm.
Shape into 36 balls.  Place on wax paper-covered baking sheet.
Melt remaining chocolate.  Use fork to dip truffles; return to baking sheet.  Decorate then refrigerate 1 hour.
