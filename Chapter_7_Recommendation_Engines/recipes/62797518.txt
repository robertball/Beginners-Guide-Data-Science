Title:
Taco Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/654722.jpg
Ingredients:
1 prepared baked pie crust
1 lb lean ground beef
1 small onion, diced
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 small jar salsa
1 can refried beans
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 tomato, seeded and diced
1/4 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
additional shredded cheese (1/2 cup)
sour cream (optional)
chopped olives (opional)
guacamole (optional)
hot sauce (optional)

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onions in skillet. Drain any oil. Add taco seasoning according to envelope directions. Add salsa to mix and cook over medium heat until liquid is reduced, stirring occasionally. Stir in refried beans until heated. Fill prepared pie crust with beef mixture. Top with shredded cheddar cheese. Place in preheated oven at 350 degrees until cheese is melted, approximately 10-15 minutes. Remove from oven and top with lettuce and tomato and additional cheese. Cut into 6 wedges and serve with choice of optional toppings.
