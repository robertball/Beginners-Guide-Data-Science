Title:
Homemade Italian Cream Soda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
10 ounces club soda
4 ounces heavy cream
4 fluid ounces raspberry syrup
2 cups ice cubes, or as needed

Directions:
Stir together club soda, heavy cream, and raspberry syrup in a pitcher. Fill 2 tall glasses with ice and pour cream mixture on top.

Submitter: 
armywife571
Description: 
Homemade Italian cream soda, just like you get at the Italian restaurants! You can use any flavored drink syrup but I personally prefer raspberry.
