Title:
Shredded Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 1/2 c Italian dressing
1T minced garlic
2 t cumin

Directions:
Place chicken in  slow cooker. Pour dressing over top and sprinkle with cumin and garlic. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours or on high for 3-4.
Remove chicken from pot, shred with a couple of forks, then put it back in the pot. Cover and cook an additional 30 minutes.
