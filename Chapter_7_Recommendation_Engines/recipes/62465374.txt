Title:
Kim's Chocolate Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1 c buttermilk
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 t. vanilla
2 eggs + 2 egg whites
2 T hershey chocolate syrup
1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and sugar a 10 inch Bundt pan.
In a large bowl, stir together mix all ingredients excepts chocolate chips.  Beat on low speed until blended. Scrape bowl, and beat 2 minutes on medium speed. Stir in chocolate chips.
Pour batter into prepared 10 inch Bundt pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-50, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
Serve plain, or top with powdered sugar or chocolate or peanut butter glaze.
