Title:
strawberry banana fruit pop Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/440696.jpg
Ingredients:
*1 banana
*1/4 cup milk
*3 tbsp. vanilla yogurt
*6 cut strawberries

Directions:
1.put all ingredients in a blender, blend well so smooth
2.pour mixture into small cups, cover cups individually in foil and stick popsicle sticks in each one
3.freeze over night
