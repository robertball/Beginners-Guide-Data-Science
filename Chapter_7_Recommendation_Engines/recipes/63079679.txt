Title:
Grilled Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777837.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T sugar
1/2 cup water, warm
1 1/4 t yeast
1 1/2 T milk
1/2 t salt
2 1/2 to 3 cups all purpose flour

Directions:
Combine sugar, warm water, and yeast and set aside to proof for 5 minutes or so.
When frothy, add milk and salt
Stir in roughly half the flour, mixing until a creamy texture is reach.
Begin working in the remaining flour (you may do my hand or use KA).
Remove and knead briefly by hand until a smooth consistency is acheived (3 minutes or so)
Set aside to rest for 10 minutes
In the meantime, head griddle to highest setting
Divide the dough into 4 balls (or 3 if you like large flatbread)
Flatten slightly, and then roll out to 1/4" thickness
Brush top with melted butter and then lay on griddle, butter side down.  Let cook 2 to 3 minutes or until golden brown.  Brush the other side with butter and repeat.
