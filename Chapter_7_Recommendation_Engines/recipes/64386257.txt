Title:
Mint Citrus Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2214966.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup citrus fruits (any type)
1/2 cup mint leaves
2 quarts water
1/2-1/4 cup cucumber (optional)

Directions:
Pour 2 quarts water into a pitcher, mix 1 1/2 cup citrus fruits, 1/2 cup mint leaves, and optional 1/2-1/4 cup cucumber together in a separate bowl and mix. Next, pour the fruit, mint leaves, and cucumber mixture into the water. Let sit for two to three days, stir the water up every day. When finished, pour into serving cups and enjoy!

Submitter: 
chloe 21
Description: 
This is a very healthy and tasty water recipe good for hot summer days and when you have guests over.
