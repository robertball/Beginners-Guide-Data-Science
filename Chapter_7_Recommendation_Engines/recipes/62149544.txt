Title:
Burbon Little Smokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C catsup
1/4 c burbon
1/4 c water
1 pkg smokies (I prefer all beef)
3/4 c brown sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together with a whisk and heat.

Submitter: 
Melissa Arenson
Description: 
My grandmother makes smokies this way, and I have to say I have never seen a similar recipe. It's a nice twist on a recipe that has only a few variations.
