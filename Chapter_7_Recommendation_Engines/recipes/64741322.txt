Title:
St Patty’s Day Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6416563.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 (18.3- 19.8 Oz.) package brownie mix
•	(2) 5.1 Oz. packages instant vanilla pudding / (+ a few drops green food coloring
•	1 8 oz container Whipped Topping
•	6 oz Creme de Menthe candies (Such as Andes) broken into pieces
•	½ c white sugar (+ a few drops green food coloring)

Directions:
1.	Prepare brownie mix as directed on package and let cool.
2.	Make pudding according to directions and mix in candies.
3.	Assemble the trifle: In a trifle bowl, or large glass bowl, crumble half of the brownies, top with half of the pudding, and half of the whipped topping.  Repeat layers.
4.	Place a large shamrock template in center of bowl and sprinkle with sugar to cover top.
5.	Cover and Chill for several hours before serving.
