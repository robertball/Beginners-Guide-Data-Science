Title:
Eggplant & Shitaki over spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant = 12 sliced rounds
1 can of shitaki mushrooms
2 cloves of garlic
5-6 TB of black olives
1/2 cup of oil
1 cup of bread crumbs
1 can of mushroom soup
1 package of sliced Hovarti cheese
1 package of spaghetti noodles

Directions:
Boil your water and add your noodles. Cook until El dante.
Wash and slice eggplant (peel if that's you preference) so you have 12 rounds. Dip in oil and dredge through the bread crumbs place on a sheet of tin foil and place it in the oven for 350F for 20-30 minutes.
Take your can of shitakis and put them in a food processor along with 2 medium cloves of garlic and roughly 6 tablespoons of black olives. Mince them to a chukny puree but not quite a paste. When the eggplant is almost done spread this mixture on top of the eggplant slices. Come back in 5-7 minutes and add slices of Hovarti cheese. Close the oven. Take your can of mushroom soup and put in a sauce pan with 1/2 a can of water a tablespoon of butter and a slice of Hovarti cheese. Cook until creamy. When the cheese as melted to your liking, remove from the oven and serve with pasta and mushroom sauce.
