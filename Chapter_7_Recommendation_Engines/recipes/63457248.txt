Title:
Peppermint Mocha Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Parts Espresso Vodka
2 Parts Kahlua Peppermint Mocha
2 Parts 2% Milk
1 Part Espresso
Crushed peppermints and Fudge Mint cookies to rim the glass (Optional)

Directions:
Moisten the rim.  Turn the glass upside down and dip it in the peppermints and cookies.
Pour all ingredients into a shaker, fill the rest with crushed ice.
Shake, pour, and enjoy!
