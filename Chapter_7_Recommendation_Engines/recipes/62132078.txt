Title:
Country Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs
1 1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 t. salt
4 t. vanilla
1 qt. 1/2 and 1/2
5 cups cold milk

Directions:
Beat the eggs till lemony (about 5 minutes)
Add sugar and beat 10 minutes until thick
Add vanilla and salt and quart of 1/2 and 1/2 and mix well.
Pour into freezer container and add the 5 cups of milk. This requires an electric ice cream freezer or hand cranked.

Submitter: 
JAN
Description: 
A family and friends favorite.  Given to me 40 years ago by my sister-in-law.  You'll never 'cook' ice cream again!
I have made this with non-fat 1/2and 1/2 and can't tell any difference but hubby says he can~
