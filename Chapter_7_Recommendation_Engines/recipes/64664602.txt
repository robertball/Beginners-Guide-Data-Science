Title:
Genevieve's Asparagus, Corn  and Penne Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz penne pasta
16 oz asparagus, cut into 1" pieces
16 oz frozen, white corn
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 jar capers
1 lemon, juiced
1/4 C. EVOO
4 T. red wine vinegar
4 T. dijon mustard
1 t. sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta in salted water until al dente.
About 2 minutes before pasta is finished cooking, add the asparagus to water to blanch.
Add corn and a small amount of water and cover. Cook in microwave for 3-4 minutes to warm.
Drain pot and cool pasta and asparagus.
In a blender, combine 1 cup corn, lemon juice, vinegar, mustard, and sugar.
Blend until smooth.
Slowly blend in the oil.
Toss together pasta, remaining corn, asparagus, green onions, capers and dressing.
Ass salt and pepper to taste.
Best if made the day before.
Let sit out at least an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This has such an unusual flavor,  light and tangy. It is an elegant salad that can be served for any occasion.  You can add chicken to complete the meal.
