Title:
Peanut Butter snack mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup honey
2-3 cups wheat or bran flakes
1/2 cup Grape Nuts cereal
1/3 cup ground flaxseed
1/3 cup wheat germ

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Place peanut butter and honey in a bowl and microwave for 30 seconds to melt.  Stir together.
3. Add remaining ingredients and stir to coat with peanut butter mixture.
4. Spread into a single layer on a baking sheet and bake on oven for 10-12 minutes.
5. Allow to cool, or eat warm right out of the oven.

Submitter: 
THERESA_M
Description: 
This is an updated version of our favorite snack as kids.  Melted peanut butter and honey are mixed with cereal, and healthy ingredients like flaxseed and wheat germ give it a nutritional boost.  Any non-sugared cereal works with this mix!
