Title:
My southern cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup cornmeal mix
3/4 cup self-rising flour
1 egg
1 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In a 10 inch iron skillet, coat with shortening or oil bottom and sides.  Mix all ingredients together to desired consistency.(The thinner, but not too thin, the lighter the cornbread will be). Add to greased skillet, and bake til golden brown.  Great served with stewed tomatoes.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I hate to say this, but most southerners do not like sweet cornbread.  This is my granmother's recipe.
