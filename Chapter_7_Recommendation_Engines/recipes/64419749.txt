Title:
Cilantro Citrus grilled chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c fresh cilantro/coriander - bail with also do
juice of 1 orange, 1 lime and 1 lemon
1/4 c white wine
2T extra virgin olive oil
2 boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
in a large bowl combine marinade ingredients and add the chicken
marinate for at least two hours
allow the chicken to come to room temperature, gently flatten the chicken breasts so that they are of even thickness - this allows for better cooking - you won't get the - one half too dried out and the other side not quite done - effect that happens the breast is thicker at one end than the other
grill on medium

this delicious dish is made with fresh herbs and white wine
