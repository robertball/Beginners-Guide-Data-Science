Title:
Raw Chia 'Porridge' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chia seeds (not meal)
1 cup almond milk
1 banana
2 dates, pitted
1/4 tsp cinnamon
fresh fruit or berries to top
salt to taste

Directions:
1. Place the chia seeds in a bowl.
2. Blend banana and dates in a blender with the milk, cinnamon, and salt.
3. Pour this mixture over the chia seeds and stir well.
4. Let it soak for 15 minutes or more, until thickened. (softer if soaked overnight)
5. Stir before serving, and top with fresh fruit.
