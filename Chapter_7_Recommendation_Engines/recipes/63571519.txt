Title:
Simple Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Butter-Melted, 1 1/2 cups milk, 4 eggs, 1 Cup Flour, 1 Tbsp sugar.

Directions:
Melt Butter.
Mix butter, milk, eggs, flour, and sugar in blender. Mix until well blended and little bubbles start to form.
Let batter rest for about 30 mins.
Preheat pan, add just a little butter to pan, pour 1/3 cup batter in to pan.
Once edges start to brown and center is set flip crepe. cook another min or two, use immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 days.
