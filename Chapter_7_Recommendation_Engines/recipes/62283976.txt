Title:
Mango-Avocado Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353245.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados, chopped
1 mango, peeled, seeded, and chopped (about 1 cup)
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped jicama
2 tlbs chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, tossing to coat. Cover and chill.

Great for fish tacos, fish or just w/ tortilla chips
