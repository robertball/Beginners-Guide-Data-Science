Title:
Green Beans and Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. fresh Green beans, Rinsed, trimmed and snapped
Boiling salted water
2 Tbsp. Butter
1/2 tsp. Dried Basil leaves
1 Clove minced or pressed garlic
2 Small tomatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
Dash of lemon pepper
Salt to taste

Directions:
After beans have been prepared as stated above, Drop them into rapidly boiling water in a large sauce pan or dutch oven
When water resumes boiling, begin timing and boil, uncovered 6-7 minutes or just until tender
Remove from heat and drain beans
Heat butter in a medium fry pan over lo-med. heat, stir in basil, lemon pepper, and garlic.
Add tomatoes, turning lighlty to heat through
Coat tomatoes with butter mixture and wait until they release a little of the tomato juices
Mix beans in tomato mixture and salt to taste if desired
Serve Immediately
