Title:
Cajun Tuna Croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C diced onion
2 Tbs butter
1/2 c and 1 Tbs Flour
1/2 tsp cajun seasoning
1/2 c milk
1 c and 1/4 c plain dry bread crumbs
1 egg
1/4 c flour
1/4 c canola oil
1 6 oz can light tuna, drained

Directions:
Sautee onion in butter until soft and translucent
Add cajun seasoning and 1 Tbs flour, stirring as you add
Add milk and stir well, cooking until thick
Put in a bowl and stir in tuna and the 1/4 c bread crumbs
Chill about 1/2 hour
Take 3 shallow dishes and put in them the following:
1 - The remaining 1/2 c flour
2 - 1 egg and 1 Tbs water, beaten together
3 - The remaining 1 c bread crumbs
Form chilled tuna mixture into small patties (about 2 Tbs each)
Coat each side in flour, then egg mixture, then the bread crumbs, and place on waxed paper lined tray
Pan fry a few at a time in the oil over medium heat, turning once, until the breading is crispy and golden, about 1 - 2 minutes each side.
Serve with greens and mashed potatoes

Submitter: 
KY Piano Teacher
Description: 
These spicy pan-fried tuna croquettes are perfect with collard greens and sweet cornbread!
