Title:
Marscapone Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bulb garlic
8 medium russet potatoes
1/4 cup half and half
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, finely minced
8 ounces Marscapone cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
1/2 teaspoon pepper (or to taste)

Directions:
To roast garlic -preheat oven to 400 degrees F, then place garlic cloves in a small baking dish. Drizzle with olive oil, cover, and bake 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
Bring a large pot of water to boil. Add potatoes, and cook until tender but firm - approx. 20 min. Drain, and transfer to a oven proof mixing bowl.
When garlic is roasted, squeeze from bulb & mash with a fork.
Add roasted garlic, rosemary, half and half, mascapone cheese, and butter into the bowl with the potatoes. Season with salt and pepper. Beat to desired consistency with an electric mixer.
Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees.
Cover mashed potatoes and place in oven to "twice bake" for 25 minutes.
