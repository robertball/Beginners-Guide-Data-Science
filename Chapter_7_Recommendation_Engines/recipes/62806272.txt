Title:
E-Mie Medan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658820.jpg
Ingredients:
1.  2-1/2 lbs Shrimp with heads
2. Lot of shrimp shell collect from previous shrimp dishes.
3. Laos- Galanga-4
4. Sereh- lemon Grass- 5 pieces
5 Kayu Manis - Cinamon Stick -3
6 Cengkeh - Whole clove - about 7
7 Daun Salam - Bayleave
8 Pala halus- Gound Nutmeg
9 Brown Sugar - 2 small brown sugar stick from Indonesia.
10 2 Md tomatoes
11 Bawang Merah- 1 big Shallot
12 Bawang Putih- Garlic 1-2 Tbsp
13. Hebi halus- Dry Ground Shrimp-7 Tbsp
Bahan Pelengkap:
1. Kentang Rebus
2. Telor Rebus
3 Toge Rebus
4 Kerupul or emping.
5 Lime
6 Md Sz Noodle Basah.

Directions:
1. Cook shrimp shell in slow cooker with ingredients from #3 to 10 overnight or 4 hrs at high temperatur.
2. Stir fry Shallot, garlic and dry ground shrimp, add to #1.
3. Add ground nutmeg,sweet soy souce, salt, chili paste.
4. Boil md siz noodle

Transitional Medan Noodle- Kids favorite, called it Bakmie with brown soup
