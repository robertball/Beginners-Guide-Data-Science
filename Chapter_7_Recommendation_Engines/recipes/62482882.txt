Title:
Stuffed Peppers Auntie Fur Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 green peppers
1 lb hamburger
1 onion, minced
3 tablespoons cooked rice
3 eggs
2 cans tomato soup
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut tops off peppers; remove seeds and membrane.  Cover with salted water; bring to a boil and drand and cool.  Mix together hamburger, onion, rice, eggs with salt and pepper to taste, stuff peppers with mixture and arrange in casserole.  Pour tomato soup over peppers, bake at 350 for 45 min.
