Title:
Carrot Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972674.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds of baby carrots
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp vanilla extract
1 Tbsp honey
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup coconut flour
1 Tbsp nutmeg
1/2 Tbsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
4 eggs, at room temperature

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Boil carrots until very soft (about 45 minutes)
Prepare a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish by coating with cooking spray or coconut oil
Drain carrots and transfer to a food processor
Add all of the ingredients except the eggs.
Process until smooth
Add the eggs one at a time, stopping to process after each addition
Spoon mixture into the dish allowing room to rise
Bake for 45-60 minutes or until the top is golden brown
***Optional: sprinkle cinnamon on top before serving.

Submitter: 
Heather Green
Description: 
This recipe follows the Paleo diet. If you aren't a carrot person, you can easily substitute with sweet potatoes. Enjoy!
