Title:
Asian Pepper Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922440.jpg
Ingredients:
One red pepper
One green pepper
One yellow pepper
One orange pepper
One can of corn

Directions:
Remove pepper seeds and stems.
Cut peppers into thin slices.
Place in bowl.
Add corn.
Toss.
Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Ross
Description: 
Fresh, nutricious and delicious! One of my Asian co-workers taught me how to make this easy culinary work of art.
