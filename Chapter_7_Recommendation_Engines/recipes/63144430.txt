Title:
Mom's Apple Juice Braised Corned Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/808889.jpg
Ingredients:
2 3lb corned beef briskets with spice packets
64 ounces apple juice( or enough to cover the meat)
1 large onion, quartered
4 carrots, quartered
4 potatoes, quartered
1 small head cabbage, halved

Directions:
Rinse the corned beef and place them in a slow cooker. Add the two spice packets, carrots, onion, potatoes and apple juice and cook for 8 hours on high. Add cabbage and cook for one additional hour. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
My mom makes the best corned beef. Corned beef brisket slow cooked in apple juice, spices and veggies. This is an easy tasty recipe to serve any day of the year.
