Title:
Ulitimate Baked Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1002130.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken Tenders (2 lbs.)
2 cups Bisquick
1 cup Parmesan Cheese
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp paprika (I use Hot Paprika)
salt to taste
2 large eggs
1/2 Milk

Directions:
Mix the dry ingredients together.
Whisk egg and milk together.
Dunk chicken tenders in egg mixture and then into the Bisquick mixture
Place onto a cookie sheet that has been sprayed with Pam
Bake at 450 until crispy!

Submitter: 
Angela Naumann
Description: 
This is a quick and yummy way to enjoy chicken tenders at home without the messy clean-up of frying.  The chicken is tasty and juicy.  Add your favorite dipping sauce and go to town.
