Title:
Chicken Fajita Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981631.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (13.8 ounce) can Pillsbury速 refrigerated classic pizza crust
1 tablespoon CRISCO速 100% Extra Virgin Olive or Pure Vegetable Oil
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves, cut into thin strips
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 cup thinly sliced onions
1 cup green or red bell pepper strips (2x1/4-inch)
1/2 cup Old El Paso速 Thick 'n Chunky Salsa
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Spray 12-inch pizza pan or 13x9-inch pan with CRISCO(R) Original No-Stick Cooking Spray. Unroll dough; place in pan. Starting at center, press out dough with hands to edge of pan.
In 10-inch skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat until hot. Add chicken; sprinkle with chili powder, salt and garlic powder. Cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Add onions and bell pepper strips; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes longer or until chicken is no longer pink and vegetables are crisp-tender.
Spoon chicken mixture evenly over partially baked crust. Spoon salsa over chicken. Sprinkle with cheese.
Bake classic crust 16 to 20 minutes, thin crust 13 to 18 minutes, or until crust is golden brown. Cut into 8 servings.
