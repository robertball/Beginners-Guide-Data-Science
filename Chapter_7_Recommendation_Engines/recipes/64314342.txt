Title:
Slow Cooker Scalloped Potatoes and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp unsalted butter
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
2.5 pounds Idaho potatoes, peeled and sliced thick
6 ounces thinly sliced black forest ham
4 ounces gruyere or swiss cheese, grated
1 c chicken stock
2 dried basil leaves
1/2 to 1 c heavy cream (depending on how soupy you want it)
1/4 c Parmesan, Asiago, and Romano cheese

Directions:
Cut up butter and place in bottom of slow cooker.
In a small bowl, mix salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
Layer half of the potatoes in slow cooker, seasoning with salt mixture as you layer. Arrange ham slices over potatoes. Sprinkle half of the gruyere. Repeat with remaining potatoes.
Pour chicken stock over layers. Tuck basil leaves on each side, down in the chicken stock.
Cook on high for 4 hours or until potatoes are tender.
Drizzle with heavy cream and cook for an additional 30 minutes.
Top with remaining Gruyere and parmesan cheese mix.
Turn off slow cooker and let potatoes set for an hour before serving.
