Title:
Chile Relleno Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans whole green chile peppers, drained and de-seeded (diced, optional)
8 ounces Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
8 ounces Longhorn or Cheddar cheese, shredded
4 eggs, beaten
1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 cup milk
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray. Pour can of chili evenly over bottom of pan.
Layer chilies and cheese, reserving about 1/4 of the cheese to sprinkle on top during baking.
In a bowl, mix together the eggs, milk, and flour, and pour over the top of the chilies.
Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
Remove from oven, remove foil and top evenly with tomato sauce and remaining cheese, then continue baking, uncovered another 15 minutes.
Remove from oven and let stand about 5 minutes, then serve.

Submitter: 
Nathan Akers
Description: 
This is just your basic *A*W*E*S*O*M*E* Chile Relleno Casserole recipe. It's a bit of a variation on the authentic relleno theme. Chile Relleno Casserole is great as a dinner entree, a potluck side dish, or for breakfast.
