Title:
Tollhouse Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ cups flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup shortening
¾ cup sugar
¾ cup brown sugar
½ tsp. water
2 eggs
2 cups quick oats
1 cup chocolate chips
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 F.
Mix dry ingredients and set aside.
Blend shortening, sugar, vanilla and water.
Beat in eggs and add flour mixture. Mix well.
Stir in oats and chocolate chips. Drop onto greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes.
