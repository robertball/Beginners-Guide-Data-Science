Title:
Best Chocolate Cake - TX sheet cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Melt in saucepan:
2 sticks of butter
3 Tbsp cocoa
1 c. water
Sift together:
2 c. sugar
2 c. flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
Then add:
2 eggs
1/2 c buttermilk
1 tsp vanilla
Frosting:
1 stick butter
2 Tbls cocoa
6 Tbls buttermilk
1 lb powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Melt in saucepan the butter, cocoa and cup of water. Bring to boil, remove from heat and let stand 1 minute.
Sift together the sugar, flour, baking soda and cinnamon.
Add to the liquid mixture, mix well by hand.
Add the eggs, buttermilk and vanilla.
Bake in a sheet pan for 20-25 min @ 380.
For the frosting, bring the last stick of butter, cocoa and buttermilk to a boil.
Add the powdered sugar and vanilla, whisk until smooth. Pour on top of cake and spread quickly, it dries fast.
