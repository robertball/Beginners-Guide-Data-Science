Title:
Seared tuna steak with salty and sweet roasted green beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4469092.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tuna steak, 3 oz.
1 teaspoon Red pepers
Green beans, 6 oz.
3 teaspoons pepper corn
1 teaspoon garlic salt
Teriyaki sauce, 4 oz.
Maple syrup, 4 oz
2 teaspoons of olive oil

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375. Mix teriyaki sauce and maple syrup in separate bowl. Pour mix over green beans. Place green beans in pan and put in oven for 25 mins, or until green beans turn dark green. Per heat olive oil in a frying pan. On a plate, mix pepper corn, red peppers, and garlic salt. Per heat olive oil in a frying pan. To get the seasoning to stick on the tuna, rub the tuna with olive oil, Roll the tuna steak on seasoning. Sear both sides of the Tuna for 2 mins.  Serve and enjoy!
