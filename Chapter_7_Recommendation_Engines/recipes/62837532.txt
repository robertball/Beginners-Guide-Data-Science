Title:
Fried Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of apple juice
4 large Golden Delicious apples - 1/2" wedges
1/2 cup apple juice
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1 teaspoon apple pie spice
1 teaspoon cinnamon
4 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
Combine apple juice and apples in a medium skillet. Simmer gently and turn apples often with spatula until fork tender but not mushy. Remove with a slotted spoon to oven dish. Mix the remaining items together until smooth. Stir into hot juice in skillet and cook, stirring constantly on med-high until it bubbles and becomes thickened and smooth. Turn heat off. Pour over apples.
