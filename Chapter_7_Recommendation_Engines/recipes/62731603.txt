Title:
Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/3 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup milk
2 eggs
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp pepper
2 jars (30 oz each) spaghetti sauce
10 oz spaghetti noodles cooked and drained

Directions:
Mix 1st 8 ingredients.
Shape into meatballs.
Bake at 325 degrees for 4--45 mins.
Bring spaghetti sauce to a boil.
Add meatballs.
Return to boil.
Reduce heat to low.
Partially cover.
Simmer for 25 mins until meatballs are cooked through.
