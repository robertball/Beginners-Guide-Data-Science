Title:
Cinnamon Crunchies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/294645.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup warm water
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp. dry yeast
1 cup cold cubed butter
2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Proof the yeast in the warm water.
Set aside for 10-20 minutes.
Mix together 6 tbsp. of the sugar and the flour.
Add in the butter and beat on medium until you get pea sized chunks of butter.
Beat in yeast mixture and vanilla until blended.
Transfer dough into a small bowl and refrigerate for 1 hour or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350*.
Mix cinnamon with the remaining sugar.
Working with small batches of dough at a time, roll into 1 1/2 inch balls and dredge into sugar mixture.
Roll balls in hands again and dip again into the sugar.
Place balls 2 inches apart on cookie sheet and bake 20-25 minutes or until lightly golden brown.
