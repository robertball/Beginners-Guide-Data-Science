Title:
Easy Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16.3 oz. jar of peanut butter
1 cup of sugar
2 eggs
Dash of real vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients until well blended.
Drop teaspoonfuls on greased cookie sheet about 1 inch apart. Criss cross tops by pressing down lightly on each mound with a fork. Bake for 10 minutes. Cool on cookie sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to cooling rack. Enjoy with cold milk.
