Title:
NANA'S SALMON PATTIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/4 cup seafood breader
1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 onion (chopped)
Old Bay seasoning to taste
Dash black pepper
Dash garlic powder
oil to cover bottom of skillet

Directions:
Drain salmon, reserving 1/4 cup juice. Discard salmon bones and skin. In a bowl, combine the flour, salmon & liquid, egg, onion, seafood breader; mix well. Mix baking powder into the salmon mixture. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Pat 1/4 cup of batter into hand and form rounded pattie. Place pattie into skillet. Cook patties for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Drain on paper towels. Serve warm.

Description: 
These salmon patties can be eaten for breakfast, lunch or dinner! Breakfast with hash browns (or grits)  and eggs. Lunch with twicebaked potatoes and salad. Dinner with mashed potatoes and green beans. My family loves this recipe!. I had co-workers that would pay me to make these on Friday mornings and bring them in to work .Modified from: Salmon cakes
