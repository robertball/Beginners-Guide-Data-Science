Title:
Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water or broth (omit bouillon if using broth)
1 T butter
1 t bouillon
1 c rice (I prefer Basmati)
1 t dried minced onion
1/2 t onion pwdr
1/4 t dried basil
1/4 t marjoram
1/4 t dried thyme

Directions:
Bring the water to a boil. Add the remaining ingredients. Reduce heat to simmer and cook for 20 minutes covered.  Done!
