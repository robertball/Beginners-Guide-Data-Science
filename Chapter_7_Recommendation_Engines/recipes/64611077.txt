Title:
Baked Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butternut Squash
Butter
Salt
Brown Sugar

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350.
2.  Wash outside of squash, cut in half & clean out seeds etc.
3.  Turn upside down on cookie sheet & bake until the meat of the squash is soft.
4.  Cut squash out of the shell.
5.  Cut up squash in a greased casserole dish & season with butter, salt & brown sugar.
6.  Bake in oven until golden brown.
Note:  You could add nuts if you want something a little different

Submitter: 
Liz Westfaul
Description: 
A sweet squash dish.  Great at Thanksgiving or any other time.
