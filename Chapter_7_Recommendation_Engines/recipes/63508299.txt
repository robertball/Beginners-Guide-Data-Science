Title:
Roberta's Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Bisquick
1 cup cubed cheese
1 cup shredded cheese
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together, taking care not to overmix.  Pour batter into a prepared loaf pan.
Bake for 45 minutes.
Cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This easy quick-bread is amazing with soup on a cold day!  It doesn't get any easier than this!
