Title:
Mom's Pecan Pie Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of sugar
1 cup of brown sugar
2 caps of vanilla
4 eggs
1 pinch of salt
1 1/2 sticks of butter
2 cups of chopped pecans
2 teaspoons of water
2 Tablespoons of milk
3, 9" pie shells

Directions:
Preheat oven at 325 degrees. Defrost pie shells. Mix all incredients except for the pecans. After all ingredients are well blended add chopped pecans. Fill each pie shell half full. Cook for 45 to 50 min. or until toothpick comes out clean in the center. Watch crust to make sure it doesn't get to brown. Make sure there is space between all the pies in the oven. If pie bubbles, insert a knife to pop the bubble.
