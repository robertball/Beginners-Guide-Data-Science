Title:
Fiesta Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) pkg cream cheese, softened
1 (2 oz) can chopped black olives
1 (4 oz) can diced green chilies
1 (4oz) jar diced pimentos, drained
2 green onions chopped
3 TBL chopped cilantro
2 - 3 TBL hot pepper sauce
1 pkg Hidden Valley Fiesta Dip
10 (10 inch) flavored flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine first 8 ingredients in a bowl. Spread the mixture onto the tortillas.  Roll the tortillas and refrigerate for one hour.
Slice the roll ups into six 1" pieces and serve with salsa.

Submitter: 
MAP
Description: 
These are a big hit and there are never any leftovers. I use flavored tortillas to add an extra dimention of flavor.
