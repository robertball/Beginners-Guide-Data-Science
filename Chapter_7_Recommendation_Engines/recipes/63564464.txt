Title:
Stuffed Lasagna Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Green Peppers
1/2 white onion
1 clove garlic
1 lb ground beef
1 jar Italian tomato sauce
shredded mozzarella
16 oz ricotta cheese

Directions:
Dice 1 of the green peppers,half of a white onion, and 1 clove of garlic.
Brown meat in a skillet and drain fat.
Mix ground beef, diced green peppers, onion, garlic, and tomato sauce in the skillet and simmer.
Cut the 3 other green peppers in half and clean out any seeds and pulp.
Place the pepper halfs into a pot of water and boil for 5 minutes or so.
Place the pepper halfs in a baking dish.
Spoon meat sauce and ricotta cheese into the pepper halfs, alternating between them in layers until they are completely filled and heaping.
Thoroughly cover all of the filled pepper halfs with shredded mozzarella.
Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees

Submitter: 
Martin Dillon
Description: 
My girlfriend and I loved lasagna and came up with this after we started cutting carbs out of our diet! I honestly like it better than the original lasagna with noodles.
