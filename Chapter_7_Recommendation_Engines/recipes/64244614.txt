Title:
Ruth's Grandma's Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 3/4 cup butter,chilled  and cut into small pieces
3 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons salt
1 egg
1/2 cup water

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, butter, sugar, and salt. Blend together with a pastry cutter until crumbly.
In a small bowl, mix egg with water. Blend into flour mixture. Chill in refrigerator for at least an hour or until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Shakeel Syed
