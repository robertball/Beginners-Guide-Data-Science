Title:
Nummy Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz (2 and 1/2 cups) noodles
1 cup cream style cottage cheese
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic
1 tablespoon Worstershire sauce
dash Tabasco sauce
1 Tablespoon poppy Seed
1/2 teaspoon salt
dash pepper

Directions:
1. Cook noodles and drain.
2. Combine with remaining ingredients.
3. Bake in greased 10 x 6 x 1 1/2" pan for 25 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
4. Sprinkle with paprika
5. Serve with parmesan
