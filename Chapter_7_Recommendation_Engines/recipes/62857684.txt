Title:
Mediterranean Chicken Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded to 1/4 inch thin
fresh spinach leaves
2 T. Kalamata Olive Tapenade (recipe on this site by Teresita79)
1/4 cup goat cheese
3 T. shredded manchego cheese
1 egg, beaten
panko bread crumbs
McCormick Italian herb grinder
olive oil spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a baking dish with olive oil spray.
Lay a few spinach leaves on center of pounded chicken breasts. Top each breast with 1/2 of the tapinade, 1/2 of the goat cheese, and 1/2 of the manchego cheese. Starting at a short end, tightly roll chicken breasts folding edges in as you go.  Fasten with a toothpick if needed.
Grind McCormick Italian seasoning into bread crumbs to taste. Dip each rollup in egg and coat with panko bread crumb mixture. Place in baking dish seam side down. Spray tops of breasts with olive oil spray.  Bake for 30-35 minutes.
