Title:
Corn State Broccoli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of Chicken in a Biskit crackers, crushed
1/4 cup margarine, melted
1 pkg. (10 oz.) frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 can corn, drained
1 can cream-style corn

Directions:
Combine crushed cracker crumbs and melted butter; reserve Â½ cup for topping.
In a bowl, combine broccoli, both cans of corn and crumb mixture.
Transfer the corn mixture into a 2 qt. baking dish. (sprayed with oil spray)
Sprinkle top with reserved butter & crumb mixture.
Bake, uncovered, at 375 for 25 minutes or until lightly browned.
6-8 servings

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is a corn & broccoli side dish make with Chicken in a Biskit crackers.  Chicken in a Biskit crackers come in a turquoise/blue box.
