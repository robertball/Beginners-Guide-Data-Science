Title:
Baked Sea Bass with Capers and Lemon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sea bass fillets (or any firm white fish available)
1 lemon
2 Tbs capers, rinsed
2 sprigs fresh dill (dried may be used if fresh dill is unavailable)
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350?.
Place sea bass fillets on a broiler pan.
Thinly slice lemon (1/8" slices).
Sprinkle the fish with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.  Top with capers and dill sprigs.  Cover with fresh lemon slices.
Bake for 10-15 minutes, until fish flakes easily with a fork.
