Title:
Kickin' Crunchy Oven "Fried" Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/463758.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup finely crushed cornflakes cereal
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp freshly ground black pepper
************************************
1/4 cup egg substitute
1 Tablespoon water
1/4 tsp hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco)
************************************
3 bone-in chicken breasts (skin removed)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 F. Spray a 9X13 glass baking dish with cooking spray or line with non-stick foil.
Mix the first seven ingredients in a gallon sized zip bag. Set aside.
In a shallow bowl, whisk together egg subsitute, water, and hot pepper sauce.
Dip chicken pieces, one at a time, in egg substitute mixture. Drop into bag and shake to coat in crumb mixture.
Place chicken pieces in baking dish and bake, uncovered, 35-40 minutes or until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Like your chicken with a spicy, crunchy breading but hate all the calories from frying? This is a great, tasty, and healthier, alternative!
