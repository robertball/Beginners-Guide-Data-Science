Title:
Julian BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
64 ounces Ketchup
2 ½ cups honey
15 ounces A-1 Sauce
¼ cup Worcestershire Sauce
1 cup mustard
4 tablespoons brown sugar or molasses
1 ½ tablespoons black pepper
2 teaspoons liquid hickory smoke flavoring
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon onion salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a very large mixing bowl until well-combined and no sugar lumps remain and pour into a lot of bottles. It keeps in the fridge for 3 months and in the freezer indefinitely.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
Hubby and I created this over 20 yrs. ago, reverse engineering a small-batch artisan sauce we got from a country store in Julian, Ca.. By the time we got the taste just right, we had almost 2 gallons of the stuff! Friends have been requesting it as gifts ever since.
