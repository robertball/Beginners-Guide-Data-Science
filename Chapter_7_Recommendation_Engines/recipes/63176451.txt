Title:
Mushroom Broccoli Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked rice
1 cup coarse chopped fresh mushrooms
1 cup chopped cooked broccoli florets
1 small onion finely chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2-3 tablespoons olive oil
pepper to taste
1/4 cup of reduced sodium dark soya sauce(or more to taste)

Directions:
In medium sauce pan, saute onion , garlic,and mushrooms in olive oil til mushrooms are tender and cooked through
Add broccoli and saute 1 minute,
Add rice and pepper to taste and until rice is heated through
Add soya sauce , stiring until throughly combined( add more soya sauce if too light coloured or to taste).
Serves 4 .

Submitter: 
BramptonMommyof2
Description: 
Whipped this up with leftover rice and steamed broccoli. Additon of soya sauce gives it a nice flavour.
