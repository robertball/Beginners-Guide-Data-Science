Title:
Mom's Easy Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/808750.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package deluxe American cheese, diced
3 (15 ounce) cans baby peas, drained
1 cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle WhipÂ®)
1 (4 ounce) jar diced pimentos, drained
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Place the diced cheese into a salad bowl. Gently stir in the peas, salad dressing, pimentos, salt, and pepper until the diced cheese separates into small squares and the salad is thoroughly mixed. Cover and refrigerate 3 hours or overnight to chill and blend the flavors.

Submitter: 
Jonna
Description: 
This has been a staple dish on my Mom's table since she's been cooking, over 60 years! It's easy and attractive to present. You can add sliced boiled eggs and finely chopped red onion, if desired.
