Title:
Sweet & Easy Peach Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/3 cup sour cream
1 cup sugar
3 egg yolks slightly beaten
4 medium peaches diced
1/4 cup maple syrup
pinch of salt
1/2 tbs butter

Directions:
Heat a pan on medium heat with a bit of butter.
Meanwhile, peel, pit and chop up peaches.
Add peaches, maple syrup and pinch of salt to pan and cook peaches for about 10 minutes or until bubbly and gooey. Don't let it boil or carmelize.
Remove peaches from heat and set aside.
Mix 1/2 tsp salt and 1 1/8 cup flour; cut in butter until size of small peas. Blend in 2 Tbs of sour cream, forming dough. Pat dough on bottom and sides of a greased 10x6 baking pan. Bake at 425 for 10 minutes. Mix sugar, egg yolks remaining flour and remaining sour cream until blended well. Pour peaches onto hot crust and spread evenly on hot crust. Pour sour cream mixture over top. Cover with foil. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 10 minutes until custard is set. Serve warm.

Description: 
This is a simple dish with lots of flavor. Perfect way to use those summer peaches. Its also versatile so feel free to experiment with other fruits.
