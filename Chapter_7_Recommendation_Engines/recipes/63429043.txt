Title:
Rice pilaf with mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Mushrooms sliced
2 Tbspn Butter
1 c uncooked white rice
2 c Chicken stock
1/2 tspn salt

Directions:
heat oven to 350
butter a 2 quart casserole
add the rice and salt to the casserole
saute the mushrooms in the butter 5 to 6 minutes
add the stock to the pan and bring to a boil
pour over the rice
cover and bake for 30 minutes
