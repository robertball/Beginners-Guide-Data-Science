Title:
Almond Joy Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz amaretto, 1 oz dark creme de cacao, 1 oz creme de coconut, splash cream.

Directions:
shake with ice and serve in rocks glass or add 2 scoops vanilla ice cream and 1/2 scoop of crushed ice and blend in blender until smooth.  Serve in a tall decorative glass.
