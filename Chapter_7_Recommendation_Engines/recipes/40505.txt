Title:
Cheddar Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986854.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11.5 ounce) package STOUFFER'S® frozen Potatoes Au Gratin
1 (10 ounce) packet STOUFFER'S® frozen Welsh Rarebit, defrosted
1 cup milk
1 tablespoon chopped green onion
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/3 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
COMBINE Potatoes au Gratin, Welsh Rarebit, milk, green onion and pepper in medium saucepan. Heat, stirring constantly, over medium heat until soup begins to simmer.
REMOVE from heat; add Cheddar cheese, stirring until melted.

Submitter: 
Stouffer's®
Description: 
Creamy and filled with potatoes, this soup is topped with a rich cheddar taste.
