Title:
Tartar Sauce I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
1 tablespoon minced onion
2 tablespoons lemon juice (optional)
2 dashes garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sweet pickle relish, and minced onion. Stir in lemon juice.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
RixiePixie
Description: 
Simple tartar sauce for deep fried seafood.  It is better if prepared several hours before you will be using it, but it can be used immediately.  Store for up to 3 days in the refrigerator.Modified from: Tartar Sauce I
