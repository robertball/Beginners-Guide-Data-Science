Title:
Pecan Pie Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/671433.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
3/4 cup butter, softened
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
Filling ingredients
1 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup whipping cream
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix together 1 cup brown sugar, butter, egg, and vanilla. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour and baking powder. Mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients until well mixed. Pat dough into a ball and wrap in plastic wrap. Chill for at least one hour.
Once dough is chilled, preheat oven to 350.
Combine filling ingredients in a small bowl.
Shape dough into 1 1/4 inch balls. Place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Make indentation into balls with your thumb. Spoon a rounded tsp of filling mixture into indent in each cookie.
Bake at 350 for approx 10 minutes. Cookies should be lightly browned.
Remove from oven and let cool on cookie sheet for 2 minutes before removing to wire rack to cool completely.
