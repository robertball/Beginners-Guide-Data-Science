Title:
Sweet 'n' Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 cup crushed crackers
1/4 cup milk
1/2 pkg. dry onion soup mix
Sauce:
2 cups brown sugar, packed
2 Tbsp. flour
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup water
2 Tbsp. soy sauce
1 Tbsp. ketchup

Directions:
Form meatballs and fry until brown in frying pan.
Stir sauce ingredients into saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring over medium heat (add more flour if needed).
Pour sauce over meatballs and enjoy
