Title:
Madison's Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Cocoa powder
1/4 C. cornstarch
3 egg yolks (beaten)
1 1/2 C sugar
pinch of salt
2 C milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients.  Gradually add milk while stirring in pot over medium heat.  Cook until thick and smooth.  Stir in vanilla.
Pour mixture into baked, homemade pie crust.  Put in frig until cool and set.  Whip set aside egg whites for a meringue topping, or top with whipped cream (Madison likes the whipped cream).  Enjoy :)

Submitter: 
Brenda Ann Grindstaff
Description: 
creamy, chocolate pie that my granddaughter absolutely loves!!!
