Title:
Thick Taco Dip/Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. pkg. softened cream cheese
1/4 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup salsa- jarred or homemade- mild- medium or hot
1 teaspoon worchestershire sauce
1 teaspoon taco seasoning- pkg or homemade

Directions:
add all ingredients in a mixing bowl- whip on medium for 3 minutes- place in serving dish- cover- chill at least 1/2 hour. serve with chips
