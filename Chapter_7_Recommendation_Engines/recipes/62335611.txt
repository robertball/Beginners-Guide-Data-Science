Title:
Colossal Caramel Apple Truffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	1 package yellow cake mix
•	6 cups cold milk
•	3 packages (3.4 oz) instant vanilla pudding mix
•	1 tsp. apple pie spice
•	1 jar (12-14 oz) caramel ice cream topping
•	2 cans apple pie filling
•	2 (16 oz) frozen whipped topping thawed

Directions:
•	Prepare and bake cake according to package directions using 2 greased 9 inch pans
•	Cool baked cakes
•	In large bowl whisk milk, pudding mixes, and apple pie spice for two minutes
•	Let stand for 2 minutes
•	Poke holes in cake and pour a third of caramel syrup over cake
•	Spread pudding mix, spoon one can of pie filling over pudding
•	Spread with one carton of whipped topping
•	Top with next cake and repeat
•	Refrigerate

Description: 
Another classic dessert my mom makes during the summertime.  It's very light and refreshing.
