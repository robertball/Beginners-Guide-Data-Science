Title:
Crispy Fried/baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole fryer chicken-cut into pieces
5 C flour
1 12oz can of beer
2 eggs
1 Tbs Seasoning Salt
2 Tbs Paprika
1 Tsp White Pepper
2 Qt frying oil

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 F. And oil to about 325 F.
In Large bowl mix 2 Cups flour, beer and eggs.
In second large bowl(or edged plate) mix the remaining flour(3c), seasoning salt, paprika, and white pepper.
First roll chicken in dry flour mix than dip in beer batter, repeat roll in dry flour mix.
Place in oil for 5-10 min. or until nice and golden brown. Place on  baking sheet and bake for 20 min (until the internal temp. is at least 165 F and no longer pink).

Submitter: 
SharonLynn Kinville
Description: 
Easy and tasty crispy chicken recipe that also has many uses as left overs. VERY crispy.
