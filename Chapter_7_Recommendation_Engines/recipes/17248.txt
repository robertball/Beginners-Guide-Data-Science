Title:
Grape Banana Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/579597.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint heavy whipping cream
6 cups seedless red grapes, halved
4 large bananas, peeled and sliced
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat the cream until stiff peaks form. Gently stir in fruit and nuts.

Submitter: 
Jan
Description: 
This is a nice blend of fruit and nuts.
