Title:
Garlicky Baked Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110365.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound peeled and deveined medium or large shrimp
4 cloves garlic, sliced
2 tablespoons white wine
kosher salt and black pepper
1/4 cup softened butter
1/4 cup panko bread crumbs
2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Directions:
Heat oven to 425º F. In a baking dish, combine the shrimp, garlic, white wine, ½ teaspoon salt, and ¼ teaspoon pepper.
In a small bowl, combine the butter, bread crumbs, and parsley.
Sprinkle over the shrimp and bake until the shrimp are opaque throughout, 15 to 18 minutes.
