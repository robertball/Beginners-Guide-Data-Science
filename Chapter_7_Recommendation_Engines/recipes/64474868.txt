Title:
Gluten Free Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2518538.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 pounds shredded cheddar cheese (a mix of cheeses work well, sharp, medium, low and no fat even)
1 pound pack of organic brown rice penne pasta
2 cups low fat buttermilk
1/4 cup fine chopped onion
1/4 cup butter, ghee, or NON GMO margarine
1/4 gluten free flour, or brown rice flour
1 teaspoon dried mustard
dash of Worcestershire sauce
dash garlic powder, salt, pepper to taste
sprinkle top with smoked paprika and Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Using 2-1/2-3 qt. casserole dish for baking set aside.  Boil in large stock pan the pound of penne pasta just under the recommended time to keep slightly firm, rinse in cold water, drain well. Meanwhile in large saute pan, heat margarine or butter and saute chopped onions a couple minutes, add the GF flour and seasonings desired, stir well.  Add 2 cups buttermilk, dash Worcestershire, cook to thicken, add all the shredded cheeses to melt, stir and combine well, pour the pasta into sauce mixture, and then stir some more, then pour into casserole sprinkle some smoked paprika on top, and bake with lid off for approximately 40 minutes or until golden on top and desired appearance.
