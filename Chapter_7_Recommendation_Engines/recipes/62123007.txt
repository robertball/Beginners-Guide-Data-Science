Title:
Helopites or Manestra- Small pasta with beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
1 onion, chopped
1 pound stew beef
1 can tomato sauce
salt and pepper to taste
3 stick cinnamon
1 package manestra/orzo (rice-shaped) or helopites (small square pasta)

Directions:
Sautee chopped onion in olive oil over medium heat until soft.
Add beef and brown each piece on all sides.
Add tomato sauce, salt and pepper, and cinnamon.
Add enough water to cover the ingredients and boil for 45 minutes.
Add the pasta and cook until it is done, stirring constantly.
