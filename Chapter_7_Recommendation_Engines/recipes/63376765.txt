Title:
Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/903498.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 Large can diced tomatoes
1 can red kidney beans
1 large onion dices
1 jalapeño minced
2 tsp chilli powder
1 tsp chipotle powder
2 tbs cumin powder
1 ½ tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
Sauté onion and cook ground beef salt and pepper to taste. Add tomatoes and dry ingredients.
Cook 10 minutes, add jalapeño and kidney beans. Cook on low heat about 20 minutes.
Top with shredded cheese and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Danielle Girouard
Description: 
I came up with this recipe because I needed to make a quick and easy meal without having to leave the house for ingredients and it really turned out great.
