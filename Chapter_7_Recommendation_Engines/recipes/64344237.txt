Title:
Laurie's Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked white rice
3 tbs sesame oil
1 cup frozen peas and carrots (thawed)
1 small onion, chopped
1tsp minced garlic
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 cup soy sauce

Directions:
On medium high heat, heat the oil in a large skillet or wok.
Add the peas, carrots, onion and garlic. Stir fry until tender.
Lower the heat to medium low and push the mixture off to one side, then pour the eggs on the other side of skillet and stir fry until scrambled.
Add the rice and soy sauce and blend all together well.
Stir fry until thoroughly heated.

Submitter: 
DebbyJean
Description: 
Quick, easy and better than take out. Add any other vegetables and/or meat that you like. Make sure to make your rice at least 1 day before for best results.
