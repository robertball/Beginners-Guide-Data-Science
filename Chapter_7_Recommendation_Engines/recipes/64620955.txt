Title:
Blended Beet & Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium-sized beets, peeled, cooked, and cubed
4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/2 onion, chopped (if tolerated)
1 teaspoon dried dill
1 tablespoon butter
8 cups vegetable stock
1/2 cup fat-free evaporated milk or dairy-free substitute
salt and pepper

Directions:
Saute onions with dill in butter until softened.
Add cubed potatoes, stock and beets. Bring to a boil.
Reduce to a simmer and cook until veggies are very soft, about 30-40 minutes.
Blend until smooth with an immersion blender.
Stir in milk. Add salt and pepper to taste.
