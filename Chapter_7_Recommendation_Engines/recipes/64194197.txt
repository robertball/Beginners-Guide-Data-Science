Title:
Crab Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 6oz package of Cream Cheese, softened
1 cup of finely chopped Celery
1 small sweet onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup of Mayonnaise
12 oz can of Crabmeat
2 teaspoons chopped Garlic
1 envelope Gelatin
1 tablespoon cold Water

Directions:
Mix first 7 ingredients together in a medium sized bowl.  In a separate small bowl, stir together gelatin and water until gelatin is dissolved.  Stir dissolved gelatin with other ingredients.  Put bowl in refrigerator for at least 1 hour to let the gelatin settle.
Serve chilled with potato chips or crackers.

Submitter: 
LonestarNR
Description: 
I have modified this recipe slightly from the initial recipe that was given to me 35 years ago.  The mousse is sure to be a hit at any party.
