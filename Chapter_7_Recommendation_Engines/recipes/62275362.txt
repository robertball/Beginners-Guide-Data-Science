Title:
Muffaletta Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. cooked pasta, set aside to cool
5-6 ounces Italian olive salad or Giardinera in oil
1 can quartered artichoke hearts (optional)
4 ounces cubed mozzarella
4 ounces cubed provolone (Monterey Jack will do in a pinch)
1 small can black olives, drained
8 ounces sliced salami cut into thin strips
8 ounces sliced baked ham cut into thin strips
1 red bell pepper cut into thin strips
1/4 bunch lightly chopped fresh parsley
2 TBSP toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Cook pasta in well salted water, drain, and set aside in a large bowl.
When cooled, add olive salad (with most to all of the oil - depends on your taste). Season with black pepper to taste.
Add in artichoke hearts, cheeses, meats, black olives, red bell pepper, and parsley. Toss until everything is well coated with the olive salad.
Place in serving bowl and top with toasted sesame seeds as a garnish.

Submitter: 
Elisa Nuccio Holt
Description: 
The great New Orleans sandwich turned into an easy cold dish.
