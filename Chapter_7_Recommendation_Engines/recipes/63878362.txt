Title:
Big Ray's Biscuit Stuffing Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075920.jpg
Ingredients:
6 pork chops, 3/4 inch thick
1 Tbl  oil
2 cans condensed cream of chicken soup(10 3/4 oz)
1 cup celery, chopped
1 cup onion, chopped
1 egg
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp poultry seasoning or 1/8 tsp sage
1  can refrigerated buttermilk biscuits (7 1/2 oz)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F.
In large skillet, brown pork chops in oil.
Place in ungreased 13x9 inch pan.
Sautee the celery and onion in a little butter and in medium bowl, combine soup, celery, onions, egg, pepper and poultry seasoning; mix well.
Separate dough into 10 biscuits; cut each into 8 pieces.
Stir biscuit pieces into soup mixture; spoon over pork chops.
Bake for 45 to 55 minutes or until biscuit pieces are golden brown.
