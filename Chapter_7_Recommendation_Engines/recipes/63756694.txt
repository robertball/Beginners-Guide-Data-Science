Title:
BBQ Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen corn
1 package lima beans
1 can black beans
1 can diced tomatoes
1 diced onion
tomato juice
chili powder
bbq sauce
optional: add pulled pork

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and place in crockpot on low for 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This is a quick soup for cool fall or winter days.
