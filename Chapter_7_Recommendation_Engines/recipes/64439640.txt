Title:
Italian Meatballs and Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS:
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup milk
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 lb ground beef
1/2 lb ground Italian sausage
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
2 Tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
SAUCE:
1/2 cup beef broth
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 onion, chopped
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp pepper
6 oz. can tomato paste
15 oz. can tomato sauce
1/3 cup water

Directions:
MEATBALLS:
1. Combine eggs and milk; add breadcrumbs, cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
2. Add beef and sausage; mix well.
3. Shape into 1" balls.
4. Brown in skillet, transfer to slow cooker....
SAUCE:
1. Combine all ingredients in a separate bowl and mix together.
2. Pour over the meatballs
3. Cook on low for 4-5 hours
