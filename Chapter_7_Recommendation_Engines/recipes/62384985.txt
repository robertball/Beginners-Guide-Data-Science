Title:
Strawberry Frozen Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Vanilla Greek Yogurt (3-5.3 oz containers of Oikos brand is my favorite)
1/4 cup Sugar (Organic Demerara Turbinado Sugar)
3 cups of frozen Strawberries (not in syrup, not sweetened)

Directions:
In food processor or blender, combine yogurt and sugar. Add frozen strawberries 1 cup at a time, blend until smooth. Serve immediately or put in freezer safe container for more solid consistency.
