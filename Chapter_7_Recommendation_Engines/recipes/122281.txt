Title:
Sour Cream Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/452551.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package elbow macaroni
3/4 cup diced green pepper
1/3 cup sweet pickle relish
1 (2 ounce) jar diced pimientos, drained
1 tablespoon grated onion
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt or salt-free seasoning blend
pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions; rinse in cold water and drain. Place in a large bowl; add green pepper, pickle relish, pimientos and onion. In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; mix well. pour over macaroni mixture and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Rita  Morris
Description: 
'This make-ahead macaroni salad never fails to stay moist,' says Rita Morris of Gastonia, North Carolina. 'I double the recipe for potluck dinners.'
