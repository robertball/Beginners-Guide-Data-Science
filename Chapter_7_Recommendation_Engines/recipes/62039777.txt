Title:
peanut butter eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 jar (1 lb, 2 oz) peanut butter
1 lb 10x sugar
3 1/2 cups rice krispies

Directions:
Mix, shape using a tbsp to shape.  Chill 2 days, coating with semi-sweet chocolate bits and some baking wax(melted)

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This is a family Easter favorite.  I grew up on this.  I can still remember my grandmother in the kitchen making this as I would lick the spoons.  You will always remember your fondest childhood memories!
