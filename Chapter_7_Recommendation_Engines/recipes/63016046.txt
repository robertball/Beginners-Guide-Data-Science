Title:
Oh Henry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk
1 1/3 cups Graham cracker crumbs
1 cup chopped nuts
whole graham crackers
ICING
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
3 tbsp. butter
5 tsp. milk
Vanilla
Cinnamon to taste.

Directions:
Line 9 x9 inch pan with whole crackers trimming to fit.  Set aside
In saucepan combine sugar, butter and milk,  Bring to boil.  Simmer for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Add cracker crumb stirring to mix.  Pour over crackers in pan.
Put another layer of crackers over top of mixture.  Cool.  Frost and let stand over night
