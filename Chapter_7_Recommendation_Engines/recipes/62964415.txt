Title:
Missy's Chicken N Rice Casseroll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless, chicken breast, diced fairly small
3 tbl butter or margarine
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp each, of salt and pepper (or to taste)
1 1/2 cups water
2 10 oz cans cream of chicken soup
1 10 oz can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz shreeded chedder cheese
8 cups cooked rice (instant or left-over works great)
1 1/2 cups frozen chopped broccoli (or 1 can of green beans,drained).

Directions:
Saute first 5 ingredience, until chicken is a golden brown all over.
Add water, cream of chicken soup and cream of mushroom soup, and simmer about 5 min
Pack cooked rice into lg casseroll dish.
Layer broccoli (or green beans) on rice.
Sprinkel cheese over broccoli (or Green beans).
Pour chicken and sauce over cheese.
Bake in pre-heated oven at 325 degrees uncovered for about 20 min.
