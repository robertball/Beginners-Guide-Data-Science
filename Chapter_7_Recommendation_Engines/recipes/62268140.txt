Title:
Sour Cream Pineapple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp coconut extract
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
pinch salt
1 can (15 oz) crushed pineapple, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Spray 12 cup muffin tin with cooking spray.
Cream together sugar, egg, butter, sour cream and coconut extract. Stir in drained pineapple.
In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Gently mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients until just blended. Do not overmix.
Divide evenly amongst 12 muffin cups. Bake for 15 - 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in centre comes out clean.
