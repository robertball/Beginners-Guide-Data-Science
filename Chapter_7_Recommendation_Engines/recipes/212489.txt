Title:
Black Friday Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon canned jellied cranberry sauce
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
2 slices bread
1 tablespoon mashed leftover sweet potato casserole with marshmallow topping
1 tablespoon gravy (optional)
1 tablespoon dry leftover stuffing
1/4 cup shredded leftover cooked turkey
1 tablespoon coarsely chopped leftover green bean casserole
1 tablespoon chopped French-fried onions

Directions:
Mash cranberry sauce in a bowl with a fork until smooth. Add mayonnaise and celery seed; mix thoroughly. Spread sauce over bread slices.
Spread sweet potato casserole onto 1 slice of bread.
Mash gravy and stuffing together in a bowl. Mix in turkey. Spoon the mixture over the sweet potatoes; top with green bean casserole and onions. Place second slice of bread on top.
