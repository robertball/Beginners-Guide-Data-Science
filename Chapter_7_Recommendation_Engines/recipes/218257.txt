Title:
Wacky Mac® Five-Ingredient Skillet Sausage Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992224.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
2 cups sliced mushrooms, preferably baby bella
1 (12 ounce) package cooked chicken and apple sausage, sliced 1/4-inch thick (see note)
3/8 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 cups Wacky Mac®, cooked according to package directions

Directions:
Heat oil in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onion and mushrooms and cook for 5 to 7 minutes or until onion is lightly browned. Add sausage slices, salt and pepper. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes to brown, stirring frequently. Stir in Wacky Mac and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Wacky Mac®
Description: 
Wacky Mac with chicken and apple sausage combined with onion and mushrooms create this easy meal that the whole family can enjoy.
