Title:
Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bunches basil, cleaned
1 3/4 C vegetable oil
1 1/2 C nuts (sliced almonds, pecans, peanuts)
2 C shredded parmesan
1 TBSP seasoning mix
1/4 C chopped garlic
water, as needed

Directions:
Take basil leaves off stems and place leaves in a food processor.  Add oil.  Blend then add nuts, parmesan, garlic and seasoning mix.  Blend.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Quick and delicious, this pesto is sure to go well on pasta, sandwiches, or anything else you can think of.
