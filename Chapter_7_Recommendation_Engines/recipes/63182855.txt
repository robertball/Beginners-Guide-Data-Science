Title:
Holly's Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup of unsweetened applesauce
1 cup of sugar
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon of almond extract
4 eggs
1 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup of semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 teaspoon of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (325 degrees F if using a glass pan). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a medium bowl, mix together the applesauce, butter ,and sugar.  Add in the almond and vanilla extracts. Beat in eggs. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cocoa, and baking powder; gradually stir into the egg mixture until well blended with a small spoon.  Add in chocolate chips and continue to stir.  Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake for 30 minutes at the most, or until the brownie begins to pull away from edges of pan. Let cool on a wire rack before cutting into squares.
