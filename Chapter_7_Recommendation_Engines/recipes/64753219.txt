Title:
Jewel's Black Raspberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6839261.jpg
Ingredients:
Double Recipe for Butter Flaky Pie Crust - https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/24094/butter-flaky-pie-crust/
2 quarts fresh black raspberries
1 1/2 cups white sugar
3 Tbsp corn starch
2 Tbsp quick cooking tapioca
4 Tbsp butter, cut into 1 Tbsp pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
In a large bowl, gently mix together the black raspberries, sugar, corn starch and tapioca until the berries are well coated. Set aside and prepare the crust.
Place one crust into the bottom of a deep dish pie plate. Add the berries. Top with the 4 portions or butter.. Add the top crust and cut slits to vent.
Bake at 425 for 15 minutes. Reduce oven to 350 degrees and continue baking for 45 minutes.
Let cool and enjoy!
