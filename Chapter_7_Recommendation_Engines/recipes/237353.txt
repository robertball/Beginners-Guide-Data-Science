Title:
Egg Salad with Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1124442.jpg
Ingredients:
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and mashed
3 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
1 tablespoon chopped onion (optional)
1 tablespoon chopped celery (optional)
salt to taste

Directions:
Mix avocado and eggs together in a bowl. Add relish, onion, celery, and salt; mix well.

Submitter: 
Karen Anderson Sorenson
Description: 
I wanted to make a healthier egg salad. This one uses avocado instead of mayonnaise. Serve in a sandwich or on crackers.
