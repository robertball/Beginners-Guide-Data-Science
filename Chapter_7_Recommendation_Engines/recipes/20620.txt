Title:
Electric Strawberry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/825464.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce butterscotch schnapps
4 fluid ounces milk
1 dash grenadine syrup
1 strawberry

Directions:
In a mixing glass half-full of ice combine butterscotch schnapps, milk and a dash of grenadine. Shake well and serve in a cocktail glass with a strawberry for garnish.

Submitter: 
MOLSONROCKS
Description: 
A quick and easy sweet after dinner drink of butterscotch schnapps, milk and grenadine.
