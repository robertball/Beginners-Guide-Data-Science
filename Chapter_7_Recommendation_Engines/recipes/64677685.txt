Title:
Slow Cooker Roast Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb boneless beef roast (outside round is nice)
1 tsp. beef bouillon powder
1/2 cup boiling water
flour (& water, optional) for gravy

Directions:
Put roast in slow cooker.
Stir bouillon powder into boiling water. Pour over beef.
Cover and cook on low x 8-10 hours or on high for 4-5 hours.
Make gravy with the remaining juices once cooked: strain juices, heat in small saucepan while stirring in either a med. thick smooth flour/water paste or use very fine flour ie. Veloutine.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
An easy way to make a tender roast while going about your day.
