Title:
Mixed Berries Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5401893.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Almond Breeze Vanilla or Unsweetened Vanilla almondmilk
1/2 cup fresh blueberries
1/2 cup fresh raspberries
1/2 cup fresh blackberries
1 tablespoon toasted slices or slivered almonds (optional)
1 teaspoon ground flax seeds (optional)

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Fresh berries and Vanilla Breeze make a tangy, refreshing smoothie. Use a frozen berry blend in place of fresh berries for a shortcut.
