Title:
Apple Brickle Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 - 8 ounces toffee baking bits

Directions:
Beat together the cream cheese, brown and white sugars, and vanilla extract in a bowl until smooth. Stir in toffee bits to taste. Serve with apples.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
Toffee bits are stirred into a sweetened cream cheese to make a yummy dip for apples.Modified from: Apple Brickle Dip
