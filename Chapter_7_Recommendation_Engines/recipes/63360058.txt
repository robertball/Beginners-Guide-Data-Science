Title:
Sam's Biscuit "Garlic" Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C butter/marg.
1/4 C Parmesan cheese
1/2 Tsp dry mustard
2 pkgs flaky refrigerator biscuits
Garlic to taste
Optional - Sesame seeds
2 Tbls Butter/marg.

Directions:
Heat Oven to 375
Coat a 9x13 pan with 2 Tbls of butter/marg.
Combine garlic, butter and dry mustard.  Separate the biscuits and  in to 4 equal pieces, arrange biscuits evenly into pan, melt and drizzle reserved butter mixture over buiscuits.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake 30-40 minutes.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Bill Falk
Description: 
A garlic version of the sweet confection made easy!!
