Title:
Diet Dr Pepper Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Diet Dr Pepper
3/4 cup ketchup, low carb
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons steak sauce, low carb
2 tablespoons lemon juice, or more to taste
1 tablespoon hot sauce, or more to taste
1 tablespoon cider vinegar, or more to taste
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
coarse salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a heavy, non-reactive saucepan and gradually bring to boil over medium heat.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer gently until thick, about 30 minutes.
Adjust to taste by adding more lemon juice, hot sauce, or cider vinegar.  Add salt to taste.
Allow to cool to room temperature before refrigerating.

Description:
