Title:
Daddy's Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
10-3/4 oz. can cream of mushroom soup
2/3 c. water
7.2-oz. pkg. home style creamy butter-flavored instant mashed potato flakes
2 c. Corn (or favorite vegetable)
8-oz. pkg. shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown beef in a skillet over medium heat; drain.  Stir in soup and water: simmer until heated through.
Meanwhile, prepare potatoes; set aside.
Place beef mixture in a 13" x 9" baking pan sprayed with non-stick spray.  Top with veggie; spread potatoes evenly across top.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake, uncovered, at 425 degrees for about 10 minutes, or until hot and cheese is melted.
Makes 6 to 8 servings.
