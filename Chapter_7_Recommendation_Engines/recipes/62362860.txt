Title:
Spicy Pasta Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# linguine, or whatever pasta
1C broccoli
1C chopped tomatoes
4 cloves garlic, pressed
2T red pepper flakes
1/2C olive oil, divided
1/2C chopped onion, or shallots
3T chopped fresh basil
salt to taste
Romano or Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to boil. Add pasta and 2T olive oil (can omit oil)
Meanwhile, steam broccoli and chop; set aside
In saucepan, saute onions, tomatoes and red pepper flakes in remaining oil until onions are tender
Drain pasta and return to pot. Add tomato mixture along with broccoli to pasta
Toss all and add basil and salt to taste. Sprinkle with cheese

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Pasta, fresh broccoli, basil and tomatoes, lots of garlic and red pepper flakes to your taste. Very good
