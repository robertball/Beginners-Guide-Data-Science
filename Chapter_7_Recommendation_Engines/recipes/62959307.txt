Title:
Panko Breaded Adobo Pork Loin. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729827.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pork loin portions or pork chops
1 1/2 teaspoons of Adobo spice
1 cup of panko bread crumbs
2 eggs
1/2 cup of flour
4 oz of milk
Pam Type Spray Cooking Oil (Optional)

Directions:
Coat meat in flour
Coat meat with scrambled egg, and milk mixture
Coat meat with bread crumbs that have Adobo spice mixed in.
Spray breaded pork with Pam type spray oil (optional)
Bake at 375F for 45 mins, check meat temp, should be cooked till 165F.

Description: 
One of my versions of breaded pork loin or pork chops.
