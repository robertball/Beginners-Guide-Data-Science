Title:
Lemon/Lime Gelatin Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 3-oz. packages of gelatin, 2 lemon, 2 lime
4 cups of boiling water
Sour cream, to taste
Crushed pineapple with juice, not the small can
Chopped pecans, optional

Directions:
Mix boiling water with the gelatin until dossolved.  Refrigerate and stir every 15 to 20 minutes.  When it starts to gel, put it into a mixer and add other ingredients.  Pour into mold and refrigerate until set.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
The 'green jello mold' has been a family favorite for over 3 generations!  It's simple to make and refreshing anytime of the year!!
