Title:
Tomato- Bisque with Blue Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. butter
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
4 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. white pepper
1/2 tsp. dried thyme leaves
1/2 tsp. dried basil leaves
1 6 oz. can tomato paste
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
3 C. chicken stock
1 13 oz. can low-fat evaporated milk
1/2 C. low-fat sour cream
3 T. flour
1/3 C. crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
In large soup pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir 5 minutes. Add tomatoes; cook and stir until liquid evaporates and mixture is thick. Add salt, pepper, thyme, basil, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and tomato sauce; cook and stir until mixture simmers. Add chicken stock; bring back to a simmer. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-10 minutes or until blended. In medium bowl, combine milk, sour cream, and flour and mix well. Add 1.2 cup of tomato mixture to milk mixture, stirring with a wire whisk till blend. Transfer all soup to the pot. Cook over low heat, stirring frequently, until soup is hot. Stir in blue cheese and serve
