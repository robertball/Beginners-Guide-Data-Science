Title:
Zucchini Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cup of finely chopped zucchini
1 cup of almond or peanut butter
1/3 cup honey
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking soda
4 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine all ingredients into a large bowl and mix everything together.
Pour better into a 9 x 9 greased baking pan or into individual cupcake liners.  Bake for 35-45 min in the pan, or 16-18 min if made into cupcakes.
Make sure they are fully cooked by inserting a toothpick.
Serve with old fashioned vanilla ice cream!
