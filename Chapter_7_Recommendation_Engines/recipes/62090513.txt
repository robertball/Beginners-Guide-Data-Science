Title:
Mexican Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
four cans of tuna in spring water, drained
one very ripe avacado
one tomato
one cup fresh chopped cilantro
one onion
one large lime or two small limes
1-2 tbs tobasco sauce
12 flour tortillas or enough crackers for four people

Directions:
Chop tomato, onion, avacado and cilantro and put in a medium bowl.
Squeese all of the lime juice into the bowl and add the tobasco sauce.
Drain all the water out of the tuna and add to the bowl and mix.
Serve on hot flour tortillas or with crackers.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
This is basically pico de gallo with tuna served with your favorite flour tortillas or crackers. Very refreshing and full of wonderful authentic Mexican flavor. The person who gave this recipe to me was from northern Mexico.
