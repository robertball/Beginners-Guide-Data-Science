Title:
Cranberry Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
1/4 cup water
1 cup whole milk
2 cups organic cane sugar
2 cups heavy cream
1/2 cup powdered skim milk
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, cook cranberries and water over medium heat until they burst.  Strain and discard seeds and skins. Set aside to cool.
Heat milk and cream to 175 degrees.  Stir in sugar until dissolved.  Mix in the juices.
Chill to between 32 and 40 degrees.  Freeze according to ice cream maker manufacturer's directions.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Cranberry sauce may be good, but sweet creamy cranberry ice cream is better.
