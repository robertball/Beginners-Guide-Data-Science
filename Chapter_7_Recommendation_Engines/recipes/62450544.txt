Title:
Angel's Chille Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/464472.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Tomatilla's
1 large onion
8 garlic cloves
3 roma tomatoes
3 jalapeno's
2 tbls. lemon juice
1/4 bunch of cilantro
salt to taste
you can also add a large can of crushed tomatoes to bulk up the recipe this is optional and also add's some thickness.  Only add the can after the salsa has been food processed.

Directions:
In a large skillet add oil to coat bottom of pan.  Take the skins off the tomatilla's and garlic.  Add all veggies to the skillet in there whole form.  Do not cut any of the veggies.  Fry in a hot skillet med-hi till the veggies start to blacken.  You may have to pull out the garlic sooner than the other veggies.  In a blender or food processor add all your veggies.  Make sure you cut the end of the jalapenos before processing.  Add your lemon juice salt and cilantro  Pulse till you get the desired consistence.  When finished you can add additional canned tomatoes if you want.  If you want a longer shelf life in fridge add all the salsa to a pot and bring to a boil.  It keeps it from turning an extra week or so.  Add jalapenos, Serrano, red peppers hot sauce to make it the temperature you like.
