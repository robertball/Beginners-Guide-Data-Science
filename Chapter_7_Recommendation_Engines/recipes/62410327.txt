Title:
"Christine's Method" for Mixture for Meatballs, Meatloaf or Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 pounds ground beef
5 cups ground oatmeal
18 eggs
3 cups grated parmessan cheese
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 1/2 cup minced onion
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup and 2 tablespoons ketchup
1 1/2 cup steak sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, oatmeal, eggs, cheese, parsley and onion and season with salt and pepper to taste. For "Christine's method": mix all ingredients together in large bowl. Use largest cookie scoop  I have to make balls for hamburgers.  Use smaller scoop for making meat balls.  When making hamburgers, use two scoops from cookie scoop and forming patty. Package 4 - 5 per freezer bag. For meatballs "Christine's method": use smaller cookie scoop. Scoop into palm and smooth into ball. In a large stock pot, bring to a boil water and add meatballs.  When meatballs begin to float, remove with slotted spoon.  Add to your favorite spaghetti sauce and simmer until meatballs are completely cooked through, about 10 to 15 minutes.
For meatloaf: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Add 3 tablespoons ketchup to mixture, mix well, form into a loaf and place in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 75 to 90 minutes.
