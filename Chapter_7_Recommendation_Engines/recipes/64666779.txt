Title:
Big Ray's Roasted Carrot Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4572745.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium carrots, peeled and chopped into quarters
1 1/2 tsp sweet paprika, divided plus more for seasoning
1/2 cup plus 2 Tbsp olive oil plus more for for drizzilng
Kosher salt
3 cloves garlic, peeled
2 15 oz cans chic peas, drained and rinsed
3 Tbsp Tahini
juice of 2 lemons
1 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Place carrots on rimmed baking sheet with 1/2 paprika, a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of salt.  Roast until soft and tender, about 30 to 40 minutes, adding garlic during last 10 minutes.  Let cool.
Place chic peas in a food processor with olive oil, Tahini, lemon juice, cumin and remaining  paprika.  Salt to taste.  Add 1/4 cup of water and blend until smooth.
Place cooled carrots into food processor and finish blending until smooth.  scoop into bowl and season with paprika and drizzle of olive oil.  Serve with pita chips and sliced veggies.   Hummus with store in airtight container in fridge for up to 7 days, if it last that long during first consumption.
