Title:
Buffalo wing sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Tapatio hot sauce
1 stick unsalted butter
2 tbsp sharp mustard
2 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
1 tbsp honey
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
salt and pepper to tast

Directions:
In a medium saucepan combine the hot sauce, mustard, vinegar and honey. simmer together on medium high heat for 5 minutes. add the garlic and oregano and cover. Reduce heat to medium and let cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Carefully pour the sauce into a blender. Season with salt and pepper and blend on high in 30 second intervals until sauce is smooth and slightly thin, about 4 intervals. Pour the sauce thru a strainer back into the saucepan. Add the butter and stir gently over low heat until the butter is melted.
Remove from the heat and whisk vigerously until the butter is completely incorperated and the sauce has thickened, about 4 minutes more.
Toss with chicken wings or drizzle on chicken pizza
