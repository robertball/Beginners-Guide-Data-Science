Title:
Italian Amore Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/736150.jpg
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS:
1/2 lb lean ground beef
1 egg
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1 cup grated Monterrey Jack cheese
2 tbsp salt
2 tbsp dried parsley
1/4 tsp white pepper
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp black pepper
_______________________________________________
SOUP
2 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, diced
1/2 cup  Vidalia onion, chopped
1 cup scallions, chopped
2 tbsp tomato paste
2 tbsp oregano
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp chicken or vegetable bullion
1/4 tsp ginger
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp white pepper
1 15 oz can cannelloni beans in liquid
1 15 oz can garbanzo beans in liquid
1 1/2 cup cooking wine
4 cups escarole, chopped
1 large tomato, chopped
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
8 cups of water
1 tbsp lemon zest
1/4 cup lemon juice

Directions:
MEATBALLS:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Combine all ingredients thoroughly
Form mixture into 1/2 inch balls and place on a baking sheet.
Bake at about 30 minutes or until tops of meatballs are browned.
_____________________________________________________
SOUP:
Combine olive oil, garlic, onions, scallions, tomato paste, oregano, salt, bullion, ginger, cumin, black pepper, and white pepper in a large pot. Cook on medium high heat until onions are soft, stirring occasionally.
Add cannelloni beans, garbanzo beans, and cooking wine. Boil on high heat for five minutes.
Add escarole, tomato, water and parmesan cheese, lemon zest, lemon juice, and prepared meatballs. Stir and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and cook on low for 2 or more hours. Season with additional salt and pepper if desired.
