Title:
Cucumber Onion Sald Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers, sliced and peeled
1 small onion, cut in small pieces
2 Tb chopped parsley, crushed
2 Tb sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 Tb celery seed, crushed to release flavor
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 Tb lemon juice

Directions:
1. Peel cucumbers and cut into thin slices.
2. Cut onions into bite size pieces
3. Mix other ingredients in a medium sized bowl.
4. Add cucumbers and onions to mix.
5. Refrigerate at least 24 hours.  Flavor improves the longer it is stored in refrigerator.
