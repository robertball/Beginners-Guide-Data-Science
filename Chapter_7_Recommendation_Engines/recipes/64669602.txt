Title:
Carrots with Balsamic Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 carrots, sliced
1 TBSP butter
3 TBSP brown sugar
1 TBSP balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Steam carrot until tender-crisp. Meanwhile, combine butter, sugar and vinegar in a small sauce pan. Cook over medium heat until well blended, 2 - 3 minutes. Combine sauce with steamed carrots and serve.
