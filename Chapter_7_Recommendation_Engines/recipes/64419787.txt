Title:
hamburger with double cheddar cheese, grilled vidala onion and horseradish mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of ground chick
salt and freshly ground pepper
8 slices of white cheddar
8 slices yello cheddar
8 hamburger buns
horsradish mustard
8 leaves romaine lettuce
grilled vidala onions
dill pickles, sliced
ketchup
horseradish  mustard
1/2 c dijon mustard
2 T prepared horseradish
grilled valalia onions
2 vidalia onions, sliced crosswice, 1/4 inch thick slices
2 tbsp olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
preheat grill or cast iron skillet to high
divide beef into 8 burgers, season both sides with salt and pepper, grill for 3-4 minutes on each side - during last minutes of cooking ad 2 slices of cheese to each burger, cover for approx 1 minutes - place burger on bun and top with horserash mustard, lettice, vidalia onions, pickles and ketchup
mustard - whisk mustard and horseradish together in small bowl
onions - brush oilive oil on both sides of the onions and season with salt and pepper - grill the onions for 3-4 minutes on both sides
