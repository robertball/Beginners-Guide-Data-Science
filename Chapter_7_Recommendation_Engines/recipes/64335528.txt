Title:
Smoking Guns BBQ Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c salt, not kosher
1/4 c sugar
1/4 c powdered brown sugar, see below
1/4 c paprika
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp white pepper
2 tsp coarse black pepper
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp coco powder

Directions:
1.  Spread out brown sugar on a baking sheet (w/foil) and bake at 150 for 5 mins to dry out.
2. After drying the brown sugar, move to Vitamix and powder.
3. Combine all ingredients and whisk until well blended.
