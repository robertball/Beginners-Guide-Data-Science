Title:
Chili-Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium red pepper, diced
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tbsp dried cilantro
1 can (7 oz) diced green chiles, drained
1-14 oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
1-14 ounce can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
salt/pepper to taste
few drops Tabasco (optional)
2-14 ounce jars Old El Paso Enchilada sauce
3 cups cubed Velveeta cheese, divided
4 cups crushed white corn tortilla chips

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook beef, onion, red pepper, garlic powder, and cilantro until beef is browned and onions are tender.
Drain any excess liquid.
Add green chiles, beans, salt/pepper, and tabasco, and cook over medium heat until heated through.
Mix in enchilada sauce.
In bottom of a 10x12 pyrex baking dish, spread 2 cups of chip crumbs.
Spoon half of meat/bean mixture over the top.
Sprinkle with half of Velveeta cheese.
Repeat layers with other half of chips, meat mixture, and cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-35 minutes, or until bubbly and cheese is melted.
Serve with sour cream and/or diced tomatoes, if desired.

Submitter: 
Mrs. L
Description: 
Beef, beans, and zesty sauce combine with creamy Velveeta cheese for a mouth-watering meal your whole family will love!
