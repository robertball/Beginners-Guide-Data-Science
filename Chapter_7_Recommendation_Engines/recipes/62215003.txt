Title:
Reindeer Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c margarine
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c brown sugar
1 egg
1 t vanilla
2 1/4 c flour
2 t cream of tartar
1 t baking soda
whole pretzels (antlers)
Green M&Ms (eyes)
Red peanut M&Ms (nose)

Directions:
Cream together margarine and sugars.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well.  Take about 2 T of dough and roll into a ball.  Put on cookie sheet and flatten with a glass dipped in sugar.  Put pretael antlers on top and bake in 350 degree oven for about 6-8 minutes.  Remove from oven and add eyes and nose.  Cool on rack.
