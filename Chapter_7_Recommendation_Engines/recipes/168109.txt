Title:
A.1. Backyard Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985064.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
4 KRAFT 2% Milk American Singles
4 hamburger buns, split, toasted
4 slices  red onion
1/4 cup A.1. Original Steak Sauce

Directions:
PREHEAT grill to medium-high heat. Shape meat into four patties.
PLACE patties on grill; cover grill with lid. Grill 4 to 6 min. on each side or until burgers are cooked through (160 degrees F). Top burgers with 2% Milk Singles; continue grilling until Singles are melted.
FILL buns with cheeseburgers; top with onions and steak sauce.
