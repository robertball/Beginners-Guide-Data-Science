Title:
Na-Na's applesauce muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon  baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1/2 cup oil
1 cup applesauce

Directions:
Mix your dry ingredients together. Then add oil and applesauce. Mix until well blended. Bake in greased mini muffin tins for 12-14 minutes.

Submitter: 
BZBMOMOF3
Description: 
Very moist flavorful mini muffins. MY great grandmas recipe, and growing up this was a family favorite. After taking these to a first grade Halloween party and the kids chose these over cakes and candy, I had to submit it!
