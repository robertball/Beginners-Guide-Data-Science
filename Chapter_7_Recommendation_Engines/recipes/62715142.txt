Title:
Strawberry Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart fresh strawberries
4 tablespoons white sugar
2 gelatin pkgs
6 egg whites
8 oz cool whip

Directions:
Chop hulled strawberries in a food processor, leaving a few chunks.  Stir in sugar and gelatin.   Fold whipped cream into strawberries.  Fold egg whites into strawberry mixture.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Dawnby
Description: 
Fresh strawberries are partially pureed and combined with whipped cream and beaten egg whites for a cool, fluffy, pink dessert.Modified from: Strawberry Mousse
