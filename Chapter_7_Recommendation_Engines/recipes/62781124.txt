Title:
Cowboy Steak Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp finely Ground Coffee
1 Tbsp Kosher Salt
2 tsp  Black Pepper
1 tsp  Chili Powder
1 tsp  Garlic Powder
1 tsp  Ground Thyme
1 tsp  White Sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingedients thoroughly in a small bowl. Apply to both sides of the steak. Cover, chill and let flavors penetrate meat for a minimum of 30 minutes, up to 4-6 hours.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
Great flavoring for any cut of beef done on a pit.
