Title:
Roasted Red Skin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-8 to 10 red skin potatoes
-1/4 cup olive oil
-1tbsp minced garlic
-1 tsp oregano
-1 tsp basil
-salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Cut potatoes into bite sized pieces
2. Combine oil and seasoning in a large mixing bowl. Add potato pieces and stir
3. Bake potatoes on greased cookie sheet at 450 for 25-30 min
*****Works well as a 1 pan meal with shake n bake chicken
