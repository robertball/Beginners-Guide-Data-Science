Title:
Cubanelle and Veal Bolognese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3762250.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil or coconut oil
2 cubanella peppers, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound ground veal
1/2 pound ground chuck
2 teaspoons sea salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
1 tablespoon Italian seasonings

Directions:
Heat a large pan over medium heat.  Add in cubanella peppers and cook for about 5 minutes then add in garlic. Cook for another 30 seconds.
Add in veal and beef then cook until brown; about 10 minutes.  Breaking while cooking by pressing a wooden spoon gently on the meat.  Add in salt, pepper, sauce and Italian Seasonings.  Stir and bring to a boil
Immediately reduce temperature, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
Enjoy with your favorite pasta, spaghetti squash or sweet potato noodles.  Enjoy
