Title:
Grandstand Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1/2 C. Butter
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Brown sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Chocolate chips
1/2 C. Raisins
1/2 C. Peanuts, chopped
1 C. Quick oats
1 Tbsp. Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
In a large mixing bowl combine softened butter and sugars together, Mix together well
Add eggs to mixture and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until mixture is light and creamy
Mix in chocolate chips, oats, chopped peanuts and raisins. Beat on medium speed until dough comes together
Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and coat ingredients evenly with flour. Pour in 1 Tbsp of milk to help keep dough together
Roll out dough in small balls with your hands and place on parchment paper 2 inches apart
Flatten rounds with a fork and place in oven for 10 minutes until light brown

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A cookie that is great for snacking with chocolate chips, peanuts, raisins and oats  that will fill you up for the day.
