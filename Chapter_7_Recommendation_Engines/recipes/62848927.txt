Title:
Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# dried black beans soaked over night.
4 pieces bacon, diced
2# pork shoulder cut into 1/3in cubes
1/4 C olive oil
5 C chicken stock
24oz dark beer (dos equis,Shiner Bock)
1 Large red bell pepper
2 poblano peppers
1 Large yellow onion
22oz canned whole tomatoes
4tbs chopped chipotle in adobo
8 cloves garlic
6 Tbs ground ancho chili powder
3 Tbs ground cumin
2 Tbs dried oregano
3 Tbs masa mixed with 3 Tbs water

Directions:
Drain and set aside beans. Place oil and bacon in large pot over medium heat. Cook until bacon is rendered and crispy. Add pork and cook until brown.
Add peppers,Chilies,onion,garlic and chipotle. Saute until onions are translucent,then add herbs and spices.
Add tomatoes, allow to simmer for 5min while stirring. Place beans,beer,and stock into pot. Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer.
Cook until beans are tender, about two hours. Stir frequently and add more stock if needed. If chili needs to be thickened use masa mixture to do so. Season with salt and pepper.
