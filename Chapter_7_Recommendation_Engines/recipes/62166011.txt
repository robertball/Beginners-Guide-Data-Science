Title:
Stuffed Pizza Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1lb pkg Whole Wheat pizza dough (frozen or fresh) thawed
1c Sauce
1/2c. peppers chopped
1/2c. pepperoni chopped
1c shredded Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
stretch out your dough in a large rectangle.  holes are A O K here!  thinnly spread the sauce, cheese, peppers and pepperoni.  roll the dough into a long 'cigar' shape. carefully lift onto a long baking sheet with the end piece of dough side down.  make small slits in the top of the roll approx 2in apart.  Bake @ 375 for approx 35-40 min.  You will know it is finished when the dough is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling thru the slits..

Submitter: 
WendyWendy
Description: 
I was tired of making the same old pizza for my kids... and now they get to help stuff it... with whatever they can come up with!  This is our simple version.
