Title:
Ang's Cold Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2041690.jpg
Ingredients:
-One bunch fresh broccoli (stems removed), chopped
-One head fresh cauliflower (stem removed), chopped
-One bunch celery, diced
-Medium red onion, diced
-14 ounce package thick sliced sandwich ham, diced into 1/2" cubes
-16 ounce block extra sharp cheddar cheese, diced   into 1/2" cubes
-12 ounce package Rainbow Rotini (tri-colored) spiral noodles
-2.5# boneless, skinless chicken breast
-1 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Boil chicken in large pot until internal temp reaches 165 degrees. Remove chicken from pot. Cube when cooled. Bring broth back to a boil. Add noodles and cook until al dente. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
Place broccoli, cauliflower, celery, red onion, ham, and cheese in a two gallon container. Add cooled chicken, noodles, and mayonnaise. Stir well.
Refrigerate for at least five hours.

Submitter: 
AlabamaAng
Description: 
This is a tasty cold pasta salad that my dear mother created many years ago. She always used two cups of real mayo. To lighten it up a bit, I now use one cup olive oil mayo and it is just as delicious. It's the perfect addition to a potluck!
