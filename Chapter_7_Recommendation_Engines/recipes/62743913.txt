Title:
Lemon Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/630900.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup red onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup celery, chopped
3/4 cup carrots, sliced thinly
1/3 cup red bell pepper, diced
1 cup cooked turkey, diced
2 cups turkey stock, fresh is best, or use low-sodium canned chicken stock
2 cups fresh baby spinach leaves
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
2 tsp grated lemon zest
2 Tbsp fresh squeezed lemon juice
fresh ground black pepper to taste
Pinch of sea salt

Directions:
Heat Olive Oil in a stock pot over medium heat, add onion and carrots and saute for 2 minutes.
Add carrots, celery and red pepper and saute vegetables for about 8-10 minutes or until carrots start to soften.
Add the turkey broth and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
Add turkey meat, lemon juice and lemon zest, stir to combine and simmer for another 5 minutes.
Add spinach leaves, salt and pepper and simmer until spinach wilts and is bright green.
Serve immediately!

Submitter: 
Gitano
Description: 
A wonderful, light soup perfect for the post-holiday turkey leftovers.  The lemon in this recipe makes your palate zing, and it is soooo friendly on the waist-line when you need a 'light' meal after the heaviness of the holidays. Enjoy!
