Title:
Cottage Cheese Salad with Egg and Radish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/899651.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container cottage cheese
1 hard-cooked egg, diced
2 tablespoons fresh chives, finely chopped
3-4 medium radishes, chopped
salt, to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.  Serve.

Submitter: 
TastesLikeChicken
Description: 
My mother made this often when I was growing up.  Lovely for breakfast, brunch or light lunch.  Serve this aside a couple slices of good quality, bakery bread, toasted and buttered.
