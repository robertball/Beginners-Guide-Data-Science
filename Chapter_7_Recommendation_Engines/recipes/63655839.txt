Title:
Cannoli in a Glass Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ricotta cheese
3 T powdered sugar
1 tsp orange zest
1/4 t vanilla
1/8 t cinnamon
2 T chopped bittersweet chocolate
2 Maraschino cherries

Directions:
Beat first five ingredients well (or use food processor).
Fold in chocolate nibs
Spoon into dessert cups or shot glasses
Top with cherry

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Adaptation from Cooking For 2's recipe (altered chocolate to bittersweet).  Light and creamy with just right amount of orange & chocolate.
