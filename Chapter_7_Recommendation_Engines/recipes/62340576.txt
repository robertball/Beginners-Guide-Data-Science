Title:
Bacon & Blue Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 slices bacon
1 small purple onion, thinly sliced
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 cup water
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed, halved and very thinly sliced
1-2 Tbsp cider vinegar (important)
1/2 cup-ish crumbled Gorgonzola cheese

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp.  Drain and crumble when cool.  Rerserve drippings.
Add onion and salt to the drippings in the pan.  Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until tender and browned, about 3-5 minutes.
Add water and mustard and scrape up any browned bits.
Add Brussels sprouts and cook, stirring often, until tender, 4-6 minutes.  Stir in vinegar.
Toss with bacon and gorgonzola.  Serve.
