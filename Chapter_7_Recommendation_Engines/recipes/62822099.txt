Title:
Bobbie's Pigs on a Stick. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666680.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Pork Tenderloin (5oz) cubed
1 Lime Juiced
1 TBS Olive Oil
1/4 tsp Dried Rosemary
1 Clove of Garlic finely chopped
1/3 Pineapple cut into chunks
1 Mango Cubed
8 Shallots cut in half

Directions:
Mix the Lime Juice, Olive Oil, Garlic & Rosemary in a bowl.
Add the Pork Cubes and refrigerate for 2 hours.
Skewer the marinaded Pork, Shallots, Pineapple & Mango onto metal skewers alternating each item along the skewer.
Cook Kebabs over a medium heated grill for approx 12-15 minutes turning frequently until pork is fully cooked.

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
Pineapple, Mango, Pork & Shallots skewered to make tasty Kebabs!
This recipe is Part 1 of my Daughters creation for the Summer 2011 Gang-Up Challenge!
