Title:
Horseradish Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/51380.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
2 tablespoons steak sauce
3/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
8 hamburger buns, split

Directions:
In a bowl, combine beef, steak sauce and seasoned salt; mix well. Shape into 16 patties. In a small bowl, combine cream cheese, horseradish and mustard. Spoon about 1 tablespoonful into the center of half of the patties; top with remaining patties. Press edges to seal.
Grill, uncovered, over medium-hot heat for 10 minutes on each side or until meat is no longer pink. Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Chris  Anderson
Description: 
'My husband and I love to grill burgers year-round,' relates Chris Anderson of Morton, Illinois. 'This variation with a creamy and flavorful filling is a bit with our family and friends.'
