Title:
Gorgonzola Sage Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup salted butter
5oz crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
2 Tablespoons of chopped fresh sage

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and mix with a fork until mixed thoroughly
We use about a tablespoon per serving.

Submitter: 
rfadler
Description: 
We had this as a topping for a pork chop at a restaurant and though we can make this. It's great as a topping for pork chops or steaks.
