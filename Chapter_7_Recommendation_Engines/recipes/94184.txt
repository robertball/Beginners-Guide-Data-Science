Title:
Quick Chili Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988766.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked white rice
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL速 Chili No Beans
1 (15 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
1 1/2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese, divided
1/2 cup chopped green onions
6 MANNY'S速 8-inch Fajita Style Flour Tortillas, warmed
Chopped lettuce
Sour cream
Salsa

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. In large bowl, combine rice, chili, diced tomatoes,1 cup shredded cheese and green onion. Fill each tortilla with chili mixture. Fold burrito-style. Place on baking sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until hot. Serve with lettuce, remaining cheese, sour cream and salsa.
