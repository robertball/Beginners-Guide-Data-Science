Title:
Lori's Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/540973.jpg
Ingredients:
•3 cups shredded Sharp cheddar cheese
•½ cup minced onion
•1 can cream of chicken soup
•16 oz sour cream
•¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
•1 teaspoon salt
•32 oz frozen hash browns
•4 cups crushed corn flakes
•½ cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Coat a 13 x 9 baking dish with 1 tablespoon of butter.
In a large bowl, mix together the first 6 ingredients, adding in the hash browns last.
Place mixture into baking dish.
Mix crushed corn flakes and melted butter and place on top.
Bake in the preheated for 1 ½ hours.

Submitter: 
Lori Bachner Carey
Description: 
This recipe is great with Honey baked ham!
