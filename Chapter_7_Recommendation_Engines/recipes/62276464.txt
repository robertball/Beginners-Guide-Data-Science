Title:
Slow Cooker Creamed Chicken & Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/348045.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large Chicken Breasts
1 (8oz) can mushrooms, pieces & stems, drained
2 (10 3/4oz) cans condensed Cream of Mushroom Roasted Garlic soup
1 (10 3/4oz) can condensed Cream of Chicken soup
2/3 cup milk
3 tbsp butter,cut into pieces
1 tsp dried Italian seasoning blend
Cooked Pasta or Rice

Directions:
In a large slow cooker, spray non-stick cooking spray. Place chicken in the bottom, then place mushrooms on top of chicken.
In a medium size bowl, mix together soups, milk, butter and seasoning.
Pour mixture over chicken and mushrooms.
Cook on high for 5 to 6 hours. Removed chicken from sauce and slice chicken. Return to sauce. Serve over your favorite cooked pasta or rice.
