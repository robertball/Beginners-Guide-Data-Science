Title:
Cinnamon Sugar Tortilla Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 inch) flour tortilla
2 Tsp Land O Lakes Cinnamon Sugar Butter Spread

Directions:
Spread tortilla with butter-flavored spread; sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Roll into a tube shape, then place on a microwave safe plate. Microwave on High for 30 seconds to warm.

Submitter: 
sixgermans
Description: 
I learned this cinnamon tortilla recipe in college. Makes a sweet, low fat and filling treat.  Best of all it's made in the microwave!Modified from: Cinnamon Sugar Tortilla Delight
