Title:
Ravioli's With Roasted Garlic & Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535578.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Butoni ravioli's (or any brand LIGHT cheese ravioli's) These are in the refrigerator section at the store.
2 small zucchini-cut into bite size pieces
1 onion- sliced
2 carrots-peeled and cut into bite side pieces
10 asparagus spears
hand full of cauliflower and broccoli cut into pieces
1 whole head of garlic cut in 1/2
olive oil
salt/pepper
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place all the vegetables onto a cookie sheet, including the whole head of garlic. Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 30 minutes on 400 degrees.
Scoop out the garlic cloves, chop any if needed, and discard the bulb.
Put all the vegetables into a large bowl, with garlic and toss.
Cook ravioli according to package directions, drain, and add to the veggies. Drizzle with olive oil and toss well.
