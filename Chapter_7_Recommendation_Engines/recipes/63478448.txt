Title:
Skin on potato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups left over skin-on mashed potatoes
4 cups cubed ham
2 cups milk
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
Add left over mashed potatoes with milk and ham in a pot, simmer on medium until warm. Salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with cheese, bacon, fried onions, etc.

Submitter: 
Moonharehayashi
Description: 
This is a recipe I use for left overs. My family hates eating the same food the next day, so we take the left overs to make something new. How many recipes can you make out of mashed potatoes?
