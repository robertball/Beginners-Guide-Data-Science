Title:
Frozen Blueberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6392961.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen blueberries
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup Greek yogurt
1 kiwi, peeled
2 teaspoons raw sugar

Directions:
Blend blueberries, milk, yogurt, kiwi, and sugar together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
lanita99
Description: 
This is a great recipe for a hot day. For a thicker texture, use cream instead of milk. You can substitute 1 packet of stevia for the sugar.
