Title:
Country Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stewing chicken (or canned chicken)
1/3 c butter
1/8 c flour (more if needed)
1 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/2 t thyme
1/2 t rosemary
2 c chicken broth
1 piecrust
1 potato, cubed and boiled until tender
2 carrots, sliced and boiled until tender
1 c light cream
1 sml can onions
1 sml can peas

Directions:
Preheat oven 450
Simmer chicken in water to cover for 45 minutes, or until tender.
Remove meat from bones and reserve stock.
Cube meat.
Melt butter in saucepan.
Stir in flour, salt, pepper, thyme and rosemary to make gravy.
Gradually add broth and cream and cook over medium heat, stirring frequently until thickened and bubbly.
Add chicken and vegetables.
Prepare the pie crust.
Line a 9 x 13 pan or 2 qt casserole with 2/3 of crust.
Put the filling in the pan.
Top with remaining crust.
Bake 15 minutes, or until crust is golden and filling is bubbling.
