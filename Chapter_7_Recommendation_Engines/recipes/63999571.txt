Title:
Easy Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tb
2 Tb flour
2 TB chili powder
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 1/2 cups water
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp teaspoon garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
salt to taste
a few splashes of hot sauce to taste (optional)

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a saucepan on medium/low heat. Whisk in the flour and chili powder. Gradually add the water & tomato sauce, stirring constantly.  Add the remaining ingredients and continue to stir until sauce has thickened, about 8-10minutes.
If you want to take an extra few minutes, add 1/2 cup chopped onion & 2 cloves of garlic, minced.  Just sautee in the oil until translucent, before adding the flour and chili powder.
