Title:
Buttercream-Coconut Cake Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/449853.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter, Softened (1 Stick)
1/2 Cup All-Vegatable Shortening
3 Cups Confectionary Sugar, Sifted
1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
2 Tablespoons Milk (More Or Less As Needed)
7 Oz. Sweetened Coconut Flakes
1 Handful Chopped Pecans

Directions:
Cream together the shortening and butter with an electric mixer until smooth.
Add vanilla. Gradually add in sugar one cup at a time and beat with an electric until well blended, the mixture should appear dry.
Add milk and beat until desired consistency. Reserve half of the coconut flakes and fold in the rest.
Ice your cake when it is completely cooled and sprinkle the reserved coconut flakes and pecans on to it. Serve and enjoy!
