Title:
Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2215081.jpg
Ingredients:
1 chicken carcass or 1 lb. equivalent
3 bugs bunny carrots, sliced
3 stalks celery, sliced
1 tbsp crushed garlic
water from the tap
1 package frz egg noodles

Directions:
In a large stock pot or crock pot, brown the garlic, then add water to cover the chicken. Boil the chicken carcass and simmer about one hour or until meal falls away from the bone.
Remove carcass to carving board and use tongs to remove meat from bone in bite size pieces, adding back to pot of chicken broth. Discard bones.
Continue soup stock on low heat, adding sliced or chopped carrots and celery. Simmer for a few hours.
About one hour out from serving, add the noodles to heat. Don't add to soon or they'll over cook.

Submitter: 
Donna Carlson
Description: 
Best if this follows roasted chicken night with garlic and herbs - great base for your chicken broth.
