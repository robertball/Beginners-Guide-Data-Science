Title:
CHOCOLATE CRUNCH COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 CUPS BUTTER
1 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1 CUP GRANULATED SUGAR
2 EGGS
1 TSP. VANILLA
2 CUPS FLOUR
1 TSP BAKING POWDER
1 TSP BAKING SODA
1 TSP SALT
2 CUPS OATS
2 CUPS RICE KRISPIES CEREAL
1-1/2 CUPS CHOCOLATE CHIPS
1/2 CUP CHOPPED PECANS

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350
MIX TOGETHER BUTTER, SUGARS,
ADD EGGS AND VANILLA
MIX UNTIL WELL BLENDED.
ADD REMAINING INGREDIENTS:
FLOUR, BAKIN POWDER, BAKING
SODA, SALT, OATS, RICE KRISPIES,
CHOCOLATE CHIPS AND PECANS AND
STIR TOGETHER.
DROP BATTER ON BAKING SHEETS IN
MOUNDS OF ABOUT 3 TBSP. 3 INCHES
APART, AND FLATTEN WITH HAND,
BAKE 8 TO 9 MINUTES OR UNTIL
PALE GOLD.

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
A RICE KRISPIES COOKIE RATHER THAN A SQUARE.  MAKE THEM EXTRA BIG FOR A REAL COFFEE HOUSE LOOK.
