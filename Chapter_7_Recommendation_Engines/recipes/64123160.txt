Title:
Low-fat Cream Cheese Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. margarine
1 8oz. pkg low fat cream cheese, room temperature
2 C. all-purpose flour
1/2 C. fruit only preserves
1/4 C. powdered sugar

Directions:
Beat margarine and cream cheese in a large bowl until smooth. Add flour and beat until well blended. Divide dough into fourths. Shape each section into a rectangle and flatten to 1 inch thickness. Wrap and refrigerate overnight. Preheat oven to 350. Have cookie sheets ready. Work with one portion of dough at a time. On a lightly floured surface with floured rolling pin, roll dough to 10x7 1/2 rectangle. cut into 2 1/4 inch squares. Place squares 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheet. For pinwheels drop 1/2 tsp of preserves in center of each square. Starting from tips slit corners of each square 1 inch towards the center. Fold over other half corner to center like a pinwheel, overlapping tips. Pinch firmly in center. Bake 13-15 minutes until tips are lightly browned. Cool on rack then sprinkle with powdered sugar.
