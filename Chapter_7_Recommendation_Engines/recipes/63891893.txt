Title:
Hot Artichoke Hearts and Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T butter, divided
1 cup finely minced button mushrooms
2 cloves garlic finely minced
1/2 cup finely minced shallots
3/4 cup mayonnaise
3/4 + 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
3/4 + 1/4 cup shredded smoked mozzarella
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 14 oz can artichoke hearts, roughly chopped
6-8 oz lump crab meat
3 green onion, minced
1/4 + 1/4 cup minced flat leaf parsley
1/2 tsp Old Bay Seasoning
1/2 cup Panko bread crumbs
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Butter a 1-2 quart baking dish.
Over medium-low heat, melt 2 T. butter in small sauce pan. Add mushrooms, garlic, and shallots.  Cook about 5 minutes or until tender.  Remove from heat and set aside.
In medium mixing bowl combine mayonnaise, 3/4 cup parmesan, 3/4 cup mozzarella and heavy cream.  Stir in artichoke hearts, crab meat, green onion, and 1/4 cup parsley, and Old Bay Seasoning.  Add mushroom, garlic, shallot mixture and blend all well.  Turn out into buttered casserole.
Topping:  Mix remaining 1/4 cup parmesan, 1/4 cup mozzarella, 1/4 cup parsley, and Panko crumbs.  Spread evenly over top of dish, melt remaining butter and drizzle over top of crumb mixture.  Cook 20-30 minutes or until golden and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
This takes all the elements of several hot dips and turns them into one incredible dip for tortilla chips, crackers, or hearty bread slices.
