Title:
What the ELLE...Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup cornmeal
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1/4 cup milk
3 teaspoons+ apple sauce
1 teaspoon of oil for pan

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the flour, cornmeal, baking powder and salt. In another bowl, beat the egg, milk and 3 teaspoons apple sauce; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Heat oil in a heavy 8-in. skillet over low heat. Pour batter into the hot skillet; cover and cook for 4-5 minutes. Turn and cook 4 minutes longer or until golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
Like a puffy pancake...has the easy-to-cut texture of conventional corn bread. It complements everything from chicken to chili.Modified from: Skillet Corn Bread
