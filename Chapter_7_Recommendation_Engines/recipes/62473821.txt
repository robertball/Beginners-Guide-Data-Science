Title:
Warm Mexican Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz thick-sliced bacon
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 stick butter, softened
2 (15.25 ounce) cans white corn, drained
2 (14 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with green chile peppers

Directions:
Slice the bacon strips crosswise into matchsticks; fry until crispy; reserve grease
Combine the bacon, 2 tablespoons of the bacon grease, cream cheese, butter, corn, and tomatoes with green chile peppers in a slow cooker.
Set the slow cooker to Low. Cook until the cream cheese and butter melt completely, about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
This is a delicious, easy, and creamy dip perfect for serving with tortilla chips.Modified from: Warm Mexican Corn Dip
