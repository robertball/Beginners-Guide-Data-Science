Title:
Pintxo Gilda (Basque Pepper, Olive, and Anchovy Skewers) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5772083.jpg
Ingredients:
12 green Manzanillo olives, pitted
12 short skewers
12 oil-packed anchovy fillets
12 pepperoncini

Directions:
Thread 1 olive onto a skewer. Thread the end of 1 anchovy fillet onto the skewer and add a piece of pepperoncini. Thread the other end of the anchovy fillet onto the skewer, wrapping around the pepper. Repeat with remaining olives, anchovies, and pepperoncini.

Submitter: 
barbara
Description: 
Gilda skewers are one of the most famous Basque pintxos (tapas). In Spain, they are made with anchovies, mancanilla olives (ideally stuffed with anchovies), and pickled piparra peppers, a Basque pepper, which are hard to find here. Use pepperoncini instead.
