Title:
Apple Pie Caramel Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 apples
1 (14 ounce) package caramels
2 tablespoons milk
1 (12 ounce) bag white chocolate chips
1 cup brown sugar
2 Tbsp. ground cinnamon
6 wooden sticks

Directions:
Wash and dry apples thoroughly.
Put the wooden sticks in the apples.
Mix together brown sugar and cinnamon.
Melt the caramels and milk either over low heat or in the microwave.
Dip each apple into the caramel and let firm up completely.
Melt the white chocolate chips in microwave.
Dip each apple in chocolate and let the excess drip off.
Let cool slightly, but before its completely hard, roll in the sugar mixture.
Sit on wax paper and put in fridge to harden completely.

Description: 
A delicious apple that tastes like and apple pie on a stick.---Jessica
