Title:
Key Lime Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12-14 Keylimes
10 med tomatoes
2 bunches cilantro
2 medium white or yellow onions
4-6 fresh jalepenos
4-6 tblspns chopped garlic
Salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Easiest thing in world.
Juice Key Limes.
Start chopping all ingredients and place in food processor.
Pulse until fine chop.
Pour into large bowl.
Continue in batches till all done.
Add Key Lime Juice.
Add Salt and pepper to taste.
Best when refrigerated 2 hours or overnight.
Gets better with age.

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
Fresh, clean key lime sweetens tradional salsa/pico recipes and gives a one of kind what is that question to eager guests!
