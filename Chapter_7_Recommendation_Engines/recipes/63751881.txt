Title:
Training Camp Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bananas (3 cups)
3 sticks margarine
3 eggs
3 cups sugar
1 cup milk
1 cup yogurt
6 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 tsp baking powder
1 cup mini chocolate chips, if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350, spray 5 loaf pans.
In a large bowl mix bananas and margarine(margarine will remain in pieces).  Add sugar, eggs, milk, and yogurt. Blend well.  Add flour, soda, salt & powder. Mix until combined.  Stir in chocolate chips, if desired.  Spoon into prepared pan, and bake 55-65 minutes.  Cool 10 minutes in pans before removing.

Submitter: 
redamber
Description: 
We are supporters of our local pro hockey team, and I help feed the players at training camp.  I have made this bread for several years and everyone LOVES this bread.  I get requests for the recipe every time I bring it somewhere.  It freezes very well too!
