Title:
chicken ragout - slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 serving chicken bullion with 8 oz boiling water
1 tbsp butter
1 tbsp garlic - minced
1 tbsp parsley paste
1/2 tsp ground pepper
2 chicken breasts - frozen
1/2 large onion - diced
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup - low fat (with 8 oz boiling water to empty can)
6 oz baby carrots
8 oz cut green beans (fresh or frozen & thawed)
1 tbsp corn starch (mixed with 4 oz cold water as slurry)

Directions:
In order listed add all except last 3 ingredients to crock pot
Cook on high for 4 hours
Shred chicken, add remaining ingredients & cook for 1 or 2 more hours
Serve with toast or over wild rice
