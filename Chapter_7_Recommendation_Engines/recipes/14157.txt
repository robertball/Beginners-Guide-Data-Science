Title:
Tomato Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/850666.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 cucumber, peeled and diced
1 onion, chopped
1 tablespoon lemon juice
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions in a salad bowl. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.  Sprinkle with lemon juice.  Chill.
