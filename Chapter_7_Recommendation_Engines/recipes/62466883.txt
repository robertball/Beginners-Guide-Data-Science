Title:
No Cream of Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sweet onion or red onion, coarsly chopped into crescents
1 t. minced garlic
1 C vegetable or wine or chicken broth
1 t. sugar substitute
1 # chopped mushrooms
I had omitted the following spices:
1/2 t. dried sage
1 t. tarragon
1/3 t. nutmeg
Additional broth
1T balsamic vinegar
salt and pepper to taste
sour cream for richness

Directions:
Cook onions, garlic, broth and sugar until onions are tender, about 15 min. Add mushrooms; cook 10 min. Add spices and addition broth to desired thickness, about 1-3 cups more and simmer 10-20 min. Add vinegar and salt and pepper to taste. Add sour cream to your liking.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This recipe has been changed drasticly from the tofu ingredient recipe. I used if in a broccoli rice casserole dish and it tasted pretty good. But the taste was deluted with the other ingredients.
