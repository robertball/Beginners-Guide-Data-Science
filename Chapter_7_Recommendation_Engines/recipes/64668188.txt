Title:
Rosie's Cozy Hambergers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs, lightly beaten, 2 cups soft bread crumbs (fresh bread chopped finely) 2 tsp. salt, 1 tsp. pepper, 4 Lbs. ground beef, 4 -8oz. cans tomato sauce, 4 Tblsp. chopped green onions, 4 Tblsp. Brown sugar,4 Tsp. Worchester sauce, 4 Tsp. prepared mustard. 16 Hamburger Buns.

Directions:
Combine ground beef, eggs and bread crumbs salt and pepper, mix well by hand.
once mixed form into 16 patties.
BROWN, the patties  in skillet on both sides (do not cook thru) , then remove them and set aside.
Drain skillet, combine the rest of the ingredients in the skillet .
return patties to the sauce.
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
You can add cheese, lettuce and tomatoes and onion  when you serve them if you like.

Local fad during the 60's.  A lot of variants for this. But I think this is the real Deal. This is for 16 burgers.
