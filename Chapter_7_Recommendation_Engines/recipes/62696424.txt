Title:
Dumplings for Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 cup milk
2 Tbsp canola oil
1/8 tsp salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.
In a another bowl combine milk and oil.
Pour liquid mixture into dry mixture and combine.
Drop batter by heaping tablespoonful onto completely cooked stew.
Cover and simmer for 12 minutes (do not lift lid).

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
My husband thinks that no beef stew is complete without a huge amount of these super-simple dumplings on top.  I almost always have to double the recipe for him!
