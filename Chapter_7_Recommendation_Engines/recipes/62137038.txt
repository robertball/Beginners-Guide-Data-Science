Title:
Oyster h'ors d'ouevre Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12-15 Ritz crackers
1 Tin of smoked oysters
1 tub soft cream cheese

Directions:
Drain oysters.
Spread each cracker with cream cheese then top with an oyster.
Garnish with fresh parsley sprig.......if you like.

Submitter: 
CraZee A
Description: 
I just eat this for a snack, but it could be dressed up for a party with a nice presentation.  I'm sure the cooks here will think of something ;-)  It may sound weird but it tastes good.
