Title:
Sayur Lodeh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp peanut or vegetable oil
1 onion, sliced
2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
2 heaping teaspoons of sambal djahe
2 handfuls cleaned and broken green beans
1 handful cleaned and halved snow peas
1 knorr vegetable bouillon block
1/2 pound of shrimp (I used size 31/40 frozen peeled, tail-on, and rinsed and de-tailed them)
1/2 a green cabbage, chopped into 2-inch squares
1-1 1/2 cups of water
80 ml coconut milk
1 envelope Bamboe Lodeh spices (vegetable stew spices)

Directions:
Heat oil and saute onion until onion is translucent (clear)
Add garlic and sambal and stir
Add green beans and snowpeas and stir
Add bouillon block
Add shrimp and saute until shrimp turn pink
Add green cabbage and stir until cabbage is coated with moisture
Add spice envelope (lodeh) and stir
Add water until vegetables are HALF covered then add coconut milk
Simmer for 3 minutes

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
I love this recipe. I make it different than my other sajoer lodeh dish. It has more liquid, which makes it more like a stew.
