Title:
Sweet Artistry Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/419051.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs shortening
3 lbs margarine, softened
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 lbs confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, shortening, and vanilla. Blend in the sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in the milk, and continue mixing until light and fluffy. Keep icing covered until ready to decorate.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
An easy to make egg-free frosting that works great for decorating cookies for holidays, birthdays and parties.Modified from: Buttercream Icing
