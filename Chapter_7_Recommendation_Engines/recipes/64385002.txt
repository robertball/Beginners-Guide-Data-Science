Title:
Sweet potato & Avocado salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2212058.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sweet potato
1 avocado
1 tbs olive oil
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp original mrs.dash
1 tsp Parmesan
Pinch of pepper

Directions:
Cut up sweet potato into bite size pieces and steam until you can pierce with a fork (5-7 mins)
while sweet potato is cooking dice up your avocado and put in a large bowl. Add in olive oil, garlic powder, mrs.dash & pepper and mix together.
When sweet potato is cooked, add it with the avocado mixture and combine. Sprinkle with Parmesan & serve
