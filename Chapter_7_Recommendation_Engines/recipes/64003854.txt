Title:
Herb Compound Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1111151.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
3 green onions, chopped fine
1/3 cup flat leaf parsley, chopped fine
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and beat to combine.
Form the butter (in a log, with a melon baller, or even a cookie cutter).
Refrigerate to firm it up, or freeze for later use.

Submitter: 
cooks4forty
Description: 
Compound butters can dress up your bread, your potatoes, even your steak!  This one is bright and fresh with parsley and green onions.
