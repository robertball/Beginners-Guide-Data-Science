Title:
cream cheese spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package dried beef, rinsed and shredded
2 blocks of cream cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup chopped pecans
2 tablespoons dried onions
garlic powder to taste (or garlic salt)
butter

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
saute pecans with butter and garlic salt
mix soft cream cheese with sour cream and dried onions. Mix in sauted pecans and dried beef.
put in baking dish and bake for about 20 min

Christine Douglas- Kaiser
Description: 
serve with hard bread or crackers
