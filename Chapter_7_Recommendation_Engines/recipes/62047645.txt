Title:
Yummy Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz pkg cream cheese
1 300g pkg Skor bits
1 384g container of caramel apple dip(in produce section)
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Cream together brown sugar & cream cheese & spread in 9-10" serving plate.
Spread on the caramel dip,then the skor bits.
Serve with apple slices(bananas,cookies....)YUMMY!!!

Submitter: 
Deb Hughes Cormier
Description: 
This easy & yummy recipe is a hit with kids & adults,uses few ingredients & takes no time to prepare.The flavours & textures blend deliciously & make it hard to stop eating once you start.
