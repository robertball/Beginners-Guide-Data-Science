Title:
Pineapple Pecan Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) pkg Cream Cheese
1 (3 1/2 oz) can Crushed Pineapple, drained
2 tbsp Green Peppers, chopped finely
1 tsp Lawry's Season Salt
1 1/2 - 2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
On a small cookie sheet lay out the pecans and set aside.
Mix together cream cheese, pineapple, green pepper and season salt.
Shape cheese mixture into a ball, and gently roll into the pan with pecans, until completely covered. Reshape as you go to form a ball or a log shape.
Refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours. Serve with crackers, thick chips or even crisp cookies

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
This fruity, nutty cheesy throw-together is a great addition to a party table full of delitable delights. Quick easy and just plain simple and tasty.
