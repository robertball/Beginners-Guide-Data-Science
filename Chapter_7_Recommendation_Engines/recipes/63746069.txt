Title:
Cool and Creamy Southern Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1039651.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C shredded cabbage
1/2 C diced cucumber
1/2 C mayonaise
3 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
2 Tbsp onion, minced
1 Tbsp white sugar
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp celery seed

Directions:
In small mixing bowl whisk together all ingredients except for cabbage.
Place cabbage in a large bowl.  Toss cabbage with dressing.  Refrigerate 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
After moving to the South 11 years ago, one of the first things I learned was how to make great coleslaw.  This recipe is slightly sweet, tangy- and delicious.  A cool and refreshing side dish that always receives compliments.
