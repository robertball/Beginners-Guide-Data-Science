Title:
Super Sunday Salsa Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken wings
1 (16 ounce) jar Newman's Own All-Natural Bandito Salsa, Medium
1/2 cup apple juice
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar

Directions:
Cut wings into 3 sections, discarding wing tips. Combine all ingredients in a 2-quart slow cooker. Cook on low for 4 to 6 hours.
