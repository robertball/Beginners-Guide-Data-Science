Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups sugar
1 lb butter, softened
3 cups flour
powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix eggs & butter.  Add sugar to mixture and blend.  Add vanilla and blend.  Add flour and blend.
Grease bundt pan.  Sprinkle with flour.  Put mix in pan.
Bake at 300 degrees for 1 hour.
Cool completely before cutting off rough edges and flipping onto a plate.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
