Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 brick of low fat cream cheese
1 large stalk of celery
1 small container of blue cheese crumbles
1.5 c. of buffalo wing sauce
1/2 tbs. red cayenne pepper
1 tbs. Emeril's Original Essence
Salt to taste
Ranch or Blue Cheese dressing to taste

Directions:
Allow cream cheese to soften.
In a food processor, grind up one celery stalk and can of chicken. Dump contents into a medium bowl.
Add blue cheese crumbles, buffalo sauce, red pepper, essence and cream cheese. Stir until mixed.
Salt to taste. If dip is too thick, it can be thinned with low fat ranch, blue cheese dressing or more buffalo sauce.
Garnish with a sprinkle of essence and celery leaves.
Tastes best if chilled first to allow spices to mingle.

Submitter: 
mkecupcakequeen
Description: 
A mild, but kicky party dip that borrows all those luscious flavors of your favorite buffalo wings, without all of the mess! Serve with celery sticks, tortilla chips and peanuts. For more kick, add more spice!
