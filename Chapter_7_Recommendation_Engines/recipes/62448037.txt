Title:
Roasted Chicken Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090965.jpg
Ingredients:
1	tablespoon kosher salt
1	tablespoon paprika
1	tablespoon garlic powder
1	tablespoon onion powder
1	tablespoon thyme
½	tablespoon ground black pepper
½	tablespoon rosemary
2	teaspoons sage
½	teaspoon ground red pepper (cayenne)
1	tablespoon celery flakes

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well using a spoon.  Then put into a spice grinder (coffee mill used only for spices) and blend until smooth.
