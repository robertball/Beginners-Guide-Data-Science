Title:
Coca Cola Asian  Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs flanken ribs
1/2 cup soy
1/2 cup brown sugar
3 T sesame oil
3 T garlic chopped
3 T rice vinegar
2 green onions chopped or a shallot chopped
1/2 tsp of red pepper flakes
1 cup of coca cola

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl being sure to add cola last. It foams. Pour marinade on beef and cover and refridge for at least 4 hours or more. Place ribs on hot grill for 3-4 minutes or until the ribs are at your desired temp.
