Title:
LTF DTox: Cocnut Curry with Chicken and Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Organic chicken breast, cubed
1 tbsp. Organic coconut oil
1/2 cup Broccoli
1 cup Spinach
1/2 cup Carrots
Red, yellow or green bell pepper - 1 pepper
1/2 cup Bok choy
1/2 cup cooked Quinoa
Curry Ingredients:
1 cup water
13.5 oz can unsweetened coconut milk
1/2 cup almond butter
1 tbsp. tomato paste
1 tbsp. mild curry powder
2 cloves garlic, minced
Ginger, to taste

Directions:
1. Heat coconut oil in a skillet; saut√© the cubed chicken until no longer pink in the middle.
2. In a medium saucepan, heat all "Curry Ingredients" while whisking regularly; cook to desired thickness, adding water if necessary to thin.
3. Steam the Broccoli, Spinach, Carrots, Bell pepper and Bok choy (be careful not to overcook!)
4. Mix Chicken, steamed Vegetables, cooked Quinoa and Curry sauce and serve.
