Title:
Leftover Italian Wedding Soup - Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover Italian Wedding Soup
3-4 potatoes
Margarin or butter
Paprika
Carrots (cut) - if your Italian Wedding Soup did not contain carrots
Peas
Corn Kernels
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Boil Potatoes.
In the meantime: Drain liquid from soup and keep mixture in a bowl.
Add (boiled or can) carrots and peas and mix with the drained soup.
Add egg and stir.
Grease pan (size depends on how much soup you have left over. I used an 8x8 pyrex)
Pat mixture down into pan.
Mash potatoes, add butter or margarin, mix until fluffy.
Top pan (with mixture) with corn kernels and then the mashed potato.
Sprinkle (a nice amount) of Paprika
Bake for 45 mins - 1 hour or until slightly browned on top.

Submitter: 
Suzanne G.
Description:
