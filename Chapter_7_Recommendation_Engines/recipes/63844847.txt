Title:
Big Ray's Hearty Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068034.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh, ground pork sausage
1 pound fresh, ground spicy pork sausage
3 (8 ounce) package Neutchatel cheese
2 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
1 /2 tsp granulated onion
hot sauce, optional

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown sausage. Drain excess fat.
Cut cream cheese blocks into small cubes. Place the cream cheese, tomatoes, onion and sausage into a slow cooker. Heat on high, stirring continually, until it starts to bubble, then turn to low. Add hot sauce to reach desired flavor if you really want it HOT!

Submitter:

Description: 
Caution!! This dip is extremely addictive.  One taste and you won't leave the crock until it's all gone.
