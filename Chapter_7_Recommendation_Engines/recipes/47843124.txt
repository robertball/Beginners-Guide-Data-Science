Title:
BLUEBERRY BRIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Brie cheese
8 ounces Blueberry  Sauce (recipe below)
1	16 ounce can of Blueberry Pie Filling
1/4	Cup of Grand Marnier
Lemon Zest (Optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place Brie cheese in a medium baking dish. Slather with Blueberry Sauce.
3. Bake in the preheated oven 10 to 12 minutes, or until warm and melted. (May also be warmed in microwave for approx. 3 mins.)
Serve with warm French baguette slices and/or crackers.
Blueberry Sauce:Slowly heat Pie filling. Gently fold in Grand Marnier and lemon zest being careful not to mash the blueberries. Thin mixture with water as desired.
