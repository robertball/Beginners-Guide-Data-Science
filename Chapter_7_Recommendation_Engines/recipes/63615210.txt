Title:
Easy cheesy basil fried rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked wild rice mix (plain rice is fine too)
1 tablespoon butter
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon veggie stock powder (sub: chicken stock if not vegetarian)
3 tablespoons water
3 tablespoons Grana Padano or other grated cheese
half a handful of fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Heat butter in a frying pan.
Throw in the rice and stir-fry for a minute.
Add garlic powder and stir-fry for 20 seconds.
Add stock powder and water. Mix well.
Sprinkle cheese all over the top.
Remove from heat and dd basil. Mix well and serve. Sprinkle with ground sea salt if required.

Submitter: 
argus
Description: 
It's basically a leftovers recipe put together on a rainy day for a quick, satisfying lunch because I had leftover rice and two basil plants that needed a bit of harvesting.
