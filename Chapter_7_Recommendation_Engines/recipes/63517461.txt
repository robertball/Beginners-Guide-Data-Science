Title:
Pepper Jack Beer fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup light beer
1 small tomato, chopped
2 cups shredded pepper jack cheese
1 cup shredded Monterrey jack cheese
1 tsp cumin
1 1/2 tbs flour
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1 finely chopped Serrano pepper

Directions:
Place chopped tomato in a sieve and drain excess juice.
In a big bowl toss cheese, Cumin and flour.
Pour beer into a fondue pot and  bring to a boil for 2 minutes. Add cheese mixture by handfuls stirring until melted.  Fold in tomatoes and cilantro and optional Serrano. . Reduce heat to warm. Serve with desired dippers.
