Title:
Really Simple Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 cup uncooked long grain white rice
1/2 a small onion - finely diced
2 cups low sodium chicken broth
garlic salt to taste

Directions:
In a medium non stick pan - heat the olive oil over medium high heat. Just before it starts to smoke, add the rice. Stir constantly for about 2-3 minutes until rice is toasty. Add the onion and continue to stir constantly for 1 more minute. Add the chicken broth. Add the garlic salt - start with a little and taste the broth. Add more if you like. Bring to a boil. As soon as it boils lower flame to very low. Cover and cook for 20 minutes. Turn off the heat and allow 5 minutes before you lift the lid. Serve with your favorite entree.

Submitter: 
Mark S.
Description: 
This is a very simple but tasty rice recipe. Makes a great side dish and leftovers are almost as good the next day.
