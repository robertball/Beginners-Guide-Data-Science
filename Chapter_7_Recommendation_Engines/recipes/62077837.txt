Title:
Basic Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup lukewarm water
pinch of sugar
1-1/4 oz pkg active dry yeast (2 1/2 tsp)
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 1/3 tsp kosher salt
about 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
1. Combine water and sugar. Sprinkle in yeast and let stand 5 mins until foamy.
2. Combine 3 cups flour and salt. Gradually stir in yeast and 1 tbsp + 1 tsp olive oil until a stiff dough has formed. Knead until smooth and elastic. Let dough rise overnight or for a few hrs until doubled in size (1 1/2 hrs).
