Title:
MeMe's Turkey Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon low-sodium Vegetable broth
1/2 gallon low-sodium Chicken Broth
1 gallon of apple juice
2 quarts water
1 cups sea Salt
3 tablespoons poultry seasoning
3 tablespoons onion powder
1 tablespoon fresh ground black pepper
1 tablespoon crushed dried rosemary
1 tablespoon rubbed dried sage
1 tablespoon dried thyme leaves
a couple of each rough chopped carrots, celery stalks (leaves and all), an onion and an apple

Directions:
In a large stock pot over medium heat, pour all ingredients with exception of the Turkey. Bring the mixture to a boil, stirring until all salt has dissolved, and boil for 30 minutes to extract flavors from herbs. Remove from heat, and allow to cool to room temperature before using as a turkey brine; Add the fresh vegetables and Turkey to container or brining bag to be used, refrigerate or keep on ice and allow brined turkey to marinate at least 12 to 24 Hours.

Submitter: 
Karyn Osborne
Description: 
This brine, made with vegetable & Chicken broth, apple juice, and plenty of seasonings, will make your holiday turkey extra moist and flavorful. Marinate your turkey overnight for the most flavorful meat.Modified from: Joe's Turkey Brine
