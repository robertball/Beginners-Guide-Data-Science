Title:
Microwave Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp garlic powder
5 cups oyster crackers
3 cups mini pretzels
2 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts
2 tbsp parmesan cheese

Directions:
In small microwaveable dish combine first 4 ingredients.  Cover and microwave for 45 sec or until butter is mealted. In a large microwaveable bowl mix crackers, pretzels, peanuts, add butter mix and stir to coat. Sprinkle with cheese and toss to coat.  microwave, uncovered on high for 7-8 min or until mix begins to toast,  stirring every 2 min.  Cool and store in air tight container.
