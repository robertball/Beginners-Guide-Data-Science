Title:
Snowballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups soft butter
1 cup sifted Confectioners sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 teaspoon almond extract
5 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Mix together first 4 ingredients thoroughly
Sift together last 3 ingredients and stir into butter mixture.  Add a few drops of water if it is too dry
Chill dough
Roll into 1" balls
Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets
Bake until set but not brown about 10 minutes
While warm roll in confectioners sugar
Cool and roll in sugar again
