Title:
Bread & Butter Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 quarts sliced cucumbers
1 quart sliced onions/peppers
3 cups vinegar
3 cups granulated sugar
1 tsp. ground mustard
1 1/2 tsp. turmeric powder
4 tsp. salt
4 tsp. mustard seed
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large pot and bring to a boil. Cook for 5 minutes.
Put in jars, seal tightly.

Submitter: 
Karalyn
Description: 
These are the pickles my mom canned when I was a child. Love them. They are the only pickles my children will eat.
