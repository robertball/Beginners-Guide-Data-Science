Title:
Healthy and Easy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/971829.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1 lb lean ground beef or turkey
- 1 28oz can peeled whole tomatoes
- 1 8oz can tomato paste
- 1 15oz can red kidney beans
- 1 15oz can pinto beans
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 4 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1/2 sweet onion, diced (optional)
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (optional)
- 1 cup reduced fat sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Pour olive oil in large pot;
Add ground meat and cook on medium/high while you break apart with spoon;
When meat is mostly cooked (some pink left), add garlic and onion (optional);
Cook 2 minutes while stiring nonstop;
Add can of peeled whole tomatoes, including liquid;
Break tomatoes apart with spoon;
Add peppers and stir;
Add both cans of beans, including the liquid;
Add tomato paste and all spices, mix well;
When chili starts to boil, reduce heat to low and cover for 30 minutes, stiring occasionally;
Keep cooking on low until ready to serve. 10 minutes before serving, remove lid and let the chili thicken up;
Top with cheddar cheese or light sour cream (optional).

Submitter: 
Desire For Fit
Description: 
This chili is quick and easy and HEALTHY! Lean meat, beans and vegetables give this yummy meal a guilt-less end to the day.
