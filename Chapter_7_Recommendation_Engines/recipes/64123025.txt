Title:
Sweet & Spicy Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb baby carrots
1 C. orange juice
2 T. butter
1/4 C. honey
2 T. brown sugar
1/2 C. apricot preserves
3 T. Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. white pepper

Directions:
In large saucepan, combine carrots and orange juice. Bring to boil over high heat. Cover pan, reduce heat to low and simmer 8-10 minutes until carrots are crisp-tender. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup of the liquid. Combine cooked carrots, reserved juice, and remaining ingredients in the saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until sugar is dissolved and carrots are glazed and tender. Serve immediately.
