Title:
Easy Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/3 cup Kikkoman Stir-Fry Sauce
4 tablespoon dry sherry or other wine
4 teaspoon cornstarch
4 pound ground pork or ground turkey
2 cup chopped onion
Corn
Water Chestnuts
8 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup coarsely chopped cilantro leaves
2 teaspoon Oriental sesame oil
Iceberg lettuce leaves

Directions:
Combine stir-fry sauce, wine and cornstarch; set aside.
Stir-fry pork/turkey, onion and garlic together in hot wok or large skillet over high heat until meat is no longer pink; drain excess fat. Add corn & water chestnuts and heat until warm. Add stir-fry sauce mixture; cook, stirring, until meat is evenly coated with sauce. Remove from heat; stir in cilantro and sesame oil. To serve, place desired amount of meat mixture in lettuce leaf and wrap to enclose.

Submitter: 
Rebekah Richards
Description: 
Lettuce leaves are great wraps for stir-fried pork. This fun recipe is bursting with Asian flavors!Modified from: Easy Lettuce Wraps
