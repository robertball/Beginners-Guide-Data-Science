Title:
Amaretti  Italian Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
1 small bottle of Almond extract
5 cups finely chopped whole roasted almonds(reserve 45 almonds for topping)
2 cups of sugar
1/2 tbsp of cocoa

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350F
mix sugar and coaco
Add eggs and almond extract
shape into small balls size of a teaspoon
roll in sugar
press finger in centre to make a small crater and add  1 whole almond
Place 2 inches apart on parchment paper lined bake pans.
Bake 15 min. do not overbake.
