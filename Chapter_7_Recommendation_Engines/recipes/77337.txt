Title:
Lemon Crumb Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981346.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package Pillsbury® Lemon Cake or Yellow Cake mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 egg
2 cups finely crushed saltine cracker crumbs
3 egg yolks
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/2 cup lemon juice from concentrate

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In large bowl, combine cake mix, butter and 1 egg with mixer until crumbly. Stir in cracker crumbs. Reserve 2 cups crumb mixture. Press remaining crumb mixture on bottom of greased 13x9 pan.
Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until golden
With mixer or wire whisk, beat sweetened condensed milk, lemon juice and 3 egg yolks. Spread evenly over prepared crust.
Top with reserved crumb mixture. Bake 20 minutes or until firm. Cool. Cut into bars. Store covered in refrigerator.
Bake 20 minutes or until set and top is golden. Cool. Cut into bars. Store covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
These layered lemon bars are beautiful as well as irresistible. A lemon cake layer is topped with a lemon cream, and then a lemony crumb topping.
