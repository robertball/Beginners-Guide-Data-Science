Title:
Grandmother Maragaret's Cherry Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 to 1.75 cups flour
1/4 cup icing sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup coconut
1 cup cherries
1 tsp almond or vanilla extract

Directions:
1 Blend first 3 ingreients together
2. place in a 8 X 8 greased pan
3. Bake at 350 for at least 8 minutes, reduce heat by 25 degrees if using a pyrex dish
4. Beat eggs and sugar together until smooth
5. Stir in coconut, sherries and flavouring to step 4
6. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes
7. Allow to cool then cut into squares
