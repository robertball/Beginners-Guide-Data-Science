Title:
Outrageously Good Holiday Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/316030.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ruby red grapefruit, peeled and sectioned
8 ounces Spring Mix, or other tender salad greens
2 bunches green onions, sliced thin
3 avocados, sliced
8 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
8 ounces poppy seed dressing (I use Marie's)

Directions:
Drain grapefruit sections and reserve juice for another use. Mix drained grapefruit and all remaining ingredients in large salad bowl. Toss with dressing just before serving.

Submitter: 
anniepie
Description: 
Outrageously Good? I'm not kidding. So simple to make, but the combination of ingredients makes it sophisticated. The mix of tangy grapefruit, sweet poppy seed dressing, buttery smooth avocados and Swiss cheese is incredible.
