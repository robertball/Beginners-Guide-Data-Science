Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/661566.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Package Frozen Broccoli Flowerettes (thawed)
5 Eggs
1 Large Can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Can of Mayonaise (use soup can)
8 oz. Shredded Cheddar

Directions:
Mix eggs, Chicken Soup, Mayonaise together. Stir in Cheese and Broccoli. Bake at 350F for 30-40 minutes.
