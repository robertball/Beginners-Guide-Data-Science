Title:
vacuum cleaner cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 box (18.25 oz.)yellow cake mix (do not use with pudding added)
3 eggs
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 cup flaked coconut
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Spray 10 by 15 inch jelly roll pan with Pam. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Combine butter, cake mix and one of the eggs. Stir together until dry ingredients are moistened. Pat mixture over bottom of prepared pan. Beat remaining eggs lightly, add cream cheese and sugar. Stir in coconut and nuts. Spread evenly on butter mixture. Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until golden brown. Cool pan on wire rack to room temperature. cut into bars. Makes 4 dozen bars.
