Title:
Banana Pudding Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bananas
Cubed Ice
Vanilla Soy  Milk (Optional)
1 pack of Banana Creme Jello(Sugar-free) if optional
2 Hand Fulls of Nilla Wafers
1 Cup of White Sugar

Directions:
First, Add your Ice cubes. Then, add your Milk.Cut up your bananas into 6pcs(It should be 12 in total). Throw in you Nilla wafers, sugar, and your Banana Creme. Lastly, blend it all together and ENJOY your creation.

Submitter: 
Edward Thomas Spivey
Description: 
It should be a smooth texture, almost thick like Ice cream...
