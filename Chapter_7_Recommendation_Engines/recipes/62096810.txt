Title:
Honey Syrup Date Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup molasses
1/3 cup orange juice
1/2 cups each:chopped dates, apricots, raisins,walnuts
1tbs corn meal
1 1/2 cups unbleached flour
1tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbs cinnamon
2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp grated nutmeg
1 tbs grated rind (orange, lemon or mixed)
Syrup:
1/2 cup honey
1/3 cup orange juice
1 tbs butter
1 tbs Cointreau (optional)

Directions:
Beat together eggs, oil, orange juice and molasses.
Chop dates, apricots and mix with other fruit and 1 tbs of cornmeal.
Mix flour , baking soda, spices and orange rind and gradually mix into the liquids. Do not overstir.
Add the fruit and nuts. and place in an oiled loaf pan.
Bake @ 375 F for 45 min or till a tester comes out clean
Syrup:
In a glass bowl add honey, orange juice and butter. Microwave for 1 min on High.
Poke holes throughout the cake and pour syrup over it.

Submitter: 
Edith
Description: 
For Roch Hashona (the Jewish New Year) we traditionally prepare honey cakes. I mostly find them either too sweet or too dry. This is a moist cake that improves with age. Prepare it a day or two before serving.
