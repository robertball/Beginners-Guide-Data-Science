Title:
Lemon Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup margarine/butter
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 cup lemon nonfat yogurt (not greek)
2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
1/2 cup confectioners sugar, sifted
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat 325 degrees
Combine flour, salt, baking soda
Mixer:  cream butter, sugar until light and fluffy
Add eggs, yogurt and lemon peel; beat until smooth
Stir in dry ingredients, combine well
Spray bundt pan
Spread batter evenly in pan
Bake 50-60 minutes
Cool on rack 15 minutes
Prepare glaze by combining conf. sugar and juice; pour evenly over cake
