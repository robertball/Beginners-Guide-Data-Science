Title:
Cioppino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. shrimp
1/2 lb. firm white fish
1/2 cup fresh Italian flat parsley - chopped
13 oz. can crushed tomatoes
1 onion - chopped
2 1/4 cups dry white wine
1 green pepper - chopped
1 cup scallions - sliced
1/4 tsp. oregano or basil
4 dozen clams - soaked and scrubbed
1 bay leaf
3 cloves of garlic - minced
1 1/3 cups tomato puree
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes (optional)
Olive oil
Salt to taste

Directions:
Add enough olive oil to coat bottom of large sauce pan and then add onion, garlic, and green pepper. Saute for 2-3 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients except seafood and simmer 5 minutes or until onion is softened.
Add clams and cook 5-10 minutes and then add shrimp and fish. Simmer until clams open.
Serve with plenty of crusty bread to dip.
