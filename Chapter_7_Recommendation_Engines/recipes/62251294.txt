Title:
My Crockpot Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332879.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 boxes macaroni of your choice, i use rotini
1 can of evaporated milk
1 cup of regular milk, i used whole
1 stick of butter
2 eggs
1 can of campbell's cheddar cheese soup
few dashes salt
few dashes pepper
1 small block of velveeta cheese
1 package of white sharp cheddar cheese, i used cabot seriously sharp
1 package of yellow sharp cheddar cheese, i used kraft aged wisconsin cheddar

Directions:
Cook macaroni until al dente about 7-8 minutes, drain.
Mix together well the evaporated milk, regular whole milk, 2 eggs, salt, pepper, and the can of cheddar cheese soup (I used a whisk).
Melt butter in crock pot.
Pour macaroni into the crock pot, seperating if stuck together at all; mix into the butter.
Pour into the crockpot the milk mixture; stir just once to coat a little bit.
Add the cheeses and stir just once to coat a little bit also.
Cover & cook for about 3-4 hours, stirring once before serving.
