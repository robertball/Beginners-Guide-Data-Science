Title:
Schrier's Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound Ground Beef
1 Onion Diced
Ketchup to taste
Worcestershire Sauce to taste
Seasoned Salt to taste
Mustard to taste
8 Hamburger Buns
8 Slices of Cheese
8 Slices of tomatos

Directions:
Mix together ground beef,ketchup,onion,Worcestershire sauce and seasoned salt.
Spread small amount of the ground beef mixture over bottom half of a hamburger bun. Squirt mustard on the meat. Place under the broiler and broil until meat is done.
Place slice of tomato on top halves of hamburger buns. Place cheese slice on the tomato. When the hamburger mixture is about done place under the broil and broil until cheese melts.
Put together both halves and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Jonathan Schrier
Description: 
This is one of my favorite recipes handed down to me from my grandmother. It always a favorite when I make this recipe for my family and friends.
