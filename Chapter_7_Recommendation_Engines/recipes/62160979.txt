Title:
Roasted New Potatoe Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium new potatoes - chunked
1 large yellow or white onion - chunked
Olive oil
Garlic powder
Salt
Pepper
4 large portabello mushroom caps - chunked
3 tblspns steak sauce
1 large brocoli crown - chunked
1 bunch green onions - chunked
1/4 cup prepared honey dijon vinegrette salad dressing

Directions:
Roasting takes 2 steps as times vary for different veggies.
Roasting 1 - toss chunked potatoes and onions together with olive oil and seasonings
400degrees for approx 25 mins - till brown and roasted
Remove from oven and pan and set aside to cool.
SAME PAN - chunked up mushrooms, brocoli, green onions
tossed with olive oil, seasonings and steak sauce.
400 degrees for approx 15 mins.
Combine all ingredients, toss with 1/4 cup prepared honey dijon dressing and serve.
This is DA-BOMB!

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
A bright fresh warm twist on potato salad.  A bowl full of roasted flavor tossed with Honey Dijon vinegrette at the end. Perfect with favorite meats - and keeps you away from the MAYO craze.
