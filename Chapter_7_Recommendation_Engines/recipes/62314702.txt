Title:
Pumpkin Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/374410.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
3 small containers of apple sauce
3cups whole wheat flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease two 12 cup muffin pans, or line with paper baking cups.
Beat the eggs in a large bowl, and mix in the sugar, pumpkin and applesauce.
In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Blend into the egg and pumpkin mixture. Fold in the chocolate chips. Transfer to the muffin pans.
Bake in the preheated oven 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Remove muffins from pans, and cool on a wire rack.
