Title:
Strawberry Lime Smoothie Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520600.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Almond Breeze Vanilla almondmilk
1 cup frozen strawberries
1/2 cup vanilla Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
1 medium banana
1/2 lime, juiced

Directions:
Blend all ingredients, then pour into ice pop molds.
Freeze for at least 2 hours (or overnight).

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Strawberry banana smoothie pops make an easy summertime frozen treat.
