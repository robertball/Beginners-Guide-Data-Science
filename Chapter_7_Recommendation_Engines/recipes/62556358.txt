Title:
Green Chile Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/541860.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (8 oz) blocks Cream or Neafatchel Cheese
2 (12.5 oz) cans White Chicken, or equivalent of cooked, shredded chicken breast
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup or 1 c. Sour Cream
1  (7 oz) can chopped green Chiles

Directions:
Put everything in crock pot on high and heat, making sure chicken is broken apart well.
Keep warm for dipping.

Submitter: 
Jannabofanna
Description: 
Hot appetizer dip served with Tortilla chips.
Easy to add to for different tastes or spiciness.
Recipe from Kathy Younger, Hays, Ks.
