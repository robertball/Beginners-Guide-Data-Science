Title:
Grilled Chicken In Green Sauce with Sugar and Spice Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
1/2 cup rice vinegar
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons sesame oil, roasted
4 tablespoons fresh ginger root, minced
2 tablespoons sugar
4 tablespoons fresh basil, minced
4 tablespoons cilantro, minced
36 chicken tenderloins, skewered
1/2 cup sugar
4 tablespoons chili powder
2 pineapple
2 limes

Directions:
Cut 2 pineapple into 12 1/2-inch rings.
In a blender, combine the first eight ingredients to make the dipping sauce.
Place chicken tenderloins on skewers.
Blend 1/2 cup dipping sauce, sugar and chili powder and baste sliced pineapple and chicken with mixture. Set the rest of the dipping sauce aside until chicken is ready to serve.
Grill chicken and pineapple 3-4 minutes on each side.
Serve grilled chicken on top of stacked pineapple with fresh dipping sauce and a lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
Inspired by the Caribbean, this dish adds some fire and flavor to grilled chicken.
