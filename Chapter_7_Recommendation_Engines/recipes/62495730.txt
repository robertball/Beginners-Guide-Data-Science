Title:
Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. uncooked rice
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 whole chicken - cut up
1 can onion soup

Directions:
place rice, cream of chicken and celery soup in a dutch oven; mix ingredients.  Place chicken pieces on top of rice mixture; pour onion soup over all.  Bake at 350 deg.  for 1 1/2 hrs.
