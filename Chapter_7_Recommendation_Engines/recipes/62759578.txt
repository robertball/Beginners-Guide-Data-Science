Title:
Perfect Pork chops for slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 pork chops trimmed of fat
2c Coke - not diet
1 can pineapple chunks
1c ketchup

Directions:
Arrange chops in slow cooker.  Add rest of ingredients in bowl and stir.  Pour over chops.  cover with lid and cook on low 7 hours.
