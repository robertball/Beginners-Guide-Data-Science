Title:
Japanese Shrimp Sauce I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1-1/2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons rice vinegar
2 tablespoons melted butter
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise, white sugar, rice vinegar, melted butter, paprika and garlic powder. Mix well, cover and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
DGLONG
Description: 
This is the shrimp sauce found in Japanese steakhouses. It is sweet and pinkish-orangish in color. You can substitute fat-free mayo, butter, and 0-calorie sugar, and it still tastes great.Modified from: Japanese Shrimp Sauce I
