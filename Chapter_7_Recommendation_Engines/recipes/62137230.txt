Title:
Grandma's Old Fashioned Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 half 5lb bag red potatoes
1 half onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup of relish, drained
1 small jar pimentos, drained
1 tsp celery seeds
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp mustard
1/2 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Boil potatoes for 30 minutes with skins on. Then drain, peel and cut into cubes.
Place in large mixing bowl and add onions, relish and pimentos.
Combine last 5 ingredients in a small mixing bowl and pour over potatoes. Mix together until evenly coated.
Let potato salad cool on counter for 30 minutes before placing in refridgerator. Chill for one hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Karen Tinsley Medlin
Description: 
This recipe has been a staple at all of our family gatherings for generations. The simple ingredients reflect back on a time when you made do with what you had around the house.
