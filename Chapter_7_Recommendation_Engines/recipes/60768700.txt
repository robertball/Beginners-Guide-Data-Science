Title:
Cream of Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. Potatoes, peeled and diced
1.5 medium onions chopped
1 stick butter
2 stalks celery chopped (or 1/2 tsp. celery seed)
3 cups Chicken broth
Fresh ground black pepper
1 pint half and half

Directions:
Melt butter in stock pot. Add chopped onions and celery (if using celery seed add later) and cook until translucent stirring frequently. Add 3 cups broth (can use 3 cups water with 3 chicken bouillon cubes). Add black pepper to taste. Add Celery seed if using seed. Cook until potatoes fall apart easily. Mash in stock pot until thick and some chunks remain. Add half and half.
