Title:
Linguine with Jumbo Shrimp & Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb linguine
2 cups low-sodium chix/veg broth
1 purple onion minced
2 cloves garlic passed through garlic press
1 lb large shrimp
2/3 yogurt cheese
4 tbsp fresh chopped basil
2 tbsp fresh marjoram
2 tsp grated lemon rind
Sea salt & fresh ground pepper
1 cup cherry tomatoes halved
4 cups shredded spinach

Directions:
1. Bring 6 quarts of salted water to a boil.  Add pasta cook al dente, drain and rinse under cold water and return to pot
2. Small saucepan heat chicken stock.  Add onions, garlic and shrimp and let simmer, covered , for 3 to 5 minutes or until shrimp are cooked through.  Remove shrimp from cooking liquid and transfer to glass bowl.  Cover with a plate and wrap a clean kitchen towel around bowl to keep warm.
3.  In a small bowl whisk together yogurt cheese, chopped basil, marjoram, lemon zest, sea salt & black pepper.  Pour over pasta.  Add cherry tomatoes, cooked shrimp and spinach.  Toss gently evenly.
