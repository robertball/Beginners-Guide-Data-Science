Title:
Crispy Crisp Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken legs or thighs
30 potato chips
3/4 cup Rice Krispies
1/4 tsp ground red pepper
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
1. Place potato chips and Rice Krispies in a medium size plastic bag.  Crush with a rolling pin. Mix in red pepper and place on a plate.
2. Mix egg and milk in separate bowl.
3. Coat chicken pieces with milk mixture and then coat with chips mixture.
4. Place chicken skin side up on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees for 35-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz

A healthier version of fried chicken.
