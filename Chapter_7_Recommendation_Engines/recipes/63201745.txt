Title:
Captain Jack's Peach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/827929.jpg
Ingredients:
Recipe for 2 pie crusts, or 2 store bought crusts
8 good sized peaches sliced and peeled (or 6 cups sliced canned peaches, drained or frozen peaches-thawed and drained)
2   Tbps rum
1/4 cup butter, melted
1   cup white sugar
1/3 cup all purpose flour
1   Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp cardamom
Wash:
1/4 cup of milk

Directions:
Preheat over to 400 degrees.
Press one of the pie crusts into the bottom of a lightly buttered pie dish.
In medium mixing bowl, stir together flour, cornstarch, sugar, cinnamon, allspice and cardamom. Slowly add butter and mix until all butter is added. In seperate large mixing bowl, toss rum with peaches til coated. Add sugar mixture to peaches and gently toss/stir til peaches are coated with sugar mixture.
Pour peach filling into the crusted pie dish - top with remaining pie crust and seal crust to dish. Cut 4-5 slits in crust. Brush top of crust with milk. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 mins, then lower the oven temp and bake at 350 degrees for 30 mins. Leave pie in cooling oven to set for at least 1 hr.
