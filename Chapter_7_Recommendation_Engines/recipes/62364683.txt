Title:
Crockpot Oatmeal-Fruit Porridge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups old-fashioned oats
2 apples, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup dried cranberries or raisins
6 cups water
2 Tbsp. butter, melted
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
SERVE, IF DESIRED, WITH:
brown sugar
milk or cream

Directions:
1. Spray 3- to 4-quart crockpot with cooking spray.n cooker, mix porridge ingredients.
2. Cover; cook on Low for 6-8 hours.
3. Serve with brown sugar and cream.

Submitter: 
Christine Westrom
Description: 
Yummy first thing in the morning and too easy, as it's made overnight in the crockpot. Also good with dried figs, apricots, or dried mixed tropical fruit.
