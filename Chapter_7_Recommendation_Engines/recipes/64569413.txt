Title:
Asian Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-- DRESSING --
1/3 cup creamy organic peanut butter
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
1/4 cup cold, filtered water
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp brown sugar
1 or 2 tsp sriracha sauce
1/4 tsp ginger powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
-- SALAD --
12 ounces cooked pasta (about 4-5 oz uncooked regular linguine or sweet potato linguine noodles)
1/8 cup freshly chopped cilantro
1/2 cup grated carrots
1/4 cup chopped scallions
3/4 cup chopped cucumber
2-3 cups chopped napa cabbage
1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
1/2 cup fresh broccoli
3 tbsp roasted sesame seeds
1/8 cup crushed peanuts (roasted & salted)

Directions:
In mixing bowl, start by mixing together soy sauce, cold water, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. Whisk together. Add in peanut butter, ginger powder, garlic powder, sriracha sauce and brown sugar. Whisk until perfectly smooth.  Set aside.
In large salad bowl, mix together all salad ingredients except sesame seeds and crushed peanuts. Toss together so that they are mixed. Pour dressing on top and toss again to ensure salad is coated evenly.
Put in fridge for 30-40 minutes until cool. After removing from fridge, sprinkle sesame seeds on top of salad as well as crushed peanuts. Serve.
