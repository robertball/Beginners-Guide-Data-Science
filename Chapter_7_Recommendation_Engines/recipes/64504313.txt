Title:
Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups pumpkin
2/3 cup sugar
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp each ginger and salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
2 eggs
1/2 cup each milk and cream

Directions:
mix together pumpkin and spices add to the 2 well beaten eggs and milk then pour into pie crust and bake 10 mins at 450 then 40 mins at 350
