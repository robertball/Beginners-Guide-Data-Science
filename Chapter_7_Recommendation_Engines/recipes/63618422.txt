Title:
white beans and Italian sausage links Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS olive oil, divided, plus more for drizzling
1 Lb. fresh Italian sausage links
1 large onion, thinly sliced
4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
1 sprig thyme
2 15oz cans cannellini (white kidney)beans, rinsed
2 cups chicken broth
Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper
Pack of baby spinach 5oz(about 10 cups)

Directions:
Heat 1 Tbsp. oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and cook, turning occasionally, until browned and cooked through, about 15-20 minutes. Transfer sausage to platter.
Reduce heat to medium. Heat remaining 1tbsp. oil in same skilet. Add onion, garlic, and thyme sprig.. Cook stirring until onion is softened. 5-8 minutes. Add beans and broth and cook, crushing a few beans with the back of a spoon to thicken sauce until slightly thickened, 8-10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Add spinach by handfuls and cook just until wilted, about 2 minutes.
Add sliced sausage and fold into stew. Add to water to thin if needed. Served in bowls
