Title:
Tuna & Vegetable Farro Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6243423.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Cooked Farro (Following cooking instructions on package)
1/2 Tomato, seeded, diced
1 can Tuna Fish, in water, divided
1 Hard Boiled Egg, sliced, divided
2 oz Jarlsberg Cheese, diced
1/2 Avocado, sliced thin
Croutons (optional)
Favorite Dressing

Directions:
Divide farro into two bowls, top with tuna, tomatoes, avocado, egg and croutons.
Add dressing and croutons. Enjoy!

Description: 
A twist on the quinoa bowl. Leftover fish work beautiful in this dish.
