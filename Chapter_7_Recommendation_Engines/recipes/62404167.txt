Title:
Banana Bread 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4C flour
2tsp baking powder
1/4tsp soda
3/4tsp salt
1/3C shortening
2/3C sugar
2 eggs
1C mashed bananas(2-3)

Directions:
Grease and flour loaf pan. Cream shortening add sugar. Add the eggs one egg at a time beat after each one. Stir in bananas. Gradually add flour mixture in 4 intervals. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes.
