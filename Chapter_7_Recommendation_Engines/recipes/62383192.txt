Title:
Hot Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cube butter
1/2 c. semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
1 can evaporated milk
2 1/2 c. powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Boil all ingredients (except vanilla) 8 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Add vanilla.

Submitter:

Description: 
Warm, gooey, perfect hot fudge for Sundaes.
