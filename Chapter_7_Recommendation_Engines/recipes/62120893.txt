Title:
Shrimp with Angel Hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs peeled and deveined shrimp 20-25 count
1 28 oz can whole tomatoes in juice (Hunt's)
1/2 cup white wine
4 cloves of garlic minced
3 T Olive Oil
1/3 cup fresh parsley chopped
pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
sea salt
1 lb Angel Hair Pasta

Directions:
Boil salted water for pasta while preparing sauce.
Puree tomatoes in blender or food processor.
In a saute pan or large skillet add the olive oil, pepper flakes and garlic. Saute for 2 minutes on medium heat using care to not let the garlic brown.  Add tomatoes, parsley, and white wine.  Simmer for 8-10 minutes. Salt to taste.  Boil pasta for 4 minutes or follow package directions. Add shrimp to sauce and simmer for 4-5 minutes.  Drain pasta and place in large bowl, add sauce and shrimp. Gently toss and serve in pasta bowls.

Submitter: 
Juanita/Jane
Description: 
Simple, quick way to serve pasta and shrimp.  Grampa Nicola showed us how to take a few ingredients and prepare a wonderful comfort food dinner for our families. Manga!
