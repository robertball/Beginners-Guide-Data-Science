Title:
Strawberry Orange Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3214697.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup orange juice
1 cup fresh strawberries
1 ripe banana
ice cubes

Directions:
Blend the orange juice, strawberries, banana, and ice cubes in a blender until smooth and frothy.
