Title:
Dill-icious Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons olive oil
2 carrots, peeeled and chopped
1 parsnip, peeled and chopped
1 small onion, peeled and chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
bay leaf
8 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 LB skinless/bonelss chicken chicken breast, diced
1 12 oz package Reemes egg noodles (like those found in the freezer section)
1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
1/2 cup fresh dill, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place a large soup pot over medium heat and add the olive oil.  Add the vegetables and saute until just tender, and the onion is opaque, about 5 minutes.  Add the bay leaf and broth, and raise heat to high.  Bring mixture to a boil, and add the chicken.  Boil for 5 minutes, then bring the mixture down to a simmer over medium low heat.  Add the noodles, dill, and parsley, and simmer until noodles are tender, about 15 minutes.  Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This is a quick and very simple soup featuring my favorite herb, fresh dill.  Add a little more if you like!  This freezes very nicely, too.
