Title:
Savory Montreal Steak Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980813.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon McCormick® Grill Mates® Montreal Steak Seasoning
1 tablespoon light brown sugar
1 teaspoon McCormick® Onion Powder
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Oregano Leaves
1 pound sirloin or New York strip steak

Directions:
Blend first four ingredients. Rub over surface of steak.
Grill or broil steak 8-10 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
A zesty burst of flavor from the Grill Mates® Montreal Steak Seasoning combined with sweet brown sugar will give your tastebuds a reason to smile.
