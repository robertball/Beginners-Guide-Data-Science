Title:
Creamy Broccoli Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken broth
1/4 tsp pepper
2 cloves garlic minced
8 cups chopped fresh broccoli
3 large potatoes, sliced 1/4" thick
1 3/4 cups milk
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix broth, pepper, garlic, broccoli, potatoes and onion in sauce pot.
Heat to a boil.
Cover and cook over low heat 15 mins or until veggies are tender.
Remove from heat
Blend broth mixture in blender or food processor until smooth.
Return to pan, stir in milk and cheese
Heat through
