Title:
DDD BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
oil for frying
12 garlic cloves
2 bunches of green onions
2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup molasses
2 cups maple syrup
1 cup honey
4 tables spoons rub
2 litres ketchup

Directions:
prepare garlic and onions. use all of the onion.
in a large pot, caramelize garlic and onion
add sugar and rub
add molasses, syrup and honey
puree in blender
back in pot, blend with ketchup
useimmediately or store in jars
