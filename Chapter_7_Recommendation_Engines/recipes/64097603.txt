Title:
McCormick Chicken & Mushroom Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
McCormick Seasoning Mix
1 cup uncooked regular long-grain white rice
1 can (4 oz) sliced mushrooms, drained
1 can green beans
2 T butter, cut into pieces (optional)
1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Place rice, mushrooms, beans and butter in 13x9x2 baking dish.  Top with chicken.
Mix milk, water and seasoning mix until well blended.  Pour over chicken and rice.  Cover with foil.
Bake 45 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and rice is tender. Let stand 5 min.  Cut chicken into serving-size pieces.  Stir rice before serving
