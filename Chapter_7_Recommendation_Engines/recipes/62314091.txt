Title:
Sausage and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter
1 clove garlic, minced
1 pound smoked sausage, sliced
1 28oz. can sweet peas, drained
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in non- stick skillet over medium- high heat. Add garlic and sausage. Cook about 5-10 minutes or until sausage is browned. Reduce heat to low, and add the peas. Cook 3-5 minutes longer, unitl the peas are heated through and liquid is reduced. Use salt and pepper to taste.
