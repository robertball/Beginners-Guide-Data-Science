Title:
Easy Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32 oz bag Sourthern Style Hashbrowns
1 pint of half and half
1 stick butter or margarine
1 cup cheddar cheese
2/3 lb. Velveeta cheese
2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  In a greased 9X13
pan mix the hasbrowns, half and half and salt.  On top put chunks of the Velveeta cheese and butter and bake for an hour, making sure to stir thoroughly every 15 minutes.  After the last stirring, sprinkle the cheddar cheese over the top and allow to bake the final 15 minutes.
