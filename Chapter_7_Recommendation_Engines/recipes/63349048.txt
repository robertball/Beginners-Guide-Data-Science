Title:
Cho-Cho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 gallons chopped cabbage
1 gallon chopped green tomatoes
15 large green bell peppers
8 large red bell peppers
25/30 Cayenne peppers
3 tsps. Turmeric
3 tsps. Dry Mustard
4 tsps. Salt
1/2 gallon 5% Vinegar
4 lbs. Sugar

Directions:
*Chop all vegetables in food processor to desired texture.
*Mix vegetables well and add sugar, spices & vinegar.
*Bring to a boil for 10 minutes.
*Pour mixture into hot sterilized jars.
*Seal with lids.
