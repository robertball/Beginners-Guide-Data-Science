Title:
Hot Broccoli Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120688.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 envelope Good Seasons Zesty Italian Dressing Mix
1 package (10 oz.) frozen chopped broccoli, thawed, well drained
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F.
Beat cream cheese, sour cream and dressing mix with mixer until blended. Add broccoli and 1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese; mix well.
Spread into 9-inch pie plate.
Bake 20 min. Sprinkle with remaining cheddar cheese; bake 5 minutes or until melted. Serve with crackers.
