Title:
soup d'olives frittes (olive soup with fries) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 roasted chicken, store bought or home made.
one chicken piece of your choosing, uncooked.
1-2 cups pitted green olives, soaked to remove excess salt.
1/4 large onion, chopped.
3 cloves garlic, chopped
chicken boullion cubes
salt and pepper
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp ras el hanout (optional)
oil for saute and frying
1 yellow potato per person, peeled and cut into fries.

Directions:
in a soup pot, brown the chicken piece in a little oil. add onions and cook until soft, add garlic, and pepper, cover with water, about 5 cups. add boullion cubes and olives. cook until chicken piece is cooked through, season to your liking. simmer on medium heat while you prepare the fries.
heat one inch of oil in a frying pan. add a pinch of salt. fry potatoes until crisp.
serve in bowls with fries on top and a scoop of olives in each, and roast chicken on a platter, serve with a baguette.

Submitter: 
jacqueline senouci
Description: 
this was such a treat the first time i tasted it, olives are expensive in the usa and i couldnt imagine making a soup of them. but once i tried it it was worth it. the french fries absorb the broth and come to life.
