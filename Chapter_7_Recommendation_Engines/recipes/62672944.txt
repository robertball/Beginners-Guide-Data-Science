Title:
Baked Sweet Potatoes w/ Chipotle Honey Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sweet potatoes
8 T. unsalted butter, softened
1 T. chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
1 t. honey
Salt & Pepper, season to taste

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Pierce sweet potatoes and bake 45 minutes, or until tender.  Serve with Chipotle Honey Butter.
2.  Combine butter, chipotles, honey, salt &amp; pepper in food processor and blend well.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
I love baked sweet potatoes, but wanted something sweet/smoky to serve them with.  Whipped these up to try and they are a great combination - and even better with BBQ!
