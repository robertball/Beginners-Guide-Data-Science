Title:
Terri's Marinaded Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/336622.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs. chicken drummets/wings
1/3 cup soy sauce
3 TBLS white vinegar
3 TBLS white sugar
3 TBLS Brown sugar
1 tsp ground ginger
2 cloves garlic (crushed)

Directions:
Place chicken drummies/wings in a gallon ziplock. Mix together last 6 ingredients and place in ziplock with chicken. Seal ziplock, removing as much air as possible and marinate in fridge overnight.
Line 9x13 or cookie sheet with foil.
Bake chicken at 350* for 1 1/2 to 2 hours turning drummies/wings every half hour.
Enjoy!
