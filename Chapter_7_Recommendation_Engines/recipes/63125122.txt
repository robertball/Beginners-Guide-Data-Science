Title:
Spicy Beef Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound flank steak, trimmed
1/3 cup sliced shallots
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
3 tbs fresh lime juice
1 tbs fish sauce
2 tsp sliced thai red chilies or serrano chilies
2 medium tomatoes, cut into 1/4 inch thick wedges

Directions:
Prepare grill or broiler.  Place steak on a grill rack or broiler pan coated with cooking spray; cook 6 minutes on each side or until desired degree of doneness.
Cut the steak diagonally across grain into thin slices; cut each slice into 2 inch pieces.
Combine the steak, shallots, and remaining ingredients, and toss gently.
