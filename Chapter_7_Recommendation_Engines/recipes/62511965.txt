Title:
Big Daddy's Broccoli Chicken Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
2 cups fresh chopped broccoli
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons Dijon-style prepared mustard
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoon Big Daddy.s Chia Roasted Garlic
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the chicken, broccoli, onions, bell pepper, cheese, mayonnaise, mustard, salt, pepper and Big Daddy's Chia Roasted Garlic. Mix all together.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Cover a cookie sheet with aluminum foil. Roll out crescent rolls , before you fold rolls, poon some of the chicken mixture onto the thick part of each crescent roll. Fold the pointy end of each roll over the top of the mixture and tuck in the middle.   Arrangement rolls where they do not touch. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until rolls are golden brown.
