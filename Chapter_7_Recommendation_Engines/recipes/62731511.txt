Title:
Peach Polka Dot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/624846.jpg
Ingredients:
9 inch pie crust (unbaked- I use a frozen one)
4 large peaches, peeled, pitted and halved or quartered
1/2 cup blueberries
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup whipping cream
1/4 cup slivered almonds (optional)

Directions:
1) Place peaches cut side down in pierced pie shell
2) Sprinkle blueberries over peaches
3) In a bowl combine sugar, flour, cinnamon and salt, stir together, then add cream and stir until combined well
4) Pour over fruit, then top with almonds
5) Bake until done

Submitter: 
KaraC
Description: 
I got this from my Aunt, and it is the easiest and tastiest peach pie I have ever tasted!  I didn't like peach pie before I ate this.  Blueberries add a little color and flavor to this easy easy pie!
