Title:
Crunchy Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
salt
pepper
Coating:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup paprika
2 tablespoons salt
2 tablespoons pepper
1 tablespoon ground mustard
1 tablespoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon dry mustard
1 tablespoon celery salt ( I grind celery seed and add salt in a 2:1 ratio)
3 tablespoons MSG
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS:
1 egg
1 can evaporated, or 8 oz whole
4 bone in skinless chix breast and 6 bone in skinless thighs
vegetable oil

Directions:
Place chicken in bowl. Add 2 TBS salt and water to cover.  Let stand in fridge 1 hour.  Remove, drain and dry chicken with paper towel.  Salt, pepper, and cayenne chicken pieces.  Mix evap milk and egg in one bowl and the coating ingredients in a container.  Dip chicken pieces in milk/egg mixture then shake in bag with some of coating in it.  (Keeping the coating mixture in container and adding to bag as needed keeps coating dry)  Repeat process with all chicken and place on rack over cookie sheet in fridge to dry.  30 minutes later, repeat process and let dry again in fridge for 30 minutes.  Fry chicken in 325 oil till brown then drain and cook in 325 oven for 30 minutes or till done.

Submitter: 
batya
Description: 
I've tried several fried chicken recipes and came up with this one.  It is skinless, the coating sticks and it has the intense flavor and cunchy coating found in fast food chicken.Modified from: Fried Chicken Coating
