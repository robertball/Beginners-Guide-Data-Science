Title:
Vegan Tuscan Pumpkin Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup strained tomatoes (tomato puree)
1 cup pumpkin puree
1/4 cup water
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
4 tsp pure maple syrup
1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
1 tsp fresh minced sage, plus extra for garnish

Directions:
Pour the olive oil into a large pot over medium heat and sauce the onion until tender, about 10 minutes.
Add in the garlic and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute more.
Add in the tomato puree, pumpkin, water, salt, cinnamon and maple syrup and bring to a boil.
Lower the heat and stir in the coconut milk and minced sage.
Adjust seasoning to taste and service warm over pasta. (about 1 lb of pasta)
