Title:
Favorite Things (Pomegranate) Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987492.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce Stirrings Simple Syrup
1 1/2 ounces Smirnoff Citrus Vodka
1 ounce fresh unsweetened pomegranate juice
1 wedge lemon

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker combine Stirrings Simple Syrup, Smirnoff Citrus Vodka and pomegranate juice with plenty of ice.
Cover and shake vigorously.
Strain into a martini glass.
Garnish with a wedge of lemon.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Fresh pomegranate juice, simple syrup and citrus vodka make a tasty, colorful martini.
