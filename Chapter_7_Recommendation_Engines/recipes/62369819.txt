Title:
Fragomammella (Strawberry breasts) - Low Carb dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413391.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb strawberries
1 T lemon juice
1 T superfine sugar
1 1/4 lb ricotta cheese
1/4 pint creme fraiche or double cream
3 T Campari
4 T powdered sugar

Directions:
Set a dozen small berries.  Chop the rest. Place in a bowl with lemon and sugar. Soak for about an hour. {ush Ricotta through a sieve. Add cream and Campari. Mix well. Sift powdered sugar into mixture to taste. Place a third of thhe mixture aside. Mix in strawberries into remaining Ricotta.  Divide mixture into 2 mounds each on six plates. Smooth plain Ricotta over mouns. Top each mound with a small strawberry. Refrigerate 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Parizienne
Description: 
A rather unusual low carb dessert option. Strawberry and ricotta combine with a bit of sugar and Campari.
