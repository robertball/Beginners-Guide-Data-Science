Title:
Garlic Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
12oz button mushrooms
8 cloves garlic, finely chopped
3 tablespoons dry sherry
2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
1. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.  Add mushrooms and saute until just softened.  2-3 minutes
2. Add garlic, sherry, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, paprika, salt, and pepper.  Simmer until the garlic and mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes
3. Sprinkle with parsley and serve hot with bread.
