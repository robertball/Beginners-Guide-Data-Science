Title:
Butternut Squash and Curried Apple Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045563.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 medium sweet onion
1 bay leaf
2 cloves
1 large butternut squash, peeled and cubed, seeds removed
2 Granny Smith green apples, peeled and cubed
4 medium Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 tbsp. brown sugar
2 tbsp. curry powder
5 cups chicken stock
whipping cream

Directions:
In a stock pot melt butter.
Add onion, bay leaf, cloves, season with salt and pepper to your taste.
Saute until onion is tender about 5 minutes.
Add squash, apples, potatoes, brown sugar and curry powder.
Stir to evenly coat the cubes and saut√© over medium heat for 15-20 minutes.
Add stock, cover and cook until the veggies are done and able to mash easily with a fork.
Remove bay leaf and cloves.
Puree in batches in the blender, food processor or use hand held blender.
If you find it too thick, you may add more stock at this point and give a good stir.
Serve in bowls with a swirl of whipping cream on the top. About a tablespoon or two.
