Title:
Ritz Cracker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Chicken breasts
1-2 rolls of crackers crushed up
1 cube butter (melted)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°.
Soak chicken in melted butter
Coat chicken in crushed Ritz crackers
Place in 9x13 pan. Cover with foil. Bake 25 min. covered at 350°
Bake an additional 5 min. uncovered

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a recipe from my sister-in-law. It is among my favorite all-time recipes and it has so many variations to play with.
