Title:
Chili Shrimp Kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
2 teaspoons sesame oil
1 tablespoon chili bean sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce
2 teaspoons tomato paste
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 lbs large raw shrimp

Directions:
Combine the crushed garlic, ginger, sesame oil, chili bean sauce, soy sauce, tomato paste, and lemon juice.
Shell shrimp and devein, leaving tail shells on.
Add the shrimp to the marinade.
Cover and marinate for 10-15 minutes.
Skewer shrimp on skewers. (If using wooden skewers make sure to soak in water for about 30 minutes).
7 Cook on a preheated lightly oiled barbecue grill for 4 minutes each side. Baste with the remaining marinade during cooking
