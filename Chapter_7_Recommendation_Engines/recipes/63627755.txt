Title:
Skillet Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cornmeal
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
2 TBL butter, melted
1 1/2 cups frozen corn kernals, thawed
1 scallion, chopped, abt 1/4 cup
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
additional butter, optional

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Coat 10" oven proof skillet with cooking spray.
2. In large bowl, stir together cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Add eggs, milk, melting butter, corn kernels, scallions and parsley; with whisk, stir until just blended.
3. Pour batter into skillet. Bake 20-23 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Cool in pan on rack for about 10 minutes before serving. If desired, serve with additional butter.
