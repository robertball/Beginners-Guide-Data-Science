Title:
Shipley Donuts Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen day old donuts
2c whole milk
1c heavy whipping cream
4 eggs
1c sugar
4T butter, melted
1T vanilla
1 stick butter
1c sugar
1t vanilla
1 egg

Directions:
Break up donuts and soak in milk/cream combined- let sit until milk is absorbed.
Combine eggs, sugar, butter, and vanilla and beat well.
Mix donuts (drain if necessary) gently with egg mixture.
Place into greased 9x13 pan and bake at 325 until set (45-60 minutes).
For glaze, cream 1 stick butter with 1c sugar over low heat until sugar disoolves.
Add 1t vanilla and 1 egg and beat well.
Pour over warm bread pudding.
