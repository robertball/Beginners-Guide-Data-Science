Title:
Carrot & Cumin Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. carrots, peeled
2 Tbsp. olive oil, plus more for finishing
2 whole garlic cloves, peeled
1-2 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. cumin powder
1 tsp. ground ginger
3 tsp. lemon juice
Sea salt and pepper
1 Tbsp. cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cut carrots into large chunks and place in roasting dish. Drizzle with olive oil, add garlic, chili powder, cumin, and ginger and toss well to coat.
Roast in oven until tender and aromatic, about 40-50 minutes.
Let cool slightly before blending in food processor.
Add lemon juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Add additional olive oil for creamier texture.
Stir in cilantro and serve.
