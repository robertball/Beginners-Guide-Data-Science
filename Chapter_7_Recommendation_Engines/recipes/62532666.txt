Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
soup base:
3 cups raw or cooked chicken
1 cup fresh or frozen carrots
1 cup fresh or frozen peas
1 can or 1 cup diced potatoes
2 can cream chicken soup
1 can corn with juice
1 soup can water
dumplings:
1 can refridgerated pop roll dough each roll quartered
or 2 cup biscuit mix mixed with 2/3 cup milk and 1 t italian seasoning

Directions:
bring soup base to boil
simmer until chicken cooked through
return to soft boil
drop quartered rolls or spoonfulls dough into boiling base
stir and let boil 5 min to keep dumplings from sticking together
reduce to simmer cover with foil or lid
simmer approximately 30 additional minutes until biscuits cooked through.
