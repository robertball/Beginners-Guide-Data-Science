Title:
Orange Snowball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970200.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
14 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine orange juice concentrate, milk, water, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, vanilla and ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
