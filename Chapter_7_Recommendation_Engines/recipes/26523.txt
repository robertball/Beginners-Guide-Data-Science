Title:
Tofu Tacos II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package silken firm tofu
1 (1.25 ounce) package ORTEGA® Taco Seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) can ORTEGA® Refried Beans
1 package (12) ORTEGA® Taco Shells, warmed
SARGENTO® Fancy Shredded Colby-Jack Cheese
sliced green onions
chopped fresh cilantro
ORTEGA® Thick & Smooth Taco Sauce

Directions:
PLACE tofu and seasoning mix in food processor; process until smooth.  Transfer to a medium, microwave-safe bowl.  Fold in beans.  Microwave on MEDIUM (50%) power, stirring halfway through, for 2 to 3 minutes.
FILL taco shells with tofu mixture.  Top with cheese, green onions, cilantro and taco sauce.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Our customers have been requesting this recipe for awhile, and now we know why! The addition of refried beans creates a pleasing texture. Even meat-eaters will love these tofu tacos.
