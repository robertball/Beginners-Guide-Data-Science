Title:
Crispy Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614307.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium russet potatoes cut into large wedges
1 tbs vegetable oil
Â¼ tsp ground black pepper
1/8 tsp salt
2 cloves garlic minced (optional)

Directions:
Place potatoes in large bowl filled with cold water. Let stand for 15 minutes.
Preheat oven to 425 F.
Drain potatoes in colander and dry using paper towels.
Transfer potatoes to a clean bowl sprinkle with oil, pepper and salt, toss gently to combine.
Arrange seasoned potatoes in a single layer on prepared baking sheet.
Sprinkle with garlic if desired.
Bake potatoes for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
