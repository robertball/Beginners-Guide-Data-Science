Title:
Italian Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups baking mix
1/2 cup hot water
2 TBSP butter
1 cup shredded cheddar jack cheese
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 tsp parsley flakes
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Add hot water to baking mix an mix until stiff dough forms. Let stand for 10 minutes. Turn dough onto a generously floured surface; gently roll dough in flour to coat. Shape into ball; knead 60 times.
Pat dough into a 11- inch square on an ungreased cookie sheet.   Spread melted butter on dough. Mix remaining ingredients; sprinkle over dough.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are light golden brown. Cut with a pizza cuter and serve.
Refrigerate any leftovers.
