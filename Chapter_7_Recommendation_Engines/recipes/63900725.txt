Title:
Winter Refresher Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1082262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water or water kefir
1-2 handfuls fresh spinach
1 apple, quartered, stem removed
1 orange, peeled
1 cucumber, peeled and quartered
1/2 cup fresh (or frozen) cranberries
1/2 inch ginger, peeled

Directions:
Place ingredients in order listed into the pitcher of a high-speed blender.
Beginning on low speed, quickly move to high speed.
Blend for one minute, or until smooth and liquefied.
Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
A bright and fresh green smoothie, sure to blast away the winter blues!
