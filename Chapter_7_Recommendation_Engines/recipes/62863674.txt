Title:
Mom's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T mustard
3 T sugar
1 cup mayo
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic salt
2 scallions, sliced thin
8 medium potatoes, cooked and cubed
2 large harboiled eggs, chopped
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, cubed
2 stalks celery, diced

Directions:
Boil potatoes.  Slice celery and add to potatoes.  Sprinkle with spices.  Mix gently to combine.  Add eggs, and cheese when cooled.  In a small bowl, combine sugar, mustard, mayo, and just enough milk to make creamy.  Pour over potatoes.  Coat well.  Chill before serving.
