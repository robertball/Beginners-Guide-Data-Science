Title:
Rocky Road Brownies in a Gift Jar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/4 cups chopped pecans
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup  unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup mini marshmallows

Directions:
Layer all of the ingredients except the marshmallows in a quart sized jar, packing each layer slighlty in preperation of the following layer.
Place marshmallows in a resealable sandwich bag and remove the air sealing tightly.
Place the marshmallow bag ontop of the other ingredients layers.
Cover the jar with the lid and decorate with decorative fabic, raffia or ribbon.
Attach following preperation instructions on a recipe card or gift card:
1 Jar Rocky Road Brownies Mix
1/2 cup melted butter
1/4 cup buttermilk
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease 8 x 8-inch baking pan. Remove marshmallows from the plastic bag in the jar and set aside.
Place remaing brownie mix in a large mixing bowl. Add melted butter, buttermilk, egg and vanilla. Stir until well blended.
Spread batter evenly in prepared pan and baked 25 to 30 minutes or until set. Sprinkle with reserved marshmallows. Bake 3 to 5 minutes or until marshmallows are fuffed and lightly melted. Allow to cool and enjoy!
