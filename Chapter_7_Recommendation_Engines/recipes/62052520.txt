Title:
Sweet Milk Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
2 cups sugar
1 can evaporated milk
1 stick margarine

Directions:
prepare cake mix as directed.  Mix sugar, milk and margarine in a 3 quart pot on top of stove.  Bring to boil, and boil until thick, a little less thick than a milkshake.  punch holes in cake with bottom end of a wooden spoon and spread icing on cake.
