Title:
Carmel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/690513.jpg
Ingredients:
6 c. unsalted popped popcorn
1 1/2 c. pecan halves
1/2 c.brown sugar
1/4 c. butter
1/8 c. light corn syrup
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200 degrees.  Place both popcorn and pecans on an ungreased 13x9 pan.  On stovetop, cook brown sugar, butter, corn syrup and salt over med, heat - continuing to stir - until bubbly around edges.  Continue to cook for 5 minutes - remove from heat.  Add baking soda and stir til foamy.  Pour over popcon and pecans.  Toss until well coated.  Bake 1 hr - stirring every 15 minutes.
