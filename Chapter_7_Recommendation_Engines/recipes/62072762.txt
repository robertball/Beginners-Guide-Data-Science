Title:
Squash Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large yellow squash
1 small onion
1/2 cup self-rising flour
1/2 cup self-rising cornmeal
salt to taste
pepper to taste
Vegetable oil

Directions:
Heat one inch of oil in a skillet over medium high hear. Grate squash and onion; add remaining ingredients and mix into a stiff batter.  Drop by heaping tablespoonfulls into hot oil and fry until brown on both sides.  (No liquid needs to be added.  There will be enough liquid from the squash and onion when you grate them to make a batter.)

Submitter: 
UCMOM
Description: 
This is a great way to use fresh yellow squash.  The batter will keep for up to a week in the refrigerator so it's convenient to cook only what you need at a time.
