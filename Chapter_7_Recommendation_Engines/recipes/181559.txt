Title:
Easy Turkey Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993227.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken and mushroom soup
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth
4 cups cut-up cooked turkey
1 (12 ounce) package Green Giant® Valley Fresh Steamers™ frozen mixed vegetables
2 cups Bisquick Heart Smart® mix
1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1 1/2 cups fat-free (skim) milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F (350 degrees F for glass pan). In 4-quart saucepan, heat soup, broth, turkey and vegetables to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Spread in ungreased 13x9-inch pan.
In medium bowl,stir remaining ingredients until blended. Pour evenly over soup mixture (crust will rise during baking).
Bake about 30 minutes or until light brown.
