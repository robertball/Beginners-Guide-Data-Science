Title:
Spicy Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBSP chopped garlic
1 pound large shrimp (head off, shells on, de-veined)
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 cup butter substitute
Kosher salt
1 tsp red pepper flakes
2 bay leaves
1 cup chunky pasta sauce
2 cup chicken stock
1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice or white wine
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

Directions:
Add oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat until butter melts. Add garlic, bay leaves and pepper flakes and saute until garlic starts to brown slightly. Add chicken stock and pasta sauce and stir to incorporate. Allow broth to come to a simmer. Add shrimp and cook until  the tail is pink on bottom and then flip to cook the other side about 2 minutes or so for each side.
Add lemon juice and parsley and toss to combine. Season to taste with salt. Remove Bay leaves and Serve immediately over pasta or rice if you like.
