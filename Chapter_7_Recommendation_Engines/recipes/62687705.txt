Title:
Versatile Spinach Salad with Poppy Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/604115.jpg
Ingredients:
DRESSING:
1/2c. sugar
1/3c. white wine vinegar (or cider vinegar)
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tbsp. grated onion
1 c. canola oil
1 tbsp. poppy seeds
SALAD:
6 to 7 cups torn fresh spinach
Peeled, diced fresh apple or sliced fresh strawberries
5 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/4 to 1/2 small sweet onion, sliced and separated into rings'
Croutons

Directions:
DRESSING:  Combine sugar, vinegar, salt, dry mustard and onion in a blender or food processor; cover and process until smooth.
While processing, gradually add oil in a steady stream.
Stir in poppy seeds.
SALAD:  In a salad bowl, toss the spinach, bacon, fruit, onion rings and croutons.
Drizzle salad dressing on salad below to taste and toss lightly to coat.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Basic spinach salad with Poppyseed Dressing  is versatile.  Be creative with substituting other ingredients to take advantage of seasonal fruits and vegetables.Modified from: Vidalia Onion Spinach Salad
