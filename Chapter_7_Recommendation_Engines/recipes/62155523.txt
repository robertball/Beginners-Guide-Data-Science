Title:
Bacon-Cheese Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces bacon, cooked and crumbled
8 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1 medium onion, peeled and quartered
8 hoagie sandwich rolls

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
Place bacon, cheese and onion into bowl of food processor, and process until well mixed. Spread roll halves generously with the mixture. Arrange on a baking sheet and broil until cheese is melted and bubbly, 3 to 5 minutes. Fold roll halves together and serve immediately.
