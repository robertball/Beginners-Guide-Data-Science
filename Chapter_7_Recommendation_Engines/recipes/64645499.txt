Title:
Big Ray's Coriander-crusted Tilapia with Couscous and Vegatables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4526249.jpg
Ingredients:
4 6 oz tilapia fillets
2 Tbsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt, divided
2 Tbsp olive oil, divided
8 small carrots chopped
4 green onions, thinly sliced
1/4 cup dry white wine
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup couscous
3/4 vegetable broth
3 cups loosely packed baby spinach

Directions:
Pat tilapia dry with paper towels.  Combine coriander, pepper and 3/4 tsp salt in small bowl.
Season tilapia on both bides with mixture.
Heat 1 Tbsp oil in large skillet over medium-high.  Add tilapia and cook until golden brown and crispy, about 4 minutes.  Flip and cook until just opaque, about 1 minute.  Transfer to plate and cover to keep warm.
Add remaining 1 Tbsp oil to skillet.  Add carrots and cook, stirring often, until almost tender, about 3 minutes  Add onions and cook, stirring often, until almost tender, about 2 minutes.  Add wine and lemon juice and bring to a boil.  Add couscous, broth and 1/4 tsp salt, bring back to a boil.  Cover and remove from heat.  Let sit about 10 minutes until liquid is gone.  Add spinach, stir and cover, allowing to sit about 5 minutes until spinach is wilted.
