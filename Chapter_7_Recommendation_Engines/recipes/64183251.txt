Title:
Fast stuffed mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 button mushrooms, stalks removed
2 tsb dried garlic flakes, divided
4 tsp tomato paste, divided
2 ounces cheese, divided
Salt

Directions:
Add 1/2 tsp dried garlic flakes to each mushroom.  Then add 1 tsp tomato paste per mushroom.  Sprinkle mushrooms with salt.  Top with 1/2 ounce cheese (1/2 a slice) cheese.  Cook on greased tin foil, 425 degrees for 10 minutes or until cheese is slightly brown

Scott Simmons

Very quick appetizer.
