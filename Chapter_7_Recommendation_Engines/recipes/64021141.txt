Title:
Chocolate Peanut Buddy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
6 tbsp. butter, softened
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/4 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 cup flour
2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Beat peanut butter and butter until creamy.
Add sugar, eggs and vanilla and beat until creamy.
Blend in flour and salt.
Spread in ungreased 13x9 pan.
Bake 25-30 minutes.
Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle chocolate chips over the top.
Let sit for 5 minutes to soften, then spread chocolate evenly.
Let cool completely before cutting.
