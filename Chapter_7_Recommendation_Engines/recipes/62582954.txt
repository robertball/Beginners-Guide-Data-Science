Title:
Meatloaf Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP Olive Oil
1 small onion, finely chopped (1/3 cup)
1 pound ground beef
1/2 pound ground sausage
2 eggs
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
3/4 cup Progresso Italian Bread Crumbs
2 TBSP chopped fresh basil
1 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1 TBSP balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 tsp sea salt
1 1/2 tsp pepper
1 1/2 cups marinara sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Oil 8x4 inch loaf pan. In saucepan heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion; cook just until soft. Remove from skillet to plate; cool completely.
Place cooled onion in large bowl. Add all remaining meat loaf ingredients except sauce. Press into loaf pan. Spread marinara sauce evenly over top of loaf
Bake 50 to 60 minutes or until meat thermometer inserted in center reads 160 degrees.  Remove from oven, let stand 5 minutes.  Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
LaFonda Merrick
Description: 
Pork and beef makes for a delicious Italian style meatloaf.
