Title:
New England Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5   large eggs, beaten
5   cups  milk
3/4  cup  sugar
1  1/2  teaspoons vanilla
6  slices potato bread
1   cup raisins
Cinnamon Sugar

Directions:
In a large (3 qt) casserole dish, combine eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla.  Mix until well blended.  Add raisins. Cut bread into 2" cubes and drop into mixture. Sprinkle top with cinnamon sugar.  Place entire casserole dish into a pan of hot water. Water should be about 1 inch deep in pan.
Bake 1 hour 30 minutes in 350 degree oven, or until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Center should puff up in center. Serve with Whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Brenda Laughlin Sumner
Description: 
I make this for friends and family anytime a pick-me-up is needed.  Serve with whipped cream on top or your favorite rum sauce.
