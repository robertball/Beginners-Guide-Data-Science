Title:
Big Ray's  Sweet Potato & Bean Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4551294.jpg
Ingredients:
3 medium sweet potato, peeled and grated with large hole s on box grater
1/4 cup olive oil
3/4 chipotle powder
1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
1 1/2 black pepper, divided
2 plum tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 cup fresh or frozen corn
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
1 Tbsp fresh lime juice
2 15 oz cans pinto or black beans, drained
8 oz sharp cheddar cheese, grated
8 large burrito-sized flour torillias

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Toss sweet potatoes with oil, chili powder, 3/4 tsp salt and 3/4 tsp pepper.  Spread on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.  Let roast 35 to 40 minutes, stirring halfway through until browned.  Let cool completely.
Combine tomatoes, onion, corn, lime juice, 1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper in medium bowl.  Set aside for 15 minutes, strain and discard liquid.  Toss beans with remaining salt and pepper.
Divide beans, cheese, corn salsa and sweet potatoes evenly among tortillas.  Roll into burritos, wrap each separately in foil and freeze in single layer.  Freeze for up to 3 months.  To heat,  remove foil, wrap each burrito in a paper towel and microwave until heated through, 3 to 6 minutes.
