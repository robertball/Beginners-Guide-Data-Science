Title:
Banana Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992654.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package 1/3 less fat cream cheese
3/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 bananas
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 (10 ounce) cans reduced fat crescent rolls

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, cream cheese, bananas and vanilla in a medium mixing bowl using a hand mixer.  Mix until creamy.
Place crescent rolls on an ungreased baking sheet.  Spread about 1 tablespoon of filling onto each crescent roll and roll into crescent shape.
Bake in preheated oven 12 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Serve these kid-friendly treats warm or cold.
