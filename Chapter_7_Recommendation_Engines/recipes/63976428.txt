Title:
Cocoa Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups confectioners' (powdered) sugar
3/4 c Hershey's Cocoa
1/8 tsp salt
6 T milk or cream
2 tsp vanilla
1/3 c butter

Directions:
Combine sugar, cocoa and salt in a large mixer bowl.  Add milk and vanilla.  Blend until smooth, either by mixer or by hand.  Add butter and beat until the mixture is spreading consistency.  Makes about 2 cups frosting.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
A cocoa-flavored frosting, great over Wacky Cake.
