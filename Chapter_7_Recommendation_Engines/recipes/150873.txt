Title:
Coconut Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981085.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ Coconut
1 ounce milk
5 ounces pineapple juice
1 spear pineapple

Directions:
Add Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) Coconut, milk, and pineapple juice in a blender.
Blend 10-15 seconds and pour into a specialty glass.
Garnish with pineapple spear.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add fruit flavor to your drinks without all the slicing by using Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ in Coconut, Mango, Pineapple or Passion Fruit.
