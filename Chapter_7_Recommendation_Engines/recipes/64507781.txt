Title:
Pizza Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boboli personal pizza shells
1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
1 1/2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 tbsp red wine vinegar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
3 roma tomatoes, sliced
1 tbsp roasted minced garlic
1/2 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
3 tbsp kalamata olives, sliced
1/2 cup arugula or fresh basil, thinly shredded (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine olive oil, both vinegars, salt and pepper.   Whisk briskly to combine.   Add tomatoes and stir to coat.   Allow to marinate until ready to build pizza.
Brush the crust with some of the tomato marinade, spread roasted garlic, tomatoes, cheese, olives and greens between the two pizzas.
Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes or until mozzarella is melted and tomatoes are warmed through.
