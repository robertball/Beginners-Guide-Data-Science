Title:
Raspberry Balsamic Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/798803.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 tablespoon raspberry preserves
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, raspberry preserves, onion powder, black pepper, and salt in a small bowl; continue whisking until thick and smooth.
