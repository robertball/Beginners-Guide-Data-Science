Title:
Kathleen's Fake-out Chilaquiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/363166.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of Green Enchilada sauce or Salsa Verde
1/2 cup of Shredded Pork, Chicken, or Beef
10-12 large Tortilla Chips
2 TBS Queso Fresco

Directions:
Heat enchilada sauce and shredded pork until hot.
Add tortilla chips and cook until chips are soft and covered in sauce.
Place in a bowl and top with crumbled Queso Fresco
