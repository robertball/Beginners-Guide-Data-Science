Title:
Minted Fresh Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Sugar
1/3 Cup Orange Juice
1/3 Cup Fresh Squeezed Lemon Juice
1/3 Cup Water
1/4 tsp Mint Extract
8 to 10 Cups Assorted Cut Fresh Fruit (watermelon, pineapple, strawberries, granny smith apples, kiwi and seedless red grapes work well)
3 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Mint Leaves, loosley packed.

Directions:
In Large bowl, combine all ingredients except fruit. Whisk until sugar dissolves.
Place fruit in bowl and toss to coat.  Refrigerate for 2 - 4 hours, allowing flavors to meld, stirring occasionally. Serve in pretty bowl, garnished with sprigs of mint.

Submitter: 
SAVVYHOSTESS
Description: 
Tired of the same old heavy creamy fruit salad recipies?  Give this healthy and flavorful recipe a try!  It's best in summer, when the fruit is at it's best.  Trust me...you wont miss the marshmallow-y, whipped topping laiden fruit salads out there!
