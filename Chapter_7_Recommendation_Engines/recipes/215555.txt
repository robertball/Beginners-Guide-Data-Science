Title:
Spicy Ham and Havarti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989470.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice Havarti cheese
2 slices rye bread
2 slices tomato
2 slices cooked, skinless, lean ham, chopped
1 spicy banana pepper, chopped
1 tablespoon chopped parsley
1 tablespoon Hellmann's® 1/2 the Fat mayonnaise-type dressing
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix ham, peppers, parsley and mayonnaise. Layer everything between two slices of bread and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Hellmann's®
Description: 
Chopped spicy banana pepper and slices of Havarti cheese and tomato add a tasty twist to a traditional ham salad sandwich.
