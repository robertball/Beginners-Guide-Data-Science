Title:
Grasshopper Pudding Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Famous chocolate wafers, divided (or use sm. package 100 calorie chocolate wafers)
1-1/2 C cold fat-free milk
1/4 t peppermint extract
1 pkg. (1 oz) pistachio flavor sugar free instant pudding mix
2 C thawed sugar free whipped topping
1 square semi-sweet chocolate, chopped

Directions:
Crush 2 wafers (or 1/2 pkg.) and sprinkle onto bottom of 9in pie plate sprayed with cooking spray
Beat milk, extract, and pudding mix with whisk 2 min.
Stir in whipped topping and chopped chocolate; spread into pie plate; top with remaining wafers that have been broken into chunks
Freeze 6 hrs. or until firm. Remove from freezer 10 min. before serving; let stand at room temperature to soften slightly fefore cutting to serve
