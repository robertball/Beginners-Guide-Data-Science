Title:
Easy Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
2 cups salsa
1 3/4 cup black beans, drained
1 T chili powder
1/4 cup green chili peppers
3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
2 cups sour cream
1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1 cup chopped diced  tomato, drained
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded Monteray Jack Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook ground beef until no longer pink. Drain and return to skillet. Stir in salsa, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed. Stir in beans, chili peppers and chili powder, and heat through.
Spray a pans with cooking spray. Spread crushed tortilla chips in dish, and then spoon beef mixture over chips. Spread sour cream over beef, and sprinkle olives, green onion, and tomato over the sour cream. Top with cheeses.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.

Description: 
This is an easy and very tasty dish. I often substitute ground turkey and low fat dairy products and it is still delicious! Serve with chips, salsa and green salad.Modified from: Easy Mexican Casserole
