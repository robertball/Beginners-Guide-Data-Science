Title:
Wilted Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:1 pkg. raw spinach, 1 small head lettuce, 4 medium potatoes, 4 hard-boiled eggs, 1 sweet Bermuda or Vidalia onion, 1/2 lb. bacon, cut-up.   Dressing: 3 rounded T. sugar, 4T. mayonnaise, 1/8t. salt,3 T.vinegar, bacon grease, 1 t, spicy brown mustard.

Directions:
Boil and dice potates with skin on, Brown bacon, drain but keep grease. In bowl tear spinach and lettuce into bite sized parts. Add diced potatoes, chopped eggs, onion-all chopped small, and bacon. Just before serving heat dressing mixture with bacon grease, heat really HOT, Pour over salad, mix well and serve.Will amply feed 8/10 people very nicely. Enjoy!!  Sunshine
