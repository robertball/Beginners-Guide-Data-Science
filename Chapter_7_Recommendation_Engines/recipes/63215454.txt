Title:
Five Ingredient Meatballs! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/834218.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Pounds of lean ground beef
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 (26 ounce) spaghetti sauce
1 egg
1/2 cup of bread crumbs

Directions:
Thourougly mix together all ingredients. Shape into 2" metaballs. Brown meatballs in a lightly greased skillet. Drain oil. Return to pan and pour spaghetti sauce over meatballs; cover and simmer for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
Carlene Davis
Description: 
Simple yet tasty homemade meatballs if your on a budget or short time frame! Can be used for spaghetti or meatball subs!
