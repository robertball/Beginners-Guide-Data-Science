Title:
Classic Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, shredded
1 carrot
1/4 onion, chopped
1/2 cup Mayo
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup white sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cabbage, carrot, onion, mayo, vinegar and sugar. Stir until the ingredients are well mixed. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
