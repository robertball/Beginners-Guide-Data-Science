Title:
Rice Verlynne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbls butter
1/4 cup pastini (small soup pasta)
1/2 cup rice
1 14 oz can beef broth
2 oz water
1 Tbls dehydrated onion flakes
1 rounded tsp beef boullion
1/4 tsp freshly grated black pepper
1/2 tsp Kitchen Bouquet

Directions:
Melt the butter over medium heat in a small sauce pan.  Add the pastini, and cook until pastini are golden, stirring occasionally.  Add the rice, stir, and cook until rice is golden, stirring occasionally.  Add the broth, water, onion flakes, beef boullion and black pepper.  Increase heat to high, bring to vigorous boil, stir.  Reduce heat to just less than medium, and wait for water to calm.  Stir again, cover, and cook for 20 minutes.  Turn heat off, but leave rice on ring, covered, for an additional 10 minutes.  Serve.

Description: 
A vigorously seasoned hearty beefy rice, ideal to pair with steaks and shish kababs.
