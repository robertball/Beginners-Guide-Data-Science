Title:
Quick and Easy Dirt Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of chocolate cream filled cookies
3 small boxes of instant french vanilla pudding
1 c. powdered sugar
5 c. milk
12 oz. frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Crunch up half of the bag of cookies and put on bottom of 9x13 dish.  Mix pudding, powdered sugar and milk together.  Fold in whipped topping.  Pour this mixture on top of cookies.  Crunch up remaining bag of cookies and put on top of pudding mixture.  Chill in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This is a version of dirt cake but but alot quicker and easier to make.  I fix it in a 9x13 dish but can also be prepared in a flower pot.
