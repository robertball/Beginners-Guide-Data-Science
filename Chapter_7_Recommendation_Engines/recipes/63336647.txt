Title:
Fruit Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/4 cup powdered sugar
7 oz jar marshmallow cream
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups light juice, such as apple, white grape or white cranberry
1/2 cup sugar
3 Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 cup lemon juice
various fruits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees..
In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, and sugar.   Using fingers, mix in butter until well blended.   Press dough into an ungreased pizza pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly browned. Cool.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, marshmallow creme, powdered sugar and vanilla until completely blended.   Spread on cooled crust.  In small saucepan, whisk together juice, sugar, cornstarch and lemon juice.  Turn on medium heat and cook until bubbling and thickened.   Arrange desired fruit on top of filling, paint fruit with glaze and chill.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
This is a delicious, attractive dessert, perfect for a children's birthday party. The crust is made from cookie dough pressed into a pizza pan. After the crust is baked and cooled, it's frosted with sweetened cream cheese and decorated with slices of fresh fruit. Chill well before serving.Modified from: Fruit Pizza I
