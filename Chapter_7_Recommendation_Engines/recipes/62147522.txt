So-Lo Parmesean Crusted Tilapia Recipe

1t	Kraft Parmesan cheese
1t	Unseas. Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Place fish in microwave safe dish.
Brush olive oil on top.
Sprinkle Mrs. Dash, Bread crumbs and Cheese.
Cover with wrap and poke holes in top.
Cook in microwave for 3 minutes.
Remove and let cool 2 minutes before opening wrap and serving.
