Title:
Tootsie Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cracked ice
1/2 ounce Kahlua
1 1/2 ounce dark Creme De Cacao
3/4 ounce orange juice
prechilled shot glass

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with cracked ice, Kahlua, dark Creme De Cacao, and orange juice.  Stir well.  Pour drink into shot glass.
