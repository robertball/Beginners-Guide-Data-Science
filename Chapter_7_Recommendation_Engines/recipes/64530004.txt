Title:
Simple Pho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pho spice mix
Mushrooms
Bok Choy
Green Onions
Beef, Pho, or Vegetable broth
Rice Noodles

Directions:
Boil noodles and broth separate. For broth, bring Pho spice pack, one portion of your broth of choice, and two portions of water to a boil. When it comes to cooking, avoid tap water. Simmer for 15 minutes.
Drain noodles and serve in a soup bowl. Add mushrooms, bok choy, and green onions to bowl. Pour Pho broth and enjoy!

Submitter: 
ModishHouseWife
Description: 
A simple Pho recipe is handy for a rushed rainy day. Sometimes a shortcut to our favorite recipes is just as comforting and delicious. We bought the Pho Spice Mix at our local Asian Market, it makes things simple and it taste just perfect!
