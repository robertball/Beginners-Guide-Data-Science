Title:
Ginger Marmalade Jelly Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/849925.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 C. Granulated sugar
1 C.  All Purpose flour
1 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 C. Ginger Marmalade jam

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
In a large mixing bowl break eggs and beat with an electric hand mixer until frothy and smooth
Add sugar to eggs and beat until light and fluffy
Slowly add dry ingredients to egg mixture on medium speed until all the flour is thoroughly mixed in and batter is nice and smooth
Pour batter into 10 by 15 inch jelly roll pan and carefully level batter to all corners of the pan
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned on top
Prepare a dry dish towel coated lightly with icing sugar and invert cake roll onto towel and roll up gently into the towel
When cooled unroll jelly wrap and spread ginger marmalade over the inside of roll and roll up and store in refrigerator for longer storage period

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A plain and simple jelly roll cake with ginger marmalade on the inside to make a nice dessert
