Title:
Ukranian  Stuffed Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 big heads of cabbage
2 packages Pork Salt Fat Back
4 cups Rice
3 lb. Chop Meat
4-6 Onions
4 strips of bacon
1 16oz of tomato sauce
16 oz water
2-3 packages of george washington broth
a lot of salt

Directions:
1.Core Cabbage.
2.Par Boil for 10 minutes
3.Remove leaves while in pot, trim if needed
4.Cook Rice in a large pot, set aside.
5.Cook Bacon, leave bacon fat in pan, cut up bacon into pieces.
6.Add diced onions to pan and fry till yellowish and soft.
7.In other pan, cook meat and drain.
8.Add onions, salt, pepper and broth packages to meat and mix well.
9.Pour meat mixture into rice pot and mix well. Add salt and pepper if needed.
10.Roll and fill cabbage.
11.Put cabbage in Roaster pan, placing chopped salt fat back in between leaves.
12.Mix 1 can of water and tomato sauce and pour into pan.
13.Cover with left-over leaves. And place cover on.
14.Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
