Title:
Spicy Spinach and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken stock
1 can great northern beans
3 cups fresh spinach
1 small onion
2 lg cloves of garlic minced
3/4 cups salsa
1/2 tbs canola oil
1 tsp oregano
1 1/2 tsp roasted cumin
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
*optional 1 1/2 lbs sausage sliced
Butter flavored croutons and freshly grated parmesean cheese to top

Directions:
Chop the onion and sautee in 1/2 tbs canola oil till brown.  Add the minced garlic and sautee another 60 seconds.  Add to crockpot along with the rest of the ingredients (excpet fo the croutons and cheese).  Stir well and cook on high for 4 hours.  Top with croutons and parmesean cheese just before serving

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
Without the sausage this is a very low fat recipe.  The toppings really make the soup though, so don't skip the parmesean and croutons!
