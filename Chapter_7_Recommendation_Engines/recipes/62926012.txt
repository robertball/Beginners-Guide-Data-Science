Title:
Balsamic Slow Cooker Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714797.jpg
Ingredients:
4-large center cut pork chops
3-12 oz cans diced tomatoes
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup balsalmic vinegar
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp basil leaves
1 tsp bacon flavored salt

Directions:
Brown the pork chops in a hot skillet for about 3 mins on each side. Remove them toi the slow cooker. Add the diced tomatoes,balsalmic vinegar, garlic,oregano,Italian seasoning, basil leaves,parmesan cheese,soy sauce and bacon flavored salt to the slow cooker
Cook on low for 6 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
Pork chops that are browned then put in with savory crushed tomatoes in the slow cooker until it melts in your mouth
