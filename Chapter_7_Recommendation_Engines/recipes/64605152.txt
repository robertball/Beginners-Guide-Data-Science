Title:
Chicken stew with tomato, olives and cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken thighs
Salt pepper
2 T olive oil
1/2 c balsamic or red wine vinegar
1 small head cauliflower
1 medium onion
5-8 cloves garlic
1 tsp cinnamon or more
2 tsp cumin or more
1 tsp paprika or more
1 T oregano or more
2 T fresh thyme plus sprigs
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes, partially blended
1/2 to 1 c chicken broth
Handful olives
goat cheese (optional)
fresh parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven 450
Chop and roast cauliflower with olive oil and salt at 450 top shelf
Season chicken with salt and pepper
Brown chicken thighs (I did 10 min, 6-7 first then turned,  maybe little too long) on medium high heat
Remove chicken and put in baking dish
Deglaze pan with balsamic and scrape bits
Pour balsamic over chicken
Put chicken in oven on bottom shelf
Sauté onion and garlic with salt until soft and start to brown
Add herbs and spices until fragrant
Add tomatoes (purée some)
Add broth
Add olives and some or all cauliflower
Bring back to simmer
Pour tomato Sauce over chicken
Bake at 450 for another 30 min or until heated
served with goat cheese and parsley
Might have cooked too long or browned too long. Better of bake at lower heat
