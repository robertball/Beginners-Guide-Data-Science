Title:
Taco Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/652652.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons dried minced onion
3 teaspoons chili powder*
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine all of the ingredients. Store in an airtight container in a cool dry place for up to 1 year.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home Test Kitchen
Description: 
Our Test Kitchen developed this spicy, salt-free alternative to packaged taco seasoning. Stir it into dips or ground beef, sprinkle on chicken or wherever you want a Southwestern flair.
