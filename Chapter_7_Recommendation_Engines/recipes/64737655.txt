Title:
Chip's Cowboy Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6310677.jpg
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon
1 medium onion, chopped
1 lb. ground beef
1 15 oz. can EACH
kidney beans (drained)
pinto beans (drained)
black beans (drained and rinsed)
Great Northern beans (drained)
pork and beans
1 c. ketchup
1/2 c. dark brown sugar
2 T. molasses
1 tsp. dry/ground mustard
Coarse Kosher salt and coarse ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Fry bacon in Dutch oven to desired crispness.  Set aside on paper towel to drain. When cool chop or break into pieces.
Sauté onion in rendered bacon fat until slightly opaque.  Add ground beef to pan and brown, breaking up into 1/2 to 1 inch chunks, until no longer pink.
Add beans and bacon to pot.  Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl, then add to pot.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Gently stir to combine all.
Cover pot and cook in 350° oven for 45 minutes.
