Title:
Kevin's Amazing Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds lean ground beef.
4 10 oz cans tomato soup.
1 5.5 oz can tomato paste
1 large onion chopped.
1 large clove garlic
15-20 quartered white mushrooms
1\4 cup canola oil.
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp curry powder
1 tsp crushed chilli
0.5 tsp salt
0.5 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Brown ground beef in large pot and drain.  Add vegitable oil, chopped onion and garlic, curry powder, crushed chilli, brown sugar, salt and pepper. Cook at high heat stirring frequently until onions become translucent.  Add tomato soup, tomato paste and mushrooms.  Cover bring to boil and reduce heat to low.  Cook at least 1 hour stirring frequently enough so that sauce does not burn to the bottom of the pot.  Boil spaghetti al dente or until just cooked and serve immediately.  Please enjoy this family favorite!

Submitter: 
whooley
Description: 
This recipe was obtained by my great grandfather in Italy during the First World War.  As the story goes, he was served the recipe at a small restarant and asked for the recipe.  Originally, the sauce was made on a large scale and bottled.  He scaled down the recipe to the following.
