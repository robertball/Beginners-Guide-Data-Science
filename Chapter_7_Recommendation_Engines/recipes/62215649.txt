Title:
Polish Turkey Cotletti Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/312796.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet Turkey Mince
1 handful of chopped parsley
1 beaten egg
1 handful of breadcrumbs
1 teaspoon of salt (and the same with black pepper)
Olive oil to fry

Directions:
Place your mince into a deep bowl and add all the remaining ingredients. Mix very well.
Mould the burgers into oval shapes, no bigger than your palm.
Fry on a moderate heat with a lid on the frying pan.
Once cooked turn up the heat until the burgers are crispy on both sides.

Submitter: 
Tatiana
Description: 
This is a recipe passed down to me from my grandmother. They are hugely delicious and can be eaten both hot or cold. They go very well with Russian potato salad or just simply in a bun.
