Title:
Best Ever Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup Buttermilk
1 1/2 cup Flour
2 teaspoon Baking Powder
1 teaspoon Baking Soda
1 teaspoon Salt
3 teaspoon Sugar
6 tablespoon Vegetable Oil, divided
1 Egg

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt & sugar.  Stir in buttermilk, vegetable oil & beaten egg.  Stir until smooth.  Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in skillet over medium heat.  Pour batter in a 5" pancake.  When batter forms small bubbles on the top, flip the pancake.  Remove when golden brown on each side.  Remove from skillet & repeat by adding additional tablespoon of oil & pour batter until completely used.  Serve with warm syrup.

Submitter: 
Stefany Grider Baldwin
Description: 
Light & fluffy!  These are by far the best pancakes I've ever tasted!!  Just like mom's!
