Title:
Easy Sweet and Sour Goat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs goat stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
1 - 18 oz bottle BBQ sauce
1 - 20 oz can diced pineapple, drained
1/2 yellow onion, chopped
2 sweet bell peppers, large diced

Directions:
1. Put goat and BBQ sauce in a slow cooker, set on high for 4 hours.  Turn the slow cooker down to low and continue cooking for another 4 hours.
2. Stir in pineapple.
3. Lightly coat a medium skillet with cooking spray, saute onion and bell peppers till slightly softened.
4. Stir vegetables into slow cooker.
5. Viola!  Tender, delicious, and tangy.

Submitter: 
Tera Taylor
Description: 
I'm new to cooking goat.  I came up with this recipe while trying to find easy ways to do it.  The secret is long, slow cooking and a flavorful sauce.  I love that it is so easy.  Serve it over rice or quinoa for a super filling dinner!
