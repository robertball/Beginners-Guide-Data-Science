Title:
Farmhouse BBQ Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1076683.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can buttermilk biscuits
1 lb ground beef
1/2 c honey bbq sauce
3 t brown sugar
1 t cider vinegar
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Separate biscuits; flatten into 5" circles. Grease muffin tins, put dough in.
Brown ground beef, drain. Mix bbq sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, & chili powder. stir until smooth. Add to meat & mix well.
Divide mix among biscuits using 1/4 c. Sprinkle with cheese.
Cook at 375 for 18-20 mins. Cool 5 mins.
