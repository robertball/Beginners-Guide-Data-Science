Title:
Kim Chee Squats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cabbage, chinese
1 tbsp salt
2 tbsp scallion, chopped
2 tsp garlic clove, mashed
1 tbsp chili powder
2 tsp ginger, finely chopped
1/2 cup soy sauce, light
1/2 cup vinegar, wine
2 tsp sugar, granulated
oil, sesame

Directions:
1> Chop the cabbage coarsely and place in a glass dish.  Sprinkle with the salt and let stand for 3 to 4 hours.  The cabbage will witl.  Mash with fingers until the cabbage is still softer, then drain off excess liguid and add the remaining ingredients except the sesame oil.
2> Transfer to a large glass jar, seal and leave in a cool place for at least 24 hours before using.  It will keep for up to a week in the refrigerator.  Sprinkle with a few drops of sesame oil before using.
