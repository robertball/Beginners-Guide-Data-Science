Title:
Caramel Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293474.jpg
Ingredients:
5 large Granny Smith apples
4 Snickers candy bars
8 oz cream cheese, softened
7 oz marshmallow fluff

Directions:
Cut apples into bite size chunks.  Cut each snicker bar small pieces.  Mix together in a large bowl.  In a separate bowl mix together softened cream cheese and marshmallow fluff.  Fold into apple/candy mixture.
Chill in refrigerator at least an hour and serve.

Submitter: 
23DAISYS
Description: 
Granny Smith apples, cream cheese mixture and Snickers candy combine to make a tasty salad great for picnics or anytime for that matter.
