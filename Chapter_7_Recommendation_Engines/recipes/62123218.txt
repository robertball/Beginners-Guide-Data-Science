Title:
Easy chicken delish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 package of onion soup mix
2 cans golden mushroom soup
1/2 cup of dry white wine or milk (I use 1/4 cup of each)
package of fresh sliced mushrooms (optional)
fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix the soups, wine or milk, pepper and mushrooms.
Spray the bottom of a baking dish with oil.
Place chicken at the bottom of the pan
Pour mixture over the chicken
Bake for 1 hour
Tip you can do this in the crock pot on low for 5-6 hours
