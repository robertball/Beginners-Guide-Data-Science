Title:
Greek Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked Quinoa
2 cups water
1/3 cup sliced, pitted Kalamata Olives
1/4 cup Feta Cheese Crumbles
1/3 cup halved Cherry Tomatoes
2 tbsp shredded, fresh Basil Leaves
1/2 cup Olive Oil
You can choose what ingredients you would like more or less of inorder to match your tastes.

Directions:
Rinse quinoa. Put quinoa and water in medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmmer for 10-15 minutes or until all water is absorbed. Let the quinoa cool as you prepare the rest of the ingredients.  Slice the Kalamata Olives and Cherry Tomatoes. Shred Basil leaves. Pour Olive Oil over the cooled quinoa, stir. Fold in the remaining ingredients.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lindsay
Description: 
Great to bring as a side dish to a BBQ or as lunch for the week. Easy to make in advance.
