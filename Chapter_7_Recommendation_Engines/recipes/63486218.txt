Title:
Spanish Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1 cup white onion, chopped
1 cup green pepper, chopped
1 - 10 oz bag of egg noodles
1 - 32 oz can of crushed tomatoes
1/4 teaspoon of hot pepper flakes - optional
1 tsp Red Hot sauce
8 - 10 slices American Cheese

Directions:
In an electric skillet cook ground beef and hot pepper flakes, until just past pink and drain.
In skillet saute onion and green pepper along with the red hot sauce.
Add ground beef back to skillet, combine with onion and peppers; spread evenly in bottom of the skillet.
Add bag of noodles over top of beef mixture and dumped crushed tomatoes over top of the tomatoes.
Cover and cook, 20 minutes until noodles are soft and moisture is absorbed, stirring twice.
Add 8 - 10 slices of American Cheese, cover to melt.
