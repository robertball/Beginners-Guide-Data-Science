Title:
Fresh Strawberry Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 CUPS FRESH STRAWBERRIES CRUSHED
1 (6 OZ PACKAGE STRAWBERRY JELLO
3 CUPS MINIATURE MARSHMALLOWS
1 PACKAGE YELLOW CAKE MIX

Directions:
Pour strawberries in 9x13 cake pan
Sprinkle Strawberry Jello on top
Add min. marshmallows
prepare cake mix from package directions and pour over it all. Bake in 350 oven about 40 to 50 minutes when toothpick comes out clean let sit about 15 min. run a knife around the edges and turn onto serving tray. Refrigerate.
