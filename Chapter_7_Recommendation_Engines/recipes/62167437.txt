Title:
Festive Finger Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Deli Roast Beef sliced medium
1 container Boursin Cheese w/chives
1 c. mayonaise
2 T. horseradish
1 pkg. Pepperidge Farm Pumpernickel Ryes
2 jars roasted red peppers in a jar
Sliced green onions(to taste)

Directions:
Place cheese in center of roast beef, roll up tucking sides in, then roll in plastic wrap and refrigerate.
Julienne the roasted red peppers
Mix 1 c. mayo and 2 T horseradish & spread on the Ryes.
Cut beef in 1-1/2‚Äù slices - place on Ryes top w/ red peppers & onions.
