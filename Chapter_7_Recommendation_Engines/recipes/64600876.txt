Title:
Sassy's Sweet Muffins Recipe

Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2c milk
1/4c oil
1 1/2c flour
1/2c sugar
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F and spray muffin pan. Mix, batter will be lumpy, 2/3 full in muffin pan, bake 20 to 25 minutes.
