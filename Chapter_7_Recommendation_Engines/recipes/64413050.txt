Title:
Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2281186.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cup flour
2 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup almond milk
1 tablespoon butter
1 package of yeast (1/4 oz)
butter
cinnamon sugar

Directions:
One bowl put all dry ingredients and mix (first 4 ingredients listed above)
Microwave almond milk and butter until melted, but not boiling.
Put liquid into 1 package yeast and let rest for 10 minutes
Add dry ingredients to yeast mixture, 1/2 cup at a time.
Knead for 10 minutes
Cover dough lightly with cooking oil and let rest until doubled (in a warm place).
Flatten into rectangle using a rolling pin.
Lightly cover bread with butter and sprinkle cinnamon sugar until covered but not more than the butter can absorb.
Roll into a log shape
slice into 1/2 inch circles.
Place slices in prepared pan and cover. Let rise until doubled.
Bake at 325 degrees for about 30 minutes or until done.
