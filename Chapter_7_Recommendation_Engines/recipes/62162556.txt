Title:
No-cinammon streusel topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons cold butter, chopped
3 tablespoons brown sugar
3 tablespoons chopped pecans or walnuts
6 tablespoons flour

Directions:
Place all ingredients together and cut with two knives until mixture looks like small crumbs.  Sprinkle on unbaked muffins or cakes and bake as directed for your recipe.

Submitter: 
Kim C.
Description: 
My boyfriend doesn't like cinammon, but I love streusel topping on muffins and cakes, so I created my own version that is cinammon-free, simple to make, and still delicious.
