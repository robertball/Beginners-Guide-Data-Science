Title:
Ed's Blueberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1039812.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Pts whole Blueberries
2 Cups Sugar
3 Tbs. Powdered Pectin
2 Tbs. of Lemon Juice
1/2 Tbs. Butter
5 Half Pint Jelly jars, lids and bands

Directions:
Wash blueberries and remove any stems that might remain.  Pulse in Vita-mix a pint at a time for 30 seconds.
In FreshTech cook, add Pectin, Fruit, Lemon Juice and butter and process on Jam cycle.  When prompted add Sugar.
Place in Jelly jars remembering to wipe the rim of the jars and process in a boiling water bath for at least 10 minutes.  Remove jars and allow to cool.
Check seals, date and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Ed Canipelli
Description: 
Great for when the blueberries come into season and to be able to enjoy all year long.
