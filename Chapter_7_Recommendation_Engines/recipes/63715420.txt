Title:
Caramel Heathbar Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chocolate cake mix
1 can condensed milk
8 oz caramel sauce
8 oz cream cheese room temperature
8 oz cool whip
1 8 oz package Heath toffee bits

Directions:
Prepare and bake cake mix in 13 X 9 pan as directed. Cool cake for 10 minutes, then using a drinking straw, poke holes all over cake. Mix caramel and condensed milk together. When cake is fully cooled, poor milk evenly over cake.  Sprinkle half heath bits over cake. Beat cream cheese and cool whip; spread over cake; sprinkle with remaining heath bits. Store covered in refrigerator.

Description: 
Kids love this cake. Also good with butterfinger bits.
