Title:
Dutch Oven Cherry Pecan Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 21 oz cans of cherry pie filling
2 cans of crushed pineapple (in heavy or light syrup as desired)
1 Orange Supreme cake mix
1 yellow cake mix
1 medium bag of shredded coconut
2 sticks butter or margerine
2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
Line Dutch oven with parchment liner or heavy aluminum foil.
Layer the ingredients into the oven as follows, without stirring: 1 can of cherry pie filling, one can of pineapple, half the coconut, 1 cup of the pecans, the Orange Supreme cake mix, one stick of butter/margerine (cut into pats), last can of cherry pie filling, last can of pineapple, 1/2 cup of the pecans, 1/2 of the remaining coconut, the yellow cake mix, the last stick of butter/margerine (cut into pats), remaining coconut and pecans.
Cover with the lid and cook with medium coals under and above the oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour, turning and replenishing the coals periodically.
