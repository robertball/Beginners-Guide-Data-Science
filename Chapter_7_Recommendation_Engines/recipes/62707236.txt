Title:
Bavariran Style Round Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large onions, cut in half and sliced
2 Tbls Packed brown sugar
2 Tbls cider vinegar
1 tps salt
1 Tbls beef base
2 1/2 lbs beef, round steak, stew meat, roast
1 Tbls flour
1 package wide egg noodles

Directions:
Place onion in 4-5 quat slow cooker, top with brown sugar and vinegar, salt and beef base, top with beef, sprinkle flour over the beef. Cook 7-8 hours.
