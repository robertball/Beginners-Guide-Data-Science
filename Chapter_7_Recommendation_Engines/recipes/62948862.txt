Title:
Spicy mango scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds sea scallops
1 Tbsp. peanut oil
1 medium red onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 cup unsweetened pineapple juice
1/4 cup mango chutney

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute scallops in oil for 2-3 minutes on each side or until firm and opaque.  Remove and keep warm.
In the same skillet, saute onion until tender.  Add garlic and pepper flakes; cook 1 minute longer.  Stir in pineapple juice.  Bring to a boil; cook until liquid is reduced by half.  Remove from the heat.  Add chutney and scallops; stir to coat.

Submitter: 
Kinza
Description: 
This sweet and spicy seafood combo gives off enough heat to make the whole family warm up to its great flavors!  Be sure to buy the larger sea scallops for this recipe; cooking times would be off for the smaller bay scallops.  Courtesy of Healthy Cooking July 2011
