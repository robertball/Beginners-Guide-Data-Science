Title:
Chicken Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cooked chicken
3/4 C mayonaise
1/2 C dried cranberries or 1 cup grape halves
1/4 C sliced almonds
1/4 C chopped green onions
1/4 C finely chopped celery

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Chill for an hour.  Spread on crescents, or wheat bread with cream cheese and sprouts.

Submitter: 
Sara Slade
Description: 
This salad combines sweet and tart cranberries with savory chicken, green onions, sliced almonds and celery.  A favorite with many of my friends, though my husband calls this girl chicken salad, as he likes his chicken salad a little less dressy.
