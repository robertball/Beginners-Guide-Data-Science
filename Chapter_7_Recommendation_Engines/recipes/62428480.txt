Title:
Watermelon Refresher Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 water
1/3 cup agave nectar
10 cups of sliced seedless watermelon (usually 1/2 large watermelon)
1 1/2 cups tequila (Patron)
1 cup frozen blueberries

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix water and agave nectar.
Place watermelon slices in a blender and blend until smooth.
In your pitcher,
Mix your water & agave nectar. Place your watermelon in a blender until smooth.  Pour the blended watermelon into a separate pitcher, then your mixed sweetener, and lastly the tequila. Mix together well with a wooden spoon.
Garnish with the frozen blueberries and some mint leaves.
Serve and enjoy.
