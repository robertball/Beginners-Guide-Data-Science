Title:
Parmesan-Pepper Bread Maker Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C plus 2 T Water
1 T Olive or Vegetable Oil
3 1/4 C Bread Flour
1/2 C Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 T Sugar
1 tsp. Salt
3/4 tsp. Black or Green Peppercorns, freshly ground
1 3/4 tsp. Active Dry Yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients in bread machine bread pan in the order listed.
Bake on White Bread setting.
