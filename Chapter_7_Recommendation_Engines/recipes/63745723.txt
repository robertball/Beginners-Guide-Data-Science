Title:
Caprese Hummus Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1053509.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fresh pesto
1 small container prepared classic hummus
8 oz. crumbled feta
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup black olives

Directions:
Spread pesto on bottom of serving dish.  Spread hummus over pesto.  Top with feta, tomatoes and olives.  Serve with pita chips.

Submitter:

Description: 
Quick and easy beautiful caprese dip that is full of flavor and beautiful.
