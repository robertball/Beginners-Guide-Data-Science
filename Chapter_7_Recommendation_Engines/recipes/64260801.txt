Title:
ALLRECIPES’ HURRICANE Recip
Image:

Ingredients:
1	ounce fresh lime juice
1	ounce dark rum
1	ounce light rum
4	ounces passion fruit nectar, passion fruit syrup, or thawed frozen
passion fruit concentrate
2	ounces pineapple juice
8	ice cubes

Directions:
Shake liquid ingredients with 2 ice cubes, then strain into a 14-ounce glass filled with remaining ice cubes.
