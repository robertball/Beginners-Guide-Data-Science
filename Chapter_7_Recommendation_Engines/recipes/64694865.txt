Title:
Beef Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb Carne Piccata or sirloin steak, cut into bite-sized pieces
salt and pepper , to taste
2 1/2 Tablespoons sugar, divided
1 1/2 Tablespoons corn starch
4 Tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
3 large eggs, beaten
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons grated ginger
1 large onion, diced
1 1/2 cup frozen peas
4 1/2 cup cooked rice
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 Tablespoon vinegar
1/2 Tablespoon sesame oil

Directions:
1. Rinse and pat meat dry with several paper towels.  In a large bowl, season meat with salt and pepper and 1/2 Tablespoon of sugar.  Toss seasoned meat in the cornstarch until well coated.
2. in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat, add 1/2 Tablespoon vegetable oil.  Add eggs and scramble until firmly cooked.  Remove from skillet and keep warm.
3. Increase heat to high and add 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil to pan.  Fry 1/2 of the coated meat mixture until crispy.  Remove from skillet and keep warm.  Add 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil and fry remaining meat until crispy.  Remove meat from skillet and keep warm.
4. Reduce heat to medium-low and add remaining vegetable oil to skillet and add  onion, garlic and ginger and cook until onions are translucent, 2-3 minutes.  Add peas and 3 Tablespoons water and cook until peas are tender.  Add rice and cook, stirring often for 2-3 minutes.
5.  Stir in soy sause, vinegar, remaining sugar and sesame oil.  Add back the meat and eggs to the skillet, toss together and serve.
