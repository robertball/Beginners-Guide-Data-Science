Title:
Cheeseburger Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sesame seed hamburger bun tops
1 small red onion, divided
2 plum tomatoes
3/4 pound 95% lean ground beef
1/2 c finely diced dill pickles
3/4 c ketchup
1 T yellow mustard
8 c romaine lettuce, thinly sliced
1 c (4 oz) shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Slice bun tops into 1/4 in. strips (if bun tops are very thick, slice bottoms off horizontally before slicing into strips.)  Arrange in a single layer and bake 8-10 minutes or until lightly toasted; cool completely.
2.  Slice half of the onion crosswise into thin rings.  Remove and discard stems from tomatoes; cut tomatoes into quarters lengthwise and slice crosswise.  Set onion and tomatoes aside.
3.  Cook ground beefo over medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes or until no longer pink, breaking beef into crumbles.
4.  Chop remaining onion.  Finely dice pickles.  Compbine chopped onion, pikles, ketchup and mustard.  Add cooked ground beef; mix well.
5.  To serve, arrange lettuce on a large serving platter.  then spoon beef misture over lettuce.  Top with cheese, tomatoes, and sliced onion.  Arrange hambuger bun croutons around edge of platter.  Serve immediately.
