Title:
Chicken Salsa Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hellmanns mayo
1/2 cup thick and chunky salsa
2 cups thinly slicked, grilled or cooked chicken
1/2 cup canned black beans or corn, drained
shredded lettuce
5 (8") flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine may and salsa. Stir in chicken and beans.
Layer lettuce on center of each tortilla; top with chicken mixture. Roll up tortillas.
