Title:
"Good Cake" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/593046.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
4 eggs
1c water
2/3c oil
1 can BC Coconut Pecan creamy frosting
1c chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Blend cake mix, water, eggs, and oil for 2 minutes on low speed.
Stir in frostine and pecans.
Pour into greased bundt pan and cook 50 minutes.
Let cool before serving.
