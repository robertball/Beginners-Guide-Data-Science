Title:
RaRa's chicken wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
36 divided wing sections, tips removed,
1 stick butter,
1 6 ounce bottle hot sauce(whatever brand you prefer),
oil for frying.

Directions:
Heat oil til hot, and it bubbles when you add a touch of flour.  Cook chicken wingettes in oil, in2-3 batches, until done(about 20 Minutes).  In a saucepan, melt butter, and add hot sauce.  Place wings in hot sauce( the longer they sit in the sauce the spicier they will be).  Serve with your favorite wing dip.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I learned this from a friend, and it was so easy.  You can have restaurant style wings any day of the week.. Enjoy!
