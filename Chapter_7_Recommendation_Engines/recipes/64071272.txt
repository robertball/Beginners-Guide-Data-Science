Title:
Cindy's Chocolate Tofu Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132692.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) Bag Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips, 1 (12 oz) block of silken tofu, 1/4 cup strongly brewed black coffee, 1-2 Tablespoons Vanilla Extract, 1 graham cracker pie crust, Raspberries (or other berries) and whipped topping for garnish.

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips in a microwave or on the stove top. Blend together tofu, chocolate chips, coffee and vanilla. Pour into graham cracker crust and chill 1-2 hours in the freezer, or 4 or more hours in the refrigerator. Garnish with raspberries and whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Val's Mom
Description: 
This is a dairy less chocolate cream pie.
