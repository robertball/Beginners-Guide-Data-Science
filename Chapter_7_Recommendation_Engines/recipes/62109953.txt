Title:
Chow Mein salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce
2/3 cup thawed and shelled edamame beans
2/3 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
2/3 cup chow mein noodles
1/2 cup creamy ranch dressing (or enough to coat the salad)
Black Ground Pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop lettuce and put into salad bowl
mix in the edamame beans, chow mein noodles, cheese
Toss with the ranch dressing and pepper
Serve

Submitter: 
Heidi Habeck-Johnson
Description: 
This is a salad that combines chow mein noodles with lettuce, edamame beans and ranch dressing.
