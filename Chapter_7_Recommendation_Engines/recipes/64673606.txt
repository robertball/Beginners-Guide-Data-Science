Title:
Steak Garlic Bread Grinder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4590323.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound beef round steak, cut into thin strips
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 onions, sliced into strips
2 green bell peppers, cut into strips
2 tablespoon butter
Garlic Powder
Provolone Cheese (12 Slices)
Steak Sauce
2 Grinder Rolls

Directions:
Heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in green pepper, garlic and onion, and season with salt and pepper. Saute until tender, and remove from skillet.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook beef until lightly browned and remove from heat.
Spread each grinder roll generously with butter and dust with garlic powder.
Under broiler lightly brown garlic bread and then layer provolone cheese and return to broiler until cheese melts.
Spread steak sauce onto top half of roll.
Divide peppers and onions onto top half of roll.
Divide beef onto bottom half of roll.
Add slices of provolone cheese on top and return to broiler until cheese melts.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
If you like garlic bread then why not turn it into a steak grinder. Nothing like green peppers and onions saute on top. Some steak sauce and melted provolone cheese served with french fries.
