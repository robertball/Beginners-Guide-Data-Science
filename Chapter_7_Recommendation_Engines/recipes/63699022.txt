Title:
Hearty Hamburger Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 to 2 lbs. hamburger  (93%-7%)
3- 4 cups sliced  baby carrots
4 cups  cubed potatoes
1 large onion chopped
1 lg can stewed tomatoes
1 8oz can tomatoe sauce
1 tsp minced garlic
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put diced potatoes, sliced baby carrots and chopped onion in a soup pot.  Season with salt.   Add water just enough to cover.  Bring to a boil and cook for about 20 min.   Add hamburger and minced garlic to boiling pot.  Season with garlic salt and pepper.  Add tomatoes and tomato sauce and cook for additional 10 min.

Submitter: 
Vicki Wilson
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by my daughter's God mother when I first started cooking..
To this day, this is one of my kids and grandkids  favorite stews.  It is heavy with the carrots and garlic but that is the way we like it.
