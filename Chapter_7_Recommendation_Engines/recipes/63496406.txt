Title:
Salami & Tomato Folds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. thinly sliced deli salami (3.5" diameter) 22 slices
1/2 cup garlic herb cheese spread
22 grape tomatoes

Directions:
1. Spread each salami slice with garlic cheese spread.
2. Fold ech salami slice in half.  Place tomato in center Roll salami around tomato overlapping edges; secure with toothpick.
