Title:
Blueberry Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker crust
1 large package of cream cheese
1 cup powdered sugar
1 large cool whip
1 large package vanilla pudding mix
1-2 cans blueberry pie filling

Directions:
Start by either making graham cracker crust in bottom of 9X13" pan by following directions on back of graham cracker crust box or buy two premade graham cracker pie crusts.
Make vanilla pudding to use later.
Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar, and 1/2 of the tub of cool whip. Spread this mixture on to crust.
Next layer vanilla pudding.
Next layer pie filling (you can use whatever fruit filling you like, but blueberry is best).
Top with remaining cool whip and chill overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Julie Moon
Description: 
The yummiest cold dessert ever. A favorite in our house during Easter.
