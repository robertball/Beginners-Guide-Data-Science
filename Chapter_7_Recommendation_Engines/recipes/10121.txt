Title:
Make Ahead Cookie Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970979.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
1 tablespoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
6 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the butter, salt and baking powder. Gradually stir in flour. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator for up to 4 weeks.

Submitter: 
Lynn
Description: 
This is a make ahead mix that can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 4 weeks.  The recipes below are 6 different varieties that can be made with the mix.
