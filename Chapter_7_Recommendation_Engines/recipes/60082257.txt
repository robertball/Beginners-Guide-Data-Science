Title:
Wheat Germ Whole-Wheat Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup canola oil
2 cups buttermilk (can sub 1/2 c yogurt and 1 1/2 c milk)
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 cup wheat germ (optional)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour (or reg whole wheat flour)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix eggs with oil and buttermilk. Stir in baking soda, wheat germ, salt and flour; mix until blended.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides, turning once.

Submitter: 
FrogsMom
Description: 
Pairs wheat germ and whole-wheat pastry flour for a healthy, wholesome breakfast treat. If you use a Teflon griddle you do not have to use oil.Modified from: Wheat Germ Whole-Wheat Buttermilk Pancakes
