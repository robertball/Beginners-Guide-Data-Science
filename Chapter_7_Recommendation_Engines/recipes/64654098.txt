Title:
Sodium free whole wheat bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4546270.jpg
Ingredients:
9c Whole wheat flour
1/4c vital wheat gluten
1/2c honey
3 1/2c warm water
2Tbls yeast
1/4c olive oil
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2tsp cloves

Directions:
In a large bowl combine flour, wheat gluten, cinnamon, and cloves.
In a separate bowl, combine water, honey, yeast and oil and allow to stand for 5 minutes or until frothy.
Add the wet ingredients to the dry and knead until a smooth dough is formed or use a stand mixer and dough hook. (Water or flour may need to be added as needed depending on humidity. Add small amounts at a time until dough is smooth but not very sticky)
Transfer to greased bowl and cover with a clean towel. Place covered bowl in a warm, draft free area and allow dough to double in size. (1-2 hours)
Divide dough into 3 equal portions and place them in greased 9x5 loaf pans. Cover and allow the dough to rise (30-45 minutes).
Bake at 375 for 35 minutes or the crust reaches desired crispness.
