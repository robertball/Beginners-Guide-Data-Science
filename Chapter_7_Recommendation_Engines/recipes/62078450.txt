Title:
Italian Eggplant Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large eggplants
1 clove garlic crushed
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tsp. dried parsley
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp. dried basil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Pierce eggplants with a fork and place on a baking sheet. Cook eggplants for 1.5 hours or until soft, turning occasionally.  When eggplants are done let cool.  Peel eggplants and then chop in a mixing bowl.  Drain off any excess liquid.  Add all of the ingredients and mix well. Place in the refrigerator for 2 hours to chill.

Submitter:

Description: 
If you like eggplant, you will love this recipe. This is very easy to make. Can be used as a side dish or for a spread on crackers.
