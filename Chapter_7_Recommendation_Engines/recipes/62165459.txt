Title:
Ez Glazed Orange Ginger Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of carrots peeled and cut into 2 inch diamond shape or 1 inch slices
1  teaspoon  grated orange rind
1/4  cup orange juice concentrate
2/3 cup of water
2  tablespoon of  butter
2  teaspoon  of honey or sugar
1  teaspoon of freshly grated ginger
1/4  teaspoon  salt
dash of white or black pepper to taste
1 tablespoon of chopped parsley

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients except parsley in a pan over medium heat, and bring to a boil. Then turn down to low heat for 30 to 40 minutes or until the carrots are glazed and tender to fork or knife.
If you prefer less ginger taste omit or slice ginger into two small pieces rather than grating.
Sprinkle parsley to garnish.

Submitter: 
abstractj
Description: 
This recipe is a great holiday recipe or just regular Sunday dinner side dish. The carrots are glazed with orange juice and honey with a hint of ginger.
