Title:
Potato Spinach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1/2 large onion (if tolerated)
1/4 cup flour
3 cups water
2 cups potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 teaspoons salt
1 cup spinach, chopped (fresh or frozen)
1/2 cup evaporated skim milk

Directions:
Melt butter in a pan and add onions. Cover and cook until tender - about 15 minutes.
Blend in flour, and cook for a couple minutes.
Add potatoes, water, and salt. Stir constantly until boiling, then reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.
Add spinach and cook an additional 5-6 minutes until spinach is very well cooked.
Puree, if desired, using an immersion blender, food processor, or blender.
Gradually stir in milk.
