Title:
Mozzarella En Carrozza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices of white bread
4 eggs, beaten
breadcrumbs
flour
1 lb of mozzarella
1 cup of milk
canola oil for frying

Directions:
Dip the bread in a little milk.  Cut the mozzarella into slices the same size & thickness of the bread.  Place it between 2 slices of the bread.  Coat with flour, dip in the eggs, then coat it with breadcrumbs.  Fry in hot oil until golden brown on both sides, approx. 5 minutes total.  Serve with your favorite marinara sauce to dip.
