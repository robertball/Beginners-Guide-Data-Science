Title:
Pizza Burgers  (w/Spam???) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of Hamburger Buns
1 can Grated Spam
8 ounces Mozzarella Cheese
6 ounces Tomato Paste
1/2 Small Onion or Onion Flakes
1/4 cup Melted Butter or Margarine
1/8 teaspoon Pepper
1/4 teaspoon Oregano
1/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together
Spread on open-faced buns or bread
Optional: Add Pepperoni, peppers or other favorites toppings.
Broil - 5 to 10 minutes

Submitter: 
Jay Janssen
Description: 
Don't let the Spam scare you; they'll be surprised when you tell them what they're eating.  ;-)  A great quick meal or after-school snack.  These have been a favorite of mine since I was a kid.
