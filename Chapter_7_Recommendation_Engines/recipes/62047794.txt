Title:
Pasta with vodka cream sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
4 garlic cloves chopped
2 shallots chopped
1 cup vodka
1 cup chicken broth
1 28oz. can crushed tomatoes
3/4 teaspoon dried italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup heavy cream
1 pound penne pasta cooked and drained
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat oil and butter. Saute shallots and garlic for 5 minutes. Add vodka simmer 3 minutes. Add broth, tomatoes, and seasonings simmer 15 minutes. stir in cream heat through. Toss pasta with sauce.serve with parmesan cheese.
