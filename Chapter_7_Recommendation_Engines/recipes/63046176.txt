Title:
Creamy Shrimp and Mushroom Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/765226.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Ounces Pasta (Spaghetti, Linguine, Fettucini, etc.)
10 Tablespoons Butter, Divided (2 Tbsp / 8 Tbsp)
8 Ounces Fresh Mushrooms, Sliced
2 Garlic Cloves, Minced
3 Ounces Cream Cheese, Cubed
1 Pound Fully Cooked Frozen Shrimp, Thawed
2/3 Cup Boiling Water (From Pasta Water)

Directions:
Boil pasta in large stock pot to your desired firmness.
Run frozen shrimp under cool water in a collander for 5 minutes to thaw.
Rinse fresh mushrooms under cool water in a collander until all residue is gone.
Melt 2 tbsp butter in a large skillet on medium heat.
Add mushrooms, and saute on medium heat until soft and light brown.
Remove mushrooms from skillet and set aside.
Melt remaining 8 tbsp butter in large skillet on medium heat.
Add cubed cream cheese, and minced garlic.
Stir butter, cream cheese, and garlic together until the cream cheese has almost fully melted, and is a soft, chunky, curdled consistancy.
Add 2/3 cup water from your pasta pot to the skillet to thin sauce.
Stir together until cream cheese is fully melted.
Add reserved mushrooms and thawed shrimp, stir to combine.
Simmer sauce for 10 minutes, until sauce has thickened, and mushrooms and shrimp are warmed throughout.
Drain pasta, and return to pot.
Toss sauce mixture with drained pasta.
