Title:
APPLESAUCE SPICE CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
3/4 tsp nutmeg (fresh grated is best)
3/4 tsp ground cloves
1 tsp salt
1 ½ C sugar
1 C oil
1 ½ C applesauce
3 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 C raisins
1 C slightly chopped walnuts
Powder sugar to sprinkle

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Spray bundt pan with Pam. Sift together on a piece of waxed paper
(OR into a bowl):
2 1/4 C flour 1 tsp baking soda 3/4 tsp baking powder 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
3/4 tsp nutmeg (fresh grated is best) 3/4 tsp ground cloves 1 tsp salt
In large bowl whisk together:
1 ½ C sugar 1 C oil 1 ½ C applesauce 3 eggs 2 tsp vanilla
Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients; whisk til smooth.
Add: 1 C raisins 1 C slightly chopped walnuts
Bake 45-55 minutes or til done. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pan & completely.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar....if desired.
