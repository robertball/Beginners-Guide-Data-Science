Title:
Passover brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz chocolate chips or semi-sweet chocolate
1 cup butter or margarine
1 3/4 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla flavoring
1 cup cake meal
pinch of salt
6 oz package chocolate chips
2 cups cut up  mini marshmallows

Directions:
Melt 4oz chocolate and butter together. Add sugar.
Cool slightly.
Add eggs one at a time.
Add vanilla, cake meal and salt.
Mix to blend well.
Fold in chocolate chips and marshmallows.
Put in greased 9 X 13.
Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
SANDI
Description: 
Delicious and moist Passover brownies! These will disappear quickly and will actually be requested during Passover!
