Title:
Crock Pot Chicken Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken
1 onion, thinly sliced
1 red or green bell pepper, sliced
2 teaspoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
8 warm flour tortillas
1/2 cup cheddar cheese (grated)
Fixin's as desired!

Directions:
Cut chicken into 1/2 inch strips. Place in crock pot. Top with onion, bell pepper, and lime.
In a small bowl, combine chili powder, cumin, salt, and broth. Pour mixture over chicken. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours or until chicken is tender.
Fill up a tortilla, load it with fixin's and enjoy!
