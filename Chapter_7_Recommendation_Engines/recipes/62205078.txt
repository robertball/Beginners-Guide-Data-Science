Title:
Spinach Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 t butter
4 c onion
1/4 c flour
1/4 t nutmeg
1 c cream
2 c milk
3 lbs spinach, thawed, drained
1 c parmeasan cheese
1 T kosher salt
1/2 t pepper
1/2 c gruyere

Directions:
Cook butter and onion for 15 min, add flour and nutmeg, cook 2 min. Add cream and milk till thick.
Mix spinach with 1/2 c parmesean, then combine with onions.
Combine rest to serve as topping.
Bake 425 for 20 min.
