Title:
Perfect Frozen Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
50ml   Golden Tequilla
50ml   Grand Marnier
125ml  Lime Cordial or fresh lime Juice
125ml  Margarita Mix
1/2 fresh lime, juiced
1/2 lime cut into wedges
Salt
lemon zest
600g  ice cubes

Directions:
Mix Tequilla, Grand Marnier, Lime Cordial, Margarita Mix, fresh lime juice and lemon zest in a blender. Blend well.
Add ice cubes and blend until preferred consistency is reached
Salt the Margarita glass
Pour the Margarita into the salted glass and add 1 lime wedge on glass edge

Submitter: 
Anita Chen
Description: 
I fine-tuned a favoriate Margarita recipe of a local bar by cutting the alcohol half and adding lemon zest to. Perfect strength for everyone -- even those who usually can't take alcohol! Add 1/2 cup of frozen strawberries or fresh mango to make strawberry or mango margarita.
