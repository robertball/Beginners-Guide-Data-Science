Title:
Honey Maid Holiday Cheesecake Presents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1544106.jpg
Ingredients:
10 HONEY MAID Honey Grahams, finely crushed
1/3 cup butter, melted
3 tablespoons sugar
3 (8 ounce) packages brick cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 eggs
Suggested Decorations:
Decorating gels
Colored sprinkles

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix graham crumbs, butter and 3 Tbsp. sugar; press onto bottom of 13x9-inch pan.
Beat cream cheese, 3/4 cup sugar and vanilla with mixer until well blended. Add eggs; mix just until blended. Pour over crust.
Bake 30 min. or until center is almost set. Cool. Refrigerate 3 hours. Cut into bars. Decorate with gels and sprinkles to resemble presents.

Submitter: 
Honey Maid
Description: 
Cheesecake bars on a graham cracker crust are decorated to look like festive holiday gifts.
