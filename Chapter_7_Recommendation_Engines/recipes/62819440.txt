Title:
Bacon Ranch Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs potatoes
2 cups mayo
4 eggs
1 medium onion
2 celery stalks
1 package of ranch dressing powder (I use hidden valley)
1 lb bacon

Directions:
Peel potatoes and cut into half inch pieces; place into cold water to boil. Boil until fork tender. When done, let cool before mixing in remaining ingredients.
While potatoes are boiling, hard boil the eggs. When they are done, let cool and then peel and chop.
Cook bacon until crisp and then chop into small pieces.
In a separate bowl, mix in half the package of ranch powder into the mayonnaise.
Mince onion and mix into mayo mixture.
Chop celery and mix into mayo mixture along with the bacon.
When potatoes are cooled mix mayo mixture and chopped eggs into potatoes and mix well.
Refrigerate until cold and serve.
