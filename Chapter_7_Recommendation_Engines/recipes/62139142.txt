Title:
caramel pecan crunch pumpkin pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker crust
about 3/4 cup chopped pecans
carmael ice cream topping
1 cup pumpkin puree
6 oz coolwhip
1tsp cinnamon
1/4tsp nutmeg
1/4tsp ground cloves
2 pkgs instant pudding mix,vanilla
1 cup cold milk

Directions:
drizzle bottom of pie crust with enough caramel to coat the bottom.
sprinkle the chopped pecans.
in a large bowl combine pudding(i use one vanilla and one cheescake flavored)milk,pumpkin,and spice untill smooth.
pour into pie crust.
top pie with coolwhip smoothing surface with a spatula.
drizzle with more caramel and pecans
refridgerate untill firm and chilled through.

Submitter: 
mistycolleen
Description: 
chilled pumpkin pie layered with yummy caramel and pecans, so simple and always a favorite.
