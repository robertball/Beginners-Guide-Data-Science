Title:
Spicy Sweet-N-Sour BBQ sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1117955.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 Cup Heinz Ketchup
3/4 cup of brown sugar
1 teaspoon paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, chili powder.
1/2 teaspoon of salt and black ground pepper
1 tablespoon, favorite hot sauce (I use Louisiana), lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke, and honey.
3 tablespoons of cider vinegar
dash of celery seed
1 tablespoon of Whiskey (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a pot and simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes. Poor into airtight glass container, such as canning jars and store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
*Sherri*
Description: 
Since most sauces first ingredient is High Fructose corn syrup, I started making my own sauce. It is a basic sweet tangy with a touch of heat sauce. You can easily add or delete spices for your desired taste.
