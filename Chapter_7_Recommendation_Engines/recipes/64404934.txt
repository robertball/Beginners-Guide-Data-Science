Title:
Refried Bean and Leafy Green Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2259973.jpg
Ingredients:
1 14 oz can Refried Beans
1 739 ml Jar of your favourite tomato sauce
2 1/2 C. Cottage cheese
1 Large bunch Fresh spinach, chopped coarsely ( about 4 to 6 cups of chopped spinach
12 Lasagna noodles
2 C. Mozzarella cheese, grated

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
2.  Cook Lasagna according to package directions. Allow to cool
3.  Chop spinach leaves coarsely and mix with cottage cheese in a large mixing bowl.
4.  Mix together refried beans and tomato sauce in a large frying pan over medium heat until mixture is heated through
5.  Spread 1 part of bean mixture on the bottom of dish and place 3 lasagna noodles over top of the sauce. Then put a layer of spinach and cottage cheese mixture. Repeat step 1 alternating with bean mixture and cottage cheese and spinach mixture finishing with bean mixture on  the last three noodles. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of everything and bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes. Remove from oven and allow to cool before cutting into squares for serving.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A 4 layer lasagna made with refried beans and leafy greens to make a very satisfying meal for the dinner table.
