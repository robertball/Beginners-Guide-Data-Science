Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068487.jpg
Ingredients:
Parchment Paper
2 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 (12 ounce) bag semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Line cookie sheets parchment paper; set aside.
Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl and set aside.
Beat together butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar in a large mixing bowl, with an electric mixer, until light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Add flour mixture gradually to butter mixture; beat until well blended.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop by rounded tablespoons onto parchment-lined cookie sheets.
Bake at 350Â° for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned.
Cool.
