Title:
Orange Dreamsicle Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup 2% reduced-fat Greek yogurt
2/3 cup ice
1/2 cup chopped, peeled, ripe mango, frozen
1/2 cup orange juice, fresh
1 medium orange
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Segment the orange over a bowl to catch the tasty juices, and add to the blender.
Place all other ingredients in blender and process 1 minutes or until completely smooth. Divide evenly between 2 glasses.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
You can buy frozen mangoes, but for the creamiest sip, buy fresh and freeze overnight.
