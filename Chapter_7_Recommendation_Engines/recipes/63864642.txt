Title:
Apple Pie with oatmeal crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1072584.jpg
Ingredients:
Bottom Crust
1 1/4 cup of whole wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon of Domino Stevia Blend sugar
1/4 cup of cold water
1 pinch of salt
1/2 cup of cold butter
Apple Pie Filling
1/2 cup of butter
1/4 cup of hot water
3 tablespoons of whole wheat flower
1/2 lemon
1 teaspoon of cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
1/4 cup of Domino Stevia Blend sugar
1/4 cup of Splenda Brown Sugar Blend
3 Granny Smith apples cut into small pieces
1 Red Delicious apple cut into small pieces
Top crust
1/2 cup butter
1 1/4 cup of old fashion oats
2 tablespoons Splenda Brown Sugar Blend
1/2 cup whole wheat flour

Directions:
Bottom Crust  -  Combine the flour, sugar, water, salt and butter and mix with hand blender and hands. Form in a disc shape and cover in plastic wrap.
Let sit in fridge for at least 2 hours.
Once dough is ready shape it into a 9 inch pie tin and bake at 350 degrees for 5 minutes. Let cool.
Filling  -  Cut the 4 applies into small slices and set aside. Melt butter in a small sauce pot then mix in juice of half of a lemon, flour and water. Add sugar and bring to a boil. Once boiling reduce heat, add cinnamon and nutmeg and let simmer.
Mix 3/4 of the sauce in with apples and mix well. Pour apples with sauce in pan with pie crust.
Top crust  -  Mix butter, old fashion oats, splenda Brown Sugar Blend, whole wheat flour and the remaining 1/4 of the sauce in the pot that was mixed with apples. Mix with blender and place on top of apples.
Bake for 15 minutes at 400 degrees and then reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake for an additional 30-45 minutes depending on oven.
