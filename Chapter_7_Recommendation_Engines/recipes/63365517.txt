Title:
Peanut Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup creamy peanut bu tter
6 tbsp soy margarine
1 cup water
2 cups all-purpose gluten free flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup canned coconut milk
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp pure vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
Frosting
1/4 cup soy margaring, cubed
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
2 tbsp lactose free milk
1 3/4 cups confectioners' sugar
1 tsp pure vanilla

Directions:
In large saucepan, bring peanut butter, margarine and water just to boil. Immediately remove from heat; stir in the flour, sugar, coconut milk, applesauce, eggs, baking powder, vanilla, salt and baking soda until smooth.
Pour into a 15x10x1 in baking pan coated with cooking spray. Bake at 375 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted new the center comes out clean. Cook on a wire rack for 20 minutes.
In a small saucepan, melt margarine and peanut butter over medium heat; add milk. Remove from heat. Gradually whisk in confectioners' sugar and vanilla until smooth. Spread over warm cake. Cool completely on a wire rack. Refridgerate leftover.
