Title:
Crunchy Bagel Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993075.jpg
Ingredients:
2 unsliced bagels
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Refrigerate bagels for 1 hour. Cut into 1/4-in. slices. Place on an ungreased baking sheet. Combine butter and garlic salt; brush over bagel slices. Bake at 325 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on a wire rack. Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Robin Boynton
Description: 
This savory snack from Robin Boynton of Harbor Beach, Michigan tastes just like store-bought bagel chips...and it's a great way to use up leftover bagels.
