Title:
Lazy Longanisa (Filipino Style Breakfast Sausage) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb smoked sausage (fully cooked, sliced thick)
1 tbsp soy sauce
4 tsp white vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp oil

Directions:
1. Combine sliced sausage, soy sauce, white vinegar, and brown sugar in plastic bag. Seal and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
2. Add water, oil, sausage, and any remaining unabsorbed marinade in a skillet over medium high heat. Cook until liquid is reduced and sausage has caramelized.
3. Serve over white rice with a fried egg.

Submitter: 
Jose Napoleon Cuasay Artiaga I
Description: 
Mimic a popular Filipino breakfast dish by marinating fully cooked smoked sausage (like andouille or kielbasa) in a blend of sugar and soy sauce.  The sausage is caramelized and best served with white rice and a fried egg.
