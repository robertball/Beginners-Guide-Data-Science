Title:
Beef Stew VI, modified Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
1/2 c. flour
1 t. garlic powder
1/2 t. onion powder
1/2 t. basil
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 c. red wine, optional
4 c. low sodium beef broth
1 teaspoon dried rosemary, or more to taste
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 lg. potatoes, peeled and cubed into bite-sized pieces
3 carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces
3 stalks celery, cut into 1-inch pieces
1-2 cups (or more) additional root vegetables such as turnips, parsnips, and daikon, or use more potato. Peel and cut into 1-inch pieces.
1 large onion, chopped
2 teaspoons cornstarch
2 teaspoons cold water

Directions:
Place flour, garlic powder, onion powder, and basil in a large plastic bag and mix.  Add cubed stew beef and shake to coat.
Heat oil over medium heat in a large pot or dutch oven.  Add beef in batches and brown on all sides - do not crowd.  Return all beef to the pot and add 1/2 c. wine. Cook 1 min while scraping bottom of pot. Add beef broth, rosemary, parsley, and pepper.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 hour.
Stir potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, and other vegetables into the pot.  Dissolve cornstarch in 2 teaspoons cold water and stir into stew.  Cover and simmer 1 hour more.
