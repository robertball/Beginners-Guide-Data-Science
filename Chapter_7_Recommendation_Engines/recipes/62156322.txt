Title:
Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/570551.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 tsp garlic pepper seasoning
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground ginger
Chicken drumsticks or Chicken Breast

Directions:
Mix all ingredients togeter
barbecue chicken drumsticks until almost done
brush glaze onto drumsticks and cook till done

Submitter: 
lynda
Description: 
Tangy honey glaze for barbecued chicken
can be used for baked chicken as well
