Title:
Chopped liver Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken liver
1 c rendered fat
2 c onion
1/3 c madeira wine
4 hard cooked eggs
1/4 c parsley
2 t thyme
2 t kosher salt
1 t pepper
pinch cayenne

Directions:
Cook liver in fat for 5 min. Add onion till cooked. Add wine.
Process all together, not to much.
