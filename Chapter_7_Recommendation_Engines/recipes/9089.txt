Title:
Cherry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1410551.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Directions:
Mix whipped topping, sweetened condensed milk, and cherries in a large mixing bowl.  Transfer to a serving bowl, and chill.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Powell
Description: 
I can almost guarantee the bowl will be empty at the end of the meal.  Happy Thanksgiving!
