Description: 
Upgrade your s'more experience with these extreme versions that trade out graham crackers for easy-to-grip supermarket cookies. Roast up oversize marshmallows to sandwich in-between the cookies along with your favorite filling. Make sure to bring plenty of cookies so everyone can join in the fun! Enjoy immediately and repeat as needed for total campsite fun!
