Title:
Ellensburg Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-12 large peeled tomatoes
2     large onions
2      medium green peppers
2     medium red peppers
2     medium jalapeno peppers
1/2 cup distilled vinegar
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 4.5 oz can chopped chilies
chop and combine with
1 Cup tomato paste
1 T salt
2 Garlic cloves
1/2 t oregano
1/2 t cilantro or 2T fresh
Boil stirring frequently for 30 min
put in jars
can in boiling water bath 15 minutes

Directions:
Boil first 8 ingredients stirring frequently for 30 min
combine with the last 5 ingredients,
put in jars, can in boiling water bath 15 minutes
