Title:
Lori's Creamy Strawberry Tequila Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4472486.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 pint half-and-half cream
1/2 cup vodka
1/2 cup strawberry schnapps
1/3 cup tequila
3 tablespoons vanilla extract
2 tablespoons strawberry syrup
1 cup ice, or as needed

Directions:
Pour sweetened condensed milk, half-and-half, vodka, schnapps, tequila, vanilla extract, syrup, and ice into a blender. Blend until smooth. Drink as shots or on the rocks.

Submitter: 
SARAH FROM IOWA
Description: 
I got this recipe from my mother-in-law. Tequila and strawberry flavors merge deliciously in this creamy delight.
