Title:
Pork mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium-small Potatoes, chopped in chunks
4 medium-small Carrots, diced
1 Onion, diced
1 cup Broccoli, chopped
1/2 lb lean Pork fillet, diced
2 tbsp Cider Vinegar
2 tbsp Soy Sauce
2 tsp Paprika
2 tsp Garlic, crushed or chopped
2 tbsp Butter
1 tbsp Mayonnaise
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil Potatoes, Carrots and Broccoli until potatoes ready to mash.
Stir fry Pork, Onion & Garlic with 1/2 tbsp Butter in a frying pan until Pork is cooked.
Add 1 tbsp water from the pan that the Potatoes were boiled in.
Add Soy Sauce, Vinegar, Salt and Pepper.
Reduce, then add 1/2 cup water from the Potato pan.
Add Paprika, let simmer.
Drain Potatoes etc, and mash together with remaining butter and Mayonnaise.
Divide potatoes on plates, leaving valley in the middle, then divide stir-fry into the valleys. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Oisín Ó Searcóid
Description: 
A delightful and filling stir-fry and mashed potato dish that will make you want to cook more at home!
