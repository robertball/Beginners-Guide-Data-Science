Title:
Aunty Ivy's Crack Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sauerkraut, drained
1 can whole cranberry sauce
12 oz (354 ml)  jar chili sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Bring all ingredients to a boil.
Simmer x 10 min.
Put into jars. Store in fridge.
Easy Peasy!

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This is addicting!! and can be eaten on everything... cheese, fish, beef, chicken, eggs, sandwiches - use your imagination!! Sooooo tasty!
