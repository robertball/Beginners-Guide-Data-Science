Title:
Sweet and Sour Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white vinegar
2 tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp pepper
1/3 cup salad oil
1 tsp oregano
1 medium onion
3 medium sized cucumbers (very thinly sliced)

Directions:
Prepare about 1 hour before serving or up to 2 days ahead of time.  In large bowl, combine all ingredients, cover and refrigerate at least 45 minutes to blend flavors, stirring occasionally.  Makes 8 accompaniment servings.  110 calories per serving.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This is one of my childhood favorites.  My grandmother would make it on those hot summer days and we would eat it outside along with pizza that she also made from scratch.  My grandmother truley was a good cook!
