Title:
Chocolate Hazelnut Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese, softened
1 13oz jar of chocolate Hazelnut spread, such as nutella
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups confectioners sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl whip the cream cheese with a hand-held mixer until fluffy. Add the chocolate hazelnut spread,vanilla and salt and mix on slow speed until well combined. Add the sugar slowly until combined then raise the speed to high for several minutes until frosting is stiff. Chill for 1 hour before using.
