Title:
Gypsies' Schnitzel /  "Zigeuner Schnitzel" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz Veal or Pork Sirloin;   thin cross cut of Roast
1 med. Tomato
1/2 small onion
1 ea. med. green and Red;  Bell Pepper
Salt and Pepper, flour
Butter
1 tbsp. Tomato paste
Worchester sauce.
Tabasco
1/2 cup of Broth or water
1/2 tsp. Chopped fresh Parsley

Directions:
Chop, Dice the Tomato and Onion,  Slice the Peppers into inch long julliene. Fry the veg. in Butter until Soft and glassy.  Add the Tomato paste, worchester, and a dash of Hot sauce.  continue sauteeing, adding broth as needed for a thick Sauce. taste for salt. set aside, keep warm.
Tenderize the Slice of Meat'  salt and pepper,  then Flour and Fry in hot Butter until done through.
arrange on a plate and Dress with the "Zigeuner" Sauce. Sprinkle with chopped Parsley.
Serve rest Sauce apart;     serve:  with Macaroni or Spaetzle.
