Title:
Red Beet Eggs for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12-18 hard-boiled eggs,peeled.
3 15oz. cans red beets
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
approximately 1 cup water
1 medium sweet or vadalia onion,sliced.(optional)

Directions:
Drain juice from red beets into large container.(A 5 quart ice cream container or large Tupperware bowl works the best).Add vinegar,sugar & water. Mix well til sugar is dissolved.Add peeled eggs &(onions if desired),and red beets.Cover & leave eggs to soak in juice stirring once or twice a day, for 1-3 days. The longer they soak,the deeper the color into the eggs.

Description: 
Great side dish for Holiday get-togethers.
