Title:
red curry paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
13 small dried chilies soaked in hot water for 15 min
3 T chopped shallot
4 T garlic
1 T chopped galangal
2 T chopped lemon grass
2 t chopped kaffir lime rind
1 T chopped coriander root
20 pepper corns
1 t shrimp paste
1 T coriander seed
1 t cumin seed

Directions:
In a wok over low heat toast coriander seeds and cumin seeds dry for about 5 min then grind into a powder.
In a blender put the rest of the ingredients except shrimp paste and blend.
Add remaining ingredients and blend again to obtain a fine-textured paste. Store in a glass jar in the fridge for 4 months.
