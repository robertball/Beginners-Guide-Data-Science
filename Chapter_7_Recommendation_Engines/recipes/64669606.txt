Title:
Italian Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound potatoes, unpeeled, cut into wedges
1/2 cup Zesty Italian dressing
1/2 up grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Place potatoes into a 13 x 9 inch baking pan that has been sprayed with Pam. Pour salad dressing over potatoes. Top with Parmesan cheese. Bake for 1 hour or until potatoes are browned and soft with pierced with a fork.
