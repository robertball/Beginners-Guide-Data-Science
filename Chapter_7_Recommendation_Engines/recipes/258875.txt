Title:
Mediterranean Zucchini Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 zucchini, ends trimmed
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 lemons, juiced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon white vinegar
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 lemon, zested
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (4 ounce) package crumbled goat-milk feta cheese

Directions:
Cut zucchini into noodles using a spiralizer (fitted with the large shredding blade). Place in a colander.
Mix artichoke hearts, tomatoes, and olives together in a large bowl. Add the zucchini noodles and toss to combine.
Combine olive oil, lemon juice, parsley, garlic, vinegar, oregano, salt, lemon zest, and black pepper in a bowl; stir well. Pour over the zucchini noodle mixture. Top with the feta cheese.
