Title:
Toasted Flax Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup    Bob's Red Mill 10 Grain Cereal
1 Tsp.     Salt
1/4 Cup    Flax Seeds
1 Tsp      Sesame Oil
1 Tblsp.   Soy Sauce
1/4 Cup    Lukewarm Water
1 Tblsp.   Active Dry Yeast
1 Cup      Unbleached Flour
1/4 Cup    Sunflower Oil
2 Cups     Unbleached Flour
1 Tblsp.   Sunflower Oil
1 Tsp.     Shortening
1          Egg
1 Tsp.     Milk

Directions:
Combine cereal, salt and water.
Boil, then cover and reduce heat to low.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
While simmering, in a skillet, combine flax seeds with the sesame oil and soy sauce.
Over medium-hi, toast while stirring frequently.
Once all the moisture has been cooked off, remove from heat.
Combine yeast and the lukewarm water. Let sit for 5 minutes.
Lightly stir.
In a large bowl, combine yeast mixture, cereal, flax seeds, and 1 cup unbleached flour.
(NOTE: when you add the cereal, it MUST have cooled to lukewarm.)
Stir until fluffy. Cover and let set in a draft-free, warm place for 30 minutes.
(This is where those rings come off)
Stir in 2 cups unbleached flour.
Knead for 5 minutes.
Lightly cover a large bowl in oil.
Cover and let set in a draft-free, warm place and let rise until doubled.
While rising, grease a bread pan by spreading a thin layer of shortening on the bottom and walls of it.
Once doubled, punch dough down, and knead for a minute.
Shape into a loaf and put in the bread pan.
Cover and let set in a draft-free, warm place and let rise until doubled.
Once almost doubled, preheat oven to 375 F Degrees.
Beat the egg and milk together and lightly brush the mixture over the loaf.
Bake loaf for 40-45 minutes.
Let cool on a wire rack.
Wait 30 minutes before serving.
Enjoy!!!
