Title:
Cheesey Curried Cauliflower Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of cauliflower(3-4 cups cauliflower pieces)
1 can mushroom soup
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tsp curry powder
1 tbsp butter
3/4 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Cut up cauliflower into bite-size pieces.  Rinse under water and place in casserole dish with 1/2 cup water.
3. Cover cauliflower and microwave on high 3-4 minutes.
4. In a medium-size mixing bowl, mix together mushroom soup, mayonnaise, cheddar cheese and curry powder.
5. Add semi-cooked cauliflower to cheese mixture.  Stir to combine, and scoop it all back into the casserole dish.
6.  In a small microwave-safe bowl, melt butter.  Stir in bread crumbs, mix until crumbly.  Sprinkle over top of cauliflower mix.
7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.

Submitter: 
MONICA DEREGT
Description: 
This is our favorite way to eat cauliflower.  The cheesey savory sauce with a hint of curry brings out the flavor of the cauliflower, and the crunchy topping finishes it off marvelously! Wonderful way to serve a plain vegetable to company.
