Title:
Carrot Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans sliced carrots
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 tablespoons all purpose flour
3 large eggs
1 stick butter (1/4 lb), softened
Cinnamon
Salt
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Drain carrots well
Add sugar, baking powder, & vanilla
Beat with mixer until smooth
Add flour & mix well
Add a dash of salt & cinnamon
Taste and adjust to suit taste
Whip eggs separately & add to mixture, blending well.
Add butter & blend well.
Pour mixture into 8x8 baking dish that has been sprayed with Pam.
Bake @ 350 about 1 hour or until top is light brown.
Sprinkle lightly with powdered sugar.
