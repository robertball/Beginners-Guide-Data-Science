Title:
Boiled Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1/2 cup milk
1 stick butter
1/2 tsp vanilla
3 cups Quick-cook oatmeal (uncooked)
pinch of salt

Directions:
Mix sugar and cocoa together in saucepan.  Add milk and stir together.  Add butter. Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until mixture comes to a boil.  Boil for 2 FULL minutes.
Remove from heat.  Stir in vanilla.  Stir in oatmeal and salt, until all the oatmeal is covered in chocolate.  Immediately drop by tablespoon onto foil or wax paper.  Cookies will set up within a  few minutes.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
BBCHIL
Description: 
No-bake chocolate and oatmeal cookies.  I've been making these since I was 10, and now my 10-year-old wants to make them.
