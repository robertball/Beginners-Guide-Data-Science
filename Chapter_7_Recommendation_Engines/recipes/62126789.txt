Title:
butterbeer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons honey
1 tablespoon butter
1 1/2 cups milk (can use soy milk)
2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 ounce butterscotch syrup (or schnapps)
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoons hot chocolate powder

Directions:
In a small pot, melt the butter and honey together at medium heat.
Once the butter has melted, add the milk, sugar, vanilla,and butterscotch syrup. Heat until just about to a boil, then turn off the heat.
Whisk in the hot chocolate powder and cinnamon and transfer to a mug.

Submitter: 
rcragun
Description: 
Smooth, buttery, butterscotch concoction mean to resemble a drink you'd find in Harry Potter's magical world. It's perfect for a cool, snowy or rainy day and a great alternative to hot chocolate. You could top this with some whip cream.
