Title:
Easy Shrimp & Broccoli Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3190467.jpg
Ingredients:
For the Shrimp:
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
28 ounces frozen broccoli florets, thawed a few hours before cooking
2 pounds medium shrimp, peeled & deveined
optional: 1 tsp sesame seeds & sliced green onion for topping
For the Sauce:
2 tbsp oyster sauce
3 tbsp gluten free soy sauce
1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 tbsp grated ginger
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp cornstarch
1 tsp Sriracha

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the ingredients for the sauce then set aside
Heat a large skillet over medium heat
Add thawed broccoli florets to the skillet stirring frequently 2-3 minutes
To the broccoli, add the shrimp stirring occasionally just until the shrimp turn pink 3-4 minutes
Stir in the sauce and combine with shrimp and broccoli until well combined 1-2 minutes
Serve immediately and garnish with sesame seeds & green onions
