Title:
Spicy pan-fried trout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 Fillet Trout
1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions:
Sprinkle both sides of fillet with the cayenne pepper, salt and pepper. Put olive oil in medium hot frying pan. Once hot, put fish in pan, skin side down. Fry until just about cooked trough (3-5 minutes). Turn the fish over and cook for the final minute.

Submitter: 
ahoffort
Description: 
This is such a simple fish recipe with a kick. I have used it with trout and salmon fillets.
