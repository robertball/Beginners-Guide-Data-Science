Title:
Butterscotch Pecan Sandies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1-1/3 cups unsalted butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1 large egg
3/4 cup chopped pecans
2 cups butterscotch chips
sugar for rolling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Combine flour and soda in small bowl; set aside.
Cream butter, sugar, salt and vanilla in mixing bowl on medium speed until ligt.  Beat in egg until fluffy.  Reduce speed to low, add flour mixture one cup at a time until just combined.  Stir in pecans and butterscotch chips.
Form 1" balls of dough and roll each in sugar.  Place 2" apart on cookie sheet.  Flatten balls with the bottom of a glass; dip the glass in sugar if the dough starts to stick to the glass.
Bake for 8-10 minutes or until they begin to brown around the edges.  Transfer to racks for cooling; store in airtight container.
