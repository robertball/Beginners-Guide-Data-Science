Title:
Hearty Tartar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/823460.jpg
Ingredients:
2 green onion, chopped
2 medium sweet pickles, chopped
1 dill pickle, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup pitted and chopped green olives
2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon taco sauce
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Place green onion, sweet and dill pickles, garlic, olives, mayonnaise, Worcestershire, taco sauce and lemon juice into a food processor or blender and process briefly. Do not puree. Refrigerate before serving.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
Great tasting homemade tartar sauce for seafoods. It's packed with pickles green onions, olives and garlic. Definitely a winner.
