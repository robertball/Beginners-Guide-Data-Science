Title:
Chili Rellenos Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound extra lean ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 large can Hatch whole green chile peppers, drained
2 cups shredded mexican cheese blend (1-16 ounce bag)
4 eggs, beaten
1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 (10 ounce) can enchilada sauce
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Brown ground beef with onion, garlic, cumin, salt and pepper.
Lay half of the chilies evenly in bottom of baking dish. Sprinkle with half of the meat mixture and 1/3 of the cheese. Repeat layers.
In a bowl, mix together the eggs, milk and flour. Pour over the top of the dish.
Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until set and brown on top.
Remove from oven, pour enchilada sauce evenly over the top and continue baking another 15 minutes.
Sprinkle with the remaining cheese, allow to stand for 10 minutes and serve with sour cream.
