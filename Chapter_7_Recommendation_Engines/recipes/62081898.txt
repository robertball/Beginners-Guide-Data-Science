Title:
Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8 oz pkg light cream cheese
1 cup light mayonnaise
1 can (120 grams)crab meat, rinsed & drained
1 tin waterchestnuts chopped fine
1 finely chopped green onion
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
3 cloves garlic chopped
oregano to taste
black pepper & cayenne pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together.
It can be baked in an ovenproof dish for 1 hour or slice the top off a round loaf of bread (sourdough preferably) and hollow it out.  Put dip in it and put lid back on.   Bake for 1 hour with the lid on and 1/2 hour off.  Serve with pieces of bread or crackers.

Lynn Mott

fantastic flavor, I tasted this at a bakery staff party and just had to try it.
