Title:
Pasta With Creamy Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP Olive Oil
1 Pound Hot Italian Sausage cut in 1 inch pieces
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2 large cloves of garlic
1 28 oz can of tomato sauce
1 TBSP dried basil leaf
1 TBSP dried parsley
1/4 tsp black pepper (fresh ground is best)
8 oz Mostaccioli or Rotini macaroni
1/4 cup of creamy parmesan salad dressing

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot over med high heat and cook sausage until it is almost done.  Add onion and garlic and cook until tender.  Draining really isn't necessary.  Add tomato sauce and seasonings.  Heat to a mild boil and reduce heat.  Simmer for 15 minutes.
Meanwhile cook pasta per the package directions.
Remove sauce from heat and stir in the salad dressing.  Pour desired amount over pasta and serve.
