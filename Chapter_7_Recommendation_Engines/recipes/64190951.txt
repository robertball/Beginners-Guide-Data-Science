Title:
Sandy's favorite Chili Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 to 1 pound of hamburger
1/2 small onion, diced
1/2 green pepper,chopped
1 14.5 can pinto beans, drained
1 14.5 can pink beans, drained
1 29 ounce  can of tomato sauce
1 14.5 ounce can of diced tomatoes
1 1/2 tablespoon Chili seasoning
1/2 cup water
1 teaspoon of salt
3/4 teaspoon of pepper

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a frying pan. Drain. Add burger, onion, green pepper pinto beans, pink beans, sauce, diced tomatoes, chili seasoning, water, Salt and pepper to crock pot. Cook on low for 8 to 10 hours or on high for 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Sandy Lafleur
Description: 
This Very good chili. I make it a lot. It's one of my family's favorite. This is a very versatile recipe that can be changed to suite your taste.
