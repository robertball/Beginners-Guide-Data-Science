Title:
Long Grain & Wild Rice with Ginger Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package UNCLE BEN'S® Long Grain & Wild Rice Original Recipe
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Nonstick pan coating spray
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon ginger powder

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions.
Season chicken with salt and pepper.
Grill, bake or pan-sear chicken breasts.
In a small saucepan, add chicken stock, brown sugar, honey, mustard and ginger. Over medium heat, stir mixture often until glaze forms, about 4 minutes.
Remove from heat and let stand until chicken is done.
Apply glaze to chicken and serve with UNCLE BEN'S(R) Long Grain & Wild Rice.

Submitter: 
UNCLE BEN'S®
Description: 
No matter how you choose to prepare your chicken, the rice and sweet sauce in this recipe will make this one of your new favorites.
