Title:
Easy Baked BBQ Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 thin pork chops
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 cup Tomato Ketchup
2 tablespoons White Wine Vinegar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
2 tablespoons Honey
1 tablespoon Yellow Mustard
2 tablespoons Brown Sugar
1 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, mix together Onion, ketchup, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, honey, mustard, brown sugar, and cayenne pepper.
Place the pork chops on a baking tray and season with salt and pepper.
Top each pork chop with 2 tablespoons of mixture.
Bake in a pre-heated oven of 440 degrees F / 230 degrees C for 30 minutes or until meat is cooked through.

Submitter: 
A. Apolskis
Description: 
If you love BBQ sauce, this is a tasty and easy dish to whip up when you're short on time. We like these BBQ chops with long grain rice mixed with turmeric and peas.
