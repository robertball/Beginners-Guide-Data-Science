Title:
Olive Butter Sauce Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. uncooked spaghetti noodles
1/4 tsp. minced garlic
1 Tbsp. butter
1/4 c. olive oil
3 small, pimento stuffed green olives
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp sea salt (or to taste)
dash ground black pepper (or to taste)
grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook spaghetti per package directions, drain, place into bowl. Melt butter over medium heat, cook garlic until fragrant. Pour garlic/butter sauce over spaghetti and toss immediately. Sprinkle basil onto pasta. Heat olive oil over medium heat, meanwhile, dice olives and pimentos. Cook olives in oil until fragrant and heated (slightly cooked, only 30 seconds to 1 minute). Pour sauce over noodles. Grind salt and pepper over spaghetti. Toss spaghetti a second time. Serve, and sprinkle parmesan cheese over spaghetti before eating.

Submitter: 
Denise Russell
Description: 
A light, quick, flavorful meal. Smoothy combines the flavors of pasta and olives.
