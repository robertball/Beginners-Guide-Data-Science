Title:
Swamp Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart prepared sweet tea
1 quart fresh squeezed lemonade

Directions:
Make sweet tea, using your favorite recipe, or if you must, a mix
Add to pitcher one quart of fresh lemonade, or use frozen/powdered mix
Allow to chill in the ice box for at least an hour

Submitter: 
mhurst86
Description: 
This is a  traditional drink in the south. Looks less than appetizing, but it really is great on a hot summer day. This version has no alcohol, so you can serve it to the kids!
