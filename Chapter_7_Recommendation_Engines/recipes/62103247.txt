Title:
Creamy Carrot-Broccoli Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 carrots, peeled & sliced
1/2 - 1 lb. frozen broccoli
1 lb. penne
1 c. cooked, diced chicken
3 Tbsp. butter
3 Tbsp. flour
1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. carrot juice
1/2 c. heavy cream

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.  Add carrots and sauté for a couple of minutes to give them a head start on softening.
Add garlic and broccoli.  Sauté for a couple more minutes, then add 1/4 c. water, cover, and let simmer until broccoli and carrots are tender, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, cook penne.  Drain penne and return to pot.
Drain any excess water from the vegetables and dump them into the pot with the penne.  Add cooked chicken to skillet and cook, stirring frequently, just until good and hot.
Melt butter in small saucepan.
Add flour and stir well.  Let brown for a minute or so.
Add milk, carrot juice, and cream, and cook until heated.
.  Pour sauce into pot with penne, veggies, and chicken, and stir well.
Add seasonings as desired, and serve.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Michael Ramey
Description: 
This healthy recipe can easily be made vegetarian by omitting the cooked chicken (which was added to keep hubby happy ;) )
