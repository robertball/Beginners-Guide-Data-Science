Title:
Sun Dried Tomato Crusted Chicken with Sunny Butter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
2 cups fresh bread crumbs
1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes,oil packed, drained, sliced
4 large garlic clove, peeled
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 eggs
2 tablespoons water
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup dry white wine
2 tablespoons capers, crushed
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1/2 cup cold, unsalted butter
1/2 cup fresh parsley
1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, oil-packed

Directions:
Pre heat the oven to 375. Prepare breasts by trimming fat. Pound each breast to 1/2" thick.  Season with pepper and salt.  Pulse bread crumbs, tomatoes and garlic in the food processor. Transfer to a shallow dish.  Place flour in a second dish.  Blend eggs and water with fork in a third dish.  Dredge both sides of chicken in flour, then dip into egg mixture to coat.  Transfer chicken to crumb mixture and pat onto both sides.  Heat oil in an ovenproof nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken to skillet and saute 3 minutes or until golden brown.  Carefully flip chicken, then place pan oven.  Roast 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through. Rest 5 minutes before serving.
Sunny Butter Sauce-  Boil wine in a skillet until reduced by half.  Add capers and lemon juice. Boil 1 minute.  Reduce heat to low.  Whisk in butter one piece at a time, stirring constantly.  As each melts, add another. Stir in tomatoes and parsley and spoon over chicken.
