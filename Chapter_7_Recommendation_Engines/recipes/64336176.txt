Title:
Overnight Oatmeal Ideas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the oatmeal:
1/3 cup Rolled Oats
1 Tbsp Chia seeds (optional)
¼ cup Greek Yogurt
1/3 cup Almond Milk
Apple Walnut
2 Tbsp Dried Apple, chopped
2 Tbsp Walnuts, chopped
1 Tbsp Raisins
¼ tsp Cinnamon
½ tsp Brown Sugar
Blackberry
1/4 cup Fresh Blackberries
1 tsp Blackberry Jam
½ tsp Vanilla Extract
2 Tbsp Slivered Almonds
Peaches and Cream
¼ cup Peaches, chopped
¼ tsp Cinnamon
½ tsp Maple Syrup
1 Tbsp Cream
Chocolate Cherry
1 tsp Cocoa Powder
1 tsp Cherry Preserves
¼ Fresh Pitted Cherries, chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a mason jar or plastic container.
Shake thoroughly to combine.
Refrigerate overnight.
Eat cold from the fridge or heat in the microwave (without the lid).

Submitter:

Description:
