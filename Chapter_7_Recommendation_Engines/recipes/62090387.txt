Title:
Shrimp & Tomatoes in Green Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb shrimp, peeled, cleaned && devained
3 cloves garlic
1/2 inch piece ginger
5 green onions, roughly chopped
olive oil
1-2 jalepeno chilies, seeded and chopped
3 tablespoon ground coriander
1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds
1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
1/4 cup water
4 tomatoes cut into wedges
2 cups frozen peas
1/2 cup basil leaves, chopped
Cooked Rice

Directions:
In a food processor grind the garlic, ginger, green onions and chilies to a paste with the oil.
Transfer to a large skillet and cook for 2 minutes. Add the ground coriander, fennel and mustard seeds and coconut cream and cook for 3-4 minutes.
Add water to the garlic and spice mixture, cook uncovered over a medium heat for about 4 minutes.
Add tomatos and peas and cook for 2 minutes.
Add the shrimp and cook until shrimp are pink. Add basil, stir in and serve immediately with rice.
