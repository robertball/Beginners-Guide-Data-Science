Title:
Peach Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/971063.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
2 canned peach halves
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
1/4 cup crushed ice

Directions:
In a blender combine lemon-lime soda, peach halves and lemon juice. Blend until smooth and pour into glasses. Serve topped with the vanilla ice-cream and crushed ice.
