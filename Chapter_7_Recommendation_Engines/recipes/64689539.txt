Title:
Pineapple Mango Mandarin Jello Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5102389.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages 3 ounce orange jello
1 mango diced
4 mandarin orange segments
1/2 cup seedless red grapes
1/4 cup crushed pinapple
1/4 cup diced pinapple
1/4 cup walnuts, minced
4 tablespoons sour cream
1/4 cup half and half

Directions:
Follow jello instruction and set it aside in a 2 quart oblong baking dish. Chill for at least 6 hours and then cut jello into 1/2 squares.
In a bowl add mango, orange, red grapes, and pinapple. Mix sour cream and half and half togetther and add to bowl.
Fold into mixture 2/3 of jello squares.
Top with remaining jello squares and walnuts.
