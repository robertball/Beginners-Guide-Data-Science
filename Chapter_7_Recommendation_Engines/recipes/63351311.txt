Title:
Chicken (or Fish) with Tomatoes, Olives and Capers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tsp. olive oil
4 chicken cutlets or fish fillets
1 small onion - diced (about 1 cup)
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup canned tomatoes with juice
1 clove garlic - chopped
1/2 cup pitted and chopped Kalamata olives
2 tbsp. capers - drained
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
2 oz. baby spinach
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat 2 tsp. oil in a large skillet.
Salt and pepper chicken and place in skillet.
Cook until browned well on both sides and cooked through.
Remove from pan and add remaining oil to pan.
Add onion and garlic and cook for about 2 minutes.
Add wine and reduce by half - about 2 minutes.
Add tomatoes and simmer for about 5 minutes.
Add olives, capers and red pepper and cook about 1 minute.
Stir in spinach and cook until it's just wilted - about 3 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper and serve over chicken.
