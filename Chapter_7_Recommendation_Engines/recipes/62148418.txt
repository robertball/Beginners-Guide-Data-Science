Title:
Diane's Chocolate Walnut Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup granulated sugar
2 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 cup flour
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
Chocolate Fudge Walnut Icing
1 1/3cup icing sugar
3 1/2teaspoon butter
2 tablespoon cocoa
2 tablespoon milk
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In large bowl blend butter,sugar and vanilla. Add eggs beat well. In small bowl combine flour,cocoa, baking powder and salt. blend into wet mixture gradually.Stir in walnuts. Pour batter into greased 8 inch square pan. Bake @ 350degrees for 25 min, or until they pull away from sides of pan. prepare icing and frost while still warm.
Icing
In medium bowl place icing sugar and set aside. In small saucepan melt butter over low heat. stir in cocoa and blend until smooth. Add milk.stirring constantly over low heat until mixture has thickened abit. Pour hot mixture over icing sugar. beat until smooth. Stir in vanilla and chopped walnuts. Spread warm icing over warm brownies and enjoy!
