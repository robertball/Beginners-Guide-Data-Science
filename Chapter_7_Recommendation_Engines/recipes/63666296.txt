Title:
Blueberry Oat Dream Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018600.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup quick oats
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
8oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1TB lemon zest
1/8 cup lemon juice
1t vanilla
1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a bowl, mix oats, flours and  brown sugar. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Take half of the oat mixture and press into a greased 9x9 pan. Reserve other half for topping.
Bake for 12 minutes to set crust. When done baking set aside to cool.
Using an electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add egg, vanilla, lemon zest and lemon juice and mix until well combined. Carefully fold in blueberries.
Pour filling over baked crust and top with the remaining crumb mixture.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Allow to cool and place in the refrigerator 1-2 hours before serving.

Description: 
A creamy and dreamy mix of oats, blueberries and cream cheese.
