Title:
Spicy Curried Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites (or egg substitute, like Eggbeaters)
1 teaspoon curry powder
1/4 cup jarred salsa
cooking spray

Directions:
Spray a small frying pan with cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat. Whisk egg whites or egg substitute with curry powder. Pour into pan and allow to cook slightly. Move eggs around the pan with a spatula until they are scrambled and fully cooked. Transfer to a plate, top with salsa and enjoy!

Submitter: 
RHOV
Description: 
Egg whites are mixes with curry powder, scrambled, and topped with jarred salsa in a fast, protein-packed, delicious breakfast! Perfect for hurried mornings or lazy weekends.
