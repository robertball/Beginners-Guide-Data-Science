Title:
White Chocolate Raspberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (18.25 oz) yellow or white cake mix
1 box (5 oz) instant vanilla or white chocolate pudding mix
4 large eggs
3/4 C oil
1 1/4 C raspberry yogurt
1/2 C milk
2 C white chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and grease a 12 cup capacity Bundt pan, set aside.
Mix all ingredients except chips in a large bowl until well blended, don't over mix.
Carefully fold in chips until well incorporated.
Pour batter into cake pan and put it in the oven.
Immediately reduce the temperature to 325. Bake 40-50 minutes until top springs back when pressed. Cool completely before turning out onto a service plate.
