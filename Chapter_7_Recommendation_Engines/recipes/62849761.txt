Title:
Simple Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c tarragon vinegar or white wine vinegar
4 tsp sugar
1 T Dijon mustard
1 tsp minced garlic
1/4 t salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp italian herb seasoning blend
3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Blend all (except oil) for 1 minute until sugar and salt dissolve.
Slowly drizzle oil while mixing.
Mix another minute.
Cover and chill
