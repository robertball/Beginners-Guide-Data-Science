Title:
Hot Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4642992.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups boiling water
3 tablespoons honey
1 lemon, juiced
3 tablespoons rum (optional)

Directions:
In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Remove from heat, and stir in honey, lemon juice and rum.
