Title:
Awesome Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(8oz.)can tomato sauce                              1(6oz.) can tomato paste                             1 teaspoon ground oregano                            1 teaspoon Italian seasoning                         1 teaspoon paprika                                   1 1/2 teaspoons dried minced garlic or 2 or 3 cloves fresh minced garlic                                               1/2 teaspoon fennel seed                             1/2 Tablespoon dried basil                               1/2 teaspoon sugar                                   1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Blend ingredients well.Chill at least one hour before using. Covers two large pizza crusts.
