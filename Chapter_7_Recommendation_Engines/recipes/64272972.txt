Title:
Shepherds pie (On the Menu) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbl grapes oil
1/2 lb short ribs
3/4 cup beef stock
1/2 lb Veal
1/2 lb ground pork
4 tbl unsalted butter
1/2 onion diced
1/2 carrot diced
3 cloves garlic diced
3 star anise
1/2 fresh jalapeño seeds removed
2 red bell peppers, roasted and confit, diced
3/4 tbl chili powder
11/2 tbl tomato puree
1/2 cup Jack Daniels
1/2 cup milk
potato puree,,,,to follow
2 tbls minced chives

Directions:
This can be done the day before or morning of.  Preheat the oven to 250, heat 2 tbl of the oil in a medium dutch oven over medium high heat and brown the short ribs.  Remove the meat and place in a pot with the stock.  Cover and cook the short ribs in the oven for 8 to 9 hours until falling apart. Allow the meat to cool, then remove from the pot, reserving the cooking liquid.  Shred the meat.
Heat the remaining 2 tbl of the oil in the dutch oven.  Brown the veal and pork over high heat, remove the meat and st aside.  In butter, lightly  brown the onions, garlic, carrot star anise, jalapeño, rd bell pepper, and the chili powder over medium heat about 3 minuets.  Add the veal, pork, short ribs, tomato puree, whiskey, milk, and 1/2 cup of the reserved short rib cooking liquid.  Cover, but leave a small vent, and cook for about 4 hours over low heat, until tender.
Spoon or pipe about 3 to 4 cups of potato puree over the meat, covering completely.  Please under a broiler for 2 minuets, until browned.  Top with a sprinkling of minced chives.
