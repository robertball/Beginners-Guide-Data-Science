Title:
Crunchy Bacon Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1922415.jpg
Ingredients:
1-500 g macaroni pasta
8 slices thick-cut bacon, finely chopped
2 Tbsp. butter
2 Tbsp. flour
3 cups milk
1-2 cups aged white cheddar cheese
1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup Panko Japanese-style breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat broiler. Cook pasta in boiling water, following package directions but omitting salt, until tender, 10-12 minutes. Drain, then return to pot.
Cook bacon until crisp. Discard fat and wipe with paper towel.
Melt butter in same pan, whisk flour until mixture turns into a paste. Gradually add milk and stir constantly until sauce thickens. Stir in cheddar, mozzarella, and salt. Season with salt and pepper.
Stir in pasta and bacon until combined. Scrape into a 9 x 13 baking dish (or 8 ramekins set on a baking sheet). Sprinkle with Panko bread crumbs.
Broil in centre of oven until top is golden, 2-3 minutes.
