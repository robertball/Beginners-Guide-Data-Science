Title:
Vietnamese Crispy Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2x (1 lb) snapper
Sea or Kosher salt
4 Tbs veggie oil
3 cloves garlic chopped
6 tomatoes, deseeded and rough chopped
2 red chilies deseeded, finely sliced(Bird's eye) though you can sub.
1 Tbs palm sugar
2 Tbs nam pla (fish sauce)
6 Tbs water
1 tsp cornstarch
2 green onions chopped
2 Tbs roughly chopped coriander

Directions:
1.Cut the heads of the fish off, slash the fillets on each side and season with a bit of salt.
Heat half of the oil in a large frying pann add fish and pan fry on each side for 6-8 min. Or until cooked through
2.Remove fish from pan, add remaining oil,garlic, tomatoes and chilies. Cook over a high heat until the tomatoes have softened. Add palm sugar,fish sauce and water cook for a min or two until the mixture is reduced and sticky. Stir in the cornstarch,onions and coriander cook for another min.
3. Plate fish and spoon sauce over the top serve with rice. Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Chef Lucky
Description: 
Sweet and Spicy, Snapper holds up really well to this cooking, but feel free to use a fish of your liking. Also you can just use fillets instead of whole fish, but for wow factor whole fish is the way.
