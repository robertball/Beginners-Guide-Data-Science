Title:
Slow-cooked Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980437.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium white or yellow onion, sliced
2 pounds Sutton & Dodge boneless beef top round steak, cut into 6 pieces
1 (.87 ounce) package brown gravy mix
1 1/2 cups Market Pantry â„¢ vegetable juice
1 (12 ounce) package frozen home-style egg noodles
1 tablespoon Market Pantry butter
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Place onion in 3 1/2- to 4-quart slow cooker. Place beef on onion, overlapping slightly. Add gravy mix and vegetable juice, making sure all of the gravy mix is moistened; cover. Cook on low setting for 8 to 10 hours.
Before serving, cook noodles according to package directions. Toss with butter and salt. Serve meat and sauce over noodles. Sprinkle with parsley.
