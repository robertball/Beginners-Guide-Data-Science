Title:
Mrs. PIE's Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces angel hair pasta
1 jar ragu meat flavored sauce
1/4 jar classic alfredo sauce
italian seasoning
garlic salt
1 pound lean ground beef

Directions:
cook pasta to package directions.
brown meat. drain fat.
add ragu sauce, alfredo sauce, garlic salt, italian seasoning. heat.
top pasta with sauce.
