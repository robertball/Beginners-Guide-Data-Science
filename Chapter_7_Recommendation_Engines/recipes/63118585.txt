Title:
Sassy Cucumber Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793400.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz Cream Cheese softened
1/4 C Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp Dill
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1/2 tsp Worcestershire
1/4 tsp Salt
dash of pepper, cayenne, onion powder, parsley
1-2 English Cucumbers sliced
Bread - cocktail rye or pumpernickel, french, multigrain

Directions:
Mix all ingredients ,except cucumbers and bread, and refrigerate overnight or several hours to let flavors meld. Spread on top of cocktail rye/pumpernickel or sliced loaves of french, sourdough, multigrain breads. Wash English Cucumbers, score sides and slice cukes to put on top of cream cheese mixture. If using regular cucumbers peel and slice. Sprinkle a little dill on top. Gone in an instant!

Description: 
Flavored cream cheese appetizer, spread on bread with sliced cucumbers on top.
