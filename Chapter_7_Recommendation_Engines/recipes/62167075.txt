Title:
Beefy Tator Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/362848.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 lbs tator tots
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 jar Alfredo sauce
1 Teaspoon Tony Cachere's Seasoning
1 Teaspoon Cavendars Seasoning

Directions:
Spread raw ground beef on bottom of 9 x 13 inch glass dish, season with Tony Cachere's and Cavendars.  Pour and spread Alfredo sauce on beef, and then sprinkle with cheese.  Spread tator tots on top of cheese and cover with foil.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes, remove foil and continue baking 10-15 minutes or until tator tots are browned.

Description: 
Tator Tots, ground beef and cheese make for an easy, filling, and family favorite meal!
