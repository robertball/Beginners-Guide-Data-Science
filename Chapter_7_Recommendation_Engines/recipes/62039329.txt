Title:
Cream Puff dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes instant french vanilla pudding mix
1 8oz. pkg. cream cheese
1 C. water
1 stick butter
1 C. flour
4 eggs
3 C. milk
Cool whip

Directions:
Boil water and butter. Add flour and eggs, one at a time. Spread in jelly roll pan. Bake at 400 degrees for 25-30 minutes; cool. Mix pudding, milk and cream cheese. Spread on crust and top with cool whip.
