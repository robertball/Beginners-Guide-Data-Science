Title:
Shrimp Avocado Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package uncooked penne pasta
1/2 pound bacon-cooked and crumbled
1 pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 avocados - peeled, pitted and diced- spray with lemon juice to keep from browning
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup diced celery or cucumber for crunch
1 cup mayonnaise or 2/3 mayo and 1/3 sour cream
2 tblsps capers
1/4 cup lemon juice
salt, garlic powder, parsley to taste
2 tomatoes, diced- preferably roma tomatoes or cherry tomatoes halved (1 cup)
1 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
4 cups shredded lettuce
or serve in hollowed out tomato shells on a bed of lettuce.

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Place pasta in the pot, cook for 8 to 10 minutes, until al dente, and drain. Rinse under cold running water to cool.
Place bacon in a skillet over medium high heat, and cook until evenly brown. Drain and crumble.
In a large bowl, gently toss together the pasta, bacon, shrimp, avocados, Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, lemon juice, tomatoes, and red pepper. Serve over lettuce.
