Title:
Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rolled oats
2 cups almond milk (any milk will do)
Chia seeds (optional)
1 apple, slivered

Directions:
Mix ingredients and leave in fridge overnight.
Top with yogurt, walnuts, strawberries, bananas, or a topping of your choice.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This is a great recipe to make in bulk so you have breakfast for a few days.
