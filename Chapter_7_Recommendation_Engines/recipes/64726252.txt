Title:
Instant pot Philly cheesesteak stuffed peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6108326.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bell peppers, halved
1 small onion, diced
8 oz. cremini mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 lb. sirloin steak, thinly sliced
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
8 slices provolone
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp garlic
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Cut bell peppers in half, remove membrane and seeds. Set aside.
Set instant pot to sauté mode. Add the oil, butter, mushrooms, and onions. Sauté for about 5 minutes add the minced garlic sauté about 30 more seconds.
Remove mixture and add the sirloin, Italian seasoning, garlic salt, and pepper. Sauté about 5 minutes or until browned.
Add the sirloin to the onion mixture. Place one slice of provolone in the bottom of each pepper half. Top with sirloin mixture.
Clean out insert. Add about 1/2 cup water to the bottom of the insert.
Place peppers on a steamer rack. Cover peppers with foil. Cook on high pressure for 25 minutes.
Quick release pressure.
Remove foil and transfer peppers to a rimmed baking sheet. Top with remaining provolone slices.
Broil until lightly golden.

This takes the Philly cheesesteak to new levels, plus cooks in the instant pot for a quick meal.
