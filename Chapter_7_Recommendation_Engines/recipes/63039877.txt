Title:
Individual Quiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/763324.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs beaten
1 and 1/2 cups tasty grated cheese
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 pound bacon, cooked and chopped up
1/2 cup self raising flour
1 and 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup chopped parsley (optional, use anything you like)
salt & freshley ground black pepper
2 cups of your choice of - chopped mushrooms, peppers, cherry tomatoes, corn, peas, beans, grated pumpkin etc

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees C.
Spray muffin tins WELL with baking spray.
Mix milk, spices, and flower in blender.
Put a small qty of the "other" stuff into each individual muffin.
Pour the egg mixture into the tins to 3/4 full (they will puff up then fall after they are out of the oven),
Pour into the prepared pans.
Bake for 30-45 minutes until set and golden brown.
Can be eaten hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Caroline Ashwood
Description: 
I was looking for something to serve at a New Years buffet, but I think this would work well anytime.

I am doing an experiment to see if they can be frozen.
