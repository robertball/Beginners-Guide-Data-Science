Title:
Party Punch VIII Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (12 fluid ounce) cans frozen concentrated raspberry-kiwi juice, partially thawed
2 (2 liter) bottles raspberry creme soda, chilled
1/2 gallon raspberry sherbet

Directions:
Place raspberry-kiwi juice concentrate in a large punch bowl, Slowly pour in creme soda. Using an ice cream scoop, drop in scoops of sherbet.

Submitter: 
Joyce Paski
Description: 
I made a double batch of this recipe and served it to 40 people at a wedding shower. It was a big hit.
