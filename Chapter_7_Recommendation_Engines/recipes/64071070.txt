Title:
Pineapple Cream Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix
1/2 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3 eggs
12 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 eggs
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup crushed pineapple, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray 9x13 cake pan with pam.
Mix first 5 ingredients together and pour 1/2 into cake pan.
Mix next 5 ingredients together and spoon over cake mix. Spoon the remaining cake mixture over the pineapple mixture and cut through with a knife to marble.
Bake for 1 hour or until done. Cool 10 minutes and invert onto a cake plate.
Cover with thin lemon frosting
