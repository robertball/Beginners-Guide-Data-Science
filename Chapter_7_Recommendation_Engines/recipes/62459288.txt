Title:
Kugel for the Holidays Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042639.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg 8oz softened cream cheese
1/2 cup margarine softened
3/4 cup sugar
8 eggs well beaten
41/2 cups milk
2 tsp vanilla
1tsp lemon juice
dash of salt
8oz fine egg noodles
1/2 cup graham crackers crumbs
1tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Beat together cream cheese, margarine, sugar, mix well. Blend in eggs. Stir in next 4 ingredients. Cook noodles- drain and combine with cheese mixture.Pour in 9x13 greased dish. Mix graham crackers with cinnamon. Sprinkle over the top. Bake 350 for 11/4 hr. Let rest before cutting.
