Title:
Strawberry Romaine Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup vinegar
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons poppy seeds
1 head romaine lettuce, washed
1 pint fresh strawberries, washed
1 avocado, sliced
1/2 cup walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, milk and poppy seeds.
Lightly toss dressing with lettuce, strawberries, avocado and nuts, and serve.
