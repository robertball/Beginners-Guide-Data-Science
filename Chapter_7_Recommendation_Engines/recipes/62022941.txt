Title:
Mexican Fruit Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 ounces crushed pineapple
2 cups flour
2 teaspoons soda
1 cup nuts
1 3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
Frosting
1 stick butter
8 ounces cream cheese
2 cups powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Mix together the dry ingredients and add the wet ones.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
Frosting
Melt the butter and add the softened cream cheese and the vanilla, add the powdered sugar, mix well.  Spread over cooled cake
