Title:
Buttercream Dew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cubes ice, or half-glass crushed ice
1 can Mountain Dew
1/4 cup butterscotch schnapps

Directions:
Add ice to glass.
Add Mountain Dew to glass.
Add schnapps to glass.
Stir throughly.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
E
Description: 
A simple mixture of Mountain Dew, butterscotch schnapps and ice to make a drink that tastes exactly like buttercream frosting! Warning, this is not for dentists! This concoction is the sweetest, most decadent thing you will ever taste!
