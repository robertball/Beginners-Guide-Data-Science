Title:
Chili Pepper Lemongrass Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Coconut Aminos
1/4 Cup Coconut Vinegar
1 Red Jalapeno, Seeded & Diced
1/4 Cup Cilantro, Chopped
1 TBSP Chopped Lemongrass
1 Tsp Salt
1/2 Tsp Japanese Seven-Spice Chili Powder(Togarashi)

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender and puree.

Submitter: 
elizabethanderson1
Description: 
Taking advantage of the fact that we can get coconut anything now, here's a good use for coconut vinegar. You can omit the peppers if you want a milder version.
