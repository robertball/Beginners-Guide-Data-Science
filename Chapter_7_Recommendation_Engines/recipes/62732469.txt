Title:
No Bake Lemon Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small package lemon Jello
1 cup boiling water
3 Tbs. lemon juice
1 8oz cream cheese
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 can evaporated milk -chilled
Crust
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
2 tsp melted margarine
1 Tbs. sugar

Directions:
Make graham cracker crust and press into a 9 x 13 pan.
Stir lemon Jello into boiling water until well dissolved.  Add lemon juice then set aside to cool.
Cream together cream cheese, sugar and vanilla till smooth.
In a separate bowl whip the evaporated milk until stiff and forms peaks when you lift the blender.
Mix together Jello and evaporated milk to the cream cheese mixture until well blended.
Pour mixture onto crust and refrigerate for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Amanda Elaine
Description: 
This is a light and fluffy cheesecake.  You can make it less fattening by using fat free cream cheese, evaporated milk and sugar free Jello, which still makes it just as yummy.
