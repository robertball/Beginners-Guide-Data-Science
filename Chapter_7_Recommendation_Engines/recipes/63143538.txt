Title:
Allie's Eggs in Clouds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, separated per directions
3/8 Cup grated pecorino-romano cheese
3/8 Cup chopped fresh chives or 3 teaspoon dried chives
3/8 Cup cooked crumbled bacon (regular or turkey bacon)
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
Line a baking heet with parchment.
Separate eggs, putting whites in large bowl and yolks in separate small bowls.
Whip eggs whites until stiff.
Fold in cheese, chives and bacon.
Spoon egg mixture into mounds (1 mound for each yolk)  on parchment lined baking sheet.
Make a deep well in center of each egg white mound..
Bake at 450 degrees for 3 minutes then add 1 yolk to each well.
Season with pepper and bake until yolks are just set, about 2 to 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
AllieGeekPi
Description: 
Pretty eggs in clouds formed by baked whipped egg yolks. Simple enough for every day.  Fancy enough for guests. These eggs can be breakfast, lunch or dinner.
