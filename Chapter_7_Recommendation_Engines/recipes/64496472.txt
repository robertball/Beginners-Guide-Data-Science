Title:
Big Ray's Apricot-Glazed Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2610006.jpg
Ingredients:
8 chicken drumsticks (2 lb.)
1/2 cup apricot jam
1/2 cup Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
2 Tbsp.  Dijon Mustard
1 Tbsp. canola oil
1 small red pepper, chopped
1 onion, finely chopped
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup couscous, uncooked
1/4 cup dried apricots, chopped

Directions:
Mix jam, dressing and mustard until well blended. Reserve 1 Tbsp. jam mixture for mixing with the cooked couscous.   Divide mixture in half, placing it with the chicken legs in resealable bag, turning to coat well and allowing to  marinating for 8 hours to overnight. Refrigerating the other half of jam mixture  for later.
Heat oven to 400°F.
Place chicken in single layer in shallow pan. Bake 20 min., turning after 10 min.
Brush chicken with remaining jam mixture; bake 20 min. or until done (165ºF). Meanwhile, heat oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Add vegetables; cook 3 to 5 min. or until crisp-tender. Add broth, apricots and couscous; stir. Bring to boil; cover and remove from heat letting it sit 10-15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed. Stir in reserved jam mixture.
Serve chicken with couscous mixture.
