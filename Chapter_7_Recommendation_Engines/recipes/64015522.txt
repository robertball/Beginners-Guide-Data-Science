Title:
Peanut butter blossoms chex mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c. corn Chex
3c. honey nut Chex
1 tbsp. butter
1/2c. peanut butter
1/4c brown sugar
1/2c. milk chocolate and peanut butter chips

Directions:
1. In large microwave dish mix cereal.
2. In 2c mixing bowl microwave butter, peanut butter, brown sugar 1-2 min until boiling mix after 1 min.  Pour over cereal.
3.Microwave 4 min stirring every mi.do 2 more min. just until cereal begins to brown stir in half of chips cool 5 min than add rest of chips pour out and cool.
