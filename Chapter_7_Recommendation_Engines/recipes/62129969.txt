Title:
Fiesta Pita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole-Wheat Pita
1/2 cup Refried Vegetarian Beans
1/4 Ripe Avocado
2-3 Tablespoons Fresh Salsa
2 Tablespoons Feta Cheese
Hot sauce to taste
Fresh Cilantro to taste

Directions:
Warm the pita in a toaster over and cut in half
Heat up the beans and then mix in 1/4 of a ripe avocado
Add bean mixture to pitas and top with salsa and feta cheese
Finish with hot sauce and chopped cilantro to taste

Submitter: 
Piratefrog
Description: 
A very filling and delicious twist on a bean burrito.
