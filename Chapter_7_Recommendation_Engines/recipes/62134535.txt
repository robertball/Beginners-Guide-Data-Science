Title:
Grandma Jane's Tomato & Cucumber salad (kicked up version) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 large Tomatos diced
2 large cucumbers diced
1 to 1 1/2 cups mayo
1/2 lb thick black pepper bacon crisp and crumbled
Salt and pepper to taste (kosher and 5 pepper blend cracked peper is prefered)
1/4 tsp cumin (optional)

Directions:
Fold the mayo into the tomato, cucumber, bacon and spice  mixture. For a little added flavor, add a tablespoon of the bacon drippings.
It's better if left to marinate over night or at least one hour.

Submitter: 
cj
Description: 
This is the kicked up version of our family favorite of a Tomato, cucumber salad, with added spices that blend into a mouth watering experience.
