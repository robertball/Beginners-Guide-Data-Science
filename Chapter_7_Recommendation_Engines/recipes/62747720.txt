Title:
Chicken Francaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
.
1 egg, beaten
1/2 lemon, juiced
.
2 pats butter
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, sliced
.
1/2 stick butter
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth, divided
1+1/2 lemons, zested and juiced
1/2 cup white wine
1/8 cup heavy cream
.
2 tablespoons cornstarch
.
4 oz cooked whole wheat noodles
.
1 bunch cooked asparagus

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200º.
In a shallow dish, mix together the cornstarch, garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper.
In another shallow dish, mix together the egg and juice of 1/2 lemon.
Dip chicken breasts in cornstarch mixture, then egg mixture, then cornstarch mixture again.
Heat 2 pats of butter in a large sauté pan over medium heat.
Add chicken breasts and cook until golden on each side and cooked through; place in a baking dish as finished.
Loosely tent chicken with foil and place in the oven to keep warm.
Meanwhile, in a small bowl, stir together cornstarch and 2 tablespoons of the broth.
Melt 1/2 stick of butter in the same sauté pan and mix in remaining broth and zest and juice of 1+1/2 lemons and wine; bring to a boil on high heat.
Stir in the cornstarch mixture, reduce heat and cook, stirring, until thickened.
Stir in the cream and heat through.
Pour sauce mixture over cooked noodles and top with crispy chicken.
Serve with asparagus.

Description: 
Cooking the chicken and the sauce separately so the chicken stays crispy.Modified from: Famous Chicken Francaise
