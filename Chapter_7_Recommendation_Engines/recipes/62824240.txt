Title:
Korean Rice Cake - tteok Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1.5 cups milk
1 tsp baking powder
.5 tsp salt
4 T sugar
2 cups glutinous rice flour
2 cups cooked beans, dried fruit, seeds, or nuts

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. In one bowl, whisk together egg and milk.
3. In another bowl, sift together the baking powder, salt, sugar, and flour.
4. Add the egg and milk mixture to the dry ingredients.  Mix well.
5. Stir in the other ingredients and pour into a very well-oiled 9x13 baking pan.
6. Bake for 60 minutes, or until edges brown.  Cool completely before cutting into pieces.
