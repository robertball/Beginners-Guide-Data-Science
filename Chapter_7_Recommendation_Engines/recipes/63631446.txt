Title:
Cranberry, Pistachio and Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick unsalted butter, softened
1 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
2 large eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 tsp. salt
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup chopped pistachios
6 oz. milk chocolate, chopped

Directions:
1.  In large bowl with mixer on medium high speed, beat butter and confectioners sugar.  Add eggs, vanilla and salt and beat until combined.  Reduce speed to low, add flour and mix until dough is smooth.  ( At this point, dough can be refrigerated for up to 5 days or frozen for up to 3 months: thaw in refrigerator before using)  Mix cranberries, pistachios and chocolate into dough.  Divide into 3 2-inch thick logs and wrap tightly in plastic wrap.  Chill about 4 hours, or until firm.
2.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Slice logs into 1/4 inch thick rounds.  Place on parchment lined baking sheet and bake 8 -10 minutes, or until firm and lightly browned.  Transfer to wire rack and cool.
