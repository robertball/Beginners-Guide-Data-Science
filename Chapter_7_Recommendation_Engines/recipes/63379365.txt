Title:
Hot Dog stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
2 tablespoons olive oil
6 potatoes, peeled and cut in chunks
1 (14.5 ounce ) can tomato sauce
1 (9 ounce) package frozen green beans
1 (16 ounce) package beef frankfurters,cut in pieces
6 carrots cut in chunks
6 sauage dogs put in whole
2 T garlic
2 T basil
2TSP of salt
3T garlic

Directions:
Place the onion and olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Slowly cook and stir until the onion is soft. Mix in the potatoes and and carrots green beans and tomato sauce ,add hot dogs , in seperate pan jut brown sauage dogs and add to mixture of veggies and tomato sauce , add basil,salt and pepper, and garlic
