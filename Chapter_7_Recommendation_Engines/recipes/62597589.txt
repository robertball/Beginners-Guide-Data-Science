Title:
Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz cream cheese
1/3c grated Parmesan
1/4c mayonnaise
1/2t oregano
1/8t garlic salt or powder

Directions:
Mix ingredients and form into ball.
Refrigerate for a few hours until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Delicious with crackers!
