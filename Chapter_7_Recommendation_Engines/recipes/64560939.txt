Title:
Oat Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large, old bananas
1 cup oats
Optional: chocolate chips, crushed walnuts, cinnamon, raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Mash up bananas and mix ingredients together.
Cook at 350 degrees for 15 minutes on a greased cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
The nice thing about this is you can either stick with the 2 required ingredients or you can dress up your cookies.
