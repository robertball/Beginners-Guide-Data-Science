Title:
Cheese, Vegetable & Olive Snack Board Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6159342.jpg
Ingredients:
Roasted Peppers
Cut vegetables
Sliced Mozzarella
Honey Virginia Ham, rolled
Barrel Pickle
Bruschetta (I make my own but store bought works too)
Spinach Dip
Mozzarella Balls
Olives with Feta in olive oil
Avocado
Crackers
Baguette cut on the bias

Directions:
Cut bread, slice mozzarella, cut vegetables and put dips and olives into bowls.
Arrange everything on board.

Submitter:

Description: 
We do snack boards all the time! Most of the time I use whatever I have in the fridge & place it on the largest cutting board I have. Very versatile, no two snack boards are the same. Make it your own.
