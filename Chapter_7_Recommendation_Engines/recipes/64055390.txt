Title:
Crockpot Barbacoa and Cilantro-Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Barbacoa:
3-4 lb. beef chuck roast
2 limes, juiced
1/4 c. apple cider vinegar
3 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce (just the peppers, not the sauce)
4 garlic cloves
1 heaping Tbsp. cumin
1 Tbsp. oregano
1½ tsp. salt
1½ tsp. ground black pepper
½ tsp. ground cloves
1 c. chicken broth
3 bay leaves
For the Cilantro-Lime Rice
3 c. water
2 c. Jasmine Rice
3 Tbsp. butter
2 tsp. salt
¾ c. fresh cilantro, minced
1 lime, juiced

Directions:
For the barbacoa:
In food processor (or blender), combine vinegar, lime juice, chipotle peppers, garlic cloves, cumin, oregano, pepper, salt and ground cloves. Process on high until smooth.
In large crockpot, add roast, pepper puree, bay leaves, and chicken broth. Heat on low 6-8 hours.
Shred beef and place back in juice, remove bay leaves. Serve on a tortilla with cilantro-lime rice, Colby cheese, lettuce and guacamole.
For the cilantro lime rice:
Combine rice, water, butter and salt in large saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
Reduce to low and simmer for 20 minutes. When done, add lime juice and cilantro.
