Title:
Waldorfy Kale Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 cups chopped kale (stems removed)
1 red apple chopped
1 cup thinly sliced celery
1/2 cup walnuts, toasted and chopped
1/4 cup plus 2 T raisins
2 T Dijon mustard
2 T water
1 T red wine vinegar
1/8 t sea salt

Directions:
Place kale in large bowl.  Add 1/2 apple, all celery, 1/4 cup walnuts, and 1/4 cup raisins.
Put remaining ingredients in a high power blender and blend until smooth.  If it is too thick add more water to thin.
Pour dressing over kale.  Toss and serve.
