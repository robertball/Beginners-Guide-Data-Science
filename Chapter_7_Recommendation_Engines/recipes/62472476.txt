Title:
Chicken Francais Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c egg substitute
1/4 cup (1 oz) grated fresh parmesan cheese
1/4 c chopped fresh parsley
1/4 c dry white wine (I substitute chicken stock)
2 Tbl. fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp hot pepper sauce (I add more)
3 garlic cloves, minced
8 (4oz each) skinless boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 c all purpose flour
1 Tbl. olive oil, divided
cooking spray
2 Tbl. butter
1/4 cup dry white wine (again, I sub chicken stock)
3 Tbl. fresh lemon juice

Directions:
1. Combine first 8 ingredients in a shallow dish.
2. Place each chickenbreast half between 2 sheets of plastic wrap and pound to 1/4 inch thickness using a meat mallet or rolling pin. Dredge chicken in flour and dip in egg substitute mixture.
3. Heat 1 1/2 tsp oil in a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray over medium heat. Add 4 chicken breast halves; cook 4 minutes on each side or until done. Remove chicken from pan and keep warm. Wipe drippings from pan with paper towel. Repeat procedure with 1 1/2 tsp oil and remaining chicken.
4. Melt butter in pan; add 1/4 cup wine (or chicken stock) and 3 Tbl. lemon juice. Bring to a boil, cook 10 seconds. Serve immediately over chicken.
