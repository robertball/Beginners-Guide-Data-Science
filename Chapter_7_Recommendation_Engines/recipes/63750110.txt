Title:
Margaret's Favorite Poultry Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp granulated onion
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp granulated garlic
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
2 teaspoons vegetable oil.

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients.  Select one whole chicken, 4 to 5 lbs, or 8 pieces chicken.  Rub chicken with two teaspoons vegetable oil.  Apply and rub in dry rub; grill as usual.  Triple the recipe for a turkey.
