Title:
Ham and Cheese Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 cups milk
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp salt
16 slices white bread crust removed
3/4 lb shaved deli ham, cubed
4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups crushed cornflakes cereal
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Combine eggs, milk, dry mustard, and salt.
Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Line the bottom of the dish with half the bread. Sprinkle with 1/2 ham, 2 C cheese, 1/2 egg mixture, Repeat.  Cover, and refrigerate overnight.
Preheat oven to 350  degrees.  Bake 30 mins. In a small bowl, blend the crushed cornflakes and melted butter. Spread evenly over the casserole. Bake additional 20-25 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
