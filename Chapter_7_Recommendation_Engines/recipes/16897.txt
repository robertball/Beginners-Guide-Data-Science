Title:
Hubby's Pizza Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/328758.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups milk
1 1/2 tablespoons butter, softened
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
16 ounces sliced pepperoni sausage
1 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
3 1/2 cups bread flour

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
Select the Bread/Light Crust cycle, and press Start.

Submitter: 
Tamara Neeley
Description: 
This is my husband's recipe for pizza bread with cheese and pepperoni. He makes it in the bread machine and it's yummy!
