Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
1 1/2 cups choco-chips (optional)

Directions:
Cream together all ingredients but the flour and then  slowly add the flour. Add Chocolate chips if wanted.
Cook at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes
