Title:
Jerk Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRY RUB:
2 tsp. ginger
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. ground red pepper
1/2 tsp. allspice
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
......................
6 whole chicken legs
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
3 1/2 cups chopped onion
1 Tbsp. minced garlic
3/4 cup chicken broth
1(8 oz.) can tomato sauce

Directions:
Remove skin from chicken. Combine dry rub ingredients in large bowl. Add chicken and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour.
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 4 minutes each side. Transfer chicken to plate. Discard all but 1 Tbsp. drippings. Add onion and cook, stirring, until translucent, about 4 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook another 30 seconds. Add chicken broth and tomato sauce. Return chicken to skillet. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes or until chicken is done.
Transfer chicken to platter. Season sauce to taste, and pour over chicken.
