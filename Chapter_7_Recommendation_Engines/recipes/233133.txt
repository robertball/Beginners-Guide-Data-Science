Title:
Chicken Salad with Bacon and Dates Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F1034516.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cubed cooked chicken
1 cup crumbled cooked bacon
½ cup mayonnaise
¼ cup dates, finely chopped
¼ cup chopped pecans
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix chicken, bacon, mayonnaise, dates, and pecans together in a bowl; season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Wildman Groves
Description: 
Simple chicken salad with bacon and dates. Best I've ever had! Makes great appetizers when served on crackers or slider rolls.
