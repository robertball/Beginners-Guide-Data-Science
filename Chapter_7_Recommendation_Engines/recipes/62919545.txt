Title:
Down Home Country Breakfast Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735088.jpg
Ingredients:
6 slices of bacon or 1/3 cup bacon grease
1/4 cup + 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
12 ounces evaporated milk
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoon butter
1/2 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Cook the bacon in a frying pan. Remove bacon leaving the grease and add the flour. Stir with a fork and let the flour brown for about two minutes. Once the flour is a medium brown add the milks stirring frequently. Add the butter, pepper and sugar and stir until thickened. Serve over warm biscuits.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
My fathers aunt used to make this gravy for breakfast every time we came to visit. It has been a staple in our family for as long as I can remember. I have tweeked it a little to suit my husbands taste but for me it's still the brown breakfast gravy I grew up with.
