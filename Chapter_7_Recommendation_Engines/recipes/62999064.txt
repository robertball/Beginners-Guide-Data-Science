Title:
Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of Cream of Mushroom soup
1 cup of milk
8 cups of cooked cut green beans
3 cups of French fried Onions
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine soup, milk, beans and 1/2 of onions in casserole dish(4qt)
Bake 350 F for 30 minutes
Remove and cover, refrigerate overnight.
When required, stir, top with remaining onion and reheat in microwave or oven at 350

George. P. Cooper
