Title:
Yogurt Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989300.jpg
Ingredients:
4 avocados, halved, seeded, peeled and mashed
1 lime, juiced
1 cup Dannon® All Natural Plain Lowfat Yogurt
2 plum tomatoes, seeded and chopped
1/2 medium onion, chopped
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 green onions, finely chopped
Tortilla chips

Directions:
In a large bowl combine avocado and lime juice. Fold in yogurt, tomatoes, onion, salt and pepper. Transfer to serving bowl. Top with green onions. Serve at once, or cover and chill up to 4 hours.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Dannon® All Natural
Description: 
The classic avocado guacamole gets a fresh and bright update with the addition of creamy yogurt.
