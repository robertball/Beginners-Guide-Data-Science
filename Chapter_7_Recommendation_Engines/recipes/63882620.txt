Title:
Cheddar Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9-inch baked pie crust
3 cups shredded sharp cheddar
1 tsp minced onion
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
3 eggs
6 peeled medium tomatoes
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 325F(170C)
Heat cheese, onion, salt, mustard and Worcestershire sauce in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until cheese is melted. Remove from heat.
In a large bowl, beat eggs until foamy and gradually add cheese mixture, continuing to beat until smooth.
Pour into baked pie shell for 25 minutes or until filling is just set. Remove from oven.
Cut tomatoes into thin slices, overlap slices around edge of pie to form a wreath and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Bake an additional 15 minutes.
