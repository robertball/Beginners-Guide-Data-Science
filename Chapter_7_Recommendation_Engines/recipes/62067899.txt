Title:
Baked Fish with Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Alaskan cod fillets
8 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon minced onion
1/2 teaspoon dried dill
1 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon thyme
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Preheat 375 degrees. Place fish in a single layer in greased baking dish. In small pan, melt butter. Remove from heat and stir in remaining ingredients. Spread sauce over fish fillets. Bake uncovered for 30 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
The sauce is extremely light and has balanced flavors. Kids love this.
