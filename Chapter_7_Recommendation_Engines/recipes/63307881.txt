Title:
Turkey Crock-Pot Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb. lean ground turkey
1/2c. diced tomatoes
8oz. whole kernel corn, canned
1/2 onion, diced
2 garlic cloves
8oz. black beans, canned
8oz. pinto, chili, or kidney beans, canned
1 Tbsp. tomato paste
1 package Lawry's Chili Seasoning
4oz. low-fat cheddar cheese, shredded (optional)

Directions:
Brown turkey. Drain and discard any fat.
Place all ingredients except cheese into a slow cooker.
Cook for 4 hours on low, or 2 hours on high
Top with 1oz. cheese, if desired.
