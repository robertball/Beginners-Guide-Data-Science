Title:
Corn Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. Hot Dogs - cheap work well
2. 1/2 cup corn meal
3. 1 1/2 cups flour and xtra flour
3. 1/4 c sugar
5. 1 1/3 cups milk
6. 2 teas baking powder
7. 1 egg
8. oil to fry hot dogs in

Directions:
roll uncooked ( cold) hot dogs in some flour ( xtra flour)
coat the dogs so the batter will stick to the dog
beat together 2. 3. 4. 5. 6. 7. in a bowl.
Dip each floured dog in the batter and fry in hot oil turning gently...drain on paper towel

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
we first made these in Cameron Texas...make a large batch and freeze them
