Title:
Mexican Tuna Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/622937.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 8oz package macaroni noodles
3 - cans tuna in water, drained
8 oz mixed vegetables
1 - 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes with green chiles, drained
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 cup mexican crema
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon latin seasoning
1/2 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Cook noodles as directed. Drain and place noodle in bowl.  Add remaining ingredients to bowl and mix well.  Store in the fridge or serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick weeknight meal. Serve with tostadas and hotsauce.
