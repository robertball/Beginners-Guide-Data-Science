Title:
Porcupine Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
White Minute rice
1 small onion, diced
1/2 lb bacon slices (cut in half if desired)
salt, pepper, garlic seasonings to taste (optional)
toothpicks
bar-b-que sauce (or spaghetti sauce)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350
In sauce pan, add water for 4 - 6 servings of rice, adding diced onion to the water. Bring water to boil, add rice and cook as directed.
Mix cooked rice with hamburger, add seasonings as desired. Shape into meatballs.  Wrap one piece of bacon around meatball, skewer with toothpick.  Repeat until all meat/rice mixture has been used.
Bake meatballs at 350 for approx 30 minutes or until browned and cooked through.  Carefully drain grease from pan.  Cover each meatball with small amount of sauce, return to oven for 10 minutes.
