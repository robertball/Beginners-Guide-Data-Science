Title:
Beef Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4664187.jpg
Ingredients:
16 ounces portabella mushroom, sliced
1 medium onion, sliced
2 tablespoons bacon grease
2 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 cups boiling water
1 tablespoon beef base
2 teaspoons worcester sauce
2 tablespoons red cooking wine
2 tablespoons ketchup
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard (Optional)
freshly ground black pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
Heat bacon grease and butter in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute onion until translucent. Stir in mushrooms, and cook until tender.
Add flour and garlic powder to mixture and stir until blended and remove from heat.
In a 1 quart pot boil 2 cups of water and add beef base, Worcester sauce, red cooking wine, ketchup, and Dijon mustard. Reduce heat to a simmer and add saute mushroom and onions to pot, and simmer for 10 to 20 minutes while stiring occasionally.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
We love sauteed mushroom and onions in this house and from time to time I turn them into a beef mushroom gravy when I want over steak, or rice, or mashed potatoes.
