Title:
Hot Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp grated lemon peel
2 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp grated onion
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups cooked, diced chicken
1 cup chopped almonds
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 cup crushed potato chips

Directions:
Blend mayo with lemon peel, juice, onion and salt.  Mix lightly with chicken, and  almonds.  Spoon into individual serving dishes or a 1 1/2 quart caserole.  top with a sprinkling of cheese and potato chips.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes or until piping hot.  serve on a bed of Romaine lettuce.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I found this recipe in a friends Grandmothers recipes, and had to try it.  I am not much on chicken salad, but this one is tasty.
