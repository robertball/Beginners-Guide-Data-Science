Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cubed beef stew meat
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, undrained
1 (15 ounce) can green beans
1 (15 ounce) can carrots with juice
1 (15 ounce) can sliced potatoes with juice
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1  Small head of cabbage
1 (1.25 ounce) package beef with onion soup mix
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place meat, corn, green beans, carrots, potatoes, tomatoes, soup mix, and salt and pepper to taste into the slow cooker; stir to combine.
Cook on LOW for at least 6 hours. Add water if necessary.

Submitter: 
Nana
Description: 
Cans of carrots, corn, green beans, potatoes and tomatoes are combined in a slow cooker with stew meat, sauted cabbage and onion soup mix in this recipe for a one dish meal.  Allow 6 hours cooking time.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Vegetable Soup
