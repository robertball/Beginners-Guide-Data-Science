Title:
"Weak Knees" Sriracha Guacamole Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5094264.jpg
Ingredients:
4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and halved
4 slices cooked hickory bacon, divided (small slices for garnish and rest finely minced)
1 medium avocado - peeled, pitted and diced (save 2 sliced halves for garnish)
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lime juice
1 clove garlic, finely minced
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 small jalapeno (sliced for garnish and 1/4 teaspoon finely minced)
Kosher salt to taste
Weak Knees Cochujang Sriracha to top (Amount...you decide. The hotter the better for me!)

Directions:
Hard Boil 4 eggs then peel and halved
Scoop out egg yolks into medium bowl; add avocado, lime juice, minced bacon, minced garlic, minced jalapeno, cayenne pepper and kosher salt.
Mix/mash together until filling is smoothly combined evenly.
Spoon egg/guacamole mixture into a piping bag.
Pipe filling into each shelled out egg white and garnish with remaining bacon slices, jalapeno slices and drown with as much Sriracha you can handle.
Just enjoy.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
...as title says...weak knees. I love spicy/hot food variations on classic finds.  This version is kinda tame to me.  If you like heat, replace jalapeno with a little Habanero pepper or if you are bold, use a ghost pepper (as always be aware of your personal heat index tolerance).
