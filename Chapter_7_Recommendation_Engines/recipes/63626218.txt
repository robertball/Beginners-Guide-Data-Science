Title:
Maple frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package of cream cheese (softened)
1/2 cup unsalted butter
4 cups confectioners sugar
1 tsp vanilla
3 TBS brown sugar
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 tsp maple flavoring (or to taste)

Directions:
Cream together cheese and butter.
Stir in confectioners sugar, brown sugar, vanilla, maple syrup.
Add maple flavoring, mix well, then add more if needed to taste.
