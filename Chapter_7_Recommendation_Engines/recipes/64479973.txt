Title:
Duck, Feta and Basil Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2519725.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 - 3/4 cup cooked duck meat
1/4 loaf of Italian bread
2 slices tomato, chopped
2 T olive oil
1/4 cup, or about 4 oz feta cheese
1/4 cup torn basil leaves
Shredded parmesan
S&P; to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 365
Cut the quarter loaf in half, the long way
Drizzle some olive oil on both halves
Spread basil on both halves equally
Layer crumbled feta and tomato
Break, or cut duck into small pieces, and layer on top
Sprinkle parmesan on top of both halves
Drizzle more olive oil over both
Salt and Pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Patrick
Description: 
This pizza is a great way to use leftover duck meat. The flavor is amazing!
