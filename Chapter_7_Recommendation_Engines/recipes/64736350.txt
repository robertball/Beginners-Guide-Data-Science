Title:
Ramp Leaf Sea Salt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6276731.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces ramp leaves
1/2 cup sea salt

Directions:
Chop ramp leaves roughly and place them in the bowl of a food processor or vita mix
Process until fine
Transfer the ramps to a bowl, add the salt and mix very well
Spread the green, wet salt thinly on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper
Allow to air dry, stirring a turning occasionally with clean hands or spoon
Depending on the humidity it may take up to 24 hours
Bottle when the salt is dry

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Double or triple this recipe if you have enough ramp leaves.  A wonderful seasoning that has the flavor of spring and will last long after ramps are out of season.
