Title:
Holiday quick bread with variations Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Basic batter:
1 c sugar
4 T shortening or butter
1 c milk
1 egg
1 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 c flour
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
See below for changes to ingredients if Variations are desired.

Directions:
Basic Bread Batter:  Cream one cup of sugar with 4 T shortening.  Add 1 cup milk (or juice if using variations), 1 egg and 1 tsp vanilla extract; beat well.  Mix together 2 cups flour, 3 tsp baking powder, one tsp salt. Add dry mixture to wet mixture and blend. If nuts are desired, stir in at this time. Pour into greased and floured 9x5x3 loaf pan and bake at 350F 45-60 minutes.
Variations:
Cranberry:  Omit Vanilla; add 1 tsp Orange Extract, 1/2 tablespoon orange zest, and 1 tsp Cinnamon to Basic Batter. May sub orange juice for milk for stronger orange flavor. Stir in 1 1 /2 c chopped cranberries.
Apple:  Toss 2 cups chopped apples with 1/4 c of the sugar and 1 1/2 tsp Apple Pie Spice.  Pour half of the basic Batter into pan; top with nuts, if desired, then add other half the apple mixture and top with nuts again.
Pumpkin:  Add 1/4 c milk and 1 T Pumpkin Pie Spice to basic batter.  Stir in 1 c  canned pumpkin.

Description: 
A basic recipe that is easily varied for different tastes.
