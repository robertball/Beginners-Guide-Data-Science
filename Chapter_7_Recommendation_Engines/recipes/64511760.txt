Title:
Funfetti Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2750558.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box of funfetti cake mix
1/3 cup of vegetable oil
2 eggs
1/3 cup powdered sugar
5 tablespoons of rainbow sprinkles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
In a large bowl, mix together oil, cake mix, and eggs to form the dough.
Set the dough aside and in a small bowl mix together the powdered sugar and sprinkles.
Using the cookie dough, form about 1-inch thick balls and roll each ball in the powdered sugar mix.
Set the cookie balls on a cookie sheet and bake in the oven for approximately 6 minutes or until golden on top.
Take the cookies out and let them sit on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
