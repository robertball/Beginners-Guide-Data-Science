Title:
Slow Cooker Island Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/366969.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds cubed stew beef
2 large onions, cut into wedges
1 20 ounce can unsweetened pineapple chunks, juice reserved
1/2 cup beef broth
5 Tablespoons red wine vinegar
2 garlic cloves, crushed
3/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
3/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 green bell pepper, cut into squares
1 red bell pepper, cut into squares
3 Tablespoons brown sugar
1 1/2 Tablespoons cornstarch
2 Tablespoons soy sauce
2 large tomatoes, cut into wedges

Directions:
In a 4 quart slow cooker, add the beef, onions, pineapple juice, broth, 3 T vinegar, garlic, seasoned salt, paprika and black pepper. Mix well.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 6 1/2 hours.
Increase the setting to high, stir in the peppers and brown sugar.
In a small bowl, mix together the cornstarch, soy sauce and remaining 2 Tablespoons of vinegar.
Stir mixture into the slow cooker blending well.
Cook covered for 1 hour stirring occasionally until the sauce has thickened slightly.
Just before serving stir in tomatoes and pineapple.
Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This easy meal using the slow cooker combines stew beef and pineapple, for a wonderful sweet and sour dish that is delicious served on a bed of steamed white rice. Enjoy!
