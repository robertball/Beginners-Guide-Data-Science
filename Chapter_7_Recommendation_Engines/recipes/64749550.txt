Title:
Lemon mug cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/3 cup light brown sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/3 cup milk
1 small egg
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 tbsp lemon zest
1/2 tsp olive oil

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients; flour, salt baking power, baking soda in a small bowl.
Whisk in egg, oil, milk, sugar, lemon juice, lemon zest.
Mix well but not too much, leave a few lumps.
Butter  a large mug.
Place batter into the mug.
Place in microwave for 3 mins.
Take out and leave to dry 5 mins.
OPTIONAL: sprincle mixture of lemon zest and powdered sugar on top.
