Title:
Slow Cooker Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009961.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb skinless/boneless chicken breasts
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup salsa
1 tbsp taco seasoning
1 can black beans
1 cup sour cream
Cilantro (optional)

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker.
Mix together soup, salsa, taco seasoning and black beans.
Pour over chicken and move chicken around to get liquid under it too.
Cook on low for 6 hours or on high for 4 hours.
If desired, remove chicken and shred it then return to slow cooker.
Add sour cream, stirring well and let heat through.
Serve over rice and garnish with cilantro if desired.
