Title:
Black Beans & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup onion  finely chopped
½ cup green pepper  chopped ( I use any color…other peppers are sweeter and give a different taste.)
4 cloves of garlic - minced
1- 29 oz can black beans, undrained
1 ½ cup water
2 packets Sazon Goya without Annatto
2 tablespoons white cooking wine or cider vinegar

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, pepper and garlic, cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in remaining ingredients. Reduce heat a simmer 5-10 minutes. Serve over rice.
