Title:
Alexander's Incredibly Rich Buttermilk Candy Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup buttermilk
2 tablespoons
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups pecan, chopped
2 cups granulated sugar
2 tablespoons white Karo syrup
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine buttermilk and soda.
Pour over Granulated sugar|Sugar.
Let stand for 5 minutes.
Add syrup.
Cook slowly, stirring constantly, until it forms a soft ball in the water.
Remove from heat.
Add margarine and vanilla.
Beat well and add pecans.
Spoon onto buttered foil.

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes rich buttermilk candy that it is so sweet that everyone who tastes this candy will crave more!
