Title:
Reuben Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354573.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (13.8 ounce) package refrigerated pizza crust
1 cup sauerkraut, drained
1/4 cup thousand island salad dressing
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
8 slices corned beef
8 slices Swiss cheese

Directions:
Roll dough into a 12-in. x 9-in. rectangle.  Cut into eight 3-in. x 4-1/2-in. rectangles.  Combine sauerkraut, salad dressing and horseradish.  Place a slice of beef on each rectangle.  Top with about 2 tablespoons of the sauerkraut mixture and a slice of cheese. Tuck sides in and roll up.  Place with seam side down on a greased baking sheet.  Bake at 425 degrees F for 12-14 minutes or until golden.
