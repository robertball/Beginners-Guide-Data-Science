Title:
Classic Caesar Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5oz Clam Tomato Juice (Clamatto juice)
1 oz. vodka
Celery stalk
2 tsp. Lime juice
pepper to taste
salt to taste
2 Dash Tabasco Hot Pepper Sauce (or to taste)
4 Dashes Worcestershire Sauce (or to taste)
Celery salt or table salt(to rim glass)

Directions:
1. Rim glass with celery salt or regular salt.
2. Fill with a few ice cubes, add vodka, tabasco and worchesterchire sauce.
3. Squeeze in a lime wedge.
4. Fill rest of glass with clamatto juice.
5. Dash with salt/pepper to taste.
6. Place celery stalk in glass

Submitter: 
ericasalad
Description: 
This is a classic Canadian cocktail and my favorite cocktail of all. So great you'll wonder who Bloody Mary was. Really great if the drink you're craving is a savory drink instead of sweet.
