Title:
Strawberry Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix (prepare as directed)
2 pints fresh strawberries, sliced thinly lengthwise
1 pint heavy whipping cream (or large container Cool Whip)
8 oz. cream cheese
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Cake: Prepare cake mix as instructed on box.
Divide into THREE 9 inch round cake pans to make 3 layers.
Bake and set aside until fully cooled.
Frosting: In a high speed mixer, beat whipping cream until fluffy (best to chill mixing bowl).
Set aside.
Next, beat cream cheese and powdered sugar until fluffy.
Slowly add whipped cream to cream cheese mixture until thoroughly blended.
Layer: layer each 3 cake layers with whipped frosting.
Place sliced strawberries on top of each layer.
Cover strawberries with a light layer of more frosting to blend with strawberries.  Garnish with whole strawberries.
Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
Bonnie Lang Turnage-Mortgage O
Description: 
This is a great summer cake and can be made simpler by using Cool Whip.  It's easy to make but great on taste.  This is a family favorite that's always requested!
