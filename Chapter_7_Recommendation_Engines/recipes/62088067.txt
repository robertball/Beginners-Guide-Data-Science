Title:
Cake mix Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg white cake mix, 2 layer size
1 t baking soda
1/2 c milk
1 egg
1 c mashed banana
1/2 c chopped nuts

Directions:
Blend all ingredients except nuts. Beat 2 minutes, stir in nuts. Pour into 2 greased 9x5 inch bread pans. Bake 35-40 min at 350F. Cool 10-15 minutes before removing from pans.
