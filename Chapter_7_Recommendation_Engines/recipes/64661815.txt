Title:
Moms Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4565170.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
6 Red Onions
9 cloves Garlic
3 ¼ lbs. Ground beef
2 ¼ lbs. Cubed beef
3 cans (16 oz.) Diced Tomatoes
7 cans (6oz.) Tomato paste
2 cans  (16 oz.) of Tomato Sauce
2 cups Dark Beer
2 cups Black Coffee
1 can (14 oz.) beef broth
1 tbl. Oregano
2/3 cup Chili powder
1 tbl. Cayenne pepper
1 tbl. Coriander
Salt & Pepper
¼ cup Crushed red pepper
1 ½ cup Brown Sugar
3 tbl. Cumin
3 tbl. unsweetened Cocoa powder
12 fresh hot Chile peppers, seeded and chopped
4 cans (16 oz.)  White beans (drained)
4 cans (16 oz.)   Black beans (drained)
4 cans (16 oz.)  Kidney beans (drained)
Grated Cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
1.	Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Cook onions, garlic, ground beef and cubed beef in oil for 10 minutes, or until the meat is well browned and the onions are tender.
2.	Mix in: Diced tomatoes, Tomato Paste, Tomato Sauce, Dark beer, Coffee, and Beef broth.
3.	Season with Brown sugar, Chili powder, Cumin, Cocoa powder, Oregano, Cayenne pepper, crushed Red Pepper, Coriander and Salt & Pepper to taste.
4.	Stir in 2 cans of each type of the beans and hot Chile peppers.
5.	Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
6.	Stir in the 2 remaining cans of each type of beans, and simmer for another 30 minutes.
7.	Add grated Cheese and Sour Cream on top of serving.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
This is my go-to recipe when I want to make a large pot of chili for 50 people!
