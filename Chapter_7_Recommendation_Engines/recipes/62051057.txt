Title:
Carrot cake of my dreams! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded carrots (3-4 large)
1 cup shredded zucchini (1 medium)
3 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ginger
1 20-oz can crushed pineapple
1 cup brown sugar
1 8-oz carton Egg-Beaters
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1 Tbsp vanilla
1 cup raisins
3/4 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
1. Shred carrots to make 2 cups; shred zucchini to make 1 cup.  Set aside.
2. In large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, soda, baking powder, salt & ginger.
3. Drain pineapple well, reserving juice.  In medium bowl, with wire whisk or fork, mix drained pineapple, brown sugar, Egg Beaters, oil and vanilla until smooth.  Still pineapple mixture, shredded carrots & zucchini, and raisins into flour mixture just until flour is moistened.
4. Pour batter into greased Bundt pan.  Bake 55-65 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.  Cool in pan 10 minutes; remove cake from pan, cool completely on rack.
5. When cool, glaze if desired.  Make glaze from powdered sugar and enough reserved pineapple juice to make it spreadable.  Drizzle over cake.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
Very dense, moist carrot cake, full of fruit.  In a taste test against a more traditional butter-based carrot cake, my co-workers thought mine *must* be the more sinful!  But no, it's practically VIRTUOUS!
