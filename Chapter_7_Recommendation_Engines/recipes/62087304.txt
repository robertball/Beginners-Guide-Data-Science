Title:
Chicken Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts diced
1 can petite diced tomatoes
1/4 lb Italian prosciuto thin sliced &diced
2 cups thinly sliced baby bella mushrooms
8 oz smoked gouda shredded finely
2 cups fat free milk
1 TBSP butter

Directions:
Double boiler:bring water to boil in bottom
heat milk in top, whisk in cheese.
Be sure to whisk often to ensure blending.
Over med heat melt butter in large skillet
Lightly brown chicken pieces
Add prosciuto, tomatoes and mushrooms
Sautee on med heat 2 minutes, reduce to low
Once blended, poor sauce on chicken mixture
Stir until well blended.
Increase heat back to med, stir often
Cook over med until sauce reduces, 5 mins
Serve over linguine.

Submitter: 
Karen D.
Description: 
I came up with this recipe on a snowy day when I was really bored; it is now one of our favorite dishes. Chicken, salty prosciuto, smoky cheese, sweet tomatoes, and mushrooms go into making this great dish
