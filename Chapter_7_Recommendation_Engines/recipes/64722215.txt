Title:
Pumpkin Whoopie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. flour
1 ½ c.  sugar
3 ½ tsp. cinnamon
2 tsp. Baking soda
2. tsp. Baking powder
1 tsp. Salt
1 ¼ Crisco oil
4 eggs
2 c. pumpkin
Icing:
5 Tbsp. flour
1 cup milk
1 cup (2 sticks) butter
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
For the Whoopie Pies:
Mix all dry ingredients together.  Mix all wet ingredients together.
Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.  Beat until smooth.
Drop onto cookie sheet and bake at 375 for 15 minutes.
For the icing:
Place 5 T. flour in a small saucepan.  Slowly whisk in 1 c. milk.  Cook on medium heat until thick.  Cover with plastic wrap and cool (I put it in the freezer to speed it up)
In separate bowl, cream 2 sticks of butter, 1 c. sugar, 1tsp. Vanilla.  Whip well.    Mix in flour and milk – beat until smooth and creamy– will take awhile.
Do not ice the whoopie pies until they are completely cooled.
