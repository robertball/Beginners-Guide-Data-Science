Title:
Sicilian Blood Orange Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 blood oranges, ripened (ripened Naval oranges will also work)
1/4 cup green onions, sliced
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
black pepper

Directions:
Peel and section the oranges, then slice each section in half, removing any seeds. Place in medium bowl and toss orange sections and green onions. Drizzle with E.V.O.O. just until coated. Lightly sprinkle with pepper. Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Rochelle Brooks Thompson
Description: 
This is one of my favorite summer dishes, refreshing and deliciously healthy! I was given this recipe by a Sicilian friend while living in Sicily Italy. My family adores this dish.
