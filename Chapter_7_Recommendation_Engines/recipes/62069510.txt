Title:
Old Fashioned Oatemal Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups bread flour
1 t. salt
1 t. baking powder
1 t. baking soda
1/3 c. sugar
2 c. quick-cooking oatmeal
1/2 c. molasses
2 T. shortening, melted
2 c. buttermilk
1 cup raisins (optional)

Directions:
place ingredients in your bread machine per instructions. Select basic bread cycle.

Submitter: 
SLEEEPY
Description: 
This recipe was converted by my grandma for the bread machine. it is originally from The Betty Furness Westinghouse cookbook from the 50's.  Enjoy!
