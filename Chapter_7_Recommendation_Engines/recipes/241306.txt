Title:
Vegan Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2107369.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5.5 ounce) can tomato paste
2/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup maple syrup
2 tablespoons molasses
2 tablespoons mustard powder
2 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons smoked paprika
2 tablespoons sea salt
2 tablespoons fresh ground black pepper
2 teaspoons natural turbinado sugar (such as Sugar InThe RawÂ®)

Directions:
Blend tomato paste, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, molasses, mustard powder, garlic powder, smoked paprika, sea salt, ground black pepper, and sugar together in a blender.
Pour blended mixture into a saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until just heated through, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
chefcs
Description: 
This vegan barbecue sauce is a wonderful alternative to more-traditional sauces, giving you a quick and easy option to opening a jar. You can use it right away or allow to cool and store in a glass jar in the fridge.
