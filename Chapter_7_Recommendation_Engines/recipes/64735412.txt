Title:
Sour Cream Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
3 cups flour
1/2 pound butter or shortening
1/2 pint sour cream
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
6 eggs, beaten separately
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Bake at 325 degrees
Mix all ingredients
Pour into tube pan

Submitter: 
CaroleJordan
Description: 
Keeps well.  Makes a large cake.
