Title:
Moms Heavenly Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Whipped cream
1  half pint whipping cream
1  teaspoon sugar (or to taste)
1/4  teaspoon vanilla (or to taste)
Salad
1 can fruit cocktail
1 small banana, sliced
2 tablespoons cornstarch
4-5 tablespoons water

Directions:
Put glass, ceramic or metal bowl along with electric mixer beater attachments to chill, in refrigerator. For making the whipped cream.
Open and drain fruit cocktail, reserving liquid.  set fruit aside.  in a small saucepan over medium heat bring canned fruit liquid to a boil.
Meanwhile, mix cornstarch and water. Drizzle the cornstarch mixture into the boiling liquid whisking constantly until it thickens to a gel consistency.  remove from heat and cool, when cool enough put in fridge to get cold, about 30 minutes to an hour.
In the meantime make the whipped cream.  get bowl and beaters from refrigerator, add whipping cream to bowl, add sugar and vanilla and mix on medium speed until it gets a bit frothy then turn up the mixer to high.  Mix until you get firm peaks. taste while mixing for sugar and vanilla adjust if necessary.  put in refrigerator until fruit gel is cold.
Slice banana into bowl with fruit cocktail.  set aside.  Gently fold gel into whipped cream, start with half of the gel first and see if you want the rest added.  then mix in fruit.
