Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot Sweet Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6637930.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1 medium sweet bell pepper, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
1 Tbsp curry powder
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp dried thyme
1 Tbsp packed brown sugar
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup raisins
1 can (14-1/2 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
Hot cooked Couscous or orzo or egg noodles or mashed potatoes or rice
Chopped fresh parsley, optional

Directions:
Place onion, pepper and garlic in a 6-qt. slow cooker. Arrange chicken pieces over vegetables.
Whisk the next 5 ingredients with the chicken broth. Pour over chicken. Cover and cook on high for 1 hour. Add raisins and tomatoes. Reduce heat to low and cook until chicken reaches 165Â°, 2-1/2 hours. Serve over desired side; if desired, sprinkle with parsley.

This is a hearty and slightly sweet chicken dish that changes depending on the starchy side you choose.
