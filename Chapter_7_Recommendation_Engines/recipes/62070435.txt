Title:
Quick & Easy White Chocolate Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 2 cups cold milk, divided
- 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
- 2 packages (3.3 oz. each) instant white chocolate pudding
- 8 oz. frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 9 oz. prepared graham cracker crust
- 1 tsp. chocolate deocorating sprinkles (or shavings from chocolate bar)

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and 1/2 cup of milk in a large bowl with mixer on low speed, until smooth.  Add pudding and remaining milk and beat on high speed for 1 minute, until blended.  Fold in whipped topping and stir with spatula until smooth and well blended.  Spoon into crust.  Sprinkle chocolate sprinkles on top.  Chill for 2 hours or until firm.
