Title:
Best Smoothie Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4512885.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt
2 scoops vanilla ice cream
8 frozen strawberries
1 banana, frozen and chunked
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 tablespoon chocolate syrup
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Blend the yogurt, ice cream, strawberries, banana, sugar, vanilla, chocolate syrup, and milk together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
KATHLEEN20
Description: 
A very smooth and creamy treat! Chocolate, strawberry, and banana make a perfect combination for this smoothie.
