Title:
Quick and Easy Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium Idaho potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp cornstarch, 2 Tbsp water
1 c whole milk, warmed
1/2 tsp hot sauce
1 1/2 c. cheddar cheese, shredded
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place potatoes in a medium saucepan and fill with water one inch above potatoes. Boil for 10 min.
Meanwhile, in large microwave safe bowl, microwave butter for 30 seconds on high or until melted.
Combine cornstarch and water in a small container with a tight fitting lid. Shake vigorously.
To the butter, add warmed milk, hot sauce, cornstarch slurry, and cheese. Whisk until combined. Microwave on high for 3 minutes, stirring after each minute. Season with salt and pepper.
Drain potatoes and return to pan. Stir in cheese sauce, cooking over low heat until thickened.
