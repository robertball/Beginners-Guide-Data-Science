Title:
Lemon Souffle Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/873351.jpg
Ingredients:
two tablespoons butter, room temperature (plus extra for greasing ramekins.)
1/2 cup plus two tablespoons caster sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup double-cream milk or half-and-half
1/6 cup fresh-squeezed lemon juice
1/2 tablespoon lemon zest
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 eggs, separated
confectioners' sugar to garnish (optional)

Directions:
Grease four small ramekins with butter and dust with caster sugar.
In a mixing bowl, combine sugar and butter. Add egg yolks, one at a time. Add lemon zest, vanilla, milk, lemon juice and flour.
In a clean bowl, whip the egg whites to soft peaks. Add one tablespoon of caster sugar. Fold into the batter.
Pour into the ramekins and bake in a Bain Marie at 350 for 40 minutes.
Garnish with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Ross
