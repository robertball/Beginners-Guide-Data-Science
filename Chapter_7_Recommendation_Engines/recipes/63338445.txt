Title:
Pork Tenderloin Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
3 Tbsp Dijon mustard
1 Tbsp honey
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
2lb pork tenderloin

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
Place pork tenderloin into a ziploc bag and pour marinade over tenderloin.
Place in refrigerator and marinate for at least 6 hours.
Remove tenderloin from refer and allow to reach room temperature.
Start BBQ and bring to 350*F.
Cook on indirect heat for 45 minutes or until juices run clear.
Remove from grill and allow to stand for 10 minutes before serving.
