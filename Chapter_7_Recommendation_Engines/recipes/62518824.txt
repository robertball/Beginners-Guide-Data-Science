Title:
Italian Salad Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511389.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
5 slices white bread, cut into 1/2-inch cubes

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the butter, Italian seasoning and garlic powder. Add bread cubes and toss to coat, being careful to evenly coat all pieces.
Arrange in a single layer on an ungreased baking sheet.
Bake at 325 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until lightly browned, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description:
