Title:
Melon Salad with Honey-Yogurt Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cantaloupe
1 cup watermelon
1 cups honeydew melon
Sauce
1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
1tbsp raw unfiltered honey
1-2 tsp fresh lime juice
1/2 tsp grated lime peel
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
mint

Directions:
Chop and mix all melons together in a bowl
In a separate bowl, mix all ingredients of the "Sauce" until fully mixed together
When serving, portion out melon into a personal bowl and then top with desired amount of sauce.
Garnish with mint

Submitter: 
Casey McMahon
Description: 
Easy easy and delicious when melon is in season!  This is a healthy option for a summer salad or even a summery dessert.
