Title:
Yu Xiang Tofu--Tofu in Hot Garlic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/406037.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon rice vinegar
1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
4 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon corn starch
(Mix everything above in a bowl and set it aside)
1 box of firm tofu (13 oz)--cut into thin small squares
2 tablespoon chopped green onion
1 tablespoon pressed garlic
2 1/4 teaspoon pressed ginger
2 tablespoon chopped red pickled chili pepper
4 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
Heat up 2 tablespoon vegetable oil until you can feel the heat above the frying pan, pan fry the tofu until golden yellow. Set it aside.
Heat up 2 tablespoon vegetable oil until you can feel the heat above the frying pan, put in chopped red pickled chili pepper. Stir fry the chili pepper until the oil turns red.
Put in ginger and garlic, stir everything evenly until you can smell the aroma.
Put in the tofu, and then the sauce. Turn the stove to medium heat and cook the mixture until the sauce is reduced and thickened.
Then add the chopped green onion, and stir everything evenly and gently, you are ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Tao,RN
Description: 
Yu Xiang sauce is one of most famous Sichuan sauces. Yu Xiang mean fish aroma. It originally came from a poor family a long time ago and it was totally a surprise. A housewife wanted to save the leftover sauce that was made for something else. But when she put it together, it had a nice flavor that is similar to the aroma of fish. You may use this sauce for tofu, eggplant, pork, etc. Today we are making Yu Xiang Tofu (Tofu in Hot Garlic Sauce).
