Title:
Grandma's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/820467.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb Ground Beef
2 Large Onions
2 - 29 oz cans Tomato Sauce
2 - 12 oz cans Tomato Paste
1 1/2 tbsp Parsley Flakes
1 1/2 tbsp Garlic Powder
1 1/2 tbsp Italian Seasoning
1 tsp Oregano
1/2 tsp Ground Cayenne Pepper
1 tsp Sea Salt

Directions:
Mince Onions
Brown Ground Beef
Add the rest of the ingredients to a pot
Simmer until hot
