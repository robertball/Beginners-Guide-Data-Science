Title:
Mexican Wedding Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 T. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup sifted flour
1 cup finely chopped walnuts
Confectioner's sugar

Directions:
1. Thoroughly mix butter, sugar and vanilla.  Gradually stir in flour and walnuts.
Chill.
2. Heat over to 350 degrees.  Shape dough into marble-size balls.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet for 15 min. or until firm but not brown.  Roll gently in confectioners sugar; cool and roll in sugar again.
