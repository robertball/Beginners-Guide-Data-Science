Title:
Oven Roasted Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951770.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8 red potatoes, quartered
Garlic salt 1 tsp (or to taste)
Montreal's Chicken Seasoning 1 Tbsp or as desired
Dill weed 2 tsps or as desired
2 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
quarter red potatoes, place in pyrex dish (oven proof) that has been prepped with 2 tbsp olive oil in bottom.
Add seasonings.
place uncovered 350 degree oven for 45 mins to 1 hr. or until potatoes are soft, and slightly browned. Stir to mix potatoes in oil once or twice.

Submitter:

Description: 
Red potatoes make a nice addition to any meat dish, especially chicken
