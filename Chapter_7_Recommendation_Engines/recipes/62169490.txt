Title:
D's Light Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 lb 93% lean ground beef
1 cup chopped onions
1 can (10.5 oz) 98% fat free condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can (10.5 oz) 98% fat free condensed cream of celery soup
1 cup reduced fat shredded cheddar cheese
1 package (16 oz) frozen mixed vegetables
1 package (32 oz) frozen tater tots

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Spray 9x13 glass baking dish with cooking spray.
Brown ground beef with onion and garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste,
Drain fat.
Add soups and frozen vegetables to seasoned ground beef, mixing thoroughly.
Spread beef mixture into a sprayed 9x13 baking dish.
Sprinkle shredded cheese over hamburger mixture.
Arrange tater tots in a single layer, covering entire dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes or until tater tots are golden brown and crispy.
Place under broiler for last 5 minutes for additional crisping of tater tots, if desired.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
This is a lightened up version of the original tater tot casserole my family has enjoyed over the years.  Sometimes I make it a bit more spicy by replacing half of the cheddar cheese with pepperjack!
