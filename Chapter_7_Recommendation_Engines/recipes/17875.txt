Title:
Lisa's Sunflower Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/845901.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
1 1/4 cups warm skim milk
2 tablespoons margarine, melted
3 tablespoons molasses
1 teaspoon white sugar
3 cups bread flour
1/2 cup wheat bran
1/4 cup sunflower seeds

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select cycle; press Start.

Submitter: 
LISA GALLOWAY
Description: 
The sunflower seeds give this bread a great crunchy texture and the molasses makes it warm and sassy. You can substitute whole wheat flour for some of the bread flour if you like.
