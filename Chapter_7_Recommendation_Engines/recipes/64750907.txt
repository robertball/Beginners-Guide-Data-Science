Title:
Keto Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs
(8oz) Block of cream cheese
1 1/2 cups almond flour
1 cup butter (softened)
1/2 cup coconut flour
1 cup Stevia
2 tbsp baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
In a mixer, add room temperature cream cheese, softened butter, and swerve together. Blend well. Add in the eggs and mix with a spatula.
In a different bowl, mix the flour and baking powder together. Combine and fold in that with the wet mixture a little at a time.
Grease a bundt pan.
Bake for approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Let cool.
Add toppings and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Joseph Alvarado
Description: 
If you’re on a low carb diet or the keto diet, try this pound cake! You won’t miss the carbs one bit! It is great topped with heavy whipping cream and strawberries.
