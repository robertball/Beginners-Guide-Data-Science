Title:
Quick Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cans White meat chicken
1 chicken boullion cube
Texjoy steak seasoning
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
Sour Cream
Butter
Milk
Noodles

Directions:
Mix together chicken, soups, boullion, TexJoy, butter and milk.
Bring to boil and add noodles. Cook for 5 minutes, then add sour cream and more milk if needed.
Serve with cornbread or rolls.
