Title:
Good for You Choc-Oat-Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 t baking soda
1/2 t ground cinnamon
1/2 t salt
1 1/4 C packed dark brown sugar
1/2 C granulated sugar
1/2 C butter
1/2 C unsweetened applesauce
2 lg. egg whites
1 T vanilla extract
2 1/2 C quick or old-fashioned oats
1/2 C chopped nuts (optional)
2 C (12 oz. pkg) semi-sweet Chocolate morsels

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt in a small bowl.  Beat sugars, butter and applesauce in a large mixer bowl until smooth.  Beat in egg whites and vanilla extract.  Gradually beat in flour mixture.  Stir in oats, morsels and nuts, mix well.  Drop by rounded tablespoon onto prepared baking sheets.
Bake for 9-20 minutes for chewy cookies or 12-13 minutes for crisp cookies.  Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes; remove to wire rack to cool completely.
