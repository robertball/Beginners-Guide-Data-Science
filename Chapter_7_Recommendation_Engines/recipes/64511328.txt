Title:
Ham 'n Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. butter or margarine, divided
2 tbsp. flour
1 cup milk
1 cup shredded cheddar or marble cheese
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups diced fully cooked ham
1 1/2 cups medium noodles, cooked & drained (about 1 cup dry pasta)
1 cup frozen mixed veggies (or veggie of choice)
dill
thyme
1 tbsp. mustard
1/4 cup (or more) crushed cornflakes or croutons

Directions:
Melt 2 tbsp. butter in saucepan.
Stir in flour until smooth.
Gradually add milk.
Bring to boil over medium heat; cook & stir x 2 minutes.
Remove from heat.
Stir in cheese, mustard and salt till cheese is melted.
Add dill & thyme to taste.
Add ham, cooked noodles and veggies.
Pour into a greased 1 quart baking dish.
Melt remaining 1 tbsp. butter and stir in cornflakes.
Sprinkle over casserole.
Bake, uncovered, @ 350 x 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A great way to use leftover ham in a creamy sauce with pasta.
