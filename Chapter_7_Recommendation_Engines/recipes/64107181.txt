Title:
Proznick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds small-curd cottage cheese, 6 eggs, 6 T butter (melted), 6 T flour, 2 cups (8 oz) cheddar cheese  (shredded), 1 10-oz package frozen chopped broccoli or spinach (defrosted)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Pour into a greased 9x13-inch pan and bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for one hour. Serves 8-10.

Submitter: 
Rina Donna A. Schwartz
Description: 
A good, cheesy, vegetarian luncheon dish.
