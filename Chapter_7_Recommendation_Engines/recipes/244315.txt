Title:
Chocolicious Ice Cream (Dairy-Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 cups hemp milk
3 tablespoons chia seeds
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1/2 cup agave nectar
2 ounces semi-sweet baking chocolate
2 teaspoons almond extract
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat hemp milk in a saucepan over medium heat until warmed, about 5 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat and stir chia seeds into hemp milk. Let mixture sit, stirring occasionally every hour, until thickened, 2 to 3 hours.
Mix cocoa powder, agave nectar, baking chocolate, almond extract, vanilla extract, salt, and pepper into chia-milk mixture; blend in a high-powered blender until smooth. Cool mixture in refrigerator for 3 hours.
Blend mixture in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions until frozen, 25 to 30 minutes.

Chocolate ice cream with chia seeds and hemp milk! A healthy alternative ice cream for vegans and lactose-intolerant individuals.
