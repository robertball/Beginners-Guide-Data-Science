Title:
Almond Jello with Mandarin Oranges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup Milk
2/3 cup Sugar
2 packets Knox Blox gelatin
2 cups Boiling Water
2 teaspoon Almond Extract
1 can Mandarin Oranges (11 or 15 oz can)

Directions:
Mix Milk and Sugar
Sprinkle gelatin on surface of liquid and let stand for 1 minute before mixing.
Add Boiling water and mix well.
Add Almond extract.
Refrigerate several hours until hard.
Cut jello into 1 inch squares
Add the whole can of mandarin oranges and juice
Mix and enjoy

Submitter: 
beeko
Description: 
This is an old chinese favorite for desert. Unsweetened soy milk can be substituted for milk. I make this in a casserole dish about 9x7.
