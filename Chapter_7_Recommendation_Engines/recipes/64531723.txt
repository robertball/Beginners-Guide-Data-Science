Title:
Sausage Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3056411.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c. Croutons
2 c. Shredded Cheese
2 lb Sausage (cooked and cut into pieces)
6 Eggs
2 1/2 c. Milk
1/2 tsp. Dried Mustard
1 can Cream of Mushroom
1 c. Milk

Directions:
Arrange croutons on bottom of 13 x 9 in. pan
Mix eggs, mustard and milk together and pour over the croutons evenly
Sprinkle 2 c. of shredded cheese
Evenly spread sausage over the top.
Cover and place in fridge over night.
In morning...
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix can of cream of mushroom soup with 1 c. milk and spread evenly over the top.
Bake for 1 - 1/2 hours until cooked.
