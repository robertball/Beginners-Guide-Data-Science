Title:
Bacon Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz Fresh Whole White Mushrooms
3 Tbs melted butter
4 strips of bacon
6 medium pitted black olives chopped fine
4 Tbs green onion chopped fine
1 tsp marinated minced garlic
½ tsp salt - ¼ tsp pepper
Dash ground red pepper
1 Tbs flour
1/4 C Milk
4 slices Swiss American cheese (chopped)
3 Tbs grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Fry bacon in small pan on medium, remove bacon, reserve drippings.
Remove stems from mushrooms. Place mushrooms on baking sheet caps down Brush mushrooms with melted butter.
Chop fine: black olives, green onion, stem pieces and bacon.
Saute chopped ingredients in bacon drippings, Add seasonings and minced garlic.
Press sautéed ingredients to the side of the pan, add the flour to the drippings in the pan and stir to make paste. Slowly pour in milk. Mix sautéed ingredients into gravy mixture. Add cheeses cook until melted through.  Mixture will be thick. Stuff prepared mushroom caps with mixture and bake 20 min. Remove mushrooms from baking sheet, cool 2 min on platter.
