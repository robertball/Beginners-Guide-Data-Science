Title:
Green Bean Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 strips bacon fried & crumbled, save drippings
1/2 onion, minced
2 Tab flour
1/2 tea. sugar
1/2 tea. salt (optional - I don't use it)
Dash pepper
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
2 cans cut green beans, drained
1 cup swiss cheese shredded
1/3 cup cornflakes
1 Tab butter

Directions:
In skillet fry bacon & until done.  Set aside.  Use the drippings from the bacon & saute the minced onion about 3 minutes.  Blend in flour, sugar, & (salt, if you choose to use) & pepper.  Add milk all at once and cook til thick & bubbly.  Remove from heat; stir in sour cream.  Add beans.  Spread 1/3 of the bean mixture in a 1-quart casserole.  Sprinkle 1/2 of the Swiss Cheese over beans; repeat layers ending with beans.
Melted butter in a pan, coat the corn flakes in the melted butter until well mixed.  Top the beans with the corn flakes.  Bake 400 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Virginia
Description: 
An alternative to the regular Green Bean Casserole.  This recipe has the crunch on top with creamy, cheesy center.  Definitely a twist to the norm.  Hope you enjoy it as much as my family does!  :)
