Title:
Smoked Salmon Mousse Tomato Tartlet’s Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/368152.jpg
Ingredients:
20	        Campari tomato’s
1 (8 oz)	Cream cheese, softened
4-5 oz	        Smoked salmon, finely chopped
1 TBLSP         Lemon juice, and fresh lemon zest
2-3 TBLSP       Worcestershire sauce
3-4 TBLSP       Heavy Cream
2-3 TBLSP       Black olives, chopped
To taste        Ground black pepper
To taste        Garlic powder
To taste        Green onions, chopped
10 large        Green olives with pimento

Directions:
Wash and cut off tomato tops. Core out the  inside of tomatoes and set aside.
Cut the green olives with stuffed pimento into rings for garnish. Take the unused ends of the green olives and finely chopped to add with other ingredients.
Mix all ingredients together and spoon into tomatos.
Garnish top with sliced green olives and green onion spears.

Submitter: 
Marietta Higgins-Spillone
Description: 
Salmon mousse with a creative presentation worth the effort. Works as an appetizer or side dish
