Title:
CHARLESTON CHEESE DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ C. mayonnaise
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 C. grated sharp cheddar cheese
½ C. grated Monterey jack cheese
2 green onions, finely chopped
Dash of cayenne pepper
8 Ritz crackers, crushed
8 slices bacon, cooked & crumbled (dice before frying)

Directions:
Dice and cook bacon; drain well and set aside.
Preheat oven to 350°F. In a medium bowl, mix the mayonnaise, all the cheeses, green onions & cayenne pepper. Transfer mixture to a shallow baking dish.
Top mixture with cracker crumbs; bake for 15 min., or until heated through. Remove the pan from the oven; top with the bacon.
Serve immediately with corn chips, bagel chips or crackers.
