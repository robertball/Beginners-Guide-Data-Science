Title:
Fried Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon cornstarch
2 1/4 cups self-rising flour
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
1 egg
2  cups water
1 quart vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Combine flour, 2 tablespoons oil, 2 tablespoons cornstarch, salt, white pepper, and egg.  Add 2 cups water gradually to make a thick batter.  Stir to blend thoroughly.  Add chicken pieces, and stir until chicken is well coated.
Heat oil in skillet to 4 or wok to 360 degrees F (180 degrees C).  Fry chicken pieces in hot oil until golden.  Remove chicken, and drain on paper towels.
Serve with fried rice and chow mein noodles.
