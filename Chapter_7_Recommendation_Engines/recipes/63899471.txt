Title:
Cranberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup almonds, slivered and toasted (see below for toasting)
1 pound spinach lettuce
4 tbsp red onion, sliced
3 tbsp good blue cheese crumbles
2 apples, chopped into bite size pieces
1 cup dried cranberries
2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds (to toast: 350 degrees ungreased pan for 5-8 min, watching and stirring every couple min)
1 tbsp poppy seeds
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup good olive oil (can substitute with veg or canola oil)

Directions:
In 2 separate oven safe  pans toast almonds and sesame seeds in 350 degree preheated oven for about 5-8 min, stirring and checking every 2 min until lightly toasted. Remove from heat, and let cool.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the sesame seeds, poppy seeds, sugar, paprika, white wine vinegar, cider vinegar, and olive oil. (Toss with spinach just before serving)
In a large bowl, combine the spinach, apples, onions and blue cheese with the toasted almonds and cranberries.

Description: 
Spinach, toasted almonds, and dried cranberries are tossed with a sweet and tangy, homemade dressing creating a crowd-pleasing spinach salad.Modified from: Jamie's Cranberry Spinach Salad
