Title:
Super Beef Strogonoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C minced onion
1/2 C butter
1/2 lb fresh mushrooms
1 lb round steakm cut in to 1/2 in strips
2 T flour
salt and pepper
1/8 t garlic powder
1/2 t dry mustard
1/2 C consomme
3 T sherry
1/2 C dry white wine
1/2 C sour cream

Directions:
Saute mushroom and onions in butter. Remove. Brown meat in same sauce, then add flour, seasonings, comsomme, and white wine. Just before serving, stir in sour cream and sherry. Heat but do not boil. Serve over egg noodles.
