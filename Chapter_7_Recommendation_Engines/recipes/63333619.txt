Title:
Mound Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups crushed graham crackers (chocolate or regular)
1/2 cup margarine or butter
2 cups flaked coconut
1 can Eagle Brand Condensed Milk (low fat is OK)
1 package (11.5 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix cracker crumbs and margarine or butter.  Press in 9 x 13 pan.
Mix coconut and Condensed Milk.  Carefully spread on top of crumb mixture.
Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
Empty bag of chocolate chips over baked mixture.  Let melt and carefully spread with spatula.
Cool, cut into bars and refrigerate.
