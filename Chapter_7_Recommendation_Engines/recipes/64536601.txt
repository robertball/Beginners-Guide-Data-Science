Title:
Shorecook's Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3110351.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ribs celery
1 small onion
1/2 lb.  left-over beef
2 medium potatoes
1 16 oz. pkg. frozen peas and carrots
6 cups water
2 Tablespoons roasted beef base (such as Better Than Bouillon)
1/2 teaspoon parsley flakes
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon basil

Directions:
Dice celery, onions, and potatoes and add to a large pot.
Mix 6 cups of water with beef base and add to the pot.
Bring  to a boil, then add beef,  frozen peas and carrots and reduce heat.
Add parsley flakes, oregano, and basil.
Simmer for 40 minutes, covered, or until vegetables are tender.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
Whenever I cook a roast, I use whatever beef is left over to make this soup.  Any left-over vegetables may be used as well.  It's a family favorite!
