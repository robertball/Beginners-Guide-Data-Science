Title:
Stuffed Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4eggs
1/4 milk
1/2 salt and black pepper
1/4c. prepared stuffing
2T. canned mushrooms
1/4c. monzerella cheese
1t. butter

Directions:
Heat a 10in. skilet.
Mix together the eggs, milk, salt and pepper.
Pour into the buttered skillet. When eggs start to cook, top 1/2 of it with stuffing, sprinkle with cheese and mushrooms. When cooked on one side, flip 1/2 of it on top of the other side. Cook all the way through.
Cooks note: I used Stove Top's Sourdough and Wild Rice stuffing mix.

Submitter: 
The Del Sisters

Hearty and savory. A great exchange from your usual omelet
