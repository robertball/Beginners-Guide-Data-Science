Title:
Yeast Camping Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup     Chopped dried fruit
1/4 Cup     Bob's Red Mill 10 Grain Cereal
1/4 Cup     Bob's Red Mill Scotch Oats
1 1/2 Cup   Boiling Water
1/4 Cup     Lukewarm Water
1/2 Tblsp   Yeast
1/4 Cup     Light Brown Sugar
3/4 Cup     Unbleached Flour
1 1/2 Cup   Wheat Flour
1/2 Tblsp   Salt
1/4 Cup     Oil

Directions:
Put fruit and cereals into a large mixing bowl, and pour boiling water over it.
When cooled to lukewarm, add the additional water and yeast.
Set aside for 5 minutes.
Stir in sugar, beat in unbleached flour and 1/2 cup whole wheat flour.
Beat vigorously 1 minute.
Cover and set sponge aside in a warm, non-drafty spot for 30 minutes.
Beat in salt and oil into the sponge.
Add remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time.
Knead gently at first, minimizing stickiness for 10 minutes.
Put in lightly oiled bowl.
Turn dough over so that both sides are covered in oil.
Cover and set sponge aside in a warm, non-drafty spot until doubled.
Dust baking pan with cornmeal.
When dough has doubled, punch down and knead breifly and form loaf into a round, oval shape (like a football).
Cover and set sponge aside in a warm, non-drafty spot until doubled.
Once almost doubled, preheat to 375 F Degrees.
Bake for 45 minutes.
Let cool for 30 minutes before slicing.
Enjoy!!!

Description: 
This is the first yeast loaf we tried over a fire. It was very enjoyable. Hearty free-form loaf.
