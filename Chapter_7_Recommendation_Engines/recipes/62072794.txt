Title:
vienna sausage wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans vienna sausages
1 pkg pillsbury crescent rolls

Directions:
Pre-heat oven per crescent roll instructions.
Unroll crescent rolls and cut triangles in 1/2.  Roll each triangle around a single vienna sausage.
Bake approx 10 - 15 minutes until golden brown.
