Title:
Spinach Strawberry Agua Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5388188.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups water, divided
1 (16 ounce) package fresh strawberries, hulled
2 cups fresh spinach, with stems
2 tablespoons sugar, or to taste

Directions:
Combine 2 cups water, strawberries, spinach, and sugar in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a pitcher; stir in remaining 4 cups water.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
This agua fresca (Mexican fruit water) is not only refreshing but also very healthy. Serve cold and over ice.
