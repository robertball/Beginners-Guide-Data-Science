Title:
Warm Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
1 pkg (8oz) Cream Cheese, at room temperature
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded swiss cheese
1 can artichokes, drained and chopped
1 pkg frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1/4 cup diced onion
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until lightly browned on top. Serve with crackers, tortilla chips, or bread.
