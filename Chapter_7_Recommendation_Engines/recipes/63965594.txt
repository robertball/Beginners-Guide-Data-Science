Title:
Homemade Strawberry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups hulled strawberries
4 cups lemon juice
6 cups granulated sugar

Directions:
1.	Prepare canner, jars and lids.
2.	In a blender or food precessor, puree strawberries until smooth.  Transfer to a large saucepan and add lemon juice and sugar.  Stir to combine.  Heat to 190 degrees over medium high heat, stirring occasionally.  Do not boil.  Remove from heat and skim off foam.
3.	Ladle hot concentrate into hot jars, leaving 1/4 inch headspace.  Wipe rim.  Put on lids.
4.	Place jars in canner, cover with water.  Bring to a boil and process for 15 minutes.  Remove canner lid.  Wait 5 miinutes, then remove jars, cool and store.
5.	To Reconstitute:  mix one part concentrate with one part water, tonic water or ginger ale.  Adjust concentrate to taste.
