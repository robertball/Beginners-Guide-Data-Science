Title:
OSSO BUCCO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 veal or lamb shanks
4 Tbl flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
8 Tbl olive oil
onion, celery blend
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 jalepeno, seeded & chopped
8 oz beef broth
8 oz white wine (or cooking wine)
2 (14 oz) canned tomatoes, undrained
1 tsp basil

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
Coat shanks in seasoned flour. Saute in heavy pan until browned on all sides. Set shanks aside. Saute onion/celery mix, garlic & jalepeno until a little soft. Place shanks on top of veggies. Place them on end to keep marrow from running out. Add broth, wine, tomatoes,& basil. Cover & place in oven for about 3 hrs until tender.
