Title:
Alligator Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Alligator - ground
1 15oz can of Black Beans
1 15oz can of Kidney Beans
1 35oz can of Crushed Tomatoes
1 32oz bottle of V8
1 Vidalia Onion - diced
1 Green Pepper - diced
1 TB Olive Oil
1 TB Chili Powder - heaping
1 TB Chipotle in Adobo
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp Cinnamon
1 tsp Celery Salt
1/2 tsp Ginger
Salt & Pepper to taste
Hot Sauce

Directions:
Grind or chop the alligator in a food processor. Dice the Onion & Green Pepper. Saute the Onion & Pepper in a large pot with the olive oil until onion is translucent. Add alligator and brown slightly. Combine crushed tomatoes, V8, black beans, kidney beans & spices and let simmer slightly covered for 2 to 3 hours. May add a little water from time to time as you see fit. Serve with a dash or two of hot sauce if you so desire.
