Title:
Decadent Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Package Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
4 T water
4 large eggs - separated
1 tsp Vanilla
2 T Kahlua Liquor
1 C Heavy Cream

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips and water in a double boiler over low heat. (I use a bowl sitting over a pot of simmering water)Remove from heat and whisk in egg yolks one at a time. Add vanilla and Kahlua and set aside.
In another bowl, beat egg whites till stiff, fold into chocolate mixture. Beat heavy cream and fold into chocolate mixture, Transfer to serving bowl or several small individual serving ramekins and chill for several hours before serving.

Submitter: 
MARYANNEQ
Description: 
This is a wonderfully rich and decadent mousse that has quickly become a holiday tradition in our family.
