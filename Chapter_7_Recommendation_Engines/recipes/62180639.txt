Title:
Island Style Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz. Linguine
1 lb. ripe tomatoes peeled,seeded &chopped;
(or 3 cans Hunts Italian Stle w/juice)
1 T. Anchovy paste
12 Pitted black Olives, chopped or
(1 heaping T.minced from a can)
1 Lg. clove garlic, minced or
(1 T. minced from the jar)
1 T. capers with 1 tsp.juice
Pinch of sugar
1 tsp. Oregano
1 tsp. Parsley
2 T fresh basil if possible or equivalent dried
1/2 c. Extra Virgin Olive oil
Salt and fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Slowly cook garlic in oil until golden. Add anchovy paste and capers with juice, stirring gently. Add tomatoes, sugar, chopped olives and oregano.  Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes.  Remove from heat and stir in the parsley and basil.  Pour over pasta that has been cooked "al dente".
