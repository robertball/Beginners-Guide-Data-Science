Title:
Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750113.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Italian sausage
2 14 oz cans cut tomatoes
1 12 oz can tomato paste
3/4 cup tomato sauce
14 oz water
1 tsp dry chopped garlic
1 TBS sugar
1 TBS basil
1 TBS oregano
1 bay leaf
2 tsp salt
2 tsp pepper
2 TBS dry chopped onion
1/2 green pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients
Simmer sauce for at least two (2) hours or all day
