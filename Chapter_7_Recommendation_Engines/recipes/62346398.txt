Title:
Rosemary Drop Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563275.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup baking mix
1/4 cup milk plus a little more if needed
1/2 tsp dried rosemary
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a medium bowl mix baking mix and milk with a fork.  Mix until a stiff wet dough forms.  If mix is dry add a tsp of milk at a time until proper consistency.
Add remaining ingrediants and mix well.
Drop dough into rounded mounds on a baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until tops are golden.
