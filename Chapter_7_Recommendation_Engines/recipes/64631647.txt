Title:
The Best Potato Soup! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4479054.jpg
Ingredients:
5 pound bag of potatoes, peeled and cubed
4 pieces of bacon, chopped
6 cups chicken broth
3 tablespoons minced onion
4 Tablespoons butter
1/3 cup sour cream
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups whole milk
1 green onion chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Fry bacon in a large pot then remove the bacon and set aside.
Drain most of the bacon grease from the pot.
Add potatoes and onions to the pot and cook for about 3 minutes.
Pour chicken broth over them and let boil until softened.
Gently mash the potatoes with a potato masher or an electric hand mixer.
Add butter, sour cream, cheddar cheese, salt and pepper, and blend to the consistency you like best.
Add milk to the soup to thicken it.
Add more salt and pepper to taste.
Serve topped with cheese, bacon and chives.

Description: 
Delicious and simple potato soup that makes the perfect comfort food dinner!
