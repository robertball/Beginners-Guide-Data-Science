Title:
Grandma's Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups breadcrumbs
1/4 cup oil
1/2 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon paprika
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 lb chicken
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Mix breadcrumbs, oil, and seasonings together in a large bowl.
Mix milk and water together in a separate bowl.
Dip chicken in milk mixture, then into coating mixture.
Bake chicken at 350 degrees for 45 mins.
