Title:
Eggplant and Tofu Vegetarian Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078449.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant (sliced in 1/2 inch slices)
1 zucchini (sliced in 1/4 inch slices)
1 onion (minced)
2 garlic bulbs (minced)
1 or 2 tomatoes (sliced)
100 g of fine herbs Tofu (sliced in 1/2 inch slices)
Shredded cheese
Italian spices
Olive oil

Directions:
Sprinkle salt on your eggplant slices. Leave it on for about half an hour, then rince the salt off and dry your eggplant slices with a paper towel.
Layer your your eggplant slices on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper, drizzle with olive oil and grill until they turn golden. Set them aside.
Repeat step 2 with your zucchini slices.
Repeat step 2 with your tofu slices.
In a frying pan, cook your minced onion and minced garlic bulbs in olive oil for about 2 minutes. Make sure they don't burn. Add your tomato slices and sprinkle with italian spices (to your liking).
Assemble your lasagna: half of your eggplants slices, then all the zucchini slices. The third layer will be your tofu. Add shredded cheese on top leaving some to sprinkle on top of your assembled lasagna. Add you tomato mix with the garlic and onion. Your last layer will be the rest of your eggplant slices.
Sprinkle with more shredded cheese. Leave it in the oven for 25 minutes at 425 F. Broil for the last 3-4 minutes.
