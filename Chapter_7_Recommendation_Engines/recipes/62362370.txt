Title:
Rhubarb Custard Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 c. flour
1/4 c. sugar
1 c. cold margarine
Filling:
2 c. sugar
7 Tbsp. flour
1 c. whipping cream
3 eggs, beaten
5 c. rhubarb pieces
Topping:
2-3oz blocks cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 c. whipping cream, whipped

Directions:
Combine crust ingredients and press into a greased 9 x 13 pan. Bake at 350 for 10 minutes. Cool.
For filling: Combine sugar and flour. Stir in whipping cream and eggs then stir in rhubarb. Pour over crust and bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes or until custard is set. Cool.
For topping: Blend cream cheese, sugar & vanilla. Fold in whipped cream. Spread over custard and refrigerate overnight.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great recipe to use up the abundance of rhubarb in your garden.  My grandmother gave me this recipe and has been making it for years.
