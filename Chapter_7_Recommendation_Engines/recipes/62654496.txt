Title:
APPLE CAKE COBBLER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans apple pie filling
1 butter recipe cake mix
1 stick marg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Pam the bottom of 9x13 pan
spread apples over bottom
Sprinkle cake mix over it
pour melted butter on top
Bake 35-40min. Serve warm
