Title:
Mel's Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332589.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups cabbage, finely shredded and diced
1/2 cup carrots, finely shredded
2 green onions, minced
1/3 cup & 2 tsp white granulated sugar
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tsp celery seed
1/4 cup milk
1/4 buttermilk
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 tbsp white vinegar
2 1/2 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Mix together cabbage, carrots, green onions in a large bowl. Set aside.
Mix all remaining ingredients together in a medium size bowl. Pour over cabbage mixture. Mix well.
Cover and chill for 2-4 hours. Occassionally stir to make sure everything is well coated.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
This is my verison of a family classic. Crunchy goodness with a nice zing.
