Title:
Shannon's Breakfast Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups all purpose flour
1 Cup of sugar
1 Egg (I use egg replacer due to my son's allergy)
1/3 Cup of oil
1/2 Teaspoon salt
2 Teaspoon ground Cinnamon
1 cup water
1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1/2 Teaspoon Nutmeg

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. In a large bowl, Mix all ingredients together until a consistant color appears.
3.  Pour all ingredients into a well greased 9x5 loaf pan
4. Bake for 50-60 minutes.  Top crust should be brown and crispy.
5.  Let cool before slicing, serve with margarine

Submitter: 
Shannon Silva
Description: 
I made this recipe for my 3 year old son who is allergic to eggs.  I use an egg replacer product every time. I have never made this using real eggs, so please let me know how it turns out.
