Title:
Simple Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844321.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup apple juice
1 tablespoon honey, or to taste
2 ripe bananas
1 teaspoon milk, or to taste
4 ice cubes

Directions:
Blend yogurt, apple juice, honey, bananas, milk and ice cubes in a blender until smooth.
