Title:
Asian Lettuce Wraps / Marinade / Stir Fry sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the marinade / sauce:
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2" ginger, minced
1/4 cup Olive Oil
1/4 cup Ketchup
2 Tbls. red wine vinegar or sherry
2 tsp. chile garlic paste
2 Tbls. dark soy sauce
-
Meat of Choice.
Shrimp / Pork / chicken
-
For the stir fried Vegetables:
2 cloves garlic, minced with
1/2" fresh ginger
Cabbage
diced carrots
onions
red and yellow peppers
pinch of sugar
Raw Veggies for wraps
Jicama - Julianne d
carrots - Julianne d
Cilantro leaves
green onions - sliced
peanuts (optional)
lettuce leaves for wraps

Directions:
Mix all marinade ingredients together. You can use a food processor.  Store in the refrigerator.
Dice or slice meat - add enough sauce to coat, a couple of tablespoons should do. Place in storage bag and refrigerate.
Heat 2 Tablespoons oil in wok.
Stir fry meat - remove and set aside.
Stir fry carrots for a couple of minutes then add garlic and ginger paste, onions, peppers, toss add cabbage last - add 1-2 Tablespoons sauce.  Stir to combine.  Remove from wok to a serving dish.
Add the meat back to the hot wok and re-heat / sear.
Serve with the raw julienne d vegetables in lettuce cups with plum sauce and peanut sauce.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This is an all purpose marinade and stir fry sauce.  A low carb hit with the family.  Get the napkins ready - these are messy but finger licking delicious.
