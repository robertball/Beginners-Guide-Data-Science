Title:
Mochi - Microwave Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250 ml glutinous rice flour
210 ml cold water

Directions:
In a microwaveable bowl, combine water and flour until smooth.
Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave at 600 W for 50 seconds. Do not allow the steam to escape.
Remove the film, mix well. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap again and  microwave at 600 W for 50 seconds.
Repeat this step once more for a total of three, 50-second cycles in the microwave.
The dough should be slightly translucent and elastic.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Adaptation of mochi for the microwave. No sugar added.
