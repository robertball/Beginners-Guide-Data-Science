Title:
Loaded Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/813645.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices of bacon
1 cup of chopped onion
4 cups of frozen diced potatoes (1/2 of a 2# bag)
4 oz. cream cheese (room temperature)
3 C. milk
2 Tbsp. butter
4 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
1-1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
In a dutch oven cook bacon until crisp.  Remove bacon and set aside.
Meanwhile, whisk cream cheese until smooth slowly add milk, whisking until smooth.
Brown onions in bacon fat over medium heat.
Add diced potatoes and butter, cover until tender.
Pour milk mixture over potatoes and heat through.
Add salt, pepper, and cheese.  Stir until melted.
Serve in bowl, top with bacon, green onion, more cheese.

Submitter: 
Carol Castellucci Miller
Description: 
Creamy rich goodness with the added flavors of caramelized onions and bacon.  A family favorite.
