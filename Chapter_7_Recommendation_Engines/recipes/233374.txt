Title:
Frozen Berry and Spinach Ice Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3448290.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 cup frozen blueberries
1 cup frozen cherries
1/2 cup Greek yogurt
1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
3 tablespoons honey

Directions:
Blend milk, blueberries, cherries, yogurt, spinach, and honey in a blender until smooth; pour into ice pop molds. Freeze until solid, at least 2 hours.
