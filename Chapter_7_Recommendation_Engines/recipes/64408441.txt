Title:
Tigerman Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2270091.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package puff pastry
2 T. Olive oil
1 large yellow onion, finely diced
2 lbs. flat iron steak (original recipe called for blade steak)
1/2 tsp. Tahini
1/2 tsp. tomato paste
1/2 tsp. soy sauce
3/4 c. beef stock
3/4 c. tomato sauce
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1 T. Tabasco sauce
1 T. BBQ sauce
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. ground sage
1/4 tsp. ground coriander
1/4 tsp. parsley
2 T. corn flour
1 egg, beaten
Frozen peas
Sugar
Heavy cream
Mashed potatoes
Prepared gravy

Directions:
Thaw puff pastry according to box directions.
Fry up onion until soft; then add steak cubes. Fry until steak is cooked through.
In a small bowl, combine tahini, soy sauce and tomato paste, put into cooked meat mixture.  Add the stock, corn flour, sauces and spices.  Turn heat down and simmer, reduce until thickened.
Layer pie dish with puff pastry.  Pour in thick pie filling, top with puff pastry.  Brush the top of the pie with beaten egg.  Bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.
While baking, prepare peas and mix with heavy cream and sugar. Smash with potato masher or immersion blender.  Prepare potatoes and gravy.
When the pie comes out of the oven, let stand for 5 minutes.  Cut pie pieces and serve with a layered scoop of potatoes, peas and top with gravy.

Description: 
Originally called Harry's Tiger Pie from Australia, we've added a few of our own ingredients.  Tigerman Austen T. Hagood thinks its the best meat pie he's ever had and has adopted this recipe and renamed it Tigerman Pie.
