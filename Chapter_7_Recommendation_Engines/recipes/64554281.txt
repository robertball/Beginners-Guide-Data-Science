Title:
Glazed Strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3348882.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Lbs fresh ripe strawberries
1 cup sugar
1 tbsp vanilla
3 tbsp corn starch
1 cup cold water

Directions:
Slice strawberries on a mandolin into thin slices
Place strawberries, sugar, vanilla into a medium sauce pan
Heat over medium heat for 1-2 minutes until sugar is dissolving into strawberry juice
Place corn starch in a cup or small bowl
Pour cold water over the corn starch slowly
Mix corn starch and cold water with a fork until completely dissolved
Power water mixture into pan slowly while stirring strawberries constantly
Heat over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes until sauce is thickened to your liking
Remove from heat, and chill until ready to serve

Submitter: 
Andrew Taylor
Description: 
Sweet glazed strawberries perfect for topping you desserts, pancakes, or just eating by itself.
