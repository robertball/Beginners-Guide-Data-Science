Title:
Caramel Cinna Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/751541.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Kraft Traditional Caramels, melted
2/3 Cup Half-and-Half
1 1/2 Cup Confectioners Sugar
2 Teaspoons 1% Milk
1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1/8 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon

Directions:
Place Caramels and Half-and-Half in saucepan. Cook on low heat until Caramels are completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring frequently.
Stir in Milk, Butter, Vanilla, Salt, Cinnamon. Blend well.
Stir In Confectioners Sugar, slowly until well blended.
Put In bowl, etc. and glaze your cake! Make sure cake is completely cool before glazing. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
wilson.megan21
Description: 
This warm sweet glaze will be delicious on any fruit cake or bread! Put leftovers in the fridge and warm it up later!
