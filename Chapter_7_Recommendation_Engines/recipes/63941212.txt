Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Roll Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	1 1/2 pounds ground beef
•	1 pound ground Italian sausage
•	1 tablespoon oil
•	1 large onion, chopped
•	1 clove garlic, minced
•	1 small head cabbage, chopped (small chopped pieces)
•	2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
•	1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
•	1/2 cup water
•	1 teaspoon black pepper
•	1 teaspoon sea salt
cooked rice, optional

Directions:
In large skillet heat oil over medium heat. Add both meats stirring until meat is breaking up; drain if necessary.
Add the chopped onion, cook until onion is tender; add garlic cook one minute longer.
Transfer meat mixture to a soup pot; add cabbage, tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, pepper and salt. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce to simmer. Simmer with lid on for 20-30 minutes or until cabbage is tender.
Serve in  a bowl; top with a spoon of cooked rice if desired                                           (but it's not necessary to use the rice).

Description: 
This is an easy fix to satisfy a craving for stuffed cabbage .
