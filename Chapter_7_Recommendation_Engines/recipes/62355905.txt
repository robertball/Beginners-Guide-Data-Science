Title:
Salmon with Brown Sugar Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/401649.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
2 tablespoons soy sauce
4 (6 ounce) boneless salmon fillets
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk together the brown sugar, soy sauce and Dijon mustard in a small bowl; spoon mixture evenly onto top of salmon fillets.
Cook in preheated pan drizzled with olive oil on medium heat, turning halfway until done. About 10 - 15 minutes.
