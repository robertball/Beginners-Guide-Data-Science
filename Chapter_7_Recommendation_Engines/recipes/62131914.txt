Title:
Chocolate Molten Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups unsalted butter (room temperature)
12 ounces semisweet chocolate-coarsely chopped
8 Large Egg Yolks, plus 7 large eggs
1 1/2 cups Confectioners Sugar
3/4 cup all purpose flour
For Ramekins:
Butter
Granulated Sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Butter 6 ramekins ( 6 ounce size)
Sprinkle the butter ramekins with granulated sugar and tap out excess sugar
In a double boiler set over barely simmering water, melt the butter and chocolate and then remove from heat.
Beat eggs in a mixer with the whisk on low speed for about 2 minutes
Continue to mix and add the chocolate mixture to the eggs, mix for another two minutes
Add the flour and confectioners sugar to the mix and beat for 30 seconds or until well mixed on Medium speed
Pour the batter in the ramekins leaving about a 1/4 of space from the top.
Bake on a cookie sheet for 18 to 20 minutes.
Remove from oven and turn upside down onto a plate immediately.
Centers will be soft.
We like to serve with fresh whipped cream and berries.

Description: 
I am madly in love with these chocolate cakes with the warm liquid center.  I was always trying to duplicate a box mix until a friend gave me this recipe and now these are the only ones I make.
