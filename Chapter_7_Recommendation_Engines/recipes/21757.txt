Title:
Fluff Dip for Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/82840.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (3 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
1 cup milk
1/2 cup amaretto liqueur
1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the vanilla pudding mix, milk and amaretto liqueur. Beat in the thawed frozen whipped topping. Chill 1 hour in the refrigerator before serving.

Submitter: 
CMJ721
Description: 
A lovely vanilla flavored dip for fresh fruit.  A friend shared this recipe with me.   Especially good with strawberries, pineapple and bananas.
