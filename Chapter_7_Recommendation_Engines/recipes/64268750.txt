Title:
Potatoes Romanoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes
2 c. sour cream
1 bunch green onions
1 1/2 c. grated cheddar cheese
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
Paprika

Directions:
Cook potatoes in jackets until fork-tender.  Peel and shred into a large bowl.  Stir in sour cream, onion, 1 cup cheese, salt and pepper.  Turn mixture into buttered casserole. Top with remaining cheese; sprinkle paprika.  Cover; refrigerate for several hours or bake immediately.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.
