Title:
No-Bake Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. confectioners sugar
1 c. peanut butter
1 c. chocolate chips
1/2 c. eagle brand milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Drop by spoonful onto waxed paper.
Let set until no longer tacky.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
A great way to satisfy a sweet-tooth craving without heating up the house.
