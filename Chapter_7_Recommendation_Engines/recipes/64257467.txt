Title:
Creamy Holiday Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1/3 cup honey, (or sugar)
5 cups milk
1 tablespoon rum extract
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup whipping cream, whipped
nutmeg

Directions:
In large bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form.  Add yolks and honey; beat well.  Stir in mil, rum extract and vanilla.  Chill.  Just before serving, fold in whipped cream and pour into punch bowl.  Sprinkle with nutmeg.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
The kids ask me to make this every year.  I don't have the heart to tell them how easy it is to make.
