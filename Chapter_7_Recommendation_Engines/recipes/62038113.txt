Title:
Old Settlers Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. chopped bacon
1/2 lb. ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup barbecue sauce
1 Tablespoon mustard
1 Tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (16 ounce) can pork and beans
1 (16 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (16 ounce) can butter beans, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brown bacon and ground beef. Remove meat and  cook onion in drippings. Mix together sugars, ketchup, barbecue sauce and spices.  Add to drained meat.  Add cooked onion and beans.  Bake in casserole dish, uncovered, for 1 hour.

Description: 
This is one of my family's favorites! It combines three kinds of beans with bacon, ground beef and spices. It is always a big hit at picnics and BBQ's!
