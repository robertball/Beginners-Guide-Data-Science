Title:
Sausage, Potato and Kale Soup (lowfat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1094953.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch kale, clean and torn into bite-sized pieces
6 red potatoes, cubed
1 onion, chopped
14 oz Butterball turkey smoked sausage, sliced
pinch of red pepper flakes
1 cup low sodium chicken broth
3 cups soy milk
dried oregano
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil cubed potatoes until just tender; drain. In a soup pot, brown the sausage and drain if needed.  Add in the chicken broth, soy milk, red pepper flakes, black pepper, oregano, and simmer for 25 minutes. Add cooked potatoes, then the kale pieces and simmer for another 10 minutes.  Serve with crusty bread.

Submitter: 
lamorris
Description: 
This is what I came up with in my quest to make a potato kale soup that was creamy but lower in fat, and no dairy.
