Title:
Manchego Cheese Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 ½ cups brown rice
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon coriander
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes or to taste
3 cups chicken stock
1 can drained and rinsed black or pinto beans
Handful flat leaf parsley, finely chopped
1 cup grated Manchego cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat EVOO over medium heat in a medium sauce pan. Add rice, cumin, coriander and red pepper flakes and toast in EVOO 2 minutes.  Add stock and bring to a boil. Cook rice 20 – 25 minutes until tender and stock has been absorbed. Remove from heat, fluff rice with fork, stir in beans and parsley and cheese. Season rice with salt and pepper and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Chris Galbraith
Description: 
Brown rice cooked in stock with seasonings and Manchego cheese.
