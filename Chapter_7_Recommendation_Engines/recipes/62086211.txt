Title:
Super-Yummy Artichoke Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounce package cream cheese, softened
1 (14 ounce) cans artichoke hearts, drained & roughly chopped
1 (4 ounce) can crab meat, drained
8 ounces (1/2 Jar) Alfredo-style pasta sauce
1  7.25oz jar roasted red peppers, drained & roughly chopped
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
5 Green Onions, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
¼ tsp fresh ground black pepper
Assorted Crackers, sliced baguette, bread sticks, pita chips for dipping

Directions:
Beat cream cheese until light and fluffy, scraping down sides of bowl occasionally.
Add Alfredo Sauce, green onions, garlic and black pepper; beat thoroughly.
By hand, stir in cheeses, crab, artichokes and roasted red peppers
Transfer to a greased baking dish (9 x 9, or 2 qt round works well)
Cover and refrigerate until ready to bake.
When ready to bake, preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Bake 40-45 minutes, or until bubbly.
Serve with crackers, baguette, breadsticks or pita chips

Submitter: 
SAVVYHOSTESS
Description: 
I created this recipe, as an alternative to the mayo based artichoke crab dips.  It seems less greasy because of the modifications I have made.  Note, I have successfully used light alfredo sauce and it has turned out just fine!
