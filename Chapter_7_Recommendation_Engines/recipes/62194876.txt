Title:
Bacon Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup bacon ends, chopped (or 6 slices thick cut)
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper flakes (optional)
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 cabbage head, cut into thin wedges
1 1/2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
salt and pepper

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until well browned.
Add onion and garlic and pepper flakes; cook until tender.
Add cabbage and sprinkle with salt and pepper; cover and reduce to simmer.
After 10 minutes, drizzle with blasamic and turn, coating cabbage with drippings as evenly as possible.
Cook another 10-15 minutes, until cabbage is soft, turning occasionally.
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Krystal
Description: 
Cabbage, onions and garlic are sauteed in bacon drippings and balsamic for an amazingly delicious side dish.Modified from: Fried Cabbage II
