Title:
Simple Baked Apples (or pears) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 apples (and/or pears)- cored and sliced
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp vanilla
a few dots of butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart casserole dish, or coat with non-stick cooking spray.
Place apples in a large bowl. In a small bowl, mix together sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves. Stir spice mixture into apples until evenly distributed (Or toss together in a bag). Fold in  walnuts. Spoon into prepared dish. Pour milk mixed with vanilla evenly over apple mixture. Dot with butter.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 60 minutes, or until soft and bubbly. Allow to cool slightly before serving.
