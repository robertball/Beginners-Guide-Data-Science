Title:
Delicious Turkey Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1x - large flour tortilla
2 tbsp. - Basil ranch salad dressing
6 thin slices - honey roasted turkey breast
2 thin slices - colby cheese
1 thin slice - provolone cheese
4 strips - green bell pepper
2 tbsp. - sun dried tomatoes
1 - large romaine leaf

Directions:
Spread dressing on tortilla.  Layer turkey, cheese, pepper and tomato on top.  Toast under oven broiler for 3-5 minutes until cheese is melted, but underside of tortilla is still soft.  Remove from oven and add romaine.  Fold tortilla burrito-style and enjoy.

Description: 
A wonderful wrap made from ingredients I had on hand.  The end result was delicious!
