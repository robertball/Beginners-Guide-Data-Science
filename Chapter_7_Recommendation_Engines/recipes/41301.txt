Title:
Sesame Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983405.jpg
Ingredients:
6 fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
1 teaspoon butter or margarine
1 teaspoon lemon juice
3/4 teaspoon sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
Place asparagus in a skillet; sprinkle with salt if desired. Add 1/2 in. of water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes.
Meanwhile, melt butter; add lemon juice and sesame seeds. Drain asparagus; drizzle with the butter mixture.
