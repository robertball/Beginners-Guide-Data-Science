Title:
Superb Strawberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/416590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint strawberries, hulled and chopped
3 Tablespoons granulated sugar
1 Tablespoon cornstarch
1/4 cup water

Directions:
1. In saucepan, over medium-high heat bring half of strawberries, sugar, cornstarch and water to a boil.   Cook for two minutes or until thick; stirring frequently.
2. Remove from heat; carefully pour sauce into blender and puree.  Add more sugar if needed, one teaspoon at a time.
3. Stir in remaining half pint of strawberries, pour into air-tight container and store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Jaay
Description: 
This sauce has just the right amount of sweetness and is a great way to use fresh strawberries in summer.  A perfect topping for cheesecake, ice cream or pound cake.
