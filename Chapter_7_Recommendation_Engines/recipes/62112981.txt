Title:
Deep Dark Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups flour
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup Hershey's cocoa powder
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup veg. oil
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup boiling water

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients. Add remaining ingredients, except boiling water, and beat until smooth. Stir in boiling water. Batter will be very thin.
Pour into two 9" or three 8" layer pans, or one 13x9" sheet pan. Make 350 for 30-35 minutes for layers, 35-40 minutes for sheet. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans.
