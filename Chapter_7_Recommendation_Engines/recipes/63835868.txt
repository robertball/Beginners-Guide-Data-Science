Title:
Sirloin Tips and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065971.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin
1 (16 ounce) can mushrooms, with liquid
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
salt and pepper to taste
3/4 cup red wine

Directions:
Cut beef into cubes. In a large skillet over medium/high heat, heat the olive oil and brown beef cubes with the garlic.
Add mushrooms with liquid, tomato sauce, salt, pepper and red wine.
Cook for 30 minutes or until beef cubes are tender.
Add a little more wine while cooking if desired.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Beef sirloin cubes browned with garlic then simmered in tomato sauce and red wine until tender. Great over buttered noodles.Modified from: Sirloin Tips and Mushrooms
