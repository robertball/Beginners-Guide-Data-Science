Title:
Holiday Butterscotch Chocolate Chewies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351959.jpg
Ingredients:
1 9.5oz package yellow cake mix
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons softened butter
2 tablespoons honey
2 eggs
1 cup chocolate morsels
1 cut chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large bowl just until blended.
Spread into well greased 9x13 baking dish
Bake 350F 25-30 minutes.
Let cool for 4 hours
Cut into 1.5 inch squares
