Title:
Slow Cooker Moroccan-Style Chicken and Potato Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986725.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 teaspoons finely chopped garlic
1 1/2 pounds chicken drumsticks
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Diced Potatoes with Onion
1 1/2 cups baby-cut carrots
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground red pepper
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
1 (14 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, undrained
1/2 cup raisins
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
In 10-inch skillet heat oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken and garlic.  Cook, turning chicken occasionally, until browned.  Drain grease.
In 3 1/2 to 4 quart slow cooker add Simply Potatoes(R) and carrots.  Top with browned chicken and garlic.  In small bowl stir cumin, ginger, ground red pepper and crushed red pepper until well blended.  Sprinkle over chicken and Simply Potatoes(R).  Add chicken broth.  Cover; cook on LOW heat for 6 to 7 hours.
Stir in tomatoes and raisins.  Increase heat to HIGH.  Cover; cook for 15 minutes.  Stir in lemon juice just before serving.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
Slow cooking in your Crock Pot® blends exotic spices into a rich, flavorful broth. Cumin, ginger, garlic and more enliven this delicious homemade stew.
