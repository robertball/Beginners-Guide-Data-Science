Title:
Scrumptious Stuffed Brownie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1606417.jpg
Ingredients:
1 brownie recipe (your favorite)
1 bag of mini rolo's, reeces pieces, babe ruth, snickers, mars bar, turtles etc. (any thing gooey). make sure to cut your chocolate of choice into bite size pieces, if not already that size (mini rolo's)
1 recipe of your favourite frosting

Directions:
heat oven according to your brownie recipe's directions. spray your mini muffin tin with cooking spray. fill your muffin tin 2/3's full with brownie batter. bake approx 12-15 mins. as soon as you remove them from the oven, push a piece of chocolate into the center of the brownie. once cooled, remove brownies from the tin and frost with your favourite frosting.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
these little bites are always a hit. great for a crowd. very versatile in that you can use all of your favourite combinations of brownie, candy and frosting!
