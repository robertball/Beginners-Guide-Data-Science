Title:
Wine Tender Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2- 2 lb. chuck roast
1 cup thinly sliced onion
1/2 cup chopped apple, peeled
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1 cup red wine
salt and pepper

Directions:
Put roast in slow cooker.  Layer onions, apples and garlic on top of roast.
Carefully pour wine over roast without disturbing it's toppings.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Cover.  Cook on low for 8-10 hours, or until meat is tender but not dry.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description:
