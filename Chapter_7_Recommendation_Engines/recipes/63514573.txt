Title:
Cheater Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. package of spaghetti
1 1/4 lbs. ground turkey (or beef)
1 cup onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
40 oz marinara sauce
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped fine
freshly grated Parmesan, to taste
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Over medium-high heat, add ground turkey to a large pan with a pinch of salt and pepper.  Cook ground turkey until browned.  Remove from pan and reserve the fat.
Over medium heat, saute the onion in reserved fat.  When onion is almost translucent, add the garlic and stir for 30 seconds.
Add marinara sauce and ground turkey and increase heat to medium-high.  When boiling, reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.
While sauce is simmering, cook spaghetti according to package directions.
Add parsley to sauce and simmer for a few more minutes.
Check the sauce for salt and pepper.
Serve over spaghetti and top with freshly grated Parmesan.

Submitter:

Description: 
This was a fast and easy sauce that my family really enjoyed!  I purchased a higher-end marinara sauce for this recipe and believe that made all the difference.
