Title:
Easy Chicken Dumpling Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (10 3/4 oz each) condensed cream of chicken soup
3 1/3 c. milk, divided
1 2/3 c. biscuit baking mix

Directions:
In saucepan combine soup and 2 2/3 c. of milk.  Bring to a boil over medium heat; reduce heat.  In a bowl combine baking mix and remaining milk until blended.  Drop by spoonfuls into simmering soup.  Cook uncovered for 10 minutes.  Cover and simmer 10-12 min until dumplings test done.

Submitter: 
Traci Caha Myers
Description: 
Easy, fast, and a great cheat for the dumplings!
