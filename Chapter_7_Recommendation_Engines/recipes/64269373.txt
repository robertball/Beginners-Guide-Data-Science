Title:
Rumaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (5 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained (estimated can size)
1 lb lean bacon, cut in half (for easier wrapping)
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/8 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Wrap water chestnuts with 1/2 bacon slice and secure with toothpick
cook brown sugar, ketchup and soy sauce
Pour over water chestnuts
Bake @ 350 fo 30 minutes

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
This is easy and delicious and will disappear as fast as you make them!
