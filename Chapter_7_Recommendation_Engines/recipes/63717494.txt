Title:
Big Ray's Southwest-Style Cod Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032853.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small onion, chopped
1 small bell pepper, chopped
2 teaspoons olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1 can (2-1/4 ounces) sliced ripe olives, drained
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/8 teaspoon pepper
4 cod fillets/ or other white fish (6 ounces each)

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute onion and green pepper in oil until tender. Add garlic; saute 1 minute longer. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomatoes, olives, cumin, pepper sauce and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes.
Add fillets. Cover and cook over medium heat for 10-14 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork, turning once
