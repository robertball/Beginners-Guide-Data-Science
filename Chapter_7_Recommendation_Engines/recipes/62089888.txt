Title:
Blue Cheese and Bacon Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
8 slices thick sliced bacon, crisp-cooked and drained
4 oz Blue Cheese
1/2 tsp course ground black pepper
4 Kaiser rolls, split
lettuce leaves (optional)
tomato slices (optional)
onion slices (optional)

Directions:
Shape beef into four beef patties.  Grill patties on the rack of an uncovered grill over medium coals for 14 to 18 minutes or until done (160 degrees F)
Place two bacon strips on each burger.  Sprinkle with pepper.  Top with blue cheese.  Grill about 2 minutes more or until cheese begins to melt.  Serve on rolls with lettuce, tomato and onion, if desired.
