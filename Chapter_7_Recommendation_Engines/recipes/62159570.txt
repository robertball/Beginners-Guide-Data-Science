Title:
Mediterranean Three Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can Cannellini beans (white kidney beans)
1 15 oz. can Garbanzo beans (chickpeas)
1 15 oz. can Kidney beans (I prefer dark red, but use light red if you prefer)
1 lemon, squeezed
4 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 onion finely diced
handful fresh parsley, minced
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain & rinse cannellini, garbanzo, and kidney beans well with cold water. Add to medium mixing bowl.
Add lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, onion, parsley, and salt & pepper to taste.
Mix well & serve.
Flavors tend to blend better after time.

Submitter: 
Trina Cosgrave
Description: 
A refreshing blend of flavors great for summer or simple dinners!
