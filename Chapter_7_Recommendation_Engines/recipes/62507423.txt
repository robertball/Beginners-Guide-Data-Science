Title:
Easter Nests Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. margarine
2 cups marshmallows
2 1/2 cups Rice Krispies
1/4 tsp. vanilla
Chocolate Easter eggs
Colored Coconut:
1/4 tsp. water
1/4 tsp. food coloring
1/4 cup shredded coconut

Directions:
Grease 12 muffin cups with margarine.
In a saucepan, over medium-low heat, melt margarine. Add marshmallows, stir until melted and well-blended.
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Add cereal and stir till well coated.
Divide mixture evenly among 12 muffin cups. Press firmly against sides and bottom of pan. Sprinkle insides of muffin cups with colored coconut and fill with Easter eggs.
