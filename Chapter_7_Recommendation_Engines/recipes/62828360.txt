Title:
Nut Free Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup light corn syrup
2/3 cup sunflower seed butter, creamy or crunchy
3 cups quick cooking oats
1/3 cup raisins
1 cup bittersweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Spray 9x13 pan with cooking spray. Mix brown sugar, butter, corn syrup & sunflower seed butter in large bowl until combined.  Add chocoalte chips and raisins and mix.  Mix in oats.  Press into pan and bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool for 1 hour before cutting.

Submitter: 
Catherine McCann Shelton
Description: 
I make these as a nut free option for my daugher.  Chewy granola bars made with sunflower seed butter.
