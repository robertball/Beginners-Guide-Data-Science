Title:
Frosting: Cream Cheese with Coconut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz.) pouch of The Frosting Queens Velvety Vanilla
1 ( 8 oz.) package cream cheese, softened
2 cups sweetened coconut flakes.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Using a mixer, beat frosting and cream cheese together until smooth.
Pour coconut flakes evenly onto baking sheet.  Place in oven for 2-4 minutes allowing flakes to turn golden brown in color.  Watch carefully.  Frost your dessert with cream cheese frosting.  Garnish with toasted coconut
