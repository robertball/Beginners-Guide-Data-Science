Title:
Butterbeer II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon butterscotch topping
1 cup sodar water
1 cup cream soda
whipped cream
2 tablespoon butterscotch topping/caramel
pinch of salt

Directions:
Prepare mug. Put it in a freezer for 15 mins. Pour butterscotch topping and soda water. Mix it thoroughly. Add in the cream soda
In a separate container, whip cream or get a store bougth cream, mix it with butterscotch or caramel and a pinch of salt
Add whipped cream mixture on top of drink.
stir lightly - it will froth - Enjoy!
