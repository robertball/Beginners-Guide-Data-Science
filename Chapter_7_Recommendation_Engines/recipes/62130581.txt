Title:
Shipwreck Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2 onion
3 medium potato
1 lb hamburger, uncooked
1/2 cup uncooked white minute rice
1 (10 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
salt
pepper

Directions:
Directions
1.  Slice onions in the bottom of a baking dish. Add thinly sliced potatoes.
2.  Over potatoes spread hamburger. Next add uncooked rice.
2.  As each layer is added season with salt and pepper to taste.
4.  Pour soup over top
5.  Cover and bake for 2 hours at 350Â°F.
