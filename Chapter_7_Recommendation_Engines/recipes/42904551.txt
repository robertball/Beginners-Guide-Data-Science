Title:
Lemon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup milk
2 eggs
1 tsp baking powder
Grated rind of 1 lemon
1 tsp salt
Topping:
Juice of 1 Lemon
1/3 Cup sugar

Directions:
Cream Butter, Sugar, Eggs together
Mix Flour and other dry ingredients
Fold the liquid into the dry ingredients
Add Milk
Add Lemon Peel and some juice from the lemon into the batter
Bake at 350
Allow to cool slightly
Poke holes in top of loaf
Pour lemon juice mixture over the top
