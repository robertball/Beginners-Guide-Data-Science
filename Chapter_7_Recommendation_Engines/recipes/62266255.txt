Title:
Key Lime Sherbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 cups (1 pint) heavy whipping cream
1/2 cup lime juice (from a bottle)
Drops of green food coloring until it looks limey!

Directions:
Mix and pour into a 9x9 dish (I use my corning ware)
Freeze overnight
Serve with a squirt of cool whip on top or fresh fruit or both or.....

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
A very refreshing dessert.  Great for the summer time when it is pretty dang gum hot outside.  Oh and it also has about 5,000 calories!
