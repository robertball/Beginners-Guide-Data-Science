Title:
Moist Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup oil
½ cup sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
¾ cup buttermilk
1 cup flour
1/3 cup molasses
2 eggs
Pinch of salt
1 cup bran
1 tsp. baking powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Bake at 425 for 13 minutes.
