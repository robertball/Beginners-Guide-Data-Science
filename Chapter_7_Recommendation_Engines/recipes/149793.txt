Title:
Orange Cream Chiller Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985011.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packets SPLENDA® Flavors for Coffee, French Vanilla
2 tablespoons reduced fat milk
1/4 cup sugar free nondairy whipped topping
1/3 cup orange juice
Ice, as desired

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a tall serving glass.  Add ice, as desired.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
This drink will take you back to your childhood with the wonderful orange and vanilla flavors of the Creamsicle frozen treat.
