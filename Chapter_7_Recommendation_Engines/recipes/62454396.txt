Title:
Vegetable Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red potatoes, thin sliced
1 zucchini, thin sliced
1 squash, thin sliced
1 small onion, thin sliced
1 sweet pepper, thin sliced
4 Roma tomatoes, thin sliced
1 package low flat mozzarella
1 jar spaghetti sauce or tomato sauce & add italian spices

Directions:
Spray the bottom of a 9x9 casserole with olive oil.
Add a layer of potatoes. Add sauce.
Add a layer of tomatoes and peppers. Add sauce.
Add a layer of cheese.
Add a layer of squash. Add sauce.
Add a layer of tomatoes and onions. Add sauce.
Add a layer of zucchini. Add sauce.
Add a layer of cheese.
Bake at 375 for 30 minutes.
Cover with foil. Bake another 30 minutes

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This is a great way to eat all those summer zucchini in late summer.
