Title:
Southern Pot Liquor Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rutabeggar
1 gallon of water
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Peel and slice rutabeggar into silver dollar size pieces.
Place in a large boiler with other ingredients, and cook til tender.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
A rutabeggar sliced and boiled til tender forms a delicious liquor the whole family will love. Serve with cornbread.
