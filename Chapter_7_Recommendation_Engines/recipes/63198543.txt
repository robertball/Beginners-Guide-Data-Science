Title:
Peanut butter Chocolate & Reese chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of flour
1 cup oatmeal
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup butter
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 1/4 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
4 tsp vanilla
4 eggs
1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1 cup reese chips

Directions:
PRE-HEAT OVEN TO 375
1. Measure flour, oats, baking soda and salt. Put in a medium bowl, stir to blend and set aside.
2. Cream together butter and peanut butter.
3. Add sugars and beat 2 minutes until well blended.
4. Add vanilla and eggs, beat a little after each egg until blended. Batter should be creamy, scrap sides as needed.
5. Add flour mixture in 4 parts, blending after each addition just until mixed. Batter will be somewhat thick.
6. Stir in chips.
7. Scoop by the tablespoon full and place on cookie sheet (or baking stone) bake 10-12 minutes until tops are lightly browned.
8. Allow to cool slightly on cookie sheet for a few minutes, then remove and cool on rack.

Submitter: 
Corrine Munro
Description: 
Chocolate chip cookie with a twist, peanut butter and reese chips, with oats.
