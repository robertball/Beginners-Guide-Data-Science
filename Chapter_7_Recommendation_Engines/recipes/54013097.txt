Title:
Tomato, mozzarella, basil salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 red onion, sliced
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves chopped
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
fine sea salt to taste
freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, cheese, onion, oil, vinegar,basil and salt and pepper.  Toss and chill for one hour.  Serve on a large platter.  Use small mozzarella cheese balls if youcan find them.
