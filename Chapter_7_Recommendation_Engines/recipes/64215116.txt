Title:
Hashbrown Potato Casserole with herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag (32 oz) frozen hashbrowns, southern style
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup (or less) onion, chopped
1 can (10.75 oz) cream of chicken soup, diluted
8 oz. sour cream
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
2 cups corn flakes, crushed
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 tsp. thyme
1/2 tsp. basil
1 tsp. chervil

Directions:
Combine potatoes, 1/2 cup butter, onion, soup, sour cream, cheese, and herbs in a large bowl. Mix well. Spoon into a greased flat 2-1/2 quart casserole dish.
Crush cereal and mix with remaining butter. Sprinkle over potato mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees (F) for 50 minutes. Garnish with lemon twist and parsley if desired.

Submitter: 
Janet Stern
Description: 
A wonderful, cheesy and creamy side dish that is great comfort food. You can add ham to make this more of a meal.
