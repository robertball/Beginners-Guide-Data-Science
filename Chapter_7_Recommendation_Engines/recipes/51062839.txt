Title:
Stuffed Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large firm round tomatoes
1 or more bell peppers of your color you prefer
small onion
mozzarella cheese, finely diced
olive oil
minced parsley
salt & pepper
clove of garlic

Directions:
1.  Wash the tomatoes an cut in half.  Discard seeds and juice within.  With back of spoon, press the flesh down.  Salt the cavities and invert on paper towel for about a half hour while the salt draws out the juice.
2.  Chop peppers and dice onion and garlic.  In olive oil, saute the mixture covered for about 15 minutes.  Salt to taste.
3.  Pat the cavities of the tomatoes dry and arrange on ovenproof dish.  Sprinkle the bottoms with the diced cheese, then follow with the pepper mixture.  Any leftover cheese, I sprinkle on the top as well.  Cover the dish with foil and bake for about 15 minutes at 380 degrees.  Garnish with parsley.
