Title:
My Best Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32-oz carton of chicken broth or stock
3 or 4 6.5-oz cans minced or chopped clams, reserve liquid
8-oz Half & Half cream (cold*)
8-oz bottle clam juice
5 med. peeled, diced potatoes
1 small onion, chopped fine
2 stalks celery, chopped fine (or 1 carrot)
3 strips extra thick bacon, chopped fine
1 tablespoon olive oil
3 tablespoon of flour
1 tsp. thyme
2 bay leaves
1 tsp. hot sauce
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. onion powder
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place olive oil and bacon in a pot, turn the heat to medium and cook until bacon is crispy.  Add onions and celery and 1/2 tsp. salt; cook until vegetables are soft stirring often.  Carefully drain off about half the fat.
Add potatoes and cook for 5 minutes.  Stir in the garlic powder, onion powder, and flour.  Cook and gently stir until flour is mixed in then stir in the clam juice's (including reserved), mixing well; add thyme, bay leaves, chicken stock, half & half and hot sauce.  Simmer for 15 minutes, adding clams the last 5.  Taste for salt and pepper and remove bay leaves before serving.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
This Clam Chowder is pretty near perfect in my opinion; it has nice clam flavor, is rich & creamy.  The chicken broth cuts down the fat but not flavor!..
