Title:
Zucchini Noodles with Avocado Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large zucchinies
1 large (or 2 small) avocados, pit removed
15 fresh basil leaves
1 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper (optional)
1 clove garlic, crushed
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 lemon, squeezed

Directions:
Julienne zucchini with V-slicer. Make the noodles as long as you can.
Place avocado, basil, salt, pepper(optional), garlic and lemon juice in blender and blend until smooth.
Saute the noodles in a pan with a bit of oil for 2 minutes.
Place noodles on serving dish.
Heat up the sauce and serve on top of the noodles.

Submitter: 
Amy Lee
Description: 
Can add sorrel to sauce for extra flavour.
