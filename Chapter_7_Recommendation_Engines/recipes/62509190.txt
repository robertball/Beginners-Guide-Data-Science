Title:
Mom's Microwave Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506254.jpg
Ingredients:
6 slices white bread
4 eggs
1 1/2 cups half and half cream
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
pinch of nutmeg

Directions:
Cut bread into 1/2 inch cubes to measure about 3 cups.
Place in a microwave safe bowl and microwave uncovered, on high for 4 minutes, stirring once.
Whisk eggs with remaining ingredients. Slowly pour over bread, pressing bread gently into custard.
Microwave, uncovered, on high for 8 minutes.
Cover and let stand for 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
This is my mother's recipe. I changed a few things to make it my own. It is so good and easy to prepare.
