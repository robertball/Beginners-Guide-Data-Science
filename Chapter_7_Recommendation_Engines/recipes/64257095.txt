Title:
Spicy Chicken Enchilada Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4# Boneless Chicken Breasts, diced
1  large Onion, diced
4  4oz. cans Green Chilies, diced Hot
1  16oz. pkg. Pepper-Jack Cheese, diced or shredded
1  24oz. jar Red Roasted Peppers, diced
2  pkgs. Taco Seasoning
1  16oz. Sour Cream
1  Cup  Chicken Bouillon (2 cubes to 1 cup water for
more chicken flavor)
2  28 oz. Tomato Sauce
3  TBS. Minced Garlic
1  Tsp. Oregano
1  Tsp. Basil
1/2 cup cooking oil
1  Bag of Doritos chips

Directions:
Cut up chicken, onions, peppers and cheese. Sautee chicken in hot oil until cooked. Add onion and garlic and cook until tender. Add remaining seasonings, spices, peppers, tomato sauce and bouillon. Cook until bubbly. Add cheese and sour cream and simmer for about 1 hour. Crunch up Doritos on top to serve.
