Title:
THE PERFECT CREAM CHEESE FROSTING FROM GEORGETOWN CUPCAKES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBS Unsalted Butter, room temp
4 cups Confectioner's Sugar, sifted
1/4 teas Pure Vanilla Extract
6 oz. Cream Cheese, room temp

Directions:
Place all ingredients in large bowl and mix on medium speed until well blended, creamy and fluffy.

Submitter: 
abapplez
Description: 
This cream cheese frosting is absolutely perfect. It is the recipe used at Georgetown Cupcakes in Washington, D.C.
