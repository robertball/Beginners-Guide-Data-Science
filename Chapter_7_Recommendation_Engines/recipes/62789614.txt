Title:
Chipotle Yogurt Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head garlic (unpeeled)
1 tsp olive oil
1 cup plain greek yogurt
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
1/4 cup green onion, chopped
1 Tbsp chipotle pepper in sauce, finely chopped
1 tsp sweet paprika
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp grated lime zest
1 tsp fresh lime juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Cut one quarter off top of garlic head and place on a piece of foil.  Drizzle with olive oil and wrap tightly.  Roast until soft, about 1 hour.  Let cool and then sqeeze the inside of garlic into a bowl.
Add remaining ingredients to the garlic and stir until combined.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
My absolute favourite sauce to drizzle over grilled beef!
