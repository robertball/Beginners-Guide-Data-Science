Title:
cream cheese iceing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups sifted confectioner sugar
1-2(8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup of milk (optional)

Directions:
1.In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy. Mix in the vanilla, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar. Store in the refrigerator until/after use.
