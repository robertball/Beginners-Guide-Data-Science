Title:
Dagwood Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 inch sub bread
4 slices of deli ham
4 slices of deli turkey
4 slices of deli salami
4 slices swiss cheese
2-3 slice papper cheese
2 leaves of green leaf lettuce
or spread shredded lettuce
4 slices of tomato
5-6 slices of pickle (your choice)
5-6 olives (your choice)
2 jalapenos, chopped (optional)
di-jon mustard
little red vinegar

Directions:
Cut sub bread in the middle length wise. Spread di-jon mustard on both sides (inside) of sub. Layer meats, cheeses then toast opened so cheeses melt. Then finish topping with lettuce, tomato, pickle, and olives. Sprinkle red vinegar over that and top with jalapenos.
