Title:
Grandma's "Rotten" Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 cups buttermilk
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon shortening

Directions:
1. Combine eggs, flour, sugar, salt and baking soda in mixing bowl and mix well.
2. Heat shortening in skillet or frying pan.
3. Pour pancake batter into hot skillet and spread by maneuvering the skillet manually. Flip once when edges begin to bubble.
4. Remove from heat and eat immediately!

Submitter: 
James Neeb
Description: 
Delicious, crepe-like, buttermilk pancakes. The only thing rotten about these tasty gems is when the last of the batter is gone! Usually served best one at a time. Get 'em while they're hot!
