Title:
Mahogany Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter softened
2 cups sugar
1 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
6 eggs
2 ½ cups all purpose flour
½ cup cocoa
1 cup sour cream and 1/4 tsp baking soda combined
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs one at a time.  Add cocoa.  Alternate flour and sour cream mixture adding a little at a time, starting and ending with flour.  Add vanilla.  Pour in a greased and floured bundt pan.  Bake at 325 for 1h 15m or until clean toothpick.  Cool in pan 10 minutes then remove onto wire rack.

Description: 
All things good about pound cake.  This recipe has brown sugar, sour cream, and chocolate.
