Title:
Cheesy Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts (1/4")
1 can cream of chicken soup
2c shredded cheddar
1/2 c milk
1 pkg taco seasoning
3c corn chips, crushed

Directions:
Place chicken in 9x13 baking dish
Mix soup, 1-1/2c cheese, milk, and taco mix.
Spoon over chicken and top with chips.
Bake covered for 30 minutes.
Remove cover, top with rest of cheese, bake 10 minutes uncovered.
Serve with rice.
