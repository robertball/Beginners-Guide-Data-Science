Title:
Linguine and Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Linguine and Clam Sauce
Time: fast!  meal will be finished when pasta is done.
2 cans chopped clams
1/4 c. olive oil
2 TB butter
1 4oz bottle clam juice
1-2 TB white wine
1 tsp oyster sauce
1/4 c. water
1/4 c. choppeed fresh parsley or 1 TB dry
6 galic cloves, chopped
1 lb. linguine
grated cheese for individual topping

Directions:
Saute garlic in butter and oil on medium heat no longer than one minute. Add juice from canned clams, bottled clam juice, water, wine, and oyster sauce. Raise heat to med-high and boil gently for 2 min. (Might have to lower heat so it boils gently.) Add clams and cook 2 min. Add parsley. Stir. Pour over cooked, drained linguine. Let the eaters shake some grated cheese over top. Mangia!

Submitter: 
Caramia
Description: 
I adapted this old favorite by adding butter, white wine and oyster sauce. My kids ask for this meal... all five of them.
