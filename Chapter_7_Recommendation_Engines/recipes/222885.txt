Title:
Grasshopper Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6106934.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 fluid ounce creme de menthe
3/4 fluid ounce white creme de cacao
1/4 fluid ounce heavy cream
1 cup ice

Directions:
Combine creme de menthe, creme de cacao, cream, and ice in a cocktail shaker. Cover and shake until chilled. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
This cocktail is like a chocolate after-dinner mint in a glass. Skip dessert and go straight for a grasshopper.
