Title:
Gluten free pumpkin spice cake cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gluten free spice cake mix
1 15 oz can of pumpkin
12 oz chocolate chip cookies
1tsp vanilla
2 tbsp ground chia seeds
6 tbsp warm water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix chia seeds in water until gel forms. Cobine all ingredients in a medium bowl and stir until combined . Drop cookies in 1-in balls onto cookie sheet lined w/ Silat. Manipulate dough so that it is a little flatter.
Bake cookies 15-20 minutes

Apron Appeal
Description: 
A quick delicious cookie for gluten intolerant eater in your life.
