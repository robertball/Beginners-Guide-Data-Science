Title:
Prosciutto and Asparagus Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130814.jpg
Ingredients:
Bunch of thin asparagus
Bunch of green onions, sliced thin
5-10 slices of prosciutto
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 lb. linguine
Optional add-ons:
4 tablespoons pine nuts
Zest and juice of one lemon
4 tablespoons parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Boil water and heat frying pan
2. Crisp prosciutto in pan, then remove, and chop when cool.
While crisping prosciutto, snap woody ends off asparagus and boil until al dente.
3. Heat oil in frying pan, add green onions
4. Remove frying pan from heat and add asparagus to green onions, add linguine to still boiling water.
5. Combine cooked pasta and 1/4 cup of pasta cooking water with asparagus and green onions.
6. Top with prosciutto and serve.

Submitter: 
Khatche
Description: 
We love prosciutto wrapped asparagus and this was a great way to stretch the relatively expensive prosciutto. Our whole family loves it.
