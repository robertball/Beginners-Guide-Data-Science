Title:
pull-apart pineapple pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pork roast
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup catsup
2 Tbs. soy sauce
1 cup vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup pineapple juice w/ 1/3 cup flour blended (and Pineapple chunks from juice)

Directions:
Fill a crockpot with your pork roast (trim all visible fat) w/small amount of water (2 cups to keep it moist)
Cook on low all day until tender
Drain the juice from the meat.  If you're using the Pork Roast, let cool until you can handle it, then pull it apart while using a fork.Mix all the ingredients together & pour over meat:
Cook on high for an hour or two, or whenever it's thick enough to suit you.
