Title:
My Mom's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 pounds beef stew meat
2tbsp olive oil
1 package dry beef stew seasoning
1tbsp cajun seasoning
1 can (about 10-3/4 oz) beef gravy
1 can (14oz) beef broth
1 cup water
4-5 medium potatoes; peeled and cut
4-5 carrots; peeled and cut into small chunks
1 small can of mushroom pieces and stems
1 small onion; quartered
3/4 cup frozen peas

Directions:
1. Brown stew meat in a dutch oven in olive oil.
2. Add beef stew seasoning,cajun seasoning, beef gravy, beef broth, and water and mix well.
3. Add remaining ingredients except for the frozen peas.
4. Simmer on medium heat for about 45 minutes.
5. Add frozen peas and simmer for an additional 15 minutes.
6. Serve with crusty breadsticks.

Submitter: 
morgens_aunt
Description: 
During the winters as a child, my mother would spoil my family with delious homemade soups and stews. This is my mother's recipe in which I added a few indgredients as an adult. This stew goes well with crusty homemade breadsticks.
