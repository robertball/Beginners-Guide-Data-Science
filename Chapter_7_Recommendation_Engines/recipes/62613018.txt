Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c butter- room temperature
3/4c sugar
3/4c brown sugar
2 eggs
1t vanilla
1 1/2c flour
1/2t salt
1t baking soda
2 1/2c oats
2c chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Grease baking sheets.
Cream butter and sugars.
Stir in eggs one at a time.
Add vanilla.
Stir together flour, soda, and salt, add to wet mix.
Add oats and chocolate chips.
Drop dough 2" apart on cookie sheet.
Bake 10-12 minutes.
Cool on wire rack.
