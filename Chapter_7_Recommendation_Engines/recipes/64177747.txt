Title:
Mexican Chopped Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients for the tortilla strips:
6 6-inch corn tortillas
1-2 tsp coconut oil
½ teaspoon sea salt
Ingredients for the salad:
1 medium head romaine lettuce, chopped
1 medium bell pepper, 1/4" diced
½ medium red onion, 1/4" diced
½ medium jicama, peeled and /4" diced
1 medium zucchini, 1/4" diced
4 medium tomatoes, seeded and diced
4 ears corn (or use frozen or canned)
1½ cups canned black beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
For the corn tortilla strips, preheat oven to 400°F. Brush tortillas lightly with oil(opt)and sprinkle with salt (opt)
Stack corn tortillas on a cutting board. Cut in half. Cut each stack of halves into thin strips, crosswise, about ¼ inch thick.
Transfer tortilla strips to a sheet pan.
Bake for 10-15 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes, or until light golden brown and crisp. Set aside to cool.
For the salad, If using whole corn, cook corn and allow to cool for 5 minutes. After cooling, cut bottom end of corn off, about 1½ inches from end. Cut kernels from cob, keeping in larger pieces if possible and set aside.
Combine corn and other salad ingredients in a large bowl. Stir to combine. Add dressing and stir to coat all ingredients.
Serve with tortilla strips on top or place a bowl on the side.
