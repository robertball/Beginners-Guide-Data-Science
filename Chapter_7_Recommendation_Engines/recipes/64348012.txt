Title:
Dairy Free Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2106740.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground pork sausage
1 small onion diced
2 baking potatoes, small dice
1 c chopped celery including leaves
8 Cups chicken broth
1/2 C Canned coconut milk (blend liquids and solids before measuring)
1 Cup chopped Kale
1 Cup frozen corn
1 TB marjoram
2 tsp ground sage
2 tsp tarragon
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown the sausage well adding the chopped onion after the sausage has cooked for about 5 minutes.  Cook together on medium-low heat for 10 minutes.  Add diced potatoes and cook for 5 minutes.  Add celery, corn and kale and stir together.  Add broth and spices.  Boil until potatoes are soft.  Stir in coconut milk.  Serve as is or to make a thicker soup puree slightly with an immersion blender.

Submitter: 
Julie Lowrance Acomb
Description: 
Corn chowder was one of my family's favorite dishes.  We started on a dairy free/ gluten free diet this year and this is what I came up with to make one of our favorite soups dairy free.
