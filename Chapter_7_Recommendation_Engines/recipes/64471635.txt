Title:
Dino Mites Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2474022.jpg
Ingredients:
10 medium Yukon Gold potatoes
Half a stick salted butter
1/3 c. sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 c. whipping cream
1 tsp. salt
pinch of pepper
2 Tbs. finely minced kale, steamed till tender. (You can use fresh or dried parsley in place of the kale)
1 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c. crumbled bacon plus  slices for garnish

Directions:
In a large pot, boil the potatoes until they are fork tender. Drain the potatoes and place back in the pot.
In a bowl, mix the heavy cream, salt, pepper, cheese, Worcestershire sauce, and kale.
Add the butter to the hot potatoes and use a hand mixer to whip the potatoes till smooth, adding the cream mixture until fully incorporated.
Add in the bacon crumbles and stir well.
Spoon or pipe the potatoes onto a baking tray and sprinkle with paprika.
Broil in the oven until the tops are golden, remove, and top with the sliced bacon as a garnish.
Enjoy!
