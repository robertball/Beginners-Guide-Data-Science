Title:
Enchilada Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 can (14oz) stewed tomatoes, undrained
1 10oz can enchilada sauce
1-2t ground cumin
1 egg, beaten
1-1/2c cottage cheese
3 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
8 flour tortillas, cut in half
1c shredded cheddar

Directions:
Brown beef with onion and garlic until meat is done; drain.
Stir in tomatoes, enchilada sauce and cumin.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer uncovered 20 minutes.
Combine egg and cottage cheese and set aside.
Spread meat sauce into a greased 9x13 dish.
Layer with half of cheese blend, tortillas, cottage cheese mixture, and remaining meat sauce.
Repeat layers and top with shredded cheddar.
Cover and bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
Uncover and bake 10 minutes longer.
Let stand 15 minutes before cutting.
