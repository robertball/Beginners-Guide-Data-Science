Title:
BUSH'S® Texas Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979777.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean top round steak, cut into thin strips
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 (15 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Pinto Beans
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/3 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/3 cup chopped sweet red pepper
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a large pan, cook meat in oil until browned on all sides; drain.
Add remaining ingredients; stir to combine. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat and cook for 30 to 40 minutes or until meat is tender.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
Even non-Texans are sure to love this Texas-style chili with lean steak, pinto beans and cayenne pepper.
