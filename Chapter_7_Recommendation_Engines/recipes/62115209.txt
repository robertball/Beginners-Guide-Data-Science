Title:
Cajun Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4oz. of Linguine
2 boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
2 T. butter
2 T. cajun seasoning
2 T. sun-dried tomatoes
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. basil
2 green onions diced
1/4 t. pepper
1/8 t. garlic powder
1 1/2 c. heavy whipping cream
1/4 c. parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook linguine according to package instructions.
Coat the chicken cubes with the cajun seasoning in a mixing bowl.
Saute chicken in butter over medium heat in a large skillet until chicken is throughly cooked.  About 7 to 10 minutes.
Reduce heat and add the tomatoes, green onions, cream, pepper, garlic powder, basil and salt.
Pour over cooked linguine and toss with Parmesan cheese.
**For a thicker sauce add small incriments of flour until it is the desired thickness.

Submitter: 
Abby Jarrard
Description: 
An updated creamy cajun style alfredo sauce with seasoned chicken and linguine pasta. Perfect for a dinner for two or double the recipe and make it for guests!
