Title:
Grilled Salmon with Jalapeno Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4" piece of ginger root
2 large cloves garlic
1 - 2 large fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded
1/4 cup losely packed fresh cilantro leaves
1/2 cup butter, softened
grilled fresh salmon

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, combine ginger root, garlic, and japaleno pepper.  Cover & process until finely chopped.  Add cilantro leaves; cover and blend.  Stir mixture into softened butter.  On a piece of waxed paper or plastic wrap, shape butter mixture into a 6" log.  Seal tightly and chill till serving or freeze for up to 3 months.
Serve slices of japaleno butter atop grilled fresh salmon.

Submitter: 
sunnie
Description: 
My family loves salmon grilled over mesquite wood.  The jalapeno butter adds a great and unique flavor that compliments but doesn't overpower the salmon.  The recipe makes several servings that can be frozen for future use.
