Title:
Grilled Shrimp and Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb medium shrimp, peeled, deveined
salt
4 cups thinly sliced romaine lettuce
SALSA:
2 large mangoes, pared, seeded, diced
1 small red onion, finely chopped
2 TBL fresh lime juice
1 TBL snipped chives
1 TBL olive oil
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/8 tsp salt
MARINADE:
1/2 cup plain lowfat yogurt
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp ground cumin seed
1/2 tsp paprika

Directions:
In small bowl, combine salsa ingredients; mix well. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
In plastic bag, combine marinade ingredients; add shrimp, turning to coat. Close bag securely and marinate in refrigerator 30 minutes to 12 hrs.
Remove shrimp from marinade; discard marinade. Thread shrimp onto 8 skewers, dividing evenly. Grill over medium coals 4 to 6 minutes or until shrimp turn pink and are just firm to the touch, turning once. Season with salt, as desired.
Place lettuce on 4 plates, dividing evenly. Top each with 2 shrimp kabobs and 1/2 cup salsa.
