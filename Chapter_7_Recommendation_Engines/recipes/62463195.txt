Title:
Buffalo Wing Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/473173.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Cups Mashed Potatoes
3 eggs
1 packet Hidden Valley Ranch Powder mix
3 Tbsp Frank's hot sauce
2-3 Tyson's frozen chicken strips (heated & diced)
2 Tbsp Cooking Oil
1/2 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
12oz sour cream (optional)

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium high heat.
Mix the Mashed potatoes, Eggs, and Ranch powder in a large bowl till evenly blended.
In a separate bowl toss wings and hot sauce together.
Drop large spoonfuls of the potato batter into the skillet and flatten cakes slightly with a spatula.
Add tossed wings to pancakes(the same as you would blueberries).
Cook for about 4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
Garnish with shredded cheese just before removing pancakes from skillet. Serve immediately.
(optional) sour cream for dipping.
