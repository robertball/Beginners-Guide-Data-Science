Title:
Beef Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. lean ground beef
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1/2 cup chopped red pepper
2 cups of salsa, divided
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup shredded monterrey jack cheese, divided
3 tbsps.Italian salad dressing
8 flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400*F Cook beef and peppers in a large skillet until beef is no longer pink. Add one cup of the salsa and all of the beans; simmer for 3-4 minutes until peppers are tender. Remove from heat and stir in 1/2 of the cheese. Spread 1/2 of the remaining salsa on the bottom of 9x13 inch casserole. Brush each tortilla with a light coating of the salad dressing. Place 1/3 cup of the beef filling down the center of each tortilla and roll up. Place seam side down on top of the salsa in the casserole dish. pour remaining salsa on top of the tortillas. Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Top with remaining cheese and bake for another 5 minutes.
