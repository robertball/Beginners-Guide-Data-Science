Title:
Bleu Cheese Potato Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups mashed potatoes
1 egg, beaten
1/2 c wheat flour
1/2 can corn or 1/2 cup frozen corn, thawed
1/2 c chopped red bell pepper
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp garlic salt
butter
1/4 c crumbled bleu cheese

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine potatoes, egg, flour, cayenne, garlic salt, corn and red pepper.
Melt generous amount of butter in a large non stick skillet over med high heat.
Drop spoonfuls of batter onto preheated pan, shaping into small pancakes.
Sprinkle a small amount of bleu cheese on each cake.  Add another small amount of batter on top to cover cheese.
Cook cakes for several minutes until crisp and golden brown, about 5 minutes.
Flip cakes and cook until golden on both sides.
Repeat with remaining batter, adding more butter as necessary.
Serve hot.

Submitter: 
JennyB
Description: 
This is a great way to use up leftover mashed potatoes.  Cook up a batch and freeze any unused cakes.  Goes great with everything that mashed potatoes would go great with from meatloaf to brats and beans!
