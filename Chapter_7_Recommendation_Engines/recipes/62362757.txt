Title:
Provencale Chicken supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (6 oz. ) skinned, bone-in chicken breasts
2 tea. dried basil
salt/pepper
1 cup diced yellow bell pep
1 (16 oz. ) can navy beans, rinsed and drained
1 (14 1/2 oz.) can pasta style chunky tomatoes, undrained

Directions:
place chicken incrock pot. season w/ salt/pepper, basil
combine remaining stuff and pour over chicken
cook on high 1 hour, then on low 5 hours
to serve, place one breast in a deep bowl and top w/ bean mixture
