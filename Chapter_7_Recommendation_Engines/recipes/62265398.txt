Title:
Thai Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 T. minced garlic
2 T. fresh cilantro
1 T. soy sauce
1 T. curry powder
1 1/2 T. vegetable oil
1/4 t. salt
1/2 t. ground pepper
Prepared chili garlic sauce

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, combine garlic, cilantro, soy sauce, curry powder, oil, salt and pepper.  Puree until smooth.
Scrape mix into a gallon-sized zippered plastic bag and add chicken.  Close bag, remove most of the air and move chicken around so it's completely coated.
Marinate 12-24 hours.
Grill or broil chicken.  Use prepared chili garlic sauce as a condiment.

Description: 
This is my favorite chicken, hands down.  I've tweaked the original recipe to make it quick and seasoned just the way I like it.
