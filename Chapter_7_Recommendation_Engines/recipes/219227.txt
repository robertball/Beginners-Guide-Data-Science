Title:
Hunt's速 Slow Cooker Beef Stew Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
PAM速 Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cups baby carrots
2 cups sliced celery
2 pounds red potatoes, unpeeled, cubed
2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into bite-size chunks
2 teaspoons dried thyme leaves
1 (14.5 ounce) can Hunt's速 Diced Tomatoes, undrained
1/4 cup water
1 (6 ounce) can Hunt's速 Tomato Paste

Directions:
Spray inside of 3 1/2-quart or larger slow cooker with cooking spray. Place onion evenly on bottom of slow cooker. Top with layers of carrots, celery, potatoes and meat; sprinkle with thyme. Pour undrained tomatoes and water over meat.
Cover; cook on LOW 8 to 10 hours or on HIGH 4 to 5 hours or until meat is tender.
Stir in tomato paste; cover. Cook 10 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Hunts.com
Description: 
Tender onions, carrots and potatoes slow cooked with chunks of beef in a beefy tomato broth.
