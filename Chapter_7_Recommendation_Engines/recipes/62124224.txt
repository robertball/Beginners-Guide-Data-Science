Title:
Raw Veggie Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C nuts (any nut works fine)
3t Sesame Oil
1/4C Agave Nectar
3 Lemons, squeezed and zested
1-2 dried red chile (depending on desire of spicy)
1.5T Nama Shoyu or Soy Sauce
2/3C Soynut Butter
1/2 head cabbabe, shredded
1 Large Carrot, sliced into sticks
1 Mango, sliced into sticks
sprouts
fresh basil and mint
Large leaf lettuce

Directions:
1.Process nuts and sesame oil in food processor until crumbly. Set aside.
2.Blend agave, lemon juice & zest, chile, nama shoyu, soynut butter in food processor.
3.Shred cabbage and toss with agave mixture. Set aside.
4.Lay lettuce leaf on plate, put some cabbage mixture down the center. Add carrots, mango, sprouts, fresh basil and mint. Top with some of the cashew mixture.
5.Roll & Eat!
