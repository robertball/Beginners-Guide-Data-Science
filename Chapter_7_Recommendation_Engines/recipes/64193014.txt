Title:
Fried Chicken Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1543462.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Fully Cooked/frozen Breaded Chicken Tenders OR strips!  (semi-defrosted is fine)
1 1/2 cups Pizza/Marinara Sauce
2 cups Shredded Mozzarella
1 cup Pepperoni

Directions:
Layer the pan with an even layer of the Fried Chicken.
Top the Chicken with an even layer of the sauce, then cheese and finally pepperoni.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 20 - 25 minutes.
