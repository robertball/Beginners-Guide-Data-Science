Title:
Peanut Butter Dark Chocolate Double Decker Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1   7 or 9 oz jar marshmallow creme
4   1/2 cups sugar
1   12 oz can evaporated milk
dash of salt
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup peanut butter chips
1 cup dark chocolate baking chips
1 cup miniature chocolate chips

Directions:
Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Stirring constantly, over medium heat combine butter and marshmallow creme until melted.  Gradually add sugar until incorporated.  Add milk and salt.
Stirring constantly, cook over medium heat to a low boil.  Reduce heat slightly and boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat.  Pour half of mixture into a bowl.  Add to the bowl peanut butter and mix until incorporated.  Allow to cool about 5 minutes and stir in peanut butter chips.  Stir to distribute throughout, but not until completely melted.  Pour into prepared baking dish and refrigerate 5 minutes.
To remaining half, stir in dark chocolate baking chips until melted and smooth.  Stir in miniature chips just to distribute throughout.  Pour over refrigerated peanut butter mixture.
Chill in refrigerator for at least 4 hours.  Store in refrigerator.
