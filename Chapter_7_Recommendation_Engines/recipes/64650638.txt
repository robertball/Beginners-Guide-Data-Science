Title:
Beef marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4538428.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 TBSP pure apple cider vinegar
2 TBSP dijon mustard
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and pour over meat in freezer bag. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, but you can leave overnight.

Frank David
