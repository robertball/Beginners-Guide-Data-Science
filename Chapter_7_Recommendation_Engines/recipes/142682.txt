Title:
Cool Fizzies for the Kiddies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4504363.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cranberry juice
1 (12 ounce) can lemon-lime soda
1/2 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup orange juice
8 ice cubes

Directions:
Stir together the cranberry juice, lemon-lime soda, pineapple juice, and orange juice in a pitcher. Place ice cubes in two tall glasses; pour drink over ice to serve.

Submitter: 
CookinginFL
Description: 
Here's a kid-approved drink that adults enjoy too! Be sure ALL ingredients are super cold before preparing this beverage, and just multiply to make more for a crowd.
