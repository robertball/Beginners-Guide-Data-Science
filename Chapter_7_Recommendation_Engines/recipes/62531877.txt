Title:
Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz room temp Cream Cheese
2 cans Sweetened Condensed Milk
3/4 C Lemon Juice or to taste
24 oz Cool Whip Thawed

Directions:
Beat Cream Cheese, Condensed Milk and Lemon Juice,  add Cool Whip and beat just uhntil incorporated. Place in 4 Graham Cracker Pie Crusts.
