Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 oz) cans chunk chicken, drained ( or about 2 chicken breasts cooked and diced)
2 (8 oz) packages of cream cheese, softened
1 cup Ranch dressing
3/4 cup hot wing sauce
1 1/2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Mix all ingredients into a cockpot and stir well.  Cook on low until mixture is hot and bubbly, about 2 hours.  Switch to "keep warm".  Serve with crackers.
