Title:
Vegan Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy milk
1/2 cup water (use 1 cup if making night before)
1/4 cup applesauce
1 tablespoon sugar
2 tablespoons maple syrup
1 cup whole wheat flour, gf mix or spelt
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, blend soy milk, water, 1/4 cup applesauce, sugar, syrup, flour, and salt. Cover and chill the mixture for 2 hours or overnight.
Using a non-stick 5 to 6 inch skillet, heat the skillet until hot. Pour approximately 3 tablespoons batter into the skillet.  Swirl to make the batter cover the skillet's bottom.  Cook until golden, flip and cook on opposite side. May not be necessary to flip.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This makes a great batter, which then makes a great crepe. Simple. Mix the batter, chill at least 2 hrs and pour into a hot pan.Modified from: Vegan Crepes
