Title:
Elegant Baked Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces sliced turkey breast
1 loaf pumpernickel
3 ounces sliced gouda
3-4 tbsp grainy mustard
1/3 c mayonnaise
Fresh spinach
Caramalized Red Onions(opt)

Directions:
Slice loaf horizontally, hero style. spread one half with mustard, the other with mayonnaise. Layer sliced turkey, onions, and spinach on one half, and top with the other. Wrap sandwich in aluminum foil, and bake 40 minutes @ 350 or until sandwich is warm in the middle. Cut into 1.5 inch slices. Serve warm.
Serves 8-10 depending on loaf size

Submitter: 
ChefMom
Description: 
Elegant flavors blend together in this decadent Turkey sandwich.
