Title:
Overnight Rifrigerator Steel Cut Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c steel cut oats
1/4 c plain yogurt
1/2 c skim milk
1 tbs flax seed meal
pinch salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/3 banana - sliced
1/2 tbs natural peanut butter
1/4 c berries of choice

Directions:
1. Combine oats, yogurt, milk, flax and pinch of salt in a glass jar and stir well.
2. Seal jar and refrigerate overnight.
3. In the morning, stir in cinnamon, banana slices, peanut butter and berries.
