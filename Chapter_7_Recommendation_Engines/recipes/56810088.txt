Title:
Creole Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (1 inch thick) pork chops
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (15 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
garlic clove, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 1/4 teaspoons salt

Directions:
Coat the pork chops with flour, shaking off the excess. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the onion and bell pepper before adding the pork chopsAdd pork chops and cook until browned on each side, about 5 minutes per side. Reduce heat to low and add the tomatoes. Season with Worcestershire sauce and salt. Cover and simmer for about 1 hour, until chops are fork tender.

Submitter: 
SOUTHERNBELLE
Description: 
A homey recipe for pork chops simmered in a savory, chunky tomato sauce. This is also delicious simmered in a slow cooker for 5 or 6 hours.Modified from: Creole Pork Chops
