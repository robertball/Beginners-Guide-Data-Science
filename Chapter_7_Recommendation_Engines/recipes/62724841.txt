Title:
Reuben Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened, cut into bits
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup drained sauerkraut, chopped
1/2 pound cooked lean corned beef, chopped fine
2 tablespoons red onion, chopped
1 tablespoon ketchup
2 teaspoons spicy brown mustard
1 cup Swiss cheese, grated (about 3 ounces)
Rye crackers

Directions:
1.  Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
2.  In a large bowl combine the cream cheese, sour cream, sauerkraut, corned beef, onion, ketchup, mustard, and Swiss cheese.  Mix well.
3.  Transfer dip to a small ovenproof casserole dish and bake it, covered for 30 minutes, or until bubbles form around the edges.  Remove the cover and bake the dip 5 minutes more, or until it begins to turn golden.  Serve dip warm with crackers.

Submitter: 
kirklandcook
Description: 
My husband and I both love Reuben sandwiches.  This party appetizer satisfies those cravings.  Serve with rye crackers as an accompaniment.
