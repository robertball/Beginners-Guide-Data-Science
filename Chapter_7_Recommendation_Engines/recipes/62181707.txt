Title:
Roasted Butternut Squash Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Butternut squash peeled, cleaned and cut into 1" cubes
1 diced onion
1/2 cup of dried cran-raisins
1/3 cup of chopped walnuts
olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and drizzle with olive oil. Add salt and pepper to taste and roast in the oven 400 degrees until squash is tender, about 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Toni Losada
Description: 
This is not your ordinary squash dish.  It has cran-raisins, walnuts and onions, sweet and savory! SOOOOO easy!
