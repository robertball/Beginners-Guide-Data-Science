Title:
Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups sliced zucchini
3.5 cups water
3 medium onion, chopped
4 tablespoon chopped green pepper
4 eggs
2 cups mayonnaise
1 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 1/3 Cups crushed ritz crackers

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the zucchini, water, onion and green pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender; drain.
In a bowl, beat the egg. Add mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper; mix well. Stir into vegetable mixture. Transfer to a greased 3-qt. baking dish; sprinkle with cracker crumbs. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
