Title:
Pineapple Orange Ice Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz. can Pineapple chunks, undrained
1 c. orange sherbert
1 c. orange juice
12 small paper cups
12 Popsicle sticks

Directions:
Process all ingredients in a food processor
Divide among cups and place in freezer 1-2 hours
Insert Popsicle sticks and freeze solid 1-2 hours
