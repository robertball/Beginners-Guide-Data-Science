Title:
Spicy Indian Cranberry Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2878188.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried apricots, finely chopped
1/2 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
1/2 cup raisins
1 cup water
3 cups fresh cranberries
1 Granny Smith apple - peeled, cored and diced
1 teaspoon lemon zest
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup finely chopped crystallized ginger
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
In a  medium saucepan, mix dried apricots, dark brown sugar, raisins and water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Mix in cranberries, apple and lemon zest. Simmer 10 minutes.
Mix in lemon juice, crystallized ginger and red pepper flakes. Serve at room temperature or chilled.
