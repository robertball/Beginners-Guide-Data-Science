Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985485.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/3 cup cold margarine, or slightly softened butter
1 1/2 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
6 cups sliced, peeled cooking apples

Directions:
In large bowl mix together sugar, flour and cinnamon. Using pastry blender, cut in margarine until resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in cereal.
Spread apple slices evenly in 23 cm (9-inch) square baking pan. Sprinkle cereal topping over apples.
Bake at 180 degrees C (350 degrees F) about 30 minutes or until apples are tender. Serve with frozen yogurt or whipped topping, if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* add a bit of crunch to the topping of this classic apple crisp.
