Title:
Beef and Bow Ties Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups bow tie pasta
1 lb. ground beef
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups tomatoes, chopped
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
2 tbsp. basil, chopped
3 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and keep warm.
In a large skillet, cook the ground beef on medium heat for 10 minutes or until browned; drain. Add the garlic and cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
Add the tomatoes, salt and black pepper. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
In a serving dish, combine the pasta, ground beef mixture and basil and toss. Sprinkle Parmesan on top.
