Title:
Cream Cheese and Parmesan Bread Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/773860.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup softened butter
1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
4 cloves garlic, pressed
1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Directions:
Mix the butter, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and Italian seasoning in a bowl until evenly blended.

Submitter: 
Mark S.
Description: 
This is a great spread for bread or crackers. It goes particularly well spread on bread with any pasta meal. 
Serve with sliced French bread or any other bread you like. While the bread is warming, mix all the other ingredients in a bowl. Spread on warmed bread and enjoy!
