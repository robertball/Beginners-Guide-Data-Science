Title:
Oatmeal  Cookies with fresh rasins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cups flour
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cinnamon
3/4 c butter
3/4 c packed light brown sugar
1 egg
1 T water
1 T vanilla
3 C quick or old fashion oats
1 C fresh raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 grease cookie sheets
Combine flour, salt, soda and cinnamon in a small bowl
Beat butter both sugars,on med. speed until fluffy.
Add egg, water and vanilla.  Beat Well.  Add flour mixture on low until just blended.  Stir in oats then stir in raisins.
Drop Dough 2 inches apart
Bake 10 mins or until edges brown.  Transfer on racks.  Cook completely.
