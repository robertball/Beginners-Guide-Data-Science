Title:
Pohany Recipe
Image:
NA
Ingredients:
2  eggs
¼ cup white sugar
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
⅛ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon lemon extract
1 cup vegetable oil for frying
⅓ cup confectioners' sugar for decoration

Directions:
Beat eggs until fluffy, add remaining ingredients and mix well.  Knead until smooth and elastic.
Roll thinly on floured board. Cut into strips 1 x 5 inches.  Cut a 1 inch slit in the center of each strip and pull one end thru.
Fry in 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) oil until golden brown. Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.

Submitter: 
Wendy Mello
Description: 
My Grandma made these cookies for us.   We make an 'imitation' of these by cutting flour tortillas into strips and deep-frying and then sprinkling with confectioners' sugar.  To make the cookies or tortillas fancier my Grandma used a  serrated knife to give a jagged edge.
