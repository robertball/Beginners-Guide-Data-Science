Title:
Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pine nuts
9 cloves chopped garlic
5 cups fresh basil
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 cups extra virgin olive oil
1 cup grated parmesean

Directions:
Place nuts and garlic in food processor, process for 30 seconds.  Add basil, salt and pepper.  With processor slowly running, pour olive oil through the feed tube and process until finely pureed.  Add parmesean, puree for one more minute.
