Title:
Mae's Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream style corn
1 can corn, not drained
1 box Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
2 eggs, beaten
1 stick melted butter
1 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix ingredients except shredded cheese. Bake 1/2 hour at 350 degrees. Sprinkle cheese on top. Bake 10 minutes.
Can also be made in a crock pot. Follow above directions but cook on low for 4-6 hours. Sprinkle cheese and cover for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
MRSJPVAN2
Description: 
This was given to me by Mae Van Artsdalen. We always make it for Thanksgiving, but I think it would be good anytime.
