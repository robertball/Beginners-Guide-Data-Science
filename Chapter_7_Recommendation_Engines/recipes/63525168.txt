Title:
Nutella Fudge Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962434.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour
1 egg
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter chips
1 cup Nutella

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl until smooth
Place dough in freezer for ten minutes
After, role dough into one-inch balls and spread two inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
bake for 8-10 minutes
remove from oven and let cool until cookies are set
