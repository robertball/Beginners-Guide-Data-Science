Title:
Holy Smoke Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 1/2 sticks butter
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans
Second layer:
8 oz softened cream cheese
1 cup confectioner's sugar
1 large tub whipped topping
Third layer:
2 3 oz boxes chocolate pudding, prepared according to box directions
Fourth layer:
1 large tub whipped topping
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Crust:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In bowl, combine flour, softened butter and pecans.  Cream together until well mixed.  Press onto bottom of glass 9x13 inch baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.  Let cool.
Second Layer:
Combine cream cheese, confectioner's sugar and whipped topping.  Mix on high speed until smooth.  Spread over crust.
Third Layer:
Pour prepared chocolate pudding over cream cheese mixture.
Fourth Layer:
Spread whiped topping over pudding layer, then sprinkle with chopped pecans and chocolate chips.  Chill for one hour.  Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description: 
A creamy, chocolatey, delicious layered summertime dessert.  This is a star of Southern church potlucks -- hence the name!
