Title:
Thick & Zesty Slow Cooker Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 28 oz cans crushed tomatoes with basil
4 6 oz. cans tomato paste (italian style)
2 lbs ground beef browned & drained
1 onion peeled & pureed
2 bell peppers seeded & pureed
2 tablespoons sugar
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tsp garlic salt
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp crushed rosemary
1 tsp oregano
4 tsp black pepper
1 1/2 cup water
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a 5.5-6 quart crockpot and stir. Slow cook on low for 8 hours. If possible, stir occasionally.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Turnage Slater
Description: 
This is a versatile thick & zesty spaghetti or lasagna sauce. It doubles as a meaty marinara also for breadsticks. Easy and convenient, just dump together ingredients the night before & refrigerate. Turn on the crock pot the next morning and it will be ready when you get home!
