Title:
Easy Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds chuck roast "marbelized"
1 package dry onion soup
2 cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 can chicken broth
1 package dry brown gravy mix
1 clove garlic, minced
3 carrots, chopped
4 russet potatoes, quartered

Directions:
Preheat oven or crock pot to 350 degrees F.
Place meat in a roasting pan. Place carrots,  potatoes, and minced garlic around roast.
In a small bowl, mix dry onion soup, 1 can of     mushroom soup, and chicken broth.
Pour over the roast.
Cover pan/pot and cook for 3 hours in the preheated oven/pot.
Turn roast over and coat the roast with the last can of mushroom soup.
Mix in the package of dry brown gravy in the sauce and cook 1 more hour.
Remove from oven and let meat rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

Description:
