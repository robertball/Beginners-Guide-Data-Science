Title:
Strawberry Banana Salsa with Cinnamon Sugar Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c frozen banana/strawberry mix, thawed
2 tbl honey
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1 c fresh strawberries, diced
2 large, whole wheat tortillas
1/2 c sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
3 tbl butter, melted
3 tbl coconut oil, melted

Directions:
reheat oven to 350 degrees.
Puree frozen fruit, honey and 1/8 tsp cinnamon until just blended and a bit chunky. Stir in fresh strawberries. Put in decorative bowl and set aside.
In small bowl, mix sugar and 1/2 tsp cinnamon together.
In another small bowl, mix butter and oil together.
Brush tortillas with butter/oil mixture. Sprinkle with reserved cinnamon sugar. Cut in to equal wedges of 8.
Place tortilla wedges on 1 or 2 cookie sheets. Bake for 8-10 minutes until hard, flipping after 5 minutes.
Serve.
