Title:
Irish soon bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
224 g Philadelphia Cream Cheese, Fat Free
8 tbsp Pure Irish Butter
2 cups Yellow Sweet Corn, Canned
2 cups Cream Corn
2 Large Eggs
1/2 cup Onions
4 oz Dubliner Cheese
1 1/2 cups Corn Muffin Mix Unprepared

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°.
2. Place cream cheese and butter into a microwavable mixing bowl, microwave for 1 minute until slightly melted.
3. Add remaining ingredients mixing very well until smooth and creamy.
4. Pour mixture into a buttered baking dish.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes or until the center feels firm.

Freeda Bennett
