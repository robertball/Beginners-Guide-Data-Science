Title:
Crab Boil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole live dungeness crabs
4 half ears of corn
8 andoille sausage
8 red potatoes
4 onions quartered
crab boil seasoning (old bay recomended)

Directions:
boil all ingredients despite crab by direction
30 minutes for potatoes
steam crabs in rolling boil for a minimum of 10 minutes
