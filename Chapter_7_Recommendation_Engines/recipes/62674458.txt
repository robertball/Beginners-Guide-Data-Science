Title:
Carol's Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c oil
2 t vanilla
2 c sugar
3 eggs
3 c chopped apples
1 1/2 c chopped pecans
3 c all-purpose flour
1 t salt
1 t baking soda
1 t cloves
1 t nutmeg
1 t cinnamon
For topping:
1 c brown sugar
1/4 c milk
1 stick (1/2 c butter)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease tube pan.  Mix together oil, vanilla, sugar and eggs and set aside.  In large bowl, mix together apples, pecans, flour, salt, soda, cloves, nutmeg and cinnamon and then add first mixture and mix well. Pour into prepared pan and bake for 1 hour.  Meanwhile, prepare topping by combining brown sugar, milk and butter, heat and cook 2 1/2 minutes.  Pour over cake while cake is hot and still in pan.  Let cool and invert on cake plate.

Submitter: 
It's A New Day
Description: 
My good friend Carol made this for a group meeting many years ago and the subject of the meeting got sidetracked to discuss how good this was! You can use whatever apples you have but Carol used Red Delicious. You can sub raisins for the pecans if you prefer.
