Title:
Celery-Parmesan Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stalks celery
2 cloves garlic
3/4 of a lemon
1 oz kalmata olives
2 tablespoons toasted almonds
1/4 cup shaved parmesan cheese
salt and pepper
olive oil
parsley, to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the celery, olives, garlic, toasted almonds, lemon zest, cheese and half of the parsley; season with salt and pepper. Add the juice of the 3/4 of the lemon and a drizzle of olive oil; toss to coat and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Jessica Wilson
Description: 
I'm not usually a big fan of celery, but this is a great, light salad to accommodate  buffalo shrimp or chicken wings.
