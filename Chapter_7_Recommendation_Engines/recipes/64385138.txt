Ingredients:
5 Swai fillets (1/2" thick, about 2-3 oz each)
1 tbsp. Olive Oil
2 tbsp. Butter
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp. dried Jamaican Jerk seasoning

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat in frying pan.
After oil is warm, add butter.
Sprinkle salt and Jamaican Jerk seasoning evenly over fish.
Cook fish gently in oil/butter over medium heat, in single layer, about 3-4 minutes per side, turning only once.
Add up to 1 tbsp. butter to oil between batches if necessary.
