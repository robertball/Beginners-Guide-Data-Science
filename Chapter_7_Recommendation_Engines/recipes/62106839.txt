Title:
Spicy Chili Con Carne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of sundried tomatoes
4 onions, minced
8 cloves of garlic, minced
4 lbs Chuck steak
2 cinnamon sticks
2 teaspoons of cumin
4 teaspoon of chili powder
4 fresh chilies, minced
4 cans of diced tomatoes
1 can of water

Directions:
Saute the garlic and onion in olive oil until wilted, about 5 minutes.
Then add the cumin and chili and simmer for 2 minutes.
Pulse the chuck beef into a medium mince and add to the pot.
Add the sun dried tomatoes into the mixer with the chilies to pulse then add to the pot.  Add the cinnamon stick
Next add the diced tomatoes and water.  Stir, cover and cook for 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
A very hearty meal for any day; try this dish on BBQ days.  Chuck steak cooked with sundried tomatoes, garlic,cumin, chili powder, tomatoes, and chilies.
