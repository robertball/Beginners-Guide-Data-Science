Title:
No bake chocolate macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of quick cooking oats
1/2 cup shredded coconut
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup milk
1/4 butter
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
(1/4 cup walnuts optional)

Directions:
In a medium size bowl combine, oats, and coconut and set aside.
In a saucepan combine sugar, milk, butter and cocoa powder.  Cook over medium heat stirring constantly until mixtures comes to a boil.
Remove from stove and stir in oat mixture.
Drop on waxed or parchment paper before it gets hard.
Put in the refrigerator and let them set until cool.

Description: 
This delicious snack is VERY easy to make. It requires no baking.  Have the kids help with this healthy snack.
