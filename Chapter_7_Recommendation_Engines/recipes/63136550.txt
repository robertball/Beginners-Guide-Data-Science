Title:
Big Smokey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/801038.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 (pound) ground beef
3/8 (medium) onion, grated
1 1/2 (clove) garlic, minced
1 1/2 (tablespoon) Worcestershire sauce
3 (slice) cheddar cheese
2 1/4 (tablespoon) steak sauce
3/16 (teaspoon) Tabasco sauce
3 hamburger buns for serving

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
Combine all ingredients (except buns and cheese) and form the mixture into 4 patties. Season with salt and pepper.
Place burgers on preheated grill and cook 5-7 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. Place a slice of Cheddar cheese on top of each burger one minute before they are ready. Serve on hamburger buns.
Note: Canned Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce may be substituted for the Tabasco and A-1 Sauce.
