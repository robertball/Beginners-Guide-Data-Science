Title:
Carrots, Broccoli and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rice
1/2 cup broccoli
1/2 cup carrots
1/8 cup soy sauce
3/8 cups water
1/4 tsp ginger

Directions:
mix ginger, water, soy sauce and rice in microwave safe bowl. Microwave for 5 minutes.
Miax carrots and broccoli in microwave safe bowl or bag. microwave for 2-3 minutes.
Fluff rice. Mix in veggies.
Serve

Submitter: 
Kristyn
Description: 
I make this as a quick side for asian / stir fry dishes.
