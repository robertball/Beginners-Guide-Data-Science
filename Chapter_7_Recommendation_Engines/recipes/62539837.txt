Title:
SCD Best Honey Mustard Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup prepared mustard
1/3 cup honey
1/2 cup scd mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
1 teaspoon dried dill weed

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk the yellow mustard, honey,  mayonnaise, garlic powder and dill weed together until well blended.
