Title:
Peppers and Sausauges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion
2 bell peppers (I like red & yellow)
3-4 Tbs. olive oil
2 cloves of garlic
2 soy sausages
salt
pepper
pasta

Directions:
Heat the oil in a big skillet.
Put some salted water on to boil the pasta.
Saute the chopped onions in the oil over low heat until soft, add the minced garlic, and sliced soy sausage (into rounds).  Let the soy sausage slices cook on each side until golden brown.
**********************
Put the pasta in the now boiling water.  Cook until done
**********************
Chop the peppers and add to the onion/sausage skillet.  Cook until soft.
Serve the peppers/onions/sausage over the pasta.
