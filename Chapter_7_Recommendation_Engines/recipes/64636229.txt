Title:
Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Salt and pepper
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup Italian breadcrumbs
½ cup vegetable oil
1 cup onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 cups tomato sauce
1 sprig basil
½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
4 oz mozzarella cheese
¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
200g (about ½ lb) thin linguine, cooked

Directions:
1. Pound chicken until ?-inch thick.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Dip the chicken in the beaten eggs, then roll in breadcrumbs.
4. Heat oil in a large oven-safe skillet.
5. Brown chicken on all sides. Remove and set aside.
6. Preheat oven to 350°F.
7. Discard excess oil from pan, leaving about 1 tablespoon of oil in the skillet.
8. Sauté chopped onion and garlic until onions are slightly translucent.
9. Stir in tomato sauce, drop in a sprig of basil and bring to simmer for 10 minutes.
10. Remove the basil.
11. Place chicken in the sauce.
12.Sprinkle the Parmesan over the chicken and place a round of mozzarella on top of each chicken breast.
13. Transfer to oven and bake at 350°F for 15 minutes, or until cheese has melted and browned.
14. Serve with linguine, and garnish with parsley.
