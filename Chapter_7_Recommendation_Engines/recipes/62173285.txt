Title:
Blueberry Cobbler Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup quick-cook oatmeal
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1-1 1/4 cups water

Directions:
Mix together in a microwave safe bowl.
Microwave on high for 2-3 minutes.
Stir.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
A Turtle in the Kitchen
Description: 
A quick, warm and sweet way to start the day! Ingredients can be varied depending on personal preference, but this is my favorite version.
