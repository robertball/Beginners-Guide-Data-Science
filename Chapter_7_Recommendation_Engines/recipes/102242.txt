Title:
Bill's Seasoned Ketchup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1110005.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) bottle ketchup
2 teaspoons hot pepper sauce
1 tablespoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
1 tablespoon dried onion granules
2 teaspoons granulated garlic
2 teaspoons chili powder

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the ketchup, hot pepper sauce, cayenne pepper, dried onion, dried garlic and chili powder. Return to the ketchup bottle or another squeeze bottle and store in the refrigerator. Use as you would ketchup.

Submitter: 
WHATS HIS FACE
Description: 
We see recipes for seasoned salt all over the place. Why not make you own seasoned ketchup? Remember, if you like an ingredient, add more. If you don't, just don't put it in. This recipe is a base only. The brand of ketchup you start with will have a bearing on the ingredients you want. HAVE FUN!
