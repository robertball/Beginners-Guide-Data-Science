Title:
Spicy Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2058290.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shrimp, peeled, deveined and cut up
1/2 teaspoon onion juice
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened

Directions:
In a small bowl, blend onion juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot pepper sauce, mayonnaise, cream cheese. Stir in shrimp.

Submitter: 
Tracey Orr McGill
Description: 
Serve this flavorful dip with your favorite dipping crackers.
