Title:
Crab and Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen green peas, thawed
1 pound imitation crab meat, flaked
1/2 cup  Lite Miracle Whip
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine peas, imitation crab,  Lite Miracle Whip, garlic powder and onion powder. Mix thoroughly. Cover and refrigerate until serving.
