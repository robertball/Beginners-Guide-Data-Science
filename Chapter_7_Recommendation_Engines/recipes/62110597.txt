Title:
Seafood Stuffed Jumbo Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 Jumbo pasta shells
2 6 oz. cans crabmeat, drained and cartilage removed
1 4 oz. can tiny shrimp, drained
3/4 cup shredded Swiss cheese
2/3 cup mayonnaise
2 tbsp. finely chopped celery
1 tbsp. finely chopped onion
1 tbsp. finely chopped pimento

Directions:
Cook jumbo shells to al dente according to package directions.  Drain shells in collander, rinse with cold water and invert onto paper towels to dry, discarding any broken or torn shells.
Combine crabmeat, shrimp, cheese, mayonnaise, celery, onion and pimento in medium size bowl.  If mixture seems too dry, add a little more mayonnaise.
Spoon mixture into cooled shells, arrange on serving platter, and chill in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
Jumbo shells are stuffed with a delicous seafood salad then chilled for a scrumptious summer appetizer.
