Title:
Beef Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 c. sour cream
salt & pepper to taste
8 flour tortillas (12 inch size)
1 c. monterrey jack cheese, shredded
Homemade Enchilada Sauce:
15 oz. can tomato sauce
1/4 t. garlic powder
1 1/2 t. chili powder
1/2 t. oregano leaves
1/4 t. cumin
1/2 c. taco sauce (smooth variety)

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion, salt and pepper; drain thoroughly.  Stir in sour cream.  Grease a 9x13 pan and spread 1/2 cup of enchilada sauce in bottom.  Fill each tortilla with 1/4 cup ground beef mixture and sprinkle with 2 tablespoons cheese.  Roll up and place seam side down in prepared pan.  Pour remaining sauce over top.  Bake uncovered at 350ยบ for 30 minutes.  If desired, sprinkle additional cheese on and bake 5 more minutes.
