Title:
Apple Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Cups Granny Smith Apples, peeled, cored, and diced into 1/2 inch chunks
12 Tbsp flour
1-1/2c of sugar
4 heaping teaspoons of cinnamon
1/4-1/2 teaspoon of nutmeg (optional)
1 tsp lemon juice
4 tablespoons of chilled butter cut into 24 equal portions.
two boxes of refrigerated pie crusts (four chilled NOT frozen crusts)

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients in a bowl. Mix Well.
Using a wide mouth mason jar ring, cut out pie crusts.
Line Muffin Tins with Pie Dough.
gently fill the crusts with the apple mixture and top with a dot of butter.
Cover with another  pie dough circle and crimp edges. prick with fork to vent.
Brush tops with melted butter.
Bake at 400 for 18-22 minutes.
Remove and place on cooling racks.
