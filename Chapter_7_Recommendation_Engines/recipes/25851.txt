Title:
Cape Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/14247.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger coconut flavored rum
2 fluid ounces cranberry juice
2 fluid ounces grapefruit juice
1/2 teaspoon superfine sugar

Directions:
In a highball glass over ice, combine coconut rum, cranberry juice, grapefruit juice and sugar. Stir.

Submitter: 
TOPH2NZ
Description: 
A refreshing beverage recalling the delightful offshore breeze that greeted our family on vacation on Cape Cod.
