Title:
Crock Pot Spinach & Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) Package cream cheese, softened
2/3 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
2/3 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 (10-12 oz.) bag of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 can quartered artichoke hearts (strained)
6 cloves garlic, chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crock pot. Cover and cook about 90 minutes (until hot in the center) stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle the top evenly with cheese & spoon salsa / tomatoes in a ring around the edges if desired.
Continue cooking another 15 minutes on low setting.
Serve warm with chips or crusty bread.
