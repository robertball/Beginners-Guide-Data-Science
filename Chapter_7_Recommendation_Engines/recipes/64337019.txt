Title:
Korey's Hot Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of frozen corn - thawed
1 4oz can green chillies
1 1/2 cup Monterrey Jack cheese - shredded
1/2 cup mayonnaise
6 slices of bacon - cooked and broken in small pieces

Directions:
Mix together corn, green chillies, 1 cup Monterrey Jack cheese, and mayonnaise. Grease a pie dish with cooking spray. Place mixture in pie dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake 25 minutes at 350.
Sprinkle bacon on top and cook an additional 5 minutes.
