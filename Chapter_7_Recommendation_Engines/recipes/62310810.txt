Title:
Panko Crusted Mac n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-oz macaroni
8-oz sharp cheddar cheese
12-oz container of small curd cottage cheese
8-oz heavy cream
1/4-cup parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1-cup panko crumbs
1/4-cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 degrees.
Cook pasta in boiling salted water.
Mix together all ingredients except for bread crums and butter.
Place in a 9x13 sprayed with pam.
Mix melted butter and panko crumbs and spread on top of casserole.
Bake 30 min. till brown on top.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
The terrific crunch of a panko topping combined with the savory taste of mac n cheese! Comfort done right.
