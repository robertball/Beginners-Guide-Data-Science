Title:
Bourbon Spiced Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. McCormick Bourbon Spiced Pork Recipe & seasoning mix
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
2 lb pork tenderloin
1tbsp. Vegetable oil
1/4 cup orange juice
3 medium sweet potatoes,  peeled & cut into 1-inch cubes
2 unpeeled apples, cored and halved

Directions:
Mix seasoning mix and brown sugar. Reserve 1/4 cup. Brush pork with oil. Rub with remaining seasoning mixture.  Place pork tenderloins in center of large shallow foil-lined baking pan.
Mix reserved seasoning mixture and orange juice in large bowl. Add sweet potatoes and apples;  toss to coat well.  Arrange sweet potatoes and apples, cut side down, around pork.
roast in 425 oven 30-45 minutes or until pork is desired doneness and sweet potatoes are tender.  Toss sweet potatoes and apples with pan juices; serve with sliced pork.
