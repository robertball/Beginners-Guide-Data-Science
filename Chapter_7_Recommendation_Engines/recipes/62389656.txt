Title:
The Next Best Thing to Robert Redford Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter; melted
1 cup sifted flour
1 cup finely chopped pecans
8 oz. package cream cheese
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon maple flavoring
8 oz. whipped cream (divided)
1 large package instant chocolate pudding
1 large package instant vanilla pudding
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
3 cups cold milk

Directions:
Mix melted butter, flour and chopped pecans and press into buttered 9 x 13 pan.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until lightly brown.  Cool completely.
Mix cream cheese, sugar and maple flavoring until smooth.  Add 4 oz. whipped cream last and spread over cooled crust.
Beat pudding mixes, milk and almond extract until smooth and thick.  Spread over cream cheese layer.
Top with remaining 4 oz. whipped cream.  Sprinkle grated chocolate and chopped pecans.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
