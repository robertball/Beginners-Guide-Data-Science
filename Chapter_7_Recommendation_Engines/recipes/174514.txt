Title:
Tortellini Salad Italiana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993133.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package BUITONIÂ® Refrigerated Family Size Mixed Cheese Tortellini, prepared according to package directions
2 cups thinly sliced yellow bell peppers
1 cup halved small fresh mozzarella cheese balls (bocconcini)
1 cup bottled Italian vinaigrette dressing
1/2 cup thinly sliced prosciutto
1/2 cup drained, diced sun-dried tomatoes
1/2 cup thinly sliced basil
Fresh cracked black pepper (optional)

Directions:
Combine prepared pasta, bell peppers, cheese, vinaigrette, prosciutto, tomatoes and basil in medium bowl. Sprinkle with pepper. Refrigerate for 1 hour.

With tender tortellini, colorful vegetables and fresh bocconcini, this contemporary Italian classic is a feast for the eyes and the mouth!
