Title:
Strawberry Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/355539.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
1 8oz. package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp almond extract
1 quart fresh strawberries, hulled and rinsed
1/4 cup water
4 tsp corn starch
2/3 cup granulated sugar

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and confectioners' sugar in medium bowl.
Beat with electric mixer at medium speed until smooth.
Beat in vanilla and almond extracts.
Spread in bottom of cooled pastry shell.
Crush or puree enough strawberries to make one cup.
In small saucepan, mix water, granulated sugar and cornstarch.
Add crushed berries.
Bring to a boil.
Cook for 2 minutes or until thick.
Cool.
Slice remaining berries and fill pastry shell.
Spoon cooled strawberry mixture evenly over berries.
Chill for 3 or more hours before serving

Submitter: 
Charissa Simonson Okerstrom
Description: 
This is a pie that is almost as easy to make as it is to eat.
