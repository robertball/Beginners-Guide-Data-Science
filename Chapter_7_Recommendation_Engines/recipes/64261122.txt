Title:
Spinach and Sausage Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1822682.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb sweet Italian Sausage, slightly cooked
10 oz frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1 egg
1 lb cheddar, or cheddar/mozzarella mix, shredded
1 Cup self-rising flour
1/4 tsp salt
pinch of nutmeg (opt.)

Directions:
Preheat oven and baking stone to 375 degrees.
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl until well combined.
Form mixture into balls and place on preheated baking stone.
Bake for 20-25 min.
Cool slightly before enjoying!
