Title:
5 Grain cereal cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/930809.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
1 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Baking Soda
1 C. Cooked 5 Grain Cereal (1/2 cup raw cereal)
1/2 cup of reserved plum juice from one 14 oz can of prune plums
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1 C. Quick Oats
1 C. Raisins
1 C. Butter (softened)
3/4 C. Granulated sugar
3/4 C. Brown sugar
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
In a large mixing bowl cream sugar and softened butter together.
Add plum juice to cooked cereal and let stand for a few minutes before adding to the dough
With an electric hand mixer beat in the eggs and cereal combining well
Add the dry ingredients to the dough and mix on medium speed until flour is fully incorporated into the dough
Add the quick oats and stir in with the raisins to make a drop cookie dough
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper and put rounded teaspoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart on the cookie sheet
Bake in preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes and then transfer to a cookie rack to cool

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A soft and chewy cookie with the goodness of 5 grain cereal to make a healthy cookie for your snack time
