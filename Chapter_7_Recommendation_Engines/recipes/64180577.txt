Title:
Arkansas Fried Veggie Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red (garden fresh) taters (cubed), half an onion (diced), 2 green tomatoes (cubed), 3 or 4 sticks okra (cut into lil round slices), 2 squash (sliced), half a green bell pepper (diced), salt n pepper, corn meal, left over bacon grease or or veggie or olive oil (your choice) 1 egg and 1 cup milk

Directions:
Pour about 1 cup of corn meal in a large bowl. Mix egg and milk together in another large bowl. Dip all the veggies in the milk and egg mixture and then dip into corn meal and fry in grease til good n brown. Sprinkle salt n pepper.
