Title:
Easter M&M; Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 cup butter, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
2 1/2 all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups whole M&M;'S easter colored candies

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
2. Beat sugars and butter in large bowl on medium speed until fluffy, about 5 min
3. Beat in vanilla and eggs and mix
4. Add flour, salt, and baking soda and mix on low speed
5. Stir in candies
6. Refridgerate (optional) for 1 hr (this usually helps me)
7. Put dough with a tablespoon a little bit apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten slightly with palm.
8. Bake until cookies are flatter, light brown, for about 8-14 minutes (depending on your oven)
9. Cool on sheet for a few minutes, then remove and place on cooling rack.
10. Enjoy!
