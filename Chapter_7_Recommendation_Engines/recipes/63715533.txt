Title:
BOP Energy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Old Fashioned Rolled Oats
1/2 cup ground Cacao Nibs
1/2 cup ground flaxseed
1/2 cup unsweetened coconut
1/2 cup almond butter
1/2 cup agave nectar
1/4 tsp sea salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Add all ingredients in a medium sized bowl. Mix until evenly combined and moist. You can add more or less of the agave nectar to suit your taste. Refrigerate for approximately 30 minutes. Remove and roll into 1" balls and place into air tight container with wax paper in between layers. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
kolinka
Description: 
I am an esthetician and often don't have time to eat in between appointments. Creating this recipe allowed me to combine healthy foods with cacao nibs which I dearly love to use in any recipe as the health benefits are phenomenal!
