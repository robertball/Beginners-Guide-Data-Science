Title:
Cheesecake Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup butter, melted
Cheesecake
5 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
5 eggs
2 egg yolks
1 3/4 cups confectioners sugar
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
DO NOT preheat oven.
Mix the graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 cup of the white sugar, and the melted butter together. Press mixture into the bottom of one 10 inch springform pan.
In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, vanilla, eggs and egg yolks; mix until smooth. Add the 1 3/4 cups confectioners sugar, the flour and the heavy cream. Blend until smooth. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake at 200 degrees F (100 degrees C) for 3 hours 10 minutes. Turn oven off and leave in oven for 1 hour, after baking time with oven door partially open. Let cheesecake cool, then refrigerate for 24 hours.
