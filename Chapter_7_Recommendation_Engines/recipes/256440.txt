Title:
Purple Power Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4272742.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bananas
5 large kale leaves, stems removed
1 1/2 cups frozen blueberries
1/2 cup almond milk
1/2 cup water
3 tablespoons ground flax seeds
2 dates, pitted, or more to taste

Directions:
Combine bananas, kale, blueberries, almond milk, water, ground flax seeds, and dates in a blender; blend until smooth.
