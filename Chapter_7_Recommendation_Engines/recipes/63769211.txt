Title:
Filet Mignon with Rich Balsamic Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (4 ounce) filet mignon steaks
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper to taste
salt to taste
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup dry red wine

Directions:
Sprinkle freshly ground pepper over both sides of each steak, and sprinkle with salt to taste.
Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add balsamic vinegar and red wine. Cover, and cook for 4 minutes, until sauce thickens.
Heat a separate nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.  Place steaks in hot pan, and cook for 1 minute on each side, or until browned. Cover, and cook for about 4 minutes on each side, medium rare: 145-150°F, Medium: 150-165°F.
Remove steaks to two warmed plates, spoon one tablespoon of balsamic glaze over each, and serve immediately.
