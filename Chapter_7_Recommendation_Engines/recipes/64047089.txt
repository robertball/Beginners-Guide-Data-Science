Title:
Italian Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lb ground beef
1 egg
1/2 cup diced onion
3/4 cup crushed saltine crackers
12 oz can tomato sauce (1-1/2 cups)
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine ground beef, egg, onion, crackers, oregano, salt, pepper and half of the tomato sauce.  Spread on a greased 10x12 inch pan and top with cheese. Roll up or pat like a jelly roll and seal edges.  Bake for 55 minutes.  Pour remainder of tomato sauce over meat and bake 10 minutes longer.
