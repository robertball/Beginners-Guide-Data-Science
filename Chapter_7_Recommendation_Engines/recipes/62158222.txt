Title:
Mahi Mahi salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs of Mahi Mahi (boiled)
1/2 cup Mayonnaise
4-6 Limes
1 small onion,diced
1 small green pepper, diced
Cayenne Pepper
Salt
Black Pepper

Directions:
Boil Mahi Mahi for 5 minutes or until white and flaky.Let cool. In large bowl mix together mayonnaise with onions,green pepper and dry seasonings. Squeeze in limes to preferred taste.
In seperate bowl flake fish using a fork. Add to prepared ingredients.Mix together well. Chill in refrigerator. Serve on crackers

Submitter: 
manda85
Description: 
A wonderful fish salad that can be served as an appetizer with crackers or great for an afternoon snack
