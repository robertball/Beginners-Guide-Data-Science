Title:
Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breast with Bread Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 4oz Chicken Breast
1/2 Cup Chicken Gravy
1 Cup PEPPERIDGE FARM Classic Style seasoned suffing
Half package of frozen Spinach, (5oz)
Salt, 1 dash
Pepper, black, 1 dash or lemon pepper seasoning

Directions:
Directions
Fille' the chicken breast and prepare for stuffing
Season with salt and pepper or seasoning or your choice. then add Lawry's 30 min marinade. Set aside
In a small bowl,
1 cup of stuffing mix
1/2 cup of gravy mix (prepared in advance)
1/2 box of spinach (already thawed)
Blend it altogether till all the bread is nice and moist. take chicken and suff the middle of each and fold over. Top left over stuffing over the chicken, cover with foil and bake 350 degrees for 60 mins
Number of Servings: 2
