Title:
Spinach Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922538.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 Oz pkg Of Cream Cheese
1 500 ml tub of Thick Sour Cream
1 Lg Garlic bulb -Roasted
1 8 oz Pkg Fresh Pre-washed Spinach-Chopped
1/4 c Diced Green Onion
1 Small Can of Finely Diced Artichoke
2 Cups of Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 Cup Old Cheddar Cheese- Shredded

Directions:
Let The Cream Cheese stand at Room Temperature for 30 Min. Meanwhile, Cut The Garlic in Half drizzle with oil and bake at 300 for 15 min or until the garlic is soft when poked with a fork. Remove from oven and let cool remove garlic from skin and puree with your fork.set Cheddar Cheese aside thats the topping.I use my kitchen Aid mixer for this recipe but a handheld mixer would be fine.blend cream cheese and sourcream together . add remaining ingredients 1 at a time while constantly blending on medium speed. Pour into a non-Greased 12 inch glass/stone baking dish. Sprinkle with Cheddar Cheese Bake in a 300degree oven for 15-20 min or until edges are bubbly. Serve Hot with Whole wheat Tortilla Chips.
