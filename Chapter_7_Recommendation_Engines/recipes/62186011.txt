Title:
Tamale rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/838956.jpg
Ingredients:
2 16oz cans of tamales
3 tsp chili powder
4 cups beef broth
2 cups water
3 dried bay leaves
3 cups of long grain parboiled rice
sharp cheddar(shredded)optional
green onions (sliced)optional

Directions:
Put both cans of tamales in a large pot and mash with potato masher. Add chili powder, beef broth, water, and bay leaves.
Set on high and bring to a boil.
When boiling add rice turn to low and set timer for 20 minutes.
After the timer goes off turn off eye and let it sit covered for 5 minutes.
Plate and add cheddar and green onions if desired.
