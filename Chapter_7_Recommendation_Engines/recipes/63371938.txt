Title:
One Pot Spanish Chicken and Yellow Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 8 oz. package of Vigo Yellow Rice
2/3 c. frozen peas
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2-1/3 c. water
3 T olive oil

Directions:
1. Add the onion, garlic and olive oil to a large pot, saute for 2 minutes on medium heat
2. Add chicken and cook another 4 minutes, flipping halfway
3. Add water and bring to a boil
4. Stir in rice, cover and reduce to medium heat for 20 minutes
5. Add peas on top (still frozen!), and cook for another 2 minutes. Mix it up and voila!

Submitter: 
Kel Kel
Description: 
An easy one pot meal that tastes gourmet for nights you just don't feel like cooking!
