Title:
Herb Italian Bread Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729854.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
2 oz of Crisco
14 ounces of warm water
1 1/2 teaspoons of sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
2 1/4 (One 7g Packet) teaspoons yeast
1 teaspoon of Basel
1 teaspoon of Oregano
1 teaspoon of Rosemary
1 teaspoon of Parsley
2 cloves of Garlic
2 tablespoons of olive oil

Directions:
If your using a bread machine, Plug-in your bread machine, and get it setup.
Mix warm water sugar together allow to proof, the yeast should start to multiply and start foaming at the top.
If your using a bread machine you can just dump everything in the bread maker at this point, allow for longer raise, and select dark mode, and then let the bread maker do the rest.
Without A Bread Machine
Mix all ingredients together by hand, and kneed, or mixer with a dough hook, for about 10 mins.
Allow the dough to rise for about 45 mins in a lightly greased bowl, turning the dough to coat the dough ball, this will prevent drying of the outside dough, and then cover the bowl, put in a warm place
punch down, put dough into a loaf pan, then Allow the dough to rise for about 60 mins, place in a warm place.
Pre-heat oven to 375Â° F.
Bake for about 40 mins, or until golden brown.
You can also cook this without a bread pan, on a baking stone.
