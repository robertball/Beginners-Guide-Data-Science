Title:
Chocolate Ricotta Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz unsweetened chocolate, melted
1 pound ricotta cheese
1 tsp vanilla
1/3 cup honey

Directions:
Blend melted chocolate,cheese, vanilla and honey in blender until smooth.
Pour into desert cups or glasses and chill.
Optional. Top with whipping cream.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
This 4-ingredient mousse is very rich and oh so easy to make!
