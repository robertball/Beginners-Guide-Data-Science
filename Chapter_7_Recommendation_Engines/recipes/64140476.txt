Title:
Spicy Meatload Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1365601.jpg
Ingredients:
1	lb ground beef, extra lean
8	ounce can petite diced tomatoes
2.5	oz chilie, can
1/2	cup bell pepper, chopped
1	tbsp garlic
1/2	cup chopped onion
1	tsp dried red peppers
1 Egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Blend can tomatoes if they are whole. Drain some of the water.
Finely chop chilies.
Mix all ingredients well.
Place in meatloaf pan.
Cook until center of meatloaf is at least 160 degrees.
