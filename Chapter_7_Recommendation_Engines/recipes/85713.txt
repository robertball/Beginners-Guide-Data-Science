Ingredients:
1/2 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI速 Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri mix
1/2 cup BACARDI速 Rum
1 cup vanilla ice cream
5 cups ice
Strawberries for garnish
Whipped cream for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri mix, BACARDI(R) Rum, ice cream and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with fresh strawberries and whipped cream.
