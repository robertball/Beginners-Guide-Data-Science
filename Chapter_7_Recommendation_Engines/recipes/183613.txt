Title:
Baked Spaghetti Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/183611.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
1 (14 ounce) jar Newman's Own Tomato and Roasted Garlic Pasta Sauce
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta until al dente and heat up Newman's Own pasta sauce.
Combine together in pan and then drop circular "cakes" of spaghetti onto a tin-foil-lined baking sheet.
Top with parmesan cheese and bake in oven at 350 degrees for about 15 minutes or until slightly crispy.
