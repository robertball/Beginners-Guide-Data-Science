Title:
Jims Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound bulk Italian sausage

Directions:
Combine the beef and Italian sausage.
Form into desired sized burgers.
Grill (cook) as you usually grill your burgers.
Serve with the normal burger toppings; cheese (I like Swiss or Provolone on these burgers), lettuce, tomatoes, pickles and onions.

Description: 
This makes a nice flavorful burger.
