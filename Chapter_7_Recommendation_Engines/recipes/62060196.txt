Title:
Shrimp & Veggie Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb shrimp peeled and deveined
1/4 cup lime juice
cooking spray
4 jalapenos (2 seeded)
6 hot banana peppers (4 seeded)
1 small to medium onion
1 cup sliced summer squash
2 cups okra
2 cans diced tomatoes with juice
1 TBSP tomato paste
1/4 tsp dried thyme

Directions:
Put shrimp in a ziplock bag with the lime juice and turn to coat shrimp. Spray a saute pan. In food processor chop both kinds of peppers, onion and okra. On med-hi heat saute mixture 3-5 minutes, stirring so it doesn't stick. Add tomatoes and squash. Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 10 minutes. Add shrimp making sure it is all covered by the sauce. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 5 minutes or until shrimp is pink.
