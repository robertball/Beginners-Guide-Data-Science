Title:
No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/926269.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/4 pound butter (NO substitutions)
3 tbsp cocoa powder
3 cups quick cook oatmeal
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa powder in saucepan.
Bring to a boil.
Remove from heat and stir in remaining ingredients.
Beat until well blended.
Drop by teaspoonful onto waxed paper.
Let stand until firm (about 10 minutes).
Store in refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Beth Mower Dodge
Description: 
A great combination of peanut butter and chocolate. All it involves is a saucepan and some stirring. My dad always called these Bird Turds because of how they look. We always thought that was hilarious as kids.
