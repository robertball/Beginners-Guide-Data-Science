Title:
Michigander Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz coleslaw mix
2 tbl minced onion
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper, to taste
1 1/2 tbl white wine vinegar
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup buttermilk
2 1/2 tbl lemon juice
1/2 tsp poppyseeds if desired

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.
Chill at least 2 hours.
Serve chilled.
