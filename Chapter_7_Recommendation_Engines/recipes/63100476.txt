Title:
Oreo Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of instant chocolate pudding
1 package of oreo's
1 pre-made pie shell
1 package of whipped cream(optional)

Directions:
Crush up the Oreo's
Make the pudding. mix with the Oreo's. chill for 10 min
Bake the pie shell. Once it is baked fill the shell with oreo filling.
Top with whipped cream(optional)
