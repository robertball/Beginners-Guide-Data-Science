Title:
Momma's Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 tbsp ground sage
2 tbsp EVOO
2 tbsp butter
1 medium green bell pepper, coarsely chopped
2 medium onions, coarsely chopped
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms (3 oz)
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves (1 1/2 tsp chopped, if using fresh)
1/4 teaspoon dried basil leaves (1 tsp chopped, if using fresh)
up to 1/2 teaspoon salt, optional
Grated Parmesan cheese, if desired

Directions:
Combine flour & sage.
Coat chicken with flour mixture. Shake off excess.
In 12-inch skillet, heat oil and butter over medium-high heat.
Cook chicken in oil/butter 15 to 20 minutes or until brown on all sides; drain on paper toweling.
Stir bell pepper, onion, and garlic in remaining oil/butter in pan. Cook over medium heat approximately 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, mushrooms, oregano, and basil to pan. Stir to combine. (Add salt, to taste.)
Return chicken to pan. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer 30 to 40 minutes.
Serve chicken over spaghetti, topped with sauce and grated parmesan cheese.
