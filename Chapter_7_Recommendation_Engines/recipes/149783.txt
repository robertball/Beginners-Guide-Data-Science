Title:
'Bananas Foster' Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983508.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packets SPLENDA® Flavors for Coffee, Caramel
3/4 cup reduced fat milk
1 tablespoon orange juice
1/4 teaspoon banana extract
1 (2 inch) slice of banana

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except banana, in a microwaveable bowl or cup.  Heat in microwave 50 to 60 seconds or until hot.  Pour into a blender and add banana.  Cover tightly and blend 15 to 20 seconds or until banana is pureed.  Pour into serving glass and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Take the flavors of a classic Bananas Foster and blend them up in this satisfying caramel and banana smoothie. This quick and easy drink is perfect for breakfast or a quick afternoon pick-me-up.
