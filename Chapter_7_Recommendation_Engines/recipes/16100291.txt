Title:
Souper Easy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
12 oz Monterrey Jack cheese, shredded
8 flour tortillas
1 can cream of mushroom soup
8 oz. sour cream
1 small can chopped green chili peppers

Directions:
Mix chicken and 2/3 of the cheese together and divide evenly among the tortillas.  Roll and place seam side down in 13x9 baking dish.  Mix soup, sour cream, and chili peppers together.  Pour over the top of the enchiladas and top with remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 for about 20-25 minutes until bubbly.
Hint: if you like your food spicy, add some chopped pickled jalapenos to the chili peppers and substitute pepper jack cheese!

A great tasting dish that's quick and easy.  I get many requests for this dish and it is always the first to go at our church pot luck suppers.
