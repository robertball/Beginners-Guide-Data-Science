Title:
Easy and Yummy Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14 oz cans sweet peas, well drained
1 2.25 oz can sliced black olives, drained
1 or 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1/4 cup very thinly sliced onion
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, diced or shredded
1/2 cup mayonaise
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Toss all in large bowl to combine. Refrigerate at least 4 hours, overnight is best.

Submitter: 
NICOSMOMDEE
Description: 
My friend's Pea Salad is so fast and simple, it earned a place immediately in my recipe box!It's easy to vary the cheeses with what you have on hand, or toss in some crumbled bacon or garlic to jazz it up, too.
