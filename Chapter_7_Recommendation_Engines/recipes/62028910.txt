Title:
SOUR CREAM LEMON PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 baked 8 inch pie shell.
1 cup sugar (white)
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/4 cup margarine
3 egg yolks
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 tbsp. grated peel
1 cup milk
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Mix sugar & cornstarch in pan.  Add margarine, egg yolks, lemon juice, peel and milk.  Cook over medium heat - stirring constantly until thickened.  Cover and cool COMPLETEY and then add 1 cup sour cream.  Blend and put in baked shell.
