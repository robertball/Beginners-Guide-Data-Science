Title:
Easy Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977192.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 1/2 cups firmly packed brown sugar
3/4 cup canola oil
1/4 cup water
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Make a well and add brown sugar, canola oil, water, eggs and vanilla. Mix until smooth, fold in raisins.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto non-stick cookie sheet. Bake 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
The Canola Info Virtual Kitchen
Description: 
A healthy twist on an old standard. Replacing solid fats with canola oil in baked goods lowers total fat as well as trans and saturated fats.
