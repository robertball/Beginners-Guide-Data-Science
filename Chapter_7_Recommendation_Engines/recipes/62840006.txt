Title:
Crock pot Stewed Tomatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 6 to 8 ripe tomatoes
* 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
* 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
* 3/4 cup chopped celery
* 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
* 3 tablespoons sugar (more or less)
* 1 Tablespoon minced garlic
* 1 Teaspoon oregano
* 1 bay leaf
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Core tomatoes, place in boiling water for about 15 to 20 seconds, then into ice water to cool quickly, peel tomatoes. Cut tomatoes in wedges.
In Crock Pot, combine all ingredients. Cover and cook on low 8-9 hours. Remove bay leaf
Place in canning Jars and Can as per canning Directions

Submitter: 
Darin Terwilliger
Description: 
Home canned Stewed tomatoes, easy to do and great in Chili
