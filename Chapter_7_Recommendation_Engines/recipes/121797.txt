Title:
Strawberry-Banana Gelatin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980234.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package strawberry Jell-OÂ®
1 cup boiling water
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen sweetened sliced strawberries, partially thawed
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, undrained
1 cup mashed firm bananas
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
2 cups sour cream
2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a bowl, dissolve gelatin in water. Stir in strawberries, pineapple, bananas and nuts. Pour half of the mixture into a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. dish. Refrigerate for 1 hour or until set. Set the remaining gelatin mixture aside. Combine the sour cream, sugar and vanilla; mix well. Spread over the chilled gelatin. Spoon remaining gelatin mixture over top. Chill overnight.

Submitter: 
Betty  Claycomb
Description: 
Our family gatherings wouldn't be the same without this classic gelatin salad with its festive fruit layers. Since it stays nice and firm, it travels well and is ideal for taking to covered-dish suppers and church potlucks.
