Title:
Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
two cups of cooked pumpkin mashed
three eggs
pinch of salt
1/3 cup of heavy cream
1/4 cup of brown sugar
one cup of granulated sugar
one cup and a half of butter
one ts of cinnamon
one ts of nutmeg
one ts of vanilla

Directions:
combine all above ingrediates until light and fluffy then pour into unbaked pie shell bake at 350 degrees for one hour and 15 minutes are until golden brown.
