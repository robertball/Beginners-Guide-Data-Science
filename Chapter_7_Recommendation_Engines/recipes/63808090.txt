Title:
Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. butter
1 T. olive oil
1/2 c. onion, finely chopped
1/4 c. celery, finely chopped
1/2 t. minced garlic
1 16 oz. can white beans, rinsed
1 16 oz. can tomatoes
1 1/2 c. water
1/2 lb. mild Italian sausage
salt and pepper to taste
2/3 c. carrots, sliced
2/3 c. green beans, diced
1/2 c. cabbage, finely shredded
1 med. zucchini. cubed
1/4 c. small shell pasta
1/4 c. fresh basil, chopped or 1/2 T. dried basil

Directions:
Heat butter and olive oil in a large kettle. Cook onion and celery until transparent. Add garlic, white beans tomatoes and water. Bring to a boil and simmer 15 minutes.
Add remaining ingredient except pasta shells. Simmer gently about 45 minutes. Add more water or tomato juice if a thinner consistency is desired.
Add pasta about 10 minutes before serving, or omit for now if you are planning to freeze the soup for later use.
Serve with grated parmesan cheese and breadsticks.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
A Walker family favorite! We usually make a double batch. Freezes well, just omit the pasta until you thaw to heat and serve.
