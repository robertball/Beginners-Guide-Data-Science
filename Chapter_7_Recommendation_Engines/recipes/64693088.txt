Ingredients:
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
2 large cloves garlic, finely minced
1-2 tablespoons Avocado oil, enough to cover the bottom of the pot
1 cup long grain rice
1/2 cup tomato sauce
1 1/2 cup chicken stock, low sodium
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cumin
pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
Press the saute function and adjust to med.
When the pot is hot add the oil and onion.
Saute until soft, about 4-5 minutes.
Add the garlic and cooked until fragrant, 30 seconds.
Add the rice and mix until the rice is coated and slightly browned.
Use the stock to deglaze the pot.
Add the tomato sauce, salt, cayenne and cumin and mix.
Put the lid on the pot and close the vent.
Use the manual function and adjust the timer to 7 minutes.
When the timer goes off use the quick release method.
Carefully remove the lid and stir to mix.
