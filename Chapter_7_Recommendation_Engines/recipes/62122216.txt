Title:
Date Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Chopped Dates
1/2 cup Margarine
3/4 cup White Sugar
2 1/2 cups Rice Krispies
1 cup chopped pecans
2 cups Coconut or Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Boil chopped dates, margarine & sugar for 3 minutes stirring constantly in a pot.
Add Rice Krispies & pecans to pot and let cool a bit.
Make into small balls and roll in coconut or powdered sugar depending on preference.

Submitter: 
Jasmine Gonzalez
Description: 
A wonderful mixture of dates, rice krispies and nuts. A definite for people who love dates!
