Title:
decar48's enchilada sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small or 1/2 of a large habanero, seeded & chopped
4 Tbsp flour
1/4 c cooking oil
2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp salt
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups hot water
1 8oz can tomato sauce

Directions:
Brown flour in large fry pan, then add the spices. Add the oil and stir constantly. When smooth add the water slowly, stirring constantly. Finally add the tomato sauce and simmer til desired thickness is achieved.
