Title:
Shamrock Shakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972184.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Vanilla Ice Cream
1 1/4 Cups Milk
1/4 to 1/2 tsp Mint Extract
9 Drops Green Food Coloring
Optional:
Chocolate Syrup
Whip Cream
Green Sugar

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together until smooth.
Drizzle some chocolate syrup around your glass.
Pour shake into glass.
Top with whipped cream and green sugar.

Submitter: 
Julie Kimball Fitzgerald
Description: 
This has become a St. Patrick's Day tradition in our house. The kids love it (and so do I).
