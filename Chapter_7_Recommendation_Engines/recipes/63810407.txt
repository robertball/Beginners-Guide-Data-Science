Title:
Low Calorie "Ice Cream" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana, cut into slices
2 T peanut butter
Splash of milk (almond, cow, or soy)
1 T cocoa (optional)

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a food processor and process until smooth. It comes out creamy JUST like ice cream and is SO good! Perfect if you are watching what you eat.
