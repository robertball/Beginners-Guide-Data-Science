Title:
Gluten Free Moist Choc Chip Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Tbls unsweetened applesauce
1.5 C sugar OR 1.25 C Agave OR Stevia equivelant
generous 1/2 C Fat Free Greek Plain Yogurt
1 egg or 2 egg whites
3 ripe bananas (puree in food processor)
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
2 C Red Mill Gluten Free all purpose baking flour
2 tsp. Xantan Gum
1/2 to 1 C MINI chocolate chips
*Optional 1 C chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Prehead oven to 350F, grease loaf or line muffin pan
Cream sugar (or substitue) and applesauce, add egg and yogurt, bananas and vanilla, mix well.
Mix dry ingredients seperately first (food processor works well).
Beat flour mixture into mixture banana quickly at low speed, do not over beat. (90 sec max)
Fold in mini chips with large spoon.
Spoon batter into pan, no more than 1/3 full.
Bake until toothpick/knife is inserted in middle and comes out clean/trace amount crumbs,1 large loaf: check after 45 min,4 mini loafs (or muffins): check after 25 min
** Will be very moist, freezing is ok if servings are wrapped seperately

Submitter: 
celenacollins
Description: 
Ya, this is really moist, and hard to tell its gluten free for sure!  Perfect with coffee or as a desert, this banada bread will melt in your mouth. Be sure to use Mini chips, as the big ones just sink to the bottom while baking.
