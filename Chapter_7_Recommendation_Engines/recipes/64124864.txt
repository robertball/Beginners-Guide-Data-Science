Title:
Hearty Zucchini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped (1 1/2 cups)
2 minced garlic cloves
2 tsp curry powder
1 large potato, peeled and diced
2 medium zucchini, diced
4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
In medium saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion; cook about 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add garlic and curry powder; cook, stirring until fragrant (about 1 minute).
Add remaining ingredients; heat to boiling. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
To make this recipe vegetarian, use vegetable broth instead of the chicken broth.
