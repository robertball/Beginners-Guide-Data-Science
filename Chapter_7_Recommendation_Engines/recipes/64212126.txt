Title:
Irish Cream Liqueur II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 6 ounces milk chocolate
2 (14 ounce) cans sweetened condensed milk
1 (750 milliliter) bottle Irish whiskey
1/4 teaspoon instant coffee granules
2 (12 fluid ounce) cans evaporated milk
2 1/2 cups heavy cream
1 tsp vanilla
Scant 1/4 tsp almond extract

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, heat the chocolate and the sweetened condensed milk over medium low heat, stir with a wire whisk frequently until chocolate is melted.  Once chocolate is completely melted, take the saucepan off the heat.
Pour whiskey into a large bowl.  Add the instant coffee granules and whisk until dissolved. Stir in the evaporated milk, cream, vanilla, and the almond extract.  Slowly pour the chocolate/sweetened condensed milk mixture into the bowl, stirring with the whisk constantly while pouring.
Pour mixture into 3 (750 ml) bottles, seal and store in refrigerator. Best if kept at least 1 month before using (shake the bottles at least once a week).  Shake bottle before serving.
