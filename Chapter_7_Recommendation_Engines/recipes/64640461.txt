Title:
Nana's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box of lasagna
1 large can of crushed tomatoes
1 small can of stewed tomatoes
1 large jar of marinara sauce
Grated Mozzarella
Grated sharp Cheddar
RicottaCheese mixed with 1 egg
Parmesan Cheese (Toppping)
2lbs of Ground Beef
Italian Seasons
Salt
Pepper
Garlic
Cooking Red Wine
Chopped Onions

Directions:
Mix Ricotta and Egg
Sautee onions and garlic
Brown ground beef with seasonings
Mix all Tomato ingredients
Spray Cooking dish with Pam
Light layer of sauce on bottom
Layer Noodles and Meat and Cheese 2 layers
Include Ricotta on to layer
Bake for 350 for 1hr
