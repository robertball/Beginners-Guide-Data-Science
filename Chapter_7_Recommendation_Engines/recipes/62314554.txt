Title:
Asian Chicken Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package boneless and skinless chicken breasts, cubed
1 package multi-grain spaghetti
1 medium cucumber, julienne
1 cup matchstick carrots
1 cup honey teriyaki marinade
2 tbl. sesame oil
1 tbl. teriyaki sauce
1 tbl. soy sauce
2 tsp. shiraca (Asian hot sauce)
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. ground ginger

Directions:
Marinade chicken for at least 30 minutes in honey teryiaki marinade.  Cook over medium high heat until cooked through.
Meanwhile, heat water for pasta.  Cook pasta until done (15 to 20 minutes).  Drain pasta and return to pot.  Add sesame oil, teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, garlic powder, ground ginger, carrots and cucumbers.  Mix well.  Taste the paste and adjust seasonings to your liking.
Add chicken once you have the noodles seasoned and mix well.  Serve hot.
