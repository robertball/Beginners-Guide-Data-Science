Title:
Mexican taco meat biscuits - a delicious dish for breakfast! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1375965.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Can Pillsbury Grands Homestyle/Southern Style biscuits (16.3 oz)
1 cup Taco Meat (cold)
1 cup Shredded Monterrey Jack Cheese
1/2 cup Sour Cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F on Bake.
Lightly spray 13"x9" baking dish with oil.
Open biscuits and arrange in middle with edges touching.
Using a sharp knife, make two 1.5 inch crossing slices in center of each biscuit but without cutting all the way through.
Ladle 1 tablespoon of cold taco meat into center of each biscuit.
Place 1 tablespoon of shredded Monterrey Jack cheese over each mound of meat.
Place into oven and cook for 20-24 minutes, or until biscuit edges are golden brown.
Remove from oven, and allow to cool for 5 minutes.
Garnish with dollop of Sour Cream and whatever other toppings you desire.

Description: 
Got about a cup of leftover taco meat and a can of biscuits? Then here's a great, simple, and delicious way to make a breakfast that everyone will truly love!
