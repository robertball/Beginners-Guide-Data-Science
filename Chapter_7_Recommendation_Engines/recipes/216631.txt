Title:
Strawberry-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988341.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen strawberries
2 medium bananas
1 1/2 cups Silk Pure Almond Original or Vanilla
1 cup orange juice
1 tablespoon honey, or to taste (if using Silk Pure Almond Original)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
