Title:
Delicious and Nutritious Whole Wheat Banana and Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2 c whole wheat flour
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup coconut oil, melted and cooled
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/3 cup pure maple syrup
2 very ripe bananas, mashed
1 cup blueberries, fresh or frozen
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Grease 12 cup muffin tin
Blend whole wheat flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder in a medium mixing bowl. make a well in the center. Add eggs, coconut oil, applesauce and maple syrup. stirl by hand until just combined. Add mashed bananas, blueberries, and nuts, if using... and mix until just incorporated.
Divide batter evenly into prepared muffin tin.
Bake at 425 deg. for 6-7 mins. Reduce heat to 350 and continue baking for another 15 mins. Check center muffin with a toothpick for doneness.
Cool in pan for five mins. before transferring to a cooling rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
Chalk full of sweet ripened bananas and juicy blueberries. These muffins are great for a mid-day snack or as part of your breakfast. Made with whole wheat flour and sweetened with pure maple syrup make these moist muffins delicious, nutritious and deeply satisfying!
