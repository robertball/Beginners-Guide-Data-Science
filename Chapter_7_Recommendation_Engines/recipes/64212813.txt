Title:
Zesty Slow Cooker Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground turkey
1 can tomato paste
1 can stewed tomatoes
1 jar marinara sauce
2 cups Trader Joe's Roasted Red Pepper & Tomato Soup
1 can kidney beans
1 can chili beans
1 cup green bell pepper, diced
3/4 cup onion, chopped
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Brown turkey in skillet and place into slow cooker. Mix all other ingredients into the slow cooker and cook on low for 5-8 hours.
