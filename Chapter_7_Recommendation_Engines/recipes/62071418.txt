Title:
Paprika Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons of butter
1 onion, sliced thin
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
4 boneless pork chops
1/3 cup drained sauerkraut
1/3 cup sour cream

Directions:
1. Preheat broiler
2. Melt butter in large skillet on medium-high heat.  Add onions and cook until tender and golden.
3. Meanwhile, season pork chops with garlic salt, pepper, and 1 teaspoon of paprika.  Broil pork chops 5 inches from heat for 10 to 12 minutes, turning once, until almost done.
4. Combine remaining paprika, sauerkraut, and sour cream with cooked onions.  Spread mixture over pork chops and broil for 1 or 2 more minutes or until heated through.

Submitter:

Description: 
Low Carb!  Pork chops seasoned with paprika served over a blend of sour cream,, sauerkraut, onions and paprika. Don't be thrown off by the ingredients!  I got this recipe from my aunt and was skeptical at first, but now it is one of my family's favorites.
