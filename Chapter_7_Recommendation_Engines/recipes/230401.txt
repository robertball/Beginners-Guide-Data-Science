Title:
Quick Christmas Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/934219.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 (16 ounce) packages mixed baby salad greens
1 1/2 cups pecans
8 ounces dried cherries
1/4 pound Parmesan cheese, shaved
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing, or to taste

Directions:
Mix baby salad greens, pecans, dried cherries, and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl. Drizzle vinaigrette over the salad; toss to coat evenly.

Submitter: 
Cathy Gonzales
Description: 
This is a super quick salad with for those hectic holiday times. It has that Michigan twist on it with the dried cherries.
