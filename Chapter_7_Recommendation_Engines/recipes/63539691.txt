Title:
Red Wine Vinegar Reduction Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup red wine vinegar
2 cinnamon sticks
4 cloves
1/4 cup white sugar
Zest of one lemon

Directions:
In a sauce pan combine all ingredients and simmer for 10 minutes.  Let it cool for 30 minutes and serve with pork, fruit or potatoes.

Submitter: 
Melissa Vincent
Description: 
This is fantastic marinade to add to any meat while cooking.
