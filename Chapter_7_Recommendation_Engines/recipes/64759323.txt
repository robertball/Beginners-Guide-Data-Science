Title:
Chocolate Zucchini Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine
1/2 cup vegetable oil (I like canola) or coconut
1 3/4 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup buttermilk (or sour regular milk with some vinegar)
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. soda
4 tbsp. cocoa powder
1/2 tsp. each cinnamon & cloves
2 cups shredded zucchini
1/2 cup + 1/4 cup chocolate chips, optional, separated

Directions:
Cream first 6 ingredients together.
Add the next 5 & stir to blend.
Add final 2 and spread in greased 9" x 13" cake pan.
Top with additional 1/4 cup choc. chips.
Bake @ 350 x 35-40 min. Cool.
Frost with chocolate or cream cheese icing or a glaze, if desired.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Some extra flavor in this moist chocolate cake from unexpected cinnamon & cloves.
