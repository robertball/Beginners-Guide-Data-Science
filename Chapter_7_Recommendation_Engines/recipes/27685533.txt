Title:
Rasberry Pastries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-1/2 oz. (sheet of) frozen puff pastry
rasberry jam
sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Unfold 1 sheet of pastry onto a cutting board, cut crosswise into 5 equal strips.
Spread 4 strips with jam and sprinkle with sugar.  Stack coated strips; top with remaining strip.  Freeze until firm, 10-15 minutes.
Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.  With a serated knife, slice stack crosswise 1/4" thick, placing slices on baking sheets as you work, 4" apart.  Sprinkle with 2 TBS. sugar.
Bake until puffed and golden, 15-20 minutes.  Cool slightly on baking sheet, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
