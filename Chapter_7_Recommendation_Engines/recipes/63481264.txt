Title:
Greek Fishermen's soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of mixed white fish and shellfish such as cod, flounder, halibut monkfish, see bass whiting and peeled shrimp
2/3 sup olive oil
2 large onions, sliced
2 celery stalks, sliced thinly
2 garlic cloves, chopped
2/3 cup white wine
4 canned tomatoes, chopped
pared rind of 1 orange
1 tsp chopped fresh thyme
2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
2 bay leaves
salt and pepper
lemon wedges, to serve
croutons, to garnish

Directions:
Cut the fish into fairly large, thick, serving portions, discarding any skin.  Heat the oil in a large pan, add the onion, celery, and garlic and fry for 5 minutes, until softened.
Add the fish and shrimp to the pan, then add the wine, tomatoes, pared orange rind, thyme, parsley, bay leaves, salt, pepper and enough cold water to cover.  Bring to a boil, then simmer, uncovered, for 15 minutes.
Serve the soup hot, with lemon wedges, and garnished with croutons.
