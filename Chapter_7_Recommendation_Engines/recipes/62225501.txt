Title:
One-rise Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping:
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup whipping cream (no substitute)
Rolls:
3 - 31/2 cups flour
1 pkg yeast
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1 cup hot water
2 tbs. soft butter
1 egg
Filling:
1/2 cup soft butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 tbsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix brown sugar and cream in an unreased 9 X 13 inch baking pan.
In large bowl mix 1 1/2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, salt, water and butter.  Beat 3 minutes at medium speed. Stir in egg and 1 1/2 to 2 cups more flour. Knead for 1 minute or until smooth. Roll into a 7 X 15 inch rectangle.
For filling, combine butter, sugar and cinnamon. Spread filling over dough. Starting at the long side, roll tightly, seal edges. Cut into 16 to 20 rolls. Place cut side down on topping mixture. Cover, let rise until double, about 1 hour. Bake at 400 for 20-25 minutes. Cool 10 munutes before turning out on tray.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
This is a great recipe for Christmas morning as it only rises once so you have time to make them and have them fresh from the oven for brunch.
