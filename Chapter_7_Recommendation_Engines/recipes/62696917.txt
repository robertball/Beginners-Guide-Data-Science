Title:
Oven Barbecued Round Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds beef round steak, 1 inch thick, cut into strips
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup onion
3/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup water
3/4 cup cider vinegar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon ground mustard
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 teaspoon pepper
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown beef and onions in oil over medium-high heat; drain. Add ketchup, vinegar, water, brown sugar, w. sauce, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer until thickens. Place in a baking dish and bake at 350 for 2 hours.
