Title:
Turkey Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
5 T flour
2 cups turkey stock
1 cup milk
2 cups cooked turkey, cut up
12 small white onions, cooked
1 cup diced cooked potatoes
1 10 oz. package frozen peas and carrots, cooked
salt and pepper to taste
biscuit dough

Directions:
melt butter in a saucepan, add flour and stir until smooth.
Gradually add stock and milk, stirring constantly; continue to cook and stir until thickened.
Add turkey, vegetables, salt and pepper.
Turn into a greased casserole.
Cover with biscuit dough; cut several slits.
Bake at 450 for 25-30 minutes.
Biscuit dough:  Measure 1 1/2 cups flour into a bowl  Add 1 1/2 t. baking powder, 1/2 t. salt.  Mix together.  Cut in 5 T shortening.  Gradually add about 1/2 cup milk, stirring until soft dough is formed.  Turn out on slightly floured board and knead 30 seconds,  Roll out to fit the casserole.

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
Great way to use leftover turkey.
