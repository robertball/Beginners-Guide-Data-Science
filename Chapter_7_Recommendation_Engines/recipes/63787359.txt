Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lb. cubed beef stew meat
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup finely chopped onion
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup water
1 tsp. minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 tsp. paprika
1 bouillon cube
1 pkg. onion soup mix
1 Tb. tomato paste
1/2 lb. saut√©ed mushrooms (optional)
1 container of IMO

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine the meat, soup, onion, Worcestershire sauce, seasonings, and water.
Cook on Low setting for 5 hours, or on High setting for about 3 hours. Stir in mushrooms 1/2 hr. before serving, & IMO just before serving.
Serve over noodles

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
Beef, condensed golden mushroom soup, chopped onion, and Worcestershire sauce simmer in a slow cooker for easy beef stroganoff.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I
