Title:
Chocolate Velvet Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Splenda
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/3 cup egg beaters
4 cups fat free half and half
1/3 cup semisweet chocolate chips
pinch of salt

Directions:
In large bowl, stir together sugar, salt and cocoa.  Add egg beaters and blend with electric mixer.  Add half and half a little at a time, beating well after each addition.  Chill mixture in refrigerator.
While cream mixture is chilling, grate chocolate chips in blender or food processor or using a rotary grater, until fine.  Stir into cream mixture.  Freeze in canister of ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions.
