Title:
Bok Choy Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Head of Bok Choy or Green Lettuce
2 Packs Ramen Noodles Crushed
3 Teaspoons of Canola oil
1/2 Cup Slivered Almonds
1 Bunch Spring Onions Chopped
For the Dressing:
1/2 Cup Canola Oil
1/4 Cup Balsamic Vinegartte Dressing
1/2 Cup Sugar
1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce

Directions:
Put oil in small frying pan. Add almonds and brown them on medium high heat and add to salad bowl when done.
Cut off very bottom of Bok Choy. Chop the green leafy and white stalk into bite size pieces. Wash after cutting and add to salad bowl.
Clean and chop the spring onions and add to salad bowl.
Crush the 2 packs of ramen noddles and add to salad bowl.
Mix the dressing in different bowl once mixed add to salad bowl.
mix all contents in bowl together and keep chilled until served.
