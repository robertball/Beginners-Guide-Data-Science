Title:
Whole Wheat Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101012.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups unbleached white whole wheat flour
1 tbsp sugar
2 tsp baking powder
½ tsp sea salt
2 eggs, separated
1¾ cup 1% milk
1 ½ Tbsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
? cup extra light olive oil
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Separate egg yolks from the whites and beat the whites in a small bowl until thick and foamy. Set aside.
In a large bowl, combine lemon juice and milk and let it sit for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt and mix with a wire whisk.
To the bowl with milk, add the sugar, egg yolks, olive oil and vanilla and stir to combine.
Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir together.  Do not over-mix.
Fold egg whites into batter.
Pour onto a hot waffle iron (use a brush to apply a little oil to the iron’s surface before each waffle).
Follow directions and cooking time for your waffle iron
Serve with pure maple syrup or other toppings of your choice.
