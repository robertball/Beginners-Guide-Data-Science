Title:
linguini with bacon and scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/971346.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. linguini
6 slices bacon, diced
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 lb sea scallops, halved horizontally or sliced in thirds if large
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/3 cup chopped italian parsley
lemon zest

Directions:
cook pasta as directed
saute bacon and onion, about 6 mins
add scallops, garlic, pepper flakes, salt and pepper, saute about 1 min
stir in wine, cook 1 min
remove from heat, stir in parsley.
top pasta with scallop mixture and add a touch of lemon zest
