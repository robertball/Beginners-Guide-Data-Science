Title:
Whole Wheat Honey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/452547.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/8 cup milk
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup honey
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3 1/8 cups whole wheat flour
1 1/2 teaspoons bread machine yeast

Directions:
Put milk and water in a pyrex cup and microwave for 1 minute.
Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order given. Select "Dough" setting, and then press Start.
Bake in a loaf pan at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
