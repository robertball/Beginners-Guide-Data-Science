Title:
Chili Brie in Sourdough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985536.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon McCormick速 Chili Powder
1/2 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Mustard
1/2 teaspoon McCormick速 Garlic Powder
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1 (1 pound) loaf round sourdough bread
1 tablespoon softened butter
1 (8 ounce) wheel Brie cheese

Directions:
Combine spices and sugar; set aside.  Cut circle in top of bread and remove bread center to make room for the Brie.
Spread butter in bread; sprinkle with 2 teaspoons of  spice mixture.  With knife, make 2-inch cuts around edge of bread at 1-inch intervals.
Remove rind from Brie and place in bread.  Sprinkle Brie with remaining spice.  Replace top of bread.  Bake on baking sheet in 350 degrees F oven 20-30 minutes. To serve, remove bread top and break into bite size pieces.  Dip bread pieces in hot Brie.
