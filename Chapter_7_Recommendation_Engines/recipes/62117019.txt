Title:
Bacon Water Chestnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of water chestnuts
2 lbs of bacon
8 heaping tbs of brown sugar
1 bottle of catsup
1 tbs vingar

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350
Cut bacon slices in to halves
Wrap a piece of bacon around each chestnut and secure with toothpick
Bake in a 9x13 pan for 3 min
Pour off grease and place in fridge over night
Mix brown sugar, catsup, and vingar
Pour over chestnuts and bake for 45 min
Place in small crock pot to keep warm through party
