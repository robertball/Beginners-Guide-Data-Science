Title:
Swedish Meatballs For The Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# lean ground beef
1/4# ground pork
1 1/2C bread crumbs
1C milk, cream or half and half
2 eggs
1 med. onion, finely chopped
1 1/2t salt
1/2-1t dillweed
1/4t allspice
1/8t nutmeg
1/8t cardamon, opt.
3T butter
1 (10oz) can beef broth
1/8t pepper
1/2C light cream

Directions:
Soak bread crumbs in milk for 5 minutes. Add ground meats, eggs, onion, 1 tsp. salt, 1/4 tsp. dillweed, allspice, nutmeg and cardamon. Mix well and refrigerate covered for 1 hour.
Shape mixture into 1-in. diameter balls and brown lightly in hot butter.
Put meatballs in crockpot as they brown. Add beef broth, pepper, light cream, remaining 1/2 tsp. salt, and remaining dillweed to crockpot.
Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours (high 1 1/2-2 1/2 hours)

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Brown the meatballs, then add to crockpot with a highly spiced sauce. Yum! Have made this with all ground beef and just as good.
