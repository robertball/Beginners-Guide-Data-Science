Title:
Canadian Multi-grain Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
?2 tsp. sugar?
?1 cup warm water?
?2 Pkgs. or 2 Tblsp. dry yeast?
?1 cup water ?
?1/3 cup uncooked 7grain or 12 grain porridge cereal ?
?½ cup sugar?
?½ cup vegetable oil?
?2 tsp. salt?
?2 cups cold water?
?5 cups whole wheat flour?
?4-5 cups all purpose flour?
?¼ cup large flake rolled oats?

Directions:
Stir sugar into warm water until dissolved.  Sprinkle yeast over sugar ?water and allow to work.?
Bring the second cup of water to a boil in a medium saucepan.  Add the ?uncooked multi-grain porridge cereal and cook until softened and thick.  ?Empty into a large mixing bowl.?
Add the sugar, vegetable oil, salt, cold water, yeast mixture and whole ?wheat flour.  Stir until well mixed.  Add all purpose flour, a cup at a time, to ?form a stiff, elastic dough.  Dough should not be dry.?
Knead well.  Cover with plastic wrap or plastic bag.  Allow to rise until ?double in bulk.  Punch down.
Divide into 4 or 5 loaves, depending on the ?size of your bread pans.  Shape into loaves.  Coat loaves in rolled oats and ?place into greased loaf pans.
Allow to rise.
Bake at 375º for 35 to 45 ?minutes.?

Submitter: 
SEAMSTERRIFIC
Description: 
A healthy, medium dense bread that slices easily and toasts well.  It is excellent with jam or jelly and makes wonderful sandwiches.  This bread freezes well and, when placed in a closed plastic bag, will keep on your counter  for up ?to 5 days without tasting stale.  ?
