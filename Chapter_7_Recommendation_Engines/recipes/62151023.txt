Title:
Caramel Stuffed French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pieces white bread.
1 egg
1/4 cup milk
2 Tbs. cinnimon/sugar mix
2 Tbs. raspberry preserves
2 Tbs. caramel sauce.
Oil for pan.
Open two pieces of  bread and smother preserves on them. Put two pieces together. (Like a sandwich)
Mix egg, milk and cinn/sugar mix all together.
Dip bread into egg mixture.
Put sandwich onto pan.
Cook for 2 min on each side.
Remove from pan and put onto dish. Drizzle with caramel sauce.

Directions:
Preheat oil in pan.
Medium high heat.
Take two pieces of bread and smother middle with raspberry preserves.
Make egg mixture with egg, milk and cinn/sugar mix.
Place bread into egg mixture make sure bread is completely covered in egg mixture.
Put into pan.
Cook 2 min on each side.
Take off pan and drizzle with caramel sauce.
Repeat with other two pieces of bread.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Fluffy french toast stuffed with raspberry preserves and drizzled with caramel.
