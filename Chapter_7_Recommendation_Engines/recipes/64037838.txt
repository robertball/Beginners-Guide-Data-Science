Title:
Sinfully Delightful Homemade Hot Cocoa Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 to 3/4 cup dark chocolate chips (53% to 60%)
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup nonfat instant milk powder
1/3 cup cocoa powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
To prepare Hot chocolate, place 1/4 cup of mix in a mug.
Heat 1 cup milk to 180 degrees (just to the point where tiny bubbles are beginning to form on sides of pan.
Pour milk over mix and stir thoroughly.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Finally, a tried and true recipe for Hot Chocolate Mix.  Original recipe called for 3/4 cup of chocolate chips ... I recommend adjusting amount to personal taste.
