Title:
Cheesy Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/4 cup butter
1 cup sour cream
1-2 tsp seasoned salt
1 cup grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
In large pot, boil potatoes until soft.  Drain water and mash.
Add butter, sour cream and seasoned salt.
Place mashed potatoes in greased baking dish and sprinkle with cheddar cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 25 minutes.
