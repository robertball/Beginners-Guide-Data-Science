Title:
Chocolate Monkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/768705.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
1/2 fluid ounce creme de cacao
1/2 fluid ounce banana liqueur
1 cup ice

Directions:
Combine the vodka, creme de cacao, banana liqueur in a shaker with ice. Shake, and strain into a martini glass.
