Title:
Quick Cranberry Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 8 ounce cans crushed pineapple
16 ounce can whole berry cranberry sauce
2 Tablespoons Splenda brown sugar blend
1/2 Teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 Teaspoon sea salt
1 to 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded, deveined and minced
3 green onions, chopped.

Directions:
Drain pineapple well; use paper towels to pat even drier.
Combine pineapple, cranberry sauce, brown sugar blend, ground ginger and sea salt in small saucepan; bring to a boil over medium heat; reduce heat and simmer, stirring to prevent scorching, for about 5 minutes, or until mixture thickens; remove from heat.
Cool for about 10 minutes, and then stir in jalapenos and green onions.
Cover and chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
empebi
Description: 
This goes together in minutes, and is actually better if prepared the day before you serve.  I use this with grilled herb pork tenderloin, served on cormeal biscuits for special appetizer occasions.
