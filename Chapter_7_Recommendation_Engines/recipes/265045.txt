Title:
Vegan Butter Bean and Mushroom Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil, or as needed
2 onions, finely chopped
2 (8 ounce) packages sliced fresh mushrooms
2 (14 ounce) cans butter beans, drained and liquid reserved
1 teaspoon dried tarragon, or to taste
1/4 cup unsweetened soy creamer, or more to taste

Directions:
Warm oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add onions; cook and stir until soft, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms and cook until soft, 6 to 8 minutes more. Add beans and reserved liquid, tarragon, and enough creamer to cover the beans. Bring to a simmer. Cook down until cream thickens, 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from heat and serve.

Submitter: 
CookingCatherine
Description: 
An easy vegan main meal that can withstand much flexibility and still turns out like it's a really special meal. This is for 4. Best served over rice or pasta for a complete meal.
