Title:
Buffy's Turtle Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of mini (traditionally shaped) pretzels (in the chips section)
1 bag of Rolos candy
1 bag of whole pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lay mini pretzels out on cookie sheet.
Place one Rolo in the center of each pretzel.
Bake for 4 minutes.
Remove and place one pecan in the melted center of the Rolo and press down.
Cool and Serve.

Submitter: 
lara
Description: 
Super easy and fast cookie/snack. Absolutely delicious! Good to make around the holidays when you're filling up cookie plates with all different types of treats!
