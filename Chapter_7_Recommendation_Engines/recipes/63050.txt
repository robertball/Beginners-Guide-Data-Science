Title:
Tangy Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup Equal速 Spoonful*
1/8 teaspoon salt
6 tablespoons cold stick butter or margarine, cut into pieces
1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
3/4 cup Equal速 Spoonful**
1/2 cup lemon juice
4 tablespoons stick butter or margarine, melted
1 tablespoon grated lemon peel

Directions:
Combine flour, 1/3 cup Equal(r) Spoonful* and salt in medium bowl. Cut in 6 tablespoons butter with pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Sprinkle with 1 teaspoon lemon peel and vanilla; mix with hands to form dough. Press dough evenly on bottom and 1/4-inch up sides of 8-inch square baking pan. Bake in preheated 350 degrees F oven 8 to 10 minutes. Cool on wire rack.
Beat eggs and 3/4 cup Equal(r) Spoonful**; mix in lemon juice, 4 tablespoons butter and 1 tablespoon lemon peel. Pour mixture into baked pastry.
Bake until lemon filling is set, about 15 minutes. Cool completely on wire rack.
