Title:
Hearty Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces dried split peas
2 cups diced fully cooked lean ham and a left over ham bone
1 cup diced carrots
1 celery stalk, diced
1 medium onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 bay leaves
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
6 cups boiling chicken stock
1 cup hot milk

Directions:
In a slow cooker, layer the first ten ingredients in order listed (do not stir). Cover and cook on high for 4-5 hours or until vegetables are tender. Stir in milk. Discard bay leaves before serving.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
'This slow-cooker soup is one of my favorite meals to make during a busy workweek,' pens Deanna Waggy of South Bend, Indiana. 'When I get home, I just add the milk...and supper is served!'Modified from: Hearty Split Pea Soup
