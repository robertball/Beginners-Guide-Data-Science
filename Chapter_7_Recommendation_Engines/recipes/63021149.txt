Title:
Broccoli with Red Pepper and carmalized onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 cups broccoli florets
2 teaspoons minced fresh gingerroot
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 sweet red pepper, cut into strips
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 onion

Directions:
In a skillet, over low heat carmalize, ginger and garlic and onions. steam broccoli and red peppers and until it is crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. Add water chestnuts; and onion mixture, cover and heat through.  sprinkle with canned deepfried onions and Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
Karen Davies of Wanipigow, Manitoba shares this colorful side dish. The crisp snap of water chestnuts adds to its great taste.Modified from: Broccoli with Red Pepper
