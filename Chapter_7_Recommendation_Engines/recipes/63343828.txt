Title:
cheflucas's beef egg bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/888314.jpg
Ingredients:
5 large potatoes,cubed
1 medium onion,coarsely chopped
4 tablespoons butter,divided
1 lb. hamburger,raw
1 tablespoon seasoned salt
18 eggs
12 slices of bread
1/2 c. milk
2 teaspoons garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees and grease 9x13 pan.
Lay slices of bread single layer in pan.
Heat large skillet add 3 tablespoons butter.
When butter is melted add potatoes and cook until done
While the potatoes are cooking add the rest of the Butter to another skillet. once this is melted, saute Onions and add beef and cook until done.
Beat eggs in large bowl and add garlic powder,season Salt,and milk,
Lay beef in pan on top of bread.
Add potatoes
Pour eggs on potatoes
Bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
Cool, slice, and enjoy!
