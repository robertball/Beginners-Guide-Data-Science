Title:
Mock Olive Garden Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup mayo
1/3 cup white vinegar
1 tsp. veg/olive oil
2 Tbsp. corn syrup
2 Tbsp. parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp. Romano cheese
¼ tsp. garlic salt
½ tsp. Italian seasoning
½ tsp. dried parsley
1 Tbsp. lemon juice

Directions:
Combine in blender, let chill 30 minutes.
Salad: Lettuce, red onion, olives, tomatoes, croutons, shredded parmesan cheese.
