Title:
Christmas Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz. pkgs cream cheese
1 8 oz. pkg shredded cheddar cheese
1 pkg turkey pepperoni, finely chopped
4 green onions, finely chopped
2 tsp. worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl combine pepperoni and cream cheese. Soften cream cheese mixture in the microwave. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Pour out onto plastic wrap and roll into a ball. Put in fridge for at least 24 hours.
