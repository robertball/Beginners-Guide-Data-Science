Title:
Fabulous Ham and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small bag of pinto beans
ham bone
1 large white onion
1 bell pepper, chopped
3 stalks of celery, chopped
1/4 cup parsley
1/8 c butter
3 T worcheshire sauce
tobasco sauce to taste

Directions:
Soak beans over night. Simmer ham bone and beans for 2 hours. Add other ingredients and simmer for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This is a yummy way to use that ham bone from a baked ham. Perfect for a cold winter day!
