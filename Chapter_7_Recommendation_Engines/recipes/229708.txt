Title:
Sweet Apple Crispers with Sugar and Cinnamon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988285.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (21 ounce) can LUCKY LEAFÂ® Premium Apple Fruit Filling
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions:
In a small bowl combine sugar and cinnamon; set aside.
Spoon LUCKY LEAF Premium Apple Pie Filling down the center of each tortilla. Roll tortillas up tightly, and secure with a wooden toothpick.
In a 12-inch skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add rolls to the skillet and cook 8 to 10 minutes, or until browned, turning every few minutes.
Brush rolls with melted butter and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar to coat. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Apple pie filling is rolled in tortillas, browned in butter, and sprinkled with cinnamon sugar for a delicious dessert or snack.
