Title:
Pork and Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1050175.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. coconut oil
1 5-6 lb. Boston Butt pork roast
2 large onions, red or white
2 large jars sauerkraut
little salt
lots of pepper
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 cup apple cider vinegar
2 T. Worcestershire sauce
3 T. Dijon coarse grain mustard

Directions:
In a dutch oven, brown the seasoned roast on all sides.
Turn heat to low, cover and cook until tender, several hours.
Refrigerate and scrape off all fat.
Pull meat apart and remove bones.
Return to heat and add all other ingredients.
Simmer about 30 minutes more or until onions are tender.
Taste and adjust seasoning for sweet/sour.
