Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese softened, cubed
8 oz Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
3/4 cup chopped boneless ham steak
1/2 cup sliced black olives, drained
1 cup chopped cherry tomatoes
1/2  cup marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1/2 Tbsp. fresh chopped basil
1 tsp. Italian Seasoning
3 oz feta cheese
3 cloves garlic, pressed

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium size bowl. Once mixed pour into a 1.75 Quart round glass baking dish and cover with foil.
Pour 1 cup water into the pot in a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot(R)). Set the metal trivet inside. Place baking dish on top.
Close and lock the lid. Select manual pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 10 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
Release pressure carefully using the quick-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 5 minutes. Unlock and remove the lid. Stir and serve immediately.
