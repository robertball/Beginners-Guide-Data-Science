Title:
Emily's Sweet'n'Sour Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound bacon
4 large onions, chopped
1 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
1-1/2 teaspoons dry mustard
1/2 cup cider vinegar
2 16 oz. cans dry butter beans (California limas)
1 15-1/2 oz. can green lima beans
1 15 oz can kidney beans
1 large can (28 oz) New England style baked beans
1 can black beans

Directions:
Fry bacon until crisp. Lift. drain & crumble.  Set aside.
Discard all but 1/2 cup of the drippings.
Add chopped onions
Stir in brown sugar, mustard & vinegar
Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until juices are reduced by half.  (10 - 15 minutes)
Drain all beans except baked
Combine in a large casserole
Add onion-vinegar mixture & the crumbled bacon
Stir gently to blend
Bake, covered, in a 350 degree oven until hot & bubbly (1 to 1-1/2 hours)

Submitter: 
Mardi
Description: 
I ask my daughter-in-law to bring this flavorful dish to most of our family potlucks, everybody likes them.  They go especially well with BBQ.
