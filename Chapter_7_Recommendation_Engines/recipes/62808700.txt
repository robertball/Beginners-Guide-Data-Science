Title:
Dude, Fish Tacos! with awesome creamy cilantro slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Coleslaw:
1 small green cabbage
2/3 cup sour cream
3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/2 sliced red onion
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
Fish:
2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
2 tbsp. fresh lime juice
4 tsp. ground cumin
1 - 2 pounds white fish fillets
2 tsp kosher salt and black pepper
corn tortillas
2 or 3 ripe avacados, peeled, pitted and sliced
2 diced ripe tomatoes
extra cilantro

Directions:
Coleslaw:
Quarter and thinly slice the cabbage put in a large bowl. In a separate bowl, combine sour cream, lime juice, cumin, garlic, salt, red onion and cilantro. Mix together and pour over cabbage. Toss to combine and set aside for 30 minutes. (Can also be made ahead, one day in advance.)
Fish:
Mix olive oil, lime juice, cumin and 1 tsp salt in a bowl. Place fish in bowl and marinate for at least 30 minutes.  Set grill to medium or oven to 400 degrees Sprinkle fish with remaining salt and pepper and cook fish for 10 minutes or until firm.

Description: 
This is a simple and fantastic quick dinner recipe. Adapted from an Epicurious recipe, this dish is less spicy, but has tons of flavor.
I also buy any firm white fish that's on sale (cod, talapia, red snapper, halibut).
