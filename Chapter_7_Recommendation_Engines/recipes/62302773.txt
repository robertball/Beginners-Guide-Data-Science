Title:
Simple Overnight Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of white bread
soft butter or spreadable margerine
1 lb. bulk sausage
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
6 eggs, beaten
2 cups milk
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Spray 9 x 13 pan with cooking spray. Spread bread with soft butter/margerine. Cook sausage until brown, drain. Spoon over bread. Sprinkle with cheese. Combine beaten eggs, milk and salt. Mix well and pour over cheese. Cover and chill over night. Remove from refrigerate for 15 minutes, preheating oven to 350 degrees. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes.
