Title:
Tangy Tuna Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978855.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup MIRACLE WHIP LIGHT Dressing
1 teaspoon dill weed
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3 cups pasta (such as bow tie or macaroni), cooked, drained
2 (6 ounce) cans white tuna in water, drained, flaked
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 cup broccoli flowerets
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup sliced celery

Directions:
Mix dressing, dill and pepper in large bowl.  Add remaining ingredients; mix lightly.  Refrigerate several hours or overnight.
