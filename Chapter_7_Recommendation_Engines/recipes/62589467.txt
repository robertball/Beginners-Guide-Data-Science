Title:
Mom's Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 or 5 Large Potatoes
1 Cup Onion, sliced or julienned
1 Cup Celery, sliced
3 T. Butter
1/2 t. Celery Seed
Salt and Pepper to taste
4-5 Cups Milk
3/4 Cup Light Cream

Directions:
Cook potatoes in salted water til just cooked through. Saute onion and celery in 1T. butter. Add to drained cooked potatoes along with milk. Heat on low until hot and steaming, then add 2T.butter,Celery Seed,Salt and Pepper. Cook for about 5 Min. Then add cream and heat. If you want a creamier soup remove some potatoes and blend them with a stick blender and return to soup.
