Title:
Pan-Seared and Crusted Ling Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs. ling cod
1 stick butter
3 tablespoons salt
Salt and pepper
1-1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
1 lemon
1 teaspoon lemon pepper

Directions:
Dissolve 3 tablespoons salt in 8 cups of water and brine cleaned and deboned fish at room temperature for 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cut lemon in half; zest one half and cut the other on wedges to serve with the fish.
Melt 1/2 stick of butter in skillet over medium-high heat.
Rinse and dry fish fillets; lightly salt and pepper one side.
Quickly pan-sear fillets until golden brown on both sides.
Transfer fillets to a baking dish.  Squeeze juice of reserved lemon over fish.
Combine bread crumbs, parmesan and lemon pepper.  Top fish fillets with bread crumbs and dot top with remaining butter, cut into small pieces.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown on top and fish flakes easily with a fork.

Submitter: 
Renee
Description: 
This is a simple and excellent way to serve ling cod or other bottom fish.  It makes a delicious meal served with rice pilaf and Caesar salad.
