Title:
Easy Portuguese Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/852144.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion (chopped)
1 whole kale (cut into ribbons)
3 cloves garlic (chopped)
2 cans chicken broth (with sodium)
3 carrots chopped
1 can red kidney beans (drained)
1 cup water
1 tbs shaved parmesan cheese to bowl (optional)
3 potatoes cubed
1 tbs salt
1 tbs pepper
1 pkg polish kielbasa
2 tbs olive oil
1 cup small elbow macaroni

Directions:
Add olive oil to large pan and let heat up. Add chopped onion and garlic and allow to simmer for about a minute.
Toss in the kielbasa and stir until browned. Add potatoes, salt, and pepper and stir for about 2 minutes or until beginning to brown.
Add broth, water, kale, beans, and carrots. Bring to boil and simmer for about one hour.
Add macaroni when soup has boiled for about 45 minutes.
Add parmesan cheese to individual bowl as garnish.
Bon appetite!
