Title:
Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 garlic clove, minced
1 (10.5 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
approx. 1 to 1 1/2 cups beef broth
1 (4 ounce) can mushroom stems and pieces, undrained
3 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon pepper
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS
cooked spiral pasta or mashed potatoes
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat, cook beef, onion and garlic until meat is no longer pink; drain. Stir in consomme, mushrooms, cream soup and pepper. Add sour cream; heat through (do not boil).
Place over noodles or mashed potatoes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Great over mashed potatoes or noodles.

Add broth a little at a time so as to not make it to thin.  It may not take all of it.Modified from: Hamburger Stroganoff
