Title:
Yum Yum Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs. Ground Beef
8 oz package Egg Noodles
1 Tbsp Butter
1 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Garlic Salt
1/2 tsp Onion Salt
1 Cup Sour Cream
2 small cans Tomato Sauce
1 Cup Small Curd Cottage Cheese
1 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cook the Noodles.
Brown the meat, drain, and add the butter.  Add the salts and tomato sauce and simmer for about 5 minutes
While the meat mixture is cooking, drain the noodles and add the sour cream and cottage cheese to the drained noodles.
Layer 1/2 of the noodle mixture, then all of the meat mixture, then the remaining noodle mix.  Top with shredded cheddar.
Bake at 350 for about 25 min.
