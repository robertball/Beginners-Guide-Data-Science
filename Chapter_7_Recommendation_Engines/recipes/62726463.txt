Title:
Soup To Nuts Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C shortening
1 C sugar
2 eggs, separated
2 C sifted flour
1/2 t salt
2 t baking powder
1 t ground cloves
1 t cinnamon
2 t nutmeg
1 can tomato soup
1 t baking soda
1/2 C chopped nuts
1/2 C chopped raisins (opt.)

Directions:
Cram sugar and shortening well; add egg yolks, beating until light and creamy.
Add baking soda to soup and stir.
Sift together twice the flour, salt and spices.
Alternate sifted flour mixture and soup to creamed mixture, beating well.
Add nuts and raisins
Beat whites until stiff and fold into cake batter.
Bake in 2 loaf pans (greased and floured) or a small form pan at 350 degrees for 45-50 min.
Cool and ice with cream cheese frosting (recipe follows).
Recipe can be doubled for large form pan and 1 loaf cake.
Cream Cheese Frosting:
3 oz pkg. cream cheese
1 box powered sugar
2 t vanilla
Few drops of milk
Beat all together until light and creamy. Add more milk if necessary. Spread on top and sides of cake. Decorate with nut halves.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Mom made this a lot for the family. Was Dad's favorite.  I've seen other recipes similar to this but this one has more steps.  Maybe that's why it's so good.
