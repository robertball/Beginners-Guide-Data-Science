Title:
linguine with zucchini, grape tomatoes, and  creamy lemon yogurt sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz whole wheat linguine
1/2 cup nonfat plain greek yogurt
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 tsp grated lemon zest
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
3 medium zucchini, cut into thin strips
2 garlic cloves thinly sliced the zucchini
1/2 pint grape or cherry tomatoes, halved lengthwise

Directions:
cook pasta as usual,reserve 1/4 cup of the cooking water. Stir together the yogurt,parmesan, lemon zest and salt and pepper. set aside.
In a large skillet heat olive oil. Add the zucchini and cook until wilted. set aside. Add garlic to the pan and cook for 1 minute. Stir in zucchini with the garlic, stir in the tomatoes and cook until softened.
Transfer the zucchini mixture to the yogurt mixture and stir gently. Add drained linguine. Add reserved pasta cooking water one tablespoon at a time until you like the consistency.
