Title:
German Oven Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup butter
2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Spray 8" cake pan with no-stick spray or butter a 10" cast iron skillet and heat the skillet in  oven.
Beat eggs at high speed with an electric mixer or immersion blender. Slowly add the milk and flour. Mix well. Pour batter into cake pan or hot skillet. Plase in 425 degree oven for 20 minutes. It will rise like a souffle, then fall when taken out of oven.

Submitter: 
Ellen Janet Nestler
Description: 
Quick, easy and delicious. Serve with lemon wedges and powdered sugar, warm maple syrup or jam.Modified from: German Baby
