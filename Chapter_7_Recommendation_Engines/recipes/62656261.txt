Title:
WW Cajun Tuna Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/589477.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (12-oz) drained cans Chunk Light Tuna in Spring Water
1 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
2 large eggs, beaten
1/2 cup red bell peppers, diced
1/2 cup green bell peppers, diced
1/2 cup green onions, chopped
1/4 cup Cajun seasoning or spice mix
2 Tbsp capers, drained
1 tsp hot pepper sauce
2 Tbsp oil
6 light hamburger buns
6 Lettuce leaves
6 slices tomato
6 slices onions
Cajun Mayo
1/3 cup garlic or plain mayonnaise
3/4 teaspoon Cajun seasoning or spice mix

Directions:
1. In a bowl, combine tuna, breadcrumbs and eggs.
2. Add bell peppers, green onions, Cajun seasoning, capers, and hot pepper sauce to tuna mixture; blend well.
3. Evenly divide and shape tuna mixture into six patties.
4. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of oil until hot over medium-high heat. Cook 3 tuna burgers on both sides until browned and heated through, about 5 minutes. Repeat withn remaining oil and 3 burgers.
5. Meanwhile, whip together mayonnaise and Cajun seasoning until well blended.
6. Spread hamburger buns with Cajun mayonnaise and fill buns with tuna burgers, lettuce, tomatoes and onions
Serving Size: 1 burger
