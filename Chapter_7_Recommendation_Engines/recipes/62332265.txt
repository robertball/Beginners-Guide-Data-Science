Title:
Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. smoked sausage
4 (150z.) cans of red beans
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 bay leaf
1 pinch of rosemary, crushed
1/8 cup liquid smoke (hickory)
1 (4oz.) can of green chilies, drained
1 cup of beef broth

Directions:
Add all ingredients, except one can of beans, to crock pot and mix well.
cook on low 6-8 hours; high 2-4 hrs.
Remove bay leaf.
Pour mix into food processor and mix the ingredients.
Return mix to crock pot and add last can of beans; mix.
Cook for additional hour.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
k-k-k-katie
Description: 
After searching for Popeye's Red Beans and Rice recipe, I settled for this one. It's not bad; it's not Popeye's, but it runs a decent second.
