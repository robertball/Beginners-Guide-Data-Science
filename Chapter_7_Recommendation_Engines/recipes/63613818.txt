Title:
Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Box of fettuccine noodles
1 c. cottage cheese
1/4 c. Parmesan cheese
1/4 c. milk
1 egg
1 med clove of garlic or 1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper (1 tsp regular pepper)
dash of salf

Directions:
Bring large pan of water to boil for the pasta. In the blender, process the cottage cheese until very smooth. Then add the parmesan, milk, egg, garlic,nutmeg, pepper and salt. Process until smooth. Cook 1/2 box (6-8 oz) fettucccine noodles accorrding to package directions. Drain and put back into pan. Add the cheese sauce and stir. The heat of the pasta and pan will warm the sauce. Add basil or parsley and serve.
