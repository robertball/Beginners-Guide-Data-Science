Title:
Old English Crabmeat Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (5oz) jar Old English cheese spread
1 (6oz) can good quality crabmeat, drained
1/4C softened butter
1T mayo
dash garlic powder
dash salt
1 8oz pkg English muffins

Directions:
Preheat oven 400 degrees. Combine all ingredients except muffins in bowl. Split muffins and spread mixture on each muffin half. Put on baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes or until toasted and bubbling. Cool slightly and cut each muffin into 4 pieces. Serve
