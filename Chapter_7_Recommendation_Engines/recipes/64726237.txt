Title:
Smoked Gouda Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoons red onions minced or may substitute white or green onion
8 ounces smoked gouda shredded
splash of Worcestershire sauce
a dash of garlic powder (not salt)
a dash of onion powder (not salt)
3 oz bacon crumbles (optional)

Directions:
Place mayonnaise in a bowl, add onion, spices and Worcestershire sauce  (and bacon if desired). Shred cheese (or use already shredded) and mix the cheese into the mayonnaise. Let rest in a covered dish in your refrigerator for about 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Willow Mckim
Description: 
Make smoked gouda cheese dip, it is perfect for a snack, or party.
It's so easy to make! Similar to Kroger gouda cheese dip but better. 
 Note: You can find already shredded gouda at Meijer, it comes in 6 oz packages, so buy 2.
