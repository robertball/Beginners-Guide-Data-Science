Title:
Tasty Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6695998.jpg
Ingredients:
6 lemons, juiced
1 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ice cubes

Directions:
In a large pitcher, combine fresh lemon juice and water. Stir in brown sugar until it dissolves. Stir in ice cubes.

Submitter: 
EAMON G
Description: 
This lemonade is good on hot days, and very successful at lemonade stands.
