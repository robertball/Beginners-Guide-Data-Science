Title:
Crab Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crepes:
1 cup cold water
4 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups flour
2 Tbsp melted butter
Filling:
1/2 cup chopped green onion
4 Tbsp butter
3/4 cups dry sherry
3 cups diced cooked FRESH crabmeat
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Sauce:
4 Tbsp butter
6 Tbsp flour
2 cups hot milk
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 egg yolks
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 cup grated swiss cheese

Directions:
Crepes: Put all ingredients for crepes in a blender, cover and blend for 2 minutes. Refrigerate for two hours.
Using a 6 inch crepe pan, make crepes as thin as possible.
Filling: Saute onion in butter. Add sherry, salt and pepper.  Mix with crab and set aside.
Sauce: Melt butter and add flour and blend. Add milk, seasonings and boil for one minute. Remove from heat.
In a small bowl, beat yolks and cream. Add egg mixture to sauce mixture stirring constantly. Fold in grated cheese. Sauce should be thick.
Reserve one cup of sauce to pour over crepes. Add remaining sauce to filling and mix well.
Place two heaping Tbsp of mixture across one side of each crepe and roll up.
Top each crepe with reserved sauce.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
Can substitute fresh lobster meat for crab.  Delicious on spring or summer evening with a green salad on the side.
