Title:
Dark Chocolate Cookies Kisses Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 cups semisweet dark chocolate chips
6 tablespoons butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 cup confectioners sugar
1 bag foil-wrapped milk chocolate pieces, unwrapped

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degress F.
Melt 1 cup of chocolate chips over low heat.
Cream butter and sugars.
Add melted chocolate chips and vanilla and eggs, Set aside
Combine flour and baking powder.
Put dough on a plastic wrap; wrap the dough tight and secure.
Place on refrigerator for about 45 min allow to chill.
Make small balls (1 inch)
Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes at 350 degrees F.
Top each cookie immediately with an unwrapped chocolate piece, pressing down firmly so that cookie cracks around edge.
Allow to cool for about 5-10 min on wire rack.
