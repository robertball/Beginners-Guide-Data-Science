Title:
Essanaye's Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/606181.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbs olive oil
3 cloves fresh minced garlic
1 28 oz can tomato sauce
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
2 tsp thyme
2 tsp dry Italian seasoning
2 tsp dry basil
2 tsp dry oregano
pinch of salt
1-2 tbs brown sugar (based on desired sweetness)

Directions:
Combine ingredients and simmer on low for 45 minutes.  Cool and refrigerate overnight before using.  Makes enough for 4 large pizzas.

Submitter: 
Essanaye
Description: 
After trying several brands of store bought sauce, I decided it was time to make my own.  This sauce is great, and super easy to make, but you should refrigerate it overnight to allow the flavors to meld.
