Title:
Hearty Meatball Soup II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 carrot, diced
1 onion, diced
1 celery, diced
4 cups beef broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, with juice
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
8 ounce frozen mixed vegetables
10 ounce ground beef
1/2 egg, mixed in cup
1/2 cup italian bread crumbs, more or less
1 piece Parmesan cheese rind
1 pinch ground black pepper
1 cup pasta shells

Directions:
In a large pot over medium high heat, saute carrot, onion, and celery 5 to 8 minutes.
Add broth, canned tomatoes, tomato sauce, cheese rind, and frozen vegetables, and simmer for 15 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, bread crumbs, and ground black pepper.
Roll into tiny, bite size meatballs and briefly saute in skillet until nicely browned.  Add to the soup.
Simmer for another 20 minutes, then add the pasta. Simmer for another 15 minutes, or until meatballs and pasta are fully cooked.
