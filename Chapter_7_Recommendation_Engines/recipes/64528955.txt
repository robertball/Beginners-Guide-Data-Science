Title:
Ravioli di Magro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten (-1Tbsp to close the rivioli sheets)
200g cooked spinach, minced
200g ricotta
100g Parmigiano, grated
2 tsp grated nutmeg
8 pasta sheets

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together. Put 8 spoonfuls on four of the sheets. Brush a bit of eggwash around each of the mixture mounds. Lay a clean pasta sheet on top of the filled ones, pressing down between each of the mounds. Using a ravioli cutter, cut along the edges of each filling creating a ravioli. Press down on the edges sealing each ravioli. Allow to rest at least one hour before cooking or freeze immediately. Add raw ravioli to boiling, salted water for about 3-5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Classic spinach and ricotta ravioli to be eaten with a light tomato sauce or just a light butter sauce.
