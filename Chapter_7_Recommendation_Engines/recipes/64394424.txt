Title:
Chipotle Chicken Black Bean and Rice Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2233870.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 medium onion
1 cup brown rice
1/2 lime Juice
1/4 cup water
2 teaspoons chipotle powder
1/3 cup black beans
1-2 handfuls spinach
1/4 cup cilantro
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1-2 eggs
1 Avocado

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Place chicken stripes in a greased 13Ã—9 baking dish for 20-25 minutes
Cook brown rice and follow the directions from the package.
In an 8'' cast iron skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion and cook for 5-6 minutes. Add cooked chicken and rice, lime, and chipotle powder, black beans (drained and rinsed), and water. Cook and stir until rice and black beans are heated.
Incorporate spinach and cilantro, stirring until spinach begins to wilt. Create well in the center of the skillet and crack egg(s) into the center. cover, and let cook until egg whites are set and yolk is done to desired firmness, 7-12 minutes. Sprinkle with extra cilantro and serve. Top with avocado or salsa, if desired.
