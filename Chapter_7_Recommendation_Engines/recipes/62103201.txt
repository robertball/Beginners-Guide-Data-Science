Title:
Oriental Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small package slivered almonds
1 package Ramen noodles, Oriental flavor
1 Tablespoon sugar
1 bag of broccoli coleslaw mix
2 green onions, chopped
Dressing:
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 Tablespoons sugar
flavor packet from noodles
2 Tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar

Directions:
Brown almonds in 1 Tablespoon sugar until coated and light brown.  Remove from heat, cool and break apart.  Add almonds to coleslaw mixture, chopped green onions, and crumbled, uncooked Ramen noodles in a glass bowl.  Toss with dressing.  Cover and refrigerate 6-8 hours before serving. Stir several times over the chilling time, and right before serving.

Submitter: 
MOBILITY61
Description: 
An Asian twist on an favorite side dish.  Instead of shredded cabbage, this recipe uses shredded broccoli.  Add Ramen noodles and toasted almonds for crunch, and an oriental dressing to tie it all together.  Share at a potluck or BBQ and be ready to share the recipe too!
