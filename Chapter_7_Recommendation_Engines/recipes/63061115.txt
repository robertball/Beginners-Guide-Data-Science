Title:
Chef Andy’s Mardi Gras Cajun Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup paprika
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp cumin
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp onion powder
1 tbsp basil
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tbsp dried lemon peel
1/2 tbsp gumbo file

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and add to your dish of choice.

Submitter: 
Nate Thee
Description: 
If you’re looking for a way to spice up meat dishes, this Cajun-inspired rub will do the trick. It’s the perfect salt-free seasoning that will kick up the flavor in any meal.
