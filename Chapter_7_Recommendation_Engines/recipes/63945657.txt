Title:
Yummy Quinoa Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093873.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup quinoa, dry
1 zucchini, chopped
1 head of broccoli, chopped
1 cup of fresh mushrooms, chopped
3/4 cup of carrots, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup peas, frozen
3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
1 can of low sodium chicken broth
3/4 cup of soy sauce (low sodium preferred)
1/2 tablespoon of fresh ginger, grated
2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
1/2 cup of scallions, chopped
Optional:
Chicken breast or tenderloin tips, season with salt and pepper and browned

Directions:
Whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, 1-2 cloves of minced garlic and ginger for the sauce. Set aside.
If including meat in the dish, lightly brown seasoned chicken or steak in a small amount of extra virgin olive oil or grape seed oil and keep warm in the oven or microwave.
Salt and pepper all the veggies.
Drop the garlic, onions, and carrots into a large skillet with a small amount of grape seed oil and cook on medium for 4-5 minutes.
Add broccoli, zucchini, and peas into the skillet with the other veggies and pour on chicken broth. Close the lid and steam the veggies for 5-6 minutes.
While cooking the veggies,cook the quinoa according to the box directions. Boil with half water, half broth.
When quinoa is done cooking, drop it into the pot with the veggies, add the sauce and mix well. Stir frying the quinoa and veggies until warmed through. Add meat, if including.
Top with scallions and serve with sriracha.
