Title:
Rod's Taco Guacamole Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados
1 small onion - red
1 clove of garlic
1 small tomato
1 1/2 tablespoon lime juice
Salt and pepper to taste
One 8 ounce package cream cheese, softened
One 8 ounce container sour cream
One envelope Taco Seasoning
Two green onions chopped
Romaine leaves -  washed, torn into bite size pieces
Shredded tex mex cheese

Directions:
Peel avocados, remove pit. Peel and mince onion and garlic. Chop tomato. Mash avocados in bowl, stir in minced onion and garlic and chopped tomato. Cover and refrigerate. In large bowl cream together the cream cheese and sour cream cheese. Add taco seasoning packet and mix well. Spread in large deep round dish. Spread the guacamole over top of this. Add a layer of chopped green onions, romaine and finally a layer of shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
This is a  great dish when it's too hot to cook. Very flavourful as well as festive looking.
