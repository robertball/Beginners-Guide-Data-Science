Title:
Easy Banana Bran Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups flour
1/2 bran cereal flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
2/3 cups sugar
2 eggs
1/3 cup butter
1 cup mashed bananas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Stir together dry ingredients
Mix eggs, melted butter, and bananas together in large bowl
Stir in dry ingredients until well blended
Pour into greased loaf pan
Bake 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
