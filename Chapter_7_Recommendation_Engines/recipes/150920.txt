Title:
Smirnoff Green Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977783.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Smirnoff ® Green Apple vodka
1/4 ounce apple schnapps
1 tablespoon sour mix
1 slice apple

Directions:
Fill shaker with ice; pour Smirnoff(R) Green Apple Vodka, apple schnapps, and sour mix over the ice.
Shake and strain into a chilled martini glass; garnish with apple slice.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Add that bit of extra flavor to this or other cocktail recipes easily with the natural fruit flavors of Smirnoff® Flavored Vodkas.
