Title:
Honey Dijon Mustard and Poppy Seed Coleslaw with Cranberries and Toasted Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Coleslaw mix:
5 cups of shredded cabbage or 1 large bag of prepared cabbage.
1 and a half cups of dried cranberries
1 half cup of finely diced green bell pepper
1 half a cup of finely diced celery
2 tablespoons of minced chives
1 half a cup toasted slivered almonds
Coleslaw dressing:
1 cup of mayonnaise
3 tablespoons of honey dijon mustard
2 tablespoons of honey
1 tablespoon of poppy seeds
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a small saute pan misted lightly wIth olive oil, toast almonds til golden brown.
Prepare coleslaw ingredients, combine (adding toasted almonds) and refrigerate until ready.
Combine dressing ingredients, whisk in a bowl and refrigerate until ready.
When ready, combine coleslaw and dressing and serve.
