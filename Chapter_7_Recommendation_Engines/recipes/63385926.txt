Title:
chicago style pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 t sugar
1 pkg dry yeast
1 c warm water
1/2 c yellow cornmeal
1/4 t salt
1 T olive oil
vegetable cooking spray
1 T olive oil
1 1/2 c chopgreen bell pepper
1 1/2 c chop onion
1 garlic clove crushed
2 1/2 c sliced mushrooms
2 t dried oregano
1/4 t salt
2 T yellow cornmeal
1 1/2 c canned crushed tomatoes
1 1/4 c shredded monterey jack cheese (5 oz)
3/4 c shredded provolone cheese (3 oz)
oregano sprigs (optional)

Directions:
Dissolve sugar and yeast in warm water in small bowl.  Let stand 5 minutes.
Place flour, 1/2 c cornmeal, and 1/4 t salt in a fodd processor and pulse 2 times or until blended.  With processor on,slowly add yeast mixture and 1 T of oil through chute, process until dough forms a ball.  Process for 1 additional minute.
Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and knead lightly 4 -5 times.  Place the dough in a bowl coated with cooking spray, turning dough to coat top.  Cover the dough and let rise in a warm place (85') free from drafts, 45 minutes or until doubled in bulk.
Heat 1 T in a large nonstick pan over med heat.  Add peppers, onion, and crushed garlic and saute for 5 minutes.  Add mushrooms, dried oregano and 1/4 t salt.  Saute 3 minutes or until tender.  Remove from heat.  Let cool.
Punch dough down and let stand for 5 min.  Divide dough in half.  Roll each 1/2 into 11 inch circle on lightly floured surface.  Place dough into 2 (9 inch) round cake pans.  Coated with cooking spray and each sprinkled with 1 T cornmeal, press dough up sides of pan.  Cover and let rise 20 minutes or until puffy.
Spread half of vegetable mixture over each prepared crust. and top each with half of the crushed tomatoes.  Sprinkle cheeses evenly over pizzas.
Bake at 475' for 15 minutes.  Reduce oven temp to 375' and bake for additional 15 minutes.
