Title:
Rosemary and Herb Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051426.jpg
Ingredients:
2	leg and thigh portion pieces of chicken
Â¼ 	cup olive oil
2 	teaspoons fresh minced rosemary
1	teaspoon fresh thyme, minced
2	teaspoons Italian seasoning
3	small garlic cloves, minced
1/2     teaspoon onion powder
1/4     teaspoon paprika
1/3	teaspoon salt
1/3	teaspoon pepper

Directions:
1.	 Pull chicken from refrigerator 1 hour before cooking time.
2.	Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, warm oil and add all herbs, garlic, seasonings,salt and pepper.  stir; remove from heat.  Let set until room temperature.
3.	Preheat oven to 425.  Place the chicken in a baking dish and loosen the skin from the meat.  Rub olive oil and herbs under skin making sure it is coated well, then pull the skin back over the meat.  Using remainder of oil mixture on top of skin.
4.	Bake chicken uncovered 50 to 55 minutes or until juices run clear. about half way through the cooking process using a turkey baster.  remove as much of the oil and fat as you can.  return chicken to oven and continue cooking until done.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
The herbs make this a comforting earthy tasting dish, the olive oil crisps the skin beautifully & Moistens the chicken until it melts in your mouth.
