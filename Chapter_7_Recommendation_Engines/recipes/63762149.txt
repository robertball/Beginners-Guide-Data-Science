Title:
Apple Maple Chicken Sausage and Sauteed Apples Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8 oz pkg al fresco Apple Maple Breakfast Chicken Sausage, sliced small
1/2 apple, peeled and grated
1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
4 eggs
4 tbsp water
2 tsp canola oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute sliced sausage and shredded apple in 1 tsp of oil for about 5 minutes over medium heat. Remove from pan.
Beat eggs and water until blended.
Heat 1 tsp of oil in pan until hot, pour in egg mixture. Mixture should set at edgesâ€”tilt pan and move as necessary.
Continue cooking until egg is set and will not flow. Fill omelet with sauteed sausage and apple mixture and add cheese.
Fold in half and invert on plate and serve.
