Title:
La Crema per Pandoro e Panettone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1702934.jpg
Ingredients:
500 Mascarpone
4 eggs
6 Tbsp white sugar (very fine)
a splash of brandy or rum

Directions:
Separate the eggs.
Beat the egg whites until fluffy.
In another bowl cream together the sugar with the egg yolks until pale yellow.
Add the mascarpone to the egg/sugar cream mixture. Add the brandy or rum. Mix until completely blended and creamy.
Fold in the egg whites.
Pour over panettone or pandoro and/or serve on the side.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
This was given to me by my Zia Natalina but is a common traditional sweet sauce poured over panettone and pandora at holiday time.
