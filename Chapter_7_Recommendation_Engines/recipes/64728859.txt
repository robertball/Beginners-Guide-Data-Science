Title:
Lemon Poppy seed cookies - soft Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/3 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter at room temp
1 cup sugar
1 large egg
zest of two lemons
2 tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp vanilla
1 tbsp poppy seeds
2-4 drops yellow food coloring (optional)

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350. Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.
2) In a medium bowl, mix flour, baking powder and soda, salt.
3) Cream together butter and sugar until fluffy - about 3 minutes.
4) Beat in egg, vanilla, zest, lemon juice, poppy seeds and food color.
5) With mixer on lowest speed, gradually add flour mixture. Scrape the sides of the bowl and mix until well combined.
6) You can chill the dough at this point. Scoop mixture onto cookie sheets, 2 inches apart, sprinkle a few extra poppy seeds on top. Bake 10-12 minutes. Cool on sheet 5 minutes then transfer to rack.
Store in airtight container for up to a week.
