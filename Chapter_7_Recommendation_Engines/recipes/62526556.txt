Title:
Cranberry Sour Cream Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 C sugar
2 eggs
1 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
2 C flour
1/2 t salt
1/2 pint sour cream
1 t almond extract
1/2 c ocean spray whole berry cranberry sauce
icing:
3/4 c powdered sugar
1/2 t almond extract
warm water (as needed to thin)

Directions:
Cream together first 3 ingredients.
Add remaining ingredients (except cranberries)
Layer and swirl in bundt pan - batter, cranberries, batter, cranberries
Bake 350' for 50 minutes
when cool mix topping together (powdered sugar, almond and water and drizzle)

Submitter: 
Sara Jennings Spittel
Description: 
A wonderful fall coffee cake - perfect for holiday brunches.
