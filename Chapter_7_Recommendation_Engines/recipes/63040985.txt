Title:
Sensational Satay Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced
1/2 cup peanut butter
3/4 cup water
3 TBL soy sauce
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper

Directions:
Trim fat from steak. In large oiled skillet, brown meat over high heat, stirring constantly. Reduce heat and stir in peanut butter until smooth. Blend in water, soy sauce, garlic powder, ginger and red pepper flakes. Cook until blended and heated thru. Garnish with cilantro, sliced green onions and chopped peanuts, if desired. Makes 5 servings.
