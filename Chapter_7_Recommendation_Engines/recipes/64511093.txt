Title:
Roasted Sweet Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Sweet Potato
1 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon White Pepper
1/2 teaspoon Onion  Powder
1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
3/4 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
1 tablespoon Coconut Oil
2 tablespoons Olive Oil
1 cup Half and Half
1 cup Skim Milk
1 1/2 chicken or vegtable stock

Directions:
Peel and dice sweet potato. Coat lightly in olive oil and add tablespoon of Coconut oil (or butter). Add salt, onion powder, garlic powder, cinnamon, and white pepper. Toss potato cubes to coat well. Bake 45 mins at 400 degrees or until mushy.  Remove and place in blender. Add Half and Half and Skim Milk and stock. Blend/liquefy.  Add additional salt to taste. Pour and serve.

Submitter: 
Adam Caskey
Description: 
Nice fall seasonal comfort soup with a sweet and slightly savory taste.
