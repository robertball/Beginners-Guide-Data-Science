Title:
Basic Muffins with Variations Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg
3/4 cup milk
3/8 cup oil(1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons)
1 cup any optional ingredient

Directions:
Stir dry ingredients in large bowl. Add wet ingredients and add in until moistened. Adjust milk depending on your add in. Less for banana, crushed pineapple or pumpkin but more if using dried fruit. Scoop into greased muffin pan. At this point you can sprinkle with cinnamon/sugar or decorations if desired.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
This muffin can be made with any flavour and dry ingredients can store in pantry for a quicker muffin. Very easy to prepare.
