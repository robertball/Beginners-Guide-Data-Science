Title:
Mocha Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/715421.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups brewed coffee
1/4 cup sugar
4 cups milk
4 cups chocolate ice cream, softened

Directions:
In a container, combine coffee and sugar; stir until sugar is dissolved. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
Just before serving, pour into a punch bowl. Add milk; mix well. Top with scoops of ice cream and stir well.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
Coffee lovers will delight in this yummy beverage that tastes like a chilled cappuccino. Add chocolate ice cream to make it rich and creamy.
