Title:
Mary's Slow Cooked Chicken Stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chicken carcass with skin
2 Carrots
2 Ribs of Celery
1/2 tbs olive oil
1 onion
2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp dried parsley
Water

Directions:
Place chicken carcass with the skin in the crock pot.  Cut the carrots and celery into large chunks and add to the pot.  Coarsely cut the onion and saute it in 1/2 tbs of olive oil.  Add the onions to the pot along with the salt, pepper and parsley.  Fill the pot with water stopping 1/2 inch from the top.  Cook on high for 4 hours.  When finished, strain the stock keeping only the liquid.  All solid pieces should be discarded.

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
This is a basic stock made in a slow cooker and it is a great way to get the most value out of a roasted chicken.
