Title:
Jake's Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588480.jpg
Ingredients:
1 14oz can tomato soup
3/4 cup milk
20 saltine crackers
Handful of cheese

Directions:
Pour tomato soup and milk in a saucepan and heat on low.
Once hot, pour into bowls and crumble crackers and add cheese to top it off. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
jennifer_russell
Description: 
Jake has a few recipes that he has perfected and this is definitely one of them! A very addicting bowl of tomato soup. Lots of crackers and cheese, more please!
