Title:
Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. tub Shedd's Spread (Country Crock)
3/4 tsp. garlic powder
3/4 tsp. Italian herb seasoning (Spice Islands)
1 tsp. parsley flakes (dried)
1 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix all together right in the tub. Use on fresh bread or toast, or make garlic bread. Keeps well in the refrigerator
