Title:
Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked and drained spaghetti
5 chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery or mushroom soup
1/2 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup red pepper, chopped
1 3/4 cups water or use part milk
1/4 cup wine
1/4 cup butter
2 cubes  chicken bouillon
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup Crushed Ritz crackers

Directions:
Simmer the chicken and use the water for the pasta
Mix cooked spaghetti with chicken and veggies
In medium saucepan heat together soup, water, wine, butter, and bouillon. Bring to a boil and then pour over the pasta and chicken. Mix in about 1/2 cup of the cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for about 15 minutes in a 9 X 13 baking pan. Sprinkle remaining cheese over top and then put the crushed crackers over it.
Bake for another 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
Description: 
Bake spaghetti and chicken in a delicious sauce of cream of chicken soup, butter and bouillon. Top with Cheddar cheese and finish in the oven for a family-pleasing meal.Modified from: Easy Chicken Tetrazzini
