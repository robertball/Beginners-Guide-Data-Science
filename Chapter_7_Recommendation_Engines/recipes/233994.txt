Title:
Slow Cookers Savory Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045133.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 1/2 pound) boneless chuck, well trimmed
1 1/2 cups cut-up fresh carrots
1 1/2 cups cut-up fresh potatoes
1 cup cut-up fresh celery
1 cup cut-up fresh onions
1 package McCormick® Slow Cookers Savory Pot Roast Seasoning
1 cup water

Directions:
Place beef and vegetables in slow cooker.
Mix Seasoning Mix and water until blended. Pour over beef and vegetables. Cover.
Cook 8 hours on LOW or 4 hours on HIGH. Remove roast and vegetables to serving platter. Stir sauce before serving.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Everyday Cooking
Description: 
Savory aromas entice you as this easy meal-in-one gently simmers in the slow cooker. Choose a boneless chuck roast for the richest taste or a rump roast if you prefer lower fat.
