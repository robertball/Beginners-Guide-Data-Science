Title:
Creamy chicken chili for the instant pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5714944.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
1 Tbsp butter
8 ounces reduced-sodium chicken stock, divided
1 pound chicken breasts
1 15 ounce can cannellini beans , rinsed and drained
1 4 ounce can diced green chilis
1 8 ounce brick Neufchâtel cheese (low fat cream cheese, cut into cubes
2 teaspoons cumin
1 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper, to taste
Avocado for garnish
Cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Put instant pot onto sauté mode (low heat) add butter and onions sauté  for 3-5 minutes.
Take half of the chicken breasts and dice them into bite size pieces, take the other half of chicken breasts and fillet them.
Place chicken into instant pot, and pour in half of the broth.
Switch from sauté to high pressure. Cover with lid and cook for 15 minutes. Quick release.
Remove fillet pieces of chicken and shred. Return shredded chicken to pot.
Take half of the cannellini beans and mix into chicken. Take the remaining broth and cannellini beans and using a blender, blend together. Pour blended beans into the chicken mixture. Add in the green chili’s and return pot to low sauté.
Add in the low-fat cream cheese stir constantly until melted and fully incorporated.
Add in the seasonings. Stir to combine. Garnish with cilantro and avocado if desired
