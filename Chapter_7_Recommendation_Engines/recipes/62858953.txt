Title:
chipotle redneck burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb gound burger
1/2 c chopped onions
chopped chipotle peppers to taste, I added 3T
2 t adobo sauce
3T garlic chives
2T worcestershire sauce
2 eggs
1/4 c wheat germ
2T ketchup
1T mustard
salt and pepper to taste
toppings
12 strips bacon
spicy BBQ sauce
sliced raw onions OR fry onions and mushrooms in bacon grease
cheddar cheese

Directions:
add ingredients, make patties. Put a hole in the middle of patties with your finger so the burgers do not shrink while grilling. (lets the steam escape)
Grilling is best but could also be broiled. Serve on toasted buns with slices of bacon, cheese and favorite toppings

Submitter: 
recipes need gluten
Description: 
a local food chain brings out a spicy mama burger every year, this year I tried to copycat it
