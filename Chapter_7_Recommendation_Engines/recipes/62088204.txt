Title:
Bama's Easy Cheesy Spaghetti and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 one lb box of Vermicelli or thin spaghetti.
1 lb EXTRA sharp cheese
1 Tbs Olive Oil
Salt
Milk

Directions:
Add spaghetti to boiling water with olive oil. Boil for 8 to 10 minutes until al dente. Drain.
Slice cheese into 1/8" to 1/4" pieces.
Add 1/3 of the spaghetti to the bottom of a 3 quart casserole dish.
top with a layer of cheese slices.
Salt to taste.
Continue layering spaghetti, cheese and salt until all ingredients are used.
Add milk until you can see it rising to the top of the dish.  Approximately 1 pint of milk.
Bake uncovered in a 350 degree oven for 30 to 45 minutes or until golden brown and crusty on top.  Best when served hot.

Submitter: 
Jeanni Kistler
Description: 
As long as I can remember, my mother made this version of macaroni and cheese.  Her mother made it for her family as well.  I have been serving it to my family for 20 years and it is always a favorite at every potluck dinner I go to.
