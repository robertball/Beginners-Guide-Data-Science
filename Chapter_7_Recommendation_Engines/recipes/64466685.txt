Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2443130.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz of cream cheese
8 oz (8 squares) of Bakers Chocolate
1/3 cup coconut (if preferred)
1/4 cup pecan pieces (if preferred)
1/3 cup crushed graham cracker (if preferred)
1 package flat marshmallows (if preferred)

Directions:
1) Beat cream cheese with mixer until creamy.
2) Melt 8 squares of chocolate and blend into "creamy" cream  cheese.
3) Refrigerate mixture for about 1 hour or until firm.
4) Once firm shape into about 24 to 30 balls.
5) Roll balls in whatever topping you like you can use the "if preferred" or select your own.**
6) Refrigerate about 1 hour after decorating before serving.
7) Refrigerate any leftovers.
