Title:
Papas con Chorizo (Potatoes and Chorizo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/439650.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tblspn vegetable oil
10 oz Mexican pork chorizo, casings removed
1 small onion, diced
1 lb red skinned new potatoes, cut into small (1/4-inch) dice, and boiled
1/2 Cup Cojita cheese (Mozzarella can be substituted also)
seasoned salt and black pepper

Directions:
Peel and boil potatoes until their tender.  Drain water off.  Dice potatoes into small peices.  Set aside.
Heat oil in skillet, add onion and chorizo.  Break the chorizo up into small chunks (like hamburger).  Cook until done, about 10 minutes.
Add diced potatoes and saute until brown, about 12 minutes.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Remove from heat.
Add cheese.  Enjoy!
