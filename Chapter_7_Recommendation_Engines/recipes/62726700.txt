Title:
German-Style Beef Roast In Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 - 3 # boneless beef chuck pot roast
1 T cooking oil
2 C sliced carrots (2)
2 C chopped onions (2 large)
1 C sliced celery (2 stalks)
3/4 C chopped kosher-style dill pickles
1/2 C dry red wine or beef broth
1/3 C German-style mustard
1/2 t black pepper
1/4 t ground cloves
2 bay leaves
2 T flour
2 T dry red wine or beef broth
Hot cooked noodles
Snipped fresh parsley (opt)

Directions:
Trim fat from meat. In large skillet brown meat on all sides in hot oil. Drain fat.
In slow cooker, combine carrots, onions, celery and pickles. Place meat to top of vegetables. In sm. bowl combine the 1/2 cup of wine, mustard, pepper, cloves and bay leaves. Pour over meat and veggies.
Cover and cook on low heat for 8-10 hrs. or on high for 4-5 hrs. Remove meat from cooker to plate. Cover w/foil to keep warm.
For gravy: Transfer vegetables and cooking liquid to a 2 qt. saucepan. Discard bay leaves. In sm. bowl, stir together flour and 2 T wine. Stir into mixture in saucepan. Cook and stir over med. heat until thickened and bubbly. Cook and stir for 1 min. Serve over noodles. If desired, sprinkle w/parsley

Description: 
Pot roast cooked with carrots, onions, celery, mustard and surprisingly dill pickles.
