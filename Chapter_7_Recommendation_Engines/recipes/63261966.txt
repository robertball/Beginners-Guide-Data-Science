Title:
Orange Sticky Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling
1/4c chopped raisins
1T sugar
1/2t cinnamon
Topping
1/2c chopped nuts
1/3c brown sugar
2T butter
2T honey
1/4t cinnamon
Muffin
2c flour
3t baking powder
1/2t salt
1 egg
2/3c milk
1/4c honey
1/3c butter, softened
2T orange zest

Directions:
Combine filling ingredients and set aside.
Combine topping ingredients, place 1t in each muffin cup.
Mix together muffin ingredients, fill 1/2 full, sprinkle in raisin mixture and top with remaining batter.
Bake 375* for 16-20 min, cool and invert pan.
