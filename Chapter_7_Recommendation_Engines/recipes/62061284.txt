Title:
Sausage and Cream Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Sausage
8 oz. Cream Cheese ( Softend)
2 8 Count Cresent Rolls

Directions:
cook sausage until no longer pink, drain.
add softend cream cheese and mix well.
layer one can of cresent rolls in a 9X 13 pan spread mixture and top with second can of cresent rolls bake as directed. serve warm! for a spicer taste add a 4 oz can of green chiles to the sausage/cream cheese mixture!
