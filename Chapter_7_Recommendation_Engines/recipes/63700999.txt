Title:
7-Layer Greek Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028695.jpg
Ingredients:
1 10-ounce container of plain humus
1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
1 5-ounce container of feta cheese
1/2 of a 6-ounce jar of pitted kalamata olives, sliced
1 pound container of tzatziki sauce (gyro sauce)
1 pint of grape tomatoes, diced
1 large English cucumber, diced
3 green onions-both white and green parts, diced
1 bag pita chips

Directions:
In a pie dish, spread humus evenly.
Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of Greek seasoning over humus.
Layer feta cheese over humus, breaking up any large pieces.
Add sliced kalamata olives and diced cucumber.
Layer tzatziki sauce over olives and cucumber carefully.
Top with tomatoes and diced green onion.
Chill until ready to serve.
Serve with pita chips.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a Greek inspired version of the Mexican 7-Layer Dip and it's vegetarian!
