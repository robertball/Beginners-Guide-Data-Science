Title:
Stuffed Green Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Ground Beef
3 Large Green Bell Peppers
1 Tbsp. Milk
1 Egg
1 Cup Grated Romano Cheese
1 Cup Italian Bread Crumbs
1 28oz. Can Crushed Tomato
1 Tsp. Basil

Directions:
Cut peppers in half from top to bottom and deseed, remove the stem.
In a large bowl mix the ground beef, milk, egg, romano cheese, and bread crumbs.
Fill pepper halves with meat mixture
Place peppers meat side down in a large glass casserole dish and pour sauce into dish.
Spinkle basil over peppers and sauce.
Bake for 1/2 hour.
Flip peppers meat side up and bake for another 1/2 hour or until peppers are fork tender
Mix and spoon sauce over peppers and serve.
