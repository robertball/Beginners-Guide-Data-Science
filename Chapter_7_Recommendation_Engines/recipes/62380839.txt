Title:
Rick's Gooey Mocha Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/438246.jpg
Ingredients:
Baker's Joy spray
8 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
(2-4Oz. Ghirardelli chocolate bars)
3/4 cup (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter
2 cups sugar
6 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup Hershey's cocoa powder
1 tube Starbucks "Via" instant coffee
4 tsp Organic Coconut Oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup sour cream
6 oz Hershey's Dark Choclate chips
3/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
* Powdered sugar

Directions:
reheat oven to 375Â°F. Spray 13x9x2-inch metal baking pan with Bakers Joy nonstick spray. Stir chocolate and butter in heavy small saucepan over low heat until melted. Cool.
Using electric mixer, beat 2 cups sugar, eggs and vanilla in medium bowl until fluffy, about 5 minutes. Add chocolate mixture; beat just until blended. Add flour and next 4 ingredients; beat just until blended. Stir in sour cream, chocolate chips and nuts (if using).
Pour batter into greased pan. Bake until tester inserted into center comes out with moist crumbs attached, about 35 minutes. Transfer pan to rack; cool completely. Cut into squares. Top with powdered sugar if you like.
