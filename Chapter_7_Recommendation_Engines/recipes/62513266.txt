Title:
Becky's Killer Swiss Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 lbs Boneless Skinless chicken breasts
8 swiss cheese slices (We prefer Havarti)
2 10oz cans cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup white wine
1 1/2 cups Peppridge Farms Stuffing Crumbs
2Tbs melted butter

Directions:
spray 13x9 with Pam. Place chicken in bottom of pan. Top with cheese slices. In a bowl mix soup, milk, and wine. Pour over chicken. Sprinkle with dry stuffing crumbs. Drizzle melted butter on top. Bake at 350. Serves 8
