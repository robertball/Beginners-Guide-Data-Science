Title:
Squash and Bean Soup (Nkrakra) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/711679.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds beef for stew, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
2 cups water
2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
1 1/2 pounds Hubbard squash, pared and cut into 1 inch cubes (can substitute butternut squash) OR 1 package (12 oz) frozen cooked squash, thawed)
2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or canned)
1 package (10 oz) frozen baby lima beans

Directions:
Heat beef, water, salt, ginger and red pepper in a Dutch oven; reduce heat. Cover and simmer 1 1/2 hours. Add squash; cover and cook until beef and squash are tender, 30-45 minutes.
Remove squash; mash or puree in blender. Return squash to Dutch oven. Add tomatoes and beans. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer until beans are tender, about 15 minutes. Top each serving with hot cooked rice.
