Title:
Vanilla Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup White Chocolate Chips
1 tbsp. Milk
Food coloring if desired
(If you're really adventurous, add a drop or two of vanilla or almond flavoring, Yum!)

Directions:
Combine chocolate chips and milk in a microwave safe bowl
Microwave on high about 1 minute.
Stir and microwave again for another 25 seconds, remove from microwave and stir. Continue to do above (heat 25 seconds and stir) until chocolate is melted.
You may choose to color the glaze with food coloring for different occasions or divide it and color it two different colors if desired.
(Green and Red For Christmas etc.)
The chocolate will set when it cools so you can stack cookies.
