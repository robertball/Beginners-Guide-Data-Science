Title:
Pecan Pie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crescent rolls
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup corn syrup
2 tbsp butter, melted
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Unroll dough and press in bottom and 1/2 inch up sides of 9x13-inch pan. Bake 8 minutes.
Meanwhile, in medium bowl, mix remaining ingredients. Pour filling over partially baked crust. Bake 18-22 minutes longer until golden brown.
Cool completely, about 1 hour, and cut into bars.
