Title:
Squash casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
2 Tbsp. butter
3 cups squash, cooked and drained with all water squeezed out
1 cup crushed Ritz crackers, plus additional for topping
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
1 cup grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat; saute onion in butter for 5 minutes and remove from pan.
Mix onions with all other listed ingredients.
Pour squash mixture into buttered casserole dish and top with additional crushed crackers.
Bake for 25-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
This recipe is from my grandmother.  I like it best with acorn squash, but any type will work!
