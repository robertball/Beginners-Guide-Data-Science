Title:
Vegetarian Bodacious Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1041094.jpg
Ingredients:
8 vegetarian bacon strips
1 head fresh broccoli, chopped
1 small head cauliflower, chopped
1 1/2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1/2 large red onion, chopped
1/3 cup light mayonnaise
1/3 cup plain yogurt
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Cook bacon in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain fat and crumble bacon.
Mix broccoli, cauliflower, Cheddar cheese, onion, and crumbled bacon together in a large bowl.
Whisk mayonnaise, yogurt, red wine vinegar, sugar, ground black pepper, salt, and lemon juice in a bowl until thick and creamy. Pour dressing over broccoli mixture and toss to combine. Cover and refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
EMIAUKEA
Description: 
Chopped broccoli, crumbled veggie bacon, lots of grated cheese, and red onion are doused with a lovely red wine vinegar and mayonnaise dressing.
