Title:
Mediterranean Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
juice and zest of 1 lemon
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 T chopped fresh rosemary
2 t chopped fresh thyme
1 t salt
3 T plain greek yogurt
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
pepper to taste

Directions:
Place chicken breasts in a bag. Combine all other ingredients in a small bowl and whisk together. Pour mixture over chicken and let marinate for several hours or overnight. Grill on medium high for 8-10 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.

Moist grilled chicken perfect with your favorite greek side dishes.
