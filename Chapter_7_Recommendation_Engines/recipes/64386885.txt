Title:
Acini De Peppe Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Acini De Peppe Macaroni
15 oz. Crushed Pineapple in Juice
15 oz. Chunk Pineapple in Juice
15 oz. Mandarin Oranges
1 c. sugar
2 T. flour
1/2 tsp. salt
3 beaten egg yolks
8 oz. frozen non dairy topping, defrosted.

Directions:
Cook maccaroni as directed on box.
Rinse in cold water, drain and set aside.
Drain and save juice of fruit.  Juice should be 1 3/4 cup if not add water.
In a medium pot bring to a boil the sugar, juice, flour and salt with the egg yolks.
After mixture comes to a boil set aside to cool.
When cool add macaroni, and refridgerate 24 hours.
Next day add fruit and cool whip.
Refridgerate and enjoy.
