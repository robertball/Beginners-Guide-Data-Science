Title:
Whipped Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Whipping Cream
2 Large Eggs
1 Cup Sugar
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
1.5 Cup cake flour
2 Teaspoons salt
Buttercream Frosting

Directions:
Grease 3(6x2)rnd cakepans; line wax paper
Beat whipping cream until foamy.
Combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
Fold into whipping cream mixture.
Pour into prepared pans.
Bake at 350 for 18 minutes.
Cool 10 minutes.
Remove from pans.
Let cool completely.
Spread 2.5 frosting in between layers and on top and sides.
Spoon one cup frosting in to a bag fitted with a No. 5 round tip.  Pipe curly lines over top and sides.  Do not overlap lines. Spoon remaining frosting  in to a bag fitted with a No. 2 large basketweave tip.  Pipe a ruffle round the base.
