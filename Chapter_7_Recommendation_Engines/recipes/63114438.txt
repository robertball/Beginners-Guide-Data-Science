Title:
Mexican Tortilla Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large jar of Cookwell Tortilla Soup
1 bag fully cooked chicken fajita meat cut in strips.
14 corn tortillas
1 can black beans (rinsed)
1 can corn (drained)
1 bag shredded cheddar cheese (8oz)
Pico and sour cream for garnish

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cut up tortillas into bite size pieces. In a bowl, mix together above ingredients.
2. Spread evenly in a 9 x 13 pan and top with shredded cheese. Cover with foil.
3. Bake for 30 minutes until cheese is melted and edges are boiling.
4. If casserole is still soupy after 30 minutes, remove foil and let bake for another five minutes.

Submitter: 
momtoolive
Description: 
I think of this as Mexican inspired comfort food.  A casserole made with tortilla soup, chicken fajita meat, tortillas, beans, corn, and cheese.  All of these are prepackaged processed ingredients.  Good when you are pressed for time and feeding a large family.
