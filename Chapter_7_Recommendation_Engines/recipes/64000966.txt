Title:
Big Ray's Reuben in a Crock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110876.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans sauerkraut, drained, squeezed and chopped,(16 ounce)
2 pkg. Neutchatel cheese,(8 ounce)
1 lb. shredded Swiss cheese
1 lb. shaved and chopped corned beef
1/4 cup sour cream
1 1/2 Tbsp dijon mustard
1/4 tsp caraway seeds
3 Tbsp horseradish
3/4 cup thousand island dressing

Directions:
Allow Neutchatel cheese to reach room temperature and cut into cubes.  Combine the sauerkraut,caraway seeds, horseradish, sour cream, Neutchatel cheese, Swiss cheese, corned beef, thousand island dressing and mustard in the slow cooker. Cover, and cook on high for 45 minutes until cheese is melted, stirring occasionally. Serve with crackers.

Submitter:

Description: 
Set it and forget it, until everyone starts asking for the recipie! Woven wheat crackers (Tricuits) are probably my favorite with this dip, although others will work as well.  I have also served this for dinner (what can I say, we really love reubens!).
