Title:
Roasted Garlic-Red Pepper Dipping Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Filippo Berio Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 tablespoons roasted garlic puree
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
Pinch of salt

Directions:
In a pasta dish or other shallow bowl, whisk the oil, garlic, pepper, and salt. Break off small chunks of bread for dipping.

Submitter: 
Filippo Berio Olive Oil
Description: 
This delicious, deeply flavored dipping oil combines roasted garlic puree and a dash of cayenne pepper with extra virgin olive oil. Serve with sliced Italian bread.
