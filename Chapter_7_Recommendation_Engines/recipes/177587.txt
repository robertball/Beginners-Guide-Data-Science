Title:
Prego® Easy Party Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980876.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (4 pounds 3 ounces) jar Prego® Traditional Italian Sauce or Prego® Fresh Mushroom Italian Sauce
12 uncooked lasagna noodles
1 (32 ounce) container ricotta cheese
4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Spread about 2 cups sauce in a 13 1/2 x 9 1/2 x 3-inch disposable foil pan. Top with 6 lasagna noodles, the ricotta cheese, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, the Parmesan cheese and about 2 cups sauce. Top with the remaining lasagna noodles and sauce. Cover the pan.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour 30 minutes or until the noodles are tender. Uncover and top with the remaining mozzarella cheese. Let stand for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Cheeses, Italian sauce and noodles are layered and baked until hot and bubbling.
