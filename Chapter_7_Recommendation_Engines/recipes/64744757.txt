Title:
Big Ray's Greek Frittata Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6526145.jpg
Ingredients:
8 large eggs
1 5-ounce bag baby spinach
1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
4 scallions (white and green parts), thinly sliced
8 ounces feta, crumbled
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â° F.
Grease large muffin pan.
Meanwhile, in a bowl, whisk together the eggs, salt, and pepper. Seperate the spinach, tomatoes, scallions and feta equally into muffin sections.
Pour the egg mixture equally into muffin sections. Bake until the frittata is browned around the edges and slightly puffed and a knife comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
