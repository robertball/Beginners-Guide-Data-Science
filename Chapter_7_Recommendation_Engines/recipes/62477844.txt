Title:
Easy Vegetarian Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 PKG "no boil" lasagna noodles
32 oz low fat Ricotta cheese
6 oz shredded parmasan cheese
8 c blanched fresh veggies... ie: broccoli, shredded carrots, cauliflower,zuchini, yellow squash,mushrooms, asparagus, artichoke hearts etc
4 c loosly packed  fresh spinach leaves
32 oz jar spagetti sauce
4-6 cloves crushed garlic
1/4 c chopped sundried tomatoes
1/2 t salt
1/4 t ground pepper
1 t Italian seasoning
1 egg
1/4 c mayonaise
1/4 c chopped fresh parsley
4 c shredded mozzerella

Directions:
Bring large pot of water to boil and blanche 8 cups  of uniformly chopped fresh veggies for 4 minutes..use your favorites or what in season! Drain and set aside
meanwhile
combine ricotta, egg, parmesan cheese, mayo,garlic,sun dried tomatoes fresh parsley,italian seasoning, salt and pepper... mix well and set aside.
In 9x13 pan lay 1c spagetti sauce. Spread  3 uncooked lasagna sheets with thick layer of ricotta mixture (heaping tablespoon per sheet)and lay in pan. Add ... 2c blanched veggies,1 c spinach leaves, 1 c mozzerella and 1c sauce, repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
Refigerate for up to 24 hours or bake immediatley
Bake at 375 for 1 hour wrapped in foil, 15 minutes uncovered and resting for 10 minutes.
Slice and serve with cold salad and crusty bread

Submitter: 
Katie
Description: 
Easy Lasagna means no boil pasta...flexible veggies and prepared ingredients..this satisfies the whole house ...even the carnivores!
