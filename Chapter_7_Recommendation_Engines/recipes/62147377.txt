Title:
Delightful Cornish Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cornish Hens
2 Medium Onions (uncut)
2 Cloves Garlic
1 Green Pepper
1 Red Pepper
1 Handful of Fresh Cilantro Leaves and Stems
2 Tablespoons of Butter
2 Tsp Onion Powder
1 Tsp Adobo Seasoning
1 Packet of Sazon Seasoning
2 Tbsp of Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2) Dice both peppers until they are in very small pieces. (I usually use a chopper for this) Repeat with the cilantro.
3) Mix all seasoning in a small/medium bowl and add olive oil to mixture. Add Set aside.
4) Place one clove of garlic inside each hen followed by the onions.
5) Spread mixture of seasoning and oil ontop of hens and work with your hands to evenly cover surface of hens.
6) Place hens on a greased or foiled baking pan.
7) Top each hen with one tbsp of butter. Don't worry about spreading the butter it will melt over the hens.
8) Place hens in the oven for about 45 minutes or until juices run clear.
9) After the hens are cooked cut in half to serve 4

Description: 
Cornish hens are a bit smaller than chicken but cook faster and I find that they are much more juicy.
