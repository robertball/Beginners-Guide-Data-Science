Title:
Mom Madsen's Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-8 oz brick of Cream Cheese
1/2 cup unsalted butter
8 Cups powdered sugar
2 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon almond extract
Milk - if needed

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and butter in mixer until softened.
Add powdered sugar to cream cheese and blend well.
Add more sugar until spreading consistence.
Add vanilla and almond extract.
Add a little milk if needed.
