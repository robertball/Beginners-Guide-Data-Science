Title:
Special Hamburger Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/962321.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup creamy French dressing
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon dried minced onion
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients.  Store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Cairol  Ostrander
Description: 
'This thick sauce is the secret to making children happy,' promises Cairol Ostrander of Bainbridge, Georgia. 'It turns an ordinary burger into their favorite fast-food version.'
