Title:
Salmon Paillards with Rice and Pea SAlad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
3/4 teaspoon finely grated fresh lemon zest
2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon heavy cream or whole milk yogurt
1 tablespoon finely chopped chives
6 ounces sugar snap peas, trimmed and strings discarded
1/4 cup frozen baby peas (not thawed)
1 1/2 cups baby arugala
1 cup cooked rice
4 pieces skinless center-cut wild salmon fillet

Directions:
1. Stir together 2 tbs of olive oil and 1/2 teaspoon of lemon zest in a bow land set aside. Combine lemon juice, garlic, 1/8 teaspoon of salt, 1/8 teaspoon of pepper and remaining lemon zest ina  small bowl, then add remaining two tbs olive oil in a slow stream, whisking until combined. Whisk in cream or yogurt and chives.
2. Blank sugar snap peas in 3-4 saucepan of boiling salted water about 2 mins. Transfer sugar snaps into a bowl of ice. Cook baby peas in boiling water 5 minutes, then drain in a sieve and set sieve in ice.
3. Cut sugar snaps diagonally in 1/4 inch thick slices and transfer to a bowl, then toss with peas, arugala and rice.
4. Preheat broiler. Arrange 1 fillet, skin side down between two sheets of plastic. Wrap and lightly sprinke top of plastic with water. then pound to an even 1/4 inch thickness.
5. lightly brush bottom of 2 shallow baking pans with some of the lemon oil. flip each paillard over and carefully peel off sheet of plastic from skinned side, then invert fillets into baking pans. Carefully peel off remaining plastic wrap.
6. Sprinkle fillets with remaining 1/8 teaspoon salt and 1/8 teaspoon pepper, then brush with remaining lemon oil. Working in 2 batches, broil fillets 4 inches from heat until just cooked through, about 1 1/2 minutes per batch. Keep first batch warm, covered with foil while cooking second batch.
7. Toss salad with just enough dressing to coat.
8. Carefully transfer paillards with a metal spatula to 4 plates and spoon salad on on top.
