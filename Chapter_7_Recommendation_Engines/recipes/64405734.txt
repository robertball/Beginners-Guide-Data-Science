Title:
Mom's Apricot Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C oil - I use less
Small onion, minced
1/2 to 1 C apricot preserves
1 C ketchup
2 T Worchestershire sauce
Few drops hot sauce
5 # chicken wings

Directions:
Cook all ingredients except wings together for 5 mins. Pour over wings and marinate overnight.
Bake 350-400 degree oven for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
We all loved this when Mom made it and it was always a favorite of Melissa's.
