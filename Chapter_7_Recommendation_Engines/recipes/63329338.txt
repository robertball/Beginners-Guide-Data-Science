Title:
Freezer Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans refried black beans
3 cans refried pinto beans
1 cup salsa
3 Tbsp chili powder
1 lb. shredded cheese
60 flour tortillas (burrito size)

Directions:
Mix filling ingredients in a very large bowl.  Using an ice cream scoop, place about 1/4 cup in each tortilla and roll up, sealing both ends.  Wrap each burrito in aluminum foil and store in the freezer in plastic freezer bags.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
great to have on hand for days when there are no leftovers to send with hubby to work.
