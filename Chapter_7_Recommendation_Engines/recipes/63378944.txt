Title:
Mexican Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 cup salsa
1 package taco mix
2 cups water
2 cups instant brown rice
1 cup black beans
2 fresh sliced fresh jalapenos
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine salsa, seasoning, water, and chicken in a skillet and heat until it boils. Stir in rice, beans, jalapenos and cheese. Cook on low for 5-7 minutes or until rice is tender.
