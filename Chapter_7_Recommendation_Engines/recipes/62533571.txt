Title:
Vegetable Soup with Sweet Potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 small onions, finely chopped
4 garlic cloves,minced
1-28oz-can crushed tomatoes
6 cups vegetable stock
2 large sweet potatoes, peeled & cut into 1/2" cubes
15oz can white kidney beans (cannellini)
2 cups frozen green beans
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
Basil (1 Tbl fresh,minced)
20 oz frozen corn kernals

Directions:
In large soup pot, heat oil over med heat. Add onions & saute 6-7 min until translucent. Add garlic & saute 2-3 minutes longer.
Add tomatoes, stock, sweet potatoes. Bring to boil. Add white beans, green beans, salt, pepper & basil. Return to boil. Lower heat & cook for at least 30min. Stir in corn & heat through.
