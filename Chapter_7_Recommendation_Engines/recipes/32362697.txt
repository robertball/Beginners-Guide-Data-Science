Title:
Chili Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1/2 c. Chopped Onion
1/4 c. Chopped Green Pepper
8 oz. Tomato Sauce
2 tsp. Chili Powder
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
2 Cans Biscuits
1 c. Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Brown beef with onion and pepper.  Drain.  Add tomato sauce, chili powder and garlic powder. Quarter biscuits and sprinkle 1/2 on bottom of greased baking pan.  Top with beef mixture.  Add remaining biscuits on top.  Bake for 20 minutes.  Remove from oven and sprinkle with cheese.  Bake an additional 5 - 10 minutes until cheese is melted and begins to brown around edges.
