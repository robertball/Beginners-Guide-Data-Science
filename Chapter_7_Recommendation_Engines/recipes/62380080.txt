Title:
Vanilla Pudding-Microwave Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. Sugar
1/4 C. Cornstarch
3 C. Milk
4 egg yolks, beaten well
3 T. butter
1 1/2 t. vanilla

Directions:
In 1 1/2 qt. Casserole, stir together sugar and cornstarch. Stir in milk
Cook uncovered on high for 7-9 min. stirring every 2min. until slightly thickened,
then every 30 seconds for the remainder until thick and bubbly.
Cook one more minute.
In a small mixing bowl, gradually stir 1 C. of hot mixture into the egg yolks.
Return all to casserole.
Cook on high for 2-3 min. stirring every 30 sec. until edges are bubbly.
Cook for 30 sec. more.
Stir in butter and vanilla.
For butterscotch, substitute packed brown sugar for white
and increase butter to 1/4 C.
Cover with plastic to avoid skin when chilling.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Easy, from-scratch recipe for vanilla pudding in the microwave.
