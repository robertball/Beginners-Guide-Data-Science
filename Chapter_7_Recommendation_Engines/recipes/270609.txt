Title:
Instant Pot® Honey-Sriracha Glazed Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6352054.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup water
3 pounds boneless, spiral cut whole ham
1/2 cup honey
2 tablespoons sriracha sauce

Directions:
Add water to the pot of a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as an Instant Pot(R)) and place ham on top of water. Whisk honey and sriracha together in a bowl and pour over ham. Close and lock the lid.
Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 10 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 10 to 40 minutes. Transfer ham to a plate and let rest 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Change things up by adding some heat to your honey-glazed ham. Just a few minutes of your time and you can walk away while your Instant Pot® or multi-functional pressure cooker does the rest.
