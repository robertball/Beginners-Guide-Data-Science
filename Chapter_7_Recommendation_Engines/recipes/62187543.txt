Title:
Bread and Celery Stuffing - Budget Friendly Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/295669.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) loaf sliced white bread
3/4 cup butter
1 onion, chopped
4 stalks celery, chopped
2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon chicken bouillon
1 cup warm water

Directions:
Let bread slices air dry for 1 to 2 hours, then cut into cubes.
In a medium sized pot, melt butter over medium heat. Cook onion and celery until soft. Season with poultry seasoning, salt, and pepper. Stir in bread cubes until evenly coated. Disolve bouillon in warm water and pour over dish to moisten; mix well.
Bake in a buttered casserole dish at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Thanksgiving Budget Menu
Description: 
Replace the chicken stock with chicken bouillon for a budget saving option. This basic bread stuffing incorporates chopped celery, onion and seasoning for a delicious Thanksgiving side dish.Modified from: Bread and Celery Stuffing
