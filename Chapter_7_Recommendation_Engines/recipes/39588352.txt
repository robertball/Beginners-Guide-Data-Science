Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Rigatoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable oil for frying
1 eggplant, thinly sliced crosswise
flour for dredging
salt
16 oz rigatoni
2 t extra-virgin olive oil
2 cloves garlic, halved
28 oz can tomatoes
8 oz shredded mozzarella
8 fresh basel leaves, thinly sliced
freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. In lg. skillet, heat 1/4 cup veg. oil over med-high heat until hot but not smoking. Dredge eggplant slices in flour., shaking off any excess. Add enough eggplant slices to skillet to fill it without crowding and fry until golden brown, about 2 min on each side. Transfer to paper-towel-lined plate and sprinkle with salt. Repeat with remaining slices and more oil. Let cool, then cut into 1/2 in strips. Set aside
2. In large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta until al dente, drain and return to pot.
3. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in lg. saucepan over med. heat. Add garlic and cook, stirring, until golden, about 2 min. Stir in tomatoes and bring to boil, then cover and simmer until thickened, about 10 min.
4. Add tomato sauce to the cooked pasta and toss to coat, then stir in mozzerella. Divide the pasta among shallow bowls and top with reserved eggplant strips, basil and Parmesan
