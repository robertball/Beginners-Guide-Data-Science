Title:
Balsamic-Tomato Dipping Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C olive oil
1/4 C balsamic vinegar
2 cloves minced garlic
1/2 tsp seasoned salt
1TBS tomato paste
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in an airtight container. Shake vigorously to combine.
Store at room temp for 30 minutes prior to use.
Shake well, divide between dipping dishes.

Description: 
This is a flavorful bread dipping sauce that combines the sweet-tartness and richness of both tomato and balsamic with the nuttiness and freshness of olive oil- add in some seasoned salt and fresh pressed garlic with just a hint of heat and its a super combination for bread dipping. 
If you like yours spicy double the red pepper.
