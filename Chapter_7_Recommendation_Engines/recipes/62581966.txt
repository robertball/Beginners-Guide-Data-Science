Title:
Grandmama McGuire's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c flour
2 c sugar
1/2 tsp salt
3 eggs
3/4 c oil
1 1/3 tsp baking soda
1/2 c buttermilk
1 tsp vanilla
1 c nuts
4 lg ripe bananas

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, salt, add eggs, oil & dissolve the baking soda in the buttermilk & add it to the mixture. Add vanilla & beat by hand till smooth. Add nuts & mashed bananas. Bake in 2 well grease bread pans for 1 hr @ 350

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is my husband's grandmother's banana bread recipe. It's the best one I've ever tried & we all love it!
