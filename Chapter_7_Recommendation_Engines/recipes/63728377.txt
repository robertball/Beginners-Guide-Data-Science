Title:
Grilled Red Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4# pre-boiled new red potatoes
s &p to taste
veg. oil
4T mustard (your choice)
red onion, cut into 1/2in. rounds
1/2C olive oil
1/4C apple cider vinegar
4 green onions, chopped
fresh herbs of choice (parsley, chives, dill)

Directions:
Pre-heat grill
Cut potatoes in half.
Toss potatoes with veg. oil and salt and pepper.
Brush red onion slices with veg. oil
Grill potatoes cut side down with the onion slices turning the onions during the cooking process. Grill until tender.
Make the dressing combining the olive oil, cider vinegar, mustard and s&p to taste.                Cut red onions into bite size pieces and put in bowl with the grilled potatoes. Add the green onions, chopped parsley, chives and dill. Pour dressing over ingred. Stir to coat and serve.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
No mayo potato salad is perfect for potluck dinners and picnics.
