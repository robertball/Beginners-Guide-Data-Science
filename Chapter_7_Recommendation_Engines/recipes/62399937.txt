Title:
Tuscan Chicken Schtew - yes, Schtew!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can low-sodium chicken broth (49.5 oz)
1 can northern or other white beans (15 oz)
1 can stewed diced tomatoes with herbs (15 oz)
1 medium onion, diced
3 cloves garlic
2 tbs olive oil
1 cup frozen mixed veggies (such as peas, corn, carrots)
1 bay leaf
1 tbs dry italian seasoning
2 large boneless-skinless chicken breasts (frozen okay!)

Directions:
In a large heavy-bottomed soup pot over medium heat, saute onions and garlic in olive oil until softened and they begin to brown.
Add chicken breasts and all other ingredients - canned and dry.
Stir and simmer for 1 hour.
Remove chicken breasts.  Shred chicken with two forks and return to pot.
Remove bay leaf.  Serve or continue to simmer until reduced even further - up to one additional hour.  Enjoy!

Description: 
A low-fat and tummy warming dish for winter or summer nights.  Add fresh veggies from your local farmer's market and serve with crusty parmasean-garlic bread.  The wole family will love it! Mmmmm!
