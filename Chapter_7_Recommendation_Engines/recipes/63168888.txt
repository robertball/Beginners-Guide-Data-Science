Title:
Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856223.jpg
Ingredients:
French bread slices
6-8 eggs (or may add a few more)
3 c. milk
2 c grated sharp cheddar cheese
1 lb sausage (cooked and drained)
2 tbsp fresh parsley
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp dry mustard
pepper to taste

Directions:
Butter a 9x13 baking dish.  Cover bottom of dish with sliced French bread.  Layer cheese, sausage and parsley.  Combine eggs, milk, salt and dry mustard.  Pour over mixture.  Let set in the fridge overnight and bake for one hour in 350 degree oven (until set)  Can add onion, dill and a few more eggs.  This is good left over and reheated also.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
An easy brunch main dish to make up the night before and bake the morning of.  Try adding some different ingredients like fresh basil and fresh tomato (just before baking), and top with freshly grated parmesan cheese.  Try with fresh spinach.
