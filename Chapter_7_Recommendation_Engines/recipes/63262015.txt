Title:
Couscous and veggie salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c cooked couscous
8 cups coarsely chopped veggies-cucumber, tomatoes, radish, celery, peppers, red onions.
Dressing
1c olive oil
1/4c Dijon mustard
1/4c lemon juice
2T fresh basil
2t fresh thyme
2 cloves garlic, minced
1t salt
4 oz goat cheese or feta

Directions:
Process dressing ingredients together.
Add couscous and veggies together pour dressing and toss.
Sprinkle with cheese.
