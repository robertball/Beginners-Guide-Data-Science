Title:
weeknight Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground sirloin
1tbs choped garlic
1 onion, chopped
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
pepper to taste
season salt to taste
splash of worstershire sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons corn starch
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 small can beef broth
1 1/4 cups  sour cream
1 (8 ounce) package egg noodles

Directions:
Brown the beef add season salt and garlic  in a large skillet over medium high heat and drain excess fat. Add the onion and mushroom  saute until tender. Stir in the , paprika and ground black pepper.
mix the soup, beef broth  in a seperate bowl . season with worstershire sauce.add corn starch
Add the soup to beef mixture, reduce heat to medium low and let simmer for 15 minutes. Then add the sour cream, stirring well.
Prepare egg noodles according to package directions. Serve beef mixture over the egg noodles.

Submitter: 
melanie
Description: 
Ground Sirloin  , condensed cream of mushroom soup, and paprika are combined with onions and  sour cream for a different version of the traditional dish. It is served over egg noodles.Modified from: Beef Stroganoff II
