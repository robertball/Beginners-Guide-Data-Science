Title:
Carrot and Raisin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded carrots
1/2 cup raisins
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
1-2 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the carrots, raisins, mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar. Mix together and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
hoglundks
Description: 
Make a big batch of this terrific salad, because it will go fast. Shredded carrots, a scoop of raisins, and mayonnaise with a dash of vinegar and sugar.Modified from: Carrot and Raisin Salad I
