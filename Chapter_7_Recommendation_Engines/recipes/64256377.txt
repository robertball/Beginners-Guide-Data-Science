Title:
Sauce for Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C water
1/4 C orange juice
1/3 C Rice Vinegar
2 1/2 T soy sauce
1 T orange zest
1 C brown sugar
1/2 t ginger root
1/2 t garlic
1/4 t red pepper flakes
2 T green onion
3 T cornstarch
1/4 C water

Directions:
Combine cornstarch and 1/4 cup water, set aside. In large saucepan add remaining water, orange juice, rice vinegar and soy sauce. Heat until warm through. Add brown sugar, ginger garlic, red pepper flakes and orange zest. Bring to simmer.  Add cornstarch mixture, cook until sauce thickens.
