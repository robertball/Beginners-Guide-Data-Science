Title:
Boursin on a Budget Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic minced
2 (8oz) packages softened cream cheese
1 cup butter or margarine (I use real butter)softened
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp. dried basil
1/4 tsp. dried dillweed
1/4 tsp. marjoram
1/4 tsp. dried thyme
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Blend all the ingredients below in your food processor.  If you don't have one you can use a mini-food chopper in batches, if you really don't have anything else, use your own elbow grease and a wooden spoon.  Just get it all mixed up good.
