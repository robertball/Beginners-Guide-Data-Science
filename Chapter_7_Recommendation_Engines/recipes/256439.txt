Title:
Almond Butter Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/4400006.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 tablespoon cacao powder
2 packets stevia powder
1 ½ teaspoons almond butter
1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions:
Combine almond milk, cacao powder, stevia powder, almond butter, and cinnamon in a small pot over medium heat; cook, whisking occasionally, until mixture is hot, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Steamy chocolate and almond butter melt in your mouth in this hot chocolate. A decadent and healthy drink! Top with GF marshmallows or whipped cream!
