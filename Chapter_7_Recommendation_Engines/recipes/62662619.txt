Title:
Parmesan Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pound Yukon Gold Potatoes (about 3 medium)
1 egg white, lightly beaten
3/4 t garlic salt
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Oven to 425
Wedge potatoes, then combine with egg white, garlic salt, and parmesan cheese.
Spread onto greased baking sheet and bake until crispy tender - roughly 35 to 45 minutes
