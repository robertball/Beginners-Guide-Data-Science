Title:
Granola Susage and Cheese Sandwich Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bread
20 grams butter, melted
4 tablespoon granola
1/4 piece onion, minced
4 pcs Japanese-style wiener sausages
1 slice cheddar cheese
1 slice Camembert cheese

Directions:
Remove the bread crusts if you prefer. Mince the onions and slice the sausages thinly.
Put the 2 cheese on top of a bread then add 2 tablespoons of granola on top. Add the onions and te sausage then sandwich it with another slice of bread.
Preheat the sandwich maker then once done, grease the sandwich plates using the melted butter.
Place the sandwiches on the the sandwich maker then toast for 5 minutes or until crispy.
Slice the sandwich in half before serving.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
Granola, cheese, and sausage - all your favorite breakfast food packed in one sandwich toast. This is a great energy food before you start your day.
