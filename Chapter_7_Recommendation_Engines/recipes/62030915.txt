Title:
Cookie Cutter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup margarine (Parkay or Blue Bonnet)
1/4 cup shortening can use butter flavor
1/2 cup
3 eggs
1 1/2 flour
Flavorings choose 1:
Egg nogg 1 tesp. nutmeg + 2 tesp rum flavoring
Cherry 1 tesp. almond extract 6 to
8oz chopped Marachino cherries
Lemon or Orange 2 tesp extract +
1 tesp. lemon or orange peel
Vanilla 1  tesp.

Directions:
cream sugar and shorten and margarine together add eggs, then milk. Sift dry ingredients together and add a litle at a time until all is in.  Grease and flour a jelly roll pan.  Pour mix into pan and spread evenly.  Bake in preheated oven at 350' for 20 to 25 minutes.  Test for doneness
Cool on rack 10 minutes.  Choose a 1" cookie cutter shape and cut as many shapes out as possible.  Cool  completely before decorating.  These can be frozen and decorated later.  Use candy or pipe icing to decorate.
