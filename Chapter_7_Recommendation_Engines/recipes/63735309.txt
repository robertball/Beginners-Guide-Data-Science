Title:
Super Easy Crockpot Chicken Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
• 4 thawed chicken breasts
• 6oz. package of Stove Top stuffing mix
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1 can of cream of chicken soup

Directions:
Place chicken in crock pot. Pour stuffing mix on top. Mix sour cream, soup and 1/4 water in bowl. Pour over chicken and stuffing. Cook on low for 4 hours.
