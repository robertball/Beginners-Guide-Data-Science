Title:
Tipsy vanilla fruit pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
1 package sugar free vanilla pudding mix
1 cup reduced fat sour cream
1 cup low fat all purpose cream
milk for consistency (optional)
2 tablespoons brandy 2 tablespoons sherry
1 can pinapple pieces
1 can mandarin pieces
handfull of chopped walnuts (or other nut)
Handful of sweet cocunut shavings (bakery aisle)

Directions:
Directions: Combine pudding mix, sour cream, cream,alcohol, 1 tablespoon of juice from each fruit can.Add milk if necessary to get pudding consistency.
Drain fruits and mix in nuts.
Fold fruits into pudding mixture.
Sprinkle with coconut flakes and refrigerate for 4 hours or until serving.

Description: 
This recipe is great if you have little time but want to make a dessert with a twist for entertaining.  Grown adults were licking the bowl!
