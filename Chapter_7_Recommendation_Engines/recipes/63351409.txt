Title:
Pauline Yowell's Enchilada's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 can tomato sauce
2 cans enchilada sauce
1/2 tsp garlic
1 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1 doz corn tortillas
1 lb grated cheese
1 sm can ripe olives, sliced

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2.  Butter 9x12" baking dish.
3.  Combine enchilada and tomato sauce, set aside.
4.  Fry ground beef, garlic, salt and pepper.  Drain well.
5.  Dip corn tortillas in sauce.
6.  Layer tortilla, meat, cheese, onions, olives.  Repeat layers and cover with remaining sauce.
7.  Bake 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lucy Dee
Description: 
This recipe was given to my grandmother by a good friend.  I found it in an old recipe box.  My grandmother had it enclosed in a plastic cover to keep the recipe from fading.
