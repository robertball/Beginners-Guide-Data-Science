Title:
Turkey Stuffing Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package stuffing mix
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
3/4 cup milk
1 pound sliced deli smoked turkey
1 (2.8 ounce) package French-fried onions, crushed

Directions:
Prepare stuffing mix according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a bowl, combine soup and milk; set aside.
Spoon about 1/4 cup stuffing onto each turkey slice. Roll up and place in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Pour soup mixture over roll-ups.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with onions. Bake 5 minutes longer or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
This family-pleasing recipe is quick with boxed stuffing mix in place of homemade dressing. It's wonderful with salad and green beans.
