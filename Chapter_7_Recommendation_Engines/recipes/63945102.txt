Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of browned sausage
1 package frozen hash browns
2 cups cheddar cheese
9 eggs
1 package crescent rolls

Directions:
Press crescent rolls into bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
Mix sausage, hash browns, and cheese and spread over rolls.
Whisk eggs together and pour over sausage and potatoes.
Bake at 375 degree oven for 40-45 minutes.
