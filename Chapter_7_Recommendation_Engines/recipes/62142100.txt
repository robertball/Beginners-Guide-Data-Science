Title:
Tangy Mustard Baked Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. wild salmon filets
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup mustard
Juice from 1/2 a lemon
1 tablespoon garlic powder
salt & pepper to taste
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Heat oven to 425.
Line a baking dish with aluminum foil & place rinsed salmon filets in dish, skin side down.  In a mixing bowl combine mayonnaise, mustard, lemon juice, garlic powder, & salt & pepper.
Pour sauce evenly over salmon filets.
Top salmon with chopped pecans.
Bake for about 25 minutes, or until sauce & pecans turn golden brown.

Submitter: 
Mommy Epstein
Description: 
Wild salmon filets are topped with a simple tangy mustard sauce and baked with ease in the oven.  Delicious when served over brown rice.
