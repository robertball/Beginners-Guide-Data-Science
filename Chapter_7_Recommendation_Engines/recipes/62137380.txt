Title:
Creamy Cheesy Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289729.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter, melted
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1/4 cup melted butter
1/4 cup flour
2 cups heavy cream
2 cups chicken broth
steamed broccoli (as much or as little as you want)
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
12 ounces shredded cheese

Directions:
Saute onion in butter. Set aside. Cook melted butter and flour using a whisk over medium heat for 3-5 minutes in a large pot. Stir constantly while adding heavy cream. Add the chicken broth whisking all the time. Add the steamed broccoli, onions, dijon mustard, nutmeg, and cheese. Cook over low or simmering heat for 15 minutes stirring often. Add salt and pepper to taste.
