Title:
Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon butter or margarine
1 Tortilla
1/4 cup shredded mexican or taco cheese
Fajita seasoning to taste
Taco Sauce to taste
Salsa
Also great with chicken!

Directions:
1) Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
2) Place tortilla flat in melted butter and spread cheese evenly over half of the exposed side.
3) Sprinkle fajita seasoning over cheese and evenly drizzle with taco sauce.
4) Fold plain side over to cover cheese.
5) When bottom is light golden brown, flip quesadilla.
6) Cook until both sides are light golden brown and cheese is melted inside.  Serve warm with cold salsa.
