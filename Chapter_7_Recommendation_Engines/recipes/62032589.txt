Title:
Blue Cheese Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. unpeeled new potatoes, quartered
1 bottle (8 oz.) creamy blue cheese dressing
3/4 cup blue cheese, crumbled
1 jar real bacon pieces
1 Tbs. dried parsley flakes
1 Tbs. dried sweet pepper flakes
1 tsp. ground black pepper
1 tsp. dried chopped onion

Directions:
Place potatoes in large saucepan and cover with salted water. Bring to a boil and cook until potatoes are tender; drain and rinse with cold water to stop cooking process. In large bowl, combine remaining ingredients; stir in potatoes. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours for flavors to blend. Serve chilled.
