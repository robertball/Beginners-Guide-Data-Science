Title:
Spaghetti Carbonara-Paleo Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027951.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large spaghetti squash cooked
8 Pieces of Bacon
4 Large Egg Yolks
1 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Pepper
4 tbs. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Large Tomato diced
3 Sprigs of Fresh Basil (For garnish)

Directions:
Cook Spaghetti Squash so that you can shred it into strands.
Dice Bacon and put in large pan with olive oil, cook on medium-high heat until cooked thoroughly.
Add cooked and shredded spaghetti squash, cook with bacon for 3-5 minutes or until soft.
Add Pepper, Salt and Tomato, stir and pull off of heat.
Add egg yolks while dish is still hot, but not hot enough to make egg strands in dish. Avoid touching eggs to the bottom of pan. Stir until creamy.
Stir together and garnish with basil or desired ingredients.
