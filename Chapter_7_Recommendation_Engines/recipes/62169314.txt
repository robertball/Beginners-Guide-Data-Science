Title:
Joan's Thanksgiving Cake with Phoebe G's Caramel Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031824.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. spice cake mix
1 c. pumpkin
1/2 c. oil
1 pkg. instant vanilla pudding
3 eggs
1/2 cup water
1 t. cinnamon
1/2 c. chopped nuts (optional)
1 c. raisins (optional)
Frosting:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup light brow sugar
1/2 cup milk
2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 °
Grease and flour a 9"x13" pan or two 9" rounds
Combine all ingredients and pour into prepared pan(s)
Bake @ 350° for 40-45 minutes for 9x13 or 25-30 for layers.
FOR FROSTING:
Melt butter; add brown sugar and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until sugar is dissolved.  Add milk and continue stirring until boiling.  Remove from heat and cool.  Add confectioner's sugar and beat w/ electric mixer.  If frosting becomes too thick, add a small amount of boiling water.
*If you are unable to find a spice cake mix, use a yellow cake mix with the following spices:  1/2 t. ground cinnamon, 1/4 t. ground ginger, 1/4 t. ground nutmeg and 1/2 t. ground cloves.
