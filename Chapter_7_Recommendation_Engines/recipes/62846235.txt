Title:
Cashew Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts, cut in pieces
1 T. cornstarch
1 T. soy sauce
1 T. sherry
salt
3 T. oil
1 T. minced onion
1/2 c. chopped celery
1 bell pepper, cut in squares
1 can sliced water chestnuts
1/2 c. cashews
rice
Gravy Mix:
1 T. cornstarch
1/2 c. chicken broth
1 T. soy sauce

Directions:
Combine cornstarch, soy sauce, sherry, salt, oil & ginger. Marinate meat for 20 min. Saute onion, celery and pepper  with 1 T. oil over hight heat for 1-2 min. Remove to platter. Saute chicken in 1 T. oil over high heat until browned. Add cashews and water chestnuts. Mix well. Add gravy mix and stir over med.high heat until thick. Serve over rice.
