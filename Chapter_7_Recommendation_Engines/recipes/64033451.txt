Title:
Onion Soup Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup instant mince donion
4 tsp onion powder
1/3 cup beef boullion powder
1/4 tsp celery seed, rushed
1/4 tsp sugar

Directions:
Mix all.  Store in airtight container.  To use as soup, combine 2 tbsp mix to 1 cup boiling water.  Cover and simmer for 15 min.
