Title:
Tomato Gorgonzola Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 cup red onion, diced
1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup celery, diced
3 cloves garlic, grated
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup heavy cream
2 (14.5 oz.) cans diced tomatoes
3 cups tomato juice
2 tsp. white sugar
1/2 tsp. white pepper
basil

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion, pepper, celery and garlic; cook and stir 4-5 minutes or until vegetables are soft but not browned.
Add the cream cheese, gorgonzola, milk and cream; heat mixture until the cheeses melt and the mixture is simmering.
Stir in the undrained tomatoes, tomato juice, sugar and pepper. Continue to simmer mixture for 15-20 minutes while stirring constantly. Do not boil.
