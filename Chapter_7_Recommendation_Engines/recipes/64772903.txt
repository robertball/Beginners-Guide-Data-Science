Title:
Macarons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Ground almond flour
1 cup powdered sugar
2 large egg whites room temperature
1/4 cup granulated sugar
food coloring

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350. Line two bakin sheets with silicone mats or parchment paper.
2. Mix powdered sugar and almond flour. Sift mixture through a mesh strainer.
3. Place egg whites in a very clean bowl of an electric mixer and beat until soft peaks form. Slowly add the sugar and continue whipping until very stiff. Add desired food coloring.
4. Fold in flour mixture 35-40 times.
5. Place a pastry bag fitted with a plain 1/2 inch tip, upright in a tall drinking glass. Pour batter into bag.
6. Pipe 1 1/2 inch rounds onto the prepared pans, leaving 1 inch between each cookie. Tap the pan several times fimly on the counter to get out bubbles. Let sit at room temperature for 10-15 minutes.
7. Bake for 13 minutes, rotating the pan halfway through.
8. Cool before removing and piping filling.
Variation: Add vanilla beans. Chocolate - reduce powdered sugar to 3/4 cup and add 2 tablespoons cocoa powder.
