Title:
Easy Angel Food Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 angel food cake mix
1 20 ounce can of crushed pineapple

Directions:
Fold pineapple into dry cake mix.  Do not beat with a mixer. Put in ungreased 13 x 9 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes. Serve with whipped cream.
