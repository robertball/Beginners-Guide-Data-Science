Title:
Sunburst Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can refried beans
1 16 oz sour cream
1 pkg taco seasoning
3 cups gratied cheddar cheese
4 tomotoes diced fine
1 bundle green onion diced fine
1 9oz can diced black olives
1-2 bags tortilla chips

Directions:
1.  On a large round platter spread the refried beans-stop about 2 inches from the edge of platter.
2.  combine the sourcream and taco seasoning.  Spread over beans.
3.  edge the sour cream and beans with the grated cheese still leaving the 2inch edge of the platter empty.
4.  mix tomatoes, onion, and olives together in a bowl.
5.  put the mixture on the very edge of the platter.
When your done it should look like a sun-with the outer rim salsa, next rim cheese and the middle sourcream.
Serve with tortilla chips!!OLe
