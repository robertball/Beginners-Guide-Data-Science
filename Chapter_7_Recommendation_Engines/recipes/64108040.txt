Title:
Gluten Free Pancakes or Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c rice flour
1/3 c tapioca flour
2/3 c potato starch flour
3 tbs sucanat (coconut sugar)
1 tbs baking powder
1.5 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
2 beaten eggs
2.25 c buttermilk (pancakes or 2.5 c buttermilk for wafffles)
4 tbs butter
1tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix up dry ingredients.
Mix in wet ingredients.
Use on hot griddle or waffle iron.  Pancakes are flipped once they bubble and cooked for another minute until second side is lightly browned/golden.

Submitter: 
MONIQUEWS
Description: 
Easy GF pancake recipe for the whole family.  I pre-mix the dry ingredients so I need only use 3 cups of dry for one set of wet ingredients.
