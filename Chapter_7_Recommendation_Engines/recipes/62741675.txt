Title:
Spicy Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs.chicken wings
3/4 cup apple cider vinegar
3/4 cup sugar
2 Tbsp.hot sauce
1 Tbsp.chili powder
1 Tbsp.onion powder
1 Tbsp.garlic powder
1/2 tsp.cayenne powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.Set aside 3/4 cup of mixture for dipping sauce.
Grill wings for 15-20min.Brush often with sauce.Flip wings to other side,cook additional 15-20 min.Bast well with sauce.
Serve with remaining sauce.

Description: 
Grilled wings with a spicy hot sauce and chili flavor.
