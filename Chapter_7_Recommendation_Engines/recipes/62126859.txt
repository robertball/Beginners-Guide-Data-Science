Title:
Caramel Pecan Cinnamon Roll Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, melted
2 1/4 cups bread flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tbsp. cinnamon
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
2 tbsp. milk
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup pecan pieces
caramels

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.
Whisk together dry ingredients. Set aside.
Mix together melted butter and sugars. Add egg, yolk, milk, and vanilla. Slowly add flour mixture until thoroughly combined. Add white chocolate chips and pecan pieces.
Chill dough. Scoop onto baking sheet by rounded tablespoons. Bake for 15 min. or until golden brown. Rotate baking sheet for even browning. Cool completely.
Melt caramels then drizzle over cooled cookies.

I nice chewy cookie that has the flavors of a good cinnamon roll. A family favorite.
