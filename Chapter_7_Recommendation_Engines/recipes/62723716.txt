Title:
Joe's Spicy Hot and Sour Soup with Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 tablespoons tom yum paste
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
4 stalks lemon grass, chopped
4 lime leaves
7 ounces fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
1 tablespoon fish sauce
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 jalapeno pepper, chopped (seeds included
8 ounces raw shrimp, shelled and deveined
Chopped cilantro (to taste)
Chopped basil (to taste

Directions:
1.  In a medium to large saucepan, bring the chicken broth to a boil.  Stir in the tom yum paste and garlic and simmer for about 2-3 minutes.  Stir in the lemongrass and lime leaves.
2.  Mix in the mushrooms.  Add the fish sauce, lime juice, and chopped jalapeno with seeds.  Simmer for 5-6 minutes.  Add the raw shrimp and cook until the shrimp turn pink.
3.  Remove from the heat and add chopped cilantro and basil to saucepan and serve.
