Title:
Baked Potatoe Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993868.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Red Potatoes, sliced wedges
3 TBSP Garlic Salt (Johnny's Garlic Salt)
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1/8 cup  Parmesan Cheese
1 gallon Ziploc Bag

Directions:
Wash and slice into wedges.
Preheat oven 375'
Place potatoes in ziploc bag with oil, garlic salt, and cheese; shake well to coat.
Spread out wedges on a foil lined baking sheet (greased).
Bake for 25 minutes or until cooked through; turn heat up to 400' for 10 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Rachael Welch
Description: 
Goes great with chicken caesar wraps or pulled pork sandwiches.
