Title:
Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
yellow onions
buttermilk
cayenne & paprika
flour

Directions:
mix cayenne and paprika to flour mixture
slice onions and add to buttermilk
dredge onions in flour mixture for the first time and then dredge again, remove and shake off excess - add to oil, cook for 1 minute, remove and sprinkle with salt
