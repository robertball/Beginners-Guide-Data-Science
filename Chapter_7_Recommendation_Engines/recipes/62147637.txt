Title:
Almond Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup raw, unsalted almonds
1 tbsp. Tapioca Flour
32 fl oz. water
1 tsp soy lecithin
vanilla extract
sweetener

Directions:
Boil tapioca flour with 8 oz. water in microwave, which takes about 3 minutes.
Add boiled tapioca mixture to high-powered blender with the rest of the ingredients.
Blend on high until well blended.
Strain through cheese cloth layers or through two 1 gallon paint straining bags.
Refridgerate and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kyko
Description: 
This is a lighter almond milk, more like the store bought varieties. It's only around 40 calories per cup, which makes it a great option for dieters and health nuts alike. It is vegan and nutritious.
