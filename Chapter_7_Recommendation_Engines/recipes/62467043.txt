Title:
White Clam Sauce for Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (51oz.) Chopped Sea Clams
1/4# Butter
3/4 C. olive Oil
1 1/4 Teaspoon pepper
2 1/2 Teaspoon Oregano
5 Tablespoon Basil
5 Tablespoon Crushed Garlic (or to taste)
1 48oz. can Chicken Broth
7 1/4 Tablespoon Parsley
1 Teaspoon salt
Pasta of your choice
Grated Parmasan/Romano Cheese (optional)

Directions:
In a skillet heat the butter and olive oil to 350. Drain the clams and set the stock asside. Add the clams to the oil and cook till they start to "pop". Reduce the heat and add the clam stock and the chicken broth. Now add the pepper, oregano, basil, garlic, salt and parsley. Bring this to a boil and then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour. Prepair the pasta. Pour the sauce ove the pasta as desired. Sprinkle with the parmasan/romano cheese
