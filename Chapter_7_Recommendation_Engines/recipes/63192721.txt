Title:
Moravian Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/823473.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs Stew Beef (lean)
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
3 Cups thinly sliced onions
1 12oz can stewed tomatoes
4 teaspoons paprika
1 Cup sliced dill pickle spears

Directions:
Heat stew meat on medium high in oil until thoroughly browned.
Remove from pan and set aside, leaving drippings in the pan.
Cook the onions in the drippings until soft on medium heat, approximately 5 minutes.
Add paprika, tomatoes and meat.  Cook on low heat, covered, for 20-30 minutes.
For the last five minutes of heating, add dill pickle spear slices.  Best served over rice.

Submitter: 
Bonnie D
Description: 
Great dish for serving with rice.  Stew beef, tomatoes and a little surprise tang from sliced pickles.  A favorite of my family's, we always request this as our birthday dinner.
