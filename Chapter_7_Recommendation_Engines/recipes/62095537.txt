Title:
Strawberry Peach Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 peaches, peeled, pitted, and sliced
2 cups strawberries, sliced
1/2 C white sugar
1/2 C Splenda (for baking)
2 C whole wheat flour
1 C all purpose flour
1/2 T cinnamon
1 t salt
1 t baking soda
3/4 C vegetable oil or applesauce
4 eggs, beaten
1 t vanilla
a C chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 for muffins or 325 for loaves. Prepae muffin or loaf pans for baking (for loaf pans, coat with cinnamon sugar instead of flour for a treat).
Combine fruit, sugar, and Splenda in a bowl. Toss together. Puree about half of this mixture in food processor or blender, let the other half sit.
Combine flour, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda in large bowl.
Blend eggs, oil, and vanilla. Add to dry ingredients.
Add fruit puree and fruit mixture to batter. Stir until all ingredients are moistened, then stir in nuts.
Divide batter into pans or muffin tins.
For loaf pans, bake 60-70 minutes (time may very depending on juicyness of fruit) or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. For muffins, bake 20-25 minutes. Let cool in pans for about 10 minutes, then turn out onto cooling rack to cool completely.

Description: 
I was looking to use up an excess of summer fruit, but in a way that was healthier than the many dessert recipes out there. This turned out to be a healthy treat!
