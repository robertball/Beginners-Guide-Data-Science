Title:
Christmas Brunch Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 tablespoon butter, melted
1 tablespoon dry minced onion
4 cups frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
6 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1 pinch dried parsley
2 cups shredded Sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).  Grease a 9x13 inch casserole dish.
In a large bowl, beat eggs with milk and parsley.  Add the rest of the ingredients EXCEPT bacon, cheese and french fried onions.  Mix well and add to prepared baking dish.  Top with grated cheese. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Before baking, top with bacon and french fried onion rings.  Bake in preheated oven for 45 mins to 1 hour or until set. If preparing and baking the same day, cook for 30-45 minutes.
