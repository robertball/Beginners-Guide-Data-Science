Title:
Ham & Cheese Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 dinner rolls
24 slices sweet deli ham
12 slices swiss cheese
1 tbsp poppy seeds
1 1/2 tbsp yellow mustard
1/4 cup melted butter
1 tbsp onion powder
1/2 tsp worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together poppy seeds, mustard, butter, onion powder & worcestershire. Spread sauce onto 1 side of roll. Place 2 slices of ham & slice of swiss cheese in roll. Replace top of the rolls & bunch them closely together into a baking dish.
Cover with aluminum foil and let sit about 10 minutes.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Heidi O
Description: 
These don't last long in my house! Great, quick & tasty warm ham & cheese slider sandwiches that even picky eaters will love.
