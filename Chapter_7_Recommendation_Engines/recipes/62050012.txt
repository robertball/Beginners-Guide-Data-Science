Title:
Roasted Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant, quartered and sliced into 1/2 inch pieces
2 small yellow squash, halved and sliced
1 red bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips
1 bunch asparagus, cut into 2 inch pieces
1/2 red onion, sliced
4 cloves of garlic
2 lemons juiced
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
3 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste.

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a large baking sheet.
Spread the sliced eggplant, squash and garlic cloves out onto the prepared baking sheet and place into the oven. Bake for 15 minutes.
In a large serving bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, vinegar, parsley, oregano, salt and pepper; set aside.

Submitter: 
your mom
Description: 
This is a delicious vegetable salad made with eggplant, yellow squash, asparagus and red pepper. It is colorful as well as delicious.
