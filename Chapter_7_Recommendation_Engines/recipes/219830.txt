Title:
Classic Nachos with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988512.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (300 g) package tortilla chips
2 cups KRAFT Habanero Heat Shredded Cheese
1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Spread tortilla chips onto baking sheet; top with cheese.
Bake 6 to 8 min. or until cheese is melted.
Top with tomatoes and cilantro.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
Fiery shredded cheese adds a kick to these nachos that are topped with fresh chopped tomatoes and cilantro.
