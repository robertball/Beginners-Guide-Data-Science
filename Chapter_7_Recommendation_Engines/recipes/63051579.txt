Title:
Chicken and Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Uncooked Rice
1 pkg of Lipton Dry Onion Soup Mix
4-6 Pieces of Chicken (use your preference of cut)
2 Cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 Can water

Directions:
Put uncooked rice in the bottom or a greased 9x13 casserole pan.
Sprinkle 1/2 a pkg of onion soup mix over the rice.
Add cut up pieces of chicken.
Mix together Mushroom soup and water.
Pour over chicken.
Sprinkle the remaining onion soup mix over the top of all.
Seal the pan with aluminum foil and bake at 300* for 2 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Rachael Barney Smith
Description: 
Good for putting in the oven before going to church or a concert or a movie.  Cooks for 2 1/2 hours.
