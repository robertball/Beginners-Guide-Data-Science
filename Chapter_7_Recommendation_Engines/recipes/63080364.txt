Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819712.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup long grain rice
2 cups water
4oz tomato sauce
1 large chicken bouillon cube or 2 small cubes
oil

Directions:
In medium sauce pan add oil turn heat to medium high add rice and toast until rice is golden and puffy.
Add water, tomato sauce, and bouillon to the rice and bring to boil for 2-3 minutes.
Reduce heat and cover rice, simmer for 20 minutes DO NOT STIR!
