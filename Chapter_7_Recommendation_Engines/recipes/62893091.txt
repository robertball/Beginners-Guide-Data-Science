Title:
Puff Pastry With Sugar And Orange Zest Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/699464.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of sugar
zest from 3 oranges
2 rolls of puff pastry dough

Directions:
take zest and mix it with sugar
Roll out dough on top of cling wrap
spread 1/4 of the sugar on each of the sides
pull on the cling wrap on one side and roll dough up to the center.
pull on the cling wrap on the other side and roll dough up to the center.
cover with cling wrap and put back into the fridge for 20-30 Min's
get back out of the fridge, and slice 1/2 inch cookies
put on parchment paper or a silicone mat on a jelly roll pan
put in oven at 400F for about 20 Min's till golden brown.
let cool, serve
