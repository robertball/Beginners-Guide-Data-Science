Title:
Roasted Sweets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2170873.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 Tbsp. Coconut Oil
1 Sweet Onion, chopped
1 Sweet Apple, chopped
3 Sweet Potatoes, Peeled & Cubed
1 Garlic clove, minced
1 Tbsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Chipotle pepper or Cayenne
Salt to taste

Directions:
Drop coconut oil on baking pan. Add prepared ingredients. Sprinkle with cinnamon, chipotle & salt. Roast at 400Â° for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
