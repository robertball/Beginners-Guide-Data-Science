Title:
Peach Dessert Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter or margarine, softened
1 c sugar
1 t lemon rind
2 eggs
1 c flour
1 t baking powder
1/4 t salt
4 fresh peaches, peeled and cut into small pieces
Cinnamon, sugar and nuts if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cream together softened butter, sugar and eggs.  Gradually add flour followed by lemon rind, baking powder, and salt.  Mix until well blended.  Place half of the mix in an 8x8x2" greased pan.  Palce cut up peaches on top.  Pour the rest of the dough over the peaches.  Sprinkle the top with cinnamon, sugar and nuts if desired.  Bake for 50 minutes.
