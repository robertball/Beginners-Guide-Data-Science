Title:
Special Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 TBS extra virgin olive oil
4 garlic cloves minced or put through a press
1 onion diced
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
Salt and black pepper
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup Marsala, dry not sweet
1/2 cup beef broth
1 28 ounce can tomato puree
1 cup fresh basil chopped

Directions:
Brown beef with oil, onions, and garlic in a large nonstick skillet over med-high heat and drain off fat.  Then add red pepper flakes, Worcestershire, and salt and pepper and cook about 5 minutes.  Then add Marsala and let it cook off about a minute, then add beef broth.  Stir in the tomato puree and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to med-low and simmer for 20 minutes.  Sprinkle with basil just before serving.
