Title:
German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large potatoes, peeled, cut into 1 inch chunks
4 slices bacon, cut into small pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 tbs sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
2 hard boiled eggs

Directions:
Boil the potatoes until they are easily forked.  Drain and set aside.
Cook the bacon bits until crisp, remove from pan and set aside.  Stir the onion into the grease, cooking till tender.  Stir bacon back in adding all other ingredients.  Fold gently to mix.
Pour out into a serving bowl, garnish with hard boiled egg wedges and serve while still hot!
