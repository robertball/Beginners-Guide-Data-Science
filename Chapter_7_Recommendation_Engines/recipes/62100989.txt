Title:
EASY Chocolate Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Grease or spray a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Place the sugar and evaporated milk into a large heavy pot.  Over high heat, bring to a rolling boil, stirring frequently; reduce the heat to medium and continue to boil for 8 minutes (NO LONGER), stirring occasionally.  Remove from the heat.
Add the peanut butter, butter, and vanilla and stir until well blended.  Add the marshmallows and chocolate bits (walnuts are optional).  Stir together until marshmallows and chocolate bits are melted and well blended.
Spread into the prepared pan and allow to become almost completely cool.
Turn out onto a cutting board and slice into 1" squares.
Refrigerate, covered, until ready to serve.

Directions:
4 1/3 cups granulated sugar
12 oz. can evaporated milk
8 oz. mini-marshmallows
18 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup butter or margarine, sliced
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1 cup chopped walnuts -- optional

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
NO COOKING THERMOMETER REQUIRED!!  Simple, sure-fire, quick and easy to make!  A hit whenever I have made it.  GREAT FOR BEGINNINERS!!
