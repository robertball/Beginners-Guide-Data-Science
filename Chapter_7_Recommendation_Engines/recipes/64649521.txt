Title:
Easy Bake Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123339.jpg
Ingredients:
3 T. bacon drippings
2 coarsely chopped onion
1/2 # ground beef
1 1/2 t. salt
3 1/2 cups or one can tomatoes
1/8 t. pepper
1 t. chili powder
8 oz. raw spaghetti
1 cup water
1 cup grated cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 325 degrees.
Heat drippings in a large skillet, add onions and cook slowly for 5 minutes.
Add beef and cook, stirring occasionally until browned.
Stir in next 4 ingredients, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
Break half the raw spaghetti in two and put in a greased 2 qt. casserole dish.
Add water to sauce and pour half over half of spaghetti.
Sprinkle with half of cheese.
Break rest of spaghetti and put on top and add other half of sauce and cheese.
Bake for 35 minutes covered and 15 minutes uncovered.
