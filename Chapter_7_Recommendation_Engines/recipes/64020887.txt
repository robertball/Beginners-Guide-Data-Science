Title:
Meusili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c. oats
1/2 c. sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, slivered almonds, coconut flakes
1 c. raisins

Directions:
Mix together. Eat either with almond milk or heat some water to soften.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
I like to soften with hot water, then add a little crunchy granola and some fresh fruit. Nutritious, delicious, and inexpensive.
