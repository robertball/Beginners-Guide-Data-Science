Title:
Whitney Millers Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup self rising flour
2 Tab. butter flavored shortening, chilled
1/3 cup milk
1 Tab. canola oil
2 Tab. unsalted butter, melted
butter and cane syrup for serving

Directions:
425 degrees
Place flour in med. bowl. Using pastry cutter or fork cut the shortening into the flour. stir in the milk until incorprated. Lightly flour your hands and form dough into a ball. Divide into 4 portions. Form each into a smooth ball by rolling it between your palms 5 times. Tuck any rough edges into the top of the ball. Roll between your hands 10 more times to form smooth ball.
Grease a cast iron griddle w/ the oil. Place a dough ball, smoother side down, in the pan to coat w/ oil and flip over so that the smooth side is facing up. With 3 fingers, press the dough down a bit making a slight indention on the top of each.
Bake until golden brown, 16-18 min. Remove from oven and brush w/ the melted butter.
