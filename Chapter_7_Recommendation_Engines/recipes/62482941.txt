Title:
Chicken and Dressing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stalks celery, chopped
3/4 cup onion, chopped
3 Tablespoons butter
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 1 ounce envelope dry chicken or turkey gravy mix
1 10 ounce can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1 4 ounce can mushroom pieces, with liquid
4 1/2 cups hot water, not boiling
2 cups chopped cooked chicken or turkey
4 cups seasoned bread cubes

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 pan.  In a skillet, cook celery and onion until they start to soften.  Set aside.
In a large bowl combine milk, egg, gravy mix, soup and poultry seasoning.   Stir until smooth. Add mushrooms, butter/vegetable mixture and chicken then stir until blended.  Add bread cubes and mix until the bread starts to absorb the liquid.  Pour into the prepared pan.  Bake at 350 degrees until browned, about 45-50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lilcat
Description: 
This is an easy to make comfort food and a good way to use leftover chicken or turkey.
