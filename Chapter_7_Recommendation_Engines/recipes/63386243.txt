Title:
Olf Fashioned Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c all purpose flour
1 c butter, softened
3/4 c confectioners sugar
1 1/2 t vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350'.  In a medium sized bowl, mix all the ingredients together with your fingers until the dough is smooth and holds together.  Divide the dough in half.  Press each ahlf into an un greased 8 inch round tin (be sure to spread the dough evenly and smooth out the top).  Using the tines of a fork, make a decorative border around the edges.  Then, using a sharp knife cut the shortbread into 8 wedges.  Bake for 15 - 20 minutes, or until the edges begin to lightly brown.  Remove the shortbread from the oven adn immediately recut the 8 wedges with the sharp knife.  Cool in the pan for 30 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool thoroughly.
