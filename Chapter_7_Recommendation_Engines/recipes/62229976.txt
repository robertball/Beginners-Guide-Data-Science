Title:
Cajun Mash Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. potatoes, peeled and quartered
1/2 cup unsalted margarine
1  -8 oz packages cream cheese, softened
1  1 1/2 cups shredded Regular or Sharp Cheddar cheese (varies on how cheesy you want it)
1/2 bunch scallions tops, chopped
1/2  ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp Spicy Seasoning to your taste or can use salt and pepper. I use Slap Ya Mamma Cajun seasoning
¼ tsp Garlic Salt
1 tablespoon any type of Hot Sauce

Directions:
Cook potatoes in boiling water to cover 15 minutes or until tender; drain and mash.
Add butter, milk, hot sauce and cream cheese.
Beat at low speed with an electric mixer until smooth or desired texture.
Mix in-1/2 cup of the Cheddar/Sharp Cheddar cheese
½ cup of the Parmesan cheese
Green onions
Spoon into a 9 x 13 baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Sharp Cheddar cheese; and bake an additional 5 minutes until cheese melts.
