Title:
Oatmeal Pecan Pie Michael's Favorite (aka Picky eater boy) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/305517.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
3/4 cup corn syrup
3/4 cups old fashioned oats
Pie Crust
2 cups all purpose flour
3/4 tsp salt
2/3 cup butter shortening
2 tsp apple cider vinegar
1 egg
3 1/2 tbsp ice water
1/2 to 1 cup whole pecans

Directions:
Filling
In a mixer cream sugar, eggs and melted butter, add rest of ingredients except pecans, pour into crust, place pecan pieces on pie. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
Pie Crust
In Mixer add flour, salt, cut in shortening. In another bowl mix everything else, add to mixer mix together, and roll out crust.

Submitter: 
Kathryn Weidenheimer
Description: 
Chewy Pie that taste like Pecan. My son is one of the most pickiest eater's on the planet, but this pie he begs me to bake for him. The pie crust I use for all my pies.
