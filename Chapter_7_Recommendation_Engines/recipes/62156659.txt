Title:
Bean and Beef Stew (Slow Cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb dried white beans
1lb stewing beef
2 turnips, peeled and chopped
2 carrots, peeled and chopped
1 large potato diced
2 stalks of celery, chopped
2 medium onions chopped
1 can diced italian style tomatoes
2 tsp. basil
1/8 tsp.  crushed red pepper
1/4 tsp. cilantro leaves
2 tbs. salt
2 beef bouillion cubes
2 tbs. olive oil.

Directions:
Soak the beans in 8 cups of water overnight.
Rinse beans.  Take 2-3 cups of beans and set aside for another meal.
Put rinsed beans in crockpot.
Add all other ingredients.
Add water to cover ingredients.
Cook on low 8-10 hours...

Description: 
Hearty Stew that is chock full of fiber.  Delicious.  Great for those who are gone at work all day!
