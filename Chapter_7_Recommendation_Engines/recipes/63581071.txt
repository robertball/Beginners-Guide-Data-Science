Title:
Hamburger Yakisoba Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995978.jpg
Ingredients:
6 lbs Of Dry Spaghetti Noodles (Cook According To Package Directions)
30 lbs Beef, Ground,Bulk,Raw,90% Lean
5-1/4 lbs Peppers, Green, Red, Yellow, Julienne
4-1/4 lbs Onions, Fresh, Chopped
2 cup Soy Sauce
1/4 cup 1/3 tbsp Salt
2-2/3 tbsp Garlic Powder
1/4 cup 1/3 tbsp Ginger, Ground
1 tbsp Pepper, Black, Ground
2 qts Water
3-1/2 Cups Of Onions, Green, Fresh, Sliced

Directions:
Cook spaghetti in Boiling salted water until for 8 mins.
Cook beef in steam-jacketed kettle or stock pot until beef loses its pink color, stirring to break apart. Drain and skim off excess fat.
Combine beef with onions, peppers, soy sauce, salt, garlic powder, ginger, and pepper. Cook until onions are tender, about 10 minutes.
CCP: Internal temperature must reach 155 F. or higher for 15 seconds.
Add water, green onions, and spaghetti. Mix thoroughly. Heat to serving temperature.
CCP: Internal temperature must reach 155 F. or higher for 15 seconds. Hold for service at 140 F. or higher.
