Title:
Peach Citrus Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/361945.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. crushed ice
1 c. orange juice
4 oz strawberry yogurt
2 Tbsp dry milk powder
2 peach slices
1 Tbsp powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
clarinetlover
Description: 
This can be made with any variety of juice, yogurt, and fruit.
