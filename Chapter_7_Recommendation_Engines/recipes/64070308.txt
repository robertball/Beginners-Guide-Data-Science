Title:
Blueberry Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz. can crushed pineapple
4-6 cups fresh blueberries
1 box moist butter cake mix
1 stick margarine
1/4 c. sugar

Directions:
In a 10 x 14 pan, layer pineapple (with juice), then blueberries, sprinkle sugar over berries.
Sprinkle dry cake mix over blueberries
Melt butter & pour over cake mix
Bake in a pre-heated oven at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
