Title:
Chick Pea Korma Version2 (Presidents Choice Recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511759.jpg
Ingredients:
1tbsp olive oil
1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded and cut in 1/2-inch (1 cm) cubes
1 bottle (400 mL) PC Indian Korma Cooking Sauce
1/2 cup (125 mL) water
1 can (19 oz/540 mL) no name chick peas, rinsed and drained
2 green onions, sliced
2 cups (500 mL) baby spinach
1/4 of a block of firm tofu cut into 1cm chunks

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place squash in large ziplock bag with 1tbsp olive oil and toss to spread oil evenly on cubes.
2.Spread squash cubes on prepared baking sheet. Bake in centre of oven for 20 minutes, turning halfway, or until tender when pierced with tip of sharp knife.
3.In large saucepan, combine korma sauce, water, chick peas, green onions and squash. Cook over medium heat, stirring, for 5 minutes or until mixture starts to bubble. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in spinach until wilted.
4.Serve with cooked PC Basmati Rice and garnish with additional sliced green onion, if desired.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
Korma is a creamy, nut-based mild curry sauce. You could spend hours making it from scratch, or just open a bottle of PC’s korma sauce! Squash and chick peas make a hearty dish, perfect for vegetarians. (try with tofu or paneer)Modified from: Chick Pea Korma (Presidents Choice Recipe)
