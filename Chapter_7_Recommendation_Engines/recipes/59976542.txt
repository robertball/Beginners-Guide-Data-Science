Title:
Toaster Oven Grilled Salmon I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271368.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds salmon fillets
lemon pepper to taste
garlic powder to taste
salt to taste
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
In a small bowl, stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag with the soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Preheat toaster oven to 375 degrees
Place salmon in baking pan that will fit in your toaster oven, and discard marinade. Cook salmon for 12 - 18 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

Submitter: 
Grillin' Dude
Description: 
No grill?  Too cold or rainy to grill outside?  No worries!  This lovely grilled salmon recipe translates perfectly to a toaster oven.  A simple soy sauce and brown sugar marinade, with hints of lemon and garlic, are the perfect salty-sweet complement to rich salmon fillets.Modified from: Grilled Salmon I
