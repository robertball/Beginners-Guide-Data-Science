Title:
Party Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9-12 potatoes
salt and pepper
8oz cream cheese
1c sour cream
1t garlic salt
1 stick butter

Directions:
Boil, drain, and mash potatoes.
Salt and pepper lightly.
Mix in remaining ingredients and mix well.
Bake at 350 for 35-45 minutes.
