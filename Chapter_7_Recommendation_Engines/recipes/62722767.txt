Title:
Ultimate Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 mushrooms
3 T butter
2 T finely chopped onions
2 T finely chopped red peppers
14 Ritz crackers, finely crushed (about 1/2 C crumbs)
2 T grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 t. Italian seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Remove stems from mushrooms. Finely chop enough of the stems to measure 1/4 C; set aside. Cover and refrig. remaining stems for other use.
Melt butter in lg. skillet on med. heat. Add 1/4 C chopped mushroom stems, the onions and peppers; cook and stir until vegetables are tender. Stir in cracker crumbs, cheese and Italian seasoning. Spoon crumb mixture evenly into mushroom caps. Place on baking sheet.
Bake 15 min or until heated through
