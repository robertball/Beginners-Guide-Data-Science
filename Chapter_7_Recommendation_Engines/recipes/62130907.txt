Title:
Soba Noodle Salad With Chicken and Seasame Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp soy sauce
2 tsp fresh grated ginger
2 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 boneless/skinless chicken breast (thinly sliced)
2 tsp vegetable oil
1 tsp garlic (chopped)
salt & pepper (to taste)
1 bundle (4 oz) Japanese Buckwheat Noodles (Yamaimo Soba)
1/2 red bell pepper (coarsely chopped)
2 tbsp cup green onions (chopped)
1 stalk celery (sliced) - optional
1/4 cup cilantro (coarsely chopped)
1 carrot (sliced)
3 tsp sesame seeds

Directions:
To make the sauce, whisk together first 6 ingredients
Boil noodles for 4-5 min (according to pkg directions) then rinse them in cold water
Pan-fry chicken with vegetable oil, garlic, salt and pepper until thoroughly cooked
Combine noodles, sauce, chicken and remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl and toss to coat
Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
This tasty salad with Japanese buckwheat noodles is both quick & healthy--great for lunch or dinner!  Most larger supermarkets have buckwheat soba noodles in the Asian food section.
