Title:
Raspberry Chipotle Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package boneless pork chops
1 teaspoon garlic salt
3/4 cup Archer Farmsâ„¢ raspberry chipotle sauce
1 cup frozen raspberries, thawed

Directions:
Heat grill.  Sprinkle pork chops with garlic salt.  Mix 2 tablespoons chipotle sauce and raspberries; set aside.
Place pork chops on grill over medium heat, brushing tops of chops with some of remaining sauce.  Cover and cook 4 minutes.  Turn chops and brush with remaining sauce.  Cook an additional 4 to 5 minutes or until pork is no longer pink in center.
Serve pork chops with reserved raspberries.

Description: 
Serve up dinner quickly with this saucy approach to classic pork chops. It is berry, berry delicious!
