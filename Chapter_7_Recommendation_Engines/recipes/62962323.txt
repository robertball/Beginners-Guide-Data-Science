Title:
Five Cheese Macaroni And Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/731181.jpg
Ingredients:
1 2-Pound package elbow macaroni
1/2 Cup(1 stick) unsalted butter
8 Medium Eggs
1 Cup Milk
1 14-Ounce can Sweetened milk
2 Cups shredded mozzarella
1 Cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 Cup shredded mild cheddar cheese
3/4 Cup shredded Parmesan cheese
3/4 Cup crumbled feta cheese
1 tablespoon seasoned salt
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325 F. Bring a large pot of water to boil; and macaroni. Cook according to package directions until tender; drain. Place cooked macaroni in a large bowl. Add the butter, stirring until melted; set aside.
2. In a separate bowl place eggs , milk and sweetened condensed milk; beat with a whisk until smooth. Pour mixture over macaroni and stir gently to a coat.
3.In another bowl combine all the cheeses. Set aside about one-fourth of the cheese mixture. Add remaining cheese mixture, the seasoned salt, salt and pepper to macaroni mixture. fold together with a rubber scraper. If mixture is to stiff, add more milk.
4. Sprinkle a little of the reserve cheese mixture over the bottom of a 3-quart casserole dish. Spoon macaroni and cheese mixture into dish; smooth over top with rubber scraper. Sprinkle remaining cheese mixture on top. Bake, uncovered about 30 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
