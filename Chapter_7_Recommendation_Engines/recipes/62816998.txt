Title:
Sassy Skillet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664090.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Idaho potatoes, peeled and sliced thin
8 oz Heavy Cream
½ clove Garlic, minced
½ Shallot, minced
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F. In 6” iron skillet, put down an overlapped layer of potato slices, sprinkle some garlic and shallots over, salt and pepper, add a little heavy cream, repeat until you have three layers, add rest of heavy cream. Bake in oven about 45 minutes or until potatoes are tender. You could also do these on the grill.
