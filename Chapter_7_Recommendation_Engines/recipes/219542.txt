Title:
Quick and Flavorful Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987391.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh baby-cut carrots or green beans
2 packets Swanson® Flavor Boost™ Concentrated Vegetable Broth

Directions:
Place the broccoli into a 3-quart saucepan and add water to cover. Cook over medium-high heat until the broccoli is tender-crisp. Drain the broccoli well in a colander. Return the broccoli to the saucepan.
Add the concentrated broth to the saucepan and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Here's a quick simple way to enhance the flavor of fresh veggies. Even the kids will ask for more!
