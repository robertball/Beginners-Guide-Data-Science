Title:
Fireman's Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. poultry seasoning
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1 cup apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together.  Use wisk to mix.  Brush marinade on chicken or vegetables frequently while grilling.

Submitter: 
jeanneferg
Description: 
Wonderful for chicken on the grill.  You can also use for peppers and mushrooms on the grill.
