Title:
Jennifer's Cinnamon Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/545737.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tbsp sugar
4 tsp baking powder
1/4 cup butter
1 cup milk
1/3 cup butter
2 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Sift together dry ingredients.  Cut butter in evenly.  Pour in milk and mix.  Add more flour if dough is sticky.
Turn out onto floured surface and knead 7 or 8 times.  Roll into a rectangle 1/2" thick.
Mix butter, cinnamon, nutmeg and brown sugar for filling.  Spread over dough and roll up width-wise.
Cut into 1" pieces and place in greased baking dish.  Brush the top with butter.
Bake at 400 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes.
