Title:
Pumpkin Tiramisu Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3811738.jpg
Ingredients:
1 angel food cake, cut or torn into bite-size pieces
2 cups milk
1 cup pure pumpkin puree
1/2 cup corn syrup
2 tbsp. corn starch
1 tsp. ginger
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 egg yolks
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups whipping cream
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup chopped candied ginger (optional)

Directions:
Whisk together milk, pumpkin, corn syrup, corn starch, ginger and cinnamon in a large saucepan.
Cook over med. heat; stirring frequently, until mixture is boiling and thickened.
Whisk the egg yolks with vanilla; whisk into pumpkin mixture.
Cook, whisking constantly, for 2 min. or until very thick.
Strain through a fine sieve.
Place a piece of wax paper directly on surface; chill.
Whip 1/2 cup whipping cream until thick peaks form; fold into cooled pumpkin custard. Set aside.
Pulse the sugar with ginger in a blender/processor until very finely chopped.
Whip remaining whipping cream with ginger mixture until thick peaks form. Set aside.
Layer half the cake pieces, half the pumpkin custard and half the ginger whipped cream into an 8 cup glass bowl.
Repeat layers.
Refrigerate for up to 1 day.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This would be a nice light dessert after turkey dinner.
