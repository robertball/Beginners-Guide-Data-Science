Title:
Banana-Peanut Butter Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978117.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1 small ripe banana
1/4 cup Jif® Natural Creamy Peanut Butter Spread
2 tablespoons honey
6 ice cubes
Whipped cream (optional)
Chocolate sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
Combine milk, banana, peanut butter, honey and ice cubes in blender container. Cover and process until smooth.
Pour into serving glasses. Add whipped cream and sprinkles, if desired. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
For a quick breakfast or energizing snack, a peanut butter and banana smoothie is the perfect solution!
