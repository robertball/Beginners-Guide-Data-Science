Title:
DVO/Cook'n Cobbler Gobbler-Fresh Corn, Bacon, and Potato Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 ears corn
8 cups water
1 stick unsalted butter
2 medium diced red onion
6 stalks diced celery
3 large red bell peppers
10 medium diced red potatoes
1 TB fennel seeds
1 TB celery seeds
1/2 TB red pepper flakes
3 cups heavy cream
1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
6 slices applewood smoked bacon

Directions:
in dutch oven, cook bacon until crispy, drain fat and set aside.
melt butter in the dutch oven over medium-high heat.
add onion, celery, pepper, potatoes, fennel, celery seeds, pepper flakes, and corn kernels.
cook until onions are tender and vegetables are fragrant. add additional liquid, if desired
add cream (or milk)
bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until potatoes are tender.
just before serving, stir in dill.
garnish with crumbled bacon

Submitter: 
KirbyZmom
Description: 
i would replace the heavy cream with 2% milk, and the red onion with yellow onion. Also, I would used canned unsalted corn drained of all liquid instead of corn cobs.
