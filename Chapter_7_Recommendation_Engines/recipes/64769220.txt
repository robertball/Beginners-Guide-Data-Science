Title:
Mom's Chicken Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked rice
1/2 lb boneless skinless chicken
1 cup frozen peas and carrots
1 onion chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2 eggs
3 tablespoons sesame oil
1/4 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Heat sesame oil in a large saucepan.
Add onion, garlic, and frozen peas and carrots. Stir until tender
Add chicken and soy sauce.
Crack eggs into mix. Scramble.
Add cooked rice.
Mix all ingredients and remove from heat.
