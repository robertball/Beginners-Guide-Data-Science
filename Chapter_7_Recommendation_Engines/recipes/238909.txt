Title:
Prosciutto e Melone on a Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1328420.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cantaloupe balls
8 slices prosciutto
8 green grapes
8 red grapes
8 bamboo toothpicks

Directions:
Thread a cantaloupe ball, prosciutto slice, green grape, and red grape onto each toothpick, respectively.

Submitter: 
Lindsey
Description: 
Prosciutto and cantaloupe are thread on a bamboo toothpick with red and green grapes creating a colorful and delicious appetizer!
