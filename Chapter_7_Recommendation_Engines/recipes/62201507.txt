Title:
Turkey Cranberry Toasted Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crustini Baguette or Your Favorite Bread
Turkey slices, light or dark meat
Left Over Stuffing
Havarti Cheese Slices
Cranberry Mayo
Butter
Cranberry Mayo:
1/2 cup cranberry sauce
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons sour cream
2 tablespoons spicy brown mustard
1 tablespoon hot sauce
2 tablespoons fresh chives, thinly sliced

Directions:
Butter OUTSIDE of bread slices
Layer in the following order
Bread, turkey slice, spoonful of stuffing, cheese slice, mayo, bread slice butter side out
Toast in Panini Maker or in non-stick frying pan until golden brown and cheese has melted

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
Great left over turkey sandwich with a touch of deli flair...Panini Style.
