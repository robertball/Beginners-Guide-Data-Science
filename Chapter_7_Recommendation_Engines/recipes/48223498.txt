Title:
CHICKEN CURRY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4  	skinless, boneless chicken breasts (approx. 8 lbs.) cut crosswise in 1/2 inch slices.
Oil
3 cups 		chopped onion
1/4  cup 	minced fresh ginger
3 cloves 	garlic, minced
3 Tbsp. 	curry powder (more if you want it hotter)
1 tsp. 		Cumin
1/4  tsp. 	Cinnamon
2 Tbsp. 	flour
1 cup 		plain yogurt
3 Tbsp. 	tomato paste
3 cups 		chicken broth
1 cup 		applesauce
1/2  cup 	sour cream
1/2  cup 	coconut milk
Condiments
Mango chutney, diced cucumber, diced bananas, currants, shredded coconut and chopped peanuts.

Directions:
Brown chicken in oil.  Remove and set aside.  Add onion and sauté until golden.  Add ginger and garlic and sauté another minute.  Add curry, cumin and cinnamon and mix well.  Cook for 1 minute.  Add flour, yogurt and tomato paste, whisking until sauce is smooth.  Pour in broth and applesauce and simmer uncovered, until mixture thickens (about 30 minutes).  Add browned chicken (and any juice), sour cream and coconut milk.  Simmer another 15 minutes.
