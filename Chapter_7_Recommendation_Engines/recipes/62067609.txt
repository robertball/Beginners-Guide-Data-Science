Title:
Something Different Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cloves of garlic
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 tsp. fresh rosemary
2 tsp. fresh dill weed
1/2 cup Newman's Own Italian Salad Dressing
1 tbsp. soy sauce

Directions:
Put all ingrediants into a blender or food processer. Blend until smoothe. Put meat,chicken,or seafood into pan or shallow bowl and poor marinade over the top. If using steak,poke holes with a fork on both sides. Let sit in refrigerator for at least one hour,turning each half an hour. The longer it marinades the more flavor it absorbs. It's VERY important to use FRESH herbs in this recipe. After done marinading, grill steak,chick,pork, or sautee seafood until desired doneness. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
With a little experimentation in my kitchen I came up with this different marinade for steak,chicken,pork,or even shrimp. Very flavorful!
