Title:
Uncle Warren's Johnny Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/575125.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cornmeal
2 cups white flour
1 1/2 cups oats (quick or regular)
1 1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
4 eggs
1/4 cup oil
3/4 cup applesauce
2 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Mix cornmeal, flour, oats, salt, baking soda, baking powder and sugar.
Beat in eggs, oil, applesauce and buttermilk.
Pour into greased 9x13 pan and back for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
MikesGirl
Description: 
I got this wholesome cornbread recipe from my uncle - it is a thick, moist cornbread.  We grew up calling this johnny cake.  It is delicious served with applesauce or syrup; my husband loves this paired with hot chili on a cold day!
