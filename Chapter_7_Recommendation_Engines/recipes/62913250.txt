Title:
Old Fashioned Sunday Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box beefy onion soup mix (two envelopes)
1 31/2# beef rump roast
2# small red potatoes, halved
3 large carrots, peeled & sliced
2 celery ribs, sliced
7 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp garlic pepper
2 tsp thyme
1 bay leaf
11/2 cup zinfandel
1 14oz can beef broth
1/4 cup flour

Directions:
In a 5qt crock pot place the potatoes, carrots, celery and garlic
Sprinkle evenly with the garlic pepper and thyme
Tuck in the bay leaf
Pour in liquid ingredients
Place roast on top and sprinkle with soup mix
Cover and cook on low heat setting 9-10 hours, or until meat is tender
