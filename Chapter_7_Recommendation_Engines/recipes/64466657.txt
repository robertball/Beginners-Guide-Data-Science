Title:
Dinomite  Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2460999.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Sugar
1 cup Flour
3/4 cup Instant Hot Cocoa Mix
3/4 cup melted Butter
3 eggs (dinosaur or dragon if in season)
1/2 cup Chocolate Chips
Pinch of Unicorn Horn Powder
1/4 teaspoon Pixie tears (optional)
1 can Chocolate Frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Mix all ingredients except frosting.
Taste frosting to be certain you bought the right brand.
Pour batter into mini muffin pans (fill each half way)
Bake for 15 to 18 minutes
When cooled frost BOTTOM and eat immediately.
