Title:
Steamed broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large stalk or head of broccoli
Boiling hot water

Directions:
Place steamer basket into large pot. Fill with water up to but not touching steamer basket. Place pot of water on high heat; Bring to boil. While waiting for water to heat, prepare fresh broccoli: remove hard outer cover on stem. Cut into serving size pieces. Place broccoli into steamer basket using tongs. Steam for 3 to 8 minutes - test for your preference of doneness by piercing stem with fork.
