Title:
Cheesy Potato and Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
3 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1/4-1/2 cup flour
2 cups milk
1 cup shredded Mexican-style cheese
1/2 cup processed cheese

Directions:
In large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.  Add celery and onion; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
Add chicken broth; bring to a boil.  Add potatoes; cook over low heat for 20 to 25 minutes or until potatoes are soft, stirring occasionally.
Stir in corn and chiles; return to boiling.  Dissolve flour in milk; stir into boiling mixture.  Add cheese; cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted.
