Title:
Stevia Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1/2 c sugar
6 pkg NuStevia
2 1/2 T cornstarch
1/8 t salt
1/2 c cocoa
2 1/2 c milk
1 t vanilla

Directions:
Mix egg and sweeteners together in a large pan.
Mix in rest of ingredients in order.  Stir and cook over med-hi until thick. Pour into 9" baked pie shell.
