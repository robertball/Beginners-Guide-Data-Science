Title:
Fryed "Taters" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. of potatoes
Shredded Cheddar Cheese to taste
Salt
Pepper
Olive Oil (about 2 tablespoons)

Directions:
Rinse the Potatoes with warm water, and cut out any bad spots. Dice potatoes unevenly; It doesn't have to be perfect! Next, heat a large cast iron skillet over medium heat; once hot, add the oil, and swirl to coat bottom. Next add potatoes. Fry the potatoes until golden brown. Remove from heat, and dump potatoes into a large bowl. Season to taste. Add cheese if desired. Best if served hot.

Submitter: 
Jane Austen
Description: 
This is an amazing tradition in my family. Works like a charm every time!
This will soon become a favorite in your household! Try it with eggs, bacon/sausage, and toast! Enjoy!
