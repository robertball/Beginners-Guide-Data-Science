Title:
Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large tomatoes, chopped
2 tbs capers
2 tbs minced garlic
2 tbs minced shallot
2 tbs minced scallion
1 tbs Italian seasoning
1/4 c lemon juice
1/4 c olive oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate at least 1 hour.  Overnight allows the flavors to really meld.  Spread on toasted bread rounds or garlic bread.

Submitter: 
KitchenWitch
Description: 
Bruschetta is a great addition to garlic bread, or even leftover toasted bread.  This is a nice topping for salads, wraps, or fajitas.
