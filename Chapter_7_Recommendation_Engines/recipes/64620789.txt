Title:
Sweet & Salty Coconut Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons raisins
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
3 tablespoons soy sauce
3 tablespoons tomato paste
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon cayenne or paprika
2.5 cups coconut ribbons
3/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
1 tablespoon hemp seeds (optional)
1 table spoon flax seeds (optional)

Directions:
Soak raisins until soft.
In a blender, blend raisins, sugar, apple cider vinegar, soy sauce, tomato paste, garlic powder, cumin and cayenne/paprika. Blend until raisins are all broken up.
In a bowl, mix coconut and all the seeds together.
Pour liquid with coconut mixture and mix well.
Divide mixture onto 2 dehydrator sheets. Press mixture together so they're sticking together.
Dehydrator at 150-160 F for 5-6 hours or until dry and crispy.
