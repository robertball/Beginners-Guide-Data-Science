Title:
Nan's Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 kg lean hamburger
1 white onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, smashed
28 oz can tomatoes
12 oz tomato paste
salt& pepper to taste
Optional Chopped red peppers, spinach, cottage cheese
2 tsp basil
2 tsp oregano
2 bay leaves
454 gm mushrooms, chopped
1 package lasagne noodles
454 gm mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Brown meat and drain fat
Add onion, garlic, tomatoes, tomato paste, spices and bay leaves, cover and simmer for 1-1.5 hours (may place in a slow cooker on low for 6-8 hours)
Cook noodles as per package
layer dish starting with the noodles, meat sauce, noodles, cheese, meat, noodles,cheese, meat noodles, cheese and parmesan cheese (optional) on top
bake in oven at 350 for about 45 minutes
Sauce can be made ahead of time and frozen
