Title:
Jello Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c shortening
1/2 c sugar
One 3-oz pkg fruit jello
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 c flour
1 tsp salt
1 t baking powder

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F.  Mix thoroughly shortening, sugar, gelatin, eggs, and vanilla.  Blend in dry ingredients.  Roll dough into 3/4" balls.  Place 3 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet (I use parchment paper).  Flatten each with bottom of glass dipped in sugar.  Bake 6-8 minutes.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
Great when you need an inexpensive, quick batch of cookies.
