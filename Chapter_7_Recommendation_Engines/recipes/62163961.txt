Title:
Zucchini Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium zucchini
1 large sweet onion
1 red bell pepper
1 ear of sweet corn
1 jalapeno pepper
1 T olive oil
1 t. minced garlic
1 1/2 t. seasoned salt

Directions:
Cut zucchini, onion and red bell pepper into large dice.
Cut kernels from sweet corn cob
Slice jalapeno lengthwise
Remove white seeds of jalapeno for milder flavor or leave for more heat
Dice jalapeno
Heat oil in non-stick skillet over medium high heat
Add garlic and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute
Add vegetables to saute pan
Season with seasoned salt
Saute until vegetables start to carmelize, about 10 minutes

Submitter: 
DRFISH
Description: 
This wonderful recipe combines many summer garden items into a delicious side dish or vegetarian entree.  It's got both heat and sweet for a very flavorful addition to your dinner plate.  Great way to use up an abundance of garden veggies!
