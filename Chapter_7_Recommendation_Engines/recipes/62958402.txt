Title:
HG Upside down Pineapple Applesauce cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Glaze:
1 Tbl lite butter spread
1/4 cup brown sugar (not packed)
Cake:
half box (1 1/2 cups) moist style yellow cake mix
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup club soda
1/4 cup no sugar applesauce
7 pineapple rings packed in juice, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Spray 9" round cake pan with Pam (butter flavor)
Glaze: put butter in small microwave safe bowl & microwave for 15 seconds, until just melted. Add brown sugar & mix well. Set aside
Combine cake mix with baking powder in large bowl. Mix well. Add club soda & applesauce; stir until smooth. Set aside
Spread glaze evenly along bottom of cake pan. Blot pineapple rings with paper towels to remove excess moisture. Lay rings in even layer in cake pan.
Evenly pour cake batter into cake pan over the fruit layer. Bake in oven until toohtpick comes out clean, 30-35 min
Cool for 15-20min. Carefully flip pan onto plate. If doesnt pop out, put back in oven for few min
