Title:
Taquilla Lime Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422419.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Taquilla
1/3 cup lime juice
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 TBSP Salt
1/2 TBSP Black Pepper
1/2 tsp Chili powder
1/2 white onion minced
1 1/2 lbs. Chicken breast cut into strips
2 Green or other variety of sweet peppers cut into strips
Flour Tortillas
Grated cheese
Salsa
Sour Cream

Directions:
In a large ziplock bag add Taquilla, lime juice, vegetable oil, onion, salt, pepper, and chili powder. Add chicken and allow to marinade for 20 minutes to an hour. Cook pour chicken with all marinade into a hot pan and cook until chicken is no longer pink and mixture begins to thicken. Add peppers and continue to cook until the peppers soften. Serve on tortillas with cheese, salsa, and sour cream.

Fajitas that grown ups will enjoy as well as picky children.
