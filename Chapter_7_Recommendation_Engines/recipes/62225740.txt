Title:
Garlic Parm Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup olive oil
10 cloves crushed garlic or 5 tsp. bottled minced garlic
1 1/2 cups seasoned dry bread crumbs
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp. Italian seasoning
2 Tbsp. "saltless" garlic herb seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In a small saucepan warm olive oil with garlic.  While garlic oil cools for 5 minutes, combine bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, italian seasoning and salt and pepper in a separate dish. Dip chicken pieces in garlic olive oil, then coat completely with bread crumb mixture.  Place coated chicken in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
