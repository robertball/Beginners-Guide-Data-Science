Title:
Risotto with pigeon peas and pulled pork (Risotto con gandules y pernil) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup arborio rice
6 cups chicken stock
1 can of pigeon peas (gandules), washed and drained
1/2 onion, finely chopped
2 lbs. front shoulder pork roast
2 cups chicken stock
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper (or adobo seasoning) to taste
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Fresh cilantro

Directions:
Season the pork chunks with salt/pepper or adobo
Heat oil in a heavy pot and brown the pork. Once browned, add the 2 cups of chicken stock and cook at low heat until very tender. After soft, shred with a fork and set aside. Empty the pot.
For the risotto: In pot, put a little bit of oil and cook the onions and peas for 2 min. Then add the arborio rice and cook for another 2 min.
Set burner to medium and begin to add the 6 cups of chicken stock little by little, stirring constantly each time with a wooden spoon until the stock is absorbed.
Once all the stock has been added and absorbed and the rice is cooked, add the shredded pork and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Serve at once and garnish with chopped cilantro.
