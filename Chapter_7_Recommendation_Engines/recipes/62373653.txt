Title:
Best Strawberry Lemonade Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cups white sugar
7 cups water
1 cup lemon juice
2 cups of ice
12 strawberries
1/4 cup sugar for strawberries

Directions:
Wash and quarter the strawberries. Add 1/4 cup of sugar and stir in the sugar and set aside to allow the strawberries time to release juices.
In a small saucepan, bring to boil 1 cup water,   stir in sugar to dissolve. Remove from stove and let cool a bit.
Remove seeds from lemon juice, but leave pulp. In pitcher, stir together simple syrup, ice, lemon juice and remaining 6 cups water. Give it a good stir then add in the sweetened strawberries, another good stir and serve chilled.
