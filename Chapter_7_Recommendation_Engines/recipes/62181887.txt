Title:
LEFTOVER SOUP FOR 2 OR MORE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. EVOO
1/4C. CHOPPED ONION
2 CLOVES GARLIC MINCED
3 CANS LOW SODIUM CHICKEN
1/2 CAN DRAINED WHITE BEANS
1/2 CAN FIRE ROASTED DICED TOMATOES
2 TBS CHOPPED MIXED BELL PEPPERS(OPT)
1/2 C. SMALL PASTA SUCH AS DITALINI
3 LEAVES OF KALE--TORN FROM RIBS
SALT TO TASTE

Directions:
SAUTE ONION AND GARLIC IN OIL UNTIL SOFT BUT NOT BROWNED. ADD BROTH, BEANS, TOMATOES, BELL PEPPERS AND KALE AND BRING TO A BOIL. TURN HEAT OFF AND LET SIT UNTIL 30 MINUTES BEFORE SERVING. BRING BACK TO BOIL ADD KALE TURN DOWN HEAT AND COOK 25 MINUTES OR UNTIL KALE IS TENDER.

Submitter: 
Mary Stephenson
Description: 
COOKING FOR 2 LEAVES ME WITH LEFTOVER CANNED/FRESH VEGETABLES PLUS DINNER IS WHENEVER MY HUSBAND GETS HOME FROM WORK. I CAN MAKE THIS IN THE AFTERNOON, LET IT SIMMER FOR A WHILE, TURN STOVE OFF AND TOSS IN THE KALE WHEN HE WALKS IN THE DOOR. USE YOUR CHOICE OF STOCK, VEGETABLES AND SEASONINGS.
