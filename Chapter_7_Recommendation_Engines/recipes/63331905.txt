Title:
Grandma's Taco Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can (10 3/4 oz) tomato soup, or equivalent amount of leftover homemade tomato soup
1 cup salsa
1/2 cup milk
6 flour tortillas or 8 corn tortillas (6" to 8"), cut into 1" pieces
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef until browned, stirring to separate meat.  Pour off fat.
Add soup, salsa, milk, cut-up tortillas, and half the cheese.
Spoon into 2-quart shallow baking dish.  Cover.
Bake at 400* F. for 30 minutes or until heated through.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This dish is easy to make ahead.  Just keep it in the baking pan until you're ready to pop it in the oven.
