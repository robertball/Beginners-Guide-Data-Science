Title:
Nana's Tilapia with Garlic and Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tilapia fillets
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons garlic oil
2 minced cloves, garlic
1 tablespoon cayenne, or to taste
1 cup Alfredo sauce
Old bay seasoning to taste
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet place butter and garlic oil. While the oil heats up season fillets with Old Bay, black pepper and cayenne. Now add garlic cloves to skillet. Once color has begin to change, add fillets cook about (3) minutes each side, then remove from skillet. Add Alfredo sauce, once sauce begins to bubble place filets into the sauce, simmer (15 to(20)minutes. Serve over pasta.
