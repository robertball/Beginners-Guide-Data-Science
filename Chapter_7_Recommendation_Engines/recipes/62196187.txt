Title:
Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 packages frozen broccoli
6 large boned and boiled chicken breasts
2 cans cream of chicken soupl
1 c mayonaise
1/2 t curry powder (I use about 2T)
1 t lemon juice
1/2 c shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c bread crumbs
1 T. butter

Directions:
Place the broccoli on the bottom of a 9x13 plan
Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces and layer over the broccoli.
Mix the soup, mayo, curry powder, and lemon juice togeter.
Pour the mixture over the chicken.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Mix the bread crumbs with the butter, and sprinkle over the cheese.
Bake at 375 for 40 min.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
This is a crowd pleaser.  It can be assembled and refrigerated the night before, making having dinner guests a snap. I had it at a friends house and just had to have the recipe.
