Title:
Super Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4557706.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups frozen mango cubes
1 cup baby kale
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
2 cups orange juice
2 packets Stevia In The Raw®
1 tablespoon chia seeds

Directions:
In a blender, combine mango, kale, almond milk, orange juice, Stevia In The Raw(R), and chia seeds. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
In The Raw
Description: 
Start your morning off right with this delicious, quick and easy smoothie packed mango, kale and chia seeds.  Sweetened with Stevia In The Raw®!
