Title:
Yorkshire Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup beef roast drippings
shortning, if needed
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
4 eggs
2 cups whole milk

Directions:
Line pan wit non-stick foil wrap.
Pre-heat oven to 425^F; put pan in oven while pre-heating
In bowl combine flour, salt, eggs, and milk.
Blend until smooth.
Pour juice into hot pan and then batter.
Bake 35-40 minutes or until golden brown.
Serve hot with brown gravy on top.

Submitter: 
Chef HamDog
Description: 
You can use this with any beef roast but be careful what you seaso the roast with.  If you don't have as much drippings as needed then use melted shortning for the rest.
