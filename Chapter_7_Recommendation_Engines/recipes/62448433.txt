Title:
Fig-E-Licious Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462959.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
2/3 cup milk
2 tbsps. vegetable oil
2 cups Bisquick
¼ cup sugar
¼ tsp ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1 cup fig preserves, mashed well

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Line muffin cups with paper baking cups.
Beat egg then add milk and vegetable oil, mixing well.  Add dry ingredients, mix well, then fold in fig preserves, blending well.
Divide batter evenly into muffin cups (using an ice cream scooper helps).
Bake 13 – 18 minutes until golden brown.  Cool slightly; remove from pan and place on wire rack.

Description: 
A super-moist delicate sweet muffin I created with an over abundance of fig preserves.
