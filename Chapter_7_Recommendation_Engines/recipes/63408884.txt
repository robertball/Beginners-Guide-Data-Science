Title:
Pumpkin Spice Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/918596.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Milk
1/3 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream
1/4 Cup Milk Chocolate Chips
1 Teaspoon Cocoa Powder
1/2 Tablespoon Pure Pumpkin
1/4 Teaspoon Pumpkin Pie Spice
1/4 Cup Whipped Cream
Sprinkle of Cinnamon & Nutmeg (Optional)

Directions:
Place a saucepan on the stove on medium heat.
Add milk, heavy whipping cream, chocolate chips, and cocoa powder and whisk until smooth. This may take a few minutes until chocolate chips melt.
Stir in pumpkin and pumpkin pie spice. Whisk until fully incorporated.
Remove from heat and pour into a mug.
Pipe whipped cream on top and add a sprinkle of cinnamon and nutmeg. Serve.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Rich and creamy pumpkin spice hot chocolate, a great treat for a fall day, quiet evening, or to share with friends and family!
