Title:
Kale Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bunch of kale washed and dried
3 Tbs olive oil
2 Tbs apple cider vinegar
2 Tbs  sugar
1  1/2 tsp garlic powder
1. 1/2 tsp salt
1/4. tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Mix all spices and oils together
Place kale in bowl and toss
Bake 350 for 20 min
