Title:
Orzo-Rice Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs butter
1/2 cup orzo
1 cup white rice
1 clove of garlic minced
3 cups water
2 chicken bouillon cubes
2 cups fresh spinach stems removed
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place butter in a 3 quart pot and heat over medium heat.
Add the orzo and rice and cook until orzo turns golden- stirring occasionally; add garlic and cook an additional 2 minutes.
Add water and bouillon, bring to a boil- reduce heat and cover.  Cook about 20 minutes.
While rice and orzo are cooking- gather spinach into flat bunches.  Roll slightly and cut into thin slivers.
Once rice mixture is finished cooking add spinach and mix well; stir in parmesan cheese.
