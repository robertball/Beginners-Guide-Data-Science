Title:
Meat Loaf 4/1 meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  Lbs lean ground beef
1  egg
1  4 oz. can tomato sauce
1/2 cup chopped Celery
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tbsp woostershire sauce
1 tsp liquid smoke
1 pkg [1.5 oz.] dry onion soup mix
1 cup bread crumbs [enough to make mix
form a loaf]
GRAVY
1 can [10 1/2 oz.] Ro-tel Tomatoes
[original recipe]
1 can [10 oz.] mushroom soup
1 can [10 oz.] Campbells golden mush-
room soup
1 pkg. [1.5 oz.] dry onion soup
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 cup chopped green onions
NO SALT [the soups have sufficient amt.]

Directions:
Mix all meat loaf ingredients.  In a 12
quart roaster, mold a loaf down the center. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for one hour or until the loaf begins to
brown.  While this is cooking, mix the
gravy ingredients in another bowl, adding enough water to get the consistancy you prefer your gravy. Have
1 pound of potatoes and 1 pound carrots
diced.  Remove the browned meat loaf
from oven, remove excess fat, place
carrots on one side, and potatoes on
the other, pour gravy over them, cover,
and cook in the oven for 2 hours 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
