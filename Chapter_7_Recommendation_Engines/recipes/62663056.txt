Title:
Pork Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 eggs
2.5 oz Peanut Oil
30 cups rice, cooked
3 cups ham/roast pork, diced
3 cups scallions, thinly sliced
6 cups frozen peas, defrosted
5 oz soy sauce

Directions:
heat oil till very hot, but not smoking, add eggs.  scramble till still slightly wet, add scallions, peas, rice, ham/pork stir frying with each addition.  when heated through, add soy sauce. toss and adjust seasonings.

Submitter: 
Woodbine United Methodist
Description: 
Light and festive, this is a crowd pleaser for kids and adults.
