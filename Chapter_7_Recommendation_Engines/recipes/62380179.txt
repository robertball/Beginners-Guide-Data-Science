Title:
Conference Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
3 oz. Cream cheese. (less than 1/2 8 oz. Pkg.)
1/4 C butter
2 C biscuit mix
1/2 C milk
1/2 C jam (works better than jelly)
Icing
1 C. powder sugar
1T milk
2 T butter
1/4 t. vanilla

Directions:
Cake: Preheat oven to 425°
Cut cream cheese and butter into biscuit mix 'til crumbly
Blend in milk
Turn onto floured surface and knead 8-10 strokes.
On wax paper, roll dough to 8x12 rectangle
Turn onto greased foil in baking sheet.
Remove wax paper. Spread jam down center of dough.
Make 2 1/2 in. cuts every 1 in. along the sides of the jam.
Fold strips over jam.
Bake 10-15 min. at 425°
Icing: Soften butter. Add vanilla.
Stir in milk.
Add powder sugar until desired consistancy.
Drizzle over cake while still hot.
