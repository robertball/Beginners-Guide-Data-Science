Title:
SEPT OCT NOV Dill-Vodka Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups dill-infused vodka
1/3 cup dry vermouth
ice
sliced kirby cucumbers
capers
springs of fresh dill

Directions:
To make a pitcher, mix 3 cups dill-infused vodka with 1/3 cup dry vermouth. Stir over ice and strain into chilled martini glasses. Garnish with sliced Kirby cucumbers, capers, and sprigs of fresh dill. Herbs Aromatic Dill Leaf Infused Vodka
