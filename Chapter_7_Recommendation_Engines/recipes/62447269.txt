Title:
Crock Pot Lentil, Rice, Chicken and Veggie Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462187.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup uncooked brown rice
4 cups water
1 cup brown lentils
1 large chicken breast
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon onion powder
3 cloves garlic, pressed
1 fresh tomato, chopped
1 stalk celery, sliced
1 med carrot, coined
1 med zuchini, quartered and sliced
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute garlic, onion powder and all veggies in oil. Add all spices. Transfer to crock pot.  Add tomato sauce and mix well.  Add rice, lentils and water.  Mix well.  Add chicken breast.  Cook on low for 6 hours.  Remove chicken breast and shred with forks.  Cook 2 hours more.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
