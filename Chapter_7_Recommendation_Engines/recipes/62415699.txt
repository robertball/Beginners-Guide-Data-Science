Title:
Fresh Fruit Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Fresh Fruit (peaches, strawberries, bananas, cantaloupe, pineapple or blueberries)
3/4 Cup Fat Free Half and Half
3/4 Cup Fat Free Milk
1/4 Cup
Pinch of Salt
1/8t Pure Vanilla

Directions:
Puree fruit in food processor until desired consistency.
In measuring cup measure milk, half and half, Splenda, salt and vanilla together until combined.  Add fruit puree.
Pour into pop molds and freeze at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
These creamy fresh tasting bars are loaded with fresh fruit and made sugar free using Splenda.
