Title:
BLT aka BST Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two slices of thick slab bacon per sandwich
Four thick sliced beefsteak tomatoes
One bag spinach leaves
Good multi-grained bread
Two Large avocados
2Tbsp limejuice
¼tsp coriander powder
¼ tsp cumin powder
¼ tsp kosher salt

Directions:
Directions for Avocado Spread:
Cut each avocado in half-length wise...  Remove the seed and scoop out the fruit of the avocados.  Cut the avocado into chunks and place into a blender or food processor, add salt, coriander, cumin and lime juice to blender or processor and blend until smooth.
Directions for Sandwich Construction:
Cook bacon either on stovetop or in the oven (375F), pat fat from bacon.
Apply avocado spread to toasted multi-grained bread slices, tomato, fresh spinach leaves and add two strips of thick slice bacon.  Cut the sandwich in halves or quarters.  Enjoy!
