Title:
Olive Garden Chicken Caprese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 plum tomatoes, (Roma) (2 pounds)
30 medium fresh basil leaves (no stems)
2 teaspoons black pepper
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts or chicken breast fillets
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
1/2 cup white wine
3 tablespoons garlic, minced, divided
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
3 1/2 teaspoons italian seasoning, divided
1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1 1/2 teaspoons salt, plus
1 tablespoon salt, divided
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 1/2 cups flour, plus
1 tablespoon flour, divided
1 lb dry capellini (angel hair)

Directions:
Core & cut tomatoes into 1-inch pieces. Cut basil leaves into 1-inch pieces. Combine tomatoes, basil, 2 tbls of EVOO, 2 tbls of minced garlic, 1½ tsp of Italian seasoning and 1½ tsp of salt in a large bowl; Mix. Cover, set aside & refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Coat a lg, non-stick skillet with vegetable oil & heat over med-high heat. Combine 1½ cups of flour, 1 tbls of salt, black pepper & 2 tsp of Italian seasoning in a shallow dish. Dredge chicken in the mixture, shaking off any excess. Place chicken in skillet & sauté for about 2 minutes on each side, or until just golden brown. If skillet is not large enough, sauté the chicken in batches. When finished, transfer chicken to a large baking dish & place in oven to cook for approx 10 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain & set aside until needed.
While pasta is cooking, heat 2 tbls of EVOO in a sauce pan over medium heat. Add 1 tbls of minced garlic and sauté for approx 1 minute. Do not brown. Slowly add 1 tbls of flour & stir to combine. Add white wine & bring to a boil. Boil for approx 1 minute. Remove tomato & basil mixture from frige. Drain tomatoes & basil from liquid & set aside until needed. Add ½ cup of the marinade liquid and sauté for approx 1 minute. Add heavy cream. Lower heat & bring to a simmer. Add grated Parmesan cheese.
Preheat broiler. Remove chicken from baking dish & set aside until needed. Transfer pasta to baking dish & partly coat with sauce. Pour desired amount of the remaining sauce on pasta & evenly distribute tomatoes & basil over pasta. Place chicken on top of pasta. Sprinkle mozz cheese over chicken. Place baking dish under broiler for 2-3 minutes or until cheese is melted.
