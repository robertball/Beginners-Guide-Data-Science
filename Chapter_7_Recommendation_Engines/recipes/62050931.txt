Title:
Cold Cucumber Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large cucumber, peeled and seeded
1 can of cream of celery soup
1 cup sour cream
1 cup half and half
dash of hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This soup is great on a summer day for lunch.  It's creamy and great tasting and so easy to prepare.  I chill the individual soup bowls in the freezer which makes it cool and refreshing.
