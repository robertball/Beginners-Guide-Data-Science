Title:
Curried Garlic Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, quartered
1/2 cup butter
3 teaspoons minced garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1 lemon, juiced
2 tablespoon curry powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Place potatoes in an 8x12 inch baking dish.
In a small sauce pan melt butter and add garlic. Saute garlic over med low heat for two minutes.  Add salt, curry and lemon juice and continue cooking for 2 minutes. Pour over potatoes and stir to coat.
Bake, covered, in preheated oven for 30 minutes.  Uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
