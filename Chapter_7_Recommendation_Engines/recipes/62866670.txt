Title:
Buttermilk Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
4 or 5 chicken breasts
flour
salt
pepper
2 cans of cream of chicken soup
buttermilk

Directions:
Melt 1 stick of butter in a casserole dish. Salt, pepper, and flour the chicken breasts. Place the chicken in casserole dish skin side down. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Turn over and bake 15 additional minutes. Mix 2 cans of Cream of Chicken Soup with 1 can of buttermilk. Pour over the chicken. Cover and bake 15 to 20 more minutes.

Angela Morris

Baked chicken cooked in buttermilk & cream of chicken soup
