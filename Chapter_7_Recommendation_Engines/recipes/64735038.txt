Title:
Garden Fresh Farro Bowl w/ Balsamic Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6243344.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cooked Farro (Follow cooking instructions on package instructions)
1/2 Tomato, seeded & diced
1/2 Cucumber, cut small
2-3 Mini Peppers, diced
1/2 Avocado, sliced thin
8 Kalamata Olives, sliced
2 oz Crumbled Feta
Balsamic Dressing:
1/2 Cup Olive Oil
2 Tablespoons Balsamic Vinegar
1 Tablespoon Dijon Mustard
1 Clove Garlic, minced
Salt & Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Seed, dice and cut all of the vegetables.
Put Farro into bowl, then add the chopped vegetables. Top with feta cheese.
Add 1-2 tablespoons of Balsamic dressing. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
tcasa
Description: 
I make farro bowls all the time since reading an article about how good farro is for our bodies. Plus after having kids I had a few extra pounds to loose. Any variety of vegetable would work for this so make it your own:)
