Title:
Veal Scallopini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices veal steak (thin, must be about 2 pounds)
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1/4 small can tomato sauce
Small can of mushrooms, reserve liquid
1/4 cup white wine

Directions:
Trim veal; salt/pepper each small piece
Saute in oil and butter until lightly browned
Remove veal from pan
Add onion and green pepper, saute until clear
Add mixture of tomato sauce and liquid from mushrooms (about 1/2 cup)
Add veal pieces and mushrooms; cook about 5 minutes
Pour in wine, cover and cook until tender
