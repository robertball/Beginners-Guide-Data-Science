Title:
Mississippi Spiced Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Sticks of butter
2 Cups sugar
2 Eggs
2 Cups Applesauce
3 t Ground Cinnamon
2 t Ground Allspice
1 t Ground Cloves
1 t Salt
2 t Baking Soda
4 Cups AP Flour
1tbs Orange Flavor
1/4 cup Powder Sugar
1 Cups nuts (optional)

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar, add eggs 1 at a time. Mix in applesauce, and spices. Sift together salt, soda and flour, add to mixer a little at a time. Add flavor last. Stir in nuts. Bake in light greased mini muffin tins at 350 for about 10 min, cool and sprinkle with powder sugar. For the nuts I use the minced nuts just for textur.

Submitter: 
SHYLIE
Description: 
Lightly sweet mini muffin filled with flavor of the season. I end up making them twice a year because they go so fast. 
Great for parties and as a gift because of the yield.
