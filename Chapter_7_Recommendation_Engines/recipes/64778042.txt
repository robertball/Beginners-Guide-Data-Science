Title:
Meatloaf Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Meatloaf Diced in 1/2" pieces
1/3 C Mayonnaise (or more to taste)
2 Tbsp Milk
1/4 C finely diced onion
1/4 C finely diced celery
Salt and Pepper to taste
Lettuce (optional)
Tomato (optional)
8 Slices of Rye Bread

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except bread in a medium bowl. Spread on 4 Slices of Bread and top with the rest of the bread.

Submitter: 
peppini
Description: 
This is a play on the traditional next day, cold, meatloaf sammy. Use a favorite meatloaf recipe.
