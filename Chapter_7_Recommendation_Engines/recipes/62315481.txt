Title:
Shrimp & Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Lemon Juice Concentrate
1/4 cup Sour Cream
1/4 cup Mayo
1/4 cup Fresh Cilantro, chopped
1/2 tsp Cumin
1/2 Tsp Ground Cayenne Pepper
1 lb Medium Shrimp, cooked, peeled and chopped
(Can subsitute canned small shrimp, if preferred)
1 can Corn (15-16oz), drained
1/3 cup Purple Onion, finely chopped

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until well combined.  Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Allison
Description: 
Cool and refreshing dip!  Goes wonderfully with Frito Scoops! Absolutely LOVE this dip!
