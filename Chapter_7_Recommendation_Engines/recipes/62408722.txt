Title:
Jan's Chocolate Chip Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436132.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter
8 ounces of cream cheese
2 cups of sugar
4 large eggs
3 cups all purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 cup whole milk
12 ounce bag of mini chocolate chips (large chips tend to sink to the bottom)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Grease a 10 inch tube pan.
Beat the butter and cream cheese together until light and fluffy.
Add in sugar and blend well.
Mix in eggs one at a time.
Gradually add in the flour, baking powder, salt, vanilla and milk.
Fold in chocolate chips.
Put batter in prepared pan and bake for approximately 60 minutes or until a knife inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
Cool 15 minutes and remove to wire rack to cool completely.

Description: 
Moist and delicious pound cake that is simple to prepare.  This recipe works well with conversions to soy products for a lactose free desert as well.
