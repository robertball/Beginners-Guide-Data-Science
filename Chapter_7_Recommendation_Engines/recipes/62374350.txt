Title:
Memom's Polo Picante Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413478.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked rice
1 can of cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup of milk
1 can sliced mushrooms
1 small onion chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp. chili powder (opt)
3 cups cooked diced chicken
1 lb. montery Jack or Cheddar Cheese
1 16 oz jar picante sauce

Directions:
Combine soup, milk, mushrooms, onion, salt, pepper, and chili powder.  Mix thoroughly.  Layer ingredients in a greased 13 X 9 pan in following order: Rice, soup mix, picante, cheese.  Keep repeating untill finished bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until done.

Description: 
This recipe is easy to make, most of the indregients you have on hand.  You can make ahead, and make as much or as little as you want.
