Title:
Mexican Beef in Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840495.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1/3 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
Corn Bread:
3/4 cup cornmeal
1 cup milk
1 cup flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 slightly beaten egg
1/4 cup oil
Salsa

Directions:
Cook beef, onion, and garlic until meat is browned; drain.  Stir in ketchup and salt.
Meanwhile prepare cornbread batter.
Mix together cornmeal and milk and set aside for 5 minutes. Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
Stir egg and oil into cornmeal mixture.
Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until combined.
Spread 1/2 the cornbread mixture into a greased 8x8x2 inch pan.
Spoon beef mixture on top and sprinkle with cheese.  Spread remaining cornbread batter on top of beef mixture.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 - 40 minutes or until nicely browned.  Let stand for five minutes before slicing into squares.
Top each slice with salsa.

Submitter: 
Davena
Description: 
An easy and quick recipe: the corn bread-ground meat mixture bakes together as one.
