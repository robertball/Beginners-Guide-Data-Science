Title:
Microwave Steamed Plain Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
1 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Wash the rice carefully then drain.
Put the rice in a microwave-safe bowl. Pour 1 1/2 cup of water then soak for 30 minutes.
Microwave the rice (covered) for 4 minutes using 600W.
Take out the rice then mix. Put back the cover then put back in the microwave. Cook for 10 more minutes using 200W.
Keep the bowl covered for 10 more minutes once done then you can serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This is how you can cook rice much faster than a rice cooker or on stove top.
