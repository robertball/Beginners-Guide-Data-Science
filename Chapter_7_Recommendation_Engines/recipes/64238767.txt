Title:
Grandma Hazel's Famous Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1725568.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lg Jar of Cheese Whiz
1 Onion (Chopped)
1/2 Tsp  Salt
1/2 Tsp  Pepper
1/2 C.  Butter
1 Tsp  Soy Sauce
1/4 C.  Flour
1/2 C.  Slivered Almonds
1 Can   Water Chestnuts
1 C.    Milk
2 Shakes of Tabasco Hot Sauce (Optional)
2 Cans  French Style Green Beans

Directions:
Saute onions in butter. Add Flour. Cook until smooth.
Add Milk, Cheese Whiz, Tabasco Sauce, Soy Sauce, Salt & Pepper. Cook a few minutes. Mix Green Beans & Water Chestnuts with Sauce. Place in buttered casserole dish (or disposable 13x9x2 aluminum foil pan). Cover with Slivered Almonds. Bake @ 375 degrees for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jodi Waldrup
Description: 
This is one of my all time favorite recipies. I have to always make a double batch, otherwise it will be gone in 10 minutes!
