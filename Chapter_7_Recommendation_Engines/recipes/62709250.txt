Title:
Venison Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614743.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb Venison Roast or Venison Steak
1 Medium Onion
2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
6oz Sliced Mushrooms
1/2 Teaspoon Seasoning Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
3/4 Cup Water
1 1/4 Tablespoon Corn Starch
1/2 Box of Penne Noodles

Directions:
Dice up onion and set aside.
Trim and slice venison thinly 1/4in or thinner and set aside.
In a large pan/skillet brown onion and mushrooms over medium heat in vegetable oil until onion is clear or to desired tenderness.
Meanwhile prepare noodles as box directions provide.
Add venison, seasoning salt, pepper, garlic powder to skillet of onions and mushrooms, and brown venison evenly on both sides.
Add Worcestershire sauce and Soy sauce to skillet, reduce heat to low cover and let simmer for 10-15min.
Meanwhile in a separate small mixing bowl mix water and corn starch until completely dissolved, then add mixture to skillet and until broth thickens.
Serve Over Noodles and Enjoy!

Description: 
This is my dad's original recipe, it was always one of my favorite dishes he would make! This recipe is a great way to prepare venison especially if you don't care for the gamy taste of venison!
