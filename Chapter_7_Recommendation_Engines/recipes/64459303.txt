Title:
Flax Seed Crackers - CWP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Wholewheat Flour or whole spelt flour
1/2 cup Golden Flax Seeds, ground or whole
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Celtic Sea Salt and more for topping, optional
1 tbs Nutritional Yeast optional
1/2 cup Almond Milk or plant milk of choice
1/2 cup sesame seeds (opt) or
1/2 cup ground nuts (opt)

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients except the almond milk and mix well.
Add almond milk to the dry mix and combine into a dough.
Put dough in fridge for 10 minutes to let it cool and rest.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Roll dough flat and evenly on non-stick parchment paper and score shapes using a pizza cutter or knife.
Sprinkle salt over the top if wanted and roll lightly using rolling pin.
Place in oven for 20 to 30 minutes until desired crispiness is achieved. This will vary depending on how thinly you have rolled the dough.
