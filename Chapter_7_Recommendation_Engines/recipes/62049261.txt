Title:
Hot Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz frozen spinach; 1 can (16.5 oz) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped; 8 oz mayonnaise; 2 cups grated parmesan cheese (divided); 4 oz shredded Swiss cheese; 8 oz shredded mozarella cheese; 3 oz cream cheese, softened; 4 oz sour cream

Directions:
Prepare spinach according to directions.  Drain in colander and use the bottom of a glass to press excess water out of the spinach.  In a large bowl, mix spinach and artichokes.  Add mayonnaise, 1 cup parmesan, swiss cheese, mozarella cheese, cream cheese and sour cream.  Spray a 2 qt baker with nonstick spray.  Spread mixture in the baker.  Top with 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese.  Bake 40 min or until bubbly around the edges.  Turn on the broiler and broil until parmesan is golden brown.

Submitter: 
JUNEBUFF1
Description: 
Many different kinds of cheese make this dip a gooey treat at parties.  Fresh parmesan cheese that you have grated yourself makes the difference.  Serve with cut up veggies or sturdy crackers.  Herbs or hot pepper sauce can be added.
