Title:
Big Ray's Egg Roll Noodle Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4563840.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tTbsp sesame oil
1/2 pound ground pork
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground turmeric
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
6 cups shredded cabbage (about 1 small head)
2 large carrots, shredded (about 2 cups)
1 package Ramen noodle noodles
3 green onions, thinly sliced
Additional soy sauce, optional

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; cook and crumble pork until browned, 4-6 minutes. Stir in soy sauce, garlic and seasonings. Add cabbage and carrots; cook 4-6 minutes longer or until vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally.
Cook Ramen noodles according to package directions (without flavor packet); drain and immediately add to pork mixture, tossing to combine. Sprinkle with green onions. If desired, serve with additional soy sauce.
