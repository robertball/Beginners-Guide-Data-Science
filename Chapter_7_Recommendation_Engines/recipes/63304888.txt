Title:
Eleanor's Barbeque Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 brisket (about 4 lbs, if larger then double recipe)
2 TBSP liquid smoke
garlic powder
2 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
pepper

Directions:
Place meat on sheet of foil.  Place inside baking pan and fold up edges.  Add liquid smoke and worcestershire sauce.  Sprinkle generously with garlic and pepper.  Fold foil edges together to seal in meat.  Place another piece of foil over top of pan and seal over edges of pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 3-6 hours depending on size of meat.  Add barbeque sauce 30 minutes before meat is done.
