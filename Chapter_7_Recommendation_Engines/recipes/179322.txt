Title:
Smirnoff Passion Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982979.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Smirnoff Passion Fruit Vodka
1 splash cranberry juice
1 splash pineapple juice
1 splash grapefruit juice
2 dashes bitters
1 splash lemon-lime soda
1 piece pineapple
1 piece orange
1 piece cherry

Directions:
Build in a Collins glass over ice.
Top with lemon-lime soda.
Garnish with pineapple wedge, an orange slice and a cherry.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
A delicious fruity punch cocktail combines Smirnoff Passion Fruit Vodka with cranberry, pineapple and grapefruit juice in a tall glass over ice.
