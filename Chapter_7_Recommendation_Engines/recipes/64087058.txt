Title:
Stone Fruit Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sliced peeled peaches (~6 medium)
2 cups sliced pitted plums (~3 medium)
2 1/2 cups sliced nectarines (~3 large)
1 cup organic cane sugar, divided
2 tbsp corn starch
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp almond extract
2 cups Bisquick Heart Smart mix
1/2 cup milk
2 tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  In a large bowl, toss fruit, 3/4 cup sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, and almond extract until coated.  Spoon into an ungreased 11x7 baking dish.
In a medium bowl, stir together Bisquick and 2 tbsp sugar.  Stir in milk and butter until soft dough forms.  Drop by heaping tablespoons onto fruit mixture.  Sprinkle with remaining 2 tbsp sugar.
Bake 45 to 50 minutes until filling is bubbly and topping is golden brown.  Cool for 15 minutes and serve warm.
