Title:
Pasta with Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tsp. olive oil
2 tsp. minced garlic
1 cup chopped onion
12 oz. lean ground beef or turkey
2 cups spaghetti sauce
3 cups cooked whole wheat pasta

Directions:
In a medium skillet over medium heat, heat garlic and onion in oil until lightly browned. Add beef/turkey and cook 4 minutes until browned. Add sauce. Simmer 10 minutes. Serve over pasta.
