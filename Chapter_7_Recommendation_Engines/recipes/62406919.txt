Title:
Chipotle Chicken Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/435062.jpg
Ingredients:
non-stick cooking spray
4(6 inch) corn tortillas
1 small  tomato, chopped
2 green onions, sliced
4-1/2 tsp.  lime juice
1 Tbsp. chopped cilantro
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 Tbsp. finely chopped canned chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
1-1/2 cups  shredded cooked chicken
3/4 cup Mexican Style Shredded cheese
1/3 cup  sliced black olives
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 400ÂºF.
Spray both sides of each tortilla with cooking spray; place on baking sheet.
Bake 6 to 8 min. or until crisp and lightly browned.
While tortillas are baking, combine next 4 ingredients; set aside. Mix cream cheese and chipotle peppers until well blended.
Spread tortillas with cream cheese mixture; top with chicken and cheese.
Bake 5 min. or until cheese is melted. Top with tomato mixture, olives and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Jaay
Description: 
These are perfect served as a simple weeknight meal or when you want to wow guests without spending all day in the kitchen.  Just the right amount of smoky spiciness from the peppers, and a nice creaminess from the cheese.
