Title:
Cannoli Cream FIlling and or Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz marscapone
48 oz ricotta strained
2 cups powdered sugar
1 tsp orange zest
3/4 chips
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
If marscapone is cold beat with paddle until smooth, add ricotta, beat again until smooth, add remaining ingredients and mix well.
