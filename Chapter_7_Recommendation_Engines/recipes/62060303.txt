Title:
Broccoli Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz. pkg frozen chopped broccoli
1 cup uncooked rice
2 cups water
1 pkg. onion soup mix
1/2 stick margerine

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a large casserole.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees for one hour or until water is absorbed.  Stir well before serving.

Description:
