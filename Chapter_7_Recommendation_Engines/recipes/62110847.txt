Title:
Chicken Amazing! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. boned chicken thighs skinned
8 Tblsp. Worchestire Sauce
4 Tblsp. Spicy Garlic Marinade
4 Tblsp. Terryaki Sauce
1 Can cream of mushroom soup
Pepper to taste
Salt to taste
1 tsp. chili powder
2 Tblsp. Terragon
1/2 small red onion
8 oz. fresh mushroom
4 cloves fresh garlic chopped
4 Tblsp. Olive Oil
1 tsp. parsley

Directions:
Poke holes in chicken with fork.  Mix Worchestire, Spicy Garlic Marinade, Terryaki Sauce and apply to each chicken thigh.  In a seperate bowl, mix mushroom soup with salt, pepper, chili powder & terragon.  Pour evenly over each chicken thigh, cover and set in fridge over night.  Next day, place chicken thighs in a baking pan and cover, place in oven at 365 degrees for 25 mins.  In a seperate pan saute' red onion, mushrooms & garlic in olive oil until desired tenderness.  Remove chicken from oven and top each thigh with the saute mix. Return chicken to oven and bake for an additional 10-15 mins. Remove from oven, top with parsley and be amazed!

Submitter: 
Christy Sprouse Hilbun
Description: 
A little bit of work but worth the result! These chicken thighs are marinaded with a garlic & terryaki sauce then baked and topped with cooked mushrooms, garlic & onions.
