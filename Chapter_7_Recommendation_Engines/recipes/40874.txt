Title:
Cheddar-Ranch Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983231.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups Cheez-It® White Cheddar Baked Snack Crackers
2 cups mini pretzel braids
2 cups crispy cereal squares
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon lemon and herb seasoning
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed
1 (1 ounce) package Ranch-style dip mix

Directions:
Mix White Cheddar Cheez-It(R) crackers, pretzels and cereal squares in a large microwavable bowl.
In a separate bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  Pour seasoning mixture over the crackers, pretzels and cereal squares and blend well.
Microwave on high for 1 minute.  Dry on paper towels and store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Keebler® Kitchens
Description: 
Looking for something to serve the gang on game day? This mixture of tempting  Cheez-It® White Cheddar crackers, pretzel braids, and crunchy cereal squares bathed in savory garlic, dill, and ranch flavors is sure to please even the rowdiest bunch.
