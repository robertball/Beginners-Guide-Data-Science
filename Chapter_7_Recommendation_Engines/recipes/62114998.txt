Title:
Sfoof (Lebanese cake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup semolina flour
1/2 cup  wheat flour
1 1/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 tablespoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
dash nutmeg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 tablespoon anise
1 cup milk
1/2 cup cooking oil
1/4 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven at 180 degree c.
Mix all powder ingredients except for sugar in one bowl.
Whisk oil and sugar in another bowl, then add milk.
Gradually add the flour mixture to the liquid mixture.
Pour into a greased baking tray.
Garnish with almonds.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Cut into squares.
Serve cold.
