Title:
Pizzelle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 cup white sugar
3/4 cup vegetable oil
2.5 cups flour
1-1.5 oz anise extract

Directions:
Preheat pizzelle iron
Combine eggs, sugar, and oil
Slowly add flour
Once fully mixed, add anise
Dollop approximately 1 tsp-1 Tbsp of batter onto each pizzelle form
Allow to cook for approximately 10 seconds, depending on pizzelle iron and desired color
Remove and let cool on wire rack or baking tray
Store in sealed container
