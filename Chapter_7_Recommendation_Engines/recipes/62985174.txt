Title:
Mel Tellis Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs (beaten)						1 large onion (chopped)
10oz pkg frozen chopped broccoli (thawed & drained)	1 ½ sticks melted butter
2 cups cottage cheese						2 tbls sugar
2-7oz pkgs Jiffy Corn Bread Mix

Directions:
Mix all together and put into a greased 9”x13” pan.  Bake 1 hour at 350°F or until done.  Cauliflower can also be substituted or maybe even corn.
