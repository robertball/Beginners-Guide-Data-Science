Title:
Burrito Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 pkg. taco seasoning
1 can refried beans
1 jar of salsa
1 C. grated cheddar cheese or monterey jack cheese
sour cream (optional)
sliced black olives (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef and taco seasoning.  Form into a loaf shape and place in a loaf pan for baking.  Cook for 30-45 minutes at 375 degrees.
Remove loaf from pan and drain and drippings from pan.  Spread the refried beans into the bottom of the pan and place meatloaf on top of the beans.  Top the loaf with your favorite salsa.  Sprinkle with your choice of cheese and bake until cheese is melted (about 10 minutes).  Slice and serve.
You can top the meatloaf with sour cream and sliced olives if you desire.
