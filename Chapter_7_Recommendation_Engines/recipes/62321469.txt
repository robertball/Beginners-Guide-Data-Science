Title:
Oma's Spetsula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 quarts water, salted to taste
2 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
2 eggs, slightly beaten
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Bring water, salted to taste, to a boil.
Stir flour, salt, eggs and milk together in a medium bowl.
Dump dough onto a cutting board.
Dip a knife blade into the boiling water.  Cut strips of dough into the water (about 2-3 inches long and 1/2 inch thick.)  Repeat until all dough is in boiling water.
Boil and stir for 5 minutes
