Title:
Sweet and Savory Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 Frozen Pre-Cooked Meatballs
3 12 oz. jars of pizza sauce
2 8 oz jars of red currant jelly
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Cook frozen meatballs as directed
Place sauce and jelly into slow cooker
Stir in salt and pepper
When the meatballs are done stir in with the sauces
Cook on Medium heat for 2-3 hours
Simmer to keep hot

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
These meatballs have a sweet and savory flavor that people love! Great for parties!
