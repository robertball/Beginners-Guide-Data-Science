Title:
Rotel Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar rotel mild
1 package velveeta, cubed
1 pound lean ground beef
1 package taco seasoning

Directions:
brown meat. drain fat.
stir in taco seasoning (according to directions).
stir in rotel and velveeta until warm.
serve with tortilla chips.
