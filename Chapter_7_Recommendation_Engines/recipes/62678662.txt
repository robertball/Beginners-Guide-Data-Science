Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBL butter
2 TBL flour
2 Milk
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar
Elbow noodles (cooked)
1 medium onion
1 cup cubed sharp cheddar
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)
Topping:
1/4 cup sharp cheddar
1/2 cup Bread crumbs

Directions:
Saut√© butter, add flour, stir for a coupled minutes while flour cooks.  Slowly add milk, once thickened, add shredded cheeses
Butter casserole bottom, add half the noodles, half the dice onion and half the cubed cheese, salt, pepper and pour cheese sauce over top, add second half noodles, remaining onion and cubed cheese, slightly mix in cubes.  Top with fine layer of shredded cheese and bread crumbs.
Cook 375 covered for 20 minutes, uncover and cook 15-20 minutes more or until top slightly browns.
