Title:
tortillini pasta salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 2 packages of tortellini pasta
2. 1 green bell pepper
3. 1 red bell pepper
4. 1 red onion
5. 1 package of Italian seasoning
6. 1 lb of mozzarella cheese
7. banana peppers
8. 1 small can of olives
9. pickles
10. 1 can of artichokes
11. parm

Directions:
cook totellini according to package directions, drain and chill in fridge. prepare italian dressing acccording to packaging. dice peppers and onions. pickle onions with dill, vinegar/water and sugar. cube mozz cheese. add all ingredients togerther with dressing and let sit for 4 hours. serve cold

Submitter: 
Desmond Molina
Description: 
Italian style tortillini antpasto pasta salad with salami/pepperoni and veggies and olives.
