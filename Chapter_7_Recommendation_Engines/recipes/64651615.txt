Title:
Tomato Basil Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4	Boneless & Skinless Chicken Breasts
1 jar	Tomato & Basil Sauce
1 cup	Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
6 cloves	Minced Garlic
1½ tsp	Onion Powder
1 tsp	Oregano
1 tsp	Basil
½ tsp	Rosemary

Directions:
1.Put the chicken breasts into a crock pot and cook them for 3 hours on high setting. Afterwards, drain the any juices from the crock pot.
2.Add the tomato and basil sauce, minced garlic, onion powder, oregano, basil, and rosemary into a bowl and mix all ingredients together well.
3  Add the sauce on top of the chicken breasts along with the Mozzarella cheese. Place a lid on the crock pot and keep cooking the chicken in the crock pot for 30 more minutes

Submitter: 
Alissa
Description: 
This chicken is covered with tasty tomato and basil sauce, Mozzarella cheese, and a variety of fresh spices. This filling and satisfying meal is impossible to resist. Use a crock pot to make this slow cooked meal for the entire family.
