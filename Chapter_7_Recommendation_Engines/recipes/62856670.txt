Title:
stewed apricot, ricotta, sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c water
1c sugar
2 cinnamon sticks
4 star anise
1/4 t peppercorns, crushed
6 fresh apricots, pitted and quartered
1/2 fennel sliced crosswise into 1/8 strips
4 slices country bread
1/2 lb ricotta
2 T toasted pistachios
salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine water-pepper and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 min-Add the apricots and fennel and stir. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 min.
Grill bread on both sides. Spread ricotta on each slice, top with remaining.
