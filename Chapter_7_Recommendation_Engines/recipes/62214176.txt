Title:
Cream of Wheat Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c milk
1/2 c cream of wheat
1 c marg
1 tsp vanilla
3/4 c sugar
I box graham wafers
1 box cream cheese
1/2 to 3/4 cup icing sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 c melted chocolate chips
1/2 tsp butter

Directions:
Bring milk to almost boiling temperature, add cream of wheat, cook for 3 to 4 minutes, set aside and let cool.
Beat together the marg, vanilla and sugar then add the cooled cream of wheat mixture, beat well. Line a 9 X 13 inch cake pan with graham wafers, spread half the filling over top and cover with graham wafers...repeat.
Beat cream cheese and add icing sugar and vanilla. Spread over graham wafers. Melt chocolate chips and butter in microwave and drizzle over top of cream cheese. Cool for 2 hours.
