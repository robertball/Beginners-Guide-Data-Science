Title:
Raisin Noodle Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4581939.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz)  medium extra wide yock free noodles (cooked to pkg instructions)
1/2 c sweet butter
2 eggs slightly beaten
2 Tbsp. vanilla
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c raisins
16 oz  creamed cottage cheese
1 pt. sour cream
Topping:
1/4 c brown sugar mixed with 1/4 c corn flake crumbs.

Directions:
Mix butter with cooked noodles. Add all other ingredients (except topping) and place in a greased 9 by 13 baking dish. Sprinkle topping and bake at 450 degrees for 30 minutes or till well done.
