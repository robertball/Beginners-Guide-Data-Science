Title:
Broccoli Chicken Casserole  for 40 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
6 pound frozen broccoli florets, thawed
6 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
6 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups milk
6 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
6 boxes stuffing mix- prepared

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
Boil chicken until tender and shred or cut into bite size pieces.
In a small bowl mix together the soups and milk then stir in remaining ingredients. Divide in half and put into 2 large church pans. Sprinkle some cheese on top. Cover loosely with foil and bake at 300 for 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
Prepared stuffing can be mixed in or layered on top- your choice.Modified from: Broccoli Chicken Casserole I
