Title:
Miso Maple Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the glaze (make enough for 1 large rounded tablespoon per piece of fish):
1 part yellow miso paste
1 part seasoned rice vinegar
1 part real maple syrup
hot sauce to taste
*you should taste and adjust these proportions to your liking

Directions:
Prepare glaze – mix everything together – it should taste intense. Use a whisk until smooth. Set aside.
Preheat broiler
In a COLD pan (that can go in the oven) brush vegetable oil in skillet. Put salmon, skin side down in the pan. Heat the pan and let heat for a few minutes. Then put pan in the oven and broil 8 inches from heat – about 4 or 5 minutes. Use spatule to gently peel salmon from the skin that should remain in the pan.
