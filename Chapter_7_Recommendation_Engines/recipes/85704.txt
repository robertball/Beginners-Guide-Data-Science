Title:
Strawberry Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986866.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI® Mixers Pina Colada frozen mix
1/2 cup strawberries
2 ounces crushed pineapple
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
5 cups ice
Strawberries and fresh pineapple for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, BACARDI(R) Rum, strawberries and pineapple.
Add ice and blend for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with strawberries and pineapple.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Strawberries and pineapple share a starring role in these rummy blended ice drinks.
