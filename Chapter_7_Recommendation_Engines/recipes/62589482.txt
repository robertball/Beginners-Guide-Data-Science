Title:
Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Med. Onion, chopped
1 Med. Carrot, chopped
3 cloves Garlic, chopped
1-1/2# Gr. Beef
1/2 Cup Grape Jelly
1-1oz pkg. dry Onion soup mix
1 28oz can Crushed Tomatoes
1 6oz can Tomato Paste
12oz Water
2 Teas. dried Oregano
2 Teas. dried Basil
1/2 Teas. Cinnamon
1 Tbls. Worchestershire sauce
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Sweat onion and carrot in a little butter. Add garlic after onion has turned translucent. Cook for about 2 min. and then add Gr. Beef and cook til it is brown. Drain any excess fat. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for about an hour of so.

This is a variation of a copycat recipe that I used years ago and have adapted to my own taste.
