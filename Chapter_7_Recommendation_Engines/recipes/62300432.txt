Title:
Vegan Four Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364384.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12 oz package TVP crumbles
2 15 oz cans black beans
2 15 oz cans white navy beans
2 15 oz cans dark red kidney beans
1 15 oz can light red kidney beans
2 14.5 oz cans stewed tomatoes
3 onions, chopped
1 12 oz can tomatoe juice
3 Tbsp red chili powder
1 1/2 Tbsp ground cumin
1 Tbsp garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain and rinse all beans.
In a large pot combine all ingredients, bring to a boil, then cover and summer for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Deborah Mackenzie
Description: 
A hearty chili with plenty of texture and a good thick sauce to it that defies belief in it's actual vegan origins.
