Title:
Thai One On - Exotic Sweet Potato and Coconut Soup with Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp Olive Oil
1 cup White Onion (Chopped)
1 Cup Red Pepper (Chopped)
3/4 Cup Celery (Chopped)
2 tsp Garlic (minced)
4 Cups Sweet Potato (Peeled and cubed)
2tbsp Ginger Root, fresh (Grated)
3/4 tsp Cumin
3/4 tsp Coriander
3/4 tsp Curry
1/4 tsp Cinnamon, ground
2 Cups Chicken Broth, Sodium reduced
1 Can (398 ml) Coconut Milk (Light)
1/4 tsp Black Pepper (fresh ground)
3 tbsp Fresh Cilantro, minced
3 tbsp Fresh Basil, minced
2 tbsp Peanut Butter, Light
1 tbsp Brown sugar
1 tbsp Lime Juice, Fresh
1 pound shrimp, cooked

Directions:
Heat Olive Oil in large pot.
Add onions, red pepper, celery and garlic.
Cook and stir until vegetables begin to soften, about 4 minutes.
Stir in sweet potatoes, ginger root, cumin, coriander, curry and cinnamon.
Cook and stir for 30 more seconds.
Add chicken broth, coconut milk and pepper.
Bring soup to a boil.
Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.
Carefully transfer half the soup to a blender and puree until smooth.
Return pureed soup to pot with remaining soup.
Stir in cilantro, basil, peanut butter, brown sugar and lime juice and mix well.
Add shrimp and heat for about 2 more minutes, or just until the shrimp are hot.
