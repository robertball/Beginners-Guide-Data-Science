Title:
Triple-Layered Delight Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521606.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup chopped nuts
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup coconut
1/2 cup chopped macaschino cherries
2 tbsp flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 cup butter, softened
2 cups icing sugar, sifted
1 tbsp milk
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp grated lemon rind

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine flour and sugar; cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse meal. Press into greased 9-inch square cake pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 15 minutes or until golden.
Beat eggs. Stir in sugar, vanilla, nuts, raisins, coconut, cherries, flour and baking powder. Spread mixture evenly over base. Bake for 30 minutes or until set. Cool completely.
Combine all ingredients beating until smooth. Spread on filling. Chill until icing is firm. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
With the shortbread crust and lemon butter icing, these fruit filled bars are sure to become a favorite. They are always a must when I do my holiday baking.
