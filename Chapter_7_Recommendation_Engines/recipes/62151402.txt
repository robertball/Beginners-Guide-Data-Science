Title:
Chocolate Pudding and Sea Salt Carmel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz. dark chocolate (I use Callebeaut or Lindt)
1c Whole Milk
1c Chocolate Milk( it can even be mocha milk if you like)
1c Heavy Cream
3/4c granulated sugar
1/8 tsp salt
1/4c. Cornstarch
1/4c Unsalted butter NO MARGARINE!(if you MUST use salted butter, omit the salt)
3/4 tsp vanilla
Carmel Sauce
1/4c Sugar
1/2c Corn Syrup
1T Water
1/4c COLD butter(NO MARGARINE!)
1/4-1/2c Heavy Cream(depends how thick you want it)
1/4tsp sea salt

Directions:
Chocolate Pudding
Chop the chocolate into fine pieces.
Combine 1&3/4cups milk, cream, sugar, cocoa powder,chopped chocolate and salt into a large sauce pan. Stir vigorously using a whisk over medium heat until mixture simmers and is snooth and thick.
Stir remaining 1/4 cup milk and cornstarch together and wisk into chocolate mixture. Simmer for one minute while constantly stiring. Add the butter and cook for one more minute. Then remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Transfer to a bowl and put plastic wrap directly on top of the pudding so it won't from a skin. Cool in fridge.
Carmel Sauce:
Combine the sugar, corn syrup, and water in a heavy bottem saucepan over Medium heat till mixture boils and turns amber brown.
Reduce the LOW heat and whisk in cold butter, heavy cream and sea salt until the mixture is smooth. Transfer to heat proof container and cool. Store in fridge. To use, immerse in hot water and stir,till mixture is thick but pourable.. Yield: about 1 cup.

Description: 
Deep chocolate pudding that is VERY rich! It has not only cocoa powder but regular brick chocolate as well. Exceptional :) Serve with a carmel sauce or by it's self :)   Don't be freaked out by the sea salt in the carmel sauce it is really taste and adds a dimention to the whole dessert! Try it :) The pudding makes an excellent filling for anything!
