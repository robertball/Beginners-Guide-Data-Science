Title:
Cheesy Pasta w Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1lb jars light Alfredo Sce
1/2 cup grated Parmesan Chs
1 lb mini-penne pasta (or other shapes)
1 10-ounce package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
1 tblspns plain bread crumbs

Directions:
1.  Cook pasta as directed on package; drain. Set aside.  Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly spray shallow 3 qrt baking dish with vegetable cooking spray.
2.  Heat the Alfredo sce in saucepan over medium heat until hot.  Stir in 1/4 cup grated Parmesan.  Stir until cheese melts and sauce is smooth.
3.  Return pasta to pot and add the broccoli, cheese sauce and nutmeg.  Toss until pasta is evenly coated.  Pour into prepared baking dish.
4.  Combine the remaining Parmesan cheese with bread crumbs; sprinkle over top of casserole.  Bake about 30 minutes until browned on top.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
