Title:
Super Easy Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons canola oil
1 medium Spanish onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 fresh Poblano chile, deseeded and chopped
1 lb lean ground beef
1 14oz can undrained black beans
1 14oz can refried black beans
1 cup water or beer
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon fresh-ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat oil over medium heat.
Add onion, garlic and chile and cook for 1 minute, stirring to evenly cook.
Increase heat to medium-high.
Add beef and cook until meat is browned.
Drain off excess fat.
Add remaining ingredients.
Bring chili to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer.
Simmer chili, covered, for one hour, stirring occasionally.
Serve garnished with grated cheddar cheese, sour cream, chopped jalapeno or serrano peppers, chopped raw onion, chopped fresh cilantro, and/or a squeeze of lime.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Jones
Description: 
There's nothing fancy about this easy meat and bean chili, yet it is as flavorful as it is simple.  Experiment w/ different beans & meats.  Add fresh veggies fried in the pan w/ the onion, garlic & pepper.  Go crazy w/ this wonderfully fulfilling chili, best served w/ fresh cornbread & honey-butter.
