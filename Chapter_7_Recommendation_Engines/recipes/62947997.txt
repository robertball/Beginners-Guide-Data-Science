Title:
FIREHOUSE CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/725282.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs.ground round
1 lb.stewing beef,cut in smaller chuncks
1 large onion,chopped
1 1/2 green pepper, chopped
2 jalapenos,chopped[remove seeds if desired]
2 cups fresh mushrooms,quartered
6 cloves garlic,minced
3 Tbsp.cumin
2 Tbsp.chili powder
1 tsp.extra hot chili powder
1 tsp.paprika
1 tsp.pepper
1 tsp.oregano
1 tsp.salt
1/2 can beer
1 large can tomato sauce
1 large canned tomatos
1 can red kidney beans,strain & rinse
1 can white kidney beans,strain & rinse
1 can pork and beans in tomato sauce
1 can green chilies

Directions:
Brown stewing beef in dutch oven,add ground round and brown well.
Add onion,green pepper,jalapenos and mushrooms.Cook for 5-10 minutes.
Add garlic with all the spices and cook for another 5 minutes.
Add beer and cook another 5 minutes.
Add all remaining ingredients,stir well.Cook for another 10 minutes.
Finish cooking in oven at 350 for 45-1 hour.
This recipe can also be done in a crock pot.
Brown the meats and just add every thing to it and let it cook for 5-6 hours.
