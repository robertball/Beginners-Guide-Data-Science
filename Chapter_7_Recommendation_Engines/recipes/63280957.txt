Title:
SICILIAN SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 HARD RIPE TOMATOES
1/2 CLOVE GARLIC (CHOPPED)
1/4 CUP OLIVE OIL
1/2 SALT
1/2 TSP PEPPER
1/2 TSP WINE VINEGAR
1 TBSP BASIL LEAVES

Directions:
WASH TOMATOES & CUT INTO LARGE PIECES. SPRINKLE
WITH GARLIC, OIL, SALT, PEPPER AND VINEGAR, ADD BASIL LEAVES. CHILL 5 MINUTES
