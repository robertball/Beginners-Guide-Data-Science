Title:
Dirty Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977353.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1 tablespoon olive juice
1 olive

Directions:
In a shaker with ice, add Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka and olive juice.
Shake and strain into a martini glass.
Garnish with olive.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Clean and ultrasmooth Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka gives this cocktail a clear, pure taste unmuddied by impurities or harshness.
