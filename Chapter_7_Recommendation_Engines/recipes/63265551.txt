Title:
Walnut Pesto Chicken over a White Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
White Bean Salad
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/2 small red onion, diced
2 cloves garlic
2 tbs sun dried tomatoes
(in summer use fresh grape tomates)
1 15 oz can cannelini beans, drained
2 cups sliced fresh greens(kale,chard or mixed greens)
Pinch of sea salt
Pepper to taste
------------------------------------------------------
Walnut Pesto Chicken
1/4 lb skinless boneless chicken breasts
pinch of sea salt
1 tbs grape seed oil
2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup raw walnuts
2 cups fresh basil leaves
2 garlic cloves
pinch of sea salt

Directions:
White Bean Salad--------------------------------------
In a saute pan, heat the olive oil on medium heat and then add the onions, garlic, and sun dried tomatoes.  Saute until the onions begin to brown. Remove from heat and cool.  Place the drained beans in a medium bowl.  Add onion mixture, fresh greens, salt, and pepper. Garnish with Walnut Pesto Chicken.
Walnut Pesto Chicken----------------------------------
Slice the chicken into thin strips.  Toss with salt.  Heat 1 tbs of grape seed oil in a saute pan or griddle over medium high heat.  Cook the chicken on each side until cooked through.  Set aside on a paper towel to cool.  In a food processor grind the walnuts to fine.  Rinse the basil & pat dry.  Add the basil, garlic, and salt to the food processor. While its running drizzle in the olive oil. Toss with chicken strips, saving any unused pesto in a jar in the fridge.  Serve over White Bean Salad
