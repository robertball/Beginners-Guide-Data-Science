Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Oatmeal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups quick cooking oats
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp. soda
3/4 cup butter, softened
14 oz. sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine brown sugar, flour, oatmeal, soda, salt and butter. Mix well.
Pat 1/2 of mixture in lightly greased 9 x 13 baking dish. Combine sweetened condensed milk and peanut butter and drizzle over top. Sprinke chocolate chips on top and remaining crumbles. Bake at 350 degrees for 18-20 minutes. Do not overbake.

Submitter: 
Deborah
Description: 
My sisters and I would bake out of bordem in the long Minnesota winters! This was one of our favorites!
