Title:
Creamy Sweet Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984084.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh or frozen corn
1/4 cup half-and-half cream
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine all ingredients. Bring to a boil over medium heat; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, for 6-8 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Florence  Jacoby
Description: 
In Granite Falls, Minnesota, Florence Jacoby uses cream to dress up fresh or frozen corn. The simple side dish tastes rich and takes just minutes to simmer together.
