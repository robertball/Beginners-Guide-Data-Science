Title:
Curried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut into 8 pieces & skin removed
salt & ground black pepper
2 Tbs paprika
1 Tbs butter
1 apple, cored & chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 Tbs curry powder
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of mushroom/celery soup
1 cup milk/sour cream

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350*
Arrange the chicken pieces in single layer in 9x13" baking dish.
Season chicken liberally with salt, pepper, paprika; set aside.
Melt butter in skillet over medium heat.
Add apple, onion to melted butter, season with curry
cook & stir until tender, 7 to 10 minutes.
Stir the mushroom/celery soup & milk/sour cream into mixture until completely combined; spoon over chicken pieces.
Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink at bone & juices run clear, about 75 minutes.  An instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh, near the bone should read 180*.
