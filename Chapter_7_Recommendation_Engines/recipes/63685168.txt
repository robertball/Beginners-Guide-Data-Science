Title:
Pan-Roasted Salmon and Bread Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 1-inch slices of ciabatta (from a 12 oz. loaf), cut into large chunks
5 Tbs. EVOO, divided
2 Pints grape tomatoes, halved
2 large garlic cloves, minced
2 Tbs. salted capers, rinsed and coarsely chopped
1 lemon, halved lengthwise and very thinly sliced
1/4 C chopped flat-leaf parsley
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper
2 Lbs. center-cut skinless salmon filet, cut into 2 inch chunks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. In a large roasting pan, toss the ciabatta chunks with 2 Tbs. EVOO. Roast for 5 minutes, until the bread is lightly toasted.
In a large bowl, toss the tomato halves,with the garlic, capers, lemon, parsley, crushed red peppers, and 2 Tbs. of EVOO;season with salt and pepper. Stir the tomatoes into the toasted bread. Roast for 10-12 minutes, until the tomatoes begin to soften and break down.
Meanwhile, in the same bowl, toss the salmon with the remaining 1 Tbs of EVOO and season with salt and pepper. Nestle the salmon into the bread and tomatoes, spooning some of the tomatoes on top. Roast for about 6-7 minutes until the salmon is just cooked through. Serve right away.
