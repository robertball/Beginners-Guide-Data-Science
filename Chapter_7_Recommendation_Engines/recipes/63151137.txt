Title:
Beefy Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 small onion, minced
1 tea granulated garlic
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon worchestshire sauce
1 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes
1 cup shredded Cheddar and/or Monterey jack cheese
2 loaves frozen white bread
1/3 cup yellow or white corn meal

Directions:
In a skillet, brown beef and onion; drain.
Add the next eight ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Cool.
Sprinkle on counter surface one half the corn meal for each bread loaf.
Roll each loaf of dough into a 16-in. x 8-in. rectangle, about 1/4 in. thick. Cut each into eight 4-in. squares; top each with 1 TBS cheese, 1/4 cup filling.
Bring the four corners together up over filling; pinch seams to seal.
Place on greased baking sheets. Cover and let rise for 15 minutes. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until browned. Serve immediately, or freeze and reheat in microwave at 50% power for 1-1/2 minutes each.
