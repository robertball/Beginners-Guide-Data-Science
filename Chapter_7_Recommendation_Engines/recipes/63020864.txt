Title:
Special K Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 c. white syrup (Karo)
1 c. peanut butter
6 c. Special K cereal
1 pkg. chocolate chips
1 pkg. butterscotch chips

Directions:
Bring the first 3 ingredients to a boil.
Add the cereal. Spread mixture in 9x13 greased pan.
Slowly melt the chocolate and butterscotch chips. Pour over mixture in pan. Let cool
