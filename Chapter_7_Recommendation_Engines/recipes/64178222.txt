Title:
Ginger Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. carrots, sliced
1 onion, thinly sliced
2 tsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. coriander
salt and pepper
1/3 cup water
1/3 cup ginger marmalade
2 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine carrots, onion, sugar, coriander, 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper.  Stir in water.  Cover and cook on low about 4 hours.  Add butter and marmalade and stir to coat.  Serve sprinkled with parsley.
