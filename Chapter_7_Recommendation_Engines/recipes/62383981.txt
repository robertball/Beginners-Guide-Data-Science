Title:
Fiesta Rice Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4464133.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. raw brown rice (or 2 c. cooked brown rice)
1 t. coconut oil
1/2 t. sea salt
2 1/4 c. water (using the liquid from the tomatoes as part of the water)
1 can tomatoes with green chilis
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can corn
1 small sweet onion, diced
4-6 oz. cooked, shredded or diced chicken breast
Salt & Pepper to taste, optional
Toppings:
Shredded cheese
Julienne cucumbers
Sliced black olives
Sliced green onions
Salsa
Sour cream

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, stir the rice, salt and coconut oil into the 2 1/4 c. liquid.  Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer for 50 minutes.
Add tomatoes, black beans, corn, onions and shredded chicken and heat through. Serve in bowls. Top with cheese, salsa, veggies or sour cream as desired. Serve with tortilla chips.

Description: 
Here is a super easy recipe for a Mexican style Rice Bowl.
