Title:
Yummy Red Thai Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz can Coconut Milk
1 to 2 Tbsp red curry paste (Thai Kitchen)
1 to 2 Tbsp fish sauce
2 Tbsp brown sugar
12 fresh basil leaves
1 red bell pepper - cut into small chunks
1 green bell pepper - cut into small chunks
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts - cut into pieces

Directions:
Place coconut milk, fish sauce, sugar and curry paste in freezer bag. Place bell peppers in freezer bag. Place chicken in freezer bag. Wrap basil in plastic wrap.
Serving Day:
Saute chicken pieces in a pan with 1 Tbsp oil. Salt and pepper chicken to taste.
In large sauce pan add 1/3 cup water to coconut milk mixture. Simmer for 15 minutes. Add bell peppers - simmer until bell peppers are crisp-tender. Stir in chicken. Simmer an additional 3 minutes. Stir in basil leaves. Serve over rice.
