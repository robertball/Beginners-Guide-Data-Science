Title:
Gingerbread Men Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup light molasses
1/2 cup cold coffee
6 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/4 tsp. cloves

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.
Beat in molasses and coffee.
Stir together flour, soda, salt and spices; add to molasses mixture and mix well.
Chill dough 1-2 hours or until easy to handle. If needed, add a little additional flour before rolling.
On a lightly floured surface, roll dough to a 1/4" thickness. Cut with gingerbread men cookie cutters.
Bake on cookie sheets @ 350 x 12-15 min.
Cool on wire racks.
Add eyes, noses, smiles and buttons to your men with icing.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
I like the molasses & spices in this recipe. My go-to for Christmas gingerbread men.
