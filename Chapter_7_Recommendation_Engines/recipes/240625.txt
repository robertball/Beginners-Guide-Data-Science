Title:
Pumpkin Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1925092.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup elbow macaroni
1 cup pumpkin puree
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes; drain.
Stir pumpkin puree, butter, and cheese with the drained macaroni in a large bowl until the butter is melted and the noodles completely coated.

Description:
