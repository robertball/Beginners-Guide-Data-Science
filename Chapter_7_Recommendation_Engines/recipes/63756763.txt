Title:
Buffalo Chicken and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packs (8 oz each) cream cheese
1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
1/3-1/2 cup hot sauce such as "Frank's"
1 cup ranch dressing
1 can (15 oz) quartered artichoke hearts
1/4 cup bacon bits
1 cup sour cream
2 cans (10 oz each) white chicken

Directions:
Dice quartered artichoke hearts.  Mix first 7 ingredients in a pan over medium heat.  Stir frequently until cream cheese and cheddar cheese have melted.  Drain chicken and flake with a fork.  Add chicken to cream cheese mixture.  Cook until heated through.  Dish can be prepared in a slow cooker.

Submitter: 
jojo
Description: 
Chicken buffalo dip with a twist.  This is a quick and easy recipe that goes over well at parties, luncheons and family gatherings.  A great tailgate item.
