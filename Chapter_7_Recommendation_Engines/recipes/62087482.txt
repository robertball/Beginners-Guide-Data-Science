Title:
Peanut Butter Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 container frozen whipped topping, thawed
2 tbsp. creamy peanut butter
chopped peanuts for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Mix peanut butter into whipped topping using a whisk or fork. Blend until smooth. For a nuttier taste, add another tablespoon of peanut butter.
That's it! Sprinkle chopped peanuts on top for a garnish if you'd like.
Serve with apples (sprinkle with lemon juice to prevent them from browning) and bananas.

Submitter: 
SJRJA
Description: 
Just two ingredients are combined to make this delicious fruit dip, but no one will believe it! Fabulous with apples and bananas!
