Title:
A Little Taste of Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cubed
1/2 cup snow peas
1/2 cup chopped peppers
Pasta noodles
Sauce:
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 Tbsp. peanut butter
1 Tbsp. cilantro
3 Tbsp. basil
Pinch of sugar
1 clove garlic
1 tsp. cumin
2 tsp. red pepper

Directions:
Saute chicken breasts, peppers and snow peas in frying pan.
Blend ingredients.
Mix snow peas, chicken, peppers and pasta with sauce.
