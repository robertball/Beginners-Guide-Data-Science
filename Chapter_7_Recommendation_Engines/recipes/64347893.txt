Title:
Passion Potion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups mangos, peeled and cubed
1 cup pineapple, cubed
1-1/2 cups orange juice, chilled
1/2 cup passion fruit nectar, chilled

Directions:
Place the mango cubes and pineapple cubes in freezer and freeze for 1 hour or until firm.  Remove from freezer; let stand 10 minutes.
Combine mango, orange juice and nectar in blender and process until smooth.  With blender on, add pineapple and process until smooth.  Serve immediately.
