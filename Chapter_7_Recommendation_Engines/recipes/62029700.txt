Title:
New England Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb lower sodium bacon diced
4 6oz cans of chopped clams
2 onions chopped
3 tablespoons flour
3 med. potatoes diced
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
3 cups half & half
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Open canned clams and reserve clam juice (add water to clam juice to bring it up to 2 cups)
Cook diced bacon in large saucepan med heat until browned. Add onions cook until tender. Add flour to onion mixture. Mixed together until blended. Add clam juice liquid, stir and cook until it thickens. Stir in potatoes, salt,celery salt,and pepper. Cover and cook for about 15 min. or until the potatoes are tender. Add clams, half and half and butter. Cook until heated through, stirring constantly. Do not bring to boil.

Submitter: 
Patricia Terranova Bergstrom
Description: 
This New England clam chowder is rich and great on a cold winter's night.
