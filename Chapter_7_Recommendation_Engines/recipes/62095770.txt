Title:
Gumdrop Stained Glass Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-3/4 cups flour
1 teaspoon bakng powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon ginger
1-1/2 cups snipped gumdrops
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger and gumdrops.
In another large bowl, beat butter and sugar until fluffy.  Beat in egg.  Add flour mixture alternately with cream, blending after each addition.
Divide dough in half.  Shape each section into a log 2 inches in diameter.  Wrap in wax paper.  Chill overnight.
Cut rolls into 1/4 inch slices with knife dipped in hot water.  Place 1/2 inch apart on sheets.
Bake 12 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
mommymeggy
Description: 
Gumdrops melt to give this cookie its name.  Slice the batter log into pieces and bake!
