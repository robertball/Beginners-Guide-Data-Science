Title:
Slow Cooker Baby Back Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Rack of Baby Back Ribs - 3lbs
1/2 Cup of Water
1 Medium Onion - Sliced
1 Clove of Garlic - Minced
1 Tbsp Chili Powder
1 Tbsp Paprika
1 Tbsp Salt
1 Tbsp Cumin
Barbeque Sauce

Directions:
In a bowl, mix together the chili powder, paprika, salt, and cumin.
Rub the mix of spices onto both sides of the rack of meat.
Cut the rack of ribs in half.
Pour the water into the slow cooker.
Place half the rack of ribs in the slow cooker
Place half of the onion slices and half of the garlic in the slow cooker, on top of the meat.
Place the rest of the rack of ribs on top of the first rack.
Place the rest of the onion slices and garlic on top of the second half of the ribs.
Cook for 8 hours on high (6 hours on low) in the slow cooker.
Preheat your oven for 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
Brush your cooked ribs with barbeque sauce and place on a baking sheet.
Bake for 20 minutes or until barbeque sauce caramelizes.
