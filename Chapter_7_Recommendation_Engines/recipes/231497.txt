Title:
Spring Asparagus Ham and Egg Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972207.jpg
Ingredients:
6 asparagus spears, grilled
1 ounce prosciutto ham, very thinly sliced
2 slices Roman Meal Bread, toasted
2 large eggs, soft poached
4 slices avocado
2 teaspoons shaved Parmesan cheese
Black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Wrap sliced ham around asparagus spears.
Top each slice of toasted bread with 3 ham wrapped asparagus spears, 1 egg and 2 slices avocado.
Sprinkle with cheese and black pepper.
