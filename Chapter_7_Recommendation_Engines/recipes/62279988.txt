Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
1 Tbsp. nutmeg
1 cup of oil
4 eggs
2/3 cup of water
2 cups of canned pumpkin
3 cups of sugar

Directions:
Grease loaf pans. Mix ingredients together. Fill loaf pans. Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour.
