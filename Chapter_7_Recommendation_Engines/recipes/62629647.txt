Title:
Penne with Vodka Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/577284.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box of penne pasta
1 24oz. can of crushed tomatoes
1/4 lb of cooked ham
2 tbs of butter
1 cup of chopped onion
1/2 cup of vodka
3 cloves of garlic (minced)
1/2 a  cup of heavy cream
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbs dried oregano
1tp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Cook penne until al dente. In a pan melt the butter and add chopped ham. Saute until slightly browned and add the onion. Cook until the onion is softened (about 5 minutes). Add vodka and cook until it reduces by half. Add the crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper and the herbs. Simmer for about 30 minutes, then add the cream and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the pasta and mix. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese before serving.
