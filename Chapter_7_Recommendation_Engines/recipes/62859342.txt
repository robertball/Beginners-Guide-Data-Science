Title:
SHRIMP AND ANGEL HAIR   (*/+) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/678914.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package angel hair pasta
1/2 cup butter
2 cloves minced garlic
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined (you can use frozen)
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (more or less to your taste)
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
If you use frozen shrimp, defrost them and clean 'em up...and drain really well.
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Stir in pasta and return pot to boil. Cook until al dente. Drain well.
Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in garlic and shrimp. Cook, stirring constantly, for 3 to 5 minutes.
Stir in wine and pepper. Bring to a boil and cook for 30 seconds while stirring constantly.
Mix shrimp with drained pasta in a serving bowl. Sprinkle with cheese and parsley. Serve immediately.
