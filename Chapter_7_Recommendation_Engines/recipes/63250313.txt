Title:
Jewel's Chicken Giardino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SAUCE:
4 Tbsp butter
1 tsp dry Thyme
1 tsp dry Rosemary
1 tsp sea salt
4 tsp Garlic Pepper
4 Tbsp Corn Starch
1 cup Chicken Broth
1 cup Water
1 cup White Wine
4 Tbsp Milk
4 tsp Lemon Juice
CHICKEN:
2 lbs Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts, cut into 1/2" strips
1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 Small Rosemary Sprigs
1 Clove Garlic
Juice of Half Lemon
VEGETABLES:
1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Bunch Fresh Asparagus, Cut into 1" pieces
1 Zucchini, coin cut
1 Yellow Summer Squash, coin cut
2 Roma Tomatoes, diced
1/2 Red Bell Pepper, cut into strips
1 cup Broccoli Florets
1/2 cup Frozen Peas
1 cup Spinach, cut into 1/2" pieces
1/2 cup Carrots, julienne cut
1 lb pox Farfalle pasta, cooked according to package directions.

Directions:
Combine all of the chicken ingredients in a mixing bowl and mix to blend well. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes.
Melt the butter in a sauce pan over medium heat. Stir in thyme, garlic pepper and roesmary. Whisk to blend and cook for one minute.
Combine chicken broth, water, wine, milk, and lemon juice in a mixing bowl.
Add cornstarch and whisk until dissolved and no lumps.
Add cornstarch mixture into sauce pan. Whisk and bring to a boil. Season with the salt and pepper, then remove from heat.
Heat a saute pan over med-high heat. Add 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil.
Saute chicken strips until no longer pink, about 8 minutes.
Add all the vegetables and saute until cooked through.
Add the cooked, drained pasta and sauce to saute pan.
Stir until thoroughly coated. Serve hot.

Description: 
In Italian, giardino means garden. This Italian-inspired dish is loaded with fresh garden veggies and tossed in a delicious white wine and herb sauce.
It's light and satisfying, and it tastes just like a meal that used to be served at a popular restaurant chain. This feeds a crowd too!
