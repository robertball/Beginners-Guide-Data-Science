Title:
Minestrone Soup Recipe

Ingredients:
MINESTRONE SOUP
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup diced carotts
1/2 cup diced turnip
1 cup chopped onions
1 large can diced tomatoes ( aprox 2 cups )
3/4 cup red kidney beans ( I use canned beans )
1 cup hamburg ( 1/2 pound )
4 cloves garlic minced
1 egg
1/4 cup of bread crumbs
2 teaspoons oregano
2 teaspoons basil
1/2 teaspoon powdered cloves
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon ground pepper
6 to 8 cups beef stock

Directions:
Meatballs : mix hamburg, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1 teaspoon each of oregano and basil, pepper, egg, bread crumbs.
Shape 1 tablespoon of mixture into large olive sized balls and cook in a little oil for about 10 min or until browned, then set aside.
In large pot saut√© veggies and 2 cloves of the minced garlic in the oil.
Add beef stock, tomatoes, sugar, 1 teaspoon each of oregano, basil , 1/2 teaspoon powdered cloves, bay leaf and bring to boil. Add the meatballs, red beans and simmer at least 1 hour.
Serve with grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Mikey's Kitchen
Description: 
This is a tweaked version of a few Minestrone soup recipes I found. There are many variations to this recipe. This one has no pasta and has red kidney beans and is a hit with my kids and adults alike. It can easily serve as a meal with some bread.
