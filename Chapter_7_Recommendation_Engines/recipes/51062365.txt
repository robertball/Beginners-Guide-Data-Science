Title:
Sugar Snap Peas with Orange-Ginger Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar snap peas
1 tsp. grated fresh ginger
1 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. orange marmalade
1 tsp. cider vinegar
1/8 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Cook fresh peas, covered in small amount of boiling salted water 2 to 4 minutes.  Drain well.
Meanwhile, in small pan cook ginger in butter for 1 minute.  Stir in marmalade, vinegar and pepper and cook until marmalade melts.  Pour over hot cooked peas.
