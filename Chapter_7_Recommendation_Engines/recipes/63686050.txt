Title:
Slow cooker cheesy potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025071.jpg
Ingredients:
5-7 medium-sized russet potatoes
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup butter
2 cups shredded cheddar
4 cups milk
1 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
Peel potatoes and slice thinly.
In a 2 quart saucepan, heat butter and flour over medium low heat until combined and foamy.  Add milk, salt and pepper, stirring constantly.
Heat milk mixture until thickened and lightly bubbling, stirring.  Add cheddar and stir until melted into mixture.
Drop potatoes into slow cooker and pour sauce over it.  Mix and cover, cooking on low for 9-10 hours or until potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
magicallydelicious
Description: 
These are easy and delicious and are a great way to keep from heating up the kitchen.  Set them in the morning and enjoy them for dinner.
