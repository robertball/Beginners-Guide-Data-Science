Title:
Sausage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Owens Lite Sausage
1 c chopped celery
1 c chopped onion
1 c uncooked rice
2 cups water
1 1/2 package Lipton Chicken Noodle Soup

Directions:
Saute sausage until brown. Remove from pot.
Saute celery & onion until soft.
Add rice, water & soup mix.
Put in casserole and cover. Cook 1 hour at 350 degress.
