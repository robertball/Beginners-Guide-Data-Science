Title:
Lemony Black-Eyed Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup dried black-eyed peas
2 yellow onions, diced
1 T olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 T fresh ginger, grated
2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
zest of 1/2 lemon
juice of 1/2 lemon

Directions:
Rinse peas, cover with 5 cups of water and cook for 1 1/2 hours, until tender. (If peas have been pre-soaked, cooking time will be reduced to 45 min. Do not add salt until pease are completely softened.
Heat 1 T of olive oil and saute the onion. After several minutes, add garlic, ginger, oregano, salt and pepper, and continue cooking a minute or two.
Drain the cooked peas, reserving the liquid, and add them to the onion.  Add back desired amount of bean liquid.  Finish the peas with the lemon peel and juice. Check the seasoning, especially the levels of salt, pepper and lemon.
