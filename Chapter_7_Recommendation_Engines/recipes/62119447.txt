Title:
British Pub Shepherds Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1 cup frozen vegetables, mixed
1/2 cup onion
1/2 cup celery
2 cups beef gravy
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
4 cups browned hash browns
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown hamburger and add onion and celery and mixed vegetables to pan.
Place in a 3 quart buttered oven proof casserole dish.
In saucepan, heat gravy. Pour over mixture in casserole dish. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
Add hash browns and cheese to the top
Bake at 325 for 1/2 hour until thoroughly heated and the cheese is melted
