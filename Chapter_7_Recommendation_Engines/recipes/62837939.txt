Title:
Mediterranean Zucchini and Chickpea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced zucchini
1/2 cup chopped Vidalia or red onion
3/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1 15-oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed (about 1 1/2 cups)
1 clove garlic, minced
1/3 cup packed fresh basil leaves, chopped
1 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 tsp dried Greek oregano
Pinch red pepper flakes (optional)
1 Tbsp capers, drained and chopped
1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
1/4 cup white balsamic vinegar
1/3 cup olive oil
Salt & pepper to taste
1/2 cup feta cheese crumbles

Directions:
Mix everything together.
Not necessary, but best if chilled at least a couple hours to let flavors blend. (Chill time not included in prep time)

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Chopped zucchini, chickpeas, tomatoes and fresh basil in a light balsamic dressing...or you can use red wine vinegar!  Makes a great side dish or light lunch.
