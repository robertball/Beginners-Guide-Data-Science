Title:
Turkey Italian Sausage and Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Hot Italian Turkey Sausage (or regular bulk Italian sausage)
2 cups water
2-1/2 cups chopped cabbage
2 cans (14-1/2 oz) Italian Stewed tomatoes, undrained and cut up.
3 cans (14-1/2 oz) beef broth
1 can (10-1/2 oz. condensed French onion soup
1 pkg. (9 oz.) refrigerated cheese tortellini
grated Parmesan

Directions:
In a large pot, cooks sausage over medium heat until no longer pink: - drain.  Stir in water, cabbage, tomatoes, broth and soup.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 8 minutes.
Stir in tortellini; coke 10 minutes longer or until pasta is tender.  Serve with Parmesan cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
Ready in minutes.  A heartly and delicious Italian Soup.  Great with a tossed salad and a thick slice of Italian buttered bread.
