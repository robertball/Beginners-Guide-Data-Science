Title:
Deep Fried Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cup whole pecans
1 quart oil
1 tbsp salt

Directions:
Heat oil to medium heat in a deep pan
Drop one handful of peanuts in the hot oil and fry for only 1 minute.
Remove and place in a metal collender or on a paper towel to drain.
Salt to taste.
Repeat steps til desired amount is obtained.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
A southern favorite. Regular pecans transformed into a delicious appetizer that all of my family enjoys.
