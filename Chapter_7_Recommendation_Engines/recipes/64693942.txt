Title:
Vegan, Gluten Free Orange Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5205456.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups rolled oats
1 cup coconut sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 tablespoons orange zest
1 cup fresh squeezed orange juice
1 cup almond milk (or any non dairy milk)

Directions:
Preheat over to 375' F.
Add all ingredients to a blender.
Fill 12 muffin lines to the tip top.
Bake for 22 minutes, check with a toothpick.
Remove muffins from pan and cool for 10 minutes.
Enjoy with some delicious vegan butter!
