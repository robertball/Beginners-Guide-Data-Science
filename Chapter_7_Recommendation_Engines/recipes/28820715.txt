Title:
Tomato Spinach Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. uncooked multigrain penne pasta
1 medium sized yellow onion, chopped
1 (10oz.) pack meatless Italian sausage, sliced
1 TBS. olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
2(14.5oz) cans diced tomatoes
1(6oz.) package fresh baby spinach leaves
2TBS. chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, saute chopped onion and sliced sausage in hot oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat 4 minutes or until tender.  Add minced garlic, and saute 1 minute.  Add tomatoes, and bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer, stirring occasionally, 20 minutes.  Add spinach and basil; cook 5 more minutes.
Place pasta in a large bowl.  Add onion mixture, and toss to combine.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
