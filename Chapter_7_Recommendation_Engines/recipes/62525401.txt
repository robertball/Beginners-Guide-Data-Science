Title:
Chocolate chip brownie bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 c. flour (I use whole wheat)
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 c. sugar
1/4 c. vegan butter
1/3 c. oil
1 1/2 c, silken tofu
1/3 c. unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. walnuts, chopped
1 c. choc. chips
3/4 c. coconut

Directions:
Combine flour and baking soda; set aside.  Combine butter, sugar, and oil in a food processor and process 2 minutes or until light and fluffy.  Add the tofu and process 2 minutes more.  Add the cocoa powder and vanilla and process.  Add the flour mix a little at a time, processing after each addition.  Pour into a bowl and stir in nuts, chocolate chips, and coconut.  Pour into lightly oiled, 9x12 baking pan and bake for 25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean, at 350 degrees.
