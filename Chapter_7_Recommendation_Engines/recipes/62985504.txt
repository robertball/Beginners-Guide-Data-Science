Title:
Snowball Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter - softened
2 cups flour
1 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup sugar
1/8 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. vanilla
Powdered sugar for rolling cookies

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream together butter, sugar, vanilla, salt and baking soda.
Gradually add flour and nuts to mixture.
Roll into balls and bake for 20-30 minutes or until golden brown.
Cool 30 minutes then roll in powdered sugar.
Cool 30 more minutes and roll in powdered sugar again.
