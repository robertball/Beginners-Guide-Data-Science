Title:
PHILLY STICKY BUNS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 CUP MILK
1/2 CUP SUGAR
1 1/4 TSP. SALT
1/2 CUP MARGARINE
1/2 CUP WARM WATER (105-115F)
2 PACKAGES YEAST
3 BEATEN EGGS
5 1/2 CUPS SIFTED FLOUR
3/4 CUP MARGARINE, SOFTENED
2 CUPS BROWN SUGAR, PACKED
1 CUP CHOPPED PECANS
3/4 CUP LIGHT CORN SYRUP
6 TBSP. MARGARINE SOFTENED

Directions:
SCALD MILK, STIR IN SUGAR, SALT AND MARGARINE, COOL TO LUKEWARM. MEASURE WARM WATER INTO LARGE WARM MIXING BOWL. SPRINKE IN YEAST, STIR UNTIL DISSOLVED. ADD LUKEWARM MILK MIXTURE, EGGS AND 1/2 OF THE FLOUR. BEAT 2 MINUTES. AT MEDIUM SPEED. STIR IN ENOUGH ADDITIONAL FLOUR WITH A SPOON TO MAKE A SOFT DOUGH. TURN OUT ONTO A LIGHTLY FLOURED BOARD AND KNEAD UNTIL SMOOTH AND ELASTIC, ABOUT 8 MINUTES. PLACE IN A GREASED BOWL, TURNING ONCE TO GREESE THE TOP. COVER AND LET RISE UN A WARM PLACE UNTIL DOUBLED, ABOUT 45 MINUTES.
WHILE THE DOUGH IS RISING, PREPATE PANS.
SPREAD 4 TBSP. OF THE SOFTENED MARGARINE IN EACH OF 3, EIGHT INCH SQUARE PANS. SPRINKLE 1/2 CUP BROWN SUGAR, 1/3 CUP PECANS AND 1/4 CUP CORN SURUP IN EACH PAN.
PUNCH DOUGH DOWN AND TUNN OUT ONTO A LIGHTLY FLOURED BOARD. DIVIDE INTO 3 PARTS. ROLL EACH PART INTO A 9X5 IN. RECTANGLE. SPREAD EACH WITH 2 TBSP. MARGARINE, SPRINKLE WITH REMAINING BROWN SUGAR. ROLL EACH UP FROM THE LONG SIDE AND CUT INTO 9, 1 in. SLICES. ARRANGE  ROLLS IN EACH PAN, CUT SIDES UP. COVER, LET RISE IN WARM PLACE FREE FROM DRAFTS, UNTIL DOUBLED IN SIZE, 1/2 HOUR. BAKE IN PREHEATED OVEN @ 400 F  FOR 25 MINUTES. COOL IN PANS FOR 10 MINUTES THEN INVERT ONTO PLATES TO FINISH COOKING.
