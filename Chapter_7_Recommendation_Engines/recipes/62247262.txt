Title:
Thai hot and sour soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb shrimp
2 oz thin rice noodles
8 c fish stock or chicken
1 stalk lemon grass
1/4 c thai fish sauce
2 T chili oil
1 T lemon juice
1 T lime juice
1/2 pickled chili
salt and pepper
1/3 c straw mushrooms
1/4 c chopped cilantro

Directions:
Cook shrimp in boiling water for 3 min. Cook noodles in same water. Rinse both to stop cooking.
Combine broth with remaining ingredients, except cilantro. Simmer 10 min. Remove lemongrass.
Mix all and serve.
