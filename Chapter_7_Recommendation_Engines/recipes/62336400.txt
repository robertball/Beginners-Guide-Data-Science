Title:
Mushrooms Berkeley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Fresh Mushrooms, cut in half
1 sweet onion, halfed then sliced
1/2 c butter
2 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
2 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
1/3 c. Brown Sugar
1/4 c Red Table Wine
Salt/Pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine mustard, worcestershire, brown sugar and wine.  Set aside.
Melt the butter in a sauce pan
Saute onion until transparent.
Add the mushrooms and pepper, saute approx 3 minutes
As the mushrooms reduce in size and begin to brown
add the wine mix.
Simmer a minute or so...and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cathy Brown-Bergmann
Description: 
Wonderful sauted mushroom sauce !  I use it over roasted pork loin, but it's so good that the uses are only limited by your imagination.
