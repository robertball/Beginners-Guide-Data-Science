Title:
Sate Ayam-Chicken Kebob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cut chicken in strip or pieces. For 100 sate
2 Sate souce 1 package
3. Salt and Pepper
4  Sweet Soy Souce
5  Chilli Paste
6 Coriender (ketumbar)
7 Ground Garlic and Shallots
8 lemon Juice

Directions:
Marinade the meat overnight.

Submitter: 
JFKR
Description: 
Can make 1 week or more in advance to be freezed.  Use tighs(dark meat) instead of breast (white meat) Costo sell boneless chicken tighs.  1 tigh makes 3 stick of sate, 5 tighs in a bag - 6 bags in a package.  So 1 package makes about 100 sate.
