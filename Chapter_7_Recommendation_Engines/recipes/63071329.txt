Title:
Seafood Melange Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886902.jpg
Ingredients:
4 sole, patted dry
1 cup crabmeat
1 cup cooked shrimp
3/4 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1/2 cup butter
2 egg yolks
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Butter two 2-cup au gratin dishes. Place 1 fillet on bottom of each, then layer with crabmeat, shrimp, cheese and a second fillet; set aside.
Melt butter. In a medium mixing bowl, combine yolks, lemon juice, mustard and salt; mix on high and slowly add butter in a steady stream until sauce is thick and creamy.
Pour sauce over fillets.
Bake at 450ยบ for 10 to 15 minutes; sprinkle with parsley and paprika. Serve.
