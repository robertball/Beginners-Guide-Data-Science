Title:
Chipotle Aioli (Spicy) V2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup mayonnaise
3/4 cup chopped chipotles in adobo sauce (1 7oz Can)
1 tbls. fresh lime juice
1 tbls. Minced Garlic
1 tbls. salt

Directions:
Chop Chipotles into little bits
Mix all ingredients in bowl
Let chill in fridge

Submitter: 
Miller
Description: 
Great on chicken or tuna sandwiches...  or as a dip..Modified from: Chipotle Aioli (Spicy)
