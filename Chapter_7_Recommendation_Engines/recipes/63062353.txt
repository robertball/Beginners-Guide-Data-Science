Title:
Chipotle Chicken Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bonesless, skinless chicken thighs
1 cup plus 1 TBL vegetable oil
8 6" corn tortillas
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
salt and pepper
2 TBL adobo sauce from 1 7-oz can chipotle peppers
2 cups shredded iceberg lettuce
1 medium tomato, chopped
cheddar and sour cream, optional

Directions:
1. Place chicken in a single layer in large pot; cover with water. Bring to a boil, cover; reduce heat and simmer until cooked, about 7 minutes. Drain, transfer to a bowl, let cool slightly and shred.
2. In a large, deep skillet, heat 1 cup oil until it registers 360 degrees on a candy thermometer. Fry 1 totilla until crisp, about 1 minute, turning halfway through. Remove to a plate. Let oil return to 360 degrees and repeat.
3. Warm remaining 1 TBL oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper, season with salt and pepper and cook 7 minutes. Stir in adobo sauce and chicken; saute 2 minutes. Top torillas with chicken mixture, lettuce and tomato, as will as Cheddar and sour cream, if desired
