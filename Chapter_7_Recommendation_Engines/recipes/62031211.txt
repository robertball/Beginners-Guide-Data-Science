Title:
ZUCCHINI SAUTE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
1 tablespoon oil
1 onion sliced thin
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 lbs zucchini, peeled, cut into 1" thick slices
1 green pepper, seeded and chopped
Salt and pepper
1/4 cup raw rice
1/2 cup water
Heat oil in pan. Add onion and saute over medium. Heat 3 minutes. Add tomatoes, zucchini, green pepper, salt and pepper. Cover and simmer 5 minutes. Add rice and water; cover and cook on low 20 minutes.
