Title:
Tender Barbecued Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/690010.jpg
Ingredients:
1 broiler/fryer chicken (3 to 4 pounds), cut up
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
1 medium lemon, thinly sliced
1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce*
3/4 cup regular cola

Directions:
Place chicken in a slow cooker.  Top with onion and lemon slices.  Combine barbecue sauce and cola; pour over all.  Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or until chicken juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline  Blanton
Description: 
'I'm a teacher and work most of the day, so slow-cooked meals are a great help,' relates Jacqueline Blanton from Gaffney, South Carolina. One of her family's favorites is this moist slow-simmered chicken. For an appealing look, choose a darker brown barbecue sauce.
