Title:
Tuscan Bean and Prosciutto Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. olive oil
1/2 c. chopped prosciutto, about 2 oz.
1 c. chopped onion
3/4 c. chopped celery
3/4 c. sliced carrots
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 c. water
2 19 oz. cans of cannellini beans or Great Northerns
undrained
1 15.75 oz. can fat-free, less-sodium chicken broth
2 bay leaves
2 Tbs. minced fresh parsley
2 Tbs. sherry (optional)
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in a large stock pot over medium heat.  Add chopped onion, celery and carrots, stirring until tender.  Add prosciutto and garlic, stirring another 2 minutes or so.
Add water, beans, broth and bay leaves, bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Add parsley, sherry (if desired) and pepper, simmer an additional minute.  Remove from heat, discard bay leaves and serve.

Submitter: 
Bibi
Description: 
The convenience of canned Cannellini beans creates a shortcut to a warm, satisfying soup with Italian flair.
