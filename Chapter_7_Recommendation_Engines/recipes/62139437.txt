Title:
Here's the Beef Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 lb top round steak, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
6 c water
1 6 oz can low sodium V8
1 14 1/2 oz can petite diced tomatoes
1/2 c chopped onion
4 beef bouillion cubes
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp celery salt
1 10 oz pkg frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 c of your favorite soup pasta or barley

Directions:
Heat the oil until shimmery (not smoking) in stock pot.
Brown the beef well, stirring occasionally.
Add the water, tomatoes, V8, onion, bouillion, and spices.
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 1/4 hrs,
covered.
Add vegetables and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add pasta and simmer for 30 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Elisabeth Grenier Shoupp
Description: 
I really don't like hamburger or potatoes in my vegetable beef soup.  This recipe is inexpensive and easy, tastes great and is nutritious.  Plus, it makes your house smell awesome!
