Title:
Grandma's Best Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 cup butter
4 (10 ounce) packages broccoli spears
1 can cream of mushroom soup
12 ounces garlic cheese
3 (4 ounce) cans sliced mushrooms
1 cup herb seasoned stuffing, crushed

Directions:
Saute onions in butter; melt cheese with soup and add mushrooms and sauteed onions. Cook broccoli according to package direction and drain. Alternate layers of broccoli and sauce in lightly buttered 2 quart casserole dish. Crush stuffing lightly with rolling pin and sprinkle over top. Bake at 300 degrees for 25 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Suzanne Cook
Description: 
My grandmother made this recipe at all family gatherings. It is very easy to make.
