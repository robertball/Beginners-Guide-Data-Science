Title:
Provencale Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 French or Italian loaf
4 T. olive oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
1/2 tsp. cilantro
1/2 tsp. thyme
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 14 oz. can tomatoes, drained and chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tomatoes, thinly sliced and seeded
8 black olives, pitted and chopped
2 T. grated cheese

Directions:
Preheat broiler, setting rack 6 inches from the heat.Cut bread crosswise into 4 pieces and halve each piece horizontally. Arrange bread, cut sides up, on a baking sheet and toast under brioler for 1-2 minutes.
Preheat oven to 450. In a 10 inch skillet, heat 2 T. of oil over moderate heat. Add onion and spices, saute, stirring occasionally. Add canned tomatoes and garlic and cook mixture  for about 2 minutes, until thick.
Spread the onion/tomato mixture, dividing it evenly, on the pieces of bread. Top with sliced tomato and garnish with olives and cheese. Drizzle the tops with the remaining 2 T. of oil and bake them for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and the topping is hot.
