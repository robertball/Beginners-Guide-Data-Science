Title:
Cream of Tomato Basil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 large or 2 small red onions, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 28 oz. cans whole tomatoes, with juice (or 4 lbs. fresh tomatoes)
2 tsp sugar
1 6 oz. can tomato paste
1/3 cup packed basil leaves, chopped
3 cups chicken or vegetable stock
1/2 cup very fresh heavy cream
salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot over medium heat.  Saute onion and carrots until tender, about 10 minutes.  Add garlic and cook about 1-2 minutes.  Add all other ingredients except the cream and stir well.   Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer about 30 minutes.  Blend with immersion blender until smooth.  Stir in cream and season with salt to taste.
