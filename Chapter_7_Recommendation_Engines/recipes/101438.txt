Title:
Salmon with Pineapple Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983060.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups Lawry's® Hawaiian Marinade With Tropical Fruit Juices
1 1/4 pounds salmon fillets or steaks
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained and cut into small pieces*
1/3 cup finely chopped red onion
1/2 small red bell pepper, finely chopped
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 medium jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped (optional)

Directions:
In large resealable plastic bag, pour 3/4 cup Lawry's(R) Hawaiian Marinade With Tropical Fruit Juices over salmon; turn to coat. Close bag, and marinate in refrigerator 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, in medium bowl, combine 1/4 cup Marinade with remaining ingredients.  Let stand at room temperature up to 1 hour.
Remove salmon from Marinade, discarding Marinade.  Grill or broil salmon, turning once and brushing frequently with remaining 1/4 cup Marinade, 12 minutes or until fish flakes with a fork. Serve salmon with pineapple salsa.
