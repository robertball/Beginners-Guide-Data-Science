Title:
Grape salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups seedless grapes
8oz cream cheese
8 oz sour cream
1/2 c sugar
1/4 c brown sugar
1/2 c walnut

Directions:
Beat for 30 seconds the cheese, sour cream and white sugar.  Fold in grapes.
Sprinkle brown sugar over top and walnuts. Refrigerate.
