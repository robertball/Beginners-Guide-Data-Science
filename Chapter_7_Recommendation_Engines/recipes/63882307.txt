Title:
Mom's Hush Puppies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups corn meal mix
3/4 cup milk
1 egg, beaten
1 small onion, finely chopped

Directions:
Heat deep-fryer oil to 375F(190C)
Combine mix well and drop by rounded spoonful into hot oil, only a few at a time til golden brown.
Drain on absorbent paper towel.

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
These are the first hush puppies I was introduced to as a child and no other hush puppy has come close to mom's!
