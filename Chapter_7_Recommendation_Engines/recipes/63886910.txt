Title:
Ham & Cheese Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs beaten
3/4 cup milk
Salt
Pepper
1cup chopped cooked ham
1cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 9" unbanked pie shell

Directions:
Beat eggs with salt & pepper in bowl
Add ham & cheese
Pour into pie shell
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
Let stand for several minutes before serving
