Title:
Dairy-Free Pumpkin Spice Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup canned pumpkin (or 4 oz. jar of baby food squash)
1/2 frozen banana
3/4 cup almond milk
1/2 scoop (about 2 Tbsp) vanilla protein powder
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
Add water or more almond milk, if needed, until the mixture reaches the desired consistency.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Recipe from the Gastroparesis-Friendly Kithchen. Pumpkin has a moderate amount of fiber but may be well-tolerated in moderation, especially when pureed and thinned.
