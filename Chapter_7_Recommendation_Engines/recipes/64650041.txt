Title:
Almond Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups ground almonds
3 cups water
1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Directions:
Combine and blend for several minutes in a blender. Drain through a colander lined with cheese cloth.
