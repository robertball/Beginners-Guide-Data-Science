Title:
Banana Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter, softened
4 bananas, pureed
1/4 cup half-and-half
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
1/2 cup sultana raisins

Directions:
Preheat 350 degrees. Grease loaf pan. Combine sugars and mix together. Add butter and mix until well combined. Incorporate bananas, half-and-half and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, beating well between each one. Sift flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt into second bowl. Mix into egg mixture using a beater. Fold in walnuts and raisins. Pour into loaf pan and bake 60 minutes.
