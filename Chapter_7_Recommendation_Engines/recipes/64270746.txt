Title:
Citrus Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed and undiluted
1 c. water
3/4 c fresh lime juice
1/2 c. minced fresh basil
1 T minced fresh tarragon
3 T Olive oil
1 T Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl; stir well with a wire whisk.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description: 
Citrus dressing to use on lettuce salad.
