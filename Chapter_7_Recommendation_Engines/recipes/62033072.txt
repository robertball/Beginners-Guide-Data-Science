Title:
Orange Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz. Cottage Cheese
16 oz. Cool Whip
6 oz. Box Orange Jello (use dry)
2 Cans Mandarin Oranges

Directions:
Mix together cottage cheese and cool whip.  Sprinkle in jello and stir until mixed well.  Add mandarin oranges.  Put in refrigerator for about 2 hours or until chilled.  You can also add green grapes if desired.

Submitter: 
CAROL46
Description: 
This jello salad is perfect with any meal.  We have it with barbecues and at Thanksgiving.  It is a big hit with the kids.
