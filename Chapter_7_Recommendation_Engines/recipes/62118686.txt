Title:
Pork Chops and Orange Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Pork Chops
Bell Pepper
Onion
1 cup rice
1 1/3 cup water
1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Salt and pepper the pork chops, and brown them in a large skillet. Remove pork chops.
2. Mix water, rice, and tomatoes. Put in skillet.
3. Place pork chops on top of rice mixture.
4. Cut onion and bell pepper into rings and place on top of pork chops.
5. Bring to a boil, then simmer on a low heat for approx. 30 minutes, or until rice is done.

Lauren Bass Swenson
Description: 
This is a quick, easy, and tasty one-dish meal.
