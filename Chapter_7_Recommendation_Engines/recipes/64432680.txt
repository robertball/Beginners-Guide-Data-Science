Title:
Braised Beef Cheeks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs trimmed beef cheeks
1 cup wine
1/3 cup porcini mushrooms, dried
1 large onion, small dice
4 clove garlic diced
1 carrot small dice
4 cups beef stock
2 bouillon cubes
2 bay leaves
1 tsp thyme or 3 stems of thyme
olive oil

Directions:
preheat oven to 275.  Salt and pepper cheeks.   In a 6 quart dutch oven, Brown cheeks in 2 tbl olive oil until nicely browned.  Do in separate batches if needed to not crowd.  When finished add onions and carrots and cook until tender about 20 min.  Add garlic and cook for 2 more min.  Add 1 cup wine and 4 cup beef broth, boil for another 5 min.  Add remaining ingredients cover with parchment paper and put a tight lid on and cook for 5-6 hours.
