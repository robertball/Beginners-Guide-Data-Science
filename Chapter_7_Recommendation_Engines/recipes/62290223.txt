Title:
Chinese Chicken Fried Rice II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg
3 tablespoon water
3 tablespoon butter
3 tablespoon vegetable oil
3 onion, chopped
6 cups cooked white rice, cold
6 tablespoons soy sauce
3 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
3 cloves garlic minced
1/2 tablespoon of ginger
1 tablespoon of sesame oil

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat egg with water. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium low heat. Add egg and leave flat for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from skillet and cut into shreds.
Heat oil in same skillet; add onion and saute until soft. Then add rice,garlic, ginger, sesame oil, soy sauce, pepper and chicken. Stir fry together for about 5 minutes, then stir in egg. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Jack Pleugers
Description: 
Cooked egg is shredded and mixed with a stir fry of chicken, rice and onion in soy sauce in this fundamental Chinese dish.Modified from: Chinese Chicken Fried Rice II
