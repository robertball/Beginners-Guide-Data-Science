Title:
Crispy Crunchy Sweet Apple Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332873.jpg
Ingredients:
3 - 4 Medium size apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
1/4 Cup packed brown sugar
3/4 Cup all purpose flour
3/4 Cup white sugar
1/4 Tsp salt
1/4 - 1/2 Tsp cinnamon, to taste
1 Egg
1/3 Cup Melted butter or margarine

Directions:
Peel, core and slice apples and place in a greased casserole or baking dish.
Mix in hrown sugar until apples are thorougly coated.
In a separte bowl, mix flour, sugar, salt, and cinnamon until well blended, set aside.
Beat egg and mix with flour mixture, spread over top of fruit in baking dish.
Pour melted butter or margarine evenly over top of flour mixture,
Bake at 375* for 45 minutes until lightly browned.
Serve warm with ice cream, cream, milk or just by itself.

Description: 
Crispy, crunchy topped tender and juicy apple dish that just gives you that all over warm comfy feeling.
