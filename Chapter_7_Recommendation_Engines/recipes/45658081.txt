Title:
Red Curry Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl canola oil
1.5 Tbl red curry paste
1 Tbl soy sauce
1 lb ground beef
1/4 cup coconut milk
6 scallions, thinly sliced
5 oz bag baby spinach
1/2 lime, zest and juice
1/2 cup shredded fresh basil
1/2 cup crushed unsalted peanuts

Directions:
In a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat, combine the oil, curry paste, soy sauce and sugar.
Cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
Add the ground beef and saute until cooked through, about 8 minutes.
Stir in the coconut milk.  Return to a simmer.
Mix in the scallions and spinach and continue cookiing until the greens are just wilted, about 2 to 3 minutes.
Mix in the lime juice and zest and the basil.
Serve over rice, garnished with peanuts.
