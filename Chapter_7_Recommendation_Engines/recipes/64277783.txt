Title:
Touchdown Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. can refried beans
1 8 ounce cream cheese, softened
1 package taco seasoning mix
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 small tomato, cored and chopped
1/4 cup onions, chopped
1/4 cup black olives, chopped
2 tablespoons cilantro, snipped
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
sour cream
pita chips, baked on Stone

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.  Spread refried beans into bottom of Deep Dish Baker with Super Scraper.  Combine cream cheese and taco seasoning in Batter Bowl using Bamboo Spoon.  Press garlic with Garlic Press; add to cream cheese mixture.  Core tomato with Tomato Corer, cut tomato with 5-inch Self-Sharpening Utility Knife.  Chop onion and olives with Food Chopper.  Snip cilantro using Kitchen Cutters.  Sprinkle tomato, olives, onion, and cilantro over cream cheese mixture.  Top with Cheddar cheese.  Bake 25-30 minutes or until hot.  Serve with sour cream and pita chips.
NOTE:  May also be heated in microwave.  Heat on HIGH 3 minutes, turn.  Heat on HIGH 2-3 minutes more or until mixture is bubbly around edges.
