Title:
Easy Chocolate Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons cocoa
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 tablespoons hot water
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cups icing sugar

Directions:
cream butter
add cocoa, sugar, water and vanilla and mix
add icing sugar and stir until creamy
spread on cooled cake/brownies

Submitter: 
Brian Slowleigh
Description: 
simple and smooth icing for cakes, brownies or whatever you need icing for...
