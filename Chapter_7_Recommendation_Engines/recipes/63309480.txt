Title:
Easy Hawaiian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/874730.jpg
Ingredients:
6 dark meat chicken pieces, skin removed
2 tablespoons canola oil
1 medium onion, sliced into 1/4 inch rings
1 cup chicken broth
1 8 oz. can pineapple chunks in natural juice
2 tablespoons lemon juice
3 tablespoons reduced sodium soy sauce
1 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1 small bell pepper sliced into 1/4 inch rings

Directions:
In skillet, heat canola oil on medium heat.  Saute onion rings in oil for 3 minutes.
Carefully add chicken broth, juice from pineapple (reserve chunks), and lemon juice.  Bring to a boil.
Mix soy sauce with cornstarch and dry mustard.  Add to hot liquid stirring 2-3 minutes or until clear and thickened.
Add chicken pieces.  Cover and simmer 25-30 minutes or until done, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
Add bell pepper rings and pineapple chunks.  Simmer 5 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
GINGERPET
Description: 
The slight tanginess of this one skillet dish is balanced perfectly by the sweetness of pineapple.  Serve over brown or white rice.
