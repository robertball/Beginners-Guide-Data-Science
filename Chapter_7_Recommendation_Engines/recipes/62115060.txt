Title:
Pat's Easy Shrimp Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1 lb box of elbow macaroni
1 medium onion finely chopped
2 stalks of celery finely chopped
1 bag medium cooked,frozen and peeled shrimp
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tbsp Old Bay Seasoning

Directions:
Defrost the shrimp and keep refrigerated until ready to use.
Boil the macaroni until cooked approx 8-10 mins making sure not to overcook. Do not rinse the macaroni. Let sit till cooled.
Chop the onion and celery.
Mix the macaroni,shrimp,onion,salt,pepper and Old Bay in a large bowl.
Add mayonnaise.
Refrigerate at least 3 hours.

Submitter: 
NainInCandia
Description: 
My mother has been making this for years and every man that has ever tried it can not get enough of it. Few ingrediants but full of flavor. The simplicity of the dish really brings out the shrimp flavor. Best the longer it is refrigerated.
