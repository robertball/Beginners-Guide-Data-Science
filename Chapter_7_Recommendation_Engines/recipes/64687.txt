Title:
BOCA® Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cholesterol-free egg product
1/4 cup fat free milk
3 frozen BOCA Meatless Breakfast Patties, coarsely chopped
2 green onions, sliced
1/2 cup shredded non-fat cheddar cheese
4 (8 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
Salsa (optional)

Directions:
BEAT egg product and milk in medium bowl with wire whisk until well blended.  Add patty pieces and onion; mix well. Pour into nonstick or lightly oiled skillet.
COOK on medium heat until set, stirring frequently. Sprinkle with cheese; cover.  Cook until cheese is melted.
SPOON a scant 1/2 cup egg product mixture onto each tortilla; fold in ends and roll up burrito style. Serve with salsa, if desired.

An easy grab 'n go breakfast burrito you'll make again and again.
