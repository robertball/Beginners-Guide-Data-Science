Title:
Warm Italiano Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982850.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (250 g) package Philadelphia Light Brick Cream Cheese Spread, softened
1/4 cup pesto
1 plum tomato, chopped
1/4 cup KRAFT 4 Cheese Italiano Shredded Cheese
Triscuit Original Crackers

Directions:
Heat barbecue to medium heat.
Place cream cheese spread on 8-inch square sheet of heavy-duty foil; top with pesto, tomatoes and shredded cheese. Place on barbecue grate.
Grill 8 to 10 min. or until shredded cheese is melted and cream cheese is softened, but still holding its shape.
Serve with crackers.
