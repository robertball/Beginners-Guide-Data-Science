Title:
Artichoke Spinach Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
12 uncooked no boil lasagna noodles, Barilla works well
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary or basil
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed, drained and squeezed dry or use fresh, chopped.
1 (28 ounce) jar tomato pasta sauce
4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1 (15 oz ricotta or cottage cheese)
1 egg
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 (4 ounce) package herb and garlic feta, crumbled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).   Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Spray a large skillet with cooking spray and heat on medium-high.  Saute onion and garlic for 3 minutes, or until onion is tender-crisp.  Stir in broth and rosemary or basil; bring to a boil.  Stir in artichoke hearts and spinach; reduce heat, cover and simmer 5 minutes.  Stir in pasta sauce.
Combine egg, cottage cheese, parmesan, and 1 cup mozzarella.
Spread 1/4 of the artichoke mixture in the bottom of the prepared baking dish; top with 4 noodles.
Spread 1/2 of cottage cheese mixture on noodles, then 1/4 of the sauce and sprinkle 1 cup mozzarella cheese over noodles.  Repeat layer again. On the last layer of noodles end with artichoke mixture and mozzarella cheese.  Sprinkle crumbled feta on top.
Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.  Uncover, and bake 15 minutes more, or until hot and bubbly.  Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.

Daryl
Description: 
This is a fabulous lasagna made with an artichoke and spinach mixture which has been cooked with vegetable broth, onions and garlic.  The mixture is layered with lasagna noodles, pasta sauce, mozzarella cheese, and topped with crumbled feta.Modified from: Artichoke Spinach Lasagna
