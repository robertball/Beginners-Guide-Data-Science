Title:
Duo de salsas de queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006370.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. Herdez Tomatillo sauce
1/2 c. Herdez Guajillo sauce
8 ounces Queso fresco cheese, divide in 2 portions
1/2 t garlic powder. divided in 2 portions
2 T. chopped cilantro, divided in 2 portions
2 T olive oil, divided in 2 portions
salt and fresh cracked pepper to taste

Directions:
In food processor, mix Herdez tomatillo sauce with 4 ounces quesco fresco, 1/4 t garlic powder, 1 T chopped cilantro, 1 T olive oil and salt and pepper. Remove and chill until serving time
In food processor, mix Herdez guajillo sauce with 4 ounces quesco fresco, 1/4 t garlic powder, 1 T chopped cilantro, 1 T olive oil and salt and pepper. Remove and chill until serving time.

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Good as a dip for chips, as a sandwich spread or on top of grilled chicken
