Title:
Super Simple Nacho Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992079.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Fiesta Nacho Cheese Soup
1/2 cup milk
4 cups corkscrew-shaped pasta, cooked and drained

Directions:
Stir the soup, milk and pasta in a 3-quart saucepan. Heat through over medium heat.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Twenty minutes and three ingredients make a flavorful pasta dish with a kicked up cheese sauce.
