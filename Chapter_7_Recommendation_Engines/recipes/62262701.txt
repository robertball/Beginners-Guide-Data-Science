Title:
Quick Collard Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch collard greens washed and chopped
1 small sweet potato peeled and diced
3/4 medium red onion
4 cloves garlic sliced
1/2 inch ginger chopped
salt to taste
olive oil
2 T water

Directions:
Heat oil in a non-stick skillet.
Add onion and saute for two minutes.
Add garlic, sweet potato and stir for one minute.
Add to this collards, ginger and salt. Cook without covering for 3 minutes.
Add water and cover.  Cook for 5 minutes.

A vegetarian collard green recipe that's a great side.
