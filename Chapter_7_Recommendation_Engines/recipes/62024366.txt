Title:
Chicken with Mushrooms and  Prosciutto and Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken thighs (skin on or off)
2 tbs butter or margarine
4-6 slices of thinly cut prosciutto (or slices of bacon)
salt and pepper to taste
2 tbs of minced garlic
1 Cup of sliced mushrooms
1 cup of sour cream
1/4 C Dry White Wine (optional)

Directions:
Melt butter in pan or in microwave and pour into oven safe casserole dish. Season chicken with salt, pepper and 1 tbs of the minced garlic.  Wrap chicken in prosciutto (or bacon) and set on top of melted butter.  Sprinkle the remaining garlic and all of the mushrooms on top of chicken and in the butter.
Bake chicken, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until juices run clear.
For Sauce:
Remove chicken and keep warm.
Pour butter and drippings into a medium preheated pan.  Whisk in sour cream and wine let heat for about 5-7 minutes until just hot. Pour over chicken and serve!

Submitter: 
TIFFUHNY
Description: 
This chicken is quick, simple, delicious and has an elegant presentation!  If you don't have prosciutto handy you can use bacon. 
Make it really special and throw in a variety of exotic mushrooms, shiitake, portabello, oyster, etc.
It can be prepared ahead of time and thrown in the oven when ready.
Serve with garlic mashed potatoes, buttered noodles or basmati rice.
