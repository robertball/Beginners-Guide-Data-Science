Title:
Cobbled Blueberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh blueberries
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon self-rising flour, plus 1/2 cup
1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
2 tablespoons butter, plus 1 cup
2/3 cup cornmeal
Pinch salt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
Stir together in bowl blueberries, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1 teaspoon self-rising flour and balsamic vinegar. Set aside to macerate for 20 minutes, stirring occasionaly
Grease six 8oz ramekins using 2 tablespoons butter; place ramekins on baking sheet and divide berries equally in each
Combine remaining 1/2 cup sugar, remaining 1/2 cup flour, the cornmeal and pinch of salt.
Cut in 1 cup butter with a fork to make pea-like texture; then sprinkle on top of the berries in ramekins
Bake for 25 -30 minutes until berries are bubbley and topping is a golden color

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
A brilliantly simple and elegant dessert dish. Sweet and juicy with a crunchy textured top. Served alone or with a little vanilla ice cream, you will love it from the first bite!
