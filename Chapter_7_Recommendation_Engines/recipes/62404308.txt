Title:
Cherry and Banana Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe bananas
1 (16oz) package frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (8oz) package cream cheese
1 (21oz) can cherry pie filling
1 cup white sugar
2 (9 inch)single pie crusts
sugar and cinnamon, to taste
1/2 cup chopped pecans, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Press pecans into unbaked pie shells.
Bake crusts until light brown.
Slice bananas into cooled crusts.
Cream sugar and cream cheese together.
Add non-dairy whipped topping to cream cheese mixture.
Pour mixture over bananas in both pie pans.
Top pies with cherries.
Chill at least 4 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Christal Kuchera
Description: 
Simple and fun!  Less filling than a full cheese cake, but still on the rich side! Perfect balance!
