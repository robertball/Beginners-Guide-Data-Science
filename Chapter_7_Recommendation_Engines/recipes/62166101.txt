Title:
old fashioned bbq's ( sloppy joe's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger fried and chopped
1/2 med onion fried w/ hamburger
1 can tomato soup
1 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons mustard
1/2 to 3/4 cup sugar (depending on how tangy/sweet you desire)
1/2 teaspoon white vinegar
heat and serve on buns

Directions:
fry hamburger on med heat
add onions
when hamburger is browned, strain
add tomato soup, ketchup, mustard, sugar and vinegar
mix all together
heat and serve with favorite sides:)

Submitter: 
mom4all4
Description: 
My family has always used this very easy recipe for birthday party's and get togethers, the kids love it, and so do I...from prep to done is about 20 min!!!
