Title:
Heirloom Honey Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl unsalted marg---for greasing pans
2 1/3 cups sifted unbleached all-purpose flour, plus 2 Tbl for dusting pans
2 Large eggs, room temp
scant 2/3 cup sugar
1 cup strong brewed tea (make w/3 tea bags), cool
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 cup honey
1/2 med-ripe banana, thoroughly mashed
grated zest of 1 orange
5/8 tsp cinammon
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
[

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Grease 2 5x9 loaf pans with marg, dust w/flour. Tap out excess flour
Beat eggs at medium speed, gradually adding sugar until mixture is pale & bubbles appear, about 5 min. Lower speed & beat in tea, oil, honey, banana, orange zest & cinnamon. Combine thoroughly
With rubber spatula, gradually fold in flour, baking powder & baking soda, combining well after each addition. No traces of flour should be visible
Pour batter evenly into 2 pans. bake on middle self for 15min
Increase oven to 350 & bake another 30min until tests done.
Cool on wire rack. Loosen cakes . Invert each pan onto serving plate.
