Title:
Asparagus Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972075.jpg
Ingredients:
One stalk Asparagus, rinsed chopped as you like
4 button mushrooms, diced
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper diced
2 whole eggs
6 egg whites
1/3 cup skim milk
1 cup cheddar shreds
Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat to 375 degrees
Clean and cut all veggies
Saute in olive oil till onions are clear
In a bowl mix all eggs and milk.
Spray an 8x8 dish with cooking spray
Pour in veggies and distribute evenly on the bottom of dish
Pour mixed eggs/milk
Sprinkle cheddar on top
Bake for 20 mins. If the middle is still not done, cook for 5 more mins. It's done when a fork inserted comes out clean. DON'T OVERCOOK.
