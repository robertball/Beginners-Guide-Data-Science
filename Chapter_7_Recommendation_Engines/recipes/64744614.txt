Title:
Saucy Spaghetti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef cooked
1/2 cup onion chopped and sautéed
1/2 cup green pepper chopped and sautéed (optional)
1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese grated
1 -10 ounce can tomato soup
1-10 ounce can cream of mushroom soup
1 minced garlic clove
6 cups cooked spaghetti

Directions:
mix all ingredients together with 3/4 cup grated cheddar, put in a 3 quart casserole dish and top with remaining 3/4 cup grated cheddar.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick and easy way to use leftover cooked spaghetti
