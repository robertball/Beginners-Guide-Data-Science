Title:
Vegetarian Chili Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980581.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7.25 ounce) package KRAFT Macaroni & Cheese Dinner
1 cup kidney beans, rinsed, drained
1 cup chopped canned tomatoes
1 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
Prepare Dinner as directed on package. Stir in remaining ingredients; heat thoroughly, stirring occasionally.
