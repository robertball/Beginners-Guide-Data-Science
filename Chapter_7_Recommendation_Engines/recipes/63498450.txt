Title:
Mushroom, Bacon and Red Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
30g/1oz Butter
¼ Onion - finely chopped
2 rashers Streaky Bacon - chopped
100g/3½oz Mushrooms - sliced
100ml/3½fl oz Red Wine
60ml/2fl oz Beef Stock
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Heat the olive oil and butter in a pan, add the onion and bacon and, stirring occasionally, cook gently for some 4 to 5 minutes, or until the onion is softened and the bacon is lightly browned;
Add the mushrooms and, stirring occasionally, cook for a further 1 to 2 minutes or so;
Add the red wine and stock, increase the heat and, still stirring occasionally, simmer until the liquid has reduced by half or to desired consistency;
Season, to taste, with salt and pepper;
Serve.
