Title:
Bleu Moon Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Potatoes, peeled and sliced into rounds
16 oz. sour cream
8 oz. crumbled bleu cheese
1/4 c. mayo
1 large onion sliced into rings

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender. Drain. Layer into 2 quart dish. Top with onion rings. Mix together sour cream, mayo and bleu cheese. Pour over potatoes and onions. Cover and bake 25 minutes.
