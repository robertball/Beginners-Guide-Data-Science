Title:
World's Best Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups white sugar
1 cup evaporated milk
1 t. vanilla
1/4 t. peanut butter flavoring
1/2 cup butter
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
12 ounces peanut butter
2/3 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish, set aside.
Cream the marshmallow cream, peanut butter, and flour.
In a saucepan, combine sugar, milk, butter, vanilla and peanut butter flavoring.
Bring to a rolling boil, and cook 5 minutes. Remove from the heat.
Stir in the marshmallow creme,peanut butter, and flour mixture.
Spread into the prepared pan, and let cool.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
This simple recipe combines milk, butter, marshmallow cream and peanut butter, for a rich and creamy fudge you'll love.Modified from: World's Best Peanut Butter Fudge
