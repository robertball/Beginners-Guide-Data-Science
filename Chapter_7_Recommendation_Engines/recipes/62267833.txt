Title:
Butterfly Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
100g butter
100g sugar
100g self raising flour
2 eggs
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
1. Cream the butter and sugar together until combined.
2. Add in baking powder, vanilla extract and eggs.
3. Gradually sieve flour into recipe until fully mixed.
4. Pace 2 tablespoons of mixture into each paper cupcake holder.
5. Bake at 325F for 15 minutes, until lightly golden brown on top.
6. whilst cooling combine powdered sugar and butter for the butter cream frosting.
