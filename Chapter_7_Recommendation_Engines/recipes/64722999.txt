Title:
Instant Pot Butternut Squash Breakfast Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5999502.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cut into large chunks
2 tablespoons maple syrup
1 tablespoon cinnamon
½ teaspoon ginger
¼ teaspoon nutmeg
¼ teaspoon nutmeg
½ teaspoon dried rosemary
Pinch sea salt
For topping:
Fresh or jarred cranberry relish
¼ cup chopped pistachios
zest of one orange

Directions:
Place ingredients into stainless insert of Instant Pot in order listed.
Cover and make sure valve is set to “seal;” set to manual, high pressure, for 6 minutes.
Quick release and use an immersion blender or hand mixer to blend ingredients.
Serve as a side or top with cranberry relish, chopped pistachios and orange zest for a delicious Instant Pot Paleo breakfast bowl.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
Creamy, rich butternut squash makes a delicious breakfast.  Dairy free and grain free!
