Title:
Chinese Ramen Noodle  Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Italian wine vinegar ( red or white)
1 tablespoon Balsamic Vinegar
2 Tablespoons Apple Cider Vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cloves fresh garlic minced
2 teaspoons herb de province ( optional)
1 (3 ounce) package chicken flavored ramen noodles, crushed, seasoning packet reserved
1/2 cup olive oil
1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix bag of fresh cabbage,carrots
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 red pepper,washed and cut small pieces
2 stalks celery,washed and sliced thin
4 ounces slivered almonds, toasted
1/4 cup sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
oven at 350 spray cookie sheet with spray oil and roast the sesame and almonds for 3-5 min till turn light brown
in small pan cook sugar and vinegar's till sugar dissolved....remove and coll down a little
add to pan olive oil and spices...garlic... and seasoning packet and stir
in large bowl add cabbage,onions,celery,red pepper
add pkg ramen noodles- breaking up the noodles
toss with vinegar and sugar mix- add almond slivers and seeds and toss...chill till cold and till serve

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Ramen noodles and toasted almonds,sesame seeds give great flavor
