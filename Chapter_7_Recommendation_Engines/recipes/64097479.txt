Title:
Coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 C all purpose flour
2/3 C beer
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
2 C flaked unsweetened coconut
1 Lb. (20-26) raw shrimp, peeled and deveined with tails left on
3 C canola oil for frying

Directions:
In a bowl, combine egg, 1/2 C flour, beer, and baking powder. place 1/4 C flour in one bowl and the coconut in another. Hold shrimp by tail, and dredge in flour shaking off excess flour. Dip in egg/beer batter; allow excess to drip off. Roll shrimp in coconut and place on a baking sheet lined with wax paper. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile, heat oil to 350 degrees in a deep frier. Fry shrimp in batches; cook, turning once for 2 to 3 minutes or until golden brown.Using tongs, remove shrimp to paper towels to drain. Serve warm with dipping sauce
