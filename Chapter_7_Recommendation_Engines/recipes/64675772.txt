Title:
Heath Bar Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4  teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1/4 cup chopped nuts
6 small or 3 large heath bars, broken
1/4 cup butter melted

Directions:
Preheat oven at 325
Combine sugar and cinnamon, set aside
Combine other ingredients, except nuts, candy and butte
Pour 1/2 batter into bundt pan
Sprinkle 2 T sugar mixture over batter.
Pour remaining batter and sprinkle rest of sugar, broken heath bars, and nuts
Drizzle with 1/4 cup of melted butter
Bake 45 minutes
Sprinkle powdered sugar over cooled cake
