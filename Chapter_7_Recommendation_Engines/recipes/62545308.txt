Title:
Mississippi Marinated Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup vinegar
3/4 cup sugar
1 TBS water
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 bag (16 oz) frozen peas
1 can (12 oz) shoe peg corn, drained
1 jar (2 oz) pimentos, drained
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients and boil for 1 minute.
Remove from heat and let cool.
Combine everything else and pour cooled liquid over vegetable mixture and mix well.
Chill for at least an hour.

Submitter: 
Love2Cook
Description: 
Contrasting flavors and textures make this a delightful salad, always popular with any crowd. Quick, easy, and can be made a day or so ahead of time.
