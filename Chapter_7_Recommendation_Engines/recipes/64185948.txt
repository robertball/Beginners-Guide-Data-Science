Title:
Christa's Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages frozen broccoli
2 cups of cooked, cubed chicken
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon lemon juice
½ teaspoon curry powder
½ cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 cup bread crumbs
2 tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
Cook broccoli until slightly tender. Arrange in a large baking dish. Sprinkle chicken on top. Mix all ingredients except cheese, bread crumbs and melted butter. Pour over chicken evenly. Now sprinkle cheese. Then breadcrumbs. And top with melted butter.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes. Serve over rice.
