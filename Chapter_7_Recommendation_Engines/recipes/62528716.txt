Title:
Winter Warm-Up Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/516686.jpg
Ingredients:
1 litre of chicken stock
2 cups of water
4 large white potatoes peeled and cubed
2 carrots, peeled and diced small
1 small onion diced
1 stalk of celery diced small
2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
1 bayleaf
1 1/2 cups of shredded cooked turkey or chicken
1 12oz can of corn niblets
1 10oz can of cream corn
**  6 slices of bacon, fried and cut to bacon bits
salt & pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups of table cream
3 tbsps of cold water combined with 1 tbsp cornstarch
2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Directions:
In a large soup pot combine stock, water, potatoes, carrot, onion, celery, parsley, bayleaf, chicken or turkey, both cans of corn, bacon (if desired) and salt & pepper.  Bring to a boil and reduce to simmer for 1 1/2 hours.  Remove bayleaf and slowly stir in cornstarch mixture, bring back to low boil for a remaining 10 minutes.  Sprinkle with parsley garnish and bacon garnish (if desired)
*** Note if you want your bacon bits to be crispy, reserve for garnish, otherwise put in the soup with the vegetables

Submitter: 
Carrie Elizabeth
Description: 
This beautiful thick chowder full of vegetables and flavour will warm up on the coldest of winter days.  Serve it with some crusty bread. Great way to use leftover turkey or chicken
