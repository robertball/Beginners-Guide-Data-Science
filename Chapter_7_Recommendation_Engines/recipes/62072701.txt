Title:
Grilled Salmon with Creamy Horseradish Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 salmon fillets (6 ounces each)
1-2 tbsp. olive oil
salt (to taste)
pepper (to taste)
---For the Sauce---
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 tbsp. prepared horseradish
1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. dijon mustard
1/4 tsp. onion powder

Directions:
Rub salmon with oil and season to taste with salt and pepper.
Place ingredients for sauce in a food processor or blender and puree until smooth.
Grill salmon over medium-hot coals for about 4 minutes per side, or until done to your liking (check with fork).
Serve sauce spooned over salmon.

Submitter: 
Sue Lau
Description: 
The sauce has a mild horseradish flavor that marries perfectly with the grilled salmon.
