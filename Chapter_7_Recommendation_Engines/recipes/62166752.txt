Title:
Drommar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/752704.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cubes of butter
3 cups of all purpose flour
3/4 cup of white sugar
1 t. ammonium carbonate
1t. vanilla
blanched whole almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Brown butter over medium heat. I mean really get it brown. The butter will get all foamy: don't be alarmed. Keep stirring occasionally.. don't want it to burn
Remove from heat.
Stir in flour, sugar, ammonium carbonate and vanilla and mix until well combined.
Let mixture cool until easy to handle, but don't refrigerate.
Roll cookies into balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Press almonds into the tops of the cookies. slightly flattening the cookie
Bake 12-15 minutes. Let these cool on the sheet for a few minutes then transfer to wire racks to cool completely

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Delicious and authentic browned butter Swedish cookies. You can get the ammonium carbonate at a good German or Scandanavian deli or even online.
