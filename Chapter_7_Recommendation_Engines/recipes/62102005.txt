Title:
Cheesy beef and rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 or 2 (depending on how much rice/filler you want) or boxes (6 to 8 oz size) of spanish rice mix, or other depending on the ammount of spice you like
1 can of diced tomatoes, or diced tomatoes and chilis
1 bag of shredded cheese -mexican blend or other
1-1 1/2 pounds of ground beef

Directions:
cook rice according to package directions
cook beef
when rice is done cooking add beef, cheese, and can of tomatoes, add any additional spices, mix together and serve

Submitter:

Description:

very versatile depending on your taste for or not for spice
