Title:
Maple Bacon Snap Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups  stringless Snap Peas
2 T Maple Syrup
2 T chopped Bacon
1/4 t Chili flakes
1 T chopped Shallot
1 T  oil
1/2 T lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  in a large frying pan or wok cook chopped bacon for 2-5 minute over high heat
add pepper flakes  cook 1 minute
add shallots cook 1 minute
add 1 t oil heat 1 minute
add sweet peas, toss well and spread around the surface of the pan and cook at high temperature
cook sweet peas undisturbed until lightly blackened ( 3-5 minutes depending on temp and pan)
Stir and redistribute evenly on the pan surface
add maple syrup  and  let sear for 2 more minutes
add lemon juice  and taste for salt an pepper
plate and serve

Katie
Description: 
Sweet, savoury and super quick
take out the bacon for an even quicker vegetarian side!
