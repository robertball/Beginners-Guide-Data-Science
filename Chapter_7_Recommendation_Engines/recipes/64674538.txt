Title:
Honey Mustard Apple BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4610993.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup spicy brown mustard
1/4 cup cinnamon apple sauce
1/4 cup olive oil
3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons ground mustard
1 tablespoons onion powder
2 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Mix ingredients together until well blended; refrigerate. Makes 1 3/4 cups of sauce.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This is a simple recipe to make for a BBQ sauce. Simply apply sauce to poultry or pork toward the last 15 to 20 minutes of cook time. Use it as a dip or a glaze for your next ham dinner.
