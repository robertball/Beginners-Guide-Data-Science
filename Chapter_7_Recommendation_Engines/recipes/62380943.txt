Title:
Katie's Creamy Cheesy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs russet potatos
1 can Campbell's Cheddar Soup
8 oz  of 1/2 & 1/2 creamer
16 oz cooked ham diced
1 lbs good shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 cup dried chives
4 chicken bouillon cubes
2 tsp celery salt
2 tsp seasoned salt
3 tbsp dried parsley
1 tbsp black pepper
Herbed croutons

Directions:
Fill a large pot a little above half way with water and add 4 chicken bouillon cubes, 2 tsp celery salt, 2 tsp of seasoned salt, and 1 tbsp of black pepper. Bring to a boil.
Peel and chop your 4lbs of potatos into medium sized chunks. Add to boiling water.
Let potatos boil for 18 minutes.
After 18 minutes drain off the water, reserving one cup of the water and seasoning mixture for later.
In a medium size bowl mix the can of cheddar soup, pound of shredded cheese, chives, parsley, ham, and 1/2 & 1/2. When throughly mixed add to potatos. Slowly add in the water/seasoning mixture until there is enough liquid to cover all the potatos. Put back on stove and once soup is hot and you can taste it add salt and pepper to taste.
Finish cooking on stove top on low until the cheese is melted, the potatos are soft, and everything is heated through.
Serve in soup bowls with croutons on top to garnish!
