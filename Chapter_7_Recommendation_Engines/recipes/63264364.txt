Title:
V's Grilling Marinade 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2563062.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon Lapsang Souchong loose Tea
1/4 cup Water
1/2 cup Tamari Soy sauce or regular soy sauce
3/4 cup Pineapple Juice
1/2 cup Cooking Oil
2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped or pressed
1 1/2" piece of Ginger Root,peeled,chopped or pressed
1 Tablespoon Honey
1 Tablespoon Dark Brown Sugar or Demerara Raw Sugar
2 Tablespoons Sherry

Directions:
Heat water and brew tea for 3 minutes, strain, add brown sugar to melt it and allow to cool while you prepare other ingredients.
Mix all ingredients in a tight closing jar and shake well. Pour over meat.
Marinate for 4 hours turning at least once.
Grill on medium heat until done to your preference.

Submitter: 
Vanessa C
Description: 
This marinade works well with beef, chicken, pork, and portobello mushrooms.  I like to use sirloin tips or a flank steak sliced on the bias and cooked medium rare.
