Title:
Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894047.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all purpose flour
1 cup quick-cooking oats
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup canned pumpkin
1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars.  Beat in egg and vanilla.  Combine flour, oats, baking soda and cinnamon; stir into creamed mixture alternately with pumpkin.  Fold in chocolate chips.  Drop by tablespoonfuls onto greased baking sheets.  Bake at 350 degrees for 12-13 minutes or until lightly browned.

A wonderful cookie recipe to celebrate fall!
