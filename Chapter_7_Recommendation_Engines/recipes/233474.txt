Ingredients:

½ fluid ounce sour mix
1 fluid ounce soda water

Submitter:

Description: 
A refreshing cross between an Old Fashioned and a whiskey sour. You'll splash a slice of orange with bitters and simple syrup and muddle it up; then you'll add ice, whiskey, and a little sour mix, and give things a nice shake.
