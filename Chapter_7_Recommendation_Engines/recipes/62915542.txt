Title:
Caeser Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Anchovies
2 Garlic Cloves
2 Tablespoons Grey Poupon
1/2 Lemon Juiced
1 Teaspoon Worchester Sauce
Pinch of Salt
Pinch of Pepper
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
2 Heads of Romaine Lettuce
1/2 Cup Shaved Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
With a mortar and pestel, grind together the anchovies and garlic till they are like a paste. Add the mustard, lemon, worchester, salt and pepper and mix well. Taste to confirm the flavor profile and add more of any ingredient you like best. When you are satisfied, whisk in the oil till the mixture is homogeneous. Mix cheese, dressing and salad well and then serve.
