Title:
Chocolate Blueberry Torte  ( Gluten Free ) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1103626.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. Gluten free flour blend
2 tsp. Baking powder
3 Tbsp. Premium dry cocoa
1/2 tsp. Xanthan gum
6 Eggs, separated
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 C. Fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease and flour a 9" springform pan. Set aside
Blend flour, B. powder, xanthan gum and cocoa together in a small bowl. Set aside
Separate egg yolks from whites and put whites aside
Beat egg yolks and sugar together in a large mixing bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until yolks are light and lemon coloured
Mix in blueberries and dry ingredients into wet mixture and mix until just combined
In a medium size bowl whip egg whites until stiff but not dry.  Gently fold egg whites into cake batter and blend until all the whites have been absorbed into the mixture
Pour batter into springform pan and bake for 45 to 50 minutes until cake is done in the middle or cake springs back when touched in the middle.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light and chocolatey cake with blueberries for a fancy dessert with ice cream or whipped cream.
