Title:
Delightful Pork Roast with out of this world potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pound Pork Roast (sirloin)
Rosemary,Pignoli Infused Oil with Peppercorns (olive oil won't give the same taste but will work)
Garlic Powder
Onion Powder
1 small onion (optional)
Paprika
Italian Seasoning
Kosher Salt
Pepper
4 Red potatoes

Directions:
Preheat overn to 450
Cut Potatoes about 1.5 inch think place in glass baking pan and toss with oil, sprinkle with garlic salt, onion powder, italian seasoning, salt and pepper to taste. Add slices of onion if you'd like.
Don't underdo seasoning they should coat potatoes.
Cook potatoes for about 15 min on 450 degrees while you prepare roast.
Rub oil on roast and place a few peppercorns on top.
Sprinkle garlic salt, kosher salt, onion powder, pepper and rub into entire roast.
Sprinkle top with paprika
Take Potatoes out change temp to 350 and add roast to middle of pan so potatoes surrond roast.
Cook for one hour of until temp is 160

A moist and delicious pork roast that is super simple and requires hardly any ingredients.
