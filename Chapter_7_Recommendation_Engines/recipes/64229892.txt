Title:
Mixed Veggie Casserole(Erlene's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large pkg. frozen mixed veggies(up to 22 oz)
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup diced celery
1 cup mayo
1 cup grated cheddar
1 cup cracker crumbs(butter crackers like ritz) about 1 sleeve
1/4 stick butter(melted)

Directions:
crush crackers add melted butter- mix well-set aside
bring 2 quarts water to boil-add veggies-return to boil- drain
add onion-celery-mayo-and cheese-mix well-place in buttered baking dish
top with cracker mixture
set oven to broil-place baking dish on middle rack of oven & broil til medium brown
chill well in fridge(about an hour)
serve chilled
