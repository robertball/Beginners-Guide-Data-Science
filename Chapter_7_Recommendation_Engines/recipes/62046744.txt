Title:
Cookies-n-Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Oreos, crushed
8 oz cool whip

Directions:
Reserve 1/2 cup cookies.  Mix the rest of the cookies with the cool whip.  Sprinkle reserved cookie crumbs on top.  Serve immediately or chill for later.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This is one of the easiest desserts you will ever make.  It also tastes great.  It is such a super easy recipe that even kids can make it.
