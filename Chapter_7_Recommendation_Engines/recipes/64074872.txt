Title:
Delicious Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1134045.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger meat
garlic powder
1 cup cottage cheese
4 oz. mozzarella cheese
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup mayonnaise
10-12 manicotti shells
1 small jar spaghetti sauce
oregano
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook manicotti shells as directed on box while preparing meat mixture in skillet. Drain.
Brown hamburger meat in skillet; sprinkle with garlic powder to taste. Add cottage cheese, mozzarella cheese, salt and mayonnaise. Mix well.
Fill each shell with meat mixture and place in 11 x 13 baking dish. Cover with spaghetti sauce. Sprinkle with oregano and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes. Uncover, and bake 10 minutes longer.
