Title:
Easy Rum Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 cup chopped, toasted pecans or walnuts
1 18 1/2 oz. yellow cake mix
1 1 3/4 oz. instant vanilla pudding mix
4 eggs
1/2 cup cold milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup Bacardi dark rum
Glaze:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup water
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup Bacardi dark rum

Directions:
Cake: Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.  Grease and flour 12-cup Bundt pan.  Sprinkle nuts on bottom of pan.  Combine all cake ingredients. Beat for 2 minutes on high.  Pour into prepared pan.  Bake for 1 hr. Let cool in pan.  Invert on serving plate. Prick top with fork.
Glaze: Melt butter in saucepan.  Stir in water and sugar. Boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and stir in rum.
Drizzle glaze over the top of cake.

Submitter: 
MochaMe
Description: 
A moist and delicious cake. Our family must have this during the holidays. With or without the glaze, it is wonderful. There are a lot of Rum Cakes, but this is a favorite.
