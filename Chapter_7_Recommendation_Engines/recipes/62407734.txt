Title:
Beef Kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean mince-meat
2 cups of gram lentils (yellow) pre-soaked overnight.
1 large onion
4 tlbs minced ginger garlic
1 cup of fresh mint chopped, or 2 tlbs of dried mint
1 tlbs ground black peppercorn
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp clove
1 tsp ground cardamom or all spice
1 tsp fennel seeds
1 tsp red chilli powder
1 tsp turmeric
2 tsp salt
3 tlbs oil, vegetable, canola or sunflower for cooking + 2tlbs for frying.

Directions:
Heat oil in a pan on medium heat
Add cinnamon, cardamom, clove powders, wait a minute for the spices to release their oils
Add chopped onion, ginger and garlic, wait till onions are soft.
Add mince meat,
Add dry spices and salt, cook on open pan till beef is browned, but not dry.
Transfer contents of pan to a medium sized pan, add soaked lentils, and 1 cup of water. Place lid with a vent and cook  for 15 minutes on medium-high heat.
Once the mixture is cool, add chopped mint .
Press and squeeze the dough till the lentils and beef are evenly mixed.
Make 2 inch balls and gently flatten and shape them with your hand into uniform circular shapes. Set aside for 15 minutes in fridge.
Heat oil in frying pan on medium to low heat, fry till top and bottom surfaces are crisp.
Garnish with spring onions or coriander to serve(optional)
