Title:
Festive Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981804.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound bulk pork sausage
6 cups Pepperidge Farm速 White Sandwich Bread cut in cubes
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup Pace速 Picante Sauce OR Pace速 Thick & Chunky Salsa
4 eggs
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Cook the sausage in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat, stirring frequently to break up meat. Pour off any fat.
Arrange sausage in a 2-quart shallow baking dish. Top with bread cubes and cheese. Mix picante sauce, eggs and milk and pour over all. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
