Title:
Deer Hunter's Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 cups plain flour
* 2 cups Sugar
* 2 teaspoons baking soda
* 1 teaspoon vanilla flavoring
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 cup chopped pecans
* 2 cups (20 oz can) crushed pineapple (do not drain)
Icing:
* 8 oz cream cheese
* 1 stick butter
* 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar or 2 cups
* 1/2 cup chopped pecans
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract flavoring

Directions:
Mix together cake ingredients and bake in a greased 13x9in pan at 350 degrees f for 35 to 40 minutes.
For icing, allow cream cheese and butter to soften. Add sugar and vanilla, beat until smooth, then add pecans. When cake is done, remove from oven and punch holes in cake. Pour icing over cake while the cake is hot.
