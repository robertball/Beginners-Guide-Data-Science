Title:
Apple Crisp Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/2 cups quick oats
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup kamut flour
1/3 cup brown sugar, packed
1/3 cup honey
2 Tbsp. baking powder
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
3/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
3/4 tsp. cardamom, optional
2 cups buttermilk
2 large eggs
1/2 cup canola oil
4 medium apples, peeled and diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease or line muffin pan.
In a large bowl, combine oats, flours, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg and cardamom.
Stir together to combine.
Add diced apples and gently toss to coat.
Set aside.
In a separate bowl, whisk eggs and add oil and milk.
Whisk together.
Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients.
Pour egg mixture into the oats mixture.
Mix just until moistened.
Allow to sit for 5 minutes to soften the oats.
Generously fill muffin pan with batter.
Bake for about 20 - 25 minutes, until muffins are golden brown and springs back when lightly touched.

Submitter: 
Beth
Description: 
This is a tastey, moist muffin that's good for you too.  I named this Apple Crisp because it has the same ingredients as warm apple crisp except in a muffin.  To top this muffin off, it's delicous with a streusel topping.
