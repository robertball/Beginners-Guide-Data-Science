Title:
Roasted Sweets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon coconut oil, or more as needed
3 sweet potatoes, cubed
1 sweet onion, chopped
1 sweet apple, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Spoon coconut oil onto a baking sheet; add sweet potatoes, onion, apple, and garlic. Sprinkle cinnamon, chipotle pepper, and salt over sweet potato mixture.
Roast in the preheated oven, stirring occasionally, until sweet potatoes are tender, 20 to 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Fast Family Feasts
Description: 
Sweet potatoes, sweet onions, and sweet apples are roasted with cinnamon and chipotle pepper creating a sweet and savory, paleo-friendly roasted side dish or snack.Modified from: Roasted Sweets
