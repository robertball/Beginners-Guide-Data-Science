Title:
Lemon Coconut Whipped Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/560916.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup icing sugar
1/2 tsp lemon extract (or 20 drops lemon essential oil)
Zest of 1 lemon
3/4 shredded coconut, unsweetened
1-285g jar lemon curd

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
In a large bowl, combine butter, flour, and icing sugar alternately and beat with an electric mixer for 2 minutes in between each addition.  Beat for at least 10 minutes in total. Dough should be light and fluffy. Add lemon extract and lemon zest.
Pour coconut onto a dinner plate or cake pan. Scoop 1 tsp batter and drop onto coconut.  Roll dough in palm of hand with coconut to coat the entire ball. Place on cookies sheet 2 inches apart.  Press finger in centre of each cookie to leave indent.
Bake for 9-12 minutes until golden brown. Remove from oven and let cool on wire rack.  With a small spoon, scoop 1/4 tsp of lemon curd onto each cookie. Store in a airtight container.
