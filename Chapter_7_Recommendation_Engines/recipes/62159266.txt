Title:
Drunken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284478.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 8 ounce package cream cheese, softened
1- 8 ounce package processed cheese, (ie: Velveeta) brought to room temperature
1/2 cup beer
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste

Directions:
Mix together cream cheese and processed cheese, using an electric mixer, until completely smooth and creamy.
Add beer and garlic powder and beat until fully  incorporated and smooth.
Refrigerate until cold.
Serve.
NOTE: Preparation time does not include time for chilling.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This dip is great for parties. It has a smoother, creamier texture than other dips like this. Dip anything you like, chunks of bread, crackers, veggies, etc... This one is ALWAYS a hit! Enjoy!
