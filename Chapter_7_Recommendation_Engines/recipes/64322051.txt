Title:
Cherry Tomatoes Stuffed with Low Fat Cottage Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 medium tomato(es), cherry
1/2 cup(s) cottage cheese, 1% fat

Directions:
Slice tops off of tomatoes and scoop the centers out. Fill each tomato with about 1 teaspoon of cottage cheese.
