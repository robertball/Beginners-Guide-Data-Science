Title:
Pan-Fried Salmon with Green Beans and Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. Salmon (one fillet)2. 1/2 cup trimmed green beans3. 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes4. 1 tsp. chopped garlic5. 2 tbsp. brown sugar6. 2 tbsp. olive oil7. 1/2 tbsp. butter (room temp.)8. 2 tbsp. lime juice9. 1 tsp. salt10. 1 tsp. pepper11. 1 tsp. chili powder

Directions:
1. Combine butter, brown sugar, lime juice, and 1 tbsp. olive oil in a small bowl until smooth and creamy to make a brown sugar glaze.2. Pour remaining olive oil into a saute pan and heat to medium high. Add garlic and saute until golden brown.3. Add green beans and tomatoes, and pour brown sugar glaze into pan. Place lid over pan, stirring occasionally, for 2-4 minutes, or until the brown sugar glaze has begun caramelizing and bubbling.4. Add the salmon fillet, and place lid over pan again. Let cook on one side for 4 minutes or until golden brown, and then flip to the other side and cook for 3-4 minutes, making sure the brown sugar glaze has coated the salmon.5. Add salt, pepper, and chili powder to taste before removing the salmon and vegetables from the heat. Makes enough for one. Serve hot from the pan!

Submitter: 
Aurora
Description: 
Mildly sweet salmon paired with crunchy green beans and cherry tomatoes. Goes great with a tall glass of iced tea or lemonade in the middle of summer.
