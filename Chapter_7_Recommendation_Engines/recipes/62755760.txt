Title:
Breaded Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pork Tenderloins, cut into 4 pieces, sprinkled with:
Salt and Pepper
2 Ebbs, beaten
1/2 Cup flour
1Cup bread crumbs (water misted)
2 Cups veg. oil
4 Cups Shredded red cabbage
1/2 cucumber, sliced thin in halves
3/4 Cup carrots, julienned
parsley leaves (garnish)
sesame dressing (opt)

Directions:
On separate plates, dip pork into flour, then egg, then bread crumbs. Heat veg oil to medium in frying pan and place pork in oil. Turn several times. Fry each about 10 minutes, until golden brown and cooked through. Remove particles from oil after each tenderloin. Slice and overlap over cabbage. Pour dressing over cabbage. Garnish with carrots, cucumber, and parsley.
