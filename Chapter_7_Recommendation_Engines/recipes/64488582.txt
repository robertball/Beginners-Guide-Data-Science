Title:
The Ultimate Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2568013.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
3/4 C graham cracker crumbs
1/4 C ground almonds
4 Tbsp white sugar
4 Tbsp melted butter
Filling:
4 (8 oz) pkg cream cheese- room temperature
2 C white sugar
3 Tbsp corn starch
16 oz sour cream- room temperature
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 C amaretto liqueur
1/2 tsp salt
6 eggs- room temperature

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine first 4 ingredients and press into 9" springform pan.  Bake 10 minutes.  Set aside.
2. In large bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, cornstarch, and mix until smooth.  Add sour cream, vanilla, liqueur, and salt.  Mix well.  On low speed beat in one egg at a time.
3. Pour over crust.  Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.  Turn off oven, crack door, and let sit 30 min.  Watch that it doesn't dry!
4. Remove and run knife around edges to loosen.  Chill completely (overnight).
