Title:
Creamy Pesto Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound linguine pasta
1/2 cup butter
2 cups half & half
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/3 cup pesto
2 garlic cloved minced
8 oz. sliced mushrooms
1/2 lb. asparagus cut in 1" pieces
2 tomatoes diced and seeded
1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add linguine pasta, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
In a large skillet, heat 2 Tbsp. butter, add mushrooms, garlic & asparagus and cook until al dente.
In a large skillet, melt the rest of the butter over medium heat. Stir in cream, and season with pepper. Cook 6 to 8 minutes, stirring constantly.
Stir Parmesan cheese into cream sauce, stirring until thoroughly mixed. Blend in the pesto, and cook for 3 to 5 minutes, until thickened.
Stir in the shrimp, and cook until they turn pink, about 5 minutes. Add the cooked vegetables.  Serve over the hot linguine.
