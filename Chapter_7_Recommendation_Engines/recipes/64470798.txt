Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of large marshmallows
1 jar Nutella
1 jar Marshmallow cream

Directions:
Cut your large marshmallows in half. Spread nutella in the middle, and re-assemble marshmallow. Cover in marshmallow cream, and freeze before serving. Sticky, fluffy, sweet bites... perfect for the Dinosaurs in your life.
