Title:
Green Bean and Mozzarella Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981424.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh green beans, cooked, drained
6 plum tomatoes, chopped
1 cup KRAFT FREE Shredded Non-Fat Mozzarella Cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1/3 cup prepared GOOD SEASONS Italian Salad Dressing Mix for Fat Free Dressing

Directions:
Place beans, tomatoes, cheese and basil in large bowl.
Pour dressing over bean mixture.  Refrigerate at least 1 hour to marinate.
