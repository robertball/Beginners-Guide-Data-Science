Title:
Baked apple and kale casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(1) 10.75 ounce can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup nonfat plain yogurt
1 egg, beaten
1 slice white onion, chopped
2 red apples, peeled and chopped
10 oz bag of kale, chopped
6 oz monterey jack cheese, shredded
salt, pinch
coarse black pepper, to taste
paprika, to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. In a 9" square baking pan, pour in soup, yogurt, and egg. whisk
3. Grate the cheese
4. Chop kale, slice and chop onion, peel and chop apples
5. Add the cheese, kale, onion and apples to the baking pan mixture and stir with a spatula to coat the kale and evenly distribute ingredients throughout pan
6. Cover with foil
7. Bake 45 minutes

This easy vegetarian side dish can be used as a dip, too.
