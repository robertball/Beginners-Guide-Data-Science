Title:
Sweet Lassi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups plain yogurt
1/2 cup cold milk
1/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons rosewater
1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/8 teaspoon (pinch) of salt
6 ice cubes

Directions:
In a blender combine the yogurt with cold milk, sugar, rosewater, cardamom and salt.  Blend until smooth.  Add ice cubes and blend until ice cracking can no longer be heard.  Pour into tall chilled glasses and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Michael St. Laurent
Description: 
A popular, traditional, yogurt-based drink originating from India's Punjab region.
