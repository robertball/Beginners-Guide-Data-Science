Title:
Tomato & Artichoke Tortellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ripe tomatoes
1 6 ounce jar marinated artichoke hearts
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup coarsely chopped yellow onion
2 cloves chopped garlic
1/8 cup dried basil
1/4 Tablespoon dried oregano
1/4 cup finely chopped parsley
1/2 - 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 1/2 Tablespoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup shredded Romano cheese
2 pounds cheese tortellini, cooked and chilled

Directions:
Cook tortellini.  Drain and chill.
Scald tomatoes and peel.  Drain artichokes and reserve marinade.
Heat olive oil in a large, deep saucepan.  Saute onions, garlic, basil, oregano, parsley and red pepper over medium heat for 5 minutes.  Add pepper.
Add tomatoes and 1/2 teaspoon salt and simmer, uncovered over medium heat for 1 hour.
Add artichoke marinade and simmer, stirring often, another 30 minutes.
Stir in artichokes and continue to simmer until sauce is rich and thick, another 10-15 minutes or so.
Stir in Romano.  Serve over cooked & chilled tortellini.
