Title:
Rice a Roni Spanish Rice Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 (6.8-ounce) package RICE-A-RONIÂ® Spanish Rice
1 (16-ounce) jar salsa, or 2 cups chopped fresh tomatoes or 1 (14-1/2 ounce) can tomatoes, undrained and chopped
1 cup canned black or red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup frozen or canned corn, drained
8(6-inch) flour tortillas, warmed

Directions:
1. In large skillet, over medium-high heat, melt butter. Saute rice-vermicelli mix until vermicelli is golden brown.
2. Slowly stir in 2 cups water, salsa, and Special Seasonings. Bring to a boil. Cover; reduce heat to low. Simmer 15 to 20 minutes or until rice is tender.
3. Stir in beans and corn; let stand 5 minutes before serving. Serve in tortillas with sour cream, if desired.
