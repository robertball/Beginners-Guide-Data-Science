Title:
slow cooker chicken curry with squash & stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of diced onions
3 cups diced yellow & zucchini squash
1 tablespoon low fat butter
1 pound chicken tenders
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 cup light mayo
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1 teaspoon lemon juice
3/4 cup of low fat cheddar cheese
1/2 cup diced onion
1/2 tablespoon butter
1/2 cup diced celery
2 cups of prepackaged stuffing mix
1 1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Saute squash and onion in butter until onions are tender and place in the bottom of butter sprayed crock pot.
Place chicken on top of sauteed squash. Combine soup, mayo, curry powder, lemon juice and cheese and pour over chicken. Saute onion and celery in butter, remove from stove and stir in stuffing and broth. Place stuffing on top of sauce and cook on low for 4 hours.
