Title:
Jess' Best I Dunno Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18oz) bottle rice vinegar
1 (8 oz) bottle of oyster sauce
5 TBLS sesame oil
5 TBLS roasted sesame seeds
2 TBLS minced garlic
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1 lg round onion chopped

Directions:
Combine above mentioned ingredients in a bowl
Place meat of your choice in a zip lock bag
Place marinade in bag
Set some of the marinade on side for basting
Place in fridge for no less than 6 hours
Lomi (turn, or massage) meat every so often
Cook on grill till done
Make sure to baste while grilling

Submitter: 
Cooking from the Hip
Description: 
This is a marinade that I came up with by mistake one day. I was messing around in the kitchen and trying to come up with a marinade for some chicken that I was going to grill. I made this tried it and fell in love with it. People would ask me what the sauce was and I would just say I dunno? Hence the name...
