Title:
Allyson's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. Italian dressing
2 lb. beef stew meat
6 slices of bacon
1 lg. onion, chopped
3 C. sliced mushrooms
3 C. sliced carrots
3 medium potatoes peeled and chunked
14oz. can stewed tomatoes, undrained
14 oz. can beef broth

Directions:
Place dressing and beef in a ziplock bag and marinate for 30 minutes.
Cook bacon in a skillet until crisp and drain grease.
Place mushrooms and onions in a pot and cook until soft.
Add the beef, carrots, potatoes, stewed tomatoes, beef broth and crumbled bacon to the pot.
Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 15 minutes or until meat is tender and sauce has thickened.

Description: 
This is a great recipe for those cold wintery evenings. My family loves it. Enjoy!!!
