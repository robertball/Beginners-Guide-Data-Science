Title:
Mommy's Easy Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/504290.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs extra lean ground beef
1 (10 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 (2 lb) package frozen frozen tater tots

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Brown ground beef until all pink is gone and drain any grease.
Put ground beef in the bottom of a greased 13x9x2-inch pan.
Spread cream of chicken soup on top of ground beef. Cover with 1 cup of the grated cheese
Top with tater tots
Cover with foil and bake covered 45 minutes
Uncover and top with remaining cheese.
Bake for 15 more minutes.

Description: 
Simple, easy, and tasty meal. Comfort food at it's best. My kids love to help by puting the tater tots on top of the meal. With only 4 ingrediants it's a mom's best friend.
