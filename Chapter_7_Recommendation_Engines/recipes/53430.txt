Title:
Old Bay速 Steamed Shrimp Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup vinegar
4 teaspoons Old Bay速 Seasoning
1/2 cup water
1 pound shrimp

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients in saucepan. Bring to a boil.
Add shrimp, stir gently. Cover; steam until tender, about 5 minutes.
Drain, remove shells and devein.

Submitter: 
Old Bay速
Description: 
Steamed shrimp, served warm or cold, get great flavor from OLD BAY速 Seasoning. Serve cold with cocktail sauce as a shrimp cocktail, warm as a main dish, or use to make shrimp salad.
