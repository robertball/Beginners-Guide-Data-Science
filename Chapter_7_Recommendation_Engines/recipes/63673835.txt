Title:
Slow Cooker Mexican Chicken Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts
15 oz. can Enchilada sauce
2/3 C. (2 packets) taco seasoning
1 C. Salsa
4.5 oz. can green chilis
15 oz. can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 c. canned sweet corn, drained
Chicken & Garlic Rice a Roni, prepared

Directions:
Mix enchilada sauce, taco seasoning, salsa, and green chilis in slow cooker pot. Add chicken and cover with sauce. Cook on low for 8 to 10 hours or high for 4 to 5 hours. Add black beans and sweet corn. Prepare Rice a Roni according to package about 25 minutes before desired dinner time. Serve chicken over prepared Rice a Roni.

Submitter: 
JulezinWI
Description: 
This is a quick and easy goulash recipe using salsa, beans, corn, green chilis, seasonings and rice. Fix it and forget it!
