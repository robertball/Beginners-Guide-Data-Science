Title:
Sweet Green Bean Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068337.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (14.5 ounce) cans whole green beans, drained
1 pound bacon, cut in half
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Wrap 7 green beans with bacon and place in prepared dish.
Repeat, using all the green beans and bacon.
Combine butter with brown sugar.
Pour over green bean bundles and sprinkle with garlic salt.
Cover with foil and bake at 350Â° for 45 minutes.
