Title:
Greek Style Marinated Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. small mushrooms
1/2 c olive oil
1/4 c dry white wine
2 T lemon juice
1/2 c water
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 t salt
1/4 t black pepper
1 t sugar
1/4 t fennel seeds
1/2 t ground coriander

Directions:
Wipe mushrooms with a damp cloth.
Place in a large heat-proof bowl, set aside.
In a small saucepan, combine remaining ingredients and bring to a boil over medium heat, simmer about 2 minutes.
Immediately pour mixture over mushrooms, cover and cool to room temperature.
Refrigerate 6 hours or overnight.
Serve with toothpicks for an appetizer or serve as a side with beef dishes.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
These robust mushrooms may be served as an appetizer with toothpicks and also make a wonderful side for beef. The fennel seeds give the mushrooms a very slight licorice flavor that's absolutely delicious.
