Title:
Apple Berry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636214.jpg
Ingredients:
2 apples, peeled and chopped
2 cups seasonal berries
Juice of one lime
1/4 c applesauce
.25 c softened butter (or nondairy cooking spread)
1 c old fashioned oats
1 tsp cinnamon
.5 c brown sugar
.25 c chopped pecans or walnuts

Directions:
Combine apples and berries
Combine apple sauce and lime juice
Toss fruit in sauce
Place in greased pie plate or small backing dish
With fork, combine butter, oats, cinnamon, sugar and nuts
Crumble oat mixture on fruit
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until fruit bubbles and top is crisp.
Cool for 5-10 minutes
Swerve with full fat Greeek yogurt sweetened with maple syrup.  Omit for vegan option.

Description: 
Goes great with the Bean Salad with Lime and Cilantro Dressing.  A healthy breakfast option as well, with whole grains, fresh fruit and Greek yogurt.
