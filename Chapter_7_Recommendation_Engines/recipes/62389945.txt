Title:
Vanilla Frozen Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups nonfat Greek yogurt
2/3 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix Greek yogurt, sugar and vanilla until smooth.
Refrigerate for 1 hour.
Freeze according to your ice cream makers directions.

Submitter: 
TeenChef14
Description: 
This frozen yogurt is so much easier than homemade ice cream!
