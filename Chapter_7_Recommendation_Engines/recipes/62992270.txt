Title:
Skor toffee bits cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744556.jpg
Ingredients:
2-1/4 cups AP flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 eggs
1-1/3 cups SKOR Toffee Bits (Chipits)
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheet (or use parchment paper).
Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; set aside.
Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla in a large bowl until well blended. Add eggs; beat well.
Gradually add flour mixture, beating until well blended. Stir in toffee bits.
Drop by rounded teaspoons onto prepared cookie sheet. Bake 9 to 11 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely.
Makes about 4 dozen cookies.
