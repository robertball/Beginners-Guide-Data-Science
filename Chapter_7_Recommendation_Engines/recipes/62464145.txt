Title:
Corn Dog Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8.5 ounce) packages corn bread/muffin mix
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 eggs
1 cup milk (up to 1 1/2 cups)
1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
10 hot dogs, chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine corn bread mix and brown sugar. Combine eggs and milk; stir into dry ingredients until moistened. Stir in corn, cheese and hot dogs (batter will be thin).
Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full  Bake at 400 degrees F for 14-18 minutes or until golden brown. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
