Title:
savory sausage pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sausage
1 small onion finely choppen
4 c all pourpose flour
5 t baking powder
1 t salt
2 eggs slightly beaten
3 c milk

Directions:
Pan fry sausage and onion untill cooked thoroughly
drain and pat off all excess grease and set aside to cool.
Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.
In a separate bowl, combine egg and milk; add to flour mixture stirring only until smooth.
Add the meat mixture to the batter.
Cook on a hot griddle, using about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake Cook until brown on one side and then flip and cook untill brown and remove.
Top with some butter or margarine and syrup.
