Title:
Korean Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
prepare
4 oz dry rive vermicelli
2 tbsp thinly sliced scallions
1 tbsp toasted sesame 0il and toasted sesame seeds
then simmer
1/4 cup of each brown sugar and rice vinegar
2 tbsp each low sodium soy sauce and giner
1 tbsp minced fresh garlic
2 tsp each of sesame oil and chili garlic sauce
1 tbsp water
1 tsp cornstarch
season 12 oz rib eye steak cut into 1/4 inch thich strips
salt and black pepper
sliced scallions
toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Prepare vermicelli according to directions.  Toss with 2 tbsp scallions 1 tbsp sesame oil and 1 tbsp sesame seeds
Preheat grill to high and brush grate with oil
Simmer brown sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, 2tsp sesame oil and chili garlic sauce for the sauce in a small saucepan over high heat.  Combine water and cornstarch, then wisk into sauce.  Return sauce to a boil and simmer for 1 minutes.
Season rib ey strips with salt and pepper, grill 2 minutes per side and then toss in sauce - arrange strips on two plates and garnish with scallions and sesame seeds
serve beef with vermicelli, and remaining sauce
