Title:
Bread Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pizza skins
1/2 C garlic butter spread

Directions:
Spread garlic butter on pizza skins.  Let proof for 2 1/2 to 3 hours.  Bake at 350 degrees with high fan for 15 minutes.  Cook and cut into breadsticks.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Light and airy with delicious garlicky flavor.
