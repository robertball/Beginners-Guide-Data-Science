Title:
Mama Enie's Cookie Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sifted confectioners sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
1 1/2 Tablespoons water

Directions:
Blend sugar, salt, and extracts in a small bowl.
Add water to make it easy to spread.
If desired, tint with a few drops of food coloring.
Spread on cookies with an offset spatula.

Submitter: 
mauigirl
Description: 
My mom was a stellar baker. She was also a pro wedding cake and cookie decorator. This is the icing that she used to flood her cookies. She probably found it in one of her many old cookbooks. This is very easy to spread.
