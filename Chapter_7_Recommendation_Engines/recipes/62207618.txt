Title:
Sausage Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mild groud pork Sausage
1 lb chopped Mushrooms
1 teaspoon dried crumbled Thyme
2 tablespoons Dijon Mustard
1/2 cup Sour Cream
2 packages refrigerated Crescent Rolls

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350.
Cook Sausage according to package, Chopping up into tiny pieces.  Drain grease.
Add Mushrooms & Thyme and Cook over Medium Heat 5 mins.
Remove from heat and Stir in Mustard & Sour Cream.
Lay out Half of 1 Crescent Rolls pkg on floured board.  Press perforations to make 1 rectangle.
Spoon 1/4 Filling down the Center and Roll up.
Crimp Ends and Repeat 3 times w/remaining Rolls and Filling.
Place each Roll (not touching) Seam side Down on Baking Sheets and Bake 15 mins until golden brown.
Let Cool slightly and Slice into 1 inch Pieces.
