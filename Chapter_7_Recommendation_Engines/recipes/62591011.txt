Title:
Citrus Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup orange juice
1/2 tsp. lime zest
2 tbsp. fresh lime juice
2 tbsp. fresh thyme
2 tsp. garlic - minced
1 tsp. orange zest
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
4 chicken cutlets
1 tbsp. olive oil
6 cups baby spinach

Directions:
Combine first 8 ingredients in small bowl, stirring well with a whisk.
Pour 1/4 cup of mixture in a ziploc bag and add chicken.
Toss to coat chicken and let stand 5 minutes.
Add oil to remaining mixture, stirring well with a whisk.
Heat large skillet over medium heat and coat with cooking spray.
Add chicken to pan and cook about 4 min. on each side or until done.
Place 1/4 of spinach on each serving plate, top with chicken and top with 1 tbsp. juice mixture.
