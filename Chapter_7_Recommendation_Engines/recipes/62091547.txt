Title:
Quick and easy broccoli casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz frozen broccoli
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1 sleeve ritz crackers

Directions:
cook broccoli following package instructions.
Put cooked broccoli in a 1 quart oven safe bowl or dish. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Layer entire package of cheese on top of broccoli.
Layer entire sleeve of crackers on top of cheese.
Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes.
Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until crackers look toasty.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
a package of broccoli, a package of shredded cheese, and a sleeve of crackers is all you need for this delicious side dish.
