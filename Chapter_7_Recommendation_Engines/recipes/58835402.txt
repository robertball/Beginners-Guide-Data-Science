Title:
BUDGET Best Rolled Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/264602.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C butter, chilled cut into small cubes
2 cups white sugar
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
5 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In the bowl kitchen-aid mixer with paddle attachment, place flour, sugar, and baking powder.  On low speed at the cubed butter in 3 additions. Allow to mix until flour/butter mixture resembles coarse sand.
In small bowl whisk together eggs, milk, and vanilla extract.  Add to flour/ butter mixture on low speed. all at once.  Mix until dough forms smooth ball.  Wrap and chill dough for at least 1 hour or overnight.
Pre-heat oven to 350* F.
Bake 8-10 mins or until edges are slightly browned.

These rolled sugar cookies are perfect for frosting!! The dough is not too sweet, and can withstand re-rolling without making the dough too tough. I use store brand baking products to make these delicious cookies cheap to make.Modified from: The Best Rolled Sugar Cookies
