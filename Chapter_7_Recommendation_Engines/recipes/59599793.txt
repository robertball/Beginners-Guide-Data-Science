Title:
Baked Ziti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz.) package ziti
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 (15 oz.) container ricotta
1/2 C. grated Parmesan, divided
2 (28 oz.) jars spaghetti sauce
pepperoni slices to taste (opt)
2 C. shredded mozzarella

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta.  Set aside.
Mix egg, ricotta and Parmesan together.
Add spaghetti sauces and mix well. Pour over pasta and stir together.
Pour into greased 9 x 13 baking pan.
Top w/ pepperoni slices and shredded mozzarella.
Bake at 375 for 50-60 minutes or until heated through and bubbly.
