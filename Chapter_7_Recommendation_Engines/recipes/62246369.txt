Title:
Jen's Hamburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb extra lean ground beef
1/2 packet of low-sodium taco seasoning
large bag of baby carrots, whole
package of fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 cups of corn niblets
1/2 a head savoy cabbage, sliced
3 cups 1" cubed butternut squash
1 medium crushed tomatoes
1/2 cup water
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt (optional)
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt (optional)

Directions:
Put the carrots in the bottom of a large stewing pot and then add the rest of the ingredients (except the beef and taco seasoning) on top.  Cover and heat at med-high to get things started.
Brown the ground beef in a skillet with the taco seasoning.  Drain, then add to top of loaded stock pot.
Simmer for at least an hour, until the carrots are at desired softness.

A hearty, healthy dish that I've made a hundred different ways by varying the ingredients, but this time I found a winning combination!  Enjoy!
