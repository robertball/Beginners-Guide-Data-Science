Title:
KM style watermelon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397143.jpg
Ingredients:
Medium-large seedless watermelon, 10 to 20 lbs.
Fresh mint leaves, about 3 to 4 mint sprigs worth.
1 fresh lemon or orange.

Directions:
Cube melon into bite sized pieces.
Mince mint leaves, and toss with melon cubes in large bowl.
Hand squeeze 1/2 of lemon juice over top of melon & mint and toss again to coat.
Serve right away, but better if chilled in refrigerator for 1/2 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
jason
Description: 
KM style watermelon is a really cool refreshing way to do watermelon!  It is especially satisfying on a hot day.  GREAT addition to any Barbecue!
