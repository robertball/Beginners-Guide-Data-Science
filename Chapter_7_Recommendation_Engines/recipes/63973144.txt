Title:
Jenny's Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yeast
1 tsp. sugar
1 c. water
3 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp. salt
2 T. melted butter
1 egg
1/4 c. shortening

Directions:
Combine sugar and water and add yeast.
Let stand about 5 min. until yeast blooms.
Beat egg and add to melted butter.
Add flour and salt to food processor fitted with dough blade and pulse to combine.
With processor running, add yeast mixture, egg mixture, and shortening.
Continue to pulse until combined.
Turn dough out into a slightly oiled bowl, cover with a towel, and let rise overnight or until doubled.
Punch down, roll out, and par-bake on pizza stone or pan before adding toppings.
