Ingredients:
1 cup water
2 pounds chicken wing pieces
3/4 cup buffalo wing sauce
2 teaspoons dry ranch powder

Directions:
1. Add water to Instant Pot, place the trivet inside, and place wings on top of the trivet.
2. Secure the lid, seal, choose the manual setting, and cook for 10 minutes.
3. While the wings are cooking mix wing sauce and ranch powder in a large bowl and line a baking sheet with foil.
4. When the wings are done cooking do a quick release and carefully open the lid. Transfer the wings to the bowl of sauce using tongs. Toss to coat.
5. Place a wire baking rack over the foil lined baking sheet and arrange wings on top.
6. Set the broiler on the oven to high. Broil wings for 5 minutes. Flip the wings and broil for an additional 5 minutes or until crispy.  Toss with additional sauce if desired.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Buffalo wings made simple in an Instant Pot and without the fry. The Instant Pot cooks these to perfection. They hold together for easy handling but as soon as you sink your teeth into them, they are fall off the bone tender. Serve with carrot sticks, celery, and ranch if desired.
