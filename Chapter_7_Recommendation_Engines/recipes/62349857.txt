Title:
Easy Rhubarb Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - Yellow Cake Mix
5C. Chopped Rhubarb
1 C. Sugar
1 Tsp Cinnamon
1 C. Hot Water

Directions:
Mix cake as directed on package. Pour into 9x13 greased & floured cake pan. Pour rhubarb over cake mix. Sprinkle with sugar & cinnamon. Pour water overtop. Bake 350 for 50-60 minutes or until done.

Submitter: 
Michelle Peterson
Description: 
From a neighbor, very simple and great rhubarb pudding bottom. Reminds me of a pineapple upside down cake. I'm not a big fan of rhubarb but this is excellent and easy.
