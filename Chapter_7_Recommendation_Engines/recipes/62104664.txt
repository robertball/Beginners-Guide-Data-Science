Title:
Vegetable Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups leftover rice
1 cup leftover vegetables (ie corn, green beans, peas, carrots)
1 can beef broth
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 tablespoon olive oil or veg. oil
1 tablespoon cooking sherry
1/4 tsp. thyme
1/4 salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
2 cubes chicken bouillon
1/2 cup heavy cream or whole milk

Directions:
In a large soup pot over med. high heat saute butter and oil with the rice and vegetales. Break up any rice chunks. Add thyme, pepper, sherry, and salt. Add can of beef broth and chicken cubes. Add heavy cream or milk and stir.
Cover and simmer for about 30 min. or until soup bubbles slightly. Stir frequently and taste, add extra spices if needed.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
I develped this recipe when I wanted a homemade soup but didn't have many ingredients. You can easily customize it to taste---add chopped chicken or crumbled sausage, or experiment with a variety of vegetables. A great way to use leftovers!
