Title:
Best Ever Scalloped Cheesy Potatoes and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
3/4 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped ham
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 3/4 cups chicken broth
2 tablespoons sour cream
2 tablespoons cream cheese
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
Paprika

Directions:
In a greased 2-1/2-qt. baking dish, layer potatoes,cheese, and ham and onion.
In a saucepan, melt the butter; stir in flour until smooth. Gradually add broth, sour cream, cream cheese, salt and pepper; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thick and bubbly. Pour over potatoes. Sprinkle with paprika.
Cover and bake at 325 degrees for 2 hours or until tender.
