Title:
Malted Milk Ball frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Tablespoons butter room temp
1/3 cup chocolate malted milk powder
2 cups powder sugar
Pinch of salt
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
Malted milk balls for decoration

Directions:
Beat all ingredients together while slowly adding milk and vanilla until creamy.
Frost cake or cupcakes
