Title:
Fudge Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635170.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup flour
½ tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup butter
1 cup cocoa
2 cups white sugar
4 eggs
1 ½ tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
Frosting:
3 Tbsp melted butter
¼ cup cocoa
¼ cup milk
½ tsp vanilla
2 cups icing sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Mix flour, salt and baking powder.
Melt butter, remove from heat. Stir in cocoa, blend in sugar, 4 eggs and vanilla. Blend in dry ingredients. Add nuts.
Pour batter into 9x13-inch pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
Cool and frost.
Frosting: Combine butter with cocoa. Blend in milk, vanilla and sugar until smooth. Spread over brownies.
