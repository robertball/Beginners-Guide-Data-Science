Title:
Big Ray's Corny Spoon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018023.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs, beaten.
2 packages of corn-muffin mix,8.5 oz.
1 can cream corn, 15 oz.
3/4 cup milk
1 medium onion, chopped
1 small sweet pepper, chopped
1 can green chile peppers,4oz.
1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican-style four cheese blend)

Directions:
Spray inside of 4 quart slow cooker with nonstick cooking spray
In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients but the cheese.  Spoon into slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or until toothpick inserted near center comes out clean.  Turn off heat and sprinkle cheese on top.  Cover and let stand for 30 to 45 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A hearty addition to a bowl of chili, this cornbread side dish also hits the spot with nicely grilled steak.
