Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package of Cream Cheese (room Temperature)
1 4oz Can Medium Deveined Shrimp
Garlic Powder
1 Tablespoon of Milk

Directions:
Lay down the block of cream cheese flat in a medium sized bowl
add garlic powder enough to cover the top of the cream cheese. Add a tablespoon of milk. Drain the shrimp and then add to bowl. Using the bottom of a fork start to smash the cream cheese this also will shred the shrimp just keep doing that until well mixed. Take a taste and adjust garlic powder to your liking. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Description: 
My all time favorite shrimp dip. We use pretzels to dip in it yum.
