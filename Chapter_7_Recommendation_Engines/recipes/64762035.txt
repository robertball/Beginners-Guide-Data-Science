Title:
Red Curry Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7162063.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
2 Tbsp Crushed ginger
1 medium onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 Tbsp brown sugar
4 cups vegetable broth
42 oz. coconut milk
4 tsp red curry paste
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
1/2 cup uncooked white rice
2 medium potatoes, diced
3 medium carrots, sliced
1 cup fresh sliced white mushrooms

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat in a large saucepan. Add ginger, onions, and red peppers. Cook until tender.
Stir in brown sugar and let it heat for about a minute or until it melts. Add the vegetable broth, coconut milk, curry paste, soy sauce, and lime juice.
Add rice, potatoes, carrots, and mushrooms, then bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer about 20 minutes or until rice and vegetables are cooked.
