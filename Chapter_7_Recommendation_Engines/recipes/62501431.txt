Title:
Chili in a hurry Sweettreats way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
4 cups water
1 6 oz can tomato paste
24 ounces tomato sauce
1 14.5 ounce can tomatoes with diced chili's
1 onion chopped
1 bell pepper chopped
1/2 cup celery
1/4 cup chili powder
2 teaspoon cumin
1 jalapeno, diced (optional)
1/4 cup chopped pepperoncini's (optional)
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon Jane's Krazy mixed up salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/8 teaspoon cayenne
4 16 ounce cans chili beans
6 tablespoons cornstarch

Directions:
In a large pot brown ground beef, onions, bell peppers, celery and jalapeno. Drain the fat. Add all ingredients except the cornstarch. Cook for twenty minutes. In a small bowl remove one cup of liquid and stir in the cornstarch. Add to the pot and let simmer until the chili starts to thicken about twenty to thirty minutes.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
A quick flavorful chili that can be ready to serve in one hour. Since I work full time I am always trying to come up with fast filling meals for my family. Serve with cornbread for a quick family pleasing meal.
