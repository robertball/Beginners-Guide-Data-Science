Title:
Fire In Your Pants Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1/2 onion
1 red pepper
1 green pepper
2 jalapeno peppers
1 can diced tomatoes
1 cup water
1/2 cup celery
1 can of mushrooms
1 tablespoon chili Powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon crushed peppers
1 can red kidney beans
1/2 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Brown Beef and add onions
Drain Beef mixture
Add chopped peppers, tomatoes with juice, water, celery, chili powder, cumin, oregano, crushed peppers and beans.
Bring to boil
Turn on low and simmer for an hour.
Sprinkle cheese over the chili when serving it

Submitter: 
Maryet Mann
Description: 
This recipe is from my mother-in-law and the first time I had this I couldn't feel my lips for days. Now it's the only I want to eat on a cold winter night.
