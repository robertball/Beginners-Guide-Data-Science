Title:
Sauza® Southwest Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981022.jpg
Ingredients:
7 1/2 ounces Sauza® Gold Tequila
4 1/2 ounces Dekuyper® Blue Curacao
18 ounces Margarita Mix
6 ounces Orange Juice
GARNISH
Lime Squeeze

Directions:
Combine the ingredients without ice and shake vigorously. Then just pour the stunningly blue mixture over ice in a 14 oz glass, garnish and serve.

Submitter: 
Sauza
Description: 
A beautiful blue margarita with the tangy flavors of orange and lime is perfect for sipping on a warm summer day or night, thinking of a cool blue ocean.
