Title:
Spicy Cheeseburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water
2 cups cubed, peeled potatoes
2 small carrots, grated
1 small onion, chopped
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 jalepeno pepper, seeded and chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 T beef bouillon
1/2 salt
1 pound ground beef, cooked and drained
2 1/2 cups milk, divided
3 T flour
8 ounces Velveeta
1/4 to 1 t cayenne pepper
1/4 cup yellow mustard
1/2 pound sliced bacon, cooked, drained and crumbled

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine the first nine ingredients; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Stir in beef and 2 cups of milk; heat through.
Combine flour and remaining milk until smooth; gradually stir into soup.
Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened and bubbly.
Reduce heat; stir in cheese until melted.  Add cayenne and mustard.
Stir in bacon or put on top when serving.
