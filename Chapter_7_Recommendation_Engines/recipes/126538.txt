Title:
Raspberry Topped Lemon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/25922.jpg
Ingredients:
3 egg yolks
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 (6 ounce) prepared graham cracker pie crust
1 (10 ounce) package frozen red raspberries, thawed
1 tablespoon cornstarch
Whipped topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. In large bowl, with mixer, beat egg yolks and EAGLE BRAND(r) until well blended. Stir in lemon juice. Pour into crust. Bake 30 minutes.
In saucepan, combine raspberries and cornstarch; cook and stir until mixture thickens and is clear. Spoon on top of pie. Chill at least 4 hours.
Top with whipped topping. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
A lemon flavored pie is topped with a delectable red raspberry sauce. Serve this creamy pie with whipped topping.
