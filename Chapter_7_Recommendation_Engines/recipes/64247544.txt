Title:
Best in the West Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
10 slices bacon
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup catsup
1/4 cup BBQ sauce
2 Tbsp mustard
2 Tbsp molasses
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp pepper
1 16 oz can kidney beans, drained
1 16 oz can butter beans, drained
1 16 oz can pork and beans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a Dutch oven kettle, brown ground beef and bacon.  Add onion and saute until onion is soft. Drain fat.
Add brown sugar, catsup, BBQ sauce, mustard, molasses, chili powder and pepper. Mix well.  Add beans and mix well.  Bake for one hour.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Nice side dish for a picnic or BBQ.
