Title:
Cranberry Apple-Almond Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground sausage (sage or breakfast flavored)
2 celry stalk, chopped
1 apple, peeled and chopped
3/4 c dried cranberries
3/4 slivered almonds
1 6 oz (box) commercial stuffing
1 1/2 cups apple cider
1/4 c butter

Directions:
Cook suasage, and drain. Toss in chopped celery, diced apple, cranberries and almonds; set aside.
Bring cider and butter to a boil in a saucepan. Stir in stuffing mix, cover. Remove from heat. Let stand 5 min. Fluff with fork, and let cool for 10 mins.
Combine fruit and suasage misture with stuffing mix and place into a 9x13 inch glass pan. Cover with foil.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 20 mins. Uncover and bake for 10 mins more.
Serve with gravy if desired.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Using commercial packaged stuffing mix, but with a fresh additional flavor of apple, cranberries almonds.
