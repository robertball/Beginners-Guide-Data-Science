Title:
Baron's Fiber One速 Chocolate-Peanut Butter Coconut Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787451.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) bag semisweet chocolate chips
1 (10)ounce bag Reese's Peanut Butter chips
3 3/4 cups Fiber One速 original bran cereal
1 10 ounce bag of shredded coconut

Directions:
Line cookie sheets with waxed paper. In large microwaveable bowl, microwave chocolate chips and peanut butter chips uncovered on High 1 minute, stirring after 30 seconds. Microwave 30 seconds to 1 minute longer, stirring every 15 seconds, until melted and smooth.
Stir in cereal and coconut until well coated. Microwave again for 20-30 seconds if mixture becomes too stiff. Drop mixture by rounded tablespoons full onto waxed paper. Refrigerate until chocolate is firm.
