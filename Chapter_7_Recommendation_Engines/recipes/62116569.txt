Title:
Bacon Cheeseburger Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz uncooked tube or spiral pasta
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup onion
6 bacon strips, diced
1 can (10 3/4 oz) tomato soup, undiluted
1 cup (4 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
salt and pepper to taste
Barbecue sauce, optional

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat unitl beef is no longer pink; drain and set aside. In the same skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove with a slotted spoon to paper towels. Discard drippings. Drian pasta; add to skillet. Add soup, beef, bacon, and salt and pepper to taste heat through. Sprinkle with cheese; cover and cook until the chese is melted. Server with barbecue sauce if desired.
