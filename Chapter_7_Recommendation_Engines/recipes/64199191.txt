Title:
ROTEL FIESTA CHICKEN MAC N CHEESE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1569324.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box spiral or regular Mac n cheese
I can ROTEL, drained
1 lb chicken, cut up into cubes
I can corn, drained
1 2/3 cup hot water
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
-Melt 2 tblspns butter in sauce pan
-Cook chicken med-high for 3 mins
-Add noodles, water, corn, ROTEL, milk, cheese pack, stir
-Boil and reduce heat; cover
-Cook about 25 mins or until not watery anymore

Submitter: 
Amanda Herman
Description: 
This is a recipe I just threw together one day molded after a chicken helper recipe but much better! ;-) It's quick, simple and very easy to make! :-)
