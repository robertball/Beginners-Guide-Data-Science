Title:
Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound frozen Peas
1 - 1-1/2 Cups Mayonaisse (reg. or lite)
2 - 3 teasp. Curry
4 - 6 oz smoked Almonds

Directions:
Mix last three ingredients together.  Add frozen peas and mix well.  Put in refrigerator until ready to serve, but at least 5 or 6 hours to allow peas to defrost.  Serve.

Submitter: 
Cynda Baker
Description: 
This is absolutely the easiest recipe and everyone wants the recipe when I bring it.  East to double or triple.
