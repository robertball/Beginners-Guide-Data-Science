Title:
Applesauce Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/775912.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups unsweetened applesauce
1 egg
1 cup sugar
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 cup raisins - optional
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the first five ingredients.In another bowl combine flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg. Add to applesauce mixture; mix well. Stir in the raisins and walnuts. Pour into a greased 8-in. x 4-in. x 2-in. loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 50-55 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks.
