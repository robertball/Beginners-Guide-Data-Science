Title:
Beef and Blue Cheese Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz Roast Beef, thin sliced
Romaine lettuce, 2 leaves
1/4 cup sliced red pepper strips
Blue Cheese crumbles
8-inch flour tortilla
Blue Cheese dressing

Directions:
Assemble first 4 ingredients on 8-inch flour tortilla.
Top with Blue Cheese Dressing.
