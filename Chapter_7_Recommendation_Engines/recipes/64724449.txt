Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6051645.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. block softened cream cheese
1/2 cup softened butter
3/4 cup powdered sugar
2 T. light brown sugar
1/4 to 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
3/4 to 1 cup miniature chocolate chips, divided

Directions:
In bowl of stand mixer, combine cream cheese and butter and beat with paddle attachment until creamy.
Add powdered sugar, brown sugar and vanilla and mix until combined.
Mix in 1/2 cup of the chocolate chips
Scoop mixture into a small bowl, cover and chill for at least 2 hours or more.
Remove from refrigerator and scoop out the mixture onto a piece of wax paper or plastic wrap.  Using the wax paper or wrap to keep it from sticking to your hands, form mixture into a ball.
Place remaining 1/4 cup of chocolate chips into a clean bowl.  Turn cream cheese ball into bowl and roll in chocolate chips.
Turn back out onto a clean sheet of wax paper or plastic wrap, continuing to mold ball and a gently press chocolate chips all over surface.  Pick up any loose chips and press into any area not covered.
Wrap and chill for at least 1 hour before serving with various cookies and snacks such as vanilla wafers, ginger snaps,  graham crackers or pretzels.
