Title:
Chicken dumplins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 t oil
1 garlic
10 chicken breast
10 thighs
2 T shiro miro
1 egg yolk
2 T flour
POACHING LIQUID
8 c water
1 T sake
2ND PAN
2 c water
3/4 c soy
1/3 c sugar
1/4 c sake
2 T mirin
AFTER ALL ARE COOKED
3 T cornstarch
1/3 c water

Directions:
Cook garlic in oil. Add all to processer till chopped.
Make 1/4 " balls, bring liquid to boil, drop in and simmer till they rise.
Place in 2nd pan for 5 min to finish cooking.
After all are cooked add cornstarch to make a sauce.
Serve over balls.
