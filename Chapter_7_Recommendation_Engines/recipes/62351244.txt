Title:
Shortcut Chicken Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound box rotelli pasta, cooked according to package directions
1 pound of boneless chicken breast, cubed
2 jars of Rago Cheesy Alfredo sauce
4 oz can whole mushrooms
4 oz cans stems and pieces
2 Tablespoons fresh Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta until tender and rinse under running water.  Set aside.  Cook chicken by frying in a non-stick skillet with no extra oil.  In a large pan place Rago, mushrooms (liquid too) and cooked chicken.  Heat until it just starts to boil stirring occasionally. Add pasta and heat through. Top with Parmesan just before serving.
