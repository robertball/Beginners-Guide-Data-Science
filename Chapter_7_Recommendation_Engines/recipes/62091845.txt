Title:
Baked Chicken & Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts
1 Package of Frozen Spinach (drained)
6 oz of Heavy Whipping Cream
1 Tablespoon of Flour
4 Tablespoons of Butter
1 cup shredded swiss

Directions:
Brown chicken breasts in 2 tablespoons of butter and place in lightly greased baking dish.  Cook and completely drain spinach, set aside.  In separate sauce pan melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and add flour over medium high heat.  Allow flour to brown.  Slowly add cream, whipping the butter and flour into cream.  When cream froths up, add spinach to the mixture.  Pour spinach mixture over chicken breasts and cover with mozzarella cheese.  Cover dish and bake for 45 minutes.  Uncover and continue baking for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great and easy dish to make that everyone loves.  My bestfriend's mother used to prepare it ahead of time and then freeze until she was ready to bake it.  It is best served with rice.
