Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium, peeled, and sliced tart apples (Granny Smith)
3/4 c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c.flour
1/2 c. oats
3/4 tsp. cinnamon
3/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/3 c. softened butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. Butter a square baking pan. Place apple slices on pan. Mix remaining ingredients and sprinkle over apples. Bake for 30 minutes or until apples are tender and topping is golden brown.
