Title:
Tomato Mac 'n' Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup
1 cup Prego® Traditional Italian Sauce
1/3 cup milk
4 cups cooked elbow pasta
grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix soup, pasta sauce, milk and pasta in saucepan.  Heat through.
Serve with cheese.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Elbow macaroni is dressed with robust Prego® pasta sauce and a cheesy sauce for a double-good version of this classic favorite.
