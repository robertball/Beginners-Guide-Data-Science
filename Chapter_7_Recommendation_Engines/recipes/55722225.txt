Title:
Cream Cheese Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
sweet pickles, patted dry
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
2 1/2 ounces thin-sliced beef luncheon meat,turkey or ham
can add diced japanelo to cream cheese

Directions:
Spread or pat cream cheese around each pickle. Wrap 2 sheets of meat around each pickle. Refrigerate pickles overnight, slice before serving.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
These tasty snacks are whole kosher dill pickles that are covered with cream cheese and wrapped with thin slices of cooked beef.Modified from: Cream Cheese Pickles
