Title:
Paleo Sunflower Banana Bread/Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c Coconut Flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1-2 tsp cinnamon (depends how much you like cinnamon)
6 eggs
3 ripe bananas
1/4 c coconut sugar (use extra coconut sugar for glazing)
1 tsp alcohol free vanilla extract
1/3 c Sunflower Seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*F
Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl (coconut flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon)
Mash bananas in separate bowl for wet ingredients. Crack eggs into bowl, and mix with bananas. Add vanilla extract and coconut sugar. Whisk together.
Combine dry and wet ingredients. Use a wooden spoon to fold until it's a doughy consistency.
Place into a rectangular pyrex or metal tray with parchment paper OR muffin tins.
Sprinkle sunflower seeds on top of loaf/muffins. Sprinkle extra coconut sugar on top of the bread/muffins for glazing)
Bake for 45 minutes.
Once done, take out of oven, and let cool in the fridge for 20 minutes.
Serve when cool.
