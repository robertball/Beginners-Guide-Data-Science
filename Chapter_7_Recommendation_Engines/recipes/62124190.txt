Title:
Trishie's BBQ Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs Vegetable oil
3 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
3 Tbs Tomato paste
3 Tbs Chicken stock
1 Spring Onion finely chopped (optional)
1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1 1/2 tsp Brown Sugar

Directions:
First measure the oil into a bowl
Then stir in the tomato paste.
When blended stir in the worcestershire sauce and stock.
Finally stir in the cayenne pepper, brown sugar and spring onion.

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
This BBQ marinade can be used with beef, chicken, pork or whatever is your particular favourite. 
Its absolutely fabulous!!
