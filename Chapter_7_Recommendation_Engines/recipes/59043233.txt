Title:
Budget Chantal's New York Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/265892.jpg
Ingredients:
10 graham crackers, crushed
2 tablespoons butter, melted
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup white sugar
4 eggs
3/4 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter. Press onto bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.
In a large bowl, mix cream cheese with sugar until smooth. Mix in the eggs one at a time, mixing just enough to incorporate. Mix in sour cream and vanilla until smooth. Pour filling into prepared crust.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until center is set. Remove from oven and cool on counter for an hour.  Chill in refrigerator until serving, preferably overnight.
