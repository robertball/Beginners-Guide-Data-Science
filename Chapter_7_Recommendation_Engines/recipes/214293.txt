Title:
Lemonade Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990420.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA FREE Fat Free Cream Cheese, softened
1 teaspoon CRYSTAL LIGHT Lemonade Flavor Drink Mix
1/4 cup cold fat-free milk
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP FREE Whipped Topping, thawed, divided
1 (6 ounce) ready-to-use reduced fat graham cracker crumb crust

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and drink mix in large bowl with mixer until well blended. Gradually add milk, mixing until well blended.
Stir in COOL WHIP; spoon into crust.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia
Description: 
Lemonade-flavor drink mix is the flavor secret to this simple low-fat cheesecake dessert, made in a ready-to-use graham cracker crust.
