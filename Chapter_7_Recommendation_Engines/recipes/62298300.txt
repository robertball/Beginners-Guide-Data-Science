Title:
Peanut Butter Crunch Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PEANUT BUTTER CRUNCH BARS
2/3 c. corn syrup
2/3 c. sugar
1 Â½ c. crunchy peanut butter
3 c. corn flakes
6 oz.  choc. chips

Directions:
Grease 9-inch square pan.  In 3-quart saucepan combine corn syrup and sugar.  Stirring constantly, bring to boil over medium heat.  Remove from heat.  Add peanut butter; stir until blended.  Add corn flakes; toss to coat well.  Stir in half of the chocolate chips.  Press evenly into pan; sprinkle remaining chocolate over top.  Let stand 5 minutes to soften chocolate; spread evenly.  Cool until firm
