Title:
Linnie's Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup uncooked white rice
1 teaspoon minced garlic
2 cups chicken broth
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa

Directions:
Saute the rice and garlic with butter in a large saucepan. Pour chicken broth and salsa into the rice mixture. Bring the chicken broth to a full boil and then reduce the temperature to simmer. Simmer for 20 minutes or until the rice is tender. Fluff the rice when finished.
