Title:
Chipotle Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
chopped garlic
orange juice
2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, chopped fine
salt
pepper
cumin

Directions:
combine all ingredients in a large zip-lock plastic bag.  Add pork tenderloin and marinade in refrigerator for at least 2 hours.  Grill to medium.  Best cooked on an outdoor grill.  When done, let rest 5 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
SharonBaker
Description: 
Tender and moist marinated and grilled pork tenderloin.  It is great chopped or shredded and served with soft tortillas, salsa or pico de gallo, and other taco condiments to make pork soft tacos.
