Title:
Chewy Coconut Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Shredded Coconut
1/2 Dark Brown Sugar
1/3 Light Brown Sugar
1/2 Slivered Almonds (toasted)
2 C Bisquick
1 stick butter
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix all till blended, press into 8 X 8 pan coated with cooking spray 350 for 20-24 minutes.
