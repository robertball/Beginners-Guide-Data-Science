Title:
Roasted Lemon Herb Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 Whole chicken equivalent (8-piece cut, Halved, or Qtr'd Chicken)
3 lemons
4 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine the seasoning, salt, mustard powder, garlic powder and black pepper; set aside.
Rinse the chicken thoroughly
Place chicken in a baking dish.
Rub the seasoning mixture over the chicken
Squeeze the juice of 2 lemons into a small bowl or cup, and mix with the olive oil.
Drizzle oil/juice mixture over the chicken.
Cover Pan with Aluminum foil, bake in the preheated oven for 90 minutes at 350(F), or until juices run clear.
Remove foil, lower heat to 275-300 and continue baking until the skin is brown and crisp.
Squeeze juice from remaining lemon over chicken before serving.
