Title:
Dick Clark's Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, minced
1 clove garlic, split
3T butter
1T olive oil
1# fresh mushrooms
1/4 cup tomato paste
5 cups chicken broth
1t marjoram
salt and pepper
1t lemon juice
3T red wine

Directions:
In a heavy saucepan, cook onion and garlic in 1T butter and olive oil until just beginning to brown.  Discard garlic.  Separate mushroom stems from caps.  Mince stems and 1 cup mushroom caps in food processor.  Add to onions.
Thinly slice remaining mushrooms.  Add slices and remaining 2T butter to onions and mushroom caps.  Saute about 5 minutes.  Add tomato paste and chicken stock, mixing well.  Add seasonings, lemon juice, and wine.  Simmer 10 minutes
