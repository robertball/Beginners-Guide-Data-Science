Title:
"Rockin"  Good smashed cauliflower with garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of frozen cauliflower
4 cloves of garlic peeled
1 ounce of chives chopped
3 TBSP of margerine or butter
3 TBSP of light cream
2 TBSP of grated parmesa regiano
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. peel 4 cloves of garlic
2. steam cauliflower and garlic until soft about 15-20 minutes
3. remove from heat and smash garlic cloves, and add back to cauliflower in a medium size pot.
4. Add margerine and chives and smash with a fork or potatoe masher, then add the cream
5. Sprinkle with paremesan and salt and pepper to taste and cook for another 12 minutes on a low flame to absorb the cream and margerine

Submitter: 
cookin' social worker
Description: 
I substituted this dish for mashed potatoes as a low carb alternative and and my husband said it was rockin' good. Flavors  of parmesan, garlice and chives brought cauliflower to a new level and we didn't miss the potatoes
