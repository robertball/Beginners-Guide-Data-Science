Title:
Rich Italian Sausage and Potato Soup by Robbie Lynn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups chopped onion
4 slices pancetta bacon, diced or regular bacon
2.5 teaspoons garlic, minced
4 cups chicken broth
4 potatoes, cubed
4 cups kale, rinsed and julienned
1 cup heavy cream or half and half
1 cup white wine
1 15oz can of navy beans drained
1 pound cooked Italian sausage cubed
parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a 4 quart saucepan over medium heat, saute the onions and the pancetta for 10 minutes, or until onions are almost clear.
Add the garlic and saute for one more minute.
Add the chicken broth and potatoes and bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Add the kale, cream, wine, beans and sausage.
Simmer for 5 more minutes and serve with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
Strips of kale, cream, and cooked sausage are added in the final stage to this potato soup made with onions and pancetta cooked in chicken broth.Modified from: Rich Italian Sausage and Potato Soup
