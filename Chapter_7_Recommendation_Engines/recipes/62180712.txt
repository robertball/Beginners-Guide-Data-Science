Title:
Granny's Buckeyes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 c powdered sugar, sifted (1 1/2 pounds)
1 1/4 c butter, softened
1 (18 oz) crunchy or creamy peanut butter
3 c semisweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 c shortening

Directions:
Position knife blade in food processor bowl; add half of 3 ingredients. Process untill thoroughly blended. Shape mixture into 1-inch balls; cover and chill. Repeat procedure with remaining powdered sugar, butter and peanut butter.
Combine chocolate morsels and shortening in top of double boiler; bring water to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cook until chocolate melts. Dip each ball in chocolate, coating 3/4's; place on wax paper, and let stand until chocolate hardens. Store in an airtight container in the refridgerator.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Our Granny would make a gift plate of delightful candy, and buckeyes was my favorite i looked forward to every year. Now my 5 children make sure i dont forget to make these peanut butter fudge nuggets dipped in chocolate.
