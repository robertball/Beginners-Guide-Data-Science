Title:
Healthy and Wholesome Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups oats
1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt
1 15 oz. can of pumpkin
3 egg whites
1 1/2 Tbsp. Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 Tbsp. Vinegar
1/4 cup hot water
1/4 cup flour (whole wheat is best)
3/4 cup worth (equal to about 10 packets) Stevia or other sweetener
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a food processor or blender and blend until smooth ( there will be small pieces of oats left). Pour into lined muffin tins and bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
chosenbygrace
Description: 
I took the base of this recipe from a recipe for chocolate muffins that I found on Pintrest. With oats, fat-free yogurt, and pumpkin at the core, these muffins are both healthy and delicious!
