Title:
Creamy Zucchini and Lemon Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil - Plus Extra for Drizzling
200g/7oz frozen Peas - defrosted
2 Large Courgettes (Zucchini) - coarsely grated
½ - 1tsp or to taste Chilli Flakes/crushed Dried Chilli
200g/7oz Creamy Soft Cheese
1 Lemon - Zest and Juice
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
400g/14oz Spaghetti

Directions:
In a large pan full of water, drop in some salt and 1tbsp olive oil, and bring to boil;
Add spaghetti, stirring well to ensure strands remain separate and cook for some 10 minutes or so until “al dente”;
Drain BUT reserving some 4tbsp cooking water;
Return the pasta to the pan and drizzle with olive oil;
Meanwhile, blanch the peas in a pan of boiling water for a minute or so and then drain;
In a sauté pan, add and heat 2tbsp olive oil;
Add the courgettes (zucchini), peas, chilli, season with salt and pepper and sauté for some 2 minutes or so;
Add the cheese and half the lemon juice and zest, stirring well until heated through;
If required, add some reserved water to loosen consistency;
Add the spaghetti, tossing well to coat thoroughly with the sauce;
Taste for seasoning and add the remaining lemon juice and zest to taste;
Serve in bowls with crusty bread.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A pasta dish with a difference, creamy, lemony with a touch of heat. Fresh and tasty, especially on a sunny afternoon.
