Title:
Beef and Rice Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup diced onion
3 cloves minced garlic
2 1/2 cups beef broth
1 cup uncooked rice, i used pearl rice
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes w/green chili's
1 can corn, drained
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
1 (8 ounce) package mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup sour cream, optional
1/4 cup sliced black olives, optional

Directions:
Place ground beef, garlic and onion in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain excess fat. Stir in water and rice. Season with beef bouillon, black pepper and garlic salt. Simmer 25 to 30 minutes, or until water is absorbed. Stir in diced tomatoes, corn, beans and green peppers. Simmer another 10 minutes, until green pepper is tender. Sprinkle top with cheese and mix well.
Can top with sour cream and olives if desired.

Description: 
This recipe is quick and easy to prepare and very delicious! Ground beef, rice, onion and green pepper in a one-pot stovetop meal. Use whatever you have as an add in! I tried corn and black beans!Modified from: Beef and Rice Medley
