Title:
Alfredo Mostaccioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package mostaccioli
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.  Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Combine heavy cream and butter in a Dutch oven or large, heavy saucepan over medium heat.  Heat until butter melts, stirring occasionally; be careful not to bring mixture to a boil.  Stir in Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, pepper and garlic powder.  Toss with cooked pasta and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Janis Neill
Description: 
My friends love this Alfredo sauce and it can be used with any type pasta that will hold sauce well.  I've also used this as a base for making a white pizza and it is excellent!  Enjoy!Modified from: Alfredo Mostaccioli
